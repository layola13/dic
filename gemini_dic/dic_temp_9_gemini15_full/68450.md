fames:/feɪmz/| n.|名声，名誉|fame is the state of being known and admired by many people for your achievements or qualities.  It often refers to widespread recognition and respect.|  Her fame spread throughout the country after her victory.|近义词： renown, reputation, glory|反义词：obscurity, anonymity, infamy

一般现在时:
His fame grows with each successful project. = 他在每个成功的项目之后名声越来越大。
The band enjoys considerable fame worldwide. = 这个乐队在全世界享有相当高的知名度。

一般过去时:
The actor's fame faded after a series of unsuccessful films. = 在一系列不成功的电影之后，这位演员的名气逐渐消退。
Her fame reached its peak in the 1980s. = 她的名声在20世纪80年代达到顶峰。

现在完成时:
His contributions to science have earned him lasting fame. = 他对科学的贡献为他赢得了持久的名声。
She has achieved international fame for her work. = 她因其作品而获得了国际声誉。

过去完成时:

Before the scandal, her fame had been untarnished. = 在丑闻发生之前，她的名声一直未受损。

将来时:

The young artist's fame will surely grow in the coming years. =这位年轻艺术家的名声在未来几年肯定会越来越大。

虚拟语气:
