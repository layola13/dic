ripped:/rɪpt/ | adj., v. | 撕裂的；肌肉发达的；被剥夺的；|

英文解析:As an adjective, "ripped" means torn or shredded.  It can also describe someone with very well-defined muscles, often implying a lean and muscular physique. As a verb, it means to tear or shred something violently.

例句:

adj.(撕裂的):
The old jeans were ripped at the knees. = 那条旧牛仔裤膝盖处撕破了。

adj.(肌肉发达的):
He had a ripped physique after months of intense training. =经过几个月的强化训练后，他拥有了健硕的体格。


v.(撕裂):
The strong wind ripped the sail. = 大风撕裂了船帆。

v.(剥夺):
They were ripped off by a fraudulent company. = 他们被一家欺诈公司骗了。


近义词:

adj.(撕裂的):torn, tattered, shredded
adj.(肌肉发达的):muscular, well-built, buff

v.(剥夺):cheated, defrauded, robbed


反义词:

adj.(撕裂的):intact, whole, undamaged
adj.(肌肉发达的):flabby, weak, skinny

词性:adjective, verb
