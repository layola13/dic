nonfinites:/ˌnɒnˈfaɪnaɪts/| n. |非限定动词|Nonfinite verbs are verb forms that do not show tense, person, or number.  They include infinitives (to + verb), participles (present and past), and gerunds (verb + -ing acting as a noun).|Example:  To run is good exercise. (Infinitive)  Running is good exercise. (Gerund)  The running water was beautiful. (Participle)|近义词：verbals|反义词：finites


USAGE->
Infinitives:
To err is human. = 人非圣贤，孰能无过？
To understand this concept requires careful study. = 要理解这个概念需要仔细研究。


Participles:
The sleeping child looked peaceful. = 睡觉的孩子看起来很平静。
Having finished his work, he went home. = 完成工作后，他回家了。


Gerunds:
Swimming is my favorite sport. = 游泳是我最喜欢的运动。
She enjoys reading novels. = 她喜欢阅读小说。


PHRASE->
in order to = 为了
so as to = 以便
going to = 将要
to be honest = 说实话
due to = 由于
