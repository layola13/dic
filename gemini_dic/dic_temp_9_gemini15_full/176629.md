reaccompanies:/ˌriːəkəmˈpʌniːz/| v. |再次陪伴；重新伴奏 |To accompany again; to provide musical accompaniment again.  The word implies a previous instance of accompaniment. |例句：The pianist reaccompanied the singer after a brief intermission.  钢琴家在短暂的中场休息后再次为歌手伴奏。

例句：After the storm subsided, the birds reaccompanied the quiet morning. 风暴过后，鸟儿再次陪伴着宁静的早晨。


近义词：rejoin, accompany again, escort anew | 反义词：abandon, forsake, leave
