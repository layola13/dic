lousier:/ˈluːziər/| adj.|更糟糕的，更差劲的|Worse; of inferior quality.  It is the comparative form of "lousy".|例句：The weather today is lousier than yesterday. 今天的天气比昨天更糟糕。

例句：This plan is lousier than the previous one. 这个计划比之前的更糟糕。


近义词：worse, inferior, poorer, shabbier

反义词：better, superior


词源:源自"louse" (虱子)，最初表示"充满虱子的"，后来引申为"糟糕的"，"劣质的"。  The comparative form "-ier" indicates a higher degree of badness.
