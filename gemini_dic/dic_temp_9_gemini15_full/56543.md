dominator: /ˈdɒmɪneɪtər/ | n. | 支配者；统治者；控制者 | A person or thing that dominates.  Someone or something that exerts control or influence over others. |例句：The company is a dominator in the market. 这家公司在市场上占据主导地位。

例句：He was a dominator in the chess tournament. 他是象棋锦标赛的统治者。


近义词：ruler, master, controller, conqueror, leader

反义词：subordinate, follower, subject


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bully dominates the playground. = 那个恶霸控制着操场。
The company dominates the market. = 这家公司主导着市场。


一般过去时:
The empire dominated the region for centuries. = 这个帝国统治了这个地区几个世纪。
He dominated the conversation. = 他主导了谈话。


现在完成时:
She has dominated the sport for years. = 她多年来一直主导着这项运动。
This company has dominated the industry for decades. = 这家公司几十年来一直主导着这个行业。


过去完成时:
The team had dominated the game before the final minutes. = 在最后几分钟之前，这支球队一直占据主导地位。
Before the merger, they had dominated their market segment. = 在合并之前，他们一直主导着他们的细分市场。


将来时:
They will dominate the industry next year. = 他们明年将主导这个行业。
She will likely dominate the competition. = 她很可能会在比赛中占据主导地位。


虚拟语气:
If he were a dominator, he would be rich. = 如果他是统治者，他会很富有。
If the team had dominated the game, they would have won. = 如果那支球队一直占据主导地位，他们就赢了。


被动语态:
The market is dominated by a few large corporations. = 市场被几家大型公司所控制。
The conversation was dominated by his loud voice. = 谈话被他洪亮的声音所主导。


疑问句:
Who dominates the political landscape? = 谁主导着政治格局？
Does one company dominate the entire industry? = 有一家公司主导整个行业吗？


否定句:
He didn't dominate the debate. = 他并没有主导这场辩论。
The small team did not dominate the larger opponent. = 小型团队没有压制较大的对手。


PHRASE->
Dominate the conversation: 主导谈话
Dominate the game: 统治比赛
Dominate the market: 占据市场主导地位
Dominate the landscape: 占据主导地位
Dominate the discussion: 主导讨论


词源:
From Latin *dominator, from dominari "to rule, control, dominate".  The ultimate root is likely the Proto-Indo-European *dom- "house, home," suggesting a connection between ruling and control of a household or territory.
