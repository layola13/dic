cotton-to: /ˈkɑːtn tuː/ | v. | 变得喜欢；开始认同；习惯于 | To begin to like or accept someone or something.  |例句：I'm starting to cotton to this new job.  我开始喜欢这份新工作了。  |近义词：take to, grow fond of, warm to |反义词：dislike, reject, abhor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He always cottoned to his grandmother's stories. = 他总是喜欢他祖母讲的故事。
They cotton to the new policy. = 他们开始接受这项新政策。


一般过去时:
She quickly cottoned to the rhythm of the music. = 她很快适应了音乐的节奏。
I cottoned to him instantly. = 我立刻就喜欢上他了。


现在完成时:
We have cottoned to the new working environment. = 我们已经适应了新的工作环境。
The team has cottoned to the new strategy. = 团队已经接受了新的策略。


过去完成时:
She had cottoned to his true nature before he confessed. = 在他坦白之前，她就了解了他的真实本性。
They had cottoned to the idea of moving to the countryside. = 他们已经习惯了搬到乡村的想法。


将来时:
I think I'll cotton to this city soon. = 我想我很快就会喜欢上这个城市。
They will cotton to the new technology eventually. = 他们最终会接受这项新技术的。


虚拟语气:
If I had known they would cotton to my plan, I would have proposed it sooner. = 如果我知道他们会接受我的计划，我就会早点提出来。
I wish they would cotton to the truth. = 我希望他们能接受真相。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The new ideas were not easily cottoned to by the older generation. = 老一代人不容易接受这些新想法。(比较生硬)
His proposals were eventually cottoned to by the board. = 他的提案最终被董事会接受了。(比较生硬)


疑问句:
Will they cotton to the changes? = 他们会接受这些变化吗？
Do you think they will cotton to the new rules? = 你认为他们会接受新规吗？


否定句:
He didn't cotton to the idea at first. = 他一开始并不喜欢这个主意。
She never cottoned to his jokes. = 她从来不喜欢他的笑话。



PHRASE->
cotton to someone = 开始喜欢某人
cotton to an idea = 开始接受一个想法
cotton to a situation = 适应某种情况
cotton to a place = 开始喜欢某个地方
cotton to a style = 开始喜欢某种风格
