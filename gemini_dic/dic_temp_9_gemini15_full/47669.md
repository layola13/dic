debris:/ˈdɛbriː/| n.|残骸；碎片；瓦砾|The remains of something that has been destroyed or broken up.  It often refers to scattered pieces of a larger object or structure after an accident, disaster, or destruction.|例句：The debris from the explosion littered the street. 爆炸后的碎片散落了一地。

例句：Rescue workers sifted through the debris looking for survivors. 救援人员在瓦砾中搜寻幸存者。


近义词：rubble, wreckage, fragments, remnants|反义词：intact, whole, complete|名词
