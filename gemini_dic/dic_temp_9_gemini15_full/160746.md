pose:/poʊz/| /pəʊz/| n., v.|摆姿势；姿态；难题；提出；假装|

英文解析：As a noun, "pose" refers to a particular way of holding one's body, a stance or attitude.  As a verb, it means to hold a particular posture, especially for a photograph or painting; to present something, especially a problem or question; or to deliberately behave in a particular way to impress or deceive others.

例句：
The model struck a graceful pose for the photographer. = 模特摆出一个优雅的姿势给摄影师拍照。

She posed a difficult question to the panel. = 她向小组提出了一个难题。


近义词： (n.) posture, attitude, stance; (v.) present, propose, pretend


反义词： (n.) naturalness, spontaneity; (v.) answer, solve


词性：noun, verb
