interindependence:/ˌɪntərɪnˈdɪpɛndəns/| n. |相互依赖，互相依存|The state of being mutually dependent; a relationship in which two or more things depend on each other.  Interdependence highlights the reciprocal nature of the relationship, where each element affects and is affected by the others.  For example, in an ecosystem, plants depend on animals for pollination and animals depend on plants for food, creating a web of interdependencies.|Example: The global economy is characterized by a high degree of interindependence between nations. 全球经济的特点是国家之间高度相互依赖。

The success of the project relies on the interindependence of the team members.  项目的成功依赖于团队成员之间的相互依赖。|近义词：mutual dependence, reciprocity, interconnectedness|反义词：independence, autonomy, self-sufficiency|名词
