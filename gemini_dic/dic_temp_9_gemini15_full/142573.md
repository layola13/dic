osteochondroma:/ˌɒstiːoʊkɒnˈdroʊmə/|/ˌɒstiːəʊkɒnˈdrəʊmə/| n. |骨软骨瘤|A benign (noncancerous) tumor that arises from the growth plate of a bone. It's composed of both cartilage and bone. Osteochondromas are the most common type of benign bone tumor. They typically occur in adolescents and young adults and often cause no symptoms. However, they can sometimes cause pain, pressure on nerves or blood vessels, or fracture.|例句：The X-ray revealed a small osteochondroma on his femur. X光显示他的股骨上有一个小的骨软骨瘤。|例句：  She experienced persistent pain due to the large osteochondroma pressing on a nerve.  由于大的骨软骨瘤压迫神经，她经历了持续的疼痛。|近义词：bone spur (sometimes, depending on the presentation)|反义词：malignant bone tumor|名词
