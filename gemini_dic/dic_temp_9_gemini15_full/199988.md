skater:/ˈskeɪtər/| n. |滑冰者，滑板运动员 |A person who skates, especially on ice or on a skateboard.  |例句：My son is a talented skater.  我的儿子是一位有天赋的滑冰者。
例句：The skater landed a perfect triple axel.  这位滑冰运动员完成了完美的阿克塞尔三周跳。|近义词：ice skater, skateboarder, roller skater|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He skates every Saturday. = 他每个星期六都滑冰。
She skates with grace and skill. = 她优雅而熟练地滑冰。

一般过去时:
I skated on the frozen lake yesterday. = 我昨天在冰冻的湖面上滑冰。
They skated until it got dark. = 他们滑冰直到天黑。

现在完成时:
He has skated since he was five years old. = 他从五岁就开始滑冰了。
She has never skated before. = 她以前从未滑冰过。

过去完成时:
I had skated for hours before I felt tired. = 我滑了几个小时才感到疲倦。
They had already skated when I arrived. = 我到达时他们已经滑冰了。

将来时:
We will skate in the park tomorrow. = 我们明天将在公园里滑冰。
She will be skating in the competition next week. = 她下周将参加比赛滑冰。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would skate more often. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常滑冰。
I wish I could skate like a professional. = 我希望我能像职业选手一样滑冰。

被动语态:
The ice rink is often skated on by many people. = 许多人经常在这个溜冰场上滑冰。(被动)
The competition will be skated in the new arena. = 比赛将在新的竞技场举行。(被动)

疑问句:
Do you skate? = 你滑冰吗？
Where do they skate? = 他们在哪里滑冰？

否定句:
I don't skate very well. = 我滑冰不太好。
He didn't skate yesterday because it rained. = 他昨天因为下雨没有滑冰。
