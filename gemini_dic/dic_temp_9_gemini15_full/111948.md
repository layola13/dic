lollopy:/ˈlɒləpi/| adj.| 笨拙的；懒散的；无精打采的 |  Describes something clumsy, ungainly, or lacking energy. It suggests a loose, floppy, or shambling movement.  |例句：The dog had a lollopy gait after its long run.  这只狗长跑后步态蹒跚。

The old chair had a lollopy leg, threatening to collapse. 那把旧椅子一条腿松垮垮的，快要塌了。|近义词：clumsy, awkward, ungainly, languid, slack |反义词：nimble, agile, energetic, spry
