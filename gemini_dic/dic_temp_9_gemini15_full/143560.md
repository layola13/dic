outhired:/ˌaʊtˈhaɪərd/ | vt. | 外包；雇佣外部人员 | To hire an outside company or individual to perform tasks or services that were previously done internally.  | Example: The company outhired its IT support to a specialized firm. 公司将IT支持外包给了一家专业公司。|近义词：outsource, contract out |反义词：in-house, internalize |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They outhire their marketing to a freelance agency. = 他们将市场营销外包给了一家自由职业者机构。
The company outhires its cleaning services. = 公司将清洁服务外包。


一般过去时:
We outhired the project to a consulting firm. = 我们将项目外包给了一家咨询公司。
The factory outhired its security to a private company. = 工厂将安保外包给了一家私营公司。


现在完成时:
They have outhired several projects this year. = 他们今年已经外包了好几个项目。
The government has outhired many services to private companies. = 政府已将许多服务外包给私营公司。


过去完成时:
The company had outhired its accounting before the merger. = 公司在合并之前已经将会计工作外包了。
They had outhired the software development before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经将软件开发外包了。


将来时:
We will outhire the data analysis to a third-party vendor. = 我们将把数据分析外包给第三方供应商。
The organization will outhire the training program to an external provider. = 该组织将把培训项目外包给外部提供商。


虚拟语气:
If we needed more staff, we would outhire a temporary agency. = 如果我们需要更多员工，我们会外包给一家临时机构。
It would be more efficient if we outhired the design work. = 如果我们将设计工作外包，效率会更高。


被动语态:
The project was outhired to a reputable firm. = 该项目外包给了一家信誉良好的公司。
The cleaning services are being outhired to a new company. = 清洁服务正在外包给一家新公司。


疑问句:
Will they outhire the development of the new app? = 他们会将新应用程序的开发外包吗？
Has the company outhired its legal services? = 公司是否已将法律服务外包？


否定句:
We did not outhire the construction work. = 我们没有将建筑工作外包。
They will not outhire the customer support. = 他们不会将客户支持外包。

词源:
The word "outhired" is a relatively recent addition to the English vocabulary, formed by combining the prefix "out-" (meaning "to a greater extent" or "outside") with the verb "hired."  The usage reflects the increasing trend of outsourcing in modern business.
