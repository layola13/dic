ego:/ˈiːɡoʊ/|/ˈiːɡəʊ/| n. |自我；自负；自我意识 |The ego is the part of the mind that mediates between the conscious and the unconscious and is responsible for reality testing and a sense of personal identity.  自我指心灵中介于意识和潜意识之间的那部分，负责现实测试和个人认同感。|He has a huge ego. 他非常自负。|self, self-esteem, pride |humility, modesty, selflessness |名词
