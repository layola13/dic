button-downs:/ˈbʌtən daʊnz/| adj. n.|纽扣衬衫，扣子衬衫|An adjective describing shirts with buttons down the front, or a noun referring to such shirts.  The term often implies a more formal or dressy style compared to other shirts.|例句：He wore a crisp white button-down shirt to the interview. 他在面试时穿了一件挺括的白色纽扣衬衫。  例句：Button-downs are a staple in any professional wardrobe. 纽扣衬衫是任何职业衣橱的必备品。|近义词：dress shirt, collared shirt|反义词：t-shirt, polo shirt|形容词，名词
