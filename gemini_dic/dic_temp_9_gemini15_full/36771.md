collusory:/kəˈluːzəri/ | adj. | 欺诈的；虚假的；串通的 |  Deceptive; intended to deceive; fraudulent; sham.  It often refers to an agreement or act made with the intention to defraud. | 例句：The collusory agreement between the two companies to fix prices was uncovered by the investigation.  两家公司串通起来操纵价格的欺诈性协议被调查揭露了。


The collusory bankruptcy filing was designed to avoid paying creditors.  虚假的破产申请是为了避免偿还债权人。 | 近义词：fraudulent, deceptive, sham, collusive | 反义词：genuine, honest, legitimate | 词性:形容词
