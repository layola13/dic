Sheridan:/ˈʃɛrɪdən/|n.|谢里丹（姓氏）|A surname of Irish origin.|The name Sheridan is a surname of Irish origin. It is derived from the Gaelic name Ó Séarraid or O'Sheridan, meaning "descendant of Séarraid".  Séarraid was a personal name, and its meaning is uncertain, but possible interpretations include "the cheerful one" or "the charmer".|Many famous people share this surname, including Richard Brinsley Sheridan, the famous playwright.|近义词:无|反义词:无|名词
