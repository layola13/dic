miraculously:/mɪˈrækjʊləsli/| adv. |奇迹般地，不可思议地 |  In a way that is surprising and seems impossible or unlikely.  It suggests an event that defies normal expectations and is often attributed to divine intervention or sheer luck. |例句：Miraculously, he survived the car crash. = 奇迹般地，他从车祸中幸存下来了。

例句：She miraculously recovered from a serious illness. = 她奇迹般地从重病中康复了。


近义词：wonderfully, astonishingly, incredibly, amazingly, unexpectedly

反义词：predictably, expectedly, unsurprisingly


词性: 副词
