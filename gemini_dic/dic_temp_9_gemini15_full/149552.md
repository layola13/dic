pager:/ˈpeɪdʒər/| n. |寻呼机，传呼机 |A pager is a small, portable electronic device that receives messages.  It typically displays a number or short message indicating who is trying to contact the user. |例句：He carried a pager in his pocket, constantly waiting for calls. 他口袋里带着寻呼机，不停地等待着电话。

例句：Before cell phones, pagers were the primary way for doctors to be contacted during emergencies. 在手机出现之前，寻呼机是医生在紧急情况下被联系的主要方式。


近义词：beeper |反义词：cell phone, smartphone |词性：名词
