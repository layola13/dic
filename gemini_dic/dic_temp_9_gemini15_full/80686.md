gorger:/ˈɡɔːrdʒər/| n. |暴食者；贪婪者；吞食者|A gorger is someone who eats greedily or excessively.  It can also refer to something that swallows or consumes something else voraciously.|例句：The python was a gorger, swallowing the rabbit whole.  这条蟒蛇是个贪婪的吞食者，它整个吞下了兔子。  The gorger at the buffet table left little for others.  自助餐桌旁的那个暴食者几乎没给其他人留下什么东西。|近义词：glutton, gourmand, devourer |反义词：abstemious person, ascetic |名词
