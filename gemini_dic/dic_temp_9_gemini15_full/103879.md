jubilee:/ˈdʒuːbɪliː/| n. |禧年，庆祝；欢庆 |A jubilee is a special anniversary, especially one celebrating 25 or 50 years.  It's often a time of celebration and rejoicing.  The term can also refer to a period of great celebration or rejoicing. |例句：The Queen's Golden Jubilee was a huge national celebration. 女王的黄金禧年是一个盛大的全国性庆祝活动。

The company celebrated its 50th jubilee with a lavish party. 这家公司以盛大的派对庆祝其50周年禧年。


|近义词：anniversary, celebration, festival |反义词：mourning, sorrow |名词
