overpay:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪ/ | vt. | 付款过多；多付钱 | To pay more money for something than it is worth or than is necessary.  |例句：I overpaid for the tickets by at least $20. 我至少多付了20美元的票款。

例句：The company overpaid its employees significantly last year.  去年公司给员工支付了过多的薪水。


近义词：overspend, pay too much


反义词：underpay


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overpays for everything. = 他总是付钱太多。
They overpay their taxes every year. = 他们每年都多交税。


一般过去时:
I overpaid for that car. = 我买那辆车付了太多钱。
She overpaid her rent last month. = 她上个月房租付多了。


现在完成时:
I have overpaid on my mortgage. = 我已经多付了抵押贷款。
They have overpaid their employees for years. = 他们多年来一直给员工付了过多的工资。


过去完成时:
We had overpaid for the hotel room before we realized it. = 我们在意识到之前就已经为酒店房间付了太多钱。
They had overpaid their taxes and received a refund. = 他们多交了税，并收到了退款。


将来时:
I will overpay if I'm not careful. = 如果我不小心，我会付钱太多。
They will likely overpay for the property. = 他们可能会为该房产支付过高的价格。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't been in a hurry, I wouldn't have overpaid. = 如果我不那么匆忙，我就不会付钱太多。
I wish I hadn't overpaid for the house. = 我真希望我没有为这房子付太多钱。


被动语态:
The workers were overpaid by the careless accountant. = 粗心的会计多付了工人的工资。
The bill was overpaid, and a refund was issued. = 账单多付了，并已退款。


疑问句:
Did you overpay for that item? = 你买那件东西付钱太多了吗？
Have they ever overpaid on their taxes? = 他们有没有多交过税？


否定句:
I didn't overpay for the groceries. = 我买杂货没有付太多钱。
They didn't overpay their employees. = 他们没有给员工付太多钱。
