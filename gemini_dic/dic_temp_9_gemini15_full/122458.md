mobilising: /ˈmoʊbəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 动员；调动；使…活动 | The act of mobilizing resources, people, or systems for a particular purpose.  It implies bringing something into action or readiness. | 例句：The army is mobilising troops to the border.  军队正在向边境调动军队。

The government is mobilising resources to combat the pandemic. 政府正在动员资源来对抗疫情。


近义词：mobilize, muster, rally, activate, marshal


反义词：demobilize, deactivate, disband


USAGE->
