pre-claim:/priːˈkleɪm/| adj.|预先声明；预先声称|Something that is pre-claimed is stated or asserted before something else happens.|The company issued a pre-claim about their new product's features.  这家公司预先声明了他们新产品的功能。|近义词：pre-announcement, advance notice|反义词：post-claim, afterthought


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pre-claims his innocence before the trial. 他在审判前预先声明了他的清白。
They pre-claim their support for the project. 他们预先声明他们支持这个项目。


一般过去时:
She pre-claimed her intention to resign last week. 她上周预先声明了她辞职的意图。
The government pre-claimed a victory before the election results were in. 政府在选举结果出来之前就预先宣布胜利了。


现在完成时:
He has pre-claimed his right to the inheritance. 他已经预先声明了他继承遗产的权利。
They have pre-claimed their commitment to environmental protection. 他们已经预先声明了他们对环境保护的承诺。


过去完成时:
She had pre-claimed her dissatisfaction with the service before she filed a complaint. 在她投诉之前，她就预先声明了她对服务的 dissatisfaction。
They had pre-claimed their opposition to the bill before the debate began. 在辩论开始之前，他们就预先声明了他们反对该法案。


将来时:
He will pre-claim his candidacy for mayor next month. 他下个月将预先宣布他竞选市长的意图。
They will pre-claim their findings in the upcoming scientific conference. 他们将在即将举行的科学会议上预先声明他们的发现。


虚拟语气:
If he had pre-claimed his intentions, things might have gone differently. 如果他预先声明了他的意图，事情可能会有所不同。
I wish she had pre-claimed her need for assistance. 我希望她预先声明了她需要帮助。


被动语态:
The company's new policy was pre-claimed in a press release. 公司的新政策在新闻发布会上预先声明了。
His innocence was pre-claimed by his lawyer. 他的清白由他的律师预先声明了。


疑问句:
Did he pre-claim his interest in the property? 他是否预先声明了他对这处房产的兴趣？
Had they pre-claimed their objections to the plan? 他们是否预先声明了他们对该计划的反对意见？


否定句:
He did not pre-claim his intention to leave. 他没有预先声明他离开的意图。
They did not pre-claim any potential risks associated with the project. 他们没有预先声明与该项目相关的任何潜在风险。
