rasterizing:/ˈræstəraɪzɪŋ/| v.|光栅化，栅格化|The process of converting vector graphics into a raster image, consisting of a grid of pixels.|例句：The software rasterizes the vector image into a bitmap for display on the screen.  软件将矢量图像光栅化成位图以便在屏幕上显示。


例句：The printer rasterizes the document before printing it. 打印机在打印文档之前将其光栅化。


近义词：pixelating, rendering


反义词：vectorizing


词性：动词
