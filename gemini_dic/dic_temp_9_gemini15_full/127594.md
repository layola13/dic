need:/niːd/| n. v.|需要，需求；必须|The word "need" can function as both a noun and a verb. As a noun, it refers to a requirement or necessity. As a verb, it indicates a requirement or obligation.  |例句：I have a need for more sleep. 我需要更多睡眠。 I need to go to the store. 我需要去商店。|近义词：require, want, demand, necessitate|反义词：surplus, abundance, plenty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I need a new car. = 我需要一辆新车。
They need more time to finish the project. = 他们需要更多时间来完成项目。

一般过去时:
I needed help yesterday. = 我昨天需要帮助。
She needed a break from work. = 她需要休息一下。

现在完成时:
I have needed this for a long time. = 我很久以前就需要这个了。
We have needed a new computer for months. = 我们几个月来一直需要一台新电脑。

过去完成时:
I had needed the information earlier. = 我早些时候就需要这个信息了。
They had needed more resources to complete the study. = 他们需要更多资源才能完成这项研究。

将来时:
I will need your help tomorrow. = 我明天需要你的帮助。
We will need to book the tickets in advance. = 我们需要提前预订机票。

虚拟语气:
I wish I didn't need to work so hard. = 我希望我不需要这么努力工作。
It would be better if you didn't need to go. = 如果你不需要去就好了。

被动语态:
More resources are needed for the project. = 这个项目需要更多资源。
Your help is needed immediately. = 你的帮助迫在眉睫。

疑问句:
Do you need anything else? = 你还需要其他东西吗？
Does he need a ride? = 他需要搭车吗？

否定句:
I don't need your help. = 我不需要你的帮助。
They don't need to worry about it. = 他们不必为此担心。


PHRASE->
