oaky:/ˈoʊki/| adj.|橡木味的，橡木似的|Having the flavor or aroma of oak, typically from aging in oak barrels.  This is often used to describe the taste of wine, whiskey, or other spirits.|例句：The wine has a pronounced oaky flavor. 这款酒有明显的橡木味。|例句：This bourbon has a lovely, mellow oaky finish. 这款波旁威士忌有着宜人醇厚的橡木味回甘。|近义词：woody, oak-flavored|反义词：fruity (in the context of wine), lacking oaky notes
