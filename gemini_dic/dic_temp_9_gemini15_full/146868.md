overinflating:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪtɪŋ/| v.|过度充气；夸大；过分膨胀 |To inflate something to an excessive degree, beyond its normal or recommended level. This can refer to literal inflation (like a balloon) or figurative inflation (like prices or claims).| 例句：Overinflating the tires can lead to a blowout. 过度充气轮胎会导致爆胎。

The company was accused of overinflating its profits.  该公司被指控夸大其利润。|近义词：hyperinflate, exaggerate, overstate |反义词：underinflate, deflate, downplay
