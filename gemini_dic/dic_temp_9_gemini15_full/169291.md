promiser:/ˈprɑːmɪsər/| n. |承诺者，保证人 | A promiser is a person who makes a promise.  |Example: The promiser failed to keep their word.  承诺者失信了。|近义词：guarantor, pledger |反义词：breaker (of promises)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The promiser assures us of timely delivery. 承诺者向我们保证会准时送达。
He is a reliable promiser. 他是一个可靠的承诺者。


一般过去时:
The promiser guaranteed the quality of the product. 承诺者保证了产品的质量。
She was a trustworthy promiser in the past. 她过去是一个值得信赖的承诺者。


现在完成时:
The promiser has consistently fulfilled their obligations. 承诺者一直履行其义务。
He has proved himself to be a responsible promiser. 他证明自己是一个负责任的承诺者。


过去完成时:
The promiser had already given his word before the deadline. 承诺者在截止日期之前就已经给出承诺了。
They had been reliable promisers throughout the project. 在整个项目中，他们一直都是可靠的承诺者。


将来时:
The promiser will deliver the goods next week. 承诺者下周将交付货物。
