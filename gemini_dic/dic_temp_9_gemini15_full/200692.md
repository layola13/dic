sleepily:/ˈsliːpɪli/| adj.|困倦地；昏昏欲睡地|In a sleepy manner; drowsily.  The adjective "sleepily" describes an action or state characterized by sleepiness or drowsiness.|例句：He spoke sleepily, his eyes half-closed. 他困倦地说着话，眼睛半闭着。
例句：The cat stretched sleepily in the sun. 猫懒洋洋地躺在阳光下晒太阳。|近义词：drowsily, sluggishly, lethargically|反义词：alertly, briskly, energetically|形容词
