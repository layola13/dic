dualist:/ˈduːəlɪst/| n. adj.|二元论者；二元论的|A person who believes in dualism, or the theory that reality consists of two fundamental entities, such as mind and matter.  A dualist approach considers two opposing forces or perspectives.|Example:  Many philosophers are dualists, believing in a separation between mind and body. 许多哲学家是二元论者，相信身心分离。|近义词: dichotomist,  binary thinker|反义词: monist, materialist
