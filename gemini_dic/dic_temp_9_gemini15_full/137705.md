nonusurpingly: /ˌnɒnjuːˈzɜːrpɪŋli/ | adv. | 不篡夺地；不霸占地 |  Not seizing power or control unjustly or forcefully; in a way that does not usurp.  It implies a peaceful and rightful claim or possession. |


例句：

The new government promised to rule nonusurpingly, respecting the rights and freedoms of all citizens. = 新政府承诺将不篡夺权力地执政，尊重所有公民的权利和自由。

He inherited the throne nonusurpingly, as the rightful heir. = 他作为合法继承人，不费吹灰之力地继承了王位。


近义词：peacefully, legitimately, rightfully, justly


反义词：usurpatively, tyrannically, forcefully, unjustly


词性: 副词
