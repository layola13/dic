redeclared:/ˌriːˈdeklɛərd/| v. |重新声明；再次宣布|To declare something again, often with a change or correction.|The company redeclared its commitment to environmental sustainability after the recent scandal. 公司在最近的丑闻后再次声明其对环境可持续性的承诺。|redeclare, restate, reaffirm|deny, retract|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They redeclare their support for the project. = 他们再次声明支持这个项目。
The government redeclares its commitment to economic reform. = 政府再次承诺进行经济改革。


一般过去时:
She redeclared her love for him. = 她再次向他表白爱意。
The witness redeclared his testimony. = 证人再次陈述了他的证词。


现在完成时:
He has redeclared his candidacy for mayor. = 他已经再次宣布竞选市长。
The company has redeclared its profits for the quarter. = 公司已经再次公布了本季度的利润。


过去完成时:
They had redeclared their intentions before the meeting. = 他们在会议前就已经再次声明了他们的意图。
The judge had redeclared the verdict after reviewing the evidence. = 法官在审查证据后再次宣布了判决。


将来时:
She will redeclare her allegiance to the country. = 她将再次宣誓效忠国家。
The president will redeclare a state of emergency. = 总统将再次宣布紧急状态。


虚拟语气:
If they redeclared their support, we would continue the project. = 如果他们再次表示支持，我们将继续该项目。
I wish he had redeclared his intentions earlier. = 我希望他早点再次声明他的意图。


被动语态:
The policy was redeclared by the government. = 该政策由政府再次宣布。
His innocence was redeclared after a new trial. = 在重审之后，他的清白被再次宣告。


疑问句:
Will the company redeclare its dividends? = 公司会再次宣布分红吗？
Has the government redeclared its position on the issue? = 政府是否已经就此事再次表明立场？


否定句:
They did not redeclare their opposition to the plan. = 他们没有再次声明反对该计划。
The judge did not redeclare the sentence. = 法官没有再次宣布判决。


PHRASE->
redeclared assets = 重新申报的资产
redeclared income = 重新申报的收入
redeclared value = 重新申报的价值
redeclared intention = 再次声明的意图
redeclared policy = 再次声明的政策
