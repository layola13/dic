penetr:/ˈpɛnɪtər/| v.|渗透，穿透，洞察|to pierce or enter; to permeate or affect deeply.|例句：The rain penetrated the tent. 雨水渗透进了帐篷。|例句：His words penetrated her defenses. 他的话语穿透了她的防线。|近义词：perforate, pierce, permeate|反义词：surface, skim


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain penetrates the soil. 雨水渗透土壤。
The aroma penetrates the air. 香气弥漫在空气中。

一般过去时:
The bullet penetrated his chest. 子弹穿透了他的胸膛。
The scent penetrated the room. 香味充满了房间。

现在完成时:
The idea has penetrated the public consciousness. 这个想法已经深入人心。
The roots have penetrated deep into the ground. 根深深地扎入了地下。

过去完成时:
The water had penetrated the walls before we noticed. 我们注意到之前，水已经渗透了墙壁。
The rumors had penetrated the village before the official announcement. 官方宣布之前，谣言已传遍了村庄。

将来时:
The missile will penetrate the enemy's defenses. 导弹将穿透敌人的防线。
The truth will eventually penetrate. 真相终将大白。

虚拟语气:
If the knife had penetrated deeper, it would have been fatal. 如果刀子再深一些，就会致命。
If the message had penetrated, they might have acted differently. 如果信息传达到位，他们可能会采取不同的行动。

被动语态:
The wall was penetrated by the roots. 墙壁被树根穿透了。
The secret was penetrated by the spies. 秘密被间谍泄露了。

疑问句:
Does the light penetrate the fog? 光线能穿透雾霾吗？
Will your argument penetrate the opposition? 你的论点能说服反对者吗？

否定句:
The water didn't penetrate the roof. 水没有渗透屋顶。
His words didn't penetrate her hardened heart. 他的话语没有触动她冰冷的心。
