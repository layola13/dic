/ˈdɪðərər/| n. |犹豫不决的人；优柔寡断的人 | A person who hesitates or wavers in making decisions; someone who is indecisive.  | He's such a ditherer; he can never make up his mind. 他真是个犹豫不决的人，他总是拿不定主意。 | waverer, procrastinator, vacillator | decisive person, resolute person | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dithers over every little decision. = 他每一个小决定都犹豫不决。
She dithers before making important choices. = 她做重要选择之前总是犹豫不决。


一般过去时:
He dithered for hours before choosing a restaurant. = 他在选择餐馆之前犹豫了几个小时。
She dithered about the best way to approach the problem. = 她对解决这个问题的最佳方法犹豫不决。


现在完成时:
He has dithered over this project for weeks. = 他在这个项目上犹豫了几个星期。
She has dithered so much that she's missed the deadline. = 她犹豫了这么久，错过了截止日期。


过去完成时:
He had dithered for days before finally making a decision. = 他犹豫了几天才最终做出决定。
She had dithered so long that the opportunity had passed. = 她犹豫了太久，机会错过了。


将来时:
He will dither over this for days, I'm sure. = 我肯定他会为此犹豫好几天。
She will probably dither before committing to anything. = 她很可能在承诺任何事情之前都会犹豫。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't dithered, he would have finished the project on time. = 如果他没有犹豫，他就会按时完成项目。
If she hadn't dithered, she would have gotten the promotion. = 如果她没有犹豫，她就会得到晋升。


被动语态:  (This word is rarely used passively.)  *The decision was dithered over for days.* (This is grammatically correct but unnatural.) = 这个决定犹豫了好几天。(这句话语法正确，但不自然)
*The matter was extensively debated, much dithering taking place before a conclusion was reached.* (This is a more natural way to express the passive idea.) = 这件事被广泛讨论，在得出结论之前进行了大量的犹豫。


疑问句:
Does he always dither before making a decision? = 他做决定前总是犹豫吗？
Is she the kind of person who dithers? = 她是不是那种犹豫不决的人？


否定句:
He didn't dither; he made a quick decision. = 他没有犹豫；他迅速做出了决定。
She didn't dither this time; she acted decisively. = 她这次没有犹豫；她果断地采取了行动。


PHRASE->
There aren't many common phrases directly using "ditherer."  The word itself describes the action and the person.  However, phrases describing the *action* of dithering could include:

to dither about something  = 对某事犹豫不决
to dither over a decision = 对某个决定犹豫不决
to dither and dawdle = 犹豫和磨蹭
to spend time dithering = 花时间犹豫
to be caught in a dither = 陷入犹豫不决的状态
