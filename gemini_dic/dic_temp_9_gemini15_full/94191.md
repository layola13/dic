imperatory:/ɪmˈpærətɔːri/ | adj. | 命令的；强制的；权威的 |  Of, relating to, or expressing a command; commanding.  Characterized by or expressing command; imperative. |例句：The general issued an imperatory order to attack. 将军发布了进攻的命令。

The imperatory tone of his voice left no room for argument. 他命令的语气不容置疑。


近义词：mandatory, commanding, authoritative, dictatorial | 反义词：optional, permissive, voluntary


词源:  From Late Latin *imperatorius, from Latin imperator ("commander, emperor").  The ultimate root is the Latin verb impero, meaning "to command."
