chauffeur:/ʃoʊˈfɜːr/| n. |司机，专职司机| A chauffeur is a person employed to drive a private car for someone.  Chauffeurs are typically highly skilled and experienced drivers, often associated with wealth or high social status. |He hired a chauffeur to drive him to the airport. 他雇了一位司机开车送他去机场。|driver, operator| pedestrian, passenger|名词
