matweed: /ˈmætwiːd/ | n. | 垫草；草皮；草席 |  A tangled mass of low-growing plants, especially on wasteland or poor soil.  It often forms a mat-like covering. | Example: The sheep grazed on the sparse matweed that covered the hillside.  羊群啃食着覆盖山坡的稀疏垫草。


The matweed was thick enough to smother the young seedlings.  垫草很厚，足以闷死幼苗。


近义词：turf, sod, sward, vegetation


反义词：bare ground, exposed soil


词性：名词
