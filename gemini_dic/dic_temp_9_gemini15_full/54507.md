disjoint:/dɪsˈdʒɔɪnt/| adj.|不相交的；分离的；不连续的 |  Meaning:  Describes sets, events, or other entities that have no elements in common.  It can also refer to something that is disconnected or separated. | Example: The two sets of data are disjoint; they share no common values.  两个数据集不相交；它们没有共同的值。The broken bone was disjoint, requiring surgery.  骨折脱臼了，需要手术。| Synonyms: separate, distinct, unconnected, independent | Antonyms: connected, intersecting, overlapping |
