deans:/diːnz/| n.|院长们；教务长们|The word "deans" is the plural form of "dean," referring to a senior member of the clergy or a senior academic administrator.  In a religious context, a dean might be the head of a cathedral chapter. In an academic setting, a dean is typically the head of a college, faculty, or school within a larger university. |例句：The deans met to discuss the new curriculum. 院长们开会讨论新课程。

The deans of the various colleges are responsible for their respective student bodies. 各学院的院长负责各自的学生群体。


近义词：heads, administrators, principals(in some contexts)|反义词：None directly applicable, but "students" or "faculty" could be considered opposites in the academic context. |词性:Noun
