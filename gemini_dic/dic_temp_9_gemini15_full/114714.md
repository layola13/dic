mallee:/ˈmæliː/| n.| 马利树；马利灌木丛 |A mallee is a type of eucalyptus tree that grows in Australia, characterized by multiple stems arising from a lignotuber (a swollen, woody base).  These trees are well-adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions. |The mallee scrub stretched for miles across the landscape.|近义词：eucalyptus (in certain contexts)|反义词：无
