evaluates:/ɪˈvæljuˌeɪts/| vt.|评估；评价；估价|To form an idea of the amount, number, or value of something.|The teacher evaluates students' work based on several criteria.  老师根据几个标准来评估学生的工作。|The committee will evaluate the proposal and make a decision. 委员会将评估该提案并做出决定。|assess, appraise, judge|underestimates, undervalues|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He evaluates the situation carefully. 他仔细评估局势。
She evaluates her employees' performance annually. 她每年评估员工的表现。


一般过去时:
The manager evaluated the project's success. 经理评估了项目的成功。
They evaluated the risks before making the investment. 他们在投资前评估了风险。


现在完成时:
She has evaluated all the applications. 她已经评估了所有申请。
The committee has evaluated the evidence and reached a verdict. 委员会已经评估了证据并做出了裁决。


过去完成时:
They had evaluated the damage before calling the insurance company. 他们在联系保险公司之前已经评估了损失。
He had evaluated the options before making his decision. 他在做决定之前已经评估了各种选择。


将来时:
She will evaluate the results next week. 她下周将评估结果。
The experts will evaluate the environmental impact. 专家们将评估环境影响。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would evaluate the proposal more thoroughly. 如果我有更多时间，我会更彻底地评估该提案。
It is important that he evaluates the data objectively.  重要的是他客观地评估数据。


被动语态:
The proposal was evaluated by the committee. 该提案由委员会进行了评估。
The damage will be evaluated by an expert. 损失将由专家评估。


疑问句:
How will you evaluate the effectiveness of the program? 你将如何评估该项目的有效性？
Does the company regularly evaluate its employees' performance? 公司是否定期评估员工的表现？


否定句:
He did not evaluate the risks properly. 他没有正确评估风险。
They failed to evaluate the long-term consequences. 他们未能评估长远后果。
