innocentness:/ˈɪnəsəns/ | n. |天真无邪；清白；无辜 | The state or quality of being innocent; freedom from guilt or wrongdoing.  It often implies a lack of experience or knowledge of the world, as well as a purity of heart and mind. | 例句：The innocentness of her expression was touching.  她的表情天真无邪，令人感动。  The court found him innocent, highlighting his innocentness in the crime. 法院判定他无罪，突显了他在这起犯罪中的清白。| 近义词：innocence, purity, naiveté, guilelessness | 反义词：guilt, culpability, wickedness, corruption | 名词
