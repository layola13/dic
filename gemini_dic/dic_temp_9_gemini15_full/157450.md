pirouette:/ˌpɪrʊˈɛt/| v. n.|旋转，旋转跳跃|To spin on the toes or ball of one foot, especially in ballet.  A pirouette is a rapid turn on the point or demi-point of one foot, a common movement in ballet. |例句：The ballerina executed a perfect pirouette.  那位芭蕾舞演员完美地完成了一个旋转跳跃。

The dancer pirouetted gracefully across the stage. 舞者优雅地在舞台上旋转跳跃。|近义词：twirl, whirl, spin, revolve|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be related to slow movements or lack of movement.|动词，名词

词源:

The word "pirouette" comes from the French word "pirouette," which in turn is derived from the Old French word "piroette," meaning "spinning top."  The ultimate origin is likely onomatopoeic, imitating the sound of spinning.
