dunlin:/ˈdʌnlɪn/| n. |矶鹬|A small wading bird with a long, slightly down-curved bill, short legs, and brownish-grey plumage.  Dunlins are common in coastal areas and wetlands worldwide. They are known for their migratory habits and their ability to feed on a variety of small invertebrates.|例句：We saw a flock of dunlins feeding on the mudflats. 我们看到一群矶鹬在泥滩上觅食。


例句：The dunlin's plumage provides excellent camouflage in its wetland habitat.  矶鹬的羽毛在其湿地栖息地提供了极好的伪装。


近义词：sandpiper (广义上的近义词，沙锥)
