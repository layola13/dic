slobbery: /ˈslɒbəri/ | adj. | 垂涎的；邋遢的；污秽的 | Covered or dripping with saliva; messy and untidy. | 例句：The dog had a slobbery mouth after drinking from the bowl. = 狗喝完碗里的水后，嘴巴上都是口水。

The baby made a slobbery mess of his food. = 婴儿把食物弄得又脏又乱。

近义词：dribbling, drooling, messy, untidy, sloppy | 反义词：clean, tidy, neat
