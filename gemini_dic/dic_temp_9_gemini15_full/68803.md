fascias:/ˈfæʃiəz/ | n. |筋膜；饰板；[建筑]墙板 | A fascia is a sheet of tissue that surrounds muscles and other organs. In architecture, it's a flat, projecting architectural feature.  | Example: The surgeon carefully dissected the fascia to reach the underlying muscle. 外科医生仔细地解剖筋膜以到达下面的肌肉。The building's fascia was made of modern composite materials. 这座建筑的墙板是由现代复合材料制成的。|近义词：membrane, sheath, facing, panel |反义词：None specifically applicable.
