maturation:/ˌmætʃəˈreɪʃən/| n. |成熟；发育；完工|The process of becoming mature; the state of being mature.  This can refer to the development of physical, mental, or emotional characteristics.  It also applies to processes, like the maturation of wine or cheese, where time and specific conditions lead to a desired state. |例句：The maturation of the fruit takes several weeks. 水果成熟需要几周时间。

例句：His emotional maturation was evident in his calm response to the crisis. 他在危机中冷静的反应表明了他的情感成熟。

近义词：development, ripening, growth, advancement

反义词：immaturity, juvenility, underdevelopment
