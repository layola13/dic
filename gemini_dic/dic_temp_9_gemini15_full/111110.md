literality:/ˌlɪtəˈrælɪti/| n. |字面意义；逐字解读；精确性 |The literal meaning of the word;  a strict and exact adherence to the words of a text or law, without consideration of the context, implications, or other meanings.|例句：The poem's literality obscures its deeper meaning. 这首诗的字面意思掩盖了其更深层的含义。  The lawyer insisted on the literality of the contract. 律师坚持合同的字面意思。|近义词：literalness, precision, exactness|反义词：figurativeness, interpretation, implication|
