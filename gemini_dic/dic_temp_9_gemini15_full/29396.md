catabolically:/ˌkætəˈbɑːlɪkli/ | adv. | 分解代谢地，异化作用地 | In a catabolic manner; relating to catabolism.  The process of breaking down complex molecules into simpler ones, often releasing energy. | 例句：Proteins are catabolically broken down into amino acids. 蛋白质被分解代谢成氨基酸。


The body catabolically processes stored fat during periods of fasting.  身体在禁食期间会分解代谢储存的脂肪。


近义词：destructively, degradatively | 反义词：anabolically


词源：来自"catabolism"，来自希腊语"kata-" (down) + "ballein" (to throw, cast).  字面意思为“向下抛掷”，指代分解代谢过程中复杂分子被分解成更简单的分子。


USAGE->

This word is an adverb and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, tense, voice, etc., are not applicable.  Its usage is always adverbial, modifying a verb.


PHRASE->

There are no common idiomatic phrases specifically using "catabolically".  Its usage is primarily within scientific or medical contexts.
