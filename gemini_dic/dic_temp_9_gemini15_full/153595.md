pentanucleotide:/ˌpɛntəˈnjuːklɪəʊtaɪd/| n. |五核苷酸|A pentanucleotide is a nucleotide polymer containing five nucleotides linked together by phosphodiester bonds.  It's a short sequence of DNA or RNA.  Pentanucleotides are important in various biological processes, such as gene regulation and protein synthesis.|Example: The researchers identified a specific pentanucleotide sequence crucial for the activation of a particular gene.  研究人员发现了一个特定五核苷酸序列，对特定基因的激活至关重要。|近义词: oligonucleotide (more general term)|反义词: None (no direct antonym)|名词
