resounding: /rɪˈzaʊndɪŋ/ | adj. | 响亮的；巨大的；压倒性的 |  producing a deep, resonant sound; very great or impressive.  | 例句：The applause was resounding.  掌声雷动。

例句：Her victory was a resounding success.  她的胜利是巨大的成功。


近义词：deafening, overwhelming, resounding, significant, striking, thunderous


反义词：faint, quiet, insignificant, muted


词源：from Old French *resoner "to resound," from Latin resonare "to resound," from re- "again" + sonare "to sound."
