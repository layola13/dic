pro-Buddhist: /ˌproʊˈbʊdɪst/ | adj. | 亲佛教的，支持佛教的 |  Supporting or favoring Buddhism.  This term describes individuals, organizations, or movements that advocate for or promote Buddhist principles, practices, or institutions.  | 例句：Many pro-Buddhist organizations work to preserve ancient temples. 许多亲佛教组织致力于保护古老的寺庙。

He wrote a pro-Buddhist essay arguing for the benefits of mindfulness meditation. 他撰写了一篇支持佛教的文章，论述了正念冥想的益处。


近义词：Buddhist-friendly, pro-Dharma (more explicitly religious),  supportive of Buddhism


反义词：anti-Buddhist, opposed to Buddhism


词性: 形容词
