nonauthoritative: /ˌnɒnɔːˈθɒrɪtətɪv/ | adj. |非权威的，无约束力的 | Not having the authority to command or enforce obedience; lacking authority.  |例句：The committee's decision was nonauthoritative, meaning it was merely a suggestion, not a binding rule.  委员会的决定是非权威性的，这意味着它只是一个建议，而不是具有约束力的规则。

例句：His statement was nonauthoritative and lacked any supporting evidence. 他的陈述缺乏权威性，没有任何证据支持。


近义词：unofficial, informal, advisory, suggestive, persuasive


反义词：authoritative, binding, mandatory, compulsory, official
