riboflavin:/ˌraɪbəˈfleɪvɪn/| n. |核黄素，维生素B2 |Riboflavin is a vitamin B that's essential for energy production and cell function. It's also known as vitamin B2.  |Example:  A deficiency in riboflavin can lead to various health problems. 核黄素缺乏会导致各种健康问题。
