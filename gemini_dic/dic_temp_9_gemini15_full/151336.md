parity:/ˈpærɪti/| n.| 平等；同等；相等；同值；奇偶校验 | Equality, equivalence, or similarity; especially of status, amount, or value.  The state or condition of being equal, especially in status, amount, or value. In computing, a method of detecting errors in data transmission. |

例句：

Gender parity in the workplace is still a long way off.  职场中的性别平等还有很长的路要走。

The two countries are seeking parity in trade agreements. 这两个国家正在寻求贸易协议中的平等地位。


近义词：equality, equivalence, equivalence, similarity, balance


反义词：inequality, disparity, difference


词源:  From Old French *paritet, from Latin paritas, from par "equal".
