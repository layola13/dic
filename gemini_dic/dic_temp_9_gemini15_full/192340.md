scrimmaged:/ˈskrɪmɪdʒd/| v.|进行对抗练习；发生冲突 | To engage in a scrimmage; to struggle or fight in a disorganized way. |例句：The football team scrimmaged intensely before the game.  橄榄球队在比赛前进行了激烈的对抗练习。

The two groups scrimmaged in the street. 两伙人在街上发生了冲突。|近义词：scramble, wrestle, tussle |反义词：cooperate, harmonize
