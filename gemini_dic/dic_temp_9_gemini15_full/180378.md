refusenik:/ˈrɛfjuːzənɪk/| n. |拒绝者，（尤指）拒绝服兵役或与苏联当局合作的人|A person who refuses to comply, especially one who refuses to cooperate with an authority (especially a Soviet Jew who refused to emigrate).  |例句：Many refusenik families were finally allowed to leave the Soviet Union. 许多拒绝者家庭最终获准离开苏联。
例句：He was branded a refusenik for his refusal to participate in the government’s program. 他因拒绝参与政府项目而被贴上拒绝者的标签。|近义词：dissenter, objector, resister |反义词：collaborator, conformist
