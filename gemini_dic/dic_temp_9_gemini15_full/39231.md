congou:/ˈkɒŋɡuː/| n. |工夫茶，一种中国红茶|A type of strong black tea from China, typically a lower grade but still of good quality.  It's known for its malty flavor and often used in blends.|This congou tea has a rich, malty flavor. 这款工夫茶具有浓郁的麦芽香味。|近义词: black tea, strong tea |反义词:  (no direct antonym, perhaps "weak tea")
