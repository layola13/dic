road-testing:/ˈroʊdˌtɛstɪŋ/ | n. | 道路测试，试运行 | The process of testing a vehicle, machine, or system under real-world conditions.  It often involves driving a vehicle on public roads to evaluate its performance, reliability, and safety. |  例句：The new car is undergoing rigorous road-testing before its official release. 这辆新车在正式发布前正在进行严格的道路测试。

例句：The software is currently undergoing road-testing with a select group of users.  这个软件目前正在由一组选定的用户进行试运行。


近义词：field testing, trial run, real-world testing | 反义词：lab testing, simulation testing | 词性：名词
