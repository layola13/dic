discomfit:/dɪsˈkʌmfɪt/| vt.|使不安；使窘迫；挫败|To make (someone) feel uneasy or embarrassed; to defeat or frustrate (someone or something).|例句：His unexpected question discomfited her. 他的意外提问让她感到不安。

例句：The general's strategy was discomfited by the enemy's unexpected attack. 将军的策略被敌人的突然袭击挫败了。

近义词：embarrass, unsettle, frustrate, defeat|反义词：comfort, reassure, encourage, succeed|动词
