figure-in: /ˈfɪɡərɪn/ | v. | 扮演角色；列入；计算在内；考虑在内 | To include someone or something in a calculation, plan, or account; to be or become a significant part of something. | 例句：They figured in the cost of travel when they planned the trip.  他们计划旅行时把旅行费用计算在内了。 |近义词：include, incorporate, factor in, account for | 反义词：exclude, omit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We figure in the extra costs for the project. = 我们把项目的额外费用计算在内。
They figure in the profit margin when pricing the goods. = 他们给商品定价时考虑了利润率。


一般过去时:
She figured in the total expenses for the year. = 她把去年的总支出计算在内。
They figured in the potential risks before making the investment. = 他们在投资之前考虑了潜在的风险。


现在完成时:
We have figured in all the unexpected expenses. = 我们已经把所有意外的开支都计算进去了。
They have figured in the impact of the new policy on their business. = 他们已经考虑了新政策对其业务的影响。


过去完成时:
They had figured in the possible delays before setting the deadline. = 他们在设定最后期限之前已经考虑到了可能的延误。
She had figured in the travel time when scheduling the meeting. = 她在安排会议时间时已经考虑到了出行时间。


将来时:
We will figure in the inflation rate when making our budget. = 我们在做预算时将考虑通货膨胀率。
They will figure in the feedback from customers when designing the new product. = 他们在设计新产品时将考虑客户的反馈。


虚拟语气:
If we figured in all the variables, the result would be different. = 如果我们把所有变量都考虑进去，结果就会不同。
If they had figured in the market fluctuations, they wouldn't have lost so much money. = 如果他们当时考虑了市场波动，就不会损失那么多钱。


被动语态:
The additional costs were figured in the final budget. = 额外费用已计入最终预算。
The potential risks were figured in before the decision was made. = 在做出决定之前已经考虑到了潜在的风险。


疑问句:
Did they figure in the transportation costs? = 他们把运输成本计算在内了吗？
Have you figured in the time needed for travel? = 你考虑过出行所需的时间了吗？


否定句:
We didn't figure in the possibility of rain. = 我们没有考虑下雨的可能性。
They didn't figure in the impact of the economic downturn. = 他们没有考虑到经济低迷的影响。
