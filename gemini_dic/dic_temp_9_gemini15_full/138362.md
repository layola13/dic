notifies:/ˈnoʊtɪfaɪz/ | vt. | 通知，告知 | To inform someone of something officially or formally.  | The bank notified me of the fraudulent transaction. 银行通知我发生了欺诈性交易。 | inform, advise, alert | ignore, disregard | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school notifies parents of any important events. 学校会通知家长任何重要事件。
He notifies his boss of his arrival every morning. 他每天早上都会通知老板他已到达。


一般过去时:
She notified the police about the accident. 她向警方报告了事故。
They notified us of the change in schedule. 他们通知我们日程的变更。


现在完成时:
The company has notified all employees of the upcoming merger. 公司已通知所有员工即将到来的合并。
Have you notified your family of your travel plans? 你已经通知你的家人你的旅行计划了吗？


过去完成时:
He had notified the authorities before leaving the country. 他在离开国家之前已经通知了当局。
They had notified the customer of the delay before the shipment. 他们发货前已经通知了客户延误情况。


将来时:
The manager will notify us of the decision tomorrow.  经理明天会通知我们决定。
I will notify you as soon as I have the information. 我一有信息就会通知你。


虚拟语气:
If you notified them earlier, they might have been able to help. 如果你早点通知他们，他们也许能够帮忙。
I wish I had notified him sooner. 我真希望我早点通知他。


被动语态:
The employees were notified of the layoffs. 员工们被告知了裁员的消息。
The winners will be notified by email. 获奖者将通过电子邮件被告知。


疑问句:
Has he been notified of the meeting? 他被告知会议了吗？
Did she notify you about the problem? 她有通知你关于这个问题吗？


否定句:
They did not notify me of the cancellation. 他们没有通知我取消的消息。
I haven't been notified yet. 我还没有收到通知。


PHRASE->
notify someone of something: 通知某人某事
notify someone by email/phone/letter: 通过邮件/电话/信件通知某人
notify someone in writing: 书面通知某人
notify someone immediately: 立即通知某人
be notified of something: 被告知某事
