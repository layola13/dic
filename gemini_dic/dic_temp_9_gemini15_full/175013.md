radio-resistant: /ˈreɪdiəʊrɪzɪstənt/ | adj. | 抗辐射的 |  Able to withstand or survive exposure to high levels of ionizing radiation.  This term applies to organisms, materials, or systems that can tolerate significant doses of radiation without suffering irreversible damage.  | Example:  Some bacteria are remarkably radio-resistant.  某些细菌具有显著的抗辐射能力。  | 近义词: radiation-tolerant, radiotolerant | 反义词: radiosensitive
