/rɪˈsɜːrtʃ/ | vt. n. | 研究；调查 | To investigate systematically; to search for knowledge or information. |例句：Scientists are researching the causes of cancer. 科学家们正在研究癌症的成因。| 近义词：investigate, study, explore | 反义词：neglect, ignore | 名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They research the impact of climate change. = 他们在研究气候变化的影响。
We research new materials for construction. = 我们正在研究用于建筑的新材料。


一般过去时:
She researched the history of the Roman Empire. = 她研究了罗马帝国的历史。
They researched the effectiveness of the new drug. = 他们研究了新药的疗效。


现在完成时:
Scientists have researched this topic extensively. = 科学家们已经广泛研究了这个课题。
We have researched various solutions to the problem. = 我们已经研究了各种解决问题的方案。


过去完成时:
They had researched the subject thoroughly before writing the report. = 他们在写报告之前已经彻底研究了这个课题。
She had researched the market before launching her product. = 她在推出产品之前已经研究了市场。


将来时:
We will research the effects of pollution on marine life. = 我们将研究污染对海洋生物的影响。
They will research the feasibility of the project. = 他们将研究该项目的可行性。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would research this topic further. = 如果我有更多时间，我会进一步研究这个课题。
I wish I had researched the market more thoroughly. = 我希望我当时对市场进行了更彻底的研究。


被动语态:
The effects of the new drug are being researched. = 正在研究这种新药的效果。
The topic was researched extensively by several scientists. = 这个课题已被几位科学家广泛研究。


疑问句:
What are you researching at the moment? = 你现在正在研究什么？
What was researched in the study? = 研究中研究了什么？


否定句:
They did not research the topic adequately. = 他们没有充分研究这个课题。
He has not yet researched the implications of the new law. = 他还没有研究这项新法律的含义。
