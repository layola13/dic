conjoining:/kənˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ/ | v. |连接，联合|The act of joining or uniting two or more things.  It often implies a close and often permanent connection.  The word is frequently used in grammatical contexts to describe the joining of clauses or sentences.|例句：The two houses were conjoined by a covered walkway.  两个房子通过一条有顶棚的走道连接在一起。

The conjoining of the two companies created a powerful new entity.  两家公司的合并创造了一个强大的新实体。|近义词：joining, uniting, linking, connecting|反义词：separating, disconnecting, disjoining
