cross-question:/krɒsˈkwɛstʃən/| vt.|盘问；反复提问|To question someone repeatedly and rigorously, often in a challenging or suspicious manner.  It implies a thorough and sometimes adversarial line of questioning.|The detective cross-questioned the suspect for hours.  侦探盘问了嫌疑犯数小时。|interrogate, grill, quiz|support, assist|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The lawyer cross-questions the witness. = 律师盘问证人。
They cross-question each other during the debate. = 他们在辩论中互相盘问。


一般过去时:
The police cross-questioned the suspect about his alibi. = 警方就其不在场证明盘问了嫌疑人。
The teacher cross-questioned the student on the material covered in class. = 老师就课堂上讲授的内容盘问了学生。


现在完成时:
The investigator has cross-questioned all the witnesses. = 调查员已经盘问了所有证人。
The journalist has cross-questioned the politician on several occasions. = 记者多次盘问过这位政治家。


过去完成时:
The judge had cross-questioned the expert before the recess. = 休庭前法官已经盘问了专家。
The lawyer had cross-questioned the defendant extensively before presenting his closing argument. = 律师在陈述结案陈词之前已经广泛盘问了被告。


将来时:
The prosecutor will cross-question the defendant tomorrow. = 公诉人明天将盘问被告。
The interviewer will cross-question the candidate about their experience. = 面试官将盘问候选人的经验。


虚拟语气:
If the lawyer had cross-questioned the witness more effectively, the case might have been won. = 如果律师更有效地盘问证人，案件可能就赢了。
I wish the journalist had cross-questioned the politician more thoroughly. = 我希望记者能更彻底地盘问这位政治家。


被动语态:
The witness was cross-questioned relentlessly by the opposing counsel. = 证人被对方律师无情地盘问。
The suspect was cross-questioned for hours but refused to confess. = 嫌疑人被盘问了数小时，但拒绝认罪。


疑问句:
Will the lawyer cross-question the key witness? = 律师会盘问关键证人吗？
Did the police cross-question all the potential suspects? = 警方盘问了所有可能的嫌疑人吗？


否定句:
The lawyer didn't cross-question the witness effectively. = 律师没有有效地盘问证人。
The investigator didn't cross-question the suspect about his whereabouts that night. = 调查员没有盘问嫌疑人那天晚上他在哪里。


PHRASE->
