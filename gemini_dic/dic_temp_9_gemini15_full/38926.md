confessors:/kənˈfɛsər/| n. |忏悔者；告解神父|A confessor is someone who confesses their sins, or a priest who hears confessions.|例句：The confessor listened patiently to the penitent's confession.  忏悔者耐心地听着忏悔者的忏悔。|例句：Many people seek guidance from their confessors. 许多人向他们的告解神父寻求指导。|近义词：penitent,  repentant|反义词：无

一般现在时:
He confesses his sins every week. 他每周都忏悔他的罪过。
The priest confesses the sins of his parishioners.  牧师为他的教区居民忏悔。


一般过去时:
She confessed to stealing the money. 她承认偷了钱。
He confessed his love for her. 他承认爱她。


现在完成时:
He has confessed his guilt to the police. 他已经向警方承认了他的罪行。
She has confessed her mistakes to her parents. 她已经向她的父母承认了她的错误。


过去完成时:
He had confessed his crimes before the trial began.  审判开始前，他已经承认了他的罪行。
She had confessed her feelings before he left. 他离开之前，她已经表白了她的感情。


将来时:
He will confess his sins to the priest. 他将向神父忏悔他的罪过。

虚拟语气:
If he confessed his sins, he would feel better. 如果他忏悔他的罪过，他会感觉好些。
I wish I had confessed my feelings sooner. 我希望我早点表白我的感情。


被动语态:
His guilt was confessed to the police. 他的罪行被向警方承认了。

疑问句:

否定句:
