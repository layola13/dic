evolute:/ˈɛvəluːt/ | v. | 演变，进化；展开 | To unroll or unfold gradually; to develop or evolve gradually.  |The butterfly evolved from a caterpillar. 蝴蝶是由毛毛虫演变而来的。The situation evolved slowly over several years.  情况在几年内逐渐演变。| develop, unfold, progress | regress, decline | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company evolves its strategies annually. = 公司每年都会调整策略。
The situation evolves constantly. = 情况一直在变化。


一般过去时:
The design evolved over many iterations. = 这个设计经过多次迭代才最终成型。
The species evolved over millions of years. = 这个物种进化了数百万年。


现在完成时:
The technology has evolved significantly. = 技术已经有了显著的进步。
Our understanding of the universe has evolved. = 我们对宇宙的理解已经发展了。


过去完成时:
The theory had evolved before the new evidence was discovered. = 在新的证据被发现之前，这个理论已经发展完善了。
The plan had evolved significantly by the time the meeting started. = 到会议开始时，计划已经有了很大的变化。


将来时:
The project will evolve over the next few months. = 项目将在未来几个月内逐步完善。
The software will evolve to meet new demands. = 软件将会发展以满足新的需求。


虚拟语气:
If the situation evolved differently, the outcome would have been different. = 如果情况发展不同，结果也会不同。
I wish the technology had evolved faster. = 我希望技术发展得更快一些。


被动语态:
The plan was evolved by the team over several weeks. = 该计划由团队在几周内逐步完善。
The system is being evolved to improve its performance. = 系统正在不断改进以提高其性能。


疑问句:
How has the company evolved over the past decade? = 公司在过去十年里是如何发展的？
How will the situation evolve in the future? = 这种情况将来会如何发展？


否定句:
The product hasn't evolved much since its launch. = 自从发布以来，这款产品变化不大。
The plan didn't evolve as expected. = 计划的进展并不像预期的那样。
