folded:/ˈfoʊldɪd/ | adj. | 折叠的，对折的 |  Describes something that has been bent or doubled over upon itself.  It can refer to the state of being folded or the action of folding. |例句：The laundry was neatly folded in the basket. 洗好的衣服整齐地叠放在篮子里。
The map was folded into a small square. 地图被折叠成一个小方块。|近义词：creased, pleated, crumpled |反义词：unfolded, spread, open
