lucerne: /ˈluːsɜːn/ /ˈluːsɝːn/ | n. |紫花苜蓿；鲁塞恩（瑞士城市）| Alfalfa; a city in Switzerland.  Lucerne is a type of forage crop, highly nutritious and widely cultivated for animal feed.  The city of Lucerne is located in central Switzerland and is known for its picturesque scenery and historical landmarks. |The farmer planted a field of lucerne for his cattle.  农民种了一片紫花苜蓿给他的牛吃。|  alfalfa, clover |  (no direct antonym)| noun
