make use of: /meɪk juːz ɒv/ | v. | 利用，使用 | To utilize or employ something for a particular purpose.  |  We should make use of this opportunity to learn more. 我们应该利用这个机会学习更多。 They made use of every available resource. 他们利用了所有可用的资源。 | utilize, employ, exploit |  waste, neglect | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We make use of renewable energy sources. = 我们利用可再生能源。
They make use of advanced technology in their production. = 他们在生产中使用先进技术。


一般过去时:
She made use of her connections to get the job. = 她利用自己的人脉关系得到了这份工作。
The company made use of a new marketing strategy. = 公司采用了新的营销策略。


现在完成时:
We have made use of this software for years. = 我们已经使用这个软件多年了。
They have made use of every opportunity to expand their business. = 他们已经利用每一个机会来扩展他们的业务。


过去完成时:
They had made use of all their savings before they went bankrupt. = 破产之前，他们已经用光了所有的积蓄。
She had made use of her skills to create a successful business. = 她已经利用自己的技能创建了一家成功的企业。


将来时:
We will make use of this new equipment next week. = 我们下周将使用这台新设备。
They will make use of their experience to solve the problem. = 他们将利用他们的经验来解决这个问题。


虚拟语气:
If we made use of better resources, we could achieve more. = 如果我们利用更好的资源，我们可以取得更多成就。
I wish we had made use of that opportunity. = 我真希望我们当时利用了那个机会。


被动语态:
The new technology is being made use of in many industries. = 许多行业都在使用这项新技术。
These resources should be made use of effectively. = 这些资源应该有效地利用。


疑问句:
Are you making use of all the available tools? = 你正在利用所有可用的工具吗？
How are they making use of the data they collected? = 他们如何利用收集到的数据？


否定句:
We didn't make use of the opportunity. = 我们没有利用这个机会。
They didn't make use of their potential. = 他们没有发挥自己的潜能。
