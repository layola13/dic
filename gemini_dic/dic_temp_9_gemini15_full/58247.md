drumble:/ˈdrʌmbəl/| v.|咕噜咕噜地响，低沉地说话|To make a low, rumbling sound; to speak in a low, grumbling voice.  |例句：The distant thunder drumbled ominously. 远处雷声不祥地隆隆作响。  The old man drumbled his complaints. 老头子咕噜咕噜地抱怨着。|近义词：grumble, rumble, mumble|反义词：shout, scream


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The distant thunder drumbles ominously. = 远处雷声不祥地隆隆作响。
He often drumbles to himself. = 他经常自言自语地咕哝。


一般过去时:
The engine drumbled to a stop. = 发动机隆隆地停了下来。
She drumbled her dissatisfaction to her friend. = 她向朋友咕哝着表达了不满。


现在完成时:
The storm has drumbled on for hours. = 暴风雨已经持续了好几个小时。
He has drumbled about the unfairness of it all day. = 他整天都在咕哝着这件事的不公平。


过去完成时:
The train had drumbled through the countryside before I fell asleep. = 我睡着之前，火车已经隆隆地穿过乡村了。
She had drumbled about the lost keys for an hour before finding them. = 在找到钥匙之前，她已经咕哝着抱怨丢失的钥匙一个小时了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish he wouldn't drumble so much. = 我希望他不那么唠叨。


被动语态:
(Note:  "Drumble" is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes a sound or action made by a subject.)


疑问句:

否定句:

She didn't drumble, she made a clear complaint. = 她没有咕哝，而是提出了明确的抱怨。
