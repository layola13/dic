gangs:/ɡæŋz/| n.|帮派，团伙|A gang is a group of people, often young people, who associate together, usually for criminal purposes.  The word often carries negative connotations.|例句：The police are cracking down on street gangs.  警方正在严厉打击街头帮派。|例句：A gang of thieves robbed the bank. 一伙窃贼抢劫了银行。|近义词：crew, mob, clique|反义词：individuals, lone wolves
