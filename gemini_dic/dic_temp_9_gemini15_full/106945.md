lacily:/ˈleɪsɪli/| adj.| 花边地；精致地；纤细地 | delicately or intricately, like lace.  The word evokes a sense of elegance, fragility, and intricate detail. |例句：The bride's dress was lacily embroidered.新娘的婚纱绣着精美的花边。

The curtains hung lacily in the breeze. 窗帘在微风中轻盈地飘动。

近义词：delicately, intricately, elegantly, delicately, finely

反义词：roughly, coarsely, crudely

词性：形容词
