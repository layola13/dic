permute:/pɜːˈmjuːt/| vt. |排列；改变…的顺序|To permute means to change the order of things within a set.  It involves rearranging the elements of a collection in various ways.  This is often done in mathematics, computer science, and other fields where order matters.|例句：The letters in the word "APPLE" can be permuted to form many different words.  苹果这个单词的字母可以排列组合成许多不同的单词。

The software permutes the data to test for different sorting algorithms. 该软件会对数据进行排列组合以测试不同的排序算法。|近义词：rearrange, shuffle, reorder |反义词：fix, stabilize
