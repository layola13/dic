endue:/ɛnˈdjuː/|/ɪnˈdjuː/| vt.|赋予；使具有；使穿着|To provide with a quality, ability, or characteristic.|The painting is endowed with a strange power.  这幅画具有奇特的魔力。|The king endowed the university with a large sum of money. 国王给大学捐赠了一大笔钱。|bestow, grant, invest|deprive, divest|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He endows the charity annually. = 他每年都捐款给慈善机构。
The artist endows her work with emotional depth. =这位艺术家赋予她的作品以深厚的情感。


一般过去时:
She endowed her daughter with a strong sense of independence. = 她赋予女儿强烈的独立意识。
The ancient Romans endowed their gods with human characteristics. = 古罗马人赋予他们的神以人类的特征。


现在完成时:
The foundation has endowed numerous scholarships. = 该基金会已资助了许多奖学金。
This experience has endowed me with greater resilience. = 这段经历使我更有韧性。


过去完成时:
They had endowed the museum with a priceless collection of artifacts. = 他们已将一件无价的文物收藏捐赠给了博物馆。
The family had endowed the hospital wing in memory of their son. = 这个家族为了纪念他们的儿子而捐赠了医院的一个病房。


将来时:
She will endow her trust with additional funds. = 她将为信托基金追加资金。
The government will endow the research project with significant resources. = 政府将为该研究项目提供大量资源。


虚拟语气:
If he endowed the project, it would succeed. = 如果他资助这个项目，它就会成功。
I wish I could endow my children with all the wisdom of the world. = 我希望我能把我所有的智慧都传授给我的孩子们。


被动语态:
The university was endowed with a generous donation. = 这所大学获得了一笔慷慨的捐款。
The scholarship was endowed by an anonymous benefactor. = 这笔奖学金是由一位匿名捐助者捐赠的。


疑问句:
Does the company endow its employees with stock options? = 公司是否给予员工股票期权？
Will the foundation endow any new programs this year? = 该基金会今年会资助任何新的项目吗？


否定句:
He did not endow his son with any business acumen. = 他没有给他儿子任何商业头脑。
The artist did not endow her painting with sufficient detail. = 这名艺术家没有赋予她的画作足够的细节。
