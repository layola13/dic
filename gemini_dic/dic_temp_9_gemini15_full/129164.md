no-smoking: /ˈnoʊˌsmoʊkɪŋ/ | adj. | 禁止吸烟的 |  The term "no-smoking" is a compound adjective indicating a place or area where smoking is prohibited. It combines "no" (meaning "not") and "smoking" (the act of smoking).  | Example: This is a no-smoking area.  这是一个禁烟区。  This restaurant has a no-smoking section. 这家餐厅设有禁烟区。|近义词：smoke-free, non-smoking|反义词：smoking


USAGE->
This word functions primarily as an adjective and does not conjugate through tenses.  Therefore, traditional tense conjugations are not applicable.  The usage depends on the context of the sentence.


PHRASE->
no-smoking area: 禁烟区
no-smoking policy: 禁烟政策
no-smoking section: 禁烟区
no-smoking sign: 禁烟标志
designated no-smoking zone: 指定禁烟区
