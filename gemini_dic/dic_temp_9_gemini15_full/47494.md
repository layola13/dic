deanships:/ˈdiːnʃɪps/ | n. |院长职位；院长职务 |The position or office of a dean.  It refers to the role and responsibilities of a dean, often in a college or university.  |例句：The deanships are highly competitive positions. 院长职位竞争非常激烈。


例句：He applied for several deanships across the country. 他申请了全国几个大学的院长职位。


近义词：deanship (单数形式)


反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑与非管理职位相对比，例如：professorship (教授职位)


词性：名词
