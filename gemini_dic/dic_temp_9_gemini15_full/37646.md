commuters:/kəˈmjuːtərz/ | n. |通勤者，上下班往返者 |People who travel regularly between their home and their place of work.  |Example: The commuters were delayed by the train strike.  通勤者因火车罢工而延误。|近义词：travelers, riders |反义词：residents (in the context of those who don't commute)
