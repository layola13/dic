quizzed:/kwɪzd/| v.|盘问，测验|To ask someone a lot of questions, especially in a rapid or challenging way; to test someone's knowledge with a quiz.|例句：The police quizzed him for hours about the robbery.警察盘问了他几个小时关于抢劫案的事。The teacher quizzed the students on their spelling.老师用测验考查学生们的拼写。|近义词：questioned, interrogated, tested|反义词：instructed, informed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He quizzes his students every Friday. = 他每个星期五都测验他的学生。
She quizzes me on vocabulary before every test. = 每次考试前她都考我词汇。


一般过去时:
The detective quizzed the suspect intensely. = 侦探严厉地盘问了嫌疑人。
They quizzed me about my trip to Europe. = 他们盘问我关于我去欧洲旅行的事。


现在完成时:
The professor has quizzed the students on three chapters already. = 教授已经考了学生们三个章节的内容了。
I have quizzed myself on the material for the exam. = 我已经自测了考试的材料。


过去完成时:
The police had quizzed all the witnesses before arresting the suspect. = 警察在逮捕嫌疑人之前已经盘问了所有证人。
She had quizzed him thoroughly before he left for the interview. = 在他去面试之前，她已经彻底盘问了他。


将来时:
The teacher will quiz the students on the next chapter tomorrow. = 明天老师将考查学生们下一章节的内容。
They will quiz her extensively before making a decision. = 在做决定之前，他们将会广泛地盘问她。


虚拟语气:
If I had been quizzed earlier, I would have answered better. = 如果我早点被测验，我会答得更好。
I wish I had been quizzed more often during the semester. = 我希望这学期能更经常地进行测验。


被动语态:
The students were quizzed on their knowledge of history. = 学生们被考查了他们的历史知识。
He was quizzed relentlessly by the investigators. = 他被调查人员无情地盘问。


疑问句:
Was he quizzed by the police? = 他被警察盘问了吗？
Have the students been quizzed on the material yet? = 学生们已经被考查过材料了吗？


否定句:
The teacher didn't quiz the students today. = 老师今天没有考查学生们。
They haven't quizzed the witness yet. = 他们还没有盘问证人。
