comradeship:/ˈkɒmrædəʃɪp/| n. |同志情谊；友情；友谊 |The feeling of friendship and loyalty that exists between comrades.  It implies a shared experience, often in the context of a common goal or struggle.  |例句：The long march fostered a strong comradeship among the soldiers. 长征培养了士兵之间深厚的同志情谊。

例句：Their comradeship helped them overcome the many challenges they faced. 他们的友谊帮助他们克服了许多挑战。


近义词：camaraderie, fellowship, solidarity, friendship

反义词：antagonism, hostility, alienation
