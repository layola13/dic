god-fathering: /ˈɡɑːdˌfɑːðəriŋ/ | n. | 担任教父；资助；指导 | The act of acting as a godfather or mentor, providing guidance and support.  It can also refer to financial sponsorship. | 例句：The company provided god-fathering to the young startup. 这家公司为这家年轻的初创公司提供了资助。


The local businessman provided god-fathering for the community project. 当地商人资助了这个社区项目。


近义词：mentoring, sponsoring, supporting, guiding | 反义词：abandoning, neglecting, hindering | 词性: 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is god-fathering the new initiative. 他正在指导这项新计划。
The organization god-fathers many young entrepreneurs. 这个组织资助许多年轻的企业家。


一般过去时:
He god-fathered the project from its inception. 他从一开始就资助了这个项目。
They god-fathered several promising startups. 他们资助了几家很有前景的初创公司。


现在完成时:
She has god-fathered numerous charitable causes. 她已经资助了许多慈善事业。
The foundation has god-fathered many talented artists. 这个基金会已经资助了许多有才华的艺术家。


过去完成时:
The investor had god-fathered the company for years before its IPO.  这位投资者在该公司上市前已经资助该公司多年了。
They had god-fathered the research project since its early stages.  他们从早期阶段就开始资助这个研究项目。


将来时:
He will god-father the next generation of leaders. 他将培养下一代领导人。
The program will god-father many aspiring students. 这个项目将资助许多有抱负的学生。


虚拟语气:
If only he had god-fathered my business plan!  如果他当时能资助我的商业计划就好了！
I wish someone would god-father my research project. 我希望有人能资助我的研究项目。


被动语态:
The project was god-fathered by a local philanthropist. 这个项目是由一位当地慈善家资助的。
Many young businesses are god-fathered by larger corporations. 许多小型企业都得到了大型公司的资助。


疑问句:
Who is god-fathering this ambitious project? 谁在资助这个雄心勃勃的项目？
Will anyone god-father my new venture? 会有人资助我的新企业吗？


否定句:
He did not god-father the project. 他没有资助这个项目。
The company refused to god-father the risky undertaking. 公司拒绝资助这项风险很大的事业。

词源:  The word is a combination of "godfather" (a male sponsor in a baptism) and "-ing" (forming a gerund or noun).  The extended meaning implies a similar role of guidance and support, but not necessarily religious.
