congruity:/kənˈɡruːəti/ | n. |一致性；和谐；适合性 |The state or quality of agreeing or corresponding; compatibility.  It refers to the agreement or harmony between things, or the fact that two or more things are compatible.  This can apply to ideas, actions, or objects. |例句：There was a remarkable congruity between his words and his actions. 他的言行非常一致。

There is a striking congruity between the two paintings in terms of style and technique.  这两幅画在风格和技巧上惊人地一致。


近义词：accordance, agreement, consistency, harmony, coherence |反义词：incongruity, discordance, discrepancy, dissonance
