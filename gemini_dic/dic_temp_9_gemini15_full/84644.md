hand-to-mouth: /ˌhænd tu ˈmaʊθ/ | adj. |勉强糊口的；仅够维持生活的|

英文解析:  Describes a situation where someone has barely enough money to survive, earning only enough to cover basic needs like food and shelter, with little or nothing left over.  It implies a precarious and unstable financial state.

例句:

They lived a hand-to-mouth existence, never knowing where their next meal would come from.  他们过着勉强糊口的生存，不知道下一顿饭从哪里来。

Many families in the developing world are forced to live hand-to-mouth.  发展中国家的许多家庭被迫过着勉强糊口的生存。


近义词: impoverished, destitute, needy, penniless, impecunious, struggling

反义词: affluent, wealthy, prosperous, well-off, rich


词性: adjective
