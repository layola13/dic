inverts:/ɪnˈvɜːrts/ | vt. n. |颠倒；反转；使倒置；转化；倒置者；无脊椎动物 |To turn upside down or inside out; to reverse the position, order, or condition of something.  Also refers to animals that lack a backbone, especially insects, arachnids, and crustaceans. |例句：The magician inverted the cup, revealing a hidden rabbit. 魔术师把杯子倒过来，露出了一只藏着的兔子。

The company inverted its organizational structure. 公司颠倒了其组织结构。


例句：Spiders are invertebrates. 蜘蛛是无脊椎动物。


近义词：reverse, overturn, turn upside down,  upend (vt.); invertebrates (n.)

反义词：upright, erect (adj.); vertebrates (n.)

词性：vt. n.
