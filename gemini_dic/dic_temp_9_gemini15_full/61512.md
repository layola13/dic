/ɪmˈbrɔɪdə(r)/ | vt. | 刺绣；绣花；润色；装饰 | To decorate (cloth or other material) with patterns sewn with thread.  It can also mean to embellish or elaborate (a story, for example).|  She embroidered a beautiful flower on the dress. 她在一件裙子上绣了一朵漂亮的花。 |He embroidered the story with fanciful details. 他用奇特的细节润色了这个故事。| decorate, embellish, adorn | simplify, strip down | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
She embroiders beautiful patterns. = 她绣制美丽的图案。
He embroiders his stories with false claims. = 他用虚假说法来润色他的故事。

一般过去时:
My grandmother embroidered my christening gown. = 我祖母绣制了我的洗礼袍。
