nonnebular:/ˌnɒnˈnɛbjʊlə(r)/| adj.|非星云的，非星云状的|Not relating to or resembling a nebula.  The term often describes celestial objects or formations that lack the diffuse, cloud-like appearance characteristic of nebulae. |Example: The newly discovered celestial body exhibited a nonnebular structure, unlike the surrounding gaseous clouds.  新发现的天体呈现出非星云状结构，与周围的气体云不同。|近义词：non-nebulous, diffuse (as an antonym in the sense of lacking the characteristics of a nebula)|反义词：nebular
