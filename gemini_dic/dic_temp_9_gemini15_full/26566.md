cables:/ˈkeɪbəlz/| n.|电缆，缆索|Thick wires or strands of metal or other material bound together to form a strong, flexible cord or rope, used for transmitting electricity, data, or supporting heavy objects.  |例句：The power cables were damaged in the storm.  暴风雨损坏了电缆。|例句：The bridge was supported by thick steel cables. 这座桥由粗钢缆支撑。|近义词：wires, cords, ropes|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with wireless systems.
