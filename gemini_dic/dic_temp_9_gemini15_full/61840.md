employ:/ɪmˈplɔɪ/| vt. n.|雇佣；使用；采用|To make use of something, someone's skills, or their time.  To give someone a job. |例句：The company employs over 500 people. 这家公司雇佣了500多名员工。
例句：We need to employ new strategies to improve sales. 我们需要采用新的策略来提高销售额。|近义词：hire, use, utilize, engage|反义词：fire, dismiss, discharge|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company employs many engineers. = 这家公司雇佣了许多工程师。
She employs her free time wisely. = 她明智地利用她的空闲时间。

一般过去时:
They employed a new marketing strategy last year. = 他们去年采用了一种新的营销策略。
He employed several people for the project. = 他为这个项目雇佣了几个人。

现在完成时:
We have employed several new techniques this year. = 我们今年已经采用了多种新技术。
The company has employed over 1000 people since its founding. = 自成立以来，该公司已雇佣了1000多名员工。

过去完成时:
They had employed a similar strategy before, but it failed. = 他们以前曾采用过类似的策略，但失败了。
She had employed many interns during her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她曾雇佣过许多实习生。

将来时:
We will employ more workers next month. = 我们下个月将雇佣更多工人。
The company will employ advanced technology in the future.  = 公司将来会采用先进技术。


虚拟语气:
If they employed more staff, they could handle the workload. = 如果他们雇佣更多员工，他们就能处理工作量了。
I wish they employed a more efficient system. = 我希望他们采用一个更有效的系统。


被动语态:
Many new techniques were employed in the project. = 该项目采用了多种新技术。
More staff will be employed next year. = 明年将雇佣更多员工。


疑问句:
Does this company employ many people? = 这家公司雇佣很多人吗？
How many people do you employ? = 你雇佣了多少人？


否定句:
The company does not employ anyone under the age of 18. = 这家公司不雇佣任何18岁以下的人。
They did not employ the new technology. = 他们没有采用新技术。
