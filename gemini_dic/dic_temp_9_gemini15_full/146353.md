overfish:/ˈoʊvərˌfɪʃ/ | vt. |过度捕捞|To catch too many fish from an area of water, resulting in a decline in fish populations.| The coastal waters have been overfished for decades, leading to a collapse of the local fishing industry.  沿海海域几十年来过度捕捞，导致当地渔业崩溃。| deplete, exhaust | conserve, protect |
