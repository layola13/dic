quasi-eligibly:/ˈkwɑːziˈɛlɪdʒəbli/ | adj. | 准合格地，近乎合格地 |  Describes something that almost but not quite meets the criteria for eligibility.  It suggests a state of being close to, but not fully achieving, the required standards. |  The applicant was considered quasi-eligibly for the grant, lacking only one minor requirement.  申请人被认为几乎符合拨款资格，只缺少一项次要要求。|  nearly eligible, almost eligible | ineligible, disqualified | 形容词
