dementation:/ˌdɛməˈneɪʃən/| n. |痴呆；精神错乱|The progressive deterioration of cognitive functions, including memory, thinking, and judgment, often associated with aging but also caused by various diseases.  It's characterized by significant impairment in multiple cognitive domains, impacting daily life. |例句：Her grandmother's dementation progressed rapidly, requiring full-time care.  她祖母的痴呆症进展迅速，需要全天候护理。

例句：The doctor suspected dementation after observing the patient's difficulty with simple tasks and memory loss.  医生在观察到病人完成简单任务和记忆力减退的困难后，怀疑是痴呆症。


近义词：dementia, cognitive decline, senility|反义词：cognitive health, lucidity|词性：名词
