词源:
The word "pom-pom" is of French origin, likely derived from the French word "pompon," meaning "tuft" or "small ball."  Its use to describe decorative balls of yarn or fluff likely arose in the late 19th or early 20th century, coinciding with the rise of organized cheerleading.
