endowment:/ɪnˈdaʊmənt/| n.|捐赠；天赋；基金；捐款|A gift of money or property, especially to a charity or educational institution; a natural aptitude or ability.  |例句：The university received a large endowment from an anonymous donor.  大学收到一位匿名捐赠者的大笔捐款。|例句：His musical endowment was evident from a young age. 他从小就显露出音乐天赋。|近义词：donation, grant, gift, talent, aptitude|反义词：deprivation, lack


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The university relies on its endowment for funding. = 大学依靠其捐赠基金进行资助。
This scholarship is funded by a generous endowment. = 这项奖学金由一笔慷慨的捐款资助。

一般过去时:
The foundation received a substantial endowment last year. = 该基金会去年收到一笔巨额捐款。
She inherited a large endowment from her grandfather. = 她继承了她祖父的巨额遗产。

现在完成时:
The hospital has benefited greatly from its endowment. = 这家医院从其捐赠中获益匪浅。
They have built a new wing thanks to the endowment. = 多亏了捐赠，他们建了一座新的侧楼。

过去完成时:
Before the expansion, the school had relied heavily on its endowment. = 在扩建之前，学校严重依赖其捐款。
The project had been delayed because the endowment was smaller than expected. = 由于捐款少于预期，项目已被推迟。

将来时:
The college will use its endowment to fund new research. = 这所大学将利用其捐赠基金资助新的研究。
The endowment will provide scholarships for many years to come. = 这笔捐款将在未来许多年提供奖学金。

虚拟语气:
If the endowment were larger, they could hire more staff. = 如果捐款更多，他们就能雇佣更多员工。
I wish the endowment had been sufficient to cover all expenses. = 我希望捐款足够支付所有费用。

被动语态:
The new building was constructed with funds from the endowment. = 新建筑是用捐赠基金建造的。
The scholarship is funded by an endowment established in 1990. = 这项奖学金是由1990年设立的捐赠基金资助的。

疑问句:
What is the size of the university's endowment? = 大学捐赠基金的规模是多少？
Does the charity have a significant endowment? = 该慈善机构是否有大量的捐赠？

否定句:
The project lacked sufficient endowment to proceed. = 该项目缺乏足够的捐款以继续进行。
The school did not receive the endowment it had hoped for. = 学校没有收到它希望得到的捐款。
