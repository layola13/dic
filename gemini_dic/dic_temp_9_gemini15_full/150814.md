paraffiny: /ˈpærəfɪni/ | adj. | 石蜡似的，像石蜡的 | Resembling paraffin wax; having the properties or appearance of paraffin wax.  The word suggests a texture that is waxy, smooth, and slightly greasy. |


例句：

The candles had a paraffiny sheen. = 蜡烛有一种石蜡般的光泽。

The surface of the old furniture had a slightly paraffiny feel. = 那件旧家具的表面摸起来有点像石蜡。


近义词：waxy, greasy, oily


反义词：dry, rough


词性: adjective
