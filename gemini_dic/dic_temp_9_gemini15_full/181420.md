reiterations:/ˌriːɪtəˈreɪʃənz/| n.|重复，反复陈述|The act of repeating something again and again.|例句：The constant reiterations of the same message became tedious.  反复重复同样的信息变得令人厌烦。|例句：His reiterations of his innocence failed to convince the jury. 他反复强调自己的清白未能说服陪审团。|近义词：repetitions, reiterations, repetitions|反义词:uniqueness, originality.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher reiterates the instructions. = 老师反复强调说明。
He reiterates his point constantly. = 他不断地重申他的观点。

一般过去时:
She reiterated her concerns to the manager. = 她向经理重申了她的担忧。
The speaker reiterated the key findings of the report. = 演讲者重申了报告的主要发现。

现在完成时:
He has reiterated his commitment to the project. = 他已经重申了他对项目的承诺。
They have reiterated their support for the cause. = 他们已经重申了他们对这项事业的支持。

过去完成时:
She had reiterated her position several times before the meeting. = 在会议之前，她已经几次重申了自己的立场。
He had reiterated his apology before leaving. = 他在离开之前已经再次道歉。

将来时:
The president will reiterate his stance on the issue tomorrow. = 总统明天将重申他对这个问题的立场。
She will reiterate the importance of punctuality. = 她将再次强调准时的重要性。

虚拟语气:
If he reiterated his apology, she might forgive him. = 如果他再次道歉，她可能会原谅他。
I wish he had reiterated his support earlier. = 我希望他早些时候就重申了他的支持。

被动语态:
The message was reiterated throughout the meeting. = 在整个会议期间，这条信息被反复强调。
The company's policy has been reiterated in the new handbook. = 公司的政策在新手册中得到了重申。

疑问句:
Will the government reiterate its promise? = 政府会重申其承诺吗？
Did he reiterate his request? = 他是否重申了他的请求？

否定句:
He did not reiterate his claims. = 他没有重复他的说法。
The witness did not reiterate her testimony. = 证人没有重复她的证词。


PHRASE->
constant reiterations = 反复强调
repeated reiterations = 重复的重申
unnecessary reiterations = 不必要的重复
fruitless reiterations = 无效的重复
meaningless reiterations = 无意义的重复
