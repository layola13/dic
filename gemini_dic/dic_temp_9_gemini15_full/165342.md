preinvolvement: /ˌpriːɪnˈvɒlvmənt/ | n. |预先参与；事前介入 | Involvement before the main event or activity.  It suggests a preparatory or anticipatory involvement, rather than direct participation in the central action.  |例句：The preinvolvement of stakeholders ensured a smoother project launch. 事先让利益相关者参与确保了项目的顺利启动。

The preinvolvement of local communities in the planning process was crucial to its success.  在规划过程中当地社区的预先参与对其成功至关重要。|近义词：prior involvement, advance participation, preliminary engagement |反义词：postinvolvement, aftereffect, nonparticipation |
