routine:/ruːˈtiːn/ | n. |例行公事；常规；程序 | a sequence of actions regularly followed; a fixed program |  She follows a strict morning routine. 她遵循严格的早晨例行程序。|  He broke his routine by going to the beach. 他打破了他的常规，去了海滩。| habit, schedule, regimen | irregularity, spontaneity | 名词
