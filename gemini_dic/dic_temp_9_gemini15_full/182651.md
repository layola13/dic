reorient:/ˌriːˈɔːriənt/ | vt. |重新定向；使适应新情况；改变方向 |To change the position or direction of something; to change someone's attitude or thinking. |例句：The company is reorienting its marketing strategy to target a younger demographic. 公司正在调整其营销策略以瞄准更年轻的人群。|例句：After the accident, he needed time to reorient himself to normal life. 事故后，他需要时间来适应正常生活。|近义词：redirect, readjust, reposition |反义词：disorient, confuse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reorients his business strategy every year. = 他每年都会调整他的商业策略。
The teacher reorients the students to the new curriculum. = 老师引导学生适应新的课程。


一般过去时:
She reoriented her life after the divorce. = 离婚后，她重新规划了自己的生活。
The pilot reoriented the plane to avoid the storm. = 飞行员调整了飞机的方向以避开风暴。


现在完成时:
They have reoriented their priorities since the merger. = 自从合并以来，他们已经重新调整了优先级。
The company has reoriented its marketing towards social media. = 公司已经将市场营销转向社交媒体。


过去完成时:
He had reoriented his goals before starting the project. = 他在开始项目之前已经调整了自己的目标。
The team had reoriented its approach after the initial failure. = 团队在最初失败后已经调整了方法。


将来时:
We will reorient the furniture to make more space. = 我们将重新摆放家具以腾出更多空间。
The government will reorient its policies towards sustainable development. = 政府将调整其政策，使其更注重可持续发展。


虚拟语气:
If we reoriented our resources, we could achieve more. = 如果我们重新分配资源，我们可以取得更大的成就。
I wish I had reoriented my career path earlier. = 我希望我早点改变我的职业道路。


被动语态:
The map was reoriented to show the new road. = 地图被重新定向以显示新道路。
The company's strategy is being reoriented to focus on innovation. = 公司的战略正在调整以专注于创新。


疑问句:
How can we reorient the company culture to promote teamwork? = 我们如何才能重新调整公司文化以促进团队合作？
Will the project be reoriented to meet the new deadline? = 项目是否会重新调整以满足新的截止日期？


否定句:
The company did not reorient its strategy despite the market changes. = 尽管市场发生了变化，该公司并没有调整其战略。
He refused to reorient his thinking even after the evidence was presented. = 即使证据摆在他面前，他仍然拒绝改变想法。
