nondefinability:/ˌnɒnˌdɛfɪnəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不可定义性，无法定义 |The inability to be defined or explained precisely.  It often refers to concepts or entities that resist clear, concise definition. For example, in mathematics, certain numbers or sets may possess nondefinability. In philosophy, abstract concepts like consciousness or existence may be subject to nondefinability. | The nondefinability of beauty is a frequent topic of philosophical discussion.  美学的不可定义性是哲学讨论中的一个常见话题。

The nondefinability of certain mathematical concepts led to the development of new theoretical frameworks.  某些数学概念的不可定义性导致了新的理论框架的开发。


近义词：undefinability, inexplicability, indescribability

反义词：definability, clarity, explicability
