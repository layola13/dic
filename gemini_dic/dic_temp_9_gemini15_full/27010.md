calculations:/ˌkæl.kjəˈleɪ.ʃənz/ | n. | 计算，计算结果 | The process of mathematical computation; the result of such a process.  It involves using numbers and mathematical operations to find an answer or solution. | Example: The calculations for the project were complex and time-consuming.  这个项目的计算复杂且耗时。 The final calculations showed a profit. 最终计算显示盈利。|近义词：computations, estimations, reckonings |反义词：guesses, approximations
