polygamous:/pəˈlɪɡəməs/| adj.| 一夫多妻制的；一妻多夫制的；多配偶制的 | Practicing or characterized by having more than one spouse at a time.  |例句：Some cultures are polygamous, allowing men to have multiple wives.  一些文化是一夫多妻制的，允许男人拥有多个妻子。

例句：The study examined the social implications of polygamous relationships in various societies.  这项研究考察了多配偶关系在不同社会中的社会影响。


近义词：polygynous (一夫多妻制的), polyandrous (一妻多夫制的), bigamous (重婚的)

反义词：monogamous (一夫一妻制的)


词性:形容词
