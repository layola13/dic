enfree:/ɪnˈfriː/ | vt. |使自由；释放；免除|To set free; to liberate; to exempt.  The verb "enfree" means to grant freedom to someone or something, to release from confinement or restraint, or to exempt from an obligation or restriction. |例句：The prisoner was enfreed after serving his sentence.  犯人服刑期满后被释放了。
The new law enfreed many businesses from unnecessary regulations. 新法律使许多企业免受不必要的规章制度的约束。|近义词：liberate, release, free, exempt |反义词：imprison, confine, restrict, bind
