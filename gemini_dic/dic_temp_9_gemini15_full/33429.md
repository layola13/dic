churchwomen:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃˌwɪmɪn/| n.|教会妇女，女教徒|Women who are actively involved in the affairs of a church.  The term often implies a level of dedication and involvement beyond simply attending services.|例句：The churchwomen organized a bake sale to raise money for the new church building. 教会妇女们组织了一个烘焙义卖活动来为新的教堂大楼筹款。|例句：Many churchwomen volunteered their time to help the needy in the community.许多教会妇女自愿奉献时间帮助社区中的弱势群体。|近义词：churchgoers, female parishioners, devout women|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "non-religious women" or "secular women".|名词
