presentoir:/ˌprɛzɒ̃ˈtwaːr/| n. |展示架，陈列架 | A presentoir is a display stand or case, typically used in shops or museums to showcase products or artifacts. It's designed to attractively present items for viewing or purchase. |例句：The jeweler displayed his most precious gems on an elegant presentoir. 珠宝商将他最珍贵的宝石陈列在一个优雅的展示架上。  The museum used a glass presentoir to protect the ancient artifacts. 博物馆用玻璃展示架来保护古代文物。|近义词：display stand, showcase, display case, stand |反义词：hiding place, storage
