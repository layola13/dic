reliabler:/rɪˈlaɪəbəl/ | adj. | 可靠的，可信赖的 |  A reliable person or thing can be trusted to do what is expected.  It implies consistency and dependability.  |  He's a reliable worker; you can always count on him to finish his tasks on time. 他是一位可靠的工人；你总能指望他按时完成任务。


The car is reliable and has never let me down. 这辆车很可靠，从未让我失望过。|近义词：dependable, trustworthy, steadfast |反义词：unreliable, undependable, untrustworthy |形容词


词源:

The word "reliable" comes from the verb "rely," which itself has roots in Old French "relier" meaning "to bind back, to reunite".  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or suitability.  Therefore, "reliable" literally means "capable of being relied upon."
