parisyllabical: /ˌpærɪsɪˈlæbɪkəl/ | adj. | 等音节的 | Having the same number of syllables.  Relating to or characterized by having an equal number of syllables in successive words or lines of verse.  |The poem displayed a parisyllabical structure, each line echoing the syllable count of the preceding one.| 近义词:  isosyllabic | 反义词:  anisosyllabic
