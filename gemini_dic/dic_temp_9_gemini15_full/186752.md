rigour:/ˈrɪɡər/ | n. |严格；严密；精确；刻苦；艰难 |Rigour refers to the quality of being extremely thorough, exhaustive, or accurate.  It can also describe the severity or harshness of something, or the strictness of a system or process.  It implies a demanding standard of excellence and precision. |The scientific study requires great rigour in its methodology. 科学研究需要非常严格的方法论。

The judge's rigour in applying the law was evident. 法官在适用法律方面的严格性是显而易见的。
