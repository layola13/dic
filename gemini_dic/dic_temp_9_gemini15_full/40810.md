convolution:/ˌkɒnvəˈluːʃən/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈluːʃən/ | n. |卷积；旋绕；盘旋 | The mathematical operation of convolution involves combining two functions to produce a third function that expresses how the shape of one is modified by the other.  It's widely used in signal processing, image processing, and other fields. For example, in image processing, convolution can be used to blur an image or detect edges. | Example:  The convolution of two Gaussian functions is another Gaussian function.  卷积运算涉及到组合两个函数以产生第三个函数，该函数表达了一个函数的形状如何被另一个函数修改。它广泛应用于信号处理、图像处理和其他领域。例如，在图像处理中，卷积可以用来模糊图像或检测边缘。 例如：两个高斯函数的卷积是另一个高斯函数。|近义词：coiling, winding, folding |反义词：unfolding, unraveling | noun
