change hands:/tʃeɪndʒ hændz/| v. |易手；转让；被转让|To pass from one person or group to another; to be transferred from one owner to another.  |The antique vase changed hands several times before ending up in a museum. 古董花瓶在最终进入博物馆之前易手了好几次。 The company changed hands last year. 这家公司去年易主了。 |transfer, pass, shift|retain, keep|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The property changes hands every few years. = 这处房产每隔几年就易手一次。
The painting changes hands frequently at auction. = 这幅画在拍卖会上经常易主。


一般过去时:
The business changed hands unexpectedly last month. = 上个月这家公司意外易主。
The antique car changed hands at a high price. = 这辆古董车以高价易主。


现在完成时:
The farm has changed hands three times since 1990. = 自1990年以来，这个农场已经易手三次了。
The leadership of the organization has changed hands several times. = 该组织的领导层已经易手数次。


过去完成时:
The painting had changed hands several times before it was stolen. = 这幅画在被盗之前已经易手好几次了。
The company had changed hands before the scandal broke. = 丑闻爆发前，该公司已经易主了。


将来时:
The house will change hands next week. = 这栋房子下周将易主。
The ownership of the land will change hands soon. = 这块土地的所有权很快就会易手。


虚拟语气:
