scrublands:/ˈskrʌblændz/| n. |灌木丛地，荒地 |Dry, sparsely vegetated land dominated by low-lying shrubs and bushes.  Scrublands are often found in arid or semi-arid regions.  They can be a challenging environment for both plants and animals due to limited water availability and harsh conditions.| The sheep grazed peacefully in the scrublands.  绵羊在灌木丛地里平静地吃草。 The explorers carefully navigated their way through the dense scrublands. 探险家们小心翼翼地穿过茂密的灌木丛地。 |wastes, bushlands, heathlands|forests, grasslands|名词
