overhearer:/ˌoʊvərˈhɪrər/| n. |偷听者，无意听到者 | A person who overhears a conversation or other private communication without the speakers' knowledge or consent.  |Example:  The overhearer quickly pretended to be reading a book.  偷听者迅速假装在看书。 The overhearer walked away quietly, pretending he hadn’t heard anything.  偷听者悄悄走开了，假装什么都没听到。|近义词：eavesdropper, listener |反义词：speaker, participant
