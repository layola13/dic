numbing: /ˈnʌmɪŋ/ | adj. | 麻木的；使麻木的 | 使失去感觉或知觉；令人麻木的；使人感觉迟钝的 | The numbing pain in my arm made it difficult to move. 我胳膊麻木的疼痛使我难以移动。 | 近义词：deadening, benumbing, paralyzing | 反义词：sensitive, alert


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cold weather numbs my fingers. = 冷天使我的手指麻木。
This medicine numbs the pain. = 这药能减轻疼痛。


一般过去时:
The anesthetic numbed my leg before the surgery. = 手术前麻醉剂使我的腿麻木了。
The shock numbed her for a moment. = 震惊使她愣住了片刻。


现在完成时:
The long hours of work have numbed my senses. = 长时间的劳作使我的感官麻木了。
The constant negativity has numbed her to the suffering. = 持续的消极情绪使她对痛苦麻木不仁。


过去完成时:
The trauma had numbed him to all emotion. = 那次创伤使他对所有情感都麻木了。
She had numbed herself with alcohol. = 她用酒精麻痹自己。


将来时:
The cold will numb my toes if I don't wear socks. = 如果我不穿袜子，寒冷会使我的脚趾麻木。
This medication will numb the pain quickly. = 这药很快就会麻痹疼痛。


虚拟语气:
If I took that drug, it would numb my pain. = 如果我服用那种药物，它会使我的疼痛麻木。
I wish the pain would numb itself. = 我希望疼痛能自行麻木。


被动语态:
His feelings were numbed by the tragedy. = 他的感情因悲剧而麻木了。
The area was numbed by the local anesthetic. = 该区域被局部麻醉剂麻痹了。


疑问句:
Does the cream numb the area quickly? = 这种药膏能很快使该区域麻木吗？
Is your hand numb from the cold? = 你的手被冻麻了吗？


否定句:
The cold didn't numb my feet. = 冷天没有使我的脚麻木。
The anesthetic didn't completely numb her. = 麻醉剂并没有完全使她麻木。


PHRASE->
numbing pain = 麻木的疼痛
numbing cold = 刺骨的寒冷
numbing effect = 麻痹作用
emotionally numbing = 情感麻木
numbing routine = 单调乏味的生活


词源:
The word "numbing" comes from the Old English word "numen," meaning "benumbed, numb."  The modern English word developed through Middle English.
