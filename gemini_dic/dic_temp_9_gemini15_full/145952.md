overdiscussing:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskʌsɪŋ/| v.|过度讨论，讨论过多|The act of discussing something excessively or to an unnecessary extent.  It often implies that the discussion has become unproductive, tedious, or even counterproductive.|例句：Overdiscussing the project's timeline only led to more confusion and delays.  过度讨论项目的期限只会导致更多混乱和延误。
例句：We've been overdiscussing this issue for hours, and we haven't reached a conclusion. 我们已经讨论这个问题几个小时了，还没有达成结论。|近义词：overanalyzing, overthinking, belaboring,  rehashing|反义词：briefly discussing,  summarizing, overlooking|词性：动词
