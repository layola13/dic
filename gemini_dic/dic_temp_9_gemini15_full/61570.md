emends:/ɪˈmɛndz/| n.|修正，改正；赔偿|Corrections or improvements made to a text or other work.|The editor made several amends to the manuscript before publication. 编辑在出版前对稿件做了几处修改。|近义词：corrections, improvements, reparations|反义词：errors, mistakes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He amends his mistakes regularly. = 他定期改正自己的错误。
She amends her tax return. = 她修改了自己的纳税申报单。


一般过去时:
The author amended the final chapter. = 作者修改了最后一章。
They amended the contract to include a new clause. = 他们修改了合同，增加了一项新条款。


现在完成时:
She has amended her statement several times. = 她已经多次修改了自己的声明。
He has amended the document to reflect the new information. = 他已经修改了文件以反映新的信息。


过去完成时:
They had amended the policy before the incident occurred. = 他们在事件发生之前已经修改了政策。
She had amended her testimony before taking the stand. = 她在出庭作证之前已经修改了自己的证词。


将来时:
He will amend the document tomorrow. = 他明天将修改该文件。
They will amend the law next year. = 他们明年将修改这项法律。


虚拟语气:
If he amended his behavior, he would have a better relationship. = 如果他改正自己的行为，他就会有更好的关系。
I wish she had amended her application before submitting it. = 我希望她在提交申请之前已经修改了它。


被动语态:
The document was amended by the committee. = 该文件由委员会修改。
The law will be amended next session. = 该法案将在下一届会议上进行修改。


疑问句:
Will the amendment be made soon? = 修改案会很快通过吗？
Has the contract been amended? = 合同已经修改了吗？


否定句:
He did not amend his report. = 他没有修改他的报告。
They will not amend the rules. = 他们不会修改规则。


PHRASE->
make amends = 赔罪，补偿
amend a bill = 修改法案
amend a document = 修改文件
amend a law = 修改法律
amend a statement = 修改声明
