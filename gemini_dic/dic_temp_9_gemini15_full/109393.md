lentigo:/lɛnˈtiːɡoʊ/| n.|雀斑，老年斑|A small, usually brown spot on the skin, often caused by sun exposure. Lentigines are benign (non-cancerous) and are typically flat and vary in size and color.|例句：The dermatologist examined the lentigo on her arm to rule out melanoma. 皮肤科医生检查了她手臂上的雀斑以排除黑色素瘤的可能性。  The sun exposure caused numerous lentigines to appear on her face.日晒导致她脸上出现了许多老年斑。|近义词：freckle, sunspot, macule|反义词：无
