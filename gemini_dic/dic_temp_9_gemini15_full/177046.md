reannotate: /ˌriːˈænəʊteɪt/ | vt. |重新注释；重新标注 | To annotate something again, often with corrections or additions.  |例句：The researchers reannotated the genome after discovering new data.  研究人员在发现新的数据后重新注释了基因组。

例句：The editor reannotated the manuscript to clarify some ambiguous points. 编辑重新注释了手稿以阐明一些含糊不清的点。


近义词：re-mark, revise, amend  | 反义词：None specifically, but "to remove annotations" would be an opposite action. | 动词
