misestimation: /ˌmɪsˌɛstɪˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 估计错误，误估 | The act of incorrectly estimating something; a wrong calculation or judgment of amount, size, value, etc.  |  例句：The misestimation of the project's cost led to significant budget overruns. 项目成本的误估导致了严重的预算超支。

例句：Their misestimation of the market demand resulted in unsold inventory. 他们对市场需求的误估导致了滞销库存。


近义词：miscalculation, misjudgment, underestimation, overestimation, error, mistake


反义词：accurate estimation, precise calculation


词性：名词
