gentrification:/ˌdʒɛntrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |士绅化，中产阶级化 | The process of renovating and improving a house or district so that it conforms to middle-class taste.  This often results in the displacement of lower-income residents. | Example: The gentrification of the neighborhood has driven up property values, making it unaffordable for long-time residents. 士绅化使该社区的房价上涨，让长期居住的居民无力承受。


Example:  The rapid gentrification of the area has led to increased property taxes and a loss of cultural diversity.  该地区的快速士绅化导致房产税增加和文化多样性的丧失。


Near synonyms:  urban renewal, upgrading, revitalization


Antonyms:  decay, deterioration, slumming


Word forms:  gentrify (verb)  士绅化（动词）
