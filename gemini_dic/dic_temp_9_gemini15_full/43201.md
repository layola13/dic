crabby:/ˈkræbi/| adj.|脾气暴躁的；易怒的；抱怨的|easily annoyed or irritable; bad-tempered; complaining|例句：He's been awfully crabby lately.  他最近脾气很暴躁。

例句：Don't be so crabby; it's a beautiful day! 不要这么暴躁；今天天气很好！


近义词：grumpy, irritable, grumpy, crotchety, surly, cantankerous|反义词：cheerful, happy, pleasant, amiable


词性:形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is always crabby in the morning. = 他早上总是脾气暴躁。
She is crabby because she didn't get enough sleep. = 她因为睡眠不足而脾气暴躁。

一般过去时:
He was crabby yesterday because of the rain. = 昨天因为下雨他脾气很暴躁。
She was crabby after the long meeting. = 漫长的会议之后她脾气很暴躁。

现在完成时:
He has been crabby all week. = 他整个星期都脾气暴躁。
She has been crabby since she lost her job. = 自从她失业以来，她就一直脾气暴躁。

过去完成时:
He had been crabby for days before he finally snapped. = 在他最终爆发之前，他已经脾气暴躁好几天了。
She had been crabby all morning, so I avoided her. = 她整个早上都脾气暴躁，所以我避开了她。

将来时:
He will be crabby if he doesn't get his coffee. = 如果他不喝咖啡，他会脾气暴躁。
She will probably be crabby after the exam. = 考试后她可能会脾气暴躁。

虚拟语气:
If he weren't so crabby, people would like him more. = 如果他不那么脾气暴躁，人们会更喜欢他。
I wish she weren't so crabby all the time. = 我希望她不要总是脾气暴躁。

被动语态:  (Crabbiness is rarely used passively.)

疑问句:
Is he always this crabby? = 他总是这么脾气暴躁吗？
Why is she so crabby today? = 她今天为什么脾气这么暴躁？


否定句:
He isn't usually crabby. = 他通常不会脾气暴躁。
She wasn't crabby at all this morning. = 她今天早上一点也不脾气暴躁。
