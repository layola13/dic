out-despatches:/ˌaʊtˈdɛspætʃɪz/ | n. | 外发；发出；发出的信件或电报 |  A collective noun referring to dispatches sent out or published.  It emphasizes the act of sending out multiple documents or messages.  The term suggests a formal or official nature, often associated with news or military communications. | Example: The out-despatches from the front detailed the day's battles.  前线的对外发文详细描述了当天的战役。|近义词：dispatches, communications, bulletins, reports | 反义词：in-coming messages, received communications | 名词
