despatches:/ˈdɪspætʃɪz/ | n. | 捷报；急件；派遣；|Official reports sent from one place to another, especially by soldiers or government officials.  Dispatches often contain important news or information.|The general sent a dispatch to headquarters reporting the victory.将军向总部发去了报告胜利的急件。|The journalist received a dispatch detailing the latest developments in the conflict.记者收到一份详细报道冲突最新进展的新闻稿。|news reports, messages, communications |no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with "casual reports" or "unofficial accounts"|名词
