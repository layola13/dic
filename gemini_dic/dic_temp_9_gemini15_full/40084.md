container:/kənˈteɪnər/| n. |容器，集装箱|A container is an object used for holding or storing something. It can be anything from a simple box to a large shipping container.|例句：The box is a container for the toys.  盒子是玩具的容器。|例句：The ship carried many large containers across the ocean. 这艘船运载了许多大型集装箱横渡海洋。|近义词：receptacle, vessel, holder|反义词：content(as a noun, referring to what's inside)|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The box contains my old photos. = 箱子里装着我的旧照片。
This container holds ten liters of water. = 这个容器能装十升水。

一般过去时:
The jar contained jam. = 罐子里装着果酱。
She used a plastic container to store the leftovers. = 她用塑料容器储存剩菜。

现在完成时:
The warehouse has contained many tons of goods this year. = 今年仓库已储存了数吨货物。
We have contained the oil spill. = 我们已经控制住了漏油事故。

过去完成时:
The bottle had contained wine before it was filled with water. = 这瓶子装过酒，后来才装的水。
They had contained their emotions throughout the meeting.  他们在整个会议期间都克制住了自己的情绪。

将来时:
The new building will contain a library and a gym. = 新楼将包含一个图书馆和一个健身房。
This container will hold all your belongings. = 这个容器可以容纳你所有的物品。

虚拟语气:
If the container were larger, we could fit more items inside. = 如果容器更大，我们就能装更多东西。
I wish this container held more. = 我希望这个容器能装更多。

被动语态:
The goods are contained in a secure warehouse. = 货物存放在安全的仓库里。
The spill was contained quickly by the emergency services. = 紧急服务部门迅速控制了泄漏。

疑问句:
What does this container hold? = 这个容器装的是什么？
Does this container hold enough? = 这个容器够用吗？

否定句:
The bottle doesn't contain any liquid. = 瓶子里没有任何液体。
The box did not contain the expected documents. = 箱子里没有预期的文件。
