generatrix: /ˈdʒɛnətrɪks/ | n. | 母线；生成线；产生者 | A line, curve, or surface that, by its motion, generates a surface or solid.  For example, a line revolving around an axis generates a surface of revolution.  In mathematics, it's often used to describe the curve or line that, when rotated or transformed, creates a specific geometric shape. | The generatrix of a cone is a straight line.  = 圆锥的母线是一条直线。

The rotating generatrix creates a paraboloid. =旋转的母线产生抛物面。 |近义词：generator, originator, producer | 反义词：consumer, destroyer
