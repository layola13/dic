echidnas: /ˈɛkɪdnəz/ | n. |针鼹;  | Echidnas are spiny anteaters belonging to the family Tachyglossidae. They are monotremes, meaning they lay eggs, and are native to Australia and New Guinea. They are characterized by their spiny bodies, long snouts, and powerful claws.  |  Example:  The echidnas of Australia are fascinating creatures.  澳大利亚的针鼹是令人着迷的生物。   | 近义词: spiny anteater | 反义词:  (无直接反义词)
