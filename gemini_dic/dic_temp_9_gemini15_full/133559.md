nonfisherman:/ˌnɒnˈfɪʃərmən/| n. |非渔民|A person who does not fish.  This word is typically used in contrast to fisherman. It describes someone who doesn't engage in fishing as a hobby, profession, or activity.|Example: The nonfisherman watched the fishermen patiently cast their lines.  这位非渔民耐心地看着渔民们抛竿。 The survey showed a significant percentage of the local population were nonfishermen. 调查显示当地居民中很大一部分是非渔民。|近义词: non-angler, non-piscator|反义词: fisherman, angler
