non-obedience:/ˌnɒnəˈbiːdiəns/| n.|不服从，违抗|The refusal or failure to obey rules, laws, or commands.  It often implies a conscious and deliberate act of defiance.|例句：Their non-obedience to the school rules resulted in suspension. 他们不服从学校规定导致停学。

例句：The widespread non-obedience to the curfew led to further restrictions.  人们广泛违反宵禁导致进一步的限制。|近义词：disobedience, defiance, insubordination, recalcitrance|反义词：obedience, compliance, submission|词性：名词
