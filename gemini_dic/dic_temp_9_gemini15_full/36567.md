collapsing:/kəˈlæpsɪŋ/| v. adj.|坍塌的；崩溃的；衰竭的 |The act of falling down or inwards suddenly; breaking down or failing.  The process of something decreasing rapidly in value or strength. |例句：The old bridge is collapsing.  那座旧桥正在坍塌。

The building collapsed after the earthquake. 地震后，那栋建筑物倒塌了。


近义词：crumbling, falling, breaking down, failing

反义词：standing, stable, firm, strong

词性：动词（现在分词形式）,形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bridge is collapsing. = 桥梁正在坍塌。
The economy is collapsing. = 经济正在崩溃。


一般过去时:
The building collapsed yesterday. = 建筑物昨天坍塌了。
The company collapsed last year. = 公司去年倒闭了。


现在完成时:
The roof has collapsed. = 屋顶已经坍塌了。
The stock market has collapsed. = 股市已经崩盘了。


过去完成时:
The bridge had collapsed before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，桥梁已经坍塌了。
The system had collapsed before we could fix it. = 在我们能够修复它之前，系统已经崩溃了。


将来时:
The dam will collapse if the water level rises. = 如果水位上升，水坝将会坍塌。
The government might collapse if the crisis continues. = 如果危机持续下去，政府可能会垮台。


虚拟语气:
If the supports were removed, the structure would collapse. = 如果移除支撑物，结构将会坍塌。
If the bank had lent them more money, the business would not have collapsed. = 如果银行多借给他们一些钱，这家企业就不会倒闭了。


被动语态:
The building was collapsed by the earthquake. = 建筑物被地震摧毁了。(注意：此句被动语态略显不自然，更常用主动语态)
The economy is being collapsed by the pandemic. = 经济正被疫情摧毁。(注意：此句被动语态略显不自然，更常用主动语态)


疑问句:
Is the building collapsing? = 建筑物正在坍塌吗？
Is the government collapsing? = 政府正在垮台吗？


否定句:
The building is not collapsing. = 建筑物没有坍塌。
The economy is not collapsing yet. = 经济还没有崩溃。
