eases:/ˈiːzɪz/| n. v.|轻松，容易；缓和，减轻 |The plural form of "ease".  It refers to instances or periods of comfort and relaxation, or the act of making something easier or less severe. |例句：The eases in his life were few and far between.他生活中轻松的时刻少之又少。|近义词：reliefs, comforts |反义词：difficulties, hardships |verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The warm water eases my aching muscles. = 温水缓解了我酸痛的肌肉。
This new software eases the workload considerably. = 这个新软件大大减轻了工作量。

一般过去时:
The medication eased his pain. = 药物缓解了他的疼痛。
The storm eased after sunset. = 日落后风暴减弱了。

现在完成时:
The new policy has eased traffic congestion. = 新政策缓解了交通拥堵。
The situation has eased somewhat since yesterday. = 自从昨天以来，情况有所好转。

过去完成时:
The tension had eased before the meeting began. = 会议开始前，紧张气氛已经缓和了。
After the rain had eased, we continued our hike. = 雨停后，我们继续远足。

将来时:
The doctor hopes the new treatment will ease his symptoms. = 医生希望新的治疗方法能缓解他的症状。
They expect the economic situation to ease next year. = 他们预计明年经济形势将会好转。

虚拟语气:
If only the pain would ease! = 但愿疼痛能减轻！
I wish the traffic would ease up. = 我希望交通能好转。

被动语态:
His anxiety was eased by the good news. = 好消息缓解了他的焦虑。
The pressure on the company is being eased by increased sales. = 销售额的增加减轻了公司的压力。

疑问句:
Will this cream ease the itch? = 这款乳霜能缓解瘙痒吗？
Does this method ease the process? = 这种方法能简化流程吗？

否定句:
Nothing seemed to ease her worries. = 似乎没有什么能减轻她的担忧。
The situation didn't ease despite our efforts. = 尽管我们努力了，情况也没有好转。
