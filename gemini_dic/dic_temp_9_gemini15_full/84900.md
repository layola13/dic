hankerings:/ˈhæŋkərɪŋz/| n.|渴望，热望，向往|A strong desire or longing for something.|例句：He had strong hankerings for the old days. 他非常渴望回到过去的日子。


例句：Her hankerings for travel led her to quit her job and backpack through Europe.  她的旅行渴望使她辞去工作，背着背包游历欧洲。

近义词：longings, yearnings, cravings, desires

反义词：aversion, dislike, indifference

词性:名词

词源:源自动词hankering，其词根可追溯到古英语*hancian，意为“渴望，渴望”。
