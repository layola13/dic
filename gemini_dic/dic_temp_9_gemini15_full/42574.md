counterpicketed:/ˈkaʊntərˈpɪkɪtɪd/| v. | 反抗性静坐示威 | To engage in a counter-picket, a protest against a picket line.  It involves setting up a demonstration to oppose the views or actions of the original picketers. | Example:  The company's employees counter-picketed the striking union members. 公司的员工对罢工的工会成员进行了反罢工示威。  The workers counter-picketed the factory gates, protesting the unfair dismissal. 工人们在工厂门口进行了反罢工示威，抗议不公平解雇。|近义词：counter-demonstrate, oppose, protest against |反义词：support, endorse, picket
