heart-rending: /ˈhɑːrtˌrendɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人心碎的；悲痛欲绝的 | Extremely sad and moving; causing great sorrow. |

英文解析:The word "heart-rending" describes something that is so sad or distressing that it causes deep emotional pain and anguish. It evokes a feeling of intense sorrow and sympathy in the listener or reader.  The word emphasizes the profound emotional impact of the event or situation.

例句:

The news of the child's death was heart-rending. = 孩子死亡的消息令人心碎。

The story of their separation was a heart-rending tale of lost love. = 他们分手的经历是一个令人心碎的失恋故事。


近义词: heartbreaking, agonizing, devastating, poignant, moving, sorrowful, pathetic, distressing


反义词: joyful, happy, cheerful, exhilarating, uplifting, joyous


词性: adjective
