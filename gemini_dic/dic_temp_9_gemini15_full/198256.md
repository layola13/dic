shortsighted:/ˈʃɔːrtsaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 近视的；目光短浅的；缺乏远见的 | Lacking foresight or intellectual insight;  unable to see or plan for the future.  It can refer to literal nearsightedness (inability to see distant objects clearly) or figurative shortsightedness (lack of foresight). |

例句：

He was shortsighted and didn't consider the long-term consequences of his actions. 他目光短浅，没有考虑他行为的长期后果。

Her shortsighted decision cost the company millions of dollars.  她目光短浅的决定使公司损失了数百万美元。


近义词：myopic, narrow-minded, unperceptive, farsighted (as an antonym, focusing on the opposite meaning of having foresight)


反义词：farsighted, long-sighted, prescient, visionary


词性：形容词
