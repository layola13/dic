nanoparticles:/ˈnænəʊˌpɑːrtɪklz/ | n. |纳米粒子 | Extremely small particles ranging in size from 1 to 100 nanometers.  They exhibit unique physical and chemical properties due to their small size, leading to a wide range of applications in various fields. | Example: Scientists are researching the use of nanoparticles in drug delivery. 科学家们正在研究纳米粒子在药物递送中的应用。 |近义词: ultrafine particles, nano-sized particles |反义词:macroscopic particles
