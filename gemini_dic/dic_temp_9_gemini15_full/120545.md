miracles:/ˈmɪrəklz/| n. |奇迹，奇事 |An event that appears inexplicable by natural or scientific laws and is considered to be the work of a divine agency.  A miracle is something extraordinary and wonderful that is not explicable by ordinary means. |例句：The doctor called his recovery a miracle. 医生称他的康复是个奇迹。

例句：The healing of the sick child was considered a miracle.  治愈生病的孩子被认为是一个奇迹。


近义词：wonder, marvel, prodigy, phenomenon |反义词：impossibility, normality
