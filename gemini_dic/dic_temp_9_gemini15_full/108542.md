lazied:/ˈleɪzɪd/| v.|懒散度日，懒惰地度过| to spend time in a lazy way; to act lazily |例句：He lazied around the house all afternoon. 他整个下午都在家里懒洋洋地待着。例句：She lazied away the summer, doing little work. 她懒洋洋地消磨了整个夏天，几乎没做什么工作。|近义词：loafed, idled, dawdled|反义词：hustled, bustled, worked
