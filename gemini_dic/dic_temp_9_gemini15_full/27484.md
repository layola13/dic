campanologist:/ˌkæmpəˈnɒlədʒɪst/| n. |钟铃学家，撞钟专家|A campanologist is an expert in bells, particularly church bells, their history, making, ringing, and musical potential. They may be involved in bell-ringing, bell-making, or the study of bells' history and cultural significance. |The campanologist meticulously tuned the church bells before the Sunday service.  钟铃学家在周日礼拜前仔细地调整了教堂的钟声。|bell ringer, bell founder| (no direct antonym, perhaps "someone ignorant of bells")
