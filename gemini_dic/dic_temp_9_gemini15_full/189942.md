sanctificate:/ˈsæŋktɪfeɪt/| vt.| 使神圣；奉献；使圣洁 | To make holy; to consecrate; to purify spiritually.  The priest sanctified the bread and wine. | 近义词：consecrate, hallow, bless | 反义词：desecrate, profane, defile | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The church sanctifies the bread during the mass. = 教堂在弥撒中将面包圣化。
He sanctifies his life through prayer and meditation. = 他通过祈祷和冥想使自己的生活变得神圣。


一般过去时:
The king sanctified the new temple. = 国王将新庙宇奉为圣地。
The priest sanctified the land before the building began. = 在建筑开始之前，牧师将这块土地圣化了。
