perjury:/ˈpɜːrdʒəri/| n.|伪证罪；伪证；作伪证|The act of willfully telling a lie under oath in a judicial proceeding.  It is a serious crime that can result in significant penalties, including imprisonment.|例句：He was found guilty of perjury for lying under oath during the trial. 他在审判中作伪证而被判犯有伪证罪。|例句：The witness committed perjury when they denied seeing the suspect.  证人否认见过嫌疑人，犯了伪证罪。|近义词：false swearing, lying under oath, mendacity|反义词：truthfulness, veracity|名词
