presentations:/ˌprɛzənˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 演示，陈述，介绍 | The act of presenting something, such as an idea, product, or report, to an audience.  It can refer to the act itself or the material presented. |例句：The presentations at the conference were very informative.  会议上的演示非常有信息量。

例句：Her presentation skills are excellent. 她的演示技巧很棒。


近义词：demonstrations, displays, talks, lectures, addresses | 反义词：concealment, suppression, withholding | 词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company gives presentations to potential clients every week. = 公司每周都会向潜在客户进行演示。
He makes presentations regularly as part of his job. = 他定期进行演示作为他工作的一部分。


一般过去时:
She gave a compelling presentation yesterday. = 她昨天做了一个引人入胜的演示。
We presented our findings at the annual meeting. = 我们在年度会议上展示了我们的研究结果。


现在完成时:
They have presented their project to several investors. = 他们已经向几位投资者展示了他们的项目。
I have given many presentations throughout my career. = 我在我的职业生涯中做过许多演示。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, he had prepared his presentation thoroughly. = 在会议之前，他已经认真准备了他的演示。
She had given similar presentations many times before. = 她以前做过很多次类似的演示。


将来时:
She will present her research at the international conference. = 她将在国际会议上展示她的研究成果。
We will be giving a series of presentations next month. = 我们下个月将进行一系列的演示。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would prepare a more detailed presentation. = 如果我有更多时间，我会准备一个更详细的演示。
I wish I had practiced my presentation more. = 我希望我多练习我的演示。
