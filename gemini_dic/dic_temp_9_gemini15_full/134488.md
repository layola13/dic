nonlandowners:/ˌnɑːnˈlændˌoʊnərz/ | n. |非土地所有者|Those who do not own land.  This term often refers to a group of people lacking land ownership, often contrasted with landowners in discussions of land rights, social structures, or economic disparities. |Example: The nonlandowners protested the new tax policies that disproportionately affected them.  非土地所有者抗议对他们影响过大的新税收政策。The rights of nonlandowners were often overlooked in the past.  过去，非土地所有者的权利常常被忽视。|近义词:tenant farmers, landless people |反义词: landowners
