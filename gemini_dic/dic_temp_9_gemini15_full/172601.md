pyrosis:/paɪˈroʊsɪs/| n.|胃灼热，烧心|A burning sensation in the chest, often associated with acid reflux.  It is caused by stomach acid backing up into the esophagus.|例句：She suffered from frequent episodes of pyrosis after eating spicy food. 她吃辛辣食物后经常出现胃灼热。|例句：The doctor prescribed medication to alleviate his pyrosis. 医生开了药来减轻他的胃灼热。|近义词：heartburn, acid reflux, acid indigestion|反义词：None specifically, but relief from pyrosis could be considered an antonym.|名词
