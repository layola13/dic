perniciously:/pərˈnɪʃəsli/| adv.|有害地；恶性地；阴险地 |  In a harmful way, especially in a gradual or subtle way; insidiously.  | 例句：The rumors spread perniciously throughout the town. 谣言在镇上恶性传播。  | 例句：The disease perniciously attacks the body's immune system.  这种疾病恶性地攻击人体的免疫系统。| 近义词：insidiously, subtly, deleteriously, wickedly | 反义词：beneficially, harmlessly, innocuously
