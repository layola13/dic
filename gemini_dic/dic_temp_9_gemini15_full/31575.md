chasm's:/ˈkæzəm/| n. |深渊；裂缝；分歧 |A chasm is a deep, narrow opening in the earth's surface, or a profound difference between opinions or feelings.  |例句：The chasm between the rich and the poor is widening.  富人和穷人之间的差距越来越大。

例句：A vast chasm opened up in the earth after the earthquake. 地震后，地面出现了一个巨大的裂缝。


近义词：abyss, gulf, fissure, rift |反义词：unity, agreement, harmony |词性:名词(复数形式)
