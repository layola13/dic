preopposed:/ˌpriːəˈpoʊzd/| adj.|预先对立的，先期对抗的|  Describes something that is opposed or in conflict beforehand; existing in a state of opposition before another event or action.  It suggests a pre-existing antagonism or conflict. |例句：The two factions held preopposed views on the treaty negotiations.  = 这两个派系对条约谈判持有预先对立的观点。

例句：Their preopposed political stances made collaboration difficult. = 他们预先对立的政治立场使得合作变得困难。


近义词：conflicting, antagonistic, opposed, contrary


反义词：harmonious, compatible, concordant, aligned


词性:形容词
