locomotivity:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊtɪviːti/ | n. | 运动能力；机动性；运动系统 | The capacity for movement or locomotion; the power or ability to move from one place to another.  It can also refer to a system of transportation or the ability of a system to move. |例句：The improved locomotion system of the robot allows it to traverse rough terrain.  改进后的机器人运动系统使其能够穿越崎岖的地形。

例句：The study focused on the locomotion of insects. 这项研究的重点是昆虫的运动方式。


近义词：mobility, motility, movement, propulsion | 反义词：immobility, stillness, stagnation | 词性:名词
