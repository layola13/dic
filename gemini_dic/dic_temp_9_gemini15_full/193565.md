self-amendment:/ˌsɛlf əˈmɛndmənt/| n. |自我修正；自我改进|The process by which something corrects or improves itself without external intervention.  This term is often used in the context of legal documents, constitutions, or even biological systems.  |例句：The constitution allows for self-amendment through a specific process.  宪法允许通过特定程序进行自我修正。

The software features self-amendment capabilities, automatically updating itself to fix bugs. 该软件具有自我修正功能，可以自动更新自身以修复错误。|近义词：self-correction, self-regulation, improvement, rectification|反义词：deterioration, decline, worsening|名词
