fluffs:/flʌfs/ | n., v. |绒毛；蓬松物；使蓬松；轻抚；轻飘飘地移动 |  to make something fluffy or to become fluffy; to touch or stroke something lightly; to move lightly and airily. |例句：She fluffed her pillows before going to bed.  她睡觉前把枕头弄蓬松了。  The kitten fluffed its fur in alarm. 小猫受惊吓，毛都竖起来了。 |近义词：ruffles, puffs, softens |反义词：compacts, flattens
