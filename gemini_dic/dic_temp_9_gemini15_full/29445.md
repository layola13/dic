catalogue: /ˈkætəlɒɡ/ /ˈkætəlɔːɡ/ | n. |目录，商品目录，系列|A catalogue is a list of items, typically with descriptions and prices, often found in a book or online.  It can refer to a comprehensive list of things, such as a catalogue of stars or a catalogue of human emotions. |例句：The museum has a comprehensive catalogue of its collection.  博物馆有一份详尽的馆藏目录。

The online catalogue allows you to search for specific items.  在线目录允许你搜索特定商品。


|近义词：list, inventory, register, index |反义词：N/A |名词|

词源:  From Middle English *catalog, from Old French catalogue, from Late Latin catalogus, from Greek katálogos "a register, list," from katalambanein "to enroll, register," from kata- "down" + lambanein "to take."
