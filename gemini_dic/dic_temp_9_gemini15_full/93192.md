ignoramus:/ɪɡnəˈreɪməs/| n. |无知的人；不懂装懂的人|A person who is ignorant; especially : one who pretends to knowledge he does not possess.  The term carries a strong connotation of deliberate ignorance or affected stupidity.|例句：He acted like an ignoramus in the meeting, refusing to acknowledge the obvious solution. 他在会议上表现得像个无知的人，拒绝承认显而易见的解决方案。

例句：They called him an ignoramus for his blatant disregard of established facts.  他们称他为无知的人，因为他公然无视既定事实。


近义词：dunce, fool, simpleton, blockhead|反义词：expert, scholar, sage, intellectual|词性:名词
