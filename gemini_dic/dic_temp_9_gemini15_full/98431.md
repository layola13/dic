inspections:/ɪnˈspɛkʃənz/| n. |检查，视察 |The act of officially examining something to make sure it meets standards or is in good condition.  It often involves a detailed and systematic review to identify any defects or problems.|例句：Regular safety inspections are required by law.  定期安全检查是法律规定的。|例句：The building passed its final inspections with flying colors. 这座建筑物顺利通过了最后的检查。|近义词：examinations, surveys, reviews |反义词：neglect, oversight |名词


USAGE->

The factory undergoes regular inspections. = 工厂定期进行检查。
He conducts building inspections every week. = 他每周进行建筑检查。
