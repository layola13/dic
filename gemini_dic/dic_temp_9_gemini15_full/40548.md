controversy:/ˈkɒntrəvɜːsi/ /ˈkɑːntrəvɜːrsi/| n. |争议，论战|A controversy is a serious public disagreement or argument about something. It often involves strong opinions on both sides.  The word suggests a prolonged and intense debate, often involving passionate and sometimes conflicting viewpoints. |Example: The new law has sparked considerable controversy. 这项新法律引发了相当大的争议。

The controversy surrounding the election results continues.围绕选举结果的争议仍在继续。

|近义词：dispute, debate, argument, contention |反义词：agreement, harmony, consensus |名词
