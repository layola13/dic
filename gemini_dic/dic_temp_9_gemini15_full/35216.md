clude:/kluːd/ | v. | 关闭；结束；包含；决定 | To bring or come to an end; to shut; to contain or comprise; to decide or determine.  | The meeting concluded at 5 pm.  会议在下午5点结束。 | The evidence concludes that he is guilty.  证据表明他有罪。 |  end, finish, terminate, encompass, include | begin, start, open | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He concludes his speech with a joke. 他用一个笑话结束了他的演讲。
The box includes all necessary tools.  这个盒子包含所有必要的工具。

一般过去时:
She concluded her presentation successfully. 她成功地完成了她的演示。
The investigation concluded that there was no foul play. 调查结论是没有任何不当行为。

现在完成时:
They have concluded their research. 他们已经完成了他们的研究。
We have concluded that the project is feasible. 我们已经得出结论，该项目是可行的。

过去完成时:
He had concluded his work before the deadline. 他在截止日期前完成了他的工作。
They had concluded the meeting before the rain started.  他们在下雨前结束了会议。

将来时:
We will conclude the negotiations tomorrow. 我们明天将结束谈判。
The conference will conclude with a banquet.  会议将以宴会结束。

虚拟语气:
If I concluded the deal, I would be very happy. 如果我完成了这笔交易，我会非常高兴。
I wish I had concluded the report earlier.  我希望我早点完成了报告。

被动语态:
The meeting was concluded early. 会议提前结束了。
The report was concluded with a summary of findings. 报告以调查结果的总结结尾。

疑问句:
How did they conclude the experiment? 他们是如何结束实验的？
What did the doctor conclude about his condition? 医生对他的病情有什么结论？

否定句:
The trial did not conclude today. 审判今天没有结束。
He did not conclude his presentation on time. 他没有按时完成他的演讲。
