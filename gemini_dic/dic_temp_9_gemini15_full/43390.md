cranked-up: /kræŋkt ʌp/ | adj. |  兴奋的；亢奋的；已启动的；调高了的 |  Describes something that is highly excited, energized, or operating at a high level. It can also refer to something that has been turned on or increased in intensity.  | 例句：The band was completely cranked up and ready to rock. 乐队完全兴奋起来，准备摇滚了。

The amplifier was cranked up to eleven.  功放音量开到了十一。

近义词：excited, energized, hyped up, revved up, amplified, intensified | 反义词：calm, relaxed, subdued, quiet, low, deactivated
