misidentified:/ˌmɪsɪˈdɛntɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 误识别的，被误认的 |  Incorrectly identified or categorized.  The word implies a mistake in identification, often due to a lack of sufficient information or a flawed process. | 例句：The suspect was misidentified by the witness. 目击证人误认了嫌疑犯。

The rare bird was misidentified as a common species.  这种稀有鸟类被误认为是普通物种。


近义词：mistaken, wrongly identified, mislabeled, confused


反义词：correctly identified, accurately identified


词性：形容词 (过去分词作形容词)
