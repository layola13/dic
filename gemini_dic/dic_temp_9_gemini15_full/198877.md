sideswipes:/ˈsaɪdswaɪps/| vt. n.|擦撞；轻微碰撞；攻击；批评|To hit the side of something, especially a vehicle, while passing it.  A glancing blow or collision.  Also, to make a brief, critical remark.|例句：The car sideswiped a parked motorcycle. 这辆车擦撞了一辆停放的摩托车。

The politician sideswiped his opponent with a sarcastic comment.  这位政治家用一句讽刺的评论攻击了他的对手。|近义词：brush, graze, clip, slam, criticize, attack|反义词：avoid, miss, praise, compliment|动词，名词
