minster:/ˈmɪnstər/ | n. |主教座堂；大教堂；修道院教堂 | A minster is a large and important church, often a cathedral or abbey church.  It is historically associated with a monastery or a bishop's see. |Example: York Minster is a magnificent cathedral.  约克大教堂是一座宏伟的大教堂。|近义词：cathedral, abbey church, collegiate church |反义词：chapel, small church
