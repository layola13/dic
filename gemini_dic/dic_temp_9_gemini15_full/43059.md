cowling:/ˈkaʊlɪŋ/| n.|整流罩，罩壳|A cowling is a cover, usually made of metal or plastic, that protects or encloses something, such as the engine of an aircraft or a machine.|例句：The mechanic removed the cowling to inspect the engine. 机械师拆掉了整流罩来检查发动机。|例句：The aircraft's cowling was damaged in the crash.  飞机的整流罩在坠机事故中受损。|近义词：casing, shroud, hood|反义词：exposure

词源:
The word "cowling" comes from the Middle English word "cowel," meaning a hood or cowl.  This in turn is likely related to the Old Norse word "kafla," meaning a hood or cap. The modern spelling evolved over time.
