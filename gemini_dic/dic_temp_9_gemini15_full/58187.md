drowsiness: /ˈdraʊziːnəs/ | n. |困倦；嗜睡 | A feeling of sleepiness and a strong desire to sleep.  It is a state of being tired and heavy-lidded, often accompanied by a lack of energy and mental alertness. |  例句：The drowsiness induced by the medication made driving dangerous.  药物引起的困倦使驾驶变得危险。

例句：After a long day of work, I felt overwhelming drowsiness.  一天辛苦的工作后，我感到非常困倦。

近义词：sleepiness, lethargy, somnolence, slumber

反义词：wakefulness, alertness, vigilance

词性：名词
