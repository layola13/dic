guideways:/ˈɡaɪdweɪz/| n. |导轨；导向装置；轨道 |A guideway is a structure that guides the movement of vehicles or other objects.  It can be a physical track, such as a railway track, or a more abstract system that provides direction and control.  Guideways are used in a variety of applications, including transportation systems, industrial machinery, and robotics. |例句：The high-speed train runs on a dedicated guideway. 高速列车在专用导轨上运行。

The automated guided vehicle follows the guideway precisely. 自动导引车精确地沿着导轨行驶。|近义词：track, path, channel, runway |反义词：obstacle, impediment
