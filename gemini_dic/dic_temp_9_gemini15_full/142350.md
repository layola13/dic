orthography:/ɔːrˈθɒɡrəfi/| /ɔːrˈθɑːɡrəfi/ | n. |拼写法；正字法 |The study of spelling and the rules of spelling.  It also refers to a particular writing system.  |例句：English orthography is notoriously inconsistent.  英文拼写法出了名的不一致。

例句：The orthography of ancient Greek is quite different from modern Greek. 古希腊的拼写法与现代希腊语大相径庭。


近义词：spelling system, writing system

反义词：None.  (There isn't a direct opposite concept.)
