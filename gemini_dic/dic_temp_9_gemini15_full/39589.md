consecraters:/ˈkɒnsɪkreɪtərz/| n. |奉献者；祝圣者|Those who perform the act of consecration, dedicating something to a sacred purpose.  They might be religious figures performing a ritual, or individuals dedicating their lives to a cause.|The consecraters chanted ancient hymns as they blessed the new temple. 奉献者在为新庙宇祝福时吟唱着古老的圣歌。|近义词：dedicators, sanctifiers |反义词：desecrators, profanners
