phantasms:/ˈfæntæzəm/| n.|幻影；幽灵；幻想|A phantasm is a ghostly apparition; something unreal seeming real.  It often refers to something imagined or dreamed, a figment of the imagination.|例句：The flickering shadows created phantasms in the old house.  古老的房子里，闪烁的阴影创造了幻影。

例句：He dismissed the whole incident as mere phantasms of his overactive imagination. 他把整个事件都当成是他过度活跃的想象力产生的虚幻景象而忽略了。


近义词：apparition, specter, ghost, illusion, hallucination|反义词：reality, truth, fact|名词
