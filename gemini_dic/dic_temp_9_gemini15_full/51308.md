detailing:/diˈteɪlɪŋ/ | v. | 详细说明；细致描述 | To give a detailed account or description of something.  |  The report detailing the accident was very thorough. 这份详细描述事故的报告非常详尽。 The architect provided detailed drawings, detailing every aspect of the building's design. 建筑师提供了详细的图纸，详细说明了建筑设计的各个方面。 | describing, explaining, elaborating | summarizing, generalizing | 动词
