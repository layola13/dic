palliates:/ˈpælieɪts/ | vt. | 减轻；缓和；安慰 | To make something bad less severe or serious.  |  例句：The medication palliates the symptoms of the disease, but it doesn't cure it.  这种药能减轻疾病的症状，但不能治愈它。


例句：The nurse tried to palliate the patient's suffering. 护士试图减轻病人的痛苦。


近义词：alleviate, mitigate, ease, soothe | 反义词：aggravate, worsen, exacerbate | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The medicine palliates his pain. = 这药减轻了他的疼痛。
This policy palliates the economic inequality. = 这项政策缓和了经济不平等。


一般过去时:
The doctor palliated the patient's anxiety. = 医生减轻了病人的焦虑。
The government's measures palliated the social unrest. = 政府的措施缓和了社会动荡。


现在完成时:
This treatment has palliated her symptoms considerably. = 这种治疗已大大减轻了她的症状。
The new law has palliated the effects of the crisis. = 新法减轻了危机的后果。


过去完成时:
They had palliated the situation before the crisis worsened. = 在危机恶化之前，他们已经缓解了局势。
The previous administration had palliated the problem, but it remained unresolved. = 前一届政府曾缓解过这个问题，但它仍未解决。


将来时:
The new drug will palliate the side effects of chemotherapy. = 新药将减轻化疗的副作用。
The upcoming reforms will palliate the burden on small businesses. = 即将来临的改革将减轻小型企业的负担。


虚拟语气:
If they had palliated the problem earlier, the situation wouldn't be so bad. = 如果他们早点缓解这个问题，情况就不会这么糟糕了。
I wish the government would palliate the suffering of the refugees. = 我希望政府能减轻难民的苦难。


被动语态:
The pain was palliated by the medication. = 疼痛被药物减轻了。
The economic hardship was palliated by government assistance. = 经济困难得到了政府的援助而有所缓解。


疑问句:
Does this treatment palliate the symptoms effectively? = 这种治疗能有效缓解症状吗？
Will the new policy palliate the social divide? = 新政策能否缓和社会分歧？


否定句:
The treatment didn't palliate her condition at all. = 治疗根本没有减轻她的病情。
The measures failed to palliate the growing tensions. = 这些措施未能缓和日益增长的紧张局势。


PHRASE->
palliate the effects of = 减轻…的影响
palliate the symptoms of = 减轻…的症状
palliate the pain of = 减轻…的痛苦
palliate the suffering of = 减轻…的痛苦
palliate a problem = 缓解一个问题


词源:
From Latin *palliāre "to cloak, cover," from pallium "cloak."  The sense development is from covering something up to masking or lessening the severity of something unpleasant.
