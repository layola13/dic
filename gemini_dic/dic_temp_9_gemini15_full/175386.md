rallying: /ˈræliɪŋ/ | v. n. adj.|  集会；恢复；重整；团结；振奋 | The act of bringing people together for a common cause; the process of regaining strength or recovering from a setback; inspiring or encouraging. | 近义词：gathering, recovering, reviving, encouraging | 反义词：dispersing, collapsing, discouraging


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team is rallying around their injured captain. =  球队正在团结在他们受伤的队长周围。
She is rallying her supporters for the upcoming election. = 她正在为即将到来的选举团结她的支持者。


一般过去时:
The troops rallied after the initial setback. = 部队在最初的挫折后重新集结。
He rallied his strength and finished the race. = 他鼓起力量完成了比赛。


现在完成时:
The stock market has rallied significantly since the announcement. = 自公告发布以来，股市已大幅反弹。
They have rallied the community to support the local school. = 他们已经团结社区来支持当地学校。


过去完成时:
The army had rallied before the enemy launched a counterattack. = 在敌人发动反攻之前，军队已经集结完毕。
She had rallied her courage before facing her fears. = 在面对恐惧之前，她已经鼓起了勇气。


将来时:
We will rally together to overcome this challenge. = 我们将团结起来克服这个挑战。
The economy is expected to rally in the coming months. = 预计经济将在未来几个月复苏。


虚拟语气:
If we rallied our resources, we could achieve our goal. = 如果我们团结我们的资源，我们就能实现我们的目标。
I wish the team had rallied more effectively during the game. = 我希望球队在比赛中能更有效地团结起来。


被动语态:
The troops were rallied by their commander. = 部队由他们的指挥官集结。
The community was rallied to support the victims of the disaster. = 社区团结起来支持灾难的受害者。


疑问句:
Will the people rally behind the new leader? = 人们会支持新领导人吗？
Is the market rallying or falling? = 市场是在上涨还是在下跌？


否定句:
The team did not rally after their star player was injured. = 球队在他们的明星球员受伤后没有团结起来。
The economy failed to rally despite government intervention. = 尽管政府干预，经济仍未能复苏。


PHRASE->
rally around: 团结在...周围
rally to:  向...集结；支持
rally cry:  战斗口号
rally behind: 支持；拥护
a political rally:  政治集会
