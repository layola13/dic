phototelescope: /ˈfoʊtoʊˌtɛləskoʊp/ | n. |照相望远镜|A telescope that is used to take photographs of celestial objects.  It combines the functions of a telescope and a camera. |例句：The astronomer used a phototelescope to capture stunning images of the nebula. 天文学家使用照相望远镜拍摄了令人惊叹的星云图像。|近义词：astrograph, astronomical camera|反义词：None
