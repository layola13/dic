dysphoric:/dɪsˈfɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 不安的；烦躁的；苦闷的 | Feeling unwell or unhappy; characterized by feelings of unease, dissatisfaction, or disquiet.  It often refers to a state of unease or dissatisfaction, often associated with mood disorders.  | Example:  She felt dysphoric after the argument. 她争吵后感到不安。 He experienced periods of dysphoric mood. 他经历过一段时期情绪低落。 | 近义词：unhappy, miserable, depressed, anxious, uneasy | 反义词：euphoric, happy, content, joyful, satisfied
