ditziness:/ˈdɪtizɪnəs/ | n. |头晕眼花；迷糊；傻气 | A feeling of being unsteady or faint; lightheadedness; silliness or foolishness.  |例句：The heat caused a feeling of dizziness. 炎热导致头晕的感觉。

例句：Her dizziness prevented her from driving. 她的头晕让她无法开车。


近义词：lightheadedness, giddiness, faintness, wooziness | 反义词：clearheadedness, alertness, sharpness


词源: 源自于dizzy (头晕的)+ -ness (名词后缀)  The word "dizzy" itself has origins in Middle English *dizzy, from Old Norse *dísa ("to be foolish, giddy").
