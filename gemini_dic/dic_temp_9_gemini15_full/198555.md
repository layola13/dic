shrubby:/ˈʃrʌbi/| adj. |灌木丛生的；矮树丛的 |Having many shrubs; resembling a shrub or shrubs in appearance or growth.  The area is characterized by its shrubby vegetation. 这个地区以其灌木丛生的植被为特征。|近义词：bushy, scrubby |反义词：treeless, open
