embitterers: /ɪmˈbɪtərərz/ | n. | 使人痛苦的人或事 | Those who cause bitterness or resentment; things that make someone feel bitter. |例句：The constant criticism from her colleagues were embitterers in her life.  她的同事不断批评，是她生活中痛苦的根源。|近义词：tormentors, afflicters,  sources of unhappiness |反义词：comforters,  solaces, sources of joy |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The constant failures embitter him. = 持续的失败使他痛苦。
His actions embitter many people. = 他的行为使很多人痛苦。


一般过去时:
The loss of his job embittered him for years. = 失去工作使他痛苦了好几年。
The betrayal embittered her relationship with her friend. = 背叛使她和朋友的关系变得痛苦。


现在完成时:
Years of hardship have embittered her. = 多年的苦难使她痛苦不堪。
His disappointments have embittered his outlook on life. = 他的失望使他对生活感到痛苦。


过去完成时:
He had been embittered by previous experiences. = 他之前的经历使他痛苦不堪。
She had been embittered by a long history of injustice. = 长期以来遭受的不公正待遇使她痛苦不堪。


将来时:
Further setbacks will only embitter him more. = 进一步的挫折只会让他更加痛苦。
This decision will likely embitter many of his supporters. = 这个决定可能会让他的许多支持者感到痛苦。


虚拟语气:
If he had not experienced such hardships, he would not be so embittered. = 如果他没有经历那么多的苦难，他就不会这么痛苦了。
I wish I hadn’t done that; it has embittered our relationship. = 我真希望我没有那样做；那让我们的关系变得痛苦了。


被动语态:
He was embittered by the unfair treatment. = 他因为不公平的待遇而痛苦。
She felt embittered by the lack of recognition for her work. = 她因为工作没有得到认可而感到痛苦。


疑问句:
What embittered him so much? = 是什么让他如此痛苦？
What experiences have embittered her so deeply? = 什么经历让她如此痛苦？


否定句:
The success of his project didn't embitter him. = 他项目的成功并没有让他痛苦。
The positive feedback didn't embitter her.  积极的反馈并没有让她痛苦。
