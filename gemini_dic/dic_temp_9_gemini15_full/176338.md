re-erection: /ˌriːɪˈrekʃən/ | n. |重建，复兴，再次竖立 | The act of erecting something again; the process of rebuilding or restoring something to its former state.  It often implies a previous erection that has been destroyed or taken down.  |例句：The re-erection of the statue took several months.  重建雕像花了几个月的时间。

The re-erection of the bridge after the flood was a significant engineering feat.  洪水过后桥梁的重建是一项重大的工程壮举。


近义词：reconstruction, rebuilding, restoration | 反义词：destruction, demolition,teardown | 词性: noun
