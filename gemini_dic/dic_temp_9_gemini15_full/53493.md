The disassemblage of the sculpture was a fascinating process, revealing its hidden internal structure. 雕塑的分解是一个引人入胜的过程，揭示了其隐藏的内部结构。


近义词：dismantling, deconstruction, disassembly | 反义词：assembly, construction, synthesis |  词性: noun
