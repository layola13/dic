lifebelt:/ˈlaɪfbelt/| n. |救生圈，救生衣 |A lifebelt is a buoyant ring or jacket worn to keep a person afloat in water.  It's designed as a safety device to aid in survival during water emergencies. |Example: The captain ordered everyone to put on their lifebelts before the ship left the harbor. 船长下令在船离港前所有人穿上救生衣。


Example: He threw a lifebelt to the drowning man. 他把救生圈扔给了落水的人。|近义词：life preserver, life jacket |反义词：None
