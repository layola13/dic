preduplicate:/ˌpriːˈdjuːplɪkeɪt/ | v. |预先复制，预先重复 |To make a copy of (something) before the original is needed or used.  This usually implies preparation or anticipation of a future need. |例句：The system preduplicates critical data files to a remote server for disaster recovery.  系统将关键数据文件预先复制到远程服务器，以进行灾难恢复。


例句：The software preduplicates the database each night to ensure quick recovery in case of a failure. 该软件每晚都会预先复制数据库，以确保在发生故障时能够快速恢复。


近义词：copy beforehand, pre-copy, replicate in advance |反义词：delete, erase

词性：动词
