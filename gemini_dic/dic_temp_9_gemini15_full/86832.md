hematosin: /ˌhiːməˈtɒsɪn/ | n. |血红素| A complex iron-containing protein in red blood cells that carries oxygen from the lungs to the tissues and carbon dioxide from the tissues to the lungs.  | Example:  Hemoglobin is vital for transporting oxygen throughout the body. 血红蛋白对于输送全身氧气至关重要。  |近义词:  hemoglobin |反义词:  None  |名词
