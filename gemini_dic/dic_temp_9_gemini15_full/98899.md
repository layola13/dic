intensified:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪfaɪd/| v. | 加剧的；强化的 | Made more intense or stronger. | The conflict intensified after the assassination.  冲突在暗杀事件后加剧了。 The storm intensified as the night went on.  夜幕降临，暴风雨加剧了。| heighten, aggravate, exacerbate | weaken, diminish, alleviate | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sun intensifies the heat. = 太阳增强了热量。
He intensifies his training. = 他加紧训练。

一般过去时:
The argument intensified quickly. = 争论迅速升级。
She intensified her efforts to find a solution. = 她加大了寻找解决方案的努力。

现在完成时:
The drought has intensified in recent weeks. = 近几周干旱加剧了。
They have intensified their security measures. = 他们加强了安全措施。

过去完成时:
The tension had intensified before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，紧张气氛已经加剧了。
He had intensified his study before the exam. = 他在考试前加紧了学习。

将来时:
The situation will intensify if we don't act now. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，情况将会恶化。
They will intensify their marketing campaign next quarter. = 下个季度他们将加强营销活动。

虚拟语气:
If the pressure intensified further, the dam might break. = 如果压力进一步加大，大坝可能会决堤。
I wish the pain wouldn't intensify. = 我希望疼痛不要加剧。

被动语态:
Security measures were intensified after the incident. = 事件发生后，安全措施得到了加强。
The debate was intensified by the media. = 媒体加剧了这场辩论。

疑问句:
Did the storm intensify last night? = 暴风雨昨晚加剧了吗？
Will the conflict intensify further? = 冲突还会进一步加剧吗？

否定句:
The pain didn't intensify after taking the medicine. = 服药后疼痛并没有加剧。
The government didn't intensify its efforts to control inflation. = 政府没有加大力度控制通货膨胀。
