outwaters:/ˈaʊtwɔːtərz/| n. |外水；外海；远洋|The term "outwaters" refers to waters that are far from the shore or coast, often implying a sense of remoteness and the open sea.  It suggests a region beyond the familiar and easily accessible coastal areas.  The word evokes a sense of adventure, exploration, and perhaps even danger.|例句：The sailors ventured into the outwaters, facing unpredictable storms and vast distances. 水手们冒险驶入外海，面临着不可预测的风暴和遥远的距离。


The ship was lost in the outwaters, never to be seen again. 这艘船在茫茫大海中失事，再也没有出现过。|近义词：deep sea, open ocean, high seas|反义词：inshore waters, coastal waters, near waters|名词
