improvable:/ɪmˈpruːvəbl/ | adj. | 可改进的，可以改善的 | Capable of being improved or made better.  | The system is improvable, but it's a good start. 这个系统可以改进，但这已经是个好的开始了。 | betterable, amendable, perfectible | unimprovable, perfect  |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The design is improvable. = 这个设计可以改进。
The situation is improvable with more effort. = 只要多加努力，情况就能改善。


一般过去时:
The process was improvable, so we made some changes. = 这个流程是可以改进的，所以我们做了一些改变。
The product was improvable in several aspects. = 这个产品在几个方面都可以改进。


现在完成时:
The software has been improvable since the last update. = 自从上次更新以来，这个软件已经有了改进的空间。
The team has made the system improvable. = 团队已经使系统变得更易于改进。


过去完成时:
The plan had been improvable, but we ran out of time. = 计划本来是可以改进的，但是我们时间不够了。
The design had been improvable before the final review. = 在最终审核之前，设计是可以改进的。


将来时:
The system will be improvable after the next update. = 下次更新后，系统将更易于改进。
The performance will be improvable with better training. = 通过更好的培训，表现将会得到提升。


虚拟语气:
If the design were improvable, we would have changed it. = 如果设计可以改进，我们就会改变它。
I wish the situation were more improvable. = 我希望情况更容易改善。


被动语态:
The report is being made improvable. = 报告正在被修改得更容易改进。
The process is being made improvable by the team. = 团队正在使流程更易于改进。


疑问句:
Is the system improvable? = 这个系统可以改进吗？
Are there any parts of the design that are improvable? = 设计中有哪些部分是可以改进的？


否定句:
The product is not improvable in its current state. =  产品在其当前状态下无法改进。
The situation is unfortunately not improvable without further resources. = 不幸的是，如果没有更多资源，情况就无法改善。
