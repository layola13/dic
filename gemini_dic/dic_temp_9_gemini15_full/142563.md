ostentatious: /ɒsˈtɛnˈteɪʃəs/ | adj. | 炫耀的；夸示的；卖弄的 | Characterized by vulgar or excessive display; designed to impress or attract notice.  It suggests a deliberate and often showy attempt to attract attention, often implying a lack of taste or genuine substance. |例句：Her ostentatious jewelry was the talk of the party.  她的炫耀性珠宝是派对上人们议论的焦点。

例句：The ostentatious mansion was a clear sign of his newfound wealth.  那座炫耀的豪宅清楚地表明了他新近获得的财富。


近义词：showy, flamboyant, pretentious, conspicuous, extravagant

反义词：modest, humble, understated, simple, unassuming
