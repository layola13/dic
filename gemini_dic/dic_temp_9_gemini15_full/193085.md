sectionalism:/ˈsɛkʃənəlɪzəm/| n. |地方主义；本位主义；局部主义 |The excessive regard for regional interests; the belief that the interests of one's own region are superior to those of the nation as a whole.  It often involves political conflicts between different sections of a country. |The Civil War was largely a result of growing sectionalism between the North and the South.  南北战争很大程度上是南北之间地方主义加剧的结果。|nationalism, regionalism |national unity, internationalism |名词
