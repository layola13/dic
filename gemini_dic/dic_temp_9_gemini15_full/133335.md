nonextant: /ˈnɒnɪkˈstænt/ | adj. | 不存在的；已灭绝的 |  Not existing or surviving; extinct.  The word typically refers to things that once existed but no longer do,  often species or languages. |例句：The dodo is a nonextant bird.  渡渡鸟是一种已灭绝的鸟类。

The woolly mammoth is a nonextant species.  猛犸象是一种已灭绝的物种。

近义词：extinct, nonexistent, defunct, vanished

反义词： extant, existing, surviving
