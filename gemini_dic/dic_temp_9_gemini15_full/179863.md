reexportation:/ˌriːˌɛkspɔːrˈteɪʃən/| n. |再出口，转口贸易 |The act of re-exporting goods; the goods that are re-exported.  It refers to the process of importing goods into a country and then exporting them to another country, often with minimal processing or modification.  |Example: The reexportation of textiles from China to other Asian countries is a significant part of the global trade.  再出口纺织品从中国到其他亚洲国家是全球贸易的重要组成部分。  The company specialized in the reexportation of agricultural products. 这家公司专门从事农产品的再出口业务。|近义词：re-export, transshipment |反义词：importation, import
