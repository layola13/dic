cutbacks:/ˈkʌtˌbæks/| n.|削减，紧缩|Reductions in spending, staff, or production, often as a way to save money or improve efficiency.|The company announced significant cutbacks in its marketing budget.  这家公司宣布大幅削减其营销预算。|The recent economic downturn led to widespread cutbacks in government spending. 最近的经济衰退导致政府支出大幅削减。|increases, expansions|no antonym provided.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Cutbacks are necessary to improve profitability. = 削减成本是提高盈利能力的必要措施。
The company makes regular cutbacks in its operational expenses. = 公司定期削减运营费用。


一般过去时:
They made substantial cutbacks last year. = 他们去年进行了大幅度的削减。
The government implemented harsh cutbacks during the crisis. = 政府在危机期间实施了严厉的削减措施。


现在完成时:
The company has implemented cutbacks in several departments. = 公司已经在几个部门实施了削减措施。
We have made significant cutbacks to our budget this year. = 我们今年对预算进行了大幅削减。


过去完成时:
They had already made cutbacks before the recession hit. = 经济衰退来临之前，他们就已经进行了削减。
The company had implemented cutbacks in staffing prior to the merger. = 在合并之前，公司已经对员工进行了裁减。


将来时:
We will make further cutbacks if necessary. = 如果需要，我们将进一步削减。
The government will announce new cutbacks next week. = 政府将于下周宣布新的削减措施。


虚拟语气:
If they hadn't made cutbacks, the company would have gone bankrupt. = 如果他们没有进行削减，公司早就破产了。
It would be better if they made fewer cutbacks. = 如果他们少进行一些削减就好了。


被动语态:
Cutbacks were announced across the board. = 削减措施已全面宣布。
Significant cutbacks are being made to the healthcare system. = 医疗系统正在进行大规模的削减。


疑问句:
Are there any planned cutbacks in the near future? = 近期是否有计划进行削减？
What kind of cutbacks are they considering? = 他们正在考虑什么样的削减措施？


否定句:
There were no cutbacks in personnel this year. = 今年没有裁员。
The company didn't make any significant cutbacks last quarter. = 公司上个季度没有进行任何大幅度的削减。
