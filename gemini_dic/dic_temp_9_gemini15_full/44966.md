crystallizes: /ˈkrɪstəlaɪz/ | v. | 使结晶；使明确；具体化 | To form crystals; to become clear or definite; to take on a definite form. | 例句：The solution crystallized as it cooled.  溶液冷却后结晶了。 | 例句：Her ideas crystallized into a concrete plan. 她的想法具体化为一个具体的计划。 | 近义词：solidify, clarify, define | 反义词：dissolve, confuse, obscure
