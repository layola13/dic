liberals:/ˈlɪbərəlz/ | n. adj. |自由主义者；自由主义的 |  A person with liberal views.  Those who support social reforms and individual rights, often emphasizing tolerance and individual freedoms.  |例句：Many liberals advocate for social justice. 许多自由主义者倡导社会正义。

例句：The liberal party supports environmental protection. 自由党支持环保。


近义词：progressives, radicals (in some contexts) |反义词：conservatives, reactionaries


词性: 名词和形容词
