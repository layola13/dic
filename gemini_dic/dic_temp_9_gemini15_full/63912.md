epiphragm: /ˈɛpɪfræɡm/ | n. |隔膜；隔板；（软体动物壳口的）隔膜| A thin membrane or partition, especially one separating cavities or structures in an organism.  In some contexts, it specifically refers to the calcareous plate closing the aperture of a mollusk's shell. |例句：The epiphragm sealed the snail's shell against the harsh winter.  隔膜将蜗牛壳封闭起来，以抵御严酷的冬季。

The epiphragm in the lung helps separate the heart and lungs from the abdominal cavity. 肺部的隔膜有助于将心脏和肺与腹腔隔开。|近义词：membrane, septum, diaphragm |反义词：opening, aperture (in the context of a mollusk shell)
