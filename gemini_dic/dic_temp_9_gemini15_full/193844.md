self-diffidence: /ˌsɛlfˈdɪfɪdəns/ | n. | 自卑，缺乏自信 | Lack of self-confidence; a lack of faith in one's own abilities or qualities.  It often manifests as shyness, hesitancy, and a tendency to undervalue oneself. | Example: Her self-diffidence prevented her from applying for the promotion, even though she was highly qualified.  她的自卑感阻止了她申请晋升，即使她非常合格。


Example: His self-diffidence was evident in his hesitant speech and downcast eyes. 他的自卑从他犹豫的言语和低垂的眼睛中显而易见。


近义词: self-doubt, insecurity, shyness, timidity, lack of confidence


反义词: self-confidence, self-assurance, boldness, assertiveness


词性: noun
