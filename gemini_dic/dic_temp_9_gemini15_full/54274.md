disguisable: /dɪsˈɡaɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 可伪装的，可以掩饰的 | Capable of being disguised or concealed.  Something that is disguisable can be hidden or made to appear different from its true nature. |


例句：

The spy's skills made him highly disguisable.  间谍的技能使他非常容易伪装。

His nervousness was barely disguisable. 他的紧张几乎无法掩饰。


近义词：concealable, maskable, hidden


反义词：unconcealable, obvious, apparent


词性：形容词
