disavowment: /dɪsˈeɪvəʊmənt/ | n. | 否认；拒绝承认；放弃 | The act of disavowing or denying; repudiation.  It implies a previous acknowledgement or claim that is now being rejected.  This can apply to actions, beliefs, or relationships. |


例句：

The company issued a statement of disavowment, denying any responsibility for the environmental damage.  公司发表声明否认对环境破坏负有任何责任。

His disavowment of his past actions shocked his family and friends. 他否认自己过去的所作所为，震惊了他的家人和朋友。


近义词：repudiation, denial, renunciation, rejection


反义词：acknowledgment, acceptance, confirmation, avowal


词性: 名词
