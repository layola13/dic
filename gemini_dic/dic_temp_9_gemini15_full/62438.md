endopleura: /ˌɛndoʊˈplʊərə/ | n. |内果皮|The innermost layer of the pericarp of a fruit, surrounding the seeds.  It is often membranous or papery in texture. | Example: The endopleura of the orange is thin and white, easily separated from the juicy segments.  橙子的内果皮很薄很白，很容易与多汁的果瓣分开。|近义词: inner pericarp layer, seed coat (partially, as it is distinct)|反义词:exocarp, mesocarp|名词
