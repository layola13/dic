preexpedition: /ˌpriːˌɛkspəˈdɪʃən/ | adj. |远征前的，探险前的 | Relating to the time or activities before an expedition.  Describes something that happens or is prepared before a journey or exploration, especially a large-scale one. |例句：Preexpedition preparations included meticulous planning and extensive equipment checks.  远征前的准备工作包括细致的规划和广泛的设备检查。


例句：The preexpedition briefing covered potential hazards and survival techniques. 远征前的简报涵盖了潜在的危险和生存技巧。


近义词：antecedent, preliminary, prior, preparatory


反义词：postexpedition, post-expeditionary
