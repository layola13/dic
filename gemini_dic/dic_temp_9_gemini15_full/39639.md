consequently:/ˈkɒnsɪkwentli/ /ˈkɑːnsɪkwentli/| adv. |结果，因此，所以 |As a consequence or result.  It indicates a cause-and-effect relationship between two events or ideas.  |例句：It rained heavily; consequently, the game was postponed.  雨下得很大；因此，比赛被推迟了。
He failed to study; consequently, he failed the exam. 他没有学习；结果考试不及格。|近义词：therefore, thus, hence, accordingly, as a result |反义词：despite, notwithstanding, nevertheless, however
