saccharin:/ˈsækərɪn/| n. |糖精；甜味剂 |A synthetic, intensely sweet substance used as a sugar substitute. It is about 300-700 times sweeter than sucrose (table sugar) but has a slightly bitter aftertaste. | The diet soda was sweetened with saccharin. 这款代糖汽水是用糖精甜化的。  | sweetener, artificial sweetener | sugar, natural sweetener |名词
