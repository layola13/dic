lacustral:/ləˈkʌstrəl/| adj.|湖泊的；湖成的；生长在湖泊中的|Relating to or formed in lakes.  The term often describes geological formations, sediments, or organisms associated with lakes.|例句：The lacustral deposits revealed evidence of past lake levels.  湖泊沉积物揭示了过去湖泊水位的证据。
例句：Many lacustral species are adapted to survive in low-oxygen environments. 许多湖泊物种都适应在低氧环境中生存。|近义词：lake-formed, limnetic |反义词：marine, terrestrial
