heat-seeking: /ˈhiːtˌsiːkɪŋ/ | adj. |寻热的，热寻的 |  capable of detecting and following sources of heat.  Heat-seeking missiles, for example, are guided by the heat signature of their target. |例句：The heat-seeking missile locked onto the enemy jet.  这枚热寻导弹锁定了敌机。

例句：The snake used its heat-seeking senses to locate its prey.  蛇利用其热感应能力寻找猎物。


近义词：heat-tracking, infrared-guided


反义词：cold-seeking (though rarely used)


词性：形容词
