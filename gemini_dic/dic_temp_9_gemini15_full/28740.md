carnassial:/kɑːˈnæʒəl/| adj. |裂齿的；食肉的| Relating to or denoting the carnassial teeth.  These are the shearing teeth located towards the back of the jaw in carnivores, used for cutting flesh. |例句：The lion's carnassial teeth were clearly visible as it tore into its prey.  狮子撕咬猎物时，它的裂齿清晰可见。

The carnassial teeth are crucial for the efficient consumption of meat. 裂齿对于高效食用肉类至关重要。|近义词：predatory, carnivorous |反义词：herbivorous, vegetarian
