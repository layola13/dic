redefinition:/ˌriːˌdɛfɪˈnɪʃən/| n. |重新定义；再定义 |The act of defining something again, often with a changed or updated meaning.  It involves reconsidering and altering the established understanding or interpretation of a concept, term, or object. |例句：The redefinition of marriage to include same-sex couples was a landmark legal decision.  重新定义婚姻以包含同性伴侣是一项具有里程碑意义的法律决定。

例句：The company's redefinition of its brand image led to increased sales. 公司对其品牌形象的重新定义导致销售额增长。


近义词：reinterpretation, re-evaluation, reformulation |反义词：original definition,  first definition |词性：名词
