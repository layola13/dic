outswell:/ˈaʊtswel/| v.|外涌，涌出；膨胀；突出 |To rise and spread outward; to swell outward; to project outward.  |例句：The crowd outsweled from the stadium after the game.  比赛结束后，人群从体育场涌出。

The waves outsweled onto the beach. 海浪涌向海滩。


近义词：overflow, surge, swell, expand |反义词：recede, contract, shrink


USAGE->

The river outswells its banks during the rainy season. 雨季时，河水漫过河岸。
The balloon outswells as more air is pumped in.  往气球里充气越多，它就膨胀得越大。
