nondemobilization: /ˌnɒnˌdiːməʊbɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 非复员；未复员 | The state or condition of not being demobilized; the continued state of mobilization.  It refers to the absence of the process of disbanding or returning soldiers or military equipment from active service. | Example: The prolonged nondemobilization of troops led to economic strain on the country.  长期未复员的军队给国家带来了经济压力。 | 近义词: continued mobilization, sustained mobilization | 反义词: demobilization
