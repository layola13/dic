mbti:/ˈɛm biː ˈtaɪ/ | n. |迈尔斯-布里格斯类型指标|Myers-Briggs Type Indicator, a psychometric questionnaire designed to measure psychological preferences in how people perceive the world and make decisions.|例句：He took the MBTI test and discovered he was an INTJ. 他做了MBTI测试，发现自己是INTJ型。|近义词：personality test, psychological assessment|反义词：无


USAGE->

My MBTI type is ENFP. = 我的MBTI类型是ENFP。
She's studying MBTI theory. = 她正在学习MBTI理论。


Interrogative sentence:
What's your MBTI type? = 你的MBTI类型是什么？
Have you ever taken the MBTI test? = 你做过MBTI测试吗？


Exclamatory sentence:
I finally understand my MBTI results! = 我终于理解我的MBTI结果了！
MBTI is so fascinating! = MBTI真迷人！
