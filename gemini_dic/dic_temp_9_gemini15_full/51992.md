diagnose:/ˈdaɪəɡnoʊz/| vt. |诊断，判断|To identify a disease or problem by examining its symptoms.|The doctor diagnosed her with pneumonia.  医生诊断她患有肺炎。|identify, determine, ascertain|treat, cure|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor diagnoses the patient's illness. = 医生诊断病人的病情。
She diagnoses problems quickly and efficiently. = 她快速有效地诊断问题。


一般过去时:
The doctor diagnosed the patient with the flu. = 医生诊断病人患有流感。
They diagnosed the engine problem as a faulty fuel pump. = 他们诊断出发动机问题是燃油泵故障。


现在完成时:
The doctor has diagnosed several cases of the same illness this week. = 本周医生已经诊断出几例相同的疾病。
Scientists have diagnosed the environmental problem as pollution. = 科学家已将环境问题诊断为污染。


过去完成时:
The doctors had diagnosed the disease before the symptoms became severe. = 医生在症状恶化之前就诊断出了这种疾病。
By the time the patient arrived, they had already diagnosed the issue. = 当病人到达时，他们已经诊断出了问题。


将来时:
The doctor will diagnose the patient tomorrow. = 医生明天将诊断病人。
They will diagnose the cause of the malfunction after further testing. = 他们将在进一步测试后诊断故障原因。


虚拟语气:
If I were a doctor, I could diagnose the disease. = 如果我是一名医生，我就能诊断出这种疾病。
If the symptoms were different, the diagnosis would be different. = 如果症状不同，诊断结果也会不同。


被动语态:
The illness was diagnosed as a rare form of cancer. = 这种疾病被诊断为一种罕见的癌症。
The problem was quickly diagnosed and repaired. = 问题很快就被诊断出来并修复了。


疑问句:
Can you diagnose the problem with my computer? = 你能诊断出我的电脑问题吗？
What did the doctor diagnose the patient with? = 医生诊断病人患了什么病？


否定句:
The doctor couldn't diagnose the patient's illness. = 医生无法诊断出病人的病情。
They failed to diagnose the underlying problem. = 他们未能诊断出根本问题。


PHRASE->
diagnose a problem = 诊断一个问题
diagnose a disease = 诊断一种疾病
diagnose a situation = 判断一种情况
receive a diagnosis = 得到诊断结果
accurate diagnosis = 准确的诊断
