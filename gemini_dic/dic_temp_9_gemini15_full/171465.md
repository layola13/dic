puffily:/ˈpʌfɪli/ | adv. | 蓬松地；喘不过气地；自负地 | In a puffy manner; as if puffed up.  Describes something that is swollen, inflated, or pompous. | 例句：The clouds hung puffily in the sky.  云朵蓬松地悬挂在空中。


The dog, puffily panting, lay down in the shade.  小狗喘着粗气，蓬松地躺在了阴凉处。| 近义词：bloatedly, swollenly, pompously | 反义词：flatly, deflatedly
