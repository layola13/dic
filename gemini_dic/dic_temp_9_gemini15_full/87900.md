hierarchization:/ˌhaɪəˈrɑːrkɪzeɪʃən/ | n. |等级制度化；等级划分；层级结构的形成 | The process of arranging things or people into a hierarchy; the establishment of a hierarchy.  This involves creating a system of ranking or ordering, typically based on authority, power, or importance. | Example: The hierarchization of society led to significant social inequalities. 社会的等级制度化导致了严重的社会不平等。 |近义词：stratification, ranking, ordering, classification |反义词：egalitarianism, democratization
