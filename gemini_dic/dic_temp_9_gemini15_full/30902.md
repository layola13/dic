chagrinned:/ʃæˈɡrɪnd/| adj. |懊恼的，苦恼的 |Feeling great disappointment or humiliation.  |例句：He was chagrinned by his poor performance in the exam. 他对考试中的糟糕表现感到懊恼。

例句：She was chagrinned to discover her mistake. 她发现自己的错误后感到懊恼。


近义词：disappointed, humiliated, mortified, embarrassed, ashamed |反义词：pleased, delighted, satisfied, proud


词源:  From French *chagriner* "to vex, grieve," from Old French *chagrin*, from Vulgar Latin *gragnum*, related to Latin *gravis* "heavy, serious."
