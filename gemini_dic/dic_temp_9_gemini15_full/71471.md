flamethrowers:/ˈfleɪmˌθroʊərz/| n. |喷火器|A flamethrower is a weapon that shoots out a long stream of burning fuel.  It's used to burn things, often in warfare or for controlled burns.|例句：The soldiers used flamethrowers to clear out the enemy bunker.士兵们用喷火器清理敌人的掩体。

The firefighters used a flamethrower to create a firebreak. 消防员用喷火器制造防火隔离带。|近义词：incendiary device, flame projector|反义词：fire extinguisher
