hermes:/ˈhɜːrmiːz/ /ˈhɜːrmiz/ | n. |赫尔墨斯；爱马仕（希腊神话中的信使神）；爱马仕品牌 | The name Hermes derives from the Greek god Hermes, the messenger of the gods, known for his swiftness, cunning, and eloquence.  It is also the name of a luxury French fashion house. |例句：Hermes is known for its high-quality leather goods. 爱马仕以其高品质的皮具而闻名。  The statue depicted Hermes delivering a message.  雕像描绘了赫尔墨斯传递信息。|近义词：Mercury (Roman equivalent)|反义词：无
