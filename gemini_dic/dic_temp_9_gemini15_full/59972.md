edifying:/ˈɛdəfaɪɪŋ/| adj.| 有益的；启迪的；教化的 | Instructive or informative, especially in a way that improves moral character.  |例句：The lecture was edifying, offering valuable insights into ancient history.  讲座很有启发性，提供了关于古代历史的宝贵见解。

例句：Reading classic literature can be a highly edifying experience. 阅读古典文学是一种非常有益的经历。


近义词：instructive, enlightening, uplifting, enlightening, informative, beneficial |反义词：baneful, demoralizing, corrupting, harmful


词源：源自于动词edify，它来自中古英语 edifién，最终源于拉丁语 aedificāre，意思是“to build up, construct, establish”。  因此，“edifying” 本身就包含了“建设性地构建思想和道德”的含义。
