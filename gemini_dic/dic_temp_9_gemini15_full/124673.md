moved-up:/muːvd ʌp/| adj.| 提前的；晋升的；改进的 |  Describes something that has been advanced or improved in position, rank, or time.  It often implies a positive change or advancement. |例句：The meeting has been moved up to 9 am.  会议提前到了上午9点。

The company moved him up to a senior position. 公司把他提升到了一个高级职位。

|近义词：advanced, promoted, upgraded, improved |反义词：delayed, postponed, demoted, downgraded |词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is moved up to the top of the priority list. 项目被提升到优先级列表的顶部。
The meeting is moved up to an earlier time. 会议被提前到更早的时间。


一般过去时:
The deadline was moved up by a week. 截止日期提前了一周。
They moved the presentation up to accommodate the guest speaker. 他们提前了演示的时间以适应客座演讲者的安排。


现在完成时:
The launch date has been moved up several times. 发射日期已经提前了好几次。
The schedule has been moved up to meet the client's demands. 时间表已经被提前以满足客户的需求。


过去完成时:
The project had been moved up before the new manager arrived. 新经理到来之前，项目已经被提前了。
The meeting had been moved up because of the impending storm. 由于即将到来的暴风雨，会议已经被提前了。


将来时:
The concert will be moved up to avoid the expected rain. 音乐会将提前举行，以避免预期的降雨。
The exam will be moved up to next week. 考试将提前到下周。


虚拟语气:
If the funding were secured, the project would be moved up. 如果资金到位，项目将被提前。
I wish the deadline had been moved up earlier. 我希望截止日期能更早地提前。


被动语态:
The date was moved up by the organizers. 日期由组织者提前。
The project is being moved up in the schedule. 项目正在被提前安排。


疑问句:
Has the deadline been moved up? 截止日期是否提前了？
Will the meeting be moved up? 会议会提前吗？


否定句:
The deadline was not moved up. 截止日期没有提前。
The project will not be moved up. 项目不会提前。
