imperturbed:/ˌɪmpəˈtɜːbd/ | adj. | 泰然自若的；沉着的；冷静的 |  Not disturbed or upset; calm and tranquil.  The word implies a resilience to disturbance, a state of composure even in the face of adversity or challenging circumstances. | 例句：Despite the chaos around him, he remained imperturbable.  尽管周围一片混乱，他依然保持着泰然自若。


He remained imperturbable even when faced with the prospect of losing his job.即使面临失业的风险，他仍然保持着镇定。| 近义词：calm, composed, serene, unruffled, tranquil | 反义词：agitated, disturbed, flustered, perturbed, upset
