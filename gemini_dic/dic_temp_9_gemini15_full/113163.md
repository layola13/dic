lupuline:/ˈluːpjuːlaɪn/| n. |啤酒花腺体分泌物，蛇麻素|A yellow, granular powder secreted by the lupulin glands of the hop plant (Humulus lupulus), which contains the bitter acids and aromatic oils responsible for the flavor and aroma of beer.|The lupuline glands give beer its characteristic bitterness and aroma.|近义词：hop resin|反义词：无
