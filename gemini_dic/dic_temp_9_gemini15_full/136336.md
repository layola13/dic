nonresidence:/ˌnɑːnˈrezɪdəns/| n. |非居住，不住，不在籍 |The state or fact of not residing in a particular place; absence of residence.  It can also refer to a lack of permanent dwelling or a temporary absence from one's usual residence. |例句：His nonresidence in the state prevented him from voting in the election. = 他不在该州居住，因此无法在选举中投票。

His nonresidence at the address listed on his application caused a delay in processing. = 他未居住在其申请表上列出的地址，导致处理延误。|近义词：absence, non-occupancy |反义词：residence, occupancy
