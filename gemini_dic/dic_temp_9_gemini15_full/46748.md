dandy:/ˈdændi/| n. adj.|花花公子；漂亮的；极好的|A dandy is a man who pays excessive attention to his clothes and appearance.  A dandy can also describe something excellent or splendid. |例句：He was a dandy, always impeccably dressed. 他是个花花公子，总是穿着考究。|例句：That's a dandy idea!  那是个绝妙的主意！|近义词：fop, beau, swell, splendid, excellent|反义词：sloven, drab, mediocre
