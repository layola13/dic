neglective: /nɪˈɡlektɪv/ | adj. | 疏忽的；忽略的；玩忽职守的 | Neglectful; showing or characterized by neglect.  |  例句：His neglective attitude towards his studies led to poor grades. 他对学习的疏忽态度导致了差的成绩。

例句：The neglective parenting style contributed to the child's behavioral problems. 忽视的养育方式导致了孩子的行为问题。

近义词：negligent, careless, remiss, inattentive

反义词：attentive, careful, diligent, conscientious
