Multicultures are characterized by the presence of multiple ethnic, racial, and religious groups. |例句：The city boasts a vibrant multicultures, with people from all over the world living and working together.  这个城市拥有充满活力的多元文化，来自世界各地的人们在这里生活和工作。

例句：Our school celebrates multicultures through various events and programs.  我们的学校通过各种活动和项目来庆祝多元文化。
