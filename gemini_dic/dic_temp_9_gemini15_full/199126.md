silenter:/ˈsaɪləntər/| n.|消音器，减音器|A silencer is a device fitted to a firearm or other noisy equipment to reduce the noise it makes.|例句：The silencer reduced the gunshot's loudness significantly. 消音器大大降低了枪声的响度。|近义词：muffler, suppressor|反义词：amplifier
