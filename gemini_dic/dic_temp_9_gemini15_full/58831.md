duping:/ˈduːpɪŋ/| n. |欺骗；愚弄|The act of deceiving or tricking someone.  It often involves making someone believe something false. |例句：The duping of investors led to significant financial losses. 欺骗投资者导致了巨大的经济损失。

例句：His elaborate scheme involved the duping of several wealthy individuals. 他的精心策划的计划涉及到欺骗几位富人。


近义词：deception, fraud, trickery, swindle

反义词：honesty, truthfulness


词性: 名词
