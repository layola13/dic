plaintively:/ˈpleɪntɪvli/ | adv. | 哀怨地；伤心地；凄楚地 | In a sad and mournful way, expressing sorrow or melancholy.  |例句：She sang a plaintive song about lost love. 她唱了一首关于失恋的哀怨歌曲。

例句：The child cried plaintively for its mother.  孩子伤心地哭着喊妈妈。


近义词：mournfully, sadly, sorrowfully, wistfully

反义词：joyfully, happily, cheerfully

词性：副词
