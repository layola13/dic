pro-Haitian: /ˌproʊ heɪˈʃeɪn/ | adj. | 亲海地的；支持海地的 |  Supporting or favorable to Haiti and its people.  The term often implies advocacy for Haitian rights, interests, or culture.  | 例句：The pro-Haitian organization raised funds for earthquake relief.  亲海地组织为地震救灾筹集资金。  |近义词：Haitian-supporting, pro-Haiti | 反义词：anti-Haitian
