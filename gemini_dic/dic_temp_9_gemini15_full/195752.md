semitraditionally:/ˌsemi træˈdɪʃənəli/| adv.| 半传统地，部分传统地 |  Partially or to some extent traditionally.  It indicates a practice, belief, or method that is partly based on tradition but also incorporates modern or other influences. |例句：The company maintains a semitraditionally managed production line, using some modern techniques alongside older methods.  这家公司维持着一条半传统管理的生产线，同时使用一些现代技术和旧方法。


例句：They approach the problem semitraditionally, combining ancient wisdom with modern scientific analysis.他们半传统地处理这个问题，将古代智慧与现代科学分析相结合。


近义词：partly traditionally, somewhat traditionally, conventionally (to a degree)


反义词：untraditionally, unconventionally, radically, modernly


词性:副词
