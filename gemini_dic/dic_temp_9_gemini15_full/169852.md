proselytizes:/ˈprɒsəlaɪtaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 传教；劝诱改宗 | To attempt to convert someone to a different religion or belief. | The missionaries proselytized in the villages, trying to convert the locals to Christianity.  传教士在村庄里传教，试图将当地人改信基督教。| evangelize, convert,  missionarize |  remain unconverted, oppose conversion | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He proselytizes on street corners every Sunday. 他每个星期天都在街角传教。
She actively proselytizes for her political beliefs. 她积极地为自己的政治信仰进行宣传。


一般过去时:
The cult leader proselytized to his followers relentlessly. 邪教领袖对他的追随者们不遗余力地进行传教。
They proselytized among the students on campus. 他们在校园里向学生传教。


现在完成时:
He has proselytized for decades, spreading his message far and wide. 他几十年来一直在传教，把他的信息传播得很广。
She has proselytized to many people, changing some lives. 她已经向许多人传教，改变了一些人的生活。


过去完成时:
Before he became a priest, he had proselytized in several countries. 在他成为牧师之前，他曾在几个国家传教。
They had proselytized for years before they finally established a church. 他们在建立教堂之前已经传教多年了。


将来时:
He will proselytize at the upcoming festival. 他将在即将到来的节日里传教。
She will continue to proselytize even in the face of opposition. 即使面对反对，她也会继续传教。


虚拟语气:
If he proselytized less aggressively, he might have more converts. 如果他不那么咄咄逼人地传教，他可能会有更多皈依者。
I wish he wouldn't proselytize to me every time I see him. 我希望他每次见到我时都不要向我传教。


被动语态:
The new religion is being proselytized aggressively in many parts of the world.  许多地方都在积极地宣传这种新宗教。
His beliefs were proselytized to him by his parents.  他的信仰是由他的父母灌输给他的。


疑问句:
Does he proselytize to strangers? 他是否向陌生人传教？
Why do they proselytize so openly? 为什么他们如此公开地传教？


否定句:
He does not proselytize; he simply shares his faith. 他不传教；他只是分享他的信仰。
They did not proselytize to anyone who wasn't interested. 他们没有向任何不感兴趣的人传教。
