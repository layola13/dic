scrummaging:/ˈskrʌmɪdʒɪŋ/| v.|乱抓；拼抢；翻找|The act of searching frantically or roughly for something;  In rugby, the act of competing for possession of the ball at a scrum.|例句：The players were scrummaging fiercely for the ball. 球员们为了争夺球而激烈地拼抢。 He was scrummaging around in the drawer for his keys. 他在抽屉里乱翻找钥匙。|近义词：rummaging, foraging, scrabbling|反义词：organizing, tidying
