handin:/ˈhændɪn/| vt.|提交，交上|To give something to a person in authority, such as a teacher or employer.|例句：Please hand in your assignments by Friday.  请在星期五之前提交你的作业。|例句：He handed in his resignation yesterday. 他昨天提交了他的辞职信。|近义词：submit, deliver, turn in|反义词：receive, take back


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hands in his homework every day. = 他每天都交作业。
She hands in her report to the manager. = 她把报告交给经理。

一般过去时:
I handed in my application last week. = 我上周提交了我的申请。
They handed in their projects on time. = 他们按时提交了他们的项目。

现在完成时:
She has handed in all her assignments. = 她已经提交了所有的作业。
He has handed in his resignation letter. = 他已经提交了他的辞职信。

过去完成时:
I had handed in my paper before the deadline. = 我在截止日期前已经提交了我的论文。
They had handed in their forms before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他们已经提交了表格。


将来时:
I will hand in my essay tomorrow. = 我明天将提交我的论文。
She will hand in the report next week. = 她下周将提交报告。

虚拟语气:
If I had handed in my work earlier, I wouldn't be in trouble. = 如果我早点交作业，就不会有麻烦了。
I wish I had handed in my application sooner. = 我真希望我早点提交申请。

被动语态:
The assignments were handed in on time. = 作业按时提交了。
The report has been handed in to the committee. = 报告已提交给委员会。

疑问句:
Have you handed in your form yet? = 你提交你的表格了吗？
When will the project be handed in? = 项目什么时候提交？

否定句:
I haven't handed in my homework yet. = 我还没交作业。
He didn't hand in his report on time. = 他没有按时提交他的报告。
