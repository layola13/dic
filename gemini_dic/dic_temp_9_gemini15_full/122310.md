mittent:/ˈmɪtənt/| adj.|间歇的，断断续续的|Occurring or appearing at intervals; not continuous.  Characterized by periods of activity or inactivity that alternate.  |例句：The intermittent rain made driving difficult.  间歇性的雨使得驾驶变得困难。
例句：He suffered from intermittent headaches. 他间歇性地头痛。|近义词：periodic, sporadic, occasional, fitful|反义词：continuous, constant, incessant

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The light is intermittent. = 灯光忽明忽暗。
The pain is intermittent. = 疼痛时有时无。

一般过去时:
The rain was intermittent throughout the day. = 雨水一整天断断续续。
The signal was intermittent, causing disruptions. = 信号时断时续，导致中断。


现在完成时:
The engine has been intermittent since the last service. = 自上次保养以来，发动机一直间歇性工作。
There have been intermittent power outages this week. = 本周断断续续地停电。


过去完成时:
The heating had been intermittent all winter. = 整个冬天供暖一直断断续续。
The traffic had been intermittent, causing delays. = 交通时断时续，导致延误。


将来时:
The project will be intermittent, with work spread over several months. = 项目将是间歇性的，工作将分散在几个月内进行。
The weather forecast predicts intermittent showers. = 天气预报预测会有间歇性阵雨。


虚拟语气:
If the supply were intermittent, we'd have problems. = 如果供应间歇性，我们将会有问题。
I wish the internet connection weren't so intermittent. = 我希望网络连接不要这么间歇性。


被动语态:
The power supply was intermittently interrupted. = 供电被间歇性中断。
The project is being completed intermittently due to funding issues. = 由于资金问题，项目正在间歇性地完成。


疑问句:
Is the pain intermittent or constant? = 疼痛是间歇性的还是持续性的？
Are the symptoms intermittent? = 症状是间歇性的吗？


否定句:
The machine wasn't running intermittently; it was completely stopped. = 机器并非间歇性运行；它是完全停止的。
The traffic flow wasn't intermittent; it was consistently heavy. = 车流并非间歇性；它一直很拥堵。


PHRASE->
intermittent fever: 间歇性发烧
intermittent reinforcement: 间歇性强化
intermittent fasting: 间歇性禁食
intermittent hypoxia: 间歇性缺氧
intermittent spraying: 间歇性喷洒


词源:
From Latin *mittēns, present participle of mittere "to send," hence "letting go," "releasing," thus suggesting a pattern of starting and stopping.  The prefix "inter-" means "between" or "among,"  indicating the breaks or intervals in the action.
