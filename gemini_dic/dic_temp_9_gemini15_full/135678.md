nonprepositionally: /ˌnɒnˌpreˌpɒzɪˈʃənəli/ | adv. | 非介词地 |  Without a preposition.  This adverb describes a word, phrase, or clause that does not include a preposition.  It indicates the absence of a prepositional phrase in a particular grammatical construction.


例句：

The verb "arrive" can be used nonprepositionally.  = 动词“到达”可以非介词地使用。

The sentence is structured nonprepositionally, lacking any prepositional phrases. = 这句话的结构是非介词的，缺少任何介词短语。


近义词： prepositionlessly (less common)


反义词： prepositionally


词性: 副词
