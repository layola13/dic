infusing:/ɪnˈfjuːzɪŋ/| v.|灌输；注入；冲泡；|to fill something or someone with a particular quality or feeling; to steep (tea, etc.) in hot water|例句：The teacher infused the class with a love of learning.  老师给课堂灌输了对学习的热爱。|例句：She infused the tea leaves in boiling water for five minutes. 她用沸水冲泡茶叶五分钟。|近义词：injecting, instilling, steeping|反义词：removing, extracting


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He infuses his speeches with humor. = 他在演讲中融入幽默。
She infuses the fabric with a unique dye. = 她用独特的染料给织物染色。


一般过去时:
The artist infused the painting with vibrant colors. = 艺术家给画作注入了鲜艳的色彩。
They infused the community with a sense of hope. = 他们给社区注入了希望感。


现在完成时:
She has infused her life with purpose. = 她给自己的生活注入了目标。
He has infused the story with suspense. = 他给故事注入了悬念。


过去完成时:
They had infused the fabric with a special treatment before dyeing it. = 他们在染色之前已经给织物进行了特殊处理。
She had infused the room with the scent of lavender. = 她已经让房间充满了薰衣草的香味。


将来时:
We will infuse the new product with cutting-edge technology. = 我们将把尖端技术融入新产品中。
They will infuse the music with a modern feel. = 他们将使音乐具有现代感。


虚拟语气:
If I infused the tea longer, it would be stronger. = 如果我多泡一会儿茶，茶就会更浓。
I wish I could infuse my writing with more passion. = 我希望我的写作能更有激情。


被动语态:
The cake was infused with vanilla extract. = 蛋糕中注入了香草精。
The room was infused with the aroma of freshly baked bread. = 房间里弥漫着新鲜烘焙面包的香味。


疑问句:
How do you infuse the herbs into the oil? = 你是如何将香草注入油中的？
Will this process infuse the fabric with color permanently? = 这个过程能永久地给织物染色吗？


否定句:
The artist did not infuse the sculpture with any emotion. = 艺术家并没有在雕塑中注入任何情感。
He didn't infuse enough energy into his presentation. = 他在演示中没有注入足够的精力。


PHRASE->

infuse with:  注入...；使充满...
infuse into: 注入...；融入...
infuse a sense of: 灌输一种...的感觉
infused water: 注入水的；泡好的水
infused oil: 注入油的
