reengage:/ˌriːɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/| vt.|重新参与，重新投入，重新接合|To begin to engage again; to become involved or interested again.|例句：After a long break, she decided to reengage with her painting.  在她长时间休息后，她决定重新开始绘画。|例句：The company is trying to reengage its employees by offering new training opportunities. 公司正试图通过提供新的培训机会来重新调动员工的积极性。|近义词：reinvolve, restart, resume|反义词：disengage, withdraw, abandon|动词
