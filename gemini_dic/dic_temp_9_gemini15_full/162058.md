pre-bookable: /ˌpriːˈbʊkəbəl/ | adj. | 可预订的，可提前预订的 |  Able to be booked in advance.  This adjective describes something that can be reserved or scheduled ahead of time. | 例句：The concert tickets are pre-bookable online.  网上可以提前预订演唱会门票。

The hotel rooms are pre-bookable up to three months in advance.  酒店房间可以提前三个月预订。


近义词：bookable in advance, reservable, pre-orderable


反义词：unbookable, unavailable


词性：形容词
