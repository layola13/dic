prestorage:/ˌpriːˈstɔːrɪdʒ/| n. |预存储；预先存储|The act of storing something beforehand, typically data or resources, for later use.  This ensures faster access and improved efficiency when needed. |例句：The application uses prestorage of frequently accessed data to enhance performance.  应用程序使用预先存储频繁访问的数据来提高性能。

例句：Efficient prestorage of images reduced loading times significantly.  有效地预存储图像显著缩短了加载时间。


近义词：caching, buffering, preloading


反义词：on-demand storage, just-in-time storage


词性: 名词
