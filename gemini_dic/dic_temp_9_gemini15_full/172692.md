/kjuːˈsiː/|/ˈkjuːsiː/|abbr.|质量控制|quality control; a system of maintaining standards in manufactured products by testing a sample of the output against the specifications.|例句：The factory has a strict QC procedure in place.  这家工厂实施了严格的质量控制程序。|近义词：quality assurance, inspection|反义词：negligence, carelessness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
QC checks every product before shipment. = 质量控制在发货前检查每一件产品。
Our company implements rigorous QC standards. = 我们公司实施严格的质量控制标准。


一般过去时:
The QC team identified several defects last week. = 质量控制团队上周发现了几个缺陷。
They conducted a thorough QC inspection of the finished goods. = 他们对成品进行了彻底的质量控制检查。


现在完成时:
QC has improved significantly in recent years. = 近年来质量控制有了显著提高。
The company has implemented new QC protocols. = 公司实施了新的质量控制规程。


过去完成时:
