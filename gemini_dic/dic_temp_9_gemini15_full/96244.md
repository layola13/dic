indication:/ˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 指示；迹象；表明 | A sign or piece of information that indicates something.  It suggests the presence or existence of something. |例句：The high fever is an indication of infection. 高烧是感染的迹象。

例句：There were clear indications that the economy was slowing down. 有明显的迹象表明经济正在放缓。


近义词：sign, symptom, signal, hint, suggestion | 反义词：concealment, hiding, disproof


词源:  From Old French *indication (14c.), from Latin indicatio, from indicare "to point out," from index, indic- "pointer, informer," from *indic- "to point to."  The root is likely related to the Proto-Indo-European root *weik- "to show, point out."
