nonpreservative:/ˌnɒnprɪˈzɜːvətɪv/|/ˌnɑːnprɪˈzɜːrvətɪv/| adj.|非防腐的；不含防腐剂的|Not containing preservatives; designed not to preserve food.  This term describes food, products, or methods that do not utilize substances to inhibit microbial growth or delay spoilage.|Example:  The company promotes its nonpreservative jam as a healthier option.  这家公司宣传其不含防腐剂的果酱是一种更健康的选择。

The bakery uses only nonpreservative ingredients in its bread. 这家面包店的面包只使用不含防腐剂的配料。|近义词：unpreserved, additive-free|反义词：preserved, preservative-containing
