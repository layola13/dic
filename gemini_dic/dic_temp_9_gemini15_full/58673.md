dummiest: /ˈdʌmiɪst/ | adj. | 最愚蠢的，最笨的 |  The superlative form of "dummy," meaning the most lacking in intelligence or good judgment. It emphasizes the extreme degree of foolishness or stupidity. | 例句：He made the dumbest mistake imaginable. 他犯了一个难以想象的愚蠢错误。

That was the dumbest movie I've ever seen. 那是我看过的最愚蠢的电影。

| 近义词：most foolish, most stupid, most idiotic, most senseless | 反义词：most intelligent, most brilliant, most clever, most wise | 词性:形容词
