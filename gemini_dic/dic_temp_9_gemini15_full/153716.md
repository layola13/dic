Examples include pumpkins, melons, and squashes.  |例句：The farmer harvested a bountiful crop of pepos this year. 今年农民收获了大量的葫芦科果实。|例句：Many pepos are popular as both food and decoration during the autumn season.  许多葫芦科果实在秋季既作为食物又作为装饰很受欢迎。|近义词：fruit, gourd, berry (in some cases)|反义词：None specifically, but terms like "seed" or "flower" could be considered opposites in a botanical context.|名词
