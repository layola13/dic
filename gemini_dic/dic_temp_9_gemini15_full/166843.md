pressing:/ˈprɛsɪŋ/| adj.|紧迫的，重要的，令人压抑的|Urgent; demanding immediate attention; causing anxiety or distress.|例句：We have a pressing need for more funding. 我们迫切需要更多资金。|例句：The situation is becoming increasingly pressing. 情况变得越来越紧急。|近义词：urgent, crucial, critical, important|反义词：unimportant, trivial, minor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The deadline is pressing. = 截止日期迫在眉睫。
This matter is pressing. = 这件事很紧急。

一般过去时:
The problem was pressing. = 问题很紧迫。
The need for action was pressing. = 行动需求迫在眉睫。

现在完成时:
The situation has become increasingly pressing. = 情况变得越来越紧急。
The need for a solution has been pressing for months. =  几个月来，对解决方案的需求一直很迫切。

过去完成时:
The need for a decision had been pressing for weeks. = 几周来，做出决定的需求一直很紧迫。
The situation had become increasingly pressing before the meeting. = 会议之前，情况已经变得越来越紧急。

将来时:
The situation will become even more pressing next week. = 下周情况将更加紧急。
The need for reform will be pressing in the coming years. = 未来几年改革的需求将是紧迫的。

虚拟语气:
If the deadline were less pressing, I would take more time. = 如果截止日期不那么紧迫，我会花更多时间。
I wish the problem weren't so pressing. = 我希望问题不要那么紧迫。

被动语态:
The matter is being pressed by the committee. = 这件事正在由委员会施压。
The issue is being pressed upon us. =  这个问题正强加于我们。

疑问句:
Is the deadline pressing? = 截止日期紧迫吗？
Is there anything pressing I should know? = 有什么紧急的事情我应该知道吗？

否定句:
The matter is not pressing. = 这件事不紧迫。
The deadline is not pressing at all. = 截止日期一点也不紧迫。


PHRASE->
pressing need: 迫切的需求
pressing issue: 紧迫的问题
pressing concern: 令人担忧的问题
pressing engagement: 重要的约会
pressing matter: 重要的事情
