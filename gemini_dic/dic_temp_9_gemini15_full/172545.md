pyrochlore:/ˈpaɪrəˌklɔːr/| n. |烧绿石|A complex oxide mineral, typically containing niobium, tantalum, titanium, and other elements. It is often found in pegmatites and alkaline igneous rocks and is a source of rare earth elements.  |Example: Pyrochlore is an important ore of niobium and tantalum.  烧绿石是铌和钽的重要矿石。|近义词：microlite (a related mineral)|反义词：无
