prewired:/ˌpriːˈwaɪərd/| adj.|预先布线的；预先安装的；先天具备的 | Already equipped or installed; having inherent qualities or characteristics from birth.  |例句：The house came with prewired internet access. 这房子自带预先布线的网络接入。

例句：He seemed to have a prewired talent for music. 他似乎天生就具有音乐天赋。

反义词：unwired, acquired, learned
