词源:
The word "Mexican" comes from "Mexico," which in turn originates from the Nahuatl word "Mēxihco," referring to the Aztec capital city of Tenochtitlan. The suffix "-an" is an adjectival suffix, meaning "of or relating to."  Therefore, "Mexican" means "of or relating to Mexico."
