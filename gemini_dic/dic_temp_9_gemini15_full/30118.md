ceded:/siːdɪd/ | v. |割让；放弃；让步 | To yield or surrender possession of (something); to give up.  |例句：France ceded Alsace-Lorraine to Germany after the Franco-Prussian War. 法国在普法战争后将阿尔萨斯-洛林割让给德国。
例句：He ceded his place in line to his elderly mother. 他把队伍里的位置让给了他年迈的母亲。|近义词：yield, relinquish, surrender, concede |反义词：retain, keep, acquire, seize


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He cedes his rights to the property. 他放弃了该房产的所有权。
She cedes control to her successor. 她把控制权移交给她的继任者。

一般过去时:
The country ceded a large portion of its territory.  这个国家割让了它很大一部分领土。
They ceded the point in the argument. 他们在争论中让步了。

现在完成时:
The government has ceded control of the industry. 政府已经放弃了对该行业的控制。
She has ceded her position on the board. 她已经辞去了她在董事会中的职务。

过去完成时:
They had ceded the territory before the treaty was signed.  条约签署之前，他们已经割让了那块领土。
He had ceded his claim to the inheritance. 他已放弃了他对遗产的索赔。

将来时:
The company will cede its operations to a foreign firm. 这家公司将把业务转让给一家外国公司。
He will cede his seat to the pregnant woman. 他将把座位让给孕妇。

虚拟语气:
If he ceded the argument, the tension would ease. 如果他让步，紧张气氛就会缓和。
I wish he had ceded control sooner. 我希望他早点放弃控制权。

被动语态:
The land was ceded to the neighboring country.  这片土地被割让给了邻国。
The right to vote was ceded to women. 妇女被赋予了投票权。


疑问句:
Will the government cede control?  政府会放弃控制权吗？
Did they cede the disputed islands?  他们割让了有争议的岛屿吗？


否定句:
The government refused to cede any land. 政府拒绝割让任何土地。
He would not cede an inch in the negotiations. 他在谈判中寸步不让。



PHRASE->

cede to:  让步于
cede control: 放弃控制
cede power: 放弃权力
cede territory: 割让领土
cede a point:  在某一点上让步


词源:

源自拉丁语 *cedere，意思是“to go, yield, withdraw”。  这个词根也与许多其他英语词汇相关，例如“proceed,” “precede,” 和 “recede.”
