sinologue:/ˈsɪnəˌlɒɡ/ | n. |汉学家；中国问题专家 |A sinologue is a scholar specializing in the study of China, its language, culture, history, and politics.  Sinologues often have expertise in multiple areas related to China. |Example: Professor Li is a renowned sinologue, particularly known for her work on Tang dynasty poetry.  李教授是一位著名的汉学家，尤其以其唐诗研究而闻名。|近义词：China specialist, Chinophile (if positive connotation is intended), expert on China |反义词：None directly opposite, but perhaps someone with anti-China sentiment could be considered an indirect antonym. |词性:noun
