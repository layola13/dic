derogated:/ˈdɛrəɡeɪtɪd/| v.|贬低；贬损；降低；剥夺…的权利|To speak of or treat disrespectfully; belittle; disparage.  To lessen the force, authority, or value of; impair.|例句：His behavior derogated from his reputation. 他的行为损害了他的名誉。
例句：The judge's decision derogated the rights of the accused.  法官的判决剥夺了被告的权利。|近义词：debase, demean, depreciate, belittle|反义词：exalt, enhance, elevate, praise|词性：动词

词源:
From Old French *deroguer (from Latin derogare "to take away," from de- "from" + rogare "to ask").  The sense evolution reflects the idea of taking away something's value or importance.
