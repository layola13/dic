It encompasses a wide range of options, including graduate studies (master's and doctoral degrees), professional certifications, and other specialized training programs.  | Example:  Many students pursue posttertiary education to enhance their career prospects.  许多学生追求高等教育以提升他们的职业前景。|近义词： postgraduate, graduate, advanced |反义词：pre-tertiary, secondary, undergraduate
