pro:/proʊ/|prefix|  前缀；有利于…；支持…|表示“赞成”、“支持”、“有利于”的意思，常用作前缀，加在名词或形容词前。|例句：pro-choice 支持堕胎选择的；pro-environment 环保的|近义词：for, in favor of, supportive of|反义词：anti-, con-, against


USAGE->
This word is a prefix, not a standalone word with its own verb conjugations or tenses.  Therefore, standard grammatical tense examples (present, past, future, etc.) are not applicable.  The usage is demonstrated through examples of words it prefixes.

pro-American:  支持美国的
He is pro-American in his political views. 他在政治观点上亲美。

pro-democracy: 支持民主的
The protesters were fiercely pro-democracy.  抗议者坚决支持民主。

pro-life: 反堕胎的
The pro-life movement advocates for the protection of unborn fetuses.  反堕胎运动倡导保护未出生胎儿。

pro-active: 主动的；积极主动的
A proactive approach to problem-solving is essential.  积极主动地解决问题至关重要。

pro-rata: 按比例的；成比例的
The payment will be made pro-rata based on the work completed.  付款将根据完成的工作按比例进行。


PHRASE->
pro bono: 免费的；公益的
The lawyer offered pro bono services to the underprivileged client.  律师为贫困客户提供免费服务。

pro forma:  形式上的；例行的
The meeting was a pro forma affair, with little actual discussion.  会议只是例行公事，几乎没有实际讨论。

pro tempore: 暂时的；临时的
The senator served as pro tempore speaker of the house. 参议员担任众议院临时议长。
