self-manifest:/ˌsɛlfˈmænɪfɛst/| adj.|自明的，显而易见的|Clearly evident or obvious; needing no explanation or proof.|The evidence was self-manifest, requiring no further investigation.  证据是不言自明的，不需要进一步调查。|近义词：evident, obvious, apparent, clear|反义词：obscure, hidden, ambiguous|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem is self-manifest. = 问题是显而易见的。
The solution self-manifests in the data. = 解答在数据中是显而易见的。


一般过去时:
The error was self-manifest. = 错误是显而易见的。
The results self-manifested quickly. = 结果很快显现出来。


现在完成时:
The truth has self-manifested. = 真相已经显露。
The benefits have self-manifested over time. = 好处随着时间推移而显现。


过去完成时:
The flaw had self-manifested before the launch. = 在发布之前，缺陷就已经显现出来。
It had self-manifested itself as a major problem. = 它已经显现为一个主要问题。


将来时:
The consequences will self-manifest soon. = 后果很快就会显现。
The pattern will self-manifest in the next few days. = 模式将在未来几天显现出来。


虚拟语气:
If the answer were self-manifest, we wouldn't need to investigate. = 如果答案是显而易见的，我们就不需要调查了。
I wish the solution were self-manifest. = 我希望解决方案是显而易见的。


被动语态:
The problem is self-manifested in the report. = 问题在报告中已明确指出。
The pattern was self-manifested through the experiments. = 通过实验，模式得以显现。


疑问句:
Is the solution self-manifest? = 解答是显而易见的吗？
Are the results self-manifest? = 结果是显而易见的吗？


否定句:
The meaning is not self-manifest. = 含义并非显而易见。
The cause is not self-manifest. = 原因并非显而易见。


PHRASE->

self-manifest destiny:  命中注定（很少用此搭配）
