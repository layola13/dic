parts:/pɑːrts/|ˈpɑːrts/| n.| 部分，零件，部件 | The word "parts" refers to the pieces or components that make up a whole.  It can refer to physical objects, like the parts of a machine, or to abstract concepts, like the parts of a story. |  The car engine has many parts. 这辆汽车的发动机有很多零件。 The play was divided into three parts. 这出戏分成了三部分。 | sections, components, elements, pieces | whole, entirety | 名词
