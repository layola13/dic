reinhabit:/ˌriːɪnˈhæbɪt/| vt.|重新居住，重新栖息|To inhabit again; to live in or occupy again.  The abandoned village was slowly reinhabited by families returning from the city.|近义词：repopulate, reoccupy|反义词：abandon, vacate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reinhabit the old house every summer. = 他们每年夏天都重新居住在老房子里。
The birds reinhabit their nests after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，鸟儿们重新栖息在它们的巢穴里。

一般过去时:
The farmers reinhabited the deserted farmland. = 农民们重新耕种了荒废的农田。
After the war, the city was slowly reinhabited. = 战争之后，这座城市慢慢地重新住满了人。

现在完成时:
The area has been reinhabited since the environmental cleanup. = 自从环境清理之后，这个地区已经重新有人居住了。
The wolves have reinhabited the forest after decades of absence. = 狼群在消失了数十年后，重新回到了这片森林。

过去完成时:
They had reinhabited the island before the hurricane hit. = 飓风来临之前，他们已经重新居住在这个岛屿上了。
The birds had reinhabited the damaged nests before nightfall. = 鸟儿们在夜幕降临前已经重新栖息在受损的鸟巢里了。

将来时:
We will reinhabit the old farmhouse next spring. = 我们明年春天将重新居住在老农舍里。
The scientists predict that the species will reinhabit the region within a decade. = 科学家预测该物种将在十年内重新栖息在这个地区。

虚拟语气:
If the government provided incentives, people might reinhabit the rural areas. = 如果政府提供激励措施，人们可能会重新居住在农村地区。
I wish the endangered species would reinhabit their former habitat. = 我希望濒危物种能够重新回到它们以前的栖息地。

被动语态:
The abandoned building was reinhabited by squatters. = 被遗弃的建筑物被棚户区居民重新占据。
The forest is being reinhabited by various animal species. = 森林正在被各种动物物种重新栖息。

疑问句:
Will the town be reinhabited after the flood? = 洪水过后，这个小镇会重新有人居住吗？
Have the beavers reinhabited the river? = 河狸们重新栖息在这条河里了吗？

否定句:
They did not reinhabit the damaged house after the earthquake. = 地震后，他们没有重新居住在受损的房子里。
The area has not been reinhabited due to the ongoing pollution. = 由于持续的污染，该地区一直没有重新有人居住。
