primrose:/ˈprɪmˌroʊz/ | n. |报春花；淡黄色 | A primrose is a small, flowering plant with bright yellow, white, or pink flowers, typically blooming in early spring. It's known for its delicate beauty and association with springtime.  |例句：The primroses were blooming in the garden, adding a splash of yellow to the green landscape. 花园里的报春花开了，为绿色的风景增添了一抹黄色。

例句：She received a bouquet of primroses as a birthday gift. 她收到了一束报春花作为生日礼物。


近义词：cowslip (某些种类的报春花)

反义词：无


词源：源自古英语 *primrose, 字面意思是“第一朵玫瑰”，暗示其早春开花。
