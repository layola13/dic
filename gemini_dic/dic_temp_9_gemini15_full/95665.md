incompressible: /ˌɪnkəmˈprɛsəbl/ | adj. | 不可压缩的 |  incapable of being compressed; not yielding to pressure.  | Example:  Water is nearly incompressible. 水几乎不可压缩。  |近义词：unyielding, rigid | 反义词：compressible
