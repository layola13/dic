canis:/ˈkeɪnɪs/| n.|犬属；犬科动物|The word "canis" is a genus of the family Canidae, encompassing various species of dogs, wolves, jackals, and coyotes.  It refers to the taxonomic classification of these animals. |Example:  Wolves belong to the *Canis* genus.  狼属于犬属。|近义词：dog (informal), canine|反义词：无
