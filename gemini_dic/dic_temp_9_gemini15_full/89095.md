holiday:/ˈhɒlədeɪ/ /ˈhɑːlədeɪ/| n. |假日，节日，假期 |A holiday is a day on which people celebrate a religious or national event, or a day on which they do not have to work.  It can also refer to a period of time spent away from work or school for rest or recreation.|例句：Christmas is a major holiday in many countries. 圣诞节是许多国家的重要节日。

We're planning a holiday to the beach next month. 我们计划下个月去海滩度假。


近义词：vacation, break, festival |反义词：workday, workday
