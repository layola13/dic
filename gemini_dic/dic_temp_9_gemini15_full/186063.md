rewriters:/ˈriːˌraɪtərz/ | n. |改写者，润色者 |People who rewrite or revise texts, often to improve clarity, style, or grammar. They may work on various types of writing, from academic papers to marketing materials.  |Example:  The article was improved significantly by the skilled rewriters.  这篇文章经过熟练的改写者润色后有了显著的提高。|近义词：editors, revisers, revisors |反义词：original authors (in the context of rewriting existing work)
