outstank:/aʊtˈstæŋk/| v.|超过…的气味；臭味盖过|To surpass in odor, usually unpleasant; to have a stronger or more offensive smell than.|The garbage outstank the entire neighborhood. 垃圾的气味超过了整个街区。|The rotten eggs outstank the fish. 烂鸡蛋的气味比鱼更臭。|近义词：overshadow, outdo|反义词：be outdone by
