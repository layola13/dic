giving:/ˈɡɪvɪŋ/| n. adj. |给予；施舍；慷慨；给予的；赠送的 |The act of giving something to someone.  The quality of being generous. |例句：Her giving nature is well-known.她的慷慨是众所周知的。

The giving of gifts is a common practice during the holidays. 节日期间赠送礼物是一种常见的做法。|近义词：donation, bestowal, generosity |反义词：receiving, selfishness, stinginess|名词，形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His giving inspires others. = 他的给予激励着他人。
She is giving away her old clothes. = 她正在捐赠她的旧衣服。


一般过去时:
They gave generously to charity. = 他们慷慨地捐赠给了慈善机构。
He gave a speech at the conference. = 他在会议上发表了演讲。


现在完成时:
She has given much to the community. = 她为社区贡献良多。
We have given all we can. = 我们已经尽力了。


过去完成时:
He had given away all his possessions before he left. = 他离开之前已经捐赠了他所有的财产。
They had given up hope before the rescue arrived. = 在救援到来之前，他们已经放弃了希望。


将来时:
I will give you a call later. = 我稍后会给你打电话。
They will be giving a performance tonight. = 他们今晚将进行演出。


虚拟语气:
If he gave more, he would feel better. = 如果他多给予一些，他会感觉更好。
I wish I had given her more support. = 我希望我给了她更多的支持。


被动语态:
The award was given to the best student. = 奖项颁发给了最好的学生。
The money was given away to the needy. = 这笔钱被捐给了穷人。


疑问句:
Are you giving anything to charity? = 你有捐赠给慈善机构吗？
What are you giving him for his birthday? = 你要送他什么生日礼物？


否定句:
He didn't give me an answer. = 他没有给我答案。
She wasn't giving up easily. = 她没有轻易放弃。
