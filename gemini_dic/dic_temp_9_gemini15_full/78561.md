ghoulish:/ˈɡuːlɪʃ/| adj.| 可怕的；令人毛骨悚然的；嗜杀成性的 |  Suggestive of a ghoul; gruesome; horrifying.  It often implies a morbid fascination with death or violence. | 例句：The ghoulish details of the murder were enough to make anyone's stomach churn.  凶杀案中那些可怕的细节足以让任何人的胃都翻江倒海。

The ghoulish laughter echoed through the empty house.  令人毛骨悚然的狂笑声在空荡荡的房子里回荡。


近义词：gruesome, grisly, macabre, morbid, ghastly, horrific | 反义词：pleasant, cheerful, delightful, wholesome
