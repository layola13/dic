shoing:/ʃoʊɪŋ/| v.|展示；炫耀；显示|To show or display something; to exhibit proudly.|例句：He was shoing off his new car. 他正在炫耀他的新车。|近义词：displaying, exhibiting, flaunting|反义词：hiding, concealing|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is showing his paintings. = 他正在展示他的画作。
She shows her skills every day. = 她每天都展示她的技能。

一般过去时:
They showed us their new house. = 他们向我们展示了他们的新房子。
He showed great courage in the face of danger. = 面对危险，他表现出极大的勇气。

现在完成时:
I have shown him the way. = 我已经给他指了路。
She has shown remarkable progress. = 她已经取得了显著的进步。

过去完成时:
They had shown their support before the event. = 他们在那次活动之前就已经表达了支持。
He had shown me the documents already. = 他已经给我看过文件了。

将来时:
We will show you the results tomorrow. = 我们明天会把结果给你们看。
She will be showing her work at the gallery next week. = 下周她将在画廊展出她的作品。

虚拟语气:
If I showed him the evidence, he would believe me. = 如果我给他看证据，他会相信我的。
I wish I had shown more compassion. = 我真希望我当时表现出更多同情心。

被动语态:
The painting was shown at the museum. = 这幅画在博物馆展出。
The results will be shown on the screen. = 结果将在屏幕上显示。

疑问句:
Will you show me how to do it? = 你能教我怎么做吗？
Are they showing the movie tonight? = 他们今晚放映这部电影吗？

否定句:
He didn't show any interest. = 他没有表现出任何兴趣。
She didn't show up for the meeting. = 她没有参加会议。


PHRASE->
show off = 炫耀
show up = 出席；露面
show around = 带领参观
put on a show = 表演；炫技
show one's hand = 摊牌；亮出底牌
