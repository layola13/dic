out-and-away: /ˌaʊt ænd əˈweɪ/ | adj. | 显著地；远远地；大大地 |  Clearly superior; by a considerable margin.  This phrase emphasizes a significant difference or superiority. | 例句：Her performance was out-and-away the best.  她的表演明显是最好的。

Her design was out-and-away superior to the others. 她的设计远远优于其他设计。


近义词：significantly, considerably, markedly, substantially, overwhelmingly, decisively


反义词：slightly, marginally, barely, insignificantly


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His skills are out-and-away the best in the team. = 他的技能在团队中明显是最好的。
This product is out-and-away superior to its competitors. = 此产品明显优于其竞争对手。


一般过去时:
His presentation was out-and-away the most engaging. = 他的演讲明显是最引人入胜的。
That solution was out-and-away the most effective. = 那个方案明显是最有效的。


现在完成时:
Her contributions have been out-and-away the most impactful. = 她的贡献无疑是最有影响力的。
Their efforts have been out-and-away successful. = 他们的努力已经取得了显著的成功。


过去完成时:
His work had been out-and-away the most thorough. = 他的工作明显是最彻底的。
Their preparations had been out-and-away superior to the opposing team's. = 他们的准备工作明显优于对方队伍。


将来时:
Her invention will be out-and-away the most revolutionary. = 她的发明将是革命性的。
Their new strategy will be out-and-away the most profitable. = 他们的新战略将是远比其他更赚钱的。


虚拟语气:
If he were to compete, his performance would be out-and-away the best. = 如果他参加比赛，他的表现将会是最好的。
If they had invested more, their returns would have been out-and-away greater. = 如果他们投资更多，他们的回报将会远远更高。


被动语态:
His design was judged to be out-and-away the most innovative. = 他的设计被认为是最具创新性的。
Their product was considered out-and-away superior by the judges. = 他们的产品被评委认为明显优越。


疑问句:

否定句:
His answer wasn't out-and-away the best, but it was good. = 他的答案并非最好，但还不错。
Their plan wasn't out-and-away successful, but it made progress. = 他们的计划并非完全成功，但取得了进展。
