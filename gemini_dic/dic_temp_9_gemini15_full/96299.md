indigenity: /ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛnɪti/ | n. |土著性；本土性；原住民身份 | The state or quality of being indigenous; the fact of originating in and being native to a particular place.  It often refers to the rights and cultures of indigenous peoples. |例句：The museum aims to celebrate the indigenity of the local tribes.  博物馆旨在庆祝当地部落的土著性。

例句：  The government is working to protect the indigenity of the Amazon rainforest. 政府正在努力保护亚马逊雨林的本土性。


近义词：nativeness, aboriginality, autochthony | 反义词：foreignness, alienness
