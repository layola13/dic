noncorporate:/ˌnɑːnˈkɔːrpərət/| adj. |非公司的；非企业性质的 |Not relating to or characteristic of a corporation or corporations.  It emphasizes the lack of corporate structure, ownership, or influence. |例句：The noncorporate sector of the economy is growing rapidly.  = 经济的非公司部门正在快速发展。

例句：He prefers working for a noncorporate organization that values its employees. = 他更喜欢为一家重视员工的非公司组织工作。


近义词：independent, private, unincorporated, individual


反义词：corporate, incorporated, business
