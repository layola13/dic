nonaccessions:/ˌnɒnækˈseʃənz/ | n. | 非加入；未加入者 |  The term "nonaccessions" refers to items or materials that have not been formally added to a collection, library, or database.  This could be due to various reasons, such as being rejected, deemed unsuitable, or simply overlooked.  It's the opposite of accessions, which are items formally added. | Example:  The museum's nonaccessions list included several potentially valuable artifacts that were initially rejected.  博物馆的未加入清单包括几件最初被拒绝的、可能很有价值的文物。| 近义词：rejections, exclusions | 反义词：accessions
