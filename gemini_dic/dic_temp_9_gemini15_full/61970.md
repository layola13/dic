en brochette:/ˌɑ̃ bʁɔʃɛt/| n.|串烧；(食物)串在签子上|Food cooked on a skewer.  The term is French in origin and refers to food items, typically meat or vegetables, that are threaded onto a skewer and grilled, broiled, or roasted.|例句：The restaurant is famous for its delicious chicken en brochette. 这家餐馆以其美味的鸡肉串烧而闻名。

例句：We grilled vegetables en brochette for the barbecue. 我们在烧烤会上烤了蔬菜串。


近义词：kebab, shish kebab, barbecue


反义词：无


词性:名词
