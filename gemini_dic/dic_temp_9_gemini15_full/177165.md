reargue:/ˌriˈɑːrɡjuː/|/ˌriːˈɑːrɡjuː/| vt.|再争论，重新辩论|To argue again; to present an argument again, often with new evidence or a different approach.|The lawyer decided to reargue the case based on newly discovered evidence.  律师决定根据新发现的证据重新辩论此案。

They re-argued the contract terms after discovering a loophole. 他们发现了一个漏洞后，重新协商了合同条款。


近义词：reiterate, re-examine, reconsider, debate again, dispute anew|反义词：concede, accept, agree, acquiesce|动词
