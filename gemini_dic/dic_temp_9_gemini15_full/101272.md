involucred:/ɪnˈvɒlvd/ | adj. | 卷入的；涉及到的 | Involved means deeply connected or complicated. It suggests a significant degree of participation or entanglement. |例句：He was involved in a car accident. 他卷入了一场车祸。 |例句：The investigation involved many people. 这项调查涉及许多人。|近义词： implicated, embroiled, entangled |反义词：uninvolved, detached, unconnected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is involved and complex. = 这个项目复杂且涉及面广。
He is involved in the community. = 他参与社区事务。

一般过去时:
She was involved in a heated debate. = 她卷入了一场激烈的辩论。
The police were involved in the investigation. = 警方参与了调查。

现在完成时:
They have been involved in several successful ventures. = 他们参与过几次成功的商业项目。
I have been involved in this project for three years. = 我参与这个项目三年了。

过去完成时:
He had been involved in similar projects before. = 他以前参与过类似的项目。
The company had been involved in a scandal before the merger. = 在合并之前，该公司卷入过一起丑闻。

将来时:
She will be involved in the decision-making process. = 她将参与决策过程。
We will be involved in the planning of the event. = 我们将参与活动的策划。

虚拟语气:
If he were involved, he would know the details. = 如果他参与其中，他会知道细节。
I wish I hadn't been involved in that argument. = 我真希望我没有卷入那场争论。

被动语态:
Many people were involved in the accident. = 许多人卷入了事故。
The company was involved in a lawsuit. = 这家公司卷入了一场诉讼。

疑问句:
Were you involved in the planning? = 你参与了策划吗？
Is he involved in this matter? = 他参与此事吗？

否定句:
He was not involved in the crime. = 他没有参与犯罪。
I am not involved in their personal affairs. = 我不参与他们的私事。
