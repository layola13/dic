monocotyledon:/ˌmɒnəʊkɒtɪˈliːdən/ /ˌmɑːnəʊkɑːtɪˈliːdən/| n. |单子叶植物|A monocotyledon is a flowering plant with one cotyledon (seed leaf) in its embryo. Monocots are a large and diverse group, including grasses, lilies, orchids, and palms. They are characterized by their parallel leaf venation, fibrous root systems, and floral parts typically in multiples of three.|Example:  Grasses are a common example of a monocotyledon.  禾本科植物是单子叶植物的常见例子。|近义词：monocot|反义词：dicotyledon
