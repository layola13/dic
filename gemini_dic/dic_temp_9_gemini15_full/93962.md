immutable:/ɪˈmjuːtəbəl/ | adj. |不变的；不可变的 | Unchangeable; unable to be altered or changed.  The word refers to something that cannot be changed or modified once it has been established.  This applies to various contexts, from physical objects to abstract concepts. | Example: The laws of physics are immutable. 物理定律是不可改变的。 The past is immutable. 过去是不可改变的。 |近义词：unchangeable, unalterable, invariable, constant |反义词：mutable, changeable, variable, alterable
