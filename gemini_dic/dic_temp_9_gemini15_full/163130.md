precombating:/ˌpriːkəmˈbeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 预防性打击的，先发制人的 |  relating to or denoting actions taken to prevent or counter an anticipated attack or problem.  This word is a relatively rare and newly coined term, a combination of "pre-" (before) and "combating" (fighting against). It implies proactive measures taken to prevent a problem before it arises.  |  The army initiated a precombating strategy to neutralize the enemy's planned offensive.  军队启动了一项先发制人的战略以挫败敌人的计划进攻。|  proactive, preventive, anticipatory | reactive, responsive
