shampooed:/ʃæmˈpuːd/| v.|洗发；用洗发剂洗|过去式和过去分词形式，表示用洗发剂洗头发的动作。|例句：I shampooed my hair this morning.  我今天早上洗了头。

例句：She shampooed her daughter's long hair. 她给女儿的长发洗了洗。

近义词：washed (hair), cleansed (hair)
反义词：None (It's an action, not a state.)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I shampoo my hair every other day. = 我每隔一天洗一次头。
He shampoos his hair with a special conditioner. = 他用特殊的护发素洗头。

一般过去时:
I shampooed my hair yesterday. = 我昨天洗了头。
She shampooed her dog's fur. = 她给她的狗洗了毛。


现在完成时:
I have shampooed my hair twice today. = 我今天洗了两次头。
She has shampooed her hair with that new product. = 她用那个新产品洗了头。


过去完成时:
I had shampooed my hair before I went out. = 我出门前洗了头。
He had shampooed his hair before the important meeting. = 他在重要的会议之前洗了头。


将来时:
I will shampoo my hair tomorrow. = 我明天会洗头。
She will shampoo her hair after her workout. = 她锻炼后会洗头。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would shampoo my hair. = 如果我有时间，我会洗头。
I wish I had shampooed my hair before the party. = 我真希望在派对前洗了头。


被动语态:
My hair was shampooed by a professional stylist. = 我的头发由专业的造型师洗的。
His hair is being shampooed right now. = 他的头发正在被洗。


疑问句:
Did you shampoo your hair today? = 你今天洗头了吗？
Have you ever shampooed a horse? = 你曾经洗过马吗？


否定句:
I didn't shampoo my hair this morning. = 我今天早上没有洗头。
She doesn't shampoo her hair every day. = 她不是每天都洗头。


PHRASE->

dry shampoo = 干洗发剂
