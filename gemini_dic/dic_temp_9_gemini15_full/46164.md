cynics:/ˈsɪnɪks/| n.|犬儒主义者；愤世嫉俗者；怀疑论者|A cynic is a person who believes that people are motivated purely by self-interest;  someone who distrusts human sincerity or integrity.|例句：He's such a cynic; he doesn't believe anyone is truly altruistic. 他是个愤世嫉俗的人；他不相信任何人真正具有利他主义。|例句：Her cynical remarks about the politician's promises left everyone feeling disillusioned. 她对这位政治家承诺的愤世嫉俗的评论让每个人都感到幻灭。|近义词：pessimist, skeptic, doubter|反义词：optimist, idealist
