词源:
The word "outswaggering" is formed by adding the prefix "out-" to the word "swaggering."  "Out-" indicates exceeding or surpassing, while "swaggering" describes a boastful and arrogant way of walking or behaving.  Therefore, "outswaggering" implies surpassing others in arrogance and boastfulness.
