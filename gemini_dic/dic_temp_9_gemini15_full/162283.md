preaccidental: /ˌpriːæksɪˈdentəl/ | adj. | 预先意外的；先于事故的 |  Describing something that happens before an accident or incident, often implying a contributing factor. It suggests a cause or precursor that is not immediately obvious but plays a role in the later event.  | The preaccidental damage to the brakes was a major contributing factor to the car crash.  |  antecedent, precursor | postaccidental | adj.
