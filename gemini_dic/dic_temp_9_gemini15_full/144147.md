outshames: /ˌaʊtˈʃeɪmz/ | vt. | 使蒙羞，胜过…的羞耻 | to surpass someone in shame or disgrace; to put someone to shame by being even more shameful or disgraceful | 例句：His actions outshamed even the worst criminals.  他的行为甚至比最恶劣的罪犯还要可耻。 | 近义词：disgrace, shame, humiliate | 反义词：honor, glorify

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outshames his rivals in every aspect. = 他在各个方面都让他的对手蒙羞。
The scandal outshames the entire organization. = 这场丑闻让整个组织蒙羞。


一般过去时:
