detesters:/dɪˈtɛstərz/| n. |厌恶者，憎恨者 |Those who intensely dislike or hate something or someone.  They feel strong aversion or repugnance. |例句：The protesters were met with a barrage of insults from detesters of their cause.  抗议者遭到了那些厌恶他们事业的人的辱骂。

例句：The film's detesters launched a campaign to have it banned.  这部电影的厌恶者发起了一场运动，要求禁播这部电影。


近义词：haters, enemies, opponents, adversaries

反义词：admirers, fans, supporters, lovers

词性:名词
