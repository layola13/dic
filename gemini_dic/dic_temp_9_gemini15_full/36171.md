cofferer:/ˈkɒfərər/| n. |宝库管理员；财务主管|A cofferer is a treasurer or keeper of a treasury.  Historically, this role involved managing the finances of a royal household, a large institution, or a wealthy individual. The term evokes a sense of responsibility for significant funds and valuable assets.  |The royal cofferer was responsible for managing the king's vast wealth. 皇家财务主管负责管理国王的巨额财富。|treasurer, bursar |  debtor (反义词并非完全对立，而是指职责相反的角色)|名词
