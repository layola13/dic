resailed:/ˌriːˈseɪld/| v.|再次航行；重新启航|To sail again; to set sail again after a stop or interruption.|The ship resailed after a brief stop for repairs.  船只在短暂修理后再次启航。|The captain decided to re-sail after the storm subsided. 船长决定风暴平息后重新起航。|set sail, sail again|grounded, docked|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He resails every summer. 他每年夏天都再次航行。
They re-sail to the same island every year. 他们每年都重新启航到同一个岛屿。

一般过去时:
She resailed the next morning. 她第二天早上再次启航。
We resailed after the storm passed. 风暴过后我们再次起航。

现在完成时:
The ship has resailed after the repairs. 船只修理后已经再次启航了。
They have resailed many times this season. 这个季节他们已经多次重新启航了。

过去完成时:
They had resailed before the news reached them. 消息传到他们那里之前，他们已经再次启航了。
She had resailed several times before the accident happened. 事故发生之前，她已经多次重新启航了。

将来时:
He will re-sail tomorrow morning. 他明天早上将再次启航。
We will re-sail as soon as the weather improves. 天气一好转我们就将再次启航。

虚拟语气:
If the weather were better, we would re-sail. 如果天气更好，我们将再次启航。
I wish we had resailed sooner. 我希望我们早点再次启航。

被动语态:
The ship was resailed after the storm. 船只在风暴后再次启航了。(被动语态不太常用)
The boat was resailed under emergency conditions. 小船在紧急情况下重新起航了。（被动语态不太常用）

疑问句:
Did they re-sail yesterday? 他们昨天再次启航了吗？
Will they re-sail next week? 他们下周会再次启航吗？

否定句:
They did not re-sail due to bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，他们没有再次启航。
The ship will not re-sail until the repairs are completed. 在修理完成之前，船只不会再次启航。
