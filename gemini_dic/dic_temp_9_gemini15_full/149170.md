oxyacetic: /ˌɒksiəˈsiːtɪk/ | adj. | 乙酰氧基的；羟乙酸的 |  Describes a chemical compound containing both an acetyl group (CH₃CO) and a hydroxyl group (-OH) or a derivative of hydroxyacetic acid (glycolic acid).  The term often refers to compounds containing the acetoxy group (-O-COCH₃). | Example: Oxyacetic acid esters are used in various applications. 乙酰氧基酸酯被用于各种应用。|近义词:acetoxy (referring to the group), glycolic (referring to the acid) | 反义词: N/A
