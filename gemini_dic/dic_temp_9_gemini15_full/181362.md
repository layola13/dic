reinvestigation: /ˌriːɪnˌvɛstɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 再调查，重新调查 | The act of investigating something again, often after new evidence or information has emerged.  It implies a more thorough or detailed examination than the initial investigation. | 例句：The police announced a reinvestigation into the unsolved murder case after new evidence was discovered.  警方宣布在发现新的证据后将对这起未破的谋杀案进行重新调查。

The reinvestigation of the financial records revealed significant fraud. 对财务记录的重新调查揭示了严重的欺诈行为。| 近义词：re-examination, review, second look | 反义词：conclusion, settlement
