co-op:/ˈkoʊɑːp/| n. |合作社；合作经营；合作公寓|A cooperative enterprise or organization.  It can refer to a business owned and operated by its members, a housing complex owned and managed by its residents, or a student-run business.|例句：The farmers formed a co-op to sell their produce.  农民们成立了一个合作社来销售他们的农产品。|例句：She lives in a student co-op. 她住在学生合作公寓里。|近义词：cooperative, partnership|反义词：competition, individual enterprise|名词
