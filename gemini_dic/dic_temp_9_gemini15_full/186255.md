rhinoscopy:/raɪˈnɒskəpi/|/ˈraɪnɑːskəpi/| n. |鼻镜检查；鼻腔镜检查 |Examination of the nasal passages using a rhinoscope.  A rhinoscopy allows a doctor to visualize the nasal cavity and identify any abnormalities. |Example: The doctor performed a rhinoscopy to examine the patient's nasal polyps. 医生进行了鼻镜检查以检查病人的鼻息肉。|近义词：nasal endoscopy |反义词：N/A
