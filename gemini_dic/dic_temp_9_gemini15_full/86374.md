heddle:/ˈhedl/| n.|综丝器，综框|A heddle is a device used in weaving to lift and lower the warp threads, allowing the weft thread to pass through.  It typically consists of a frame with a series of heddles, each with a loop or eye through which a warp thread passes.|例句：The weaver carefully adjusted the heddles to create the desired pattern. 织工仔细调整综丝器以创造所需的图案。

The intricate design required a loom with multiple heddles. 复杂的设计需要一台带有多个综丝器的织机。|近义词：shedder, harness (in the context of weaving)|反义词：None specifically.|词性:名词
