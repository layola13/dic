patching:/ˈpætʃɪŋ/| v.|修补；弥补；拼凑；|The act of repairing something by covering a hole or damaged area with a patch.  It can also refer to the process of making temporary fixes or combining different things together.|例句：He spent the afternoon patching the hole in the roof. 他下午花了时间修补屋顶上的洞。

例句：The programmer spent hours patching the security vulnerabilities in the software. 程序员花了数小时修补软件中的安全漏洞。


近义词：mending, repairing, fixing, patching up, piecing together |反义词：damaging, destroying, ruining


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are patching the road. = 他们在修补道路。
He patches his old jeans regularly. = 他定期缝补他旧的牛仔裤。


一般过去时:
She patched the tear in her dress. = 她缝补了她衣服上的破洞。
The team patched the server overnight. = 团队通宵修补了服务器。


现在完成时:

We have patched the software several times this month. = 这个月我们已经几次修补了软件。


过去完成时:

They had patched the system before the presentation. = 他们在演示之前已经修补了系统。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
