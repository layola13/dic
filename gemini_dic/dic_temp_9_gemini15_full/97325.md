informations:/ˌɪnfərˈmeɪʃənz/| n.|信息（复数）|The word "informations" is the plural form of "information," but its usage is generally considered incorrect in standard English.  "Information" is considered an uncountable noun, meaning it doesn't have a plural form. While you might hear it used, it's grammatically inaccurate. |  The informations provided were inaccurate. (Incorrect usage)| information | misinformation | noun


USAGE->
This word is not usually used.  The singular "information" is preferred in all grammatical contexts. Examples using "information" are provided below:


一般现在时:
The report contains important information. = 这份报告包含重要信息。
This website provides useful information. = 这个网站提供有用的信息。


一般过去时:
He gave me some helpful information. = 他给了我一些有用的信息。
She received the information yesterday. = 她昨天收到了信息。


现在完成时:
We have gathered all the necessary information. = 我们已经收集了所有必要的信息。
Have you found the information you need? = 你找到你需要的信息了吗？


过去完成时:
They had already received the information before the meeting. = 他们早在会议之前就已经收到了信息。
I had not seen that information before. = 我之前没有见过那条信息。


将来时:
