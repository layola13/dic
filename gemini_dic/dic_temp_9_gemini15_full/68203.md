fair-trader:/ˈferˌtreɪdər/| n. |公平贸易者，公平贸易支持者|A person who supports or participates in fair trade.  Someone who believes in and practices fair trade principles, ensuring fair prices and working conditions for producers in developing countries. | Example:  Many fair-traders are passionate about ethical consumption.  许多公平贸易者热衷于道德消费。  The fair-trader advocated for better wages for coffee farmers.这位公平贸易者倡导为咖啡农争取更高的工资。|近义词：ethical consumer, socially responsible consumer |反义词：exploiter, sweatshop owner
