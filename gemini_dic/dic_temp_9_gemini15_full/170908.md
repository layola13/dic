pseudomembranous:/ˌsuːdoʊˈmbrænəs/ | adj. | 假膜的；伪膜的 |  Relating to or resembling a false membrane; a membrane that is not a true membrane but rather a layer of exudate (e.g., fibrin, cells, etc.) that forms on a mucous membrane surface.  | Example:  Pseudomembranous colitis is a severe intestinal infection that causes the formation of a pseudomembrane in the colon.  假膜性结肠炎是一种严重的肠道感染，会导致结肠形成假膜。  | 近义词：membrane-like, film-like | 反义词：true membranous
