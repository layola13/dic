outboxed:/ˌaʊtˈbɑːkst/ | v. | 超过；击败；胜过 | To defeat or surpass someone, especially in a competition or contest.  |例句：He completely outboxed his opponent in the third round.  他第三回合彻底击败了他的对手。|例句：The smaller company outboxed the larger one in terms of innovation. 这家规模较小的公司在创新方面胜过了规模较大的公司。|近义词：defeat, outmaneuver, outsmart, surpass |反义词：be defeated by, lose to


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outboxes his opponents regularly. = 他经常击败对手。
She outboxes her rivals in terms of sales. = 她在销售方面胜过竞争对手。

一般过去时:
The champion outboxed his challenger decisively. = 冠军果断地击败了他的挑战者。
They outboxed the opposition with superior strategy. = 他们凭借更优秀的策略击败了对手。

现在完成时:
He has outboxed every opponent he's faced this year. = 今年他击败了他遇到的每一个对手。
Our team has outboxed the competition in the market. = 我们的团队在市场上击败了竞争对手。

过去完成时:
She had outboxed her opponent before the final round. = 在最后一轮之前，她已经击败了对手。
The underdog had outboxed the favorite throughout the match. =  在比赛中，弱势一方一直胜过热门一方。


将来时:
He will outbox his opponent with his superior speed. = 他将凭借更快的速度击败对手。
They believe they will outbox their rivals in the next quarter. = 他们相信在下个季度会击败竞争对手。

虚拟语气:
If he trained harder, he would outbox his opponent. = 如果他训练更努力，他就能击败对手。
I wish I could outbox my anxieties. = 我希望我能战胜我的焦虑。

被动语态:
He was outboxed by a younger, faster opponent. = 他被一个更年轻、更快的对手击败了。
The strategy was outboxed by a more innovative approach. = 这种策略被一种更具创新性的方法击败了。

疑问句:
Can he outbox such a strong opponent? = 他能击败如此强大的对手吗？
Will they be able to outbox the market leader? = 他们能够击败市场领导者吗？

否定句:
He couldn't outbox his opponent due to injury. = 由于受伤，他无法击败对手。
They failed to outbox their competitors in the crucial market. = 他们在关键市场上未能击败竞争对手。


PHRASE->

outbox someone in a fight = 在战斗中击败某人
outbox someone in sales = 在销售方面击败某人
outbox the competition = 击败竞争对手
outbox someone strategically = 战略上击败某人
outbox a problem =  巧妙地解决问题 (比较宽泛的用法)
