lithiasis:/lɪˈθaɪəsɪs/| n. |结石病；石症|The formation of calculi (stones) in the body, typically in the kidneys, gallbladder, or urinary tract.  This condition can cause significant pain and may require medical intervention.| Example: Kidney lithiasis is a common condition affecting millions worldwide.  肾结石是一种影响全球数百万人的常见疾病。|近义词:calculosis, stone formation|反义词:absence of stones, stone-free|名词
