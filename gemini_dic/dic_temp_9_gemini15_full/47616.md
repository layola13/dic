debellate:/dɪˈbɛleɪt/| vt.|征服；击败；压制|To conquer or subdue (an enemy or opponent) completely; to vanquish.  The term often implies a thorough and decisive defeat.|例句：The Roman legions debelated the barbarian tribes. 罗马军团彻底击败了野蛮部落。

The general debelated the rebellion with swift and decisive action. 将军迅速果断地平息了叛乱。


近义词：conquer, subdue, vanquish, defeat, overcome|反义词：surrender, yield, succumb

词性:动词
