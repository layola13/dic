clinician:/klɪˈnɪʃən/| n. |临床医生，临床实践者 |A clinician is a healthcare professional who provides direct patient care.  They are often involved in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of illnesses and injuries. Clinicians work in a variety of settings, including hospitals, clinics, and private practices. |例句：The clinician examined the patient carefully and ordered some tests. 临床医生仔细检查了病人，并安排了一些检查。|例句：Experienced clinicians often rely on their intuition as well as scientific data.  经验丰富的临床医生经常依靠他们的直觉以及科学数据。|近义词：doctor, physician, practitioner, medical professional |反义词：patient, researcher (in a contrasting context)
