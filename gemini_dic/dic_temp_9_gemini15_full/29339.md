词源:
The word "casting" comes from the verb "cast," meaning to throw or fling.  In the context of metalworking, it refers to the act of pouring molten metal into a mold.  The "-ing" suffix indicates the gerund or noun form.  Therefore, "castings" refers to the objects produced through this process.
