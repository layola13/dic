kneeled: /niːld/ | v. | 跪下，屈膝 | To be or cause to be in a kneeling position.  |例句：He kneeled before the altar. 他在祭坛前跪下。

例句：She kneeled down to tie her shoelaces. 她跪下系鞋带。


近义词：knelt, prostrated, genuflected | 反义词：stood, rose


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kneels in prayer every morning. 他每天早上都跪着祈祷。
She kneels to tend her garden. 她跪下侍弄她的花园。


一般过去时:
He knelt before the king. 他跪在国王面前。
They knelt to pray. 他们跪下祈祷。


现在完成时:
She has knelt for hours. 她已经跪了好几个小时了。
He has knelt before many obstacles in his life. 他一生中曾多次克服许多障碍。


过去完成时:
She had knelt for so long her knees were aching. 她跪了太久，膝盖都疼了。
They had knelt in silent prayer before the announcement. 在宣布之前，他们默默地跪下祈祷。


将来时:
He will kneel before the judge. 他将在法官面前下跪。
She will kneel and ask for forgiveness. 她将跪下请求原谅。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would kneel and beg for mercy. 如果我是你，我会跪下求饶。
I wish I had knelt down to help her. 我希望我当时跪下帮她。


被动语态:
The prisoner was made to kneel. 囚犯被迫下跪。
The flag was knelt before.  人们向旗帜下跪。


疑问句:
Did he kneel? 他跪了吗？
Will she kneel? 她会跪吗？


否定句:
He didn't kneel. 他没有跪下。
She wouldn't kneel. 她不会跪下。


PHRASE->
kneel down: 跪下
kneel before: 跪在...面前
fall to one's knees:  突然跪下
bend the knee: 下跪（表示屈服）
get down on one's knees: 跪下


词源:
源自古英语 *cnēol,  意为“膝盖”。  与现代英语knee (膝盖) 相关。
