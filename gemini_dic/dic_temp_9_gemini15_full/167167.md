pretasted:/prɪˈteɪstɪd/| adj.| 预先品尝过的；事先尝试过的 | Having been tasted beforehand; experienced or tried in advance.  |The pretasted wine was declared excellent.  预先品尝过的酒被宣布为极好的。  The pretasted recipe was a success. 事先尝试过的食谱很成功。|  pre-tested, pre-experienced |untasted, unexperienced|形容词


USAGE->

The chef pretests all new recipes. = 厨师预先测试所有新菜谱。
This is a pretested method, so it should work. = 这是一个预先测试过的方法，所以它应该有效。

They pretested the software before release. = 他们在发布前预先测试了软件。
