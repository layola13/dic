outpolled:/ˌaʊtˈpoʊld/| vt.|超过…的票数，在投票中胜过|To receive more votes than (someone or something).  It implies a victory in an election or similar voting process.|例句：The incumbent was outpolled by a young challenger.  现任官员被一位年轻的挑战者在投票中击败。

例句：The referendum question was outpolled by a significant margin.  全民公决问题以显著的优势被否决。


近义词：outscore, outvote, defeat, surpass


反义词：be outvoted by, lose to, be defeated by
