ozone:/ˈoʊzoʊn/ /ˈəʊzəʊn/| n. |臭氧|A colorless unstable toxic gas with a pungent odor and powerful oxidizing properties, formed from oxygen by electrical discharges or ultraviolet light. It is present in the earth's atmosphere and is a major component of smog.  |例句：The ozone layer protects us from harmful UV radiation. 臭氧层保护我们免受有害的紫外线辐射。

例句：Air pollution can deplete the ozone layer. 空气污染会消耗臭氧层。


近义词：trioxygen


反义词：无


词性:名词
