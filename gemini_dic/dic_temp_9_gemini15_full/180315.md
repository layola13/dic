refrying:/ˌriːˈfraɪɪŋ/| v.|复炸，再次油炸|The act of frying something a second time.  This is often done to achieve a crispier texture or to remove excess moisture. |例句：The refrying of the potatoes gave them a wonderfully crisp exterior.  再次油炸土豆使它们的外皮非常酥脆。


例句：She improved the texture of the fish by refrying it. 她通过再次油炸改善了鱼的口感。


近义词：reheating, frying again


反义词：None directly opposite, but could consider "not frying" or "boiling" as indirect opposites.


词性：动词
