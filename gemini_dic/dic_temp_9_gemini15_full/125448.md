multiplexers:/ˈmʌltɪplɛksərz/| n. |多路复用器，多路选择器 |A multiplexer is a device that selects one of several analog or digital input signals and forwards the selected input into a single line.  It is essentially an electronic switch that allows several inputs to share a single output. |Example: The video multiplexer combines signals from multiple cameras into a single feed for broadcasting.  这个视频多路复用器将来自多个摄像机的信号组合成一个用于广播的单一馈送。|近义词：selector, switch |反义词：demultiplexer
