PHRASE->
chawal and dal (米饭和扁豆): A common South Asian dish.
khichdi chawal (粥米饭): Rice used in khichdi (a type of porridge).
jeera chawal (孜然米饭): Rice seasoned with cumin seeds.
masala chawal (香料米饭): Rice cooked with spices.
coconut chawal (椰奶米饭): Rice cooked in coconut milk.


词源:

The word "chawal" is derived from the Hindi word चावल (cāval), which ultimately traces back to Proto-Indo-European roots related to words for "grain" or "food."  The word has spread through various South Asian languages and is now commonly used in South Asian English.
