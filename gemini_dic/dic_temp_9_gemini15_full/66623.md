exile:/ˈɛɡzaɪl/| n. |流放；放逐；被流放者 |The act of being sent away from one's own country;  a person who has been forced to leave their country. |例句：He was sentenced to exile for his political activities. 他因政治活动被判流放。  例句：Many political exiles sought refuge in neighboring countries. 许多政治流亡者在邻国寻求庇护。 |近义词：banishment, deportation, expulsion |反义词：return, repatriation |名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government exiles dissidents. = 政府流放持不同政见者。
He lives in exile. = 他流亡海外。


一般过去时:
The king exiled his enemies. = 国王流放了他的敌人。
They were exiled from their homeland. = 他们被流放到家乡之外。


现在完成时:
She has been in exile for many years. = 她流亡多年了。
He has never been exiled from his country. = 他从未被流放到国外。


过去完成时:
They had been exiled before the revolution. = 他们在革命前就被流放了。
The family had already been exiled when the war began. = 战争开始时，这家人已经被流放了。


将来时:
They will exile him if he continues his protests. = 如果他继续抗议，他们将会流放他。
The dictator will likely exile his opponents soon. = 独裁者可能会很快流放他的反对者。


虚拟语气:
If I were to be exiled, I would miss my home. = 如果我被流放，我会想念我的家。
I wish I had not been exiled from my country. = 我真希望我没有被流放到我的国家之外。


被动语态:
He was exiled for treason. = 他因叛国罪被流放。
Many people were exiled during the war. = 战争期间许多人被流放。


疑问句:
Was he exiled for his beliefs? = 他是因为信仰而被流放的吗？
Why were they exiled? = 他们为什么被流放？


否定句:
He was not exiled; he chose to leave. = 他没有被流放；他是选择离开的。
They were not exiled unjustly. = 他们没有被不公平地流放。
