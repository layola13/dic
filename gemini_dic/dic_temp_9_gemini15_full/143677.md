outlaughs:/ˌaʊtˈlæfs/| v.|大声笑过；笑得比…响亮|To laugh louder or more heartily than someone else.  It implies a reaction to someone else's laughter or a situation that provokes laughter.|例句：She outlaughed him at his joke. 她被他自己的笑话逗得比他还笑得厉害。|例句：The comedian's joke was so funny that the audience outlaughed each other.|喜剧演员的笑话太好笑了，以至于观众们都互相大声笑着。|近义词：outdo, surpass, exceed|反义词：weep, cry
