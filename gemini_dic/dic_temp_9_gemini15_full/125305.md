multigrain:/ˈmʌltiɡreɪn/ | adj. | 多谷物的；多种谷物的 | Containing several types of grain.  This adjective is used to describe foods that are made with a mixture of different grains, such as wheat, oats, rye, barley, and corn.  | Example:  I prefer multigrain bread to white bread.  我喜欢多谷物面包胜过白面包。  |近义词：mixed-grain, whole-grain (in some contexts) | 反义词：single-grain, refined-grain
