job-hunts: /ˈdʒɒb hʌnts/ | n. |求职，找工作 |The act of searching for a job; the period of time spent looking for a job.  This is a compound noun, combining "job" and "hunts." It emphasizes the active and potentially extensive search involved in finding employment. |例句：My job-hunts have been unsuccessful so far. =到目前为止我的求职并不顺利。  He's been on job-hunts for months. = 他已经找工作好几个月了。|近义词：job search, employment seeking |反义词：employment, work
