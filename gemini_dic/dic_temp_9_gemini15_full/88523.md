hit-and-run:/ˈhɪt ænd rʌn/| n. adj.| 肇事逃逸；打带跑 |  A hit-and-run incident is an accident, typically a traffic accident, in which a vehicle collides with a person or another vehicle and the driver leaves the scene without providing assistance or identifying themselves.  It can also refer to any act of attacking or harming someone and then fleeing. | 例句：The police are investigating a hit-and-run accident that occurred last night.  警方正在调查昨晚发生的一起肇事逃逸事故。

The suspect was charged with hit-and-run after fleeing the scene of the accident.  嫌疑人因事故后逃逸而被指控犯有肇事逃逸罪。


近义词：肇事逃逸,  leaving the scene of an accident,  drive-by shooting (if referring to a violent act) | 反义词：remaining at the scene, taking responsibility
