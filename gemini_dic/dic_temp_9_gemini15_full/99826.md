intermingles:/ˌɪntərˈmɪŋɡəlz/| v. |混合；交错；掺杂|To mix or blend together; to become combined or associated.|The different cultures intermingled in the city's vibrant marketplace.  这座城市充满活力的市场里，不同的文化交融在一起。|The sounds of laughter and music intermingled in the air.  欢笑声和音乐声在空中交织在一起。|blend, mingle, fuse, combine|separate, segregate, isolate|动词


USAGE->

The colors intermingle beautifully in the painting. = 画中的颜色混合得非常漂亮。
The threads intermingle to create a unique texture. = 线相互交织，创造出独特的纹理。
