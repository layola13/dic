shoreward:/ˈʃɔːrwərd/| adj. adv.|向岸的，朝岸的；|Moving or directed toward the shore.  It is used to describe the direction of movement or orientation towards the coast or land from the sea or water.|例句：The boat drifted shoreward. 小船向岸漂去。

The tide is coming in shoreward. 潮水正向岸边涌来。|近义词：coastward, landward|反义词：seaward, offshore
