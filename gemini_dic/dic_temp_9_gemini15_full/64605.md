erupting:/ɪˈrʌptɪŋ/ | v. |喷发；爆发；突然发生 | The act of erupting, especially of a volcano or geyser; a sudden, violent outburst.  |例句：The volcano is erupting, sending ash and lava into the air. 火山正在喷发，向空中喷射火山灰和熔岩。

例句：The crowd erupted in cheers when their team scored.  当他们的球队得分时，人群爆发出欢呼声。


近义词：bursting, exploding, breaking out | 反义词：dormant, inactive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The volcano is erupting violently. = 火山正在猛烈喷发。
He is erupting with anger. = 他正在勃然大怒。


一般过去时:
The geyser erupted spectacularly yesterday. = 间歇泉昨天壮观地喷发了。
She erupted into laughter. = 她突然大笑起来。


现在完成时:
The volcano has erupted several times this year. = 火山今年已经喷发了几次。
He has erupted in rage at the news. = 他听到这个消息后勃然大怒。


过去完成时:
The volcano had erupted before the villagers could evacuate. = 在村民们能够撤离之前，火山已经喷发了。
She had erupted in tears before I could comfort her. = 在我能安慰她之前，她已经哭了起来。


将来时:
Scientists predict the volcano will erupt soon. = 科学家预测火山很快就会喷发。
He will erupt if you tell him the truth. = 如果你告诉他真相，他会暴跳如雷。


虚拟语气:
If the volcano erupted, the town would be destroyed. = 如果火山喷发，小镇将被摧毁。
If he erupted, I would leave. = 如果他发脾气，我就离开。


被动语态:
The city was erupted by the riot. = 城市被暴乱冲击了。(注意：被动语态用法较少见，通常用其他动词代替)
The situation was erupted into chaos. = 局势陷入混乱。(注意：被动语态用法较少见，通常用其他动词代替)


疑问句:
Is the volcano erupting? = 火山正在喷发吗？
Is he erupting with emotion? = 他是不是情绪激动？


否定句:
The volcano isn't erupting yet. = 火山还没有喷发。
He didn't erupt in anger, but remained calm. = 他并没有生气，而是保持冷静。


PHRASE->
volcanic eruption: 火山喷发
sudden eruption: 突然爆发
