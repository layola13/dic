contd:/kənˈtɪnjuːd/ | adj. |（书面语）续；接下；继续 | Continued; to be continued. This abbreviation is used to indicate that something is continued from a previous page or section.  | Example:  The story continued on the next page. (故事在下一页继续。) The meeting continued after a short break. (会议在短暂休息后继续。) |近义词：continued, following, subsequent | 反义词：ended, finished
