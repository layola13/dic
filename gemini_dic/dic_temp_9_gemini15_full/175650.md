ransomed:/ˈrænsəmd/| v. |赎回；赎买|To pay a ransom for the release of (someone or something).  This word implies the payment of money or goods to secure the release of a captive or stolen property.|The family ransomed their kidnapped son.  家人赎回了他们被绑架的儿子。|The company ransomed its stolen data from the hackers. 公司从黑客手中赎回了被盗的数据。| redeemed, recovered | enslaved, captured | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They ransom their daughter every year. = 他们每年都赎回他们的女儿。
The company ransoms its data from hackers regularly. = 这家公司定期从黑客手中赎回数据。


一般过去时:
The family ransomed their son from the kidnappers. = 这家人从绑匪手中赎回了他们的儿子。
The police ransomed the hostages. = 警方赎回了人质。


现在完成时:
They have ransomed their property multiple times. = 他们已经多次赎回了他们的财产。
The government has ransomed several citizens this year. = 政府今年已经赎回了数名公民。


过去完成时:
He had ransomed his car before the police arrived. = 他在警察到来之前已经赎回了他的车。
She had ransomed her jewels from the thieves. = 她已经从窃贼手中赎回了她的珠宝。


将来时:
They will ransom their child if he is kidnapped. = 如果他们的孩子被绑架，他们将会赎回他。
The company will ransom the data if necessary. = 必要时，公司将赎回数据。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would ransom my sister. = 如果我有钱，我会赎回我的妹妹。
If they had more time, they would have ransomed the painting. = 如果他们有更多的时间，他们本来可以赎回这幅画。


被动语态:
The hostages were ransomed by the government. = 人质被政府赎回。
The stolen artwork was ransomed after a long negotiation. = 被盗的艺术品经过长时间的谈判后被赎回。


疑问句:
Was the painting ransomed? = 这幅画被赎回了吗？
Has the kidnapped child been ransomed yet? = 被绑架的孩子已经被赎回了吗？


否定句:
They did not ransom the stolen goods. = 他们没有赎回被盗的货物。
The company refused to ransom the data to the hackers. = 公司拒绝向黑客赎回数据。


PHRASE->
