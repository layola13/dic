injectables:/ɪnˈdʒɛktəbəlz/ | n. |注射剂，可注射药物 | Medicines or other substances that are administered by injection.  Injectables are used to deliver medication directly into the bloodstream, muscles, or other tissues. They are often preferred for their rapid onset of action and high bioavailability. | Example:  Many vaccines are available as injectables. 许多疫苗以注射剂的形式提供。

The doctor prescribed injectables to manage the patient's pain.  医生开了注射剂来控制病人的疼痛。|近义词：injections, shots |反义词：orals (oral medications)
