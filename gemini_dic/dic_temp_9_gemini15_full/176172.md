re-adapted:/ˌriːˈæptɪd/| v. |重新适应，改编 | To adapt again; to modify or adjust something that has already been adapted.  This implies a further adaptation after an initial adaptation. |例句：The software was re-adapted for the new operating system.  软件为新的操作系统重新进行了适配。

例句：The play was re-adapted for a younger audience. 这部戏剧为更年轻的观众重新改编了。

近义词：readjusted, modified, revised, remade

反义词：maladapted, unadapted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company re-adapts its marketing strategies every year. = 公司每年都会重新调整其营销策略。
They re-adapt the curriculum to meet the students' needs. = 他们重新调整课程以满足学生的需求。


一般过去时:
The film was re-adapted from a novel. = 这部电影是根据一部小说重新改编的。
She re-adapted her presentation after receiving feedback. = 她收到反馈后重新调整了她的演示文稿。


现在完成时:
The team has re-adapted its approach to problem-solving. = 团队已经重新调整了解决问题的方法。
He has re-adapted to life in the city after living in the countryside. = 他在乡下生活后重新适应了城市生活。


过去完成时:
They had re-adapted the machine before the final test. = 他们在最终测试之前已经重新调整了机器。
She had re-adapted her speech several times before the conference. = 在会议之前，她已经多次重新调整了她的演讲。


将来时:
We will re-adapt the software to support the latest hardware. = 我们将重新调整软件以支持最新的硬件。
They will re-adapt their business model to compete in the new market. = 他们将重新调整他们的商业模式以在新市场中竞争。


虚拟语气:
If we re-adapted the design, it would be more efficient. = 如果我们重新调整设计，它将更有效率。
I wish I had re-adapted my strategy sooner. = 我希望我早点重新调整我的策略。


被动语态:
The plan was re-adapted to include the new data. = 该计划被重新调整以包含新的数据。
The script is being re-adapted for television. = 剧本正在被重新改编成电视剧。


疑问句:
Will they re-adapt the product for the international market? = 他们会为国际市场重新调整产品吗？
Has the company re-adapted its policies? = 公司是否已经重新调整了其政策？


否定句:
The organization did not re-adapt its structure. = 该组织没有重新调整其结构。
They will not re-adapt the system without further testing. = 在没有进一步测试的情况下，他们不会重新调整系统。


PHRASE->
re-adapted to = 重新适应于……
re-adapted for = 重新调整以适应……
re-adapted from = 根据……重新改编
re-adapted by = 由……重新改编
re-adapted version = 重新改编的版本
