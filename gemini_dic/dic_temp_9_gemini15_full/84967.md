/hæp/ | n. | 偶然发生的事，运气；运气好 |  a chance occurrence; luck; good fortune | Example:  What a happy hap! 真走运！ | 近义词：chance, accident, incident | 反义词：misfortune, ill luck


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What happens next? = 接下来的事情会发生什么？
What usually happens in this situation? =在这种情况下通常会发生什么？


一般过去时:
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？
What happened to your car? = 你的车怎么了？


现在完成时:
What has happened to him? = 他怎么了？
What has happened since I left? = 我离开后发生了什么？


过去完成时:
What had happened before the accident? = 事故发生前发生了什么？
What had happened to cause the delay? = 发生了什么导致延误？


将来时:
What will happen tomorrow? = 明天会发生什么？
What will happen if we fail? = 如果我们失败了会发生什么？


虚拟语气:
What would happen if the volcano erupted? = 如果火山爆发会发生什么？
I wish I knew what would happen next. = 我真希望知道接下来会发生什么。


被动语态:
What was happened to the painting? = 那幅画怎么了？（被动语态在此处略显不自然，通常用主动语态）
What is being happened to the environment?  (不自然，应改为：What is happening to the environment?) = 环境正在发生什么？


疑问句:
What happened? = 发生了什么事？
What will happen to us? = 我们将会怎样？


否定句:
Nothing happened. = 什么事也没发生。
Nothing happened that day. = 那天什么事也没发生。


PHRASE->
by hap = 偶然地
hap and hazard = 偶然；运气
a lucky hap = 幸运的事
a stroke of hap = 一阵好运
ill hap = 不幸
