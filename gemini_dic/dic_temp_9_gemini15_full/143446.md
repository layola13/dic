outfoot:/ˌaʊtˈfʊt/| vt.|胜过，超过|To surpass or exceed someone in performance or achievement, especially in a competition or contest.|例句：He completely outfooted his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中完全胜过了对手。|近义词：outshine, outdo, surpass, exceed |反义词：be outdone by, be surpassed by


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outfoots his rivals in every competition. = 他在每一次比赛中都胜过他的对手。
The new software outfoots the older versions in speed and efficiency. = 新软件在速度和效率上都胜过旧版本。


一般过去时:
She outfooted her classmates in the science exam. = 她在科学考试中胜过了她的同学们。
Our team outfooted the opposing team in the final match. = 我们队在决赛中胜过了对方球队。


现在完成时:
He has outfooted all his previous records in this marathon. = 他在这场马拉松比赛中打破了他以往所有的纪录。
The company has outfooted its competitors in the market share. = 这家公司在市场份额上已经超过了竞争对手。


过去完成时:
They had outfooted their expectations by achieving such a significant victory. = 他们取得如此重大的胜利，已经超出了他们的预期。
The research team had outfooted previous studies in its findings. = 研究团队的研究结果已经超过了之前的研究。


将来时:
She will outfoot her opponents with her superior skills. = 她将凭借其高超的技能胜过对手。
We believe our product will outfoot the competition in terms of innovation. = 我们相信我们的产品在创新方面将胜过竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If he trained harder, he would outfoot his rivals. = 如果他训练更刻苦，他就能胜过对手。
I wish I could outfoot my procrastination and finish this project on time. = 我希望我能克服拖延症，按时完成这个项目。


被动语态:
He was outfooted by a younger, faster runner. = 他被一位更年轻、速度更快的跑步者超越了。
The old technology was outfooted by the latest advancements. = 旧技术被最新的进步所超越。


疑问句:
Can he outfoot the current champion? = 他能战胜目前的冠军吗？
Will their new strategy outfoot the competitors' plans? = 他们的新战略能胜过竞争对手的计划吗？


否定句:
He didn't outfoot his opponent in the final round. = 他在最后一轮没有胜过对手。
The team failed to outfoot their rivals in the tournament. = 该队未能在此次比赛中胜过对手。


PHRASE->

outfoot someone in skill = 在技能上胜过某人
outfoot someone in intelligence = 在智力上胜过某人
outfoot the market = 领先市场
