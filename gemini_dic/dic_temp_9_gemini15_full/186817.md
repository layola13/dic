rine:/raɪn/| n. |雨水；雨；(尤指)小雨|a form of water that falls from the sky in drops|例句：The rine fell steadily all day.  = 雨水一整天都在持续地下着。|例句：The gentle rine soothed the parched earth. = 细雨滋润了干涸的土地。|近义词：rain, shower, drizzle|反义词：sunshine, drought


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rine falls gently. = 雨轻轻地下着。
It rains a lot in this region. = 这个地区雨水很多。

一般过去时:
The rine fell heavily last night. = 昨晚雨下得很大。
It rained cats and dogs yesterday. = 昨天下了倾盆大雨。

现在完成时:
It has rained all day. = 一整天都在下雨。
The rine has stopped. = 雨停了。

过去完成时:
It had rained for three days before the flood. = 在洪水来临之前已经下了三天雨。
They had not realized how much it had rained. = 他们没有意识到下了多少雨。

将来时:
It will rain tomorrow. = 明天会下雨。
We will go out if it doesn't rain. = 如果不下雨，我们就出去。

虚拟语气:
If it rained tomorrow, we would stay home. = 如果明天下雨，我们就待在家里。
I wish it would stop raining. = 我希望雨能停。

被动语态:
The fields were watered by the rine. = 田地被雨水浇灌了。
The crops were damaged by the heavy rine. = 庄稼被大雨破坏了。

疑问句:
Is it raining? = 下雨了吗？
Does it often rain here? = 这里经常下雨吗？

否定句:
It didn't rain yesterday. = 昨天没下雨。
It hasn't rained for weeks. = 好几个星期都没下雨了。
