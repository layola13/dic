outfox:/ˌaʊtˈfɑːks/ | vt. |智胜，以计谋胜过 | to be more clever and skillful than someone else, especially in deceiving them | 例句：The detective outfoxed the cunning criminal.  侦探智胜了狡猾的罪犯。

The clever fox outfoxed the hunter.  狡猾的狐狸智胜了猎人。


近义词：outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver | 反义词：be outfoxed by


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outfoxes his opponents in every game. 他在每一场比赛中都智胜对手。
She outfoxes her rivals with her clever strategies. 她凭借聪明的策略智胜了她的对手。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:
