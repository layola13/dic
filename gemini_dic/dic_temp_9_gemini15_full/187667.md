roofless:/ˈruːfləs/| adj.| 无屋顶的，无家可归的 | Lacking a roof; without shelter or protection.  |例句：The storm left many houses roofless.  暴风雨过后许多房屋失去了屋顶。 |例句：The homeless man was roofless and destitute.  那个无家可归的男人无家可归且一贫如洗。|近义词：unroofed, uncovered, homeless (in the context of people)|反义词：roofed, sheltered
