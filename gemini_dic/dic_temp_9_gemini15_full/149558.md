paginate:/ˈpeɪdʒɪneɪt/ | vt. |分页；将…分页|To divide (text) into pages; to number the pages of (a document or book).  The process involves determining where page breaks should occur, often based on factors like line length, margins, and column layout.  |The document needs to be paginated before printing.  这个文档需要分页后再打印。|  number, section | --- | v.
