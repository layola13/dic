minotaurs: /ˈmɪnəˌtɔːrz/ | n. | 米诺陶洛斯（希腊神话中的牛头人） |  Plural of minotaur.  A mythical creature with the head of a bull and the body of a man, confined in the Labyrinth by King Minos of Crete.  | Example: The hero Theseus slew the minotaurs in the labyrinth.  英雄忒修斯在迷宫中杀死了米诺陶洛斯。 |近义词：monster, beast |反义词：human
