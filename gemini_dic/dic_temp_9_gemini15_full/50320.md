deplorably:/dɪˈplɔːrəbli/| adj.|令人遗憾地；非常糟糕地；悲惨地 | extremely bad or unsatisfactory; in a deplorable manner.  |The conditions in the refugee camp were deplorably unsanitary.难民营的条件极其不卫生。|近义词： lamentably, regrettably, sadly, terribly, awfully |反义词： admirably, commendably, splendidly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The situation is deplorably bad. = 情况糟透了。
He behaves deplorably in public. = 他在公共场合行为极其糟糕。


一般过去时:
The building was deplorably neglected. = 这栋建筑物被令人遗憾地忽视了。
They lived in deplorably poor conditions. = 他们生活在极其贫困的条件下。


现在完成时:
The government has deplorably failed to address the issue. = 政府令人遗憾地未能解决这个问题。
Things have turned deplorably since the accident. = 事故发生后，情况变得非常糟糕。


过去完成时:
The project had been deplorably mismanaged. = 这个项目被极其糟糕地管理。
The situation had become deplorably worse before the intervention. = 在干预之前，情况已经变得非常糟糕。


将来时:
The situation will likely become deplorably worse without intervention. = 如果不干预，情况很可能会变得非常糟糕。
If we don't act now, the environment will become deplorably polluted. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，环境将会变得极其污染。


虚拟语气:
If only the situation weren't so deplorably bad. = 但愿情况不会这么糟糕。
It would be deplorable if we ignored the problem. = 如果我们忽视这个问题，那就太糟糕了。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
