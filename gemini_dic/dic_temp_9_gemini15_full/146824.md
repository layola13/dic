overimpressed:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈprɛst/| adj. |印象深刻的，过分钦佩的|Having an excessively favorable impression of someone or something; overly impressed.|He was overimpressed by the celebrity's charm and forgot to ask the important questions.|近义词：awestruck, overwhelmed, impressed|反义词： unimpressed, indifferent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is overimpressed by her achievements. = 他对她的成就印象深刻。
They are overimpressed with the new technology. = 他们对这项新技术印象深刻。


一般过去时:
I was overimpressed by the scale of the project. = 我对项目的规模印象深刻。
She was overimpressed by his confident demeanor. = 她被他自信的举止所倾倒。


现在完成时:
I have been overimpressed by many things in my life. = 我一生中被许多事情所倾倒。
We have been overimpressed by his performance. = 我们对他这次的演出印象深刻。


过去完成时:
I had been overimpressed by his previous work. = 我之前就被他的作品所倾倒。
They had been overimpressed by the initial results. = 他们对最初的结果印象深刻。


将来时:
I will be overimpressed by the view from the top of the mountain. = 我将会被山顶的景色所倾倒。
They will be overimpressed by the grandeur of the palace. = 他们将被宫殿的宏伟所倾倒。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn’t be so overimpressed by his wealth. = 如果我是你，我不会被他财富所倾倒。
I wish I weren’t so overimpressed by his flattery. = 我希望我不那么容易被他奉承的话所打动。


被动语态:
His charm was overimpressed upon the audience. = 他的魅力给观众留下了深刻的印象。（注意：被动语态在此处略显不自然，很少使用）
The audience was overimpressed by his performance. = 观众对他的表演印象深刻。(更自然的表达)


疑问句:
Are you overimpressed by his achievements? = 你对他取得的成就印象深刻吗？
Is she overimpressed by the expensive car? = 她对那辆昂贵的车印象深刻吗？


否定句:
He wasn't overimpressed by the new design. = 他对新的设计并不怎么印象深刻。
She isn't overimpressed by empty promises. = 她不会被空洞的承诺所打动。


PHRASE->
be overimpressed by/with: 对……印象深刻
feel overimpressed: 感到印象深刻
become overimpressed: 变得印象深刻
seem overimpressed: 看起来印象深刻
remain overimpressed: 保持印象深刻
