collaged:/kəˈlɑːʒd/| v.|拼贴的；构成拼贴画的 |To create a collage.  To combine disparate elements into a unified whole.|例句：She collaged various fabrics to create a unique textile art. 她用各种织物拼贴出一件独特的纺织艺术品。

例句：The artist collaged images from magazines to create a powerful statement.  艺术家将杂志上的图像拼贴起来，以表达强烈的观点。


近义词：assembled, combined, juxtaposed
反义词：separated, disintegrated, divided
词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He collages photos for his art projects. = 他用照片制作拼贴画。
She collages different textures in her artwork. = 她在她的艺术作品中拼贴不同的纹理。

一般过去时:
The artist collaged images from old newspapers. = 艺术家用旧报纸上的图片制作拼贴画。
They collaged various materials to create a three-dimensional artwork. = 他们拼贴各种材料来创作一件三维艺术品。

现在完成时:
She has collaged many pieces of art over the years. = 多年来，她已经创作了许多拼贴画。
He has collaged his memories into a visual narrative. = 他已将他的记忆拼贴成一个视觉叙事。


过去完成时:
Before the exhibition, she had collaged all the pieces. = 展览前，她已经完成了所有的拼贴作品。
They had collaged the images before adding the text. = 他们在添加文字之前已经完成了图像的拼贴。

虚拟语气:
