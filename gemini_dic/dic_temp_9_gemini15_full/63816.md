epidermises:/ˌɛpɪˈdɜːrmɪsiːz/| n. |表皮（复数）|The outer layer of skin;  the plural of epidermis.  It refers to multiple outer skin layers, potentially across different organisms or body parts.|例句：The researcher studied the epidermises of various amphibians to understand their adaptation to different environments. 研究人员研究了各种两栖动物的表皮，以了解它们对不同环境的适应性。|近义词：cuticles, outer skins |反义词：dermises (dermis is the singular)
