effervescence:/ˌɛfərˈvɛsns/| n. |兴奋；活力；冒泡|The state of bubbling or fizzing; a lively or enthusiastic state of excitement.  It describes the bubbling of a liquid due to the release of gas, or the lively and enthusiastic feeling of excitement.|例句：The effervescence of the champagne added to the celebratory mood. 香槟的泡沫增添了庆祝的气氛。

The effervescence of the crowd was infectious.  人群的兴奋劲儿是具有感染力的。|近义词：excitement, vivacity, animation, fizz, sparkle|反义词：dullness, lethargy, flatness, stagnation
