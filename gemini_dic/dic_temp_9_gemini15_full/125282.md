multifid:/ˈmʌltɪfɪd/| adj.|多裂的，多瓣的；多歧的|Having many divisions or parts; cleft or divided into many segments.  The term often describes anatomical structures.|例句：The multifid muscles of the spine are crucial for stability.  脊柱的多裂肌对于稳定性至关重要。


例句：The multifid leaf of the plant was strikingly beautiful. 植物的多裂叶片非常漂亮。


近义词：many-cleft, multipartite, dissected


反义词：undivided, simple, entire


词性:形容词
