pto:/ˈpiːtiːoʊ/|abbr.|带薪休假|Paid Time Off;  refers to time off from work that is paid for by the employer.|例句：I'm taking some PTO next week to visit my family. 我下周要请带薪假去看望家人。|近义词：vacation, leave, holiday|反义词：unpaid leave


USAGE->

He takes PTO every year. = 他每年都请带薪假。
She uses her PTO wisely. = 她明智地使用她的带薪假。

They took PTO last month. = 他们上个月请了带薪假。
He took all his PTO this year. = 他今年用完了所有的带薪假。
