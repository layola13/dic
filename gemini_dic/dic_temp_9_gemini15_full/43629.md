creatinin:/krɪˈætɪnɪn/| n. |肌酸酐|Creatinine is a waste product produced by muscles.  It is filtered out of the blood by the kidneys and excreted in urine. High levels of creatinine in the blood can indicate kidney problems.  |例句：High creatinine levels often signal kidney dysfunction. 高肌酸酐水平通常预示着肾功能障碍。

例句：The doctor ordered a blood test to check her creatinine levels. 医生下令进行血液检查以检查她的肌酸酐水平。|近义词：creatine (肌酸, chemically related but different)|反义词:|无|词性|名词|
