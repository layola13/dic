columba:/kəˈlʌmbə/| n. |鸽子|The word "columba" is the genus name for pigeons and doves.  It refers to a large group of birds characterized by their stout bodies, small heads, and cooing calls. Many species within this genus are widely distributed across the globe.  |Example:  The *Columba livia*, the rock pigeon, is a common sight in many cities.  城市里经常可以看到岩鸽（*Columba livia*）。|近义词：dove, pigeon|反义词：无|名词
