resuppression:/ˌriːsəˈprɛʃən/| n. |再压制；再次抑制|The act of suppressing something again, especially after a previous attempt at suppression has failed.  It often implies a renewed or intensified effort to control or subdue something. |例句：The resuppression of dissent was met with widespread protests. 再度压制异见引发了广泛抗议。
例句：After a brief period of freedom, the resuppression of the media was complete.  短暂的自由之后，媒体再次遭到完全压制。|近义词：suppression, repression, subdual|反义词：liberation, release, emancipation|名词
