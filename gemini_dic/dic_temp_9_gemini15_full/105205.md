keylogger:/ˈkiːlɒɡər/ | n. |键盘记录器，按键记录程序 | A keylogger is a type of surveillance technology that records every keystroke a user types on a computer or other device.  This information can include passwords, credit card numbers, and other sensitive data. Keyloggers can be hardware or software based.  | Example: The hacker installed a keylogger on the victim's computer to steal their banking information.  键盘记录器被黑客安装在受害者的电脑上以窃取其银行信息。 |近义词：spyware, monitoring software |反义词：None
