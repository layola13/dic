overviewed:/ˌoʊvərˈvjuːd/ | v. | 概述；综述；对…作概览 |  To give a general description or summary of something.  |The professor overviewed the main points of the lecture before the exam. 教授在考试前概述了讲座的主要内容。 The project was overviewed by the senior manager.  项目由高级经理进行了概述。| reviewed, summarized, surveyed | detailed, specific | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He overviews the marketing strategy every week. 他每周都会概述营销策略。
She overviews the progress of the project regularly. 她定期概述项目的进展。


一般过去时:
The CEO overviewed the company's financial performance last quarter. 首席执行官概述了公司上季度的财务业绩。
They overviewed the research findings in their presentation. 他们在演示文稿中概述了研究结果。


现在完成时:
He has overviewed the situation and decided on a course of action. 他已经概述了情况并决定采取行动。
She has overviewed all the proposals and selected the best one. 她已经概述了所有提案并选择了最佳方案。


过去完成时:
After he had overviewed the data, he presented his conclusions. 在他概述数据之后，他提出了他的结论。
They had overviewed the previous year's results before starting the new project. 在开始新项目之前，他们已经概述了去年的结果。


将来时:
She will overview the team's progress at the next meeting. 她将在下次会议上概述团队的进展。
They will overview the market trends in their upcoming report. 他们将在即将发布的报告中概述市场趋势。


虚拟语气:
If I had overviewed the contract carefully, I would have avoided this mistake. 如果我仔细阅读了合同，我就能避免这个错误。(注意：这里overviewed更常用被动语态，例如：If the contract had been overviewed carefully...)
It is important that the report be overviewed before submission.  报告在提交之前必须经过概述。(被动语态)


被动语态:
The report was overviewed by the committee.  该报告由委员会进行了概述。
The situation is being overviewed by the management team.  情况正在由管理团队进行概述。


疑问句:
Has the project been overviewed yet? 项目是否已经概述过了？
Will the findings be overviewed in the final report?  最终报告中是否会概述这些发现？


否定句:
The manager did not overview the details of the plan. 经理没有概述计划的细节。
They haven't overviewed the latest data yet. 他们还没有概述最新的数据。


PHRASE->

overview of: 对…的概述  an overview of the market 市场概述
provide an overview: 提供概述
give an overview: 给出概述
a brief overview: 简要概述
comprehensive overview: 全面概述
