rawhiding:/ˈrɔːhaɪdɪŋ/| n. |生皮处理；鞭打；严厉对待|The process of preparing rawhide, typically involving stretching and drying animal hides to make leather.  It can also refer to the act of whipping someone severely or treating someone harshly.|例句：The rancher spent hours rawhiding the hides before tanning them.  这位牧场主花了数小时处理生皮，然后才进行鞣制。

例句：The cruel overseer was known for his rawhiding of the slaves.  那个残忍的监工以严厉对待奴隶而闻名。|近义词：tanning, flogging,  bullying,  |反义词：gentle treatment, kindness, coddling|名词
