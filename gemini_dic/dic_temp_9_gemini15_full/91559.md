hyper-oxygenation:/ˌhaɪpərɒkˈsɪdʒəneɪʃən/| n.|高氧疗法，高氧血症|The process of increasing the oxygen levels in the blood or tissues above normal levels.  This can be achieved through various methods, including breathing supplemental oxygen.  It's often used in medical settings to treat conditions where oxygen levels are low.|例句：Hyper-oxygenation therapy is sometimes used to treat carbon monoxide poisoning. 高氧疗法有时用于治疗一氧化碳中毒。

例句：The diver experienced hyper-oxygenation after a deep-sea dive.  潜水员在深海潜水后经历了高氧血症。|近义词：oxygenation,  superoxygenation|反义词：hypoxia, anoxia|名词
