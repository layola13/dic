catheter:/ˈkæθɪtər/| n. |导管，导尿管|A thin, flexible tube inserted into a body cavity, duct, or vessel to permit the passage of fluids or to keep a passage open.|The doctor inserted a catheter to drain the fluid from his lungs.|近义词：tube, cannula|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The nurse inserts a catheter regularly. = 护士定期插入导管。
A catheter drains the fluid from the patient's bladder. = 导管排出病人膀胱里的液体。

一般过去时:
The surgeon used a catheter during the operation. = 外科医生在手术中使用了导管。
They inserted a catheter to relieve the blockage. = 他们插入导管以缓解阻塞。

现在完成时:
The patient has had a catheter for several days. = 病人已经放置导管几天了。
They have successfully inserted the catheter. = 他们成功地插入了导管。

过去完成时:
The doctor had already placed the catheter before the examination. = 医生在检查前已经放置了导管。
The patient had never had a catheter before. = 病人以前从未使用过导管。

将来时:
The patient will need a catheter after the surgery. = 病人在手术后需要导管。
We will insert a catheter to monitor the pressure. = 我们将插入导管来监测压力。

虚拟语气:
If I were the doctor, I would use a different type of catheter. = 如果我是医生，我会使用不同类型的导管。
It would be better if the catheter were removed sooner. = 如果导管能早点移除就好了。

被动语态:
The catheter was inserted carefully by the nurse. = 导管由护士小心地插入。
A new catheter is being used for this procedure. = 此程序正在使用新的导管。

疑问句:
What type of catheter is being used? = 使用的是哪种类型的导管？
Is a catheter necessary for this treatment? = 这种治疗需要导管吗？

否定句:
The patient does not need a catheter at this time. = 病人目前不需要导管。
They did not use a catheter during the procedure. = 他们在手术过程中没有使用导管。
