nerds:/nɜːrdz/| n. |书呆子，电脑迷|A person who is very interested in technical or academic subjects, and is often considered to be socially awkward.  Nerds are typically highly intelligent and knowledgeable in their area of expertise, but may lack social skills or have difficulty relating to people who are not as interested in the same subjects. | Example: He's a total nerd when it comes to computers; he can fix anything. 他是个电脑迷，什么电脑问题都能解决。|近义词：geek, dweeb, wonk |反义词：jocks, popular kids
