micrography:/maɪˈkrɒɡrəfi/ | n. |显微摄影术；显微镜照相术 |The art or practice of producing photographs of microscopic objects.  It involves using a microscope to magnify the object and then capturing an image using a camera.  |例句：The scientist used micrography to capture detailed images of the cells.  科学家用显微摄影术拍摄细胞的详细图像。|例句：Micrography is a crucial technique in many scientific fields. 显微摄影术是许多科学领域的关键技术。|近义词：microphotography|反义词：macrophotography
