deported:/dɪˈpɔːrtɪd/| v. |驱逐出境，递解出境 |To force someone to leave a country, typically their home country, because they have broken the law or for other reasons of national security or public interest.  |The government deported the illegal immigrants.  政府驱逐了非法移民。|  expelled, extradited | welcomed, admitted |动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government deports illegal immigrants. = 政府驱逐非法移民。
He is deported from the country. = 他被驱逐出境。


一般过去时:
They deported him last week. = 他们上周驱逐了他。
The country deported hundreds of refugees. = 该国驱逐了数百名难民。


现在完成时:
They have deported thousands of people this year. = 今年他们已经驱逐了数千人。
The authorities have deported him to his home country. = 当局已将他遣返回国。


过去完成时:
They had deported him before the news broke. = 在新闻爆出之前，他们已经将他驱逐出境了。
The judge had deported the criminal before the appeal. = 法官在申诉之前已经驱逐了罪犯。


将来时:
They will deport him next month. = 他们下个月将驱逐他。
The government will deport all undocumented workers. = 政府将驱逐所有无证工人。


虚拟语气:
If he had not committed a crime, he would not have been deported. = 如果他没有犯罪，他就不会被驱逐出境。
I wish they hadn't deported my friend. = 我真希望他们没有驱逐我的朋友。


被动语态:
He was deported for violating immigration laws. = 他因违反移民法而被驱逐出境。
Many refugees were deported back to their war-torn homeland. = 许多难民被遣返回他们饱受战争蹂躏的家乡。


疑问句:
Was he deported? = 他被驱逐了吗？
Will they be deported? = 他们会被驱逐吗？


否定句:
They did not deport him. = 他们没有驱逐他。
He was not deported. = 他没有被驱逐。
