plug-along:/plʌɡ əˈlɒŋ/ | v. | 缓慢而持续地前进；坚持不懈地做某事 | To continue steadily, though slowly and perhaps with difficulty.  It implies a lack of excitement or drama in the progress. |例句：The old car plugged along the dusty road. 这辆旧车沿着尘土飞扬的道路缓慢而持续地行驶。

The project plugged along, despite numerous setbacks.  尽管屡遭挫折，项目仍在缓慢而持续地进行。|近义词：trudge, plod, lumber, persevere |反义词：race, sprint, rush


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They plug along with their daily routines. = 他们日复一日地坚持着他们的日常工作。
The river plugs along to the sea. = 这条河缓慢地流向大海。


一般过去时:
We plugged along until we finished the task. = 我们坚持不懈地工作直到完成任务。
The hikers plugged along despite the heavy rain. = 尽管下着大雨，徒步旅行者们还是坚持前行。


现在完成时:
They have plugged along for years without a break. = 他们多年来一直坚持不懈地工作，没有休息过。
The business has plugged along, making a small profit each year. = 这家公司一直在缓慢但稳定地发展，每年都略有盈利。


过去完成时:
They had plugged along for hours before they finally reached the summit. = 他们坚持不懈地走了几个小时才最终到达山顶。
The writer had plugged along on the novel for months before he finally felt satisfied. = 这名作家在小说上苦干了几个月才最终感到满意。


将来时:
We will plug along until we reach our goal. = 我们会坚持不懈地努力直到达成目标。
The company will plug along, even in the face of competition. = 这家公司即使面对竞争，也会继续坚持下去。


虚拟语气:
If we had plugged along a little harder, we would have finished earlier. = 如果我们再努力一点，就能早点完成了。
I wish I had plugged along with my studies instead of slacking off. = 我真希望当时能坚持学习而不是偷懒。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The work was plugged along by the team.  =  这个工作由团队缓慢而持续地推进。（不自然）
The project was plugged along, despite the delays. = 项目尽管延误，还是缓慢地继续进行。（不自然，通常用主动语态）


疑问句:
Will they plug along with their project despite the difficulties? = 他们会克服困难坚持完成他们的项目吗？
How long will they plug along before they give up? = 他们会在放弃之前坚持多久？


否定句:
They didn't plug along; they rushed to finish the job. = 他们没有缓慢而持续地工作；他们匆忙完成工作。
The negotiations didn't plug along; they broke down completely. = 谈判并没有缓慢而持续地进行；它们彻底破裂了。

plug along through something = 坚持克服困难做某事
