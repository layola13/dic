nonapologetically:/ˌnɒnəpɒləˈdʒɛtɪkli/| adj.|不道歉地；理直气壮地；毫不悔改地 |Without apology; shamelessly; defiantly.  It describes an action or behavior performed without expressing or showing any remorse or regret. |例句：He nonapologetically defended his actions. 他理直气壮地为自己辩护。

例句：She nonapologetically embraced her unconventional lifestyle. 她毫不悔改地拥抱她非传统的生活方式。


近义词：unrepentantly, shamelessly, defiantly, unapologetically

反义词：apologetically, regretfully, remorsefully


词性：形容词
