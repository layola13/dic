deactivate:/ˈdiːæktɪveɪt/ | vt. |使失效；使无效；停用 |To make something inactive or inoperative.  |例句：The police deactivated the bomb before it could explode.  警方在炸弹爆炸前将其拆除。

例句：We deactivated the alarm system after we left the house. 我们离开房子后就关闭了报警系统。


近义词：disable, inactivate, turn off | 反义词：activate, enable, energize | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He deactivates his account every night. 他每天晚上都会停用他的账户。
They deactivate the security system during maintenance. 他们在维护期间会停用安全系统。


一般过去时:
She deactivated her social media profiles last week. 她上周停用了她的社交媒体账号。
The company deactivated the old software. 公司停用了旧软件。


现在完成时:
I have deactivated my old email address. 我已经停用了我的旧邮箱地址。
They have deactivated all the emergency exits. 他们已经停用了所有紧急出口。


过去完成时:
He had deactivated his credit card before he left for vacation. 他在去度假之前就已经停用了他的信用卡。
She had deactivated the account because of security concerns. 她因为安全问题已经停用了该账户。


将来时:
We will deactivate the system after the test. 我们将在测试后停用系统。
They will deactivate the account if they don't pay the bill. 如果他们不付账单，他们将停用账户。


虚拟语气:
If I deactivated my account, I wouldn't receive any more emails. 如果我停用我的账户，我就不会再收到任何邮件了。
It would be better if we deactivated the program for a while. 如果我们暂时停用该程序会更好。


被动语态:
His account was deactivated due to inactivity. 他的账户由于长期未活动而被停用。
The software was deactivated by the administrator. 该软件由管理员停用。


疑问句:
Have you deactivated your account yet? 你已经停用你的账户了吗？
When will the system be deactivated? 系统什么时候停用？


否定句:
I haven't deactivated my phone yet. 我还没有停用我的手机。
They didn't deactivate the emergency alarm. 他们没有停用紧急警报。
