clamation:/kləˈmeɪʃən/| n.| 声明；欢呼；感叹 |A clamation is a loud cry of joy or protest. It can also refer to a formal statement or declaration.  |例句：The crowd erupted in a clamation of victory.  人群爆发出一阵胜利的欢呼。  The politician issued a clamation against the new law.  这位政治家发表声明反对这项新法律。|近义词：declaration, exclamation, outcry|反义词：silence, quiet
