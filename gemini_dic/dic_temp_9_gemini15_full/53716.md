disclosure: /dɪˈskloʊʒər/ | n. | 披露；揭露；公开；泄露 | The act of making something known or public that was previously secret or unknown.  It can refer to the information itself or the act of revealing it. |例句：The disclosure of the company's financial problems led to a drop in the stock price. 公司财务问题的披露导致股价下跌。

The disclosure of classified information is a serious offense.  泄露机密信息是严重的罪行。


近义词：revelation, exposure, unveiling, divulgence | 反义词：concealment, secrecy, suppression | 词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company makes regular disclosures about its financial performance. = 公司定期披露其财务业绩。
This report discloses important findings about climate change. = 这份报告披露了关于气候变化的重要发现。


一般过去时:
The journalist disclosed the politician's secret affair. = 记者披露了这位政治家的秘密恋情。
They disclosed their plans to the public. = 他们向公众公布了他们的计划。


现在完成时:
The government has disclosed new evidence in the case. = 政府已经披露了此案的新证据。
She has disclosed all her assets to the tax authorities. = 她已向税务机关披露了她所有的资产。


过去完成时:
Before the trial, the lawyer had disclosed all the relevant documents. = 在审判之前，律师已经披露了所有相关的文件。
He had disclosed his identity to the police. = 他已经向警方透露了他的身份。


将来时:
The company will disclose its earnings next week. = 公司将于下周公布其收益。
We will disclose the results of the investigation soon. = 我们很快就会公布调查结果。


虚拟语气:
If the company had disclosed the information earlier, the damage would have been less. = 如果公司早些披露信息，损害就会小一些。
I wish they had disclosed the truth sooner. = 我希望他们早点说出真相。


被动语态:
The information was disclosed to the media. = 信息被披露给了媒体。
The details of the agreement were disclosed by the company. = 该协议的细节由公司披露。


疑问句:
Has the company made any disclosures about its new product? = 公司是否披露了关于其新产品的任何信息？
What disclosures have been made in this report? = 本报告中披露了哪些信息？


否定句:
The company did not disclose the full extent of the damage. = 公司没有披露损害的全部程度。
He refused to disclose any further information. = 他拒绝透露任何进一步的信息。



PHRASE->
