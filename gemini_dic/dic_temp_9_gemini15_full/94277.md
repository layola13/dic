impersonating:/ɪmˈpɜːrsənˌeɪtɪŋ/ | v. |假扮；模仿；扮演 | The act of pretending to be someone else;  assuming the character or appearance of another person.  It often involves mimicking their voice, mannerisms, and behavior. | 例句：He was arrested for impersonating a police officer. 他因假扮警官而被捕。

例句：The actor was brilliant at impersonating the famous singer.  这位演员模仿那位著名歌手模仿得非常出色。


近义词：imitating, mimicking, simulating, feigning, posing as


反义词：being oneself, being authentic


词性：动词(现在分词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is impersonating Elvis. = 他正在模仿猫王。
She impersonates many different characters. = 她模仿许多不同的角色。

一般过去时:
He impersonated a doctor to gain access. = 他假扮医生以获得进入许可。
She impersonated her boss on the phone. = 她打电话时假扮她的老板。

现在完成时:
He has impersonated numerous celebrities. = 他已经模仿过许多名流。
She has never impersonated anyone before. = 她以前从未模仿过任何人。

过去完成时:
He had impersonated the prime minister before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他曾假扮过总理。
She had impersonated her sister to avoid trouble. = 她曾假扮她妹妹以避免麻烦。

将来时:
