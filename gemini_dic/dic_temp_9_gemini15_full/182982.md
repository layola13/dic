replays:/ˈriːpleɪz/| n.|重播；重放；反复；回应|The act of playing something again, or a repeated performance or action.  It can also refer to responses or answers.|例句：The coach replays the game tape to analyze the team's performance. 教练重放比赛录像以分析球队的表现。

例句：He replays the scene in his mind, trying to understand what went wrong. 他在脑海中重放那一幕，试图弄明白哪里出了错。


近义词：repetition, rerun, playback, responses, answers|反义词：original, first time, premiere|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The radio replays the news every hour. = 收音机每小时重播新闻。
She replays the song repeatedly. = 她反复播放这首歌。

一般过去时:
The stadium replays the winning goal on the big screen. = 体育场在大屏幕上重播致胜进球。
He replays the argument in his head. = 他在脑子里重放那场争论。

现在完成时:
They have replayed the highlights several times. = 他们已经重播了高光片段好几次。
She has replayed that memory countless times. = 她已经无数次地回想起那段记忆。

过去完成时:
The referee had replayed the controversial call before making his decision. = 裁判在做出决定之前已经重播了有争议的判罚。
They had replayed the entire match before they found the error. = 他们在找到错误之前已经重播了整场比赛。


将来时:
They will replay the concert on television next week. = 他们下周将在电视上重播这场音乐会。
We will replay the game if there's time. = 如果有时间，我们会重播这场比赛。


被动语态:
The game was replayed due to a controversial call. = 由于一个有争议的判罚，比赛被重播了。
The video is being replayed in slow motion. = 视频正在慢动作回放。


疑问句:
Will the game be replayed? = 比赛会重播吗？
Is the song being replayed? = 这首歌正在重播吗？


否定句:
The movie wasn't replayed due to technical difficulties. = 由于技术故障，电影没有重播。
