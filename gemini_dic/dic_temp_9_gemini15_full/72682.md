foams:/foʊmz/| n. v.|泡沫；起泡沫|The word "foam" derives from Old English "fōm," meaning "foam, froth."  It's related to other Germanic words with similar meanings.|例句：The sea foams against the rocks. 海浪拍打着岩石，溅起泡沫。The soap foams easily. 这肥皂很容易起泡。|近义词：froth, lather, suds|反义词：stillness, calm
