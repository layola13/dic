quantizing:/ˈkwɒntaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 量化；量子化 |  The process of converting a continuous signal or data into a discrete, digital representation. This involves dividing the range of values into a finite number of levels or "quanta."  Quantizing is crucial in digital signal processing, image processing, and other fields where continuous data needs to be processed by digital systems. | Example:  The audio signal was quantized before being stored on the digital music player.  音频信号在存储到数字音乐播放器之前进行了量化。 |近义词：discretizing, digitizing | 反义词：continuizing
