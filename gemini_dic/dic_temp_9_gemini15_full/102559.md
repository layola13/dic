jaggeder:/ˈdʒæɡədər/ | adj. | 更参差不齐的，更锯齿状的 | More uneven and rough; having more jagged edges or points.  The path became increasingly jaggeder as we climbed higher.  | 近义词：more jagged, rougher, more uneven, serrated | 反义词：smoother, even, regular


例句：

The mountain's silhouette was jaggeder than I remembered. = 山的轮廓比我记得的更参差不齐。

The edges of the broken glass were incredibly jaggeder. = 打碎的玻璃边缘极其参差不齐。
