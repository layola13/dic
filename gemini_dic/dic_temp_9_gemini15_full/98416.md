insouciant:/ɪnˈsuːsiɑːnt/| adj. |漠不关心的；无忧无虑的；漫不经心的 |Unconcerned; carefree; nonchalant.  It suggests a casual indifference, often to things that others might find important or worrying. |例句：She had an insouciant attitude towards her studies. 她对学习采取了一种漠不关心的态度。

例句：He strolled through life with an insouciant air. 他漫不经心地走过人生。


近义词：carefree, nonchalant, unconcerned, blase, indifferent |反义词：anxious, worried, concerned, apprehensive, solicitous


词性：形容词
