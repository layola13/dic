nonshrinkable: /ˌnɒnˈʃrɪŋkəbl/ | adj. | 不缩水的；不会收缩的 | Not shrinking or likely to shrink.  This adjective describes materials or fabrics that retain their size and shape even after washing or exposure to heat. | Example:  This fabric is nonshrinkable, so you don't need to worry about it shrinking in the wash. 这块布料是不缩水的，所以你不必担心它在洗涤时会缩水。


近义词: wash-and-wear, shrink-resistant, unshrinkable

反义词: shrinkable

词性: adjective
