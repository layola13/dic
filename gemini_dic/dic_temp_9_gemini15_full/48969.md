degenerates:/dɪˈdʒɛnəreɪts/ | v. | 堕落；衰退；退化 | To decline or deteriorate in quality, character, or function; to become worse.  | The once vibrant city has degenerated into a slum.  曾经充满活力的城市已经堕落成贫民窟了。 The quality of their work has degenerated over time.  他们的工作质量随着时间的推移而下降了。 | deteriorates, declines, worsens | improves, enhances, elevates | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The building is degenerating rapidly. = 这栋建筑正在迅速恶化。
His health is degenerating. = 他的健康状况正在恶化。


一般过去时:
The empire degenerated after the death of the emperor. = 皇帝死后，帝国开始衰败。
The situation degenerated into violence. = 局势恶化成暴力冲突。


现在完成时:
His moral character has degenerated significantly. = 他的道德品质已经严重败坏。
The environment has degenerated due to pollution. = 环境由于污染而恶化。


过去完成时:
The society had degenerated long before the revolution. = 早在革命之前，社会就已经堕落了。
The relationship had degenerated to the point of no return. = 关系已经恶化到无法挽回的地步。


将来时:
The situation will degenerate further without intervention. = 如果不干预，情况将会进一步恶化。
His health will likely degenerate if he doesn't change his lifestyle. = 如果他不改变生活方式，他的健康可能会恶化。


虚拟语气:
If he continued down this path, he would degenerate completely. = 如果他继续走这条路，他将会彻底堕落。
I wish the situation hadn't degenerated so quickly. = 我希望情况没有恶化得这么快。


被动语态:
The building was allowed to degenerate. = 这栋建筑被允许荒废。
The negotiations were allowed to degenerate into a shouting match. = 谈判被允许恶化成一场争吵。


疑问句:
Is the situation degenerating? = 情况正在恶化吗？
Has his behavior degenerated over time? = 他的行为随着时间的推移而恶化了吗？


否定句:
The situation did not degenerate into violence. = 局势没有恶化成暴力冲突。
His health has not degenerated significantly yet. = 他的健康状况目前还没有严重恶化。


PHRASE->

词源:
From Latin *dēgenerāre, from dē- ("away from") + genus ("kind, race, birth").  The original meaning referred to a decline from a previous state of excellence or nobility.
