sapwoods:/ˈsæpwʊdz/ | n. | 边材 | The lighter-colored, outer wood of a tree, containing living cells and responsible for sap conduction.  It's usually softer and less durable than heartwood. | Example: The sapwood of the oak tree was lighter in color than its heartwood.  这棵橡树的边材比心材颜色浅。 |近义词：alburnum |反义词：heartwood
