preopening: /ˌpriˈoʊpənɪŋ/ | adj. | 开业前的；预开业的 | Relating to the period of time immediately before a business or event officially opens.  It often involves preparations and activities leading up to the grand opening. | 例句：The preopening preparations for the new restaurant included staff training and menu finalization.  新餐厅的开业前准备工作包括员工培训和菜单最终确定。

例句：The preopening party was a great success, attracting many potential customers. 开业前的派对非常成功，吸引了许多潜在客户。


近义词：preparatory, preliminary, antecedent

反义词：postopening, following


词性：形容词
