industrialism: /ɪnˈdʌstriəlɪzəm/ | n. | 工业化 | The social and economic system based on the development of large-scale industries.  It refers to the process of transforming an economy from primarily agrarian to one dominated by manufacturing and industry. This transformation involves technological advancements, urbanization, and shifts in social structures and relationships. | Example: The Industrial Revolution led to unprecedented industrialism in Europe.  欧洲的工业革命导致了前所未有的工业化。 | 近义词：industrialization, mechanization | 反义词：agrarianism, pre-industrialization
