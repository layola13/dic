rovers:/ˈroʊvərz/ | n. | 漫游者；流浪者；探测车 |  A rover is a person who travels from place to place, especially without a permanent home.  It can also refer to a vehicle designed to explore the surface of a planet or moon. | 例句：The Mars rover sent back stunning images of the Martian landscape.  火星探测车发回了火星景观的惊人图像。

The nomadic rovers traversed the vast desert.  这些游牧的漫游者穿越了广阔的沙漠。


近义词：wanderer, explorer, traveler, itinerant | 反义词：settler, resident
