overestimation: /ˌoʊvərˌɛstɪˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 高估，过高估计 | The act of estimating something to be greater than it actually is.  It involves judging the value, size, or importance of something to be higher than it truly is. | Example: The overestimation of the project's cost led to significant budget problems.  项目成本的高估导致了严重的预算问题。 |近义词: exaggeration, overvaluation | 反义词: underestimation, undervaluation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team's overestimation of their capabilities leads to missed deadlines. = 团队对自身能力的过高估计导致了错过最后期限。
His overestimation of the market demand results in unsold inventory. = 他对市场需求的过高估计导致了滞销库存。


一般过去时:
The overestimation of the risk caused the company to lose a lot of money. = 对风险的过高估计导致公司损失了很多钱。
Her overestimation of her own strength led to her injury. = 她对自身力量的过高估计导致了她的受伤。


现在完成时:
The repeated overestimation of project timelines has damaged our credibility. =  反复高估项目时间表已经损害了我们的信誉。
Their consistent overestimation of sales figures has misled investors. = 他们持续高估销售额误导了投资者。


过去完成时:
The failure was due to their having overestimated the resources needed. = 失败是由于他们先前高估了所需的资源。
Because they had overestimated the difficulty, they finished the task early. = 因为他们先前高估了难度，他们提前完成了任务。


将来时:
We predict that the overestimation of demand will cause a surplus. = 我们预测需求的过高估计将导致供应过剩。
The company fears that an overestimation of costs will delay the project. = 公司担心成本的过高估计会延误项目。


虚拟语气:
If they hadn't overestimated the market, they would have been successful. = 如果他们没有高估市场，他们就会成功。
I wish I hadn't overestimated my abilities. = 我真希望我没有高估我的能力。


被动语态:
The project's delay was caused by the overestimation of the time required. = 项目的延误是由对所需时间的过高估计造成的。
The losses were attributed to the overestimation of the asset value. = 损失归因于对资产价值的过高估计。


疑问句:
Was the failure due to an overestimation of the resources? = 失败是由于对资源的过高估计吗？
Could the poor results be explained by an overestimation of the potential? = 糟糕的结果能否用对潜力的过高估计来解释？


否定句:
The success of the project wasn't due to an underestimation of the difficulties, but rather a careful plan. = 项目的成功不是因为低估了困难，而是因为周密的计划。
The company avoided losses by not overestimating the market risk. = 公司通过不夸大市场风险避免了损失。
