offsetting:/ˈɒfˌsetɪŋ/| v. adj. n.|抵消，补偿；偏置；抵销项目|To counteract or compensate for (something).  It can also refer to a displacement or a balancing item in accounting.|例句：The increase in wages was offset by a rise in productivity.  加薪被生产率的提高所抵消。

The beautiful scenery offset the long journey.  美丽的风景弥补了漫长的旅程。

近义词：counterbalance, compensate, neutralize|反义词：reinforce, exacerbate, intensify|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company offsets its carbon emissions by investing in renewable energy. = 公司通过投资可再生能源来抵消其碳排放。
Rising temperatures are offset by increased cloud cover. = 气温上升被增加的云层覆盖所抵消。


一般过去时:
The tax cut offset the increase in spending. = 减税抵消了支出的增加。
They offset their losses with profits from other ventures. = 他们用其他投资的利润来抵消损失。


现在完成时:
We have offset our carbon footprint through various initiatives. = 我们通过各种举措抵消了我们的碳足迹。
The government has offset the economic downturn with stimulus packages. = 政府通过刺激方案来抵消经济衰退。


过去完成时:
They had offset the risk by diversifying their investments. = 他们通过多元化投资来抵消风险。
The company had offset its losses from the previous year. = 该公司抵消了去年的亏损。


将来时:
The new policy will offset the negative impact on the environment. = 新政策将抵消对环境的负面影响。
We will offset the cost of the project through fundraising. = 我们将通过筹款来抵消项目的成本。


虚拟语气:
If we invested in renewable energy, we could offset our carbon emissions. = 如果我们投资可再生能源，我们就能抵消我们的碳排放。
If they had acted sooner, they could have offset the damage. = 如果他们早点采取行动，他们就能减轻损失。


被动语态:
The negative effects were offset by the positive outcomes. = 负面影响被积极成果所抵消。
The cost of the project will be offset by government funding. = 项目的成本将由政府资金来支付。


疑问句:
How can we offset the impact of climate change? = 我们如何才能抵消气候变化的影响？
Does this investment strategy offset the risks involved? = 这种投资策略是否抵消了相关的风险？


否定句:
The benefits did not offset the costs of the project. = 收益没有抵消项目的成本。
The increase in revenue did not offset the decline in profits. = 收入的增加并没有抵消利润的下降。
