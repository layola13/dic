seaward:/ˈsiːwərd/| adj.|向海的；朝海的|Moving or directed towards the sea.  |例句：The ship sailed seaward.  船向大海驶去。|例句：The wind blew seaward. 风向大海吹去。|近义词：oceanward, seaward-bound|反义词：inland


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wind blows seaward. = 风向大海吹。
The birds fly seaward. = 鸟儿飞向大海。


一般过去时:
The ship sailed seaward yesterday. = 船昨天驶向大海。
The current flowed seaward. = 水流向大海流动。


现在完成时:
The tide has receded seaward. = 潮水已经退向大海。
The debris has washed seaward. = 残骸已被冲向大海。


过去完成时:
The boat had already drifted seaward before we noticed. = 我们注意到之前，船已经漂向大海了。
The fishermen had sailed seaward early that morning. = 渔民们那天早上很早就出海了。


将来时:
We will sail seaward tomorrow. = 我们明天将出海。
The storm will push the water seaward. = 暴风雨会把水推向大海。


虚拟语气:
If the wind blew seaward, the boat would drift faster. = 如果风向大海吹，船就会漂得更快。
I wish the current flowed seaward more strongly. = 我希望水流更强劲地流向大海。


被动语态:
The debris was pushed seaward by the waves. = 残骸被海浪推向大海。
The boat was carried seaward by the strong current. = 小船被强劲的洋流带向大海。


疑问句:
Does the river flow seaward? = 这条河是流向大海的吗？
Is the path seaward? = 这条路通向大海吗？


否定句:
The ship did not sail seaward. = 船没有驶向大海。
The wind was not blowing seaward. = 风没有向大海吹。
