foresay:/fɔːˈseɪ/ | vt. |预言，预示|To say or predict something before it happens.  The verb implies a prediction based on intuition, insight, or prior knowledge, rather than a purely logical deduction.|例句：The fortune teller foresaw a difficult time ahead.  算命先生预言前方会有艰难时期。

例句：Experts foresaw the economic downturn months in advance. 专家们提前几个月就预见了经济衰退。


近义词：predict, foretell, prophesy, anticipate


反义词： None directly, but words implying the opposite of prediction could be used, like disprove or refute.


词性：动词
