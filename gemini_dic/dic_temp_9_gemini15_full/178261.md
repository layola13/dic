recompose:/ˌriːkəmˈpoʊz/| vt.|重新组合；改写；重新创作|To compose again; to rearrange the parts or elements of something.|The artist recomposed the photograph to highlight the central figure.  艺术家重新组合了照片以突出中心人物。|She recomposed herself after the shock.  她受到惊吓后镇定了下来。|compose, rearrange, reconstruct|destroy, discompose, dismantle|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recomposes his thoughts before speaking. 他在说话前整理思绪。
They recompose the music for a different instrument. 他们为不同的乐器重新编排了音乐。

现在完成时:
