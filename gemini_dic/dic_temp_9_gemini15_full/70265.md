ficus:/ˈfaɪkəs/| n. |无花果树；榕树|A ficus is a genus of about 850 species of woody trees, shrubs, and vines in the family Moraceae.  They are native to tropical regions around the world and are known for their often large, broad leaves. Many species are popular houseplants or ornamental plants. |例句：The ficus tree in the courtyard provides welcome shade. 庭院里的榕树提供了令人欢迎的阴凉。|例句：He carefully pruned his indoor ficus to maintain its shape. 他仔细修剪他的室内榕树以保持其形状。|近义词：fig tree (referring specifically to the edible fig tree)|反义词：无
