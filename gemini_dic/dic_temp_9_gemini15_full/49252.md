deletions: /dɪˈliʃənz/ | n. | 删除；删节；省略 | The act of deleting something, or the things that have been deleted.  It often refers to removing parts of a text, recording, or other data.  | Example: The editor made several deletions to the manuscript before publication. 编辑在出版前对稿件做了几次删改。  The deletions in the contract were significant. 合同中的删节很重要。 | 近义词：omissions, expungements, removals | 反义词：additions, insertions |  名词


USAGE->

The editor makes deletions to improve the manuscript. = 编辑进行删除以改进稿件。
She often makes deletions in her emails before sending them. = 她经常在发送电子邮件之前进行删除。
