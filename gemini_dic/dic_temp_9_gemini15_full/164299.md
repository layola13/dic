preediting: /ˌpriːˈɛdɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预编辑；事前编辑 | The process of editing a text or other media before it is finalized or released.  It often involves checking for errors, inconsistencies, and improving clarity and style. | Example:  Preediting the manuscript saved the publisher considerable time and money later.  预编辑稿件为出版商节省了大量的时间和金钱。 | 近义词：proofreading, copyediting, reviewing | 反义词：post-editing
