pityriasis:/ˌpɪtɪˈraɪəsɪs/| n. |糠疹，皮屑过多症 |A skin condition characterized by scaling and flaking of the skin.  It can affect various parts of the body and have different causes.  The most common type is pityriasis rosea, which often presents as a characteristic "herald patch" followed by a widespread rash.|The doctor diagnosed her with pityriasis rosea. 医生诊断她患有玫瑰糠疹。|  eczema, psoriasis |healthy skin | 名词
