invents:/ɪnˈvɛnts/| vt.|发明，创造，虚构|To create or produce (something, typically an invention) for the first time.|例句：He invented a new type of engine. 他发明了一种新型发动机。|例句：She invented a story to explain her lateness. 她编造了一个故事来解释她迟到的原因。|近义词：create, devise, fabricate|反义词:destroy, abolish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He invents new gadgets every year. = 他每年都发明新的装置。
Scientists invent new medicines to fight diseases. = 科学家发明新药来对抗疾病。

一般过去时:
Edison invented the light bulb. = 爱迪生发明了电灯泡。
They invented a new method to solve the problem. = 他们发明了一种新的方法来解决这个问题。

现在完成时:
She has invented several successful products. = 她已经发明了几种成功的产品。
Scientists have invented many life-saving drugs. = 科学家们发明了许多救命的药物。

过去完成时:
Before the war, they had invented a new type of weapon. = 在战争爆发前，他们已经发明了一种新型武器。
He had invented a clever solution before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经想出了一个巧妙的解决办法。

将来时:
They will invent more efficient energy sources in the future. = 他们将来会发明更高效的能源。
I believe they will invent a cure for cancer soon. = 我相信他们很快就会发明出治疗癌症的方法。

虚拟语气:
If I had invented that machine, I would be rich. = 如果我发明了那台机器，我就富有了。
I wish I had invented the iPhone. = 我真希望是我发明了iPhone。

被动语态:
The telephone was invented by Alexander Graham Bell. = 电话是由亚历山大·格雷厄姆·贝尔发明的。
A new vaccine is being invented to combat the virus. = 正在发明一种新的疫苗来对抗这种病毒。

疑问句:
Who invented the printing press? = 谁发明了印刷机？
What did he invent that made him famous? = 他发明了什么东西使他成名？

否定句:
They didn't invent anything new this year. = 他们今年没有发明任何新东西。
He didn't invent the theory, he only improved it. = 他并没有发明这个理论，他只是改进了一下。
