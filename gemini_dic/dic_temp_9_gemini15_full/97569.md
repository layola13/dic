ingrowth:/ˈɪŋɡroʊθ/| n.|内生；内长；向内生长|The inward growth of something, especially tissue or a plant.|例句：The ingrowth of scar tissue limited the range of motion in his knee.  他的膝盖疤痕组织的内生限制了他的活动范围。

例句：The rapid ingrowth of weeds choked the young seedlings.  杂草迅速的内生扼杀了幼苗。

近义词：inward growth, internal growth|反义词：outgrowth

词性:名词
