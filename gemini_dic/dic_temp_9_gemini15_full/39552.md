consciencelessness:/ˈkɒnʃənsləsnəs/| n. | 没有人性；无情；良心丧失 | The complete absence of conscience; lack of moral sense; ruthlessness.  It refers to a state of being devoid of moral scruples or a sense of right and wrong.  | Example: The consciencelessness of the dictator was evident in his brutal treatment of his people. 独裁者的冷酷无情体现在他对人民的残酷统治中。  The sheer consciencelessness of their actions shocked everyone. 他们行为的无情令人震惊。|近义词：heartlessness, ruthlessness, remorselessness, callousness |反义词：conscientiousness, morality, scrupulousness
