incertainty: /ˌʌnˈsɜːtənti/ | n. | 不确定性，不确定的事物 | The state of being uncertain; doubt.  It refers to a lack of sureness or confidence about something.  This could be about facts, future events, or one's own abilities. | Example: The incertainty of the future weighed heavily on her mind. 未来充满了不确定性，这让她忧心忡忡。

Example: There's a great deal of incertainty surrounding the election results. 大选结果充满了不确定性。


近义词: doubt, uncertainty, precariousness, ambiguity, vagueness,  indeterminacy

反义词: certainty, sureness, confidence, assurance
