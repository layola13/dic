medaillons:/ˌmɛdəˈlʌnz/| n. |小圆肉饼，奖章 |Small, flat, round pieces of meat, often served as a main course.  The word can also refer to medallions, which are small decorative metal disks. |Example: The restaurant is famous for its delicious chicken medaillons. 这家餐馆以其美味的鸡肉小圆肉饼而闻名。|近义词：medallions, medallions of meat |反义词：无

词源:
The word "medaillon" is derived from the French word "médaillon," which ultimately comes from the Old French "medaillon," meaning "small medal."  The culinary use of the word likely emerged due to the round, flat shape of the meat, resembling a medal.
