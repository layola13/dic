pallial:/ˈpæliəl/| adj.|外套的；边缘的；边缘区的|Relating to or characteristic of a pallium (a mantle or cloak, especially in mollusks).  It often refers to the mantle of a mollusk, the fold of tissue that encloses the visceral mass. |例句：The pallial line on the shell marks the extent of the mantle.  壳上的外套线标志着外套膜的范围。|例句：The pallial cavity houses the gills and other organs. 外套腔容纳着鳃和其他器官。|近义词：mantle, marginal|反义词：central, visceral
