rice:/raɪs/| n. |米饭；稻米|Grains of the cereal plant Oryza sativa, used as food.  The word is derived from Old French "ris," ultimately from the Italic languages.|例句：We had rice and beans for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了米饭和豆子。

例句：I prefer brown rice to white rice. 我更喜欢糙米而不是白米。

近义词：grain, cereal |反义词：无
