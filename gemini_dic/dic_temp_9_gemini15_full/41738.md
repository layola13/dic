corrosively:/kəˈroʊsɪvli/ | adv. | 腐蚀性地；苛刻地 | In a corrosive manner; capable of causing corrosion or deterioration.  It describes something that causes gradual damage or destruction, either physically or emotionally. |例句：The acid corrosively ate away at the metal.  酸腐蚀性地侵蚀着金属。

The critic's review was corrosively sarcastic. 批评家的评论尖刻讽刺。|近义词：destructively,caustically,bitterly,sharply |反义词：gently, kindly, soothingly

词源:
