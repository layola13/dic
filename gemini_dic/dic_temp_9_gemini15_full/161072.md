postcritical:/ˌpoʊstˈkrɪtɪkəl/| adj.|后批判的；后批评的；超越批判的|Postcritical theory moves beyond the limitations of traditional critical theory, exploring new ways of understanding power, knowledge, and subjectivity.|例句：His work is considered postcritical because it challenges the very foundations of traditional critical analysis. 他的作品被认为是后批判的，因为它挑战了传统批判分析的根本基础。  The postcritical approach to literature avoids binary oppositions.  后批判的文学方法避免了二元对立。|近义词：transcritical, metacritical|反义词：critical, traditional
