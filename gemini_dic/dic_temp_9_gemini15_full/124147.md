mortifyingly:/ˈmɔːrtɪfaɪɪŋli/ | adv. |令人羞愧地，令人尴尬地 | Extremely embarrassing; causing intense shame or humiliation.  | 例句：Mortifyingly, I tripped and fell in front of everyone.  令人羞愧的是，我在大家面前绊倒了。

例句：He mortifyingly forgot his wife's birthday. 他令人尴尬地忘记了他妻子的生日。


近义词：embarrassingly, humiliatingly, shamefully, ridiculously

反义词：gloriously, triumphantly, proudly

词性：副词
