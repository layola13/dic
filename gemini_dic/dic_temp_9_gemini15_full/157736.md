placarded:/pləˈkɑːrdɪd/| v. |张贴海报；用海报装饰|To display or announce something by means of a placard or poster.|The walls were placarded with election posters. 墙上贴满了竞选海报。|近义词：postered, advertised, displayed|反义词：removed, erased


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They placard the town with political slogans. = 他们用政治标语张贴全镇。
The shop placards its daily specials. = 这家商店张贴每日特价商品。


一般过去时:
The students placarded the campus with protest signs. = 学生们在校园里张贴抗议标语。
He placarded the building with his artwork. = 他用他的艺术作品装饰了这栋建筑物。


现在完成时:
They have placarded the entire city with their advertisements. = 他们已经在整个城市张贴了他们的广告。
The organizers have placarded the event venue with information posters. = 组织者已经在活动场地张贴了信息海报。


过去完成时:
They had placarded the building before the demonstration began. = 示威活动开始前，他们已经在建筑物上张贴了海报。
She had placarded the notice board with important announcements. = 她已经在布告栏上张贴了重要的通知。


将来时:
They will placard the streets with campaign posters. = 他们将在街道上张贴竞选海报。
The company will placard the new product launch in all major cities. = 公司将在所有主要城市张贴新产品发布的海报。


虚拟语气:
If they placarded the town more effectively, more people would know about the event. = 如果他们更有效地张贴海报，更多的人就会知道这个活动。
I wish they had placarded the instructions more clearly. = 我希望他们能更清晰地张贴说明。


被动语态:
The walls were placarded with graffiti. = 墙壁上贴满了涂鸦。
The announcement was placarded throughout the building. = 通告张贴在整个建筑物里。


疑问句:
Were the posters placarded legally? = 海报张贴合法吗？
Have the notices been placarded on all the bulletin boards? = 通知都张贴在所有布告栏上了吗？


否定句:
They didn't placard the building with any warnings. = 他们没有在建筑物上张贴任何警告。
The organizers didn't placard the necessary information. = 组织者没有张贴必要的信息。


PHRASE->
placarded notices = 张贴的通知
placarded walls = 布满海报的墙壁
publicly placarded = 公开张贴
placarded announcements = 张贴的公告
illegally placarded = 非法张贴

词源:
From placard (noun) + -ed (past participle suffix).  The noun "placard" ultimately derives from the Old French word *placard*, meaning "a notice, poster,"  itself possibly related to the Old French word *placier*, "to place."
