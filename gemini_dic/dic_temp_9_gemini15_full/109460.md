lepidopter:/ˌlɛpɪˈdɒptər/| n. |鳞翅目昆虫|The study of lepidoptera is a fascinating branch of entomology.  |近义词：butterfly, moth (depending on context)|反义词：无


英文解析:Lepidoptera is an order of insects that includes butterflies and moths.  They are characterized by their scales on their wings, which give them their characteristic colors and patterns.  The word itself comes from the Greek words "lepis" (scale) and "pteron" (wing).
