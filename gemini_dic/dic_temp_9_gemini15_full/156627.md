pictograph:/ˈpɪktoʊɡræf/| n. | 象形文字；图象；图表 | A pictograph is a pictorial symbol or image used to represent a word, phrase, or idea.  It is a form of writing that uses pictures to convey meaning, often used in early writing systems.  Modern pictographs are frequently used in signs and symbols for easy understanding, regardless of language. | Example:  The ancient Egyptians used pictographs extensively in their writing system.  古代埃及人广泛使用象形文字。 The road sign used a pictograph of a car to indicate a parking lot. 路牌用汽车的象形图来表示停车场。 |近义词： ideogram, symbol, pictogram | 反义词：alphabet(文字系统)
