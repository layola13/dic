champions:/ˈtʃæmpiənz/| n.|冠军，优胜者|A champion is a person or team that has won a competition, especially in sports.|例句：She is a world champion in swimming. 她是一位世界游泳冠军。|例句：Our team are champions! 我们的团队是冠军！|近义词：winner, victor, titleholder |反义词：loser


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He champions human rights. 他捍卫人权。
They champion the cause of the poor. 他们拥护穷人的事业。

一般过去时:
She championed the underdog. 她支持弱者。
He championed the rights of the oppressed. 他为受压迫者的权利辩护。

现在完成时:
She has championed environmental protection for many years. 她多年来一直倡导环境保护。
They have championed this cause since the beginning. 他们从一开始就支持这一事业。

过去完成时:
He had championed the cause of education before he became president. 他在当总统之前就一直倡导教育事业。
They had championed the rights of workers for decades. 他们几十年来一直为工人的权利而斗争。

将来时:
She will champion the cause of social justice. 她将为社会正义而奋斗。
They will champion the rights of the marginalized. 他们将捍卫边缘群体的权利。

虚拟语气:
If she were a champion, she would be very proud. 如果她是一位冠军，她会非常自豪。
If they had championed that cause earlier, things might have been different. 如果他们早些时候支持那项事业，情况可能会有所不同。

被动语态:
The cause was championed by many activists. 这项事业得到了许多活动家的支持。
His ideas were championed by his colleagues. 他的想法得到了同事的支持。

疑问句:
Who champions this cause? 谁支持这项事业？
Does she champion the rights of women? 她是否捍卫妇女的权利？

否定句:
He does not champion violence. 他不支持暴力。
They did not champion the views of the opposition. 他们不支持反对派的观点。


PHRASE->
champion of:  …的拥护者
champion for: 为…而斗争
champion against: 反对…
champion the cause of: 为…的事业而奋斗
a champion athlete: 一位冠军运动员
