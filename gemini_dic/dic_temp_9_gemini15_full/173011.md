quarantined:/ˈkwɒrəntiːnd/ | /ˈkwɔːrəntiːnd/ | adj. |隔离的，检疫的 |  Placed in quarantine; isolated to prevent the spread of disease.  |例句：The infected patient was quarantined in a special ward. 受感染的病人被隔离在一个特别的病房里。 |例句：The entire village was quarantined after the outbreak of the disease.  疾病爆发后，整个村庄都被隔离了。|近义词：isolated, isolated, confined |反义词：free, released


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The city is quarantined due to the virus outbreak. = 由于病毒爆发，这座城市处于隔离状态。
He is quarantined because he showed symptoms. = 因为他出现了症状，他被隔离了。


一般过去时:
The ship was quarantined for several weeks. = 这艘船被隔离了几周。
They were quarantined after returning from the affected area. = 他们从疫区回来后被隔离了。


现在完成时:
The town has been quarantined since last month. = 自从上个月以来，这个城镇一直处于隔离状态。
She has been quarantined for two weeks now. = 她现在已经被隔离了两周了。


过去完成时:
The passengers had been quarantined before they were allowed to disembark. = 乘客在获准下船之前已被隔离。
The area had been quarantined for a month before the outbreak was contained. = 在疫情得到控制之前，该地区已经被隔离了一个月。


将来时:
The suspected cases will be quarantined immediately. = 疑似病例将立即被隔离。
The government will quarantine anyone arriving from the affected region. = 政府将隔离任何来自疫区的人。


虚拟语气:
If he had been quarantined earlier, the spread of the disease might have been prevented. = 如果他早些被隔离，疾病的传播也许就能得到预防。
If I were quarantined, I would miss my family. = 如果我被隔离，我会想念我的家人。


被动语态:
The infected animals were quarantined to prevent the disease from spreading. = 受感染的动物被隔离以防止疾病传播。
The area is being quarantined as a precaution. = 作为预防措施，该地区正在被隔离。


疑问句:
Is the entire city quarantined? = 整个城市都被隔离了吗？
Has he been quarantined yet? = 他已经被隔离了吗？


否定句:
The patient was not quarantined in time. = 病人没有及时被隔离。
They were not quarantined despite showing symptoms. = 尽管他们表现出症状，但他们没有被隔离。
