self-tolerantly:/ˌsɛlfˈtɑːlərəntli/| adv.|  自我宽容地；宽容地对待自己|  The adverb "self-tolerantly" describes an action or state characterized by accepting one's own flaws and imperfections without excessive self-criticism. It suggests a degree of self-acceptance and forgiveness.  | 例句：He self-tolerantly forgave himself for the mistake, learning from it instead of dwelling on it. 他自我宽容地原谅了自己的错误，从中吸取教训而不是沉溺于错误之中。

She reacted self-tolerantly to the criticism, understanding that not everyone would agree with her. 她宽容地对待批评，理解并非每个人都会同意她的观点。| 近义词：forgivingly, indulgently, leniently| 反义词：self-critically, harshly, severely| 副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't have tenses like verbs.  Its usage depends on the verb it modifies.  Examples show its use in different sentence structures.

Example 1 (Simple Sentence): He responded self-tolerantly to the setback. 他宽容地回应了挫折。

Example 2 (Complex Sentence):  Although she made a mistake, she handled it self-tolerantly, focusing on improvement. 虽然她犯了一个错误，但她宽容地处理了它，专注于改进。

Example 3 (Sentence with an adverbial clause):  Knowing her flaws, she acted self-tolerantly towards herself.  了解自己的缺点后，她宽容地对待自己。

词源:

The word is a combination of:

* **self-**:  Prefix indicating something related to oneself.
* **tolerant**:  Adjective meaning showing tolerance or acceptance.
* **-ly**:  Suffix turning an adjective into an adverb.

Therefore, "self-tolerantly" literally means "in a manner that is tolerant of oneself."
