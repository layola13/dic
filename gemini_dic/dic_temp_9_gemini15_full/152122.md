pathologist:/pəˈθɒlədʒɪst/| n. |病理学家|A pathologist is a doctor who studies the nature of diseases and their causes, especially by examining tissues and body fluids.|例句：The pathologist examined the tissue samples to determine the cause of death. 病理学家检查了组织样本以确定死因。|近义词：physician, doctor, medical examiner|反义词：N/A
