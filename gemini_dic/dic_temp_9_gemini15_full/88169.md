hillstone:/ˈhɪlstoʊn/| n. |山石，（尤指）用于建筑的石头|A hillstone is a stone found on a hill, often used in construction or as a building material.  Hillstones can vary greatly in size, shape, and type depending on the geological composition of the hill.|The old farmhouse was built from local hillstone.|近义词：rock, boulder, stone|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "sand" or "soil".
