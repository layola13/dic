emote:/ɪˈmoʊt/| vt.|表达（情感）|To express (an emotion) strongly.|He emoted his grief openly. 他公开表达了他的悲伤。|express, convey, show|suppress, hide, conceal|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He emotes his anger through loud shouting. = 他通过大声喊叫来表达他的愤怒。
She emotes her joy through song and dance. = 她通过唱歌跳舞来表达她的快乐。

一般过去时:
The actor emoted sadness convincingly. = 这位演员令人信服地表达了悲伤。
She emoted her frustration to her friend. = 她向朋友表达了她的沮丧。

现在完成时:
He has emoted his feelings throughout the entire performance. = 他在整场演出中都表达了他的情感。
She has emoted her love for her family in many ways. = 她以多种方式表达了她对家人的爱。

过去完成时:
He had emoted his disappointment before the results were announced. = 在结果公布之前，他已经表达了他的失望。
She had emoted her fear to her parents before the trip. = 在旅行之前，她已经向父母表达了她的恐惧。

虚拟语气:
