duke-it-out: /dʒuːk ɪt aʊt/ | v. | 决斗；激烈争斗；奋力拼搏 | To fight or struggle fiercely; to engage in a prolonged and intense conflict. | 例句：The two rivals decided to duke it out in the boxing ring. = 这两位对手决定在拳击台上决一胜负。

例句：The two teams duked it out on the basketball court for the championship. = 这两支球队为了冠军在篮球场上激烈争夺。


近义词：fight, battle, struggle, contend, grapple


反义词：cooperate, collaborate, compromise, concede


词性: 动词
