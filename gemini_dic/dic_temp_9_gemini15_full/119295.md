micro-evolutionary:/ˌmaɪkroʊˌɛvəˈluːʃənɛri/ | adj. | 微观进化论的；微进化论的 | Relating to or characterized by microevolution, the changes in allele frequencies that occur within a species or population over relatively short periods of time.  | Example:  The study focused on micro-evolutionary changes in the insect population.  这项研究集中在昆虫种群的微观进化变化上。 |近义词：minor evolutionary, small-scale evolutionary |反义词：macro-evolutionary
