词源:
The word "overglut" is a combination of "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessive") and "glut" (meaning "an excessive supply").  "Glut" itself comes from the Middle English word "glutten," meaning "to swallow greedily."  Thus, "overglut" literally means "an excessively greedy supply."
