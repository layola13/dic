frisking:/ˈfrɪskɪŋ/| n. v.|搜身；轻快地跑；|The act of searching someone's body for weapons or contraband.|例句：The police officer frisked the suspect before placing him in the police car. 警官在把嫌疑犯送进警车之前对其进行了搜身。|近义词：patting down, searching|反义词：None
