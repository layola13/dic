cutgrass:/ˈkʌtˌɡræs/| n.|割草；杂草|Cutgrass refers to various grasses that are cut or mowed, often implying a relatively short, unkempt growth.  It can also refer to the act of cutting grass.|例句：The cutgrass in the field was knee-high.  田里的割草齐膝高。|例句：We spent the afternoon cutting the cutgrass. 我们下午花了时间割草。|近义词：grass, weeds, turf|反义词：None specifically, but  "long grass" or "uncut grass" could be considered opposites.|名词
