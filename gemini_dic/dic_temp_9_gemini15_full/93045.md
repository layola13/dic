idly:/ˈaɪdli/| adv.|懒散地；无所事事地；缓慢地|In an idle manner; lazily; without purpose or occupation.|例句：He idly flipped through the channels. 他懒洋洋地翻看着频道。|例句：She idly watched the children play. 她懒洋洋地看着孩子们玩耍。|近义词：lazily, sluggishly, listlessly|反义词：busily, actively, diligently|副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They idly chat. = 他们闲聊。
He idly watches TV. = 他懒洋洋地看电视。

一般过去时:
She idly wandered through the park. = 她漫无目的地在公园里闲逛。
They idly discussed the weather. = 他们漫不经心地讨论天气。

现在完成时:
We have idly spent the afternoon. = 我们悠闲地度过了下午。
He has idly dreamed of travel. = 他一直漫不经心地想着旅行。

过去完成时:
They had idly hoped for a miracle. = 他们曾漫不经心地希望出现奇迹。
She had idly imagined a different life. = 她曾漫不经心地想象过不同的生活。

将来时:
I will idly sit and watch the sunset. = 我将悠闲地坐着观看日落。
They will idly spend their vacation. = 他们将无所事事地度过假期。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would idly spend it reading. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会悠闲地用来阅读。
I wish I could idly relax on the beach. = 我希望我能悠闲地躺在沙滩上放松。

被动语态:  (Idly is an adverb, it doesn't usually participate in passive voice constructions directly.)
The time was idly spent. = 时间被懒散地消磨掉了。(This is a less common usage)
The afternoon was idly passed. = 下午被懒洋洋地度过了。(This is a less common usage)


疑问句:
Did he idly wait for the bus? = 他是不是懒洋洋地等公交车？
Does she idly daydream during class? = 她上课时是不是漫不经心地做白日梦？


否定句:
They didn't idly stand by. = 他们没有袖手旁观。
She didn't idly ignore his pleas. = 她并没有无视他的请求。


PHRASE->
