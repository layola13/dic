crocodiles:/ˈkrɒkədaɪlz/ | n. |鳄鱼 |Large reptiles that live in rivers and swamps. They are known for their powerful jaws and sharp teeth.  |Example: Crocodiles are ambush predators, waiting patiently for their prey. 鳄鱼是伏击性捕食者，耐心地等待猎物。|近义词：alligators (but with key differences in snout shape and habitat) |反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting small, harmless animals could be considered antonyms in a broad sense.
