giggliest:/ˈɡɪɡliɪst/| adj. |最爱咯咯笑的；最爱傻笑的 |Most prone to giggling; exhibiting the most giggling.  It is the superlative form of "giggly".|例句：The giggliest child in the class kept disrupting the lesson.  = 班上最爱咯咯笑的孩子一直在扰乱课堂。The giggliest moment of the evening was when the birthday cake collapsed. = 晚上最让人傻笑的一刻是生日蛋糕倒塌的时候。|近义词：giggly, chuckling, tittering |反义词：serious, solemn, stoic |
