frescos:/ˈfrɛskoʊz/| n.|壁画|Frescoes are paintings done on fresh, wet plaster.  They are a type of mural painting.  The pigments are absorbed into the plaster, creating a durable and long-lasting image.|例句：The Sistine Chapel is famous for its breathtaking frescoes.  西斯廷教堂以其令人叹为观止的壁画而闻名。The ancient Roman frescoes are well-preserved. 古罗马壁画保存完好。|近义词：murals, paintings|反义词：N/A|名词
