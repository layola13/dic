foolishly:/ˈfuːlɪʃli/| adv. |愚蠢地，傻傻地 |in a foolish or unwise manner;  showing a lack of good sense or judgment. |例句：He foolishly believed her lies. 他愚蠢地相信了她的谎话。

例句：She foolishly spent all her savings. 她愚蠢地花光了所有的积蓄。

近义词：stupidly, unwisely, senselessly, absurdly

反义词：wisely, cleverly, intelligently, shrewdly
