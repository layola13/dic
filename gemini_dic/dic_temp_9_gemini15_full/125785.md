muriated:/ˈmjʊəriˌeɪtɪd/ | adj. |含盐的；用盐腌制的| Containing or treated with salt, especially common salt (sodium chloride).  The term often implies a process of salting or treating with brine.  It's an older, less common term now largely replaced by more specific terms. | Example: The muriated beef was preserved for a long journey. 这腌制的牛肉是为长途旅行而保存的。 |近义词：salted, brined, chlorinated (in some contexts)|反义词： unsalted, fresh
