reattempting:/ˌriːəˈtɛmptɪŋ/| v.|再次尝试，重新尝试 | Trying again; making another attempt. |例句：After failing the first time, she is reattempting the exam.  第一次考试失败后，她正在再次尝试。|例句：He's reattempting to climb Mount Everest after his previous unsuccessful attempt. 他在上次尝试失败后，正试图再次攀登珠穆朗玛峰。|近义词：retrying, trying again, making another attempt |反义词：giving up, abandoning


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reattempting the experiment. = 他们正在重新尝试实验。
She reattempts the puzzle every day. = 她每天都重新尝试解这个谜题。


一般过去时:
He reattempted the jump after a short break. = 短暂休息后，他又尝试了跳跃。
They reattempted the project with a new strategy. = 他们用新的策略重新尝试了这个项目。


现在完成时:
I have reattempted the task several times. = 我已经尝试过这项任务好几次了。
She has reattempted to contact him, but without success. = 她试图再次联系他，但没有成功。


过去完成时:
He had reattempted the climb before giving up completely. = 他在彻底放弃之前已经再次尝试过攀登。
They had reattempted the negotiation several times before reaching an agreement. = 在达成协议之前，他们已经多次重新谈判。


将来时:
We will reattempt the mission next week. = 我们下周将再次尝试这项任务。
He will reattempt the test after studying harder. = 他在更加努力学习后将再次参加考试。


虚拟语气:
If I had known better, I would have reattempted the shot. = 如果我早知道，我会再次尝试投篮。
I wish I had reattempted the interview. = 我真希望我再次尝试了面试。


被动语态:

否定句:
