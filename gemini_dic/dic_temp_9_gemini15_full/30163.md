celebrating:/ˈsɛləˌbreɪtɪŋ/| v. |庆祝；赞美|The act of expressing happiness or joy, usually for a special occasion or achievement.  It involves showing appreciation and marking a significant event.|例句：We are celebrating our anniversary this weekend. = 我们这个周末庆祝我们的结婚纪念日。
例句：The whole city is celebrating the team's victory. = 全城都在庆祝球队的胜利。|近义词：commemorating, observing, marking|反义词：mourning, grieving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We celebrate Christmas every year. = 我们每年都庆祝圣诞节。
They celebrate their birthdays with a party. = 他们用派对庆祝他们的生日。

一般过去时:
We celebrated our wedding last year. = 我们去年庆祝了我们的婚礼。
They celebrated their graduation with a big feast. = 他们以盛大的宴会庆祝他们的毕业。

现在完成时:
We have celebrated many holidays together. = 我们一起庆祝过许多节日。
She has celebrated her success with a new car. = 她用一辆新车庆祝她的成功。

过去完成时:
We had celebrated New Year's Eve before the power went out. = 在停电之前，我们已经庆祝了除夕。
They had celebrated their engagement before announcing it publicly. = 他们在公开宣布之前就已经庆祝了他们的订婚。

将来时:
We will celebrate our daughter's birthday next month. = 我们下个月将庆祝我们女儿的生日。
They will celebrate their anniversary in Paris. = 他们将在巴黎庆祝他们的结婚纪念日。

虚拟语气:
If we won the lottery, we would celebrate wildly. = 如果我们中了彩票，我们会疯狂庆祝。
I wish I could celebrate with them in person. = 我希望我能亲自与他们一起庆祝。

被动语态:
The victory was celebrated throughout the nation. = 这次胜利在全国各地得到庆祝。
The anniversary is being celebrated with a grand ball. = 周年纪念日正在以盛大的舞会庆祝。

疑问句:

What are they celebrating today? = 他们今天庆祝什么？

否定句:
