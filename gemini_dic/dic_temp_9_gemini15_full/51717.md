devisions:/dɪˈvɪʒənz/ | n. |部门；分割；划分 | Divisions are the separate parts of a larger organization or group.  |例句：The company is divided into several divisions, each with its own responsibilities.  这家公司被分为几个部门，每个部门都有其自身的职责。

The marketing division launched a new campaign. 市场部启动了一个新活动。|近义词：sections, departments, branches, segments |反义词：unification, merger, consolidation |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company has many divisions. = 这家公司有很多部门。
The project is divided into three divisions. = 这个项目被分为三个部分。


一般过去时:
The company reorganized its divisions last year. = 公司去年重组了其部门。
They divided the land into several divisions. = 他们把土地分成了好几个部分。


现在完成时:
The company has created several new divisions this year. = 公司今年创建了几个新的部门。
They have divided the work among different divisions. = 他们已经将工作分配给了不同的部门。


过去完成时:
The project had already been divided into several divisions before the meeting. = 在会议之前，项目已经被分成了几个部分。
They had completed the division of tasks among the divisions before the deadline. = 他们早在截止日期之前就完成了各部门的任务分配。


将来时:
The company will create new divisions next year. = 公司明年将创建新的部门。
They will divide the profits among the divisions according to their contributions. = 他们将根据各部门的贡献分配利润。


虚拟语气:
If the company had more divisions, it could handle more projects. = 如果公司有更多部门，它就能处理更多项目。
I wish the divisions were more cooperative. = 我希望各部门更能互相合作。


被动语态:
The work was divided equally among the divisions. = 工作被平均分配到各个部门。
The new policy will be implemented across all divisions. = 新政策将在所有部门实施。


疑问句:
How many divisions does the company have? = 这家公司有多少部门？
Into how many divisions was the project divided? = 项目被分成了几个部分？


否定句:
The company does not have many divisions. = 这家公司没有很多部门。
The work was not evenly divided among the divisions. = 工作没有平均分配到各个部门。
