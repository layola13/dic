fitly:/ˈfɪtli/| adv.|适当地，恰当地，恰如其分地 | suitably; appropriately; in a fitting manner.  It describes something that is appropriate or suitable for a particular situation or purpose. | 例句：The music fitly accompanied the dance.  音乐恰当地伴奏舞蹈。


例句：He fitly responded to the criticism with a calm explanation. 他恰当地用平静的解释回应了批评。


近义词：appropriately, suitably, properly, aptly, becomingly


反义词：inappropriately, unsuitably, improperly, ineptly


词性:副词
