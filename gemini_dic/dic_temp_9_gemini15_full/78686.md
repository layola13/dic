gig's:/ɡɪɡz/ | n. |演出，工作；活儿 |A gig is a single performance or job, especially one that is temporary or freelance.  It often refers to a musician's performance or a short-term contract. |例句：He landed a great gig playing at a music festival. 他得到一个很棒的演出机会，在音乐节上演出。|例句：She's doing a few gigs to make some extra money. 她做了一些零工来赚点外快。|近义词：job, performance, engagement, assignment |反义词：permanent position, long-term contract |名词
