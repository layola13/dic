remeasurement: /ˌriːˈmɛʒərmənt/ | n. | 再测量；重新测量 | The act of measuring something again.  This often implies a need for greater accuracy or a correction of a previous measurement. |例句：After the initial survey, a remeasurement was conducted to ensure accuracy. 初步勘测之后，进行了重新测量以确保精度。

例句：The bridge's dimensions required a remeasurement due to concerns about structural integrity.  由于对结构完整性的担忧，桥梁的尺寸需要重新测量。


近义词：re-measurement, second measurement, repeated measurement


反义词：initial measurement, first measurement


词性: noun
