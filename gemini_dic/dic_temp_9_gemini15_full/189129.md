saccoglossa:/ˌsækəˈɡlɒsə/| n. |舌形动物|A marine invertebrate belonging to the phylum Hemichordata, characterized by a long proboscis, a collar, and a trunk. Saccoglossans are often found in shallow coastal waters and are known for their unique feeding habits, often consuming algae.  They are considered a primitive chordate, sharing some characteristics with echinoderms and chordates.|Example: The researcher studied the unique feeding mechanisms of the saccoglossan mollusk.|近义词:hemichordate|反义词:None|
