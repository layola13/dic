dominant:/ˈdɒmɪnənt/ /ˈdɑːmɪnənt/| adj. |主要的；支配的；占优势的；显性的|  Describes something that is more powerful, important, or noticeable than other things. It can refer to a person, a characteristic, a gene, or an idea.  |例句：His dominant personality influenced the entire team.  他的支配性人格影响了整个团队。

例句：The dominant color in the painting is blue. 这幅画的主要颜色是蓝色。

近义词：prevailing, leading, chief, predominant, superior |反义词：subordinate, recessive, minor, insignificant |词性:adjective


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dominant factor is cost. = 主要因素是成本。
His dominant hand is his right hand. = 他的惯用手是右手。

一般过去时:
Red was the dominant color in her wardrobe. = 红色是她衣橱里最主要的颜色。
He was the dominant player on the team last year. = 去年他是队里最主要的球员。

现在完成时:
This company has become the dominant player in the market. = 这家公司已经成为市场上的主要参与者。
This trend has remained dominant for several years. =  这种趋势已经占据主导地位好几年了。

过去完成时:
Before the merger, they had been the dominant force in the industry. = 在合并之前，他们一直是该行业的主导力量。
It had been the dominant theme throughout his work. = 这在他的作品中一直是主要的主题。


将来时:
We expect this technology to become the dominant force in the future. = 我们预计这项技术将在未来成为主导力量。
This trend will likely remain dominant for the next decade. = 这种趋势可能会在未来十年保持主导地位。

虚拟语气:
If only the dominant gene were recessive! = 要是显性基因是隐性基因就好了！
If he were less dominant, the team would work better. = 如果他不那么强势，团队合作会更好。


被动语态:
The issue was dominated by political considerations. =  这个问题受政治因素支配。
The market is dominated by a few large companies. = 市场由几家大公司主导。


疑问句:
Which gene is dominant? = 哪一个基因是显性的？
Is this species dominant in this area? = 这个物种在这个区域是优势物种吗？


否定句:
The non-dominant hand is often weaker. = 非惯用手通常较弱。
His personality wasn't dominant, but rather collaborative. = 他性格并非强势，而是合作型。
