gastritides:/ˌɡæstrɪˈtaɪdɪz/| n.|胃炎|Inflammation of the stomach lining.  Gastritis can be caused by various factors, including infection (e.g., Helicobacter pylori), excessive alcohol consumption, prolonged use of nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), and autoimmune disorders. Symptoms can range from mild discomfort to severe pain and nausea.  |The doctor diagnosed him with gastritis after a series of tests.|近义词：stomach inflammation|反义词：无
