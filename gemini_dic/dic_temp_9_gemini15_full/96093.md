indefensibly:/ɪnˈdɪfɛnsəbli/| adj. |不可辩解地，无法辩护地 |Incapable of being defended; inexcusable.  It describes an action or situation that is so wrong or bad that there is no justification or excuse for it. |例句：His actions were indefensibly cruel.  他的行为残忍至极，令人无法原谅。

例句：The company's response to the crisis was indefensible. 公司对危机的回应是不可辩护的。


近义词：unjustifiably, inexcusably, unpardonably, unforgivably

反义词：justifiably, excusably, pardonably


词源：

* **in-:**  prefix meaning "not" or "without"
* **defensible:**  adjective meaning "capable of being defended"  (from "defend" + "-able")
* **-ly:** suffix forming adverbs from adjectives


USAGE->
(由于indefensibly是形容词，不涉及时态变化。以下例句展示其在不同句型中的用法)


肯定句:
His behavior was indefensibly rude. = 他的行为粗鲁得令人无法容忍。
The decision was indefensibly flawed. = 这个决定有不可挽回的缺陷。


否定句:
His actions were not indefensibly cruel, there were mitigating circumstances. = 他的行为并非残忍到不可饶恕，存在一些减轻罪责的情况。
It wasn't indefensibly bad, just poorly executed. = 它并非糟糕到不可救药，只是执行不力。


疑问句:
Was the judge's ruling indefensibly biased? = 法官的裁决是否带有不可辩驳的偏见？
Could the government's policy be considered indefensibly unfair? = 政府的政策能否被认为是不可接受的不公平？
