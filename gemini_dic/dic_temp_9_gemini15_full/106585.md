kubernetes:/ˌkuːbəˈnɛtiːz/| n. |库伯内蒂斯（容器编排系统）|Kubernetes is an open-source container orchestration system for automating computer application deployment, scaling, and management.  It groups containers that make up an application into logical units for easy management and discovery.|Example: We use Kubernetes to manage our microservices in the cloud. 我们使用Kubernetes来管理我们在云中的微服务。|近义词：container orchestration system, container management platform |反义词：无
