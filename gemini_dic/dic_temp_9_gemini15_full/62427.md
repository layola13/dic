endophloeum:/ˌɛndoʊˈfloʊiəm/| n. |内皮层，内树皮|The inner bark of a tree, lying between the phloem and the wood. It is often composed of parenchyma cells and is involved in storage and other metabolic functions.  |Example:  The endophloeum of the oak tree was particularly thick and spongy.  橡树的内皮层特别厚实而多孔。

|近义词:inner bark, bast|反义词:exophloeum(外皮层)|名词|
