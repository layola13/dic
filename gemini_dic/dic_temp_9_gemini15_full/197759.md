shilly-shallies:/ˈʃɪli ˈʃæliz/ | v. |犹豫不决，畏缩不前 |To hesitate or waver in making a decision; to be indecisive.  |例句：He shilly-shallied for weeks before finally deciding to accept the job. 他犹豫了几个星期才最终决定接受这份工作。


例句：Stop shilly-shallying and make up your mind! 不要再犹豫不决了，下定决心吧！


近义词：hesitate, waver, vacillate, dither, procrastinate


反义词：decisive, resolute, determined


词性：动词
