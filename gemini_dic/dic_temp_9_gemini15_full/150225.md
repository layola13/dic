pandemias:/pænˈdɛmiəz/| n.|大流行病（复数）|Pandemics are widespread outbreaks of infectious diseases affecting a large population across a wide geographic area.  They are characterized by rapid spread and high morbidity and mortality rates.|例句：The world is still grappling with the effects of recent pandemias. 全球仍在努力应对近期大流行病的影响。  The historical record shows that pandemias have repeatedly shaped human societies. 历史记录显示，大流行病反复地塑造着人类社会。|近义词：epidemics (on a smaller scale), outbreaks|反义词：endemic (confined to a particular region)|名词
