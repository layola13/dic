slipways:/ˈslɪpˌweɪz/| n.|滑道；船台|A slipway is a paved or concrete slope leading down to water, used for launching and hauling out boats.  It's often found in harbors, boatyards, and shipyards.|例句：The small fishing boats were launched from the slipway. 小渔船是从船台上发射的。|例句：The damaged yacht was hauled out of the water onto the slipway for repairs. 受损的游艇被拖出水面，放到船台上修理。|近义词：boat ramp, launching ramp, slip|反义词：dry dock (in some contexts)
