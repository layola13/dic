deferment's:/dɪˈfɜːrmənts/| n.|延期；缓期；暂缓|The postponement of something to a later time.  It is the possessive form of "deferment", indicating that something belongs to or is related to a deferment.|例句：The student's deferment of military service was approved. = 学生的服役延期申请得到了批准。
The loan's deferment period is three months. = 贷款的延期期限为三个月。|近义词：postponement, delay, suspension|反义词：acceleration, hastening
