celestials:/səˈlɛstriəl/| n. |天体，天上事物|Celestials are heavenly bodies, such as stars, planets, and moons.  They are often associated with mythology and spirituality.|例句：The ancient Greeks believed that the celestials influenced human destiny. 古希腊人相信天体影响人类的命运。|例句：Many cultures have myths and legends about the celestials. 许多文化都有关于天体的传说和神话。|近义词：heavenly bodies, astral bodies|反义词：terrestrial
