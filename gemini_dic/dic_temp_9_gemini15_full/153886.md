percolates:/ˈpɜːrkəleɪt/| v. |渗透；过滤；慢慢扩散 |To filter (a liquid) through a porous substance; to spread gradually.  |Example: The coffee percolates slowly through the filter. 咖啡慢慢地通过滤纸过滤。

The aroma of freshly brewed coffee percolates throughout the room. 新鲜冲泡咖啡的香味弥漫在整个房间。|近义词：filter, seep, diffuse, spread |反义词：concentrate, stagnate |动词


USAGE->

The water percolates through the soil. = 水渗透到土壤中。
The news percolates slowly through the community. = 消息慢慢地在社区中传播。
