machining:/məˈʃiːnɪŋ/| n. v.|机械加工，机械制造|The process of shaping or cutting materials using machine tools.  This encompasses a wide range of techniques used to create precise parts and components. |例句：The precision machining of the engine parts is crucial for its performance.  精密加工发动机部件对其性能至关重要。

例句：The company specializes in CNC machining for high-volume production. 这家公司专门从事大批量生产的数控加工。


近义词：fabrication, manufacturing, milling, turning, shaping

反义词：handcrafting (in the sense of manual creation)


词性: noun, verb
