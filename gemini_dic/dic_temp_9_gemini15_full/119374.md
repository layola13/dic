microcosms:/ˈmaɪkrəʊkɒzəm/| n. |微观世界；缩影；缩图|A miniature world or universe; something that represents a larger system on a smaller scale.  It often implies a similarity in structure and function between the microcosm and the macrocosm (the larger system).|Example: The small village served as a microcosm of the nation's political divisions.|近义词：miniature, epitome, model, representation|反义词：macrocosm
