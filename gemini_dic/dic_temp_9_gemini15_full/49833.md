demonstratives:/ˈdɛmənˌstreɪtɪvz/| n.|指示词，指示代词|Demonstratives are words that point out specific things.  They can be pronouns (this, that, these, those) or adjectives (this car, that house).  They show whether the thing being referred to is singular or plural, and whether it is near or far from the speaker.|例句：This is my car. 这我的车。  Those books are very interesting. 那些书很有趣。|近义词：pronouns, pointers|反义词：indefinites


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This is a pen. = 这是一个钢笔。
These are my keys. = 这些是我的钥匙。


一般过去时:
That was a great movie. = 那是一部很棒的电影。
Those were difficult times. = 那些是艰难的时期。


现在完成时:
This has been a long day. = 今天过得很长。
These have all been sold. = 这些都卖掉了。


过去完成时:
That had already happened. = 那已经发生了。
Those had been my best years. = 那些是我的最好时光。


将来时:
This will be exciting. = 这将会令人兴奋。
These will be delivered tomorrow. = 这些明天送达。


虚拟语气:
If this were true, I would be surprised. = 如果这是真的，我会很惊讶。
If those were available, I would buy them. = 如果那些有货，我会买下它们。


被动语态:
This is being repaired. = 正在修理这个。
These are being considered. = 这些正在被考虑。


疑问句:
Which one is this? = 这是哪一个？
What are those? = 那些是什么？


否定句:
This isn't mine. = 这不是我的。
Those aren't the ones I ordered. = 那些不是我订购的。
