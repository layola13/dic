cereal:/ˈsɪəriəl/| n. |谷物；谷类食物；麦片；早餐麦片|A cereal is a type of food made from grains, such as wheat, oats, rice, or corn.  It is often eaten for breakfast.| 例句：I usually eat cereal for breakfast. 我通常早餐吃麦片。  I prefer whole grain cereals. 我更喜欢全谷物麦片。|近义词：grain, breakfast food|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be described as "non-grain foods" like meat or vegetables.
