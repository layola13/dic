countertops:/ˈkaʊntərˌtɑːps/ | n. | 橱柜台面；操作台面 |  A countertop is a horizontal surface, often made of stone, wood, or synthetic materials, that is installed on top of cabinets in a kitchen or bathroom.  It provides a work surface for food preparation, washing, etc.  | Example: We are replacing the old countertops in our kitchen with granite. 我们正在用花岗岩更换厨房里的旧台面。  The bathroom countertops are made of marble. 浴室的台面是大理石做的。|近义词：work surface, kitchen counter, benchtop | 反义词：floor, wall
