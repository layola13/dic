leones:/leˈoʊniz/| n.|狮群；（西班牙语）狮子|The word "leones" is the plural form of the Spanish word "león," meaning "lion."  It refers to a group of lions or multiple lions.|Example:  There were many leones in the savanna.  稀树草原上有许多狮子。|近义词：lions (English)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with words like "gazelles" or "herbivores".|名词
