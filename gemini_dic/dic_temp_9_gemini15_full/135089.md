nonobligatory:/ˌnɒnˈɒblɪɡətəri/| adj. |非强制性的，非义务性的 | Not required or mandatory; optional.  |例句：Attendance at the meeting is nonobligatory. 参加会议是非强制性的。

例句：The extra credit assignment is nonobligatory; you can choose to do it or not.  额外的作业是非强制性的；你可以选择做或不做。


近义词：optional, voluntary, discretionary

反义词：mandatory, compulsory, obligatory

词源：
* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not"
* **obligatory**:  adjective meaning "required or mandatory"  源于拉丁语 "obligatio" (obligation, bond)
