keplerian:/kɛˈplɛəriən/| adj.|开普勒的；开普勒体系的 | Relating to Johannes Kepler or his laws of planetary motion.  The term describes anything consistent with, or derived from, Kepler's laws of planetary motion, which describe the elliptical orbits of planets around the sun.  |Example: Keplerian orbits are elliptical, not circular.  开普勒轨道是椭圆形的，而不是圆形的。


Example: The simulation accurately modeled the keplerian motion of the planets. 模拟精确地模拟了行星的开普勒运动。


近义词:  Planetary (in the context of motion), elliptical (referring to orbits)


反义词:  There isn't a direct antonym, but "non-elliptical" or "circular" (for orbits) could be considered opposites in the context of Keplerian motion.


词性: adjective
