dps:/ˌdiːpiːˈɛs/ | n. |每秒伤害，每秒输出|Damage Per Second, a measure of damage output in video games and other contexts where damage is inflicted over time.|The DPS of that weapon is incredibly high.  这把武器的DPS非常高。|近义词：damage output, damage per second |反义词：无
