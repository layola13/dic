fusspot:/ˈfʌsˌpɑːt/ | n. | 挑剔的人；过分讲究的人 | A fusspot is a person who is excessively concerned with minor details and is often critical of others. They are often seen as overly particular and fastidious.  | Example:  He's such a fusspot about the way his food is presented. 他对食物摆盘非常挑剔。

| Example: Don't be such a fusspot; it doesn't have to be perfect. 别那么挑剔；它不必完美无缺。 | 近义词：perfectionist, nitpicker, stickler | 反义词：slapdash, careless, unconcerned |  词性: 名词
