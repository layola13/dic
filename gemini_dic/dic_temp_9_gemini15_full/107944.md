laryngoscopy:/ˌlærɪŋˈɡɒskəpi/| n. |喉镜检查|The examination of the larynx using a laryngoscope.  This procedure allows visualization of the vocal cords and other structures within the larynx. |Example: The doctor recommended a laryngoscopy to determine the cause of the patient's hoarseness.  医生建议进行喉镜检查以确定病人声音嘶哑的原因。|近义词：laryngeal examination|反义词：N/A
