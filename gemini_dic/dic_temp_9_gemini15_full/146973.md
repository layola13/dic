overjoyed:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɔɪd/| adj. |欣喜若狂的，非常高兴的 |Extremely happy; delighted beyond measure.  Overjoyed implies a feeling of intense joy and happiness, often exceeding expectations.  It suggests a state of overwhelming elation.|例句：She was overjoyed at the news of her son's engagement. = 她听到儿子订婚的消息后欣喜若狂。

She was overjoyed to finally receive the award she'd worked so hard for. = 她终于获得了自己努力争取的奖项，欣喜若狂。


近义词：thrilled, ecstatic, elated, delighted, jubilant |反义词：dejected, despondent, miserable, unhappy


词源:  The word "overjoyed" is a compound word formed from the prefix "over-" meaning "excessively" or "to a great extent," and the adjective "joyed," which is an older form related to "joy."  The combination signifies a level of happiness that surpasses the usual.
