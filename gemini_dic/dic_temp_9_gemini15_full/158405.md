pleasurable:/ˈplɛʒərəbl/ | adj. |令人愉快的，舒适的 | Enjoyable, giving pleasure; causing gratification.  The adjective "pleasurable" describes something that provides pleasure or enjoyment.  It suggests a feeling of satisfaction and contentment. |例句：The trip was a pleasurable experience. 这趟旅行是一次愉快的经历。

The concert was a pleasurable evening for everyone. 音乐会对每个人来说都是一个愉快的夜晚。


近义词：delightful, enjoyable, gratifying, pleasant, agreeable | 反义词：unpleasant, disagreeable, painful, distressing


词源:  Derived from the word "pleasure," ultimately from Old French *plesir, from Latin *placere "to please."  The suffix "-able" indicates something capable of being or worthy of being.
