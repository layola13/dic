mercuric:/mərˈkjʊərɪk/| adj.| 汞的；含汞的；水银的 | Relating to or containing mercury.  Mercuric compounds are toxic. | 例句：Mercuric chloride is a highly poisonous compound.  氯化汞是一种剧毒化合物。

例句：The researchers found high levels of mercuric contamination in the fish. 研究人员发现鱼类中汞污染程度很高。


近义词：mercurous (指较低价态的汞化合物，但通常不作为mercuric的直接同义词使用)


反义词：无


词性：形容词


词源:  From Latin *mercurius (mercury), from Greek Hermes. The element mercury was named after the Roman god Mercury, known for his swiftness, mirroring the liquid metal's fluidity.
