homogony:/həˈmɒɡəni/ | n. |同源；同种；同性；同基因 |The study of homogony in plants focuses on the similarities in genetic makeup between offspring and parents, particularly in cases of asexual reproduction or self-fertilization.  同源性在植物中的研究侧重于后代和亲本之间遗传构成上的相似性，尤其是在无性繁殖或自花授粉的情况下。|近义词：homology, consanguinity |反义词：heterogony, heterogeneity |名词
