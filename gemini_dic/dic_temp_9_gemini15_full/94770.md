imprudencies:/ɪmˈpruːdənsɪz/| n.|轻率；鲁莽；不谨慎|Plural of imprudence.  Imprudencies refer to instances of acting without careful consideration of the consequences; a lack of caution or wisdom in judgment or action.|His financial imprudencies led to bankruptcy. 他的财务上的轻率导致了破产。| indiscretions, recklessness, rashness | prudence, caution, wisdom | 名词
