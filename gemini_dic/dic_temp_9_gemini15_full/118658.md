mesures:/məˈʒʊərz/| n.|措施，办法|Measures are being taken to improve safety. 正在采取措施以提高安全。|近义词：steps, actions, methods|反义词：inaction, neglect|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government takes measures to control inflation. = 政府采取措施控制通货膨胀。
These measures ensure the project's success. = 这些措施确保项目的成功。


一般过去时:
The company took drastic measures to cut costs. = 公司采取了严厉的措施来削减成本。
What measures did they take to prevent the accident? = 他们采取了什么措施来防止事故发生？


现在完成时:
They have taken several measures to address the problem. = 他们已经采取了几项措施来解决这个问题。
What measures have been implemented so far? = 到目前为止已经实施了什么措施？


过去完成时:
They had already taken preventive measures before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经采取了预防措施。
What measures had they taken before the crisis? = 危机发生前他们采取了什么措施？


将来时:
The government will take further measures to stimulate the economy. = 政府将采取进一步措施刺激经济。
What measures will be implemented next year? = 明年将实施什么措施？


虚拟语气:
If they had taken more effective measures, the outcome would have been different. = 如果他们采取更有效的措施，结果就会不同。
I wish they had taken more serious measures earlier. = 我希望他们早些采取更严厉的措施。


被动语态:
Measures are being developed to combat climate change. = 正在制定措施来应对气候变化。
Strict measures were implemented to maintain order. = 实施了严格的措施来维持秩序。


疑问句:
What measures are being considered? = 正在考虑什么措施？
What measures can we take to improve the situation? = 我们能采取什么措施来改善这种情况？


否定句:
They did not take any effective measures to solve the problem. = 他们没有采取任何有效的措施来解决这个问题。
No measures were taken to prevent the spread of the disease. = 没有采取任何措施来防止疾病的传播。
