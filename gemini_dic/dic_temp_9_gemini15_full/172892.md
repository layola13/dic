quailery:/ˈkweɪləri/ | n. | 鹌鹑养殖场；鹌鹑肉的供应 | The breeding and raising of quails, often for their meat or eggs.  It can also refer to a place where quails are raised or a supply of quail meat. | Example: The local quailery supplies restaurants with fresh quail eggs. 当地鹌鹑养殖场为餐馆提供新鲜鹌鹑蛋。 | 近义词：quail farm, game farm | 反义词：无
