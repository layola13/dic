fairest:/ˈferɪst/| adj.|最公平的，最美丽的|Most fair; most beautiful; most just.  It is the superlative form of "fair".|例句：She is the fairest maiden in the land. = 她是这片土地上最美丽的少女。

例句：The fairest solution would be to divide the resources equally. = 最公平的解决办法是平均分配资源。

近义词：most beautiful, most just, most equitable, loveliest

反义词：ugliest, most unjust, most inequitable

词性:形容词

词源:
From Old English fæger, meaning "beautiful, pleasing, bright," related to Old High German fagar "beautiful," and Gothic fagrs "beautiful, bright."  The superlative "-est" suffix was added to create the superlative form "fairest".
