endorse:/ɛnˈdɔːrs/ | vt. |背书；认可；支持；宣传 | to publicly declare your support for someone or something | 例句：The Prime Minister endorsed the new policy.  首相支持这项新政策。 | 例句：Many celebrities endorse products on television. 许多名人都在电视上为产品做宣传。|近义词：approve, support, sanction, advocate |反义词：reject, oppose, condemn, denounce


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He endorses the candidate. = 他支持这位候选人。
She endorses the new product. = 她为新产品代言。

一般过去时:
The bank endorsed the check. = 银行在支票上背书。
They endorsed the proposal. = 他们认可了这个提案。


现在完成时:
She has endorsed several charitable causes. = 她已经支持过几个慈善事业。
He has endorsed the book on social media. = 他已经在社交媒体上推荐了这本书。


过去完成时:
They had endorsed the plan before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就已经认可了这个计划。
The company had endorsed the product for years. = 这家公司多年来一直为该产品代言。


将来时:
She will endorse the new initiative. = 她将支持这项新倡议。
They will endorse the agreement. = 他们将认可这项协议。


虚拟语气:
If he endorsed the project, it would receive more funding. = 如果他支持这个项目，它就会获得更多资金。
I wish he had endorsed my application. = 我希望他当时已经认可了我的申请。


被动语态:
The product was endorsed by a famous athlete. = 该产品由一位著名运动员代言。
The policy was endorsed by the committee. = 该政策得到了委员会的认可。


疑问句:
Does he endorse the new law? = 他支持这项新法律吗？
Will they endorse our proposal? = 他们会认可我们的提案吗？


否定句:
He didn't endorse the controversial idea. = 他不支持那个有争议的想法。
The company did not endorse the harmful practice. = 公司不认可这种有害的做法。


PHRASE->

endorse a check = 在支票上背书

endorse a bill = 批准法案
