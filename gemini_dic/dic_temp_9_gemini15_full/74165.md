fountainheads:/ˈfaʊntənˌhedz/| n. |源头；发源地；源泉；首脑；领导者 |The original source or beginning of something, especially a river or stream;  a person or thing that is the origin of something.  |例句：The Amazon River has its fountainheads high in the Andes Mountains. 亚马逊河的源头在安第斯山脉的高处。

例句：The fountainheads of the revolution were the intellectuals and students.  革命的源头是知识分子和学生。


近义词：sources, origins, beginnings, wellsprings, leaders, pioneers

反义词：ends, conclusions, outcomes
