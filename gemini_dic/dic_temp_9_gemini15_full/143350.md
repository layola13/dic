outexecuting:/ˌaʊtɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪŋ/| v. |胜过，超越（在执行方面）|To surpass or exceed in execution or performance.|The new system outexecuted the old one in speed and efficiency. 新系统在速度和效率方面都胜过了旧系统。|outperform, surpass, excel|underperform, fail|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outexecutes his competitors in every aspect of the business. 他在业务的各个方面都胜过竞争对手。
The new software outexecutes the previous version in terms of functionality. 新软件在功能方面超越了旧版本。


一般过去时:
Our team outexecuted the opponent's team in the final game. 我们的团队在决赛中击败了对方球队。
Last year, the company outexecuted its projections for revenue growth. 去年，该公司超额完成了收入增长预期。


现在完成时:
She has consistently outexecuted her sales targets this year. 今年她一直超额完成了销售目标。

过去完成时:

The team had outexecuted all previous records in terms of productivity before the merger. 在合并之前，团队的生产力已经超过了以往所有记录。
