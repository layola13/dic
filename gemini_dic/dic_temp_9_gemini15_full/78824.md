gimmick:/ˈɡɪmɪk/| n.|噱头；小装置；骗人的把戏|A gimmick is a trick or device intended to attract attention, especially one that is considered to be gimmicky or not very effective.  It can also refer to a small, often ingenious device. |例句：The advertisement used a clever gimmick to attract viewers.  这个广告使用了巧妙的噱头来吸引观众。

The magician's disappearing act was just a gimmick. 魔术师的消失表演只是一个噱头。|近义词：trick, stunt, gadget, ploy, scheme|反义词：genuineness, authenticity
