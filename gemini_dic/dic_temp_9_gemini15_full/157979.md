plank:/plæŋk/| n. v.| 厚木板；(体操)平板支撑；计划；策略 | A plank is a long, flat, thick piece of wood.  It can also refer to a specific exercise or a component of a plan or policy. | 例句：He used a plank of wood to cross the stream. 他用一块木板过河。 The new government's economic plan includes tax cuts as a central plank. 新政府的经济计划将减税作为核心策略。|近义词：board, timber, beam, strategy, policy|反义词：fragment, splinter
