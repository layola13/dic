plumes:/pluːmz/| n. |羽毛；烟羽；(饰物上的)羽毛；浓密的云；气流|A plume is a long, feather-like shape, often of smoke, steam, or cloud.  It can also refer to a feather or a group of feathers. |例句：The volcano sent a plume of ash into the sky.  火山喷发出大量的火山灰直冲云霄。

例句：She wore a hat adorned with plumes of ostrich feathers. 她戴着一顶饰有鸵鸟羽毛的帽子。

近义词：feather, trail, streamer, wisp

反义词：N/A

词性:名词
