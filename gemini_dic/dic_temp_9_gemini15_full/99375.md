interdetermined: /ˌɪntərdiˈtɜːrmɪnd/ | adj. |相互决定的，互相制约的 | Mutually determined;  involving reciprocal causation or influence.  The outcome is not predetermined but arises from the interaction of multiple factors. | 例句：The success of the project is interdetermined by the efforts of the team and the availability of resources.  项目的成功取决于团队的努力和资源的可用性。 The political landscape is interdetermined by economic factors and social movements.  政治格局受经济因素和社会运动的相互影响。| 近义词：mutually dependent, reciprocally influenced, interdependent | 反义词：independent, unilateral, predetermined | 词性: 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The success of the project is interdetermined by many factors. = 项目的成功由许多因素相互决定。
Their decisions are interdetermined by their individual goals. = 他们的决定由各自的目标相互决定。


一般过去时:
The outcome of the negotiations was interdetermined by the conflicting interests of the parties. = 谈判的结果由各方的利益冲突相互决定。
The final design was interdetermined by the client's feedback and the designer's vision. = 最终的设计方案由客户的反馈和设计师的构思相互决定。


现在完成时:
The development of the city has been interdetermined by its history and its current economic climate. = 城市的发展一直受到其历史和当前经济环境的相互影响。
Their relationship has been interdetermined by compromise and conflict. = 他们的关系一直受到妥协和冲突的相互影响。


过去完成时:
The decision had been interdetermined by prior commitments and unforeseen circumstances. = 该决定是由先前的承诺和不可预见的情况相互决定的。
The outcome of the election had been interdetermined by various factors, including media coverage and campaign strategies. = 选举的结果受到各种因素的相互影响，包括媒体报道和竞选策略。


将来时:
The future of the company will be interdetermined by market trends and technological advancements. = 公司的未来将受到市场趋势和技术进步的相互影响。
The success of the new product will be interdetermined by consumer demand and marketing efforts. = 新产品的成功将由消费者需求和营销工作相互决定。


虚拟语气:
If the factors were interdetermined differently, the result would have been different. = 如果这些因素相互作用不同，结果就会不同。
If their actions had been less interdetermined, the crisis could have been avoided. = 如果他们的行动没有那么相互影响，危机本来是可以避免的。


被动语态:
The final decision was interdetermined by a series of events. = 最终的决定是由一系列事件相互决定的。
The outcome is being interdetermined by numerous variables. = 结果正在受到众多变量的相互影响。


疑问句:
How are the factors interdetermined in this system? = 在这个系统中，这些因素是如何相互作用的？
Is the outcome truly interdetermined, or is there a hidden pattern? = 结果真的是相互决定的，还是隐藏着某种模式？


否定句:
The results were not interdetermined; they were largely predictable. = 结果并非相互决定的；它们在很大程度上是可以预测的。
Their choices were not interdetermined; they were made independently. = 他们的选择并非相互决定的；它们是独立做出的。
