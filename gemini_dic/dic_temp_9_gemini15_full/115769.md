marinations:/ˌmærɪˈneɪʃənz/| n. |腌制；腌料|The process of marinating food, typically meat or fish, in a seasoned liquid to tenderize and flavor it.  Marinations usually involve acidic ingredients like vinegar or lemon juice, along with herbs, spices, and oil.|Example: The marinations for the chicken involved soy sauce, ginger, and garlic.  鸡肉的腌料包括酱油、生姜和大蒜。|近义词：pickling, brining|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be cooking without any prior seasoning.|名词
