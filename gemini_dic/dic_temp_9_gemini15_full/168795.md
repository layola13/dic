produced:/prəˈduːst/| v. |生产，制造，创作| to bring into existence; to create or make something.|例句：The factory produced thousands of cars last year.  这家工厂去年生产了数千辆汽车。|例句：She produced a stunning painting. 她创作了一幅令人惊叹的画作。|近义词：manufactured, created, generated, yielded|反义词：consumed, destroyed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They produce high-quality goods. = 他们生产高质量的商品。
The company produces software for mobile devices. = 这家公司生产移动设备软件。


一般过去时:
The artist produced a masterpiece. =这位艺术家创作了一幅杰作。
Ford produced the Model T. = 福特生产了T型车。


现在完成时:
She has produced several best-selling novels. = 她已经创作了几部畅销小说。
The team has produced excellent results. = 团队取得了优异的成果。


过去完成时:
They had produced the report before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经完成了报告。
The company had produced similar products in the past. = 这家公司过去生产过类似的产品。


将来时:
We will produce a new product next year. = 我们明年将生产一种新产品。
The factory will produce more cars in the future. = 工厂将来会生产更多的汽车。


虚拟语气:
If they produced more, they would make more profit. = 如果他们生产更多，他们就会赚更多钱。
I wish I had produced better work. = 我真希望我做得更好。


被动语态:
The movie was produced by a famous director. = 这部电影是由一位著名导演执导的。
Thousands of cars are produced daily. = 每天生产数千辆汽车。


疑问句:
What kind of products does this company produce? = 这家公司生产什么产品？
Who produced this film? = 谁制作了这部电影？


否定句:
The factory didn't produce enough cars this month. = 这个月工厂没有生产足够的汽车。
They haven't produced anything new lately. = 他们最近没有生产任何新东西。
