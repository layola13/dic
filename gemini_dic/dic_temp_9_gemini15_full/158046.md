plaque:/plæk/| n.|斑块；匾；牙菌斑|A plaque is a flat, usually decorative, plate or tablet, often bearing an inscription or design. It can also refer to a patch of abnormal tissue, such as a buildup of fatty material in an artery (atherosclerotic plaque), or a film of bacteria on teeth (dental plaque).|例句：They unveiled a plaque commemorating the 50th anniversary of the event. 他们揭幕了一块纪念活动50周年的匾牌。  The doctor warned him about the dangerous plaque buildup in his arteries. 医生警告他动脉里危险的斑块堆积。|近义词：tablet, plate, patch, film|反义词：cleanliness
