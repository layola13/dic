puffingly:/ˈpʌfɪŋli/| adv.| 喘着气地，蓬松地，夸张地 |  Describes an action or manner performed while puffing or in a puffed-up way, often implying breathlessness, exaggeration, or self-importance.  |例句：He spoke puffingly about his accomplishments. 他夸夸其谈地讲述了他的成就。

例句：She puffingly announced her engagement. 她喘着气地宣布了她的订婚。


近义词：breathlessly, boastfully, pompously, extravagantly| 反义词：calmly, modestly, humbly | 词性:副词
