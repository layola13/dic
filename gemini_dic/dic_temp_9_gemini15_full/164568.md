preexaminers: /ˌpriːɪɡˈzæmɪnərz/ | n. | 预审员；预先审查者 |  Those who conduct a preliminary examination or review before a formal examination or trial.  Preexaminers ensure that the main examination process is efficient and effective by weeding out unsuitable candidates or cases beforehand. | The preexaminers reviewed the applications to ensure only qualified candidates proceeded to the next stage. 预审员审查了申请，以确保只有合格的候选人才能进入下一阶段。 | examiners, reviewers, screeners |  (no direct antonyms, but the opposite function would be those who conduct the final examination) | 名词
