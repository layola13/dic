rhododendron: /ˌroʊdəˈdɛndrən/ | n. | 杜鹃花 | A woody plant of the heath family, typically having large, showy flowers.  Rhododendrons are known for their diverse colors and forms, ranging from small shrubs to large trees. They are popular ornamental plants in gardens worldwide.  | Example: The hillside was ablaze with the vibrant colors of rhododendrons.  山坡上杜鹃花盛开，色彩艳丽。

The garden boasts a stunning collection of rhododendrons. 花园里拥有令人惊叹的杜鹃花收藏。| azalea, mountain laurel |  (No direct antonym, as it describes a specific type of plant) | 名词
