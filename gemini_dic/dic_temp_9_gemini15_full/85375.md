harrying:/ˈhærɪŋ/| v.|  骚扰；折磨；驱赶 | To harass, worry, or torment persistently.  The word often implies a relentless and aggressive pursuit.|例句：The constant harrying of the enemy troops finally broke their morale.  敌军持续不断的骚扰最终摧垮了他们的士气。
The villagers were harried by bandits for months.  村民们几个月来一直受到土匪的骚扰。

近义词：pestering, tormenting, harassing, worrying | 反义词：soothing, comforting, reassuring | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harries his employees with excessive demands. = 他用过多的要求来骚扰他的员工。
The dog harries the cat relentlessly. = 这只狗无情地追赶着猫。


一般过去时:
The army harried the retreating enemy. = 军队骚扰撤退的敌人。
She was harried by debt collectors. = 她被讨债人纠缠。


现在完成时:
The constant noise has harried me all day. = 一整天不断的噪音都让我心烦意乱。
They have been harried by critics since the film's release. = 自从电影上映以来，他们一直受到评论家的抨击。


过去完成时:
The villagers had been harried for weeks before the army arrived. = 在军队到达之前，村民们已经被骚扰了几个星期。
He had been harried by self-doubt for years. = 多年来，他一直被自我怀疑所困扰。


将来时:
The rebels will harry the government forces in the mountains. = 反叛者将在山区骚扰政府军。
She will harry him until he agrees. = 她会一直纠缠他直到他同意为止。


虚拟语气:
If they harried us again, we would fight back. = 如果他们再次骚扰我们，我们会反击。
I wish they hadn't harried me so much. = 我真希望他们没有那么烦我。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
