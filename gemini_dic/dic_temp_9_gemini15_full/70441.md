figurant:/ˈfɪɡjʊrənt/| n. |群众演员，临时演员|A figurant is an actor with a non-speaking role, often part of a crowd scene or background action in a film, play, or other performance.  They typically do not have individual lines or significant character development.|Example:  Many figurants were hired for the battle scene in the movie.  许多群众演员被雇佣来拍摄电影中的战斗场景。|近义词：extra, background artist, supporting artist|反义词：lead actor, star, principal
