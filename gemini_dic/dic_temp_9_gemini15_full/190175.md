sanskritist:/ˈsænskɹɪtɪst/| n. |梵文学者；梵文学家| A sanskritist is a scholar specializing in the study of Sanskrit, the ancient classical language of India.  They are experts in Sanskrit literature, grammar, philology, and often related aspects of Indian culture and history.|  Professor Sharma is a renowned sanskritist, having published several groundbreaking works on ancient Indian epics.  |近义词：Sanskrit scholar, Indologist|反义词：无
