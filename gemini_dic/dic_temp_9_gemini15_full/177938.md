rechromatograph:/ˌriːˈkroʊmətoʊɡræf/| v.|再色谱分析|To chromatograph again; to subject to chromatography a second time, typically to improve separation or purification.  This usually implies a further refinement of the initial chromatographic process. |例句：The researchers rechromatographed the sample to achieve better separation of the isomers. 研究人员再次对样品进行色谱分析，以更好地分离异构体。

例句：After the initial chromatography yielded impure product, the chemist decided to rechromatograph it using a different stationary phase. 初次色谱分析得到不纯的产物后，化学家决定使用不同的固定相再次进行色谱分析。


近义词：reanalyze, re-separate, purify further|反义词：无


词源:
* **re-**: prefix indicating repetition or again.
* **chromato-**: combining form from Greek *chroma* (colour) referring to the separation of colored substances.
* **-graph**: suffix indicating an instrument or process of recording or writing.
