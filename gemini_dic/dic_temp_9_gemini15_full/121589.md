misoccupying: /ˌmɪsˈɒkjupaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 误占，滥用，错用 |  The act of using or occupying something incorrectly, inappropriately, or to someone's detriment.  It implies a misuse of resources, time, space, or a position. |


例句：

The company was accused of misoccupying public land for its private development.  公司被指控为其私人开发而占用公共土地。

He was misoccupying his position of power by using it for personal gain. 他滥用职权，为个人利益谋取私利。


近义词：misusing, abusing, misapplying, wrongly using


反义词：using properly, utilizing effectively, employing appropriately
