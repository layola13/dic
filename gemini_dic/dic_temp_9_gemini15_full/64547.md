erratically:/ɪˈrætɪkli/ | adv. | 不稳定地；反复无常地；不规则地 |  Acting or behaving in an erratic manner; characterized by lack of consistency, regularity, or predictability.  |例句：The car's engine was running erratically. 这辆车的引擎运转不稳定。

例句：The stock market behaved erratically throughout the week.  本周股市波动剧烈。


近义词：unpredictably, inconsistently, irregularly, capriciously, haphazardly

反义词：predictably, consistently, regularly, steadily
