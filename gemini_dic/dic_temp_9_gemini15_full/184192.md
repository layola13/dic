resocialization: /ˌriːˌsoʊʃəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 再社会化 | The process of adapting or reintegrating individuals back into society, often after a period of isolation, incarceration, or significant life change.  It involves learning new norms, values, and behaviors to fit into a new social environment or to re-establish a positive role within the existing one. |  Example: The prison's resocialization program aims to help inmates successfully reintegrate into society upon release.  再社会化项目旨在帮助囚犯在释放后成功重返社会。  The resocialization of refugees can be a long and complex process.难民的再社会化可能是一个漫长而复杂的过程。|近义词：rehabilitation, reintegration, reform |反义词：isolation, alienation, marginalization |
