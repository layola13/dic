forecasts:/ˈfɔːrkæsts/| n. |预测，预报|A forecast is a statement about what will happen in the future, based on information that is available now.  It often involves an element of uncertainty. |例句：The weather forecast predicts rain tomorrow.  明天天气预报有雨。|例句：The company's sales forecasts for the next quarter are optimistic. 公司对下个季度的销售预测比较乐观。|近义词：prediction, projection, prognosis|反义词：retrospect, review


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weather forecast predicts rain. = 天气预报说要下雨。
This model forecasts a significant increase in sales. = 这个模型预测销售额将大幅增长。

一般过去时:
The meteorologist forecast a hurricane. = 气象学家预测会有飓风。
Analysts forecast lower profits last year. = 分析师去年预测利润会下降。

现在完成时:
They have forecast a strong economic recovery. = 他们预测经济将强劲复苏。
The experts have forecast a rise in inflation. = 专家预测通货膨胀将会上升。

过去完成时:
Economists had forecast a recession before the crisis hit. = 危机爆发前，经济学家们曾预测会有经济衰退。
Scientists had forecast the volcanic eruption weeks earlier. = 科学家们几周前就预测了火山爆发。

将来时:
They will forecast the market trend next week. = 他们下周将预测市场趋势。
Experts will forecast the election outcome soon. = 专家们很快就会预测选举结果。

虚拟语气:
If the forecast were accurate, we would have prepared better. = 如果预报准确的话，我们会准备得更好。
I wish the weather forecast had been more precise. = 我希望天气预报更准确些。

被动语态:
The storm was forecast by meteorologists. = 暴风雨是由气象学家预测的。
The economic downturn was forecast by many experts. = 经济衰退已被许多专家预测到。

疑问句:
What does the forecast say about tomorrow's weather? = 天气预报对明天的天气怎么说？
What are the forecasts for the upcoming year? = 未来一年的预测是什么？

否定句:
The forecast did not predict the severity of the storm. = 预报没有预测到风暴的严重程度。
They did not accurately forecast the stock market crash. = 他们没有准确地预测股市崩盘。
