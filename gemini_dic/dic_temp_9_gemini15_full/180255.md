refrained:/rɪˈfreɪnd/| v.|克制，避免，忍住|To stop oneself from doing something.|He refrained from commenting on the matter. 他避免就此事发表评论。|She refrained from eating sweets. 她忍住没吃糖果。|近义词：abstained, resisted, forbore|反义词：indulged, yielded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refrains from smoking. 他避免吸烟。
She refrains from criticizing others. 她避免批评别人。

一般过去时:
I refrained from speaking. 我忍住没说话。
They refrained from interfering. 他们避免干涉。

现在完成时:
He has refrained from drinking alcohol for a year. 他已经一年没喝酒了。
She has refrained from expressing her opinion. 她一直避免表达自己的意见。

过去完成时:
I had refrained from telling him the bad news. 我之前一直没告诉他坏消息。
They had refrained from making any decisions. 他们之前一直没有做任何决定。

将来时:
I will refrain from interrupting. 我将避免打断。
She will refrain from gossiping. 她将避免说闲话。

虚拟语气:
I would have refrained from going if I had known. 如果我知道的话，我就不会去了。
It would be best if you refrained from commenting. 最好你不要发表评论。

被动语态:  (Refrained is rarely used passively)
*Not applicable in a meaningful way.*

疑问句:
Did he refrain from eating the cake? 他没吃蛋糕吗？
Will she refrain from asking questions? 她会避免问问题吗？

否定句:
He did not refrain from laughing. 他忍不住笑了。
She did not refrain from expressing her anger. 她没有克制住自己的愤怒。


PHRASE->
refrain from doing something  避免做某事
refrain from comment  避免评论
refrain from speaking  避免说话
refrain from eating  避免饮食
refrain from drinking  避免饮酒

词源:
From Old French refraindre, from re- + fraindre "to break," from Vulgar Latin *frangere, from Latin frangere "to break."  The sense evolution is from "to break off, interrupt" to "to restrain, check."
