geared:/ɡɪərd/ | adj. |  装备的；准备好的；适应的；专注于…的 | Designed or equipped for a particular purpose; adapted or suited to a particular task or situation.  | 例句：The company is geared towards producing high-quality products. 这家公司致力于生产高质量的产品。 | 例句：His speech was geared to a young audience. 他的演讲针对年轻观众。|近义词：adapted, equipped, prepared, suited | 反义词：unprepared, unsuitable, ill-equipped


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car is geared for speed. = 这辆车为速度而设计。
The program is geared towards young children. = 该节目面向幼儿。


一般过去时:
The factory was geared towards mass production. = 这家工厂致力于大规模生产。
The entire team was geared up for the challenge. = 全队都为挑战做好了准备。


现在完成时:
The economy has been geared towards export growth. = 经济已转向出口增长。
The company has geared itself towards sustainability. = 公司已将自身导向可持续发展。


过去完成时:
The company had geared its marketing towards a younger demographic. = 公司之前已将市场营销导向更年轻的人群。
The team had geared up for the long journey. = 团队已为长途旅行做好了准备。


将来时:
The new system will be geared towards improving efficiency. = 新系统将致力于提高效率。
We will gear our efforts towards achieving our goals. = 我们将把精力集中在实现目标上。


虚拟语气:
If the engine were geared differently, it would be more fuel-efficient. = 如果引擎的齿轮比不同，它会更省油。
I wish the curriculum were geared more towards practical skills. = 我希望课程更侧重于实践技能。


被动语态:
The entire operation was geared towards maximum profit. = 全部运营都致力于最大利润。
His efforts were geared towards solving the problem. = 他的努力都致力于解决这个问题。


疑问句:
Is the software geared towards beginners? = 这个软件适合初学者吗？
Is the training geared towards our specific needs? = 培训是否针对我们的具体需求？


否定句:
The system isn't geared towards user-friendliness. = 该系统并不注重用户友好性。
His presentation wasn't geared towards the audience's level of understanding. = 他的演讲并不符合听众的理解水平。


PHRASE->
geared to/towards:  适应于……
geared up:  准备好；准备就绪
geared for: 为……而设计的
high-geared: 高速运转的
low-geared: 低速运转的
