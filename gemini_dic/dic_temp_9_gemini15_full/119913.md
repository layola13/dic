militiamen:/ˌmɪlɪʃmən/ | n. |民兵，民兵队员 | Members of a citizen army.  Militiamen are typically unpaid volunteers who are trained to defend their community in times of war or emergency. They are distinct from regular, professional soldiers. | 例句：The town's militiamen were called up to defend against the approaching enemy.  这个镇的民兵被召集起来抵御来犯之敌。 | 例句：During the revolution, many militiamen fought alongside the regular army. 在革命期间，许多民兵与正规军并肩作战。 | 近义词：citizen soldiers, volunteers,  armed civilians | 反义词：regular soldiers, professional soldiers
