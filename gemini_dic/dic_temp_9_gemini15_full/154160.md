perinephritis:/ˌpɛrɪnɪˈfraɪtɪs/| n. |肾周炎|Inflammation of the tissue surrounding the kidneys.  This condition can be caused by various factors, including infection (most commonly bacterial), trauma, or autoimmune diseases. Symptoms can include flank pain, fever, and tenderness to the touch in the affected area. Diagnosis often involves blood tests, urine tests, and imaging studies such as ultrasound or CT scan. Treatment typically involves antibiotics (if bacterial infection is the cause), pain relief medication, and supportive care. |The patient presented with perinephritis, requiring immediate antibiotic treatment.|近义词：renal inflammation, nephritis (though nephritis is inflammation of the kidney itself)|反义词：无
