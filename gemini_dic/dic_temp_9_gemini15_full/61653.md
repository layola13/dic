emissions:/ɪˈmɪʃənz/ | n. |排放物，散发物 |The release of something, especially gas or radiation.  Emissions from cars and factories contribute to air pollution. 汽车和工厂的排放物加剧了空气污染。 |近义词：releases, discharges, emanations |反义词：absorption, intake |名词

The factory emits harmful emissions every day. = 工厂每天排放有害气体。
Our car's emissions are within the legal limits. = 我们汽车的排放量在法律限额以内。

一般过去时:
The volcano emitted a large amount of ash and gas. = 火山喷发出大量的火山灰和气体。
