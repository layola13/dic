pull-rank:/pʊlˈræŋk/| v.|滥用职权，凭借职位压制|To use one's authority or rank improperly to get one's way or to intimidate others.  This often involves circumventing normal procedures or ignoring legitimate concerns. |例句：He tried to pull rank to get out of cleaning the mess he made. 他试图滥用职权来逃避打扫他弄乱的烂摊子。
例句：The sergeant pulled rank on the private, ordering him to do extra duty without explanation.  中士滥用职权命令下士做额外的工作，没有任何解释。|近义词：browbeat, bully, intimidate, lord it over|反义词：cooperate, collaborate, defer, respect authority appropriately|词性:动词
