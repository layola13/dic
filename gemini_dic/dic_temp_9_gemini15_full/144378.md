outstorming:/aʊtˈstɔːrmɪŋ/ | v. | 风暴来袭；超越；压倒 |  To surpass or overwhelm; to be more powerful or intense than. It's a relatively rare and informal word, often used figuratively. | 例句：The new marketing campaign completely outstormed the competition. = 新的营销活动完全超越了竞争对手。

The relentless rain outstormed the city's drainage system. = 持续不断的暴雨压垮了城市的排水系统。


近义词：outdo, overshadow, overwhelm, surpass, exceed | 反义词：underwhelm, be outdone by


词性: 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The new product outstorms its competitors in terms of features. = 新产品在功能方面胜过竞争对手。
His talent outstorms the others in the team. = 他的才能胜过团队中的其他人。


一般过去时:
Last year's hurricane outstormed all previous records. = 去年飓风的强度超过了以往所有记录。
Her performance outstormed everyone's expectations. = 她的表现超出了所有人的预期。


现在完成时:
The company has outstormed its financial goals this year. = 公司今年已经超额完成了财务目标。
Their success has outstormed any previous attempts. = 他们的成功超越了以往的任何尝试。


过去完成时:
By the time the rescue team arrived, the flood had outstormed the town's defenses. = 当救援队到达时，洪水已经冲垮了城镇的防御工事。
The storm had outstormed the old bridge before they could repair it. = 在他们能够修复之前，暴风雨已经摧毁了那座老桥。


将来时:
The upcoming election will likely outstorm the previous one in terms of media coverage. = 即将到来的选举可能会在媒体报道方面超过之前的选举。
We believe that our new technology will outstorm the current market leaders. = 我们相信我们的新技术将超越目前的市场领导者。


虚拟语气:
If only our team had outstormed the opposing team, we would have won the championship. = 如果我们的团队能胜过对方球队，我们就能赢得冠军。
I wish the marketing campaign had outstormed the competitor's efforts. = 我希望营销活动能够胜过竞争对手的努力。


被动语态:
The small town was outstormed by the unexpected blizzard. = 小镇被突如其来的暴风雪袭击了。
The old building was outstormed by the recent earthquake. = 老建筑在最近的地震中被摧毁了。


疑问句:
Will their new product outstorm the competition? = 他们的新产品会胜过竞争对手吗？
Does their performance outstorm others in the same field? = 他们的表现是否胜过同一领域的其他人？


否定句:
The team did not outstorm their opponents in the final match. = 该队在决赛中没有战胜对手。
Our efforts did not outstorm the challenges we faced. = 我们的努力没有克服我们面临的挑战。



PHRASE->
There are no established idiomatic phrases using "outstorming".  The word itself is quite rare.
