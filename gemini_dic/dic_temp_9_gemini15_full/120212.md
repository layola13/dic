mineralogists:/ˌmɪnəˈrælədʒɪsts/| n. |矿物学家|Scientists who study minerals.  They examine the chemical composition, physical properties, and origins of minerals. |例句：Mineralogists use various techniques, including X-ray diffraction, to identify minerals. 矿物学家使用各种技术，包括X射线衍射，来鉴定矿物。
例句：Many mineralogists are employed by universities and museums. 许多矿物学家受雇于大学和博物馆。|近义词：geologists, petrologists|反义词:无
