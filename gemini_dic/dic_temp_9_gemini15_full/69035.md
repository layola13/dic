fatuously:/ˈfætʃuəsli/| adj.|愚蠢地；傻气地；轻率地 |Acting in a foolish or silly way; showing a lack of intelligence or good judgment.  |例句：He fatuously believed he could win the lottery. 他愚蠢地相信自己能中彩票。

例句：She fatuously ignored the obvious dangers. 她轻率地无视了明显的危险。


近义词：foolishly, silly, absurdly, inanely,  pointlessly

反义词：wisely, intelligently, shrewdly, cleverly, sensibly

词性:形容词
