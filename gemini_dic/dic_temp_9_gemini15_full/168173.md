pro-Chilean: /ˌproʊ tʃɪˈliːən/ | adj. |亲智利的；支持智利的|

英文解析:  Supporting or favoring Chile or its people and government.  The prefix "pro-" indicates support or advocacy for something.

例句:
He expressed his pro-Chilean sentiments during the debate. 他在辩论中表达了他亲智利的观点。

She's known for her pro-Chilean activism. 她以其亲智利的行动主义而闻名。


近义词: Chilean-supporting, pro-Chile,  Chile-friendly


反义词: anti-Chilean


词性: adjective (形容词)
