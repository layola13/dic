discuses:/dɪˈskʌsɪz/| v.|讨论，议论|To talk about something with someone else, often in order to reach a decision or make a plan.|例句：We discussed the problem for hours, but we couldn't find a solution. 我们讨论了这个问题几个小时，但找不到解决办法。|例句：They are discussing the new project. 他们正在讨论新项目。|近义词：talk about, debate, consider|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They discuss the latest news every morning. = 他们每天早上都讨论最新的新闻。
We discuss our work plans weekly. = 我们每周讨论工作计划。

一般过去时:
They discussed the project in detail yesterday. = 他们昨天详细讨论了这个项目。
We discussed the problem at the meeting. = 我们在会议上讨论了这个问题。

现在完成时:
They have discussed this topic many times. = 他们已经讨论过这个话题很多次了。
We have discussed the proposal thoroughly. = 我们已经彻底讨论了这个提案。

过去完成时:
They had discussed the issue before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他们已经讨论过这个问题了。
We had discussed the budget before making the decision. = 我们在做决定之前已经讨论过预算。

将来时:
They will discuss the matter further tomorrow. = 他们明天将进一步讨论此事。
We will discuss the contract next week. = 我们下周将讨论合同。

虚拟语气:
If they discussed the problem openly, they might find a solution. = 如果他们公开讨论这个问题，他们可能会找到解决办法。
I wish they had discussed the plan more carefully. = 我希望他们当初能更仔细地讨论这个计划。

被动语态:
The matter was discussed at length during the meeting. = 会议期间对这个问题进行了长时间的讨论。
The proposal will be discussed by the committee next week. = 该提案将在下周由委员会讨论。

疑问句:
What did they discuss at the meeting? = 他们在会议上讨论了什么？
Have they discussed the possibility of a merger? = 他们讨论过合并的可能性吗？

否定句:
They didn't discuss the sensitive topic. = 他们没有讨论那个敏感的话题。
We haven't discussed the next steps yet. = 我们还没有讨论下一步的计划。


PHRASE->
discuss a problem = 讨论一个问题
discuss a topic = 讨论一个话题
discuss the matter = 讨论这件事
discuss the issue = 讨论这个问题
discuss the details = 讨论细节
