egoist:/ˈiːɡoɪst/| n. |利己主义者；自我中心者 |A person who is excessively self-centered and self-absorbed; someone who is only concerned with their own needs and interests, often at the expense of others.  |He's such an egoist; he never considers anyone else's feelings. 他是个十足的利己主义者，从不考虑别人的感受。| narcissist, self-seeker, individualist | altruist, selfless person |名词

词源:

The word "egoist" comes from the Latin word "ego," meaning "I."  The suffix "-ist" indicates a person who practices or believes in something. Thus, an egoist is someone who centers their actions and thoughts primarily on themselves.
