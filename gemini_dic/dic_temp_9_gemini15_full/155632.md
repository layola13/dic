phish:/fɪʃ/| n. vt.|网络钓鱼，钓鱼网站|A phish is a fraudulent attempt, usually made through email, to trick someone into revealing their username, password, credit card number, or other sensitive information.  Phishing is often done by sending emails that appear to be from a legitimate organization, such as a bank or online retailer.| Example:  He received a phish pretending to be from his bank, asking him to update his account information. 他收到一封伪装成他银行发来的钓鱼邮件，要求他更新账户信息。|近义词：scam, fraud, hoax |反义词：legitimate, genuine |动词
