heterogenist:/ˌhɛtəˈrɒdʒənɪst/ | n. | 异质论者，多元论者 | A person who believes in or advocates for heterogeneity, diversity, or pluralism, especially in a particular field or context.  They often emphasize the importance of diverse perspectives and experiences.  | Example: The conference featured several heterogenists who presented compelling arguments for inclusive policies.  |近义词:pluralist, inclusionist |反义词:homogenist, uniformitarian
