heating:/ˈhiːtɪŋ/| n. |供暖，加热|The process of making something warm or hot; a system or method for warming a building or area.|例句：The central heating is broken.  中央供暖坏了。|例句：We need to improve the heating in this room. 我们需要改善这个房间的供暖。|近义词：warming, heating system|反义词：cooling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun is heating the pavement. = 太阳正在加热路面。
The radiator is heating the room slowly. = 暖气片正在慢慢地加热房间。


一般过去时:
They heated the soup before serving. = 他们在上菜前加热了汤。
We heated the house with a wood stove. = 我们用柴火炉给房子取暖。


现在完成时:
They have heated the pool for the party. = 他们已经为派对加热了游泳池。
She has been heating up leftovers all afternoon. = 她整个下午一直在加热剩菜。


过去完成时:
