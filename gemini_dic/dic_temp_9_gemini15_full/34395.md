classism:/ˈklæsɪzəm/| n.|阶级歧视；阶级偏见|The prejudice against or in favor of people belonging to a particular social class.|例句：Classism is a pervasive problem in many societies. 阶级歧视是许多社会中普遍存在的问题。

例句：Her comments revealed a deep-seated classism. 她的评论暴露出根深蒂固的阶级偏见。


近义词：elitism, snobbery, social prejudice|反义词：egalitarianism, class equality|词性 n.
