词源:
The word "nanoscale" is a combination of "nano-" (from Greek "νάνος" (náinos) meaning "dwarf") and "scale," referring to the size or magnitude.  The prefix "nano-" indicates one billionth (10⁻⁹). Thus, nanoscale refers to dimensions on the order of nanometers.
