/ˈsɪmpli/| adv.|简单地，仅仅，只不过|Simply put, it's too expensive. 简单来说，它太贵了。|近义词：easily, merely, just|反义词：complexly, intricately


USAGE->

She simply reads a book. = 她只是在看书。
