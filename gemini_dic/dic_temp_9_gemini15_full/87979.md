high-level:/ˈhaɪ levl/| adj.|高级的；高层次的；高水平的|Of or relating to a high level of skill, importance, or status.  It describes something that is complex, sophisticated, or strategic, often involving a broad perspective or overview.|例句：The company hired a high-level executive to lead the project. 这家公司聘请了一位高级主管来领导这个项目。

例句：They conducted a high-level analysis of the market trends. 他们对市场趋势进行了高层次的分析。

近义词：advanced, superior, sophisticated, senior, strategic

反义词：low-level, basic, elementary, fundamental
