landlords:/ˈlændlɔːrdz/| n. |房东，地主|A landlord is a person who owns a property and rents it out to others.  They are responsible for maintaining the property and collecting rent.|例句：My landlords are very responsive to maintenance requests. 我的房东对维修请求非常积极响应。|例句：The landlords increased the rent significantly this year.  今年房东大幅提高了租金。|近义词：property owner, lessor|反义词：tenant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The landlord collects rent monthly. = 房东每月收取租金。
Our landlord maintains the property well. = 我们的房东维护房产很好。

一般过去时:
The landlord fixed the leaky faucet last week. = 房东上周修好了漏水的龙头。
They contacted their landlord about a broken window. = 他们联系了房东关于一个破窗户的事。

现在完成时:
The landlord has raised the rent twice this year. = 房东今年已经两次涨租了。
We have lived in this apartment for five years under the same landlord. = 我们在这个公寓住了五年，同一个房东。

过去完成时:
The landlord had already repaired the roof before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，房东已经修好了屋顶。
Before moving in, they had checked with the landlord about pet policies. = 入住前，他们已向房东询问了宠物政策。

将来时:
The landlord will inspect the apartment next month. = 房东下个月将检查公寓。
They will ask their landlord to renew the lease. = 他们将要求房东续租。

虚拟语气:
If the landlord were more understanding, we would stay longer. = 如果房东更通情达理，我们会住久一点。
I wish the landlord would lower the rent. = 我希望房东能降低租金。

被动语态:
The rent was raised by the landlord without notice. = 房租在没有通知的情况下被房东提高了。
The property is managed by a professional landlord management company. = 该房产由一家专业的房东管理公司管理。

疑问句:
Does the landlord allow pets? = 房东允许养宠物吗？
What are the landlord's responsibilities regarding repairs? = 房东在维修方面有哪些责任？

否定句:
The landlord didn't respond to our complaints. = 房东没有回应我们的投诉。
They haven't yet met their new landlord. = 他们还没有见过他们的新房东。
