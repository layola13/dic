purple-medic: /ˈpɜːrpəl ˈmɛdɪk/ | n. |紫花苜蓿| Purple medic refers to a species of clover, *Medicago sativa*, known for its purple flowers and use as a forage crop.  It's a vital component in many agricultural systems, providing high-quality feed for livestock.  The name combines "purple," describing the flower color, and "medic," a shortened form of "medicago," the genus name. |The farmer planted a field of purple medic for his cattle.  农场主种了一片紫花苜蓿给他的牛吃。|近义词：alfalfa, lucerne|反义词：None directly applicable.
