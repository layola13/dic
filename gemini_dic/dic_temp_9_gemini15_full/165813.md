preobtruded:/ˌpriːəˈbtruːdəd/| v.| 突出地显现；冒昧地提出；强加 |To thrust or push forward; to intrude or obtrude oneself or something upon someone or something.  It suggests a forceful or unwelcome intrusion. |例句：He preobtruded his opinions into the conversation, much to the annoyance of others. 他在谈话中强加自己的意见，这让其他人很恼火。|例句：The mountain preobtruded its jagged peaks against the stormy sky.  山峰在暴风雨的天空中突兀地耸立。|近义词：obtrude, thrust, impose, intrude |反义词：retire, withdraw, conceal
