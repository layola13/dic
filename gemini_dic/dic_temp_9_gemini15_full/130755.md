nonamorousness:/ˌnɒnˈæmərəsnəs/ | n. |非爱情的；缺乏爱情的；无情欲的 |The absence of amorous feelings or behavior; lack of romance or passion.  It describes a state or quality devoid of romantic love or sexual desire.| 例句：The nonamorousness of their relationship was evident in their lack of physical affection. 他们关系中缺乏爱情在他们缺乏身体上的爱抚中显而易见。

The nonamorousness in his tone suggested a purely platonic friendship. 他语气中的冷漠暗示了一种纯粹柏拉图式的友谊。


近义词：apathy, coldness, indifference, unromanticism

反义词：amorousness, romance, passion, affection
