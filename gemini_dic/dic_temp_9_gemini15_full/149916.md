pallah:/ˈpælə/| n.|帕拉赫（一种羚羊）|A medium-sized antelope found in eastern and southern Africa, characterized by its reddish-brown coat and lyre-shaped horns.  |The pallah is known for its graceful movements and its ability to blend in with its surroundings. 帕拉赫以其优雅的动作和融入周围环境的能力而闻名。|impala, gazelle|—|noun
