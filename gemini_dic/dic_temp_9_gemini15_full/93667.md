imbuing:/ɪmˈbjuːɪŋ/| v.|灌输；使充满；赋予|The act of imbuing something with a particular quality, feeling, or idea.  It implies a gradual process of permeation or infusion. |例句：The teacher was imbuing her students with a love of literature.  老师正在给她的学生灌输对文学的热爱。
例句：The sunset imbued the sky with vibrant colours.  日落给天空染上了鲜艳的色彩。|近义词：instilling, infusing, injecting, permeating|反义词：removing, depleting, draining


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is imbuing the fabric with a rich dye. = 他正在用丰富的染料给织物染色。
They are imbuing the young minds with knowledge. = 他们正在给年轻的心灵灌输知识。


一般过去时:
She imbued her paintings with deep emotion. = 她给她的画作注入了深厚的情感。
The artist imbued the sculpture with life. = 艺术家赋予了雕塑生命。


现在完成时:
He has imbued his work with a sense of humour. = 他已经在他的作品中注入了幽默感。
The experience has imbued her with strength and resilience. = 这段经历已经赋予了她力量和韧性。


过去完成时:
They had imbued the story with a sense of mystery. = 他们已经给故事注入了神秘感。
She had imbued her speech with passion and conviction. = 她已经给她的演讲注入了激情和信念。


将来时:
He will imbue his next novel with historical detail. = 他将在他的下一部小说中融入历史细节。
They will imbue the ceremony with solemnity. = 他们将给仪式增添庄严感。


虚拟语气:
If she imbued her writing with more feeling, it would be more powerful. = 如果她给她的写作注入更多的情感，它就会更有力量。
I wish I could imbue my work with the same passion as he does. = 我希望我能像他一样在我的工作中注入同样的热情。


被动语态:
The painting was imbued with a sense of peace. = 这幅画充满了宁静感。
The culture was imbued with ancient traditions. = 这文化充满了古老的传统。


疑问句:
What qualities are you trying to imbue in your students? = 你试图给你的学生灌输什么品质？
How can we imbue our community with a sense of belonging? = 我们怎样才能给我们的社区带来归属感？


否定句:
He did not imbue his speech with enough conviction. = 他没有在他的演讲中注入足够的信念。
The director failed to imbue the film with suspense. = 导演未能给电影注入悬念。

imbued with life: 充满活力
imbued with passion: 充满激情
