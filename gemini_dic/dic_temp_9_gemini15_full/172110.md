purposelessness:/ˈpɜːrpəsləsnəs/ | n. | 无意义；毫无目的；空虚 | The feeling or state of lacking purpose or meaning; a lack of direction or goal.  It often manifests as a sense of emptiness or apathy. | Example:  He felt a deep sense of purposelessness after losing his job. 他失业后感到深深的无意义。  The purposelessness of his life led him down a dark path. 他生活的毫无目标让他走上了一条黑暗的道路。 | 近义词：meaninglessness, pointlessness, futility, aimlessness | 反义词：purposefulness, meaningfulness, significance
