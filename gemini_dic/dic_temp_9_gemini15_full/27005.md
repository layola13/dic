calculates:/ˈkælkjuleɪts/| vt.|计算，估计，推测|To determine or ascertain by mathematical methods; compute.  To make a calculation or estimation of.|例句：The computer calculates the trajectory of the rocket.  电脑计算火箭的轨迹。

例句：She carefully calculates the cost of the project before starting. 她开始之前仔细计算了项目的成本。


近义词：compute, figure, reckon, estimate|反义词：guess (in the sense of imprecise estimation)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He calculates the area of the circle. 他计算圆形的面积。
She calculates the total cost of the groceries. 她计算杂货的总价。

一般过去时:
They calculated the distance between two points. 他们计算了两点之间的距离。
He calculated the risk before making the investment. 他在投资之前计算了风险。

现在完成时:
I have calculated the results of the experiment. 我已经计算出了实验的结果。
She has calculated the amount of tax she owes. 她已经计算了她欠的税款。

过去完成时:
They had calculated the trajectory before launching the satellite. 他们在发射卫星之前已经计算好了轨迹。
We had calculated the time needed, but it took longer. 我们已经计算过所需的时间，但实际花了更久。

将来时:
We will calculate the profit margin after the sales are completed. 我们将在销售完成后计算利润率。
He will calculate the interest on the loan. 他将计算贷款的利息。

虚拟语气:
If I had calculated correctly, I wouldn't have made a mistake. 如果我计算正确，我就不会犯错误。
If he had calculated the risk, he wouldn't have invested. 如果他计算过风险，他就不会投资了。

被动语态:
The area was calculated using a complex formula. 面积是用一个复杂的公式计算出来的。
The final score was calculated by the computer.  最终分数是由电脑计算出来的。

疑问句:
How do you calculate the average speed? 你如何计算平均速度？
How is the tax calculated? 税是如何计算的？

否定句:
He didn't calculate the cost of materials. 他没有计算材料的成本。
They did not calculate the risks involved. 他们没有计算所涉及的风险。


PHRASE->
