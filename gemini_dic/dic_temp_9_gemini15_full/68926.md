fatalistic:/ˌfeɪtəˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|宿命论的，听天由命的|Believing that all events are predetermined and inevitable; accepting of fate.|例句：He had a fatalistic attitude towards his illness, believing it was his destiny to suffer. 他对自己的病情采取了一种宿命论的态度，认为受苦是他的命运。

例句：She adopted a fatalistic view of the situation, accepting that nothing could be done to change it. 她对这种情况采取了宿命论的观点，认为没有什么可以改变它。


近义词：resignation, acquiescent, deterministic|反义词：optimistic, hopeful, proactive

词源：源于fate（命运）+ -istic（形容词后缀），表示“与命运相关的”。  The word evolved from the noun "fatalist" (a person who believes in fatalism), which in turn derives from "fate."
