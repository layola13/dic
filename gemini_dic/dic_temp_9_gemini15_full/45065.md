cubical:/ˈkjuːbɪkəl/ | adj. | 立方体的；立方形的；单调乏味的 | Having the shape of a cube; relating to or shaped like a cube.  The term can also be used informally to describe something monotonous or repetitive. |例句：The room was cubical and rather small.  这个房间是立方体的，而且相当小。
The repetitive nature of the work made it feel cubical.  工作的重复性让它感觉很单调。 |近义词：cubic, square (in some contexts), boxy |反义词：spherical, round, varied, interesting
