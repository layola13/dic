sensuosity:/senʃuˈɒsəti/| n. |感官享受；性感；韵味；曲线美|The quality of being sensual; the power of appealing to the senses, especially in a sexual way.  It often refers to the pleasing qualities of something that stimulates the senses, such as the curves of a body or the richness of a texture. |例句：The painting was praised for its sensuosity and vibrant colours. 这幅画因其感官享受和鲜艳的色彩而受到赞扬。

The sculptor captured the sensuosity of the female form in her work.  这位雕塑家在她作品中捕捉到了女性形体的性感。|近义词：sensuality, voluptuousness, eroticism |反义词：asceticism, austerity
