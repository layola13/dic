nonacademician:/ˌnɒnˌækədəˈmɪʃən/| n. |非学术人员，非学者|A person who is not a member of an academy or who does not engage in academic pursuits.  Someone outside of academia.|Example:  Many nonacademicians find the jargon used in academic papers difficult to understand. 许多非学术人员发现学术论文中使用的术语难以理解。|近义词：layperson, non-scholar, amateur|反义词：academician, scholar, professor
