parachuting:/ˈpærəʃuːtɪŋ/| n. |跳伞运动；跳伞 |The act of jumping from an aircraft and descending to the ground using a parachute.  It can be a sport, a military operation, or an emergency procedure. |例句：He enjoys parachuting as a hobby. 他喜欢跳伞作为一项爱好。|例句：The soldiers underwent rigorous parachuting training.士兵们接受了严格的跳伞训练。|近义词：skydiving|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite could be described as staying on the ground.
