resweetened:/ˌriːˈswiːtn(d)/| adj. | 再加糖的；重新加糖的 |  Having had more sweetener added. This word describes something that has already been sweetened and has then had more sweetener added to it.  | Example: The resweetened beverage was overly sugary.  再加糖的饮料太甜了。 |近义词: sweetened again, re-sugared | 反义词: unsweetened


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chef resweetens the dessert. = 厨师给甜点再加糖。
She resweetens her coffee every morning. = 她每天早上都给咖啡再加糖。

一般过去时:
He resweetened the tea after tasting it. = 他尝过茶后又加了糖。
They resweetened the lemonade because it was too tart. = 他们因为柠檬水太酸了，所以又加了糖。

现在完成时:
I have resweetened the iced tea several times. = 我已经给冰茶加了好几次糖了。
She has resweetened the fruit salad, making it too sweet. = 她给水果沙拉又加了糖，结果太甜了。

过去完成时:
They had resweetened the cake before serving it. = 他们上菜之前已经给蛋糕加了糖。
She had resweetened the drink, but it was still not sweet enough. = 她已经给饮料加了糖，但是仍然不够甜。
