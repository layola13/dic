nonrevertible: /ˌnɒn.rɪˈvɜːtəbl/ | adj. | 不可逆的，不能撤销的 |  Incapable of being reversed or undone.  It refers to a process, action, or change that cannot be returned to its previous state. | Example: The damage caused by the fire was nonrevertible.  这场大火造成的损失是不可逆转的。 The decision to delete the file was nonrevertible. 删除文件的决定是不可撤销的。 | 近义词: irreversible, irrevocable, irreparable | 反义词: reversible, revocable
