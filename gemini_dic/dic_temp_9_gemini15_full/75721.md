full-size:/fʊlˈsaɪz/| adj. | 全尺寸的，标准尺寸的 |  Describing something that is the standard or normal size, not reduced or miniaturized.  It often refers to cars, appliances, or other products. | Example: He bought a full-size pickup truck to haul his supplies.|近义词：standard-sized, regular-sized, complete|反义词：miniature, compact, small
