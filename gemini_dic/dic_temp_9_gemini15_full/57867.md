dreys:/dreɪz/| n.|松鼠巢，鸟巢|A drey is a nest built by a squirrel, typically in the branches of a tree.  It is usually made of twigs, leaves, and other materials.|例句：The squirrel scurried up the tree to its drey.  这只松鼠窜上树去它的巢穴。|例句：The bird's drey was hidden amongst the leaves. 这只鸟的巢隐藏在树叶中。|近义词：nest, burrow|反义词：无
