curation:/kjʊəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |策展；馆藏管理；选择；编辑 |The careful selection and preservation of something.  The act of curating.  This can refer to the selection of items for a museum exhibition, the choice of content for a website, or the careful gathering and presentation of information. |例句：The museum's curation of the ancient artifacts was meticulous.  博物馆对古代文物的馆藏管理非常细致。

例句：The curation of this online course involves selecting the most relevant materials. 这门在线课程的策划包括选择最相关的材料。


近义词：selection, editing, organization, preservation |反义词：neglect,abandonment,disorganization |词性:名词
