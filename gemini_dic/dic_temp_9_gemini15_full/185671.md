reverie:/ˈrevəri/| n. |白日梦；幻想；沉思 |A state of being lost in thought; daydreaming.  It often involves pleasant or dreamy thoughts. |例句：She was lost in a reverie, gazing out the window. 她陷入沉思，凝视着窗外。

例句：Lost in reverie, he didn't hear the doorbell.  沉思之中，他没有听到门铃声。


近义词：daydream, fantasy, musing, contemplation |反义词：reality, alertness, wakefulness |词性：名词


词源：源自古法语 *riverie，意为“漫游，闲逛”，最终来源于拉丁语 *riparia，意为“河岸”。  这反映了在河边漫步时容易陷入沉思的状态。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often drifts into a reverie. 他经常陷入沉思。
She has a reverie every afternoon. 她每天下午都会做白日梦。


一般过去时:
He fell into a reverie while listening to music. 他听音乐时陷入了沉思。
She had a pleasant reverie about her childhood. 她对童年做了一个美好的白日梦。


现在完成时:
I have had many reveries about my future. 我对我的未来有过许多幻想。
She has never experienced such a profound reverie before. 她以前从未有过如此深刻的沉思。


过去完成时:
He had already fallen into a reverie before I spoke to him. 我和他说话之前，他已经陷入了沉思。
She had been lost in reverie for hours. 她已经沉思了几个小时。


将来时:
I will have a reverie while traveling on the train. 我将在火车上做白日梦。
She will likely fall into a reverie during the long ceremony. 在漫长的仪式中，她可能会陷入沉思。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would indulge in a long reverie. 如果我有更多的时间，我会沉思很久。
I wish I could escape into a peaceful reverie. 我希望我能逃进平静的沉思中。


被动语态:
His attention was easily captured by a reverie. 他的注意力很容易被沉思所吸引。
Her thoughts were often lost in reverie. 她的思绪常常迷失在幻想中。


疑问句:

What kind of reverie was she lost in? 她沉迷于什么样的幻想？


否定句:
He didn't allow himself to fall into a reverie. 他不允许自己陷入沉思。
She wasn't capable of a reverie; she was too focused on her work. 她无法沉思；她太专注于她的工作了。
