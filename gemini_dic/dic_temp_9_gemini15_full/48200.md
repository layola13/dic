decompressors:/dɪkəmˈprɛsərz/ | n. |减压器，解压缩程序 | Devices or software that reduce pressure or extract data from a compressed file.  They are used in various applications, from scuba diving equipment to data processing. | Example:  The scuba diver checked his decompressors before the deep dive.  = 潜水员在深潜前检查了他的减压器。 The software uses advanced decompressors to quickly extract the files. = 该软件使用先进的解压缩程序快速提取文件。 |近义词：pressure reducers (for physical pressure), unzip utilities (for data compression) |反义词：compressors
