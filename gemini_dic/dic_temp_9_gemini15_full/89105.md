homager:/ˈhɒmɪdʒə(r)/| n. |效忠者；尊崇者；敬意表达者 |One who pays homage; one who shows respect or reverence.  A homager is someone who expresses homage, often through formal acts of respect or loyalty. |例句：The knight was a loyal homager to the king.  这位骑士是国王忠诚的效忠者。

例句：Many scholars are homagers to the great works of Shakespeare.  许多学者都对莎士比亚的伟大作品表示敬意。

近义词：worshipper, devotee, admirer, follower |反义词：traitor, rebel, opponent


词性：名词
