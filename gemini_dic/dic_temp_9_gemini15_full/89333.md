homogeneous:/ˌhɒməˈdʒiːnɪəs/ /ˌhoʊməˈdʒiːnɪəs/| adj.|同质的；均匀的|consisting of parts or elements that are all of the same kind; of the same kind or nature; uniform in structure or composition.|The mixture is homogeneous, with the components evenly distributed.|近义词：uniform, consistent, uniform|反义词：heterogeneous, diverse, varied
