freewheelers:/ˈfriːˌwiːlərz/| n.|自由车手，自由职业者，不受约束的人|People who are independent and unconventional, often disregarding rules or conventions.  They are typically characterized by their self-reliance and aversion to traditional structures.  The term often carries a slightly positive connotation, suggesting a spirit of adventure and freedom.|例句：The freewheelers of the 1960s challenged societal norms and embraced alternative lifestyles.  六十年代的自由主义者挑战社会规范，并接受另类生活方式。

The freewheelers in the cycling community often prefer mountain biking to road cycling. 自行车界的自由车手通常更喜欢山地自行车而不是公路自行车。|近义词：individualists, nonconformists, mavericks, adventurers|反义词：conformists, traditionalists, rule-followers
