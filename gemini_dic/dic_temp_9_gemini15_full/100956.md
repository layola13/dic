inuredness: /ɪnˈjʊərnəs/ | n. |习以为常；无动于衷；适应性 |  The state of being inured;  habituation to something unpleasant or difficult.  It implies a degree of emotional or physical resilience developed through repeated exposure.  | 例句：His years of hardship had fostered a remarkable inuredness to pain.  他多年的苦难培养了他对痛苦的惊人承受能力。

His inuredness to criticism was both a strength and a weakness. 他对批评的无动于衷既是优点也是缺点。

| 近义词：hardiness, resilience, callousness, insensitivity | 反义词：sensitivity, vulnerability, susceptibility
