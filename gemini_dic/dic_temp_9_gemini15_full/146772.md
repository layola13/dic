overidealism: /ˌoʊvərˌaɪdiəlɪzəm/ | n. | 过度理想化；理想主义过头 |  The belief in or pursuit of ideals that are unrealistic or impractical, often leading to disappointment.  It refers to a state where one's ideals are excessively high or divorced from reality.  |  His overidealism led him to believe he could single-handedly solve world hunger.  他的过度理想化让他相信自己可以单枪匹马解决世界饥饿问题。


近义词: utopianism, romanticism, impracticality, naivete

反义词: realism, pragmatism, cynicism
