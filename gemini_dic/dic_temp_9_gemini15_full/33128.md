christmases:/ˈkrɪsməsɪz/| n. |圣诞节们；许多圣诞节|The plural of Christmas.  It refers to multiple instances of Christmas, either across many years or in a metaphorical sense.  |例句：We've had some wonderful Christmases together over the years.  多年来我们一起度过了许多美好的圣诞节。

例句：He reminisced about his childhood Christmases. 他回忆起他童年的圣诞节们。


近义词：Christmas celebrations, Yuletides (less common)
反义词：无


词性:名词
