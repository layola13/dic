godlinesses:/ˈɡɑːdlɪnəsiz/| n. |虔诚(复数) |The various forms or manifestations of piety and devotion to God.  It emphasizes the plural nature, suggesting multiple aspects or expressions of godliness. |例句：The priest spoke of the many godlinesses he had witnessed in the congregation.  牧师谈到了他在会众中所目睹的许多虔诚行为。  The different godlinesses expressed in prayer, charity, and service are all valuable. 在祈祷、慈善和服务中表达的不同虔诚都是宝贵的。|近义词：pities, devotions, sanctities |反义词：wickedness, ungodliness, impiety |名词
