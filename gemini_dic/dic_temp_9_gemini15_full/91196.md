hydraulics:/haɪˈdrɔːlɪks/ | n. |水力学；液压学 |The branch of science and technology dealing with the practical application of liquid pressure and flow.  It encompasses the generation, control, and transmission of power using liquids.  |例句：The dam's construction required advanced knowledge of hydraulics. 这座大坝的建造需要先进的水力学知识。

例句：The car's braking system utilizes hydraulics to efficiently stop the vehicle. 这辆车的制动系统利用液压来有效地制动车辆。


近义词：hydrodynamics (更侧重于理论) | 反义词：无
