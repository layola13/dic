replicable:/ˈrɛplɪkəbl/| adj.| 可复制的，可重复的 | capable of being replicated or repeated; able to be reproduced or duplicated.  The experiment's results were replicable, confirming the initial findings.  实验结果可重复，证实了最初的发现。|近义词：reproducible, repeatable, duplicable |反义词：unreproducible, unique


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The study's methods are replicable. = 这项研究的方法是可复制的。
This software is easily replicable. = 这个软件很容易复制。


一般过去时:
The experiment was replicable. = 实验是可以重复的。
The process was easily replicable last year. = 去年这个过程很容易复制。


现在完成时:
Scientists have found the results replicable. = 科学家们已经发现结果是可重复的。
The company has made the design replicable. = 公司已经使设计可复制。


过去完成时:
The researchers had found the data replicable before publishing. = 研究人员在发表之前已经发现数据是可复制的。
They had made the software replicable by that point. = 到那时他们已经使软件可复制。


将来时:
The findings will be replicable in future studies. = 在未来的研究中，这些发现将是可以重复的。
This design will be easily replicable in mass production. = 这种设计在大规模生产中很容易复制。


虚拟语气:
If the experiment were replicable, we would have more confidence in the results. = 如果实验是可重复的，我们对结果就会更有信心。
If the process were easily replicable, it would be widely adopted. = 如果这个过程很容易复制，它就会被广泛采用。


被动语态:
The experiment's results are easily replicated. = 实验结果很容易被复制。
The software is being replicated by many users. = 该软件正在被许多用户复制。


疑问句:

Are the findings replicable across different populations? = 这些发现能否在不同人群中重复？


否定句:
The results were not replicable. = 结果不可重复。
The experiment's findings were not easily replicable. = 实验结果不容易复制。
