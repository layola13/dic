crossbreeding:/ˈkrɒsbriːdɪŋ/| n. |杂交育种；混种；异种交配|The process of breeding animals or plants of different breeds or varieties to produce offspring with desirable characteristics.  It aims to combine the strengths of different parent lines, potentially resulting in improved yield, disease resistance, or other beneficial traits. |Example:  Crossbreeding different breeds of cattle can lead to increased milk production.  杂交不同的牛品种可以提高牛奶产量。

Example: The farmer used crossbreeding techniques to create a new strain of wheat that was resistant to drought. 农民使用杂交育种技术培育出抗旱的小麦新品种。|近义词：hybridization, interbreeding |反义词：pure breeding, inbreeding
