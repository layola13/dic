self-designers:/ˈsɛlf dɪˈzaɪnərz/| n.|自主设计师；自行设计者|People who design and create things independently, often without formal training or the backing of a large organization. They may work on personal projects, freelance for clients, or build their own businesses around their designs.|例句：Many self-designers are finding success selling their creations online. 许多自主设计师通过在线销售自己的作品而获得成功。

The increasing popularity of online platforms has enabled many self-designers to reach a wider audience. 在线平台日益普及，使许多自主设计师能够接触到更广泛的受众。|近义词：independent designers, freelance designers|反义词：employed designers, corporate designers|名词
