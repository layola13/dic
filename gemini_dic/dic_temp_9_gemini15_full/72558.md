flutterers:/ˈflʌtərərz/| n.|飞舞的人或物；颤动的东西|Things that flutter; people or things that move lightly and quickly.|The butterflies were beautiful flutterers in the garden. 花园里蝴蝶飞舞，美极了。|The leaves were flutterers in the gentle breeze. 轻柔的微风中树叶轻轻飘动。|近义词：vibrators, fliers, dancers|反义词：stillness, stability


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The butterflies flutter in the garden. 蝴蝶在花园里飞舞。
The flag flutterers in the wind. 旗帜在风中飘扬。


一般过去时:
The birds fluttered their wings. 鸟儿拍打着翅膀。
The leaves fluttered down from the tree. 叶子从树上飘落下来。


现在完成时:
The birds have fluttered away. 鸟儿已经飞走了。
The leaves have fluttered to the ground. 叶子已经飘落到地面上了。


过去完成时:
The butterflies had fluttered away before I arrived. 我到达之前蝴蝶已经飞走了。
The leaves had fluttered down before the storm. 暴风雨来临之前叶子已经飘落下来了。


将来时:
The birds will flutter around the feeder. 鸟儿将会在喂食器周围飞舞。

虚拟语气:
If the birds fluttered closer, I could see them better. 如果鸟儿飞得更近一些，我就能看得更清楚了。

被动语态:

疑问句:
