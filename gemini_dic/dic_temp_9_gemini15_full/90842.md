hundredweights:/ˈhʌndrɪdweɪts/| n. |百磅重；百磅重的物品 |A hundredweight is an old unit of weight equal to 112 pounds (50.8 kilograms) in the avoirdupois system.  It's rarely used now, mostly in the UK and some Commonwealth countries. It refers to both the unit of weight and the amount of something weighing that much.|The farmer delivered five hundredweights of potatoes.  = 农民送来了五百磅土豆。  The shipment weighed several hundredweights. = 这批货重达几百磅。|近义词：quintal (metric hundredweight)|反义词：无
