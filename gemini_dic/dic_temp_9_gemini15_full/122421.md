moan:/moʊn/| /məʊn/ | n., v.|呻吟，抱怨 | To make a low, long sound expressing pain, unhappiness, or pleasure; to complain or express discontent. |例句：The wounded soldier moaned in pain. 受伤的士兵痛苦地呻吟着。

The baby moaned in its sleep.  婴儿睡梦中发出呻吟声。


例句：She moaned about the unfairness of it all. 她抱怨这一切的不公平。

He constantly moans about his job. 他不断抱怨他的工作。


近义词：groan, whine, complain, lament | 反义词：rejoice, celebrate, exult


词性：名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He moans every day. 他每天都抱怨。
She moans about the weather. 她抱怨天气。


一般过去时:
He moaned in his sleep last night. 他昨晚睡梦中呻吟。
She moaned about the long line. 她抱怨队伍太长。


现在完成时:
He has moaned about this problem for weeks.  他几周来一直在抱怨这个问题。
She has moaned and groaned all day. 她整天都在唉声叹气。


过去完成时:
He had moaned about the injustice before he left. 他离开之前就抱怨过这种不公正。
She had moaned all afternoon before finally falling asleep. 她整个下午都在呻吟，最后才睡着。


将来时:
He will moan about the workload tomorrow. 他明天会抱怨工作量。
She will moan if you don't help her.  如果你不帮她，她会抱怨的。


虚拟语气:
If he knew it was broken, he would moan. 如果他知道它坏了，他会抱怨的。
I wish she wouldn't moan so much. 我希望她不要抱怨那么多。


被动语态:
His complaints were moaned throughout the meeting. 他的抱怨在整个会议中都被表达出来。（被动语态用法较少见）
The problem was moaned about by many people. 这个问题被很多人抱怨。（被动语态用法较少见）


疑问句:

Why is she moaning? 她为什么在呻吟？


否定句:

She doesn't moan about small things. 她不会为小事抱怨。
