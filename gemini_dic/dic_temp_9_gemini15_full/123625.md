months:/mʌnθs/ | n. |月份；月数 |Months are periods of time typically consisting of 28 to 31 days.  Months are used to organize a year into twelve divisions. |Example: There are twelve months in a year.  一年有十二个月。  The project will take several months to complete.  这个项目需要几个月才能完成。|近义词：periods, cycles |反义词:days, weeks, years


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The months of the year are January, February, March and so on. = 一年的月份是一月、二月、三月等等。
Which months are the busiest for your business? = 哪几个月对你的生意来说是最繁忙的？


一般过去时:
The months passed quickly. = 月份过得很快。
We travelled extensively during the summer months. = 我们在夏季的几个月里进行了广泛的旅行。


现在完成时:
The months have flown by since we last saw each other. = 自从我们上次见面以来，几个月已经过去了。
How many months have you been working on this project? = 你在这个项目上工作了几个月了？


过去完成时:
By the time they arrived, several months had passed. = 当他们到达时，已经过去了几个月。
She realized that many months had gone by since she last contacted him. = 她意识到自从她上次和他联系以来，已经过去了许多个月。


将来时:
In the coming months, we will launch a new product. = 在接下来的几个月里，我们将推出一个新产品。
The project will be finished within the next six months. = 该项目将在未来六个月内完成。


虚拟语气:
If the months were longer, we would have more time. = 如果月份更长，我们会更多的时间。
I wish the months were shorter during this difficult time. = 我希望在这段艰难的时期里月份能短一些。


被动语态:
The months were meticulously planned. = 几个月被精心计划。
The calendar months are traditionally named after various Roman gods and figures. = 日历月份传统上是以各种罗马神和人物命名的。


疑问句:
How many months are there in a year? = 一年有多少个月？
Which months are considered peak season? = 哪几个月被认为是旺季？


否定句:

It wasn't the months of hard work that bothered him, but the outcome. = 困扰他的不是几个月的辛勤工作，而是结果。
