reconceptualizes:/ˌriːkənˈsɛptʃuəlaɪzɪz/ | v. |重新概念化；重新构想 | To rethink and redefine a concept or idea; to present a concept in a new way.  |The artist reconceptualizes traditional landscapes by incorporating elements of modern urban life.  艺术家通过融入现代都市生活的元素来重新构想传统的风景画。|reimagine, reformulate, reinterpret |  conform, maintain |动词
