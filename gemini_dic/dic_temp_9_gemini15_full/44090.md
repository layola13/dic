crippler:/ˈkrɪplər/| n. v.|使残废的人或事；严重削弱者；严重破坏者|Something or someone that cripples; something that severely weakens or damages.  A person who is crippled.|例句：The accident was a crippler, leaving him unable to walk.  这场事故使他残废，无法行走。The new regulations are a crippler for small businesses. 新规对小型企业来说是毁灭性的。|近义词：incapacitator, debilitator, destroyer, wrecker|反义词：strengthener, enhancer, restorer


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The storm cripples the power grid. = 暴风雨瘫痪了电网。
That policy cripples small businesses. = 那项政策严重损害小型企业。


一般过去时:
The earthquake crippled the city. = 地震摧毁了这座城市。
The injury crippled his athletic career. = 这伤势毁了他的运动员生涯。


现在完成时:
The drought has crippled the agricultural sector. = 干旱已经重创了农业部门。
The sanctions have crippled the country's economy. = 制裁已经使该国经济瘫痪。


过去完成时:
The previous war had crippled the nation's infrastructure. = 先前的战争已经摧毁了国家的基础设施。
Years of neglect had crippled the school system. = 多年的忽视已经使学校系统瘫痪。


将来时:
The coming recession will cripple many businesses. = 即将来临的经济衰退将使许多企业瘫痪。
Further cuts will cripple the hospital's services. = 进一步的削减将使医院的服务瘫痪。


虚拟语气:
If the virus were to spread, it would cripple the healthcare system. = 如果病毒蔓延，它将使医疗系统瘫痪。
I wish the disease hadn't crippled my grandmother. = 我真希望这种疾病没有使我祖母残废。


被动语态:
The city was crippled by the flood. = 该城市被洪水摧毁了。
The economy is being crippled by inflation. = 经济正在被通货膨胀拖垮。


疑问句:
What crippled the airplane's engine? = 什么导致飞机引擎瘫痪？
Will the new taxes cripple the local businesses? = 新税收会使当地企业瘫痪吗？


否定句:
The storm didn't cripple the transportation system completely. = 暴风雨并没有完全瘫痪交通系统。
The reforms didn't cripple the economy as predicted. = 改革并没有像预测的那样使经济瘫痪。


PHRASE->

a crippler of a blow = 一记重击
a crippler of a disease = 一种严重的疾病
a crippler of a problem = 一个极难解决的问题
economic crippler = 经济杀手
crippler of hope = 令人绝望的事物
