point-to-point:/ˈpɔɪnt tuː ˈpɔɪnt/| adj.|点对点的，逐点的|Direct connection between two points without intermediate points or nodes.  This term is often used in communications, transportation, and other contexts where a direct link is established.|例句：The new communication system offers point-to-point connections for enhanced security.  新的通讯系统提供点对点连接以增强安全性。|例句：The race is a point-to-point event, starting in the city center and finishing at the stadium. 这场比赛是一场点对点赛事，从市中心开始，在体育场结束。|近义词：direct, straight-through|反义词：indirect, circuitous|形容词
