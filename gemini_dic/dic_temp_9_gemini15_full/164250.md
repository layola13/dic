predoubters:/prɪˈdaʊtərz/| n.|怀疑者，持怀疑态度的人|People who doubt or express skepticism beforehand, especially regarding a new idea, project, or person.  They often voice their concerns or objections before sufficient evidence is available. |例句：The predoubters dismissed the new CEO's plan as unrealistic before even hearing the full presentation.  怀疑者们甚至在听完完整的陈述之前就认为新任CEO的计划不切实际。|近义词：skeptics, doubters, cynics, naysayers|反义词：believers, supporters, optimists
