pseudo-human: /ˈsjuːdoʊˌhjuːmən/ | adj. | 似人的，伪人的，非真正的人的 |  Appearing or seeming human but not genuinely so; artificial or imitative of human qualities.  | 例句：The AI exhibited pseudo-human behavior, responding emotionally to certain prompts.  人工智能表现出类似人类的行为，对某些提示做出情绪化的回应。

例句：The advanced robot had a pseudo-human appearance but lacked genuine empathy. 这台先进的机器人拥有类似人类的外表，但却缺乏真正的同理心。


近义词：artificial, synthetic, simulated, quasi-human, automaton-like


反义词：genuine, authentic, real, human


词性：形容词


词源：

* **pseudo-** (from Greek *pseudēs* meaning "false," "lying")  伪的，假的
* **human** (from Latin *humanus* meaning "human," "of mankind") 人类的


USAGE->
(由于pseudo-human是一个形容词，它本身不构成谓语，因此没有时态变化。以下例句展示了其在不同句式中的用法。)

肯定句:
The program created a pseudo-human character for the game. = 该程序为游戏创建了一个类似人类的角色。
His pseudo-human responses were unnervingly realistic. = 他伪人般的回应令人不安地逼真。


否定句:
That wasn't a genuine human; it was a pseudo-human construct. = 那不是真正的人类；它是一个伪人结构。
The robot, despite its appearance, wasn't truly pseudo-human; it was simply programmed well. = 尽管这机器人看起来很像，但它并非真正地类似人类；它只是编程得很好。


疑问句:
Is that a real person, or a pseudo-human simulation? = 那是真人吗，还是伪人模拟？
Could this pseudo-human interaction be improved? = 这种类似人类的互动可以改进吗？



PHRASE->
(由于pseudo-human是形容词，它通常不与其他词构成固定词组。以下例句展示其在短语中的用法。)
