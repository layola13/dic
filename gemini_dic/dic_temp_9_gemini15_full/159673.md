polyadelphia:/ˌpɒliəˈdɛlfiə/| adj.|多体雄蕊的|Having stamens united into more than two bundles.  This term is primarily used in botany to describe the arrangement of stamens in a flower.  The stamens, the male reproductive organs, are grouped together in multiple bundles or brotherhoods.  This is in contrast to flowers with diadelphous (two bundles) or monadelphous (one bundle) stamens.|例句：The flower exhibits a polyadelphous condition, with its stamens fused into several distinct bundles.  多体雄蕊的这种状态在花朵中可见，其雄蕊融合成几个不同的束。


The flower's polyadelphous stamens are a key characteristic for identification. 花的多体雄蕊是鉴定的一个关键特征。|近义词：multiadelphous (though less common)|反义词：monadelphous, diadelphous, polyandrous (many stamens, but not bundled)|词性：形容词
