eschewing:/ɪˈskuːɪŋ/| v.|避免；避开；放弃|To avoid or shun (something, typically something regarded as unpleasant or undesirable).  It implies a deliberate and conscious decision to stay away from something.|例句：He eschewed the use of violence. 他避免使用暴力。

例句：She eschewed luxury and lived a simple life. 她避开奢华，过着简朴的生活。


近义词：avoiding, shunning, rejecting, forgoing, abstaining from

反义词：embracing, accepting, adopting, welcoming


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eschews all forms of violence. = 他避免一切形式的暴力。
They eschew unnecessary risks in their business dealings. = 他们在商业交易中避免不必要的风险。

一般过去时:
She eschewed the opportunity to travel abroad. = 她放弃了出国旅行的机会。
He eschewed the temptation of instant gratification. = 他拒绝了立即满足的诱惑。

现在完成时:
I have eschewed processed foods for the past year. = 在过去的一年中，我一直避免食用加工食品。
They have eschewed political endorsements in their campaign. = 在他们的竞选中，他们一直避免进行政治背书。

过去完成时:
Having eschewed a college education, he learned a trade. = 他放弃了大学教育，学习了一门技术。
She regretted having eschewed the advice of her mentor. = 她后悔没有听从导师的建议。

将来时:
He will eschew any involvement in the scandal. = 他将避免卷入任何丑闻。
They will eschew risky investments in the future. = 他们将来会避免进行高风险投资。

虚拟语气:
If he eschewed gambling, he would be wealthier. = 如果他不赌博，他会更有钱。
I wish I had eschewed that impulsive purchase. = 我真希望我没有做过那次冲动购物。

被动语态:
Violence was eschewed by the peace negotiators. = 和平谈判者避免了暴力。
Luxury was eschewed by the ascetic monk. = 苦行僧避开了奢华。

疑问句:
Does he eschew social media? = 他避免使用社交媒体吗？
Will they eschew the use of chemicals in their farming? = 他们在农业生产中会避免使用化学物质吗？

否定句:
He did not eschew the challenges ahead. = 他没有回避未来的挑战。
They did not eschew the opportunity to expand their business. = 他们没有放弃扩大业务的机会。
