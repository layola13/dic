rerack:/ˌriːˈræk/| vt.|重新架上；重新排列|To place (something) back on a rack or in a rack-like arrangement.|例句：He reracked the weights after his workout. 他锻炼后把杠铃重新放回架子上。|例句：The librarian reracked the returned books. 图书管理员把归还的书重新放回书架上。|近义词：re-shelve, rearrange, restock|反义词：remove, unload
