electrotyper:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌtaɪpər/| n. |电铸工，电版制作人|A person who makes electrotypes.  Electrotypers create printing plates by using an electrolytic process to deposit a layer of metal onto a mold of the original type. |The electrotyper carefully prepared the plates for the printing press. 电铸工仔细地为印刷机准备了印版。|  |  |noun
