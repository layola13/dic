lexicology: /ˌlɛksɪˈkɑːlədʒi/ | n. | 词汇学 | The branch of linguistics that studies the vocabulary of a language.  It examines the origins, meanings, development, and use of words.  Lexicology explores the relationships between words, their semantic fields, and how they are organized within a language system. | Example:  Lexicology is a fascinating field that reveals the rich history and evolution of language.  词汇学是一个迷人的领域，它揭示了语言丰富多彩的历史和演变。 | 近义词: word study, vocabulary study | 反义词: (no direct antonym)
