marquess:/ˈmɑːkwɪs/| n. |侯爵|A marquess is a nobleman ranking above an earl and below a duke.  The title is hereditary, passed down through the male line of the family.  The female equivalent is marchioness.|例句：He inherited the title of Marquess from his father. 他继承了他父亲的侯爵爵位。

例句：The Marquess hosted a grand ball at his stately home.  这位侯爵在他的庄严的家中举办了一场盛大的舞会。


近义词：duke (higher rank), earl (lower rank)
反义词：commoner


词性：名词
