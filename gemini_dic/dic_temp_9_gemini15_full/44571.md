croucher:/ˈkraʊtʃər/| n.|蹲伏者，蜷缩者|A person who crouches.  Someone who habitually assumes a crouching posture.|例句：The cat was a skilled croucher, able to remain hidden for hours.  这只猫是一个熟练的蹲伏者，能够隐藏数小时。
The croucher in the shadows startled the guard.  阴影中的蹲伏者吓到了警卫。|近义词：squatter, huncher|反义词：stander, upright person|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He crouches behind the wall. = 他蹲在墙后。
The cat crouches before pouncing. = 猫在蹲伏准备跳跃。


一般过去时:
She crouched down to examine the flower. = 她蹲下检查花朵。
The thief crouched behind the bush. = 小偷躲在灌木丛后面。


现在完成时:
He has crouched here for hours. = 他在这里蹲了几个小时了。
The dog has crouched patiently waiting for its food. = 狗耐心地蹲着等食物。


过去完成时:
They had crouched there before the explosion. = 爆炸前他们就躲在那里了。
The bird had crouched low to the ground to avoid being seen. = 鸟儿为了不被看见而低伏在地面上。


将来时:
She will crouch down to tie her shoelaces. = 她将蹲下系鞋带。
He will crouch behind the rock for cover. = 他将躲在岩石后面掩护。


虚拟语气:
If I were a cat, I would crouch silently. = 如果我是一只猫，我会悄无声息地蹲下。
I wish I could crouch as low as that snake. = 我真希望我能像那条蛇一样低伏。


被动语态: (被动语态在描述“蹲”这个动作时不常见，因为通常是主动行为)
The figure was crouched in the corner.（描述状态，而非动作）=  那个身影蜷缩在角落里。  


疑问句:
Is he crouching behind the tree? = 他躲在树后吗？
Why are they crouching? = 他们为什么蹲着？


否定句:
He didn't crouch down to pick it up. = 他没有蹲下来捡它。
She didn't crouch low enough to avoid being seen. = 她蹲得不够低，以至于被看见了。


PHRASE->
crouch down = 蹲下
crouch low = 低伏
crouch in fear = 害怕地蹲下
crouch behind = 躲在...后面
crouch over = 弯腰伏在...上
