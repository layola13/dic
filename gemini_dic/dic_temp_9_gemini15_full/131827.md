nonconsideration:/ˌnɒnkənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃən/| n. |未予考虑；忽视；忽略|The failure to consider something; disregard.  It often implies a lack of due diligence or attention to detail.  The contract was void due to nonconsideration of the crucial safety aspects.|近义词：neglect, disregard, oversight, omission |反义词：consideration, attention, regard
