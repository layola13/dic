reitemized:/ˌriˈaɪtəmaɪzd/| adj.|重新列举的，重新详细说明的|Reitemized refers to something that has been listed again, often with more detail or in a different way than the original listing.  It implies a process of revising or refining a previous itemization.|例句：The accountant reitemized the expenses to clarify the discrepancies. 会计重新列举了支出以澄清差异。The client requested a reitemized invoice to better understand the charges. 客户要求重新列出发票以更好地理解费用。|近义词：re-listed, detailed, specified|反义词：summarized, consolidated


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, and thus doesn't have verb conjugations like "what" does.  It describes the state of being re-itemized.  Therefore, tense variations are not applicable.  Example sentences will demonstrate its usage in different sentence structures.
