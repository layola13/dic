double-jointed:/ˈdʌbəlˈdʒɔɪntɪd/| adj.|关节松弛的，柔软的|Having joints that bend more than usual.  This condition allows for an unusual range of motion.  It is often considered a genetic trait.|例句：He's double-jointed and can bend his thumb backward. 他关节松弛，可以把拇指向后弯曲。

例句：The contortionist was double-jointed, allowing her to perform amazing feats of flexibility.  这位柔术演员关节很软，可以做出令人惊叹的柔韧动作。

近义词：hypermobile, flexible, supple

反义词：stiff, inflexible, rigid

词性:形容词
