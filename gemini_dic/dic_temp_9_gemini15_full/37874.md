complacence:/kəmˈpleɪsns/| adj.|自满；满足；沾沾自喜|A feeling of smug or uncritical satisfaction with oneself or one's achievements.|His complacency about his work led to his downfall. 他的工作上的自满导致了他的失败。|近义词：self-satisfaction, smugness, complacency|反义词：humility, dissatisfaction, insecurity|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is complacent about his success. = 他对自己的成功感到沾沾自喜。
She shows a complacent attitude towards her work. = 她对自己的工作表现出一种自满的态度。


一般过去时:
He was complacent after winning the competition. = 比赛获胜后，他很自满。
They were complacent about their team's performance. = 他们对球队的表现感到自满。


现在完成时:
He has been complacent since he got promoted. = 自从升职以来，他就一直很自满。
She has remained complacent despite the criticism. = 尽管受到了批评，她仍然保持着自满的态度。


过去完成时:
He had been complacent for years before the crisis hit. = 在危机来临之前的几年里，他一直都很自满。
They had become complacent about safety procedures. = 他们对安全规程已经变得麻痹大意了。


将来时:
He will be complacent if he wins again. = 如果他再次获胜，他会很自满。
She will likely remain complacent unless she faces serious challenges. = 除非她面临严重的挑战，否则她很可能会继续保持自满。


虚拟语气:
If he were not so complacent, he might improve. = 如果他不那么自满，他可能会进步。
I wish he weren't so complacent about his skills. = 我希望他不那么自满于自己的技能。


被动语态:
His success was wrongly interpreted as complacency. = 他的成功被错误地理解为自满。
Complacency is often seen as a major obstacle to progress. = 自满往往被视为进步的主要障碍。


疑问句:
Is he complacent about his achievements? = 他对自己的成就感到自满吗？
Are they complacent in the face of danger? = 面对危险，他们是否自满？


否定句:
He is not complacent; he is always striving for improvement. = 他并不自满；他总是努力寻求改进。
They were not complacent; they worked hard to maintain their lead. = 他们并不自满；他们努力保持领先地位。
