expansively:/ɪkˈspænsɪvli/ | adv. | 开阔地；广阔地；详尽地；滔滔不绝地 | In a way that is expansive; covering a large area;  characterized by breadth, scope, or range; in a free and open manner;  at great length. | 例句：He spoke expansively about his travels. 他滔滔不绝地谈论了他的旅行。


例句：The landscape stretched expansively before them.  景色在他们面前广阔地延伸。


近义词：widely, broadly, comprehensively, volubly, profusely


反义词：briefly, concisely, narrowly, restrictively
