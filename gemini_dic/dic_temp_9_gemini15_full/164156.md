predisrupting:/ˌpriːdɪsˈrʌptɪŋ/| v.|预先扰乱，提前破坏|The act of disrupting something before it even happens; anticipating and preventing disruption.|例句：The company implemented predisrupting measures to prevent potential supply chain issues.  公司实施了预先扰乱措施，以防止潜在的供应链问题。


例句：The government's predisrupting strategy aimed to neutralize the threat before it escalated. 政府的预先扰乱策略旨在在威胁升级之前将其消除。


近义词：preempting, forestalling, preventing, anticipating


反义词：allowing, facilitating, enabling


词性:动词
