maintenance: /ˈmeɪntənəns/ | n. | 保养；维护；维修 | The act of maintaining something in good working order.  It encompasses the activities involved in keeping something in its original condition, preventing deterioration, or repairing damage. | 例句：The maintenance of this building is costly.  这栋建筑的维护费用很高。

例句：Regular maintenance of your car will prolong its life. 定期保养你的汽车会延长它的使用寿命。


近义词： upkeep, preservation, repair, servicing | 反义词：neglect, destruction, demolition | 词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company performs regular maintenance on its equipment. = 公司定期维护其设备。
Maintenance costs are a significant part of our budget. = 维修成本是我们预算中相当大的一部分。


一般过去时:
They performed extensive maintenance on the bridge last year. = 他们去年对这座桥进行了大规模的维修。

现在完成时:
We have completed all scheduled maintenance for this quarter. = 我们已经完成了本季度的所有计划维护。

过去完成时:

将来时:
