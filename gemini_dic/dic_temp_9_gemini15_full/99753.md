intermeddle:/ˌɪntərˈmɛdəl/| vt.|干涉；干预；多管闲事|To interfere in something that is not one's concern.|例句：It's not wise to intermeddle in other people's affairs.  不要插手别人的事。|例句：She always intermeddles in her neighbors' business.|她总是干涉邻居的事务。|近义词：interfere, meddle, intrude|反义词：ignore, mind one's own business|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He intermeddles in everyone's business. = 他干涉每个人的事。
They intermeddle in affairs that don't concern them. = 他们干涉与自己无关的事情。


一般过去时:
She intermeddled in her sister's marriage. = 她干涉她妹妹的婚姻。
He intermeddled in the company's affairs. = 他干预了公司的业务。


现在完成时:
He has intermeddled in too many projects. = 他干预了太多的项目。
They have intermeddled in our plans. = 他们干预了我们的计划。


过去完成时:
She had intermeddled in their relationship before. = 她以前就干涉过他们的关系。
They had intermeddled in the investigation. = 他们已经干预了调查。


将来时:
He will intermeddle again if given the chance. = 如果有机会，他会再次干涉。
She will intermeddle in his life again. = 她还会再次干涉他的生活。


虚拟语气:
If he intermeddled less, he would have more friends. = 如果他少管闲事，就会有更多朋友。
I wish she hadn't intermeddled in our argument. = 我真希望她不要干涉我们的争吵。


被动语态:
Their plans were intermeddled with. = 他们的计划被干预了。
His affairs were intermeddled in by his mother. = 他的事务被他母亲干预了。


疑问句:
Did he intermeddle in the negotiations? = 他是否干预了谈判？
Will they intermeddle in the election? = 他们会干预选举吗？


否定句:
He did not intermeddle in the matter. = 他没有干预此事。
She does not intermeddle in her colleagues' work. = 她不干涉同事的工作。


PHRASE->
intermeddle in = 干涉...
intermeddle with = 干预...
intermeddle in other people's business = 干涉别人的事
intermeddle in politics = 干预政治
intermeddle unnecessarily = 不必要的干涉


词源:
inter- (between, among) + meddle (to interfere)  源于古法语 *mesler,  最终源于拉丁语 *miscere (to mix).
