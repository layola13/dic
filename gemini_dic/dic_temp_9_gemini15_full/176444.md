re-keyboards:/ˌriːˈkiːbɔːrdz/| n. |重新设置键盘；重新配置键盘 |The process of changing the keyboard layout or replacing the keyboard with a new one.  This could involve changing key mappings, replacing a faulty keyboard, or installing a new keyboard entirely.|例句：The IT department had to re-keyboards all the computers after the software update.  IT部门在软件更新后不得不重新设置所有电脑的键盘。 The old keyboard was malfunctioning, so we had to re-keyboards the entire system. 旧键盘失灵了，所以我们不得不重新配置整个系统的键盘。|近义词：reconfigure, replace, reinstall|反义词：N/A
