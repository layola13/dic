misunderstands:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstændz/ | v. |误解，误会 | To fail to understand correctly; interpret wrongly.  | 例句：He misunderstands my instructions. 他误解了我的指示。

He misunderstands the situation completely. 他完全误解了情况。


近义词：misinterpret, misconstrue, mistake | 反义词：understand


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misunderstands my intentions. = 他误解了我的意图。
They misunderstand the rules of the game. = 他们误解了游戏的规则。


一般过去时:
I misunderstood the question. = 我误解了这个问题。
She misunderstood his silence. = 她误解了他的沉默。


现在完成时:
I have misunderstood you all along. = 一直以来我都误解你了。
He has misunderstood the instructions and made a mistake. = 他误解了指示，犯了一个错误。


过去完成时:
I had misunderstood the map, and therefore got lost. = 我误解了地图，所以迷路了。
They had misunderstood the agreement, which led to the conflict. = 他们误解了协议，导致了冲突。


将来时:
I fear he will misunderstand my actions. = 我担心他会误解我的行为。
They will misunderstand the message if we don't explain it clearly. = 如果我们不解释清楚，他们会误解这个信息。


虚拟语气:
If I had explained it better, he wouldn't have misunderstood. = 如果我解释得更好，他就不会误解了。
I wish I hadn't misunderstood her. = 我真希望我没有误解她。


被动语态:
The instructions were misunderstood by many students. = 许多学生误解了指示。
His words were misunderstood by his colleagues. = 他的话被同事们误解了。


疑问句:
Do you think they misunderstood me? = 你认为他们误解了我吗？
Could he have misunderstood the email? = 他可能误解了电子邮件吗？


否定句:
I didn't misunderstand him; he was deliberately vague. = 我没有误解他；他故意含糊其辞。
She didn't misunderstand the assignment; she just didn't do it. = 她并没有误解作业；她只是没有做。


PHRASE->
misunderstand completely = 完全误解
misunderstand each other = 彼此误解
misunderstand the situation = 误解情况
misunderstand someone's intentions = 误解某人的意图
misunderstand the instructions = 误解指示


词源:
mis- (错误地) + understand (理解)
