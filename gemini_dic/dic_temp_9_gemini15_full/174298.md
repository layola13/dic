queued:/kjuːd/| v. |排队；(使)等候|To form or join a line or queue; to wait in line.|例句：Many people queued up for the concert tickets. 很多人排队买演唱会门票。

例句：The customers queued patiently for their turn.  顾客耐心地排队等候。


近义词：lined up, waited, stood in line|反义词：skipped, bypassed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I queue for coffee every morning. = 我每天早上都排队买咖啡。
They queue at the bus stop. = 他们在公交车站排队。

一般过去时:
We queued for hours to get tickets. = 我们排了好几个小时的队才买到票。
She queued outside the cinema. = 她在电影院外面排队。

现在完成时:
I have queued for this movie for an hour. = 我为这部电影排队已经一个小时了。
They have queued since early morning. = 他们从清晨就开始排队了。

过去完成时:
We had queued for two hours before the doors opened. = 门打开之前我们已经排了两个小时的队了。
She had queued earlier, so she got a good seat. = 她之前就排队了，所以她拿到一个好座位。

将来时:
I will queue for the new iPhone. = 我将会排队购买新的iPhone。
They will queue early to secure a place. = 他们会早点排队以确保一个位置。

虚拟语气:
If I had known it would be this long, I wouldn't have queued. = 如果我知道要排这么久，我就不会排队了。
I wish I hadn't queued; it was a waste of time. = 我真希望我没有排队；那是浪费时间。

被动语态:
The customers were queued efficiently. = 顾客们被有效地安排排队。
The tickets were queued for by many fans. = 很多粉丝都排队购买门票。

疑问句:
Are you queued up for the concert? = 你在为演唱会排队吗？
How long have they been queued? = 他们排队多久了？

否定句:
I didn't queue for the tickets. = 我没有排队买票。
They didn't queue because they arrived late. = 他们因为到的晚所以没有排队。


PHRASE->

jump the queue: 插队
at the head of the queue: 排在队伍的最前面
