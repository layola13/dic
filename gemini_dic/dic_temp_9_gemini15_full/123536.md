monotropa:/məˈnɒtrəpə/| n.|单花木，水晶兰|A plant of the genus Monotropa, typically lacking chlorophyll and having whitish or yellowish stems and flowers.  These plants are myco-heterotrophic, meaning they derive their nutrients from fungi rather than photosynthesis.|Example: The ghostly white monotropa, also known as ghost plant or corpse plant, thrived in the dimly lit forest undergrowth.|近义词：ghost plant, corpse plant, Indian pipe|反义词：photosynthetic plant
