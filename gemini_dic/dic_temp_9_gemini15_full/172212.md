push-start:/ˈpʊʃstɑːrt/| n. | 推启动装置；启动方式| A push-start system is a method of starting an engine without using a traditional key ignition.  It typically involves pressing a button to engage the starter motor. |例句：My new car has a push-start ignition system.  我的新车有推启动点火系统。|例句：The motorcycle uses a push-start mechanism. 这辆摩托车使用推启动机构。|近义词：keyless ignition, button start |反义词：key ignition, manual start |名词
