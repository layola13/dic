mythologizer:/ˈmɪθəlɒdʒaɪzər/ | n. |神话化者，赋予神话色彩的人 | One who creates or interprets myths; one who presents something as mythical.  Someone who mythologizes tends to exaggerate or romanticize events or figures, often obscuring the truth in the process. | Example:  The biographer was accused of being a mythologizer, portraying the subject's life as far more adventurous than it actually was.  | 近义词: romanticizer, fabulist, legend-maker | 反义词: realist, chronicler
