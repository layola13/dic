draw-off:/drɔːˈɔːf/| vt. |抽出，放出；转移；调走|To remove (a liquid) from a container or vessel; to withdraw or divert something.|例句：The engineer drew off the excess water from the tank. 工程师将水箱中多余的水抽掉。

例句：The company drew off some of its best employees to a new project. 公司将一些最好的员工调到一个新的项目中。


近义词：withdraw, extract, divert, transfer|反义词：add, pour in, replenish|词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He draws off the wine from the barrel. 他从酒桶里抽出葡萄酒。
The factory draws off waste water into a special tank. 工厂将废水排放到一个特殊的储水罐中。


一般过去时:
They drew off the fuel from the damaged vehicle. 他们从受损车辆中抽出了燃料。
The farmer drew off water from the irrigation canal. 农民从灌溉渠中抽水。


现在完成时:
We have drawn off all the contaminated water. 我们已经抽干了所有受污染的水。
The company has drawn off a significant portion of its investment. 公司已经撤回了一大部分投资。


过去完成时:
Before the flood, they had drawn off most of the water from the dam. 洪水来临之前，他们已经从水坝中排出了大部分水。
The project had drawn off considerable resources from the main business. 这个项目已经从主要业务中抽调了相当多的资源。


将来时:
They will draw off the oil from the well tomorrow. 他们明天将从油井中抽油。
The city will draw off extra water from the reservoir during the drought. 在干旱期间，城市将从水库中抽取额外的水。


虚拟语气:
If we had drawn off the water sooner, the damage would have been less. 如果我们早点把水抽掉，损失就会小一些。
I wish we had drawn off more funds for research. 我希望我们为研究拨出更多资金。


被动语态:
The water was drawn off from the lake. 湖里的水被抽干了。
The funds were drawn off from the account without authorization. 资金未经授权就被从账户中划走了。


疑问句:
How much water did they draw off? 他们抽了多少水？
Will the extra water be drawn off before the storm? 暴风雨来临之前会把多余的水抽掉吗？


否定句:
They didn't draw off the water in time. 他们没有及时抽水。
The company did not draw off its investment. 公司没有撤回投资。
