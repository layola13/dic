contemporaries:/kənˈtɛmpərɛriz/| n. |同时代的人；同龄人 |People who live or exist at the same time.  The term often refers to people of similar age or within the same historical period, sharing similar experiences and cultural contexts. |例句：Shakespeare's contemporaries included other renowned playwrights like Christopher Marlowe.  莎士比亚的同代人包括其他著名的剧作家，如克里斯托弗·马洛。

例句：Many of his contemporaries admired his groundbreaking work in physics. 他的许多同时代的人都钦佩他在物理学上的开创性工作。


近义词：peers, contemporaries, fellows

反义词：predecessors, successors


词性: 名词
