honestness:/ˈɑːnɪsti/| n. |诚实；正直|The quality or fact of being honest; truthfulness or trustworthiness.  It refers to a person's disposition to be truthful and sincere in their actions and words. |例句：Her honesty impressed everyone.  她的诚实给每个人留下了深刻的印象。

例句：His honesty is beyond question.  他的诚实毋庸置疑。


近义词：integrity, truthfulness, probity, uprightness, sincerity |反义词：dishonesty, deceit, deception, fraud, duplicity|词性：名词
