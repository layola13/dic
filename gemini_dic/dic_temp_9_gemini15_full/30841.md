chachaed:/tʃætʃt/ | v. |恰恰舞; 兴奋地跳舞 | To dance the cha-cha, a lively dance characterized by its rhythmic steps and hip movements.  It can also imply dancing excitedly or playfully. | 例句：They cha-chaed the night away at the wedding reception. 他们在婚礼招待会上跳了一整晚的恰恰舞。

They chachaed with unrestrained joy after hearing the good news. 听到好消息后，他们兴奋地跳起了恰恰舞。| 近义词：danced, swayed, jived | 反义词：stood still, remained motionless | 动词
