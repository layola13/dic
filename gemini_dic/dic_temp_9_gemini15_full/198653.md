shutterless:/ˈʃʌtərləs/| adj.|无快门的；无百叶窗的|Lacking a shutter or shutters.  The term often refers to cameras without a mechanical shutter, relying instead on electronic methods for exposure control.  It can also describe windows or doors without shutters.|例句：The new camera boasts a shutterless design, enabling faster shooting speeds. 新相机采用无快门设计，能够实现更快的拍摄速度。

The photographer preferred a shutterless camera for its ability to capture fast-moving subjects. 摄影师更喜欢无快门相机，因为它能够捕捉快速移动的物体。|近义词：shutter-free|反义词：with shutter
