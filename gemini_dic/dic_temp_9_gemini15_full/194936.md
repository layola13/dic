semi-isolated:/ˈsɛmiˌaɪsəleɪtɪd/| adj.|半隔离的，相对孤立的|Partially isolated; neither completely isolated nor fully integrated.  It suggests a degree of separation or independence, but not total seclusion or complete integration.|例句：The village is semi-isolated, accessible only by a rough track.  这个村庄相对孤立，只能通过一条崎岖的小路到达。


例句：The researcher worked in a semi-isolated laboratory to prevent contamination.  研究人员在半隔离的实验室工作以防止污染。


近义词：partially isolated, relatively isolated, secluded (to a lesser extent)


反义词：integrated, connected, fully accessible


词性：形容词
