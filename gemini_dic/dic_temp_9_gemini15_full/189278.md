sadhu:/ˈsɑːdhuː/ | n. |圣人，苦行僧 | A sadhu is a Hindu ascetic who has renounced worldly life and dedicated themselves to spiritual pursuits.  They often live a life of poverty and simplicity, and may travel extensively.  Many sadhus are recognizable by their distinctive orange robes and long hair. |例句：The sadhu sat meditating under the banyan tree.  这位苦行僧坐在榕树下冥想。

Many sadhus live in the Himalayas. 许多苦行僧居住在喜马拉雅山脉。|近义词：ascetic, hermit, yogi |反义词：materialist, hedonist |名词
