lamest: /ˈleɪməst/ | adj. | 最无趣的，最乏味的 |  The most boring or uninspired.  It is the superlative form of "lame". | 例句：That was the lamest excuse I've ever heard.  = 那是我听过最烂的借口。

That movie was the lamest thing I've ever seen. = 那部电影是我看过的最无聊的东西。


近义词：most boring, dullest, least interesting,  most uninspired,  weakest,  most pathetic


反义词：most exciting, most interesting, most thrilling, most inspiring


词性：形容词
