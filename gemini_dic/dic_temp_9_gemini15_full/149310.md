/pæk/| n. v.|包裹；捆；背包；一群；一组|a bundle of things tied or wrapped together; a container for carrying things on one's back; a group or collection of things or people|例句：He packed his bag for the trip. 他为旅行打包了行李。
He packed the boxes carefully. 他仔细地打包了箱子。|近义词：bundle, package, group, collection|反义词：unpack, scatter, disperse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He packs his lunch every day. = 他每天都打包午餐。
She packs her suitcase for the journey. = 她为旅程打包行李箱。

一般过去时:
They packed their belongings before moving. = 搬家前他们打包了他们的行李。
He packed the fragile items with extra care. = 他非常小心地打包了易碎物品。

现在完成时:
I have packed all my clothes. = 我已经打包好所有衣服了。
She has packed the gifts for her friends. = 她已经为朋友们打包好礼物了。

过去完成时:
We had packed our bags before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，我们已经打包好行李了。
They had packed the car before the rain started. = 下雨前他们已经把车装满了。

将来时:
I will pack my things tomorrow. = 我明天会收拾东西。
We will pack the boxes for shipping. = 我们将打包箱子以便运输。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pack more carefully. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更仔细地打包。
I wish I had packed a raincoat. = 我真希望我带了雨衣。

被动语态:
The boxes were packed and ready to ship. = 箱子已经打包好，准备发货了。
The luggage was packed by the hotel staff. = 行李由酒店工作人员打包。

疑问句:
Have you packed your camera? = 你打包好你的相机了吗？
Did he pack enough food for the hike? = 他为远足带够食物了吗？

否定句:
I didn't pack my swimsuit. = 我没有打包泳衣。
They haven't packed the presents yet. = 他们还没有打包礼物。


PHRASE->

pack a bag = 打包行李
pack your bags = 收拾行李
pack a lunch = 打包午餐
