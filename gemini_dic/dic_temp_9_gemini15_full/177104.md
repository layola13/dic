reappointment:/ˌriːəˈpɔɪntmənt/| n. |重新任命，再次委任 |The act of appointing someone to a position again.  It implies a previous appointment to the same or a similar role. |例句：His reappointment to the board was met with widespread approval.  他的再次当选董事会成员受到了广泛赞扬。

例句：The professor's reappointment was announced at the faculty meeting. 教授的重新任命在系里会议上宣布了。


近义词：re-election, reinstatement, reappointing


反义词：dismissal, termination, removal


词性：名词
