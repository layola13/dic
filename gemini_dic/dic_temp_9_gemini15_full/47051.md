datapoint:/ˈdeɪtəpɔɪnt/ | n. |数据点，资料点 |A single piece of information recorded as part of a larger dataset.  It represents a specific observation or measurement.  For example, in a graph, each point plotted represents a data point. |例句：Each customer's purchase history is a data point in the company's database.  每个顾客的购买历史都是公司数据库中的一个数据点。

例句：The sensor recorded a new data point every second. 传感器每秒记录一个新的数据点。|近义词：datum, observation, measurement |反义词：N/A
