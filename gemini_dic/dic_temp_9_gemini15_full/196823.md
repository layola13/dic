sexualizations: /ˌsɛkʃuəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 性化；性暗示；性感化 | The act of making something sexual in nature or emphasizing its sexual aspects.  It often involves objectifying or reducing a person or thing to their sexual attributes.  The term can be used in a variety of contexts, from media portrayals to interpersonal interactions. |例句：The excessive sexualizations of women in advertising is a cause for concern. 过度将女性性感化在广告中令人担忧。

The sexualizations of the characters in the movie was criticized by many viewers. 电影中角色的性暗示受到了许多观众的批评。


近义词：eroticization, objectification, sensualization |反义词：desexualization, de-eroticization |词性: n.
