debatably:/dɪˈbeɪtəbli/| adv.| 可以争论地，有争议地 |  Arguably; open to discussion or dispute.  It suggests that a statement or claim is not universally accepted and may be subject to debate. | Example:  Debatably, the best way to learn a language is through immersion.  = 可以争论的是，学习语言最好的方法是沉浸式学习。


Example: The effectiveness of this new drug is debatably superior to the older one. =这种新药的疗效是否优于旧药还有待商榷。


近义词：arguably, arguably,questionably,contestably


反义词：undeniably, undoubtedly, indisputably


词源:  Derived from the verb "debate," meaning to discuss a topic or issue formally.  The suffix "-ably" transforms the verb into an adverb, indicating the manner in which something can be done or is characterized.  The root traces back to Old French *debatre and ultimately to Latin *battuere ("to fight").
