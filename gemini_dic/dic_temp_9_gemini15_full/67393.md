exterminations:/ɪkˈstɜːrmɪneɪʃənz/| n.|灭绝；根除；消灭|The act of exterminating; the complete destruction of a group of people or animals.  It often refers to the systematic and intentional killing of a large number of people or animals.|例句：The mass exterminations carried out during the war were horrific.  二战期间进行的大规模屠杀令人震惊。

例句：The government launched a campaign for the extermination of rats. 政府发起了一场消灭老鼠的运动。


近义词：annihilation, eradication, destruction, elimination

反义词：preservation, conservation, protection
