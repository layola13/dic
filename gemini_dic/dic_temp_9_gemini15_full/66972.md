experimented:/ɪkˈsperɪmɛntɪd/| v. |进行实验；尝试|To carry out experiments; to try something new or different.|例句：Scientists experimented on animals to test the new drug. 科学家们用动物做实验来测试新药。
例句：She experimented with different hairstyles until she found one she liked. 她尝试了不同的发型，直到找到一个她喜欢的。|近义词：tested, tried, assayed|反义词：observed, confirmed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They experiment regularly. = 他们定期进行实验。
We experiment with new recipes. = 我们尝试新的食谱。

一般过去时:
I experimented with different colors. = 我尝试了不同的颜色。
The scientists experimented on mice. = 科学家们用老鼠做实验。

现在完成时:
She has experimented with various techniques. = 她已经尝试了各种技巧。
They have experimented extensively in this field. = 他们在这个领域进行了广泛的实验。

过去完成时:
He had experimented for years before achieving success. = 他在取得成功之前已经尝试了很多年。
They had experimented with several designs before settling on one. = 在确定一个设计之前，他们已经尝试了几个设计。

将来时:
We will experiment with new materials next week. = 我们下周将尝试新的材料。
They will experiment further to confirm the results. = 他们将进一步实验以确认结果。

虚拟语气:
If I had experimented more, I might have succeeded. = 如果我多尝试一些，我可能会成功。
I wish I had experimented with other approaches. = 我希望我尝试过其他方法。

被动语态:
The new drug was experimented on animals. = 新药在动物身上进行了实验。
Many different theories were experimented with. = 许多不同的理论都被尝试过。

疑问句:
Have you ever experimented with baking bread? = 你曾经尝试过烘焙面包吗？
What were they experimenting with in the lab? = 他们在实验室里尝试什么？

否定句:
They did not experiment enough. = 他们没有进行足够的实验。
She has not experimented with that technique before. = 她以前没有尝试过那种技巧。
