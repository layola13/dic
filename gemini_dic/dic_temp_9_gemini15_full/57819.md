drenches:/ˈdrentʃɪz/ | v. | 湿透；使湿透；使充满 | To make something or someone completely wet.  |例句：The rain drenched me to the bone.  大雨把我淋透了。

The heavy storm drenched the entire field.  暴风雨把整个田地都淋湿了。| soak, saturate, inundate | dry | 动词
