pundit:/ˈpʌndɪt/| n. |权威人士；专家；评论员 | A pundit is a person who is an expert in a particular subject and is frequently called on to give their opinions about it to the public.  They often appear on television or radio to comment on current events or to offer their expertise.  | Example:  The political pundit offered insightful commentary on the election results.  这位政治评论员对选举结果发表了富有见地的评论。

Example: Many economic pundits predicted a recession.  许多经济专家预测经济衰退。


近义词：expert, specialist, commentator, authority, analyst

反义词：amateur, novice, layman


词性：名词
