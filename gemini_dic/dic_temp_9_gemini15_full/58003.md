driveshaft:/ˈdraɪvʃæft/| n.|传动轴，驱动轴|A driveshaft is a rotating mechanical component that transmits torque from a power source (like an engine) to another rotating component (like the wheels of a vehicle). It typically consists of a long shaft with universal joints to allow for changes in angle and flexibility in movement.|例句：The driveshaft connects the engine to the rear wheels in a rear-wheel-drive car.  传动轴将发动机连接到后轮驱动汽车的后轮。|例句：A broken driveshaft can leave you stranded on the side of the road.  断裂的传动轴会让你滞留在路边。|近义词：propeller shaft, transmission shaft|反义词：无
