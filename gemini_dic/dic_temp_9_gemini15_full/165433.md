prelection:/prɪˈlɛkʃən/| n. |演讲，讲课|A formal lecture or discourse.  A prelection is a usually formal and often lengthy speech or lecture, typically one delivered on a learned or academic subject.  |例句：The professor delivered a fascinating prelection on the history of philosophy. 教授发表了一篇关于哲学史的精彩演讲。

The renowned scholar's prelection captivated the audience.  这位知名学者的演讲吸引了观众。|近义词：lecture, discourse, address, sermon|反义词：conversation, chat
