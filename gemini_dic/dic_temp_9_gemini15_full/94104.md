impeacher:/ɪmˈpiːtʃər/| n. |弹劾者；控告者 |A person who impeaches someone, especially a public official.  Someone who brings formal charges against a public official.| The impeacher presented compelling evidence of the president's misconduct.  弹劾者提供了总统行为不端的令人信服的证据。|近义词：accuser, prosecutor |反义词：acquittal, defender
