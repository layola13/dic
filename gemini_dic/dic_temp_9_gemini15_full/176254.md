re-colored: /ˌriːˈkʌləd/ | adj. |重新着色的，重新涂色的 |  Having been colored again; having had its colors changed.  This word describes something that has undergone a second coloring process, potentially altering its original hue. | 例句：The re-colored painting looked vibrant and new.  重新着色的画看起来鲜艳而新颖。

The artist re-colored the old photograph to enhance its details.艺术家重新给旧照片着色以增强细节。| 近义词：repainted, re-dyed, refurbished | 反义词：uncolored, original
