first-class: /ˌfɜːrstˈklɑːs/ | adj. | 头等舱的；一流的；最好的 | Of the highest quality; excellent.  |例句：He travelled first-class to London. 他乘坐头等舱前往伦敦。

例句：This restaurant offers first-class service. 这家餐厅提供一流的服务。


近义词：top-notch, premium, superior, excellent | 反义词：inferior, second-rate, subpar


词源:  The term "first-class" originates from the system of classifying travel accommodations, particularly on trains and ships.  "First" indicates the highest level of service and comfort, while "class" refers to a category or rank.  The usage has expanded to describe anything of superior quality.
