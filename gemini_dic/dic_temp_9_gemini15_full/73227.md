forcibly:/ˈfɔːrsəbli/| adv. |强行地，暴力地 |By using force; in a forceful manner.  The action is done against someone's will or resistance.|例句：The door was forcibly opened. 门被强行打开了。  The suspect was forcibly restrained by the police. 嫌疑人被警方强行制服了。|近义词：violently, forcefully, compulsorily |反义词：gently, voluntarily, peacefully
