outstation:/ˌaʊtˈsteɪʃən/| n. |外地办事处；偏远地区；外派地点 | A branch office located away from the main office or headquarters.  It often refers to a smaller office in a remote location. |例句：The company opened a new outstation in the rural area. 这家公司在农村地区开设了一个新的外地办事处。

例句：He was transferred to an outstation in a remote mountain village. 他被调到一个偏僻山村的外派地点工作。


近义词：branch office, field office, remote office, subsidiary

反义词：headquarters, main office, central office

词性:名词
