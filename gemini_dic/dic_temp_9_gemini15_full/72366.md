flown:/floʊn/| v.|飞过；飞翔；飘扬|The past participle of "fly".  It indicates a completed action of flying.|例句：The birds have flown south for the winter.  鸟儿们已经飞往南方过冬了。
The kite flown high in the sky. 风筝飞得很高。|近义词：soared, soared, glided|反义词：grounded, landed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I fly kites every Sunday. = 我每个星期天都放风筝。
Birds fly south for the winter. = 鸟类冬天飞往南方。

一般过去时:
The plane flew over the mountains. = 飞机飞越了山脉。
He flew a kite in the park. = 他在公园里放风筝。

现在完成时:
The birds have flown away. = 鸟儿们飞走了。
I have flown in a plane before. = 我以前坐过飞机。

过去完成时:
The birds had flown before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，鸟儿们已经飞走了。
We had flown to Paris before we went to London. = 我们去伦敦之前已经飞到巴黎了。

将来时:
They will fly to New York tomorrow. = 他们明天将飞往纽约。
I will fly a drone later. = 我一会儿将放无人机。

虚拟语气:
If I had wings, I would fly. = 如果我有翅膀，我会飞。
I wish I could fly like a bird. = 我希望我能像鸟一样飞翔。

被动语态:
The kite was flown by a child. = 风筝是一个孩子放飞的。
The plane was flown safely by the pilot. = 飞机由飞行员安全地驾驶。

疑问句:
Have you ever flown in a hot air balloon? = 你曾经乘热气球飞过吗？
Did they fly directly to their destination? = 他们直接飞往目的地了吗？

否定句:
The plane didn't fly because of bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，飞机没有起飞。
She hasn't flown before. = 她以前从未飞过。
