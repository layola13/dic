fishskin:/ˈfɪʃˌskɪn/| n. |鱼皮|Fishskin is the skin of a fish, often used in various applications such as leather goods, food, and glue.  It can be processed to create a leather-like material.|Example: The craftsman used fishskin to create a unique wallet.  工匠用鱼皮制作了一个独特的钱包。|近义词：fish hide, fish leather|反义词：无
