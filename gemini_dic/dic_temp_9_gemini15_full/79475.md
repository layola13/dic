global:/ˈɡloʊbəl/| adj.|全球的，世界的，总体的| relating to the whole world; worldwide.  |例句：Global warming is a serious threat to the environment. 全球变暖是对环境的严重威胁。|例句：The company has a global reach. 这家公司拥有全球影响力。|近义词：worldwide, universal, international |反义词：local, regional, national


USAGE->

The company operates globally. = 这家公司在全球运营。
Global temperatures are rising. = 全球气温正在上升。
