detestation:/ˌdɪtɛˈsteɪʃən/| n.| 厌恶；憎恨；强烈反感 |  A feeling of intense dislike or disgust.  |  例句：His detestation of violence was well known.  他的厌恶暴力是众所周知的。

例句：The public expressed its detestation of the crime. 公众表达了对这起罪行的强烈厌恶。


近义词：abhorrence, aversion, loathing, hatred | 反义词：love, affection, adoration


词源:  From Late Middle English *detestacioun, from Old French *detestation, from Latin detestatio, from detestari "to bear witness against, detest," from de- "away, from" + testari "to bear witness."
