It suggests a level of happiness that is overwhelming, potentially unsettling, or even slightly disturbing to others.  It implies a lack of restraint or appropriateness in expressing joy. |例句：Her over-merriness at the funeral was considered quite inappropriate.  她的葬礼上的过度欢乐被认为相当不合适。The children's over-merriness quickly wore their parents out. 孩子们的过度欢乐很快把他们的父母累坏了。|近义词：excessive gaiety, undue cheerfulness, boisterousness, ebullience|反义词：gloom, sadness, solemnity, dejection|名词
