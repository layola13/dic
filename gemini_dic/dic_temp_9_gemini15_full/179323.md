redominates:/rɪˈdɒmɪneɪts/ | v. | 重新占主导地位，再次支配 | To dominate or rule again; to regain dominance.  The word implies a previous period of dominance followed by a period of decline, and then a return to power. | Example: After a period of decline, the company redominated the market.  这家公司在经历了一段时间的衰退后，再次主导了市场。| 近义词: regain dominance, reassert control, prevail again | 反义词: lose dominance, be superseded, be eclipsed | 动词
