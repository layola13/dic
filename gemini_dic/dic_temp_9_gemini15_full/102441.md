jacket:/ˈdʒækɪt/| n. |夹克衫，外套|A short coat.  It can be made from various materials and is worn over other clothing for warmth or protection.|例句：He wore a leather jacket. 他穿着一件皮夹克。|例句：She put on her jacket before going outside. 她出门前穿上了外套。|近义词：coat, blazer, vest|反义词：shirt, undershirt
