pitpat:/pɪtpæt/ | v. | 轻轻地走动；轻拍声 | To move lightly and quickly, often with a soft sound; a light tapping sound. |例句：The rain pit-patted against the window. 雨轻轻地拍打着窗户。|例句：The child pit-patted down the hallway. 孩子轻轻地跑过走廊。|近义词： patter, tiptoe, tap |反义词： stomp, thunder, crash
