rutabaga:/ˈruːtəbeɪɡə/ | n. |芜菁甘蓝，瑞典芜菁 | A root vegetable that is a hybrid of cabbage and turnip, having a purplish-red or yellowish skin and a firm, yellowish-white flesh. It is typically eaten boiled or roasted. | The rutabaga is a hearty root vegetable that's perfect for a cold winter stew.  芜菁甘蓝是一种耐寒的根茎类蔬菜，非常适合做冬季炖菜。|turnip, swede |  (no direct antonym)| 名词
