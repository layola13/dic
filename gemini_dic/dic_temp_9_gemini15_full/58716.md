dunce:/dʌns/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A dunce is a slow-witted or stupid person, especially a child who is slow to learn.|He was considered a dunce in school, always struggling with his studies.|近义词：fool, idiot, simpleton|反义词：genius, prodigy, intellect


USAGE->
