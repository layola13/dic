premotion:/prɪˈmoʊʃən/| n.|晋升；提升；预感|The act of promoting someone to a higher rank or position; a feeling that something is about to happen, especially something bad.|例句：His premotion to manager was well-deserved.  他的晋升为经理是当之无愧的。|例句：She had a premonition of disaster. 她预感到灾难将要发生。|近义词：advancement, promotion, foreshadowing|反义词：demotion, relegation|名词
