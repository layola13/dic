mezcal:/ˈmɛzkæl/| n.|梅斯卡尔酒|A distilled alcoholic beverage made from the agave plant, particularly in Mexico.  It is similar to tequila but can be made from a wider variety of agave plants and often has a smokier flavor.|例句：We sampled several varieties of mezcal during our trip to Oaxaca. 我们在去瓦哈卡的旅行中品尝了几种梅斯卡尔酒。


例句：The bartender recommended a mezcal cocktail with a hint of lime. 酒保推荐了一款加了少许酸橙的梅斯卡尔鸡尾酒。

近义词：tequila (but with important distinctions), pulque (the agave-based base liquor)

反义词：无
