evangelizes:/ɪˈvændʒəlaɪz/| vt.|传福音；布道；使信奉福音|To preach the gospel or Christian message; to convert someone to Christianity.|例句：He evangelizes in the streets, hoping to share his faith with others. 他在街上布道，希望能与他人分享他的信仰。|例句：The missionaries evangelized the indigenous people, introducing them to Christianity. 传教士向土著人民传教，向他们介绍基督教。|近义词：preach, proselytize, convert |反义词：suppress, deny


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He evangelizes in the streets every Sunday. = 他每个星期天都在街上布道。
She evangelizes through her writing and speeches. = 她通过写作和演讲来传福音。


一般过去时:
The missionaries evangelized the local communities for decades. = 传教士们向当地社区传教了几十年。
He evangelized the poor and the marginalized. = 他向穷人和边缘人群传福音。


现在完成时:
They have evangelized across the country, spreading their message far and wide. = 他们已经在全国各地传福音，把他们的信息传播得很广。
She has evangelized to many people through her online platform. = 她通过她的在线平台向许多人传福音。


过去完成时:
Before the arrival of missionaries, they had never been evangelized. = 在传教士到来之前，他们从未被传教过。
The community had been evangelized by a traveling preacher years ago. = 多年前，一个巡回传教士向这个社区传过福音。


将来时:
He will evangelize in the town square next week. = 他下周将在城镇广场布道。
The church plans to evangelize the surrounding villages. = 教堂计划向周围的村庄传福音。


虚拟语气:
If I were to evangelize, I would focus on social justice. = 如果我要传福音，我会关注社会正义。
I wish I could evangelize more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地传福音。


被动语态:
The people were evangelized by the traveling preacher. = 人们受到了巡回传教士的传教。
The new doctrine is being evangelized through various media channels. = 新教义正在通过各种媒体渠道传播。


疑问句:
Does he evangelize regularly? = 他经常布道吗？
How does she evangelize her message? = 她如何传播她的信息？


否定句:
He doesn't evangelize aggressively. = 他不会咄咄逼人地传教。
She didn't evangelize in a way that felt coercive. = 她传教的方式让人感觉不到强迫。
