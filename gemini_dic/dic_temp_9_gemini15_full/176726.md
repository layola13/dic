reacts:/riˈækts/| v.|反应，回应|To respond or behave in a particular way to something.|The chemical reacts violently with water.  这种化学物质与水剧烈反应。|The audience reacted enthusiastically to the performance. 观众对演出反应热烈。|responds, interacts|inert, unresponsive|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reacts calmly to stressful situations. 他对压力情况反应冷静。
How does she react to criticism? 她如何应对批评？


一般过去时:
The patient reacted badly to the medication. 病人对药物反应不良。
She reacted quickly to the emergency. 她迅速应对紧急情况。


现在完成时:
He has reacted well to the treatment. 他对治疗反应良好。
How have you reacted to the news? 你对这个消息作何反应？


过去完成时:
He had reacted angrily before realizing his mistake. 他在意识到自己的错误之前已经生气地做出反应了。
They had reacted defensively to the accusations. 他们对指控做出了防御性的反应。


将来时:
She will react differently next time. 她下次会做出不同的反应。
How will the market react to the new policy? 市场将如何应对新政策？


虚拟语气:
If he reacted more quickly, the accident could have been avoided. 如果他反应更快，事故本来是可以避免的。
I wish he had reacted differently. 我希望他的反应有所不同。


被动语态:
The chemical is reacted with acid.  这种化学物质与酸发生反应。 (被动语态较少用在此词上)
The proposal was reacted to negatively by the board.  董事会对该提案的反应是负面的。(被动语态较少用在此词上)


疑问句:
How did he react to the news? 他对这个消息作何反应？
How will they react to our proposal? 他们将如何回应我们的提案？


否定句:
He didn't react at all. 他根本没有反应。
She didn't react favorably to my suggestion. 她对我的建议反应不佳。


PHRASE->
react to = 对...作出反应
react against = 反抗；反对
react with = 与...反应 (化学)
overreact = 过度反应
underreact = 反应不足
