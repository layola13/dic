jigging:/ˈdʒɪɡɪŋ/ | n. v. | 抖动；(钓鱼)垂直抖动鱼饵；活力四射的音乐 |

英文解析：Jigging as a noun refers to a lively dance, typically accompanied by lively music. As a verb, it means to move up and down quickly, often in a jerky or irregular manner, or to fish by repeatedly raising and lowering a lure to attract fish.  In the context of fishing, it specifically describes a vertical fishing technique.

例句：

The dancers were performing a lively jigging routine. = 舞者们正在表演一段活泼的抖动舞蹈。

He caught a large fish by jigging his lure near the bottom. = 他通过在底部附近上下抖动鱼饵钓到了一条大鱼。


近义词：

(n.) dancing, bobbing, jumping, jolting
(v.) bouncing, bobbing, shaking, pulsating


反义词：

(v.) stillness, steadiness, stability


词性:

Noun, Verb
