outclerk:/ˈaʊtklɜːrk/| n. |店员，售货员|A person who works in a shop or store, typically assisting customers and handling transactions.  This term is largely archaic or used in specific contexts.|例句：In the 19th century, an outclerk was responsible for many aspects of running a small retail business.  十九世纪，店员负责小型零售业务的许多方面。|近义词：shop assistant, sales assistant, clerk, shopman|反义词：manager, owner
