concentricity:/ˌkɒnˈsɛntrɪsɪti/| n. |同心性，同轴性 |The state of having a common center; the degree to which something is concentric.  It refers to the degree to which multiple circles, or other shapes, share a common center point.  High concentricity indicates a close alignment of centers, while low concentricity signifies a significant deviation. |例句：The high concentricity of the rings ensured smooth operation.  同心环的高同心度确保了平稳运行。

The engineer checked the concentricity of the shaft and bearing.工程师检查了轴承的同心度。|近义词：coincidence, alignment |反义词：eccentricity, misalignment
