scams:/skæmz/| n.|欺诈；骗局；诡计|A scam is a dishonest scheme or trick intended to cheat someone out of money or other valuables.  It often involves false promises or misleading information.|例句：He was arrested for running a sophisticated investment scam. 他因策划一起复杂的投资骗局而被捕。

例句：The police warned the public about a phone scam targeting the elderly.警方警告公众注意针对老年人的电话诈骗。


近义词：fraud, swindle, hoax, racket, deception

反义词：honesty, truthfulness, legitimacy
