The word "{responsibler}" is not a valid word in English.  There is no such word in standard dictionaries.  The correct word is "responsible".

Here's the analysis for "responsible":

responsible: /rɪˈspɑːnsəbəl/ | adj. | 负责任的，可靠的 | Accountable; obligated to answer for something.  Showing a sense of duty; dependable. |

例句：

He is responsible for the project's success. = 他对项目的成功负责。

She is a responsible employee and always meets her deadlines. = 她是一位负责任的员工，总是按时完成工作。


近义词：accountable, answerable, reliable, dependable, trustworthy, conscientious

反义词：irresponsible, unreliable, negligent, careless


词性: adjective


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is responsible for the team's performance. = 他对团队的表现负责。
She is responsible for managing the budget. = 她负责管理预算。

一般过去时:
He was responsible for the accident. = 他对事故负责。
She was responsible for training new employees. = 她负责培训新员工。

现在完成时:
He has been responsible for many successful projects. = 他曾负责许多成功的项目。
She has been responsible for the company's growth. = 她一直负责公司的发展。

过去完成时:
He had been responsible for the department before his promotion. = 在升职之前，他负责该部门。
She had been responsible for the marketing campaign. = 她曾负责营销活动。

将来时:
He will be responsible for overseeing the merger. = 他将负责监督并购。
She will be responsible for leading the team. = 她将负责领导团队。

虚拟语气:
If he were responsible, he would have apologized. = 如果他负责任的话，他就会道歉了。
If she had been responsible, this wouldn't have happened. = 如果她负责任的话，就不会发生这件事了。

被动语态:
The manager is responsible for the decision. = 经理对这个决定负责。（主动语态更常用）
The project was overseen by someone responsible. = 这个项目由一个负责任的人监督。（更强调监督者）

疑问句:
Who is responsible for this mess? = 谁应该为这烂摊子负责？
Is he responsible enough for the job? = 他够胜任这份工作吗？（此处responsible更侧重能力）


否定句:
He is not responsible for the damage. = 他不应对损坏负责。
She was not responsible for the delay. = 她不应对延误负责。


PHRASE->

be responsible for = 对…负责
take responsibility for = 对…承担责任
responsible to = 对…负责（通常指对上级或机构负责）
hold someone responsible = 认为某人负责
a responsible position = 一个重要的职位(隐含需要责任感)
