circulative:/ˈsɜːrkjʊleɪtɪv/| adj.|循环的，流通的 | Relating to or involving circulation; causing or involving the movement of something in a circuit or system.  | Example: The circulatory system is responsible for the circulative movement of blood throughout the body.  循环系统负责血液在全身的循环运动。|近义词：circulatory, rotating, recurrent |反义词：static, stationary


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The blood circulates constantly. = 血液不断循环。
This information circulates widely. = 这个信息广泛传播。


一般过去时:
The rumour circulated quickly. = 谣言迅速传播。
The newsletter circulated amongst the employees. = 内部通讯在员工中传播。


现在完成时:
The news has circulated throughout the town. = 这个消息已经在镇上广为流传。
The idea has circulated for years. = 这个想法已经流传多年了。


过去完成时:
The letter had circulated before the meeting. = 信件在会议之前就已经传阅过了。
The story had circulated widely before it was confirmed. = 故事在得到证实之前就已经广泛流传了。


将来时:
The report will circulate next week. = 报告将于下周分发。
The new policy will circulate among the staff. = 新政策将分发给全体员工。


虚拟语气:
If the information circulated more effectively, we would have better results. = 如果信息传播得更有效率，我们会得到更好的结果。
I wish the proposal circulated more widely. = 我希望这项提议能得到更广泛的传播。


被动语态:
The document was circulated to all members. = 文件分发给了所有成员。
The news is being circulated through social media. = 消息正在通过社交媒体传播。


疑问句:
Does this information circulate internally? = 这个信息在内部传播吗？
How does the blood circulate in the body? = 血液在体内如何循环？


否定句:
The rumour did not circulate widely. = 谣言并没有广泛传播。
The memo hasn't circulated yet. = 备忘录还没有分发。
