rebates:/ˈriːbeɪts/| n.|折扣；回扣；退款|A rebate is a partial refund of the original price of something.  It is often offered as an incentive to purchase a product or service.|Example:  The car dealership offered a $500 rebate on all new vehicles. 汽车经销商为所有新车提供500美元的回扣。

The store is giving rebates on all winter coats. 这家商店正在对所有冬衣进行打折。|近义词：discount, refund, reduction|反义词：surcharge, markup


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company offers rebates on its products. = 这家公司在其产品上提供折扣。
Customers receive rebates after they send in their receipts. = 客户在寄回收据后会收到回扣。
