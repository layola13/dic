insincerest: /ɪnˈsɪnsɪərɪst/ | adj. | 最不真诚的，虚伪的 | Most lacking in sincerity; completely hypocritical.  It is the superlative form of insincere. | 例句：His insincerest apology only deepened her distrust.  他的虚伪道歉只会加深她的不信任。

His insincerest smile didn't fool anyone.  他虚假的笑容没有骗到任何人。


近义词：dishonest, hypocritical, deceitful, false, artificial, affected

反义词：sincere, genuine, authentic, truthful, heartfelt


词源:  The word "insincerest" is derived from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") + "sincere" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Sincere" itself originates from the Latin "sine" (without) and "cera" (wax), referring to  unwaxed, and thus genuine, wood.  The original meaning referred to something genuine and unadulterated,  later evolving to describe honesty and genuineness of feeling.  The addition of "in-" negates this meaning, and "-est" makes it the superlative form, indicating the highest degree of insincerity.
