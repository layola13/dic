distrait:/dɪˈstreɪt/ | adj. |心不在焉的，精神恍惚的 |Mentally distracted; absent-minded.  Characterized by lack of attention and concentration.  |例句：She seemed distraite and preoccupied. 她看起来心不在焉，思绪万千。

例句：He gave a distraite answer to my question. 他心不在焉地回答了我的问题。


近义词：absent-minded, preoccupied, inattentive, daydreaming, woolgathering

反义词：attentive, focused, concentrated, alert, mindful


词源:  From Old French *destrét, from destreindre "to constrain, distress," from Latin stringere "to bind, tighten."  The original sense involved being mentally constrained or burdened, leading to the current meaning of being distracted or absent-minded.
