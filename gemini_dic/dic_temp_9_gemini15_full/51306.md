detailer:/diˈteɪlər/| n. |细节描述者；详述者；汽车美容师|A person who provides detailed descriptions or explanations; also, a person who details cars, meticulously cleaning and polishing them.|The detailer meticulously cleaned every inch of the car.  细节描述者一丝不苟地清洁了汽车的每一个角落。The car detailer used high-quality products to achieve a showroom shine. 汽车美容师使用高品质产品来达到展厅般的光泽。|describer, explainer, polisher|generalizer, summarizer|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He details cars for a living. = 他以汽车美容为生。
She details every step of the process. = 她详细地描述了流程的每一步。


一般过去时:
He detailed the car in the garage yesterday. = 他昨天在车库里给车做了美容。
She detailed the incident to the police. = 她向警方详细描述了事件经过。


现在完成时:

She had detailed the plan before presenting it to the team. = 在向团队展示计划之前，她已经详细地阐述了计划。
