preformulate:/priːˈfɔːrmjuleɪt/|/ˈpriːfɔːrmjuleɪt/| vt.|预先制定；预先构想|To formulate (something) beforehand.  To plan or prepare something in advance.|例句：The company preformulated its marketing strategy before launching the new product. 公司在推出新产品之前预先制定了其营销策略。

例句：The architect preformulated the building's design, considering all the necessary factors. 建筑师预先构想了建筑的设计，考虑了所有必要的因素。|近义词：plan, prepare, draft, devise|反义词：improvise, ad-lib
