phlegm:/ˈflem/| n.|痰，粘液；冷漠，迟钝 |Thick, viscous secretion from the mucous membranes of the respiratory tract.  It can be expelled by coughing.  The word can also refer to a sluggish or unemotional temperament.|例句：He coughed up a large amount of phlegm. 他咳出一大块痰。  He spoke with phlegm, showing no emotion. 他说话冷漠，毫无表情。|近义词：mucus, sputum, apathy, indifference|反义词：enthusiasm, passion, excitement|名词
