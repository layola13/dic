melanism:/ˈmɛləˌnɪzəm/| n.|黑化病，黑变病|An increased amount of black or dark-colored pigment in an organism, resulting in unusually dark skin, hair, or feathers.|The panther is a striking example of melanism, exhibiting a completely black coat due to an excess of melanin.|近义词：hyperpigmentation|反义词：albinism
