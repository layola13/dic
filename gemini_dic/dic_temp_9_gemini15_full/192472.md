scrummages:/ˈskrʌmɪdʒɪz/| n.|争夺；混战；激烈竞争|A scrummage is a confused struggle or a period of intense competition.  It often refers to a chaotic melee, particularly in rugby.|例句：The scrummage for the ball was fierce.  球的争夺非常激烈。|例句：The two teams engaged in a brutal scrummage near the goal line.  两队在球门线附近展开了一场激烈的混战。|近义词：melee, brawl, struggle, scramble|反义词：order, calm, peace
