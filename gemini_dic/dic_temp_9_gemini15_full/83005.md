词源:
The word "gunpowdered" is a modern formation, derived from "gunpowder" + "-ed".  "Gunpowder" itself comes from "gun" (a firearm) and "powder" (a fine dry substance).  The "-ed" suffix indicates being treated with or covered in gunpowder.
