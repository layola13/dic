garishly:/ˈɡɛəriʃli/| adv.| 俗丽地，过分艳丽地 |  Describes something that is excessively bright, showy, or flashy in a way that is considered tasteless or offensive. It implies a lack of subtlety or good taste. | The clown's costume was garishly colored.  小丑的服装颜色过于鲜艳。| gaudily, flashily, ostentatiously | subtly, tastefully, understatedly
