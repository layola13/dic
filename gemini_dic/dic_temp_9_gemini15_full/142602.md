osteoma:/ɒstiˈoʊmə/| n.|骨瘤|A benign bone tumor consisting of compact or cancellous bone.|例句：The X-ray revealed a small osteoma on his skull. X光显示他的头骨上有一个小的骨瘤。|近义词：bone tumor (but not all bone tumors are osteomas)|反义词：osteosarcoma (malignant bone tumor)


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.  However, we can show its usage in different sentence structures:


Declarative Sentence:
The patient presented with a solitary osteoma of the mandible. = 病人出现了一个下颌骨的孤立性骨瘤。

The osteoma was successfully removed during surgery. = 骨瘤在手术中被成功切除。

Interrogative Sentence:
Is the growth an osteoma or something more serious? = 这种生长是骨瘤还是更严重的东西？

Was the osteoma causing any pain or discomfort? = 骨瘤是否引起任何疼痛或不适？

Exclamatory Sentence:
What a relief that it was only a benign osteoma! = 真庆幸它只是一个良性骨瘤！

The osteoma was surprisingly large! = 骨瘤出乎意料地大！


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases directly using "osteoma".  However, it's often used in conjunction with other medical terms:


osteoma cutis: 皮肤骨瘤
osteoma spongiosum: 海绵状骨瘤
ivory osteoma: 象牙骨瘤
multiple osteomas: 多发性骨瘤
Gardner syndrome (associated with osteomas): 伽德纳综合征（与骨瘤相关）
