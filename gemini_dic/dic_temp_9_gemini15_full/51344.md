detective:/dɪˈtɛktɪv/| n.|侦探，侦察员|A detective is a person whose job is to investigate crimes and find out who committed them.  Detectives often work for police departments or private investigation agencies. They gather evidence, interview witnesses, and solve mysteries.|例句：The detective interviewed the suspects one by one. 侦探逐个讯问了嫌疑人。  The private detective was hired to find the missing person.  私人侦探受雇寻找失踪人员。|近义词：investigator, sleuth, investigator|反义词:criminal, culprit
