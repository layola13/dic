nab:/næb/| vt.|抓住，逮捕，夺取|to seize or snatch something quickly; to arrest someone|例句：The police nabbed the thief red-handed.  警察当场抓获了小偷。|例句：She nabbed the last cookie before anyone else could. 她抢在其他人之前拿走了最后一块饼干。|近义词：grab, seize, arrest, apprehend|反义词：release, free


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police nab criminals every day. = 警察每天都逮捕罪犯。
He nabs the best seats at every concert. = 他每次演唱会都抢到最好的座位。

一般过去时:
They nabbed the suspect near the bank. = 他们在大银行附近逮捕了嫌疑人。
She nabbed a beautiful scarf at the sale. = 她在甩卖中抢到一条漂亮的围巾。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
