demonetize:/ˌdiːˈmʌnɪtaɪz/ | vt. |取消…的货币地位；使…贬值 |To officially stop using a particular unit of money.  |The government decided to demonetize the old banknotes. 政府决定取消旧钞票的货币地位。|近义词：devalue, invalidate |反义词：monetize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government demonetizes old currency regularly. = 政府定期取消旧货币的地位。
They demonetize the coin due to inflation. = 由于通货膨胀，他们取消了这种硬币的货币地位。


一般过去时:
The country demonetized its currency in 2016. = 该国在2016年取消了其货币的地位。
India demonetized high-denomination banknotes to curb black money. = 印度取消了面值较高的钞票以遏制黑钱。


现在完成时:
The central bank has demonetized several currencies in the past decade. = 在过去的十年中，中央银行已经取消了几种货币的地位。
They have demonetized the old notes to combat counterfeiting. = 他们已经取消了旧钞票以打击伪造。


过去完成时:
Before the economic crisis, they had demonetized the national currency. = 在经济危机之前，他们已经取消了本国货币的地位。
The government had demonetized the old 500 rupee note before introducing the new one. = 政府在发行新版500卢比钞票之前已经取消了旧版500卢比钞票的货币地位。


将来时:
The government will demonetize the current 1000 rupee notes next year. = 政府明年将取消目前1000卢比钞票的货币地位。
They will demonetize the old coins to simplify the monetary system. = 他们将取消旧硬币以简化货币体系。

If the government demonetized the currency, the economy would suffer. = 如果政府取消货币地位，经济将会遭受损失。
