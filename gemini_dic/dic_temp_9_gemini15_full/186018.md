rewidened:/ˌriːˈwaɪdn̩d/ | v. |重新加宽；再次拓宽 | The road has been rewidened to accommodate the increased traffic. 道路已被重新拓宽以适应增加的交通流量。 | widen, broaden, expand | narrow, constrict, reduce |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They widen the road every year. = 他们每年都拓宽这条路。
The river widens as it approaches the sea. = 河流在接近大海时变宽了。


一般过去时:
The company widened its product line last year. = 公司去年扩大了其产品线。
He widened his eyes in surprise. = 他惊讶地睁大了眼睛。


现在完成时:
They have widened the bridge to handle the extra weight. = 他们已经拓宽了桥梁以承受额外的重量。
The government has widened the scope of the investigation. = 政府已经扩大了调查范围。


过去完成时:
They had widened the road before the new houses were built. = 新房子建好之前，他们已经拓宽了道路。
The river had widened significantly due to the heavy rains. = 由于大雨，河水已经明显加宽了。


将来时:
