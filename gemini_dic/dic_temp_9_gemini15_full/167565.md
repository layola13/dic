prewelcomes:/ˌpriːˈwɛlkəmz/|  n. | 预先欢迎；提前欢迎 | A prewelcome is an expression of welcome given before an event or person's arrival.  It might involve preparatory actions or communications designed to make someone feel welcome in advance.  For example, a hotel might send a prewelcome email to guests detailing check-in procedures and local amenities.  |  Example: The organizers sent out prewelcomes to all the conference attendees, outlining the schedule and providing helpful travel information.  | 近义词：advance greeting, anticipatory welcome | 反义词：unwelcoming, snubbing | 名词
