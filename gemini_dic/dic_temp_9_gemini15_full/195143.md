semidangerously: /ˌsɛmiˈdeɪndʒərəsli/ | adv. | 半危险地，略微危险地 |  The word "semidangerously" is an adverb formed by combining the prefix "semi-" (meaning "half," "partly," or "partially") with the adverb "dangerously." It describes an action or state that is somewhat dangerous, but not extremely so.  It suggests a degree of risk that is less than fully dangerous.  |  例句：He semidangerously climbed the crumbling wall. 他半危险地爬上了摇摇欲坠的墙。


They semidangerously navigated the icy path. 他们略微危险地走过了冰滑的小路。|  近义词：somewhat dangerously, slightly perilously, a little riskily | 反义词：safely, securely, cautiously
