hatchetmen:/ˈhætʃɪtmən/ | n. | 凶狠打手；暗杀者；暴力分子 |  A hatchetman is someone employed to perform violent or ruthless acts, often secretly or illegally, on behalf of another person or organization.  They are typically involved in intimidation, violence, or assassination. | The mob boss used hatchetmen to silence his rivals.  黑帮老大用打手来压制他的竞争对手。  The corrupt politician hired hatchetmen to smear his opponent's reputation.  那个腐败的政客雇佣打手来抹黑对手的名声。 | assassins, thugs, enforcers, goons | peacemakers, negotiators, diplomats | 名词
