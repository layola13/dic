gryphons:/ˈɡrɪfɒnz/| n.|狮鹫|A mythical creature with the body of a lion and the head and wings of an eagle.  Gryphons are often depicted as guardians or symbols of power and strength.|Example: In Greek mythology, gryphons guarded gold in remote mountains.  在希腊神话中，狮鹫守护着偏远山区的黄金。|近义词：griffin|反义词：None
