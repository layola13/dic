crepon:/krɪˈpɒn/| n. |绉纱；皱纹织物|A fabric with a pebbled or crinkled surface.  It is often made of silk, wool, or synthetic fibers.  The crinkled effect is created through special weaving or finishing techniques.|例句：The dress was made of a luxurious crepon fabric. 这件裙子是用奢华的绉纱面料制作的。|近义词：crêpe, seersucker|反义词：plain fabric (平纹织物)
