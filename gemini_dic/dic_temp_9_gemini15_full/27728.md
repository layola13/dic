canicula:/kəˈnɪkjʊlə/ | n. | 小犬座；伏天；酷暑 | The canicula refers to the Dog Star, Sirius, and the hottest part of summer when it is prominent in the sky.  It is also used to describe the hottest part of summer itself. | Example: The canicula brought sweltering heat to the region.  伏天给该地区带来了酷暑。 The appearance of the canicula in the night sky signaled the start of the hottest season.  夜空中小犬座的出现预示着最热季节的开始。| 近义词：dog days, heat wave, summer solstice | 反义词：winter, cold snap | 名词
