materializes:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪz/| v.|实现；显现；具体化 |To become real or visible, especially suddenly.  |例句：His dreams finally materialized. 他的梦想最终实现了。
例句：A figure materialized out of the fog.  一个身影从雾中显现出来。|近义词：appear, emerge, manifest, become real |反义词：disappear, vanish, fade


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plan materializes slowly. = 计划正在缓慢实现。
His ideas materialize in his art. = 他的想法在他的艺术中具体化。


一般过去时:
The ghost materialized before our eyes. = 鬼魂在我们眼前显现。
Her vision materialized into a successful business. = 她的远见变成了成功的企业。


现在完成时:
My fears have materialized. = 我的恐惧已经变成了现实。
The project has finally materialized after years of planning. =  经过多年的规划，项目终于实现了。


过去完成时:
Before my eyes, the image had materialized. = 在我眼前，图像已经显现出来。
It had materialized that he was not the person he claimed to be. = 他不是他自称的那个人，这件事已经很明显了。


将来时:
The project will materialize next year. = 该项目将于明年实现。
I hope my dreams will materialize in the future. = 我希望我的梦想将来能够实现。


虚拟语气:
If only my hopes would materialize! = 但愿我的希望能够实现！
If the plan materialized, we would be very successful. = 如果计划实现了，我们会非常成功。


被动语态:
The vision was materialized into a successful product. = 远见被具体化成一个成功的产品。
The app idea was materialized by a team of talented engineers. =  这个应用程序的想法由一支才华横溢的工程师团队实现。


疑问句:
When will the changes materialize? = 这些变化什么时候会实现？
Will his ambition ever materialize? = 他的雄心壮志最终会实现吗？


否定句:
His dreams did not materialize. = 他的梦想没有实现。
The plan failed to materialize. = 计划未能实现。
