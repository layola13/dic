restressing:/ˌriːˈstrɛsɪŋ/ | v. | 再强调；重新施加压力；重新强调 | The act of stressing again or applying stress anew.  It can refer to re-emphasizing a point, reapplying physical pressure, or re-engineering a structure for increased stress resistance. |例句：The engineer is restressing the bridge to withstand heavier loads. =工程师正在重新加固桥梁以承受更大的载荷。

例句：The teacher restressed the importance of studying for the exam. =老师再次强调了考试学习的重要性。


近义词：re-emphasizing, reiterating, reinforcing | 反义词：de-emphasizing, downplaying

He restresses his commitment to the project every week. = 他每周都重申他对项目的承诺。
