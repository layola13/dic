eulogistic:/ˌjuːləˈdʒɪstɪk/| adj.|赞美的；颂扬的；溢美之词的| Expressing praise; commendatory.|The eulogistic obituary praised the deceased's many virtues.  这篇赞美辞高度赞扬了死者的诸多美德。|近义词：laudatory, commendatory, complimentary, panegyrical|反义词：critical, derogatory, disparaging, condemnatory


USAGE->

The speech is eulogistic in tone. = 这篇演讲的语气是赞美的。
His writing is always eulogistic towards his friends. = 他写文章总是对朋友赞扬有加。
