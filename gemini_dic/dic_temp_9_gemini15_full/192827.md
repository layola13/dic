search:/sɜːtʃ/| /sɝtʃ/ | v. n. |搜索，查找；探求|to try to find something or someone by looking or otherwise seeking carefully.|例句：I searched my bag for my keys. 我翻遍了我的包找钥匙。|例句：The police searched the house for clues. 警察搜查了房子寻找线索。|近义词：seek, hunt, explore|反义词：find (in some contexts)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I search the internet for information every day. = 我每天都在网上搜索信息。
She searches for a new job. = 她在找一份新工作。

一般过去时:
They searched the forest for the lost child. = 他们在森林里寻找丢失的孩子。
He searched his memory for the answer. = 他努力回忆答案。

现在完成时:
I have searched everywhere for my glasses. = 我到处都找过我的眼镜了。
She has searched for a solution to the problem. = 她一直在寻找解决这个问题的方法。

过去完成时:
I had searched for hours before I finally found it. = 我找了好几个小时才最终找到它。
They had searched the building thoroughly before concluding their investigation. = 他们在结束调查前彻底搜查了整栋大楼。

将来时:
I will search the database for the data. = 我将搜索数据库以查找数据。
We will search the area for survivors. = 我们将搜寻该区域的幸存者。

虚拟语气:
If I searched harder, I might find it. = 如果我再努力搜索一下，我可能会找到它。
I wish I had searched the attic. = 我真希望我搜查过阁楼。

被动语态:
The house was searched by the police. = 房子被警察搜查了。
The database is being searched for relevant information. = 正在搜索数据库以查找相关信息。

疑问句:
What are you searching for? = 你在找什么？
Where did you search? = 你在哪里搜索的？

否定句:
I didn't search the room properly. = 我没有好好搜查那个房间。
They haven't searched the entire area yet. = 他们还没有搜索整个区域。
