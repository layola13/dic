prepare:/prɪˈpeər/| vt.|准备，预备|To make something ready for use or action; to get ready for something.|例句：I'm preparing dinner. 我正在准备晚餐。|例句：She prepared herself for a long journey. 她为长途旅行做好了准备。|近义词：ready, arrange, get ready|反义词：neglect, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I prepare breakfast every morning. = 我每天早上准备早餐。
They prepare the documents before the meeting. = 他们在会议前准备文件。


一般过去时:
I prepared a presentation yesterday. = 我昨天准备了一个演示文稿。
She prepared a delicious meal for her guests. = 她为她的客人准备了一顿美味的饭菜。


现在完成时:
We have prepared everything for the party. = 我们已经为派对准备好了所有东西。
He has prepared himself for the upcoming exam. = 他已经为即将到来的考试做好了准备。


过去完成时:
They had prepared the room before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经准备好了房间。
She had prepared a speech, but she didn't deliver it. = 她已经准备好了演讲，但她没有发表。


将来时:
I will prepare a report next week. = 我下周将准备一份报告。
We will prepare the food for the picnic tomorrow. = 我们明天将为野餐准备食物。


虚拟语气:
If I had prepared better, I would have passed the test. = 如果我准备得更好，我就通过考试了。
I wish I had prepared more for the interview. = 我真希望我为面试做了更多准备。


被动语态:
The food was prepared by a famous chef. = 这道菜是由一位名厨准备的。
The documents are being prepared for the board meeting. = 文件正在为董事会会议准备。


疑问句:
Are you prepared for the exam? = 你准备好考试了吗？
Have they prepared the materials yet? = 他们准备好材料了吗？


否定句:
I didn't prepare for the test, so I failed. = 我没有为考试做准备，所以考试不及格。
She hadn't prepared her presentation, so she was nervous. = 她没有准备她的演示文稿，所以她很紧张。


PHRASE->
prepare for = 为...做准备
prepare to = 准备...
prepare a meal = 准备饭菜
prepare a speech = 准备演讲
prepare oneself = 做好准备
