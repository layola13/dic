phaenogam:/ˈfiːnəɡæm/| n. |显花植物；种子植物|A flowering plant; a plant that produces seeds enclosed within an ovary.  Phaenogams are characterized by the production of flowers, which are the reproductive structures of the plant. They represent the vast majority of plant species on Earth.| Example: Roses, oaks, and sunflowers are all examples of phaenogams. 玫瑰、橡树和向日葵都是显花植物的例子。|近义词：flowering plant, seed plant|反义词：cryptogam
