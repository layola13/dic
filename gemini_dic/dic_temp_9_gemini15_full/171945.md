puppetries:/ˈpʌpɪtriːz/| n.|木偶戏；木偶表演艺术|The art of making and performing with puppets.  It encompasses various forms, from simple hand puppets to elaborate shadow puppets and marionettes.  The term can also refer to the manipulation or control of people or events as if they were puppets.|例句：The children were mesmerized by the puppetries at the festival.孩子们被节日上的木偶戏迷住了。

The puppeteer's skill was evident in the intricate puppetries he created. 木偶师的技巧在他制作的复杂的木偶戏中显而易见。|近义词：puppet shows, marionette shows|反义词：live theater,  real-life drama|名词
