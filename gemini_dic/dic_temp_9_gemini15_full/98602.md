instructing: /ɪnˈstrʌktɪŋ/ | v. | 指导，教导，命令 |  To give someone instructions or directions; to teach someone.  | 例句：The teacher is instructing the students on how to solve the problem.  老师正在指导学生如何解决这个问题。

例句：The manual instructs users on how to assemble the product.  说明书指导用户如何组装产品。


近义词：teaching, guiding, directing, commanding, tutoring | 反义词：ignoring, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He instructs his students daily. 他每天指导他的学生。
She instructs the class in proper grammar. 她指导班级学习正确的语法。

一般过去时:
The professor instructed us to read Chapter 3. 教授指示我们阅读第三章。
He instructed the workers to be careful. 他指示工人们要小心。

现在完成时:
She has instructed the team on the new project. 她已经指导团队开展新项目。
They have instructed me to complete the report by Friday. 他们已指示我周五前完成报告。

过去完成时:
The captain had instructed the crew to abandon ship. 船长已指示船员弃船。
She had instructed her assistant to prepare the documents. 她已指示她的助理准备文件。

将来时:
He will instruct the new employees next week. 他下周将指导新员工。
They will instruct the students on the use of the equipment. 他们将指导学生如何使用设备。

虚拟语气:
If he instructed them properly, they would have succeeded. 如果他正确地指导他们，他们就会成功。
I wish he had instructed me more clearly. 我希望他指导我更清晰一些。

被动语态:
The students were instructed to be quiet. 学生们被指示要安静。
The workers are being instructed on safety procedures. 工人们正在接受安全程序的指导。

疑问句:
Who instructed you to do that? 谁指示你那样做的？
What did the instructions say? 指示说了什么？

否定句:
He didn't instruct them clearly enough. 他没有足够清楚地指导他们。
She did not instruct her team properly. 她没有正确地指导她的团队。
