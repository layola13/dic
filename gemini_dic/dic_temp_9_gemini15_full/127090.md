nation:/ˈneɪʃən/| n. |国家，民族 |A nation is a large body of people united by common descent, history, culture, or language, inhabiting a particular country or territory.  A nation can also refer to a group of people sharing a common culture and language, even if they don't have their own country. |Example:  The United Nations is an organization of many nations.  联合国是许多国家的组织。

The nation celebrated its independence day with parades and fireworks.  这个国家以游行和焰火庆祝独立日。|近义词：country, state, people |反义词：None (反义词需要根据语境而定，例如，可以是“individual” 指的是个人而非国家)
