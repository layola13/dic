desolately:/ˈdɛsələtli/| adj.|凄凉地；孤独地；悲惨地 |Adjective describing a state of bleakness, loneliness, and misery.  It suggests a feeling of emptiness and despair, often associated with desolation or abandonment. |例句：She wandered desolately through the deserted streets. 她凄凉地徘徊在空荡荡的街道上。

例句：The abandoned house stood desolately on the hill.  那座废弃的房子孤零零地矗立在山上。


近义词： forlornly, miserably, bleakly, sadly, lonely

反义词：joyfully, happily, cheerfully, comfortingly
