embathe:/ɪmˈbeɪð/ | vt. | 沉浸；沐浴；笼罩 | To surround or envelop completely; to immerse.  | 例句：The warm sunlight embathed the valley in a golden glow.  温暖的阳光给山谷披上了一层金色的光芒。

The gentle rain embathed the thirsty land.  温柔的雨水滋润着干渴的土地。


近义词：immerse, envelop, surround, flood | 反义词：dry, deprive


词性：动词
