lithograph:/ˈlɪθəɡræf/| n.|平版印刷术；平版印刷品|A lithograph is a picture made by a process of printing from a flat surface, typically a stone or metal plate, on which the image has been drawn or etched.|例句：The museum displayed a collection of rare lithographs by famous artists.  博物馆展出了著名艺术家创作的一系列珍稀平版印刷品。|近义词：print, impression|反义词：original (in the sense of a unique, non-printed artwork)
