motorman:/ˈmoʊtərmæn/ /ˈməʊtərmæn/| n. |司机（尤指电车司机）, 机车司机 |A motorman is a person who drives a motor vehicle, especially a tram or train.  |例句：The motorman skillfully navigated the tram through the busy city streets. 电车司机熟练地驾驶电车穿过繁忙的城市街道。

例句：The experienced motorman smoothly brought the train to a stop at the platform. 经验丰富的机车司机平稳地将火车停靠在站台上。


近义词：driver, operator, conductor (in some contexts)


反义词：passenger


词性：名词
