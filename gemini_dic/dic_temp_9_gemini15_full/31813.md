checks:/tʃɛks/ | n. v. |支票；检查；核实 |  A check is a written order directing a bank to pay money from a person's account.  To check something means to examine it carefully to ensure accuracy or safety. |例句：I wrote a check for $100. 我开了一张100美元的支票。  The mechanic checked the car's engine. 技工检查了汽车的发动机。|近义词：examination, inspection, verification, bill, draft |反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He checks his email every morning. 他每天早上都查看他的电子邮件。
She checks the spelling of every word. 她检查每个单词的拼写。

过去完成时:
I had checked the documents before the meeting. 我在会议前已经检查过了文件。
