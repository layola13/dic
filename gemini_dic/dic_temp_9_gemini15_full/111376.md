liveryman:/ˈlɪvəriˌmæn/| n.|行会会员；（尤指伦敦）行会成员；穿制服的人|A liveryman is a member of a livery company, particularly in London.  Livery companies are ancient guilds or associations of craftsmen or merchants.  The term can also refer to someone who wears a livery, a distinctive uniform.  |例句：He is a liveryman of the Worshipful Company of Goldsmiths. 他是金匠行会（Worshipful Company of Goldsmiths）的会员。|近义词：guild member, freeman|反义词：N/A
