phobic:/ˈfoʊbɪk/| adj.|患有恐惧症的；对…有恐惧症的| suffering from or relating to a phobia.  A phobic person experiences intense and irrational fear of a specific object, animal, situation, or activity. This fear significantly impacts their daily life and can lead to avoidance behaviors.|例句：He is phobic about spiders. 他有蜘蛛恐惧症。

例句：She is intensely phobic about flying. 她对飞行有强烈的恐惧症。


近义词：fearful, afraid, terrified

反义词：unafraid, fearless, bold


词源：源于希腊语“phobos”（恐惧）。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is phobic about heights. = 他恐高。
She is phobic of dogs. = 她怕狗。


一般过去时:
He was phobic about public speaking. = 他以前害怕公开演讲。
She was phobic of enclosed spaces. = 她以前害怕密闭空间。


现在完成时:
He has been phobic about needles since childhood. = 他从小就害怕针。
She has been phobic of water since the accident. = 自从那次事故后，她就害怕水。


过去完成时:
He had been phobic about snakes all his life. = 他一辈子都怕蛇。
She had been phobic of the dark before therapy. = 治疗之前，她一直害怕黑暗。


将来时:
He will still be phobic about crowds. = 他仍然会害怕人群。
She will likely remain phobic of dentists. = 她很可能仍然害怕牙医。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't phobic about the ocean, he would go scuba diving. = 如果他不怕大海，他会去潜水。
If she weren't phobic about driving, she would have a car. = 如果她不怕开车，她就会有一辆车。


被动语态: (很少用于此词)  该词通常不用于被动语态。


疑问句:
Is he phobic about thunderstorms? = 他怕雷暴吗？
Is she phobic of cats? = 她怕猫吗？


否定句:
He is not phobic about insects. = 他不怕昆虫。
She is not phobic of heights. = 她不怕高。



PHRASE->
specific phobic = 特定恐惧症
social phobic = 社交恐惧症
agoraphobic = 广场恐惧症
claustrophobic = 幽闭恐惧症
arachnophobic = 蜘蛛恐惧症
