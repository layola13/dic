counter-agent:/ˈkaʊntərˌeɪdʒənt/| n. |反代理人，对立代理人|A counter-agent is a person or entity who acts in opposition to or against another agent, often in a competitive or adversarial context.  This term is frequently used in contexts like espionage, law enforcement, or business competition.|例句：The spy discovered that his contact was actually a counter-agent working for the enemy. 这个间谍发现他的联络人实际上是为敌人工作的反间谍。|近义词：opponent, adversary, rival, antagonist|反义词：agent, ally, partner|名词
