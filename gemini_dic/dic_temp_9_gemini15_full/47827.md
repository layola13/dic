decategorized:/ˌdiːˈkætəɡəraɪzd/| adj.|取消分类的，未分类的|Removed from a category or classification system; lacking categorization.  The data was decategorized to protect individual privacy.|例句：The decategorized data was difficult to analyze because of its lack of structure.  取消分类的数据由于缺乏结构而难以分析。

例句：The research required decategorizing the existing data before new analysis could begin. 研究需要在开始新的分析之前取消现有数据的分类。


近义词：uncategorized, unclassified, uncategorized, unsorted


反义词：categorized, classified, sorted


词性:adjective
