carburize:/ˈkɑːrbəraɪz/|/ˈkɑːrbəraɪz/| vt.|渗碳；使渗碳|To impregnate (iron or steel) with carbon, typically by heating it in the presence of carbon monoxide.|The blacksmith carburized the steel to increase its hardness.  铁匠对钢进行渗碳以增加其硬度。

The process increased the carbon content of the surface layer of the steel. 这个过程增加了钢的表层碳含量。


近义词：carbonize, case-harden|反义词：decarburize
