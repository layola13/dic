sexualised: /ˈsɛkʃuəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 性化了的，色情化的 |  Having sexual connotations or suggestions; made sexually attractive.  It implies an emphasis on or exploitation of sex appeal, often in a way that is objectifying or degrading. |

例句：

The advertisement was heavily sexualised, using suggestive imagery to sell the product. = 这则广告严重色情化，使用暗示性的图像来销售产品。

The lyrics of the song are sexualised and inappropriate for children. = 这首歌的歌词色情化且不适合儿童。


近义词：eroticised, sensualised, sexed-up


反义词：de-sexualised, asexual, chaste


词源:  Derived from the verb "sexualise" (itself derived from "sexual"), which means to give sexual characteristics or connotations to something or someone.  The suffix "-ised" (-ized in American English) indicates a process of being made or becoming something.
