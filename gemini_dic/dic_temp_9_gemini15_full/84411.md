hallowe'ens:/ˌhæləˈwiːnz/| n. |万圣节之夜（复数）|The plural of Halloween, referring to multiple instances of Halloween or the celebrations surrounding it.  It is less common than using "Halloweens" as a general term for multiple years of the holiday.|例句：Many children enjoyed multiple Hallowe'ens in their childhood.  许多孩子在童年时期享受过多次万圣节。

例句：We have different plans for Hallowe'ens this year and next year. 今年和明年我们对万圣节有不同的计划。


近义词：All Hallows' Eve (sing.), Halloween celebrations (pl.)|反义词：无|词性：名词
