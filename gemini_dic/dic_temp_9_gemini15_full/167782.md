prime-ministership:/ˈpraɪm ˈmɪnɪstrʃɪp/ | n. |首相职位；首相任期 | The position or period of office of a prime minister.  It refers to the role and responsibilities of the head of government,  as well as the duration of their time in that office. | Example:  His prime-ministership was marked by significant economic reforms.  他的首相任期以重大的经济改革为标志。  The length of his prime-ministership is still debated.  他的首相任期持续多久仍在争论中。 |近义词：premiership |反义词：无
