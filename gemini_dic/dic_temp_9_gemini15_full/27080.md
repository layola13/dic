calibrators:/ˈkælɪbreɪtərz/| n.|校准器，校准仪|Instruments or devices used to calibrate other instruments or equipment to ensure accuracy.|例句：The technician used precision calibrators to check the accuracy of the scales. 技术人员使用精密校准器来检查秤的准确性。  The laboratory invested in new calibrators to improve the reliability of its test results. 实验室投资购买新的校准器以提高测试结果的可靠性。|近义词：calibrating devices, gauging instruments|反义词：None.
