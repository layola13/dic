improvisatore:/ɪmˌprɒvɪzəˈtɔːreɪ/| n. |即兴表演者；即兴创作的人 |A person who improvises, especially in music or theatre.  An improvisatore is skilled at creating and performing spontaneously, often without prior preparation. They rely on their creativity and quick thinking to produce original works. |The improvisatore captivated the audience with his witty and spontaneous performance.  这位即兴表演者以其诙谐而即兴的表演吸引了观众。|  improviser, ad-libber |  planner, pre-meditator | 名词
