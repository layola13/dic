oleoresin:/ˌoʊliˈoʊrəzɪn/| n. |树脂油；油树脂|A resin dissolved in its own volatile oil;  a concentrated extract of a plant's essential oils and resins.  It's often sticky and fragrant.|例句：The oleoresin of capsicum is used to make chili powder.  辣椒的树脂油用于制作辣椒粉。|例句：Extraction of the oleoresin from hops is an important step in brewing. 从啤酒花中提取树脂油是酿造过程中的重要步骤。|近义词：resin, extract |反义词:  N/A
