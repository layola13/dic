linenless:/ˈlɪnənˌlɛs/| adj.| 无亚麻布的；不含亚麻的 | Without linen; not containing linen.  The term refers to fabrics or items that are not made from or do not include linen. | Example:  The hotel offered linenless bedding as an alternative for guests with allergies.  这家酒店为对亚麻过敏的客人提供无亚麻床单作为替代选择。 | 近义词：cotton, non-linen | 反义词：linen
