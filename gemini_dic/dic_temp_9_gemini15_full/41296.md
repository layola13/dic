cordon:/ˈkɔːrdən/| n. v.|警戒线；（用警戒线）包围；| A cordon is a line or barrier formed by police, soldiers, or other officials to prevent people from entering or leaving an area.  It can also refer to a decorative fringe or trimming. | 例句：Police have thrown a cordon around the building.  警察已经用警戒线包围了这座建筑物。

例句：The soldiers established a tight cordon to prevent the enemy from escaping.  士兵们设置了严密的警戒线以防止敌人逃跑。


近义词：barrier, barricade, enclosure|反义词：opening, access
