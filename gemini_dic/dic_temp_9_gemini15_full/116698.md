maturing:/məˈtʃʊərɪŋ/| v.|成熟；使成熟 |The process of becoming mature; developing fully.  |例句：The wine is maturing nicely in the cellar. 这葡萄酒在地窖里慢慢成熟。

The fruit is maturing quickly in the warm weather. 水果在温暖的天气里快速成熟。


近义词：ripening, developing, aging |反义词：decaying, deteriorating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cheese is maturing nicely. = 奶酪正在慢慢成熟。
He is maturing into a fine young man. = 他正在成长为一个优秀的年轻人。


一般过去时:
The fruit matured quickly in the sun. = 水果在阳光下迅速成熟。
She matured significantly after her travels. = 旅行后她成熟了很多。


现在完成时:
The wine has matured well. = 这葡萄酒酿造得很好。
He has matured considerably since college. = 自从大学毕业后，他成熟了很多。


过去完成时:
The plan had matured by the time the meeting began. = 会议开始时，计划已经成熟了。
They had matured their ideas before presenting them. = 他们在提出想法之前已经完善了它们。


将来时:
The mangoes will mature in a few weeks. = 芒果几周后就会成熟。
She will mature into a strong woman. = 她将会成长为一个坚强的女人。


虚拟语气:
If the project matured on time, we would have been successful. = 如果项目按时完成，我们就成功了。
I wish my ideas had matured sooner. = 我希望我的想法早点成熟。


被动语态:
The plan was matured over several months. = 这个计划经过几个月才成熟。
The fruit is being matured in controlled conditions. = 水果在受控条件下进行成熟。


疑问句:
Is the fruit maturing properly? = 水果成熟得怎么样？
How long does it take for the whiskey to mature? = 威士忌需要多长时间才能成熟？


否定句:
The plan didn't mature as quickly as expected. = 计划没有预料的那么快成熟。
The fruit isn't maturing properly due to the cold weather. = 由于天气寒冷，水果成熟得不好。


PHRASE->
maturing process = 成熟过程
maturing market = 成熟市场
maturing debt = 到期债务
maturing age = 成熟年龄
accelerated maturing = 加速成熟
