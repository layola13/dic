byer:/ˈbaɪər/| n. |买方，购买者 |A buyer is a person or organization that buys goods or services.  |例句：The buyer was satisfied with the quality of the goods. 买方对货物的质量很满意。|例句：The company is looking for a reliable buyer for its products. 这家公司正在寻找一个可靠的买家来购买其产品。|近义词：purchaser, customer, consumer |反义词：seller, vendor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The buyer inspects the goods carefully. = 买方仔细检查货物。
He is a regular buyer of our products. = 他是我们产品的常客。

一般过去时:
The buyer paid for the goods in cash. = 买方用现金支付了货款。
She was a shrewd buyer who always got the best deals. = 她是一位精明的买家，总是能得到最好的交易。

现在完成时:
The company has found a new buyer for its assets. = 公司已经为其资产找到了新的买家。
They have been loyal buyers for many years. = 他们多年来一直是忠实的买家。

过去完成时:
The seller had already found a buyer before we contacted him. =在我们联系他之前，卖家已经找到了买家。
The buyer had inspected the goods before making the purchase. = 买家在购买之前已经检查了货物。

将来时:
The company will find a buyer for its outdated equipment. = 公司将会为其过时的设备找到买家。
We expect to have a new buyer by next week. = 我们预计下周就会有新的买家。

虚拟语气:
If I were a buyer, I would negotiate a better price. = 如果我是买家，我会协商一个更好的价格。
I wish I had been a more discerning buyer. = 我真希望我以前是一个更有眼光的买家。

被动语态:
The goods were inspected by the buyer. = 货物被买方检查了。
The contract was signed by both the buyer and the seller. = 合同由买方和卖方双方签署。

疑问句:
Who is the buyer of this property? = 这处房产的买家是谁？
Are you the buyer or the seller? = 你是买方还是卖方？

否定句:
He is not a potential buyer for this type of product. = 他不是这种产品的潜在买家。
She wasn't the buyer we expected. = 她不是我们期望的买家。
