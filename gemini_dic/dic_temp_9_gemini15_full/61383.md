emblazoning:/ɪmˈbleɪzənɪŋ/ | v. |装饰；炫耀；显著地显示 | To decorate (something) conspicuously; to display prominently or boldly.  The act of emblazoning. | 例句：The knight's shield was emblazoned with his family crest. = 骑士的盾牌上饰有他的家族纹章。

The company logo was emblazoned across the side of the building. = 公司的标志醒目地印在建筑物的侧面。

近义词：decorate, adorn, display, blazon, showcase | 反义词：obscure, conceal, hide


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They emblazon their message on every billboard. = 他们在每个广告牌上都炫耀他们的信息。
The artist emblazons vibrant colors onto the canvas. = 艺术家在画布上装饰鲜艳的色彩。
