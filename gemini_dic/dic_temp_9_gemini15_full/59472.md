easeled:/ˈiːzəld/ | n. | 画架 | An easel is a frame that holds a picture or piece of artwork, usually while it is being painted.  |Example: The artist set up his easel in the garden to paint the landscape.  艺术家在花园里支起了画架来画风景画。|近义词：painting stand, stand|反义词：无
