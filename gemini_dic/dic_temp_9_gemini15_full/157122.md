pinchbeck:/ˈpɪntʃbɛk/ | n. |黄铜；劣质金饰 |A cheap imitation of gold made from an alloy of copper and zinc.  Pinchbeck is a pale brassy yellow in color and was popular in the 18th and 19th centuries for jewelry and other decorative items.  | The brooch was made of pinchbeck, not real gold. 这枚胸针是用黄铜做的，不是真金。| brass, imitation gold | real gold, platinum| 名词
