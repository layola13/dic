词源:
* **naso-**:  From Latin *nasus*, meaning "nose".
* **pharyng-**: From Greek *pharynx*, meaning "throat".
* **-eal**:  Suffix indicating "relating to" or "pertaining to".

Therefore, nasopharyngeal literally means "relating to the nose and throat (specifically the nasopharynx)".
