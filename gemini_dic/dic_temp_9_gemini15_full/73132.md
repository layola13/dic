forager:/fəˈreɪdʒər/| n. |觅食者，搜寻者 | A person or animal that searches for food.  |例句：The birds are foragers, constantly searching for insects and seeds.  这些鸟是觅食者，不断寻找昆虫和种子。

例句：Ants are efficient foragers, able to find food sources quickly and efficiently. 蚂蚁是高效的觅食者，能够快速有效地找到食物来源。


近义词：seeker, scavenger, hunter, gatherer |反义词：hoarder, provider (in the sense of providing rather than searching for food) |词性:n.

The birds forage for food in the fields. = 鸟儿在田野里觅食。
