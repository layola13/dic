kiddies:/ˈkɪdiz/| n. |孩子们，小孩们 |A diminutive of "kids," referring to children, often used in a playful or slightly condescending way.  It emphasizes the youth and innocence of the children. |例句：The kiddies loved the bouncy castle at the party. 孩子们非常喜欢派对上的充气城堡。|近义词：children, kids, tots, little ones |反义词：adults, grown-ups
