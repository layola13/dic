rebores:/ˌriːˈbɔːrz/ | v. |重新镗孔，重新钻孔 | To bore again; to enlarge a hole by boring.  This word is less common and often replaced by synonyms. | 例句：The mechanic had to rebore the engine cylinder to fix the damage. = 机械师不得不重新镗孔发动机汽缸以修复损坏。

The engineer decided to rebore the existing pipe to accommodate a larger flow. = 工程师决定重新镗孔现有管道以适应更大的流量。


近义词：rebore, enlarge, widen, redrill
反义词：narrow, constrict, reduce


词性：动词
