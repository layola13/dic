monoculous: /məˈnɒkjʊləs/ | adj. | 独眼的；只有一个透镜的 | Having only one eye;  relating to a single lens.  The word describes something having only one eye or a single lens, often in a metaphorical sense implying a limited or one-sided perspective. |

例句：

The monoculous cyclops terrorized the villagers.  独眼巨人吓坏了村民。

He viewed the situation through a monoculous lens, ignoring crucial details. 他用单一的视角看待局势，忽略了关键细节。


近义词：one-eyed (having only one eye)

反义词：biculous (having two lenses, though this word is rarely used)


词性：形容词
