lode:/loʊd/| n.|矿脉；大量；丰富；|A lode is a vein of metal ore in rock.  It can also refer to a large quantity or abundance of something.|例句：The miners struck a rich lode of gold. 矿工们发现了一条富含黄金的矿脉。

例句：The company boasts a lode of talented employees. 这家公司拥有大量有才华的员工。


近义词：vein, deposit, abundance, wealth|反义词：scarcity, dearth, lack|词性：名词
