/ˈriːliɪŋ/ | v. | 依靠；依赖；信任； reeling (过去分词) | to depend on someone or something for support, help, or information |例句：He was reeling from the shock of the news. 他正从震惊的消息中恢复过来。|近义词：depending, relying, trusting |反义词：independent, self-reliant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am relying on you to finish the project on time. = 我依靠你按时完成项目。
She relies heavily on her parents for financial support. = 她严重依赖父母的经济资助。


一般过去时:
They relied on outdated maps during their hike. = 他们徒步旅行时依赖过时的地图。
He relied on his instincts to make the right decision. = 他依靠直觉做出正确的决定。


现在完成时:
I have been relying on public transportation for years. = 我多年来一直依赖公共交通工具。
She has relied on her friends for emotional support throughout the difficult times. = 在艰难时期，她一直依靠朋友的情感支持。


过去完成时:
They had relied on the weather forecast, which turned out to be inaccurate. = 他们依赖天气预报，结果却是不准确的。
We had relied on him to deliver the goods, but he failed to do so. = 我们依赖他送货，但他没有做到。


将来时:
I will be relying on you to help me move next weekend. = 下周末我将依靠你帮我搬家。
They will rely on modern technology to complete the task efficiently. = 他们将依靠现代技术高效地完成任务。


虚拟语气:
If I were relying on anyone, it would be my brother. = 如果我依赖任何人，那将是我的兄弟。
I wish I hadn't relied so heavily on his promises. = 我真希望我没有那么依赖他的承诺。


被动语态:
The decision was heavily relied upon by the board of directors. = 该决定受到董事会的极大依赖。
His advice is often relied upon by his colleagues. = 他的建议经常被他的同事所依赖。


疑问句:
Are you relying on me to help you? = 你依赖我帮你吗？
Can we rely on the information provided? = 我们可以依赖提供的信息吗？


否定句:
I don't rely on anyone for anything. = 我不依赖任何人任何事。
They did not rely on luck to achieve their success. = 他们没有依靠运气取得成功。


PHRASE->
rely on = 依靠
rely upon = 依靠
rely heavily on = 严重依赖
to rely on oneself = 自力更生
rely for support = 依靠支持
