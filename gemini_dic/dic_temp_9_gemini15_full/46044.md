cycles:/ˈsaɪkəlz/ | n. |循环；周期；自行车；一系列 | A series of events that are regularly repeated.  |例句：The water cycle is essential for life. 水循环对生命至关重要。|例句：The business operates in annual cycles. 这家公司以年度周期运作。|近义词：series, sequence, period, rotation |反义词：stasis, standstill


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water cycle continues endlessly. = 水循环永无止境地继续着。
He cycles to work every day. = 他每天骑自行车上班。

一般过去时:
The economic cycle experienced a downturn last year. = 经济周期去年经历了低迷。
She cycled through the park yesterday. = 她昨天在公园里骑自行车。

现在完成时:
We have completed three cycles of testing. = 我们已经完成了三个测试周期。
