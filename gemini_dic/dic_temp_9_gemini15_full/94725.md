impropriety:/ɪmˈprɑːpraɪəti/| n. |不当行为；不合适；无礼|An impropriety is something that is not considered proper or appropriate behavior, often violating social norms or ethical standards. It can range from minor social faux pas to serious ethical breaches. |例句：His comments were considered an impropriety by many attendees. 他的评论被许多与会者认为是不当的。


例句：The judge ruled that the lawyer's behavior constituted an impropriety. 法官裁定律师的行为构成不当行为。


近义词：inappropriateness, indecency, unsuitableness, misconduct, impropriety


反义词：propriety, appropriateness, correctness, decency


词性:名词
