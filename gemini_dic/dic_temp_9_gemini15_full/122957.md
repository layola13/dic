moms:/mɑmz/| n.|妈妈们，母亲们|The plural form of "mom," referring to multiple mothers.  It can also be used informally to refer to a group of mothers.|例句：The moms were chatting at the school gate. 妈妈们在学校门口聊天。|例句：All the moms are invited to the school picnic. 所有妈妈都邀请参加学校野餐。|近义词：mothers, mums|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The moms bake cookies every week. = 妈妈们每周都烤饼干。
The moms volunteer at the school. = 妈妈们在学校做志愿者。

一般过去时:
The moms attended the PTA meeting. = 妈妈们参加了家长会。
The moms prepared a delicious dinner. = 妈妈们准备了一顿美味的晚餐。

现在完成时:
The moms have finished the school fundraiser. = 妈妈们完成了学校的筹款活动。
The moms have supported their children's dreams. = 妈妈们一直支持着孩子们的梦想。

过去完成时:
The moms had already left before the children woke up. = 孩子们醒来之前，妈妈们已经离开了。
The moms had prepared everything for the party. = 妈妈们已经为派对准备好了所有东西。

将来时:
The moms will attend the graduation ceremony. = 妈妈们将参加毕业典礼。
The moms will be very proud of their children. = 妈妈们将为他们的孩子感到非常自豪。

虚拟语气:
If the moms had more time, they would travel more. = 如果妈妈们有更多时间，她们会多旅行。
I wish the moms could relax more often. = 我希望妈妈们能更经常地放松一下。

被动语态:
The cookies were baked by the moms. = 饼干是由妈妈们烤的。
The school event was organized by the moms. = 学校的活动是由妈妈们组织的。

疑问句:
Are the moms coming to the party? = 妈妈们会来参加聚会吗？
How many moms are there in the group? = 这个小组有多少位妈妈？

否定句:
The moms didn't have time to shop. = 妈妈们没有时间去购物。
The moms weren't able to attend the meeting. = 妈妈们没能参加会议。
