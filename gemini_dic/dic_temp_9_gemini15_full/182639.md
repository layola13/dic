reorganised:/ˌriːˈɔːɡənaɪzd/| v. |重新组织；改组|To organize something again, often in a more efficient or effective way.  This involves restructuring, rearranging, or reforming a system, group, or process.|例句：The company reorganised its sales department to improve efficiency.  公司为了提高效率重新组织了销售部门。

例句：The team reorganised its strategy after the initial failure.  团队在第一次失败后重新调整了策略。


近义词：restructured, rearranged, reformed, revamped

反义词：disorganised, disordered


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reorganise the office every year. = 他们每年都会重新安排办公室。
The company reorganises its marketing strategy regularly. = 公司定期调整其营销策略。

一般过去时:
She reorganised her files after the move. = 她搬家后重新整理了她的文件。
The government reorganised the education system last year. = 政府去年重组了教育体系。


现在完成时:
They have reorganised the entire department. = 他们已经重组了整个部门。
The company has reorganised its operations to cut costs. = 公司已经重组了运营以降低成本。


过去完成时:
He had reorganised his schedule before the trip. = 他在旅行前已经重新安排了他的日程。
The team had reorganised its plan before the presentation. = 团队在演示之前已经重新组织了他们的计划。


将来时:
We will reorganise the library next month. = 我们下个月将重新整理图书馆。
The school will reorganise its curriculum for the next academic year. = 学校将为下一个学年重新调整课程。


虚拟语气:
If we reorganised the workflow, we would be more efficient. = 如果我们重新组织工作流程，我们会更高效。
I wish they had reorganised the meeting agenda beforehand. = 我希望他们事先已经重新安排了会议议程。


被动语态:
The team was reorganised after the merger. = 并购后团队进行了重组。
The project is being reorganised to meet the new deadlines. = 项目正在重新组织以满足新的截止日期。


疑问句:
Will the company be reorganised? = 公司会重组吗？
Have they reorganised the production line yet? = 他们已经重新组织生产线了吗？


否定句:
They did not reorganise the department this year. = 他们今年没有重组部门。
