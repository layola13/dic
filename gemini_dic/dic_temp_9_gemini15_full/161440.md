pot:/pɒt/|ˈpɑːt/| n.|罐；壶；盆；锅|A container, typically round and usually made of earthenware or metal, used for cooking or storing food.  It can also refer to a quantity or amount of something.|例句：She put the stew in a large earthenware pot. 她把炖菜放在一个大陶罐里。

She needs a new pot for her plants. 她需要一个新的花盆。|近义词：container, vessel, kettle, pan|反义词：None
