gypping:/ˈdʒɪpɪŋ/| n. v.|欺骗；敲竹杠；诈骗|The act of cheating or swindling someone, typically by charging excessive prices or providing substandard goods or services.  It often implies a less serious form of fraud than outright theft. |例句：He accused the taxi driver of gypping him on the fare. 他指控出租车司机在他的车费上敲竹杠。  The used car salesman was known for gypping his customers.  那个二手车销售员以欺骗顾客而闻名。|近义词：cheating, swindling, defrauding, ripping off, fleecing|反义词：honesty, fairness
