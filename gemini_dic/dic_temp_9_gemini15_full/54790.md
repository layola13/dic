dispensationalist: /ˌdɪˌspɛnˈseɪʃənəlɪst/ | n. | 灾变论者；期盼论者 | A dispensationalist is someone who adheres to dispensationalism, a theological system that interprets biblical prophecy by dividing history into distinct periods or dispensations, each with its own unique relationship with God.  Dispensationalists generally believe in a literal interpretation of biblical prophecy and anticipate a pre-tribulation rapture of believers. |  Many dispensationalist theologians believe in a literal interpretation of the Book of Revelation. | 近义词：premillennialist (for some aspects) | 反义词：amillennialist, postmillennialist
