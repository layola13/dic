oratorian:/ˌɔːrəˈtɔːriən/| n. |奥拉托里会修士；雄辩家|A member of the Oratory of Saint Philip Neri, a Roman Catholic religious order known for its emphasis on preaching and spiritual direction.  Also, someone skilled in public speaking; an eloquent speaker.|例句：He was an oratorian known for his powerful sermons. 他是一位以其充满力量的布道而闻名的奥拉托里会修士。|例句：The young oratorian impressed the audience with his mastery of rhetoric.这位年轻的雄辩家以其娴熟的修辞技巧给听众留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：priest, preacher, speaker, rhetorician |反义词：mute, silent, inarticulate|名词
