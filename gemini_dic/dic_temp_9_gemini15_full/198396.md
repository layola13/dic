showmen:/ˈʃoʊmən/ | n. |表演者；展览人；（尤指）马戏团演员 | A showman is a person who is skilled at putting on a show, especially one who is entertaining and dramatic. They often have a flair for the dramatic and captivating their audience.  They can be involved in various forms of entertainment, from circus performers to stage actors to television presenters. | The showman captivated the audience with his dazzling performance.  这位表演者以其炫目的表演迷住了观众。| entertainer, performer, impresario |  (无明确反义词，可考虑：amateur, unskilled) | 名词
