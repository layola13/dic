relativeness:/ˌrɛlətɪvnəs/| n. |相对性，相关性|The state or quality of being relative; the fact or condition of being relative to something else.  It emphasizes the dependence of something on its context or relationship to other things.  This concept is crucial in various fields, including physics (theory of relativity), ethics (relativism), and linguistics (relative pronouns).|The relativeness of truth is a topic debated by philosophers for centuries.|近义词：relationship, connection, dependence|反义词：absoluteness, independence
