orange:/ˈɔːrɪndʒ/| n., adj.|橙色，橙子|A round fruit that is a deep yellowish-red color when ripe, and is a member of the citrus family.  It can also refer to the color of this fruit.|例句：I love to eat oranges for breakfast. 我喜欢早餐吃橙子。

The walls of the room were painted a vibrant orange. 房间的墙壁漆成了鲜艳的橙色。|近义词：tangerine, mandarin, citrus|反义词:blue, green
