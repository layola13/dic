dropped:/drɒpt/ /drɑːpt/| v.|掉下；放弃；使下降|To fall or let something fall to the ground.  To discontinue or give up something.|例句：The vase dropped to the floor and shattered. 花瓶掉到地上摔碎了。


The child dropped his ice cream cone. 孩子掉了他的冰淇淋蛋筒。

近义词：fell, abandoned, relinquished, lowered|反义词：raised, lifted, retained, kept|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drops his keys every day. 他每天都把钥匙弄丢。
She drops her children off at school. 她把孩子们送到学校。


一般过去时:
I dropped my phone and cracked the screen. 我把手机掉了，屏幕裂了。
They dropped the project due to budget constraints. 由于预算限制，他们放弃了这个项目。


现在完成时:
He has dropped out of college. 他已经退学了。
She has dropped the lawsuit. 她已经放弃了诉讼。


过去完成时:
I had dropped the ball before realizing my mistake. 我在意识到我的错误之前就犯了错误。
They had dropped the price before the sale. 他们在促销前就降价了。


将来时:
I will drop you off at the airport. 我会送你去机场。
They will drop the charges against him. 他们将撤销对他的指控。


虚拟语气:
If I dropped the course, I would have more free time. 如果我退了这门课，我就有更多空闲时间了。
I wish I hadn't dropped the ball on that project. 我真希望我没有在那个项目上犯错。


被动语态:
The letter was dropped in the mailbox. 信件被投入邮箱。
The price was dropped by 10%. 价格下降了10%。


疑问句:
Did you drop the package? 你把包裹弄丢了吗？
Has she dropped the class yet? 她退课了吗？


否定句:
I didn't drop the glass. 我没有打碎玻璃杯。
He hasn't dropped the subject yet. 他还没放弃这个话题。


PHRASE->
drop by:顺便拜访
drop off:放下；让…下车
drop out:退出；辍学
drop in:顺便拜访
let it drop:别再提了
