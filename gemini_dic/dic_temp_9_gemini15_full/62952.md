enneapetalous: /ˌɛniəˈpɛtələs/ | adj. | 九瓣的 | Having nine petals.  This adjective describes a flower with nine petals.  It is formed from the Greek elements "ennea" (nine) and "petalous" (having petals).


例句：

The enneapetalous flower was a rare and beautiful sight.  九瓣的花朵是一种罕见而美丽的景象。

The botanist carefully examined the enneapetalous specimen. 植物学家仔细检查了这个九瓣的标本。


近义词：nine-petaled (更常用)


反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑用描述瓣数不同的词，如monopetalous (单瓣的), dipetalous (双瓣的)等。


词性：形容词
