chamfering:/ˈtʃæmfəriŋ/| n. v.|斜角加工；倒角；斜面|The act of cutting or beveling an edge or corner to remove sharpness or create a decorative effect.  It can refer to the process itself or the resulting angled surface.|例句：The chamfering of the wood improved its appearance.  木材的倒角处理改善了它的外观。

The carpenter chamfered the edges of the table to prevent splintering. 木匠将桌子的边缘倒角以防止劈裂。|近义词：beveling, easing, rounding|反义词：squaring, sharpening|词性:noun, verb
