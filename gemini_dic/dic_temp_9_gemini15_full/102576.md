jaguar:/ˈdʒæɡjuːər/| n. |美洲豹|A large, spotted cat found in the Americas, known for its powerful build and spotted coat.  It is the third-largest feline in the Americas, after the jaguarundi and cougar.|Example: The jaguar silently stalked its prey through the dense jungle. 美洲豹悄悄地穿过茂密的丛林追踪猎物。|近义词：leopard, panther|反义词：无

词源:
The word "jaguar" comes from the Tupi-Guarani word *yaguara*, meaning "beast" or "animal that kills with one leap".  It entered English through Spanish and Portuguese.
