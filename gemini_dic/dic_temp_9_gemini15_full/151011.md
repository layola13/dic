paranoic:/ˌpærəˈnɔɪk/| adj.|妄想狂的，偏执狂的|Characterized by or exhibiting paranoia; suffering from paranoia.  A paranoic person is excessively distrustful and suspicious of others, often believing that others are plotting against them. |例句：His paranoic behavior alienated his friends.他的妄想行为疏远了他的朋友们。


例句：The paranoic man saw enemies everywhere.  那个妄想狂到处都看到敌人。


近义词：delusional, suspicious, distrustful, obsessive


反义词：trusting, confident, secure, calm
