misevaluation: /ˌmɪsˌɪvæljuˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 错误评估，误判 | The act of misjudging or wrongly assessing something.  It involves a failure to accurately evaluate something's worth, importance, or significance. | The misevaluation of the company's assets led to a significant financial loss. 公司资产的错误评估导致了严重的财务损失。 | misjudgment, misappraisal, underestimation, overestimation | accurate evaluation, correct assessment | 名词
