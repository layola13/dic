objectist:/ˈɑːbdʒɛktɪst/| n. |客观主义者|A person who adheres to objectivism, a philosophical system emphasizing reason, individualism, and laissez-faire capitalism.  Objectivists believe in the importance of objective reality and individual rights.|  Ayn Rand was a prominent objectivist and her novels often explore objectivist themes.  |近义词：rationalist, individualist|反义词：subjectivist, collectivist
