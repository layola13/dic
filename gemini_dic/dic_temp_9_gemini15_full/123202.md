monochromic:/ˌmɒnəˈkrəʊmɪk/|/ˌmɑːnəˈkroʊmɪk/| adj. |单色的，黑白的|Having only one color.  It refers to something that uses only one color or shade, often black and white, but can also apply to any single color. |The photograph was monochromic, showcasing a striking contrast of light and shadow.|近义词：monochromatic, one-colored |反义词：polychromatic, multicolored
