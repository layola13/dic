odinism:/ˈoʊdɪnɪzəm/| n. |奥丁教；奥丁崇拜|A modern Pagan religion that centers on the worship of the Norse god Odin.  It often involves practices such as rune reading, seidr (Norse shamanism), and ancestor veneration.  Odinism emphasizes personal growth, self-reliance, and connection to nature. |Example:  Some practitioners of Odinism incorporate elements of Heathenry into their beliefs and practices.  一些奥丁教的信徒将异教徒的元素融入他们的信仰和实践中。|近义词：Norse paganism, Heathenry|反义词：Christianity, Islam
