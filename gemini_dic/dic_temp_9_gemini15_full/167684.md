prickshaft:/ˈprɪkʃæft/| n. |中间轴，传动轴|A prickshaft is an intermediate shaft in a machine, often used to transmit power or motion between other components.  It's a crucial element in many mechanical systems, particularly those with complex power transmission requirements.|例句：The prickshaft connects the engine to the gearbox, transferring rotational power. 中间轴将发动机连接到变速箱，传递旋转动力。


The worn prickshaft was the cause of the machine's malfunction. 磨损的中间轴是机器故障的原因。|近义词：intermediate shaft, countershaft|反义词：无|
