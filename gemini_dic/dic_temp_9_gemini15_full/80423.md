gonad:/ˈɡoʊnæd/ | n. | 腺；性腺 |  A gonad is a reproductive gland that produces gametes (sex cells).  In vertebrates, gonads are typically the testes in males and the ovaries in females.  They are responsible for producing hormones that regulate sexual development and reproduction. | Example: The  gonads produce both gametes and sex hormones. 性腺产生配子和性激素。 | 近义词: reproductive gland, sex gland | 反义词: N/A
