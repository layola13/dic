sixth-former:/sɪkθˈfɔːrmər/| n.|六年级学生（英国）|A student in the sixth form of a British secondary school.  Sixth form is the final two years of secondary education in England and Wales, roughly equivalent to grades 11 and 12 in the US. |Example: Many sixth-formers apply to universities after completing their studies.  许多六年级学生在完成学业后申请大学。|近义词: upperclassman (US equivalent)|反义词: primary school pupil
