centipede:/ˈsɛntɪpiːd/| n.|蜈蚣|A centipede is a predatory arthropod belonging to the class Chilopoda, characterized by a segmented body with numerous legs, typically one pair per segment. They are known for their elongated bodies and fast movement. |Example: The centipede scurried across the floor.  这只蜈蚣快速地穿过地板。  I saw a giant centipede in the garden. 我在花园里看到一只巨大的蜈蚣。|近义词：millipede (though millipedes have two pairs of legs per segment)|反义词：None specifically, but words implying lack of legs or slow movement could be considered antonyms in a contextual sense.|名词
