prevolunteered:/ˌpriːvɒlənˈtɪərd/| v.|主动提前自愿(做某事)|To volunteer to do something before being asked or before it is necessary.  This implies a proactive and enthusiastic offer of help or participation.|例句：She prevolunteered to lead the project, demonstrating her initiative. 她主动请缨领导这个项目，展现了她的主动性。


例句：Several students prevolunteered for extra tutoring sessions, eager to improve their grades.  几个学生主动要求参加额外的辅导课程，渴望提高他们的成绩。


近义词： volunteered in advance, offered oneself, readily stepped forward


反义词：delayed, procrastinated, refused
