fakest:/ˈfeɪkɪst/| adj.| 最假的，最虚伪的 | Most artificial or untrue; the superlative form of "fake".  It emphasizes the extreme degree of artificiality or untruthfulness.  |例句：That's the fakest smile I've ever seen.  那是我见过的最虚伪的笑容。

例句：His apology felt so fakest, I knew he wasn't truly sorry.他的道歉听起来如此虚伪，我知道他并非真心悔过。


近义词：most artificial, most counterfeit, most sham, most phony, most insincere

反义词：most genuine, most authentic, most real, most sincere

词性：形容词
