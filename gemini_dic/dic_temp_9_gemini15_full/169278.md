prominents:/ˈprɒmɪnənts/| adj.|杰出的，显著的，突出的|  Prominent refers to something that stands out or is easily noticeable. It can describe people who are well-known or important, or things that are clearly visible or significant.|例句：She is a prominent figure in the art world.  她是一位艺术界的杰出人物。|例句：The building has a prominent location in the city center. 这座建筑在市中心占据显眼的位置。|近义词：outstanding, notable, leading, important, conspicuous|反义词：insignificant, obscure, unnoticed, humble
