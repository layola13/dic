shatterer: /ˈʃætərər/ | n. | 粉碎者；破坏者 | One who shatters something; something that shatters.  A person or thing that causes great destruction or devastation. |例句：The earthquake was a shatterer of lives and homes.  地震摧毁了无数生命和家园。

例句：The storm was a shatterer of the fragile peace.  暴风雨摧毁了脆弱的和平。


近义词：destroyer, breaker, pulverizer, ruiner | 反义词：preserver, protector, restorer


词性：名词
