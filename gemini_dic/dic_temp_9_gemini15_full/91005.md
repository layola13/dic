husbander:/ˈhʌzbəndər/ | n. |  农夫；饲养员；管理人 | A husbander is a person who manages or cultivates something, particularly land or livestock.  The term often implies a careful and diligent approach to stewardship. |  He was a skilled husbander, coaxing bountiful harvests from his small farm. 他是一位熟练的农夫，从他那小农场里获得了丰收。|  farmer, cultivator, keeper |  (近义词缺乏直接的反义词，可以根据语境选择例如：neglecter, destroyer) | 名词
