overstepping:/ˌoʊvərˈstɛpɪŋ/| v.|超越；逾越；越界|Going beyond established limits or boundaries.  It implies exceeding what is permitted or appropriate.|例句：He was reprimanded for overstepping his authority. 他因超越职权而受到谴责。

例句：Her constant criticism overstepped the boundaries of acceptable behavior. 她持续不断的批评已经超出了可接受行为的界限。


近义词：transgressing, exceeding, infringing, encroaching


反义词：respecting, adhering to, complying with


USAGE->

He often oversteps his boundaries. 他经常越界。
She oversteps her authority in meetings. 她在会议上越权。


一般过去时:
