reexports:/ˌriːˈɛkspɔːrts/| n. |再出口，转口货物|The act of exporting goods that have been previously imported.  Re-exports are often used to take advantage of favorable trade agreements or to add value to goods before reselling them.  |例句：The country's economy relies heavily on re-exports of manufactured goods. 这个国家的经济严重依赖于制成品的再出口。

例句：Many Asian countries have thriving economies based on re-exports. 许多亚洲国家的经济都蓬勃发展，这得益于再出口。


近义词：re-exportation, transshipment|反义词：domestic sales, original export|词性:noun
