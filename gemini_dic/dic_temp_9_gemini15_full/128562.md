nibble:/ˈnɪbəl/ | n., v. | 轻咬；少量；一点儿 | To bite gently or repeatedly; a small bite or piece of something.  |例句：The mouse nibbled the cheese.  老鼠啃着奶酪。
The bird nibbled at the seeds. 鸟啄食着种子。|近义词：bite, peck, munch, gnaw | 反义词：devour, gobble


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nibbles his food. 他细嚼慢咽。
She nibbles on a carrot. 她啃着胡萝卜。

一般过去时:
The dog nibbled the bone.  狗啃着骨头。
He nibbled at the cookie. 他小口地吃着饼干。

现在完成时:
The rabbit has nibbled all the lettuce. 兔子已经把所有的莴苣都啃光了。
She has nibbled at the problem for weeks. 她已经琢磨这个问题好几个星期了。

过去完成时:
The mouse had nibbled through the wire. 老鼠已经啃断了电线。
They had nibbled all the snacks before the movie started. 电影开始前他们已经把所有零食都吃光了。

将来时:
I think the dog will nibble at the toy. 我认为狗会啃咬玩具。
She will nibble on some fruit later. 她一会儿会吃点水果。

虚拟语气:
If I were hungry, I would nibble on an apple. 如果我饿了，我会啃个苹果。
I wish I had nibbled less at the buffet. 我真希望在自助餐上少吃点。

被动语态:
The carrots were nibbled by the rabbit. 胡萝卜被兔子啃了。
The problem is being nibbled at by the committee. 委员会正在慢慢解决这个问题。

疑问句:
Is the dog nibbling on the shoe? 狗在啃鞋吗？
Are you nibbling at your nails? 你在啃指甲吗？

否定句:
The baby didn't nibble the food. 婴儿没有啃食物。
He didn't nibble at the bait. 他没有轻咬鱼饵。


PHRASE->
nibble at something:  少量地吃某物
a nibble of chocolate: 一小块巧克力
a nibble here and there:  这里那里吃一点
a nibble of information: 一点儿信息
take a nibble: 小口吃一口

词源:
The word "nibble" originated in Middle English, likely from a combination of the Old English word "nib" (meaning "beak" or "point") and a diminutive suffix "-le".  It originally referred to the pecking action of a bird, and later broadened to include the gentle biting of other creatures.
