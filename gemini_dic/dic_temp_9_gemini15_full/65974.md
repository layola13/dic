exaggerators:/ɪɡˈzædʒəreɪtərz/ | n. |夸大其词者，言过其实者 | People who exaggerate; those given to hyperbole.  They often embellish stories or facts to make them more interesting or impactful, sometimes unintentionally. | 例句：The journalist's article was criticized for being full of exaggerators.  记者的文章因充满夸大之词而受到批评。  | 例句：Many of the exaggerators in the meeting were later proven wrong.  会议上许多夸大其词的人后来被证明是错的。 |近义词：hyperbolists, overstaters, embellishers |反义词：understaters, minimizers |名词
