pregenerate: /ˌpriːˈdʒɛnəreɪt/ | vt. | 预先生成；预先产生 | To generate something beforehand or in advance.  This often involves creating something that will be used later, such as data, code, or content.  | 例句：The system pregenerates reports every night for the next day's review. 系统每晚预先生成报告，以便第二天审查。


The software pregenerates thumbnails for all uploaded images. 软件为所有上传的图像预先生成缩略图。| 近义词：precreate, generate in advance, prepare beforehand | 反义词：generate afterward, create on demand
