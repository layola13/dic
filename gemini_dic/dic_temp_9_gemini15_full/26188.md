butlers:/ˈbʌtlərz/ | n. |管家，男仆 |A butler is a senior male servant in a household, responsible for managing the household staff and overseeing the smooth running of the household.  They often have a high level of skill and experience in household management, serving food and drinks, and managing the household's finances. | The butler announced dinner.  管家宣布晚餐。|  servant, valet |  (无明显的反义词，可以考虑 mistress 或 housekeeper 但语义差异较大) |名词
