moonlighted:/ˈmuːnˌlaɪtɪd/| adj. |月光照射的；兼职的| Having or relating to the moon's light; working at a second job in addition to one's regular job. |例句：The garden was moonlighted, casting long shadows. 月光照射着花园，投下长长的影子。  The moonlighted room was filled with a magical atmosphere. 月光照射的房间充满了神奇的氛围。|近义词：illuminated,  part-time (for the second job meaning)|反义词：sunlit, full-time (for the second job meaning)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He moonlights as a taxi driver. = 他兼职做出租车司机。
She moonlights at a local bar. = 她在当地一家酒吧兼职。


一般过去时:
He moonlighted as a bartender last year. = 他去年兼职做调酒师。
She moonlighted at a bookstore during college. = 她大学期间在书店兼职。


现在完成时:
He has moonlighted as a musician for many years. = 他多年来一直兼职做音乐家。
She has moonlighted in various roles to make ends meet. = 她做过各种兼职来维持生计。


过去完成时:
He had moonlighted as a waiter before he got his current job. = 在找到现在的工作之前，他做过一段时间的服务员兼职。
She had moonlighted at several places before finding her perfect part-time job. = 在找到她理想的兼职工作之前，她曾在几个地方兼职。


将来时:
He will moonlight as a tutor during the summer. = 他将在夏天兼职做家教。
She will moonlight at a coffee shop to save money for a trip. = 她将在咖啡店兼职来攒钱旅行。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would moonlight as a writer. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会兼职写作。
I wish I could moonlight, but I'm too busy. = 我希望我能兼职，但我太忙了。


被动语态:
His extra income is moonlighted. (This usage is less common) = 他的额外收入是通过兼职获得的。(这种用法不太常见)
This job is not meant to be moonlighted. (This usage is less common) = 这份工作不适合兼职。(这种用法不太常见)


疑问句:
Does he moonlight? = 他兼职吗？
Does she moonlight often? = 她经常兼职吗？


否定句:
He doesn't moonlight anymore. = 他不再兼职了。
She doesn't plan to moonlight this semester. = 她这学期不打算兼职。



PHRASE->
moonlight a job = 兼职一份工作
moonlight on the side = 额外兼职
moonlight as a...= 兼职做...
moonlight to supplement income = 兼职以补充收入
moonlight to pay off debt = 兼职还债


词源:
The word "moonlighted" originates from the idea of doing a second job secretly, often under the cover of darkness, like the moon.  The implied secrecy is less emphasized in modern usage, where it primarily means holding a second job.
