reimmigration:/ˌriːɪmɪˈɡreɪʃən/ | n. |重新移民，回返移民 | The act of immigrating again, especially after a period of living elsewhere.  It often refers to the return of immigrants to their home country or to another country they have previously lived in. |例句：The government is studying the effects of reimmigration on the national economy. 政府正在研究重新移民对国民经济的影响。

例句：Many factors contribute to the patterns of reimmigration in this region. 许多因素促成了该地区重新移民的模式。


近义词：return migration, repatriation (if returning to one's home country) | 反义词：emigration, immigration (initial migration)


词性：名词
