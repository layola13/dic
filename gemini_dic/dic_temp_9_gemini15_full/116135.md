maryolatry:/ˌmɛriˈɑːlətri/ | n. |对圣母玛利亚的崇拜 |The excessive or misguided veneration of the Virgin Mary.  It is considered a form of hyperdulia, a higher degree of veneration than that given to ordinary saints.  Some critics argue that it borders on or constitutes idolatry. | Example:  Some critics accused the sect of maryolatry due to their excessive devotion to the Virgin Mary and the numerous shrines erected in her honor. |近义词: hyperdulia, Mariolatry (same meaning)| 反义词: iconoclasm (destruction of religious images),  disrespect. |名词
