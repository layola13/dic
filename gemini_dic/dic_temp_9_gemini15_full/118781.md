metallophone:/ˈmɛtəlˌoʊfoʊn/| n. |金属敲击乐器|A musical instrument made of metal bars that are struck with mallets to produce sounds.  Metallophones are found in various cultures and musical traditions around the world, ranging from simple instruments to complex orchestral ones. They are often tuned to specific scales or pitches. |The orchestra included a vibrant metallophone solo in the third movement.  乐队在第三乐章中加入了充满活力的金属敲击乐器独奏。

The xylophone is a type of metallophone.  木琴是一种金属敲击乐器。|近义词：xylophone, glockenspiel, vibraphone |反义词：string instrument, wind instrument
