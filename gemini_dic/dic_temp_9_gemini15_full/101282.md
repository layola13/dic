involve:/ɪnˈvɒlv/| vt.|包含；涉及；使参与|To include or contain as a part or component; to have as a necessary consequence; to engage or participate in something.|The project involves a lot of work.  这个项目涉及很多工作。|The accident involved three cars. 这起事故涉及三辆车。|include, encompass, engage|exclude, detach


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project involves many people. = 这个项目涉及许多人。
This recipe involves several steps. = 这个食谱包含好几个步骤。


一般过去时:
The meeting involved a lengthy discussion. = 会议进行了一场冗长的讨论。
The incident involved a misunderstanding. = 这起事件源于一场误会。


现在完成时:
The investigation has involved interviewing numerous witnesses. = 调查已经涉及到采访许多证人。
She has involved herself in many charitable causes. = 她参与了许多慈善事业。


过去完成时:
The police had involved themselves in the case before the evidence was discovered. = 在证据被发现之前，警方已经介入此案。
He had involved himself in activities he later regretted. = 他参与了一些后来让他后悔的活动。


将来时:
The new policy will involve significant changes to the system. = 新政策将对系统带来重大改变。
The experiment will involve testing various materials. = 实验将涉及测试各种材料。


虚拟语气:
If the plan involved more risk, we would reconsider. = 如果计划包含更多风险，我们将重新考虑。
I wish the project didn't involve so much paperwork. = 我希望这个项目不需要这么多文书工作。


被动语态:
Many people were involved in the accident. = 许多人卷入了事故。
He was involved in a car crash last week. = 他上周卷入了一场车祸。


疑问句:
What activities are involved in this program? = 这个项目包含哪些活动？
Are you involved in any extracurricular activities? = 你参与任何课外活动吗？


否定句:
The decision did not involve me. = 这个决定没有涉及到我。
The problem doesn't involve any technical difficulties. = 这个问题不涉及任何技术难题。


PHRASE->
be involved in = 参与…
get involved = 卷入
involve oneself in = 积极参与
deeply involved = 深度参与
involved in a relationship = 处于恋爱关系
