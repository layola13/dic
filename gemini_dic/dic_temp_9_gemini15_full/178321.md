recondense:/ˌriːkənˈdens/| vt.|重新凝结；再浓缩|To condense again; to become condensed again.  The process involves a change of state from a gaseous or vaporous form back to a liquid or solid form.|例句：The water vapor recondensed into droplets on the cold surface.  水蒸气在冰冷的表面上重新凝结成水滴。The concentrated solution was recondensed after evaporation. 浓缩溶液蒸发后重新浓缩。|近义词：condense, re-condense|反义词：evaporate, vaporize|动词
