/ˈsiːvər/| n.|筛子；筛选器|A sieve is a utensil with a mesh bottom used for separating solids from liquids or for sifting dry ingredients.|例句：I used a sieve to separate the flour from the lumps. 我用筛子把面粉里的结块筛出来。|近义词：strainer, filter|反义词：None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chef sieves the flour before adding it to the batter. = 厨师在把面粉加入面糊之前先过筛。
She sieves the rice to remove any stones. = 她筛米以去除任何石子。


一般过去时:
He sieved the sand to find gold. = 他筛沙子寻找金子。
They sieved the soil looking for fossils. = 他们筛土寻找化石。


现在完成时:
She has sieved all the flour for the cake. = 她已经把做蛋糕的所有面粉都筛过了。
We have sieved the gravel and removed the large stones. = 我们已经筛过碎石并去除了大石头。


过去完成时:
He had sieved the grain before the birds could reach it. = 在鸟儿吃到之前，他已经筛过谷物了。
They had sieved the sand, but found no gold. = 他们已经筛过沙子了，但没有找到金子。


将来时:
She will sieve the berries before making the jam. = 她将在制作果酱之前把浆果过筛。
They will sieve the soil to analyze its composition. = 他们将筛土以分析其成分。
