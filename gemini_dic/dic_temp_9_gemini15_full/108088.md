latex:/ˈleɪtɛks/ | n. |乳胶；乳汁；莱特克斯 | A natural rubber obtained as a milky fluid from certain plants, especially the rubber tree, and used in the manufacture of rubber products.  | Example: Latex gloves are commonly used in medical settings.  乳胶手套通常用于医疗环境。 |近义词：rubber, sap |反义词：无
