self-helpfulness:/ˌsɛlfˈhɛlpflənɪs/| n. |自助；自我帮助；自立性 |The quality or state of being self-helpful; the ability to help oneself.  It emphasizes the capacity to solve one's own problems and overcome challenges independently. |例句：Her self-helpfulness allowed her to overcome many obstacles in life.  她的自立性使她克服了生活中的许多障碍。

例句：The program encourages self-helpfulness among its participants. 该项目鼓励参与者自助。


近义词：self-reliance, independence, autonomy, resourcefulness


反义词：dependence, reliance on others, helplessness


词性：名词
