orientation: /ˌɔːriənˈteɪʃən/| n. |方向，方位；定向；情况介绍；性取向 |The process of adapting or becoming familiar with a new situation, environment, or set of ideas.  It can refer to physical direction, mental adjustment, or sexual preference.|例句：The map helped with our orientation in the unfamiliar city.  地图帮助我们在陌生的城市中找到方向。

例句：The company provides orientation for all new employees. 公司为所有新员工提供入职培训。

近义词：introduction, familiarization, adjustment, alignment, positioning

反义词：disorientation, misalignment, confusion
