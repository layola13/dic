relevering:/ˌriːˈliːvərɪŋ/| v.|再杠杆化，重新利用杠杆|The process of increasing a company's financial leverage by taking on more debt.|例句：The company is considering relevering its capital structure to fund expansion.  这家公司正在考虑通过增加债务来重新调整其资本结构以资助扩张。|近义词：recapitalization, refinancing|反义词：deleveraging

一般现在时:
The company relevers its debt regularly. = 这家公司定期调整债务杠杆。
They are relevering their operations to boost profits. = 他们正在重新调整运营杠杆以提高利润。


一般过去时:
The firm relevered its balance sheet last year. = 该公司去年重新调整了资产负债表杠杆。
They relevered their investment portfolio to maximize returns. = 他们重新调整了投资组合杠杆以最大化回报。
