hospitalize:/ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪz/ /ˈhɑːspɪtəlaɪz/ | vt. |送进医院；住院治疗 |To place (someone) in a hospital for treatment.  |The doctor decided to hospitalize him after the accident.  医生决定在事故后让他住院。|admit, confine | discharge, release |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hospital hospitalizes many patients every day. =这家医院每天收治许多病人。
They hospitalize children with serious illnesses. =他们收治患有严重疾病的儿童。


一般过去时:
They hospitalized her immediately after the car crash. =车祸后他们立即将她送往医院。
The surgeon hospitalized the patient for observation. =外科医生让病人住院观察。


现在完成时:
They have hospitalized over 100 patients this month. =这个月他们已经收治了100多名病人。
The hospital has hospitalized many victims of the earthquake. =医院收治了许多地震的受害者。


过去完成时:
He had been hospitalized twice before this incident. =在此事件之前，他已经被送进医院两次了。
She had already been hospitalized for a week when her family visited.  =她家人探望她时，她已经被住院一周了。


将来时:
They will hospitalize him for surgery next week. =他们下周将送他去医院做手术。
The doctors will hospitalize the patient if his condition worsens. =如果病人的病情恶化，医生会将他送进医院。


虚拟语气:
If he had been hospitalized sooner, he might have recovered faster. =如果他早点住院，他可能会恢复得更快。
It's time he was hospitalized; his condition is critical. =他该住院了；他的病情危急。


被动语态:
He was hospitalized for three weeks. =他住院三个星期。
Many patients are hospitalized due to traffic accidents. =许多病人因交通事故而住院。


疑问句:
Was she hospitalized after the accident? =事故后她住院了吗？
Will he be hospitalized for his heart condition? =他因心脏病而住院吗？


否定句:
They did not hospitalize him because his condition wasn't serious enough. =他们没有让他住院，因为他的病情还不够严重。
She wasn't hospitalized; she was treated at home.  =她没有住院；她在家里接受治疗。
