peritoneoscopy:/ˌpɛrɪtəˈnɒskəpi/ | n. | 腹腔镜检查 | The examination of the abdominal cavity using a peritoneoscope.  A minimally invasive surgical procedure that allows a surgeon to view the abdominal organs without making a large incision. | Example: The doctor recommended a peritoneoscopy to investigate the cause of the patient's abdominal pain.  医生建议进行腹腔镜检查以调查病人腹痛的原因。 |近义词：laparoscopy (if referring to the same procedure) |反义词：laparotomy (open abdominal surgery)
