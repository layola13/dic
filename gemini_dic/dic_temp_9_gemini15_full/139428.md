obsessively:/əbˈsɛsɪvli/ | adv. |  痴迷地；着魔地 |  In a way that shows excessive preoccupation with something.  It implies a compulsive or uncontrollable focus. | He obsessively checked his phone for messages. 他痴迷地检查手机是否有信息。|  例句：She obsessively cleaned her house every day. 她每天都痴迷地打扫她的房子。 |近义词：compulsively, maniacally, frantically | 反义词：casually, indifferently
