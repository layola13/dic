reaccentuates: /ˌriːˈæksənˌtjuːeɪts/ | vt. |重新强调；再次强调 | To emphasize or stress something again.  The speaker reaccentuated the importance of education in his closing remarks.  | 近义词：reiterate, re-emphasize, stress again | 反义词：downplay, de-emphasize
