/mʌltiɪɡzɔːsts/ | n. | 多重排气 | A multiexhaust system refers to an engine or machine with multiple exhaust pipes or outlets, designed to disperse exhaust gases more efficiently or effectively.  This is often seen in high-performance vehicles or industrial machinery where increased exhaust capacity is needed. | Example: The race car's multiexhaust system roared as it accelerated down the track.  The factory's new multiexhaust system reduced noise pollution significantly. | 近义词: multiple exhausts, manifold exhaust | 反义词: single exhaust
