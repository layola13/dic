gauger:/ˈɡeɪdʒər/| n. |计量员；评估者；估价者 |A gauger is a person who measures the contents of casks or other vessels, especially of liquids, to determine the amount of tax due.  They also assess or estimate the value or quantity of something. |The customs official acted as a gauger, meticulously measuring the contents of each barrel. 海关官员担任计量员，仔细测量每个桶的内容物。| assessor, estimator, measurer |  |n.
