epee:/ˈeɪpeɪ/| n. |重剑|A type of fencing weapon, longer and heavier than a foil or saber, used in the sport of épée.  It has a three-sided blade and a larger protective cup at the pommel.|例句：He won the épée competition with a decisive victory. 他以决定性的胜利赢得了重剑比赛。|近义词：foil (though different weapon), saber (though different weapon)|反义词：无
