/feɪz/ | n. |阶段；方面；相；面貌 | A period or stage in a process.  |The project is in its final phase.  这个项目处于最后阶段。|  stage, period, step |  completion, end | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This project is in its initial phase. = 这个项目正处于初始阶段。
What phase of the moon is it tonight? = 今晚是哪个月相？


一般过去时:
The first phase of the construction was completed last month. = 上个月完成了施工的第一阶段。
What phase of the project were you working on yesterday? = 昨天你在做项目的哪个阶段？


现在完成时:
We have completed the first phase of the research. = 我们已经完成了研究的第一阶段。
What phases have you completed so far? = 到目前为止你完成了哪些阶段？


过去完成时:
They had already finished the second phase before we joined the project. =在我们加入项目之前，他们已经完成了第二阶段。
What phase had they reached before the funding was cut? = 在资金被削减之前，他们达到了哪个阶段？


将来时:
The next phase of the development will begin next year. = 下一阶段的开发将在明年开始。
What phase will the project enter next? = 项目接下来将进入哪个阶段？


虚拟语气:
If we had more funding, we could have completed all phases on time. = 如果我们有更多资金，我们就能按时完成所有阶段。
I wish we were in a more advanced phase of the project. = 我希望我们的项目处于更高级的阶段。


被动语态:
The different phases of the process were explained in detail. = 过程的不同阶段被详细解释了。
All phases of the production are being monitored closely. = 生产的所有阶段都受到严格监控。


疑问句:
What phase are we in now? = 我们现在处于哪个阶段？
What phase of the treatment is he currently in? = 他目前处于治疗的哪个阶段？


否定句:
This isn't the right phase to make such a decision. = 现在不是做出这个决定的时候。
We haven't yet entered the final phase of the negotiations. = 我们还没有进入谈判的最后阶段。


PHRASE->
in phase  = 同步的；处于同一阶段
out of phase =  不同步的；脱节的
transition phase = 过渡阶段
experimental phase = 实验阶段
initial phase = 初始阶段
