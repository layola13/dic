recycles:/ˌriˈsaɪkəlz/| v. |回收利用；再循环|To convert waste materials into reusable objects.|The city recycles paper, plastic, and glass.|近义词：reprocesses, reuses|反义词：discards, wastes|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recycles newspapers every week. = 他每周都回收报纸。
They recycle plastic bottles and cans. = 他们回收塑料瓶和罐子。

一般过去时:
She recycled all the cardboard boxes. = 她回收了所有的纸板箱。
We recycled our old clothes last month. = 我们上个月回收了旧衣服。

现在完成时:
I have recycled over 100 pounds of aluminum cans this year. = 今年我已经回收了超过100磅的铝罐。
They have recycled a significant amount of waste since the program began. = 自从项目开始以来，他们已经回收了大量的废物。

过去完成时:
Before the new recycling plant opened, they had recycled very little. = 在新的回收厂开业之前，他们回收的很少。
The company had recycled thousands of tons of materials before it went bankrupt. = 在破产之前，该公司已经回收了数千吨的材料。

将来时:
We will recycle more in the future. = 我们将来会回收更多。
The government will implement stricter recycling policies next year. = 政府明年将实施更严格的回收政策。

虚拟语气:
If everyone recycled, we would have less waste. = 如果每个人都回收利用，我们的废物就会减少。
I wish I had recycled that plastic bottle. = 我真希望我当时回收了那个塑料瓶。

被动语态:
Paper is recycled to make new paper products. = 纸被回收用来制造新的纸制品。
Waste materials are recycled in many different ways. = 废弃物以许多不同的方式被回收利用。

疑问句:
Does your community recycle? = 你的社区回收吗？
What materials does your local recycling center accept? = 你当地回收中心接受哪些材料？

否定句:
He doesn't recycle plastic. = 他不回收塑料。
They didn't recycle the glass bottles. = 他们没有回收玻璃瓶。


PHRASE->
recycle waste = 回收废物
recycle materials = 回收材料
recycle paper = 回收纸张
recycle plastic = 回收塑料
recycle cans = 回收罐子
