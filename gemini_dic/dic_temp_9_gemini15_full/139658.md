occupation:/ˌɒkjʊˈpeɪʃən/ /ˌɑːkjʊˈpeɪʃən/| n. |职业，工作；占领；居住|A person's occupation is their job or profession.  It can also refer to the act of occupying a place or space. |例句：His occupation is a software engineer. 他的职业是软件工程师。
例句：The army's occupation of the city lasted for many years.  军队占领该城市持续多年。|近义词：profession, job, vocation, employment, calling|反义词：leisure, idleness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your occupation? = 你的职业是什么？
What occupation does she have? = 她从事什么职业？


一般过去时:
What was his occupation before retirement? = 他退休前从事什么职业？
What occupation did she pursue in her youth? = 她年轻时从事什么职业？


现在完成时:
What occupation has he held for the longest time? = 他从事哪个职业的时间最长？
What occupation has she had the most success in? = 她在哪种职业上取得了最大的成功？


过去完成时:
What occupation had he held before joining the company? = 他加入公司之前从事过什么职业？
What occupation had she pursued before she became a teacher? = 在成为老师之前，她从事过什么职业？


将来时:
What occupation will you pursue after graduation? = 毕业后你打算从事什么职业？
What occupation will she likely have in the future? = 她将来可能会从事什么职业？


虚拟语气:
If I had a different occupation, I would be happier. = 如果我有不同的职业，我会更快乐。
I wish I had a more fulfilling occupation. = 我希望我有一个更令人满足的职业。


被动语态:
The position is currently unoccupied. = 职位目前空缺。 (Note: This uses "unoccupied" which relates to the meaning of occupation as "holding a place.")
Many jobs were lost due to the factory's closure. = 由于工厂倒闭，许多工作岗位流失。(Note: This uses "jobs" which is related to the meaning of occupation as "work")


疑问句:
What is your chosen occupation? = 你选择的职业是什么？
What occupation are you interested in? = 你对什么职业感兴趣？


否定句:
He doesn't have a steady occupation. = 他没有稳定的职业。
She hasn't decided on her future occupation yet. = 她还没有决定未来的职业。
