nearing:/ˈnɪəriŋ/| v. adj.| 接近；临近 |  Approaching in time or distance; about to happen. |例句：The end of the year is nearing.  年末将至。


The deadline is nearing fast. 截止日期越来越近了。


近义词：approaching, approaching, imminent, close at hand


反义词：distant, far off, remote


词性:动词，形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm is nearing the coast. = 暴风雨正在逼近海岸。
We are nearing the end of the project. = 我们正在接近项目的尾声。


一般过去时:
The train was nearing the station. = 火车正在接近车站。
We were nearing our destination. = 我们正在接近目的地。


现在完成时:
The hikers have neared the summit. = 登山者已经接近山顶。
We have neared our goal. = 我们已经接近我们的目标。


过去完成时:
They had neared the finish line when the accident occurred. = 事故发生时，他们已经接近终点线了。
The sun had neared the horizon. = 太阳已经接近地平线了。


将来时:
The ship will be nearing port tomorrow. = 船明天将接近港口。
We will be nearing the city soon. = 我们很快就会接近城市。


虚拟语气:
If we neared the target, we would celebrate. = 如果我们接近目标，我们会庆祝。
If the deadline neared sooner, we would be in trouble. = 如果截止日期提前到来，我们会陷入困境。


被动语态:
The city was neared by the army. = 城市被军队逼近了。(较少用被动语态)
The summit was neared by the climbers. = 山顶被登山者接近了。(较少用被动语态)


疑问句:
Is the train nearing the station? = 火车快到站了吗？
Are we nearing the end of the meeting? = 我们快结束会议了吗？


否定句:
The ship is not nearing the coast. = 船没有接近海岸。
We are not nearing a solution. = 我们还没接近解决方案。


PHRASE->
nearing completion: 接近完成
nearing the end: 接近尾声
nearing retirement: 临近退休
nearing its peak: 接近顶峰
nearing disaster: 濒临灾难
