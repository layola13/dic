okayy:/oʊˈkeɪ/| int.| 好吧，可以，同意 |  An informal variant of "okay," expressing agreement, acceptance, or acknowledgment.  It often conveys a slightly more emphatic or drawn-out feeling than "okay." |例句：Okayy, I'll do it. = 好吧，我会做的。  Okayy, let's go. = 好吧，我们走吧。|近义词：okay, alright, all right, yes, fine |反义词：no, nope
