quasi-neutral:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈnjuːtrəl/| adj.|准中性的，近似中性的|Approaching neutrality; almost but not completely neutral.  It often describes a state where there is a near balance but not perfect equality. For example, in plasma physics, a quasi-neutral plasma has approximately equal numbers of positive and negative charges, but there might be small deviations.|例句：The plasma in the fusion reactor is maintained in a quasi-neutral state.  等离子体在聚变反应堆中保持准中性状态。

The quasi-neutral conditions were carefully monitored during the experiment.  实验期间仔细监测了准中性条件。|近义词：near-neutral, almost neutral, approximately neutral|反义词：strongly charged, highly ionized
