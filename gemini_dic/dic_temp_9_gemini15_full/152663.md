peakiest:/ˈpiːkiːst/| adj. |最顶峰的；最尖的；最显著的 | Most prominent or highest; characterized by peaks or summits.  It is the superlative form of "peaky". |例句：The peakiest mountain in the range was breathtaking.  这个山脉中最高的山峰令人叹为观止。

The peakiest point of the graph showed record sales. 图表中最顶峰的点显示了创纪录的销售额。


近义词：highest, most prominent, steepest, sharpest |反义词：lowest, flattest, dullest


词源:  Derived from "peak," ultimately from Old French *pic, and Latin *pix, meaning "point" or "peak."  The "-iest" suffix indicates the superlative degree, signifying the highest degree of "peakiness."
