fairly:/ˈferli/| adv.|相当，公平地，公正地|To a reasonable extent; moderately.  It suggests a degree between "slightly" and "very".|例句：The exam was fairly difficult.  考试相当难。

例句：She was treated fairly in court.  她在法庭上受到了公平的待遇。|近义词：reasonably, moderately, quite, pretty|反义词：unfairly, unjustly, improperly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He treats everyone fairly. = 他公平对待每一个人。
She fairly distributes the workload. = 她相当公平地分配工作量。

一般过去时:
The judge ruled fairly in the case. = 法官在该案中公正判决。
We fairly divided the profits among ourselves. = 我们相当公平地分配了利润。

现在完成时:
They have fairly completed the project. = 他们已经相当完成了这个项目。
She has always been treated fairly by her boss. = 她一直受到老板的公平对待。

过去完成时:
We had fairly finished our work before the deadline. = 我们在截止日期前已基本完成了工作。
The committee had fairly considered all the proposals. = 委员会已经相当认真地考虑了所有提案。

将来时:
I will fairly assess the situation before making a decision. = 我将在做出决定之前公平地评估情况。
We will fairly allocate resources to each department. = 我们将公平地分配资源给每个部门。

虚拟语气:
If he were treated fairly, he wouldn't be so angry. = 如果他受到公平对待，他就不会那么生气。
I wish the competition were fairer. = 我希望比赛更公平一些。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
