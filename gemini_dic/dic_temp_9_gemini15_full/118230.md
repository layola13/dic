mercatante:/ˌmɜːrkənˈtɑːnteɪ/| n.|商人，贸易商|A mercatante is a person who is engaged in trade, especially a wholesale merchant.|例句：The mercatante sold spices from the East.  这位商人出售来自东方的香料。|例句：Many mercatantes gathered at the market square. 许多商人聚集在市场广场。|近义词：trader, merchant, dealer|反义词：consumer, buyer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mercatante sells silk. = 这个商人卖丝绸。
He trades in rare books. = 他做稀有书籍的买卖。

一般过去时:
The mercatante sold all his goods. = 这个商人卖掉了所有的货物。
She traded her jewels for a horse. = 她用她的珠宝换了一匹马。

现在完成时:
The mercatante has expanded his business. = 这个商人已经扩大了他的生意。
He has traded successfully for many years. = 他多年来一直成功地经商。

过去完成时:
The mercatante had already sold his stock before the market closed. = 在市场关闭之前，这位商人已经卖掉了他的存货。
She had traded her inheritance for a vineyard. = 她用她的遗产换了一个葡萄园。

将来时:
The mercatante will open a new shop next month. = 商人下个月将开一家新店。
He will trade in antiques. = 他将做古董生意。

虚拟语气:
If I were a mercatante, I would travel the world. = 如果我是一个商人，我会周游世界。
I wish I had been a mercatante in the Silk Road era. = 我希望我在丝绸之路时代是一个商人。

被动语态:
The silk was sold by the mercatante. = 丝绸是由这位商人卖的。
The goods were traded fairly. = 货物交易得很公平。

疑问句:
Is he a mercatante? = 他是商人吗？
What goods does the mercatante sell? = 这个商人卖什么货物？

否定句:
He is not a mercatante. = 他不是商人。
She did not trade fairly. = 她交易不公平。


PHRASE->
mercatante ambulante = street vendor (流动摊贩)
mercatante di tessuti = textile merchant (纺织品商人)
mercatante d'arte = art dealer (艺术品经销商)
mercatante all'ingrosso = wholesale merchant (批发商)
mercatante al dettaglio = retail merchant (零售商)
