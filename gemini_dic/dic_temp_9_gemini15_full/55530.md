distributes:/dɪˈstrɪbjuːts/| vt.|分发，分配，散布|To divide and give out something to several people or places.|The teacher distributes the textbooks to the students.老师把课本分发给学生。|近义词：allocate, dispense, share|反义词：collect, gather


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company distributes its products worldwide.  这家公司在全球范围内分销其产品。
He distributes flyers in the city center. 他在市中心分发传单。


一般过去时:
She distributed the candy to the children. 她把糖果分给孩子们。
The government distributed aid to the flood victims. 政府向洪灾受害者分发了援助物资。


现在完成时:
They have distributed all the invitations. 他们已经分发了所有的请柬。
The company has distributed its new software to its clients. 公司已将其新软件分发给客户。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, they had distributed the agenda. 会议之前，他们已经分发了议程。
She had distributed the questionnaires before the survey began.  调查开始之前，她已经分发了问卷。


将来时:
We will distribute the profits among the partners. 我们将把利润分配给合伙人。
The organization will distribute food to the refugees. 该组织将向难民分发食物。


虚拟语气:
If the company distributed its products more effectively, it would increase sales. 如果公司更有效地分销其产品，销售额就会增加。
I wish they had distributed the information earlier. 我希望他们早点分发信息。


被动语态:
The gifts were distributed fairly among the participants. 礼物在参与者中公平地分发。
The leaflets are being distributed throughout the town. 传单正在全城分发。


疑问句:
How are the resources distributed? 资源是如何分配的？
Where will the new products be distributed? 新产品将在哪里分销？


否定句:
He didn't distribute the emails to everyone. 他没有把邮件分发给所有人。
The company does not distribute its products in that region. 该公司不在那个地区分销其产品。


PHRASE->
distribute evenly 平均分配
distribute widely 广泛传播
distribute among 在…之间分配
distribute throughout 遍布
