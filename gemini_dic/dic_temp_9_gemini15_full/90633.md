humanitarianism: /hjuːˈmænɪtəriənɪzəm/ | n. |人道主义|The principle of humanitarianism is the belief in the value and importance of human life and well-being, and the promotion of human dignity and rights. It emphasizes compassion, empathy, and a commitment to alleviating suffering.  Humanitarianism often involves practical actions such as providing aid to those in need, advocating for social justice, and promoting peace. |例句：The organization's humanitarian efforts provided much-needed food and medical supplies to the disaster-stricken region.  该组织的人道主义努力为灾区提供了急需的食品和医疗用品。

The doctor's humanitarian work in war-torn countries earned her international recognition.  这位医生在饱受战争蹂躏的国家从事人道主义工作，赢得了国际赞誉。|近义词：philanthropy, altruism, benevolence |反义词：inhumanity, cruelty, barbarity |词性: noun
