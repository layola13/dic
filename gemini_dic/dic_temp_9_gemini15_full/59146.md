dynamometry:/daɪnæˈmɒmɪtri/|/ˌdaɪnəˈmɑːmɪtri/| n. |测力法；测力学|The science or act of measuring force, especially muscular force.  It involves using a dynamometer to quantify the strength of a muscle or muscle group.|例句：The physiotherapist used dynamometry to assess the patient's muscle strength after the injury. 物理治疗师使用测力法来评估病人受伤后的肌肉力量。

例句：Research on handgrip dynamometry is crucial for understanding age-related decline in muscle strength.  关于握力测力法的研究对于理解与年龄相关的肌肉力量下降至关重要。
