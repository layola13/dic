interinstitutionally: /ˌɪntərɪnstɪˈtjuːʃənəli/ | adv. | 机构之间地；跨机构地 |  Relating to or involving more than one institution.  This adverb highlights the collaborative or interconnected nature of actions or processes between different institutions.  |

例句：

The project requires interinstitutional cooperation to be successful.  = 这个项目需要机构间的合作才能成功。

Interinstitutionally shared resources improved efficiency across the board. = 机构间共享资源全面提高了效率。


近义词：cross-institutionally, collaboratively (in an institutional context), interagency

反义词：intrainstitutionally (within a single institution), independently


词性：副词
