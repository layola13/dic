nondefilements:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈfaɪlmənts/| n. |不染污，清净|The state of being free from defilement or corruption, especially in a moral or spiritual sense.  It often refers to the absence of negative mental states like greed, hatred, and delusion in Buddhist philosophy.|例句：The practice of meditation aims for nondefilements of the mind, leading to inner peace.  冥想练习旨在使心灵不受污染，从而获得内心的平静。


例句：Through diligent practice of ethical conduct, one can cultivate nondefilements and achieve spiritual liberation. 通过勤奋地实践道德行为，人们可以培养清净，并获得精神解脱。

近义词：purity, cleanliness, innocence, sanctity

反义词：defilement, corruption, contamination, pollution

词性：名词
