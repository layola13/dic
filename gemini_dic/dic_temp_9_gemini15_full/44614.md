crowdfunding:/ˈkraʊdfʌndɪŋ/| n. |众筹 |The practice of funding a project or venture by raising many small amounts of money from a large number of people, typically via the Internet.| 例句：Many startups rely on crowdfunding to secure initial funding. 许多初创企业依靠众筹来获得初始资金。

例句：The artist successfully used crowdfunding to fund his new album.  这位艺术家成功地利用众筹为他的新专辑筹集资金。


近义词：crowd-funding, collective funding | 反义词：self-funding, traditional financing
