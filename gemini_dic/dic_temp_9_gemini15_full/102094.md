isosceles: /aɪˈsɒsəliz/ | adj. | 等腰的 | Having two sides of equal length.  An isosceles triangle has two sides of equal length. | The isosceles triangle has two equal sides and two equal angles.  这个等腰三角形有两条相等的边和两个相等的角。


例句：

He drew an isosceles triangle on the blackboard. 他在黑板上画了一个等腰三角形。

That's an isosceles right-angled triangle. 那是等腰直角三角形。


近义词：equilateral (if referring to a triangle with all sides equal)


反义词：scalene (triangle with no sides equal)


词源:

From Late Latin *isosceles, from Greek ἰσόσκελης (isoskelēs), from ἴσος (isos, “equal”) + σκέλος (skelos, “leg”).  The term originally referred to having equal legs, a description that aptly applies to the sides of a triangle.


USAGE->

(Isosceles is an adjective, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like "What" in the example.  The following shows how it's used in different sentence structures.)


肯定句:

The triangle is isosceles. 这个三角形是等腰的。

This is an isosceles trapezoid. 这是一个等腰梯形。


疑问句:

Is the triangle isosceles? 这个三角形是等腰的吗？

Are all the sides of this shape isosceles? 这个形状的所有边都是等长的吗？（Note:  This usage is less common and implies equal sides, not necessarily an isosceles shape.)


否定句:

The triangle is not isosceles; it's scalene. 这个三角形不是等腰的，它是斜三角形。

That figure is clearly not isosceles.  那个图形显然不是等腰的。
