misnarrating:/ˌmɪsˈnæreɪtɪŋ/| v.|歪曲叙述；错误地叙述 |To give a false or misleading account of something.|例句：He was accused of misnarrating the events leading up to the accident. 他被指控歪曲了导致事故发生的事实。  例句：The media is often accused of misnarrating complex political issues. 媒体经常被指控歪曲复杂的政治问题。|近义词：distorting, misrepresenting, falsifying|反义词：accurately narrating, truthfully representing|动词
