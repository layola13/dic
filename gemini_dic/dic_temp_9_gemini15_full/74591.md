freaker:/ˈfriːkər/| n.|怪人，狂热爱好者|A freaker is a person who is extremely enthusiastic or obsessed with something; someone considered strange or eccentric.|例句：He's a real freaker when it comes to vintage cars. 他是一个真正的古董车狂热爱好者。|近义词：fanatic, enthusiast, maniac, freak|反义词：indifferent, apathetic|名词
