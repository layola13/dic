chlorination:/ˌklɔːrɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |氯化；加氯消毒|The process of adding chlorine to a substance, typically water, to disinfect it or to remove impurities.  Chlorination is a common method used to purify drinking water and swimming pool water. |例句：The water undergoes chlorination to kill harmful bacteria.  水经过氯化处理以杀死有害细菌。|例句：The municipality ensures regular chlorination of the public water supply. 市政部门确保定期对公共供水进行氯化处理。|近义词：disinfection, purification|反义词：dechlorination
