deflexion:/dɪˈflɛkʃən/ | n. |挠曲；偏斜；偏离；弯曲 | The bending or turning aside of something from its course or position.  It often refers to a physical deflection, but can also be used metaphorically to describe a change of direction or purpose. | Example: The deflexion of the light beam was caused by the prism. 光束的偏斜是由棱镜引起的。The unexpected deflexion in his career path surprised everyone. 他职业道路上的意外偏离让每个人都感到惊讶。 |近义词：deviation, deflection, divergence, bending |反义词：straightness, directness |名词
