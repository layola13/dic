shortfall:/ˈʃɔːrtfɔːl/|/ˈʃɔːrtfɔːl/| n. |不足，缺点；亏空 |A shortfall in funding has delayed the project.  资金不足导致项目延误。|There was a significant shortfall in the number of attendees. 出席人数严重不足。|deficiency, deficit, shortage |surplus, excess |名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company faces a significant shortfall in profits this quarter. = 这家公司本季度面临着严重的利润不足。
A shortfall in rainfall is causing a drought. = 降雨量不足导致干旱。


一般过去时:
Last year's harvest resulted in a large shortfall of grain. = 去年收成导致粮食严重短缺。
The project experienced a shortfall in its budget. = 该项目预算不足。


现在完成时:
We have experienced a shortfall in donations this year. = 今年我们的捐款不足。
There has been a shortfall in the supply of essential goods. = 重要商品供应不足。


过去完成时:
By the end of the month, we had a significant shortfall in our sales targets. = 到月底，我们的销售目标严重不足。
They had already anticipated a shortfall in resources before starting the project. = 他们在项目开始之前就已经预料到资源不足。


将来时:
We predict a shortfall in energy production next winter. = 我们预测明年冬天能源生产不足。
The company expects a shortfall in revenue next year. = 该公司预计明年收入不足。


虚拟语气:
If there were no shortfall in funding, the project would be completed on time. = 如果资金充足，项目就能按时完成。
I wish there weren't a shortfall in skilled workers. = 我希望不会缺少熟练工人。


被动语态:
The shortfall in production was caused by unexpected equipment failure. = 生产不足是由意外的设备故障造成的。
The budget shortfall was blamed on poor management. = 预算不足归咎于管理不善。


疑问句:
Is there a shortfall in the budget? = 预算不足吗？
What is the shortfall in this year's harvest? = 今年收成的不足是多少？


否定句:
There was no shortfall in the supply of water. = 水供应充足。
The company reported no shortfall in its profits. = 该公司报告利润充足。
