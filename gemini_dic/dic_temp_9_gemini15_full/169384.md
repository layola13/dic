proneness:/ˈproʊnəs/|/ˈprəʊnəs/| n. |倾向，癖性，易于…的特性|A predisposition or susceptibility to something, especially something undesirable.  It refers to the likelihood or tendency of something happening.|His proneness to accidents made his parents worry.  他的事故倾向让他的父母很担心。|  His proneness to exaggeration is well known. 他夸大的倾向是众所周知的。|likelihood, susceptibility, tendency, vulnerability|resistance, immunity, imperviousness|名词
