deferential:/ˌdɛfəˈrɛnʃəl/ | adj. |恭敬的；尊敬的；顺从的 | showing deference; respectful.  It implies a respectful submission to the opinions or wishes of another.  | 例句：He was deferential to his elders. 他对长辈很恭敬。

例句：She adopted a deferential manner towards her boss. 她对老板采取了恭敬的态度。


近义词：respectful, submissive, obedient, compliant, humble

反义词：disrespectful, arrogant, insolent, defiant, contemptuous


词源：From Latin *deferentialis, from deferre "to carry away, bear, defer, yield," from de- "away, off" + ferre "to carry, bear."  The sense of "yielding to" developed from the idea of "carrying away" someone's opinion or will.
