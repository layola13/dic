rereviewed:/ˌriːrɪˈvjuːd/| v. |再次审查；重新审核|The manuscript was rereviewed by a different editor.  这份手稿由另一位编辑重新审核。|近义词：reviewed again, re-examined |反义词：accepted (without further review)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rereview the data regularly. = 他们定期重新审查数据。
The committee rereviews the policy annually. = 委员会每年重新审核该政策。

一般过去时:
She rereviewed the contract before signing. = 她在签字之前重新审核了合同。
The professor rereviewed the student's paper. = 教授重新审查了学生的论文。

现在完成时:
They have rereviewed the evidence multiple times. = 他们已经多次重新审查了证据。
The court has rereviewed the case and upheld the original verdict. = 法院已重新审理此案并维持原判。

过去完成时:
He had rereviewed the document before submitting it. = 他在提交文件之前已经重新审核过了。
The team had rereviewed the project plan before the meeting. = 团队在会议之前已经重新审查了项目计划。

将来时:
They will rereview the budget next month. = 他们下个月将重新审查预算。
The board will rereview the proposal after further consideration. = 董事会在进一步考虑后将重新审查该提案。

虚拟语气:
If they had rereviewed the data, they might have reached a different conclusion. = 如果他们重新审查了数据，他们可能会得出不同的结论。
I wish they had rereviewed my application. = 我希望他们重新审查我的申请。

被动语态:
The report was rereviewed by an independent panel. = 这份报告由一个独立小组重新审查。
The decision will be rereviewed by the higher court. = 此决定将由上级法院重新审查。

疑问句:
Has the committee rereviewed the proposal yet? = 委员会是否已经重新审查了该提案？
Will the findings be rereviewed? = 这些调查结果将会被重新审查吗？

否定句:
They did not rereview the evidence thoroughly. = 他们没有彻底地重新审查证据。
The company has not rereviewed its safety procedures. = 公司尚未重新审查其安全程序。
