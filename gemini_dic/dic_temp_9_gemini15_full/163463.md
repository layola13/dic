precontinental:/ˌpriːkɒntɪˈnɛntl/| adj.| 前大陆的；大陆之前的 |  Relating to or denoting the period or a rock formation  preceding the formation of a continent.  It often refers to geological periods and formations. | Example: The precontinental crust is significantly different in composition from continental crust. 前大陆地壳的成分与大陆地壳显著不同。| 近义词：protocontinental | 反义词：postcontinental
