nonperception:/ˌnɒnpəˈsɛpʃən/| n. |非感知，无知觉|The lack of perception or awareness; failure to perceive something.  It often refers to a failure to notice or understand something important.|例句：His nonperception of the danger led to his injury.  他的无知觉导致了他受伤。

例句：The study showed a high rate of nonperception among participants regarding the subtle cues.  研究表明，参与者对细微线索的感知缺失率很高。


近义词：unawareness, insensibility, obliviousness|反义词：perception, awareness, cognizance|词性:名词
