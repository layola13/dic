flamingo:/fləˈmɪŋɡoʊ/| n. |火烈鸟|A flamingo is a type of wading bird with long legs and a long neck, known for its bright pink or reddish plumage.  They are found in tropical and subtropical regions around the world. |Example:  We saw a flock of flamingos wading in the shallow lagoon. 我们看到一群火烈鸟在浅泻湖里涉水。  The vibrant pink flamingo stood out against the blue water.鲜艳的粉色火烈鸟在蓝色的水面上格外醒目。|近义词：bird |反义词：无
