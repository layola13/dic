reanimating:/ˌriːˈænɪmeɪtɪŋ/| v. |使复苏；使复活；使恢复活力 |The process of restoring life or vitality to something that was lifeless or inactive.  It implies bringing something back from a state of near death or inactivity. |例句：The doctor was reanimating the patient after the car accident. = 医生在车祸后正在抢救病人。

例句：The rain reanimated the parched land. = 雨水使干旱的土地恢复了生机。


近义词：reviving, revitalizing, resuscitating, restoring


反义词：killing, destroying, extinguishing


词性：动词
