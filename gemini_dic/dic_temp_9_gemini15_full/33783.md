circumcise:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌsaɪz/|/ˈsɝːkəmˌsaɪz/| vt. |割包皮；行割礼|To cut off the foreskin of (a male).  This is a religious or cultural practice in some communities.  The procedure can be performed for religious, cultural, or medical reasons.|例句：Many Jewish boys are circumcised shortly after birth. 许多犹太男孩在出生后不久就接受了割礼。

例句：The doctor recommended circumcision to prevent future infections. 医生建议进行包皮环切术以预防未来的感染。


近义词：excise,  remove (foreskin) | 反义词：None  | 词性：动词
