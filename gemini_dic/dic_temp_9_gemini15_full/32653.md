architecture:/ˈɑːrkɪˌtɛktʃər/ | n. | 建筑学；建筑风格；建筑结构 | The art and science of designing and constructing buildings.  It encompasses various aspects, from planning and design to construction and materials.  例句：The architecture of the cathedral is breathtaking. 这座大教堂的建筑令人叹为观止。  例句：Modern architecture often incorporates sustainable design principles. 现代建筑通常融入可持续的设计理念。|近义词：building design, construction, design |反义词：destruction, demolition |名词
