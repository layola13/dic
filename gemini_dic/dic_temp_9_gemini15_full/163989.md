predigestion:/ˌpriːdaɪˈdʒɛstʃən/ | n. | 预消化；初步消化 | The process of partially digesting food before it enters the stomach or intestines.  This often involves breaking down complex molecules into simpler ones, making digestion easier.  | Example: The predigestion of food in the mouth through chewing and saliva action is crucial for efficient digestion.  预先在口腔中通过咀嚼和唾液作用对食物进行预消化，对于有效消化至关重要。 | 近义词：partial digestion, preliminary digestion | 反义词：postdigestion (Not a common word) | 名词
