nativistic:/næˈtɪvɪstɪk/ | adj. | 本土主义的；排外主义的 | Relating to or characteristic of nativism; advocating the protection of native inhabitants against immigrants.  Nativism is the political position of prioritizing the interests of native-born inhabitants over those of immigrants. This can manifest in various ways, from advocating for stricter immigration policies to expressing prejudice against immigrants. | Example: The rise of nativistic sentiment in the country led to increased hostility towards immigrants.  本土主义情绪在该国的兴起导致对移民的敌意加剧。 | 近义词：xenophobic, protectionist, insular | 反义词：cosmopolitan, internationalist
