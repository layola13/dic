desexualization: /ˌdiːˌsɛksʃuəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |去性化；去除性意味；淡化性吸引力 | The process of removing or reducing sexual characteristics or connotations.  It can refer to the removal of sexual elements from media, art, or other forms of expression, or the suppression of sexual expression in individuals or groups.  This can be done intentionally or unintentionally, and the motivations behind it can vary widely. | 例句：The desexualization of female characters in video games is a topic of ongoing debate.  电子游戏中女性角色的去性化是一个持续争论的话题。


The desexualization of advertising campaigns aimed at children is crucial for protecting their innocence.  针对儿童的广告宣传去性化对于保护他们的纯真至关重要。


近义词：neutralization, asexualization, de-eroticization | 反义词：sexualization, eroticization | 词性：名词


词源:  The word is formed from the prefix "de-" (meaning "to remove" or "to reverse"), the word "sexual" (relating to sex), and the suffix "-ization" (indicating a process or action).  Therefore, "desexualization" literally means the process of removing or reversing sexual characteristics.
