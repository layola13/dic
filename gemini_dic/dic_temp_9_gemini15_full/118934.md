meteorically:/ˌmiːtɪəˈrɒkəli/ | adv. | 迅速地；像流星一样地 |  characterized by extremely rapid increase or growth.  It implies a sudden and dramatic rise or increase, often unexpected.  The word derives from "meteor," referring to a celestial body that streaks across the sky. |例句：His career rose meteorically after the success of his first novel.  他的事业在他第一部小说成功后迅速崛起。

His company's value increased meteorically. 他的公司价值迅速增长。

近义词：rapidly, swiftly, dramatically, explosively | 反义词：slowly, gradually, steadily
