extrudable: /ɪkˈstruːdəbəl/ | adj. | 可挤出的，可压出的 | Capable of being extruded.  This adjective describes a material that can be shaped by being forced through a die, typically under pressure. | The plastic used in this product is highly extrudable, allowing for intricate designs.  这种产品使用的塑料具有很高的可挤出性，可以制作出复杂的图案。|  moldable, formable |  brittle, inflexible
