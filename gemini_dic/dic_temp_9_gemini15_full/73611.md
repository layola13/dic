forewarners:/fɔːˈwɔːrnərz/ | n. |预警者；预先警告的人 | People who give warnings in advance.  They may be individuals, organizations, or systems that provide advance notice of potential dangers, risks, or problems. | Example: The early forewarners of the impending storm saved many lives.  预警人员的及时预警挽救了许多生命。 | 近义词：warner, alarmist, prophet (in some contexts) | 反义词： none specifically, but antonyms could relate to those who conceal or ignore warnings.  |  词性:名词
