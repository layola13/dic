opted:/ɒptɪd/ | v. | 选择；决定 | To have chosen or made a decision.  |  She opted for the vegetarian option. 她选择了素食。 He opted to stay home instead of going to the party. 他选择待在家里而不是去参加派对。 | chose, selected, decided | refused, rejected | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He opts for the easier path. = 他选择更容易的路。
They opt to remain silent. = 他们选择保持沉默。


一般过去时:
I opted out of the competition. = 我退出比赛了。
She opted to study abroad. = 她选择出国留学。


现在完成时:
He has opted to retire early. = 他选择提前退休了。
They have opted for a different approach. = 他们选择了不同的方法。


过去完成时:
I had opted for the red dress before I saw the blue one. = 在我看到蓝色的裙子之前，我已经选择了红色的裙子。
She had opted out of the project before the deadline. = 截止日期之前，她已经退出了这个项目。


将来时:
I will opt for the train instead of flying. = 我将选择坐火车而不是坐飞机。
They will opt to wait and see. = 他们将选择等待观望。


虚拟语气:
If I had opted for a different career, I might be happier now. = 如果我选择不同的职业，我现在可能会更快乐。
I wish I had opted to go to the concert. = 我真希望我选择了去参加音乐会。


被动语态:
The blue option was opted for by most people. = 大多数人选择了蓝色选项。(被动语态使用较少)
A different strategy was opted for by the team. = 团队选择了不同的策略。(被动语态使用较少)


疑问句:
Did he opt for the job offer? = 他选择了这份工作吗？
Will you opt to stay or leave? = 你会选择留下还是离开？


否定句:
He did not opt for the surgery. = 他没有选择手术。
They opted not to participate in the study. = 他们选择不参加这项研究。
