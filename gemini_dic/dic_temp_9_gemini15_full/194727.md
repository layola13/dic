selfhood:/ˈsɛlfhʊd/ | n. |自我；自我意识；个性 | The state or quality of having a distinct identity;  the sense of being a separate, unique individual.  It encompasses one's self-awareness, self-concept, and the totality of one's being. |  例句：Developing a strong sense of selfhood is crucial for teenagers.  发展强烈的自我意识对青少年至关重要。


例句：The artist's selfhood is clearly expressed in her bold and unique style.  这位艺术家的自我意识清晰地体现在她大胆独特的风格中。


近义词：identity, individuality, self, personality | 反义词：anonymity, impersonality
