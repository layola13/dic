pressure-suits:/ˈprɛʃərsuːts/| n. |加压服|A pressure suit is a specialized garment worn to protect the wearer from the effects of low pressure, such as in high-altitude flight or space travel.  It maintains a breathable atmosphere around the body and protects against decompression sickness. |Example: Astronauts wear pressure suits during spacewalks to survive in the vacuum of space. 宇航员在太空行走时穿着加压服，以在太空真空环境中生存。  The pilot donned his pressure suit before the high-altitude flight.  飞行员在高空飞行前穿上了他的加压服。|近义词：space suit, pressure garment|反义词：None
