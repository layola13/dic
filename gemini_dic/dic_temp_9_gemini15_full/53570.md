disbelieves:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːvz/ | v. |不相信；怀疑 | To refuse to believe something; to doubt the truth of something. |例句：He disbelieves the rumors circulating about the company's financial troubles. 他不相信关于公司财务困境的传闻。

例句：Many people disbelieved the scientist's claims until they were proven with evidence.  许多人都不相信这位科学家的说法，直到这些说法被证据证实。


近义词：doubts, questions, distrusts, suspects,  incredulous

反义词：believes, accepts, trusts


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disbelieves everything he reads online. = 他不相信他在网上读到的任何东西。
She disbelieves the politician's promises. = 她不相信这位政治家的承诺。


一般过去时:
They disbelieved the witness's testimony. = 他们不相信证人的证词。
I disbelieved his explanation for being late. = 我不相信他迟到的解释。


现在完成时:
I have disbelieved his stories for years. = 多年来我一直不相信他的故事。
She has disbelieved him since the incident. = 自从那件事发生后，她就一直不相信他。


过去完成时:
He had disbelieved her until he saw the evidence. = 直到他看到证据，他才不相信她。
We had disbelieved the rumors before the truth emerged. = 在真相大白之前，我们都不相信那些谣言。


将来时:
I will disbelieve any claims without proof. = 我不会相信任何没有证据的主张。
They will disbelieve his excuses next time. = 下次他们不会相信他的借口。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't seen it with my own eyes, I would have disbelieved it. = 如果我没有亲眼所见，我不会相信它。
I wish I disbelieved the gossip, but I can't. = 我希望我不相信这些闲话，但我做不到。


被动语态:
His claims were disbelieved by many. = 许多人不相信他的说法。
The story was disbelieved by the public. = 公众不相信这个故事。


疑问句:
Do you disbelieve the news report? = 你不相信这个新闻报道吗？
Does she disbelieve his alibi? = 她不相信他的不在场证明吗？


否定句:
I don't disbelieve everything you say, but some things are hard to accept. = 我并非不相信你说的所有话，但有些事情很难接受。
He doesn't disbelieve her completely; he just needs more evidence. = 他并非完全不相信她；他只是需要更多证据。


PHRASE->
disbelieve in something:  对…不相信 (e.g., disbelieve in magic)
disbelieve someone: 不相信某人 (e.g., disbelieve a witness)
disbelieve a claim: 不相信某种说法 (e.g., disbelieve a politician's claim)
disbelieve wholeheartedly: 全盘否定 (e.g., disbelieve wholeheartedly the conspiracy theory)
disbelieve completely: 完全不相信 (e.g., disbelieve completely his excuse)
