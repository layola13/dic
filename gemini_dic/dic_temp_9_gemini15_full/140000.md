odysseys:/ˈɒdɪsiz/ /ˈɑːdəsiz/ | n. |史诗般的旅程；冒险；历险记 | A long and eventful or adventurous journey or experience.  |例句：Homer's *Odyssey* is a famous epic poem about a long journey home. 荷马的《奥德赛》是一部关于漫长回家旅程的著名史诗。

例句：Their backpacking trip across South America turned into an odyssey of unexpected challenges and rewarding discoveries. 他们在南美洲的背包旅行变成了一次充满意想不到的挑战和有益发现的历险记。


近义词：adventure, journey, expedition, quest, pilgrimage | 反义词：routine, sedentary existence
