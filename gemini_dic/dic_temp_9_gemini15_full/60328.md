egoisms: /ˈiːɡoʊɪzəm/ | n. | 自我中心主义；利己主义 | The excessive preoccupation with oneself and one's own interests.  It often manifests as a disregard for the feelings and needs of others. | Example: His egoism prevented him from seeing the impact of his actions on others.  他的自我中心主义使他看不到自己的行为对别人的影响。  His blatant egoism alienated his friends. 他公然的自私自利疏远了他的朋友。|近义词：narcissism, selfishness, self-centeredness, conceit |反义词：altruism, selflessness, empathy
