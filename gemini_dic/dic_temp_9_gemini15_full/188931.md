ruthlessness: /ˈruːθləsnəs/ | n. | 无情；冷酷；残忍 | The quality or state of being ruthless; a lack of pity or compassion.  It often implies a willingness to do whatever is necessary to achieve a goal, even if it involves harming others. | 例句：Her ruthlessness in business helped her build an empire.  她的商业上的无情帮助她建立了一个帝国。


例句：The ruthless dictator suppressed any opposition.  那位冷酷的独裁者压制了任何反对势力。


近义词：heartlessness, mercilessness, cruelty, callousness, pitilessness


反义词：compassion, mercy, kindness, gentleness, benevolence


词性：名词
