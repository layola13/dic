neophyte:/ˈniːəfaɪt/| n. |新手，初学者 | A neophyte is a person who is new to a subject, skill, or belief.  They are inexperienced and still learning.  | Example:  As a neophyte in the world of finance, she sought the advice of a seasoned investor.  作为金融界的新手，她寻求了一位经验丰富的投资者的建议。 |近义词：beginner, novice, tyro |反义词：expert, veteran, master |名词
