expunged:/ɪkˈspʌndʒd/| v. |删除；消除；涂掉 |To remove or delete something completely; to erase or obliterate.  |例句：The judge ordered the offending passages to be expunged from the record.  法官下令将有问题的段落从记录中删除。|例句：They tried to expunge the memory of that terrible day. 他们试图抹去那可怕的一天记忆。|近义词：erase, delete, remove, obliterate |反义词：add, insert, include


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He expunges sensitive information from the document. = 他从文件中删除敏感信息。
The government expunges the names of informants from the official record. = 政府从官方记录中删除告密者的姓名。


一般过去时:
The editor expunged several paragraphs from the manuscript. = 编辑从手稿中删除了几段。
She expunged the embarrassing details from her autobiography. = 她从自传中删除了令人尴尬的细节。


现在完成时:
The court has expunged his criminal record. = 法院已删除了他的犯罪记录。
They have expunged all traces of the scandal. = 他们已经抹去了所有丑闻的痕迹。


过去完成时:
The historian had expunged all references to the controversial figure. = 历史学家已经删除了所有关于有争议人物的参考资料。
Before the book was published, the author had expunged several chapters. = 在书出版之前，作者已经删除了好几章。


将来时:
The judge will expunge the evidence from the court record. = 法官将从法庭记录中删除证据。
The company will expunge all negative reviews from its website. = 公司将从其网站上删除所有负面评价。


虚拟语气:
If the court expunged the evidence, the case would be dismissed. = 如果法院删除证据，案件就会被驳回。
I wish they had expunged my name from that list. = 我希望他们能把我从那个名单上删除。


被动语态:
The offending passages were expunged from the manuscript. = 有问题的段落从手稿中被删除了。
His criminal record was expunged after five years. = 五年后，他的犯罪记录被删除了。


疑问句:
Will the judge expunge the evidence? = 法官会删除证据吗？
Have they expunged the sensitive information from the database? = 他们是否已从数据库中删除敏感信息？


否定句:
The government did not expunge the names of all the suspects. = 政府没有删除所有嫌疑人的姓名。
They failed to expunge all traces of the virus from the system. = 他们未能从系统中清除所有病毒痕迹。
