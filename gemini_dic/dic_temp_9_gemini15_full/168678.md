procrastinatingly:/prəˈkræstɪneɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 拖延地，磨蹭地 |  In a manner characterized by procrastination; delaying or postponing something. |例句：He procrastinatingly finished his assignment at the last minute. 他拖延到最后一刻才完成作业。
He worked procrastinatingly, leaving everything to the last minute. 他工作拖拖拉拉，把所有事情都留到最后一刻。|近义词：lazily, tardily, dilatorily |反义词：promptly, expeditiously, diligently
