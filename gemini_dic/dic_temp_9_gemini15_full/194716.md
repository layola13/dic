self-worshipping:/ˈsɛlf ˈwɜːʃɪpɪŋ/| adj.| 自我崇拜的；自恋的 |  Describes someone who excessively admires and praises themselves, often to the point of narcissism. It implies an inflated sense of self-importance and a lack of genuine humility. | Example:  His self-worshipping behavior alienated many of his former friends.  他的自我崇拜行为疏远了他许多以前的的朋友。|近义词：narcissistic, egotistical, self-centered, conceited |反义词：humble, modest, self-effacing
