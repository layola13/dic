hustlers:/ˈhʌslərz/| n. |骗子；努力工作的人；（尤指）街头小贩|A hustler is someone who is skilled at making money, often in a slightly dishonest or illegal way.  The term can also refer to someone who works very hard and is determined to succeed, even if it means taking risks.  In some contexts, it can specifically refer to a street vendor.|例句：He was a smooth-talking hustler who always seemed to find a way to make a profit. 他是个能说会道的骗子，总能找到赚钱的方法。

例句：Despite the long hours, the young hustlers were determined to build their business. 尽管工作时间很长，但这些年轻的奋斗者们决心要发展自己的事业。|近义词：con artist, scammer, go-getter, grinder, entrepreneur|反义词：slacker, idler, loafer|名词
