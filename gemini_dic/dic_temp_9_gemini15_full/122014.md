misstated:/ˌmɪsˈsteɪtɪd/ | adj. | 叙述错误的；说法错误的 |  Incorrectly stated or represented; wrongly expressed.  | The report misstated the company's financial position. 这份报告错误地陈述了公司的财务状况。 | inaccurate, incorrect, wrong | accurate, correct | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The witness misstates the facts.  目击者歪曲了事实。
He constantly misstates his qualifications. 他不断地夸大自己的资历。


一般过去时:
She misstated the amount of money she earned. 她错误地陈述了她赚取的金额。
The politician misstated his position on the issue.  这位政治家错误地陈述了他对这个问题的立场。


现在完成时:
They have misstated their intentions from the beginning.  他们从一开始就误报了他们的意图。
The company has misstated its profits for the last quarter. 公司夸大了上一季度的利润。


过去完成时:
He had misstated his age on his application. 他在申请表上谎报了年龄。
The newspaper had misstated the details of the accident. 报纸歪曲了事故的细节。


将来时:
They will misstate the facts to protect themselves. 他们会歪曲事实以保护自己。
She will likely misstate the figures again. 她很可能会再次错误地陈述数据。


虚拟语气:
If he had not misstated his experience, he would have got the job. 如果他没有夸大他的经验，他就能得到这份工作。
I wish I hadn't misstated my opinion. 我真希望我没有错误地表达我的意见。


被动语态:
The facts were misstated in the article. 这篇文章歪曲了事实。
The figures have been misstated in the financial report. 财务报告中的数字被错误地陈述了。


疑问句:
Were the figures misstated intentionally?  这些数字是故意错误陈述的吗？
Has the information been misstated in any way? 信息有没有被歪曲？


否定句:
The company did not misstate its earnings. 公司没有错误地陈述其收益。
He has not misstated his intentions. 他没有歪曲他的意图。
