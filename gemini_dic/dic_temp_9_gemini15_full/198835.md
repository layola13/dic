sidelines:/ˈsaɪdlaɪnz/| n.| 边线；场外；次要地位 | The sidelines refer to the area bordering a playing field, especially in sports.  It can also refer to a position of non-participation or a secondary role. | 例句：The coach watched the game from the sidelines.  教练在边线观看比赛。

The injured player was forced to watch from the sidelines. 受伤的球员被迫在边线观看比赛。| 近义词：outskirts, periphery, margins | 反义词：center, forefront, main event | 名词
