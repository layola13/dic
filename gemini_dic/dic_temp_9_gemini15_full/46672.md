dampeners:/ˈdæmpənərz/| n. |减震器；抑制因素；阻尼器 |A dampener is something that reduces or suppresses something, like enthusiasm, sound, or vibration. It can refer to a physical device or a more abstract influence.  |例句：The rain was a dampener on the picnic.  雨水让野餐扫兴了。

例句：The new regulations act as a dampener on economic growth. 新的法规抑制了经济增长。


近义词：suppressor, inhibitor, restrictor, muffler

反义词：stimulant, accelerator, enhancer

词性:名词
