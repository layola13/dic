epipubis:/ˌɛpɪˈpjuːbɪs/| n. |耻骨上部|The epipubis is a small bone or cartilage found in some mammals, located above the pubic bone.  It's part of the pelvic girdle and its function varies among species.  In some animals, it provides additional support for abdominal muscles. In humans, it's rudimentary or absent. |Example:  The study examined the variations in the epipubic bone across different marsupial species.  这项研究考察了不同有袋动物物种中耻骨上部骨骼的变化。|近义词：prepubic bone (in some contexts)|反义词：无
