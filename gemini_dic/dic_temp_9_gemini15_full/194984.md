semi-steel:/ˈsɛmiˌstiːl/| n.|半钢, 半钢制品|A material that is a composite of steel and other materials, typically having properties intermediate between those of steel and cast iron.  It often refers to a type of cast iron with improved mechanical properties compared to ordinary cast iron.|例句：The wheels were made of semi-steel for increased durability. 车轮由半钢制成，以提高耐用性。  The bridge components were constructed from high-strength semi-steel. 桥梁部件采用高强度半钢建造。|近义词：cast iron (in some contexts), ductile iron|反义词：steel, pure cast iron|名词
