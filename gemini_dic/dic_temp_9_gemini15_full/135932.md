nonpunishable: /ˌnɒnˈpʌnɪʃəbl/ | adj. | 不可惩罚的，免于惩罚的 | Not subject to punishment.  This adjective describes actions or offenses that cannot be legally punished.  | Example:  The judge ruled the minor's actions nonpunishable due to his age.  法官裁定未成年人的行为由于其年龄而不可惩罚。

The judge deemed the offense nonpunishable under the circumstances.  法官认为根据情况，该罪行不应受到惩罚。|近义词：unpunishable, exempt from punishment |反义词：punishable, liable to punishment
