reprints:/ˈriːprɪnts/| n.|重印本，再版|Copies of a book, article, or other printed material produced after the original printing.|例句：The journal will publish reprints of the most important articles.  期刊将出版最重要文章的重印本。|例句：He collected reprints of his favorite author's works. 他收集了他最喜欢的作者的作品的重印本。|近义词：reissues, reproductions|反义词：originals


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The publisher reprints the best-selling book every year. = 出版社每年都重印这本畅销书。
She reprints the article for her students. = 她为学生们重印这篇文章。

一般过去时:
They reprinted the newspaper article because of its popularity. = 由于这篇文章很受欢迎，他们重印了它。
The museum reprinted the old maps for visitors. = 博物馆为游客重印了旧地图。


现在完成时:
The company has reprinted the brochure several times this year. = 今年该公司已经重印了几次小册子。
He has reprinted his thesis for distribution. = 他已重印了他的论文以供分发。


过去完成时:
They had reprinted the book before the conference. = 他们在会议前已经重印了这本书。
The author had reprinted her short stories in a new collection. = 作者已将她的短篇故事收录在一个新的合集中并重印。


将来时:
We will reprint the magazine next month. = 我们下个月将重印杂志。
The university will reprint the textbook for the upcoming semester. = 大学将为即将到来的学期重印教科书。


虚拟语气:
If they had more money, they would reprint the rare book. = 如果他们有更多钱，他们就会重印这本珍稀书籍。
I wish they would reprint that out-of-print novel. = 我希望他们能重印那本绝版的长篇小说。


被动语态:
The article was reprinted in several newspapers. = 这篇文章在几家报纸上被重印。
The book is being reprinted due to high demand. = 由于需求量大，这本书正在被重印。


疑问句:
Will the magazine be reprinted? = 杂志会重印吗？
Have the posters been reprinted yet? = 海报重印了吗？


否定句:
The library didn't reprint the damaged book. = 图书馆没有重印损坏的书。
They won't reprint the article this year. = 他们今年不会重印这篇文章。
