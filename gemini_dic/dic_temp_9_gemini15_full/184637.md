restrictedly: /rɪˈstrɪktɪdli/ | adv. | 受限制地，严格地 | In a restricted manner; only under specific conditions or limitations.  | The information was released restrictedly to authorized personnel only.  信息仅限授权人员查看。 | narrowly, limitedly, selectively | freely, openly, liberally |  无


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It doesn't have tense.

Example sentences showing its usage in different sentence structures:

Declarative sentence:
The project was conducted restrictedly, following strict protocols. 项目是在严格遵守规程的条件下进行的。


Interrogative sentence:
Was the access to the data restrictedly controlled?  对数据的访问是否受到严格控制？


Exclamatory sentence:
Restrictedly! That's the only way we can ensure security!  严格限制！这是我们确保安全唯一的方法！


Negative sentence:
The information was not released restrictedly; it was made public. 信息并非严格保密；而是公开的。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "restrictedly".  Its usage is generally standalone.  Phrases would involve the words it modifies,  like "restrictedly available information".
