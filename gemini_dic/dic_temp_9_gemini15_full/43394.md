crankiest:/ˈkræŋkiːst/| adj.|最脾气暴躁的，最古怪的 | Most irritable or eccentric.  The word describes someone who is easily annoyed, bad-tempered, or eccentric. It is the superlative form of "cranky". |例句：He's been the crankiest person I've ever met. 他是我见过的最脾气暴躁的人。

例句：The old car was crankiest in cold weather. 这辆旧车在寒冷的天气里最难启动。


近义词：irritable, grumpy, crotchety, ill-tempered, cantankerous, testy


反义词：pleasant, cheerful, good-natured, amiable


词性：形容词


词源：The word "cranky" ultimately derives from the Middle English word "cranke," meaning "sick," or "ailing." This likely refers to a physical ailment that causes irritability. Over time, the meaning shifted to emphasize the behavioral aspect of irritability and eccentricity rather than its physical cause.  The "-est" suffix indicates the superlative degree.
