recultivating: /ˌriːˈkʌltɪveɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 再耕作；重新培养；复垦 | The act of cultivating again; restoring land to cultivation after it has been abandoned or damaged.  It implies a process of renewal and improvement. |  例句：The farmers are recultivating the exhausted fields. 农民们正在重新耕作那些贫瘠的土地。

例句：The organization is recultivating the abandoned mine site, transforming it into a park. 该组织正在对废弃的矿场进行复垦，将其改造成一个公园。


近义词：reclaim, rehabilitate, restore, regenerate | 反义词：abandon, deplete, destroy | 词性：动词

We are recultivating the old orchard. = 我们正在重新种植老果园。
