exert:/ɪɡˈzɜːrt/ | vt. |运用；施加；尽力 | To make a great effort; to use your power, influence, etc. to affect someone or something. |例句：She exerted all her strength to lift the box. 她使出全身力气抬起箱子。 |例句：The government exerted pressure on the company to change its policy. 政府向公司施压，要求其改变政策。|近义词：apply, employ, use,  |反义词：relax, neglect, forbear


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exerts his influence to help others. = 他运用他的影响力去帮助他人。
They exert pressure on the government. = 他们向政府施压。

一般过去时:
She exerted herself to finish the project on time. = 她努力按时完成项目。
The team exerted considerable effort to win the game. = 球队为了赢得比赛付出了巨大的努力。

现在完成时:
He has exerted himself beyond his limits. = 他已经竭尽全力。
The company has exerted its power to dominate the market. = 这家公司已经运用其力量主导市场。

过去完成时:
They had exerted all their energy before they collapsed. = 他们精疲力尽之前已经用尽了全部精力。
She had exerted considerable influence before she retired. = 她退休前已经施加了相当大的影响力。

将来时:
He will exert all his strength to achieve his goal. = 他将竭尽全力实现他的目标。
The company will exert more pressure on its suppliers. = 公司将对其供应商施加更大的压力。

虚拟语气:
If he exerted himself more, he would succeed. = 如果他更加努力，他就会成功。
I wish I had exerted more effort in my studies. = 我希望我在学习中更加努力。

被动语态:
Pressure was exerted on him to resign. = 他被迫辞职。
Influence was exerted by the media to change public opinion. = 媒体施加影响力来改变公众舆论。

疑问句:
How much effort will you exert? = 你将付出多少努力？
What kind of pressure is being exerted on them? = 他们正在承受什么样的压力？

否定句:
He didn't exert himself enough. = 他没有足够努力。
They didn't exert any control over the situation. = 他们对局势没有任何控制。


PHRASE->

exert control = 行使控制
exert force = 运用力量
