nuisancey:/ˈnuːsənsɪ/| adj.|讨厌的，令人讨厌的，烦人的|  Annoying or troublesome; causing inconvenience or annoyance.  The word is informal and often implies a mildly irritating quality rather than something seriously disruptive. |例句：That constant dripping faucet is so nuisancey!  那个不断滴水的龙头真烦人！

例句：The neighbor's loud music was nuisancey. 邻居的大声音乐很烦人。


近义词：annoying, bothersome, irritating, pesky, troublesome

反义词：pleasant, agreeable, delightful

词性：形容词
