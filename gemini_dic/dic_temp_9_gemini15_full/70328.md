fielded:/ˈfiːldɪd/| v.|处理，接球，布置（军队）|to handle (a question, problem, etc.); to catch (a ball) in a game; to arrange (troops) in the field|例句：The customer service representative fielded all the complaints patiently.  客服代表耐心地处理了所有投诉。

例句：The shortstop fielded the grounder cleanly.  游击手干净利落地接住了滚地球。


近义词：handled, caught, addressed, deployed|反义词：ignored, neglected, overlooked

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fields questions from the audience every week. = 他每周都处理来自观众的问题。
She expertly fields difficult interview questions. = 她熟练地应对了棘手的采访问题。

一般过去时:
The team fielded a strong defense last night. = 球队昨晚的防守很强硬。
The police fielded numerous calls about the incident. = 警方接到了许多关于此事件的电话。

现在完成时:
They have fielded hundreds of calls since the announcement. = 自从公告发布以来，他们已经接到了数百个电话。
She has fielded many challenges in her career. = 她在职业生涯中克服了许多挑战。

过去完成时:
The manager had fielded similar complaints before. = 经理以前处理过类似的投诉。
The team had fielded a weaker lineup in the previous game.  球队在上一场比赛中使用了较弱的阵容。

将来时:
He will field questions from the press after the meeting. = 会后他将回答记者的提问。
The company will field a team of experts to investigate. = 公司将派出一支专家团队进行调查。

虚拟语气:
If I were to field this question, I would say... = 如果我要回答这个问题，我会说……
If she had fielded the ball differently, they might have won. = 如果她以不同的方式接球，他们可能就赢了。

被动语态:
The questions were fielded by the panel of experts. = 这些问题由专家小组处理。
The complaints have been fielded efficiently. = 投诉得到了有效的处理。

疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't field the question properly. = 他没有正确地回答这个问题。
