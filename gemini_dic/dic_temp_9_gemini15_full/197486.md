sheets:/ʃiːts/ | n. |床单；薄片；纸张；表格 | A sheet is a large rectangular piece of cloth used to cover a bed.  It can also refer to a single layer or a thin flat piece of material, like a sheet of paper or a sheet of ice. |例句：I need to change the bed sheets. = 我需要换床单。

例句：The ice skater glided across the sheet of ice. = 滑冰运动员在冰面上滑行。


近义词：blanket (for bed covering), layer, lamina | 反义词：None specifically, depends on context.


词源：源自古英语 *scīte,  意为“片状物，薄片”。
