peeled:/piːld/| v. |剥皮，削皮；剥落；|to remove the skin or outer covering of something, especially fruit or vegetables.  |例句：She peeled the potatoes before boiling them. 她把土豆削皮后再煮。|例句：The sun had peeled the paint from the old house. 太阳把旧房子的油漆晒脱落了。|近义词：skinned, pared, shucked |反义词：applied, added


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He peels an orange every morning. = 他每天早上剥一个橙子。
She peels the potatoes for dinner. = 她为晚餐削土豆皮。


一般过去时:
I peeled the apples yesterday. = 我昨天削了苹果皮。
He peeled the banana before eating it. = 他吃香蕉前先剥了皮。


现在完成时:
She has peeled all the carrots. = 她已经把所有胡萝卜都削皮了。
They have peeled and chopped the onions. = 他们已经剥好并切碎洋葱了。


过去完成时:
I had peeled the garlic before adding it to the sauce. = 我在把蒜加到酱汁里之前已经剥好了蒜。
She had peeled the oranges before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经剥好了橙子。


将来时:
I will peel the potatoes later. = 我一会儿再削土豆皮。
She will peel the fruit for the salad. = 她会为沙拉准备水果并削皮。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would peel all the apples. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把所有苹果都削皮。
I wish I had peeled the potatoes earlier. = 我真希望我早点把土豆削皮。


被动语态:
The potatoes were peeled by my mother. = 土豆是我妈妈削的皮。
The oranges are being peeled by the chef. = 橙子正在由厨师削皮。


疑问句:
Have you peeled the oranges yet? = 你把橙子削皮了吗？
Did he peel the bananas himself? = 他是自己削香蕉皮的吗？


否定句:
I didn't peel the onions. = 我没有削洋葱皮。
She hasn't peeled the potatoes yet. = 她还没削土豆皮。
