self-loving: /ˈsɛlfˈlʌvɪŋ/ | adj. | 自恋的；自我中心的 |  Self-loving describes someone who has an excessive love or admiration for themselves, often to the point of narcissism.  It implies a preoccupation with oneself and one's own needs and desires, sometimes at the expense of others. |


例句：

His self-loving behavior alienated many of his friends.  他的自恋行为疏远了他的许多朋友。

She was criticized for her self-loving attitude and lack of empathy. 她因为自恋的态度和缺乏同情心而受到批评。


近义词：narcissistic, egotistical, self-centered, self-absorbed, vain


反义词：selfless, altruistic, humble, modest


词性：形容词
