pedograph:/ˈpɛdəɡræf/| n. |足迹图；足部描记器|A pedograph is a device used to record the plantar pressure distribution of a foot. It can also refer to the image or recording produced by such a device.  A pedograph provides valuable information for diagnosing and treating foot problems.|例句：The podiatrist used a pedograph to assess the patient's foot pressure distribution. 足病医生使用足迹图来评估病人的足底压力分布。The pedograph showed significant pressure on the heel. 足迹图显示脚后跟有明显的压力。|近义词：footprint, plantar pressure map|反义词：无
