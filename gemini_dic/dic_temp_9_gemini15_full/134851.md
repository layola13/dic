nonmodernistic: /ˌnɑːnˌmɑːdərnɪˈstɪk/ | adj. |非现代主义的，反现代主义的 | Not characteristic of or conforming to modernism; exhibiting qualities of styles predating modernism.  It implies a rejection or avoidance of modernist principles in art, architecture, design, or other fields. | Example: The building's nonmodernistic design harkened back to classical styles. 这座建筑物的非现代主义设计让人联想起古典风格。 | 近义词: traditional, classical, conventional, premodern | 反义词: modernistic, modernist, avant-garde
