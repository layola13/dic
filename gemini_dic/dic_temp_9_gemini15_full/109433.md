leopardwood:/ˈlepərdˌwʊd/| n. |豹纹木|A type of wood, usually from trees of the genus *Dalbergia*, characterized by its distinctive dark streaks against a lighter background, resembling a leopard's spots.  It is often used in high-end furniture and musical instruments.|例句：The inlaid leopardwood on the guitar gave it a luxurious look. 这把吉他的豹纹木镶嵌使其外观奢华。

The table is made of beautiful leopardwood. 这张桌子是用美丽的豹纹木制成的。|近义词：zebrawood,  (depending on the specific species) rosewood, cocobolo|反义词：无
