disbanding:/dɪsˈbændɪŋ/| v. |解散，解体|The act of formally dissolving an organization, group, or assembly.  It implies a planned and usually official end. |例句：The army is disbanding after the war.  战争结束后，军队将解散。

例句：The band decided to disband after their final concert. 乐队决定在最后一场演唱会后解散。


近义词：dissolving, breaking up, scattering, dispersing

反义词：forming, assembling, uniting, consolidating

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is disbanding the committee. 政府正在解散委员会。
They disband the club every year after the school year ends. 他们每年学年结束后都会解散俱乐部。


一般过去时:
The company disbanded last year due to financial difficulties.  由于财务困难，该公司去年解散了。
The protesters disbanded peacefully after their demonstration.  抗议者在示威后和平地散去了。


现在完成时:
The project team has disbanded after successfully completing the project. 项目团队在成功完成项目后解散了。
The rebel group has disbanded, leaving behind a legacy of violence. 反叛组织已解散，留下了暴力遗产。


过去完成时:
The band had disbanded before I had a chance to see them perform live.  在我有机会看到他们现场表演之前，乐队就已经解散了。
The army had disbanded before the peace treaty was signed.  在签署和平条约之前，军队就已经解散了。


将来时:
The parliament will disband after the next election.  议会将在下次选举后解散。
The organization will disband once its goals have been achieved.  一旦实现其目标，该组织将解散。


虚拟语气:
If the team had performed better, they wouldn't have disbanded. 如果团队表现更好，他们就不会解散了。
I wish they hadn't disbanded the club so quickly. 我真希望他们不要这么快解散俱乐部。


被动语态:
The club was disbanded by the university.  俱乐部被大学解散了。
The committee is being disbanded due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，委员会正在被解散。


疑问句:
Will the team be disbanding soon?  球队很快就会解散吗？
Has the group disbanded yet?  该组织已经解散了吗？


否定句:
The group did not disband despite the internal conflicts.  尽管内部冲突不断，该团体并没有解散。
The company hasn't disbanded yet, but it's expected soon.  公司还没有解散，但预计很快就会解散。
