educate:/ˈɛdʒʊkeɪt/| vt.|教育，培养，训练|To provide someone with knowledge or skill, often through teaching.|例句：The school aims to educate children to become responsible citizens. 学校旨在教育孩子们成为负责任的公民。|例句：We need to educate the public about the dangers of smoking. 我们需要教育公众吸烟的危害。|近义词：instruct, train, teach|反义词：misinform, mislead


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They educate their children at home. = 他们在家教育孩子。
The school educates students from diverse backgrounds. = 这所学校教育来自不同背景的学生。

一般过去时:
She educated herself through online courses. = 她通过在线课程自学成才。
My parents educated me to be respectful. = 我的父母教育我要尊重他人。

现在完成时:
The government has educated the public on the importance of hygiene. = 政府已经教育公众卫生的重要性。
They have educated themselves on the topic of climate change. = 他们已经学习了气候变化的知识。

过去完成时:
He had educated himself in law before becoming a judge. = 他在成为法官之前已经自学法律。
She had educated many students before retiring. = 她退休前教育过许多学生。

将来时:
The program will educate thousands of people about new technology. = 该项目将教育成千上万的人了解新技术。
We will educate our children about the importance of saving money. = 我们将教育我们的孩子储蓄的重要性。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would educate myself in astronomy. = 如果我有更多时间，我会学习天文学。
It's important that parents educate their children about the dangers of drugs. = 父母教育孩子远离毒品的危害很重要。

被动语态:
Children are educated in various ways. = 孩子们通过各种方式接受教育。
The public is being educated about the new health guidelines. = 公众正在接受新的健康指南的教育。

疑问句:
How do you educate your children? = 你如何教育你的孩子？
What methods do you use to educate your students? = 你用什么方法教育你的学生？

否定句:
They did not educate their children properly. = 他们没有正确地教育他们的孩子。
The program failed to educate the target audience. = 该项目未能教育目标受众。
