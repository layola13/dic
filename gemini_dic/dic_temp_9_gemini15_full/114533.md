malamute: /ˈmæləmjuːt/ | n. |阿拉斯加雪橇犬 | A large, powerful breed of working dog originating in Alaska, known for its strength, endurance, and thick double coat.  Malamutes are used for sledding and as companion animals. | Example:  The malamute pulled the sled effortlessly through the deep snow. 阿拉斯加雪橇犬毫不费力地拉着雪橇穿过厚厚的积雪。  | 近义词: sled dog, husky (though huskies are generally smaller) | 反义词:  N/A (no direct antonym) | 名词
