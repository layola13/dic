fractioned: /ˈfrækʃənd/ | v. | 分馏；分离；分割 | To separate (a substance) into its constituent parts, especially by distillation.  | 例句：The crude oil was fractioned into gasoline, kerosene, and other products.  原油被分馏成汽油、煤油和其他产品。

The mixture was carefully fractioned to obtain pure components.混合物被仔细分馏以获得纯组分。


近义词：separated, divided, distilled, refined | 反义词：combined, unified, merged | 词性：动词
