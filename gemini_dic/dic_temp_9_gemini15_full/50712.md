dermoskeleton:/ˌdɜːrməʊˈskɛlətən/| n. |皮肤骨骼，皮骨骼|A dermoskeleton is an external skeleton that is part of or is derived from the skin.  It contrasts with endoskeletons, which are internal. Many invertebrates possess dermoskeletons, providing protection and support. Examples include the shells of mollusks and the carapaces of crustaceans.|The turtle's dermoskeleton provides excellent protection from predators.|近义词：exoskeleton|反义词：endoskeleton
