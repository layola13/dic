frejol:/freˈxɔl/| n. |豆子，菜豆|Kidney bean; a type of bean, often small and dark-colored.  It is commonly used in Latin American cuisine.| 例句：We had frejoles for dinner last night. = 昨晚我们吃了豆子。

例句：The recipe calls for a cup of cooked frejoles. = 这个食谱需要一杯煮熟的豆子。


近义词：bean, kidney bean, pinto bean (depending on the specific type of frejol)|反义词：无|词性：名词
