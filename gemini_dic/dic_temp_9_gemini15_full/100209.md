interring: /ɪnˈtɜːrɪŋ/ | v. | 埋葬；安葬 | The act of placing a deceased person or their remains in a grave or tomb.  |例句：They are interring the body later today.  他们今天晚些时候将下葬遗体。

例句：The family decided to inter their loved one in the family plot.  家人决定将他们所爱的人安葬在家族墓地。


近义词：burying, entombment, inhuming | 反义词：exhuming, unearthing


USAGE->

They inter their loved ones in the cemetery. = 他们在墓地安葬他们的亲人。
He interrs his ancestors' remains respectfully. = 他恭敬地安葬祖先的遗骸。
