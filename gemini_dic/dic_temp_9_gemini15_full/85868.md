headaches:/ˈhedˌeɪks/| n.|头痛|A headache is a pain in the head.  Headaches can range from mild to severe and have various causes. | I have a terrible headache. 我头痛得厉害。  |She suffers from frequent headaches.|她经常头痛。|migraine, pain|relief from pain.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I often get headaches when I'm stressed. = 我压力大的时候经常头痛。
He usually takes aspirin for his headaches. = 他通常服用阿司匹林来缓解头痛。


一般过去时:
I had a really bad headache yesterday. = 我昨天头痛得很厉害。
She took some medicine because she had a headache. = 她因为头痛吃了一些药。


现在完成时:
I have had headaches all day. = 我一整天都头痛。
She has had persistent headaches for weeks. = 她几周来一直持续头痛。


过去完成时:
I had already taken some painkillers before the headache got worse. = 头痛加剧之前我已经服用了一些止痛药了。
He had had several headaches before he decided to see a doctor. = 他头痛了好几次才决定去看医生。


将来时:
I think I will have a headache after all that noise. = 我想在听了那么多噪音之后我会头痛。
She anticipates she will have a headache tomorrow after her long flight. = 她预计在明天长途飞行后会头痛。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't have so much stress, I wouldn't get so many headaches. = 如果我没有那么大的压力，我就不会这么频繁地头痛。
I wish I didn't have this throbbing headache. = 我真希望我不头痛。


被动语态:
Headaches are often caused by stress. = 头痛往往是由压力引起的。
His headaches were treated with medication. = 他的头痛是用药物治疗的。


疑问句:
Do you often get headaches? = 你经常头痛吗？
What causes your headaches? = 你的头痛是什么原因引起的？


否定句:
I don't have a headache right now. = 我现在不头痛。
He doesn't get headaches very often. = 他不经常头痛。
