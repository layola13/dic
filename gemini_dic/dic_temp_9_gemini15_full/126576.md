naffest:/ˈnæfɪst/| adj.|最差的，最劣质的，最俗气的|The superlative form of "naff," meaning extremely bad, tasteless, or unfashionable.  It expresses a strong degree of disapproval.|例句：That outfit is the naffest thing I've ever seen! 那套衣服是我见过的最俗气的！

例句：His attempt at stand-up comedy was the naffest performance of the night. 他的单口喜剧表演是当晚最糟糕的演出。


近义词：awful, terrible, dreadful, tacky, cheesy, rubbish,  crap


反义词：amazing, fantastic, superb, stylish, chic
