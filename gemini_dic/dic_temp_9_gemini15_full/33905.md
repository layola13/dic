circumventing: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈvɛntɪŋ/ | v. |规避，绕过，避开 | To avoid or get around something, especially a problem or rule.  | 例句：They found a way of circumventing the law. 他们找到了规避法律的方法。 | 例句：The company tried to circumvent the environmental regulations.  这家公司试图规避环境法规。| 近义词：avoid, bypass, evade, outmaneuver | 反义词：confront, face, encounter | 动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
They circumvent the problem by using a different approach. = 他们用不同的方法规避了这个问题。
We circumvent obstacles with careful planning. = 我们通过仔细的计划绕过了障碍。

一般过去时:
She circumvented the traffic jam by taking a detour. = 她绕道避开了交通堵塞。
The thief circumvented the security system. = 小偷绕过了安全系统。

现在完成时:
They have circumvented many challenges throughout the project. = 在整个项目中，他们已经克服了许多挑战。
We have successfully circumvented the initial difficulties. = 我们已经成功地克服了最初的困难。

过去完成时:
He had circumvented the rules before anyone noticed. = 在任何人注意到之前，他已经违反了规定。
They had circumvented the issue by delaying the decision. = 他们通过推迟决定来回避了这个问题。

将来时:
We will circumvent the issue by seeking alternative solutions. = 我们将通过寻求替代方案来规避这个问题。
They will circumvent the road closure by using a different route. = 他们将使用不同的路线来绕过道路封闭。

虚拟语气:
If we had circumvented the problem earlier, we would have saved time. = 如果我们早点解决这个问题，我们会节省时间。
I wish I had circumvented the risks before investing. = 我希望在投资之前我已经规避了风险。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
