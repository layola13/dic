overwash:/ˈoʊvərˌwɑːʃ/| vt. n.|冲刷；溢流；海浪冲刷|To wash over or beyond a usual limit; the action of waves washing over something.|例句：The high tide overwashed the beach. 高潮冲刷了海滩。The storm overwashed the seawall. 暴风雨冲垮了海堤。|近义词： inundate, flood, wash away |反义词： dry, drain


USAGE->

The river overwashes its banks during heavy rain. = 大雨时，河水会漫过河岸。
