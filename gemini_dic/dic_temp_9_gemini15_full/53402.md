disallows:/dɪsˈælaʊz/| vt.|不允许，禁止|To refuse to allow or permit something.  It is the third-person singular present tense form of the verb "disallow".|The rules disallow the use of calculators during the exam.  规则不允许考试期间使用计算器。

The referee disallowed the goal because of a foul.  裁判因为犯规而取消了进球。|allows, permits|allows, permits|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disallows smoking in his office. 他不允许在他的办公室吸烟。
The school disallows cell phones during class. 学校不允许上课期间使用手机。

一般过去时:
The judge disallowed the evidence.  法官驳回了证据。
They disallowed my application. 他们拒绝了我的申请。

现在完成时:
The committee has disallowed the proposed changes. 委员会已经否决了提议的更改。
She has disallowed any further discussion on the matter. 她已经不允许对此事进行进一步讨论。

过去完成时:
He had disallowed the use of outside materials before the test. 他在考试前就禁止使用外部资料。
They had disallowed any form of protest. 他们之前已经禁止任何形式的抗议。

将来时:
The new regulations will disallow the import of certain goods. 新规将禁止进口某些商品。
He will disallow any attempt to cheat. 他将不允许任何作弊企图。


虚拟语气:
If the rules disallowed it, we wouldn't do it. 如果规则不允许，我们不会这样做。
I wish the government disallowed this practice. 我希望政府禁止这种做法。

被动语态:
The use of drones was disallowed by the authorities.  无人机的使用被当局禁止了。
His application was disallowed due to incomplete paperwork. 他的申请由于文件不完整而被拒绝了。


疑问句:

Does the rule disallow outside food? 规则是否不允许携带外来食物？

否定句:
The policy doesn't disallow personal opinions. 这项政策并不禁止个人意见。
They didn't disallow the use of dictionaries. 他们没有禁止使用字典。
