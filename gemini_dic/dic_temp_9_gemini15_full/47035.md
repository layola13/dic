data-processing:/ˈdeɪtə ˈprɑːsesɪŋ/ | n. |数据处理 |The process of collecting, manipulating, storing, retrieving, and presenting data.  This involves various techniques and technologies to transform raw data into meaningful information.  |Example:  The company uses advanced data-processing techniques to analyze customer preferences.  这家公司使用先进的数据处理技术来分析客户偏好。|近义词：data manipulation, information processing |反义词：data destruction, data loss|名词
