impounded:/ɪmˈpaʊndɪd/ | v. | 扣押；没收；（水）被围困 | To seize and hold in legal custody.  This often refers to property, but can also apply to animals or even water. | The police impounded his car after the accident.  警方在事故后扣押了他的汽车。  |The stray dog was impounded by animal control.  这只流浪狗被动物控制部门扣押了。 |confiscate, seize, detain | release, free | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The police impound vehicles involved in crimes. = 警方扣押涉及犯罪的车辆。
They impound stray animals at the shelter. = 他们把流浪动物扣押在收容所。


一般过去时:
The authorities impounded the illegal drugs. = 当局没收了非法毒品。
Customs officials impounded the shipment of counterfeit goods. = 海关官员扣押了这批假货。


现在完成时:
The government has impounded several properties suspected of being used for illegal activities. = 政府已经扣押了几处涉嫌用于非法活动的房产。
The city has impounded numerous abandoned vehicles this year. = 今年市政府已经扣押了许多被遗弃的车辆。


过去完成时:
The judge had impounded the evidence before the trial began. = 审判开始前，法官已经扣押了证据。
The police had impounded the suspect's car prior to the investigation. = 在调查之前，警方已经扣押了嫌疑人的汽车。


将来时:
The court will impound the assets of the convicted criminal. = 法院将没收被判有罪的罪犯的资产。
They will impound the contaminated water to prevent further spread. = 他们将把受污染的水封存起来，以防止进一步蔓延。


虚拟语气:
If they had impounded the faulty product earlier, they could have prevented further damage. = 如果他们早些扣押有缺陷的产品，他们就能防止进一步的损害。
If the court impounded the documents, the case would be significantly weakened. = 如果法院扣押了这些文件，案件就会大大减弱。


被动语态:
His car was impounded for speeding. = 他的车因为超速被扣押了。
The illegal goods were impounded by customs. = 非法货物被海关扣押了。


疑问句:
Was the car impounded due to a parking violation? = 这辆车是因为违章停车而被扣押的吗？
Will the seized money be impounded by the government? = 政府会没收这笔被查获的钱吗？


否定句:
The police did not impound the vehicle because there was insufficient evidence. = 由于证据不足，警方没有扣押车辆。
They did not impound the suspect's phone, despite having a warrant. = 尽管有搜查令，他们也没有扣押嫌疑人的手机。


PHRASE->
impound a car = 扣押一辆车
impound evidence = 扣押证据
impound assets = 没收资产
impound water = 拦截水流 (如因水坝或其他障碍物)
impound funds = 扣留资金
