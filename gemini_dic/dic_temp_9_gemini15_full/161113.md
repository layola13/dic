postencephalon: /ˌpoʊstɛnˈsɛfəlɒn/ | n. | 后脑 | The hindbrain; the part of the brain that develops from the posterior part of the neural tube.  It includes the metencephalon (pons and cerebellum) and myelencephalon (medulla oblongata). | Example:  The postencephalon plays a crucial role in coordinating movement and maintaining vital functions. 后脑在协调运动和维持重要功能方面起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: hindbrain | 反义词: prosencephalon
