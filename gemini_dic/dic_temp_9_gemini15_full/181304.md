reinterpreting: /ˌriːɪnˈtɜːrpətɪŋ/ | v. |重新解释；重新诠释 | To interpret something again or in a different way.  |例句：The data was misinterpreted, requiring reinterpreting the results. 数据被误解了，需要重新解释结果。

例句：He was reinterpreting the classic novel for a modern audience. 他正在为现代观众重新诠释这部经典小说。


近义词：re-examining, re-evaluating, re-analyzing | 反义词：misinterpreting, misunderstanding | 词性：动词
