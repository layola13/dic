feel-good:/fiːl ɡʊd/| adj.|令人愉快的，令人感到美好的|Evoking feelings of happiness and well-being; pleasant and enjoyable.  It often describes things that make you feel positive and optimistic.|例句：The movie had a feel-good ending. 这部电影有一个令人愉快的结局。

例句：That feel-good song always lifts my spirits. 那首令人愉快的歌曲总是能振奋我的精神。


近义词：uplifting, cheerful, positive, pleasant, enjoyable

反义词：depressing, gloomy, disheartening, negative, unpleasant
