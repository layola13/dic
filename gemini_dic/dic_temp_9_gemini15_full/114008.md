maglev:/ˈmæɡlev/| n. |磁悬浮(列车)|Magnetic levitation, often shortened to maglev, is a system of transportation that uses magnetic fields to lift and propel vehicles, allowing for very high speeds and smooth travel.  Maglev trains are a prime example of this technology.|The Shanghai Maglev Train is the world's fastest commercial maglev train.|近义词：magnetic levitation train, high-speed rail|反义词:conventional rail
