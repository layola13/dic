prolong:/prəˈlɒŋ/|/prəˈlɔːŋ/| vt.|延长；拖延；使延长|To make something last longer.  |例句：The doctor prescribed medication to prolong his life. 医生开了药以延长他的寿命。|例句：They prolonged the meeting until late at night. 他们把会议拖延到深夜。|近义词：extend, lengthen, continue, stretch out |反义词：shorten, curtail, abbreviate
