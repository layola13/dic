livened:/ˈlaɪvənd/| v.|使活跃；使有生气；使振奋|To make or become more lively, animated, or energetic.|The party livened up considerably after he arrived.  派对在他到来后热闹多了。|The music livened the atmosphere. 音乐使气氛活跃起来。|animated, invigorated, enlivened |dull, lifeless, subdued|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music livens the atmosphere. = 音乐使气氛活跃起来。
He livens up every party he attends. = 他参加的每个聚会都被他活跃起来。

一般过去时:
The news livened her spirits. = 这个消息使她精神振奋。
