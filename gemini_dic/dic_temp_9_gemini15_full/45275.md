cultrate:/ˈkʌltreɪt/| v.|耕作，培养，陶冶|To cultivate (land) or to cultivate (a skill or quality).  It implies a process of careful and diligent effort to improve or develop something.|例句：The farmer will cultrate the field before planting. 农民将在播种前耕作田地。|例句：She spent years culturing her musical talent. 她花了数年时间培养她的音乐天赋。|近义词：cultivate, foster, develop|反义词：neglect, destroy
