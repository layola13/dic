re-dialled:/ˌriːˈdaɪld/| v.|重新拨打（电话）|To dial a telephone number again.|例句：I re-dialled the number several times, but no one answered. 我重新拨打了这个号码几次，但没有人接。|例句：She re-dialled the wrong number, causing further frustration. 她又拨错了号码，导致更加沮丧。|近义词：redial, call back, try again|反义词：disconnect, hang up


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-dials the number every few minutes. = 他每隔几分钟就重新拨打号码。
They re-dial frequently when they get a busy signal. = 他们遇到忙音时会频繁地重新拨打。


一般过去时:
I re-dialled the number after the call dropped. = 电话中断后，我重新拨打了号码。
She re-dialled her mother's number because she didn't hear her answer. = 她因为没听到她母亲的回答而重新拨打了她母亲的号码。


现在完成时:
He has re-dialled that number countless times. = 他已经无数次重新拨打过那个号码了。
She has re-dialled the wrong number three times already. = 她已经三次重新拨打错号码了。


过去完成时:
I had re-dialled the number several times before she finally answered. = 在她最终接电话之前，我已经重新拨打了好几次号码。
They had re-dialled the office number many times before the secretary answered. = 在秘书接电话之前，他们已经多次重新拨打办公室号码了。


将来时:
I will re-dial the number if I don't get an answer. = 如果我没有得到答复，我将重新拨打号码。
She will re-dial the number tomorrow if she still hasn't heard back. = 如果她明天还没收到回音，她会重新拨打电话。


虚拟语气:
If I had re-dialled sooner, I might have reached him. = 如果我早点重新拨打，也许就能联系到他了。
I wish I had re-dialled before the office closed. = 我真希望在办公室关门前我重新拨打过电话。


被动语态:
The number was re-dialled repeatedly without success. = 号码被反复拨打，但没有成功。
The number was re-dialled by the automated system after the initial call failed. = 初次呼叫失败后，号码被自动系统重新拨打。


疑问句:
Did you re-dial the number? = 你重新拨打号码了吗？
Will he re-dial the number later? = 他稍后会重新拨打号码吗？


否定句:
He didn't re-dial the number. = 他没有重新拨打号码。
She didn't re-dial the number because she was frustrated. = 她没有重新拨打号码，因为她很沮丧。
