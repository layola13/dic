humiliation:/ˌhjuːmɪliˈeɪʃən/| n. |羞辱；丢脸；耻辱 |The feeling of being humbled and degraded.  It involves a loss of pride, dignity, or self-respect, often caused by public embarrassment or exposure of flaws. |例句：The public humiliation he suffered was unbearable.  他所遭受的公开羞辱是难以忍受的。

例句：Her humiliation at failing the exam was profound.  她考试不及格带来的羞辱感非常强烈。


近义词：shame, disgrace, embarrassment, degradation, mortification |反义词：honor, pride, glory, exaltation |词性：名词
