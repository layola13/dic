ruination:/ˌruːɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |毁灭；破产；败落 |The complete destruction or ruin of something.  It can refer to the state of being ruined, or the act of ruining something. |例句：The ruination of the city was complete after the earthquake. 地震之后，这座城市彻底被毁。

The ruination of his business was a result of poor management. 他事业的败落是管理不善造成的。|近义词：destruction, devastation, downfall, ruin |反义词：salvation, prosperity, success
