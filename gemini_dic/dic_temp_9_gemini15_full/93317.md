ill-mannered: /ˌɪlˈmænəd/ | adj. | 没教养的；粗鲁的 | Ill-mannered describes someone who behaves in a rude or impolite way, lacking good manners and social graces.  They often disregard social etiquette and norms of respectful interaction. |  例句：He was ill-mannered and interrupted the speaker constantly. 他没教养，不断打断发言者。

例句：It's considered ill-mannered to talk with your mouth full.  满嘴食物说话是不礼貌的。


近义词：rude, impolite, discourteous, uncivil, boorish, uncouth, vulgar

反义词：polite, courteous, well-mannered, refined, civil


词性：形容词
