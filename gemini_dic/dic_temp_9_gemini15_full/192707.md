scything:/ˈsaɪðɪŋ/| v.|割草；收割；猛烈打击 |The act of cutting with a scythe;  the act of mowing or reaping with a scythe;  a sweeping or powerful blow.|例句：The farmer was scything the wheat.  农民正在收割小麦。
The wind scythed through the trees. 风猛烈地穿过树林。|近义词：mowing, reaping, cutting, slashing|反义词：planting, sowing

She scythes the field with great skill. 她熟练地收割着田里的庄稼。
