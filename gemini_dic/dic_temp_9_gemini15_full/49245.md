deleteriously:/ˌdɛləˈtɪəriəsli/| adv. |有害地，败坏地|harmfully; in a way that is harmful or destructive.  The chemical is known to deleteriously affect the environment.这种化学物质已知会对环境造成有害影响。|近义词：harmfully, detrimentally, injuriously|反义词：beneficially, helpfully


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pesticide deleteriously affects the bees. = 这种杀虫剂对蜜蜂有害。
Pollution deleteriously impacts air quality. = 污染对空气质量有害。


一般过去时:
The drought deleteriously affected the crops. = 干旱对庄稼造成了有害影响。
His actions deleteriously impacted his reputation. = 他的行为严重损害了他的名誉。


现在完成时:
Industrial waste has deleteriously affected the river's ecosystem. = 工业废料已对河流生态系统造成了有害影响。
Years of neglect have deleteriously affected the building's structure. = 多年来的忽视已对建筑物的结构造成了有害影响。


过去完成时:
The company had deleteriously impacted the local economy before the intervention. = 在干预之前，该公司已对当地经济造成了有害影响。
The prolonged war had deleteriously affected the country's infrastructure. = 长期的战争已经严重损害了国家的基础设施。


将来时:
Climate change will deleteriously affect coastal communities. = 气候变化将对沿海社区产生有害影响。
The new policy will deleteriously affect small businesses. = 新政策将对小型企业产生有害影响。


虚拟语气:
If we continue to pollute, it will deleteriously affect future generations. = 如果我们继续污染，它将对子孙后代产生有害影响。
I wish the government hadn't implemented policies that deleteriously affect the poor. = 我希望政府没有实施那些对穷人有害的政策。


被动语态:
The environment was deleteriously affected by the oil spill. = 环境受到了石油泄漏的有害影响。
The patient's health was deleteriously affected by the medication. = 病人的健康受到了药物的有害影响。


疑问句:
Will the new regulations deleteriously affect our business? = 新规章会对我们的业务产生有害影响吗？
Does smoking deleteriously affect lung health? = 吸烟会对肺部健康产生有害影响吗？


否定句:
The new technology did not deleteriously affect the environment. = 新技术没有对环境产生有害影响。
The treatment did not deleteriously affect the patient's recovery. = 该治疗没有对病人的康复产生有害影响。

词源:
From Late Latin *dēletēriōsus, from Latin dēlēter "destroyer," from dēlēre "to destroy, erase."
