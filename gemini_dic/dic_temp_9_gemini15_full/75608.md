fugacious:/fjuˈɡeɪʃəs/| adj.|短暂的；易逝的；飞逝的 | fleeting; lasting only for a short time.  The beauty of the cherry blossoms is fugacious, lasting only a few weeks.  |近义词：transient, ephemeral, evanescent, short-lived |反义词：permanent, enduring, lasting, perpetual| 形容词


例句：

The fugacious nature of youth is often lamented by older people.  青春易逝的本性常常被老年人所惋惜。


The pleasure derived from the concert was fugacious, leaving me wanting more.  音乐会带来的快乐转瞬即逝，让我意犹未尽。
