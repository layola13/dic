lystrosaurus:/ˈlɪstrəˌsɔːrəs/| n. |丽齿兽|An extinct genus of dicynodont therapsid that lived during the Late Permian and Early Triassic periods.  Lystrosaurus was a heavily built, quadrupedal herbivore with a distinctive beak-like mouth and tusks. It was widely distributed across the supercontinent Pangaea, and its fossils have been found in Antarctica, India, China, and other regions. Its wide geographic distribution is often cited as evidence of continental drift. |例句：The discovery of Lystrosaurus fossils in Antarctica provided strong evidence supporting the theory of continental drift.  南极洲发现的丽齿兽化石为大陆漂移学说提供了强有力的证据。


例句：Many paleontologists study Lystrosaurus to understand the Permian-Triassic extinction event and the subsequent recovery of life. 许多古生物学家研究丽齿兽以了解二叠纪-三叠纪灭绝事件及随后的生命恢复。


近义词: dicynodont (a type of therapsid)
