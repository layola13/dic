irresponsibly:/ˌɪrɪˈspɑːnsəbli/| adv. | 不负责任地，鲁莽地 | Acting in a way that shows a lack of responsibility.  |例句：He acted irresponsibly by driving under the influence of alcohol. 他酒后驾车，行为不负责任。|例句：She irresponsibly left her children unattended. 她不负责任地让孩子们无人看管。|近义词：recklessly, carelessly, negligently |反义词：responsibly, carefully, cautiously |副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He acts irresponsibly. = 他行为不负责任。
They always behave irresponsibly. = 他们总是行为不负责任。

一般过去时:
He acted irresponsibly last night. = 他昨晚行为不负责任。
She irresponsibly left her work unfinished. = 她不负责任地留下未完成的工作。

现在完成时:
He has acted irresponsibly many times. = 他已经多次行为不负责任了。
She has irresponsibly neglected her duties. = 她不负责任地忽略了她的职责。

过去完成时:
He had irresponsibly spent all his money before the trip. = 旅行前他已经不负责任地花光了所有的钱。
She had irresponsibly promised more than she could deliver. = 她不负责任地许诺了她无法兑现的事情。

将来时:
He will irresponsibly ignore the warning. = 他将会不负责任地忽略警告。
They will irresponsibly damage the environment. = 他们将会不负责任地破坏环境。

虚拟语气:
If he acted responsibly, he wouldn't be in this mess. = 如果他行为负责，他就不会陷入这种困境。
I wish she hadn't acted so irresponsibly. = 我希望她没有那么不负责任地行事。

被动语态: (Irresponsibly is rarely used in passive voice)
The task was completed irresponsibly.  = 任务完成得不负责任。
The project was handled irresponsibly. = 项目处理得不负责任。


疑问句:
Did he act irresponsibly? = 他行为不负责任吗？
Is this a irresponsible decision? = 这是一个不负责任的决定吗？


否定句:
He didn't act irresponsibly. = 他没有行为不负责任。
She didn't handle the situation irresponsibly.  = 她处理情况时并没有不负责任。



PHRASE->
acted irresponsibly = 行为不负责任
behaved irresponsibly = 行为不负责任
spoke irresponsibly = 言行不负责任
decided irresponsibly = 决定不负责任
spent money irresponsibly = 不负责任地花钱
