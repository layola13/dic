例句：

He fast-forwarding through the boring parts of the movie. = 他快进电影中无聊的部分。

We need to fast-forwarding the project to meet the deadline. = 我们需要加快项目进度以赶上截止日期。


近义词：speeding up, accelerating, advancing, rushing

反义词：rewinding, slowing down, delaying
