poppa:/ˈpɑːpɑː/| n. |爸爸，爹地 |A colloquial term for "father" or "dad".  It's often used affectionately or informally. | Example:  "I'm going to ask Poppa for help." = 我要去找爸爸帮忙。|近义词：dad, daddy, father, papa |反义词：None

Poppa works hard to support us. = 爸爸努力工作养活我们。
