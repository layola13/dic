overkills:/ˈoʊvərˌkɪlz/ | n. | 过度杀伤；杀伤过剩；过度使用；矫枉过正 | Excessive force or action; more than is necessary.  It refers to a situation where the response to a problem is far greater than what is required, often resulting in unintended negative consequences. |例句：The military response was overkill; a smaller force could have achieved the same objective.  军事回应是过度反应；较小的部队就能达到同样的目标。

例句：Using a sledgehammer to crack a nut is overkill. 用大锤砸坚果是杀鸡用牛刀。


近义词：excess, superfluity, overdoing, extravagance, overreaction


反义词：underkill, insufficiency, inadequacy


词性: 名词
