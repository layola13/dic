clingiest: /ˈklɪŋɡiɪst/ | adj. | 最依恋的，最粘人的 |  Most inclined to cling; excessively dependent or attached. This word is the superlative form of "clingy". It describes someone who is overly dependent and affectionate, often to an excessive degree.  |  He was the clingiest boyfriend she'd ever had, constantly needing reassurance and attention. 他是她交往过的最粘人的男朋友，总是需要安慰和关注。


近义词:  dependent, attached, needy, possessive, adhesive


反义词: independent, detached, self-reliant, autonomous


词性: 形容词
