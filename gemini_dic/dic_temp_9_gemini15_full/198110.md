shooting:/ˈʃuːtɪŋ/ | n. v. |射击；拍摄；拍摄场景；  |The act of firing a weapon; the act of filming a movie or television show; a scene being filmed.|例句：The shooting of the film took place in Hollywood. 电影的拍摄发生在好莱坞。  The police are investigating the shooting. 警方正在调查这起枪击案。|近义词：firing, filming, photography |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shoots hoops every day. = 他每天都打篮球。
She shoots photos for a living. = 她以拍摄照片为生。

一般过去时:
They shot a movie last year. = 他们去年拍了一部电影。
He shot a basket from half-court. = 他在半场投篮得分。

现在完成时:
I have shot over a hundred photos today. = 我今天已经拍了超过一百张照片。

过去完成时:

将来时:
We will shoot the wedding next month. = 我们下个月将拍摄婚礼。
