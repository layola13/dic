informality:/ˌɪnfɔːrˈmæləti/ | n. |非正式；随意；不拘礼节 | The lack of formality; a casual or relaxed manner.  It refers to a style of communication or behavior that is relaxed, friendly, and unconcerned with strict rules of etiquette.  This can manifest in language, dress, and social interactions. |例句：The meeting was characterized by a surprising degree of informality.  会议的特点是出乎意料的随意性。

The informality of their relationship allowed them to speak freely. 他们关系的随意性使他们能够畅所欲言。|近义词：casualness, familiarity, looseness |反义词：formality, stiffness, rigidity
