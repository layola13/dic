migrant:/ˈmaɪɡrənt/| n. |移民，候鸟|A person who moves from one place to another, especially to find work or better living conditions; also an animal that moves regularly from one place to another.|例句：Many migrants work in the agricultural sector. 许多移民在农业部门工作。

例句：The swallows are migratory birds. 燕子是候鸟。|近义词：immigrant, emigrant, traveler|反义词：native, resident|名词
