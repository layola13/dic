sermoning:/ˈsɜːrmənɪŋ/| v.|说教；训斥|The act of delivering a sermon or of giving a long, tedious, and often critical lecture or reprimand.  It implies a moralizing or didactic tone, often unwelcome.|例句：His constant sermoning about my lifestyle annoyed me. 他不断地对我的人生方式说教，让我很烦。

例句：The teacher's sermoning on the importance of hard work fell on deaf ears. 老师关于努力工作重要性的说教没有引起学生的重视。


近义词：preaching, lecturing, moralizing, reproving, scolding

反义词：praising, encouraging, supporting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is sermoning at the congregation. = 他正在向会众布道。
She sermonizes constantly about the evils of social media. = 她不断地教训人社交媒体的危害。


一般过去时:
The priest sermoned on the importance of forgiveness. = 牧师就宽恕的重要性进行了布道。
My mother sermoned me for staying out late. = 我妈妈因为我晚归而训斥了我。


现在完成时:
He has been sermoning to his children about their responsibilities for years. = 多年来，他一直在教训他的孩子们要承担责任。
She has sermonized against the dangers of smoking her whole life. = 她一生都在告诫吸烟的危害。


过去完成时:
The pastor had sermoned on the same topic several times before. = 牧师之前已经就同一个话题布道过几次了。
My grandfather had sermoned me about the value of hard work before I left for college. = 我去上大学之前，爷爷就勤奋工作的重要性训诫过我。


将来时:
The minister will sermon on the parable of the Good Samaritan. = 牧师将要就好撒玛利亚人的比喻进行布道。
She will sermonize her children about their table manners before the dinner party. = 晚宴之前，她将教训她的孩子们餐桌礼仪。


虚拟语气:
If he sermoned less, people might listen more. = 如果他少说教一些，人们可能会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't sermonize so much. = 我希望他不那么爱说教。


被动语态:
The congregation was sermoned on the importance of charity. = 会众被教诲了慈善的重要性。
The students were sermoned for their poor behavior. = 学生们因为行为不端而被训斥。


疑问句:
Is he sermoning again? = 他又在说教了吗？
Was she sermoning to the children about their chores? = 她是不是正在教训孩子们做家务？


否定句:
He didn't sermon at the meeting. = 他在会议上没有说教。
She didn't sermonize to the employees about their work habits. = 她没有教训员工的工作习惯。
