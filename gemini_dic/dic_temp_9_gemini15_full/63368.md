entomology:/ˌɛntəˈmɒlədʒi/ | n. |昆虫学|The scientific study of insects.  Entomology encompasses various aspects of insect life, including their morphology, physiology, behavior, ecology, and classification.  It also plays a crucial role in various fields like agriculture, medicine, and forensics.|例句：She is pursuing a degree in entomology. 她正在攻读昆虫学学位。

例句：The entomologist collected samples of various insects for research. 昆虫学家收集了各种昆虫样本用于研究。


近义词：insect science, study of insects | 反义词：无
