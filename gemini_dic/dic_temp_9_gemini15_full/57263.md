downpayment: /ˈdaʊnˌpeɪmənt/ | n. | 首付，定金 |  A partial payment made at the time of purchase, with the balance to be paid later.  It's typically used when buying expensive items like houses or cars. |例句：They made a down payment of $20,000 on the house. = 他们付了2万美元的房屋首付。

They put down a 10% downpayment on the new car. = 他们为新车支付了10%的首付。


近义词：deposit, initial payment | 反义词：balance, remaining payment

词性: 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The buyer makes a down payment. = 买家支付首付。
He always makes a large down payment. = 他总是支付一大笔首付。


一般过去时:
She made a down payment last week. = 她上周付了首付。
We made a small down payment on our new apartment. = 我们为新公寓支付了一笔小额首付。


现在完成时:
They have made their down payment. = 他们已经支付了首付。
We haven't made the down payment yet. = 我们还没付首付。


过去完成时:
They had already made the down payment before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经付了首付。
She had not made a down payment when the offer expired. = 当报价过期时，她还没有支付首付。


将来时:
They will make a down payment next month. = 他们下个月将支付首付。
We will need to save more money before we can make a down payment. = 我们需要存更多钱才能支付首付。


虚拟语气:
If I had a larger down payment, I would buy that house. = 如果我有更大的首付，我会买那栋房子。
I wish I had made a larger down payment. = 我真希望我付了更大的首付。


被动语态:
A down payment was required for the purchase. = 购买需要支付首付。
The down payment is being processed. = 首付正在处理中。


疑问句:
What is the required down payment? = 需要的首付是多少？
How much down payment can you afford? = 你能支付多少首付？


否定句:
They didn't make a down payment on time. = 他们没有按时支付首付。
We couldn't afford the required down payment. = 我们支付不起所需的首付。


PHRASE->
large down payment: 大额首付
small down payment: 小额首付
minimum down payment: 最低首付
affordable down payment: 可负担的首付
down payment amount: 首付款项
