shepster:/ˈʃɛpstər/| n. |牧羊人，牧羊犬|A shepster is a person who tends sheep, or, less commonly, a sheepdog. The term is chiefly used in dialectal English, particularly in Scotland and northern England.|例句：The old shepster led his flock across the moor.  老牧羊人带领他的羊群穿过荒原。|近义词：shepherd, herdsman|反义词：N/A
