nonelected: /ˌnɑːnɪˈlɛktɪd/ | adj. | 未经选举的，非民选的 | Not elected to a position; not chosen by voting.  This adjective describes individuals or bodies that haven't been chosen through an electoral process.  | 例句：The nonelected officials were appointed by the governor. = 未经选举产生的官员是由州长任命的。


The nonelected board members lacked the legitimacy of their elected counterparts. =  未经选举产生的董事会成员缺乏民选成员的合法性。


近义词：appointed, unchosen, unelected


反义词：elected, chosen
