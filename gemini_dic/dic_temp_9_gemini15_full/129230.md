noctiferous:/nɒkˈtɪfərəs/| adj. |夜生的；发光的；夜间活动的 |Producing light at night; relating to or characteristic of the night. The word combines "noct-" (night) and "-ferous" (bearing, carrying).  |例句：The noctiferous fireflies illuminated the forest.  萤火虫照亮了森林。

The rare noctiferous fungi glowed faintly in the dark cave.  稀有的夜光真菌在黑暗的洞穴中发出微弱的光芒。|近义词：luminescent, phosphorescent, nocturnal |反义词：diurnal
