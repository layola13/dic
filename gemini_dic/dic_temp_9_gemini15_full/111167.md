lithoclast:/ˈlɪθəʊklæst/| n. |碎石器；用于碎石的器械|A lithoclast is a surgical instrument used to break up stones, typically kidney stones or gallstones, into smaller fragments that can be more easily passed through the body.|The surgeon used a lithoclast to shatter the kidney stone into smaller pieces.  外科医生用碎石器将肾结石粉碎成更小的碎片。|  stone crusher, stone breaker|—|
