coscripted:/koʊˈskrɪptɪd/| adj.|强制征募的，被强制征召入伍的|  Describes someone or something that has been forcibly conscripted into service, typically military service.  It implies a lack of choice or consent.| Example: The coscripted soldiers were forced to fight in a war they didn't believe in.  强制征募的士兵被迫参加他们不相信的战争。|近义词：drafted, conscripted, impressed|反义词：voluntary, volunteer
