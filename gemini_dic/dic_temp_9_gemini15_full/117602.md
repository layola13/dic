meganucleus:/ˌmɛɡəˈnjuːkliəs/| n. |大核；巨核|A large nucleus, especially one found in certain ciliates.  It is typically polyploid and responsible for maintaining the genetic material of the organism. |Example: The paramecium's meganucleus controls its daily functions.  变形虫的大核控制着它的日常功能。  The researchers observed the meganucleus's activity during cell division. 研究人员观察了细胞分裂过程中大核的活动。|近义词：macronucleus|反义词：micronucleus|名词
