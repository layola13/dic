semester:/səˈmɛstər/| n. |学期|A semester is one of the two periods into which a school year is divided.|例句：The fall semester begins in September. 秋季学期在九月开始。|近义词：term|反义词:  None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The semester starts next week. = 学期下周开始。
This semester is very challenging. = 这个学期很有挑战性。

一般过去时:
Last semester I took five classes. = 上个学期我选了五门课。
The semester ended earlier than expected. = 学期比预期提前结束。

现在完成时:
I have completed all my assignments this semester. = 这个学期我已经完成了所有的作业。
She has already registered for next semester. = 她已经注册了下一学期的课程。

过去完成时:
By the end of the semester, I had finished all my exams. = 到学期结束时，我已经完成了所有的考试。
They had already decided on their majors before the semester began. = 学期开始之前他们就已经决定了自己的专业。

将来时:
Next semester I will study abroad. = 下个学期我将出国留学。
The new semester will begin on August 28th. = 新学期将于8月28日开始。

虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder last semester, I would have gotten a better grade. = 如果我上个学期学习更努力的话，我的成绩会更好。
I wish I had taken that course last semester. = 我真希望上个学期选修了那门课。

被动语态:
The exam schedule will be announced before the semester starts. = 考试时间表将在学期开始前公布。
The course was cancelled due to low enrollment. = 由于报名人数较少，课程被取消了。

疑问句:
When does the semester end? = 学期什么时候结束？
How many credits are required this semester? = 这个学期需要多少学分？

否定句:
I didn't do well in that class last semester. = 我上学期那门课没考好。
The professor did not extend the deadline for the assignment. = 教授没有延长作业的截止日期。


PHRASE->
fall semester = 秋季学期
spring semester = 春季学期
summer semester = 夏季学期
semester break = 学期假期
semester system = 学期制度
