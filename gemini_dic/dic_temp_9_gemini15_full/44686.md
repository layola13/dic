crucigerous:/kruːˈsɪdʒərəs/| adj.| 十字形的；有十字架的 | Bearing a cross or crosses; cruciform. The term is often used in botany to describe plants with cruciform flowers.  | Example:  The crucigerous plant displayed its characteristic four-petaled flowers.  这株十字花科植物展示了其特征性的四瓣花朵。 |近义词：cruciform, cross-shaped | 反义词：crossless
