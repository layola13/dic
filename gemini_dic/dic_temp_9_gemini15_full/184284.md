resoundingly:/rɪˈzaʊndɪŋli/ | adv. | 响亮地；彻底地；压倒性地 |  The adjective "resounding" means producing a deep, resonant sound; or,  in a figurative sense, very great, complete, or emphatic.  The adverb "resoundingly" intensifies this meaning, indicating that something happened in a very clear, decisive, and emphatic manner. |例句：The audience resoundingly applauded the performance.  观众热烈鼓掌。

The proposal was resoundingly defeated.  提案被彻底否决了。| 近义词：overwhelmingly, decisively, emphatically, triumphantly | 反义词：slightly, marginally, narrowly
