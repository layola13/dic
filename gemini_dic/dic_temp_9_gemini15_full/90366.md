how-come:/haʊ kʌm/ | interjection | 怎么会；为什么 | This is an informal phrase used to express surprise, disbelief, or to ask for an explanation. It's a contraction of "how comes it that...?" or "how does it come that...?"  It functions similarly to "why?". |例句：How come you're so late?  你怎么这么晚？

例句：How come she didn't tell us? 她怎么没告诉我们？


近义词：why, wherefore


反义词：无


词性：感叹词
