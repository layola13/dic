counterinfluences: /ˌkaʊntərˈɪnflʊənsɪz/ | n. | 反作用力；对抗性影响 | Influences that oppose or counteract other influences.  Counterinfluences can be deliberate attempts to change someone's mind or actions, or they can be unintended consequences of other actions.  |  The government's propaganda campaign faced strong counterinfluences from independent media outlets.  政府的宣传活动面临着来自独立媒体的强烈反作用力。
|近义词: opposing forces, countervailing forces, counteractions |反义词: reinforcing influences, supporting influences |名词
