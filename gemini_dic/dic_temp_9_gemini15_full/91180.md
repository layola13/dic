hydrarthrosis:/ˌhaɪdrɑːrˈθroʊsɪs/| n. |水关节积液，关节积水 |  The accumulation of serous fluid in a joint cavity.  This condition is often characterized by swelling, pain, and limited range of motion in the affected joint.  It can be caused by various factors, including injury, infection, or underlying diseases like rheumatoid arthritis. | Example: The patient presented with hydrarthrosis of the knee, necessitating aspiration and analysis of the synovial fluid.  患者膝关节积水，需要进行抽吸和滑液分析。|近义词：joint effusion, synovial effusion |反义词：joint dryness (a relative term)
