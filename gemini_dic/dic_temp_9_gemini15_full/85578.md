hating:/ˈheɪtɪŋ/| v. |憎恨，厌恶|The act of feeling intense dislike or ill will toward someone or something.  It often involves a strong emotional response and a desire to avoid or harm the object of hatred.|例句：I'm hating this weather. 我讨厌这个天气。

例句：He was hating his job and wanted to quit. 他讨厌他的工作，想辞职。


近义词：detesting, loathing, abhorring, despising

反义词：loving, adoring, cherishing


词性:动词(Verb)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am hating this traffic. = 我讨厌这交通堵塞。
She is hating her new boss. = 她讨厌她的新老板。


一般过去时:
I hated my childhood. = 我讨厌我的童年。
He hated the noisy environment. = 他讨厌吵闹的环境。


现在完成时:
I have been hating this job for months. = 我已经讨厌这份工作好几个月了。
She has hated him since they broke up. = 从他们分手以来她就一直讨厌他。


过去完成时:
I had hated him long before he betrayed me. = 在他背叛我很久以前我就讨厌他了。
She had hated the idea from the start. = 她从一开始就讨厌这个主意。


将来时:
I will hate this place if I have to stay longer. = 如果我不得不待更久，我会讨厌这个地方。
He will hate the consequences of his actions. = 他将讨厌他行为的后果。


虚拟语气:
I would hate to see him fail. = 我不想看到他失败。
I wish I didn’t hate public speaking. = 我真希望我不讨厌公开演讲。


被动语态: (Hating is rarely used passively)
*This aspect is not applicable for this verb*


疑问句:
Are you hating your new apartment? = 你讨厌你的新公寓吗？
Does she hate spicy food? = 她讨厌辛辣食物吗？


否定句:
I don't hate him, I just don't like him. = 我不恨他，我只是不喜欢他。
She doesn't hate cats; she's just allergic. = 她不讨厌猫；她只是过敏。



PHRASE->
be hating something/someone: 讨厌某事/某人
can't stand hating: 受不了讨厌
get tired of hating: 厌倦讨厌
stop hating: 停止讨厌
full of hating: 充满仇恨
