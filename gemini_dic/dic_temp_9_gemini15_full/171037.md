pseudostratified:/ˌsuːdəʊˈstrætɪfaɪd/| adj.|伪复层的；假复层的|Appearing to be layered or stratified, but actually composed of a single layer of cells of varying heights.|The respiratory epithelium is pseudostratified, giving the appearance of multiple layers.  呼吸道上皮是伪复层的，看起来像多层。|近义词：falsely stratified, apparently stratified|反义词：simple, stratified

词源:

pseudo- (from Greek ψευδής (pseudēs), meaning "false" or "lying") + stratified (from Latin *strātum, past participle of sternere, meaning "to spread out" or "to strew").  因此，pseudostratified字面意思是“假性分层”或“貌似分层”。
