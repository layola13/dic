fellowed:/ˈfæləʊd/| v.|结伴而行；陪伴；追随|The word "fellowed" is the past tense and past participle of the verb "fellow," which is an archaic or dialectal form of "follow."  It means to accompany, follow, or go with someone. Its usage is quite limited in modern English.|例句：The children fellowed their teacher to the museum.孩子们跟着老师去了博物馆。 He fellowed his father into the business. 他跟随父亲进入商界。|近义词：followed, accompanied, trailed|反义词：led, preceded
