lustration:/lʌˈstreɪʃən/| n. |清洗；净化；赎罪；查明；揭露|The act of cleansing or purifying, especially ritually or symbolically;  the process of investigating and exposing wrongdoing, especially in a political context.|例句：The lustration process aimed to cleanse the government of corrupt officials.  清洗过程旨在清除政府中的腐败官员。

例句：The ritual lustration involved washing away sins with holy water.  仪式上的清洗是用圣水洗去罪孽。


近义词：purification, cleansing, expiation, purgation, investigation, exposure


反义词：contamination, pollution, corruption


词性:名词
