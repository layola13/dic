gunge:/ɡʌndʒ/| n. |烂泥；污泥；脏东西|Thick, messy, and usually unpleasant substance.  Often refers to mud, slime, or other similar materials. |例句：The children were covered in gunge after playing in the mud.孩子们在泥地里玩耍后浑身都是烂泥。

例句：The floor was covered in a thick layer of gunge after the party.派对结束后地板上覆盖着一层厚厚的污泥。


近义词：sludge, muck, mire, filth|反义词：cleanliness, purity|名词
