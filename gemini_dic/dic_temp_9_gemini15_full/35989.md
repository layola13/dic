codesigning:/ˌkoʊˈsaɪnɪŋ/| v.| 代码签名，联合签名 |The process of digitally signing software or other code to verify its authenticity and integrity.  This is often done to ensure that the code hasn't been tampered with and comes from a trusted source. | Example:  The app developer needs to codesign their software before distribution.  应用开发者需要在分发软件之前对其进行代码签名。|近义词：digital signing, authentication |反义词：None
