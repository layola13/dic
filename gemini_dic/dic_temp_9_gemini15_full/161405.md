postulating:/ˈpɒstʃʊleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 假定；假设；提出（理论） | To suggest or assume the existence, truth, or occurrence of something as a basis for reasoning, discussion, or belief.  |  Scientists are postulating a new theory about the origins of the universe. 科学家们正在提出关于宇宙起源的新理论。  | hypothesizing, assuming, suggesting |  refuting, denying, disproving | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He postulates a connection between diet and disease. 他假设饮食和疾病之间存在联系。
They are postulating a new model for economic growth. 他们正在提出一个新的经济增长模型。

一般过去时:
The philosopher postulated the existence of a supreme being. 这位哲学家假设存在一个至高无上的存在。
She postulated that the accident was caused by driver error. 她假设事故是由驾驶员错误造成的。

现在完成时:
Scientists have postulated a link between stress and illness. 科学家们已经假设压力和疾病之间存在联系。
Researchers have postulated that climate change is accelerating. 研究人员已经假设气候变化正在加速。

过去完成时:
They had postulated that the experiment would fail before they even began. 他们在实验开始之前就假设实验会失败。
Historians had postulated a different timeline for the events. 历史学家们已经假设了这些事件不同的时间线。

将来时:
They will postulate a new approach to the problem. 他们将提出解决这个问题的新方法。
The researchers will postulate that the results are statistically significant. 研究人员将假设结果具有统计学意义。

虚拟语气:
If we postulated a different scenario, the outcome might be different. 如果我们假设不同的场景，结果可能会不同。
I wish they had postulated a more realistic solution. 我希望他们能提出一个更现实的解决方案。

被动语态:
A new theory was postulated by the research team. 一个新的理论是由研究小组提出的。
The existence of dark matter is postulated by many physicists. 许多物理学家都假设存在暗物质。

疑问句:
What are you postulating in your research? 你的研究中假设了什么？
What did the author postulate as the main cause of the war? 作者假设战争的主要原因是什么？

否定句:
The study did not postulate any causal relationship. 这项研究没有假设任何因果关系。
He did not postulate a direct link between the two events. 他没有假设这两个事件之间存在直接联系。
