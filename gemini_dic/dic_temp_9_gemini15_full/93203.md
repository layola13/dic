ignore:/ɪɡˈnɔːr/| vt.|忽略，忽视，不理睬|to refuse to take notice of or acknowledge; disregard.|例句：He ignored my warnings. 他无视我的警告。
例句：She tried to ignore the pain. 她试图忽略疼痛。|近义词：disregard, neglect, overlook, disregard|反义词：heed, attend to, consider


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They ignore the problem. = 他们忽略了这个问题。
I ignore his rude comments. = 我忽略了他的粗鲁评论。


一般过去时:
She ignored his calls. = 她无视了他的来电。
He ignored the traffic sign. = 他无视了交通标志。


现在完成时:
They have ignored all my emails. = 他们忽略了我所有的邮件。
I have ignored his advice for too long. = 我已经无视了他的建议太久了。


过去完成时:
She had ignored the danger signs. = 她此前忽略了危险信号。
He had ignored my warnings before the accident. = 事故发生前，他忽略了我的警告。


将来时:
They will ignore the consequences. = 他们将无视后果。
I will ignore his provocation. = 我将无视他的挑衅。


虚拟语气:
If I ignored the problem, it would get worse. = 如果我忽略这个问题，情况会变得更糟。
I wish I had ignored his advice. = 我真希望我当初忽略了他的建议。


被动语态:
The problem was ignored by them. = 这个问题被他们忽略了。
His warnings were ignored. = 他的警告被忽略了。


疑问句:
Why did you ignore me? = 为什么你忽略我？
Will they ignore the evidence? = 他们会忽略证据吗？


否定句:
I didn't ignore his request. = 我没有忽略他的请求。
She couldn't ignore the evidence. = 她无法忽略证据。
