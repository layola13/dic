prattlement:/ˈprætəlmənt/| n. |喋喋不休；唠叨；废话 |Idle and foolish talk; babble.  It refers to a continuous flow of insignificant or meaningless conversation. |例句：Her prattlement about her day bored everyone. = 她喋喋不休地讲述她的一天，让每个人都感到厌烦。

例句：The child's prattlement was a constant background noise. = 孩子们的喋喋不休是持续不断的背景噪音。


近义词：babble, chatter, twaddle, drivel, gibberish | 反义词：silence, eloquence, wisdom


词源:  From "prattle," meaning to talk idly or childishly, which itself comes from Middle English *pratelen, from Old French *brabiller ("to babble").  The "-ment" suffix is a noun suffix indicating an action or state.
