greave:/ɡriːv/| n.|护腿甲|A greave is a piece of armor that protects the shin.  It's typically made of metal or leather and was commonly worn by soldiers and knights in the medieval period. |The knight's greaves were dented from the battle.  骑士的护腿甲在战斗中凹陷了。|shin guard, leg armor| (no direct antonym, but could consider "unprotected shin")|名词
