differentiation:/ˌdɪfərenʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 分化；区别；微分 | The process of distinguishing or being distinguished.  It can refer to the biological process of cell specialization, the cognitive process of distinguishing between things, or the mathematical process of finding a derivative. |例句：The differentiation of cells leads to the formation of tissues and organs.  细胞的分化导致组织和器官的形成。|例句：The teacher emphasized the differentiation between fact and opinion. 老师强调了事实和观点的区别。|近义词：discrimination, distinction, divergence |反义词：integration, uniformity, similarity
