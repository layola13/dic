piedmont:/ˈpiːdˌmɒnt/|/ˈpiːdˌmɑːnt/| n. |山麓；山脚平原|A piedmont is a gently sloping plain formed at the foot of a mountain range, typically characterized by alluvial deposits from the mountains.  It's often fertile and suitable for agriculture.|The vineyards of the Piedmont region of Italy are famous worldwide.  意大利皮埃蒙特地区的葡萄园闻名世界。|plain, lowland, foothills|mountaintop, highland|
