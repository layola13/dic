eluvium:/ɪˈluːviəm/| n. |坡积物，残积物|Material that has accumulated on a slope by the action of gravity.  It is typically unconsolidated and consists of weathered rock fragments, soil, and other debris. Eluvium is distinct from alluvium, which is deposited by water.|例句：The hillside was covered in eluvium, a mixture of soil and broken rock.  山坡上覆盖着坡积物，一种土壤和碎石的混合物。
The geologist studied the eluvium to understand the history of the slope. 地质学家研究了坡积物以了解斜坡的历史。|近义词：colluvium, talus|反义词：alluvium
