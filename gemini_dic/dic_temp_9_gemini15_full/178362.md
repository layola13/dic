reconfirmations:/ˌriːkənˌfɜːrˈmeɪʃənz/ | n. |再次确认；重复确认 |The act of confirming something again, usually to ensure accuracy or agreement.  It implies a previous confirmation has already taken place. |例句：The airline sent reconfirmations of our flight bookings. 航空公司再次确认了我们的航班预订。

例句：We received several reconfirmations of the meeting time. 我们收到了几次关于会议时间的再次确认。


近义词：reassurances, verifications, confirmations (again) | 反义词：denials, cancellations | 词性：名词
