middle-European: /ˌmɪdəl juərəˈpiːən/ | adj. | 中欧的 | Relating to the countries of Central Europe.  The term can be geographically imprecise, with varying definitions depending on the context. It generally includes countries in the heart of Europe, but the specific inclusion or exclusion of certain nations can be debated. | Example:  The architecture of the building is distinctly middle-European. 这座建筑的风格明显是中欧风格的。  |近义词: Central European | 反义词:  Western European, Eastern European (depending on context) | 词性:形容词
