islamicized: /ɪˈzlæmɪkˌaɪzd/ | adj. | 伊斯兰化的；具有伊斯兰教特色的 |  Made to conform to the principles or practices of Islam.  The process of adopting Islamic culture, values, or practices. |

例句：

The country has become increasingly islamicized in recent years.  近年来，这个国家越来越伊斯兰化了。

The islamicized version of the text removed all references to alcohol.  伊斯兰化版本的文本删除了所有关于酒精的参考。


近义词：Islamified,  Muslimized (less common)

反义词：Secularized, Westernized


词性:形容词
