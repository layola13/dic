prebargaining: /ˌpriːˈbɑːrɡənɪŋ/ | n. | 预先谈判，事前协商 | The process of negotiating before formal negotiations begin.  It involves informal discussions and exploring possibilities to lay the groundwork for a more structured negotiation.  | Example:  Prebargaining with the union helped avoid a strike.  预先与工会协商有助于避免罢工。


近义词: preliminary negotiations, advance discussions, prior consultations


反义词:  final negotiations, concluding talks


词性: 名词
