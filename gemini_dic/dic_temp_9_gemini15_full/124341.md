motif's:/moʊˈtiːfs/| n.|主题，动机；图案|A motif is a recurring subject, theme, idea, etc., especially in a literary, artistic, or musical work.  It can also refer to a distinctive feature or dominant idea in a design or pattern.|例句：The recurring motif of betrayal runs throughout the novel.  这部小说的贯穿主题是背叛。The fabric featured a repeating floral motif. 这块布料上有一个重复的花卉图案。|近义词：theme, subject, pattern, design|反义词：randomness, chaos
