homesick:/ˈhoʊmsɪk/| adj.|想家的，思乡的|Feeling unhappy because you are a long way from your home and family.|例句：She felt homesick after being away from her family for so long. 她离开家人很久后感到想家。|例句：The new student was homesick and missed his parents terribly. 新来的学生想家，非常想念他的父母。|近义词：longing for home, wistful for home|反义词：content, at ease


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is homesick. = 他想家。
She feels homesick. = 她感到想家。

一般过去时:
I was homesick last night. = 我昨晚想家。
They were homesick during their trip. = 他们在旅行期间想家。

现在完成时:
I have been homesick all week. = 我整个星期都想家。
She has felt homesick since leaving home. = 自从离开家后，她就一直想家。

过去完成时:
I had been homesick for months before I finally went home. = 在我最终回家之前，我已经想家好几个月了。
He had felt homesick all summer before the school year began. = 在学年开始之前，他整个夏天都感到想家。

将来时:
I will be homesick when I go to college. = 当我去上大学的时候，我会想家。
She thinks she will be homesick next year. = 她认为她明年会想家。


虚拟语气:
I wish I weren't so homesick. = 我真希望我不那么想家。
If I were closer to home, I wouldn't be so homesick. = 如果我离家近些，我就不会这么想家了。

否定句:
I am not homesick. = 我不想家。
He wasn't homesick at all. = 他一点也不想家。
