homeward:/ˈhoʊmwərd/| adj. adv.|朝家的；回家方向的|Going or directed toward home.  The word combines "home" with the suffix "-ward," indicating direction.|例句：The birds flew homeward at sunset.  傍晚时分，鸟儿们飞回了家。
例句：She set off on her homeward journey. 她踏上了回家的旅程。|近义词：homebound, houseward|反义词：away, outward
