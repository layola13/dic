multimodel:/ˈmʌltiˌmɒdəl/ | adj. |多模态的；多模式的 | Relating to or involving multiple modalities or modes of representation, typically involving text, images, audio, and video.  Multimodal learning systems leverage diverse data types to improve performance and understanding. | Example:  The multimodal system analyzed the image and the accompanying text to understand the scene.  多模态系统分析图像和相应的文本以理解场景。|近义词:polymodal, multisensory |反义词: unimodal, monomodal
