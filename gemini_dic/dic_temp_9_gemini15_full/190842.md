sawyer:/ˈsɔːjər/| n. |锯木工，伐木工人；（尤指）淘金热时期为淘金者锯木头的人|A sawyer is a person who saws timber, especially one who did so for gold miners during the gold rushes.|例句：He worked as a sawyer in the logging camp. 他在伐木营地里当锯木工。|例句：During the California Gold Rush, sawyers were in high demand. 加州淘金热期间，锯木工非常抢手。|近义词：lumberjack, woodcutter|反义词：N/A|名词
