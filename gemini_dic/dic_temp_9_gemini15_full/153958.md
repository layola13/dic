perfected:/pərˈfɛktɪd/| v. | 使完美；完成；改进 |  To make something perfect or complete; to bring something to a state of completion or excellence.  |  She perfected her technique over years of practice. 她通过多年的练习使她的技巧臻于完善。| The artist perfected the painting with meticulous detail. 艺术家一丝不苟地完善了这幅画。| improved, completed, refined | imperfect, flawed | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He perfects his golf swing every day. = 他每天都在完善他的高尔夫挥杆动作。
They perfect their product design through user feedback. = 他们通过用户反馈来完善他们的产品设计。

一般过去时:
She perfected her recipe after many trials. = 她经过多次尝试后完善了她的食谱。
The engineers perfected the engine design before launch. = 工程师在发布之前完善了引擎设计。

现在完成时:
He has perfected his cooking skills over the years. = 多年来，他已经完善了他的烹饪技巧。
The company has perfected its marketing strategy. = 公司已经完善了它的营销策略。

过去完成时:

She had perfected her presentation before the conference. = 她在会议之前已经完善了她的演示文稿。

They will perfect the software before releasing it to the public. = 他们将在向公众发布软件之前完善它。

虚拟语气:
