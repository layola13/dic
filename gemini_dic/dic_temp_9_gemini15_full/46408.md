daffy:/ˈdæfi/| adj.|傻的，古怪的，疯疯癫癫的 |  Silly, eccentric, or crazy.  The word suggests a charming or endearing silliness rather than outright madness. |例句：He's a bit daffy, but I love him anyway. 他有点傻乎乎的，但我还是爱他。

例句：She wore a daffy hat with feathers and ribbons. 她戴着一顶古怪的帽子，上面装饰着羽毛和丝带。


近义词：silly, goofy, wacky, zany, crazy, eccentric

反义词：serious, sensible, sane, rational


词性:形容词
