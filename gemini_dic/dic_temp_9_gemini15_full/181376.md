reinvitation:/ˌriːˌɪnvɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |再次邀请；重新邀请 |The act of inviting someone again; a second invitation.  It often implies a previous invitation was declined or unsuccessful.|例句：After the initial invitation was declined, a reinvitation was sent.  = 首次邀请被拒绝后，又发出了再次邀请。

例句：The company issued a reinvitation to the conference after several key speakers became available. = 在几位重要演讲人能够出席后，公司再次发出了会议邀请。


近义词：second invitation, renewed invitation

反义词：withdrawal of invitation, cancellation

词性：名词
