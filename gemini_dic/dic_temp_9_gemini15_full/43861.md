crescentic:/krɛˈsɛntɪk/| adj.| 新月形的，月牙形的 |  Shaped like a crescent; having the form of a crescent.  Crescentic refers to something that resembles a crescent moon in shape, which is a curved shape with pointed ends. | Example: The dunes formed a crescentic shape along the coastline.  沙丘沿着海岸线形成了新月形。 The patient presented with a crescentic-shaped scar on their arm. 病人手臂上有一道新月形的疤痕。|近义词：lunate, sickle-shaped |反义词：circular, straight

词源:
From Latin *crescens, present participle of crescere "to grow," referring to the waxing moon.  The suffix "-tic" forms adjectives.  Thus, "crescentic" literally means "related to growing" in the context of the crescent moon's shape.
