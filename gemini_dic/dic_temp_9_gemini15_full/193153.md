sedan:/səˈdæn/ | n. |轿车，四门小汽车 | A sedan is a car with four doors, two rows of seats, and a trunk.  Sedans are known for their fuel efficiency and comfortable ride. | 例句：He drives a sleek, black sedan. 他开一辆光滑的黑色轿车。  She prefers a sedan to an SUV for its better fuel economy. 她更喜欢轿车而不是SUV，因为它更省油。| 近义词：saloon, car | 反义词：SUV, truck, van
