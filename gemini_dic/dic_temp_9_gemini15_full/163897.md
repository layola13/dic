predetailing: /ˌpriːˈdiːteɪlɪŋ/ | v. | 预先详细说明；预先详细规划 |  The act of providing detailed information or plans in advance. It often involves anticipating potential problems and developing solutions beforehand.  | 例句：The project manager emphasized the importance of predetailing every aspect of the construction before starting. 项目经理强调在开工前预先详细说明施工的各个方面的重要性。


The team spent weeks predetailing their marketing strategy, leaving no stone unturned.  团队花了数周时间预先详细规划他们的营销策略，做到面面俱到。| 近义词：planning, outlining, preparing, scheming | 反义词：improvising, winging it | 动词

词源:
The word "predetailing" is a relatively modern coinage, formed by combining the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before") with the verb "detailing" (meaning "giving detailed information").  The word reflects a growing emphasis on thorough planning and preparation in various fields.
