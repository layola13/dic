defrauding:/dɪˈfrɔːdɪŋ/| v.|欺诈，诈骗|The act of obtaining something, typically money, through deception.|例句：He was accused of defrauding the company of millions of dollars. 他被指控诈骗公司数百万美元。

例句：The con artist was found guilty of defrauding elderly people out of their life savings.  这个骗子被判犯有诈骗老年人毕生积蓄的罪行。


近义词：cheating, swindling, scamming, conning|反义词：honesty, fairness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are defrauding investors of their money. = 他们正在诈骗投资者的钱。
He defrauds his clients regularly. = 他经常诈骗他的客户。

一般过去时:
The company defrauded its customers. = 公司欺诈了它的客户。
She defrauded the bank of a large sum of money. = 她诈骗银行一大笔钱。

现在完成时:
He has defrauded many people. = 他已经诈骗了许多人。
They have been defrauding the government for years. = 他们多年来一直在诈骗政府。

过去完成时:
The accountant had defrauded the company before he was caught. = 会计被抓住之前就已诈骗了公司。
They had defrauded several banks before they were finally arrested. = 他们在最终被捕之前已经诈骗了几家银行。

将来时:
They will defraud more people if they are not stopped. = 如果不阻止他们，他们将会诈骗更多的人。
He will be defrauding the public unless the authorities intervene. = 除非当局介入，否则他将诈骗公众。

虚拟语气:
If he had not defrauded the bank, he would not be in jail. = 如果他没有诈骗银行，他就不会在监狱里。
I wish they hadn't defrauded the charity. = 我希望他们没有诈骗慈善机构。

被动语态:
Many people were defrauded by the investment scheme. = 许多人被投资计划所欺诈。
The company was defrauded out of millions of dollars. = 公司被诈骗了数百万美元。

疑问句:

Who was defrauded in this case? = 在此案中谁被欺诈了？

否定句:
He did not defraud the company. = 他没有诈骗公司。
They were not defrauded; they willingly participated. = 他们没有被欺诈；他们是自愿参与的。


PHRASE->

be defrauded out of something = 被诈骗某物

defraud the system = 诈骗体制


词源:
The word "defraud" comes from the Old French word "defrauder," which means "to deceive, cheat."  The prefix "de-" means "away from" or "completely," and the root "fraud" comes from the Old French "fraude," ultimately from the Vulgar Latin *frauda, related to *fraus ("fraud, deceit").
