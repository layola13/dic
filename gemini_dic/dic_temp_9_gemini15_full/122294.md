mitre:/ˈmaɪtər/| n.|尖顶帽；斜接；斜接缝 |A mitre is a tall pointed hat worn by bishops and other clergy.  It can also refer to a type of joint where two pieces of material meet at an angle, often 45 degrees. |例句：The bishop wore a magnificent mitre during the ceremony. 主教在仪式上戴着一顶华丽的尖顶帽。The carpenter used a mitre saw to cut the wood at a 45-degree angle. 木匠用斜切锯以45度角切割木材。|近义词： headdress,  joint, bevel |反义词：flat cap,  butt joint
