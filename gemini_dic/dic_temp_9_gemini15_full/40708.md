convertibility:/ˌkɒnvɜːtəˈbɪləti/ /ˌkɑːnvɜːrtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可兑换性；可转换性 | The ability to be converted into something else, especially money into a different currency.  This often refers to the ease with which a currency can be exchanged for another.  It can also refer to the ability of an asset to be easily changed into cash or another form. | Example:  The high convertibility of the US dollar makes it a popular reserve currency.  美元的高兑换性使其成为一种流行的储备货币。

The free convertibility of the pound was a key policy goal.  英镑的自由兑换是一个关键的政策目标。


近义词：exchangeability, interconvertibility, liquidity | 反义词：inconvertibility, inflexibility
