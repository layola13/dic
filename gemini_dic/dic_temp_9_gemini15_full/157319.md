pioned:/paɪənd/|v.|开创，倡导|to have been a pioneer of something; to have pioneered something.  The word implies a significant first step or achievement, often involving innovation or leadership.|The company pioneered the use of AI in customer service.|近义词：initiated, launched, inaugurated|反义词：abandoned, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pioneers new techniques in surgery. = 他在外科手术中开创了新技术。
She pioneers sustainable farming practices. = 她倡导可持续的农业耕作方式。


一般过去时:
They pioneered the development of the internet. = 他们开创了互联网的发展。
Marie Curie pioneered research in radioactivity. = 居里夫人开创了放射性研究。


现在完成时:
Scientists have pioneered many life-saving treatments. = 科学家们已经开创了许多挽救生命的疗法。
She has pioneered a new approach to teaching English. = 她开创了一种新的英语教学方法。


过去完成时:
Before the invention of the printing press, monks had pioneered the copying of books by hand. = 在印刷术发明之前，僧侣们已经开创了手工抄写书籍的方法。
They had pioneered the use of solar energy in their village before the government program started. = 在政府项目启动之前，他们已经在村里开创了太阳能的使用。


将来时:
Researchers will pioneer new ways to combat climate change. = 研究人员将开创新的方法来应对气候变化。
We believe that this technology will pioneer a new era in communication. = 我们相信这项技术将开创一个新的通信时代。


虚拟语气:
If they had pioneered this technology earlier, we would be in a better position now. = 如果他们早些开创这项技术，我们现在的情况会更好。
I wish I had pioneered this field, but it was already taken. = 我真希望我能开创这个领域，但是它已经被别人占领了。


被动语态:
The new technique was pioneered by a team of young scientists. = 这种新技术是由一组年轻科学家开创的。
The use of renewable energy is being pioneered in many countries. = 许多国家正在开创可再生能源的使用。


疑问句:
Who pioneered the use of antibiotics? = 谁开创了抗生素的使用？
Which company pioneered this revolutionary product? = 哪家公司开创了这款革命性的产品？


否定句:
They did not pioneer this particular technology. = 他们没有开创这项特定技术。
The company failed to pioneer a new market for their product. = 这家公司未能为其产品开创一个新的市场。
