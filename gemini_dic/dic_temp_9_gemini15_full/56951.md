dotting:/ˈdɒtɪŋ/ | v. | 点缀；在…上点小点；溺爱 |  To mark with dots; to decorate with small spots; to excessively indulge or spoil (a child). |例句：She was dotting the "i"s with a flourish. 她用华丽的笔势在“i”上点上小点。 |例句：The parents were dotting on their only child. 父母溺爱着他们的独生子。|近义词：sprinkling, embellishing, spoiling, pampering |反义词：neglecting, ignoring

USAGE->
一般现在时:
She dots the i's carefully. = 她仔细地给i点上小点。
He dots his paintings with bright colors. = 他用鲜艳的颜色点缀他的画作。


一般过去时:
She dotted the line on the form. = 她在表格上画了个点。
He dotted the landscape with small cottages. = 他在风景画中点缀着许多小别墅。


现在完成时:
She has dotted all the i's and crossed all the t's. = 她已经把所有的i都点上了，并且把所有的t都划掉了。（一丝不苟）
He has dotted the entire page with notes. = 他已经在整页纸上都做了笔记。


过去完成时:
She had dotted the map before starting the journey. = 她出发前已在地图上标注了点。
He had dotted the cake with chocolate chips before serving it. = 他在端上蛋糕之前已经用巧克力屑点缀了它。


将来时:
She will dot the fabric with sequins. = 她将用亮片点缀面料。
He will dot the "i"s with a fine pen. = 他将用细笔在“i”上点小点。


虚拟语气:
If she dotted the line, the form would be valid. = 如果她画上这条线，表格就有效了。
If he had dotted the map, they wouldn't have gotten lost. = 如果他在地图上做了标记，他们就不会迷路了。


被动语态:
The page was dotted with ink stains. = 页面上布满了墨水渍。
The cake was dotted with fresh berries. = 蛋糕上点缀着新鲜的浆果。


疑问句:
Is the document dotted with corrections? = 文件上是否有很多修改痕迹？
Are the stars dotted across the night sky? = 星星是否散布在夜空中？


否定句:
She didn't dot the i's. = 她没有给i点上小点。
He didn't dot the line, so the form is invalid. = 他没有在表格上画线，所以表格无效。
