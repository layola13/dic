regearing:/ˌriːˈɡɪərɪŋ/| n. |重新设置齿轮；调整齿轮比；改变策略|The process of changing the gear ratios in a machine, especially an engine or transmission, to optimize performance or efficiency for a different purpose or condition.  It can also refer to a metaphorical change in approach or strategy.|例句：The car's performance improved significantly after regearing.  这辆车的性能在重新设置齿轮后得到了显著提高。|例句：The company's regearing strategy involved focusing on a new market segment.  公司的改变策略包括专注于新的细分市场。|近义词：retooling, refitting, readjustment|反义词：maintaining the status quo|名词
