promising:/ˈprɑːmɪsɪŋ/| adj.|有希望的；有前途的；有希望成功的|Showing signs of future success or promise; likely to be good or successful.|The young scientist is showing promising results in his research.这位年轻的科学家在他的研究中显示出令人鼓舞的结果。|近义词：hopeful, encouraging, auspicious|反义词：discouraging, bleak, unpromising

USAGE->
一般现在时:
This project is promising. = 这个项目很有希望。
The new drug shows promising results. = 这种新药显示出令人鼓舞的结果。

一般过去时:
The initial results were promising. = 初步结果令人鼓舞。
The situation looked promising last week. = 上周形势一片大好。

现在完成时:
The company has had a promising year. = 公司度过了令人鼓舞的一年。
Their efforts have been promising. = 他们的努力已经取得了可喜的成果。

过去完成时:
The forecast had been promising, but the weather changed. = 预报曾很乐观，但天气变了。
Everything had seemed promising until the last minute. = 直到最后一刻，一切看起来都很有希望。

将来时:
The future looks promising for the company. = 公司的未来一片光明。
The upcoming season promises to be promising. = 即将来临的赛季有望取得好成绩。

虚拟语气:
If the results were promising, we would continue the research. = 如果结果令人鼓舞，我们将继续研究。
I wish the situation were more promising. = 我希望情况更有希望。

被动语态:
The project is considered promising by the investors. = 投资者认为该项目很有希望。
The new treatment was described as promising. = 新疗法被描述为很有希望。

疑问句:
Is the situation promising? = 情况乐观吗？
Are the prospects promising? = 前景光明吗？

否定句:
The outlook is not promising. = 前景并不乐观。
The results were not as promising as we had hoped. = 结果不如我们希望的那么乐观。


PHRASE->
a promising start = 一个良好的开端

a promising young artist = 一个有前途的年轻艺术家
a promising future = 一个光明的未来
promising signs = 乐观的迹象
