commerce:/ˈkɒmɜːrs/ /ˈkɑːmɜːrs/| n. |商业，贸易|The exchange of goods and services, especially on a large scale.  It involves the activities related to buying, selling, and transporting goods and services.|例句：E-commerce has revolutionized the way we shop.  电子商务彻底改变了我们的购物方式。
例句：International commerce plays a vital role in global economic growth. 国际贸易在全球经济增长中发挥着至关重要的作用。|近义词：trade, business, industry |反义词：barter (in some contexts)|名词
