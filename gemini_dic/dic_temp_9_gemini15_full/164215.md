predoctorates: /ˌpriːˈdɒktərɪts/ | n. | 预博士学位阶段 |  A period of study or training before a doctoral program, often involving advanced coursework or research.  This term is less common than "pre-doctoral" and often refers to a specific program or period of study rather than the general state of being before a doctorate. | Example:  Several universities offer predoctorate fellowships to support students during their pre-doctoral research.  几个大学提供预博士奖学金来支持学生在预博士研究期间的学习。  |近义词: pre-doctoral studies, pre-doctoral training | 反义词: postdoctoral | 词性: 名词
