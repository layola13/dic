overprotection: /ˌoʊvərprəˈtɛkʃən/ | n. |过度保护|The act of protecting someone or something excessively, often to the point of hindering their development or independence.  It can stifle growth, create dependence, and lead to anxiety and insecurity in the protected individual. |例句：Her overprotection of her son prevented him from developing necessary life skills.  她的过度保护使得她的儿子无法发展必要的生存技能。


例句：The parents' overprotection of their child led to his lack of self-confidence. 父母对孩子的过度保护导致了他缺乏自信。


近义词：excessive protection, sheltering, coddling, pampering |反义词：neglect, abandonment, unconcern


词性：名词
