numbedness:/ˈnʌmbnəs/ | n. | 麻木；感觉迟钝 | A loss of feeling or sensation, typically in a limb, caused by damage to or pressure on nerves.  It can also refer to emotional numbness, a lack of feeling or response. | Example: The cold weather caused numbness in my fingers.  寒冷的天气导致我的手指麻木。  The trauma left her in a state of emotional numbness.  创伤让她陷入情感麻木的状态。 | 近义词：insensitivity, paralysis, deadness | 反义词：sensitivity, feeling, awareness | 名词
