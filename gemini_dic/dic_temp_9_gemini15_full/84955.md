hapless:/ˈhæplɪs/ | adj. | 不幸的；倒霉的；运气不好的 | unfortunate; unlucky; characterized by or experiencing bad luck.  The word describes someone who is consistently experiencing misfortune, often through no fault of their own. It implies a sense of helplessness and vulnerability in the face of adversity. |例句：The hapless hiker got lost in the woods.  =  那个倒霉的徒步旅行者在树林里迷路了。

The hapless victim of the crime was left traumatized. = 犯罪的倒霉受害者受到了创伤。


近义词：unfortunate, unlucky, ill-starred, luckless |反义词：lucky, fortunate, blessed, auspicious
