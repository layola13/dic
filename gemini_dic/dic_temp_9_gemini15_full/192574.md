sculpt:/skʌlpt/| vt. n.|雕刻；塑造；雕塑|To shape (a material) with one's hands or tools; to create something by shaping a material.|例句：The artist skillfully sculpted the clay into a beautiful bird.  艺术家巧妙地将粘土雕塑成一只美丽的鸟。|近义词：carve, mold, shape, fashion|反义词：destroy, demolish

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sculpts beautiful statues. = 他雕刻美丽的雕像。
She sculpts with clay and bronze. = 她用粘土和青铜进行雕塑。

一般过去时:
Michelangelo sculpted the David. = 米开朗基罗雕刻了大卫像。
The artist sculpted a stunning portrait. = 艺术家雕刻了一幅令人惊叹的肖像。

现在完成时:
She has sculpted many works of art. = 她已经创作了许多艺术作品。
He has sculpted for over twenty years. = 他从事雕塑工作已超过二十年。

过去完成时:
They had sculpted the figures before the exhibition. = 他们在展览前已经雕刻好了这些雕像。
The artist had sculpted numerous pieces before finding his unique style. = 在找到自己独特的风格之前，这位艺术家已经创作了许多作品。

将来时:
She will sculpt a new piece next month. = 她下个月将创作一件新的作品。
He will be sculpting at the art fair. = 他将在艺术博览会上进行雕塑创作。

虚拟语气:
If I could sculpt, I would create a masterpiece. = 如果我会雕塑，我会创作一件杰作。
I wish I could sculpt as well as Michelangelo. = 我希望我的雕塑技艺能像米开朗基罗一样精湛。

被动语态:
The statue was sculpted from marble. = 这尊雕像是用大理石雕刻而成的。
The clay was carefully sculpted into a human form. = 粘土被小心地塑造成人形。

疑问句:
Who sculpted this amazing piece? = 谁雕刻了这件令人惊叹的作品？
What materials will be used to sculpt the figure? = 将用什么材料来雕刻这个雕像？

否定句:
He did not sculpt the statue himself. = 这尊雕像不是他自己雕刻的。
She doesn't sculpt anymore. = 她不再进行雕塑创作了。
