captainry:/ˈkæptənri/| n. |船长职位；指挥权；首领地位|The authority or power of a captain; the office or rank of a captain.  It can also refer to the skill or expertise of a captain.|例句：The captainry of the ship was challenged by the mutinous crew.  船长的权威受到了哗变船员的挑战。

例句：His captainry on the field led the team to victory. 他在球场上的指挥能力带领球队取得了胜利。


近义词：command, leadership, authority, mastery

反义词：subordination, followership, obedience


词性：名词
