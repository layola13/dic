resist:/rɪˈzɪst/| vt.  |抵抗，反抗，抵制|to withstand the effect of; oppose|The soldiers resisted the enemy attack.  士兵们抵抗了敌人的进攻。

He resisted the temptation to eat the entire cake. 他抵制住了吃掉整个蛋糕的诱惑。| withstand, oppose, defy | succumb, yield, give in |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She resists all attempts to change her mind. = 她抵抗所有试图改变她主意的人。
They resist the pressure to conform. = 他们抵制了从众的压力。

一般过去时:
The army resisted the invasion for months. = 这支军队抵抗入侵长达数月。
He resisted arrest. = 他拒捕。

现在完成时:
She has resisted the urge to quit her job. = 她一直抵制着辞职的冲动。
They have resisted all attempts at negotiation. = 他们抵制了所有谈判的尝试。

过去完成时:
He had resisted temptation for years before he finally gave in. = 在他最终屈服之前，他已经抵制诱惑多年了。
The city had resisted the siege for many weeks. = 这个城市已经抵抗围困数周了。

将来时:
I will resist any pressure to compromise my values. = 我将抵制任何损害我价值观的压力。
They will resist the new policy. = 他们将会抵制这项新政策。

虚拟语气:
If I had resisted the temptation, I wouldn't be in this mess. = 如果我抵制了诱惑，我就不会陷入这种困境。
I wish I had resisted the urge to spend so much money. = 我真希望我没有抵制住乱花钱的冲动。

被动语态:
The new law was strongly resisted by the public. = 这项新法律受到了公众的强烈抵制。
His suggestions were resisted by the board. = 他的建议遭到了董事会的抵制。

疑问句:
Will you resist the temptation? = 你会抵制诱惑吗？
Do you think they will resist the changes? = 你认为他们会抵制这些变化吗？

否定句:
He didn't resist the urge to laugh. = 他没能忍住笑。
She couldn't resist buying that beautiful dress. = 她忍不住买了那件漂亮的衣服。


PHRASE->
resist temptation = 抵制诱惑
resist arrest = 拒捕
resist change = 抵制改变
resist pressure = 抵制压力
resist the urge = 抵制冲动

词源:
resist源自拉丁语resistere，由re-（加强语气）和sistere（站立，停留）构成，字面意思是“站得住脚，挺住”。
