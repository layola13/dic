micrology:/maɪˈkrɒlədʒi/ | n. | 微观学；显微镜学 | The study of minute objects or structures, usually with the aid of a microscope.  It encompasses various fields, including microbiology, histology, and cytology. | Example:  Her research involved advanced micrology techniques to analyze the cellular structure of the organism.  她的研究涉及先进的显微技术来分析该生物的细胞结构。 | 近义词：microscopy, microscopic study | 反义词：macroscopy, macroscopic study
