crescent:/ˈkrɛsnt/| n.|新月；新月形；增长；增加|A crescent is a shape like a half-moon.  It can refer to the moon itself in its early stages or to objects shaped like a half-moon.|例句：The new moon was a slender crescent in the night sky.  夜空中新月是细细的一弯新月。 The bakery sold delicious crescent rolls. 面包店卖美味的新月形面包。|近义词：half-moon, sickle|反义词：full moon
