goldfields:/ˈɡoʊldfiːldz/ | n. |金矿区，金矿田 |Areas where gold is found and mined.  Goldfields are often characterized by their rugged terrain and history of gold rushes. |Example: The Australian goldfields attracted thousands of prospectors during the gold rush.  澳大利亚的金矿区在淘金热期间吸引了成千上万的淘金者。  The California goldfields were the scene of intense activity in the mid-19th century. 加利福尼亚州的金矿区是19世纪中期剧烈活动的地点。|近义词：gold mines, gold region |反义词：无
