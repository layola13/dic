magnitudes:/ˈmæɡnɪˌtʃuːdz/| n. |量级；大小；重要性|The word "magnitudes" refers to the sizes, extent, or importance of something.  It can be used in various contexts, from the size of a physical object to the significance of an event.  The plural form indicates multiple sizes or levels of importance.|例句：The magnitudes of the earthquakes varied greatly.  地震的震级差异很大。


The magnitudes of the challenges facing the company are immense. 公司面临的挑战规模巨大。|近义词：sizes, amounts, extents, importance, significance|反义词：insignificance, triviality|词性 n.
