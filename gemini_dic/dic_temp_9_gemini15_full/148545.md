oversweetened:/ˌoʊvərˈswiːtn̩d/| adj.|过甜的，甜度过高的| excessively sweet; containing too much sugar.  The cake was oversweetened and cloying.|近义词：too sweet, sugary, saccharine|反义词：unsweetened, undersweetened


例句：

The dessert was oversweetened; it was almost inedible.  甜点太甜了，几乎不能吃。

She found the lemonade oversweetened and diluted it with more water. 她觉得柠檬水太甜了，就加了更多水稀释。


词源:

* **over-**: prefix indicating "too much," "excessive," or "beyond." (来自表示“太多”、“过度”或“超出”的前缀)
* **sweeten**: verb meaning "to make sweet," derived from "sweet" + "-en" (一个动词，意思是“使变甜”，源自“sweet”+“-en” )
