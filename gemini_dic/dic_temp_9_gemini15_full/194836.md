semester:/səˈmɛstər/ | n. |学期，半学年 | A semester is one of the two periods into which an academic year is divided.  |例句：The fall semester begins in September. 秋季学期在九月开始。

例句：This semester's workload is particularly heavy.  本学期的工作量特别大。

近义词：term | 反义词：N/A


词源:  The word "semester" comes from Latin *semestre, meaning "half-year".  It is a combination of the prefix "semi-" (half) and "mester" (a term of indeterminate length, roughly a year).  The modern usage evolved from the medieval university system.
