semiprimitive:/ˌsɛmiˈprɪmətɪv/| adj.| 半原始的；半原始社会的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a society or culture that is partly primitive, showing some but not all features of a primitive society.  It often suggests a transition between a fully primitive state and a more advanced one. | Example: The semiprimitive tools found at the archaeological site suggest an early stage of human development.  这个考古遗址发现的半原始工具表明人类发展的一个早期阶段。| 近义词：partially primitive, underdeveloped, rudimentary | 反义词：advanced, sophisticated, highly developed |
