non-tenant:/ˈnɑːnˈtɛnənt/| adj. n.| 非租户的；非承租人 |Not a tenant; not occupying property as a tenant.  A person who does not rent or lease a property.|The non-tenant was asked to leave the building.  非租户被要求离开大楼。|近义词：non-resident, outsider |反义词：tenant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The non-tenant is not allowed to enter the building. = 非租户不允许进入大楼。
He is a non-tenant and doesn't pay rent. = 他是非租户，不付房租。

一般过去时:
She was a non-tenant last year. = 去年她是非租户。
The non-tenant was evicted from the property. = 非租户被驱逐出该房产。

现在完成时:
They have been non-tenants since they moved. = 自从他们搬家以来，他们就是非租户。
He has remained a non-tenant for many years. = 他多年来一直是非租户。

过去完成时:
Before the renovation, he had been a non-tenant. = 在装修之前，他是非租户。
She had been a non-tenant for a decade before buying her own house. = 在买下自己的房子之前，她已经是非租户十年了。

将来时:
They will remain non-tenants for the foreseeable future. = 在可预见的未来，他们将仍然是非租户。
He will become a non-tenant next month. = 他下个月将成为非租户。

虚拟语气:
If he were a non-tenant, he wouldn't have access. = 如果他是非租户，他将无法进入。
I wish I were a non-tenant and could avoid the rent increase. = 我希望我是个非租户，可以避免租金上涨。

被动语态:
Non-tenants are not permitted on the premises. = 非租户不得进入该处所。
The non-tenant's request was denied. = 非租户的请求被拒绝了。

疑问句:
Is he a non-tenant? = 他是非租户吗？
Are they non-tenants of this apartment building? = 他们是这座公寓楼的非租户吗？

否定句:
He is not a non-tenant; he lives here. = 他不是非租户；他住在这里。
They are not non-tenants; they are long-term residents. = 他们不是非租户；他们是长期居民。
