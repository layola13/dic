re-begin:/ˌriːbɪˈɡɪn/ | v. |重新开始，再开始 | To begin something again after it has been stopped or interrupted.  |例句：After a brief pause, the orchestra re-began playing.  乐队短暂暂停后又重新开始演奏。

例句：He re-began his studies after a year's break. 他休学一年后重新开始学习。

近义词：restart, recommence, resume | 反义词：end, finish, cease


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-begin their work every Monday. = 他们每个星期一重新开始工作。
We re-begin the project after the holiday. = 我们假期后重新开始这个项目。


一般过去时:
The company re-began operations after the fire. = 那家公司火灾后重新开始运营。
She re-began writing her novel after a long break. = 她休息很久后重新开始写她的小说。


现在完成时:
They have re-begun their renovations. = 他们已经重新开始装修了。
We have re-begun our search for a new house. = 我们已经重新开始寻找新房子了。


过去完成时:
He had re-begun his training before the competition. = 他在比赛前已经重新开始训练了。
They had re-begun the meeting after the interruption. = 他们中断后已经重新开始会议了。


将来时:
We will re-begin the discussion tomorrow. = 我们明天将重新开始讨论。
They will re-begin their journey in the spring. = 他们将在春天重新开始他们的旅程。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would re-begin the project. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会重新开始这个项目。
I wish I could re-begin my life. = 我希望我能重新开始我的生活。


被动语态:
The meeting was re-begun after a short delay. = 会议在短暂延误后重新开始了。
The game will be re-begun next week. = 比赛将在下周重新开始。


疑问句:
Will they re-begin the negotiations? = 他们会重新开始谈判吗？
When will the construction be re-begun? = 建设工程什么时候重新开始？


否定句:
They did not re-begin the project. = 他们没有重新开始这个项目。
She won't re-begin her studies this semester. = 她这个学期不会重新开始学习。


PHRASE->
re-begin the conversation:重新开始对话
re-begin the process:重新开始流程
re-begin the task:重新开始任务
re-begin the game:重新开始游戏
re-begin from scratch:从头开始


词源:
re- (prefix indicating repetition or renewal) + begin (verb meaning to start)  re-是表示重复或更新的前缀，begin是动词，意思是开始。
