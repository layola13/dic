hurtle:/ˈhɜːrtl/| v.|猛冲，猛掷，飞驰|To move or cause to move with great speed and force.  The word often implies a lack of control or a sense of danger.|例句：The car hurtled down the hill.  车子飞速冲下山坡。

The runaway train hurtled towards the bridge. 失控的火车猛冲向桥梁。


近义词：rush, dash, fling, speed, shoot|反义词：crawl, creep, meander, drift
