pacha:/ˈpɑːtʃə/| n. |帕恰(印加帝国统治者称号)|A title used by the Inca emperors, meaning roughly "world" or "universe".  The Pachacuti Inca Yupanqui was a famous ruler who used this title.  It also refers to the Inca concept of the world, encompassing both the physical and spiritual realms. |The Pachacuti Inca Yupanqui was a powerful ruler.  帕恰库特克·印卡·尤潘基是一位强大的统治者。|emperor, ruler, sovereign|subject, peasant|名词
