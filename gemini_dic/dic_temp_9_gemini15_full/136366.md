nonrespondents:/ˌnɒn rɪˈspɒndənts/ | n. |未回复者，无回应者 |People who did not respond to a survey, questionnaire, or other request for information.  They are characterized by their lack of participation or feedback. |Example: The researchers analyzed the data from the respondents, but also considered the implications of the high number of nonrespondents.  研究人员分析了受访者的数据，但也考虑了大量未回复者带来的影响。|近义词：nonparticipants,  unrespondents |反义词：respondents
