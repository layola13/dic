reforestations:/ˌriːˌfɒrɪˈsteɪʃənz/ | n. | 再造林；重新造林 | The act or process of replanting forests on lands that have been deforested.  It involves planting trees and other vegetation to restore a forested area. |例句：The government is investing heavily in reforestations programs to combat deforestation.  政府正在大力投资重新造林项目以应对森林砍伐。

例句：Large-scale reforestations efforts are underway in many parts of the world.  世界许多地区正在开展大规模的重新造林工作。


近义词：reforestation, afforestation (afforestation is broader, encompassing planting trees where there were never forests before)

反义词：deforestation


词性:名词
