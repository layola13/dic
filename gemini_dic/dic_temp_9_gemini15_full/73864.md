formule:/ˈfɔːrmjʊli/ /ˈfɔːrmjuːleɪ/ | n. | 公式；配方；程式；客套话 | A formule is a mathematical expression or a set of rules for solving a problem. It can also refer to a recipe or a set of instructions for making something. In a more informal sense, it can describe a fixed or conventional way of doing something. | Example:  The formula for the area of a circle is πr².  公式的计算方法是πr²。

The chemist developed a new formula for the drug.  化学家研制了一种新药的配方。


近义词：equation, recipe, method, procedure, expression

反义词：无
