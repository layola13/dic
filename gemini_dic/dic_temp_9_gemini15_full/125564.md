multitaskings:/ˌmʌltiˈtæskɪŋz/ | n. |多任务处理，多工处理 |The ability to perform multiple tasks at the same time or to switch quickly between tasks.  It often refers to the ability of a computer system or a person. |例句：The multitasking capabilities of the new operating system are impressive.  这个新操作系统的多任务处理能力令人印象深刻。

例句：Her multitasking skills allowed her to manage her work, family, and social life effectively.  她熟练的多任务处理技巧让她有效地兼顾工作、家庭和社交生活。


近义词：parallel processing, concurrent processing, juggling

反义词：single-tasking, focusing on one thing at a time


词源：multi- (many) + task (a piece of work to be done) + -ing (present participle suffix)  表示“多任务处理”的动作或状态。  The word evolved from the computing field and later extended to describe human behavior.
