lustral:/ˈlʌstrəl/| adj.|净化的；用于净化的；赎罪的 | Relating to purification or cleansing, especially by ritual washing or sprinkling.  The word often carries connotations of religious or ceremonial cleansing. |例句：The lustral water was sprinkled on the altar to purify it.  净水洒在祭坛上以净化它。

例句：The priest performed a lustral rite before the ceremony.  仪式前，祭司进行了净化仪式。


近义词：purifying, cleansing, expiatory, cathartic |反义词：defiling, polluting, contaminating |词性：形容词


词源：源自拉丁语 *lustrālis,  来自 lustrāre "to purify by sacrifice or ritual,"  这词本身又源于 lux, lucis "light,"  暗示着净化过程带来光明和纯洁。  The connection lies in the idea that purification removes darkness or impurity, bringing forth light and purity.
