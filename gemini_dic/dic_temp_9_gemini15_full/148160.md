overseriously:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪəriəsli/ | adv. | 过分认真地，过于严肃地 |  Describes an action or behavior that is excessively serious or earnest.  It implies a lack of lightness or humor, often to a detrimental effect. |例句：He took the game overseriously, ruining the fun for everyone. 他对比赛过于认真，弄得大家都没乐趣了。  He overseriously considered every detail, delaying the project. 他过于认真地考虑每一个细节，延误了项目。| 近义词：excessively seriously, overly seriously, too seriously | 反义词：lightly, casually, jokingly | 词性：副词
