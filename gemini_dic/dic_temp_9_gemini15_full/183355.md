reproduced:/ˌriːprəˈduːst/ | v. | 再现；复制；繁殖 | To make or do something again; to create a copy of something; to produce offspring. | 例句：The painting was beautifully reproduced in the book. 这幅画在书中被精美地复制了。


The experiment was reproduced by another research team.  另一个研究团队复制了这个实验。


近义词：duplicate, replicate, copy, recreate | 反义词：destroy, erase, annihilate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reproduces the painting accurately. = 他精确地复制了这幅画。
They reproduce rapidly. = 他们繁殖迅速。


一般过去时:
She reproduced the image on the computer. = 她用电脑复制了图像。
The birds reproduced successfully in their new habitat. = 这些鸟类在新栖息地成功繁殖。


现在完成时:
Scientists have reproduced the experiment several times. = 科学家们已经重复了这个实验好几次。
The company has reproduced thousands of copies of the book. = 这家公司已经复制了数千本这本书。


过去完成时:
The artist had reproduced the masterpiece before his death. = 艺术家在他死前已经复制了这幅杰作。
The plants had reproduced extensively before the drought. = 在干旱之前，这些植物已经大量繁殖。


将来时:
They will reproduce the results in their next report. = 他们将在下一份报告中复制结果。
The farmers expect the crops to reproduce abundantly next year. = 农民们预计明年庄稼会大量繁殖。


虚拟语气:
If they reproduced the experiment correctly, they would get the same results. = 如果他们正确地复制了实验，他们就会得到相同的结果。
I wish I could reproduce the sound of the birdsong. = 我真希望我能重现鸟鸣声。


被动语态:
The music was reproduced with high fidelity. = 音乐以高保真度再现。

疑问句:
Can you reproduce the data from the survey? = 你能复制调查数据吗？
How do these animals reproduce? = 这些动物是如何繁殖的？


否定句:

The plants failed to reproduce in the harsh conditions. = 植物在恶劣的环境中未能繁殖。


PHRASE->
reproduce sexually = 有性繁殖
reproduce asexually = 无性繁殖
reproduce sounds = 再现声音

faithfully reproduce = 忠实地复制


词源:
The word "reproduced" comes from the Latin prefix "re-" (again, back) and the verb "producere" (to bring forth, to lead forth), which itself is composed of "pro-" (forward) and "ducere" (to lead).  Therefore, the core meaning points to bringing something forth again or creating a copy.
