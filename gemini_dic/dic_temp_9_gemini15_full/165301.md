preinterpreting: /ˌpriːɪnˈtɜːrprɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先解释; 预先解读 | To interpret or explain something before it is fully understood or experienced.  This often involves making assumptions or predictions based on limited information. |  The team spent hours preinterpreting the data before the full analysis began.  团队在进行全面分析之前花了数小时预先解读数据。 |  interpreting, analyzing, anticipating |  misinterpreting, misunderstanding | 动词
