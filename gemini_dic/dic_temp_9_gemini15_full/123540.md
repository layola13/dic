monounsaturated: /ˌmɒnəʊˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/ /ˌmɑːnoʊˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 单不饱和的 | Containing one double bond per molecule; referring to fatty acids or fats.  Monounsaturated fats are considered healthier than saturated fats. | Example: Olive oil is a good source of monounsaturated fats.  橄榄油是单不饱和脂肪酸的良好来源。|近义词：unsaturated (in the context of fats)|反义词：saturated, polyunsaturated
