ditsy:/ˈdɪtsi/| adj.|傻乎乎的；轻浮的；不切实际的 | Foolish and flighty; scatterbrained; giddy.  It often describes someone who is charming but impractical and easily distracted. | 例句：She's a bit ditsy, always losing her keys.  她有点傻乎乎的，总是丢钥匙。

She wore a ditsy floral dress. 她穿着一件印有碎花的轻浮的连衣裙。| 近义词：flighty, scatterbrained, giddy, airheaded, frivolous | 反义词：sensible, practical, grounded, serious
