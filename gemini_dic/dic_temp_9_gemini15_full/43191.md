crabber:/ˈkræbər/| n. |爱抱怨的人，脾气坏的人|A crabber is a person who is habitually grumpy, complaining, or irritable.  They often express dissatisfaction or negativity. |例句：He's such a crabber; he complains about everything. 他是个爱抱怨的人；他什么都抱怨。|例句：She became a real crabber after her retirement. 她退休后变得脾气很坏。|近义词：grumbler, complainer, grouch, cynic |反义词：optimist, happy person, cheerful person


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He crabs about the weather every day. = 他每天都抱怨天气。
She constantly crabs about her job. = 她不断抱怨她的工作。

一般过去时:
He crabbed about the service at the restaurant. = 他抱怨餐厅的服务。
She crabbed at her children for making a mess. = 她因为孩子们弄脏了东西而对他们发脾气。

现在完成时:
He has crabbed about this problem for weeks. = 他已经抱怨这个问题几周了。
She has crabbed at me all day. = 她整天都在对我发脾气。

过去完成时:
He had crabbed about the project before the meeting even started. = 会议还没开始，他就已经抱怨这个项目了。
She had crabbed at him repeatedly before he finally snapped. = 她反复对他发脾气，直到他最终爆发。

将来时:
He will crab about the traffic if it's delayed again. = 如果交通再次延误，他会抱怨交通。
She will crab at her boss if she doesn't get a raise. = 如果她没有加薪，她会对她老板发脾气。

虚拟语气:
If he didn't crab so much, people would like him better. = 如果他不那么爱抱怨，人们会更喜欢他。
I wish she wouldn't crab at me all the time. = 我希望她不要总是对我发脾气。

被动语态:  (This word is rarely used passively)
His complaints were crabbed at the meeting.  = 他的抱怨在会议上被斥责了。(This is an awkward construction)
His negativity was often crabbed by his colleagues. = 他的消极情绪经常被同事们批评。(This uses "crabbed" in a different, less common sense)


疑问句:
Is he always such a crabber? = 他总是这么爱抱怨吗？
Does she crab about everything? = 她什么都抱怨吗？

否定句:
He doesn't crab as much as he used to. = 他不像以前那么爱抱怨了。
She didn't crab at all during the meeting. = 会议期间她一点也没有抱怨。


PHRASE->
a crabber of a person = 一个爱抱怨的人
old crabber = 老爱抱怨的人
a grumpy crabber = 一个脾气暴躁的爱抱怨的人
crabber's attitude = 爱抱怨的态度
professional crabber = 职业抱怨者 (讽刺说法)
