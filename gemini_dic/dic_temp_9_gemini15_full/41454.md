cornicle:/ˈkɔːrnɪkl/ | n. |角状突起；小角| A small horn-like projection, especially on an insect or plant.  |例句：The aphid's cornicles secreted a sweet liquid.  蚜虫的角状突起分泌出甜液。  |例句：The plant's cornicles were covered in tiny hairs. 植物的角状突起上覆盖着细小的绒毛。|近义词：tubercle, projection, process|反义词：无
