loudhailers:/ˈlaʊdˌheɪlər/| n. |扩音器，喇叭|A loudhailer is a device for making one's voice louder, often used for public announcements or addressing a crowd.  It amplifies sound, allowing a speaker to be heard over a greater distance.|例句：The protesters used a loudhailer to make their demands heard.  抗议者使用扩音器来表达他们的诉求。|例句：The politician addressed the crowd through a loudhailer.  这位政治家通过扩音器向人群讲话。|近义词：megaphone, amplifier, speaker|反义词：whisper, murmur
