nutraceutical:/ˌnuːtrəˈsuːtɪkəl/| n. |营养保健品，功能性食品| a food or food product that provides health benefits beyond basic nutrition.  Nutraceuticals are often used to prevent or treat diseases. | Example:  Many people take nutraceuticals to improve their overall health.  许多人服用营养保健品来改善他们的整体健康状况。|近义词：functional food, dietary supplement|反义词：unhealthy food, junk food
