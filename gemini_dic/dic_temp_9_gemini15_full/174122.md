quayside:/ˈkiːsaɪd/| n. |码头; 岸边 |The area along the side of a quay or harbor.  It refers to the land immediately adjacent to a quay, where ships dock and unload cargo.  |例句：We strolled along the quayside, watching the boats come and go. 我们沿着码头散步，看着船只来来往往。

例句：The quayside was bustling with activity as workers loaded crates onto ships. 码头上熙熙攘攘，工人们正把箱子装上船。

近义词：dockside, waterfront, wharf

反义词：None directly opposite in meaning, but words like "inland" or "upstream" could suggest the opposite location.
