proceeded:/prəˈsiːdəd/ | v. |继续进行；开始；着手；发出；源于 | To continue or move forward after a pause or interruption; to begin or start something; to take action; to originate from. |例句：After a brief pause, the speaker proceeded with his presentation.  短暂的停顿后，演讲者继续他的演讲。

例句：The construction workers proceeded to demolish the old building. 建筑工人开始拆除旧楼。


近义词：continued, went on, commenced, advanced, followed | 反义词：stopped, ceased, halted, paused


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He proceeds with caution. = 他谨慎地继续。
She proceeds to the next step. = 她继续进行下一步。


一般过去时:
The meeting proceeded smoothly. = 会议顺利进行。
He proceeded to explain his plan. = 他开始解释他的计划。


现在完成时:
They have proceeded with the investigation. = 他们已经继续调查。
She has proceeded to the final round. = 她已经进入决赛。


过去完成时:
They had proceeded to the next chapter before I arrived. = 我到达之前，他们已经继续到下一章了。
He had proceeded with his work before the interruption. = 在被打扰之前，他已经继续他的工作了。


将来时:
We will proceed with the project next week. = 我们下周将继续进行该项目。
She will proceed to the airport. = 她将前往机场。


虚拟语气:
If he had proceeded more carefully, the accident might not have happened. = 如果他更小心地继续，事故可能就不会发生了。
I wish I had proceeded differently. = 我希望我采取不同的做法。


被动语态:
The matter was proceeded with swiftly. = 这件事迅速地得到处理。
The case is being proceeded with diligently. = 此案正在认真处理中。


疑问句:
How did you proceed with the experiment? = 你是如何进行实验的？
What will you proceed to do next? = 你接下来将做什么？


否定句:
He did not proceed with the plan. = 他没有继续执行计划。
They did not proceed to the next stage. = 他们没有进入下一阶段。


PHRASE->
proceed with = 继续进行…
proceed to = 开始…；接着…
proceed against = 对…提起诉讼
proceed from = 源于；出自
proceed as planned = 按计划进行


词源:
The word "proceed" comes from the Old French word "proceder," which itself originates from the Latin word "procedere," meaning "to go forward," "to advance," or "to come forth."  The Latin word is formed from the prefix "pro-" (forward) and "cedere" (to go, yield).
