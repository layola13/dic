rockstar:/ˈrɒkˌstɑːr/|/ˈrɑːkˌstɑːr/| n. |摇滚明星，巨星|A rockstar is a very famous and successful rock musician.  The term often implies a lifestyle of fame, fortune, and excess.|例句：He dreams of becoming a rockstar and playing sold-out stadiums. 他梦想成为一名摇滚明星，在爆满的体育场演出。

He's a rockstar in his own field, a true innovator. 他在自己领域里是个巨星，一位真正的创新者。|近义词：celebrity, superstar, icon|反义词：nobody, unknown
