nimblest: /ˈnɪmbləst/ | adj. |最敏捷的，最灵活的 | Most agile or quick-moving.  It is the superlative form of "nimble".|例句：The nimblest dancer captivated the audience with her effortless grace. 最敏捷的舞者以她毫不费力的优雅征服了观众。

The cheetah, the nimblest of all land animals, can reach speeds of up to 75 mph.  猎豹，所有陆地动物中最敏捷的，时速可达75英里。|近义词：most agile, most dexterous, most spry, quickest |反义词：clumsiest, slowest, most awkward
