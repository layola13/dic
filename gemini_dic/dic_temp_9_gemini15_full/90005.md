horticulturists:/ˌhɔːrtɪˈkʌltʃərɪsts/ | n. | 园艺学家 | A horticulturist is a specialist in horticulture, the science and art of cultivating plants, especially fruits, vegetables, and flowers.  They may work in research, education, or commercial settings, applying scientific principles to improve plant growth and yield. | Example: Many horticulturists are involved in developing new varieties of roses.  许多园艺学家都参与了培育新品种玫瑰的工作。  The horticulturist advised on the best soil and fertilizer for the garden. 园艺学家就花园最好的土壤和肥料提出了建议。|近义词：gardener, cultivator, plant specialist | 反义词：None specifically.
