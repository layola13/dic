overlies:/ˈoʊvərˌlaɪz/ | v. |覆盖；位于…之上 |  To lie on or be situated above something else.  |例句：The sedimentary rock overlies the igneous rock. 沉积岩覆盖在火成岩之上。
The carpet overlies the wooden floor. 地毯覆盖在木地板上。|近义词：covers, superimposes, rests upon |反义词：underlies

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The blanket overlies the bed. = 毛毯盖在床上。
The older layer of rock overlies the younger layer. = 更古老的岩层覆盖在更年轻的岩层之上。

一般过去时:
The snow overlied the town. = 雪覆盖了城镇。
The painting overlied the original sketch. = 画覆盖了最初的素描。

现在完成时:
The ice has overlied the lake all winter. = 冰整个冬天都覆盖着湖面。

过去完成时:
The floodwaters had overlied the entire village. = 洪水已经淹没了整个村庄。

将来时:

The new building will overlie the old foundations. = 新建筑将建在旧地基之上。

虚拟语气:
