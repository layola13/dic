on-the-up:/ɒn ði ʌp/| adj.|上升的；进步的；兴旺的；走运的|Improving; progressing; thriving; experiencing good fortune.  The phrase describes something that is improving or getting better, often referring to a person's career, business, or general circumstances.|例句：His business is on the up.他的生意正在蓬勃发展。

例句：Things are on the up for her since she got the promotion.自从她升职以来，她的情况越来越好。


近义词：improving, progressing, thriving, flourishing, prosperous, successful


反义词：declining, worsening, failing, deteriorating, unsuccessful


词性：形容词
