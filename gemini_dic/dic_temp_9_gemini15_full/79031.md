give-back:/ɡɪvˈbæk/| n. v.|回馈，归还|The act of returning something or giving something in return;  to return something to its rightful owner or place; to contribute to a cause or community.|例句：The company's give-back program supports local charities. 公司的回馈计划支持当地慈善机构。|例句：Please give back the book you borrowed. 请把借的书还回来。|近义词：return, repay, contribute, donate|反义词：keep, withhold, take, seize|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They give back to the community through volunteering. = 他们通过志愿服务回馈社区。
The company gives back a percentage of its profits to charity. = 这家公司将部分利润捐献给慈善机构。

一般过去时:
He gave back the money he had found. = 他把捡到的钱还了回去。
She gave back the borrowed tools after she finished the job. = 她完成工作后归还了借来的工具。

现在完成时:
We have given back more than we’ve taken. = 我们已经付出超过我们所获得的。
They have given back to their alma mater for many years. = 他们多年来一直回馈母校。

过去完成时:
They had given back the lost wallet before the owner even noticed it was missing. = 在失主发现钱包丢失之前，他们已经归还了。
She had given back the book to the library before the due date. = 她在到期日前已将书还给了图书馆。

将来时:
I will give back the car tomorrow. = 我明天会把车还回来。
We will give back to the environment by planting trees. = 我们将通过植树来回馈环境。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would give back more to charity. = 如果我有更多的钱，我会向慈善机构捐赠更多。
I wish I could give back more time to my family. = 我希望我能多花些时间陪伴家人。


被动语态:
The stolen items were given back to their owners. = 被盗物品被归还给了失主。
The money will be given back to the rightful owner. = 钱将被还给 rightful owner。


疑问句:
Will you give back the money you owe me? = 你会还你欠我的钱吗？
When will the borrowed equipment be given back? = 借来的设备什么时候归还？


否定句:
He didn't give back the book. = 他没有还书。
They refused to give back the money. = 他们拒绝还钱。


PHRASE->
give back to the community = 回馈社区
give something back =  归还某物
give back one's time = 奉献时间
give back control =  交还控制权
give back the favor =  回报恩情
