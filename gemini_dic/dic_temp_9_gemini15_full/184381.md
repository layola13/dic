respliced:/riːˈspaɪst/| v.|重新拼接，重新接合|To join or connect something again after it has been separated or cut.  It implies a previous splicing action has been undone and then redone.|例句：The film was respliced after a section was damaged.  电影损坏了一部分后被重新拼接。  The broken DNA strand was respliced in the lab.  断裂的DNA链在实验室里被重新拼接。|近义词：rejoin, reconnect, mend, repair|反义词：sever, disconnect, separate
