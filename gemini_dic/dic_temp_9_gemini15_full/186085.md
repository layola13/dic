rezoned: /ˌriːˈzoʊnd/ | v. | 重新分区，重新划区 | To divide (an area) into new zones, especially for different purposes (such as residential, commercial, or industrial use).  | The city council rezoned the area to allow for the construction of high-rise buildings.  城市议会重新划分了该区域，允许建造高层建筑。 | redistrict, remap |  (无明确反义词，但可以考虑"leave unchanged" 或 "maintain the existing zoning") | 动词
