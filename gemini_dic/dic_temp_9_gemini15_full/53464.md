disarmament:/dɪˈsɑːrməmənt/ | n. |裁军；解除武装 | The act of reducing or eliminating weapons and armed forces.  It often refers to international agreements or policies aimed at limiting military capabilities. |例句：The conference focused on nuclear disarmament.  会议的重点是核裁军。|例句：The country's disarmament program was met with skepticism. 该国的裁军计划受到了怀疑。|近义词：demilitarization, demilitarisation, arms reduction |反义词：armament, militarization
