crannog:/ˈkrænɒɡ/| n.|湖中居，湖中建筑|An artificial island in a lake or loch, often used as a dwelling, typically of timber, wattle, and clay, and sometimes surrounded by a palisade.|例句：The crannog was remarkably well-preserved, giving archaeologists a rare glimpse into ancient life.  这个湖中居保存得非常好，让考古学家难得一窥古代生活。  |例句：  Researchers discovered evidence of a crannog dating back to the Iron Age. 研究人员发现了可追溯到铁器时代的湖中居的证据。|近义词：lake dwelling, artificial island|反义词：None.
