openbill:/ˈoʊpənˌbɪl/ | n. |非洲秃鹳 |A large African bird with a long, broad bill that is open at the end.  They are scavengers known for their distinctive appearance. |Example: The openbill stork is a common sight in the wetlands of Africa.  非洲秃鹳在非洲湿地很常见。|近义词：African openbill, saddle-billed stork (related species)|反义词：None
