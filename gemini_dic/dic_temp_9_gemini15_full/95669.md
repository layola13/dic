inconceivability: /ˌɪnkənˈsiːvəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不可想象性，不可思议 | The state or quality of being inconceivable; something that is impossible to imagine or believe.  It refers to the inability to comprehend or grasp something due to its extreme improbability or strangeness. |例句：The sheer inconceivability of time travel boggles the mind. 时间旅行的不可思议性令人难以置信。

例句：The inconceivability of his sudden death shocked everyone. 他突然去世的不可思议震惊了所有人。


近义词：unimaginability, implausibility, incredibility

反义词：conceivability, plausibility, credibility
