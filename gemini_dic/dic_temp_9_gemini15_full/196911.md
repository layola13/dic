shadoof:/ʃəˈduːf/| n. |灌溉用的长柄提水器|A shadoof is a type of water-lifting device used for irrigation, consisting of a long pole balanced on a support, with a bucket on one end and a counterweight on the other.  It's a simple but effective way to lift water from a lower level to a higher one.  |例句：The farmer used a shadoof to water his crops. 农民用灌溉用的长柄提水器浇灌庄稼。
