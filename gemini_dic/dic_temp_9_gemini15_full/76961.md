garbaging:/ˈɡɑːrbɪdʒɪŋ/| v.|垃圾处理；乱扔垃圾；胡乱处理|The act of treating something carelessly or discarding it without proper consideration.  It often implies a lack of respect or value for the discarded item or information.|例句：He accused the company of garbaging important data. 他指责该公司乱扔重要的数据。
例句：The city council is cracking down on street garbaging. 市议会正在严厉打击乱扔垃圾的行为。|近义词：discarding, dumping, trashing, junking|反义词：preserving, cherishing, valuing

词源:
The word "garbaging" is derived from the noun "garbage," meaning waste or refuse.  The verb form "-ing" is added to indicate the action of treating something as garbage or discarding it.  The word "garbage" itself has origins in Old French "gargabel," likely from a medieval Italian word.
