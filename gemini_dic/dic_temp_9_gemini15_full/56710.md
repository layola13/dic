doomscrolling: /ˈduːmˌskroʊlɪŋ/ | n. | 持续滚动浏览负面新闻或社交媒体，即使感到沮丧或焦虑 |  The act of compulsively scrolling through negative news or social media feeds, even when it causes distress or anxiety.  It's characterized by a feeling of helplessness and a morbid curiosity that keeps users engaged despite the negative impact. | Example:  After hearing about the accident, she spent hours doomscrolling, reading every disturbing detail online. = 听到事故后，她花了好几个小时不停地浏览负面新闻，阅读网上每一个令人不安的细节。  |  近义词：  obsessive scrolling, compulsive browsing, negative news consumption | 反义词：  mindful media consumption, positive news seeking |  名词
