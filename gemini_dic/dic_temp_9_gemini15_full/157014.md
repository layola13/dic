pillowlike: /ˈpɪloʊlaɪk/ | adj. | 像枕头的；柔软舒适的 | Resembling a pillow in softness, shape, or comfort.  The word combines "pillow" and the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance. | 例句：The clouds were pillowlike and fluffy.  云朵像枕头一样柔软蓬松。

The soft, pillowlike cushions made the couch incredibly comfortable. 柔软舒适的枕头般的靠垫使沙发非常舒适。


近义词：cushionlike, soft, fluffy, downy, comfortable


反义词：hard, stiff, uncomfortable, rigid


词性:形容词
