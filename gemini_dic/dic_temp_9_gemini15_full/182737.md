repanelling:/ˌriːˈpænəlɪŋ/| n. |重新镶板；重新安装面板|The act of replacing or reinstalling panels, typically in a structure or vehicle.  This involves removing old panels and fitting new ones. Repanelling can be applied to various things, from walls and doors to car dashboards and boat hulls.|例句：The old kitchen needed repanelling to improve its appearance and functionality. 老厨房需要重新镶板以改善其外观和功能。

The extensive repanelling of the aircraft took several weeks.  飞机的广泛重新安装面板花了几个星期。|近义词：repaneling, refitting, renovation|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be leaving the panels as they are.
