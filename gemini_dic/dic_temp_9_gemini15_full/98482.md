installer:/ɪnˈstɔːlər/ | n. |安装程序，安装人员 | A person or thing that installs something, such as software or hardware.  An installer can refer to a program that guides the user through the process of installing software, or a person who physically installs hardware. | Example: The software installer guided me through the setup process. 安装程序指导我完成了设置过程。  The installer connected the new air conditioning unit. 安装人员连接了新的空调装置。 |近义词：setup program, installation program, technician (for hardware) | 反义词：uninstaller, remover


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The installer checks the system requirements. = 安装程序检查系统要求。
He installs software daily. = 他每天安装软件。


一般过去时:
The installer completed the setup. = 安装程序完成了设置。
She installed the new printer yesterday. = 她昨天安装了新打印机。


现在完成时:
We have installed the new operating system. = 我们已经安装了新的操作系统。
He has finished installing all the necessary drivers. = 他已经完成了所有必要驱动程序的安装。


过去完成时:
