grimacing: /ɡrɪˈmeɪsɪŋ/ | v. | 做鬼脸；痛苦地扭曲着脸 | Making a face showing disgust, pain, or disapproval.  | He grimaced at the bitter taste of the medicine. 他对药的苦味做了个鬼脸。| 近义词：wincing, scowling | 反义词：smiling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grimaces every time he eats broccoli. = 他每次吃西兰花都做鬼脸。
She grimaces when she hears that song. = 她听到那首歌就做鬼脸。

一般过去时:
He grimaced at the sight of the accident. = 他看到事故现场皱起了眉头。
She grimaced in pain after falling. = 她摔倒后痛苦地皱起了脸。

现在完成时:
He has grimaced throughout the entire movie. = 他在整部电影中一直都在做鬼脸。
She has grimaced since she bit into the lemon. = 从她咬了一口柠檬开始，她就一直皱着眉头。


过去完成时:
He had grimaced all day because of his headache. = 他因为头痛而整天都愁眉苦脸。
She had grimaced before she finally spoke. = 在她最终开口说话之前，她一直愁眉苦脸。

将来时:

She will grimace if she has to eat that again. = 如果她不得不再次吃那个，她会做鬼脸。
