duettino:/ˌduɛˈtiːnoʊ/| n. |小二重奏；小型二重唱|A duettino is a short duet, typically for two voices or instruments.  It's often a lighter, less elaborate piece than a full-scale duet. | Example: The opera concluded with a charming duettino between the soprano and tenor.  这部歌剧以女高音和男高音之间一段动听的小二重奏结束。|近义词：short duet, miniature duet |反义词：full-scale duet, opera.
