nursery:/ˈnɜːrsəri/| n. |托儿所；苗圃；（植物的）培育室 |A nursery is a place where young children are cared for while their parents are at work or otherwise occupied.  It can also refer to a place where plants are grown. |例句：My child attends a nursery near our home. 我的孩子在我们家附近的一家托儿所上学。

例句：The gardener carefully tended the plants in the nursery. 园丁仔细地照料苗圃里的植物。

近义词：creche, kindergarten, plant nursery

反义词：school (for older children)
