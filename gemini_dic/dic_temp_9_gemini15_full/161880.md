praeoral:/ˌpriːˈɔːrəl/| adj. |口前的，在口之前的| Situated or occurring before the mouth.  The term is primarily used in zoology and comparative anatomy, referring to structures or processes located anterior to the mouth opening.  |例句：The praeoral tentacles of the jellyfish are used for sensing prey.  水母的口前触手用于感知猎物。|近义词：preoral, anterior (in relation to the mouth)|反义词：postoral
