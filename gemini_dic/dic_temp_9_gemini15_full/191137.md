scapus: /ˈskeɪpəs/ | n. | 花茎；柄；轴 |  The stalk of a plant, especially one that bears a flower or inflorescence, but is not clearly differentiated from the stem.  A scape is a peduncle that arises from the ground, not from a stem. | 例句：The daffodil has a long, slender scape.  水仙花有一根又长又细的花茎。


The scape of this plant supports a single flower. 这株植物的花茎支撑着一朵花。


近义词：peduncle, stalk, stem | 反义词：无  | 词性：名词


词源：源自拉丁语 *scapus，意为“茎干；柄”。
