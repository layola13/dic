plasticizing: /ˈplæstɪsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 增塑；使塑化 |  The process of making a substance more plastic or pliable, typically by adding a plasticizer.  This often involves increasing flexibility, reducing brittleness, and improving workability. |

例句：

The manufacturer plasticized the vinyl to make it more flexible.  = 制造商对乙烯基进行了增塑处理，使其更具柔韧性。

Plasticizing the clay allowed the sculptor to mold it more easily. = 对粘土进行塑化处理使雕塑家更容易塑造它。


近义词：softening, rendering pliable, making flexible


反义词：hardening, solidifying, making brittle


词性：动词
