hemmed:/hɛmd/ | v. | 包边；限制；犹豫；|To sew a hem on (a piece of cloth). To surround and restrict the movement or progress of something. To hesitate or be unable to decide.|例句：She hemmed the dress carefully. 她仔细地给连衣裙缝了边。 The mountains hemmed the valley. 群山环绕着山谷。 |近义词：edged, bordered, confined, hesitated |反义词：unhemmed, opened, unrestricted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She hems her own clothes. = 她自己缝衣服边。
He hems and haws before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前犹豫不决。


一般过去时:
I hemmed the skirt last night. = 我昨晚给裙子缝了边。
They hemmed the prisoner in. = 他们把囚犯围住了。


现在完成时:
She has hemmed all the napkins for the wedding. = 她已经为婚礼缝好了所有的餐巾。
The city has been hemmed in by expanding suburbs. = 城市已经被不断扩张的郊区包围了。


过去完成时:

将来时:

The new development will hem in the existing park. = 新的开发项目将包围现有的公园。


虚拟语气:

否定句:
