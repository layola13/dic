puppetman:/ˈpʌpɪtmæn/| n. |木偶人，操纵木偶的人|A puppetman is a person who operates puppets, often in a theatrical performance.  It can also refer to someone who manipulates or controls others. |Example: The puppetman skillfully manipulated the marionettes, bringing them to life.  操纵木偶的人熟练地操纵着木偶，使它们栩栩如生。  The CEO was accused of being a puppetman, controlling his employees through fear.  首席执行官被指控是一个操纵者，通过恐惧来控制他的员工。|近义词：puppeteer, manipulator, controller |反义词：puppet (in the sense of being controlled)
