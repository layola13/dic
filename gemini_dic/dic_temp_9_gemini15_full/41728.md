corrodiate:/kəˈroʊdieɪt/ | v. | 证实；确证 | To confirm or verify the truth or accuracy of something.  | Example:  The witness's testimony corroborated the suspect's alibi. 目击证人的证词证实了嫌疑人的不在场证明。


The evidence corroborated his story.证据证实了他的说法。


近义词：confirm, verify, substantiate, support | 反义词：contradict, refute, deny | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The witness corroborates the suspect's alibi. 目击证人证实了嫌疑人的不在场证明。
Scientific data corroborates the theory. 科学数据证实了这个理论。


一般过去时:
The experiment corroborated the hypothesis. 实验证实了假设。
Historical records corroborated the legend. 历史记录证实了这个传说。


现在完成时:
Recent studies have corroborated earlier findings. 最近的研究证实了早期的发现。
Multiple sources have corroborated the report. 多个来源证实了这份报告。


过去完成时:
Further investigation had corroborated the initial findings. 进一步的调查证实了最初的发现。
The newly discovered documents had corroborated the historical narrative. 新发现的文件证实了历史叙述。


将来时:
Future research will corroborate or refute these findings. 未来的研究将证实或反驳这些发现。
We expect the upcoming data to corroborate our predictions. 我们预计即将到来的数据将证实我们的预测。


虚拟语气:
If more evidence corroborated the claim, we could proceed. 如果有更多证据证实这一说法，我们就可以继续进行。
It would be helpful if independent researchers corroborated the results. 如果独立的研究人员证实了结果，那将很有帮助。


被动语态:
His alibi was corroborated by several witnesses. 他的不在场证明得到了几位证人的证实。
The theory is corroborated by extensive research. 这一理论得到了大量研究的证实。


疑问句:
Does the evidence corroborate the defendant's story? 证据是否证实了被告的说法？
Will this new data corroborate our hypothesis? 这项新数据会证实我们的假设吗？


否定句:
The witness testimony did not corroborate the police report. 证人的证词并未证实警方的报告。
The results failed to corroborate the initial findings. 结果未能证实最初的发现。


PHRASE->
corroborate evidence 证实证据
corroborate a statement 证实一份声明
corroborate findings 证实研究结果
corroborate a theory 证实一个理论
corroborate a claim 证实一个说法


词源:
From Latin *corroboratus, past participle of corroborare "to strengthen," from cor- "together" + robur "strength, oak."  The original meaning implied strengthening or supporting something, which evolved into the sense of confirming or verifying.
