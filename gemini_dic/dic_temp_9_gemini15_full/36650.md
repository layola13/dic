college:/ˈkɒlɪdʒ/ /ˈkɑːlɪdʒ/| n. |学院，大学；学院；（某些）专科学校|A college is an institution of higher education that awards degrees or certificates.  Colleges typically offer undergraduate programs, and some also offer graduate programs.  |例句：She's attending college in California. 她正在加利福尼亚州上大学。|例句：He graduated from a prestigious college. 他毕业于一所著名的大学。|近义词：university, institute, academy|反义词：school (in the sense of primary or secondary education)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He attends college. = 他上大学。
She studies at a community college. = 她在社区大学学习。

一般过去时:
I graduated from college last year. = 我去年大学毕业。
They went to college together. = 他们一起上大学。

现在完成时:
She has been attending college for three years. = 她已经上大学三年了。
He has completed his college degree. = 他已经完成了他的大学学位。

过去完成时:
I had already finished college before I got married. = 我结婚前就已经大学毕业了。
They had applied to several colleges before receiving an acceptance. = 他们在收到录取通知书之前已经申请了几所大学。

将来时:
I will go to college next year. = 我明年将上大学。
She will graduate from college in May. = 她五月将从大学毕业。

虚拟语气:
If I had gone to college, I would have a better job. = 如果我上了大学，我会有一份更好的工作。
I wish I had gone to college. = 我真希望我上了大学。

被动语态:
The college was founded in 1850. = 这所大学成立于1850年。

疑问句:

Which college did she apply to? = 她申请了哪所大学？

否定句:
He didn't go to college. = 他没上大学。
