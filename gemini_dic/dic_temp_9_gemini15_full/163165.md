precomparing:/ˌpriːkəmˈpɛərɪŋ/| v.|预先比较，事先比较 |The act of comparing things before a decision is made.  It involves evaluating options or data in advance to inform a subsequent choice or action. |例句：Precomparing different investment strategies is crucial before making a decision. 预先比较不同的投资策略在做决定之前至关重要。

例句：The team engaged in precomparing the various project proposals to identify the most feasible option. 团队参与了对各种项目提案的预先比较，以确定最可行的方案。


近义词：pre-evaluation, advance comparison, prior assessment

反义词：post-comparison, retrospective analysis


词性:动词
