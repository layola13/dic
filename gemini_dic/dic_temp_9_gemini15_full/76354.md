gaddingly:/ˈɡædɪŋli/| adv.|闲逛地；嬉戏地；漫无目的地|Moving about in a carefree or aimless manner; idly or frivolously.|例句：They were gaddingly about the town, enjoying the summer evening. 他们漫无目的地在城里闲逛，享受夏日的夜晚。  例句：She spent the afternoon gaddingly through the shops, buying nothing of importance. 她下午漫无目的地逛商店，什么也没买。|近义词：casually, aimlessly, idly, frivolously|反义词：purposefully, deliberately, seriously
