priested:/ˈpriːstɪd/| v. |使成为神父；授予圣职|To ordain someone as a priest.|例句：He was priested at a young age. 他年轻时就受了圣职。|近义词：ordained, consecrated|反义词：defrocked, unfrocked


USAGE->

The church priests new candidates every year. 教堂每年都会为新的候选人举行圣职授任仪式。
