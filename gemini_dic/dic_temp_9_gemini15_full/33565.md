ciggy's:/ˈsɪɡiz/| n. |香烟（复数）；某人的香烟|A shortened, informal plural form of "cigarette".  It often implies possession, like "those are John's ciggies".|例句：He lit one of his ciggies. 他点燃了一根他的香烟。

例句：Those are my ciggy's, don't touch them! 那是我的香烟，别碰！


近义词：cigarettes, smokes, fags (British English)


反义词：无


词性：名词
