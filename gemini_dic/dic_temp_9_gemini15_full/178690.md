recross:/ˌriːˈkrɒs/ | vt. | 再横过，重新越过 | To cross again; to cross over or through something a second time. |例句：He recrossed the river to retrieve his lost hat. 他再次过河去取回他丢失的帽子。

例句：After reaching the other side, she recrossed the bridge to ensure she hadn't left anything behind. 到达对岸后，她又返回桥上，确保自己没有落下任何东西。


近义词：repass, cross again, recross, return across | 反义词： remain on one side, stay put
