headiness:/ˈhedinɪs/| n.|轻率；鲁莽；得意忘形；兴奋；醉意|The quality or state of being heady; characterized by or exhibiting a lack of caution or judgment; impetuousness; recklessness; exhilaration; intoxication.|例句：The headiness of youth led him to make some poor choices. 青年时代轻率的举动使他做出了一些糟糕的选择。

例句：His headiness after winning the race was evident in his boastful behavior. 他赢得比赛后的得意忘形在他的夸夸其谈的行为中显而易见。


近义词：recklessness, impetuosity, rashness, exuberance, intoxication|反义词：caution, prudence, deliberation, sobriety|词性:名词


词源:源自于形容词heady，其含义为“令人兴奋的；使人头晕的”，最终源自于古英语heafod，意为“头部”。  “headiness”则是在这个基础上发展而来的名词形式，强调的是这种“兴奋”或“轻率”的状态。
