purgatives:/ˈpɜːrɡətɪvz/ | n. |泻药；通便剂 | Substances that cause evacuation of the bowels.  Purgatives are used to treat constipation and cleanse the bowel before medical procedures.  |Example: The doctor prescribed strong purgatives to relieve the patient's severe constipation.  医生开了强效泻药来缓解病人的严重便秘。

The patient was given purgatives before the colonoscopy. 病人在结肠镜检查前服用了泻药。|近义词：laxatives, cathartics |反义词：constipants (物质导致便秘)
