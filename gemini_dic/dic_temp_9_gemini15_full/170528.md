psammite:/ˈsæmaɪt/| n. |砂岩|A sedimentary rock consisting predominantly of sand-sized grains.  Psammites are classified based on the composition of the sand grains (e.g., quartzose psammite, arkose).|例句：The geologist identified the sample as a quartzose psammite.  地质学家将样本鉴定为石英砂岩。

例句：The cliff face was composed largely of psammite, easily eroded by the sea.  悬崖峭壁主要由易受海浪侵蚀的砂岩构成。


近义词：sandstone|反义词：shale, mudstone


词源:From Greek *psammos "sand".  The suffix "-ite" is a common suffix used to form nouns denoting rocks and minerals.
