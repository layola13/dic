non-selection:/ˌnɒn.sɪˈlɛkʃən/| n. |非选择，未选择 |The absence of selection or the state of not being selected.  It often implies a passive or accidental lack of choice, rather than an active decision against selection. | Example: The non-selection of the candidate surprised many.  许多人对候选人未被选中感到惊讶。 The non-selection of that particular treatment option was due to a lack of available resources.  未选择该特定治疗方案是由于缺乏可用资源。 |近义词：omission, exclusion, neglect |反义词：selection, choice
