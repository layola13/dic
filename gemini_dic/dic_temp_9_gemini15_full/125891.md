muscular: /ˈmʌskjələr/ | adj. | 强壮的；肌肉发达的；有力的 | Relating to or denoting muscle.  Possessing great physical strength or power. |

例句：He has a muscular physique. 他拥有健美的体格。

例句：She gave a muscular performance in the final. 她在决赛中表现强劲。


近义词：strong, powerful, brawny, robust, athletic

反义词：weak, feeble, frail, delicate


词源:  The word "muscular" derives from the Latin word "musculus," meaning "little mouse." This is because the bulging muscles, especially in the forearm, were thought to resemble small mice.  The suffix "-ar" is an adjectival suffix indicating "relating to" or "characteristic of."
