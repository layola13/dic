consultancy:/kənˈsʌltənsi/ | n. | 咨询；顾问服务；咨询公司 | The act of giving expert advice to someone.  It can also refer to a company that provides such advice. |例句：The company hired a consultancy to improve its marketing strategy. 这家公司聘请了一家咨询公司来改进其营销策略。
例句：She sought consultancy on her investment portfolio. 她就投资组合寻求咨询。

近义词：advisory service, consulting, advice

反义词：无
