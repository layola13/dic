cauterizations:/ˌkɔːtəraɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. | 烙术；烧灼术；止血 | The process of burning tissue to destroy it or to stop bleeding.  It's a medical procedure often used to seal blood vessels or destroy abnormal tissue. | 例句：The surgeon used cauterizations to stop the bleeding. = 外科医生使用烧灼术来止血。

The cauterizations were successful in preventing further infection. = 烧灼术成功地防止了进一步感染。| 近义词：burn, searing, coagulation | 反义词：healing, repair
