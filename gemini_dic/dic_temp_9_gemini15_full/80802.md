gothic:/ˈɡɒθɪk/ /ˈɡɑːθɪk/| adj. n.|哥特式的；哥特人；哥特体|

英文解析:Relating to the Goths, a Germanic people who invaded the Roman Empire in the 4th and 5th centuries.  As an adjective, it describes a style of architecture, literature, and art characterized by pointed arches, ribbed vaults, and ornate decoration, prevalent in Europe from the 12th to 16th centuries.  It also describes a more recent subculture or aesthetic characterized by a dark, gloomy, and often romantic atmosphere.

例句:

The cathedral is a magnificent example of Gothic architecture. = 这座大教堂是哥特式建筑的伟大范例。

He enjoys reading Gothic novels. = 他喜欢阅读哥特式小说。


近义词: (depending on context) medieval, dark, gloomy, romantic,  grotesque, macabre


反义词: (depending on context) classical, modern, bright, cheerful, simple


词性: adjective, noun
