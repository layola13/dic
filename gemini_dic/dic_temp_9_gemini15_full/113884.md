madrague: /mæˈdrɑːɡ/ | n. | 鱼群聚集地，鱼梁；（尤指地中海的）陷阱，围栏 | A madrague is a type of fish trap, particularly associated with the Mediterranean Sea.  It's a complex system of nets and enclosures designed to guide and capture large schools of fish.  The term can also refer to the place where such a trap is located, or even to the fish that are caught. |  The fishermen set their madrague in the calm waters of the bay.  渔民们在海湾平静的水域中设置了他们的鱼梁。 The ancient madrague yielded a bountiful catch. 古代的鱼梁带来了丰收。 | 近义词：fish trap, weir, pound | 反义词：open sea,  free area | 名词
