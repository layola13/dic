former:/ˈfɔːrmər/ /ˈfɔːrmɚ/| adj.|以前的，前任的|Former refers to something or someone that existed or held a position in the past but no longer does.|例句：He is a former president. 他是前任总统。|例句：My former roommate moved to California. 我以前的室友搬到了加利福尼亚。|近义词：previous, prior, past, ex-|反义词：current, present, future|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a former teacher. = 他是一位前教师。
She is a former student of this university. = 她是这所大学的前学生。


一般过去时:
He was a former employee of the company. = 他曾是这家公司的员工。
She was a former member of the club. = 她曾是俱乐部成员。


现在完成时:
He has been a former smoker for five years. = 他戒烟五年了。(暗示过去吸烟)
She has been a former athlete since her injury. = 自从受伤后，她就不再是运动员了。


过去完成时:
He had been a former champion before his retirement. = 他在退役前曾是冠军。
She had been a former colleague of mine before she moved. = 在她搬家之前，她曾是我的同事。


将来时:
He will be a former employee next month. = 他下个月将成为前员工。
She will be a former student when she graduates. = 她毕业后将成为前学生。


虚拟语气:
If he were a former employee, he would know the procedure. = 如果他是前员工，他会知道程序。
If she had been a former member, she would have received the invitation. = 如果她是前会员，她本会收到邀请。


被动语态:
He was considered a former leader. = 他被认为是前领导。
She is regarded as a former star athlete. = 她被视为前明星运动员。


疑问句:
Was he a former member of the team? = 他以前是球队成员吗？
Is she a former student of yours? = 她以前是你的学生吗？


否定句:
He is not a former employee. = 他不是前员工。
She was not a former resident of this town. = 她以前不是这个镇的居民。


PHRASE->
former colleague = 前同事
former employer = 前雇主
former president = 前任总统
former spouse = 前配偶
former home = 旧居
