roughhoused:/ˈrʌfhaʊzd/| v.|粗鲁地玩耍；打闹|To play roughly and boisterously; to engage in rowdy play.|例句：The children roughhoused in the backyard until their parents called them in for dinner. 孩子们在后院粗鲁地玩耍，直到父母叫他们进屋吃饭。|例句：The dogs roughhoused playfully, tumbling over each other.  狗们嬉戏打闹，互相翻滚。|近义词：tumble, wrestle, horse around|反义词：behave politely, be gentle
