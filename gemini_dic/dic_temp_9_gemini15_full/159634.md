polly:/ˈpɒli/| /ˈpɑːli/ | n. |波莉（女子名）；鹦鹉；（非正式）傻瓜 |A Polly is a girl's name, a parrot, or (informally) a fool.  It's often used as a diminutive of Pollyanna. |例句：My aunt's parrot, Polly, can say "Hello." 我的姑妈的鹦鹉波莉会说“你好”。|例句：Don't be such a polly! 别那么傻！|近义词：Parrot (for the bird),  (for the person) silly, fool |反义词： (for the person) clever, intelligent |名词
