preparer:/prɪˈpɛərər/| n. |准备者，制备者 |A preparer is a person who prepares something, such as documents, food, or materials.  |例句：The tax preparer helped me file my return.  税务筹备员帮我申报了我的纳税申报表。|例句：The chef is a skilled food preparer.  这位厨师是一位熟练的食品准备者。|近义词：preparatory, maker, provider |反义词：consumer, destroyer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chef prepares the meal every day. = 厨师每天准备饭菜。
She prepares her presentation carefully. = 她认真准备她的演示文稿。


一般过去时:
He prepared the documents for the meeting. = 他为会议准备了文件。
They prepared a delicious dinner for their guests. = 他们为客人准备了一顿美味的晚餐。


现在完成时:
She has prepared all the necessary materials. = 她已经准备好了所有必要的材料。
He has prepared for the exam for weeks. = 他已经准备考试几周了。


过去完成时:
They had prepared the food before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经准备好食物了。
She had prepared a speech for the conference. = 她为会议准备了一篇演讲稿。


将来时:
He will prepare a detailed report next week. = 他下周将准备一份详细的报告。
They will prepare the classroom for the lesson. = 他们将为课程准备教室。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would prepare more thoroughly. = 如果我是你，我会更彻底地准备。
I wish I had prepared better for the interview. = 我希望我为面试准备得更好。


被动语态:
The documents were prepared by the secretary. = 文件是由秘书准备的。
The food was prepared according to the recipe. = 食物是按照食谱准备的。


疑问句:
Who prepared this delicious cake? = 谁准备了这个美味的蛋糕？
Has the report been prepared yet? = 报告准备好了吗？


否定句:
He didn't prepare for the test. = 他没有为考试做准备。
She hadn't prepared her presentation before the meeting. = 会议开始前她还没有准备好她的演示文稿。
