oven:/ˈʌvən/| n. |烤箱，烤炉 |A heated compartment used for baking, roasting, or heating food.  |Example: I preheated the oven to 350 degrees before baking the cake. 我在烤蛋糕之前把烤箱预热到350度。|近义词：bake,roaster|反义词：refrigerator


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The oven bakes the bread evenly. = 烤箱均匀地烘烤面包。
She uses the oven every day. = 她每天都使用烤箱。

一般过去时:
The oven baked the pizza perfectly. = 烤箱完美地烤制了披萨。
We used the oven to heat up the leftovers. = 我们用烤箱加热剩菜。

现在完成时:
I have used this oven for ten years. = 我已经使用这个烤箱十年了。
They have never cleaned the oven. = 他们从未清洁过烤箱。

过去完成时:
The cake had already burnt before I opened the oven. = 我打开烤箱前，蛋糕已经烤焦了。
She had preheated the oven for an hour before she started cooking. = 她开始做饭前已经预热了烤箱一个小时。

将来时:

We will preheat the oven before baking the cookies. = 我们会在烤饼干之前预热烤箱。

词源:
The word "oven" comes from the Old English word "ofen," which itself has Germanic roots.  The ultimate origin is Proto-Germanic *auhōn.  Many Germanic languages have cognates showing a similar evolution.
