skunkweed:/ˈskʌŋkˌwiːd/| n. |臭鼬草，恶臭草|A type of weed, often referring to various plants with a strong, unpleasant odor, similar to that of a skunk.  The term is not precise and can refer to different plants depending on the region.  It's often used informally.|Example:  The skunkweed near the creek was so pungent, it made my eyes water. 臭鼬草在小溪附近的气味如此刺鼻，以至于我的眼睛都流泪了。|近义词：stinking weed, foul-smelling plant|反义词：fragrant flower, sweet-smelling plant|名词
