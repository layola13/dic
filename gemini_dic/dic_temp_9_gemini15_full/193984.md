self-exhibited:/ˌsɛlf ɪɡˈzɪbɪtɪd/| adj.| 自我展示的；自己展览的 |  Describes something (usually art or oneself) that is displayed or shown without the assistance or intervention of a gallery, curator, or other intermediary. It implies a direct presentation to the public by the creator or subject.  |例句：Her self-exhibited paintings at the local cafe attracted considerable attention. 她自行在当地咖啡馆展出的画作吸引了相当多的关注。

例句：The artist chose a self-exhibited approach, bypassing traditional gallery representation.这位艺术家选择了一种自我展示的方式，绕过了传统的画廊代理。


近义词：self-presented, independently shown, independently exhibited


反义词：professionally exhibited, gallery-represented


词性：形容词
