listen: /ˈlɪsən/ | vt. vi.| 听，倾听 | To pay attention to sound; to hear attentively.  | 例句：Listen carefully to the instructions.  仔细听指示。  | 例句：I listened to the music all evening. 我整个晚上都在听音乐。 | 近义词：hear, attend, heed | 反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I listen to music every day. = 我每天都听音乐。
She listens to the radio in the morning. = 她早上听收音机。

一般过去时:
I listened to the birds singing yesterday. = 我昨天听了鸟儿唱歌。
He listened attentively to the speaker. = 他专心致志地听着演讲者。

现在完成时:
I have listened to that song many times. = 我已经听过那首歌很多次了。
She has listened to all the evidence. = 她已经听取了所有证据。

过去完成时:
I had listened to the whole album before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经听完了整张专辑。
They had listened to the news report before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前已经听取了新闻报道。

将来时:
I will listen to your advice. = 我会听取你的建议。
She will listen to the concert tomorrow. = 她明天将去听音乐会。


虚拟语气:
I wish I had listened to my parents. = 我真希望我听了父母的话。
If I had listened more carefully, I would have understood. = 如果我更仔细地听的话，我就明白了。


被动语态:
The music was listened to by everyone. =  音乐被每个人都听了。
His advice was listened to with respect. = 他的建议受到了尊重的倾听。


疑问句:
Are you listening to me? = 你在听我说吗？
Did you listen to the lecture? = 你听了讲座吗？


否定句:
I didn't listen to a word he said. = 我一句他说的都没听。
She doesn't listen to gossip. = 她不听闲话。


PHRASE->
listen to: 听…
listen up: 注意听
listen in: 偷听
listen attentively: 专心倾听
listen carefully: 仔细聆听
