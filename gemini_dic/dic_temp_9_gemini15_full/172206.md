push-around: /pʊʃ əˈraʊnd/ | vt. | 欺凌；摆布；随意对待 | To treat someone in a rude or unfair way, especially by ordering them around or ignoring their opinions. |例句：Don't let them push you around. 不要让他们欺负你。


He was constantly pushed around by his older brother. 他经常被他哥哥欺负。


近义词：bully, intimidate, manipulate, domineer |反义词：respect, support, empower |词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They push him around every day. = 他们每天都欺负他。
She pushes her employees around. = 她对她的员工颐指气使。


一般过去时:
They pushed him around yesterday. = 他们昨天欺负了他。
The boss pushed his staff around. = 老板对他的员工颐指气使。


现在完成时:
They have pushed him around for years. = 他们多年来一直欺负他。
She has pushed her employees around since she became manager. =自从她当上经理以来，她就一直对她的员工颐指气使。


过去完成时:
They had pushed him around before he finally stood up to them. = 在他最终反抗他们之前，他们已经欺负了他很久了。
The manager had pushed her staff around for months before they complained. = 老板对员工颐指气使了好几个月，员工们才开始抱怨。
