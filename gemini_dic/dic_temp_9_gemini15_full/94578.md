impoverishment:/ɪmˈpɒvərɪʃmənt/ | n. |贫困化；贫穷；使贫困|The process of becoming poor or the state of being poor; the act of making someone or something poor.  It often refers to a decline in economic well-being, affecting individuals, communities, or even nations.|例句：The economic downturn led to widespread impoverishment in the region. 经济衰退导致该地区普遍贫困。|例句：Years of conflict have resulted in the impoverishment of the entire country. 多年的冲突导致整个国家贫困。|近义词：destitution, poverty, deprivation, indigence|反义词：enrichment, prosperity, affluence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government's policies contribute to the impoverishment of the rural population. = 政府的政策导致农村人口贫困化。
Increased unemployment leads to widespread impoverishment in many cities. = 失业率增加导致许多城市普遍贫困。


一般过去时:
The drought impoverished the farmers and their families. = 干旱使农民及其家人陷入贫困。
The war severely impoverished the nation. = 战争使这个国家严重贫困。


现在完成时:
Decades of neglect have impoverished the educational system. = 几十年的忽视使教育系统变得贫困。
Poor governance has impoverished the local community. = 糟糕的治理使当地社区贫困。


过去完成时:
By the time the aid arrived, the famine had impoverished the entire village. = 当援助到达时，饥荒已经使整个村庄陷入贫困。
The mismanagement had already impoverished the company before the new CEO took over. = 在新任首席执行官上任之前，管理不善已经使公司陷入贫困。


将来时:
Without intervention, the rising cost of living will impoverish many families. = 如果不采取干预措施，不断上涨的物价将使许多家庭陷入贫困。
Automation may impoverish workers in certain industries. = 自动化可能会使某些行业的工人陷入贫困。


虚拟语气:
If the government hadn't implemented those policies, the situation wouldn't have led to such widespread impoverishment. = 如果政府没有实施那些政策，情况就不会导致如此普遍的贫困。
If only they had acted sooner, the crisis could have prevented widespread impoverishment. = 如果他们早点采取行动，这场危机本来可以避免大范围的贫困。


被动语态:
Many families were impoverished by the economic crisis. = 许多家庭在经济危机中陷入贫困。
The region was impoverished by years of drought and conflict. = 该地区因多年的干旱和冲突而贫困。


疑问句:
Has the recent recession impoverished many people? = 最近的经济衰退是否使许多人陷入贫困？
What factors contribute to the impoverishment of the urban poor? = 哪些因素导致城市贫困人口的贫困？


否定句:
The new program aims to prevent the impoverishment of vulnerable families. = 新项目旨在防止弱势家庭贫困。
The government's efforts did not prevent the impoverishment of the rural areas. = 政府的努力并没有阻止农村地区的贫困。
