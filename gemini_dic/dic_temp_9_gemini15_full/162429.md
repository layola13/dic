preadministrative: /ˌpriːædmɪnɪˈstreɪtɪv/ | adj. | 预先行政的；行政前的 | Relating to actions or processes that occur before formal administrative procedures begin.  This word is rarely used and often considered informal or non-standard. It combines "pre-" (before) and "administrative" (relating to administration).  |例句：The preadministrative discussions were crucial in setting the agenda for the formal meeting. 预先的讨论对于正式会议的议程制定至关重要。


The preadministrative phase involved gathering data and consulting stakeholders. 预先行政阶段包括收集数据和咨询利益相关者。


近义词： preliminary, preparatory, antecedent | 反义词：postadministrative, subsequent


词性: adjective
