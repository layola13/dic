pacemakers:/ˈpeɪsmeɪkərz/ | n. |起搏器 |A pacemaker is a small device that is implanted in the chest to help the heart beat regularly. It is used to treat bradycardia (slow heart rate) and other heart rhythm problems.  |Example: The doctor implanted a pacemaker to regulate his irregular heartbeat.  医生植入了起搏器来调节他紊乱的心跳。|近义词：heart stimulator |反义词：无
