prognostic:/prɒɡˈnɒstɪk/ /prɑːɡˈnɒstɪk/| adj. n.|预后的，预兆的；预言|Relating to prognosis; having the nature of a prognosis.  A statement or judgment about what may happen in the future, especially in connection with a disease.|例句：The prognostic indicators suggested a poor outcome. 预后指标提示预后不良。

The doctor offered a positive prognostic about her recovery.  医生对她康复做出了积极的预后判断。


近义词：predictive,forecasting,prophetic|反义词：certain,definite
