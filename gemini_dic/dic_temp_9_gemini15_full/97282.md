influentials: /ˌɪnfluˈɛnʃəlz/ | n. | 有影响力的人 |  People who have significant influence on the opinions and behaviors of others, often in a specific field or area.  They are often thought leaders, celebrities, or experts whose opinions are highly valued and sought after. |  The marketing campaign targeted influentials in the fashion industry.  市场营销活动的目标是时尚行业的意见领袖。


The company's new product launch relied heavily on the endorsements of key influentials. 公司新产品的发布很大程度上依赖于关键意见领袖的认可。


近义词: opinion leaders, key players, thought leaders, trendsetters, authorities


反义词: nobodies, unknowns, inconsequentials


词性: 名词
