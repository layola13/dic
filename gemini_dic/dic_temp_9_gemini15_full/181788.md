relinking:/ˌriːˈlɪŋkɪŋ/ | n. |重新链接，重新连接 |The act of re-establishing a connection or link that has been broken or severed.  This can refer to physical connections (like wires or networks), or metaphorical connections (like relationships or ideas).| Example: The technicians performed a relinking of the severed fiber optic cable.  技术人员对断裂的光纤电缆进行了重新连接。

The relinking of the two companies created a powerful synergy.  两家公司的重新合并产生了强大的协同效应。|近义词：reconnection, reattachment, rejoining |反义词：disconnection, severance, separation |名词
