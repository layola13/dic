navaratri:/ˌnɑːvəˈrɑːtri/ | n. |九夜节，九夜祭|A nine-night Hindu festival celebrated in the autumn, dedicated to the divine feminine Shakti in its various forms.  It involves worship, fasting, and the performance of various rituals.  Different regions celebrate different forms of the goddess during the nine nights.|例句：Navaratri is a vibrant festival full of color, music, and devotion. 九夜节是一个充满色彩、音乐和虔诚的热闹节日。|例句：Many people observe fasts during Navaratri. 许多人在九夜节期间进行斋戒。|近义词：Dasara, Dussehra (related festivals)|反义词：无
