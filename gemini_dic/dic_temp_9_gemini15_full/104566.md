kanazawa:/ˌkænəˈzɑːwɑː/|/ˈkænəzɑːwɑː/| n. |金泽|Kanazawa is a city located in Ishikawa Prefecture, Japan. It is known for its well-preserved samurai district, Kenrokuen Garden (considered one of Japan's three most beautiful landscape gardens), and its traditional crafts and culture.  |Kanazawa is famous for its gold leaf.  金泽以金箔而闻名。|Kyoto, Nara|None|Noun
