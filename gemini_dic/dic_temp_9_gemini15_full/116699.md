maturities:/məˈtʃʊərɪtiz/| n. |到期日；成熟期；满期|The plural form of "maturity," referring to the dates when debts or investments become due, or the stage of full development.|例句：The bond's maturities range from one to ten years.  这批债券的到期日从一年到十年不等。|例句：The company is focusing on accelerating the maturities of its short-term debt. 公司正致力于加快其短期债务的到期。|近义词：due dates, expiration dates|反义词：inception, commencement|名词

词源:
From maturity + -ies (plural suffix).  "Maturity" itself comes from the Old French *maturité, ultimately from the Latin *maturitas, from maturus "ripe, mature."
