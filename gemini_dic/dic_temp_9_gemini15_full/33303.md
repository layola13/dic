chuck-it:/tʃʌk ɪt/| vt.|扔掉，抛弃，放弃|To throw something carelessly or forcefully; to abandon or give up something.|例句：He chucked the ball to his friend. 他把球扔给了他的朋友。|例句：She decided to chuck it all and travel the world. 她决定放弃一切去环游世界。|近义词：toss, throw, abandon, discard|反义词：keep, retain, preserve|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chucks the old newspapers every week. 他每周都扔掉旧报纸。
They chuck their empty cans into the bin. 他们把空罐子扔进垃圾桶。

一般过去时:
She chucked the broken vase into the trash. 她把破花瓶扔进了垃圾桶。
He chucked his job and started his own business. 他辞掉了工作，自己创业。

现在完成时:
I have chucked all my old clothes. 我已经把所有旧衣服都扔掉了。
They have chucked the project after many failures. 他们经过多次失败后放弃了这个项目。

过去完成时:
She had chucked the letter before she realized it was important. 她意识到信很重要之前就已经扔掉了。
They had chucked the idea before the meeting even started. 会议还没开始他们就已经放弃了那个想法。


将来时:
I will chuck this broken chair away tomorrow. 我明天会把这张坏椅子扔掉。
He will chuck his studies if he doesn't pass the exam. 如果考试不及格，他会放弃学业。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would chuck that old car. 如果我是你，我会把那辆旧车扔掉。
I wish I had chucked that bad investment earlier. 我真希望我早点放弃那笔糟糕的投资。

被动语态:
The old furniture was chucked out last week. 旧家具上周被扔掉了。
The plan was chucked because of the high costs. 计划因为成本过高而被放弃。

疑问句:
Will you chuck the rubbish out please? 请你把垃圾扔出去好吗？
Did they chuck the broken computer? 他们把坏电脑扔掉了吗？

否定句:
I didn't chuck the letter; I put it in my drawer. 我没有扔掉信，我把它放在抽屉里了。
He won't chuck his dream of becoming a doctor. 他不会放弃成为医生的梦想。


PHRASE->
chuck it in:  放弃 (give up)
chuck it out: 扔掉 (throw away)
chuck something away: 扔掉某物 (discard something)
chuck something over: 扔过 (throw something across)
chuck a wobbly: 发脾气 (to get angry or upset)
