disconfirmatory:/ˌdɪskənˈfɜːrmətɔːri/ | adj. | 反驳的；不支持的；与事实不符的 |  Relating to or tending to disprove or contradict something.  It suggests evidence that goes against a hypothesis or belief. | The disconfirmatory evidence led the researchers to reconsider their initial hypothesis.  反驳性证据导致研究人员重新考虑了他们的最初假设。 | contradictory, refutative, negative | confirmatory, supportive, corroborative | 形容词
