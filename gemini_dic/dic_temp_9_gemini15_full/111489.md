loaf:/loʊf/| n.|面包；一块；懒汉|A loaf is a shaped mass of bread, typically baked in an oven. It can also refer to a shapeless mass of something or a lazy person.|例句：I bought a loaf of bread this morning.  今天早上我买了一条面包。

例句：He spent the afternoon loafing around the house. 他整个下午都在家里懒洋洋地混日子。|近义词：bread, chunk, bum|反义词:active, diligent
