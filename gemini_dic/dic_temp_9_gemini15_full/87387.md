herpetologic:/ˌhɜːrpɪˈtɒlədʒɪk/| adj.|爬虫学的；两栖爬行动物学的|Relating to the branch of zoology that deals with reptiles and amphibians.|例句：He has a deep interest in herpetologic research. 他对爬虫学研究有着浓厚的兴趣。  He presented a fascinating herpetologic lecture. 他做了一个关于爬虫学的精彩讲座。|近义词：amphibious (部分情况下), reptilian (部分情况下)|反义词：ornithological (鸟类学的),mammalogical (哺乳动物学的)
