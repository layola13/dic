pseudolinguistically:/ˌsuːdoʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪkli/ | adv. |似是而非地(从语言学的角度) |  In a manner that superficially resembles a linguistic phenomenon but lacks its genuine characteristics.  It often involves the imitation of linguistic structures or processes without a full understanding of their underlying principles.  This can be seen in the creation of artificial languages, the analysis of unconventional communication systems, or the study of language disorders. |The researcher analyzed the child's speech pseudolinguistically, identifying patterns that resembled grammatical structures but lacked true linguistic function. |近义词：apparently, seemingly, superficially | 反义词：authentically, genuinely, truly

The child's babbling was analyzed pseudolinguistically, revealing patterns that mimicked adult speech but lacked semantic meaning. =  孩子的咿呀学语被似是而非地从语言学角度分析，揭示了模仿成人说话的模式，但缺乏语义意义。
