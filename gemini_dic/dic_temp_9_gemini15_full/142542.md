osteitis:/ˌɒstiˈaɪtɪs/ | n. | 骨炎 | Inflammation of bone.  Osteitis is a general term referring to inflammation of bone tissue.  It can be caused by various factors, including infection, injury, and autoimmune diseases.  Different types of osteitis exist, depending on the cause and location. | Example:  The patient presented with osteitis in the jawbone, requiring antibiotic treatment.  患者的下颌骨出现骨炎，需要抗生素治疗。|近义词:  bone inflammation,  osteoarthritis (if degenerative)|反义词:  None directly, but healthy bone tissue could be considered an antonym.

词源:

Osteitis is a compound word derived from:

* **Osteo-**: From Greek "ὀστέον" (ostéon), meaning "bone".
* **-itis**: From Greek "-ῖτις" (-itis), a suffix denoting inflammation.

Therefore, osteitis literally means "inflammation of the bone".
