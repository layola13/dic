deceleration: /ˌdiːˌsɛləˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 减速；减缓 | The process of decreasing speed or rate.  It refers to a reduction in velocity, often gradual.  |例句：The car's deceleration was smooth and controlled. 这辆车的减速平稳而受控。

例句：The pilot initiated deceleration as the plane approached the runway. 飞机接近跑道时，飞行员开始减速。


近义词：slowdown, retardation, braking


反义词：acceleration


词性：名词
