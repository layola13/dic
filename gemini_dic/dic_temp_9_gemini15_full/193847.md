self-directions:/ˌsɛlf dəˈrɛkʃənz/| n.|自我指导，自主学习|The ability to guide one's own learning and behavior without external control or direction.  It often involves setting goals, planning actions, monitoring progress, and making adjustments as needed. |例句：The program emphasizes self-directions, empowering students to take ownership of their education.  这个项目强调自我指导，使学生能够掌握自己的教育。

例句：He demonstrated excellent self-directions in completing his independent research project. 他在完成独立研究项目中展现了极佳的自我指导能力。


近义词：self-management, self-governance, autonomy, independence|反义词：dependence, reliance on others, external control|名词
