micropantograph: /ˈmaɪkrəʊˈpæntəɡræf/ | n. | 微型绘图仪，微型等比例复制器 | A micropantograph is a small-scale device used to reproduce drawings or designs at a reduced or enlarged size. It operates on the principle of similar triangles, using levers and linkages to precisely transfer movements from a master drawing to a smaller or larger copy.  |  The engineer used a micropantograph to create a miniature replica of the bridge design. | 近义词：reducer, copier (in specific context) | 反义词：enlarger (in specific context)
