erupts:/ɪˈrʌpts/ | v. | 爆发；喷发 | To burst forth suddenly and violently, especially with regard to a volcano or similar phenomenon.  It can also refer to a sudden, violent outbreak of something, such as anger or conflict. |例句：The volcano erupted violently, spewing ash and lava into the air.  火山猛烈爆发，喷出火山灰和熔岩。|近义词：burst forth, explode, blow up |反义词： subside, calm down


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The volcano erupts every few years. = 这座火山每隔几年就会爆发一次。
Anger erupts easily in him. = 他很容易发脾气。


一般过去时:
The geyser erupted spectacularly yesterday. = 间歇泉昨天壮观地喷发了。
The crowd erupted in cheers. = 人群爆发出欢呼声。


现在完成时:
The volcano has erupted three times this century. = 这座火山在本世纪已经爆发了三次。
Tension has erupted into violence. = 紧张局势已经演变成暴力。


过去完成时:
The situation had erupted into chaos before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，局势已经陷入混乱。
The argument had erupted earlier that day. = 争论发生在当天早些时候。


将来时:
Scientists predict the volcano will erupt soon. = 科学家预测火山很快就会爆发。
If you continue to provoke him, a fight will erupt. = 如果你继续激怒他，就会爆发冲突。


虚拟语气:
If the volcano erupted, the town would be evacuated. = 如果火山爆发，小镇将被疏散。
I wish the fighting hadn't erupted. = 我真希望战斗没有爆发。


被动语态:
The town was evacuated after the volcano erupted. = 火山爆发后，小镇被疏散了。
The conflict was erupted by a misunderstanding. = 冲突是由误解引起的。(注意被动语态在此处用法相对较少)


疑问句:
Will the volcano erupt again soon? = 火山还会很快爆发吗？
When did the violence erupt? = 暴力是什么时候爆发的？


否定句:
The volcano didn't erupt as expected. = 火山没有按预期爆发。
The argument didn't erupt until much later. = 争论直到很久以后才爆发。

erupt in laughter = 突然大笑起来

erupt in protest = 爆发抗议
