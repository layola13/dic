go on:/ɡoʊ ɑːn/ | v. |继续，发生，进行|To continue; to proceed; to happen; to take place.|例句：Please go on with your story. 请继续讲你的故事。

例句：What's going on here? 这里发生了什么事？


近义词：continue, proceed, happen, occur | 反义词：stop, cease, end


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He goes on talking even when nobody is listening. 他即使没有人听也继续说个不停。
They go on working late into the night. 他们一直工作到深夜。


一般过去时:
The meeting went on for three hours. 会议持续了三个小时。
She went on to explain her reasons. 她接着解释了她的理由。


现在完成时:
The construction has been going on for months. 建设已经持续了好几个月了。
They have gone on holiday. 他们去度假了。


过去完成时:
The rain had gone on for days before it finally stopped.  雨下了好几天才最终停下来。
She had gone on a diet before the wedding. 她在婚礼前就开始节食了。


将来时:
The concert will go on for two hours. 音乐会将持续两个小时。
We will go on a trip next month. 我们下个月将去旅行。


虚拟语气:
If you went on like this, you would fail the exam. 如果你继续这样下去，你考试就会不及格。
I wish they would go on with the project. 我希望他们能继续进行这个项目。


被动语态:
The project is going on as planned.  项目按计划进行中。
The discussion was going on when I entered the room. 我进房间的时候讨论还在进行。


疑问句:
What's going on? 发生了什么事？
Is the party still going on? 派对还在继续吗？


否定句:
The show didn't go on as scheduled. 演出没有按计划进行。
He refused to go on with the conversation. 他拒绝继续谈话。


PHRASE->
go on and on:  喋喋不休
go on a trip: 去旅行
go on a diet: 开始节食
go on holiday: 去度假
go on strike: 罢工
