restabilize: /ˌriːˈsteɪbəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使恢复稳定；使重新稳定 | To restore stability to something that has become unstable.  The process involves returning a system or situation to a previous state of equilibrium or balance.  This often implies a previous period of instability or disruption. |

例句：

The government implemented new policies to restabilize the economy after the financial crisis. = 政府实施了新的政策，以在金融危机后稳定经济。

The doctor administered medication to restabilize the patient's blood pressure. = 医生施用了药物以稳定病人的血压。


近义词：stabilize, steady, consolidate, re-establish

反义词：destabilize, upset, disrupt
