essaylet:/ˈɛseɪlɛt/| n. |小论文；短文 |A short essay; a brief composition.  It's often used to describe a piece of writing that is shorter and less formal than a full-length essay. |例句：She submitted an essaylet on the impact of social media. 她提交了一篇关于社交媒体影响的小论文。|近义词：short essay, brief composition, short piece |反义词：treatise, monograph
