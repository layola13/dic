committee:/kəˈmɪti/ | n. |委员会，全体委员 | A group of people appointed for a specific function, typically to investigate, consider, or act upon a particular matter.  | The committee decided to approve the budget.  委员会决定批准预算。 | board, panel, council |  (无明确反义词，可考虑individual, single person) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee meets every Tuesday. = 委员会每周二开会。
The finance committee reviews the budget annually. = 财务委员会每年审查预算。


一般过去时:
The committee approved the proposal last week. = 委员会上周批准了该提案。
The selection committee interviewed several candidates. = 甄选委员会面试了几位候选人。


现在完成时:
The committee has already made its decision. = 委员会已经做出了决定。
The ethics committee has investigated the matter thoroughly. = 伦理委员会已经彻底调查了此事。


过去完成时:
The committee had finished its report before the deadline. = 委员会在截止日期前完成了报告。
The planning committee had already chosen the location for the event. = 规划委员会已经选定了活动的举办地点。


将来时:
The committee will announce its findings next month. = 委员会将于下个月公布调查结果。
The committee will vote on the amendment tomorrow. = 委员会明天将对修正案进行投票。


虚拟语气:
If the committee were to approve the project, we could start immediately. = 如果委员会批准该项目，我们就能立即开始。
I wish the committee had considered my suggestion. = 我希望委员会考虑过我的建议。


被动语态:
The budget was approved by the committee. = 预算得到了委员会的批准。
The matter is being reviewed by the special committee. = 此事正在由特别委员会审查。


疑问句:
When does the committee meet next? = 委员会下次什么时候开会？
What are the committee's recommendations? = 委员会的建议是什么？


否定句:
The committee did not approve the request. = 委员会没有批准请求。
The committee has not yet reached a consensus. = 委员会尚未达成共识。
