over-react: /ˌoʊvərɪˈækt/ | v. | 过度反应，反应过激 | To react in an excessive or disproportionate way to a situation or event.  |例句：He overreacted to the minor criticism. 他对微小的批评反应过度了。  |近义词：exaggerate, overestimate, overindulge | 反义词：underreact, remain calm


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overreacts to everything. = 他对任何事情都反应过度。
They overreact to minor setbacks. = 他们对小的挫折反应过度。

一般过去时:
She overreacted to the news. = 她对这个消息反应过度了。
He overreacted and yelled at his colleague. = 他反应过度，冲着他的同事大喊大叫。

现在完成时:
I have overreacted in the past. = 我过去有过反应过度的经历。
They have overreacted to several situations recently. = 最近他们在好几个场合都反应过度了。

过去完成时:
She realized she had overreacted after she calmed down. = 她冷静下来后才意识到自己反应过度了。
He understood that he had overreacted to his boss's comment. = 他明白自己对老板的评论反应过度了。

将来时:
I think he will overreact if you tell him the truth. = 我想如果你告诉他真相，他会反应过度的。
She might overreact if she finds out about the accident. = 如果她发现事故的事情，她可能会反应过度。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't overreact to such a trivial matter. = 如果我是你，我不会对这么一件小事反应过度。
I wish I hadn't overreacted to his harsh words. = 我真希望自己没有对他那些刻薄的话语反应过度。

被动语态:
The situation was overreacted to by the media. = 媒体对这种情况反应过度了。
His criticism was overreacted to by his subordinates. = 他的批评受到了下属的过度反应。

疑问句:
Did she overreact to his proposal? = 她对他的提议反应过度了吗？
Will they overreact to the changes in policy? = 他们会对政策的改变反应过度吗？

否定句:
He didn't overreact to the bad news. = 他对坏消息没有反应过度。
She tried not to overreact to the pressure. = 她努力不去对压力反应过度。
