leashed: /liʃt/ | adj. | 用皮带栓住的；受约束的 |  Attached to a leash; restrained or controlled. |例句：The dog was leashed to a tree.  那条狗被拴在一棵树上。


例句：The project felt leashed by the strict deadline.  这个项目感觉受到了严格截止日期的约束。


近义词：restrained, tied, bound, controlled | 反义词：unleashed, free, unbound


词性：形容词

词源:
The word "leashed" comes from the noun "leash," which ultimately derives from the Old French word "laisse," meaning "band," "thong," or "rein."  The word's evolution reflects its historical use in controlling animals, particularly hunting dogs.
