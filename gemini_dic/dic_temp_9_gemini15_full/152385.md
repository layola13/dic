pave:/peɪv/ | vt. |铺设（道路等）；为…铺平道路 | to cover a surface with hard, flat material such as stone or brick, or to make a surface smoother and more level |  例句：They are paving the road in front of my house. 他们正在我家门前铺路。  例句：His hard work paved the way for his success. 他的努力为他铺平了成功的道路。 | 近义词：surface, lay,  | 反义词：destroy, ruin | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They pave the streets every year. = 他们每年都铺路。
He paves the way for his children's future. = 他为孩子们的未来铺平道路。

一般过去时:
They paved the driveway last week. = 他们上周铺了车道。
The Romans paved many roads throughout their empire. = 罗马人在他们的帝国各地铺设了许多道路。

现在完成时:
They have paved the entire street. = 他们已经铺好了整条街。
She has paved the way for a new generation of artists. = 她为新一代艺术家铺平了道路。

过去完成时:
They had paved the road before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经铺好了路。
The builders had paved the foundation before constructing the house. = 建造房屋之前，建筑工人已经铺好了地基。

将来时:
They will pave the new highway next year. = 他们明年将铺设新的高速公路。
The city council will pave the way for economic growth. = 市议会将为经济增长铺平道路。

虚拟语气:
If they paved the road, it would be easier to drive. = 如果他们铺路，开车就会更容易。
I wish they had paved the path; it's so muddy. = 我真希望他们已经铺好了这条路；太泥泞了。

被动语态:
The road was paved last month. = 这条路是上个月铺设的。
The way to success is paved with hard work. = 通往成功的道路是用辛勤工作铺就的。

疑问句:
Will they pave the parking lot? = 他们会铺设停车场吗？
Have they paved the new road yet? = 他们已经铺好新路了吗？

否定句:
They didn't pave the street this year. = 他们今年没有铺路。
They haven't yet paved the path to the beach. = 他们还没有铺好通往海滩的路。


PHRASE->
pave the way for = 为…铺平道路
pave the path = 铺设小路
pave a road = 铺设一条路
pave the streets = 铺设街道
pave the way to success = 为成功铺平道路
