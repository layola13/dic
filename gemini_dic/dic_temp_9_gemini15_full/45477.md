curbs:/kɜːrb/| /kɝb/ | n. v. | 路边石；抑制；控制 | A curb is a stone or concrete edge at the side of a road or pavement, or a raised edge next to a road or other area.  It can also mean to restrain or control something. |例句：The city council is planning to install new curbs along Main Street. 市议会计划沿着主街安装新的路边石。

The government needs to implement stricter curbs on pollution. 政府需要对污染实施更严格的控制。| 近义词：restraint, restriction, control, check | 反义词：freedom, license, unrestraint | 名词，动词

将来时:

否定句:
