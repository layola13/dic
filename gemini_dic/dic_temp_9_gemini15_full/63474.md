entropium: /ɛnˈtroʊpiəm/ | n. | 睑内翻 |  Inversion of the eyelid, turning the edge of the eyelid inward.  This condition can cause irritation and discomfort as the eyelashes rub against the eye.  | Example: The ophthalmologist diagnosed her with entropium in her lower eyelid. 眼科医生诊断她下眼睑内翻。 | 近义词: trichiasis (if caused by ingrown eyelashes) | 反义词: ectropion |  词性:名词
