/ˌnɒnɪmˈbezlmənts/ | n. | 非挪用；未挪用款项 | Instances where funds or assets were not misused or stolen.  The audit revealed a significant number of nonembezzlements, indicating strong internal controls. | 近义词: non-theft, honest transactions | 反义词: embezzlement, theft, fraud
