shunt:/ʃʌnt/| v. n.|转移，调车，分流|to push or turn something to one side; to move (railway carriages) from one track to another; to bypass or divert (something, typically a flow of electricity or liquid)|例句：The doctor had to shunt the blood from the damaged artery.医生不得不将血液从受损的动脉中转移出去。|例句：The railway worker skillfully shunted the carriages onto a siding.铁路工人熟练地将车厢转移到侧线。|近义词：divert, switch, bypass, deflect|反义词：direct, concentrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They shunt the wagons every morning. = 他们每天早上都调动货车。
He shunts the problem onto someone else. = 他把问题推给别人。

一般过去时:
The workers shunted the train to another track. = 工人们把火车转移到另一条轨道上。
The surgeon shunted the blood flow. = 外科医生改变了血流方向。

现在完成时:
They have shunted all the carriages. = 他们已经把所有的车厢都调好了。
The hospital has shunted the patient to a different ward. = 医院已将病人转移到不同的病房。

过去完成时:
They had shunted the damaged train before the accident. = 他们在事故发生前已经把损坏的火车转移了。
The engineers had shunted the excess water away from the dam. = 工程师们已将多余的水从水坝转移开。

将来时:
They will shunt the traffic away from the construction site. = 他们将会把交通从建筑工地转移开。
The mechanic will shunt the engine to the repair shop. = 机械师将会把发动机转移到修理厂。

虚拟语气:
If they shunted the traffic earlier, the accident could have been avoided. = 如果他们早点转移交通，事故本来是可以避免的。
I wish they had shunted the faulty equipment before it caused damage. = 我希望他们在故障设备造成损坏之前就将其转移了。


被动语态:
The traffic was shunted through a back road. = 交通被转移到了一条偏僻的道路上。
The patient was shunted to a specialist. = 病人被转诊给了一位专家。

疑问句:
Will they shunt the cars to the other side? = 他们会把车转移到另一边吗？
Why was the electricity shunted? = 为什么电力被分流了？

否定句:
They didn't shunt the train on time. = 他们没有按时调动火车。
The system didn't shunt the excess water. = 系统没有分流多余的水。
