schizognath:/ˈskɪzəɡnɑːθ/| n. |裂颚类动物|A schizognath is a bird whose palatine bones are not fused together.  This is a characteristic used in avian classification, distinguishing them from desmognaths. Schizognaths typically have a more flexible palate.|Example:  Many songbirds are schizognaths.  许多鸣禽属于裂颚类动物。|近义词:  (no direct synonym, but could be described as a type of "bird" or "avian")|反义词: desmognath |名词
