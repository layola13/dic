nonvacant: /ˌnɒnˈveɪkənt/ | adj. | 非空闲的，被占用的 | Not vacant; occupied.  This word is formed by the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "vacant" (meaning "empty").  It implies a space or position that is not available or is already filled.

例句：

The nonvacant parking lot forced us to park far away. =  非空的停车场迫使我们把车停得很远。

All the nonvacant hotel rooms were booked weeks in advance. = 所有被占用的酒店房间都在几周前就预订满了。


近义词：occupied, filled, booked, taken, engaged

反义词：vacant, empty, available, unoccupied


词性：形容词
