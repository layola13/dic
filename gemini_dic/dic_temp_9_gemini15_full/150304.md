pang:/pæŋ/| n.|砰的一声，剧烈疼痛|a sudden, sharp, loud noise; a sudden, sharp feeling of pain.  |例句：The door slammed with a loud pang. 门砰的一声关上了。|例句：He felt a sharp pang of guilt. 他感到一阵强烈的内疚。|近义词：thump, thud, ache, twinge|反义词：silence, calmness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The door pangs shut. = 门砰地一声关上了。
I feel a pang of hunger. = 我感到一阵饥饿。


一般过去时:
The bell pang loudly. = 铃铛响亮地砰砰作响。
A pang of sadness struck her. = 一阵悲伤袭上心头。


现在完成时:
The headache has given me a few pangs. = 头痛已经给我带来了一些阵痛。
She has felt many pangs of regret. = 她感到过许多悔恨。


过去完成时:
The pain had already given her several pangs by the time the doctor arrived. =  医生到达时，疼痛已经给她带来了好几次剧痛。
He had experienced many pangs of conscience before confessing. = 他在坦白之前经历过许多良心的谴责。


将来时:
The explosion will produce a deafening pang. = 爆炸将发出震耳欲聋的巨响。
I anticipate a pang of disappointment if I don't win. = 如果我没有赢得比赛，我预料会感到一阵失望。


虚拟语气:
If the bomb exploded, there would be a tremendous pang. = 如果炸弹爆炸，会发出巨大的声响。
If I had known, I would have avoided that pang of jealousy. = 如果我知道的话，我就不会有那种嫉妒的感觉了。


被动语态:
The door was panged shut by the wind. = 门被风砰地关上了。(此用法不常见，通常用被动语态描述疼痛感受)
The pain was felt as a sharp pang. = 疼痛感被感觉为一阵剧痛。


疑问句:
Did you hear that loud pang? = 你听到那一声巨响了吗？
Did you feel a pang of remorse? = 你感到一丝悔恨吗？


否定句:
There wasn't a pang of fear in his heart. = 他心里没有一丝恐惧。
She didn't experience a single pang of conscience. = 她没有丝毫的良心不安。
