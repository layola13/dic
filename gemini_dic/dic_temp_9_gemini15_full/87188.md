herdsmen:/ˈhɜːrdzmən/| n. |牧民；牧羊人 |People who work tending livestock, especially cattle, sheep, or goats.  |Example: The herdsmen moved their flocks to higher pastures in the summer.  牧民们在夏天将他们的牲畜迁移到较高的牧场。  The nomadic herdsmen followed the rains across the vast plains. 游牧的牧民们追随着雨水穿过广阔的平原。|近义词：shepherd, cattleman, goatherd |反义词：farmer (in some contexts)
