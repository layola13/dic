heroology:/ˌhɪəˈrɒlədʒi/| n. |英雄学，英雄研究|The study of heroes, their myths, legends, and impact on culture and society.  It encompasses various fields like mythology, history, psychology, and sociology to understand the phenomenon of heroism.|例句：Heroology examines the archetypes and societal functions of heroic figures across different cultures. 英雄学研究不同文化中英雄人物的原型和社会功能。|例句：A course in heroology might explore the evolution of heroic narratives throughout history. 一门英雄学课程可能会探讨贯穿历史的英雄叙事演变。|近义词：hero studies, mythology studies|反义词：N/A
