doggy-paddled:/ˈdɒɡiˌpædld/ | v. |狗刨式游泳 | To swim using a dog paddle; a method of swimming on the surface of the water by moving both arms and legs in a circular motion.  |例句：The children doggy-paddled in the shallow end of the pool. 孩子们在游泳池的浅水区狗刨式游泳。

例句：She doggy-paddled frantically towards the shore. 她疯狂地用狗刨式向岸边游去。


近义词：paddle, sprawl (in water)


反义词：swim (using proper technique)


词性：动词
