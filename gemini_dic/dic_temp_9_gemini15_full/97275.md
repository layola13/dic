influencers:/ˈɪnfluənsərz/ | n. | 影响者，网红 | People who have a significant impact on the opinions, behaviors, or purchases of others, often through social media or other online platforms. They build a following and leverage their influence to promote products, ideas, or causes. | Example:  Many young people look to social media influencers for fashion advice. 许多年轻人向社交媒体网红寻求时尚建议。 |近义词：opinion leaders, trendsetters, spokespeople | 反义词：无
