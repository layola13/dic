drawing-pin:/ˈdrɔːɪŋpɪn/| n. |图钉|A small pin with a flat head, used for fastening paper to a board.  It is also called a thumbtack. |例句：I used a drawing-pin to attach the poster to the wall. 我用图钉把海报钉在墙上。

例句：She accidentally stepped on a drawing-pin. 她不小心踩到了一枚图钉。


近义词：thumbtack, pushpin


反义词：None (no direct antonym exists)


词性：Noun
