historiographer:/ˌhɪstɔːriˈɒɡrəfər/ | n. |历史学家，史学家 |A person who studies and writes about history, often with a focus on the methods and interpretations of historical writing.  | Example:  The historiographer meticulously researched the sources before writing her account of the revolution.  这位史学家在撰写关于革命的记述之前，仔细研究了各种来源。|近义词：historian, chronicler |反义词：N/A
