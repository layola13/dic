/pɑːrts/ | n. | 部分，部件，零件 | The word "parts" refers to the separate components or sections that make up a whole.  It can refer to physical objects, ideas, or concepts. |  The car engine has many parts. 这辆汽车引擎有很多部件。 The play was divided into three parts. 这部戏剧被分成三部分。 | sections, components, pieces, elements | whole, entirety
