seeding:/ˈsiːdɪŋ/| n. v.|播种；播撒；植入；(数据)填充|The act of planting seeds; the scattering of seeds; the introduction of something new or into something else; in computing, the act of populating a database with initial data. |例句：The farmer is seeding his field with wheat. 农民正在用小麦播种他的田地。 The company is seeding the market with a new product. 公司正在向市场投放新产品。|近义词：sowing, planting, implanting|反义词：harvesting, reaping


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He seeds his garden every spring. = 他每年春天都播种他的花园。
They seed the clouds to induce rain. = 他们播撒云层以诱导降雨。

一般过去时:
She seeded the lawn with new grass. = 她用新的草籽播种草坪。
The scientists seeded the experiment with a control group. = 科学家们用一个对照组来进行实验。

现在完成时:
We have seeded the database with sample data. = 我们已经用样本数据填充了数据库。
They have seeded the area with wildflowers. = 他们已经在这个区域播种了野花。

过去完成时:
The farmers had seeded the fields before the rain came. = 农民们在下雨之前已经播种了田地。
The researchers had seeded the petri dish with bacteria before the experiment. = 研究人员在实验前已经在培养皿中接种了细菌。


将来时:
They will seed the new field next week. = 他们下周将播种新的田地。
We will seed the cloud with silver iodide to make rain. = 我们将用碘化银来播云造雨。

虚拟语气:
If they seeded the field earlier, they would have a better harvest. = 如果他们早些播种田地，他们就会有更好的收成。
I wish I had seeded the garden last month. = 我希望我上个月就播种了花园。

被动语态:

The database is being seeded with customer information. = 数据库正在用客户信息填充。

疑问句:

When will the fields be seeded? = 田地什么时候播种？

否定句:
