outvenoming: /ˌaʊtˈvɛnəmɪŋ/ | v. |  超过毒性；毒性更强 |  To surpass in venomousness or poisonous effect; to exceed in toxicity.  | The new snake venom was found to outvenom the previously known most potent toxin. = 新发现的蛇毒被发现其毒性超过了以前已知的最强毒素。  |  surpass, exceed, outdo |  less venomous, less toxic |
