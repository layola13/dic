/ˈklɪərɪst/| adj. |最清晰的，最明白的 | Most free from obscurity or ambiguity; easily understood.  It is the superlative form of "clear". |例句：The clearest explanation was given by the professor. 教授给出了最清晰的解释。

例句：The clearest picture was taken with the new camera. 用新相机拍的照片最清晰。


近义词：distinct, lucid, transparent, unambiguous | 反义词：obscure, vague, ambiguous, unclear | 词性：形容词
