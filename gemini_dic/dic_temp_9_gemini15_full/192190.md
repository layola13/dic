scrappiness:/ˈskræpɪnɪs/| n.|拼搏精神；顽强；零碎；不整洁|The quality of being scrappy; determined and spirited despite being small or lacking resources.  It can also refer to something being disorganized or messy.|例句：The underdog team showed remarkable scrappiness, fighting hard until the final whistle.  这支弱旅展现了非凡的拼搏精神，一直战斗到终场哨响。

例句：The artist's studio was filled with a delightful scrappiness, a chaotic collection of half-finished projects.  这位艺术家的工作室里充满了令人愉悦的零碎感，一堆半成品项目杂乱无章地堆放在一起。|近义词：grit, tenacity, determination, fight, resilience|反义词：passivity, weakness, surrender, orderliness, neatness
