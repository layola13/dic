disgust:/dɪsˈɡʌst/| n. v.|厌恶；恶心；反感|Disgust is a strong feeling of dislike or disapproval. It can be caused by something unpleasant or offensive, such as a bad smell, a dirty place, or someone's behavior.|例句：The sight of the blood filled me with disgust.  看见血让我感到恶心。

The smell of rotting meat caused disgust among the onlookers.  腐烂肉的气味让旁观者感到厌恶。|近义词：abhorrence, aversion, loathing|反义词：delight, pleasure|名词

His behavior disgusts everyone. = 他的行为让每个人都厌恶。

现在完成时:
