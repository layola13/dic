plazas:/ˈplɑːzəz/| n.|广场；商业中心|A plaza is an open public space, often found in the center of a city or town, typically surrounded by buildings.  It can also refer to a shopping center or commercial complex.|Example: The city's main plaza was filled with people enjoying the festival.  这个城市的中心广场挤满了享受节日的人们。  The new plaza offers a variety of shops and restaurants.  新的商业中心提供各种商店和餐馆。|近义词：square, marketplace, public square|反义词：private property, enclosed space|名词
