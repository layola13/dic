orthopercussion: /ˌɔːrθoʊpərˈkʌʃən/ | n. |叩诊|  The act of tapping the body surface with the fingers to determine the size, consistency, and borders of the underlying organs. It is a diagnostic method used in medicine to assess the condition of various body parts, often the lungs and heart, by producing sounds through percussion.  | Example: The physician performed orthopercussion to evaluate the extent of the patient's lung consolidation.  医生进行叩诊以评估病人肺实变的程度。 |近义词：percussion, auscultation (though auscultation uses a stethoscope) |反义词：N/A
