coxswaining: /ˈkɒksweɪnɪŋ/ | n. |舵手工作；掌舵；指挥  |The act of steering and controlling a boat, especially a racing shell.  It also involves coordinating the rowing crew's efforts.|例句：The coxswain's calm instructions guided the crew to victory. 舵手的冷静指示引导船员取得胜利。|近义词：steering, directing, guiding |反义词：None specifically
