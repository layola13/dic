carve-up: /kɑːrv ʌp/ | vt. |分割，瓜分，破坏|To divide something, especially land or property, into smaller parts, often in a forceful or unfair way.  It can also imply a chaotic or destructive division.|例句：The company was carved up after the CEO's sudden death.  公司在首席执行官突然去世后被瓜分了。|例句：The vandals carved up the park benches.  破坏者破坏了公园的长椅。|近义词：divide, partition, dissect, split up |反义词：unite, consolidate, integrate
