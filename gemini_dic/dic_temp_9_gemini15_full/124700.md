mowed:/moʊd/| v. |割草，修剪|To cut down (grass, etc.) with a mowing machine or scythe.  It implies a relatively short, even cut.|例句：I mowed the lawn yesterday. = 我昨天割了草坪。
I mowed the grass with a push mower. = 我用推式割草机割草。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mows the lawn every Saturday. = 他每周六都割草坪。
She mows the grass before lunch. = 她午饭前割草。

一般过去时:
They mowed the field last week. = 他们上周割了田里的草。
He mowed the lawn before it rained. = 他在下雨前割了草坪。

现在完成时:
We have mowed the entire garden. = 我们已经把整个花园的草都割了。
She has mowed the lawn twice this week. = 她本周已经割了两次草坪。

过去完成时:
They had mowed the lawn before the party started. = 他们在派对开始前已经割了草坪。
He had mowed the field before the storm hit. = 他在暴风雨来临之前已经割好了田里的草。

将来时:
I will mow the lawn tomorrow. = 我明天要割草坪。
They will mow the field next week. = 他们下周将割那块田里的草。

虚拟语气:
If I had a lawnmower, I would mow the lawn myself. = 如果我有割草机，我会自己割草坪。
I wish I had mowed the lawn earlier. = 我真希望我早点割草坪。

被动语态:
The lawn was mowed yesterday. = 草坪昨天被割了。
The field is being mowed now. = 田地正在被割草。

疑问句:
Have you mowed the lawn yet? = 你割草了吗？
Did he mow the grass this morning? = 他今天早上割草了吗？

否定句:
I didn't mow the lawn because it was raining. = 我没有割草，因为下雨了。
They haven't mowed the field yet. = 他们还没有割那块田里的草。
