ownerships: /ˈoʊnərʃɪps/ | n. | 所有权；所有制 | The legal right to possess, use, and enjoy something.  It refers to the state or fact of owning something. |  例句：The company disputes the ownerships of the intellectual property. 这家公司对知识产权的所有权存在争议。

例句：He transferred the ownerships of his house to his daughter. 他把房子的所有权转给了女儿。


近义词：possession, control, title, right

反义词：lack of ownership, dispossession


词性：名词
