orbiters:/ˈɔːrbɪtərz/| n. |轨道器，环绕飞行器|A spacecraft designed to orbit a celestial body.  Orbiters are often used for scientific observation and data collection.|例句：The Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter has been sending back stunning images of the Martian surface.  火星侦察轨道器一直在发送火星表面的惊人图像。|例句：The Hubble Space Telescope, although not strictly an orbiter in the sense of orbiting a planet, is a type of orbiter in that it orbits the Earth.|哈勃太空望远镜，虽然从严格意义上来说不是绕行星运行的轨道器，但因为它绕地球运行，所以也是一种轨道器。|近义词：satellites, spacecraft|反义词：lander (着陆器)
