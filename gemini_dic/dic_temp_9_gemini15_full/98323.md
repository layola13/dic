insignificative:/ˌɪnsɪɡˈnɪfɪkətɪv/| adj.|无足轻重的；微不足道的；不重要的|Not significant; unimportant; trivial.  The contribution he made was insignificant. |His efforts were insignificant compared to the overall project.|近义词：trivial, negligible, unimportant, inconsequential |反义词：significant, important, crucial, vital

USAGE->
一般现在时:
His contribution is insignificant to the project. = 他对项目的贡献微不足道。
The difference is insignificant. = 区别微不足道。

一般过去时:
Her role in the play was insignificant. = 她在剧中的角色微不足道。
The impact of the news was insignificant. = 这个消息的影响微不足道。


现在完成时:
His efforts have been insignificant in solving the problem. = 他的努力对解决这个问题微不足道。
Their changes have made an insignificant difference. = 他们的改变几乎没有产生任何影响。


过去完成时:
The damage had been insignificant before the storm worsened. = 在暴风雨恶化之前，损害微不足道。
His previous work had been insignificant compared to his current achievements. = 他以前的工作与他现在取得的成就相比微不足道。


将来时:
The impact of this decision will be insignificant in the long run. = 从长远来看，这个决定的影响将微不足道。
His contribution to the future of the company will be insignificant. = 他对公司未来的贡献将是微不足道的。


虚拟语气:
If his contribution were significant, we would have noticed it. = 如果他的贡献很重要，我们会注意到的。
I wish his efforts were more significant. = 我希望他的努力更有意义。


被动语态:
His work was considered insignificant by the committee. = 他的工作被委员会认为微不足道。
The problem was deemed insignificant and ignored. = 这个问题被认为微不足道而被忽略了。


疑问句:
Is his contribution insignificant? = 他的贡献微不足道吗？
Are these changes insignificant? = 这些变化微不足道吗？


否定句:
His role was not insignificant; he was crucial to the success. = 他的作用并非微不足道；他对成功至关重要。
The impact of the event was not insignificant; it changed everything. = 此事件的影响并非微不足道；它改变了一切。
