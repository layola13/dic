corindon:/ˈkɒrɪndən/ | n. |刚玉|Corundum is a crystalline form of aluminum oxide (Al₂O₃) and is the second hardest mineral after diamond.  It's valued for its use in abrasives and gemstones.  |Example: Rubies and sapphires are varieties of corundum.  红宝石和蓝宝石是刚玉的变种。|近义词:aluminum oxide, abrasive|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑与软质矿物对比)|
