commiserating:/kəˈmɪzəreɪtɪŋ/| v. |同情；怜悯；表示慰问| Expressing sympathy or sorrow for someone's misfortune.  It often involves sharing in the feeling of sadness or disappointment.|例句：I commiserated with her on the loss of her job. 我为她失去工作而表示同情。

例句：He commiserated with his friend who had failed the exam. 他同情他的朋友考试不及格。


近义词：sympathizing, empathizing, consoling, sympathizing with| 反义词：rejoicing, celebrating, gloating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I commiserate with you on your loss. = 我对你遭受的损失表示同情。
They commiserate with each other regularly. = 他们经常互相慰问。

一般过去时:
She commiserated with him after his breakup. = 她在他分手后安慰他。
We commiserated over our shared misfortunes. = 我们为我们共同的不幸遭遇表示同情。

现在完成时:
I have commiserated with her many times. = 我已经多次安慰她了。
They have commiserated with the victims of the disaster. = 他们已经对灾难的受害者表示了慰问。

过去完成时:
She had commiserated with him before he even began to speak. = 他还没开始说话，她就对他表示同情了。
They had commiserated over their lost opportunities. = 他们已经为他们失去的机会感到惋惜了。

将来时:
I will commiserate with her when I see her. = 我见到她的时候会安慰她。
We will commiserate with the family. = 我们将向这个家庭表示慰问。

虚拟语气:
If I were in his shoes, I would commiserate with him. = 如果我处在他的位置，我会同情他。
I wish I could have commiserated with her sooner. = 我希望我能早点安慰她。


被动语态:
His misfortune was commiserated with by many. = 许多人对他遭受的不幸表示同情。
Her loss was commiserated by her friends and family. = 她失去的东西得到了朋友和家人的同情。


疑问句:
Are you commiserating with him about his situation? = 你在为他目前的境况表示同情吗？
Did anyone commiserate with the bereaved family? = 有人对失去亲人的家庭表示慰问吗？


否定句:
I didn't commiserate with him, I just felt indifferent. = 我没有同情他，我只是感到冷漠。
They did not commiserate with the bully. = 他们没有同情那个恶霸。



PHRASE->
commiserate with someone = 对某人表示同情
commiserate over something = 为某事表示同情
commiserate on something = 对某事表示同情
feel commiseration for someone = 对某人感到同情
express commiseration = 表达同情
