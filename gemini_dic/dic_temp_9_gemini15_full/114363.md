majorette:/ˌmæʒəˈrɛt/| n. |女乐队指挥，仪仗队女队员|A majorette is a woman who leads a marching band or performs baton twirling as part of a marching band or similar group.  They often wear elaborate uniforms and perform highly coordinated routines.|例句：The majorette led the band with confident precision.  女乐队指挥以自信精准的动作带领乐队。

The majorettes' dazzling baton twirling captivated the audience. 仪仗队女队员们炫目的棍棒表演迷住了观众。|近义词：bandleader (if leading a band), baton twirler|反义词：None directly applicable, but perhaps "musician" (if focusing on the contrast of leadership vs. playing an instrument).|词性:名词
