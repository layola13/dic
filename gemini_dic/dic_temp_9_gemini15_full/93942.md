immunologist:/ˌɪmjuːˈnɒlədʒɪst/| n. |免疫学家|A scientist who studies immunology, the branch of biology concerning immunity.  Immunologists research the immune system's function, how it protects against disease, and how it can be manipulated to treat or prevent illness. |Example:  The immunologist presented her findings on the new vaccine at the conference.  免疫学家在会议上展示了她关于新型疫苗的研究成果。|近义词：immunobiologist|反义词：无
