shorthand:/ˈʃɔːrtˌhænd/ | n. |速记；速记法；简写 | A system of writing using abbreviated forms of words.  It is a method of writing quickly by using symbols and abbreviations. | Example:  She took shorthand notes during the lecture. 她在上课时做了速记笔记。  He used shorthand to record the meeting. 他用速记记录了会议。 | 近义词：stenography, abbreviation,  short form | 反义词：longhand,  verbatum
