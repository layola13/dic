macro-engineering:/ˈmækroʊˌɛndʒɪˈnɪərɪŋ/| n. |宏观工程学；大型工程 |The study and practice of designing and constructing extremely large-scale projects, often involving significant environmental and societal impacts.  It encompasses the planning, design, construction, and operation of massive infrastructure projects such as dams, canals, large-scale transportation systems, and environmental remediation projects.  |例句：Macro-engineering projects often require international collaboration and significant funding. 宏观工程项目通常需要国际合作和大量资金。

例句：The Three Gorges Dam is a prime example of a successful, albeit controversial, macro-engineering undertaking. 三峡大坝是一个成功但有争议的宏观工程项目的典型例子。


近义词：large-scale engineering, mega-engineering, massive engineering |反义词：micro-engineering,  small-scale engineering


词源:  The term is a combination of "macro," meaning large-scale or overall, and "engineering," the profession of designing and building structures and systems.  The prefix "macro" derives from the Greek word "makros," meaning "large" or "long."
