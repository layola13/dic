reregulations: /ˌriːˌreɡjuˈleɪʃənz/ | n. | 再法规，重新制定法规 | The reregulations aim to clarify ambiguities in the original legislation and improve its effectiveness.  重新制定的法规旨在澄清原始立法中的歧义并提高其有效性。|近义词：rewriting of regulations, amendments, revisions |反义词：deregulation
