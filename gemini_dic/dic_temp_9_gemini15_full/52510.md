differentials:/ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəlz/| n.|微分；差异；差额 |The word "differentials" refers to the differences between things, or to the process of finding these differences.  It can also refer specifically to mathematical differentials, used in calculus. |例句：The differentials in pay between men and women are still significant. 男女之间的工资差异仍然很大。

The price differentials between the two products are minimal. 这两种产品的价格差异极小。

近义词：differences, variations, disparities |反义词：similarities, identities, equalities |词性：n.
