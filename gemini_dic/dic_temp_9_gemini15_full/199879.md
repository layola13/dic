USAGE->

一般现在时:
There are six apples on the table. = 桌上有六个苹果。
Six plus four equals ten. = 六加四等于十。


一般过去时:
There were six birds in the tree. = 树上有六只鸟。
She bought six books yesterday. = 她昨天买了六本书。


现在完成时:
I have seen that movie six times. = 我看过那部电影六次了。
We have had six quizzes this semester. = 这学期我们已经做了六次小测验了。


过去完成时:
They had already eaten six cookies before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经吃了六块饼干了。
She had written six letters before she went to bed. = 她睡觉前已经写了六封信了。


将来时:
I will buy six oranges tomorrow. = 我明天将买六个橙子。
We will have six guests for dinner next week. = 下周我们将有六位客人来吃晚饭。


虚拟语气:
If I had six million dollars, I would travel the world. = 如果我有六百万美元，我会环游世界。
I wish I had six more hours to finish this project. = 我希望我有六个小时来完成这个项目。


被动语态:
Six houses were destroyed by the fire. = 六座房子被大火烧毁了。
Six students were chosen for the competition. = 六名学生被选中参加比赛。


疑问句:
How many sides does a hexagon have? (Answer: Six) = 六边形有多少条边？（答案：六条）
Are there six people in your group? = 你们小组有六个人吗？


否定句:
There are not six chairs in the room. = 房间里没有六把椅子。
She didn't eat six apples. = 她没有吃六个苹果。
