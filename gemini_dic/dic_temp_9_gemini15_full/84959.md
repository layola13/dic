haplogroup:/ˈhæpləˌɡruːp/| n. |单倍群 |A haplogroup is a large group of individuals sharing a common ancestor who can be identified by specific genetic markers.  It's a way to classify populations based on shared DNA variations, often inherited from a single ancestor. |Example:  The study identified a new haplogroup in the ancient DNA samples. 这项研究在古代DNA样本中识别出一个新的单倍群。|近义词: genetic lineage, clade |反义词: N/A
