horrify:/ˈhɒrɪfaɪ/ /ˈhɔːrɪfaɪ/ | vt. |使恐惧；使震惊；使毛骨悚然 |to cause someone to feel extreme fear or shock.  |例句：The news horrified everyone.  新闻让每个人都感到恐惧。

The gruesome details of the accident horrified the witnesses. 事故的可怕细节让目击者毛骨悚然。

近义词：terrify, frighten, shock, appall |反义词：delight, please, soothe


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sight of the accident horrifies me.  看到事故的景象让我感到恐惧。
The thought of public speaking horrifies many people.  想到公开演讲让很多人感到害怕。


一般过去时:
The movie horrified the audience. 这部电影让观众感到恐惧。
The sudden noise horrified the child.  突然的噪音吓坏了孩子。


现在完成时:
The news reports have horrified the public. 新闻报道让公众感到震惊。
The details of the crime have horrified everyone who heard them. 犯罪细节让所有听到的人感到毛骨悚然。


过去完成时:
I had been horrified to learn of the disaster. 我得知灾难的消息后感到非常震惊。
They had already been horrified by the violence they had witnessed. 他们已经被他们目睹的暴力事件吓坏了。


将来时:
The announcement will horrify many people.  这个公告将让许多人感到震惊。
The results of the investigation will likely horrify the public. 调查结果可能会让公众感到震惊。


虚拟语气:
If I saw that, I would be horrified. 如果我看到那样的事情，我会感到恐惧。
I would be horrified if I were in her shoes. 如果我是她，我会感到非常害怕。


被动语态:
Many people were horrified by the speech.  许多人被那篇演讲吓坏了。
The audience was horrified by the violence in the movie.  观众被电影中的暴力吓坏了。


疑问句:
Would you be horrified if you saw a ghost?  如果你看到鬼，你会害怕吗？
What would horrify you the most?  什么事情最让你感到恐惧？


否定句:
The situation didn't horrify me as much as I expected.  情况并没有像我预期的那样让我感到恐惧。
The news, surprisingly, didn't horrify her.  令人惊讶的是，这个消息并没有让她感到恐惧。
