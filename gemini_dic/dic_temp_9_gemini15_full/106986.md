laconically: /ləˈkɒnɪkli/ | adv. | 简洁地；简洁地；惜字如金地 | Using very few words; tersely.  |例句：He laconically replied, "No." 他简洁地回答：“不。”  例句：She answered my long question with a laconic “Maybe.” 她用简洁的“也许”回答了我冗长的问题。| 近义词：briefly, tersely, succinctly, concisely | 反义词：verbosely, garrulously, wordily


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He laconically answers questions. = 他惜字如金地回答问题。
She laconically greets her colleagues. = 她简洁地向同事打招呼。

一般过去时:
He laconically answered the question. = 他简短地回答了这个问题。
She laconically dismissed my concerns. = 她轻描淡写地打消了我的顾虑。

现在完成时:
He has laconically responded to all inquiries. = 他已简短地回复了所有询问。
She has laconically summarized the meeting's outcome. = 她已简明扼要地总结了会议结果。

过去完成时:
He had laconically explained the situation before leaving. = 他在离开之前简短地解释了情况。
She had laconically hinted at the problem earlier. = 她早些时候简短地暗示了这个问题。

将来时:
He will laconically respond to the press. = 他将简短地回应媒体。
She will laconically address the issue in her speech. = 她将在演讲中简短地谈及这个问题。

虚拟语气:
If he were laconic, he wouldn't elaborate. = 如果他惜字如金，他就不会详细解释。
I wish she had been more laconic in her explanation. = 我希望她在解释时更简洁一些。

被动语态:
The message was laconically delivered. = 信息被简洁地传达了。
The problem was laconically described in the report. = 问题在报告中被简短地描述了。

疑问句:
Did he speak laconically? = 他说话简洁吗？
Was her response laconic? = 她的回答简短吗？

否定句:
He did not speak laconically; he was quite verbose. = 他说话并不简洁；他相当啰嗦。
Her response was not laconic; it was quite detailed. = 她的回答并不简短；它相当详细。

词源:
The word "laconically" derives from Laconia, a region in ancient Greece known for its concise and pithy speech.  The Spartans, who inhabited Laconia, were particularly renowned for their brevity of speech, leading to the adjective "laconic" and its adverbial form "laconically."
