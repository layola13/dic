fabricant:/ˈfæbrɪkænt/ | n. | 制造商；厂商 | A fabricant is a manufacturer or maker of goods, especially on a large scale.  | Example:  The fabricant of these shoes is known for its high-quality materials. 这双鞋的制造商以其高质量的材料而闻名。 | 近义词：manufacturer, producer, maker | 反义词：consumer, retailer | 名词
