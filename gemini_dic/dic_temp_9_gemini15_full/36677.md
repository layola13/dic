collier:/ˈkɒliə(r)/|/ˈkɑːliər/| n. |煤矿工人；运煤船；（尤指17-19世纪的）煤船|A collier is a person who works in a coal mine, or a ship that carries coal, especially one of the type commonly used in the 17th to 19th centuries. |The collier worked tirelessly in the dark mines.  煤矿工人不知疲倦地在黑暗的矿井里工作。  He sailed on a collier to deliver coal to London. 他乘运煤船把煤运到伦敦。|miner, coal miner, coal ship |(None particularly fitting)|
