decliners:/dɪˈklaɪnərz/| n. |下降者；衰退者；拒绝者|People or things that decline; those who refuse.  The term can refer to a variety of contexts, from economic decline to a refusal of something offered.|例句：The number of decliners in the survey was surprisingly high.  调查中拒绝者的数量出奇的高。

The economic decliners are struggling to stay afloat. 经济衰退者正在努力维持生计。|近义词：refusers, deniers, rejecters, fallers (in the context of decline)|反义词：accepters, supporters, improvers, ascenders (in the context of decline)
