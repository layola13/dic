inedible: /ɪˈnɛdəbəl/ | adj. | 不可食用的，不能吃的 |  Not suitable or fit to be eaten; not edible.  This word implies that the item is unpleasant or harmful to eat, not merely unpalatable. | Example: The mushrooms we picked were inedible. 我们采摘的蘑菇不能吃。 The overripe fruit was inedible.  这水果太熟了，不能吃了。|近义词：uneatable, unpalatable, unfit to eat | 反义词：edible, palatable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The berries are inedible. = 这些浆果不能吃。
That food looks inedible. = 那食物看起来不能吃。


一般过去时:
The meat was inedible after being left out all night. = 肉放在外面一夜之后就不能吃了。
The cake was inedible due to being burnt. = 蛋糕烤焦了，不能吃了。


现在完成时:
The leftovers have become inedible. = 剩菜已经不能吃了。
The milk has gone bad and is inedible. = 牛奶坏了，不能喝了。


过去完成时:
The food had become inedible before we even noticed. = 我们还没注意到的时候，食物就已经不能吃了。
The bread had been inedible since yesterday. = 面包从昨天开始就不能吃了。


将来时:
The fish will be inedible if you don't refrigerate it. = 如果你不冷藏鱼，它就会不能吃了。
That food will likely be inedible by tomorrow. = 那食物到明天可能就不能吃了。


虚拟语气:
If the meat was properly cooked, it wouldn't be inedible. = 如果肉煮熟了，它就不会不能吃了。
I wish the soup wasn't inedible. = 我希望汤不是不能吃的。


被动语态:
The food was deemed inedible by the health inspector. = 食品检验员认为食物不能吃。
The cake was found to be inedible due to contamination. = 由于受到污染，蛋糕被发现不能吃。


疑问句:
Is this mushroom inedible? = 这个蘑菇不能吃吗？
Are these berries inedible? = 这些浆果不能吃吗？


否定句:
The fruit is not inedible; it's just a little overripe. = 水果并非不能吃；只是有点太熟了。
This food is definitely not inedible; it's quite delicious. = 这食物绝对能吃；它很好吃。
