heep:/hiːp/| n.|堆；大量；(尤指不好的东西)一堆；大量|a large quantity of something, especially something that is not wanted|例句：There was a huge heep of rubbish in the street. 街上有一大堆垃圾。  例句：He has a heep of problems. 他有很多问题。|近义词：pile, stack, mass, heap, mound|反义词：少量, 少数, little, few|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There is a heap of books on the table. = 桌子上有一堆书。
A heap of troubles awaits him. = 一堆麻烦等着他。

一般过去时:
There was a heap of snow in the yard. = 院子里堆满了雪。
A heap of dirty clothes lay on the floor. = 一堆脏衣服扔在地板上。

现在完成时:
We have accumulated a heap of debts. = 我们积累了一大笔债务。
She has made a heap of mistakes. = 她犯了一大堆错误。

过去完成时:
They had already created a heap of problems before the project started. = 项目开始前他们已经制造了一大堆问题。
He had collected a heap of stamps. = 他已经收集了一大堆邮票。

将来时:
There will be a heap of work to do tomorrow. = 明天会有很多工作要做。
They will face a heap of challenges in the future. = 未来他们将面临诸多挑战。

虚拟语气:
If I had a heap of money, I would travel around the world. = 如果我有很多钱，我会环游世界。
I wish I didn't have a heap of homework to do. = 我真希望我没有那么多家庭作业要做。

被动语态:
A heap of rubbish was collected by the workers. = 工人们收集了一堆垃圾。
A heap of complaints were received by the company. = 公司收到了很多投诉。

疑问句:
Is there a heap of work to be done? = 有很多工作要做吗？
Do you have a heap of free time? = 你有很多空闲时间吗？

否定句:
There isn't a heap of food left. = 没有剩下很多食物。
He doesn't have a heap of patience. = 他没有多少耐心。
