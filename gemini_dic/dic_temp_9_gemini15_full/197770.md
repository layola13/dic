shimmer:/ˈʃɪmə(r)/| n., v.|微光，闪光；闪烁，闪耀|A faint, wavering light; to shine with a soft, unsteady light.|例句：The heat caused the air to shimmer. 热气使空气闪闪发光。

The distant lights shimmered on the water. 远处灯光在水面上闪烁。


近义词：glitter, gleam, twinkle, flicker|反义词：dull, dim
