prearresting: /ˌpriːəˈrɛstɪŋ/ | adj. |逮捕前的；逮捕之前的|  Describing the events or actions that occur before an arrest is made. It refers to the period of investigation, gathering evidence, and building a case leading up to the apprehension of a suspect.  |例句：The prearresting investigation revealed crucial evidence linking the suspect to the crime. 逮捕前的调查揭示了将嫌疑人与犯罪行为联系起来的关键证据。


The prearresting surveillance was meticulously documented. 逮捕前的监控被一丝不苟地记录下来。| 近义词：antecedent, prior, preceding | 反义词：postarrest, subsequent
