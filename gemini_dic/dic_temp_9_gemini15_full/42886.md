courts:/kɔːrts/| n.| 法院，球场，朝廷 |  A court is a place where legal cases are heard.  It can also refer to a playing area for various sports, or historically, the royal court. | 例句：The Supreme Court ruled in favor of the defendant. = 最高法院判决支持被告。 | 例句：He plays tennis on the courts every weekend. = 他每个周末都在球场上打网球。|近义词： tribunal, arena, yard | 反义词：N/A
