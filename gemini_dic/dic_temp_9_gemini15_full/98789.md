integrates:/ˈɪntɪɡreɪts/| vt.|整合；使结合；使成为整体|To combine (one thing) with another so that they become a whole.|The new system integrates all the company's databases.|近义词：combine, unify, consolidate|反义词：separate, divide, segregate|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company integrates new technologies into its products. = 公司将新技术整合到其产品中。
He integrates well with the team. = 他与团队相处融洽。


一般过去时:
They integrated the two departments last year. = 他们去年合并了这两个部门。
She integrated the new data into her analysis. = 她将新数据整合到她的分析中。


现在完成时:
We have integrated a new security system. = 我们已经整合了一个新的安全系统。
The company has successfully integrated its operations across multiple countries. = 公司已成功地将其业务整合到多个国家。


过去完成时:
They had integrated the feedback before releasing the final product. = 他们在发布最终产品之前已经整合了反馈意见。
Before the merger, the two companies had already integrated some of their systems. = 在合并之前，两家公司已经整合了一些系统。


将来时:
We will integrate the new software next month. = 我们下个月将整合新软件。
They will integrate the findings into their report. = 他们将把调查结果整合到他们的报告中。


虚拟语气:
If we integrated the two systems, we would improve efficiency. = 如果我们将这两个系统整合起来，我们将提高效率。
It is important that the new design integrates seamlessly with the existing infrastructure. = 重要的是，新设计能够与现有基础设施无缝集成。


被动语态:
The new features will be integrated into the next update. = 新功能将在下一个更新中集成。
The data was integrated into the report using a sophisticated algorithm. = 数据使用复杂的算法集成到报告中。


疑问句:
Does this software integrate with other applications? = 这个软件与其他应用程序集成吗？
How do we integrate the feedback from customers? = 我们如何整合来自客户的反馈？


否定句:
The two systems do not integrate well. = 这两个系统无法很好地集成。
The company failed to integrate the new employee into the team. = 公司未能将新员工融入团队。


PHRASE->
integrate into = 融入；整合进
integrate with = 与...整合
fully integrate = 完全整合
seamlessly integrate = 无缝整合
successfully integrate = 成功整合
