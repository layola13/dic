for:/fɔːr/| /fɔːr/ | prep. conj. |为了，因为，对于，至于 |  A preposition and conjunction indicating purpose, reason, duration, or direction. It can also express a recipient or beneficiary. | 例句：I bought a gift for my mother. = 我为我母亲买了一件礼物。  | 例句：He ran for his life. = 他拼命地跑。 | 近义词：because of, due to, to, since | 反义词:against, despite


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I work for a large company. = 我为一家大公司工作。
She cooks for her family every night. = 她每天晚上都为她的家人做饭。


一般过去时:
He waited for the bus for an hour. = 他等公交车等了一个小时。
They searched for their lost dog all day. = 他们整天都在寻找他们丢失的狗。


现在完成时:
I have been waiting for you for ages. = 我等你很久了。
She has worked for this company for ten years. = 她在这家公司工作十年了。


过去完成时:
He had waited for her for two hours before she arrived. = 他等了她两个小时她才来。
They had searched for the key for days before finding it. = 他们找了几天钥匙才找到。


将来时:
I will wait for you at the station. = 我将在车站等你。
We will work for peace and prosperity. = 我们将为和平与繁荣而努力。


虚拟语气:
I would do anything for you. = 我会为你做任何事。
If I were you, I would apply for the job. = 如果我是你，我会申请这份工作。


被动语态:
The cake was made for her birthday. = 蛋糕是为庆祝她的生日而做的。
This medicine is used for treating headaches. = 这药用于治疗头痛。


疑问句:
What are you doing this for? = 你为什么要这么做？
What is this machine used for? = 这台机器是用来做什么的？


否定句:
I didn't work for that company. = 我没在那家公司工作。
He didn't do it for the money. = 他不是为了钱才这么做的。


PHRASE->
for example = 例如
for instance = 例如
for now = 目前
for sure = 肯定地
for a long time = 很长时间
