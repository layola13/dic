pendulate:/ˈpɛndʒəleɪt/| v.|摆动；摇摆；犹豫不决|To swing or oscillate rhythmically from side to side or back and forth.|The pendulum pendulated back and forth.  钟摆来回摆动。The negotiations pendulated between hope and despair. 谈判在希望与绝望之间摇摆不定。|oscillate, swing, sway|remain still, stay put|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pendulum pendulates smoothly. = 钟摆平稳地摆动。
He pendulates between two opinions. = 他在两种意见之间摇摆不定。

一般过去时:
The chandelier pendulated gently in the breeze. = 吊灯在微风中轻轻摇摆。
She pendulated between accepting and rejecting the offer. = 她犹豫着是否接受这个提议。

现在完成时:
The branch has pendulated in the wind all day. = 树枝整天都在风中摇摆。
He has pendulated between different career paths for years. = 多年来，他一直在不同的职业道路之间摇摆不定。

过去完成时:
The old clock had pendulated for over a century. = 那座老钟摆动了一个多世纪。
Before making the decision, she had pendulated for weeks. = 在做出决定之前，她犹豫了几个星期。

将来时:
The mobile will pendulate gently above the crib. = 移动装置将轻轻地悬挂在婴儿床的上方。
He will pendulate between his family and his work for the next few months. = 在接下来的几个月里，他将在家庭和工作之间摇摆不定。

虚拟语气:
If the pendulum pendulated faster, the clock would run faster. = 如果钟摆摆动得更快，钟就会走得更快。
If he hadn't pendulated so much, he might have made a better decision. = 如果他不那么犹豫，他可能会做出更好的决定。

被动语态:
The pendulum was pendulated by the clock mechanism. = 钟摆是由钟的机械装置摆动的。(This is less common usage)
His decision was influenced by his pendulating feelings. = 他的决定受到了他犹豫不决情绪的影响。

疑问句:
Does the mobile pendulate too much? = 移动装置摆动得太多吗？
Does he pendulate between two jobs? = 他在两份工作之间摇摆不定吗？

否定句:
The pendulum doesn't pendulate anymore. = 钟摆不再摆动了。
He doesn't pendulate between different ideas as much anymore. = 他不再那么犹豫不决了。


PHRASE->
pendulate between… = 在…之间摇摆
pendulate from… to… = 从…到…摆动
pendulate rhythmically = 有节奏地摆动
pendulate gently = 轻柔地摆动
pendulate wildly = 剧烈地摆动
