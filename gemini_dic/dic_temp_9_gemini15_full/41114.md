coproducers:/koʊˈproʊdʒʊsɚz/| n.|联合制片人|People who share the responsibility of producing a film, television show, or other media project.| 例句：The film's coproducers worked tirelessly to secure funding and manage the production. 这部电影的联合制片人夜以继日地努力争取资金和管理制作。

例句：The two coproducers had very different visions for the film, leading to creative tension.  两位联合制片人对这部电影有截然不同的设想，导致了创作上的紧张关系。


近义词：associate producers, joint producers
反义词：sole producer, independent producer

词性:名词
