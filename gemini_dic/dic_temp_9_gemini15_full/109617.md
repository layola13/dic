letterer:/ˈlɛtərər/ | n. | 书法家；写信人；字母书写者 | A person who writes or draws letters, especially one who is skilled in calligraphy.  | The letterer created beautiful, flowing script for the wedding invitations.  书法家为婚礼请柬创作了优美流畅的字体。 | calligrapher, scribe |  illiterate (反义词并非直接反义，而是指缺乏书写能力的人) | 名词
