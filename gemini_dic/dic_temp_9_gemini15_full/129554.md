non-business: /ˌnɑːnˈbɪznɪs/ | adj. | 非商业性的，非营利的 | Not related to or connected with business or commerce.  This adjective describes things, activities, or organizations that are not involved in making a profit or are not focused on commercial goals. |例句：The organization provides non-business services to the community.  该组织为社区提供非商业性服务。

例句：He pursued non-business interests such as painting and writing. 他从事绘画和写作等非商业性爱好。


近义词：noncommercial, nonprofit, voluntary, amateur


反义词：commercial, for-profit, business-related


词性:形容词
