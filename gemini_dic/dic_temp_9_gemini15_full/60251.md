effuse:/ɪˈfjuːz/| vt. vi.|流出；散布；（感情等）洋溢；表达|To flow out; to spread or diffuse; to express freely and abundantly.|例句：Lava effused from the volcano.  熔岩从火山喷涌而出。|例句：Her joy effused in a radiant smile.  她的快乐在她灿烂的笑容中洋溢。|近义词：pour out, emanate, overflow, exude|反义词：restrain, repress, contain|动词
