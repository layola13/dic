outpitying: /ˌaʊtpɪtiɪŋ/ | adj. | 过分怜悯的；过于同情地 |  Excessively pitying; showing excessive sympathy. This word is rare and not commonly used in modern English. It suggests an overabundance of pity, potentially to the point of being condescending or intrusive.  |  例句：Her outpitying gaze made him feel even more inadequate.  她的过度同情眼神让他感觉更加无能。


近义词：overly compassionate, excessively sympathetic, condescendingly pitiful


反义词：indifferent, unsympathetic, callous
