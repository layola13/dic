painters:/ˈpeɪntərz/| n.|画家们，油漆工们|People who paint, either artistically or as a profession.  Painters can be artists who create fine art paintings or tradespeople who apply paint to surfaces.|例句：The painters are working on the mural. 画家们正在创作壁画。

例句：The house painters finished the job quickly.  房屋油漆工很快就完成了工作。|近义词：artists (for fine art painters), decorators (for tradespeople)|反义词：None specifically, but could consider "sculptors" or "architects" as contrasting professions.|名词
