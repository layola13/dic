nodosity:/nɒˈdɒsəti/ | n. |节；瘤；粗糙 | A knot or swelling on a plant stem or other part; a knob or lump.  It can also refer to something that is uneven or bumpy in texture. | Example: The sculptor carefully rendered the nodosity of the tree's bark in the carving.  雕塑家仔细地刻画了树皮上的节瘤。 The roughness of the surface, its nodosity, made it difficult to grip. 表面的粗糙，它的凹凸不平，使得它难以抓握。|近义词：knot, lump, knob, swelling, protuberance |反义词：smoothness, evenness
