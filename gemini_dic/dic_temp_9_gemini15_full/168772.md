prodigiousness:/prəˈdɪdʒəsnəs/ | n. |惊人；非凡；巨大 | The prodigiousness of her talent was evident from a young age.  她的天赋惊人，从小就显而易见。|近义词：immensity, enormity, vastness |反义词：insignificance, triviality


英文解析:The quality or state of being prodigious; extraordinary in size, amount, extent, degree, or power.  指事物在大小、数量、程度或力量方面极其非凡的特性或状态。


例句:

The prodigiousness of the task ahead was daunting.  面前任务的艰巨性令人望而却步。

The prodigiousness of the Roman Empire's infrastructure is still visible today. 罗马帝国基础设施的宏伟至今仍可见。


词源:

From prodigious + -ness.  "Prodigious" itself comes from Old French *prodigieux, from Latin *prodigiosus, from prodigiosus ("wonderful, marvellous," from prodigium ("omen, prodigy").
