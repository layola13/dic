persulphuret:/pɜːˈsʌlfjʊrɛt/| n. |多硫化物|A persulphuret is a chemical compound containing more sulfur than a simple sulfide.  It typically refers to compounds with a higher proportion of sulfur atoms compared to the other element(s) in the molecule.  The term is less commonly used now, with more precise chemical nomenclature preferred.|The old literature describes the formation of persulphurets of metals under specific conditions. |近义词: polysulfide|反义词:monosulfide
