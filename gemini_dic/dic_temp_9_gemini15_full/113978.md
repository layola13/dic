magical:/ˈmædʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 魔术的；不可思议的；有魔力的 | Relating to or characteristic of magic; extraordinarily good or effective.  |例句：The magician performed a magical trick.  魔术师表演了一个神奇的戏法。

例句：The sunset created a magical atmosphere.  日落创造了一种神奇的氛围。


近义词：enchanted, miraculous, wondrous, supernatural | 反义词：ordinary, mundane, commonplace


词源:  From Middle English *magik, from Old French magique, from Late Latin magicus, from Greek magikos, from magoi "Magi," a priestly class in ancient Persia.  The suffix "-al" is a common adjectival suffix in English, indicating "relating to" or "characteristic of".
