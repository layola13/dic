preentails:/priːˈenteɪlz/ | vt. |预示；预先包含；隐含 | To imply or suggest beforehand; to contain as a necessary condition or consequence.  |例句：The success of the project preentailed careful planning and diligent execution.  这个项目的成功预示着需要仔细的计划和勤奋的执行。

The initial investment preentailed significant risks.  最初的投资隐含着巨大的风险。|近义词：implies, suggests, foreshadows, indicates |反义词：contradicts, refutes, belies |动词
