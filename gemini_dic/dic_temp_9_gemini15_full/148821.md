overviewer:/ˈoʊvərˌvjuːər/ | n. | 概述者；监察员；审核员 | A person who provides a general overview or summary of something; someone who oversees or supervises a project or process.  | Example: The project manager acted as an overviewer, ensuring all teams stayed on schedule. 项目经理担任监察员，确保所有团队都能按时完成任务。 |近义词：supervisor, manager, superintendent, inspector | 反义词：subordinate, participant

He oversees the entire operation. = 他监督整个运作。
