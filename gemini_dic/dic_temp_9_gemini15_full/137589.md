nontreatments:/ˌnɒnˈtriːtmənts/ | n. | 非治疗措施，不治疗 |  Nontreatments refer to actions or approaches that do not involve medical or therapeutic intervention aimed at curing or alleviating a condition.  They can encompass observational studies, watchful waiting, or the absence of any specific treatment. | Example:  The study compared the outcomes of patients receiving active treatment with those receiving nontreatments.  这项研究比较了接受积极治疗的患者和接受非治疗措施的患者的预后。|近义词: inaction, non-intervention, watchful waiting | 反义词: treatments, therapies, interventions | 名词
