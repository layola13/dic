rustless:/ˈrʌstləs/| adj. | 不锈的；不会生锈的 | Not subject to rust; resistant to rust.  | Example:  The rustless steel is ideal for outdoor use.  这不会生锈的钢材非常适合户外使用。The rustless finish on the car is impressive. 这辆车的防锈涂层令人印象深刻。|近义词：stainless, corrosion-resistant |反义词：rusty, corroded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The stainless steel is rustless. = 不锈钢是不锈钢的。
This material remains rustless. = 这种材料保持不生锈。

The tools were rustless when we bought them. = 我们买这些工具的时候，它们是不生锈的。
