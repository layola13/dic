curated:/kjʊˈreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 精选的；策展的 |  Selected and organized with care and expertise, often relating to art or cultural items.  |  The museum's curated collection of Impressionist paintings was breathtaking.  博物馆精心策划的印象派绘画收藏令人叹为观止。|  selected, chosen, handpicked |  random, unorganized, haphazard |  形容词


USAGE->

The curator curated a fantastic exhibition. = 策展人策划了一个很棒的展览。
This shop curates unique gifts. = 这家商店精选独特的礼物。
