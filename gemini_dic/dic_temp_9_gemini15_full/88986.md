holistic:/həˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|整体的；全面的；注重整体的|Relating to or concerned with wholes or the entirety of something.  Holistic approaches to a problem consider all aspects, not just individual parts.|例句：The doctor took a holistic approach to treating the patient, considering their physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being.  医生采用了一种整体的方法来治疗病人，考虑到了他们的身心和精神健康。|近义词：comprehensive, integrated, global, systematic|反义词：partial, fragmented, piecemeal
