formalize:/ˈfɔːrməlaɪz/ | vt. | 使正式化；正式确定；使制度化 | To give a definite or official form to (something); to make something official or formal. | 例句：The company formalized its new policy. 公司正式确定了它的新政策。
They formalized their agreement in a written contract. 他们通过书面合同正式确定了他们的协议。

近义词：officialize, systematize, codify, regularize
反义词：informalize, casualize
