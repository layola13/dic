piquing: /ˈpiːkiŋ/ | adj. | 引起兴趣的；刺激的；激怒的 |  Arousing interest or curiosity; stimulating.  It can also mean provoking or irritating. |

例句：

The museum's new exhibit is piquing the interest of many art lovers. = 博物馆的新展览引起了许多艺术爱好者的兴趣。

His arrogant behavior was piquing her anger. = 他傲慢的行为激怒了她。


近义词：interesting, stimulating, intriguing, provoking, irritating, infuriating

反义词：boring, dull, uninteresting, uninspiring


词源:

From the verb "pique," meaning "to excite," "to arouse," or "to provoke."  Ultimately derived from the Old French word "piquer," meaning "to prick" or "to sting," suggesting a sharp or sudden stimulation.
