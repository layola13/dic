remodeling:/ˌriˈmɑːdəlɪŋ/| n. |改建；翻新；改造 |The act of changing the structure or appearance of something, especially a building, to improve it.  It often involves significant alterations and updates.|例句：The kitchen remodeling project took longer than expected.  厨房改造工程比预期的要长。|例句：The house is undergoing extensive remodeling. 这栋房子正在进行大规模的翻修。|近义词：renovation, refurbishment, renovation, reconstruction|反义词：destruction, demolition|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company is remodeling several houses this year. = 今年公司正在翻新几栋房子。
Remodeling the bathroom is a costly undertaking. =  浴室改造是一项昂贵的工程。


一般过去时:
They remodeled their kitchen last year. = 他们去年翻修了他们的厨房。
We remodeled the old building into a modern office. = 我们把旧建筑改造成现代化的办公室。


现在完成时:
We have remodeled our living room. = 我们已经重新装修了我们的客厅。
They have remodeled the entire house since they bought it. = 自从他们买下这栋房子以来，他们已经对其进行了全面翻修。


过去完成时:
They had remodeled the shop before the grand opening. = 他们在盛大开幕之前已经对商店进行了装修。
She had remodeled her apartment twice before she was satisfied. = 她对公寓进行了两次装修才满意。


将来时:
We will remodel the basement next spring. = 我们明年春天将改造地下室。
They will be remodeling the hotel throughout the winter. = 他们将在整个冬天对酒店进行装修。


虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would remodel the entire house. = 如果我们有更多钱，我们会对整栋房子进行装修。
I wish we could remodel the bathroom this year. = 我希望我们今年能改造浴室。


被动语态:
The house is being remodeled by a professional team. = 这栋房子正在由一个专业团队进行翻修。
Several rooms were remodeled before the new tenants moved in. = 在新租户搬进来之前，几个房间进行了装修。


疑问句:
Are they remodeling the old factory? = 他们正在改造旧工厂吗？
When will you start remodeling the kitchen? = 你什么时候开始装修厨房？


否定句:
We are not remodeling the garage this year. = 我们今年不装修车库。
They did not remodel the bathroom as planned. = 他们没有按照计划装修浴室。
