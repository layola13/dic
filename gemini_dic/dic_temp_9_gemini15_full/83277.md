gymnotus:/dʒɪmˈnoʊtəs/| n. |电鳗|A gymnotus is a genus of Neotropical freshwater fish, commonly known as electric eels, though they are not true eels.  They are characterized by their ability to generate powerful electric discharges used for hunting, defense, and communication.  They are found in the Amazon and Orinoco river basins. |例句：The gymnotus electricus, or electric eel, is capable of generating a powerful electric shock.  电鳗能够产生强大的电击。|近义词：electric eel |反义词：N/A
