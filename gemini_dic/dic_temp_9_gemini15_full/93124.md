ified:/ˈɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 变成……的；具有……性质的；(使)成为…… |  The suffix "-ified" is used to create adjectives meaning "made or rendered into the state or condition of," or "having the characteristics of." It often implies a change in form or nature, often artificially or through some process. |  The simplified explanation was easy to understand.  简化的解释很容易理解。The problem was quickly computerified. 问题很快就被计算机化了.|近义词：transformed, modified, changed | 反义词：unmodified, natural
