self-educated:/ˌsɛlfˈɛdʒukeɪtɪd/| adj.|自学的，自修的|Someone who is self-educated has learned things by themselves, without going to school or having a teacher.  They have acquired knowledge and skills through independent study and personal initiative.|She is a self-educated programmer, having learned coding through online courses and personal projects. 她是一位自学成才的程序员，通过在线课程和个人项目学习编程。|近义词：autodidactic, self-taught|反义词：formally educated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is self-educated in astronomy. = 他是自学天文的。
They are self-educated in many fields. = 他们在许多领域都是自学的。

一般过去时:
She was self-educated in art history. = 她自学了艺术史。
He was self-educated in several languages. = 他自学了几门外语。

现在完成时:
I have been self-educated in computer science for five years. = 我自学计算机科学已经五年了。
They have become self-educated experts in their field. = 他们已经成为他们领域的自学专家。

过去完成时:
She had been self-educated in music before she started formal lessons. = 在她开始正式学习之前，她就已经自学了音乐。
Before the conference, he had self-educated himself on the latest research. = 在会议之前，他已经自学了最新的研究成果。

将来时:
He will be self-educated in philosophy by next year. = 到明年他将自学哲学。
She will have self-educated herself in medicine within a decade. = 在十年内她将自学成才成为一名医生。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would be self-educated in astrophysics. = 如果我有时间，我会自学天体物理学。
I wish I had been self-educated in a more practical field. = 我希望我以前自学的是一个更实用的领域。

被动语态:
The skills were self-educated through online resources. = 这些技能是通过在线资源自学的。
The knowledge was self-educated over many years. = 这些知识是多年自学积累的。

疑问句:
Is he self-educated in mathematics? = 他是自学数学的吗？
Are they self-educated in any technical skills? = 他们在任何技术技能方面都是自学的吗？

否定句:
He is not self-educated; he holds a PhD. = 他不是自学的；他拥有博士学位。
She is not entirely self-educated; she took some night classes. = 她并非完全自学成才；她上过一些夜校课程。
