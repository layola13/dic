heavyweights:/ˈheviˌweɪts/| n.|重量级人物；重量级拳击手；重要因素|Those who are considered important or influential in a particular field or industry.  They are often powerful, successful and hold significant weight in their respective domains.|例句：The CEOs of major tech companies are considered heavyweights in the business world.  大型科技公司的首席执行官被认为是商界的重量级人物。

例句：The debate featured heavyweights from both sides of the political spectrum.  这场辩论邀请了两方政治阵营的重量级人物。


近义词：big shots, power players, leading figures, key players, giants

反义词：lightweights, unknowns, nobodies
