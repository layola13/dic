policing:/pəˈliːsɪŋ/| n.| 警方执法；警务工作；维持治安 |The act of policing involves maintaining order and enforcing laws.  警务工作包括维持秩序和执行法律。|The increased policing in the area has reduced crime.  该地区加强的警务工作减少了犯罪。|law enforcement, crime prevention | anarchy, lawlessness |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police are policing the streets tonight. = 今晚警察正在巡逻街道。
Effective policing requires community involvement. = 有效的警务工作需要社区参与。

一般过去时:
They policed the protest effectively. = 他们有效地控制了抗议活动。
The city policed the event with a large number of officers. = 该市出动了大量警力来维持该活动的秩序。

现在完成时:
The government has increased policing in response to recent attacks. = 政府为了应对最近的袭击事件增加了警力。
They have been policing this area for many years. = 他们多年来一直负责这个地区的治安。

过去完成时:
The police had already policed the area before the incident occurred. = 事件发生前，警方已经控制了该地区。
They had been policing the demonstrations for hours before the violence broke out. = 在暴力爆发前的几个小时里，他们一直在维持示威活动的秩序。

将来时:
The government will increase policing in high-crime areas. = 政府将在高犯罪率地区增加警力。
They will be policing the event with additional security measures. = 他们将在活动中采取额外的安全措施来维持秩序。


虚拟语气:
If the city had better policing, crime rates would be lower. = 如果该市有更好的警务工作，犯罪率就会降低。
It is crucial that policing be fair and impartial. = 警务工作必须公平公正至关重要。

被动语态:
The protest was policed heavily. = 抗议活动受到了严密的控制。
The area is being policed more effectively now. = 现在该地区的治安管理更有效了。

疑问句:
How is the department policing the new regulations? = 该部门如何执行新的规章制度？
What methods are being used in policing this area? = 在这个地区治安管理中使用了什么方法？

否定句:
The police did not effectively police the situation. = 警方没有有效地控制住局面。
The area was not adequately policed. = 该地区治安管理不足。
