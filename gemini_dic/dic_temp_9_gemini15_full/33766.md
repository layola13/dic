circulations:/ˌsɜːrkjuˈleɪʃənz/ | n. |循环；发行量；流通；血液循环 | The movement of something, typically blood, air, or water, through a closed system.  It can also refer to the distribution of something, such as newspapers or information. | 例句：The circulation of blood is essential for life. = 血液循环对生命至关重要。

The newspaper boasts a high circulation. = 这家报纸拥有很高的发行量。


近义词：distribution, dissemination, flow, movement | 反义词：stagnation, standstill
