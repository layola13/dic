precertification:/ˌpriːˌsɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |预先认证，预先批准 |The process of obtaining approval for a medical procedure or treatment before it is performed.  This helps to ensure that the procedure is medically necessary and covered by insurance. |例句：My doctor needs to get precertification for my upcoming surgery. 我的医生需要为我即将进行的手术获得预先批准。
例句：The insurance company requires precertification for all elective procedures. 保险公司要求所有选择性手术都必须预先批准。|近义词：prior authorization, pre-approval|反义词：post-authorization, retroactive approval|
