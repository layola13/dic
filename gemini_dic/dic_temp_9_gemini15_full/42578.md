It often refers to someone who actively works against another's plans or interests, especially in a game or competition. | Example: The counterplayer in the chess match was incredibly skilled.  这个象棋比赛中的对手非常厉害。  The government's new policy faced strong opposition from its counterplayers in the media. 政府的新政策面临媒体中反对者的强烈反对。 | 近义词：opponent, adversary, rival, antagonist | 反义词：ally, collaborator, supporter | 名词
