freeloading:/ˈfriːloʊdɪŋ/| n. |免费搭伙; 白吃白住; 占便宜 |The act of accepting the hospitality or generosity of others without making a reciprocal contribution.  It often implies taking advantage of someone's kindness or resources without offering anything in return. |例句：He's been freeloading off his parents for years. 他多年来一直靠父母养活。|例句：Stop freeloading; it's time to get a job and contribute. 别再白吃白住了，该找个工作自己赚钱了。|近义词：sponging, mooching, scrounging |反义词：contributing, reciprocating
