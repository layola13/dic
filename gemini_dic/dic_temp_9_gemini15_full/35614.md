coastguard:/ˈkoʊstɡɑːrd/|/ˈkəʊstɡɑːd/| n. |海岸警卫队；海岸警卫队队员|A coastguard is a government agency responsible for protecting the coastline and waters of a country.  It often performs search and rescue operations, enforces maritime laws, and prevents smuggling. |The coastguard rescued the sailors from the sinking ship.  海岸警卫队将水手们从沉船中救了出来。| maritime police, lifeguards | army, navy |名词
