resuscitator:/rɪˈsʌsɪteɪtər/| n. |复苏器；人工呼吸器| A resuscitator is a device used to assist or support breathing, often in emergency situations where a person is not breathing adequately or at all.  It may involve delivering oxygen or providing positive pressure ventilation.  |Example: The paramedics used a bag-valve-mask resuscitator to help the victim breathe. 急救人员用一个麻醉呼吸器帮助受害者呼吸。|近义词：ventilator, respirator|反义词：None.
