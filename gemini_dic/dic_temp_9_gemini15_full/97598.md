inhabiters:/ɪnˈhæbɪtərz/ | n. | 居民，住户 | The people who live in a particular place.  | 例句：The islanders are friendly inhabitants of this remote archipelago.  岛民是这个偏远群岛友好的居民。

例句：The city's inhabitants are diverse in terms of their backgrounds and cultures. 这个城市的居民在背景和文化方面都很多样化。


近义词：residents, occupants, dwellers, population


反义词：None (反义词需根据语境而定，例如“outsiders”或“visitors”)


词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The inhabitants of the village celebrate their harvest festival annually.  村庄的居民每年庆祝丰收节。
Many inhabitants rely on fishing for their livelihood. 许多居民依靠捕鱼为生。


一般过去时:
The inhabitants of Pompeii perished during the volcanic eruption. 庞贝城的居民在火山爆发中丧生。
The ancient inhabitants left behind many artifacts. 古代居民留下了许多文物。


现在完成时:
The inhabitants have adapted to the harsh climate over generations. 居民们世世代代适应了恶劣的气候。
The inhabitants have witnessed significant changes in their community. 居民们目睹了他们社区的巨大变化。


过去完成时:
Before the invasion, the inhabitants had lived peacefully for centuries. 在入侵之前，居民们已经和平地生活了几个世纪。
By the time the rescue team arrived, many inhabitants had already evacuated. 当救援队到达时，许多居民已经撤离了。


将来时:
The inhabitants will likely face challenges due to rising sea levels. 居民们可能会面临海平面上升带来的挑战。
The new policies will affect how the inhabitants live. 新政策将影响居民的生活方式。


虚拟语气:
If the inhabitants had been warned earlier, more lives could have been saved. 如果居民们早点得到警告，就能挽救更多生命。
I wish the inhabitants had access to better healthcare. 我希望居民们能够获得更好的医疗保健。


被动语态:
The area's inhabitants were affected by the earthquake. 该地区的居民受到了地震的影响。
The needs of the inhabitants are being addressed by the government. 政府正在解决居民的需求。


疑问句:
How many inhabitants does this town have? 这个镇有多少居民？
What are the concerns of the inhabitants? 居民们关心的问题是什么？


否定句:
The deserted town had no inhabitants.  荒废的城镇没有居民。
Not all inhabitants agreed with the proposed changes.  并非所有居民都同意拟议的改变。
