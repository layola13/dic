re-anchored:/ˌriˈæŋkərd/| v. |重新固定，重新确立|To secure or fasten something again; to re-establish something firmly.|例句：The boat was re-anchored in a safer location.  船在更安全的地方重新抛锚了。|例句：After the storm, they re-anchored their beliefs in the power of community. 风暴过后，他们重新确立了对社区力量的信念。|近义词：refastened, resecured, reinstated |反义词：unmoored, loosened

USAGE->

We re-anchor our strategy every quarter. = 我们每个季度都会重新调整战略。
