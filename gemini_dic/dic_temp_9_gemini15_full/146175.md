overexaggerates:/ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzædʒəreɪts/| v.|夸大其词，言过其实|To exaggerate excessively; to overstate.  It implies going beyond a simple exaggeration and making a claim that is clearly untrue or grossly inflated.|He overexaggerates his accomplishments to impress potential employers. 他夸大自己的成就以给潜在雇主留下深刻印象。|exaggerate, amplify, inflate|understate, downplay, minimize|动词
