reenjoin:/ˌriːɪnˈdʒɔɪn/| vt.|重新加入；再次命令；再结合|To join again; to command or order again; to unite or join together once more.|例句：The separated pieces were reenjoined with glue.  分离的碎片用胶水重新粘合在一起。|例句：The judge reenjoined the parties to settle their dispute. 法官再次命令双方解决他们的争议。|近义词： rejoin, recombine, reunite, reorder|反义词：separate, disconnect, disjoin
