conformable:/kənˈfɔːrməbl/| adj.|一致的；符合的；顺从的|Agreeable; in accordance with or compliant to; readily yielding to; submissive.  |例句：The new data are conformable with the existing theory.  新的数据与现有理论一致。

例句：He is a conformable child, always obeying his parents. 他是一个很听话的孩子，总是服从父母。


近义词：compatible, consistent, compliant, obedient

反义词：inconsistent, discordant, rebellious, disobedient

词源：from Old French *conformable, from Latin *conformabilis, from conformāre "to shape, fashion, conform," from com- "together" + formāre "to shape."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The data is conformable to the model. = 数据与模型一致。
The fabric is conformable to the body. = 这布料贴合身体。


一般过去时:
The results were conformable to our predictions. = 结果与我们的预测一致。
His behavior was conformable to the rules. = 他的行为符合规定。


现在完成时:
The evidence has been conformable to the hypothesis. = 证据一直符合假设。
She has remained conformable to her principles. = 她一直坚持自己的原则。


过去完成时:
The previous findings had been conformable to the current research. = 之前的发现与目前的研究所符合。
He had always been conformable to his father's wishes. = 他一直都顺从父亲的意愿。


将来时:
The new regulations will be conformable to international standards. = 新规章将符合国际标准。
The design will be conformable to the client's requirements. = 设计将符合客户的要求。


虚拟语气:
If the results were conformable to the theory, we would publish the findings. = 如果结果符合理论，我们就发表研究结果。
I wish the evidence were more conformable to our hypothesis. = 我希望证据更符合我们的假设。


被动语态:
The design was made conformable to the building codes. = 设计符合建筑规范。
The software is being made conformable to the new standards. = 软件正在修改以符合新的标准。


疑问句:
Is the proposal conformable to company policy? = 这个提案符合公司政策吗？
Are the measurements conformable to the specifications? = 测量结果符合规格吗？


否定句:
The data are not conformable to the model. = 数据与模型不一致。
His actions were not conformable to the law. = 他的行为不符合法律。
