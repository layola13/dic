signifiers:/ˈsɪɡnɪfaɪərz/| n. |能指；标志物|The word "signifiers" refers to the linguistic or visual elements that convey meaning in a system of signs.  In semiotics, it's the perceptible aspect of a sign, distinct from its signified meaning.  For example, the word "tree" is a signifier for the concept of a tree.  |The image of a dove is often used as a signifier of peace.  The red light is a signifier of danger.|近义词：symbols, indicators, markers|反义词：signified (in semiotic terms)
