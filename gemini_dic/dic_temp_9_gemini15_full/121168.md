misdialing:/ˌmɪsˈdaɪəlɪŋ/ | n. |拨错号码，误拨 |The act of dialing a wrong telephone number.  It can be caused by human error or technical malfunction. |例句：My misdialing led to a very awkward conversation.  我的误拨导致了一场非常尴尬的谈话。

例句：The frequent misdialing suggests a problem with the phone system.  频繁的拨错号码表明电话系统存在问题。

近义词：wrong number, accidental dialing, incorrect dialing | 反义词：correct dialing, accurate dialing | 词性：名词
