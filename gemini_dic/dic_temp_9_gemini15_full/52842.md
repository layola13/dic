dimension: /daɪˈmɛnʃən/ | n. |维度；方面；尺寸；大小 |

英文解析:A measurable extent of something, such as length, breadth, depth, or height; a measure in one direction.  It can also refer to a particular aspect or feature of a situation, problem, or thing.

例句：
The dimensions of the room are 10 feet by 12 feet. = 这个房间的尺寸是10英尺乘12英尺。

The social dimension of the problem is very important. = 这个问题的社会方面非常重要。


近义词：aspect, facet, side, parameter, extent, size, measurement

反义词：point (in the sense of lacking extension)


词性: 名词
