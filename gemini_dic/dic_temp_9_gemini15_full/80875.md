governabilities:/ˌɡʌvərˈnəbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可治理性；管理能力 | The ability of a system or organization to be governed effectively.  This encompasses factors such as the clarity of its structure, the effectiveness of its processes, and the responsiveness of its leadership.  It refers to the ease with which a system can be controlled and managed. | Example:  The governabilities of the two countries differed significantly, leading to contrasting political outcomes.  两个国家的可治理性差异显著，导致了截然不同的政治结果。 |近义词：manageability, controllability,  ruleability | 反义词：ungovernability, uncontrollability, chaos | 名词
