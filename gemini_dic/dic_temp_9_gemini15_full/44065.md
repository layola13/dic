crinkle:/ˈkrɪŋkəl/| v. n. adj.|起皱，褶皱；发出沙沙声|To form or become creased or wrinkled; to make a crackling or rustling sound.|例句：The paper crinkled in his hand. 纸在他手里皱了。 The leaves crinkled underfoot. 叶子在脚下沙沙作响。|近义词：wrinkle, crease, crumple|反义词：smooth, flatten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fabric crinkles easily. = 这布料很容易起皱。
The leaves crinkle in the breeze. = 叶子在微风中沙沙作响。

一般过去时:
The old photograph crinkled when I touched it. = 我触摸旧照片时，它皱了起来。
The paper crinkled as I folded it. = 我折叠纸张时，它发出沙沙声。

现在完成时:
The sun has crinkled the paint on the car. = 太阳把汽车上的油漆晒皱了。
The constant use has crinkled the edges of the book. = 经常使用已使书的边缘起皱。

过去完成时:
The letter had crinkled in his pocket. = 信在他口袋里已经皱了。
The newspaper had crinkled from being in the rain. = 报纸淋雨后已经皱了。

将来时:
The heat will crinkle the plastic. = 热量会使塑料起皱。
The wind will crinkle the surface of the water. = 风会使水面起皱。

虚拟语气:
If I squeezed it harder, it would crinkle more. = 如果我更用力挤压它，它会更皱。
I wish my clothes wouldn't crinkle so easily. = 我希望我的衣服不会这么容易起皱。

被动语态:
The fabric was crinkled by the heat. = 布料被热量弄皱了。
The paper was crinkled by the machine. = 纸张被机器弄皱了。

疑问句:
Does the material crinkle easily? = 这材料容易起皱吗？
Does the sound of crinkling leaves annoy you? = 沙沙作响的树叶声会让你烦吗？

否定句:
The silk doesn't crinkle easily. = 这丝绸不容易起皱。
The new fabric didn't crinkle when I washed it. = 我洗了新布料后它没有起皱。
