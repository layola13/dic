homeopathic:/ˌhoʊmiəˈpæθɪk/| adj.|顺势疗法的；同种疗法的|Relating to or denoting a system of medicine based on the belief that a disease can be cured by a substance that produces similar symptoms in healthy people.  It involves using highly diluted substances.| 例句：She believes in homeopathic remedies for her allergies. 她相信顺势疗法可以治疗她的过敏症。
例句：The clinic offers both conventional and homeopathic treatments. 这家诊所提供常规治疗和顺势疗法。|近义词：alternative, complementary|反义词：allopathic, conventional
