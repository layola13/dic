英文解析:
The word "slipshodnesses" is the plural form of "slipshodness," which refers to the state or quality of being slipshod.  Slipshod means careless, negligent, or done in a hurried and haphazard manner. It implies a lack of attention to detail and a disregard for proper standards.


例句:

The slipshodnesses in their work led to numerous errors.  他们工作中的马虎导致了无数的错误。

His slipshodnesses were evident in the poorly constructed argument. 他的粗心在他的论证结构中显而易见。

词源:

The word "slipshod" originates from the combination of "slip" and "shod."  "Slip" refers to something loose or easily coming undone, while "shod" relates to footwear.  The original image was likely that of a shoe that was loosely attached or worn down at the heel.  This sense of looseness and lack of precision extended to encompass a general carelessness.
