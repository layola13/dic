masticating:/ˈmæstɪkeɪtɪŋ/| v.|咀嚼；反复思考|The act of chewing food thoroughly with the teeth.|例句：He sat there masticating his food slowly and thoughtfully. 他坐在那里慢慢地、若有所思地咀嚼着食物。

例句：The lawyer was masticating the details of the contract, trying to find a loophole.  律师正在仔细推敲合同的细节，试图找到漏洞。


近义词：chewing, munching, ruminating, pondering


反义词：swallowing (直接吞咽)


词性：动词
