preassumption:/ˌpriːəˈsʌmpʃən/| n.|预设，预想，预先假设|An assumption made before having sufficient evidence.  A thing that is assumed to be true before there is proof. |例句：His preassumption that she would agree proved to be incorrect.  他的预想她会同意被证明是错误的。

例句：The analysis rests on a number of questionable preassumptions.  这项分析基于许多值得怀疑的预设。


近义词：presupposition, assumption, premise


反义词：proof, evidence, verification


词性：名词
