exfoliated:/ɪkˈsfoʊliˌeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 脱落的；剥落的；已去角质的 |  Describes something that has had its outer layer removed, often through a process of shedding or peeling.  This typically refers to skin, but can also apply to other materials. | 例句：Her skin looked smoother after she used the exfoliated cream. 她用了去角质霜后，皮肤看起来更光滑了。

The outer layers of the rock had exfoliated over time.  岩石的外层随着时间的推移而剥落了。|近义词：desquamated, peeled, shed, sloughed | 反义词：intact, unshed, unpeeled |形容词
