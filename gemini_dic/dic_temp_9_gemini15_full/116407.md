mat:/mæt/| n.|垫子，席子|A piece of material, typically thick and flat, used as a covering for a floor or other surface, or as something to sit or lie on.|例句：I put a mat down by the door to wipe my feet. 我在门边放了一块垫子擦脚。|例句：The yoga mat was sticky with sweat. 瑜伽垫上粘着汗水。|近义词：rug, carpet, doormat|反义词:None
