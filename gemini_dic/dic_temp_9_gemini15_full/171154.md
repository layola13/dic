词源:
The word "psychodramatic" is derived from "psychodrama," a combination of "psycho" (from Greek *psychē*, meaning "soul" or "mind") and "drama" (from Greek *dran*, meaning "to do" or "to act").  Therefore, psychodramatic literally refers to something related to acting out psychological issues.
