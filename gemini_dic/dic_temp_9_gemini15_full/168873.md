profaning: /prəˈfeɪnɪŋ/ | v. |亵渎；污损；辱骂 |The act of treating something sacred with contempt or disrespect; defiling or violating something considered holy or sacred.  It can also refer to the use of profane or obscene language. |例句：Profaning religious symbols is a serious crime in many cultures.  在许多文化中，亵渎宗教象征是一种严重的罪行。

Profaning the sanctity of the temple resulted in widespread outrage. 亵渎圣殿的圣洁激起了广泛的愤怒。


近义词：desecrating, defiling, violating, abusing, insulting | 反义词：reverencing, honoring, respecting, sanctifying

词源：
From Old French *profaner, from Latin profanus ("outside the temple," "unholy"), from pro- ("before," "in front of") + fanum ("temple").  The meaning shifted from simply being "outside the temple" to implying disrespect or contamination of sacred things.
