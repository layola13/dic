comprehensibly:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhensəbli/ /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhensəbli/ | adv. | 可理解地；明白地 |  In a way that is easy to understand.  It describes how something is expressed or done. | 例句：He explained the problem comprehensibly. 他清晰地解释了这个问题。  The instructions were written comprehensibly. 说明书写得很清楚。 | 近义词：understandably, clearly, intelligibly | 反义词：unintelligibly, confusingly, obscurely
