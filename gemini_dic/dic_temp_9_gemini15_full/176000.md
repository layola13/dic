raucously:/ˈrɔːkəsli/ | adj. |喧闹地；粗声粗气地 | Making or characterized by a disturbingly loud and harsh noise.  |例句：The crowd cheered raucously.  人群喧闹地欢呼。

例句：The children played raucously in the park. 孩子们在公园里喧闹地玩耍。


近义词：noisily, boisterously, clamorously, loudly

反义词：quietly, softly, silently


词源:  Derived from "raucous," meaning harsh and grating, which in turn comes from the Old French "rauque," meaning hoarse.
