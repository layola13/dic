separation:/ˌsɛpəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |分离；隔开；分居；断裂 | The state of being separated; the act of separating or the process of being separated.  It can refer to physical separation, emotional separation, or legal separation. |例句：The separation of church and state is a fundamental principle in many countries.  教与国的分离是许多国家的根本原则。

The separation of the two groups caused conflict.  两组的分离导致了冲突。


近义词：division, parting, divorce, detachment, severance | 反义词：union, joining, combination, merger
