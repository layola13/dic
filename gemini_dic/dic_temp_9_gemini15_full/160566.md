porous: /ˈpɔːrəs/ /ˈpʊərəs/ | adj. | 多孔的；透水的；渗透性的 | Having minute spaces or holes through which liquid or air may pass.  | The porous soil allowed the water to drain quickly.  这多孔的土壤让水快速排干了。  The porous membrane allowed small molecules to pass through. 这层多孔的膜允许小分子通过。| permeable, permeable, pervious | impermeable, impervious, solid

一般现在时:
The sponge is porous. = 这个海绵是多孔的。
This material is porous and breathable. = 这种材料多孔且透气。

过去完成时:
The ancient vase had become porous over time. = 古代花瓶随着时间的推移变得多孔。

虚拟语气:
If the material were more porous, it would absorb more water. = 如果材料更透气，它就会吸收更多水。
I wish the soil were less porous so the plants wouldn't dry out so quickly. = 我希望土壤不那么多孔，这样植物就不会很快干枯。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
