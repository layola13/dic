commissionable: /kəˈmɪʃənəbl/ | adj. | 可委托的；可授权的；可获得佣金的 | Capable of being commissioned; able to be given a commission;  able to earn a commission.  |


例句：

The artwork is commissionable, meaning you can request a custom piece from the artist.  = 这件艺术品是可以委托创作的，这意味着你可以向艺术家请求定制一件作品。

This position offers a commissionable salary structure, meaning your earnings are partly based on sales. = 这个职位提供可获得佣金的薪酬结构，这意味着你的收入部分取决于销售额。


近义词：commissionable,  payable


反义词：uncommissionable, non-commissionable


词性：形容词
