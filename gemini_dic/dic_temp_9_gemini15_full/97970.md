inobedient: /ˌɪnəˈbiːdiənt/ | adj. | 不服从的，顽皮的 | Disobedient; refusing to obey.  | The inobedient child refused to eat his vegetables. 这个不服从的孩子拒绝吃他的蔬菜。 |近义词：disobedient, unruly, defiant, refractory |反义词：obedient, compliant, docile


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is inobedient to his parents. = 他对父母不服从。
The dog is inobedient and won't sit when told. = 这只狗很顽皮，叫它坐下也不听。


一般过去时:
She was inobedient as a child. = 她小时候很顽皮。
The students were inobedient and disrupted the class. = 学生们不服管教，扰乱了课堂。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
