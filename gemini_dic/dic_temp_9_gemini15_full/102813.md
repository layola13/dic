jargonist:/ˈdʒɑːɡənɪst/| n. |行话使用者；专业术语滥用者|A person who habitually uses jargon, often excessively or inappropriately.  They may be perceived as obscure or pretentious. |例句：He's a jargonist; his presentations are full of technical terms that most people don't understand. 他是个行话使用者；他的演讲充满了大多数人听不懂的专业术语。|近义词：technician, specialist, expert (in a negative context)|反义词：layperson, non-specialist
