crosshead:/ˈkrɒsˌhed/| n. |十字头；交叉头；阀杆；活塞杆端部|A crosshead is a component in a reciprocating engine that converts the reciprocating motion of the piston into rotary motion, or vice versa.  It typically has a sliding connection to the piston rod and a rotating connection to the crankshaft through connecting rods. |例句：The crosshead guides the piston rod's movement in a straight line. = 十字头引导活塞杆沿直线运动。The engine's crosshead was worn and needed replacing. = 发动机的十字头磨损了，需要更换。|近义词：crossbeam, headstock (in some contexts)|反义词：N/A
