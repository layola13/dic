plateful: /ˈpleɪtfl/ | n. | 一盘食物；满满一盘 | A plateful refers to the amount of food that can fill a plate.  It implies a generous portion. | Example:  He ate a plateful of spaghetti. 他吃了一大盘意大利面。

Example: She served a plateful of cookies. 她端上了一盘满满的饼干。


近义词: helping, portion, serving | 反义词:  少量 (a small amount),  a little
