refills:/ˈriːfɪlz/| n. |补充；续杯；替换装|Refills refer to the act of filling something again, or the items used to refill something.  This can refer to anything from refilling a coffee cup to replacing ink cartridges in a printer.|例句：I need some refills for my pens. 我需要一些笔芯替换装。

例句：The restaurant offers free refills on soft drinks. 这家餐厅提供软饮免费续杯。


近义词：replacements, replenishments |反义词：emptyings, depletions |词性:名词
