nonpolemically: /ˌnɒnˌpəʊləˈmɪkəli/ | adv. | 不好斗地；不争论地；不引发争议地 |  Without controversy or argument; in a way that avoids causing contention. | 例句：The author presented the complex issue nonpolemically, focusing on facts rather than opinions. 作者以不引发争议的方式呈现了这个复杂的问题，关注事实而非观点。


例句：He addressed the controversial topic nonpolemically, aiming for a neutral perspective. 他以不争论的方式处理了这个有争议的话题，力求保持中立的视角。


近义词：impartially, objectively, neutrally, dispassionately


反义词：contentiously, polemically, aggressively, controversially


词源：来自 "non-" (否定前缀) + "polemic" (争论，辩论) + "-ally" (副词后缀).  "Polemic" 源自希腊语 "polemos" (战争)。
