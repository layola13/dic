rechromatographs:/ˌriːˈkroʊmə.tə.ɡræfs/| v.|再色谱分析|To chromatograph again; to subject to chromatography a second time.  This term implies a repetition of the chromatography process, often to improve separation or purification.|例句：The scientists rechromatographed the sample to achieve better separation of the components.  科学家们再次对样品进行色谱分析，以更好地分离各组分。

例句：After the initial chromatography yielded impure results, the researchers decided to rechromatograph the sample using a different stationary phase. 初次色谱分析结果不纯后，研究人员决定使用不同的固定相再次进行色谱分析。


近义词：reanalyze, rerun (in the context of chromatography)
