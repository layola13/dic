refloat:/riːˈfloʊt/ | vt. |使重新浮起；使恢复；使重新活跃|To cause (something that has sunk or become submerged) to float again; to revive or restore something to a better condition or position.|例句：The crew managed to refloat the damaged ship. 船员们设法使受损的船只重新浮起。
The company is trying to refloat its image after the scandal.  公司正试图在丑闻后重塑其形象。|近义词：float, revive, restore |反义词：sink, submerge
