handbasket:/ˈhændˌbæskɪt/| n. |提篮；困境|A handbasket is a small basket carried by hand.  The idiom "to go to the dogs/handbasket" means to deteriorate rapidly or completely fail.|Example: She carried her groceries in a handbasket. 她用提篮提着杂货。|Example: The project went to the handbasket after the funding was cut.  在资金被削减后，项目彻底失败了。|近义词：basket, hamper|反义词：None (for the noun);  success (for the idiom)|名词
