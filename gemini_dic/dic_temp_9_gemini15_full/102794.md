jardine:/ˈdʒɑːrdɪn/| n. |雅典娜花园；贾丁（姓氏）|Jardine is primarily a surname of Scottish origin, but it can also refer to a garden or a park, particularly one associated with a grand estate or stately home.  The name's meaning is often linked to "garden" or a place of cultivation.|The Jardine family is known for its extensive business dealings in China during the 19th century.  Many places and institutions bear the Jardine name, reflecting this history. |近义词:garden, park (when referring to a place) |反义词:None specifically.
