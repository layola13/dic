copy-edit:/ˈkɒpiˌɛdɪt/ /ˈkɑːpiˌɛdɪt/ | vt. |校对，编辑（文本）|To review and correct written material for errors in grammar, style, and content.  |The editor will copy-edit the manuscript before publication.  编辑会在出版前校对稿件。|edit, proofread, revise|


近义词：edit, proofread, revise
反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She copy-edits the newspaper articles every day. = 她每天都校对报纸文章。
He copy-edits his own work before submitting it. = 他在提交自己的作品之前会自己校对。


一般过去时:
I copy-edited the report last night. = 我昨晚校对了这份报告。
They copy-edited the book before sending it to the printer. = 他们把书送去印刷厂之前校对过了。


现在完成时:
She has copy-edited three chapters this week. = 她本周已经校对了三章。
He has copy-edited the entire manuscript. = 他已经校对了整篇稿件。


过去完成时:
I had copy-edited the article before the deadline. = 我在截止日期前已经校对了这篇文章。
They had copy-edited the document before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经校对了文件。


将来时:
She will copy-edit the document tomorrow. = 她明天将校对这份文件。
He will copy-edit the thesis before submission. = 他会在提交论文前校对论文。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would copy-edit the entire book. = 如果我有更多时间，我会校对整本书。
I wish I had copy-edited the essay more carefully. = 我真希望我更仔细地校对过这篇论文。


被动语态:
The manuscript was copy-edited by a professional editor. = 这份稿件由一位专业编辑校对。
The article is being copy-edited at the moment. = 这篇文章正在被校对。


疑问句:

否定句:
