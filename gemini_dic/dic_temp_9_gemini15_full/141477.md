opinionately:/əˈpɪnjənətli/ | adv. | 固执地；武断地 | In a stubborn or obstinate manner, expressing opinions forcefully and without regard for others' views.  | He opinionately refused to consider any alternative solutions. 他固执地拒绝考虑任何其他解决方案。 | stubbornly, dogmatically, headstrong | flexibly, openly, considerately | 副词
