purflew:/ˈpɜːrfluː/ | n. | 滚边；镶边 | A decorative border of a contrasting color or material, often added to clothing or other textiles.  | The dress had a delicate purflew of lace. 这件衣服有一圈精致的花边滚边。 |近义词：border, edging, trim |反义词：plain (lacking decoration) |名词

词源:
