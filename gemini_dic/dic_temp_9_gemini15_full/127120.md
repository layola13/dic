native:/ˈneɪtɪv/| adj. n.|本国的；本地的；土著的；天生的；|a person, animal, or plant that originates in a particular place; belonging originally to a place; inherent; natural|The kangaroo is native to Australia. 袋鼠原产于澳大利亚。|He is a native speaker of English. 他是英语母语人士。|近义词：indigenous, aboriginal, inherent, innate|反义词：foreign, alien, acquired

USAGE->
一般现在时:
His native language is Spanish. = 他的母语是西班牙语。
She is a native of California. = 她是加州人。

一般过去时:
His grandparents were natives of Ireland. = 他的祖父母是爱尔兰人。
The ancient civilization thrived in their native land. = 古代文明在他们的故乡蓬勃发展。

现在完成时:
She has remained a native of this small town all her life. = 她一生都居住在这个小镇。
They have only recently become natives of this country. = 他们最近才成为这个国家的居民。

过去完成时:
Before moving to the city, she had always been a native of the countryside. = 在搬到城市之前，她一直是乡下人。
He had lived in his native village for twenty years before leaving. = 他离开之前，在他的家乡村庄住了二十年。

将来时:
She will remain a native of this town for many years to come. = 她还会在这个镇上住很多年。
Will he ever return to his native country? = 他会回到他的祖国吗？


虚拟语气:
If I were a native speaker, I would understand this better. = 如果我是母语人士，我会更好地理解这一点。
I wish I were a native of this beautiful place. = 我希望我是这个美丽地方的本地人。

被动语态:
This species is considered native to the Amazon. = 这个物种被认为原产于亚马逊地区。
The plants were native to the mountainous regions. = 这些植物原产于山区。


疑问句:
Are you a native of this city? = 你是这个城市的本地人吗？
Is this plant native to North America? = 这种植物是北美特产吗？

否定句:
He is not a native English speaker. = 他不是以英语为母语的人。
That species is not native to this environment. = 那个物种并非原产于这个环境。
