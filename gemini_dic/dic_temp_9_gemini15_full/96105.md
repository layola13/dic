indefinitenesses:/ˌɪndɪˈfɪnɪtnəsɪz/| n. |不确定性；模糊性；含糊不清 |The multiple interpretations of the poem contribute to its overall indefinitenesses.  |This lack of clarity leads to several indefinitenesses in the contract.|近义词：vagueness, ambiguity, uncertainty|反义词：clarity, precision, definiteness|名词


英文解析:The plural form of "indefiniteness," referring to instances or aspects of being indefinite; lacking clear, precise definition or meaning; vague, ambiguous, or uncertain.  It signifies a multiplicity of unclear or imprecise elements.


例句：

The artist's use of blurred lines and muted colors contributes to the painting's indefinitenesses.  艺术家的模糊线条和柔和色彩的使用增强了绘画的不确定性。


The committee's report was riddled with indefinitenesses, making it difficult to understand their conclusions.  委员会的报告充满了含糊不清之处，使人们难以理解他们的结论。


近义词：ambiguities, uncertainties, vaguenesses, obscurities


反义词：certainties, clarities, precisions, specificities


词性：名词 (plural)
