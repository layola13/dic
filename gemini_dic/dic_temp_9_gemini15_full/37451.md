commissar:/ˈkɒmɪsɑːr/ | n. |人民委员；专员；政治委员 | A commissar is a member of a communist party appointed to supervise or control a particular area or activity.  The term is particularly associated with the Soviet Union. |Example:  During the Soviet era, commissars held significant political power within the military and other organizations.  在苏联时代，政治委员在军队和其他组织中拥有重要的政治权力。|近义词：commissioner, official, supervisor |反义词：subordinate, follower
