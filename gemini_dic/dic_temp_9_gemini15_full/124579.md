mournings:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋz/| n.|哀悼；丧服；悼念活动|The period of sorrow and grief following a death;  acts of expressing sorrow or grief.  It can refer to the period of mourning itself, the rituals associated with it, or the clothing worn as a sign of mourning.|例句：The mournings after his death lasted for weeks. 他死后的哀悼持续了数周。

例句：Her black clothing was a sign of her mournings. 她的黑衣是她哀悼的标志。


近义词：grieving, lamentations, sorrow, bereavement|反义词：celebration, rejoicing, festivities|词性:名词
