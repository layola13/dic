dissonance:/ˈdɪsənəns/| n. |不和谐，不一致；不协调音；冲突 |A lack of harmony or agreement.  It can refer to a clash of sounds, ideas, or feelings. |例句：The dissonance between his words and actions was striking.  他的言行不一令人震惊。|例句：The harsh dissonance of the music jarred the listener. 刺耳的乐音使听者感到不快。|近义词：discord, conflict, inconsistency |反义词：harmony, consonance, agreement
