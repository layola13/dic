philhellenic:/ˌfɪləˈhɛnɪk/| adj.|亲希腊的；亲希腊主义的|Showing a strong interest in and admiration for Greece, especially its ancient culture and history.  The term often implies support for Greece, especially during times of conflict or political instability.|例句：Many philhellenic intellectuals in the 19th century actively supported Greek independence from the Ottoman Empire.  许多19世纪的亲希腊知识分子积极支持希腊脱离奥斯曼帝国的独立。|近义词：pro-Greek, Graecophil, Hellenophile|反义词：anti-Greek, Turcophil
