orbiculated:/ˈɔːrbɪkjuleɪtɪd/| adj.|  环状的；圆形的；球形的 | Having a series of concentric circles or rings; resembling an orb.  The term often describes patterns in rocks or other natural formations. |例句：The orbicular granite showed distinct concentric rings of different minerals.  这块球状花岗岩展现出不同矿物的清晰同心环。

例句：The orbiculated texture of the stone was quite striking. 这块石头的环状纹理非常引人注目。


近义词：circular, ringed, concentric, spherical

反义词：linear, angular, irregular


词性:形容词
