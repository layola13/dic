medivaced:/ˈmɛdɪveɪkt/| vt.|医疗后送，医疗撤离|To evacuate someone by medical transport, especially by air.|例句：The injured soldiers were medivaced to a field hospital. 受伤的士兵被医疗后送至野战医院。


例句：The civilian casualties were immediately medivaced from the conflict zone.  平民伤亡人员立即从冲突地区被医疗后送。
