charbroiler:/ˈtʃɑːrbroʊlər/ | n. |炭火烤架；烧烤炉 | A charbroiler is a cooking appliance that uses intense heat from below to cook food, typically meat, by charring or searing the surface.  It's often used in restaurants and kitchens for grilling steaks, burgers, and other similar items. | The restaurant boasts a new charbroiler for perfectly seared steaks. 这家餐厅拥有一个新的炭火烤架，可以烤出完美的牛排。  | grill, griddle |  (无明显反义词) | 名词
