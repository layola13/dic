USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and doesn't have verb conjugations.  The usage is entirely dependent on the context of the sentence, primarily indicating possession or ownership relating to a cent.

Example 1: The cent's value has decreased over time.  一分钱的价值随着时间推移而下降。

Example 2: That toy only cost a cent's worth.  那个玩具只值一分钱。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "cent's" as a possessive form.  Phrases typically involve "cent" without the possessive apostrophe,  such as "a few cents", "make cents", etc.
