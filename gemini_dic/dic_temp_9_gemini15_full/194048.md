self-gotten: /ˌsɛlfˈɡɑːtn̩/ | adj. | 自得的；自己获得的 |  Acquired or obtained by oneself through one's own efforts, often implying a lack of external help or inheritance.  It suggests the achievement was earned independently, sometimes with connotations of questionable or illicit means. | 例句：His self-gotten wealth was the source of much envy.  他自得的财富是许多人羡慕的对象。

His self-gotten education was a source of pride. 他自力更生的教育让他引以为傲。


近义词：self-acquired, self-made, earned, own


反义词：inherited, bequeathed, given, unearned


词性:形容词
