purposelessly:/ˈpɜːrpəsləsli/ | adv. | 无目的地；漫无目的地；毫无意义地 |  Acting or done without purpose or aim.  It implies a lack of direction or intention. |例句：He wandered purposelessly through the streets. 他漫无目的地在街上游荡。

He stared purposelessly out the window. 他无目的地望着窗外。


近义词：aimlessly, pointlessly, without purpose, randomly | 反义词：purposefully, intentionally, deliberately
