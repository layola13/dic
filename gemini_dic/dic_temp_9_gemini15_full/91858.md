hyperimmunize:/ˌhaɪpərˈɪmjʊnaɪz/| vt.|高免疫，超免疫接种|To produce a high level of immunity in (a person or animal), typically by repeated injections of an antigen.  This process aims to create a much stronger and more sustained immune response than a typical immunization.|例句：The scientists hyperimmunized the mice with the specific antigen to study the immune response. 科学家们用特异性抗原对小鼠进行了超免疫接种，以研究其免疫反应。

例句：The patient was hyperimmunized with a series of booster shots to combat the severe infection. 病人接受了一系列加强注射以对抗严重的感染。


近义词：immunize, vaccinate | 反义词：weaken immune system
