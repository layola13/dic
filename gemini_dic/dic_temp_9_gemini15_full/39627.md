consequencing:/ˈkɒnsɪkwɛnsɪŋ/|/ˈkɑːnsɪkwɛnsɪŋ/| v. |产生后果；导致；招致|The act of causing consequences; bringing about results, often negative.  It emphasizes the process of causing consequences rather than the consequences themselves.|例句：His reckless actions are consequencing significant financial losses for the company. = 他鲁莽的行为正在给公司造成巨大的经济损失。
例句：The government's policies are consequencing widespread social unrest. = 政府的政策正在导致广泛的社会动荡。|近义词：resulting in, leading to, causing, bringing about|反义词：preventing, averting, avoiding|动词
