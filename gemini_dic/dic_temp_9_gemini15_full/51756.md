devote:/dɪˈvoʊt/| vt.|献身；致力于；专心于|to give one's time, effort, or attention earnestly to something: to dedicate oneself to a cause or activity.|例句：She devoted her life to helping others. 她把一生都献给了帮助他人。|例句：He devoted himself to his work. 他全身心地投入到工作中。|近义词：dedicate, commit, consecrate|反义词：neglect, ignore|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He devotes his weekends to gardening. = 他把周末都用来园艺。
She devotes much of her time to charity work. = 她把大部分时间都花在慈善工作上。

一般过去时:
They devoted years to researching the disease. = 他们花了数年时间研究这种疾病。
He devoted his youth to the pursuit of knowledge. = 他把青春献给了知识的追求。

现在完成时:
She has devoted her whole career to education. = 她把她整个职业生涯都献给了教育事业。
He has devoted countless hours to this project. = 他已经为这个项目投入了无数的时间。

过去完成时:
They had devoted themselves to the cause long before I joined. = 在我加入之前，他们就已经长期致力于这项事业了。
She had devoted many years to perfecting her technique. = 她已经花了许多年时间来完善她的技术。

将来时:
I will devote more time to my family next year. = 明年我将花更多时间陪伴家人。
They will devote their resources to developing new technology. = 他们将把资源投入到开发新技术上。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would devote it to learning a new language. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会把它用来学习一门外语。
I wish I could devote more time to my hobbies. = 我希望我能花更多时间在我的爱好上。

被动语态:
Much time was devoted to the discussion of the budget. = 大量时间被用于讨论预算。
His entire life has been devoted to serving his country. = 他的一生都致力于为国家服务。


疑问句:
How much time do you devote to exercise each week? = 你每周花多少时间锻炼？
What aspects of your life do you devote the most time to? = 你生活中哪些方面投入了最多时间？

否定句:
He doesn't devote enough time to his studies. = 他没有花足够的时间学习。
She didn't devote herself fully to the task. = 她没有全身心地投入到这项任务中。


PHRASE->
devote oneself to = 献身于
devote time to = 花时间在…上
devote attention to = 专注于
devote resources to = 将资源用于
devote energy to = 投入精力于
