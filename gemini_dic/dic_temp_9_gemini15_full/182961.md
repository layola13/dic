replaces:/rɪˈpleɪsɪz/ | vt. |取代，替换 |To take the place of (someone or something).|例句：The old system has been replaced by a new one. 旧系统已被新的系统取代了。|例句：She replaced the broken vase with a new one. 她用一个新的花瓶替换了破损的花瓶。|近义词：substitute, supplant, exchange |反义词：retain, keep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new model replaces the old one. = 新型号取代了旧型号。
This medication replaces the previous one. = 这种药取代了之前的药。

一般过去时:
They replaced the damaged parts. = 他们更换了损坏的部件。
He replaced the broken window. = 他更换了破损的窗户。

现在完成时:
The company has replaced its CEO. = 公司已经更换了首席执行官。
She has replaced all the old furniture. = 她已经更换了所有旧家具。

过去完成时:
They had replaced the engine before the race. = 他们在比赛前已经更换了引擎。
She had replaced the tires before the long journey. = 长途旅行前，她已经更换了轮胎。

将来时:
We will replace the outdated equipment next year. = 我们明年将更换过时的设备。
They will replace the roof soon. = 他们很快就会更换屋顶。

虚拟语气:
If they replaced the software, the problems would be solved. = 如果他们更换软件，问题就能解决。
I wish they had replaced the faulty component earlier. = 我希望他们早点更换有故障的部件。

被动语态:
The old car was replaced by a new one. = 旧车被一辆新车取代了。
The broken screen is being replaced. = 破损的屏幕正在被更换。

疑问句:
What will replace the current system? = 什么将取代目前的系统？
Does this part replace the old one? = 这个部件能替换旧的吗？

否定句:
The software didn't replace the existing one. = 该软件没有替换现有的软件。
They haven't replaced the batteries yet. = 他们还没更换电池。


PHRASE->
replace with = 用...替换
replace A with B = 用B替换A
replace something broken = 更换损坏的东西
be replaced by = 被…取代
replace oneself = 替代自己
