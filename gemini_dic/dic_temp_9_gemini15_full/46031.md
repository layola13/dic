cyberthreat: /ˈsaɪbərθrɛt/ | n. | 网络威胁 | A threat that exploits vulnerabilities of computer systems or networks to gain unauthorized access, disrupt services, or steal data.  It encompasses a wide range of malicious activities, from viruses and malware to phishing scams and denial-of-service attacks. | Example: The company invested heavily in cybersecurity to mitigate the growing cyberthreat landscape. 这家公司大力投资网络安全以减轻日益增长的网络威胁态势。 | 近义词：cyberattack, online threat, digital threat, internet threat | 反义词：cybersecurity, network protection, data security | 名词
