oaks:/oʊks/| n.|橡树；橡树林|The word "oaks" is the plural form of "oak," referring to multiple trees of the genus *Quercus*.  Oaks are known for their acorns, strong wood, and longevity. They are often symbols of strength and endurance.|例句：The ancient oaks stood tall against the stormy wind. 古老的橡树在暴风雨中屹立不倒。
The park is famous for its majestic oaks.  这个公园以其雄伟的橡树而闻名。|近义词：trees, timber|反义词：无|名词
