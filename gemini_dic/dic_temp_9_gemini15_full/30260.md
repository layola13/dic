celtiberian:/ˌsɛltiˈbɪəriən/| adj. n.|凯尔特伊比利亚的；凯尔特伊比利亚人|Of or relating to the Celtiberians, a people of ancient Hispania (Spain and Portugal) who were a mixture of Celtic and Iberian peoples.  The term can also refer to their language and culture.|例句：The Celtiberian artifacts unearthed at the site offer valuable insights into their culture.  凯尔特伊比利亚人在该遗址出土的文物为了解他们的文化提供了宝贵的见解。

The Celtiberian language, though largely extinct, is still being studied by linguists. 尽管凯尔特伊比利亚语在很大程度上已经灭绝，但语言学家仍在对其进行研究。|近义词：Hispano-Celtic|反义词：无
