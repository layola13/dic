for-real:/fɔːrˈriːəl/|/adj.|真的；确实的；认真的|Seriously; genuinely; in earnest.  The phrase emphasizes truthfulness and sincerity.|例句：Is that for real?  = 那是真的吗？

例句：He was for-real angry. = 他是真的生气了。


近义词：really, truly, genuinely, seriously

反义词：fake, pretend, phony, insincere


词性:形容词
