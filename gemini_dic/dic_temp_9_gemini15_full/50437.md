deprecates:/ˈdɛprɪkeɪts/| vt.|贬低；批评；反对；使贬值；(计算机)弃用|to express disapproval of; to belittle; to represent as being of little worth; (computing) to discontinue the use of (a feature or element)|The company deprecates the use of its older software.  这家公司不鼓励使用旧软件。|The critic deprecates the author's style. 批评家贬低作者的文风。|appreciate, praise, commend|extol, laud, acclaim|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He deprecates the use of violence. 他反对使用暴力。
She deprecates his efforts. 她贬低他的努力。


一般过去时:
They deprecated the old system. 他们否定了旧系统。
The manager deprecated the employee's behavior.  经理批评了员工的行为。


现在完成时:
The company has deprecated several features in the latest update. 公司在最新更新中弃用了一些功能。
We have deprecated that approach. 我们已经放弃了那种方法。


过去完成时:
They had deprecated the old method before introducing the new one. 他们在引进新方法之前已经否定了旧方法。
The committee had deprecated the proposal before the final vote.  委员会在最终投票前已否决了该提案。


将来时:
They will deprecate the outdated technology. 他们将淘汰过时的技术。
The government will deprecate harmful practices. 政府将取缔有害行为。


虚拟语气:
If they deprecated the project, we would have to find another solution. 如果他们否决了这个项目，我们就得另寻他法。
I wish they had deprecated that policy earlier. 我希望他们早点废除那项政策。


被动语态:
The old system was deprecated by the company. 旧系统已被公司弃用。
His ideas were deprecated by his colleagues. 他的想法遭到同事们的反对。


疑问句:
Does the company deprecate the use of this software?  公司是否不鼓励使用这款软件？
Does he deprecate your work? 他是否贬低你的工作？


否定句:
He doesn't deprecate her achievements. 他并没有贬低她的成就。
The company does not deprecate the use of open-source tools.  公司并不反对使用开源工具。
