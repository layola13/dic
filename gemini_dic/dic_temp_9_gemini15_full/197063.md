shamateurs:/ˌʃæmətˈɜːrz/| n.|半吊子，冒牌专家|Someone who pretends to have more skill or knowledge than they actually possess, often in a pretentious or boastful way.  They appear to be amateur but try to present themselves as professionals.|例句：He's a shamateur when it comes to cooking; his dishes look impressive but taste awful. 他在烹饪方面是个半吊子；他的菜看起来很不错，但味道很糟糕。


例句：The shamateur investor lost all his money in the stock market. 这个半吊子投资者在股市中赔光了所有的钱。|近义词：dilettante, pretender, charlatan, poser|反义词：expert, professional, master
