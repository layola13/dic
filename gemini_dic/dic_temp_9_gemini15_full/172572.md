pyromalate:/paɪrəˈmeɪleɪt/ | n. |焦苹果酸盐 |Pyromalate is a salt or ester of pyromalic acid.  It's formed by the heating of malic acid, resulting in the loss of a water molecule. Pyromalates are less common than malic acid itself and are often encountered in the context of chemical research or specialized industrial applications. |例句：The study investigated the synthesis and properties of various metal pyromalates. 这项研究调查了各种金属焦苹果酸盐的合成和特性。 |近义词：malate (related compound)|反义词：无 |名词
