expurgator:/ˈɛkspərɡeɪtər/| n.| 删节者；净化者 | A person who expurgates, especially a person who removes objectionable material from books or other texts.  | Example: The expurgator meticulously removed all references to violence from the children's book.  删节者一丝不苟地从儿童读物中删除了所有关于暴力的内容。|近义词：censor, editor (in a specific context)|反义词：none specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who adds inflammatory content.
