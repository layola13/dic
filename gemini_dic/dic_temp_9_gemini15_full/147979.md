overretentions:/ˌoʊvərˌriˈtɛnʃənz/| n.|过度保有；滞留；保留过多|The excessive retention of information or resources beyond what is necessary or optimal.  This can apply to various contexts, including inventory management, data storage, and even emotional baggage.  |例句：The company's overretentions of inventory led to significant storage costs. 公司库存积压过多导致了巨大的仓储成本。|例句：Her overretentions of past grievances prevented her from moving on. 她对过去的不满耿耿于怀，无法向前看。|近义词：hoarding, accumulation, retention, surplus|反义词：depletion, release, disposal|名词
