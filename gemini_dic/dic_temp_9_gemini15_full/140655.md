ombre:/ˈɒmbr/ /ˈɔːmbr/| adj. n.| 阴影；渐变色；光影效果 |  Describing a gradual shading or blending of colors, often from dark to light.  As a noun, it refers to this effect or technique.  | 例句：Her hair featured a beautiful ombre effect, transitioning from dark brown to honey blonde.  她的头发呈现出美丽的渐变色效果，从深棕色过渡到蜂蜜金色。

The ombre dress showcased a stunning gradient of blues. 这件渐变色连衣裙展示了令人惊叹的蓝色渐变。|近义词：gradation, shading, gradient, blend |反义词：uniform, solid, monochromatic |形容词，名词
