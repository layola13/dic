cosiest:/ˈkoʊziːst/ | adj. | 最舒适的，最温馨的 | Most comfortable and warm; giving a feeling of great comfort, warmth, and relaxation. |  例句：This is the cosiest room in the house. 这是房子里最舒适的房间。

例句：The fire made the room feel incredibly cosiest.  壁炉使房间感觉非常舒适。


近义词：comfiest, snuggliest, most comfortable, warmest | 反义词：uncomfortable, drafty, cold, bleak


词性：形容词 (最高级)
