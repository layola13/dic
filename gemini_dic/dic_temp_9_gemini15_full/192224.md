scrawled:/skrɔːld/| v. |潦草地写，乱涂 |To write or draw something in a careless, hurried way, often illegibly.  |例句：He scrawled his signature on the document. 他在文件上潦草地签了名。|例句：The graffiti artist scrawled obscene words on the wall.  涂鸦艺术家在墙上乱涂写着淫秽的字眼。|近义词：scribble, doodle, scrawl, daub |反义词：write neatly, print


USAGE->

He scrawls his notes quickly. = 他快速地潦草地记笔记。
She scrawls a message on the napkin. = 她在一张餐巾纸上潦草地写下一条信息。
