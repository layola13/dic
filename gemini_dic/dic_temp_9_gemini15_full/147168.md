overloyalties:/ˌoʊvərˈlɔɪəltiz/ | n. |过度忠诚；盲目效忠 | Excessive or misplaced loyalty.  It often implies a loyalty that is detrimental or harmful to oneself or others.  The term suggests a loyalty that surpasses reason or appropriate boundaries. |Example:  His overloyalties to his old boss prevented him from seeing the flaws in the company's new strategy. = 他对他前任老板的过度忠诚，让他无法看到公司新战略中的缺陷。


His overloyalties to his family led him to make poor financial decisions. = 他对家人的过度忠诚导致他做出了糟糕的财务决定。|近义词：blind loyalty, uncritical allegiance, fanatical devotion |反义词：objectivity, impartiality, critical thinking |名词
