pressies:/ˈpresiz/| n. |小礼物，小赠品 |Small gifts, typically presents given to children or as small tokens of appreciation.  The word is informal and chiefly used in British English. |例句：The children were thrilled with their pressies.孩子们对他们的礼物很兴奋。

例句：She gave everyone little pressies as a thank you. 她给了每个人一些小礼物表示感谢。


近义词：gifts, presents, souvenirs, trinkets

反义词：None directly opposite, but could consider "nothing" or "no gifts" as antonyms in context.

词性：名词
