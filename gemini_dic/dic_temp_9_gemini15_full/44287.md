crook:/krʊk/| n., v.| 骗子；弯曲；欺骗；使弯曲 | A crook is a dishonest person, especially one who is involved in crime.  The word can also describe something bent or curved. As a verb, it means to bend or to cheat. |例句：He's a crook; he stole the money. 他是个骗子；他偷了钱。 The road crooks sharply to the left. 这条路急剧向左弯曲。近义词：thief, swindler, cheat, bend, curve | 反义词：honest person, straight, upright |
