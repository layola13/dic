词源:
The word "nonhydraulic" is a compound word.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "hydraulic" comes from the Greek words "hydor" (ὕδωρ), meaning "water," and "aulos" (αὐλός), meaning "pipe."  Therefore, "hydraulic" refers to systems using water or other liquid under pressure.  "Nonhydraulic" simply negates this meaning.
