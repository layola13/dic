malignancies:/məˈlɪɡnənsiz/| n. |恶性肿瘤，恶性疾病|Harmful growths of cells that tend to spread to other parts of the body.  Malignancies are a serious health concern and require prompt medical attention.|Example: The doctor confirmed the presence of malignancies in his lungs. 医生确认他的肺部存在恶性肿瘤。  The early detection of malignancies is crucial for successful treatment.  早期发现恶性肿瘤对于成功治疗至关重要。|近义词：cancers, tumors, neoplasms|反义词：benign growths, healthy tissue|名词
