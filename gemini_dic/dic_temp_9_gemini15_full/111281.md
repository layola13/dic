litters:/ˈlɪtərz/| n. v.|垃圾；杂乱；一窝（幼崽）|The act of scattering or spreading something over an area; a large number of things scattered untidily.|The dog littered the yard with toys.  狗把玩具扔得到处都是。|The messy room was littered with clothes.  凌乱的房间里到处都是衣服。|近义词：mess, clutter, debris|反义词：cleanliness, order


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The cat litters in the garden. = 猫在花园里乱扔垃圾。
He often litters the street with trash. = 他经常在街上乱扔垃圾。


一般过去时:
She littered the park with candy wrappers. = 她把糖纸扔得到处都是公园里。
They littered the beach with plastic bottles. = 他们把塑料瓶扔得到处都是海滩上。


现在完成时:
The city has implemented a new litter prevention program. = 该城市实施了一项新的防止乱扔垃圾的计划。
We have littered this beautiful place. = 我们把这个美丽的地方弄脏了。


过去完成时:
They had littered the area before the cleanup crew arrived. = 清洁人员到达之前，他们已经把这个区域弄脏了。
The park had been littered with rubbish all week. = 公园里一星期以来都堆满了垃圾。


将来时:
I will not litter the streets anymore. = 我以后不会再乱扔垃圾了。
They will litter the park if we don't put bins there. = 如果我们不放垃圾桶，他们会在公园里乱扔垃圾。


虚拟语气:
If I had known that littering was illegal, I wouldn't have done it. = 如果我知道乱扔垃圾是违法的，我不会那样做的。
I wish he hadn't littered the park with his rubbish. = 我真希望他没有把垃圾扔在公园里。


被动语态:
The streets are littered with rubbish. = 街道上到处都是垃圾。
The park was littered with broken glass. = 公园里到处都是碎玻璃。


疑问句:
Does he litter often? = 他经常乱扔垃圾吗？
Did they litter the forest? = 他们在森林里乱扔垃圾了吗？


否定句:
We did not litter the beach. = 我们没有在海滩上乱扔垃圾。
She doesn't litter. = 她不乱扔垃圾。

street litter: 街头垃圾
