interflow:/ˈɪntərfloʊ/| n. |交互流动；互相影响；相互作用|The mutual exchange or passage of something between places or groups.  It often refers to the movement of goods, information, or ideas between different entities.|例句：The interflow of ideas between the two countries led to significant cultural exchange.  两个国家之间的思想交流导致了重大的文化交流。

例句：The interflow of capital between developed and developing nations is crucial for global economic growth. 发达国家和发展中国家之间的资本流动对于全球经济增长至关重要。


近义词：interaction, exchange, interplay, circulation |反义词：isolation, separation, stagnation |词性：名词
