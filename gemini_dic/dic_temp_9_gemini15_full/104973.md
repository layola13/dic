ken:/ken/| n. v.|知识，理解；能；了解；认识|To know or understand something; to be aware of something; to have the ability to do something.|例句：I ken the way. 我知道路。 He kens his limitations. 他了解自己的局限性。|近义词：know, understand, comprehend, perceive|反义词：ignore, misunderstand, be ignorant of|名词，动词
