re-polished:/ˌriːˈpɒlɪʃt/| adj. |重新抛光的；重新润色的|  Having been polished again; improved or refined further.  It implies a previous polishing and a subsequent improvement. |例句：The re-polished silverware gleamed under the lights.  重新抛光的银器在灯光下闪闪发光。

The re-polished manuscript was much clearer.  重新润色的手稿清晰多了。|近义词：refined, improved, enhanced|反义词：rough, unpolished, tarnished
