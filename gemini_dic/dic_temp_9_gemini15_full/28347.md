carapaces:/ˈkærəpeɪsɪz/ | n. |甲壳（复数） |A carapace is a shell or hard outer covering of an animal, such as a turtle or crab.  It provides protection and support. |例句：The tortoises' carapaces were beautifully patterned. 这些乌龟的甲壳图案精美。|例句：The crabs scuttled away, their carapaces clicking against the rocks.  螃蟹迅速逃走，它们的甲壳在岩石上发出咔嗒声。|近义词：shell, exoskeleton,  crust |反义词：soft skin, unprotected body
