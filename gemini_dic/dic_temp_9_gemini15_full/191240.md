scath:/skæθ/| vt.|痛斥；严厉批评；伤害|To criticize severely or angrily; to inflict injury or damage on.  |例句：The critic scathed the author's new novel. 批评家严厉批评了作者的新小说。
The storm scathed the crops.暴风雨损坏了庄稼。|近义词：criticize, rebuke, denounce, lambaste, lacerate|反义词：praise, commend, applaud


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scaths his opponent's arguments. 他痛斥对手的论点。
The editor scaths the poorly written article. 编辑严厉批评了这篇文章写得不好。
