regret:/rɪˈɡrɛt/ | v. n. | 后悔，遗憾；惋惜 | to feel sorry about something you have done or about something that has happened |  I regret telling him the truth. 我后悔告诉他真相了。|近义词：repent, rue | 反义词：rejoice, celebrate | 动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I regret my decision. = 我后悔我的决定。
We regret to inform you that... = 我们很遗憾地通知你…


一般过去时:
I regretted missing the concert. = 我后悔错过了这场音乐会。
She regretted what she had said. = 她后悔自己说过的话。


现在完成时:
I have regretted this action ever since. = 从那以后我就一直后悔这个举动。
They have regretted their hasty decision. = 他们一直后悔自己仓促的决定。


过去完成时:
I had regretted my decision before I even made it. = 我甚至在做决定之前就已经后悔了。
He had regretted his words before he finished speaking. = 他话还没说完就后悔了。


将来时:
I will regret this if I don't try. = 如果我不尝试，我会后悔的。
She will regret not studying harder. = 她会后悔没有更努力学习。


虚拟语气:
I would regret it if I didn't go. = 如果我不去，我会后悔的。
I wish I hadn't said that; I regret it now. = 我真希望我没说过那些话；我现在后悔了。


被动语态:
His absence was deeply regretted. = 他的缺席令人深感遗憾。
The decision was regretted by many. = 许多人都对这个决定感到遗憾。


疑问句:
Do you regret anything? = 你后悔什么吗？
Will you regret this later? = 你以后会后悔吗？


否定句:
I don't regret anything I've done. = 我不后悔我所做的一切。
He didn't regret his choice. = 他不后悔他的选择。


PHRASE->
regret to say = 遗憾地说
regret to inform you = 遗憾地通知你
deep regret = 深切的遗憾
express regret = 表达遗憾
feel regret = 感到遗憾
