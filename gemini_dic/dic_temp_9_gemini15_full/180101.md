reflourish:/ˌriːˈflʌrɪʃ/| v.|再次繁荣；重新兴盛|To flourish again; to become prosperous or successful once more.  The word combines "re-" (again) and "flourish" (to thrive).|例句：After a period of decline, the town is beginning to reflourish.  这个小镇在经历了一段衰落期后，开始重新繁荣起来。

例句：The company, after years of losses, is reflourishing thanks to new management.  这家公司在经历了多年的亏损后，由于新的管理层而重新兴盛起来。


近义词：revive, recover, regenerate, revitalize

反义词：decline, decay, deteriorate, fade
