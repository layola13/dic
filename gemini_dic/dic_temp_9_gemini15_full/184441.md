resprinkle: /ˌriːˈsprɪŋkəl/ | vt. | 再次撒上；再洒上 | To sprinkle again; to sprinkle something over something else a second time. | 例句：He resprinkled the cake with powdered sugar. 他再次在蛋糕上撒上糖粉。


例句：She resprinkled the lawn with water after the hot sun dried it out. 炎热的太阳晒干了草坪后，她又洒了一遍水。


近义词：re-dust, re-scatter,  sprinkle again | 反义词：collect, gather


词性：动词
