cleave-to:/kliːv tuː/ | v. |坚持；忠于；依附 | To adhere firmly to something, especially a belief, principle, or person.  |例句：He cleaved to his principles despite the pressure. 他不顾压力坚持自己的原则。 He cleaved to his wife through thick and thin.  他风雨同舟地陪伴着妻子。|近义词：adhere to, cling to, stick to |反义词：abandon, forsake, desert


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cleaves to his beliefs. = 他坚持他的信仰。
They cleave to tradition. = 他们坚持传统。


一般过去时:
She cleaved to her family during difficult times. = 在困难时期，她坚持依靠她的家人。
He cleaved to his promise. = 他遵守了他的诺言。


现在完成时:
They have cleaved to their original plan. = 他们一直坚持最初的计划。
I have cleaved to my principles throughout my career. = 在我的职业生涯中，我一直坚持我的原则。


过去完成时:
She had cleaved to her faith for many years. = 她多年来一直坚守她的信仰。
He had cleaved to the idea even after it proved impractical. = 即使这个想法被证明不切实际，他仍然坚持。


将来时:
She will cleave to her ideals, no matter what. = 无论发生什么，她都会坚持自己的理想。
He will cleave to his decision. = 他将坚持他的决定。


虚拟语气:
If he cleaved to his training, he would succeed. = 如果他坚持训练，他就会成功。
I wish I had cleaved to my original plan. = 我真希望我坚持最初的计划。


被动语态: (Cleaving to something is rarely used passively)  此短语很少用被动语态。  难以构造自然的被动语态例句。


疑问句:
Does he still cleave to those outdated beliefs? = 他仍然坚持那些过时的信仰吗？
To what principles do they cleave? = 他们坚持什么原则？


否定句:
He did not cleave to the rules. = 他没有遵守规则。
She chose not to cleave to her family's expectations. = 她选择不屈从于她家人的期望。


PHRASE->
cleave to tradition = 坚持传统
cleave to one's principles = 坚持原则
cleave to one's faith = 坚守信仰
cleave to the truth = 坚持真理
cleave to the past = 缅怀过去


词源:
The word "cleave" itself has Old English roots, stemming from *cleofan, meaning "to split" or "to divide."  However,  "cleave to" developed a completely opposite meaning over time, signifying a firm adherence rather than a separation. This is an example of semantic drift in language.
