helm:/hɛlm/| n. |舵，方向盘；领导地位；掌舵者 |The ship's helm was broken.  船的舵坏了。|  The company is under new helm. 这家公司由新的领导掌管。|近义词：rudder, control, leadership |反义词：following, subordination


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The captain holds the helm. = 船长握着舵柄。
She helms the project. = 她领导这个项目。


一般过去时:
He helmed the ship through the storm. = 他驾船驶过暴风雨。
The experienced sailor helmed the boat skillfully. = 经验丰富的船员熟练地掌舵。


现在完成时:
They have helmed the company for ten years. = 他们掌管这家公司十年了。
She has skillfully helmed the organization through many challenges. = 她巧妙地带领组织克服了许多挑战。


过去完成时:
He had helmed the vessel for many years before retirement. = 他退休前掌舵多年。
The captain had helmed the ship safely through many storms. = 船长曾安全地驾驶船只度过多次暴风雨。


将来时:
She will helm the new department. = 她将领导新部门。
He will helm the ship across the ocean. = 他将驾船横渡海洋。


虚拟语气:
If he helmed the ship, we would have arrived sooner. = 如果他掌舵，我们本会早点到达。
If she had helmed the project differently, it might have succeeded. = 如果她采用不同的方式领导项目，它也许会成功。


被动语态:
The ship was helmed by an experienced captain. = 船由一位经验丰富的船长掌舵。
The project is being helmed by a new team. = 项目由一个新的团队领导。


疑问句:
Who is helming the project? = 谁在领导这个项目？
Who helmed the ship during the emergency? = 紧急情况下谁在掌舵？


否定句:
He didn't helm the ship properly. = 他没有正确地掌舵。
She didn't helm the company effectively. = 她没有有效地领导公司。
