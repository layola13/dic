recutting:/ˌriːˈkʌtɪŋ/| n. |重新剪辑；再剪辑|The act of cutting something again, typically film or audio, to improve or change it.  This often involves rearranging existing footage or audio to create a different effect or narrative.|例句：The director ordered a recutting of the film to improve its pacing.  导演下令重新剪辑电影以改善其节奏。

The editor spent weeks on the recutting of the documentary. 编辑花了数周时间重新剪辑纪录片。|近义词：re-editing, reassembly|反义词：original cut, first cut|名词
