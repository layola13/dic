plateaus:/plæˈtoʊz/ | n. | 高原；平台期；稳定时期 | A plateau is a relatively high, flat area of land.  It can also refer to a period of little or no change in a particular trend or process. | 例句：The hikers reached a high plateau offering stunning views.  徒步旅行者到达了一个高高的高原，那里风景优美。

The company's growth has plateaued in recent months. 近几个月来，公司的增长已经进入了平台期。| 近义词：highland, tableland, standstill, stagnation | 反义词：decline, descent, drop, fall


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The plateau stretches for miles. = 这片高原绵延数英里。
This project is currently on a plateau. = 这个项目目前处于平台期。

一般过去时:
The economy plateaued after a period of rapid growth. = 经济在经历了一段快速增长后趋于平稳。
The temperature plateaued at 30 degrees Celsius. = 温度稳定在摄氏30度。

现在完成时:
The sales figures have plateaued since last quarter. = 自上一季度以来，销售额一直保持稳定。
The patient's condition has plateaued, showing no significant improvement or decline. = 病人的病情已趋于稳定，没有明显好转或恶化。

过去完成时:
The negotiations had plateaued before the final breakthrough. = 在最终取得突破之前，谈判已经陷入僵局。
The research had plateaued, leading to a shift in research direction. = 研究工作已经停滞不前，导致研究方向发生了转变。

将来时:
The project is expected to plateau next year. = 预计该项目明年将进入平台期。
We anticipate the market will plateau in the coming months. = 我们预计市场将在未来几个月趋于平稳。

虚拟语气:
If the negotiations had not plateaued, a deal would have been reached sooner. = 如果谈判没有陷入僵局，协议会更快达成。
I wish the project hadn't plateaued so early. = 我希望这个项目不要这么早进入平台期。

被动语态:
The progress of the research was plateaued by funding limitations. = 研究的进展因资金限制而停滞不前。
The growth of the business was plateaued by the economic downturn. = 该业务的增长因经济低迷而停滞不前。

疑问句:
Has the disease plateaued yet? = 这种疾病已经稳定了吗？
Will the stock price plateau soon? = 股价很快会稳定吗？

否定句:
The project did not plateau as expected. = 该项目并未如预期般进入平台期。
The negotiations did not plateau, leading to a quick resolution. = 谈判没有陷入僵局，从而迅速达成解决方案。
