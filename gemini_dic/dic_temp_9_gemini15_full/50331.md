deplores:/dɪˈplɔːrz/| vt.|谴责，对…感到遗憾|To express strong disapproval of something.  It often implies a feeling of sadness or regret as well as disapproval.|例句：She deplores the violence in the city. 她谴责这座城市的暴力行为。|例句：He deplores the lack of opportunity for young people. 他对年轻人缺乏机会感到遗憾。|近义词：condemns, criticizes, disapproves of, regrets|反义词：approves of, praises, commends|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government deplores the recent attacks. = 政府谴责最近的袭击事件。
She deplores the state of the environment. = 她对环境状况感到遗憾。

一般过去时:
The committee deplored the company's actions. = 委员会谴责了该公司的行为。
He deplored the loss of innocent lives. = 他对无辜生命的丧失感到悲痛。

现在完成时:
They have deplored the decision for weeks. = 他们已经谴责这个决定几周了。
The public has deplored the lack of transparency. = 公众对缺乏透明度表示谴责。

过去完成时:
The judge had deplored the defendant's behavior. = 法官曾谴责被告的行为。
We had deplored the situation for months before acting. = 在采取行动之前，我们已经对这种情况感到遗憾了几个月。


将来时:
They will deplore the consequences of their actions. = 他们将会为他们的行为承担后果而感到遗憾。
The world will deplore the loss of such a talented artist. = 世界将为失去如此有天赋的艺术家而感到悲痛。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would deplore the way they treat their staff. = 如果我是你，我会谴责他们对待员工的方式。
I wish they would deplore the injustice. = 我希望他们能谴责这种不公正行为。

被动语态:
The policy was deplored by many. = 许多人谴责这项政策。
His actions were deplored by his family and friends. = 他的行为受到家人和朋友的谴责。

疑问句:
Does she deplore the current political climate? = 她是否谴责当前的政治气候？
Do you deplore the violence in the news? = 你谴责新闻中的暴力事件吗？

否定句:
He does not deplore the government's decision. = 他并不谴责政府的决定。
She didn't deplore the changes, in fact she welcomed them. = 她并不谴责这些改变，事实上她欢迎这些改变。


PHRASE->
deplore the lack of…= 谴责…的缺乏
deplore the violence = 谴责暴力
deplore the situation = 对局势表示遗憾
deplore the loss of…= 对…的损失表示遗憾
deeply deplore = 深感遗憾


词源:
From Old French *deplorer, from Latin deplorare "to weep over, lament," from de- "down, away" +plorare "to weep."
