mimer:/ˈmaɪmər/| n. |哑剧演员，模仿者 |A mimer is a person who performs mime, a form of theatrical performance using gestures and body movements without speech. They often tell stories or express emotions through their physical expressions. |例句：The mimer captivated the audience with his expressive movements.  哑剧演员以其富有表现力的动作吸引了观众。


The mimer's performance was both humorous and poignant.  哑剧演员的表演既幽默又感人。


近义词：mime artist, pantomimist |反义词：speaker, vocalist |词性:Noun
