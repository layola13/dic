reoxygenates:/ˌriːˈɒksɪdʒəneɪts/ | vt. | 再充氧；使重新充满氧气 | To supply with oxygen again; to restore the oxygen supply to.  | Example: The treatment reoxygenates the damaged tissue.  治疗使受损组织重新充满氧气。 The hyperbaric chamber reoxygenates the body. 高压氧舱使身体重新充满氧气。|近义词：re-oxygenate, revitalize (in the context of oxygen) |反义词：deoxygenate, asphyxiate
