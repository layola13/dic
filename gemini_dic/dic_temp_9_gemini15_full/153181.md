pelletable:/ˈpɛlətəbl/| adj. | 可制成颗粒的；适于制成颗粒的 | Capable of being made into pellets.  This term typically refers to materials that can be compressed or formed into small, cylindrical granules.  |例句：The feed was pelletable, making it easy to handle and store.  饲料可以制成颗粒状，便于搬运和储存。

The raw materials were thoroughly tested to ensure they were pelletable. 原材料经过彻底测试以确保其可以制成颗粒。


近义词：granulable, compactable | 反义词：ungranular, uncompactable | 词性：形容词
