non-interference: /ˌnɒnɪnˈtɜːfərəns/ | n. |不干涉；不干预 | The principle or policy of avoiding interference in the affairs of others, especially in the internal affairs of other countries.  This term emphasizes a hands-off approach, suggesting a deliberate choice to refrain from involvement. | Example: The country adopted a policy of non-interference in the internal affairs of other nations. 这个国家采取了不干涉别国内政的政策。


Example:  The international community's policy of non-interference allowed the civil war to continue unabated. 国际社会的非干涉政策导致内战持续不断。


Near synonyms:  neutrality, abstention, non-intervention


Antonyms: interference, intervention, meddling
