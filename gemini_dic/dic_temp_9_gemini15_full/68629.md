fantom:/ˈfæntəm/ | n. |幽灵；幻影；虚构的事物 | A phantom is a ghost or apparition; something seemingly unreal. It can also refer to something illusory or imagined.  |例句：The phantom of the opera haunted the theatre. 歌剧魅影萦绕着剧院。

The rumors proved to be a phantom. 谣言被证明是虚构的。|近义词：ghost, specter, apparition, illusion, chimera |反义词：reality, substance, truth
