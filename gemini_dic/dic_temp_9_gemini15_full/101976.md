isocercal:/ˌaɪsoʊˈsɜːrkəl/| adj. |等尾的，尾鳍对称的 | Having a tail fin that is symmetrical, with the upper and lower lobes approximately equal in size.  This is in contrast to a heterocercal tail, where the upper lobe is significantly larger.  The term is primarily used in ichthyology (the study of fish).| Example:  Many modern bony fishes possess an isocercal tail.  许多现代硬骨鱼拥有等尾鳍。

The shark's heterocercal tail, unlike the isocercal tails of most bony fish, provides lift.  鲨鱼的歪尾鳍与大多数硬骨鱼的等尾鳍不同，它可以提供升力。| 近义词：homocercal (often used interchangeably, though isocercal is more precise)| 反义词：heterocercal |  adj.
