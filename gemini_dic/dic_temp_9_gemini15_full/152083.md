paternoster:/ˈpætərˌnɒstər/| n.|念经，主祷文；电梯；|A paternoster is a continuous loop of chairs or compartments that move around a track, used for transporting people, typically in a church or monastery.  It can also refer to the Lord's Prayer. |例句：The old church still has a working paternoster. 这座古老的教堂仍然有一个可以使用的循环电梯。 The priest recited the paternoster.  牧师诵读了主祷文。|近义词：elevator (for the mechanical device), Lord's Prayer|反义词：None specifically, depends on context. |名词
