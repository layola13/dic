heightener: /ˈhaɪtnər/ | n. | 增強劑，增強物 | Something that intensifies or increases something else.  It can refer to a substance, a device, or an experience that boosts or magnifies a particular effect or quality. | The new software is a heightener of productivity.  这款新软件能提高生产力。| intensifier, amplifier, booster | reducer, diminisher


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The spice acts as a flavour heightener. = 这香料可以增强风味。
This music heightens the atmosphere. = 这音乐增强了气氛。


一般过去时:
The drama heightened the tension. = 这戏剧加剧了紧张气氛。

现在完成时:

She has heightened her awareness of her surroundings. = 她提高了她对周围环境的意识。


过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

I wish the music would heighten the mood of the party. = 我希望音乐能提升聚会的氛围。
