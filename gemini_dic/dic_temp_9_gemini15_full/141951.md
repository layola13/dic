ore:/ɔːr/| n.|矿石，矿砂|A naturally occurring mineral or rock containing one or more valuable minerals, typically metals, that can be profitably mined.|例句：Iron ore is used to make steel.  铁矿石用于制造钢铁。|例句：Gold ore is often found in veins in the earth. 金矿石通常在地球的矿脉中发现。|近义词：mineral, metal, vein|反义词：refined metal, processed material


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mine extracts iron ore daily. = 矿井每天开采铁矿石。
We process different types of ore. = 我们加工不同类型的矿石。

一般过去时:
They mined gold ore in that region last year. = 他们去年在那个地区开采金矿石。
