relocking:/ˌriːˈlɒkɪŋ/| v. |重新锁定；再次锁定|The act of locking something again, typically after it has been unlocked or opened.  This can refer to physical locks, digital security measures, or metaphorical locking mechanisms.|例句：The security system initiated automatic relocking after the door was opened. 安全系统在门被打开后自动重新锁定。

例句：After the power outage, we needed to perform a manual relocking of the database. 断电后，我们需要手动重新锁定数据库。


近义词：resecuring, refastening, locking again

反义词：unlocking, opening


词性:动词 (gerund)
