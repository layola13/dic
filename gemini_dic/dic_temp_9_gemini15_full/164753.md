prefectures:/ˈpriːfɛktʃərz/| n. |县；府；管区|Prefectures are administrative divisions, typically found in Japan, similar to counties or districts in other countries.  They are subdivisions of larger regions and manage local affairs. |Example:  Kyoto Prefecture is known for its beautiful temples and gardens. 京都府以其美丽的寺庙和花园而闻名。|近义词：counties, districts, regions|反义词：nation, country
