cripplehood:/ˈkrɪpəlˌhʊd/| n. |残疾状态，残疾生活|The state or condition of being crippled; the experience of living with a disability.  It often refers to the social and emotional challenges faced by individuals with disabilities, beyond the physical limitations.|例句：His cripplehood didn't prevent him from achieving his goals. = 他的残疾并没有阻止他实现目标。

例句：She wrote movingly about her experiences of cripplehood and the societal barriers she encountered. = 她动情地写下了她关于残疾和遇到的社会障碍的经历。


近义词：disability, impairment, handicap, affliction|反义词：abledness, health, wellness|词性：名词
