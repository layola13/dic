sateen:/sæˈtiːn/| n.|缎子|A type of fabric with a glossy surface, typically made of cotton, but sometimes silk or other fibers. It is woven with a satin weave, which gives it its characteristic sheen.  |Example: The dress was made of a luxurious, smooth sateen.  这件裙子是用奢华柔软的缎子做的。|近义词：satin,  glossy fabric|反义词：暂无
