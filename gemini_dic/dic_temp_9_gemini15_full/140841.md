on-the-beam: /ˌɒn ðə ˈbiːm/ | adj. | 正确的；精确的；符合标准的 | Accurate; precise; exactly right; perfectly aligned.  The phrase refers to something being perfectly aligned or functioning correctly, often in a technical or metaphorical sense.  It originates from the nautical term describing a ship's course being perfectly aligned with its intended direction. | 例句：His calculations were on-the-beam. 他的计算非常精确。

His prediction of the market trend was on-the-beam. 他对市场趋势的预测非常准确。
