ostracean:/ˈɒstreɪʃən/| n. | 牡蛎；贝类动物 | An ostracean is a mollusk belonging to the class Bivalvia, characterized by a shell composed of two hinged parts.  They are filter feeders and play a significant role in marine ecosystems. | Example:  The oyster is a common example of an ostracean.  牡蛎是贝类动物的一个常见例子。|近义词：bivalve, shellfish |反义词：无
