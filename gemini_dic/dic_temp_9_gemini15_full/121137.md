misdelivering:/ˌmɪsdɪˈlɪvərɪŋ/ | v. |错误投递，送错 |The act of delivering something to the wrong place or person.  It implies a failure in the proper delivery process. |例句：The postal service is being investigated for misdelivering sensitive documents. 邮政服务因错误投递敏感文件而正在接受调查。

The company apologized for misdelivering the package to the wrong address. 公司为包裹送错地址而道歉。

近义词：misdirecting, wrongly delivering, sending to the wrong place |反义词：delivering correctly, delivering accurately

USAGE->

The company misdelivers packages occasionally. = 公司偶尔会送错包裹。
