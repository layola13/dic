proselytises: /ˈprɒsəlaɪtaɪzɪz/ | v. | 传教；劝诱改宗 | To attempt to convert someone to one's own religious faith. | The missionary proselytized in the streets, sharing his beliefs with anyone who would listen.  传教士在街上传教，向任何愿意倾听的人分享他的信仰。| evangelize, convert, preach | remain unconverted,  resist conversion | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He proselytizes on street corners. = 他在街角传教。
She proselytizes for her new religion. = 她为她的新宗教传教。

一般过去时:
They proselytized among the villagers. = 他们在村民中传教。
The cult leader proselytized to his followers. = 邪教教主向他的追随者传教。

现在完成时:
He has proselytized for many years. = 他传教多年了。
She has proselytized extensively in Asia. = 她曾在亚洲广泛传教。

过去完成时:
They had proselytized in that region before. = 他们以前曾在那个地区传教。
The group had proselytized to many people before being discovered. = 该组织在被发现之前已经向许多人传教。

将来时:
He will proselytize in South America next year. = 他明年将在南美洲传教。
She will continue to proselytize despite opposition. = 她将继续传教，尽管遭到反对。

虚拟语气:
If he proselytized less aggressively, he might have more success. = 如果他不那么积极地传教，他可能会取得更大的成功。
I wish he wouldn't proselytize to me. = 我希望他不向我传教。


被动语态:
The new religion was proselytized throughout the country. = 这种新宗教在全国各地都被传教。
His ideas were proselytized by his followers. = 他的思想被他的追随者传播。


疑问句:
Does he proselytize often? = 他经常传教吗？
Why does she proselytize so aggressively? = 她为什么如此积极地传教？


否定句:
He doesn't proselytize anymore. = 他不再传教了。
She refused to proselytize to the unconverted. = 她拒绝向未皈依者传教。


PHRASE->
proselytize for (a cause)  为（某个事业）传教
proselytize to (someone) 向（某人）传教
aggressively proselytize  积极地传教
secretly proselytize 秘密传教
effectively proselytize  有效地传教
