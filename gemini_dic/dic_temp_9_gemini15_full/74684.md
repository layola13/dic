freedom-fighters: /ˈfriːdəmˌfaɪtərz/ | n. | 自由战士，斗士 | People who fight for freedom from oppression or injustice, often through armed struggle or other forms of resistance.  They are typically involved in a political or social movement aimed at achieving greater autonomy or liberation. | Example: The freedom fighters launched a guerrilla campaign against the occupying forces. 自由战士对占领军发起了游击战。|近义词：revolutionaries, insurgents, rebels, militants |反义词：oppressors, collaborators, tyrants | 名词
