pneumatophore:/ˈnjuːmætəfɔːr/|/ˈnjumætəˌfɔːr/| n. |气根；呼吸根 |A pneumatophore is an upward-growing root found in certain plants, typically those growing in swampy or waterlogged soils.  These roots facilitate gas exchange, allowing the plant to access oxygen in anaerobic conditions. |Example: Mangrove trees possess pneumatophores to obtain oxygen from the air.  红树林利用气根从空气中获取氧气。|近义词：aerial root, respiratory root |反义词：submerged root
