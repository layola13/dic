/ˌriːəˈfɒrɪstɪd/| adj. | 再造林的；重新造林的 | Having been replanted with trees after deforestation.  This term describes an area where trees have been planted to replace those that were previously cut down.  | Example: The reforested area is now teeming with wildlife.  再造林的区域现在充满了野生动物。  The hillside, once barren, is now beautifully reforested.  曾经荒凉的山坡现在被美丽的树林重新覆盖。|近义词：replanted, rewooded |反义词：deforested, denuded
