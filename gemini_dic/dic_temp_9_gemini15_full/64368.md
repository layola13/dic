erect:/ɪˈrekt/ | adj., v. | 直立的；竖立的；建造；建立 |  to be or make something straight up or upright; to build or construct something |近义词：upright, vertical, construct, build |反义词：lying down, prostrate, demolish, destroy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He erects a new fence every year. = 他每年都竖起一个新的栅栏。
The building stands erect. = 这座建筑物直立着。


一般过去时:
They erected a statue in the park. = 他们在公园里竖立了一座雕像。
The workers erected the scaffolding quickly. = 工人们迅速地搭起了脚手架。


现在完成时:
They have erected a new monument. = 他们已经竖立了一座新的纪念碑。
The company has erected several buildings in the city. = 这家公司已经在城里建造了几座建筑物。


过去完成时:
They had erected the tent before the rain started. = 在下雨之前，他们已经搭好了帐篷。
The builders had erected the framework by nightfall. = 建筑工人到夜幕降临前已经建好了框架。


将来时:
They will erect a new bridge across the river. = 他们将在河上建造一座新桥。
The government will erect a new hospital next year. = 政府明年将建造一所新医院。


虚拟语气:
If they erected a taller fence, the animals couldn't jump over it. = 如果他们竖起更高的栅栏，动物们就跳不过去了。
I wish they had erected a stronger structure. = 我希望他们建造了一个更坚固的结构。


被动语态:
A new building is being erected in the town center. = 在市中心正在建造一座新建筑。
The monument was erected in 1990. = 这座纪念碑是1990年竖立的。


疑问句:
Did they erect the fence properly? = 他们是否正确地竖起了栅栏？
Will the new building be erected on time? = 新建筑能否按时建成？


否定句:
They did not erect the building according to the plan. = 他们没有按照计划建造建筑物。
The workers haven't erected the scaffolding yet. = 工人们还没有搭好脚手架。
