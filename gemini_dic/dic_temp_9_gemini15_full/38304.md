conceal: /kənˈsiːl/ | vt. |隐藏；隐瞒；遮蔽 | to hide or keep secret; to prevent from being seen or known | 例句：She concealed her disappointment well. 她很好地掩盖了自己的失望。

例句：He tried to conceal the truth from the police. 他试图向警方隐瞒真相。


近义词：hide, disguise, mask, secrete, suppress | 反义词：reveal, disclose, expose, unveil


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conceals his feelings. 他隐藏他的感情。
They conceal the truth from the public. 他们向公众隐瞒真相。


一般过去时:
She concealed the evidence. 她隐藏了证据。
The magician concealed the rabbit in his hat. 魔术师把兔子藏在帽子里。


现在完成时:
He has concealed his true identity for years. 他多年来一直隐瞒着自己的真实身份。
They have concealed the documents from the authorities. 他们一直向当局隐瞒这些文件。


过去完成时:
She had concealed the letter before her husband came home. 她在她丈夫回家之前已经藏好了信。
They had concealed their intentions until the last moment. 他们直到最后一刻才透露他们的意图。


将来时:
He will conceal his intentions from his rivals. 他将向他的对手隐瞒他的意图。
They will conceal the weapons before the inspection. 他们将在检查前隐藏武器。


虚拟语气:
If he concealed the truth, he would avoid trouble. 如果他隐瞒真相，他就能避免麻烦。
I wish I had concealed my feelings better. 我真希望我当时能更好地掩饰我的感情。


被动语态:
The truth was concealed from the public. 真相被隐瞒了公众。
The documents were concealed in a secret compartment. 文件被藏在一个秘密隔间里。


疑问句:
Did he conceal the money? 他是否藏起了钱？
Where did she conceal the evidence? 她把证据藏在哪里了？


否定句:
He did not conceal his anger. 他没有掩饰他的愤怒。
She did not conceal her surprise. 她没有掩饰她的惊讶。


PHRASE->

词源:
Old French *conceler, from Latin *con- + celare "to hide".  The prefix "con-" indicates intensification or completion.
