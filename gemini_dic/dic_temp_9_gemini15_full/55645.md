dither:/ˈdɪðər/| v., n.|犹豫；颤抖；焦虑不安|To be indecisive; to act nervously or in a hesitant way; a state of trembling or nervous agitation.|例句：He dithered for weeks before finally making a decision. 他犹豫了好几周才最终做出决定。|例句：The old man's hands dithered as he tried to light his pipe. 老人试图点燃他的烟斗时，双手颤抖不已。|近义词：hesitate, waver, vacillate, tremble|反义词：decide, resolve, determine|动词，名词
