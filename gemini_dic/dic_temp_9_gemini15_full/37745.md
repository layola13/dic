compartner:/kəmˈpɑːrtnər/ | n. |合伙人，伙伴 | A compartner is someone who shares in a business or enterprise with another.  This word is largely obsolete and rarely used in modern English.  It implies a close partnership, often implying equal standing and shared responsibility. | Example:  He and his compartner invested heavily in the new venture. 他和他的合伙人大量投资了这个新企业。 |近义词：partner, associate, colleague |反义词：competitor, rival |名词
