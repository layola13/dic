polymathic:/ˌpɒliˈmæθɪk/ /ˌpɑːliˈmæθɪk/ | adj. | 博学的；多才多艺的 | Having or demonstrating knowledge or skill in many different areas.  A polymathic individual possesses expertise across a wide range of subjects. |  例句：Leonardo da Vinci was a remarkably polymathic figure, excelling in art, science, and engineering.  达芬奇是一位非常博学的杰出人物，在艺术、科学和工程方面都表现出色。


例句：Her polymathic interests ranged from ancient history to modern astrophysics. 她的博学兴趣涵盖了从古代历史到现代天体物理学等各个领域。


近义词：erudite, learned, scholarly, versatile, multitalented


反义词：ignorant, uneducated, unlearned, specialized


词性：形容词
