clutchingly: /ˈklʌtʃɪŋli/ | adv. | 紧紧地；抓紧地；拼命地 |  Describes an action performed in a grasping, desperate, or clinging manner, often implying anxiety or fear.  It suggests a strong, almost desperate effort to hold onto something. | 例句：He clutched his briefcase clutchingly as he ran through the crowded street. 他穿过拥挤的街道时紧紧地抓着公文包。


She clutched the railing clutchingly, her knuckles white with fear. 她紧紧抓住栏杆，害怕得指关节都发白了。


近义词：desperately, frantically, convulsively, tightly | 反义词：loosely, casually, calmly | 词性: 副词
