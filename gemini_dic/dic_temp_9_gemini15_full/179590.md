reemission:/ˌriːɪˈmɪʃən/| n. |再发射；再辐射；再散发|The process of emitting something again, especially light, radiation, or particles.  It often refers to the re-radiation of absorbed energy.  |Example: The reemission of infrared radiation from the Earth's surface contributes to the greenhouse effect.  地球表面的红外线再辐射导致温室效应。

The reemission of light from the phosphor in a fluorescent lamp causes it to glow. 荧光灯中磷光体的再发射使它发光。|近义词：re-radiation, secondary emission|反义词：absorption, attenuation
