developability:/ˌdɛvəlˈoʊpəbɪləti/ | n. |可发展性，可开发性 | The potential for something to be developed or improved.  It refers to the capacity or capability of something to be developed further. This could apply to various contexts, including land, technology, resources, or even individuals' skills. | Example: The developability of the land was assessed before construction began.  土地的可开发性在施工开始前进行了评估。 The developability of his musical talent is remarkable. 他音乐天赋的可发展性令人瞩目。|近义词：potential, feasibility, improbability |反义词：impossibility, infeasibility
