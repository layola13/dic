disobediently:/ˌdɪsoʊˈbiːdiəntli/| adv. |不服从地，违抗地 |In a disobedient manner; without obedience.  It describes the manner in which an action is performed, indicating a lack of compliance or submission to authority or rules.|例句：The child acted disobediently, refusing to clean his room.  孩子不服从地行事，拒绝打扫房间。

The dog disobediently ignored the command to sit.  狗不服从地无视了坐下指令。|近义词：rebelliously, defiantly, contumaciously |反义词：obediently, submissively, compliantly
