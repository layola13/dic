outwrites:/ˈaʊtraɪts/| v.|公开写作；公开表达；泄露|To write or express something publicly or openly; to reveal something that was previously secret or confidential.|例句：The journalist outwrote the government's attempts to suppress the truth.  记者公开报道了政府试图压制真相的企图。|例句：The whistleblower outwrote the company's illegal activities.  举报人公开了该公司非法的活动。|近义词：divulge, reveal, disclose, expose|反义词：conceal, suppress, hide, keep secret|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outwrites his colleagues on the blog. = 他在博客上比他的同事写得更多/更好。
She outwrites her opponents in the debate. = 她在辩论中比她的对手表达得更清晰有力。


一般过去时:
The newspaper outwrote the official statement. = 报纸公开报道的内容与官方声明相悖。

现在完成时:

虚拟语气:
