incidence:/ˈɪnsɪdəns/| n. |发生率；影响；事件|The incidence of disease is higher in this region.  该地区疾病的发病率较高。|The incidence of the problem is increasing.  这个问题的发生率正在上升。|近义词：occurrence, frequency, rate |反义词：absence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The incidence of heart disease is high among smokers. = 吸烟者的心脏病发病率很高。
The incidence of accidents is decreasing this year. = 今年事故发生率正在下降。


一般过去时:
The incidence of flu was particularly high last winter. = 去年冬天流感的发生率特别高。
The incidence of crime decreased significantly last year. = 去年犯罪率显著下降。


现在完成时:
The incidence of childhood obesity has risen dramatically in recent years. = 近年来儿童肥胖症的发病率急剧上升。
The incidence of cyberbullying has increased significantly since the advent of social media. = 自社交媒体出现以来，网络欺凌的发生率显著增加。


过去完成时:
By the end of the study, the incidence of the disease had decreased significantly. = 到研究结束时，该疾病的发病率已显著下降。
Before the new safety measures were implemented, the incidence of workplace accidents had been alarmingly high. = 在实施新的安全措施之前，工作场所事故的发生率高得惊人。


将来时:
Experts predict that the incidence of this disease will increase in the coming years. = 专家预测，未来几年这种疾病的发病率将会增加。
We hope that the incidence of traffic accidents will decrease next year. = 我们希望明年的交通事故发生率会下降。


虚拟语气:
If the incidence of crime were lower, people would feel safer. = 如果犯罪率降低，人们会感到更安全。
I wish the incidence of poverty were lower in our community. = 我希望我们社区的贫困发生率更低。


被动语态:
The incidence of the disease was reported to be increasing. = 据报道，该疾病的发病率正在上升。
The incidence of the problem is being carefully monitored by the government. = 政府正在密切监测这个问题的发生率。


疑问句:
What is the incidence of lung cancer in this population? = 在这个群体中，肺癌的发病率是多少？
What was the incidence of that disease last year? = 去年那种疾病的发病率是多少？


否定句:
The incidence of this particular disease is not high. = 这种特定疾病的发病率并不高。
The incidence of childhood leukemia has not decreased significantly. = 儿童白血病的发病率并没有显著下降。


PHRASE->
incidence rate = 发病率
incidence of disease = 疾病发生率
high incidence = 高发病率
low incidence = 低发病率
incidence and prevalence = 发病率和患病率
