gables:/ˈɡeɪbəlz/| n.|山墙；屋脊；三角墙 |The triangular portion of a wall at the end of a pitched roof.|The house has charming gables and dormer windows. 这栋房子有迷人的山墙和老虎窗。|近义词：roof, apex|反义词：base, foundation
