non-American: /ˌnɒn əˈmɛrɪkən/ | adj. | 非美国的 | Not relating to or characteristic of the United States of America.  This adjective describes something or someone that is not from or associated with the United States.  It can refer to people, cultures, products, or ideas.


例句：

He has a non-American accent. = 他有一个非美国口音。

The company uses non-American components in its products. = 这家公司在其产品中使用非美国组件。


近义词：foreign, international, overseas


反义词：American, US, domestic


词性：形容词
