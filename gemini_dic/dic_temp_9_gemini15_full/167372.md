prettying:/ˈprɛtiɪŋ/| v.|使漂亮；打扮；美化 |The act of making something pretty or more attractive.  It often implies a process of adornment or embellishment.|例句：She spent hours prettying herself up for the party. = 她花了几个小时打扮自己去参加派对。

例句：He was prettying the garden with colorful flowers. = 他正在用五彩缤纷的花朵美化花园。


近义词：beautifying, adorning, decorating, embellishing

反义词：uglifying, defacing, spoiling


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
She is prettying her room with new curtains. = 她正在用新的窗帘装饰她的房间。
They are prettying the cake with frosting. = 他们正在用糖霜装饰蛋糕。


一般过去时:
She prettied her nails with a fresh coat of polish. = 她用新涂的一层指甲油打扮指甲。
He prettied the presentation with relevant images. = 他用相关的图片美化了演示文稿。


现在完成时:
She has prettied up the living room for the guests. = 她已经为客人打扮了起居室。
They have prettied the garden with new plants. = 他们已经用新的植物美化了花园。


过去完成时:
She had prettied herself up before going to the date. = 在去约会之前，她已经打扮好了自己。
They had prettied the house before the arrival of their parents. = 在父母到达之前，他们已经把房子打扮好了。

虚拟语气:

否定句:
