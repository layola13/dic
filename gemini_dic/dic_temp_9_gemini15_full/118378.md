merl:/mɜːrl/| adj.|  杂色；混色的|  Describing a pattern of coloration in which different colors are mixed together irregularly, often in a mottled or dappled way.  This term is most commonly used to describe the coat of animals, particularly horses and birds.| 例句：The horse had a beautiful merl coat, a mix of brown and white. 这匹马有着美丽的杂色皮毛，棕色和白色混合在一起。| 例句：The bird's feathers showed a striking merl pattern.  这只鸟的羽毛呈现出醒目的杂色图案。|近义词：mottled, dappled, piebald, roan|反义词：uniform, solid, plain|形容词
