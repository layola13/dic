hurd:/hɜːrd/| n.|障碍；困难；跳栏；|A hurdle is an obstacle or difficulty that must be overcome.  It can also refer to a barrier in a race that must be jumped over.|例句：The biggest hurdle to overcome was funding the project.  最大的障碍是项目的资金问题。

The runner cleared the hurdle with ease.  跑步者轻松越过了栏杆。|近义词：obstacle, impediment, barrier, difficulty|反义词：ease, simplicity

The high jump hurdle presents a significant challenge. = 高台跳远中的栏杆带来了很大的挑战。
This hurdle requires significant effort to overcome. = 克服这个障碍需要付出巨大的努力。
