encrusting: /ɪnˈkrʌstɪŋ/ | adj. | 附着覆盖的；结壳的 |  Describes something that is covered or coated with a hard substance, often a mineral deposit or a layer of another material. It suggests a process of gradual accumulation. |


例句：

The coral reef was encrusting the old shipwreck.  = 珊瑚礁覆盖着那艘古老的沉船。

The ancient pottery was encrusting with a thick layer of grime. = 古代陶器上覆盖着一层厚厚的污垢。


近义词：coated, covered, crusted, encrusted


反义词：bare, uncovered, clean


词性：形容词
