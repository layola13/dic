desmoid:/ˈdɛzmɔɪd/| n. |纤维瘤；肌纤维瘤|A desmoid tumor is a benign (non-cancerous) tumor of the connective tissue, specifically fibroblasts.  They are slow-growing and rarely metastasize (spread to other parts of the body), but can cause significant local problems due to their infiltrative growth pattern.  They are most often found in the abdominal wall, but can occur in other areas.  Treatment options vary depending on size, location, and symptoms. | Example:  The patient presented with a desmoid tumor in her abdominal wall requiring surgical resection.|近义词：fibromatosis|反义词：无
