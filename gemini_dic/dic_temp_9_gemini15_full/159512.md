politbureaus:/ˈpɑːlɪtˌbjʊroʊz/| n. |政治局（复数）|The politbureaus are the supreme political bodies of communist or socialist states.  They make key decisions on behalf of the party.  |The politbureaus of many communist countries held immense power.|近义词：ruling bodies, central committees|反义词：opposition, electorate
