词源:
The word "multiphase" is a combination of "multi-" (meaning many or multiple) and "phase" (referring to a distinct stage or state of matter).  The term originates from physics and chemistry, where it describes systems containing multiple phases of matter (solid, liquid, gas, plasma) or multiple stages in a process.
