poi:/pɔɪ/| n. |兴趣点，点；兴趣点数据；(地图上的)兴趣点|A point of interest, often used in geographic information systems (GIS) and mapping applications to represent locations of interest.  It can refer to landmarks, businesses, or other significant locations.|例句：The map shows several POIs in the city center. 这张地图显示了市中心的一些兴趣点。|例句： We collected POI data for our market research. 我们为市场调研收集了兴趣点数据。|近义词：landmark, location, place, site|反义词：N/A
