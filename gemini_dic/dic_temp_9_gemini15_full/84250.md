half-smothered:/ˈhɑːf ˈsmʌðərd/ | adj. | 半窒息的，几乎窒息的 | Partially suffocated; suppressed but not completely eliminated.  The term often implies a feeling of being overwhelmed or constrained. | 例句：The fire was almost extinguished, but the room was still half-smothered in smoke.  房间里几乎没有火了，但仍然弥漫着浓烟，令人几乎窒息。

The cake, half-smothered in chocolate sauce, was decadent and delicious.  这块蛋糕淋了一半的巧克力酱，既颓废又美味。| 近义词：partially suffocated, suppressed, stifled, muffled | 反义词：fully ventilated, openly expressed, unconstrained

词源:
The word is a compound of "half" and "smothered".  "Half" indicates incompleteness, while "smothered" comes from the Old English *smorian, meaning "to suppress, suffocate, or stifle."  The word thus describes a state of being partially suppressed or suffocated.
