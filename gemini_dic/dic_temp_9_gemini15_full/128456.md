newsfeed:/ˈnuːzfiːd/| n. |新闻推送，信息流|A newsfeed is a constantly updated list of items—typically news articles, blog posts, or social media updates—that are displayed on a website or app.|例句：I check my newsfeed several times a day to stay updated on current events. 我每天查看几次我的新闻推送以了解最新的事件。|例句：The social media platform's newsfeed algorithm prioritizes engagement over accuracy.  该社交媒体平台的新闻推送算法优先考虑互动性而非准确性。|近义词：feed, stream, updates|反义词：N/A
