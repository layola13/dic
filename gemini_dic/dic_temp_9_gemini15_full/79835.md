glyoxime:/ˈɡlaɪɒksaɪm/| n.|乙二醛肟|Glyoxime is a chemical compound with the formula (HON=CH)2. It's an organic compound containing two oxime groups (-C(=NOH)-) connected to a central carbon atom.  It exists in several isomeric forms.  It's used as a reagent in various chemical reactions, most notably in the detection and determination of nickel.  |Example: The formation of nickel glyoximate is a classic example of a precipitation reaction used in analytical chemistry. 镍乙二醛肟的形成是分析化学中使用的沉淀反应的一个经典例子。|近义词：dioxime (a broader term)|反义词：None specifically.
