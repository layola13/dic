incentivize:/ɪnˈsɛntaɪz/ | vt. |激励；鼓励；刺激 | To provide someone with an incentive; to motivate someone to do something.  | 例句：The company incentivized employees to work harder by offering bonuses. 公司通过提供奖金来激励员工更加努力工作。| 例句：We need to incentivize our sales team to meet their targets. 我们需要激励我们的销售团队达到他们的目标。| 近义词：motivate, encourage, stimulate, inspire | 反义词：discourage, demotivate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company incentivizes employees with performance-based bonuses. = 公司以绩效奖金激励员工。
They incentivize early bird registration with a discounted rate. = 他们以折扣价鼓励尽早注册。


一般过去时:
The government incentivized renewable energy development with tax credits. = 政府通过税收优惠激励可再生能源发展。
They incentivized participation by offering free gifts. = 他们通过提供免费礼物来激励参与。


现在完成时:
The new policy has incentivized significant investment in the sector. = 新政策已激励了对该行业的重大投资。
Increased wages have incentivized many to seek new employment. = 工资上涨已激励许多人寻求新的工作。


过去完成时:
The prior tax breaks had incentivized a surge in construction. = 先前的税收优惠已激励了建筑业的激增。
The previous advertising campaign had incentivized a significant rise in sales. = 此前的广告宣传活动已促使销售额大幅增长。


将来时:
The new program will incentivize more people to adopt sustainable practices. = 新计划将激励更多人采用可持续措施。
The proposed legislation will incentivize investment in infrastructure projects. = 提出的立法将激励对基础设施项目的投资。


虚拟语气:
If they incentivized participation more effectively, more people would join. = 如果他们更有效地激励参与，更多人就会加入。
I wish they would incentivize employees with better benefits. = 我希望他们能用更好的福利来激励员工。


被动语态:
Employees were incentivized to improve their skills through training programs. = 员工通过培训项目受到激励以提高技能。
Investment in renewable energy was incentivized by government subsidies. = 可再生能源投资受到政府补贴的激励。


疑问句:

否定句:

The company failed to incentivize its employees effectively. = 公司未能有效地激励员工。

词源:
incentivize is a relatively modern word, coined in the 20th century.  It's derived from the noun "incentive," which comes from the Latin word "incentivum," meaning "something that incites or encourages."  The "-ize" suffix is a common verb-forming suffix in English, meaning "to make or become."
