caroled:/ˈkærəld/| v.|吟唱，颂歌，欢唱|To sing carols; to sing joyfully or in a celebratory manner.|例句：The children caroled joyfully through the snowy streets. 孩子们在雪地里快乐地吟唱。|例句：The choir caroled a beautiful Christmas hymn. 唱诗班唱了一首优美的圣诞赞美诗。|近义词：sang, chanted, warbled|反义词：muttered, whispered
