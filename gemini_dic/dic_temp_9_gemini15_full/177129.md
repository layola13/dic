reapproaching:/ˌriːəˈproʊtʃɪŋ/| v.|重新接近，再次靠近|The act of approaching something or someone again.  It implies a previous approach. |例句：After the initial rejection, he decided to reapproach the investor with a revised proposal.  重新被拒绝后，他决定带着修改后的提案再次接近投资者。

例句：The ship, having lost its way, was reapproaching the coast.  船迷路后，正再次靠近海岸。


近义词：recontacting, approaching again, returning to


反义词：moving away from, departing from, distancing from


词性：动词
