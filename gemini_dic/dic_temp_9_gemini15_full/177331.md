reattachment's: /ˌriːəˈtætʃmənts/ | n. | 再连接；重新连接；再附着 | The act of attaching something again.  It refers to the process of reconnecting something that has been detached or separated.  The word is a possessive form, indicating ownership or relationship. |例句：The surgeon performed a reattachment of the severed finger. 外科医生进行了断指再植手术。

The reattachment's success depended on the speed of treatment. 再植手术的成功取决于治疗的速度。
