modillion:/məˈdɪljən/| n. | 卷叶装饰 | A small, ornamental bracket, typically scrolled or curved, used under a cornice or in other architectural details.  Modillions are often found in classical and neoclassical architecture, adding a decorative element to the structure. They are usually arranged in a repeating pattern. | The building's cornice was adorned with intricately carved modillions.  这座建筑的檐口装饰着精美的卷叶装饰。|  bracket, console | plain, undecorated
