moist:/mɔɪst/| adj.|潮湿的，湿润的|Slightly wet; damp.|The air was moist and warm.|近义词：damp, humid, wet|反义词：dry, arid


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The air is moist in the rainforest. = 雨林中的空气潮湿。
The cake is moist and delicious. = 蛋糕湿润可口。

一般过去时:
The ground was moist after the rain. = 雨后地面潮湿。
Her eyes were moist with tears.  = 她的眼睛湿润了，含着泪水。

现在完成时:
The soil has been moist all week. = 土壤整个星期都湿润。
The towel has become moist from the spilled water. = 毛巾因为洒了水而变得潮湿。

过去完成时:
The bread had become moist in the humid weather. = 面包在潮湿的天气里已经变得潮湿了。
The cake had been moist before it dried out. = 蛋糕在变干之前是湿润的。

将来时:
The ground will be moist tomorrow after the forecast rain. = 预报有雨，明天地面将会湿润。
The air will likely be moist near the lake. = 湖边空气很可能潮湿。

虚拟语气:
If the soil were moist, the plants would grow better. = 如果土壤湿润，植物就会长得更好。
I wish the air were less moist today. = 我希望今天空气没那么潮湿。

被动语态:
The land was moistened by the morning dew. = 土地被清晨的露水湿润了。
The cake is moistened with milk. = 蛋糕用牛奶弄湿润。

疑问句:
Is the soil moist enough to plant seeds? = 土壤足够湿润可以播种了吗？
Is the air moist today? = 今天空气潮湿吗？

否定句:
The soil is not moist enough for planting. = 土壤不够湿润，无法种植。
The cake is not moist enough; it's dry. = 蛋糕不够湿润，太干了。
