darkliest:/ˈdɑːrklɪst/ | adj. | 最黑暗的，最阴暗的 |  The superlative form of "dark," indicating the greatest degree of darkness or obscurity. It can refer to physical darkness, metaphorical darkness (e.g., a dark time in history), or emotional darkness (e.g., darkest hour). |例句：The darkest night of the year fell upon the city.  一年中最黑暗的夜晚降临在这个城市。

The darkest secret was finally revealed. 最黑暗的秘密最终被揭露了。|近义词：blackest, deepest, most obscure |反义词：brightest, lightest, most luminous |
