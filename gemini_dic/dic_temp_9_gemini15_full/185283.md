retraxit:/rɪˈtræksɪt/ | v. |撤回；收回；取消 | To withdraw or retract a statement, claim, or action.  It often refers to a formal withdrawal of a legal pleading or claim. | Example: The plaintiff retraxit his lawsuit after reaching a settlement with the defendant. 原告在与被告达成和解后撤回了诉讼。|近义词：withdraw, revoke, rescind |反义词：affirm, reaffirm, confirm |动词
