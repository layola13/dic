preexplain:/ˌpriːɪkˈspleɪn/| vt.|预先解释，提前说明|To explain something before it happens or is done, in order to prepare someone for it.|例句：The teacher preexplained the difficult concepts before the students started the assignment. 老师在学生开始作业之前预先解释了那些难懂的概念。|近义词：forewarn, preempt, clarify|反义词：misinterpret, obscure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preexplains the upcoming test to his students. 他向学生预先解释即将到来的考试。
She preexplains the project's objectives to the team. 她向团队预先解释了项目的目标。

一般过去时:
The professor preexplained the experiment's procedure. 教授预先解释了实验的步骤。
They preexplained the risks involved before the surgery. 他们在手术前预先解释了所涉及的风险。

现在完成时:
He has preexplained the main points of the presentation. 他已经预先解释了演示文稿的要点。
She has preexplained the potential difficulties to avoid confusion. 她已经预先解释了潜在的困难以避免混淆。

过去完成时:
He had preexplained the rules before the game began. 游戏开始前，他已经预先解释了规则。
She had preexplained the steps so everyone understood. 她已经预先解释了步骤，以便每个人都理解。

将来时:
He will preexplain the lesson tomorrow. 他明天将预先解释这节课。
She will preexplain the itinerary to the tourists. 她将向游客预先解释行程安排。

虚拟语气:
If I had preexplained the instructions, they would have understood better. 如果我预先解释了说明，他们会理解得更好。
I wish I had preexplained the consequences more clearly. 我希望我更清楚地预先解释了后果。

被动语态:
The instructions were preexplained to the participants. 说明已预先向参与者解释。
The risks involved were preexplained to the clients before signing the contract. 在签署合同之前，已向客户预先解释了所涉及的风险。

疑问句:
Did he preexplain the methodology before the experiment? 他在实验前是否预先解释了方法论？
Has she preexplained the policy changes to the staff? 她是否已向员工预先解释了政策变更？


否定句:
He did not preexplain the potential issues. 他没有预先解释潜在的问题。
She did not preexplain the complicated terms. 她没有预先解释那些复杂的术语。
