preclassification: /ˌpriːˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 预分类，初步分类 | The process of classifying something before a more thorough or final classification.  It often involves initial sorting or grouping based on readily observable characteristics. This step helps to streamline the subsequent, more detailed classification process. | Example: Preclassification of the documents allowed for a more efficient full indexing process later. 预分类文件使得之后的完整索引过程更高效。  The preclassification of the samples saved significant time in the laboratory. 样品的预分类节省了实验室大量时间。 | 近义词： preliminary classification, initial sorting, preliminary categorization | 反义词： final classification, definitive categorization | 名词
