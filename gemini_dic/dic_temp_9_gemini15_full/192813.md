seannachie:/ˈʃænəkiː/| n. |说书人，吟游诗人 | A seanachie is a traditional storyteller, often found in Gaelic-speaking cultures, who recites tales, poems, and genealogies, preserving and transmitting oral history and cultural traditions.  They are often skilled in memorization and performance, captivating their audiences with their narratives. |  The seanachie captivated the villagers with his tales of ancient battles and mythical creatures.  说书人用他关于古代战争和神话生物的故事迷住了村民们。| storyteller, bard, narrator |  (无明确反义词，可考虑：silent, uncommunicative) | 名词
