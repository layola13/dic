raker:/ˈreɪkər/| n. |耙子；（船舶上的）挠板；（发动机上的）排气阀|A raker is a tool with teeth used for gathering and moving loose materials, such as leaves or hay.  It can also refer to a part of a ship or engine.|例句：He used a raker to gather the fallen leaves. 他用耙子收集落叶。

The ship's rakers helped remove debris from the hull. 船上的挠板帮助清除船体上的碎片。|近义词：rake, comb, scraper|反义词：None specifically, but actions that oppose raking, like scattering.
