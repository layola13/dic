multi-party:/ˌmʌltiˈpɑːrti/| adj. |多党的；多方参与的 |Having or involving more than two parties or sides.  The term often refers to political systems or negotiations. |例句：The country adopted a multi-party system after the revolution. = 革命后，该国采用了多党制度。

例句：The peace talks involved a multi-party negotiation between the warring factions. = 和谈涉及交战各派之间的多方谈判。

近义词：pluralistic, multilateral, polycentric

反义词：bipartite, bilateral, unicameral


词源:

* **multi-**:  From Latin *multus*, meaning "much," "many," or "several."
* **party**: From Old French *partie*, ultimately from Latin *partire*, meaning "to divide" or "to share."  In the context of politics, it refers to a group of people organized under a common political program.
