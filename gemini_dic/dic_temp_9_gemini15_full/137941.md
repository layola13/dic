nonwar: /ˈnɒnˌwɔːr/ | adj. |非战争的；和平的 |  Describing a situation or period that is not characterized by war or armed conflict; peaceful or non-violent.  |Example:  The country enjoyed a period of nonwar after the signing of the peace treaty.  这个国家在签署和平条约后享受了一段和平时期。|近义词：peaceful, tranquil, pacific |反义词：warlike, belligerent
