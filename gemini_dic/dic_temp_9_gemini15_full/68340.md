fall-over-backwards: /ˌfɔːlˈoʊvərˈbækwərdz/ | phrasal verb | 竭尽全力；拼命地；极力 | To do something with excessive enthusiasm or effort, often to an unnecessary degree.  It implies going to extreme lengths to please or accommodate someone. | 例句：He fell over backwards to impress his new boss. 他为了给他的新老板留下好印象而竭尽全力。


例句：She fell over backwards to make sure her guests were comfortable. 她拼命地确保她的客人感到舒适。


近义词：go out of one's way, bend over backwards,  make every effort


反义词：do the minimum, make no effort, be indifferent


词性: phrasal verb


USAGE->

They fall over backwards to please their customers. = 他们竭尽全力取悦他们的顾客。
He falls over backwards to help his friends. = 他拼命地帮助他的朋友们。
