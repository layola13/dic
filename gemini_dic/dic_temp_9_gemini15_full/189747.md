saltires:/ˈsɔːltərz/| n.|圣安德鲁十字架；斜十字形图案|A saltire is a heraldic cross, formed by placing two diagonal lines across each other, typically forming an X shape.  It's a common symbol in heraldry and is also known as a St. Andrew's Cross.  The name derives from the diagonal lines resembling a leaping or dancing figure.  The saltire is a prominent symbol in the flag of Scotland.|The Scottish flag features a white saltire on a blue background.|近义词：St. Andrew's Cross, diagonal cross|反义词：无
