USAGE->

一般现在时:
The creek flows gently to the sea. = 小溪缓缓流向大海。
Many creeks run through the valley. = 许多小溪流经山谷。


一般过去时:
The creek dried up during the drought. = 干旱期间，小溪干涸了。
We followed the creek upstream yesterday. = 昨天我们沿着小溪向上游走去。


现在完成时:
The creek has overflowed its banks several times this year. = 今年这条小溪已经多次泛滥成灾。
We have never seen such a clear creek before. = 我们以前从未见过这么清澈的小溪。


过去完成时:
The creek had already frozen over before the snowstorm. = 在暴风雪来临之前，小溪就已经结冰了。
They had followed the creek for miles before finding a bridge. = 他们沿着小溪走了几英里才找到一座桥。


将来时:
The creek will probably be flooded after the heavy rain. = 大雨过后，小溪可能会被淹没。
We will explore the creek next weekend. = 我们下周末将探索这条小溪。


虚拟语气:
If the creek were deeper, we could swim in it. = 如果小溪更深一些，我们就能在里面游泳。
I wish the creek were cleaner. = 我希望小溪更干净。


被动语态:
The creek is often polluted by farm runoff. = 小溪经常受到农田径流的污染。
The creek was cleared of debris after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，小溪里的碎石被清理干净了。


疑问句:
Does the creek run all year round? = 这条小溪一年四季都流水吗？
Where does the creek originate? = 这条小溪发源于何处？


否定句:
The creek doesn't run very fast. = 这条小溪流速不快。
There wasn't much water in the creek last summer. = 去年夏天，小溪里的水不多。
