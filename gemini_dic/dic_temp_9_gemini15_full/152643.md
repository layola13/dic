peacocklike:/ˈpiːkɒklʌɪk/| adj.|孔雀似的，华丽的|Resembling a peacock, especially in its showy or ostentatious appearance.  It suggests something beautiful but potentially vain or self-conscious.|例句：Her peacocklike dress attracted everyone's attention. 她那孔雀似的衣服吸引了所有人的注意。|例句：He gave a peacocklike performance, full of dramatic gestures. 他表演得像孔雀一样，充满了戏剧性的姿态。|近义词：showy, ostentatious, flamboyant, magnificent|反义词：plain, modest, understated
