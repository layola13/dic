/priːˈlaɪsnsɪz/ | n. | 预先许可；预先授权 |  Prelicenses are permits or approvals granted in advance of an activity.  They ensure compliance and streamline processes. For example, a prelicense for construction might be required before starting work on a building project. | Example: The company obtained prelicenses for all necessary environmental impact assessments before beginning construction.  = 公司在开始建设之前获得了所有必要的环境影响评估的预先许可。


The company secured prelicenses for its new software, ensuring a smooth launch. = 公司为其新软件获得了预先许可，确保了顺利发布。 | 近义词：prior authorization, advance approval, preliminary permission | 反义词：post-license, retroactive approval
