This is usually measured by a high cephalic index (head length to width ratio).  The condition can be congenital (present at birth) or acquired. | The child was diagnosed with dolichocephalism after a series of head measurements.  这个孩子在一系列头部测量后被诊断为长头畸形。| brachycephalism |  (no direct antonym, but could be considered the opposite of brachycephalism)
