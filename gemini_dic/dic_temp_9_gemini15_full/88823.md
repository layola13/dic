hogged:/hɒɡd/| vt.|霸占；独占；贪婪地吃； |to use more than your fair share of something, often selfishly. |例句：He hogged the conversation all evening. 他整个晚上都在霸占着谈话。|例句：She hogged all the food. 她把所有的食物都吃光了。|近义词：monopolize, dominate, grab, seize|反义词：share, contribute, relinquish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hogs the remote control. = 他霸占着遥控器。
They hog all the resources. = 他们独占了所有资源。

一般过去时:
She hogged the blanket last night. = 她昨晚霸占了毯子。
He hogged the spotlight during the presentation. = 他在演示过程中抢尽了风头。

现在完成时:
He has hogged the conversation for an hour. = 他已经霸占谈话一个小时了。
They have hogged the best seats. = 他们霸占了最好的座位。

过去完成时:
She had hogged the bathroom for so long that I was late for work. = 她霸占浴室太久以至于我上班迟到了。
They had hogged all the cookies before I got there. = 我到那里之前，他们已经把所有的饼干都吃光了。

将来时:
He will hog the computer all day tomorrow. = 他明天一整天都会霸占电脑。
They will hog the stage during the performance. = 他们将在表演中占据舞台中心。

虚拟语气:
If he hogged all the food, we would have nothing to eat. = 如果他把所有的食物都吃光了，我们就什么都吃不到了。
I wish he hadn't hogged the microphone. = 我真希望他不要霸占麦克风。

被动语态:
The resources were hogged by the big corporations. = 资源被大公司垄断了。
The conversation was hogged by him. = 谈话被他霸占了。

疑问句:
Is he hogging the internet again? = 他又在霸占网络吗？
Are they hogging the limelight? = 他们在抢风头吗？

否定句:
He didn't hog the ball during the game. = 他在比赛中没有霸占球。
They didn't hog the credit for the project. = 他们没有为这个项目独占功劳。
