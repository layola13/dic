non-lawyer:/ˈnɑːnˈlɔɪər/ | adj. n. | 非律师的；非律师 |  Describes a person or thing that is not a lawyer.  It can be used as an adjective to modify a noun or as a noun referring to a person who is not a lawyer. | Example:  The non-lawyer witness gave compelling testimony.  非律师证人提供了令人信服的证词。 The document was reviewed by both a lawyer and a non-lawyer for clarity. 这份文件由律师和非律师都审核过，以确保清晰易懂。 | 近义词：layperson, layperson, nonprofessional | 反义词：lawyer
