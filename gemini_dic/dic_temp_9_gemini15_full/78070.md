geodephagous:/ˌdʒioʊˈdæfəɡəs/| adj.| 食地的，食土的 | Relating to or characteristic of geodephagous insects, which are predatory beetles that typically live in the ground and feed on other insects.  | Example:  Many geodephagous beetles are important predators in agricultural ecosystems.  许多食地甲虫是农业生态系统中重要的捕食者。|近义词：terrestrial (in the context of habitat), predacious|反义词：herbivorous, phytophagous
