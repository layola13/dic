rebathing:/ˌriːˈbeɪðɪŋ/| v.|再洗澡；重新沐浴|The act of bathing again.  It implies a second bath, often after a previous one, perhaps for cleanliness or relaxation.|例句：After a long hike, rebathing was a welcome relief.  长途跋涉后，再次沐浴是一种令人欣慰的解脱。

例句：The child, covered in mud, needed a rebathing immediately.  孩子浑身是泥，需要立刻再洗个澡。


近义词：bathing again, washing again, showering again |反义词：None specifically, but the antonym of the action is foregoing a bath.


词性：动词
