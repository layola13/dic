machairodus:/ˌmækəɪˈroʊdəs/| n. |剑齿虎属|A genus of extinct saber-toothed cats, characterized by their exceptionally long upper canines.  Machairodus lived during the Miocene and Pliocene epochs and were widespread across North America, Eurasia, and Africa.
