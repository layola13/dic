myotome:/ˈmaɪətoʊm/| n. |肌节，肌纤维束|A group of muscles innervated by a single spinal nerve root.  Myotomes are important in assessing neurological function because damage to a specific spinal nerve root will result in weakness or paralysis of the muscles in the corresponding myotome.  |Example:  The doctor tested the patient's myotomes to determine the extent of the spinal cord injury.  医生检查了病人的肌节以确定脊髓损伤的程度。|近义词: muscle group,  innervated muscle segment |反义词: N/A
