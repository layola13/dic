mites: /maɪts/ | n. | 螨虫；极少量 | Tiny arachnids, often parasitic.  They are extremely small and can be found in various environments, including dust, food, and on animals.  | Example:  Dust mites are a common cause of allergies.  尘螨是过敏的常见原因。

The house was infested with mites. 这房子里充满了螨虫。|近义词：arachnids, insects (loosely), pests |反义词：N/A |名词

词源:
The word "mite" comes from the Old English word "mīte," which is of uncertain origin, possibly related to other Germanic words for small creatures or insects.  It's ultimately linked to the Proto-Germanic *mīta, the ancestor of words in other Germanic languages with similar meanings.
