insolency:/ˈɪnsələnsɪ/| n. |傲慢；无礼；放肆|  The quality or state of being insolent; rude or disrespectful behavior.  It implies a defiant or contemptuous disregard for others. |例句：Her insolency towards her elders was shocking.  她的长辈面前的傲慢无礼令人震惊。

例句：The student's insolency resulted in suspension from school. 学生的放肆行为导致停学。


近义词：impudence, impertinence, audacity, disrespect, rudeness

反义词：respect, politeness, deference, humility


词源：源自于古法语 *insolence，进一步源自于拉丁语 *insolēns，意思是“不寻常的，不寻常的；傲慢的”。  *insolēns 由 *in- ("not") + *solēns ("usual, accustomed") 构成。
