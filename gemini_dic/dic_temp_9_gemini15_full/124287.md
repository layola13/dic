mother's:/ˈmʌðərz/| n.|母亲的；妈妈的|This word is a possessive form of the noun "mother," indicating ownership or relationship. It's used to show that something belongs to or is associated with a mother.|例句：This is my mother's car. 这是我妈妈的车。

例句：That's mother's day gift. 那是母亲节礼物。


近义词：maternal, mum's (informal)


反义词：无


词性: 名词所有格
