predominated:/prɪˈdɒmɪneɪtɪd/| v.| 占主导地位；支配；盛行 |to be the most noticeable or important | The red color predominated in the painting.  这幅画中红色占主导地位。  |近义词：dominate, prevail, outweigh |反义词：subordinate, be secondary to


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The blue color predominates in this picture. = 这张图片中蓝色占主导地位。
His opinion predominates in the decision-making process. = 他的意见在决策过程中占主导地位。


一般过去时:
Traditional music predominated in the concert. = 音乐会中传统音乐占主导地位。
Democratic ideas predominated in the political landscape. = 民主思想在政治格局中占主导地位。


现在完成时:
Modern technology has predominated in recent years. = 近年来现代科技占主导地位。
His influence has predominated throughout the company. = 他的影响力在整个公司中占主导地位。


过去完成时:
Before the war, agricultural practices had predominated in the region. = 战争前，该地区农业耕作方式占主导地位。
Classical music had predominated in the cultural scene before the rock and roll era. = 在摇滚乐时代之前，古典音乐在文化领域占主导地位。


将来时:
Sustainable energy sources will predominate in the future. = 未来可持续能源将占主导地位。
Online shopping is expected to predominate in the coming decade. = 预计未来十年在线购物将占主导地位。


虚拟语气:
If the blue color predominated, the painting would be more calming. = 如果蓝色占主导地位，这幅画就会更令人平静。
If his opinion had predominated, the decision would have been different. = 如果他的意见占主导地位，决定就会不同。


被动语态:
The design was predominated by simple lines. = 该设计以简洁的线条为主。
The meeting was predominated by the CEO's views. = 会议由首席执行官的观点主导。


疑问句:
Does any particular color predominate in this artwork? = 这幅作品中是否有任何特定的颜色占主导地位？
Which style of architecture predominated in the city during that period? = 在那个时期，该城市哪种建筑风格占主导地位？


否定句:
No single style predominated in the art exhibition. = 艺术展上没有一种单一风格占主导地位。
The small businesses did not predominate in the market. = 小型企业并没有在市场上占据主导地位。


PHRASE->
predominate in = 在...中占主导地位
predominate over = 支配；压倒
predominantly = 主要地；显著地
predominating factor = 主要因素
predominating influence = 主要影响
