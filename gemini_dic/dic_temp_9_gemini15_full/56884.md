dosing:/ˈdoʊzɪŋ/ | v. | 给…服药；昏昏欲睡；|The act of administering a dose of medicine or other substance.  It can also refer to the state of being drowsy or sleepy.|例句：The nurse is dosing the patient with medication. 护士正在给病人服药。  He was dosing off during the lecture. 他在讲座期间昏昏欲睡。|近义词：medicating, administering, sleeping, dozing off|反义词：being awake, alert|动词
