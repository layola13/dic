luffa:/ˈlʌfə/| n. |丝瓜, 丝瓜络 |A luffa is a tropical and subtropical vine plant that produces a fruit with a fibrous interior that is used as a sponge or scrubber.  It is also known as a loofah. |例句：I use a luffa sponge to exfoliate my skin. 我用丝瓜络来去角质。

例句：The gardener harvested a bountiful crop of luffas. 园丁收获了一大批丝瓜。

近义词：loofah, vegetable sponge

反义词：无
