finicky:/ˈfɪnɪki/| adj.| 挑剔的；难以取悦的；精密细致的 | Difficult to please; fastidious; requiring meticulous care or attention.  |例句：He's a finicky eater and won't touch anything green. 他是个挑剔的食客，不吃任何绿色蔬菜。 |例句：The recipe is quite finicky, requiring precise measurements. 这个食谱很精密，需要精确的测量。|近义词：fussy, picky, fastidious, particular |反义词：undemanding, easygoing, tolerant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chef is finicky about his ingredients. = 这个厨师对他的食材很挑剔。
She is always finicky when choosing clothes. = 她选衣服总是很挑剔。

一般过去时:
He was finicky about the presentation of his work. = 他对作品的展示很挑剔。
The customer was finicky and returned the dress three times. = 这位顾客很挑剔，把衣服退了三次。

现在完成时:
She has been finicky about her diet since her illness. = 她自从生病以来就对饮食很挑剔。
They have become increasingly finicky with their investments. = 他们对投资越来越挑剔了。

过去完成时:
He had been finicky about his appearance all his life. = 他一生都很注意自己的外表。
The mechanic had been finicky with the car's engine before the race. = 赛车之前，技师对汽车的引擎进行了细致的检查。

将来时:
I will be finicky about the choice of venue for the wedding. = 我将会对婚礼场地的选择很挑剔。
They will likely be finicky about the details of the contract. = 他们很可能会对合同的细节吹毛求疵。

虚拟语气:
If he were less finicky, he'd have more friends. = 如果他不那么挑剔，他就会有更多朋友。
I wish I weren't so finicky about my food. = 我真希望我不那么挑剔食物。

被动语态:
The food was prepared with finicky precision. = 这食物是经过精细准备的。
The details of the contract are being reviewed with finicky attention. = 合同的细节正在被仔细审查。

疑问句:

Are you finicky about the way your clothes are ironed? = 你对衣服熨烫方式很挑剔吗？

否定句:
He isn't finicky at all; he'll eat anything. = 他一点也不挑剔；什么都吃。
She wasn't finicky about the decorations for the party. = 她对聚会的装饰并不挑剔。
