reim:/raɪm/| n.|韵，押韵|A rhyme is a repetition of similar sounds in two or more words, especially at the end of lines in poems or songs.|例句：The poem has a perfect AABB rhyme scheme. 这首诗有完美的AABB韵律格律。|近义词：rhythm, verse|反义词:prose


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The poem rhymes perfectly. = 这首诗押韵完美。
Does this word rhyme with that one? = 这个词和那个词押韵吗？


一般过去时:
The song rhymed beautifully. = 这首歌押韵优美。
The poet rhymed the words skillfully. = 诗人巧妙地押韵。


现在完成时:
They have rhymed the verses successfully. = 他们成功地使诗句押韵。
She has rhymed  the final stanza perfectly. = 她把最后一段诗节押韵完美。


过去完成时:
He had rhymed the poem before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经完成了这首诗的押韵。
They had rhymed all the lines before they sang the song. = 他们在唱歌之前已经把所有的诗句都押韵了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I could rhyme as well as he does. = 我希望我的押韵能像他一样好。


被动语态:
The poem was rhymed with great skill. = 这首诗押韵技巧很高超。

疑问句:

否定句:
The words do not rhyme. = 这些词不押韵。
The poem didn't rhyme well. = 这首诗押韵不好。
