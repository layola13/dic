musa:/ˈmuːzə/| n. |缪斯；灵感；香蕉属植物|The word "musa" refers to the genus of flowering plants that includes bananas and plantains.  It's a Latin word that was adopted into English. The name is derived from the Greek Muses, the goddesses of arts and sciences, likely because of the abundance and usefulness of banana plants. | Example:  The Musa genus encompasses a wide variety of banana cultivars.|近义词:banana (as a common name), plantain|反义词:None directly applicable.
