cubic:/ˈkjuːbɪk/| adj.|立方的，三次方的|Of or relating to a cube; having three dimensions.  It can also refer to something measured in cubic units (like cubic meters or cubic feet).|例句：The room has a cubic capacity of 100 meters.  这间房间的容积是100立方米。|例句：The equation is a cubic equation. 这个方程是三次方程。|近义词：cubical|反义词：planar, two-dimensional
