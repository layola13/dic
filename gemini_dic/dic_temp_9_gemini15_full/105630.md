kinetophone:/ˈkaɪnɪtoʊfoʊn/| n. |活动电影机；活动电影放映机|A kinetophone was an early device that combined moving pictures with synchronized sound.  It was an attempt to create a truly integrated audiovisual experience before the development of more sophisticated technologies.|Example:  Edison's kinetophone was a significant step towards the development of motion pictures with sound. 爱迪生的活动电影机是朝着发展有声电影迈出的重要一步。|近义词：early sound film projector,  sound movie machine|反义词：silent film projector
