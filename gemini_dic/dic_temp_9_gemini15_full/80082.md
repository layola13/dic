goatees:/ɡoʊˈtiːz/ | n. |山羊胡子；下巴上的小胡子 | A goatee is a style of facial hair where a man grows hair only on his chin, usually in a small, pointed shape.  It's named after a goat's beard. | 例句：He sports a neatly trimmed goatee. 他留着修剪整齐的山羊胡子。| 例句：His goatee was starting to turn grey. 他的山羊胡子开始变灰了。|近义词：chin beard, small beard |反义词：full beard, mustache
