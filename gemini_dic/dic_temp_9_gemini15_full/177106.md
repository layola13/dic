reappointments:/ˌriːəˈpɔɪntmənts/| n. |重新任命；再次委任 |The act of appointing someone to a position again.  It implies a previous appointment to the same or a similar role. |例句：The board voted for his reappointments to the committee. 董事会投票决定再次任命他为委员会委员。

The company announced the reappointments of its CEO and CFO. 公司宣布再次任命其首席执行官和首席财务官。|近义词：re-elections, reinstatements |反义词：dismissals, removals |名词
