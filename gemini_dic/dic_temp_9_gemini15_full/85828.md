hazing:/ˈheɪzɪŋ/| n. v.|  老生对新生的欺凌；戏弄；使朦胧；|The act of harassing, initiating, or otherwise subjecting someone to abusive or humiliating treatment, especially as part of an initiation rite.  |例句：The fraternity was under investigation for hazing incidents.  该兄弟会正因其欺凌事件而接受调查。|例句：The heavy fog hazed the landscape. 浓雾使景色朦胧。|近义词：bullying, initiation, harassment, persecution|反义词：protection, kindness, support
