haloing:/ˈheɪloʊɪŋ/| v.|晕轮效应，光环效应 |The act or process of creating a halo effect;  surrounding something with a halo or aura of positive qualities.  |例句：The company's haloing of its new product led to inflated expectations. 公司对其新产品的过度宣传导致了过高的期望。|近义词：glorifying, idealizing, romanticizing |反义词：denigrating, criticizing, disparaging |动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The media is haloing the celebrity's latest project. = 媒体正在为这位名人的最新项目营造光环效应。
Haloing often leads to unrealistic expectations. = 光环效应经常导致不切实际的期望。


一般过去时:
The marketing campaign haloed the product's features. = 营销活动为产品的功能营造了光环效应。
They haloed the candidate with promises of prosperity. = 他们用繁荣的承诺为候选人营造了光环。


现在完成时:
The company has haloed its image through charitable donations. = 公司通过慈善捐款提升了自己的形象。
The media has haloed this new technology, creating unrealistic expectations. = 媒体已经为这项新技术营造了光环，造成了不切实际的期望。


过去完成时:
The public had already haloed the athlete before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，公众已经为这位运动员树立了光环。
They had haloed the idea for years, preventing any realistic assessment. = 他们多年来一直为这个想法营造光环，阻止了任何现实的评估。


将来时:
The advertising campaign will halo the product's benefits. = 广告活动将突出产品的优点。
They will halo the new CEO, hoping to boost investor confidence. = 他们将为新任首席执行官营造光环，希望提振投资者信心。


虚拟语气:
If they haloed the project more effectively, they would have received more funding. = 如果他们更有效地为项目营造光环，他们就会获得更多资金。
I wish they hadn't haloed the situation so much; it created unrealistic expectations. = 我真希望他们没有对这种情况过于美化；这造成了不切实际的期望。


被动语态:
The product was haloed by the positive reviews. = 该产品因正面评价而备受好评。
The new policy is being haloed by the government to gain public support. = 政府正在为这项新政策营造光环以争取公众支持。


疑问句:
Is the company deliberately haloing its performance? = 公司是否故意美化其业绩？
Are they haloing the situation to avoid criticism? = 他们是否在美化情况以避免批评？


否定句:
The report did not halo the company's actions. = 报告没有美化公司的行为。
They did not want to halo the problem, preferring a realistic approach. = 他们不想美化问题，更倾向于采取现实主义的方法。
