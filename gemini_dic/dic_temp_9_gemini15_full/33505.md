cicadas:/sɪˈkɑːdəz/| n. |蝉；知了|A cicada is a large insect with transparent wings and a loud, high-pitched buzzing sound. They are often associated with hot summer days.|The cicadas were buzzing loudly in the trees. 蝉在树上大声鸣叫。|locust, grasshopper|—|名词
