hymn:/hɪm/| n. |圣歌，赞美歌 |A hymn is a religious song or poem, typically one of praise to God.  |例句：The congregation sang a hymn of praise.  会众唱了一首赞美诗。

例句：We sang hymns during the church service.我们在教堂礼拜仪式上唱了圣歌。


近义词：anthem, psalm, ode |反义词：None.

词源:  Old English *hymn, from Latin hymnus, from Greek hymnos 'song of praise'.  The Greek word is possibly related to the verb hympnein 'to celebrate in song', which may be of pre-Greek origin.
