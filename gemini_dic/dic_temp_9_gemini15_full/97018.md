infect:/ɪnˈfɛkt/ | vt. | 传染；感染；侵染 | To contaminate or affect (a person, animal, or plant) with a disease or infectious organism.  | 例句：The virus infected many people in the city.  病毒感染了城里许多人。
The bacteria infected the wound. 细菌感染了伤口。|近义词：contaminate, contaminate, pollute | 反义词：cure, heal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The virus infects many people every year. = 病毒每年感染许多人。
He infects others with his negativity. 他用他的消极情绪感染他人。

一般过去时:
The doctor treated the patient who was infected with the flu. = 医生治疗了感染流感的病人。
The wound became infected after the surgery. 手术后伤口感染了。

现在完成时:
Many people have been infected with this new strain of the flu. = 许多人感染了这种新的流感病毒株。
The disease has infected thousands of people.  这种疾病已经感染了数千人。

过去完成时:
The doctor discovered that the patient had been infected before the surgery. = 医生发现病人早在手术前就已经感染了。
They realized that the water supply had been infected. 他们意识到供水已被污染。

将来时:
Scientists predict that the disease will infect more people next year. = 科学家预测这种疾病明年将感染更多的人。
I fear that the plant will be infected by the pest. 我担心植物会被害虫感染。

虚拟语气:
If the wound had not been infected, it would have healed faster. = 如果伤口没有感染，就会愈合得更快。
If I were infected, I would seek medical attention immediately. 如果我被感染，我会立即就医。

被动语态:
Many people were infected by the virus. = 许多人被病毒感染。
The crops were infected by a fungus. 庄稼感染了一种真菌。

疑问句:
Was the food infected? = 食物被感染了吗？
How many people have been infected so far? 到目前为止有多少人被感染？

否定句:
The doctor confirmed that the patient was not infected. = 医生确认病人没有感染。
The test results showed that the sample was not infected. 测试结果表明样本未被感染。
