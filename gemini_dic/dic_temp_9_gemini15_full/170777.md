pseudocrisis:/ˌsuːdəʊˈkraɪsɪs/| n. |假危机，貌似危机|A pseudocrisis is a situation that appears to be a serious crisis, but which is actually less serious than it seems, often due to exaggeration, misinterpretation, or a lack of proper understanding.  It might involve inflated concerns or a misreading of events. | Example: The media frenzy surrounding the actor's minor traffic violation was a classic pseudocrisis.  媒体对这位演员轻微交通违规的狂热报道是一场典型的假危机。|近义词：false alarm, near miss, scare, tempest in a teapot |反义词：genuine crisis, real emergency, true threat |名词
