digitizes:/ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪzɪz/| vt.|数字化；将…转换成数字形式|To convert information or data into a digital format suitable for use by a computer or other digital device.  This typically involves representing analog information (like sound or images) using numerical codes.|The company digitizes old photographs and converts them into digital files.  这家公司将旧照片数字化并转换为数字文件。|The museum is digitizing its vast collection of artifacts. 这个博物馆正在将其大量的文物数字化。|analogize, convert|manualize, analogize
