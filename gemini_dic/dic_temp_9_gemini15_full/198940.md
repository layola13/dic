sifter:/ˈsɪftər/ | n. |筛子，筛选者 | A sifter is a tool used to separate finer particles from coarser ones, typically by shaking or vibrating a container with a mesh bottom.  It can also refer to a person who carefully examines something to remove unwanted elements. |例句：She used a sifter to remove the lumps from the flour. 她用筛子去除面粉里的结块。  The editor acted as a sifter, removing inaccuracies from the manuscript. 编辑充当筛选者，从手稿中删除不准确之处。|近义词：sieve, strainer, filter |反义词：mixer (in the context of separating, not combining)
