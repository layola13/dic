derrick:/ˈdɛrɪk/| n.|起重机；井架；[美俚]瘦高的人|A derrick is a tall structure, typically consisting of a mast and supporting cables or beams, used for lifting heavy objects, such as in construction or oil drilling.  It can also refer informally to a very tall and thin person.|例句：The derrick lifted the heavy steel beams into place.  这台起重机将沉重的钢梁吊放到位。

The oil derrick swayed slightly in the wind.  石油井架在风中轻轻摇晃。|近义词：crane, hoist, lift|反义词：N/A

词源:
The word "derrick" is derived from the name of a 17th-century English hangman, Thomas Derrick, who was known for his efficiency in his gruesome profession.  The gallows he used was apparently similar in structure to the lifting device, thus leading to the name being transferred to the machine.
