guiding:/ˈɡaɪdɪŋ/ | v. adj. |指导的；引导的 | guiding means showing someone the way or helping someone to do something.  It can be used as a verb or an adjective. |例句：He was guiding the tourists through the museum. 他正在引导游客参观博物馆。|近义词：directing, leading, instructing |反义词：misleading, confusing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She is guiding the students through the project. = 她正在指导学生完成这个项目。
The teacher is guiding the class discussion. = 老师正在引导课堂讨论。

一般过去时:
He guided me to the nearest train station. = 他指引我到最近的火车站。
The map guided us through the forest. = 地图引导我们穿过森林。

现在完成时:
She has guided numerous students to success. = 她已经指导了许多学生取得成功。
They have been guiding the company's growth for years. = 他们多年来一直指导着公司的发展。

过去完成时:
I had guided him through the process before. = 我以前已经指导过他完成这个过程了。
The captain had guided the ship safely through the storm. = 船长已经安全地引导船只度过了暴风雨。

将来时:
She will guide the team through the challenging task. = 她将指导团队完成具有挑战性的任务。
He will be guiding the workshop next week. = 他下周将指导研讨会。

虚拟语气:
If I had a better map, I would have been able to guide myself. = 如果我有一张更好的地图，我就能自己导航了。
I wish I had guided her better. = 我真希望我指导得更好一些。

被动语态:
The students are being guided by a skilled professor. = 学生们正在接受一位经验丰富的教授的指导。
The visitors were guided through the historical site. = 游客们被引导参观了历史遗迹。

疑问句:
Who is guiding the tour? = 谁在带队？
Are you guiding the new employees? = 你在指导新员工吗？

否定句:
He didn't guide me properly. = 他没有正确地指导我。
They were not guiding the children safely. = 他们没有安全地引导孩子们。
