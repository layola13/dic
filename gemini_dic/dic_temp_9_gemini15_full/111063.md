listened:/ˈlɪs(ə)nd/ | v. | 倾听，聆听 | To pay attention to someone or something that is making a sound.  |例句：I listened carefully to the teacher's explanation. 我仔细听了老师的解释。|例句：She listened to the music with her eyes closed. 她闭着眼睛听着音乐。|近义词：heard, attended to |反义词：ignored


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I listen to music every day. = 我每天都听音乐。
He listens attentively in class. = 他在课堂上认真听讲。


一般过去时:
I listened to the radio last night. = 我昨晚听了收音机。
She listened to her friend's problems. = 她听了朋友的倾诉。


现在完成时:
I have listened to that song many times. = 我已经听过那首歌很多次了。
They have listened to the evidence carefully. = 他们已经仔细听取了证据。


过去完成时:
I had listened to the whole album before he arrived. = 在他到达之前我已经听完了整张专辑。
She had listened to the news report before she called. = 她打电话之前已经听了新闻报道。


将来时:
I will listen to your advice. = 我会听你的建议。
He will listen to the concert tomorrow. = 他明天将去听音乐会。


虚拟语气:
If I had listened to him, I wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果我听了他的话，我就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I had listened more carefully in class. = 我希望我上课时能更认真地听讲。


被动语态:
The music was listened to by everyone. =  音乐被每个人都听了。 (This is less common in active usage)
His advice was listened to by the committee. = 他的建议被委员会采纳了。(This implies they acted on the advice)


疑问句:
Did you listen to the news? = 你听了新闻吗？
Were they listening to the conversation? = 他们当时在听那个谈话吗？


否定句:
I didn't listen to his warnings. = 我没有听他的警告。
She didn't listen to a word he said. = 她一句他说的都没听。


PHRASE->
listen to: 倾听…
listen attentively: 专注地听
listen carefully: 仔细地听
listen in: 偷听
listen up: 注意听
