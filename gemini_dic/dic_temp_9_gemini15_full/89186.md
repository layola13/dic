homeowning: /ˈhoʊmoʊnɪŋ/ | adj. | 房屋拥有；拥有住房的 |  Relating to or characteristic of the ownership of a home.  It often describes the status or experience of owning a house or property. |  例句：Homeowning is a significant financial commitment.  拥有住房是一项重大的经济承诺。

例句：The high cost of homeowning is deterring many young people from buying houses.  高昂的购房成本正在阻止许多年轻人买房。


近义词：house ownership, home possession

反义词：renting, tenancy
