monoenergetic:/ˌmɒnoʊˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪk/|/ˌmɑːnoʊˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪk/| adj. |单能的；单能量的 |Having or exhibiting only one energy.  This term is often used in physics, particularly in nuclear physics and particle physics, to describe a beam of particles or photons that all have the same energy. |Example: The experiment utilized a monoenergetic neutron beam to precisely measure the cross-section.  实验利用单能中子束精确测量了截面。 The researchers generated a monoenergetic electron beam for their scattering experiments. 研究人员为他们的散射实验产生了单能电子束。|近义词：monochromatic (in the context of light)|反义词：polyenergetic
