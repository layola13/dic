oscillating:/ˈɒsɪleɪtɪŋ/ | /ˈɑːsɪleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 振荡的；摇摆的 |  Describes something that is moving or varying back and forth regularly between two points or states.  It implies a continuous, rhythmic motion or change. | 例句：The pendulum was oscillating back and forth.  摆钟来回摆动。

The needle on the meter was oscillating wildly.  仪表上的指针剧烈摆动。


近义词：vibrating, fluctuating, swinging, wavering, fluctuating

反义词：stationary, static, fixed, constant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fan is oscillating gently. = 风扇轻轻地摆动着。
The price of oil is oscillating between $70 and $80. = 油价在70美元到80美元之间波动。


一般过去时:
The needle oscillated erratically before stopping. = 指针不规则地摆动后才停止。
The temperature oscillated between hot and cold throughout the day. = 温度整天都在冷热之间波动。


现在完成时:
The stock market has been oscillating for the past few weeks. = 股市在过去几周一直在波动。
The building has been oscillating slightly since the earthquake. = 自地震以来，建筑物一直在轻微地晃动。


过去完成时:
The machine had been oscillating for hours before it finally broke down. = 机器在最终损坏之前已经摆动了好几个小时。
The pendulum had oscillated countless times before it came to rest. = 摆钟在静止之前摆动过无数次。


将来时:
The satellite will oscillate in its orbit around the earth. = 卫星将在绕地球的轨道上摆动。
The pendulum will continue to oscillate unless stopped. = 除非停止，否则摆钟将继续摆动。


虚拟语气:
If the pendulum were oscillating faster, it would complete more cycles per minute. = 如果摆钟摆动得更快，它每分钟完成的循环次数就会更多。
I wish the fan were oscillating more quietly. = 我希望风扇能摆动得更安静一些。


被动语态:
The image was oscillating due to a faulty camera lens. = 由于相机镜头故障，图像在摆动。
The data was oscillating, making it difficult to analyze. = 数据在波动，难以分析。


疑问句:
Is the needle oscillating? = 指针在摆动吗？
Are the prices oscillating too much? = 价格波动太大了吗？


否定句:
The machine is not oscillating properly. = 机器没有正常摆动。
The signal was not oscillating as expected. = 信号没有按预期摆动。

词源:
From Latin *oscillāre "to swing, sway,"  related to *os "mouth".  The connection is thought to be through the image of a swinging mouth.
