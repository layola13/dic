salad:/ˈsæləd/| n. |沙拉，凉拌菜 |A salad is a dish typically made of a mixture of greens, vegetables, or fruits, often with a dressing.  It is usually served cold or at room temperature. |Example: I had a delicious Caesar salad for lunch. 我午餐吃了一份美味的凯撒沙拉。  |近义词：mixed greens, side dish |反义词:main course, hot dish
