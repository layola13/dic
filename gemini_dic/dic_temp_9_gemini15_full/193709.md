self-confirmation: /ˌsɛlf ˌkɑːnfərˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 自我肯定；自我确认 | The act of confirming or verifying something about oneself;  the process of reinforcing one's beliefs or identity through one's own actions or thoughts.  It involves actively seeking out and interpreting information in a way that supports one's existing self-perception. | 例句：Her successful presentation provided her with a much-needed self-confirmation.  她的成功演讲给了她急需的自我肯定。


His consistent efforts at the gym led to self-confirmation of his fitness goals. 他在健身房持续的努力让他对自己的健身目标有了自我肯定。


近义词：self-validation, self-assurance, self-belief, self-acceptance | 反义词：self-doubt, self-deprecation, lack of confidence |  词性：名词
