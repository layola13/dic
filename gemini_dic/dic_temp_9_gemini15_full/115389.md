manslaughter:/ˈmænˌslɔːtər/| n. | 误杀；非预谋杀人 | The unlawful killing of a human being without malice aforethought.  This means it's a killing that is unlawful but not planned or intended with malice (ill will or desire to harm).  It can be voluntary (in the heat of passion) or involuntary (due to negligence).|  He was charged with manslaughter after a bar fight. 他在酒吧打架后被指控犯有误杀罪。

The driver was convicted of manslaughter after causing a fatal accident due to reckless driving. 司机因鲁莽驾驶导致致命事故而被判犯有误杀罪。


近义词：homicide, killing | 反义词：murder
