replough:/riːˈplaʊ/| vt.|再耕，重新耕作|To plow (land) again; to cultivate (land) a second time.|例句：The farmer decided to replough the field after the heavy rain.  农民决定大雨过后再耕地一次。
例句：They reploughed the field to improve the soil quality. 他们重新耕作田地以改善土壤质量。|近义词：retill, recultivate|反义词：harvest, reap|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They replough the fields every spring. = 他们每年春天都重新耕种田地。
He reploughs the garden before planting new seeds. = 他在种植新种子之前重新翻耕花园。

一般过去时:
The farmer reploughed the field after the drought. = 旱灾过后，农民重新耕种了田地。
She reploughed the garden last week. = 她上周重新翻耕了花园。

现在完成时:
The farmer has reploughed the field twice this year. = 今年农民已经两次重新耕作了这块田地。
They have reploughed the land to prepare for the next crop. = 他们已经重新耕作了土地，为下一季作物做准备。

过去完成时:
They had reploughed the field before the heavy rain came. = 大雨来临之前，他们已经重新耕作了田地。
The farmer had reploughed the land before planting the new seeds. = 农民在播种新的种子之前已经重新耕作了土地。

将来时:
They will replough the field next week. = 他们下周将重新耕作田地。
The farmer will replough the land after the harvest. = 收获后，农民将重新耕作土地。

虚拟语气:
If it rained, they would replough the field. = 如果下雨，他们就会重新耕作田地。
I wish we had reploughed the field earlier. = 我希望我们早点重新耕作田地。

被动语态:
The field was reploughed last month. = 这块田地在上个月重新耕作了。
The land will be reploughed before the next planting season. = 在下一个种植季节之前，土地将被重新耕作。

疑问句:
Will they replough the field this year? = 他们今年会重新耕作这块田地吗？
Have they reploughed the land yet? = 他们已经重新耕作土地了吗？

否定句:
They did not replough the field this year. = 他们今年没有重新耕作这块田地。
The farmer will not replough the land until after the harvest. = 在收获之后，农民才不会重新耕作土地。
