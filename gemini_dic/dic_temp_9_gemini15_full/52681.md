dignification: /ˌdɪɡnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |尊严；提升；赋予尊严 |The act of giving or restoring dignity to someone or something.  It involves elevating someone's status, self-respect, or importance.  |例句：The dignification of labor is crucial for a just society.  劳动的尊严对于一个公正的社会至关重要。

例句：The ceremony was intended to be a dignification of the victims' memories.  这个仪式旨在提升受害者记忆的尊严。


近义词：elevation, exaltation, ennoblement, upgrading

反义词：degradation, humiliation, debasement, denigration


词性：名词
