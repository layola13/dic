forefend:/fɔːrˈfɛnd/| vt.|阻止；防止；保佑；避免|To prevent or avert (something bad or undesirable); to ward off; to protect from harm.|例句：May God forefend us from such a fate.  愿上帝保佑我们免受如此命运。|例句：We must forefend against the spread of the disease. 我们必须防止疾病蔓延。|近义词：prevent, avert, ward off, protect|反义词：allow, permit, encourage


USAGE->

She forefends any potential problems. = 她防止任何潜在的问题。
