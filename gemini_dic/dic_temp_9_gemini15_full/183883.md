resend:/riːˈsɛnd/ | vt. |重新发送，再次发送 |To send something again.  This usually implies that the first attempt to send was unsuccessful or that the recipient did not receive it. |例句：Please resend the email; I didn't receive it. 请重新发送邮件；我没有收到。|例句：I had to resend the package because the address was incorrect. 我不得不重新寄送包裹，因为地址错误。|近义词：retransmit, forward, send again |反义词：delete, discard
