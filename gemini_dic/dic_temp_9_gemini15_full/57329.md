downturned:/ˈdaʊntɜːrnd/| adj. | 向下的；低落的；不景气的 | Turned or directed downward; depressed or pessimistic; experiencing a decline in economic activity. |例句：The corners of her mouth were downturned in a frown.  她的嘴角向下撇着，皱着眉头。

The downturned economy led to many job losses. 不景气的经济导致许多人失业。

近义词：depressed, dejected, pessimistic, declining | 反义词：upturned, cheerful, optimistic, thriving
