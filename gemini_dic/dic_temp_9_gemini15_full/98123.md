insan:/ɪnˈsæn/| adj.|疯狂的；精神错乱的 |insane means having a severely disordered mind; mentally ill.  It implies a loss of contact with reality and often involves delusions or hallucinations.  The term is often used informally and can be considered stigmatizing.  More clinical terms are preferred in professional settings.|例句：He was declared legally insane after the incident. 他在事件发生后被宣告为精神错乱。|例句：Her behaviour became increasingly insane as the day went on.  随着一天的过去，她的行为越来越疯狂。|近义词：mad, crazy, lunatic, deranged, demented, psychotic |反义词：sane, rational, lucid, sensible
