non-finite:/nɒnˈfaɪnaɪt/| adj. |非限定的，非谓语的|Not limited or restricted; specifically (of verbs) not limited to a particular tense, person, or number, and therefore able to function as nouns, adjectives, or adverbs.  Non-finite verbs include infinitives, participles, and gerunds.|例句：The non-finite verb "running" acts as a noun in the sentence "Running is good exercise."  跑步是一项很好的运动（其中“running”是非限定动词，充当名词）。|近义词：unlimited, unrestricted, unbound|反义词：finite, limited, restricted
