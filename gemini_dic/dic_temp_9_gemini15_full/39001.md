confirmers:/kənˈfɜːrmərz/ | n. | 确认者；证实者 | People or things that confirm something.  Those who verify or validate a statement, action, or event. | Example: The lab results served as confirmers of the doctor's diagnosis.  实验室结果证实了医生的诊断。 The witnesses acted as confirmers of the suspect's alibi. 目击者充当了嫌疑人不在场证明的证实者。|近义词：verifiers, validators, witnesses |反义词：deniers, refuters, disprovers |名词
