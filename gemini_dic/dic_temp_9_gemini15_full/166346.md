preremittance:/ˌpriːrɪˈmɪtəns/| n. |预付，预先汇款 |A preremittance is a payment made in advance of a shipment or service.  It's a way to ensure the seller that the buyer has the funds available to complete the transaction.  Preremitting is common in international trade to mitigate risk and ensure timely delivery. |Example: The importer made a preremittance to secure the timely shipment of the goods.  进口商预先汇款以确保货物及时运输。|近义词：advance payment, prepayment, down payment |反义词：postpayment, payment in arrears |名词
