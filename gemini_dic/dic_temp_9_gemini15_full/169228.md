prologuists:/proʊˈlɒɡɪsts/ | n. |序言撰写者，前言作者 | A prologuist is a person who writes prologues.  They are often writers, authors, or editors who craft introductory sections for books, plays, or other works. | Example: The prologuist skillfully set the stage for the novel's dramatic events.  序言撰写者巧妙地为小说的戏剧性事件奠定了基础。|近义词:prefacer, introducer |反义词:N/A
