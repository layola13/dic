drippier:/ˈdrɪpiər/| adj.|更滴落的，更潮湿的，更伤感兮兮的 |More dripping; wetter; more sentimental.  It describes something that is dripping more excessively or something that exhibits more sentimentality. |例句：The paint was drippier than I expected, making a mess on the floor.  油漆比我预期的更滴落，弄脏了地板。

The drippier the cake, the more delicious it seemed to the children. 蛋糕越湿润，孩子们就觉得越美味。


近义词：wetter, soggier, more sentimental |反义词：dryer, drier, less sentimental
