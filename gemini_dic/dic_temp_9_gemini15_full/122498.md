mock-up:/ˈmɒkʌp/| /ˈmɑːkʌp/| n. |样机；模型；模拟；草图 |A mock-up is a full-scale model of a design or device, often used for testing or presentation purposes.  It's a preliminary version, not necessarily functional, but showing the overall appearance and functionality.|例句：The designers created a mock-up of the new car before starting production. 设计师们在开始生产之前制作了一个新汽车的样机。|例句：We built a mock-up of the website to show the client. 我们制作了一个网站模型来展示给客户。|近义词：prototype, model, simulation, dummy |反义词：final product, finished product |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team makes a mock-up of the product every week. = 团队每周制作一个产品样机。
He creates a mock-up for his presentation. = 他为他的演示文稿制作了一个模型。

We have completed the mock-up of the new software interface. = 我们已经完成了新软件界面的模型。
