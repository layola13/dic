psychobabble:/ˈsaɪkoʊˌbæbl/| n. |心理呓语；心理学术语滥用；伪心理学言论 |  Language or talk that sounds impressively psychological but is actually meaningless jargon, often used to impress or obfuscate. It often uses technical-sounding psychological terms in a superficial or inaccurate way. | Example:  His therapy sessions were filled with psychobabble, leaving him more confused than before.  他的治疗课程充满了心理呓语，让他比以前更困惑了。|近义词：jargon, gobbledygook, pseudo-psychology | 反义词：clear language, plain speaking


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, typical verb tense conjugations are not applicable.  The word itself describes a *type* of language, not an action.

PHRASE->

meaningless psychobabble: 无意义的心理呓语

esoteric psychobabble:  深奥难懂的心理呓语
utter psychobabble:  完全是胡扯的心理学说法
