lexigraphic:/ˌlɛksɪˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|词典的；词典编纂的|Relating to lexicography or a lexicon (dictionary).  The arrangement is lexigraphic, following alphabetical order.|例句：The entries in this dictionary are arranged in strict lexigraphic order. 这本词典的词条按严格的词典顺序排列。|近义词：alphabetical, dictionary-based|反义词：random, unsorted
