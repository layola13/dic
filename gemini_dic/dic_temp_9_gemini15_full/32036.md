cherishing: /ˈtʃerɪʃɪŋ/ | v. | 珍爱；珍惜；爱护 |  To hold something dear; to treat with great care and affection. | 例句：She cherishes the memories of her childhood. = 她珍藏着她童年时的回忆。

例句：He cherishes his friendship with his brother. = 他珍视与他兄弟的友谊。


近义词：treasure, value, prize, hold dear, adore

反义词：neglect, despise, disregard, scorn


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She cherishes her family. = 她珍爱她的家人。
They cherish every moment together. = 他们珍惜在一起的每一刻。


一般过去时:
He cherished the antique watch. = 他珍藏着那块古董表。
We cherished the opportunity to travel abroad. = 我们珍惜出国旅行的机会。


现在完成时:
I have cherished this photograph for years. = 我多年来一直珍藏着这张照片。
She has cherished the memory of that day ever since. = 从那以后，她一直珍藏着那一天的回忆。


过去完成时:
They had cherished the hope of a reunion. = 他们一直珍藏着重逢的希望。
He had cherished the belief that he would succeed. = 他一直坚信他会成功。


将来时:
I will always cherish this moment. = 我将永远珍惜这一刻。
We will cherish the memories we made together. = 我们将珍藏我们一起创造的回忆。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have cherished the time more. = 如果我知道的话，我会更珍惜那段时间。
I wish I had cherished those opportunities more. = 我希望我当时更珍惜那些机会。


被动语态:
The moment was cherished by all who were present. = 在场的所有人都珍惜那一刻。
The memories are cherished by the family. = 这段回忆受到家人的珍视。


疑问句:
Do you cherish your friendships? = 你珍惜你的友谊吗？
Does he cherish his family? = 他珍惜他的家人吗？


否定句:
They did not cherish their time together. = 他们没有珍惜在一起的时光。
He doesn't cherish material possessions. = 他不珍惜物质财富。


PHRASE->
cherish a hope/dream = 怀抱希望/梦想
cherish the memory of = 珍藏...的回忆
cherish a secret = 珍藏秘密
cherish one's health = 保重身体
cherish the illusion = 珍藏幻想
