semi-final:/ˈsɛmiˌfaɪnəl/| n. |半决赛|A semi-final is a match or competition that is one stage before the final.  It determines which competitors will go on to the final.|例句：The semi-final match was extremely close and exciting. 半决赛的比赛非常激烈和精彩。|例句：Our team lost in the semi-final, but we are still proud of our performance. 我们的球队在半决赛中输了，但我们仍然为我们的表现感到自豪。|近义词：semifinal, penultimate round|反义词:final

词源:
Semi- comes from Latin "semi-" meaning "half".  Final comes from the Latin "finalis" meaning "final".  Therefore, semi-final literally means "half-final," referring to the stage of a competition that is halfway to the final.
