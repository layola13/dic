pass on:/pæs ˈɒn/| vt.|传递，转交，去世|to give something to someone else; to die|例句：Please pass on this message to John. 请把这个消息转告约翰。

例句：My grandmother passed on last year. 我的祖母去年去世了。|近义词：transmit, convey, forward, decease|反义词：keep, retain, survive|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He passes on the news to his colleagues every morning. 他每天早上都把新闻传达给他的同事。
She passes on her wisdom to her students. 她把她的智慧传授给她的学生们。


一般过去时:
They passed on the secret to their children. 他们把秘密传给了他们的孩子。
He passed on peacefully in his sleep. 他在睡梦中安详地去世了。


现在完成时:
I have passed on the information to the relevant department. 我已经把信息传给了相关部门。
She has passed on all her belongings to charity. 她已经把所有财产捐给了慈善机构。


过去完成时:
They had passed on the message before he arrived. 在他到达之前，他们已经传达了这个消息。
My grandfather had passed on before I was born. 我爷爷在我出生前就去世了。


将来时:
I will pass on your comments to my supervisor. 我会把你的评论转达给我的主管。
He will pass on soon, the doctor said. 医生说他很快就会去世。


虚拟语气:
If I had known earlier, I would have passed on the information sooner. 如果我早知道，我就会早点传达这个信息。
I wish I could have passed on my knowledge to him before he left. 我真希望在他离开之前能把我的知识传授给他。


被动语态:
The message was passed on to the authorities. 这条消息被传达给了当局。
The inheritance was passed on to his son. 遗产传给了他的儿子。


疑问句:
Will you pass on the invitation to your friends? 你会把邀请函转达给你的朋友们吗？
Did he pass on the information to the police? 他有没有把信息告诉警察？


否定句:
I didn't pass on the message because I forgot. 我没有传达这个消息，因为我忘记了。
He didn't want to pass on the bad news to her. 他不想把坏消息告诉她。
