impanelments:/ɪmˈpæn(ə)lmənts/ | n. |陪审团成员的选拔；组成陪审团 |The process of selecting and assembling a jury.  This often involves summoning potential jurors, questioning them (voir dire), and selecting those who will serve on the jury.  |Example: The impanelments of the jury took longer than expected due to numerous challenges. 陪审团的选拔比预期时间长，因为有很多异议。 |近义词：jury selection, empaneling |反义词：dismissal of jurors
