curcumin:/ˈkɜːrkjʊmɪn/| n. |姜黄素|A bright yellow chemical compound found in turmeric, a spice derived from the Curcuma longa plant. It has been studied for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.  |Example:  Studies have shown that curcumin possesses potent anti-inflammatory effects.  研究表明，姜黄素具有强大的抗炎作用。
