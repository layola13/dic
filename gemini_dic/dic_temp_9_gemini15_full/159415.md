polarization:/ˌpoʊləraɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |极化；偏振；分歧；对立|The process of causing something to become polarized, or the state of being polarized.  This can refer to light waves, electric charges, or opinions within a group.  |例句：The polarization of light waves can be controlled using a polarizing filter.  光波的偏振可以通过偏振滤光片来控制。

例句：The political polarization in the country is alarming.  该国的政治极化令人担忧。

|近义词：division, split, cleavage, dichotomy |反义词：unification, unity, consensus, harmony |名词
