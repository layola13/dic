overencouraging: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈkɜːrɪdʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 过分鼓励的，过度鼓励的 | Excessively encouraging;  going beyond what is appropriate or helpful in encouraging someone.  It implies that the encouragement is excessive and potentially detrimental. |


例句：

His overencouraging of his son led to the boy's unrealistic expectations.  他的过度鼓励导致了儿子不切实际的期望。

The coach's overencouraging attitude made the players complacent. 教练过分鼓励的态度使得队员们变得自满。


近义词：excessive, immoderate, overzealous, overenthusiastic

反义词：discouraging, discouraging, underestimating


词性：形容词
