reclaimer:/rɪˈkleɪmər/| n. |回收者；开垦者；索回者 |A reclaimer is a person or thing that reclaims something.  This can refer to reclaiming land from the sea, reclaiming waste materials for reuse, or reclaiming something that was lost or stolen. |例句：The farmer is a reclaimer of marginal land.  这个农民是一个开垦边际土地的人。

The company uses a reclaimer to process plastic waste. 这家公司使用回收机来处理塑料垃圾。


近义词：recycler, redeemer, recoverer |反义词：waster, destroyer, abandoner |名词
