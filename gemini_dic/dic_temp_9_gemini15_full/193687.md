self-communication:/ˌsɛlf kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 自我沟通，内省 | The process of communicating with oneself; internal dialogue or mental processes involved in reflection, planning, or problem-solving.  It includes internal speech, visualization, and emotional processing. | Example:  Through self-communication, she identified the root cause of her anxiety. 通过自我沟通，她找到了焦虑的根源。


Example: He engaged in self-communication to plan his next move. 他进行自我沟通来计划他的下一步行动。| 近义词：introspection, self-reflection, internal dialogue | 反义词：external communication, dialogue with others |  名词
