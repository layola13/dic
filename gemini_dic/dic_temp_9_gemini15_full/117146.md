meaningless:/ˈmiːnɪŋləs/| adj.|无意义的，毫无价值的|Lacking significance, purpose, or value.|The movie was utterly meaningless; I couldn't understand the plot.|近义词：pointless, futile, senseless, nonsensical|反义词：meaningful, significant, purposeful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His words are meaningless to me. = 他的话对我来说毫无意义。
This task feels meaningless. = 这项任务感觉毫无意义。

一般过去时:
The meeting was completely meaningless. = 那次会议完全没有意义。
Yesterday's argument was meaningless. = 昨天的争论毫无意义。

现在完成时:
All my efforts have been meaningless. = 我所有的努力都白费了。
His promises have proven meaningless. = 他的承诺已被证明是空话。

过去完成时:
The decision had been meaningless from the start. = 这个决定从一开始就没有意义。
I realized my actions had been meaningless. = 我意识到我的行为毫无意义。

将来时:
Their actions will be meaningless. = 他们的行为将毫无意义。
This plan will be meaningless without funding. = 没有资金，这个计划将毫无意义。

虚拟语气:
If only I hadn't wasted my time on that meaningless task. = 要是我没有把时间浪费在那项毫无意义的任务上就好了。
It would be meaningless to continue without more information. = 没有更多信息继续下去是没有意义的。

被动语态:

The research was deemed meaningless by the scientific community. = 这项研究被科学界认为毫无意义。

疑问句:

Was the whole project meaningless? = 整个项目毫无意义吗？

否定句:
The situation is not meaningless; it has implications. = 情况并非毫无意义；它有影响。
The work is not meaningless; it has a purpose. = 这项工作并非毫无意义；它有目的。
