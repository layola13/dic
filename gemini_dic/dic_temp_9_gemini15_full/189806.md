salvaging:/ˈsæl.vɪdʒɪŋ/| v.|抢救；打捞；挽救；挽回 |The act of saving something from loss or destruction.  It implies rescuing something from a difficult or dangerous situation. |例句：Firefighters are still salvaging belongings from the burned-out houses. 消防队员仍在从烧毁的房屋中抢救财物。

例句：The company is attempting to salvage its reputation after the scandal.  这家公司正试图在丑闻后挽回名誉。

近义词：recovering, rescuing, retrieving, preserving

反义词：abandoning, destroying, losing, ruining

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They salvage valuable items from the shipwreck. = 他们从沉船中打捞贵重物品。
We salvage what we can from the failed project. = 我们从失败的项目中挽救我们能挽救的东西。


一般过去时:
Divers salvaged the sunken treasure. = 潜水员打捞了沉没的宝藏。
The team salvaged the situation with a last-minute goal. = 球队凭借最后一分钟的进球挽救了局面。


现在完成时:
They have salvaged many artifacts from the excavation site. = 他们已经从挖掘现场打捞出许多文物。
We have salvaged our reputation after a difficult period. = 艰难时期之后，我们已经挽回了声誉。


过去完成时:
The crew had salvaged the ship before the storm hit. = 船员在暴风雨来临之前已经抢救了船只。
They had salvaged some data from the damaged computer. = 他们已经从损坏的电脑中抢救了一些数据。


将来时:
They will salvage the damaged aircraft. = 他们将抢修受损的飞机。
The crew will salvage the cargo from the sinking vessel. = 船员将从下沉的船只上打捞货物。


虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, we could have salvaged the deal. = 如果我们早点行动，就能挽回这笔交易。
I wish we had salvaged more from the fire. = 我真希望我们从火灾中抢救出更多东西。


被动语态:
The sunken ship was salvaged last year. = 那艘沉船去年被打捞上来了。
The damaged documents were salvaged by the archivists. = 受损的文件被档案管理员抢救了。


疑问句:
Can we salvage this relationship? = 我们能挽回这段关系吗？
Will they be able to salvage the situation? = 他们能够挽回局面吗？


否定句:
They couldn't salvage the situation. = 他们无法挽回局面。
We failed to salvage any useful information. = 我们未能抢救任何有用的信息。
