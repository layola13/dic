rumours:/ˈruːmərz/| n.|谣言，传闻|Unsubstantiated statements or beliefs that are circulated among people.|例句：Rumors of a merger spread quickly through the company. 谣言迅速在公司里传播开来。|近义词：gossip, hearsay, speculation|反义词：facts, truths


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Rumors spread quickly in a small town. = 谣言在小城镇里传播很快。
Many rumors circulate online. = 许多谣言在网上流传。


一般过去时:
Rumors flew around after the accident. = 事故发生后，谣言四起。
She denied the rumors that were spread about her. = 她否认了关于她的谣言。


现在完成时:
Rumors have circulated about a possible resignation. = 关于可能辞职的谣言已经传开了。
Have you heard any rumors about the new project? = 你听说过关于新项目的任何谣言吗？


过去完成时:
Before the announcement, rumors had already spread. = 在宣布之前，谣言已经传播开来。
They had heard many rumors before they finally met. = 在他们最终见面之前，他们已经听说了许多谣言。


将来时:
Rumors will likely spread if the truth isn't revealed. = 如果真相不揭露，谣言可能会蔓延。
I predict that more rumors will surface soon. = 我预测很快就会出现更多谣言。


虚拟语气:
If those rumors were true, it would be a disaster. = 如果那些谣言是真的，那将是一场灾难。
I wish those nasty rumors would just disappear. = 我希望那些难听的谣言消失。


被动语态:
The rumors were spread by a disgruntled employee. = 谣言是由一名不满的员工散布的。
The truth is being obscured by the rumors. = 真相正被谣言掩盖。


疑问句:
Are there any rumors about the upcoming election? = 关于即将到来的选举有什么谣言吗？
What rumors have you heard lately? = 你最近听到什么谣言了？


否定句:
There were no rumors of any wrongdoing. = 没有关于任何不当行为的谣言。
I don't believe those rumors. = 我不相信那些谣言。


PHRASE->
spread rumors = 散布谣言
start rumors = 开始传谣
deny rumors = 否认谣言
believe rumors = 相信谣言
fuel rumors = 煽风点火 (使谣言加剧)
