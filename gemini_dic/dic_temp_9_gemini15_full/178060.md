reclassing: /ˌriːˈklæsɪŋ/ | v. |重新分类；重新归类 | The act of assigning something to a different class or category.  This often involves a reassessment or reorganization of existing classifications. |例句：The company is reclassing its assets to reflect the new accounting standards. 公司正在重新分类其资产以反映新的会计准则。|例句：The librarian is reclassing the books according to a new Dewey Decimal system. 图书管理员正在根据新的杜威十进制系统重新分类书籍。|近义词：recategorizing, reclassifying, reorganizing |反义词：grouping, classifying (in its original categorization) |动词
