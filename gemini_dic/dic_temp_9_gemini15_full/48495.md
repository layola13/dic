deepening:/ˈdiːpənɪŋ/ | n. |加深；变深；加剧|The deepening of the river made navigation difficult. 河水的加深使航行变得困难。|近义词：intensification, aggravation |反义词：shallowing, alleviation |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river is deepening. = 河水正在加深。
His understanding of the problem is deepening. = 他对这个问题的理解正在加深。

一般过去时:
The hole deepened overnight. = 洞一夜之间变深了。
Her anxiety deepened as the day went on. = 随着一天的过去，她的焦虑加剧了。

现在完成时:
The crisis has deepened significantly. = 危机已经显著加剧。
My knowledge of the subject has deepened considerably through study. = 通过学习，我对这个主题的知识有了相当大的提高。

过去完成时:
The recession had deepened before the government intervened. = 在政府干预之前，经济衰退已经加剧了。
His distrust had deepened after the incident. = 那件事之后，他的不信任感加深了。

将来时:
The conflict will deepen if a solution isn't found. = 如果找不到解决办法，冲突将会加剧。
The color of the dye will deepen with time. = 染料的颜色会随着时间的推移而加深。

虚拟语气:
If the wound deepened, it would require surgery. = 如果伤口加深，就需要手术。
I wish the mystery didn't deepen with each passing day. = 我希望这个谜团不要随着每一天的过去而加深。

被动语态:
The mystery is being deepened by new evidence. = 新证据正在加深这个谜团。
The well was being deepened by the workers. = 工人们正在加深井。

疑问句:
Is the problem deepening? = 问题正在加剧吗？
Is the color deepening as it should? = 颜色是否按预期加深了？

否定句:
The relationship did not deepen. = 这段关系没有加深。
The color didn't deepen as expected. = 颜色没有像预期的那样加深。
