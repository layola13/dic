locomotor:/ˈloʊkəmoʊtər/| adj. |运动的；运动器官的；有关运动的| Relating to movement or locomotion.  The word describes something that relates to the act of moving from one place to another, either by the organism itself or by the parts responsible for that movement. |例句：Locomotor skills are essential for children's development. 运动技能对儿童的发展至关重要。

例句：The locomotor system includes bones, muscles, and joints. 运动系统包括骨骼、肌肉和关节。


近义词：motor, motile, kinetic|反义词：stationary, immobile, static|词性: adjective
