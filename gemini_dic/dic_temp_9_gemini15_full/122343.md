mixer:/ˈmɪksər/ | n. |搅拌器；混音器；混合器 | A device used for mixing ingredients, such as a kitchen appliance for blending food or a device for combining audio signals.  | Example: She used a hand mixer to make the cake batter. 她用手动搅拌器来制作蛋糕糊。 |近义词：blender, homogenizer, consolidator |反义词：separator


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mixer blends the ingredients smoothly. = 搅拌器将食材混合得非常顺滑。
He mixes the drinks expertly. = 他很熟练地调制饮料。


一般过去时:
She mixed the colors to create a unique shade. = 她混合了颜色以创造出独特的色调。
The sound engineer mixed the tracks for the album. = 音响工程师为专辑混音。


现在完成时:
They have mixed the cement for the foundation. = 他们已经混合了地基的混凝土。

过去完成时:
