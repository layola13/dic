ebbing:/ˈɛbɪŋ/| v.|逐渐减少；衰退；退潮 |To gradually decrease or decline; to recede (as of a tide).|例句：The river is ebbing. 河水正在退去。
例句：His enthusiasm is ebbing away. 他的热情正在消退。|近义词：decreasing, waning, receding, subsiding|反义词：increasing, waxing, flowing, surging|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tide is ebbing slowly. = 潮水正在慢慢退去。
The river is ebbing towards the sea. = 河水正流向大海。

一般过去时:
The water ebbed away after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，水退去了。
The crowd ebbed away as the concert ended. = 音乐会结束后，人群散去了。

现在完成时:
The floodwaters have ebbed significantly. = 洪水已经明显消退。
His strength has ebbed since his illness. = 他自从生病以来，体力就衰退了。

过去完成时:
The tide had ebbed before we reached the shore. = 我们到达海岸之前，潮水就已经退了。
His confidence had ebbed after the defeat. = 比赛失利后，他的信心已经减弱了。

将来时:
The tide will ebb later this evening. = 今晚晚些时候潮水将会退去。
His influence will ebb as his power declines. = 随着权力的下降，他的影响力也会减弱。

虚拟语气:
If the tide ebbed faster, we could have reached the island. = 如果潮水退得更快，我们就能到达岛上了。
If his energy ebbed any further, he would have to stop. = 如果他的精力再衰退一些，他将不得不停止。

被动语态:
The water level is being ebbed by the low tide. = 水位正在被低潮退去。 (此用法较少见，通常用被动语态表达退潮现象时，会用其他动词)

疑问句:
Is the tide ebbing now? = 潮水现在正在退吗？
Is his influence ebbing? = 他的影响力正在减弱吗？

否定句:
The tide isn't ebbing yet. = 潮水还没退呢。
His popularity isn't ebbing; it's growing. = 他的名气没有减弱，反而在增长。


PHRASE->
ebbing tide = 退潮
ebbing away = 逐渐消失
ebbing strength = 衰退的力量
ebbing resources = 减少的资源
ebbing enthusiasm = 减少的热情
