piper:/ˈpaɪpər/| n. |吹笛者；(尤指)牧羊人；胡椒粉贩子；|A piper is a person who plays the pipe, especially a shepherd or a musician who plays a bagpipe.  The word can also refer to someone who sells pepper.|例句：The piper played a lively tune. 吹笛者奏起了欢快的曲子。 The old piper led the sheep across the fields. 老牧羊人吹着笛子带领羊群穿过田野。|近义词：musician, player, bagpiper|反义词：listener
