/ˌɜːɡəˈnɒmɪks/ | n. | 人体工程学 | The study of people's efficiency in their working environment.  It aims to improve workplace design to reduce discomfort and increase productivity. | Example: Good ergonomics in the office can prevent repetitive strain injuries. 良好的办公室人体工程学设计可以预防重复性劳损。  | 近义词：human factors,  ergometry | 反义词：None
