post-note:/ˈpoʊstˌnoʊt/| n. |便签；便条 |A small piece of paper, often sticky-backed, used for writing down short messages or reminders.  |Example: I left a post-it note on his desk. 我在他的桌子上留了一张便签。|近义词：sticky note, memo, reminder |反义词：N/A
