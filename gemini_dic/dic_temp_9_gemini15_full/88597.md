hitting:/ˈhɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 打击；碰撞；影响；成功 | The act of striking something;  having a strong effect or impact; achieving success. |例句：He was hitting the ball hard. 他用力击球。

The storm hit the coast hard. 风暴猛烈地袭击了海岸。


近义词：striking, impacting, affecting, succeeding | 反义词：missing, failing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is hitting the ball well. = 他击球很好。
She hits the gym every morning. = 她每天早上都去健身房。


一般过去时:
He hit the nail on the head. = 他说对了。
The car hit a tree. = 车撞到了一棵树上。


现在完成时:
He has hit a home run. = 他打出了全垒打。
She has hit a rough patch in her life. = 她的人生经历了一段艰难时期。


过去完成时:
He had hit the ball before the umpire called a foul. = 在裁判判罚犯规之前，他已经击球了。
She had hit the jackpot before she realized it. = 在她意识到之前，她已经中了大奖。


将来时:
He will hit the ball over the fence. = 他将把球击过围栏。
She will hit the road tomorrow. = 她明天就要上路了。


虚拟语气:
If he hit the ball harder, he would have won the game. = 如果他击球更用力，他就会赢得比赛。
I wish I had hit the brakes sooner. = 我真希望我早点刹车。


被动语态:
The ball was hit out of the park. = 球被击出了球场。
The window was hit by a stone. = 窗户被石头砸中了。


疑问句:
Did he hit the target? = 他击中目标了吗？
What did he hit? = 他撞到了什么？


否定句:
He didn't hit the target. = 他没有击中目标。
She didn't hit the brakes. = 她没有踩刹车。
