planing:/ˈpleɪnɪŋ/| v.|刨平；计划；设计|The act of making something smooth and flat, usually with a plane.  It can also refer to making plans or designs.|例句：He spent the afternoon planing the wood for the table. 他花了整个下午刨平做桌子的木头。

例句：We need to carefully plan the marketing planing for the new product. 我们需要仔细计划新产品的营销计划。


近义词：smoothing, shaping, designing, scheming |反义词：roughening, disrupting
