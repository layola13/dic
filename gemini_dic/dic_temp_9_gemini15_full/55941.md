dizzily:/ˈdɪzɪli/| adj.|头晕目眩地；令人晕眩地|In a dizzy or confused manner; causing dizziness.|例句：He walked dizzily after hitting his head. 他撞到头后头晕眼花地走着。  The rapid descent made me feel dizzily.|快速下降让我感到头晕目眩。|近义词：giddily, lightheadedly, woozily|反义词：steadily, calmly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He walks dizzily after drinking too much. = 他喝太多酒后步履蹒跚。
She speaks dizzily from exhaustion. = 她因疲惫而说话含糊不清。


一般过去时:

被动语态:
(This adjective is rarely used in the passive voice, as it describes a state of being rather than an action performed upon someone/something.)
