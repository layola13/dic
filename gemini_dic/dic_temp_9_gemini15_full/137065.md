nonstratified: /ˌnɒnˈstrætɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 非分层的；未分层的 | Not arranged or classified into layers or strata.  It refers to a lack of layering or stratification, typically in contexts like geology, sociology, or sampling. |


例句：

The soil in this region is nonstratified, making it difficult to determine its age.  = 这个区域的土壤是非分层的，这使得难以确定它的年龄。

The nonstratified sampling method ensures a more representative sample of the population. = 非分层抽样方法确保对总体更具代表性的样本。


近义词：unlayered, homogeneous (in some contexts)


反义词：stratified, layered


词性：形容词
