cantabrigian:/ˌkæntəˈbrɪdʒɪən/ | adj. n. |剑桥的；剑桥大学的；剑桥人 | Of or relating to Cambridge, especially the University of Cambridge.  A person who is from Cambridge or who is associated with the University of Cambridge. |例句：He's a Cantabrigian, having graduated from Cambridge University. 他是剑桥人，毕业于剑桥大学。

例句：Many famous writers and scientists are Cantabrigians. 许多著名的作家和科学家都是剑桥人。


近义词：Cambridge graduate, Cambridge alumnus/alumna


反义词：Oxonian (a member of the University of Oxford)


词性：形容词，名词
