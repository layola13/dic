concluded:/kənˈkluːdɪd/| v.| 总结；结束；推断出 | To bring something to an end; to arrive at a judgment or decision based on reasoning or evidence. |例句：The meeting concluded with a vote.  会议以投票结束。|例句：We concluded that the experiment was a success. 我们得出结论，实验是成功的。|近义词：finished, ended, determined, inferred |反义词：began, started, commenced


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He concludes his presentations with a Q&A session. = 他以问答环节结束他的演讲。
She concludes every chapter with a brief summary. = 她每一章都以简短的总结结尾。


一般过去时:
The judge concluded the trial after hearing all the evidence. = 审判员在听取所有证据后结束了审判。
They concluded their research after several months of hard work. = 经过几个月的努力，他们完成了研究。


现在完成时:
The committee has concluded its investigation. = 委员会已经完成了调查。
Scientists have concluded that climate change is real. = 科学家们已经得出结论，气候变化是真实的。


过去完成时:
After he had concluded his speech, the audience applauded. = 他演讲结束后，观众鼓掌。
They had concluded the negotiations before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前完成了谈判。


将来时:
We will conclude the meeting in ten minutes. = 我们十分钟后结束会议。
She will conclude her book by next year. = 她明年将完成她的书。


虚拟语气:
If he had concluded the deal earlier, he would have made more money. = 如果他早点达成协议，他就会赚更多钱。
I wish I had concluded my report sooner. = 我希望我早点完成我的报告。


被动语态:
The meeting was concluded at 5 pm. = 会议在下午5点结束。
The investigation was concluded without any findings. = 调查结束时没有任何发现。


疑问句:
Have they concluded their research yet? = 他们完成研究了吗？
When will the conference conclude? = 会议什么时候结束？


否定句:
The meeting did not conclude until late at night. = 会议直到深夜才结束。
He did not conclude his argument effectively. = 他没有有效地结束他的论证。


PHRASE->
