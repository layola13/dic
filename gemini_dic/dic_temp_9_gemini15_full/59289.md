eagre:/ˈiːɡər/| adj.|渴望的；急切的；汹涌的 | Eager means having or showing intense and impatient desire or anticipation for something.  It can also describe a strong current of water, especially a tidal bore. |例句：She was eager to start her new job. 她渴望开始她的新工作。  The river was eagre and dangerous.  河水汹涌而危险。|近义词：avid, keen, ardent, zealous |反义词：apathetic, indifferent, reluctant
