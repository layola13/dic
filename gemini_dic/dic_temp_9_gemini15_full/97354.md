infra-reds:/ˈɪnfrəˌrɛdz/| n.|红外线|Infrared radiation is electromagnetic radiation with wavelengths longer than those of visible light.  It's invisible to the human eye but can be detected by special instruments.|例句：The remote control uses infra-red light to communicate with the television. 遥控器使用红外线与电视机通讯。|例句：Infrared cameras are used to detect heat signatures.红外线摄像机用于探测热信号。|近义词：infrared radiation, IR|反义词：ultraviolet, visible light|名词
