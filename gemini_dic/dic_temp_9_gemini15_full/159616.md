pollock:/ˈpɒlək/| n. |狭鳕；波洛克鱼|A pollock is a type of fish belonging to the Gadidae family, similar to cod.  They are commonly found in the North Atlantic and North Pacific oceans.  They are a commercially important fish, often used in fish sticks, fillets, and other processed seafood products.|例句：We had pollock for dinner last night. 昨晚我们吃了狭鳕。|例句：The fisherman caught a large quantity of pollock. 渔民捕获了大量的狭鳕。|近义词：cod, haddock (partially, as they share some characteristics)|反义词：无
