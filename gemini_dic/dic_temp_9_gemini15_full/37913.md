complemented:/ˈkɒmplɪmɛntɪd/|/ˈkɑːmplɪmɛntɪd/| v. |补充，称赞，使完善 |To enhance or complete something by adding another element; to praise or express admiration for someone or something. |例句：Her red shoes complemented her dress perfectly.  她的红鞋子完美地衬托了她的裙子。

例句：The chef complemented the meal with a delicious sauce.  厨师用美味的酱汁来补充这道菜。


近义词：enhanced, improved, praised, lauded |反义词：criticized, belittled, diminished, marred


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He complements his wife daily. = 他每天都称赞他的妻子。
The blue curtains complement the sofa. = 蓝色的窗帘与沙发相得益彰。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:

She has complemented his efforts on many occasions. = 她多次称赞了他的努力。


过去完成时:
