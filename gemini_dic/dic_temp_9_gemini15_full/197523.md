shelf:/ʃɛlf/| n. |架子，搁板|A shelf is a flat, horizontal surface attached to a wall or other structure, used for storing things.|例句：I put the books on the shelf.我把书放在架子上。

例句：There are many shelves in the library. 图书馆里有很多书架。


近义词：rack, ledge,  |反义词:floor, ground


词源:
源自古英语 *scylf，意为“船的侧边”，可能与古诺尔斯语 *skelf（“碎片”）有关。  其含义演变为指类似船侧边的水平表面。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The books are on the shelf. = 书在架子上。
He keeps his trophies on the shelf. = 他把奖杯放在架子上。


一般过去时:
I put the vase on the shelf. = 我把花瓶放在架子上。
She removed the old books from the shelf. = 她从架子上移走了旧书。


现在完成时:
We have placed all the files on the shelf. = 我们已经把所有文件都放在架子上了。
She has organized all the items on the shelf. = 她已经整理好了架子上的所有物品。


过去完成时:
They had already filled the shelves before the store opened. = 商店开门前他们就已经把货架摆满了。
He had removed the books from the shelf before the earthquake. = 地震发生前他已经把书从架子上拿下来了。


将来时:
I will put the new plant on the shelf tomorrow. = 我明天会把新植物放在架子上。
They will arrange the items on the shelf neatly. = 他们会把架子上的物品摆放整齐。


虚拟语气:
If I had a bigger shelf, I would display all my collectibles. = 如果我有一个更大的架子，我会把我的所有收藏品都展示出来。
I wish I had a shelf for all my shoes. = 我希望我有一个放鞋子的架子。


被动语态:
The books were arranged on the shelf by the librarian. = 书籍是由图书管理员摆放在架子上的。
The shelf is being cleaned. = 架子正在被清洁。


疑问句:
Is the book on the shelf? = 书在架子上吗？
Where is the shelf located? = 架子在哪里？


否定句:
The shelf isn't strong enough to hold all those books. = 架子不够结实，无法承受所有那些书。
There are no books on the shelf. = 架子上没有书。


PHRASE->
on the shelf:  搁置；束之高阁
off the shelf:  现成的；现货的
a shelf life:  保质期
book shelf: 书架
upper shelf: 上层货架
