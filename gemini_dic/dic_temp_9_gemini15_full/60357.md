egregiousness:/ɪˈɡriːdʒəsnəs/ | n. | 恶劣；极其糟糕；令人震惊 | The egregiousness of his crime was undeniable.  他的罪行极其恶劣，不容否认。|近义词：atrocity, enormity, monstrosity | 反义词：excellence, perfection


英文解析:The quality or state of being egregious; extremely bad or shocking.  It refers to something that is outstandingly bad, shocking, or reprehensible.

例句:

The egregiousness of the dictator's actions led to widespread international condemnation. 独裁者的行为极其恶劣，导致国际社会广泛谴责。


The egregiousness of the environmental damage caused by the oil spill was immediately apparent. 石油泄漏造成的环境破坏极其严重，立即显而易见。


近义词:

outrageousness, heinousness, flagrancy, depravity


反义词:

excellence, impeccability, virtue, rectitude


词性:noun
