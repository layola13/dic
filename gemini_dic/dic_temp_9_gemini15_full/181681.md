relent: /rɪˈlɛnt/ | v. | 变温和；心软；减弱；放松 | to become less severe, harsh, or strict; to soften in feeling; to slacken in intensity | 近义词：soften, yield, give in, ease up | 反义词：persist, continue, harden, intensify |

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain relentlessly falls. = 雨持续不断地下着。
He never relents in his pursuit of justice. = 他从不放弃追求正义。


一般过去时:
The storm finally relented in the evening. = 暴风雨最终在晚上减弱了。
She relented and agreed to help us. = 她心软了，同意帮助我们。


现在完成时:
The government has not relented on its austerity measures. = 政府尚未放松其紧缩措施。
He has relented and apologized for his behavior. = 他已经心软并为自己的行为道歉了。


过去完成时:
After hours of pleading, she had finally relented. = 经过数小时的恳求，她终于心软了。
The pressure had relented slightly before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，压力略微减轻了。


将来时:
Will the harsh winter finally relent? = 严冬最终会过去吗？
I hope the boss will relent and give us a raise. = 我希望老板会心软并给我们加薪。


虚拟语气:
If only the pain would relent! = 但愿疼痛能减轻！
I wish the sun would relent and give us some shade. = 我希望太阳能稍微温和一些，给我们一些阴凉。


被动语态:
His demands were not relented to. = 他的要求没有得到满足。
The pressure on the dam was slowly relented. = 大坝上的压力慢慢减轻了。


疑问句:
Will the teacher relent on the deadline? = 老师会延长截止日期吗？
Will the government relent and lower taxes? = 政府会心软并降低税收吗？


否定句:
The enemy refused to relent. = 敌人拒绝退让。
The judge did not relent in his sentencing. = 法官在判刑时毫不手软。


PHRASE->
relentlessly pursue = 不懈追求
relentless pressure = 持续不断的压力
relentless criticism = 无情的批评
relentless effort = 不懈的努力
relentless determination = 坚定的决心
