hunter:/ˈhʌntər/| n. |猎人，狩猎者|A person who hunts animals.  |例句：The hunter tracked the deer for miles.  猎人追踪鹿走了好几英里。|例句：Experienced hunters know how to follow animal tracks. 经验丰富的猎人知道如何追踪动物的足迹。|近义词：tracker, huntsman, sportsman|反义词：prey


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hunts deer in the forest. = 他在森林里打猎鹿。
The hunter tracks his prey carefully. = 猎人仔细追踪他的猎物。


一般过去时:
She hunted rabbits when she was young. = 她年轻的时候打猎兔子。
The hunters followed the bear's trail. = 猎人沿着熊的足迹追踪。


现在完成时:
They have hunted in this area for years. = 他们在这个地区打猎多年了。
The hunter has already bagged a deer. = 猎人已经打到了一只鹿。


过去完成时:
He had hunted before he became a ranger. = 他在成为护林员之前就打猎了。
The hunters had prepared their traps before nightfall. = 猎人天黑前就准备好了陷阱。


将来时:
We will hunt ducks tomorrow. = 我们明天将去打猎鸭子。
The hunter will use a new rifle next season. = 猎人下个季度将使用一支新步枪。


虚拟语气:
If I were a hunter, I would hunt lions. = 如果我是个猎人，我会去猎狮。
I wish I had hunted more responsibly in the past. = 我希望我过去打猎更负责任一些。


被动语态:
The deer was hunted by the hunter. = 鹿被猎人猎杀了。
The area has been hunted extensively. = 这个地区已经被过度猎捕了。


疑问句:
Does he hunt often? = 他经常打猎吗？
Where do they hunt? = 他们在哪里打猎？


否定句:
He doesn't hunt anymore. = 他不再打猎了。
They didn't hunt illegally. = 他们没有非法打猎。
