eureka:/juˈriːkə/| int.|尤里卡！（我找到了！）|An exclamation of triumph at a discovery or solution.  It is often used to express sudden realization or a moment of insight.|例句：Eureka! I've solved the puzzle!  尤里卡！我解开了谜题！
例句：He shouted "Eureka!" when he found the lost key. 他找到丢失的钥匙时喊了一声“尤里卡！”|近义词：I found it!, I got it!, Aha!, I have it!|反义词：None.
