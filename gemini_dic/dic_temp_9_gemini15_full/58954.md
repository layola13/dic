dusting:/ˈdʌstɪŋ/| n. v.|轻微的灰尘；掸去灰尘；|The act of removing dust.  It can refer to a light covering of dust or the process of cleaning something by removing dust.|例句：The furniture needs a dusting.  家具需要掸掸灰尘。

The room was covered in a light dusting of snow.  房间覆盖着一层薄薄的雪。|近义词：cleaning, wiping, brushing|反义词：soiling, dirtying


USAGE->

一般现在时:
She dusts the furniture every week. = 她每周都擦拭家具。
He dusts his bookshelves regularly. = 他定期清理他的书架。


一般过去时:
I dusted the shelves yesterday. = 我昨天擦了架子上的灰尘。
They dusted the antique table carefully. = 他们小心翼翼地擦拭古董桌子。


现在完成时:
I have dusted the entire house. = 我已经打扫了整个房子。
She has dusted the piano twice this week. = 她本周已经擦拭过钢琴两次了。


过去完成时:
I had dusted the room before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前我已经打扫了房间。
They had dusted the artifacts before putting them on display. = 他们把文物展出之前已经擦拭过了。


将来时:
I will dust the living room tomorrow. = 我明天将打扫客厅。
She will dust the car before the show. = 她将在展览前擦拭汽车。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would dust the attic. = 如果我有时间，我会打扫阁楼。
I wish I had dusted the shelves before the party. = 我真希望在聚会前我已经擦拭了架子。


被动语态:
The furniture is dusted weekly. = 家具每周都打扫。
The antique vase was carefully dusted by the curator. = 古董花瓶被馆长小心地擦拭过。


疑问句:
Have you dusted the bedroom yet? = 你已经打扫卧室了吗？
Will you dust the mantlepiece today? = 你今天会打扫壁炉架吗？


否定句:
I didn't dust the furniture today. = 我今天没有打扫家具。
She hasn't dusted the bookshelves for months. = 她几个月都没打扫书架了。
