ethernet:/ˈiːθərnɛt/| n. |以太网，以太网络|A system for connecting computers and other devices on a local area network (LAN) using a standard set of rules and protocols.  It's the most common type of wired networking technology used in homes, offices, and businesses.|Example:  Our office uses Ethernet cables to connect all the computers.|近义词：LAN (local area network)|反义词：Wi-Fi (wireless network)
