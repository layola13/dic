overactivates:/ˌoʊvərˈæktɪveɪts/ | vt. |过度激活；使过度活跃|  To make something excessively active or stimulate to an excessive degree.  It implies an unhealthy or unsustainable level of activity. |例句：Stress can overactivate the immune system, leading to autoimmune diseases.  压力会过度激活免疫系统，导致自身免疫性疾病。

例句：The medication overactivates certain brain regions, causing unwanted side effects.  这种药物会过度激活大脑某些区域，从而导致不良副作用。


近义词：hyperactivates, overstimulates, excites excessively


反义词：underactivates, suppresses, inhibits


词性:动词
