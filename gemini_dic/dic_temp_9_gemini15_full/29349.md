castling:/ˈkæsəlɪŋ/| n. |国际象棋中的车马换位|The act of moving a king two squares toward a rook, and then placing the rook on the square the king crossed.  This special move is only allowed under specific conditions. |例句：Castling is a crucial strategic move in chess. 车马换位是国际象棋中一项至关重要的战略性移动。


例句：He tried to castle, but his king was in check. 他试图车马换位，但他的国王处于被将军的状态。


近义词：kingside castling (王翼车马换位), queenside castling (后翼车马换位)


反义词：无


词性:名词
