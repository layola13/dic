retakers:/riːˈteɪkərz/| n. | 重考者；再次尝试者 | People who are taking an exam or test again, usually because they failed the first time.  They might be retaking a course or a specific exam. | Example: The retakers were nervously reviewing their notes before the exam. 重考者们在考试前紧张地复习笔记。 The number of retakers has increased this year. 今年重考的人数增加了。|近义词：repeaters, those who retake|反义词：first-timers, those who pass on the first attempt|名词
