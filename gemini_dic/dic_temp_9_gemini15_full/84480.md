halteres:/ˈhæl.tə.riːz/| n.|平衡棒；平衡器|The halteres are a pair of small, club-shaped organs found in the thorax of most flying insects, except for the Diptera (flies).  They are believed to function as gyroscopic sensors, helping the insect maintain balance and stability during flight.|例句：The halteres of a fly enable it to maintain stability in flight.  苍蝇的平衡棒使它能够在飞行中保持稳定。The rapid rotation of the halteres provides sensory feedback for flight control. 平衡棒的快速旋转为飞行控制提供感觉反馈。|近义词：gyroscope (in function)|反义词:|
