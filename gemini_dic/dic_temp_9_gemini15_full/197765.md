shim:/ʃɪm/| n. vt.|垫片；调整；填塞|A thin piece of material, such as metal or wood, inserted between two objects to fill a space or make them level.  Also, to adjust or level something using a shim.|例句：We used a shim to level the cabinet. 我们用垫片来使橱柜平整。  The mechanic shimmed the engine block. 技工用垫片调整了发动机缸体。|近义词：spacer, wedge, packing|反义词：None specifically.
