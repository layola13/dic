fully:/ˈfʊli/ | adv. | 完全地，彻底地 | completely, entirely, thoroughly; to the full extent |  The report fully investigated the incident.  这份报告对事件进行了彻底调查。| He was fully aware of the risks. 他完全意识到风险。| partially, incompletely |  partially, incompletely


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She fully understands the instructions. = 她完全理解了指示。
They fully support the new policy. = 他们完全支持这项新政策。


一般过去时:
I fully explained the situation to him. = 我向他充分解释了情况。
We fully enjoyed the concert. = 我们非常享受这场音乐会。


现在完成时:

They have fully implemented the plan. = 他们已完全实施了计划。


过去完成时:
She had fully prepared for the presentation. = 她已为演示做好了充分准备。
They had fully understood the consequences. = 他们已经完全理解了后果。


将来时:
We will fully investigate the matter. = 我们将对此事进行全面调查。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
