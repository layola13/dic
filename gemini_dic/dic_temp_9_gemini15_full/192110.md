scoutmaster:/ˈskaʊtˌmɑːstər/ | n. |童子军领队，童军辅导员 | A scoutmaster is the adult leader of a troop of Boy Scouts or Girl Scouts, responsible for their training, safety, and activities.  | Example: He was a dedicated scoutmaster who instilled valuable life skills in his troop. 他是一位尽职尽责的童子军领队，在他的队伍中灌输了宝贵的生存技能。  |近义词： troop leader,  group leader | 反义词：scout (as a contrasting role)
