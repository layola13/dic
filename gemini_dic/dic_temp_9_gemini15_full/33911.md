circumvents: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈvɛnt/ | vt. |规避；绕过；巧妙地避开 | To avoid something, especially cleverly or illegally.  |例句：The company circumvented the law by using a loophole. 公司利用漏洞规避了法律。|例句：He circumvented the problem by finding a creative solution. 他通过找到一个创造性的解决方案来规避了这个问题。|近义词：bypass, evade, avoid, circumvent, outmaneuver |反义词：confront, face, encounter |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He circumvents the rules. = 他规避规则。
The program circumvents security measures. = 该程序规避安全措施。

一般过去时:
She circumvented the traffic jam. = 她绕过了交通堵塞。
They circumvented the problem with a clever trick. = 他们用一个巧妙的计策绕过了这个问题。

现在完成时:
We have circumvented many obstacles. = 我们已经克服了许多障碍。
The company has circumvented regulations for years. = 这家公司多年来一直在规避法规。

过去完成时:
They had circumvented the security system before the alarm went off. = 警报响起之前，他们已经绕过了安全系统。
He had circumvented the issue by ignoring it completely. = 他通过完全忽略这个问题来规避了这个问题。

将来时:
She will circumvent the problem by using a different approach. = 她将通过使用不同的方法来规避这个问题。
They will circumvent the blockade by sea. = 他们将通过海上绕过封锁。

虚拟语气:
If we circumvented the rules, we could finish the project faster. = 如果我们规避规则，我们可以更快地完成项目。
I wish I had circumvented the traffic. = 我真希望我绕过了交通堵塞。

被动语态:
The law was circumvented by several companies. = 多家公司规避了法律。
The problem was circumvented by a clever workaround. = 这个问题通过一个巧妙的解决方法得到了规避。

疑问句:
How did he circumvent the issue? = 他是如何规避这个问题的？
Can we circumvent the regulations? = 我们能规避这些规章制度吗？

否定句:
We cannot circumvent the law. = 我们不能规避法律。
He did not circumvent the rules; he followed them precisely. = 他没有规避规则；他严格遵守了规则。


PHRASE->
circumvent obstacles = 克服障碍
circumvent difficulties = 克服困难
circumvent a problem = 规避问题
circumvent the rules = 规避规则
circumvent the system = 绕过系统


词源:
circumvent 源自拉丁语词组 *circumvenīre，由circum-  (周围，环绕) + venire (来) 构成，字面意思是“来回走动，包围”。  其含义逐渐演变为“巧妙地避开”或“规避”。
