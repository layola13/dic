flipflop:/ˈflɪpˌflɑːp/ | n. v. |人字拖；轻拍声；反复改变；快速翻转 | A flip-flop is a type of sandal that is held onto the foot by a thong between the big toe and the second toe.  It can also refer to a quick, repeated change or the sound of something flapping. |例句：I wore my flip-flops to the beach. 我穿着人字拖去海滩。  The sound of the flag flip-flopping in the wind was soothing. 旗帜在风中拍打的声音很舒缓。|近义词：thong sandal (n.),  flop (v.), vacillate (v.) |反义词：stay consistent (v.)
