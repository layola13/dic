distributive:/dɪˈstrɪbjʊtɪv/| adj.| 分布式的；分配的；分发的 | Relating to or characterized by distribution or the act of distributing.  It can refer to something that is spread out over an area or to a system where tasks or resources are shared among multiple parts. | Example: The company uses a distributive system to manage its inventory across multiple warehouses. 这家公司使用分布式系统来管理其跨多个仓库的库存。The distributive property of multiplication over addition states that a(b + c) = ab + ac.  乘法对加法的分配律指出 a(b + c) = ab + ac。|近义词：dispersive, apportioning, sharing |反义词：centralized, concentrated, unified


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company distributes its products nationwide. = 这家公司在全国范围内分销其产品。
He distributes flyers in the neighborhood. = 他在附近分发传单。

一般过去时:
They distributed food to the victims of the earthquake. = 他们向地震受害者分发了食物。
She distributed the questionnaires to the participants. = 她把问卷分发给了参与者。

现在完成时:
We have distributed all the invitations. = 我们已经分发了所有的邀请函。
The government has distributed aid to the affected areas. = 政府已经向受灾地区分发了援助物资。

过去完成时:
They had distributed the pamphlets before the conference began. = 会议开始前，他们已经分发了小册子。
She had distributed the workload evenly among her team. = 她已经平均分配了团队的工作量。

将来时:
We will distribute the profits among the shareholders. = 我们将把利润分配给股东们。
They will distribute the new software next week. = 他们下周将发布新的软件。

虚拟语气:
If we had a better distribution system, we would have reached more customers. = 如果我们有一个更好的分销系统，我们将能接触到更多的客户。
I wish we could distribute the resources more equitably. = 我希望我们能够更公平地分配资源。

被动语态:
The resources were distributed fairly among the members. = 资源在成员之间得到了公平的分配。
The new policy will be distributed to all employees. = 新政策将分发给所有员工。

疑问句:
How will the tasks be distributed among the team? = 任务将如何分配给团队？
Where are the products distributed? = 产品在哪里分销？

否定句:
The company did not distribute the bonus this year. = 公司今年没有发放奖金。
They haven't distributed the updated instructions yet. = 他们还没有分发更新后的说明。
