premices:/ˈprɛmɪsɪz/| n.|房屋，厂房；前提；房产| premises refers to a building and the land around it, often used in a business or commercial context.  It can also refer to a statement or proposition assumed to be true in order to deduce a conclusion.  The plural form is always used, even when referring to a single building.|例句：The company's premises are located downtown.  这家公司的厂房位于市中心。 The premises of his argument are flawed. 他论证的前提是错误的。|近义词：building, property, grounds, assumptions, propositions|反义词：conclusion, consequence
