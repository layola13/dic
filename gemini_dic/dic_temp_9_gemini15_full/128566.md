nibbles:/ˈnɪbəlz/ | n. |轻咬；少量食物 | Small bites of food, often eaten casually.  |Example: The baby nibbled on a cracker.  婴儿啃着饼干。|近义词：bites, pecks, munches |反义词：devours, gulps


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nibbles his food slowly. = 他慢慢地吃东西。
She nibbles on an apple during the meeting. = 她在会议期间啃苹果。


一般过去时:
The mouse nibbled the cheese. = 老鼠啃了奶酪。
He nibbled at his sandwich during the lecture. = 他在讲座期间啃着三明治。


现在完成时:
The dog has nibbled on my shoes. = 狗啃了我的鞋子。
She has nibbled all the cookies. = 她把所有的饼干都啃了。


过去完成时:
The rabbit had nibbled all the carrots before I arrived. = 我到达之前，兔子已经把所有的胡萝卜都啃了。
They had nibbled most of the cake before the party started. = 派对开始之前，他们已经啃掉了大部分蛋糕。


将来时:
The bird will nibble the seeds. = 鸟会啄食种子。
I think the cat will nibble on the fish later. = 我想猫晚些时候会啃鱼。


虚拟语气:
If I were a mouse, I would nibble the cheese. = 如果我是一只老鼠，我会啃奶酪。
I wish I hadn't nibbled all the chocolates. = 我真希望我没有把所有的巧克力都啃了。


被动语态:
The carrots were nibbled by the rabbit. = 胡萝卜被兔子啃了。
The cookies have been nibbled by someone. = 饼干被某人啃了。


疑问句:
Is the baby nibbling on something? = 婴儿在啃什么东西吗？
Has the mouse nibbled the whole cheese? = 老鼠把整个奶酪都啃了吗？


否定句:
The dog didn't nibble on the bone. = 狗没有啃骨头。
She didn't nibble the cake because she wasn't hungry. = 她不饿，所以没有啃蛋糕。


PHRASE->
a nibble of cheese 一小口奶酪
a gentle nibble 轻咬一口
a quick nibble 快速啃一口
a satisfying nibble 令人满足的一口
repeated nibbles 反复啃咬
