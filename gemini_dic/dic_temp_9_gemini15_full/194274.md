self-offense:/ˌsɛlfˈɒfɛns/| n.|自我攻击；自我伤害|The act of harming oneself, either physically or emotionally.  This can manifest in various ways, from self-harm behaviors like cutting to negative self-talk and destructive patterns of behavior.|例句：His self-offense manifested as excessive drinking and reckless driving.  他的自我攻击表现为酗酒和鲁莽驾驶。


例句：She sought therapy to address her underlying issues contributing to her self-offense. 她寻求治疗以解决导致她自我攻击的潜在问题。|近义词：self-harm, self-destruction, self-abuse|反义词：self-care, self-love, self-preservation|名词
