sequencing:/ˈsiːkwənsiŋ/ | n. | 测序；排序；顺序排列 | The arrangement of things in a particular order.  The process of determining the order of nucleotides in a DNA or RNA molecule. |例句：The sequencing of the human genome was a major scientific achievement.  人类基因组测序是一项重大的科学成就。

The company is working on improving its sequencing process to reduce costs. 这家公司正在努力改进其测序流程以降低成本。

|近义词：ordering, arrangement, ordering, alignment |反义词：randomization, disarray |名词
