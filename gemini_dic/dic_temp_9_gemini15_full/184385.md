respon:/rɪˈspɒn/ | v. | 回答；回应；反应 | To make a reply; to react. | He responded to my question with a smile. 他笑着回答了我的问题。| reply, answer, react | ignore, neglect | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He responds to emails promptly. 他及时回复邮件。
She responds well to treatment. 她对治疗反应良好。


一般过去时:
He responded quickly to the emergency. 他迅速应对紧急情况。
She responded positively to the news. 她对这个消息反应积极。


现在完成时:
He has responded to all the inquiries. 他已经回复了所有询问。
She has responded well to the new medication. 她对新药反应良好。


过去完成时:
He had responded before I could ask. 我还没来得及问，他就已经回答了。
She had responded angrily to his criticism. 她对他的批评愤怒地回应了。


将来时:
He will respond to your request soon. 他会很快回复你的请求。
She will respond to the challenge bravely. 她会勇敢地应对挑战。


虚拟语气:
If he responded honestly,  we would believe him. 如果他诚实地回答，我们会相信他。
I wish she had responded more politely. 我希望她能更礼貌地回应。


被动语态:
His question was responded to immediately. 他的问题得到了立即答复。
The proposal was responded to with enthusiasm.  该提案受到了热情的回应。


疑问句:
How did he respond to your suggestion? 他对你的建议作何回应？
Will they respond to our complaints? 他们会回应我们的投诉吗？


否定句:
He didn't respond to my call. 他没有回应我的电话。
She didn't respond to the treatment. 她对治疗没有反应。


PHRASE->
respond to: 对…作出反应
respond in kind: 以牙还牙
respond favorably: 积极回应
respond negatively: 消极回应
respond promptly: 及时回应
