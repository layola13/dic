hybridize:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪz/| vt. |杂交；混合；使杂交|To breed or produce a hybrid.  To combine or merge different elements or things.|例句：Scientists hybridized different types of wheat to create a more resistant strain. 科学家将不同类型的麦子杂交，以创造出更抗病的品种。

例句：The artist hybridized several artistic styles in his latest work.  这位艺术家在他的最新作品中融合了几种艺术风格。


近义词：crossbreed, interbreed, mix, blend, combine|反义词：purify, separate, segregate|动词
