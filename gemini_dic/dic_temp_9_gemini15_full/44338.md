crosier:/ˈkroʊʒər/| n. |牧杖；主教权杖|A crosier is a ceremonial staff carried by a bishop or abbot as a symbol of his office and authority.  It is typically a shepherd's crook, signifying the bishop's role as a shepherd of his flock.|The bishop entered the cathedral, his crosier held high.|近义词：pastoral staff, shepherd's crook|反义词：N/A
