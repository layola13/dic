cockneyfying: /ˈkɒkniˌfaɪɪŋ/ | v. |使具有伦敦东区口音的；用伦敦东区口音说| To cause (someone or something) to adopt or exhibit the characteristics of Cockney speech, especially the accent and rhyming slang.  It implies an alteration of speech patterns and vocabulary to mimic the Cockney dialect. | 例句：He was cockneyfying his pronunciation to fit in with the local crowd. 他为了融入当地人群而故意用伦敦东区口音说话。


例句：The comedian cockneyfied his act to appeal to a wider audience.  这位喜剧演员为了吸引更广泛的观众而用伦敦东区口音表演。


近义词：Cocknifying (less common variant), affecting a Cockney accent


反义词：Speaking in Received Pronunciation (RP), speaking in a standard accent


词性:动词
