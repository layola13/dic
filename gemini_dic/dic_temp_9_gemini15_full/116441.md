matching:/ˈmætʃɪŋ/ | n., v. |匹配；相配；相称 |The act of finding things that are similar or alike; to find something similar or alike.  |例句：The matching of socks is a tedious task. 袜子的配对是一项乏味的工作。

The colors in the room create a perfect matching.  房间里的颜色搭配完美。|近义词：corresponding, pairing, fitting |反义词：mismatching, unsuited, disparate |词性：名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The colors are matching perfectly. = 这些颜色完美地匹配。
He is matching the socks. = 他正在配对袜子。


一般过去时:
They matched the furniture to the walls. = 他们把家具和墙壁搭配起来。
She matched the fabric perfectly. = 她完美地搭配了面料。


现在完成时:
I have matched all the keys to their locks. = 我已经把所有的钥匙都和它们的锁配对好了。
They have matched the donations with the needs. = 他们已经将捐款与需求相匹配。


过去完成时:
He had matched the samples before the presentation. = 他在演示之前已经将样品匹配好了。
She had matched the colors before painting the room. =  她在粉刷房间之前已经搭配好颜色了。


将来时:
