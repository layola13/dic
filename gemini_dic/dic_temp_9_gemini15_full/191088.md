/ˈskæntɪst/ | adj. | 最少的；不足的；缺乏的 |  Meaning:  The word "scantest" is the superlative form of "scant," meaning the least or most insufficient. It describes something that is present in the smallest amount or degree, often implying a lack or deficiency.  | Example: The scantest amount of rain fell, leaving the crops parched.  最少的雨水落下，使农作物干涸。 The scantest evidence was presented in court. 法庭上提供的证据最少。 | 近义词:  minimal, meager, paltry, insufficient, inadequate | 反义词: abundant, plentiful, ample, copious
