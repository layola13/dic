pseudandry:/ˌsuːdoʊˈændri/| n. |假雄性，伪雄性|  The condition in some plant species where the female flower resembles a male flower in appearance. This mimicry can attract pollinators.  This term is primarily used in botany. |Example:  The pseudandry in this species of orchid helps it to attract more bees. =  这种兰花的假雄性特征有助于吸引更多的蜜蜂。


The pseudandry in this plant is a result of evolutionary adaptation. =  这种植物的假雄性特征是进化适应的结果。|近义词：female mimicry (in the context of plant reproduction)|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be a clearly differentiated female flower.|名词
