prepackaged:/ˌpriːˈpækɪdʒd/| adj. |预先包装的，预先打包的 |Already packaged before sale or use.  Prepackaged foods are convenient but often less healthy than fresh ingredients.|例句：The supermarket sells prepackaged salads.  超市出售预先包装好的沙拉。

例句：I prefer fresh vegetables to prepackaged ones. 我更喜欢新鲜蔬菜而不是预先包装的蔬菜。

近义词：ready-made, prepared, processed

反义词：fresh, unpackaged, homemade
