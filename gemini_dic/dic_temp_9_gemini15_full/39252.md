congregation:/ˌkɒŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən/| /ˌkɑːŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. |会众；聚集；集合 |A congregation is a group of people assembled for religious worship.  It can also refer to a gathering of people for any purpose. |例句：The congregation sang hymns during the service.  会众在仪式上唱赞美诗。

The large congregation filled the church to capacity.  大量的会众挤满了教堂。


近义词：assembly, gathering, meeting, audience | 反义词：dispersion, scattering
