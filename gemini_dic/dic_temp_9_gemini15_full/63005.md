enouncement:/əˈnaʊnsmənt/| n. |宣告；声明；公告 |The official enouncement of the new policy was met with mixed reactions. 官方对新政策的宣布引起了褒贬不一的反应。| announcement, proclamation, declaration | concealment, secrecy |名词


例句：

The president's enouncement of the new economic plan was widely reported in the media. 总统宣布新的经济计划的消息被媒体广泛报道。

The company's enouncement of its merger with a competitor caused a stir in the market. 公司宣布与竞争对手合并的消息在市场上引起了轰动。


近义词：

announcement, declaration, proclamation, statement


反义词：

concealment, secrecy, withholding
