convertors:/kənˈvɜːrtərz/| n. |转换器，变换器|A device or program that changes data or signals from one form to another.  For example, a voltage converter changes the voltage of electrical current.  A data converter changes the format of data. |例句：The AC/DC converter is essential for powering the device.  交流/直流转换器对于为设备供电至关重要。

The video converter improved the quality of the old footage.  视频转换器提高了旧镜头的质量。|近义词：transformer, transducer, encoder, decoder|反义词：N/A|名词

词源:
The word "converter" is derived from the verb "convert," which comes from the Old French word "convertir," ultimately from the Latin word "convertire," meaning "to turn around," "to change."  The suffix "-er" indicates an agent or instrument. Therefore, a "converter" is something that performs the action of converting.
