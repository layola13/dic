hyposulphite:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈsʌlfaɪt/| n. |次硫酸盐|A salt of hyposulphurous acid (now usually called thiosulphuric acid).  Hyposulphites are used as reducing agents in photography and other chemical processes.  They are also known as thiosulphates.|The photographer used hyposulphite to fix the developed film.|thiosulfate|sulfate|
