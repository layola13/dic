latitudinally:/ˌlætɪˈtjuːdɪnəli/ | adv. | 纬度地；按纬度地 | In a manner relating to latitude;  across or along lines of latitude.  |例句：The climate changes latitudinally.  气候随纬度变化。

例句：The study examined the latitudinal distribution of bird species. 这项研究考察了鸟类物种的纬度分布。


近义词：longitudinally (经度地), meridionally (子午线地)


反义词：longitudinally (经度地)
