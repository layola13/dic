quasi-compliantly: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ kəmˈplaɪəntli/ | adv. | 几乎顺从地；近乎服从地 |  Describes an action or behavior that shows a degree of compliance but not complete or full obedience. It suggests a reluctant or hesitant agreement. | 例句：He answered the questions quasi-compliantly, his eyes darting nervously. 他几乎顺从地回答了问题，眼睛紧张地四处乱瞟。 | 近义词：submissively, reluctantly, halfheartedly | 反义词：defiance, resistance, rebellion
