maradona:/ˌmærəˈdoʊnə/| n. |马拉多纳|Diego Maradona, a legendary Argentinian football player.  He is widely considered one of the greatest players of all time, renowned for his exceptional skill, dribbling ability, and goal-scoring prowess.  His career spanned several clubs and the Argentinian national team, culminating in a World Cup victory in 1986. | Example: Maradona's "Hand of God" goal remains one of the most controversial and memorable moments in football history.  马拉多纳的“上帝之手”进球仍然是足球史上最具争议和最令人难忘的时刻之一。|近义词:Pele (another legendary footballer) |反义词:None  (This is a proper noun, not easily comparable) |名词
