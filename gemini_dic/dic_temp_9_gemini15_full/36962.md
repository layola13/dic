colourway:/ˈkʌləweɪ/| n. |配色方案，颜色搭配|A colourway is a particular combination of colours used in a design, especially in textiles or footwear.  It refers to the specific arrangement and selection of colours used in a product, not just the colours themselves. |Example: The new sneaker release features a striking red and black colourway. 这款新运动鞋采用醒目的红黑配色。


The limited-edition trainers are available in several exciting colourways.  限量版运动鞋有多种令人兴奋的配色方案。|近义词：color scheme, color palette, combination|反义词：monochrome (in the context of a single color scheme)
