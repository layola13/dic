greasing:/ˈɡriːsɪŋ/| n. v.|涂油，润滑；贿赂|The act of applying grease to something, typically to lubricate it; also, the act of bribing someone.|例句：The greasing of the machine prevented it from seizing. 机器润滑避免了卡住。  The scandal involved the greasing of palms to secure the contract. 丑闻涉及为了获得合同而行贿。|近义词：lubrication, oiling, bribing|反义词：drying, neglecting


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He greases the car engine regularly. = 他定期给汽车发动机加油。
She greases the gears before starting the machine. = 她启动机器前给齿轮上油。


一般过去时:
They greased the wheels to make them turn smoothly. = 他们给轮子上油以使其平稳转动。
I greased the hinges to stop the squeaking. = 我给铰链上油以停止吱吱作响。


现在完成时:
He has greased the bike chain twice this month. = 这个月他已经给自行车链条上过两次油了。
She has greased the door hinges so they don't squeak anymore. = 她已经给门铰链上过油了，所以它们不再吱吱作响了。


过去完成时:
They had greased the tracks before the train arrived. = 火车到达之前，他们已经给轨道上过油了。
He had greased the lock to prevent it from freezing. = 他已经给锁上过油以防止它冻住。


将来时:
I will grease the car's chassis before the long trip. = 长途旅行之前我会给汽车底盘上油。
She will grease the moving parts of the machine tomorrow. = 她明天会给机器的活动部件上油。


虚拟语气:
If I greased the wheels, the cart would move more easily. = 如果我给轮子上油，手推车就会更容易移动。
If she had greased the pan, the eggs wouldn't have stuck. = 如果她给锅上油，鸡蛋就不会粘住了。


被动语态:

疑问句:
Have you greased the moving parts? = 你给活动部件上油了吗？
Did he grease the lock before leaving? = 他离开前给锁上油了吗？


否定句:
I didn't grease the hinges, that's why they squeak. = 我没有给铰链上油，这就是它们吱吱作响的原因。
She hadn't greased the pan, so the food stuck to it. = 她没有给锅上油，所以食物粘在了上面。
