menostasis:/ˌmɛnəˈsteɪsɪs/| n. |月经暂停|The cessation or suspension of menstruation.  This can be caused by various factors, including pregnancy, breastfeeding, hormonal imbalances, stress, or certain medical conditions.  The term often implies a temporary stoppage rather than permanent cessation of menstruation.|Example:  She experienced menostasis due to intense stress from work.|近义词：amenorrhea (a more formal and medical term)|反义词：menstruation
