groomless: /ˈɡruːmlɪs/ | adj. | 不修边幅的，邋遢的 | Unkempt; lacking in neatness and order.  It implies a lack of care in personal appearance. | Example: He looked groomless and dishevelled after his long journey. 他长途跋涉后看起来不修边幅，蓬头垢面。 | Near synonyms: unkempt, scruffy, dishevelled, slovenly, messy | Antonyms: groomed, neat, tidy, well-groomed |  词性：形容词
