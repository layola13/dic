mouse: /maʊs/ /mɑːs/ | n. |老鼠，鼠标 | A small rodent with a pointed snout, relatively large ears, and a long tail.  It is often considered a pest.  In computing, a mouse is a hand-held pointing device used to control the cursor on a computer screen. |例句：The cat chased the mouse.  猫追赶老鼠。|例句：I use a wireless mouse with my laptop. 我用无线鼠标连接我的笔记本电脑。|近义词：rodent, computer mouse |反义词：cat (predator), keyboard (alternative input device)
