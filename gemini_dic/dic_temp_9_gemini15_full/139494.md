obstruction:/əbˈstrʌkʃən/| n. |障碍；阻塞；妨碍|The act of obstructing or the state of being obstructed; something that obstructs.  It can refer to a physical blockage, a hindrance to progress, or an impediment to something. |例句：The fallen tree caused a major obstruction on the highway. 倒下的树木造成了高速公路上的严重阻塞。

The heavy snow created an obstruction to air travel. 大雪阻碍了航空旅行。

|近义词：impediment, hindrance, blockage, barrier, obstacle |反义词：clearance, opening, access, free passage |
