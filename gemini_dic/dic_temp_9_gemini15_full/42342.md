counteraction:/ˌkaʊntərˈækʃən/ | n. |对抗；反作用；抵消作用 |The act of counteracting; opposition or resistance to something.  It refers to an action taken to counteract or oppose something else.  |例句：The drug's side effects can be lessened by counteraction with another medication.  这种药物的副作用可以通过另一种药物的对抗作用来减轻。


例句：We need a plan of counteraction against their aggression.我们需要一个对抗他们侵略的计划。


近义词：opposition, countermeasure, antidote, neutralization | 反义词：cooperation, assistance, support | 词性：名词
