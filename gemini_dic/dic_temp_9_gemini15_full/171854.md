punishing: /ˈpʌnɪʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 惩罚性的；艰巨的；繁重的 | Extremely demanding or difficult; requiring great effort or stamina; severely critical or disapproving.  |例句：The hike was punishingly steep. 这段徒步旅行的路非常陡峭，令人筋疲力尽。 |近义词：arduous, strenuous, grueling, demanding, severe |反义词：easy, gentle, mild, lenient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The workout is punishing. = 这项训练非常艰苦。
This job is punishingly demanding. = 这份工作要求极高，令人难以承受。

一般过去时:
The exam was punishingly difficult. = 那次考试难到令人难以忍受。
The journey was punishingly long. = 这段旅程漫长而艰辛。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
