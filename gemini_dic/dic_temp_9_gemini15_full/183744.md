rescheduling: /ˌriːˈskedʒuːlɪŋ/ | n. |重新安排；改期 | The act of changing the planned time for an event or activity.  It involves moving something to a different time. |例句：The rescheduling of the meeting was necessary due to the CEO's illness.  会议由于首席执行官生病而不得不重新安排。

例句：The rescheduling of the flight caused significant inconvenience to many passengers. 航班的重新安排给许多乘客带来了很大的不便。


近义词：replanning, postponement, rearrangement | 反义词：scheduling, confirmation
