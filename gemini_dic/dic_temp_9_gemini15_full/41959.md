cosplayer:/ˈkɒspleɪər/| n. |角色扮演者 |A person who participates in cosplay, dressing up as a character from a video game, movie, book, or other fictional work.  | Example:  She's a talented cosplayer; her  Stormtrooper costume was incredibly realistic. 她是一位有才华的角色扮演者；她的冲锋队员服装非常逼真。 |近义词： role-player, costume player |反义词：None
