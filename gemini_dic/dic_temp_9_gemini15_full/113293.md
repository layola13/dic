luth:/luːθ/| n. |鲁特琴|A lute is a stringed musical instrument with a pear-shaped body and a long neck.  It was popular in Europe during the Renaissance and Baroque periods.|Example:  He played a beautiful melody on his lute. 他用鲁特琴演奏了一首优美的旋律。|近义词：guitar, oud|反义词：无
