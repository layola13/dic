mobilise:/ˈmoʊbəlaɪz/ /ˈməʊbəlaɪz/| vt.|动员；调动；使…活动；|To prepare and organize (something, especially people or resources) for a particular purpose, especially action in war.  To stimulate or encourage the activity of; to make active.|例句：The army was mobilised to quell the rebellion.  军队被调动来平息叛乱。

The government is trying to mobilise support for its new policies. 政府正试图争取对其新政策的支持。


近义词：muster, rally, activate, marshal, assemble|反义词：demobilize, disband, deactivate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company mobilises its resources efficiently. = 公司有效地调动其资源。
They mobilise their troops quickly. = 他们迅速动员军队。

一般过去时:
The country mobilised its entire population during the war. = 在战争期间，全国动员了全体人民。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
