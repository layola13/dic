The Ten Commandments are a prime example.|例句：The Ten Commandments are fundamental to the Jewish and Christian faiths. 十诫是犹太教和基督教信仰的基础。|例句：He issued a commandment that all soldiers should report for duty immediately. 他下令所有士兵立即报到。|近义词：command, precept, injunction, order|反义词：permission, allowance, freedom
