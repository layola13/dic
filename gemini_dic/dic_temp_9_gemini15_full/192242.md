screamed:/skriːmd/| v.|尖叫；大声喊叫|to utter a loud, sharp cry, especially one expressing pain, fear, or excitement.|例句：The child screamed when he saw the spider.  孩子看到蜘蛛时尖叫起来。|例句：She screamed with laughter. 她笑得尖叫起来。|近义词：shriek, yell, shout|反义词：whisper, murmur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He screams at the top of his lungs. = 他大声尖叫。
She screams when she is scared. = 她害怕的时候会尖叫。

一般过去时:
The baby screamed all night. = 婴儿整夜都在哭喊。
He screamed in terror. = 他恐惧地尖叫。

现在完成时:
She has screamed so loud that she lost her voice. = 她尖叫得太大声，嗓子都哑了。
The audience has screamed with delight. = 观众们高兴地尖叫。

过去完成时:
He had screamed before he fainted. = 他昏过去之前尖叫过。
She had screamed for help but no one heard her. = 她曾大声呼救，但没有人听到。

将来时:
I think she will scream when she sees the results. = 我想她看到结果会尖叫。
The crowd will scream when the band comes on stage. = 乐队上台时，人群会尖叫。

虚拟语气:
If I saw a ghost, I would scream. = 如果我看到鬼，我会尖叫。
I wish I hadn't screamed so loud. = 我真希望我当时没有叫那么大声。

被动语态:
(Screamed is rarely used in the passive voice.  A better phrasing would be needed to express a passive meaning.)  例如：A scream was heard in the night.  = 夜晚听到一声尖叫。
His name was screamed by the crowd. = 他的名字被人群喊叫出来。（较为不自然）

疑问句:
Did she scream? = 她尖叫了吗？
Why did he scream? = 他为什么尖叫？

否定句:
He didn't scream. = 他没有尖叫。
She didn't scream for help. = 她没有呼救。


PHRASE->
scream at someone = 对某人大声喊叫
scream with laughter = 笑得尖叫
scream for help = 呼救
scream blue murder = 大声喊叫（通常指抗议）
let out a scream = 发出一阵尖叫
