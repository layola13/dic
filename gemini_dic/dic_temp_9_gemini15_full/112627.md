low-key:/ˈloʊkiː/| adj.|低调的，不张扬的，不引人注目的|Low-key describes something that is understated, quiet, or not flashy.  It can refer to a person's personality, an event, or an object. |例句：He kept a low-key profile after the scandal. 他在丑闻后保持低调。

例句：The wedding was a low-key affair, with only close family and friends attending. 婚礼很低调，只有近亲和朋友参加。|近义词：subtle, understated, muted, inconspicuous|反义词：high-profile, flashy, ostentatious, flamboyant|词性:形容词
