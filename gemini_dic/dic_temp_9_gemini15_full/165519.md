premaintenance: /ˌpriːˈmeɪntənəns/ | n. | 预防性维护 |  Preventive maintenance; maintenance performed before a problem occurs to prevent future problems.  It aims to reduce the likelihood of equipment failure and extend its lifespan. | Example:  Regular premaintenance checks on the machinery helped avoid costly breakdowns.  定期对机器进行预防性维护有助于避免代价高昂的故障。 |近义词: preventive maintenance, preventative maintenance | 反义词: reactive maintenance, corrective maintenance | 名词
