mime:/maɪm/| n. vt.|哑剧；模仿；戏仿|A mime is a type of performance art that uses gestures and body movements to tell a story or express an idea, without using words.  It can also refer to the act of miming itself.|例句：The mime artist captivated the audience with his expressive movements.  哑剧演员以其富有表现力的动作吸引了观众。|近义词：pantomime, impersonation, imitation|反义词：speech, vocalization|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mimes a fight scene. = 他模仿打斗场面。
She mimes her feelings. = 她用肢体表达她的感受。

一般过去时:
The comedian mimed a drunkard. = 喜剧演员模仿了一个醉汉。

过去完成时:
