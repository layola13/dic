词源:
The word "hyperreal" is a combination of "hyper-" (meaning "above," "beyond," or "excessive") and "real."  It emerged in the late 20th century, reflecting the growing influence of technology and simulation on our perception of reality.  The concept is closely tied to the philosophical and artistic movement known as hyperreality.
