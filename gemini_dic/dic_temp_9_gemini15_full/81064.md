grainer:/ˈɡreɪnər/| n. |仿木纹饰面工匠；木材纹理模拟者；(旧指)谷物商|A grainer is a person who applies a graining technique to surfaces, typically wood, to imitate the appearance of a different type of wood or a specific wood grain pattern.  They create a realistic imitation of wood grain using paints and specialized tools.|The skilled grainer meticulously replicated the oak grain on the doors.|近义词：wood grainer, painter, finisher|反义词：无
