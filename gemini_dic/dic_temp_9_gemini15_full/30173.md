celebrity:/səˈlɛbrəti/ | n. | 名人，明星 | A person who is famous, especially in the entertainment business.  | Example:  She's a celebrity chef known for her innovative recipes. 她是一位以创新食谱而闻名的名厨。 |近义词：star, famous person, icon | 反义词：nobody, unknown person

词源:
The word "celebrity" comes from the Latin word "celeber," meaning "frequented," "celebrated," or "renowned."  It entered English in the 16th century, initially referring to a person who is well-known or famous.  The modern meaning, emphasizing fame in entertainment, developed later.
