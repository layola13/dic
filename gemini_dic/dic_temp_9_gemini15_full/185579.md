revelations:/ˌrevəˈleɪʃənz/ | n. |启示；揭露；泄露 | The act of revealing something previously unknown or hidden; a surprising and previously unknown fact that is made known.  |例句：The book contains shocking revelations about the politician's past. 这本书中包含了一些关于这位政治家过去的令人震惊的揭露。

例句：His revelations about the company's illegal activities led to a major investigation. 他关于公司非法活动的揭露导致了一项重大调查。


近义词： disclosures, discoveries, exposures | 反义词： concealments, secrets, mysteries | 词性：名词
