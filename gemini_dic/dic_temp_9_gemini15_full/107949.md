larynx:/ˈlærɪŋks/| n. |喉，喉头 |The larynx is the voice box, an organ in the neck involved in breathing, protecting the trachea, and producing sound.  It contains the vocal cords, which vibrate to create sound when air passes over them. |The doctor examined his larynx to determine the cause of his hoarseness.  医生检查了他的喉咙以确定他声音嘶哑的原因。|pharynx, voice box |mute, silent  (as antonyms relate to sound production)|名词
