influenza:/ˌɪnfluˈɛnzə/ | n. | 流行性感冒，流感 |  Influenza is a highly contagious viral infection of the respiratory system.  It is commonly known as the flu. Symptoms include fever, cough, sore throat, muscle aches, and fatigue. |例句：The influenza outbreak closed several schools.  流行性感冒的爆发导致几所学校关闭了。

例句：Many people get the influenza vaccine each year to protect themselves. 每年许多人接种流感疫苗来保护自己。

近义词：flu, grippe | 反义词：health, wellness | 词性：名词
