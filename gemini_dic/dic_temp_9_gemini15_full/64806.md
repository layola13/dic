esops:/ˈɛsɑːps/| n.|员工持股计划|Employee Stock Ownership Plan; a company-sponsored retirement plan that invests primarily in company stock.  It allows employees to purchase company stock, often at a discounted price, and provides tax advantages.|例句：Many companies offer ESOPs as a retirement benefit to their employees. 许多公司提供员工持股计划作为员工的退休福利。|例句：Participating in the company's ESOP is a great way to build wealth and retirement savings.参与公司员工持股计划是积累财富和退休储蓄的好方法。|近义词：employee stock ownership trust (ESOT)|反义词：无|名词
