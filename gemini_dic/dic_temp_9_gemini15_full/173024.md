quarreling:/ˈkwɒrəlɪŋ/| v. |争吵，吵架 |The act of arguing angrily.  It implies a disagreement involving heated exchanges and often personal attacks. |例句：The children were quarreling over a toy.孩子们为了一个玩具而争吵。

例句：The couple's constant quarreling was driving their neighbors crazy. 这对夫妻不断的争吵让他们的邻居快要疯了。

近义词：arguing, bickering, disputing, wrangling |反义词：agreeing, harmonizing


词源:  The word "quarreling" derives from the Old French word "quarreler," meaning "to quarrel." This, in turn, has roots in the Old Norse word "krorr," meaning a complaint or grievance, which also contributed to the English word "quarrel."


用法说明:

一般现在时:
They are always quarreling. = 他们总是吵架。
The neighbors are quarreling loudly. = 邻居们正在大声争吵。


一般过去时:
They quarreled about the inheritance. = 他们为遗产争吵。
We quarreled last night. = 我们昨晚吵架了。


现在完成时:
They have quarreled several times this week. = 他们本周已经吵了好几次架了。
We haven't quarreled in a long time. = 我们很久没吵架了。


过去完成时:
They had quarreled before I arrived. = 我到之前他们就已经吵过架了。
She had quarreled with her sister before the party. = 派对之前她就和她姐姐吵过架了。


将来时:
They will quarrel again, I'm sure. = 我确信他们还会再吵架。
I think they will quarrel over the money. = 我认为他们会为了钱而吵架。


虚拟语气:
If they quarreled, I would intervene. = 如果他们吵架，我会介入。
I wish they wouldn't quarrel so much. = 我希望他们不要吵那么多架。


被动语态:  The verb "quarrel" is rarely used in the passive voice because it is an intransitive verb (doesn't take a direct object).  However, a related phrase could be used:  The matter was constantly being quarreled over. = 这件事一直在被争论。


疑问句:
Are they quarreling again? = 他们又在吵架了吗？
Why are you quarreling? = 你为什么吵架？


否定句:
They are not quarreling. = 他们没有吵架。
We didn't quarrel. = 我们没有吵架。


PHRASE->
quarrel with someone = 与某人争吵
quarrel about something = 为某事争吵
quarrel over something = 为某事争吵
have a quarrel = 发生争吵
pick a quarrel = 找茬吵架
