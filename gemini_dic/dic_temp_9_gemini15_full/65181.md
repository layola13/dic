ethnologist:/ˌeθˈnɒlədʒɪst/|/ˈeθnɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |人类学家|A person who studies the origins and cultures of different races and peoples.|例句：The ethnologist spent years living with the tribe to understand their customs.  这位人类学家花了数年时间与部落一起生活，以了解他们的习俗。|例句：Many ethnologists believe that language shapes thought.许多人类学家认为语言塑造思想。|近义词：anthropologist, cultural anthropologist|反义词：N/A
