parfleche:/ˈpɑːrfleʃ/| n. |皮革包裹；皮袋|A parfleche is a type of container made from rawhide, traditionally used by Native American Plains tribes.  It is softened and stretched, sometimes painted or decorated, and used for storage. |The museum displayed several beautifully decorated parfleches from the Sioux tribe.|近义词：hide container, rawhide bag|反义词：无
