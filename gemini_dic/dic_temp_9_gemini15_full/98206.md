insectivora:/ˌɪnsekˈtɪvərə/| n. |食虫动物；食虫目|A group of mammals that primarily feed on insects.  The term is now largely obsolete in modern taxonomy, as the group is not considered monophyletic.  However, it is still sometimes used informally to refer to mammals that eat insects.|Example:  Shrews are classic examples of insectivora, though the term is outdated taxonomically.  食鼩鼱是食虫动物的典型例子，尽管这个术语在分类学上已经过时了。|近义词：insectivore (n.)|反义词：carnivora (animals that primarily eat meat),herbivore (animals that primarily eat plants)|
