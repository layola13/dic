forewarner:/fɔːrˈwɔːrnər/ | n. |预警者，预告者 | A person or thing that gives warning in advance.  |例句：The rumbling sound served as a forewarner of the approaching storm. 隆隆声预示着暴风雨即将来临。|例句：The canary is a forewarner of dangerous gases in a mine. 金丝雀是矿井中有毒气体的预警者。|近义词：precursor, harbinger, omen |反义词：后见之明（无直接反义词） |名词
