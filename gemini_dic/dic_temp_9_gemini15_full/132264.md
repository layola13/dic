nondeductible:/ˌnɑːn.dɪˈdʌktɪbl/ | adj. | 不可扣除的 |  Not able to be deducted, especially from taxable income.  This term is commonly used in accounting and tax contexts.  | Example:  The medical expenses were nondeductible because they exceeded the IRS limit.  这笔医疗费用不可扣除，因为超过了国税局的限额。 | 近义词：nonallowable, undeductible | 反义词：deductible
