plebiscitum:/ˌplɛbɪˈsaɪtəm/| n. |公民投票，全民投票 |A plebiscite is a vote by which the people of a country or region express an opinion for or against a proposal.  It is often used to decide on a significant political question, such as independence or constitutional changes.  |Example: The government held a plebiscite on whether to join the European Union.  政府就加入欧盟的问题进行了一次全民投票。The people were given the opportunity to voice their opinion on the matter via a plebiscite. 人民有机会通过公民投票表达他们对这件事的意见。|近义词：referendum, poll, vote |反义词：dictatorship, autocracy |名词
