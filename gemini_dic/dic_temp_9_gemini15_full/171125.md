psycho-aesthetic: /ˌsaɪkoʊɛsˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 心理美学的；心理审美的 | Relating to the interaction between psychological processes and aesthetic experience.  It explores how our minds and emotions shape our perception and appreciation of beauty and art.  | The psycho-aesthetic impact of the painting was undeniable; it evoked a profound emotional response in viewers. | 近义词: psychophysiological (in relation to art), psychological, emotional | 反义词:  N/A (no direct antonym, but the opposite would be a purely objective, emotionless approach to art)
