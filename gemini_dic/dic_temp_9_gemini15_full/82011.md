gripingly:/ˈɡrɪpɪŋli/| adv.|令人紧张地；令人焦虑地；令人难以忘怀地|In a way that is extremely exciting, intense, or memorable, often in a slightly negative way, causing anxiety or suspense.|例句：The film ended on a gripingly suspenseful note, leaving the audience breathless. 这部电影以令人紧张不安的结局告终，让观众屏住呼吸。


例句：The novel’s gripingly detailed description of the war left a lasting impression on me. 这部小说的战争描写细节令人印象深刻，给我留下了持久的印象。


近义词：thrillingly, excitingly, intensely, suspensefully


反义词：calmly, tranquilly, placidly


词性:副词
