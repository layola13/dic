cashed:/kæʃt/| v.|兑现；付清；(非正式)撞坏|To exchange a check or other negotiable instrument for cash.  To pay a debt or settle a bill. (Informal) To crash or severely damage something. |例句：I cashed my paycheck at the bank. 我在银行兑现了我的工资支票。 He cashed in his savings bonds to buy a car. 他兑现了他的储蓄债券来买一辆车。The car was cashed in the accident. 那辆车在事故中撞坏了。|近义词：redeemed, paid, wrecked, totaled|反义词：unpaid, outstanding


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He cashes his checks every Friday. = 他每个星期五都兑现支票。
She cashes in her loyalty points for discounts. = 她用积分兑换折扣。


一般过去时:
I cashed a check yesterday. = 我昨天兑现了一张支票。
They cashed their winning lottery ticket. = 他们兑现了他们中奖的彩票。


现在完成时:
He has cashed all his savings bonds. = 他已经兑现了他所有的储蓄债券。
She has cashed in her chips and retired. = 她已经放弃了所有，退休了。


过去完成时:
He had cashed the check before the bank closed. = 他在银行关门前兑现了支票。
They had cashed in their investment before the market crashed. = 他们在市场崩盘前兑现了他们的投资。


将来时:
I will cash my paycheck tomorrow. = 我明天将兑现我的工资支票。
She will cash in her frequent flyer miles for a free flight. = 她将用她的飞行常客里程兑换免费机票。


虚拟语气:
If I had cashed my check earlier, I wouldn't be short of money now. = 如果我早点兑现支票，我现在就不会缺钱了。
I wish I had cashed in my stocks before the crash. = 我真希望我在股市崩盘前兑现了我的股票。


被动语态:
The check was cashed at the bank. = 支票在银行兑现了。
His investment was cashed in to cover the debt. = 他的投资被兑现来偿还债务。


疑问句:
Did you cash your check? = 你兑现支票了吗？
Have you ever cashed a large sum of money? = 你曾经兑现过一大笔钱吗？


否定句:
I didn't cash my check today. = 我今天没有兑现支票。
She hasn't cashed in her lottery ticket yet. = 她还没兑现她的彩票。
