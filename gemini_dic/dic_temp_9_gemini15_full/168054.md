privatised: /ˈpraɪvətaɪzd/ | v. | 私有化 | To transfer (a business, industry, or service) from public to private ownership and control.  | The government privatised the railway system. 政府将铁路系统私有化了。 | nationalised, socialised | nationalised, socialised | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government privatises failing industries. = 政府将亏损的行业私有化。
They rarely privatise essential services. = 他们很少将基本服务私有化。

一般过去时:
The company was privatised in 1990. = 这家公司在1990年被私有化。
They privatised the water company last year. = 他们去年将水务公司私有化了。

现在完成时:
Many state-owned enterprises have been privatised. = 许多国有企业已被私有化。
The government has privatised several key sectors recently. = 政府最近已将几个关键部门私有化。

过去完成时:
By the time the deal was finalized, the company had already been privatised. = 到交易完成时，该公司已经被私有化了。
The workers had already protested before the government privatised the factory. = 在政府将工厂私有化之前，工人们就已经抗议了。


将来时:
They will privatise the postal service next year. = 他们明年将把邮政服务私有化。
The airport is likely to be privatised in the near future. = 机场很可能在不久的将来被私有化。

虚拟语气:
If they privatised the healthcare system, it would be disastrous. = 如果他们将医疗系统私有化，那将是灾难性的。
It would have been better if they hadn't privatised the education sector. = 如果他们没有将教育部门私有化就好了。

被动语态:
The utilities were privatised under the new legislation. = 公用事业在新的立法下被私有化了。
The process of privatisation is being closely monitored. = 私有化进程正在受到密切关注。

疑问句:
Has the energy sector been privatised? = 能源部门已经被私有化了吗？
Will the airline be privatised soon? = 航空公司很快会被私有化吗？

否定句:
The government did not privatise the healthcare system. = 政府没有将医疗系统私有化。
They haven't yet decided whether to privatise the rail network. = 他们还没有决定是否要将铁路网络私有化。
