numbskulls:/ˈnʌmskʌlz/| n.|蠢货，笨蛋|A numbskull is a stupid or foolish person.  The term is informal and often considered rude.|例句：He's such a numbskull; he can't even change a tire. 他真是个笨蛋，连换个轮胎都不会。

例句：Don't be such a numbskull!  Think before you act! 不要这么傻！行动前先想想！


近义词：fool, idiot, moron, dunce, blockhead|反义词：genius, intellect, mastermind|词性:名词
