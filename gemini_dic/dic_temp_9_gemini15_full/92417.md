hypogynous:/haɪˈpɒdʒɪnəs/| adj.| 子房上位花的；子房下位的|Having the sepals, petals, and stamens attached to the receptacle below the ovary.  This arrangement is characteristic of many flowering plants and is important in plant classification.|例句：The flower is hypogynous, with the ovary superior.|近义词：inferior, episepalous (for stamens and petals)|反义词：epigynous, perigynous
