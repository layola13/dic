disappointment's:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntmənts/| n.|失望的；令人失望的 |The possessive form of "disappointment," indicating belonging or relationship.  It refers to something that belongs to or is associated with disappointment. |例句：His disappointment's clear on his face. 他脸上清楚地写着失望。
例句：The project's disappointment's impact was significant.  该项目的失败影响深远。|近义词：frustration's, letdown's|反义词：satisfaction's, fulfillment's|名词


USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It shows possession or relationship.  Therefore, typical verb tenses don't apply.
