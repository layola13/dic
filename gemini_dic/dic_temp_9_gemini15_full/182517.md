rent-charger:/ˈrɛntˌtʃɑːrdʒər/| n. |租金收取者，包租公/婆| A rent-charger is someone who receives rent, especially a large landowner who derives most of their income from renting out property.  They may or may not be actively involved in managing the properties they own.|例句：The large estate was owned by a rent-charger who lived overseas and rarely visited his properties.  这位大地主是一个居住在海外的租金收取者，很少去拜访他的房产。|近义词：landlord, landlady, property owner|反义词：tenant, renter
