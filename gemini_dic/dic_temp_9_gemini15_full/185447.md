returner:/rɪˈtɜːrnər/| n. |归还者；返回者；回归者 |A person or thing that returns.  Someone who returns to a place or activity after a period of absence.  |例句：He's a seasoned returner to the competition. 他是比赛经验丰富的回归者。

例句：The package returner is responsible for processing returned goods.  包裹退货员负责处理退货。


近义词：returnee, comeback, revert |反义词：stayer, newcomer


词源:  Derived from the verb "return," ultimately from Old French *retourner (to turn back), from re- (again) + tourner (to turn).
