gulch:/ɡʌltʃ/| n.|峡谷，沟壑|A gulch is a narrow, steep-sided ravine, often with a stream running through it.  It is typically smaller than a canyon.|例句：The hikers carefully descended into the deep gulch.  徒步旅行者小心翼翼地走下了深深的峡谷。

例句：A rushing stream carved a deep gulch into the hillside. 一条奔腾的小溪在山坡上冲刷出一个深深的峡谷。|近义词：ravine, gully, canyon|反义词：plain, plateau
