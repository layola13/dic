sigmoid:/ˈsɪɡmɔɪd/ | adj. n. | S形曲线；乙状的 |  Describing an S-shaped curve, often used in mathematics, statistics, and machine learning to represent a function whose output is bounded between 0 and 1.  The sigmoid function is often used to model probability. | Example: The sigmoid function is commonly used in logistic regression.  S形函数通常用于逻辑回归。|近义词：S-shaped, curvilinear | 反义词：linear, straight
