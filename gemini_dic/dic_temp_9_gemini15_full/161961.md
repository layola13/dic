pranker:/ˈpræŋkər/| n. |恶作剧者，开玩笑的人 | A person who plays pranks.  Someone who enjoys playing tricks or jokes on others, often harmless ones. | Example: He's a notorious pranker, always pulling silly jokes on his friends. 他是个臭名昭著的恶作剧者，总是捉弄他的朋友。|近义词：trickster, joker, jester |反义词：serious person, straight-laced individual
