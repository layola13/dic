initializing:/ɪˈnɪʃəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 初始化，启动 |  The act of setting up or starting something, often a program or system, by assigning initial values or configurations.  It involves preparing something for use or operation. | 例句：The computer is initializing the operating system. = 计算机正在初始化操作系统。

The system is initializing; please wait. = 系统正在初始化；请稍候。


近义词：starting, booting, setting up | 反义词：terminating, shutting down, closing
