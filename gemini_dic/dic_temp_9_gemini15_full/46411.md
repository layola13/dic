daftest:/ˈdæfɪst/| adj.|最愚蠢的，最荒唐的 | Most foolish or absurd.  It is the superlative form of daft. |例句：That's the daftest idea I've ever heard.  那是我听过的最愚蠢的主意。 |例句：He made the daftest remark during the meeting. 他在会议上说了最荒唐的话。|近义词： silliest, most foolish, most absurd, most ridiculous |反义词： wisest, most sensible, most intelligent
