overpass:/ˈoʊvərˌpæs/| n. |天桥；立交桥 |A structure that carries a road, railroad, etc., over another road, railroad, etc., or over a valley.  |例句：We crossed the highway via the overpass. 我们通过天桥穿过高速公路。
例句：The new overpass significantly reduced traffic congestion. 新建的天桥大大缓解了交通拥堵。|近义词：flyover, bridge, viaduct |反义词：underpass
