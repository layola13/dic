haberdasher:/ˈhæbədæʃər/| n. |男装帽饰品经销商；杂货商|A haberdasher is a person who sells men's clothing and accessories, such as shirts, ties, and socks.  In the past, haberdashers also sold a wider variety of goods. |The haberdasher offered a wide selection of ties to match any suit.  这家男装帽饰品经销商提供各种各样的领带以搭配任何西装。| clothier, tailor |  (无明确反义词，可考虑：general store (泛指杂货店，与之相对的是专业性)) |名词
