overbrags:/ˈoʊvərbræɡz/| v.|吹牛，过分夸耀|To boast excessively or exaggerate one's accomplishments or possessions. 例句：She constantly overbrags about her children's achievements, ignoring their shortcomings. 她不断夸耀孩子们的成就，忽略了他们的缺点。|近义词：boast, brag, exaggerate, show off|反义词：understate, downplay, minimize
