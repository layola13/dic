dichromic:/ˌdaɪˈkroʊmɪk/ | adj. | 双色的；二色的 | Having two colors; exhibiting two different colors depending on the angle of observation.  | Example: The dichromic mineral showed a green color from one angle and a red color from another.  这种双色矿物从一个角度看是绿色，从另一个角度看是红色。|近义词：bicolor, two-toned | 反义词：monochromatic, unicolorous
