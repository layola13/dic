intervertebral: /ˌɪntərˈvɜːrtɪbrəl/ | adj. |椎间的，椎骨间的| Relating to the spaces between vertebrae.  The term describes the structures, spaces, or processes located between individual vertebrae in the spinal column. |例句：Intervertebral discs act as cushions between the vertebrae, absorbing shock and allowing for flexibility. 椎间盘作为椎骨之间的缓冲垫，吸收冲击并提供灵活性。

The intervertebral foramina allow the spinal nerves to exit the vertebral column. 椎间孔允许脊神经离开椎骨。


近义词：interspinal (less common) | 反义词：intravertebral (within a vertebra) | 词性：adjective

词源:

* **inter-**:  Latin prefix meaning "between" or "among"
* **vertebra**: Latin word meaning "joint of the spine"
* **-al**:  Suffix forming adjectives, meaning "relating to" or "pertaining to"

Therefore, "intervertebral" literally means "between the vertebrae."
