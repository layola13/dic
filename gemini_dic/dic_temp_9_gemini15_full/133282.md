nonexpansively:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspænɪvli/ | adj. | 不扩张地；非扩张性地 | Not expanding or tending to expand; not increasing in size, scope, or intensity.  The term often appears in mathematical contexts, particularly in fixed-point theory and related areas. |

例句：

The algorithm converges nonexpansively to the solution.  = 该算法不扩张地收敛到解。

The company maintained a nonexpansively growth strategy in the face of market fluctuations. = 面对市场波动，该公司保持了不扩张的增长战略。


近义词：constantly, steadily,  (in mathematical context) non-expansive


反义词：expansively, increasingly


词性：形容词
