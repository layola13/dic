counterintuitive: /ˌkaʊntərɪnˈtuːɪtɪv/ | adj. | 反直觉的，违反常理的 | Contrary to intuition or common sense; seemingly impossible or unlikely.  |  例句：The solution to the problem was counterintuitive; it involved doing the opposite of what seemed logical.  解决方案是反直觉的；它涉及做与逻辑似乎相反的事情。

例句：It's counterintuitive that exercising can actually help you lose weight. 锻炼实际上可以帮助你减肥，这听起来很反直觉。


近义词： paradoxical, illogical, unexpected, surprising, anomalous | 反义词：intuitive, logical, expected, predictable

词源:
The word "counterintuitive" is a compound word.  "Counter" means "opposite" or "against," and "intuitive" refers to knowledge gained through instinct or feeling.  Therefore, "counterintuitive" literally means "against intuition."
