co-determination:/ˌkoʊdɪˌtɜːrmɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |共同决定；共同管理；参与管理|The process by which employees participate in the decision-making of their company, typically through works councils or similar structures.  It's a system aimed at balancing the interests of management and workers.|Example:  German companies often practice co-determination, giving employees a voice in corporate governance. 德国公司经常实行共同决定制，让员工在公司治理中拥有发言权。|近义词：joint decision-making, participatory management, employee involvement|反义词：autocratic management, unilateral decision-making|名词
