hobnob:/ˈhɒbnɒb/| v.|闲聊，随意交谈|To chat in a friendly and informal way, often while socializing.|例句：They were hobnobbing with celebrities at the party.他们在派对上与名人们闲聊。  例句：We hobnobbed over drinks after work. 我们下班后边喝边聊天。|近义词：chat, socialize, mingle|反义词：ignore, shun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hobnob with their neighbors every weekend. = 他们每个周末都和邻居们闲聊。
We hobnob at the pub after work. = 我们下班后在酒吧里闲聊。

一般过去时:
They hobnobbed at the conference. = 他们在会议上互相闲聊。
We hobnobbed with some interesting people at the party. = 我们在派对上与一些有趣的人闲聊。

现在完成时:
They have hobnobbed with many important people in their careers. = 他们在职业生涯中与许多重要人士有过交谈。
I have hobnobbed with several authors at literary events. = 我在文学活动中与几位作家有过交谈。

过去完成时:
They had hobnobbed for hours before the meeting began. = 会议开始前他们已经闲聊了几个小时。
We had hobnobbed with the guests before the official ceremony started. = 在正式仪式开始之前，我们已经和宾客们聊过天了。

将来时:
They will hobnob at the upcoming festival. = 他们将在即将到来的节日上闲聊。
We will hobnob with our friends at the restaurant tomorrow night. = 明晚我们将在餐厅与朋友们闲聊。

虚拟语气:
If they had hobnobbed more, they might have made more connections. = 如果他们当时多聊聊，可能会建立更多联系。
I wish I had hobnobbed with that famous actor. = 我真希望当时能和那位著名的演员聊聊天。

被动语态:  (Hobnob is rarely used in the passive voice)
It's rare that people are hobnobbed with by strangers. = 陌生人很少被别人主动搭讪闲聊。
The guests were mostly hobnobbing amongst themselves. = 客人们大多都在自己人之间闲聊。


疑问句:
Do they often hobnob with their colleagues? = 他们经常和同事们闲聊吗？
Will you hobnob with the new recruits at the welcome party? = 你会在欢迎会上和新员工们闲聊吗？

否定句:
They did not hobnob with anyone at the event. = 他们在活动中没有和任何人闲聊。
We don't usually hobnob with strangers. = 我们通常不和陌生人闲聊。


PHRASE->

hobnob in the bar = 在酒吧里闲聊
hobnob and network = 闲聊和建立人脉
