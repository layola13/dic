rhetorically:/rɪˈtɒrɪkli/|/ædriˈtɒrɪkli/| adv. |修辞地；从修辞角度；在修辞上|In a rhetorical manner; using or relating to rhetoric.  It often implies that a question or statement is not meant to be answered literally, but rather to make a point or create an effect.|例句：He rhetorically asked if we really believed the politician's promises. 他反问我们是否真的相信这位政治家的承诺。(暗示他不相信)。

例句：The professor rhetorically questioned the validity of the argument. 教授从修辞角度质疑了论证的有效性。(意在指出论证的缺陷)。


近义词：figuratively, stylistically, eloquently

反义词：literally, plainly, directly

词性:副词
