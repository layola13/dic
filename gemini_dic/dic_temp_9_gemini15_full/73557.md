forestallers:/fɔːrˈstɔːlərz/ | n. |囤积居奇者；预先阻止者 |Those who buy up goods in large quantities to create artificial scarcity and raise prices.  They act to prevent something from happening. |Example: The government cracked down on forestallers who were driving up the price of grain. 政府打击了哄抬粮价的囤积居奇者。|近义词：monopolists, speculators, hoarders |反义词：providers, benefactors
