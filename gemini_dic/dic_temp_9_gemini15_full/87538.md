heterogeny:/ˌhɛtərəˈdʒɛni/ | n. |异质性，多样性，不均匀性 | The state or quality of being heterogeneous; diversity in character or content.  Heterogeny is often used in scientific contexts to describe a lack of uniformity within a system or sample. For example, a soil sample might exhibit heterogeny in its mineral composition.  | Example: The heterogeny of the student body enriched the classroom discussions.  学生群体的多样性丰富了课堂讨论。 |近义词：diversity, variety, heterogeneity |反义词：homogeneity, uniformity |名词
