crewnecks:/ˈkruːneks/ | n. |圆领衫，高领衫 | A crewneck is a type of shirt or sweater with a round neckline.  The term refers to the style of the neckline, not necessarily the material or fit of the garment. | Example: He wore a simple crewneck sweater over his shirt. 他穿着一件简单的圆领毛衣在衬衫外面。  She purchased a stylish crewneck t-shirt in a bold color. 她买了一件颜色鲜艳的时尚圆领T恤。|近义词：round-neck, turtleneck (if high enough) |反义词：V-neck, scoop neck
