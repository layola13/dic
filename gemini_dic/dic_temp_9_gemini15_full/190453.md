saskatoons:/ˌsæskəˈtuːnz/ | n. |萨斯卡通莓|The berries of the *Amelanchier alnifolia*, a shrub native to North America, commonly used in jams, pies, and other desserts.  They are small, dark purple or blue-black, and sweet-tart in flavor.|例句：Saskatoons are a delicious and nutritious berry often used in pies and jams.萨斯卡通莓是一种美味且营养丰富的浆果，常用于制作馅饼和果酱。|例句：We picked a basket full of saskatoons during our hike in the mountains.我们在山区徒步旅行时采摘了一篮萨斯卡通莓。|近义词：serviceberry, juneberry |反义词：无
