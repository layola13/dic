busters:/ˈbʌstərz/| n.|破坏者，摧毁者，打击者|Someone or something that destroys or defeats something else.  The term often implies a forceful or overwhelming nature.|例句：Ghostbusters are experts at eliminating ghosts.  捉鬼敢死队是消灭鬼魂的专家。

例句：The economic busters caused many businesses to fail.  经济危机导致许多企业倒闭。


近义词：destroyers, demolishers, vanquishers|反义词：creators, builders, preservers|名词
