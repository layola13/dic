ceased:/siːst/| v.|停止，终止，结束|To stop; bring or come to an end.|The rain ceased and the sun came out. 雨停了，太阳出来了。 The factory ceased production. 工厂停止生产了。|continued, resumed|started, began|动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ceases to complain. 他不再抱怨了。
The machine ceases functioning. 机器停止运转了。

一般过去时:
The music ceased abruptly. 音乐突然停止了。
She ceased her work to answer the phone. 她停止工作接电话。

现在完成时:
The fighting has ceased. 战斗已经停止了。
He has ceased to care. 他已经不再关心了。


过去完成时:
The rain had ceased before we arrived. 我们到达之前雨已经停了。
They had ceased their activities by nightfall. 到夜幕降临的时候，他们已经停止了活动。

将来时:
The meeting will cease at 5 pm. 会议将在下午五点结束。
The pain will cease once the medication takes effect. 药效一旦发挥作用，疼痛就会停止。

虚拟语气:
If only the fighting would cease! 但愿战斗能停止！
I wish the noise would cease. 我希望噪音能停止。

被动语态:
Production was ceased due to the strike. 由于罢工，生产被停止了。
The project has been ceased. 项目已经终止了。

疑问句:
Has the bleeding ceased? 出血停止了吗？
When did the activity cease? 活动什么时候停止的？

否定句:
The pain didn't cease. 疼痛没有停止。
The rain hasn't ceased yet. 雨还没有停。
