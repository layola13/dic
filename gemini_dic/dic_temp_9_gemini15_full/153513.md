pensiveness: /ˈpɛnsɪvnəs/ | n. |沉思；忧思；冥想 | A state of being pensive; thoughtful reflection, often with a hint of sadness or melancholy.  It implies a deep and often quiet contemplation, often involving introspection and a degree of sadness or seriousness. | 例句：The pensiveness in her eyes hinted at a troubled past.  她眼中的忧思暗示了她不平静的过去。


例句：His pensiveness was palpable; he sat staring out the window for hours. 他的沉思是显而易见的；他坐在那里几个小时盯着窗外。


近义词：thoughtfulness, reflectiveness, contemplation, meditation, musing, brooding


反义词：lightheartedness, cheerfulness, gaiety, joviality


词源：源自于动词"pensive"，其词根追溯到拉丁语的"pendere"，意思是"悬挂"或"悬而未决"。引申意义为"悬念"、"沉思"。  The sense of "hanging" evolved to refer to something hanging in the mind, an unresolved thought or feeling, thus leading to the meaning of thoughtful contemplation.
