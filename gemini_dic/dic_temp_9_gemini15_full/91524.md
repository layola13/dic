hyoscine:/ˈhaɪəʊsiːn/ | n. |东莨菪碱|Hyoscine, also known as scopolamine, is a tropane alkaloid with anticholinergic properties. It is used medically to treat motion sickness, postoperative nausea and vomiting, and other conditions.  | Example: The doctor prescribed hyoscine patches to alleviate her severe motion sickness during the cruise.  医生开了东莨菪碱贴剂来减轻她在邮轮旅行中严重的晕船。 |近义词:scopolamine|反义词:None specifically, but drugs with opposite cholinergic effects could be considered.|名词
