heronry:/ˈhɛrənri/| n. |苍鹭群巢；苍鹭栖息地 |A heronry is a large group of heron nests, usually located in trees or other elevated places.  It is a colony of herons. |例句：We spotted a large heronry in the tall cypress trees along the riverbank.  我们在河边高高的柏树上发现了一个大型的苍鹭群巢。

例句：The heronry was bustling with activity as the birds prepared for the breeding season.  在鸟类准备繁殖季节的时候，苍鹭群巢里一片繁忙景象。


近义词： heron colony, bird rookery |反义词：无


词源:  The word "heronry" is derived from the word "heron" (a type of wading bird) + the suffix "-ry," which denotes a place or collection of something.  Therefore, it literally means "a place where herons are."
