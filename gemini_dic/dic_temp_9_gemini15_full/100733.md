intransient: /ɪnˈtrænzɪənt/ | adj. |短暂的，瞬时的，非永久的 | Not lasting long; temporary.  |例句：The pain was transient, lasting only a few minutes.  疼痛是短暂的，只持续了几分钟。

例句：His fame proved to be transient; he was quickly forgotten. 他的名声证明是短暂的；他很快就被遗忘了。


近义词：fleeting, ephemeral, momentary, temporary | 反义词：permanent, lasting, enduring, perpetual


词性：形容词
