chattels:/ˈtʃætəlz/ | n. |动产，个人财产 | Movable possessions or goods; personal property as opposed to real estate.  Chattels are items of personal property that can be moved from one place to another.  This contrasts with real property, which is land and anything permanently attached to it. |例句：He left all his chattels to his daughter in his will.  他遗嘱中将所有动产留给了女儿。

The antique furniture was among the most valuable chattels in the estate.  这些古董家具是遗产中价值最高的动产之一。|近义词：goods, effects, personal property |反义词：real estate, realty, immovable property
