rucksacked:/ˈrʌksækt/ | adj. | 背着背包的；携带背包的 | Carrying a rucksack or backpacks; equipped with or having a rucksack.  | Example: The rucksacked hikers ascended the mountain.  背着背包的远足者攀登了这座山。|近义词：backpack-laden, pack-carrying | 反义词：unburdened, unloaded
