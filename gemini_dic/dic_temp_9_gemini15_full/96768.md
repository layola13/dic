inequality:/ɪnɪˈkwɒləti/ /ˌɪnɪˈkwɑːləti/ | n. | 不平等，不均等 | The state of being unequal; difference in size, degree, circumstances, etc.; lack of equality.  It often refers to unfairness or disparity in opportunities, resources, or treatment among individuals or groups. | 例句：The widening income inequality is a major social problem.  日益扩大的收入不平等是一个主要的社会问题。

The gender inequality in the workplace needs to be addressed. 工作场所的性别不平等需要解决。


近义词：disparity, unevenness, imbalance, inequity | 反义词：equality, evenness, balance, equity | 词性：名词
