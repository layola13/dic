noncompetitiveness:/ˌnɒnkɒmpˈɛtɪtnəs/| n. |非竞争力，缺乏竞争力|Lack of competitiveness; inability to compete successfully.|The company's noncompetitiveness in the market led to its eventual downfall.  这家公司在市场上的缺乏竞争力最终导致了它的倒闭。|competitiveness, uncompetitiveness|competitiveness|名词

一般现在时:
The company's noncompetitiveness is a major concern. = 该公司的非竞争力是一个主要问题。
Their noncompetitiveness shows in their low market share. = 他们缺乏竞争力体现在他们低的市场份额上。

一般过去时:
Last year's noncompetitiveness hurt the company's profits. = 去年缺乏竞争力损害了公司的利润。
The noncompetitiveness of the product was evident in its poor sales. = 该产品的缺乏竞争力在其糟糕的销售额中显而易见。

现在完成时:
Years of noncompetitiveness have weakened the industry. = 多年的缺乏竞争力已经削弱了这个行业。
Their noncompetitiveness has resulted in significant job losses. = 他们的缺乏竞争力导致了大量的失业。

过去完成时:
Their noncompetitiveness had already been apparent before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们的缺乏竞争力就已经很明显了。
The company's poor performance was a result of years of noncompetitiveness that had gone unaddressed. = 公司业绩不佳是多年来未解决的缺乏竞争力造成的。

将来时:
Continued noncompetitiveness will likely lead to bankruptcy. = 持续的缺乏竞争力可能会导致破产。
Without improvement, their noncompetitiveness will continue to hinder growth. = 如果没有改进，他们的缺乏竞争力将继续阻碍增长。

虚拟语气:
If their noncompetitiveness were addressed, they might be more successful. = 如果解决了他们的缺乏竞争力问题，他们可能会更成功。
I wish their noncompetitiveness wasn't such a significant problem. = 我希望他们的缺乏竞争力不是一个如此严重的问题。
