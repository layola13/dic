gauffering:/ˈɡɔːfərɪŋ/| n. |压花；压褶；(糕点等的)花边装饰|The decorative process of creating a textured surface, often with raised patterns, by pressing or embossing.  This is commonly used in textiles, paper, and confectionery.|例句：The gauffering on the wedding cake was exquisite. 婚礼蛋糕上的压花装饰精美绝伦。|例句：She admired the delicate gauffering on the antique tablecloth. 她欣赏古董桌布上精美的压花。|近义词：embossing, embossment, pleating, crimping|反义词：smooth, plain|名词
