mateys:/ˈmeɪtiz/ | n. |伙伴们，朋友们 | A term of address used to refer to one's companions, especially among sailors or pirates. It's a plural form, expressing familiarity and camaraderie.  | Example: "Ahoy, mateys! Let's hoist the sails and set course for adventure!"  伙伴们！让我们扬帆起航，去冒险吧！ |近义词：friends, companions, buddies, pals |反义词：enemies, adversaries |名词
