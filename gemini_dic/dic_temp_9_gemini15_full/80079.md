goat:/ɡoʊt/ /ɡəʊt/| n.|山羊|A goat is a horned ruminant mammal typically kept for its milk or meat.|例句：We saw a herd of goats grazing on the hillside. 我们看到一群山羊在山坡上吃草。|近义词：sheep, ibex|反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He keeps goats on his farm. = 他在农场养山羊。
The goat eats grass. = 山羊吃草。

一般过去时:
She milked the goat yesterday. = 她昨天挤了山羊奶。
The farmer sold his goats last week. = 农民上周卖了他的山羊。

现在完成时:
I have never seen such a big goat. = 我从未见过这么大的山羊。
They have raised goats for generations. = 他们世世代代养山羊。

过去完成时:
The goat had already eaten all the leaves before I arrived. = 我到达之前，山羊已经把所有的叶子都吃光了。
We had never owned a goat before. = 我们以前从未养过山羊。

将来时:
We will buy a goat next month. = 我们下个月会买一只山羊。
He will shear the goat in the spring. = 他将在春天给山羊剪毛。

虚拟语气:
If I had a goat, I would make cheese. = 如果我有一只山羊，我会做奶酪。
I wish I had a goat to keep as a pet. = 我真希望有一只山羊当宠物养。

被动语态:
The goat was milked by the farmer. = 山羊被农民挤了奶。
The goats were sold at the market. = 山羊在市场上卖掉了。

疑问句:
Do you have any goats? = 你有山羊吗？
Is that a goat or a sheep? = 那是山羊还是绵羊？

否定句:
We don't have any goats. = 我们没有山羊。
He didn't milk the goat this morning. = 他今天早上没有挤山羊奶。
