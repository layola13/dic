handed-off: /ˈhændɪd ɔːf/ | v. |移交；交给；让...接管|  To pass something to someone else; to transfer responsibility or control. |例句：The project was handed off to the new team.  项目移交给新的团队了。|例句：He handed off the baton to his teammate. 他把接力棒交给了队友。|近义词：transfer, delegate, relinquish, pass on |反义词：retain, keep, hold onto


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hands off the documents every Friday. = 他每周五都移交文件。
She hands off the responsibility to her assistant. = 她把责任移交给她的助理。


一般过去时:
The manager handed off the project last week. = 经理上周移交了项目。
They handed off the children to their grandparents. = 他们把孩子们交给祖父母照顾。


现在完成时:
We have handed off all the urgent tasks. = 我们已经移交了所有紧急的任务。
She has handed off the reins of the company to her son. = 她已经把公司的管理权移交给她的儿子了。


过去完成时:
They had handed off the case before the new lawyer arrived. = 新律师到来之前，他们已经移交了案件。
The previous team had handed off the data before leaving. = 之前的团队在离开之前已经移交了数据。


将来时:
He will hand off the report tomorrow morning. = 他明天早上将移交报告。
They will hand off the responsibility to the next generation. = 他们将把责任移交给下一代。


虚拟语气:
If he handed off the project earlier, we would have finished on time. = 如果他早点移交项目，我们就能按时完成了。
I wish she had handed off the burden to someone else. = 我希望她早点把负担交给别人。


被动语态:
The report was handed off to the committee yesterday. = 报告昨天被移交给了委员会。
The case was handed off to a different department. = 案件被移交给了不同的部门。


疑问句:
When will the project be handed off? = 项目什么时候移交？
Who will hand off the package? = 谁来移交包裹？


否定句:
He didn't hand off the documents on time. = 他没有按时移交文件。
They haven't handed off the responsibility yet. = 他们还没有移交责任。


PHRASE->

hand off the responsibility = 移交责任
