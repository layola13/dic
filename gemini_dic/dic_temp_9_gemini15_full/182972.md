replastered:/ˌriːˈplæstərd/| v. |重新粉刷，重新抹灰|To plaster again; to apply a new layer of plaster to a surface.|例句：The walls were replastered after the leak.  墙壁在漏水后重新粉刷了。

例句：The damaged section of the ceiling needed to be replastered. 天花板受损的部分需要重新抹灰。


近义词：recoat, refinish | 反义词：strip, remove plaster


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They replaster the walls every five years. = 他们每五年粉刷一次墙壁。
He replasters the damaged areas of the house. = 他粉刷房屋受损的区域。

一般过去时:
She replastered the bathroom after the flood. = 洪水过后，她重新粉刷了浴室。
The builders replastered the entire room. = 建筑工人重新粉刷了整个房间。

现在完成时:
They have replastered the kitchen this week. = 他们本周已经粉刷了厨房。
We have replastered the damaged walls. = 我们已经粉刷了受损的墙壁。

过去完成时:
The landlord had replastered the apartment before we moved in. = 我们搬进来之前，房东已经粉刷了公寓。
They had replastered the walls before painting them. = 他们粉刷墙壁之前已经重新抹灰了。

将来时:
We will replaster the damaged areas next week. = 我们下周将粉刷受损的区域。
The contractor will replaster the entire building. = 承包商将粉刷整栋建筑物。

虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would replaster the walls myself. = 如果我有时间，我会自己粉刷墙壁。
It would be best if they replastered the ceiling before painting it. = 最好他们在粉刷天花板之前重新抹灰。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
