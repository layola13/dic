scorningly:/ˈskɔːrnɪŋli/| adv. |轻蔑地；嘲讽地；不屑地 |expressing or showing contempt or derision.  It implies a feeling of superiority and disdain towards the subject being scorned. |He looked at her scornfully. 他轻蔑地看着她。She dismissed his offer scornfully. 她轻蔑地拒绝了他的提议。|contemptuously, disdainfully, derisively, mockingly|respectfully, admiringly, approvingly
