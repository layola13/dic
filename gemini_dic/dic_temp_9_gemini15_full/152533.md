pbr:/ˌpiːbiːˈɑːr/|/ˌpiːbiːˈɑːr/|abbr.|基于物理渲染|Physically Based Rendering; a rendering technique that aims to simulate the physical behavior of light and materials.|PBR is a rendering technique that uses physically accurate models to simulate the interaction of light with surfaces.
