once-over:/ˈwʌnsˈoʊvər/| n.|粗略一看，匆匆一看|A quick and superficial examination or inspection.|例句：He gave the room a once-over before leaving. 他离开前匆匆看了一下房间。|例句：She gave her outfit a quick once-over in the mirror.她在镜子前匆匆打量了一下自己的衣服。|近义词：glance, survey, scan|反义词：detailed examination, thorough inspection
