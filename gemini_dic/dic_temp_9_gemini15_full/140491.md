olde:/oʊld/| adj.|古老的，旧的|Old is used to describe something that has existed for a long time or is no longer new.  It can refer to age, style, or condition. |例句：That's an olde house. = 那是一座古老的房子。|例句：He has an olde car. = 他有一辆旧车。|近义词：ancient, antique, aged, venerable|反义词：new, modern, recent
