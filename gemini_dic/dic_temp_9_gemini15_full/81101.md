graminivorous:/ˌɡræmɪˈnɪvərəs/| adj. | 食草的，以禾本科植物为食的 |  An adjective describing animals whose diet consists primarily of grasses and other herbaceous plants of the Gramineae (grass) family.  | Example:  Many graminivorous animals, such as cows and sheep, are essential to agricultural economies. 许多食草动物，例如牛和羊，对农业经济至关重要。 |近义词：herbivorous, grass-eating | 反义词：carnivorous
