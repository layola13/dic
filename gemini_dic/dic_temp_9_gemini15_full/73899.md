forsakes:/fərˈseɪks/| vt.|抛弃；放弃；遗弃 | to abandon or leave completely and finally; to give up or renounce |例句：He forsook his family and went to live abroad. 他抛弃了他的家人，到国外去住了。

例句：She forsook her career to raise her children. 她放弃了事业来抚养孩子。


近义词：abandon, desert, relinquish, leave, quit |反义词：retain, keep, cherish, embrace


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He forsakes his principles. 他放弃了他的原则。
She forsakes her dreams. 她放弃了她的梦想。


一般过去时:
He forsook his post. 他放弃了他的职务。
She forsook her friends. 她抛弃了她的朋友们。


现在完成时:
He has forsaken his old ways. 他已经放弃了他过去的生活方式。
She has forsaken her faith. 她已经放弃了她的信仰。


过去完成时:
He had forsaken his family before he left. 他离开之前已经抛弃了他的家人。
She had forsaken her hopes by then. 到那时她已经放弃了她的希望。


将来时:
He will forsake his job. 他将会放弃他的工作。
She will forsake her comfortable life. 她将会放弃她舒适的生活。


虚拟语气:
If he forsook his pride, he might be happier. 如果他放下他的骄傲，他可能会更快乐。
If she forsook her ambition, she would have more time. 如果她放弃她的野心，她就会有更多的时间。


被动语态:
His principles were forsaken. 他的原则被放弃了。
Her dreams were forsaken by her. 她的梦想被她自己放弃了。


疑问句:
Did he forsake his family? 他抛弃了他的家人吗？
Will she forsake her career? 她会放弃她的事业吗？


否定句:
He did not forsake his friends. 他没有抛弃他的朋友们。
She will not forsake her beliefs. 她不会放弃她的信仰。
