kangaroos:/ˌkæŋɡəˈruːz/| n.|袋鼠|A kangaroo is a marsupial mammal found in Australia.  They are known for their powerful legs, which allow them to hop at great speeds, and their pouch, in which they carry their young.|例句：Kangaroos are iconic symbols of Australia. 袋鼠是澳大利亚的标志性象征。|例句：I saw a group of kangaroos grazing in the field. 我看到一群袋鼠在田里吃草。|近义词：wallaby (较小的袋鼠)|反义词:无
