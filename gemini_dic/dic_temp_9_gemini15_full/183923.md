reservedly:/rɪˈzɜːvdli/ | adv. | 保留地；谨慎地；含蓄地 | In a reserved manner; without openness or enthusiasm.  It implies a restraint in expressing feelings or opinions. |例句：He answered my question reservedly. 他含蓄地回答了我的问题。

She greeted her guests reservedly. 她拘谨地接待了她的客人。


近义词： cautiously, shyly, taciturnly, quietly, subtly


反义词： openly, frankly, enthusiastically, effusively


词源:  Derived from the adjective "reserved," which ultimately comes from the Old French word "reserver" meaning "to keep back," "to retain," or "to save."  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective into an adverb.
