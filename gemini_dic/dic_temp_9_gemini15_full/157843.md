plagiostome:/ˈplædʒiəstoʊm/| n.|软骨鱼；板鳃类动物|A cartilaginous fish, especially a shark, ray, or skate, characterized by having gill slits that open directly to the exterior.  |例句：Sharks, rays, and skates are all examples of plagiostomes.  鲨鱼、鳐鱼和滑冰鱼都是软骨鱼的例子。|例句：The study focused on the reproductive behavior of several plagiostome species. 这项研究关注几种软骨鱼的繁殖行为。|近义词：elasmobranch (more technical)|反义词：osteichthyes (bony fish)|名词
