guineans:/ˈɡɪniənz/| n. |几内亚人（复数）|The plural of Guinean, referring to people from Guinea.  This can refer to either Guinea (in West Africa) or Papua New Guinea.  Context is important to determine which country is being referenced.|例句：Many Guineans migrated to France during the colonial period.  许多几内亚人在殖民时期迁移到法国。

例句：The Guineans celebrated their independence day with great enthusiasm. 几内亚人以极大的热情庆祝他们的独立日。


近义词：People from Guinea, Guineans (singular: Guinean)


反义词：无


词性: 名词
