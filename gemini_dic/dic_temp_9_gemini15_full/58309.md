dry-up:/draɪ ʌp/| v.| 干涸；枯竭；停止；沉默 | to become completely dry; to stop flowing or producing something; to stop talking |例句：The river dried up during the drought.  旱季河流干涸了。

The well has dried up. 这口井干涸了。


例句：The conversation dried up after he left. 他离开后谈话就停止了。

The paint dried up in the tin. 油漆在罐子里干涸了。


近义词：dehydrate, desiccate, exhaust, cease,  silence

反义词：wet, flood, inundate, replenish, flow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The well dries up every summer. = 这口井每年夏天都会干涸。
The river dries up during the drought. = 旱季河流干涸了。


一般过去时:
The stream dried up last year. = 小溪去年干涸了。
The paint dried up quickly. = 油漆很快就干了。


现在完成时:
The lake has dried up completely. = 湖泊已经完全干涸了。
The well has dried up, so we need to find another source of water. = 井干涸了，所以我们需要寻找另一个水源。


过去完成时:
The river had dried up before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，河水已经干涸了。
The plants had dried up because of the lack of rain. = 由于缺乏降雨，植物已经枯萎了。


将来时:
The pond will dry up if it doesn't rain soon. = 如果不久不下雨，池塘就会干涸。
This plant will dry up without enough water. = 这棵植物如果没有足够的水分就会干枯。


虚拟语气:
If it didn't rain, the river would dry up. = 如果不下雨，河水就会干涸。
If the well had not dried up, we would not have had to find another source of water. = 如果井没有干涸，我们就不用寻找另一个水源了。


被动语态:
The land was dried up by the sun. = 这片土地被太阳晒干了。
The riverbed was dried up by the long drought. = 长期干旱使河床干涸。


疑问句:
Has the well dried up? = 井干涸了吗？
Will the river dry up this summer? = 河水今年夏天会干涸吗？


否定句:
The river didn't dry up completely. = 河水并没有完全干涸。
The well hasn't dried up yet. = 井还没干涸。
