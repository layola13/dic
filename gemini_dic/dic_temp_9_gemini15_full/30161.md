celebraters:/ˈsɛləˌbreɪtərz/| n.|庆祝者，参加庆祝活动的人|People who are celebrating.  They are actively involved in a celebration, either participating in the activities or simply being present and enjoying the festivities.|The street was filled with celebraters, cheering and dancing.|近义词：participants, revelers, partygoers|反义词：mourners
