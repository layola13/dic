hallowmas:/ˈhæləʊmæs/| n. |万圣节前夕|All Hallows' Eve, also known as Halloween, is the evening before All Saints' Day (November 1).  It is a time of year associated with ghosts, goblins, and other supernatural beings.  Many traditions and celebrations are connected to Hallowmas, including trick-or-treating, costume parties, and carving pumpkins.|The children went trick-or-treating on Hallowmas.|All Saints' Day, Halloween|Christmas|名词
