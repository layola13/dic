nontypical:/ˌnɒnˈtɪpɪkəl/| adj. |非典型的，不典型的 |Not conforming to type; unusual or unexpected.  |例句：His reaction was nontypical of his usual calm demeanor.他的反应与他通常的冷静态度不符。

例句：The unusually warm weather in December was a nontypical event for the region. 十二月里异常温暖的天气对该地区来说是不寻常的事件。


近义词：atypical, unusual, aberrant, anomalous, exceptional, uncommon
