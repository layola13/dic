sedation:/seɪˈdeɪʃən/| n. |镇静；镇静剂；镇痛；催眠|The state of being calmed or soothed, typically by medication.  It refers to a medically induced state of reduced awareness and responsiveness.  This can range from mild drowsiness to a deeper state of unconsciousness, depending on the medication and dose. |例句：The patient received sedation before the procedure.  病人手术前接受了镇静治疗。

The doctor prescribed sedation to help the anxious patient relax. 医生开了镇静剂帮助焦虑的病人放松。|近义词：tranquilization, calming, anesthesia (for deeper sedation)|反义词：stimulation, excitation|名词
