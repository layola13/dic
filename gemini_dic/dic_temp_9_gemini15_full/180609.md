regiving: /ˌriːˈɡɪvɪŋ/ | v. | 再给予；重新给予；反复给予 |  The act of giving something again or repeatedly;  a renewed or repeated act of giving.  It often implies a continuation or resumption of giving, rather than a single, isolated act. | 例句：The regiving of the awards was necessary due to an administrative error.  = 由于行政错误，需要重新颁奖。

The regiving of donations after the initial appeal demonstrated the community's generosity. = 初次呼吁后的再次捐款体现了社区的慷慨。


近义词：re-donation,  redonation,  re-gifting, giving again, repeated giving | 反义词：taking away, withholding, depriving
