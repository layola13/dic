daughters:/ˈdɔːtərz/ | n. | 女儿们 | The plural form of "daughter," referring to more than one female offspring.  It is used to describe a woman's female children. |  My daughters are all successful in their chosen careers.  我的女儿们都在各自的职业生涯中取得了成功。 He has three daughters and two sons. 他有三个女儿和两个儿子。 | sons, offspring, children | sons, sons-in-law  | noun
