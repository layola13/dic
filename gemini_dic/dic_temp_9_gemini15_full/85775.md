hayfield:/ˈheɪfiːld/| n. |干草地，晒草场 |A field of grass that has been cut and dried to make hay.  A hayfield is typically used for feeding livestock. |例句：The farmer's hayfield is full of golden hay ready for harvest.  这位农民的干草地里堆满了准备收割的金黄色的干草。

The horses grazed peacefully in the lush hayfield.  马儿们在茂盛的干草地上平静地吃草。|近义词：meadow, pasture |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a field used for other crops.
