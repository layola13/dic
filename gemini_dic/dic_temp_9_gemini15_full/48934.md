defuels:/diːˈfjuːlz/| vt.|  除燃料；去除燃料 |To remove fuel from something.  The process of removing fuel from a vehicle or system. |例句：The mechanics defueled the aircraft before maintenance.  机械师在维护飞机之前排空了飞机的燃料。

例句：The engineers defueled the rocket engine after the test. 工程师在测试后排空了火箭发动机的燃料。


近义词：de-fuel, drain, empty


反义词：fuel, refuel


词性：动词
