incommensurabilities:/ˌɪnkəˈmɛnzərəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |不可通约性；无法比拟；无法衡量 |  Plural of incommensurability.  The state of being incommensurable;  the lack of a common standard of measurement or comparison. This often refers to ideas, values, or systems that are fundamentally different and cannot be compared or judged against each other using a single framework.  | The incommensurabilities between different cultures make cross-cultural understanding challenging.  不同的文化之间的不可通约性使得跨文化理解具有挑战性。 |  differences, discrepancies, disparities | commensurabilities, similarities, congruities | 名词
