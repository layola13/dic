collaborative:/kəˈlæbərətɪv/| adj. |合作的，协作的| involving or characterized by collaboration; done by working jointly|The project was a collaborative effort between two universities. 这个项目是两所大学合作的成果。|近义词：cooperative, joint, concerted|反义词：individual, independent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This team works collaboratively on all projects.  = 这个团队所有项目都合作完成。
They collaboratively design new products. = 他们合作设计新产品。

一般过去时:
The artists collaborated on a mural. = 这些艺术家合作创作了一幅壁画。
We collaboratively planned the event. = 我们合作策划了这次活动。

现在完成时:
We have collaboratively developed a new strategy. = 我们已经合作制定了一个新的策略。
They have collaboratively written several books. = 他们已经合作撰写了几本书。

过去完成时:
They had collaboratively finished the report before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经合作完成了报告。
We had collaboratively decided on the best course of action. = 我们此前已经合作决定了最佳行动方案。

将来时:
We will collaborate on a new research project. = 我们将合作一个新的研究项目。
They will collaboratively launch a new marketing campaign. = 他们将合作发起一个新的营销活动。

虚拟语气:
If we collaborated more effectively, we would achieve better results. = 如果我们更有效地合作，我们将取得更好的成果。
It would be better if they collaborated on the design. = 如果他们在设计上合作会更好。

被动语态:
The project was collaboratively managed by a team of experts. = 该项目由专家团队合作管理。
The decision was collaboratively reached after much discussion. = 经过多次讨论，最终大家合作达成了决定。

疑问句:
Are they collaborating on the project? = 他们正在合作这个项目吗？
Is this a collaborative effort? = 这是合作努力吗？

否定句:
They did not collaborate on the project. = 他们没有合作这个项目。
The two companies did not collaborate effectively. = 这两家公司没有有效地合作。
