guiders:/ˈɡaɪdərz/| n. |指导者，向导，领导者 |People who guide or lead others.  They provide direction, instruction, and support.  This word often implies expertise and experience in the area they are guiding.|例句：The mountain guides led the climbers safely to the summit.  山地向导安全地带领登山者到达山顶。  The tour guides provided valuable insights into the city's history.  导游们提供了关于这座城市历史的宝贵见解。|近义词：leaders, instructors, mentors, conductors, escorts|反义词：followers, misled, obstructs
