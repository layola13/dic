innate:/ˈɪneɪt/| adj.|天生的，固有的|Existing in or belonging to a person or thing from birth; inherent.|His innate talent for music was evident from a young age. 他从很小的时候就显现出与生俱来的音乐天赋。

|近义词：inherent, inborn, intrinsic, congenital|反义词：acquired, learned, developed|词性:形容词
