miasma:/ˈmɪæzmə/| n. |瘴气；恶臭的空气；有害的氛围|A miasma is a noxious atmosphere or vapor, especially one emanating from decaying matter and considered to cause disease.  The term is often used figuratively to describe a generally unpleasant or unhealthy atmosphere.| Example: The city was enveloped in a miasma of pollution and decay.  这座城市笼罩在污染和腐烂的瘴气之中。|近义词：stench, foul air, noxious fumes, unhealthy atmosphere|反义词：fresh air, pure air, wholesome atmosphere| 名词
