humpback:/ˈhʌmpbæk/| n.|座头鲸；驼背的人；驼峰|A humpback is a large whale known for its humped back and long pectoral fins.  It's also used to describe a person with a humped back.|例句：The humpback whale is famous for its acrobatic displays.|近义词：whale (general term),  bunched-up (for a person)|反义词：straight-backed (for a person)|名词
