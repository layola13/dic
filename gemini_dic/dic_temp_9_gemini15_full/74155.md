foundress:/ˈfaʊndrəs/| n.|女创始人，创建者|A woman who founds or establishes something, especially a religious order or colony.  |例句：Hildegard of Bingen was a foundress of a significant religious order.  希尔德加德·冯·宾根是一位重要的宗教组织的创建者。

例句：The foundress of the company built it from the ground up. 这家公司的创始人白手起家创建了它。


近义词：founder (applicable if the founder is female), originator, establisher, pioneer

反义词：destroyer, dismantler


词性: 名词
