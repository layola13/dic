ligates:/ˈlaɪɡeɪts/| vt.|连接，捆绑；结扎；(法律)使具有约束力|To tie or bind together; to connect; to ligate (in surgery) means to tie off a blood vessel or other duct.|例句：The surgeon ligated the artery to stop the bleeding.  外科医生结扎动脉以止血。|例句：The contract ligates both parties to their respective obligations. 合同使双方对其各自的义务具有约束力。|近义词：bind, tie, fasten, unite|反义词：disconnect, separate, sever|动词
