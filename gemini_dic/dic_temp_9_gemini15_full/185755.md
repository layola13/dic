review:/rɪˈvjuː/ | n., vt. | 评论；复习；回顾 |  To consider something again or to examine it in detail.  |例句：I need to review my notes before the exam. 我需要在考试前复习我的笔记。|例句：The film received positive reviews. 这部电影获得了好评。 |近义词：examine, assess, reconsider, study | 反义词：ignore, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I review my notes every day. = 我每天复习我的笔记。
She reviews the company's financial reports monthly. = 她每月审查公司的财务报告。

一般过去时:
He reviewed the contract carefully before signing. = 他在签署合同之前仔细审查了合同。
They reviewed the film and gave it a good rating. = 他们观看了这部电影并给了它很高的评价。

现在完成时:
I have reviewed the document and found no errors. = 我已经审查了这份文件，没有发现错误。
She has reviewed all the applications for the job. = 她已经审查了所有求职申请。

过去完成时:
He had reviewed the data before presenting his findings. = 他在陈述他的发现之前已经审查了数据。
They had reviewed the plan multiple times before implementation. = 他们在实施计划之前已经多次审查了该计划。

将来时:
I will review the project proposal next week. = 我下周将审查项目提案。
She will review the evidence before making a decision. = 她将在做出决定之前审查证据。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would review the entire textbook. = 如果我有更多时间，我会复习整本教科书。
I wish I had reviewed the material more thoroughly. = 我希望我更彻底地复习了材料。

被动语态:
The contract will be reviewed by the legal department. = 合同将由法律部门审查。
The proposal is being reviewed by the committee. = 提案正在由委员会审查。

疑问句:
Have you reviewed the latest data? = 你复习了最新的数据吗？
Will they review the decision again? = 他们会再次审查这个决定吗？

否定句:
I didn't review the chapter properly. = 我没有好好复习那一章。
They haven't reviewed the application yet. = 他们还没有审查申请。


PHRASE->
book review: 书评
peer review: 同行评审
movie review: 影评
under review: 正在审核中
critical review: 批评性评论
