revaccinating:/ˌriːˈvæksɪneɪtɪŋ/| v. |再次接种疫苗；重新接种疫苗 |The act of vaccinating someone again, usually after a period of time or with a different vaccine.  This is often done to boost immunity or to address a new variant of a virus.|例句：Many people are choosing to get revaccinated against the flu this year. 今年许多人选择再次接种流感疫苗。

例句：The government is urging everyone to consider revaccinating against COVID-19.  政府敦促每个人考虑再次接种新冠疫苗。

近义词：reimmunizing, reinoculating

反义词：deimmunizing (though not a direct opposite, it represents the removal of immunity)

词性：动词
