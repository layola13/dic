fireplace:/ˈfaɪərpleɪs/| n. |壁炉|A structure of brick, stone, or metal built into a wall or forming a recess, containing a fire for heating a room or building.  It typically has a chimney to carry away smoke.|例句：We sat by the fireplace, warming ourselves on a cold winter's evening. 我们坐在壁炉旁，在一个寒冷的冬夜里取暖。

例句：The old house boasted a magnificent marble fireplace in the living room. 老房子里客厅有一座宏伟的大理石壁炉。


近义词：hearth (炉膛), fire grate (炉栅)|反义词：None|词性:名词
