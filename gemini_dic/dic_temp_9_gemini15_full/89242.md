homilist:/ˈhɒməlɪst/| n. |讲道者，训诫者 |A homilist is a person who delivers homilies, typically a sermon in a religious context.  They often focus on moral or religious instruction. |The homilist delivered a powerful sermon on forgiveness.  这位讲道者发表了一篇关于宽恕的有力布道。| preacher, orator, lecturer | critic, antagonist |名词
