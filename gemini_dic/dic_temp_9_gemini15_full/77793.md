generalize:/ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪz/|/ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪz/| vt. |概括；推广；普遍化 |To make a general statement about something; to form a general opinion.  |The study generalized the findings to the entire population. 这项研究将调查结果推广到整个人群。|  He tended to generalize about people. 他倾向于对人进行概括。| specialize, summarize | specify, particularize |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Scientists generalize from specific observations. = 科学家从具体的观察中进行概括。
They generalize their findings to a wider audience. = 他们将他们的研究结果推广到更广泛的受众。


一般过去时:
The researcher generalized the results of the study. = 研究人员概括了研究的结果。
He generalized about the behavior of teenagers. = 他对青少年的行为进行了概括。


现在完成时:
Researchers have generalized the findings to a larger population. = 研究人员已将调查结果推广到更大的人群。
Scientists have generalized about the effects of climate change. = 科学家们对气候变化的影响进行了概括。


过去完成时:
The author had generalized about the political climate before writing the book. = 作者在写书之前已经对政治气候进行了概括。
They had generalized the data before presenting their findings. = 他们已经在展示研究结果之前对数据进行了概括。


将来时:
The team will generalize their results in the upcoming report. = 该团队将在即将发布的报告中概括他们的结果。
She will generalize her experience in her presentation. = 她将在她的演示文稿中概括她的经验。


虚拟语气:
If we generalized these findings, we might reach a different conclusion. = 如果我们对这些发现进行概括，我们可能会得出不同的结论。
I wish I hadn't generalized so quickly about his character. = 我真希望我没有对他的人品如此迅速地进行概括。


被动语态:
The data were generalized to fit the model. = 数据被概括以适应模型。
The results have been generalized by the researchers. = 结果已被研究人员概括。


疑问句:
Can we generalize these findings to other populations? = 我们能将这些发现推广到其他人群吗？
How did the author generalize his arguments? = 作者是如何概括他的论点的？


否定句:
We cannot generalize from a single case. = 我们不能从单个案例中进行概括。
It is dangerous to generalize about entire groups of people. = 对整个人群进行概括是很危险的。


PHRASE->
generalize about = 对...进行概括
generalize from = 从...概括
generalize to = 推广到
tend to generalize = 倾向于概括
fail to generalize = 无法概括
