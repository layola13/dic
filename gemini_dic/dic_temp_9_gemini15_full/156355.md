physiognomists:/ˌfɪziˈɒɡnəmɪsts/ | n. |相面家；观相家 |  People who practice physiognomy, the art of judging character from facial features.  They believe that a person's character can be determined by analyzing their facial features and expressions. | Example:  Ancient  physiognomists believed that a person's nose could reveal their temperament. 古代的相面家认为一个人的鼻子可以揭示他们的气质。   Many modern people dismiss physiognomists as pseudoscientists.许多现代人认为相面家是伪科学家。|近义词：face reader, character analyst |反义词：N/A
