egality:/ɪˈɡælɪti/| n. |平等；同等；均等 | Equality means the state of being equal, especially in status, rights, and opportunities.  It often refers to social, political, and economic equality.  The concept of egality is central to many social justice movements. |例句：The declaration promotes equality before the law. 这份宣言提倡法律面前人人平等。


例句：  Striving for gender equality is crucial for a just society.  争取性别平等对于一个公正的社会至关重要。


近义词：equality, equivalence, parity |反义词：inequality, disparity, discrimination |词性：名词
