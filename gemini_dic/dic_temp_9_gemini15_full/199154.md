/ˌsɪlɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |硅化作用；硅化物|The process by which silica is deposited in organic tissues, replacing the original material and preserving the structure.  It's a common process in fossilization.|例句：The silicification of the wood preserved its intricate cellular structure for millions of years.  硅化作用使木材的复杂细胞结构保存了数百万年。|例句：The paleontologists studied the silicifications found in the ancient seabed. 古生物学家研究了在古代海底发现的硅化物。|近义词：petrification, mineralization|反义词：decomposition, decay|名词
