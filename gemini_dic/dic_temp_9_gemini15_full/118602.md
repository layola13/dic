mess around:/ˌmɛs əˈraʊnd/| v.|瞎搞，胡闹，浪费时间 |To spend time doing unimportant things without a clear purpose; to fool around.  |例句：Stop messing around and get to work!  别瞎搞了，赶紧工作！
例句：They spent the afternoon messing around on the beach. 他们在海滩上消磨了一下午。|近义词：fool around, dawdle, trifle|反义词：work diligently, be productive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mess around a lot. = 他们经常瞎搞。
He messes around with his computer all day. = 他整天瞎鼓捣他的电脑。

一般过去时:
We messed around in the park yesterday. = 我们昨天在公园里瞎玩了一阵。
She messed around with the settings and broke the machine. = 她乱改设置把机器弄坏了。

现在完成时:
They have been messing around all morning. = 他们整个上午都在瞎混。
I have messed around too much and now I'm behind schedule. = 我瞎混了太久，现在落后于进度了。

过去完成时:
He had messed around all day, so he was unprepared for the test. = 他整天都在瞎混，所以考试没准备。
They had messed around with the chemicals and caused an explosion. = 他们乱动化学药品，导致了爆炸。

将来时:
I will mess around with this project later. = 我稍后再研究一下这个项目。
They will mess around until the deadline. = 他们会一直混到截止日期。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't messed around, I would have finished the assignment on time. = 如果我没有瞎混，我就能按时完成作业了。
I wish I hadn't messed around with my career choices. = 我真希望我当初没有在职业选择上瞎折腾。

被动语态:  (This phrase is rarely used passively.)  这个短语很少用被动语态。


疑问句:
Are they messing around again? = 他们又在瞎搞了吗？
What are you messing around with? = 你在瞎鼓捣什么？


否定句:
Don't mess around with that dangerous equipment. = 不要乱动那些危险的设备。
He doesn't mess around when it comes to his work. = 他工作起来从不马虎。


PHRASE->

mess around in: 在…瞎混
mess around with someone: 与某人调情
mess around on something: 在…上浪费时间
mess around doing something: 瞎搞…
