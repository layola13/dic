civic:/ˈsɪvɪk/| adj.| 市民的；公民的；市政的 | Relating to a city or town, especially its government, or to its citizens.  | Example: Civic duty requires citizens to vote.  公民责任要求公民投票。|近义词：municipal, municipal, public | 反义词：private, personal
