dot-comer:/ˈdɑːtˌkɒmər/|/ˈdɑːtˌkɑːmər/| n. |网络公司；互联网公司|A dot-com is a company that does most of its business on the internet.  Dot-coms were particularly prominent during the dot-com bubble of the late 1990s.|Example:  Many dot-coms went bankrupt during the dot-com bust.  许多网络公司在互联网泡沫破裂期间破产了。|近义词：internet company, online business, web-based company|反义词：brick-and-mortar business (实体店)|名词
