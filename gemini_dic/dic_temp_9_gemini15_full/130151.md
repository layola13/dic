non-relational: /ˌnɒn rɪˈleɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非关系型的；非关系数据库的 | Not based on the relational model of data; referring to databases that do not use tables with rows and columns to organize data.  Instead, they employ various data models like key-value stores, document databases, graph databases, or wide-column stores. | Example:  Non-relational databases are often chosen for their scalability and flexibility.  非关系型数据库通常因其可扩展性和灵活性而被选择。 | 近义词: NoSQL, distributed | 反义词: relational
