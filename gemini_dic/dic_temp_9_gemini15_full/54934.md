dispositor:/dɪˈspɒzɪtə(r)/| n. |支配者；管理者；配置者|A dispositor is a person or thing that disposes or arranges something.  It can refer to someone who controls or manages something, or a device that arranges or distributes something. In astrology, it specifically refers to the planet ruling the sign a planet is in.|例句：The dispositor of the Mars in Aries is Mars itself.  火星在白羊座的支配星是火星本身。|近义词：controller, administrator, manager, arranger|反义词：subordinate, follower
