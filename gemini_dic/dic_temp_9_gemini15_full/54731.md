disparagements: /dɪˈspærɪdʒmənts/ | n. |轻蔑；贬低；诋毁 |The act of disparaging; an instance of being disparaged.  It involves belittling or expressing contempt for someone or something. |例句：His constant disparagements of her work deeply wounded her.  他不断贬低她的工作深深伤害了她。


例句：The article was full of disparagements aimed at the opposing political party. 这篇文章充满了针对反对党的诋毁。


近义词：belittlements, criticisms, insults, slights, denigrations


反义词：praises, compliments, accolades, approvals


词性：名词
