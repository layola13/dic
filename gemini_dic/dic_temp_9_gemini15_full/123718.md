moonlighting:/ˈmuːnˌlaɪtɪŋ/ | n. v. |兼职工作；月光族 |Having a second job besides one's regular employment.  |例句：He's moonlighting as a taxi driver to make extra money. 他在兼职做出租车司机以赚取额外收入。  |近义词：second job, side hustle, part-time job |反义词：full-time job


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He moonlights as a bartender. = 他兼职做调酒师。
She moonlights as a writer. = 她兼职做作家。

一般过去时:
He moonlighted as a waiter during college. = 他大学期间兼职做过服务员。
She moonlighted as a singer last summer. = 她去年夏天兼职做过歌手。

现在完成时:
He has been moonlighting as a tutor for years. = 他多年来一直兼职做家教。
She has moonlighted at various jobs to pay off her debts. = 她做过各种兼职工作来偿还债务。

过去完成时:
He had moonlighted for months before he finally quit his day job. = 他在最终辞去全职工作之前已经兼职了好几个月。
She had moonlighted as a cleaner before she got her current position. = 在获得现在职位之前，她曾兼职做过清洁工。

将来时:
He will moonlight as a DJ this weekend. = 他这个周末将兼职做DJ。
She will moonlight at the bakery to save for a vacation. = 她将在面包店兼职以攒钱去度假。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would moonlight as a photographer. = 如果我有更多时间，我就会兼职做摄影师。
I wish I could moonlight, but I'm too busy with my full-time job. = 我真希望我能兼职，但是我的全职工作太忙了。

被动语态:
(This is less common for "moonlighting" as it's typically an active choice.)  His skills were moonlighted by the company for extra projects.  他的技能被公司利用来做额外的项目。(被动语态，侧重技能被利用)
The extra work was moonlighted by the employees after hours. 额外的任务是在下班后由员工兼职完成。(被动语态，侧重工作被兼职)

疑问句:
Does he moonlight? = 他兼职吗？
Does she moonlight often? = 她经常兼职吗？

否定句:
He doesn't moonlight anymore. = 他不再兼职了。
She doesn't have time to moonlight. = 她没有时间兼职。



PHRASE->
moonlighting opportunities = 兼职机会

moonlighting job = 兼职工作
moonlighting hours = 兼职时间
moonlighting gigs = 兼职演出（指表演性质的兼职）
