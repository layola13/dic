salaam:/səˈlɑːm/| n. int.|  问候，平安| An Arabic word meaning "peace," used as a greeting.  It is a common greeting in many Muslim cultures.  |例句：He offered a salaam to the elders. 他向长者行了个礼。  

例句：They exchanged salaams before beginning the meeting.  他们在会议开始前互相问好。


近义词：greeting, peace, hello,  as-salamu alaykum (more formal Arabic equivalent)

反义词：N/A (not applicable; it's a greeting, not something with a direct opposite)

词性: Noun, Interjection
