ilate:/aɪˈleɪt/ | vt. |使高兴；使兴奋；使得意 |To make someone feel very happy and proud.  |The good news elated her.  好消息使她欣喜若狂。|近义词：exhilarate, exhilarate, uplift |反义词：depress, dispirit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He elates her with his jokes. = 他用他的笑话使她高兴。
The victory elates the team. = 胜利使球队兴奋。


一般过去时:
The news elated us greatly. = 这个消息使我们非常高兴。
The success of the project elated the whole team. = 项目的成功使整个团队都兴奋不已。


现在完成时:
The news has elated me all day. = 这个消息让我一整天都兴高采烈。
His promotion has elated him considerably. = 他的晋升使他非常高兴。


过去完成时:
I had been elated by the prospect of the trip. = 我曾经对这次旅行的前景感到兴奋。
They had been elated by their recent success. = 他们因最近的成功而感到兴奋。


将来时:
The announcement will elate many people. = 这一宣布将使许多人高兴。
Winning the lottery will elate him beyond measure. = 赢得彩票将使他欣喜若狂。


虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would be elated. = 如果我中了彩票，我会非常高兴。
I wish I could be elated by something positive. = 我希望我能因为一些积极的事情而感到高兴。


被动语态:
She was elated by the praise she received. = 她对收到的赞扬感到高兴。
The team was elated by their unexpected victory. = 球队对他们意外的胜利感到兴奋。


疑问句:
What elates you the most in life? = 你生活中什么事情最让你高兴？
Does success always elate you? = 成功总是让你兴奋吗？


否定句:
The news didn't elate her at all. = 这个消息一点也没让她高兴。
The bad weather did not elate the hikers. = 恶劣的天气并没有让徒步旅行者感到兴奋。


PHRASE->
elate oneself:  使自己高兴
elate the spirit: 振奋精神
elate with joy: 欣喜若狂
feel elated: 感到兴奋
be elated by: 因...而兴奋
