microbes:/ˈmaɪkroʊbz/| n. |微生物|Microscopic organisms, such as bacteria, viruses, fungi, and protozoa, that are too small to be seen without a microscope.  They can be found everywhere in the environment, including soil, water, air, and on the surfaces of objects.|Example:  The scientist studied the microbes in the soil sample.  这位科学家研究了土壤样本中的微生物。
|The experiment showed the effects of microbes on plant growth. 实验显示了微生物对植物生长的影响。|germs, bacteria, microorganisms|macrobes
