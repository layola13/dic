harder:/ˈhɑːrdər/| adj.| 更难的，更费力的 | More difficult; requiring more effort.  |例句：The exam was harder than I expected.  考试比我预期的难。

例句：This task is harder than it looks. 这项任务比看起来更难。


近义词：more difficult, tougher, challenging, strenuous

反义词：easier, simpler, less difficult


词性:形容词

USAGE->
比较级:
The second question was harder than the first. 第二个问题比第一个问题更难。
This route is harder than that one. 这条路比那条路更难走。


最高级:
This is the hardest problem I've ever encountered. 这是我遇到的最难的问题。
That was the hardest test of my life. 那是我人生中最难的考试。


PHRASE->
work harder: 更努力地工作
try harder: 更努力地尝试
get harder: 变得更难
think harder: 更努力地思考
push harder: 更努力地推动


词源:
源自古英语 *heard,  形容词的比较级形式，其原型hard意为“坚硬的，困难的”。  比较级harder体现了程度上的递增。
