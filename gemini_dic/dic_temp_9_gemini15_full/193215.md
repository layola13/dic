seductions:/sɪˈdʌkʃənz/| n.|诱惑；引诱；煽动|The act or practice of seducing; the state of being seduced.  It often refers to the tempting of someone into doing something that is wrong or unwise, usually involving sexual attraction but also applicable to other forms of enticement. |例句：Her beauty and charm were her greatest seductions. 她的美丽和魅力是她最大的诱惑。

例句：The seductions of power led him to corruption. 权力的诱惑导致了他的腐败。


近义词：temptations, allurements, enticements, charms

反义词：repulsions, deterrents


词性:名词
