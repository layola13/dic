calculi:/ˈkælkjʊlaɪ/ | n. |结石；计算；微积分 |Small, hard masses that form in the body, typically in the kidneys, bladder, or gallbladder.  The plural of calculus.  Also refers to calculations or symbolic manipulations in mathematics. |例句：Kidney stones are a type of calculi.  肾结石是一种结石。

The patient was suffering from urinary calculi. 病人患有尿路结石。


近义词：stones, concretions | 反义词：None specifically


词源：From Latin *calculus, diminutive of calx "pebble, limestone".  The mathematical sense developed later, probably from the use of pebbles in counting.
