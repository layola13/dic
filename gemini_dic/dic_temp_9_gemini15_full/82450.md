grumblings:/ˈɡrʌmblɪŋz/| n. |抱怨声，牢骚，低沉的隆隆声 |Low, continuous sounds of complaining or muttering;  a series of complaints or expressions of discontent.|例句：The grumblings of the workers grew louder as their wages remained stagnant. 工人们的抱怨声越来越大，因为他们的工资停滞不前。

例句：We could hear the distant grumblings of thunder. 我们能听到远处隆隆的雷声。


近义词：complaints, murmurs, mutterings, rumbles |反义词：praises, approvals, compliments


词性：名词
