landlouper:/ˈlændˌluːpər/| n. |流浪者，游民|A landlouper is a vagrant or wanderer, often implying someone without a fixed home or occupation.  The term carries a slightly archaic or dialectal feel, particularly associated with Scotland and the Scottish Borders.|例句：He was a landlouper, drifting from town to town, seeking work where he could find it. 他是个流浪汉，在城镇间漂泊，四处寻找工作。  The old woman, a landlouper for most of her life, finally found a place to settle.  这位老妇人大半辈子都是流浪汉，最终找到了一处定居的地方。|近义词：vagabond, tramp, wanderer, drifter, hobo|反义词：settler, resident, homeowner
