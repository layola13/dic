chapelling:/ˈtʃæpəlɪŋ/| n.|小教堂礼拜；小教堂活动|The act of attending or conducting a religious service in a chapel.  It can also refer to the building itself when used in a context clearly implying religious activity.|例句：The students enjoyed the quiet chapelling in the small university chapel. 学生们喜欢在大学小教堂里安静的礼拜。|近义词：worship, service, devotion|反义词：irreverence, blasphemy
