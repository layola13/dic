misconfident:/ˌmɪskˈɒnfɪdənt/| adj.|缺乏自信的，不自信的|Lacking self-confidence; unsure of oneself.  This word describes someone who is not confident and often doubts their abilities. It implies a negative self-perception.|He felt misconfident about his ability to win the race. 他对赢得比赛的能力缺乏信心。She gave a misconfident presentation, stumbling over her words. 她做了一个缺乏信心的演示，说话结结巴巴的。|self-assured, confident|confident, self-reliant|形容词
