prefigure:/priːˈfɪɡər/| vt.|预示，预想，预表|To represent or foreshadow (something to come); to anticipate or predict.  The early symptoms prefigured the serious illness to come.|The ancient myths prefigure many modern themes. 古代神话预示了许多现代主题。| foreshadow, anticipate, portend |  |v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist's early works prefigure his later, more mature style. = 艺术家的早期作品预示着他后来更成熟的风格。
These events prefigure a major shift in the political landscape. = 这些事件预示着政治格局的重大转变。


一般过去时:
The rising tide prefigured the coming storm. = 涨潮预示着即将到来的风暴。
His nervous behavior prefigured the bad news he was about to deliver. = 他紧张的行为预示着他即将宣布的坏消息。


现在完成时:
Recent economic indicators have prefigured a period of recession. = 最近的经济指标预示着一轮经济衰退。
The architect's sketches have prefigured the building's final design. = 建筑师的草图预示了建筑物的最终设计。


过去完成时:
The ancient prophecies had prefigured the rise of the empire. = 古代预言预示了帝国的兴起。
The initial tremors had prefigured the catastrophic earthquake. = 初始的震颤预示着灾难性的地震。


将来时:
Experts predict that the current trends will prefigure a future crisis. = 专家预测，当前的趋势将预示未来的危机。
The latest research suggests that the new technology will prefigure a revolution in healthcare. = 最新研究表明，这项新技术将预示医疗保健领域的革命。


虚拟语气:
If the signs prefigured a disaster, we should have taken action sooner. = 如果这些迹象预示着灾难，我们应该早点采取行动。
Had the initial signs prefigured the severity of the problem, more resources would have been allocated. = 如果最初的迹象预示了问题的严重性，就会分配更多资源。


被动语态:
The coming storm was prefigured by ominous clouds. = 即将到来的风暴被不祥的乌云预示着。
The success of the project was prefigured by careful planning and execution. = 项目的成功是由周密的计划和执行预示的。


疑问句:
Does this trend prefigure a change in consumer behavior? = 这种趋势是否预示着消费者行为的改变？
Do the current political tensions prefigure an escalation of conflict? = 当前的政治紧张局势是否预示着冲突的升级？


否定句:
The initial reports did not prefigure the scale of the damage. = 初始报告没有预示损害的规模。
The seemingly benign symptoms did not prefigure the serious illness that followed. = 看似良性的症状并没有预示随后的严重疾病。

prefigure something's outcome = 预示某事的结果
prefigure a pattern = 预示一种模式
