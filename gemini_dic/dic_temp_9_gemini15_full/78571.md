/ˈɡɪvɪŋ/| v. |给予；捐赠；产生；让步|Giving is a fundamental human act.  给予是人类的基本行为。|He gave a generous donation to the charity. 他向慈善机构慷慨捐款。|receiving, donating, contributing|taking, withholding|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I give you my word. = 我向你保证。
She gives generously to charity. = 她慷慨地捐赠给慈善机构。


一般过去时:
He gave me a book. = 他给了我一本。
They gave a speech yesterday. = 他们昨天做了一个演讲。


现在完成时:
I have given you all the information I have. = 我已经把我知道的所有信息都告诉你了。
She has given birth to a healthy baby. = 她生了一个健康的婴儿。


过去完成时:
He had given up smoking before he met her. = 他在她之前就已经戒烟了。
They had given their best effort. = 他们已经尽力了。


将来时:
I will give you a call tomorrow. = 我明天会给你打电话。
They will give a presentation next week. = 他们下周将做一个演示。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would give it to the poor. = 如果我有更多钱，我会把它给穷人。
I wish I had given him a second chance. = 我真希望我给了他第二次机会。


被动语态:
The award was given to the most deserving candidate. = 奖项颁给了最合格的候选人。
The money was given to the victims of the disaster. = 这笔钱给了灾难的受害者。


疑问句:
What did you give him? = 你给了他什么？
Who gave you that book? = 谁给了你那本书？


否定句:
I didn't give him any money. = 我没有给他任何钱。
She didn't give up easily. = 她没有轻易放弃。
