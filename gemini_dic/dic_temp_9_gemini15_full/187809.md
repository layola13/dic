rose-coloured: /ˈroʊzˌkʌləd/ | adj. | 玫瑰色的；乐观的；理想化的 |  Seeing things through rose-tinted spectacles; characterized by optimism or idealism, often to an unrealistic degree.  |例句：She had a rose-coloured view of the future. 她对未来抱有玫瑰色的憧憬。

例句：He wore rose-coloured glasses, ignoring the problems. 他戴着玫瑰色的眼镜，无视问题。


近义词：optimistic, idealistic, utopian, rosy


反义词：pessimistic, realistic, cynical, bleak


词性：形容词
