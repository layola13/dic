re-reviewed:/ˌriːrɪˈvjuːd/| v. |重新审查；复审|To review something again, often with more detail or a different perspective.  This implies a previous review has already taken place.|The proposal was re-reviewed by the committee after initial concerns were raised.|近义词：reexamined, reassessed |反义词：accepted (without further review)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-review the contract every year. = 他们每年都会重新审查合同。
We re-review the safety procedures regularly. = 我们定期重新审查安全程序。


一般过去时:
The judge re-reviewed the evidence before making a decision. = 法官在做出决定之前重新审查了证据。
The team re-reviewed the data to find any errors. = 团队重新审查了数据以查找任何错误。


现在完成时:
They have re-reviewed the budget several times this month. = 这个月他们已经多次重新审查了预算。
She has re-reviewed her application and corrected the mistakes. = 她已经重新审查了她的申请并纠正了错误。


过去完成时:
The board had re-reviewed the report before the meeting. = 董事会在会议之前已经重新审查了报告。
He had re-reviewed the manuscript before submitting it. = 他在提交手稿之前已经重新审查了它。


将来时:
The committee will re-review the application next week. = 委员会下周将重新审查申请。
They will re-review the policy after the public hearing. = 他们在公开听证会之后将重新审查这项政策。


虚拟语气:
If they had re-reviewed the data, they might have found the error. = 如果他们重新审查了数据，他们可能会发现错误。
It's important that the report be re-reviewed before publication. = 在出版之前，报告必须重新审查这一点很重要。


被动语态:
The document was re-reviewed by the legal team. = 文件由法律团队重新审查。
The proposal is being re-reviewed by the board of directors. = 该提案正在由董事会重新审查。


疑问句:
Will the report be re-reviewed before submission? = 报告在提交之前会重新审查吗？
Has the contract been re-reviewed yet? = 合同已经重新审查了吗？


否定句:
The committee did not re-review the proposal. = 委员会没有重新审查该提案。
They haven't re-reviewed the budget yet. = 他们还没有重新审查预算。


PHRASE->
re-review the findings = 重新审查调查结果
re-review the evidence = 重新审查证据
re-review the process = 重新审查流程
re-review the decision = 重新审查决定
re-review the document = 重新审查文件
