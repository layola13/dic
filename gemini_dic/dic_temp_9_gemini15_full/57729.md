dreadlocks:/ˈdrɛdˌlɑːks/| n. |脏辫；雷鬼辫|Matted or tightly twisted strands of hair.  Dreadlocks are a hairstyle associated with Rastafarian culture, but are also worn by people of various cultures and backgrounds.|例句：He wore his dreadlocks in a long, flowing style. 他留着长长的飘逸的脏辫。|例句：Many people admire the intricate patterns in her dreadlocks. 很多人欣赏她脏辫上复杂的图案。|近义词：locks, braids, twists|反义词：short hair, straight hair
