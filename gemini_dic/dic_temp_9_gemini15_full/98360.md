insist:/ɪnˈsɪst/| vt.|坚持，坚决主张|To state something firmly and repeatedly.  To demand something forcefully.|例句：He insisted on paying the bill. 他坚持要付账。|例句：She insisted that she was innocent. 她坚决否认有罪。|近义词：persist, urge, maintain|反义词：yield, concede, acquiesce


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He insists on his rights. 他坚持他的权利。
They insist that the project is feasible. 他们坚持这个项目是可行的。

一般过去时:
She insisted on seeing the manager. 她坚持要见经理。
He insisted that he had told the truth. 他坚持说他说了实话。

现在完成时:
They have insisted on a full investigation. 他们一直坚持要进行全面调查。
She has insisted that everything is under control. 她一直坚持说一切都在掌控之中。

过去完成时:
He insisted that he had already finished the work. 他坚持说他已经完成了工作。
They insisted that they had never seen him before. 他们坚持说他们以前从未见过他。

将来时:
I will insist on my opinion. 我将坚持我的意见。
She will insist that he attend the meeting. 她将坚持要他参加会议。

虚拟语气:
I insisted that he be present at the meeting. 我坚持要他出席会议。(should be present)
He insisted that he had been there. 他坚持说他曾经在那里。(had been there)


被动语态:
It was insisted that the rules be followed.  有人坚持必须遵守规则。
His innocence was insisted upon by his lawyer. 他的律师坚持认为他是无辜的。

疑问句:
Does he insist on his opinion? 他坚持他的意见吗？
Do they insist that it's true? 他们坚持这是真的吗？

否定句:
He didn't insist on going. 他没有坚持要去。
They didn't insist that we stay late. 他们没有坚持让我们待到很晚。


PHRASE->
insist on/upon: 坚持…
insist that: 坚决认为…
insistently: 坚持地
cannot insist enough: 再怎么强调也不为过
insist on doing sth: 坚持做某事
