nonforeignness: /ˌnɒnˈfɒrɪnəs/ | n. |非外来性，本土性 | The quality or state of not being foreign;  indigenousness.  It often refers to the linguistic or cultural aspects of something being native to a particular place or region, rather than originating from elsewhere.  |例句：The nonforeignness of the dialect was evident in its unique vocabulary and grammar.  该方言的非外来性在其独特的词汇和语法中显而易见。

The nonforeignness of the local cuisine is a testament to its long history. 当地菜肴的非外来性证明了其悠久的历史。

|近义词：indigenousness, nativeness, domesticity |反义词：foreignness, exoticism |名词
