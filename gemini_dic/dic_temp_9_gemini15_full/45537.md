curio:/ˈkjʊriˌoʊ/| n. |珍品；古玩；稀罕物 |A curio is an object that is interesting or unusual because it is old, rare, or strange.  It is often collected as a souvenir or for its aesthetic value. |例句：The antique shop was filled with curious and fascinating curios. 这家古董店里摆满了稀奇古怪的珍品。

例句：He brought back several curios from his trip to Asia. 他从亚洲旅行带回了几件珍品。


近义词：artifact, antique, rarity, oddity, collectible

反义词：commonplace, ordinary


词源:  The word "curio" comes from the French word "curiosité," meaning "curiosity" or "rare object."  This, in turn, derives from the Latin word "curiosus," meaning "careful," "inquisitive," or "curious." The shift in meaning from "curiosity" to "curious object" happened over time.
