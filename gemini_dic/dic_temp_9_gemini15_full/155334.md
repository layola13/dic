pharmaceuticals: /ˌfɑːrməˈsuːtɪkəlz/ | n. |制药业；药品 |  Medicines or drugs, especially those that are manufactured by chemical processes. The term often refers to the industry involved in the research, development, production, and marketing of these products. |


例句：

The company is a leader in the pharmaceuticals industry. 这家公司是制药业的领导者。

Many new pharmaceuticals are being developed to treat various diseases. 许多新的药物正在被研发以治疗各种疾病。


近义词：medicines, drugs, remedies, medications


反义词：N/A (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与“草药疗法”或“自然疗法”之类的概念相对比)


词性: 名词
