overreports:/ˌoʊvərˈriːpɔːrts/ | vt. | 夸大报告；过高报告 | To report something as being larger, more significant, or more frequent than it actually is.  |例句：The company overreports its profits to attract investors.  公司夸大其盈利以吸引投资者。
The study overreports the number of participants. 这项研究夸大了参与者的数量。|近义词：exaggerate, inflate, overstate |反义词：underreport, downplay

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government overreports the economic growth. = 政府夸大经济增长。
He frequently overreports his workload. = 他经常夸大他的工作量。


一般过去时:
The media overreported the impact of the storm. = 媒体夸大了风暴的影响。
