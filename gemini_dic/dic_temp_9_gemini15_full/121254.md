misestimated:/ˌmɪsˈɛstɪmeɪt/| vt.| 估计错误，低估或高估 | To make a wrong judgment about the amount, size, or value of something.  | 例句：The project was misestimated, and we ended up over budget.  项目估计错误，最终超支了。


例句：They grossly misestimated the time it would take to complete the project. 他们严重低估了完成项目所需的时间。


近义词：underestimate, overestimate, misjudge


反义词：accurately estimate


词性：动词
