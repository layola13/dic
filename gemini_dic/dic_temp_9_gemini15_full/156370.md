physiologically:/ˌfɪziəˈlɒdʒɪkli/| adv.|生理上地；从生理学角度|In a way that relates to the normal functions of living organisms.|The drug affects the body physiologically.  这种药物对人体有生理作用。|近义词： biologically, organically |反义词：psychologically

He physiologically responds to stress by sweating. = 他生理上对压力做出反应，会出汗。
Plants physiologically react to sunlight. = 植物生理上对阳光做出反应。
