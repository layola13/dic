duboisia:/ˌduːbɔɪˈsiːə/| n. |杜氏烟草属植物|Duboisia is a genus of flowering plants in the family Solanaceae, native to Australia.  The genus is known for its alkaloids, which have been used traditionally by indigenous Australians and are now used in pharmaceutical products.
