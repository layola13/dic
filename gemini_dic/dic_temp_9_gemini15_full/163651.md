predebater:/ˌpriːdɪˈbeɪtər/ | n. | 预备辩论者，预先辩论者 | A person who engages in preliminary debate or prepares for a debate.  A predebater might research arguments, practice delivery, or strategize with a team before a formal debate. | Example: The predebater meticulously reviewed the evidence before the main event.  预备辩论者在主要活动之前仔细审查了证据。| 近义词：debater, arguer, disputant | 反义词：listener, observer | 名词
