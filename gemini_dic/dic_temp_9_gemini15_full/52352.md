dictatorate:/ˈdɪkteɪtəreɪt/| n. |独裁统治；独裁政权 |The rule of a dictator; a country governed by a dictator.  A dictatorship is characterized by absolute power, often achieved and maintained through force and suppression of opposition.  |例句：The dictatorate imposed strict censorship on the press.  独裁政权对新闻媒体实行严格的审查制度。

The military's seizure of power led to a brutal dictatorate.  军方夺取政权导致了残酷的独裁统治。

|近义词：tyranny, autocracy, despotism |反义词：democracy, republic |名词
