keratoderma:/ˌkɛrətəˈdɜːrmə/| n. |角化过度症，角质化皮肤病 |A skin disorder characterized by thickening of the epidermis, typically on the palms and soles.  It can be inherited or acquired and may be associated with other medical conditions. | Example: The patient presented with keratoderma on the palms and soles, consistent with a diagnosis of palmoplantar keratoderma.  患者的手掌和脚底出现角化过度症，与掌跖角化过度症的诊断相符。|近义词：hyperkeratosis, ichthyosis (depending on the specific type) |反义词：N/A
