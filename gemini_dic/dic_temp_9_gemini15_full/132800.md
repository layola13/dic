none's: /nʌnz/ | pron. | 没有任何人的；无人的 |  This is a contraction of "no one's," meaning "belonging to no one" or "not anyone's."  It indicates a lack of possession or ownership. | 例句：That book is none's; it's been left on the table. 那本书不属于任何人；它被留在桌上了。


例句：There's none's responsibility for this mess. 这烂摊子没有人负责。


近义词：nobody's, no one's


反义词：someone's, everybody's


词性：代词
