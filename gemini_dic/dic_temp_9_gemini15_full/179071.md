redeploying:/ˌriːdɪˈplɔɪɪŋ/| v.|重新部署，重新配置 |The act of moving resources (personnel, equipment, software, etc.) to a new location or task.  It implies a strategic shift or reallocation of assets.|例句：The army is redeploying its troops to the northern border.  军队正在将其部队重新部署到北部边境。

例句：The company is redeploying its resources to focus on its core business. 公司正在重新配置资源以专注于核心业务。


近义词：relocate, redistribute, reallocate, reposition

反义词：withdraw, remove


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They redeploy resources regularly. = 他们定期重新部署资源。
The company redeploys its staff based on project needs. = 公司根据项目需要重新部署员工。


一般过去时:
The military redeployed its forces after the battle. = 战斗结束后，军方重新部署了部队。
We redeployed the servers to improve performance. = 我们重新部署了服务器以提高性能。


现在完成时:
They have redeployed all the affected personnel. = 他们已经重新部署了所有受影响的人员。
The IT team has redeployed the application to a new server. = IT团队已将应用程序重新部署到新的服务器。


过去完成时:
The company had redeployed its marketing budget before the product launch. = 公司在产品发布之前已经重新部署了其营销预算。
They had redeployed the software before the system upgrade. = 他们已经在系统升级之前重新部署了软件。


将来时:
We will redeploy the team to a new project next week. = 我们下周将把团队重新部署到一个新的项目。
The company will redeploy its assets to expand into new markets. = 公司将重新部署其资产以拓展新市场。


虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we would redeploy them to the crisis area. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们将把它们重新部署到危机地区。
It would be wise to redeploy the troops if the situation deteriorates. = 如果情况恶化，重新部署军队将是明智之举。


被动语态:
The troops were redeployed to the front lines. = 部队被重新部署到前线。
The software is being redeployed to address the security vulnerability. = 正在重新部署软件以解决安全漏洞。


疑问句:
Will the company be redeploying its staff? = 公司会重新部署员工吗？
When are they planning to redeploy the servers? = 他们计划何时重新部署服务器？


否定句:
The company did not redeploy its resources effectively. = 公司没有有效地重新部署其资源。
They decided not to redeploy the team to that location. = 他们决定不将团队重新部署到那个地点。

词源:
re- (prefix indicating repetition or again) + deploy (from Old French *despleier, from des- + ployer "to fold, unfold," from Latin *plicare "to fold")
