ketching:/ˈkɛtʃɪŋ/ | v. | 捕获；抓住；理解；染上(疾病) | catching refers to the act of capturing something, usually something moving quickly. It can also mean understanding something quickly or becoming infected with a disease. |例句：The cat is catching a mouse.  猫正在抓老鼠。|例句： I'm catching a cold. 我感冒了。|近义词：grabbing, seizing, apprehending, catching on |反义词：releasing, letting go, missing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is catching the ball. 他正在接球。
She catches the bus every morning. 她每天早上都赶公共汽车。


一般过去时:
I caught a fish yesterday. 我昨天钓到了一条鱼。
They caught the thief red-handed. 他们当场抓住了小偷。


现在完成时:
I have caught three colds this year. 今年我已经感冒三次了。
She has caught up with her studies. 她已经赶上了她的学习。


过去完成时:
He had caught the train before the storm hit. 在暴风雨来临之前他已经赶上了火车。
They had caught the burglar before the police arrived. 在警察到达之前，他们已经抓住了窃贼。


将来时:
I will catch the next train. 我将乘坐下一班火车。
We will catch the sunrise tomorrow. 我们明天将赶上日出。


虚拟语气:
If I caught a fish, I would cook it. 如果我钓到鱼，我会把它煮了。
I wish I had caught that bus. 我真希望我赶上了那辆公共汽车。


被动语态:
The thief was caught by the police. 小偷被警察抓住了。
The ball was caught by the player. 球被球员接住了。


疑问句:
Did you catch what I said? 你听懂我说的了吗？
Are you catching a cold? 你感冒了吗？


否定句:
He didn't catch the ball. 他没有接住球。
I didn't catch your name. 我没记住你的名字。
