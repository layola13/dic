mesenteron:/ˌmɛsənˈtɛrɒn/| n. |中肠，中肠腔|The mesenteron is the midgut of an insect or other arthropod, the section of the alimentary canal between the foregut and the hindgut.  It is where most digestion and absorption of nutrients occur. |Example: The mesenteron of the locust showed significant changes in morphology after exposure to the pesticide.|近义词：midgut |反义词：foregut, hindgut
