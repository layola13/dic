intermediatory:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiˌeɪtəri/| adj., n.|中间的；调解人；中间体|Acting as a mediator or go-between; relating to or characterized by mediation.  A person or thing that acts as a mediator or intermediary.|The company acted as an intermediary between the buyer and the seller.  这家公司充当买卖双方之间的中间人。|近义词：mediator, go-between, intermediary agent|反义词：direct, principal
