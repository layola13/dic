damnableness:/ˈdæmnəblnis/| n. |该诅咒的性质；该受谴责的程度|The state or quality of being damnable; deserving of condemnation or damnation.  It refers to the degree to which something is deserving of strong disapproval or punishment.  The word emphasizes the severity and wickedness of the action or thing being described.|例句：The damnableness of his actions was evident to everyone.  他的行为之恶劣昭然若揭。

The damnableness of the situation was undeniable.  局势的糟糕程度不容否认。

|近义词：condemnableness, reprehensibility, culpability|反义词：praiseworthiness, commendableness|名词
