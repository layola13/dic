originates:/ˈɒrɪdʒɪneɪts/ /ˈɔːrɪdʒɪneɪts/| v.|起源于，发源于 | To begin or come into existence; to start or arise from a particular place, source, or cause. |例句：The river originates in the mountains. 这条河发源于群山。

例句：The tradition originates from ancient Greece.  这个传统起源于古希腊。


近义词：begins, starts, stems from, derives from, emanates from

反义词：ends, terminates, concludes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem originates in a lack of communication. = 这个问题源于沟通不足。
This custom originates in ancient China. = 这一习俗起源于古代中国。


一般过去时:
The fire originated in the kitchen. = 火灾起源于厨房。
The rumor originated with a single unreliable source. = 这个谣言来自一个不可靠的单一来源。


现在完成时:
The misunderstanding has originated from a simple error. = 误解源于一个简单的错误。
Many of our modern customs have originated from older traditions. = 我们的许多现代习俗都起源于古老的传统。


过去完成时:
It was discovered that the disease had originated in a nearby village. = 人们发现这种疾病起源于附近的村庄。
By the time we arrived, the argument had already originated from a trivial matter. = 我们到达时，争论已经由于一件琐事而开始了。


将来时:
The project will originate from a collaborative effort. = 该项目将源于合作努力。
The next fashion trend will originate in Paris, perhaps. = 下一个时尚潮流可能会起源于巴黎。


虚拟语气:
If the problem originated in a misunderstanding, it could be easily resolved. = 如果问题源于误解，它很容易解决。
I wish the conflict had not originated from such a petty argument. = 我希望冲突不是因为如此琐碎的争论而引起的。


被动语态:
The book is believed to have originated in the 17th century. = 这本书据信起源于17世纪。
The technology is being hailed as originating from a breakthrough in material science. = 这项技术被誉为材料科学突破的成果。


疑问句:
Where did this idea originate? = 这个想法起源于哪里？
Does the name originate from a specific family? = 这个名字起源于某个特定家族吗？


否定句:
The rumor did not originate from a credible source. = 这个谣言并非来自可靠的来源。
The problem does not originate in a lack of funding. = 这个问题并非源于资金不足。



PHRASE->
originate from = 源于...
originate in = 起源于...
originate with = 发源于...
trace its origins back to = 追溯其起源到...
the origin of = ...的起源
