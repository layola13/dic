modularly:/ˈmɒdʒʊləli/| adv.| 模块化地；按模块方式 |  In a modular manner;  using or relating to modules.  It describes something done or organized in a way that uses separate, interchangeable modules. |

例句：The software is designed to be modularly updated, allowing for easy integration of new features.  软件设计为模块化更新，便于轻松集成新功能。

The house was built modularly, with prefabricated sections assembled on-site. 这座房子是模块化建造的，预制部分在现场组装。


近义词：sectionally, segmentally


反义词：monolithically, integrally


词性:副词
