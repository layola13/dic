partaken:/pɑːrˈteɪkən/| v.|参与，分享，食用|Past participle of partake.  It means to participate in something, or to eat or drink something.|例句：He partook in the festivities. 他参与了庆祝活动。

They partook of the bread and wine. 他们分享了面包和葡萄酒。


近义词：participated, shared, consumed, enjoyed|反义词：abstained, avoided, refused
