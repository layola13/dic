crucified:/ˈkruːsɪfaɪd/| v. |钉死在十字架上；受极大痛苦；严厉批评|  Past participle of crucify.  To put someone to death by nailing or binding them to a cross.  It can also be used figuratively to describe someone experiencing intense suffering or harsh criticism.|例句：Jesus was crucified on a cross. 耶稣被钉死在十字架上。

He felt crucified by the criticism. 他感到被批评折磨得痛苦不堪。|近义词：executed, impaled, condemned, chastised|反义词：saved, rescued, praised, exonerated|
