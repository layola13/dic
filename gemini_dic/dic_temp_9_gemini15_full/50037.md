denotations: /ˌdɛnoʊˈteɪʃənz/ | n. |  词义；指称；含义 | The literal or dictionary meanings of a word, in contrast to its connotations.  The denotations of a word are its explicit, objective meanings, while the connotations are the implied, subjective, or emotional associations. | Example: The denotation of "home" is a place where one lives, but its connotation might be warmth, comfort, and family. | 近义词: meanings, definitions, significations | 反义词: connotations
