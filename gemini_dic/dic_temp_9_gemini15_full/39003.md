confirmingly:/kənˈfɜːrmɪŋli/ | adv. | 肯定地；确信地 |  In a way that confirms or shows certainty.  It expresses a manner of speaking or acting that confirms something previously stated or implied. | Example: He nodded confirmingly, his silence speaking volumes. 他肯定地点了点头，他的沉默说明了一切。  |近义词：certainly, assuredly, definitely | 反义词：doubtfully, uncertainly


USAGE->

She answers confirmingly to all questions. = 她对所有问题都肯定地回答。

PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases specifically using "confirmingly". The adverb is used to modify verbs and describes a manner of confirmation.
