runt:/rʌnt/| n.|矮小的人或动物，次品|A runt is a small person or animal, especially one that is much smaller than others of its kind or age. It can also refer to something of poor quality or inferior size.  |例句：The runt of the litter was much smaller than its siblings.  这窝幼崽中最小的一个比它的兄弟姐妹小得多。

The farmer culled the runt of the piglets. 农民淘汰了那些瘦小的猪崽。|近义词：undersized, dwarf, shrimp, small fry |反义词：giant, large, colossal, enormous
