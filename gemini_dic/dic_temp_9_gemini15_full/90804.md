humphing:/ˈhʌmfɪŋ/| v.|哼哼声，发出哼哼声 |The act of making a sound like a humph.  It often expresses displeasure, annoyance, or skepticism. |例句：She responded to his suggestion with a loud humphing sound. 她对他的建议哼了一声表示不满。  He was humphing to himself as he walked down the street. 他边哼哼着边走下街。|近义词：grumbling, snorting, muttering|反义词：praising, approving

He humphs whenever he disagrees. = 他每当不同意的时候就会哼哼。
She humphs a little tune while she works. = 她工作时哼着小曲。


一般过去时:
He humphed in response to her question. = 他对她的问题哼了一声回应。
She humphed dismissively when he suggested a movie. = 当他建议去看电影时，她不屑地哼了一声。
