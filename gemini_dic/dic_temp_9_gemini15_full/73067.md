footprint:/ˈfʊtprɪnt/ | n. |足迹；足印；影响；碳足迹 | The mark left by a foot, shoe, or other object on a surface.  It can also refer to the impact or effect of something. |例句：The footprints in the sand showed where the animal had walked. 沙滩上的脚印显示了动物走过的路线。  The company's carbon footprint is surprisingly low. 这家公司的碳足迹出奇地低。|近义词：track, mark, impact, influence |反义词：N/A


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The dog leaves footprints in the mud. = 狗在泥里留下脚印。
His work leaves a significant footprint on the industry. = 他的工作对这个行业产生了重大影响。

一般过去时:
The explorer left footprints on the untouched snow. = 探险家在未经触碰的雪地上留下了脚印。
The conference left a lasting footprint on environmental policy. = 会议对环境政策产生了持久的影响。

现在完成时:
We have reduced our carbon footprint significantly. = 我们已经大大减少了我们的碳足迹。
The project has left a positive footprint on the community. = 这个项目给社区留下了积极的影响。

过去完成时:
They had already left their footprints on the moon. = 他们已经在月球上留下了足迹。
The disaster had left a devastating footprint on the landscape. = 这场灾难给地貌留下了毁灭性的影响。

将来时:
We will minimize our environmental footprint in the future. = 我们将在未来尽量减少我们的环境足迹。
This new technology will leave a large footprint on the market. = 这项新技术将对市场产生巨大的影响。

虚拟语气:
If we acted now, we could reduce our carbon footprint dramatically. = 如果我们现在行动，我们就能大幅减少碳足迹。
I wish the company had a smaller footprint on the environment. = 我希望这家公司对环境的影响更小。

被动语态:
The footprints were quickly erased by the tide. = 脚印很快就被潮水冲走了。
The company's impact was felt across the globe; its footprint was undeniable. = 该公司的影响遍及全球；其影响是不可否认的。

疑问句:
What kind of footprint does this action leave? = 这种行为会留下什么影响？
What is the company’s carbon footprint? = 这家公司的碳足迹是多少？

否定句:
The suspect left no footprints. = 嫌疑人没有留下任何脚印。
The project did not leave a noticeable footprint on the economy. = 这个项目并没有对经济产生显著的影响。
