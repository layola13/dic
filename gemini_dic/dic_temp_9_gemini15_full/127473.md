necessitate:/nəˈsɛsɪteɪt/ | vt. | 使成为必要；需要 | To make something necessary.  It implies a strong need or requirement, often due to circumstances or conditions. | The urgency of the situation necessitated immediate action.  紧急情况需要立即采取行动。 |require, demand, call for | dispensable, unnecessary | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project necessitates a large budget. = 这个项目需要一笔巨额预算。
This situation necessitates careful consideration. = 这种情况需要仔细考虑。


一般过去时:
The storm necessitated the cancellation of the flight. = 暴风雨迫使航班取消。
Her illness necessitated a long period of rest. = 她的病需要长时间的休息。


现在完成时:
Recent events have necessitated a change in our plans. = 最近发生的事件使我们不得不改变计划。
The increased demand has necessitated the hiring of new staff. = 需求增加使得我们不得不雇佣新员工。


过去完成时:
The previous delays had necessitated a revision of the schedule. = 先前的延误迫使我们修改了时间表。
The lack of funding had necessitated a cutback in the research program. = 资金短缺迫使我们削减了研究项目。


将来时:
Future economic challenges will necessitate a reassessment of our strategies. = 未来的经济挑战将需要我们重新评估我们的战略。
Increased traffic will necessitate the construction of a new highway. = 交通量的增加将需要修建一条新的高速公路。


虚拟语气:
If the situation were different, it wouldn't necessitate such drastic measures. = 如果情况不同，就不需要采取如此严厉的措施。
It would necessitate a miracle to finish the project on time. = 要按时完成这个项目，需要奇迹发生。


被动语态:
Immediate action was necessitated by the crisis. = 危机迫使必须立即采取行动。
Further investigation is necessitated by the ambiguous results. = 模棱两可的结果需要进一步调查。


疑问句:
Does this really necessitate a complete overhaul of the system? = 这真的需要彻底检修系统吗？
What factors necessitate this decision? = 哪些因素使这个决定成为必要？


否定句:
The situation doesn't necessitate extreme measures. = 情况不需要采取极端措施。
This minor problem doesn't necessitate a meeting. = 这个问题不需开会讨论。


PHRASE->
