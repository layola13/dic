personifiers:/pərˈsɒnɪfaɪərz/ | n. |拟人者；赋予人格者 | Those who personify, giving human qualities to inanimate objects or abstract concepts.  They create vivid imagery and engage the reader's emotions. | Example: The poets are masterful personifiers, breathing life into the mountains and rivers in their verses.  那些诗人是技艺精湛的拟人化大师，赋予山河以生命。 | 近义词：anthropomorphizers, animators | 反义词：dehumanizers
