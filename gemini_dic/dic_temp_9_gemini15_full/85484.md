hassles:/ˈhæsəlz/ | n. |麻烦，困难，争吵 |  指令人不快或费力的事件或情况，也指争吵或冲突。 It refers to unpleasant or difficult events or situations, and also to arguments or conflicts. |例句：Moving house is always full of hassles.  搬家总是充满麻烦。

The constant hassles with the neighbors wore her down.  与邻居不断的争吵让她筋疲力尽。

近义词：troubles, problems, difficulties, annoyances, disputes | 反义词：ease, comfort, simplicity

The project involves a lot of hassles. 这个项目涉及很多麻烦。
