incommunicated: /ˌɪnkəmˈjuːnɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 无法沟通的；不通的；隔绝的 | Not communicated; uncommunicated.  Lacking communication or contact. Isolated. |


英文解析:  The word "incommunicated" describes a state of being cut off from communication or contact with others.  It suggests a lack of connection, often implying isolation or seclusion, either by choice or circumstance.


例句:

He remained incommunicated for days after the accident.  = 事故发生后，他与外界隔绝了好几天。

The island community was left incommunicated after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，岛上的社区与外界失去了联系。


近义词: isolated, secluded, cut off, unreachable, disconnected


反义词: connected, communicative, accessible, in touch


词性: adjective
