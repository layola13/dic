granddaddy:/ˈɡrændˌdædi/| n. |爷爷；(非正式)老家伙；祖先|A grandfather;  informally, an old man or something very old.  It can also refer to something that is the oldest or original version of something.|例句：My granddaddy told me many stories about his childhood.  我的爷爷给我讲了很多关于他童年的故事。  He's got a granddaddy car, a vintage model from the 1950s. 他有一辆老爷车，是20世纪50年代的古董车。|近义词：grandfather, ancestor, forefather |反义词：grandchild, descendant
