noncaffeine: /ˌnɒnˈkæfiːn/ | adj. | 不含咖啡因的 |  Containing no caffeine.  | Example:  I prefer noncaffeine drinks in the evening to avoid sleeplessness.  晚上我比较喜欢喝不含咖啡因的饮料，以免睡不着。 | 近义词: decaffeinated | 反义词: caffeinated


USAGE->
This word is an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  Therefore, tense, voice, and mood are not applicable.  Examples will demonstrate usage in different sentence structures.


Declarative Sentence:
The noncaffeine tea helped me relax. = 不含咖啡因的茶帮助我放松了。
This coffee is advertised as noncaffeine, but I still feel jittery. = 这款咖啡号称不含咖啡因，但我还是感觉有点紧张。


Interrogative Sentence:
Is this drink noncaffeine? = 这种饮料不含咖啡因吗？
Are there any noncaffeine options on the menu? = 菜单上有不含咖啡因的选择吗？


Negative Sentence:
The drink was not noncaffeine; it contained a significant amount of caffeine. = 这饮料并非不含咖啡因；它含有大量的咖啡因。
That's not a noncaffeine beverage. = 那不是不含咖啡因的饮料。
