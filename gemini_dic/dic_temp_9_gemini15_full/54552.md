dislocated:/ˈdɪsloʊkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 脱臼的；错位的；混乱的 |  Describes a joint that has been forced out of its normal position.  It can also describe something that is out of place or disordered. | Example: He suffered a dislocated shoulder in the accident. 他在事故中肩膀脱臼了。The political situation is completely dislocated. 政治局势完全混乱。|近义词：displaced, out of joint, disordered | 反义词：in place, aligned, ordered


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The bone is dislocated. = 骨头脱臼了。
His thinking is dislocated. = 他的思维混乱了。


一般过去时:
I dislocated my shoulder playing football. = 我踢足球时肩膀脱臼了。
The earthquake dislocated the railway line. = 地震使铁路线路错位了。


现在完成时:
He has dislocated his knee twice. = 他膝盖两次脱臼。
The changes have dislocated the entire industry. = 这些变化扰乱了整个行业。


过去完成时:
She had dislocated her ankle before the race. = 比赛前她脚踝已经扭伤了（脱臼了）。
The storm had dislocated the power grid. = 暴风雨已经打乱了电网系统。


将来时:
I think I will dislocate my finger if I keep doing this. = 我想如果我继续这样做，我会弄伤手指（脱臼）。
The new policy may dislocate the market. = 新政策可能会扰乱市场。


虚拟语气:
If I were to fall, I might dislocate my hip. = 如果我摔倒，我的髋关节可能会脱臼。
If the system were dislocated, it would cause chaos. = 如果系统混乱，将会造成混乱。


被动语态:
His jaw was dislocated during the fight. = 他在打斗中下巴脱臼了。
The schedule was dislocated by the unexpected delay. = 计划由于意外延误而被打乱了。


疑问句:
Is your shoulder dislocated? = 你的肩膀脱臼了吗？
Is the communication system dislocated? = 通信系统是否混乱？


否定句:
My knee is not dislocated. = 我的膝盖没有脱臼。
The company's operations are not dislocated. = 公司的运营没有混乱。
