hangers:/ˈhæŋərz/ | n. | 衣架；吊钩 | A hanger is a device, usually made of wire or plastic, used for hanging clothes.  | Example: I need more hangers for my closet. 我需要更多衣架来放我的衣服。  |近义词: clothes peg, hook | 反义词: None


USAGE->

Many hangers are needed for the new clothes. =  很多衣架才能挂下这些新衣服。
