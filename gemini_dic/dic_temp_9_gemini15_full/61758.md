empathies:/ˈɛmpəθiz/| n.|同感，同情，移情作用|The ability to understand and share the feelings of another.|例句：She showed great empathy for the victims of the earthquake. 她对地震的受害者表现出极大的同情。|近义词：sympathy, compassion, understanding|反义词：apathy, indifference, callousness|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shows empathy towards his colleagues. = 他对同事表现出同情心。
My friend expresses her empathy for my loss. = 我的朋友表达了她对我失去的同情。


一般过去时:
She empathized with the character in the novel. = 她对小说中的人物感同身受。
The audience felt empathy for the protagonist's struggles. = 观众对主人公的挣扎感同身受。


现在完成时:
I have felt deep empathy for homeless people since volunteering at the shelter. = 自从在收容所做志愿者以来，我对无家可归的人产生了深深的同情。
She has shown empathy in many situations throughout her career. = 在她职业生涯中，她在许多情况下都表现出了同情心。


过去完成时:
He had never felt such empathy for a stranger before. = 他以前从未对陌生人产生过如此强烈的同情心。
Having already experienced similar hardships, she had a great deal of empathy for their situation. = 因为已经经历过类似的苦难，她对他们的处境感同身受。


将来时:
I will try to show more empathy in my future interactions. = 我将在未来的交往中努力表现出更多的同情心。
She believes that future generations will have more empathy for different cultures. = 她认为子孙后代将对不同的文化有更多的同情心。


虚拟语气:
If I were in his shoes, I would feel more empathy for his actions. = 如果我站在他的立场上，我会对他所做的事情更有同情心。
I wish I had more empathy when dealing with conflict. = 我希望在处理冲突时我能更有同情心。


被动语态:
His empathy was felt by everyone in the room. = 房间里的每个人都能感受到他的同情心。
Empathy is often misunderstood as simply feeling sorry for someone. = 同情心经常被误解为仅仅是为某人感到抱歉。


疑问句:
Do you feel empathy for the characters in this movie? = 你对这部电影中的人物有同情心吗？
Can empathy be taught or is it an innate trait? = 同情心是可以教的吗，还是一种先天性特征？


否定句:
He showed no empathy towards his victims. = 他对他的受害者没有表现出任何同情心。
The lack of empathy in society is a growing concern. = 社会缺乏同情心是一个日益增长的担忧。
