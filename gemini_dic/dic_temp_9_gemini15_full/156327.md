physicianed:/fɪˈzɪʃənd/| v.|  (非正式) 诊治；治疗 | To treat or examine medically, often informally or without proper credentials.  This word is a back-formation from "physician," implying the act of a physician.  It carries a connotation of informality or perhaps even unauthorized medical practice.|例句：The old woman claimed she was physicianed by a traveling healer.  老妇人声称她接受过一位云游医生的治疗。


例句：He physicianed the injured child with some herbs. 他用草药治疗了受伤的孩子。

近义词：treated, doctored, medicated

反义词： neglected, ignored
