old-womanism:/ˈoʊld ˈwʊmənɪzəm/| n. |老年女性主义；对老年妇女的歧视|The term "old-womanism" refers to the prejudice and discrimination directed towards older women, often stemming from ageist and sexist beliefs.  It encompasses negative stereotypes, societal devaluation, and the invisibility of older women's experiences and contributions. This discrimination can manifest in various forms, including workplace ageism, healthcare disparities, and the underrepresentation of older women in media and politics. |The portrayal of older women in media often reinforces negative stereotypes, contributing to old-womanism.  |近义词：ageism, sexism, misogyny |反义词：respect for elders, gender equality
