enviable:/ˈɛnviəbl/| adj.|令人羡慕的，令人嫉妒的|Something that is enviable is so good that other people wish they had it.  It implies a feeling of admiration and perhaps a slight tinge of resentment.|Her success is truly enviable.|近义词：desirable, admirable, coveted|反义词：undesirable, lamentable


USAGE->

His lifestyle is enviable. = 他的生活方式令人羡慕。
Her new car is enviable. = 她的新车令人羡慕。
