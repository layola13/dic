emigrants:/ˈɛmɪɡrənts/| n. |移民（指移居国外者）|People who leave their native country to settle permanently in another.  Emigrants are often driven by factors such as political instability, economic hardship, or seeking better opportunities.|Many emigrants left Ireland during the potato famine.|近义词：expatriates, migrants|反义词：immigrants
