philia:/ˈfɪliə/| n. |嗜好；友爱；爱情|A strong liking for something or someone; fondness;  affection; love.  It often refers to a particular type of love or affection, often with a specific object or focus.  The term is often used in compound words to describe specific types of love or fondness.|例句：He has a philia for classic cars. 他酷爱老爷车。|近义词：fondness, affection, love, liking|反义词：hatred, aversion, dislike
