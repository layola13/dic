demonstrativeness:/ˌdɛmənˈstreɪtɪvnəs/| n. |夸示性；表现欲；爱炫耀；好表现|The quality of being demonstrative; the tendency to express feelings openly and freely.  It can refer to showing off or making a display of one's feelings, possessions, or abilities.  |例句：Her demonstrativeness was sometimes embarrassing to her children.  她的好表现有时让她的孩子们感到尴尬。

例句：His demonstrativeness towards his new girlfriend was quite obvious to everyone. 他对新女友的炫耀之情路人皆知。


近义词：expressiveness, effusiveness, ostentation, showiness|反义词：reserve, restraint, reticence|词性:名词


词源:  Derived from the word "demonstrative," which comes from the Latin "demonstrativus," meaning "to show" or "to point out."  The suffix "-ness" adds the quality or state of being.
