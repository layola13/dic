polyamorous:/ˌpɒliˈæmərəs/ | /ˌpɑːliˈæmərəs/ | adj. | 多角恋的，多重伴侣关系的 | Relating to or characterized by polyamory.  Polyamorous relationships involve more than two people in romantic or sexual relationships, with the knowledge and consent of all involved. | Example:  She is openly polyamorous and has multiple partners. 她公开承认自己是多角恋者，有多个伴侣。  |近义词: non-monogamous | 反义词: monogamous
