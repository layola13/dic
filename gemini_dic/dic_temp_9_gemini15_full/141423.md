ophiuroidea:/oʊˈfjʊrɔɪdiə/| n. |蛇尾纲动物|Ophiuroidea is a class of echinoderms, including brittle stars and basket stars.  They are characterized by their slender, flexible arms that are distinctly separate from the central disk. They are found in all the world's oceans, from shallow waters to the deep sea.| Example:  Many species of ophiuroidea are found in the deep-sea trenches.|近义词: brittle stars, basket stars|反义词: None specifically, but could be contrasted with other echinoderm classes like Asteroidea (sea stars).
