dwellers:/ˈdwɛlərz/| n. |居住者，居民|People or animals that live in a particular place.  The word refers to those who inhabit a specific area, whether it's a city, a building, or even a specific environment. |Example: The city dwellers enjoyed the vibrant nightlife. 城市居民享受着充满活力的夜生活。 The cave dwellers left behind fascinating artifacts. 穴居人留下了引人入胜的文物。|近义词：inhabitants, residents, occupants|反义词：visitors, transients

一般现在时:
The city dwellers enjoy the parks. = 城市居民喜欢公园。
These forest dwellers hunt for food daily. = 这些森林居民每天都狩猎食物。


一般过去时:
The cave dwellers lived a simple life. = 穴居人过着简朴的生活。
The building dwellers evacuated during the fire. = 建筑物居民在火灾期间撤离了。


现在完成时:
The mountain dwellers have adapted to the harsh climate. = 山区居民已经适应了恶劣的气候。
The city dwellers have witnessed many changes over the years. = 城市居民多年来见证了许多变化。


过去完成时:
The island dwellers had already harvested their crops before the storm. = 岛屿居民在暴风雨来临之前已经收割了庄稼。
The forest dwellers had never seen such a large animal before. = 森林居民以前从未见过这么大的动物。


将来时:
The apartment dwellers will move out next month. = 公寓居民下个月将搬出去。
The desert dwellers will face water shortages in the future. = 沙漠居民将来将面临缺水问题。


虚拟语气:
If I were a city dweller, I would use public transportation. = 如果我是城市居民，我会使用公共交通工具。
I wish I were a forest dweller, living in peace and quiet. = 我希望我是一个森林居民，过着平静的生活。


被动语态:
The needs of the urban dwellers are often overlooked. = 城市居民的需求往往被忽视。
The concerns of the rural dwellers were addressed by the government. = 政府解决了农村居民的担忧。


疑问句:
Are the building dwellers satisfied with their living conditions? = 建筑物居民对他们的居住条件满意吗？
How do the desert dwellers survive in such harsh conditions? = 沙漠居民如何在如此恶劣的条件下生存？


否定句:
The apartment dwellers are not happy with the noise levels. = 公寓居民对噪音水平不满意。
The villagers are not accustomed to city life. = 村民们不习惯城市生活。
