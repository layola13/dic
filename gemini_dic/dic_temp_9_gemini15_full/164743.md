USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preferably works from home. = 他最好在家工作。
She preferably chooses organic vegetables. = 她最好选择有机蔬菜。


一般过去时:
They preferably stayed in a quiet hotel. = 他们最好住在一个安静的酒店。
He preferably walked to work that day. = 他那天最好是步行上班。


现在完成时:
I have preferably finished all my work. = 我最好已经完成了我的所有工作。
She has preferably read the entire book. = 她最好已经读完了整本书。


过去完成时:
They had preferably completed the project before the deadline. = 他们最好在截止日期前完成了项目。
He had preferably arrived early for the meeting. = 他最好早到了会议。


将来时:
We will preferably travel by train. = 我们最好坐火车旅行。
They will preferably be there by noon. = 他们最好在中午之前到达那里。


虚拟语气:
I would preferably have a cup of tea. = 我最好喝杯茶。
It would preferably be sunny tomorrow. = 明天最好是晴天。


被动语态:
The work is preferably done by professionals. = 这项工作最好由专业人士完成。
The decision is preferably made by the committee. = 这个决定最好由委员会做出。


疑问句:
Would you preferably eat dinner now or later? = 你最好现在还是晚点吃晚饭？
Would they preferably take the bus or the train? = 他们最好是坐公共汽车还是火车？


否定句:
I would not preferably go there alone. = 我最好不要独自去那里。
She would not preferably miss the important meeting. = 她最好不要错过重要的会议。
