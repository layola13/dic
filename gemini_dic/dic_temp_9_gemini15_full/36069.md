coeducation:/ˌkoʊˌɛdʒuˈkeɪʃən/| n. |男女同校教育；男女合校|The system of educating male and female students together in the same school or class.  Coeducation challenges traditional gender roles and promotes equality. |Many schools now practice coeducation, offering a diverse learning environment.|近义词：mixed education, integrated education |反义词：segregated education, single-sex education
