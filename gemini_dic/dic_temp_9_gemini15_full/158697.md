plotter:/ˈplɒtər/| n. |绘图仪；阴谋者；策划者 |A plotter is a machine that produces drawings, graphs, or other visual representations from computer data.  It can also refer to a person who makes secret plans, especially to do something harmful. |例句：The architect used a plotter to create detailed blueprints for the building. 建筑师用绘图仪绘制了建筑物的详细蓝图。

The plotter was arrested for his involvement in the assassination attempt.  策划者因参与暗杀未遂而被捕。|近义词：machine, conspirator, planner, schemer |反义词：collaborator (in the sense of working openly), innocent
