conformably:/kənˈfɔːrməbli/ | adv. | 一致地；符合地；顺从地 | In a way that conforms to a standard, rule, or expectation.  It indicates agreement or compliance. | The new building was designed conformably to the local building codes. 新建筑的设计符合当地建筑规范。 The experiment was conducted conformably with established protocols. 实验按照既定的规程进行。 |  accordingly, consistently, in accordance with | unconformably, disobediently | 副词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He acts conformably to his beliefs. 他按照自己的信念行事。
They always dress conformably to the occasion. 他们总是穿着符合场合的衣服。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:
