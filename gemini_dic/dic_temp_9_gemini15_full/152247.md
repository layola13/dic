patronee:/ˌpætrəˈniː/| n. |被资助者，受庇护者 |A person who is supported or protected by a patron.  This term often implies a more formal or institutional relationship than simply being a recipient of charity.  It suggests a degree of dependence and obligation on the part of the patronee. |例句：The young artist was a patronee of the wealthy art collector.  这位年轻的艺术家是那位富有的艺术收藏家的被资助者。

The scholarship recipient felt a sense of obligation to her patronee.  奖学金获得者对她的资助者感到一种责任感。|近义词： protégé, beneficiary, dependent |反义词： patron, benefactor
