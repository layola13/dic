outvie:/aʊtˈvaɪ/| vt.|胜过；超过|To surpass or exceed in excellence, achievement, or influence.|例句：Her talent outvied all the others in the competition.  她的才能胜过比赛中的所有其他人。|例句：His ambition outvied his common sense. 他的野心超过了他的常识。|近义词：surpass, excel, exceed, outdo|反义词：be inferior to, be surpassed by


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new design outvies the old one in efficiency. = 新的设计在效率上胜过旧的设计。
His performance outvies all expectations. = 他的表现超出了所有人的预期。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
