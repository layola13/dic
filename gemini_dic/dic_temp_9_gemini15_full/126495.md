myxinoid:/ˈmɪksɪnɔɪd/ | adj. | 盲鳗样的；黏盲鳗科的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Myxiniformes, an order of jawless fishes including hagfish.  Myxinoids are characterized by their eel-like body, slime production, and parasitic lifestyle. | Example: The myxinoid's slime is a potent defense mechanism. 盲鳗的粘液是一种有效的防御机制。 | 近义词:hagfish-like | 反义词:  None readily available.  (Antonyms would describe features *opposite* to those of myxinoids, not the myxinoid condition itself.)
