nonoriginal: /ˌnɒnəˈrɪdʒɪnəl/ | adj. | 非原创的；非原生的 | Not original; not of the original kind or source.  It implies a lack of originality or novelty, often suggesting a copy or imitation. | 例句：The painting is a nonoriginal; it's a copy of a famous masterpiece. 这幅画是赝品；它是名作的复制品。

The essay was criticized for its nonoriginal ideas.  这篇论文因其缺乏原创性而受到批评。


近义词：derivative, unoriginal, copied, imitated, second-hand


反义词：original, innovative, novel, groundbreaking, pioneering


词性：adjective


词源:  The word is a combination of the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "original".  "Original" itself comes from the Old French word "original," ultimately deriving from the Latin "originalis," meaning "belonging to the origin."
