nonintimidating: /ˌnɒnɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不吓人的，不威胁的，使人感到轻松的 | Not causing fear or apprehension; reassuring.  It describes something or someone that is unlikely to make someone feel afraid or uncomfortable. | 例句：The teacher created a nonintimidating classroom environment.  老师创造了一个轻松的课堂环境。

The new manager has a nonintimidating approach to leadership. 新经理采用了一种不吓人的领导方式。


近义词： reassuring, comforting, friendly, approachable, welcoming

反义词： intimidating, threatening, frightening, daunting, scary
