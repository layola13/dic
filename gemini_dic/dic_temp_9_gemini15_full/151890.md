passportless:/ˈpɑːspɔːrtlɪs/ | adj. | 无护照的；不需护照的 | Without a passport.  This adjective describes a person or situation lacking a passport.  | Example:  The agreement allows for passportless travel between the two countries.  这个协议允许两国之间免护照旅行。


The new initiative aims to create a passportless world. 这项新举措旨在创造一个无护照的世界。


近义词：visa-free (in the context of travel), without a passport, undocumented (in certain contexts)


反义词：passport-holding, with a passport


词性：形容词
