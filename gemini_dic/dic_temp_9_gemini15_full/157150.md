pineapples:/ˈpaɪnˌæpəlz/| n. |菠萝|A tropical plant with a large fruit that is edible and juicy, typically having a rough, spiky skin and yellow flesh.  Pineapples are a popular fruit enjoyed worldwide for their sweet and tangy flavor.|例句：I bought some fresh pineapples at the market. 我在市场上买了一些新鲜的菠萝。|例句：Pineapples are a good source of vitamin C.菠萝是维生素C的良好来源。|近义词：ananas|反义词：无
