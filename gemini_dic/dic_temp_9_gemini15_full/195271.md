semifunctionally:/ˌsemiˈfʌŋkʃənəli/| adv. |半功能地，部分功能地 |  Describes something that functions partially or incompletely.  It implies a level of functionality, but not to its full extent.  | The system is operating semifunctionally after the power surge.  系统在电源浪涌后部分功能正常运行。 |  partially, incompletely, suboptimally | fully functionally, completely | 副词
