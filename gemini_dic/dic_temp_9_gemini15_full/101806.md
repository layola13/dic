irritable-bowel-syndromes: /ˌɪrɪtəbəlˈbaʊəl ˈsɪndrəʊmz/ | n. | 肠易激综合征 | Irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) is a group of symptoms that affect the large intestine (colon) and cause abdominal pain, cramping, bloating, gas, and changes in bowel habits.  The exact cause of IBS is unknown, but it's thought to be related to problems with the way the gut and brain communicate.  |  She suffers from irritable bowel syndromes, experiencing frequent abdominal pain and changes in bowel movements. 她患有肠易激综合征，经常腹痛和排便习惯改变。 |  functional bowel disorder, spastic colon |  (无明确反义词，可以考虑healthy gut) | n.
