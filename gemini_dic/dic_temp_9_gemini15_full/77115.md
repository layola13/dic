garth:/ɡɑːrθ/| n.|围场；院子；花园 |An enclosed area of land, especially one surrounding a building.  A garth is typically a courtyard or a garden, often associated with older buildings or historical sites.  |例句：The old manor house boasted a beautiful, walled garth. 这座古老的庄园拥有一个美丽的围墙花园。
The abbey's garth was a peaceful oasis in the bustling town.  修道院的院子是繁华小镇中一片宁静的绿洲。|近义词：courtyard, yard, enclosure|反义词：open field, expanse
