fertilize:/ˈfɜːrtəlaɪz/ | vt. |施肥；使肥沃；使受精 | To make (land) fertile or productive; to apply fertilizer to (land). To make (an egg or ovum) capable of developing into a new organism by uniting it with a sperm; to cause (an ovum) to develop into a new organism. |例句：Farmers fertilize their fields to increase crop yields.  农民给田地施肥以提高作物产量。 The sperm fertilizes the egg. 精子使卵子受精。|近义词：enrich, impregnate, inseminate |反义词：sterilize, deplete
