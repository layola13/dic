noninvestor: /ˌnɑːnɪnˈvɛstər/ | n. |非投资者 | A person or entity that does not invest in something, typically stocks, bonds, or other financial assets.  This term often contrasts with "investor," highlighting the lack of participation in financial markets or specific investment opportunities. | Example:  The noninvestors were largely unaffected by the stock market crash.  非投资者们基本上没有受到股市崩盘的影响。| 近义词: nonparticipant, bystander | 反义词: investor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a noninvestor in the tech industry. 他不是科技行业的投资者。
They are noninvestors in this particular project. 他们不是这个项目的投资者。

一般过去时:
She was a noninvestor before she started her own business. 在她自己创业之前，她不是投资者。
He was a noninvestor in the real estate market. 他不是房地产市场的投资者。

现在完成时:
They have been noninvestors throughout their careers. 他们职业生涯中一直都不是投资者。
She has remained a noninvestor despite the booming economy. 尽管经济蓬勃发展，她仍然不是投资者。

过去完成时:
He had been a noninvestor until he received a large inheritance. 直到他继承了一大笔遗产，他才成为投资者。
They had been noninvestors for many years before deciding to invest. 在决定投资之前，他们多年来一直都不是投资者。

将来时:
She will likely remain a noninvestor for the foreseeable future. 在可预见的未来，她可能会继续保持非投资者的身份。
They will continue to be noninvestors in high-risk ventures. 他们将继续不会投资高风险的项目。

虚拟语气:
If I were a noninvestor, I wouldn't worry about market fluctuations. 如果我不是投资者，我就不会担心市场波动。
I wish I had been a noninvestor during the last recession. 我希望在上次经济衰退期间我不是投资者。

被动语态:
Noninvestors were not consulted during the decision-making process. 在决策过程中没有咨询非投资者。
The needs of noninvestors are often overlooked. 非投资者的需求往往被忽视。

疑问句:
Are you a noninvestor in the stock market? 你是不是股市中的非投资者？
Were they noninvestors before the company went public? 在公司上市之前，他们是不是非投资者？

否定句:
He is not a noninvestor; he actively participates in the market. 他不是非投资者；他积极参与市场。
They are not noninvestors; they have significant investments in several companies. 他们不是非投资者；他们在几家公司有大量投资。
