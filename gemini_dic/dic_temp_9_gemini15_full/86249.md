heaved:/hiːvd/| vt.|用力举起；抛；呕吐；喘息|To lift or throw something heavy with great effort; to vomit; to breathe heavily and with difficulty. |例句：He heaved the heavy box onto the truck. 他费力地把沉重的箱子搬到卡车上。
He heaved a sigh of relief. 他长舒了一口气。|近义词：lift, hoist, toss, throw, vomit |反义词：lower, set down


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He heaves the bag onto his shoulder. = 他把包用力扛到肩上。
She heaves a sigh of relief. = 她长舒一口气。


一般过去时:
He heaved the anchor. = 他起锚了。
The exhausted runner heaved for breath. = 精疲力尽的跑步者大口喘气。


现在完成时:
He has heaved the old furniture to the curb. = 他已将旧家具扔到路边了。
She has heaved several times this morning. = 她今天早上呕吐了好几次。


过去完成时:
He had heaved the stone before he fell. = 他在摔倒之前已经用力扔出了石头。
They had heaved the boat onto the shore. = 他们已经把船拖到岸上了。


将来时:
He will heave the sack of potatoes into the truck. = 他将会把那袋土豆搬上卡车。
She will heave with exhaustion after the marathon. = 马拉松过后她会累得喘不过气。


虚拟语气:
If he had heaved the box properly, it wouldn't have fallen. = 如果他把箱子搬得更稳妥些，它就不会掉下来了。
I wish I hadn't heaved up all that food last night. = 我真希望昨晚没吃那么多东西。


被动语态:
The heavy stone was heaved into the air. = 那块大石头被用力抛向空中。
The anchor was heaved up by the sailors. = 锚被水手们起锚了。


疑问句:
Did he heave the anchor? = 他起锚了吗？
Has she heaved lately? = 她最近呕吐过吗？


否定句:
He didn't heave the rock far enough. = 他没有把石头扔得够远。
She couldn't heave the suitcase onto the luggage rack. = 她无法把行李箱搬到行李架上。
