词源:
The word "foot" comes from the Old English word "fōt," which has cognates in other Germanic languages (e.g., German "Fuß," Dutch "voet").  It ultimately traces back to Proto-Indo-European *ped- meaning "foot."  Many words related to feet and walking share this root.
