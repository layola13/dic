The term often arises in historical or political contexts, particularly during periods of conflict or territorial disputes involving Alsace. The term can be used neutrally, but can also carry connotations depending on the context. | Example:  During the Franco-Prussian War, public opinion was sharply divided, with some being strongly pro-Alsatians and others fiercely pro-French.  | 近义词:  Alsace supporters, Germanophiles (in relevant contexts) | 反义词:  anti-Alsatian, Francophile (in relevant contexts)
