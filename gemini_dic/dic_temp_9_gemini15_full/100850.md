introspection:/ˌɪntrəˈspɛkʃən/| n. |内省，反省 |The examination of one's own thoughts and feelings.  It involves looking inward to understand oneself better. |例句：Through introspection, she realized the source of her anxiety. 通过内省，她意识到了自己焦虑的根源。|近义词：self-examination, self-reflection, contemplation |反义词：obliviousness, unawareness
