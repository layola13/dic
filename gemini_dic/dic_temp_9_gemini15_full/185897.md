revolutionary: /ˌrevəˈluːʃənəri/ | adj. | 革命的；革命性的；创新的 |  A revolutionary change or idea is very important and causes great change.  It often implies a radical or drastic shift. |例句：The invention of the internet was a revolutionary moment in human history.  互联网的发明是人类历史上具有革命意义的时刻。

例句：His revolutionary ideas transformed the company's approach to marketing.  他革命性的想法改变了公司对营销的策略。


近义词：radical, groundbreaking, transformative, innovative, pioneering | 反义词：conservative, traditional, conventional | 词性：形容词
