ill-assorted:/ˌɪl əˈsɔːtɪd/ | adj. | 不相称的；搭配不当的；种类繁多的 | poorly matched or assorted; incongruous; heterogeneous.  The term implies a lack of harmony or suitability between the elements involved. |  例句：The ill-assorted group of students struggled to work together.  这群搭配不当的学生难以合作。

The ill-assorted furniture in the room created a chaotic atmosphere.  房间里不相称的家具营造出一种混乱的氛围。|近义词：mismatched, incongruous, disparate, heterogeneous |反义词：well-matched, harmonious, compatible, homogeneous |词性：形容词
