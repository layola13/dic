pollen-analytically: /ˈpɑːlən ænəˈlɪtɪkli/ | adj. | 花粉分析地 |  Relating to or employing pollen analysis;  in a manner characteristic of pollen analysis.  The study of pollen grains in sediments to reconstruct past vegetation and climates. | 例句：The pollen-analytically derived data showed a significant shift in vegetation during the late glacial period.  花粉分析得出的数据表明，在晚冰期植被发生了显著变化。


例句：The lake sediments were analyzed pollen-analytically to determine past environmental changes.  对湖泊沉积物进行了花粉分析，以确定过去的環境变化。


近义词：palynologically (more formal)


反义词：无


词性：形容词
