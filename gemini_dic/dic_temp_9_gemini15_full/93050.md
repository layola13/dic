It suggests a degree of uncritical devotion and potentially negative consequences from this devotion.|例句：He became an idolastre of the rock star, neglecting his studies and responsibilities. 他成了那位摇滚巨星的狂热崇拜者，忽视了他的学业和责任。|近义词：fanatic, devotee, worshipper |反义词：critic, skeptic, iconoclast |名词
