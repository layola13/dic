mailed:/meɪld/ | v. | 邮寄；发送邮件 | To send something by mail.  | I mailed the letter yesterday. 我昨天寄了信。  He mailed the package to his friend. 他把包裹寄给了他的朋友。 | sent, posted | received | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I mail letters every week. = 我每周都寄信。
She mails her rent payment online. = 她在线支付房租。

一般过去时:
He mailed the package last Friday. = 他上周五寄了包裹。
They mailed the invitations a month ago. = 他们一个月前寄出了邀请函。

现在完成时:
I have mailed the documents already. = 我已经寄出文件了。
She has mailed him several emails. = 她已经给他发了多封邮件。

过去完成时:
He had mailed the report before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经寄出了报告。
They had mailed the package before realizing the address was wrong. = 他们寄出包裹后才意识到地址错了。

将来时:
I will mail the check tomorrow. = 我明天会寄支票。
She will mail the application next week. = 她下周会寄申请表。

虚拟语气:
If I had mailed the letter earlier, it would have arrived on time. = 如果我早点寄信，它就能按时到达了。
I wish I had mailed the package sooner. = 我真希望我早点寄出包裹。

被动语态:
The letter was mailed yesterday. = 信件是昨天寄出的。
The package is being mailed today. = 包裹今天正在邮寄。

疑问句:
Did you mail the application? = 你寄申请表了吗？
Has she mailed the payment yet? = 她寄付款了吗？

否定句:
I didn't mail the letter. = 我没有寄信。
He hasn't mailed the package yet. = 他还没寄包裹。
