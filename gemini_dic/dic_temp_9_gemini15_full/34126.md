clade:/kleɪd/| n. |进化枝，支系|A clade is a group of organisms believed to have evolved from a common ancestor, including all descendants of that ancestor.  Clades are determined using phylogenetic analysis, which examines shared characteristics to infer evolutionary relationships.|Example:  The primates form a clade that includes monkeys, apes, and humans. 灵长类动物构成一个包括猴子、猿和人类的进化枝。|近义词：group, lineage, taxon|反义词：N/A
