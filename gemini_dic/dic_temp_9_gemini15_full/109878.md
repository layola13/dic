liabilities:/ˌlaɪəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 负债；债务；责任 |  Liabilities are a company's financial obligations or debts.  They represent what a company owes to others. This includes things like loans, accounts payable, and deferred revenue.  |  例句：The company's liabilities exceeded its assets. 这家公司的负债超过了资产。

例句：  He accepted full liability for the accident. 他对这起事故负全部责任。


近义词：debts, obligations, responsibilities | 反义词：assets


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company's liabilities are high this quarter. = 本季度公司的负债很高。
His liabilities include several outstanding loans. = 他的负债包括几笔未偿还的贷款。


一般过去时:
Their liabilities were significantly lower last year. = 他们去年的负债明显较低。
The court found him liable for the damages. = 法院认定他对损害赔偿负有责任。


现在完成时:
The company has accumulated substantial liabilities. = 公司已经积累了巨额债务。
She has accepted liability for the broken vase. = 她已经承担了打破花瓶的责任。


过去完成时:
Before the merger, their liabilities had been manageable. = 在合并之前，他们的负债是可以控制的。
He had already assumed liability for the project's failures. = 他已经承担了项目失败的责任。


将来时:
Their liabilities will likely increase next year. = 他们的负债明年可能会增加。
The company will be liable for any future damages. = 公司将对任何未来损害负责。


虚拟语气:
If the company had fewer liabilities, it would be more profitable. = 如果公司负债较少，它将更赚钱。
I wish I hadn't incurred so many liabilities. = 我真希望我没有承担这么多债务。


被动语态:
The liabilities were carefully assessed by the auditors. = 负债由审计员仔细评估。
Liability for the accident was assigned to the driver. = 事故责任被归咎于司机。


疑问句:
What are the company's total liabilities? = 公司的总负债是多少？
Are there any undisclosed liabilities? = 有任何未公开的负债吗？


否定句:
The company has no significant liabilities. = 公司没有重大负债。
He is not liable for the damages. = 他对损害赔偿不负责任。


PHRASE->
current liabilities = 流动负债
long-term liabilities = 长期负债
limited liability = 有限责任
strict liability = 严格责任
liability insurance = 责任保险

词源:
From Old French *liabilitē, from liable + -ité.  Ultimately from Latin līgāre "to bind".  The sense evolution reflects the binding nature of debt and responsibility.
