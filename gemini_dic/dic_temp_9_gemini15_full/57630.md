draughtsboard:/ˈdrɑːftsbɔːrd/|/ˈdrɑːftsbɔːrd/| n. |跳棋棋盘；方格图案|A draughtsboard is a board used to play the game of draughts (checkers).  It is typically made of wood or other material and has 64 squares arranged in an 8x8 grid, alternating in color (usually black and white or dark and light).|The children were engrossed in a game of draughts on the draughtsboard.|chessboard, checkerboard|  (no direct antonym, but perhaps "plain surface")|名词
