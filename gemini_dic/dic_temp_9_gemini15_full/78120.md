geomancer:/ˈdʒiːoʊmænsər/| n. |地相家，风水师|A geomancer is a person who practices geomancy, a divination method that interprets patterns in the earth or other natural phenomena to predict the future or gain insight into events. Geomancers use various techniques, such as observing patterns in soil, rocks, or constellations, to make their interpretations.| The geomancer predicted a prosperous year for the village based on the arrangement of stones.|近义词：diviner, fortune teller, augur|反义词：N/A
