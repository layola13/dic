distraughtly:/dɪˈstrɔːtli/| adv.| 忧心忡忡地；心烦意乱地 | in a distraught manner; showing extreme distress and agitation.  |例句：She paced the floor distraughtly, wringing her hands. 她焦虑不安地在房间里踱步，绞着手。|例句：He spoke distraughtly about the accident. 他忧心忡忡地谈论了那场事故。|近义词：frantically, wildly, anxiously, desperately|反义词：calmly, peacefully, serenely|副词
