lyricists:/ˈlɪrɪsɪsts/| n. |作词家；填词人|A lyricist is a person who writes the lyrics (words) of songs.  They are often involved in the creative process of music production, collaborating with composers and musicians.|The lyricist for that song is incredibly talented.  那位歌曲的作词人非常有才华。|songwriters, poets|prose writers|名词
