jawbreakingly:/ˈdʒɔːˌbreɪkɪŋli/| adj.|令人难以置信的，难以发音的，极其困难的 | Extremely difficult to pronounce or understand; incredibly difficult.|例句：The name of that chemical compound is jawbreakingly difficult to pronounce.  那个化合物的名称难以置信的难以发音。

例句：The instructions were jawbreakingly complex, even for an experienced engineer.  即使对于经验丰富的工程师来说，这些说明也极其复杂。


近义词：unpronounceable,  impossibly difficult, extremely complex, mind-bogglingly difficult

反义词：easy, simple, straightforward


词源：源自"jawbreaker" (下巴碎裂者)，指一种又硬又大的糖果，难以咀嚼，引申为难以处理或理解的事物。  The "-ingly" suffix transforms the noun into an adverb, modifying the adjective or verb it describes.
