manx:/mæŋks/| adj. n.|曼岛的；曼岛语的；曼岛猫的|Of or relating to the Isle of Man, its people, or its language.  A breed of tailless cat originating from the Isle of Man.|例句：The Manx cat is known for its lack of tail. 曼岛猫以无尾而闻名。
