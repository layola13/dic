half-woven: /ˈhæfˌwoʊvən/ | adj. | 半织的，部分编织的 | Partially woven; not completely finished in the weaving process.  Describes textiles or fabrics that are only partly woven, often leaving some areas unfinished or with a loose structure. | Example: The half-woven tapestry showed the outline of a knight, but the details were still incomplete.  半织的挂毯显示出一位骑士的轮廓，但细节仍不完整。 | 近义词: partly woven, incompletely woven | 反义词: fully woven, completely woven
