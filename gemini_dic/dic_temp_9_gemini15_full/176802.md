readmission:/ˌriːædˈmɪʃən/| n. |重新接纳；复学；再次入院|The act of admitting someone or something again, especially after a period of exclusion or suspension.  This often refers to being allowed back into a school, hospital, or organization. |例句：The university considered his application for readmission. 大学考虑了他的复学申请。

例句： After a period of rehabilitation, she was granted readmission to the hospital for further treatment. 康复一段时间后，她被允许再次入院接受进一步治疗。


近义词：re-entry, reinstatement, acceptance


反义词：expulsion, dismissal, rejection


词性：名词
