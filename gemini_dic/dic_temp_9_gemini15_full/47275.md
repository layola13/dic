de-automating: /ˌdiːˈɔːtəmeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 去自动化；取消自动化 | The process of removing automation from a system or process.  It involves reverting to manual or semi-manual methods.  This might be done to improve quality control, address unforeseen issues caused by automation, or to reduce costs associated with maintaining automated systems. | 例句：The factory is de-automating its production line to improve product quality. 工厂正在取消其生产线的自动化以提高产品质量。

例句：The company decided to de-automate the data entry process after discovering several errors caused by the automated system. 公司在发现自动化系统导致的几个错误后，决定取消数据录入过程的自动化。


近义词：disautomate,  manualize,  demechanize


反义词：automate, mechanize


词性:动词
