picnicker:/ˈpɪknɪkər/| n. |野餐者；参加野餐的人|A person who participates in a picnic.  Picnickers often bring their own food and drinks.|例句：The picnickers spread their blankets on the grass.  野餐者们在草地上铺开毯子。|例句：Many picnickers gathered by the lake.  许多野餐者聚集在湖边。|近义词：picnicker, excursionist|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The picnicker eats a sandwich. = 野餐者吃三明治。
She brings a blanket to the picnic. = 她带了一条毯子去野餐。

一般过去时:
The picnickers played games after lunch. = 野餐者们午餐后玩游戏。
He brought a frisbee to the picnic yesterday. = 他昨天带了一个飞盘去野餐。

现在完成时:
They have finished their picnic. = 他们已经完成了野餐。
We have eaten all the snacks. = 我们已经吃掉了所有的零食。

过去完成时:
The children had already eaten before the adults arrived. = 孩子们在大人们到达之前就已经吃过了。
They had set up the picnic blanket before the rain started. = 下雨开始之前，他们已经铺好了野餐毯。

将来时:
We will have a picnic in the park tomorrow. = 我们明天将在公园野餐。
She will pack a lunch for the picnic. = 她将为野餐准备午餐。

虚拟语气:
If I were a picnicker, I would bring a kite. = 如果我是野餐者，我会带一个风筝。
I wish I had brought more drinks to the picnic. = 我真希望我带了更多饮料去野餐。

被动语态:
The picnic basket was packed by her mother. = 野餐篮是她母亲准备的。
The picnic area was cleaned up after everyone left. = 大家离开后，野餐区被清理干净了。

疑问句:
Are you a picnicker? = 你是野餐者吗？
