polyphone:/ˈpɒlifoʊn/ /ˈpɑːlifoʊn/| n. |多音词|A word that has more than one pronunciation.|The word “read,” for example, is a polyphone because it can be pronounced /riːd/ (present tense) or /red/ (past tense).|近义词：homophone (if the spellings are different)|反义词：monophone
