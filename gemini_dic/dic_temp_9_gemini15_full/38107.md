compounders:/kəmˈpaʊndərz/| n.|混合物制造者；复合词构成者|Those who or that which combines or mixes different elements to create a new substance or word.  Compounders can refer to individuals, machines, or processes involved in this combination.|例句：The pharmaceutical company employs skilled compounders to create new medications. 制药公司雇佣熟练的混合物制造者来制造新药。The linguist analyzed the compounders of the ancient language. 语言学家分析了古代语言的复合词构成者。|近义词：mixers, combiners, blenders |反义词：separators, dividers
