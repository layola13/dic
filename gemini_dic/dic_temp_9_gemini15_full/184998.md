retention: /rɪˈtɛnʃən/ | n. | 保留；保持；记忆力 | The act of keeping something, especially a quality, skill, or ability.  It can also refer to the ability to remember things. | 例句：The company's retention rate for new employees is surprisingly high. = 这家公司新员工的留存率出奇地高。

The retention of information is crucial for successful learning. = 保持信息对于成功的学习至关重要。

近义词：preservation, keeping, memory, recall | 反义词：loss, release, forgetfulness

词源:  From Old French *retencion, from retenir "to retain," from re- "back" + tenir "to hold."  The ultimate origin is from the Latin tenere "to hold."
