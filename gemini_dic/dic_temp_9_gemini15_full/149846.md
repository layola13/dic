pales:/peɪlz/| v.  adj. | 变得苍白；显得逊色 | to become or appear pale; to lose its color or intensity; to seem insignificant in comparison |例句：His face paled when he heard the news.  他听到这个消息后脸色变得苍白。 |近义词：fade, blanch, weaken |反义词：flourish, brighten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun pales the colors of the painting. = 太阳使油画的颜色褪色。
Her beauty pales beside her sister's. = 和她姐姐相比，她的美貌逊色了。


一般过去时:
His courage paled in the face of danger. = 面对危险，他的勇气消失了。
The vibrant colors paled after exposure to the sun. = 暴露在阳光下后，鲜艳的颜色褪去了。


现在完成时:
The excitement has paled since the event ended. = 事件结束后，兴奋感已经减退了。
His confidence has paled considerably after the setback. = 挫折之后，他的信心大大减弱了。


过去完成时:
His hopes had paled considerably before the final results were announced. = 在最终结果公布之前，他的希望已经大大减弱了。
The initial enthusiasm had paled by the time the project reached its conclusion. = 到项目结束时，最初的热情已经减退了。


将来时:
His joy will pale when he discovers the truth.  = 当他发现真相时，他的快乐将会消失。
The impact of the news will pale with time. = 消息的影响力会随着时间的推移而减弱。


虚拟语气:
If he had known the truth, his enthusiasm would have paled. = 如果他知道真相，他的热情就会减退。
I wish my initial excitement hadn’t paled so quickly. = 我希望我最初的兴奋不会这么快就消退。


被动语态:
His achievements were paled by his brother's greater success. = 他的成就被兄弟更大的成功所掩盖。
The beauty of the landscape was paled by the pollution. = 风景的美丽被污染所破坏。


疑问句:
Does his performance pale in comparison to his previous work? = 他的表现与以前的作品相比是否逊色？
Does the evidence pale in the face of new findings? = 在新的发现面前，证据是否显得苍白无力？


否定句:
His determination did not pale despite the challenges. = 尽管面临挑战，他的决心并没有减弱。
The beauty of the sunset did not pale even as darkness approached. = 即使夜幕降临，日落的美丽也没有减弱。
