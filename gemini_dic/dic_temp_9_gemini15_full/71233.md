fittedness:/ˈfɪtɪdnəs/ | n. | 适合性，适应性 | The degree to which something is suitable or appropriate for a particular purpose or situation.  It often refers to the compatibility or congruency between an organism and its environment, or a solution and a problem. | Example: The fittedness of the new software to our existing system is questionable.  新的软件对我们现有系统的适合性值得怀疑。  The fittedness of the design to the user's needs was a primary concern.  设计对用户需求的适合性是首要考虑因素。 |近义词：suitability, appropriateness, aptness, congruity | 反义词：inappropriateness, unsuitability
