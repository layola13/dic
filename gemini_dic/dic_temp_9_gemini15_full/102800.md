jarfuls:/ˈdʒɑːrfʊlz/| n.|罐子满的量；满满一罐|The plural form of "jarful," indicating multiple containers filled to capacity.  It refers to the quantity contained in several jars, not the jars themselves.|例句：She brought jarfuls of homemade jam to the picnic. 她带了好几罐自制果酱去野餐。


例句：The shelves were lined with jarfuls of colorful preserves. 架子上摆满了色彩鲜艳的果酱罐。


近义词：potsful, containerfuls


反义词：无


词性:名词
