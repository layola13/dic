misallocations:/ˌmɪsˌæləˈkeɪʃənz/| n.|资源错配，错误分配|The act of allocating resources incorrectly or inefficiently.  This can refer to financial resources, human resources, time, or any other type of resource.|例句：The company's misallocations of funds led to its eventual bankruptcy.  公司的资金错误分配导致了最终的破产。|例句：The project suffered from serious misallocations of personnel, resulting in delays and cost overruns.  该项目人员配置严重失误，导致延误和成本超支。|近义词：maldistribution, mismanagement, misapplication|反义词：optimal allocation, efficient allocation
