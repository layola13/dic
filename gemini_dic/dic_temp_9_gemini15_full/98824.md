intellectuals: /ˌɪntəlˈɛktʃuəlz/ | n. |知识分子， интеллигенция |  People who are highly educated and who work in areas such as education, literature, science, or the arts. They are often characterized by their engagement with ideas, critical thinking, and intellectual pursuits.  | Example: The intellectuals debated the merits of different political philosophies.  知识分子们就不同政治哲学的优缺点进行了辩论。|近义词： academics, scholars, thinkers, literati | 反义词：ignoramus, philistine
