discernibly:/dɪˈsɜːrnəbli/ | adv. | 可辨别地，明显地 |  Clearly or obviously perceptible to the eye or mind.  | The improvement in his health was discernibly better. 他的健康状况明显好转了。The difference between the two products is discernibly small. 这两种产品的区别微乎其微。| noticeably, perceptibly, appreciably | imperceptibly, subtly | 副词

词源:
From discern + -ably.  Discern ultimately derives from the Latin *discernere, meaning "to separate," "to distinguish," composed of dis- ("apart") + cernere ("to separate," "to decide").  The suffix "-ably" indicates "in a manner that is."
