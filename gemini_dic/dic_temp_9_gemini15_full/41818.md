cortisones:/ˈkɔːrtɪˌsoʊnz/| n. |皮质酮（类）|Cortisones are a group of steroid hormones produced in the adrenal cortex.  They are involved in a wide range of physiological processes, including the regulation of metabolism, immune response, and stress response.  They are also used medicinally to reduce inflammation.|例句：The doctor prescribed cortisone injections to reduce the inflammation in his knee.  医生开了皮质酮注射剂来减轻他膝盖的炎症。|近义词：corticosteroids, glucocorticoids|反义词：无直接反义词，但其作用可以被拮抗剂阻断|名词
