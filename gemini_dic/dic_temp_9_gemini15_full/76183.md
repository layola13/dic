fussify:/ˈfʌsɪfaɪ/| vt.|使烦扰；使混乱；使琐碎化 |To make fussy or more complicated than necessary; to overcomplicate.  |例句：Don't fussify the simple instructions; just follow them. 不要把简单的说明弄得过于复杂；只需按照说明操作即可。|近义词：complicate, complicate, overcomplicate,  |反义词：simplify, clarify
