philosophically:/ˌfɪləˈsɒfɪkli/|/ˈfɪləˈsɑːfɪkli/| adv. |哲学上地；从哲学角度来看 |In a way that relates to or is characteristic of philosophy.  It suggests considering a matter from a philosophical perspective, emphasizing abstract thought, reason, and fundamental principles. |He philosophically accepted the loss. 他从哲学角度接受了这个损失。
She analyzed the situation philosophically. 她从哲学角度分析了这种情况。| philosophically, abstractly | empirically, practically |副词
