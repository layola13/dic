creation:/kriˈeɪʃən/ | n. |创造，创作；宇宙万物；作品 |The act of creating something new.  It can refer to the act itself, the thing created, or the process of bringing something into existence.  |例句：The creation of the universe is a mystery. 宇宙的诞生是一个谜。|例句：Her latest novel is a work of brilliant creation. 她的最新小说是一部才华横溢的创作。|近义词：invention, formation, production |反义词：destruction, annihilation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist creates beautiful paintings. =艺术家创作美丽的画作。
God creates the world. =上帝创造了世界。

一般过去时:
Shakespeare created many famous plays. =莎士比亚创作了许多著名的戏剧。
They created a new company last year. =他们去年创建了一家新公司。

现在完成时:
Scientists have created new medicines to fight diseases. =科学家们已经研制出治疗疾病的新药。
She has created a stunning sculpture. =她创作了一个令人惊叹的雕塑。

过去完成时:
They had created a detailed plan before starting the project. =他们在开始项目之前已经制定了一个详细的计划。
The artist had created many masterpieces before his death. =这位艺术家在他去世之前创作了许多杰作。

将来时:
They will create a new website next month. =他们下个月将创建一个新的网站。
The company will create more jobs in the future. =公司未来将创造更多就业机会。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would create a beautiful garden. =如果我有更多的时间，我会创造一个美丽的庭院。
I wish I could create a masterpiece. =我希望我能创作出一部杰作。

被动语态:
The universe was created billions of years ago. =宇宙是在数十亿年前创造的。
This painting was created by a famous artist. =这幅画是一位著名艺术家创作的。

疑问句:
Who created this amazing piece of art? =是谁创作了这件令人惊叹的艺术品？
What will be created in the future? =未来将会创造什么？

否定句:
He didn't create that sculpture. =他并没有创作那个雕塑。
They haven't created a solution to the problem yet. =他们还没有找到解决这个问题的办法。
