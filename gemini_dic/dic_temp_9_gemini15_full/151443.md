paroling:/pəˈroʊlɪŋ/ | n. |假释；释放 | The act or process of releasing a prisoner from prison before the completion of their sentence, under certain conditions.  |Example:  The parole board is considering his application for paroling.  假释委员会正在考虑他的假释申请。

|Example: After serving five years, he was granted paroling. 服刑五年后，他被准予假释。|近义词：release, liberation, discharge |反义词：imprisonment, incarceration |名词
