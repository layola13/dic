sipping:/ˈsɪpɪŋ/| v.|小口喝，啜饮|The act of drinking in small amounts, usually slowly and delicately.  |例句：She was sipping her tea quietly. 她正静静地啜饮着茶。|例句：He sat sipping his wine, lost in thought. 他坐着细细品酒，陷入沉思。|近义词：drinking, tasting, gulping (反义)|反义词：gulping, quaffing|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She sips her coffee every morning. = 她每天早上都小口喝咖啡。
He sips tea while reading a book. = 他看书的时候小口喝茶。

一般过去时:
I sipped the hot chocolate slowly. = 我慢慢地啜饮着热巧克力。
She sipped her wine throughout the dinner. = 她整个晚餐都在小口喝着葡萄酒。

现在完成时:
I have sipped all the juice. = 我已经把所有的果汁都喝完了。
She has sipped the tea carefully. = 她已经仔细地啜饮了茶。

过去完成时:
He had sipped his beer before the game started. = 在比赛开始之前，他已经喝了一点啤酒。
They had sipped their champagne before the announcement. = 宣布之前，他们已经喝了一点香槟。

将来时:
I will sip the wine slowly to enjoy the flavor. = 我会慢慢地品尝葡萄酒以享受它的风味。
She will sip her tea after her work. = 她下班后会喝茶。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would sip my coffee leisurely. = 如果我有更多时间，我会悠闲地喝咖啡。
I wish I could sip this delicious wine all day. = 我真希望我能整天喝这种美味的葡萄酒。

被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)
The wine was sipped delicately by the connoisseur. = 这款酒被鉴赏家细细品尝。
The tea was being sipped slowly by the elderly woman. = 老妇人正慢慢地啜饮着茶。

疑问句:
Are you sipping your drink? = 你在喝饮料吗？
Was he sipping his coffee when you arrived? = 你到的时候他在喝咖啡吗？

否定句:
I did not sip the soup; it was too hot. = 我没有喝汤，太烫了。
She doesn't sip her drinks quickly. = 她不快速地喝饮料。


PHRASE->
sip by sip = 一口一口地
sip on = 慢慢地喝
sip down = 一饮而尽 (与sip本身含义相反，但可以表达“快速地喝完少量的东西”)
sip slowly = 慢慢地啜饮
sip thoughtfully = 若有所思地啜饮
