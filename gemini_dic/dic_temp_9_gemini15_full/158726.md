plover:/ˈplʌvər/| n.|鸻（一种涉禽）|A plover is a small to medium-sized shorebird with a short, stout bill and often a distinctive breast pattern.  They are known for their diverse habitats and foraging behaviors.|例句：We saw a ringed plover foraging on the beach. 我们在海滩上看到一只环颈鸻在觅食。|例句：The golden plover migrates thousands of miles each year. 金鸻每年迁徙数千英里。|近义词：sandpiper, lapwing|反义词:无
