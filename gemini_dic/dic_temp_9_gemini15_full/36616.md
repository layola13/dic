collecting:/kəˈlɛktɪŋ/ | v.ing | 收集；采集 | The act of gathering and accumulating items of a particular kind.  | 例句：He enjoys collecting stamps. 他喜欢集邮。

例句：She is collecting donations for charity.  她在为慈善机构募捐。


近义词：gathering, accumulating, assembling

反义词：dispersing, scattering, distributing

词性：动名词 (verb-ing form)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am collecting seashells. = 我正在收集贝壳。
He is collecting data for his research. = 他正在收集数据用于他的研究。

一般过去时:
I collected all the scattered papers. = 我收集了所有散落的纸张。
They collected money for the victims. = 他们为受害者募捐。

现在完成时:
She has collected a vast number of antique toys. = 她已经收集了大量的古董玩具。
We have been collecting donations all week. = 我们整个星期都在募捐。

过去完成时:
He had collected enough evidence before he went to court. = 他在去法院之前已经收集了足够的证据。
By the time the show ended, they had collected all the costumes. = 演出结束时，他们已经收集了所有的服装。

将来时:
I will be collecting the packages tomorrow. = 我明天将收集包裹。
They will collect the garbage on Thursday. = 他们将在星期四收集垃圾。

虚拟语气:
If I were collecting butterflies, I would need a net. = 如果我在收集蝴蝶，我需要一个网。
I wish I had collected more data for my experiment. = 我真希望我为我的实验收集了更多数据。

被动语态:
The data is being collected by the research team. = 数据正在由研究小组收集。
Donations are collected regularly by the organization. = 捐款由该组织定期收集。

疑问句:
Are you collecting anything special? = 你正在收集什么特别的东西吗？
What are they collecting for the museum? = 他们为博物馆收集什么？

否定句:
I am not collecting stamps anymore. = 我不再集邮了。
He did not collect the package yesterday. = 他昨天没有取包裹。
