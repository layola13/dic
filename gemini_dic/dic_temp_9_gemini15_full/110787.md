liniments:/ˈlɪnɪmənts/ | n. |药膏，搽剂 |A liniment is a liquid or semiliquid preparation, usually oily, for rubbing on the skin to relieve pain or inflammation.  Liniments often contain counterirritants that cause a mild burning sensation, which can distract from deeper pain. | Example:  He applied a liniment to his aching muscles after a strenuous workout. 他在剧烈运动后将药膏涂抹在酸痛的肌肉上。|近义词：ointment, balm, salve |反义词：None directly applicable.
