fruitlessly:/ˈfruːtlɪsli/| adv.|徒劳地，无效地|Without producing any useful result.|例句：They searched fruitlessly for the lost child.他们徒劳地寻找丢失的孩子。

例句：He worked fruitlessly for many years trying to solve the problem. 他多年来努力尝试解决这个问题，但都徒劳无功。


近义词：unsuccessfully, in vain, to no avail, ineffectually|反义词：successfully, effectively, productively|词性:副词
