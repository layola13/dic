do up:/duː ʌp/| vt.|装饰；打扮；整理；扣上；修缮；使恢复生机|to improve the appearance or condition of something, especially a building or room, by repairing and decorating it; to fasten something; to dress oneself smartly|例句：They're doing up the old house. 他们在装修那栋旧房子。


They did themselves up for the party. 他们为了聚会精心打扮了一番。


近义词：renovate, refurbish, decorate, dress up|反义词：destroy, demolish, neglect, undress


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We do up the house every weekend. = 我们每个周末都整理房子。
They do up their hair before going out. = 他们出门前都会把头发梳理好。

一般过去时:
She did up her coat before leaving. = 她离开前扣上了外套。
He did up the old car and sold it for a profit. = 他修缮了那辆旧车并高价卖掉了。

现在完成时:
We have done up the kitchen. = 我们已经装修好了厨房。
They have done themselves up for the wedding. = 他们为了婚礼盛装打扮。

过去完成时:
She had done up her hair before the interview. = 面试前她已经把头发梳理好了。
They had done up the flat before moving in. = 他们搬进去之前已经装修好了公寓。

将来时:
We will do up the bathroom next month. = 我们下个月将装修浴室。
She will do herself up for the date. = 她将为约会精心打扮。

虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would do up the whole house. = 如果我们有钱的话，我们会把整栋房子都装修一遍。
I wish I had done myself up more for the occasion. = 我真希望当时我打扮得更隆重些。

被动语态:
The house is being done up at the moment. = 这栋房子目前正在装修中。
The old building is to be done up next year. = 这栋旧建筑计划明年翻修。

疑问句:
Are you going to do up your garden this year? = 你今年打算整理你的花园吗？
Is she going to do herself up for the prom? = 她会为舞会精心打扮吗？

否定句:
They didn't do up the room before the guests arrived. = 客人来之前他们没有整理房间。
She didn't do herself up for the casual meeting. = 她没有为这次非正式的会议打扮。
