/priːˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənz/ | n. | 预展；预展会 |  Preexhibitions are exhibitions held before the main exhibition, often showcasing selected works or offering a sneak peek.  They serve as a build-up of anticipation for the main event. | The preexhibitions generated significant buzz for the upcoming art fair.  预展为即将到来的艺术博览会制造了大量的关注。|  preview, sneak preview | main exhibition | 名词
