indiscrimination:/ɪnˌdɪskrɪmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |不加区别；不加选择；任意；滥用 | The act of not making or showing distinctions; a lack of discrimination. It often implies a negative connotation, suggesting a failure to distinguish between different things or people, leading to unfair or harmful consequences. |例句：The indiscriminate use of pesticides is harming the environment.  滥用杀虫剂正在损害环境。

例句：His indiscriminate spending led to bankruptcy. 他的任意挥霍导致破产。

近义词：bias, prejudice, partiality, favoritism

反义词：discrimination, selectivity, discernment, judgment
