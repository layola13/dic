commentaries:/ˈkɒmənˌtɛriz/ /ˈkɑːmənˌtɛriz/| n. |评论；注释；解说词 |Explanations or interpretations of something, often a text or event; a series of explanatory notes or comments.  A commentary can be written, spoken, or visual. |例句：The sports commentator provided insightful commentaries on the game. 体育评论员对比赛进行了深入的评论。

The professor's commentaries on the novel were highly illuminating. 教授对小说的评论非常有启发性。

|近义词：interpretations, explanations, analyses, annotations, elucidation|反义词：silence, omission|名词
