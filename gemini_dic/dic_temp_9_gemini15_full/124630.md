mouth:/maʊθ/ /mauθ/| n.|嘴，口 |The opening in the lower part of the human face, surrounded by the lips, through which food is taken in and sounds are made. It is also used for speaking, tasting, and kissing.  |例句：She opened her mouth to speak. 她张开嘴说话。

He hurt his mouth while eating. 他吃饭时弄伤了嘴。


近义词：oral cavity, maw |反义词：N/A

词性:名词
