international: /ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəl/ | adj. | 国际的，国际性的 | Relating to or involving more than one nation or country.  |例句：The Olympics are an international sporting event. 奥运会是一项国际体育赛事。 |近义词：global, worldwide, transnational |反义词：national, domestic, local |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company operates internationally. = 这家公司在国际上运营。
This issue is of international concern. = 这个问题受到国际关注。


一般过去时:
She travelled internationally for her job. = 她因工作而进行国际旅行。
The agreement was signed internationally. = 该协议在国际上签署。


现在完成时:
We have worked internationally for many years. = 我们多年来一直在国际上工作。
The organization has expanded internationally. = 该组织已在国际上扩张。


过去完成时:
They had already collaborated internationally before the project began. = 项目开始前他们已经在国际上合作了。
The company had established international branches. = 这家公司已经建立了国际分支机构。


将来时:
We will expand our business internationally next year. = 我们明年将把业务扩展到国际市场。
The conference will attract international participants. = 会议将吸引国际参与者。


虚拟语气:
If we had more funding, we would participate in more international projects. = 如果我们有更多资金，我们将参与更多国际项目。
I wish I could attend the international conference. = 我希望我能参加国际会议。


被动语态:
The product is marketed internationally. = 该产品在国际市场上销售。
The decision was reached internationally.  = 该决定是在国际上达成的。


疑问句:

Are you involved in any international projects? = 你参与任何国际项目吗？


否定句:
