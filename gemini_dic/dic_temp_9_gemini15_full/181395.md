reisolate:/ˌriːˈaɪsəleɪt/| vt.|再次隔离；重新隔离|To isolate again; to subject to isolation a second time.|例句：After a brief period of freedom, they had to reisolate the patient due to a relapse.  再次隔离患者，因为病情复发。

例句：The researchers reisolated the virus from the infected cells to study its mutations. 研究人员从受感染的细胞中重新分离出病毒以研究其突变。


近义词：reconfine, sequester again, isolate anew
反义词：release, liberate, free
