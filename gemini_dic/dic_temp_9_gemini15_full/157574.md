pitches:/pɪtʃɪz/| n. v.|投球；音高；倾斜；推销；|The act of throwing a ball, especially in baseball; the highness or lowness of a sound; the slope or inclination of something; the act of trying to sell something. |例句：The pitcher threw a fast pitch. 投手投了一个快速球。The singer's pitch was perfect. 歌手的音调很完美。The roof has a steep pitch. 屋顶坡度很陡峭。He made a pitch for a new project. 他为一个新项目进行推销。|近义词：throw, tone, slant, sales pitch|反义词：flat (for pitch as slope), low (for pitch as sound)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pitches the ball well. = 他投球很准。
She pitches her voice high. = 她把声音提高了。

一般过去时:
The band pitched their tents. = 乐队搭起了帐篷。
He pitched the idea to the investors. = 他向投资者推销了他的想法。

现在完成时:
They have pitched several successful products. = 他们已经成功推销了几种产品。
She has pitched her voice to a higher octave. = 她把嗓音提高了一个八度。

过去完成时:
They had already pitched the tent before it started raining. = 在下雨之前他们已经搭好了帐篷。
He had pitched the idea several times before it was accepted. = 他的想法在被接受之前已经被推销过好几次了。

将来时:
She will pitch the new marketing strategy tomorrow. = 她明天将推销新的营销策略。
He will pitch his tent near the river. = 他将在河边搭帐篷。

虚拟语气:
If he pitched better, he would win more games. = 如果他投球更好，他会赢得更多比赛。
If she pitched her voice lower, it would be easier to listen to. = 如果她把声音放低，听起来会更容易。

被动语态:
The ball was pitched perfectly. = 球被完美地投掷了。
The idea was pitched to the board. = 这个想法被提交给了董事会。

疑问句:
How high did he pitch his voice? = 他把声音提高到多高？
Where did they pitch their tents? = 他们把帐篷搭在哪里了？

否定句:
He didn't pitch the ball hard enough. = 他投球不够用力。
She didn't pitch her idea effectively. = 她没有有效地推销她的想法。
