roboto:/roʊˈboʊtoʊ/ | n. | 机器人字体 |Roboto is a geometric sans-serif typeface designed by Christian Robertson for Google.  It's known for its clean, modern, and highly legible design, making it suitable for a wide range of applications, from websites and apps to print materials.  | Example: The website uses the Roboto font for its body text.  网站使用Roboto字体作为正文字体。 |近义词:  Open Sans, Lato,  Helvetica (though less similar in style)| 反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a font style) |名词
