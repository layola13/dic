congratulated:/kənˈɡrædʒuleɪtɪd/| vt.|祝贺，恭喜|To express pleasure to (someone) on the occasion of success or good fortune.|例句：I congratulated him on his success. 我祝贺他成功了。|近义词：felicitated, commended|反义词：condoled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I congratulate you on your achievement. = 我祝贺你取得的成就。
They congratulate the winners every year. = 他们每年都祝贺获胜者。


一般过去时:
She congratulated me on my graduation. = 她祝贺我毕业。
We congratulated them on their wedding. = 我们祝贺他们的婚礼。


现在完成时:
I have congratulated her on her promotion. = 我已经祝贺她升职了。
They have congratulated the team on their victory. = 他们已经祝贺团队取得胜利。


过去完成时:
He had already congratulated her before I could. = 在我之前他已经祝贺她了。
They had congratulated the finalists before the ceremony began. = 典礼开始前他们已经祝贺了决赛选手。


将来时:
I will congratulate her when she wins. = 当她获胜时，我会祝贺她。
We will congratulate them on their anniversary. = 我们将在他们的周年纪念日祝贺他们。


虚拟语气:
If I saw him, I would congratulate him. = 如果我看到他，我会祝贺他。
I wish I could have congratulated her in person. = 我真希望我能亲自祝贺她。


被动语态:
He was congratulated by his boss on his hard work. = 他因辛勤工作而受到老板的祝贺。
The team was congratulated for their outstanding performance. = 该团队因其杰出的表现而受到祝贺。


疑问句:
Did you congratulate her on her new job? = 你祝贺她找到新工作了吗？
Have they been congratulated yet? = 他们已经被祝贺了吗？


否定句:
I didn't congratulate him on his birthday. = 我没有祝贺他的生日。
They haven't been congratulated on their engagement. = 他们还没有被祝贺订婚。


PHRASE->
congratulated on = 祝贺…
congratulated themselves on = 为…而沾沾自喜
warmly congratulated = 热烈祝贺
sincerely congratulated = 衷心祝贺
heartily congratulated = 衷心祝贺
