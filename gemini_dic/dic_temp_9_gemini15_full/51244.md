destructive: /dɪˈstrʌktɪv/ | adj. | 破坏性的，毁灭性的 | Causing damage or destruction.  Something that is destructive causes damage, harm, or ruin. | 例句：The hurricane had a destructive effect on the coastal towns.  飓风对沿海城镇造成了破坏性的影响。

例句：His destructive criticism hurt her feelings. 他的破坏性批评伤了她的感情。


近义词：damaging, ruinous, harmful, detrimental | 反义词：constructive, beneficial, helpful, restorative


词源:  The word "destructive" comes from the Old French word "destructif," which itself derives from the Latin word "destructivus."  The Latin "destruere" means "to destroy."  The suffix "-ive" indicates an adjective.
