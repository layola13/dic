indomitablenesses:/ˌɪnˈdɑːmɪtəbəlnəsɪz/ | n. |不屈不挠的品质（复数）|The indomitablenesses of the human spirit are often tested in times of adversity. 人类精神的不屈不挠的品质往往在逆境中经受考验。|近义词：resolutions, perseverances, steadfastnesses |反义词：weaknesses, surrenders, give-ups |名词


英文解析：The plural form of "indomitableness," which refers to the quality or state of being indomitable;  characterized by a refusal to yield or be subdued.  It emphasizes the multiple instances or aspects of this unwavering spirit.


例句：

The indomitablenesses shown by the survivors of the disaster were truly inspiring. 灾难幸存者们所展现的不屈不挠的精神，真是令人鼓舞。

Her indomitablenesses in the face of overwhelming odds earned her the respect of her peers. 面对巨大的困难，她不屈不挠的精神赢得了同行的尊重。


词源：

* **in-**: prefix indicating negation or intensification.
* **domit-**: from Latin "domitare," meaning "to tame" or "to subdue."
* **-able**: suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of" or "worthy of."
* **-ness**: suffix forming nouns of quality or state.
* **-es**: plural suffix for nouns.


因此，“indomitableness”字面意思是“无法驯服的状态”，引申为“不屈不挠，坚韧不拔”。  “indomitablenesses”是其复数形式，表示多种不屈不挠的品质或表现。
