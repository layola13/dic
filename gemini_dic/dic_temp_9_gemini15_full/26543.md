cabinetmakers:/ˈkæbɪnɛtˌmeɪkərz/ | n. |橱柜制造商，细木工|skilled craftspeople who design, build, and install cabinets and other fine wood furniture.  They often work with custom orders, creating unique pieces to fit specific spaces and client needs. |The cabinetmakers crafted a beautiful mahogany bookcase for the library.  橱柜制造商为图书馆制作了一个漂亮的桃花心木书柜。 The antique cabinet was clearly made by master cabinetmakers. 这件古董橱柜显然是由大师级橱柜制造商制作的。| furniture makers, woodworkers, joiners |  (无明显反义词)| 名词
