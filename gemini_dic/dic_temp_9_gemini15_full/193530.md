self-acceptance:/ˌsɛlfəkˈsɛptəns/ | n. | 自我接纳 | The state of accepting oneself completely, including both strengths and weaknesses.  It involves acknowledging and appreciating all aspects of oneself without judgment or criticism. | Example:  Self-acceptance is crucial for mental well-being.  自我接纳对心理健康至关重要。 |近义词: self-love, self-compassion, self-regard | 反义词: self-criticism, self-hatred, self-rejection | 名词
