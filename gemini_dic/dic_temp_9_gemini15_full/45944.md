cute:/kjuːt/| adj.|可爱的，讨人喜欢的|  Attractive and appealing in a charmingly innocent way.  Often used to describe children, animals, or objects.|例句：The baby is so cute!  那个婴儿真可爱！|例句：That puppy is so cute with its big eyes. 那只小狗大大的眼睛真可爱。|近义词：adorable, charming, sweet, lovely|反义词：ugly, unattractive, repulsive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The kitten is cute. = 小猫很可爱。
That dress is cute on you. = 你穿那件衣服很可爱。

一般过去时:
The puppy was cute when it was a baby. = 小狗小时候很可爱。
The cake was cutely decorated. = 蛋糕装饰得很可爱。

现在完成时:
She has always been cute. = 她一直都很可爱。
We have found a cute little cafe. = 我们找到了一家很可爱的小咖啡馆。

过去完成时:
The toy had been cute, but it was broken now. = 这个玩具以前很可爱，但是现在坏了。
They had already seen the cute panda before. = 他们之前已经看过那只可爱的熊猫了。

将来时:
The baby will be even cuter when it grows up. = 这个婴儿长大后会更可爱。
I think the new design will be cute. = 我认为新的设计会很可爱。

虚拟语气:
If only I had a cute pet! = 要是我有一只可爱的宠物就好了！
I wish that dress were cute. = 我希望那件衣服很可爱。

被动语态:
The doll is considered cute by many. = 许多人认为这个娃娃很可爱。
The design was deemed cute by the judges. = 设计师们认为这个设计很可爱。

疑问句:
Is that kitten cute? = 那只小猫可爱吗？
Isn't the puppy cute? = 小狗不可爱吗？（反问句）

否定句:
The car isn't cute. = 这辆车不可爱。
That painting is not cute. = 那幅画不可爱。


PHRASE->
cute as a button:  非常可爱
