chough:/tʃuː/| n. |寒鸦|A chough is a type of bird, a black crow-like bird with a red bill and legs, found in rocky coastal areas.  Choughs are known for their distinctive calls and acrobatic flight.|Example: We saw a chough soaring high above the cliffs. 我们看到一只寒鸦在悬崖峭壁上空翱翔。|近义词：jackdaw, crow|反义词：无
