propernesses:/ˈprɒpənəsɪz/ | n. | 正确性；适当性；得体性 | The propernesses of behavior in different cultures vary greatly.  不同文化中行为的正确性差异很大。 |近义词：appropriateness, correctness, decorum |反义词： impropriety, incorrectness, indecency |


英文解析：The plural form of "properness," referring to the state or quality of being proper, appropriate, or correct. It often implies conformity to standards of etiquette, morality, or good taste.


例句：

The propernesses of formal dining etiquette are often overlooked. 正式宴会礼仪的得体性常常被忽视。


He questioned the propernesses of the proposed solution. 他质疑了所提议解决方案的合理性。

suitability, decency, propriety


反义词:

improprieties, indecencies, inappropriateness
