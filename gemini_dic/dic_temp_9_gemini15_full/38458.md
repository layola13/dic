concessions:/kənˈseʃənz/| n.|让步，妥协；特许权；承认|The act of conceding or yielding, as a point or fact in an argument; a thing conceded; a grant or privilege.  It can also refer to something given up or yielded in negotiation or compromise.|例句：The company made concessions to the workers' demands. 公司对工人的要求做出了让步。
例句：The government offered concessions to the rebels. 政府向叛军做出了让步。|近义词：compromise, yield, allowance|反义词：resistance, insistence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government makes concessions to avoid strikes. = 政府做出让步以避免罢工。
She always concedes points in arguments. = 她总是会在争论中做出让步。

一般过去时:
He made significant concessions during the negotiations. = 他在谈判中做出了重大让步。
They conceded defeat after the election results. = 选举结果出来后，他们承认失败。

现在完成时:
Both sides have made concessions in the peace talks. = 双方在和平谈判中都做出了让步。
The company has conceded to employee requests for better benefits. = 公司已经同意了员工关于更好福利的要求。

过去完成时:
They had already made several concessions before the final agreement. = 在最终协议达成之前，他们已经做了几次让步。
She had conceded that her argument was flawed. = 她已经承认她的论点有缺陷。

将来时:
We will make concessions to reach a compromise. = 我们将做出让步以达成妥协。
The company expects to make further concessions next year. = 公司预计明年将进一步让步。

虚拟语气:
If they had made more concessions, the deal would have been successful. = 如果他们做出更多让步，这笔交易就会成功。
I wish they had conceded to our requests earlier. = 我希望他们早点答应我们的请求。

被动语态:
Concessions were made by both parties to reach an agreement. = 双方都做出了让步以达成协议。
Significant concessions were demanded by the union. = 工会要求做出重大让步。

疑问句:
What concessions are you willing to make? = 你愿意做出什么让步？
Were any concessions made during the talks? = 会谈中是否做出了任何让步？

否定句:
They refused to make any concessions. = 他们拒绝做出任何让步。
No concessions were offered by the management. = 管理层没有提供任何让步。
