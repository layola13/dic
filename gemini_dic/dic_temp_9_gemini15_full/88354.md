hippophile:/ˈhɪpəˌfaɪl/| n. |恋马癖，爱马者 |A person who loves horses.  This term often implies a strong, perhaps obsessive, love for horses, going beyond a casual appreciation. |例句：He's a confirmed hippophile, spending hours each day grooming and riding his beloved steeds. 他是一个十足的恋马癖，每天花好几个小时梳理和骑乘他心爱的坐骑。|近义词：equestrian (emphasizing riding skills), horse lover |反义词：hippophobe (fear of horses)
