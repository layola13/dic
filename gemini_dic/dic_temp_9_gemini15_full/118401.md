merostomata:/ˌmɛrəˈstoʊmətə/| n. |肢口纲动物|The Merostomata is a class of marine arthropods that includes horseshoe crabs.  They are characterized by their horseshoe-shaped carapace, chelicerae, and book gills.  The group has a long evolutionary history, with fossil records dating back to the Paleozoic Era.  They are considered living fossils due to their relatively unchanged morphology over millions of years.|例句：The horseshoe crab, a member of the Merostomata, is an ancient creature.|近义词：Xylosoma (an obsolete term for a related group)|反义词：X
