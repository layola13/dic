easing:/ˈiːzɪŋ/ | n. v. | 缓解；缓和；放松；使缓和 | The process of making something easier or less severe;  to make something easier or less severe. | 例句：The easing of traffic congestion is a priority.  交通拥堵的缓解是当务之急。 The government is easing restrictions on foreign investment. 政府正在放松对外国投资的限制。 | 近义词：alleviation, mitigation, relaxation, relief | 反义词：intensification, aggravation, tightening


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is easing trade restrictions. = 政府正在放宽贸易限制。
Easing tension is crucial for peace. = 缓和紧张局势对和平至关重要。

一般过去时:
The medicine eased his pain. = 药物缓解了他的疼痛。
They eased the burden on the employees. = 他们减轻了员工的负担。

现在完成时:
The new policy has eased the financial burden on many families. = 新政策已经减轻了许多家庭的经济负担。
The negotiations have eased tensions between the two countries. = 谈判缓和了两国之间的紧张关系。

过去完成时:
The rain had eased before we left. = 我们离开之前雨已经停了。
They had eased the pressure before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前减轻了压力。

将来时:
The doctor hopes the new treatment will ease his symptoms. = 医生希望新的治疗方法能够缓解他的症状。
We expect the traffic to ease after rush hour. = 我们预计交通高峰期过后会缓解。

虚拟语气:
If only the pain would ease! = 但愿疼痛能缓解！
I wish the situation would ease soon. = 我希望情况很快能好转。

被动语态:
Tensions were eased by the peace talks. = 和谈缓和了紧张局势。
The symptoms were eased by the medication. = 症状被药物缓解了。

疑问句:
Will this medication ease the pain? = 这药能缓解疼痛吗？
Does this policy ease the burden on taxpayers? = 这项政策能减轻纳税人的负担吗？

否定句:
The new measures did not ease the problem. = 新措施并没有缓解问题。
The situation hasn't eased despite our efforts. = 尽管我们努力了，情况也没有好转。
