herdbook:/ˈhɜːrdbʊk/ | n. | 家畜登记簿；(纯种动物的)血统书 | A herdbook is a register of pedigree animals, usually livestock such as cattle, horses, or sheep.  It records the ancestry and other important information about each animal, ensuring the purity of the breed. |  The farmer meticulously updated the herdbook with the birth of each new calf.  这个农场主一丝不苟地更新家畜登记簿，记录每头新出生的小牛的信息。| stud book, pedigree register |  — | 名词
