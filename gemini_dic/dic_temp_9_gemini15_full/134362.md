noninterest:/ˌnɑːnˈɪntrəst/| adj.|非利息的，无息的|Not involving or relating to interest (especially interest on money).  It typically refers to a loan or investment that does not accrue interest.|Example: The noninterest-bearing account offered minimal returns but guaranteed capital preservation.|近义词：interest-free|反义词：interest-bearing
