flatterable: /ˈflætərəbl/ | adj. | 易受奉承的；爱听恭维话的 | Easily flattered; susceptible to praise or compliments.  | The young artist was flatterable and easily swayed by positive feedback, sometimes to his detriment. 年轻的艺术家很容易受奉承，很容易被积极的反馈所左右，有时甚至会适得其反。 |  susceptible, impressionable, vain |  unimpressed, indifferent, unyielding | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is flatterable. = 他容易受奉承。
She is always flatterable by her boss's praise. = 她总是被老板的赞扬所奉承。


一般过去时:
He was flatterable in his youth. = 他年轻时容易受奉承。
She was easily flatterable by compliments on her appearance. = 她很容易被对她外貌的赞美所奉承。


现在完成时:
He has always been flatterable. = 他一直都很容易受奉承。
She has become less flatterable with age. = 她随着年龄的增长变得不那么容易受奉承了。


过去完成时:
He had been flatterable before he gained experience. = 在他获得经验之前，他很容易受奉承。

虚拟语气:
