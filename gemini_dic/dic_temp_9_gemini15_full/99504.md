interfolds:/ˈɪntərfoʊldz/ | v. |相互折叠；互相交错|To fold or be folded one within another or into each other.  The term suggests a reciprocal action, implying multiple layers or components folding together. |例句：The layers of sedimentary rock interfold beautifully in this geological formation.  这个地质构造中沉积岩层相互折叠得非常漂亮。

The fabric's design interfolds different colors and textures.  这种布料的设计将不同的颜色和纹理相互交错。

近义词：interweave, interlace, intertwine |反义词：separate, unfold, unroll

词性:动词
