inter-influence:/ˌɪntərˈɪnflʊəns/| n. |相互影响，互相作用|The mutual impact or effect of one thing on another, and vice versa.  It often refers to a reciprocal relationship where each element affects and is affected by the other.|例句：The inter-influence of culture and technology is undeniable. 文化与科技之间的相互影响是不可否认的。

例句：The inter-influence between the two countries' economies is significant. 两国经济之间的相互影响是巨大的。

近义词：interaction, reciprocal influence, mutual impact

反义词：independence, unilateral influence, one-way effect

词性:名词
