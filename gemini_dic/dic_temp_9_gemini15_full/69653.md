fence-in: /fɛnsˈɪn/ | vt. | 围起来；圈起来 | To enclose or surround with a fence.  | The farmer fenced in his cattle. 农民把他的牛圈了起来。 They fenced in the garden to keep out the rabbits. 他们用篱笆围住花园以防兔子进入。 | enclose, surround, confine | leave open, unfence | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They fence in the yard every year. = 他们每年都把院子围起来。
The farmer fences in his sheep to protect them from wolves. = 农民把羊圈起来以保护它们免受狼的攻击。

一般过去时:
We fenced in the garden last spring. = 我们去年春天把花园围了起来。
The rancher fenced in his horses after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，牧场主把他的马圈了起来。

现在完成时:
They have fenced in the entire property. = 他们已经把整个房产都围起来了。
The city has fenced in the park to improve safety. = 城市已经把公园围起来以提高安全性。

过去完成时:
They had fenced in the field before the harvest. = 他们在收割前就把田地围了起来。
The builders had fenced in the construction site before starting work. = 建筑工人动工前就把建筑工地围了起来。

将来时:
We will fence in the new pasture next week. = 我们下周将把新的牧场围起来。
The company will fence in the factory grounds for security. = 公司将为安全起见把工厂场地围起来。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would fence in the whole area. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会把整个区域都围起来。
I wish we had fenced in the garden sooner to prevent the dogs from getting out. = 我希望我们早点把花园围起来，以防止狗跑出去。

被动语态:

The garden is being fenced in by the gardener. = 花园正在被园丁围起来。

疑问句:

否定句:
They did not fence in the property. = 他们没有把房产围起来。
