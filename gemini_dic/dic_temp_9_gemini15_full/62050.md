encapsulating: /ɪnˈkæpsjuleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 封装；概括；包含 |  To enclose or encase something completely; to summarize or express the main points of something concisely.  | The software encapsulates several different functions.  这个软件封装了几个不同的功能。 |The essay encapsulates the main arguments of the book. 这篇文章概括了本书的主要论点。|  enclose, contain, summarize, encompass |  disperse, expand, detail | 动词
