nonelastic: /ˌnɒnɪˈlæstɪk/ | adj. | 非弹性的； inelastic | 不具有弹性的；无法恢复原状的；指经济学中需求或供给对价格变化不敏感的 |  Nonelastic materials do not return to their original shape after being stretched or compressed.  非弹性材料在拉伸或压缩后不会恢复到原来的形状。The demand for gasoline is relatively nonelastic in the short term.  短期内汽油的需求相对缺乏弹性。| 近义词：rigid, inflexible, inelastic | 反义词：elastic, flexible
