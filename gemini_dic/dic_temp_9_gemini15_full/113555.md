macaques:/məˈkæks/| n. |猕猴|A macaque is a type of monkey belonging to the genus Macaca.  They are characterized by their relatively short tails and are found across a wide range of habitats in Asia and Africa.  They are highly intelligent and adaptable primates, often exhibiting complex social behaviors.|Example:  The researchers observed a troop of Japanese macaques bathing in a hot spring.  研究人员观察到一群日本猕猴在温泉中沐浴。  The zoo's macaque enclosure is a popular attraction. 动物园的猕猴围栏是一个热门景点。|近义词：monkey (general term)|反义词：无
