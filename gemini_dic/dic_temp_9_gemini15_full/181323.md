reintervention:/ˌriːˌɪntərˈvɛnʃən/| n. |再次干预；再次介入|The act of intervening again, especially in a situation that was previously addressed.  It often implies a failure of the initial intervention or the emergence of new complications. |例句：The patient required reintervention due to complications from the initial surgery.  患者由于初次手术的并发症需要再次手术。|例句：The government's reintervention in the economy was met with mixed reactions. 政府再次干预经济受到了褒贬不一的反应。|近义词：reinvolvement, second intervention, renewed intervention|反义词：non-intervention, laissez-faire
