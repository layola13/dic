USAGE->
一般现在时:
She reads a fashion magazine every week. = 她每周都读一本时尚杂志。
He buys a new magazine at the airport. = 他在机场买了一本新杂志。

一般过去时:
I read a very interesting magazine yesterday. = 我昨天读了一本非常有趣的杂志。
They bought several magazines at the bookstore. = 他们在那家书店买了数本杂志。

现在完成时:
I have read this magazine twice. = 我已经读过这本杂志两次了。
She has subscribed to that magazine for years. = 她已经订阅那本杂志多年了。

过去完成时:

They had finished reading the magazine before the movie started. = 电影开始之前，他们已经读完杂志了。

将来时:
I will buy a new magazine tomorrow. = 我明天会买一本新杂志。
She will read the magazine on the plane. = 她会在飞机上阅读这本杂志。
