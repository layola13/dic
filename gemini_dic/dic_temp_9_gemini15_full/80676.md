gorge:/ɡɔːrdʒ/| n., v.|峡谷；（尤指）山峡；狼吞虎咽；大吃；塞满；|A deep narrow valley with steep sides, often with a river running through it.  To eat something very quickly and greedily.|例句：The hikers were awestruck by the magnificent gorge.  徒步旅行者们被壮丽的峡谷所震撼。

The bird gorged itself on berries.  这只鸟狼吞虎咽地吃着浆果。|近义词：canyon, ravine, chasm (n.); devour, gobble, stuff (v.)|反义词：plain, plateau (n.); nibble, sip (v.)|名词，动词
