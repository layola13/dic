patron:/ˈpeɪtrən/| n. |赞助人；顾客；保护人 | A patron is a person who gives financial or other support to a person, organization, or cause.  A patron can also be a customer or regular client of a business. |例句：The museum is grateful to its generous patrons.  博物馆感谢其慷慨的赞助人。 |例句：The café has many regular patrons. 这家咖啡馆有很多常客。|近义词：sponsor, supporter, customer, client |反义词：opponent, adversary


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is a patron of the arts. = 他是一位艺术赞助人。
She is a loyal patron of that restaurant. = 她是那家餐厅的忠实顾客。


一般过去时:
He was a patron of the local library. = 他是当地图书馆的赞助人。
They were patrons of the theater for many years. = 他们多年来一直是这家剧院的赞助人。


现在完成时:
The museum has had many generous patrons over the years. = 多年来，博物馆拥有许多慷慨的赞助人。
She has been a patron of that shop since it opened. = 自从这家商店开业以来，她一直是它的顾客。


过去完成时:
Before the renovation, the theatre had had several wealthy patrons. = 翻修前，剧院曾有几位富有的赞助人。
He had been a patron of the arts for decades before his death. = 他去世前几十年一直是艺术的赞助人。


将来时:
The new museum hopes to attract many patrons. = 新博物馆希望吸引许多赞助人。
She will be a regular patron of this coffee shop. = 她将成为这家咖啡店的常客。


虚拟语气:
If he were a patron of the arts, he would donate more money. = 如果他是艺术赞助人，他会捐更多钱。
I wish I could be a patron of that charity. = 我希望我能成为那个慈善机构的赞助人。


被动语态:
The artist was supported by several wealthy patrons. = 这位艺术家得到几位富有的赞助人的支持。
The restaurant is well-loved by its patrons. = 这家餐厅深受顾客喜爱。


疑问句:
Are you a patron of this gallery? = 你是这家画廊的赞助人吗？
Who are the patrons of this event? = 这次活动的赞助人是谁？


否定句:
He is not a patron of the arts. = 他不是艺术赞助人。
She is no longer a patron of that store. = 她不再是那家店的顾客了。


PHRASE->

patron saint:守护神

patronize: 惠顾；屈尊俯就


词源:

From Old French *patron, from Latin patronus, literally "protector," related to pater "father."  The sense of "customer" developed later.
