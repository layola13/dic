oncost: /ˈɒnkɒst/ | n. | 附加成本，间接成本 | Indirect costs or expenses that are not directly related to production but are necessary for the business to operate.  These costs are often fixed and recurring. Examples include rent, utilities, and administrative salaries. | The oncosts of running the business are significant.  这家企业的附加成本相当大。| overhead, indirect costs | direct costs, variable costs | 名词
