pleyt:/plet/| n. |游戏，娱乐；戏剧；玩法；花招| a form of play, typically one governed by rules and involving competition, amusement, or skill.  It can also refer to a dramatic performance or a deceptive maneuver.|例句：Children enjoy playing games. 孩子们喜欢玩游戏。
The play was a great success. 这出戏非常成功。|近义词：game, sport, drama, trick|反义词：work, labor
