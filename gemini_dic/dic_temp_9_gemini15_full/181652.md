relearning:/ˌriːˈlɜːrnɪŋ/| n. |再学习；重新学习|The act of learning something again, often after forgetting it or needing to reinforce understanding.  It implies a previous learning experience.|例句：The relearning of a forgotten language can be challenging but rewarding.  重新学习一门外语可能具有挑战性，但也令人欣慰。

例句：His relearning of the material proved essential for the exam. 他对学习材料的重新学习证明对考试至关重要。


近义词：repetition, review, restudy |反义词：unlearning, forgetting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Relearning is a crucial part of mastering a skill.  再学习是掌握一项技能的关键部分。
He relearns the material every week. 他每周都会重新学习这些材料。


一般过去时:
She relearned the song after forgetting the lyrics. 她忘记歌词后又重新学习了这首歌。
They relearned the procedures following the accident. 事故发生后，他们重新学习了规程。


现在完成时:
I have relearned several programming languages this year. 今年我已经重新学习了几种编程语言。
She has relearned the importance of patience. 她已经重新认识到耐心的重要性。


过去完成时:
He had relearned the route before embarking on the journey. 他在开始旅程之前已经重新学习了路线。
They had relearned the safety protocols before starting the experiment. 他们在开始实验之前已经重新学习了安全规程。


将来时:
I will relearn the piano next year. 明年我将重新学习钢琴。
She will relearn the techniques after the break. 休息之后她将重新学习这些技巧。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would relearn Spanish. 如果我有更多时间，我会重新学习西班牙语。
I wish I could relearn how to ride a bike. 我希望我能重新学会骑自行车。


被动语态:
The material is being relearned by the students. 这些材料正在被学生们重新学习。
The lesson will be relearned next week. 这节课下周将被重新学习。


疑问句:
Will you be relearning the material for the test? 你会为考试重新学习这些材料吗？
Is she relearning any foreign languages? 她正在重新学习任何外语吗？


否定句:
He did not relearn the lesson. 他没有重新学习这节课。
They will not be relearning the steps. 他们不会重新学习这些步骤。
