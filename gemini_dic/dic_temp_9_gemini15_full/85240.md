harm:/hɑːrm/| n. vt.|伤害，损害；危害| to inflict injury or damage on something or someone.  It can refer to physical injury, emotional distress, or damage to reputation or property.|例句：The storm caused significant harm to the crops. 暴风雨对庄稼造成了巨大的损害。|例句：His words caused her great harm. 他的话深深地伤害了她。|近义词：injury, damage, hurt, impair|反义词：benefit, advantage, help


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harms his body by smoking. = 他吸烟损害了他的身体。
The policy harms small businesses. = 这项政策损害了小型企业。

一般过去时:
The accident harmed several people. = 那次事故伤害了几个人。
His actions harmed his reputation. = 他的行为损害了他的名誉。

现在完成时:
The scandal has harmed the company's image. = 这场丑闻损害了公司的形象。
Smoking has harmed his health. = 吸烟损害了他的健康。

过去完成时:
They had harmed the environment before the new laws were implemented. = 新法实施之前，他们已经破坏了环境。
He realized he had harmed his friend's feelings. = 他意识到他伤害了朋友的感情。

将来时:
The new regulations will harm the economy. = 新规将损害经济。
This decision will harm our chances of success. = 这个决定将损害我们成功的几率。

虚拟语气:
If I had known it would harm him, I wouldn't have done it. = 如果我知道这会伤害他，我不会这么做的。
I wish I hadn't said that; it might harm our relationship. = 我真希望我没有说过那样的话；那可能会损害我们的关系。

被动语态:
His reputation was harmed by the false accusations. = 他的名誉被不实的指控所损害。
The environment is being harmed by pollution. = 环境正在受到污染的损害。

疑问句:
Will this action harm the project? = 这个行动会损害项目吗？
Does smoking harm your health? = 吸烟会损害你的健康吗？

否定句:
The medicine won't harm you. = 这药不会伤害你。
This plan won't harm the environment. = 这个计划不会损害环境。


PHRASE->
do harm to = 对...有害
come to harm = 受到伤害
without harm = 安然无恙
beyond harm's reach = 远离危险
harm's way = 危险之地
