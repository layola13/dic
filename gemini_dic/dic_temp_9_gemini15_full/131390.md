noncitizen: /ˈnɑːnsɪtɪzən/ | n. | 非公民 | A person who is not a citizen of a particular country.  | Example:  Noncitizens are not eligible to vote in this election.  非公民无权参加本次选举。  |近义词: alien, foreigner | 反义词: citizen
