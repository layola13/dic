pounce:/paʊns/| v. n.|猛扑，突然袭击|To jump or spring suddenly upon something or someone.|The cat pounced on the mouse.  猫猛地扑向老鼠。|The police pounced on the suspect. 警察突然抓捕了嫌疑犯。|leap, spring, swoop|retreat, withdraw|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pounces on any opportunity to speak. 他抓住任何机会发言。
The cat pounces on mice. 猫扑老鼠。

一般过去时:
The lion pounced on its prey.  狮子猛扑向猎物。
She pounced on the mistake in his report. 她发现了报告中的错误。

现在完成时:
The cat has pounced on several mice this evening. 猫今晚已经扑了好几只老鼠。
He has pounced on the chance to travel abroad. 他抓住了出国旅行的机会。

过去完成时:
The bird had pounced before I could react.  在我反应过来之前，鸟已经猛扑下来了。
She had pounced on the opportunity to ask for a raise. 她已经抓住了机会要求加薪。

将来时:
The tiger will pounce when it sees its prey. 老虎看到猎物就会猛扑过去。
He will pounce on any evidence against his opponent. 他会抓住任何对他对手不利的证据。

虚拟语气:
If he pounced, he might catch the thief. 如果他猛扑过去，可能会抓住小偷。
If the cat had pounced sooner, it would have caught the mouse. 如果猫早点扑过去，它就能抓住老鼠了。

被动语态:
The mouse was pounced upon by the cat. 老鼠被猫扑住了。
The opportunity was pounced upon by the ambitious young man.  这个雄心勃勃的年轻人抓住了这个机会。

疑问句:
Did the cat pounce? 猫扑了吗？
Will the lion pounce? 狮子会猛扑吗？

否定句:
The cat didn't pounce. 猫没有扑。
He didn't pounce on the chance. 他没有抓住机会。
