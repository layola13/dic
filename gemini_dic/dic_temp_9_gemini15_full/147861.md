It suggests an overemphasis on logic and a disregard for other aspects of human experience. | 例句：His overrational approach to problem-solving prevented him from seeing the emotional needs of his team.  他的过度理性的解决问题的方法妨碍了他看到团队的情感需求。


His overrational analysis ignored the crucial role of chance in the event.  他过于理性的分析忽略了偶然性在这个事件中起到的关键作用。 | 近义词：hyperrational, overly logical, excessively analytical | 反义词：intuitive, emotional, impulsive
