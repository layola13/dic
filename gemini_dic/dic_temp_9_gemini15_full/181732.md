relicenses: /ˌriˈlaɪsnsɪz/ | v. | 再许可；重新授权 | To grant a license again; to reissue a license.  This verb is the third-person plural present tense of "relicense." It describes the act of granting or renewing licenses multiple times. | 例句：The software company relicenses its flagship product annually to its subscribers. = 软件公司每年都会向其订阅者重新授权其旗舰产品。


The company relicenses its patents to other manufacturers. = 这家公司将其专利重新授权给其他制造商。

近义词：reissue, renew, grant (a new license) | 反义词：revoke, cancel
