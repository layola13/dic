funder:/ˈfʌndər/| n. |资助者，捐助者，出资人 | A person or organization that provides money for a project or activity.  | The university's new science building was built thanks to a generous funder. 这所大学的新科学大楼建成要感谢一位慷慨的资助者。 | sponsor, patron, benefactor, investor | recipient, borrower | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government funds many research projects. = 政府资助许多研究项目。
This company is a major funder of the arts. = 这家公司是艺术的主要资助者。

一般过去时:
They funded the project last year. = 他们去年资助了这个项目。
The foundation funded several charitable organizations.  = 该基金会资助了几个慈善组织。

现在完成时:
We have funded several startups this year. = 今年我们已经资助了几家初创公司。
They have funded the research for five years. = 他们已经资助这项研究五年了。

过去完成时:
They had already funded the project before we applied. = 我们申请之前，他们已经资助了该项目。
The company had funded numerous initiatives before declaring bankruptcy. = 公司在宣布破产之前已经资助了许多倡议。


将来时:
They will fund the project next year. = 他们明年将资助该项目。
We will be funding more research in the future. = 我们将来会资助更多的研究。

虚拟语气:
If we had more funding, we could do more research. = 如果我们有更多资金，我们可以做更多研究。
I wish the funder would provide more money. = 我希望资助者能提供更多资金。


被动语态:
The project was funded by a private company. = 该项目由一家私营公司资助。
The research is being funded by a government grant. = 这项研究正在由政府拨款资助。

疑问句:
Who is funding this project? = 谁在资助这个项目？
Where did you get the funding? = 你从哪里获得资金？

否定句:
They did not fund our application. = 他们没有资助我们的申请。
The project was not funded due to lack of resources. = 该项目由于缺乏资源而没有获得资助。
