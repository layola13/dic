gondola:/ˈɡɒndələ/|/ˈɡɑːndələ/| n. |贡多拉；吊篮；缆车|A gondola is a long, narrow, flat-bottomed boat used on the canals of Venice, Italy.  It is typically propelled by a gondolier using a single oar.|例句：We took a romantic gondola ride through the canals of Venice. 我们在威尼斯的运河上乘坐了一艘浪漫的贡多拉。

例句：The gondola was decorated with colorful flowers. 这艘贡多拉装饰着五颜六色的鲜花。


近义词：boat, barge (for some contexts) |反义词：N/A


词源:The word "gondola" ultimately derives from the Greek word "κόνδυλος" (kondylos), meaning "small boat" or "lump, knot"  This term passed into Italian via Venetian.  The specific Venetian evolution involved the diminutive suffix "-ola".
