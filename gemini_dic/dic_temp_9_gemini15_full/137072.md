nonstrike:/ˈnɒnstraɪk/| adj.|非罢工的，未参与罢工的|Not involving a strike; not participating in a strike.  This adjective describes situations, periods, or individuals that are not affected by or involved in a strike.|例句：The nonstrike period allowed for continued productivity.  非罢工时期允许持续生产。

例句：Many employees chose to remain in nonstrike mode. 许多员工选择保持非罢工状态。


近义词：unstruck, non-union (in the context of labor relations), unaffected, continuing


反义词：on strike, striking, involved in a strike

词性:形容词
