poppet:/ˈpɒpɪt/| /ˈpɑːpɪt/| n.|玩偶；小娃娃；心爱的人|A poppet is a small doll or puppet, often used as a child's toy.  It can also be used as a term of endearment for a beloved person, especially a child.|例句：She made a beautiful poppet for her granddaughter. 她为她的孙女做了一个漂亮的玩偶。

The children played with their poppets all afternoon. 孩子们整个下午都在玩他们的玩偶。

词源:
The word "poppet" originates from the Middle English word "popet,"  a diminutive of "puppe," meaning "puppet" or "doll."  Ultimately, it traces back to the Old French word "poupee," also meaning "doll."  The word's evolution reflects the long history of dolls and toys as cherished items for children.
