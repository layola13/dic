/tʃɛks/ | n. |捷克人（复数）；捷克语|The word "Czech's" is the possessive form of the noun "Czech," referring to something belonging to a Czech person or people.  It's the plural possessive form, indicating ownership by more than one Czech person.| Example:  These are Czech's traditional costumes. 这些是捷克人的传统服装。|近义词:  Czechs’ (alternative plural possessive) |反义词: N/A
