lip-reads: /ˈlɪpˌriːdz/ | vt. | 唇读；通过观察说话人的嘴唇动作来理解说话内容 | To understand speech by watching the speaker's lip movements. | 例句：She lip-reads because she is deaf. 她因为失聪而唇读。

She skillfully lip-read the newscaster despite the poor audio quality.  她巧妙地唇读了新闻播音员的话，尽管音频质量很差。| 近义词： decipher (from lip movements) | 反义词：  None.
