outsoaring: /ˌaʊtˈsɔːrɪŋ/ | v. | 超越；凌驾；胜过 |  To surpass or exceed in height, power, or achievement.  |例句：The eagle outsoared all other birds in the sky.  雄鹰超越了天空中的所有其他鸟类。

例句：Her ambition outsoared her capabilities. 她的雄心壮志超过了她的能力。

近义词：surpass, transcend, excel, outstrip, overshadow

反义词：underperform, lag behind, fall short

词源:

The word "outsoaring" is derived from the verb "to soar," meaning to fly high in the air. The prefix "out-" adds the sense of surpassing or exceeding.  "Soar" itself has Old English roots, ultimately related to words signifying "to rise" or "to ascend."
