consolidant: /kənˈsɒlɪdənt/ | n. | 巩固剂，胶结剂 | A substance used to strengthen or consolidate something, especially a porous material such as stone or paper.  |例句：The conservator applied a consolidant to the crumbling fresco to prevent further deterioration.  修复人员在易碎的壁画上涂抹了巩固剂以防止进一步损坏。

例句：The artist used a consolidant to strengthen the weak areas of the canvas before repainting.  艺术家在重新绘画之前使用了巩固剂来加强画布的薄弱区域。


近义词：binder, stabilizer, hardener | 反义词：disintegrant, solvent
