round-table: /ˈraʊndˌteɪbl/ | n. | 圆桌会议；圆桌讨论 | A meeting or discussion in which people of equal status sit around a round table to discuss something, usually implying informal and collaborative discussion.  |  例句：The company held a round-table discussion to brainstorm new product ideas. 公司举行了一次圆桌会议，集思广益，构思新的产品创意。

例句：The peace negotiators attended a round-table conference. 和平谈判代表参加了一次圆桌会议。


近义词：conference, discussion, meeting, forum

反义词：none (no direct antonym)

词性: noun
