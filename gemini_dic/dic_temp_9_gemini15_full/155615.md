philosophized:/fɪˈlɒsəfaɪzd/| v.| 哲学思考；深思熟虑；空谈哲学 | To engage in philosophical thought or speculation; to discuss or consider something in a philosophical manner. |例句：He philosophized about the meaning of life. 他对人生的意义进行了哲学思考。|例句：She philosophized for hours about the nature of reality. 她花了几个小时思考现实的本质。|近义词：pondered, reflected, speculated |反义词：acted, ignored
