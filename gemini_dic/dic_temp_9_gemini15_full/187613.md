rompers:/ˈrɒmpərz/ | n. |连体衣，爬服 |A romper is a one-piece garment, typically worn by babies and young children, that combines a top and bottom into a single outfit.  It often has buttons or snaps at the crotch for easy changing.  Rompers for adults are also fashionable, often resembling a jumpsuit.|例句：My baby looks adorable in her new floral rompers. 我的宝宝穿着她新的花朵图案连体衣看起来很可爱。


例句：She wore a stylish denim romper to the summer party. 她穿着时尚的牛仔连体衣参加夏季派对。


近义词：jumpsuit, playsuit, overall


反义词：separate top and bottom (分开的衣裤)


词性：名词

词源:
The word "romper" likely evolved from the verb "to romp," meaning to play boisterously.  The garment's association with children's active play contributed to its name.  The earliest recorded usage of "rompers" as a garment appears in the early 20th century.
