nonsegregation: /ˌnɒnˌseɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 非隔离；取消种族隔离 | The absence of segregation; the state of not being separated or kept apart, especially on the basis of race.  It often refers to the integration of previously segregated groups, such as in schools or housing. | Example:  The city council voted in favor of nonsegregation in public housing.  市议会投票赞成公共住房的取消种族隔离。


The movement towards nonsegregation was a long and difficult struggle.  争取取消种族隔离的运动是一场漫长而艰难的斗争。| 近义词：integration, desegregation, inclusion | 反义词：segregation, separation, apartheid |  词性: 名词
