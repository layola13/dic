despots:/ˈdɛspɒts/| n. |暴君，专制统治者 |A despot is a ruler with absolute power, often oppressive and tyrannical.  Despots rule through fear and intimidation, often disregarding the rights and needs of their people. | Example:  The despot ruled his kingdom with an iron fist, suppressing any dissent.  暴君铁腕统治他的王国，压制任何异议。|近义词：tyrant, autocrat, dictator |反义词：democrat, egalitarian
