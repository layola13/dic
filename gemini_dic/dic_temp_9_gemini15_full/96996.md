infantlike: /ˈɪnfəntlaɪk/ | adj. | 婴儿似的；幼稚的 | Resembling an infant in behavior or characteristics; childish, immature.  |例句：Her infantlike behavior was surprising for someone her age.  她的婴儿般行为对于她这个年龄的人来说令人惊讶。
例句：He displayed an infantlike dependence on his mother. 他表现出对母亲婴儿般的依赖。|近义词：childlike, childish, babyish, immature |反义词：mature, adult, grown-up |形容词
