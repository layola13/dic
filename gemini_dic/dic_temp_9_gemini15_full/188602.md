rumbas:/ˈrʌmbɑːz/| n. |伦巴舞；伦巴舞曲 |A rumba is a lively dance of Cuban origin, characterized by its rhythmic hip movements and close embrace.  It is also a type of music associated with this dance.|例句：They danced a rumba on the beach.他们在沙滩上跳了伦巴舞。|They played a rumba that got everyone dancing. 他们演奏了一首伦巴舞曲，让每个人都跳了起来。|近义词：dance, cha-cha, salsa |反义词:None specifically
