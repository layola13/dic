jerrycan:/ˈdʒɛriˌkæn/| n. | 军用油桶，携行汽油桶 | A jerrycan is a square or rectangular metal can, typically used for carrying fuel, especially gasoline.  The term originates from the German word "Kanister".  It's known for its robust construction and convenient carrying handle. | Example: The soldiers filled their jerrycans with gasoline before embarking on their mission.  士兵们在开始任务前用汽油灌满了他们的军用油桶。
