nonacting: /ˌnɒnˈæktɪŋ/ | adj. | 不表演的；非表演的 | Not performing; not acting.  This adjective describes something or someone that is not involved in acting or performance. It often refers to roles or individuals in a theatrical or film production who are not directly involved in the performance aspect.


例句：

The nonacting crew members were responsible for set design and lighting.  = 不参与表演的剧组成员负责布景和灯光设计。

He had a nonacting role in the movie, working behind the scenes. = 他在电影中担任非表演角色，在幕后工作。


近义词：inactive, non-performing, backstage


反义词：acting, performing, onstage


词性：形容词
