dischurch:/dɪsˈtʃɜːrtʃ/|  n. |  (非正式)驱逐出教会；开除教籍 |  The word "dischurch" is an informal term meaning to expel someone from a church or religious community. It's not a standard or widely used word.  | Example:  He was dischurched for his heretical views. 他因异端邪说而被逐出教会。 | 近义词:excommunicate, oust, expel | 反义词:ordain, admit
