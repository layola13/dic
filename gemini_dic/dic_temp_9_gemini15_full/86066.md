heard:/hɜːrd/| /hɪərd/| v.|听到，听说|The past tense and past participle of "hear".  It means to perceive sound through the ears. It can also imply understanding or receiving information.|例句：I heard a bird singing. 我听到一只鸟在唱歌。

I heard the news on the radio. 我在收音机上听到了这个消息。

近义词： listened to, perceived, detected |反义词：missed, ignored


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I hear you. = 我听到了。
They hear the music. = 他们听到音乐。


一般过去时:
I heard a loud noise. = 我听到一声巨响。
She heard the doorbell ring. = 她听到门铃响了。


现在完成时:
I have heard that before. = 我以前听过这个。
They have heard the good news. = 他们已经听到了好消息。


过去完成时:
I had heard the story many times. = 我已经听过很多次这个故事了。
She had heard him speak before. = 她以前听过他说话。


将来时:
I will hear from you soon. = 我很快就会收到你的消息。
They will hear the results tomorrow. = 他们明天会听到结果。


虚拟语气:
If I had heard him, I would have helped. = 如果我听到他，我会帮忙的。
I wish I had heard the concert. = 我真希望我听了这场音乐会。


被动语态:
The news was heard by everyone. = 这个消息被每个人都听到了。
His voice was heard clearly. = 他的声音听得很清楚。


疑问句:
Did you hear that? = 你听到那个了吗？
What did you hear? = 你听到了什么？


否定句:
I didn't hear anything. = 我什么都没听到。
They didn't hear the announcement. = 他们没有听到通知。


PHRASE->
heard tell = 听说
have heard = 听说

heard from = 收到...的来信/消息
