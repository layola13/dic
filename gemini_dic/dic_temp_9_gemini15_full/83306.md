gyneocracy:/ˌɡaɪnəˈkrəsi/| n. |女性统治；女权政治|A system of government ruled by women.  A society where women hold the primary power and authority. This term often implies a matriarchal society, but it is not synonymous with matriarchy, as it specifically refers to political power.|Example:  Some feminist thinkers have envisioned a gyneocracy as a utopian society.  一些女权主义思想家设想过一个女性统治的乌托邦社会。|近义词: matriarchy (though not entirely synonymous), female rule|反义词: androcracy, patriarchy
