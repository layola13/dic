girlfriend: /ˈɡɜːrlfrɛnd/ | n. | 女朋友，女友 | A female friend, especially a romantic partner.  |例句：She's my girlfriend. = 她是我的女朋友。

例句：I've been dating my girlfriend for two years. = 我和我女朋友已经约会两年了。


近义词：sweetheart, partner, significant other, love

反义词：boyfriend (for a male perspective)


词性：名词


USAGE->
