hyperpatriotic: /ˌhaɪpərˌpætriˈɑːtɪk/ | adj. | 极端爱国的 | Excessively patriotic; characterized by extreme nationalism and often xenophobia.  It suggests a level of patriotism that goes beyond reasonable or healthy expressions of national pride and can manifest as aggressive or intolerant behavior towards other nations or cultures. |  His hyperpatriotic views led him to believe that his country was superior to all others. 他极端的爱国主义观点使他相信自己的国家优于其他所有国家。


近义词: ultranationalistic, jingoistic, chauvinistic | 反义词: unpatriotic, cosmopolitan, internationalist
