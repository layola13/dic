devolved:/dɪˈvɒlvd/| /dɪˈvɑːlvd/| v.|转移；衰退；退化|To transfer (power or responsibility) from a central government to a lower level of government or to individuals; to deteriorate or decline.|例句：Power has devolved to local authorities.权力已下放给地方当局。|例句：His health devolved rapidly after the accident.事故后他的健康迅速恶化。|近义词：transfer, delegate, deteriorate, decline|反义词：centralize, improve, ascend

Responsibilities are devolved to individual team members. = 责任下放给各个团队成员。
