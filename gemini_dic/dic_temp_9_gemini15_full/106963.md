USAGE->
This word is primarily used as an interjection and therefore doesn't conjugate through verb tenses or grammatical moods like other parts of speech.  The following examples illustrate its usage in different sentence structures:

Declarative Sentence:
Lackaday, the day is done. = 唉，一天结束了。
Lackaday, my hopes are dashed. = 哎哟，我的希望破灭了。


Exclamatory Sentence:
Lackaday! = 唉！
Lackaday, what a mess! = 哎哟，真是一团糟！


Interrogative Sentence (though unusual):
Lackaday, is this all there is? = 唉，难道就只有这些了吗？（This usage is less common）


PHRASE->
Lackaday is rarely used in phrases, as it functions primarily as a standalone interjection. There are no common idiomatic phrases using "lackaday".
