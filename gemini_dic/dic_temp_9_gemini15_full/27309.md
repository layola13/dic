calycular:/kæˈlɪkjʊlə(r)/| adj.|小杯状的；杯状的 | Relating to or resembling a small cup or cupule;  small cup-shaped.  The term often describes small, cup-like structures in botany, particularly associated with the calyx (the sepals of a flower).|例句：The flower had a calycular bract subtending each flower. 这朵花每个花朵下面都有一个小杯状的苞片。

The plant exhibited calycular appendages near the base of its petals. 这植物的花瓣底部有杯状附属物。|近义词：cup-shaped, cupulate |反义词：flat, open
