pot-boiler:/ˈpɒtˌbɔɪlər/| n. |粗制滥造的通俗作品，为赚钱而写的作品|A pot-boiler is a piece of writing, usually a novel or play, that is produced quickly and without much thought or effort, primarily to make money.  The term implies a lack of artistic merit, often sacrificing quality for commercial success.  |Example: He churned out pot-boiler after pot-boiler just to pay the bills. 他为了付账单，不断地创作粗制滥造的作品。|近义词：hack work, commercial fiction, pulp fiction|反义词：masterpiece, classic, work of art|名词
