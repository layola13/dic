rephases:/riːˈfeɪz/| vt.|重新表达，改写|To express something in a different way, often to make it clearer or more effective.  |例句：The spokesperson rephrased his statement to avoid any misunderstanding. 发言人改写了他的声明以避免任何误解。
例句：Could you rephrase that question?  你能重新表达一下那个问题吗？|近义词：reword, reformulate, paraphrase|反义词：leave unchanged


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rephrases his arguments frequently. = 他经常重新表达他的论点。
She rephrases complex ideas into simple terms. = 她将复杂的思想重新表达成简单的术语。


一般过去时:
The teacher rephrased the question for the students. = 老师为学生们重新表达了这个问题。
He rephrased his answer to make it clearer. = 他重新表达了他的答案以使其更清晰。


现在完成时:
She has rephrased the essay several times. = 她已经修改了这篇论文好几次了。
They have rephrased the contract to reflect the new agreement. = 他们已经修改了合同以反映新的协议。


过去完成时:
He had rephrased his speech before delivering it. = 他在发表演讲之前已经修改了他的演讲稿。
She had rephrased the instructions to make them easier to follow. = 她已经修改了说明，使它们更容易理解。


将来时:
He will rephrase his comments after reviewing the data. = 他将在审查数据后重新表达他的评论。
She will rephrase the sentence to improve its flow. = 她将修改句子以改善其流畅性。


虚拟语气:
If I were to rephrase it, I would say this. = 如果我要重新表达它，我会这么说。
I wish I had rephrased my response more carefully. = 我希望我当时更仔细地重新表达我的回应。


被动语态:
The instructions were rephrased to be more user-friendly. = 说明被改写得更方便用户使用。
The proposal will be rephrased before it is submitted. = 该提案将在提交前重新表达。


疑问句:
Should we rephrase this section? = 我们应该改写这部分吗？
How would you rephrase this sentence? = 你会如何改写这句话？


否定句:
He did not rephrase his controversial statement. = 他没有改写他那有争议的声明。
She refused to rephrase her criticism. = 她拒绝改写她的批评。


PHRASE->
rephrase a question = 改写一个问题
rephrase an answer = 改写一个答案
rephrase a sentence = 改写一个句子
rephrase a paragraph = 改写一段话
