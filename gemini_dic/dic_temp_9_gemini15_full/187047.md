rivaling: /ˈraɪvəlɪŋ/ | v. | 与…竞争；比得上 |  Equaling or surpassing in quality or effect.  This word is the present participle of the verb "to rival".|

例句：

Her latest novel is rivaling the bestsellers.  她的最新小说正在与畅销书竞争。

The beauty of the sunset was rivaling that of a painting. 日落之美堪比一幅画作。


近义词：competing with, matching, equaling, surpassing, emulating

反义词：inferior to, falling short of


词性：动词 (verb)

一般现在时:
His new restaurant is rivaling the others in popularity.  他的新餐厅在受欢迎程度上与其他餐厅竞争。
The team is rivaling its previous record. 这个团队正在与之前的记录竞争。

一般过去时:
Last year's sales rivaled those of the previous year. 去年销量与前年相当。
Her performance rivaled the best in the competition. 她的表现与比赛中的最佳选手不相上下。

现在完成时:
His work has rivaled the best in the field for years. 他的作品多年来一直与该领域的最佳作品不相上下。
The company has rivaled its competitors in innovation. 这家公司在创新方面一直与竞争对手不相上下。

过去完成时:
By the end of the game, their score had rivaled the opponent's.  比赛结束时，他们的比分与对手不相上下。
The artist's early works had already rivaled those of established masters.  这位艺术家的早期作品已经可以与已确立地位的大师们相媲美。

将来时:
Next year's model will rival its predecessor in performance. 明年的型号将在性能上与前代产品相媲美。
The new technology is expected to rival existing methods in efficiency. 预计这项新技术在效率上将与现有方法相媲美。

虚拟语气:
If his work rivaled the greats, he would be famous. 如果他的作品能与大师们相媲美，他就会成名。
I wish my skills rivaled those of a professional. 我希望我的技能能与专业人士相媲美。

被动语态:
His achievements were rivaled by few.  他的成就鲜有能与之匹敌的。
The quality of this product is rivaled only by its price. 这个产品的质量只有它的价格才能与之匹敌。

疑问句:
Does his latest song rival his previous hits? 他的最新歌曲能否与他之前的热门歌曲相媲美？
Does the new building rival the old one in grandeur? 这座新建筑的宏伟程度能否与旧建筑相媲美？

否定句:
His performance didn't rival the others.  他的表现没有与其他人相媲美。
The quality of the food didn't rival the restaurant's reputation. 这家餐馆的食物质量配不上其名声。
