puff:/pʌf/| n., v.|一阵轻风；喷出；喘气；膨胀；蓬松；自吹自擂|a small, quick burst of something, especially smoke, air, or steam; to blow out air from the mouth; to swell or inflate; to boast or brag|例句：A puff of smoke rose from the chimney. 一缕轻烟从烟囱升起。He puffed out his chest with pride. 他骄傲地挺起了胸膛。|近义词：gust, blast, exhale, swell, boast|反义词：whimper, deflate, humble
