scriptwriters:/ˈskrɪptˌraɪtərz/| n. |编剧，剧作家 |People who write scripts for films, television programs, plays, or other media.  They are responsible for creating the dialogue, characters, and storyline.|Example:  The scriptwriters worked tirelessly to meet the deadline. 编剧们夜以继日地工作以赶上最后期限。 The scriptwriters for the show are known for their witty dialogue.  这个节目的编剧以其诙谐的对话而闻名。|近义词：screenwriters, playwrights, authors (of scripts)|反义词：directors (in terms of the creative process, not necessarily antagonistic)|名词
