cozy-up:/ˈkoʊziː ʌp/| v.| 偎依；亲近；使舒适；变得舒适 | To move closer to someone or something for warmth or comfort; to become more comfortable or intimate. | 例句：Let's cozy up by the fire. = 让我们在火炉旁取暖。

例句：The couple cozied up on the sofa, watching a movie. = 这对夫妇依偎在沙发上看电影。


近义词：cuddle, snuggle, nestle, huddle


反义词：distance oneself, separate, isolate


词性：动词

We cozy up together when it's cold outside. = 天冷的时候我们都会互相依偎。
