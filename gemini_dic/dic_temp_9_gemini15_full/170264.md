proudling:/ˈpraʊdlɪŋ/| n.|骄傲的小家伙，自负的人|A proudling is a young person who is excessively proud or arrogant.  It is a relatively uncommon word, often used in a slightly humorous or condescending way.|例句：He was quite the proudling, boasting endlessly about his achievements. 他真是个骄傲的小家伙，没完没了地吹嘘自己的成就。|近义词：upstart, braggart, show-off|反义词：humble, modest|名词
