hom:/hɒm/|n.|家；家园；家乡|home refers to the place where a person lives, often with family. It can also refer to a person's native place or country.|例句：My home is in the countryside. 我的家在乡下。|例句：She returned to her childhood home. 她回到了她童年时的家。|近义词：house, residence, dwelling|反义词：abroad, away


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is at home. = 他在家。
They are at home now. = 他们现在在家。

一般过去时:
I was at home yesterday. = 我昨天在家。
She went home early. = 她早早回家了。

现在完成时:
We have been home for hours. = 我们在家好几个小时了。
He has lived in this home for ten years. = 他在这个家住了十年了。

过去完成时:
They had already left home when I arrived. = 我到达时他们已经离开家了。
She had been home for a week before she felt better. = 她在家一个星期后才感觉好些。

将来时:
I will be home by six o'clock. = 我六点钟之前会到家。
They will go home tomorrow. = 他们明天回家。

虚拟语气:
If I had a home, I would be happy. = 如果我有一个家，我会很幸福。
I wish I were at home now. = 我希望我现在在家。

被动语态:
The house was broken into while they were away from home. = 他们不在家的时候，房子被盗了。
The home was destroyed by the fire. = 房子被大火烧毁了。

疑问句:
Is he at home? = 他在家吗？
Are you going home soon? = 你很快就要回家了吗？

否定句:
I am not at home. = 我不在家。
They were not at home last night. = 他们昨晚不在家。
