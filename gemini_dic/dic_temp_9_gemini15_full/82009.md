gripes:/ɡraɪps/| n., v.|抱怨，牢骚；抓牢|Complaints or criticisms; to complain or criticize.|例句：He had a few gripes about the new schedule. 他对新日程有一些抱怨。|近义词：complaints, complaints, grumbles, whines|反义词：praise, compliments|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gripes about his job every day. = 他每天都抱怨他的工作。
She often gripes about the weather. = 她经常抱怨天气。


一般过去时:
He griped about the long meeting yesterday. = 他昨天抱怨了冗长的会议。
She griped to her friends about her boss. = 她向朋友们抱怨她的老板。


现在完成时:
He has griped about this problem for weeks. = 他已经抱怨这个问题好几个星期了。
She has griped repeatedly about the unfair treatment. = 她一再抱怨不公平的待遇。


过去完成时:
He had griped to his supervisor before he resigned. = 他辞职前已经向他的主管抱怨过。
She had griped many times about the poor service before writing the letter. = 在写信之前，她已经多次抱怨过糟糕的服务。


将来时:
He will gripe about the noise again if it continues. = 如果噪音继续，他会再次抱怨。
She will gripe to the manager if the issue is not resolved. = 如果问题得不到解决，她会向经理抱怨。


虚拟语气:
I wish he wouldn't gripe so much. = 我希望他不那么抱怨。
If he had griped earlier, the problem might have been solved. = 如果他早点抱怨，问题可能就解决了。


被动语态:
His complaints were often griped about by his colleagues. = 他的抱怨经常被他的同事们抱怨。
The new policy was griped about by many employees. = 许多员工抱怨新的政策。


疑问句:
What are you griping about? = 你在抱怨什么？
Why is he always griping? = 他为什么总是抱怨？


否定句:
He didn't gripe about the workload this time. = 他这次没有抱怨工作量。
She didn't gripe even though she was clearly unhappy. = 即使她显然不高兴，她也并没有抱怨。



PHRASE->
have a gripe = 抱怨
air one's gripes = 发泄不满
a gripe session = 抱怨大会
get one's gripes off one's chest = 吐露苦水
a bag of gripes = 一堆抱怨
