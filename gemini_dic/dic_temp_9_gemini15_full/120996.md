misclassification:/ˌmɪsklæsifiˈkeɪʃən/| n. |错误分类，误分类|The act of classifying something incorrectly; the state of being incorrectly classified.  It often refers to errors in assigning data points or objects to categories. |Example: The misclassification of this plant led to incorrect treatment.  这种植物的错误分类导致了错误的处理。

The misclassification of patients into different risk groups resulted in inappropriate treatment strategies.  将患者错误地分类到不同的风险组导致了不合适的治疗策略。|近义词：wrong classification, incorrect categorization, misidentification|反义词：correct classification, accurate categorization
