out-voter:/ˌaʊtˈvoʊtər/| n. |投票淘汰者；得票多者|A person who votes someone out of office or a position.  Someone who casts a decisive vote to defeat a candidate or proposal.  |例句：The incumbent mayor was the out-voter in the recent election. 近期选举中，现任市长是被投票淘汰者。|近义词： victor, winner, successful candidate |反义词：loser, defeated candidate |名词
