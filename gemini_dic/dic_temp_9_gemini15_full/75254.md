forward:/ˈfɔːrwərd/| adv.|向前，前进；将来|Moving towards the front or a future time.|例句：He stepped forward to greet her. 他向前一步迎接她。|近义词：ahead, onward|反义词：backward, behind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car moves forward. = 车子向前行驶。
She walks forward confidently. = 她自信地向前走。


一般过去时:
The soldiers marched forward. = 士兵们向前行进。
He leaned forward to hear better. = 他向前倾听以便听得更清楚。


现在完成时:
We have moved forward significantly. = 我们已经取得了显著进展。
She has looked forward to this day for years. = 她多年来一直期待着这一天。


过去完成时:
They had already moved forward before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经向前移动了。
He had looked forward to the vacation for months. = 他几个月来一直期待着假期。


将来时:
We will move forward with the project. = 我们将继续推进这个项目。
She will look forward to seeing her family. = 她将期待着见到她的家人。


虚拟语气:
If we moved forward, we would succeed. = 如果我们向前推进，我们将成功。
I wish we could move forward more quickly. = 我希望我们能够更快地取得进展。


被动语态:
The plan was moved forward. = 计划被提前了。
The meeting has been moved forward to next week. = 会议已被提前到下周。


疑问句:
Will you move forward? = 你会向前吗？
Can we move forward with the discussion? = 我们可以继续讨论吗？


否定句:
He didn't move forward. = 他没有向前移动。
She didn't look forward to the task. = 她不期待这项任务。


PHRASE->
look forward to: 期待
move forward: 向前移动；取得进展
go forward: 前进；继续
come forward: 挺身而出；站出来
from forward: 从前；自始至终
