prophy:/ˈprɒfi/| n.|预防性治疗；预防措施|Prophylaxis, short for prophylactic treatment, refers to measures taken to prevent disease.  It often involves actions taken before a person becomes ill to prevent the onset of disease, such as vaccination or taking medication to prevent infection. | 例句：The dentist recommended a prophy to prevent future cavities.  牙医建议进行预防性治疗以防止未来出现龋齿。


例句：Regular dental prophy is important for maintaining good oral hygiene. 定期进行牙齿预防性治疗对于保持良好的口腔卫生很重要。


近义词：prevention, precaution, prophylaxis


反义词：treatment (of existing disease)


词性：名词
