consumables:/kənˈsjuːməblz/| n.|消耗品，易耗品|Items that are used up or consumed during the course of a process or operation.  These are often regularly replaced.|The office needs to replenish its stock of consumables, such as paper, toner, and pens.|近义词：supplies, provisions, expendables|反义词：durables, assets
