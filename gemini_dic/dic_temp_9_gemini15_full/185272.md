retransplantation:/ˌriːtrænzˌplænˈteɪʃən/| n. |再移植；再种植|The act of transplanting something again, especially a plant or organ.  It involves removing something from one location and planting or implanting it in a new location after a period of time. |例句：The retransplantation of the kidney was successful.  肾脏再移植手术成功了。
The farmer decided on retransplantation of the rice seedlings after the flood.  洪水过后，农民决定对水稻秧苗进行再移植。|近义词：replanting, reimplantation|反义词：removal, extraction
