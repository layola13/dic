lethargic: /ləˈθɑːrdʒɪk/ | adj. | 倦怠的；无精打采的；懒散的 |  Feeling or showing a lack of energy and enthusiasm.  Characterized by or suffering from lethargy. |

例句：

After a long day's work, she felt lethargic and went straight to bed.  一天辛苦的工作后，她感到倦怠，直接上床睡觉了。

He was lethargic and uninterested in the conversation. 他无精打采，对谈话不感兴趣。


近义词：apathetic, sluggish, drowsy, listless, indolent, languid

反义词：energetic, enthusiastic, lively, vigorous, active


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is lethargic today. 他今天很懒散。
She feels lethargic after a long flight. 长途飞行后她感到倦怠。

一般过去时:
He was lethargic after the surgery.  手术后他很倦怠。
She was lethargic and couldn't get out of bed. 她无精打采，起不了床。

现在完成时:
I have been lethargic all week. 我整个星期都无精打采。
She has felt lethargic since her illness. 她自从生病以来就一直很懒散。

过去完成时:
He had been lethargic for days before he finally went to the doctor. 他几天都无精打采，最后才去看医生。
They had been lethargic all morning, so they decided to take a break. 他们整个上午都无精打采，所以决定休息一下。

将来时:
I will probably be lethargic tomorrow after the party. 派对后我明天可能会很疲惫。
She thinks she will be lethargic if she doesn't get enough sleep. 她认为如果睡眠不足，她会变得懒散。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't stayed up so late, I wouldn't be so lethargic now. 如果我没有熬夜到那么晚，我现在就不会这么懒散了。
I wish I weren't so lethargic; I have so much to do. 我真希望自己不要这么懒散；我有很多事情要做。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)  This is not commonly used in passive voice.

疑问句:
Are you feeling lethargic today? 你今天感觉很懒散吗？
Is he always so lethargic? 他总是这么懒散吗？

否定句:
He is not lethargic; he's just tired. 他不是懒散，只是累了。
She was not lethargic; she was simply bored. 她不是懒散，只是无聊。


PHRASE->

feel lethargic: 感觉倦怠
become lethargic: 变得懒散
seem lethargic: 看起来懒散
look lethargic: 看起来无精打采
remarkably lethargic: 非常懒散


词源:

From Late Latin *lethargicus, from Greek lethargikos, from lethargos "forgetfulness, drowsiness, lethargy," from lethe "forgetfulness" (related to letō "oblivion") + -argos "-working" (from argos "idle, inactive").  The suffix "-ic" means "relating to".
