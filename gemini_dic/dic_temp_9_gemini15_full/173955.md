quasi-skillfully:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈskɪlflɪli/ | adv. | 几乎熟练地，似是而非地熟练地 |  Almost skillfully; in a way that seems skillful but is not truly so.  It implies a lack of genuine mastery, suggesting an attempt at skill that falls short of true proficiency. | Example: He quasi-skillfully navigated the crowded marketplace, bumping into several people along the way. 他几乎熟练地穿梭于拥挤的市场，沿路撞到了几个人。 | 近义词:  clumsily skillful,  apparently skillful, seemingly adept | 反义词: ineptly, clumsily, unskillfully |  词性: 副词
