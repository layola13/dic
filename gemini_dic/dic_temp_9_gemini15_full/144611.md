outwhirled:/ˌaʊtˈhwɜːrld/| v.|超过…旋转；旋转得超过 |To surpass in whirling; to whirl beyond.  This word is a relatively rare and literary usage, implying a forceful and surpassing action of whirling. |例句：The tornado outwhirled the previous record-breaking storm, causing widespread devastation. 龙卷风旋转的速度超过了之前的破纪录的风暴，造成了大范围的破坏。The dancer's pirouette outwhirled all others in grace and speed. 舞者的旋转在优雅和速度上都超过了其他人。|近义词：outspin, surpass, exceed|反义词：underwhelm, underspin
