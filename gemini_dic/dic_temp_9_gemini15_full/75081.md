The specific type of bean can vary depending on region and context. |例句：We had frijoles for dinner last night. 昨晚我们吃了豆子。|例句：The recipe calls for a cup of cooked frijoles. 这个食谱需要一杯煮熟的豆子。|近义词：bean, legume |反义词：无
