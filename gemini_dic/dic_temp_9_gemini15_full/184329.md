respecters:/rɪˈspɛktərz/| n.|尊重者，敬重者|Those who show respect.  People who admire and honor others or things.  The word is the plural form of "respecter".|例句：The respecters of tradition often resist change.  尊重传统的人往往抵制变革。

例句：The artist's respecters flocked to the gallery opening. 艺术家的尊重者们蜂拥而至画廊开幕式。


近义词：admirers, venerators, honorers, followers


反义词：despisers, scorners, contemners


词性: 名词
