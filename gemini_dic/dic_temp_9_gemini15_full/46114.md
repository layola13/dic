cylindrically:/sɪˈlɪndrɪkli/| adj.|圆柱形地；柱状地|In a cylindrical manner; having the form of a cylinder.  The cake was decorated cylindrically.|例句：The candles were arranged cylindrically around the cake.  蜡烛被圆柱形地排列在蛋糕周围。

例句：The data was presented cylindrically in a 3D graph. 数据以3D图形的圆柱形方式呈现。

近义词：tubularly, columnarly

反义词：spherically, cubically


词源:  From cylindrical + -ly.  "Cylindrical" itself derives from "cylinder," ultimately from Greek *kulindros ("roller"), from *kylindrein ("to roll").
