quincewort:/kwɪntsˈwɜːrt/| n. |金丝桃属植物|A plant of the genus *Cotinus*, especially *Cotinus coggygria*,  also known as smoke bush or smoke tree,  valued for its attractive foliage and fluffy flower heads.  The name likely derives from a resemblance of its leaves to those of the quince tree.|The quincewort's vibrant autumn colors make it a popular choice for landscaping.|近义词：smoke bush, smoke tree|反义词：无
