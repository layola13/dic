overcriticisms:/ˌoʊvərˈkrɪtɪsɪzəm/| n. |过度批评；吹毛求疵|Excessive criticism; fault-finding.  It often implies that the criticism is unfair or excessive in amount. The plural form is used because it refers to multiple instances of criticism.|例句：His work has been subject to overcriticisms, which has discouraged him. 他的作品受到了过度批评，这让他很沮丧。


例句：The project received overcriticisms from the committee, delaying its implementation.  该项目受到了委员会的过度批评，延误了项目的实施。


近义词：hypercriticism, excessive criticism, fault-finding, nitpicking


反义词：praise, commendation, approval
