overhauls:/ˈoʊvərˌhɔːlz/ | n. vt. |彻底检修；大修；彻底改革 |  A complete and thorough repair or renovation.  It can also refer to significant changes or reforms. |


例句：
The car needs a major overhaul. = 这辆车需要大修。

The company is undergoing a complete overhaul of its management structure. = 公司正在对其管理结构进行彻底改革。


近义词：repair, renovate, revamp, reform, overhaul


反义词：neglect, deterioration


词性:
名词 (n.):  a thorough repair or renovation.
动词 (vt.): to thoroughly repair or renovate; to make significant changes to.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mechanic overhauls engines every day. = 机械师每天都检修发动机。
She overhauls her wardrobe every season. = 她每个季度都会彻底更新她的衣橱。


一般过去时:
They overhauled the engine last week. = 他们上周检修了发动机。
The government overhauled the tax system. = 政府彻底改革了税收制度。


现在完成时:
He has overhauled his investment strategy. = 他已经修改了他的投资策略。
The company has overhauled its marketing plan. = 公司已经修改了它的市场营销计划。


过去完成时:
They had overhauled the machine before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经检修了机器。
She had overhauled her life before she met him. = 在遇到他之前，她已经彻底改变了自己的生活。


将来时:
We will overhaul the website next month. = 我们下个月将改版网站。
The factory will overhaul its production line. = 工厂将对其生产线进行大修。


虚拟语气:
If we overhauled the system, it would be more efficient. = 如果我们彻底改革系统，它将会更高效。
I wish they had overhauled the building sooner. = 我希望他们早点对这栋建筑进行大修。


被动语态:

The curriculum is being overhauled for next year. = 明年的课程正在进行改革。


疑问句:

否定句:
They didn't overhaul the equipment. = 他们没有检修设备。
