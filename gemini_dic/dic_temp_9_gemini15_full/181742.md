relied:/rɪˈlaɪd/| v.|依赖，依靠，信赖|to depend on someone or something for help or support|例句：I relied on my friend for help. 我依靠我的朋友帮忙。

例句：She relied heavily on her parents' financial support. 她非常依赖父母的经济支持。


近义词：depend on, count on, trust in|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He relies on his intuition. = 他依靠他的直觉。
They rely on each other for support. = 他们互相依靠以获得支持。


一般过去时:
I relied on him to finish the project. = 我依赖他完成项目。
She relied on her memory for the details. = 她依靠记忆来回忆细节。


现在完成时:
We have relied on this system for years. = 我们多年来一直依赖这个系统。
He has relied on his family throughout his life. = 他一生都依靠他的家庭。


过去完成时:
They had relied on the old equipment until it broke down. = 他们一直依赖旧设备直到它坏掉。
She had relied on him to help, but he was unavailable. = 她一直依靠他帮忙，但他不在。


将来时:
I will rely on you to get this done. = 我将依靠你完成这件事。
They will rely on new technology to improve efficiency. = 他们将依靠新技术来提高效率。


虚拟语气:
If I were to succeed, I would rely on my team. = 如果我要成功，我会依靠我的团队。
I wish I had relied on my instincts earlier. = 我希望我早点相信我的直觉。


被动语态:
The decision was heavily relied upon by the committee. = 委员会非常依赖这个决定。
The project's success is relied upon by many people. = 许多人依赖项目的成功。


疑问句:
Can you rely on him to keep a secret? = 你能相信他会保守秘密吗？
Do they rely on outside funding? = 他们依赖外部资金吗？


否定句:
I don't rely on luck. = 我不依赖运气。
They didn't rely on anyone for help. = 他们没有依靠任何人帮忙。


PHRASE->
rely on/upon = 依赖，依靠
rely heavily on = 非常依赖
rely for support = 依靠支持
rely entirely on = 完全依赖
rely on oneself = 自力更生
