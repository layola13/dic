reaccompany:/ˌriːəkˈʌmpəni/ | vt. |再次陪伴；重新陪伴 |To accompany again; to go with someone or something again.  |例句：After a brief separation, the family reaccompanied each other on their journey.  再次分别后，这家人在旅途中又重新陪伴在一起。  |例句：The musician reaccompanied the singer for the encore. 音乐家为返场演出再次为歌手伴奏。 |近义词：rejoin, accompany again |反义词：leave, abandon |动词


USAGE->

He reaccompanies his wife to the theatre every week. 他每周都再次陪妻子去剧院。
They reaccompany the tourists on their guided tour. 他们再次陪同游客进行导览游。

现在完成时:
