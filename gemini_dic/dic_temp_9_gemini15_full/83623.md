haemochromometer:/ˌhiːməʊˈkrɒmɪtə(r)/| n. |血色计|An instrument used to measure the concentration of hemoglobin in blood.  It typically involves comparing the color of a blood sample to a standard. |The haemochromometer provided an accurate reading of the patient's hemoglobin levels.|近义词：hemoglobinometer|反义词：无
