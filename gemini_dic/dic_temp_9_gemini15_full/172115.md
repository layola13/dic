purposing:/ˈpɜːrpəsɪŋ/| v.|有意图地做；目的在于|Intending to do something; aiming at something.|例句：He was purposing to leave early. 他打算早点离开。|例句：She purposed to finish the project by Friday. 她打算在星期五之前完成这个项目。|近义词：intending, aiming, planning|反义词：accidentally, unintentionally


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is purposing to visit his parents. = 他打算去看望他的父母。
They are purposing to start a new business. = 他们打算开始一项新业务。

一般过去时:
She purposed to call him later. = 她打算稍后再给他打电话。
He purposed to finish the report by the deadline. = 他打算在截止日期前完成报告。

现在完成时:
We have purposed to donate to the charity. = 我们已经打算向慈善机构捐款。
They have purposed to travel to Europe next year. = 他们已经打算明年去欧洲旅行。

过去完成时:
I had purposed to go to the concert, but I got sick. = 我本来打算去听音乐会，但我病了。
She had purposed to buy a new car, but changed her mind. = 她本来打算买一辆新车，但改变了主意。

将来时:
He will purpose to propose to his girlfriend. = 他将打算向他的女朋友求婚。
They will purpose to renovate their house next spring. = 他们打算明年春天翻新他们的房子。

虚拟语气:
If I had purposed to study harder, I would have passed the exam. = 如果我本来打算更努力学习，我就会通过考试。
I wish I had purposed to save more money. = 我真希望我本来打算存更多钱。

被动语态:
The meeting was purposed to discuss the new strategy. = 会议的目的是讨论新战略。(注意：被动语态在此处略显不自然，通常用更简洁的表达。)
The project was purposed to improve efficiency. = 项目的目的是提高效率。(注意：被动语态在此处略显不自然，通常用更简洁的表达。)

疑问句:
What are you purposing to do this weekend? = 你这个周末打算做什么？
What did they purpose to achieve with this project? = 他们打算通过这个项目实现什么？

否定句:
He did not purpose to offend anyone. = 他并不打算冒犯任何人。
She did not purpose to stay out late. = 她并不打算在外面待到很晚。
