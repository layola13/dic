/ˈrɛkwɪəˌtɔːri/ | n. |安魂曲，悼念音乐 | A requiem is a musical composition, typically a Mass, intended to be sung for the repose of the souls of the dead.  It is often a solemn and moving piece of music. | Example: The choir sang a beautiful requiem at the funeral.  合唱团在葬礼上演唱了一首优美的安魂曲。|近义词：dirge, lament |反义词：celebration, jubilation |名词
