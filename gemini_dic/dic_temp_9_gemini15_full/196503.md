services:/ˈsɜːrvɪsɪz/ | n. | 服务；业务；服役；宗教仪式 |  Services refer to acts of help or work done for someone.  It can also refer to a system supplying a particular need (e.g., public services).  In a religious context, it means a religious ceremony. | Example:  The hotel provides excellent room service. 这家酒店提供优质的客房服务。  

The church holds services every Sunday. 教堂每周日举行礼拜仪式。|近义词：assistance, aid, help, ministry, functions|反义词：disruption, hindrance, obstruction|名词
