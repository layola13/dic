gowning:/ˈɡaʊnɪŋ/| n. |穿长袍；穿手术衣；穿礼服|The act of putting on a gown, especially a surgical gown or a ceremonial robe.  It can also refer to the process of getting dressed in a gown.|例句：The pre-operative gowning procedure is crucial for maintaining sterility.  术前穿手术衣的程序对于维持无菌状态至关重要。

例句：The gowning ceremony for the graduation was very formal and impressive. 毕业典礼上的穿袍仪式非常正式和令人印象深刻。

近义词：robing, dressing (in a gown)
反义词：undressing (from a gown), disrobing


词源:  Derived from the noun "gown," meaning a long loose robe, which itself comes from the Old French "goune" and ultimately from the Germanic root.
