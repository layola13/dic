emit:/ɪˈmɪt/| vt.|发出，放射，散发|to send out something such as light, heat, sound, gas, or liquid|The sun emits light and heat. 太阳发出光和热。The factory emitted a large amount of pollutants into the atmosphere. 工厂向大气中排放了大量的污染物。|近义词：give off, release, radiate, discharge|反义词：absorb, receive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun emits light and heat. = 太阳发出光和热。
The factory emits pollutants into the air. = 工厂向空气中排放污染物。


一般过去时:
The volcano emitted a huge cloud of ash. = 火山喷发出巨大的火山灰云。
The speaker emitted a long, low sigh. = 演讲者发出一声长长的叹息。


现在完成时:
The car has emitted a strange noise all day. = 这辆车整天都在发出奇怪的噪音。
The plant has emitted toxic fumes for years. = 这家工厂多年来一直在排放有毒烟雾。


过去完成时:
The machine had emitted a warning signal before it broke down. = 机器在故障前发出过警告信号。
The factory had emitted excessive pollutants before the regulations were implemented. = 在实施法规之前，这家工厂排放了过量的污染物。


将来时:
The device will emit a signal when it detects movement. = 当设备检测到移动时，它将发出信号。
The new technology will emit less pollution. = 新技术将排放更少的污染。


虚拟语气:
If the device emitted a stronger signal, we would be able to detect it. = 如果设备发出更强的信号，我们就能探测到它。
If the factory had not emitted so much pollution, the river would not be so polluted. = 如果工厂没有排放那么多污染物，这条河就不会被污染得这么严重。


被动语态:
A high-pitched sound was emitted from the machine. = 机器发出了高亢的声音。
Large amounts of greenhouse gases are emitted by vehicles. = 大量的温室气体是由车辆排放的。


疑问句:
What kind of radiation does the sun emit? = 太阳发出什么类型的辐射？
Does the device emit any harmful radiation? = 该设备会发出任何有害辐射吗？


否定句:
The device did not emit any signal. = 该设备没有发出任何信号。
The factory does not emit as much pollution as it used to. = 工厂现在排放的污染物不像以前那么多了。
