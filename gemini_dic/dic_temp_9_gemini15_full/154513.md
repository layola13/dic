perpetrators:/ˈpɜːrpətreɪtərz/ | n. | 作恶者；犯罪者 | Those who commit a crime or other harmful act.  The perpetrators of the crime were finally caught.  犯罪者最终被抓获了。 |近义词：offenders, culprits, wrongdoers | 反义词：victims, sufferers
