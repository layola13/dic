plow:/plaʊ/| n., v.|犁；耕地；费力地前进 | a farming tool used to turn over the soil before planting; to turn over soil with a plow; to move forward with great effort |例句：The farmer used a plow to till the field.  农民用犁耕地。

The ship plowed through the heavy seas. 船费力地驶过汹涌的大海。|近义词：plough(英式拼写), till, furrow |反义词：harvest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plows the field every spring. 他每年春天都耕地。
Farmers plow their land to prepare for planting. 农民耕地为播种做准备。


一般过去时:
They plowed the field yesterday. 他们昨天耕地了。
The farmer plowed the land before planting the seeds. 农民在播种前耕了地。


现在完成时:
He has plowed all the fields. 他已经耕完所有的地了。
The farmers have plowed the land in preparation for the harvest. 农民们已经耕好了地，为收割做准备。


过去完成时:
They had plowed the field before the rain started.  在下雨之前他们已经耕好了地。
The farmer had plowed the entire field by sunset. 农民在日落前耕完了整块地。


将来时:
We will plow the field next week. 我们下周将耕地。
They will plow the land after the snow melts. 雪融化后他们会耕地。


虚拟语气:
If I had a plow, I would plow the field myself. 如果我有一台犁，我会自己耕地。
I wish I had plowed the field earlier. 我真希望我早点耕地。


被动语态:
The field was plowed by the farmer. 田地是被农民耕种的。
The land is being plowed in preparation for planting.  土地正在被耕种，为播种做准备。


疑问句:
Did they plow the field? 他们耕地了吗？
Have you ever plowed a field? 你曾经耕过地吗？


否定句:
He didn't plow the field today. 他今天没有耕地。
They haven't plowed the field yet. 他们还没耕地。


PHRASE->
plow through:  努力完成（工作等）
plow back:  (利润)再投资
plow under:  掩盖，压制
break the plow:  停止耕作
plow ahead:  继续努力
