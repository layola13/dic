skilfulness:/ˈskɪlfʊlnəs/ | n. |熟练；技巧；技术 |The ability to do something well; expertise.  It implies a high level of proficiency and dexterity, often developed through practice and experience. |例句：Her skilfulness in handling the difficult situation was remarkable.  她的出色技巧处理了这个棘手的情况。

例句：The surgeon's skilfulness saved the patient's life.  外科医生的熟练技术挽救了病人的生命。


近义词：expertise, proficiency, dexterity, mastery, adeptness, skill

反义词：clumsiness, ineptitude, awkwardness
