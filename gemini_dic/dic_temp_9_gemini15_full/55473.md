distortive: /dɪˈstɔːrtɪv/ | adj. | 扭曲的；歪曲的；失真的 | Causing distortion; tending to distort.  Something that is distortive changes the appearance, sound, or meaning of something else, often in a negative way.  | 例句：The artist used distortive techniques to create a sense of unease.  艺术家运用扭曲的技巧来营造不安感。

The government's policies have had a distortive effect on the market. 政府的政策对市场产生了扭曲的影响。


近义词：perverting, warping, falsifying, misrepresenting | 反义词：accurate, truthful, undistorted, objective


词源：源自动词distort，由拉丁语 *dis- (表示否定)+ torquēre (扭曲)演变而来。
