self-helpful: /ˌsɛlfˈhɛlpfəl/ | adj. | 自助的；有益于自己的；利己的 |  Self-helpful refers to something that is beneficial or advantageous to oneself. It can describe actions, methods, or resources that enable someone to solve their own problems or improve their own situation.  However, it can also carry a negative connotation, implying selfishness or a lack of seeking help from others. | 例句：The self-help book offered practical, self-helpful advice on managing stress. 这本自助书籍提供了关于压力管理的实用且有益于自己的建议。

The self-helpful nature of the program allowed participants to learn at their own pace.  该计划自助的特性允许参与者以自己的节奏学习。


近义词：beneficial, advantageous, helpful (in a positive sense); selfish, egoistic (in a negative sense) | 反义词：harmful, detrimental, unhelpful, selfless, altruistic | 词性：形容词
