red-hot:/ˌredˈhɑːt/| adj. |火热的；极好的；最新的|extremely hot;  very exciting or popular; brand new or very recent |例句：The new band is red-hot.  这个新乐队非常火爆。
例句：The iron was red-hot.  铁烧红了。|近义词：fiery, scorching, popular, brand-new, latest|反义词：cold, lukewarm, unpopular, outdated, old
