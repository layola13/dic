rethoryke:/rɛˈtɔːrɪk/| n. |修辞学，雄辩术|The art of effective or persuasive speaking or writing, especially the use of figures of speech and other compositional techniques.|Example:  He mastered the art of rethoryke, using eloquent speeches to sway the masses. 他掌握了修辞的艺术，用雄辩的演讲来影响大众。|近义词：eloquence, rhetoric, oratory|反义词：inarticulateness, silence
