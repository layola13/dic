chomps:/tʃɑːmps/| v.|用力咀嚼；大声咀嚼|To bite or chew something noisily and forcefully.|例句：The dog chomped on the bone.  那条狗用力啃着骨头。|例句：He chomped his food loudly, disturbing everyone at the table. 他大声咀嚼食物，扰乱了桌上的每个人。|近义词：chew, munch, gnaw|反义词：sip, nibble


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chomps his food loudly. = 他大声咀嚼食物。
The cow chomps the grass in the field. = 母牛在田里吃草。

一般过去时:
She chomped on an apple after school. = 放学后她啃了一个苹果。
The bear chomped the fish with gusto. = 熊津津有味地啃着鱼。

现在完成时:
He has chomped through half the cake already. = 他已经吃掉了半块蛋糕。
The rabbits have chomped all the lettuce. = 兔子把所有的莴苣都啃光了。

过去完成时:
The dog had chomped the toy before I could stop him. = 在我阻止它之前，狗已经啃坏了玩具。
She had chomped all the cookies before the party started. = 派对开始前，她已经把所有的饼干都吃掉了。

将来时:
The baby will chomp on that rattle soon. = 婴儿很快就会啃那个拨浪鼓。
He will chomp through this assignment quickly. = 他会很快完成这项任务。


虚拟语气:
If he were hungrier, he would chomp down that whole pizza. = 如果他更饿的话，他会把整个比萨饼都吃掉。
I wish I could chomp on a delicious burger right now. = 我真希望我现在能吃一个美味的汉堡。

被动语态:
The carrot was chomped in half by the rabbit. = 胡萝卜被兔子啃成两半。
The book was chomped on by the puppy. = 小狗啃坏了书。

疑问句:
Is he chomping on something? = 他在啃什么东西吗？
Does the baby chomp on everything he finds? = 婴儿是不是什么东西都啃？

否定句:
She didn't chomp her food; she ate it delicately. = 她没有大声咀嚼食物；她吃得很优雅。
The horse didn't chomp on the hay. = 马没有啃干草。
