phyllophorous: /ˌfaɪləˈfɔːrəs/ | adj. | 叶状的，有叶的 | Bearing leaves; leaf-bearing.  The term typically refers to structures resembling leaves, not necessarily true leaves in the botanical sense.  It can describe plants with leaf-like structures or organisms with appendages that resemble leaves. | 例句：The phyllophorous branches of the coral provided shelter for small fish.  = 珊瑚的叶状分支为小鱼提供了庇护。


The phyllophorous fronds of this seaweed are remarkably delicate. =  这种海藻的叶状叶片非常精致。


近义词：foliaceous, leafy | 反义词：leafless, aphylous


词源:  From Greek *phyllon* ("leaf") + *phoros* ("bearing").  The combining form *phyllo-* is common in botanical terminology.
