heelpost: /ˈhiːlpoʊst/ | n. |后跟柱，后跟桩 | A heelpost is a strong vertical post, often made of wood or metal, used to support the heel of a structure, such as a staircase, a gate, or a piece of furniture.  It provides stability and prevents sagging. | Example: The sturdy heelpost supported the weight of the heavy oak gate.  这根结实的后跟柱支撑着沉重的橡木门的重量。  The heelposts of the staircase were intricately carved.  楼梯的后跟柱雕刻精美。| 近义词：support post, upright, stanchion | 反义词：N/A
