eaters:/ˈiːtərz/| n.| 食客；食者 |Those who consume food.  People or animals that eat.|例句：The restaurant was full of hungry eaters. 这家餐馆挤满了饥饿的食客。
例句：The birds are the main eaters of the insects in this area.  鸟类是该地区主要的昆虫捕食者。|近义词：consumers, devourers, diners|反义词：producers (in an ecological context)
