policy:/ˈpɒləsi/ /ˈpɑːləsi/| n.|政策；策略；保险单| A course or principle of action adopted or proposed by a government, party, business, or individual.  A document stating the terms of an insurance contract.|例句：The government announced a new economic policy. 政府宣布了一项新的经济政策。

例句：She took out a life insurance policy. 她购买了一份人寿保险单。


近义词：strategy, plan, program, guideline, principle

反义词：chance, accident


词性:名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company's policy is to promote from within. = 公司的政策是从内部提拔。
Our policy dictates that we must respond within 24 hours. = 我们的政策规定我们必须在24小时内回复。


一般过去时:
Their policy was to ignore the problem. = 他们的政策是忽视这个问题。
The government's policy changed dramatically last year. = 政府的政策去年发生了巨大的变化。


现在完成时:
The company has implemented a new environmental policy. = 公司已经实施了一项新的环保政策。
They have consistently followed a policy of transparency. = 他们一直坚持透明的政策。


过去完成时:
Before the crisis, their policy had been to expand aggressively. = 在危机之前，他们的政策是一直积极扩张。
The company had already changed its return policy before I returned the item. = 我退货之前，公司已经更改了其退货政策。


将来时:
The government will announce its new education policy next week. = 政府将于下周宣布其新的教育政策。
We will adopt a more flexible working policy in the future. = 我们将来会采取更灵活的工作政策。


虚拟语气:
If they had a better policy, the situation would be different. = 如果他们有更好的政策，情况就会有所不同。
I wish the company had a clearer return policy. = 我希望公司有更清晰的退货政策。


被动语态:
The new policy was approved by the board. = 新政策得到了董事会的批准。
A comprehensive environmental policy is being developed. = 正在制定一项全面的环境政策。


疑问句:
What is the company's policy on sick leave? = 公司关于病假的政策是什么？
What is the return policy for this item? = 这件商品的退货政策是什么？


否定句:
The company does not have a written policy on social media use. = 公司没有关于社交媒体使用的书面政策。
Their policy did not address the issue of data security. = 他们的政策没有解决数据安全问题。
