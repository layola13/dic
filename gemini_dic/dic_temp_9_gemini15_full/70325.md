field-tests:/ˈfiːldˌtɛsts/| n. |现场测试，实地测试|Tests conducted in real-world conditions, rather than in a controlled laboratory setting.  These tests aim to evaluate the performance and reliability of a product or system under actual usage conditions.|例句：The engineers conducted extensive field tests to assess the durability of the new bridge. 工程师们进行了广泛的现场测试以评估新桥的耐久性。

The software underwent rigorous field tests before its official release.  该软件在正式发布前进行了严格的现场测试。|近义词：practical tests, real-world tests, operational tests|反义词：laboratory tests, simulated tests|名词
