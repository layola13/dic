philosopher:/fɪˈlɒsəfər/ /ˈfɪləsʌfər/ | n. |哲学家 |A philosopher is a person who studies or writes about philosophy.  They engage in critical thinking about fundamental questions of reality, existence, knowledge, values, reason, mind, and language. |Example: Socrates is considered one of the most influential philosophers in Western history. 苏格拉底被认为是西方历史上最有影响力的哲学家之一。|近义词：thinker, scholar, sage |反义词：ignorant, simpleton
