callosum:/kəˈloʊsəm/ | n. |胼胝体|The callosum is a large bundle of nerve fibers connecting the left and right cerebral hemispheres in the brain. It facilitates communication between the two hemispheres, enabling the coordination of functions and information processing.  | Example:  Damage to the corpus callosum can result in a range of neurological deficits, impacting motor skills, language processing, and cognitive abilities.  胼胝体的损伤会导致一系列神经系统缺陷，影响运动技能、语言处理和认知能力。|近义词: corpus callosum (more formal)|反义词: N/A
