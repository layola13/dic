esteems:/ɪˈstiːmz/| v.|尊重；重视；珍视|To have a high opinion of someone or something; to regard with respect.|例句：I esteem her highly for her kindness and intelligence. 我非常尊重她，因为她善良又聪明。例句：He esteems his family above all else. 他把家人看得比什么都重要。|近义词：respect, admire, value, regard|反义词：despise, scorn, disdain, undervalue|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He esteems his colleagues. = 他尊重他的同事们。
She esteems honesty above all else. = 她把诚实看得比什么都重要。

一般过去时:
They esteemed him for his bravery. = 他们敬重他的勇敢。
I esteemed my grandfather’s wisdom. = 我敬重我爷爷的智慧。

现在完成时:
We have esteemed your contributions to the project. = 我们一直很重视你对项目的贡献。
She has always esteemed her teachers’ guidance. = 她一直很重视老师们的指导。

过去完成时:
They had esteemed his opinion before the decision was made. = 在做出决定之前，他们一直很重视他的意见。
I had esteemed the advice given by my mentor. = 我一直很重视我的导师给我的建议。

将来时:
They will esteem the new employee's hard work. = 他们将会重视新员工的辛勤工作。
We will esteem his dedication to the cause. = 我们将会尊重他对于事业的奉献。

虚拟语气:
If he were here, we would esteem his judgment. = 如果他在这儿，我们会重视他的判断。
I wish I had esteemed the warnings more. = 我希望我当时更重视那些警告。

被动语态:
His contributions were esteemed by the whole team. = 他的贡献得到了整个团队的重视。
Her work is esteemed worldwide. = 她的作品在全世界受到推崇。

疑问句:
Do you esteem his work? = 你尊重他的工作吗？
Does she esteem her friends' opinions? = 她重视她朋友们的意见吗？

否定句:
They did not esteem his advice. = 他们没有重视他的建议。
I do not esteem dishonesty. = 我不尊重不诚实。


PHRASE->
hold in esteem = 高度评价
in high esteem = 受人尊敬
to esteem it an honor = 视为荣幸
self-esteem = 自尊心
to be highly esteemed = 非常受人尊敬
