improportionate: /ɪmˌprəˈpɔːrʃənət/ | adj. |不成比例的，不相称的 |  Disproportionate means too large or too small in comparison to something else. It suggests a lack of balance or harmony in size, amount, or degree.  | The punishment was disproportionate to the crime.  惩罚与罪行不成比例。 |  unbalanced, unequal, excessive, undue | proportionate, balanced, equitable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The punishment is disproportionate to the crime. = 惩罚与罪行不成比例。
His reaction is disproportionate to the situation. = 他的反应与情况不相称。


一般过去时:
The response was disproportionate to the threat. = 反应与威胁不成比例。
Her anger was disproportionate to the offense. = 她的愤怒与其受到的冒犯不相称。


现在完成时:
The impact of the storm has been disproportionate to its size. = 暴风雨的影响与其规模不成比例。
The media coverage has been disproportionate to the actual event. = 媒体报道与其实际事件不相称。


过去完成时:
The damage had been disproportionate to the initial assessment. = 损失与最初的评估不相称。
His fear had been disproportionate to the actual danger. = 他的恐惧与其实际危险不相称。


将来时:
The effect of the policy will be disproportionate to its intended goal. = 该政策的影响将与其预期目标不成比例。
The consequences of his actions will be disproportionate to his intent. = 他行为的后果将与其意图不相称。


虚拟语气:
If the punishment were proportionate, it would be more just. = 如果惩罚成比例，将会更加公正。
If the response had been proportionate, the damage would have been less. = 如果反应成比例，损失就会更小。


被动语态:
The resources were disproportionately allocated to the project. = 资源分配给该项目是不成比例的。
The benefits are disproportionately enjoyed by a small group. = 好处不成比例地被一小部分人享有。


疑问句:
Is the response disproportionate to the problem? = 反应与问题不成比例吗？
Are the resources disproportionately distributed? = 资源分配是否不成比例？


否定句:
The effort was not disproportionate to the result. = 努力与结果成比例。
The reward was not disproportionate to the work. = 酬劳与工作量成比例。



PHRASE->
disproportionate allocation  不成比例的分配
disproportionate representation  不成比例的代表
disproportionate growth  不成比例的增长
disproportionate influence  不成比例的影响
disproportionate share 不成比例的份额


词源:
dis- (否定前缀) + pro- (向前) + portion (部分) + -ate (形容词后缀)  字面意思为“不成比例的部分”。  它源自于拉丁语的 *proportio* (比例)。
