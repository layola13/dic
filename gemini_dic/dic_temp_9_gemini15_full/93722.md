immanent:/ˈɪmənənt/ | adj. | 内在的；固有的；即将发生的 | Existing or operating within; inherent.  Something immanent is present within something else, and is an inherent part of it.  It's often contrasted with "transcendent," which suggests something existing outside or beyond. |例句：The danger was immanent in the situation.  情况中潜藏着危险。|例句：The poem's meaning is immanent in its imagery. 这首诗的意义蕴含在其意象中。|近义词：inherent, intrinsic, innate, resident |反义词：transcendent, extrinsic, external
