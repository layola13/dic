ponton:/ˈpɒntən/| n.|浮桥； pontoon bridge |A pontoon is a flat-bottomed boat, often used as part of a floating bridge or dock.  They are typically made of buoyant materials and can be easily assembled and disassembled.  Pontoons are used in various applications, from temporary bridges to floating platforms for various purposes.|例句：The army built a pontoon bridge across the river.  军队在河上架起了一座浮桥。

The damaged pier was supported by several pontoons. 受损的码头由几艘浮桥支撑。|近义词：float, barge, raft|反义词：bridge (fixed)|词性:名词
