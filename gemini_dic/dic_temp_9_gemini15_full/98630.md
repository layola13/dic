instrumenting:/ˌɪnstrəˈmɛntɪŋ/| v.|仪器化；装备；安装仪器|The act of equipping something with instruments or devices for monitoring, measuring, or controlling.  It often involves adding sensors, actuators, or other hardware to a system to gather data or automate processes. |例句：The engineers are instrumenting the aircraft to monitor its performance in flight. 工程师们正在给飞机安装仪器以监测其飞行性能。

例句：We are instrumenting the experiment to collect precise data. 我们正在为实验安装仪器以收集精确的数据。


近义词：equipping, fitting, installing|反义词：dismantling, removing


词性:动词
