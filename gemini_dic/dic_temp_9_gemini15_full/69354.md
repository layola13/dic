federary:/ˈfɛdəreri/| n.|同谋者，叛徒|A federary is a person who is involved in a conspiracy or treasonous act with others.  The term often implies betrayal of trust or allegiance.|例句：The king discovered his closest advisor was a federary in the plot against him. 国王发现他最亲密的顾问是密谋反对他的同谋。
The general's federary leaked vital military secrets to the enemy. 将军同谋将重要的军事机密泄露给了敌人。|近义词：conspirator, accomplice, traitor, betrayer|反义词：loyalist, patriot
