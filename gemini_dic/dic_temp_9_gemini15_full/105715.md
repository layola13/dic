kinsfolk:/ˈkɪnzfəʊk/| n. |亲属，亲戚 |Relatives; members of one's family.  The term often implies a broader range of relatives than just immediate family. |例句：We invited all our kinsfolk to the wedding. 我们邀请了所有的亲戚来参加婚礼。

例句：Many of her kinsfolk live in the same town. 她的许多亲戚都住在同一个镇上。


近义词：relatives, family, kinfolk, kin, kindred


反义词：None (no direct antonym, but "strangers" or "unrelated people" could be considered opposites in context)


词性：Noun
