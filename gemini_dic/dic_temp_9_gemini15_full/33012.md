chorographer:/kɔːriˈɒɡrəfər/ | n. | 编舞者 | A choreographer is a person who creates and arranges dances.  They design the steps, movements, and patterns of a dance performance. | The choreographer created a stunning ballet about the life of a swan.  这位编舞者创作了一部关于天鹅生活的精彩芭蕾舞剧。| dancer, dance maker |  (无明确反义词) | 名词
