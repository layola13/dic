causally:/ˈkɔːzəli/| adv.|随意地；偶然地；因果地|In a causal manner; indicating cause and effect.  It describes something happening by chance or as a result of a cause.  |例句：They met causally at a coffee shop. = 他们在咖啡店偶然相遇。
They causally linked the two events. = 他们将这两个事件因果地联系起来。|近义词：incidentally, accidentally, randomly, consequently|反义词：intentionally, deliberately, purposefully|副词
