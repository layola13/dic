multipropellants:/ˌmʌltiˈprɒpələnts/ | n. | 多元推进剂 | A propellant consisting of more than one component, often a fuel and an oxidizer, that are mixed before combustion.  Multipropellants are used in various rocket and propulsion systems to achieve specific performance characteristics. | Example:  The spacecraft utilized a hypergolic multipropellant system for its maneuvering thrusters.  这艘宇宙飞船利用双组元自燃推进剂系统来控制其姿态推进器。 | 近义词: bipropellant (if two components), hybrid propellant (if solid and liquid components are combined) | 反义词: monopropellant (single-component propellant) | 名词
