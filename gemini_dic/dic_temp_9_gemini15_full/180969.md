reimburses:/ˌriːɪmˈbɜːrs/| vt.|偿还；报销；赔偿|To repay (a person) a sum of money spent or lost; to compensate for.  |例句：The company reimburses employees for travel expenses. 公司报销员工的差旅费。

例句：They reimbursed me for the cost of the repairs. 他们赔偿了我修理的费用。


近义词：refund, repay, compensate, recompense|反义词：deduct, withhold|动词


USAGE->
