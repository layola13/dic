overhead:/ˈoʊvərˌhed/| n. adj. adv.| 经常性开支；头顶上的；在头顶上；|英文解析：Overhead can refer to the costs of running a business that are not directly related to production, such as rent, utilities, and salaries.  It can also describe something located above, or the act of lifting something above one's head.|例句：The company's overhead costs are too high. 这家公司的经常性开支太高了。

The plane flew overhead.  飞机飞过头顶。|近义词：indirect costs, above, aloft|反义词：direct costs, below, underneath|名词，形容词，副词
