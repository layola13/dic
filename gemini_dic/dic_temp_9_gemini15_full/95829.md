incorporators:/ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪtərz/ | n. | 公司发起人；法人；注册人 |  Those individuals who are responsible for forming a corporation. They are named in the articles of incorporation and are responsible for the initial steps in establishing the company.  | Example: The incorporators of the new tech startup met to finalize the company's bylaws.  新科技创业公司的发起人们会面，敲定了公司的章程。 |近义词:founders, organizers | 反义词: None specifically, but words like liquidators or disbanders represent the opposite action.
