desertifies:/dɪˈzɜːrtɪfaɪz/ | v. |沙漠化；变成沙漠 | To transform into a desert; to become arid and barren, losing its vegetation and fertility.  The process often involves soil degradation, water scarcity, and loss of biodiversity. |  例句：Overgrazing and deforestation are contributing factors to the desertification of the Sahel region. 过度放牧和森林砍伐是萨赫勒地区沙漠化的主要因素。

例句：The once fertile land is now slowly desertifying due to climate change.  曾经肥沃的土地由于气候变化正在慢慢沙漠化。


近义词：aridify, dry up, dehydrate

反义词：reforest, irrigate, green
