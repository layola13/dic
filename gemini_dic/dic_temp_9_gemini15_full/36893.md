colorimeter:/kəˈlɒrɪmɪtər/ | n. |比色计；测色计 | An instrument used to measure the intensity of color.  It works by comparing the color of a sample to a known standard.  Different types of colorimeters exist, each with its own method of measurement. |The scientist used a colorimeter to determine the concentration of the dye in the solution.  科学家用比色计测定溶液中染料的浓度。|spectrophotometer, chromameter |  |名词
