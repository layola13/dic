conspicuous:/kənˈspɪkjuəs/| adj.|显著的，显眼的，易见的 |easily seen or noticed; readily visible.  |例句：Her red dress was conspicuous among the crowd.  她的红裙子在人群中很显眼。|例句：The building's conspicuous height made it a landmark. 这座建筑物显著的高度使它成为一个地标。|近义词：noticeable, prominent, obvious, striking|反义词：inconspicuous, unobtrusive, hidden


consumptions:/kənˈsʌmpʃənz/| n.|消费，消耗，消耗量 |the using up of a resource. |例句：The consumption of energy has increased dramatically. 能源消耗急剧增加。|例句：The high consumption of water is unsustainable. 高度的用水量是不可持续的。|近义词：expenditure, use, outlay, depletion|反义词：production, creation, generation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The consumption of resources is a major concern. = 资源消耗是一个主要问题。
He consumes a lot of coffee every day. = 他每天喝很多咖啡。

一般过去时:
Energy consumption decreased last year. = 去年能源消耗减少了。
They consumed all the food at the party. = 他们在派对上吃光了所有的食物。

现在完成时:
We have consumed all the milk. = 我们已经喝完所有的牛奶了。
The company has significantly reduced its energy consumption. = 公司已经大幅减少了能源消耗。

过去完成时:
They had consumed all the supplies before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，他们已经消耗了所有补给。
She had consumed a large amount of caffeine before the exam. = 考试前她摄入了大量的咖啡因。


将来时:
We will consume less electricity next year. = 我们明年将少用电。
They will have consumed all the resources by the end of the year. = 到年底他们将消耗掉所有资源。

虚拟语气:
If we consumed less, we would have less waste. = 如果我们少消费，就会少产生浪费。
I wish I had not consumed so much sugar. = 我真希望我没有吃那么多糖。

被动语态:
A large amount of energy is consumed by the factory. = 工厂消耗了大量的能源。
Resources are being consumed at an alarming rate. = 资源正在以惊人的速度被消耗。

疑问句:
How much energy is consumed daily? = 每天消耗多少能量？
What is being consumed? = 正在消耗什么？

否定句:
They did not consume all the food. = 他们没有吃光所有的食物。
We do not consume as much as we used to. = 我们不像以前那样消费那么多了。
