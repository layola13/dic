coelia:/ˈsiːliə/| n.| 腹腔疾病；乳糜泻 | Celiac disease is an autoimmune disorder affecting the small intestine.  It's triggered by gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye.  | 例句：My aunt suffers from coeliac disease and has to follow a strict gluten-free diet.  我的阿姨患有乳糜泻，必须遵循严格的无麸质饮食。 |近义词：gluten intolerance, gluten-sensitive enteropathy |反义词：None.
