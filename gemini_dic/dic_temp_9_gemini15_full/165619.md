premiership:/ˈprɛmɪəʃɪp/| n. |首相任期；总理任期；冠军地位|The period during which someone holds the position of prime minister or premier.  It can also refer to the period of dominance of a particular team or individual in a sport.|例句：His premiership was marked by economic reforms. 他的首相任期以经济改革为标志。

例句：The team's premiership lasted for five consecutive years.  这个队的冠军地位持续了五年。


近义词：prime ministership, leadership, reign, dominance


反义词：opposition, defeat


词性:名词
