outhammer:/aʊtˈhæmər/| v.|超过…；胜过；以锤击之法超越|to surpass or exceed someone or something, especially in a forceful or aggressive way; to defeat decisively.|例句：He outhammered his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中胜过了他的对手。|近义词：outshine, outdo, surpass, exceed|反义词：be surpassed by, be outdone by
