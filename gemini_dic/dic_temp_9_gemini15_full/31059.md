chamferer: /ˈtʃæmfərər/ | n. | 倒角器，斜角加工机 | A chamferer is a tool or machine used to create a chamfer, a beveled edge, on a workpiece.  It typically involves removing a small amount of material from the edge to create a sloped surface.  | Example: The carpenter used a chamferer to create a smooth, angled edge on the wooden board.  The automated chamferer significantly sped up the production process. | 近义词： beveler, edge trimmer | 反义词： (no direct antonym, the opposite action would be adding material to create a sharper edge) |  名词
