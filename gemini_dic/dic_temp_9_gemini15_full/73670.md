forges:/fɔːrdʒ/| v. n.|锻造；铸造；制造；伪造；创造；形成|To shape metal by heating it and hammering it.  Also, to create or produce something, often in a forceful or determined way.|例句：The blacksmith forges horseshoes in his shop.  铁匠在他的铺子里锻造马蹄铁。

例句：She forged a new career path for herself after leaving her old job. 她离开旧工作后为自己开辟了一条新的职业道路。
