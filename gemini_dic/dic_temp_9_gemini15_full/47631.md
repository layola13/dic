debilitatingly: /dɪˈbɪlɪteɪtɪŋli/ | adv. |使衰弱地；虚弱地；严重地 |  In a way that weakens or disables.  It describes the manner or extent to which something weakens or incapacitates. |例句：His illness left him debilitatingly weak.  他的病使他虚弱不堪。

例句：The debilitatingly hot weather forced the cancellation of the outdoor concert.  令人虚弱的高温天气迫使户外音乐会取消。


近义词：weakeningly, cripplingly, severely, incapacitatingly

反义词：strengtheningly, invigoratingly


词源:  Derived from the verb "debilitate," which comes from the Latin word "debilitare," meaning "to weaken."  The suffix "-ingly" transforms the adjective "debilitating" into an adverb.
