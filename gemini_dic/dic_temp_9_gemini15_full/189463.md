saintish:/ˈseɪntɪʃ/| adj.|假装圣洁的，伪善的，圣人气的|Affecting or suggesting sanctity or piety, often in a hypocritical or affected manner.  It implies a display of holiness or virtue that is not genuine. |例句：Her saintish demeanor concealed a ruthless ambition.  她的圣洁外表掩盖了她残酷的野心。

例句：He adopted a saintish attitude to impress his new girlfriend. 他装出一副圣人的样子来给他的新女朋友留下好印象。


近义词：pious, sanctimonious, self-righteous, holier-than-thou

反义词：wicked, sinful, ungodly, irreligious


词性：形容词
