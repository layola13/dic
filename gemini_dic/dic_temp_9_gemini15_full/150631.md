papillomatosis:/ˌpæpɪləʊməˈtoʊsɪs/| n. |乳头瘤病|The excessive growth of papillomas (small, nipple-like benign tumors). It can affect various parts of the body, often involving the skin, mucous membranes, or internal organs.  The specific type of papillomatosis depends on the location and cause. For example, oral papillomatosis affects the mouth, while respiratory papillomatosis affects the airways.  |例句：The patient presented with laryngeal papillomatosis, requiring surgical intervention.|近义词：papilloma proliferation, verrucous hyperplasia|反义词：无
