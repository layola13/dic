scamming:/ˈskæmɪŋ/ | v. | 诈骗；欺诈 | The act of obtaining money or property by fraudulent means.  It involves deceiving or tricking someone into giving up something of value. |  例句：He was arrested for scamming elderly people out of their savings. 他因诈骗老年人的积蓄而被捕。

例句：The company was accused of scamming customers through deceptive advertising.  这家公司被指控通过虚假广告欺骗顾客。


近义词：fraud, swindling, conning, cheating, defrauding | 反义词：honesty, integrity
