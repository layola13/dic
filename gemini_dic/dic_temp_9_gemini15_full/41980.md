cost-benefit:/ˈkɒst ˈbenɪfɪt/| n. |成本效益，利弊分析 |The cost-benefit analysis showed that the project was worthwhile. 成本效益分析表明该项目是值得的。|近义词：profitability, return on investment |反义词：loss, deficit


USAGE->

The cost-benefit analysis considers all aspects. = 成本效益分析考虑了所有方面。
We regularly assess the cost-benefit of our decisions. = 我们定期评估我们决定的成本效益。


一般过去时:

They reviewed the cost-benefit of the previous project. = 他们审查了先前项目的成本效益。
