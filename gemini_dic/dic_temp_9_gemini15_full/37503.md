commodate:/kəˈmədeɪt/ | vt. | 提供住宿；供应；使适应；容纳 | To provide lodging or sufficient space for; to supply with what is needed; to adapt or adjust to |


例句：
The hotel can accommodate up to 500 guests.  = 这家酒店最多可容纳 500 位客人。

The new software accommodates the needs of all users. = 新软件满足了所有用户的需求。


近义词：accommodate, lodge, house, supply, adapt, adjust


反义词：reject, refuse, deny, exclude


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hotel accommodates many tourists every year. = 这家酒店每年接待许多游客。
This design accommodates the needs of disabled people. = 此设计满足残疾人的需求。

一般过去时:
The campsite accommodated over a hundred people last summer. = 去年夏天，这个营地容纳了超过一百人。
They accommodated their plans to the sudden change in weather. = 他们根据突变的天气调整了他们的计划。

现在完成时:
The company has accommodated the growth of its business. = 公司已经适应了业务的增长。
We have accommodated several guests in our home this month. = 这个月我们已经在家里接待了几位客人。

过去完成时:
They had accommodated all the refugees before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他们已经安置了所有难民。
The school had accommodated the increased number of students by adding new classrooms. = 学校通过增加新的教室来容纳增加的学生人数。

将来时:
The new stadium will accommodate 80,000 spectators. = 新体育场将容纳8万名观众。
We will accommodate your request as soon as possible. = 我们将尽快满足您的要求。

虚拟语气:
If they accommodated our needs, we would be very happy. = 如果他们满足我们的需求，我们会很高兴。
I wish they could accommodate more people at the conference. = 我希望他们能在会议上容纳更多的人。

被动语态:
The guests were accommodated in comfortable rooms. = 客人们被安排在舒适的房间里。
Our needs were well accommodated by the organizers. = 我们的需求得到了组织者的很好满足。

疑问句:
Can the venue accommodate all the attendees? = 这个场地能容纳所有与会者吗？
Does this car accommodate five passengers comfortably? = 这辆车能舒适地容纳五名乘客吗？

否定句:
The small apartment couldn't accommodate all their furniture. = 这套小公寓容不下他们所有的家具。
The hotel was unable to accommodate any more guests that night. = 那晚酒店无法容纳更多客人。


PHRASE->
accommodate oneself to = 适应
accommodate a request = 满足要求
accommodate a large number of people = 容纳大量的人
accommodate the needs of = 满足…的需求
be well accommodated = 得到妥善安置

词源:
Comes from the Latin "commodare," meaning "to make fit, adjust, or adapt."  This in turn is related to "modus," meaning "measure, manner, or way." The sense of providing lodging developed later.
