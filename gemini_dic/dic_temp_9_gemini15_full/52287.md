dichroite:/ˈdaɪkroʊaɪt/| n. | 二色性矿物，堇青石 | Dichroite is a name for iolite, a mineral that exhibits dichroism, meaning it shows different colors when viewed from different directions.  It's often found in metamorphic rocks. | Example: The jeweler admired the dichroite's striking color change depending on the viewing angle.  珠宝匠欣赏堇青石随着视角变化而产生的引人注目的颜色变化。|近义词：iolite, cordierite |反义词：无
