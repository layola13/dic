licensees:/ˌlaɪsənˈsiːz/| n. |被许可人，领有执照者 |Those who have been granted a license to do something, often involving a business or activity.  They operate under the terms and conditions of the license. |Example: The company's licensees are responsible for ensuring their products meet quality standards.  这家公司的被许可人有责任确保他们的产品符合质量标准。 The theater's licensees are required to pay royalties to the playwright. 剧院的被许可人必须向剧作家支付版税。|近义词：license holders, permit holders |反义词：licensors
