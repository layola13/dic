hard:/hɑːrd/| adj., adv., n.|硬的；困难的；努力地；硬；困难|The word "hard" can function as an adjective, adverb, or noun. As an adjective, it describes something that is firm, solid, or difficult.  As an adverb, it modifies verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs to indicate intensity or difficulty. As a noun, it refers to a difficult situation or task. |例句：The rock is hard. (adj.) 这块石头很硬。The exam was hard. (adj.) 考试很难。He worked hard. (adv.) 他努力工作。It's a hard life. (n.)  生活很艰难。|近义词:(adj.) firm, solid, difficult, tough, arduous; (adv.) diligently, strenuously, intensely|反义词:(adj.) soft, easy, simple; (adv.) lazily, easily|形容词，副词，名词
