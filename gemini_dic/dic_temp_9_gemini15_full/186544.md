riddle:/ˈrɪdl/| n. v.|谜语；难题；迷惑；使困惑|A riddle is a question or statement intentionally phrased so as to require ingenuity in ascertaining its answer or meaning.  It can also refer to something that is mysterious or difficult to understand.|例句：Can you solve this riddle? 你能解开这个谜语吗？

例句：The disappearance of the painting remains a riddle. 这幅画的失踪仍然是一个谜。


近义词：puzzle, enigma, mystery|反义词：answer, solution|词性：名词，动词
