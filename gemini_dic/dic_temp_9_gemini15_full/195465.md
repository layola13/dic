semiography:/ˌsɛmiˈɑːɡrəfi/| n. |符号学；记号学 |The study of signs and symbols and their use or interpretation. Semiography explores the systems of signs, their structure, and their relationship to meaning.  It is closely related to semiotics, but often focuses more on the formal aspects of signs rather than their broader cultural context. |Example:  Her research focuses on the semiography of ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs.  她的研究集中在古埃及象形文字的符号学上。|近义词: semiotics, semiology |反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑与非符号化研究相对) |名词
