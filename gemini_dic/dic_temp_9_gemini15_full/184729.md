result:/rɪˈzʌlt/| n. |结果，成果，成绩 |The result of the experiment was unexpected. 实验的结果出乎意料。|近义词：outcome, consequence, effect |反义词：cause, origin


result:/rɪˈzʌlt/| v. |导致，结果是 |His hard work resulted in success. 他的努力最终取得了成功。|近义词：lead to, cause, produce |反义词：prevent, stop


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The experiment results in a new discovery. = 实验导致了一项新发现。
What results from your hard work? = 你的努力带来了什么结果？


一般过去时:
The meeting resulted in a compromise. = 会议的结果是双方达成了妥协。
The rain resulted in the cancellation of the game. = 雨导致了比赛的取消。


现在完成时:
Their efforts have resulted in significant progress. = 他们的努力已经取得了显著的进展。
Has your hard work resulted in any positive changes? = 你的努力带来了任何积极的变化吗？


过去完成时:
The accident had resulted from careless driving. = 事故是由于粗心驾驶造成的。
Had the investigation resulted in any arrests? = 调查是否导致了任何逮捕？


将来时:
The new policy will result in lower taxes. = 新政策将导致税收降低。
What do you think will result from this decision? = 你认为这个决定会带来什么结果？


虚拟语气:
If you studied harder, you would have better results. = 如果你学习更努力，你就会有更好的成绩。
I wish the experiment had resulted in a different outcome. = 我希望实验的结果不一样。


被动语态:
The game was resulted in a draw. = 比赛结果是平局。
The project was resulted in a failure. = 项目最终失败了。


疑问句:
What resulted from the accident? = 事故导致了什么？
What will result from this change in policy? = 政策的这一变化将会导致什么结果？


否定句:
The treatment did not result in any improvement. = 治疗没有带来任何改善。
His efforts did not result in the desired outcome. = 他的努力并没有达到预期的结果。


PHRASE->
as a result = 结果
result in = 导致
result from = 由…造成
end result = 最终结果
the result is = 结果是
