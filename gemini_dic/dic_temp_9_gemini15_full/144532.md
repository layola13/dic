outvenomed:/ˌaʊtˈvɛnəmd/| adj.| 被毒性更强的毒蛇咬伤；毒性更强 | Surpassed in venom; exceeded in poisonousness.  The snake was outvenomed by the larger, more venomous species.  |  例句：The smaller snake was outvenomed by the cobra. (那条较小的蛇被眼镜蛇的毒性超过了。) The potency of the new toxin outvenomed all previous attempts. (新的毒素的效力超过了以往所有的尝试。) | 近义词：overshadowed, surpassed, exceeded | 反义词：undermined, weakened
