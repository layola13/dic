quietly:/ˈkwaɪətli/ | adv. |安静地，悄悄地 |The adverb "quietly" means in a quiet manner; without making a noise or causing a disturbance. It describes the way an action is performed. |例句：She quietly closed the door. 她轻轻地关上门了。

例句：He spoke quietly so as not to wake the baby. 他小声说话，以免吵醒婴儿。


近义词：silently, softly, subtly, hush-hush

反义词：loudly, noisily, boisterously


词源:

The word "quietly" is derived from the adjective "quiet,"  which comes from the Old French "quiet" and ultimately from the Latin "quietus," meaning "restful," "tranquil," or "still." The "-ly" suffix is an adverbial suffix that transforms adjectives into adverbs.


用法说明:

quietly用作副词，修饰动词，表示动作的方式。它通常描述一种安静、不吵闹、不引人注目的方式。


例句：

He quietly reads his book every evening. 他每天晚上都静静地看书。
She quietly observes the birds in the garden. 她静静地观察花园里的鸟儿。

将来时:
