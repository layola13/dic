mid-point:/ˈmɪdˌpɔɪnt/| n. |中点，中间点 |The point that is exactly halfway between two other points.  It is the average of the two points' coordinates. |例句：The midpoint of the line segment connecting (1,2) and (3,4) is (2,3).  这条连接(1,2)和(3,4)的线段的中点是(2,3)。

例句：We met at the midpoint of our respective homes. 我们在我们各自家之间的中点见面。


近义词：middle, center, average point |反义词：endpoint, extremity
