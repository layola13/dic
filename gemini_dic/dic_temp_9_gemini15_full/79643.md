glossingly:/ˈɡlɒsɪŋli/ | adv. | 光滑地；有光泽地；浮夸地 |  describing something that appears smooth, shiny, or superficially attractive;  also can describe something that is superficially impressive but lacks substance. | 例句：The salesperson glossingly described the car's features.  销售员浮夸地描述了汽车的特性。


The varnish dried glossingly, leaving a beautiful sheen.  清漆干了以后非常光滑，留下美丽的亮泽。| 近义词：smoothly, brilliantly, superficially, superficially attractively | 反义词：dully, roughly, plainly
