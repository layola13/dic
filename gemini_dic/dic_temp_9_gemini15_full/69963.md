festivals:/ˈfɛstɪvəlz/ | n. |节日，庆典 | Festivals are celebrations of a particular event, activity, or culture.  They often involve music, food, and entertainment. |例句：Many countries celebrate Christmas festivals. 许多国家都庆祝圣诞节。|例句：The town held a summer festivals to attract tourists. 这个小镇举办夏季节日以吸引游客。 |近义词：celebrations, festivities, holidays |反义词：routine, everyday life
