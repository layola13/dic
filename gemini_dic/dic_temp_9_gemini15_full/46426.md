daggering: /ˈdæɡərɪŋ/ | v. | 刺；刺伤；用匕首攻击 |  To stab or wound with a dagger.  The term can also be used figuratively to describe a sharp, sudden, or intense action or feeling. | 例句：He daggered the thief in self-defense. 他为了自卫而刺伤了小偷。 The criticism daggered his confidence. 批评刺伤了他的自信心。| 近义词：stabbing, wounding, puncturing | 反义词：healing, soothing
