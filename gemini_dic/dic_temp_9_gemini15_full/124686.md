movie-goer:/ˈmuːviˌɡoʊər/|/ˈmuːviˌɡəʊə(r)/| n. |电影观众|A person who frequently attends movies.  A movie-goer is someone who regularly goes to the cinema to watch films. |Example: The new movie theater is attracting a large number of movie-goers. 这家新的电影院吸引了大量的电影观众。  The movie-goer was thrilled by the special effects.  这位电影观众对特效感到兴奋。|近义词: film fan, cinema-goer, movie buff|反义词:  N/A
