foolhardiness:/ˈfulˈhɑːrdinɪs/ | n. |鲁莽；蛮勇；轻率 | Reckless disregard for danger or consequences; a rash and impetuous act.  It implies a lack of caution and a willingness to take unnecessary risks. |His foolhardiness in climbing the treacherous mountain almost cost him his life. 他鲁莽地攀登险峻的山峰，差点丢了性命。|近义词：recklessness, rashness, temerity, audacity |反义词：caution, prudence, circumspection
