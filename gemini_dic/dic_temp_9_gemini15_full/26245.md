butterwort:/ˈbʌtərˌwɔːrt/ | n. |捕蝇草，茅膏菜 |A carnivorous plant of the genus *Pinguicula*, having succulent leaves that trap insects by means of sticky glands.  Butterworts are typically found in moist, boggy habitats. |例句：The butterwort's sticky leaves lured the unsuspecting fly to its doom.  捕蝇草粘乎乎的叶子引诱了毫无戒心的苍蝇走向死亡。

例句：Several species of butterwort thrive in the alpine meadows.  几种茅膏菜在高山草甸中茁壮成长。

近义词：carnivorous plant, sundew (部分种类) |反义词：无
