forevouched:/fɔːrˈvaʊtʃt/| v.|预先证明；预先保证；担保|To vouch for something or someone in advance; to guarantee beforehand.  The word is largely archaic and infrequently used in modern English.|例句：The merchant forevouched the quality of his goods, promising a full refund if they proved unsatisfactory.  商人预先保证了他的货物质量，承诺如果货物达不到要求将全额退款。|近义词：guarantee, warrant, assure, vouch for |反义词：disavow, deny, repudiate
