empathy:/ˈɛmpəθi/ | n. | 同理心，共情 | The ability to understand and share the feelings of another.  Empathy involves stepping into someone else's shoes and experiencing the world from their perspective.  It's more than just sympathy, which is feeling sorry for someone; empathy is feeling *with* someone. |  She showed great empathy for the victims of the earthquake. 她对地震的受害者表现出极大的同情心。  He lacked the empathy to understand her grief. 他缺乏理解她悲伤的同理心。 | sympathy, compassion, understanding | apathy, indifference, unconcern | 名词
