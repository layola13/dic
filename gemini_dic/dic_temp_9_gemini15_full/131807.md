nonconsent: /ˌnɒn kənˈsɛnt/ | n. | 未经同意，不同意 | The lack of consent;  the state of not having given permission or agreement.  It often refers to actions performed without the explicit agreement of the involved party.  | 例句：The police investigated the nonconsent of the victim in the case.  警方调查了受害者在此案中的未经同意的情况。
