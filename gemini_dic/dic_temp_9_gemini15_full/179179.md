redirections:/ˌriːdaɪˈrɛkʃənz/| n.|重定向，转向，改道|The process of changing the course or direction of something, especially data or traffic on a network.  It can also refer to the instances of such changes.|例句：The website automatically performs redirections to ensure users reach the correct page.  网站自动执行重定向以确保用户到达正确的页面。

例句：The traffic redirections caused significant delays during rush hour. 交通改道在高峰时段造成了严重的延误。|近义词：rerouting, diversions, redirects|反义词：directing, straight path
