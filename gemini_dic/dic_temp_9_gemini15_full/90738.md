humint:/ˈhjuːmɪnt/| n. |人力情报|Human intelligence; information gathered from human sources.  This term is commonly used in intelligence agencies and refers to information collected through direct interaction with people, such as interviews, interrogations, and surveillance. |例句：The agency relies heavily on humint to gather information about terrorist activities. 该机构严重依赖人力情报来收集有关恐怖主义活动的信息。|近义词：human intelligence, intelligence from human sources|反义词：SIGINT (signals intelligence), IMINT (imagery intelligence)
