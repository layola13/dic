词源：The term "ladyfingers" emerged in the late 18th or early 19th century,  reflecting the biscuit's elongated, finger-like shape. The association with "lady" likely suggests a delicacy or refinement.  The exact origin is difficult to pinpoint, but the name is descriptive and widely understood.
