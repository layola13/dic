scintillant: /ˈsɪntɪlənt/ | adj. |闪光的；闪烁的；活泼的 | Sparkling or shining brightly; lively and exciting.  |The scintillant diamonds caught the light. 闪闪发光的钻石吸引了光线。 The party was scintillant with excitement.派对因兴奋而充满活力。|近义词：sparkling, glittering, brilliant, radiant |反义词：dull, dim, lackluster

USAGE->

The city lights scintillate in the night. = 城市的灯光在夜空中闪烁。
