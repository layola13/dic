metastases:/məˈtæstəsiz/| n.|转移（癌细胞）|The spread of a disease from one part of the body to another.|例句：The cancer had metastasized to her lungs.  癌症已经转移到她的肺部。|近义词：spread, dissemination|反义词：localized


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cancer metastasizes quickly. = 癌症迅速转移。
His illness metastasizes to other organs. = 他的疾病转移到其他器官。


一般过去时:
The tumor metastasized to the brain. = 肿瘤转移到了大脑。
The disease rapidly metastasized throughout his body. = 疾病迅速转移到他的全身。


现在完成时:
The cancer has metastasized to the bones. = 癌症已经转移到骨骼。
The doctors discovered that the tumor has metastasized. = 医生发现肿瘤已经转移。


过去完成时:
The patient had already experienced metastases before diagnosis. = 患者在诊断前就已经经历了转移。
It was found that the cancer had metastasized extensively before treatment began. = 发现癌症在治疗开始前已经广泛转移。


将来时:
The doctor fears the cancer will metastasize soon. = 医生担心癌症很快就会转移。
Without treatment, the disease will likely metastasize. = 不治疗，疾病很可能会转移。


虚拟语气:
If the cancer hadn't metastasized, the prognosis would be much better. = 如果癌症没有转移，预后会好得多。
I wish the tumor hadn't metastasized so aggressively. = 我希望肿瘤没有这么积极地转移。


被动语态:
The lymph nodes were found to be affected by metastasized cells. = 发现淋巴结受到转移细胞的影响。
It was determined that the cancer had metastasized to distant sites. = 确定癌症已经转移到远端部位。


疑问句:
Has the cancer metastasized yet? = 癌症已经转移了吗？
Where has the tumor metastasized to? = 肿瘤转移到哪里了？


否定句:
The cancer has not yet metastasized. = 癌症尚未转移。
Fortunately, the tumor did not metastasize. = 幸好肿瘤没有转移。



PHRASE->
bone metastases = 骨转移
brain metastases = 脑转移
lung metastases = 肺转移
distant metastases = 远处转移
metastatic metastases = 转移性转移


词源:
From Late Latin *metastasis, from Greek μετάστασις (metástasis), from μεταστῆναι (metastênai) "to displace, remove, transfer," from μετά (metá) "after, beyond" + ἵστημι (hístēmi) "I stand, place."  The medical term refers to the secondary growth of cancerous tissue.
