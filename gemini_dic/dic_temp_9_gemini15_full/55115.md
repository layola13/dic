disrupted:/dɪsˈrʌptɪd/| adj.|被打扰的；中断的；混乱的|  Past participle of disrupt.  Describes something that has been interfered with, interrupted, or thrown into disorder. It emphasizes the negative impact of the interruption.|例句：The protest disrupted traffic for hours. 抗议活动使交通中断数小时。

例句：The unexpected storm disrupted the airline's schedule.  突如其来的暴风雨扰乱了航空公司的航班安排。


近义词：interrupted, disturbed, interfered with, disorganized, hampered, upset

反义词：ordered, organized, uninterrupted, smooth, seamless


词性：形容词
