reunified:/ˌriːˈjuːnɪfaɪd/| adj.|重新统一的；重新结合的|  Having been united again after a period of separation or division.  The term implies a previous state of separation or division that has been overcome. |例句：Germany was reunified in 1990.  德国在1990年重新统一。

The reunification of Korea remains a distant dream.  朝鲜半岛的重新统一仍然是一个遥远的梦想。|近义词：united, rejoined, consolidated, integrated|反义词：divided, separated, fragmented, disunited|词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The country is reunified under a strong leader. = 这个国家在一个强有力的领导下重新统一。
This company is reunified under new management. = 这家公司在新管理层领导下重新统一。


一般过去时:
The two countries were reunified after a long war. = 两国经过一场漫长的战争后重新统一。
The family was reunified after many years of separation. = 分离多年后，这个家庭重新团聚。


现在完成时:
The divided nation has been reunified after decades of conflict. = 经过数十年的冲突，分裂的国家已经重新统一。
They have been reunified with their long-lost relatives. = 他们与失散多年的亲人团聚了。


过去完成时:
The kingdom had been reunified before the new emperor's coronation. = 在新皇帝加冕之前，王国已经重新统一。
Before the celebration, the family had been reunified. = 在庆祝活动之前，这个家庭已经团聚了。


将来时:
The two regions will be reunified in the near future. = 这两个地区将在不久的将来重新统一。
Hopefully, the family will be reunified next year. = 希望明年这个家庭能够团聚。


虚拟语气:
If the two sides had cooperated, the country would have been reunified sooner. = 如果双方合作，国家本来可以更快地重新统一。
If only they had been reunified earlier. = 但愿他们早点团聚。


被动语态:
The country was reunified by force. = 该国被迫重新统一。
The family was eventually reunified by social workers. = 这个家庭最终在社会工作者的帮助下团聚了。


疑问句:
Was the country ever reunified after the civil war? = 内战后国家是否曾经重新统一？
Has the family been reunified yet? = 这个家庭已经团聚了吗？


否定句:
The country was not reunified peacefully. = 这个国家并没有和平地重新统一。
The separated twins were not yet reunified. = 分离的双胞胎还没有团聚。
