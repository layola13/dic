laconical: /ləˈkɒnɪkl/ | adj. | 简洁的；言简意赅的；沉默寡言的 | Using very few words; brief and to the point.  | 例句：His reply to my question was laconic. 他对我的问题的回答很简短。

例句：He gave a laconic nod. 他简短地点点头。


近义词：terse, succinct, brief, concise, taciturn


反义词：verbose, loquacious, garrulous, effusive


词源:  The word "laconic" originates from Laconia, a region in ancient Greece. The Laconians, inhabitants of Laconia, were known for their brevity and succinctness of speech.  The word entered English in the 16th century.
