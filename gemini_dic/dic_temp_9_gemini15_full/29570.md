catchup: /ˈkætʃʌp/ | n. v. |番茄酱；追赶；弥补 |

英文解析:As a noun, it refers to a thick sauce made from tomatoes, vinegar, and spices. As a verb, it means to bring oneself up to date or to reach the same level as someone else.

例句 (Noun):
I put ketchup on my fries. = 我在薯条上放了番茄酱。

I love the taste of homemade ketchup. = 我喜欢自制番茄酱的味道。


例句 (Verb):
I need to catch up on my work. = 我需要赶上我的工作。

Let's catch up over coffee sometime. = 咱们找个时间喝咖啡聊聊吧。


近义词 (Noun):  tomato sauce, catsup
近义词 (Verb):  overtake, come up to, make up for


反义词 (Verb): fall behind, lag


词性: noun, verb
