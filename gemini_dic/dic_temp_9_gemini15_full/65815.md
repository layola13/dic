evil-doing: /ˈiːvəlˈduːɪŋ/ | n. |作恶；坏事；恶行 | The act of doing evil; wickedness; wrongdoing.  It refers to actions that are morally wrong, harmful, or wicked.  The term emphasizes the deliberate nature of the harmful act. |例句：His evil-doing finally caught up with him.  他的恶行最终受到了惩罚。

例句：The evidence of their evil-doing was overwhelming. 他们作恶的证据确凿。


近义词：wrongdoing, wickedness, villainy, mischief, malfeasance

反义词：benevolence, good deed, virtuous act

词性：名词
