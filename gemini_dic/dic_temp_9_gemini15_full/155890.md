photobomb: /ˈfoʊtoʊbɑːm/ | v. | 抢镜头，破坏照片 | To unexpectedly appear in someone else's photograph, often in a humorous or disruptive way.  | Example:  A stranger photobombed our wedding photo.  一个陌生人抢了我们婚礼照片的镜头。 | 近义词：crash, intrude, interrupt | 反义词：pose, cooperate
