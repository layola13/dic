quaffers:/ˈkwɑːfərz/| n. |痛饮者；狂饮者 |A person who drinks deeply and frequently.  |例句：The quaffers were enjoying the local ale at the pub.  痛饮者们正在酒吧里享用当地的啤酒。  |近义词：drunkard, drinker, tippler, toper |反义词：teetotaler, abstainer |名词

He quaffs beer every evening. = 他每晚都痛饮啤酒。
They quaff wine at every party. = 他们在每个派对上都痛饮葡萄酒。
