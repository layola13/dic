redissolve:/ˌriːdɪˈzɒlv/| vt.|再溶解，重新溶解|To dissolve again; to go into solution again.  The process of dissolving something that has already been dissolved, often after it has solidified or precipitated.|例句：The chemist redissolved the precipitate in order to perform further analysis. 化学家再次溶解沉淀物以进行进一步分析。

The sugar crystals redissolved quickly in the hot water. 糖晶体很快溶解在热水中。|近义词：redissolve, resolubilize|反义词：precipitate, solidify|动词
