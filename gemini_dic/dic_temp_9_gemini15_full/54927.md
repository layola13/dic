disposition:/ˌdɪspəˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 性情；倾向；安排；处理 | The arrangement or placement of something.  A person's inherent qualities of mind and character.  A tendency or inclination. |例句：He has a cheerful disposition. 他性格开朗。

He showed a disposition to help others. 他表现出乐于助人的倾向。


近义词：temperament, nature, character, tendency, inclination | 反义词：N/A (反义词取决于具体的语境) | 词性：名词


USAGE->

His disposition is quite cheerful. = 他的性格相当开朗。
Her disposition towards hard work is commendable. = 她勤奋工作的倾向值得称赞。


一般过去时:
