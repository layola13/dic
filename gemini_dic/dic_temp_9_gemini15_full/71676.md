flatworm:/ˈflætˌwɜːrm/| n. |扁虫|A flatworm is a type of invertebrate animal with a long, flat body. They are typically found in aquatic environments, and some species are parasitic. They lack a coelom (body cavity) and have a simple nervous system.  Many are hermaphrodites, meaning they possess both male and female reproductive organs.|Example:  The researchers discovered a new species of flatworm in the Amazon rainforest.  研究人员在亚马逊雨林中发现了一种新的扁虫物种。|近义词: platyhelminthes|反义词: (no direct antonym,  consider organisms with different body plans)|名词
