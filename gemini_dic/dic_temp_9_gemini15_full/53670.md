disciplelike: /dɪˈsaɪpəlˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像弟子的；虔诚的；顺从的 | Resembling a disciple; showing the qualities of a disciple, such as obedience, devotion, and eagerness to learn.  It suggests a humble and devoted following of a leader or a belief system. |


例句：

He showed a disciplelike devotion to his mentor, absorbing every word and following every instruction. 他对他的导师表现出像弟子一样的奉献精神，吸收每一个字并遵循每一个指示。


His disciplelike obedience to the rules was remarkable, given his independent nature.  考虑到他独立的性格，他对规则的服从令人钦佩。


近义词：obedient, submissive, devoted, faithful, pious


反义词：rebellious, disobedient, defiant, independent, skeptical


词源:

The word is formed by combining "disciple" (meaning a follower of a teacher or leader) with the suffix "-like," which indicates resemblance or similarity.  "Disciple" itself originates from the Old French word "disciple," which ultimately comes from the Latin word "discipulus," meaning "learner" or "pupil."


USAGE->
(Since "disciplelike" is an adjective, tense does not apply. The following examples show its use in different sentence structures.)

肯定句:
Her disciplelike attitude impressed the master. 她像弟子一样的态度给师傅留下了深刻的印象。
The student displayed a disciplelike eagerness to learn.  这位学生表现出像弟子一样渴望学习的态度。


否定句:
His behavior wasn't disciplelike; he openly questioned authority.  他的行为不像弟子；他公开质疑权威。
The group's actions were not disciplelike; they demonstrated a lack of respect for tradition. 这个团体的行为不像弟子；他们表现出对传统的缺乏尊重。


疑问句:
Was his response disciplelike or rebellious? 他的回应是像弟子的还是叛逆的？
Is this disciplelike behavior acceptable in this context? 在这种情况下，这种像弟子的行为是可以接受的吗？
