fenders:/ˈfɛndərz/| n.|挡泥板；护舷；防御物|A fender is a device used to protect something, typically a vehicle or a boat, from impacts or damage.  In cars, they prevent mud and water from splashing onto the body. In boats, they prevent damage during docking.  It can also refer to a protective device in other contexts.|例句：The car's fenders were dented in the accident.  这辆车的挡泥板在事故中凹陷了。

The boat's fenders were essential to prevent damage during docking. 这艘船的护舷对于防止停靠时受损至关重要。


近义词：guard, protector, bumper, shield|反义词：none.
