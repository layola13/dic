residencies:/ˈrezɪdənsiz/| n.|居住权；住所；居住期间；实习期|The right to live in a particular place; a place of residence; a period of living somewhere; a period of practical training.|例句：The artist had a residency at the museum for six months.  艺术家在博物馆进行为期六个月的驻留创作。|例句：She applied for a residency program in a hospital. 她申请了医院的住院医生实习项目。|近义词：dwellings, residences, stays, appointments|反义词：evictions, departures


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He holds several residencies in different cities. 他在不同的城市拥有多个住所。
The artist enjoys her residency at the prestigious art center. 艺术家享受她在著名艺术中心的驻留创作。


一般过去时:
They had a short residency in Paris last year. 他们去年在巴黎短期居住。
The doctor completed her residency in cardiology. 这名医生完成了她的心脏病学住院医师培训。


现在完成时:
She has held numerous residencies throughout her career.  她在职业生涯中拥有过无数的居住权/驻留机会。
He has just finished his residency and is now a fully qualified surgeon. 他刚刚完成住院医师培训，现在是一位合格的外科医生。


过去完成时:
Before moving to London, she had completed a residency at the Royal Academy. 在搬到伦敦之前，她曾在皇家艺术学院完成了驻留创作。
By the time he graduated, he had already completed his residency.  在他毕业时，他已经完成了住院医师培训。


将来时:
Next year, he will have a residency at a university in Japan. 明年，他将在日本的一所大学进行驻留。
She will complete her residency next month and begin her practice.  她下个月将完成住院医师培训并开始执业。


虚拟语气:
If I had the opportunity, I would apply for a writer's residency. 如果我有机会，我会申请一个作家驻留项目。
I wish I had known about the residency program earlier. 我真希望我早点知道这个驻留项目。


被动语态:
The residency program was canceled due to unforeseen circumstances.  由于不可预见的情况，驻留项目被取消了。
The artist's residency is being funded by a private grant. 艺术家的驻留项目由私人捐款资助。


疑问句:
How long is your residency going to last? 你的居住/驻留时间有多长？

否定句:
