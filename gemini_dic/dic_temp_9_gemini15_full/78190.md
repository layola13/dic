geosynchronous:/ˌdʒioʊˈsɪŋkrənəs/| adj.| 地球同步的 | Relating to or denoting a satellite that orbits the earth with a period equal to the earth's period of rotation on its axis, so that it always remains above the same point on the earth's surface.  |Example: Geostationary satellites are a type of geosynchronous satellite. 地球同步卫星是一种地球同步卫星。


The geostationary satellites provide continuous coverage of a specific region. 地球同步卫星为特定区域提供持续覆盖。


近义词:geostationary


反义词:geosynchronous orbit (as opposed to other types of orbits)


词性:形容词
