owe:/oʊ/| vt. |欠（债、恩情等）；感激；归功于 |to be obliged to pay or repay (something to someone); to be indebted to someone|例句：I owe you ten dollars. 我欠你十美元。
I owe my success to my hard work. 我的成功归功于我的努力工作。|近义词：debit, be indebted to|反义词：pay back, repay


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I owe you a favor. = 我欠你一个人情。
They owe taxes to the government. = 他们欠政府税款。


一般过去时:
She owed him a large sum of money. = 她欠他一大笔钱。
We owed our parents a great deal. = 我们欠父母很多。


现在完成时:
I have owed him money for months. = 我欠他钱好几个月了。
He has owed his success to his talent and hard work. = 他把成功归功于他的天赋和努力工作。


过去完成时:
I had owed him money since college. = 从大学起我就欠他钱了。
They had owed their happiness to each other's support. = 他们的幸福是彼此支持的结果。


将来时:
I will owe him a big thank you. = 我将要好好感谢他。
They will owe a great deal to future generations. = 他们将对子孙后代负有很大的责任。


虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would owe everyone a drink. = 如果我中了彩票，我会请大家喝酒。
I wish I didn't owe so much money. = 我真希望我不欠这么多钱。


被动语态:
The money is owed to the bank. = 这笔钱欠银行的。
A great deal is owed to the pioneers. = 我们应该非常感谢那些先驱者。


疑问句:
Do you owe anyone money? = 你欠别人钱吗？
How much do you owe them? = 你欠他们多少钱？


否定句:
I don't owe you anything. = 我不欠你任何东西。
They don't owe anyone an explanation. = 他们不需要向任何人解释。


PHRASE->
owe it to someone = 应归功于某人
owe someone an apology = 欠某人一个道歉
owe a debt of gratitude = 欠下感激之情
owe one's life to someone = 欠某人一条命
owe something to something = 把…归因于…
