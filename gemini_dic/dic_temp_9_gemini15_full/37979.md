complicate:/ˈkɒmplɪkeɪt/ /ˈkɑːmplɪkeɪt/| vt. adj.|使复杂化；复杂的|To make something more difficult or complex; to be difficult to understand or deal with.|The new regulations have complicated matters considerably. 新的规章制度使事情变得相当复杂。|simplify, entangle|simplify, clarify|动词，形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He complicates everything he touches. 他碰什么都弄复杂了。
The instructions complicate the process unnecessarily.  这些说明不必要地使流程复杂化。


一般过去时:
The unexpected rain complicated our travel plans.  突如其来的雨水打乱了我们的旅行计划。
The witness's testimony complicated the investigation.  证人的证词使调查更加复杂。


现在完成时:
They have complicated the issue with their conflicting statements. 他们相互矛盾的陈述使问题更加复杂。
Recent events have complicated the political landscape.  最近的事件使政治局势更加复杂。


过去完成时:
He had complicated the situation by lying to the police. 他对警方撒谎，使情况更加复杂。
The team had complicated the project by introducing unnecessary features.  团队通过引入不必要的特性使项目复杂化。


将来时:
The new software will complicate the workflow.  新的软件将使工作流程复杂化。
Further delays will complicate the already challenging project.  进一步的延误将使这个本来就具有挑战性的项目更加复杂。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't interfered, it wouldn't have complicated matters. 如果他不干涉，就不会使事情复杂化。
I wish they hadn't complicated the simple solution. 我真希望他们没有把简单的解决方案搞复杂。


被动语态:
The situation was complicated by unforeseen circumstances.  情况因不可预见的情况而变得复杂。
The issue is being complicated by a lack of communication.  缺乏沟通正在使问题复杂化。


疑问句:
Does this further complicate the problem? 这会使问题更加复杂吗？
Will this new rule complicate things for small businesses? 这条新规会给小企业带来麻烦吗？


否定句:
Don't complicate things more than they already are. 不要让事情比现在更复杂。
This solution doesn't complicate the existing system. 这个解决方案不会使现有系统复杂化。
