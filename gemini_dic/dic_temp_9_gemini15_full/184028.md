residence:/ˈrezɪdəns/| n.|住所，居住地，居住权|a place where someone lives; the fact of living somewhere|例句：The house is her main residence. 这房子是她的主要住所。

例句：He took up residence in a small apartment. 他住进了一间小公寓。


近义词：dwelling, abode, home, domicile|反义词：absence, departure


词源：Middle English: from Old French residence, from Latin residentia, from residere ‘sit remaining’, from re- + sedere ‘sit’.
