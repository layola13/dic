cottier:/ˈkɒtiər/| n.|佃农；小农|A cottier is a tenant farmer who occupies a small cottage or plot of land, often paying rent in the form of labor or a share of the crop.  The term often implies a low social and economic status.|Example: Many cottiers in the 19th century Ireland were forced to subsist on potatoes.  许多19世纪爱尔兰的佃农被迫靠土豆为生。|近义词：tenant farmer, smallholder|反义词：landowner, landlord|名词
