pluggable:/ˈplʌɡəbəl/ | adj. | 可插入的；可替换的；可插入模块的 |  Something that is pluggable can be easily connected or disconnected, often implying modularity and ease of replacement. It suggests a design that allows for flexibility and customization.  | Example: This software uses pluggable modules, allowing users to customize its functionality.  这个软件使用可插入模块，允许用户自定义其功能。

The system uses a pluggable authentication module.  这个系统使用可插入的身份验证模块。|近义词：interchangeable, replaceable, modular |反义词：fixed, integrated, permanent

词源:
The word "pluggable" derives from the verb "plug," meaning to insert something into a socket or opening.  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or possibility.  Therefore, "pluggable" describes something that can be plugged in.
