icebird:/ˈaɪsbɜːrd/| n. |冰鸟|A bird, typically a seabird, that inhabits icy regions. The term is not a formal scientific classification but a descriptive term for various species found in polar or high-altitude icy environments.|Example:  The icebird soared gracefully over the frozen landscape. 冰鸟优雅地飞过冰冻的景观。|近义词：polar bird, arctic bird, antarctic bird|反义词：tropical bird
