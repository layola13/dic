feudalists:/ˈfjuːdəlɪsts/| n.|封建主义者|People who support or practice feudalism, a social system in which land is held by nobles in exchange for military service or loyalty.|The feudalists controlled vast tracts of land and exerted considerable power over the peasantry.|近义词：noblemen, lords, aristocrats|反义词：commoners, peasants
