depopulates:/diːˈpɒpjʊleɪts/| vt.|使人口减少；使…荒芜|To reduce the population of (a place or area).  The process of a population decreasing in a particular area.|The plague depopulated the village. 瘟疫使村庄人口锐减。

The industrial revolution depopulated many rural areas. 工业革命使许多农村地区人口减少。|近义词：thin out, deplete|反义词：populate, increase
