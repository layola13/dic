feel up to:/fiːl ˈʌp tuː/| phrasal verb|感觉能够胜任；感觉有能力做…|to feel well enough or have enough energy to do something |I don't feel up to going out tonight.  今晚我感觉没精力出去。|近义词：be capable of, be equal to |反义词：be unable to, be incapable of


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I feel up to running a marathon. = 我感觉能够跑马拉松。
She feels up to tackling that challenging project. = 她感觉能够胜任那个具有挑战性的项目。

一般过去时:
He didn't feel up to the task. = 他感觉无法胜任这项任务。
I didn't feel up to cooking dinner after a long day. = 长了一天后，我感觉没精力做饭。

现在完成时:
I haven't felt up to much lately. = 最近我感觉什么事都做不了。
She has finally felt up to resuming her studies. = 她终于感觉能够恢复学业了。

过去完成时:
He had not felt up to attending the meeting. = 他之前感觉无法参加会议。
Before the surgery, she hadn't felt up to even simple tasks. = 手术前，她连简单的任务都感觉做不了。

将来时:
I think I will feel up to hiking tomorrow. = 我想我明天会有精力去远足。
Will you feel up to driving after your long flight? = 长途飞行后你感觉能开车吗？

虚拟语气:
If I felt up to it, I would join you. = 如果我感觉能够胜任，我会加入你。
I wish I felt up to climbing that mountain. = 我希望我有精力爬那座山。

被动语态:  (This phrasal verb is not typically used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Do you feel up to the challenge? = 你感觉能够应对这个挑战吗？
Does she feel up to presenting her research? = 她感觉能够展示她的研究成果吗？

否定句:
I don't feel up to working overtime. = 我感觉无法加班。
They didn't feel up to the physical demands of the job. = 他们感觉无法胜任这项工作的体力要求。


PHRASE->
feel up to the challenge = 胜任挑战
feel up to the job = 胜任工作
feel up to it = 感觉能够胜任
feel up to something = 感觉能够做某事
feel up to the task = 胜任任务
