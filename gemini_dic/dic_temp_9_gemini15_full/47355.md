deacon:/ˈdiːkən/ | n. |执事；助祭 | A deacon is a person, typically a member of the clergy, who assists a priest or minister, often in religious services.  Deacons may also have administrative or pastoral responsibilities within a church or religious community. |  He was ordained a deacon last year. 他去年被任命为执事。 The deacon led the prayers. 执事带领祈祷。 | minister, priest (in some contexts) | layman, parishioner | 名词
