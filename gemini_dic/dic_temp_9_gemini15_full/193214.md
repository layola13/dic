seductionist: /sɪˈdʌkʃənɪst/ | n. | 诱惑者；煽动者 | A person who practices seduction, often in a manipulative or exploitative way.  Someone who skillfully uses charm and persuasion to influence or control others, often for personal gain.  | Example: He was a master seductionist, charming his way into the hearts and trust of his victims. 他是一位熟练的诱惑者，用魅力赢得受害者的信任。 |近义词：tempter, deceiver, manipulator, charmer |反义词：saint, innocent, virtuous
