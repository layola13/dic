fallen:/ˈfɔːlən/| adj.| 掉落的；倒下的；堕落的；已逝的 |  Past participle of "fall," describing something that has fallen or something resulting from a fall.  It can also describe a person who has fallen from grace or into a state of decline. | 例句：The fallen leaves covered the ground.  落叶覆盖了地面。

The fallen soldier was honored for his bravery.  阵亡的士兵因其勇敢而受到表彰。


近义词：dropped, downed, prostrate, deceased (for people) | 反义词：standing, upright, risen, elevated
