kapoks:/ˈkæpɒks/| n.|木棉纤维；木棉树纤维|The fiber from the kapok tree, known for its buoyancy and softness.  It's used in stuffing for pillows, life jackets, and insulation.|例句：Kapok is a lightweight, buoyant fiber often used in life jackets. = 木棉纤维是一种轻盈、浮力大的纤维，常用于救生衣。|例句：The pillows were filled with soft, fluffy kapok. = 枕头里塞满了柔软蓬松的木棉纤维。|近义词：cotton, silk (in some applications)|反义词：无|名词
