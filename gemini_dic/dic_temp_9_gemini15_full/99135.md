interception:/ˌɪntərˈsɛpʃən/| n.|拦截；截取；阻截|The act of intercepting or the state of being intercepted.  It can refer to physically stopping something in transit, or figuratively interrupting a communication or plan. |例句：The interception of the enemy's message was crucial to the victory.  敌人的信息被拦截对胜利至关重要。

例句：The police made an interception of the smuggled drugs. 警方拦截了走私的毒品。


近义词：capture, seizure, obstruction, interruption|反义词：delivery, transmission, completion


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team practices interception drills every day. = 球队每天练习拦截训练。
He intercepts emails from his competitor. = 他拦截了他竞争对手的邮件。


一般过去时:
The goalkeeper made a spectacular interception. = 守门员做了一个精彩的拦截。
She intercepted a secret message. = 她截获了一条秘密信息。


现在完成时:
The police have intercepted several suspicious packages. = 警方已经拦截了几个可疑包裹。
They have intercepted communications between the two leaders. = 他们已经截获了这两位领导人之间的通讯。


过去完成时:
The spy had intercepted the coded message before the enemy could decode it. = 间谍在敌人破译密码之前截获了该密码信息。
She had intercepted the ball before it reached the opponent's goal. = 在球到达对方球门前，她已经拦截了球。


将来时:
The air defense system will intercept any incoming missiles. = 防空系统将拦截任何来袭导弹。
They will intercept the shipment at the border. = 他们将在边境拦截这批货物。


虚拟语气:
If they had intercepted the message earlier, they could have prevented the attack. = 如果他们早点拦截到信息，他们就能阻止袭击。
I wish I had intercepted that email before it was sent. = 我真希望在邮件发送前我就能拦截到它。


被动语态:
The message was intercepted by the enemy. = 信息被敌人拦截了。
The pass was intercepted by the opposing team. = 传球被对方球队拦截了。


疑问句:
Was the pass intercepted? = 传球被拦截了吗？
Were the communications intercepted? = 通讯被拦截了吗？


否定句:
The police did not intercept the criminal. = 警方没有拦截到罪犯。
They did not intercept the package. = 他们没有拦截包裹。
