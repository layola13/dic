Masochism is a complex psychological phenomenon that can manifest in various ways, from mild self-punishment to extreme self-harm. It's often linked to feelings of guilt, shame, or a need for control.  |例句：His masochistic tendencies led him to seek out increasingly painful experiences. 他的受虐倾向使他寻求越来越痛苦的经历。
