preutilizing: /ˌpriːˈjuːtɪlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |预先利用；提前使用 | Using something before its intended or usual time.  This word is not commonly used and may be considered informal or even neologistic (newly coined). It combines "pre-" (before) and "utilizing" (using).|


例句：

The company is preutilizing its resources to prepare for the upcoming project. 公司正在预先利用其资源为即将到来的项目做准备。


The farmer was preutilizing the rainwater by storing it in tanks. 这位农民正在通过储水罐预先利用雨水。


近义词：pre-empting,  anticipating (in the context of use), preparing


反义词：postponing, delaying, neglecting


词性：动词
