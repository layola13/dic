fry-pan: /ˈfraɪpæn/ | n. | 煎锅 | A shallow, broad pan used for frying food.  | Example:  I cooked the eggs in a fry-pan. 我用煎锅煎了鸡蛋。 | 近义词：frying pan, skillet | 反义词：None.
