purge-out:/pɜːrdʒ aʊt/ | vt. |清除；肃清；彻底清除|To completely remove or eliminate something unwanted or undesirable, often forcefully or thoroughly.  |例句：The dictator purged out all opposition to his rule. 独裁者清除掉了所有反对他的统治的人。

例句：The company purged out all outdated equipment to modernize its production line. 公司清除所有过时的设备以使其生产线现代化。


近义词：eliminate, remove, eradicate, expunge, cleanse


反义词：introduce, add, insert, retain


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They purge out the old data every month. = 他们每月清除旧数据。
The government tries to purge out corruption. = 政府试图清除腐败。


一般过去时:
The police purged out the illegal gambling dens. = 警察清除掉了非法赌场。
She purged out all the negative thoughts from her mind. = 她从脑海中清除掉了所有消极的想法。


现在完成时:
They have purged out most of the bugs from the software. = 他们已经从软件中清除掉了大部分的错误。
We have purged out all the unnecessary files from the hard drive. = 我们已经从硬盘中清除掉了所有不必要的文件。


过去完成时:
They had purged out the evidence before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们已经销毁了证据。
She had purged out all her old clothes before moving house. = 她搬家前已经清理掉了所有旧衣服。


将来时:
They will purge out the contaminated water from the reservoir. = 他们将从水库中清除受污染的水。
The company will purge out all obsolete products from the market. = 公司将清除所有过时的产品。


虚拟语气:
If they purged out the corruption, the country would prosper. = 如果他们清除腐败，国家就会繁荣。
I wish they would purge out the harmful chemicals from the environment. = 我希望他们能从环境中清除有害化学物质。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
