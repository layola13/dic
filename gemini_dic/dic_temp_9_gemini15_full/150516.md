pantomiming:/ˈpæntəˌmaɪmɪŋ/| v.|哑剧表演；用哑剧表演|The act of expressing oneself or telling a story without words, using only gestures and facial expressions.  It often involves mimicking actions or events.|例句：He was pantomiming a scene from a famous play. 他正在用哑剧表演一出著名戏剧的场景。

例句：The children were pantomiming a fight between a lion and a tiger. 孩子们正在用哑剧表演狮子和老虎之间的打斗。


近义词：miming, acting out, gesturing


反义词：speaking, vocalizing


词性:动词
