frontispieces:/ˈfrɒntaɪspiːsiz/ | n. |扉页(复数) |An illustration or decorative design facing the title page of a book.  It is often an engraving or a picture relevant to the book's content.|例句：The book's frontispieces depicted scenes from the author's life.|近义词：title page illustration, introductory image|反义词：None
