canelé:/kænəˈleɪ/| n. |卡内雷(一种法国小蛋糕)|A small, cylindrical French pastry with a caramelized crust and a soft, custardy center.  It is traditionally made with a batter containing rum and often eggs, butter, flour, and milk. | Example: The canelé's crunchy exterior contrasted beautifully with its tender interior. 卡内雷酥脆的外皮与它柔软的内部形成了美丽的对比。|近义词：pastry, cake |反义词：无
