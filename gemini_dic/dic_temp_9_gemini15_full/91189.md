hydrator:/ˈhaɪdreɪtər/ | n. |保湿剂，补水器 | A substance or device that adds water or moisture.  A hydrator can refer to a skincare product that moisturizes the skin, or a device that adds moisture to the air. | Example:  She uses a facial hydrator every morning. 她每天早上都使用保湿面霜。  The new hydrator in our office has significantly improved air quality. 我们办公室的新加湿器大大改善了空气质量。 | 近义词：moisturizer, humectant, humidifier | 反义词：dehydrator

词源:
The word "hydrator" is derived from the word "hydrate," which comes from the Greek words "hydro" (ὕδωρ), meaning "water," and "-ate," a suffix indicating a process or state.  Therefore, "hydrator" literally means something that performs the action of hydrating, or adding water.
