evangelistic:/ˌiːvænˈdʒɛlɪstɪk/ | adj. |布道式的；传教的；热心的；狂热的 | Relating to or characteristic of evangelism; zealous in spreading a cause or belief.  | The pastor delivered an evangelistic sermon on the importance of faith.  牧师发表了一篇关于信仰重要性的布道式讲道。 | zealous, missionary, fervent, passionate | apathetic, indifferent, unconcerned, lukewarm
