connusance: /kɒˈnuːsəns/ | n. | 知悉；认识；了解 | Knowledge or awareness of something.  The term is largely obsolete except in legal contexts.  | 例句：He had no connusance of the matter. 他对此事毫不知情。  |近义词：knowledge, awareness, cognizance | 反义词：ignorance, unawareness | 名词


USAGE->
This word is largely archaic and doesn't have common usage across different tenses.  The following examples demonstrate how it *could* be used in different tenses if one were writing in a very formal or legalistic style:

一般现在时:
He has connusance of the agreement. = 他知悉该协议。
The court requires connusance of all relevant facts. = 法院要求知悉所有相关事实。

一般过去时:
She had connusance of the impending danger. = 她知悉迫在眉睫的危险。
The witness claimed connusance of the defendant's activities. = 证人声称知悉被告的行为。

现在完成时:
They have had connusance of the situation for some time. = 他们已经知悉这种情况一段时间了。
The lawyer has gained connusance of the case details. = 律师已经了解了案件的细节。

过去完成时:
He had had connusance of the plan before it was implemented. = 在计划实施之前，他已经知悉该计划。
The manager had had connusance of the employee's misconduct. = 经理此前已经知悉员工的失职行为。

将来时:
She will have connusance of the results soon. = 她很快就会知悉结果。
The board will be given connusance of the financial report. = 董事会将收到财务报告。

虚拟语气:
If he had connusance of the problem, he would have acted differently. = 如果他知道这个问题，他会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had connusance of the future. = 我希望我能知道未来。

被动语态:
The agreement was brought to his connusance. = 协议已让他知悉。
The information was kept from his connusance. = 信息没有让他知悉。

疑问句:
Did he have connusance of the crime? = 他是否知悉这起犯罪？
Does the witness have connusance of the events? = 证人是否知悉这些事件？

否定句:
He had no connusance of the meeting. = 他不知道这次会议。
She claimed to have no connusance of the matter. = 她声称对此事毫不知情。


PHRASE->
Because of its archaic nature,  "connusance" doesn't readily form common phrases.  Its usage is primarily standalone.  However,  phrases such as the following could be constructed in highly formal or legal contexts:

to have connusance of = 知悉
to bring something to someone's connusance = 使某人知悉某事
lack of connusance = 不知情
plea of lack of connusance = 辩称不知情
prior connusance = 预先知情
