deadened:/ˈdɛdnəd/ | adj. | 减弱的；麻木的；迟钝的 |  Reduced in intensity or effect; made less sensitive or responsive.  | 例句：The pain medication deadened the throbbing in his head.  止痛药减轻了他头痛的剧烈程度。

The constant noise deadened her senses.  持续的噪音使她的感官麻木。


近义词：dull, numb, blunted, muted, weakened | 反义词：sharpened, intensified, heightened, acute
