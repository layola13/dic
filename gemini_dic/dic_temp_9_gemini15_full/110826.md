linoleum:/lɪˈnoʊliəm/| n. |油毡；油地毡 |A floor covering made from a mixture of linseed oil, powdered cork, and rosin, often with a canvas backing.  It is durable and relatively inexpensive. |The kitchen floor is covered in linoleum. 厨房的地板铺着油毡。|近义词：vinyl flooring, vinyl, floor covering |反义词：hardwood, tile, carpet
