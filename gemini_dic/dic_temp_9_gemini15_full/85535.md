hatchbacks:/ˈhætʃbæks/| n.|掀背车|A hatchback is a car with a rear door that opens upwards, providing access to the luggage compartment.  This design combines features of a sedan and a station wagon.|Example:  Many young drivers prefer hatchbacks for their practicality and fuel efficiency.  许多年轻司机更喜欢掀背车，因为它们实用且省油。|近义词：liftback, tailgate (less common)|反义词：sedan, saloon (in British English)
