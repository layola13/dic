eggier:/ˈɛɡiər/| adj.|更像鸡蛋的；更富蛋味的|More like an egg; having a stronger taste or smell of eggs.|The eggier the omelet, the better I like it.|近义词：eggy|反义词：less eggy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This cake is eggier than the last one. = 这个蛋糕比上一个更像鸡蛋。
The custard is eggier this time. = 这回的蛋羹蛋味更浓。


一般过去时:
The souffle was eggier yesterday. = 昨天的苏芙蕾蛋味更浓。
The quiche tasted eggier than I expected. =  quiche 的蛋味比我想象的更浓。


现在完成时:
The chef has made the eggs eggier this year. = 厨师今年做的鸡蛋蛋味更浓了。
I have never tasted such an eggy, eggier cake before. = 我以前从未吃过这么浓蛋味的蛋糕。


过去完成时:
The omelet had been eggier before they added the cream. = 在他们加奶油之前，煎蛋卷的蛋味更浓。
The eggs had become eggier after being stored for a long time. = 鸡蛋存放久了，蛋味更浓了。


将来时:
The cake will be eggier if we add more eggs. = 如果我们再加些鸡蛋，蛋糕的蛋味会更浓。
The scrambled eggs will be eggier than usual tomorrow. = 明天的炒鸡蛋会比平时更浓蛋味。


虚拟语气:
If the sauce were eggier, it would be perfect. = 如果酱汁蛋味更浓，那就完美了。
I wish the pudding were eggier; it lacks flavour. = 我希望布丁蛋味更浓一些；它缺乏味道。


被动语态:
The eggs were made eggier by adding more yolks. = 通过添加更多的蛋黄，鸡蛋变得更浓蛋味了。
The recipe was designed to make the cake eggier. = 这个食谱的设计是为了让蛋糕的蛋味更浓。


疑问句:
Is this cake eggier than the other one? = 这个蛋糕比另一个更像鸡蛋吗？
Isn't the batter eggier now that we added extra eggs? = 我们加了额外的鸡蛋后，面糊是不是蛋味更浓了？


否定句:
The cake isn't eggier than I thought it would be. = 蛋糕的蛋味没有我想象的那么浓。
The soufflé wasn't as eggy/eggier as I had hoped. =  苏芙蕾的蛋味没有我期望的那么浓。
