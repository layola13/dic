recidivation:/ˌriˈsɪdɪveɪʃən/| n. |累犯；再犯；反复；重犯|The act of reverting to a previous pattern of behavior, especially criminal behavior after a period of apparent rehabilitation.  It often refers to a criminal's relapse into criminal activity after having been convicted and punished for a previous crime.|例句：The high rate of recidivation among young offenders is a serious concern.  年轻罪犯的高再犯率是一个严重的问题。|例句：His recidivation led to a longer prison sentence. 他的再次犯罪导致了更长的刑期。|近义词：relapse, repetition, recurrence|反义词：reformation, rehabilitation
