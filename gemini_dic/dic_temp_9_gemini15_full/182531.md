rentier:/rɛnˈtiːr/|/ˈræntiər/| n. |食利者；靠租金生活的人；地主|A rentier is a person who lives on income from property or investments, rather than from employment or business.  They derive their income primarily from rent, dividends, interest, or other forms of passive income.|例句：Many wealthy individuals are rentiers, relying on their investments for a comfortable lifestyle.  许多富人都是食利者，依靠投资过着舒适的生活。

例句：The economic model favors rentiers, enabling them to accumulate wealth without active participation in production.  这种经济模式有利于食利者，使他们能够在不积极参与生产的情况下积累财富。


近义词：investor, landlord, capitalist|反义词：worker, laborer, entrepreneur|名词
