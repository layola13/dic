judas:/ˈdʒuːdəs/| n.|犹大，背叛者|A person who betrays someone, especially a friend or a cause.  The name comes from Judas Iscariot, the disciple who betrayed Jesus.|例句：He was branded a Judas for revealing the company's secrets. 他因泄露公司秘密而被贴上叛徒的标签。

例句：His actions made him a Judas in the eyes of his former colleagues.  他的行为使他在前同事眼中成了叛徒。


近义词：traitor, betrayer, informer, double-crosser

反义词：loyalist, confidant, friend


词性:名词
