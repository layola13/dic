glyceride:/ˈɡlɪsərɪd/| n.|甘油酯，甘油三酯|A glyceride is an ester derived from glycerol and one or more fatty acids.  Glycerides are a major component of fats and oils.  They are also known as triglycerides when three fatty acids are attached.|例句：Fats and oils are composed primarily of glycerides.  脂肪和油主要由甘油酯组成。|例句：The analysis revealed a high concentration of saturated glycerides in the sample. 分析显示样品中饱和甘油酯浓度很高。|近义词：triglyceride, fat, oil|反义词：无
