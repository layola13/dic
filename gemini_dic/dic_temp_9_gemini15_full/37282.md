commandeering: /ˌkɒmənˈdɪərɪŋ/ | /ˌkɑːmənˈdɪrɪŋ/ | vt. | 霸占；强占；征用 | To take and control (something, typically property, a vehicle, or a resource) without permission.  | 例句：The rebels commandeered a truck and drove away. 反叛者霸占了一辆卡车然后逃走了。


The army commandeered several buildings for use as hospitals.  军队征用了几栋建筑物作为医院。


近义词：seize, usurp, expropriate, requisition, appropriate | 反义词：cede, relinquish, surrender, yield | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He commandeers the meeting every time. = 他每次都控制会议的走向。
They commandeer resources without permission. = 他们未经许可就占用资源。

一般过去时:
The soldiers commandeered the local school. = 士兵们征用了当地的学校。
The pirates commandeered the ship. = 海盗们劫持了那艘船。

现在完成时:
They have commandeered all the available supplies. = 他们已经征用了所有可用的物资。
The rebels have commandeered several vehicles. = 反叛分子已经霸占了几辆车。

过去完成时:
He had commandeered the car before the police arrived. = 警方到达之前他已经抢走了那辆车。
The protesters had commandeered the town square. = 抗议者们已经占领了市中心广场。

将来时:
They will commandeer the airport if necessary. = 必要时他们将控制机场。
The government will commandeer private land for the project. = 政府将为该项目征用私人土地。

虚拟语气:
If they needed to, they would commandeer the train. = 如果他们需要的话，他们会霸占火车。
I wish they hadn't commandeered my car! = 我真希望他们没有霸占我的车！

被动语态:
The building was commandeered by the military. = 这栋建筑物被军队征用了。
The resources were commandeered for the war effort. = 资源被征用用于战争努力。

疑问句:
Did they commandeer the bridge? = 他们占领了桥吗？
Has the government commandeered any private property? = 政府征用了任何私人财产吗？

否定句:
They did not commandeer the building. = 他们没有占领那栋建筑物。
The army will not commandeer private homes. = 军队不会征用民宅。


PHRASE->
commandeer a vehicle = 征用车辆
commandeer resources = 征用资源
commandeer property = 征用财产
commandeer a building = 征用建筑物
commandeer supplies = 征用物资


词源:
From French *commander* "to command," from Latin *commendare* "to entrust, commend," from *com-* "together" + *mandare* "to order." The sense shifted from "to entrust" to "to take control of" over time.
