词源:
The word "sinological" is derived from "Sino-", a combining form meaning "Chinese," and "-logical," meaning "relating to the study of."  "Sino-" itself comes from Latin "Sinae," an ancient name for China.  Therefore, "sinological" literally means "relating to the study of things Chinese."
