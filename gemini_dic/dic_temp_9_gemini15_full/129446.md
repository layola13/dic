non-acidic:/ˌnɑːn əˈsɪdɪk/| adj.|非酸性的；不含酸的|Not acidic; lacking acidity.  The term refers to substances that do not exhibit the characteristic properties of acids, such as a sour taste, the ability to react with bases to form salts, and a low pH value.  |例句：The soil is non-acidic, making it ideal for growing certain plants. 这土壤是非酸性的，非常适合种植某些植物。

The solution is non-acidic and safe for use on sensitive skin.  该溶液是非酸性的，对敏感肌肤安全无害。|近义词：neutral, alkaline, base|反义词：acidic, sour
