pickets:/ˈpɪkɪts/| n.|纠察队；罢工纠察线；尖桩；木桩|People who stand outside a workplace during a strike to try to persuade other workers not to go to work.  A picket line is a group of pickets.  It can also refer to pointed stakes or wooden posts.|例句：The workers formed a picket line outside the factory. 工人们在工厂外组成了一条纠察线。

例句：The farmer used pickets to build a fence. 农民用尖桩来建篱笆。


近义词：demonstrators, protesters, strikers|反义词：employers, management|词性:名词
