hopelessly:/ˈhoʊpləsli/| adj.|绝望地；无望地；极度地|Completely without hope; in a way that shows a lack of hope.  It can also mean to an extreme degree.|例句：He was hopelessly in love with her. 他对她爱得死去活来。

例句：The situation seemed hopelessly complicated.  情况看起来复杂到了绝望的地步。


近义词：desperately, despairingly, irrevocably, irretrievably|反义词：hopefully, optimistically


词性:形容词
