meteor:/ˈmiːtiɔːr/| n.|流星，陨星|A meteor is a small body of matter from outer space that enters the earth's atmosphere, becoming incandescent as a result of friction and appearing as a streak of light.  Meteors are also known as shooting stars.|例句：A bright meteor streaked across the night sky. 一颗明亮的流星划过夜空。

例句：We saw several meteors during our camping trip.  我们在露营期间看到了几颗流星。


近义词：shooting star, meteoroid (before entering atmosphere)


反义词：无


词性：名词
