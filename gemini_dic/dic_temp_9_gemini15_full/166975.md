prestudies:/ˈpriːstʌdiz/| n. |预研，预习，前期研究 |Preliminary studies or investigations conducted before a larger project or research undertaking.  These studies often lay the groundwork for a more extensive investigation by identifying key variables, refining methodologies, or exploring preliminary hypotheses. |例句：The prestudies for the new drug showed promising results. = 新药的预研结果显示前景广阔。

例句：Extensive prestudies were conducted before the construction of the dam began. = 在大坝建设开始之前进行了大量的预研。


近义词： preliminary studies, preparatory research, pilot studies, exploratory studies

反义词：final studies, conclusive research


词性:名词
