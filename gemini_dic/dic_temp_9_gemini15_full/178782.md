recumbently:/rɪˈkʌmbəntli/ | adv. | 懒散地；倚卧地；斜躺地 | In a reclining or lying-down position.  The adverb describes a state of being relaxed and lying down, often implying a lack of energy or a sense of leisure. | Example: He lay recumbently on the grass, gazing at the clouds. 他懒洋洋地躺在草地上，凝视着云朵。| 近义词：supinely, languidly, indolently | 反义词：uprightly, energetically, actively | 副词
