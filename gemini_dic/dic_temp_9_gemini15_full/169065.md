programme:/ˈprəʊɡræm/ /ˈproʊɡræm/| n. v.|节目；规划；程序；课程|A planned series of future events; a set of instructions for a computer; a course of study.|例句：The television programme was very popular.  电视节目很受欢迎。|例句：The government has a programme to reduce poverty. 政府有一个减少贫困的计划。|近义词：plan, schedule, scheme, course|反义词：chaos, disorder


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school programmes a variety of extracurricular activities. = 学校规划了各种课外活动。
This software programme runs smoothly on my computer. = 这个软件程序在我的电脑上运行流畅。


一般过去时:
They programmed the robot to perform specific tasks. = 他们对机器人进行了编程以执行特定任务。
The company programmed a new marketing strategy last year. = 公司去年制定了一个新的营销策略。


现在完成时:
