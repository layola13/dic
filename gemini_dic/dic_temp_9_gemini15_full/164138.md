predispose:/ˌpriːdɪˈspoʊz/| vt.|预先处置；使易于患病；使有倾向|to make someone or something more likely to suffer from a particular disease or problem, or to behave in a particular way|例句：A family history of heart disease predisposes you to the condition. 家族心脏病史使你更容易患上这种疾病。
例句：His upbringing predisposed him to violence. 他的成长经历使他容易走向暴力。|近义词：predestine, incline, make susceptible|反义词：protect, shield, safeguard|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Her genetic makeup predisposes her to certain illnesses. = 她的基因构成使她容易患某些疾病。
Does smoking predispose people to lung cancer? = 吸烟是否使人更容易患肺癌？


一般过去时:
The stressful environment predisposed him to depression. = 充满压力的环境使他容易患上抑郁症。
Their upbringing predisposed them to a life of crime. = 他们的成长环境使他们容易走上犯罪的道路。


现在完成时:
Years of poor diet have predisposed her to heart disease. = 多年的不良饮食习惯使她容易患上心脏病。
His lack of exercise has predisposed him to a variety of health problems. = 他缺乏运动使他容易出现各种健康问题。


过去完成时:
His childhood trauma had predisposed him to anxiety disorders. = 他童年的创伤使他容易患上焦虑症。
The previous drought had predisposed the region to wildfires. = 先前的干旱使该地区容易发生野火。


将来时:
Genetic testing will help determine if she is predisposed to breast cancer. = 基因检测将有助于确定她是否容易患乳腺癌。
His unhealthy lifestyle will predispose him to serious health issues in the future. = 他不健康的生活方式将来会使他容易出现严重的健康问题。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't smoked, he might not have been predisposed to lung cancer. = 如果他不吸烟，他可能就不会容易患肺癌。
If she had eaten healthier, she might not have been predisposed to diabetes. = 如果她吃得更健康，她可能就不会容易患糖尿病。


被动语态:
He was predisposed to depression by the loss of his job. = 他因为失业而容易患上抑郁症。
Many people are predisposed to certain diseases by their genes. = 许多人由于基因原因而容易患上某些疾病。


疑问句:
Is he predisposed to any allergies? = 他对任何过敏原过敏吗？
Are certain ethnic groups predisposed to specific diseases? = 特定的种族群体是否容易患上某些特定疾病？


否定句:
He wasn't predisposed to violence, despite his difficult childhood. = 尽管童年坎坷，但他并不容易走向暴力。
She wasn't predisposed to any particular health problems. = 她不容易患上任何特别的健康问题。
