seaboards:/ˈsiːbɔːrdz/ | n. |海岸线；海滨；海边地区 |The term "seaboards" refers to the coastal regions of a country or continent.  It emphasizes the land areas adjacent to the sea, often including features like beaches, cliffs, and ports.  The plural form "seaboards" suggests multiple coastal areas. |例句：The west seaboards of the United States are known for their diverse ecosystems. 美国西海岸以其多样化的生态系统而闻名。

The eastern seaboards of Asia are densely populated.亚洲东部沿海地区人口稠密。|近义词：coastline, coast, seashore, shore |反义词：inland, interior
