maxes out: /mæksˈaʊt/ | v. |达到最大值；达到极限；用完；耗尽 | To reach the maximum level or limit; to use up completely. |例句：The computer's memory maxed out during the video game.  电脑在玩游戏时内存用完了。

The engine maxed out at 150 mph. 这台引擎的最高时速达到了150英里。|近义词：saturate, saturate, peak, top out |反义词：bottom out, minimize
