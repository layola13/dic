coranto:/kəˈræntoʊ/| n.|早报；新闻简报|A coranto was a type of early newspaper, typically published weekly or more frequently, containing news from various sources.  It often featured brief reports and was less analytical than later newspapers. |Example:  The coranto brought news of the king's death from distant lands.  早报带来了国王在远方去世的消息。|近义词：newspaper, gazette, bulletin|反义词：无
