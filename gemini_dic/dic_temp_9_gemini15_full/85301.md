harnesser: /ˈhɑːrnɪsər/ | n. | 利用者；驾驭者；控制者 | A person or thing that harnesses something.  Someone who utilizes or controls a force or resource for a specific purpose.  | Example: The skilled harnesser of wind energy developed a groundbreaking new turbine.  熟练利用风能的人开发了一种突破性的新型涡轮机。 | 近义词: controller, manipulator, user, exploiter | 反义词:  uncontroller, neglecter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The harnesser controls the power of the river. = 利用者控制着河流的力量。
He harnesses the energy of the sun. = 他利用太阳能。

一般过去时:
The farmer harnessed the horses to the plow. = 农民把马套在犁上。
She harnessed her anger to fuel her work. = 她利用她的愤怒来激励她的工作。

现在完成时:
They have harnessed the power of the atom. = 他们已经利用了原子的力量。
We have harnessed renewable energy sources. = 我们已经利用了可再生能源。

过去完成时:
He had harnessed the wild horses before the competition. = 他在比赛前已经驯服了野马。
She had harnessed her creativity to produce a masterpiece. = 她运用她的创造力创作了一部杰作。

将来时:
They will harness the power of the tides. = 他们将利用潮汐的力量。
We will harness the power of AI to improve efficiency. = 我们将利用人工智能的力量来提高效率。

虚拟语气:
If we harnessed the power of the wind, we could reduce our carbon footprint. = 如果我们利用风能，我们就能减少碳足迹。
I wish I could harness my emotions better. = 我希望我能更好地控制我的情绪。

被动语态:
The energy was harnessed for the benefit of the community. = 能量被利用来造福社区。
The horses were harnessed to the carriage. = 马被套在马车上。

疑问句:
How can we harness the power of nature? = 我们如何利用大自然的力量？

否定句:
They did not harness the full potential of the technology. = 他们没有充分利用这项技术的潜力。
We cannot harness the power of the storm. = 我们无法利用暴风雨的力量。
