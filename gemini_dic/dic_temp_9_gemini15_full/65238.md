etter: /ˈɛtər/ | n. |信件，函件 | A written or printed message, typically one sent in an envelope by mail. |例句：I received a letter from my grandmother. 我收到了奶奶的一封信。|例句：She wrote a letter of complaint to the company. 她给公司写了一封投诉信。|近义词：missive, communication, note |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He writes letters every week. 他每周都写信。
She sends letters to her pen pal. 她给她的笔友写信。

一般过去时:
They wrote letters to each other. 他们互相写信。
He posted the letter yesterday. 他昨天寄了那封信。

现在完成时:
I have written several letters this month. 这个月我已经写了几封信。
She has received many letters from her fans. 她已经收到很多粉丝的来信。

过去完成时:
He had already written the letter before she arrived. 她来之前他已经写好信了。
They had sent the letter before the deadline. 他们早在截止日期之前就寄出了信。

将来时:
I will write a letter to my parents tomorrow. 我明天将写信给我的父母。
She will send the letter by express mail. 她将通过快递邮寄这封信。

虚拟语气:
If I had written a letter, I would have felt better. 如果我写了封信，我会感觉好一些。
I wish I had written to him sooner. 我希望我早点给他写信。

被动语态:
The letter was written by hand. 这封信是手写的。
The letters are being sent to the recipients. 信件正在寄给收件人。

疑问句:
Did you receive my letter? 你收到我的信了吗？
Have you written a letter yet? 你写信了吗？

否定句:
I didn't write a letter to him. 我没有给他写信。
She hasn't received my letter yet. 她还没收到我的信。
