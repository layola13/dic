drug: /drʌɡ/ | n., v. | 药物；毒品；使服药；使服用毒品 |

英文解析:A drug is a substance that is used as a medicine or that has a physiological effect on the body. It can be legal, such as prescription medication, or illegal, such as heroin or cocaine.  As a verb, it means to give someone a drug, often forcibly or secretly.

例句:
He is taking prescription drugs for his high blood pressure. 他正在服用处方药来治疗高血压。

The police arrested him for drug trafficking.  警方逮捕了他，罪名是贩毒。


近义词: medicine, medication, narcotic, opiate, substance

反义词: antidote (in the context of counteracting a drug's effects)


词性: noun, verb
