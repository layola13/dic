scepterless:/ˈsɛptərləs/| adj.|  无权杖的，缺乏权威的 |  Lacking a scepter; lacking authority or power. The word combines "scepter" (a symbol of royal authority) and the suffix "-less" (meaning "without").  | Example: The once powerful king, now scepterless and deposed, wandered the streets alone.  曾经强大的国王，如今失去权杖，被废黜，独自一人在街上游荡。|近义词：powerless, authorityless,  un-kingly|反义词：powerful, authoritative, regal
