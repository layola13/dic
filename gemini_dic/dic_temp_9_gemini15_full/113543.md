macac: /məˈkæk/ | n. |猕猴 | A macaque is any of several species of Old World monkeys with short tails and prominent cheek pouches.  They are known for their intelligence and social complexity. | Example:  The researcher observed the macaques grooming each other.  研究人员观察到猕猴互相梳理毛发。|近义词：rhesus monkey, baboon (less precise)|反义词：None  |Noun
