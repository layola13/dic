keyboard:/ˈkiːbɔːrd/| n. |键盘|A panel of keys, typically used to input data into a computer or other electronic device.  It consists of a set of keys, each associated with a character, function, or command.|Example: I typed my essay on the keyboard. 我在键盘上打出了我的论文。|近义词：keypad, keyset |反义词：None
