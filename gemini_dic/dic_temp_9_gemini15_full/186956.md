ripplingly:/ˈrɪplɪŋli/| adv.|波纹般地，涟漪般地|In a way that resembles ripples or waves; with a gentle undulating movement.|例句：The sunlight rippledly danced on the water's surface.  阳光波纹般地跳跃在水面上。 The silk fabric fell ripplingly to the floor. 丝绸面料波纹般地垂落到地板上。|近义词：undulatingly, wavily, smoothly|反义词：abruptly, jaggedly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water ripples gently. = 水轻轻地泛起涟漪。
The wind ripplingly moves the wheat. = 风波纹般地吹动着麦子。


一般过去时:
The flag rippled in the breeze. = 旗帜在微风中飘动。
