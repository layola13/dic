pseudoeconomically:/ˌsjuːdoʊˌiːkəˈnɑːmɪkli/ | adv. | 似经济地；伪经济地 | In a way that appears economical but is not actually so; falsely economical.  This word describes actions or processes that seem to save money or resources but ultimately don't, often due to hidden or unforeseen costs. | Example:  The company pseudoeconomically outsourced its manufacturing, only to find that transportation and quality control costs far outweighed any savings.  这家公司表面上经济地将制造业外包，但却发现运输和质量控制成本远远超过任何节省。|近义词：unprofitably, uneconomically, inefficiently | 反义词：economically, profitably, efficiently
