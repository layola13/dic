selfish:/ˈsɛlfɪʃ/| adj.|自私的，利己的|Characterized by or showing concern only for oneself and one's own needs and interests, at the expense of others.  Selfish people often prioritize their own desires and needs above the well-being of others. They may be unwilling to share, cooperate, or consider the feelings of others.|例句：It was selfish of him to eat all the cake without offering any to his friends. 他把所有的蛋糕都吃掉了，却没有分给朋友们，真是自私。|例句：Her selfish behavior alienated her from her family and friends. 她的自私行为使她与家人和朋友疏远了。|近义词：egoistic, self-centered, narcissistic, inconsiderate|反义词：altruistic, selfless, unselfish, generous
