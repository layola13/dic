gabbling:/ˈɡæblɪŋ/ | v. |喋喋不休地说话，含糊不清地说 |To speak rapidly and indistinctly; to chatter.  |例句：He was gabbling so fast I couldn't understand a word. 他说得飞快，我一个字都听不懂。|例句：The children were gabbling excitedly about their trip. 孩子们兴奋地喋喋不休地谈论着他们的旅行。|近义词：babbling, chattering, mumbling, slurring |反义词：articulating, enunciating
