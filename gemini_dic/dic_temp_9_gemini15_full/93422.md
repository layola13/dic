illtreat:/ɪlˈtriːt/ | vt. |虐待；粗暴对待 | To treat (a person or animal) cruelly or badly.  |例句：He ill-treated his wife and children. 他虐待了他的妻子和孩子。|例句：The animals were ill-treated in the factory farm.  这些动物在工厂化农场里受到了虐待。|近义词：abuse, mistreat, maltreat, persecute |反义词：cherish, pamper, care for |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They ill-treat the animals. = 他们虐待动物。
She ill-treats her employees. = 她虐待她的员工。

一般过去时:
He ill-treated his dog. = 他虐待了他的狗。
They ill-treated the prisoners. = 他们虐待了囚犯。

现在完成时:
They have ill-treated the refugees. = 他们虐待了难民。
She has ill-treated her pet for years. = 她多年来一直虐待她的宠物。

过去完成时:
He had ill-treated the horse before the race. = 他在比赛前虐待了那匹马。
The workers had ill-treated the machinery for a long time. = 工人们长期虐待机器。

将来时:
They will ill-treat the captives. = 他们将会虐待俘虏。
She will ill-treat anyone who opposes her. = 她会虐待任何反对她的人。

虚拟语气:
If he ill-treated his children, he would regret it. = 如果他虐待了他的孩子，他会后悔的。
I wish they hadn't ill-treated the animals. = 我希望他们没有虐待动物。

被动语态:
The animals were ill-treated. = 动物们受到了虐待。
The workers were ill-treated by the manager. = 工人们受到了经理的虐待。

疑问句:
Did they ill-treat the children? = 他们虐待了孩子们吗？
Were the animals ill-treated? = 动物们受到了虐待吗？

否定句:
They did not ill-treat the animals. = 他们没有虐待动物。
She would never ill-treat her children. = 她永远不会虐待她的孩子。


PHRASE->
ill-treat somebody badly = 严重虐待某人
ill-treat somebody physically = 肢体虐待某人
ill-treat somebody emotionally = 情绪虐待某人
ill-treat somebody verbally = 语言虐待某人
ill-treat somebody systematically = 系统性地虐待某人


词源:
ill- (表示坏的，不好的) + treat (对待)  The prefix "ill-" adds a negative connotation to the verb "treat," indicating bad or cruel treatment.
