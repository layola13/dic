empower:/ɪmˈpaʊər/| vt.|授权；使能够；使自主|To give someone more control over their own life or the power to do something.|例句：The course is designed to empower women to start their own businesses. 这门课程旨在帮助女性创业。|例句：Education empowers people to make informed decisions. 教育使人们能够做出明智的决定。|近义词：authorize, enable, strengthen|反义词：disable, weaken, restrict|动词
