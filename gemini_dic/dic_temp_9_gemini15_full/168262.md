pro-Japanese: /ˌproʊ ˈdʒæpəˌniːz/ | adj. | 亲日的 |  Supporting or favoring Japan or Japanese people and culture.  This term often carries a political or ideological connotation, indicating a positive attitude towards Japan, its policies, or its people. It can be used neutrally to describe someone who is simply fond of Japanese culture, or it can be used more critically to describe someone who uncritically supports Japanese actions, even if those actions are controversial.


例句：

He holds pro-Japanese sentiments stemming from his years spent living in Japan. = 他对日本怀有亲日情绪，这源于他在日本居住的那些年。

The pro-Japanese demonstrations were a clear sign of public support for the government's policies. = 亲日的示威游行清楚地表明公众支持政府的政策。


近义词：Japanophile (n.), pro-Japanese (adj.) ,  phil-Japanese (adj.)


反义词：anti-Japanese (adj.), Japanophobic (adj.)
