demonstration: /ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃən/ | n. | 示范；演示；游行；证明 |  A demonstration is a public display of opinion or feeling, often involving a march or protest.  It can also refer to a practical exhibition showing how something works or is done. |


例句：

The scientist gave a demonstration of the new invention.  科学家演示了新发明。

Thousands of people took part in a demonstration against the war.  成千上万的人参加了反对战争的示威游行。


近义词：display, exhibition, protest, march, proof, showing


反义词：concealment, hiding, refutation


词性：名词
