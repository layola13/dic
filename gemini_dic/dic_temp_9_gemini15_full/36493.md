coleorhiza:/ˌkoʊliˈraɪzə/ | n. |胚芽鞘(植物学)|The coleorhiza is a protective sheath covering the radicle (embryonic root) of a germinating seed, particularly in grasses and other monocotyledons.  It protects the delicate radicle as it pushes through the soil. |The coleorhiza of the corn seed emerged first from the soil.|近义词：radicle sheath (部分情况)|反义词：无
