musclehead:/ˈmʌsəlhed/| n. |肌肉男，大力士|A person, especially a man, who is very muscular and often considered to be excessively so.  This term often carries a slightly negative connotation, implying a lack of intelligence or sophistication.|例句：He's a real musclehead; all brawn and no brain. 他是个十足的肌肉男；四肢发达，头脑简单。 He's a musclehead who spends hours in the gym. 他是个肌肉男，在健身房花费数小时。|近义词：bodybuilder, weightlifter, strongman |反义词：intellectual, weakling
