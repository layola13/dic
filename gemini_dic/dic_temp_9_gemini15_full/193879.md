self-dispatch:/ˌsɛlfˈdɪspætʃ/| n.|自主调度；自行分配|The act of an entity (person, system, etc.) managing its own tasks or resources without external control or direction.  It often implies a degree of automation or self-management. |例句：The smart grid allows for self-dispatch of renewable energy sources based on real-time demand. 智能电网允许根据实时需求自主调度可再生能源。
