norroy:/ˈnɔːrɔɪ/| n. |诺罗伊(英国古代王室纹章官)|The Norroy King of Arms is one of three principal Kings of Arms in the College of Arms in England.  It's a heraldic office responsible for the northern parts of England.|The Norroy King of Arms granted a coat of arms to the newly ennobled family.  诺罗伊纹章官授予这个新贵族家庭纹章。|近义词:King of Arms (泛指)|反义词:无
