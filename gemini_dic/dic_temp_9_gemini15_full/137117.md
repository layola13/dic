nonsubmissiveness:/ˌnɑːnˌsəbˈmɪsɪvnəs/| n. |不顺从；不服从；桀骜不驯|The quality or state of not being submissive; defiance; resistance to authority or control.  It implies a refusal to yield or comply, often characterized by independence and assertiveness. |例句：Her nonsubmissiveness to her boss's unreasonable demands led to her dismissal.  她的不服从老板无理的要求导致她被解雇了。

例句：The students' nonsubmissiveness during the protest shocked the authorities. 学生们在抗议中的不服从震惊了当局。


近义词：defiance, insubordination, recalcitrance, resistance, rebellion, noncompliance


反义词：submissiveness, obedience, compliance, subservience
