pollution: /pəˈluːʃən/ | n. |污染|The presence in or introduction into the environment of a substance or thing that has harmful or poisonous effects.|例句：Air pollution is a major problem in many cities.  空气污染是许多城市的主要问题。
例句：Water pollution can have devastating effects on marine life. 水污染会对海洋生物造成毁灭性影响。|近义词：contamination, contamination, fouling|反义词：cleanliness, purity|
