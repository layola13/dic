pseudodipteral:/ˌsuːdəʊˈdɪptərəl/| adj.|仿双柱式|  Relating to or denoting a type of ancient Greek temple in which the inner columns of a double colonnade are omitted, giving the appearance of a dipteral temple but with a narrower cella.  It creates the illusion of a double colonnade but only has a single row of columns along the sides.|例句：The Temple of Artemis at Ephesus was originally a pseudodipteral structure.|近义词：distyle, amphiprostyle|反义词：dipteral, peripteral
