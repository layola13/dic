hampers:/ˈhæmpərz/ | v. n. | 妨碍；阻碍；篮子 |

英文解析:As a verb, "hamper" means to hinder or impede the progress or movement of something or someone. As a noun, it refers to a large basket, typically used for carrying food or laundry.

例句(Verb):

The heavy rain hampered our progress. = 大雨阻碍了我们的进展。

Regulations hampered the development of the project. = 法规阻碍了项目的开发。


例句(Noun):

She packed a hamper of food for the picnic. = 她为野餐准备了一篮食物。

He carried a laundry hamper down to the basement. = 他把装脏衣服的篮子搬到地下室。


近义词(Verb):hinder, obstruct, impede, restrict, inhibit

反义词(Verb):aid, assist, facilitate, help


词性:Verb & Noun
