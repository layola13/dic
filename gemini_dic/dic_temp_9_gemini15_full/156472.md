piation:/pieɪʃən/| n.|赎罪；赔罪；平息；抚慰|The act of making amends for a wrong or sin; appeasing a deity.  It often involves offering a sacrifice or making restitution. |例句：The priest offered a prayer of piation for the sins of the congregation.  牧师为会众的罪过做了赎罪祈祷。

例句：She sought piation for her hurtful words with a sincere apology. 她试图以真诚的道歉来弥补她伤人的话。


近义词：atonement, expiation, propitiation, reconciliation |反义词：transgression, offense, sin

词性:名词
