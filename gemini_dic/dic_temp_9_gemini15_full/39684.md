considerables:/kənˈsɪdərəblz/| n.|相当大的数量；相当大的事情|Things of considerable importance or size.|The project requires considerable resources. 这个项目需要大量的资源。|近义词：substantial, significant, appreciable|反义词：insignificant, negligible, trifling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There are considerable differences between the two plans. = 这两个计划之间存在相当大的差异。
The task requires considerable skill and patience. = 这项任务需要相当的技巧和耐心。

一般过去时:
He faced considerable challenges in his career. = 他在事业上面临着相当大的挑战。
The company incurred considerable losses last year. = 该公司去年遭受了相当大的损失。


现在完成时:
We have made considerable progress on the project. = 我们在这个项目上取得了相当大的进展。
She has shown considerable improvement in her studies. = 她的学习成绩有了相当大的提高。


过去完成时:
They had already invested considerable sums of money in the venture. = 他们已经在该企业投资了相当大的一笔资金。
He had overcome considerable obstacles to reach his goal. = 他克服了相当大的障碍才达到他的目标。


将来时:
The new regulations will bring about considerable changes. = 新规将带来相当大的变化。
The project is expected to generate considerable revenue. = 预计该项目将产生可观的收入。


虚拟语气:
If there were considerable improvements, we would be happy. = 如果有相当大的改进，我们会很高兴。
I wish I had considerable influence in the decision. = 我希望我对这个决定有相当大的影响力。


被动语态:
Considerable damage was caused by the storm. = 暴风雨造成了相当大的损失。
Considerable attention has been paid to this issue. = 这个问题受到了相当大的关注。


疑问句:
Are there considerable risks involved in this investment? = 这项投资有相当大的风险吗？
Is considerable effort required to complete this task? = 完成这项任务需要付出相当大的努力吗？


否定句:
There were not considerable differences between the two options. = 这两个选项之间没有太大的区别。
The impact of the decision was not considerable. = 这个决定的影响并不大。
