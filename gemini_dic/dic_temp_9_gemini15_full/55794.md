diverticulitises:/ˌdaɪvɜːrtɪkjʊˈlaɪtɪsiːz/| n. |憩室炎（复数）|The plural form of diverticulitis,  referring to multiple instances of inflammation of small pouches (diverticula) that can form in the digestive tract, most commonly in the large intestine.  Diverticulitis is a common condition, and diverticulitises implies the presence of multiple instances of the condition, either in different locations within the digestive tract or potentially affecting the same area repeatedly.  |例句：The patient presented with symptoms consistent with multiple diverticulitises, requiring extensive treatment.|近义词：diverticular disease (a broader term)|反义词：N/A
