sawdusts:/ˈsɔːdʌstz/| n. |木屑(复数)|The fine particles of wood produced by sawing.  Sawdust is often used in woodworking projects, gardening, or as animal bedding.|例句：The workshop floor was covered in sawdusts.  车间的地板上覆盖着木屑。

例句：He swept up the sawdusts after cutting the wood. 他锯完木头后扫掉了木屑。


近义词：wood shavings, wood dust

反义词：无


词性：名词
