outsulk:/aʊtˈsʌlk/| v.|愠怒地不理人，赌气|To remain silent and sulky in order to express displeasure or resentment.  This is typically done in response to a perceived slight or injustice, often in a passive-aggressive manner.|例句：He outsulked her after their argument, refusing to speak for days. 他和她在争吵后赌气不理她，几天都不说话。|例句：She outsulked her parents by ignoring their requests until they relented. 她不理会父母的要求直到他们让步，以此来赌气。|近义词：sulk, pout, mope, sulk it out|反义词：conciliate, appease, placate
