hyperemotionally:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmoʊʃənəli/ | adj. |过度情绪化地，极度情绪化地 | Extremely or excessively emotional.  The word combines "hyper" (meaning excessive or over) with "emotionally" (meaning in a manner relating to emotions). It describes someone reacting with an intensity of feeling that is beyond what is considered typical or normal. |例句：He reacted hyperemotionally to the news, bursting into tears. 他对这个消息反应过度情绪化，放声大哭。

She behaved hyperemotionally throughout the trial, disrupting proceedings.  她在整个审判过程中表现得过度情绪化，扰乱了诉讼程序。|近义词：overemotionally, excessively emotionally, sentimentally (in a less negative context) |反义词：calmly, rationally, stoically
