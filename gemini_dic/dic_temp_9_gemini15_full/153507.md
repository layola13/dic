pensiones:/penˈsjɔːniz/| n.|养老金；退休金；津贴| pensions are regular payments made to people after they retire from work. They provide financial support during retirement.  |Example:  Many retirees rely on their pensions for their living expenses. 许多退休人员依靠养老金维持生活。


Example: The company offers a generous pension plan to its employees. 这家公司为其员工提供了一项丰厚的养老金计划。


近义词：retirement benefits, retirement income, annuity


反义词：None (no direct antonym, but concepts like "active employment" or "personal savings" could be considered opposites in the context of financial security)


词性: 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government pays pensions to its citizens. = 政府向其公民支付养老金。
He receives a monthly pension. = 他每月领取养老金。


一般过去时:
She received a lump-sum pension payment. = 她收到了笔养老金。
They paid out pensions to all eligible retirees. = 他们向所有符合条件的退休人员支付了养老金。


现在完成时:
He has received his pension for ten years. = 他已经领取了十年的养老金。
The company has paid out millions of dollars in pensions. = 公司已经支付了数百万美元的养老金。


过去完成时:
She had already received her pension before she moved. = 在她搬家之前，她已经领取了养老金。
The company had paid out all pensions before the bankruptcy. = 在破产之前，公司已经支付了所有的养老金。


将来时:
He will receive his first pension payment next month. = 他下个月将收到他的第一笔养老金。
The government will increase pensions next year. = 政府明年将提高养老金。


虚拟语气:
If I had a larger pension, I would travel more. = 如果我有更多的养老金，我会旅行更多。
I wish my pension was higher. = 我希望我的养老金更高。


被动语态:
Pensions are paid monthly. = 养老金每月支付。
The pension plan was reformed last year. = 养老金计划去年进行了改革。


疑问句:
How much pension do you receive? = 你领取多少养老金？
When will you start receiving your pension? = 你什么时候开始领取养老金？


否定句:

She did not qualify for a pension. = 她没有资格领取养老金。
