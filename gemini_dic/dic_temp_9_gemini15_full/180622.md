reglued:/riːˈɡluːd/| v. |重新粘合；重新胶合|To glue something again after it has been broken or separated.  This word implies a previous gluing that has failed.|例句：He reglued the broken vase. 他把破花瓶重新粘了起来。
例句：The artist reglued the shattered pieces of the sculpture.艺术家把雕塑破碎的碎片重新粘合起来。|近义词：repaired, mended, rejoined|反义词：unglued, separated
