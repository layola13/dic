gild:/ɡɪld/ | vt. n. |镀金；装饰；使灿烂|to cover something with a thin layer of gold; to make something appear more attractive than it really is|例句：They gilded the frame of the painting.他们给画框镀了金。  The politician gilded his image with promises of prosperity.这位政客用繁荣的承诺来粉饰自己的形象。|近义词：plate, adorn, embellish |反义词：tarnish, dull


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gilds the lily. = 他画蛇添足。
They gild the edges of the picture frame. = 他们给相框边缘镀金。


一般过去时:
She gilded the cake with edible gold leaf. = 她用可食用的金箔装饰蛋糕。
The artist gilded the statue to enhance its beauty. = 艺术家给雕像镀金以增强其美感。


现在完成时:
They have gilded the dome of the church. = 他们已经给教堂的圆顶镀金了。
He has gilded his reputation with carefully chosen words. = 他用精心挑选的词语来粉饰自己的名声。


过去完成时:
The craftsman had gilded the entire altar before the ceremony. = 工匠在仪式前已经给整个祭坛镀金了。
She had gilded the handle of the sword, making it gleam. = 她已经给剑柄镀金，使其闪闪发光。


将来时:
They will gild the entire building with gold leaf. = 他们将用金箔装饰整栋建筑物。
The company will gild its image with a new advertising campaign. = 公司将通过新的广告宣传来提升形象。


虚拟语气:
If they gilded the statue, it would look magnificent. = 如果他们给雕像镀金，它会看起来很壮观。
I wish they had gilded the frame; it would have looked better. = 我希望他们已经给相框镀金了；那样看起来会更好。


被动语态:
The frame was gilded with 24-karat gold. = 画框是用24克拉金镀金的。
The building is being gilded by skilled artisans. = 这座建筑物正在由熟练的工匠镀金。


疑问句:
Will they gild the statue? = 他们会给雕像镀金吗？
Have they gilded the dome yet? = 他们已经给圆顶镀金了吗？


否定句:
They did not gild the picture frame. = 他们没有给相框镀金。
He hasn't gilded his reputation; it's tarnished. = 他没有粉饰自己的名声；它已经玷污了。
