nought:/nɔːt/| n. |零；无；一点也不|The value of zero; nothing; not at all.  It represents the absence of quantity or magnitude.  In some contexts, it can also mean a very small amount.|Example: The thermometer read nought degrees Celsius.  温度计显示零摄氏度。|Example: His efforts came to nought. 他的努力付诸东流。|近义词：zero, nothing, zilch |反义词：something, anything
