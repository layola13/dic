eliminations:/ˌɛlɪmɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. |排除；消除；淘汰；去除 |The process of removing or getting rid of something.  Eliminations can refer to removing elements from a set, candidates from a competition, or problems from a system. |例句：The eliminations in the cooking competition were brutal.  烹饪比赛中的淘汰赛非常残酷。

The eliminations of errors from the software improved its performance.  从软件中消除错误提高了其性能。


近义词：removal, exclusion, deletion, eradication, expungement |反义词：inclusion, addition, retention
