safranin:/ˈsæfrənɪn/| n. |番红，藏红花红|A dye derived from the plant *Carthamus tinctorius*, used as a counterstain in histology to stain cell nuclei a reddish-pink color.  It is also used as a biological stain. |The safranin stained the cell nuclei a vibrant pink.|近义词：basic fuchsin, neutral red |反义词：None specifically, but other stains with contrasting colors could be considered opposites in application.|名词
