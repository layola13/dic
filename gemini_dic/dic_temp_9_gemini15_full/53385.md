disagreed:/ˌdɪs.əˈɡriːd/| v.|不同意，意见不合|To have a different opinion or belief; to not agree.|They disagreed about the best way to proceed. 他们对最佳的处理方法意见不一。| The committee members disagreed on the proposed budget. 委员会成员对拟议的预算意见不一。|agreed, concurred|agreed, concurred


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They disagree on many issues. = 他们在许多问题上意见不一。
We disagree about the best approach. = 我们对最佳方法意见不一。

一般过去时:
She disagreed with his assessment. = 她不同意他的评估。
They disagreed on the terms of the contract. = 他们对合同条款意见不一。

现在完成时:
We have disagreed on this several times. = 我们在这件事上已经多次意见不一了。
They have disagreed about the project's timeline. = 他们对项目的进度安排意见不一。

过去完成时:
He had disagreed with the plan before the meeting. = 他在会议之前就不同意这个计划了。
They had disagreed fiercely about the decision. = 他们对这个决定曾激烈地争论过。

将来时:
I think they will disagree about the cost. = 我认为他们会在成本问题上意见不一。
We predict they will disagree on the next step. = 我们预测他们会在下一步行动上意见不一。

虚拟语气:
If they disagreed, they would have to find a compromise. = 如果他们意见不一，就必须找到一个折衷方案。
I wish they hadn't disagreed so publicly. = 我希望他们不要当众发生争执。

被动语态:
His ideas were disagreed with by many people. = 他的想法遭到许多人的反对。
The proposal was disagreed upon by the board. = 该提案未获得董事会的批准。

疑问句:
Do you disagree with my analysis? = 你不同意我的分析吗？
Does he disagree with the decision? = 他不同意这个决定吗？

否定句:
I don't disagree with your point of view. = 我不反对你的观点。
They didn't disagree on the main points. = 他们在主要问题上没有意见分歧。
