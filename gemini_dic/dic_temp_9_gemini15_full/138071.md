normality:/nɔːˈmæləti/ | n. |常态；正常；标准浓度 |The state or quality of being normal; the usual or expected state of things.  It can also refer to the concentration of a solution, especially in chemistry. |例句：The normality of family life was disrupted by the war.  战争扰乱了正常的家庭生活。|例句：The normality of the acid solution is 1M. 酸溶液的当量浓度为1摩尔/升。|近义词：regularity, normalcy, typicality |反义词：abnormality, irregularity, exception
