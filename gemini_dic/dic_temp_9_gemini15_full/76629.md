gallotannic: /ˌɡæləˈtænɪk/ | n. | 五倍子单宁酸，没食子单宁 | A complex polyphenol composed of gallic acid and glucose.  It is a type of tannin found in gallnuts, sumac, and other plants.  It's used in various applications, including leather tanning, ink making, and medicine. | The leather was treated with gallotannic acid to make it durable. 这皮革用五倍子单宁酸处理过以使其耐用。|  tannic acid, tannin |  (No direct antonym;  terms like "non-tannic" or "untanned" describe the absence of the property) | 名词
