outwork: /ˌaʊtˈwɜːrk/ | vt. |胜过；超过；比…干得多；努力工作以超过 | To surpass or exceed in work or effort; to work harder than. |例句：He outworked all his competitors and won the promotion. 他比所有竞争对手都努力工作，赢得了晋升。


例句：The team outworked their opponents and secured a decisive victory.  这个团队比对手更加努力工作，并取得了决定性的胜利。


近义词：outshine, outperform, surpass, excel | 反义词：underperform, lag behind | 词性：动词
