machiavellian:/ˌmækiəˈvɛliən/| adj.| 马基雅维利的；权谋的；工于心计的 | Characterized by cunning, deceit, and a lack of moral scruples, especially in politics.  It refers to the principles of governance advocated by Niccolò Machiavelli in his book *The Prince*. | Example:  His machiavellian tactics ensured his victory in the election. 他工于心计的策略确保他在选举中获胜。 |近义词：cunning, shrewd, devious, scheming, manipulative |反义词：honest, straightforward, upright, ingenuous
