epidemical: /ˌɛpɪˈdɛmɪkəl/ | adj. |流行的；传染性的 | relating to or characteristic of an epidemic; affecting many individuals at the same time.  |例句：The city is facing an epidemical outbreak of influenza.  这个城市正面临着流感的流行爆发。 |近义词：pandemic, contagious, infectious |反义词：sporadic, isolated


USAGE->

The disease is epidemical in this region. = 这种疾病在这个地区很流行。
This type of behavior is epidemical among teenagers. =  这种行为在青少年中很流行。


一般过去时:
The flu was epidemical last winter. = 去年冬天流感很流行。
The rumour was epidemical throughout the town. = 谣言在整个城镇迅速传播。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
