nonmanagerial: /ˌnɑːnˌmænɪˈdʒɪəriəl/ | adj. |非管理层的；非管理人员的 |  Describing employees or tasks that are not related to management or supervision.  It refers to the roles and responsibilities within an organization that do not involve overseeing or directing the work of others. |

例句：

The nonmanagerial staff are responsible for the day-to-day operations.  非管理层员工负责日常运营。

She held a nonmanagerial position in the marketing department.她在市场部担任非管理职位。


近义词：subordinate, rank-and-file, operational

反义词：managerial, supervisory, executive


词性：形容词
