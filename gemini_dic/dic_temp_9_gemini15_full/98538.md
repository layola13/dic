instigator:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪtər/ | n. |煽动者，挑起者 | A person who instigates something.  Someone who provokes or initiates an event, especially a violent or undesirable one. | The instigator of the fight was quickly identified.  这场斗殴的煽动者很快就被认出来了。|近义词：provocateur, agitator, inciter |反义词：peacemaker, mediator
