outbuy:/ˌaʊtˈbaɪ/| vt.|超过…购买；比…买得多|To buy more of something than someone else.|The small retailer was outbought by the large chain store.|近义词：outspend, surpass|反义词：underbuy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They outbuy their competitors in the market. = 他们在市场上比竞争对手购买更多商品。
We outbuy our rivals every year. = 我们每年都比竞争对手买得多。

一般过去时:
Last year, we outbought our competitors on several key products. = 去年，我们在几个关键产品上比竞争对手购买更多。
The company outbought its rivals in the acquisition of the startup. = 公司在收购这家初创公司时，比竞争对手购买更多。

现在完成时:
They have outbought their rivals in the past decade. = 在过去的十年里，他们一直比竞争对手购买更多。
The investor has outbought all other participants in the auction. = 投资者在拍卖会上比所有其他参与者都买得多。

过去完成时:
By the end of the quarter, they had outbought their competitors in the market. = 到本季度末，他们在市场上的购买量已经超过了竞争对手。
Before the merger, the larger company had outbought the smaller one significantly. = 在合并之前，较大的公司已经大幅超过较小的公司。

将来时:
We will outbuy our rivals in the upcoming bidding war. = 在即将到来的竞标大战中，我们将比竞争对手买得多。
They expect to outbuy their competitors next year. = 他们预计明年将比竞争对手买得多。

虚拟语气:
If we had more capital, we would outbuy our competitors easily. = 如果我们有更多资金，我们将很容易超过竞争对手。
I wish we could outbuy our rivals in this deal. = 我希望我们在这笔交易中能够超过竞争对手。

被动语态:
The small business was outbought by the multinational corporation. = 这家小企业被跨国公司收购。
Our bids were outbought by a larger company. = 我们的出价被一家更大的公司超过。

疑问句:
Did they outbuy their rivals in the last auction? = 他们在上次拍卖会上是否超过了竞争对手？
Will we be able to outbuy them in the future? = 我们将来能否超过他们？

否定句:
We didn't outbuy them this time. = 我们这次没有超过他们。
They failed to outbuy their competitors in that market. = 他们在那个市场上未能超过竞争对手。


PHRASE->
outbuy the competition = 超过竞争对手购买
outbuy rivals = 超过对手购买
outbuy the market = 超过市场购买
outbuy significantly = 大幅超过购买
outbuy consistently = 一直超过购买
