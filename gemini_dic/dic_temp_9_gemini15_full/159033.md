pneumaticity:/ˌnjuːməˈtɪsəti/| n. |充气性；气动性；多气性|The quality of being pneumatic; the presence of air or gas in a structure or organism.  It often refers to the presence of air sacs or cavities within bones, reducing weight and increasing buoyancy.  |例句：The pneumaticity of bird bones contributes to their ability to fly. 鸟骨的充气性有助于它们的飞行能力。

例句：The high degree of pneumaticity in the bones of pterosaurs allowed for efficient flight. 翼龙骨骼高度的充气性使其能够有效飞行。


近义词：airiness, aeration|反义词：solidity, density|词性：名词
