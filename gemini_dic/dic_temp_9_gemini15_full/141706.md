orache: /ˈɒrætʃ/ | n. |菠菜属植物，山菠菜|A plant of the genus *Atriplex*, several species of which are cultivated as leafy vegetables, especially *Atriplex hortensis* (garden orache).  Or ache is sometimes used as a generic term for any of these plants.|例句：We added some orache to the salad for extra flavor.  我们往沙拉里加了一些山菠菜来增加风味。

The orache plants are known for their nutritional value. 山菠菜以其营养价值而闻名。|近义词：good-king-henry (a related species), spinach (a similar leafy vegetable) |反义词：无
