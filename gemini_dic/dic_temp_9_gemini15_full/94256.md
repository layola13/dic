impermanently:/ɪmˈpɜːrmənəntli/ | adv. |暂时地，非永久地 |  Not permanently; for a limited time.  This describes something that is not intended to last forever or for a long time. | 例句：The exhibit will be displayed impermanently in the city hall. = 展览将在市政厅暂时展出。

The artwork was impermanently installed in the park. = 艺术品被暂时安装在公园里。


近义词：temporarily, provisionally, transiently | 反义词：permanently, perpetually, eternally
