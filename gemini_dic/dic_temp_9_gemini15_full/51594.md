devanagaris:/ˌdɛvənəˈɡɑːrɪs/| n. |梵文书写系统|The Devanagari script is an abugida writing system used primarily for writing Sanskrit and other Indo-Aryan languages.  It is characterized by its distinctive cursive style and the use of a horizontal line above the letters.  The script is widely used in India and Nepal.|例句：The Bhagavad Gita is traditionally written in the Devanagari script.  《博伽梵歌》传统上是用梵文书写系统写的。|近义词：Sanskrit script|反义词：n/a|名词
