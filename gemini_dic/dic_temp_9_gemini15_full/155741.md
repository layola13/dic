phonetics: /fəˈnɛtɪks/ | n. |语音学|The scientific study of speech sounds; their physical properties, the way they are produced, and how they are perceived.  It deals with the sounds of human speech, irrespective of their meaning. |例句：Phonetics is a branch of linguistics that analyzes the physical properties of speech sounds. 语音学是语言学的一个分支，它分析语音的物理特性。


例句：The course covered both articulatory phonetics (how sounds are produced) and acoustic phonetics (the physical properties of sounds). 这门课程涵盖了发音语音学（声音是如何产生的）和声学语音学（声音的物理特性）。


近义词：phonology (but phonology studies the sound systems of languages, while phonetics studies the sounds themselves)


反义词：无


词性：名词
