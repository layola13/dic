halisauria:/ˌhæliˈsɔːriə/| n. |海龙类|A clade of extinct marine reptiles from the Triassic period, characterized by their long necks and small heads. They were primarily aquatic and likely fed on fish and other small marine animals.  |例句：The discovery of a new halisaurian fossil sheds light on the evolution of marine reptiles in the Triassic.  发现新的海龙类化石揭示了三叠纪时期海洋爬行动物的进化。|近义词：暂无精确近义词，可根据语境用 marine reptile 或 extinct reptile 代替。|反义词：暂无|词性:名词
