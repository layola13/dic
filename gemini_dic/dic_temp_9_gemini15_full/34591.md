clearway:/ˈklɪəweɪ/ | n. | 清道，净路；（禁止停车的）畅通路段 | A clearway is a section of road where parking is prohibited to ensure the free flow of traffic.  It's designed to keep traffic moving smoothly, especially during peak hours or in emergency situations. | Example:  The city council implemented new clearways to alleviate congestion during rush hour.  市议会实施了新的畅通路段以缓解高峰时段的交通拥堵。  | 近义词：freeway, thoroughfare | 反义词：bottleneck, obstruction |  名词
