feeder:/ˈfiːdər/| n. |喂食器；饲养者；支流；馈线|A feeder is a device or person that provides food or supplies.  It can also refer to a smaller stream or river that flows into a larger one, or a wire or cable that supplies power or data.|例句：The birds gathered around the feeder. 鸟儿聚集在喂食器周围。

例句：The farmer is a feeder of livestock.  这位农民是牲畜的饲养者。

近义词：supplier, provider, tributary, conduit

反义词：consumer, recipient
