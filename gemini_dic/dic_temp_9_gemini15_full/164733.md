prefascist: /ˌpriːˈfæʃɪst/ | adj. | 预法西斯的；法西斯主义之前的 |  Describing something that exhibits characteristics similar to or foreshadows Fascism, but does not fully constitute Fascism itself. It often refers to political ideologies, movements, or policies that share some elements with Fascism but lack its full totalitarian features.  | Example:  The rise of nationalist and authoritarian sentiments in the country was seen by many as a prefascist development.  预法西斯主义的发展。| 近义词:proto-fascist, authoritarian, ultranationalist | 反义词:democratic, liberal, progressive |
