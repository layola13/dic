epicure:/ˈɛpɪkjʊər/| n.|美食家，享乐主义者| a person with refined tastes, especially in food and drink.  Someone who enjoys sensual pleasures, especially good food and drink.|例句：He is a renowned epicure, known for his exquisite palate and vast wine cellar. 他是一位著名的美食家，以其精致的味觉和巨大的酒窖而闻名。|例句：Many epicures consider the region's cuisine to be among the finest in the world. 许多美食家认为该地区的美食是世界上最好的美食之一。|近义词：gourmand, gourmet, connoisseur|反义词：ascetic, puritan
