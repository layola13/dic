misbelieve:/ˌmɪsbɪˈliːv/ | vt. |误信；不相信 | To have a mistaken belief; to fail to believe.  |例句：Don't misbelieve the rumors; they're not true. 不要轻信谣言；它们不是真的。

例句：He misbelieved the politician's promises and was later disappointed. 他误信了这位政治家的承诺，后来感到失望。


近义词：disbelieve, distrust, doubt | 反义词：believe, trust


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misbelieves everything he reads online. = 他不相信他在网上读到的任何东西。
They misbelieve the claims made by the company. = 他们不相信该公司提出的说法。


一般过去时:
I misbelieved his intentions. = 我误解了他的意图。
She misbelieved the weather forecast and got caught in the rain. = 她不相信天气预报，结果淋雨了。


现在完成时:
He has misbelieved many things in his life. = 他一生中有很多事情都误信了。
We have misbelieved the evidence presented to us. = 我们误信了向我们提出的证据。


过去完成时:
I had misbelieved her story until I saw the proof. = 直到我看到证据我才不信她的故事。
They had misbelieved the warnings and suffered the consequences. = 他们不相信警告，并为此付出了代价。


将来时:
I fear they will misbelieve my explanation. = 我担心他们会误解我的解释。
She thinks he will misbelieve her intentions. = 她认为他会误解她的意图。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't misbelieved him, I would have acted differently. = 如果我没有误信他，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I hadn't misbelieved her words. = 我真希望我没有误信她的話。


被动语态:
His claims were misbelieved by many. = 他的说法被许多人误信。
The evidence was misbelieved by the jury. = 证据被陪审团误信。


疑问句:
Did they misbelieve the report? = 他们是否误信了这份报告？
Do you think people will misbelieve the news? = 你认为人们会误信这个消息吗？


否定句:
I didn't misbelieve his story at first. = 我一开始并不怀疑他的故事。
They didn't misbelieve the doctor's diagnosis. = 他们没有误信医生的诊断。


PHRASE->
misbelieve in something = 不相信某事
misbelieve someone's words = 不相信某人的话
misbelieve the evidence = 误信证据
misbelieve a rumour = 轻信谣言
misbelieve the facts = 误解事实

词源:
mis- (错误的) + believe (相信)  The prefix "mis-" indicates a wrong or faulty action.
