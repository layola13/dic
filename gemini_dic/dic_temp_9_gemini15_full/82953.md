gunfighters:/ˈɡʌnˌfaɪtərz/| n. |枪手，枪战人员 |People skilled in using guns, especially in a fight or duel.  They are often depicted in Westerns as outlaws or lawmen. |例句：The gunfighters faced off in the dusty street.  那些枪手在尘土飞扬的街道上对峙。  |例句：The legendary gunfighters of the Wild West are still romanticized today.  美国西部传奇枪手的形象至今仍被浪漫化。|近义词：shooters, pistoleros, duelists |反义词：pacifists
