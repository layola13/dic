politzerization:/ˌpɒlɪtəraɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |波利策化；通气法|The process of inflating a body cavity, typically the middle ear, with air to equalize pressure.  This is primarily used in the treatment of otitis media (middle ear infection).|例句：Politzerization is a technique used to treat middle ear infections by inflating the Eustachian tube.|近义词：inflation, aeration |反义词：deflation, vacuum
