revalidates:/ˌriːˈvælɪdeɪts/| vt.|重新确认有效性；使再次生效|To make something valid or effective again.  This usually refers to something that has expired or become invalid and is now being renewed or reinstated.|例句：The company revalidated its safety certificate. 公司重新确认了其安全证书的有效性。

例句：The court revalidated the previous ruling. 法院重新确认了之前的裁决有效。


近义词：renew, reaffirm, reinstate, revive|反义词：invalidate, annul, cancel|动词
