divider:/dɪˈvaɪdər/ | n. | 分隔物，分界线 | A divider is something that separates or divides things.  It can be a physical object or an abstract concept. For example, a road divider separates lanes of traffic, and a cultural divider separates different groups of people. | 例句：The road divider prevented the cars from colliding.  路上的分隔物阻止了汽车相撞。

The ideological divider between the two countries made cooperation difficult.  两国之间的意识形态分隔线使合作变得困难。|近义词：separator, partition, barrier, boundary |反义词：unifier, connector, joiner |名词
