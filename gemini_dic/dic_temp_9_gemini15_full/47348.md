de-stratifying:/ˌdiːˈstrætɪfaɪɪŋ/| v.|消除分层；使均匀化； |The process of removing or reducing stratification, especially in a social, economic, or environmental context.  It involves breaking down existing layers or divisions to create a more uniform or equitable distribution. |例句：The government implemented policies aimed at de-stratifying society by improving access to education and healthcare.  政府实施旨在通过改善教育和医疗保健的获取来消除社会阶层的政策。

例句：The farmer used a technique to de-stratify the soil, ensuring even distribution of nutrients. 这位农民使用了一种技术来使土壤均匀化，确保养分的均匀分布。


近义词：homogenizing, equalizing, leveling, unifying

反义词：stratifying, layering, differentiating
