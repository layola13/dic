glitches:/ˈɡlɪtʃɪz/| n.|小故障，小毛病，短暂的失误|A glitch is a small problem or fault that prevents something from working correctly, often temporarily.  It can refer to a brief malfunction in a system, a minor error, or a slight imperfection.|例句：There was a glitch in the system, and my payment didn't go through. 系统出现故障，我的付款没有成功。

There were a few glitches in the software, but overall it worked well. 软件有一些小故障，但总的来说运行良好。|近义词：fault, flaw, malfunction, error, bug|反义词：perfection, flawlessness
