self-analyses:/ˈsɛlf əˈnælɪsiːz/| n.|自我分析，自我剖析|The act of analyzing one's own thoughts, feelings, and behaviors.  It often involves introspection and critical self-evaluation to understand one's motivations, strengths, weaknesses, and patterns of behavior.|例句：Through self-analyses, she identified her limiting beliefs and developed strategies to overcome them. 通过自我分析，她发现了自己的局限性思维，并制定了克服这些思维的策略。

例句：His self-analyses revealed a deep-seated fear of failure. 他的自我分析揭示了他内心深处对失败的恐惧。|近义词：self-reflection, introspection, self-examination|反义词：self-deception, denial|名词
