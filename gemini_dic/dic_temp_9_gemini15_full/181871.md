relubrication:/ˌriːluːbrɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |再润滑；重新润滑|The process of lubricating something again after it has become dry or worn.  It's crucial for maintaining the efficiency and longevity of machinery. |例句：The relubrication of the engine components is scheduled for next week. = 引擎部件的再润滑计划在下周进行。

例句：Regular relubrication is essential to prevent premature wear and tear on the machine. = 定期重新润滑对于防止机器过早磨损至关重要。


近义词：re-lubrication, lubrication (if referring to the act, not the process) |反义词：de-lubrication, drying out
