feudal:/ˈfjuːdl/| adj.|封建的；采封建制度的 | Relating to or denoting the feudal system, a system of land tenure that prevailed in Europe from the 9th to the 15th centuries, based on the holding of land in exchange for service or labor.  |The feudal system was characterized by a hierarchical structure of land ownership and loyalty.  |封建制度的特点是土地所有制和忠诚等级制度。| 近义词：feudalist, manorial | 反义词：modern, democratic
