imminence:/ˈɪmɪnəns/| n. |迫在眉睫，即将发生|The state or quality of being about to happen; nearness in time or space.  It suggests a sense of urgency and often a feeling of impending danger or excitement. |例句：The imminence of war filled the citizens with fear.  战争迫在眉睫，使市民们充满了恐惧。

例句：The imminence of the deadline spurred her to work late into the night.  截止日期迫在眉睫，促使她工作到深夜。


近义词：proximity, nearness, closeness, approach, threat


反义词：remoteness, distance, postponement, delay


词性: 名词
