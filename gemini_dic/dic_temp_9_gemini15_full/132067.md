noncovetous: /ˌnɒnˈkʌvɪtəs/ | adj. | 不贪婪的，不贪心的 | Not wanting to possess more than one needs or deserves; free from greed or avarice.  | Example:  He led a noncovetous life, content with what he had. 他过着不贪婪的生活，对拥有的一切感到满足。 |近义词：unambitious, ungreedy, content, modest | 反义词：covetous, avaricious, greedy, rapacious

词源:
The word "noncovetous" is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "covetous" (meaning "greedy").  "Covetous" ultimately derives from the Old French "coveiter" (to desire), which in turn comes from the Latin "cupīdō" (desire, longing).
