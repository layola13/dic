maximised:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzd/| v. |最大化；使最大限度地发挥作用 |To increase or make the most of something.  |例句：The company maximised its profits by cutting costs. 公司通过削减成本最大限度地提高了利润。
例句：We need to maximise the impact of our advertising campaign. 我们需要最大限度地发挥我们的广告宣传活动的影响。|近义词：optimize, amplify, enhance |反义词：minimize, reduce, lessen |动词
