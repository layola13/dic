kofta:/ˈkɒftə/| n.|肉丸，丸子|A kofta is a type of meatball, typically made from ground meat (lamb, beef, chicken, or a mixture) and spices, often formed into a ball or patty shape and then cooked.  They are a popular dish in many Middle Eastern and South Asian cuisines.|Example:  I had a delicious kofta kebab for dinner last night.  昨晚我吃了美味的烤肉丸。  The restaurant is famous for its spicy lamb koftas. 这家餐馆以其辛辣的羊肉丸而闻名。|近义词：meatball, meatball, patty|反义词：N/A
