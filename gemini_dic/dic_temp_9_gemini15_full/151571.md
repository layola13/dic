partaker:/pɑːrˈteɪkər/| n.|参与者，分享者|One who participates or shares in something.|The partakers of the feast enjoyed the delicious food.  宴会参加者们享用了美味的食物。|He was a partaker in the crime. 他参与了这起犯罪活动。|participant, sharer|non-participant, outsider|名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a partaker in the project. 他是这个项目中的参与者。
They are partakers of the bounty. 他们是这笔赏赐的分享者。

一般过去时:
She was a partaker in the celebration. 她是庆祝活动中的参与者。
They were partakers of the inheritance. 他们是遗产的分享者。

现在完成时:
He has been a partaker in many successful ventures. 他已经参与了许多成功的企业。
She has been a partaker of numerous blessings. 她已经分享了无数的祝福。

过去完成时:
He had been a partaker in the discussions before the decision was made. 在做出决定之前，他已经参与了讨论。
They had been partakers of the harvest before the storm hit. 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经分享了丰收的成果。

将来时:
She will be a partaker in the upcoming conference. 她将参加即将举行的会议。
They will be partakers of the profits. 他们将分享利润。

虚拟语气:
If he were a partaker in the scheme, he would be in trouble. 如果他参与了这个计划，他就会有麻烦。
If she had been a partaker in the project, she would have received credit. 如果她参与了这个项目，她就会得到认可。

被动语态:
He was chosen as a partaker in the experiment. 他被选为实验的参与者。
The inheritance was shared by all partakers. 遗产由所有分享者共享。

疑问句:
Are you a partaker in this agreement? 你是这项协议的参与者吗？
Who were the partakers of the reward? 谁是奖赏的分享者？

否定句:
He was not a partaker in the conspiracy. 他没有参与阴谋。
She was not a partaker of the spoils. 她没有分享战利品。

词源:
The word "partaker" comes from the Old English "part-taker," combining "part" (meaning a portion or share) and "taker" (meaning one who takes).  It evolved through Middle English to its modern form.
