nanny:/ˈnæni/| n.|保姆， няня|A nanny is a person employed to care for children in their own home.  They typically provide childcare, supervision, and sometimes education. |She hired a nanny to help care for her twins. 她雇了一个保姆来帮忙照顾她的双胞胎。|babysitter, childminder|—|n.
