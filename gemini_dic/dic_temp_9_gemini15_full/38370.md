concept:/ˈkɒnsɛpt/ /ˈkɑːnsɛpt/| n. |概念，观念，想法 |A concept is a general idea or understanding of something. It's an abstract notion formed by mentally combining all the characteristics of a particular thing.  Concepts are fundamental to understanding and communicating about the world.  |Example: The concept of time is difficult to grasp fully.  时间这个概念很难完全理解。
The concept of justice is central to a fair society. 正义的概念对公平的社会至关重要。|近义词：idea, notion, thought, principle |反义词：reality, fact
