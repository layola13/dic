reinsulated:/ˌriːˈɪnsjuleɪtɪd/| v. |重新隔热，再次隔热 |To insulate something again.  This usually implies that the previous insulation was inadequate or has deteriorated. |The old house was reinsulated to improve its energy efficiency.  老房子进行了重新隔热以提高能源效率。  The roof needed to be reinsulated after the leak.  屋顶漏水后需要重新隔热。|insulate, refit| poorly insulated, bare |动词
