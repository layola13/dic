chlorometry:/klɔːˈrɒmɪtri/| n. |比色法，氯量测定|The quantitative determination of a substance by comparing the intensity of its color with that of a standard solution.  Chlorometry is used in various fields, such as water quality analysis and chemical analysis. |例句：The researchers used chlorometry to measure the chlorine concentration in the water sample. 研究人员使用比色法测量水样中的氯浓度。|例句：Chlorometry is a simple and cost-effective method for determining the concentration of certain substances. 比色法是一种简单且经济有效的测定某些物质浓度的方法。|近义词：colorimetry, spectrophotometry|反义词:None
