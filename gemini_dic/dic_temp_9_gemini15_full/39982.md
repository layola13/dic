consular:/ˈkɒnsjʊlə(r)/|adj.|领事的；领事馆的|Relating to a consul or a consulate.  A consular official is a representative of a country's government, stationed abroad to assist its citizens and promote its interests.|例句：He worked in a consular capacity for many years. 他多年从事领事工作。|例句：She sought consular assistance after her passport was stolen. 护照被盗后，她寻求领事协助。|近义词：diplomatic|反义词：domestic
