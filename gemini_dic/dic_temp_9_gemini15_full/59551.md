eavesdrop:/ˈiːvzdrɒp/| v.|偷听，窃听|To listen secretly to a private conversation.|例句：I accidentally eavesdropped on their conversation. 我不小心偷听了他们的谈话。

例句：He was eavesdropping outside the window. 他在窗外偷听。


近义词：overhear, listen in, spy


反义词：broadcast, announce


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They eavesdrop on their neighbors regularly. = 他们经常偷听邻居的谈话。
I sometimes eavesdrop on my colleagues' conversations. = 我有时会偷听同事们的谈话。


一般过去时:
She eavesdropped on the phone call. = 她偷听了那个电话。
We eavesdropped on their argument last night. = 我们昨晚偷听了他们的争吵。


现在完成时:
They have eavesdropped on us several times. = 他们已经偷听了我们几次了。
I have never eavesdropped on anyone intentionally. = 我从来没有故意偷听过任何人的谈话。


过去完成时:
He had eavesdropped on their conversation before they realized it. = 在他们意识到之前，他已经偷听了他们的谈话。
She had eavesdropped on the meeting before she entered the room. = 她进房间之前就已经偷听了会议。


将来时:
