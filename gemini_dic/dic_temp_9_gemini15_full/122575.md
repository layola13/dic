moderato:/mɒdəˈrɑːtoʊ/| adj.|中速的；适度的|Moderate tempo or speed, neither fast nor slow.  It can also refer to something that is average or within reasonable limits.|例句：The piece should be played at a moderato tempo. 这首曲子应该以中速演奏。

例句：He adopted a moderato approach to the problem. 他采取了一种适度的方法来解决这个问题。

近义词：moderate, temperate, measured|反义词：fast, rapid, slow, excessive
