byway:/ˈbaɪweɪ/| n.|小路，小径，偏僻道路|A byway is a minor road, typically a quiet and less-traveled route, often leading through scenic countryside or less developed areas.  It's often narrower and less well-maintained than a main road.|Example: We took a scenic byway through the mountains.|近义词：lane, path, track, side road|反义词：highway, freeway, motorway
