druggie:/ˈdrʌɡi/| n. |瘾君子，吸毒者|A person who is addicted to drugs.  The term is often used in a derogatory way. |例句：He was a druggie and spent most of his money on heroin. 他是个瘾君子，把大部分钱都花在了海洛因上。|例句：The rehabilitation center helps druggies overcome their addiction. 康复中心帮助瘾君子克服毒瘾。|近义词：addict, junkie, user |反义词:  N/A
