minimises:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪzɪz/ | v. | 最小化；使减到最少 | To reduce something to the smallest possible amount or degree.  | They are trying to minimise the risk of infection. 他们试图将感染的风险降到最低。  | reduce, lessen, decrease | maximise, increase, amplify | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He minimises his expenses. = 他尽量减少开支。
The company minimises its environmental impact. = 公司将对环境的影响降至最低。

一般过去时:
She minimised the damage caused by the storm. = 她将暴风雨造成的损失降到了最低。
They minimised their losses in the stock market crash. = 他们在股市崩盘中将损失降到了最低。

现在完成时:
We have minimised our carbon footprint this year. = 我们今年已经将碳足迹降到了最低。
The government has minimised the spread of the disease. = 政府已经将疾病的传播降到了最低。

过去完成时:
They had minimised their risk before investing. = 他们在投资前已经将风险降到了最低。
She had minimised her involvement in the project. = 她之前已经尽量减少了她对项目的参与。

将来时:
We will minimise the disruption to services. = 我们将把对服务的中断降到最低。
They will minimise their exposure to the sun. = 他们将尽量减少日晒。

虚拟语气:
If we acted quickly, we could minimise the damage. = 如果我们迅速行动，就能将损失降到最低。
I wish I had minimised my debt earlier. = 我希望我早点就将我的债务降到最低。

被动语态:
The risks were minimised by careful planning. = 通过仔细的计划，风险被降到了最低。
The impact on the environment is being minimised by new technologies. = 新技术正在将对环境的影响降到最低。

疑问句:
How can we minimise the cost? = 我们如何才能将成本降到最低？
What steps can be taken to minimise the risks? = 可以采取哪些措施来降低风险？

否定句:
He didn't minimise the importance of the problem. = 他没有低估这个问题的重要性。(这里否定的是“最小化”本身，并非指“最小化到零”)
They failed to minimise the damage. = 他们未能将损失降到最低。
