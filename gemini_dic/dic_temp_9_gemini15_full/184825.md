resuspension:/ˌriːsəˈspɛnʃən/| n. | 再悬浮；再悬挂 | The act or process of suspending something again, especially sediment in a liquid.  This often refers to the re-entry of previously settled particles back into the liquid phase, often due to agitation or other disturbances. | Example: The resuspension of sediment in the river caused the water to become murky.  河流中沉淀物的再悬浮导致水变得浑浊。|近义词：redispersion, re-suspension|反义词：settling, sedimentation
