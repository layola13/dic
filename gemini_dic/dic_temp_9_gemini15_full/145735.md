overcriticize:/ˌoʊvərˈkrɪtɪsaɪz/| vt.|过度批评；吹毛求疵|To criticize someone or something too much or too harshly.|例句：Don't overcriticize his work; he's still learning. 不要过度批评他的作品；他还在学习。|例句：She tends to overcriticize her children, making them feel insecure. 她倾向于过度批评她的孩子，使他们感到没有安全感。|近义词：criticize harshly, faultfind, nitpick|反义词：praise, commend, compliment|动词
