intentions:/ɪnˈtɛnʃənz/ | n. |意图，目的，意向 |The plans or actions that someone intends to do.  It refers to a person's aim or objective. |例句：Her intentions were good, but the results were disastrous.  她的意图是好的，但结果却很糟糕。

例句：He denied any hostile intentions. 他否认有任何敌意。


近义词：purposes, aims, goals, objectives, designs |反义词：accident, chance, coincidence |词性：名词


USAGE->

What are your intentions regarding this project? = 你对于这个项目的意图是什么？

词源:
The word "intentions" comes from the Old French word "intencion," which itself derives from the Latin word "intendere," meaning "to stretch out, to aim, to direct."  The suffix "-tion" indicates the action or process.
