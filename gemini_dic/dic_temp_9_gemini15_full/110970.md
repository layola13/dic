liquid:/ˈlɪkwɪd/ | n. | 液体，流体 | A substance that flows freely but is of constant volume, having a consistency like that of water or oil.  | Example: Water is a liquid at room temperature. 水在室温下是液体。

Example:  The doctor prescribed a liquid medicine for the cough. 医生开了液体药治咳嗽。


近义词：fluid,  solution

反义词：solid, gas
