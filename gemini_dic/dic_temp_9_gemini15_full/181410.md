reiter:/ˈriːtər/| v.|重复，重申|to say or do something again or more than once|The president reiterated his commitment to economic reform. 总统重申了他对经济改革的承诺。|近义词：repeat, restate, reaffirm|反义词：retract, deny


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reiterates his point every meeting. 他每次会议都重复他的观点。
She reiterates the instructions clearly. 她清楚地重复了指示。

一般过去时:
The teacher reiterated the rules to the students. 老师向学生们重申了规则。
He reiterated his apology for the mistake. 他再次为错误道歉。

现在完成时:
She has reiterated her support for the project multiple times. 她多次重申了她对该项目的支持。
They have reiterated their commitment to the agreement. 他们已重申他们对协议的承诺。

过去完成时:
He had reiterated his concerns before the board meeting. 他在董事会会议之前已重申了他的担忧。
She had reiterated the instructions several times, but they still didn't understand. 她已经多次重复了指示，但他们仍然不明白。

将来时:
He will reiterate his position in the upcoming debate. 他将在即将到来的辩论中重申他的立场。
She will reiterate the importance of safety procedures. 她将重申安全程序的重要性。

虚拟语气:
If he reiterated his request, they might reconsider. 如果他再次提出请求，他们可能会重新考虑。
I wish he had reiterated his offer. 我希望他当时重申了他的提议。

被动语态:
The statement was reiterated by the spokesperson. 该声明由发言人重申。
The instructions were reiterated several times to ensure understanding.  为了确保理解，这些指示被重复了好几次。

疑问句:
Will he reiterate his accusations? 他会重申他的指控吗？
Did she reiterate her concerns to the manager? 她是否向经理重申了她的担忧？

否定句:
He did not reiterate his offer. 他没有重申他的提议。
She did not reiterate the instructions. 她没有重复指示。
