half-indignantly: /ˌhɑːfɪnˈdɪɡnəntli/ | adv. | 半是愤愤不平地；带着几分愤慨地 |  Expressing indignation only partially or mildly; somewhat indignantly.  It suggests a restrained or hesitant expression of anger or annoyance. |例句：She responded half-indignantly, "Well, I didn't expect that!" 她半是愤愤不平地回应道：“好吧，我没想到会这样！”


He mumbled half-indignantly about the unfairness of it all. 他带着几分愤慨咕哝着这一切的不公平。


近义词：resentfully, huffily, petulantly, crossly


反义词：calmly, placidly, serenely


词性：副词
