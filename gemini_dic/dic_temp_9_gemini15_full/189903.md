sampled:/ˈsæmpld/ | adj. v. | 样品的；抽样的；采样的；(对声音或图像)取样 |  selected and tested as a representative example of a whole; to take a sample of (something) | 例句：The sampled data showed a significant trend.  抽样数据显示出显著的趋势。

The scientist sampled the water for pollutants. 科学家采集水样以检测污染物。|近义词：tested, examined, chosen, selected | 反义词：whole, entire, complete |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We sample the products regularly. 我们定期抽检产品。
They sample the soil for nutrients. 他们采集土壤样本以检测养分。

一般过去时:
The researchers sampled the population. 研究人员对人口进行了抽样调查。
She sampled the wine before ordering a bottle. 她先品尝了葡萄酒，然后再点一瓶。

现在完成时:
I have sampled several wines from this vineyard. 我已经品尝了这家葡萄园的几种葡萄酒。
They have sampled the air quality in the city. 他们已经对该城市的空气质量进行了采样。

过去完成时:
They had sampled the water before the rain started. 下雨前他们已经采集了水样。
She had sampled the cake before offering it to her guests.  她在给客人提供蛋糕之前已经尝过了。

将来时:
We will sample the new software next week. 我们下周将测试新软件。
The team will sample the blood for analysis.  团队将采集血液样本进行分析。

虚拟语气:
If we sampled more data, we would get better results. 如果我们采集更多数据，我们会得到更好的结果。
I wish I had sampled the food before I ordered it. 我真希望我在点菜之前尝过食物。

被动语态:
The water was sampled for contamination.  水样被采集来检测污染。
The products are sampled regularly for quality control. 产品定期进行抽样检查以进行质量控制。

疑问句:
Have they sampled the air quality yet? 他们已经采集空气样本了吗？
Will the data be sampled randomly? 数据将随机抽样吗？

否定句:
We did not sample enough data for the study. 我们没有为这项研究采集足够的数据。
They haven't sampled the new product yet.  他们还没有试用新产品。
