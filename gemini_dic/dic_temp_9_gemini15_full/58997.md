duumvirate:/ˈduːʌmvaɪreɪt/| n. |二人执政官；二人统治；二人小组 |A duumvirate is a government or body consisting of two people with equal authority.  The term often refers to a Roman magistracy. |Example: The city was governed by a duumvirate for many years.  这个城市多年来由二人执政官统治。  The committee formed a duumvirate to oversee the project. 委员会组成了一个二人小组来监督项目。|近义词：dyarchy, pair, duo |反义词：monarchy, dictatorship
