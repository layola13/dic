pro-marriage: /ˌproʊˈmærɪdʒ/ | adj. | 支持婚姻的；拥护婚姻的 |  Supporting or advocating for marriage, especially traditional marriage.  |例句：He is a pro-marriage activist, actively campaigning for stronger legal protections for married couples. 他是一位支持婚姻的活动家，积极为已婚夫妇争取更强的法律保护。

例句：The pro-marriage stance of the political party is well-known. 这个政党的支持婚姻立场众所周知。


近义词：marriage-affirming, pro-family, traditionalist (in the context of marriage) | 反义词：anti-marriage, pro-choice (regarding marriage)


词性:形容词
