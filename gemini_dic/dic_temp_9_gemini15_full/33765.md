circulation:/ˌsɜːrkjuˈleɪʃn/ | /ˌsɝːkjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. |循环；流通；发行量；传播 | The movement of something, such as blood, air, or water, in a circular course.  The distribution of something, such as newspapers or information. | 例句：The circulation of blood is essential for life. = 血液循环对生命至关重要。

The newspaper boasts a circulation of over a million. = 这家报纸的发行量超过一百万。


近义词：distribution, dissemination, flow, spread | 反义词：stagnation, standstill


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The blood circulates throughout the body. = 血液在全身循环。
The magazine has a wide circulation. = 这本杂志发行量很大。

一般过去时:
The rumour circulated quickly throughout the town. = 谣言迅速传遍了整个城镇。
The newspaper's circulation decreased last year. = 这家报纸去年的发行量下降了。

现在完成时:
The news has circulated widely online. = 这个消息在网上广泛传播。
The company has improved its product circulation. = 公司已经改善了其产品的流通。

过去完成时:
The story had circulated before the meeting. = 故事在会议之前就已经传开了。
The book had a large circulation before the new edition. = 这本书在新版发行之前就有很大的发行量。

将来时:
This information will circulate among the employees. = 此信息将在员工之间传播。
We expect the new product's circulation to increase next year. = 我们预计新产品的流通量明年将增加。

虚拟语气:
If the blood didn't circulate, we would die. = 如果血液不循环，我们就会死。
If the information had circulated sooner, we could have acted faster. = 如果信息传播得早一些，我们就能更快地采取行动。

被动语态:
The leaflet is being circulated throughout the neighborhood. = 传单正在整个街区散发。
The book was circulated widely in the academic community. = 这本书在学术界广泛流传。

疑问句:
What is the circulation of this magazine? = 这本杂志的发行量是多少？
How is the air circulation in this room? = 这个房间的空气流通怎么样？

否定句:
The rumour did not circulate widely. = 谣言并没有广泛传播。
The blood isn't circulating properly in his leg. = 他的腿部血液循环不畅。
