/ˌpiːnˈɒlədʒi/ | n. | 监狱学；刑罚学 | The study of punishment of crime and the management of prisons.  It encompasses various aspects, including the history, philosophy, and practice of incarceration, rehabilitation, and other correctional measures. |  Penology examines the effectiveness of different punishment methods in reducing crime and recidivism. 监狱学研究不同惩罚方法在减少犯罪和累犯方面的有效性。| 近义词：corrections, criminal justice | 反义词：无  | 名词
