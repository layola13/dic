nonindulgent: /ˌnɒnɪnˈdʌldʒənt/ | adj. | 不放纵的；不纵容的；克制的 | Not giving way to one's own desires or whims; self-denying; austere.  It suggests a restraint or denial of pleasure, often for a higher purpose or principle. |  例句：He adopted a nonindulgent lifestyle, abstaining from alcohol and rich foods. 他过着克己的生活，戒酒戒油腻食物。

例句：Her nonindulgent parenting style ensured her children learned responsibility early. 她不纵容孩子的教养方式确保她的孩子们很早就学会了责任感。


近义词：abstemious, austere, self-denying, temperate, restrained, disciplined


反义词：indulgent, permissive, self-indulgent, lax


词性: adjective
