outbreathes:/ˌaʊtˈbriːðz/| vt.|呼出；吐出|To exhale; to breathe out.|例句：The runner outbreathed heavily after the race.  赛跑者赛后大口喘气。|例句：She outbreathed a sigh of relief. 她舒了一口气。|近义词：exhales, breathes out|反义词：inhales, breathes in
