pinon:/pɪˈnjɒn/| n. |松子；二叶松；齿轮；销子|A pinon is the seed of a pine tree, especially the pinyon pine.  It can also refer to the pinyon pine tree itself, or a small gear or a pin used in machinery.|例句：We roasted pinon nuts over the campfire. 我们在篝火上烤松子。

例句：The pinyon trees dotted the landscape.  二叶松点缀着这片风景。


近义词：pine nut (松子), pinyon pine (二叶松)
反义词：无


词源:  The word "pinon" comes from the Spanish word "piñón," ultimately derived from the Latin word "pinus," meaning "pine tree."
