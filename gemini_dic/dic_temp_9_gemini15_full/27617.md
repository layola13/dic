cancels:/ˈkænsəlz/| vt.|取消，作废|To end or stop something that has been planned or arranged.|The concert was canceled due to bad weather.  这场音乐会因恶劣天气而取消。|He canceled his trip to Europe. 他取消了他的欧洲之旅。|postpone, annul, void|continue, proceed|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cancels his appointments frequently. 他经常取消约会。
She cancels her subscription every year. 她每年都取消订阅。


一般过去时:
They canceled the meeting last minute. 他们最后一刻取消了会议。
I canceled my order online. 我在网上取消了我的订单。


现在完成时:
He has canceled his flight three times. 他已经取消了三次航班。
She has canceled her plans for the weekend. 她已经取消了周末的计划。


过去完成时:
They had canceled the show before we arrived.在我们到达之前，他们已经取消了演出。
We had canceled our reservation earlier that day. 我们当天早些时候已经取消了预订。


将来时:
They will cancel the game if it rains. 如果下雨，他们将取消比赛。
She will cancel her order if the product is defective.如果产品有缺陷，她将取消订单。


虚拟语气:
If I had enough money, I would cancel my debt. 如果我有足够的钱，我会偿还我的债务。
I wish I could cancel the meeting, but it's too late. 我真希望我能取消会议，但是太晚了。


被动语态:
The meeting was canceled due to unforeseen circumstances. 会议由于不可预见的情况而被取消。
The flight was canceled because of a mechanical problem. 航班由于机械故障而被取消。


疑问句:
Did you cancel the reservation? 你取消预订了吗？
Has she canceled the order yet? 她已经取消订单了吗？


否定句:
He did not cancel his trip. 他没有取消他的旅行。
She will not cancel the contract. 她不会取消合同。


PHRASE->
