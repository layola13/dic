eyecup:/ˈaɪkʌp/ | n. |眼杯；眼罩| A small cup-shaped device used to hold eye drops or other ophthalmic solutions close to the eye during application.  It is often used to help administer medication more effectively and to prevent accidental contamination. | Example:  The nurse used an eyecup to apply the eye drops to the patient's eye. 护士用眼杯将眼药水滴入病人的眼睛。|近义词：eye bath, eye wash cup|反义词：无
