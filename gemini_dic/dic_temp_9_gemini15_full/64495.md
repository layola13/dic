eroded:/ɪˈroʊdəd/| v. |侵蚀，腐蚀；逐渐削弱|To gradually destroy or wear away (something) by natural processes (such as water, wind, or ice) or by a chemical process.  It can also refer to the gradual weakening or undermining of something abstract, like power or confidence.|例句：The coastline was eroded by the relentless waves.  海岸线被无情的海浪侵蚀了。

The constant criticism eroded her confidence.  不断的批评削弱了她的信心。|近义词：corrode, wear away, undermine, weaken|反义词：build up, strengthen, fortify
