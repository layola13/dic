foreclosure:/ˌfɔːrˈkloʊʒər/ | n. | 赎回权丧失；取消抵押品赎回权；法拍 | The legal process by which a lender takes possession of a property because the borrower has failed to make loan payments.  This process typically involves a court proceeding and the eventual sale of the property to recover the debt. | The bank initiated foreclosure proceedings after the homeowner missed several mortgage payments.  银行在房主拖欠了几次抵押贷款后启动了止赎程序。| repossession, seizure | redemption, retention |  n.
