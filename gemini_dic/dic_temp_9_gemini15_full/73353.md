foregainst: /fɔːrˈeɪnst/ | adv. | 反对；不利于 |  The word "foregainst" is an archaic and now largely obsolete adverb meaning "against" or "in opposition to." It combines the Old English prefix "for-" (meaning "against" or "opposite") with "gainst," an older form of "against."  It's rarely used in modern English. | Example:  He argued foregainst the proposed law. 他反对这项拟议的法案。 | 近义词：against, opposed to | 反义词：for, in favor of | 词性: 副词
