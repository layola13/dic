/dɪˈteɪndər/ | n. | 拘留；扣押；扣留 | The legal detention or confinement of a person.  It often implies a temporary holding, pending further investigation or legal proceedings.  The term is often used in the context of law enforcement or immigration. | Example: The police placed him under detention while they investigated the crime.  警方将他拘留，以便调查犯罪。

The suspect was held in detention for questioning.  嫌疑犯被拘留接受问话。

| 近义词：custody, confinement, arrest, imprisonment | 反义词：release, liberation |  名词
