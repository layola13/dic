iconograph:/aɪˈkɒnəɡræf/| n. |肖像画；图像；图解；图示|A visual representation of an idea or concept, often using symbolic imagery.  It can be a picture, diagram, or chart used to explain or illustrate something. |Example: The iconograph of the saint depicted him with a halo and a staff.  这位圣人的肖像画描绘了他带着光环和权杖。|近义词：diagram, illustration, image, symbol|反义词：description (purely textual)
