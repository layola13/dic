clip-on: /ˈklɪpɑːn/ | adj. | 夹式；可夹上的 |  Describes something that attaches or is fastened using a clip.  It's commonly used to describe jewelry, eyeglasses, or other accessories that attach without needing additional hardware like screws or glue. | 例句：She wore clip-on earrings to the party. 她参加派对时戴着夹式耳环。

The new clip-on tie is very convenient. 这款新的夹式领带非常方便。


近义词：attachable, clasp-on | 反义词：permanently attached, fixed


词性: adjective
