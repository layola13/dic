reoccurs:/ˌriːəˈkɜːrz/| v.|再次发生；反复出现|To happen or occur again.  The word is formed from the prefix "re-" (again) and the verb "occurs." It implies a repetition of an event or phenomenon.|The problem reoccurs every spring.  这个问题每年春天都会再次出现。|The pain reoccurs periodically. 疼痛会周期性地复发。|recurs, happens again, repeats|ceases, ends, stops|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem reoccurs frequently. = 这个问题经常复发。
This pattern reoccurs throughout the book. = 这种模式贯穿全书。

一般过去时:
The symptom reoccurred after a week. =  一周后症状又出现了。
The error reoccurred despite our efforts. =  尽管我们努力了，错误还是再次发生。

现在完成时:
The pain has reoccurred several times this month. =  这个月疼痛已经复发了数次。
The issue has reoccurred since the last update. = 自上次更新以来，这个问题再次出现。

过去完成时:
The headache had reoccurred before she took the medicine. =  她吃药之前头痛又犯了。
The malfunction had reoccurred several times that year. = 那一年故障已经发生过好几次了。

将来时:
The symptoms will likely reoccur if the infection isn't treated properly. = 如果感染没有得到适当治疗，症状很可能会复发。
The issue will reoccur unless we address the root cause. = 除非我们解决根本原因，否则这个问题还会再次出现。
