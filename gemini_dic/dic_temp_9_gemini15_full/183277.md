reprinting:/ˌriːˈprɪntɪŋ/ | n. | 重印；再版 | The act of printing something again.  It often refers to the process of reproducing a previously printed work, such as a book or article.  |例句：The high demand led to a second reprinting of the novel.  高度的需求导致小说进行了第二次重印。|例句：The museum is planning a reprinting of its popular guidebook. 博物馆计划重印其广受欢迎的指南。|近义词：reissue, reproduction |反义词：first edition, original printing |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The publisher is considering a reprinting of the bestseller. = 出版商正在考虑重印这本畅销书。
Reprinting the book is costly but necessary. = 重印这本书成本很高，但很有必要。


一般过去时:
They reprinted the magazine after the error was discovered. = 他们发现错误后重印了杂志。
The company reprinted the brochures with the corrected information. = 公司用更正后的信息重印了宣传册。


现在完成时:
They have reprinted the book three times this year. = 今年他们已经重印了这本书三次。
The museum has reprinted its popular guidebook due to high demand. = 由于需求量大，博物馆已经重印了其广受欢迎的指南。


过去完成时:
They had reprinted the article before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经重印了这篇文章。
The company had reprinted all the faulty leaflets before they were distributed. = 公司在所有有缺陷的小册子分发之前已经重印了它们。


将来时:
We will reprint the flyer next week. = 我们下周将重印传单。
The author hopes the publisher will reprint her book next year. = 作者希望出版商明年能重印她的书。


虚拟语气:
If the demand were high enough, they would consider reprinting the book. = 如果需求足够高，他们会考虑重印这本书。
I wish they would consider reprinting the out-of-print novel. = 我希望他们能考虑重印这本绝版的书。


被动语态:
The book was reprinted several times. = 这本书重印了好几次。
The brochures are being reprinted right now. = 宣传册正在被重印。


疑问句:
Will the book be reprinted? = 这本书会重印吗？
Is a reprinting of the magazine planned? = 计划重印杂志吗？


否定句:
They did not reprint the book due to low sales. = 由于销量低，他们没有重印这本书。
The publisher won't reprint the book unless sales increase significantly. = 除非销量大幅增加，否则出版商不会重印这本书。
