preexclusion: /ˌpriːɪkˈskluʒən/ | n. | 预先排除；预先除外 | The act of excluding something beforehand; the state of being excluded beforehand.  It often refers to a process where potential candidates or options are eliminated before a more thorough evaluation. |  The preexclusion of unqualified applicants saved the company significant time and resources.  预先排除不合格的申请人节省了公司大量的时间和资源。|  prior exclusion, preliminary screening | inclusion |  n.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company performs preexclusion of unsuitable candidates. = 公司对不合适的候选人进行预先排除。
Preexclusion saves time and resources. = 预先排除节省时间和资源。


一般过去时:
They preexcluded several options before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前预先排除了几个选项。
