charitably:/ˈtʃærɪtəbli/| adv.| 宽厚地；慈善地；慷慨地 | In a charitable manner; generously; kindly.  |例句：He charitably interpreted her silence as shyness. 他宽厚地把她的沉默解释为害羞。

例句：She charitably donated a large sum of money to the orphanage. 她慷慨地向孤儿院捐赠了一大笔钱。


近义词：kindly, generously, compassionately, benignly


反义词：cruelly, unkindly, harshly, meanly


词性:副词
