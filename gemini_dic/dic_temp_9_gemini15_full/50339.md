deploying:/dɪˈplɔɪɪŋ/ | v. | 部署；展开；调动 | The act of putting something into action or use, especially troops or equipment.  It often implies a strategic or planned distribution of resources. | 例句：The army is deploying troops to the border.  军队正在向边境部署部队。

例句：The company is deploying a new marketing strategy. 公司正在部署一项新的营销策略。


近义词：implementing,  unfurling,  distributing,  releasing

反义词：withdrawing,  retreating,  recalling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are deploying new software. = 他们正在部署新的软件。
We deploy resources strategically. = 我们战略性地部署资源。

一般过去时:
The general deployed his troops skillfully. = 将军巧妙地部署了他的部队。
They deployed a new marketing campaign last month. = 他们上个月启动了一场新的营销活动。

现在完成时:
We have deployed all available resources. = 我们已经部署了所有可用的资源。
They have deployed the new system across all branches. = 他们已经在所有分支机构部署了新系统。

过去完成时:
The army had deployed its forces before the attack began. = 军队在攻击开始之前就已经部署了兵力。
Before the launch, they had already deployed the marketing materials. = 在发布之前，他们已经部署了营销材料。

将来时:
We will deploy additional staff next week. = 我们下周将部署更多员工。
The company will deploy its new product globally. = 公司将把其新产品推向全球市场。

虚拟语气:
If we deployed more resources, we would achieve better results. = 如果我们部署更多资源，我们将取得更好的结果。
It would be unwise to deploy the troops without proper planning. = 在没有适当计划的情况下部署军队是不明智的。

被动语态:
New software is being deployed across the company. = 新软件正在公司内部部署。
The troops were deployed to the disaster zone. = 部队被部署到灾区。

疑问句:
When will the new system be deployed? = 新系统什么时候部署？
How many troops are being deployed to the region? = 正在向该地区部署多少部队？

否定句:
They did not deploy the resources effectively. = 他们没有有效地部署资源。
The plan to deploy the new technology was abandoned. = 部署新技术的计划被放弃了。
