preen:/priːn/| vt. n.|整理羽毛；打扮；自鸣得意 |To smooth or clean one's feathers with the beak; to make oneself neat and tidy in appearance; to pride oneself; to take pride in one's appearance or achievements.|例句：The peacock preened its feathers.  孔雀整理着它的羽毛。|例句：She spent hours preening before the party.  她在聚会前花了数小时打扮自己。|近义词：groom, primp, strut|反义词：neglect, disregard|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird preens its feathers regularly. = 这只鸟经常整理羽毛。
He preens himself in front of the mirror. = 他在镜子前自我陶醉。

一般过去时:
The cat preened itself after its bath. = 猫洗完澡后自己梳理毛发。
She preened herself on her success. = 她为自己的成功而沾沾自喜。

现在完成时:
He has preened himself all morning. = 他整个上午都在自我陶醉。
The bird has preened its feathers thoroughly. = 这只鸟已经彻底梳理了羽毛。

过去完成时:
