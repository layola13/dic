hobbles:/ˈhɑːbəl/| /ˈhɒbəl/| vt.& vi.|蹒跚而行，跛行；阻碍；使…困难|To walk with difficulty, usually because of an injury to a leg or foot; to impede or restrict the movement or progress of something.|例句：The old woman hobbled along the path. 老妇人沿着小路蹒跚而行。|例句：The new regulations hobbled the company's growth. 新规章阻碍了公司的发展。|近义词：limp, limp along, shuffle|反义词：stride, run, gallop|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hobbles down the street every morning. 他每天早上都在街上蹒跚而行。
The new rules hobble our progress. 新规章阻碍了我们的进步。

一般过去时:
She hobbled home after twisting her ankle. 她扭伤脚踝后一瘸一拐地回家了。
The heavy taxes hobbled the small business.  沉重的税收阻碍了这家小企业的发展。

现在完成时:
He has hobbled around on crutches since the accident. 事故发生后，他一直拄着拐杖蹒跚而行。
The regulations have hobbled economic growth for years.  这些规章已经多年来阻碍了经济增长。

过去完成时:
He had hobbled for miles before he found help. 他走了好几英里才找到帮助。
The company had been hobbled by debt before the merger.  合并之前，这家公司一直背负着债务的重压。

将来时:
She will hobble if she doesn't rest her injured leg. 如果她不休息受伤的腿，她将会一瘸一拐地走。
The new tariffs will hobble international trade. 新关税将阻碍国际贸易。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't injured my foot, I wouldn't hobble. 如果我没有伤到脚，我就不会一瘸一拐的了。
If the regulations hadn't been implemented, the company wouldn't have been hobbled. 如果这些规章没有实施，公司就不会受到阻碍。

被动语态:
His progress was hobbled by a lack of funding. 他的进步由于缺乏资金而受阻。
The economy is being hobbled by high inflation. 经济正被高通货膨胀所拖累。

疑问句:
Does he hobble because of his knee injury? 他是因为膝盖受伤才一瘸一拐的吗？
Will the new policy hobble the industry's growth? 新政策会阻碍该行业的发展吗？

否定句:
He doesn't hobble anymore after the surgery. 手术后，他不再一瘸一拐的了。
The project wasn't hobbled by the budget cuts.  该项目没有受到预算削减的阻碍。
