catapulted:/ˈkætəpʌltɪd/| v. |弹射；突然推进；使骤然跃升| To propel or launch (something) with or as if with a catapult.  It can also mean to suddenly propel someone or something into a new situation or condition.|例句：The rocket was catapulted into space. 火箭被弹射到太空。

The scandal catapulted him to fame.  丑闻使他突然成名。|近义词：launched, propelled, hurled, flung |反义词： gently lowered, slowly moved
