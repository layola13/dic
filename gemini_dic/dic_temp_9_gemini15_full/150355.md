paninis:/pəˈniːniːz/| n. |帕尼尼（意大利烤三明治）|A panini is a grilled sandwich, typically made with Italian bread and filled with various ingredients such as meats, cheeses, and vegetables.  It's often pressed in a panini press to create a warm, crispy exterior and a melty interior.| Example: I ordered a panini with prosciutto, mozzarella, and pesto. 我点了一个普罗修特火腿、莫扎里拉奶酪和罗勒酱的帕尼尼。  The cafe serves a variety of delicious paninis for lunch. 这家咖啡馆供应各种美味的帕尼尼作为午餐。|近义词：grilled sandwich, toasted sandwich |反义词：salad (as an alternative)
