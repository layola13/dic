dvd-rom:/ˌdiːviːˈdiː ˈrɒm/| n. |数字视频光盘只读存储器 | A DVD-ROM is a type of optical disc that stores digital data, including video, audio, and computer programs.  It is read-only, meaning that data cannot be written to it. | Example: I watched the movie from the DVD-ROM. 我从DVD光盘上观看了这部电影。|近义词：optical disc, CD-ROM |反义词：DVD-R, DVD-RW


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The DVD-ROM player reads the disc. = DVD-ROM播放器读取光盘。
He uses a DVD-ROM drive to play movies. = 他用DVD-ROM驱动器播放电影。


一般过去时:
