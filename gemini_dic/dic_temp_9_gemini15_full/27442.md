camomiles:/ˈkæməmaɪlz/| n.|洋甘菊（复数）|The plural form of "camomile," referring to multiple plants of the daisy-like genus *Matricaria* and related genera, known for their medicinal and aromatic properties.|例句：The camomiles in the garden were in full bloom, their delicate white petals catching the sunlight. 花园里的洋甘菊盛开了，它们娇嫩的白花瓣沐浴在阳光下。|近义词：chamomile (singular)|反义词:无
