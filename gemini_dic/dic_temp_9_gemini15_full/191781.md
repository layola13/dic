sciopticon:/ˈsaɪ.ɒp.tɪ.kɒn/| n. |幻视仪；全景视界；透视镜|A sciopticon is a type of optical device that projects images onto a screen. It's essentially a simple type of slide projector, often used for educational purposes in the past.  It uses a lens and a light source to magnify and project images from translucent slides or other objects. |The teacher used a sciopticon to show the class images of faraway lands. 老师用幻视仪给同学们展示远方土地的图像。| projector, lantern |(no direct antonym)| 名词
