folder:/ˈfoʊldər/| n. |文件夹，文件包|A folder is a container, typically made of paper or cardboard, used to hold loose papers or documents together.  It can also refer to a digital container for files on a computer. |Example: I put all the important documents in a folder.我把所有重要的文件都放在一个文件夹里了。|近义词：file, portfolio, binder |反义词：N/A
