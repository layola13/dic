downto: /ˈdaʊntuː/ | prep. | 直到；一直到；直到…为止 |  Down to indicates a limit or endpoint, especially in terms of time, quantity, or extent.  It suggests a progression or reduction reaching a specific point. |

例句：

The shop is open down to 6 pm. = 商店营业到下午6点。

We counted down to the launch of the rocket. = 我们倒计时到火箭发射。


近义词：until, till, as far as, through

反义词：from, since (in the context of time)

词性：介词
