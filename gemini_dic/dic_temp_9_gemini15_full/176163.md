re:/riː/| pref.|再，重新，向后|A prefix meaning "again," "back," "against," or "away." It is used to form many words.|例句：rewrite (重写), reread (重读),  refund (退款)|近义词：again, anew, back|反义词：forward, ahead


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He rereads the book every year. = 他每年都重读这本书。
They rewrite their reports regularly. = 他们定期重写他们的报告。


一般过去时:
She rewrote the essay after the professor's feedback. = 教授的反馈后，她重写了论文。
He reread the email to make sure he understood. = 他重新阅读了邮件以确保他理解了。


现在完成时:
I have rewritten the entire chapter. = 我已经重写了整章。
She has reread that novel three times. = 她已经重读了那本小说三次。


过去完成时:
He had reread the contract before signing it. = 他在签署合同之前已经重读了合同。
They had rewritten the script several times before the final performance. = 在最终演出之前，他们已经重写了剧本好几次。


将来时:
I will rewrite my application. = 我将重写我的申请。
She will reread the instructions carefully. = 她将仔细地重读说明。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would rewrite the whole thing. = 如果我有时间，我会重写所有内容。
I wish I had reread the instructions more carefully. = 我希望我更仔细地重读了说明。


被动语态:
The document was rewritten by the team. = 该文件由团队重写。
The novel will be reread by many people. = 这本小说将被很多人重读。


疑问句:
Will you rewrite the letter? = 你会重写这封信吗？
Have you reread the instructions? = 你重读了说明吗？


否定句:
I did not rewrite my essay. = 我没有重写我的论文。
She will not reread that book. = 她不会再读那本书。


PHRASE->

re-evaluate = 重新评估
re-examine = 重新检查
re-organize = 重新组织
re-consider = 重新考虑
re-adjust = 重新调整
