portaged:/pɔːrˈteɪdʒd/| v.|搬运（货物）；背负（货物）通过陆地|To carry (goods) over land, especially by a difficult route, often by porters.|The explorers portaged their supplies across the mountain pass. 探险家们把他们的物资搬运过山口。|近义词：carry, transport, convey|反义词：leave, abandon


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They portage their canoes around the rapids. = 他们在激流处搬运独木舟。
He portaged the heavy pack across the swamp. = 他把沉重的包裹搬过沼泽地。

一般过去时:
We portaged our equipment over the steep trail. = 我们把装备搬运过陡峭的小路。
The pioneers portaged their belongings across the lake. = 拓荒者们把他们的财物搬运过湖泊。

现在完成时:
They have portaged this route many times. = 他们已经多次搬运过这条路线。
She has portaged her entire life's possessions. = 她已经搬运了她一生所有的财产。

过去完成时:
They had portaged the supplies before nightfall. = 他们在夜幕降临前已经搬运了物资。
We had portaged the canoe around the waterfall. = 我们已经把独木舟搬运过了瀑布。

将来时:
We will portage the kayaks around the falls tomorrow. = 我们明天将把皮划艇搬运过瀑布。
They will portage the heavy equipment to the summit. = 他们将把沉重的设备搬运到山顶。

虚拟语气:
If they had portaged the supplies earlier, they would have reached the camp sooner. = 如果他们早点搬运物资，他们就会早点到达营地。
I wish I had portaged the canoe myself. = 我真希望我自己搬运了独木舟。

被动语态:
The supplies were portaged across the river by the team. = 物资被团队搬运过了河。
The canoe was portaged around the obstacle. = 独木舟被搬运过了障碍物。

疑问句:
Was the equipment portaged safely? = 设备安全地搬运了吗？
Have they ever portaged such a heavy load? = 他们以前搬运过这么重的货物吗？

否定句:
They did not portage the goods across the difficult terrain. = 他们没有把货物搬运过那片崎岖的地形。
We couldn't portage the canoe because of the storm. = 因为暴风雨，我们没能搬运独木舟。
