enthymeme:/ˈɛnθɪmiːm/| n. |论证，省略三段论|A shortened syllogism, where one premise is implied and not explicitly stated.  It's a form of argument that relies on the audience's understanding to fill in the missing part.  |Example:  "He is a citizen, therefore he is entitled to vote." (The missing premise is: "All citizens are entitled to vote.")|近义词：syllogism, argument, inference|反义词：N/A
