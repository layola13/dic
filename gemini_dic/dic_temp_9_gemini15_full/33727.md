circlet:/ˈsɜːklɪt/| n. |小环，头环，小圈 |A circlet is a small circle or ring, often of precious metal and worn as an ornament, especially on the head. It can also refer to a ring-shaped arrangement of something.|例句：She wore a circlet of flowers in her hair. 她头上戴着花环。

例句：The artist created a circlet of light in the painting.艺术家在画中创造了一个光环。


近义词：coronet, diadem, wreath |反义词：None |词性:名词

词源:
The word "circlet" comes from the Middle English "circulet," a diminutive of "circle."  The suffix "-let" indicates smallness.  Ultimately, it derives from the Latin "circulus," a diminutive of "circus," meaning "ring" or "circle."
