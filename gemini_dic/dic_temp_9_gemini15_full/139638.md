occlusions:/əˈkluːʒənz/ | n. | 阻塞；闭塞；遮挡 | The blockage or closing of something, such as a blood vessel or airway, or the obstruction of view.  It often refers to the blocking of something from view, either partially or completely. | Example: The heavy fog caused significant occlusions in the mountain pass.  浓雾导致山隘出现严重的遮挡。

The surgeon successfully repaired the arterial occlusion. 外科医生成功修复了动脉阻塞。|近义词：blockages, obstructions, blockades |反义词：openings, clearances
