overheating:/ˌoʊvərˈhiːtɪŋ/| v. n. adj.|过热，温度过高 |The process of becoming excessively hot;  excessive heat.|例句：The engine is overheating.  发动机过热了。|近义词：superheating, hyperthermia|反义词：undercooling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine overheats when it's overworked. = 发动机超负荷运转时会过热。
My laptop overheats if I run too many programs at once. = 如果我同时运行太多程序，我的笔记本电脑就会过热。


一般过去时:
The car overheated and broke down on the highway. = 汽车过热并在高速公路上抛锚了。
The CPU overheated during the intense gaming session. = 在激烈的游戏过程中，CPU 过热了。


现在完成时:
The system has overheated several times this week. = 本周系统已经过热好几次了。
My phone has overheated because I was using it while charging. = 我的手机因为我在充电时使用它而过热了。


过去完成时:
The reactor had overheated before the safety system activated. = 安全系统启动之前，反应堆已经过热了。

将来时:

The server will overheat if the cooling fans fail. = 如果冷却风扇故障，服务器将过热。


虚拟语气:
If the engine had not overheated, we could have reached our destination on time. = 如果发动机没有过热，我们就能按时到达目的地。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
The engine didn't overheat during the test. = 发动机在测试期间没有过热。
The room wasn't overheated because the air conditioner was working properly. = 房间没有过热，因为空调工作正常。
