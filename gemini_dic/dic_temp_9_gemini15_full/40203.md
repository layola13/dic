contestant:/kənˈtɛstənt/| n. |参赛者，竞争者 |A person who takes part in a contest or competition.  |Example:  She was a contestant in the baking competition. 她是一名烘焙比赛的参赛者。|近义词：competitor, participant |反义词：judge, spectator
