overplotting:/ˌoʊvərˈplɑːtɪŋ/ | v. | 过度绘图，数据重叠绘图 | The act of plotting too many data points on a single graph, resulting in an unclear and difficult-to-interpret visualization.  It obscures underlying patterns and trends. | Example: Overplotting the data points made it impossible to discern any significant trends.  过度绘制数据点使得无法辨别任何显著趋势。 |近义词:data overcrowding,  point congestion | 反义词:data thinning, data reduction | 动词
