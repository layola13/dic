reflet:/ʁəflɛ/| n.|反射；倒影；反映|The word "reflet" is a French noun meaning "reflection," in the sense of a mirrored image or a figurative reflection of something.  It can refer to a literal reflection in water or a mirror, or a metaphorical reflection of an idea, feeling, or event. |例句：Je vois mon reflet dans l'eau. (I see my reflection in the water.)
