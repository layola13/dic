minutemen:/ˈmɪnɪtmən/| n.|民兵|A member of a group of civilian soldiers in colonial America who were ready to fight at a minute's notice.  They played a crucial role in the early battles of the American Revolutionary War.|例句：The minutemen were crucial in the early battles of the American Revolution.  民兵在美国独立战争的早期战役中至关重要。|近义词：militia, volunteer soldier|反义词：regular soldier, professional soldier
