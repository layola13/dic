mew:/mjuː/| n. v.| 猫叫声；（尤指小猫）的叫声 |The sound a cat makes, especially a kitten.  |例句：I heard a soft mew from the basket. 我听到篮子里传来一声轻柔的猫叫声。
The kitten mewed pitifully. 小猫可怜地叫着。|近义词：miaow, purr (purring is a different kind of cat sound)|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat mews loudly at night. = 猫晚上大声叫。
The kittens mew constantly for food. = 小猫不停地叫着要食物。


一般过去时:
The cat mewed at the door. = 猫在门口叫。
The kitten mewed softly before falling asleep. = 小猫睡着前轻轻地叫了一声。


现在完成时:
The cat has mewed all night. = 猫叫了一整夜。
The kittens have mewed incessantly since this morning. = 小猫从早上开始就一直在不停地叫。


过去完成时:
The cat had mewed before I opened the door. = 我开门之前猫已经叫过了。
The kittens had mewed until they were tired. = 小猫叫到累了为止。


将来时:
The cat will mew when it is hungry. = 猫饿了就会叫。
The kittens will mew for their mother. = 小猫会叫着找妈妈。


虚拟语气:
If the cat mewed, I would know it was hungry. = 如果猫叫了，我就知道它饿了。
I wish the kitten would stop mewing. = 我希望小猫能停止叫唤。


被动语态:
(Mew is rarely used in the passive voice because it's an intransitive verb)  (因为mew是不及物动词，所以很少用被动语态)


疑问句:
Does the cat mew often? = 猫经常叫吗？
Why is the kitten mewing? = 小猫为什么叫？


否定句:
The cat didn't mew last night. = 猫昨晚没叫。
The kitten doesn't mew very loudly. = 小猫叫的声音不大。


PHRASE->
a pitiful mew = 一声可怜的猫叫
a soft mew = 一声轻柔的猫叫
a loud mew = 一声响亮的猫叫
a continuous mew = 持续的猫叫
a faint mew = 一声微弱的猫叫
