porpoise:/ˈpɔːrpəs/|/ˈpɔːrpəs/| n. |海豚；鼠海豚|A porpoise is a small toothed whale, similar to a dolphin but with a shorter, more rounded snout.  They are known for their intelligence and playful behavior. |Example: We saw a pod of porpoises leaping and playing in the ocean. 我们看到一群海豚在海里跳跃嬉戏。|近义词：dolphin|反义词：无
