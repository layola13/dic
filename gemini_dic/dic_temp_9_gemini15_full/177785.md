receipt: /rɪˈsiːt/ | n. | 收据，收执，收条 | A written acknowledgment that something has been received, especially money or goods.  |  I need a receipt for my purchase. 我需要我的购物收据。  The accountant carefully filed all the receipts. 会计仔细地整理了所有的收据。| invoice, acknowledgement |  - | noun
