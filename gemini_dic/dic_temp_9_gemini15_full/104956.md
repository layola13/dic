kelvin:/ˈkɛlvɪn/| n.|开尔文（温度单位）|The Kelvin is the base unit of thermodynamic temperature in the International System of Units (SI).  It is defined by assigning the value 273.16 K to the triple point of water.|例句：The boiling point of water is 373.15 Kelvin.  水的沸点是373.15开尔文。  The temperature of the experiment was maintained at 293 Kelvin. 实验的温度保持在293开尔文。|近义词：degree Kelvin (less common)|反义词：无
