overinstructed: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌktɪd/ | adj. | 指导过度，教导过多 |  The adjective "overinstructed" describes a situation where someone has received excessive instruction or guidance, often to the point of being overwhelming or counterproductive. It implies that the person has been given too much information or direction, leading to confusion, frustration, or a lack of independent thinking.  | The student felt overinstructed and overwhelmed by the sheer volume of information provided. = 学生感觉指导过度，大量的信息让他不知所措。  The project was overinstructed, stifling creativity and initiative. = 项目指导过度，扼杀了创造力和主动性。 | underinstructed, underguided | well-instructed, adequately instructed |  形容词
