mutiny:/ˈmjuːtəni/|/ˈmjuːtɪni/ | n. |哗变；叛变；反抗 |The act of open rebellion against constituted authority, especially by sailors or soldiers against their officers.  It often involves a seizure of control. |例句：The sailors staged a mutiny after months of harsh treatment. 水手们在遭受了数月的残酷待遇后发动了兵变。

例句：The soldiers' mutiny was quickly suppressed by the government.  政府迅速镇压了士兵的叛乱。


近义词：rebellion, revolt, insurrection, uprising |反义词：obedience, compliance, submission


词源：源自于法语"mutinerie"，最终源于拉丁语"mutare"（改变）。  这反映了哗变的本质——对既定秩序的改变。
