gypped:/dʒɪpt/ | vt. | 欺骗，敲竹杠 | To cheat or swindle someone, especially by overcharging or giving less than what is due.  This word is informal and often implies a petty or unfair deception. |

例句：

I feel like I got gypped at the market; they charged me way too much for those apples. = 我觉得我在市场上被骗了；那些苹果他们收了我太多钱了。

The used car salesman really gypped me; the car broke down a week later. = 二手车销售员真的骗了我；那辆车一周后就坏了。


近义词：cheat, swindle, defraud, rip off, shortchange

反义词：  honestly deal with, fairly compensate
