journalistically:/ˌdʒɜːrnəlˈɪstɪkli/ | adv. |新闻般地；新闻记者般地 | In a manner characteristic of journalism or journalists.  This adverb describes actions or writing done in a way typical of journalistic style and practices. It often implies a focus on facts, objectivity, and a concise, engaging style. | The article was written journalistically, prioritizing factual accuracy and brevity. 这篇文章是按照新闻报道的方式撰写的，优先考虑事实的准确性和简洁性。 |  news-like, reportorially | unjournalistically
