misbuttons:/ˌmɪsˈbʌtənz/| n.| 错误扣好纽扣；扣错纽扣 |  The act of fastening buttons incorrectly. It can refer to the state of having buttons fastened incorrectly, or the act of doing so.  | 例句：He had several misbuttons on his shirt. = 他的衬衫有好几颗扣子扣错了。

The child's misbuttons were adorable. = 孩子扣错扣子的样子很可爱。


近义词：incorrectly buttoned, wrongly fastened


反义词：correctly buttoned, properly fastened


词性:名词
