词源:
From *refracto* (from *refract*) + *metric* (from *meter*).  "Refract" comes from Latin *re-*(back, again) + *frangere* (to break).  "Metric" relates to measurement. Therefore, "refractometric" literally means "related to the measurement of bending of light".
