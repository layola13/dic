overturning:/ˌoʊvərˈtɜːrnɪŋ/ | n. |推翻；倾覆；颠覆 |The overturning of the government was a surprise to many.  政府的推翻让许多人感到意外。|近义词：subversion, overthrow, reversal |反义词：upholding, confirmation, establishment


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The wind is overturning the small boats. = 风正在掀翻小船。
The company is overturning its outdated policies. = 公司正在推翻其过时的政策。


一般过去时:
The car overturned on the icy road. = 汽车在结冰的道路上翻车了。
The revolution overturned the monarchy. = 革命推翻了君主制。


现在完成时:
The scandal has overturned his career. = 这场丑闻毁了他的事业。
Recent events have overturned our initial predictions. = 最近发生的事件推翻了我们最初的预测。


过去完成时:
The evidence had overturned the initial verdict. = 证据已经推翻了最初的判决。
The new findings had overturned the previous theory. = 新的发现推翻了之前的理论。


将来时:
The storm is likely to overturn many trees. = 暴风雨可能会吹倒许多树木。
The new legislation will overturn the existing regulations. = 新法案将推翻现有的规章制度。


虚拟语气:
If the court overturned the ruling, we would appeal. = 如果法院推翻裁决，我们将上诉。
I wish the government would overturn this unfair law. = 我希望政府能推翻这项不公平的法律。


被动语态:
The decision was overturned by the higher court. = 该决定被上级法院推翻。
The old building was overturned by the earthquake. = 老建筑被地震摧毁。


疑问句:
Will the new evidence overturn the verdict? = 新证据会推翻判决吗？
Has the government overturned its previous decision? = 政府是否推翻了之前的决定？


否定句:
The arguments did not overturn his conviction. = 这些论点并没有推翻他的定罪。
The new evidence did not overturn the original findings. = 新证据并没有推翻最初的发现。


PHRASE->
the overturning of a decision = 决定的推翻
the overturning of a verdict = 判决的推翻
the overturning of a government = 政府的推翻
the overturning of a regime = 政权的推翻
the overturning of a car = 汽车的翻车
