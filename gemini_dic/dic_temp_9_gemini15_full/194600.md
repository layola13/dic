self-surveys:/ˈsɛlf ˈsɜːrveɪz/| n. |自我评估问卷，自我调查|Self-surveys are questionnaires designed for individuals to assess their own characteristics, behaviors, or opinions.|例句：The company uses self-surveys to gather employee feedback on workplace satisfaction. 公司使用自我评估问卷收集员工对工作满意度的反馈。|例句：Participants completed self-surveys about their stress levels before and after the intervention.参与者在干预前后完成了关于他们压力水平的自我评估问卷。|近义词：self-assessments, self-reports, questionnaires|反义词：external evaluations, peer reviews|名词
