curare:/kʊˈrɑːri/| n.|库拉里（箭毒）|A complex mixture of toxins derived from various plants, primarily used by indigenous peoples of South America to poison arrow tips for hunting.  It acts by blocking neuromuscular transmission, causing paralysis.| Example: The hunter dipped his arrow tips in curare before going into the jungle.  猎人进入丛林之前，将箭尖浸入库拉里毒药中。|近义词：poison, toxin, neurotoxin|反义词：antidote
