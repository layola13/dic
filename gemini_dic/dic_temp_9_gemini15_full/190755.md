saved:/seɪvd/| v. | 保存；挽救；节省 | To keep something safe from harm or loss; to rescue someone or something from danger; to avoid wasting something. | 例句：She saved the child from drowning. 她把孩子从溺水中救了出来。
She saved money for a new car. 她攒钱买新车。|近义词：preserved, rescued, conserved |反义词：wasted, lost, destroyed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He saves money every month. = 他每个月都存钱。
They save the environment by recycling. = 他们通过回收利用来保护环境。

一般过去时:
I saved a lot of money last year. = 我去年存了很多钱。
The firefighters saved the building from burning down. = 消防员把大楼从火灾中救了出来。

现在完成时:
She has saved enough money for a down payment. = 她已经存够了首付的钱。
We have saved several endangered species from extinction. = 我们已经挽救了几个濒临灭绝的物种。

过去完成时:
They had saved enough money before they started their business. = 他们在创业之前已经存够了钱。
The doctors had saved his life through a risky surgery. = 医生们通过一次高风险的手术挽救了他的生命。

将来时:
I will save this document on my computer. = 我将把这份文档保存在我的电脑上。
We will save the planet if we act now. = 如果我们现在行动，我们将拯救地球。

虚拟语气:
If I had saved more money, I could have bought a house. = 如果我存的钱更多，我就能买房子了。
I wish I had saved that email. = 我真希望我保存了那封邮件。

被动语态:
The document was saved automatically. = 文档已自动保存。
Many lives were saved by the quick response of the emergency services. = 紧急服务的快速反应挽救了许多人的生命。

疑问句:
Did you save the file? = 你保存文件了吗？
How much money have you saved? = 你存了多少钱？

否定句:
I didn't save the document. = 我没有保存文档。
They couldn't save the company from bankruptcy. = 他们没能把公司从破产中拯救出来。
