premanufacture:/ˌpriːmænjʊˈfæktʃər/| v.|预先制造，预制|To manufacture something in advance or prepare something beforehand for manufacture.|The company premanufactures components to speed up assembly. 这家公司预先制造组件以加快组装速度。|近义词：prepare, prefabricate|反义词：post-manufacture


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They premanufacture parts for their cars. = 他们预先制造汽车零件。
The factory premanufactures many components. = 这家工厂预先制造许多组件。

一般过去时:
We premanufactured the modules last week. = 我们上周预先制造了模块。
The engineers premanufactured the prototypes. = 工程师们预先制造了原型。

现在完成时:
They have premanufactured all the necessary parts. = 他们已经预先制造了所有必要的零件。
The company has premanufactured enough materials for the project. = 公司已经为该项目预先制造了足够的材料。

过去完成时:
They had premanufactured the frames before painting. = 他们在油漆之前已经预先制造了框架。
We had premanufactured the tools before starting the work. = 我们在开始工作之前已经预先制造了工具。

将来时:
We will premanufacture the housing units next month. = 我们下个月将预先制造住房单元。
They will premanufacture the engines for the new model. = 他们将为新款车型预先制造发动机。

虚拟语气:
If we premanufactured the parts, the assembly would be faster. = 如果我们预先制造零件，组装速度就会更快。
It would be better if they premanufactured the components. = 如果他们预先制造组件会更好。

被动语态:
The components are premanufactured by a subcontractor. = 这些组件是由分包商预先制造的。
The parts were premanufactured according to the specifications. = 零件是按照规格预先制造的。

疑问句:
Are the modules premanufactured? = 模块是预先制造的吗？
Were the materials premanufactured for the construction? = 建筑材料是预先制造的吗？

否定句:
They did not premanufacture the entire product. = 他们没有预先制造整个产品。
The company does not premanufacture its own chips. = 这家公司不预先制造自己的芯片。


PHRASE->
