lurement:/ˈlʊrmənt/ | n. | 诱惑；诱饵；吸引人的事物 | Something that attracts or entices, often deceptively.  It can refer to the act of enticing or attracting as well as the thing used to entice. | The lure of fame and fortune led many to seek their fortunes in the gold rush.  诱惑名利的光环导致许多人在淘金热中寻求财富。


The sweet scent of the blossoms was a lurement to the bees. 花朵的甜香吸引了蜜蜂。


近义词：enticement, temptation, attraction, bait, lure | 反义词：repellent, deterrent
