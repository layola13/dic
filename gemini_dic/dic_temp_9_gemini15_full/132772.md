nondrivers:/ˈnɑːnˌdraɪvərz/ | n. |非驾驶员，不会开车的人|People who do not have a driver's license or who choose not to drive.  This term often refers to a group or population segment rather than individuals.  It can also refer to those who cannot drive due to age, disability, or other reasons.|Example: The city is planning new public transportation options to better serve nondrivers.  这个城市正在规划新的公共交通选择，以便更好地服务于不会开车的人。|近义词：non-drivers, pedestrians (in some contexts)|反义词：drivers
