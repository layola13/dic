prod:/prɒd/|ˈprɑːd/| vt.|戳，刺，推动；激励，驱使|to poke or push something or someone|The farmer prodded the cow to move it along.  农夫戳了戳牛，让它往前走。
He prodded his friend to finish his work. 他催促朋友完成工作。|urge, goad|hinder, dissuade|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He prods the soil with a stick. 他用棍子戳土。
She prods him to study harder. 她催促他更加努力学习。


一般过去时:
The shepherd prodded the sheep towards the gate. 牧羊人把羊群赶向大门。
He prodded the sleeping man gently. 他轻轻地戳了戳睡着的人。


现在完成时:
She has prodded him repeatedly to take action. 她一再催促他采取行动。
They have prodded the government for answers. 他们一直在向政府施压要求答复。


过去完成时:
He had prodded the dough several times before baking it. 他在烘烤之前已经多次戳了面团。
They had prodded the witness for more information. 他们之前已经多次盘问证人以获取更多信息。


将来时:
I will prod him to call you tomorrow. 我明天会催促他给你打电话。
She will prod the cake with a toothpick to check if it's done. 她会用牙签戳蛋糕来检查是否烤好了。


虚拟语气:
If I prodded him hard enough, he might listen. 如果我足够用力地催促他，他可能会听。
I wish I had prodded him to apply for the job sooner. 我真希望我早点催促他申请这份工作。


被动语态:
The dough was prodded until it was smooth. 面团被反复戳压直到光滑。
The witness was prodded by the lawyer for more details. 证人被律师追问更多细节。


疑问句:
Did you prod him to apologize? 你催促他道歉了吗？
Will she prod me for an update? 她会催我更新进度吗？


否定句:
I didn't prod him; he did it of his own accord. 我没有催促他；这是他自愿做的。
She won't prod him again; he's clearly upset. 她不会再催促他了；他显然很生气。


PHRASE->
prod someone into doing something = 催促某人做某事
prod someone on = 鼓励某人继续
prod someone to action = 敦促某人采取行动
prod for information = 催问信息
prod at something = 用力戳某物
