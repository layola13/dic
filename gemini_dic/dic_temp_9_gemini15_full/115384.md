manses:/ˈmænsiːz/| n. |牧师住宅；大住宅|A manse is a large house, especially the dwelling of a minister or clergyman.  The term often implies a certain level of grandeur or importance associated with the position. |例句：The old manse stood proudly overlooking the village. 这座古老的牧师住宅自豪地俯瞰着村庄。The manse was spacious enough to accommodate the minister's family and guests.  这栋牧师住宅足够宽敞，可以容纳牧师的家人和客人。|近义词：rectory, parsonage, vicarage|反义词：humble dwelling, cottage|名词
