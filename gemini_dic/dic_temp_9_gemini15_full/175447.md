ramify:/ˈræmɪfaɪ/| vt.|分枝；分支；衍生|To branch out or spread out like branches of a tree; to divide into branches or subdivisions.  The ramifications of this decision are far-reaching.这个决定的影响深远。|近义词：branch, diverge, spread, extend|反义词：converge, unite, consolidate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river ramifies into many smaller streams. = 这条河分成了许多较小的溪流。
The company's activities ramify across several industries. = 这家公司的业务遍及多个行业。


一般过去时:
The railway ramified throughout the country. = 铁路遍布全国。
The discussion ramified into several different topics. = 讨论扩展到几个不同的主题。


现在完成时:
The rumors have ramified throughout the town. = 谣言已在镇上四处传播。
The consequences of the accident have ramified in unexpected ways. = 事故的后果以意想不到的方式蔓延开来。


过去完成时:
The problem had ramified beyond our initial understanding. = 这个问题已经超出了我们最初的理解范围。
By the time we realized it, the issue had ramified into a full-blown crisis. = 当我们意识到这一点时，问题已经演变成一场全面的危机。


将来时:
The project will ramify into several sub-projects. = 该项目将细分为几个子项目。
We expect the effects of this policy to ramify across various sectors. = 我们预计这项政策的影响将波及各个部门。


虚拟语气:
If the roots ramified more deeply, the tree would be more stable. = 如果根系更深，树木会更稳定。
If the problem had not ramified so quickly, we could have handled it better. = 如果问题没有这么快蔓延开来，我们本来可以更好地处理它。


被动语态:
The network was ramified across the entire region. = 该网络遍及整个地区。
The effects of the earthquake were ramified throughout the city. = 地震的影响遍及整个城市。


疑问句:
Does this blood vessel ramify into the heart? = 这条血管是否分支到心脏？
How does the nervous system ramify throughout the body? = 神经系统是如何遍布全身的？


否定句:
The plant's roots did not ramify extensively. = 植物的根系没有广泛地蔓延。
The company's influence did not ramify into other markets. = 该公司的影响力并没有扩展到其他市场。


PHRASE->
ramify into: 分支成
ramify throughout: 遍布于
ramify across: 遍及
ramification of: ...的结果/影响
ramify from: 源于
