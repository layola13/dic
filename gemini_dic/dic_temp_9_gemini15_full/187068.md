riverbanks: /ˈrɪvərˌbæŋks/ | n. |河岸，河边 | The land alongside a river.  Riverbanks are often fertile and support a variety of plant and animal life.  | 例句：Wildflowers bloomed profusely along the riverbanks. 河岸上野花盛开。
The children played happily on the riverbanks. 孩子们在河岸上快乐地玩耍。|近义词：riverside, banks, shores |反义词：midstream, riverbed

词源:
river + banks.  "River" comes from the Old English word "rīfere,"  meaning "river, stream." "Banks" comes from Old English "banc," meaning "bench, bank, ridge."  Therefore, "riverbanks" literally means the "banks of a river."
