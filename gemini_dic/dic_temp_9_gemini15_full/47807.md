decarbonization:/ˌdiːkɑːrbənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 脱碳；去碳化 | The process of reducing carbon dioxide emissions.  It involves transitioning to cleaner energy sources and improving energy efficiency. | Example:  The government is committed to decarbonization of the energy sector. 政府致力于能源行业的脱碳。|近义词：carbon reduction, decarbonisation | 反义词：carbonization
