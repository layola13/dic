celebrates:/ˈsɛləbreɪts/| vt.|庆祝，赞扬|To mark (an event, etc.) with a ceremony or festivities; to honour or praise (someone or something).|例句：We celebrated our anniversary with a romantic dinner. 我们用一顿浪漫的晚餐庆祝了我们的结婚周年纪念日。
例句：The speech celebrated the achievements of the company.  这篇演讲赞扬了公司的成就。|近义词：commemorate, observe, honour|反义词：mourn, lament


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They celebrate Christmas every year. = 他们每年都庆祝圣诞节。
She celebrates her birthday with a big party. = 她用一个大型派对庆祝她的生日。

一般过去时:
We celebrated New Year's Eve with fireworks. = 我们用烟火庆祝了除夕夜。
He celebrated his graduation with his family. = 他和家人一起庆祝了他的毕业典礼。

现在完成时:
They have celebrated their 20th wedding anniversary. = 他们已经庆祝了他们的结婚20周年纪念日。
She has celebrated many successes in her career. = 她已经在她的职业生涯中庆祝了许多成功。

过去完成时:
They had celebrated their victory before the awards ceremony. = 他们在颁奖典礼之前已经庆祝了他们的胜利。
She had celebrated her birthday before we arrived. =在我们到达之前，她已经庆祝了她的生日。

将来时:
We will celebrate our daughter's wedding next year. = 我们明年将庆祝我们女儿的婚礼。
They will celebrate the completion of the project with a banquet. =他们将用宴会来庆祝项目的完成。


虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would celebrate wildly. = 如果我中了彩票，我会疯狂庆祝。
I wish I could celebrate with them tonight. = 我希望今晚能和他们一起庆祝。


被动语态:
The victory was celebrated throughout the city. = 胜利在整个城市都被庆祝。
His achievements will be celebrated at the upcoming conference. = 他的成就将在即将召开的会议上被庆祝。


疑问句:
How will you celebrate your success? = 你将如何庆祝你的成功？
What are they celebrating today? = 他们今天庆祝什么？


否定句:
We didn't celebrate Thanksgiving this year. = 我们今年没有庆祝感恩节。
She doesn't celebrate her birthday anymore. = 她不再庆祝她的生日了。


PHRASE->
celebrate a victory = 庆祝胜利
celebrate an achievement = 庆祝成就
celebrate a birthday = 庆祝生日
celebrate an anniversary = 庆祝周年纪念日
celebrate a holiday = 庆祝节日
