municipally:/mjuːˈnɪsɪpəli/| adv.|市政地；市政当局地 |By means of or relating to a municipality or its governing body.|例句：The park is municipally owned and maintained.  这个公园由市政部门拥有和维护。  |例句：The city council voted to municipally fund the new library. 市议会投票决定由市政部门资助新建图书馆。|近义词：publicly, civically|反义词：privately


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The city municipally funds the school system. = 这个城市由市政部门资助学校系统。
They municipally manage the water supply. = 他们由市政部门管理供水。


一般过去时:
The town municipally operated the bus service until last year. = 直到去年，这个镇都是由市政部门运营公交服务的。
The project was municipally funded in the past. = 该项目过去是由市政部门资助的。


现在完成时:
The city has municipally provided services for decades. = 这个城市几十年来一直由市政部门提供服务。
They have municipally maintained the roads since the 1980s. = 自20世纪80年代以来，他们一直由市政部门维护道路。


过去完成时:
The town had municipally controlled the water supply before privatization. = 在私有化之前，该镇一直由市政部门控制供水。
The council had municipally approved the budget before the meeting. = 议会在会议之前已经市政批准了预算。


将来时:
The city will municipally invest in renewable energy. = 该市将投资可再生能源。
They will municipally regulate the new construction project. = 他们将对新的建筑项目进行市政监管。


虚拟语气:
If the project were municipally funded, it would be completed sooner. = 如果该项目由市政部门资助，它将更快完成。
I wish the park were municipally maintained better. = 我希望公园能得到更好的市政维护。


被动语态:
The garbage is municipally collected every week. = 垃圾每周都由市政部门收集。
The park improvements are being municipally financed. = 公园的改进正在由市政部门资助。


疑问句:
Is the service municipally provided? = 这项服务是由市政部门提供的吗？
Are the schools municipally funded in your town? = 你们镇的学校是由市政部门资助的吗？


否定句:
The library is not municipally owned; it's privately funded. = 图书馆不是市政所有的；它是私人资助的。
The roads are not municipally maintained in this area. = 这个地区的道路没有由市政部门维护。
