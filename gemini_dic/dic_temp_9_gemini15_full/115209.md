manifester:/ˈmænɪˌfɛstər/ | n. v. | 显示者；表明者；(尤指)显化者；使显现；表明 | One who or that which manifests; one who makes something evident or clear.  To show (a quality, feeling, etc.) clearly; to make evident; to reveal. |


例句：

His anger manifested itself in a violent outburst. = 他的愤怒以暴怒的方式表现出来。

The symptoms of the disease manifested quickly. = 这种疾病的症状很快显现出来。


近义词：demonstrator, revealer, indicator, exhibitor

反义词：concealer, hider, suppressor


词性:  noun, verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He manifests his beliefs through his actions. = 他通过行动来表达他的信仰。
The symptoms manifest themselves in various ways. = 症状以多种方式表现出来。

一般过去时:
She manifested a surprising talent for painting. = 她表现出令人惊讶的绘画天赋。
The problem manifested itself during the test. = 问题在测试过程中显现出来。

现在完成时:
He has manifested great courage in the face of adversity. = 面对逆境，他表现出极大的勇气。
The company has manifested a commitment to sustainability. = 这家公司已经展现出对可持续发展的承诺。

过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, the problem had manifested itself fully. = 当我到达时，问题已经完全显现出来。
It had manifested itself in a series of strange events. = 它已经通过一系列奇怪的事件显现出来。

将来时:
The effects of the medicine will manifest themselves in a few days. = 药物的效果几天后就会显现出来。
She believes her dreams will manifest in reality. = 她相信她的梦想会变成现实。

虚拟语气:
If only he would manifest a little more empathy. = 但愿他能表现出一点点同情心。
If the plan manifested itself as expected, the project would succeed. = 如果计划按预期进行，项目将会成功。

被动语态:
The illness was manifested in a high fever. = 这种疾病表现为高烧。
His genius was manifested in his groundbreaking work. = 他的天才在他的开创性工作中得到了体现。

疑问句:
How did his unhappiness manifest itself? = 他的不快乐是如何表现出来的？
Does the disease always manifest in the same way? = 这种疾病总是以同样的方式表现出来吗？

否定句:
His anxiety did not manifest itself outwardly. = 他的焦虑没有外在表现。
The results did not manifest as predicted. = 结果并非如预测的那样。


PHRASE->
manifest destiny: 天命论
manifest function: 显性功能
manifest content: 表层内容
manifest image: 表象
to manifest itself: 表现出来


词源:
源自拉丁语 *manifestāre，意思是“使明显，使清楚”，来自manus（手）和festus（固定，确定）。  因此，其根本含义与“使可见，使易于理解”相关。
