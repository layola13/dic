reunites:/ˌriːˈjuːnaɪts/| v. |使重聚；使重合|To bring people or things together again after a period of separation.  The verb "reunite" implies a prior separation or division, and emphasizes the act of bringing things or people back together. |例句：The family reunited after many years apart.  多年分离后，这个家庭团聚了。
The separated pieces of the vase were carefully reunited. 花瓶分离的碎片被小心地重新拼合在一起。|近义词：rejoin, reunite, recombine |反义词：separate, divide, sever|动词
