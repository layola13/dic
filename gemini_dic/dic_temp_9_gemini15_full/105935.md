kleptomania:/ˌklɛptoˈmeɪniə/| n. |盗窃癖；偷窃狂|An irresistible, recurring impulse to steal, typically without regard for the value of the object stolen and without the intention to keep it.  This is a psychological disorder, not simply a criminal act.  It's often associated with other mental health conditions.|例句：His kleptomania led him to steal small items from shops, even though he could easily afford them.  他的盗窃癖让他从商店偷窃小物品，即使他很容易就能买得起。|近义词：shoplifting, stealing, pilfering|反义词：honesty, integrity
