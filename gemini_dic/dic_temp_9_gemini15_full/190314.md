sarcocarp:/ˈsɑːrkəʊkɑːrp/| n. |果肉，中果皮|The fleshy part of a fruit, surrounding the seed(s).  It develops from the ovary wall during fruit development.|例句：The sarcocarp of a peach is juicy and sweet. 桃子的果肉多汁甘甜。|例句：The sarcocarp of a mango is often brightly colored and flavorful. 芒果的果肉通常颜色鲜艳，味道鲜美。|近义词：mesocarp, pulp|反义词：endocarp, exocarp
