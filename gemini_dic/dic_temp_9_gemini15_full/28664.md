carinaria:/ˌkæriˈnɛəriə/| n. |海蜗牛，翼足类动物|A carinaria is a genus of pelagic sea snails, marine gastropod mollusks in the family Carinariidae.  They are characterized by their thin, fragile shells and transparent bodies.
