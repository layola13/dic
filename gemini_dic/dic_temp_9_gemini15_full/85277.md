harmonise:/ˈhɑːmənaɪz/| /ˈhɑːrmənaɪz/ | vt. vi. |使和谐；协调；和睦相处 |To bring into harmony or agreement; to be in harmony or agreement. |例句：The colours in the painting harmonise beautifully.  这幅画中的色彩搭配得非常和谐。

The singers harmonised perfectly during the performance.  歌手们在演出中配合得天衣无缝。


近义词：accord, coordinate, unify, blend | 反义词：clash, conflict, discord


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The colours harmonise well. = 这些颜色搭配得很好。
They harmonise their efforts. = 他们协调他们的努力。


一般过去时:
The orchestra harmonised beautifully last night. = 昨晚管弦乐队演奏得非常和谐。
The two sides harmonised their differences. = 双方协调了他们的分歧。


现在完成时:
We have harmonised our schedules. = 我们已经协调了我们的日程安排。
They have harmonised their musical styles. = 他们已经协调了他们的音乐风格。


过去完成时:
They had harmonised their positions before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经协调了各自的立场。
The committee had harmonised the different viewpoints. = 委员会已经协调了不同的观点。


将来时:
We will harmonise the two systems. = 我们将使这两个系统协调一致。
The two countries will harmonise their trade policies. = 这两个国家将协调它们的贸易政策。


虚拟语气:
If they harmonised their efforts, they would succeed. = 如果他们协调努力，他们将会成功。
I wish they would harmonise their differences. = 我希望他们能协调他们的分歧。


被动语态:
The different elements were harmonised by the artist. = 不同的元素被艺术家和谐地融合在一起。
The two proposals were harmonised to create a unified plan. = 两个提案被协调以创建一个统一的计划。


疑问句:
Do the colours harmonise? = 这些颜色协调吗？
Will the two companies harmonise their strategies? = 这两家公司会协调它们的策略吗？


否定句:
The colours don't harmonise. = 这些颜色不协调。
The two groups failed to harmonise their approaches. = 这两个群体未能协调他们的方法。


PHRASE->
harmonise with: 与…协调
harmonise a song: 合唱一首歌曲
harmonise colours: 调和颜色
harmonise the different views: 调和不同的观点
harmonise relations: 改善关系

词源:
From Old French *harmoniser, from Latin harmonizare, from harmonia "harmony".  Ultimately from Greek *harmonia.
