pseudo-Muslim:/ˈsuːdoʊ ˈmʌzlɪm/| adj. |假穆斯林，冒牌穆斯林|Someone who claims to be Muslim but does not genuinely adhere to the principles and practices of Islam.  This term is often used pejoratively and implies a lack of sincere belief or commitment to the faith.  It can refer to individuals who outwardly conform to Islamic customs but privately hold different beliefs or engage in behaviors contradictory to Islamic teachings. |Example:  He was accused of being a pseudo-Muslim, performing rituals superficially while ignoring the core tenets of the faith. 他被指控是假穆斯林，表面上进行宗教仪式，却忽略了信仰的核心原则。|近义词：pretend Muslim, nominal Muslim,  insincere Muslim|反义词：devout Muslim, true believer, practicing Muslim|
