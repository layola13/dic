erythrism:/ˈɛrɪθrɪzəm/| n. |红化病；赤化病|A genetic condition characterized by reddish coloration of the skin, hair, and/or eyes, typically due to a deficiency in melanin production.|Example: Erythrism is a rare genetic disorder that affects pigmentation.|近义词：reddish coloration, albinism (in the context of a contrasting deficiency)|反义词：melanism
