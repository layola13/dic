salesmanager: /ˈseɪlzˌmænɪdʒər/ | n. | 销售经理 | A sales manager is a person who manages a sales team or department within a company.  Their responsibilities typically include setting sales targets, recruiting and training salespeople, overseeing sales activities, and analyzing sales performance. | Example:  The sales manager presented the quarterly sales report to the CEO. 销售经理向首席执行官汇报了季度销售报告。  The new sales manager implemented a new training program for the team. 新任销售经理为团队实施了一个新的培训计划。|近义词：sales director, sales executive | 反义词：salesperson, sales representative
