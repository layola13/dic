downplay:/ˈdaʊnpleɪ/| vt.|轻描淡写，贬低|To represent or treat (something) as less important than it really is.|The government tried to downplay the severity of the crisis. 政府试图轻描淡写危机的严重性。|近义词：understate, minimize, belittle|反义词：exaggerate, emphasize, highlight|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He downplays his achievements. = 他轻描淡写自己的成就。
They downplay the risks involved. = 他们轻描淡写所涉及的风险。

一般过去时:
She downplayed the significance of the event. = 她轻描淡写了事件的重要性。
The media downplayed the protests. = 媒体淡化了抗议活动。

现在完成时:
They have downplayed the problem for too long. = 他们长期以来一直轻描淡写这个问题。
The company has downplayed its environmental impact. = 公司一直淡化其对环境的影响。

过去完成时:
He had downplayed his role in the project. = 他之前一直轻描淡写自己在项目中的作用。
The report had downplayed the potential dangers. = 报告之前一直轻描淡写潜在的危险。

将来时:
They will downplay the negative aspects of the report. = 他们将会淡化报告中的负面内容。
She will downplay her contribution to the success. = 她会轻描淡写她对成功的贡献。

虚拟语气:
If he downplayed the issue, it wouldn't be resolved. = 如果他轻描淡写这个问题，它就不会得到解决。
I wish he hadn't downplayed the warning signs. = 我希望他之前没有轻描淡写警告信号。

被动语态:
The incident was downplayed by the authorities. = 这一事件被当局轻描淡写了。
His contributions were downplayed in the final report. = 他在最终报告中做出的贡献被轻描淡写了。

疑问句:
Did they downplay the evidence? = 他们是否轻描淡写了证据？
Why did she downplay her feelings? = 她为什么轻描淡写自己的感受？

否定句:
He didn't downplay the seriousness of the situation. = 他没有轻描淡写形势的严重性。
They didn't downplay the importance of the meeting. = 他们没有轻描淡写会议的重要性。
