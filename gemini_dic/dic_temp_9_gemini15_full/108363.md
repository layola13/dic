lavishly:/ˈlævɪʃli/| adv.|挥霍地，慷慨地，丰富地 | generously and abundantly; in a way that uses or spends a large amount of something | The party was lavishly decorated.  派对装饰得非常奢华。|近义词：profusely, extravagantly, sumptuously |反义词：sparingly, meagerly, frugally


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They lavishly praise their employees. = 他们慷慨地赞扬他们的员工。
She lavishly spends money on clothes. = 她挥霍地花钱买衣服。


一般过去时:
He lavishly decorated his new home. = 他慷慨地装饰了他的新家。
They lavishly celebrated their anniversary. = 他们盛大庆祝了他们的周年纪念日。


现在完成时:
She has lavishly spent her inheritance. = 她已经挥霍掉了她的遗产。
They have lavishly supported the charity. = 他们已经慷慨地支持了这个慈善机构。


过去完成时:
He had lavishly prepared for the party before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经为派对做了充分的准备。
They had lavishly donated to the cause before the campaign ended. = 活动结束前，他们已经慷慨地捐款了。


将来时:
They will lavishly reward the winner. = 他们将慷慨地奖励获胜者。
She will lavishly decorate the Christmas tree. = 她将盛大地装饰圣诞树。


虚拟语气:
If she were rich, she would live lavishly. = 如果她富有，她将会过着奢华的生活。
I wish I could live lavishly. = 我希望我能过着奢华的生活。


被动语态:
The guests were lavishly entertained. = 客人们受到了盛情的款待。
The project was lavishly funded. = 这个项目得到了大量的资金支持。


疑问句:
Did they lavishly spend their bonus? = 他们是否挥霍了他们的奖金？
Were the decorations lavishly done? = 装饰是否做得非常奢华？


否定句:
They did not lavishly spend their money. = 他们没有挥霍他们的钱。
The meal was not lavishly prepared. = 这顿饭的准备并不奢华。
