put-to-bed:/pʊt tə ˈbɛd/ | vt. |哄睡；使…就寝；|to cause someone or something to go to sleep or to be put to rest|例句：I put the baby to bed early last night. 昨晚我早早地把婴儿哄睡了。|例句：The storm finally put the raging fire to bed.暴风雨最终扑灭了熊熊大火。|近义词：settle down, lull to sleep |反义词：wake up, rouse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I put my children to bed at 9 pm every night. = 我每天晚上9点哄孩子们睡觉。
She puts her pet hamster to bed in its cage. = 她把她的宠物仓鼠放进笼子里睡觉。

一般过去时:
He put his daughter to bed after reading her a story. = 他给女儿读完故事后把她哄睡了。
They put the dog to bed in its comfortable kennel. = 他们把狗放在舒适的狗窝里睡觉。

现在完成时:
I have put the baby to bed, so it's quiet now. = 我已经把婴儿哄睡了，所以现在很安静。
She has put all the animals to bed for the night. = 她已经把所有的动物都哄睡了。

过去完成时:
The parents had put the children to bed before they went out. = 父母在外出之前已经把孩子们哄睡了。
We had put the cat to bed before the thunderstorm started. = 雷雨开始之前我们已经把猫哄睡了。

将来时:
I will put the baby to bed as soon as he finishes his milk. = 他喝完牛奶后我就把他哄睡。
She will put the dog to bed after its evening walk. = 她会在狗狗晚上散步后把它哄睡。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would put the child to bed earlier. = 如果我是你，我会让孩子早点睡觉。
I wish I had put the baby to bed earlier. = 我真希望我早点把婴儿哄睡。

被动语态:
The children are usually put to bed at 8 pm. = 孩子们通常晚上8点被哄睡。
The pets are put to bed in their respective sleeping areas. = 宠物们被放在各自的睡眠区睡觉。

疑问句:
Have you put the baby to bed yet? = 你把婴儿哄睡了吗？
Did you put the dog to bed before leaving the house? = 你离开家前把狗哄睡了吗？

否定句:
I didn't put the children to bed last night. = 我昨晚没有哄孩子们睡觉。
She hasn't put the cat to bed yet. = 她还没把猫哄睡。
