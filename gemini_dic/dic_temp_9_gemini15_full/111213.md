lithophyse:/ˈlɪθəfaɪz/| n. |岩石结核，矿物结核 |A lithophyse is a roughly spherical or ellipsoidal concretion found in some volcanic rocks, often containing cavities or amygdules filled with secondary minerals.  They are typically composed of minerals that precipitated from fluids circulating within the rock after its formation. |Example: The geologist carefully examined the lithophyse, noting its size and the minerals within its cavities.  地质学家仔细检查了岩石结核，注意到了它的尺寸和空腔内的矿物。|近义词：concretion, nodule, amygdalulite |反义词：无
