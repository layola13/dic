sermonet:/ˌsɜːrməˈnet/| n. |小训诫；简短的布道|A short sermon or moral discourse; a brief, informal lecture or address.  It often implies a less formal and more conversational tone than a full sermon.|例句：The teacher gave a sermonet on the importance of punctuality. 老师就准时的重要性做了一个简短的训诫。

例句：The priest delivered a brief sermonet before the mass.  牧师在弥撒前做了一个简短的布道。


近义词：short sermon, brief lecture, admonition, homily


反义词：long discourse, lengthy lecture


词性:名词
