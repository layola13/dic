overfemininely:/ˌoʊvərˈfɛmənɪnli/| adv.|过于女性化地；过分女性化地|Excessively feminine in manner or appearance.  It suggests an exaggeration or artificiality of feminine traits.|例句：She dressed overfemininely, with frills and bows everywhere. 她穿着过于女性化，到处都是褶边和蝴蝶结。

例句：He spoke in an overfeminine voice, which made some people uncomfortable. 他说话的声音过于女性化，这使得一些人不舒服。


近义词：excessively femininely, affectedly feminine


反义词：masculinely, mannishly, tomboyishly


词性:副词
