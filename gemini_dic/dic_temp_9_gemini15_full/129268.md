noddling:/ˈnɒdlɪŋ/ | v. | 慢吞吞地移动；无目的地摆弄；(头发)随意地摆弄；|  To move in a slow, relaxed way, often without a clear purpose; to idly fiddle with something; to casually arrange (hair). | 例句：He was noodling around on his guitar. 他在随意地拨弄着吉他。

例句：She spent the afternoon noodling around on her phone. 她下午都在无目的地玩手机。


近义词：dabbling, fiddling, messing around, trifling


反义词：working diligently, focusing intently


词性:动词
