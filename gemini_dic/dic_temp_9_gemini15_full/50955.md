designatory:/dɪˈzɪɡnətəri/ | adj. | 指定的；指示的；任命的 | Serving to designate or appoint; indicative.  The word refers to something that points out, identifies, or names something else. It often implies an official or formal act of designation. |例句：The designatory letter arrived, officially confirming his appointment.  这封任命信到了，正式确认了他的任命。

例句：The designatory remarks made it clear who would be the next chairman.  这番指定性发言清楚地表明了谁将成为下一任主席。


近义词：designative, indicative, appointing, nominating


反义词：unspecified, indeterminate, ambiguous


词性：形容词
