prisoners:/ˈprɪzənərz/| n. |囚犯，犯人 |People who are kept in prison as a punishment for a crime or while awaiting trial.  |例句：The prisoners were allowed one hour of exercise per day. 囚犯每天允许一小时的锻炼时间。|例句：Several prisoners escaped from the jail last night.  昨晚有几名囚犯越狱。|近义词：inmates, convicts, detainees |反义词：jailers, wardens

一般现在时:
The prisoners work in the fields. = 囚犯们在田里工作。
Many prisoners write letters to their families. = 许多囚犯给家人写信。

一般过去时:
The prisoners rioted last night. = 囚犯们昨晚暴动。

现在完成时:
The prisoners have been held for many years. = 这些囚犯已经被关押多年了。
Several prisoners have escaped this year. = 今年已经有几名囚犯越狱了。

过去完成时:
The guards had already left when the prisoners escaped. = 狱警离开时，囚犯们已经越狱了。

将来时:
The prisoners will be released next month. = 囚犯们下个月将被释放。
The prisoners will face trial next week. = 囚犯们下周将面临审判。

虚拟语气:
If the prisoners were given more freedom, they might be less violent. = 如果给囚犯们更多的自由，他们可能会不那么暴力。
I wish the prisoners had better living conditions. = 我希望囚犯们有更好的生活条件。

被动语态:

The prisoners are being monitored closely. = 囚犯们正受到严密的监控。

疑问句:
