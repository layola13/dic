quasi-regularly:/ˈkwɑːzi ˈrɛɡjʊləlɪ/ | adv. | 准规则地，近乎规律地 |  Describes something that is almost but not completely regular or periodic.  It suggests a pattern that is frequently observed but with occasional exceptions or deviations. | The machine functioned quasi-regularly, experiencing occasional glitches.  机器近乎规律地运转，偶尔出现故障。| The data showed a quasi-regular pattern of spikes. 数据显示出近乎规律的峰值模式。 | regularly, periodically | irregularly, randomly | 副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't have tenses like verbs.  It describes the manner in which an action occurs.

Example 1: The lights flickered quasi-regularly. 灯光近乎规律地闪烁。
Example 2:  The trains arrived quasi-regularly, sometimes running late. 火车近乎规律地到达，有时晚点。


PHRASE->
There are no common set phrases specifically using "quasi-regularly".  The word itself is used to modify verbs describing actions.
