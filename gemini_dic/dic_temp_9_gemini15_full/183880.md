resembling: /rɪˈzɛmblɪŋ/ | adj. | 相似的，类似的 |  Having a similar appearance to someone or something else.  It suggests a likeness or similarity, but not necessarily an exact match. |

例句：

The two brothers are remarkably resembling. = 这两个兄弟长得非常相像。

His handwriting is resembling mine. = 他的笔迹和我相似。


近义词：similar, alike, like, comparable

反义词：different, dissimilar, unlike


词性：形容词
