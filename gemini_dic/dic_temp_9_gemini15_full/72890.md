followed:/ˈfɒləʊd/ /ˈfɑːloʊd/| v.|跟随的，接着的|Followed is the past tense and past participle of the verb "follow". It means to come after something or someone in time or space, or to go or come after someone or something.  It can also imply adherence to instructions or a leader.|例句：The dog followed its owner.  这只狗跟着它的主人。

The meeting followed a heated debate. 会议紧随一场激烈的辩论之后。


近义词：pursued, succeeded, tracked, came after, ensued|反义词：preceded, led

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He follows the rules. 他遵守规则。
She follows her dreams. 她追逐她的梦想。

一般过去时:
I followed the instructions carefully. 我仔细地按照指示做了。
They followed the map to the treasure. 他们按照地图找到了宝藏。

现在完成时:
She has followed my advice. 她听取了我的建议。
He has followed the same routine for years. 多年来他一直保持着同样的作息。

过去完成时:
I had followed the trail for hours before I saw the cabin. 我沿着小路走了几个小时才看到小屋。
They had followed the clues carefully before finding the solution. 他们在找到答案之前仔细地研究了线索。

将来时:
I will follow your lead. 我将以你为首。
She will follow up on that later. 她稍后会跟进这件事。

虚拟语气:
If I followed his advice, I would have been successful. 如果我听从了他的建议，我就成功了。
I wish I had followed my heart. 我真希望我当时听了从自己的内心。

被动语态:
The instructions were followed precisely. 指示被精确地执行了。
His advice was followed by many. 他的建议被许多人采纳了。

疑问句:
Who followed me? 谁跟着我？
Did anyone follow the rules? 有人遵守规则吗？

否定句:
He didn't follow the instructions. 他没有按照指示去做。
She didn't follow the conversation. 她没有参与谈话。
