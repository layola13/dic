defensiveness: /dɪˈfɛnsɪvnəs/ | n. | 防御性；戒备；敏感 | The state of being defensive; a tendency to react defensively to criticism or perceived threats.  It often involves protecting oneself from perceived attack or blame, sometimes even when unjustified.  | 例句：His defensiveness during the interrogation made the police suspicious.  他的审问中的防御姿态让警察起了疑心。

His defensiveness prevented him from seeing his own mistakes.  他的戒备心使他看不到自己的错误。


近义词： protectiveness, guardedness, sensitivity, suspicion

反义词： openness, receptiveness, vulnerability
