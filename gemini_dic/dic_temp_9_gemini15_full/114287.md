mainmast:/ˈmeɪnˌmæst/| n.|主桅杆|The mainmast is the principal mast of a sailing vessel, typically the tallest and strongest.|例句：The mainmast creaked ominously in the rising wind.  主桅杆在越来越大的风中发出不祥的吱吱作响声。|例句：The storm snapped the mainmast clean in two. 风暴把主桅杆劈成了两半。|近义词：principal mast, largest mast|反义词：foremast, mizzenmast
