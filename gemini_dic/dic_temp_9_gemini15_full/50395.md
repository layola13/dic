deposes:/dɪˈpoʊzɪz/ | vt. |罢免；免职；作证；陈述| To remove someone from a high position of power, especially a throne or office. Also, to give evidence or testimony, especially in a formal setting.| 例句：The king was deposed by his rebellious son. 国王被他的叛逆儿子废黜了。 The witness deposed that she saw the suspect flee the scene. 证人证实她看到嫌疑犯逃离现场。|近义词：dethrone, oust, dismiss, testify, attest|反义词：appoint, install, enthrone|动词
