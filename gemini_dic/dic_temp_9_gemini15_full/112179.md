lookouts:/ˈlʊkaʊts/| n.|瞭望台；瞭望员；警惕；监视| A lookout is a place or a person who watches for danger or something interesting.  It can also refer to a careful watch for something. | 例句：The ship had a lookout stationed at the bow. 这艘船在船头安排了一名瞭望员。

The hikers reached a high lookout point offering stunning views.  徒步旅行者到达一个视野开阔的瞭望点，欣赏到令人惊叹的景色。


近义词：watchman, observer, sentinel, guard | 反义词：negligence, carelessness
