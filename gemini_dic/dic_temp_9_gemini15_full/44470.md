crossbeam: /ˈkrɒsbɪːm/ | n. |横梁，横木 | A horizontal beam or bar, especially one used as a structural support.  |例句：The crossbeam supported the weight of the roof. 这根横梁支撑着屋顶的重量。

The ceiling was reinforced with several sturdy crossbeams. 天花板用几根结实的横梁加固了。


近义词：transverse beam, joist, girder | 反义词：None specifically, but terms like vertical beam could be considered opposites in orientation.


词性: 名词

Etymology:

The word "crossbeam" is a compound word formed from "cross" and "beam."

* **Cross:**  From Old English *crūc, *crūce, meaning "cross," ultimately of Germanic origin.  It signifies something that intersects or crosses another.

* **Beam:** From Old English *beam, meaning "tree," "timber," or "pole."  It's related to words like "boom" and reflects its use as a long, strong piece of wood or other material.
