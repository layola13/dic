inheritance:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtəns/ | n. | 继承；遗产；遗传 | The act of inheriting something; something that is inherited.  It can refer to property, titles, genes, or cultural traditions. | Example:  He received a large inheritance from his grandmother. 他从祖母那里继承了一大笔遗产。

He inherited his father's love of gardening. 他继承了他父亲热爱园艺的品质。


近义词：legacy, succession, bequest | 反义词：disinheritance


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company's success is largely due to its strong inheritance of its founder's vision. = 这家公司的成功很大程度上归功于它继承了创始人强烈的远见。
She inherits her mother's artistic talent. = 她继承了她母亲的艺术天赋。


一般过去时:
He inherited his grandfather's estate. = 他继承了他祖父的产业。
The family inherited a long tradition of craftsmanship. = 这个家族继承了悠久的工艺传统。


现在完成时:
They have inherited a significant amount of debt. = 他们继承了巨额债务。
She has inherited a beautiful antique clock. = 她继承了一座美丽的古董钟。


过去完成时:
Before he died, he had inherited the family business. = 他死前已经继承了家族企业。
By the time she turned 30, she had inherited several properties. = 到她30岁的时候，她已经继承了几处房产。


将来时:
He will inherit the throne when his father dies. = 他父亲死后将继承王位。
She will inherit her parents' house. = 她将继承她父母的房子。


虚拟语气:
If he inherited the company, he would run it differently. = 如果他继承了公司，他会以不同的方式经营它。
I wish I had inherited a large sum of money. = 我真希望我继承了一大笔钱。


被动语态:
The property was inherited by his son. = 这处房产由他的儿子继承。
The family tradition was inherited through generations. = 家族传统代代相传。


疑问句:
What did you inherit from your parents? = 你从父母那里继承了什么？
Will she inherit her father's business? = 她会继承她父亲的事业吗？


否定句:
He did not inherit anything from his father. = 他没有从他父亲那里继承任何东西。
She will not inherit the family fortune. = 她不会继承家族财产。
