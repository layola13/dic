revising:/rɪˈvaɪzɪŋ/| v.| 修订；修改；复习 |  The act of making changes to something, typically a text or piece of work, in order to improve it or correct errors. It can also refer to reviewing something again to refresh one's memory. | 例句：I'm revising my essay for grammar errors. 我正在修改我的论文中的语法错误。

例句：She spent the weekend revising for her history exam. 她周末都在复习历史考试。


近义词：editing, amending, correcting, reviewing, proofreading

反义词：finalizing, completing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am revising my manuscript. = 我正在修改我的手稿。
He revises his work carefully. = 他仔细地修改他的作品。

一般过去时:
I revised my essay last night. = 我昨晚修改了我的论文。
She revised her plans after the meeting. = 会议之后，她修改了她的计划。

现在完成时:
I have revised the document three times. = 我已经修改了这份文件三次。
They have revised the curriculum for the next year. = 他们已经修改了明年的课程。

过去完成时:
I had revised the chapter before submitting it. = 我在提交章节之前已经修改过了。
She had revised her presentation several times before the conference. = 在会议之前，她已经修改了她的演示文稿好几次。

将来时:
I will revise my paper before submitting it. = 我将在提交论文之前修改它。
He will revise his strategy after analyzing the data. = 他将在分析数据后修改他的策略。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would revise my work more thoroughly. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更彻底地修改我的作品。
I wish I had revised my application more carefully. = 我希望我更仔细地修改我的申请。

被动语态:
The manuscript is being revised by the editor. = 手稿正在由编辑修改。
The policy has been revised several times. = 该政策已经修改过几次。

疑问句:
Are you revising for the exam? = 你在为考试复习吗？
Have you finished revising your report? = 你修改报告了吗？

否定句:
I didn't revise my notes before the test. = 我考试前没有复习笔记。
He hasn't revised his essay yet. = 他还没有修改他的论文。


PHRASE->
revise for an exam = 为考试复习

revise a law = 修改法律
revise a plan = 修改计划
revise and resubmit = 修改后重新提交
