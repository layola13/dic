coma:/ˈkoʊmə/ | n. |昏迷；逗号 |A state of prolonged unconsciousness, usually caused by injury or illness.  |例句：The accident left him in a coma for several weeks.  事故使他昏迷数周。|例句：She fell into a deep coma after the surgery.  手术后她陷入深度昏迷。|近义词：unconsciousness, stupor |反义词：consciousness, wakefulness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The patient is in a coma. = 病人处于昏迷状态。
He remains in a coma. = 他仍然处于昏迷状态。

一般过去时:
She went into a coma after the accident. = 她事故后陷入昏迷。
He was in a coma for three days. = 他昏迷了三天。

现在完成时:
She has been in a coma since the accident. = 自从事故以来，她一直处于昏迷状态。
He has remained in a coma for a week. = 他昏迷了一周。

过去完成时:
He had been in a coma for several hours before the doctors arrived. = 在医生到达之前，他已经昏迷了几个小时。
She had fallen into a coma before they could administer treatment. = 在他们能施以治疗之前，她已经陷入昏迷。

将来时:
The doctors hope he will come out of his coma soon. = 医生希望他很快能从昏迷中醒来。
They expect her to remain in a coma for several more days. = 他们预计她还要昏迷几天。

虚拟语气:
If he had not received prompt medical attention, he might still be in a coma. = 如果他没有得到及时的医疗救助，他可能仍然处于昏迷状态。
I wish she would come out of her coma. = 我希望她能从昏迷中醒来。

被动语态:
The patient was kept in a coma-inducing state for the surgery. = 病人被维持在诱导昏迷的状态进行手术。
He was found in a coma by the roadside. = 他被发现在路边处于昏迷状态。

疑问句:
Is the patient still in a coma? = 病人还在昏迷吗？
How long has she been in a coma? = 她昏迷多久了？

否定句:
He is not in a coma; he's just sleeping. = 他没有昏迷；他只是在睡觉。
She did not go into a coma after the operation. = 她手术后没有陷入昏迷。


PHRASE->

coma scale = 昏迷评分
persistent vegetative state (a type of coma) = 持续性植物状态 (一种昏迷)
