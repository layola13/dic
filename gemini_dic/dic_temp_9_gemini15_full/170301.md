proventriculus: /ˌproʊvɛnˈtrɪkjʊləs/ | n. |前胃 |The proventriculus is a glandular stomach in birds and some other animals, situated between the crop and the gizzard. It secretes digestive juices that break down food before it enters the gizzard. | Example:  The bird's proventriculus secretes hydrochloric acid and pepsin to aid digestion. = 鸟的前胃分泌盐酸和胃蛋白酶以帮助消化。|近义词: glandular stomach |反义词: gizzard
