mid-Atlantic: /ˌmɪd ətˈlæntɪk/ | adj. | 中大西洋的；大西洋中部的 | Relating to the central or middle part of the Atlantic Ocean.  The term often refers to a region, geographical area, or specific events related to this central Atlantic area.  | 例句：The ship encountered severe storms in the mid-Atlantic.  这艘船在中大西洋遭遇了严重的暴风雨。

The Bermuda Triangle is located in the mid-Atlantic. 百慕大三角位于中大西洋。


近义词：central Atlantic, transatlantic (in some contexts)


反义词：无直接反义词，可根据语境使用例如：coastal, eastern Atlantic, western Atlantic等

词性：形容词
