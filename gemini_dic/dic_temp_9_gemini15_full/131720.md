nonconferences:/ˌnɑːnˈkɒnfərənsɪz/ | n. |非正式会议；非官方会议 |  A nonconference refers to a sporting event or meeting that is not part of a formally scheduled or organized conference or league.  These events often have less rigid rules and structures than formal conferences. They might be friendlies, exhibition games, or informal gatherings. | Example:  The team played several nonconferences before the start of the regular season.  这个队在常规赛开始前打了几场非正式比赛。 | 近义词: informal meetings, exhibition games, friendlies | 反义词: conferences, official meetings, league games | 名词
