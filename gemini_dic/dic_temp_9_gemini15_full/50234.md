dependance:/dɪˈpɛndəns/ | n. |依赖，依靠；附属国；瘾；(药物)依赖性 | Dependence is a state of relying on someone or something for support, help, or supply.  It can refer to a person's reliance on another, a country's reliance on another for resources, or an addiction to a substance. |  His dependence on his parents was a source of frustration for him. 他对父母的依赖是他沮丧的根源。

The island's economic dependence on tourism is a significant risk.  这个岛屿的经济依赖旅游业是一个巨大的风险。| independence, reliance | independence, self-sufficiency | 名词

词源:
The word "dependence" comes from the Old French word "dependeance," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "dependentia," meaning "hanging down from," "depending on."  The sense of reliance or being contingent upon something else developed over time.
