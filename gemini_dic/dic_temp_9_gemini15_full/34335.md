USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and as such doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".  Adverbs modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs.  It describes *how* something is done.


例句1: The data was analyzed clasificatorily, revealing distinct patterns. 数据被分类地分析，揭示了不同的模式。
例句2: He approached the problem clasificatorily, first identifying the key categories. 他从分类的角度处理这个问题，首先确定了关键类别。


PHRASE->
This word is not typically used in common phrases.  Its usage is highly specialized within scientific or academic contexts dealing with classification systems.
