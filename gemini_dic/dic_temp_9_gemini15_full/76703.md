gambeer:/ɡæmˈbɪər/| n.|儿茶(一种植物提取物)|Gambier is a reddish-brown extract obtained from the leaves and twigs of *Uncaria gambir*, a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia. It's used in tanning leather, dyeing fabrics, and as a medicine.  |例句：The leather was tanned using a mixture of gambier and other tannins. 这皮革是用儿茶和其他单宁混合物鞣制的。|近义词：catechu (a similar extract)|反义词:|
