hamule:/ˈhæmjuːl/| n. |小钩；倒钩；(昆虫的)附钩|A small hook or barb, especially one of a pair on the genitalia of some male insects.  It is often used to grasp the female during mating. |例句：The male insect uses its hamules to hold the female during copulation.雄性昆虫用它的附钩在交配时抓住雌性。The structure of the hamules varies greatly between species. 不同物种的附钩结构差异很大。|近义词：hook, barb, clasp|反义词：N/A
