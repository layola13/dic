conglomeration: /kənˌɡlɒməˈreɪʃən/| n. |集团，集合体；混合物 | A mass or collection of things heaped together.  A conglomerate is a large corporation typically comprising a number of smaller companies, often in unrelated industries. |例句：The city is a conglomeration of different cultures and nationalities.  这座城市是不同文化和民族的大杂烩。  The company's portfolio is a conglomeration of diverse business interests. 这家公司的投资组合是各种不同商业利益的集合。|近义词：aggregate, cluster, mass, mixture |反义词：separation, division, individual
