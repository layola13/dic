quasi-patriarchal:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˌpeɪtriˈɑːrkəl/| adj. |准家长制的；类似家长制的 |  Describes a system or structure that exhibits some, but not all, of the characteristics of a patriarchal system.  It may retain some patriarchal power structures or attitudes, but with significant modifications or deviations.  The term implies an incomplete or imperfect form of patriarchy. | Example:  The company's leadership, while not strictly patriarchal, has a quasi-patriarchal structure with a few powerful men dominating decision-making.  这家公司的领导层虽然并非严格意义上的家长制，但其结构类似家长制，少数几个有权势的男性主导着决策。| 近义词: semi-patriarchal, paternalistic | 反义词: matriarchal, egalitarian
