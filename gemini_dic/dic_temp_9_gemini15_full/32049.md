cherries:/ˈtʃɛriz/| n.|樱桃|The sweet, red or dark-colored fruit of a cherry tree.  Cherries are often eaten fresh, used in desserts, or preserved. |例句：I love the taste of fresh cherries. 我喜欢新鲜樱桃的味道。

例句：We made a cherry pie for dessert. 我们做了樱桃派当甜点。


近义词：berries, stone fruits|反义词：None (no direct opposite)|词性:n.
