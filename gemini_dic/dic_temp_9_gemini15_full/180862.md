rehinged:/ˌriːˈhɪndʒd/| v. |重新装铰链；重新考虑 |To reattach hinges to; to reconsider or refocus.  The door was rehinged after the break-in.  The company rehinged its strategy after the market downturn. |近义词：reinstalled, refitted, reassessed |反义词：removed, detached


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rehinges the cabinet door every year. = 他每年都重新安装橱柜门上的铰链。
They rehinge their marketing strategy regularly. = 他们定期调整他们的营销策略。


一般过去时:
She rehinged the old window. = 她重新安装了旧窗户的铰链。
The government rehinged its economic policy after the crisis. = 危机过后，政府重新调整了其经济政策。


现在完成时:
He has rehinged the gate three times this year. = 今年他已经三次重新安装了大门铰链。
The company has rehinged its approach to customer service. = 公司已经改变了其客户服务的方式。


过去完成时:
They had rehinged the door before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经重新安装了门上的铰链。
The team had rehinged its game plan after the first half. = 在上半场之后，球队已经调整了他们的比赛计划。


将来时:

We will rehinge our business model to adapt to the new market. = 我们将调整我们的商业模式以适应新的市场。


虚拟语气:
