It often involves armed resistance and aims to overthrow or change the existing power structure. |例句：The insurrection was swiftly put down by the government.  这场叛乱很快就被政府镇压了。|例句：The country is facing a potential insurrection due to widespread discontent.  由于民众广泛的不满，这个国家正面临潜在的叛乱。|近义词：rebellion, revolt, uprising, mutiny |反义词：compliance, obedience, submission |名词
