exulting: /ɪɡˈzʌltɪŋ/ | adj. | 狂喜的；兴高采烈的 |  Feeling or expressing great joy.  | 例句：The team was exulting after their victory.  球队在获胜后欣喜若狂。


The children were exulting in their newfound freedom. 孩子们沉浸在他们新获得的自由中。


近义词：joyful, triumphant, jubilant, elated | 反义词：dejected, despondent, downcast, glum


词源:  The word "exulting" comes from the Latin word "exultare," which means "to leap for joy" or "to rejoice greatly."  It combines the prefix "ex-" (out, from) and the verb "saltare" (to leap, to jump).  Therefore, the root meaning involves a physical expression of great joy.
