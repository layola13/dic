词源:
From *matriarch* + *-alism*.  *Matriarch* is derived from Latin *mater* ("mother") + *arch* (from Greek *arkhos*, "ruler"). *-alism* is a suffix indicating a system, principle, or practice.  Therefore, matriarchalism literally means "the system or practice of rule by a mother or mothers."
