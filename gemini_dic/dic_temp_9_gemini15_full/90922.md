hurdler:/ˈhɜːrdlə(r)/| n. |跨栏运动员，跨栏障碍者|A hurdler is an athlete who competes in hurdle races.  Hurdlers must possess speed, agility, and technique to clear the hurdles efficiently.|例句：She is a talented hurdler, consistently placing in the top three.|近义词：obstacle racer, runner|反义词：None specifically.
