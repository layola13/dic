durio:/ˈdʊri.oʊ/| n. |榴莲|The durian is a fruit known for its strong, pungent odor and creamy, custard-like texture.  It is a large, spiky fruit with a hard shell. The taste is often described as sweet and savory, with hints of garlic or onion.  Its aroma is so intense that it is banned from many hotels and public transportation. |The durian fruit is loved by some and hated by others because of its strong smell.|近义词：暂无|反义词：暂无
