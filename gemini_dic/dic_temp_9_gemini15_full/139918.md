odible: /ˈɑːdəbl/ | adj. | 可听的；听得见的 | Audible means able to be heard.  It refers to sounds that are loud enough or clear enough to be perceived by the ear. | Example: The speaker's voice was audible from the back of the room.  扬声器的声音在房间的后面也能听到。  The faint whisper was barely audible. 微弱的耳语几乎听不见。 | 近义词:hearable, perceptible | 反义词:inaudible, unheard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music is audible from here. = 从这里可以听到音乐。
Her voice is audible even from afar. = 即使从远处也能听到她的声音。


一般过去时:
The explosion was audible for miles around. = 爆炸声在几英里外都能听到。
His cough was audible above the music. = 他的咳嗽声盖过了音乐。


现在完成时:
The bird's song has been audible all morning. = 鸟鸣声整个上午都能听到。
The train whistle has been audible since we left the station. = 自从我们离开车站以来，火车汽笛声就一直能听到。


过去完成时:
The phone had been audible before the power went out. = 电话铃声在断电之前还能听到。
The siren had been audible throughout the night. = 警报声整夜都能听到。


将来时:
The concert will be audible from our balcony. = 从我们的阳台上可以听到音乐会的声音。
The fireworks display will be audible for miles. = 烟火表演的声音将会传到几英里外。


虚拟语气:
If the music were audible, we could dance. = 如果音乐能听到，我们就能跳舞。
If the speaker were more audible, I could understand him better. = 如果演讲者声音更大些，我就能更好地理解他。


被动语态:
The announcement was made audible through the loudspeaker. = 通告通过扩音器清晰地播放出来。
The music was made audible by turning up the volume. = 通过调高音量使音乐变得清晰可闻。


疑问句:
Is the speaker audible at the back of the room? = 在房间后面能听到演讲者的声音吗？
Is the baby's cry audible from your room? = 从你的房间能听到婴儿的哭声吗？


否定句:
The radio is not audible at this distance. = 在这个距离，收音机听不见。
Her voice was not audible over the noise of the traffic. = 她的声音被交通噪音盖住了，听不见。

PHRASE->

perfectly audible = 非常清晰可闻
easily audible = 易于听到
