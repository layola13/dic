journals:/ˈdʒɜːrnəlz/| n.|期刊；杂志；日记；日志|A periodical publication containing scholarly articles, research findings, or other professional information.  Journals can be academic, professional, or personal.|例句：She subscribes to several medical journals. 她订阅了几种医学期刊。

例句：He kept a detailed journal of his travels. 他详细记录了他的旅行日志。


近义词：magazines, periodicals, diaries, logs|反义词：None|词性：n.


USAGE->

They publish a new journal every quarter. = 他们每季度出版一本新期刊。
