remonstrative: /rɪˈmɒnstreɪtɪv/ | adj. | 规劝的；抗议的；表示反对的 |

英文解析:  Characterized by or given to remonstrance; expressing protest or objection.  Showing strong disapproval or opposition.


例句:

He gave a remonstrative speech against the new policy. 他发表了一篇反对新政策的演说。

Her remonstrative tone made it clear she disagreed. 她抗议的语气表明了她不同意。


近义词:  protesting, objecting, dissenting, reproachful, expostulatory


反义词:  approving, accepting, consenting, acquiescent


词性: adjective
