diorama: /ˌdaɪəˈrɑːmə/ | n. |立体模型，微缩景观 | A three-dimensional scene, often miniature, created for display.  Dioramas are often used in museums and exhibitions to illustrate historical events, natural habitats, or fictional settings.  They can be made from a variety of materials, including plaster, wood, paint, and miniature figures. | Example: The museum's diorama of a prehistoric swamp was incredibly lifelike.  博物馆的史前沼泽立体模型非常逼真。|近义词：model, miniature, display |反义词：panorama (a wide view), flat representation |词性:名词
