doorstone:/ˈdɔːrstəʊn/| n. |门槛石，门坎石 | A stone forming the threshold of a door.  A doorstone is typically a large, flat stone that is placed at the base of a doorway, often serving both a practical and symbolic purpose.  | Example:  The old doorstone was worn smooth from years of use.  古老的门槛石因多年的使用而磨损光滑。


The doorstone was inscribed with the family's name. 门槛石上刻着家族的名字。| 近义词：threshold, sill | 反义词：None  | 名词
