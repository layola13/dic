lengthener:/ˈlɛŋθənər/| n. |延长物；延长器 |A device or process that increases the length of something.  It can refer to a physical object or a metaphorical process. For example, a lengthener could be a tool used to extend a piece of fabric or a software program that adds more time to a deadline. |例句：The tailor used a lengthener to make the dress longer.  裁缝用延长器把裙子加长了。

The project manager used a software lengthener to give the team more time to complete the project. 项目经理使用软件延长器为团队提供了更多的时间来完成项目。|近义词：extender, prolonger |反义词：shortener, reducer |名词
