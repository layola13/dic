rearm:/ˌriːˈɑːrm/ | vt. |重新武装；再次装备 | To equip (someone or something) again with arms or weapons.  The process of equipping a military force or nation with new weapons and equipment after a period of disarmament or reduced military capability. | 例句：The country is rearm itself after the war. = 这个国家在战争结束后重新武装自己。  The army is currently undergoing a major rearmament program. = 军队目前正在进行一项重大的重新武装计划。 | 近义词：re-equip, re-arm, refit | 反义词：disarm, demilitarize | 动词


USAGE->

The country rearms itself against potential threats. = 国家正在重新武装以应对潜在威胁。
They rearm their troops with advanced weaponry. = 他们用先进武器重新武装部队。
