caddy's:/ˈkædiz/| n.|球童；（高尔夫球场的）球车；茶叶罐；|A caddy is a person who carries a golfer's clubs, or a small cart used for carrying golf clubs.  It can also refer to a container for tea.  The possessive 's indicates ownership or association.  |例句：The caddy carried the golfer's clubs around the course.  球童把高尔夫球手的球杆扛在球场上。The caddy's bag was overflowing with golf balls. 球童的袋子装满了高尔夫球。|近义词：assistant, helper (for the person); cart, trolley (for the vehicle)|反义词：N/A|名词
