reinvigorated:/ˌriːɪnˈvɪɡəreɪtɪd/| adj.| 恢复活力的；重振的；充满活力的 |  Having been given new energy or strength; revitalized.  |例句：The team felt reinvigorated after their victory.  = 团队在胜利后感觉重振旗鼓。

The company's new marketing campaign reinvigorated its brand image. = 公司新的营销活动重振了其品牌形象。


近义词：revitalized, refreshed, renewed, energized, re-energized | 反义词：exhausted, depleted, drained, weakened, enervated | 词性：形容词
