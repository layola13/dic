liturgies:/ˈlɪtərʒiz/| n.|礼仪；仪式；宗教仪式|A liturgy is a form or pattern of public worship.  It typically involves prescribed prayers, hymns, and rituals.  The specific content and order of a liturgy can vary depending on the religious tradition.|例句：The church follows a traditional liturgy for Sunday services. 这座教堂在周日弥撒时遵循传统的礼仪。|例句：Many different liturgies are practiced within Christianity. 基督教内部实行许多不同的礼仪。|近义词：rituals, ceremonies, rites|反义词：improvisation, spontaneity
