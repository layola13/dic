coagulatory:/koʊˈæɡjʊlətɔːri/| adj.|凝固的；促凝的| Relating to or promoting coagulation.  The word describes something that causes or aids in the process of coagulation, the process of changing from a liquid to a solid or semi-solid state.| The coagulatory action of the enzyme helped to stop the bleeding.  酶的凝固作用帮助止血。|近义词：coagulant, clotting |反义词：anticoagulant, thinning
