reconnecting:/ˌriːkəˈnɛktɪŋ/| v. |重新连接，恢复联系 |The act of connecting again after a period of disconnection. It implies re-establishing a connection that was previously lost or broken. This can apply to various contexts, including physical connections (like reconnecting a wire), digital connections (like reconnecting to a Wi-Fi network), or interpersonal connections (like reconnecting with an old friend).|例句：After the storm, the technicians worked hard to reconnect the power lines.  暴风雨过后，技术人员努力重新连接电力线。

例句：It was wonderful reconnecting with my childhood friends after all these years.  多年后与儿时朋友重逢真是太好了。


近义词：reconnect, reunite, reestablish, renew |反义词：disconnect, sever, estrange
