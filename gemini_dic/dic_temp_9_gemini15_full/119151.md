mewing:/ˈmjuːɪŋ/| n. v.|猫叫声；猫叫；发出猫叫声 |The sound a cat makes; the act of a cat making this sound; to make a mewing sound. |例句：The kitten's mewing woke me up. 小猫的叫声把我吵醒了。The cat sat on the windowsill, mewing plaintively. 猫坐在窗台上，可怜巴巴地叫着。|近义词：purring, caterwauling, yowling|反义词：roaring, barking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat mews constantly. = 猫一直在叫。
He mews when he's hungry. = 他饿了的时候就喵喵叫。

一般过去时:
The cat mewed all night. = 猫叫了一整夜。
The kitten mewed softly for its mother. = 小猫轻轻地叫着找妈妈。

现在完成时:
The cat has mewed incessantly since morning. = 猫从早上开始就一直在不停地叫。
The stray cat has mewed at our door for days. = 那只流浪猫几天来一直在我们家门口叫。

过去完成时:
The cat had mewed before I even opened the door. = 我还没开门，猫就已经叫了。
The kitten had mewed pitifully before its owner found it. = 小猫在主人找到它之前可怜巴巴地叫着。

将来时:
The cat will mew again if it's still hungry. = 如果猫还饿的话，它还会再叫。
I think the cat will mew loudly when it sees the food. = 我想猫看到食物的时候会大声叫。

虚拟语气:
If the cat mewed louder, we would have heard it. = 如果猫叫得更大声一点，我们就能听到了。
I wish the cat would stop mewing so much. = 我希望猫别叫那么多了。

被动语态:
(Mewing is rarely used in the passive voice.)  The sound was heard, but it wasn't described as mewing. = 听到了声音，但没有说是猫叫。

疑问句:
Is the cat mewing again? = 猫又在叫了吗？
Why is the kitten mewing so much? = 小猫为什么叫得这么厉害？

否定句:
The cat didn't mew at all last night. = 猫昨晚一点也没叫。
The cat wasn't mewing when I got home. = 我回家的时候猫没叫。


PHRASE->
a plaintive mewing = 一种可怜的猫叫声
a continuous mewing = 持续不断的猫叫声
a soft mewing = 轻柔的猫叫声
a loud mewing = 大声的猫叫声
a hungry mewing = 饿了时的猫叫声
