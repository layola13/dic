lichening:/ˈlaɪkənɪŋ/| v.|地衣化，长满地衣|The process by which a surface becomes covered with lichens.  The verb refers to the gradual growth and spread of lichens on a surface.|Example: The old stone wall was lichening slowly over the years.|近义词：covering with lichen|反义词：removing lichen
