mutinied:/ˈmjuːtɪnid/| v.|哗变；叛变；反抗权威|To rise up against authority, especially in a military context.  It implies a collective act of defiance and rebellion against legitimate command. |例句：The sailors mutinied against the cruel captain. 水手们反抗残暴的船长。|例句：The troops mutinied after months of starvation and mistreatment.  在数月的饥饿和虐待之后，军队哗变了。|近义词：rebelled, revolted, rose up|反义词：obeyed, submitted, complied|动词
