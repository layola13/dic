interconnected:/ˌɪntərˌkəˈnɛktɪd/ | adj. |相互关联的，相互连接的 | Mutually connected or related; forming a network.  The interconnected nature of global markets means that events in one country can quickly affect others. |例句：The interconnectedness of global economies is increasingly apparent. 全球经济的相互关联性越来越明显。

例句：The various systems in the body are interconnected and work together. 人体内的各个系统相互关联并协同工作。


近义词：interlinked, interrelated, interdependent, connected, networked | 反义词：disconnected, isolated, independent, separate | 词性：形容词
