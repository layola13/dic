circumnavigation:/ˌsɜːrkəmˌnævɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |环球航行；环行|The act of sailing or otherwise traveling completely around the world.  It often implies a journey by sea, but can refer to any mode of transport. |例句：Magellan's circumnavigation of the globe was a monumental achievement. 麦哲伦环球航行是一项了不起的成就。

例句：The first successful circumnavigation by air was completed in 1924.  1924年完成了首次成功的环球飞行。


近义词：global voyage, world tour, complete circuit


反义词：local journey, short trip


词性：名词
