pressure-cooker: /ˈprɛʃərˌkʊkər/ | n. |高压锅| A pressure cooker is a sealed pot used for cooking food at a higher pressure than atmospheric pressure. This higher pressure raises the boiling point of water, allowing food to cook faster and at a higher temperature.  It's often used to cook tough meats or beans quickly and efficiently. |Example:  She used a pressure cooker to make delicious, tender beef stew in under an hour. = 她用高压锅在不到一个小时内做出了美味嫩滑的牛肉炖菜。

Example: I prefer using a pressure cooker because it significantly reduces cooking time. = 我更喜欢用高压锅，因为它能大大缩短烹饪时间。


近义词：fast cooker, pressure pot

反义词：conventional pot, slow cooker

词源:
