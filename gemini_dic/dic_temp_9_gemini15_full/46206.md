cyrillic:/sɪˈrɪlɪk/| n.|西里尔字母|The Cyrillic alphabet is a writing system used for many Slavic languages, including Russian, Ukrainian, and Bulgarian. It is named after Saint Cyril, who is credited with its creation in the 9th century.  |Example:  Many languages use the Cyrillic alphabet.  许多语言使用西里尔字母。|近义词：Slavic alphabet |反义词:Latin alphabet
