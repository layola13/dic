hydropiper: /ˌhaɪdrəˈpaɪpər/ | n. |辣蓼属植物；水胡椒|

英文解析：Hydropiper is a genus of herbaceous plants in the family Polygonaceae, commonly known as water pepper.  They are characterized by their pungent, peppery taste and are often found in wet habitats.  Different species within the genus may vary in appearance and specific properties.
