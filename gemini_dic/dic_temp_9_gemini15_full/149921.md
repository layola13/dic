palleting:/ˈpælɪtɪŋ/| n. |托盘装载；码垛；货盘堆放|The process of arranging goods onto pallets for storage, transportation, or shipment.  This involves stacking items in a stable and efficient manner to optimize space and prevent damage. |例句：The palleting of the boxes was completed before the truck arrived. 箱子已完成码垛，卡车即将到达。
The efficient palleting system reduced shipping costs significantly. 高效的码垛系统大大降低了运输成本。|近义词：palletizing, stacking, loading|反义词：unloading, unstacking|名词
