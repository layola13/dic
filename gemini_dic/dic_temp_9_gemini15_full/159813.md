polygram:/ˈpɒliɡræm/| n. |多音节词；多音符；多图记录|A polygram is a word or symbol formed from multiple sounds or images. It can refer to a word with many syllables, a musical notation with multiple notes, or a record containing multiple images.  |Example:  The composer created a complex polygram for the orchestra.  作曲家为管弦乐队创作了一个复杂的复音。|近义词:multigram, compound word |反义词:monogram, single note
