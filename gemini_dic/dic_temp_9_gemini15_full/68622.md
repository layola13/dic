fantasying:/ˈfæn.tə.saɪ.zɪŋ/| v.|幻想；梦想；沉溺于幻想|The act of indulging in fantasy; imagining or dreaming about something.  It often implies a playful or escapist element. |例句：She spent the afternoon fantasying about her upcoming vacation. 她一下午都在幻想即将到来的假期。

例句：He was fantasying about winning the lottery. 他在幻想中彩。


近义词：daydreaming, dreaming, imagining, woolgathering

反义词：facing reality, being pragmatic

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am fantasying about my next adventure. = 我正在幻想我的下一个冒险。
They are fantasying about a life in the tropics. = 他们正在幻想在热带地区的生活。


一般过去时:
I fantasied about becoming a famous writer. = 我曾经幻想成为一名著名的作家。
She fantasied about living in a castle. = 她曾经幻想住在城堡里。


现在完成时:
I have fantasied about this moment for years. = 我多年来一直幻想这一刻。
They have fantasied about traveling the world. = 他们一直幻想周游世界。


过去完成时:
I had fantasied about meeting him before we actually met. = 在我们真正见面之前，我一直幻想过见到他。
She had fantasied about this day for her entire life. = 她一生都在幻想这一天。


将来时:
I will be fantasying about my vacation all week. = 我整个星期都会在幻想我的假期。
They will fantasize about their future careers. = 他们将幻想他们未来的职业。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would fantasize more often. = 如果我有时间，我会更经常地幻想。
I wish I could fantasize as freely as I used to. = 我希望我能像以前一样自由地幻想。


被动语态: (被动语态用得较少，因为fantasize通常是主动行为)
His ideas were often fantasized about by his colleagues. = 他的想法经常被他的同事们幻想。 (比较生硬)
The possibilities were fantasized about extensively. = 这些可能性被广泛地幻想过。(比较生硬)


疑问句:
Are you fantasying again? = 你又在幻想了吗？
What are you fantasying about? = 你在幻想什么？


否定句:
I don't fantasize very often. = 我不太经常幻想。
She doesn't fantasize about unrealistic things. = 她不会幻想不切实际的事情。


PHRASE->
fantasize about: 幻想……
fantasize away:  幻想逃避现实
fantasize oneself: 自我幻想
live in a fantasy: 生活在幻想中
lost in fantasy: 迷失在幻想中
