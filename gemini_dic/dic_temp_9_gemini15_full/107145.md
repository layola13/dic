laemmergeyer:/ˈlæmərˌɡaɪər/| n. |胡兀鹫|A laemmergeyer is a large bird of prey found in mountainous regions of Europe, Asia, and Africa.  It is known for its distinctive appearance and its ability to break bones to feed on the marrow of its prey.|The laemmergeyer soared effortlessly above the jagged peaks.  胡兀鹫轻而易举地翱翔在崎岖的山峰之上。|近义词：bearded vulture (though this is more common)|反义词：None specifically, but terms like "small bird" or "bird of paradise" would be contrasting.|名词
