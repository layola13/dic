preplans:/ˈpriːplænz/| n.|预先计划，预案|Plans made in advance.  These plans often involve anticipating potential problems and developing solutions beforehand.|例句：The company's preplans for the product launch included a detailed marketing strategy and contingency plans for unexpected issues. 公司的产品发布预案包括详细的营销策略和意外问题的应急计划。|近义词：advance planning, preparations, contingency plans|反义词：improvisation, spontaneity
