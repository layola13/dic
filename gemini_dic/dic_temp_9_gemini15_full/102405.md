jacana:/ˈdʒækənə/| n. |水雉|A jacana is a bird of the family Jacanidae, characterized by their long toes and ability to walk on floating vegetation.|例句：The jacana delicately walked across the lily pads.  这只水雉优雅地走在睡莲叶子上。|近义词：water hen (部分种类)|反义词:无
