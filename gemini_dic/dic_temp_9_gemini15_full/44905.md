cryophorus:/kraɪˈfɒrəs/| n. |寒暑表，结冰器 | A cryophorus is a simple device used to demonstrate the principles of heat transfer, specifically latent heat and vapor pressure.  It consists of two bulbs connected by a tube. When one bulb is cooled, the vapor pressure in the system causes the liquid in the other bulb to freeze. |Example: The physics professor used a cryophorus to illustrate the concept of latent heat.  物理教授用寒暑表来说明潜热的概念。|近义词：thermometer (in the context of demonstrating temperature changes)  |反义词：N/A
