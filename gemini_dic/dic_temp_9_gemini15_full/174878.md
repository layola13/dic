racketeering:/ˌrækiˈtɪərɪŋ/| n.|敲诈勒索；敲诈性犯罪；欺诈活动|The illegal practice of obtaining money or other benefits through intimidation or threats, often involving organized crime.|例句：The police launched a major investigation into racketeering activities in the city.  警方对该市的黑社会活动展开了大规模调查。


例句：He was convicted of racketeering and sentenced to ten years in prison. 他被判犯有敲诈勒索罪，判处十年监禁。


近义词：extortion, blackmail, fraud, organized crime


反义词：legitimate business, honest dealings


词性:名词
