precertifies: /ˌpriːˈsɜːrtɪfaɪz/ | v. | 预先认证，预先证明合格 | To certify or approve something in advance.  This word is a relatively rare and specialized term, often used in contexts requiring formal advance approval or verification.  It implies a process of certification that happens *before* some other action or event.


例句：

The committee precertifies all applicants before they are allowed to participate in the program.  委员会在允许申请者参加项目之前会预先审核所有申请者。

The bank precertifies loans based on preliminary financial information.  银行根据初步的财务信息预先审批贷款。


近义词：preapprove, prequalify, verify in advance


反义词：post-certify (this word is not commonly used)


词性：动词
