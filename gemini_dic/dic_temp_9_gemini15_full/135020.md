nonnegotiation:/ˌnɒn.nɪˌɡoʊʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 不可谈判；不让步 | The refusal to negotiate or compromise.  It implies a firm and unyielding stance.  | Example: The company's nonnegotiation on wages led to a strike. 公司在工资问题上不让步导致了罢工。 | 近义词：intransigence, inflexibility, firmness | 反义词：negotiation, compromise, flexibility
