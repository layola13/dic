crouching: /ˈkraʊtʃɪŋ/ | v. adj. | 蹲伏；弯腰；蜷缩 |  The act of bending low with the knees bent;  a low posture assumed by a person or animal. | 例句：The cat was crouching in the bushes.  猫躲在灌木丛中蹲着。

The tiger crouched low to the ground before pouncing. 老虎在猛扑之前低伏在地。


近义词：squatting, stooping, bending, hunkering


反义词：standing, erect, upright


词性：动词（现在分词）和形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is crouching behind the tree. 他正躲在树后蹲着。
The lion is crouching, ready to spring.  狮子正蹲着，准备猛扑。


一般过去时:
She crouched down to examine the flower. 她蹲下身子检查花朵。
They crouched behind the wall for cover.  他们躲在墙后寻求掩护。


现在完成时:
He has been crouching for hours. 他已经蹲了好几个小时了。
The dog has crouched there all day. 这只狗整天都蹲在那里。


过去完成时:
She had crouched behind the rock before the explosion. 爆炸前她已经躲在岩石后面了。
The soldiers had crouched low to avoid being seen.  士兵们已经低伏着以免被发现。


将来时:
He will crouch down to tie his shoelaces. 他将蹲下系鞋带。
The cat will crouch and wait for its prey. 猫会蹲下等待猎物。


虚拟语气:
If I were a cat, I would crouch and wait. 如果我是一只猫，我会蹲下等待。
If he had crouched lower, he wouldn't have been seen. 如果他蹲得更低，就不会被发现了。


被动语态:
The figure was seen crouching in the shadows.  人们看到一个人影在阴影中蹲着。
The position was held by crouching behind a boulder.  这个位置是通过躲在一块巨石后面蹲伏来保持的。


疑问句:
Is he crouching? 他正在蹲着吗？
Are the children crouching down? 孩子们正在蹲下吗？


否定句:
He isn't crouching. 他没有蹲着。
They didn't crouch down. 他们没有蹲下。

词源:
The word "crouching" comes from the Middle English word "croken," which meant to "bend" or "crook."  This in turn likely has roots in Proto-Germanic, though the exact origin is debated among etymologists.  The modern English word developed through various stages of evolution in the language.
