quarries:/ˈkwɒriz/ /ˈkwɔːriz/| n.|采石场；采石业|A quarry is a place where stone, especially for building, is dug from the ground.|例句：The marble used in the building came from Italian quarries.  这座建筑使用的白色大理石来自意大利的采石场。|例句：Many quarries have been abandoned, leaving behind scarred landscapes. 许多采石场已被废弃，留下伤痕累累的景观。|近义词：mine, pit|反义词:N/A
