frustrates:/frʌˈstreɪts/| v.|使沮丧；使灰心；阻挠；使受挫|To cause (someone) to feel frustrated; to hinder or prevent (someone) from doing something.|例句：The constant traffic frustrates me.  =  不断的交通堵塞让我很沮丧。
例句：His attempts to climb the mountain were frustrated by bad weather. = 他攀登这座山的尝试因恶劣天气而受挫。|近义词：disappoints, thwarts, hinders, discourages|反义词：encourages, motivates, assists, helps|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The constant noise frustrates me. = 持续的噪音让我很沮丧。
This problem frustrates many people. = 这个问题让很多人感到沮丧。

一般过去时:
The delay frustrated our plans. = 延误打乱了我们的计划。
The bad weather frustrated their efforts. = 恶劣的天气挫败了他们的努力。

现在完成时:
The project has frustrated us for months. = 这个项目已经让我们沮丧了好几个月了。
Nothing has frustrated him more than this. = 没有什么比这更让他沮丧的了。

过去完成时:
He had been frustrated by numerous setbacks. = 他曾多次受挫。
We had already been frustrated by the lack of progress. = 我们已经被缺乏进展而感到沮丧了。

将来时:
The new regulations will frustrate many businesses. = 新规将使许多企业受挫。
This will frustrate our attempts to finish on time. = 这将阻碍我们按时完成的尝试。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I wouldn't be so frustrated. = 如果我有更多时间，我就不会这么沮丧了。
It would frustrate me if they didn't follow instructions. = 如果他们不按照指示去做，我会很沮丧。

被动语态:
His efforts were frustrated by a lack of funding. = 他的努力因缺乏资金而受阻。
The negotiations were frustrated by the conflicting interests. = 谈判因利益冲突而破裂。

疑问句:
What frustrates you the most about this job? = 你对这份工作最不满的是什么？
Does the complexity of the task frustrate you? = 任务的复杂性是否让你感到沮丧？

否定句:
The good weather didn't frustrate their plans. = 好天气没有打乱他们的计划。
The simple instructions didn't frustrate anyone. = 简洁的说明并没有让任何人感到沮丧。


PHRASE->
