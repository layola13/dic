foregut:/ˈfɔːrɡʌt/| n. |前肠|The foregut is the anterior part of the primitive gut tube in an embryo, which develops into several important organs of the digestive system.|The foregut gives rise to the esophagus, stomach, liver, pancreas, and parts of the duodenum.|近义词：primitive gut (embryonic)|反义词：hindgut, midgut
