outlengthens:/ˌaʊtˈlɛŋθənz/| v.|超过长度；比…长；延长超过|To exceed in length; to surpass in duration.|The vine outlengthens the supporting trellis.  藤蔓超过了支撑架的长度。

The project outlengthens our initial estimates. 项目的工期超过了我们最初的预估。


近义词：outlast, exceed, surpass, prolong

反义词：shorten, abbreviate
