craftily:/ˈkræftɪli/| adv.|巧妙地；狡猾地；偷偷摸摸地|In a clever and skillful way, often in a deceitful or secretive manner.  It implies a degree of cunning or skill in achieving a goal, often by underhanded means.|例句：He craftily avoided answering the question. 他巧妙地避开了这个问题。
She craftily manipulated the situation to her advantage. 她巧妙地操纵局势以利于自己。|近义词：skillfully, cleverly, cunningly, slyly, subtly, deviously|反义词：openly, honestly, frankly, clumsily

词源:
