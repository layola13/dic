nattiness:/ˈnætɪnəs/| n. |整洁；雅致；利落|Neatness and smartness in appearance or style; elegance.|His nattiness of dress was always admired. 他的穿着打扮总是很受人称赞。|neatness, smartness, elegance|sloppiness, shabbiness, untidiness|名词


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.

例句1: The nattiness of his attire impressed everyone at the party.  他衣着整洁，给参加派对的每个人留下了深刻印象。

例句2:  Her nattiness belied her years; she looked much younger than she was. 她的穿着打扮显得她比实际年龄年轻得多。
