decrying:/dɪˈkraɪɪŋ/| v.|公开谴责；强烈批评|Publicly expressing strong disapproval of; condemning.|例句：Many people are decrying the government's new policies. 许多人公开谴责政府的新政策。|例句：She spent the whole speech decrying the lack of funding for education.她在整个演讲中强烈批评教育经费不足。|近义词：condemning, denouncing, criticizing, reproaching|反义词：praising, commending, applauding|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He decries the injustice of the system. 他谴责这个制度的不公正。
They decry the rising cost of living. 他们谴责不断上涨的生活成本。

一般过去时:
She decried the politician's lies. 她谴责这位政治家的谎言。
The protesters decried the war. 抗议者谴责这场战争。

现在完成时:
Activists have decried the environmental damage. 活动家们一直谴责环境破坏。
We have decried the lack of transparency for years.  多年来，我们一直谴责缺乏透明度。

过去完成时:
They had decried the decision before the vote. 他们在投票前就谴责了这个决定。
She had decried the company's unethical practices for months. 她几个月来一直谴责公司的违反道德的行为。

将来时:
He will decry the new law in his upcoming speech. 他将在即将发表的演讲中谴责这项新法律。
They will decry the violence against civilians. 他们将谴责针对平民的暴力行为。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would decry such actions. 如果我是你，我会谴责这种行为。
I wish they would decry the discrimination. 我希望他们能谴责这种歧视。

被动语态:
The government's policies were decried by many. 政府的政策受到许多人的谴责。
The decision was decried as unfair.  这个决定被谴责为不公平。

疑问句:
Why are they decrying the proposed changes? 他们为什么谴责这些拟议的改变？
Does he often decry social injustice? 他经常谴责社会不公吗？

否定句:
He did not decry the government's efforts. 他没有谴责政府的努力。
She did not decry the importance of education. 她没有否认教育的重要性。

词源:
The word "decry" comes from the Old French word "decrier," which means "to cry down," "to exclaim against," or "to denounce."  The prefix "de-" means "down" or "away," and the root "crier" means "to cry" or "to shout."  Therefore, the word literally means "to cry down" or "to shout down."
