out-mastered:/ˌaʊtˈmɑːstəd/| v.|胜过，技高一筹| To surpass or exceed in skill or ability.  It implies a significant degree of mastery over the opponent or subject. |例句：He completely out-mastered his opponent in the chess match. 他在国际象棋比赛中完全击败了他的对手。
例句：After years of practice, she out-mastered the challenging piano piece.  经过多年的练习，她最终征服了这首难度很高的钢琴曲。|近义词：surpass, excel, outdo, defeat |反义词：be defeated by, be surpassed by
