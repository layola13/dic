fat-free: /ˌfætˈfriː/ | adj. | 脱脂的，不含脂肪的 |  Containing no fat or very little fat.  This adjective is used to describe food and products that have had the fat removed or are naturally low in fat. | 例句：This yogurt is fat-free and low in sugar. 这款酸奶是脱脂的，而且低糖。  Many people choose fat-free milk to reduce their calorie intake. 许多人选择脱脂牛奶来减少卡路里摄入。 | 近义词：low-fat, lean | 反义词：high-fat, fatty |  词性：形容词
