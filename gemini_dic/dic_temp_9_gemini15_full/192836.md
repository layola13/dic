searches:/ˈsɜːrtʃɪz/ | n. | 搜索；查找；探求 | The act of looking for something, typically information.  It can refer to multiple instances of searching or the results of those searches. |例句：My searches yielded no relevant results. 我的搜索没有产生任何相关结果。

例句：The police searches for the missing child continues.  警方对失踪儿童的搜寻仍在继续。


近义词：investigations, explorations, inquiries | 反义词： discoveries (in the sense of finding something after searching) | 词性：名词


USAGE->

The system performs many searches every second. = 系统每秒执行许多搜索。
Researchers conduct regular literature searches. = 研究人员定期进行文献检索。
