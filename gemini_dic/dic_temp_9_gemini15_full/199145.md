silhouettists:/ˌsɪluˈɛtɪsts/| n. |剪影艺术家，轮廓画家|A silhouettist is an artist who creates silhouettes, which are images showing the outline of something, especially a person, against a light background.|例句：The silhouettist skillfully captured the dancer's graceful movements in a single, elegant silhouette.  剪影艺术家巧妙地将舞者的优雅动作捕捉在一个优雅的剪影中。 The exhibition featured the works of several renowned silhouettists from across the country. 展览展出了来自全国各地几位著名剪影艺术家的作品。|近义词：profile artist, shadow artist |反义词：无
