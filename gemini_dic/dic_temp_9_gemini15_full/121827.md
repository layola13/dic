misrelation:/ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪʃən/| n. |错误的关系；不当的联系；误解|A misrelation is an incorrect or inappropriate relationship between things.  It can refer to a misunderstanding, a faulty connection, or a distorted representation of a relationship.  The term often implies a lack of proper understanding or a flawed connection.|例句：The study revealed a misrelation between the predicted and the actual results.  这项研究揭示了预测结果和实际结果之间的错误关系。


The misrelation of facts led to a flawed conclusion. 事实的错误联系导致了错误的结论。|近义词：misunderstanding, misinterpretation, distortion, fallacy|反义词：correct relation, accurate understanding, proper connection
