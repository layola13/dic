commemorations:/ˌkəˌmɛməˈreɪʃənz/| n. |纪念活动，庆祝活动 |The act of commemorating; a ceremony or observance in remembrance of a person or event.  Commemorations can range from simple acts of remembrance to large-scale public events.|例句：The city held a series of commemorations to mark the 50th anniversary of the war.  这座城市举行了一系列纪念活动以纪念战争50周年。

例句：The commemorations for the fallen soldiers were deeply moving.  为阵亡士兵举行的纪念活动令人深受感动。


近义词：celebrations, observances, tributes |反义词：forgetfulness, neglect |词性：名词
