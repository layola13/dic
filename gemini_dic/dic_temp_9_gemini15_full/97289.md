influxes:/ˈɪnflʌksɪz/ | n. | 大量涌入；蜂拥而至 | The influx of tourists overwhelmed the small town.  游客的大量涌入使这个小镇不堪重负。|  The sudden influx of refugees strained resources. 难民的突然涌入使资源紧张。|近义词：inrush, inflow, flood, surge | 反义词：outflow, exodus, depletion | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
A large influx of new students occurs every September. = 每年九月都会有大量新生涌入。
The city experiences a constant influx of immigrants. = 这个城市不断涌入移民。


一般过去时:
A massive influx of capital occurred after the announcement. = 公告发布后，大量资金涌入。
The company witnessed a significant influx of orders last quarter. = 上季度公司订单大量涌入。


现在完成时:
There has been a steady influx of investment into the renewable energy sector. = 可再生能源领域一直有稳定的投资涌入。
The country has experienced a dramatic influx of refugees in recent years. = 近年来，该国经历了难民的大量涌入。


过去完成时:
Before the festival, there had been a large influx of tourists to the city. = 节日前，大量游客涌入该城市。
By the end of the year, the company had seen a substantial influx of new employees. = 到年底，公司已经涌入了大量新员工。


将来时:
An influx of tourists is expected during the summer months. = 夏季预计会有大量游客涌入。
We anticipate a significant influx of applications for the program next year. = 我们预计明年该项目申请数量将大幅增加。


虚拟语气:
If there were a sudden influx of funds, the project could be completed on time. = 如果资金突然涌入，项目就能按时完成。
I wish there were a larger influx of young people into the rural areas. = 我希望农村地区有更多年轻人涌入。


被动语态:
The small town was overwhelmed by the influx of tourists. = 小镇被涌入的游客压垮了。
The system was nearly crashed by the unexpected influx of data. = 系统差点因意外的数据涌入而崩溃。


疑问句:
What caused the sudden influx of visitors? = 导致游客突然涌入的原因是什么？
Will there be a large influx of applications for the job? = 这份工作的申请会有很多吗？


否定句:
There was no significant influx of new members this year. = 今年没有大量新成员加入。
The company didn't experience an influx of orders as they had predicted. = 公司的订单没有像他们预测的那样大量涌入。



PHRASE->

a sudden influx = 突然涌入
a massive influx = 大量涌入
a steady influx = 稳定涌入
a significant influx = 大量涌入
an influx of refugees = 难民涌入


词源:

Influx源于拉丁语词根"in-" (进入) 和 "fluxus" (流动)。  "Fluxus"  本身也与现代英语单词"flux" (流动，变化)相关。因此，influx 字面意思就是“流入”。
