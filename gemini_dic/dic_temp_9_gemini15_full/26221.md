butterfly: /ˈbʌtərˌflaɪ/ | n. | 蝴蝶 | A flying insect with large, often brightly coloured wings.  Butterflies are known for their metamorphosis, transforming from a caterpillar to a pupa and finally to a winged adult.  They are often associated with beauty and transformation. | Example: The butterfly landed gently on the flower.  这只蝴蝶轻轻地落在花上。  The children chased the butterflies in the meadow. 孩子们在草地上追逐蝴蝶。| 近义词：moth, insect | 反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑与毛毛虫相对) caterpillar
