resourceful:/rɪˈsɔːrsfl/| adj. |足智多谋的；机智的； resourceful |善于利用资源的；足智多谋的；富有创造力的|英文解析: able to find clever ways to overcome difficulties; having the ability to invent clever solutions to problems.|例句：She's a very resourceful person and always finds a way to get what she needs.|She was resourceful enough to fix the problem with limited tools.|近义词：inventive, ingenious, imaginative, creative|反义词：unimaginative, uninspired, inept
