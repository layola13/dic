echoes:/ˈekəʊz/ | n. |回声；回响；余音；影响；迹象 | The sound or sounds that are heard after a sound is made, caused by the reflection of sound waves.  It can also refer to the lingering effects or reminders of something. |例句：The echoes of the gunshot still rang in my ears.  枪声的回响仍然在我的耳边回荡。

例句：The echoes of the past influenced his decisions.  过去的回响影响了他的决定。


近义词：repercussions, reverberations, reflections, aftereffects | 反义词：silence, stillness


词源：来自古法语 *eco,  来自中古拉丁语 *echo,  来自希腊语 ἠχώ (ēchō) “回声”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The canyon echoes with the sound of birds. = 峡谷回荡着鸟鸣声。
The event echoes throughout history. = 这事件在历史上引起广泛反响。


一般过去时:
The stadium echoed with cheers. = 体育场回荡着欢呼声。
Her laughter echoed in the empty room. = 她的笑声在空荡的房间里回响。


现在完成时:
The scandal has echoed across the nation. = 这丑闻在全国引起反响。
The impact of his work has echoed through generations. = 他作品的影响已经延续了好几代。


过去完成时:
The news had echoed throughout the village before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，这个消息已经在村子里传遍了。
The teacher's words had echoed in my mind for days. = 老师的话在我脑子里回响了好几天。


将来时:
The concert will echo through the night. = 音乐会的声音将回响整夜。
His bravery will echo in the hearts of many. = 他的勇气将会在许多人心目中产生回响。


虚拟语气:
If only the music could echo forever. = 但愿这音乐能永远回荡。
I wish my voice could echo across the world. = 我希望我的声音能传遍全世界。


被动语态:
The message was echoed by the media. = 这个信息被媒体广泛报道。
His ideas were echoed by many experts. = 他的观点得到了许多专家的认同。


疑问句:
Do the sounds echo in this hall? = 这个大厅的声音会回响吗？
Does your success echo your hard work? = 你的成功是否反映了你的努力？


否定句:
The mountains did not echo back the sound. = 山峦没有回响声音。
Her words did not echo the sentiments of the group. = 她的言辞并未反映该群体的观点。
