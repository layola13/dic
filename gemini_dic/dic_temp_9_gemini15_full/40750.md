convicinity:/kənˈvɪksɪti/ | n. |附近，邻近 |The state or quality of being in close proximity; nearness.  It often refers to a geographical area surrounding a specific point or location. |例句：The police searched the convicts vicinity for clues.  警方在罪犯附近搜寻线索。 |例句：He lived in the vicinity of the park. 他住在公园附近。|近义词：proximity, neighborhood, vicinity, area |反义词：distance, remoteness
