reactivation:/ˌriːˌæktɪˈveɪʃən/| n. |重新激活；再激活；恢复活动|The process of reactivating something that was inactive or dormant.  This can refer to a physical process, like restarting a machine, or a more abstract process, like reigniting an interest or relationship. |例句：The reactivation of the dormant volcano caused widespread panic.  重新激活休眠的火山引起了广泛的恐慌。

The reactivation of his old social media account surprised his friends. 他重新激活了他旧的社交媒体账号，这让他的朋友们很惊讶。|近义词：revival, reinstatement, resumption |反义词：deactivation, cessation, termination
