reclassification:/ˌriːklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |重新分类；重新归类 |The act of classifying something again, often with a different system or criteria.  It involves reassigning items or data to new categories based on updated information or a change in perspective. |例句：The reclassification of the tax code led to confusion among taxpayers.  重新分类的税法导致纳税人困惑。

The company's reclassification of its assets resulted in a significant change to its financial statements. 公司对其资产的重新分类导致其财务报表发生了重大变化。|近义词：reorganization, recategorization, reassignment |反义词：original classification, initial categorization |名词
