independents:/ˌɪndɪˈpendənts/ | n. | 独立人士，独立组织 | People or organizations that are independent.  They are not controlled by or affiliated with any larger group or authority.  | 例句：The independents in parliament often hold the balance of power.  议会中的独立人士通常掌握着权力平衡。

例句：Many small businesses are independents, competing with larger corporations. 许多小型企业都是独立经营的，与大型公司竞争。


近义词：autonomous entities, self-governing bodies, freelancers

反义词：dependents, subsidiaries, affiliates


词性：名词
