outhires:/ˈaʊtˌhaɪərz/ | v. | 外包；雇佣外部人员 | To hire an outside company or individual to perform work or services that would normally be done by in-house employees.  |  The company outhires its IT support to a specialized firm. 公司将其IT支持外包给了一家专业公司。| contract, subcontract, outsource | in-house, employ directly | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company outhires its cleaning services. = 公司将清洁服务外包出去。
They outhire freelance writers for their blog posts. = 他们雇佣自由撰稿人为他们的博客写文章。

一般过去时:
We outhired a marketing agency last year. = 我们去年外包了一家营销公司。
The project was completed because they outhired additional staff. = 项目得以完成是因为他们额外雇佣了员工。

现在完成时:
The company has outhired several consultants this year. = 公司今年已经外包了几位顾问。
They have outhired a design team to work on their new website. = 他们已经外包了一个设计团队来设计他们的新网站。

过去完成时:
They had outhired a security firm before the event. = 他们在活动之前已经外包了一家安全公司。
The company had outhired a lawyer to handle the legal dispute. = 公司已经雇佣了一名律师来处理法律纠纷。

将来时:
We will outhire a developer to build our new app. = 我们将外包一个开发者来开发我们的新应用。
They will outhire a catering company for the conference. = 他们将为会议外包一家餐饮公司。

虚拟语气:
If we needed more help, we would outhire additional staff. = 如果我们需要更多帮助，我们会额外雇佣员工。
It would have been better if they had outhired a professional translator. = 如果他们当时外包给一个专业的翻译就好了。

被动语态:
The project was outhired to a smaller firm. = 该项目外包给了一家规模较小的公司。
Additional workers will be outhired to meet the deadline. = 将会额外雇佣员工以满足截止日期。

疑问句:
Will they outhire a consultant for this project? = 他们会为这个项目外包一位顾问吗？
Have you ever outhired a cleaning service before? = 你以前曾经外包过清洁服务吗？

否定句:
We didn't outhire any extra help for the project. = 我们没有为这个项目额外雇佣任何帮助。
The company decided not to outhire a marketing firm. = 公司决定不外包营销公司。
