pre-agreed:/ˌpriːəˈɡriːd/| adj.| 预先商定的，预先约定好的 |  Describes something that has been agreed upon before a particular event or action takes place. It implies a prior understanding or arrangement. | 例句：The pre-agreed meeting time was 10 am. 预先商定的会议时间是上午10点。  The terms of the contract were pre-agreed by both parties. 合同条款是双方预先商定的。|近义词：predetermined, prearranged, settled, stipulated | 反义词：unplanned, impromptu, unexpected |  词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The terms are pre-agreed. = 条款是预先商定的。
This price is pre-agreed with the supplier. = 这个价格是与供应商预先商定的。


一般过去时:
The schedule was pre-agreed last week. = 时间表是上周预先商定的。
The payment terms were pre-agreed before the project started. = 项目开始前付款条件已预先商定。


现在完成时:

We have pre-agreed on the delivery date. = 我们已经预先商定了交货日期。


过去完成时:
