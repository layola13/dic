nonbeneficed:/ˌnɒnˈbenɪfɪst/| adj. | 无俸禄的；无收益的 | Not receiving a benefice (an endowed church position providing income).  This term typically refers to clergy who do not receive income from a specific church or parish. | Example:  He served as a nonbeneficed curate for several years before receiving a parish of his own. 他在获得自己的教区之前，做了几年无俸禄的助理牧师。 | 近义词：unbeneficed, unpaid, unendowed | 反义词：beneficed, salaried, endowed
