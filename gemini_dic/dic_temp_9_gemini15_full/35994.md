codeveloper:/koʊˈdɛvəlʌpər/| n. | 共同开发者，合作开发者 | A codeveloper is a person who works with others to develop something, typically software or a project.  They share responsibilities and collaborate throughout the development process. | Example:  The two companies acted as codevelopers on the new mobile game.  两个公司作为共同开发者参与了这款新的手机游戏的开发。 |近义词：collaborator, partner, joint developer | 反义词：sole developer, independent developer |名词
