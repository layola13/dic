misconnection:/ˌmɪskəˈnɛkʃən/| n. |错误连接，误连，理解偏差|A misconnection is a failure to connect correctly, often resulting in a misunderstanding or malfunction. It can refer to physical connections (like wires or pipes) or conceptual connections (like ideas or communication).|例句：The power outage was caused by a misconnection in the wiring. 断电是由线路连接错误造成的。

The misconnection between the two departments led to duplicated efforts. 两个部门之间的沟通不畅导致了重复的工作。|近义词：malfunction, fault, error, misunderstanding|反义词：connection, proper connection, understanding
