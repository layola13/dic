molests:/məˈlestz/| vt.| 骚扰；猥亵；侵犯 | To annoy or harass someone, especially sexually.  It often implies a violation of someone's personal space or boundaries, and can range from minor annoyances to serious sexual abuse. | 例句：He molested the young girl. 他猥亵了那个小女孩。 | 例句：The bully molested the smaller children on the playground. 那个恶霸在操场上骚扰那些年纪更小的孩子。| 近义词：harass, abuse, assault, violate | 反义词：protect, defend, comfort
