despot:/ˈdɛspət/| n. |暴君，专制统治者 |A despot is a ruler with absolute power, often oppressive and tyrannical.  They rule without regard for the rights or welfare of their people.|例句：The despot ruled with an iron fist, suppressing any dissent.|近义词：tyrant, autocrat, dictator |反义词：democrat, benevolent ruler
