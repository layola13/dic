miserability:/ˌmɪzəˈræbəlɪti/| n. |痛苦；悲惨；不幸|The state or feeling of being miserable; wretchedness; unhappiness.  It often refers to a prolonged state of unhappiness, rather than a fleeting feeling.|例句：His miserability was evident in his slumped posture and downcast eyes.  他的痛苦从他佝偻的姿势和低垂的眼睛中显而易见。


例句：The sheer miserability of the situation weighed heavily on her.  情况的极度悲惨让她感到沉重不堪。


近义词：despair, wretchedness, unhappiness, distress, misery


反义词：happiness, joy, bliss, contentment, felicity


词性:名词
