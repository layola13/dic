lunkheaded:/ˈlʌŋkˌhedɪd/| adj. |笨拙的；愚笨的；迟钝的 |  Describes someone who is stupid, clumsy, or slow-witted. It implies a lack of intelligence and often a lack of coordination.  |Example: He made a lunkheaded mistake that cost the company thousands of dollars. 他犯了一个愚蠢的错误，导致公司损失了数千美元。|近义词：stupid, clumsy, obtuse, doltish, thickheaded |反义词：clever, intelligent, bright, astute, sharp
