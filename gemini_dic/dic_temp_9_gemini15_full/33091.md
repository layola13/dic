christcross:/ˈkrɪskrɒs/| /ˈkrɪskrɔːs/ | adj., v., n.| 交叉的；杂乱的；画叉形线于；交叉；十字形图案 |  To mark or decorate with crisscross lines; a pattern of crisscrossing lines. |例句：The streets crisscrossed the town.  街道纵横交错地穿过城镇。The farmer crisscrossed the field with furrows. 农民在田里犁出纵横交错的犁沟。|近义词：intertwine, intersect, cross |反义词：parallel, straight
