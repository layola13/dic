denationalizing: /ˌdiːˌnæʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 去国有化；取消国有化 | The process of transferring ownership or control of a nationalized industry or asset from the state to private hands.  It involves removing government control and influence from an industry or sector. |

例句：

The government is considering denationalizing the railway system. = 政府正在考虑将铁路系统私有化。

The denationalizing of the energy sector led to increased competition. = 能源行业的私有化导致了竞争加剧。


近义词：privatizing, deregulating


反义词：nationalizing, socializing


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is denationalizing the airline. = 政府正在将航空公司私有化。
They are denationalizing the postal service. = 他们正在将邮政服务私有化。

一般过去时:
The country denationalized its telecommunications industry. = 该国将其电信行业私有化了。
They denationalized the banks in the 1990s. = 他们在20世纪90年代将银行私有化了。

现在完成时:
The government has denationalized several state-owned enterprises. = 政府已经将几家国有企业私有化了。
They have denationalized the steel industry completely. = 他们已经完全将钢铁行业私有化了。

过去完成时:
Before the reforms, the government had already denationalized some industries. = 在改革之前，政府已经将一些行业私有化了。
The company had been denationalized before the merger. = 在合并之前，该公司就已经私有化了。

将来时:
The party plans to denationalize the healthcare system next year. = 该党计划明年将医疗保健系统私有化。
They will denationalize the water supply company soon. = 他们很快就会将自来水公司私有化。

虚拟语气:
If they denationalized the railways, it would improve efficiency. = 如果他们将铁路私有化，效率将会提高。
I wish the government had denationalized the airlines earlier. = 我希望政府早些将航空公司私有化。

被动语态:
The airline is being denationalized by the government. = 航空公司正在被政府私有化。
The banks were denationalized in the 1980s. = 银行在20世纪80年代被私有化了。

疑问句:
Is the government denationalizing the energy sector? = 政府是否正在将能源行业私有化？
Will they denationalize the oil industry? = 他们会将石油行业私有化吗？

否定句:
The government is not denationalizing the education system. = 政府没有将教育系统私有化。
They did not denationalize the steel mills. = 他们没有将钢铁厂私有化。

de- (表示否定或取消) + nationalize (使成为国有)  + -ing (动名词后缀)
