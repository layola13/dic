oscillogram:/ˈɒsɪləɡræm/ /ˈɑːsɪləˌɡræm/| n. |示波图，示波器曲线图 |A visual representation of a varying electrical signal, typically displayed on the screen of an oscilloscope. It shows the amplitude of the signal over time.  |The oscillogram clearly showed the high-frequency oscillations.  示波图清晰地显示了高频振荡。| waveform, trace | static image |
