micas:/ˈmaɪkəz/ | n. |云母；云母片 |A mineral consisting of hydrous potassium aluminum silicate, often with other elements, that readily splits into thin, flexible, elastic sheets.  It is used in electrical insulation, lubricants, and cosmetics. |例句：The artist used thin sheets of mica to create a shimmering effect in her painting.  这位艺术家用薄薄的云母片在她的画作中创造出闪闪发光的效果。|例句：Mica is a common mineral found in igneous and metamorphic rocks. 云母是常见的一种矿物，存在于火成岩和变质岩中。|近义词：muscovite, biotite, phlogopite |反义词：N/A
