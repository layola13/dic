convalescence: /ˌkɒnvəˈlesəns/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈlesəns/ | n. | 康复；恢复期 | The state of recovering from an illness or medical treatment; the period of recovery.  |例句：His convalescence was slow but steady. 他的康复缓慢但稳定。

例句：She spent several weeks in convalescence after her surgery. 她手术后花了几个星期康复。


近义词：recovery, recuperation, rehabilitation | 反义词：illness, sickness

词源:
From Old French *convalescence, from Latin convalescens, present participle of convalēscere "to become strong again," from con- "together" + valēscere "to grow strong," from valēre "to be strong, be well."
