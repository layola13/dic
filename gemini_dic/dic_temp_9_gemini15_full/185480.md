reunited:/ˌriːjuˈnaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 团聚的，重聚的 |  Brought back together after a period of separation.  This adjective describes the state of being brought together again after being apart. |  The family was reunited after years of separation.  这个家庭在多年的分离后团聚了。

The long-lost brothers were finally reunited.  失散多年的兄弟终于团聚了。


近义词：recombined, rejoined, rejoined

反义词：separated, divided, parted


词性：形容词
