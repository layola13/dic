fearsome:/ˈfɪərsəm/| adj.|可怕的，吓人的，令人敬畏的|Inspiring fear or dread; terrifying.  The adjective "fearsome" describes something that evokes a strong feeling of fear, awe, or terror. It often implies a sense of power and danger.|例句：The storm was fearsome, with torrential rain and howling winds. 暴风雨很可怕，狂风暴雨。The fearsome dragon guarded the treasure.  这条可怕的龙守护着宝藏。|近义词：frightening, terrifying, dreadful, awesome, formidable|反义词：harmless, comforting, reassuring
