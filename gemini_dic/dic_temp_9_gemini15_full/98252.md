一般现在时:
They are inseparable. = 他们形影不离。
The two ideas are inseparable. = 这两个想法是不可分割的。

一般过去时:
They were inseparable in college. = 他们在大学时形影不离。
The parts were inseparable before the accident. = 事故发生前，这些部件是不可分离的。

现在完成时:
We have been inseparable since we met. = 从我们相遇以来，我们一直形影不离。
The two concepts have been inseparable throughout history. = 这两个概念在历史上一直是不可分割的。

过去完成时:
They had been inseparable for many years before they quarreled. = 在他们吵架之前，他们已经形影不离很多年了。
The company had been inseparable from its founder until his retirement. = 直到创始人退休，该公司一直与其创始人密不可分。

将来时:
They will be inseparable, I'm sure. = 我相信他们将形影不离。
These two elements will remain inseparable in the final product. = 这两种元素将在最终产品中保持不可分离。

虚拟语气:
If they were inseparable, they would never have broken up. = 如果他们形影不离，他们就不会分手。
I wish my best friend and I were inseparable again. = 我希望我和我最好的朋友又能形影不离了。


被动语态:
The two countries were considered inseparable by their people. = 这两个国家被人民认为是不可分离的。
These issues cannot be considered inseparable in the long run. = 从长远来看，这些问题不能被认为是不可分割的。


疑问句:

否定句:
