multiplanar:/ˌmʌltiˈpleɪnər/| adj.|多平面的，多层面的|Having or involving multiple planes.  This adjective describes something that exists or operates across multiple planes or levels.  It is often used in medical imaging and computer graphics to describe techniques that allow viewing of an object from multiple perspectives simultaneously.|例句：Multiplanar reconstruction (MPR) is a powerful tool in medical imaging. 多平面重建 (MPR) 是医学影像中一个强大的工具。

例句：The software allows for multiplanar visualization of the 3D model. 该软件允许对 3D 模型进行多平面可视化。


近义词：multifaceted, multidimensional, layered


反义词：uniplanar, single-plane

词性：形容词
