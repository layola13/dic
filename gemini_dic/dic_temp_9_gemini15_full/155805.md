phonotypical: /ˌfoʊnoʊˈtɪpɪkəl/ | adj. | 表音的；语音型的 | Relating to or denoting the sound system of a language.  It refers to the actual sounds produced in speech, as opposed to the written representation of those sounds.  | Example:  The phonotypical analysis revealed several variations in pronunciation across different dialects.  表音分析揭示了不同方言之间发音的几种差异。 | 近义词: phonetic, phonological | 反义词: orthographic
