single-handed:/ˈsɪŋɡəlˈhændɪd/| adj.| 单独的；独自一人完成的； |Alone; unaided; by oneself.  It describes an action or task completed by one person without assistance. |例句：She single-handedly managed to rescue the child from the burning building. 她独自一人成功地将孩子从燃烧的建筑物中救了出来。

例句：He single-handedly completed the entire project. 他独自一人完成了整个项目。

近义词：unassisted, solo, alone, unaided

反义词：collaboratively, jointly, with assistance
