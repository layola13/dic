complimentingly:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmɛntɪŋli/ | adj. | 赞美地；恭维地 | In a way that expresses praise or admiration.  It describes the manner in which something is done or said. |例句：She spoke complimentingly about his work. 她赞扬了他的作品。

例句：He looked at her complimentingly. 他赞赏地看着她。


近义词：flatteringly, approvingly, admiringly | 反义词：disparagingly, critically, disapprovingly
