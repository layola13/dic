flagstaves:/ˈflæɡˌsteɪvz/| n. |旗杆；旗杖|The plural of flagstaff.  Flagstaves are long poles used to display flags.  They can be made of various materials, such as wood or metal, and are often seen in ceremonies or as part of a building's decoration.|例句：The flagstaves stood tall and proud in the breeze. 旗杆在微风中高高耸立，显得格外神气。
