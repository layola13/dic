marketplaces:/ˈmɑːrkɪtˌpleɪsɪz/ | n. |市场（场所）；市场（整体） | A marketplace is a location where goods and services are exchanged, either physically or virtually.  It can refer to a physical space like a farmer's market or a virtual space like an online shopping platform. | 例句：The farmer's market was bustling with activity.  这个农贸市场热闹非凡。 | 例句：Amazon is one of the largest marketplaces in the world. 亚马逊是世界上最大的市场之一。 | 近义词：markets, exchanges, trading platforms | 反义词：monopoly (in the sense of a lack of competition)
