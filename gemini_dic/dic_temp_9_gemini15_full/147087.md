overlicentiousness: /ˌoʊvərlaɪˈsɛnʃəsnəs/ | n. |过度放纵；淫荡；纵欲过度 | Excessive indulgence in pleasure or freedom; unrestrained behavior; extreme lasciviousness.  It suggests a level of indulgence beyond what is considered acceptable or morally right.  The word implies a lack of self-control and a disregard for social norms or consequences.


例句：

The overlicentiousness of the court during the reign of King Louis XV contributed to its downfall. = 路易十五统治时期宫廷的过度放纵导致了它的垮台。

His overlicentiousness led to his ruin, both financially and socially. = 他过度放纵的行为导致了他经济和社会上的毁灭。


近义词：debauchery, libertinism, profligacy, dissoluteness, excess


反义词：abstemiousness, asceticism, restraint, moderation, temperance


词性：名词
