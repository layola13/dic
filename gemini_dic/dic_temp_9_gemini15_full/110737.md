linger:/ˈlɪŋɡər/| v.|逗留；徘徊；持续；|to stay somewhere beyond the usual or expected time|The scent of roses lingered in the air. 玫瑰的香味在空气中久久不散。|She lingered over her coffee, reluctant to leave. 她慢慢地喝着咖啡，不愿离开。|remain, tarry, persist|depart, leave, vanish


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He lingers in the garden after sunset. 他日落后还留在花园里。
The smell of baking lingers in the kitchen. 烤制食物的香味在厨房里久久不散。


一般过去时:
She lingered at the party long after everyone else had left.  她很久以后才离开聚会，那时其他人已经都走了。
The pain lingered for several days after the operation. 手术后疼痛持续了好几天。


现在完成时:
The aroma of coffee has lingered all morning. 咖啡的香味整个上午都挥之不去。
He has lingered in the city for too long. 他在这个城市逗留太久了。


过去完成时:
They had lingered too long and missed the last train. 他们逗留太久，错过了末班车。
She had lingered over her work, delaying her departure. 她工作拖延太久，耽误了她的离开。


将来时:
I think the rain will linger into the afternoon. 我想雨会持续到下午。
We will linger at the beach until sunset. 我们会在海滩上待到日落。


虚拟语气:
If only I hadn't lingered so long, I wouldn't have missed the bus. 要是我没逗留那么久就好了，我就不会错过公共汽车了。
I wish the summer would linger a little longer. 我希望夏天能多持续一会儿。


被动语态:
The sweet smell of chocolate was lingered in the shop. 巧克力甜蜜的气味在商店里久久不散。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，但语法上成立)
The decision was lingered over for several weeks.  这个决定被拖延了好几周。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，但语法上成立)


疑问句:
Will the effects of the medicine linger for long?  这种药的效果会持续很久吗？
Why are you lingering here? 你为什么在这里徘徊？


否定句:
The feeling of sadness didn't linger for long. 悲伤的感觉并没有持续很久。
He didn't linger at the door but left immediately. 他没有在门口徘徊，而是立刻离开了。



PHRASE->
linger on:  继续存在；持续
linger over:  慢慢地享用；细细品味
linger in:  在...逗留
linger around: 在...附近徘徊
linger about:  闲逛；徘徊
