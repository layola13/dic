/ˈɪntərɛst/ | n. |兴趣；爱好；利息|Interest refers to a feeling of wanting to learn more about something or someone, or a feeling of wanting to be involved in something.  It can also refer to the money earned from an investment.|例句：I have a strong interest in history. 我对历史有浓厚的兴趣。

例句：The interest rate on my loan is 5%. 我的贷款利率是 5%。|近义词：hobby, fascination, curiosity, liking |反义词：disinterest, apathy, boredom |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are your interests? = 你的兴趣是什么？
What interests you most about this job? = 这份工作最吸引你的是什么？

一般过去时:
What were his interests as a child? = 他小时候的兴趣是什么？
What interested you in this field? = 什么让你对这个领域感兴趣？

现在完成时:
What interests have you developed recently? = 你最近培养了什么兴趣？
What has interested you most in this book? = 这本书里什么最让你感兴趣？

过去完成时:
What had interested her before she became a doctor? = 在她成为医生之前，她对什么感兴趣？
What interests had he pursued before he retired? = 他退休前追求过什么兴趣？

将来时:
What will your interests be in the future? = 你未来的兴趣将会是什么？
What do you think will interest you next year? = 你认为明年什么会让你感兴趣？

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pursue my interest in painting. = 如果我有更多时间，我会追求我对绘画的兴趣。
I wish I had known about this earlier; it would have interested me greatly. = 我真希望早点知道这个；它会让我非常感兴趣。

被动语态:
What interests are being promoted by the school? = 学校正在推广哪些兴趣？
His interest in music was never encouraged by his parents. = 他对音乐的兴趣从未受到父母的鼓励。

疑问句:
What is your main interest? = 你的主要兴趣是什么？
What interests you about this topic? = 关于这个话题，你感兴趣的是什么？

否定句:
I have no interest in politics. = 我对政治没有兴趣。
That movie didn't interest me at all. = 那部电影一点也不让我感兴趣。


PHRASE->
interest rate: 利率
interest group: 利益集团
in the interest of: 为了…的利益
vested interest:既得利益
mutual interest: 共同兴趣
