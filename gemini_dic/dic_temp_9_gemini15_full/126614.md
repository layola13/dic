/naɪd/ | n. |稚虫，若虫 | A naiad is the nymph of a water insect, especially a dragonfly or mayfly.  Naiads are also sometimes used figuratively to refer to beautiful water nymphs in mythology. | Example: The shimmering naiad emerged from the stream.  闪闪发光的稚虫从溪流中出现。 | 近义词：nymph (in a mythological context) | 反义词：adult (insect)
