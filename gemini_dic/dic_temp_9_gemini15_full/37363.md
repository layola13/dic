commensation:/ˌkɒmənˈseɪʃən/ | /ˌkɑːmənˈseɪʃən/ | n. | 补偿；赔偿；报酬 |  Commensation refers to something given or received as compensation or payment, often for a loss or injury. It can also refer to the act of compensating.  | Example: The company offered commensation for the damage caused by the accident.  该公司为事故造成的损失提供了赔偿。 |近义词：compensation, indemnity, recompense | 反义词：penalty, punishment | 名词
