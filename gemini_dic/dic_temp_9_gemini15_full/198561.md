shrug:/ʃrʌɡ/ | vt. & n. |耸肩，不屑一顾 | To raise and lower your shoulders to express doubt, indifference, or lack of knowledge.  |例句：He shrugged his shoulders in response to my question. 他对我的问题耸了耸肩。
例句：She gave a shrug and walked away. 她耸了耸肩，走开了。|近义词：flick, twitch|反义词：nod, agree


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shrugs his shoulders when he doesn't know the answer. = 他不知道答案的时候会耸耸肩。
I shrug off criticism easily. = 我很容易就能把批评置之不理。


一般过去时:
She shrugged off the insult. = 她对侮辱不屑一顾。
He shrugged and walked away. = 他耸了耸肩，走开了。


现在完成时:
He has shrugged off many challenges in his life. = 他一生中克服了许多挑战。
I have shrugged off their comments. = 我对他们的评论置之不理。


过去完成时:
She had shrugged off the warning signs. = 她之前对警告标志置之不理。
He had shrugged his responsibilities. = 他之前逃避了自己的责任。


将来时:
I will shrug off the pressure. = 我将摆脱压力。
She will shrug off any criticism. = 她将对任何批评置之不理。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't care, I would shrug it off. = 如果我不在乎，我会置之不理。
I wish I could shrug off this feeling of anxiety. = 我希望我能摆脱这种焦虑感。


被动语态:
The problem was shrugged off by the authorities. = 当局对这个问题置之不理。
His concerns were shrugged off by his colleagues. = 他的担忧被同事们忽略了。


疑问句:
Did he shrug his shoulders when you asked him? = 当你问他的时候，他耸肩了吗？
Will she shrug off the responsibility? = 她会逃避责任吗？


否定句:
He didn't shrug off the responsibility. = 他没有逃避责任。
She didn't shrug at my question. = 她并没有对我的问题耸肩。
