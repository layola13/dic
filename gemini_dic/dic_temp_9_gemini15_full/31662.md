chatters:/ˈtʃætərz/ | v. |喋喋不休地说话，吱吱作响 |To talk rapidly and continuously, often about unimportant things; to make a series of short, quick sounds.  |例句：The children chattered excitedly about their trip. 孩子们兴奋地喋喋不休地谈论着他们的旅行。

例句：The monkeys chattered in the trees. 猴子在树上吱吱叫。|近义词：babble, jabber, prattle |反义词：be silent, be quiet |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds chatter incessantly. = 鸟儿不停地啁啾。
They chatter away about nothing important. = 他们喋喋不休地谈论着一些无关紧要的事情。

一般过去时:
The guests chattered merrily at the party. = 客人们在派对上愉快地聊着天。
She chattered nervously during the interview. = 面试期间她紧张地喋喋不休。

现在完成时:
The children have chattered all afternoon. = 孩子们整个下午都在喋喋不休。
We have chattered so much that we are hoarse. = 我们说得太多，嗓子都哑了。

过去完成时:

She had chattered so much that she was completely exhausted. = 她说得太多，完全筋疲力尽了。

将来时:
