/ˈbɪziɪŋ/ | v. | 使忙碌；使忙于 |  To make someone busy or keep someone occupied.  The word is chiefly used in the present participle form ("busying oneself/himself/herself/themselves with").  |  She busied herself with preparing dinner. 她忙着准备晚餐。|  occupying, engaging | resting, idling | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is busying himself with his work. = 他正忙于他的工作。
They are busying themselves preparing for the party. = 他们正忙着为派对做准备。


一般过去时:
She busied herself with cleaning the house. = 她忙着打扫房子。
He busied himself preparing a presentation. = 他忙着准备演示文稿。


现在完成时:
I have busied myself with learning a new language. = 我一直忙于学习一门外语。
They have busied themselves organizing the event. = 他们一直忙于组织这次活动。


过去完成时:
She had busied herself all day, so she was tired. = 她整天都忙个不停，所以很累。
He had busied himself with research before writing the report. = 他在写报告之前一直在忙着研究。


将来时:
I will busy myself with gardening this weekend. = 这个周末我将忙于园艺。
She will busy herself with packing for the trip. = 她将忙于为旅行打包。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would busy myself with painting. = 如果我有更多时间，我会忙于绘画。
I wish I could busy myself with something more productive. = 我希望我能忙于一些更有成效的事情。


被动语态:
He was busied with urgent tasks.  = 他忙于紧急任务。(虽然"busied"本身是主动语态，但句子表达是被动的状态)
The children were busied with their toys. = 孩子们忙于玩他们的玩具。(同上)


疑问句:
What are you busying yourself with? = 你在忙什么？
Is she busying herself with the project? = 她忙于这个项目吗？


否定句:
He wasn't busying himself with anything important. = 他并没有忙于任何重要的事情。
They didn't busy themselves with gossip. = 他们没有忙于闲聊。


PHRASE->
busy oneself with = 忙于...
busy oneself in = 忙于...
busy oneself doing = 忙于做...
keep oneself busy = 保持忙碌
get busy = 开始忙碌
