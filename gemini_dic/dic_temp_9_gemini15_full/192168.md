scrapbook:/ˈskræpˌbʊk/| n. |剪贴簿，纪念册 |A scrapbook is a book with blank pages where people paste photographs, pictures, and other items of personal significance.  It's often used to record memories or document events. |Example: I have a scrapbook filled with photos from my childhood trips.  我有一本剪贴簿，里面装满了儿时旅行的照片。


|Example: She carefully arranged the pressed flowers in her scrapbook. 她小心翼翼地把压花放在她的剪贴簿里。|近义词：memory book, keepsake album |反义词：None |词性：名词
