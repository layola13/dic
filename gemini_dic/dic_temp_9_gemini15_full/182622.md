reoppressing:/ˌriːəˈprɛsɪŋ/| v.|再次压迫，重新压迫|The act of oppressing again; subjecting to oppression a second time or repeatedly.  It implies a continuation or renewal of oppressive actions.|例句：The new laws were seen as reoppressing the already marginalized community. = 新法律被视为再次压迫那些已经边缘化的社群。

例句：The regime's reoppressing tactics stifled any dissent. = 该政权再次实施压迫策略，扼杀了任何异见。


近义词：oppressing again, suppressing anew, subjugating repeatedly, tyrannizing afresh|反义词：liberating, emancipating, empowering

词性：动词
