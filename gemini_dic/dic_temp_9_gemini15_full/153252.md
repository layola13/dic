pemmican:/ˈpɛmɪkən/| n. |硬干肉,  pemmican|一种由干燥的肉、脂肪和浆果制成的浓缩食物，通常用于长途旅行或储存。Pemmican is a high-energy food that is easy to store and transport.  It was traditionally used by Indigenous peoples of North America and other parts of the world. |The explorers relied on pemmican to survive their long journey across the plains. 探险家们依靠硬干肉在穿越平原的长途旅行中生存。|concentrated food, preserved food|fresh food|
