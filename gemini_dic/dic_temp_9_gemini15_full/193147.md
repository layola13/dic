/sɪˈkjʊərɪŋz/ | v. | 保护；获得；使安全 | The act of securing something or someone.  It refers to the process of making something safe, obtaining something, or fastening something firmly. | Examples: The securing of the building was a top priority. (建筑物的安全至关重要。)  The securing of the contract was a major accomplishment. (获得合同是一项重大成就。) |  protecting, obtaining, fastening | endangering, losing, releasing | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is securing the documents. = 他正在保护文件。
They are securing the funding for the project. = 他们正在为项目筹集资金。


一般过去时:
She secured the loan last week. = 她上周获得了贷款。
They secured the package with strong rope. = 他们用结实的绳子捆绑包裹。


现在完成时:
He has secured a place at university. = 他已经获得了一个大学名额。
We have secured all the necessary permits. = 我们已经获得了所有必要的许可证。


过去完成时:
She had secured a job before she graduated. = 她毕业前就已经找到工作了。
They had secured the perimeter before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经保护好了周边地区。


将来时:
We will secure the shipment tomorrow. = 我们明天将确保货物的安全。
They will secure the building against intruders. = 他们将保护建筑物免受入侵者侵害。


虚拟语气:
If I secured the funding, I would start the project. = 如果我获得了资金，我就会启动项目。
I wish I had secured a better job. = 我希望我找到一份更好的工作。


被动语态:
The documents were secured in the vault. = 文件被保存在金库中。
The area was secured by the police. = 该地区由警方保护。


疑问句:
Have you secured the necessary permissions? = 你已经获得必要的权限了吗？
Did they secure the building properly? = 他们妥善地保护了建筑物吗？


否定句:
He didn't secure the contract. = 他没有获得合同。
They couldn't secure the funding for the project. = 他们无法为项目筹集资金。
