pervader:/pərˈveɪdər/ | n. |弥漫者，遍布者 | A person or thing that pervades.  Someone or something that spreads throughout a place or thing. | Example: The pervasive influence of social media makes it a powerful pervader of modern culture. 社交媒体的普遍影响使其成为现代文化的有力弥漫者。 |近义词：permeator, infuser, spreader | 反义词：isolator, separator | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The aroma of coffee pervades the air. = 咖啡的香气弥漫在空气中。
Fear pervades the hearts of the villagers. = 恐惧弥漫在村民的心中。


一般过去时:
The sweet scent of honeysuckle pervaded the garden. = 忍冬花的甜香弥漫在花园里。
A sense of unease pervaded the room after the announcement. = 公告之后，不安的感觉弥漫在房间里。


现在完成时:
Doubt has pervaded his mind since the accident. = 自从事故发生以来，怀疑一直萦绕在他的心头。
A feeling of sadness has pervaded the town since the disaster. = 自从灾难发生以来，悲伤的情绪笼罩着这座城镇。


过去完成时:
A sense of dread had pervaded the castle before the attack. = 袭击发生之前，一种恐惧感已经弥漫在城堡里。
Rumors had pervaded the city before the official statement. = 在官方声明之前，谣言已经传遍了整个城市。


将来时:
The smell of baking bread will pervade the house soon. = 面包烘焙的香味很快就会弥漫整个房子。
Anxiety will pervade the nation as the election draws near. = 随着选举临近，焦虑将会弥漫全国。


虚拟语气:
If the scent of roses pervaded the air, it would be delightful. = 如果玫瑰的香味弥漫在空气中，那将会令人愉悦。
I wish calm could pervade the situation. = 我希望平静能弥漫整个局势。


被动语态:
The city was pervaded by a feeling of excitement. = 这座城市弥漫着兴奋的情绪。
The atmosphere was pervaded by a sense of mystery. =  气氛笼罩着神秘感。


疑问句:
Does a feeling of despair pervade the community? = 绝望的感觉是否弥漫在这个社区？
Will the new policy pervade all aspects of the business? = 新政策会渗透到业务的各个方面吗？


否定句:
The joy did not pervade the room. =  快乐并没有弥漫在房间里。
Silence did not pervade the bustling market. = 沉默并没有弥漫在热闹的市场。
