peppercorn: /ˈpɛpərkɔːrn/ | n. |胡椒粒，胡椒粉| A small, dried berry of the pepper plant, used as a spice.  It is typically dark brown or black and has a pungent, spicy flavor. |例句：He added a pinch of peppercorn to the stew. 他在炖菜里加了一撮胡椒粒。

例句：The peppercorns were freshly ground. 胡椒粒是现磨的。


近义词：pepper, spice |反义词：无


词源:  The word "peppercorn" is a compound word.  "Pepper" comes from the Old French "piper," ultimately from the Sanskrit "pippali," referring to long pepper. "Corn" in this context, dating back to Old English, means "grain" or "seed,"  referring to the small seed-like form of the pepper.
