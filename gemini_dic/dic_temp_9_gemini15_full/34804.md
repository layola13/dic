climbers:/ˈklaɪmərz/| n.|攀登者，登山者|People who climb mountains or rocks as a sport or hobby.  They often possess specialized skills and equipment.|例句：The experienced climbers reached the summit before sunset.  经验丰富的登山者在日落前到达了山顶。
例句：A group of climbers was rescued from the mountain after a blizzard. 一群登山者在暴风雪后被从山上救了下来。|近义词：mountaineers, alpinists|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "non-climbers" or "ground-dwellers".|名词
