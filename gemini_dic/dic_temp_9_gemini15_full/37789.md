compel:/kəmˈpɛl/ | vt. |强迫；迫使；驱使 | To force or oblige (someone) to do something.  |例句：The law compels employers to provide a safe working environment. 法律强迫雇主提供安全的工作环境。
例句：Nothing could compel him to betray his friends.  没有什么能强迫他背叛朋友。|近义词：force, oblige, coerce, require |反义词：allow, permit, let


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence compels the jury to find the defendant guilty. = 证据迫使陪审团认定被告有罪。
The new regulations compel businesses to adopt stricter safety measures. = 新规迫使企业采取更严格的安全措施。

一般过去时:
The storm compelled us to seek shelter. = 暴风雨迫使我们寻找庇护。
His passion compelled him to write a novel. = 他的热情驱使他写了一部小说。

现在完成时:
The mounting evidence has compelled a re-evaluation of the case. = 越来越多的证据迫使我们重新评估此案。
Recent events have compelled a change in our strategy. = 最近发生的事件迫使我们改变策略。

过去完成时:
The threat of war had compelled the nations to negotiate. = 战争的威胁迫使各国进行谈判。
Her guilt had compelled her to confess. = 她的罪恶感迫使她坦白。

将来时:
The deadline will compel us to work overtime. = 截止日期将迫使我们加班。
Rising sea levels will compel coastal communities to relocate. = 海平面上升将迫使沿海社区搬迁。

虚拟语气:
If the circumstances compelled me, I would resign. = 如果情况迫使我，我会辞职。
I wish the situation didn't compel me to lie. = 我希望情况不要迫使我说谎。

被动语态:
He was compelled to testify against his will. = 他被迫违背自己的意愿作证。
The company was compelled to pay a hefty fine. = 公司被迫支付巨额罚款。

疑问句:
What compelled you to make such a decision? = 什么迫使你做出这样的决定？
What factors compelled the government to intervene? = 哪些因素迫使政府介入？

否定句:
Nothing could compel her to change her mind. = 没有任何事情能够强迫她改变主意。
He refused to be compelled by threats. = 他拒绝被威胁所迫。


PHRASE->
compel obedience = 强迫服从
compel attention = 引人注目
compel admiration = 令人钦佩
compel respect = 赢得尊重
compel agreement = 迫使同意


词源:
From Old French compeler, from Latin compellere "to drive together, force," from com- "together" + pellere "to drive, push."
