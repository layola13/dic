calcule:/kælˈkjuːleɪt/ | vt. |计算，推算，估计 | To determine or ascertain by mathematical methods; compute.  | 例句：I need to calculate the total cost. 我需要计算总成本。 | 例句：She calculated the risk before making the decision.  她在做决定之前权衡了风险。|近义词：compute, figure, reckon |反义词：estimate (in the sense of a rough guess)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He calculates the area of the circle. 他计算圆的面积。
They calculate the project's budget every year. 他们每年计算项目的预算。

一般过去时:
She calculated the distance incorrectly. 她计算距离不正确。
We calculated the profit margin before investing. 我们在投资前计算了利润率。

现在完成时:
I have calculated the results. 我已经计算出了结果。
They have calculated the total expenses for the trip. 他们已经计算出了这次旅行的总费用。

过去完成时:
He had calculated the trajectory before launching the rocket. 他在发射火箭前已经计算出了轨迹。
She had calculated the amount needed before asking for a loan. 她在申请贷款前已经计算出了所需的金额。

将来时:
We will calculate the final score after the game. 我们将在比赛结束后计算最终得分。
They will calculate the taxes owed next month. 他们下个月将计算应缴税款。

虚拟语气:
If I had a calculator, I would calculate it easily. 如果我有计算器，我会很容易地计算出来。
I wish I had calculated the time more accurately. 我希望我计算时间更准确一些。

被动语态:
The area was calculated by the engineer.  面积是由工程师计算的。
The total cost will be calculated by the accounting department. 总成本将由会计部门计算。

疑问句:
How do you calculate the average? 你如何计算平均值？
Can you calculate the square root of that number? 你能计算那个数字的平方根吗？

否定句:
He didn't calculate the risk properly. 他没有正确地计算风险。
I cannot calculate the answer without a calculator. 没有计算器我无法计算答案。
