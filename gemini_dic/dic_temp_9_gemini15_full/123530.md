monotreme:/ˈmɒnəˌtriːm/|/ˈmɑːnəˌtriːm/| n. |单孔类动物|A monotreme is a mammal that lays eggs, rather than giving birth to live young.  Monotremes are characterized by their single opening (cloaca) for excretion and reproduction. They include the echidnas and platypuses.|Example: The platypus is a fascinating example of a monotreme, a unique group of mammals.|近义词：egg-laying mammal|反义词：marsupial, placental mammal
