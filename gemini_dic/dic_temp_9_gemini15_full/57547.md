drake:/dreɪk/| n. |雄鸭；雄鸟；（俚语）男子；说唱歌手|A drake is a male duck.  The word can also refer to the male of other birds, or, in slang, a man.  In recent years, it has also become strongly associated with the Canadian rapper Drake.|例句：The drake proudly displayed its iridescent feathers. 这只雄鸭骄傲地展示着它闪亮的羽毛。

例句：He's a real drake, always attracting attention. 他是个十足的花花公子，总是吸引眼球。


近义词：mallard (for a specific type of drake),  (slang) dude, guy, chap|反义词：duck (referring to the female)|词性：名词
