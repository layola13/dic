premonopolizes:/ˌpriːməˈnɒpəlaɪzɪz/| v.|预先垄断；提前垄断|To gain control of (a market, industry, etc.) before others do; to establish a monopoly in advance.|例句：The company premonopolized the market by securing all the key patents.  这家公司通过获得所有关键专利来预先垄断市场。|例句：By investing heavily in infrastructure, the government attempted to premonopolize the emerging renewable energy sector. 政府通过大力投资基础设施，试图预先垄断新兴的可再生能源领域。|近义词：corner the market, dominate, control|反义词：free market, open competition|动词


USAGE->
