conciliatory: /kənˈsɪliətɔːri/ | adj. | 安抚的；调解的；抚慰的 | Intended or likely to placate or pacify.  Showing a readiness to reconcile. | 例句：He made a conciliatory gesture by offering a small discount. 他通过提供一个小折扣做出了一个安抚的姿态。

例句：Her conciliatory tone helped to ease the tension in the room. 她安抚的语气帮助缓解了房间里的紧张气氛。


近义词：appeasing, placatory, pacifying, mollifying, propitiatory | 反义词：antagonistic, aggressive, hostile, bellicose


词源:  From Latin *conciliatōrius, from conciliāre "to reconcile, win over," from concilium "assembly, council."  The sense development reflects the original meaning of bringing together conflicting parties.
