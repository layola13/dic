cartoonish:/kɑːˈtuːnɪʃ/| adj. |卡通式的，夸张的，滑稽的 |Exaggerated and unrealistic in a way that is often humorous or appealing.  It often refers to something that looks like it belongs in a cartoon. |例句：The villain's cartoonish mustache made him look ridiculous.  这个反派的卡通式胡子让他看起来很可笑。|例句：The movie's violence was overly cartoonish and not at all realistic. 这部电影的暴力场面过于卡通化，一点也不现实。|近义词：exaggerated, unrealistic, ludicrous, comical |反义词：realistic, lifelike, subtle, understated
