revivabilities:/ˌriːvaɪvəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |复苏能力；可复苏性|The capacity or potential for revival or recovery.  It refers to the ability of something to be revived or brought back to life, activity, or effectiveness.  This term is often used in contexts related to ecosystems, economies, or even individual organisms.|例句：The study investigated the revivabilities of different bacterial strains after exposure to extreme temperatures. 这项研究调查了不同细菌菌株在暴露于极端温度后复苏的能力。

The long-term revivabilities of the coral reefs are threatened by climate change. 珊瑚礁的长期复苏能力受到气候变化的威胁。|近义词：recoverability, restorability, viability|反义词：irreversibility, irretrievability|名词
