morphia:/ˈmɔːrfiə/| n.|吗啡|Morphine is a potent opioid analgesic used to relieve severe pain.  It acts on opioid receptors in the brain and spinal cord, reducing the perception of pain.|例句：The doctor prescribed morphine to ease the patient's intense pain. 医生开了吗啡来缓解病人的剧烈疼痛。
The patient received a dose of morphine for post-surgical pain.  病人接受了一剂吗啡来缓解术后疼痛。|近义词：opioid, narcotic|反义词：analgesic (in the sense of lacking pain-relieving properties)
