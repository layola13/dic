lammergeir:/ˈlæmɜːrdʒeɪr/| n. |胡兀鹫|A lammergeir is a large bird of prey found in mountainous regions of Europe, Asia, and Africa.  It is known for its distinctive appearance and its unusual diet, which includes bones. |例句：The lammergeir soared effortlessly on the thermals, its powerful wings catching the rising air currents. 胡兀鹫轻松地利用热气流翱翔，它强壮的翅膀捕捉着上升的气流。

The lammergeir, also known as the bearded vulture, is a fascinating creature. 胡兀鹫，也被称为胡须秃鹫，是一种迷人的生物。|近义词：bearded vulture|反义词：None specifically applicable.
