lupulinic: /ˌluːpjuˈlɪnɪk/ | adj. | 啤酒花的；含啤酒花成分的 | Relating to or derived from lupulin, the yellow powder found in the hop plant.  Lupulinic acid is a major component of this powder and contributes to the bitterness and aroma of beer. | The lupulinic acid content of the hops greatly influenced the beer's bitterness.  啤酒花的苦味酸含量极大地影响了啤酒的苦味。|  hoppy, bitter |  (暂无明显的反义词，可考虑使用描述不含啤酒花成分的词语，例如:  non-hoppy) | adj.
