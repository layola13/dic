diagnostic:/ˌdaɪəɡˈnɒstɪk/ /ˌdaɪəɡˈnɑːstɪk/ | adj. n. |诊断的；诊断|Relating to the identification of the nature or cause of a disease or problem.|The doctor ordered a series of diagnostic tests.  医生开具了一系列诊断测试。|近义词：analytical, identifying|反义词：therapeutic, curative|形容词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The test is diagnostic of the disease. = 这种测试能够诊断出这种疾病。
This symptom is diagnostic of a heart problem. = 这个症状是心脏问题的诊断标志。


一般过去时:
The doctor's diagnostic skills were exceptional. = 这位医生的诊断技能非常出色。
The analysis was diagnostic of a software bug. = 分析结果诊断出软件错误。


现在完成时:
Doctors have developed many new diagnostic tools. = 医生们已经开发了许多新的诊断工具。
Researchers have made significant progress in diagnostic imaging. = 研究人员在诊断成像方面取得了重大进展。


过去完成时:
They had completed the diagnostic procedures before the surgery. = 他们在手术前完成了诊断程序。
The engineer had already run the diagnostic tests. = 工程师已经完成了诊断测试。


将来时:
We will use advanced diagnostic equipment. = 我们将使用先进的诊断设备。
The hospital will implement new diagnostic protocols. = 医院将实施新的诊断规程。


虚拟语气:
If we had better diagnostic tools, we could treat patients more effectively. = 如果我们有更好的诊断工具，就能更有效地治疗病人。
I wish we had more accurate diagnostic methods. = 我希望我们有更准确的诊断方法。


被动语态:
The patient was given a diagnostic examination. = 病人接受了诊断检查。
New diagnostic techniques are being developed constantly. = 新的诊断技术正在不断开发中。


疑问句:

否定句:
