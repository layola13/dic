incomity:/ɪnˈkɒmɪti/| n.|不和睦；无礼；粗鲁；不友好|Lack of courtesy; rudeness; discourtesy.  It often refers to a lack of civility between groups or nations. |例句：The incomity between the two countries prevented any meaningful diplomatic progress.  两个国家之间的不和睦阻碍了任何有意义的外交进展。|近义词：discourtesy, discourtesy, incivility, unfriendliness |反义词：courtesy, civility, friendliness
