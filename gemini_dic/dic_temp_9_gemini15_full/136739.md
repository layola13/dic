nonsensitized: /ˌnɒnˈsɛnsɪtaɪzd/ | adj. | 未致敏的，未敏感化的 | Not sensitized; not made sensitive or responsive to a particular stimulus or allergen.  This term often appears in medical and immunological contexts, referring to a lack of acquired immune response.  | The patient's nonsensitized state suggests a lack of prior exposure to the allergen.  未致敏的状态表明患者之前没有接触过敏原。 | sensitized, reactive | sensitized, allergic | adj.
