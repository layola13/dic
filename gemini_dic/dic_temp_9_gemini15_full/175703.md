rapid-response: /ˈræpɪd rɪˈspɒns/ | adj. n. | 快速反应，迅速回应 |  Describes a system or team designed to react quickly and effectively to a sudden event or crisis.  It emphasizes speed and efficiency in addressing urgent situations. |例句：The company implemented a rapid-response team to handle customer complaints.  这家公司实施了一个快速反应团队来处理客户投诉。

The hospital's rapid-response system saved many lives during the earthquake. 医院的快速反应系统在地震中挽救了许多生命。|近义词：swift response, immediate reaction, quick action |反义词：slow response, delayed reaction, lethargic response |词性：形容词，名词
