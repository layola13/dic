naumachy:/ˈnɔːməki/| n. | 海战；海战表演 | A mock sea battle, often staged as public entertainment in ancient Rome.  | Example: The naumachy was a spectacular event, featuring hundreds of ships and gladiators.  这场海战表演非常壮观，有数百艘船只和角斗士参加。 |近义词：naval battle, sea battle, mock battle |反义词：land battle
