horse-racing: /ˈhɔːrsˌreɪsɪŋ/ | n. | 赛马 | The sport of racing horses.  It involves breeding, training, and racing horses, often with significant betting involved.  | Example:  Horse-racing is a popular spectator sport in many countries.  赛马是许多国家流行的观赏性运动。  | 近义词: equestrian sports, horse racing (same word, different form) | 反义词: None specifically, but could be contrasted with non-equestrian sports.
