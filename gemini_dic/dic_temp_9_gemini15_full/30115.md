cedars:/ˈsiːdɑːrz/ | n. |雪松树；雪松树林 |  Cedars are evergreen coniferous trees belonging to the genus Cedrus. They are known for their fragrant wood and are often used in landscaping and for timber.  | Example: The majestic cedars stood tall against the mountain backdrop.  雄伟的雪松矗立在山峰的背景下。  The garden featured a grove of ancient cedars. 花园里有一片古老的雪松林。|近义词：pine, fir, juniper |反义词：无
