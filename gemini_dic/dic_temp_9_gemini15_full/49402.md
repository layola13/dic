deliverance:/dɪˈlɪvərəns/ | n. | 解救；解脱；拯救 | The act of being rescued or set free from danger, captivity, suffering, or oppression.  It implies a complete and often dramatic release from a difficult situation. |例句：The prisoner's deliverance from the dungeon was a joyous occasion. 囚犯从地牢中获救是一件令人高兴的事。

The deliverance of the hostages was a top priority for the government.  政府的首要任务是解救人质。


近义词：rescue, liberation, release, salvation | 反义词：imprisonment, captivity, bondage
