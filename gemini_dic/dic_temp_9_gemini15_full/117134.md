meaners:/ˈmiːnərz/| n.|刻薄的人，吝啬鬼|People who are unkind or ungenerous.|例句：He's such a meaner, always refusing to help others. 他真是个刻薄鬼，总是拒绝帮助别人。|近义词：misers, curmudgeons, skinflints|反义词：benefactors, philanthropists
