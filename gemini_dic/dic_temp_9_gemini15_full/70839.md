finishes:/ˈfɪnɪʃɪz/ | v. | 完成，结束 | To bring to an end; complete.  |例句：He finishes his work at 5 pm every day. 他每天下午5点完成工作。|例句：She finally finishes writing her novel. 她终于完成了她的小说。|近义词：complete, conclude, end | 反义词：begin, start, commence |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He finishes his homework every night. = 他每天晚上都完成作业。
She finishes her work early. = 她很早就完成了工作。


一般过去时:
They finished the project on time. = 他们按时完成了项目。
We finished dinner an hour ago. = 我们一个小时前吃完了晚饭。


现在完成时:
He has finished reading the book. = 他已经读完了这本书。
She has finished her studies. = 她已经完成了学业。


过去完成时:
They had finished eating before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经吃完了。
I had finished my work before he called. = 他打电话之前我已经完成了我的工作。


将来时:
I will finish the report tomorrow. = 我明天将完成报告。
We will finish the race soon. = 我们很快就会完成比赛。


虚拟语气:
If I had finished my work, I would have gone to the party. = 如果我完成了我的工作，我就去参加聚会了。
I wish I had finished my homework earlier. = 我希望我早点完成作业。


被动语态:
The project was finished last week. = 项目上周完成了。
The work will be finished by tomorrow. = 工作将于明天完成。


疑问句:
Have you finished your assignment? = 你完成作业了吗？
When will you finish the painting? = 你什么时候完成这幅画？


否定句:
I haven't finished my work yet. = 我还没有完成我的工作。
They didn't finish the race. = 他们没有完成比赛。


PHRASE->
finish off = 完成；吃完
finish up = 完成；结束
finish with = 完成...；结束...
to finish something off = 完成某事
put the finishing touches on = 润色；作最后的修饰
