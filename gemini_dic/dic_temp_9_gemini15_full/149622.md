painstaking: /ˈpeɪnzteɪkɪŋ/ | adj. | 费力的；一丝不苟的；极其细致的 |  characterized by or done with great care and thoroughness.  It implies a lot of effort and attention to detail, often over a long period. | 例句：The restoration of the painting was a painstaking process.  = 这幅画的修复是一个费力的过程。


The research required painstaking attention to detail. = 这项研究需要一丝不苟地关注细节。


近义词：meticulous, diligent, careful, thorough, assiduous | 反义词：careless, negligent, sloppy, cursory
