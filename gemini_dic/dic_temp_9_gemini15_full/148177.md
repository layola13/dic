overset:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛt/ | vt. |覆盖；压倒；使倾覆；(印刷术)置于…之上|To place (something) over or above something else; to dominate or overwhelm; to cause (something) to overturn or capsize. |例句：The large tree overset the small car during the storm.  暴风雨中，大树压倒了小汽车。 |例句：His anger overset his judgment. 他的愤怒让他丧失了判断力。 |近义词：cover, overwhelm, overturn, capsize |反义词：support, uphold, stabilize
