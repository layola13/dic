shuttles: /ˈʃʌtəlz/ | n. v. |梭子；穿梭；班车；航天飞机 |

英文解析:A shuttle is a vehicle that travels regularly between two places, such as a bus or train. It can also refer to a device used for weaving, or a spacecraft designed for repeated trips to and from orbit.  As a verb, it means to move quickly and repeatedly back and forth.

例句:

The space shuttle program was a significant achievement in aerospace engineering.  = 航天飞机计划是航空航天工程领域的一项重大成就。

The airport shuttle bus runs every 30 minutes. = 机场班车每30分钟运行一次。


近义词: (noun) ferry, spacecraft, conveyance,  (verb) dart, flit, zigzag


反义词: (verb) stay, remain, linger


词性: noun, verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shuttle bus shuttles passengers between the airport and the city center. = 班车往返于机场和市中心之间运送乘客。
He regularly shuttles between his office and his home. = 他经常往返于办公室和家之间。


一般过去时:
The space shuttle Atlantis shuttled astronauts to the International Space Station. =  航天飞机亚特兰蒂斯号将宇航员送往国际空间站。
She shuttled the children between school and various extracurricular activities. = 她把孩子们送去学校和各种课外活动。


现在完成时:
The company has shuttled thousands of tourists to the resort this season. = 这个公司本季已经运送了数千名游客到度假村。
We have shuttled back and forth between different departments all morning. = 我们整个上午都在不同的部门之间来回奔波。


过去完成时:
Before the accident, the train had shuttled passengers safely for decades. = 事故发生前，这列火车几十年来一直安全地运送乘客。
They had shuttled the documents between the two offices before the meeting. = 会议前，他们已将文件在两个办公室之间传递。


将来时:
The new high-speed train will shuttle passengers between the two cities in under an hour. = 新的高速列车将在不到一小时内往返于两座城市之间运送乘客。
We will shuttle the equipment to the construction site tomorrow. = 我们明天将把设备运送到建筑工地。


虚拟语气:
If we had a shuttle, we could get there much faster. = 如果我们有班车，我们可以更快到达那里。
I wish I could shuttle between different worlds like in a fantasy novel. = 我希望我像在奇幻小说里一样能够在不同的世界之间穿梭。


被动语态:

疑问句:
