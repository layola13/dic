immediateness:/ɪˈmiːdiətəs/| n. |即刻性；直接性；紧迫性 |The quality or state of being immediate; the state of being present or occurring without delay.  It refers to something happening or being done without any delay or hesitation. It can also refer to the feeling of closeness or directness in a relationship or experience.|例句：The immediateness of the crisis demanded immediate action.  危机的紧迫性要求立即采取行动。

The immediateness of his response surprised us. 他反应的迅速让我们吃惊。


近义词：instantaneity, urgency, directness, proximity|反义词：delay, remoteness, procrastination, indirectness|词性：名词
