revindication:/ˌrɛvɪnˈdeɪʃən/| n. |证明无辜；辩护；平反；恢复名誉 |The act of clearing someone of blame or suspicion; justification; vindication.  It often implies a restoration of reputation or honor after a period of injustice or wrongful accusation. |例句：The new evidence led to the revindication of the wrongly accused man. 新证据导致了被冤枉男子的平反。  The athlete's strong performance provided a complete revindication of his talent. 运动员出色的表现完全证明了他的才能。|近义词：vindication, justification, exoneration, clearance |反义词：condemnation, incrimination, culpability
