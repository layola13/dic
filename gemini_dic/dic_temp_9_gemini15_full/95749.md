inconsolable:/ˌɪnkənˈsoʊləbəl/| adj. |无法安慰的，极度悲伤的 | Extremely sad and unable to be comforted.  |例句：She was inconsolable after the death of her pet dog. 她宠物狗死后悲痛欲绝。|例句：He remained inconsolable despite our best efforts to cheer him up.  尽管我们尽力安慰他，他仍然无法自拔。|近义词：devastated, heartbroken, grief-stricken |反义词：consolable, comforted, cheerful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is inconsolable after losing his job. 他失业后非常悲痛。
She is inconsolable over the loss of her child. 她失去孩子后悲痛欲绝。

一般过去时:
He was inconsolable after the accident. 事故发生后他悲痛欲绝。
She was inconsolable when her husband passed away. 她丈夫去世时悲痛欲绝。

现在完成时:
She has been inconsolable since her dog died. 自从她的狗死了以后，她就一直悲痛欲绝。
He has remained inconsolable for months. 他已经悲伤了好几个月了。

过去完成时:
He had been inconsolable for days before he finally sought help. 在他最终寻求帮助之前，他已经悲痛了好几天了。
She had been inconsolable ever since the news broke. 自从消息传出，她就一直悲痛欲绝。

将来时:
He will likely be inconsolable when he hears the news. 当他听到这个消息时，他可能会非常悲痛。
She will probably be inconsolable if her plans fall through. 如果她的计划落空，她可能会非常悲痛。


虚拟语气:
If I lost my family, I would be inconsolable. 如果我失去我的家人，我会悲痛欲绝。
I wish I wasn't inconsolable, but the loss is too great. 我希望我不至于如此悲痛，但损失太大了。
