retiarius:/ˌrɛtɪˈɛəriəs/| n. |网手，渔网斗士|A retiarius was a type of gladiator in ancient Rome who fought using a net (rete) and trident.  They typically fought against heavily armored gladiators, using their speed and agility to ensnare and subdue their opponents.  Their equipment consisted primarily of a net, a trident, and a dagger (pugio).|Example: The retiarius, with a swift movement, cast his net over the secutor, hoping to trap him.|近义词：gladiator, net-fighter|反义词：secutor (heavily armed gladiator)
