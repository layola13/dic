cockswain:/ˈkɒksən/| n.|舵手；艇长|The person in charge of a boat's steering and navigation, especially a rowing boat.|The cockswain expertly steered the boat through the rough waters. 舵手熟练地驾驶小船穿过汹涌的海面。| helmsman, steersman |  |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cockswain steers the boat. = 舵手掌舵。
He carefully directs the crew. = 他仔细地指挥船员。


一般过去时:
The cockswain steered the boat skillfully. = 舵手熟练地掌舵。
He skillfully directed the crew yesterday. = 他昨天熟练地指挥船员。
