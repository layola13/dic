re-revision:/ˌriːrɪˈvɪʒən/| n. |再修改，复审|A second revision; a further review and alteration of something already revised.  It implies a more thorough or complete revision than the first.|The professor requested a re-revision of the thesis due to several significant errors.|近义词：second revision, further review,  amendment|反义词：final version, original draft


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The editor requests a re-revision of the manuscript. = 编辑要求对稿件进行再次修改。
She is undertaking a re-revision of her research paper. = 她正在对她的研究论文进行复审。


一般过去时:
The author completed a re-revision of the book chapter. = 作者完成了对书中章节的再次修改。
They made a re-revision of the marketing plan after the meeting. = 会议后，他们对营销计划进行了复审。
