reseeing:/ˌriːˈsiːɪŋ/| v.|重新审视，重新看待|The act of seeing something again, often with a new perspective or understanding.  It implies a deeper or more critical examination than a simple second glance. |例句：Re-seeing the painting after ten years, I noticed details I'd missed before. 十年后重新审视这幅画，我注意到之前错过的细节。


例句：After the argument, she needed time for reseeding their relationship. 争吵过后，她需要时间来重新审视他们的关系。


近义词：reconsidering, re-evaluating, reviewing, reassessing |反义词：ignoring, overlooking
