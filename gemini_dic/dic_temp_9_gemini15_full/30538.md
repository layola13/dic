cephalometry:/ˌsɛfəˈlɒmətri/ | n. |头颅测量术 |The scientific measurement of the head, especially the skull.  It involves taking precise measurements of the head and face to assess growth, development, and the presence of any abnormalities. Cephalometry is often used in orthodontics and craniofacial surgery to plan treatment and monitor progress. | Example:  Cephalometric analysis revealed a significant asymmetry in the patient's facial structure.  头颅测量分析显示患者面部结构存在明显不对称。
