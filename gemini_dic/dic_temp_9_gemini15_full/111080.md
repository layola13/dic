listing:/ˈlɪstɪŋ/ | n. | 列表；清单；登记；上市 | A detailed record or catalog of items.  A listing of all the participants is available. | 近义词：list, catalog, register, inventory | 反义词：absence, omission


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store listing includes all the products. = 商店的清单包括所有产品。
This website is listing new houses for sale. = 这个网站正在列出待售的新房。


一般过去时:
She listed all her belongings before moving. = 她搬家前列出了她的所有财产。
The company listed its shares on the stock exchange. = 公司将其股票上市。


现在完成时:
They have listed all the necessary items for the trip. = 他们已经列出了旅行所需的所有物品。
The company has been listed on the stock exchange for ten years. = 这家公司已上市十年了。


过去完成时:
He had listed the ingredients before he started cooking. = 他在开始烹饪之前已经列出了配料。
The artist had already listed his paintings in the gallery before the exhibition. = 在展览之前，艺术家已经将他的绘画作品登记在画廊里了。


将来时:
They will list the winners on their website. = 他们将在他们的网站上列出获奖者。
We will be listing our house for sale next month. = 我们下个月将把房子挂牌出售。


虚拟语气:
If I listed all my problems, it would take all day. = 如果我把所有问题都列出来，那将花费一整天。
I wish I had listed the tasks in order of priority. = 我希望我之前已按优先级列出任务。


被动语态:
The items were listed alphabetically. = 这些物品按字母顺序排列。
The new products will be listed on the website soon. = 新产品很快就会在网站上列出。


疑问句:
Is your name listed on the guest list? = 你的名字在宾客名单上吗？
What items are listed in the contract? = 合同中列出了哪些项目？


否定句:
The document doesn't list all the necessary details. = 这份文件没有列出所有必要的细节。
He didn't list his address on the form. = 他没有在表格上填写他的地址。
