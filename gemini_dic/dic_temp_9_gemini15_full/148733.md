overtreatments:/ˌoʊvərˈtriːtmənts/ | n. |过度治疗；治疗过度|The act of providing more medical treatment than is necessary or beneficial to a patient.  This can lead to adverse effects, increased costs, and unnecessary suffering. |例句：Overtreatments are a significant concern in modern healthcare, often leading to unnecessary side effects. 过度治疗是现代医疗保健中一个重要的关注点，它经常导致不必要的副作用。  The patient suffered from overtreatments, resulting in prolonged recovery time. 这位病人遭受了过度治疗，导致康复时间延长。|近义词：overmedication, excessive treatment, medical overkill |反义词：under treatment, appropriate treatment
