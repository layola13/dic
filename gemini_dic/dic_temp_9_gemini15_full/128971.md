niobite:/ˈnaɪəbaɪt/| n. |铌铁矿|Niobite is a mineral consisting of niobium and tantalum oxides.  It's an important ore for these two metals, crucial in various high-tech applications.  The exact composition can vary, leading to different names like columbite-tantalite. |The niobite deposit in this region is quite extensive and rich in valuable minerals.  这地区的铌铁矿矿藏相当广阔，富含宝贵的矿物。|近义词：columbite-tantalite (a more precise term)|反义词：无
