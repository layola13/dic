globate:/ˈɡloʊbeɪt/| v.|使成球形；全球化|To make or become spherical or globe-like.  The process of making something round like a globe.  It can also refer to the process of globalization, making something worldwide in scope.|例句：The sculptor globated the clay into a perfect sphere.  雕塑家把粘土塑造成一个完美的球体。|The company is attempting to globate its operations. 这家公司正试图将其业务全球化。|近义词：spheroid, round, globalize|反义词：flatten, angularize
