kitties:/ˈkɪtiz/| n.|小猫们|The plural of "kitty," a term of endearment for a cat, especially a young one.  It often implies multiple cats, usually small and cute.|例句：The kitties were playing with a ball of yarn. 小猫们正在玩线球。  The farmer found a litter of adorable kitties in the barn. 农场主在谷仓里发现了一窝可爱的小猫。|近义词：kittens, cats,pussycats|反义词:None
