dithers:/ˈdɪðərz/| v.|犹豫不决，颤抖，絮絮叨叨 |To be indecisive; to hesitate or waver.  To tremble or shake slightly. To talk in a rambling or confused way.|例句：He dithered over the menu for ages before ordering. 他在点菜前犹豫了很久。  The old man dithered in his chair, his hands trembling. 老人颤抖着坐在椅子上，双手发抖。|近义词：hesitate, waver, falter, tremble, shake|反义词：decide, determine, resolute
