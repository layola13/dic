gallantry:/ˈɡælənəri/| n.|勇敢；侠义；殷勤；献殷勤|Courageous behavior, especially in battle;  chivalrous attention to women;  polite and courteous behavior.|例句：He showed great gallantry in the face of danger. 他在危险面前表现出极大的勇敢。  His gallantry towards the ladies was charming. 他对女士的殷勤很迷人。|近义词：bravery, heroism, chivalry, courtesy|反义词：cowardice, cravenness, rudeness
