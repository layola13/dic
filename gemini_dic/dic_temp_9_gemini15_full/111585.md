lobotomizations:/ˌloʊbəˈtɑːmɪzeɪʃənz/ | n. |  脑叶切除术（复数） | The surgical procedure of severing connections in the prefrontal cortex of the brain.  This was once used as a treatment for mental illness, but is now considered obsolete and unethical due to its severe side effects. | 例句：The unethical lobotomizations performed in the mid-20th century are now widely condemned.  = 20世纪中期进行的不道德的脑叶切除术现在受到了广泛的谴责。


The widespread use of lobotomizations was eventually discontinued due to the development of safer and more effective treatments for mental illness. = 由于开发出更安全有效的精神疾病治疗方法，脑叶切除术的广泛使用最终停止了。
