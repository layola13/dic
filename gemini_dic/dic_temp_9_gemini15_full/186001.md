reweaving: /ˌriːˈwiːvɪŋ/ | v. |重新编织；重新组合；重新审视 |The act of weaving again; the process of reworking or reorganizing something.  It can refer to physical weaving of fabric or a metaphorical reworking of ideas, relationships, or narratives. |例句：She spent hours reweaving the torn tapestry. 她花了数小时重新编织破损的挂毯。


例句：The author is reweaving the narrative to include new evidence. 作者正在重新编排叙事以包含新的证据。


近义词：reworking, reconstructing, reorganizing, re-examining


反义词：dismantling, destroying, unraveling


词性：动词
