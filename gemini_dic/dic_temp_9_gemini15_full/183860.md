reselected:/ˌriːsəˈlɛktɪd/| adj.| 重新选择的，再次选择的 |  Having been selected again.  This is the past participle of the verb "reselect". It describes something or someone that has been chosen a second time. |Example: The reselected committee members began their work immediately. 重新选择的委员会成员立即开始工作。 The reselected candidate gave a victory speech.  再次当选的候选人发表了胜选演讲。|近义词：chosen again, selected anew, re-elected|反义词：deselected, rejected
