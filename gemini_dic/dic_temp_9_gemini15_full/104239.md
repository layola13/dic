jurisprudence:/ˌdʒʊərɪsˈpruːd(ə)ns/| n. |法理学；法学；判例法 |The theory or philosophy of law.  Jurisprudence examines the nature of law, its relationship to morality and justice, and its application in society. It explores fundamental legal concepts such as rights, duties, and justice.  |例句：He is a professor of jurisprudence. 他是一位法理学教授。|例句：The study of jurisprudence helps to understand the complexities of legal systems.  学习法理学有助于理解法律体系的复杂性。|近义词：legal theory, law, legal philosophy |反义词：N/A
