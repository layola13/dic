retrofits:/ˈrɛtroʊfɪts/| vt. n.|改装，改造；翻新|To modify (something) by adding new or different features or components.|The company retrofits older aircraft with new engines. 公司用新引擎改装旧飞机。|近义词：modify, renovate, upgrade|反义词：degrade, deteriorate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They retrofit old buildings with modern insulation. = 他们用现代绝缘材料改造旧建筑。
The mechanic retrofits cars with advanced safety features. = 技师为汽车改装先进的安全装置。


一般过去时:
The factory retrofitted the machines with new safety guards. = 工厂为机器安装了新的安全防护装置。
We retrofitted our home with solar panels last year. = 我们去年为我们的家安装了太阳能电池板。


现在完成时:
They have retrofitted several of their older models with improved fuel efficiency. = 他们已经为几款旧型号的汽车改进了燃油效率。
The city has retrofitted many of its streetlights with LED bulbs. = 该市已将许多路灯改装为LED灯泡。


过去完成时:
They had retrofitted the engine before the race. = 比赛前他们已经改装了发动机。
The engineers had retrofitted the system with a new control panel. = 工程师们已经用新的控制面板改装了系统。


将来时:
The company will retrofit the building with a new fire suppression system. = 公司将为建筑物安装新的消防系统。
We will retrofit our heating system to be more energy-efficient. = 我们将改造我们的供暖系统，使其更节能。


虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would retrofit the entire house. = 如果我们有更多钱，我们会改造整栋房子。
It would be great if they retrofitted the old stadium with better seating. = 如果他们能用更好的座位改造旧体育场就好了。


被动语态:
The old car was retrofitted with a new engine. = 旧车安装了新发动机。
The building is being retrofitted with energy-efficient windows. = 这栋建筑正在安装节能窗户。


疑问句:
Will the plane be retrofitted with new safety equipment? = 这架飞机会安装新的安全设备吗？
Have they retrofitted the software with the latest security patches? = 他们是否为软件安装了最新的安全补丁？


否定句:
They did not retrofit the old factory with modern equipment. = 他们没有用现代设备改造旧工厂。
We will not retrofit the computer with a new hard drive. = 我们不会为电脑安装新的硬盘。
