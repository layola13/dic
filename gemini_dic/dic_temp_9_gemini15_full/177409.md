rebaiting:/riːˈbeɪtɪŋ/| v.| 再诱饵，重新设置诱饵 | The act of replacing or reapplying bait.  It often refers to fishing or trapping, but can also be used metaphorically. | 例句：The fisherman spent the morning rebaiting his hooks. 渔夫花了上午的时间重新装鱼饵。The marketing team decided to re-bait their campaign with a new offer. 市场营销团队决定用新的优惠重新吸引顾客。 | 近义词：re-luring, re-attracting | 反义词：removing bait, abandoning

一般现在时:
He rebaiting his traps every day. 他每天都重新装陷阱。
They re-bait the hooks regularly. 他们定期重新装钩。


一般过去时:
She rebaited the mouse trap after it was sprung. 老鼠夹子被触发后，她重新装了诱饵。
The anglers rebaited their lines after catching a few fish. 钓客们钓了几条鱼后重新装了鱼饵。


现在完成时:
He has rebated his fishing lines three times today. 他今天已经三次重新装鱼饵了。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
If I had more bait, I would re-bait the hooks. 如果我有更多的诱饵，我会重新装鱼钩。
If they had rebaited sooner, they might have caught more fish. 如果他们早点重新装饵，可能会钓到更多鱼。


被动语态:
The traps were rebated by the gamekeeper. 这些陷阱是由猎场管理员重新装的。
The lines were rebated before the competition started. 比赛开始前，鱼线已经重新装了饵。


疑问句:

否定句:

They hadn't rebaited the hooks, which is why they didn't catch any fish. 他们没有重新装鱼钩，这就是他们没有钓到鱼的原因。
