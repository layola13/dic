punishments:/ˈpʌnɪʃmənts/| n.|惩罚；处罚|The act of punishing someone for wrongdoing.  It can refer to a single instance of punishment or a system of punishments.|例句：The punishments for stealing are severe in this country.  这个国家的偷窃惩罚很严厉。|例句：He received a variety of punishments for his bad behavior. 他因为不良行为受到了各种惩罚。|近义词：penalties, sanctions, chastisements|反义词：rewards, accolades, praise|名词

词源:
The word "punishment" originates from the Old French word "punissement," which itself comes from the verb "punir," meaning "to punish."  The ultimate origin is the Latin word "punire," which also means "to punish."  The meaning and usage have remained relatively consistent throughout history, referring to the infliction of penalty for wrongdoing.
