词源:

The word "nonpoliticizations" is formed from:

* **non-**: a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of"
* **politicization**: the act of making something political; giving something a political character.  This itself derives from "politics" + "-ization" (a suffix indicating the process of making something).  "Politics" originates from the Greek word "politikos," meaning "of, for, or relating to citizens."
