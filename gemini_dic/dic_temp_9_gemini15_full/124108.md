mortalist:/ˈmɔːrtəlɪst/| n. |凡人；必死之人|A mortalist is a person who believes that all beings are mortal, and that death is an inevitable part of life.  They often focus on the significance of life in the face of mortality. |例句：Many philosophers are mortalists, grappling with the meaning of life in the face of death.|近义词：mortal, human being|反义词：immortal, deity
