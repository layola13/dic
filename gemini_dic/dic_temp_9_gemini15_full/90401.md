howling:/ˈhaʊlɪŋ/ | v. adj. n.| 嚎叫；呼啸；大声喊叫；狂暴的；强烈的 |

英文解析:To howl means to make a long, loud, mournful cry, typically one associated with dogs or wolves.  It can also refer to the sound of wind or other things making a similar noise. As an adjective, it describes something extremely strong or intense. As a noun, it refers to the act of howling or the sound itself.

例句:

The wind was howling throughout the night.  =  整个晚上风都在呼啸。

The dogs were howling at the moon. = 狗对着月亮嚎叫。


近义词: yell, scream, roar, wail, shriek,  (for wind) roar, whistle

反义词: whisper, murmur,  (for intensity) gentle, mild


词性: Verb (v.), Adjective (adj.), Noun (n.)
