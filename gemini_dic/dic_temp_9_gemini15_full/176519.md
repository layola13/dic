re-quote:/ˌriːˈkwoʊt/| vt.|再次报价；重新报价|To quote again, especially at a different price.|例句：The supplier re-quoted the price after reviewing the specifications. 供应商在审查规格后重新报了价。
例句：We need to re-quote the project due to increased material costs. 由于材料成本增加，我们需要重新报价项目。|近义词：reprice, quote again|反义词：accept, finalize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-quote the price every year. = 他们每年都会重新报价。
The company re-quotes its services regularly. = 公司定期重新报价其服务。

一般过去时:
He re-quoted the price after negotiations. = 他在谈判后重新报价。
She re-quoted the job after finding a cheaper supplier. = 她在找到一个更便宜的供应商后重新报了价。

现在完成时:
They have re-quoted the project several times. = 他们已经多次重新报价该项目。
The client has re-quoted the contract terms. = 客户已重新报价合同条款。

过去完成时:
They had re-quoted the price before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经重新报价。
She had re-quoted the project before realizing the mistake. = 她在意识到错误之前已经重新报价了项目。

将来时:
They will re-quote the price next week. = 他们下周将重新报价。
We will re-quote the job if the materials cost increases. = 如果材料成本增加，我们将重新报价。

虚拟语气:
If the client asked, we would re-quote the project. = 如果客户要求，我们会重新报价项目。
I wish they had re-quoted the price earlier. = 我希望他们早点重新报价。

被动语态:

The contract will be re-quoted following the amendments. = 在修改后，合同将被重新报价。

疑问句:

否定句:
