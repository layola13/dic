extraneity:/ˌɛkstreɪˈneɪəti/| n. |外部性；外来性；无关性 |The state or quality of being extraneous; something extraneous.  It refers to something that is irrelevant or unrelated to the main subject or matter at hand. It can also refer to something that is external or foreign in origin. |例句：The extraneity of the details distracted from the main point of the argument.  细节的无关性分散了论点的重点。

The extraneity of the new culture shocked the immigrant. 新文化的陌生性让移民感到震惊。


近义词：irrelevance, unrelatedness, foreignness, exteriority |反义词：relevance, pertinence, intrinsicness, internality

词源：From Late Latin *extraneus (extraneous) + -ity.  The Latin *extraneus itself derives from *extra ("outside") + *aneus ("belonging to").  Essentially, it means "belonging to the outside."
