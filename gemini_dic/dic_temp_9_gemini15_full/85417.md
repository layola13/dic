harvesting:/ˈhɑːvɪstɪŋ/| v.| 收获；收割；采集 | The process of gathering a crop from the field, or the act of collecting something valuable.  |例句：Farmers are busy harvesting the wheat.  农民们正忙着收割小麦。|例句：The company is harvesting data from its users. 这家公司正在从用户那里收集数据。|近义词：gathering, reaping, collecting |反义词：planting, sowing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are harvesting apples this week. = 我们这周正在收苹果。
They harvest rice in the autumn. = 他们秋天收割稻米。

一般过去时:
The farmers harvested the corn last month. = 农民们上个月收割了玉米。
We harvested a large amount of honey from the hives. = 我们从蜂箱里收获了大量的蜂蜜。

现在完成时:
They have already harvested all the grapes. = 他们已经收完了所有的葡萄。
The team has harvested valuable data for the research. = 团队已经为研究收集到了宝贵的数据。

过去完成时:
They had harvested the wheat before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经收割了小麦。
The company had harvested enough information for the presentation. = 公司已经为演示收集到了足够的信息。

将来时:
We will be harvesting the pumpkins next month. = 我们下个月将要收南瓜。
They will harvest the coffee beans in the spring. = 他们将在春天收割咖啡豆。

虚拟语气:
If we harvested earlier, we would have had a better yield. = 如果我们早点收割，产量就会更好。
I wish we had harvested the crops before the frost. = 我真希望我们在霜冻来临之前收割了庄稼。

被动语态:
The wheat was harvested by machines. = 小麦是用机器收割的。
The data is being harvested for the upcoming report. = 数据正在被收集用于即将发布的报告。

疑问句:
When are you harvesting the tomatoes? = 你什么时候收番茄？
Have they finished harvesting the barley? = 他们收割大麦了吗？

否定句:
They haven't harvested the potatoes yet. = 他们还没有收土豆。
We didn't harvest enough corn this year. = 我们今年玉米收成不好。
