charismatics:/ˌkærɪzˈmætɪks/| n.|有魅力的人；有超凡魅力的群体|People who are charismatic, or a group of such people.  They possess a compelling charm that inspires devotion in others. This often involves exceptional communication skills, strong leadership qualities, and an ability to connect with people on an emotional level.|例句：The charismatic leader inspired his followers to great achievements.  这位有魅力的领导人激励他的追随者取得了巨大的成就。

例句：Many charismatic preachers attract large congregations. 许多有魅力的传教士吸引了大量的会众。

近义词： magnetic, captivating, enchanting, compelling

反义词：uninspiring, dull, uncharismatic, repellent
