in favor: /ɪn ˈfeɪvər/ | adj. |赞成的，支持的 |in support of or approving of something or someone.  |例句：The majority of people are in favor of the new law. 大多数人都赞成这项新法律。|例句：She spoke in favor of the proposed changes. 她发言支持拟议的变更。|近义词：supportive of, in support of, for, pro |反义词：opposed to, against, opposed

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are in favor of the proposal. = 他们赞成这项提案。
We are in favor of equal rights. = 我们支持平等权利。


一般过去时:
The committee was in favor of the project. = 委员会赞成这个项目。
He was in favor of stricter regulations. = 他赞成更严格的法规。


现在完成时:
The public has been in favor of this policy for years. = 公众多年来一直支持这项政策。
She has always been in favor of environmental protection. = 她一直以来都支持环保。


过去完成时:
They had been in favor of the idea before the new evidence emerged. = 在新的证据出现之前，他们一直赞成这个主意。
The board had been in favor of the merger until the recent financial difficulties. = 直到最近的财政困难，董事会一直赞成合并。


将来时:
They will be in favor of the new initiative. = 他们将会支持这项新举措。
The government is expected to be in favor of tax cuts. = 预计政府将支持减税。


虚拟语气:
If they were in favor, the project would succeed. = 如果他们支持，这个项目就会成功。
I wish the public were in favor of our ideas. = 我希望公众支持我们的想法。


被动语态:
The proposal was not in favor with the board.  = 董事会不支持这项提案。（注意此处的被动语态表达方式不同，更强调提案不受欢迎）
The policy has been in favor of the majority. = 这项政策得到了大多数人的支持。（此句被动语态强调政策受到支持）


疑问句:
Is the judge in favor of the defendant? = 法官支持被告吗？
Are you in favor of this plan? = 你支持这个计划吗？


否定句:
The committee is not in favor of the changes. = 委员会不支持这些改变。
He wasn't in favor of the new rules. = 他不支持新规。


PHRASE->
in favor of (something/someone): 支持…
speak in favor of:  为…说话，支持…
vote in favor of:  投票支持…
be in favor with: 受…欢迎
in someone's favor: 对某人有利
