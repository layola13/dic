loquats:/ˈlɒkwɑːts/| n. |枇杷|Loquats are small, orange-yellow fruits with a sweet and slightly tart flavor.  They are native to southeastern Asia and are now cultivated in many parts of the world.|例句：The loquats in our garden are ripe and ready to pick. 我们花园里的枇杷成熟了，可以采摘了。
例句：She made a delicious jam from the loquats she harvested. 她用收获的枇杷做了一罐美味的果酱。
