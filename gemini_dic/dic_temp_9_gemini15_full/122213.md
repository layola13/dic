misvocalization:/ˌmɪsˌvoʊkələˈzeɪʃən/| n. |发音错误，误读|The act of pronouncing a word incorrectly; a wrong or inaccurate pronunciation.  It can be caused by various factors, including speech impediments, dialectal variations, or simply a lack of knowledge about correct pronunciation.|例句：The child's misvocalization of "spaghetti" as "spaghetty" was quite common.|近义词：mispronunciation, malapropism|反义词：correct pronunciation, accurate articulation|名词
