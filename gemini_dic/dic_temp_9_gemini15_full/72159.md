floorboarding:/ˈflɔːrˌbɔːrdɪŋ/ | n. |地板铺设；地板；地板条 | The process of laying floorboards; also refers to the floorboards themselves.  | Example: The floorboarding in the old house needed replacing. 老房子里的地板需要更换。  The floorboarding creaked underfoot. 地板在脚下吱吱作响。|近义词：flooring, planking |反义词：None directly applicable.
