in-works: /ɪn wɜːrks/ | adj. |正在进行中；开发中 |  This term describes something that is currently being developed, created, or worked on, but is not yet completed or ready for release. It implies an ongoing process. | 例句：The new software is in-works, so it's not available yet.  新的软件正在开发中，所以现在还不能用。

The project is currently in-works, and we expect to launch it next month. 这个项目目前正在进行中，我们预计下个月启动。


近义词：under development, underway, in progress, being developed,  in the pipeline


反义词：completed, finished, done, finalized


词性：形容词
