orbits:/ˈɔːrbɪts/ | /ˈɔːrbɪts/| n. v.|轨道；运行轨道；环绕|The path of a celestial body or artificial satellite as it revolves around another of greater mass, such as the Earth revolving around the Sun.  The act of revolving around a center. |例句：The Earth orbits the Sun. 地球绕太阳运行。

The moon orbits the Earth. 月亮绕地球运行。


近义词：revolves, circles, revolves around | 反义词：remains stationary, is fixed

词性: noun, verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The Earth orbits the sun. = 地球绕着太阳运行。
The satellite orbits the planet. = 卫星绕着行星运行。


一般过去时:
The comet orbited the star. = 这颗彗星绕着恒星运行。
The spacecraft orbited Mars for several months. = 宇宙飞船绕火星运行了几个月。


现在完成时:
The satellite has orbited the Earth for ten years. = 这颗卫星已经绕地球运行十年了。
The planet has completed many orbits around its sun. = 这颗行星已经绕其恒星运行了许多圈。


过去完成时:
The probe had orbited the moon before transmitting data. = 探测器在传输数据之前已经绕月球运行。
The spacecraft had completed three orbits before the engine malfunctioned. = 宇宙飞船在发动机出现故障之前已经完成了三次绕轨道运行。


将来时:
The new satellite will orbit the Earth at a higher altitude. = 新卫星将在更高的轨道上绕地球运行。
The spaceship will orbit the planet for data collection. = 宇宙飞船将绕行星运行以收集数据。


虚拟语气:
If the planet orbited closer, it would be hotter. = 如果这颗行星离得更近，它就会更热。
If the satellite had orbited at a different angle, the results would have been different. = 如果卫星以不同的角度运行，结果就会不同。


被动语态:
The planet is orbited by its moons. = 这颗行星被它的卫星环绕着。
The Earth is orbited by many artificial satellites. = 地球被许多人造卫星环绕着。


疑问句:
Does the moon orbit the Earth? = 月亮绕地球运行吗？
How many times has the space station orbited the Earth? = 太空站绕地球运行了多少次？


否定句:
The asteroid did not orbit the sun. = 这颗小行星没有绕太阳运行。
The satellite is not orbiting correctly. = 卫星运行不正确。
