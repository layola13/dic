frequency:/ˈfriːkwənsi/| /ˈfriːkwɛnsi/ | n. |频率；频繁；发生率 |The rate at which something occurs over a particular period or in a given sample.  It can refer to the number of times something happens, the rate of repetition of a wave or oscillation, or how often something is encountered. |The frequency of bus services has increased recently.  公共汽车服务的频率最近增加了。| The heart beats with a certain frequency. 心脏以一定的频率跳动。|近义词：rate, occurrence, prevalence |反义词：rarity, infrequency


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The radio station broadcasts at a high frequency. = 这个电台以高频率广播。
This event occurs with great frequency. = 这件事经常发生。


一般过去时:
The frequency of earthquakes in this region was low last year. = 去年该地区地震的频率很低。
The machine operated at a lower frequency yesterday. = 机器昨天以较低的频率运行。


现在完成时:
The frequency of accidents has decreased since the new safety measures were implemented. = 自从实施新的安全措施以来，事故发生率下降了。
They have observed a higher frequency of this phenomenon recently. = 他们最近观察到这种现象的频率更高。


过去完成时:
Before the storm, the frequency of lightning strikes had increased dramatically. = 暴风雨来临之前，闪电的频率急剧增加。
The frequency of phone calls had been quite high before the internet became popular. = 在互联网普及之前，电话的频率相当高。


将来时:
The company predicts that the frequency of customer complaints will decrease next quarter. = 公司预测下个季度客户投诉的频率将会下降。
The frequency of these meetings will increase next year. = 明年这些会议的频率将会增加。


虚拟语气:
If the frequency were higher, we would get better results. = 如果频率更高，我们会得到更好的结果。
I wish the frequency of these interruptions were lower. = 我希望这些中断的频率更低。


被动语态:
The frequency is measured using a special device. = 频率是用特殊的设备测量的。
The frequency of the sound waves was carefully controlled. = 声波的频率受到仔细控制。


疑问句:
What is the frequency of this sound wave? = 这个声波的频率是多少？
What is the frequency of these occurrences? = 这些事件的频率是多少？


否定句:
The frequency of these problems is not high. = 这些问题的频率并不高。
The radio signal didn't have a consistent frequency. = 无线电信号的频率不稳定。
