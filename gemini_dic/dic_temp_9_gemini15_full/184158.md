resituates:/ˌriːˈsɪtʃuˌeɪt/| vt.|重新安置；重新定位；重新考虑|To place or establish again in a new or different position, situation, or context.  It implies a change from a previous state.|例句：The company resituated its headquarters to a more central location.  公司将其总部迁至更中心的位置。|例句：The historian resituated the events of the war within a broader social context.  这位历史学家在一个更广泛的社会背景下重新审视了战争的事件。|近义词：relocate, reposition, reinterpret|反义词：leave in place, maintain status quo|动词
