Those who are part of the regular staff or team.|例句：The bar was full of regulars enjoying their Friday night drinks. 这家酒吧挤满了享受周五晚上饮酒的常客。  The regulars at the comedy club always get the best seats. 喜剧俱乐部的常客总是能拿到最好的座位。|近义词：habitués, regulars, customers, patrons|反义词：newcomers, strangers
