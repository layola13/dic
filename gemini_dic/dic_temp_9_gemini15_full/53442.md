disappointments:/ˌdɪs.əˈpɔɪnt.mənts/| n.|令人失望的事物；失望；沮丧|The feeling of sadness or frustration caused by the non-fulfillment of one's hopes or expectations.  Disappointments can range from minor inconveniences to major setbacks. |例句：His exam results were a great disappointment. = 他的考试成绩令人非常失望。

His numerous disappointments led him to a period of deep depression. = 他接二连三的失望导致他陷入深深的抑郁。|近义词：frustrations, setbacks, letdowns|反义词：achievements, successes, fulfillments|名词
