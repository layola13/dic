reinjects:/ˌriːɪnˈdʒɛkt/| vt.|重新注射；再次注入|To inject again or anew.  The term implies a previous injection and a renewed action of injecting.|例句：The doctor reinjected the patient with the necessary medication.  医生再次给病人注射了必要的药物。

例句：The company reinjected capital into its struggling subsidiary. 这家公司向其苦苦挣扎的子公司再次注资。


近义词：reintroduce, reapply, inject again


反义词：withdraw, extract, remove


词性：动词
