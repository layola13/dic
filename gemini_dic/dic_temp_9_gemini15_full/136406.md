nonretaliation:/ˌnɒnˌrɪtæliˈeɪʃən/| n. | 不报复；不反击 | The absence of retaliation or revenge; a policy or practice of not taking retaliatory action.  | Example: The company has a strong policy of nonretaliation against employees who report wrongdoing. 公司有一项强有力的政策，不会报复举报不当行为的员工。 | 近义词：non-revenge, forbearance, leniency | 反义词：retaliation, revenge, reprisal
