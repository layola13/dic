mispriced:/ˌmɪsˈpraɪst/| adj. | 定价错误的，价格错误的 | Incorrectly priced;  priced either too high or too low.  This usually refers to a situation where the price does not accurately reflect the true value of something. |例句：The stock was mispriced by the market, leading to significant losses for some investors.  市场对这只股票的定价错误，导致一些投资者遭受重大损失。

The antique vase was grossly mispriced at a flea market; it was worth thousands.  这只古董花瓶在跳蚤市场上的价格严重错误；它值数千美元。|近义词：underpriced, overpriced, wrongly priced |反义词：correctly priced, fairly priced |词性:形容词
