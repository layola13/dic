cork:/kɔːrk/| n. |软木塞；软木；软木树皮|The outer bark of the cork oak, used to make stoppers for bottles and other items.  It is lightweight, elastic, and impermeable to liquids and gases. |例句：He popped the cork on the champagne bottle. 他打开了香槟酒瓶的软木塞。

例句：The cork was stuck fast in the bottle.  软木塞紧紧地卡在瓶子里。


近义词：stopper (as a bottle closure)


反义词：无


词性:名词
