overconcentrates: /ˌoʊvərˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪts/ | v. | 过度集中；过于专注 | To concentrate to an excessive degree; to focus too intently on something.  |例句：He overconcentrates on his work and neglects his health. 他过度专注于工作而忽略了健康。

例句：She overconcentrates on minor details, losing sight of the bigger picture. 她过于专注于细枝末节，忽略了大局。


近义词：hyperfocus, overfocus, obsess


反义词：distract, diffuse, scatter


词性:动词


USAGE->

He overconcentrates on his studies. = 他过度专注于学习。
She overconcentrates on small details. = 她过度专注于细节。

被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used passively.)  The example below is awkward and unnatural.  Avoid using the passive voice with this verb.

疑问句:

否定句:
He doesn't overconcentrate; he maintains a good work-life balance. = 他不会过度专注；他保持了良好的工作与生活平衡。
She doesn't overconcentrate on single tasks; she manages her time effectively. = 她不会过度专注于单一任务；她有效地管理时间。
