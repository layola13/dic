empathise-with:/ˈɛmpəθaɪz wɪð/| v.| 同情，共鸣 | To understand and share the feelings of another.  | 例句：I empathise with her loss; it must be incredibly painful. 我同情她的损失；一定非常痛苦。 | 例句：It's important to empathise with your colleagues' struggles.  理解同事的困境很重要。| 近义词：sympathise with, understand, relate to | 反义词：be indifferent to, be unsympathetic to | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I empathise with his situation. = 我同情他的处境。
She empathizes with the victims of the accident. = 她同情事故的受害者。

一般过去时:
I empathised with her when she told me about her problems. = 当她告诉我她的问题时，我同情她。

现在完成时:
I have empathised with many people in my life. = 我一生中同情过很多人。
She has empathized with countless individuals facing hardship. = 她同情过无数面临困境的个人。

过去完成时:
