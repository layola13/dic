piles:/paɪlz/| n.|堆；大量；一堆堆|A pile is a collection of things placed on top of each other.  It can refer to a disorganized heap or a neatly stacked arrangement.  The word can also be used figuratively to refer to a large quantity or amount.|例句：There's a pile of books on my desk. 我的书桌上有一堆书。

There are piles of dirty laundry in the bedroom. 卧室里堆满了脏衣服。


近义词：heap, stack, mass, accumulation|反义词：scatter, disperse|名词
