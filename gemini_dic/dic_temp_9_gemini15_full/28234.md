capstones:/ˈkæpˌstoʊnz/| n.|顶石；最终成果；最重要的事情|A capstone is something that is placed on top of a structure, especially a building, to complete it.  It can also refer to a culminating project or achievement that signifies the completion of a larger undertaking. |例句：The senior thesis is the capstone of our undergraduate program.  = 毕业论文是我们本科课程的最终成果。

The new statue is the capstone of the park's renovation. = 新雕像是公园翻新工程的最终成果。


近义词：culmination, climax, keystone, crowning achievement|反义词：beginning, foundation, start|名词
