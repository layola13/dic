proconservationists:/ˌproʊkɒnsərˈveɪʃənɪsts/| n. | 保护主义者 |Those who actively support and promote conservation efforts.  They advocate for the protection of natural resources and the environment.  |Example: Proconservationists rallied against the proposed dam project, citing its negative impact on the local ecosystem. 保护主义者们反对拟议中的水坝项目，理由是它对当地生态系统有负面影响。|近义词：environmentalists, conservationists, preservationists |反义词：environmental destroyers, exploitationists
