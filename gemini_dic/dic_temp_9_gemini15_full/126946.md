narrations:/nəˈreɪʃənz/| n. |叙述；讲述；解说 |The act of narrating; a story or account of events; a descriptive and often explanatory commentary.  |Example: The film's narrations were captivating and added depth to the story.|近义词：accounts, descriptions, recitals, reports |反义词：silence, abstraction
