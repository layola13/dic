lotteries:/ˈlɒtəriːz/ | n. |彩票；抽奖 |A lottery is a game of chance in which people buy tickets with numbers, and the winners are chosen randomly.  Lotteries are often used to raise money for good causes. |Example: Many people buy lottery tickets hoping to win a large sum of money.  许多人购买彩票，希望能赢得一大笔钱。  The company held a lottery to select a winner for the new car. 这家公司举行抽奖来选出新车的获奖者。|近义词：raffles, sweepstakes |反义词：N/A
