mounty:/ˈmaʊnti/| n.|加拿大皇家骑警；骑警|A member of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police.  The term is often used informally. |例句：The mounty rode his horse through the town.  加拿大皇家骑警骑着马穿过小镇。


例句：Several mountys were investigating the crime scene.  几名骑警正在调查犯罪现场。

近义词：police officer, Mountie (informal), RCMP officer

反义词：无


词性:名词
