frecklier:/ˈfrɛklɪər/| adj.|  布满雀斑的(更甚的)| More covered with freckles; having more freckles.  It is the comparative form of "freckled".|例句：Her skin became frecklier after a summer in the sun.  (她的皮肤在阳光下晒了一个夏天后，雀斑更多了。)


The child's face was frecklier than his brother's. (孩子的脸比他哥哥的脸雀斑更多。)

近义词：spottier, speckled|反义词：clear, unblemished


词性:形容词
