prevolunteering:/ˌpriːˈvɒlənˌtɪərɪŋ/| n.| 预先志愿服务；提前志愿工作 |  The act of volunteering before an event or need arises, often involving preparation or planning for future volunteer activities.  This could include training, fundraising, or organizing supplies in anticipation of a volunteer opportunity. | Example:  The prevolunteering efforts ensured a smooth and efficient disaster relief operation. = 预先的志愿服务确保了灾难救援行动顺利有效地进行。 The organization conducted prevolunteering workshops to train volunteers for the upcoming marathon. = 该组织举办了预先志愿者培训班，为即将到来的马拉松比赛培训志愿者。|近义词：proactive volunteering, advance volunteering | 反义词：reactive volunteering, ad hoc volunteering
