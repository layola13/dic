selectee:/ˌsɪˈlɛktiː/| n. |被选中者，入选者 | A person who has been selected, especially for a job or position.  | Example:  She was a selectee for the prestigious scholarship program. 她被选中参加这个著名的奖学金项目。|近义词:chosen one, candidate, appointee |反义词:reject, non-candidate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company selects many applicants every year. = 公司每年都会选拔许多申请者。
He selects the best candidate for the job. = 他选择最适合这份工作的候选人。


一般过去时:
The committee selected the winner yesterday. = 委员会昨天选出了获胜者。
They selected a new CEO last month. = 他们上个月选了一位新的首席执行官。


现在完成时:
They have selected the team for the competition. = 他们已经选好了参赛队伍。
We have selected a new design for the product. = 我们已经为产品选择了一个新的设计。


过去完成时:
She had already selected her dress before the party. = 派对前她已经选好了衣服。
They had selected the location for the conference. = 他们已经选定了会议的地点。


将来时:
The school will select students for the exchange program next year. = 学校明年将选拔学生参加交换项目。
We will select a date for the wedding soon. = 我们很快就会选定婚礼日期。


虚拟语气:
If they selected me, I would be very happy. = 如果他们选中我，我会非常高兴。
I wish they had selected a different candidate. = 我希望他们能选择一位不同的候选人。


被动语态:
The winner was selected by the judges. = 获胜者由评委选出。
A new plan was selected by the board of directors. = 一个新的计划被董事会选中。


疑问句:
Who was selected for the position? = 谁被选上了这个职位？
Has the winner been selected yet? = 获胜者选出来了没有？


否定句:
They didn't select him for the team. = 他们没有选他进队。
She wasn't selected for the scholarship. = 她没有被选中获得奖学金。
