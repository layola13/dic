pomerium:/pəˈmɪəriəm/| n. |罗马城墙内神圣边界|The pomerium was a sacred boundary in ancient Rome, marking the limits of the city's territory within which the Roman gods were believed to reside and which was considered inviolable.  It was a ritually defined space, not a physical wall.  The pomerium's sanctity extended to the prohibition of military activities within its confines and the prevention of certain types of construction. Its precise location and extent shifted slightly over time with the expansion of Rome.|例句：The construction of the new temple was halted because it encroached upon the pomerium. = 新庙的建设被叫停，因为它侵占了罗马城墙内的神圣边界。

The crossing of the pomerium by an army was considered an act of sacrilege and could lead to dire consequences.  = 军队越过罗马城墙内的神圣边界被认为是亵渎神灵的行为，可能导致可怕的后果。|近义词：boundary, limit, border|反义词：exterior, outside, beyond
