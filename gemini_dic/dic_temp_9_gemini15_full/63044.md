enrich:/ɪnˈrɪtʃ/| vt.|使富裕；充实；改善|To improve or enhance something by adding something else.|例句：Travel enriches the mind. 旅行丰富了思想。|例句：He enriched the soup with herbs. 他用香草使汤的味道更浓郁。|近义词：improve, enhance, better|反义词：impoverish, deplete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new program enriches the curriculum. = 这项新课程丰富了课程设置。
This experience enriches my life. = 这段经历丰富了我的生活。

一般过去时:
The artist enriched the painting with vibrant colors. = 艺术家用鲜艳的色彩使绘画更加丰富。
The teacher enriched the lesson with real-life examples. = 老师用真实的例子使课程更加生动。

现在完成时:
Travel has enriched my understanding of different cultures. = 旅行使我对不同文化的理解更加深刻。
This experience has enriched my perspective. = 这段经历丰富了我的视角。

过去完成时:
She had enriched the soil before planting the seeds. = 在播种之前，她已经改良了土壤。
They had enriched their vocabulary through extensive reading. = 通过大量的阅读，他们已经扩大了词汇量。

将来时:
This new technology will enrich our lives in many ways. = 这项新技术将以多种方式丰富我们的生活。
The upcoming workshop will enrich participants' skills. = 即将来临的研讨会将提高参与者的技能。

虚拟语气:
If we enriched the soil, we would have a better harvest. = 如果我们改良土壤，我们将会有更好的收成。
I wish I had enriched my life with more travel experiences. = 我希望我曾经用更多的旅行经历来丰富我的生活。

被动语态:
The soil was enriched with fertilizer. = 土壤用肥料得到了改良。
The curriculum is enriched by the inclusion of new subjects. = 通过加入新的科目，课程设置得到了丰富。

疑问句:
How can we enrich our daily lives? = 我们怎样才能丰富我们的日常生活？
What can we do to enrich our community? = 我们能做些什么来丰富我们的社区？

否定句:
The lack of creativity did not enrich the presentation. = 缺乏创造力并没有使演示更加精彩。
He didn't enrich his essay with enough supporting details. = 他没有用足够的细节来充实他的论文。
