noncontrivances:/ˌnɒnkənˈtraɪvənsɪz/ | n. |非人为的巧合；自然发生的事物 |Things that are not contrived or artificial; natural occurrences.  The term implies a contrast with deliberate design or manipulation.  It emphasizes the lack of artificiality or forcedness. |The discovery of the ancient artifact was a complete noncontrivance; it was unearthed purely by accident.  这件古代文物的发现完全是偶然的；它纯粹是偶然被挖掘出来的。|coincidence, accident, chance occurrence |deliberate design, artifice, contrivance |名词
