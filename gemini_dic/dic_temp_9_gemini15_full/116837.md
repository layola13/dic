maximalism:/ˈmæksɪməlɪzəm/ | n. |极大主义；极度主义 |  A style or philosophy characterized by the maximum use of available means; the opposite of minimalism. It can be applied to art, design, music, and other fields, emphasizing excess, opulence, and complexity.  | Example: The opulent décor of the palace is a prime example of maximalism.  宫殿奢华的装饰是极大主义的典型例子。The artist's paintings, filled with vibrant colors and intricate details, showcase their commitment to maximalism.  这位艺术家的绘画作品充满了鲜艳的色彩和复杂的细节，展现了他们对极大主义的承诺。|近义词：excess, extravagance, opulence |反义词：minimalism, simplicity, restraint |名词
