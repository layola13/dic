horded:/hɔːrdɪd/| v. |囤积；积聚|To accumulate (something, especially valuable or useful things) in a hidden or secret place.  The verb implies a large quantity and often suggests a greedy or selfish motivation.|例句：The dragon horded gold in its cave.  这条龙在它的洞穴里囤积黄金。

例句：He horded food supplies during the famine. 他在饥荒期间囤积食物。


近义词：amassed, accumulated, stockpiled, cached

反义词：dispersed, distributed, shared
