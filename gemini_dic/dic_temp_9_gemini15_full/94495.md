importer's:/ɪmˈpɔːrtərz/ | n. |进口商的；进口商所属的 |  The word "importer's" is a possessive form of the noun "importer," indicating ownership or association. It refers to something belonging to or connected with an importer, a person or company that brings goods into a country.  | Example: The importer's warehouse was filled with exotic goods. 进口商的仓库里堆满了异国商品。  The importer's contract specified delivery dates. 进口商的合同中规定了交货日期。|近义词：dealer's, supplier's | 反义词：exporter's | 名词所有格
