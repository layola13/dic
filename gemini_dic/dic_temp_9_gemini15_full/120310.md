minicyclone: /ˈmɪniˌsaɪkloʊn/ | n. | 微型气旋 | A small-scale cyclone, typically less intense and shorter-lived than a full-scale cyclone.  It's characterized by strong rotating winds but covers a smaller area. | Example: The minicyclone caused localized flooding and power outages. 微型气旋导致局部地区洪水和停电。 | 近义词: small cyclone, mesoscale convective vortex (MCV), whirlwind (less intense) | 反义词:  anticyclone, high-pressure system
