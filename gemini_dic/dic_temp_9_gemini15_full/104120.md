jumping-rope: /ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ roʊp/ | n. |跳绳；跳绳运动| The act of jumping over a rope swung under one's feet, often done as a recreational activity or exercise.  It can also refer to the rope itself. |例句：The children enjoyed jumping-rope during recess.孩子们在课间休息时喜欢跳绳。

例句：She bought a new jumping-rope for her daughter. 她为女儿买了一条新的跳绳。

近义词：rope skipping, jump roping |反义词：无
