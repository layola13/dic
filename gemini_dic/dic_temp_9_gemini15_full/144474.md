outthought:/ˌaʊtˈθɔːt/| vt.|智胜，想得比…周到|To defeat or surpass someone in intelligence or planning.  It implies a strategic victory achieved through superior intellect.|例句：The detective outthought the criminal, leading to his arrest.  侦探智胜罪犯，导致了他的逮捕。

例句：She completely outthought her opponent in the chess match.  她在国际象棋比赛中完全智胜了对手。


近义词：outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver,  surpass

反义词：be outwitted, be outsmarted
