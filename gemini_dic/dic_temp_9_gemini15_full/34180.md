clamor:/ˈklæmər/| n., v.|喧嚣，吵闹；大声要求| a loud and confused noise, especially that of a crowd of people.|近义词：noise, uproar, outcry, din|反义词：silence, quiet, calm


NOUN->
The clamor of the crowd was deafening. = 人群的喧嚣震耳欲聋。

The protestors raised a clamor for reform. = 抗议者大声要求改革。


VERB->
They clamored for his resignation. = 他们大声要求他辞职。

The children clamored for attention. = 孩子们吵着要人注意。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *clamer (to shout), from Latin clamare (to shout).


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The crowd clamors for change. = 人群吵着要改变。
They clamor for better working conditions. = 他们大声要求改善工作条件。


一般过去时:
The audience clamored for an encore. = 观众大声要求再来一首。
The protesters clamored outside the parliament building. = 抗议者在议会大厦外大声抗议。


现在完成时:
The people have clamored for justice for years. = 人们多年来一直大声疾呼要求正义。
The media has clamored for answers to the scandal. = 媒体一直大声要求对丑闻做出解释。


过去完成时:
The villagers had clamored for help before the flood. = 洪水来临之前，村民们曾大声呼救。
They had clamored for a change in leadership for months. = 他们几个月来一直大声要求更换领导人。


将来时:
The fans will clamor for tickets to the concert. = 粉丝们将争相购买演唱会门票。
The public will clamor for accountability after the accident. = 事故发生后，公众将要求追究责任。


虚拟语气:
If they clamored louder, perhaps someone would listen. = 如果他们喊得更大声，也许有人会听。
I wish they wouldn't clamor so much. = 我希望他们不要吵得这么厉害。


被动语态:
His resignation was clamored for by many. = 许多人大声要求他辞职。
Changes were clamored for by the employees. = 员工们大声要求改变。


疑问句:
Does the public clamor for change? = 公众是否大声要求改变？
Are they still clamoring for their rights? = 他们还在为争取权利而大声疾呼吗？


否定句:
The people did not clamor for war. = 人们并没有大声要求战争。
They did not clamor for his dismissal. = 他们并没有大声要求解雇他。


PHRASE->
a clamor for justice = 对正义的强烈呼吁
a clamor of voices = 一片喧嚣声
raise a clamor = 发出喧闹声
a clamor against = 对…的抗议
the clamor of the market = 市场喧嚣
