patency:/ˈpeɪtənsi/| n. |通畅；开放性；畅通无阻|The state of being open or unobstructed; the ability of a vessel or duct to remain open.  It often refers to the openness of a bodily passage or vessel, such as a blood vessel or airway. |例句：The surgeon confirmed the patency of the artery after the operation. 手术后，外科医生确认了动脉的通畅性。

The doctor checked the patency of the patient's airway. 医生检查了病人的呼吸道是否通畅。|近义词：openness, permeability, unobstruction |反义词：obstruction, blockage, occlusion
