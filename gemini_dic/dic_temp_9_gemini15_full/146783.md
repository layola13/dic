overidentify:/ˌoʊvər aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ/| vt.|过度认同；过分认同|To identify too strongly with; to associate oneself excessively with.|例句：He overidentified with the underdog, feeling their pain and struggles intensely. 他过度认同弱者，强烈地感受到他们的痛苦和挣扎。|近义词：empathize excessively, over-relate|反义词：detach, disassociate
