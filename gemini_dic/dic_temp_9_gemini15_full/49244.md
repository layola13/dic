deleterious: /ˌdɛləˈtɪəriəs/ | adj. | 有害的，有毒的 | Harmful; injurious; detrimental.  | The deleterious effects of smoking are well documented. 吸烟的有害影响已被充分证明。 | harmful, detrimental, injurious, pernicious | beneficial, advantageous, helpful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chemical has deleterious effects on the environment.  这种化学物质对环境有害。
This policy has a deleterious impact on small businesses.  这项政策对小型企业有不利影响。


一般过去时:
The storm had deleterious consequences for the coastal communities.  风暴对沿海社区造成了有害的后果。
His actions had a deleterious effect on his reputation. 他的行为对他的声誉产生了不利影响。


现在完成时:
Pollution has had deleterious effects on air quality.  污染对空气质量产生了有害的影响。
Excessive sugar consumption has had a deleterious effect on his health. 过量摄入糖分对他的健康产生了有害的影响。


过去完成时:
The company's poor management had already had deleterious consequences before the new CEO arrived. 新任首席执行官到来之前，公司糟糕的管理已经造成了有害的后果。
They had already suffered deleterious effects from the prolonged drought before the rains came.  在雨水到来之前，他们已经遭受了长期干旱的有害影响。


将来时:
Continued exposure to radiation will have deleterious effects on your health.  持续接触辐射会对你的健康产生有害的影响。
The new regulations will likely have a deleterious impact on the economy.  新规可能会对经济产生不利影响。


虚拟语气:
If we continue this way, it will have deleterious consequences for the entire ecosystem. 如果我们继续这样下去，将会对整个生态系统造成有害的后果。
If he had known the consequences, he would have avoided the deleterious actions. 如果他知道后果，他就会避免有害的行为。


被动语态:
The ecosystem is being deleteriously affected by climate change. 生态系统正受到气候变化的有害影响。
The community was deleteriously affected by the recent flooding.  社区受到了最近洪水的有害影响。


疑问句:
Are there any deleterious effects associated with this medication?  这种药物是否有任何有害作用？
Could this new policy have deleterious effects on employment?  这项新政策会对就业产生有害影响吗？


否定句:
The results of the study were not deleterious; in fact, they were quite positive. 研究结果并非有害；事实上，结果相当积极。
This treatment has not had any deleterious side effects.  这种治疗没有任何有害的副作用。


PHRASE->
deleterious effect: 有害的影响
deleterious consequences: 有害的后果
deleterious impact: 不利的影响
deleterious substance: 有害物质
potentially deleterious: 潜在有害的

词源:
From Late Latin *dēlēterius, comparative of dēlēter "destroyer," from dēlēre "to destroy," from de- "off, away" + lēre "to gather, read."
