rhemes:/riːmz/| n.|主题成分，述谓成分|In linguistics, a rheme is the part of a sentence that contains the new information, the element that is being asserted or predicated about the theme. It's what the sentence is *about*.  The rheme is typically located towards the end of the sentence, although this isn't always the case.|The rheme of the sentence "The cat sat on the mat" is "sat on the mat".|topic, predicate|theme (in some contexts)|名词
