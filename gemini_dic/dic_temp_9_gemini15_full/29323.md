castigating: /ˈkæstɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 严厉批评；斥责；惩罚 | Severely criticizing or reprimanding someone; punishing. |

例句：

The teacher castigated the student for cheating on the exam. = 老师严厉批评了那个考试作弊的学生。

The critic castigated the author's new novel for its lack of originality. = 批评家严厉批评了作者的新小说缺乏原创性。


近义词：rebuking, reprimanding, scolding, censuring, chastising

反义词：praising, commending, applauding
