cheiropterygium:/ˌkaɪrəpˈtɪrɪdʒiəm/| n. |手翼；鳍状肢|A congenital anomaly characterized by a webbed hand or foot, resembling the wing of a bat. It is a type of ectrodactyly.  The condition can range in severity from mild webbing to complete fusion of the digits.  |Example: The child was born with a severe cheiropterygium, requiring surgical intervention to separate the fused fingers.  这个孩子天生患有严重的手翼畸形，需要进行手术来分开融合的指头。|近义词: webbed hand, ectrodactyly, syndactyly (partial)|反义词: normal hand or foot|名词
