pseudo-philosophical: /ˌsuːdoʊˌfɪləˈsɒfɪkəl/ | adj. | 似是而非的哲学的；伪哲学的 | Seemingly philosophical but lacking depth or intellectual rigor; superficially philosophical.  It often refers to ideas or arguments that mimic philosophical discourse but lack genuine philosophical substance.  | 例句：His pseudo-philosophical ramblings offered little insight into the actual problem.  他的似是而非的哲学胡言乱语并没有对实际问题提供什么见解。

His pseudo-philosophical pronouncements were mostly empty rhetoric. 他的伪哲学说法大多是空洞的辞令。


近义词：specious, sophistical, shallow, superficial, pretentious


反义词： profound, insightful, substantial, genuine, rigorous


词性：形容词
