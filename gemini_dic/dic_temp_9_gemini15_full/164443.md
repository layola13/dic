preenlargement:/ˌpriːɪnˈlɑːrdʒmənt/| n. |预先扩大；提前扩大|The act of enlarging something before it is needed or used.  It often implies a proactive or anticipatory increase in size, capacity, or scope.|Example: The preenlargement of the hospital's emergency room allowed them to handle the influx of patients during the flu season. 预先扩大的医院急诊室使他们能够应对流感季节患者的涌入。|近义词：preexpansion, advance enlargement|反义词：reduction, decrease|名词
