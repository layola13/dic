failed:/feɪld/| adj. v.|失败的；未能成功的|Failing to achieve a desired outcome or result.  It can describe an attempt, a plan, a person, or a thing.|例句：My attempt to climb the mountain failed. 我尝试爬山失败了。
The project failed due to lack of funding.  这个项目由于缺乏资金而失败了。|近义词：unsuccessful, unsuccessful,  deficient|反义词：successful, accomplished


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fails to understand the instructions. 他不明白这些指示。
The engine fails to start. 引擎无法启动。


一般过去时:
The experiment failed. 实验失败了。
She failed her driving test. 她驾驶考试不及格。


现在完成时:
He has failed several times. 他已经失败了好几次。
The company has failed to meet its targets. 公司未能达到目标。


过去完成时:
They had failed to warn us of the danger. 他们没有警告我们危险。
The plan had failed before it even started. 计划甚至还没开始就失败了。


将来时:
The mission will likely fail. 任务很可能会失败。
If he doesn't study harder, he will fail his exams. 如果他不努力学习，考试就会不及格。


虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I wouldn't have failed the exam. 如果我学习更努力，我就不会考试不及格。
It's a shame he failed, for he really tried hard. 他失败了真是可惜，因为他真的努力了。


被动语态:
The proposal was failed by the committee. 该提案被委员会否决了。
The project was failed due to unforeseen circumstances. 项目由于不可预见的情况而失败了。


疑问句:
Did the attempt fail? 尝试失败了吗？
Why did the negotiations fail?  谈判为什么失败了？


否定句:
The plan did not fail. 计划没有失败。
He did not fail to impress his audience. 他没有未能给他的听众留下深刻印象。
