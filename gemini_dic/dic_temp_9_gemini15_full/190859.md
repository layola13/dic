saxonic:/ˈsæksənɪk/| adj.|萨克森的；萨克森语的；|Of or relating to the Saxons, their language, or their culture.  The term often refers to the early medieval period in England dominated by the Saxons.|例句：The church architecture exhibits strong Saxonic influences. 这座教堂的建筑风格展现了强烈的萨克森影响。|例句：Old English is sometimes referred to as Saxonic English. 古英语有时被称为萨克森英语。|近义词：Anglo-Saxon|反义词：Norman (in the context of English history)
