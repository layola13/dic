law's:/lɔːz/| n.|法律；法规；定律|The laws of physics govern the universe. 物理定律支配着宇宙。|近义词：rule, regulation, statute|反义词：anarchy, chaos|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The law states that... = 法律规定……
This law protects citizens' rights. = 这部法律保护公民权利。


一般过去时:
The old law was repealed last year. = 去年废除了旧法律。
That law applied only to certain cases. = 那项法律只适用于某些案件。


现在完成时:
The government has passed many new laws recently. = 政府最近通过了许多新法律。
They have finally amended the law. = 他们终于修改了这项法律。


过去完成时:
The court had already ruled on the case before the new law was enacted. = 新法颁布之前，法院已经对该案作出了裁决。
Before the changes, the law had been interpreted differently. = 在修改之前，这项法律的解释有所不同。


将来时:
They will introduce a new law next month. = 他们下个月将引入一项新法律。
The new law will take effect next year. = 新法将于明年生效。


虚拟语气:
If the law were different, the outcome would be different. = 如果法律不同，结果也会不同。
I wish the law allowed us to do that. = 我希望法律允许我们这样做。


被动语态:
The law is being revised. = 法律正在修订中。
The case was judged according to the law. = 此案是根据法律判决的。


疑问句:
What does the law say about this? = 这方面的法律规定是什么？
What is the law's stance on this issue? = 法律对这个问题的立场是什么？


否定句:
The law does not permit that. = 法律不允许那样做。
He was not subject to the law. = 他不受法律约束。
