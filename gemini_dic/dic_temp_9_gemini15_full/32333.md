childbearing:/ˈtʃaɪldˌberɪŋ/| n.|生育，分娩|The act or process of bearing or giving birth to children.  It encompasses the entire period of pregnancy and childbirth.  The term often implies the responsibilities and experiences associated with motherhood.|例句：She is considering childbearing in the near future. 她正在考虑近期生育。

例句：The book explores the physical and emotional aspects of childbearing. 这本书探讨了生育的生理和情感方面。


近义词：childbirth, parturition, maternity


反义词：childlessness, sterility


词性：名词
