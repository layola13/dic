seasons:/ˈsiːzənz/| n.|季节；时期|The word "seasons" refers to the four periods of the year, each marked by particular weather patterns and daylight hours: spring, summer, autumn (or fall), and winter. It can also refer to a period or phase in time, often associated with a particular event or activity.|例句：The four seasons are spring, summer, autumn, and winter. 四季是春、夏、秋、冬。|例句：This is the season for planting tomatoes. 现在是种植西红柿的季节。|近义词：periods, times, phases|反义词：无
