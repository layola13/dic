misauthorizes:/ˌmɪsˈɔːθəraɪz/ | vt. | 误授权；滥用授权 | To give someone authority or permission to do something incorrectly or inappropriately; to misuse or abuse one's authority.  | The manager misauthorized the expenditure, leading to a financial audit.  经理误批了这笔支出，导致了财务审计。  | authorize, empower |  revoke, disapprove, forbid | 动词

The company misauthorizes access to sensitive data. = 公司错误地授权访问敏感数据。

The CEO misauthorized the project's budget. = CEO 误批了项目的预算。
