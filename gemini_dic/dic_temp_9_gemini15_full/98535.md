instigation:/ˌɪnstɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |煽动；教唆；发起 |The act of instigating or provoking something, especially trouble or violence.  It refers to the process of starting something, usually something negative. |例句：The instigation of the riot led to many arrests.  煽动暴乱导致许多人被捕。

例句：His instigation of the fight resulted in serious injuries. 他挑起这场斗殴导致了严重的受伤。


近义词：incitement, provocation, stimulation, prompting |反义词：prevention, cessation, pacification |词性:名词
