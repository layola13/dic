housemistress: /ˈhaʊsmɪstrəs/ | n. | 女舍监，女宿管 | A woman who is in charge of a boarding house or school dormitory, responsible for the welfare and discipline of the residents.  | The housemistress ensured the girls were in bed by 10 pm. 女舍监确保女孩们在晚上十点前上床睡觉。| matron, warden |  (No direct antonym, but opposite concepts could include "student," "resident") | 名词
