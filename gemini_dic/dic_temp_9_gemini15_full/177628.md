reburial: /ˌriːˈbɛəriəl/ | n. | 再埋葬；重新下葬 | The act of burying someone or something again.  It often implies a previous burial that was disturbed or incomplete.  | 例句：The reburial of the ancient king's remains was a significant historical event.  再埋葬这位古代国王的遗体是一件重大的历史事件。


The reburial of the victims took place after the identification process was complete.  在身份识别过程完成后，对遇难者的重新安葬仪式举行了。


近义词：reinterment, second burial | 反义词：exhumation
