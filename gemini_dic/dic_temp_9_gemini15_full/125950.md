musicalness: /ˈmjuːzɪkəlnəs/ | n. |音乐性，音乐天赋 |The quality of being musical; the ability to understand, appreciate, and create music.  It encompasses aspects like melody, harmony, rhythm, and expression.  |例句：The musicalness of the piece was undeniable, captivating the audience from beginning to end. 这首作品的音乐性无可否认，从头到尾都吸引着观众。


The composer's musicalness shone through in every note. 作曲家的音乐天赋在每一个音符中都闪耀着光芒。|近义词：musicality, artistry, expressiveness |反义词：tonelessness, discordance, inharmoniousness
