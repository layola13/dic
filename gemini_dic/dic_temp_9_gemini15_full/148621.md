overtheorized: /ˌoʊvərˈθɪərizaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度理论化的；理论阐释过度的 |

英文解析:  Describes something that has been subjected to excessive theorizing, often to the point of being impractical or losing touch with reality.  It suggests an overreliance on abstract concepts and a lack of attention to practical considerations or empirical evidence.

例句:

His analysis of the poem was overtheorized; it missed the simple beauty of the language.  他的诗歌分析过于理论化；它错过了语言的简单美感。

The project became overtheorized and failed to deliver any tangible results.  该项目变得过于理论化，未能产生任何切实的成果。


近义词: overintellectualized, overly theoretical, abstract, speculative, impractical

反义词: practical, concrete, empirical, pragmatic


词性: adjective
