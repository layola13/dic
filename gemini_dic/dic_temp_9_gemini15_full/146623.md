overhanding: /ˌoʊvərˈhændɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度递交；悬垂的；悬挂的 |  Describes something that hangs down or is delivered excessively or carelessly.  It often implies a lack of control or precision.  |例句：The overhanding branches blocked the path.  悬垂的树枝挡住了道路。

The overhanding of documents led to significant delays. 文件递交过度导致了严重的延误。|近义词：drooping, overhanging, pendulous | 反义词：upright, erect, taut


USAGE->

The painter is overhanding the canvas. = 画家正在悬挂画布。
The clerk is overhanding the reports. = 职员正在过度递交报告。

一般过去时:
He overhanded the package to me carelessly. = 他不小心地把包裹递给了我。

They have overhanded the decorations for the party. = 他们已经把派对的装饰品悬挂起来了。

过去完成时:

The artist had overhanded the painting for display. = 艺术家已经把画悬挂起来以供展示。

虚拟语气:
