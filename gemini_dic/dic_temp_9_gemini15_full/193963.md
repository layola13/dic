self-evolution:/ˌsɛlfˌɛvəˈluːʃən/| n.| 自我进化，自主进化 | The process by which a system or organism changes and develops its own characteristics over time without external influence.  It often implies internal adaptation and improvement. |例句：The company's self-evolution was driven by its employees' innovative ideas.  这家公司的自我进化是由员工的创新理念驱动的。|例句：The self-evolution of the software allowed it to adapt to changing user needs. 该软件的自我进化使其能够适应不断变化的用户需求。|近义词：autoevolution, self-development, internal development |反义词：external evolution, forced evolution
