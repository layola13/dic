词源: The word "salmiac" comes from the medieval Latin *sal ammoniacus, meaning "ammoniac salt".  The term "ammoniac" originates from the ancient Greek word Ἄμμων (Ammōn), referring to the Egyptian god Ammon, near whose temple this salt was found.
