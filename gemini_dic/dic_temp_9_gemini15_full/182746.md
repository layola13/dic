reparations:/ˌrɛpəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. |赔偿；补偿；修复 |  The act of making amends for a wrong done; the process of repairing something that is broken or damaged.  Reparations can refer to financial compensation, apologies, or other forms of redress for past injustices, often in the context of war or historical oppression.  It can also refer to the act of repairing something physical, such as a building or a machine. |

例句：

Germany paid reparations to the Allied powers after World War I.  =  第一次世界大战后，德国向协约国支付了赔款。

The government promised reparations to the victims of the disaster. = 政府承诺对灾难的受害者进行赔偿。


近义词：compensation, amends, redress, restitution, repair, restoration

反义词：injustice, wrongdoing (as the opposite of making amends)


词性：名词
