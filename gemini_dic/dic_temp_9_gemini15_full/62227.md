encroach:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃ/ | v. | 侵占；蚕食；侵犯 | To intrude on (a person's territory, rights, personal life, etc.); to gradually take over or extend into an area belonging to someone else. |例句：The sea encroached on the coastline.  = 海水侵蚀了海岸线。

The expanding city encroached on the surrounding farmland. = 扩张的城市侵占了周围的农田。


近义词：invade, trespass, infringe, impinge | 反义词：cede, relinquish, withdraw


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government encroaches on our freedoms. = 政府侵犯了我们的自由。
He constantly encroaches on my time. = 他不断侵占我的时间。


一般过去时:
The forest encroached upon the village. = 森林侵占了村庄。
The army encroached on the neighbouring country. = 军队侵犯了邻国。


现在完成时:
The desert has encroached on fertile land. = 沙漠侵占了肥沃的土地。
The weeds have encroached on the flowerbed. = 杂草侵占了花坛。


过去完成时:
The sea had encroached significantly before the seawall was built. = 在建造海堤之前，海水已经大幅度侵蚀了。
Before the intervention, the company had encroached on the market share of its competitors. = 在干预之前，该公司已经侵占了其竞争对手的市场份额。


将来时:
The rising sea level will encroach on coastal communities. = 海平面上升将侵占沿海社区。
If we don't act now, the wildfire will encroach on more houses. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，野火将蔓延到更多的房屋。


虚拟语气:
If the government didn't encroach on our rights, we would have more freedom. = 如果政府不侵犯我们的权利，我们将拥有更多自由。
I wish the building project wouldn't encroach on the park. = 我希望建筑项目不会侵占公园。


被动语态:
Our privacy was encroached upon by the paparazzi. = 我们的隐私受到了狗仔队的侵犯。
The neighboring farm was encroached upon by the expanding factory. = 邻近的农场被扩张的工厂侵占了。


疑问句:
Does the new highway encroach on any protected areas? = 新高速公路是否侵占了任何保护区？
Is the construction project encroaching on your property? = 建设项目是否侵犯了你的财产？


否定句:
The government does not encroach on the freedom of speech. = 政府不侵犯言论自由。
The new regulations do not encroach on existing business practices. = 新规定不会侵犯现有的商业行为。
