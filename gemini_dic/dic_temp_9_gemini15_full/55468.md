distorters:/dɪˈstɔːrtərz/| n.|歪曲者；曲解者 |Those who distort facts or information. People or things that cause distortion. |例句：The distorters of history often have a political agenda.  历史歪曲者通常有政治目的。
The loudspeakers were distorters of the beautiful music. 扬声器扭曲了优美的音乐。|近义词：falsifiers, misrepresenters, perverters |反义词：truth-tellers, clarifiers


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The media distorts the truth. = 媒体歪曲了真相。
He distorts facts to suit his argument. = 他歪曲事实以符合他的论点。

一般过去时:
The artist distorted the image intentionally. = 艺术家故意扭曲了图像。
They distorted the meaning of the report. = 他们歪曲了报告的含义。

现在完成时:
The rumors have distorted the public's perception. = 谣言扭曲了公众的认知。
Propaganda has distorted the historical narrative. = 宣傳扭曲了歷史敘事。

过去完成时:
He had distorted the evidence before the trial. = 他在审判前歪曲了证据。
The media had distorted the facts before the public outcry. = 在公众抗议之前，媒体歪曲了事实。


将来时:
They will distort the results to fit their agenda. = 他们会歪曲结果以符合他们的计划。
The campaign will distort the image of the candidate. = 该活动将会扭曲候选人的形象。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't distorted the facts, the outcome would have been different. = 如果他没有歪曲事实，结果就会不同。
I wish they hadn't distorted the meaning of my words. = 我希望他们没有歪曲我的话的意思。

被动语态:
The image was distorted by the lens. = 图像被镜头扭曲了。
The facts have been distorted by the media. = 事实已经被媒体歪曲了。

疑问句:
Are they intentionally distorting the data? = 他们是在故意歪曲数据吗？
How have the media distorted the events? = 媒体如何歪曲了这些事件？

否定句:
The witness did not distort his testimony. = 证人没有歪曲他的证词。
The report does not distort the facts. = 报告没有歪曲事实。
