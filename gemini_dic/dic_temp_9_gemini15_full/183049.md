replumbing:/ˌriːˈplʌmɪŋ/| n.|重新安装管道；管道系统改造|The act of replacing or repairing a building's plumbing system.  This involves replacing pipes, fixtures, and other components of the water and drainage system. |例句：The old house needed replumbing before we could move in.  我们搬进去之前，这座老房子需要重新安装管道。  The replumbing project took longer than expected.  重新安装管道工程比预期的要长。|近义词：re-piping, pipe repair, plumbing renovation|反义词：initial plumbing installation
