non-staining: /ˌnɒnˈsteɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 不留污渍的，不染色(的) | Not leaving a stain or mark.  This adjective describes something that doesn't leave a mark or discoloration on a surface.  | 例句：This fabric is non-staining and easy to clean. = 这块布料不留污渍，易于清洗。

The new paint is non-staining, even with red wine spills. = 即使是红酒洒上去，这种新油漆也不会留下污渍。


近义词：stain-resistant, colorfast, unstainable | 反义词：staining


词性：形容词
