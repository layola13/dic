collectivizing:/kəˈlɛktɪvaɪzɪŋ/| v.| 集体化；使集体所有 |The process of bringing land, resources, or industries under the control of a collective or state.  The act of transforming something into a collective entity. |例句：The Soviet Union aggressively pursued the collectivization of agriculture in the 1930s.  苏联在20世纪30年代积极推行农业集体化。

例句：The government's plan to collectivize the fishing industry met with strong resistance from independent fishermen. 政府将渔业集体化的计划遭到了独立渔民的强烈抵制。


近义词：nationalizing, socializing, communizing| 反义词：privatizing, individualizing|动词
