greenskeepers:/ˈɡriːnzˌkiːpərz/| n. |球场管理员，绿地管理员 |People who maintain the greens (the putting surfaces) of a golf course. They are responsible for the upkeep of the lawns, including mowing, fertilizing, watering, and pest control.  |The greenskeepers worked tirelessly to ensure the course was in perfect condition for the tournament. 球场管理员们辛勤工作以确保球场状况完美，以迎接锦标赛。 The greenskeepers were commended for their exceptional work on the greens.  球场管理员们因其在果岭上的出色工作而受到赞扬。|groundskeepers, turf managers |  | n.
