calming: /ˈkɑːmɪŋ/ | adj. | 使人平静的；令人安宁的 | Having a soothing or tranquilizing effect; causing one to feel peaceful or relaxed. | 例句：The calming music helped her relax.  使人平静的音乐帮助她放松了。

The calming scent of lavender filled the room.  薰衣草的舒缓香味充满了房间。


近义词：soothing, relaxing, tranquilizing, peaceful, restful | 反义词：agitative, exciting, stimulating, stressful, disturbing | 词性：形容词


USAGE->

The atmosphere is calming and relaxing. = 气氛平静而轻松。

词源:
The word "calming" is derived from the verb "calm," which ultimately comes from the Old French word "calm," meaning "quiet, peaceful."  The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb into a present participle, functioning as an adjective.
