disloyally:/dɪsˈlɔɪəli/| adv. |不忠实地；背叛地 |Acting in a manner contrary to loyalty or allegiance.  It implies a breach of trust or faith. |例句：He disloyally revealed company secrets to a competitor. 他不忠实地向竞争对手泄露了公司机密。

例句：She acted disloyally towards her friends by gossiping about them. 她通过说朋友的闲话而背叛了她们。


近义词：treacherously, faithlessly, unfaithfully, perfidiously

反义词：loyally, faithfully, truthfully


词性: 副词
