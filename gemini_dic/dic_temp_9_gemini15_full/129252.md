nod:/nɒd/ /nɑːd/ | v. |点头，同意 |To move your head up and down as a way of agreeing, showing understanding, or greeting someone.  |He nodded his head in agreement. 他点头表示同意。|She nodded to greet me. 她点头向我打招呼。 |shake one's head, agree |shake one's head, disagree

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nods his head when he understands. = 他理解的时候会点头。
She nods to everyone she meets. = 她遇到每个人都会点头示意。

一般过去时:
He nodded in agreement. = 他点头表示同意。
She nodded politely to the old woman. = 她礼貌地朝那位老妇人点头。

现在完成时:
He has nodded off during the meeting. = 他在会议期间打盹了。(此处nod off意为打盹)
She has nodded her approval to the plan. = 她已经点头同意了这个计划。

过去完成时:
He had nodded off before the speaker finished. = 演讲者还没讲完他就已经睡着了。(此处nod off意为打盹)
She had nodded her head several times before realizing she didn't understand. = 她点头好几次才意识到自己没听懂。

将来时:
He will nod his head when he hears the good news. = 当他听到好消息时，他会点头。
She will nod to show her understanding. = 她会点头表示理解。

虚拟语气:
If he understood, he would nod. = 如果他理解的话，他会点头。
I wish she would nod in agreement. = 我希望她能点头表示同意。

被动语态:
(The verb "nod" is rarely used in the passive voice because it is an intransitive verb.)  点头的动作很少用被动语态。

疑问句:
Did he nod his head? = 他点头了吗？
Will she nod in agreement? = 她会点头表示同意吗？

否定句:
He didn't nod at all. = 他根本没有点头。
She didn't nod her head in response. = 她没有点头回应。


PHRASE->
nod off: 打盹
nod agreement: 表示同意
nod one's head: 点头
a nod of approval: 同意的点头
nod to someone: 向某人点头示意
