pickpenny:/ˈpɪkˌpɛni/| n.|拾钱者，以拾钱为生的人|A person who makes a living by picking up pennies or small coins, often implying a poor or lowly status.  The term often carries a connotation of being insignificant or unimportant.|He was nothing more than a pickpenny, scraping a meager living from the streets. 他只不过是个拾钱者，在街上勉强维持生计。|近义词：penny-pincher, scavenger, beggar|反义词：millionaire, tycoon
