humps:/hʌmps/| n.|驼峰；隆起；(尤指骆驼的)峰|A hump is a rounded protuberance or swelling on a surface, often resembling the back of a camel.  It can refer to a physical feature of land, an animal's back, or even a metaphorical "hump" representing a difficult task or obstacle. |例句：The camel crossed the desert, its humps swaying gently. 骆驼穿过沙漠，它的驼峰轻轻摇曳。|例句：The road had a series of humps that made the driving bumpy. 这条路有一系列的隆起，使驾驶颠簸。|近义词：bump, bulge, rise, swell|反义词：depression, hollow, dip
