delineations:/ˌdiːˌlɪniˈeɪʃənz/| n. |描绘；轮廓；界限 |The delineations on the map showed the boundaries of the different countries. 地图上的描绘显示了不同国家的边界。|近义词：descriptions, outlines, portrayals |反义词：vagueness, ambiguity


词源:

来自古法语 *delineation，来自拉丁语 *delineatio，由 *delineāre 派生而来，意为“描绘，勾勒”。 *delineāre 由 *de-（表示“加强”）和 *linea（表示“线”）构成。


英文解析:

Delineations are detailed descriptions or representations of something, often involving outlining its boundaries or features.  It can refer to a clear and precise description, a drawing or sketch, or the act of marking boundaries.


例句:

The artist's delineations of the landscape were breathtaking.  艺术家的风景描绘令人叹为观止。

The treaty included precise delineations of the territorial borders. 条约包含了领土边界的精确界定。


近义词:

* portrayals: 描绘，刻画
* descriptions: 描述
* outlines: 轮廓，梗概
* representations: 表达，表现
* depictions: 描绘，描述


反义词:

* vagueness: 模糊
* ambiguity: 模棱两可
* obscurity: 模糊不清
* indefiniteness: 不确定性


词性:

名词 (noun)
