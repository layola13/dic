goral: /ˈɡɔːrəl/ | n. | 鬣羚 | A small, goat-like antelope found in the Himalayas and adjacent mountain ranges.  They are known for their short, curved horns and reddish-brown coat. | Example: The Himalayan gorals were grazing peacefully on the steep slopes. 喜马拉雅鬣羚正平静地吃着陡坡上的草。 | Near synonyms: serow, tahr (though these are distinct species)| Antonyms:  None directly applicable;  perhaps "large ungulate" could be considered a contrasting term. |
