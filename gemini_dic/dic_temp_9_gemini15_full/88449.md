The histaminic reaction caused significant swelling. 组胺反应导致严重的肿胀。

近义词：histamine-related, histamine-induced

词源:  From histamine + -ic (suffix forming adjectives).  Histamine itself is derived from "histos" (Greek for tissue) and "amine" (referring to its chemical structure).
