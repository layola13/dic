freeganisms:/ˈfriːɡənɪzəm/| n. |觅食者（群体）|A group of people who practice freeganism, a lifestyle that involves obtaining food and other goods from sources that would otherwise go to waste.|例句：The freeganisms collected discarded food from supermarkets to reduce waste and feed themselves.  觅食者们从超市收集丢弃的食物以减少浪费并养活自己。|近义词：dumpster divers, scavengers|反义词：consumerists
