costello:/kɒˈstɛləʊ/| n.|科斯特洛（姓氏）|Costello is a surname of Irish origin.  It is an Anglicized form of the Irish name Ó Coistealaigh, meaning "descendant of Coisteal." Coisteal is a personal name of uncertain origin, possibly meaning "battle-leader" or related to "coiste," meaning "council" or "meeting."|例句：The famous comedian Lou Costello was part of the Abbott and Costello duo. 著名的喜剧演员卢·科斯特洛是艾伯特与科斯特洛二人组的一员。|近义词：无|反义词：无
