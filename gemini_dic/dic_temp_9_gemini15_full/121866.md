misresemblance:/ˌmɪsrɪˈzɛmbləns/| n. |不相像，差异，不同 |Lack of resemblance; dissimilarity.  It refers to a noticeable lack of similarity or likeness between things that might be expected to be alike. |The misresemblance between the two brothers was striking; one was tall and slender, the other short and stocky.  两个兄弟之间的不相像非常明显；一个高而瘦，另一个矮而壮实。|dissimilarity, difference, unlikeness|resemblance, similarity, likeness|名词
