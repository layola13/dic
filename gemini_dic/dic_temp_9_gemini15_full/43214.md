crackable: /ˈkrækəbəl/ | adj. | 可破解的，易于攻破的 | Easily broken, solved, or deciphered.  Something that is crackable can be broken open, solved, or understood relatively easily. | 例句：The code was crackable with a little effort.  这个密码稍微费点劲就能破解。


The password was crackable using a simple dictionary attack. 使用简单的字典攻击就能破解密码。


近义词：breakable, solvable, decipherable, vulnerable | 反义词：unbreakable, impenetrable, unsolvable


词源:  Derived from the verb "crack," meaning to break or solve something. The suffix "-able" indicates capability or possibility.  The word's meaning has evolved to encompass various contexts, from physical objects to codes and systems.
