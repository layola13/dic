procrastinate:/prəˈkræstɪneɪt/ | v. | 拖延；延误 | To delay or postpone something.  | 例句：I keep procrastinating on my assignments. = 我一直拖延我的作业。

例句：He procrastinated until the last minute to finish his project. = 他拖到最后一分钟才完成他的项目。


近义词：delay, postpone, defer, put off | 反义词：expedite, hasten, accelerate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I procrastinate when I'm overwhelmed. = 当我不知所措时，我会拖延。
She procrastinates on her chores every weekend. = 她每个周末都拖延家务。


一般过去时:
He procrastinated on the report and missed the deadline. = 他拖延报告，错过了截止日期。
They procrastinated, resulting in a poorly-executed plan. = 他们拖延了，导致计划执行得很糟糕。


现在完成时:
I have procrastinated all week and now I'm in trouble. = 我拖延了一整周，现在我麻烦大了。
She has procrastinated so much that she's behind on all her work. = 她拖延了太多，以至于所有工作都落后了。


过去完成时:
I had procrastinated for so long that I barely had time to study for the exam. = 我拖延了这么久，几乎没有时间准备考试了。
They had procrastinated on making a decision, leading to missed opportunities. = 他们一直拖延做决定，导致错失良机。


将来时:
I will procrastinate no more! = 我不会再拖延了！
He will likely procrastinate again, given his past behavior. = 鉴于他过去的行径，他很可能会再次拖延。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't procrastinated, I would have finished the project on time. = 如果我没有拖延，我就能按时完成项目了。
I wish I hadn't procrastinated; now I'm stressed. = 我真希望我没有拖延；现在我压力很大。


被动语态:
The project was procrastinated on until the last minute. = 项目一直拖到最后一分钟才做。
The decision was procrastinated over for weeks. = 这个决定被拖延了好几周。


疑问句:
Why do you procrastinate so much? = 你为什么总是拖延？
Are you procrastinating again? = 你又在拖延了吗？


否定句:
I don't procrastinate as much as I used to. = 我不像以前那样经常拖延了。
He didn't procrastinate on this important task. = 他没有在这个重要的任务上拖延。


PHRASE->
procrastinate on = 拖延...
procrastinate about = 犹豫是否...
procrastinate with = 用...来拖延时间
avoid procrastinating = 避免拖延
stop procrastinating = 停止拖延


词源:
From Latin *procrastināre, from pro- ("forward") + crastinus ("of tomorrow").  The word literally means "to put off until tomorrow".
