remorseful:/rɪˈmɔːrsfəl/ | adj. | 懊悔的；悔恨的；内疚的 | Feeling or expressing remorse; sorry.  | Example: He was remorseful for his actions and apologized sincerely. 他为自己的行为感到懊悔并真诚地道歉了。 |近义词： repentant, regretful, contrite, rueful | 反义词：unrepentant, remorseless, unremorseful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is remorseful about his mistakes. 他对自己的错误感到懊悔。
She is always remorseful after a fight with her sister.她和姐姐吵架后总是很懊悔。


一般过去时:
He was remorseful after he hurt her feelings. 他伤害了她的感情后感到懊悔。
She was remorseful for breaking her promise. 她违背诺言后感到懊悔。


现在完成时:
He has been remorseful ever since the accident. 自从那次事故以来，他一直很懊悔。
She has felt remorseful for years about her past actions.  多年来，她一直为过去的所作所为感到懊悔。


过去完成时:
He had been remorseful for days before he finally confessed. 在他最终坦白之前，他已经懊悔了好几天了。
She had felt remorseful about it long before she apologized. 在她道歉很久之前，她就为此感到懊悔了。


将来时:
He will be remorseful when he realizes the consequences. 当他意识到后果时，他会感到懊悔。
She will likely be remorseful if she loses her job.如果她丢了工作，她很可能会感到懊悔。


虚拟语气:
If he had apologized sooner, he wouldn't feel so remorseful now. 如果他早点道歉，他现在就不会这么懊悔了。
I wish I hadn't done that; I'd feel less remorseful. 我真希望我没有那样做；那样我会少一些懊悔。


被动语态:  (此形容词通常不用于被动语态)


疑问句:
Is he remorseful about what he did? 他为他所做的事感到懊悔吗？
Are you remorseful for your behavior? 你为你的行为感到懊悔吗？


否定句:
He is not remorseful for his actions. 他对自己的行为并不感到懊悔。
She wasn't remorseful at all; she showed no regret. 她一点也不懊悔；她没有表现出任何悔恨。


PHRASE->
feel remorseful: 感到懊悔
express remorseful: 表达懊悔
a remorseful expression: 一种懊悔的表情
look remorseful: 看起来懊悔
become remorseful: 变得懊悔
