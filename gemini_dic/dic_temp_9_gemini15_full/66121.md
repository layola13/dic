excentral:/ˌɛksˈsɛntrəl/| adj. |偏心的，离心的；非中心的|  Eccentric; not situated at the center; away from the center.  The term often describes a geometric property, such as a point, line, or circle, that is not located at the center of a figure or system.  It can also be used more broadly to describe something that is off-center or unconventional.|例句：The excentral circle is tangent to one side of the triangle and the extensions of the other two sides.  偏心圆与三角形的一条边及其另外两条边的延长线相切。|近义词：eccentric, off-center, peripheral |反义词：central, concentric
