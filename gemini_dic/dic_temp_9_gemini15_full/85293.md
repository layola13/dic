harmonizing:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使和谐；协调；和解 |  To bring into harmony or agreement; to make consistent or compatible.  | The choir harmonized beautifully during the performance. 合唱团在演出中完美地和谐一致。  They are working to harmonize their differences. 他们正在努力协调他们的分歧。 | coordinating, unifying, reconciling | conflicting, clashing, discordant | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harmonizes the colors in his paintings. = 他协调了他绘画中的色彩。
The singers harmonize beautifully. = 歌手们和谐地演唱。

一般过去时:
She harmonized the conflicting viewpoints. = 她协调了相互冲突的观点。
The two countries harmonized their trade policies. = 这两个国家协调了它们的贸易政策。

现在完成时:
They have harmonized their differences through dialogue. = 他们通过对话协调了分歧。
The committee has harmonized the various proposals. = 委员会已经协调了各种提案。

过去完成时:
He had harmonized the music before the performance. = 他在演出前已经协调好音乐了。
The team had harmonized their strategies before the competition. = 团队在比赛前已经协调好策略了。

将来时:
We will harmonize our efforts to achieve the goal. = 我们将协调我们的努力以实现目标。
The company will harmonize its operations with new regulations. = 公司将使其运营与新法规相协调。

虚拟语气:
If they harmonized their efforts, they would succeed. = 如果他们协调他们的努力，他们就会成功。
I wish they would harmonize their opinions. = 我希望他们能协调他们的意见。

被动语态:
The colors were harmonized to create a pleasing effect. = 色彩得到了协调，创造出令人愉悦的效果。
The differences were harmonized through negotiation. = 分歧通过谈判得到了协调。

疑问句:
Do these colors harmonize well together? = 这些颜色搭配得好吗？
How can we harmonize these conflicting interests? = 我们如何协调这些相互冲突的利益？

否定句:
The designs did not harmonize with the overall style. = 这些设计与整体风格不协调。
The two groups failed to harmonize their positions. = 这两个群体未能协调他们的立场。
