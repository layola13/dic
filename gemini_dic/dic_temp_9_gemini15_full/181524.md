rekindling: /ˌriːˈkɪndəlɪŋ/ | v. | 重燃；复兴；重新点燃 |  The act of rekindling something is to revive or renew it, often referring to feelings, relationships, or activities that were previously dormant or extinguished. It implies a process of bringing something back to life or intensity.  | 例句：The couple's rekindling of their romance was a beautiful thing to witness. 这对夫妇重燃爱火，真是令人欣慰。

The old campfire was rekindled with some dry leaves and twigs. 用一些干树叶和树枝重新点燃了旧篝火。


近义词：reignite, revive, renew, restore, revitalize | 反义词：extinguish, quench, suppress
