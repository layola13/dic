overprecise:/ˌoʊvərprɪˈsaɪs/| adj.|过于精确的，过分精确的|Excessively precise; going beyond what is necessary or appropriate in accuracy or detail.  It often implies that the precision is unnecessary or even detrimental.|The overprecise instructions confused the workers.  过于精确的指示让工人们感到困惑。 His overprecise language made his writing seem pedantic. 他过于精确的语言使他的写作显得迂腐。|近义词：meticulous, fussy, pedantic|反义词：imprecise, vague, general
