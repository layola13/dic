proportionally:/prəˈpɔːʃənəli/ | adv. | 成比例地，相称地 |  In a way that is proportional; according to a particular ratio or proportion.  It describes a relationship where two or more things change at the same rate. | The workload increased proportionally with the number of employees. 工作量随着员工人数成比例增加。 The price of the house is proportionally higher than others in the area. 这房子的价格比该地区其他房子的价格高得多。 | proportionally to, in proportion to | disproportionately | 副词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cost increases proportionally to the amount of materials used. = 成本随着材料用量的增加而成比例地增加。
The company's profits rise proportionally with its sales. = 公司的利润与其销售额成比例增长。


一般过去时:
The number of students proportionally decreased last year. = 去年学生人数成比例下降。
The factory's output proportionally fell due to the strike. = 由于罢工，工厂的产量成比例下降。


现在完成时:
The value of the investment has increased proportionally since last year. = 自去年以来，投资价值已经成比例增长。
The population has grown proportionally in the last decade. = 在过去的十年里，人口成比例增长。


过去完成时:
The company's shares had proportionally declined before the good news was announced. = 在好消息宣布之前，公司的股票已经成比例下降。
The project's budget had proportionally decreased before the extra funding was secured. = 在获得额外资金之前，项目的预算已经成比例减少。


将来时:
The demand will proportionally increase with the rising popularity. = 随着人气的上升，需求将成比例增加。
The production costs will proportionally rise with inflation. = 生产成本将随着通货膨胀而成比例上升。


虚拟语气:
If the ingredients were proportionally increased, the recipe would yield more. = 如果按比例增加配料，食谱的产量会更高。
If the effort were proportionally applied, the result would be better. = 如果努力按比例投入，结果会更好。


被动语态:
The resources were proportionally allocated to each department. = 资源按比例分配给各个部门。
The workload was proportionally distributed among the team members. = 工作量在团队成员之间按比例分配。


疑问句:
Are the ingredients proportionally added to the mixture? = 配料是否按比例添加到混合物中？
Were the funds proportionally distributed among the projects? = 资金是否按比例分配给各个项目？


否定句:
The rewards were not proportionally distributed among the employees. = 奖励没有按比例分配给员工。
The tasks were not proportionally assigned to each team member. = 任务没有按比例分配给每个团队成员。
