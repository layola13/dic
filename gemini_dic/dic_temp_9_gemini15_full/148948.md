overzeal:/ˈoʊvərziːl/| adj.|过分热心；过度热忱|excessive enthusiasm or diligence; often to the point of being counterproductive.|例句：His overzeal in cleaning the house resulted in broken vases.  他的过度清洁导致花瓶摔碎了。


例句：The overzealous student ended up submitting a poorly-written report because they rushed to finish it.  这个过于热心的学生最终提交了一份写得很糟糕的报告，因为他们赶着完成它。


近义词：excessive enthusiasm, fanaticism, overeagerness


反义词：apathy, indifference, lethargy


词性：形容词
