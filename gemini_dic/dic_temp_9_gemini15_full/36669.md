collider:/kəˈlaɪdər/| n. |对撞机，碰撞器|A machine that accelerates particles to very high speeds and then allows them to collide, allowing scientists to study the results.  This is used in particle physics research to explore the fundamental building blocks of matter.|例句：The Large Hadron Collider is the world's largest and most powerful particle accelerator.|近义词：accelerator, smasher|反义词：无
