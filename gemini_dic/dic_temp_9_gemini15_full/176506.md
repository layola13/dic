re-processing:/ˌriːˈprɑːsesɪŋ/| n.|再处理，重新加工|The act of processing something again, often to improve it or extract further value.|例句：The factory is undergoing re-processing of waste materials to minimize environmental impact.  这家工厂正在对废料进行再处理，以最大限度地减少对环境的影响。|例句：The data requires re-processing to correct errors and ensure accuracy.  数据需要重新处理以纠正错误并确保准确性。|近义词：reworking, refinement, recycling|反义词：disposal, discarding|名词
