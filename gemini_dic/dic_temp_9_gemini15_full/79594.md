gloriation:/ˌɡlɔːriˈeɪʃən/| n. |荣耀，光荣化，赞美|The act of glorifying or being glorified; the state of being glorified.  It often implies excessive praise or an exaggerated sense of one's own importance.|例句：The gloriation of violence in some video games is a cause for concern.  一些电子游戏中对暴力的歌颂令人担忧。

例句：His gloriation of his achievements was tiresome to listen to.  他对他成就的夸耀令人厌烦。


近义词：exaltation, glorification, magnification, celebration

反义词：denigration, belittling, humiliation, degradation

词性：名词
