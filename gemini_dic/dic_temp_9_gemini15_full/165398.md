/priːˈleɪbəlɪŋ/ | n. | 预先标记，预先贴标签 | The act of labeling something before it is used or processed.  It often involves adding information or identifiers to items in advance to streamline later processes.  This is common in manufacturing, logistics, and research. | Example: Prelabeling the samples ensured accurate tracking throughout the experiment.  预先标记样品确保了整个实验过程中的准确跟踪。The factory implemented a prelabeling system to improve efficiency. 工厂实施了预先标记系统以提高效率。|近义词：prior labeling, advance labeling | 反义词：post-labeling
