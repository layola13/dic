scarabaeus:/ˌskærəˈbiːəs/| n. |圣甲虫；蜣螂|A genus of dung beetles, often large and black, sacred to the ancient Egyptians.  They are known for rolling dung balls.  |例句：The ancient Egyptians revered the scarabaeus as a symbol of rebirth. 古埃及人尊崇圣甲虫为重生之象征。


例句：Many species of scarabaeus are found in arid and semi-arid regions. 许多种类的圣甲虫生活在干旱和半干旱地区。


近义词：dung beetle, tumblebug


反义词：无


词性：名词
