omniformity:/ˌɒmnɪˈfɔːrmɪti/ | n. | 全同性；一致性 | The state or quality of being omniform; uniformity in all respects.  Omniformity implies a complete and total sameness or uniformity, leaving no room for variation or difference.  It suggests a perfect replication across all aspects.  This word is rarely used in modern English. |  The sheer omniformity of the cloned plants was unsettling. = 这些克隆植物的完全一致令人不安。There is almost an omniformity of opinion on this matter. = 在这件事上，几乎意见一致。 | 近义词：uniformity, homogeneity | 反义词：diversity, heterogeneity | 名词
