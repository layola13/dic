makeup:/ˈmeɪkʌp/ | n., v. |化妆品；构成；弥补；编造 |

英文解析:
As a noun, "makeup" refers to cosmetics used to enhance one's appearance, or the composition or structure of something. As a verb, it means to apply cosmetics, to constitute or form something, to compensate for a deficiency, or to invent or fabricate something.


例句:
She spent an hour doing her makeup before the party.  = 她花了1个小时化妆才去参加派对。

The makeup of the team includes experts from various fields. = 这个团队的构成包括来自各个领域的专家。


近义词:
(n.) cosmetics, composition, constitution,  (v.)  compensate,  invent, fabricate


反义词:
(n.)  (没有直接的反义词，取决于语境) (v.)  destroy, deconstruct, reveal the truth


词性:
Noun, Verb
