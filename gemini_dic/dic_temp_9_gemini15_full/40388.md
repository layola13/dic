contradicts:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪkts/| /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪkts/ | vt. | 反驳；与…矛盾；与…相抵触 | To contradict something means to say or show that it is not true or correct.  It can also mean to be in conflict or opposition to something. |


例句：

His statement contradicts the evidence.  他的陈述与证据相矛盾。

Her actions contradict her words. 她的行为与她所说的话相矛盾。


近义词：deny, oppose, refute, clash with, be inconsistent with


反义词：confirm, support, corroborate, agree with


词性：动词
