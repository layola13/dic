corporify:/ˈkɔːrpərɪfaɪ/| vt.|使公司化；使具有公司特征|To give a corporate structure or character to something; to make something act like a corporation.|The government is trying to corporify the healthcare system.  政府正试图将医疗体系公司化。|近义词：incorporate, organize|反义词：decentralize, privatize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They corporify their business model. = 他们使他们的商业模式公司化。
The company corporifies its operations. = 这家公司使它的运营公司化。

一般过去时:
The company corporified its structure last year. = 该公司去年将其结构公司化。
They corporified the previously independent units. = 他们使以前独立的单位公司化。

现在完成时:
They have corporified many aspects of their work. = 他们已经将工作的许多方面公司化。
The government has corporified the public transportation system. = 政府已经将公共交通系统公司化。

过去完成时:
They had corporified the department before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们已经将该部门公司化。
The company had corporified its image before the IPO. = 在首次公开募股之前，该公司已经将其形象公司化。

将来时:
They will corporify their marketing strategy next year. = 他们明年将使他们的营销策略公司化。
The organization will corporify its structure to attract investors. = 该组织将使它的结构公司化以吸引投资者。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
