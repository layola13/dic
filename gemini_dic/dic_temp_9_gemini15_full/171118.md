psychically:/ˈsaɪkɪkli/| adj.|心灵的；超自然的；精神上的| Relating to or suggestive of the psyche or mind, especially in a way that appears to transcend the ordinary physical laws.  It often implies the ability to perceive or influence events through extrasensory perception (ESP).|例句：He claimed to have psychically predicted the lottery numbers. 他声称自己心灵感应地预测了彩票号码。

例句：She felt a psychically charged atmosphere in the old house.  她在老房子里感受到一种充满灵异氛围的环境。


近义词：mentally, spiritually, paranormally, metaphysically

反义词：physically, materially, tangibly


词性:形容词
