nonprovider:/nɒnˈprəʊvaɪdə(r)/| n. |非提供者，非供应者 |A nonprovider is someone or something that does not provide something, such as goods, services, or support.  This term often appears in contexts related to healthcare, social services, or economic analysis. |Example:  The study found that nonproviders of care often faced significant financial burdens.  这项研究发现，护理的非提供者往往面临巨大的经济负担。|近义词：non-supplier, nonsupporter|反义词：provider, supplier
