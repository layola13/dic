inembryonate:/ɪnˈɛmbrɪəneɪt/| adj.|胚胎的；处于胚胎状态的|  In embryology, inembryonate refers to something existing or occurring in the embryonic stage of development.  It emphasizes the early, undeveloped nature of something.  The word is less common than its components "embryonic" and "in embryo".|例句：The inembryonate stage of the project is crucial for its future success.  这个项目的胚胎阶段对其未来的成功至关重要。

The inembryonate cells showed rapid growth under the right conditions.在合适的条件下，胚胎细胞显示出快速生长。|近义词：embryonic, undeveloped, nascent|反义词：mature, developed, fully formed|
