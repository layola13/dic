sleighed:/sleɪd/| v.|乘坐雪橇，用雪橇运载|To ride on a sleigh; to convey on a sleigh.|例句：We sleighed down the snowy hill. 我们乘坐雪橇滑下了雪坡。  例句：The farmer sleighed his hay to the barn. 农夫用雪橇把干草运到谷仓。|近义词：rode (a sleigh), conveyed (on a sleigh)|反义词：walked, carried (by other means)
