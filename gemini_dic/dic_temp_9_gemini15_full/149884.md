palingenesia: /ˌpælɪnˈdʒɛniːʃə/ | n. | 再生；轮回；复生 | The biological process of regeneration or rebirth; the reappearance of ancestral characteristics in later generations;  a metaphorical rebirth or renewal.  | Example: The theory of palingenesia suggests a cyclical process of creation and destruction in the universe.  这个理论认为宇宙中存在一个创造和毁灭的循环过程。  | 近义词：regeneration, rebirth, resurrection, reincarnation | 反义词：destruction, annihilation, extinction |  名词
