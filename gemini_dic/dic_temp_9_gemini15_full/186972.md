risibility:/ˌrɪzɪˈbɪləti/| n. |能笑；爱笑；可笑性 |The quality or capacity for laughing; the tendency to laugh.  It can also refer to something that is laughable or amusing. |例句：Her risibility was infectious, making everyone around her laugh.  她的笑声极具感染力，让周围的人都笑了。

例句：The comedian's routine was full of risibility, eliciting laughter from the audience.  这位喜剧演员的表演充满了笑点，引得观众哈哈大笑。


近义词：mirthfulness, joviality, amusement, humor


反义词：seriousness, solemnity, gravity


词源:  From Middle French *risibilité, from Latin risibilis, from ridere "to laugh".
