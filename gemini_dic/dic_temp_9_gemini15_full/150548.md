pap's:/pæps/| n. |爸爸的；父亲的；|The word "pap's" is a shortened, informal version of "papa's" or "dad's," indicating possession.  It's primarily used in informal settings and dialects.|Example:  That's pap's old car. 那是爸爸的老车。|近义词：dad's, father's, papa's|反义词：无
