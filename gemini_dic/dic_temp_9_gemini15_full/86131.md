heartens:/ˈhɑːrtənz/| vt.|使振奋；使鼓舞|To encourage; to give someone hope and courage.|The good news heartened the team. 好消息鼓舞了整个团队。
His support heartened me greatly. 他的支持极大地鼓舞了我。|encourage, inspire, uplift|depress, discourage, dispirit|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The news heartens us. = 这个消息使我们振奋。
His words hearten her. = 他的话使她振奋。

一般过去时:
The victory heartened the troops. = 胜利鼓舞了军队。
Her kindness heartened the lost child. = 她的善良使迷路的孩子安心了。

现在完成时:
This success has heartened the whole company. = 这次成功鼓舞了整个公司。
The positive feedback has heartened the team. = 积极的反馈鼓舞了团队。

过去完成时:
The earlier good news had heartened them for the challenge ahead. = 此前的好消息使他们对未来的挑战充满信心。
The previous success had heartened the project manager. = 先前的成功鼓舞了项目经理。

将来时:
The promised reward will hearten the workers. = 承诺的奖励将鼓舞工人。
I hope this news will hearten you. = 我希望这个消息能让你振奋。

虚拟语气:
If only this news would hearten him! = 但愿这个消息能使他振奋！
I wish that the good news would hearten her. = 我希望好消息能让她振奋。

被动语态:
The team was heartened by the coach's speech. = 球队被教练的演讲鼓舞了。
She was heartened by his words of support. = 她被他的支持的话语鼓舞了。

疑问句:
Does this news hearten you? = 这个消息让你振奋吗？
Will this new strategy hearten the investors? = 这个新策略会让投资者振奋吗？

否定句:
The bad news didn't hearten them. = 坏消息没有使他们振奋。
His criticism didn't hearten her at all. = 他的批评一点也没有使她振奋。
