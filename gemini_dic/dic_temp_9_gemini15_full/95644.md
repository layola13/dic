incompletes:/ˌɪnkəmˈpliːts/| adj.|不完整的，未完成的|Incomplete means not finished or not whole. It refers to something lacking a part or parts necessary for completeness.|例句：The report is incomplete; several key facts are missing.  报告不完整；缺少几个关键事实。|例句：Her understanding of the situation is incomplete. 她对情况的理解不完整。|近义词：unfinished, partial, imperfect, fragmentary|反义词：complete, finished, whole
