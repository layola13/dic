gloppen:/ˈɡlɒpən/| v.|猛吞，狼吞虎咽|To eat or drink something quickly and greedily.|例句：He glopped down his soup in a few seconds. 他几秒钟就狼吞虎咽地喝完了汤。|例句：The children glopped the ice cream. 孩子们狼吞虎咽地吃着冰淇淋。|近义词：gulp, devour, gobble, scarf down|反义词：sip, nibble, savor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He glops down his food every day. = 他每天都狼吞虎咽地吃东西。
She glops her drink quickly. = 她很快地把饮料喝光了。

一般过去时:
He glopped down his lunch in five minutes. = 他五分钟内就狼吞虎咽地吃完了午餐。
They glopped the entire cake. = 他们狼吞虎咽地吃掉了整个蛋糕。

现在完成时:
He has glopped down three burgers already. = 他已经狼吞虎咽地吃了三个汉堡。
She has glopped all the cookies. = 她已经把所有的饼干都吃光了。

过去完成时:
He had glopped down the entire pie before anyone else even got a slice. = 在其他人甚至还没有切到一块派之前，他已经狼吞虎咽地吃掉了整块派。
They had glopped all the snacks before the party even started. = 在派对开始之前，他们就已经狼吞虎咽地吃掉了所有的零食。

将来时:
He will glop down his dinner as soon as he gets home. = 他一回家就会狼吞虎咽地吃晚饭。
She will glop the whole pizza if you let her. = 如果你让她的话，她会把整个披萨都吃光。

虚拟语气:
If I were very hungry, I would glop down my food. = 如果我很饿，我会狼吞虎咽地吃东西。
I wish I hadn't glopped down that whole jar of pickles. = 我真希望我没有狼吞虎咽地吃掉那一整罐泡菜。

被动语态:  (The verb "gloppen" is rarely used passively.)
The food was quickly glopped by the hungry children. = 饥饿的孩子们很快就狼吞虎咽地吃掉了食物。(This sounds unnatural)
The pizza was glopped up in a matter of minutes. = 比萨饼几分钟内就被吃光了。(This is more acceptable but still less common)


疑问句:
Did he glop down his breakfast this morning? = 他今天早上狼吞虎咽地吃了早餐吗？
Will she glop the candy? = 她会狼吞虎咽地吃糖果吗？


否定句:
He didn't glop down his food; he ate it slowly and carefully. = 他没有狼吞虎咽地吃饭；他吃得很慢很仔细。
She didn't glop the soup; she sipped it delicately. = 她没有狼吞虎咽地喝汤；她优雅地啜饮着。


PHRASE->
gloppen down: 狼吞虎咽地吃下
quickly gloppen: 快速地猛吞
greedily gloppen: 贪婪地猛吞
gloppen up:  迅速地吃完（通常指大量食物）
to gloppen something in one go: 一口气猛吞
