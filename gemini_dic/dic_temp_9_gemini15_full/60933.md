elevatedly:/ˈɛləveɪtɪdli/ | adv. | 高高地；崇高地；兴奋地 | In an elevated manner; at a high level; in a lofty or exalted way.  |例句：The eagle soared elevatedly above the mountains.  雄鹰高高地翱翔在山峦之上。

例句：He spoke elevatedly about the importance of art. 他高谈阔论艺术的重要性。
