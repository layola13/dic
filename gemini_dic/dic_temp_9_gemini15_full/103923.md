judgement: /ˈdʒʌdʒmənt/ | n. | 判断；判决；意见；看法 | The act of judging or forming an opinion or conclusion; a decision made or an opinion formed after consideration.  |例句：The judge's judgement was fair and just.  法官的判决公正无私。|  例句：Her judgement of character is usually quite accurate. 她对人物性格的判断通常相当准确。| 近义词：decision, verdict, opinion, assessment | 反义词：guess, speculation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shows good judgement in his investments. = 他在投资方面显示出良好的判断力。
My judgement tells me this is a bad idea. = 我的判断告诉我这是一个坏主意。


一般过去时:
The jury's judgement was delivered yesterday. = 陪审团的判决昨天宣判了。
His poor judgement led to his downfall. = 他糟糕的判断力导致了他的失败。


现在完成时:
They have shown poor judgement in handling this matter. = 他们在处理这件事上表现出糟糕的判断力。
The court has already made its judgement. = 法院已经做出了判决。


过去完成时:
I realized later that my judgement had been clouded by emotion. = 我后来意识到我的判断当时被情绪蒙蔽了。
He regretted the judgement he had made in haste. = 他后悔自己匆忙做出的决定。


将来时:
The judge will deliver his judgement next week. = 法官将在下周宣判。
We will wait for the results of the expert's judgement. = 我们将等待专家的判断结果。


虚拟语气:
If I had better judgement, I wouldn't have done that. = 如果我判断力更好，我不会那样做的。
I wish I had exercised better judgement. = 我真希望当时我更有判断力。


被动语态:
The case was judged fairly by the court. = 此案得到了法院的公平审判。(被动语态更侧重于案件本身受到审判)
His judgement was questioned by many people. = 他的判断受到了许多人的质疑。(被动语态更侧重于判断本身受到质疑)


疑问句:
What is your judgement on this matter? = 你对此事的看法是什么？
What was the judge's judgement in the case? = 法官对这起案件的判决是什么？


否定句:
I don't agree with your judgement. = 我不同意你的判断。
The evidence didn't support their judgement. = 证据不支持他们的判断。


PHRASE->
exercise judgement = 行使判断力
sound judgement = 健全的判断力
poor judgement = 糟糕的判断力
in my judgement = 依我看
pass judgement = 做出判断


词源:
The word "judgement" comes from the Old French word "juggement," which in turn derives from the Latin word "iudicium," meaning "judgment, trial, court of justice."  The Latin word is related to "iudex," meaning "judge."
