denationalization:/ˌdiːˌnæʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |国有企业私有化；取消国有化；非国有化|The process of transferring ownership or control of a nationalized industry or asset from a government to a private entity.  This can involve selling off state-owned companies, privatizing public services, or reducing government intervention in the economy. |例句：The denationalization of the railways led to increased efficiency and competition.  铁路私有化导致效率和竞争的提高。

The denationalization of the airline industry resulted in lower fares for consumers. 航空业的非国有化导致消费者票价降低。


近义词：privatization, deregulation |反义词：nationalization
