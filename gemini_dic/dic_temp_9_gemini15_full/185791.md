revisable:/rɪˈvaɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 可修改的，可修订的 | Capable of being revised or changed.  Something that is revisable can be improved or corrected. | The contract is revisable; we can negotiate some of the terms. 合同是可修改的；我们可以协商一些条款。  The manuscript is revisable before publication.  手稿在出版前是可以修改的。 | modifiable, amendable, alterable | unchangeable, immutable, fixed


USAGE->

The plan is revisable. = 计划是可以修改的。
The document is revisable. = 文件是可以修改的。
