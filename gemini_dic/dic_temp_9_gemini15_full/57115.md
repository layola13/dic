douse:/daʊs/| vt.|浇灭；泼；浸湿|To pour a liquid over someone or something; to extinguish (a fire) by pouring water over it; to wet thoroughly.|例句：He doused the flames with a bucket of water. 他用一桶水浇灭了火焰。
例句：The firefighters quickly doused the burning building. 消防员迅速扑灭了燃烧的建筑物。|近义词：quench, soak, saturate|反义词：ignite, kindle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He douses the plants every morning. = 他每天早上都给植物浇水。
She douses her hair with conditioner. = 她用护发素把头发浸湿。

一般过去时:
They doused the bonfire with water. = 他们用水浇灭了篝火。
The rain doused the parched earth. = 雨水浸湿了干旱的土地。

现在完成时:
The firefighters have doused the blaze. = 消防员已经扑灭了大火。
She has doused her hair with perfume. = 她用香水把头发浸湿了。

过去完成时:
They had doused the fire before the firemen arrived. = 消防员到达之前，他们已经把火扑灭了。
She had doused her face with cold water to wake herself up. = 她用冷水洗脸让自己清醒。

将来时:
He will douse the campfire before going to bed. = 他睡前会把营火浇灭。
They will douse the car with water to cool it down. = 他们会用水浇车来降温。

虚拟语气:
If he had doused the fire sooner, the damage would have been less. = 如果他早点把火浇灭，损失就会少一些。
I wish I had doused the burning pan with baking soda instead of water. = 我真希望我用小苏打而不是水浇灭了燃烧的平底锅。

被动语态:
The fire was doused quickly by the firefighters. = 大火被消防员迅速扑灭了。
His clothes were doused with mud. = 他的衣服被泥浆弄湿了。

疑问句:
Did he douse the fire? = 他把火浇灭了吗？
Will they douse the hot coals? = 他们会把烧红的煤浇灭吗？

否定句:
He didn't douse the flames quickly enough. = 他没有及时浇灭火焰。
She didn't douse the burning building.  = 她没有浇灭燃烧的建筑物。
