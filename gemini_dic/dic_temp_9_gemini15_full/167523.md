previsit:/ˈpriːvɪzɪt/| n.| 预先访问；事先探访 | A previsit is a visit made before a main or more important visit, often for the purpose of planning or preparation.  It's a preliminary visit. |例句：The architect made a previsit to the site to assess the feasibility of the project. 建筑师事先去现场考察项目的可行性。 The team conducted a previsit to familiarize themselves with the conference venue.  团队进行了一次预先访问，以熟悉会议场地。|近义词：advance visit, preliminary visit, reconnaissance|反义词：postvisit, follow-up visit|名词
