merlon:/ˈmɜːrlən/| n. |垛口，城垛|A merlon is a part of a battlement, a low wall with openings, that is seen between two crenels (the openings). Merlons are typically used in castles and fortifications to provide cover for soldiers while allowing them to fire weapons.  |Example: The soldiers took cover behind the merlons of the castle wall.  士兵躲在城堡城垛后面。 The medieval castle was protected by high walls with regularly spaced merlons and crenels.  这座中世纪城堡由带有规则间隔的垛口和枪眼的高墙保护着。|近义词：battlement (部分), parapet|反义词：crenel (相对而言)
