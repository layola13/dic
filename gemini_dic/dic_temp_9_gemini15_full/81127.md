grammies:/ˈɡræmiz/| n.|奶奶们；祖母们|A colloquial term for grandmothers, often used playfully or affectionately.  It can refer to multiple grandmothers or a single grandmother in a familiar context.|例句：The kids went to visit their grammies for the weekend. 孩子们周末去看望他们的奶奶们了。|例句：She calls her grandmother "Grammy". 她叫她奶奶“格莱米”。|近义词：grandmothers, nanas, grandmas|反义词：无
