honourer: /ˈɒnərər/ | /ˈɑːnərər/ | n. | 尊敬者；表彰者；授予荣誉者 | One who honours or shows respect to someone or something; one who bestows honours.  | The honourer of the award presented a heartfelt speech.  授予奖项的表彰者发表了一篇感人的演讲。 The community honourer was deeply respected for his work.  社区的尊敬者因其工作而备受尊敬。|  admirer, venerator, celebrator | dishonourer, insulter | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He honours his parents. = 他尊敬他的父母。
She honours her commitments. = 她信守承诺。


一般过去时:
They honoured their agreement. = 他们遵守了协议。
The company honoured its debt. = 公司偿还了债务。


现在完成时:
We have honoured our promise. = 我们已经信守诺言了。
She has honoured many requests. = 她已经满足了许多请求。


过去完成时:
They had honoured the request before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经满足了请求。
He had honoured his father's memory by establishing a scholarship. = 他通过设立奖学金来纪念他的父亲。


将来时:
We will honour our guests with a banquet. = 我们将用宴会款待我们的客人。
They will honour the contract. = 他们将遵守合同。


虚拟语气:
If I were rich, I would honour my commitments more generously. = 如果我有钱，我会更慷慨地履行我的承诺。
I wish I could honour her request. = 我希望我能满足她的请求。


被动语态:
His contributions were honoured with a medal. = 他的贡献被授予奖章。
The agreement was duly honoured. = 该协议得到适当履行。


疑问句:

Will they honour their promise? = 他们会信守诺言吗？


否定句:
He did not honour his word. = 他没有遵守诺言。
They failed to honour their obligations. = 他们没有履行义务。
