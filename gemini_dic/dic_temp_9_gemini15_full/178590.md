recostuming:/ˌriːˈkɒstuːmɪŋ/| v. |重新服装设计；重新为…服装设计 |The act of changing or redesigning costumes.  This often involves altering existing costumes or creating entirely new ones. It's common in theatre, film, and other performance arts. | Example: The director decided on a complete recostuming of the play to better reflect the updated setting.  导演决定对该剧进行彻底的服装重新设计，以更好地反映更新后的场景。


The production team is busy with the recostuming for the upcoming musical. 制作团队正忙于即将上演的音乐剧的服装重新设计。|近义词：redesigning costumes, refitting costumes, restyling costumes |反义词：leaving costumes unchanged, keeping original costumes
