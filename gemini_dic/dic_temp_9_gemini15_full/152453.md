paxillus:/pækˈsɪləs/| n. |小菌盖蕈；小伞菌|A genus of fungi characterized by their decurrent gills (gills that extend down the stem) and often funnel-shaped caps.  Many species are edible, but some are toxic.  The genus is known for its brown or brownish-orange colored fruiting bodies. |例句：The forest floor was covered with a variety of fungi, including several species of Paxillus.  森林地面上覆盖着各种各样的真菌，包括几种小伞菌。

The identification of Paxillus involutus is crucial, as it's toxic.  识别卷毛小伞菌至关重要，因为它是有毒的。|近义词：fungus, mushroom|反义词:N/A
