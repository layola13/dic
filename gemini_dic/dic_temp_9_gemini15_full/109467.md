The term is often used in botany and zoology to describe the texture or appearance of a surface.|例句：The leaves of the plant were lepidote, giving them a silvery sheen. 这植物的叶子布满了鳞片，呈现银色光泽。The snake's scales were lepidote and overlapped perfectly. 这条蛇的鳞片呈鳞状，完美地重叠在一起。|近义词：scaly, squamous|反义词：smooth, glabrous
