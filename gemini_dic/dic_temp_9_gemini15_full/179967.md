reffing:/ˈrɛfɪŋ/| v.|裁判, 担任裁判 |The act of officiating as a referee, especially in sports.  It involves making judgments, enforcing rules, and ensuring fair play.|例句：He's been reffing amateur soccer games for years. 他多年来一直担任业余足球比赛的裁判。|例句：The referee's reffing was questionable during the final moments of the game.  裁判在比赛最后时刻的判罚值得怀疑。|近义词：officiating, judging|反义词：playing (as a participant)
