lots:/lɒts/ /lɑːts/| n.|许多，大量|a large quantity or number of things.|例句：There are lots of books in the library. 图书馆里有很多书。|例句：She has lots of friends. 她有很多朋友。|近义词：many, much, a lot of, plenty of|反义词：few, little, a few, a little


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There are lots of apples in the basket. = 篮子里有很多苹果。
We have lots of work to do. = 我们有很多工作要做。

一般过去时:
There were lots of people at the concert. = 音乐会上有很多的人。
They had lots of fun at the party. = 他们在派对上玩得很开心。

现在完成时:
I have seen lots of movies this year. = 今年我看过很多电影。
We have had lots of rain lately. = 最近雨水很多。

过去完成时:
They had already eaten lots of food before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前他们已经吃了很多食物。
She had done lots of research for her project. = 她为她的项目做了很多研究。

将来时:
We will have lots of time for relaxation next week. = 下周我们将有很多时间放松。
There will be lots of snow in the mountains this winter. = 今年冬天山区会有很多雪。

虚拟语气:
If I had lots of money, I would travel the world. = 如果我有许多钱，我就会周游世界。
I wish I had lots of free time. = 我希望我有许多空闲时间。

被动语态:
Lots of questions were asked during the meeting. = 会议期间提出了许多问题。
Lots of damage was caused by the storm. = 暴风雨造成了很大的损失。

疑问句:
Do you have lots of homework? = 你有很多家庭作业吗？
Are there lots of cars in the parking lot? = 停车场有很多车吗？

否定句:
I don't have lots of patience. = 我没有很多耐心。
There aren't lots of opportunities in this town. = 这个城镇没有很多机会。
