graybeards:/ˈɡreɪbiːrdz/| n.|经验丰富的人；德高望重的人；老人（复数）|Experienced and respected older men;  often used to refer to a group of elderly men with significant experience and authority.  The term implies wisdom and authority gained through age.|例句：The graybeards of the company decided on the new strategy. 公司里的老人们决定了新的策略。|例句：The graybeards in the village gathered to discuss the harvest. 村里的老人聚集在一起讨论收成。|近义词：elders, veterans, sages|反义词：youths, youngsters
