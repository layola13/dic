fileable: /ˈfaɪləbl/ | adj. | 可归档的，可存档的 | Capable of being filed or archived; suitable for filing.  |例句：The documents are fileable once they are properly stamped.  这些文件盖章后就可以归档了。

The report is fileable according to company regulations.  根据公司规定，这份报告是可以归档的。


近义词：archivable, recordable, storable | 反义词：unfileable, unarchivable
