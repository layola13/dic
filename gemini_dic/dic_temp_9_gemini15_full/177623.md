rebuking:/rɪˈbjuːkɪŋ/ | adj. | 斥责的；谴责的 | Expressing strong disapproval.  |例句：The teacher gave him a rebuking look for his disruptive behavior.老师对他扰乱秩序的行为投以责备的目光。

例句：Her rebuking tone made it clear she wasn't happy with his actions. 她责备的语气清楚地表明她对他的行为不满意。


近义词：reproving, chastising, scolding, upbraiding | 反义词：praising, commending, approving | 词性:形容词


USAGE->

The manager is rebuking the employee for their mistake. = 经理正在因为员工的错误而责备他。
His tone is rebuking, even when he is trying to be helpful. = 即使他想帮忙，他的语气也是责备的。


一般过去时:

She rebuked him sharply for his rudeness. = 她严厉地斥责了他的粗鲁行为。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
