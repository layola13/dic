sexus:/ˈsɛksəs/| n. |性别，性|The biological distinction between male and female, based on reproductive organs and functions.  It refers to the inherent biological characteristics that define an organism as male or female.|Example:  The sexus of the newborn baby was determined at birth.  新生儿的性别在出生时就被确定了。|近义词：gender (though often used interchangeably, gender encompasses social and cultural aspects)|反义词：无直接反义词，可根据语境考虑 opposite sex
