semigod:/ˈsɛmiɡɑːd/| n. |半神，英雄 |A being of partly divine and partly human origin; a demigod.  Often used to describe someone with exceptional abilities or powers, but not quite divine.|例句：Hercules is a famous semigod in Greek mythology.  赫拉克勒斯是希腊神话中著名的半神。

例句：She's a semigod in the business world, achieving remarkable success at a young age.  她在商界是一位女英雄，年轻时就取得了非凡的成就。


近义词：demigod, hero, legend |反义词：mortal, ordinary
