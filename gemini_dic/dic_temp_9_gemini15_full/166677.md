preserve:/prɪˈzɜːrv/|/ˈprɛzɜːrv/| |v./n.|保存；保护；腌制；果酱|To keep something in its original state; to protect something from damage or decay; to prepare food by preserving it, such as pickling or canning.|例句：We need to preserve our natural resources. 我们需要保护我们的自然资源。

例句：She preserved the fruit by canning it. 她通过罐装来保存水果。


近义词：conserve, protect, maintain, safeguard|反义词：destroy, ruin, damage, waste|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They preserve historical buildings. = 他们保护历史建筑。
We preserve food by freezing it. = 我们通过冷冻保存食物。
