intimated:/ˈɪntɪmeɪtɪd/| v.|暗示，通知，表明|to suggest or hint something indirectly; to make known subtly or indirectly|The manager intimated that layoffs were coming.  经理暗示即将裁员。|He intimated his displeasure with a frown. 他皱着眉头表示他的不满。|suggested, hinted, implied, declared, communicated|denied, confirmed, stated explicitly|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He intimates his disapproval daily. 他每天都暗示他的不满。
She intimates her needs through subtle gestures. 她通过细微的手势暗示她的需求。


一般过去时:
He intimated to me that he was leaving. 他暗示我他要离开了。
She intimated her intention to resign. 她暗示她打算辞职。


现在完成时:
They have intimated their support for the project. 他们已经暗示他们支持这个项目。
She has intimated to us that she's unhappy. 她已经暗示我们她不开心了。


过去完成时:
He had intimated his concerns several times before. 他之前已经多次暗示过他的担忧。
They had intimated their plans to move before the announcement. 在公告之前，他们已经暗示了他们搬家的计划。


将来时:
He will intimate his decision at the meeting. 他将在会议上暗示他的决定。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
His disapproval was intimated through his body language. 他的不满通过他的肢体语言被暗示出来。
The news was intimated to the employees quietly. 这个消息悄悄地通知了员工。
