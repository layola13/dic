get-away-with:/ˌɡɛt əˈweɪ wɪð/| v.|侥幸逃脱，未受惩罚|To escape punishment or the negative consequences of an action.|He tried to get away with cheating on the exam, but the teacher caught him.|近义词：escape, evade|反义词：be punished, be caught


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They get away with breaking the rules all the time. = 他们总是逃脱违反规定的惩罚。
We rarely get away with lying to our parents. = 我们很少能骗过父母而不受惩罚。


一般过去时:
She got away with plagiarism in her essay. = 她的论文抄袭侥幸逃过惩罚。
The thief got away with stealing the jewelry. = 小偷偷走了珠宝却逃脱了惩罚。


现在完成时:
He has gotten away with reckless driving for years. = 多年来他一直侥幸逃脱鲁莽驾驶的惩罚。
They have gotten away with tax evasion for too long. = 他们逃税的时间太长了。


过去完成时:
She had gotten away with lying to her boss for months. = 她对老板撒谎好几个月都没被发现。
The criminal had gotten away with numerous crimes before being caught. = 罪犯在被捕之前犯下许多罪行却都逃脱了惩罚。


将来时:
I doubt he will get away with this lie. = 我怀疑他这次谎话能瞒天过海。
She won't get away with ignoring her responsibilities. = 她不能逃避责任。


虚拟语气:

I wish I could get away with skipping class. = 我真希望可以逃课而不受惩罚。


被动语态:
The crime was gotten away with because of lack of evidence. = 由于缺乏证据，罪行得以逃脱惩罚。
His deception was not gotten away with; he was eventually exposed. = 他的欺骗行为没有逃脱惩罚；他最终被揭露了。


疑问句:
