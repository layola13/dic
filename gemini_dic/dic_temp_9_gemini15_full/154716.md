perspicuity:/ˌpɜːrspɪˈkjuːəti/| n. |清晰；明了；透彻|Clarity or lucidity; the quality of being easily understood.  Perspicuity is essential for effective communication, whether written or spoken.  The author's perspicuity made the complex subject matter accessible to a wide audience.|近义词：clarity, lucidity, intelligibility, transparency|反义词：obscurity, ambiguity, vagueness, confusion|


例句：

The perspicuity of her explanation helped me understand the difficult concept.
她的解释很清晰，帮助我理解了这个复杂的概念。


The professor's lectures were renowned for their perspicuity.
这位教授的讲座以其清晰著称。
