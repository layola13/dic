ethicists:/ˌeθɪˈsɪsts/| n. |伦理学家|People who study ethics, moral principles, and values. They analyze moral dilemmas and offer guidance on ethical conduct. Ethicists often work in academia, government, or private organizations, providing expertise in areas such as bioethics, business ethics, and environmental ethics. |例句：Many ethicists are concerned about the ethical implications of artificial intelligence. 许多伦理学家都关注人工智能的伦理影响。|例句：The company hired an ethicist to advise on their new marketing campaign. 公司聘请了一位伦理学家为他们的新营销活动提供咨询。|近义词：moral philosophers, ethicists, moralists|反义词：N/A
