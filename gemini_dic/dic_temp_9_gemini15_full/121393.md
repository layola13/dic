mishitting:/mɪsˈhɪtɪŋ/| v.|击偏；击打不准|To hit something inaccurately or without the desired result.  It implies a lack of precision or effectiveness in the striking action.|例句：He mishit the ball and it went into the rough. 他击球不准，球进了粗糙地带。
The golfer mishit several shots during the round. 高尔夫球手在比赛中几次击球不准。|近义词：misstrike, miscue, botch|反义词：hitting accurately, making contact correctly
