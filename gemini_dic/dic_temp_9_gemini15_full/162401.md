preaddressing:/ˌpriːˈædrɛsɪŋ/| v. |预先写地址；预先印地址 |The act of writing or printing addresses on envelopes or packages beforehand.  This is often done for mass mailings or shipments to save time and effort. |例句：The company uses a machine for preaddressing envelopes to improve efficiency. 公司使用机器预先打印信封地址以提高效率。

例句：We preaddressed all the invitations before the wedding. 我们在婚礼前预先写好了所有邀请函的地址。


近义词：prelabeling, pre-printing, addressing in advance |反义词：hand-addressing (after the fact)


词性：动词
