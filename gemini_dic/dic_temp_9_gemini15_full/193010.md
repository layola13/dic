secondarily:/ˈsɛkənderəli/| adv.|次要地；间接地；其次|In a secondary manner; as a secondary consequence.  It indicates something that is of less importance or that happens as a result of something else.|例句：He was secondarily involved in the scandal. 他间接卷入了这场丑闻。

例句：The drug's side effects are secondarily related to its primary function.  这种药物的副作用与其主要功能间接相关。


近义词：indirectly, incidentally, subordinately


反义词：primarily, principally, mainly

词性：副词
