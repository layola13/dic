/ʃæmˈwɑːzɪŋ/| v.|擦拭（通常指用柔软的皮革擦拭）|To clean or polish (something) using a soft leather, typically chamois leather.  The process often involves rubbing to achieve a shine or remove marks.|例句：He chamoised the car after washing it. 他洗车后用皮革擦拭了它。|例句：She carefully chamoised the antique silver, restoring its luster. 她仔细地用绒皮擦拭古董银器，恢复了它的光泽。|近义词：polish, buff, rub down|反义词：soil, dirty
