canucks:/ˈkænʌks/| n. |加拿大人（尤指加拿大西部人）|A derogatory or informal term for Canadians, especially those from western Canada.  The term's origins are uncertain, but it may be a shortening of "Canadians".  It's often used playfully, but can be offensive depending on context.|例句：Some people consider "Canucks" a playful nickname, while others find it offensive.  有些人认为“Canucks”是一个俏皮的昵称，而另一些人则认为它具有冒犯性。

例句：He jokingly referred to his friends from Vancouver as "Canucks". 他开玩笑地称他来自温哥华的朋友为“加拿大人”。


近义词：Canadians (formal), Canucks (informal)


反义词：无


词性：名词
