predispatched:/ˌpriːdɪˈspætʃt/| adj.|预先派遣的，预先发出的|Pre-dispatched refers to something that has been sent or dispatched beforehand or in advance.  It implies preparation and planning before the main event or action.|例句：The emergency supplies were predispatched to the disaster zone. 预先将紧急物资运送到灾区。 The predispatched team arrived before the main delegation. 预先派遣的团队在主要代表团之前到达。|近义词：pre-sent, pre-arranged, advanced|反义词：delayed, late
