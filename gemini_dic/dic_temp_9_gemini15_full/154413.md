permission:/pərˈmɪʃən/ | n. |许可，允许，批准 | Permission is the act of allowing someone to do something.  It implies consent or authorization. |例句：I need your permission to use your car. 我需要你的允许才能使用你的车。|例句：She gave permission for the project to proceed. 她批准了该项目继续进行。|近义词：authorization, consent, approval |反义词：prohibition, denial, refusal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He needs permission to leave early. 他需要许可才能早退。
She requests permission to speak. 她请求发言许可。


一般过去时:
They received permission to enter the building. 他们获得了进入大楼的许可。
He asked for permission but was refused. 他请求许可，但被拒绝了。


现在完成时:
I have received permission to publish the article. 我已经获得了发表这篇文章的许可。
She has not yet obtained permission to travel. 她还没有获得旅行许可。


过去完成时:
They had already obtained permission before they started the work. 他们在开始工作之前就已经获得了许可。
He had sought permission, but it was too late. 他曾寻求过许可，但为时已晚。


将来时:
We will seek permission before we begin construction. 我们将在动工之前寻求许可。
She will need permission from her supervisor. 她将需要她主管的许可。


虚拟语气:
If I had permission, I would go to the party. 如果我有许可，我会去参加聚会。
I wish I had permission to travel abroad. 我希望我有出国旅行的许可。


被动语态:
Permission was granted to the researchers. 研究人员获得了许可。
Access was denied; permission was not given. 访问被拒绝；未给予许可。


疑问句:
Do you have permission to be here? 你有在这里的许可吗？
Did they get permission to film the documentary? 他们获得拍摄纪录片的许可了吗？


否定句:
They did not receive permission to enter. 他们没有获得进入的许可。
I was denied permission to access the files. 我被拒绝访问文件的许可。


PHRASE->
ask for permission 请求许可
grant permission 授予许可
with permission 经许可
without permission 未经许可
deny permission 拒绝许可
