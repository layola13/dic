geniuses:/ˈdʒiːniəsɪz/| n.|天才们，天才人物们|The plural form of "genius," referring to multiple exceptionally intelligent or creative people.  It implies a group of individuals with extraordinary abilities.|例句：The geniuses of the Renaissance revolutionized art and science.  文艺复兴时期的天才们彻底革新了艺术和科学。

例句：Many geniuses throughout history have struggled with mental health issues. 历史上许多天才都饱受精神健康问题的困扰。

近义词：prodigies, masterminds, virtuosos
反义词：idiots, dunces, imbeciles
词性：名词
