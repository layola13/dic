posttibial: /ˌpoʊstˈtɪbiəl/ | adj. | 胫骨后的；腓骨侧的 | Relating to the back of the tibia (shinbone).  This term is primarily used in anatomy and medicine to describe the location of structures or conditions behind the tibia. | Example: The posttibial artery supplies blood to the muscles in the back of the lower leg.  胫后动脉为小腿后部的肌肉供血。 | 近义词: posterior tibial,  | 反义词: pretibial (胫骨前的) |


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and thus does not have verb conjugations.  Its usage is limited to descriptive sentences.

Example 1:  The doctor examined the patient's posttibial muscles. 医生检查了病人的胫骨后肌。
Example 2:  Pain in the posttibial region could indicate a nerve issue. 胫骨后区域的疼痛可能表明神经问题。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "posttibial".  The term is highly specialized.
