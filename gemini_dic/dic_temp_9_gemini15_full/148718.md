overtrade:/ˌoʊvərˈtreɪd/ | v. |过度交易，过度买卖 | To trade excessively or beyond one's means, often resulting in financial difficulties.  This involves engaging in more trading activity than is prudent or sustainable, potentially leading to losses and debt. |例句：He overtraded in the stock market and lost a significant amount of money. 他在股市过度交易，损失了一大笔钱。

The company overtraded last year, resulting in a substantial loss.  这家公司去年过度交易，导致了巨额亏损。| 近义词：speculate excessively, overextend, overreach | 反义词：undertrade, cautious trading
