imbecilitate: /ɪmˌbɛsɪləˌteɪt/ | vt. | 使愚笨；使弱智；削弱 | To weaken or impair, especially mentally; to make feeble-minded.  |例句：The constant stress seemed to imbecilitate him, affecting his decision-making abilities.  持续的压力似乎使他变得迟钝，影响了他的决策能力。

例句：Years of neglect had imbecilitated the once-thriving community.  多年的忽视使曾经繁荣的社区变得衰败。


近义词：weaken, debilitate, enfeeble, cripple, incapacitate | 反义词：strengthen, invigorate, empower
