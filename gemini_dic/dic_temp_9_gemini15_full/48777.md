deflationary:/dɪˈfleɪʃənəri/| adj. |通货紧缩的，紧缩性的| Relating to or causing deflation, a reduction in the general level of prices.  Deflationary policies aim to reduce inflation by decreasing the money supply or reducing government spending. | 例句：The government implemented deflationary measures to combat rising prices.  政府实施紧缩措施以对抗物价上涨。

例句：The deflationary spiral led to decreased consumer spending and economic stagnation. 通货紧缩的螺旋式下降导致消费者支出减少和经济停滞。


近义词：contractionary, recessionary |反义词：inflationary, expansionary
