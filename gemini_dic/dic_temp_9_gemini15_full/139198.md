objections:/əbˈdʒɛkʃənz/| n.|反对；异议；拒绝|Expressions of disapproval or protest; reasons for disagreeing with or opposing something.  |例句：He raised several objections to the plan. 他对这个计划提出了几点异议。

例句：There were strong objections to the new law. 对这项新法律有很多强烈的反对意见。


近义词：protests, complaints, criticisms, reservations, dissent

反义词：approvals, agreements, acceptances


词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His objections are always well-founded. = 他的反对意见总是很有道理的。
Their objections to the proposal are clear. = 他们对这个提案的反对意见很明确。


一般过去时:
His objections were overruled by the committee. = 他的反对意见被委员会否决了。
Her objections to the changes were ignored. = 她对这些改变的反对意见被忽视了。


现在完成时:
They have raised several objections to the project. = 他们对这个项目提出了好几个异议。
We have received many objections to the new policy. = 我们收到了很多关于新政策的反对意见。


过去完成时:
He had voiced his objections before the vote. = 他在投票前已经表达了他的反对意见。
They had already submitted their objections in writing. = 他们已经以书面形式提交了他们的反对意见。


将来时:
We anticipate objections to the new rules. = 我们预料到对新规章会有反对意见。
They will likely raise objections during the discussion. = 他们很可能会在讨论中提出反对意见。


虚拟语气:
If there were no objections, we would proceed. = 如果没有异议，我们将继续进行。
I wish there were fewer objections to my ideas. = 我希望我的想法少一些反对意见。


被动语态:
His objections were ignored. = 他的反对意见被忽略了。
The objections were carefully considered. = 反对意见受到了仔细的考虑。


疑问句:
Are there any objections to the motion? = 对这个动议有异议吗？
What are your objections to this solution? = 你对这个方案有什么异议？


否定句:
There were no objections to the proposal. = 对这个提案没有任何异议。
She had no objections to the plan. = 她对这个计划没有任何异议。


PHRASE->
