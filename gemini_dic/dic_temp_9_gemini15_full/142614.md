osteoperiostitis:/ˌɒstioʊˌpɛriəˈsaɪtɪs/| n. |骨膜炎|Inflammation of the periosteum, the membrane covering the bone.  It's often caused by infection, trauma, or autoimmune diseases. Symptoms can include pain, swelling, and tenderness at the affected bone. |例句：The athlete experienced severe osteoperiostitis in his tibia after a hard fall.  这位运动员在重重摔倒后胫骨出现了严重的骨膜炎。|近义词：periostitis |反义词：无
