deescalations: /ˌdiːˈɛskəleɪʃənz/ | n. |冲突降级；缓和局势；平息事态 | The plural form of "deescalation," referring to multiple instances of reducing the intensity or severity of a conflict or tense situation.  It involves taking steps to calm things down and avoid escalation. | Example: The police were praised for their successful deescalations of several potentially violent situations.  警方因成功平息数起可能演变成暴力事件而受到赞扬。|近义词：conflict resolution, de-escalations, calming, pacification |反义词：escalations, inflammations, aggravations | 名词
