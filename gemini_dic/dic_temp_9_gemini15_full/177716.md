recapacitate:/ˌriːkəˈpæsɪteɪt/| vt.|使恢复能力；使恢复活力|To restore someone or something to a former capacity or ability; to rehabilitate.|例句：The therapy helped to recapacitate him after his stroke.  治疗帮助他中风后恢复了能力。

近义词：rehabilitate, restore, revitalize|反义词：debilitate, incapacitate, disable|词性:动词
