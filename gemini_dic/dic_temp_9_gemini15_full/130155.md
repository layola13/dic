non-representative:/ˌnɒnˌreprɛzˈɛntətɪv/| adj. |不具有代表性的，非典型的|Not typical or representative of a larger group or whole.  It implies a lack of accuracy in reflecting the characteristics of the population or group being studied. |例句：The sample was non-representative, skewing the results of the survey.  这个样本不具有代表性，歪曲了调查结果。

例句：This small group is non-representative of the entire population's views.  这个小群体不能代表全体民众的观点。

近义词： atypical, unrepresentative, untypical, biased

反义词：representative, typical, characteristic
