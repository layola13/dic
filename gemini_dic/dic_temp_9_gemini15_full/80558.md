goodwills:/ˈɡʊdwɪlz/| n.|商誉，善意|Goodwill is an intangible asset representing the value of a company's reputation and customer relationships.  It is often considered in mergers and acquisitions.|例句：The acquisition price included a significant amount of goodwill.  收购价格包含了大量的商誉。|例句：He acted with goodwill towards his former colleagues. 他对前任同事怀有善意。|近义词：reputation, brand value, favor|反义词：ill will, malice, antagonism|名词
