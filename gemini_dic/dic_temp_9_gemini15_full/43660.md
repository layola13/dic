creaturely: /ˈkriːtʃərli/ | adj. | 生物似的；有生命的；像动物的 | Relating to or characteristic of creatures; having the qualities of a creature.  It often implies a lack of higher intellectual or spiritual qualities, suggesting something base or instinctual. |例句：His creaturely desires overwhelmed his better judgment.  他的动物本能冲昏了他的头脑。

例句：The creaturely fear in her eyes was palpable. 她眼中动物般的恐惧是显而易见的。


近义词：animalistic, bestial, brutish, primal, instinctive

反义词：spiritual, intellectual, refined, civilized, cultured


词性:形容词
