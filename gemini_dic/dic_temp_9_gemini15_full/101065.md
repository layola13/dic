invention:/ɪnˈvɛnʃən/| n.|发明；创造；虚构|The creation of something new, especially a device, process, or technique.  It can also refer to a new idea or concept. |例句：The invention of the printing press revolutionized communication. 印刷术的发明彻底改变了交流方式。

例句：His latest invention is a self-cleaning oven.  他的最新发明是一款自清洁烤箱。


近义词：discovery, creation, innovation, development|反义词：destruction, demolition


词源:  The word "invention" comes from the Old French word "invention," which in turn derives from the Latin word "inventio," meaning "a finding," "discovery," or "contrivance."  The Latin word is formed from the verb "invenire," meaning "to find" or "to come upon."
