psychotherapists:/ˌsaɪkoʊˈθɛrəpɪsts/| n. |心理治疗师|A psychotherapist is a mental health professional who treats mental illness and emotional distress through talk therapy and other methods. They help individuals understand and manage their thoughts, feelings, and behaviors.  |Many psychotherapists use a combination of techniques, tailored to each client's needs.|近义词：therapist, counselor, psychologist, psychiatrist|反义词：N/A
