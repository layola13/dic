out-patient:/ˈaʊtˌpeɪʃənt/| n. |门诊病人，门诊患者 |A person who receives medical treatment at a hospital or clinic without being admitted as an inpatient.  |Example:  The doctor saw several out-patients before lunch.  医生午饭前接诊了几位门诊病人。|近义词：ambulatory patient, non-resident patient |反义词：inpatient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is an out-patient at the clinic. = 他是这家诊所的门诊病人。
She attends out-patient physiotherapy sessions. = 她参加门诊理疗。


一般过去时:
My grandmother was an out-patient at that hospital last year. = 我奶奶去年是那家医院的门诊病人。
He received out-patient treatment for his injury. = 他因伤接受了门诊治疗。


现在完成时:
She has been an out-patient at this hospital for six months. = 她在这家医院做门诊病人已经六个月了。
They have received out-patient care since the accident. = 自事故发生以来，他们一直在接受门诊护理。


过去完成时:
Before the surgery, he had been an out-patient for several weeks. = 手术前，他做了几周的门诊病人。
She had received out-patient consultations before her admission. = 她入院前已接受过门诊咨询。


将来时:
He will be an out-patient next week for a follow-up appointment. = 他下周将作为门诊病人进行复诊。
She will receive out-patient care after her discharge. = 出院后，她将接受门诊护理。


虚拟语气:
If he were an out-patient, he could go home earlier. = 如果他是门诊病人，他就能早点回家。
I wish I were an out-patient instead of being hospitalized. = 我希望我是门诊病人而不是住院病人。


被动语态:
The patient was treated as an out-patient. = 病人被当作门诊病人治疗。
Out-patient services are provided at this clinic. = 这家诊所提供门诊服务。


疑问句:
Is he an out-patient or an in-patient? = 他是门诊病人还是住院病人？
Are out-patient appointments available today? = 今天有门诊预约吗？


否定句:
He is not an out-patient; he's been admitted. = 他不是门诊病人；他已经住院了。
She did not receive out-patient treatment for that condition. = 她没有因为那种病症接受门诊治疗。
