impromptu:/ɪmˈprɒmptjuː/ | adj. | 即兴的，无预谋的 |  done without being planned, organized, or rehearsed | The singer gave an impromptu performance.  这位歌手即兴表演了一段。|近义词：improvised, spontaneous, extemporaneous | 反义词：planned, rehearsed, prepared


USAGE->

The decision to leave was entirely impromptu. = 离开的决定完全是即兴的。
