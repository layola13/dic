narrowed:/ˈnærəʊd/ | v. | 缩小；变窄；限制；集中 | To make or become narrower; to restrict or limit something; to focus or concentrate something. |例句：The road narrowed as we approached the village.  我们靠近村庄时，路变窄了。|例句：He narrowed his eyes in suspicion. 他怀疑地眯起了眼睛。|近义词：constricted, reduced, confined, focused |反义词：widened, expanded, broadened


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He narrows his focus on the project. 他把注意力集中在项目上。
The river narrows significantly downstream. 河流在下游明显变窄。


一般过去时:
They narrowed down the choices to three. 他们把选择范围缩小到三个。
The path narrowed sharply at the bend. 小路在拐弯处急剧变窄。


现在完成时:
The government has narrowed the gap between rich and poor. 政府已经缩小了贫富差距。
The investigation has narrowed the suspects to two. 调查已将嫌疑人缩小到两人。


过去完成时:
They had narrowed their search to a specific area. 他们已将搜索范围缩小到特定区域。
The company had narrowed its losses in the previous quarter. 公司在上个季度已经减少了损失。


将来时:
The team will narrow the focus to key areas.  团队将把重点放在关键领域。
This strategy will narrow the market share of our competitors.  这个策略将缩小我们竞争对手的市场份额。


虚拟语气:
If they narrowed their options, they might make a better decision. 如果他们缩小选择范围，他们可能会做出更好的决定。
If the tunnel had narrowed further, we would have been trapped. 如果隧道进一步变窄，我们就会被困住。


被动语态:
The options were narrowed down by the committee. 选择范围被委员会缩小了。
The search area was narrowed by the police. 搜索区域被警方缩小了。


疑问句:
Has the investigation narrowed the possibilities? 调查是否缩小了可能性？
How can we narrow down the list of candidates? 我们如何缩小候选人名单？


否定句:
The road didn't narrow until we reached the mountains. 直到我们到达山区，道路才变窄。
They didn't narrow their focus enough on the core issue. 他们没有足够地关注核心问题。
