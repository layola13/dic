scilicet:/ˈsɪlɪsɛt/|adv.|即，也就是，那就是|It is an abbreviation of the Latin phrase "scilicet," meaning "namely" or "that is to say."  It's used to clarify or explain something previously stated.  It functions similarly to "namely," "that is," or "to wit."|Example: The meeting was adjourned, scilicet, postponed until next week.  会议休会了，也就是说，推迟到下周。|近义词：namely, that is, to wit, i.e.|反义词：无
