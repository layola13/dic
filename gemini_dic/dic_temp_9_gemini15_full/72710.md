focuser:/ˈfoʊkəsər/| n. |聚焦器，调焦器|A device used to adjust the focus of an optical instrument, such as a camera or microscope.  It can also refer to a person or thing that focuses attention or effort.|Example: The camera's focuser allowed me to get a perfectly sharp image.  摄像机的调焦器使我能够获得非常清晰的图像。|近义词：focus adjustment, focusing mechanism|反义词：defocuser (not a common word)|名词
