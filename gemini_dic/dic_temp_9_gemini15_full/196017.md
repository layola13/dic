sentinel: /ˈsɛntɪnəl/ | n. |哨兵；岗哨；守护者 | A sentinel is a person or thing that stands guard or keeps watch.  Sentinels are often soldiers stationed to guard a particular area.  The term can also be used metaphorically to describe something that acts as a warning or protection. | Example: The soldier stood as a sentinel, guarding the bridge.  士兵作为哨兵守卫着桥梁。  The canary in the coal mine acted as a sentinel, warning the miners of danger.  矿井里的金丝雀充当了哨兵，警告矿工有危险。|近义词：guard, watchman, lookout, protector |反义词：attacker, invader
