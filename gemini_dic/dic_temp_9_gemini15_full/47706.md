debuts:/ˈdeɪbjuːz/| n.|首次亮相；初次登台；首次推出|The first public appearance of a performer or work.|例句：The band's debut album was a huge success. 乐队的首张专辑大获成功。|近义词：premiere, launch, introduction|反义词：retirement, finale


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Her new novel debuts next month. = 她的新小说下个月首发。
The band debuts their new song tonight. = 乐队今晚首发他们的新歌。


一般过去时:
The play debuted in London last year. = 这部剧去年在伦敦首演。
The artist debuted her new collection at the gallery. = 艺术家在画廊首发了她的新系列作品。


现在完成时:
The company has debuted several new products this year. = 公司今年已经推出几款新产品了。
She has debuted in several films since her first role. = 自从她第一次出演以来，她已经在几部电影中亮相了。


过去完成时:
The painting had debuted at a smaller gallery before it was shown here. = 这幅画在展出在这里之前，曾在一家较小的画廊展出过。
By the time of the concert, the band had debuted three successful singles. = 到演唱会的时候，乐队已经推出了三首成功的单曲。


将来时:
The film will debut next spring. = 这部电影将于明年春天首映。
The singer will debut her new album next week. = 歌手下周将推出她的新专辑。


虚拟语气:
If the play debuted earlier, it might have been more successful. = 如果这部剧早点首演，可能会更成功。
I wish the novel had debuted sooner, I've been waiting for it for ages. = 我希望这部小说早点首发，我已经等了很久了。


被动语态:
The new car model was debuted at the auto show. = 新款汽车车型在车展上首次亮相。
The song was debuted on a popular radio station. = 这首歌在一个热门电台首播。


疑问句:
When will the new product debut? = 新产品什么时候首发？
Where did the artist debut her work? = 艺术家在哪里首发她的作品？


否定句:
The album didn't debut as well as expected. = 专辑的销量不如预期。
The play didn't debut in New York, but in Los Angeles. = 这部剧不是在纽约首演，而是在洛杉矶。
