non-Muslim: /ˌnɒnˈmʌzlɪm/ | adj. n. | 非穆斯林的；非穆斯林 |  A person who is not a Muslim.  This term refers to individuals who do not adhere to the Islamic faith.  The term can be used as an adjective to describe something not related to Islam or as a noun to refer to a person who is not Muslim. |

例句：

The survey included both Muslim and non-Muslim respondents.  这项调查包括穆斯林和非穆斯林受访者。

Many non-Muslims live in this country.  许多非穆斯林居住在这个国家。


近义词：non-Islamic

反义词：Muslim

词性：形容词，名词
