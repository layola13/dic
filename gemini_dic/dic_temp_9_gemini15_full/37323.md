commemorative:/kəˈmɛməreɪtɪv/| adj. |纪念的；庆祝的| Relating to or intended to commemorate something.  A commemorative event or object serves to remember or honor a person or event. |例句：The commemorative stamp was issued to mark the 50th anniversary of the country's independence. 这枚纪念邮票是为了纪念该国独立50周年而发行的。

例句：They held a commemorative service to honor the victims of the war.他们举行了一场纪念活动来悼念战争的受害者。


近义词：memorial, celebratory, remembering


反义词：forgetful, oblivious


词性：adj.
