词源:
The word "irrevocable" comes from the Latin prefix "ir-" (not) + "revocable" (capable of being revoked). "Revocable" in turn derives from the Latin verb "revocare" (to call back, recall, revoke).  Therefore,  "irrevocable" literally means "not capable of being called back."
