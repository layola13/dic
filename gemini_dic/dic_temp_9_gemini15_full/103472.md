jobbie:/ˈdʒɒbi/| n.|便溺；琐碎的工作；（非正式）粪便|A jobbie is a colloquial term, primarily used in British English, referring to a small job or task, often of a menial or unpleasant nature.  It can also be a slang term for feces.  The specific meaning depends heavily on context.|例句：He's always got a load of little jobbies to do around the house. 他总是有很多零零碎碎的家务要做。  例句：The dog left a jobbie on the lawn. 狗在草坪上拉了一泡屎。|近义词：(small job) chore, task; (feces) stool, crap, dung|反义词: (for small job) major project, large undertaking; (for feces) clean
