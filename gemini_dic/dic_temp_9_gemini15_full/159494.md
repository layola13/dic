polios:/ˈpoʊliˌoʊz/ | n. |小儿麻痹症|Poliomyelitis, commonly known as polio, is a highly infectious disease caused by the poliovirus.  It can cause paralysis, and in severe cases, death.  The disease is preventable through vaccination.|例：The eradication of polio is a major public health achievement.  消灭小儿麻痹症是一项重大的公共卫生成就。|近义词：infantile paralysis |反义词：无
