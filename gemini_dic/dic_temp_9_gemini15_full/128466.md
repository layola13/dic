newsiness:/ˈnuːzinɪs/| n. |新闻性；新奇性；重要性|The quality of being new or interesting enough to be considered news.  It often refers to the degree to which a piece of information is timely, relevant, and impactful.  A high degree of newsiness makes a story more likely to be reported.|The newsiness of the story made it front-page news. 这则新闻的重要性使其成为头条新闻。  The event lacked newsiness and was therefore ignored by the media.  该事件缺乏新闻性，因此被媒体忽略了。|newness, novelty, importance |unimportance, irrelevance, oldness|名词
