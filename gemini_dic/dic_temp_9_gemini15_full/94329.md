imphee:/ɪmˈfiː/| n. |甘蔗（一种）|A type of sugarcane, particularly a variety grown in India and other parts of Asia. It is known for its high sugar content and is used in the production of sugar and other sweeteners.|Example:  The farmers harvested a bountiful crop of imphee this year.  今年农民收获了丰盛的甘蔗作物。|近义词：sugarcane, sorghum (in some contexts)|反义词：无|名词
