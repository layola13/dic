outpopulate:/ˌaʊtˈpɑːpjuleɪt/ | vt. |超过…的人口数量；人口超过 |To exceed in numbers; to surpass in population. |例句：The immigrant population outpopulated the native population in that area.  那个地区的移民人口超过了当地居民人口。

例句：The rabbits quickly outpopulated the foxes in the forest. 兔子很快就在森林里超过了狐狸的数量。


近义词：outnumber, exceed, surpass

反义词：be outnumbered by, be surpassed by

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The immigrants outpopulate the locals in this city. = 移民在这个城市的人口数量超过当地居民。
This species outpopulates all other species in this region. = 这个物种在这个地区的人口数量超过所有其他物种。

一般过去时:
The city outpopulated its neighboring towns last year. = 去年这座城市的人口超过了周边城镇。
The invasive plant species outpopulated the native flora. = 这种入侵植物物种超过了本地植物的数量。

现在完成时:
The rabbits have outpopulated the foxes in the forest. = 兔子已经超过了森林里狐狸的数量。
The human population has outpopulated many other animal species. = 人口数量已经超过了许多其他动物物种。


过去完成时:
By 1900, the city had outpopulated the county. = 到1900年，这座城市的人口已经超过了整个县。
The newcomers had outpopulated the original settlers before the end of the century. = 在世纪末之前，新来的人口已经超过了最早的定居者。


将来时:
Experts predict that the city will outpopulate the neighboring towns by 2030. = 专家预测到2030年，这座城市的人口将超过周边城镇。
It is likely that this species will outpopulate its competitors. = 这个物种很可能会超过它的竞争者。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
The town did not outpopulate the surrounding villages. = 这个城镇的人口没有超过周围的村庄。
This species has not yet outpopulated the dominant species. = 这个物种尚未超过优势物种。
