pauropoda:/pɔːˈrɒpədə/| n. |少足纲动物|Pauropoda are a class of myriapods, small, mostly soil-dwelling arthropods.  They are characterized by their small size (typically less than 2 mm in length), their lack of eyes, and their unique body segmentation.  They are often found in moist environments and feed on decaying organic matter. |Example:  Scientists discovered a new species of pauropoda in the Amazon rainforest.  科学家在亚马逊雨林中发现了一种新的少足纲动物。  |近义词:Myriapod (broad term)|反义词:None specifically|名词
