petar:/ˈpiːtɑːr/| n.|土炮，爆竹| A petard is a small bomb or explosive device, typically used in the past for military or demolition purposes.  It is also used figuratively to refer to something that backfires or has unintended consequences.|例句：The rebels used petards to breach the city walls.  叛军用土炮攻破了城墙。|近义词：firecracker, bomb, explosive|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept would be a safe, controlled device.
