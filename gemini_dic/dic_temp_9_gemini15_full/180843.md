rehearse:/rɪˈhɜːrs/| vt.|排练，预演，练习|To practice or rehearse something, especially a play or a musical performance, in preparation for a public performance.|例句：The actors rehearsed the play for several weeks before the opening night.  演员们在首映夜之前排练了这部戏好几个星期。
例句：The band spent hours rehearsing their new songs. 乐队花了几个小时排练他们的新歌。|近义词：practice, prepare, review|反义词：perform


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rehearse every day. = 他们每天排练。
The band rehearses its new songs. = 乐队排练它的新歌。

一般过去时:
We rehearsed the play last night. = 我们昨晚排练了这出戏。
She rehearsed her speech before the presentation. = 她在演讲前排练了她的演讲。

现在完成时:
They have rehearsed the play many times. = 他们已经排练了这出戏很多次了。
The actors have rehearsed all week. = 演员们排练了一整周。

过去完成时:
They had rehearsed the scene before the director arrived. = 导演到达之前，他们已经排练过那个场景了。
The musicians had rehearsed for hours before the concert. = 音乐家们在音乐会前排练了好几个小时。

将来时:
We will rehearse tomorrow. = 我们明天将排练。
The choir will rehearse the anthem for the ceremony. = 合唱团将为仪式排练赞美歌。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would rehearse more often. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们会更频繁地排练。
I wish I had rehearsed more before the audition. = 我希望我在试镜前排练得更多。

被动语态:
The play was rehearsed thoroughly. = 这出戏被彻底排练了。
The song was rehearsed by the singer many times. = 这首歌被歌手排练了很多次。

疑问句:
Did you rehearse your lines? = 你排练你的台词了吗？
Have they rehearsed the music yet? = 他们排练音乐了吗？

否定句:
They didn't rehearse the dance routine. = 他们没有排练舞蹈套路。
We haven't rehearsed enough. = 我们排练得还不够。


PHRASE->
rehearse a speech = 排练演讲
rehearse a play = 排练戏剧
rehearse a scene = 排练场景
rehearse for a performance = 为演出排练
rehearse the details = 排练细节
