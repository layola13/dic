leaseless:/ˈliːsləs/| adj.| 无租约的；不需租金的 |Without a lease; not subject to lease.  The term implies a lack of a formal rental agreement. |例句：The land was leaseless, available for anyone to use. 这块土地没有租约，任何人都可以使用。

例句：He lived on leaseless land, paying no rent. 他住在没有租约的土地上，不用付租金。


近义词：rent-free, unleased, free


反义词：leased, rented
