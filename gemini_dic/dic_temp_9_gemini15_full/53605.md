discanonize:/dɪˈskænənaɪz/| vt.|取消…的圣徒地位；废除…的教规；剥夺…的权威|To remove someone or something from the canon, especially to officially declare that a saint is not a saint.  This term is primarily used in religious contexts, particularly within the Catholic Church, referring to the removal of a person from the list of recognized saints. It can also be used more broadly to refer to the removal of something from an accepted or established body of work or belief.|例句：The church decided to discanonize the saint after new evidence emerged. 教会决定在出现新的证据后取消该圣徒的圣徒地位。The controversial painting was effectively discanonized by art critics.  这幅有争议的画作实际上已被艺术评论家们否定。|近义词：decanonize, declassify (in a broader context)|反义词：canonize, sanctify
