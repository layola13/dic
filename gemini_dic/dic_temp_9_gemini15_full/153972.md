perfectionist:/pərˈfɛkʃənɪst/ | n. |完美主义者 |A person who refuses to accept any standard short of perfection.  They often have high standards for themselves and others, and can be overly critical.  This can lead to stress, anxiety, and difficulty in completing tasks. |He's such a perfectionist; he spent three days on a single paragraph. 他是一个完美主义者；他花三天时间写了一段话。|近义词：idealist, purist |反义词：sloven, pragmatist


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is a perfectionist. = 他是个完美主义者。
She strives for perfection in everything she does. = 她力求在她做的每一件事上都做到完美。

一般过去时:
He was a perfectionist in his work. = 他在工作中是一个完美主义者。
She was always striving for perfection. = 她总是力求完美。

现在完成时:
He has always been a perfectionist. = 他一直都是个完美主义者。
She has become a perfectionist over the years. = 这些年来她已经变成了一个完美主义者。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
