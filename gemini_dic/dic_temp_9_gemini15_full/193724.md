self-conserving: /ˌsɛlf kənˈsɜːrvɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我保存的；自利的；利己的 |  Self-conserving refers to something or someone that maintains or protects itself, often at the expense of others.  It implies a focus on one's own well-being and survival, sometimes to a selfish degree. |

例句：

The self-conserving nature of the virus made it difficult to eradicate.  病毒的自我保存特性使其难以根除。

His self-conserving actions alienated him from his colleagues.  他自私的行为使他与同事疏远了。


近义词：self-protective, self-preserving, selfish, egoistic, self-interested

反义词：altruistic, selfless, unselfish, self-sacrificing


词性：形容词
