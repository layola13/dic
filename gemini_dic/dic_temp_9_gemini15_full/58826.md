duped:/djuːpt/| v.|欺骗，愚弄|To deceive someone, typically by trickery.|例句：He was duped into giving away his money. 他被骗走了钱。  例句：The con artist duped many people out of their savings. 骗子骗走了许多人的积蓄。|近义词：deceived, tricked, cheated, hoodwinked|反义词：enlightened, informed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They dupe unsuspecting victims daily. = 他们每天都欺骗毫无戒心的受害者。
The company dupes customers with false advertising. = 这家公司用虚假广告欺骗顾客。

一般过去时:
The salesman duped me into buying that useless product. = 销售员骗我买了那个没用的产品。
She was duped by a clever online scam. = 她被一个精明的网络骗局骗了。

现在完成时:
He has been duped several times by the same person. = 他已经被同一个人骗过好几次了。
They have duped investors out of millions of dollars. = 他们已经骗取了投资者数百万美元。

过去完成时:
I realized I had been duped after I had already signed the contract. = 在我签署合同后，我才意识到自己被骗了。
The police discovered that they had been duped by a sophisticated forgery. = 警方发现他们被一个复杂的伪造品所欺骗。

将来时:
They will dupe many more people if they are not caught. = 如果他们不被抓住，他们还会欺骗更多的人。
The con artist plans to dupe the wealthy businessman. = 骗子计划欺骗那位富有的商人。

虚拟语气:
If I had known the truth, I wouldn't have been duped. = 如果我知道真相，我就不会被骗了。
I wish I hadn't been duped by his charming demeanor. = 我真希望我没有被他迷人的举止所欺骗。

被动语态:
Many people are duped by online scams every day. = 每天都有很多人被网络骗局所欺骗。
He was duped into signing a contract he didn't understand. = 他被骗签署了一份他不理解的合同。

疑问句:
Were you duped by the advertisement? = 你被广告骗了吗？
Has she been duped by this company before? = 她以前被这家公司骗过吗？

否定句:
He wasn't duped; he knew exactly what he was doing. = 他没有被骗；他知道自己在做什么。
I wasn't duped by their promises; I was skeptical from the start. = 我没有被他们的承诺所欺骗；我从一开始就持怀疑态度。
