regne:/ˈreɪn/| n.|王国，统治时期|A kingdom or realm; a period of rule.|例句：The reign of Queen Victoria was a long and prosperous one. 维多利亚女王的统治时期漫长而繁荣。|近义词：kingdom, empire, dominion|反义词：anarchy, chaos


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The king reigns over the entire kingdom. = 国王统治着整个王国。
His reign brings peace and prosperity. = 他的统治带来了和平与繁荣。


一般过去时:
The queen reigned for over fifty years. = 女王统治了50多年。
His reign ended with his sudden death. = 他的统治以他的突然去世而告终。


现在完成时:
The dynasty has reigned for centuries. = 这个王朝已经统治了几个世纪。
She has reigned with wisdom and grace. = 她以智慧和优雅统治着。


过去完成时:
Before the new king reigned, there was a civil war. = 新国王统治之前，发生了一场内战。
The people had suffered greatly before his reign. = 在他的统治之前，人民饱受苦难。


将来时:
The prince will reign after his father's death. = 王子将在父亲死后继位。
It is predicted that his reign will be long and peaceful. = 预计他的统治将是漫长而和平的。


虚拟语气:
If he were to reign, he would bring great changes. = 如果他统治的话，他会带来巨大的变化。
I wish the king reigned more fairly. = 我希望国王统治得更公正一些。


被动语态:
The kingdom was reigned over by a wise king. = 这个王国由一位英明的国王统治。
The country has been reigned over by many monarchs. = 这个国家已经被许多君主统治过。


疑问句:
How long did the king reign? = 国王统治了多久？
Who will reign after him? = 他之后谁将统治？


否定句:
The tyrant did not reign justly. = 暴君统治得不公正。
He did not reign long enough to make a significant impact. = 他统治的时间不够长，无法产生重大影响。
