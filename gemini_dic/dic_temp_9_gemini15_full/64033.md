epithelioma:/ˌɛpɪθiːliˈoʊmə/ | n. | 上皮瘤 | A malignant tumor of the epithelium (the surface layer of cells covering internal and external body surfaces).  It is a type of skin cancer. | Example:  The dermatologist diagnosed the lesion as a basal cell epithelioma. 皮肤科医生诊断该病变为基底细胞上皮瘤。 | 近义词: skin cancer, carcinoma (depending on type) | 反义词: (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with benign tumor) | 名词
