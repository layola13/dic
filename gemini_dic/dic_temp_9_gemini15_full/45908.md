cutlet:/ˈkʌtlɪt/| n.|肉片，炸肉排|A cutlet is a small, thin, flat piece of meat, especially meat from a cut of meat that is typically fried or pan-fried.  It can be made from various meats, such as veal, pork, chicken, or lamb.|例句：I ordered the chicken cutlet for dinner. 我晚餐点了鸡肉排。

例句：She prepared delicious veal cutlets for the guests. 她为客人准备了美味的小牛肉排。


近义词：chop, steak (depending on the cut and preparation) |反义词：whole roast (the opposite of a cut portion) |词性:Noun
