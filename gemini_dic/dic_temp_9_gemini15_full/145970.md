overdiversified:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪd/ | adj. |过度多元化的；过于分散的 |  Describes a situation where excessive diversification has led to reduced efficiency or effectiveness, often due to spreading resources too thinly. This can apply to investments, business strategies, or even personal skills.  |  The company's overdiversified portfolio led to poor returns.  这家公司过度多元化的投资组合导致回报不佳。 The curriculum is overdiversified, leaving students with a superficial understanding of many subjects.  课程过于多元化，导致学生对许多学科的理解流于表面。 |  diversified, spread out | focused, concentrated, specialized |
