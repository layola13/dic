punctuality: /ˌpʌŋktʃuˈæləti/ | n. | 准时；守时 | The quality or state of being punctual; promptness.  |  例句：Her punctuality is commendable.  她的准时值得称赞。

例句：Punctuality is essential for this job.  这项工作需要准时。


近义词：promptness, timeliness, precision


反义词：tardiness, lateness, unpunctuality


词源:  From Middle French *ponctualité, from Latin punctualis "punctual," from punctum "point," from pungere "to prick, sting."  The sense development is from "a point in time" to "being on time."
