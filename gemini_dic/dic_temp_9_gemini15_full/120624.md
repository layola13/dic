mis-citation:/ˌmɪsˌsaɪˈteɪʃən/| n. | 误引，引用错误 | An inaccurate or incorrect quotation of a source; a misrepresentation of someone else's words or ideas.  It can involve altering the meaning of the original text, omitting crucial parts, or incorrectly attributing the quote. | Example:  The article contained a blatant mis-citation of the professor's research, distorting his findings. 这篇文章对教授的研究存在明显的误引，歪曲了他的研究结果。|近义词：misquotation, misattribution, falsification |反义词：accurate citation, correct quotation |名词
