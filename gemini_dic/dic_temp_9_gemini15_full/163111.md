precolleges:/ˌpriːˈkɑːlɪdʒɪz/| n. |大学预科课程；大学预备学校|  A precollege program is a course of study designed to prepare students for college-level work. It often includes academic enrichment, college preparation workshops, and sometimes even college credit.  | Example: Many high schools offer precollege programs to help their students transition smoothly into higher education.  许多高中提供大学预科课程来帮助学生顺利过渡到高等教育。|近义词：preparatory courses, college prep courses |反义词：postgraduate studies
