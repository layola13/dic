rotisseries:/rəˈtɪsəriz/| n.|旋转烤肉架；旋转烤肉店|A rotisserie is a machine for roasting meat on a spit. It typically involves a rotating spit over a heat source, allowing for even cooking and browning.|例句：The restaurant is famous for its delicious rotisserie chicken. 这家餐馆以其美味的烤鸡而闻名。|近义词：spit, grill, oven|反义词：None.
