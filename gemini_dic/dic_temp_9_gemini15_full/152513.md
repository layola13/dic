payor:/ˈpeɪɔːr/| n. |付款人，支付者 |A payor is a person or entity that makes a payment, especially for goods or services.  It often refers to someone paying for insurance or medical bills. |Example: The insurance company is the payor for the hospital bills. 保险公司是医院账单的付款人。|Example: The patient is the payor if they don't have insurance. 如果病人没有保险，病人就是付款人。|近义词：payer, funder |反义词：payee, recipient
