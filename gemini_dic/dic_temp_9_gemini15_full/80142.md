god-daughter: /ˈɡɑːdɔːtə(r)/ | n. | 教女，干女儿 | A goddaughter is a girl or woman who is considered a daughter by a person who is not her biological parent, usually through a religious ceremony like baptism.  This relationship is often established through a godparent's commitment to spiritual guidance and support. | Example:  She is my goddaughter and I've watched her grow up. 她是我的教女，我看着她长大。  I became her godmother at her christening. 我在她受洗时成为了她的教母。 | 近义词:  adopted daughter, spiritual daughter | 反义词:  biological daughter


USAGE->

一般现在时:
She is my goddaughter. = 她是我的教女。
He is a goddaughter to his aunt. = 他是他姑姑的教子。


一般过去时:
She became my goddaughter last year. = 她去年成为我的教女。
He was named goddaughter by his uncle. = 他被他的叔叔认作教子。


现在完成时:
I have been her godmother for ten years. = 我当了十年她的教母。
He has been her goddaughter since birth. = 他从出生起就是她的教子。


过去完成时:
I had already been her godmother before she turned five. = 在她五岁之前，我已经当了她的教母。
He had been named goddaughter by his godfather long ago. = 很久以前，他就已经由他的教父认作教子了。


将来时:
I will remain her godmother throughout her life. = 我将终生担任她的教母。
He will always be his aunt's goddaughter. = 他将永远是他姑姑的教子。


虚拟语气:
If I had a daughter, I would want to be a godmother to her. = 如果我有女儿，我希望成为她的教母。
If I were her godmother, I would support her. = 如果我是她的教母，我会支持她。


被动语态:
She was chosen as my goddaughter. = 她被选为我的教女。
He was named goddaughter at the baptism. = 他在洗礼仪式上被认作教子。


疑问句:
Is she your goddaughter? = 她是你的教女吗？
Was he named goddaughter at his baptism? = 他在洗礼仪式上被认作教子吗？


否定句:
She is not my goddaughter. = 她不是我的教女。
He was not named goddaughter at the ceremony. = 他在仪式上没有被认作教子。
