harrier:/ˈhærɪər/| n.|猎犬；鹞；一种矮小的灌木|A harrier is a type of hunting dog, typically used for hunting hares.  It can also refer to a bird of prey, or a low-growing shrub.|例句：The hunter used a harrier to track the hare across the fields.  猎人用猎犬追踪田野里的野兔。|近义词：hound, beagle (for the dog); hawk, buzzard (for the bird)|反义词：None specifically applicable.
