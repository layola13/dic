pulverizes: /ˈpʌlvəraɪzɪz/ | vt. | 将…磨成粉；彻底摧毁；使…粉碎 | To reduce to powder or dust; to crush or grind into powder; to destroy completely; to overwhelm. |

例句：

The machine pulverizes the grains into fine flour.  = 这台机器把谷物磨成细面粉。

The earthquake pulverized the city. = 地震彻底摧毁了这座城市。


近义词：crushes, grinds, destroys, annihilates, obliterates

反义词：builds, constructs, creates, preserves
