enjoyments:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪmənts/| n.|乐趣；享乐|The pleasures, gratifications, or delights derived from something.  It refers to the state of experiencing pleasure or satisfaction.|例句：His main enjoyments are gardening and reading. 他主要的乐趣是园艺和阅读。|例句：The enjoyments of travel are many and varied. 旅行的乐趣多种多样。|近义词：pleasures, delights, gratifications|反义词：miseries, sufferings, hardships|名词
