nonobservances:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɜːvənsɪz/| n. |不遵守；未遵守；忽略 |The failure to comply with rules, laws, or agreements; the act of ignoring or neglecting something.  It often refers to a plural instance of not following something. |Example:  The repeated nonobservances of safety regulations led to a serious accident.  多次违反安全规章导致了严重的事故。  The nonobservances of the contract terms resulted in legal action. 合同条款的屡次违反导致了法律诉讼。 |近义词：violations, breaches, infringements, disregard, neglect |反义词：observances, compliance, adherence
