nursling:/ˈnɜːrslɪŋ/| n. |婴儿，乳儿|A very young child or baby, especially one who is still being breastfed or bottle-fed.  The word evokes a sense of vulnerability and dependence.|The nursling cried weakly for its mother.  这个婴儿虚弱地哭着找妈妈。|  infant, baby, babe|adult|名词
