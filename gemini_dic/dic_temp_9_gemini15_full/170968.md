pseudopoetical:/ˌsuːdəʊpəˈɛtɪkəl/ | adj. | 似诗的；伪诗的 | Seemingly or affectedly poetical; resembling poetry but lacking genuine poetic qualities.  It often suggests an artificial or insincere attempt to imitate poetic style. | The poem, while technically proficient, felt pseudopoetical, lacking emotional depth. = 这首诗虽然技巧娴熟，但却感觉很做作，缺乏情感深度。 | 近义词：affected, artificial, bombastic, flowery | 反义词：genuine, authentic, sincere, heartfelt
