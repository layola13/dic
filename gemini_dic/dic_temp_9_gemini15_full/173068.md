quarterlies:/ˈkwɔːrtəliz/| n.|季刊；季度报告|Publications or reports issued four times a year.  They can refer to financial reports, magazines, or journals. |Example: The company publishes its financial quarterlies to keep shareholders informed. 公司发行季度报告以保持股东知情。|Example: I subscribe to several gardening quarterlies. 我订阅了几本园艺季刊。|近义词：periodicals, publications|反义词：annuals (yearly publications)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company releases its quarterlies every three months. = 公司每三个月发布一次季刊。
We read several financial quarterlies to track market trends. = 我们阅读几份财务季刊来追踪市场趋势。

一般过去时:
Last year's quarterlies showed strong profits. = 去年季刊显示利润强劲。
The editor reviewed the submissions for the next quarterly. = 编辑审查了下一期季刊的投稿。

现在完成时:
They have already published this year's first two quarterlies. = 他们已经出版了今年的前两期季刊。
We have been reading the same quarterly for five years. = 我们五年来一直在阅读同一本季刊。

过去完成时:
Before the meeting, they had reviewed the previous quarterlies. = 会议之前，他们已经审查了之前的季刊。
By the end of the year, they had published all four quarterlies. = 到年底，他们已经出版了所有四期季刊。

将来时:
The next quarterly will be released in June. = 下一期季刊将于六月发行。
We will subscribe to a new quarterly next year. = 我们明年将订阅一本新的季刊。

虚拟语气:
If the company released its quarterlies on time, investors would be happier. = 如果公司按时发布季刊，投资者会更高兴。
I wish the quarterly included more detailed analysis. = 我希望季刊包含更详细的分析。

被动语态:
The quarterlies are read by many industry experts. = 许多行业专家阅读这些季刊。
The financial quarterlies are compiled by a dedicated team. = 财务季刊由一个专门的团队编制。

疑问句:
When are the next quarterlies coming out? = 下一期季刊什么时候出版？

否定句:
