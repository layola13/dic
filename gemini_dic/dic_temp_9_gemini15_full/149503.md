paediatrics:/ˌpiːdiˈætrɪks/|/n.|儿科学|The branch of medicine dealing with the development, care, and diseases of children.  It encompasses a wide range of topics, from newborn care to adolescent health.|例句：She specialized in paediatrics after medical school. 她医学院毕业后专攻儿科学。|例句：The paediatrics department is located on the third floor. 儿科位于三楼。|近义词：pediatrics|反义词：geriatrics
