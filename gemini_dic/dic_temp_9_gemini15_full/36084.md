coelenterate:/səˈlɛntərət/| n. |腔肠动物|Coelenterates are invertebrate animals that have a simple body plan, typically with a hollow gut and tentacles. They include jellyfish, corals, sea anemones, and hydroids.| Example:  Jellyfish are a type of coelenterate.|近义词：cnidarian|反义词：无
