emitter:/ɪˈmɪtər/ | n. |发射器，发射体 | A device or substance that emits something, such as light, heat, or particles.  | Example: The LED is a highly efficient light emitter.  这个LED是一个高效的光发射器。 |近义词：radiator, source, dispenser |反义词：receiver, absorber |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun emits light and heat. = 太阳发出光和热。
This device emits a high-frequency signal. = 此设备发出高频信号。

一般过去时:
The volcano emitted a large cloud of ash. = 火山喷发出大量的火山灰。
The speaker emitted a loud groan. = 说话人发出一声痛苦的呻吟。

现在完成时:
The factory has emitted tons of pollutants into the atmosphere. = 工厂已经向大气中排放了数吨污染物。
She has emitted several albums since her debut. = 自她出道以来，她已经发行了多张专辑。

过去完成时:
The star had emitted a powerful burst of energy before it collapsed. = 这颗恒星在坍缩之前释放出强大的能量爆发。
They had emitted a distress signal before the ship sank. = 船沉没之前他们发出过求救信号。

将来时:
The satellite will emit data back to Earth. = 卫星将把数据发射回地球。
The new system will emit less pollution than the old one. = 新系统比旧系统排放的污染物更少。

虚拟语气:
If the device emitted a stronger signal, we would receive it better. = 如果设备发出更强的信号，我们就能更好地接收它。
I wish the machine emitted less noise. = 我希望机器发出更少的噪音。

被动语态:
Light is emitted by the sun. = 光是由太阳发出的。
A high-pitched sound was emitted from the machine. = 机器发出一种高音。

疑问句:
What kind of radiation does this emitter produce? = 这个发射器产生什么类型的辐射？
Does this device emit any harmful substances? = 这个设备会排放任何有害物质吗？

否定句:
The device does not emit any visible light. = 该设备不发出任何可见光。
The plant did not emit any noticeable odor. = 植物没有发出任何明显的异味。
