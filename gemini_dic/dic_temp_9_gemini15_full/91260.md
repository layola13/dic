hydrofoil:/ˈhaɪdrəʊfɔɪl/ | n. | 水翼船 | A boat or ship that is equipped with hydrofoils, which lift the hull out of the water at high speeds, reducing drag and increasing speed.  | Example: The hydrofoil skimmed across the water, leaving a white wake.  水翼船飞速掠过水面，留下白色的尾迹。 | 近义词: hydrofoil boat, fast boat | 反义词:  conventional boat, slow boat
