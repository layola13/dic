dampener:/ˈdæmpənər/| n.|减震器；抑制因素；阻尼器|Something that dampens or reduces the force, intensity, or effect of something.|The rain acted as a dampener on the picnic. 雨水对野餐起到了抑制作用。|近义词：depressant, inhibitor, restraint|反义词：stimulant, enhancer


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The dampener reduces the vibrations.  = 减震器减少了震动。
This policy acts as a dampener on economic growth. = 这项政策抑制了经济增长。
