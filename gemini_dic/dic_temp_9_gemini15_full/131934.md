noncontrabands: /ˌnɒnˈkɒntrəbændz/ | n. |非违禁品，非禁运品 | Goods or items that are not prohibited or restricted by law or regulation.  These are things that are legal to possess, transport, or trade. | Example:  The customs officer checked the luggage for contraband, but everything appeared to be noncontrabands. 海关官员检查了行李中的违禁品，但所有东西似乎都不是违禁品。


近义词：permitted goods, legal goods, allowed items


反义词：contraband, prohibited goods, illegal items


词性：名词
