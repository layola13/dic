proairplane:/proʊˈɛərpleɪn/| n. |专业飞机，专业用途飞机 |A proairplane is an aircraft designed and equipped for a specific professional task, often beyond typical passenger or cargo transport.  It might include specialized equipment for aerial photography, surveying, firefighting, or medical evacuation. | Example: The proairplane flew low to take high-resolution images of the archaeological site. 专业飞机低空飞行，拍摄考古遗址的高分辨率图像。|近义词：specialized aircraft, professional aircraft |反义词：general aviation aircraft, commercial airliner |名词
