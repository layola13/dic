reintegrated:/ˌriːˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪd/ | adj. |重新融合的；重新结合的 |  Having been reintegrated; restored to a whole or former state.  This adjective describes something that has been successfully brought back into a whole or original state, often after a period of separation or exclusion. | 例句：The formerly isolated community has been reintegrated into the wider society.  前些时候与世隔绝的社区已经重新融入更广阔的社会。 | 例句：After years of conflict, the fractured nation is striving for reintegrated unity.  经过多年的冲突，分裂的国家正在努力实现重新统一。 | 近义词：reunited, recombined, restored, amalgamated | 反义词：segregated, separated, isolated, fractured |
