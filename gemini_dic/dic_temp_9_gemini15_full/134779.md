nonmetamorphosis: /ˌnɒnˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəsɪs/ | adj. | 非变态的；无变态的 | Lacking metamorphosis; referring to insects that do not undergo a significant change in form during their development.  This term is primarily used in entomology. | Example:  Many insects exhibit nonmetamorphosis, developing gradually from nymph to adult.  许多昆虫表现出无变态发育，从若虫逐渐发育到成虫。|近义词:ametamorphic, direct development|反义词:metamorphic
