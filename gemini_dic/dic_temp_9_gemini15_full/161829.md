practice:/ˈpræktɪs/ | n./v.|练习，实践；实习；业务；惯例 | The act of doing something repeatedly in order to improve one's skill or ability; the customary or usual way of doing something. |例句：I need more practice playing the piano. 我需要更多练习弹钢琴。  He's in medical practice. 他从事医疗实践。 |近义词：exercise, drill, training, routine |反义词：neglect, idleness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He practices the piano every day. = 他每天练习钢琴。
She practices yoga regularly. = 她定期练习瑜伽。

一般过去时:
I practiced the violin for hours yesterday. = 昨天我练习拉小提琴练了几个小时。
They practiced their lines for the play. = 他们排练了戏剧中的台词。

现在完成时:
I have practiced speaking English for five years. = 我练习说英语已经有五年了。
She has practiced law for over a decade. = 她从事律师工作已超过十年。

过去完成时:
I had practiced my speech many times before the presentation. = 演讲之前我已经练习了很多次。
They had practiced the dance routine for weeks before the performance. = 他们在演出前已经排练了几个星期的舞蹈。

将来时:
I will practice my driving test tomorrow. = 我明天将练习我的驾驶考试。
She will practice her French pronunciation before the trip. = 她会在旅行前练习她的法语发音。

虚拟语气:
If I had practiced more, I would have done better. = 如果我练习更多，我会做得更好。
I wish I had practiced harder for the exam. = 我希望我为考试更加努力练习。

被动语态:
The technique is practiced widely across the industry. = 这种技术在业界被广泛应用。
The song was practiced by the choir many times. = 这首歌被合唱团排练了很多次。

疑问句:
Do you practice any sports? = 你练习任何运动吗？
How often do you practice your English? = 你多久练习一次英语？

否定句:
He doesn't practice enough. = 他练习不够。
They didn't practice for the game. = 他们没有为比赛练习。


PHRASE->
in practice = 实际上
medical practice = 医疗实践
legal practice = 法律实践
practice makes perfect = 熟能生巧
best practice = 最佳实践
