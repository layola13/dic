ornamenting:/ˈɔːrnəmentɪŋ/| v.|装饰，修饰|The act of decorating or embellishing something.  It implies adding decorative elements to enhance the appearance.|例句：She spent hours ornamenting the Christmas tree with colorful baubles. 她花了几个小时用五彩缤纷的饰品装饰圣诞树。


例句：The artist was ornamenting the cake with intricate sugar flowers.艺术家正在用复杂的糖花装饰蛋糕。


近义词：decorating, embellishing, adorning, garnishing|反义词：stripping, simplifying, plain
