essayist:/ˈeseɪɪst/| n. |散文家，随笔作家 |A writer of essays.  Essayists often explore a wide range of topics, expressing their personal perspectives and insights. |Example:  Ralph Waldo Emerson was a renowned essayist of the Transcendentalist movement.  拉尔夫·沃尔多·爱默生是超验主义运动中著名的散文家。|近义词: columnist, writer, author |反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑与“小说家”等相对)|
