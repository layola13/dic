planeload:/ˈpleɪnloʊd/| n.|一飞机的载重量；一飞机的乘客或货物|A planeload refers to the amount of cargo or the number of passengers that a plane can carry on a single flight.  It can also refer to the entire load itself.|例句：A planeload of tourists arrived at the airport. 一飞机的游客到达了机场。

例句：The planeload of humanitarian aid was finally delivered.  那架满载人道主义援助的飞机终于送达了。


近义词：planeful (less common)


反义词：无


词性:名词
