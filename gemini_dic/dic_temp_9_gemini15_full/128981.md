nipped:/nɪpt/| v.|轻咬；夹住；掐断；迅速移动|To bite lightly; to pinch; to cut off quickly; to move quickly.|例句：The dog nipped at my heels. 狗轻咬我的脚跟。 The frost nipped the young plants. 霜冻掐死了幼苗。|近义词：pinch, bite, clip, snap|反义词：nurture, protect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nips the flowers regularly. 他定期修剪花朵。
She often nips at her food. 她经常小口地吃东西。


一般过去时:
The gardener nipped the dead leaves from the rose bushes. 园丁从玫瑰花丛中摘除了枯叶。
He nipped the problem in the bud. 他扼杀了问题于萌芽状态。


现在完成时:
The frost has nipped the buds. 霜冻已经冻坏了花蕾。
She has nipped and tucked her way to a slimmer figure. 她通过节食和锻炼让自己变得苗条。


过去完成时:
They had nipped the wires before the alarm went off. 警报响起前他们已经剪断了电线。
The cold wind had nipped at her cheeks. 寒风已经吹红了她的脸颊。


将来时:
I will nip down to the shops. 我很快要去商店一趟。
The gardener will nip the weeds before they spread. 园丁会在杂草蔓延之前把它们拔掉。


虚拟语气:
If I had nipped the problem in the bud, it wouldn't have escalated. 如果我一开始就解决了这个问题，它就不会恶化了。
I wish I had nipped that rumour in the bud. 我真希望我当初扼杀了那个谣言。


被动语态:
The roses were nipped by the frost. 玫瑰花被霜冻损坏了。
The problem was nipped in the bud. 问题被扼杀在了萌芽状态。


疑问句:
Did he nip the buds? 他掐了花蕾吗？

否定句:
The dog didn't nip the child. 狗没有咬那个孩子。
She didn't nip the flowers. 她没有修剪花朵。
