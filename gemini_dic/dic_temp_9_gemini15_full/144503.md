outtongues: /ˌaʊtˈtʌŋz/ | v. |  滔滔不绝地说；夸夸其谈 | To speak volubly or garrulously; to talk excessively or boastfully. | 例句：He outtongued his opponent in the debate.  他辩论中滔滔不绝，压倒了对手。 | 例句：She outtongued everyone at the party with her endless stories.  她在派对上滔滔不绝地讲述着无穷无尽的故事，盖过了所有人的声音。| 近义词：babble, chatter, gabble, prattle | 反义词：be silent, be quiet
