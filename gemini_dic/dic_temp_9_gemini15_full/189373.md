sagging:/ˈsæɡɪŋ/ | adj. |下垂的，松弛的，低迷的 |  Describes something that is hanging or bending downwards because it is weak or heavy.  It can also refer to a decline or weakening in something abstract, such as the economy. |例句：The roof is sagging dangerously. 屋顶危险地塌陷了。

The sales figures show a sagging market. 销售数据显示市场低迷。|近义词：drooping, slumping, weakening |反义词：firm, taut, strong


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The old tent is sagging. = 旧帐篷下垂了。
The branches are sagging under the weight of the snow. = 树枝在雪的重量下下垂了。


一般过去时:
The bridge sagged under the heavy load. = 桥梁在重压下下沉了。
The economy sagged after the crisis. = 危机过后经济低迷。


现在完成时:
The roof has been sagging for months. = 屋顶已经下垂了好几个月了。
The morale of the team has sagged since their last loss. = 自从上次输球后，球队的士气低落了。


过去完成时:
The old building had sagged considerably before it collapsed. = 那座旧建筑在倒塌前已经严重下沉了。
The stock market had sagged significantly before the government intervention. = 在政府干预之前，股市已经大幅下跌。


将来时:
The floor will sag if we put too much weight on it. = 如果我们放上太重的物体，地板就会下陷。
The company's profits are predicted to sag next quarter. = 预计公司下一季度的利润将会下降。


虚拟语气:
If the supports were stronger, the roof wouldn't be sagging. = 如果支撑更结实，屋顶就不会下垂了。
If the economy hadn't sagged, we wouldn't be in this situation. = 如果经济没有低迷，我们也不会处于这种境地。


被动语态:
The bridge was sagged by the weight of the trucks. = 桥梁被卡车的重量压垮了。
The market was sagged by the negative news. = 市场受到负面消息的影响而低迷。


疑问句:
Is the floor sagging? = 地板下垂了吗？
Are the sales figures sagging this month? = 这个月的销售数据是否低迷？


否定句:
The new building isn't sagging. = 新建筑没有下垂。
The economy isn't sagging at the moment. = 目前经济没有低迷。
