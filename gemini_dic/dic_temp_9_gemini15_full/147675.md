overpower: /ˌoʊvərˈpaʊər/ | vt. | 压倒；制服；战胜；使…相形见绌 | to defeat or overcome (someone or something) by superior force or strength; to be too strong or intense for (someone or something) | 例句：The army quickly overpowered the rebels.  军队迅速制服了叛军。  The smell of garlic overpowered all other scents in the kitchen. 大蒜的气味盖过了厨房里所有其他的气味。 | 近义词：overwhelm, subdue, conquer, dominate | 反义词：be overpowered by, succumb to


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The strong wind overpowers the small boat. = 强风压倒了小船。
His charisma overpowers his lack of experience. = 他的魅力弥补了他缺乏经验的不足。

一般过去时:
The police overpowered the suspect. = 警方制服了嫌疑犯。
The beauty of the sunset overpowered her. = 夕阳的美丽让她心醉。

现在完成时:
The storm has overpowered the coastal defenses. = 暴风雨已经摧毁了沿海防御工事。
Her grief has overpowered her ability to function normally. = 她的悲伤让她无法正常生活。

过去完成时:
The invaders had overpowered the local militia before reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达之前，入侵者已经征服了当地民兵。
The evidence had overpowered his initial skepticism. = 证据已经打消了他最初的怀疑。

将来时:
The new technology will likely overpower existing systems. = 新技术很可能会取代现有的系统。
They will overpower their opponents with superior strategy. = 他们将凭借更胜一筹的策略战胜对手。

虚拟语气:
If only I could overpower my fear. = 但愿我能战胜我的恐惧。
If the rebels had overpowered the guards, they might have escaped. = 如果叛军制服了守卫，他们也许就能逃脱了。

被动语态:
The small village was overpowered by the invading army. = 小村庄被入侵的军队攻占了。
His arguments were overpowered by the sheer force of her evidence. = 他的论点被她确凿的证据所驳倒。

疑问句:
Can we overpower the enemy's defenses? = 我们能攻破敌人的防线吗？
Will the new software overpower the old version? = 新软件会取代旧版本吗？

否定句:
The rebels couldn't overpower the government forces. = 叛军无法战胜政府军。
He couldn't overpower his natural shyness. = 他无法克服他天生的害羞。
