/ˈskɑːpər/| v.| 匆忙逃走；飞奔；掠夺 | to run away quickly; to flee hurriedly; to steal or snatch something quickly |  He scampered away when he saw the police. 他看到警察就赶紧跑开了。  | The children scampered across the playground. 孩子们在操场上飞奔。|  flee, dash, bolt, scurry |  walk, stroll, saunter | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scampers away when he's scared. = 他害怕时就跑开了。
Birds scamper across the field. = 鸟儿飞快地穿过田野。

一般过去时:
The thief scampered off with the jewels. = 小偷带着珠宝逃跑了。
The dog scampered after the ball. = 狗追着球跑。

现在完成时:
He has scampered away before I could catch him. = 我还没抓住他就跑掉了。
The children have scampered up the hill. = 孩子们已经跑上山了。

过去完成时:
The rabbit had scampered into its burrow before the fox arrived. = 在狐狸到达之前，兔子已经窜进了洞穴。
The birds had scampered away before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，鸟儿们已经飞走了。

将来时:
He will scamper away if he sees me. = 如果他看到我，他会跑掉的。
The mice will scamper when the cat appears. = 猫出现时，老鼠会逃跑。

虚拟语气:
If he were a cat, he would scamper up the tree. = 如果他是猫，他会爬上树。
I wish I could scamper away from my problems. = 我真希望我能逃离我的困境。

被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice)
The jewels were scampered away. 珠宝被偷走了 (This sentence is awkward and not a natural way to use the word)


疑问句:
Did he scamper away? = 他跑掉了么？
Will the children scamper down the hill? = 孩子们会跑下山吗？

否定句:
He didn't scamper away; he walked calmly. = 他没有跑掉，他平静地走开了。
The thief didn't scamper away; he was caught. = 小偷没有逃走，他被抓住了。


PHRASE->
scamper away = 逃跑
scamper off = 迅速离开
scamper about = 到处跑
scamper up = 跑上去
scamper down = 跑下来
