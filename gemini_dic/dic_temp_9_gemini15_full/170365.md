provisioning:/prəˈvɪʒənɪŋ/ | n. v. |供应；预备；装备 | The act of supplying or equipping someone or something with the necessary resources.  It can refer to the process of getting something ready, or the things that are provided. |例句：The provisioning of the army with food and ammunition was crucial for the campaign's success.  军队得到食物和弹药的供应对战役的成功至关重要。

The company's provisioning process includes ordering, receiving, and stocking inventory. 这家公司的供应流程包括订购、收货和库存管理。|近义词：supplying, equipping, supplying, stocking |反义词：deprivation, lacking
