The word "remark" does not exist.  There is a word "remark" . I will analyze that word instead.


remark:/rɪˈmɑːrk/ | vt. n. | 评论；谈论；注意到 | To express a comment or opinion; to notice something. |例句：He remarked on the beauty of the scenery. 他评论了风景的美丽。  He made a remark about her dress. 他对她穿的衣服发表了评论。|近义词：comment, observation, notice |反义词：silence, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He remarks on the news every day. = 他每天都评论新闻。
She remarks that the food is delicious. = 她评论说食物很美味。


一般过去时:
He remarked on the beauty of the sunset. = 他评论了日落的美丽。
She remarked that she was tired. = 她评论说她累了。


现在完成时:
He has remarked on the importance of education. = 他已经评论了教育的重要性。
She has remarked that she's feeling better. = 她已经评论说她感觉好多了。


过去完成时:
He had remarked that he would be late. = 他之前已经说过他会迟到。
She had remarked on the strange weather. = 她之前已经评论了奇怪的天气。


将来时:
He will remark on the results of the experiment. = 他将评论实验的结果。
She will remark on the progress of the project. = 她将评论项目的进展。


虚拟语气:
If he remarked on the problem, it would be helpful. = 如果他评论这个问题，那将会有帮助。
I wish she had remarked on the mistake sooner. = 我希望她早点指出那个错误。


被动语态:
The incident was remarked upon by many people. = 许多人都评论了这一事件。
His kindness was remarked upon by his colleagues. = 他的同事们都称赞了他的善良。


疑问句:
Did he remark anything interesting? = 他有没有评论什么有趣的事情？
What did she remark about the meeting? = 她对会议有什么评论？


否定句:
He didn't remark on the incident. = 他没有评论那件事。
She didn't remark anything about her plans. = 她没有评论她的计划。


PHRASE->
make a remark = 发表评论
remarkable = 显著的；非凡的
pass remark = 顺便说说
without remark = 不加评论
remarkably = 非常；显著地

词源:
From Old French *remarquer, from re- + marquer "to mark".  The modern sense developed from the earlier meaning of "to notice, observe".
