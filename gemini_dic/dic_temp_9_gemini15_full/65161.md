ethnics:/ˈeθɪks/| n. |伦理学；道德规范|The study of moral principles and values; a set of moral principles governing a person's behavior or the conducting of an activity.|例句：The ethics of genetic engineering are complex and controversial. 基因工程的伦理问题复杂且有争议。|例句：Business ethics are important for maintaining trust with customers. 商业道德对于保持与客户的信任至关重要。|近义词：morals, principles, values |反义词：immorality, unethical behavior
