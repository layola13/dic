offering: /ˈɔːfərɪŋ/ | n. v. | 提供；祭品；奉献 |

英文解析:  As a noun, "offering" refers to something that is offered, given, or presented, often as a gift, sacrifice, or contribution. As a verb, "offering" means to present or give something to someone.

例句 (Noun):

The company's latest offering is a new line of smartphones.  公司的最新产品是一系列新型智能手机。

His offering to the gods was a simple bowl of fruit. 他献给神明的祭品是一个简单的水果碗。


例句 (Verb):

They are offering a 10% discount on all purchases this week.  他们本周为所有购买商品提供 10% 的折扣。

She offered him a cup of tea. 她给他端了一杯茶。


近义词 (Noun):  presentation, contribution, donation, sacrifice, proposal, bid

近义词 (Verb):  providing, giving, presenting, suggesting, proposing

反义词 (Noun):  demand, request, taking

反义词 (Verb):  receiving, demanding, taking


词性: Noun, Verb


USAGE->

一般现在时:

They are offering free samples today. 他们今天提供免费样品。
The shop offers a wide variety of goods. 这家商店提供各种各样的商品。


一般过去时:

She offered me a ride home. 她主动提出送我回家。
They offered a prayer before the meal. 他们用餐前做祈祷。


现在完成时:

The company has offered several new products this year.  该公司今年已经推出了几款新产品。
He has offered his services to the charity. 他已经向慈善机构提供服务。


过去完成时:

They had offered to help before we even asked. 我们还没开口，他们就已经主动提出帮忙了。
She had offered her resignation before the meeting. 会议之前，她已经递交了辞呈。


将来时:

They will be offering a special promotion next month. 下个月他们将推出一个特别的促销活动。
We will offer our support to the project. 我们将支持这个项目。


虚拟语气:

If they offered me the job, I would accept. 如果他们提供这份工作给我，我会接受。
I wish they offered better pay. 我希望他们提供更好的薪水。


被动语态:

The job was offered to him.  这份工作提供给了他。
Free drinks were offered to all attendees. 所有与会者都获得了免费饮料。


疑问句:

Are they offering any discounts? 他们提供任何折扣吗？
What are you offering for sale? 你在卖什么？


否定句:

They are not offering any refunds. 他们不提供任何退款。
He didn't offer any explanation. 他没有给出任何解释。
