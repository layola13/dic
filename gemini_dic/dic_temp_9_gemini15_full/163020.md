precivilizations: /ˌpriːˌsɪvəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 前文明时期；前文明社会 | Societies or cultures that existed before the development of writing and complex social structures.  The study of precivilizations often relies on archaeological evidence such as tools, settlements, and burial sites. | Example:  The discovery of sophisticated tools suggests a surprisingly advanced level of technology in these precivilizations.  这些前文明社会中发现的复杂工具表明，他们的科技水平出乎意料地先进。


近义词: proto-civilizations, early cultures, prehistoric societies

反义词: civilizations
