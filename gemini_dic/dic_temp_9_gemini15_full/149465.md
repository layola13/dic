paddling:/ˈpæd(ə)lɪŋ/| n. |划桨；涉水；戏水|The act of propelling a boat or other watercraft through the water using paddles.  It can also refer to the act of moving through shallow water by pushing oneself along with the hands and feet.|例句：He enjoyed the quiet paddling on the lake. 他喜欢在湖上安静地划船。|例句：The children were paddling in the shallows. 孩子们在浅滩戏水。|近义词：rowing, swimming, wading|反义词：sailing(in the context of boat propulsion)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am paddling across the lake. = 我正在划船过湖。
He paddles every morning before work. = 他每天早上上班前都划船。

一般过去时:
We paddled down the river yesterday. = 我们昨天顺流而下划船。
She paddled to the island. = 她划船去了岛上。

现在完成时:
I have paddled this river many times. = 我已经划过这条河很多次了。
They have been paddling for hours. = 他们已经划船好几个小时了。

过去完成时:
We had paddled for miles before we stopped for lunch. = 我们在停下来吃午饭之前已经划了几英里了。
They had never paddled a kayak before. = 他们以前从未划过皮划艇。

将来时:
I will paddle the canoe tomorrow. = 我明天将划独木舟。
We will be paddling across the ocean next year. = 我们明年将划船横渡海洋。

虚拟语气:
If I had a kayak, I would be paddling right now. = 如果我有一艘皮划艇，我现在就会在划船了。
I wish I were paddling on a tropical beach. = 我希望我现在正在热带海滩上划船。

被动语态:
The canoe was paddled skillfully by the expert. = 这艘独木舟由专家熟练地划动。
The river is being paddled by many tourists. = 这条河正被许多游客划船。

疑问句:
Are you paddling upstream or downstream? = 你是逆流而上还是顺流而下划船？
Will you be paddling alone? = 你会独自划船吗？

否定句:
I didn't paddle today because of the bad weather. = 因为天气不好，我今天没有划船。
They won't be paddling this weekend. = 他们这个周末不会划船。


PHRASE->
paddle a canoe = 划独木舟
go paddling = 去划船
paddle upstream = 逆流而上划船
paddle downstream = 顺流而下划船
stand-up paddleboarding (SUP) = 桨板运动


词源：
源自古英语 *paddan，意为“拍打，拍水”。  这与现代英语单词“paddle”（桨）同源。  "-ing"是现在分词后缀，表示动作或状态。
