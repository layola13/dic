containant:/kənˈteɪnənt/ | n. |容器，盛装物 | A container is something that holds or contains something else.  It can be a box, a bottle, a bag, or any other receptacle. | Example: The laboratory technician carefully placed the sample in a sterile contaminant.  实验室技术员小心地将样品放入无菌容器中。  |近义词：receptacle, vessel, container | 反义词：content (as a noun, referring to the thing contained)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The jar contains jam. = 这个罐子装着果酱。
This box contains my old photographs. = 这个盒子装着我的旧照片。

一般过去时:
The bottle contained wine. = 这个瓶子装的是酒。
The bag contained all of my belongings. = 这个包装着我所有的东西。

现在完成时:
The box has contained many valuable items over the years. = 多年来，这个盒子装过许多贵重物品。
She has contained her anger remarkably well. = 她一直很好地控制着自己的怒气。（此处引申义）


过去完成时:
The vase had contained flowers before it broke. = 花瓶碎之前装着花。
They had contained their emotions during the difficult conversation. = 在那次艰难的谈话中，他们一直克制着情绪。（此处引申义）

将来时:
The suitcase will contain all my clothes for the trip. = 行李箱将装下我旅行所需的所有衣物。
The new stadium will contain over 50,000 spectators. = 新体育场将容纳超过5万名观众。（此处引申义）

虚拟语气:
If the box contained jewels, it would be very valuable. = 如果盒子装着珠宝，它将非常值钱。
I wish the bottle contained more water. = 我希望瓶子里有更多的水。

被动语态:
The sample was contained in a special container. = 样本被装在一个特殊的容器中。
The emotions were contained with difficulty. = 情绪很难控制。（此处引申义）

疑问句:
What does this bag contain? = 这个包里装的是什么？
Does the box contain anything valuable? = 这个盒子装着什么贵重的东西吗？

否定句:
The box does not contain anything. = 盒子里什么也没有。
The bottle did not contain any liquid. = 瓶子里没有任何液体。
