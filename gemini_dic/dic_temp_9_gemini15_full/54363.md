dishonours:/dɪsˈɒnərz/ | vt. | 玷污；使蒙羞；丢脸 | To bring shame or disgrace upon; to violate or fail to fulfill an obligation or promise. | 例句：His actions dishonoured his family.  他的行为玷污了他的家族。 | 例句：He dishonoured his promise to help. 他违背了他帮忙的承诺。| 近义词：disgrace, shame, disgrace, insult | 反义词：honour, glorify
