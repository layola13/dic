purposes:/ˈpɜːrpəsɪz/|/ˈpɜːrpəsiz/| n. |目的；意图；用途 |The main purpose of the meeting was to discuss the budget.  会议的主要目的是讨论预算。  He achieved his purpose despite many obstacles. 他克服重重障碍，达到了目的。| aims, goals, intentions | meaningless, pointless | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are the purposes of this project? = 这个项目的目的是什么？
What purposes does this machine serve? = 这台机器有什么用途？


一般过去时:
What were the purposes of their visit? = 他们访问的目的何在？
What purpose did the meeting serve? = 那次会议有什么作用？


现在完成时:
What purposes have been achieved so far? = 到目前为止，已经达到了哪些目的？
What purposes has this research served? = 这项研究起到了什么作用？


过去完成时:
What purposes had been established before the project began? = 在项目开始之前，已经确定了哪些目标？
What purposes had they hoped to achieve? = 他们原本希望达到什么目的？


将来时:
What will the purposes of the new law be? = 新法律的目的将会是什么？
What purposes will this technology serve in the future? = 未来这项技术将有什么用途？


虚拟语气:
If I knew his purposes, I would act differently. = 如果我知道他的目的，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I understood their purposes better. = 我希望我更了解他们的目的。


被动语态:
The purposes of the investigation were not clearly stated. = 调查的目的并没有明确说明。
The purposes of the reform were misunderstood. = 改革的目的被误解了。


疑问句:
What are your purposes in coming here? = 你来这里的目的是什么？
What purposes do these actions serve? = 这些行动有什么目的？


否定句:
His actions had no clear purpose. = 他的行动没有明确的目的。
The meeting served no useful purpose. = 会议毫无用处。


PHRASE->
for the purpose of = 为了…的目的
on purpose = 故意地
serve the purpose = 达到目的
to this purpose = 为此目的
to no purpose = 白费力气
