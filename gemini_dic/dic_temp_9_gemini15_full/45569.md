curlier:/ˈkɜːrliər/| adj.|更卷曲的，更弯曲的|More curly; having tighter or more pronounced curls.  It describes something that is curlier than something else or has become curlier.|例句：Her hair is curlier than it used to be. = 她的头发比以前更卷了。
The wire became curlier after being heated. = 电线加热后变得更卷曲了。|近义词：wavier, crisper|反义词：straighter, smoother
