grove:/ɡroʊv/| n. |小树林，树丛|A grove is a small group of trees growing closely together.  It often implies a pleasant and secluded area.  |例句：We took a walk through the orange grove. 我们在橘子林里散步。|例句：A shady grove offered respite from the midday sun.  阴凉的小树林为正午的阳光提供了喘息的机会。|近义词：wood, thicket, copse |反义词：forest (in terms of size)
