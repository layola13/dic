resubmitting:/ˌriːsəbˈmɪtɪŋ/| v.|重新提交，再次提交 |The act of submitting something again, typically after it has been rejected or requires further review.|例句：I'm resubmitting my application because I found a mistake.  我正在重新提交我的申请，因为我发现了一个错误。


例句：The professor asked him to resubmit his paper with the corrections. 教授让他修改后重新提交论文。


近义词：re-submitting, submitting again, reapplying


反义词：withdrawing, rejecting


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They resubmit their work regularly. = 他们定期重新提交他们的工作。
He resubmits the documents every week. = 他每周重新提交文件。


一般过去时:
She resubmitted the proposal after the revisions. = 修改后，她重新提交了提案。
They resubmitted their application last night. = 他们昨晚重新提交了申请。


现在完成时:
I have resubmitted my application three times. = 我已经重新提交了三次申请。
She has resubmitted the report with the updated data. = 她已经用更新的数据重新提交了报告。


过去完成时:
He had resubmitted the assignment before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经重新提交了作业。
They had resubmitted their proposal twice before it was accepted. = 他们的提案在被接受之前已经重新提交了两次。


将来时:
I will resubmit my essay after I make the corrections. = 我修改后再重新提交我的论文。
We will resubmit the project proposal next week. = 我们下周将重新提交项目提案。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would resubmit my application. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新提交我的申请。
If she had known the rules, she would have resubmitted her work correctly. = 如果她知道规则，她就会正确地重新提交她的工作。


被动语态:
The application was resubmitted yesterday. = 申请昨天被重新提交了。
The report is being resubmitted for review. = 报告正在被重新提交以供审核。


疑问句:
Will you resubmit the form? = 你会重新提交表格吗？
Has he resubmitted the document yet? = 他已经重新提交文件了吗？


否定句:
I did not resubmit my assignment on time. = 我没有按时重新提交我的作业。
She will not resubmit the application without making changes. = 她不会在不做修改的情况下重新提交申请。


PHRASE->
resubmit a proposal = 重新提交提案
resubmit an application = 重新提交申请
resubmit a document = 重新提交文件
resubmit a report = 重新提交报告
resubmit for review = 重新提交审核
