purpleheart:/ˈpɜːrpəlˌhɑːrt/|/ˈpɜːrplˌhɑːrt/| n. |紫心勋章；紫心木|A Purple Heart is a military decoration awarded by the United States Armed Forces to those who have been wounded or killed in action.  Purpleheart wood is a type of wood known for its striking purple color.|例句：He received a Purple Heart for his bravery in combat. 他因在战斗中的英勇而获得紫心勋章。

The wood is incredibly strong and beautiful and is used in high-end furniture. 这木材极其坚固漂亮，被用于高档家具。|近义词：military decoration (for Purple Heart medal), amaranth (for Purpleheart wood)|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to its opposites in terms of the medal (e.g., cowardice) or the wood (e.g., weak wood)|名词
