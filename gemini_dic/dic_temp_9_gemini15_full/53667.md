discing:/ˈdɪskɪŋ/| n. vt.|圆盘耕作；用圆盘犁耕作|The process of cultivating land using a disc plow or harrow.  It's used to prepare the soil for planting. |例句：The farmer is discing the field to prepare it for planting.  农民正在用圆盘犁耕地准备播种。

例句：We need to disc the soil before sowing the seeds. 我们需要在播种前耕作土壤。|近义词：plowing, harrowing|反义词：none specifically, but  "leaving undisturbed" could be considered an antonym.|动词
