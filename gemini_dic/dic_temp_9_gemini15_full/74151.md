founding:/ˈfaʊndɪŋ/ | n. | 建立；创立；创办 | The act of establishing or starting something, typically an institution, organization, or settlement.  | Example: The founding of the United States marked a new era in world history. 美国建国标志着世界历史新纪元。 |近义词：establishment, foundation, institution | 反义词：destruction, demolition, dissolution |  名词
