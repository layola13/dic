la-di-da:/ˌlɑːdiˈdɑː/| adj.| 矫揉造作的，势利的 |  Affectedly elegant or refined; pretentious.  The word imitates a style of speech associated with affectation and upper-class pretension. |例句：She spoke in a la-di-da voice, full of affected elegance. 她用一种矫揉造作的声音说话，充满了做作的优雅。

例句：He's so la-di-da, he thinks he's better than everyone else. 他非常势利，认为自己比其他人优越。


近义词：posh, pretentious, affected, snobbish,  uppity

反义词：unpretentious, humble, down-to-earth, unassuming


词性:形容词
