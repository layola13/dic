ovisac: /ˈoʊvɪsæk/ | n. | 卵囊；卵泡 |  A sac or follicle containing an ovum (egg).  It is a structure found in the ovaries of female animals, including humans, where eggs develop and mature before ovulation. | Example:  The ovisac ruptured, releasing the mature egg. 卵囊破裂，释放出成熟的卵子。 | 近义词: ovarian follicle, Graafian follicle | 反义词:  (None specifically antonymous)
