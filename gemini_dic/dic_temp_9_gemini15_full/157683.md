pixel's:/ˈpɪksəlz/| n. |像素的（pixel 的所有格）|The possessive form of "pixel," referring to something belonging to or associated with a pixel.  It's often used in the context of pixel data, pixel manipulation, or pixel art.|例句：The image's pixel's color depth is 24-bit. 这张图片的像素颜色深度为24位。|近义词：pixel-related,  picture element's|反义词:N/A|名词


USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and does not conjugate through tenses like verbs.  It describes ownership or association.  Therefore, grammatical tenses are not applicable.
