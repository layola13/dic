hobos:/ˈhoʊboʊz/ | n. | 流浪汉，漂泊者 |  A hobo is a homeless and usually unemployed person who travels from place to place, often by hopping freight trains.  They are typically characterized by their itinerant lifestyle and often possess a distinctive appearance and culture. | Example: Many hobos rode the rails during the Great Depression. 许多流浪汉在大萧条时期搭乘火车旅行。  The film depicted the lives of hobos during the Dust Bowl era.  这部电影描绘了沙尘暴时期流浪汉的生活。| 近义词：tramp, vagrant, bum, drifter | 反义词：homeowner, resident
