pined:/paɪnd/| v.|渴望，苦思；憔悴；枯萎|To yearn intensely; to suffer from longing or grief; to become withered or dry.|例句：She pined for her lost love. 她渴望她失去的爱人。She pined away after her husband died. 她丈夫死后憔悴不堪。|近义词：yearned, longed, ached, withered|反义词：flourished, thrived


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pines for his homeland. 他思念他的家乡。
She pines for a better life. 她渴望过更好的生活。

一般过去时:
They pined for their families during the war. 战争期间他们思念家人。
He pined away after losing his job. 他失业后日渐消瘦。

现在完成时:
I have pined for this moment for years. 我多年来一直渴望这一刻。
She has pined for him since he left. 自从他离开后，她就一直思念他。

过去完成时:
He had pined for her for months before they finally met. 在他们最终见面之前，他已经思念她好几个月了。
They had pined for news from the front lines. 他们一直渴望得到前线的新闻。

将来时:
I will pine for this place when I am gone.  我离开后会想念这个地方。
She will pine for the sun when she is in the north. 当她在北方时，她会想念阳光。

虚拟语气:
If I were there, I wouldn't pine for home. 如果我在那里，我就不会想家。
I wish I didn't pine for him so much. 我希望我不那么想念他。


被动语态:
His absence is deeply pined for.  人们深深地想念他的缺席。
The lost paradise is pined for by many.  许多人都在渴望失去的乐园。


疑问句:
Does she still pine for him? 她还思念他吗？
Do you ever pine for your childhood? 你曾经思念过你的童年吗？


否定句:
He didn't pine for his old life. 他并不想念他过去的生活。
She does not pine for the past. 她并不怀念过去。
