campanero:/ˌkæmpəˈnɛroʊ/| n. |钟声鸟，敲钟人|A campanero is a type of bird known for its bell-like song, or it can refer to a person who rings bells.  The word is primarily used in Spanish and Portuguese, and its usage in English is relatively niche, often found in ornithological contexts. |Example: The campanero's song echoed through the rainforest.  钟声鸟的歌声回荡在雨林中。 The campanero in the church tower rang the bells for the service. 教堂塔楼里的敲钟人敲响了礼拜的钟声。|近义词: bellbird (for the bird), bell-ringer (for the person)|反义词: N/A
