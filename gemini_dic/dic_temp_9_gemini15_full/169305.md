promo:/ˈproʊmoʊ/| n. |促销，宣传 |A promo is a short advertisement or publicity piece, often used to promote a product, event, or service.  |Example: The company released a new promo video for its upcoming product launch. 这家公司发布了一段新的宣传视频来宣传即将推出的产品。|近义词：advertisement, commercial, publicity, promotion |反义词：none


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store runs a promo on Tuesdays. = 这家店在星期二进行促销活动。
This company promotes its products through social media. = 这家公司通过社交媒体推广其产品。


一般过去时:
They ran a successful promo last month. = 他们上个月进行了一次成功的促销活动。
The company promoted its new software with a series of ads. = 公司通过一系列广告宣传其新软件。


现在完成时:
We have promoted our services to a wider audience. = 我们已经向更广泛的受众宣传了我们的服务。
They have run several successful promos this year. = 他们今年已经进行了几次成功的促销活动。


过去完成时:
They had already run the promo before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他们已经进行了促销活动。
The company had promoted its new product extensively before the launch. = 公司在产品发布之前已经对新产品进行了广泛的宣传。


将来时:
We will run a promo for our new product next week. = 我们下周将为我们的新产品进行促销活动。
The company will promote its services at the upcoming trade show. = 公司将在即将举行的贸易展览会上宣传其服务。


虚拟语气:
If we had run a better promo, we would have sold more products. = 如果我们进行更好的促销活动，我们将卖出更多产品。
I wish we had promoted our brand more effectively. = 我希望我们能更有效地推广我们的品牌。


被动语态:
The new product was promoted through various channels. = 新产品通过各种渠道进行推广。
The promo was run successfully, resulting in increased sales. = 促销活动成功进行，导致销售额增加。


疑问句:

否定句:
