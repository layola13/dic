melange:/məˈlɑːʒ/| n.|混合物，混杂；混合体|A melange is a mixture of different things, often in a visually striking or interesting way.  It can refer to a mixture of colors, textures, styles, or ideas. The term often implies a heterogeneous and perhaps unexpected combination. |Example: The artist's latest work is a vibrant melange of textures and colors.  这位艺术家的最新作品是纹理和色彩的鲜艳混合。|近义词：mixture, medley,potpourri,composite|反义词：uniformity, homogeneity

词源:
