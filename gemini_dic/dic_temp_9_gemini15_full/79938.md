gnomonics:/ɡnəˈmɒnɪks/| n. |日晷学；晷影测量学 |The study of sundials and their design.  It involves the geometry of shadows cast by a gnomon (the shadow-casting object) to determine time. |Example:  Gnomonics is a fascinating blend of mathematics, astronomy, and art. 日晷学是数学、天文学和艺术的迷人融合。|近义词：sundial design, shadow reckoning |反义词：None  |名词
