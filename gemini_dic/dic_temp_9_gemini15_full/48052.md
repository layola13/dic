declaimed:/dɪˈkleɪmd/| vt.|朗诵；慷慨激昂地说；声明；断言|To speak or deliver (a speech or poem) in a formal or impassioned manner.  To state or pronounce something emphatically or forcefully.|例句：He declaimed the poem with great feeling. 他充满感情地朗诵了这首诗。

例句：The politician declaimed against the new tax laws.  这位政治家慷慨激昂地反对新的税法。


近义词：recite, pronounce, proclaim, assert, announce|反义词：whisper, mutter, murmur|动词
