roughcasts:/ˈrʌfˌkæsts/| n.|粗略的草稿；初步的模型|A roughcast is a preliminary version of something, typically a piece of writing, a plan, or a design, that is not yet refined or polished. It is often characterized by its incompleteness, imperfections, and lack of detail.  |例句：The architect presented a roughcast of the building design to the client. 建筑师向客户展示了建筑设计的草图。
The writer produced a roughcast of the novel before starting the revision process.  作家在开始修改过程之前制作了小说的初稿。|近义词：draft, sketch, outline, preliminary version|反义词：final version, polished version, finished product|名词
