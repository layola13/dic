footplates:/ˈfʊtˌpleɪts/| n.|踏板；脚踏板|A flat surface, often metal, that is used to support a person's feet.  It can be found on various machines, vehicles, or equipment.|例句：The footplates on the train were slippery. 火车上的踏板很滑。

例句：The machine operator keeps his feet firmly on the footplates. 机器操作员双脚稳稳地踩在踏板上。


近义词：footrests, pedals (if used for operation)


反义词：None directly, but depending on context could be words implying lack of support or footing.


词性:名词
