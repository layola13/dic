overcondensation: /ˌoʊvərˌkɑːndɛnˈseɪʃən/ | n. | 过度冷凝 | Excessive condensation; more condensation than is expected or desired.  This often occurs in meteorological contexts, referring to an excessive amount of water vapor condensing to form clouds or precipitation.  It can also apply to other processes involving condensation. |


例句：

The overcondensation of water vapor led to unexpectedly heavy rainfall.  过度冷凝的水汽导致了意外的大雨。

The overcondensation in the pipes caused a blockage. 管道内的过度冷凝导致堵塞。


近义词：Excessive condensation, super-saturation

反义词：Undercondensation (less condensation than expected)


词性：名词
