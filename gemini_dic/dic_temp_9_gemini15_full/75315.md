fronts:/frʌnts/| n.|前线；方面；阵线；外观；前部|The leading edge or most advanced part of an army or other force.  It can also refer to a particular area of activity or interest, or the outward appearance of something.|例句：The enemy fronts were advancing steadily. 敌军前线稳步推进。|例句：The government is tackling the problem on several fronts. 政府正在多方面努力解决这个问题。|近义词：lines, boundaries, appearances, facades|反义词：rears, backs, interiors
