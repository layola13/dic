quasi-unanimous:/ˈkwɑːzi juˈnænɪməs/| adj. |几乎一致的，接近一致的 |Almost unanimous; showing near unanimity.  It suggests a strong majority but not complete agreement.  |例句：The decision, while not perfectly unanimous, was quasi-unanimous, with only a few dissenting voices.  几乎一致的决定，虽然并非完全一致，但也只有少数异议。|近义词：nearly unanimous, virtually unanimous, overwhelmingly supported |反义词：completely divided, unanimously opposed
