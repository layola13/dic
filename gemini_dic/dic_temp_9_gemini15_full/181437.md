rejecter:/rɪˈdʒɛktər/ | n. |拒绝者，排斥者 |A person or thing that rejects something.  Someone who consistently refuses or dismisses something or someone. |The rejecter of the offer felt it wasn't in his best interest.  这个拒绝者认为这个提议不符合他的最大利益。| refuser, denier |accepter, approver |名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The committee rejects many proposals.  = 委员会拒绝了许多提案。
He rejects all forms of assistance. = 他拒绝一切形式的援助。


一般过去时:
She rejected his marriage proposal. = 她拒绝了他的求婚。
The company rejected his application. = 公司拒绝了他的申请。


现在完成时:
They have rejected all our suggestions. = 他们已经拒绝了我们所有的建议。
He has repeatedly rejected my advances. = 他反复拒绝我的示好。


过去完成时:
She had already rejected his offer before he could explain it fully. =  在她能充分解释之前，她就已经拒绝了他的提议了。
The court had rejected similar cases in the past. = 法院过去曾驳回过类似的案件。


将来时:
I think they will reject the plan. = 我认为他们会否决这个计划。
She will probably reject his offer. = 她很可能会拒绝他的提议。


虚拟语气:
If they rejected the offer, we would have to start over. = 如果他们拒绝了这个提议，我们就得重新开始。
I wish he hadn't rejected my help. = 我真希望他没有拒绝我的帮助。


被动语态:
His application was rejected. = 他的申请被拒绝了。

疑问句:

否定句:
