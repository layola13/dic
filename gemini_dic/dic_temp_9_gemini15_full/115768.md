marination:/ˌmæriˈneɪʃən/| n. |腌制；腌渍|The process of soaking food, typically meat or fish, in a seasoned liquid to tenderize and flavor it.  Marinating enhances the taste and texture of the food.|例句：The marination of the chicken took several hours to ensure it was fully flavored. 鸡肉腌制了好几个小时，以确保其充分入味。The long marination period resulted in incredibly tender and flavorful steak. 长时间的腌制使牛排变得非常嫩滑可口。|近义词：pickling, brining|反义词：roasting, grilling
