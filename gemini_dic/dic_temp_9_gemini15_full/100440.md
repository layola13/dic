intertransformable: /ˌɪntərtrænzˈfɔːrməbl/ | adj. | 可相互转换的，可互相转化的 |  Able to be transformed into each other; mutually transformable.  This adjective describes things that can change into one another, often implying a reversible process.  |


例句：

The two forms of energy are intertransformable.  两种能量形式是可相互转换的。

Isomers are intertransformable under certain conditions.  在某些条件下，异构体是可相互转换的。


近义词：interchangeable, mutually convertible, reciprocal


反义词：intransformable, immutable, irreversible


词性：形容词
