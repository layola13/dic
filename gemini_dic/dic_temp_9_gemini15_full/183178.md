repreach:/rɪˈpriːtʃ/| vt.|责备；谴责；非难|To express disapproval or criticism of (someone) for their behaviour or actions.|例句：He was reproached for his carelessness. 他因粗心大意而受到责备。|例句：She reproached herself for not trying harder. 她责备自己没有更加努力。|近义词：rebuke, reprimand, scold, upbraid|反义词：praise, commend, applaud|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reproaches his son for his bad grades. = 他责备儿子成绩不好。
She reproaches her friend for lying. = 她责备她的朋友说谎。

一般过去时:
My teacher reproached me for being late. = 我的老师因为我迟到而责备我。
They reproached him for his lack of effort. = 他们责备他缺乏努力。

现在完成时:
She has reproached him several times for his behavior. = 她已经多次因为他的行为责备他了。
I have reproached myself for making that mistake. = 我一直在责备自己犯了那个错误。

过去完成时:
He had reproached her before she even apologized. = 在她道歉之前，他就已经责备她了。
They had reproached him many times, but he never changed. = 他们已经责备他很多次了，但他从未改变。

将来时:
I will reproach him for his actions tomorrow. = 我明天将要因为他的行为责备他。
She will reproach herself if she doesn't try her best. = 如果她不尽全力，她会责备自己的。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would reproach him for his rudeness. = 如果我是你，我会因为他粗鲁而责备他。
I wish I hadn't reproached her so harshly. = 我真希望我没有那么严厉地责备她。

被动语态:
He was reproached by his boss for his mistakes. = 他因为自己的错误而被老板责备。
She was reproached for her lack of consideration. = 她因为缺乏考虑而受到责备。

疑问句:
Will you reproach him for what he did? = 你会因为他所做的事责备他吗？
Did she reproach you for being late? = 她因为你迟到而责备你了吗？

否定句:
I did not reproach him for his decision. = 我没有因为他的决定责备他。
She will not reproach him, even if he deserves it. = 即使他活该，她也不会责备他。


PHRASE->
reproach oneself for = 为...自责
reproach someone with = 用...来责备某人
reproach somebody for doing something = 因做某事而责备某人
cast reproach on = 谴责；责备
a reproach to = 对...的耻辱
