chewer:/ˈtʃuːər/| n.|咀嚼者，嚼碎者|A person or animal that chews.  Someone or something that chews a lot.|例句：The dog is a vigorous chewer of bones. 这只狗非常喜欢啃骨头。|例句：He's a notorious chewer of fingernails. 他是个出了名的咬指甲的人。|近义词：gnawer, biter|反义词：swallower (less precise antonym)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chews his food thoroughly. = 他细嚼慢咽。
The cow chews its cud. = 牛在反刍。

一般过去时:
She chewed gum all day. = 她整天嚼口香糖。
The baby chewed on a teething ring. = 婴儿咬着安抚奶嘴。

现在完成时:
He has chewed through the entire rope. = 他已经咬断了整条绳子。
The dog has chewed up my shoes. = 狗把我的鞋子咬坏了。

过去完成时:
He had chewed all the food before I arrived. = 我到之前他已经把所有食物都吃掉了。
The rats had chewed through the wires. = 老鼠已经咬断了电线。

将来时:
She will chew her food slowly. = 她将会细嚼慢咽。
The dog will chew on that bone for hours. = 这只狗会啃那根骨头好几个小时。

虚拟语气:
If he chewed his food more slowly, he wouldn't have indigestion. = 如果他吃东西慢点，就不会消化不良了。
I wish I hadn't chewed that gum, it gave me a headache. = 我真希望我没有嚼那块口香糖，它让我头疼。

被动语态:
The rope was chewed through by the rats. = 绳子被老鼠咬断了。
The wood was chewed up by the termites. = 木头被白蚁蛀空了。

疑问句:
Does he chew his food properly? = 他咀嚼食物吗？
Is the dog chewing on something again? = 狗又在啃什么东西了？

否定句:
He doesn't chew his food enough. = 他咀嚼食物不够充分。
The baby didn't chew the toy. = 婴儿没有咬玩具。
