glazing:/ˈɡleɪzɪŋ/ | n.  | 玻璃；釉；上光；(食品)糖衣 | The process of applying a smooth, typically transparent, coating to a surface.  This coating can be glass, ceramic glaze, or a similar substance. It can also refer to the act of fitting windows into a frame.  |例句：The glazing on the windows was cracked.  窗户上的玻璃破裂了。

The cake's rich glazing made it irresistible. 蛋糕上丰富的糖衣使它无法抗拒。


近义词：coating, varnish, icing | 反义词：roughness, dullness
