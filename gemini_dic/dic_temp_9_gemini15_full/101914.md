islamism:/ˈɪzlæmɪzəm/| n. |伊斯兰主义；伊斯兰教政治化|A political ideology based on Islam, advocating for the implementation of Islamic law and principles in all aspects of society.  It encompasses a wide range of views and interpretations, from moderate to extremist.  Some Islamist movements advocate for peaceful reform within existing political systems, while others support more radical approaches, including violence.|例句：The rise of Islamism in some parts of the world has caused significant concern.  伊斯兰主义在世界某些地区的兴起引起了极大的关注。  The government's policies have been criticized for fueling Islamism. 政府的政策因助长伊斯兰主义而受到批评。|近义词：Islamic fundamentalism, Islamic activism|反义词：secularism
