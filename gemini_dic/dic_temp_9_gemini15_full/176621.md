reacclimatized:/ˌriːəˈklaɪmətaɪzd/| v. |重新适应气候；重新适应环境|To become accustomed to a different climate or environment again after a period of adjustment to another.|例句：After spending a week at sea level, the climbers reacclimatized to the high altitude.  在海平面待了一周后，登山者们重新适应了高海拔环境。

例句：The plants reacclimatized quickly after being moved to a more suitable location. 植物搬到更合适的地方后很快就重新适应了环境。


近义词：readapt, readjust, reaccustom|反义词：maladapt,  disorient
