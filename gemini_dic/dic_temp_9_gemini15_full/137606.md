nontriviality:/ˌnɒnˌtrɪviˈæləti/ | n. |非平凡性；重要性；意义重大|The quality or state of being nontrivial;  significance or importance;  the property of not being trivial.  It often implies complexity, depth, or a lack of obvious or simple solutions.|The nontriviality of the problem required a sophisticated approach.  这个问题的非平凡性需要一种复杂的方法。 The nontriviality of his contribution to the field is undeniable. 他对该领域的贡献意义重大，这是不可否认的。|近义词：significance, importance, substance, complexity|反义词：triviality, insignificance, triviality, simplicity|
