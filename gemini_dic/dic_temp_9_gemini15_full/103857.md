jrs:/dʒɑːrz/ |abbr.|  Jr. 先生（用于名字之后）|Junior的缩写，意为“小”，用于区分父子或祖孙同名的情况。|例句：John Smith Jr. 小约翰·史密斯|近义词：junior|反义词：Sr. (Senior)


USAGE->
This abbreviation doesn't have verb conjugations or tenses as it's not a verb.  It's used solely as a suffix to a name.

PHRASE->
There are no phrases directly using "jrs" as it's an abbreviation used only as a suffix to a name.  However, you can have phrases *containing* names that use "Jr.":

John Smith Jr.'s house = 小约翰·史密斯的家
The accomplishments of Henry Ford Jr. = 小亨利·福特的成就
Meeting with Robert Jones Jr. = 与小罗伯特·琼斯会面
We invited both Mr. Davis Sr. and Mr. Davis Jr. = 我们邀请了戴维斯先生父子
The legacy of Martin Luther King Jr. = 马丁·路德·金小马丁的遗产


注意事项:

音标只有一种，因为它是缩写，不涉及发音变化。
