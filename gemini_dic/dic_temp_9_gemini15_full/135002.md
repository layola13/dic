nonnecessity:/ˌnɒnˈnesəsəti/| n. |非必需品，不必要的事物 |Something that is not necessary; something that can be done without.  It refers to things or actions that are not essential or required. |Example: Buying a new car is a nonnecessity; you can continue using your old one. 买一辆新车是非必需的；你可以继续使用你的旧车。|近义词：unnecessary thing, luxury, dispensable item |反义词：necessity, essential, requirement
