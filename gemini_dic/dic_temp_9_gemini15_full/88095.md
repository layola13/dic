hightail:/ˈhaɪteɪl/| v.|急忙逃走，飞快地跑|to leave quickly and hurriedly; to run away swiftly.|例句：He hightailed it out of the room after hearing the news. 他听到消息后就急忙离开了房间。
例句：The thief hightailed it down the alley when he saw the police. 小偷看到警察后就沿着小巷飞快地逃跑了。|近义词：scram, flee, bolt, dash|反义词：saunter, stroll, amble

虚拟语气:

被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice.)
The thief was hightailed away by the police. = 小偷被警察带走了。(This implies a forceful removal rather than a quick escape.)
The information was hightailed to the authorities. = 信息被迅速传递给了当局。(Here, 'hightailed' implies speed of transmission.)


疑问句:
Will you hightail it out of here if things get heated? = 如果情况变得紧张，你会立刻离开吗？
Did he hightail it away after the argument? = 争吵后，他是不是立刻逃走了？


否定句:
He didn't hightail it away; he faced the problem head-on. = 他没有逃跑；他正面面对了这个问题。
She didn't hightail it to the nearest exit, she calmly waited for the all-clear. = 她没有跑到最近的出口，而是平静地等待了警报解除。
