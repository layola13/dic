crouton:/ˈkruːtɒn/| n. |面包丁，油炸面包块 | A small piece of bread, typically toasted or fried, that is used as a garnish or added to soups and salads.  |例句：The salad was topped with croutons and a creamy dressing. 这份沙拉上面放了面包丁和奶油酱。

例句：I added some croutons to my soup to make it more filling. 我在汤里加了一些面包丁，让它更饱腹。


近义词：bread cube, toasted bread piece |反义词：无


词性：名词

词源:
The word "crouton" comes from the Old French word "croustade," meaning "crust" or "crispy piece."  The word evolved through Middle French and eventually into the modern English "crouton."
