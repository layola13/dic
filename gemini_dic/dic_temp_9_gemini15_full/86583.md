heliocentricity:/ˌhiːliəʊsɛnˈtrɪsəti/| n. |日心说；日心论|The belief that the sun is the center of the solar system, with the planets revolving around it.  This concept, central to modern astronomy, contrasts with the geocentric model which places the Earth at the center.| Example:  The acceptance of heliocentricity revolutionized our understanding of the universe. 日心说的接受彻底改变了我们对宇宙的理解。|近义词：solar centrism|反义词：geocentrism
