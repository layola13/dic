clientele:/ˌkliːənˈtɛl/| n. |顾客，客户群 |A clientele refers to the group of customers or clients that a business or professional regularly serves. It implies a loyal and often high-value group.  |The restaurant's clientele is mostly composed of wealthy professionals. 这家餐厅的顾客大多是富有的专业人士。 |customer base, customer, patronage | competitors, rivals


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hotel boasts a loyal clientele. = 这家酒店拥有忠实的顾客群。
This boutique caters to a discerning clientele. = 这家精品店迎合的是眼光独到的顾客群。

一般过去时:
The spa lost a significant portion of its clientele after the scandal. = 丑闻发生后，这家水疗中心损失了一大部分顾客。
The shop's clientele changed dramatically after it relocated. = 商店搬迁后，顾客群体发生了巨大的变化。

现在完成时:
The salon has cultivated a large and diverse clientele over the years. = 多年来，这家沙龙已经培养了庞大而多元化的客户群体。
The lawyer has built a successful practice with a prestigious clientele. = 这位律师凭借着高端客户群体建立了成功的业务。

过去完成时:
Before the expansion, the company had a much smaller clientele. = 在扩张之前，该公司拥有更小的客户群。
The restaurant had already established a solid clientele before receiving its first award. = 这家餐厅在获得第一个奖项之前就已经建立了稳定的客户群。

将来时:
The new marketing campaign will attract a wider clientele. = 新的营销活动将吸引更广泛的客户群。
We expect our clientele to grow significantly next year. = 我们预计明年的客户群将大幅增长。

虚拟语气:
If the store had better customer service, it would attract a larger clientele. = 如果这家商店的服务更好，它将吸引更多的客户。
I wish we had a more affluent clientele. = 我希望我们有更富裕的客户群。

被动语态:
The business is highly valued by its clientele. = 该企业受到其客户群的高度重视。
The new policy was well-received by the clientele. = 新政策受到了客户群的欢迎。

疑问句:
What type of clientele does your business target? = 你们的企业目标客户群体是什么样的？
Does your restaurant have a specific clientele? = 你们的餐厅有特定的目标客户群体吗？

否定句:
The shop does not cater to a young clientele. = 这家商店不迎合年轻客户群。
The company failed to retain its existing clientele. = 公司未能留住其现有客户群。
