liquidising: /ˈlɪkwɪdaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 液化；使成液体；使清算 | The process of turning something into a liquid, or the process of settling the accounts of a company that is going out of business. | 例句：He was liquidising the assets of the bankrupt company. 他正在清算破产公司的资产。  The blender is liquidising the fruit.  搅拌机正在把水果搅成汁。| 近义词：liquefying, dissolving, settling (in the business sense) | 反义词：solidifying, crystallizing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine is liquidising the mixture. = 机器正在使混合物液化。
They are liquidising their assets to pay off debts. = 他们在变卖资产以偿还债务。


一般过去时:
She liquidised the ice cream. = 她把冰淇淋融化了。
The company liquidised last year. = 这家公司去年清算破产了。


现在完成时:
They have liquidised all their holdings. = 他们已经变卖了所有的财产。
The chef has liquidised the soup to make it smoother. = 厨师已经把汤打成泥状，使其更顺滑。


过去完成时:
The factory had liquidised all its products before the inspection. = 工厂在检查之前已经清算所有产品。
She had liquidised her savings before the trip. = 在旅行之前，她已经变卖了她的储蓄。


将来时:
We will liquidise the remaining stock next week. = 我们下周将变卖剩余的库存。
The judge will order the company to be liquidised. = 法官将下令对公司进行清算。


虚拟语气:
If we liquidised our assets, we could pay off our debts. = 如果我们变卖资产，我们就能还清债务。
I wish I had liquidised my investments earlier. = 我希望我早点变卖我的投资。


被动语态:
The assets are being liquidised by the court. = 资产正在由法院清算。
The sugar was liquidised by the heat. = 糖被热融化了。


疑问句:
Is the company being liquidised? = 公司正在被清算吗？
Are they liquidising the ingredients? = 他们在把配料液化吗？


否定句:
The ice cream didn't liquidise quickly enough. = 冰淇淋融化得不够快。
They haven't yet liquidised their assets. = 他们还没有变卖他们的资产。
