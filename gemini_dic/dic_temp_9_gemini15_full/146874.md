/ˌoʊvərˈɪnflʊənsɪz/ | v. |过度影响；施加过大影响 |To exert excessive influence on someone or something.  This word is not a standard dictionary entry; it's a combination of "over" and "influences."  It describes a situation where the influence is stronger or more pervasive than is desirable or appropriate. | The media overinfluences public opinion on political issues.  媒体过度影响了公众对政治问题的看法。|influence, sway, dominate |underinfluence,  neglect |动词
