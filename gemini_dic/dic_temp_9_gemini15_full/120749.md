misanthropist's: /ˌmɪzˈænθrəpɪsts/ | n. | 厌世者(的) |  The word refers to a person who dislikes humankind and avoids human society.  It's a possessive form, indicating something belonging to a misanthropist. | Example: The misanthropist's cabin was miles from the nearest town.  厌世者的木屋离最近的城镇有几英里远。 | 近义词: misanthrope's (更正式), cynic's (侧重怀疑和不信任) | 反义词: philanthropist's (慈善家的)
