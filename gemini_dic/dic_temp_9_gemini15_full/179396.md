redry:/ˈriːdraɪ/| v.|再干，重做，再尝试|To do or try something again.|例句：I'll redry the painting. 我会重画这幅画。|例句：Let's redry the experiment.  让我们重做这个实验。|近义词：redo, retry, repeat|反义词：finish, complete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He redries his paintings every week. 他每周都会重画他的画。
She redries the laundry because it wasn't clean enough. 她重洗了衣服，因为不够干净。

一般过去时:
They redried the clothes after the rain. 雨后他们把衣服重新晾干了。
He redried the pasta because it was undercooked. 他把意面重新煮了，因为它没煮熟。


现在完成时:
I have redried the shirt three times already. 我已经把衬衫重洗了三次了。
She has redried the cake several times, but still isn't satisfied. 她已经重做了几次蛋糕，但仍然不满意。


过去完成时:
He had redried the documents before submitting them. 他在提交文件之前已经重新打印了文件。
She had redried the paint before applying the second coat. 在涂第二遍漆之前，她已经重新刷过漆了。


将来时:
I will redry the pasta tomorrow. 我明天会把意面重新煮一下。
They will redry the fabric after cleaning it. 他们会在清洗织物后重新晾干它。
