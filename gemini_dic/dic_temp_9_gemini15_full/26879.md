cakes:/keɪks/ | n. |蛋糕，饼|A sweet baked food made from flour, sugar, eggs, and other ingredients.|例句：She baked a delicious chocolate cake for my birthday. 她为我的生日烤了一个美味的巧克力蛋糕。|例句：The bakery sells a variety of cakes, from simple sponge cakes to elaborate wedding cakes. 这家面包店出售各种各样的蛋糕，从简单的海绵蛋糕到精致的结婚蛋糕。|近义词：pastries, desserts|反义词：None
