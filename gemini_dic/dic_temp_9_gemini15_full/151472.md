parquet:/pɑːrˈkeɪ/| n. |镶木地板|A type of flooring constructed from pieces of wood arranged in a pattern, often geometric.  Parquet flooring is known for its elegant and durable nature.| The living room boasts beautiful parquet flooring.|近义词：wood flooring, hardwood floor|反义词：carpet, tile

词源:
From French *parquet*, diminutive of *parchet*, from Old French *parchet*, from *parc* "enclosure, park".  The original meaning referred to a small enclosed area, later evolving to describe the small pieces of wood used in the flooring.
