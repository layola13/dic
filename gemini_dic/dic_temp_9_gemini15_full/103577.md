johnnycake:/ˈdʒɑːnikaek/| n. |玉米饼，玉米面包|A johnnycake is a type of cornbread, traditionally made from coarsely ground cornmeal, water, and salt.  The exact ingredients and preparation method vary regionally.  It's a simple, rustic bread often associated with Native American and early American cuisine.|Example:  For breakfast, we had johnnycakes with butter and syrup.  早餐我们吃了涂黄油和糖浆的玉米饼。|近义词：cornbread, hoecake|反义词：无
