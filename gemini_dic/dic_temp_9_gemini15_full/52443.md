die out:/daɪ aʊt/| v.| 灭绝；逐渐消失；逐渐减弱 | To become extinct or disappear completely; to gradually cease to exist or be used. |例句：Many species of plants and animals are dying out because of habitat loss. 许多动植物物种由于栖息地丧失而灭绝。

Many ancient traditions are dying out due to modernization.  许多古老的传统由于现代化而逐渐消失。|近义词：become extinct, vanish, fade away, disappear |反义词：flourish, thrive, proliferate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The old customs are dying out slowly. = 古老的习俗正在慢慢消失。
This type of flower is dying out in the wild. = 这种花在野外正在灭绝。


一般过去时:
The dinosaurs died out millions of years ago. = 恐龙在数百万年前灭绝了。
That fashion trend died out quickly. = 那个时尚潮流很快就消失了。


现在完成时:
Many languages have died out over the centuries. = 几百年来，许多语言已经灭绝了。
The practice has died out in recent years. = 近年来，这种做法已经消失了。


过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, the fire had died out. = 我们到达时，火已经熄灭了。
The rumour had died out before it could spread widely. = 谣言在广泛传播之前就已经消失了。


将来时:
If we don't protect them, these animals will die out. = 如果我们不保护它们，这些动物将会灭绝。
This technology will likely die out in the next decade. = 这项技术很可能在未来十年内消失。


虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, the species might not have died out. = 如果我们早点采取行动，这个物种可能不会灭绝。
I wish that harmful prejudice would die out completely. = 我希望有害的偏见能够彻底消失。


被动语态:
The ancient language was slowly dying out. = 古代语言正在慢慢消失。
The old traditions are being allowed to die out.  古老的传统被允许逐渐消失。


疑问句:
Is this kind of bird dying out? = 这种鸟快灭绝了吗？
Are these skills dying out in younger generations? = 这些技能在年轻一代中正在消失吗？


否定句:
The fire didn't die out until the morning. = 火直到早上才熄灭。
The tradition hasn't died out completely yet. = 这个传统还没有完全消失。
