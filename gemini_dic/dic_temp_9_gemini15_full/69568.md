feloniously:/fɪˈloʊniəsli/| adv.| 恶犯地；犯法地；不法地 |In a felonious manner; wickedly; criminally.|例句：He feloniously assaulted the victim. 他恶劣地袭击了受害者。

例句：The jewels were feloniously obtained. 这些珠宝是非法获得的。


近义词：wickedly, criminally, illegally, unlawfully |反义词：lawfully, legally, rightfully


词源:  Derived from the Middle English word "felon," meaning "evil-doer," which in turn comes from the Old French "felon," having the same meaning.  The suffix "-iously" denotes manner or way.  The root traces back to the Latin word "felō," meaning "gall," "bile," or "wicked."
