nonoccupation: /ˌnɒnˌɒkjʊˈpeɪʃən/ | n. |非职业；非工作；业余爱好 |The state or condition of not having a job or profession; lack of employment.  It can also refer to activities pursued outside of one's main occupation, often as hobbies. |例句：His nonoccupation allowed him more time for painting.  他的非职业状态让他有更多时间作画。

例句：Her dedication to volunteer work filled the void left by her nonoccupation. 她对志愿工作的奉献填补了失业造成的空虚。


近义词：unemployment, idleness, leisure, avocation


反义词：employment, occupation, profession, vocation

词性：名词
