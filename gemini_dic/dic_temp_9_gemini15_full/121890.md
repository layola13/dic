missa:/ˈmɪsɑː/| n.|弥撒，弥撒曲|A Mass is a Christian religious service, particularly the Eucharist, or Holy Communion.  It involves the celebration of the sacrifice of Jesus Christ through the consecration of bread and wine.  The term also refers to the musical composition associated with this service.|例句：The Mass was celebrated in Latin. 弥撒是用拉丁语举行的。|例句：The choir sang a beautiful Mass. 合唱团唱了一首优美的弥撒曲。|近义词：Eucharist, Communion, service|反义词：无
