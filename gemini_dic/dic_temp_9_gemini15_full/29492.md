catapultic:/ˌkætəˈpʌltɪk/| adj.|弹射的；突然的；猛烈的|Suddenly forceful and violent; resembling a catapult in its sudden and forceful action.|The catapultic launch of the rocket surprised everyone.|近义词：sudden, abrupt, violent|反义词：gradual, slow, gentle


USAGE->

The sudden rise in oil prices has a catapultic effect on the economy. = 石油价格的突然上涨对经济产生了弹射效应。
His catapultic rise to fame was astonishing. = 他突然成名令人惊讶。
