girondist:/ˈdʒɪrɒndɪst/| n. adj.|吉伦特派成员；吉伦特派（的）|A member of a moderate republican faction of the French Revolution, prominent from 1791 to 1793.  They favored a constitutional monarchy and opposed the more radical Jacobins.|The Girondins were eventually overthrown by the Jacobins.|近义词：moderate republican, constitutionalist|反义词：Jacobin
