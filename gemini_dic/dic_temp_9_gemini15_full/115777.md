marinism:/ˈmærɪnɪzəm/| n.|海洋主义，马里尼主义|A style of painting, particularly prevalent in Italy during the 17th century, characterized by dramatic depictions of sea scenes, often featuring turbulent waters, stormy skies, and ships battling the elements.  It's named after the Italian painter, Claudio de Lorena.|例句：The gallery showcases a stunning collection of marinisms, highlighting the power and beauty of the sea.  画廊展示了一系列令人惊叹的海洋主义绘画作品，突出了大海的力量和美丽。|近义词：seascape painting, marine art|反义词：landscape painting (in contrast to sea scenes)
