infecundous: /ɪnˈfɛkʌndəs/ | adj. | 不育的；不结果实的；不肥沃的 | Unproductive; sterile; barren.  Infertile; not producing offspring or fruit.  Lacking in generative power; unproductive. |

例句：

The infecundous soil yielded a poor harvest.  =  贫瘠的土壤收成很差。

His infecundous marriage remained childless. = 他不育的婚姻没有孩子。


近义词：barren, sterile, unproductive, infertile, unfruitful

反义词：fertile, productive, fruitful, fecund


词性: 形容词
