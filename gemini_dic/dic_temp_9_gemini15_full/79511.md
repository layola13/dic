globetrotting:/ˈɡloʊbˌtrɑːtɪŋ/ | v. |环球旅行；周游世界 |The act of traveling extensively around the world.  It often implies a sense of adventure and exploration, rather than simply visiting various countries for tourism. |例句：He spent his gap year globetrotting across Asia. 他在休学期间周游了亚洲。

例句：After retiring, she decided to fulfill her lifelong dream of globetrotting.  退休后，她决定实现环游世界的毕生梦想。


近义词：world travel, international travel, circumnavigation (if encompassing the entire globe)
反义词：staying home, being sedentary
