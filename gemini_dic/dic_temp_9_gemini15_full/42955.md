covered:/ˈkʌvərd/| adj. v. |覆盖的；遮盖的；已处理的 |Covered describes something that has something else placed over or on top of it, or that has been dealt with or discussed. It can also mean protected or insured.  |例句：The table was covered with a white tablecloth. 桌子上铺着白色桌布。

The wound is now covered and bandaged. 伤口现在已经包扎好了。|近义词：concealed, sheltered, protected |反义词：uncovered, exposed, bare


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The blanket covers the bed. = 毛毯盖在床上。
He covers a wide range of topics in his lectures. = 他在讲座中涵盖了广泛的主题。


一般过去时:
She covered her face with her hands. = 她用手捂住了脸。
They covered 100 miles in three hours. = 他们三个小时走了100英里。


现在完成时:
We have covered all the important points. = 我们已经涵盖了所有要点。
The snow has covered the mountains. = 雪覆盖了山脉。


过去完成时:
They had covered the distance before nightfall. = 他们在夜幕降临前已经走完了这段距离。
She had covered her tracks carefully. = 她小心翼翼地掩盖了自己的行踪。


将来时:
We will cover this topic next week. = 我们下周将讨论这个话题。
The insurance will cover the cost of repairs. = 保险将支付修理费用。


虚拟语气:
If only I had covered my investments better. = 要是我当初把投资处理得更好就好了。
I wish I had covered my car with a tarp before the storm. = 我真希望暴风雨前我把车用帆布盖上。


被动语态:
The furniture was covered in dust. = 家具上蒙了一层灰尘。
The event was well covered by the media. = 此事件得到了媒体的广泛报道。


疑问句:
Is the wound covered? = 伤口包扎好了吗？
Are all the bases covered? = 所有方面都考虑到了吗？


否定句:
The roof is not covered properly. = 屋顶没有正确覆盖。
The insurance policy doesn't cover accidents. = 保险单不包括意外事故。
