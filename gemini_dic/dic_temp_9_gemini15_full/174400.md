quieter:/ˈkwaɪətər/| adj.|更安静的，比较安静的 |More quiet; less noisy.  It describes something that is quieter than something else.  |例句：The library was much quieter than the cafe. 图书馆比咖啡馆安静得多。

例句：The countryside is quieter than the city.  乡村比城市安静。


近义词：calmer, more peaceful, hushed, still

反义词：louder, noisier


词源:  Derived from the adjective "quiet" + the comparative suffix "-er".  "Quiet" itself has origins in Old French "quiet" and ultimately from Latin "quietus" meaning "restful, still."
