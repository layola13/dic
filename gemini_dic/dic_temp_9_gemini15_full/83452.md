habituality:/ˌhæbɪtʃuˈæləti/| n. |习惯性，习性|The state or quality of being habitual; the tendency to do something regularly or repeatedly.  It refers to the ingrained nature of a behavior or practice.|His habituality of arriving late to meetings frustrated his colleagues.  他的会议迟到习惯让同事们很沮丧。|近义词：customariness, regularity, routine|反义词：infrequency, irregularity, novelty
