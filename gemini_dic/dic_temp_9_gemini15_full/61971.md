en-suite:/ˌɒnˈswiːt/| adj. |套房的；带卫生间的|Having a bathroom attached to a bedroom or other room.  It describes a bedroom or other room that has its own private bathroom. |例句：The hotel room was en-suite, which was very convenient.  这家酒店的房间是套房，非常方便。  The master bedroom has an en-suite bathroom. 主卧室带有连接浴室。|近义词：self-contained, private|反义词：shared
