minicity:/ˈmɪnɪsɪti/ | n. |迷你城市，小型城市 |A minicity is a small-scale model or representation of a city, often used for planning or educational purposes. It can also refer to a small but self-contained urban area.  |例句：The architects built a minicity to demonstrate their urban planning ideas. 建筑师们建造了一个迷你城市来展示他们的城市规划理念。

例句：The new development is almost a minicity in itself, with shops, restaurants, and apartments all within walking distance.  新的开发项目本身几乎就是一个迷你城市，商店、餐馆和公寓都在步行距离内。|近义词：model city, small town, urban center (in a limited context)|反义词：metropolis, megalopolis|词性：名词
