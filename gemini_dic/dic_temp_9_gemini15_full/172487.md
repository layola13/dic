pyramidion:/ˌpɪrəˈmɪdiən/| n. |金字塔尖，塔顶 |The pyramidion is the capstone of a pyramid or obelisk, often highly ornamented.  It is the final, crowning element of the structure.  |例句：The pyramidion, crafted from gleaming gold, shimmered in the sunlight.  金字塔尖，由闪亮的黄金制成，在阳光下闪闪发光。

The ancient Egyptians adorned the pyramidion with intricate carvings and precious stones. 古埃及人用复杂的雕刻和宝石装饰金字塔尖。|近义词：apex, pinnacle, summit, capstone |反义词：base, foundation
