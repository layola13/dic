cocosette:/ˌkoʊkoʊˈsɛt/| n. |小椰子；椰丝糖果|A small coconut; a confection made from coconut.  The term is less common and often used in a more poetic or descriptive context, emphasizing the small size or sweetness.  It doesn't have a widely established formal definition like "coconut" does.|例句：The baker used finely shredded cocosette in the cake. = 面包师在蛋糕中使用了细碎的椰丝。 The child enjoyed sucking on the sweet cocosette. = 孩子喜欢吮吸甜椰丝糖果。|近义词：coconut, coconut candy, coconut flake|反义词:  None specifically, but words suggesting the opposite of sweetness or small size could be considered antonyms in certain contexts. |名词
