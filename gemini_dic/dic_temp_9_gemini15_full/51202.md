destined:/ˈdestɪnd/| adj.|注定的，命中注定的| destined implies an inevitable future, often suggesting a predetermined fate or purpose. It suggests a lack of control over the outcome.|He was destined to become a great leader. 他注定要成为一位伟大的领导者。|certain, fated, inevitable|uncertain, accidental, random|形容词


USAGE->

The stars are destined to burn out eventually. = 星星最终注定会燃烧殆尽。
She is destined for greatness. = 她注定要成就一番事业。
