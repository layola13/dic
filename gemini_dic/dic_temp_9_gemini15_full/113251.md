lusting:/ˈlʌstɪŋ/| v.| 渴望；贪婪；迷恋 |  Feeling or expressing strong sexual desire.  It often implies an intense, often uncontrolled desire, not necessarily for a specific person or object. |例句：He was lusting after power. 他渴望权力。

He spent his days lusting after the unattainable. 他整天都在渴望无法得到的东西。


近义词：craving, yearning, longing, coveting | 反义词：disdaining, rejecting, despising
