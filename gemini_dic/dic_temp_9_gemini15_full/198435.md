shrew:/ʃruː/| n.| shrew鼠;泼妇;坏女人| A shrew is a small, mouse-like mammal with a long snout and tiny eyes.  It is also used to describe a bad-tempered or nagging woman. | Example:  The shrew darted across the floor.  这只鼩鼱穿过地板。| Example: She's a real shrew, always complaining. 她真是个泼妇，总是抱怨。|近义词：vixen, termagant|反义词：gentlewoman, angel
