overprovisions:/ˌoʊvərprəˈvɪʒən(z)/| n.|过度供应，超额配置|The act of providing more of a resource than is actually needed;  excessive allocation of resources.  This can apply to various contexts, including computing resources (like server capacity, bandwidth, or storage), personnel, or even inventory. |例句：The company's overprovisions of server capacity resulted in significant cost overruns. 公司服务器容量的过度配置导致了巨大的成本超支。

例句：Overprovisions in the budget led to a surplus at the end of the year. 预算的过度配置导致年底盈余。


近义词：excess, surplus, superfluity

反义词：underprovision, deficiency, shortage
