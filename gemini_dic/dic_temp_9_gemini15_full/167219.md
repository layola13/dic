pretensions:/prɪˈtɛnʃənz/ | n. |自负；炫耀； претензии |  Claims or assertions of something, especially of importance or merit, often exaggerated or unfounded.  It often implies a false or unjustified claim to something.  |例句：His pretensions to artistic genius were laughable. 他的艺术天才的炫耀可笑至极。

例句：Her pretensions of wealth were easily seen through.  她的财富炫耀很容易被识破。


近义词：affectations, boasts, claims, aspirations, assumptions


反义词：humility, modesty, unassumingness


词性:名词
