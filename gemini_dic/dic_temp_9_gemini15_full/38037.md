comport-with:/kəmˈpɔːrt wɪð/| v.|与…相符；与…一致|To be compatible or consistent with something.|The new policy comports with the company's overall goals.  新的政策与公司的整体目标相符。|His actions do not comport with his words.他的行为与他的话语不符。|agree with, accord with, conform to|conflict with, clash with, differ from|动词


USAGE->

Does your explanation comport with the facts? = 你的解释与事实相符吗？
