cornerback:/ˈkɔːrnərˌbæk/| n. |角卫|A defensive player in American football who lines up on the line of scrimmage, usually on the outside, to cover a wide receiver.  Cornerbacks are known for their speed, agility, and ability to cover receivers in man-to-man coverage.|Example: The cornerback intercepted the pass and returned it for a touchdown. 角卫拦截传球并将其回攻达阵。 The team's star cornerback was injured in the game. 球队的明星角卫在比赛中受伤了。|近义词：defensive back|反义词：offensive player
