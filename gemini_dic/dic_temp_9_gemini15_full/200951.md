slithery:/ˈslɪðəri/| adj.| 狡猾的；令人作呕的；湿滑的 |  Characterized by a smooth, sliding movement;  suggestive of something unpleasant, sneaky, or untrustworthy.  It can describe both physical surfaces and people's behavior. |例句：The snake moved with a slithery motion across the rocks.  那条蛇以一种湿滑的动作穿过岩石。
The salesman had a slithery way of talking that made me suspicious.  那个推销员说话的方式很狡猾，让我起了疑心。|近义词：slippery, sneaky, shifty, evasive, treacherous |反义词：honest, straightforward, trustworthy, reliable
