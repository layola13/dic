sixer:/ˈsɪksər/| n.|六分球；六分；六个一组的事物|A sixer in cricket is a hit that scores six runs.  It also refers to something that comes in sets of six, like a six-pack of beer.|例句：He hit a magnificent sixer to win the match. 他击出精彩的六分球赢得了比赛。

He bought a sixer of beer for the party. 他为派对买了一打啤酒（六瓶装）。|近义词：six, half dozen|反义词:无
