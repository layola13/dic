lettern:/ˈlɛtən/ | n. | 信件；字母 | A letter is a written message, usually sent by mail.  | Example: I received a letter from my grandmother. 我收到奶奶的一封信。|近义词：missive, epistle | 反义词：telegram (电报), email (电子邮件)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He writes a letter every week. 他每周写一封信。
She sends letters to her pen pal. 她给她笔友写信。

一般过去时:
I wrote a letter to my friend yesterday. 我昨天给我的朋友写了一封信。
They sent a letter to the company. 他们给公司寄了一封信。

现在完成时:
She has written many letters this month. 这个月她已经写了很多信。
He has received several letters about the job. 他已经收到几封关于工作的信了。

过去完成时:
I had written the letter before he called. 他打电话之前我已经写好了信。
They had sent the letter before the deadline. 他们在截止日期之前寄出了信。

将来时:
I will write a letter to my parents tomorrow. 我明天将给我父母写信。
She will send the letter by express mail. 她将通过快递寄出这封信。

虚拟语气:
If I had written the letter earlier, I wouldn't have missed the deadline. 如果我早点写信，我就不会错过截止日期了。
I wish I had written a thank-you letter. 我真希望我写了一封感谢信。

被动语态:
The letter was written by hand. 这封信是手写的。
The letter is being sent today. 这封信今天正在寄送。

疑问句:
Did you receive my letter? 你收到我的信了吗？
Has she written a letter yet? 她写信了吗？

否定句:
I didn't write a letter. 我没有写信。
She hasn't received the letter yet. 她还没有收到信。
