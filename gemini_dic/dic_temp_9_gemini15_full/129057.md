nitrite:/ˈnaɪtraɪt/| n.|亚硝酸盐|A nitrite is a salt of nitrous acid.  Nitrites are used as food preservatives and in the treatment of certain medical conditions.|Example:  Many processed meats contain nitrites to prevent bacterial growth.|近义词：nitrous acid salt|反义词：无
