embayment:/ɪmˈbeɪmənt/ | n. | 海湾；湾口 | A body of water, such as a cove or inlet, that is partly enclosed by land.  It often refers to a recess in a coastline. | Example: The ship sought shelter in the sheltered embayment. 这艘船在安全的海湾中寻求庇护。  The picturesque embayment was a popular spot for swimming.  风景如画的海湾是游泳的热门场所。 | 近义词：bay, cove, inlet, recess | 反义词：open sea, ocean
