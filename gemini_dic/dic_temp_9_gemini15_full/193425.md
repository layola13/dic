seizers:/ˈsiːzərz/| n.|癫痫发作；夺取者；没收者|The act of seizing or the state of being seized, often referring to an epileptic seizure.  It can also refer to someone or something that seizes something else. |例句：The doctor witnessed the patient's seizers. 医生目睹了病人的癫痫发作。  The government initiated seizers of illegal drugs. 政府开始没收非法药物。|近义词：fits, convulsions, confiscations, appropriations |反义词：releases, restorations
