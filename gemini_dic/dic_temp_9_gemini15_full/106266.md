knowledging:/ˈnɑːlɪdʒɪŋ/ | v. |承认；确认；表示感谢 | To acknowledge something means to accept or admit that it is true or real.  It can also mean to express gratitude or appreciation for something. |例句：He acknowledged his mistake. 他承认了他的错误。

She acknowledged the gift with a grateful smile. 她带着感激的笑容表示感谢这份礼物。|近义词：accepting, admitting, recognizing, appreciating |反义词：denying, ignoring, disregarding |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He acknowledges his fault every day. = 他每天都承认自己的错误。
They acknowledge the receipt of the goods. = 他们确认收到货物。

一般过去时:
She acknowledged her error in judgment. = 她承认自己判断失误。
He acknowledged the letter promptly. = 他迅速地回复了信件。

现在完成时:
I have acknowledged my shortcomings. = 我已经承认了自己的缺点。
They have acknowledged the importance of teamwork. = 他们已经承认了团队合作的重要性。

过去完成时:
She had acknowledged her mistake before the meeting. = 她在会议前就已经承认了自己的错误。
He had acknowledged the letter before leaving. = 他在离开之前就已经回复了信件。

将来时:
He will acknowledge the truth eventually. = 他最终会承认事实。
They will acknowledge the contribution of their team. = 他们将肯定团队的贡献。

虚拟语气:
If he acknowledged his fault, he would apologize. = 如果他承认自己的错误，他会道歉。
I wish he would acknowledge the problem. = 我希望他能承认这个问题。

被动语态:
His achievements were acknowledged by many. = 他的成就得到了许多人的认可。
The problem needs to be acknowledged before it can be solved. = 这个问题需要得到承认才能解决。

疑问句:
Did you acknowledge the email? = 你回复邮件了吗？（是否承认收到）
Will they acknowledge their responsibility? = 他们会承担责任吗？（是否承认责任）

否定句:
He didn't acknowledge the letter. = 他没有回复信件。
She refused to acknowledge her defeat. = 她拒绝承认失败。
