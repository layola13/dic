congressmen:/ˈkɒŋɡrəsmən/| n.|国会议员|A congressman is a male member of a congress, especially the U.S. Congress.|例句：Several congressmen voted against the bill.  几位国会议员投票反对该法案。|例句：The congressman addressed the concerns of his constituents.这位国会议员回应了他选民的担忧。|近义词：representative, legislator, member of Congress|反义词：constituent (选民)
