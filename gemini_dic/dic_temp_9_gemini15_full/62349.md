endemic:/ɛnˈdɛmɪk/ | adj. | 地方性的；（疾病）地方流行的 | Relating to or characteristic of a particular people or place; native or confined to a particular region or people.  An endemic disease is one that is constantly present in a particular region or population. | 例句：Malaria is endemic in many tropical countries. 疟疾在许多热带国家是地方性疾病。

The plant is endemic to the island.  这种植物是该岛特有的。


近义词：native, indigenous, local | 反义词：exotic, foreign, widespread


词性: 形容词
