policymaking:/ˈpɑːləsiˌmeɪkɪŋ/ | n. | 政策制定 | The process of creating and implementing policies.  It involves identifying problems, formulating solutions, and enacting those solutions through legislation, regulations, or other means.  Policymaking often involves various stakeholders, including government officials, experts, and the public. | Example:  The government's policymaking on climate change is crucial for the future of the planet.  政府在气候变化方面的政策制定对地球的未来至关重要。 |近义词：policy formulation, decision-making, governance |反义词：policy inaction, laissez-faire |名词
