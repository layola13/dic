circular:/ˈsɜːrkjʊlə(r)/| adj. |圆形的；循环的；环状的；间接的|  Relating to or having the form of a circle or circles.  It can also describe something that is indirect or involves a cycle. |例句：The garden has a circular path. 花园有一条环形小路。

The news spread through circular channels. 这个消息通过间接渠道传播开来。


近义词：round, rotary, cyclical, indirect |反义词：straight, linear, direct
