maral:/məˈræl/| n.|马鹿，(尤指)北美马鹿|A maral is a type of large deer, especially the North American elk.  The word is often used interchangeably with "elk" in some contexts, but there are subtle distinctions depending on region and specific species.|例句：The hunter spotted a magnificent maral in the forest.  猎人在一座森林里发现了一只雄伟的马鹿。|近义词：elk, wapiti|反义词：无
