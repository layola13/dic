nonpurchases: /ˌnɑːnˈpɜːrtʃəsɪz/ | n. | 未购买的商品；未完成的购买 | Items or services that were intended to be purchased but were not.  This term often refers to the analysis of consumer behavior, highlighting the potential reasons behind why a customer didn't complete a purchase. |  The study analyzed nonpurchases to understand why customers abandoned their online shopping carts.  这项研究分析了未完成的购买，以了解为什么顾客会放弃他们的在线购物车。| 近义词：unbought items, abandoned purchases, unrealized sales | 反义词：purchases, sales, acquisitions | 名词
