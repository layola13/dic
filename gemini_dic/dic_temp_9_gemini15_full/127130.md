nativity:/næˈtɪvɪti/| n. |圣诞节；诞生；出生 |The birth of Jesus Christ; the celebration of this event.  It can also refer to the place or time of someone's birth. |例句：The nativity scene depicted the holy family in a stable. 圣诞场景描绘了圣家在马厩里的情景。

The nativity of the king was celebrated with great fanfare. 国王的诞生以盛大的庆祝活动来纪念。|近义词：birth, birthdate, Christmas |反义词：death
