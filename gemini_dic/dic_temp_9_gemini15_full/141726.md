oralism:/ˈɔːrəlɪzəm/ | n. |口语主义；口语教学法 |The belief that deaf people should be taught to speak and lip-read, rather than using sign language.  This approach emphasizes oral communication and discourages the use of sign language in education and communication with deaf individuals. |Example:  Oralism was the dominant method of deaf education for many years, but its effectiveness is now questioned.  口语主义多年来一直是聋人教育的主要方法，但其有效性现在受到质疑。|近义词:oral method, speech therapy |反义词:sign language, manualism
