what:/wɒt/ /wʌt/| pron.|什么，什么东西，怎么，多么|英文解释：Used to ask for information about something, or to express surprise or anger.  It can also be used as an exclamation.|例句：What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？

What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？


近义词：which, how, why|反义词：nothing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What time is it now? = 现在几点钟了？


一般过去时:
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you done today? = 你今天做了什么？
What has she accomplished? = 她取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you eaten before you came? = 你来之前吃了什么？
What had they finished before the meeting? = 会议之前他们完成了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气怎么样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being discussed now? = 现在正在讨论什么？


疑问句:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What do you think about this? = 你对此怎么看？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
He didn't say what he wanted. = 他没有说他想要什么。



PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What's new? = 有什么新鲜事吗？
What's going on? = 发生了什么事？
What a shame! = 真可惜！
What if...? = 如果...怎么样？
