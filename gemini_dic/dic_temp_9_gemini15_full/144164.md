outshouted:/ˌaʊtˈʃaʊtɪd/| v.|大声喊过；盖过…的声音|To shout louder than someone else, thus making it impossible for them to be heard.  |例句：He outshouted the protesters, making his voice heard above the din. 他大声喊叫盖过了抗议者的声音，使他的声音在嘈杂声中被听到。  The band outshouted the audience's cheers. 乐队的演奏声盖过了观众的欢呼声。|近义词：drown out, overpower, eclipse|反义词：be outshouted, be drowned out
