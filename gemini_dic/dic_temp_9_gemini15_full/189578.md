salient: /ˈseɪliənt/ | adj. |显著的，突出的，重要的 | Prominent; conspicuous; of notable significance.  The salient points of the argument were easy to understand.  | The salient features of the landscape included towering mountains and deep valleys. = 这片风景的显著特征包括高耸的山脉和深邃的山谷。 | prominent, striking, important, significant | insignificant, unimportant, obscure
