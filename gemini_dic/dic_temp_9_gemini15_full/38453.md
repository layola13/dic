concessionaries:/kənˌseʃəˈnɛəriːz/| n. |特许经营者；特许权获得者；特许经营商|People or companies granted the right to operate a business or provide a service within a specific area or under specific conditions, often in a public space like an airport or stadium.|The concessionaries at the airport offered a wide variety of food and drinks.|近义词：licensees, franchisees, vendors|反义词：owners (in the sense of complete ownership)
