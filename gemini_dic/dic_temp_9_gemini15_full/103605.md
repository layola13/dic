joint-venturers:/ˈdʒɔɪnt ˈvɛntʃərərz/| n. |合资企业参与者，合资者 |People or organizations that participate in a joint venture.  A joint venture is a business arrangement where two or more parties agree to pool their resources for a specific project or business. Joint venturers share in the profits and losses of the venture. | Example: The joint venturers agreed to share the costs and profits equally.  合资企业参与者同意平均分担成本和利润。 The success of the project hinges on the effective collaboration of the joint venturers. 项目的成功取决于合资企业参与者的有效合作。|近义词：partners, collaborators|反义词：competitors
