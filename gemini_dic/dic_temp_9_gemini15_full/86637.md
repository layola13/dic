hella:/ˈhɛlə/| adv.|非常，极其|An intensifier, typically used in informal contexts to emphasize the degree or extent of something. It's primarily used in Californian slang and has spread to other regions.|That party was hella fun!  = 那次派对非常有趣！|very, extremely, incredibly, intensely|slightly, mildly|副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
That movie is hella good. = 那部电影非常棒。
This coffee is hella strong. = 这咖啡非常浓。

一般过去时:

Last night's party was hella crazy. = 昨晚的派对非常疯狂。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
