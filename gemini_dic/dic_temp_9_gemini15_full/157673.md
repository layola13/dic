pivotable:/ˈpɪvəteɪbəl/ | adj. | 可旋转的；可枢转的；关键的；重要的 | Able to be pivoted; capable of turning on a pivot.  Also,  crucial or important in a situation. | 例句：The table is pivotable, allowing for easy adjustment. 这张桌子可以旋转，便于调整。  The pivotable moment in the negotiation was when they agreed on price. 谈判中的关键时刻是他们就价格达成一致的时候。 | 近义词：rotatable, pivotal, crucial, key | 反义词：fixed, immobile, unimportant
