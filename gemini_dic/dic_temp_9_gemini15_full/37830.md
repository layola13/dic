compete:/kəmˈpiːt/| v.|竞争，比赛|To strive for the same objective or prize as others.|例句：Many companies compete for the same customers. 许多公司争夺相同的客户。|例句：The two athletes competed in the final race. 两位运动员参加了决赛。|近义词：rival, contend, vie|反义词：cooperate, collaborate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They compete fiercely in the market. = 他们在市场上竞争激烈。
We compete for the best talent. = 我们竞争最优秀的人才。

一般过去时:
She competed in the Olympics. = 她参加了奥运会。
He competed against the best players. = 他与最优秀的选手竞争。

现在完成时:
They have competed in many tournaments. = 他们参加过许多比赛。
She has competed successfully at a national level. = 她已在国家级比赛中取得成功。

过去完成时:
He had competed in several races before winning the championship. = 在赢得冠军之前，他已经参加过几场比赛。
They had competed for years before they finally met in the finals. = 在他们最终在决赛中相遇之前，他们已经竞争多年了。

将来时:
They will compete in the next election. = 他们将在下次选举中竞争。
She will compete for a place on the team. = 她将竞争一个队里的位置。

虚拟语气:
If I were younger, I would compete. = 如果我年轻一些，我会去竞争。
I wish I could compete in the marathon. = 我希望我能参加马拉松比赛。

被动语态:
The award was competed for by many talented artists. = 许多有才华的艺术家争夺这个奖项。
The championship is fiercely competed for. = 冠军头衔竞争激烈。

疑问句:
Do they compete often? = 他们经常竞争吗？
Will you compete in the games? = 你会参加比赛吗？

否定句:
They do not compete fairly. = 他们竞争不公平。
He didn't compete in the last tournament. = 他没有参加上次比赛。


PHRASE->
compete with = 与...竞争
compete against = 与...对抗
compete for = 竞争...
compete in = 参加...比赛
compete successfully = 成功竞争
