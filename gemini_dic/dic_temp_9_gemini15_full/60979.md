elicited:/ɪˈlɪsɪtɪd/ | v. | 引出；探出；引起；诱出 | To elicit something means to evoke or draw out a response, answer, or piece of information.  It often implies a skillful or careful approach to obtain the desired response. | The teacher elicited answers from the students through careful questioning. 老师通过仔细提问从学生那里引出了答案。 |  evoke, draw out, extract, obtain | suppress, conceal, hide | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The interview elicits key information about the candidate. = 面试会引出关于候选人的关键信息。
The survey elicits opinions on the new policy. = 调查会引出关于这项新政策的意见。


一般过去时:
The experiment elicited a strong reaction from the participants. = 实验引起了参与者的强烈反应。
His questions elicited a confession from the suspect. = 他的问题诱使嫌疑人坦白。


现在完成时:
The research has elicited surprising results. = 这项研究已经引出了令人惊讶的结果。
The campaign has elicited considerable public support. = 这场运动已经获得了相当多的公众支持。


过去完成时:
The detective had elicited crucial evidence before the trial. = 侦探在审判前已经引出了关键证据。
The therapist had elicited the patient's childhood trauma through guided imagery. = 治疗师通过引导意象法引出了病人的童年创伤。


将来时:
The new marketing strategy will elicit a positive response from consumers. = 新的营销策略将会引起消费者的积极回应。
We hope the presentation will elicit discussion among the audience. = 我们希望此次演示能够引起观众的讨论。


虚拟语气:
If the questions were framed differently, they might elicit better responses. = 如果问题措辞不同，可能会得到更好的回应。
I wish the interview had elicited more information about his background. = 我希望这次采访能更多地了解他的背景信息。


被动语态:
The information was elicited through a series of interviews. = 信息是通过一系列采访得来的。
A confession was elicited from the witness under pressure. = 证人在压力下被迫承认。


疑问句:
What kind of response did the advertisement elicit? = 广告引起了什么样的反应？
What methods will be used to elicit feedback from the customers? = 将采用什么方法来征求客户的反馈？


否定句:
The police failed to elicit any useful information from the witnesses. = 警方未能从证人那里获得任何有用的信息。
The teacher's attempts to elicit participation were unsuccessful. = 老师试图引起参与的尝试没有成功。
