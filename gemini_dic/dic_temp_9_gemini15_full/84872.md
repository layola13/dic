hanging-out: /ˌhæŋɪŋˈaʊt/ | v. | 闲逛，消磨时光，交往 | To spend time in a relaxed way, often with friends.  It implies a casual and informal activity. | 例句：We spent the afternoon hanging out at the park. 我们在公园里消磨了整个下午。

例句：They're hanging out at the mall. 他们在购物中心闲逛。


近义词：relaxing, socializing, chilling, spending time together | 反义词：working, studying, being busy | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We hang out every weekend. = 我们每个周末都一起闲逛。
They hang out at the coffee shop. = 他们经常在咖啡馆闲逛。


一般过去时:
We hung out at the beach yesterday. = 我们昨天在海滩上闲逛。
I hung out with my friends last night. = 我昨晚和朋友们一起闲逛。


现在完成时:
I have hung out with them many times. = 我已经和他们一起闲逛过很多次了。
We haven't hung out lately. = 我们最近没有一起闲逛。


过去完成时:
I had hung out with them before the party. = 我在派对之前已经和他们一起闲逛过了。
They had hung out for hours before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经闲逛了好几个小时。


将来时:
We will hang out tomorrow. = 我们明天会一起闲逛。
I'm going to hang out with Sarah tonight. = 我今晚打算和萨拉一起闲逛。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would hang out more often. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常地去闲逛。
I wish I could hang out with you tonight. = 我希望今晚能和你一起闲逛。


被动语态: (被动语态不适用于此动词)


疑问句:
Do you want to hang out this weekend? = 你这个周末想一起闲逛吗？
Where are you hanging out tonight? = 你今晚去哪里闲逛？


否定句:
I don't want to hang out today. = 我今天不想闲逛。
They didn't hang out last night. = 他们昨晚没有闲逛。


PHRASE->
hang out with = 和...一起闲逛
hang out at = 在...闲逛
hang out later = 以后再一起闲逛
just hanging out = 只是随便闲逛
hang out in = 在...地方闲逛
