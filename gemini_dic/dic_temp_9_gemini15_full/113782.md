macrospore:/ˈmækroʊspoʊr/| n. |大孢子|A macrospore is a type of spore that develops into a female gametophyte in plants that undergo alternation of generations.  It is typically larger than a microspore.|Example: In heterosporous plants, the macrospore develops into the megagametophyte, which produces the egg cell.|近义词：megaspore|反义词：microspore
