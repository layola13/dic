plica:/ˈplɪkə/| n. |褶皱；皱襞；(解剖学) 脑膜褶皱| A fold or wrinkle in a surface, especially in a membrane or organ.  The term often refers to anatomical structures.  |例句：The surgeon carefully examined the plica of the vocal cord.  外科医生仔细检查了声带的褶皱。

The plicae of the small intestine increase its surface area. 小肠的皱襞增加了它的表面积。


近义词：fold, wrinkle, crease, ruga |反义词：smoothness, flatness |词性:Noun
