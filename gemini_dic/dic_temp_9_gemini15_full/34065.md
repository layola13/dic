civil:/ˈsɪvəl/ | adj. | 公民的；民间的；文明的；有礼貌的 | Relating to citizens or their rights; relating to the state or its citizens; polite and courteous. |例句：He has civil rights. 他享有公民权利。

例句：She showed great civil courage in speaking out against injustice. 她勇敢地站出来反对不公正，展现了极大的公民勇气。


近义词：polite, courteous, civilized, municipal  | 反义词：rude, impolite, uncivilized, barbarous


词源：From Latin *civilis, from civis "citizen".
