materialised: /məˈtɪəriəlaɪzd/ | v. | 实现；具体化；显现 | To become real or actual; to take on a physical form.  | The idea finally materialized into a successful product.  这个想法最终变成了一个成功的产品。 |  realized, manifested, emerged | vanished, disappeared | 动词


USAGE->

The plan materializes slowly. = 计划正在缓慢实现。
His dreams materialize gradually. = 他的梦想逐渐实现。

The ghost materialized before my eyes. = 鬼魂在我的眼前显现。
Her fears materialized into reality. = 她的恐惧变成了现实。

将来时:
