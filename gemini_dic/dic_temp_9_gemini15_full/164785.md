prefertilizes:/ˌpriːˈfɜːrtlʌɪz/| v.| 预先施肥；提前施肥 | To fertilize (land, etc.) before the usual time or before planting.  This term implies a proactive approach to fertilization, aiming to prepare the soil for optimal plant growth. | Example: The farmer prefertilized the fields to ensure a bountiful harvest.  农民提前施肥以确保丰收。  The gardener prefertilized the seedbeds to promote early germination. 园丁预先给苗床施肥以促进早发芽。|近义词：pre-fertilize, pre-treat (in the context of soil preparation)|反义词：post-fertilize
