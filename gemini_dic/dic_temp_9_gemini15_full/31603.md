chastens: /ˈtʃeɪsnz/ | vt. | 惩戒；责备；批评；使…感到羞愧 | To reprimand or correct; to punish in order to correct; to subdue or restrain.  |例句：The teacher chastened the student for talking in class. 老师因为学生在课堂上说话而责备了他。

The judge chastened the lawyer for his unprofessional conduct.  法官因律师的不专业行为而责备了他。


近义词：rebuke, reprimand, scold, admonish, discipline | 反义词：praise, commend, applaud


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chastens his children when they misbehave. 他在孩子们行为不当时会惩戒他们。
The coach chastens the team for their poor performance. 教练因球队糟糕的表现而责备他们。


一般过去时:
She chastened him for his rudeness. 她因他的粗鲁而责备他。
The principal chastened the students for their disruptive behavior. 校长因学生扰乱秩序的行为而责备他们。


现在完成时:
He has chastened his impulsive nature through years of self-discipline. 他通过多年的自律已经抑制住了他冲动的本性。
The company has chastened its marketing strategy after the product launch failure. 公司在产品发布失败后已经修正了它的营销策略。


过去完成时:
She had chastened him many times before he finally learned his lesson. 在他最终吸取教训之前，她已经多次责备他了。
The government had chastened the offending company before the scandal broke. 在丑闻爆发之前，政府已经惩戒了那家违规的公司。


将来时:
The manager will chasten the employees for their mistakes.  经理将会因员工的错误而责备他们。
He will chasten himself for his poor judgment. 他会因自己的糟糕判断而自责。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would chasten my wayward son. 如果我是你，我会惩戒我那不听话的儿子。
I wish I had chastened him sooner. 我希望我早点责备他。


被动语态:
The student was chastened for his disrespectful attitude. 学生因其不尊重的态度而受到责备。
His behavior was chastened by the criticism he received. 他受到了批评，行为因此有所收敛。


疑问句:
Will you chasten him for his actions? 你会因他的行为而责备他吗？
Should I chasten my employees for their lateness? 我应该因为员工迟到而责备他们吗？


否定句:
He did not chasten his son for breaking the vase. 他并没有因为儿子打破花瓶而责备他。
She would not chasten him, even though he deserved it. 即使他罪有应得，她也不会责备他。
