cryology:/kraɪˈɒlədʒi/ | n. |低温学，冷冻学 |The scientific study of very low temperatures and their effects on matter.  It encompasses various fields like the properties of materials at extremely low temperatures, cryogenic engineering, and applications in medicine and industry. | Example: Cryology plays a crucial role in the development of advanced superconducting materials. 低温学在先进超导材料的开发中起着至关重要的作用。 |近义词:cryogenics|反义词:None
