eaglewood:/ˈiːɡlˌwʊd/ | n. | 沉香木 | A fragrant resinous wood from several species of Aquilaria trees, highly valued for its aroma and used in incense, perfumes, and traditional medicine.  The resin is produced in response to fungal infection. | Example: The artisan carefully carved the eaglewood into a beautiful incense burner.  工匠小心翼翼地将沉香木雕刻成一个精美的香炉。 |近义词：aloeswood (similar resinous wood) | 反义词：N/A
