fertilities:/fɜːrˈtɪlətiz/ | n. | 生育力；肥沃；多产 | The ability to produce offspring; the state of being fertile.  It can also refer to the richness or productivity of land. |例句：The soil's fertility is essential for a good harvest. 这块土地的肥沃程度对获得好收成至关重要。

The high fertility rate in this region is a concern for the government.  这个地区的高出生率让政府担忧。|近义词：fecundity, productivity, richness |反义词：sterility, barrenness, infertility |词性 n.
