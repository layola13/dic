planary:/ˈplænəri/| adj.|平面形的；平面的| Relating to a plane or flat surface; relating to a plenary session.  A plenary session is a meeting of a whole group, especially of a legislative body or a church synod. | Example: The planary projection showed the earth's surface as a flat disk.  平面投影将地球表面显示为一个扁平的圆盘。 The plenary session of the conference concluded with a vote on the resolutions. 会议全体会议以对决议进行投票而结束。 |近义词：flat, level, even |反义词：curved, three-dimensional
