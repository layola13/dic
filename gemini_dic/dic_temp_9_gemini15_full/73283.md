foreboding's:/fɔːrˈboʊdɪŋz/| n.|不祥预感，预兆|The plural form of "foreboding," referring to instances or feelings of apprehension about the future.|例句：Her forebodings about the trip proved accurate when the plane was delayed for hours.  她的旅行预感被证实是准确的，因为飞机延误了数小时。


例句：The dark clouds and ominous silence added to the forebodings of the villagers.  乌云和不祥的寂静加剧了村民们的不安预感。
