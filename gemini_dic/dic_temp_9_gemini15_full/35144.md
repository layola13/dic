clouters:/ˈklaʊtərz/| n. |（俚语）笨蛋，傻瓜 | A clouter is a slang term for a foolish or stupid person.  The word's origins are obscure and not well-documented in standard dictionaries. It might be a dialectal variation or a relatively recent coinage.  | Example: "Don't be such a clouter; think before you act!" = "别那么傻；行动前先想想！" |近义词：fool, idiot, dunce, simpleton |反义词：genius, intellectual, bright
