predepartmental: /ˌpriːˌdɪˈpɑːrtmɛntəl/ | adj. | 部门之前的，部门预先的 |  Relating to or occurring before a department is established or a matter is handled by a department.  This word is very rare and largely theoretical; it's not commonly used in everyday language or formal writing. It suggests something happening in the preparatory stages before official departmental involvement. | 例句：The predepartmental discussions laid the groundwork for the subsequent departmental initiatives. 部门前的讨论为随后的部门举措奠定了基础。


The predepartmental planning phase was crucial to the project's success. 部门前的规划阶段对项目的成功至关重要。


近义词：preliminary, preparatory, antecedent | 反义词：postdepartmental


词性:形容词
