frontman:/ˈfrʌntmæn/ | n. |乐队主唱；代言人；代表人物 |A frontman is the lead singer or the most visible member of a band or group.  They are often the spokesperson for the group and the one who interacts most with the audience.  It can also refer to a person who acts as the public face for a company or organization, often without having much real power or influence. |例句：The frontman of the band had an amazing stage presence.  乐队的主唱台风很棒。

The company's new frontman is a charismatic young executive. 公司的新代言人是一位富有魅力的年轻主管。

|近义词：lead singer, spokesperson, representative, figurehead |反义词：background member, anonymous person |
