heteromerous:/ˌhetərˈɒmərəs/ | adj. | 异形体的；异数的 | Relating to or characterized by heteromers; consisting of dissimilar parts or elements.  The term is often used in biology and chemistry to describe structures or compounds composed of different subunits. For example, a heteromeric protein is composed of different polypeptide chains. |例句：The heteromerous protein complex exhibited unique catalytic activity.  该异源蛋白复合物表现出独特的催化活性。

The newly synthesized heteromerous polymer showed improved mechanical properties. 新合成的异源聚合物显示出改进的机械性能。|近义词： dissimilar, heterogeneous, diverse | 反义词：homomerous, homogeneous, uniform
