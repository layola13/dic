ringsiders:/ˈrɪŋˌsaɪdərz/| n.|拳击场边观众；现场观众|People sitting close to a boxing ring or other sporting event, especially those who are knowledgeable about the sport.  They often have a good view of the action and can provide commentary or insights.|例句：The ringsiders gasped as the boxer went down. 拳击场边观众在拳击手倒地时倒吸一口凉气。

例句：The ringsiders were impressed by the fighter's skill. 现场观众对拳击手的技术印象深刻。


近义词：spectators, onlookers, audience


反义词：participants, combatants
