lobster:/ˈlɒbstə(r)/| n. |龙虾|A large marine crustacean with a long body, a hard shell, and powerful claws.  It is highly prized as food. |例句：We had delicious grilled lobster for dinner.  我们晚餐吃了美味的烤龙虾。|例句：The lobster was caught off the coast of Maine. 这只龙虾是在缅因州海岸捕获的。|近义词：crayfish (though smaller), crawfish |反义词：None directly applicable.
