pierce:/pɪərs/| vt. n.|刺穿；穿透；刺痛；洞察|To make a hole through something with a pointed object.  To affect deeply or suddenly.|例句：The arrow pierced the knight's armor.  箭穿透了骑士的盔甲。

The news pierced her heart. 这个消息让她心碎。|近义词：penetrate, puncture, stab|反义词：seal, close
