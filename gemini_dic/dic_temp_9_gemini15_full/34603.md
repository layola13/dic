cleaved:/kliːvd/| v.|分裂，粘合；坚持|To separate or be separated into parts, especially along a natural line of division; to adhere strongly.|The rock cleaved in two after the explosion. 爆炸后岩石裂成了两半。 The two families have cleaved together through thick and thin. 这两家人风雨同舟。|split, divided, adhered, stuck|merged, united, separated (in the sense of not sticking together)|动词

He cleaves to his principles. = 他坚持自己的原则。
