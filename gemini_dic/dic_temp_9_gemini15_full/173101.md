/kwɑːz/ | adj. | 类似的，好像的，似是而非的 |  Appearing to be something but not actually being so; seeming; apparently.  |  The quasicrystal structure is fascinating because it seems ordered but lacks the perfect repetition of a true crystal.  |类似的，近似的| 真正的，真实的 |形容词


例句：

The meeting had a quasi-formal atmosphere. = 会议气氛比较正式。

The proposed solution is quasi-legal, at best. =  提出的解决方案充其量只是似是而非的合法。


近义词：pseudo-, semi-, seeming, apparent

反义词：genuine, real, authentic


词性: 形容词
