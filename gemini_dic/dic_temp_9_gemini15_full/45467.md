curatorship:/ˈkjʊrətərʃɪp/ | n. |馆长职位；馆长职务；管理；策展权 | The act or function of a curator; the position or office of a curator.  It also refers to the care and management of a collection, often in a museum or art gallery, or the process of curating an exhibition or collection. |例句：The museum's curatorship is responsible for preserving its vast collection of artifacts.  博物馆的馆长负责保存其大量的文物收藏。


The curatorship of this exhibition was highly praised for its innovative approach. 此次展览的策展工作因其创新的方法而受到高度赞扬。|近义词：custody, guardianship, superintendence |反义词：neglect, abandonment
