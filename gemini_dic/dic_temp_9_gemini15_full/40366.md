contractual:/kənˈtræktʃuəl/| adj.|合同的；契约的；约定好的 | Relating to or contained in a contract.  |例句：The contractual obligations are clearly stated in the agreement. 合同义务在协议中已明确说明。|例句：They are bound by contractual terms to deliver the goods by next week.他们受合同条款约束，必须在下周交付货物。|近义词：conventional, agreed, stipulated |反义词：informal, voluntary
