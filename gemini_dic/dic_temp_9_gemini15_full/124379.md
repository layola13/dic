motives:/ˈmoʊtivz/| n.|动机，目的 |Reasons for acting or behaving in a particular way.|His motives for donating the money were unclear.  他的捐款动机不明确。|The company's motives are purely commercial. 这家公司的动机纯粹是商业性的。|近义词：reasons, intentions, purposes|反义词：disincentives


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are his motives? = 他的动机是什么？
What motivates her to work so hard? = 什么激励她如此努力工作？


一般过去时:
What were their motives for the attack? = 他们袭击的动机是什么？
What motivated him to quit his job? = 什么促使他辞职？


现在完成时:
What have been her motives all along? = 一直以来她的动机是什么？
What has motivated the recent surge in sales? = 最近销售额激增的动机是什么？


过去完成时:
What had been his motives before the scandal? = 丑闻发生前他的动机是什么？
What had motivated her to make that decision? = 什么促使她做出那个决定？


将来时:
What will be his motives in the future? = 他未来的动机将会是什么？
What will motivate them to change their behavior? = 什么会促使他们改变行为？


虚拟语气:
If I knew his motives, I would understand. = 如果我知道他的动机，我就会理解。
I wish I knew what her motives were. = 我希望我知道她的动机是什么。


被动语态:
His motives were questioned by the police. = 他的动机受到警方的质疑。
The company's motives are being investigated. = 公司的动机正在接受调查。


疑问句:
What are the motives behind this action? = 此举背后的动机是什么？
What motives drove him to such extremes? = 什么样的动机驱使他走向极端？


否定句:
His motives were not entirely altruistic. = 他的动机并非完全出于利他主义。
She denied having any ulterior motives. = 她否认有任何不可告人的动机。


PHRASE->
ulterior motives: 不可告人的动机
hidden motives: 隐藏的动机
mixed motives: 混合动机
selfish motives: 自私的动机
noble motives: 高尚的动机
