defoul:/dɪˈfaʊl/ | vt. |弄脏；污染；玷污 | To make something dirty or impure; to spoil or damage something by making it dirty. |例句：The oil spill defouled the beach.  石油泄漏污染了海滩。|例句：The scandal defouled his reputation.  丑闻玷污了他的名誉。|近义词：contaminate, pollute, soil, taint |反义词：cleanse, purify
