referrals:/rɪˈfɜːrəlz/ | n. |推荐；介绍；转诊 | The act of referring someone or something; a person or thing that is referred.  It often implies a recommendation or a sending to another person or place for help or treatment. |例句：She got the job through referrals from her former colleagues. 她通过前同事的推荐得到了这份工作。

例句：The doctor made several referrals to specialists for further diagnosis. 医生将病人转诊给几位专家作进一步诊断。


近义词：recommendations, endorsements, submissions, forwarding


反义词：rejections, denials


词性：名词
