deletable:/dɪˈliːtəbl/ | adj. | 可删除的，可消除的 |  Able to be deleted or removed.  This refers to data, files, or other items that can be erased or eliminated from a system or document.  | Example:  The email is deletable after 30 days.  30天后，这封电子邮件将被删除。  |近义词：erasable, removable,  removable |反义词：permanent, indelible
