photos:/ˈfoʊtoʊz/ | n. |照片；相片 | Pictures or images produced by photography.  |例句：I showed him the photos of my trip. 我给他看了我的旅行照片。|例句：The photos were beautifully taken.  照片拍得很漂亮。|近义词：pictures, images, snaps |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
These photos show our family vacation. = 这些照片展示了我们的家庭度假。
The photos capture the beauty of nature. = 这些照片捕捉了大自然的美丽。


一般过去时:
I took many photos at the party. = 我在派对上拍了很多照片。
She showed us the photos she took in Paris. = 她给我们看了她在巴黎拍的照片。


现在完成时:
I have already uploaded the photos to my computer. = 我已经把照片上传到我的电脑上了。
We have seen those photos before. = 我们以前见过那些照片。


过去完成时:
I had already printed the photos before the meeting. = 我在会议前已经把照片打印出来了。
She had taken many photos before her camera battery died. = 在她的相机电池没电之前，她已经拍了很多照片。


将来时:
I will take photos at the wedding. = 我将在婚礼上拍照。
They will send me the photos next week. = 他们下周会把照片寄给我。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better camera, I would take better photos. = 如果我有一台更好的相机，我会拍出更好的照片。
I wish I had taken more photos on my vacation. = 我希望我在假期里拍更多照片。


被动语态:
