nonprogrammable:/ˌnɒnˈprəʊɡræməbl/| adj. |不可编程的，非编程的 |Not capable of being programmed; lacking the capacity to store and execute a program.  This term often refers to calculators or other devices that cannot have their functions altered by the user. |Example:  This calculator is nonprogrammable; it only performs basic arithmetic. 这个计算器是不可编程的；它只能进行基本的算术运算。|近义词：fixed, unprogrammable|反义词：programmable
