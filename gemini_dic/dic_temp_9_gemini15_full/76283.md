/ɡɑː/ | interj. |啊；啊哈|An exclamation expressing a variety of emotions, such as surprise, realization, or mild annoyance.  It can also be used as a filler word.|例句：Ga! I found my keys! 啊！我找到钥匙了！|近义词：Oh, Ah, Hey|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Ga, I see what you mean. = 啊，我明白你的意思了。
Ga, this is delicious! = 啊，真好吃！


一般过去时:
Ga, that was unexpected. = 啊，这真出乎意料。
Ga, I didn't see that coming. = 啊，我没想到会这样。


现在完成时:
Ga, I've finally finished! = 啊，我终于完成了！
Ga, it's been a long day. = 啊，今天真漫长。


过去完成时:
Ga, I had already eaten. = 啊，我已经吃过了。
Ga, they had left before I arrived. = 啊，在我到达之前他们已经离开了。


将来时:
Ga, I'll be there soon. = 啊，我很快就会到。
Ga, we'll see what happens. = 啊，我们拭目以待。


虚拟语气:
Ga, if only I had known! = 啊，如果我知道就好了！
Ga, I wish I had gone. = 啊，我希望我去了。


被动语态:
Ga, the problem has been solved. = 啊，问题解决了。
Ga, the decision was made yesterday. = 啊，决定是昨天做出的。


疑问句:
Ga, what's that? = 啊，那是什么？
Ga, who is it? = 啊，是谁？


否定句:
Ga, I don't believe it. = 啊，我不相信。
Ga, I didn't see him. = 啊，我没有看见他。
