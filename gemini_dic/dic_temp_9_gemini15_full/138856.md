nursemaiding:/ˈnɜːrsˌmeɪdɪŋ/| n.| 保姆式照料；照顾 | The act of providing care and attention similar to that given by a nursemaid, often implying a degree of over-protection or excessive attention.  This term is not widely used and often carries a slightly negative connotation, suggesting unnecessary or excessive care. | Example:  Her parents were guilty of excessive nursemaiding, preventing her from developing independence.  她的父母过度溺爱她，妨碍了她独立性的发展。|近义词：overprotection, coddling, pampering |反义词：neglect, abandonment |名词
