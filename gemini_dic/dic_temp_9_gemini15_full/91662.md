hypercautiousness: /ˌhaɪpərˈkɔːʃəsnəs/ | n. |过度谨慎，小心翼翼 | Excessive caution; an extreme tendency to be cautious.  It often implies an excessive or unreasonable level of caution that may hinder progress or decision-making. |  His hypercautiousness prevented him from taking any risks, even when the potential rewards were high.  他的过度谨慎使他无法承担任何风险，即使潜在的回报很高。|近义词：overcautiousness, excessive caution, undue caution |反义词：recklessness, impulsiveness, daring
