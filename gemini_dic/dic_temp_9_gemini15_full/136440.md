nonrevenues:/ˌnɒnˈrevənjuːz/ | n. |非营业收入，非经营收入 | Income or revenue that is not generated from the core business operations.  It typically includes items like gains from the sale of assets, interest income, and other non-operating sources. | Example: The company reported strong nonrevenues this quarter due to the sale of a subsidiary.  这家公司本季度由于出售子公司而报告了强劲的非营业收入。|近义词：ancillary income, incidental income|反义词：operating revenues, core revenues
