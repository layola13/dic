iamb:/ˈaɪæmb/| n. |抑扬格|A metrical foot consisting of one unstressed syllable followed by one stressed syllable.  It is a basic unit of rhythm in poetry.  For example, the word "unite" is an iamb. |例句：Shakespeare's sonnets are primarily written in iambic pentameter, meaning each line consists of five iambs.  莎士比亚十四行诗主要采用五音步抑扬格，这意味着每一行都包含五个抑扬格。|近义词：trochee (反义)|反义词：trochee|名词
