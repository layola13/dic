drivetrain:/ˈdraɪvtreɪn/| n. |传动系统，动力传动系 |The system in a vehicle that transmits power from the engine to the wheels.  It typically includes the engine, transmission, driveshaft, axles, and differential. |例句：The car's drivetrain was damaged in the accident. 这车的传动系统在事故中受损了。
