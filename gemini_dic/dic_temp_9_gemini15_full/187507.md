rollout:/ˈroʊlˌaʊt/ | /ˈrəʊlˌaʊt/ | n. & v. |推出，展开，公布 |The act of making something such as a new product or service available to the public;  the process of gradually implementing something.|例句：The company is planning a major rollout of its new software next month.  公司计划下个月推出其新软件。

The rollout of the new policy has been met with mixed reactions. 新政策的推出引起了褒贬不一的反应。


近义词：launch, introduction, release, unveiling | 反义词：withdrawal, recall


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company rolls out a new product every year. = 公司每年推出一个新产品。
They rollout new marketing strategies regularly. = 他们定期推出新的营销策略。


一般过去时:
Apple rolled out the iPhone 14 last year. = 苹果去年推出了iPhone 14。
The government rolled out a new education reform. = 政府推出了一项新的教育改革。


现在完成时:
They have rolled out several updates to the software. = 他们已经为软件发布了几个更新。
The company has successfully rolled out its new marketing campaign. = 公司已成功推出了新的营销活动。


过去完成时:
Before the launch, they had rolled out a beta version of the software. = 在发布之前，他们已经推出了软件的测试版。
The team had rolled out the initial plan before encountering unexpected problems. = 该团队在遇到意外问题之前已经推出了初步计划。


将来时:
We will roll out the new feature next quarter. = 我们将在下个季度推出新功能。
They will roll out the updated website design soon. = 他们很快就会推出更新的网站设计。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would have rolled out the product sooner. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会更早推出产品。
It would be better if they rolled out the changes gradually. = 如果他们逐步推出这些更改会更好。


被动语态:
The new system was rolled out last week. = 新系统上周推出。
The marketing campaign is being rolled out across multiple channels. = 营销活动正在通过多个渠道推出。


疑问句:
When will the new app be rolled out? = 新应用何时推出？
Has the product rollout been successful? = 产品推出成功了吗？


否定句:
They didn't roll out the update as planned. = 他们没有按计划推出更新。
The new policy will not be rolled out until next year. = 新政策要到明年才会推出。



PHRASE->
product rollout = 产品发布
software rollout = 软件发布
marketing rollout = 营销推广
national rollout = 全国推广
global rollout = 全球推广
