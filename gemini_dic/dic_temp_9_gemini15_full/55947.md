dizzyingly:/ˈdɪziɪŋli/| adv.|令人头晕目眩地，令人眼花缭乱地 |  Describes something that causes dizziness or a feeling of being overwhelmed by its speed, intensity, or complexity. It suggests a sense of disorientation or being overwhelmed. | 例句：The rollercoaster ride was dizzyingly fast. 过山车行驶得令人头晕目眩地快。 The amount of information presented was dizzyingly complex.  呈现的信息量多得令人眼花缭乱。 | 近义词：vertiginously, overwhelmingly, breathtakingly, stunningly | 反义词：calmly, slowly, steadily | 副词
