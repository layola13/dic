nongraduated: /ˌnɒnˈɡrædʒuˌeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 未毕业的，非毕业的 | Not having graduated from a school, college, or university.  This adjective describes someone who hasn't completed their formal education at a particular level. |


例句：

He is a nongraduated student. = 他是一个未毕业的学生。

She's still a nongraduated senior. = 她仍然是一位未毕业的四年级学生。


近义词：ungraduated, undergraduate (if referring to someone still in university)


反义词：graduated


词性：形容词
