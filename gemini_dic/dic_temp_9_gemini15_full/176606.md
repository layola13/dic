reaccepts:/ˌriːəkˈsɛpts/| vt.|重新接受，再次接受|To accept something again after having previously rejected or refused it.|The company reaccepts returns within 30 days of purchase. 公司在购买后30天内重新接受退货。|近义词：readmit, reinstate|反义词：reject, refuse|动词


USAGE->

The school reaccepts the expelled student. 学校重新接受了被开除的学生。

现在完成时:
