decidua: /dɪˈsɪdʒuə/ | n. |蜕膜|The modified mucous membrane that lines the uterus during pregnancy and is shed with the afterbirth.  It is the thickened lining of the uterus during pregnancy, providing nourishment to the developing embryo and fetus.  |The decidua plays a crucial role in implantation and placental development.|  近义词：uterine lining (during pregnancy) |反义词：无

词源:
