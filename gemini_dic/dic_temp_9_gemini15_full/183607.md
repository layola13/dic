requite:/rɪˈkwaɪt/ | vt. | 报答；报复；回应 | To repay (a person, action, etc.) in kind; reciprocate.  |例句：He requited her kindness with a generous gift. 他以一份慷慨的礼物来回报她的好意。|例句：The company requited his years of service with a generous pension. 公司以丰厚的退休金来回报他多年的服务。|近义词：repay, reciprocate, return |反义词：neglect, ignore |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He requites kindness with kindness. = 他以善报善。
She requites her friends' support with loyalty. = 她以忠诚回报朋友的支持。


一般过去时:
They requited the favor promptly. = 他们迅速地回报了这份恩情。
He requited her insults with silence. = 他以沉默来回应她的侮辱。


现在完成时:
She has requited his generosity with a heartfelt thank you. = 她以衷心的感谢来回报他的慷慨。
Have you requited their help yet? = 你已经回报他们的帮助了吗？


过去完成时:
They had requited the debt before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经还清了债务。
She had requited his kindness long ago. = 她很久以前就已经回报了他的好意。


将来时:
I will requite your assistance in the future. = 我将来会回报你的帮助。
We will requite their hospitality with a memorable gift. = 我们将以一份令人难忘的礼物来回报他们的热情好客。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would requite his generosity. = 如果我是你，我会回报他的慷慨。
I wish I could requite their kindness adequately. = 我希望我能充分地回报他们的好意。


被动语态:
His kindness was requited with gratitude. = 他的好意得到了感激的回报。
The favor will be requited in due time. = 这份恩情会在适当的时候得到回报。


疑问句:
How will you requite their hospitality? = 你将如何回报他们的热情好客？
Will his efforts be requited fairly? = 他的努力会得到公平的回报吗？


否定句:
He did not requite her help. = 他没有回报她的帮助。
They have not yet requited the loan. = 他们还没有偿还贷款。


PHRASE->
requite a favor = 报答恩情
requite an injury = 报复伤害
requite love with love = 以爱还爱
requite good with evil = 以恶报善
requite evil with evil = 以恶制恶
