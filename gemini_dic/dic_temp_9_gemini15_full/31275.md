chapelet:/ʃæpəˈleɪ/| n. |念珠；玫瑰经；花环 |A chapelet is a string of beads, typically used for counting prayers, especially in the Roman Catholic faith.  It's often associated with the rosary.  The word can also refer to a garland or wreath of flowers. |例句：She held a chapelet in her hands as she prayed. 她祈祷时手里拿着念珠。|例句：The bride wore a chapelet of lilies in her hair. 新娘头上戴着百合花环。|近义词：rosary, beads, garland, wreath |反义词：无
