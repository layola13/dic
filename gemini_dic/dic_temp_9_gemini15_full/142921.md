out-streaming: /ˌaʊtˈstriːmɪŋ/ | n. |外流；涌出；大量外泄 | The continuous flow of something outwards.  It often implies a large volume or a rapid movement. |例句：The out-streaming of refugees from the war-torn country overwhelmed neighboring nations.  战乱国家难民的大量外流使邻国不堪重负。

例句：The out-streaming of lava from the volcano created a spectacular sight. 火山喷发的熔岩流形成了壮观的景象。


近义词：efflux, outflow, exodus, deluge

反义词：inflow, influx


词性：名词
