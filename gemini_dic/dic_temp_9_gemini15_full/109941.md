liberalisms:/ˈlɪbərəlɪzəmz/ | n. | 自由主义思想（复数形式） | The plural form of liberalism, referring to various schools of thought within the broader liberal tradition.  It emphasizes individual rights, freedoms, and limited government intervention. Different liberalisms exist, differing in their emphasis on social justice, economic policy, and the role of the state. | Example: The various liberalisms debated in the political arena often clash on issues of economic regulation and social welfare.  各种在政治领域争论的自由主义思想往往在经济管制和社会福利问题上发生冲突。 | 近义词：liberal movements, liberal ideologies | 反义词：authoritarianism, conservatism, totalitarianism | 名词
