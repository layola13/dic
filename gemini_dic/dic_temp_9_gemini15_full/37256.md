comicalnesses:/ˈkɒmɪkəlˈnɛsɪz/| n. |滑稽之处；可笑之处 |The comicalnesses of the situation were lost on him.  情况的滑稽之处被他忽略了。  |近义词：funniness, humor, absurdity |反义词：seriousness, gravity


英文解析:The plural form of "comicalness," referring to instances or aspects of something that are comical or funny. It emphasizes the multiple occurrences or facets of humor in a situation or event.


例句：

The comicalnesses of the play kept the audience laughing throughout.  这部剧的滑稽之处让观众全场笑个不停。

The numerous comicalnesses in his speech made it a memorable performance. 他演讲中多次出现的滑稽之处使之成为一次令人难忘的表演。


词源:

Derived from "comical" + "-ness" (a suffix forming nouns of state or quality). "Comical" itself originates from "comedy," ultimately from Greek "kōmōidia" (a play exhibiting humorous characters or situations).
