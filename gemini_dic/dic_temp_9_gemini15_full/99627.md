interlace:/ˈɪntəleɪs/| vt. n.| 交织；编织；（使）交错 | To weave or twine together; to intermingle.  The term implies a complex or intricate weaving or arrangement of elements. |例句：The branches of the trees interlaced overhead, forming a leafy canopy. 树枝在头顶交错，形成绿荫。

The artist interlaced the threads of gold and silver to create a shimmering effect.艺术家交织金银线，创造出闪闪发光的效果。

近义词：intertwine, interweave, braid, mesh |反义词：separate, disentangle |动词
