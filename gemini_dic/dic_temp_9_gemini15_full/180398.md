refutes:/rɪˈfjuːts/| vt.|驳斥；反驳；否认|To prove (a statement, theory, etc.) to be wrong or false.|The study refutes the theory that the disease is caused by bacteria. 这项研究驳斥了这种疾病是由细菌引起的理论。|近义词：contradicts, denies, disproves|反义词：confirms, supports, verifies|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refutes the accusations against him. 他驳斥了针对他的指控。
The evidence refutes the defendant's claims. 证据驳斥了被告的主张。


一般过去时:
She refuted the arguments presented by her opponent. 她驳斥了对手提出的论点。
The scientist refuted the previous findings. 科学家驳斥了先前的研究结果。


现在完成时:
They have refuted all the criticisms leveled at their project. 他们已经驳斥了针对他们项目的全部批评。
Scientists have refuted the earlier hypothesis. 科学家们已经驳斥了早期的假设。


过去完成时:
He had refuted the claim before the meeting even began. 会议还没开始，他就已经驳斥了这一说法。
The lawyer had refuted all the opposing arguments by the end of the trial. 审判结束时，律师已经驳斥了所有反对的论点。


将来时:
She will refute the allegations in her upcoming statement. 她将在即将发表的声明中驳斥这些指控。
The professor will refute the student's incorrect assumptions in his lecture. 教授将在他的讲座中驳斥学生的错误假设。


虚拟语气:
If he had more evidence, he would refute the claims easily. 如果他有更多证据，他就能轻松驳斥这些说法。
If I were you, I would refute his accusations immediately. 如果我是你，我会立即驳斥他的指控。


被动语态:
His arguments were refuted by the opposing team. 他的论点被对方团队驳斥了。
The theory was refuted by subsequent research. 这一理论被后续研究驳斥了。


疑问句:
Does the data refute the prevailing theory? 数据是否驳斥了现行理论？
Can you refute his claim with concrete evidence? 你能用确凿的证据驳斥他的说法吗？


否定句:
He did not refute the evidence presented. 他没有驳斥提出的证据。
The study did not refute the original hypothesis. 这项研究并没有驳斥最初的假设。
