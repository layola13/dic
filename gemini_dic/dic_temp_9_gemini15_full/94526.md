imposes:/ɪmˈpoʊz/ | vt. |施加；强加；征收 |To force someone to accept or put up with; to officially introduce a new law, rule, tax, etc. |例句：The government imposed a new tax on cigarettes. 政府对香烟征收新税。|例句：Don't impose your beliefs on others. 不要把你的信仰强加于他人。|近义词：enforce, inflict, levy |反义词：remove, abolish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government imposes new regulations every year. = 政府每年都会实施新的规章制度。
She imposes her will on everyone around her. = 她把自己的意志强加给周围所有人。

一般过去时:
The teacher imposed a strict discipline on the class. = 老师对班级实施了严格的纪律。
They imposed a curfew after the riot. = 暴乱后他们实施了宵禁。

现在完成时:
The new law has imposed significant restrictions on businesses. = 新法律对企业施加了重大限制。
He has imposed his ideas on the project, ignoring our suggestions. = 他把自己的想法强加于项目，忽略了我们的建议。

过去完成时:
Before the war, they had imposed a blockade on the country. = 战争爆发前，他们对该国实施了封锁。
She had imposed her solution, and we had no choice but to accept it. = 她已经强加了她的解决方案，我们别无选择，只能接受。

将来时:
The company will impose new safety measures next month. = 公司下个月将实施新的安全措施。
They will impose sanctions if the agreement is not met. = 如果协议没有达成，他们将实施制裁。

虚拟语气:
If they imposed stricter rules, the problem might be solved. = 如果他们实施更严格的规定，问题或许就能解决。
I wish they hadn't imposed such a heavy workload on us. = 我希望他们没有给我们强加如此繁重的任务。

被动语态:
New taxes were imposed on imported goods. = 对进口商品征收了新税。
A strict curfew was imposed on the city after the earthquake. = 地震后，城市实施了严格的宵禁。

疑问句:
What restrictions will be imposed on travelers? = 对旅客将施加什么限制？
Will any new fees be imposed on customers? = 会对顾客征收任何新的费用吗？

否定句:
The government did not impose any new taxes this year. = 政府今年没有征收任何新税。
They did not impose their opinions on the discussion. = 他们没有把自己的意见强加于讨论。
