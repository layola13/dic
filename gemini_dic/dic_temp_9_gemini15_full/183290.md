reprises:/ˈriːpriːz/ | n. v. | 重演；重播；再次演奏 |

英文解析：To reprise something means to repeat or perform it again, often after a break or in a different context.  As a noun, it refers to a repetition or a repeated performance.

例句：

The band *reprised* their hit song at the encore. 乐队在返场时再次演奏了他们的热门歌曲。

The actress *reprised* her role in the sequel.  这位女演员在续集中再次扮演了她的角色。


近义词：repeat, reiterate, replay, re-enact

反义词：omit, skip, neglect


词性：Noun and Verb
