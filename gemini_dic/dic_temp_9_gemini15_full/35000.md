cloistress: /ˈklɔɪstrɛs/ | n. |隐修女；隐居女子| A woman who lives a secluded life, often in a convent or monastery.  The word evokes a sense of seclusion, piety, and sometimes, confinement. |例句：The cloistress spent her days in prayer and contemplation.  隐修女把她的日子都花在祈祷和沉思上。


The cloistress, isolated from worldly affairs, found peace in her solitude. 这位隐修女与世俗事务隔绝，在孤独中找到了平静。|近义词：nun, recluse, hermit |反义词：socialite, extrovert
