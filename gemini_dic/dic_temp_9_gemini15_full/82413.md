grudgings:/ˈɡrʌdʒɪŋz/| n.|勉强给予的东西；怨恨；不情愿|Reluctant or unwilling agreement or compliance.  It often refers to something given or done reluctantly or resentfully.|例句：His grudging admission of guilt was barely audible. 他勉强承认有罪，声音几乎听不见。

例句：The grudging applause from the audience was a clear indication of their dissatisfaction. 观众勉强鼓掌，清楚地表明了他们的不满。


近义词：reluctance, unwillingness, resentment, begrudging, reluctant compliance

反义词：willingness, eagerness, enthusiasm, generosity
