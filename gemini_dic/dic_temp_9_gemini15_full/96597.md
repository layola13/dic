inductions:/ɪnˈdʌkʃənz/| n.|归纳；感应；就职典礼；介绍；引入|The process of reasoning from particular facts to general principles.  It can also refer to the act of installing someone into a position, or the introduction of something new.|例句：The scientist drew inductions from the experimental data. 科学家从实验数据中得出结论。

例句：The new CEO's induction ceremony was held last week. 新任首席执行官的就职典礼于上周举行。|近义词：inference, conclusion, installation, introduction|反义词：deduction
