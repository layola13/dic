repapered:/ˌriˈpeɪpərd/| v. |重新裱糊；重新贴壁纸 |To cover (a wall, etc.) again with wallpaper.  This is the past tense and past participle of the verb "repaper".|例句：They repapered the living room in a cheerful yellow.他们用明快的黄色重新裱糊了客厅。

They repapered the entire house before moving in.他们在搬进来之前重新粉刷了整栋房子。

近义词：redecorate, renovate, redo | 反义词：strip (wallpaper), remove (wallpaper)

词性：动词
