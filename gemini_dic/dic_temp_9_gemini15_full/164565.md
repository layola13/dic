preexamine:/ˌpriːɪɡˈzæmɪn/| vt.|预先检查；预审|To examine or investigate something beforehand.  It often implies a thorough and careful examination before a final decision or action is taken.|例句：The committee will preexamine the proposals before presenting them to the full council.  委员会将在向全体理事会提交提案之前对其进行预先审查。

例句：The judge will preexamine the evidence before the trial begins.  法官将在审判开始前预先审查证据。


近义词：inspect, review, scrutinize, survey


反义词：neglect, overlook


词性:动词
