mooch:/muːtʃ/| v. n.|蹭饭；蹭东西；游手好闲；流浪|To obtain something by sponging or begging; to lounge about idly; to wander about aimlessly.|例句：He mooched around the city all day. 他整天在城里游手好闲。

例句：Can I mooch a cigarette off you? 我可以向你蹭支烟吗？

近义词：sponge, bum, freeload, loaf|反义词：work, contribute, earn


USAGE->

He mooches off his friends regularly. = 他经常蹭朋友的东西。
She mooches around the house all day. = 她整天在家里游手好闲。
