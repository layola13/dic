prevaccinated: /ˌpriːˈvæksɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 预先接种疫苗的；已预先接种疫苗的 |  Having been vaccinated beforehand or previously.  This word describes a person or group that has received a vaccination before a specific event or time period.  It implies proactive vaccination rather than reactive vaccination after exposure. |


例句：

The prevaccinated group showed significantly lower rates of infection.  预先接种疫苗的人群感染率明显较低。

Many prevaccinated children avoided the flu outbreak.  许多预先接种疫苗的孩子避免了流感爆发。


近义词：vaccinated, immunized, inoculated

反义词：unvaccinated, non-immunized
