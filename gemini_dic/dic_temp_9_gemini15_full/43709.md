credits:/ˈkrɛdɪts/ | n. |学分；功劳；字幕；贷方；信用额度 |  Credits are units of academic achievement.  学分是学术成就的单位。 The film's credits rolled at the end. 电影结尾出现了字幕。 |近义词：points, acknowledgment, accolades, merit |反义词：debits


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The course gives you three credits. = 这门课给你三个学分。
She receives full credit for her work. = 她因她的工作获得充分的肯定。


一般过去时:
The professor gave extra credits for participation. = 教授因为参与给了额外的学分。
He received credit for his innovative ideas. = 他因其创新理念而受到赞扬。


现在完成时:
I have earned enough credits to graduate. = 我已经赚够了毕业所需的学分。
She has received credit for her contributions to the project. = 她因对项目的贡献而受到认可。


过去完成时:
He had already accumulated sufficient credits before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，他已经积累了足够的学分。
They had given him credit for solving the difficult problem. = 他们已经因为他解决了那个难题而给予了他赞扬。


将来时:
I will earn enough credits next semester. = 我下学期将赚够足够的学分。
She will receive full credit for her thesis. = 她将因她的论文而获得充分的肯定。


虚拟语气:
If I had more credits, I could take that advanced course. = 如果我有更多学分，我就能选修那门高级课程。
I wish I had received more credit for my hard work. = 我希望我的努力能得到更多认可。


被动语态:
Credits are awarded based on performance. = 学分根据表现来评定。
Credit was given to the original author. = 荣誉归于原作者。


疑问句:
How many credits do you need to graduate? = 你需要多少学分才能毕业？
To whom should the credit be given? = 功劳应该归于谁？


否定句:
He didn't receive credit for his work. = 他的工作没有得到认可。
The film didn't list all the credits. = 电影没有列出所有的字幕。
