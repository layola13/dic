dietitian's:/ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃənz/| n.|营养师的；|  The possessive form of "dietitian," indicating belonging to or associated with a dietitian.  It's used to describe something owned by, related to, or characteristic of a dietitian.  For example, a dietitian's advice, a dietitian's office, or a dietitian's recommendations.| 例句：The dietitian's advice was to eat more fruits and vegetables. = 营养师的建议是多吃水果和蔬菜。

The dietitian's office was very clean and organized. = 营养师的办公室非常干净整洁。|近义词：nutritionist's|反义词：无
