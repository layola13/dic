megalocephaly:/ˌmɛɡəloʊˈsɛfəli/ | n. | 巨头畸形 |  An abnormally large head size, usually due to an increase in the size of the cranium. This condition can be caused by various factors, including genetic disorders, brain tumors, hydrocephalus (fluid buildup in the brain), and certain metabolic disorders.  | Example: The child's megalocephaly was diagnosed at birth, prompting immediate medical intervention.  孩子的巨头畸形在出生时就被诊断出来，促使了立即的医疗干预。 | macrocephaly, large head | microcephaly (small head) | 名词
