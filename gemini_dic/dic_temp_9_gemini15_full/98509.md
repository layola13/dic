instantiating:/ɪnˈstænʃieɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 实例化；具体化 | To create an instance of something, typically a class in object-oriented programming.  This involves allocating memory and assigning values to the object's attributes.  In a broader sense, it means to create a concrete example of a concept or idea. | Example:  The programmer is instantiating a new user object. = 程序员正在实例化一个新的用户对象。 The philosopher attempted to instantiate the abstract concept of justice in a real-world scenario. = 哲学家试图在一个现实场景中具体化正义的抽象概念。 | 近义词：creating, generating, materializing | 反义词：abstracting, generalizing | 动词
