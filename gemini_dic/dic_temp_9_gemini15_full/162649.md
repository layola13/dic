prearrestment: /ˌpriːəˈrɛstmənt/ | n. |逮捕前；逮捕前的阶段 | The period of time before an arrest is made.  It often involves investigation and gathering of evidence.  | Example: The prearrestment investigation revealed crucial evidence linking the suspect to the crime. 逮捕前的调查揭示了将嫌疑人与犯罪联系起来的关键证据。|近义词：pre-apprehension,  investigation (in this context) |反义词：post-arrest,  after arrest
