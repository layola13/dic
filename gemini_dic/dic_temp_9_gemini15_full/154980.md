/ˈpɛtəlɪdʒɪz/| n. |花瓣数目，花瓣结构|The study of the arrangement, number, and form of petals in flowers.  It often involves analyzing petal morphology and its relation to plant taxonomy and evolution.|例句：The petalages of this species vary widely, from five to ten petals per flower.  这种植物的花瓣数量变化很大，每朵花的花瓣数从五到十不等。|近义词：petal arrangement, petal morphology|反义词:无
