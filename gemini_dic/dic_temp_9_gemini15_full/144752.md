/ˈoʊvərˌtʃɪlz/ | v. |过度寒冷；冻僵；|To experience excessive cold, often to the point of shivering uncontrollably.  This is not a standard dictionary word; it's a combination of "over" and "chills." It implies a level of cold beyond typical chills.|The hiker over-chilled after getting caught in the blizzard. = 登山者在暴风雪中被困后过度寒冷。  The baby over-chilled in the unheated room. = 婴儿在未加热的房间里过度寒冷。|近义词：freeze, shiver excessively, be very cold|反义词：overheat, be very warm|动词
