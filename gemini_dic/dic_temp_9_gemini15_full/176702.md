reactionarism:/riˈækʃənərɪzəm/ | n. | 反动主义，复古主义 | The belief in and advocacy of returning to earlier social, political, or economic policies or conditions, often characterized by resistance to progress or reform.  It often involves a rejection of modern or progressive ideas and a desire to restore traditional values and institutions. | Example: The rise of reactionarism in the country led to increased social unrest.  反动主义在该国的兴起导致了社会动荡加剧。 |近义词：conservatism (保守主义), traditionalism (传统主义), retrogression (倒退) |反义词：progressivism (进步主义), liberalism (自由主义), radicalism (激进主义)
