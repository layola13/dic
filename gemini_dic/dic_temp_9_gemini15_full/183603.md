requisitor:/rɪˈkwɪzɪtər/| n.| 索取者；请求者；提请者 |One who makes a requisition; one who demands or requests something.  A person who formally requests something, especially in writing.  |The requisitor sent a formal request for additional supplies.  索取者发送了一份正式请求，要求补充物资。|requester, claimant, applicant| provider, giver |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The requisitor requests supplies daily. = 索取者每天都请求供应物资。
He requests a meeting with the manager. = 他请求与经理会面。


一般过去时:
The requisitor requested more funds. = 索取者请求更多资金。
She requested a transfer to another department. = 她请求调到另一个部门。


现在完成时:
The company has requested a price reduction. = 公司已请求降价。
They have requested clarification on the issue. = 他们已请求对此事的澄清。


过去完成时:
He had requested the documents before the meeting. = 他在会议前已请求文件。
She had requested a leave of absence several weeks prior. = 她几周前就已请求休假。


将来时:
The client will request a progress report next week. = 客户下周将请求一份进度报告。
We will request a quotation for the project. = 我们将请求项目的报价。


虚拟语气:
If the requisitor requested it, we would provide it. = 如果索取者请求它，我们会提供它。
I wish the team had requested assistance sooner. = 我希望团队早点请求帮助。


被动语态:
The supplies were requested by the requisitor. = 物资是由索取者请求的。
A meeting was requested by the employee. = 员工请求了一次会议。


疑问句:
Who requested the additional resources? = 谁请求了额外的资源？
What did the client request? = 客户请求了什么？


否定句:
The requisitor did not request any extra time. = 索取者没有请求任何额外的时间。
They did not request a change in the schedule. = 他们没有请求更改日程安排。


PHRASE->
