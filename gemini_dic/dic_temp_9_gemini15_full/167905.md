principalship:/ˈprɪnsɪpəlʃɪp/| n. |校长职位；主任职位；首要地位|The position or duties of a principal, especially in a school.  It refers to the role, responsibilities, and authority associated with being a principal.  The term can also be used more broadly to describe the leading position or role in something.|例句：She aspired to the principalship of the prestigious high school. 她渴望担任那所著名高中的校长。

例句：The principalship of the company requires strong leadership skills. 这家公司的领导职位需要很强的领导能力。


近义词：headship, leadership, directorship, superintendency|反义词：subordination, followership
