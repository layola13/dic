kings:/kɪŋz/ | n. | 国王们；君主们 |  The word "kings" refers to plural of "king," denoting multiple male rulers of a kingdom or other territory. It can also refer to a group of powerful or influential people. | Example: The kings of Europe met to discuss a treaty.  欧洲各国国王会面讨论条约。  The kings of industry dominated the economy. 工业巨头们主宰着经济。 |近义词：monarchs, rulers, sovereigns | 反义词：subjects, citizens
