rampages:/ˈræmpeɪdʒɪz/| n. v.|暴怒；狂暴；肆虐；横冲直撞| noun: a period of uncontrolled violent behavior; verb: to move around in a wild and uncontrolled manner|例句：The protesters rampaged through the streets. 抗议者在街上横冲直撞。The bear rampaged through the forest. 熊在森林里横冲直撞。|近义词：riot, rampage, frenzy, fury|反义词：calm, peace, tranquility|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rampages through the house when he's angry. = 他生气时会在屋里乱窜。
The bull rampages across the field. = 公牛在田野里横冲直撞。

一般过去时:
The mob rampaged through the city after the game. = 比赛结束后，暴民在城市里肆虐。
He rampaged through the kitchen, breaking dishes. = 他在厨房里大肆破坏，摔碎了盘子。

现在完成时:
The vandals have rampaged through the park, destroying property. = 这些破坏分子在公园里肆虐，破坏了财产。
Rioters have rampaged across the city center. = 暴徒在市中心肆虐。

过去完成时:
After the earthquake, looters had rampaged through the devastated city. = 地震后，抢劫者在被摧毁的城市里肆虐。
The angry mob had rampaged before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，愤怒的暴民已经肆虐了。

将来时:
If they lose the game, the fans will rampage. = 如果他们输了比赛，球迷们将会暴怒。
I fear that the prisoners will rampage if their demands are not met. = 我担心如果他们的要求得不到满足，囚犯们将会暴动。

虚拟语气:
If he were angry, he would rampage. = 如果他生气了，他会发狂。
If the animals escaped, they might rampage through the town. = 如果动物逃脱了，它们可能会在镇上横冲直撞。

被动语态:
The city was rampaged by the rioters. = 城市被暴徒肆虐了。(This is less common usage)
The forest was rampaged by the wildfire. = 森林被野火肆虐了。(This is less common usage -  focus is usually on the action of the wildfire itself)

疑问句:
Will the protesters rampage again tomorrow? = 抗议者明天还会再次暴乱吗？
Did the animals rampage in the zoo? = 动物在动物园里横冲直撞了吗？

否定句:
The crowd did not rampage. = 人群没有暴乱。
He didn't rampage through the store. = 他没有在商店里横冲直撞。


PHRASE->
go on a rampage = 发狂；大肆破坏
a rampage of violence = 一场暴力事件
run rampage = 肆虐；横冲直撞
a rampage of emotions = 一阵情绪的爆发
on a rampage = 处于狂暴状态
