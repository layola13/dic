cassowary:/ˈkæsəwɛri/| n. |食火鸡|A large flightless bird with a bony casque on its head, found in New Guinea and Australia.|例句：The cassowary is a powerful bird with sharp claws. 食火鸡是一种强壮的鸟，爪子很锋利。|例句：We saw a cassowary during our trip to the rainforest. 我们在热带雨林旅行时看到了一只食火鸡。|近义词：emu, ostrich|反义词：None.

词源:
The word "cassowary" comes from the Malay word "kasuari," likely via Portuguese.  The ultimate origin may be from a Papuan language.
