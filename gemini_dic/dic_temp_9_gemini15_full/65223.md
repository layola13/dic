etiology's:/ˌiːtiˈɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 病因学；病原学 | The study of the causes or origins of diseases.  | Example:  The etiology's of many cancers remain unknown.  许多癌症的病因学仍然未知。  |近义词:pathogenesis, aetiology | 反义词:cure, remedy |  名词
