hyenoid:/ˈhaɪənɔɪd/| adj.|犬齿状的，似犬的|Resembling a hyena, especially in having prominent canine teeth.  The term often refers to the shape or appearance of something, not necessarily a biological relation to hyenas.|例句：The fossil showed hyenoid teeth, suggesting a carnivorous diet.  化石显示犬齿状的牙齿，表明是一种食肉动物。|近义词：canine-toothed, dog-like|反义词：无
