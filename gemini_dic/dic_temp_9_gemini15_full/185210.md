retracking:/ˌriːˈtrækɪŋ/| n.|重新追踪；重新定位；重新跟踪|The process of reviewing and adjusting a previously established track or path, often in navigation, surveying, or data analysis.  It involves going back over existing data or routes to correct errors, update information, or gain a new perspective.|例句：The surveying team conducted a retracking of the pipeline route to ensure accuracy.  测量队对管道路线进行了重新跟踪以确保精度。

例句：After discovering an error in the GPS data, the hikers needed to perform a retracking of their journey. 发现GPS数据错误后，远足者需要重新追踪他们的旅程。

近义词：re-tracing, re-routing, reviewing, revising|反义词：initial tracking, first pass|词性:名词
