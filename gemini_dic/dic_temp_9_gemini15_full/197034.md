shallots:/ˈʃæləts/| n.| 细香葱，小香葱 | A shallot is a type of onion with a milder, sweeter flavor than a regular onion.  They are often used in sauces, salads, and as a garnish. | Example:  The chef added finely chopped shallots to the sauce to enhance its flavor.  厨师在酱汁中加入切碎的细香葱以增强风味。|近义词：onion, garlic |反义词：无
