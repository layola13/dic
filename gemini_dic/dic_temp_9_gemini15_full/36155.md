/koʊˈfeɪvərɪts/| n. |共同喜爱的事物，共同的最爱 |A cofavorite refers to something that is a favorite of more than one person or entity.  It implies shared preference or liking.  The word is a blend of "co-" (meaning together or jointly) and "favorites."| Example: "Chocolate ice cream is a cofavorite of my children."  = “巧克力冰淇淋是我孩子们共同的最爱。”|近义词：shared favorites, mutual favorites |反义词：unshared preference, individual favorites |名词
