neossology:/ˌniːoʊˈsɒlədʒi/| n. |新词研究；新词学|The study of newly coined words and phrases, and their usage and evolution in language.  Neossology examines the processes by which new words enter a language, their initial acceptance and spread, and their eventual integration or rejection.| Example:  Her dissertation focused on the neossology of internet slang.  她的论文专注于网络俚语的新词研究。|近义词: lexicology, neologism studies|反义词: etymology (studies the origin of words)
