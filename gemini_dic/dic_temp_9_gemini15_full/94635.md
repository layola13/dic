impressers: /ɪmˈprɛsər/ | n. | 给人留下深刻印象的人或事物 | Those who or that which makes a strong impression.  The word is rarely used on its own and is more often found as part of a larger phrase, such as "powerful impressers of the public". | 例句：The new CEO's innovative strategies made a strong impression, making him one of the most successful impressers in the industry.  这位新任首席执行官的创新战略给人留下了深刻的印象，使他成为业内最成功的杰出人物之一。


The captivating performance of the band was a powerful impresser on the audience. 乐队的迷人表演给观众留下了深刻的印象。


近义词：influencers, impactors, motivators | 反义词：no impactors, those who leave no lasting impression.


词性：名词
