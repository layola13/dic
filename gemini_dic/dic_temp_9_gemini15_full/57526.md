dragoons:/dræˈɡuːnz/| n. |龙骑兵；（尤指17-19世纪的）装备精良的骑兵|A dragoon is a type of soldier, especially one who served in heavily armed cavalry regiments in the 17th to 19th centuries.  They were often used for both mounted and dismounted combat. |The dragoons charged into battle, their sabers flashing.|近义词：cavalry, horse soldiers|反义词：infantry
