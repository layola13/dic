combiner:/kəmˈbaɪnər/ | n. |组合器，联合器 | A device or system that combines or merges things.  It can refer to a machine that combines harvested crops, a component in a computer system that merges data streams, or a person who combines different elements. | Example: The combine harvester is an essential piece of agricultural machinery.  联合收割机是农业机械的重要组成部分。The audio combiner mixed the sound from several microphones. 音频组合器混合了来自多个麦克风的音频。|近义词：merger, joiner, integrator |反义词：separator, divider |名词
