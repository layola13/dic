rosiest: /ˈroʊziəst/ | adj. | 最玫瑰色的；最乐观的；最美好的 | Most rosy; most optimistic; most beautiful.  The word describes the superlative degree of something rosy.  It suggests a vibrant, optimistic, and perhaps idealized perspective.


例句：

He painted the rosiest picture of the company's future. = 他对公司未来描绘了一幅最美好的蓝图。

The rosiest projections for economic growth still show a slow recovery. = 对经济增长的最乐观预测仍然显示经济复苏缓慢。


近义词：most optimistic, most cheerful, most hopeful, most promising

反义词：most pessimistic, most gloomy, most depressing, most bleak


词性：形容词
