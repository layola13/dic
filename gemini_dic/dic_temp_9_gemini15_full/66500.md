exeunts:/ˈɛɡziːnts/| n.|演员退场|This word is a plural form of "exit," indicating that multiple actors are leaving the stage.  It's primarily used in stage directions in plays and scripts.|Example:  The scene ends with the exeunts of the king and his courtiers.  这幕以国王和他的朝臣退场而结束。|近义词：exits, departures|反义词：entrances



USAGE->
This word is not used in typical sentence structures like verbs or nouns. It's a stage direction.  Therefore, grammatical tenses and other sentence structures don't apply.  It's simply a notation indicating the action of multiple actors leaving the stage.
