precompose:/ˌpriːkəmˈpoʊz/|/ˈpriːkəmˈpoʊz/| vt.|预先组合；预先构成；预先编写|To compose or arrange beforehand; to prepare in advance.  The term often implies a combination or arrangement of elements before they are used or presented in a final form.| The architect precomposed the building's design before presenting it to the client. 建筑师在向客户展示建筑设计之前先进行了预先设计。


The marketing team precomposed the social media campaign to ensure a coordinated launch. 营销团队预先设计了社交媒体活动，以确保协调一致的启动。|compose, arrange, prepare|decompose, disarrange|动词
