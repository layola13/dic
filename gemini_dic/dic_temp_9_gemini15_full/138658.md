nudibrachiate:/ˌnuːdiˈbreɪkieɪt/| adj.|裸腮的；无壳的（指软体动物）|Having exposed gills, characteristic of nudibranchs.  Nudibrachiate refers to gastropods (snails and slugs) that lack a shell and have exposed gills. These gills are often brightly colored and feathery.|Example: The nudibrachiate sea slug displayed its vibrant, feathery gills.|近义词：shell-less (for the shell aspect), gill-bearing (for the gill aspect)|反义词：shelled (for the shell aspect), gill-less (for the gill aspect)
