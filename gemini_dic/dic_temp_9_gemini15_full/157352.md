pipelines:/ˈpaɪplaɪnz/| n. |管道；管道系统；流程；渠道 |A pipeline is a system of pipes or tubes used to transport liquids or gases over long distances. It can also refer to a series of processes or stages in a system.  |例句：The oil pipeline transports crude oil from the well to the refinery.  这条石油管道将原油从油井输送到炼油厂。

The company has developed a new pipeline for processing customer orders. 这家公司开发了一套新的客户订单处理流程。|近义词：conduits, channels, systems |反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept could be  disorganized processes or manual transport.
