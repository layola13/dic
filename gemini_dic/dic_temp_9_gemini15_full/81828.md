greybeards:/ˈɡreɪbiːrdz/| n.| 老人们，经验丰富的人 |  Experienced and often influential older men;  the collective noun for old men with long grey beards.  | 例句：The greybeards of the company decided against the risky venture. 公司里的老人们决定反对这项冒险的投资。
The greybeards in the village shared their wisdom with the younger generation. 村里的老人们把他们的智慧传授给年轻一代。 | 近义词：elders, veterans, old-timers | 反义词：youths, youngsters
