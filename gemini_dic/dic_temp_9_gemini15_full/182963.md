replait:/riːˈpleɪt/| vt.|重新编织；重新编排|To braid or weave again.  It implies a previous braiding or weaving that is being undone and redone, often with alterations or improvements.|例句：She decided to replait her daughter's hair after it got tangled. 她决定在女儿的头发缠结后重新编织。|例句：The composer replaited the melody, giving it a more modern feel. 作曲家重新编排了旋律，使其更具现代感。|近义词：rebraid, redo, rearrange|反义词：untangle, unravel
