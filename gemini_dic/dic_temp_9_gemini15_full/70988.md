firestorms:/ˈfaɪərstɔːrmz/ | n. | 火风暴，烈火风暴 | A firestorm is a very large and intense fire, often caused by extreme weather conditions or a combination of factors such as strong winds, dry vegetation, and high temperatures.  It's characterized by its rapid spread, intense heat, and the creation of its own powerful winds. |  The wildfires quickly escalated into a devastating firestorm.  这场野火迅速升级为一场毁灭性的火风暴。

The firestorm swept through the forest, consuming everything in its path. 火风暴席卷了森林，吞噬了一切。| inferno, conflagration | calm, peace | noun
