patronises: /ˈpætrənaɪzɪz/ | v. | 屈尊俯就；以高人一等的态度对待；惠顾；资助 |

英文解析:To treat (someone) as if you were superior to them.  To act condescendingly towards. To support or sponsor (a person, organization, or activity) financially. To be a regular customer of (a shop, restaurant, etc.).

例句:

He patronises the local cafe every morning. = 他每天早上都光顾当地咖啡馆。

She patronised the artist by buying several of his paintings. = 她通过购买他的几幅画来资助这位艺术家。


近义词: condescend, demean, support, sponsor, frequent

反义词:  respect (equally), ignore, neglect

She patronises the arts by donating to museums. = 她通过向博物馆捐款来资助艺术事业。
