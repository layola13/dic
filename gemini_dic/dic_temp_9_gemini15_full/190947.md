scalar:/ˈskeɪlər/ | n. |标量，数量 |A scalar is a single number, a quantity that has magnitude but no direction.  In contrast to vectors, which have both magnitude and direction, scalars are only concerned with the size or amount of something. For example, temperature, mass, and speed are scalar quantities. |Example: The scalar value of the temperature is 25 degrees Celsius.  温度的标量值为25摄氏度。

The mass of the object is a scalar quantity. 物体的质量是一个标量。|近义词：magnitude, quantity |反义词：vector
