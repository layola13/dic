mnemonic:/nɪˈmɒnɪk/ /mənˈɑːnɪk/ | n. | 助记符；记忆术 | A mnemonic is a device, such as a pattern of letters, ideas, or associations, which assists in remembering something.  It is a technique to improve memory. | Example:  "ROY G. BIV" is a common mnemonic for the colors of the rainbow (Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Indigo, Violet).  |近义词：memory aid, reminder, memorization technique |反义词：none
