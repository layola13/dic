nonconciliating: /ˌnɒnkənˈsɪlieɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不调解的；不和解的；不友好的 | Unwilling or refusing to be conciliatory; unyielding.  It suggests a stubborn refusal to compromise or find common ground.  |


例句：

His nonconciliating attitude made negotiations extremely difficult.  他的不妥协态度使谈判极其困难。

The nonconciliating response from the company only fueled the employee's anger. 公司的不妥协回应只会加剧员工的愤怒。


近义词：unyielding, uncompromising, intractable, inflexible, adamant, unaccommodating, belligerent, hostile


反义词：conciliatory, accommodating, amicable, friendly, peaceable, compromising
