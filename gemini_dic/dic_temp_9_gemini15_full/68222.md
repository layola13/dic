fairly:/ˈferli/ | adv. | 相当；公平地；相当地 |  Relatively; to a reasonable extent; in a fair manner.  | The work is fairly challenging. 这项工作相当具有挑战性。 |  She treated everyone fairly. 她公平地对待每一个人。 | quite, reasonably, pretty | unfairly, unjustly
