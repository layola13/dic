flinger:/ˈflɪŋər/ | n. | 投掷者；抛掷物；（尤指）甩动器；(俚语)轻浮的人 | A person or thing that flings; someone who throws things; a device for flinging something.  |例句：He was a flinger of words, speaking rapidly and without much thought. 他是个夸夸其谈的人，说话很快，而且不太考虑。  The child was playing with a mud flinger.  孩子正在玩一个泥巴抛掷器。|近义词：thrower, slinger, tosser (informal)|反义词：receiver, catcher


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flings the ball to his friend. = 他把球扔给他的朋友。
She flings herself onto the sofa. = 她猛地往沙发上一倒。


一般过去时:
He flung the door open angrily. = 他生气地把门猛地打开。
She flung her bag on the floor. = 她把包扔在了地板上。


现在完成时:
He has flung the trash into the bin. = 他已经把垃圾扔进了垃圾桶。
She has flung herself into her work. = 她全身心地投入到工作中。


过去完成时:
He had flung the letter into the fire before realizing his mistake. = 他在意识到自己的错误之前已经把信扔进了火里。
She had flung herself into the arms of her mother. = 她已经投入母亲的怀抱。


将来时:
He will fling the confetti into the air at the wedding. = 他将在婚礼上把五彩纸屑抛向空中。
She will fling open the windows to let in the fresh air. = 她将打开窗户让新鲜空气进来。


虚拟语气:
If he flung the stone harder, it might have reached the target. = 如果他更用力地扔石头，它可能就到达目标了。
I wish I had flung myself into the project sooner. = 我真希望我早点全身心地投入到这个项目中。


被动语态:
The ball was flung across the field. = 球被扔过了球场。
The door was flung open by the wind. = 门被风猛地吹开了。


疑问句:
Did he fling the stone intentionally? = 他是故意扔石头的吗？

否定句:
He didn't fling the garbage on the street. = 他没有把垃圾扔在街上。
She didn't fling herself into the crowd. = 她没有冲进人群。
