helped:/helpt/| v.|帮助，协助|to give assistance to; to aid|例句：She helped me with my homework. 她帮我做了作业。
He helped the old woman cross the street. 他帮助那位老太太过马路。|近义词：aided, assisted, supported|反义词：hindered, obstructed, hampered|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He helps me with my studies. 他帮助我学习。
She helps the poor regularly. 她定期帮助穷人。

一般过去时:
I helped my neighbor move yesterday. 昨天我帮助邻居搬家。
They helped us fix the car. 他们帮助我们修理汽车。

现在完成时:
She has helped many people in need. 她已经帮助了许多需要帮助的人。
He has helped to improve the community. 他帮助改善了社区。

过去完成时:
I had helped him before he asked.  在他开口之前我已经帮助过他了。
They had helped prepare the food before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前，他们已经帮忙准备好了食物。

将来时:
I will help you with your project tomorrow. 明天我将帮助你完成你的项目。
She will help us organize the event. 她将帮助我们组织这次活动。

虚拟语气:
If he had helped me, I would have finished on time. 如果他帮助我，我就能够按时完成了。
I wish she had helped me earlier. 我希望她早点帮我。

被动语态:
I was helped by a kind stranger. 我得到一位好心陌生人的帮助。
The project was helped by a generous donation. 这个项目得到了慷慨捐款的帮助。

疑问句:
Did he help you? 他帮助你了吗？
Can she help us? 她能帮助我们吗？

否定句:
He didn't help me at all. 他根本没有帮助我。
She couldn't help him with the problem. 她无法帮助他解决这个问题。


PHRASE->
help out = 帮助摆脱困境
help oneself to = 自取；随便吃
can't help but = 不禁；忍不住
help...with...= 帮助…做…
with the help of = 在…的帮助下
