jubilant:/ˈdʒuːbɪlənt/| adj. |欢欣鼓舞的；喜气洋洋的 |Feeling or expressing great joy; triumphant.  |例句：The team was jubilant after winning the championship. 赢得冠军后，球队一片欢腾。
例句：The children were jubilant at the prospect of a holiday.  孩子们对假期的到来感到欣喜若狂。|近义词：joyful, triumphant, elated, ecstatic |反义词：dejected, despondent, downcast


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is jubilant about his success. = 他对自己的成功感到欢欣鼓舞。
The crowd is jubilant after the victory. = 胜利后，人群欢欣鼓舞。


一般过去时:
She was jubilant when she heard the good news. = 当她听到好消息时，她欣喜若狂。
They were jubilant at the party. = 他们在派对上兴高采烈。


现在完成时:
They have been jubilant ever since they won the lottery. = 自从他们赢得彩票以来，他们一直兴高采烈。
We have been jubilant about the project's success. = 我们对项目的成功感到欢欣鼓舞。


过去完成时:
She had been jubilant all day before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，她一整天都兴高采烈。
They had been jubilant about the news before it was confirmed. = 在消息得到证实之前，他们对此感到非常高兴。


将来时:
They will be jubilant when they see the results. = 当他们看到结果时，他们会欣喜若狂。
We will be jubilant if our team wins the game. = 如果我们的球队赢得比赛，我们会欣喜若狂。


虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would be jubilant. = 如果我中了彩票，我会欣喜若狂。
I wish I were jubilant about my future. = 我希望我对我的未来感到欢欣鼓舞。


被动语态:
The city was jubilant at the news of peace.  = 城市对和平的消息感到欢欣鼓舞。(被动语态在此处略显不自然， jubilant 通常作主动语态)
The team felt jubilant after their win. = 球队在获胜后感到欢欣鼓舞。(主动语态更符合jubilant的用法)


疑问句:
Are you jubilant about the news? = 你对这个消息感到高兴吗？
Was she jubilant after the performance? = 演出结束后她很高兴吗？


否定句:
He wasn't jubilant about the results. = 他对结果并不感到高兴。
They were not jubilant after the defeat. = 失败后他们并不高兴。


PHRASE->
a jubilant mood = 欢欣鼓舞的心情
a jubilant cry = 欢呼声
a jubilant celebration = 欢庆的庆祝活动
feel jubilant = 感到兴高采烈
express jubilant feelings = 表达喜悦的心情


词源:
From Middle French *jubilant, from Latin *jubilare "to shout for joy," of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Hebrew yobel "ram's horn," used as a trumpet to announce the Jubilee year.
