nogo:/ˈnoʊɡoʊ/| n.|禁止，不允许，不行|A no-go is something that is not allowed or possible. It can refer to a plan, action, or situation that is unacceptable or unfeasible.  |Example: The project was a no-go because of insufficient funding.  这个项目由于资金不足而行不通。  The police declared the area a no-go zone. 警方宣布该地区为禁区。|近义词：prohibition, ban, veto, taboo|反义词：go-ahead, permission, allowance|名词
