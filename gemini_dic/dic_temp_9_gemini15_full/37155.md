come-to-play:/kʌm tuː pleɪ/| v. |来玩|To arrive for the purpose of playing or engaging in recreational activities.  This is not a single dictionary-listed word, but a phrase.|例句：The children came-to-play at the park.孩子们来到公园玩耍。|例句：They came-to-play basketball at the gym. 他们来体育馆打篮球。|近义词: come to participate, arrive to play|反义词: leave, depart

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They come to play every Saturday. = 他们每个星期六都来玩。
We come to play in the garden. = 我们来花园里玩。


一般过去时:
They came to play yesterday. = 他们昨天来玩了。
She came to play with her friends. = 她来和朋友们一起玩了。


现在完成时:
They have come to play many times. = 他们来玩过很多次了。
We have come to play since morning. = 我们从早上就开始来玩了。


过去完成时:
They had come to play before it started raining. = 在开始下雨之前他们就已经来玩了。
She had come to play before I arrived. = 我到达之前她就来玩了。


将来时:
They will come to play tomorrow. = 他们明天会来玩。
We will come to play next week. = 我们下周会来玩。


虚拟语气:
If they came to play, I would be happy. = 如果他们来玩，我会很高兴。
I wish they came to play more often. = 我希望他们能更经常地来玩。


被动语态:  (This phrase doesn't naturally lend itself to the passive voice.)  A passive construction would be awkward and unnatural.


疑问句:
Will they come to play? = 他们会来玩吗？
Did they come to play? = 他们来玩了吗？


否定句:
They didn't come to play. = 他们没有来玩。
They will not come to play today. = 他们今天不会来玩。


PHRASE->
come to play together = 一起玩
come to play games = 来玩游戏
come to play outside = 来外面玩
come to play sports = 来做运动
come to play a role = 扮演一个角色
