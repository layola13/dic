ineligible:/ˌɪnˈɛlɪdʒəbəl/| adj.|不合格的，无资格的|Not meeting the required standards or qualifications.|He is ineligible to vote because he is not a citizen. 他没有资格投票，因为他不是公民。|eligible, qualified|eligible, qualified|形容词


例句：

Because of his criminal record, he is ineligible for the job.  因为他有犯罪记录，他不适合这份工作。

She was deemed ineligible for the scholarship due to her low GPA.  由于她的GPA低，她被认为不符合奖学金的资格。


近义词：disqualified, unsuitable, unqualified

反义词：eligible, qualified
