cognizance:/ˈkɒɡnɪzəns/| /ˈkɑːɡnɪzəns/ | n. |知道；认识；觉察；了解 |Knowledge, awareness, or perception of something.  It often implies a formal or official recognition. |例句：The court took cognizance of the defendant's previous convictions. 法院注意到了被告之前的定罪记录。

例句：He brought the matter to the cognizance of the authorities. 他将此事告知了当局。


近义词：awareness, knowledge, recognition, perception, understanding

反义词：ignorance, unawareness, obliviousness

词性：名词

词源：源自古法语 *connaissance，最终源自拉丁语 *cognoscere，意思是“认识，了解”。
