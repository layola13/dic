infuser:/ɪnˈfjuːzər/| n. |注入器；冲泡器；浸渍器|A device used to steep or infuse ingredients, typically in a liquid, to extract flavor or medicinal properties.  It can be a simple tea infuser or a more complex device for making herbal infusions.|Example: I used a tea infuser to brew my loose leaf tea. 我用茶叶过滤器冲泡散叶茶。|近义词：steeper, percolator|反义词：extractor(in some contexts)

词源:

The word "infuser" is derived from the verb "infuse," which comes from the Latin word "infundere," meaning "to pour in."  The suffix "-er" indicates an agent or instrument.  Therefore, an infuser is a device that pours in (flavor or other properties) into a liquid.
