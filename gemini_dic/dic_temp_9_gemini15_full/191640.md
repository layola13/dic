schoolwork:/ˈskuːlˌwɜːrk/| n.|学校作业，功课|Schoolwork refers to the work assigned to students by their teachers to be completed outside of class time.  It can include homework, assignments, projects, and other tasks. |例句：I have a lot of schoolwork to do this weekend.  = 这个周末我有很多学校作业要做。|例句：Her schoolwork is suffering because she's been spending too much time on social media. = 因为她花太多时间在社交媒体上，她的学业受到了影响。|近义词：homework, assignments, studies|反义词：leisure, recreation
