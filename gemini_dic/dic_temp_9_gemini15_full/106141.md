knitwear:/ˈnɪtwɛr/| n. |针织品，针织服装|Clothing made from knitted fabric.  Knitwear includes sweaters, cardigans, scarves, and other items made by knitting. |例句：She loves to wear comfortable knitwear in the winter. 她喜欢冬天穿舒适的针织衫。|例句：The shop sells a wide range of knitwear for men and women. 这家商店出售各种各样的男女针织服装。|近义词：knitted garments, knitted clothes|反义词：woven garments
