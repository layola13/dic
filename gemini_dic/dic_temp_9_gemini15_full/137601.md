nontriers:/ˈnɑːnˌtraɪərz/| n.|未尝试者；未尝试过的人|People who have not tried something, especially a new product, treatment, or experience.  This term is often used in marketing and research contexts to refer to the segment of the population that hasn't adopted a particular innovation or product.|Example:  The marketing campaign targeted nontriers to increase market penetration.  市场营销活动的目标是未尝试者，以提高市场渗透率。|近义词：nonadopters, holdouts, resisters|反义词：adopters, early adopters, users|名词
