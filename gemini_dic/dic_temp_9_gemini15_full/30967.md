chalazogamy:/ˌkæləˈzɒɡəmi/| n. |合点受精|Chalazogamy is a type of fertilization in seed plants where the pollen tube enters the ovule through the chalaza, rather than through the micropyle. This is a less common method of fertilization compared to porogamy.| Example:  Chalazogamy, a less common fertilization method, has been observed in a few plant families. 合点受精这种较不常见的受精方式，已在少数植物科中观察到。|近义词：porogamy (but with contrasting entry point)|反义词：porogamy (in terms of entry point)|名词
