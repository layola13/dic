what:/wɒt/ /wʌt/ | pron. |什么，什么东西，多么|  An interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something. It can refer to things, actions, reasons, or qualities. |例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

What time is it? 现在几点了？


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does she do for a living? = 她靠什么谋生？


一般过去时:
What did you eat for lunch? = 你午饭吃了什么？
What happened at the party? = 晚会上发生了什么事？


现在完成时:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she accomplished in her career? = 她在事业上取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you done before you called me? = 你打电话给我之前做了什么？
What had they already discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们已经讨论了什么？


将来时:
What will you do this weekend? = 你这个周末打算做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built in that area? = 那个区域正在建造什么？


疑问句:
What is the meaning of life? = 生命的意义是什么？
What kind of music do you like? = 你喜欢什么类型的音乐？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
He didn't say what he wanted. = 他没说他想什么。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What if...? = 如果...怎么样？
What for? = 为什么？为了什么？
No matter what = 无论如何
What's more = 而且；更重要的是

近义词：which (在选择范围已知的情况下)

反义词：无
词性：代词
