conceals:/kənˈsiːlz/| vt.|隐藏；掩盖；隐瞒|To hide or keep secret; to prevent from being seen, known, or discovered.|例句：She concealed her disappointment well. 她很好地掩饰了自己的失望。|例句：The magician concealed the rabbit in his hat. 魔术师把兔子藏在了帽子里。|近义词：hide, mask, obscure, suppress|反义词：reveal, disclose, uncover


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conceals his true feelings. = 他隐藏着他的真实感受。
She conceals her age. = 她隐瞒了自己的年龄。

一般过去时:
They concealed the evidence. = 他们隐瞒了证据。
He concealed the truth from his family. = 他向家人隐瞒了真相。

现在完成时:
She has concealed her identity for years. = 她多年来一直隐藏着自己的身份。
They have concealed the documents from the authorities. = 他们一直向当局隐瞒文件。

过去完成时:
He had concealed the letter before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他已经把信藏起来了。
She had concealed her illness from her friends. = 她之前一直对朋友隐瞒着她的病情。

将来时:
He will conceal the truth from the investigators. = 他将向调查人员隐瞒真相。
She will conceal her intentions for now. = 她暂时会隐瞒她的意图。

虚拟语气:
If he concealed the truth, he would be in trouble. = 如果他隐瞒真相，他就会有麻烦。
I wish I had concealed my feelings better. = 我真希望我当时能更好地掩饰我的感情。

被动语态:
The truth was concealed from the public. = 真相被隐瞒了公众。
The documents were concealed in a safe. = 文件被藏在一个保险箱里。

疑问句:
Does he conceal anything from you? = 他有没有对你隐瞒什么？
Did she conceal the truth about the accident? = 她有没有隐瞒事故的真相？

否定句:
He did not conceal his anger. = 他没有掩饰自己的愤怒。
She did not conceal her surprise. = 她没有掩饰她的惊讶。
