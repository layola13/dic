murre:/ˈmɜːr/|/ˈmʌr/| n. |海雀|A small, stout, black-and-white seabird of the auk family, with a short bill.  Murre are known for their cliff-nesting habits and their ability to dive deep for food.| Example: The murre colony on the cliff was a spectacular sight.  海崖上的海雀群落是一道壮观的景象。|Example: We saw many murres diving for fish in the ocean. 我们看到许多海雀在海里潜水捕鱼。|近义词：auk, guillemot|反义词：无
