humiliate:/hjuːˈmɪlieɪt/|/ˈhjuːmɪleɪt/ | vt. |使丢脸；使蒙羞；羞辱 | To make someone feel ashamed and stupid, especially publicly.  |The comedian's jokes were intended to humiliate the politician.  喜剧演员的玩笑是为了羞辱这位政治家。 |insult, degrade, mortify|honor, praise, exalt|动词


USAGE->

He humiliates his employees regularly. 他经常羞辱他的员工。
She humiliates herself by begging for money. 她通过乞讨来羞辱自己。

The incident humiliated him deeply. 那件事让他深感羞辱。

现在完成时:
