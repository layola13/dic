crossfit:/ˈkrɒsˌfɪt/| n. |交叉健身；交叉训练|A high-intensity fitness program involving a combination of exercises from various disciplines, such as weightlifting, gymnastics, and metabolic conditioning.|例句：He's been doing CrossFit for three years and has seen amazing results. 他已经进行交叉健身训练三年了，并且看到了惊人的效果。|近义词：high-intensity interval training (HIIT), strength and conditioning|反义词：sedentary lifestyle, inactivity|名词
