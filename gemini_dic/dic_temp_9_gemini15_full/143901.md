outpractices: /ˌaʊtˈpræktɪsɪz/ | v. |胜过，技艺胜过 | To surpass or excel in skill or performance through practice.  It implies consistent and superior practice leading to better results than others. |例句：She outpractices all her rivals in tennis. =她在网球方面技艺胜过所有对手。  He consistently outpractices his colleagues, leading to faster project completion. = 他总是比同事练习得更多，从而更快地完成项目。|近义词：outskill, outdo, surpass, excel | 反义词：underperform, lag behind |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outpractices his teammates every day. = 他每天都比他的队友练习得更多。
She outpractices her competitors in the marathon. = 她每天都在马拉松比赛中超越她的竞争对手。


一般过去时:
He outpracticed his opponent and won the match. = 他比对手练习得更多，赢得了比赛。
The team outpracticed the other team and won the championship. = 这支队伍比另一支队伍练习得更多，赢得了冠军。


现在完成时:
She has outpracticed everyone in the class and is now the top student. = 她比班上所有的人都练习得更多，现在是班里最好的学生。
They have outpracticed their rivals for months and are now confident of victory. = 他们已经比他们的对手练习了几个月，现在对胜利充满信心。


过去完成时:
He had outpracticed his rivals by the time the competition began. = 到比赛开始时，他已经比他的对手练习得更多了。
The team had outpracticed their opponents for weeks, which led to their success. = 这支队伍已经比他们的对手练习了几周，这导致了他们的成功。


将来时:
He will outpractice his opponents and win the gold medal. = 他将比他的对手练习得更多，并赢得金牌。
The team will outpractice the other teams and win the tournament. = 这支队伍将比其他队伍练习得更多，并赢得锦标赛。


虚拟语气:
If he had outpracticed his opponent, he would have won the game. = 如果他比对手练习得更多，他就赢了比赛。
If she outpracticed everyone else, she would be the champion. = 如果她比其他人练习得更多，她就会是冠军。


被动语态: (此词较少用被动语态)
The champion was often outpracticed in training sessions, but still won consistently. = 冠军在训练中经常被超过，但是仍然持续获胜。 (注意此句强调的是被超过，而非主动的outpractice)


疑问句:
Does he outpractice his rivals consistently? = 他是否总是比他的对手练习得更多？
Did the team outpractice their opponents in the final match? = 在决赛中，这支队伍是否比他们的对手练习得更多？


否定句:
He didn't outpractice his opponent, so he lost the game. = 他没有比他的对手练习得更多，所以输掉了比赛。
The team didn't outpractice the other team, and therefore didn't win the trophy. = 这支队伍没有比另一支队伍练习得更多，因此没有赢得奖杯。


PHRASE->
outpractice someone in (something): 在某方面胜过某人
consistently outpractice: 始终胜过
outpractice the competition: 在竞争中胜出
outpractice oneself:  超越自我 (提升自身水平)
to be outpracticed by: 被某人/某物在技能上超越
