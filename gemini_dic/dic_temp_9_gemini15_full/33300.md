chubby:/ˈtʃʌbi/| adj.|丰满的，胖乎乎的|  Having a plump and pleasingly rounded appearance, especially of a child or baby.  The word often carries a positive connotation, suggesting a healthy and cute fullness.|例句：The baby is so chubby and adorable.  这个婴儿胖乎乎的，非常可爱。

The chubby cheeks of the toddler made him look like a cherub.  幼儿胖乎乎的脸颊让他看起来像个小天使。|近义词：plump, round, stout, chubby-cheeked|反义词：thin, skinny, slender, gaunt

词源:
The word "chubby" likely originated from the Middle English word "chobbe," meaning "a lump or swelling."  This evolved over time to describe a pleasingly plump appearance.  The addition of "-y" created the adjectival form.
