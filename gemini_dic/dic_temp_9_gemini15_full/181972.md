remasticate:/ˌriːˈmæsṭɪkeɪt/| vt.|重新咀嚼；反复思考|To chew (food) again.  To think about something again and again.|例句：He remasticated the tough piece of meat. 他又嚼了嚼那块硬肉。

例句：She remasticated the problem, trying to find a new solution. 她反复思考这个问题，试图找到新的解决方法。|近义词：chew again, reconsider, ruminate|反义词：swallow, ignore
