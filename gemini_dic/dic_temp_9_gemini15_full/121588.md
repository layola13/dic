misoccupy:/ˌmɪsˈɒkjʊpaɪ/ | vt. |滥用；误用；占用（时间、空间等）不当|To use or occupy something wrongly or inappropriately.|The company misoccupied its resources on a failing project. 公司将资源错误地用在一个失败的项目上。|近义词：misuse, misapply, waste|反义词：utilize, employ effectively, optimize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They misoccupy their time watching TV. = 他们把时间浪费在看电视上。
He misoccupies the storage space with unnecessary items. = 他用不必要的物品占据了存储空间。


一般过去时:
She misoccupied her talents by taking a low-level job. = 她接受一份低级工作，错过了发挥才能的机会。
The government misoccupied public funds on unnecessary projects. = 政府将公共资金错误地用于不必要的项目上。
