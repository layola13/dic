self-dejections:/ˌsɛlf dɪˈdʒɛkʃənz/| n.|自我贬低，自我轻视|The act of belittling or deprecating oneself; expressions of self-disparagement.|例句：Her constant self-dejections made it hard for others to believe in her abilities.  她不断的自我贬低使得别人很难相信她的能力。

例句：His self-dejections were a mask for his deep-seated insecurity. 他的自我贬低是他根深蒂固的不安全感的掩饰。


近义词：self-criticism, self-deprecation, self-denigration, putting oneself down


反义词：self-aggrandizement, self-promotion, boasting, self-importance


词性：名词
