gabbled:/ˈɡæbəld/| v.|喋喋不休地说，含糊不清地说 |To speak rapidly and indistinctly; to chatter. |例句：He gabbled something about a meeting and rushed off. 他含糊不清地说了一些关于会议的事情，然后匆匆离开了。|例句：The speaker gabbled so fast that I couldn't understand a word. 说话人说得太快了，我一个字也听不懂。|近义词：mumbled, slurred, stammered |反义词：articulated, enunciated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gabbles constantly. = 他不停地喋喋不休。
She gabbles when she's nervous. = 她紧张的时候说话含糊不清。

一般过去时:
He gabbled his excuses and left. = 他含糊地说了几句借口就离开了。
She gabbled the instructions so quickly I missed some steps. = 她快速地说着指令，我错过了一些步骤。

现在完成时:
He has gabbled through three presentations today. = 他今天已经含糊地完成了三个演示。
She has gabbled so much I've got a headache. = 她说了那么多，我头都疼了。

过去完成时:
He had gabbled the entire speech before the microphone was turned on. = 麦克风打开之前，他已经含糊地讲完了整篇演讲。
She had gabbled the recipe so fast, I couldn't write it down. = 她把食谱说得太快了，我记不下来。

将来时:
He will gabble through the next meeting. = 他会在下一次会议上喋喋不休。
She will likely gabble her response to the question. = 她可能会含糊地回答这个问题。


被动语态:
The speech was gabbled through in under ten minutes. = 演讲在不到十分钟的时间里就含糊地完成了。
The instructions were gabbled, leaving many confused. = 指令说得含糊不清，导致许多人感到困惑。

疑问句:
Did he gabble the whole story? = 他是不是含糊地讲了整个故事？
Was she gabbling because she was nervous? = 她是不是因为紧张而说话含糊不清？

否定句:
He didn't gabble; he spoke clearly and concisely. = 他没有喋喋不休；他说话清晰简洁。
She didn't gabble, but she did speak rather quickly. = 她没有说话含糊不清，但她确实说得很快。
