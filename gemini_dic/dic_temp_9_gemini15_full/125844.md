mus:/mʌs/| n.|肌肉，力量 |muscle tissue that causes movement of the body|例句：He flexed his muscles. 他炫耀他的肌肉。|近义词：muscle, brawn, sinew|反义词: weakness, frailty|名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The muscles in my legs are tired. = 我腿部的肌肉很疲劳。
He uses his muscles to lift heavy weights. = 他用他的肌肉举起重物。

一般过去时:
I strained my muscles during the workout. = 我在锻炼时拉伤了肌肉。
She felt her muscles ache after the long run. = 长跑后她感到肌肉酸痛。

现在完成时:
I have built up my muscles through regular exercise. = 我通过规律的锻炼锻炼出了肌肉。
She has lost some muscle mass due to illness. = 由于生病，她失去了一些肌肉质量。

过去完成时:
He had already torn his muscles before the competition. = 他在比赛前就已经撕裂了肌肉。
They had overworked their muscles leading to severe pain. = 他们过度使用肌肉导致剧烈疼痛。

将来时:
I will strengthen my muscles by lifting weights. = 我将通过举重来增强肌肉。
She will likely experience muscle soreness after the marathon. = 马拉松后她可能会感到肌肉酸痛。

虚拟语气:
If I had stronger muscles, I could lift that heavy box. = 如果我有更强壮的肌肉，我就能举起那个重箱子。
I wish I had more muscle mass. = 我希望我有更多的肌肉质量。

被动语态:
His muscles were stretched during the yoga session. = 他的肌肉在瑜伽课上被拉伸了。
The muscles are often damaged by overuse. = 肌肉经常因过度使用而受损。

疑问句:
Do you feel any pain in your muscles? = 你感觉肌肉有任何疼痛吗？
Are your muscles sore after the workout? = 锻炼后你的肌肉酸痛吗？

否定句:
He doesn't have much muscle definition. = 他没有多少肌肉线条。
She didn't feel any muscle strain after the climb. = 攀登后她没有感到任何肌肉拉伤。
