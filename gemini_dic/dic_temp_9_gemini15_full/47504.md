dearies:/ˈdɪəriːz/| n. |亲爱的（复数）|A term of endearment used to address multiple people, typically children or loved ones. It is a plural form of "dear."| Examples: "Come here, my dearies." "My dearies, let's have some tea." |近义词：darlings, sweethearts, loves|反义词：None (it's a term of endearment, not descriptive)|名词
