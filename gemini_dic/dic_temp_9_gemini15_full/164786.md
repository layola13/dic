prefertilizing: /ˌpriːˈfɜːrtɪlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 预先施肥；预先受精 |  The act of fertilizing before the normal time or process.  This term is primarily used in agriculture and biology, referring to the application of fertilizer before planting or the preparation of gametes for fertilization. | Example:  Prefertilizing the soil ensures a healthy crop yield. = 预先施肥能确保获得丰收。 The researchers studied the effects of prefertilizing eggs on embryonic development. = 研究人员研究了预先受精卵对胚胎发育的影响。 | 近义词：pre-fertilization, pre-treatment (in specific contexts) | 反义词：post-fertilizing
