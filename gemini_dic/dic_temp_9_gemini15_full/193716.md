self-congratulations:/ˌsɛlfkənˌɡrædʒʊˈleɪʃənz/| n. |自我祝贺；沾沾自喜|The act of praising or congratulating oneself, often excessively.  It implies an excessive or unwarranted sense of pride in one's achievements. |例句：His self-congratulations after winning the small prize were embarrassing to watch.  他赢得小奖后自我吹嘘的样子让人尴尬。


例句：Her self-congratulations on her promotion were a bit much. 她对自己升职的自我祝贺有点过了。


近义词：self-praise, boasting, vainglory, smugness


反义词：self-deprecation, humility, modesty


词性：名词
