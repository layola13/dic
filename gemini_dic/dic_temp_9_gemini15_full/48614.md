defector:/dɪˈfɛktə(r)/| n. |叛逃者，变节者 |A person who has abandoned their country or cause in favor of an opposing one.  A defector often seeks political asylum or refuge in another country. |Example:  The defector revealed state secrets to the enemy.  这个叛逃者向敌人泄露了国家机密。


The defector sought refuge in a neighboring country.  这位叛逃者在邻国寻求庇护。|近义词：renegade, deserter, traitor |反义词：loyalist, supporter
