onomatopoeia:/ˌɒnəˌmætəˈpiːə/| n. |拟声词|The formation of a word from a sound associated with what is named (e.g., cuckoo, sizzle ).  Onomatopoeia is a word that imitates the natural sounds of a thing. It creates a sound effect that mimics the thing described, making the description more expressive and interesting.  |例句：The bees buzzed lazily in the warm afternoon sun. 蜜蜂在温暖的午后阳光下懒洋洋地嗡嗡叫。


The crackling fire warmed the room.噼啪作响的火温暖了房间。|近义词：sound imitation, mimetic word |反义词:None
