polyptoton: /pɒlɪpˈtoʊtɒn/ | n. | 多词性复词法 |

英文解析：Polyptoton is a rhetorical device that involves the repetition of a word or words derived from the same root but with different grammatical forms (e.g., noun, verb, adjective, adverb).  It adds emphasis and stylistic flair to writing or speech.  The effect is often one of heightened intensity or a sense of completeness.

例句：

The general’s general strategy was a general failure.  将军的总体战略是一场彻底的失败。


近义词：同根词重复 (同义词更侧重于意义上的重复，而polyptoton更侧重于词形的变化)

反义词：无


词性：名词
