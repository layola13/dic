cosmopolitan:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtən/| /ˌkɑːzməˈpɑːlətən/ | adj., n. |世界性的；国际性的；大都市的；见多识广的；世界主义者 |

adj. (形容词):
* **英文解析:**  Belonging to or characteristic of the whole world; not limited to a single place or nation.  Having wide international experience and interests.

* **例句:**
    The city boasts a cosmopolitan atmosphere, attracting people from all over the globe.  这座城市拥有国际化的氛围，吸引着来自世界各地的人们。
    She has a cosmopolitan outlook, having lived and worked in several different countries. 她有着世界性的视野，曾在几个不同的国家生活和工作。


n. (名词):
* **英文解析:** A cosmopolitan person; someone who is sophisticated and has a wide range of interests.

* **例句:**
    He's a true cosmopolitan, equally at home in London, New York, and Tokyo. 他是个真正的世界公民，在伦敦、纽约和东京都一样自在。
    The conference attracted cosmopolitans from diverse backgrounds and professions.  这次会议吸引了来自不同背景和职业的世界主义者。


近义词: (adj.) international, global, worldly, sophisticated, urbane
近义词: (n.) global citizen, world traveler


反义词: (adj.) provincial, parochial, insular, local, narrow-minded


词源:
From Late Middle English, from Old French *cosmopolitain*, from Late Latin *cosmopolita*, from Greek *kosmopolites*, from *kosmos* ("world") + *politēs* ("citizen").  Literally, "citizen of the world."
