marlins:/ˈmɑːrlɪnz/| n. |马林鱼|Large, powerful game fish with a long, pointed bill.  Marlins are highly prized by sport fishermen for their size and fighting ability. They are found in tropical and subtropical oceans worldwide. |例句：The angler fought the marlin for hours before finally reeling it in.  钓者与马林鱼搏斗数小时后才最终将其卷起。

例句：Several species of marlins inhabit the warm waters of the Pacific Ocean. 太平洋的温暖水域中栖息着几种马林鱼。


近义词：billfish (广义上的长嘴鱼类)| 反义词：无


词源:  The word "marlin" derives from the Spanish word "marlín," which itself is likely of uncertain origin, possibly related to an earlier word for a spear or pike, reflecting the fish's spear-like bill.
