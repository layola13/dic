近义词：marksmen, snipers, expert shooters |反义词：poor shots, inaccurate shooters

词源:
The word "sharpshooter" is a compound word.  "Sharp" means accurate and precise, while "shooter" refers to someone who shoots a gun.  Therefore, a sharpshooter is literally someone who shoots accurately.  The term's origins lie in the 19th century, reflecting the increasing importance of accurate marksmanship in warfare and competitive shooting.
