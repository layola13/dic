off-key:/ˌɒfˈkiː/ /ˌɑːfˈkiː/| adj.|走调的，不和谐的|Not in tune or harmony; discordant.  The singer was hopelessly off-key.  |例句：The choir was slightly off-key during the performance.  合唱团在演出中稍微走调了。

例句：His guitar playing was completely off-key, making the song unbearable. 他的吉他演奏完全走调，使这首歌难以忍受。


近义词：out of tune, discordant, dissonant, jarring

反义词：in tune, harmonious, concordant
