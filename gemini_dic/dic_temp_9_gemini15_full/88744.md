hock:/hɒk/| n. v.|  踝关节；抵押；卖；大声叫喊|The word "hock" has multiple meanings, stemming from different origins. As a noun, it can refer to the joint between the leg and the foot of an animal (especially a horse), or to a type of wine. As a verb, it refers to to pawn or mortgage something, or to make a harsh, throaty sound.|例句：The horse injured its hock. 这匹马伤了它的踝关节。  He hocked his watch to pay his debts. 他典当了他的手表来偿还债务。|近义词：(n.) ankle, joint; (v.) pawn, mortgage|反义词：(v.) redeem, buy back|名词，动词
