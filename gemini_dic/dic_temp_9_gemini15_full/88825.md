hoggerel:/ˈhɒɡərəl/ | n. |小山羊，小绵羊 |A hoggerel is a young sheep, typically one that is less than a year old and has not yet been shorn.  It's a regional term, primarily used in Scotland and northern England.  |Example: The farmer carefully counted his hoggerels before moving them to the pasture.  农夫在把小山羊移到牧场之前仔细清点了一下。|近义词：lamb, sheepling |反义词：ewe (adult female sheep), ram (adult male sheep)
