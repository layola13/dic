otolithic:/ˌoʊtəˈlɪθɪk/| adj.|耳石的；与耳石有关的|Relating to or denoting otoliths. Otoliths are calcium carbonate structures found in the inner ear of vertebrates that are involved in balance and orientation.|The otolithic organs are crucial for maintaining balance. 耳石器官对于维持平衡至关重要。|vestibular, labyrinthine| (no direct antonym)|
