overdiversify:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪ/ | vt. |过度多元化，过度分散投资 | To diversify to an excessive degree, often resulting in reduced efficiency or increased risk.  | 例句：He overdiversified his investment portfolio, diluting his returns. 他过度分散了他的投资组合，降低了他的回报。 | 例句：The company's attempt to overdiversify its product line led to confusion and lower profits.  公司的产品线过度多元化尝试导致了混乱和利润下降。| 近义词：overextend, spread too thin | 反义词：focus, concentrate, specialize | 动词
