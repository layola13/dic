desertful: /ˈdɛzərtfʊl/ | adj. | 荒凉的，沙漠般的 |  Filled with or characteristic of a desert; barren and desolate.  |例句：The desertful landscape offered little hope of survival.  荒凉的景象几乎不给人留下生存的希望。
The desertful expanse stretched endlessly before them.  广阔无垠的沙漠景象在他们面前无尽地延伸着。|近义词：barren, desolate, arid, empty |反义词：fertile, lush, abundant | 词性:形容词
