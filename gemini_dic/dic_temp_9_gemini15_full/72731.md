foeman:/ˈfoʊmən/| n. |仇敌，敌人 |A foeman is an enemy or opponent, especially in battle.  The term carries a more archaic or literary connotation, suggesting a worthy adversary rather than simply an enemy.|例句：He faced his foeman bravely in the duel. 他在决斗中勇敢地面对他的敌人。|例句：The two foemen fought fiercely for the championship. 这两位对手为争夺冠军而激烈搏斗。|近义词：enemy, opponent, adversary, antagonist |反义词：ally, friend, comrade
