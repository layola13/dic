recomplications:/ˌriːkɑːmplɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 再并发症，新的并发症 | New or recurring complications that arise after an initial treatment or recovery period.  These complications can be related to the original condition or a consequence of the treatment itself. | Example: Despite initial success, recomplications arose, necessitating further surgery.  尽管最初手术成功，但后来又出现了新的并发症，需要再次手术。|近义词：further complications, renewed complications, secondary complications |反义词：recovery, resolution, improvement |名词
