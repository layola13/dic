low-fat: /ˈloʊ fæt/ | adj. | 低脂的 | Containing a small amount of fat.  This term is commonly used to describe food products that have been processed to reduce their fat content.  | 例句：She prefers low-fat yogurt to full-fat yogurt. 她更喜欢低脂酸奶而不是全脂酸奶。

She chose a low-fat recipe for the dinner party. 为了晚宴，她选择了一个低脂食谱。| 近义词：lean, reduced-fat | 反义词：high-fat, full-fat | 形容词
