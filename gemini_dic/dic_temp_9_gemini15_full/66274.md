excogitate:/ɪkˈskɑːdʒɪteɪt/| vt.| 仔细考虑；深思熟虑；想出（计划等）|To think out or devise (a plan, etc.); to invent.  It implies a long and careful process of thought.|例句：The architect excogitated a brilliant design for the new museum. 建筑师精心设计了新博物馆的方案。

例句：He spent weeks excogitating a solution to the problem. 他花了几个星期的时间仔细考虑解决这个问题的办法。


近义词：conceive, devise, invent, formulate, ponder, contemplate, deliberate


反义词：improvise, act impulsively


词性:动词
