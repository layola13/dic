reconfirm:/ˌriːkənˈfɜːrm/|/ˌriːkənˈfɝːm/| vt.|再次确认；重新确认 |To confirm something again, especially to ensure that it is still correct or valid.  It implies a previous confirmation has been made, and this action is a double-check. |例句：Please reconfirm your flight details before you go to the airport. 请在去机场之前再次确认您的航班信息。

例句：We need to reconfirm the reservation for the conference room.我们需要再次确认会议室的预订。


近义词：verify, confirm, check, double-check

反义词：deny, refute, invalidate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reconfirm the order every week. = 他们每周都重新确认订单。
We reconfirm the booking details before departure. = 我们在出发前再次确认预订详情。


一般过去时:
He reconfirmed his attendance at the meeting yesterday. = 他昨天再次确认了参加会议。
She reconfirmed the address with the client. = 她与客户再次确认了地址。
