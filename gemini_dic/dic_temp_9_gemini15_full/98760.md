intagliated:/ɪnˈtæliˌeɪtɪd/| adj.|雕刻的；凹雕的|Engraved or carved into a surface, especially a gemstone or other hard material, so that the design is sunk below the surface.  The process creates a recessed design.|例句：The ring featured an intagliated gemstone depicting a mythical creature. 这枚戒指上镶嵌着一块刻有神话生物的宝石。

例句：The ancient artifact was beautifully intagliated with intricate patterns.  这件古代文物上精细地雕刻着复杂的图案。


近义词：engraved, carved, incised, embossed (as an antonym, if referring to raised relief)

反义词：embossed (raised relief), cameo (raised relief)

词性:形容词
