homeboys:/ˈhoʊmˌbɔɪz/| n.|哥们儿，朋友，老乡|A homeboy is a male friend, often from the same neighborhood or social group.  The term often carries connotations of loyalty and shared background. It's primarily used in informal settings.|例句：We're all homeboys from the same block. 我们都是同一个街区的哥们儿。

例句：He considers his childhood friends his homeboys for life. 他把他的童年朋友们视为一生的兄弟。


近义词：buddies, pals, friends, mates, brothers


反义词：enemies, rivals, strangers

词性：名词
