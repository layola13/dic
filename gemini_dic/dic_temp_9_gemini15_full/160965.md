postabdomen:/ˌpoʊstˈæbdəmən/ | n. |后腹部，后体 |The postabdomen is the posterior part of the abdomen in some arthropods, particularly crustaceans and insects.  It often includes the telson and uropods.  This region is crucial for locomotion, sensory perception, and reproduction in these organisms.  |例句：The postabdomen of the lobster is equipped with powerful swimmerets for propulsion.  龙虾的后腹部装备有强大的游泳足用于推进。

The postabdomen of this insect species is significantly modified for mating.  这个昆虫物种的后腹部被显著地改变用于交配。|近义词：rear abdomen, posterior abdomen |反义词：prothorax, head
