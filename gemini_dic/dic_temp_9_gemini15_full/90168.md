hotline: /ˈhɑːtlaɪn/ | n. |热线电话，直接线路| A direct telephone line, usually for emergencies or urgent matters.  |例句：The suicide hotline offers immediate support to those in crisis.  自杀热线为危机中的人提供即时支持。|例句：The company has a hotline for customers to report problems. 公司设有热线供客户举报问题。|近义词：direct line, emergency number |反义词：indirect line
