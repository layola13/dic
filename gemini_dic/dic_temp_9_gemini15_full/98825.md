intelligence: /ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns/ | n. |智力，智慧，情报 | The ability to acquire and apply knowledge and skills.  It encompasses various cognitive abilities, including learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and memory. |  例句：Her intelligence allowed her to solve the complex puzzle quickly.  她的智慧使她能够快速解开复杂的谜题。

例句：The police are gathering intelligence on the criminal organization. 警方正在收集有关犯罪组织的情报。


近义词：intellect, understanding, acumen, brains, wit | 反义词：stupidity, ignorance, foolishness | 词性：名词
