rephrased:/ˌriːˈfreɪzd/|v.|改述，改写，重新表述|To express something in different words, often to make it clearer or more concise.|例句：I rephrased my answer to make it easier to understand.  我改述了我的回答以便更容易理解。|近义词：reword, paraphrase, restate|反义词：misstate, misrepresent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rephrases his sentences frequently. = 他经常改写他的句子。
She rephrases difficult concepts for her students. = 她为学生们重新表述了难懂的概念。

一般过去时:
The editor rephrased the article to improve clarity. = 编辑改写了这篇文章以提高清晰度。
He rephrased his request to be more polite. = 他重新表述了他的请求，使其更礼貌。

现在完成时:
I have rephrased the instructions several times. = 我已经多次改写了说明。
She has rephrased the email to avoid any misunderstandings. = 她已经改写了邮件以避免任何误解。

过去完成时:
He had rephrased his argument before presenting it. = 他在陈述论点之前已经重新表述过。
They had rephrased their proposal to address the concerns raised. = 他们已经改写了他们的提案以解决提出的担忧。

将来时:
I will rephrase my question if it's unclear. = 如果不清楚，我会重新表述我的问题。
She will rephrase the instructions for the new employees. = 她将为新员工改写说明。

虚拟语气:
If I had rephrased my email earlier, there would have been less confusion. = 如果我早点改写我的邮件，就不会有那么多的困惑了。
I wish I had rephrased my response more diplomatically. = 我希望我当时能更委婉地回应。

被动语态:
The statement was rephrased to be less offensive. = 该声明被改写得没那么冒犯人了。
The instructions have been rephrased for better understanding. = 说明已被改写以更好地理解。

疑问句:
Have you rephrased the report yet? = 你已经改写报告了吗？
Should I rephrase my comment for better clarity? = 我应该改写我的评论以使其更清晰吗？

否定句:
He didn't rephrase his statement, despite the criticism. = 尽管受到了批评，他也没有改写他的声明。
She didn't rephrase the question, leaving it ambiguous. = 她没有改写问题，让它含糊不清。
