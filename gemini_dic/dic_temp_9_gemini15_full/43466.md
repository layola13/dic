/ˈkreɪsɪs/| n. |混合，合并；(语言)音节融合|The blending of two words into one, often resulting in a change of pronunciation or spelling.  This is a phenomenon seen in linguistics, particularly in the formation of new words. For example, the word "breakfast" is a crasis of "break" and "fast".|The word "smog" is a crasis of "smoke" and "fog".|近义词：blend, fusion, amalgamation |反义词：separation, division |名词
