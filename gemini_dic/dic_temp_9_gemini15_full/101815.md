irritant:/ˈɪrɪtənt/| n. adj.|刺激物；令人恼怒的人或事|Something that irritates or causes irritation.  An irritant can be a physical substance or a situation or person.|The smoke was a respiratory irritant.  (烟是呼吸道刺激物。) His constant complaining was a real irritant. (他不断的抱怨真是让人恼火。) |近义词：annoyance, bother, nuisance, provocation|反义词：soothing, calming, comforting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
That noise is a real irritant. = 那个噪音真让人烦。
This chemical is a strong irritant to the skin. = 这种化学物质对皮肤有很强的刺激性。


一般过去时:
The dust was an irritant to his lungs. = 灰尘刺激了他的肺部。
Her behavior was a constant irritant throughout the meeting. = 在整个会议期间，她的行为一直令人恼火。


现在完成时:
His constant negativity has been a major irritant in our relationship. = 他持续的消极情绪一直是我们关系中一个主要的刺激因素。
The rash has been an irritant for several days. = 皮疹已经刺激了好几天了。


过去完成时:
The loud music had been an irritant all evening. = 吵闹的音乐整个晚上都让人烦躁。
Before the incident, their arguments had been a constant irritant in their marriage. = 事故发生前，他们的争吵一直是他们婚姻中一个持续的刺激因素。


将来时:
That new policy will be a major irritant for many businesses. = 那项新政策将对许多企业造成很大的困扰。
The construction work will be an irritant for the next few months. = 建筑工程在接下来的几个月里都会让人感到烦躁。


虚拟语气:
If that noise were not such an irritant, I could concentrate better. = 如果那个噪音不是那么烦人，我就能更集中注意力了。
I wish that constant interruption wasn't such an irritant. = 我希望不断被打扰不会这么让人烦。


被动语态:
His remarks were considered an irritant by many people. = 许多人认为他的话令人反感。
The substance was identified as a skin irritant. = 该物质被鉴定为皮肤刺激物。


疑问句:
Is the smoke from that factory an irritant? = 那家工厂的烟雾是刺激物吗？
Is his behavior an irritant to you? = 他的行为让你感到恼火吗？


否定句:
The new software isn't an irritant; it's actually quite helpful. = 新软件并没有让人感到烦，它实际上相当有用。
The cream is not an irritant to sensitive skin. = 这种面霜不会刺激敏感肌肤。


PHRASE->
