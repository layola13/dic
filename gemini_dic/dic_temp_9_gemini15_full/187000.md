risqué:/rɪˈskeɪ/| adj.| 暧昧的；令人不安的；不雅的 | Suggestive of or tending to sexual impropriety; mildly indecent.  The word implies a degree of impropriety that is more suggestive than overtly explicit.  It often refers to something that is daring or provocative, pushing the boundaries of what is considered socially acceptable. |例句：Her jokes were a little risqué for a family gathering.她的笑话对于家庭聚会来说有点露骨。

The film's risqué scenes were cut before its release. 这部电影中一些暧昧的场景在上映前被剪掉了。


近义词：suggestive, provocative, daring, indecent, racy, spicy |反义词：decent, proper, modest, innocent


词性:形容词

词源:
