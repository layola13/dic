mistutor:/ˈmɪsˌtjuːtər/| n. |糟糕的导师；不称职的老师|A mistutor is a teacher or instructor who is ineffective, incompetent, or otherwise provides poor instruction.  They may lack the necessary skills, knowledge, or pedagogical approach to effectively teach their students.  This term implies a negative judgment on the quality of their teaching. |Example:  The students complained about their mistutor's lack of preparation and unclear explanations.  学生们抱怨他们的糟糕导师准备不足且解释不清。|近义词：bad teacher, incompetent instructor, ineffective tutor|反义词：excellent teacher, skilled instructor, effective tutor|名词
