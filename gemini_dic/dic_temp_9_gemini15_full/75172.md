frivolously:/ˈfrɪvəl əsli/| adv.|轻率地；不严肃地；随意地 | acting in a way that shows a lack of seriousness or sense of responsibility; playfully;  lightheartedly. |例句：He frivolously spent his inheritance. 他轻率地挥霍了他的遗产。

例句：She frivolously dismissed his concerns. 她轻率地驳回了他的担忧。


近义词：recklessly, carelessly, flippantly, thoughtlessly, lightly


反义词：seriously, responsibly, carefully, thoughtfully


词性：副词
