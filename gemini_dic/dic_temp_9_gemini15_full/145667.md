overcontrolled:/ˌoʊvər kənˈtroʊld/| adj.|过度控制的，控制过度的| excessively controlled; showing the effects of excessive control.  The term often implies negative consequences, such as anxiety, rigidity, or lack of spontaneity.|例句：His overcontrolled behavior suggested deep-seated anxiety.他的过度控制的行为暗示着根深蒂固的焦虑。

例句：The overcontrolled environment stifled creativity. 过度控制的环境扼杀了创造力。


近义词：restrictive, repressed, stifled, rigid, uptight

反义词：uncontrolled, spontaneous, free, relaxed, uninhibited
