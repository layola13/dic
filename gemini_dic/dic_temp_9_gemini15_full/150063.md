palsied:/ˈpɔːlziːd/| adj.|瘫痪的；麻痹的；颤抖的|Affected by palsy; paralyzed; trembling.  The term often implies a shaking or trembling, rather than complete paralysis.|例句：His palsied hand trembled as he reached for the cup.  他颤抖着瘫痪的手去拿杯子。

例句：The old woman's palsied fingers fumbled with her knitting needles. 老妇人颤抖的指头笨拙地摆弄着她的编织针。


近义词：paralyzed, crippled, trembling, shaking, weak

反义词：strong, healthy, robust, vigorous


词性:形容词
