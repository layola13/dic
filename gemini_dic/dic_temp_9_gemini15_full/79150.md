glady:/ˈɡlædli/| adv.|乐意地，欣然地|Gladly is an adverb meaning willingly or cheerfully. It expresses a positive and enthusiastic acceptance of a request or suggestion.|例句：I'd gladly help you with your project. 我很乐意帮你做你的项目。|例句：She gladly accepted the invitation. 她欣然接受了邀请。|近义词：willingly, readily, cheerfully|反义词：reluctantly, unwillingly
