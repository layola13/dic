overeffusively: /ˌoʊvərɪˈfjuːzɪvli/ | adv. | 过分热情地；过于夸张地 | excessively effusive; demonstrative to an excessive degree. | 例句：He greeted her overeffusively, showering her with compliments. 他过分热情地迎接她，对她赞不绝口。  She thanked him overeffusively for the small favor. 她对他的小恩小惠表示了过分的感谢。| 近义词：excessively, extravagantly, gushingly | 反义词：reservedly, restrainedly, calmly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it modifies verbs.  There are no specific tenses to conjugate with it.  It's used to describe *how* an action is performed.

She spoke overeffusively about her trip. = 她对她的旅行夸夸其谈。
He laughed overeffusively at the joke. = 他对那个笑话笑得太过夸张。
They reacted overeffusively to the good news. = 他们对这个好消息反应过度热情。
He apologized overeffusively for his mistake. = 他对自己的错误表示了过分道歉。
She embraced him overeffusively upon his return. = 他回来时，她热情地拥抱了他。
