postings:/ˈpoʊstɪŋz/| n. |帖子；职位；公布；记账|Items posted online or in a public place, a job opening, the act of recording entries in a ledger.  |例句：The postings on the forum were mostly positive.  论坛上的帖子大多是积极的。

例句：The company is looking for postings for software engineers. 公司正在寻找软件工程师的职位空缺。


近义词：entries, announcements, advertisements, listings, placements|反义词：removals, deletions


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company posts job postings regularly. = 公司定期发布招聘信息。
He posts his thoughts on social media daily. = 他每天都在社交媒体上发布他的想法。


一般过去时:
She posted the announcement on the bulletin board. = 她把公告贴在了布告栏上。
They posted the financial reports last week. = 他们上周发布了财务报告。


现在完成时:
He has posted several articles on his blog. = 他在他的博客上发表了几篇文章。
They have posted the meeting minutes online. = 他们已将会议记录发布到网上。


过去完成时:
She had posted the letter before he called. = 他打电话之前，她已经寄出了信。
They had posted the results before the press conference. = 他们在新闻发布会之前已经公布了结果。


将来时:
We will post the updates tomorrow. = 我们明天将发布更新。
They will post the new policy next week. = 他们下周将发布新政策。


虚拟语气:
If I had posted the notice earlier, more people would have seen it. = 如果我早点发布通知，更多的人就会看到它。
I wish I had posted the pictures sooner. = 我希望我早点发布照片。


被动语态:
The results were posted on the website. = 结果已发布在网站上。
The job postings are being reviewed by the HR department. = 招聘信息正在由人力资源部审查。


疑问句:
Where were the notices posted? = 通知贴在哪里？
When will the exam results be posted? = 考试结果什么时候公布？


否定句:
He didn't post the comment. = 他没有发表评论。
They haven't posted the new schedule yet. = 他们还没有发布新的时间表。
