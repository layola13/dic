hanks:/hæŋks/| n.|感谢；谢谢|Thanks expresses gratitude or appreciation. It can be used informally or formally, depending on the context.|例句：Thanks for your help. 谢谢你帮忙。|例句：Thank you for your kind words. 谢谢你的好意。|近义词： gratitude, appreciation, thank you|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I thank you for your kindness. = 我感谢你的好意。
We thank them for their support. = 我们感谢他们的支持。

一般过去时:
He thanked her for the gift. = 他感谢她送的礼物。
They thanked us for our help. = 他们感谢我们的帮助。

现在完成时:
I have thanked them already. = 我已经感谢过他们了。
She has thanked everyone for their contributions. = 她已经感谢了所有人的贡献。

过去完成时:
He had thanked her before leaving. = 他在离开之前已经感谢了她。
They had thanked the staff for their hard work. = 他们已经感谢了员工的辛勤工作。

将来时:
I will thank them later. = 我稍后会感谢他们。
She will thank him for his assistance. = 她会感谢他的帮助。

虚拟语气:
I would thank him if I saw him. = 如果我见到他，我会感谢他。
She wished she had thanked him earlier. = 她希望她早点感谢他。

被动语态:
They were thanked for their service. = 他们因他们的服务而受到感谢。
The volunteers were thanked for their hard work. = 志愿者们因他们的辛勤工作而受到感谢。

疑问句:
Did you thank him? = 你感谢他了吗？
Have you thanked her yet? = 你感谢她了吗？

否定句:
I didn't thank him for his help. = 我没有感谢他的帮助。
She hasn't thanked them yet. = 她还没有感谢他们。


PHRASE->
Thanks a bunch! = 非常感谢！
Thanks a lot! = 非常感谢！
Thanks so much! = 非常感谢！
Thanks for nothing! = (讽刺)一点都没用！
Thanks anyway. = 无论如何还是要谢谢你。
