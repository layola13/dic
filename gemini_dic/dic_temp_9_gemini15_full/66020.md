/ˈɛɡzæmplɚ/ | n. | 例子，示例 | An exampler is something that serves as a typical example or model.  It illustrates or demonstrates a particular point or concept. | This painting is a fine exampler of Impressionist art. 这幅画是印象派艺术的绝佳范例。 | model, illustration, instance, specimen |  (无明确反义词，可根据语境使用"non-example"或"counter-example") | 名词
