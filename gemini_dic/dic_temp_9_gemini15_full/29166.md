cashabilities:/ˌkæʃəˈbɪlətiz/| n.| 可兑现能力，变现能力 |The ability to convert assets into cash quickly and easily.  This refers to the ease and speed with which something can be turned into cash.  It often applies to assets, investments, or even business opportunities.|例句：The company's high cashabilities allowed it to quickly meet its financial obligations. = 这家公司很强的变现能力使其能够迅速履行其财务义务。

The startup's strong cashabilities impressed investors. = 这家初创公司的强大变现能力给投资者留下了深刻印象。|近义词：liquidity, monetizability|反义词：illiquidity, non-monetizability|名词
