chateau:/ʃæˈtoʊ/ | n. |城堡；庄园；酒庄 | A chateau is a large French country house or castle, often associated with vineyards and wine production.  |例句：We toured the magnificent chateau and sampled its award-winning wines. 我们参观了宏伟的城堡，并品尝了它屡获殊荣的葡萄酒。|例句：The chateau boasts stunning architecture and breathtaking views. 这座城堡拥有令人惊叹的建筑和令人叹为观止的景色。|近义词：castle, mansion, manor house |反义词：hut, shack
