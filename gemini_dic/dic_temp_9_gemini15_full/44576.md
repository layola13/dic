crowd:/kraʊd/ | n. |人群，大众 | A large number of people gathered together.  |例句：A large crowd gathered to watch the parade.  一大群人聚集在一起观看游行。|近义词： throng, multitude, mass |反义词： solitude, loneliness | 名词
