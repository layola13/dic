hisher:/ˈhɪzər/ | pron. |他的/她的；他/她的 | A contraction of "his" and "her," used informally to refer to a person's possession or relationship regardless of gender.  It avoids the need to specify gender or use awkward phrasing like "his or her." |Example:  Is that hisher book? = 那是他/她的书吗？ |近义词: his, her, their |反义词:None.
