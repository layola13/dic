interparliamentary:/ˌɪntərˈpɑːrləmentəri/| adj. |议会间的，跨议会的| Relating to or involving the interaction or cooperation between two or more parliaments.  |例句：The interparliamentary meeting discussed trade relations. 议会间的会议讨论了贸易关系。  The interparliamentary delegation visited several countries. 跨议会代表团访问了几个国家。|近义词：parliamentary, intergovernmental |反义词：intraparliamentary

词源:
The word "interparliamentary" is a combination of "inter-" (meaning "between" or "among") and "parliamentary" (relating to a parliament).  "Parliament" itself originates from the Old French "parlement," referring to a place of talking or speaking.
