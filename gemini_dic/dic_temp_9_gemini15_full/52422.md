didjeridoos:/ˌdɪdʒərɪˈduːz/| n. |迪吉里杜管（复数）|A didjeridoo is a long, hollow, wind instrument traditionally made from a eucalyptus branch.  It produces a low, resonant drone-like sound.  It is indigenous to Australia and is an important part of Aboriginal culture. |The Aboriginal musicians played their didjeridoos during the ceremony.  澳大利亚原住民音乐家在仪式上演奏了他们的迪吉里杜管。 The band incorporated didjeridoos into their unique sound.乐队将迪吉里杜管融入其独特的音色中。|近义词：wind instrument, drone, tube|反义词: (无直接反义词)|名词
