osteolite:/ˈɒstiəlaɪt/| n. |骨化石；骨骼化石|A fossil bone or bone fragment.  Osteolites are often found in sedimentary rocks and provide valuable information about ancient ecosystems and the evolution of life.|例句：The paleontologists discovered numerous osteolites in the dig site, indicating a rich prehistoric fauna. 古生物学家在挖掘现场发现了许多骨化石，表明那里曾经存在丰富的史前动物群。|例句：Analysis of the osteolites revealed the presence of several extinct species of mammals. 对骨化石的分析揭示了几种已灭绝的哺乳动物物种的存在。|近义词：fossil bone, bone fossil|反义词：living bone
