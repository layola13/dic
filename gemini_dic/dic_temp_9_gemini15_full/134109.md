nonimmune:/ˌnɒnɪˈmjuːn/| adj. |非免疫的，无免疫力的 |Not immune; lacking immunity.  |Example:  The nonimmune individuals are at high risk of infection.  非免疫个体感染的风险很高。|近义词：susceptible, vulnerable |反义词：immune


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is nonimmune to the disease. = 他对这种疾病没有免疫力。
The plants are nonimmune to pests. = 这些植物对害虫没有免疫力。

一般过去时:
She was nonimmune to the effects of the poison. = 她对毒药的影响没有免疫力。
The population was nonimmune to the virus before vaccination. = 在接种疫苗之前，人口对病毒没有免疫力。


现在完成时:
They have remained nonimmune to the flu despite precautions. = 尽管采取了预防措施，他们仍然对流感没有免疫力。
The community has been nonimmune to the spread of misinformation. =  这个社区一直容易受到错误信息的传播。

过去完成时:
The children had been nonimmune to the measles outbreak. = 孩子们在麻疹爆发期间没有免疫力。
The country had remained largely nonimmune to external influences. = 这个国家在很大程度上一直容易受到外部影响。


将来时:

The new strain suggests that we will likely be nonimmune to this variant. = 新的毒株表明我们很可能对这种变种没有免疫力。


虚拟语气:
If they were nonimmune, they would be at greater risk. = 如果他们没有免疫力，他们将面临更大的风险。
I wish I were not nonimmune to this allergy. = 我希望我对这种过敏症没有免疫力。


被动语态:

No one is considered nonimmune to the effects of climate change. = 没有人被认为能够免受气候变化的影响。


疑问句:

否定句:
