eccentric:/ɪkˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj., n. |古怪的；异乎寻常的；偏心圆；离心的 |

英文解析:
As an adjective, "eccentric" describes someone or something unusual or unconventional in behavior or appearance. It suggests a departure from established norms or expectations, often in a quirky or interesting way. As a noun, it refers to a person with such unusual behavior.  The word often implies a harmless or even charming oddity, rather than a serious mental disorder.

例句:
He's known for his eccentric fashion sense. = 他以其古怪的时尚感而闻名。

She has a few eccentric habits, like talking to her plants. = 她有一些古怪的习惯，比如和她的植物说话。


近义词:
odd, unusual, quirky, strange, unconventional, peculiar, whimsical


反义词:
conventional, normal, ordinary, typical, standard


词性:
adjective, noun
