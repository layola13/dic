overhear:/ˌoʊvərˈhɪr/| /ˌəʊvəˈhɪə(r)/| vt.|无意中听到，偷听到|To hear something without intending to, or without the speaker knowing that you are listening.|I overheard them talking about a surprise party.|近义词：eavesdrop, catch|反义词：intend to hear


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I often overhear my neighbours arguing. = 我经常无意中听到我的邻居吵架。
They usually overhear their children's conversations. = 他们通常会无意中听到孩子们在谈话。


一般过去时:
I overheard a fascinating conversation on the bus yesterday. = 昨天我在公交车上无意中听到一段精彩的对话。
She overheard her colleagues gossiping in the office. = 她无意中听到她的同事们在办公室里八卦。


现在完成时:
I have overheard them mentioning the project several times. = 我已经多次无意中听到他们提到这个项目。
We have overheard snippets of their conversation throughout the day. = 我们整天都无意中听到他们谈话的片段。


过去完成时:
I had overheard them planning a trip before they announced it. = 在他们宣布之前，我已经无意中听到他们计划旅行了。
She had overheard the secret before the meeting started. = 会议开始之前，她已经无意中听到这个秘密了。


将来时:
I might overhear some interesting information at the conference. = 我可能会在会议上无意中听到一些有趣的信息。
We will likely overhear their plans if we stay nearby. = 如果我们待在附近，很可能会无意中听到他们的计划。


虚拟语气:
If I had not overheard their conversation, I wouldn't know the truth. = 如果我没有无意中听到他们的谈话，我就不会知道真相。
I wish I hadn't overheard that argument. = 我真希望我没有无意中听到那场争吵。


被动语态:
Their conversation was overheard by the neighbour. = 他们的谈话被邻居无意中听到了。
The secret was overheard by someone in the crowd. = 这个秘密被人群中的某个人无意中听到了。


疑问句:
Did you overhear anything interesting? = 你无意中听到什么有趣的事情了吗？
What did you overhear during the meeting? = 在会议上你无意中听到了什么？


否定句:
I didn't overhear anything important. = 我没有无意中听到任何重要的事情。
They didn't overhear the whispered conversation. = 他们没有无意中听到窃窃私语的谈话。
