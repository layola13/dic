nonproblem:/ˈnɒnˈprɒbləm/ | n. |非问题；小事一桩 |A nonproblem is something that is not a problem, or is easily solved; a trivial matter.  It often implies that something presented as a problem is actually insignificant or easily handled. |例句：The boss said the budget shortfall was a nonproblem. 老板说预算短缺是小事一桩。


例句：Dealing with this minor issue is a nonproblem for our experienced team. 处理这个问题对我们经验丰富的团队来说是小菜一碟。


近义词：trifle, triviality, minor issue, piece of cake


反义词：major problem, significant issue, difficulty, challenge


词性:名词
