legislate:/ˈledʒɪsleɪt/| vt.|立法，制定法律|To make or enact laws.  The process of making laws involves debate, amendment, and eventual passage by a legislative body.|例句：The government will legislate to control pollution. 政府将立法控制污染。|例句：Congress legislated new tax laws last year. 国会在去年制定了新的税法。|近义词：enact, make laws, pass laws|反义词：repeal, abolish

一般现在时:
The council legislates on environmental issues. = 理事会就环境问题立法。
They legislate new regulations every year. = 他们每年都制定新的规章制度。


一般过去时:
The parliament legislated against discrimination. = 议会制定了反对歧视的法律。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
