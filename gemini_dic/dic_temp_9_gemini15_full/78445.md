getaway:/ˈɡɛtəweɪ/| n., v.| 逃亡；度假；逃脱；|  A getaway can refer to an escape from a place or situation, or a short vacation.  As a verb, it means to escape or flee. |例句：The robbers made a quick getaway in a stolen car.  强盗们偷了一辆车迅速逃跑了。

The family took a weekend getaway to the beach.  这家人去海滩度了一个周末短假。

近义词：escape, vacation, holiday, excursion, flight|反义词：arrival, capture, arrest|词性:Noun, Verb
