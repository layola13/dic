processability:/ˌprɑːsɛsəˈbɪləti/ | /ˌprəʊsesəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可加工性，可处理性 | The ability of a material to be processed or manufactured into a desired form or product.  This includes aspects like ease of shaping, forming, machining, and the overall efficiency and cost-effectiveness of the processing.  | Example: The high processability of this new polymer allows for rapid production of complex components.  这个新型聚合物的良好可加工性使得能够快速生产复杂的部件。  | 近义词：workability, manufacturability | 反义词：unworkability, intractability
