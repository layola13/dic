pileus: /ˈpaɪliəs/ | n. |菌盖，帽状体 | A pileus is the cap of a fungus, mushroom, or toadstool.  It is the umbrella-like structure that forms the upper part of the fruiting body.  | Example: The pileus of the mushroom was a vibrant red color.  菌盖的颜色鲜艳的红色。 |近义词：cap (in the context of fungi) |反义词：stipe (the stalk of a fungus)
