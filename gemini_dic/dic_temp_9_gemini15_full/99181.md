intercirculates:/ˌɪntərˈsɜːrkjuleɪts/| v. |循环流动；相互循环 |To move or cause to move around within a system or group.  This word implies a reciprocal or cyclical movement, not simply a one-way flow. |例句：Blood intercirculates throughout the body. = 血液在全身循环流动。

The nutrients intercirculate within the ecosystem. = 营养物质在生态系统内循环流动。|近义词：circulate, recycle, exchange |反义词：stagnate, remain static
