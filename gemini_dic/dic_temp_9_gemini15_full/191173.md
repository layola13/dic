scarer:/ˈskɛərər/| n. |恐吓者，令人害怕的事物|A person or thing that scares.  It can refer to a person who deliberately frightens others, or something that causes fear, such as a scary movie or a haunted house.|例句：The haunted house was a real scarer.  那个鬼屋真吓人。|例句：The bully was a scarer to all the younger children.  那个恶霸吓唬所有年纪较小的孩子。|近义词：frightener, terror, menace |反义词：comforter, soother
