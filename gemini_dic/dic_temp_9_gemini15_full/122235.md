miswrites:/ˌmɪsˈraɪts/| vt.|写错，误写|To write something incorrectly.  This usually refers to a single word or phrase, rather than a whole text. |例句：He miswrites the word "definitely" as "definately". 他把“definitely”这个词错写成“definately”。|例句：She frequently miswrites names in her address book. 她的通讯录里经常把名字写错。|近义词： misspells, misprints, writes incorrectly |反义词：writes correctly, spells correctly
