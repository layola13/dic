nebalia:/nɪˈbeɪliə/| n. |糠虾，磷虾|A small, shrimp-like crustacean of the order Nebaliacea, characterized by its bivalve-like carapace and its importance in the marine food web.  Nebalia are typically found in shallow coastal waters and are filter feeders.|Example:  The researchers collected samples of *nebalia* to study their role in the ecosystem.  研究人员收集了糠虾样本以研究它们在生态系统中的作用。|近义词：mysid (though not a perfect synonym, as it refers to a broader group)|反义词：无|名词
