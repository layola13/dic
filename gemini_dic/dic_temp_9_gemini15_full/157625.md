pitifully:/ˈpɪtɪfli/| adj. |可怜地，悲惨地，微不足道地 | pitifully describes something done or happening in a way that evokes pity or sympathy, or in a way that is extremely inadequate or small.  It suggests a lack of strength, effectiveness, or success. |例句：He tried pitifully to lift the heavy box. 他可怜巴巴地试图抬起那个沉重的箱子。

例句：The team performed pitifully in the final match.  这支队伍在决赛中的表现非常糟糕。


近义词：pathetically, miserably, sadly, meagerly, inadequately

反义词：gloriously, triumphantly, splendidly, abundantly, successfully


词性：形容词
