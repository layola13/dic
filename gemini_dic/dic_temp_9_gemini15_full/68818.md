fascinated:/ˈfæsɪneɪtɪd/| adj.|着迷的，入迷的| greatly interested or captivated; charmed.|I was fascinated by her stories of travel.|近义词： captivated, enthralled, charmed |反义词：bored, uninterested


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am fascinated by ancient history. = 我对古代历史很着迷。
He is fascinated by the beauty of nature. = 他被大自然的美丽所吸引。

一般过去时:
I was fascinated by the magician's performance. = 我被魔术师的表演迷住了。
She was fascinated by the intricate details of the painting. = 她被绘画的复杂细节所吸引。

现在完成时:
I have been fascinated by astronomy since childhood. = 从小我就对天文学很着迷。
She has been fascinated by the mystery of the unsolved case. = 她一直被这个未解之谜所吸引。

过去完成时:
I had been fascinated by his work before I met him. = 在我遇到他之前，我就已经被他的作品迷住了。
They had been fascinated by the bird's unusual song. = 他们已经被那只鸟不寻常的歌声迷住了。

将来时:
I will be fascinated to see the results of the experiment. = 我将非常渴望看到实验的结果。
She will be fascinated by the new museum exhibit. = 她将会被新的博物馆展品所吸引。

虚拟语气:
If I were fascinated by something, I would study it in detail. = 如果我对某事着迷，我会详细研究它。
I wish I were fascinated by exercise more. = 我希望我对运动更感兴趣。

被动语态:
Many people are fascinated by the power of technology. = 许多人都被科技的力量所吸引。
He was fascinated by her intelligence. = 他被她的聪明才智所吸引。(虽然这里fascinated本身不用被动，但句子可以用被动语态)

疑问句:
Are you fascinated by anything in particular? = 你对什么特别感兴趣吗？
Was he fascinated by the movie? = 他被这部电影迷住了吗？

否定句:
I am not fascinated by horror movies. = 我对恐怖电影不感兴趣。
She wasn't fascinated by his explanation. = 她对他的解释并不感兴趣。


PHRASE->

remain fascinated = 保持着迷
