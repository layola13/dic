ossifies:/ˈɑːsɪfaɪz/ | v. | 僵化；骨化；变成僵硬 | To become hard or inflexible; to turn into bone.  The process can refer to both literal bone formation and metaphorical hardening of attitudes or systems. |例句：His ideas ossified over time, making him resistant to new perspectives. = 他的想法随着时间的推移而僵化，使他抗拒新的观点。

His injured leg slowly ossified after the accident. = 事故后，他受伤的腿慢慢骨化。|近义词：harden, petrify, rigidify |反义词：soften, flexibilize
