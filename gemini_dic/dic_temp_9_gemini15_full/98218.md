insecureness:/ˌɪnsɪˈkjʊrnɪs/| n. |不安全感，缺乏自信 |A feeling of uncertainty or lack of confidence.  It can stem from various sources, including past experiences, social comparisons, or perceived inadequacies.  Insecure people often doubt their abilities, worth, or relationships. |例句：Her insecurity stemmed from a difficult childhood.  她的不安全感源于一个艰难的童年。

例句：His insecurity manifested as arrogance. 他的不安全感表现为傲慢。


近义词：self-doubt, lack of confidence, uncertainty, apprehension, anxiety


反义词：self-assurance, confidence, certainty, security


词性：名词
