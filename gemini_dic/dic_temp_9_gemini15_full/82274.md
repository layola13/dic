grounds:/ɡraʊndz/ | n. | 理由；根据；场地；咖啡渣 | Grounds refer to the basis or reason for something, the area of land surrounding a building, or the sediment left after brewing coffee.  | The judge dismissed the case on the grounds of insufficient evidence.  法官以证据不足为由驳回了此案。  The school grounds were filled with children playing. 学校操场挤满了玩耍的孩子们。| reasons, basis, justification, premises |  (no direct antonym, depends on context;  e.g.,  "evidence" could be considered an antonym when referring to "grounds" as reasons) | 名词
