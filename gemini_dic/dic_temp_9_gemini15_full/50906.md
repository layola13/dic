deserve:/dɪˈzɜːrv/ | vt. |应得；值得 |To be worthy of or to have a right to (something, typically reward or punishment).  It implies a moral or ethical judgment about the recipient's actions or qualities. |例句：He deserves a reward for his hard work. 他应该因他的努力而得到奖励。

例句：She deserves to be punished for her lies. 她应该因说谎而受到惩罚。


近义词：merit, warrant, justify | 反义词：not merit, forfeit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He deserves a promotion. = 他值得升职。
They deserve our respect. = 他们值得我们尊重。


一般过去时:
She deserved the award. = 她当之无愧地获得了奖项。
He deserved to fail the exam. = 他活该考试不及格。


现在完成时:
You have deserved this success. = 你应得这份成功。
They have deserved a rest. = 他们应该休息了。


过去完成时:
He had deserved a better fate. = 他本应该拥有更好的命运。
She had deserved more consideration. = 她本应该得到更多的体谅。


将来时:
You will deserve a vacation after this project. = 完成这个项目后，你将值得休假。
They will deserve praise for their efforts. = 他们的努力将值得称赞。


虚拟语气:
He deserved to win, but he didn't. = 他应该赢，但他没有赢。
She deserved to be happy, but she wasn't. = 她应该快乐，但她不快乐。


被动语态:
The punishment was deserved. = 惩罚是应得的。
The reward is deserved by her hard work. = 奖励是她努力工作应得的。


疑问句:
Does he deserve another chance? = 他值得再给一次机会吗？
Do they deserve our help? = 他们值得我们帮助吗？


否定句:
He doesn't deserve your criticism. = 他不值得你批评。
She doesn't deserve to be treated like that. = 她不值得那样对待。
