disentangling: /ˌdɪsɛnˈtæŋɡlɪŋ/ | v. | 解开；理清；使摆脱；区分 |  The act of separating something that is tangled or intertwined.  It implies a process of careful separation and clarification. |

例句：
He spent hours disentangling the fishing line. = 他花了几个小时解开钓鱼线。

She is disentangling herself from a complicated legal battle. = 她正努力摆脱复杂的法律纠纷。


近义词：untangling, unravelling, separating, unraveling, extricating

反义词：tangling, intertwining, confusing, complicating

词性：动词 (现在分词)


词源:

* **dis-:**  A prefix meaning "apart," "away," or "reversal."
* **en-:** A prefix often indicating "to cause to be" or "to put into."
* **tangled:**  From Old Norse *tangr*, meaning "something that catches or holds."

The word essentially means "to cause to be apart from being tangled."
