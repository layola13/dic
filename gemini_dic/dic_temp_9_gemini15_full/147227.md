overmeddle:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛdəl/| v.|过度干涉；多管闲事 |To interfere excessively or intrude upon the affairs of others.|例句：Don't overmeddle in their family affairs. 不要过度干涉他们的家事。|例句：He tends to overmeddle in matters that don't concern him. 他倾向于多管闲事。|近义词：interfere, meddle, butt in, intrude|反义词：mind one's own business, stay out of it|动词
