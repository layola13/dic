overinfluencing: /ˌoʊvərˈɪnflʊənsɪŋ/ | v. |过度影响，过度施加影响 |The act of excessively influencing someone or something, often to a negative or harmful degree.  It suggests an undue amount of influence that may be manipulative or coercive. |例句：The company was accused of overinfluencing the government's decision on the new regulations. 公司被指控过度影响政府对新规定的决定。

The constant overinfluencing of young children by advertisements is a concern for many parents.  许多家长都担心广告对幼儿的过度影响。|近义词：manipulating, controlling, dominating, coercing |反义词：ignoring, neglecting, leaving alone, empowering
