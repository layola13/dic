nonresignations:/ˌnɒnˌrɛzɪɡˈneɪʃənz/ | n. |非辞职；未辞职人数 |  The term "nonresignations" refers to instances where employees choose to remain in their jobs despite potentially having reasons to leave, such as better offers elsewhere or dissatisfaction with their current role. It highlights the retention of employees, often contrary to expectations.  | Example:  The company celebrated the high number of nonresignations this quarter, indicating strong employee satisfaction.  公司庆祝本季度大量的员工未辞职，这表明员工满意度很高。 | 近义词: employee retention, staff retention | 反义词: resignations, employee turnover
