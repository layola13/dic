/pɜːrˈfleɪʃən/ | n. | 完美通货膨胀 |  Perflation is a neologism, a newly coined word,  combining aspects of "perfect" and "inflation." It describes a hypothetical economic scenario where inflation is moderate and stable, benefiting both businesses and consumers.  It suggests a controlled inflation rate that doesn't cause significant economic hardship, unlike hyperinflation or deflation. There is no universally agreed-upon definition or usage.  | The economists debated whether the current economic climate represented perflation or a precursor to a more serious downturn. |  Controlled inflation, stable growth | Hyperinflation, deflation, stagflation | 名词
