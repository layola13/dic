lops:/lɒps/| /lɑːps/| v.| 笨拙地移动；松弛；垂下；（使）突然落下|to move in a clumsy or heavy way; to hang or droop; to fall or drop suddenly|例句：The dog loped along the beach.  这只狗笨拙地沿着海滩走着。 He loped awkwardly across the room. 他笨拙地穿过房间。|近义词：lumber, plod, slump|反义词：dart, zip, spring

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lops the branches off the tree. 他砍掉树枝。
The curtains lop heavily. 窗帘重重地垂着。

一般过去时:
She loped gracefully through the field. 她优雅地穿过田野。
The heavy fruit loped from the branch.  沉重的果实从树枝上掉下来了。

现在完成时:
The weight of the snow has loped the branches. 雪的重量压弯了树枝。
He has loped along for hours. 他已经笨拙地走了好几个小时了。

过去完成时:
The branch had loped before the storm.  暴风雨来临前，树枝就已经垂下来了。
She had loped across the finish line. 她已经笨拙地越过终点线了。
