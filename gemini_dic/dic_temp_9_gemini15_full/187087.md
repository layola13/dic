rivet:/ˈrɪvɪt/| n. vt.|铆钉；吸引（注意力）|A rivet is a metal pin used to join pieces of metal together.  It is typically inserted through holes in the pieces and then deformed to hold them securely. As a verb, it means to fasten or secure with rivets, or to hold someone's attention completely.|例句：The workers used rivets to fasten the metal plates together. 工人们用铆钉将金属板固定在一起。

The speaker's charisma riveted the audience to their seats. 演讲者的魅力使观众全神贯注。|近义词：(n.) bolt, pin, stud; (v.) fasten, secure, attract, captivate|反义词：(v.) bore, repel
