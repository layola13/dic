decussately:/dɪˈkʌseɪtli/| adv.| 十字交叉地；对角线状地 | Arranged or occurring in a crisscross or diagonal pattern.  The term often describes the arrangement of leaves, branches, or other plant parts. | Example: The leaves of the plant were arranged decussately, forming a distinctive X pattern.  植物的叶子呈十字交叉状排列，形成独特的X形图案。| 近义词：crosswise, diagonally, intersectingly | 反义词：parallel, alongside
