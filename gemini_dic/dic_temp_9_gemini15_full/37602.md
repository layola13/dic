communicating: /kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 传达；沟通；交流 | The act of conveying information, ideas, or feelings through language or other means.  |  Example:  They were communicating effectively through email. 他们通过电子邮件有效地沟通。  He is communicating his ideas through his artwork. 他正通过他的艺术作品表达他的想法。 | conveying, expressing, imparting | isolating, silencing, withholding | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They communicate daily. = 他们每天都沟通。
We communicate through email. = 我们通过电子邮件沟通。

一般过去时:
She communicated her concerns to the manager. = 她向经理表达了她的担忧。
He communicated the news to his family. = 他把这个消息告诉了他的家人。

现在完成时:
They have communicated effectively throughout the project. = 在整个项目中，他们的沟通一直很有效。
We have communicated our needs to the supplier. = 我们已经向供应商传达了我们的需求。

过去完成时:
They had communicated their decision before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经传达了他们的决定。
She had communicated her intentions clearly. = 她已经清楚地表达了她的意图。

将来时:
He will communicate the results tomorrow. = 他明天将公布结果。
We will communicate our plans to the team. = 我们将把我们的计划传达给团队。

虚拟语气:
If they communicated better, they would avoid conflict. = 如果他们沟通更好，他们就能避免冲突。
I wish they communicated more openly. = 我希望他们能更坦诚地沟通。

被动语态:
The information was communicated clearly. = 信息传达得很清楚。
The decision will be communicated to all staff. = 决定将传达给所有员工。

疑问句:
How do you communicate with your colleagues? = 你如何与你的同事沟通？
Does he communicate well in English? = 他英语沟通得好吗？

否定句:
He didn't communicate his feelings adequately. = 他没有充分表达他的感受。
They don't communicate well as a team. = 他们作为一个团队沟通得不好。


PHRASE->
Communicating effectively = 有效沟通
Communicating with others = 与他人沟通
Means of communicating = 沟通方式
Lines of communication = 沟通渠道
Open communication = 开放式沟通
