fieriest: /ˈfaɪəriɪst/ | adj. | 最热烈的；最激烈的；最火爆的 | Most intense in feeling or character; most ardent.  The superlative form of "fiery." |

例句：

He delivered the fieriest speech of his career, igniting the crowd with his passion. 他发表了他职业生涯中最热烈的演讲，点燃了人群的热情。

Her fieriest opponent, a seasoned debater, was no match for her quick wit and sharp arguments. 她最激烈的对手，一位经验丰富的辩手，也无法与她敏捷的才智和尖锐的论点匹敌。


近义词：most ardent, most passionate, most intense, most fervent

反义词：calmest, mildest, gentlest, most placid


词源:

From "fiery," which comes from the Old English "fyrig," meaning "fiery, burning."  The "-est" suffix indicates the superlative degree, signifying the highest degree of fieriness.
