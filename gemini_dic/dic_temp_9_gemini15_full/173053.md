quarter-tone:/ˈkwɔːrtə(r)toʊn/| n.| 四分音，半音程的二分之一|A quarter-tone is a musical interval half the size of a semitone (half step).  It's smaller than the smallest interval typically used in Western music.|例句：Some microtonal music uses quarter-tones to create unique sounds. 一些微音音乐使用四分音来创造独特的声音。  The composer experimented with quarter-tones in his latest symphony.  作曲家在他的最新交响乐中尝试了四分音。|近义词：microtonal interval|反义词：whole tone, octave|

词源：

* **quarter:**  From Old French *quart*, from Latin *quartus* "fourth".  This refers to its being one-fourth of a whole tone (in some theoretical contexts).

* **tone:** From Old French *ton*, from Latin *tonus*, from Greek *τόνος* (tónos) "tone, tension, accent".  In music, this refers to a basic musical interval.


用法说明：

quarter-tone 主要用在音乐理论和实践中，描述一种比半音还小的音程。它在西方传统音乐中并不常见，但在一些现代和实验音乐中，特别是微音音乐(microtonal music)中使用较为广泛，以创造更丰富的音色和表达力。
