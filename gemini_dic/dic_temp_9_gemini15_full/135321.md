nonpayments:/ˈnɒnˈpeɪmənts/ | n. | 未付款，未支付款项 |  The failure to make payments when due.  This can refer to a single missed payment or a pattern of late or missed payments.  It often has negative consequences, such as late fees, penalties, or damage to credit score. |例句：The company is facing serious financial difficulties due to a series of nonpayments from its clients. =由于客户的一系列未付款项，该公司正面临严重的财务困难。

The accumulation of nonpayments led to the business's closure. = 未付款项的累积导致企业倒闭。|近义词：unpaid bills, overdue payments, defaults |反义词：payments, settlements
