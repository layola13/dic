preheaters:/ˌpriːˈhiːtərz/| n. |预热器|A preheater is a device used to heat a substance before it enters a main process, such as heating air before it enters a furnace or heating oil before it enters a boiler.  Preheaters improve efficiency and performance in various industrial and domestic applications.|例句：The furnace's preheaters ensure optimal combustion temperature.  炉子的预热器确保最佳的燃烧温度。

The bakery uses a preheater to warm the oven before baking bread. 面包店使用预热器在烘焙面包之前预热烤箱。|近义词：heater, warmer|反义词：cooler
