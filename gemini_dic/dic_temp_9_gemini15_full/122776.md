mol:/mɒl/| n.|摩尔，摩尔数|A unit of measurement for the amount of a substance, equal to the amount of substance that contains as many elementary entities (atoms, molecules, ions, etc.) as there are atoms in 12 grams of carbon-12.  This number is approximately 6.022 x 10^23, and is known as Avogadro's number.|例句：One mole of water contains approximately 6.022 x 10^23 water molecules. 一摩尔水大约包含6.022 x 10^23个水分子。|例句：The molar mass of oxygen is approximately 16 grams per mole. 氧气的摩尔质量大约是每摩尔16克。|近义词：gram-mole, kilomole|反义词：N/A|名词
