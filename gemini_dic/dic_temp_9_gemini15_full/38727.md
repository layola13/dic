conditionally:/kənˈdɪʃənəli/| adv.|有条件地；如果；假使|Depending on a condition or requirement; subject to a condition.  It implies that something will happen or is true only if a certain condition is met.|例句：The offer is conditionally accepted, pending further review.  这个提议有条件地被接受，需待进一步审核。|例句：We will proceed conditionally, depending on the weather. 我们将根据天气情况决定是否继续进行。|近义词：provisionally, contingently, hypothetically|反义词：unconditionally, absolutely, definitely|副词
