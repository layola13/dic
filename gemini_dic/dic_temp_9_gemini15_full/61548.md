embushment: /ɪmˈbuʃmənt/ | n. |埋伏；伏击；埋伏地点 | The act of ambushing; the state of being ambushed; a place of ambush.  |例句：The soldiers planned an ambushment near the river.  士兵们计划在河边设伏。

例句：The enemy's ambushment resulted in heavy casualties for our troops.  敌人的伏击导致我军伤亡惨重。


近义词：ambush, surprise attack, trap, snare | 反义词：open attack, frontal assault


词源：源自动词“embush”，其词根可以追溯到古法语“embuscher”，意为“隐藏在灌木丛中”。  最终词源与古日耳曼语词根相关，表示“在灌木丛中”。
