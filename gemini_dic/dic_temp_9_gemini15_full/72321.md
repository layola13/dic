flout:/flaʊt/| vt.|蔑视；违抗；炫耀；轻视|To treat with contempt; to disregard or disobey (a rule, law, or convention); to show (something) off ostentatiously.|例句：He openly flouted the law. 他公然蔑视法律。|例句：She flouted convention by wearing jeans to the wedding. 她穿着牛仔裤去参加婚礼，蔑视了传统习俗。|近义词：defy, scorn, mock, disdain|反义词：obey, respect, conform to
