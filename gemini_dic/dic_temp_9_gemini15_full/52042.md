dialing:/ˈdaɪəlɪŋ/ | /ˈdaɪlɪŋ/ | v. |拨号，打电话 |The act of using a telephone to connect to another person or service by inputting a series of numbers.  |例句：I'm dialing your number now. 我正在拨你的号码。

例句：She spent several minutes dialing the long-distance number. 她花了数分钟拨打长途电话号码。


近义词：calling, phoning, ringing


反义词：disconnecting, hanging up


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I dial my mother every Sunday. = 我每周日都给我母亲打电话。
He dials the number carefully. = 他小心翼翼地拨打号码。


一般过去时:
I dialed the wrong number. = 我拨错了号码。
She dialed her best friend immediately. = 她立即拨打了她最好的朋友的电话。


现在完成时:
I have dialed that number several times. = 我已经拨打过那个号码好几次了。
She has dialed him repeatedly without success. = 她反复给他打电话，但没有成功。


过去完成时:
I had dialed the number before the line went dead. = 电话中断之前，我已经拨打了号码。
They had dialed the emergency services before the fire spread. = 大火蔓延之前，他们已经拨打了紧急服务电话。


将来时:
I will dial you later. = 我一会儿给你打电话。
He will dial the number as soon as he gets the information. = 他一旦得到信息就会拨打号码。


虚拟语气:
If I had dialed the number correctly, I wouldn't have gotten a busy signal. = 如果我拨号正确，就不会听到忙音了。
I wish I had dialed him earlier. = 我真希望我早点给他打电话。


被动语态:
The number was dialed incorrectly. = 号码拨错了。
The emergency services were dialed immediately. = 紧急服务号码被立即拨打。


疑问句:
Are you dialing the correct number? = 你拨的号码正确吗？
Have you dialed that number before? = 你以前拨打过那个号码吗？


否定句:
I didn't dial the number. = 我没有拨打那个号码。
He couldn't dial the number because the phone was dead. = 他无法拨打号码，因为电话没电了。


PHRASE->
dial a number = 拨打一个号码
speed dial = 快速拨号
rotary dial = 旋转拨号盘
dial-up connection = 拨号连接
dial tone = 拨号音
