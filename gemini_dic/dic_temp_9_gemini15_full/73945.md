forthcoming:/ˈfɔːrθkʌmɪŋ/| adj.|即将到来的；乐于助人的；直率的 |Approaching; about to happen; readily available; frank and open.  |例句：The forthcoming election is causing much excitement.  即将到来的选举引起了极大的兴奋。 |例句：He was forthcoming with information about the accident. 他乐于提供关于事故的信息。|近义词：upcoming, impending, forthcoming (乐于助人方面), candid, open |反义词：distant, reserved, secretive, reluctant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new product is forthcoming soon. = 新产品即将上市。
His answers are always forthcoming. = 他的回答总是很直接。


一般过去时:
The results of the investigation were forthcoming yesterday. = 调查结果昨天出来了。
She was not forthcoming with details. = 她不愿透露细节。


现在完成时:
The promised report is not yet forthcoming. = 预计的报告还没有出来。
He has been forthcoming in his support of the project. = 他一直很支持这个项目。


过去完成时:
The information was not forthcoming before the meeting. = 会议前没有得到相关信息。
They had been forthcoming about their plans. = 他们已经公开谈论他们的计划了。


将来时:
The next book in the series will be forthcoming next year. = 该系列的下一本书将于明年出版。
He will be forthcoming with the necessary documents. = 他将提供必要的证件。


虚拟语气:
If the information were forthcoming, we could finish the report. = 如果能得到信息，我们就能完成报告。
I wish the solution were more forthcoming. = 我希望解决方案能更明确一些。


被动语态:
The details of the deal are not yet forthcoming. = 这笔交易的细节尚未公布。
The new policy was made forthcoming to the public. = 新政策已向公众公布。


疑问句:
Is the new album forthcoming soon? = 新专辑很快就要发行了吗？
Are you forthcoming about your feelings? = 你愿意坦诚你的感受吗？


否定句:
The promised funds are not forthcoming. = 预计的资金没有到位。
He was not forthcoming with an explanation. = 他没有给出解释。
