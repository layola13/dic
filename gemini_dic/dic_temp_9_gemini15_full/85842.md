hci:/ˌeɪtʃˌsiˈaɪ/| n.|人机交互|Human-computer interaction; the study of how people design, implement, and evaluate interactive computer systems.  It involves understanding user needs and behaviors, designing intuitive interfaces, and ensuring usability.|Example:  HCI principles are crucial for creating user-friendly software. 人机交互原则对于创建用户友好的软件至关重要。|近义词:man-machine interaction, human-machine interface|反义词:N/A|名词
