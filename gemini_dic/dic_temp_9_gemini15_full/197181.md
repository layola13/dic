shape:/ʃeɪp/| n. v.|形状，外形；塑造，影响|The shape of the mountain is like a pyramid.  这座山形状像金字塔。  He shaped the clay into a bowl. 他把粘土塑造成碗。|form, figure, mold|formless, shapeless|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The vase is a unique shape. = 这个花瓶形状独特。
The sculptor shapes the clay with his hands. = 雕塑家用手塑造粘土。


一般过去时:
The cake was in the shape of a heart. = 蛋糕是心形。
The wind shaped the sand dunes over time. = 风随着时间的推移塑造了沙丘。


现在完成时:
The artist has shaped many works of art. = 这位艺术家已经创作了许多艺术品。
The experience has shaped her into a stronger person. = 这段经历使她变得更坚强。


过去完成时:

将来时:

The new technology will shape the way we live. = 新技术将改变我们的生活方式。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
