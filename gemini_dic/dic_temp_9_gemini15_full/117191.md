measured:/ˈmɛʒərd/ | adj. | 测量的；有分寸的；沉着的；精确的 |  Measured refers to something that has been carefully assessed or determined using a standard of measurement, or to someone who is calm and controlled in their actions and speech.  It can also describe something done in a deliberate and precise manner. |例句：The room's dimensions were carefully measured.  房间的尺寸经过仔细测量。

The measured response prevented a further escalation of the conflict.  有分寸的回应阻止了冲突的进一步升级。


近义词：calculated, deliberate, controlled, precise, calibrated | 反义词：hasty, impulsive, uncontrolled, erratic, imprecise | 词性：adjective
