scans:/skænz/| n. v.|扫描；浏览；审视|The act of scanning; a quick examination.  To examine something quickly; to read something quickly.|例句：The doctor scans the patient's X-ray.医生扫描了病人的X光片。
The student scans the textbook before the exam. 学生考试前浏览课本。|近义词：examine, survey, peruse, skim|反义词：study thoroughly, analyze in depth|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scans the document for errors. = 他扫描文件查找错误。
The machine scans the barcode. = 机器扫描条形码。

一般过去时:
She scanned the newspaper headlines. = 她浏览了报纸的头条新闻。
They scanned the area for intruders. = 他们扫描了该区域是否有入侵者。

现在完成时:
I have scanned all the documents. = 我已经扫描了所有文件。
She has scanned the book for relevant information. = 她已经浏览了这本书，查找相关信息。

过去完成时:
He had scanned the report before the meeting. = 他在会议之前已经浏览了报告。
They had scanned the images several times. = 他们已经扫描了这些图像好几次。

将来时:
I will scan the photos later. = 我稍后会扫描照片。
We will scan the entire database. = 我们将扫描整个数据库。

虚拟语气:
If I had a scanner, I would scan the document. = 如果我有一台扫描仪，我会扫描这份文件。
It would be helpful if you scanned the article. = 如果你能浏览一下这篇文章就好了。

被动语态:
The document was scanned by the secretary. = 文件由秘书扫描。

疑问句:

Did they scan the area thoroughly? = 他们彻底扫描了该区域吗？

否定句:
