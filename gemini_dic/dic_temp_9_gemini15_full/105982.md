knabble:/ˈnæbəl/| vt.|小口地啃；一点一点地咬|To bite or nibble repeatedly, usually small pieces.|例句：The mice knabbled at the cheese. 老鼠一点一点地啃着奶酪。 The squirrels knabbled on the nuts. 松鼠啃着坚果。|近义词：nibble, peck, gnaw|反义词：devour, gulp


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rabbit knabbles the carrot. = 兔子啃着胡萝卜。
The birds knabble at the seeds. = 鸟儿啄食着种子。

一般过去时:

疑问句:
