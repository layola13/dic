hypermoral:/ˌhaɪpəˈmɔːrəl/| adj.|过分道德的，伪善的|Excessively moralistic; characterized by or exhibiting an exaggerated concern with morality, often to the point of hypocrisy.  It suggests a rigid and often judgmental adherence to moral principles, sometimes masking ulterior motives.|例句：His hypermoral pronouncements masked a deep-seated insecurity. 他过分道德的言论掩盖了他内心深处的不安全感。

例句：The hypermoral stance of the politician seemed unconvincing to many voters.  这位政治家的过分道德立场让许多选民觉得不靠谱。


近义词：moralistic, puritanical, sanctimonious, self-righteous, pietistic|反义词：immoral, amoral, unprincipled


词源：hyper- (excessive) + moral (relating to principles of right and wrong)
