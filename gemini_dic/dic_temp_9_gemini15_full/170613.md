pseudo-gothic:/ˈsuːdoʊɡɒθɪk/|/ˌsjuːdəʊˈɡɒθɪk/| adj.|伪哥特式的；仿哥特式的 |Imitating or suggesting the style of Gothic architecture or art, but lacking the authenticity or sophistication of true Gothic work.  Often characterized by exaggerated or simplified features of Gothic style. |The building's pseudo-gothic facade is a striking example of Victorian architecture. 这座建筑的伪哥特式外观是维多利亚式建筑的突出例子。| neo-Gothic, mock-Gothic | classical, realistic |
