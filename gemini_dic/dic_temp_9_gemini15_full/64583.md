erubescent:/ɪˌruːˈbɛsənt/| adj. |微红的，脸红的|Becoming red, especially in the face, as from embarrassment or shame.  The word implies a gradual reddening, not a sudden blush.  |例句：Her cheeks became erubescent as she listened to the compliment.  她的脸颊听到赞美后变得微红。

例句：The sunset cast an erubescent glow on the clouds.  日落给云彩投下了一层微红的辉光。


近义词：reddening, blushing, flushing, rosy


反义词：pale, wan, ashen


词性：形容词
