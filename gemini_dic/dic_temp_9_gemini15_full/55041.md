disquietment:/ˌdɪsˈkwaɪətmənt/| n. |不安；焦虑；忧虑|A feeling of worry or unease.  It often refers to a state of being disturbed or unsettled in mind. The feeling can range from mild nervousness to deep anxiety.|例句：The news of the impending storm caused considerable disquietment among the villagers.  暴风雨即将来临的消息引起了村民们相当大的不安。

例句：Her constant disquietment about her son's safety kept her awake at night. 她对儿子安全的持续担忧让她整夜睡不着觉。


近义词：anxiety, unease, worry, apprehension, perturbation, restlessness|反义词：peace, calm, tranquility, serenity, composure|词性：名词
