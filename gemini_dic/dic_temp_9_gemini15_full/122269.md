mitigates:/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪts/| v.|减轻；缓和；缓解 | To make something less harmful, serious, or painful.  |例句：The government is trying to mitigate the effects of the economic crisis. 政府正在努力减轻经济危机的影響。

例句：Emergency services worked to mitigate the damage caused by the hurricane. 紧急服务部门努力减轻飓风造成的损害。


近义词：alleviate, lessen, reduce, moderate, alleviate, assuage | 反义词：aggravate, exacerbate, worsen | 词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new policy mitigates the risk of fraud. = 新政策减轻了欺诈的风险。
Regular exercise mitigates the symptoms of depression. = 定期锻炼减轻了抑郁的症状。


一般过去时:
The firefighters mitigated the spread of the fire. = 消防员减缓了火势的蔓延。
The medication mitigated his pain. = 药物减轻了他的痛苦。


现在完成时:
These measures have mitigated the environmental impact of the project. = 这些措施已经减轻了该项目的环保影响。
The company has mitigated its carbon footprint significantly. = 公司已经大幅减少了碳足迹。


过去完成时:
They had mitigated the potential risks before launching the product. = 他们在推出产品之前已经减轻了潜在的风险。
The engineers had mitigated the structural weaknesses of the building. = 工程师们已经减轻了建筑物的结构缺陷。


将来时:
The proposed changes will mitigate the negative consequences. = 提出的变更将减轻负面后果。
Further research will help mitigate the side effects of the drug. = 进一步的研究将有助于减轻药物的副作用。


虚拟语气:
If we acted quickly, we could mitigate the damage. = 如果我们迅速采取行动，就能减轻损失。
It is crucial that we mitigate the potential harm to the environment. = 至关重要的是，我们必须减轻对环境的潜在危害。


被动语态:
The impact of the storm was mitigated by the emergency response team. = 紧急救援队减轻了风暴的影响。
The risks were mitigated through careful planning. = 通过仔细的规划，风险得到了减轻。


疑问句:
What measures can be taken to mitigate the effects of climate change? = 可以采取哪些措施来减轻气候变化的影响？
How can we mitigate the negative impact on local businesses? = 我们如何减轻对当地企业的负面影响？


否定句:
The company failed to mitigate the reputational damage. = 公司未能减轻名誉损失。
Their actions did not mitigate the problem; they exacerbated it. = 他们的行为并没有减轻问题，反而加剧了问题。


PHRASE->
mitigate the impact = 减轻影响
mitigate risks = 减轻风险
mitigate damage = 减轻损害
mitigate losses = 减轻损失
mitigate suffering = 减轻痛苦

词源:
源自拉丁语 *mitigare，由 *mittis（发送）和 *gravis（沉重）构成，字面意思是“使较轻”。
