navigators:/ˈnævɪɡeɪtərz/| n.|导航员；领航员；导航设备|People or devices that guide or direct the course of something, especially a ship or aircraft.|例句：Experienced navigators are essential for safe ocean voyages.  经验丰富的导航员对于安全的航海至关重要。
例句：The ship's navigators used GPS to chart their course.  船上的导航员使用GPS来规划航线。|近义词：guides, pilots, directors |反义词：followers, passengers
