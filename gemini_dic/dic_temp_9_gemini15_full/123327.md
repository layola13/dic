monographists:/mɒˈnɒɡrəfɪsts/|/ˈmɑːnəɡræfɪsts/| n. |专著作者；论文作者|A monographist is a writer who specializes in writing monographs, which are scholarly works on a single subject.  Monographists often conduct extensive research and analysis before writing their works. They are typically experts in their respective fields.|例句：Many renowned monographists have contributed significantly to the field of history. 许多著名的专著作者对历史领域做出了重大贡献。|  例句：The leading monographists on ancient Greece presented their findings at the conference.  关于古希腊的领先专著作者在会议上展示了他们的发现。|近义词：scholar, researcher, specialist, author, writer|反义词：generalist
