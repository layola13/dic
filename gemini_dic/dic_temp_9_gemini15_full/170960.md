pseudophilosophical:/ˌsuːdoʊˌfɪləˈsɒfɪkəl/| adj.|伪哲学的；似是而非的哲学的 | seeming philosophical but lacking intellectual depth or rigor; superficially philosophical.  It often refers to arguments or ideas that appear profound but are actually shallow, illogical, or based on flawed premises.  |例句：His arguments were pseudophilosophical, filled with grandiose pronouncements but devoid of substantial reasoning.  他的论点是伪哲学的，充满了夸夸其谈，但却缺乏实质性的推理。


例句：The essay presented a pseudophilosophical approach to the problem, using vague terms and avoiding concrete analysis. 这篇文章对这个问题采取了一种伪哲学的方法，使用了含糊不清的术语，避免了具体的分析。


近义词：specious, sophistical, quasi-philosophical, metaphysically-sounding but empty


反义词：philosophical, rigorous, logical, well-reasoned


词性：形容词
