camouflaged:/ˈkæməˌflɑːʒd/| adj.|伪装的，隐蔽的，|adj. disguised; hidden; concealed.  Camouflaged refers to something that is cleverly concealed or disguised, often to blend in with its surroundings.  It often implies a deliberate attempt to hide or deceive.|例句：The soldier was camouflaged in the forest.  士兵在森林里伪装了起来。

The chameleon camouflaged itself against the leaves. 变色龙伪装成树叶的颜色。|近义词：concealed, disguised, hidden, masked, obscured |反义词：exposed, revealed, visible, uncovered
