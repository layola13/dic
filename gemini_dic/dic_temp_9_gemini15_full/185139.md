retinting:/riːˈtɪntɪŋ/| n.|重新着色；重新染色|The act of applying a new color or tint to something, especially a photograph, painting, or hair.|例句：The retinting of the old photograph brought out the details lost over time. 重新着色旧照片使随着时间流逝而丢失的细节显现出来。
例句：The artist specialized in retinting antique maps.  这位艺术家专门从事古地图的重新着色。|近义词：recoloring, repainting, recoloring|反义词：fading, bleaching|名词
