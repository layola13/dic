digitised:/ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪzd/ | v. |数字化，数位化 | To convert information into a digital form, that is, into a form that can be processed by a computer.  This process typically involves converting analog data (such as images, sound, or text) into a series of binary digits (0s and 1s). | The library has digitised its entire collection of historical documents. 图书馆已经将其所有历史文献都数字化了。  |  digitalized, computerized | analogized, manualized | 动词
