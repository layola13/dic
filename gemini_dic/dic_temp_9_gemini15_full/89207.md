homestay:/ˈhoʊmˌsteɪ/| n. |寄宿家庭；家庭寄宿| A homestay is an arrangement where a visitor stays with a local family in their home, typically for a short period. This offers a cultural immersion experience, often including meals and interaction with the family.  | Example: I booked a homestay in a small village in Italy to experience the local culture.|近义词：home stay, family stay |反义词：hotel, hostel
