sited:/ˈsaɪtɪd/ | v. | 引用；引述；提及；位于 | To mention something as a source of information; to locate or place something. |例句：The article sited several studies to support its claims. 这篇文章引用了几项研究来支持其论点。
The factory is sited near the river. 这家工厂位于河边。|近义词：quoted, cited, mentioned, located |反义词：omitted, ignored, unmentioned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sites his sources meticulously. = 他一丝不苟地引用了他的资料来源。
The company sites its new headquarters in the city center. = 这家公司将其新的总部设在市中心。


一般过去时:
She sited the original text in her essay. = 她在论文中引用了原文。
They sited the monument on a hill overlooking the valley. = 他们把纪念碑建在俯瞰山谷的小山上。


现在完成时:
He has sited numerous examples to prove his point. = 他已经引用了许多例子来证明他的观点。
The project has been sited in a environmentally sensitive area. = 该项目已选址在一个环境敏感地区。


过去完成时:
They had sited the building before the environmental impact assessment. = 他们在环境影响评估之前就已经选址了这栋建筑。
She had sited several articles in her research paper. = 她在她的研究论文中已经引用了几篇文章。


将来时:
They will site the new hospital near the existing medical center. = 他们将在现有医疗中心附近建造新医院。
The researcher will site relevant data in his upcoming publication. = 研究人员将在即将出版的著作中引用相关数据。


虚拟语气:
If I were to write a paper, I would site credible sources. = 如果我要写论文，我会引用可靠的来源。
If they had sited the building elsewhere, the impact would have been less severe. = 如果他们把建筑物建在别处，影响就会小得多。


被动语态:
The study was sited in the Journal of Clinical Psychology. = 这项研究发表在《临床心理学杂志》上。
The new power plant will be sited in a remote location. = 新电厂将建在一个偏远地区。


疑问句:
Where was the experiment sited? = 实验在哪里进行的？
Has the article been sited correctly? = 这篇文章引用正确吗？


否定句:
He did not site any sources for his argument. = 他没有为他的论点引用任何来源。
The building was not sited appropriately for its intended purpose. = 这座建筑的选址与其预期用途不相符。
