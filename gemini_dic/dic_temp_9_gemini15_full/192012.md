scorns:/skɔːrnz/| n. v.|轻蔑；蔑视；嘲笑|The feeling or behavior of showing contempt or lack of respect for someone or something.  To show contempt for someone or something.|例句：She scorns those who don't share her beliefs. 她蔑视那些不认同她信仰的人。|例句：He scorned their offer of help. 他轻蔑地拒绝了他们提供的帮助。|近义词：contempt, disdain, derision, mockery|反义词：admiration, respect, approval|名词, 动词
