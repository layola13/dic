moribund:/ˈmɔːrɪbʌnd/| adj.|垂死的；即将结束的；衰败的|Approaching death; in a state of decline or decay.  The moribund economy is characterized by high unemployment and low growth.  |例句：The moribund industry is struggling to survive.  这个垂死的行业正在努力求生。
The moribund empire eventually collapsed.  这个衰败的帝国最终崩溃了。|近义词：dying, decaying, declining, failing, expiring|反义词：flourishing, thriving, booming, prosperous
