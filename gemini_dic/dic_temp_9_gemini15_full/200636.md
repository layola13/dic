slayer:/ˈsleɪər/| n.|杀手，屠杀者，毁灭者|A slayer is a person or thing that kills someone or something, especially in a violent way.  The word often implies a significant level of skill or power in the killing.|例句：The vampire slayer bravely faced the creature.  吸血鬼杀手勇敢地面对着那怪物。

例句：The dragon slayer became a legend.  屠龙英雄成为了传奇。


近义词：killer, murderer, assassin, destroyer|反义词：victim, savior


词源:源自古英语*slǣg-ere，由slǣgan（杀戮）和-ere（施动者）构成。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The slayer hunts vampires. = 杀手猎杀吸血鬼。
He slays his enemies with a sword. = 他用剑杀死他的敌人。


一般过去时:
The knight slayer the dragon. = 骑士杀死了巨龙。
She slayed all her opponents. = 她杀死了所有对手。


现在完成时:
The hero has slain many monsters. = 英雄已经杀死了许多怪物。
The warrior has slain countless foes. = 战士已经杀死了无数敌人。


过去完成时:
The villagers had slain the beast before dawn. = 村民们在黎明前杀死了野兽。
He had slain his rival in a duel. = 他在决斗中杀死了他的对手。


将来时:
The hunter will slay the bear. = 猎人将杀死那头熊。
She will slay her demons, both literal and metaphorical. = 她将杀死她的恶魔，无论是字面意义上的还是比喻意义上的。


虚拟语气:
If I were a slayer, I would protect the innocent. = 如果我是个杀手，我会保护无辜的人。
I wish I could slay the injustice in the world. = 我希望我能消灭世界上不公正的事情。


被动语态:
The dragon was slain by the knight. = 巨龙被骑士杀死了。
Many innocent people were slain in the war. = 许多无辜的人在战争中丧生。


疑问句:
Who is the slayer? = 谁是杀手？
Who slayed the beast? = 谁杀死了野兽？


否定句:
He did not slay the dragon. = 他没有杀死巨龙。
She would not slay the innocent. = 她不会杀死无辜的人。
