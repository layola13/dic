clypeus:/ˈklaɪpiəs/| n. |盾形物，额板|A clypeus is a shield-shaped structure, particularly referring to the anterior head region of certain insects and arachnids.  It's a hardened plate that protects the head.  In botany, it can also refer to a shield-like structure on a plant. |例句：The clypeus of the beetle was a striking iridescent green.  甲虫的额板呈现醒目的彩虹绿。|例句：The botanist identified the unusual shield-like structure as a clypeus. 植物学家将这种不寻常的盾状结构鉴定为额板。|近义词：shield (in a general sense), plate, frontal plate |反义词：无
