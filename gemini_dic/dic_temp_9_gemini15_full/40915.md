cooler:/ˈkuːlər/ | n., adj. |冷却器；凉爽的；冷静的 |

英文解析:
As a noun, a cooler refers to a container used to keep things cold, often food and drinks.  As an adjective, it describes something that is relatively cool in temperature or someone who is calm and composed.

例句:
Noun:
We took a cooler filled with ice and drinks to the beach. = 我们带了一个装满冰和饮料的冷却箱去海滩。

The refrigerator is a type of cooler. = 冰箱是一种冷却器。


Adjective:
The weather today is much cooler than yesterday. = 今天的天气比昨天凉爽得多。

He remained remarkably cooler under pressure. = 他在压力下保持了惊人的冷静。


近义词:
Noun: refrigerator, icebox, ice chest
Adjective: cold, refreshing, calm, composed


反义词:
Noun: heater, warmer
Adjective: hot, warm, agitated, flustered


词性:
Noun, Adjective
