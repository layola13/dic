heaviest:/ˈhɛviəst/| adj. |最重的；最重要的；最严重的|The heaviest object in the room is the piano.  房间里最重的东西是钢琴。|This is the heaviest rain we've had all year. 这是我们今年经历过的最大雨。|近义词：weightiest, most significant, most serious |反义词：lightest, least important, least serious
