/nɑːts/ | n. |笔记，记录；要点；简短的笔记 | Notes are brief records of information.  笔记是信息的简短记录。|  I took notes during the lecture. 我在讲座上做了笔记。| memoranda, minutes, annotations |  (无明显反义词) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He takes notes in class every day. 他每天上课都做笔记。
She makes notes of important points. 她记下重要的要点。

一般过去时:
I took notes on the meeting. 我记下了会议的笔记。
They took detailed notes during the presentation. 他们在演示期间做了详细的笔记。

现在完成时:
I have taken notes for this project all week. 我为这个项目做了一周的笔记了。
She has taken copious notes on the subject. 她在这个主题上做了大量的笔记。

过去完成时:
I had taken notes before the meeting started. 会议开始前我就已经做了笔记。
They had taken notes on the previous chapters. 他们已经记下了前几章的内容。

将来时:
I will take notes during the conference. 我将在会议期间做笔记。
She will take detailed notes for her research. 她将为她的研究做详细的笔记。

虚拟语气:
If I had taken notes, I would remember more. 如果我做了笔记，我会记得更多。
I wish I had taken more notes during the class. 我希望我上课时能多记些笔记。

被动语态:
Notes were taken by all the students. 所有学生都做了笔记。
Detailed notes are being taken by the secretary. 秘书正在做详细的笔记。

疑问句:
Did you take notes today? 你今天做笔记了吗？
Are you taking notes on this lecture? 你正在记下这个讲座的内容吗？

否定句:
I didn't take notes. 我没有做笔记。
He doesn't take notes very often. 他不经常做笔记。
