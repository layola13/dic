reminding:/rɪˈmaɪndɪŋ/ | v. |提醒；使想起 |The act of bringing something back to someone's memory or attention.  It often involves a gentle prompting or a nudge to recall something forgotten or overlooked. |例句：He kept reminding me of the deadline. 他不断提醒我截止日期。

例句：The photo reminded her of her childhood. 这张照片使她想起了童年。


近义词：recalling, prompting, reminding, jogging one's memory | 反义词：forgetting, ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reminds me to take my medicine every day. = 他每天都提醒我吃药。
She reminds her children to brush their teeth before bed. = 她提醒孩子们睡前刷牙。


一般过去时:
I reminded him about the meeting. = 我提醒了他会议的事。
She reminded me to lock the door. = 她提醒我锁门。


现在完成时:
I have reminded him several times already. = 我已经提醒他好几次了。
She has reminded me of my promise. = 她提醒了我我的承诺。


过去完成时:
I had reminded her before we left. = 我们离开之前我提醒过她。
He had reminded me of the appointment earlier. = 他早些时候提醒了我预约的事。


将来时:
I will remind you before the exam. = 我会在考试前提醒你。
She will remind him to call his mother. = 她会提醒他给妈妈打电话。


虚拟语气:
I wish I had reminded him earlier. = 我真希望我早点提醒他。
If I had reminded her, she might not have forgotten. = 如果我提醒了她，她可能就不会忘记了。


被动语态:
I was reminded of my appointment by a phone call. = 我接到电话后想起了我的约会。
He was reminded to submit his report by his boss. = 他被老板提醒要提交报告。


疑问句:
What reminded you of that? = 什么使你想起了那个？
Did anything remind you of your trip?  = 有什么东西让你想起了你的旅行吗？


否定句:
I didn't remind him about the party. = 我没有提醒他聚会的事。
She didn't remind me to buy milk. = 她没有提醒我买牛奶。


PHRASE->
remind someone of something = 使某人想起某事
remind someone to do something = 提醒某人做某事
a constant reminder = 不断的提醒
a gentle reminder = 温柔的提醒
a timely reminder = 及时的提醒
