recriminalizes: /ˌriːˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪzɪz/ | v. |重新定为犯罪；再次定罪 | To make something that was not previously illegal into a crime again.  |例句：The new law recriminalizes the possession of small amounts of marijuana. 这项新法律使持有少量大麻再次成为犯罪。
The government’s decision to recriminalize drug use has sparked widespread protests. 政府决定将吸毒重新定为犯罪行为引发了广泛的抗议。


近义词：make illegal again, reinstate criminal penalties for, re-prohibit


反义词：decriminalize, legalize


词性：动词
