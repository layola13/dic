haematosis:/ˌhiːmætˈoʊsɪs/| n. |造血作用；血细胞生成|The process of blood cell formation.  It involves the differentiation of hematopoietic stem cells into various blood cell types. |例句：The bone marrow is the primary site of haematosis. 骨髓是造血作用的主要场所。  The study focused on the regulation of haematosis in response to infection.  该研究侧重于感染反应中造血作用的调控。|近义词：haemopoiesis, hematopoiesis |反义词：无
