landforms:/ˈlændfɔːrmz/ | n. |地貌；地形 |Landforms are natural features of the earth's surface, such as mountains, valleys, plains, and plateaus.  They are shaped by a variety of geological processes, including erosion, weathering, and tectonic activity. |例句：The diverse landforms of the region include towering mountains, deep canyons, and fertile plains.  这个地区的地貌多种多样，包括高耸的山脉、深邃的峡谷和肥沃的平原。|The varied landforms of the national park attract many visitors each year. 国家公园多样的地貌每年吸引众多游客。 |近义词：terrain, topography, features |反义词：None specifically, but could consider "man-made structures" as a contrasting concept. |名词
