non-positivistic: /ˌnɒnˌpɒzɪˈtɪvɪstɪk/ | adj. |非实证主义的| Not based on or conforming to the principles of positivism; rejecting the belief that only empirically verifiable knowledge is valid.  It emphasizes the importance of subjective experience, interpretation, and values in understanding the world. |  The study employed a non-positivistic approach, focusing on the lived experiences of participants rather than solely on quantifiable data.  非实证主义的研究方法，侧重于参与者的生活体验，而不是仅仅关注可量化的数据。|近义词：interpretive, subjective, qualitative |反义词：positivistic, empirical, objective |
