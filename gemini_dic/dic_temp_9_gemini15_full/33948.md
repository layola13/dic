cirrose:/sɪˈroʊz/ | adj. | 肝硬化的；患有肝硬化的 | Affected by cirrhosis (a chronic liver disease).  Describes a condition, typically of the liver, characterized by scarring and loss of function. | The patient presented with cirrhotic liver disease.  这位病人患有肝硬化。|  cirrhotic | healthy, normal  | adj.

The liver is cirrhotic. = 肝脏是肝硬化的。
He shows signs of cirrhotic disease. = 他表现出肝硬化的迹象。

词源:
From "cirrhosis," ultimately from Greek  κίρῥωσις (kírrōsis),  from  κίρρος (kírros) "yellow," "orange," referring to the color of the affected liver.
