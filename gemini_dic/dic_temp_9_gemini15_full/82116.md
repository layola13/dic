groggy:/ˈɡrɑːɡi/ /ˈɡrɒɡi/ | adj. |昏昏沉沉的，迷迷糊糊的 |Feeling weak and confused, as if still partly asleep.  |例句：I felt groggy after the surgery. 手术后我感觉昏昏沉沉的。

例句：He woke up feeling groggy and disoriented. 他醒来时感到昏昏沉沉且迷失方向。


近义词：dazed, drowsy, sleepy, sluggish, lethargic |反义词：alert, awake, sharp, wide-awake


词源:  The word "groggy" likely originates from "grog," a type of alcoholic drink.  The connection isn't completely clear, but the implication is that the disoriented feeling is similar to the effects of being intoxicated.  The suffix "-y" adds an adjectival quality.
