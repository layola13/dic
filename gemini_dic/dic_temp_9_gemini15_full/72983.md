foolhardy:/ˈfulˌhɑːrdi/| adj. |鲁莽的；轻率的；冒失的 | Recklessly bold or rash; taking unnecessary risks.  It implies a lack of judgment and consideration for potential consequences. | Example: It was foolhardy of him to climb the mountain in such bad weather. 他在如此恶劣的天气里爬山真是鲁莽。

His foolhardy attempt to cross the river resulted in a near-drowning experience. 他鲁莽地试图渡河，差点淹死。|近义词：reckless, rash, impetuous, brash, audacious |反义词：cautious, prudent, careful, circumspect
