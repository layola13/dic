overdepressing: /ˌoʊvər dɪˈprɛsɪŋ/ | adj. | 极度令人沮丧的，过度压抑的 |  Extremely depressing; excessively suppressing emotions.  It describes a state or situation that causes a level of sadness or hopelessness that goes beyond typical depression.  This word is not a standard dictionary entry and is considered a colloquialism or a newly coined word. | 例句：The constant negativity and criticism were overdepressing.  持续的消极和批评令人极度沮丧。 The news was overdepressing, leaving him feeling completely hopeless. 这个消息令人极度沮丧，让他感到完全绝望。 | 近义词：extremely depressing, overwhelmingly sad, profoundly disheartening, utterly discouraging | 反义词： uplifting, exhilarating, encouraging, inspiring | 词性：形容词
