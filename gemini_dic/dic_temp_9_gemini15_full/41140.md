copublish:/koʊˈpʌblɪʃ/| vt.|合作出版，联合出版|To publish something jointly with another publisher or organization.  This often involves sharing costs and resources.|例句：Two universities will copublish the research findings.  两所大学将合作出版这项研究成果。|近义词：jointly publish, collaborate to publish|反义词：solely publish, independently publish
