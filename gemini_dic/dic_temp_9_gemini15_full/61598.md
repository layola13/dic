emersion:/ɪˈmɜːrʒən/| n. |浮现；出现；摆脱；沉浸|The act of emerging; the process of coming out of something.  It can also refer to the state of being deeply involved in something.|例句：The emersion of the sun from behind the clouds brought warmth and light.  太阳从云层后面出现带来了温暖和光明。The emersion of new technologies is transforming our lives. 新技术的出现正在改变我们的生活。|近义词：appearance, emergence, surfacing|反义词：submersion, immersion


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sun emerges from behind the clouds every morning. = 太阳每天早上从云层后面升起。
New technologies are constantly emerging in the modern world. = 现代世界不断涌现新技术。


一般过去时:
The submarine emerged from the depths of the ocean. = 潜艇从海洋深处浮出水面。
A new trend emerged in the fashion industry last year. = 去年时尚界出现了一种新趋势。


现在完成时:
Several new problems have emerged since the project began. = 自项目开始以来，出现了一些新问题。
The truth has finally emerged after years of investigation. = 多年调查后，真相终于大白。


过去完成时:
By the time the rescue team arrived, the survivors had already emerged from the wreckage. = 当救援队到达时，幸存者们已经从残骸中脱险了。
It had emerged that the company was involved in illegal activities. = 后来发现该公司参与了非法活动。


将来时:
New challenges will emerge as the company expands. = 随着公司的扩张，新的挑战将会出现。
We expect a new leader to emerge from the political party soon. = 我们预计很快就会有新的领导人从该政党中出现。


虚拟语气:
If a new opportunity emerged, I would take it. = 如果出现新的机会，我会抓住它。
I wish a solution had emerged sooner. = 我希望解决方案早点出现。


被动语态:
The truth was finally emerged through a thorough investigation. = 真相最终通过彻底调查浮出水面。
The problem was emerged after a series of tests. = 在一系列测试之后，问题出现了。


疑问句:
When will the new product emerge on the market? = 新产品什么时候上市？
What factors caused this new trend to emerge? = 哪些因素导致了这种新趋势的出现？


否定句:
No new evidence has emerged in this case. = 此案中没有出现新的证据。
The truth did not emerge until years later. = 真相直到数年后才大白。
