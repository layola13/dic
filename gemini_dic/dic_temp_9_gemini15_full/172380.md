puttyless:/ˈpʌtiːləs/| adj.| 无腻子的；不需腻子的 |  Free from or not requiring putty.  This term is primarily used in the context of construction and glazing, referring to materials or methods that do not require the use of putty for sealing or filling gaps. | Example:  The new window frames are puttyless, making installation much quicker and easier.  新的窗框无需腻子，安装速度更快更容易。 | 近义词：putty-free, seamless | 反义词：putty-filled
