giftedness:/ˈɡɪftɪdnəs/| n. |天赋，天资|Exceptional talent or ability, especially in a particular area.  It refers to the possession of high levels of aptitude, skill, or talent in one or more domains.  |例句：Her giftedness in mathematics was evident from a young age.  她的数学天赋从小就显而易见。

例句：His musical giftedness led him to a successful career as a composer. 他的音乐天赋使他成为一名成功的作曲家。


近义词：talent, aptitude, ability, genius, brilliance|反义词：inability, incompetence, deficiency

词源:
The word "giftedness" derives from the adjective "gifted," which ultimately comes from the Old English word "gifu," meaning "gift."  The suffix "-edness" signifies a state or quality.  Therefore, "giftedness" literally refers to the state of being endowed with a gift or talent.
