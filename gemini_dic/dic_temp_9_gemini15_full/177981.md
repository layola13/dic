recircled:/ˌriːˈsɜːrkld/ | adj. | 再循环的；重新循环的 |  Having been circulated again; reused.  This refers to something that has been put back into circulation or a process.  | 例句：The recircled water was used to cool the system. = 再循环的水被用来冷却系统。

The company uses recircled materials in its products. = 这家公司在其产品中使用再循环材料。


近义词：recycled, reused, re-circulated | 反义词：unused, new, virgin (referring to materials) | 词性：形容词

词源:
The word "recircled" is formed from the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "back") and the word "circulated," which comes from the Latin word "circulare" (meaning "to go around").  Therefore, "recircled" literally means "circulated again."
