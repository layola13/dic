quizzingly:/ˈkwɪzɪŋli/| adv.| 质问地；好奇地；追问地 |In a questioning or curious manner; inquisitively.  It suggests a probing, perhaps slightly challenging, way of asking questions. |例句：He looked at me quizzingly, waiting for an explanation. 他用质问的眼神看着我，等着我解释。

例句：She quizzingly asked about his sudden wealth. 她好奇地询问他暴富的原因。


近义词：inquisitively, questioningly, interrogatively, suspiciously

反义词：unquestioningly, passively, uncritically


词源:  Derived from the word "quiz," meaning a short test or examination, which in turn comes from the Irish word "quis," meaning "who" or "what." The "-ingly" suffix transforms the verb or adjective into an adverb, indicating the manner in which something is done.
