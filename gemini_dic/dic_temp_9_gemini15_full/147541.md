overpayments:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪmənts/ | n. | 超额支付；多付的款项 | The act of paying more than is due or required.  It often refers to situations where an individual or organization receives more money than they are entitled to. |例句：The company discovered several overpayments in its payroll system.  公司发现其工资系统中有多次超额支付。

The accounting department is investigating the overpayments to vendors. 会计部门正在调查供应商的超额支付。


近义词：excess payments, extra payments, surplus payments | 反义词：underpayments, short payments | 词性:Noun
