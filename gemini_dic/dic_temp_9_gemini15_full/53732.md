discography:/dɪˈskɒɡrəfi/ | n. |唱片目录，唱片作品集 | A discography is a list of recordings (usually phonograph records, but also including CDs, cassette tapes, and other media) by a musical artist or band.  It typically includes details such as release dates, record labels, and sometimes track listings. | Example: The band released a comprehensive discography to celebrate their 20th anniversary.  乐队发行了一份全面的唱片目录以庆祝他们的20周年纪念。  |近义词：catalogue, list of recordings | 反义词：无
