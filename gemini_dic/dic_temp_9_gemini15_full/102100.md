isostasy:/aɪˈsɒstəsi/ | n. |均衡地壳均衡|The state of gravitational equilibrium between Earth's lithosphere and asthenosphere such that the lithosphere floats at an elevation that depends on its thickness and density.|例句：The concept of isostasy helps explain the variations in elevation across the Earth's surface. 地壳均衡的概念有助于解释地球表面海拔变化。The mountain range is in isostatic equilibrium with the underlying mantle.  这个山脉与下面的地幔处于地壳均衡状态。|近义词：equilibrium, balance|反义词：disequilibrium, imbalance
