intoned:/ɪnˈtoʊnd/| v.|吟诵；语气为…地说；|to utter (words) in a particular tone or intonation.|例句：He intoned the prayer in a low, solemn voice. 他用低沉庄严的声音吟诵祈祷文。|近义词：chanted, recited, uttered|反义词：mumbled, whispered


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He intone the words clearly. = 他清晰地吟诵这些词语。
She intone a beautiful melody. = 她吟诵了一首优美的旋律。

一般过去时:
The priest intoned the blessing. = 牧师吟诵了祝福。
The singer intoned the final verse with feeling. = 歌手饱含感情地吟诵了最后一段诗句。
