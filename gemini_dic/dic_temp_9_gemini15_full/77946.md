In modern biology, it primarily refers to a group of organisms sharing certain characteristics. | Example:  The study examined the genos of the ancient civilization. 这项研究考察了古代文明的种族。  The researchers analyzed the genos of the different plant species. 研究人员分析了不同植物物种的基因。|近义词：race, lineage, ancestry, stock, strain |反义词：N/A
