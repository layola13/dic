词源:
The word "julep" comes from the Persian word "gulāb", meaning "rosewater".  Through Arabic ("ghulāb"), it entered French ("julep") and eventually English, initially referring to a medicinal syrup.  Over time, its meaning shifted to the alcoholic beverage we know today.
