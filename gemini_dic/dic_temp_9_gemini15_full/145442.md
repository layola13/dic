overcherishing: /ˌoʊvərˈtʃerɪʃɪŋ/ | v. |过度珍爱，过度珍惜 |  The act of cherishing something excessively; valuing something too highly, often to the point of detriment.  This is not a standard dictionary word, but rather a combination of "over-" (meaning excessively) and "cherishing" (meaning to hold dear).  It implies a level of care and protection that is excessive or unhealthy.

例句：

He was overcherishing his old toys, refusing to let go of them even though they were broken and outdated. = 他过度珍爱他的旧玩具，即使它们坏了过时了也不肯扔掉。

Her overcherishing of her child led to her neglecting other important aspects of her life. = 她过度溺爱孩子导致她忽略了生活的其他重要方面。


近义词：  spoiling, coddling, pampering, overprotecting

反义词： neglecting, ignoring, abandoning, disregarding


词性: 动词
