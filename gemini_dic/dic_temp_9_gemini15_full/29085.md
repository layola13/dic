carvone:/ˈkɑːrvoʊn/|/ˈkɑːrvən/| n. |香芹酮|Carvone is a cyclic ketone, a chiral molecule that occurs in two enantiomeric forms: (–)-carvone and (+)-carvone.  It is a colorless liquid with a strong, pungent odor.|The spearmint scent of spearmint gum comes from (–)-carvone, while caraway seeds smell of (+)-carvone. |近义词：none (unique chemical compound)|反义词：none (unique chemical compound)|名词
