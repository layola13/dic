grudge:/ɡrʌdʒ/ | n. v. | 怨恨；恶意；吝啬；勉强给予 | A feeling of resentment or ill will; to be unwilling or reluctant to give or allow (something). |


英文解析:A grudge is a persistent feeling of anger or resentment, especially one that is kept secret.  As a verb, it means to unwillingly or resentfully give or allow something.


例句:

She held a grudge against him for years.  = 她多年来一直对他怀恨在心。

He grudged paying so much for the tickets. = 他很不情愿花这么多钱买票。


近义词:resentment, animosity, bitterness, malice, spite

反义词:forgiveness, reconciliation, affection, goodwill


词性:Noun, Verb
