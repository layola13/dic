idiom:/ˈɪdiəm/| n. |习语，成语|A phrase or expression whose meaning cannot be understood from the meanings of the individual words. Idioms are characteristic of a particular language, and often cannot be translated literally into another language.|例句： "Kick the bucket" is an idiom meaning "to die".  “一脚踢桶”是表示“死亡”的习语。|近义词：proverb, saying, expression|反义词：literal meaning
