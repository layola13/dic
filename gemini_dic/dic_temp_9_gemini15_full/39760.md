consoles:/ˈkɒnsəʊlz/ /ˈkɑːnsoʊlz/ | n. |游戏机；控制台；操纵台 |A console is typically an electronic device used for gaming, but it can also refer to a control panel or a cabinet that houses electronic equipment.  Consoles often have various input and output devices, such as joysticks, buttons, and screens. | Examples:  My favorite game console is the PlayStation 5.  我的最喜欢的游戏机是PlayStation 5。 The flight engineer monitored the aircraft's instruments from the console. 飞行工程师从操纵台监控飞机的仪表。 |近义词：control panel, dashboard, terminal |反义词：None specifically, depends on context.
