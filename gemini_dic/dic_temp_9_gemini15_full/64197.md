equiangular:/ˌiːkwiˈæŋɡjʊlər/| adj.|等角的，多角等角的|Having all angles equal.  An equiangular triangle, for example, has three equal angles (each 60 degrees). The term is often used in geometry to describe shapes with angles of equal measure.|例句：An equilateral triangle is also equiangular.|近义词：equal-angled|反义词：scalene (for triangles)
