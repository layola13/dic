eyebrow:/ˈaɪbraʊ/| n.|眉毛|The eyebrow is the strip of hair above the eye.  It helps to protect the eye from sweat and debris.  Eyebrows also play a role in facial expression.|例句：She has beautifully arched eyebrows. 她有着漂亮的弓形眉。|例句：He raised his eyebrows in surprise. 他惊讶地扬起了眉毛。|近义词：brow|反义词:None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has thick eyebrows. = 他有浓密的眉毛。
My eyebrows are naturally dark. = 我的眉毛天生就比较深。


一般过去时:
She plucked her eyebrows yesterday. = 她昨天修了眉毛。
He shaved his eyebrows for a movie role. = 为了电影角色，他剃掉了眉毛。


现在完成时:
I have dyed my eyebrows several times. = 我已经染过几次眉毛了。
She has always had perfectly shaped eyebrows. = 她一直都有完美的眉形。


过去完成时:
Before the photoshoot, she had already shaped her eyebrows. = 拍摄之前，她已经修过眉毛了。
He had never considered eyebrow makeup before. = 他以前从未考虑过眉毛化妆。


将来时:
I will get my eyebrows threaded next week. = 我下周要去修眉毛。

虚拟语气:
If I had thicker eyebrows, I wouldn't need to use eyebrow pencil. = 如果我有更浓的眉毛，我就不用用眉笔了。
I wish my eyebrows were more symmetrical. = 我希望我的眉毛更对称一些。


被动语态:
Her eyebrows were perfectly sculpted for the event. = 为了这次活动，她的眉毛被完美地修饰了。
His eyebrows are often mistaken for being artificially enhanced. = 他的眉毛经常被误认为是人工增强的。


疑问句:

否定句:
