gigabit:/ˈɡɪɡəbɪt/| n.|千兆位, 十亿位|A gigabit is a unit of information equal to one billion bits.  It's commonly used to measure data transfer rates.|例句：The internet connection offers speeds up to 1 gigabit per second. 这款网络连接提供的速度高达每秒 1 千兆位。|例句：The server boasts a gigabit Ethernet connection for fast data transfer. 这台服务器拥有千兆以太网连接，可实现快速数据传输。|近义词：billion bits|反义词：kilobit, megabit|名词
