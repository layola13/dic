festschrift:/ˈfɛstʃrɪft/| n. |纪念文集；祝寿论文集 | A festschrift is a collection of scholarly papers written by various authors and dedicated to a person (usually a scholar) on a special occasion, such as a birthday, retirement, or anniversary.  It is often published as a book or a special journal issue. | Example:  The university published a festschrift in honor of Professor Smith's 70th birthday.  大学出版了一本纪念文集，以庆祝史密斯教授70岁生日。|近义词：scholarly tribute, commemorative volume |反义词：无
