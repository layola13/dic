parallelograms:/ˌpærəˈlɛləɡræmz/ | n. | 平行四边形 | A quadrilateral with opposite sides parallel.  Parallelograms include rectangles, squares, rhombuses, and rhombi. | Example:  The area of the parallelogram is calculated by multiplying base times height.  这个平行四边形的面积是通过底乘以高计算出来的。|近义词：quadrilateral | 反义词：无

词源:
From  parallel + -ogram (from Greek -gramma, "something drawn").  The word combines "parallel" (meaning having the same direction and never meeting) and "-gram" (a suffix indicating a geometric figure).  Thus, a parallelogram is a figure with parallel sides.
