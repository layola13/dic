counterinterpretations: /ˌkaʊntərɪnˌtɜːrprɪˈteɪʃənz/ | n. |反解释，对立解释 | Alternative or opposing interpretations of something.  These interpretations challenge the dominant or established understanding.  They often highlight overlooked aspects or offer different perspectives. | Example: The historian offered compelling counterinterpretations to the traditional narrative of the war, emphasizing the role of economic factors.  历史学家对战争的传统叙述提出了令人信服的反解释，强调了经济因素的作用。 | 近义词：alternative interpretations, opposing views, rebuttals | 反义词：confirmations, verifications | 名词
