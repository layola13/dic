imploded:/ɪmˈploʊdɪd/ | v. |内爆；崩溃；瓦解 | To collapse inward violently, especially because of internal pressure.  |例句：The old building imploded during the demolition. 旧楼在拆除过程中内爆了。|例句：His world imploded after he lost his job and his wife.  在他失去工作和妻子后，他的世界崩溃了。|近义词：collapse, cave in, crumble |反义词：explode


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The building implodes under its own weight. 这栋建筑物在自身重量下内爆。
The company's market share implodes due to poor management.  由于管理不善，公司的市场份额正在崩溃。

一般过去时:
The star imploded, creating a black hole. 这颗恒星内爆，形成了一个黑洞。
The economy imploded after the financial crisis. 金融危机后，经济崩溃了。

现在完成时:
The pressure has imploded the container. 压力已经使容器内爆。
His reputation has imploded after the scandal. 丑闻之后，他的名声已经崩溃了。

过去完成时:
The bridge had imploded before the rescue team arrived.  救援队到达之前，桥梁已经内爆了。
The company had imploded before the investors could intervene. 在投资者能够介入之前，公司就已经崩溃了。

将来时:
The reactor will implode if the safety systems fail. 如果安全系统失效，反应堆将内爆。
The project will implode if we don't secure more funding. 如果我们无法获得更多资金，项目将崩溃。

虚拟语气:
If the pressure were higher, the vessel would implode. 如果压力更高，容器就会内爆。
If he hadn't made those mistakes, his career wouldn't have imploded. 如果他没有犯那些错误，他的职业生涯就不会崩溃了。

被动语态:
The building was imploded using controlled explosives. 这栋建筑物是用受控炸药爆破的。（内爆，这里指人为爆破）
The system was imploded by a software error. 系统因软件错误而崩溃。

疑问句:
Did the building implode because of structural failure? 建筑物内爆是因为结构性故障吗？
Will the company implode if the CEO resigns? 如果首席执行官辞职，公司会崩溃吗？

否定句:
The building didn't implode; it was carefully demolished. 这栋建筑物没有内爆；它是被小心地拆除了。
The negotiations didn't implode despite the disagreements. 尽管存在分歧，谈判并没有破裂。
