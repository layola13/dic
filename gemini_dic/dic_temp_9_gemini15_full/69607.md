feme:/fem/| n.|妇女，妻子|The word "feme" is an archaic term for a married woman,  often used in legal contexts.  It's a shortened form of "femme," the French word for woman.|例句：In old legal documents, the term "feme covert" referred to a married woman under the legal control of her husband.  在旧的法律文件中，“feme covert”指的是受丈夫法律控制的已婚妇女。|近义词：woman, wife, spouse|反义词：husband, man
