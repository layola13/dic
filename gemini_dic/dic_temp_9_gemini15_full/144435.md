outswam:/aʊtˈswæm/| v.|游过；游出；超过（游泳）|To swim farther or faster than (someone or something); to surpass in swimming.|例句：He outswam all the other competitors in the race. 他在比赛中超过了所有其他竞争者。|例句：The strong swimmer outswam the current and reached the shore safely.  这位强壮的游泳者战胜了水流，安全地到达了岸边。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip|反义词：be outswam by


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outswims everyone in the team. 他在队里比任何人都游得好。
She outswims the other girls in the competition.  她在比赛中比其他女孩游得快。

一般过去时:
The experienced swimmer outswam the novice.  经验丰富的游泳者超过了新手。
He outswam the strong current. 他战胜了强劲的水流。


现在完成时:
She has outswam all her previous personal bests. 她已经超过了她之前的所有个人最好成绩。
He has outswam his brother in every race this year.  今年的每场比赛他都超过了他哥哥。


过去完成时:
By the time the race ended, she had outswam everyone else.  比赛结束时，她已经超过了其他人。
He had outswam the other competitors before the final lap.  在最后一圈之前，他已经超过了其他竞争对手。


将来时:
I believe she will outswim her rivals in the upcoming competition. 我相信她在即将到来的比赛中会超过她的对手。
He thinks he will outswim the current this time. 他认为这次他能战胜水流。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he would have outswam his opponent. 如果他训练更努力，他就会超过他的对手。
If she were a stronger swimmer, she would outswim the current.如果她是一个更强壮的游泳者，她就能战胜水流。


被动语态:
He was outswam by the younger competitor. 他被年轻的竞争对手超过了。
The team was outswam in the final race.  该队在决赛中被超过了。


疑问句:
Did he outswim the other swimmers? 他超过了其他游泳者吗？
Will she outswim her opponent in the next race?  她在下一场比赛中会超过她的对手吗？


否定句:
He didn't outswim the champion. 他没有超过冠军。
She couldn't outswim the strong current. 她无法战胜强劲的水流。
