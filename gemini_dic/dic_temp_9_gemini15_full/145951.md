overdiscusses:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskʌsɪz/| v.|过度讨论，讨论过多|To discuss something excessively or to a point of being unproductive or tiresome.  It implies that the discussion has gone on for too long or has covered unnecessary details.|例句：He overdiscusses every minor detail, making meetings unnecessarily long. 他对每一个小细节都过度讨论，使得会议不必要地冗长。
They overdiscussed the project, delaying the actual work. 他们对项目过度讨论，延误了实际工作。|近义词：overanalyze, belabor, dwell on|反义词：briefly discuss, gloss over
