noninfantry: /ˌnɒnˈɪnfən(t)ri/ | adj. | 非步兵的；非步兵部队的 | Not belonging to or relating to infantry.  This adjective describes anything that is not associated with infantry troops, their equipment, tactics, or roles.  It can apply to units, weapons, strategies, or even support roles within a larger military context. | 例句：The noninfantry support units provided crucial logistical assistance.  非步兵支援部队提供了重要的后勤支援。


The air assault was primarily a noninfantry operation. 空降突击主要是一次非步兵作战行动。| 近义词：non-infantry, noncombatant (in some contexts)| 反义词：infantry, ground troops |


词源:  The word is a compound of "non-" (meaning "not") and "infantry" (referring to soldiers fighting on foot).  "Infantry" itself originates from the Italian word *fanteria*, derived from *fante*, meaning "foot soldier."  The prefix "non-" is of Latin origin.
