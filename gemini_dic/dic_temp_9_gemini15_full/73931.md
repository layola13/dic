forte:/ˈfɔːrteɪ/| n.|擅长，强项|a person's strong point; what someone does well|Her forte is playing the piano.  她的强项是弹钢琴。|近义词：strength, talent, specialty|反义词：weakness, flaw


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your forte? = 你的强项是什么？
What is her forte in this project? = 她在这个项目中的强项是什么？


一般过去时:
What was his forte in the debate? = 他在辩论中的强项是什么？
What was their forte in the competition? = 他们在比赛中的强项是什么？


现在完成时:
What has been your forte throughout your career? = 贯穿你整个职业生涯的强项是什么？
What has consistently been her forte? = 她一直以来的强项是什么？


过去完成时:
What had been his forte before he changed careers? = 他转行之前，他的强项是什么？
What had always been their forte in the family business? = 在家族企业中，他们一直以来的强项是什么？


将来时:
What will be your forte in the future? = 你未来的强项是什么？
What will be their forte in the upcoming market? = 他们在即将到来的市场中的强项是什么？


虚拟语气:
If only I knew what my forte was. = 要是我知道我的强项是什么就好了。
If she knew her forte, she would pursue it. = 如果她知道自己的强项，她就会去追求它。


被动语态:
His forte was not highlighted in the presentation. = 他的强项在演示中没有得到突出。
Her forte was often overlooked by her colleagues. = 她的强项经常被她的同事们忽视。


疑问句:
What is your company's forte? = 贵公司的强项是什么？
What's the forte of this new software? = 这款新软件的强项是什么？


否定句:
That's not my forte. = 那不是我的强项。
Public speaking is not her forte. = 公开演讲不是她的强项。


PHRASE->
a forte of = ...的强项
his/her/their forte = 他/她/他们的强项
to be one's forte = 是某人的强项
show one's forte = 展现某人的强项
a particular forte = 特别擅长之处
