industrialising: /ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 工业化 | The process of developing industries in a region or country.  It involves transforming an economy from primarily agricultural or resource-based to one dominated by manufacturing and other industrial activities. This often includes advancements in technology, infrastructure, and labor practices. |  例句：Many developing countries are rapidly industrialising. 许多发展中国家正在迅速工业化。

例句：The government's policies are aimed at industrialising the rural areas. 政府的政策旨在将农村地区工业化。


近义词：industrialization (n.), developing (v.)

反义词：deindustrializing (v.), agrarianizing (v.)
