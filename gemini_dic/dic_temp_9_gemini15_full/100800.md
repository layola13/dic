intrigue:/ɪnˈtriːɡ/| n./v.|阴谋；激起兴趣；迷惑|The secret plot to overthrow the government was a complex intrigue.  The mystery of the missing jewels intrigued the detective.|近义词：conspiracy, plot, fascinate, puzzle|反义词：clarify, enlighten


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The spy intrigues against the enemy. = 间谍策划反对敌人的阴谋。
His strange behavior intrigues me. = 他奇怪的行为引起我的兴趣。

一般过去时:
The nobles intrigued to seize power. = 贵族们密谋夺权。
The ancient ruins intrigued the archaeologists. = 古代遗址激起了考古学家的兴趣。

现在完成时:
They have intrigued against the king for years. = 他们多年来一直密谋反对国王。
The artist's latest work has intrigued critics and audiences alike. = 艺术家的最新作品同样吸引了评论家和观众。

过去完成时:
The conspirators had intrigued for months before the assassination. = 阴谋者在暗杀之前已经密谋了几个月。
I had been intrigued by the story ever since I first heard it. = 从我第一次听到这个故事以来，我就一直被它吸引住了。

将来时:
She will intrigue her way into the highest circles of society. = 她将通过阴谋诡计进入社会最高层。
The new exhibit will intrigue visitors with its innovative designs. = 新展览将以其创新的设计吸引游客。

虚拟语气:
If they had not intrigued, the kingdom would have prospered. = 如果他们没有密谋，王国就会繁荣昌盛。
I wish I hadn't been so intrigued by that dangerous game.  = 我真希望自己没有那么被那场危险的游戏吸引。

被动语态:
The prime minister was intrigued by the opposition's proposal. = 总理对反对党的提议很感兴趣。
The audience was intrigued by the magician's performance. = 观众被魔术师的表演迷住了。

疑问句:
Does the plot of the novel intrigue you? = 这部小说的情节吸引你吗？
What intrigues you about this subject? = 关于这个主题，什么东西吸引了你？


否定句:
The simple explanation didn't intrigue her. = 简单的解释并没有引起她的兴趣。
He wasn't intrigued by the offer of a promotion. = 他对晋升的机会并不感兴趣。
