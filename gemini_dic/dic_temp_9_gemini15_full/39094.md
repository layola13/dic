confrere:/ˈkɒnˌfrɛər/| n. |同事，同行，兄弟会成员 | A fellow member of a profession or a group, especially one in a religious order.  |例句：He's a respected confrère in the legal profession. 他是法律界一位受人尊敬的同行。|近义词：colleague, associate, peer |反义词：rival, opponent, adversary |名词
