in-step: /ˌɪnˈstɛp/ | adj. |同步的，协调一致的 |  In step describes something or someone moving or working together in a synchronized or coordinated manner.  It implies a harmonious rhythm and agreement of movement or action. |例句：The dancers moved in perfect step. 舞蹈演员们动作完美一致。The team worked in step to achieve their goal.  团队齐心协力地实现了目标。|近义词：synchronized, coordinated, harmonious, in unison |反义词：out of step, discordant, uncoordinated
