muslim:/ˈmʌzlɪm/| n. |穆斯林，伊斯兰教徒 |A follower of Islam.  A Muslim is a person who follows the religion of Islam. |Example:  He is a devout Muslim who prays five times a day. 他是一位虔诚的穆斯林，每天祈祷五次。


There are many Muslims living in this city.  这个城市居住着许多穆斯林。|近义词：Islamist (sometimes used, but can be considered controversial)|反义词：None (this is a religious affiliation, not a descriptive adjective with an inherent opposite) |名词

词源:
The word "Muslim" comes from the Arabic word "مسلم" (muslim), meaning "one who submits (to God's will)".  It is derived from the verb "سَلَمَ" (salama), meaning "to be safe, be secure, be in peace, submit".  The word entered English via French and other European languages.
