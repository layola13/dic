restickable: /ˌriːˈstɪkəbl/ | adj. | 可重复粘贴的，可再粘贴的 | Able to be stuck down again after having been removed.  This typically refers to adhesives or items with adhesives that can be reused multiple times. | Example: The restickable notes are great for jotting down reminders.  这些可重复粘贴的便签纸非常适合记下提醒事项。  The label is restickable, so you can reposition it if needed. 这个标签是可以重复粘贴的，所以你可以根据需要重新定位它。 | 近义词：reusable, repositionable | 反义词：disposable, permanent
