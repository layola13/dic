ominate:/ˈɒmɪneɪt/| vt.|预示；是…的征兆|to be a sign that something bad will happen in the future |The dark clouds ominated a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨。|The strange silence ominated trouble.  奇怪的寂静预示着麻烦。|近义词：foreshadow, portend, presage|反义词：foretell (good things)

一般现在时:
The dark clouds ominate a storm. = 乌云预示着暴风雨。
The strange silence ominates trouble. = 奇怪的寂静预示着麻烦。

一般过去时:
The priest ominated misfortune for the king. = 牧师预言国王将遭遇不幸。
The eerie silence ominated the tragic event. = 怪异的寂静预示着悲剧的发生。

现在完成时:
Recent events have ominated a crisis in the banking sector. = 最近的事件预示着银行业将面临危机。
The rising tensions have ominated a potential conflict. = 紧张局势的加剧预示着可能发生冲突。


过去完成时:
The dark clouds had ominated a severe storm earlier in the day. = 早些时候乌云就预示着一场严重的暴风雨。
The unusual behavior of the animals had ominated the earthquake. = 动物们异常的行为预示着地震的到来。


将来时:
The forecast ominates heavy rain for tomorrow. = 预报预示着明天将有大雨。
Experts ominate a significant economic downturn next year. = 专家们预测明年经济将出现大幅下滑。


虚拟语气:
If the signs had ominated disaster, we would have acted differently. = 如果迹象预示着灾难，我们会采取不同的行动。
I wish the early warnings had ominated the severity of the hurricane. = 我希望早期的预警能预示出飓风的严重性。


被动语态:
A crisis was ominated by the recent stock market crash. = 最近的股市崩盘预示着危机的到来。
A potential war was ominated by the escalating tensions between the two countries. = 两国之间不断升级的紧张局势预示着可能爆发战争。


疑问句:
Does this ominous silence ominate trouble? = 这种不祥的沉默是否预示着麻烦？
Do the current economic indicators ominate a recession? = 当前的经济指标是否预示着经济衰退？


否定句:
The early signs did not ominate any serious problems. = 早期的迹象并没有预示任何严重的问题。
The weather forecast did not ominate any significant changes. = 天气预报没有预示任何重大变化。
