placement:/ˈpleɪsmənt/ | n. | 安置；职位；位置；安排；实习；投资 | The act of placing someone or something in a particular position or situation.  It can refer to the act of finding a job for someone, assigning someone to a particular role, or positioning something in a specific place. It also refers to a temporary position, often an internship.  | 例句：The placement of the furniture was carefully considered. 家具的摆放经过仔细考虑。


The company's new placement program helps graduates find jobs.  这家公司的新安置计划帮助毕业生找到工作。

近义词：location, position, arrangement, posting, appointment | 反义词：removal, dismissal
