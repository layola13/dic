rearranges:/ˌriːəˈreɪndʒɪz/ | vt. |重新安排；改组 | To change the arrangement or order of something.  |例句：She rearranges the furniture in her living room every few months. 她每隔几个月就会重新布置客厅的家具。 |例句：The company is rearranging its management structure to improve efficiency. 公司正在调整其管理结构以提高效率。 |近义词：reorder, reorganize, rearrange, reshuffle |反义词：disorganize, disorder


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rearranges his schedule frequently. = 他经常重新安排他的日程。
She rearranges the books on the shelf. = 她重新整理书架上的书。

一般过去时:
They rearranged the seating plan for the conference. = 他们重新安排了会议的座位安排。
He rearranged the files on his computer. = 他重新整理了电脑上的文件。

现在完成时:
She has rearranged her office to be more efficient. = 她已经重新布置了她的办公室，使其更有效率。
They have rearranged the meeting to a later time. = 他们已经将会议时间改到了稍后。

过去完成时:
He had rearranged his priorities after the accident. = 事故之后，他重新安排了他的优先事项。
She had rearranged the flowers before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经重新摆放了鲜花。

将来时:
I will rearrange the appointment for next week. = 我将把约会安排到下周。
They will rearrange the furniture before the party. = 他们将在派对前重新布置家具。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rearrange my work schedule. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新安排我的工作计划。
I wish I could rearrange my trip to include more sightseeing. = 我希望我能重新安排我的旅行，以便包含更多观光活动。

被动语态:
The furniture was rearranged for the party. = 家具为聚会重新布置了。
The schedule was rearranged due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，日程被重新安排了。

疑问句:
Will you rearrange the meeting for me? = 你能为我重新安排会议吗？
Have they rearranged the seating chart yet? = 他们已经重新安排座位表了吗？

否定句:
I cannot rearrange my schedule at the moment. = 我目前无法重新安排我的日程。
He did not rearrange the files before leaving. = 他离开前没有重新整理文件。
