dammed:/dæmd/ | adj. |该死的，糟糕的，被诅咒的 |  Past participle of "damn," expressing strong disapproval or condemnation. It can also refer to something obstructed by a dam. |例句：This dammed project is taking forever! = 这个该死的项目永远都完不成！

例句：The river was dammed to create a reservoir. = 河流被筑坝以形成水库。


近义词：cursed, blasted, confounded, accursed | 反义词：blessed, fortunate, lucky | 词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is dammed by bureaucracy. = 这个项目被官僚主义阻碍了。
This situation is dammed if you do and dammed if you don't. = 不管你做什么，情况都很糟糕。


一般过去时:
The road was dammed by a landslide. = 道路被山体滑坡阻断了。
The plan was dammed by a lack of funding. = 该计划因缺乏资金而失败了。


现在完成时:
The river has been dammed for centuries. = 这条河已经被筑坝了几个世纪。
The project has been dammed by unforeseen circumstances. = 该项目因不可预见的情况而受阻。


过去完成时:
The dam had been dammed before the flood. = 大坝在洪水到来之前就已经建好了。(此处"dammed"用词略显重复，实际应用中应避免)
The project had been dammed by years of delays. = 该项目因多年的延误而受阻。


将来时:
The river will be dammed next year. = 这条河明年将被筑坝。
The project will be dammed if we don't secure funding. = 如果我们无法获得资金，该项目将会失败。


虚拟语气:
If the river were dammed, there would be less flooding. = 如果河流被筑坝，洪水就会减少。
I wish this dammed problem would just go away. = 我希望这个该死的问题快点消失。


被动语态:
The river is being dammed to create hydroelectric power. = 河流正在被筑坝以产生水力发电。
The project is being dammed by a lack of resources. = 该项目因缺乏资源而受阻。


疑问句:
Is the project dammed by external factors? = 这个项目是被外部因素阻碍的吗？
Is the river dammed at this point? = 河流在这一点上被筑坝了吗？


否定句:
The project is not dammed by a lack of effort. = 该项目并非由于缺乏努力而受阻。
The river is not dammed in this section. = 这段河流没有筑坝。
