conciliate:/kənˈsɪlieɪt/| vt.| 安抚；调解；赢得…的好感 | To appease or pacify, especially by concessions; to reconcile.  To win over; gain the goodwill of. |例句：The mediator worked to conciliate the two warring factions. 调解员努力调解这两个交战的派系。

The company tried to conciliate its angry customers with a refund.  公司试图通过退款来安抚愤怒的顾客。

近义词：appease, placate, reconcile, propitiate, mollify | 反义词：alienate, antagonize, exacerbate, inflame | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conciliates his employees with regular bonuses. = 他通过定期发放奖金来安抚员工。
She conciliates her neighbors with small gifts. = 她用小礼物来讨好邻居。


一般过去时:
The king conciliated the rebels with promises of amnesty. = 国王以大赦的承诺安抚叛乱者。
They conciliated their differences through dialogue. = 他们通过对话调解了分歧。


现在完成时:
The government has conciliated the protesters with new reforms. = 政府通过新的改革措施安抚了抗议者。
She has conciliated her family after a long argument. = 经过长时间的争吵后，她已经安抚了家人。


过去完成时:
He had conciliated the angry crowd before the speech. = 他在演讲前已经安抚了愤怒的人群。
They had conciliated their dispute before the trial. = 他们在审判前已经解决了他们的争议。


将来时:
The diplomat will conciliate the opposing sides with a new proposal. = 外交官将用一项新的提案来调解对立双方。
We will conciliate our differences and move forward. = 我们将调解我们的分歧并继续前进。


虚拟语气:
If the company conciliated its customers, it might avoid a lawsuit. = 如果公司安抚了客户，它可能会避免诉讼。
I wish the government would conciliate the warring factions. = 我希望政府能够调解交战的派系。


被动语态:
The dispute was conciliated by a neutral third party. = 争端由中立的第三方调解。
The angry customers were conciliated by a prompt refund. = 愤怒的顾客通过及时的退款得到了安抚。


疑问句:
Can diplomacy conciliate the conflicting nations? = 外交能否调解冲突中的国家？
Will the peace talks conciliate the warring parties? = 和谈能否调解交战各方？


否定句:
The government failed to conciliate the protesters. = 政府未能安抚抗议者。
He could not conciliate his angry boss. = 他无法安抚他愤怒的老板。


PHRASE->
conciliate a dispute = 调解纠纷
conciliate a difference = 调解分歧
conciliate an enemy = 安抚敌人
conciliate a crowd = 安抚人群
conciliate the public = 安抚公众


词源:
From Latin *conciliāre "to unite, bring together," from concilium "council, assembly."  The sense development is from bringing people together in council to bring people together in agreement.
