nonmaternally:/ˌnɒn məˈtɜːrnəli/ | adj. |非母系的；非母方的 | Not relating to or derived from the mother.  This adjective describes things that are not related to or originating from the mother or maternal line.  It is often used in biological or genetic contexts. | Example: The study focused on nonmaternally inherited mitochondrial DNA.  这项研究集中在非母系遗传的线粒体DNA上。 |近义词: paternally inherited, non-maternal |反义词: maternally, maternal
