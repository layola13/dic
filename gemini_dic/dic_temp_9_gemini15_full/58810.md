duomo:/ˈduːomoʊ/| n. |主教座堂；大教堂 |A duomo is the cathedral church of a diocese, often the largest and most important church in a city.  It is typically a significant architectural and religious structure. |例句：The Duomo of Florence is a masterpiece of Renaissance architecture.  佛罗伦萨大教堂是文艺复兴时期建筑的杰作。|近义词：cathedral, basilica |反义词：chapel, small church
