quailing: /ˈkweɪlɪŋ/ | v. |畏缩；胆怯；衰弱| To lose courage; to cower in fear; to become weak or feeble. |例句：The soldiers quailed before the enemy's advance. 士兵们在敌人的进攻面前畏缩了。  The plant quailed in the harsh sun. 植物在强烈的阳光下枯萎了。|近义词：cowering, shrinking, weakening |反义词：bold, brave, strong |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He quails at the sight of blood. 他看到血就害怕。
The flowers quail in the frost. 花朵在霜冻中枯萎。


一般过去时:
She quailed at the sound of thunder. 她听到雷声就害怕了。
The crops quailed under the drought. 庄稼在干旱下枯萎了。


现在完成时:
His spirit has quailed under the pressure.  他的精神在压力下衰弱了。
The economy has quailed since the crisis. 自危机以来，经济已经衰弱了。


过去完成时:
The army had quailed before the superior force.  军队在强大的力量面前已经畏缩了。
The plants had quailed before the unexpected cold snap. 植物在突如其来的寒流面前已经枯萎了。


将来时:
He will quail at the challenge ahead. 他将会害怕即将面临的挑战。
The flowers will quail if we don't water them. 如果我们不浇水，花朵将会枯萎。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would not quail in the face of adversity. 如果我是你，我不会在逆境面前畏缩。
I wish I hadn't quailed at the crucial moment. 我真希望我没有在关键时刻畏缩。


被动语态:
His courage was quailed by the terrible news. 他被可怕的消息吓破了胆。 (This is less common usage, as "quail" is usually intransitive.)
The plants were quailed by the heat. 植物被高温灼伤了。(This usage emphasizes the effect of the heat, not the plant's reaction.)


疑问句:
Does he quail at the thought of public speaking? 他害怕公开演讲吗？
Will the seedlings quail in this cold weather? 幼苗会在这种寒冷的天气下枯萎吗？


否定句:
He did not quail in the face of danger. 他面对危险时没有畏缩。
The soldiers did not quail despite the heavy bombardment. 尽管遭到猛烈的炮击，士兵们也没有畏缩。


PHRASE->
quail before: 在...面前畏缩
quail at:  害怕...
quail under: 在...下衰弱
to quail and tremble: 颤抖和畏缩
to quail in one's boots:  吓得发抖
