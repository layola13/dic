skulks:/skʌlks/| v.|潜行；偷偷摸摸地走；躲藏|To move stealthily or secretly; to hide oneself.|例句：The thief skulked in the shadows. 小偷躲在阴影里潜行。|例句：He skulked away when he saw the police. 他看到警察就偷偷溜走了。|近义词：sneak, slink, lurk, prowl|反义词：strut, swagger


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He skulks around the corner. = 他在拐角处偷偷摸摸地走动。
The cat skulks in the bushes. = 猫躲在灌木丛中。

一般过去时:
The burglar skulked away after stealing the jewels. = 窃贼偷了珠宝后偷偷溜走了。
She skulked behind the tree, trying to avoid being seen. = 她躲在树后，试图避免被看到。

现在完成时:
He has skulked in the shadows for weeks. = 他几周来一直躲在阴影里。
The criminal has skulked away and remains at large. = 罪犯偷偷逃跑了，仍然逍遥法外。

过去完成时:
They had skulked in the basement all night. = 他们整晚都躲在地下室里。
The suspect had skulked away before the police arrived. = 嫌犯在警察到达之前就偷偷溜走了。

将来时:
He will skulk in the darkness until it's safe to leave. = 他会躲在黑暗中，直到离开安全为止。
She will skulk around the house, waiting for her opportunity. = 她会在房子周围偷偷摸摸地走动，等待机会。

虚拟语气:
If he skulked any less, he would be caught. = 如果他再不偷偷摸摸，就会被抓住。
I wish he hadn't skulked away so quickly. = 我真希望他不要那么快就溜走。
