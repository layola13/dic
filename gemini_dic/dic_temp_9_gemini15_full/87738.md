hexylene:/ˈhɛksɪliːn/| n. |己烯；六次甲基乙烯|Hexylene is a general term referring to any of the isomers of hexene, a six-carbon alkene with the chemical formula C6H12.  These isomers differ in the position of the double bond and/or the arrangement of the carbon atoms.  It is used in various industrial applications.  |Example:  The study examined the use of hexylene in the production of plastics.  这项研究考察了己烯在塑料生产中的用途。|近义词: hexene (more specific)|反义词:  None (it's a chemical, not something with direct opposites)|名词
