cartoonishly:/kɑːrˈtuːnɪʃli/| adj.|卡通般地，滑稽地，夸张地 | Exaggeratedly; in a way that resembles a cartoon.  The villain’s mustache was cartoonishly large. |近义词：exaggeratedly, comically, absurdly |反义词：realistically, subtly, naturally


例句：

The characters in the movie were cartoonishly exaggerated.  电影中的人物被夸张地描绘得像卡通人物一样。

The villain's laugh was cartoonishly evil. 反派的狂笑听起来卡通般邪恶。
