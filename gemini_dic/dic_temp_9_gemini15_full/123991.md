morning:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋ/ | n. |早晨，上午 |The morning is the period of time between midnight and noon.  It's often associated with the beginning of a day and a time for new beginnings. |例句：I woke up early this morning. 我今天早上起得很早。|例句：The morning sun shone brightly.  早晨的阳光灿烂。|近义词：dawn, daybreak |反义词：evening, afternoon, night


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun rises every morning. = 太阳每天早上升起。
Morning starts at 6 AM for me. = 对我来说，早晨从早上6点开始。


一般过去时:
We had breakfast this morning. = 我们今天早上吃了早餐。
The meeting was held yesterday morning. = 会议昨天上午举行。


现在完成时:
I have already finished my work this morning. = 我今天早上已经完成了我的工作。
She has been working since early morning. = 她从一大早就开始工作了。


过去完成时:
I had already left before the morning meeting. = 早晨会议之前我已经离开了。
They had finished their presentation by morning. = 他们到早上已经完成了他们的演示。


将来时:
I will go for a run tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上要去跑步。
We will have a meeting next Monday morning. = 我们下周一早上将举行会议。


虚拟语气:
If it were morning, I would go for a walk. = 如果是早上，我会去散步。
I wish it were morning already. = 我希望现在已经是早上了。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
