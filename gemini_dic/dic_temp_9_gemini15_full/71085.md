fisetin:/ˈfaɪsɪtɪn/| n. |非瑟酮|A plant-derived polyphenol with potential health benefits, found in various fruits and vegetables such as apples, grapes, and strawberries. It is being studied for its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.|例句：Studies suggest that fisetin may have neuroprotective effects.  研究表明，非瑟酮可能具有神经保护作用。|例句：The antioxidant properties of fisetin are currently under investigation. 非瑟酮的抗氧化特性目前正在研究中。|近义词：quercetin (similar polyphenol)|反义词：N/A|名词
