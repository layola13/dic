hygrometric:/haɪɡroʊˈmɛtrɪk/| adj.|测湿的；湿度计的|Relating to or indicating humidity.  Hygrometric instruments measure the moisture content of the air or other substances. |例句：The hygrometric reading showed the air was very dry. 湿度计读数显示空气非常干燥。

例句：We used a hygrometric sensor to monitor the humidity levels in the greenhouse. 我们使用湿度传感器来监测温室的湿度水平。


近义词：hydroscopic, hygroscopic

反义词：None directly opposite, but words related to dryness could be considered contextual opposites, such as arid, desiccated.

词性：形容词
