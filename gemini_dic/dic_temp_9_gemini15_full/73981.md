fortnightlies:/ˈfɔːrtnaɪtliːz/| n.|双周刊；每两周一次的刊物|A fortnightly is a publication issued every two weeks.  It refers to something that occurs or is published every fourteen days. |例句：The magazine is a fortnightly, published every other Wednesday. 这本杂志是双周刊，每隔周三出版一次。
例句：We have fortnightly meetings to discuss project progress. 我们每两周举行一次会议来讨论项目进展。|近义词：bi-weekly (but note the ambiguity)|反义词：daily, weekly
