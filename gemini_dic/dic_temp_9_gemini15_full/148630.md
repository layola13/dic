overthinking: /ˌoʊvərˈθɪŋkɪŋ/ | n. |过度思考，想太多 |The act of thinking too much about something, often to the point of anxiety or stress.  It involves dwelling on problems, possibilities, or past events excessively. |例句：Her overthinking led to sleepless nights.  她的过度思考导致了失眠。

例句：His overthinking prevented him from making a decision. 他的过度思考阻止了他做决定。


近义词：rumination, worrying, fretting, obsessing |反义词：unconcern, unconcern, nonchalance |词性：名词
