scenari:/səˈnɑːriəʊ/ | /sɪˈnærioʊ/ | n. |情景；方案；设想 | A description of a possible course of events; a hypothetical situation.  |Example:  The police prepared for several different scenarios.  警察为几种不同的情况做好了准备。

The worst-case scenario is that we lose all our investment. 最坏的情况是我们损失所有投资。


近义词：situation, case, hypothesis, possibility | 反义词：reality
