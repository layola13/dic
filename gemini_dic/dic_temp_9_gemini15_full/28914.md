carriageways:/ˈkærɪdʒweɪz/| n.|车道，行车道|A carriageway is a part of a road that is used for vehicles to travel on.  It may consist of one or more lanes.  Multiple carriageways are often separated by a central reservation or median.|例句：The motorway has three carriageways in each direction. 这条高速公路每个方向都有三条车道。|例句：The new road project will widen the carriageways to improve traffic flow. 新的道路工程将拓宽车道以改善交通流量。|近义词：lanes, roadways, thoroughfares|反义词：footpath, sidewalk|名词
