manures:/məˈnjʊərz/| n.|肥料，粪肥|Organic matter, especially animal dung, used to fertilize land.|The farmer spread manures on the fields to enrich the soil.  农民将肥料撒在田里以肥沃土壤。|Fertilizers, composts|pesticides, herbicides|名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer uses manures to enrich the soil. = 农民使用肥料来肥沃土壤。
They manure their fields regularly. = 他们定期给田地施肥。

一般过去时:
He manured the garden last week. = 他上周给花园施了肥。
The farmers spread manures on the fields yesterday. = 农民昨天把肥料撒在田里了。

现在完成时:
They have manured the fields this year. = 他们今年已经给田地施过肥了。
We have used organic manures for many years. = 我们多年来一直使用有机肥料。

过去完成时:
He had already manured the garden before the rain started. = 下雨之前他已经给花园施过肥了。
The farmers had spread manures before planting the seeds. = 农民在播种前已经施过肥了。

将来时:
They will manure the fields next week. = 他们下周将给田地施肥。
We will use more manures next season. = 下个季节我们将使用更多的肥料。

虚拟语气:
If I had more manures, I would have a better harvest. = 如果我有更多肥料，我的收成会更好。
I wish I had used more manures last year. = 我希望去年我使用了更多肥料。

被动语态:
The fields were manured last month. = 田地在上个月施过肥。
The soil is enriched by the manures. = 土壤被肥料肥沃了。

疑问句:
Do you use manures in your garden? = 你在你的花园里用肥料吗？
What kind of manures do they use? = 他们使用什么类型的肥料？

否定句:
They don't use chemical manures. = 他们不使用化肥。
He didn't manure the field this year. = 他今年没有给田地施肥。


PHRASE->
animal manures: 动物粪肥
green manures: 绿肥
liquid manures: 液肥
manure spreader: 肥料撒布机
application of manures: 施肥


词源:
From Old French *manure, from Latin manurus "fit to be tilled," from manus "hand."  The semantic shift from "fit to be tilled" to "fertilizer" likely arose because the tilling of land often involved enriching it with manure.
