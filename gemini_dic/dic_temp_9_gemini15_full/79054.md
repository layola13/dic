given-names:/ˈɡɪvən neɪmz/| n. |名字，姓名 |Given names are the names a person is given at birth or formally adopted.  They are also known as first names or Christian names.  |My given name is John, but my friends call me Jack.|近义词：first names, Christian names|反义词：surname, family name
