scampishly:/ˈskæmpɪʃli/| adv.|调皮地，顽皮地，淘气地|In a mischievous or playful way.  It suggests a lighthearted and slightly naughty behavior.|例句：He scampishly snatched the cookie from the jar. 他调皮地从罐子里偷了一块饼干。

例句：The children scampishly ran through the park, laughing and giggling. 孩子们在公园里嬉戏地跑着，笑着咯咯地笑着。


近义词：mischievously, playfully, naughtily, roguishly


反义词：seriously, solemnly, sedately


词性:副词
