ideal:/aɪˈdiːəl/ | adj., n. |理想的；完美的；典范；理想 |


英文解析:
As an adjective, "ideal" means conforming to an ultimate standard of excellence or perfection; representing the best possible example of a category. As a noun, it refers to a standard of perfection, or a perfect example of something.


例句:
She's the ideal candidate for the job. = 她是这份工作的理想人选。

This painting is an ideal representation of the era. = 这幅画是那个时代的理想写照。


近义词:
perfect, flawless, exemplary, model, paragon


反义词:
imperfect, flawed, deficient, substandard, undesirable


词性:
adjective, noun
