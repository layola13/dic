laces:/ˈleɪsɪz/| n.|鞋带；蕾丝|Thin cords used to fasten shoes; delicate ornamental fabric made of fine threads.|例句：My shoelaces came undone. 我的鞋带松开了。

例句：The dress was adorned with delicate laces. 这件裙子装饰着精美的蕾丝。|近义词：cords, ribbons, threads|反义词：None

The laces are tied tightly. = 鞋带系得很紧。
