commotion:/kəˈmoʊʃən/| n. |骚动；喧嚣；混乱 |A state of noisy confusion or disorder.  |例句：The announcement caused a great commotion in the crowd.  公告引起了人群中很大的骚动。|例句：There was a commotion outside, so I went to see what was happening. 外面一阵骚动，所以我出去看看发生了什么事。|近义词：uproar, tumult, disturbance, turmoil |反义词：calm, peace, quiet, tranquility
