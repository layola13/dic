civilize:/ˈsɪvəlaɪz/| vt. |使文明；教化；开化|To bring out of a savage or barbarous state; to instruct in the arts and sciences; to refine and polish; to make cultured or refined.|例句：Education helps to civilize people. 教育有助于使人们文明。|例句：The Roman Empire helped to civilize much of Europe. 罗马帝国帮助开化了欧洲的大部分地区。|近义词：refine, cultivate, educate, enlighten|反义词：decivilize, barbarize, brutalize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They civilize the savages. = 他们使野蛮人文明。
We civilize the rural areas. = 我们使农村地区文明化。


一般过去时:
The government civilized the country. = 政府使这个国家文明起来。
They civilized the barbarians. = 他们使野蛮人文明。


现在完成时:
Education has civilized many people. = 教育使许多人文明起来。
Modernization has civilized our lives. = 现代化使我们的生活文明起来。


过去完成时:
They had civilized the tribe before the war. = 战争爆发前他们已经使这个部落文明了。
The missionaries had civilized the natives. = 传教士们已经使当地人文明了。


将来时:
This project will civilize the whole region. = 这个项目将使整个地区文明起来。
We will civilize the remote village. = 我们将使这个偏远的村庄文明起来。


虚拟语气:
If we had better education, we could civilize the people faster. = 如果我们有更好的教育，就能更快地使人们文明起来。
If they had been civilized earlier, the war might not have happened. = 如果他们早些文明起来，战争可能就不会发生了。


被动语态:
The people were civilized by the influence of the city. = 人们受到了城市的影响而变得文明。
The tribes were civilized over many centuries. = 部落经过几个世纪才文明起来。


疑问句:
How can we civilize the society? = 我们怎样才能使社会文明起来？
Will technology civilize or destroy us? = 科技会使我们文明还是毁灭我们？


否定句:
The war didn't civilize, but brutalized the people. = 战争并没有使人民文明，而是使他们野蛮化。
They failed to civilize the remote area. = 他们没能使偏远地区文明起来。
