inhand: /ɪnˈhænd/ | adj. | 在手的；已掌握的；现有的 |  Already in possession; under control; available.  | 例句：The police have the suspect in hand.  警方已将嫌疑犯控制住。

The project is well in hand.  项目进展顺利。


近义词：under control, available, possessed, at hand


反义词：out of hand, unavailable, lost, missing


词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police have the suspect in hand. = 警方已将嫌疑犯控制住。
We have the situation well in hand. = 我们已将局势控制得很好。


一般过去时:
The detective had the evidence in hand. = 侦探掌握了证据。
They had the project well in hand by the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前项目进展顺利。


现在完成时:
We have had the necessary information in hand for weeks. = 我们已经掌握了必要的资料好几个星期了。
She has had the situation in hand since the beginning. = 从一开始她就控制住了局面。


过去完成时:
They had had the problem in hand before the crisis. = 危机发生前，他们已经解决了这个问题。
The company had had the technology in hand for years before releasing the product. = 公司在发布产品前已经掌握这项技术多年了。


将来时:
We will have the report in hand by Friday. = 我们会在星期五前拿到报告。
They will have the matter well in hand soon. = 他们很快就会把事情处理好。


虚拟语气:
If we had the resources in hand, we could finish the project earlier. = 如果我们有足够的资源，就能更早完成项目。
I wish I had the situation in hand. = 我希望我能控制住局面。


被动语态:
The situation was not easily kept in hand. = 局势不易控制。
The problem is being kept in hand by the experts. = 专家们正在处理这个问题。


疑问句:
Do you have the necessary documents in hand? = 你有必要的证件吗？
Is the situation in hand? = 局势得到控制了吗？


否定句:
The situation is not in hand. = 局势还没有得到控制。
We didn't have the resources in hand to complete the task. = 我们没有足够的资源来完成任务。


PHRASE->
in hand with = 与…一起；和…合作
keep in hand = 控制；掌握
get in hand = 开始处理；着手处理
have in hand = 掌握；控制
take in hand = 承担；处理
