jadedness:/ˈdʒeɪdɪdnəs/ | n. |厌倦；倦怠；乏味 | A state of being weary, bored, or cynical due to excessive experience or indulgence.  It often implies a loss of enthusiasm or interest. | Example:  After years of working in the fast-paced world of finance, he felt a deep sense of jadedness.  多年在快节奏的金融界工作后，他感到深深的厌倦。|近义词：weariness, fatigue, apathy, cynicism, disillusionment |反义词：enthusiasm, excitement, vigor, optimism
