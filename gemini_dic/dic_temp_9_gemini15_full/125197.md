multichannelled:/ˌmʌltiˈtʃænəld/| adj.|多通道的，多信道的|Having or using more than one channel.  This typically refers to systems that transmit or receive information through multiple channels simultaneously, such as communication networks, audio systems, or marketing campaigns. |例句：The company uses a multichannelled marketing strategy to reach a wider audience.  这家公司使用多渠道营销策略来接触更广泛的受众。


例句：This new sound system is multichannelled, providing a truly immersive audio experience. 这个新的音响系统是多声道系统，提供了真正身临其境的音频体验。


近义词：multi-channel, polychannel


反义词：single-channel, monochannel


词性:形容词
