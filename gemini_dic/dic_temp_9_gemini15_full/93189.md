ignor:/ɪɡˈnɔːr/| v.|忽略，不理睬，忽视 |to refuse to take notice of or acknowledge; disregard.|例句：He ignored my warnings. 他无视我的警告。|例句：She deliberately ignored him. 她故意不理他。|近义词：disregard, overlook, neglect|反义词：heed, attend to, consider


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ignores my calls. = 他不接我的电话。
They ignore the traffic rules. = 他们无视交通规则。

一般过去时:
I ignored his rude remarks. = 我忽略了他粗鲁的言辞。
She ignored the warning signs. = 她无视警告标志。


现在完成时:
He has ignored my emails for weeks. = 他几周来一直不回复我的邮件。
They have ignored the problem for too long. = 他们对这个问题忽视太久了。


过去完成时:
I had ignored the problem for so long that it became a crisis. = 我长期忽视这个问题，以至于它变成了危机。
She had ignored his advice, and now she regretted it. = 她忽视了他的建议，现在她后悔了。


将来时:
He will ignore the advice again. = 他还会再次忽视建议。
They will ignore the consequences. = 他们将无视后果。


虚拟语气:
If I had listened, I would not have ignored his warning. = 如果我听了，我就不会忽视他的警告。
I wish I hadn't ignored her advice. = 我真希望我没有忽视她的建议。


被动语态:
His warnings were ignored. = 他的警告被忽视了。
The problem has been ignored for too long. = 这个问题被忽视太久了。


疑问句:
Why did you ignore me? = 你为什么不理我？
Did she ignore his request? = 她是否忽视了他的请求？


否定句:
I didn't ignore his message; I just didn't see it. = 我没有忽略他的信息；我只是没有看到。
She didn't ignore the rules deliberately. = 她并非故意违反规定。


PHRASE->
ignore the fact that = 忽视…这一事实
ignore the problem = 忽略问题
choose to ignore = 选择忽视
deliberately ignore = 故意忽视
simply ignore = 简单地忽视
