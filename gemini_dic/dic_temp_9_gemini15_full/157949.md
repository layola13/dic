planetesimals: /ˌplænɪˈtɛzɪməlz/ | n. | 微行星 | Small rocky or icy bodies that existed in the early solar system and accreted to form planets.  Planetesimals are considered the building blocks of planets. They are thought to have formed from dust and gas in the solar nebula through a process of accretion.  | Example: The accretion of planetesimals is a crucial step in the formation of planets.  微行星的吸积是行星形成的关键步骤。|近义词：protoplanets (原行星), asteroids (小行星) (depending on size and composition)|反义词：planets (行星) (fully formed celestial bodies)
