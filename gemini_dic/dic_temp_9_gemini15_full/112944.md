lugs:/lʌɡz/ | n. |耳垂；提手；突出物；(非正式)脚；(车辆的)轮轴；|英文解析:The word "lugs" can refer to several things, primarily the fleshy lower part of the ear,  the handles or projections on an object used for carrying or lifting, or the projecting parts of a wheel or axle. In informal contexts, it can also mean feet.|例句：The earrings dangled from her lugs.  耳环从她的耳垂上垂下来。The lugs on the suitcase made it easy to carry. 手提箱上的提手使它很容易携带。|近义词：earlobes (for ear lugs), handles, projections, knobs|反义词：N/A
