interdenominationalism:/ˌɪntərˌdɪˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃənəlɪzəm/ | n. |超教派主义，跨教派合作 | The principle and practice of cooperation among different Christian denominations.  It emphasizes common ground and shared goals, transcending denominational differences for collaborative efforts. | Example: The interdenominationalism of the local churches led to a successful community outreach program.  这个地方教会的超教派合作导致了一个成功的社区外展项目。|近义词： ecumenism, interfaith cooperation |反义词：denominationalism, sectarianism |名词
