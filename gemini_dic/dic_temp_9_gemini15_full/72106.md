flocked:/flɒkt/| /flɑːkt/| v.|聚集，成群结队；加绒|To gather or move in a crowd or flock; to add a fuzzy surface to a fabric.|例句：Birds flocked to the lake.  鸟儿成群结队地飞向湖边。|例句：The coat is flocked for extra warmth. 这件外套加了绒，更保暖。|近义词：clustered, gathered, congregated |反义词：scattered, dispersed|动词
