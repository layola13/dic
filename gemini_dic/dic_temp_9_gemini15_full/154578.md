persecutees:/ˈpɜːrsɪkjuːtiːz/| n. |受迫害者，受害者 |Those who are persecuted because of their religion, race, or political beliefs.  People subjected to oppression and ill-treatment. |The persecutees fled their homeland seeking refuge in a safer country. 受迫害者逃离家园，寻求在更安全的国家避难。  Many persecutees found solace and support in the refugee camp. 许多受迫害者在难民营中找到了慰藉和支持。 |victims, sufferers, the oppressed |perpetrators, oppressors |名词
