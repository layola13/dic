gunmetal:/ˈɡʌnmɛtəl/ | n., adj. |青铜色；枪炮金属色 |  A dark grayish-blue color, resembling that of cast iron or bronze.  It is also used as an adjective to describe this color. |The statue was painted gunmetal gray.  那座雕像被漆成了青铜灰色。  His car is a deep gunmetal blue. 他的车是深青铜蓝色。|dark gray, bluish-gray, bronze |  (反义词较难直接定义，可考虑与其色调相反的颜色，如：bright yellow, light pink) |名词，形容词

词源:

The term "gunmetal" originates from the use of a specific alloy of copper, tin, and sometimes zinc, historically used in the casting of cannons and other firearms.  The dark grayish-blue color of this alloy became associated with the term, leading to its adoption as a color name.
