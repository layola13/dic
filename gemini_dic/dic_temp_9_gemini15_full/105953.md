kludge:/klʌdʒ/| n.|权宜之计，临时方案，粗制滥造的东西|A kludge is a clumsy or inelegant solution to a problem, often involving a complex and poorly organized collection of parts. It's typically a quick fix that works, but is not efficient, elegant, or easily maintainable.  | Example: The software's user interface is a real kludge; it's confusing and difficult to navigate.  这个软件的用户界面简直就是个粗制滥造的东西；它令人困惑且难以操作。|近义词：hack, workaround, makeshift, jury-rig|反义词：elegant solution, well-designed system, refined solution|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This system is a kludge. = 这个系统是个权宜之计。
That solution is a kludge. = 那个解决方案很粗糙。


一般过去时:
They kludged together a solution. = 他们拼凑出一个解决方案。
The programmer kludged the code. = 程序员粗制滥造地编写了代码。


现在完成时:
They have kludged the system together. = 他们已经拼凑出了这个系统。
We have kludged a temporary fix. = 我们已经想出一个临时的解决方案。


过去完成时:
They had kludged a solution before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前拼凑出了一个解决方案。
The team had kludged the software together. = 团队已经粗制滥造地把软件拼凑到一起了。


将来时:
We will kludge a solution for now. = 我们现在会想出一个权宜之计。
They will probably kludge something together. = 他们可能会拼凑出一些东西。


虚拟语气:
If they had more time, they wouldn't have had to kludge a solution. = 如果他们有更多时间，他们就不必仓促找解决方案了。
It would be better if we didn't have to kludge this together. = 如果我们不必这样拼凑起来就好了。


被动语态:
The system was kludged together quickly. = 这个系统被快速地拼凑到一起。
A temporary solution was kludged. = 一个临时的解决方案被粗制滥造地拼凑出来了。


疑问句:

否定句:
This isn't a kludge; it's a well-designed system. = 这不是个权宜之计；这是一个精心设计的系统。
They didn't kludge the software; they built it properly. = 他们没有粗制滥造地制作软件；他们正规地构建了它。
