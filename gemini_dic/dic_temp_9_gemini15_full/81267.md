granivorous:/ˈɡrænivərəs/| adj.| 食谷的，食种子的 |  Relating to or denoting animals that feed on seeds.  | Example: Many birds are granivorous, relying on seeds for a significant portion of their diet. 许多鸟类是食谷的，它们的食物很大一部分依赖种子。 |近义词: seed-eating, grain-eating | 反义词: carnivorous, herbivorous
