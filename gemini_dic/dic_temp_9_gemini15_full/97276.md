influences:/ˈɪnflʊənsɪz/| n.|影响，势力|The power to have an effect on someone or something.  Influences can be people, events, ideas, or things that shape our thoughts, actions, and beliefs.|例句：His early teachers had a profound influence on his life.  他的早期老师对他的人生有深远的影响。|例句：The company's marketing campaign had a significant influence on sales.  该公司的营销活动对销售额产生了重大影响。|近义词：impact, effect, sway, impact|反义词：negligible effect, lack of impact|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His parents' values greatly influence his decisions. = 他父母的价值观极大地影响了他的决定。
Her music influences millions of people worldwide. = 她的音乐影响着全球数百万人。

一般过去时:
The war influenced the country's political landscape. = 那场战争影响了国家的政治格局。
The invention of the printing press influenced the spread of knowledge. = 印刷术的发明影响了知识的传播。

现在完成时:
Social media has influenced how we communicate. = 社交媒体已经影响了我们的沟通方式。
Technology has influenced the way we live and work. = 科技已经影响了我们的生活和工作方式。

过去完成时:
His childhood experiences had influenced his adult personality. = 他童年的经历已经影响了他的成年后的性格。
The previous government's policies had influenced the current economic situation. = 前政府的政策已经影响了目前的经济形势。

将来时:
Climate change will influence sea levels in the future. = 气候变化将在未来影响海平面。
New technologies will influence the job market in the coming years. = 新技术将在未来几年影响就业市场。

虚拟语气:
If only he had not let those bad influences affect his judgment! = 要是他没有让那些不良影响左右他的判断就好了！
If her advice had influenced him, he would have avoided that mistake. = 如果他听取了她的建议，他就能避免那个错误。

被动语态:
His decisions were influenced by his family's expectations. = 他的决定受到了家庭期望的影响。
The design was influenced by traditional art forms. = 这个设计受到了传统艺术形式的影响。

疑问句:
What influences your choice of career? = 什么影响了你职业的选择？
What factors influence the price of oil? = 哪些因素影响着油价？

否定句:
His opinion did not influence the final decision. = 他的意见没有影响最终决定。
The weather did not influence their plans. = 天气没有影响他们的计划。
