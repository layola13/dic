geese:/ɡiːs/| n.|鹅|The plural of goose. Geese are large waterfowl with long necks and webbed feet.|例句：A flock of geese flew overhead.  一群鹅飞过头顶。|例句：Wild geese migrate south for the winter. 野鹅在冬天向南迁徙。|近义词：fowl, waterfowl|反义词：无
