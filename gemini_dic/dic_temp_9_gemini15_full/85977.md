headset:/ˈhɛd.sɛt/ | n. |耳机；头戴式耳机 | A headset is a device worn on the head that covers or is placed over the ears and is used for listening to sound or communicating.  It typically includes a microphone and headphones. | Example: I use a headset when I'm playing online games. 我玩在线游戏时会戴耳机。 I need a new headset for my computer. 我需要一副新的电脑耳机。|近义词：headphones, earphones | 反义词： None
