debunking:/dɪˈbʌŋkɪŋ/| v.|揭穿；驳斥；戳穿|The act of exposing false claims or beliefs.  It involves providing evidence to show that something is untrue or misleading.|例句：The scientist's work debunked the myth that the earth is flat.  这位科学家的工作揭穿了地球是平的这个神话。

例句：The journalist's investigation debunked the politician's lies.  记者的调查揭穿了这位政客的谎言。|近义词：exposing, refuting, discrediting, contradicting |反义词：confirming, validating, supporting, endorsing |词性:动词
