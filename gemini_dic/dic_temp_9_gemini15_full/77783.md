generalising: /ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 概括；归纳；推广 |  To make a general statement or rule from specific examples; to form a general idea or opinion from specific instances.  It often involves drawing conclusions about a larger group based on observations of a smaller sample. |

例句：
He is generalising from a small sample size. = 他根据一个小样本得出了概括性结论。

She tends to generalise about people based on their appearance. = 她倾向于根据人们的外貌对他们进行概括性的判断。


近义词：summarizing, abstracting,  generalizing,  broadening

反义词：specifying, detailing, particularizing


词性：动词
