paradigmatic:/ˌpærədɪɡˈmætɪk/| adj.|范例的；典范的；模式的 | Relating to or of the nature of a paradigm; exemplary.  A paradigmatic example is one that serves as a model or archetype.  The word often refers to a set of linguistic units that are mutually exclusive choices filling the same slot in a sentence structure.  |例句：The teacher used a paradigmatic example to illustrate the grammatical rule.  老师用一个范例来说明语法规则。

例句：His approach to problem-solving is paradigmatic of his overall methodical nature. 他解决问题的方法是他整体严谨性格的典范。


近义词：exemplary, archetypal, model, typical, representative

反义词：atypical, unusual, aberrant, exceptional


词性: adjective
