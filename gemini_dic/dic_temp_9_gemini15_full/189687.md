saloons:/səˈluːn/| n. |酒馆；沙龙；轿车车厢|A saloon is a public house, a bar, or a large room for social gatherings.  The term can also refer to the passenger compartment of a car, especially an older model. |例句：He spent his evenings at the local saloon. 他晚上都在当地酒馆消磨时光。
The old car had a spacious saloon. 那辆旧车有一个宽敞的车厢。|近义词：bar, pub, tavern, salon|反义词：private room, residence
