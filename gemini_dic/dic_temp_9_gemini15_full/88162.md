hilling:/ˈhɪlɪŋ/| n. v. |培土，堆土；覆盖；(尤指马铃薯)培土|The act of covering the base of a plant with earth to support its growth and protect it from frost or pests;  to cover or pile up earth around (a plant).|例句：Hilling potatoes helps to protect them from frost.  (给马铃薯培土有助于保护它们免受霜冻。)  The farmer was hilling the rows of corn. (这位农民正在给玉米行培土。)|近义词：mounding, banking, covering|反义词：uncovering, exposing|动词
