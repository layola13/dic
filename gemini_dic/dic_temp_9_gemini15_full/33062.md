chowed down:/tʃoʊd daʊn/| v. |狼吞虎咽地吃|To eat something very quickly and enthusiastically.|He chowed down on a burger in record time. 他以创纪录的速度狼吞虎咽地吃了一个汉堡。|gobble, devour, gulp|starve, nibble


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He chows down on his breakfast every morning. 他每天早上都狼吞虎咽地吃早餐。
They chow down on pizza every Friday night. 他们每个星期五晚上都狼吞虎咽地吃披萨。


一般过去时:
She chowed down on the entire cake. 她把整个蛋糕都吃光了。
We chowed down on the leftovers after the party. 派对结束后，我们把剩菜都吃光了。


现在完成时:
He has chowed down on three whole pizzas. 他已经吃了三个完整的披萨。
She has chowed down on a huge plate of pasta. 她已经吃了一大盘意大利面。


过去完成时:
They had chowed down on all the snacks before the movie started. 电影开始前，他们已经把所有零食都吃光了。
I had chowed down on a big lunch, so I wasn't very hungry for dinner. 我午餐吃得很饱，所以晚餐不太饿。


将来时:
I will chow down on a delicious steak tonight. 今晚我要吃美味的牛排。
We will chow down on some burgers at the game. 我们将在比赛中吃一些汉堡。


虚拟语气:
If I were hungry, I would chow down on that entire pie. 如果我饿了，我会把整个馅饼都吃掉。
I wish I could chow down on some chocolate right now. 我真希望现在能吃些巧克力。


被动语态: (This verb is rarely used passively.)  The food was chowed down quickly by the hungry crowd.  食物被饥饿的人群迅速吃光。
The pizza was chowed down in minutes.  披萨几分钟内就被吃光了。


疑问句:
Did he chow down on all the cookies? 他把所有饼干都吃了吗？
Will they chow down on the leftovers? 他们会吃剩菜吗？


否定句:
She didn't chow down on the vegetables. 她不吃蔬菜。
They didn't chow down on the dessert. 他们不吃甜点。
