nonministerially: /ˌnɒnˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəli/ | adj. |非部长的；非官方的；非政府的 | Not relating to or done by a minister of the government.  This word describes actions, roles, or appointments that are outside the official capacity of a government minister. |


例句：

The committee made a nonministerial recommendation.  委员会提出了一个非部长的建议。

He held a nonministerial position in the government. 他在政府中担任非部长职位。


近义词：unofficial, extra-ministerial, non-governmental


反义词：ministerial, official, governmental


词性：形容词
