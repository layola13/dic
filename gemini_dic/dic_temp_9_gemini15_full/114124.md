maha:/ˈmɑːhɑː/ | n. |伟大的，重要的；（印度教）湿婆神|  A Sanskrit word meaning "great," "important," or "large."  It is also used as a title or part of names, particularly in South Asia, often associated with deities or powerful figures. In Hinduism, Maha is a prefix often used with names of deities, such as "Mahadeva" (the great god Shiva). |例句：The Maha Kumbh Mela is a massive religious gathering.  (大壶节是一个盛大的宗教集会。)  The Maha Samghi is a powerful political party. (大萨吉是一个强大的政党。) |近义词：great, grand, vast, immense |反义词：small, insignificant, minor |名词
