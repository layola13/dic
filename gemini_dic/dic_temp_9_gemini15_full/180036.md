reflame:/ˌriːˈfleɪm/ | v. | 再点燃；重新点燃；重燃 (热情等) | To ignite or kindle again; to rekindle (a fire, passion, etc.). |例句：He tried to reflame the dying embers of the fire. 他试图重新点燃快要熄灭的火苗。

例句：The news reflamed his passion for the project. 这个消息重新燃起了他对这个项目的热情。


近义词：reignite, rekindle, revive | 反义词：extinguish, quench, stifle


词性：动词
