professed:/prəˈfɛst/| v. adj.|公开宣称的；自称的；声称的 |to claim, often falsely, to have a particular feeling, belief, or skill |例句：He professed ignorance of the matter. 他声称对此事一无所知。|近义词：claimed, avowed, declared |反义词：denied, concealed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He professes his love for her daily. = 他每天都表达他对她的爱。
She professes to be an expert in this field. = 她自称是这个领域的专家。


一般过去时:
He professed his innocence to the police. = 他向警方宣称自己是无辜的。
They professed a deep commitment to the cause. = 他们公开表示对这项事业的坚定承诺。


现在完成时:
She has professed her faith in God throughout her life. = 她一生都公开宣称对上帝的信仰。
He has professed his love for painting since childhood. = 他从小就声称热爱绘画。


过去完成时:
He had professed his loyalty to the king before the rebellion. = 在叛乱之前，他曾向国王宣誓效忠。
They had professed their support for the project before it began. = 在项目开始之前，他们就表示支持该项目。


将来时:
She will profess her love for him on their anniversary. = 她将在他们的周年纪念日上表达对他的爱。
He will profess his innocence in court. = 他将在法庭上宣称自己是无辜的。


虚拟语气:
If he professed his true feelings, she might forgive him. = 如果他坦白自己的真实感受，她可能会原谅他。
I wish he professed more interest in my work. = 我希望他对我的工作表现出更多兴趣。


被动语态:
His innocence was professed by many witnesses. = 许多证人都为他宣称清白。
Her expertise was professed by her colleagues. = 她的专业知识得到了同事们的认可。


疑问句:
Does he profess any belief in the supernatural? = 他是否声称相信超自然现象？
Does she profess to understand quantum physics? = 她声称理解量子物理学吗？


否定句:
He did not profess any knowledge of the crime. = 他声称对这起犯罪一无所知。
She did not profess to be an expert. = 她并不自称是专家。


PHRASE->
professed belief = 公开的信仰
professed aim = 声称的目标
professed intention = 声称的意图
professed expertise = 自称的专业知识
professed ignorance = 声称的无知
