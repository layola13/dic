discus:/dɪˈskʌs/| n. v.|讨论；谈论；论述|To talk about something with someone else, especially to exchange ideas or opinions.|The committee will discuss the proposal at its next meeting.  委员会将在下次会议上讨论该提案。
They discussed the matter thoroughly.  他们彻底讨论了这件事。|debate, conversation, conference|silence, ignoring|v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They discuss the project every week. = 他们每周都讨论这个项目。
We discuss politics during lunch. = 我们午餐时讨论政治。

一般过去时:
They discussed the problem yesterday. = 他们昨天讨论了这个问题。
She discussed her concerns with her boss. = 她和老板讨论了她的担忧。

现在完成时:
They have discussed the issue several times. = 他们已经讨论过这个问题好几次了。
We have discussed this before. = 我们以前讨论过这个。

过去完成时:
They had discussed the contract before signing. = 他们在签合同之前已经讨论过合同了。
She had discussed the matter with her colleagues. = 她已经和同事们讨论过这件事了。

将来时:
They will discuss the budget next week. = 他们下周将讨论预算。
We will discuss the plan tomorrow. = 我们明天将讨论这个计划。

虚拟语气:
If they discussed the matter openly, it might be resolved. = 如果他们公开讨论这个问题，它可能会得到解决。
I wish they had discussed the implications more thoroughly. = 我希望他们能更彻底地讨论一下影响。

被动语态:
The proposal was discussed extensively. = 该提案被广泛讨论。
The matter is being discussed at the moment. = 目前正在讨论此事。

疑问句:
Will the committee discuss the amendment? = 委员会会讨论这项修正案吗？
What are they discussing? = 他们在讨论什么？

否定句:
They did not discuss the sensitive topic. = 他们没有讨论这个敏感话题。
We don't discuss work matters at home. = 我们不在家讨论工作上的事情。


PHRASE->
discuss at length = 详细讨论
discuss in detail = 详细讨论
under discussion = 正在讨论中
open to discussion = 可以讨论的
discuss the pros and cons = 讨论优缺点
