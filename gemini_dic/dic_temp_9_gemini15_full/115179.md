manichaeans:/ˌmænɪˈkiːənz/| n.|摩尼教徒；摩尼教信徒|Followers of Manichaeism, a syncretic religious system founded by Mani in the 3rd century CE.  Manichaeism combined elements of Christianity, Zoroastrianism, and Buddhism.  The Manichaeans believed in a cosmic struggle between good and evil, light and darkness.|例句：Many early Manichaeans were persecuted for their beliefs.  许多早期的摩尼教徒因其信仰而受到迫害。

例句：The influence of Manichaeans on early Christianity is still debated by scholars. 摩尼教对早期基督教的影响至今仍在学者之间引起争议。|近义词:Manicheans|反义词:None.
