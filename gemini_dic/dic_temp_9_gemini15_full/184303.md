resown:/riːˈsoʊn/| vt.| 再播种；重新播种 | To sow again; to sow (seed) a second time.  |例句：The farmer had to resown the field after the flood. 洪水过后，农民不得不重新播种田地。

例句：After the birds ate the seeds, we had to resown the garden. 鸟儿吃了种子后，我们不得不重新播种花园。


近义词：replant, sow again, plant again |反义词：harvest, reap |词性:动词
