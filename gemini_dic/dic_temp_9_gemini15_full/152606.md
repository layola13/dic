peaceniks:/ˈpiːsˌnɪks/| n. |和平主义者；反战人士|A peacenik is a person who is strongly opposed to war and violence and actively works for peace.  They often participate in anti-war protests and advocate for peaceful solutions to conflict.|例句：The peaceniks organized a large protest against the war. 和平主义者们组织了一场反对战争的大型抗议活动。 The government ignored the pleas of the peaceniks. 政府无视了和平主义者的请求。|近义词：pacifist, anti-war activist|反义词：warmonger, hawk|名词
