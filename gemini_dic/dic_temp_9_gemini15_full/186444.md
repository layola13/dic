riboflavines:/ˌraɪbəʊˈfleɪvɪnz/| n.|核黄素（复数）|Riboflavines are a group of yellow crystalline water-soluble vitamins of the B complex, found in milk, eggs, liver, and leafy green vegetables. They are essential for growth and metabolic processes.|例句：A deficiency in riboflavines can lead to various health problems. 核黄素缺乏会导致各种健康问题。|例句：Many foods are fortified with riboflavines to improve nutritional value. 许多食物都添加了核黄素以提高营养价值。|近义词：vitamin B2, lactoflavin|反义词：无|名词
