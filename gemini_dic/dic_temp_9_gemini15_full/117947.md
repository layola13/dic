membranes:/ˈmembreɪnz/| n.|膜；薄膜|A thin layer of tissue that separates or connects parts of an organism; a pliable sheet-like structure.  It can be a biological membrane, such as a cell membrane, or a synthetic membrane used in various applications.|例句：Cell membranes regulate the passage of substances into and out of cells. 细胞膜调节物质进出细胞。

The tympanic membrane vibrates when sound waves hit it.  当声波击中鼓膜时，鼓膜会振动。|近义词：film, layer, sheet|反义词：solid mass
