hortensial: /ˌhɔːrˈtɛnʒəl/ | adj. | 绣球花的；紫阳花的 | Relating to or resembling hydrangeas.  The word evokes the image and qualities associated with hydrangeas, such as their vibrant colors and abundant blooms.  |例句：The hortensia bushes were laden with large, vibrant blooms.  绣球花丛上挂满了大型的、鲜艳的花朵。

The garden boasted a stunning array of hortensia-like flowers. 花园里展示了一系列令人惊叹的类似绣球花的花朵。


近义词：hydrangea-like (类似绣球花的)


反义词：None (没有直接的反义词，因为这是描述性形容词)


词性: 形容词


词源:  Derived from "hydrangea," ultimately from Greek *hýdor (water) and *aggeîon (vessel), referring to the plant's preference for moist environments and the shape of its seed capsules. The suffix "-ial" is added to create an adjectival form.
