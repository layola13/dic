repatriated:/ˌriˈpeɪtrieɪtɪd/| v. |遣返；送回；归还 |To return (someone) to their own country.  This often refers to people who have been forced to leave their home country,  or to goods that have been sent abroad and are returned. | 例句：The refugees were repatriated to their homeland after the war ended.  战争结束后，难民们被遣返回国。

The government repatriated the stolen artifacts. 政府将被盗的文物送回。|近义词：remit, return, send back |反义词：expatriate, exile


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government repatriates refugees regularly. = 政府定期遣返难民。
He repatriates his profits to his home country. = 他将利润汇回祖国。


一般过去时:
They repatriated the prisoners of war. = 他们遣返了战俘。
The company repatriated its overseas assets. = 公司将海外资产汇回国内。


现在完成时:
They have repatriated thousands of citizens this year. = 他们今年已遣返了数千名公民。
She has repatriated all her savings to her bank in her home country. = 她已将所有积蓄汇回国内银行。


过去完成时:
The company had repatriated its investments before the crisis hit. = 在危机爆发之前，该公司已将投资撤回。
The government had repatriated most of the refugees by the end of the month. = 到月底，政府已遣返了大部分难民。


将来时:
The government will repatriate the remaining soldiers next week. = 政府将于下周遣返其余士兵。
The company plans to repatriate its profits in the coming year. = 公司计划在来年将利润汇回。


虚拟语气:
If the war ended, they would be repatriated. = 如果战争结束，他们就会被遣返。
I wish they had been repatriated sooner. = 我希望他们早点被遣返。


被动语态:
The stolen paintings were repatriated to the museum. = 被盗的画作被送回博物馆。
The refugees are being repatriated to their villages. = 难民们正在被遣返回他们的村庄。


疑问句:
Were the hostages repatriated safely? = 人质是否安全地被遣返？
When will the artifacts be repatriated? = 文物何时被遣返？


否定句:
The government did not repatriate the dissidents. = 政府没有遣返异议人士。
The company has not yet repatriated all its assets. = 公司尚未将所有资产汇回。


PHRASE->

repatriate funds = 汇回资金
repatriate assets = 遣返资产
repatriate citizens = 遣返公民
repatriate prisoners = 遣返囚犯
repatriate refugees = 遣返难民


词源:

repatriated 源自法语 "répatrié,"  过去分词形式。  "re-" 表示 "再次" 或 "重新"，"patri-"  来自拉丁语 " patria"  (祖国)，"-ate" 是动词后缀。  因此，其字面意思是 "送回祖国"。
