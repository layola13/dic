objectives:/əbˈdʒɛktɪvz/ | n. | 目标，目的 | Things aimed at or desired as a result of effort; goals.  Objectives are specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART). |  例句：The company's objectives are to increase profits and expand into new markets.  这家公司的目标是增加利润并拓展新市场。


例句：Our main objective this year is to reduce costs. 今年我们的主要目标是降低成本。


近义词：goals, aims, targets, purposes | 反义词：outcomes, results (in the sense of unintended consequences) | 词性:名词


词源:  From Old French *objectifs, from Latin *objectivus, from objectum, past participle of obicere "to throw against, oppose," from ob- "against" + jacere "to throw."  The word's evolution reflects the idea of something "thrown before" one as a target or goal.


USAGE->

What are the objectives of this project? = 这个项目的目的是什么？
What objectives does the company hope to achieve? = 公司希望实现什么目标？


一般过去时:
What were the objectives of the previous meeting? = 上次会议的目标是什么？
What objectives did they set for themselves? = 他们为自己设定了什么目标？


现在完成时:
What objectives have we already achieved? = 我们已经实现了哪些目标？
What objectives have they accomplished so far? = 到目前为止他们完成了哪些目标？


过去完成时:
What objectives had they hoped to achieve before the setback? = 在遇到挫折之前他们希望实现哪些目标？
What objectives had the team already met by the deadline? = 到截止日期为止团队已经完成了哪些目标？


将来时:

虚拟语气:
