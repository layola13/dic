cartwheeling:/ˈkɑːrtˌwiːlɪŋ/| v.|侧手翻；翻筋斗；|The act of performing a cartwheel, a gymnastic movement where one rotates the body sideways while supporting oneself on the hands and feet.  It can also refer to something moving or progressing rapidly and erratically.|例句：The children were cartwheeling across the lawn. 孩子们在草坪上翻筋斗。

The gymnast performed a flawless cartwheeling sequence. 体操运动员完成了一套完美的侧手翻动作。|近义词：somersaulting, tumbling, rolling|反义词：standing still, remaining stationary|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cartwheels across the field. 他在田野里翻筋斗。
She cartwheels with grace and precision. 她优雅而精准地翻筋斗。

一般过去时:
The child cartwheeled into the pool. 孩子一头扎进游泳池里（翻着筋斗）。
They cartwheeled down the hill. 他们从山上翻滚而下。

现在完成时:
She has cartwheeled across the stage many times. 她多次在舞台上翻筋斗。
He has never cartwheeled before. 他以前从未翻过筋斗。

过去完成时:
Before the performance, she had cartwheeled several times to warm up. 演出前，她已经翻了好几次筋斗热身。
They had cartwheeled all afternoon. 他们整个下午都在翻筋斗。

将来时:
He will cartwheel across the finish line. 他将翻着筋斗冲过终点线。
She will cartwheel during her dance routine.  她将在她的舞蹈表演中翻筋斗。

虚拟语气:
If I could cartwheel, I would do it all day. 如果我会翻筋斗，我会整天翻。
I wish I could cartwheel as well as she can. 我希望我能像她一样翻筋斗。

被动语态: (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice)
The cartwheel was attempted but failed. 侧手翻尝试了，但失败了。 (This example highlights the action, not the person performing it)

疑问句:
Can you cartwheel? 你会翻筋斗吗？
Does she often cartwheel? 她经常翻筋斗吗？

否定句:
He cannot cartwheel. 他不会翻筋斗。
They did not cartwheel during the practice. 他们在练习中没有翻筋斗。
