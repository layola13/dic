outplod:/ˈaʊtplɒd/| v.| 沉重地走；艰难地行走；|To walk heavily and slowly, especially over a long distance.|例句：He outplodded through the mud, his boots sinking with each step. 他艰难地跋涉在泥泞中，每一步都陷得很深。例句：The weary travelers outplodded toward their destination as the sun began to set. 疲惫的旅行者在日落时分艰难地向目的地走去。|近义词：trudge, plod, lumber, slog |反义词：scurry, dart, sprint

虚拟语气:

被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used passively.)  The passive voice is not natural for this verb.
