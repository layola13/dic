inhumanely:/ɪnˈhjuːmeɪnli/| adv.|不人道地，残忍地|In a cruel and inhumane way, showing no compassion or consideration for others' suffering.|例句：The animals were treated inhumanely in the factory farm.  这些动物在工厂化农场受到了不人道的待遇。

例句：The prisoners were inhumanely treated during their imprisonment. 囚犯们在监禁期间受到了不人道的待遇。


近义词：cruelly, barbarously, savagely, brutally

反义词：humanely, compassionately, kindly


词性: 副词
