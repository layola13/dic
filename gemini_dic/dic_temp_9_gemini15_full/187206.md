roasting:/ˈroʊstɪŋ/ | v. n. adj. | 烤；烘；烘烤；嘲笑；严厉批评 | The act of cooking food by dry heat, typically in an oven;  the act of teasing or mocking someone severely; the state of being roasted. | 例句：The turkey is roasting in the oven.  烤鸡正在烤箱里烤着。

例句：He gave her a thorough roasting for her mistakes. 他因为她的错误而严厉批评了她。


近义词： (v.) baking, grilling, broiling, barbecuing,  (n.)  criticism,  rebuke,  lambasting (adj.)  roasted, grilled, baked


反义词： (v.) boiling, stewing, frying (n.) praise, compliment (adj.) raw, uncooked


词性: Verb (v.), Noun (n.), Adjective (adj.)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He roasts a chicken every Sunday. 他每周日都烤一只鸡。
She roasts vegetables with herbs. 她用香草烤蔬菜。


一般过去时:
I roasted a leg of lamb for dinner. 我烤了一只羊腿做晚餐。
They roasted marshmallows around the campfire. 他们在营火旁烤棉花糖。


现在完成时:
We have roasted several turkeys this week.  我们这周烤了好几只火鸡。
She has roasted coffee beans for years. 她已经烤咖啡豆好多年了。


过去完成时:
He had roasted the potatoes before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前他已经把土豆烤好了。
They had roasted the nuts for the pie. 他们已经为馅饼烤好了坚果。


将来时:
I will roast a chicken for your birthday. 我会烤一只鸡庆祝你的生日。
We will roast marshmallows at the beach. 我们会在海滩上烤棉花糖。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would roast a duck. 如果我有更多时间，我会烤一只鸭子。
I wish I had roasted the vegetables longer. 我希望我把蔬菜烤久一点。


被动语态:
The chicken was roasted to perfection. 鸡烤得完美无缺。

疑问句:

否定句:
