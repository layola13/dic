flapped:/flæpt/| v.|拍打；拍动；飘动|To move up and down or back and forth quickly and lightly, or to cause something to do this.|The bird flapped its wings and flew away.  这只鸟拍打着翅膀飞走了。The flag flapped in the wind. 旗子在风中飘动。|flutter, beat, wave|still, motionless|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The bird flaps its wings. = 鸟拍打着翅膀。
He flaps his hands nervously. = 他紧张地拍打着手。

一般过去时:
The flag flapped in the breeze yesterday. = 旗子昨天在微风中飘动。
She flapped her arms to keep warm. = 她拍打着胳膊来保暖。

现在完成时:
The wind has flapped the curtains all day. = 风整天都在拍打窗帘。
He has flapped his hands until they are red. = 他一直拍打着手，直到手都红了。

过去完成时:
The kite had flapped wildly before it crashed. = 风筝坠毁之前剧烈地飘动。
She had flapped her hands trying to signal for help. = 她一直拍打着手试图发出求救信号。

将来时:
The leaves will flap in the coming storm. = 叶子将在即将到来的暴风雨中飘动。
He will flap his wings and try to fly. = 他将拍打翅膀试图飞行。

虚拟语气:
If the wind were stronger, the flag would flap more vigorously. = 如果风更大，旗子就会更剧烈地飘动。
I wish the butterfly would flap its wings and land on my flower. = 我希望蝴蝶能拍打翅膀停在我的花上。

被动语态:
The curtains were flapped by the strong wind. = 窗帘被强风拍打着。
His hands were flapped until they were sore. = 他的手被拍打得酸痛。

疑问句:
Does the flag flap in the wind? = 旗子在风中飘动吗？
Are the birds flapping their wings? = 鸟儿们在拍打翅膀吗？

否定句:
The bird didn't flap its wings. = 鸟没有拍打翅膀。
The flag didn't flap in the calm air. = 旗子在平静的空气中没有飘动。
