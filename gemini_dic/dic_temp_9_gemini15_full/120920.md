miscatalogs:/ˌmɪsˈkætəlɒɡz/| v.|错误分类，归类错误|To classify incorrectly; to place in the wrong category.  This verb implies a mistake in the process of cataloging or classifying something.|The librarian miscataloged the book, placing it in the fiction section instead of non-fiction. 图书管理员把书归错了类，把它放在了小说区而不是非小说区。|  misclassify, misfile, misindex | correctly catalog, properly categorize |动词
