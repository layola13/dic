overpedal: /ˌoʊvərˈpɛdəl/ | v. |用力过猛踩踏板；过度使用踏板 | To use a pedal, especially a car's accelerator pedal, too forcefully or excessively.  | 例句：He overpedaled going uphill, causing the engine to overheat. 他上坡时用力过猛踩油门，导致发动机过热。


例句：The inexperienced driver overpedaled on the icy road, resulting in a skid.  新手司机在结冰的路面上用力过猛踩油门，导致汽车打滑。


近义词：overuse, overuse the accelerator, jam the pedal


反义词：underpedal (less common), gently press the pedal


词性：动词
