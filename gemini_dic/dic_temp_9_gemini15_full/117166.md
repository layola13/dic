meanwhile:/ˈmiːnwaɪl/|adv.|同时；在此期间；其间|In the meantime, while something else is happening.  It indicates that something is happening at the same time as something else, or during a period of time.|例句：I'll be at the library; meanwhile, you can start dinner. 我将在图书馆；与此同时，你可以开始做饭。|例句：The children were playing outside; meanwhile, their mother was cleaning the house. 孩子们在外面玩耍；与此同时，他们的母亲正在打扫房子。|近义词：in the meantime, during this time, concurrently, simultaneously|反义词：immediately, instantly, at once
