debtless:/ˈdɛt.ləs/ | adj. | 无债务的，不欠债的 | Free from debt; owing nothing.  | Example:  He finally achieved a debtless existence after years of hard work. 他经过多年的努力终于过上了无债务的生活。 |近义词：unindebted, free of debt |反义词：indebted, in debt


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is debtless. = 他没有债务。
She remains debtless despite her recent expenses. = 尽管她最近有很多开销，但她仍然没有债务。


一般过去时:
They were debtless after paying off their mortgage. = 他们还清抵押贷款后就无债了。
The company became debtless through restructuring. = 公司通过重组摆脱了债务。


现在完成时:
He has been debtless for five years. = 他五年来一直没有债务。
She has remained debtless since selling her business. = 自从她卖掉公司以来，她就一直没有债务。


过去完成时:
They had been debtless for a decade before the unexpected lawsuit. = 在意外的诉讼发生之前，他们已经十年没有债务了。
The firm had become debtless just months before the economic downturn. = 在经济衰退几个月前，该公司就已经摆脱了债务。


将来时:
They will be debtless by the end of the year. = 到年底他们将摆脱债务。
The project will leave them debtless. = 这个项目将使他们摆脱债务。


虚拟语气:
If only I were debtless! = 但愿我没有债务！
I wish I were debtless and could travel the world. = 我希望我没有债务，可以环游世界。

否定句:
