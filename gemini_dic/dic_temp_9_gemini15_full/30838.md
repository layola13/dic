chace:/tʃeɪs/| v. n.|追赶；追捕；追求；机会|To pursue or follow (someone or something) quickly in order to catch or overtake them.|The police chased the robber through the streets.  警察在街上追捕抢劫犯。 |The dog chased its tail. 狗追着它自己的尾巴跑。| pursue, hunt, follow|stop, halt, cease|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He chases the ball every day. 他每天追逐球。
She chases her dreams relentlessly. 她不懈地追求梦想。


一般过去时:
The hunters chased the deer into the forest. 猎人把鹿追进了森林。
He chased after the bus but missed it. 他追赶公交车，但错过了。


现在完成时:
They have chased away the birds from the garden. 他们把鸟从花园里赶走了。
I have chased this opportunity for years. 我已经追求这个机会多年了。


过去完成时:
She had chased the thief before the police arrived. 在警察到达之前，她已经追赶过小偷了。
We had chased all our dreams before settling down. 我们在安定下来之前已经追逐过所有的梦想了。


将来时:
They will chase the criminals across the border. 他们将追捕越过边境的罪犯。
I will chase my ambitions until I succeed. 我将追求我的抱负直到成功。


虚拟语气:
If I were rich, I would chase my dream of traveling the world. 如果我有钱，我会追求环游世界的梦想。
I wish I had chased that opportunity years ago. 我真希望几年前我抓住过那个机会。


被动语态:
The robbers were chased by the police. 抢劫犯被警察追捕。
The thief was chased into the alley. 小偷被追进了小巷。


疑问句:
Why are you chasing me? 你为什么追我？
What are you chasing after? 你在追逐什么？


否定句:
He didn't chase the cat. 他没有追猫。
They didn't chase after the fame and fortune. 他们没有追逐名利。
