cleaning-up: /ˈkliːnɪŋ ʌp/ | v. | 打扫，清理 |  The act of making something clean and tidy. This is a gerund, functioning as a noun. It describes the process of cleaning. | 例句：The cleaning-up after the party took hours.  派对后的清理工作花了几个小时。


The cleaning-up of the spill was a priority. 清理泄漏是当务之急。| 近义词：tidying up, cleaning, sprucing up | 反义词：messing up, soiling, dirtying | 动词
