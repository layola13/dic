regifted:/ˌriˈɡɪftɪd/ | adj. | 再赠送的；转赠的 |  Given as a gift a second time, after having been received as a gift.  This describes a gift that has been given to someone, and then that person gives it to someone else. |  例句：She regifted the sweater her aunt gave her because it didn't fit. 她把阿姨送她的毛衣转赠了，因为它不合身。


例句：The vase was a regifted item from a previous Christmas.  这个花瓶是前一个圣诞节转赠的礼物。

近义词：re-gifted, secondhand (in the context of gifts), passed on

反义词：newly purchased, original


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He regifts unwanted presents. = 他转送他不想要的礼物。
She regifts clothes that don't fit her. = 她转送那些不合身的衣服。

一般过去时:
I regifted the book to my brother. = 我把那本书转送给了我哥哥。
They regifted the chocolates to their neighbours. = 他们把巧克力转送给了他们的邻居。

现在完成时:
She has regifted that scarf twice already. = 她那条围巾已经转送了两次了。
He has regifted many items over the years. = 多年来他已经转送了很多东西。

过去完成时:
I had regifted the wine before realizing it was past its expiry date. = 我在意识到酒过期之前已经转送了它。
They had regifted the painting before discovering its true value. = 他们在发现画作的真正价值之前已经转送了它。

将来时:
I will regift the tie to my uncle. = 我将会把领带转送给我叔叔。
She will regift the candles to her friend. = 她将会把蜡烛转送给她朋友。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't regift the expensive watch. = 如果我是你，我不会转送那块昂贵的表。
I wish I hadn't regifted that perfume; it was my mother's favorite. = 我真希望我没有转送那瓶香水；那是我妈妈最喜欢的。

被动语态:
The present was regifted several times. = 这份礼物被转送了好几次。
Many items are regifted during the holidays. = 节日期间有很多东西被转送。

疑问句:
Have you ever regifted anything? = 你曾经转送过什么东西吗？
Is it appropriate to regift used items? = 转送用过的物品合适吗？


否定句:
I didn't regift the gift because it was sentimental. = 我没有转送这份礼物，因为它很有纪念意义。
She doesn't usually regift anything. = 她通常不会转送任何东西。
