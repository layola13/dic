duelling:/ˈduːəlɪŋ/| n. v.|决斗；争论|The act of fighting a duel, or engaging in a contest or argument.|例句：The two men engaged in a fierce duelling over the woman's affections.  这两个男人为了那个女人的喜爱而进行了激烈的争斗。|例句：The political duelling between the two parties continued throughout the election campaign.  两个政党之间的政治论战贯穿了整个竞选活动。|近义词：combat, contest, quarrelling|反义词：agreement, harmony
