chamberlet:/ˈtʃeɪmbəlɪt/| n. |小房间；小室|A small chamber; a small room.  It is often used to describe a small, private room, perhaps within a larger structure.  The word implies a sense of intimacy and seclusion. |例句：The artist retreated to his chamberlet to work on his masterpiece.  艺术家退回到他的小房间里创作他的杰作。

The old house had a tiny chamberlet tucked away in the attic. 这栋老房子阁楼里有个小小的房间。|近义词：cubicle, closet, alcove |反义词：hall, expanse
