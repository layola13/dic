intimacies:/ˈɪntɪməsiz/| n. |亲密关系；隐私；私事 |The plural form of intimacy, referring to instances or aspects of close relationships or private matters.  It suggests a collection of private or deeply personal experiences or connections. |例句：The book delves into the intimacies of their marriage. 这本书深入探讨了他们婚姻中的私密细节。


例句：Sharing such intimacies with a stranger felt incredibly vulnerable.  与陌生人分享如此私密的事情感觉非常脆弱。


近义词：confidences, secrets, private matters, close relationships


反义词：estrangement, distance, public knowledge


词性: 名词
