geologist:/dʒiˈɒlədʒɪst/| n. |地质学家|A scientist who studies the structure, origin, and composition of the Earth.  Geologists work with rocks, minerals, and landforms to understand Earth's history and processes.|例句：The geologist discovered a new mineral deposit.这位地质学家发现了一个新的矿床。|例句：Many geologists believe that the earthquake was caused by tectonic plate movement.许多地质学家认为地震是由板块运动引起的。|近义词：geomorphologist, petrologist |反义词：None
