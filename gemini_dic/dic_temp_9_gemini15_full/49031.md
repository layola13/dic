degreasing:/diːˈɡriːsɪŋ/| v.|去油，脱脂|The process of removing grease or oil from a surface.  It's often used in industrial cleaning and preparation for painting or other processes.|例句：The degreasing process is crucial before painting the car. 去油过程在汽车喷漆前至关重要。

例句：We used a solvent to degrease the engine parts before reassembly. 我们在重新组装之前用溶剂清洗发动机零件。


近义词：cleaning, defatting, oil removal|反义词：greasing, oiling|词性：动词
