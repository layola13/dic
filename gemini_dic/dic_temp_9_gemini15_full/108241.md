laughs:/læfs/| n. int.|笑声，笑；大笑|The sound of someone laughing. It can also be used as an interjection to represent the act of laughing.|例句：Her laughs filled the room.  她的笑声充满了房间。|例句：He laughs at my jokes. 他嘲笑我的笑话。|近义词：chuckle, giggle, guffaw|反义词：cry, weep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He laughs loudly. = 他大声笑。
She laughs at silly jokes. = 她嘲笑愚蠢的笑话。

一般过去时:
They laughed until they cried. = 他们笑到哭。
He laughed heartily at the comedian's performance. = 他衷心欣赏喜剧演员的表演并大笑。

现在完成时:
I have laughed so hard my stomach hurts. = 我笑得太厉害了，肚子都疼了。
She has laughed at that joke many times. = 她多次嘲笑过那个笑话。

过去完成时:
We had laughed ourselves silly before he arrived. = 他来之前我们已经笑疯了。
They had laughed at the prank earlier that day. = 他们那天早些时候就嘲笑过那个恶作剧。

将来时:
They will laugh when they hear the news. = 当他们听到这个消息时，他们会大笑。
We will laugh at the memory for years to come. = 未来几年，我们都会笑着回忆这件事。

虚拟语气:
If I told that joke, they would laugh. = 如果我讲那个笑话，他们会笑。
I wish I could laugh like that. = 我真希望我能像那样笑。

被动语态:
(Laughs is rarely used in passive voice.  A better phrasing would be needed depending on context.)  His jokes were laughed at by everyone.  = 他的笑话被大家嘲笑了。
The comedian's performance was met with laughter.  = 喜剧演员的表演博得了阵阵笑声。


疑问句:
Why are you laughing? = 你为什么笑？
Did anyone laugh at his joke? = 有人嘲笑他的笑话吗？

否定句:
They didn't laugh at the joke. = 他们没有笑那个笑话。
I didn't laugh; I was crying. = 我没有笑，我在哭。


PHRASE->
burst into laughter = 突然大笑起来
have a good laugh = 开怀大笑
laugh at someone = 嘲笑某人
laugh off something = 对...一笑置之
laugh out loud = 放声大笑
