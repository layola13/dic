contesting:/kənˈtestɪŋ/| v. |争论；质疑；竞争；反对 |The act of disputing or challenging something;  a competition or struggle. |例句：They are contesting the election results. 他们正在质疑选举结果。  |例句：Many athletes are contesting for the gold medal. 许多运动员正在争夺金牌。|近义词：disputing, challenging, competing, opposing |反义词：accepting, agreeing, conceding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are contesting the will. = 他们在争夺遗嘱。
He is contesting the charges against him. = 他正在对指控他进行辩驳。


一般过去时:
She contested the decision in court. = 她上诉法院挑战该决定。
They contested the election fiercely. = 他们激烈地竞争选举。


现在完成时:
He has been contesting this issue for years. = 他多年来一直在争论这个问题。
They have contested many races together. = 他们一起参加过许多比赛。


过去完成时:
The team had contested several matches before the final. = 在决赛之前，该队已经参加了几场比赛。
She had contested the ruling before appealing to a higher court. = 在向高级法院上诉之前，她已经对裁决提出了异议。


将来时:
They will be contesting the championship next year. = 他们明年将角逐冠军。
He will contest the case in the Supreme Court. = 他将在最高法院对该案进行辩护。


虚拟语气:
If he contested the election, he might win. = 如果他参加竞选，他可能会获胜。
I wish I had contested the decision earlier. = 我真希望我早点对这个决定提出异议。


被动语态:
The election results were contested by several candidates. = 多名候选人对选举结果提出了质疑。
The decision is being contested in the appeals court. = 这一决定正在上诉法院受到争议。


疑问句:
Are they contesting the findings of the report? = 他们是否对报告的调查结果提出异议？
Is he contesting the speeding ticket? = 他是否对超速罚单提出异议？


否定句:
He did not contest the allegations. = 他没有对这些指控提出异议。
They did not contest the will. = 他们没有对遗嘱提出异议。



PHRASE->
contesting a will = 争夺遗嘱
contesting an election = 参加竞选
contesting a claim = 对一项主张提出异议
contesting a point = 对一个论点提出异议
contesting a decision = 对一项决定提出异议
