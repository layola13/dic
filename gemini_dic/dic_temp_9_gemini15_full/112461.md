loudspeaker:/ˈlaʊdspikər/ | n. | 扬声器，扩音器 | An electronic device that converts electrical signals into sound waves, making sounds louder and audible over a distance.  | The loudspeaker blasted out the news. 扬声器大声播放新闻。 | The concert used a powerful loudspeaker system.  音乐会使用了强大的扬声器系统。| microphone, speaker |  (无明确反义词，可考虑用silent, quiet 来表达相反的状态)| 名词
