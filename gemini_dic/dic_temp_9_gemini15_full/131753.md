noncongenital: /ˌnɒnkənˈdʒɛnɪtəl/ | adj. |非先天性的，后天性的| Not present from birth; acquired after birth.  It refers to conditions or traits that develop after birth, as opposed to congenital conditions which are present at birth. | Example:  The doctor determined the child's hearing loss was noncongenital, resulting from an infection.  医生确定孩子的听力损失是非先天性的，是由感染引起的。|近义词：acquired, postnatal, postnatal,  |反义词：congenital, innate, inborn |
