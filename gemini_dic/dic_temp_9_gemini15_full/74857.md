frequently:/ˈfriːkwəntli/| adv. |频繁地，经常地 |Often or many times.  It emphasizes the repetition of an action or event.|He frequently visits his grandparents.|近义词：often, regularly, commonly|反义词：rarely, seldom, infrequently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He frequently visits his parents. = 他经常去看望他的父母。
They frequently eat out. = 他们经常出去吃饭。

一般过去时:
She frequently called me last week. = 她上周经常打电话给我。
We frequently went to the beach during the summer. = 夏天我们经常去海滩。

现在完成时:
I have frequently been to that restaurant. = 我经常去过那家餐馆。
They have frequently helped us in the past. = 他们过去经常帮助我们。

过去完成时:
He had frequently warned them about the danger. = 他曾多次警告他们危险。
She had frequently complained about the noise. = 她曾多次抱怨噪音。

将来时:
I will frequently check on the progress. = 我将经常检查进度。
They will frequently update us on the situation. = 他们将经常向我们通报情况。


虚拟语气:
If I frequently exercised, I would be healthier. = 如果我经常锻炼，我会更健康。
I wish I frequently visited my friends. = 我希望我经常去看望我的朋友。

被动语态:
The machine is frequently used. = 这台机器经常被使用。
The problem is frequently discussed. = 这个问题经常被讨论。

疑问句:
Do you frequently travel? = 你经常旅行吗？
Does he frequently attend meetings? = 他经常参加会议吗？

否定句:
I don't frequently go to the movies. = 我不经常去看电影。
She doesn't frequently use her car. = 她不经常使用她的车。
