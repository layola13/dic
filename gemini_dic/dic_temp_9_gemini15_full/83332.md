gypsum:/ˈdʒɪpsəm/| n. |石膏|A soft white or grayish mineral consisting of hydrated calcium sulfate, CaSO₄·2H₂O, occurring chiefly in sedimentary deposits and used to make plaster of Paris and other plasters.|The walls were made of gypsum plaster. 墙是用石膏灰泥砌成的。|近义词：plaster, selenite|反义词：无
