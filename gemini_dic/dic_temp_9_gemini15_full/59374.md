earphone:/ˈɪəfoʊn/| n.|耳机|A small device, typically worn in or over the ear, that receives sound from a radio, telephone, etc.|例句：I listen to music with my earphones every day. 我每天都戴着耳机听音乐。  例句：These earphones have excellent noise cancellation. 这款耳机具有出色的降噪功能。|近义词：headphones, earbuds|反义词:speaker
