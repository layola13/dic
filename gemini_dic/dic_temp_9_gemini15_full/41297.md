cordon-off:/ˌkɔːrdən ˈɒf/| v.|警戒封锁，隔离|To prevent access to an area by placing a cordon around it.  This usually involves police or other authority figures establishing a perimeter to control movement and ensure safety or security.|例句：The police cordoned off the street after the bomb threat.  警察在收到炸弹威胁后封锁了街道。

例句：The area was cordoned off to allow emergency services access. 该区域被封锁以方便紧急服务部门进入。


近义词：block off, seal off, isolate, shut off


反义词：open up, allow access
