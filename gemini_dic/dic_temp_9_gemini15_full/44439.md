cross-refers:/ˌkrɒs rɪˈfɜːrz/| v.|交叉引用，相互参照|To refer to something else, typically another part of a document or a different document, for further information or context.  This is often used in academic writing or technical documentation.|例句：The appendix cross-refers to several tables in the main body of the report. 附录交叉引用了报告正文中的几个表格。

例句：This section cross-refers the reader to chapter 5 for a more detailed explanation. 本节引导读者参阅第5章以获得更详细的解释。


近义词：refer to, cite, link


反义词：N/A


词性:动词
