rustle:/ˈrʌsəl/| v. n.|沙沙作响；轻微的摩擦声|To make a soft, crackling sound, like that of dry leaves or silk being moved.|The leaves rustled in the breeze.  树叶在微风中沙沙作响。The silk dress rustled as she moved.  她一动，丝绸衣服就发出沙沙声。|whisper, murmur, susurrus|None|动词，名词

USAGE->

The leaves rustle in the wind. = 树叶在风中沙沙作响。
The silk fabric rustles softly. = 丝绸布料轻轻地沙沙作响。

一般过去时:
The wind rustled through the trees. = 风在树林中沙沙作响。
She rustled her papers nervously. = 她紧张地沙沙地翻动着文件。
