enjoining:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 命令；吩咐；劝告；禁止 |  To enjoin means to issue an order or command, often formally or authoritatively; it can also mean to urge or advise strongly.  |例句：The judge enjoined the company from polluting the river. = 法官命令该公司停止污染河流。

The doctor enjoined him to rest. = 医生劝告他休息。

近义词：command, order, instruct, urge, advise | 反义词：permit, allow, tolerate | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher enjoins students to study hard. = 老师命令学生努力学习。
The government enjoins citizens to conserve water. = 政府劝告公民节约用水。


一般过去时:
The court enjoined the defendant from contacting the witness. = 法院禁止被告与证人联系。
My mother enjoined me to be careful. = 我妈妈告诫我要小心。


现在完成时:
The judge has enjoined the company from further construction. = 法官已禁止该公司继续施工。
She has enjoined us to be patient. = 她一直劝我们耐心等待。


过去完成时:
He had enjoined them to secrecy before the meeting. = 他在会议前已告诫他们要保密。
The lawyer had enjoined his client to cooperate with the police. = 律师已劝告他的当事人配合警方。


将来时:
The mayor will enjoin residents to evacuate before the hurricane. = 市长将命令居民在飓风来临前撤离。
The doctor will enjoin the patient to follow a strict diet. = 医生将劝告病人遵循严格的饮食。


虚拟语气:
If the judge had enjoined the demolition, the building would still stand. = 如果法官禁止拆除，那座建筑物现在仍然矗立着。
I wish the doctor had enjoined me to get more rest. = 我真希望医生当时劝我多休息。


被动语态:
The protesters were enjoined from blocking the highway. = 示威者被禁止封锁高速公路。
We were enjoined to keep the information confidential. = 我们被告知要保守秘密。


疑问句:
Was the company enjoined from releasing the toxic waste? = 公司是否被禁止释放有毒废物？
Will they be enjoined to appear in court? = 他们会被命令出庭吗？


否定句:
The judge did not enjoin the defendant from speaking to the media. = 法官没有禁止被告与媒体交谈。
The doctor did not enjoin him from participating in strenuous activities. = 医生没有禁止他参加剧烈运动。


PHRASE->
enjoin upon：命令…去做…
enjoin someone to do something: 命令某人做某事
be enjoined to: 被命令做…
enjoin silence: 命令保持安静
enjoin restraint: 命令克制


词源:
源自古法语 *enjoindre，最终源自拉丁语 *in-iungere，意为“加入；连接”。  The sense shifted from "joining" to "commanding" over time, possibly reflecting the idea of joining people together under a single command.
