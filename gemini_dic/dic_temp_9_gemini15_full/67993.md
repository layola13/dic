factionally:/ˈfækʃənəli/ | adv. |派系地，派别地 |In a way that relates to or is characteristic of factions.  It suggests actions or behaviors motivated by the interests or goals of a particular faction or group, often in a divisive or contentious manner. |The city council was factionally divided on the issue of the new stadium.  市议会就新体育馆的问题存在派系分歧。|  partially, fractionally |  unitedly, unanimously | 副词
