jar:/dʒɑːr/| n. |罐子，广口瓶|A jar is a container, typically made of glass or ceramic, with a wide mouth and usually a lid. It is used for storing food, liquids, or other items.|例句：She stored the homemade jam in a glass jar. 她把自制果酱储存在一个玻璃罐子里。|例句：He opened the jar of pickles. 他打开了腌黄瓜罐子。|近义词：container, pot, vessel|反义词:  (无明确反义词)|名词
