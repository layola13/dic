kinetic-artists:/kaɪˈnɛtɪk ˈɑːrtɪsts/| n.|动力艺术家|Kinetic artists are artists who create artworks that incorporate movement, either through mechanical means or through the interaction of light, air, or other natural forces.  Their works often explore the relationship between art, technology, and the viewer's perception of time and space.|Example:  Many kinetic artists of the 1960s explored the possibilities of motorized sculptures.  许多20世纪60年代的动力艺术家探索了电动雕塑的可能性。|近义词：motion artists, movement artists|反义词：static artists
