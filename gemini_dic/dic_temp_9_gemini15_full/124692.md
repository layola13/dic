moving: /ˈmuːvɪŋ/ | adj., v. | 移动的；感人的；动人的；搬家的 | Moving describes something that is in motion or causes strong emotion.  It can also refer to the act of changing residence. |

例句:
The moving truck arrived late. =  搬家卡车迟到了。

The film's ending was very moving. = 电影的结尾非常感人。


近义词: affecting, touching, emotional, relocating, shifting, transferring

反义词: still, stationary, unmoving, unemotional, unaffected


词性: Adjective, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river is moving swiftly. = 河水流得很快。
She is moving to a new apartment. = 她正在搬到一间新公寓。


一般过去时:
The car moved slowly down the street. = 车子缓慢地驶过街道。
They moved to the countryside last year. = 他们去年搬到了乡下。


现在完成时:
We have moved three times in the past five years. = 在过去的五年里，我们搬了三次家。
The wind has moved the leaves. = 风吹动了树叶。


过去完成时:
They had already moved before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他们已经搬走了。
The snow had moved the car slightly. = 雪微微地移动了汽车。


将来时:
I will be moving to London next month. = 我下个月将搬到伦敦。
The company will move its headquarters. = 公司将搬迁总部。


虚拟语气:
If I were moving, I would need help. = 如果我要搬家，我会需要帮助。
I wish I were moving to a warmer climate. = 我希望我能搬到气候更温暖的地方。


被动语态:
The furniture was moved carefully. = 家具被小心地搬动。
The date of the meeting has been moved. = 会议日期已被更改。


疑问句:
Is the car moving? = 车子在动吗？
Are you moving soon? = 你很快就要搬家了吗？


否定句:
The picture isn't moving. = 图片没有动。
He isn't moving to a new house. = 他没有搬到新房子。
