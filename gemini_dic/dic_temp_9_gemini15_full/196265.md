serenata:/ˌsɛrəˈnɑːtə/| n. |小夜曲；夜曲 | A serenade is a musical composition, typically one of a romantic or sentimental nature, performed outdoors at night, traditionally by a lover beneath the window of his beloved.  | Example: He serenaded her with a beautiful song under her balcony. 他在她阳台下为她唱了一首美丽的歌。|近义词：night music, nocturne|反义词：无
