magnetizes: /ˈmæɡnɪtaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使磁化；吸引；迷住 | To cause (something) to become magnetic; to attract strongly; to fascinate or captivate. |

例句：
The magnet magnetizes the iron filings. = 磁铁使铁屑磁化。

The speaker's charisma magnetizes the audience. = 演讲者的魅力吸引了观众。


近义词：attract, fascinate, charm, captivate

反义词：repel, disenchant

词源:
From "magnet" + "-ize" (to make or become).  The word "magnet" has roots in the Greek word "magnētis lithos," meaning "Magnesian stone," referring to a type of naturally occurring magnetic iron ore found in Magnesia, a region in ancient Greece.
