quahaug:/ˈkwɔːhɔːɡ/| n. |魁蛤；硬壳蛤 |A quahaug is a type of hard-shelled clam, commonly found in the northeastern United States.  They are often eaten steamed or chowdered.  The term is primarily used in New England. |例句：We dug for quahaugs on the beach all afternoon. = 我们整个下午都在海滩上挖魁蛤。

例句：The clam chowder was made with fresh, local quahaugs. = 这份蛤蜊浓汤是用新鲜的当地魁蛤做的。


近义词：hard-shell clam, round clam |反义词：N/A |词性:名词
