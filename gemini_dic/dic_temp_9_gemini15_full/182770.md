repasture: /rɪˈpæsʧər/ | n. |再放牧；重新放牧 |The act of putting livestock back into a pasture after a period of rest or after being moved to another location.  It's a relatively rare word, primarily used in agricultural contexts. |例句：After the pasture had rested for several months, the farmer initiated a repasture of his sheep.  = 牧场休养了几个月后，农民开始重新放牧他的羊。  |近义词：regrazing,  pasturing (again) |反义词： removing livestock from pasture
