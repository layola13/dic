seamstress:/ˈsiːmstrɛs/| n. |女裁缝，缝纫女工 | A woman whose job is sewing, especially making or repairing clothes.  |She is a skilled seamstress, known for her beautiful embroidery. 她是一位技艺精湛的女裁缝，以其精美的刺绣而闻名。| tailor, dressmaker |  (无明确反义词，可考虑 unskilled worker) | 名词
