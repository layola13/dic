rediscoveries:/ˌriːdɪˈskʌvəriːz/| n.|重新发现；再发现|The act of discovering something again, or the things that are rediscovered.  It implies a previous discovery that was subsequently forgotten or lost to knowledge.|例句：The rediscoveries of ancient texts shed new light on the history of the region.  重新发现古代文本为该地区的历史带来了新的光辉。

例句：His rediscoveries in the field of mathematics earned him international acclaim. 他在数学领域的重新发现为他赢得了国际赞誉。


近义词：reinventions,  re-explorations, renewed findings


反义词：losses, forgetting, oblivion


词性: 名词
