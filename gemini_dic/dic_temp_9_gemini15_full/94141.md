impeding: /ɪmˈpiːdɪŋ/ | adj. | 阻碍的；妨碍的 | Preventing movement or progress; hindering. | 例句：The heavy rain was impeding our progress.  大雨阻碍了我们的进展。  The ongoing strike is impeding production.  持续的罢工正在阻碍生产。| 近义词：hindering, obstructing, hampering, obstructing | 反义词：aiding, assisting, facilitating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heavy traffic is impeding my commute. = 拥堵的交通正在阻碍我的通勤。
This policy impedes economic growth. =这项政策阻碍了经济增长。


一般过去时:
The storm impeded travel for several days. = 暴风雨阻碍了几天行程。
His lack of confidence impeded his career advancement. = 他缺乏自信阻碍了他的职业发展。


现在完成时:
Technological advancements have impeded the spread of misinformation. = 技术进步已经阻碍了错误信息的传播。
Years of neglect have impeded the city's infrastructure. = 多年的忽视已经阻碍了城市的基建。


过去完成时:
The faulty brakes had impeded the car's ability to stop quickly. = 刹车故障已经阻碍了汽车快速停车的能力。
Previous failures had impeded their confidence in the project. = 之前的失败已经阻碍了他们对项目的信心。


将来时:
The expected snowstorm will impede air travel. = 预计的暴风雪将阻碍空中交通。
Further regulations will impede business development. = 进一步的法规将阻碍商业发展。


虚拟语气:
If the road weren't blocked, it wouldn't impede our journey. = 如果道路没有堵塞，它就不会阻碍我们的旅程。
If the government hadn't imposed restrictions, the economy would not have been impeded. = 如果政府没有施加限制，经济就不会受到阻碍。


被动语态:
Our progress was impeded by unexpected delays. = 我们的进展受到了意外延误的阻碍。
The project is being impeded by funding shortages. = 项目正因资金短缺而受阻。


疑问句:
Is anything impeding your work? = 有什么事情阻碍你的工作吗？
What is impeding the completion of the project? = 什么在阻碍项目的完成？


否定句:
Nothing impeded their relentless pursuit of success. = 没有什么能够阻碍他们对成功的执着追求。
The new system does not impede the workflow. = 新系统不会阻碍工作流程。


PHRASE->
impeding factor = 阻碍因素
impeding circumstances = 阻碍环境
impeding progress = 阻碍进展
impeding growth = 阻碍增长
impeding development = 阻碍发展

词源:
From Latin *impedire, from im- ("not") + pedis ("foot").  The original meaning related to "entangling the feet," hence hindering movement.
