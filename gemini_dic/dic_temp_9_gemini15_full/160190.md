pondering:/ˈpɒndərɪŋ/| /ˈpɑːndəriŋ/ | v. |沉思；仔细考虑 | To think deeply and carefully about something.  |She spent the afternoon pondering the problem. 她花了一下午的时间仔细考虑这个问题。|He sat pondering his next move. 他坐着思考下一步该怎么走。|considering, contemplating, reflecting | ignoring, disregarding, neglecting |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am pondering the meaning of life. = 我正在思考人生的意义。
He is pondering his options. = 他正在仔细考虑他的选择。

一般过去时:
She pondered the question for a long time. = 她长时间地思考这个问题。
They pondered the implications of their decision. = 他们仔细考虑了他们决定的影响。

现在完成时:
I have been pondering this issue for weeks. = 我已经思考这个问题好几个星期了。
She has pondered the matter carefully. = 她已经仔细考虑了这件事。

过去完成时:
He had pondered the problem for days before finding a solution. = 他在找到解决方案之前已经思考这个问题好几天了。
They had pondered the options before making their choice. = 他们做出选择之前已经考虑过各种方案。

将来时:
I will be pondering this offer for a while. = 我会考虑这个提议一段时间。
She will ponder the consequences before acting. = 她会在采取行动之前仔细考虑后果。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would ponder the matter further. = 如果我有更多时间，我会进一步思考这个问题。
I wish I had pondered the risks before investing. = 我希望在投资之前我已经考虑过风险。

被动语态:
The problem was pondered by the committee for many hours. = 这个问题被委员会仔细考虑了好几个小时。
The proposal is being pondered by the board. = 这个提案正在由董事会考虑。

疑问句:
Are you pondering a new career path? = 你正在考虑新的职业道路吗？
What are they pondering? = 他们在思考什么？


否定句:
He did not ponder the consequences of his actions. = 他没有考虑他行为的后果。
She wasn't pondering the issue at all. = 她根本没有考虑这个问题。
