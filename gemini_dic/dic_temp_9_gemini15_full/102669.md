jambooree:/dʒæmˈbʊri/ | n. |大型集会；盛会；狂欢节 | A large gathering, especially one held outdoors and often associated with Scouting or a similar organization.  It's characterized by festivities, games, and camaraderie. |例句：The Scout jamboree was a huge success, attracting thousands of participants. 童子军大会非常成功，吸引了成千上万的参与者。

例句：The town held a lively jamboree to celebrate its anniversary.  这个小镇举办了一场热闹的盛会来庆祝它的周年纪念日。


近义词：festival, celebration, rally, gathering

反义词：solitude, isolation


词性：名词
