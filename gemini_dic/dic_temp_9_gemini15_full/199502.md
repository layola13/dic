sines:/saɪnz/| n.|正弦曲线；正弦函数|The word "sines" refers to the trigonometric function that, for an acute angle in a right-angled triangle, is the ratio of the length of the side opposite the angle to the length of the hypotenuse.  It is also used to describe the graph of this function, which is a wave-like curve.|例句：The graph showed a series of sines.  图表显示了一系列正弦曲线。  The calculations involved the use of sines and cosines. 计算涉及正弦和余弦的运用。|近义词：sinusoidal curves|反义词：无
