judgers:/ˈdʒʌdʒərz/| n.|判断者；评价者|People who make judgments or assessments.  They can be individuals or groups who evaluate, criticize, or form opinions about something or someone. |例句：The judges were impressed by the contestants' talent.  评委们对参赛者的才华印象深刻。|例句：The judges’ scores were surprisingly varied. 评委的分数出奇地多样化。|近义词：critics, assessors, evaluators|反义词：observers (in a neutral sense)
