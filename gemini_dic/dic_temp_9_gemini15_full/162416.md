preadjourn:/ˌpriːəˈdʒɜːrn/| vt.|提前休会；提前结束|To adjourn or suspend (a meeting, session, etc.) before the scheduled time.  |例句：The committee decided to preadjourn the meeting due to lack of quorum. 委员会决定因缺乏法定人数而提前休会。|例句：The judge preadjourned the court hearing to allow for further investigation.  法官提前休庭以进行进一步调查。|近义词：suspend, postpone, defer|反义词：continue, prolong
