patti:/ˈpæti/| n.| 薄饼，肉饼 |A patty is a small, flat cake of minced meat, fish, or vegetables, often fried or baked.  It can also refer to a small, flat cake of other ingredients, such as potato. |例句：She made a delicious salmon patty for dinner. 她做了美味的三文鱼肉饼当晚餐。

例句：I ordered a crab patty with a side salad. 我点了一份蟹肉饼配一份沙拉。


近义词：burger, cake, fritter |反义词：无 |词性:名词
