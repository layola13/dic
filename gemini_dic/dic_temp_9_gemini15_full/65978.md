exal:/ɪɡˈzɔːl/| vt.| 提升；赞扬；使…兴奋 | to raise to a higher rank or position; to praise highly; to elevate in spirit or feeling |例句：The king exalted his loyal knight. 国王提升了他忠诚的骑士。  The preacher exalted the virtues of faith. 传教士赞扬了信仰的美德。|近义词：elevate, praise, glorify |反义词：debase, degrade, humiliate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exalts his family above all else. = 他把他的家人看得高于一切。
The church exalts God's glory. = 教堂颂扬上帝的荣耀。


一般过去时:
They exalted the athlete after his victory. = 他们在运动员获胜后赞扬了他。
The general exalted his troops' bravery. = 将军赞扬了他部队的勇敢。


现在完成时:
She has exalted the importance of education. = 她已经强调了教育的重要性。
The media has exalted the celebrity's achievements. = 媒体已经高度赞扬了这位名人的成就。


过去完成时:
They had exalted him to the position of leader. = 他们已经将他提升为领导者。
The artist had exalted the beauty of nature in his paintings. = 艺术家在他的画作中已经提升了自然的美丽。


将来时:
The company will exalt its new product line. = 公司将推广其新的产品线。
The ceremony will exalt the country's heroes. = 典礼将颂扬国家的英雄。


虚拟语气:
If they exalted honesty, society would be better. = 如果他们崇尚诚实，社会将会更好。
I wish they exalted the value of hard work. = 我希望他们能重视努力工作的价值。


被动语态:
He was exalted to a position of power. = 他被提升到一个权力职位。
Her achievements were exalted by the press. = 她的成就受到了媒体的赞扬。


疑问句:
Does the company exalt its employees? = 公司是否重视它的员工？
Will they exalt the winner of the competition? = 他们会赞扬比赛的获胜者吗？


否定句:
They did not exalt his contributions to the project. = 他们没有赞扬他对项目的贡献。
The government did not exalt the protesters' cause. = 政府没有赞扬抗议者的诉求。


PHRASE->
exalt oneself = 自我标榜
exalt to heaven = 赞美到天上
exalt above = 提升到…之上
exalt in = 欣喜于
exalt one's spirit = 振奋精神
