signor:/ˈsɪnjɔːr/| n. |先生，阁下|A title of respect used before the name of a man, especially in Italian-speaking countries.  It is equivalent to "Mr." in English.  |例句： Signor Rossi is expected to arrive at any moment. 罗西先生预计随时都会到达。|例句：The Signorina bowed gracefully.  这位小姐优雅地鞠了一躬。|近义词：Mr., sir, gentleman |反义词：madam, signora
