seagull:/ˈsiːɡʌl/| n. |海鸥|A seagull is a type of bird that lives near the sea and eats fish and other small animals.  They are known for their characteristic cry and often seen scavenging for food near beaches and harbors.|例句：A seagull swooped down and snatched a chip from the boy's hand.一只海鸥俯冲下来，从男孩手里抢走了一片薯片。
例句：I saw a flock of seagulls circling overhead.我看到一群海鸥在头顶盘旋。|近义词：gull|反义词:None
