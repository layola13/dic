nonloyally: /ˌnɒnˈlɔɪəli/ | adv. | 不忠实地，不忠诚地 | Acting in a manner that is not loyal or faithful.  It implies a lack of allegiance, trustworthiness, or devotion. |


例句：He acted nonloyally towards his friends, betraying their trust for personal gain. = 他对朋友不忠，为了个人利益背叛了他们的信任。

He handled the sensitive information nonloyally, leaking it to the press. = 他处理敏感信息不忠诚，将其泄露给媒体。


近义词：disloyally, treacherously, faithlessly, unfaithfully


反义词：loyally, faithfully, trustworthily


词性：副词
