info:/ˈɪnfoʊ/| n. |信息，情报|Information is data that has been processed, organized, structured or interpreted in a way that makes it meaningful and useful.  It provides context and meaning to raw data.|例句：The police need more info to solve the case. 警方需要更多信息来破案。|近义词：data, news, details, intelligence|反义词：ignorance, secrecy
