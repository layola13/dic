nonchronically:/ˌnɒnkrɒˈnɪkli/| adv.|非按时间顺序地，非按时间发生地 |Not in chronological order; not according to the order in which events occurred.  This adverb indicates a lack of temporal sequence or order. |例句：The memories came back to her nonchronically, a jumble of images and emotions from different points in her life.  她的记忆非按时间顺序地涌现，是她生命中不同阶段的图像和情感的混合。  |近义词：randomly, haphazardly, out of order |反义词：chronologically, sequentially, in order
