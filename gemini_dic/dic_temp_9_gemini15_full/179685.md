reenlist: /ˌriːˈɪnɪst/ | v. | 重新入伍；再次应征 | To enlist again in the armed forces after a period of service.  | Example: After a brief break, he decided to reenlist in the army.  之后短暂休息后，他决定重新入伍。| 近义词:rejoin, re-enrol | 反义词:discharge, retire

USAGE->

He reenlists every five years. = 他每五年重新入伍一次。
They reenlist to gain experience. = 他们为了获得经验而重新入伍。

He reenlisted in the navy last year. = 他去年重新加入了海军。
She reenlisted after her maternity leave. = 她休完产假后重新入伍。
