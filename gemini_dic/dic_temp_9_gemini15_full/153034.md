peeler:/ˈpiːlər/| n. v.|削皮器；剥皮者；剥皮工|A peeler is a tool used for peeling fruits or vegetables, or a person who peels fruits or vegetables.|例句：She used a potato peeler to peel the carrots. 她用土豆削皮器削胡萝卜。 He works as a peeler in a fruit processing factory. 他在一个水果加工厂当剥皮工。|近义词：parer, peeler (tool), skin remover |反义词：planter(反义词较为宽泛，指种植者，与剥皮动作相反)|名词，动词
