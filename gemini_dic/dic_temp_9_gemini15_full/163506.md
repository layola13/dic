preconvinced:/ˌpriːkənˈvɪnst/| adj.| 预先确信的；已预先说服的 | Already persuaded or convinced beforehand; having a pre-existing belief or opinion.  |例句：He was preconvinced of his own superiority. 他预先确信自己优越于人。

例句：She went into the meeting preconvinced that her ideas would be rejected. 她参加会议时，预先确信自己的想法会被否决。


近义词：prejudiced, biased, predisposed, determined


反义词：unconvinced, undecided, open-minded


词性：形容词

过去完成时:
