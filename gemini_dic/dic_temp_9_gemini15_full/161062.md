postcornu: /ˌpoʊstˈkɔːrnjuː/ | n. | 后丰饶时代 |  A period of time after a period of great abundance or prosperity, characterized by scarcity, decline, or disillusionment.  It refers to a societal state following a perceived era of plenty, often marked by resource depletion, environmental degradation, or social unrest.  The term is a neologism, playing on the word "cornucopia," which symbolizes abundance. | Example: The postcornucopia era saw a sharp decline in readily available resources.  后丰饶时代见证了 readily available 资源的急剧下降。 | 近义词: post-abundance, decline, aftermath | 反义词: cornucopia, abundance, prosperity | 名词
