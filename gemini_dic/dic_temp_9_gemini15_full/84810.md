handwriting: /ˈhændˌraɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 书法，笔迹 | The art or manner of writing by hand.  It refers to the style and quality of a person's writing, often revealing aspects of their personality. | 例句：His handwriting is so neat and legible. = 他的书法很工整易读。  The detective analyzed the handwriting on the ransom note. = 侦探分析了勒索信上的笔迹。 | 近义词：penmanship, script | 反义词：typing, printing


USAGE->

Her handwriting is elegant. = 她的书法很优雅。
His handwriting is difficult to read. = 他的字很难辨认。
