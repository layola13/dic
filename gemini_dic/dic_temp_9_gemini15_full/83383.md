/ɡaɪt/| n. |旋转；回旋；（鸟的）飞行路线|A gyte is a circular or spiral motion, often referring to the flight path of a bird of prey.  It can also refer to a whirlwind or eddy.|例句：The hawk performed a graceful gyte above the field.  这只鹰在田地上空优雅地盘旋。|近义词：spiral, circle, whirl, eddy|反义词：straight line, direct path|名词
