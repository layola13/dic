firms:/fɜːrmz/ | n. |公司，商行；企业；事务所 | A business organization that sells goods or services to make money.  It can range from a small, local shop to a large multinational corporation. | Example: Many firms are struggling in the current economic climate. 许多公司都在当前的经济环境中苦苦挣扎。  |近义词：companies, corporations, businesses |反义词：individuals, sole proprietorships |名词
