electuary:/ɪˈlɛktʃuəri/| n. |药膏；膏药|A electuary is a medicinal preparation in the form of a thick paste or confection, typically containing a variety of ingredients and intended to be swallowed whole.  It was a common form of medication in the past. |The apothecary prepared a potent electuary to treat the king's cough.|近义词：potion, paste, confection|反义词：injection, pill (depending on context)
