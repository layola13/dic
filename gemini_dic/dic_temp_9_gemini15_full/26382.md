by nature: /baɪ ˈneɪtʃər/ | adj. | 天生的；本质上；自然地 | inherently; essentially; naturally |

英文解析:The phrase "by nature" means something is inherent or intrinsic to something's character or essence.  It describes a characteristic that is innate, not learned or acquired.


例句:

He is by nature a shy person. 他天性害羞。

The task is by nature very difficult. 这项任务本质上非常困难。


近义词: inherently, intrinsically, essentially, naturally, innately

反义词: artificially, acquired, learned, cultivated


词性:形容词 (adjectival phrase)
