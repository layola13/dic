internucleotide: /ˌɪntərˈnjuːkliəˌoʊd/ | n. | 核苷酸间的；核苷酸间连接 | Relating to the linkage or connection between nucleotides in a nucleic acid molecule.  The term often refers to the phosphodiester bond that connects the 3' carbon of one nucleotide to the 5' carbon of the next. | Example:  The internucleotide bond is crucial for maintaining the integrity of the DNA double helix. 核苷酸间的键对于维持DNA双螺旋的完整性至关重要。 | 近义词: inter-nucleotidic | 反义词:  intranucleotide (within a nucleotide) | 名词
