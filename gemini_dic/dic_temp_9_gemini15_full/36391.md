coinventor: /ˌkoʊɪnˈvɛntər/ | n. |共同发明者，合作发明者 | A person who invents something with one or more other people.  | Example:  He is the coinventor of a new type of engine. 他是新型发动机的共同发明者之一。 |近义词：collaborator, co-creator |反义词：sole inventor

词源:
The word "coinventor" is a compound word formed from "co-" (meaning "together" or "jointly") and "inventor" (a person who invents something).  The prefix "co-" is derived from Latin.
