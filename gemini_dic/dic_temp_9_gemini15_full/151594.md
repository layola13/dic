parthenope:/pɑːrˈθɛnoʊpiː/| n. |帕耳塞诺珀（那不勒斯古名）,一种海妖|Parthenope is a name from Greek mythology, referring to one of the Sirens.  In a broader sense, it also refers to the ancient Greek name for Naples, Italy.
