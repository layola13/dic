cheongsam: /ˈtʃɒŋˌsæm/ /ˈtʃoʊŋˌsæm/ | n. | 旗袍 | A traditional one-piece Chinese dress for women, typically made of silk or satin and having a high collar, slits on the sides, and often some form of embroidery or other decoration.  It is known for its form-fitting style and elegant appearance. | She wore a beautiful red cheongsam to the wedding. 她穿着美丽的红色旗袍参加婚礼。| qipao (a shorter, more modern variation), gown, dress |  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with Western-style dresses) | 名词
