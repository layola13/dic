cryptonym: /ˈkrɪptənɪm/ | n. |密码名称，暗号| A cryptonym is a code name or a word or phrase used as a code to represent a person, place, thing, or operation, especially in military or intelligence contexts.  It's often used to maintain secrecy or avoid revealing sensitive information. | Example:  The operation's cryptonym was "Project Nightingale."  行动的代号是“夜莺计划”。 | 近义词：codename, code word, designation | 反义词：plaintext name, cleartext name
