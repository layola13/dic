hydrophane:/ˈhaɪdrəfeɪn/| n. |水石，吸水石 |A hydrophane is a variety of opal that absorbs water and becomes translucent or transparent when immersed in it, and opaque when dry.  It's characterized by its ability to absorb a significant amount of water, changing its appearance dramatically. |Example: The hydrophane, once dry and opaque, became beautifully translucent when submerged in water.  这块水石，干燥时是不透明的，浸入水中后变得美丽地半透明。|近义词：opal (in its water-absorbing form) |反义词：N/A |名词
