customers: /ˈkʌstəmərz/ | n. |顾客，客户 | People who buy goods or services from a business.  |例句：The shop has many loyal customers. 这家商店有很多忠实的顾客。|例句：We need to improve our customer service. 我们需要改进我们的客户服务。|近义词：clients, patrons, buyers |反义词：suppliers, vendors


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Our company attracts many new customers every month. = 我们公司每个月都吸引许多新客户。
The customers are always satisfied with our products. = 顾客们总是对我们的产品感到满意。

一般过去时:
We lost some customers last year due to the economic downturn. = 由于经济低迷，我们去年失去了一些客户。
Many customers complained about the poor service. = 许多顾客抱怨服务差。

现在完成时:
We have gained many new customers since launching our new marketing campaign. = 自从启动新的营销活动以来，我们已经获得了许多新客户。
The company has served thousands of customers over the years. = 多年来，公司已经服务了成千上万的客户。

过去完成时:
The company had already lost many customers before the new CEO took over. = 在新任首席执行官上任之前，公司已经失去了许多客户。
By the end of the year, we had satisfied all our customers' demands. = 到年底，我们已经满足了所有客户的需求。

将来时:
We expect to attract more customers next quarter. = 我们预计下个季度将吸引更多客户。
The company will provide better services to its customers in the future. = 公司将来会为客户提供更好的服务。

虚拟语气:
If we had better customer service, we would have more customers. = 如果我们的客户服务更好，我们将会有更多客户。
I wish we could satisfy all of our customers all the time. = 我希望我们能够一直满足所有客户的需求。

被动语态:
Our customers are well-served by our dedicated staff. = 我们的客户得到我们敬业的员工的周到服务。
Many complaints were received from customers about the product quality. = 收到许多顾客关于产品质量的投诉。

疑问句:
Do we have enough customers to meet our sales target? = 我们有足够的客户来实现我们的销售目标吗？
What do our customers think about our new product? = 我们的客户对我们的新产品有什么看法？

否定句:
We don't have enough customers at the moment. = 我们目前没有足够的客户。
The company did not retain all of its customers. = 公司没有留住所有客户。
