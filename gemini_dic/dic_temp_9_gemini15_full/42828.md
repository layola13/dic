couriers:/ˈkʊriərz/| n.|快递员；信使；快递公司|People or companies that deliver packages or messages.|The couriers delivered the package on time.  快递员准时送达了包裹。|We used a courier service to send the documents. 我们用快递服务发送了文件。|messengers, delivery services| (反义词较少，可考虑slow delivery services 慢速递送服务)|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The courier delivers packages every day. = 快递员每天送包裹。
Couriers often use motorcycles for fast delivery. = 快递员经常使用摩托车进行快速送货。


一般过去时:
