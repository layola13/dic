ideas:/aɪˈdiːəz/ | n. |主意，想法，观念 |Thoughts or suggestions used as a basis for action or decision making.  They can be innovative, creative, or simply ways of thinking about something. |例句：He had some interesting ideas about the project. 他对这个项目有一些有趣的想法。 |例句：Her ideas were quickly adopted by the team. 她的想法很快就被团队采纳了。|近义词：thoughts, concepts, notions, plans |反义词：reality, fact


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His ideas inspire many young people. = 他的想法激励了许多年轻人。
What are your ideas for the party? = 你对派对有什么想法？


一般过去时:
Her ideas were revolutionary for their time. = 她的想法在当时是革命性的。
We discussed his ideas in detail. = 我们详细讨论了他的想法。


现在完成时:
Have you had any new ideas recently? = 你最近有什么新想法吗？
They have implemented many of his ideas. = 他们已经实施了他的许多想法。


过去完成时:
I had already formed my ideas before the meeting. = 我在会议之前就已经形成了我的想法。
They had discussed their ideas thoroughly before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前已经彻底讨论了他们的想法。


将来时:
What ideas will you present at the conference? = 你将在会议上提出什么想法？
We will explore new ideas in the upcoming year. = 我们将在来年探索新的想法。


虚拟语气:
If I had better ideas, I would have succeeded. = 如果我有更好的想法，我就会成功了。
I wish I had more ideas for the presentation. = 我希望我对演示有更多想法。


被动语态:

New ideas are constantly being developed in this field. = 在这个领域里，不断发展新的想法。


疑问句:

否定句:
