defamation:/ˌdɛfəˈmeɪʃən/ | n. |诽谤；名誉损害 | The act of damaging someone's reputation by making false statements about them.  This can be done through spoken words (slander) or written words (libel).  Defamation requires proof that the statement was false, published to a third party, and caused harm to the reputation of the person defamed. | Example:  He sued the newspaper for defamation after they published a false article about him. 他以该报发表关于他的虚假文章为由起诉该报诽谤。


Example: The politician was accused of defamation for spreading false rumors about his opponent.  这位政治家被指控诽谤，因为他散布了关于其对手的谣言。

近义词：slander, libel, calumny, vilification

反义词：praise, commendation, extolment
