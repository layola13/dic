quasi-dumb:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ dʌm/| adj.|近乎愚笨的，似愚笨的|  Describes someone or something that appears or acts somewhat foolish or unintelligent, but not completely or entirely so. It suggests a partial or superficial lack of intelligence.|例句：His quasi-dumb remarks at the meeting embarrassed everyone. 他在会议上说的那些近乎愚笨的话让每个人都很尴尬。
例句：The quasi-dumb plot of the movie made it difficult to follow. 这部电影近乎愚笨的情节使得难以理解。|近义词：semi-stupid, somewhat foolish, obtuse|反义词：intelligent, brilliant, shrewd
