retconed:/ˈrɛtkɑːnd/| v.|逆向连续性设定，改写既定设定 | To retrospectively alter or contradict established facts or events in a narrative, typically in a work of fiction or a shared fictional universe.  This often involves changing elements of an established story to fit a new narrative or to correct inconsistencies. | Example: The writers retconned the character's backstory to make it consistent with the new plotline.  编剧们逆向改写了角色的背景故事，使其与新的情节线相符。|近义词：rewrite, revise, amend, alter|反义词：canonize, establish
