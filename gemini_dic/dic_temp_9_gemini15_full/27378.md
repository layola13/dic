came-forward:/keɪmˈfɔːrwərd/| v.|挺身而出；主动提出；自告奋勇|To come forward means to volunteer one's services or information, especially when it involves risk or requires courage.  It implies a proactive and often selfless action.|例句：Several witnesses came forward with information about the crime.  几个证人主动提供了有关这起犯罪的信息。|例句：No one came forward to claim the lost dog. 没有人主动认领这只走失的狗。|近义词：volunteer, offer oneself, step forward, present oneself|反义词：remain silent, keep quiet, stay hidden, withhold information|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He comes forward with new ideas every week. = 他每周都提出新的想法。
People come forward to help the victims of the disaster. = 人们挺身而出帮助灾难的受害者。


一般过去时:
She came forward and testified against her former boss. = 她挺身而出，指证她以前的老板。
Many people came forward after the accident to offer assistance. = 事故发生后，许多人主动提供帮助。


现在完成时:
Several witnesses have come forward with crucial evidence. = 几个证人已经提供了关键证据。
No one has come forward to take responsibility for the mistake. = 没有人站出来为这个错误负责。


过去完成时:
Before the trial, no one had come forward to support the defendant. = 在审判之前，没有人站出来支持被告。
By the deadline, only a few people had come forward to donate. = 到截止日期，只有少数人站出来捐款。


将来时:
I hope someone will come forward with a solution to this problem. = 我希望有人能提出解决这个问题的方案。
We believe that more people will come forward with information as time goes on. = 我们相信随着时间的推移，会有更多的人提供信息。


虚拟语气:
If only someone would come forward with the truth. = 但愿有人能说出真相。
I wish more people would come forward to help the homeless. = 我希望更多的人能站出来帮助无家可归的人。


被动语态:
The truth was eventually brought to light when more witnesses came forward. = 当更多证人站出来时，真相最终大白。
The missing child was finally found after someone came forward with information. = 在有人提供信息后，失踪的孩子最终被找到了。


疑问句:
Will anyone come forward and admit to breaking the window? = 会有人站出来承认打破窗户吗？
Has anyone come forward to claim the reward? = 有人站出来领取奖励了吗？


否定句:
No one came forward to defend him. = 没有人站出来为他辩护。
Despite our best efforts, no new witnesses came forward. = 尽管我们尽了最大努力，但没有新的证人站出来。
