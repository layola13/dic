conclusive:/kənˈkluːsɪv/| adj.|决定性的；结论性的；最终的|Providing a final answer or solution; leaving no doubt.  It settles a matter decisively.|The evidence is conclusive; the defendant is guilty.  证据是确凿的；被告有罪。|The experiment yielded conclusive results. 实验产生了决定性的结果。|definitive, decisive, irrefutable|inconclusive, uncertain, ambiguous|形容词
