headhunting: /ˈhedˌhʌntɪŋ/ | n. |猎头，人才寻访 |The practice of actively seeking out and recruiting high-level employees from other companies.  |例句：The company engaged in aggressive headhunting to fill the vacant CEO position.  这家公司积极进行猎头活动以填补空缺的CEO职位。

例句：The headhunting firm successfully placed the candidate in a senior management role. 这家猎头公司成功地将候选人安排在了高级管理职位。


近义词：executive search, talent acquisition, recruiting


反义词：N/A (no direct opposite, but the opposite action would be passively waiting for applicants)


词性：名词
