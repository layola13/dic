mellowed:/ˈmɛloʊd/| adj.|成熟的；醇厚的；温和的；平静的|Having become softer, smoother, or more gentle; having matured or ripened.|例句：The wine has mellowed with age. 这葡萄酒随着时间的推移变得醇厚了。  例句：His personality has mellowed considerably since he got married. 他结婚后性格温和了许多。|近义词：softened, matured, ripened|反义词：harsh, sharp, immature


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wine mellows with age. = 酒随着时间推移变得醇厚。
His personality mellows with experience. = 他的个性随着经验变得温和。

一般过去时:
The fruit mellowed in the sun. = 水果在阳光下成熟了。
His anger mellowed after a long talk. = 长谈之后他的怒气消了。

现在完成时:
The cheese has mellowed beautifully. = 奶酪已经变得非常醇厚。
His views have mellowed over the years. = 他的观点这些年来已经变得温和了。

过去完成时:
The whiskey had mellowed significantly before it was bottled. = 威士忌在装瓶前已经变得相当醇厚了。
His initially harsh criticism had mellowed by the end of the meeting. = 他最初尖锐的批评在会议结束时已经缓和了。

将来时:
The wine will mellow nicely over the next few years. = 这葡萄酒在接下来的几年里会变得很好喝。
I think he will mellow as he gets older. = 我认为他会随着年龄增长而变得温和。

虚拟语气:
If the fruit had mellowed more, it would have tasted better. = 如果水果更成熟一些，味道会更好。
I wish my temper would mellow with age. = 我希望我的脾气能随着年龄增长而变得温和。

被动语态:
The fruit was mellowed by the warm sun. = 水果被温暖的阳光晒熟了。
His opinions were mellowed by time and experience. = 他的观点随着时间和经验而变得温和。

疑问句:
Has the cheese mellowed enough? = 奶酪够醇厚了吗？
Does the music sound mellowed to you? = 你觉得这音乐听起来很柔和吗？

否定句:
The wine hadn't mellowed enough yet. = 这葡萄酒还没充分醇厚。
His demeanor didn't mellow despite his age. = 尽管上了年纪，他的举止依然不温和。
