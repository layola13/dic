enquiring: /ɪnˈkwaɪərɪŋ/ | adj. |  探询的；好问的；好奇的 |  Showing an eagerness to learn or know more.  Characterized by inquiry or investigation. |

例句：

He had an enquiring mind, always asking questions. 他有一颗求知的心，总是问问题。

Her enquiring gaze took in every detail of the room. 她好奇的目光注视着房间里的每一个细节。


近义词：inquisitive, curious, questioning, investigative

反义词：uninterested, apathetic, indifferent


词源:

源自动词enquire (询问,打听),  加上-ing构成形容词。 enquire本身源自古法语 enquerre,  最终源自拉丁语 inquirere (to seek, search)。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His enquiring nature leads him to ask many questions.  他的好问天性让他问了很多问题。
She is always enquiring about new ideas. 她总是打听新的想法。


一般过去时:
He was enquiring about the price of the car. 他正在询问汽车的价格。
They were enquiring about the availability of rooms. 他们正在询问房间的空房情况。


现在完成时:
I have been enquiring about the lost package. 我一直在询问丢失的包裹。
She has been enquiring into the matter for weeks. 她已经调查此事好几个星期了。


过去完成时:
He had been enquiring about the job for months before he applied. 在申请工作之前，他已打听那份工作好几个月了。
They had already enquired about the possibility of a refund. 他们之前已经询问过退款的可能性。


将来时:
I will be enquiring about the flight schedule tomorrow. 我明天将询问航班时刻表。
They will be enquiring about the best route to take. 他们将询问最佳路线。


虚拟语气:
If I were enquiring about the situation, I would ask more direct questions. 如果我正在询问情况，我会问更直接的问题。
I wish I had enquired about the deadline sooner. 我希望我早点询问截止日期。


被动语态:
The matter is being enquired into by the police.  此事正在由警方调查。
The new policy was being enquired about by many employees. 许多员工都在询问新的政策。


疑问句:
Are you enquiring about something specific? 你在询问什么具体的事情吗？
Is he enquiring about the job opening? 他是在询问职位空缺吗？


否定句:
He wasn't enquiring about the details. 他没有询问细节。
She is not enquiring about the price. 她没有询问价格。


PHRASE->
