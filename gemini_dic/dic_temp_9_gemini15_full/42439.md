counterdemonstrated: /ˌkaʊntəˈdɛmənstreɪtɪd/ | v. | 反驳；反证；以示威活动反驳 | To demonstrate against (something previously demonstrated); to show the opposite of (a previous demonstration).  | 例句：The scientists counterdemonstrated the initial findings with new data. 科学家们用新的数据反驳了最初的发现。

The protesters counterdemonstrated the government's policies with a march.  抗议者们通过游行来反驳政府的政策。| 近义词：refute, contradict, disprove | 反义词：demonstrate, prove, support |  动词
