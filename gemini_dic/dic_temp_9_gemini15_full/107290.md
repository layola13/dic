lama:/ˈlɑːmə/| n.|喇嘛，(藏传佛教的)僧侣|A lama is a Buddhist monk, especially of the Tibetan tradition.|例句：The lama gave a blessing to the villagers. 喇嘛为村民们祈福。|近义词：monk, priest|反义词：layperson
