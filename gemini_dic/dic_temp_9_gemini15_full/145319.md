overbroadening: /ˌoʊvərˈbrɔːdnɪŋ/ | adj. | 过于宽泛；过度概括 |

英文解析:The act of making something too broad or general, often leading to inaccuracies or misinterpretations.  It implies an excessive generalization that doesn't account for nuances or exceptions.

例句:

The law's overbroadening definition of "terrorism" led to the unjust prosecution of several peaceful activists.  法律对“恐怖主义”的定义过于宽泛，导致几名和平活动家遭到不公正起诉。


The study's overbroadening conclusions failed to consider the significant regional variations in the data.  该研究的结论过于宽泛，未能考虑数据中显著的地区差异。


近义词:overgeneralization, sweeping generalization, excessive generalization, too inclusive


反义词:precise, specific, nuanced, accurate


词性:形容词
