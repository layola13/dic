poohed:/pʊd/| vt.|轻蔑地拒绝；不屑一顾；对…嗤之以鼻|To express contempt or disbelief for something; to dismiss something as unimportant or unworthy of consideration.|例句：He poohed my suggestion. 他对我的建议嗤之以鼻。

例句：She poohed the idea of going to the party. 她对去参加派对的想法不屑一顾。


近义词：dismiss, scorn, scoff, sneer, reject


反义词：accept, approve, endorse, embrace


词性:动词
