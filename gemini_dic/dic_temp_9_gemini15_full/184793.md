resurrect:/ˈrezərekt/| vt.|使复活；使复兴；重新启用|To bring something back to life or to restore something to its former state or condition.  It can refer to literal resurrection (bringing the dead back to life) or figurative resurrection (reviving something that was lost, forgotten, or inactive).|例句：The magician claimed to be able to resurrect the dead.  魔术师声称能够使死人复活。

例句：The company was resurrected after years of financial difficulty. 这家公司在经历了多年的财务困境后又重新焕发了生机。|近义词：revive, revitalize, restore|反义词：kill, destroy, extinguish|动词
