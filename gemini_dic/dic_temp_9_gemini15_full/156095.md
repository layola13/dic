photoresponsive: /ˌfoʊtoʊrɪˈspɑːnsɪv/ | adj. | 感光性的，对光有反应的 |  Sensitive to light; responding to light.  The material exhibits photoresponsive properties, changing color when exposed to ultraviolet light.  | 近义词: light-sensitive, photosensitive | 反义词: light-insensitive


例句:

The photoresponsive polymer changed color when exposed to sunlight.
感光性聚合物在阳光照射下改变了颜色。


This photoresponsive dye is used in optical data storage.
这种感光染料用于光学数据存储。


词源:

photo-  (from Greek phōs, phot- "light") + responsive (from respond + -ive)
