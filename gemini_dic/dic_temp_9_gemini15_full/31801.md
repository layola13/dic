checkmated:/ˈtʃɛkmeɪtɪd/ | adj. v. | 将军；被将死 |  Describes a situation in chess where a player's king is under attack (in "check") and there is no legal move to remove it from attack.  It signifies defeat in the game.  Figuratively, it can also describe a situation where someone is completely defeated or trapped. | Example: The pawn advanced, checkmating the black king. 黑卒前进，将黑王将死。The company was checkmated by the sudden economic downturn. 公司被突如其来的经济衰退打垮了。 | 近义词：defeated, trapped, overwhelmed | 反义词：victorious, winning, free
