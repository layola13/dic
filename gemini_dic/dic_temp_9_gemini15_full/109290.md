lemurid:/ˈlɛmjʊrɪd/| n.|狐猴科动物|Lemurid refers to any member of the Lemuroidea superfamily, a group of primates found only on the island of Madagascar.  They are characterized by their relatively large eyes, often nocturnal habits, and a range of specialized adaptations depending on their specific species.  |例句：The lemurid population is threatened by habitat loss.  狐猴种群受到栖息地丧失的威胁。|近义词：lemur (more common term)|反义词：无
