release's: /rɪˈliːsɪz/ | n. | 释放；发行；发布；解脱 |  The possessive form of the verb "release," indicating something belonging to or associated with a release.  It describes the action or result of releasing something. | 例句：The film's release's date is highly anticipated.  这部电影的发行日期备受期待。  |近义词： liberation's, publication's, unveiling's | 反义词：retention's, confinement's | 名词
