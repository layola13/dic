duskish:/ˈdʌskɪʃ/| adj.| 微暗的，昏暗的；略带黄褐色的 | Slightly dark; somewhat dusky; of a dusky color.  The word suggests a dimness or subdued light, often associated with twilight or shadows.  It can also refer to a color that is dark and somewhat brownish or yellowish. | 例句：The duskish light of the setting sun cast long shadows across the field.  夕阳的微暗光线在田野上投下长长的阴影。

The sky was duskish, hinting at the approaching storm. 天空有些昏暗，预示着暴风雨即将来临。


近义词：darkish, shadowy, dim, dusky, twilight, brownish


反义词：bright, light, luminous, sunny


词性:形容词
