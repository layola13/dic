coproduces:/ˌkoʊprəˈduːsɪz/ | v. | 联合制作；共同制作 | To jointly produce something, such as a film, television show, or other creative work.  It implies a shared responsibility and contribution from multiple parties. | Example:  The two studios coproduced the award-winning documentary.  两个制片厂联合制作了这部获奖纪录片。


The independent filmmaker coproduced his latest short film with a local arts organization.  一位独立电影制作人与当地一个艺术组织联合制作了他的最新短片。|近义词：collaborate on, jointly produce, create together |反义词：solely produce, individually create |动词
