mislike:/mɪsˈlaɪk/| vt.|不喜欢，厌恶|To feel dislike or distaste for; to disapprove of.  It expresses a feeling of aversion or disapproval towards something or someone.|I dislike spinach.  我不喜欢菠菜。|He dislikes loud music. 他不喜欢吵闹的音乐。|like, disfavor|love, adore, appreciate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I dislike his attitude. = 我不喜欢他的态度。
She dislikes spicy food. = 她不喜欢辛辣的食物。


一般过去时:
He disliked the movie. = 他不喜欢那部电影。
They disliked the weather. = 他们不喜欢那个天气。


现在完成时:
I have disliked him since we met. = 从我们见面起我就一直不喜欢他。
She has disliked her job for years. = 她多年来一直不喜欢她的工作。


过去完成时:
I had disliked him before I even met him. = 我甚至在他之前就已经不喜欢他了。
She had disliked the plan before it was presented. = 在计划被提出之前她就已不喜欢它了。


将来时:
I will dislike this even more tomorrow. = 明天我将更不喜欢这个。
She will dislike the results if they are not positive. = 如果结果不积极，她会不喜欢。


虚拟语气:
I would dislike it if you did that. = 如果你那样做，我会不喜欢。
She wished she didn't dislike her boss so much. = 她希望她不那么讨厌她的老板。


被动语态:
His behavior is disliked by many. = 他的行为被许多人所厌恶。
The new policy is disliked by the staff. = 新政策不受员工欢迎。


疑问句:
Do you dislike this dress? = 你不喜欢这件衣服吗？
Does he dislike his new roommate? = 他不喜欢他的新室友吗？


否定句:
I don't dislike him, I just don't understand him. = 我不喜欢他，我只是不理解他。
She doesn't dislike cats, she's just allergic. = 她不喜欢猫，她只是过敏。


PHRASE->
dislike doing sth. = 不喜欢做某事
dislike sb./sth. intensely = 强烈地不喜欢某人/某事
have a dislike for = 对…反感
take a dislike to = 开始讨厌
show a dislike of = 表现出对…的反感
