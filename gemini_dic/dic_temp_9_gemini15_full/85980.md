headship:/ˈhɛdʃɪp/| n. |领导地位；领导权；首长职位；主管职位|The position or authority of a head or leader.  It refers to the role, responsibility, and power associated with being in charge of something or someone. |例句：The headship of the department was offered to Professor Smith.  部门主管职位提供给了史密斯教授。|例句：She gained headship of the firm after many years of hard work.  她经过多年的努力获得了公司的领导权。|近义词：leadership, supervision, directorship, management |反义词：subordination, followership
