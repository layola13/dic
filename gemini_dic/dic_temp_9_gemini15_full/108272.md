laundering: /ˈlɔːndrɪŋ/ | n. | 洗涤；洗钱 | The process of washing clothes or other fabrics.  In a criminal context, it refers to concealing the origins of illegally obtained money. | 例句：The laundry was piling up. = 洗衣堆积如山了。  He was arrested for money laundering. = 他因洗钱被捕。 | 近义词：cleaning, washing (for washing clothes);  money-laundering | 反义词：soiling (for opposite of washing)
