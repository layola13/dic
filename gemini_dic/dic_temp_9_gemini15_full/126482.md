mythoplasm:/ˈmɪθəʊˌplæzəm/| n. |神话母体；神话的本质；神话构成要素|The inherent, underlying structure and substance of myths, encompassing their recurring themes, archetypes, and narrative patterns.  It refers to the collective unconscious and shared symbolic imagery that shapes mythological narratives across cultures and time periods.  It is the study of the fundamental building blocks of myths.| Example:  The analysis explored the mythoplasm of creation myths, identifying common motifs like a primordial chaos and a divine creator.  = 这篇分析探讨了创世神话的神话母体，发现了诸如原始混沌和神圣创造者等共同的主题。|近义词:mythology, archetypes, narrative structures|反义词:reality, fact
