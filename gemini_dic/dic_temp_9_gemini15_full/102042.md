isolations:/ˌaɪsəˈleɪʃənz/ | n. | 隔离；孤立；孤立状态 | The state of being alone or separated from others.  It can refer to physical separation, social isolation, or emotional isolation.  |例句：The city went into strict isolations during the pandemic.  疫情期间，这座城市实行严格的隔离。

例句：Her isolations from her friends led to depression. 她与朋友们的隔绝导致了抑郁。


近义词：seclusion, quarantine, separation, loneliness

反义词：integration, togetherness, connection, company


词性: 名词
