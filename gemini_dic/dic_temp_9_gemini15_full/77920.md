词源:
The word "genito-urinary" is a compound word formed from "genito-" (from Latin *genit- "producing," related to *gignere "to beget, bear") and "-urinary" (from Latin *urinārius "relating to urine").  Therefore, it literally means "relating to the organs of generation and urine."
