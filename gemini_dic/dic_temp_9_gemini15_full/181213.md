reinoculations:/ˌriːɪnˌɒkjʊˈleɪʃənz/ | n. |再次接种；再次接种疫苗|The act of inoculating again;  repeated vaccinations or inoculations.|例句：Reinoculations are recommended every ten years for tetanus.  再次接种破伤风疫苗建议每十年一次。


例句：The doctor ordered reinoculations for the entire family after the measles outbreak. 麻疹爆发后，医生下令对全家进行再次接种疫苗。


近义词：revaccinations, booster shots


反义词：deimmunization (not a direct antonym, but represents the opposite effect)


词性：名词
