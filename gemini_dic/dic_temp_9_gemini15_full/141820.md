orbitonasal: /ˌɔːrbɪtoʊˈneɪzəl/ | adj. | 眶鼻的 | Relating to the orbit (eye socket) and the nose.  This term describes anatomical structures or processes involving both the orbit and the nasal region. For example, an orbitonasal fracture involves bones of both the eye socket and the nose. | The patient presented with an orbitonasal fracture following a car accident. 患者在车祸后出现了眶鼻骨折。 | 近义词: periorbital (if focusing more on the eye socket),  nasoorbital (less common)| 反义词:  None specifically, but terms referring to separate areas like "ocular" or "nasal" could be considered contrasting.
