interstreaks:/ˌɪntərˈstriːks/| n. |条纹间的空间；（尤指云层间的）缝隙|  A space or gap between streaks, especially between streaks of clouds.  The term is primarily used in descriptive contexts related to meteorology or similar fields. | Example:  The sun shone through the interstreaks in the storm clouds.  阳光透过暴风雨云层间的缝隙照射下来。|近义词: gaps, spaces, intervals|反义词: streaks, bands, lines|名词
