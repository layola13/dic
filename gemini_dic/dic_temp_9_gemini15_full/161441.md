pot's:/pɒts/| n. |陶罐的；盆栽的|The possessive form of "pot," indicating belonging or ownership.  It refers to something that belongs to a pot or is associated with a pot.  For example, "the pot's lid" means the lid belonging to a pot.|例句：The pot's lid was missing.  (锅盖不见了。)  The plant in the pot's soil looked wilted. (盆栽里的植物看起来枯萎了。)|近义词：belonging to a pot|反义词：N/A
