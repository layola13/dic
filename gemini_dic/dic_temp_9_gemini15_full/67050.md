词源:
The word "explicable" derives from the Latin word "explicare," meaning "to unfold, to explain, to unravel."  The prefix "ex-" means "out," and "plicare" means "to fold." Therefore, the original meaning suggests the act of unfolding or clarifying something previously complex or obscure.
