minuteman:/ˈmɪnjutmæn/| n. |民兵；迅速行动的人|A minuteman was a member of a colonial militia in the American Revolutionary War, ready to fight at a moment's notice.  The term also refers to someone who acts quickly and efficiently. |例句：The firefighters were true minutemen, responding to the alarm in record time. 消防员真是迅速行动的人，他们以创纪录的时间响应了警报。  The minutemen played a crucial role in the early battles of the American Revolution. 民兵在美国革命的早期战役中发挥了至关重要的作用。|近义词：volunteer, soldier, quick worker|反义词：dawdler, procrastinator
