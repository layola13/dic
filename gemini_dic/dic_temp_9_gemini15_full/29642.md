caters:/ˈkeɪtərz/| vt.|迎合；满足…的需要；提供饮食；|To provide food and drink, typically at a social event.|The restaurant caters to a wide range of tastes.|近义词：supplies, provides, accommodates|反义词：ignores, neglects


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company caters to the needs of its clients. = 公司满足客户的需求。
This restaurant caters to vegetarian diets. = 这家餐馆提供素食。

一般过去时:
The chef catered the wedding reception. = 厨师承办了婚礼招待会。
They catered a large party last night. = 他们昨晚承办了一个大型派对。

现在完成时:
She has catered many events over the years. = 多年来她承办过许多活动。
The company has catered to the needs of its customers for decades. = 这家公司几十年来一直满足客户的需求。

过去完成时:
They had catered the event before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经准备好了活动。
The caterers had already set up the buffet before the party started. = 派对开始之前，餐饮服务商已经摆好了自助餐。

将来时:
We will cater the conference next week. = 我们下周将承办这次会议。
They will cater the birthday party at the hotel. = 他们将在酒店承办生日派对。

虚拟语气:
If they catered the event, it would be a success. = 如果他们承办这个活动，它将会成功。
I wish they had catered to my dietary restrictions. = 我希望他们能考虑到我的饮食限制。

被动语态:
The event was catered by a professional catering company. = 这个活动由一家专业的餐饮公司承办。
The party was catered beautifully. = 派对的餐饮服务很棒。

疑问句:
Does this restaurant cater to special dietary needs? = 这家餐厅是否满足特殊的饮食需求？
Will you cater the party yourself, or hire a caterer? = 你自己承办派对，还是雇佣一个餐饮服务商？

否定句:
The restaurant doesn't cater to large groups. = 这家餐厅不接待大型团体。
They didn't cater the event themselves; they hired a professional. = 他们没有自己承办活动；他们雇佣了一个专业人士。
