discant:/ˈdɪskænt/| n. v.|高音部，高音；用高音歌唱|A descant is a high-pitched melody sung or played above a simpler melody.  It's often an independent, ornamented melody that adds a decorative or contrapuntal element to the main tune.  As a verb, it means to sing or play a descant.|例句：The soprano sang a beautiful descant above the hymn tune. 女高音在圣歌曲调之上唱了一段优美的花腔。

The choir added a lively descant to the traditional carol. 合唱团为传统的圣诞颂歌增添了活泼的高音部。|近义词：countermelody, flourish, embellishment|反义词：bass, ground bass
