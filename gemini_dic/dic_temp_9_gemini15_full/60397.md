eider:/ˈaɪdər/| n.| 海绒鸭；海鸭绒 |A large sea duck, valued for its down. Eiders are known for their ability to produce a very soft and warm down, which is used in high-quality bedding and clothing.|例句：The eider down is incredibly soft and warm.  = 海鸭绒非常柔软保暖。

例句：The eider duck nests on the rocky coast. = 海绒鸭在岩石海岸筑巢。

近义词：sea duck (泛指)

反义词：无


词源:
The word "eider" comes from Old Norse *æðr, meaning "sea duck".  This reflects the bird's habitat and the historical importance of its down. The word has passed directly into English through the centuries with minimal change in spelling or pronunciation.
