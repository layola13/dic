overwhelmer:/ˌoʊvərˈwɛlmər/| n. |压倒一切的人或事；使人难以承受的事物 |Something or someone that overwhelms; something that is too much to handle.  |例句：The sheer volume of work was an overwhelmer.  工作量之大令人难以招架。 |近义词：burden, overload, crush |反义词：relief, solace


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The workload overwhelms him. = 工作量压垮了他。
The noise overwhelms me. = 噪音让我难以忍受。


一般过去时:
The news overwhelmed her. = 这个消息让她难以承受。
The storm overwhelmed the small village. = 暴风雨袭击了这个小村庄。


现在完成时:
The project has overwhelmed us. = 这个项目让我们不堪重负。
The beauty of the scenery has overwhelmed her. = 风景的美丽让她惊叹不已。


过去完成时:
She had been overwhelmed by grief after her husband's death. = 她丈夫死后，她一直沉浸在悲痛之中。
The army had been overwhelmed by the enemy's forces. = 军队被敌军击败了。


将来时:
The demands of the job will overwhelm her. = 这份工作的要求会让她难以承受。
The flood will overwhelm the town. = 洪水将淹没小镇。


虚拟语气:
If I were to take on that project, I would be overwhelmed. = 如果我接手那个项目，我会不堪重负。
I wish I hadn't been overwhelmed by the pressure. = 我真希望我没有被压力压垮。


被动语态:
He was overwhelmed by the kindness of strangers. = 他被陌生人的好意所感动。
The city was overwhelmed by the flood. = 城市被洪水淹没了。


疑问句:
Is she overwhelmed by her responsibilities? = 她是否被她的责任压垮了？
Are you feeling overwhelmed by the amount of work? = 你是否感觉工作量过大？


否定句:
He wasn't overwhelmed by the challenge. = 他并没有被挑战压垮。
She wasn't overwhelmed by the amount of homework. = 她并没有被大量的作业压垮。

PHRASE->
be overwhelmed by/with: 被……压垮/充满
feel overwhelmed: 感到难以招架
become overwhelmed: 变得难以承受
an overwhelming feeling: 一种难以承受的感觉
an overwhelming success: 巨大的成功
