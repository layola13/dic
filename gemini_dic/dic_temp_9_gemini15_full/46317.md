dabbers:/ˈdæbərz/| n.|涂抹者；轻拍者；（印章）印油盒|A person or thing that dabs.  A dabber is often used in activities that require applying small amounts of a substance, such as paint, ink, or medicine, with a light touch.  It can also refer to a container for holding ink for stamping.|例句：The artist used a dabber to apply the watercolor paints.  这位艺术家用涂抹器来涂水彩颜料。


The children used dabbers to create colorful fingerprints on the paper. 孩子们用印油盒在纸上创作彩色指纹。|近义词：applicator, stamper|反义词：N/A
