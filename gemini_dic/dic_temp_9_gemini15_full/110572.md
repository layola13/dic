limitate:/ˈlɪmɪteɪt/ | v. | 限制，限定 | To restrict or confine within limits; to set limits to.  | The government will limitate the number of immigrants allowed into the country this year. 政府将限制今年允许进入该国的移民人数。 | restrict, confine, restrain | expand, enlarge | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They limitate the speed on this road. = 他们限制这条路上的速度。
The rules limitate our choices. = 这些规则限制了我们的选择。


一般过去时:
The teacher limitated the discussion to one hour. = 老师将讨论限制在一个小时内。
The company limitated its losses by quick action. = 公司通过迅速行动限制了损失。


现在完成时:
They have limitated the damage caused by the storm. = 他们已经限制了风暴造成的损失。
We have limitated our spending this month. = 我们这个月限制了开支。


过去完成时:
They had limitated the access to the building before the incident. = 他们在事件发生前已经限制了进入大楼的通道。
She had limitated her contact with the outside world. = 她已经限制了她与外界的联系。


将来时:
The new law will limitate the power of the corporations. = 新法将限制公司的权力。
They will limitate the use of pesticides in agriculture. = 他们将限制在农业中使用杀虫剂。


虚拟语气:
If they limitated the budget, we could save money. = 如果他们限制预算，我们可以省钱。
I wish they had limitated the noise pollution. = 我希望他们能限制噪音污染。


被动语态:
The number of participants was limitated to fifty. = 参加人数被限制在五十人。
Access to the information was limitated by security protocols. = 通过安全协议限制了对信息的访问。


疑问句:
Will the project be limitated by the budget? = 项目会受预算限制吗？
How will they limitate the spread of the disease? = 他们将如何限制疾病的传播？


否定句:
The company did not limitate its advertising budget. = 公司没有限制其广告预算。
They didn't limitate the amount of time spent on the project. = 他们没有限制在项目上花费的时间。
