petrologists:/ˌpɛtrəˈlɑːdʒɪsts/| n. |岩石学家|Scientists who study rocks, their origin, composition, structure, and history.  They analyze rock samples to understand geological processes and the Earth's history. | Example: Petrologists are currently studying the volcanic rocks found on the island. 岩石学家目前正在研究岛上发现的火山岩。|近义词：geologists (a broader term)|反义词：N/A
