outgallop:/ˌaʊtˈɡæləp/| vt.|超越…的速度；比…跑得快|To surpass in speed; to outrun.  The word combines "out-" (meaning to surpass) and "gallop" (a fast pace of a horse).|例句：The greyhound outgalloped the other dogs in the race.  猎犬在比赛中超过了其他狗。

例句：The thoroughbred outgalloped its competitors on the final stretch. 纯血马在最后冲刺阶段超越了竞争对手。


近义词：outrun, outpace, exceed


反义词：be outpaced by, be overtaken by


词性：动词
