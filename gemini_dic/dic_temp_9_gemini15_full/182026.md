remedy:/ˈrɛmədi/ | n., vt. | 补救措施；治疗；改进 |  a cure for a disease or other problem; to cure or improve something | 近义词：cure, treatment, solution, improvement | 反义词：problem, worsening, deterioration


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor remedies the patient's illness. = 医生治疗病人的疾病。
This policy remedies the economic problem. =这项政策解决了经济问题。


一般过去时:
The mechanic remedied the car's engine problem. = 机械师修理了汽车发动机的故障。
She remedied the situation with a quick solution. = 她迅速地解决了这个问题。


现在完成时:
They have remedied the leak in the roof. = 他们已经修好了屋顶的漏水。
We have remedied the software bug. = 我们已经修复了软件错误。


过去完成时:
He had remedied the situation before anyone noticed. = 他在任何人注意到之前就解决了这个问题。
The company had remedied the safety issue before the accident. = 公司在事故发生前已经解决了安全问题。


将来时:
The government will remedy the unemployment situation. = 政府将解决失业问题。
They will remedy the damage caused by the storm. =他们将修复风暴造成的损失。


虚拟语气:
If only we had a remedy for poverty. = 如果我们有解决贫困的办法就好了。
I wish there was a remedy for this headache. =我希望有治疗这种头痛的方法。


被动语态:
The problem was remedied quickly. = 问题很快得到解决。
The damage will be remedied by the insurance company. =  损坏将由保险公司赔偿。


疑问句:
Is there a remedy for this condition? =  这种病有治疗方法吗？
What remedy did they use to fix the problem? = 他们用什么方法来解决这个问题？


否定句:
There is no easy remedy for this problem. = 这个问题没有简单的解决方法。
They couldn't remedy the situation. = 他们无法解决这种情况。


PHRASE->
a home remedy 家庭疗法
legal remedy 法律补救措施
remedy a situation 补救局面
seek a remedy 寻求补救
remedy for a disease 疾病的治疗方法

词源:
From Old French *remede, from Latin remedium, from re- + mederi "to heal, cure".
