huddled:/ˈhʌdld/| adj. v.| 挤作一团的；蜷缩的；| crowded together; closely packed;  |The children huddled together for warmth. 孩子们挤在一起取暖。|近义词：clustered, crowded, bunched |反义词：scattered, dispersed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They huddle together for warmth. = 他们挤在一起取暖。
The sheep huddle in the field. = 羊群在田野里挤成一团。

一般过去时:
We huddled around the fire. = 我们挤在火堆旁。
The people huddled together in fear. = 人们害怕地挤在一起。

现在完成时:
They have huddled together all night. = 他们整夜都挤在一起。
The crowd has huddled closer to the stage. = 人群已经挤到舞台更近的地方。

过去完成时:
They had huddled together before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经挤在一起了。
The birds had huddled in their nests. = 鸟儿们已经躲在巢里了。

将来时:
We will huddle together to stay warm. = 我们将挤在一起保暖。
The animals will huddle together for protection. = 动物们将为了保护而挤在一起。

虚拟语气:
If it got colder, we would huddle closer. = 如果天气更冷，我们会挤得更近。
I wish they would huddle less and spread out. = 我希望他们少挤一点，分散一些。

疑问句:
