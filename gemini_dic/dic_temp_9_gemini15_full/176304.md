re-edit:/ˌriːˈɛdɪt/ | vt. |重新编辑，再编辑 |To edit something again, often to improve it or correct errors.  |例句：The film was re-edited to remove some violent scenes. 这部电影经过重新剪辑以去除一些暴力场景。|例句：We need to re-edit the manuscript before sending it to the publisher. 我们需要在寄给出版商之前重新编辑稿件。|近义词：revise, amend, rework |反义词：finalize, complete

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-edit the video every week. = 他们每周重新编辑视频。
He re-edits his work several times. = 他多次重新编辑他的作品。

一般过去时:
She re-edited the document yesterday. = 她昨天重新编辑了文档。
They re-edited the film for a wider audience. = 他们为了更广泛的观众重新编辑了电影。

现在完成时:
I have re-edited the chapter three times. = 我已经重新编辑了这章三次。
We have re-edited the report to include the latest data. = 我们已经重新编辑了报告，以包含最新数据。

过去完成时:
She had re-edited the manuscript before submitting it. = 在提交之前，她已经重新编辑了手稿。
They had re-edited the footage several times before the final cut. = 在最终剪辑之前，他们已经多次重新编辑了素材。

将来时:
He will re-edit the article tomorrow. = 他明天将重新编辑这篇文章。
They will re-edit the video after receiving feedback. = 他们将在收到反馈后重新编辑视频。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would re-edit the entire book. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新编辑整本书。
I wish I had re-edited the presentation before delivering it. = 我真希望在发表演示文稿之前重新编辑过它。

被动语态:
The video was re-edited by a professional editor. = 这部视频由专业的编辑重新编辑。
The manuscript is being re-edited for clarity. = 手稿正在被重新编辑以提高清晰度。

疑问句:
Will you re-edit the photos before posting them online? = 你会在网上发布照片之前重新编辑它们吗？
Has the script been re-edited yet? = 剧本已经重新编辑了吗？

否定句:
He did not re-edit the essay before submitting it. = 他在提交论文之前没有重新编辑。
They will not re-edit the film again. = 他们不会再次重新编辑这部电影。
