collection:/kəˈlɛkʃən/| n. |收集；收藏；系列；募捐|A collection is an act of gathering or accumulating things, or the things that have been gathered. It can refer to a group of objects gathered together, such as a stamp collection, or a process of gathering things, such as a fundraising collection.  |例句：He has a large collection of antique cars. 他收藏了很多古董车。

例句：The museum's collection of paintings is world-renowned.  博物馆的绘画收藏举世闻名。|近义词：accumulation, gathering, assemblage |反义词：dispersion, scattering


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He collects stamps. = 他收集邮票。
She gathers flowers. = 她采集鲜花。


一般过去时:
They collected donations for charity. = 他们为慈善机构募捐。
We assembled the furniture. = 我们组装了家具。


现在完成时:
She has collected many rare books. = 她已经收集了许多珍本书。
He has amassed a fortune. = 他已积累了一大笔财富。


过去完成时:
They had collected enough evidence before the trial. = 审判前他们已收集到足够的证据。
She had assembled her team before the project started. = 项目开始前她已组建好她的团队。


将来时:
We will collect the garbage tomorrow. = 我们明天将收集垃圾。
They will be accumulating data for their research. = 他们将为他们的研究收集数据。


虚拟语气:
If I had a larger collection, I would donate some. = 如果我的收藏更多，我会捐赠一些。
If she had assembled the parts properly, the machine would work. = 如果她正确地组装了零件，机器就能工作。


被动语态:
The donations were collected quickly. = 捐款很快就收到了。
The evidence was gathered by the police. = 证据是由警方收集的。


疑问句:
What kind of collection do you have? = 你有什么样的收藏？
What items are being collected for the charity drive? = 这次慈善活动正在收集哪些物品？


否定句:
He doesn't collect coins. = 他不收集硬币。
They didn't gather enough information. = 他们没有收集到足够的信息。
