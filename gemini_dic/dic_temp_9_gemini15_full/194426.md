self-refinement:/ˌself rɪˈfaɪnmənt/| n.|自我完善，自我提升|The process of improving oneself through self-discipline, learning, and reflection.  It involves identifying areas for improvement and actively working to enhance one's skills, knowledge, character, or behavior.|例句：Through years of self-refinement, she became a highly skilled artist. 通过多年的自我完善，她成为了一位技艺精湛的艺术家。

例句：His dedication to self-refinement is evident in his meticulous work and thoughtful demeanor. 他对自我提升的奉献在他的细致工作和体贴的举止中显而易见。|近义词：self-improvement, self-cultivation, personal growth|反义词：self-neglect, self-destruction|名词
