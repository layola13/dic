psychicism:/ˈsaɪkɪkɪzəm/| n. |心灵现象；超心理学；通灵术 |The belief in and study of psychic phenomena, such as extrasensory perception (ESP), telepathy, clairvoyance, and precognition.  It encompasses the idea that the mind can influence events or objects without any physical interaction. |Example:  Her interest in psychicism led her to explore various forms of divination.  她的超心理学兴趣使她探索了各种占卜形式。|近义词：paranormal phenomena, ESP, metapsychics |反义词：rationalism, materialism
