interims:/ˈɪntərɪmz/ | n. | 期间；过渡时期；临时措施 | A temporary arrangement or measure;  a period of time between events.  |例句：The interim government will remain in power until elections are held.  临时政府将在选举举行之前继续执政。

例句：The company appointed an interim CEO while they searched for a permanent replacement.  公司在寻找永久替代人选期间任命了一位临时首席执行官。


近义词：interval, meantime, period, transition, stopgap


反义词：permanence, permanency


词性:名词
