rattling: /ˈrætlɪŋ/ | adj. | 嘎嘎作响的；喋喋不休的；极好的 | Making a rattling sound; characterized by rapid, noisy talk; extremely good or exciting. |


英文解析:  The word "rattling" describes something that makes a series of short, sharp, knocking sounds, like the sound of something loose shaking inside a container.  It can also describe speech that is rapid and noisy, or something that is exceptionally good or exciting.

例句:

The old car made a rattling sound as we drove down the bumpy road. = 当我们沿着颠簸的道路行驶时，那辆旧车发出嘎嘎作响的声音。

The speaker gave a rattling good speech that kept the audience engaged. = 演讲者发表了一个非常精彩的演讲，让听众始终保持着兴趣。


近义词: clattering, clatter, rattling, shaking,  (for speech) rapid, voluble, garrulous

反义词: silent, quiet, hushed, smooth (for sound), slow, deliberate (for speech), dull, boring (for quality)


词性: Adjective (形容词)
