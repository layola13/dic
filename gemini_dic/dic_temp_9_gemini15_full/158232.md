plausible:/ˈplɔːzəbl/|/ˈplɔːzɪbl/| adj. |似乎真实的；可信的；有道理的 | appearing true, reasonable, or fair.  A plausible explanation or theory is one that seems likely to be true, even if it has not been proved. |例句：His story sounds plausible, but I still have my doubts. 他的故事听起来可信，但我仍然怀疑。

例句：The police have a plausible theory about the crime.警方对这起犯罪有一个看似合理的理论。


近义词：believable, credible, likely, probable, reasonable

反义词：implausible, unbelievable, improbable, unlikely, absurd
