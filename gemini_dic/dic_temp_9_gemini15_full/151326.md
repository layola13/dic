parishionership:/pəˈrɪʃənʃɪp/ | n. |教区居民关系；教区成员身份；教区共同体|The relationship between a parish and its members; the state of being a member of a parish; a sense of community among parishioners.|例句：The strength of the parishionership was evident in their collective efforts to rebuild the church after the fire.  教区居民关系在他们集体努力重建教堂后显得尤为突出。|近义词：parish community, congregational fellowship|反义词：isolation, estrangement
