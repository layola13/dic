depopulation:/ˌdiːpɒpjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. |人口减少，人口稀疏| The reduction in the number of people living in a particular place.  It can refer to a gradual decline or a rapid decrease due to various factors such as war, famine, disease, or emigration. |Example: The depopulation of rural areas is a serious concern.  乡村地区人口减少是一个严重的问题。 The depopulation of the village was attributed to the closure of the local factory. 村庄的人口减少归因于当地工厂的倒闭。|近义词：population decline, decrease in population |反义词：population growth, population increase |名词
