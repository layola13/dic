diphthongal:/dɪˈfθɒŋɡəl/| adj.| 双元音的 | Relating to or characterized by a diphthong; consisting of or containing a diphthong.  A diphthong is a sound formed by combining two vowels in a single syllable, where the sound begins at the first vowel and moves towards the second.  | Example: The word "boy" has a diphthongal pronunciation.  男孩的发音是双元音的。 The word "house" contains a diphthongal sound.  “house”这个词包含一个双元音。|近义词:diphthongic|反义词:monophthongal
