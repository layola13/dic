reconning:/rɪˈkɒnɪŋ/| v.|侦察；勘察；重新考虑 |To reconnoiter; to make a reconnaissance.  It can also mean to reconsider or re-evaluate something.|例句：The troops spent several days reconning the enemy's position before the attack.  部队在进攻前花了几天时间侦察敌人的位置。

The general is reconning his strategy after the initial setback. 将军在最初受挫后正在重新考虑他的战略。|近义词：scouting, surveying, exploring, reviewing|反义词：ignoring, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reconning the terrain. = 他们在侦察地形。
She is reconning her options. = 她正在重新考虑她的选择。


一般过去时:
The scouts reconned the area for enemy activity. = 侦察兵侦察了该地区是否有敌军活动。
He reconned his investment strategy after the market crash. = 市场崩盘后，他重新考虑了他的投资策略。


现在完成时:
They have reconned the route and found it safe. = 他们已经侦察了路线，发现它是安全的。
She has reconned her feelings and decided to move on. = 她已经重新考虑了她的感受，并决定继续前进。


过去完成时:
The team had reconned the building before the operation. = 该团队在行动前已经侦察过该建筑物。
He had reconned his mistakes before presenting his new plan. = 他在提出新计划之前已经重新考虑了他的错误。


将来时:
We will recon the area tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上将侦察该地区。
She will recon her decision after consulting with her advisor. = 她将在咨询她的顾问后重新考虑她的决定。


虚拟语气:
If they had reconned the area more thoroughly, they would have avoided the ambush. = 如果他们更彻底地侦察了该地区，他们就能避免伏击。
I wish I had reconned my options before making that commitment. = 我希望在做出承诺之前我已经重新考虑了我的选择。


被动语态:
The enemy position was reconned by the drones. = 敌人的位置被无人机侦察到了。
The plan was reconned and adjusted accordingly. = 计划被重新考虑并相应地进行了调整。


疑问句:
Have they reconned the bridge yet? = 他们已经侦察过桥了吗？
Will you recon your decision before making a final commitment? = 你会在做出最终承诺之前重新考虑你的决定吗？


否定句:
They did not recon the area properly. = 他们没有正确地侦察该地区。
She did not recon her plan before implementing it. = 她没有在实施计划之前重新考虑她的计划。
