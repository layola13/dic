dissimulators:/dɪˈsɪmjuleɪtərz/ | n. |伪装者；掩饰者 |Those who practice dissimulation; those who conceal their true feelings or intentions.  They are masters of deception, hiding their true selves behind a carefully constructed facade. |例句：The dissimulators in the court skillfully hid their true agendas. 朝廷里的伪装者巧妙地隐藏了他们的真实意图。

例句：He was a master dissimulator, able to deceive even his closest friends. 他是个伪装大师，甚至能欺骗他最亲密的朋友。


近义词：hypocrites, deceivers, pretenders, impostors

反义词：truth-tellers, honest people, sincere individuals

词性：名词
