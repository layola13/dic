nonorganic:/ˌnɒnɔːrˈɡænɪk/ | adj. |非有机的；无机的|Not derived from living organisms; relating to or denoting substances not of biological origin.  It often refers to products that are not produced using methods that comply with organic farming standards. |例句：The farmer prefers to use nonorganic fertilizers. 这个农民更喜欢使用无机肥料。|例句：She chose a nonorganic apple because it was cheaper. 她选择了一个非有机苹果，因为它更便宜。|近义词：synthetic, inorganic, artificial|反义词：organic, natural
