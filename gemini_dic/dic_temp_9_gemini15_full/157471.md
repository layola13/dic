pisciform:/ˈpɪsɪfɔːrm/| adj.|鱼形的，鱼状的|Resembling a fish in form or structure.  The term refers to the overall body shape, often elongated and streamlined, characteristic of many aquatic animals. |例句：The ancient fossil showed a pisciform body, suggesting an aquatic lifestyle.  这块古老的化石显示出鱼形的体型，表明它是一种水生生物。The larva exhibits a remarkable pisciform shape before metamorphosis. 幼虫在变态之前呈现出显著的鱼形。|近义词：fish-like, ichthyoid|反义词：mammalian, avian
