grout:/ɡraʊt/| n. v.|水泥砂浆；填缝剂；灌浆|A mixture of cement, sand, and water used to fill spaces between tiles or bricks.  It can also refer to the act of filling these spaces.|例句：We need to grout the tiles in the bathroom. 我们需要给浴室的瓷砖灌浆。
The contractor grouted the cracks in the foundation. 承包商用砂浆填补了地基的裂缝。|近义词：mortar, sealant|反义词：N/A|名词，动词

词源:
The word "grout" comes from the Old French word "grout," meaning "coarse sand," or possibly from the Middle English word "groute," also meaning "gruel" or "coarse sand."  The connection lies in the texture of the material.
