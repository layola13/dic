shoved:/ʃʌvd/| vt.|猛推，挤，塞|To push (someone or something) forcefully.|He shoved the box under the bed. 他把箱子塞到了床底下。|pushed, thrust, crammed|pulled, drawn|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shoves his lunch into his mouth. = 他把午餐塞进嘴里。
She shoves the papers into the drawer. = 她把文件塞进抽屉里。

一般过去时:
I shoved the door open. = 我猛地推开了门。
He shoved the child aside. = 他把孩子推到一边。

现在完成时:
She has shoved all the toys into the cupboard. = 她已将所有玩具塞进橱柜里。
They have shoved their way to the front. = 他们挤到了前面。

过去完成时:
He had shoved the evidence under the carpet. = 他已将证据藏匿起来。
She had shoved the letter into her pocket. = 她已把信塞进口袋里。

将来时:
I will shove the furniture to the side. = 我将会把家具推到一边。
They will shove their way through the crowd. = 他们将挤过人群。

虚拟语气:
If he shoved the button, the machine would start. = 如果他按下按钮，机器就会启动。
I wish I hadn't shoved him so hard. = 我真希望我没有那么用力地推他。

被动语态:
The box was shoved under the table. = 箱子被塞到了桌子底下。
The crowd was shoved back by the police. = 人群被警察推了回去。

疑问句:
Did he shove you? = 他推了你吗？
Who shoved the chair? = 谁推了椅子？

否定句:
I didn't shove him. = 我没有推他。
She didn't shove the food into her mouth. = 她没有把食物塞进嘴里。
