pathogens:/ˈpæθədʒɛnz/| n.|病原体|Organisms that cause disease.  Pathogens can be bacteria, viruses, fungi, or parasites.|例句：Many pathogens are transmitted through contaminated food and water. 许多病原体通过受污染的食物和水传播。|例句：The study aimed to identify the pathogens responsible for the outbreak. 这项研究旨在确定导致疫情爆发的病原体。|近义词：germs, microbes, infectious agents|反义词：None specifically, but could be considered the opposite of beneficial microorganisms.|名词
