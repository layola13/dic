millerite:/ˈmɪləraɪt/| n. |镍黄铁矿|A nickel sulfide mineral, typically occurring in yellowish-bronze, brassy, or reddish-bronze colored crystals or masses. It is an important ore of nickel.|例句：Millerite is often found in association with other sulfide minerals. 镍黄铁矿通常与其他硫化物矿物一起发现。  The sample showed distinct millerite crystals. 样本显示出明显的镍黄铁矿晶体。|近义词：pentlandite, niccolite|反义词:|
