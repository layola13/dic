photocopying: /ˈfoʊtoʊˌkɑːpiɪŋ/ | n.  |影印；复印| The act or process of making copies of documents or images using a photocopier.  |例句：The photocopying of confidential documents is strictly prohibited.  影印机密文件是被严格禁止的。|例句：The cost of photocopying all these documents is quite high.  影印所有这些文件的费用相当高。|近义词：duplicating, reproducing |反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He photocopies all the important documents every week. 他每周都复印所有重要的文件。
She photocopies the report before sending it to the boss. 她把报告复印一份后再交给老板。

一般过去时:
They photocopied the contract yesterday. 他们昨天复印了合同。
I photocopied the article for my research. 我复印了这篇文章用于我的研究。

现在完成时:
We have photocopied all the required materials. 我们已经复印了所有需要的材料。
She has photocopied the letter and sent it to her friend. 她已经复印了信件并寄给了她的朋友。

过去完成时:
He had photocopied the documents before the meeting started. 会议开始前他已经复印好了文件。
They had photocopied the blueprints before construction began.  施工开始前他们已经复印好了蓝图。

将来时:
I will photocopy the application form tomorrow. 我明天将复印申请表。
She will photocopy the report before submitting it. 她将在提交报告之前复印一份。

虚拟语气:
If I had a photocopier, I would photocopy the book. 如果我有一台复印机，我会把这本书复印下来。
I wish I had photocopied the notes before the lecture. 我真希望我在讲座之前把笔记复印下来。

被动语态:
The documents were photocopied by the secretary. 文件是由秘书复印的。
The report is being photocopied at the moment. 报告正在被复印。

疑问句:
Did he photocopy the letter? 他复印那封信了吗？
Have you photocopied the assignment yet? 你已经复印作业了吗？

否定句:
I didn't photocopy the document because I didn't have time. 我没有复印文件，因为我没有时间。
She hasn't photocopied the forms yet. 她还没有复印表格。

词源:
photo- (from Greek phōs, photós "light") + copying (from copy "to reproduce").  字面意思是“用光来复制”。  体现了早期复印技术利用光学原理的特性。
