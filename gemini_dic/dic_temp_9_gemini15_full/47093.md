daubing:/ˈdɔːbɪŋ/| v.|涂抹；乱涂；敷衍了事 |The act of applying a substance, typically paint or plaster, in a somewhat careless or uneven manner.|The artist was daubing paint onto the canvas.  艺术家正在往画布上涂抹颜料。|He daubed the wall with mud. 他用泥巴涂抹墙壁。|smearing, slapping, slathering |carefully painting, meticulously applying |动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is daubing the walls with paint. = 他正在用油漆涂抹墙壁。
She daubs the canvas with vibrant colors. = 她用鲜艳的色彩涂抹画布。

一般过去时:
They daubed the fence with bright colours. = 他们用鲜艳的颜色涂抹了栅栏。
The child daubed paint all over the table. = 孩子把油漆涂抹在桌子上到处都是。

现在完成时:
He has daubed the entire room. = 他已经涂抹了整个房间。
She has daubed a layer of plaster over the cracks. = 她已经在裂缝上涂抹了一层灰泥。

过去完成时:
They had daubed the sign before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经涂抹了标志。
The artist had daubed several layers of paint onto the canvas before he started sketching. = 艺术家在开始素描之前已经在画布上涂抹了几层油漆。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
