lifebuoy:/ˈlaɪfˌbɔɪ/| n. |救生圈，救生圈|A lifebuoy is a ring-shaped flotation device thrown to people in the water to help them stay afloat.  It is often brightly colored and may have a light attached.|例句：The crew threw a lifebuoy to the man who had fallen overboard. 船员们向落水的人扔了一个救生圈。

The ship was equipped with several lifebuoys. 这艘船配备了几个救生圈。|近义词：life preserver, life raft|反义词:None
