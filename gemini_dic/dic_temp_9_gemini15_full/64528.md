erpetology:/ˌɜːrpɪˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. |爬虫学|The branch of zoology concerned with the study of reptiles and amphibians.  |例句：Herpetology is a fascinating field of study, encompassing a wide variety of species. 爬虫学是一个引人入胜的研究领域，涵盖了各种各样的物种。
例句：The professor's lecture on herpetology covered topics such as reptile reproduction and amphibian habitats. 教授关于爬虫学的讲座涵盖了爬行动物繁殖和两栖动物栖息地等主题。|近义词：reptilian zoology, amphibian zoology |反义词：ornithology (鸟类学), mammalogy (哺乳动物学)
