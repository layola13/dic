hairworm: /ˈhɛərwɜːrm/ | n. | 毛细线虫，发状线虫 | A parasitic worm, typically a nematode, that lives in the bodies of insects and other arthropods.  Hairworms are known for their unusual life cycle, often emerging from their hosts in water. |  例句：The grasshopper was found to be infected with a hairworm.  这只蚱蜢被发现感染了毛细线虫。

例句：Scientists are studying the behavior of hairworms to understand their parasitic strategies. 科学家们正在研究毛细线虫的行为，以了解它们的寄生策略。


近义词：nematode (a more general term)


反义词：无


词性：名词
