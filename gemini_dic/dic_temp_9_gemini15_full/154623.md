persisted:/pərˈsɪstɪd/| v. |坚持；持续存在；固执 |To continue to exist or occur despite difficulty or opposition; to continue in a course of action despite difficulty or opposition. |例句：Despite the difficulties, she persisted in her studies. 尽管困难重重，她仍然坚持学习。

例句：The pain persisted for several days. 疼痛持续了好几天。

近义词：continue, endure, remain, persevere | 反义词：cease, stop, discontinue

词源:

* **persist (v.)**  Middle English *persisten*, from Latin *persistere* "to stand fast, persevere," from *per-* "through, thoroughly" + *sistere* "to stand, cause to stand" (from PIE root *stā- "to stand").

用法说明:

一般现在时:
He persists in his denial. = 他坚持否认。
She persists in her efforts to help the poor. = 她坚持努力帮助穷人。

一般过去时:
He persisted in his belief despite the evidence. = 尽管有证据，他仍然坚持自己的信念。
The symptoms persisted for several weeks. = 症状持续了好几个星期。

现在完成时:
They have persisted in their campaign for social justice. = 他们一直坚持他们的社会正义运动。
The problem has persisted for years. = 这个问题已经持续多年了。

过去完成时:
He had persisted in his attempts to climb the mountain despite the bad weather. = 尽管天气不好，他还是坚持尝试攀登那座山。
She had persisted in her studies even after failing several exams. = 即使考试不及格，她仍然坚持学习。

将来时:
She will persist in her efforts to find a cure. = 她将坚持努力寻找治疗方法。
The drought will likely persist throughout the summer. = 干旱很可能会持续整个夏天。


虚拟语气:
If he persisted, he might succeed. = 如果他坚持下去，他可能会成功。
I wish he persisted in his studies. = 我希望他坚持学习。

被动语态:  The rumour persisted (This is less common, as "persist" is usually intransitive). = 谣言持续流传。(这不太常见，因为“persist”通常是不及物动词)


疑问句:
Will the pain persist? = 疼痛会持续吗？
Does he persist in his efforts? = 他坚持他的努力吗？


否定句:
He did not persist in his complaints. = 他没有继续抱怨。
The effects of the medication did not persist. = 药物的作用没有持续。


PHRASE->
persist in = 坚持
persist with = 坚持
persistently = 持续地
insistently = 坚决地
tenaciously = 顽强地
