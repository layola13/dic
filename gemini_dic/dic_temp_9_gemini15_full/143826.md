outpeer:/ˌaʊtˈpɪər/| vt.|胜过，超越|To surpass or excel someone or something.  It implies exceeding someone's abilities or achievements.|例句：He outpeered all his rivals in the competition. 他在比赛中胜过了所有对手。|例句：Her performance outpeered all expectations. 她的表现超出了所有人的预期。|近义词：outshine, outdo, surpass, excel, exceed|反义词：be outdone by, be surpassed by
