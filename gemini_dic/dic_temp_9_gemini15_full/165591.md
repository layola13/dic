premenstrual: /ˌpriːˈmɛnstruəl/ | adj. | 经前的，月经前的 | Relating to the period of time just before menstruation.  The term refers to the days or weeks leading up to a woman's menstrual period, during which she may experience physical and emotional changes. | Example: She suffers from severe premenstrual syndrome (PMS). 她患有严重的经前综合症。|近义词: antemenstrual |反义词: postmenstrual
