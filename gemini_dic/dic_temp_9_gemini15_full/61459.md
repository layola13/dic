embrace's: /ɪmˈbreɪsɪz/ | v. |拥抱；欣然接受；包含；围绕|

英文解析：The word "embraces" is the third-person singular present tense form of the verb "embrace."  It indicates an action of holding someone closely in one's arms, usually expressing affection or warmth.  It also signifies accepting or adopting something wholeheartedly, or encompassing something within its scope.


例句：

She embraces her children tightly. = 她紧紧地抱着她的孩子们。

The new policy embraces a wide range of issues. = 这项新政策涵盖了广泛的问题。


近义词：hugs, enfolds, encompasses, accepts, welcomes


反义词：rejects, shuns, avoids, ignores


词性：verb (动词)
