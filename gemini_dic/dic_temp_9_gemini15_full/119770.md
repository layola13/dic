词源:
The word "mighties" is the plural form of "mighty," meaning powerful or strong.  It's formed by adding the plural suffix "-ies" to the adjective "mighty."  The root of "mighty" is the Old English word "mihtig," meaning powerful or strong.  This word itself is composed of "miht" (might, power) and "-ig" (suffix indicating an adjective).
