reconfines:/ˌriːkənˈfaɪnz/ | v. |重新限制，再次限制 | To confine again; to restrict or limit once more.  The word is formed by adding the prefix "re-" (again) to the verb "confines". |例句：The government reconfines the city after a spike in COVID-19 cases. = 政府在COVID-19病例激增后再次封锁了这座城市。
The new regulations reconfine the use of pesticides. = 新的法规再次限制了杀虫剂的使用。|近义词：restrict, limit, confine, restrain |反义词：liberate, free, release
