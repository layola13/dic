quasi-knowledgeable: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈnɑːlɪdʒəbl/ | adj. |似懂非懂的，貌似精通的 | Seemingly knowledgeable but lacking true expertise; possessing superficial knowledge.  |例句：He gave a quasi-knowledgeable presentation, filled with jargon but lacking substance. 他做了一个似懂非懂的演讲，充满了专业术语但缺乏实质内容。


He presented a quasi-knowledgeable opinion on quantum physics, despite having limited understanding of the subject. 他对量子物理学发表了看似精通的意见，尽管他对这个主题的理解有限。|近义词：superficial, amateurish, uninformed |反义词：expert, knowledgeable, well-informed |词性：形容词
