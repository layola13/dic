outpush:/ˌaʊtˈpʊʃ/| vt. |用力推出，超越|To push something or someone out or away with force; to surpass or exceed.|例句：He outpushed his rivals to win the race. 他用力超越对手赢得了比赛。|近义词：push out, propel, surpass, exceed|反义词：pull in, retract


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The strong wind outpushes the small boat. = 强风把小船推了出去。
He consistently outpushes his colleagues in sales. = 他在销售方面总是超越他的同事。


一般过去时:
The flood outpushed the river banks. = 洪水冲破了河岸。
She outpushed all expectations and finished the marathon. = 她超出了所有人的预期，完成了马拉松比赛。


现在完成时:
The company has outpushed its competitors in the market. = 这家公司在市场上已经超越了竞争对手。
The rising tide has outpushed the sandcastle. = 上涨的潮水冲垮了沙堡。


过去完成时:
By the end of the race, he had outpushed all his opponents. = 到比赛结束时，他已经超越了所有对手。
The army had outpushed the enemy forces from the city. = 军队已经将敌军从城市中驱逐了出去。


将来时:
The new technology will outpush the old methods. = 新技术将淘汰旧方法。
We expect that the demand will outpush the supply. = 我们预计需求将超过供应。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he might have outpushed his rival. = 如果他训练更刻苦，他可能就超越了他的对手。
If the company had invested more, it could have outpushed the competition. = 如果公司投资更多，它就能超越竞争对手。


被动语态:
The old system was outpushed by the new one. = 旧系统被新系统淘汰了。
The small boat was outpushed by the waves. = 小船被海浪推了出去。


疑问句:
Can the new product outpush the existing ones? = 新产品能超越现有产品吗？
Did the team outpush their targets this quarter? = 本季度团队是否超额完成了目标？


否定句:
The team didn't outpush their sales targets last month. = 上个月团队没有超额完成销售目标。
He couldn't outpush the boulder from the path. = 他无法将巨石从路上推开。

PHRASE->
outpush the limits = 突破极限
outpush the boundaries = 突破界限
outpush the competition = 超越竞争
outpush the envelope = 突破界限（创新）
outpush oneself = 竭尽全力
