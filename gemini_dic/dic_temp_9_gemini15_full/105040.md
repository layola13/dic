keramic:/kəˈræmɪk/| n.|陶瓷制品；陶瓷的|Ceramics are objects made from clay and other minerals, which are then hardened by heating.  They are often used in dishes, art, and construction.|例句：The museum has a stunning collection of ancient Greek ceramics. 这个博物馆收藏了大量令人惊叹的古希腊陶瓷制品。|例句：She enjoys working with ceramics and creating beautiful pottery. 她喜欢使用陶瓷并创作美丽的陶器。|近义词：pottery, earthenware, porcelain|反义词：N/A
