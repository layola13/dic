musang:/ˈmuːsæŋ/| n. |麝香猫, 亚洲棕榈猫|A musang is a nocturnal mammal belonging to the family Viverridae, known for its strong scent and sometimes used in coffee production.  They are found in Southeast Asia.|Example: The civet coffee is made from beans that have been eaten and partially digested by the musang.|近义词: civet, palm civet|反义词:  (No direct antonym)|词性:名词
