motorize:/ˈmoʊtəraɪz/ | vt. | 使…机动化；用发动机驱动 | To equip (a vehicle, etc.) with a motor; to convert to motor operation.  | 例句：They plan to motorize the entire army.他们计划使全军机动化。| 例句：The factory motorized its production line. 工厂将其生产线改用发动机驱动。| 近义词：mechanize, automate | 反义词：de-motorize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They motorize their delivery trucks. = 他们使用发动机驱动他们的送货卡车。
The company motorizes its processes. = 公司将其流程机动化。


一般过去时:
He motorized his bicycle. = 他给他的自行车装上了发动机。
The farmer motorized his equipment. = 农民将他的设备机动化了。


现在完成时:
They have motorized their entire fleet. = 他们已经将其全部车队机动化了。
She has motorized her wheelchair. = 她已经为她的轮椅装上了发动机。


过去完成时:
The army had motorized its transport before the invasion. = 在入侵之前，军队已经将其运输工具机动化了。
He had motorized his scooter before the race. = 他在比赛前已经将他的滑板车机动化了。


将来时:
We will motorize our delivery system next year. = 我们明年将把我们的送货系统机动化。
The company will motorize its production line in the future. = 公司将来会将其生产线机动化。


虚拟语气:
If they motorized their business, they would be more efficient. = 如果他们使他们的业务机动化，他们会更高效。
I wish we had motorized the process earlier. = 我希望我们早点将这个流程机动化。


被动语态:
The vehicles were motorized for the expedition. = 这些车辆为探险而进行了机动化改造。
The project is being motorized to improve efficiency. = 为了提高效率，该项目正在进行机动化改造。


疑问句:

否定句:
They did not motorize their equipment. = 他们没有将他们的设备机动化。
We will not motorize the entire factory at once. = 我们不会一次性将整个工厂都机动化。
