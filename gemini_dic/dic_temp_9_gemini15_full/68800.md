fascia:/ˈfæʃə/| n.|筋膜，织物带|A sheet of connective tissue that surrounds muscles, organs, and other structures in the body.  It provides support, protection, and helps with movement.|Example: The physiotherapist worked to release tension in the patient's fascia.  物理治疗师努力放松病人的筋膜。|近义词:aponeurosis, membrane|反义词:None.
