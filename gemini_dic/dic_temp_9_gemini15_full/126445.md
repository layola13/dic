mystifiedly:/mɪsˈtɪfaɪdəli/| adv.|迷惑地，困惑地|in a mystified manner; showing or feeling bewilderment or confusion.|The audience looked on mystifiedly as the magician pulled a rabbit from his hat.  观众迷惑地看着魔术师从帽子里变出一只兔子。|近义词：puzzledly, bewilderedly, confusedly|反义词：understandably, clearly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and as such doesn't have tenses like verbs.  It describes *how* an action is performed.  Therefore, grammatical tense is applied to the verb it modifies.


Example Sentences illustrating different tenses with "mystifiedly":

Present Simple: He looked mystifiedly at the complex equation. 他迷惑地看着复杂的方程式。
Past Simple: She stared mystifiedly at the empty box. 她迷惑地盯着空盒子。
Present Perfect: They have looked mystifiedly at the results for days. 他们几天来一直迷惑地看着结果。
Past Perfect:  He had looked mystifiedly at the instructions before giving up. 他在放弃之前迷惑地看过说明。
Future Simple: She will look mystifiedly at the unexpected news. 她会迷惑地看着这个意外的消息。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "mystifiedly".  The adverb is usually used to modify a verb directly.  For example:


* reacted mystifiedly
* listened mystifiedly
* watched mystifiedly
* responded mystifiedly
* stared mystifiedly
