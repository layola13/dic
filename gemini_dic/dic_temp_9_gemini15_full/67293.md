exsiccation: /ˌɛksɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 干燥；脱水 | The process of drying something out completely, often by removing moisture.  Exsiccation can refer to the drying of biological specimens for preservation, or to the dehydration of a body or organism due to lack of water. | Example: The exsiccation of the plant specimens was crucial for their long-term preservation. 植物标本的干燥对其长期保存至关重要。| 近义词：dehydration, desiccation, drying | 反义词：hydration, rehydration | 名词
