oxidase:/ˈɒksɪdeɪs/ /ˈɑːksɪdeɪs/| n. |氧化酶|An enzyme that catalyzes an oxidation-reduction reaction, often using oxygen as an electron acceptor.|例句：Cytochrome c oxidase is a crucial enzyme in the electron transport chain.  细胞色素c氧化酶是电子传递链中的一种关键酶。|  例句：Many oxidases are involved in metabolic processes. 许多氧化酶参与代谢过程。|近义词：enzyme, catalyst|反义词：reductase
