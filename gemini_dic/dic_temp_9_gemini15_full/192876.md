seasonality: /ˌsiːzənˈæləti/ | n. |季节性，季节变化|The regular and predictable variations in a phenomenon or process that occur with the changing seasons.  This term is often used in various fields, referring to changes in demand, supply, prices, or other factors related to the time of year. For example, the seasonality of ice cream sales is high in summer and low in winter. |例句：The resort experiences high seasonality, with many more visitors during the summer months.  这个度假胜地季节性很强，夏季的游客数量要多得多。

The strong seasonality of agricultural products often leads to price fluctuations.  农产品的强烈季节性往往导致价格波动。|近义词：periodicity, cyclicality |反义词：constancy, stability

词源:
From "season" + "-ality",  the suffix "-ality" indicates a quality or state.  Therefore, seasonality refers to the quality or state of being seasonal, or having characteristics that vary with the seasons.
