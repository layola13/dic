machoisms:/ˈmætʃoʊɪzəm/| n. |男性沙文主义；大男子主义行为；过分男子气概的行为|Behaviors and attitudes characteristic of machismo; excessive displays of masculinity.  Machoisms often involve aggressive or domineering behavior, a disregard for the feelings of others, and a strong emphasis on physical strength and dominance.  These behaviors can manifest in various ways, ranging from subtle displays of bravado to overt acts of violence or aggression.  The term carries a negative connotation, often implying harmful or undesirable traits.|例句：His machoisms alienated many of his colleagues.  他的大男子主义行为疏远了他的许多同事。

His constant boasting about his physical prowess was just another example of his machoisms. 他不断吹嘘自己的体能，这只是他大男子主义行为的另一个例子。


近义词：male chauvinism, sexism, chauvinism, braggadocio, swaggering

反义词：feminism, egalitarianism
