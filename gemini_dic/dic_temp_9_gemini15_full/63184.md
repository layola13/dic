entangled:/ɪnˈtæŋɡld/ | adj. | 纠缠的；复杂的；陷入困境的 |  involved in difficulties or complications; twisted together | The two countries are entangled in a bitter trade dispute.  这两个国家陷入了一场激烈的贸易纠纷。|  involved, complicated, complex | disentangled, simple, straightforward | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wires are entangled. = 电线缠绕在一起。
The situation is entangled. = 情况很复杂。


一般过去时:
The fishing net became entangled in the rocks. = 渔网缠在了岩石上。
His life became entangled with hers. = 他的生活和她纠缠在一起。


现在完成时:
The threads have become entangled. = 线已经缠在一起了。
The project has become hopelessly entangled. = 项目已经陷入困境，无法解决。


过去完成时:

将来时:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

词源:
The word "entangled" originates from the Old English word "entanglian," which combines "in-" (in) and "tangian" (to touch or seize).  It evolved through Middle English to its modern form.
