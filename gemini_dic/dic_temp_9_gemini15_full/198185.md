short-comer:/ˈʃɔːrtˌkʌmər/| n. |缺点；不足；缺陷 |A shortcoming or deficiency; a person who falls short of expectations.  It refers to a flaw, imperfection, or weakness in a person, plan, or system.  |例句：His biggest shortcoming is his lack of patience.  他的最大缺点是缺乏耐心。

例句：The project's main short-comer was its inadequate funding. 这个项目的主要缺点是资金不足。


近义词：defect, flaw, imperfection, failing, weakness, drawback

反义词：strength, advantage, virtue, merit
