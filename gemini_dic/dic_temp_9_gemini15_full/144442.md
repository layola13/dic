outsweetens:/ˌaʊtˈswiːtnz/| v.|胜过…的甜味；比…更甜|To surpass in sweetness; to be sweeter than.|The honey cake outsweetens all the other desserts. 这蜂蜜蛋糕比其他所有甜点都甜。|近义词：outdoes, surpasses, exceeds|反义词：outbitters


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The vanilla ice cream outsweetens the chocolate. = 香草冰淇淋比巧克力冰淇淋甜。
This perfume outsweetens all the others. = 这款香水比其他所有香水都香甜。

一般过去时:
The strawberry jam outsweetened the apricot jam. = 草莓酱比杏酱甜。
Her smile outsweetened the candy. = 她的笑容比糖果还甜。


现在完成时:
This new recipe has outsweetened all my previous attempts. = 这个新食谱比我以前所有尝试的都甜。
The nectar has outsweetened the flowers it came from. = 花蜜比它采自的花朵更甜。


过去完成时:
The cake had outsweetened all the other desserts at the party. = 蛋糕在派对上比其他所有甜点都甜。
The syrup had outsweetened the fruit itself. = 糖浆比水果本身更甜。


将来时:
This new candy will outsweeten all the others on the market. = 这种新糖果将比市场上其他所有糖果都甜。
I think this pie will outsweeten the previous one. = 我认为这个馅饼会比之前的那个更甜。


虚拟语气:
If only this dessert outsweetened the others! = 但愿这道甜点比其他的甜！
I wish this cake outsweetened the one I made last week. = 我希望这个蛋糕比我上周做的那个更甜。


被动语态:
The new flavor was outsweetened by the classic recipe. = 新口味被经典食谱的甜味所超越。
The other desserts were outsweetened by the honey cake. = 其他甜点都被蜂蜜蛋糕的甜味比下去了。


疑问句:

否定句:
