four-wheeler:/ˈfɔːrˌwiːlər/| n. |四轮车，四轮驱动车 |A four-wheeler is a vehicle with four wheels.  This term is often used to refer to cars, motorcycles with sidecars, ATVs, and other similar vehicles.|例句：He drives a rugged four-wheeler for off-road adventures. 他驾驶一辆结实的四轮车进行越野探险。|例句：The farmer uses a four-wheeler to transport goods around his large farm. 这位农民用四轮车在他的大型农场里运输货物。|近义词：quad bike (ATV), car, motorcycle with sidecar|反义词：bicycle, unicycle, walker
