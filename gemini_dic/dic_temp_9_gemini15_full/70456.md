近义词：symbolically, metaphorically, allegorically


反义词：literally, directly


词源:  Derived from "figured," the past participle of the verb "figure," meaning to represent or depict something symbolically or metaphorically.  The addition of "-edly" creates an adverbial form.  The root ultimately traces back to the Latin word "figura," meaning "shape" or "form."


USAGE->
There is no verb conjugation for adverbs.  Adverbs modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs.  The usage of "figuredly" is always the same.  It modifies a verb, indicating the manner in which the action is performed.
