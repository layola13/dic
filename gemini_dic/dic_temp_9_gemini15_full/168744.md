procuticle:/ˈproʊkjuːtɪkəl/| n. |表皮层|The procuticle is the outer layer of the insect cuticle, composed primarily of proteins and lipids.  It is a relatively thin, non-chitinous layer that lies above the exocuticle. The procuticle provides waterproofing and protection to the insect. |The procuticle is essential for insect survival. |近义词：epicuticle (部分重叠)|反义词：endocuticle
