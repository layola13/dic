redecorate:/ˌriːˈdɛkəreɪt/ | vt. |重新装饰，重新装修 | To decorate (a room, house, etc.) again.  |例句：We're going to redecorate the living room this weekend. 我们打算这个周末重新装修客厅。

例句：They decided to redecorate the kitchen after the leak. 他们在漏水之后决定重新装修厨房。


近义词：renovate, refurbish, revamp | 反义词：leave untouched, let decay


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They redecorate their house every two years. = 他们每两年重新装修一次房子。
We redecorate the office annually. = 我们每年重新装修办公室。


一般过去时:
She redecorated her bedroom last month. = 她上个月重新装修了她的卧室。
They redecorated the entire house before moving in. = 他们搬进来之前重新装修了整栋房子。


现在完成时:
We have redecorated the bathroom twice this year. = 今年我们已经两次重新装修了浴室。
They have redecorated the living room to match their new furniture. = 他们已经重新装修了客厅以搭配他们的新家具。


过去完成时:
They had redecorated the kitchen before the party. = 他们在派对之前已经重新装修了厨房。
She had redecorated the apartment before her parents visited. = 在她父母来访之前，她已经重新装修了公寓。


将来时:
We will redecorate the nursery when the baby arrives. = 宝宝出生后，我们将重新装修婴儿房。
They are going to redecorate the dining room next spring. = 他们打算明年春天重新装修餐厅。


虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would redecorate the whole house. = 如果我们有更多钱，我们会重新装修整栋房子。
I wish I could redecorate my apartment, but I can't afford it. = 我希望我能重新装修我的公寓，但我负担不起。


被动语态:
The living room is being redecorated at the moment. = 客厅目前正在重新装修。
The house was redecorated by a professional team. = 这栋房子是由一个专业团队重新装修的。


疑问句:
Are you going to redecorate your bedroom soon? = 你很快就要重新装修你的卧室了吗？
When will they redecorate the office? = 他们什么时候会重新装修办公室？


否定句:
We are not going to redecorate this year. = 我们今年不打算重新装修。
They didn't redecorate the bathroom because of the budget. = 因为预算的原因，他们没有重新装修浴室。
