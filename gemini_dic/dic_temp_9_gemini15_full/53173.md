dippiness:/ˈdɪpɪnəs/| n.|浸湿；忧郁；软弱无力|The state or quality of being dipped or dipped in something;  a feeling of sadness or depression; lack of firmness or strength.|例句：The dippiness of the bread made it difficult to eat.  面包的湿软使得它难以食用。  His dippiness was evident in his slumped posture. 他无精打采的样子显而易见。|近义词：dampness, sogginess, depression, dejection, weakness|反义词：firmness, strength, cheerfulness, buoyancy
