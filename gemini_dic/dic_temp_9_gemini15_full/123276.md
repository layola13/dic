monoecian:/ˌmɒnəˈiːʃən/| adj.|雌雄同株的；雌雄同体的|Having both male and female reproductive organs in the same individual, especially in plants.  Monoecious plants have separate male and female flowers on the same plant, unlike dioecious plants which have separate male and female plants.|Example:  The corn plant is monoecious, with separate male and female flowers on the same stalk. 玉米是雌雄同株的，同一茎上分别有雄花和雌花。

The oak tree is a monoecious plant. 橡树是雌雄同株的植物。|近义词：hermaphroditic, bisexual |反义词：dioecious
