hypernyms: /ˈhaɪpərnɪmz/ | n. | 上义词；上位词 | A hypernym is a word that is more general in meaning than a given word and can thus be used to encompass it.  For example, "animal" is a hypernym of "dog," "cat," and "bird."  It is a broader category that includes more specific terms as its hyponyms. |  "Animal" is a hypernym of "dog".  动物是狗的上义词。 | 近义词：superordinate, generic term | 反义词：hyponym
