inevitably:/ɪˈnevɪtəbli/| adv.|不可避免地，必然地|It is an adverb that means something will happen no matter what. It emphasizes the certainty of an outcome.|例句：Inevitably, the project ran over budget.  不可避免地，这个项目超支了。|例句：He will inevitably face criticism for his decision. 他不可避免地会因为他的决定而受到批评。|近义词：unavoidably, necessarily, certainly|反义词：avoidably, accidentally, perchance
