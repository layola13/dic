predictors:/prɪˈdɪktərz/| n. |预测因子，预测指标|Something that predicts or indicates a future event or trend.  Predictors are often used in statistical modeling to identify variables that are associated with an outcome.|Example:  Socioeconomic status and access to healthcare were identified as key predictors of health outcomes.  社会经济地位和医疗保健的可及性被确定为健康结果的关键预测因素。|近义词：indicators, foretellers, harbingers|反义词：outcomes, results

USAGE->

These factors are predictors of success. = 这些因素是成功的预测指标。
Age and gender serve as predictors in this study. = 年龄和性别在这个研究中充当预测因子。


一般过去时:

虚拟语气:
