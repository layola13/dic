gunman:/ˈɡʌnmæn/| n. |持枪歹徒，枪手|A gunman is a person who carries and uses a gun, especially in a crime.  The term often implies a criminal or violent intent. |例句：The gunman fired several shots before fleeing the scene.  持枪歹徒开了几枪后逃离了现场。|例句：Police are searching for the gunman who robbed the bank. 警方正在搜捕抢劫银行的枪手。|近义词：shooter, assailant, perpetrator,  armed robber|反义词：victim, bystander
