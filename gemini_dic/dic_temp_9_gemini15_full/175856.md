rat: /ræt/ | n. |老鼠，告密者 | A small rodent with a long tail, typically living in sewers or other damp places.  It can also refer to a person who informs on others, especially to authorities. | 例句：The rat scurried across the floor.  这只老鼠窜过地板。

The police were looking for the rat who betrayed the gang.  警方正在寻找出卖该团伙的告密者。 | 近义词：rodent, informer, sneak, traitor | 反义词：cat (as a predator)
