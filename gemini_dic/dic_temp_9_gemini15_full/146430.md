overformalizing: /ˌoʊvərˈfɔːrməlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过分正式化；过于正式 | The act of making something excessively formal;  going beyond what is necessary or appropriate in terms of formality.  It often implies an unnecessary stiffness or rigidity in language, behavior, or procedure. |


例句：

He was overformalizing his presentation, losing the audience's attention with overly technical jargon.  = 他在演示中过于正式，使用了过多的专业术语，从而失去了观众的注意力。

Overformalizing the company's communication style alienated employees and hindered collaboration. = 公司沟通风格过于正式，疏远了员工，阻碍了合作。



近义词：overly formal, excessively formal, stiff, rigid, pedantic, bureaucratic


反义词：informal, casual, relaxed, loose, easygoing


词性：动词 (present participle of overformalize)
