词源:
The word "jumble" ultimately derives from the Middle English word "iomblen," meaning "to stumble" or "to move clumsily."  This root suggests a chaotic, disordered movement, which is reflected in the modern meaning of the word.  The meaning evolved to encompass the resulting state of disorder.
