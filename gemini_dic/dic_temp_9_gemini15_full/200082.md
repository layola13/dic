skewered:/ˈskjuərd/| vt.|串烧；批评；戏弄|To pierce or impale on a skewer or similar pointed object; to criticize or ridicule sharply.|例句：He skewered the chicken for grilling. 他把鸡肉串起来准备烤。 He skewered her argument with a few well-placed questions. 他用几个恰当的问题驳斥了她的论点。|近义词：impale, roast, ridicule|反义词：praise, commend

USAGE->

He skewers the vegetables before grilling them. = 他在烤蔬菜之前把它们串起来。
She skewers the meat with precision. = 她精准地将肉串起来。
