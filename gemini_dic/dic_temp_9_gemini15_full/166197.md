prepunished: /ˌpriːˈpʌnɪʃt/ | adj. | 预先惩罚的，事先受罚的 |  Prepunished refers to something that has been punished beforehand or in advance.  It implies a punishment that occurred before the main event or action being discussed.  This term is less common and often found in specialized contexts. | Example:  The prepunished criminals were less likely to re-offend.  预先受罚的罪犯再次犯罪的可能性较低。  | 近义词: pre-penalized, beforehand punished | 反义词: unpunished, post-punished | 词性: 形容词
