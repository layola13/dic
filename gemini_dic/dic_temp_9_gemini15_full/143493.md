outglitter:/ˌaʊtˈɡlɪtər/| vt.|胜过…的光彩；比…更闪耀|To surpass in brilliance or splendor.|The diamonds outglittered all the other jewels. 钻石的光彩胜过所有其他的珠宝。|近义词：outshine, outsparkle, eclipse|反义词：be dimmed by, be overshadowed by


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The star outglitters all the other lights in the night sky. = 这颗星星的光芒胜过夜空中所有其他的灯光。
Her beauty outglitters everyone else in the room. = 她的美丽胜过房间里其他人。


一般过去时:
The performance outglittered all previous attempts. = 这场演出胜过所有之前的尝试。
Her dress outglittered all the other guests at the gala. = 她的礼服在晚会上比其他客人的都闪耀。


现在完成时:
His achievements have outglittered those of his predecessors. = 他的成就已经超越了他的前任。
The new design has outglittered the old one in terms of elegance. = 就优雅而言，新设计已经超过了旧设计。


过去完成时:
The painting had outglittered all other works in the exhibition. = 这幅画在展览中已经胜过所有其他作品。
Her performance had outglittered even the most seasoned actors. = 她的表演甚至胜过最资深的演员。


将来时:
The new technology will outglitter all existing solutions. = 新技术将超越所有现有的解决方案。
I believe her future accomplishments will outglitter her past ones. = 我相信她未来的成就将胜过她过去的成就。


虚拟语气:
If her talent were fully developed, it would outglitter all others. = 如果她的天赋得到充分发展，它将胜过所有其他天赋。
I wish my work could outglitter the competition. = 我希望我的作品能够胜过竞争对手。


被动语态:
The old building was outglittered by the modern skyscraper. = 老建筑的光彩被现代摩天大楼所掩盖。
Her performance was outglittered only by the breathtaking scenery. = 只有令人叹为观止的风景才能超越她的表演。


疑问句:
Does this diamond outglitter that one? = 这颗钻石的光彩比那颗更胜一筹吗？
Will the new product outglitter its competitors? = 新产品会比竞争对手更出色吗？


否定句:
The small candle didn't outglitter the bright sun.  = 小蜡烛的光芒比不上明亮的阳光。
His efforts did not outglitter his colleagues'. = 他的努力没有超过他的同事。
