probers:/ˈproʊbərz/ | n. |探测器；探测者；调查者 |A prober is a person or thing that probes.  It can refer to a physical instrument used to explore or examine something, or a person who investigates or explores a subject thoroughly. |例句：The scientists used a deep-sea prober to collect samples from the ocean floor. 科学家们使用深海探测器从海底采集样本。

The journalist was a relentless prober, always digging for the truth.  这位记者是一位不知疲倦的调查者，总是追寻真相。|近义词：investigators, explorers, researchers, detectors |反义词：ignorers, neglecters
