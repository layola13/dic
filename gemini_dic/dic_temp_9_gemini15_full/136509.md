nonroyalist:/ˌnɒnˈrɔɪəlɪst/ | adj. |非王室主义者；反对君主制的 | Opposed to or not supporting a monarchy or royal family.  Someone who is a nonroyalist does not believe in or support a system of government headed by a monarch. | Example: The nonroyalist sentiment was growing stronger in the country leading up to the revolution.  非王室主义情绪在革命前在全国范围内日益增强。  The protesters, many of whom were nonroyalists, demanded reforms.  抗议者中，许多是反对君主制的，他们要求改革。| 近义词：antimonarchist, republican | 反义词：royalist, monarchist |  形容词
