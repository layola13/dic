leper:/ˈlɛpər/| n. |麻风病人；被社会抛弃的人 |A person affected by leprosy.  Historically, lepers were often ostracized and forced to live separately from society. |例句：The leper lived a solitary life outside the village. 麻风病人独自一人住在村外。 The scandal made him a social leper.  这场丑闻使他成为被社会抛弃的人。|近义词：outcast, pariah |反义词：saint, darling
