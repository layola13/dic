interrogative:/ɪnˈtɛrəɡətɪv/| adj.|疑问的，问询的| Relating to or characteristic of a question; questioning.  An interrogative word, phrase, or sentence is used to ask a question. | The interrogative tone of his voice suggested he wasn't satisfied with the answer. 他语气中的疑问意味表明他对答案并不满意。|近义词：questioning, inquiring|反义词：declarative, assertive|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Is he happy? = 他快乐吗？
Are they coming? = 他们要来吗？


一般过去时:
Did you go to the party? = 你去参加聚会了吗？
Were they there? = 他们在那儿吗？


现在完成时:
Have you finished your work? = 你完成你的工作了吗？
Has she ever been to Paris? = 她曾经去过巴黎吗？


过去完成时:
Had you seen him before? = 你之前见过他吗？
Had they left when you arrived? = 你到达时他们已经离开了吗？


将来时:
Will you be home tonight? = 你今晚会在家吗？
Will it rain tomorrow? = 明天会下雨吗？


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would apologize. = 如果我是你，我会道歉。
I wish I knew the answer. = 我真希望知道答案。


被动语态:
Was the car repaired? = 车修好了吗？
Were the books returned? = 书还回去了吗？


疑问句:
Where is my phone? = 我的手机在哪里？
What time is it? = 现在几点了？


否定句:
It isn't raining. = 天没下雨。
They didn't come. = 他们没来。


PHRASE->
interrogative sentence = 疑问句
interrogative pronoun = 疑问代词
interrogative adjective = 疑问形容词
interrogative adverb = 疑问副词
raise an interrogative = 提出疑问
