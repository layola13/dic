outargue:/ˌaʊtˈɑːrɡjuː/| vt.|胜过…的辩论；以辩论胜过|To defeat someone in an argument.|例句：He outargued his opponent and won the debate. 他在辩论中胜过对手并赢得了辩论。|例句：She completely outargued him on the subject of climate change.  她在气候变化的论点上完全驳倒了他。|近义词：defeat, surpass, vanquish|反义词：be outargued by, lose an argument|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outargues his opponents in most debates. = 他在大多数辩论中都胜过他的对手。
She outargues her colleagues on complex legal issues. = 她在复杂的法律问题上胜过她的同事。


一般过去时:
He outargued his brother in the family discussion. = 他在家庭讨论中胜过了他的兄弟。
The lawyer outargued the prosecution's witness. = 律师在辩论中胜过了控方的证人。


现在完成时:
She has outargued every opponent she's faced this year. = 今年她已经胜过了遇到的每一个对手。
He has outargued his critics convincingly on several occasions. = 他在几个场合都令人信服地驳斥了他的批评者。


过去完成时:
He had outargued the professor before the final exam. = 在期末考试前，他已经在辩论中胜过了教授。
They had outargued their parents on the issue of curfew. = 他们在宵禁问题上已经胜过了他们的父母。


将来时:
I believe she will outargue him easily in the upcoming debate. = 我相信在即将到来的辩论中，她会轻松地胜过他。
He predicts he will outargue his opponent on the economic policy. = 他预测他会在经济政策上胜过他的对手。


虚拟语气:
If he had more evidence, he would outargue his opponent. = 如果他有更多证据，他就能胜过他的对手。
If I were better prepared, I could outargue them. = 如果我准备得更好，我就能胜过他们。


被动语态:
His arguments were outargued by the opposing team. = 他的论点被对方团队驳倒了。
The proposal was outargued in the board meeting. = 该提案在董事会上被驳回了。


疑问句:

否定句:
He couldn't outargue his boss on the matter. = 他在这个问题上无法说服他的老板。
They failed to outargue the judge's decision. = 他们未能推翻法官的裁决。
