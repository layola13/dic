overdrew:/ˌoʊvərˈdruː/ | vt. |透支；提取超过存款额 |To withdraw more money from a bank account than is actually available.  |例句：I accidentally overdrew my checking account. 我不小心透支了我的支票账户。

例句：He overdrew his account by $500. 他透支了500美元。


近义词： exceed, overspend | 反义词： underspend, deposit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overdraws his account regularly. 他经常透支他的账户。
She overdraws her credit card every month. 她每个月都透支信用卡。


一般过去时:
I overdrew my account last month. 我上个月透支了我的账户。
They overdrew their savings account to pay for the vacation. 他们透支了储蓄账户来支付度假费用。


现在完成时:
He has overdrawn his account several times this year. 他今年已经几次透支了他的账户。
She has never overdrawn her account before. 她以前从未透支过账户。


过去完成时:
I had overdrawn my account before I realized it. 我在意识到之前就已经透支了我的账户。
They had overdrawn their account by a significant amount. 他们已经透支了一大笔款项。


将来时:
I will overdraw my account if I don't deposit more money. 如果我不存入更多钱，我就会透支我的账户。
She will likely overdraw her account again next month. 她下个月很可能会再次透支她的账户。


虚拟语气:
If I had not overdrawn my account, I wouldn't have been charged a fee. 如果我没有透支我的账户，我就不会被收取费用。
I wish I hadn't overdrawn my account. 我真希望我没有透支我的账户。


被动语态:

否定句:
