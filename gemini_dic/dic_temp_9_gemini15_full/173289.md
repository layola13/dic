quasi-convenient:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ kənˈviːniənt/ | adj. | 准方便的；相对方便的 |  Almost convenient; offering a degree of convenience but not fully so.  It implies a compromise between complete convenience and significant inconvenience.  |Example: The location of the office is quasi-convenient; it's close to public transport but requires a long walk from the nearest station.  这个办公室的位置比较方便；它靠近公共交通，但需要从最近的车站步行很长一段路。|近义词：relatively convenient, fairly convenient, moderately convenient | 反义词：inconvenient, highly inconvenient | 词性：形容词
