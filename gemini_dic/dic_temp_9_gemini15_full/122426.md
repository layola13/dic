moaning:/ˈmoʊnɪŋ/|/ˈməʊnɪŋ/| n. v.|呻吟；抱怨；悲叹|The act of making a low, prolonged sound expressing pain, unhappiness, or disapproval.  It can be a noun referring to the sound itself or a verb describing the act of making the sound. |例句：The wounded soldier lay moaning in pain. 受伤的士兵痛苦地呻吟着。

The baby's constant moaning kept everyone awake. 婴儿的持续哭闹声让大家无法入睡。


近义词：groaning, complaining, whining, lamenting|反义词：rejoicing, celebrating, cheering|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He moans all day. = 他整天都在呻吟。
She moans about her job. = 她抱怨她的工作。


一般过去时:
He moaned in his sleep. = 他睡梦中呻吟。
She moaned about the unfair treatment. = 她抱怨不公平的待遇。


现在完成时:
He has been moaning about the weather all week. = 他整个星期都在抱怨天气。
She has moaned about her boss for months. = 她已经抱怨她的老板好几个月了。


过去完成时:
He had moaned about the pain for hours before the doctor arrived. = 在医生到来之前，他已经痛苦地呻吟了好几个小时。
She had moaned about the project's delays repeatedly. = 她反复抱怨项目的延误。


将来时:
He will moan if he doesn't get his way. = 如果他不顺心，他会抱怨的。
She will moan about the heat tomorrow. = 她明天会抱怨炎热的天气。


虚拟语气:
If he felt any better, he wouldn't be moaning. = 如果他感觉好一点，他就不会呻吟了。
I wish she wouldn't moan so much. = 我希望她不要抱怨那么多。


被动语态:
His pain was moaned about by everyone. = 他的痛苦被大家所抱怨。 (This is less common and sounds unnatural.)
The problem was often moaned about in meetings. = 这个问题经常在会议上被抱怨。


疑问句:
Is he still moaning? = 他还在呻吟吗？
What is she moaning about? = 她抱怨什么？


否定句:
He didn't moan at all. = 他根本没有呻吟。
She didn't moan about the workload. = 她没有抱怨工作量。

PHRASE->
moaning and groaning = 抱怨和呻吟
low moaning = 低沉的呻吟
constant moaning = 持续的呻吟
painful moaning = 痛苦的呻吟
a moaning sound = 一种呻吟声
