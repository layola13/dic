decontamination:/ˌdiːkənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |净化；去污；除污染|The removal of contaminants, especially radioactive, chemical, or biological agents, from a person, object, or area.|The decontamination process took several hours. 净化过程花费了几个小时。|purification, cleaning, sterilization|contamination


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hospital performs regular decontamination procedures. = 医院定期进行净化程序。
Decontamination is crucial after a chemical spill. = 化学品泄漏后，净化至关重要。


一般过去时:
They decontaminated the area after the bomb threat. = 他们解除炸弹威胁后对该地区进行了净化。
The firefighters decontaminated themselves after the fire. = 消防员灭火后对自己进行了净化。


现在完成时:
The team has completed the decontamination of the site. = 团队已经完成了该地点的净化工作。
We have decontaminated all the affected equipment. = 我们已经净化了所有受影响的设备。


过去完成时:
The emergency crew had decontaminated the victims before transporting them. = 紧急救援人员在转移受害者之前已经对他们进行了净化。
They had decontaminated the lab before starting the experiment. = 他们在开始实验之前已经净化了实验室。


将来时:
The authorities will decontaminate the water supply. = 当局将净化供水系统。
We will decontaminate the building tomorrow. = 我们明天将对建筑物进行净化。

If we had better equipment, we could decontaminate the area more efficiently. = 如果我们有更好的设备，就能更有效地净化该区域。
