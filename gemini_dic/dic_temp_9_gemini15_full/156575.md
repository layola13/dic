pickings:/ˈpɪkɪŋz/| n.| 拾到的东西；所得；收获；少量食物 | Things that have been picked up or gathered, especially small amounts of food or valuables.  |例句：The pickings were slim at the flea market. 跳蚤市场上的收获甚微。|例句：After the storm, the beach was full of interesting pickings. 暴风雨过后，海滩上满是有趣的拾获物。|近义词： gleanings, scraps, remnants |反义词：abundance, plenty
