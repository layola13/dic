podocephalous:/ˌpɒdəˈsɛfələs/| adj. |小头畸形的；小头的|Having a small head.  The term refers to a condition where the head is abnormally small relative to the body. This can be due to various genetic or environmental factors.  It is often associated with intellectual disability and other developmental delays.|Example: The infant was diagnosed with podocephalous microcephaly.|近义词：microcephalic|反义词：macrocephalic
