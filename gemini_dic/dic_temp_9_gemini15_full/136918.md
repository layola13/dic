nonsolublenesses:/ˌnɒnsɒljʊˈbɪlnɪsəz/ | n. |不溶解性；不溶解度 |The plural form of nonsolubility, referring to multiple instances or aspects of the inability of a substance to dissolve in a solvent.  It represents a lack of solubility across different contexts or substances.| 例句：The nonsolublenesses of these polymers in water hinder their applications in aqueous systems. 这些聚合物在水中的不溶解性阻碍了它们在水性体系中的应用。The research focused on overcoming the nonsolublenesses encountered during the drug formulation process. 研究的重点是克服药物制剂过程中遇到的不溶解性。|近义词：insolubilities, insolubleness|反义词：solubilities
