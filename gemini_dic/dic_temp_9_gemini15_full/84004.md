half-developed:/ˌhɑːfˈdɛvəlɒpt/| adj. |半发育的；未充分发展的；不成熟的 |Partially developed; not fully grown or matured.  It implies incompleteness or a lack of full potential. |例句：The half-developed country is struggling with infrastructure issues.  这个半发展国家正在努力解决基础设施问题。

例句：His half-developed ideas need further refinement before presentation. 他的想法尚不成熟，需要进一步完善才能进行展示。


近义词：underdeveloped, immature, incomplete, rudimentary

反义词：fully developed, mature, complete, advanced

词性:形容词
