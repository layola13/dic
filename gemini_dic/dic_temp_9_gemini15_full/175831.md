raspiness:/ˈræspɪnəs/| n. |沙哑；粗糙|A harsh, grating quality in a voice or sound, like the sound of a rasp.  It often indicates a throat irritation or illness.|例句：His voice had a raspiness that suggested he'd been shouting.他的声音沙哑，像是喊过嗓子。|例句：The raspiness in her voice was a clear sign of her cold. 她声音沙哑，显然是感冒了。|近义词：hoarseness, roughness, gratingness|反义词：smoothness, clarity, mellowness|名词
