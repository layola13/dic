revolvement:/rɪˈvɒlvmənt/ | n. |旋转；循环；反复；纷乱 | The continuous turning or spinning around a central point; a state of continuous change or activity; a confused or chaotic state.  |例句：The slow revolution of the Earth causes day and night. 地球的缓慢旋转造成了昼夜更替。|近义词：rotation, revolution, cycle, turmoil |反义词：stillness, stability, calm


USAGE->

The wheel shows a constant revolving movement. = 轮子展现出持续的旋转运动。
His thoughts revolve around his work. = 他的思绪围绕着他的工作打转。

The top revolved rapidly before falling. = 陀螺快速旋转然后倒下。
