hedonistically:/ˌhiːdəˈnɪstɪkli/| adv. |享乐主义地；纵欲地 |In a way that is characterized by the pursuit of pleasure and self-indulgence.  It emphasizes the importance of pleasure and gratification as the ultimate goals of life. |例句：They lived hedonistically, spending their money on lavish parties and expensive vacations.他们过着享乐主义的生活，把钱花在奢华的派对和昂贵的假期上。

例句：The novel depicts a society that is completely hedonistically driven, with no regard for consequences. 这部小说描绘了一个完全由享乐主义驱动的社会，不考虑后果。


近义词：sensuously, voluptuously, luxuriously, pleasurably


反义词：ascetically, austerely, self-denyingly


词源:  Derived from "hedonism," which comes from the Greek word "hēdonē" (ἡδονή), meaning "pleasure."  The suffix "-istically" indicates manner or style.
