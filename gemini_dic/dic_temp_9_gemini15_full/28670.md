carino: /kəˈriːnoʊ/ | adj. |可爱的，亲爱的|  A term of endearment, expressing affection or fondness. It can be used to describe someone or something that is cute, charming, or lovable.  The word often carries a sense of warmth and intimacy.| 例句：My carino, I missed you so much. 亲爱的，我非常想你。  |近义词：sweet, darling, lovely, cute, adorable |反义词：ugly, unpleasant, hateful
