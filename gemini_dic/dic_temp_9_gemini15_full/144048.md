outrigs:/ˈaʊtrɪɡz/| n.|支柱；支撑物；舷外支架|A structure extending outward from the side of a vessel or vehicle to provide stability or support, often used in boats and airplanes.|例句：The outriggers on the canoe kept it stable in the rough water.  独木舟上的舷外支架使它在汹涌的水中保持稳定。|例句：The airplane's outriggers are designed to reduce drag during takeoff.  飞机的舷外支架设计用于在起飞时减少阻力。|近义词：supports, braces, struts|反义词：N/A
