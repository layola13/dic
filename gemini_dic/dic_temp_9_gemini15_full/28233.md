capstone:/ˈkæpstoʊn/| n.|顶石；最重要的事情；最终的成就|A capstone is the final element that completes something, often a building or project.  It can also refer to a culminating academic project or experience.|例句：Her senior thesis was the capstone of her college career.  她的毕业论文是她大学生涯的最终成就。|例句：The new statue served as the capstone of the park's design.这座新雕像是公园设计的最终点睛之笔。|近义词：culmination, apex, pinnacle, climax|反义词：beginning, foundation, base|名词
