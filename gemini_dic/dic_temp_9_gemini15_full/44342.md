cross:/krɒs/ /krɔːs/| n., v., adj., adv.| 十字；交叉；穿过；越过；混合；反对；苦难；脾气暴躁|A structure built to cross a river, road, etc.; to go across or over something; to intersect; to be opposed to something; a hybrid; a difficult experience; irritable; in a diagonal direction|

例句:
He crossed the street. 他穿过街道。

They crossed swords in a fierce debate. 他们在激烈的辩论中针锋相对。


近义词: intersect, traverse, oppose, hybrid, affliction, grumpy, diagonally


反义词: parallel, agree, smooth, pleasant, calm, straight


词性:名词，动词，形容词，副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He crosses the road every day. 他每天都过马路。
She crosses her legs when she sits. 她坐着的时候总是跷二郎腿。


一般过去时:
I crossed the bridge yesterday. 我昨天过了桥。
They crossed the finish line together. 他们一起冲过了终点线。


现在完成时:
He has crossed the Atlantic several times. 他已经横渡大西洋好几次了。
She has crossed out many mistakes in her essay. 她已经划掉了论文中的许多错误。


过去完成时:
They had crossed the border before the sun rose. 他们在太阳升起之前就越过了边境。
She had crossed her fingers, hoping for good luck. 她交叉着手指，希望好运。


将来时:
I will cross the desert next year. 我明年将穿越沙漠。
They will cross the river by boat. 他们将乘船过河。


虚拟语气:
If I had crossed the street earlier, I would have avoided the accident. 如果我早点过马路，我就不会发生事故了。
I wish I had crossed the ocean when I was younger. 我真希望我年轻的时候就横渡了大洋。


被动语态:
The road was crossed by many people. 这条路被很多人穿过。
The river is crossed by a bridge. 这条河有一座桥可以通行。


疑问句:
Does this road cross the river? 这条路穿过河吗？
Have you ever crossed the Sahara Desert? 你曾经穿越撒哈拉沙漠吗？


否定句:
He didn't cross the road because of the heavy traffic. 因为交通繁忙，他没有过马路。
She hasn't crossed the finish line yet. 她还没有冲过终点线。


PHRASE->

cross-examine:  盘问 (to question thoroughly)
