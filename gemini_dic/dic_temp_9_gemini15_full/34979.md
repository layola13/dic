clogged:/klɒɡd/| adj.|堵塞的；阻塞的；充满的|Blocked or filled with something that prevents movement or flow.|The drain is clogged with hair.  排水管被头发堵住了。|The roads were clogged with traffic. 道路被交通堵塞了。|unblocked, clear|open

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pipes are clogged with grease. = 水管被油脂堵塞了。
The streets are clogged with traffic during rush hour. = 高峰期街道交通堵塞。

一般过去时:
The toilet clogged after I flushed it. = 我冲水后马桶堵住了。
The sink clogged yesterday. = 水槽昨天堵住了。

现在完成时:
The drain has been clogged for days. = 排水管堵塞了好几天了。
The road has been clogged with construction vehicles. = 道路被建筑车辆堵塞了。

过去完成时:
The engine had clogged before we reached the summit. = 我们到达山顶之前引擎就堵塞了。
The pipes had clogged before the plumber arrived. = 水管工到达之前水管就堵塞了。

将来时:
The pipes will clog if you don't clean them regularly. = 如果你不定期清洁水管，它们就会堵塞。
The highway will be clogged during the holiday weekend. = 假日周末高速公路将会堵塞。

虚拟语气:

I wish the sink wasn't clogged. = 我希望水槽没有堵塞。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
