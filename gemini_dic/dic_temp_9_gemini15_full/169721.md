propriety:/prəˈpraɪəti/ | n. |得体；适当；礼节；规矩 | The quality or state of conforming to conventionally accepted standards of behavior or morals.  It refers to what is proper or fitting in a particular situation, often concerning social etiquette and moral standards. |例句：He questioned the propriety of the judge's actions. 他质疑法官行为的得体性。

例句：She behaved with great propriety throughout the ceremony.  她在整个仪式中举止得体。


近义词：decorum, appropriateness, fitness, seemliness

反义词：impropriety, indecency, unseemliness, impropriety


词源：源自古法语 *propriete，最终源自拉丁语 proprietas，意为“所有权；特性；品质”。  其含义演变与“拥有适当的行为”相关联。
