misassumption:/ˌmɪsæˈsʌmpʃən/| n. |误解，错误的假设 |A misassumption is a wrong or mistaken assumption; a false belief or understanding. It's a misunderstanding based on incorrect information or interpretation. |例句：His misassumption about the deadline led to a missed opportunity.  他的对截止日期的误解导致了一个错失的机会。

His entire argument was based on a fundamental misassumption. 他的整个论点都是基于一个根本性的错误假设。


近义词：misconception, mistake, error, fallacy, misunderstanding |反义词：correct understanding, accurate assumption, truth

词性：名词
