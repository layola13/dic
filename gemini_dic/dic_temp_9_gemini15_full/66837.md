/ɪkˈspeɪtrieɪtərz/| n. |侨民；移居海外者；流亡者 | A person who lives in a foreign country.  Expatriates often maintain strong ties to their home country. |Example: Many expatriates in the city maintain their cultural traditions.  许多居住在这个城市的侨民都保持着他们的文化传统。|近义词：emigrant, exile |反义词：immigrant, repatriate |名词
