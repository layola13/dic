rainy:/ˈreɪni/ | adj. | 下雨的，多雨的 |  Describes something affected by or characterized by rain.  | It's a rainy day. = 今天下雨。 | The rainy season is coming. = 雨季要来了。| sunny, dry | sunny, fair |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It is rainy today. = 今天下雨。
The weather is usually rainy in this region. = 这个地区的天气通常多雨。

一般过去时:
It was rainy yesterday. = 昨天是雨天。
We stayed inside because it was rainy. = 因为下雨我们待在室内。

现在完成时:
It has been rainy all week. = 一周以来一直下雨。
We have had many rainy days this month. = 这个月我们有很多雨天。

过去完成时:
It had been rainy for days before the sun came out. = 太阳出来之前已经下了好几天雨了。
They had already experienced several rainy seasons before moving to the desert. = 他们在搬到沙漠之前已经经历过几个雨季了。

将来时:
It will be rainy tomorrow. = 明天会下雨。
The forecast predicts that it will be rainy next week. = 预报说下周会下雨。

虚拟语气:
If it were rainy, we would stay home. = 如果下雨，我们就待在家里。
I wish it weren't rainy today. = 我希望今天不下雨。

被动语态:
The game was postponed because it was rainy. = 比赛因为下雨被推迟了。
The streets were flooded because it had been rainy. = 街道被淹了，因为一直下雨。

疑问句:
Is it rainy outside? = 外面下雨了吗？
Will it be rainy during our trip? = 我们旅行期间会下雨吗？

否定句:
It is not rainy today. = 今天不下雨。
The weather forecast says it will not be rainy tomorrow. = 天气预报说明天不会下雨。


PHRASE->
