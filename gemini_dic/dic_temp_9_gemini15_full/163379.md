preconsciousness: /ˌpriːˈkɒnʃəsnəs/ | /ˌpriːˈkɑːnʃəsnəs/ | n. | 前意识 |  The level of consciousness that contains thoughts and feelings that are not currently in awareness but can be easily retrieved.  It is a state between full consciousness and the unconscious.  Preconscious material is readily available to consciousness when needed.  | Example:  He wasn't consciously thinking about his childhood trauma, but it was present in his preconsciousness, influencing his behavior. 他并没有有意识地思考他童年的创伤，但它存在于他的前意识中，影响着他的行为。


近义词: subconscious, subliminal | 反义词: consciousness, unconsciousness
