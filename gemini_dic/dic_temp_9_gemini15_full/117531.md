meeters:/ˈmiːtərz/| n.| 计量器；测量者；会面者 | A person or thing that measures or meets.  The word is a plural form of "meter" or "meeter,"  depending on the context. As "meter," it refers to a measuring device; as "meeter," it refers to someone who meets. | 例句：The scientists used various meeters to collect data. 科学家们使用了各种计量器来收集数据。

The marathon runners were all meeters at the finish line. 马拉松运动员都在终点线相遇。 | 近义词：(as meter) gauges, instruments; (as meeter) participants, attendees | 反义词：N/A
