self-prescribed:/ˌsɛlf prɪˈskraɪbd/| adj.|自行诊断的；自己开药的 |  relating to a medical treatment or medication that a person has chosen and started without the guidance of a doctor.  It implies a lack of professional medical advice. | 例句：He engaged in self-prescribed herbal remedies to alleviate his symptoms. 他自行服用草药来缓解症状。

例句：The self-prescribed medication proved ineffective and potentially harmful.  自行服用的药物证明无效且可能有害。


近义词：self-medicated, home-remediated, unprescribed

反义词：doctor-prescribed, medically supervised


词性：形容词
