crybabied:/ˈkraɪˌbeɪbid/| v.|哭哭啼啼（过去式和过去分词）|To behave in a childish and excessively emotional way, typically by crying; to act like a crybaby.|例句：He crybabied all the way home after losing the game. 他输了比赛后一路哭哭啼啼地回家。  例句：She crybabied to her mother about her unfair punishment. 她向妈妈哭诉她受到的不公平惩罚。|近义词：whined,  complained,  fussing|反义词：remained calm,  stayed composed|动词


USAGE->
