discouragements:/dɪsˈkɜːrɪdʒmənts/ | n. | 挫折；灰心；泄气 | The feeling of being disheartened or losing hope; obstacles or setbacks that hinder progress.  |例句：The constant discouragements almost led her to give up her dream.  持续不断的挫折几乎让她放弃了梦想。

例句：He faced many discouragements in his career, but he persevered. 他在事业上遭遇了许多挫折，但他坚持了下来。


近义词：setbacks, obstacles, difficulties, hardships, disappointments | 反义词：encouragement, inspiration, motivation | 词性：名词
