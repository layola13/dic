deers:/dɪrz/ | n. |鹿群；多只鹿 |The word "deers" is the plural of "deer," but its usage is considered nonstandard or dialectal in most varieties of English.  The standard plural form is also "deer."  While grammatically incorrect in formal English, it's sometimes used informally, especially in certain dialects. |  I saw a herd of deers in the forest. 我在森林里看到一群鹿。  There were several deers grazing near the river.  几只鹿在河边吃草。|deer, stags, does (female deer)|  |noun|
