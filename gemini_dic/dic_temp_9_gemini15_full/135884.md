nonprovisional:/ˌnɒnprəˈvɪʒənəl/| adj. |非临时性的；正式的|Not temporary or provisional; intended to be permanent or final.  It is often used in the context of patent applications, referring to a patent application that is complete and ready for substantive examination.|Example:  He filed a nonprovisional patent application to protect his invention. 他提交了一份非临时专利申请以保护他的发明。|近义词：permanent, definitive, final|反义词：provisional, temporary, preliminary
