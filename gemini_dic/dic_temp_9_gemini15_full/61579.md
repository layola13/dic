emergence:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒəns/| n.|出现，涌现，兴起|The process of coming into existence or becoming prominent.  It can refer to new phenomena, ideas, or entities arising from a complex system. |例句：The emergence of new technologies has transformed our lives. 新技术的出现改变了我们的生活。
例句：The emergence of a new political party surprised many. 新政党的出现令许多人感到意外。|近义词：appearance, rise, arrival, development|反义词：disappearance, decline, vanishing|名词
