incompatibility:/ˌɪnkəmˌpætəˈbɪləti/| n. |不相容性，冲突 |The state or fact of being incompatible; a lack of compatibility.  It refers to a situation where two or more things are unable to exist or work together successfully because of fundamental differences.  |Example: The incompatibility of their personalities led to the breakup. 他们的性格不合导致了分手。|近义词：conflict, discord, antagonism |反义词：compatibility, harmony, concord |名词
