locution:/ləˈkjuːʃən/| n. |措辞；说法；习语|A locution is a particular way of saying something; a phrase or expression.  It can refer to the choice of words and the manner of speaking.  The term often implies a distinctive or characteristic style of speech. |例句：Her locution was precise and elegant.  她的措辞精准而优雅。

例句：The politician's carefully chosen locutions avoided directly addressing the controversy.  这位政客精心挑选的措辞避免了直接触及争议。


近义词：phrase, expression, idiom, saying, wording

反义词：silence, reticence (lack of locution)


词性：名词
