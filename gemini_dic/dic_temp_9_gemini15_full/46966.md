dartingness:/ˈdɑːrtɪŋnəs/ | n. | 迅速移动；敏捷；闪动 | The quick, sudden movements of something.  It often implies a sense of energy, nervousness, or excitement. |例句：The dartingness of the hummingbird's wings was mesmerizing.  蜂鸟翅膀的快速移动令人着迷。

The dartingness of his eyes betrayed his nervousness. 他眼睛的闪烁出卖了他的紧张。


近义词：flicker, quickness, agility, nimbleness, swiftness


反义词：slowness, sluggishness, lethargy


词性：名词
