inveigh:/ɪnˈveɪ/ | vt. |猛烈抨击；强烈抗议 | To protest strongly or attack vehemently with words.  |例句：The senator inveighed against the proposed tax cuts.  参议员强烈反对拟议的减税方案。|例句：She inveighed against the injustices of the system. 她强烈抗议制度的不公正。|近义词：denounce, rail against, fulminate against |反义词：praise, commend, endorse |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He inveighs against the government's policies. = 他猛烈抨击政府的政策。
They inveigh against the unfair treatment. = 他们强烈抗议不公平的待遇。


一般过去时:
She inveighed against the corruption in the company. = 她强烈抨击公司里的腐败。
The protesters inveighed against the new law. = 抗议者强烈反对这项新法律。


现在完成时:
He has inveighed against the proposed changes for months. = 他几个月来一直在强烈反对拟议的变革。
They have inveighed against inequality throughout their careers. = 他们职业生涯中一直在强烈抗议不平等。


过去完成时:
She had inveighed against the injustice before the hearing. = 在听证会之前，她已经强烈抗议这种不公正行为。
They had inveighed against the decision many times. = 他们多次强烈反对这一决定。


将来时:
He will inveigh against the new regulations at the meeting. = 他将在会议上强烈反对新的规章制度。
They will inveigh against the proposed cuts in funding. = 他们将强烈反对拟议的资金削减。


虚拟语气:
If he were here, he would inveigh against this decision. = 如果他在这里，他会强烈反对这个决定。
I wish he hadn't inveighed against my ideas so harshly. = 我真希望他不要如此严厉地抨击我的想法。


被动语态:
The government's policies were inveighed against by many citizens. = 许多公民强烈反对政府的政策。
His ideas were inveighed against by the critics. = 他的想法遭到评论家的强烈抨击。


疑问句:
Does he often inveigh against social injustices? = 他经常强烈抗议社会不公吗？
Will they inveigh against the new proposals? = 他们会强烈反对新的提案吗？


否定句:
He did not inveigh against the plan. = 他没有强烈反对这个计划。
She does not usually inveigh against others. = 她通常不会强烈抨击别人。


PHRASE->
inveigh against injustice = 抨击不公
inveigh against the government = 抨击政府
inveigh against corruption = 抨击腐败
inveigh against the war = 抨击战争
inveigh bitterly against = 猛烈抨击


词源:
From Old French *enveier, from Late Latin *invectare, frequentative of Latin vectare "to carry, convey," from vehere "to carry."  The original sense was "to carry oneself violently against," hence "to attack vehemently."
