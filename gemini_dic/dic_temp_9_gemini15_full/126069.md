mustering:/ˈmʌstərɪŋ/| v.|集合；鼓起(勇气)；积聚|The act of assembling or gathering together, often people or resources; also, the act of summoning up courage or strength.|例句：The general was mustering his troops for battle. 将军正在集合他的部队准备战斗。

例句：She mustered the courage to speak to her boss. 她鼓起勇气跟老板说话。


近义词：gathering, assembling, summoning, accumulating, summoning up|反义词：dispersing, scattering, dissipating|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is mustering his strength for the marathon. 他正在为马拉松储备力量。
They are mustering support for the new project. 他们正在为新项目争取支持。


一般过去时:
The farmer mustered his cattle for the winter. 农民把他的牛群聚集起来过冬。
She mustered all her energy to finish the race. 她竭尽全力完成了比赛。


现在完成时:
The army has mustered its forces at the border.  军队已在边境集结兵力。
He has mustered the courage to finally tell her the truth. 他终于鼓起勇气告诉她真相。


过去完成时:
They had mustered their resources before embarking on the journey. 他们在启程之前已经积攒好了资源。
She had mustered her courage before facing the difficult conversation. 在面对那场艰难的谈话之前，她已经鼓足了勇气。


将来时:
The team will muster their skills for the upcoming competition.  团队将为即将到来的比赛磨练技能。
He will muster the necessary funds to start his business. 他将筹集必要的资金来创办他的企业。


虚拟语气:
If I could muster the courage, I would confront him. 如果我能鼓起勇气，我会去面对他。
If she had mustered more support, she would have won the election. 如果她能获得更多支持，她就会赢得选举。


被动语态:
The troops were mustered at dawn. 部队在黎明时分被集合起来。
The resources were mustered for the relief effort. 资源被调集起来用于救济工作。


疑问句:
Are they mustering enough support for the campaign? 他们为这次竞选集结了足够的支持吗？
Will the company be able to muster the necessary funds? 公司能够筹集到必要的资金吗？


否定句:
He couldn't muster the enthusiasm for the project. 他对这个项目提不起劲。
They failed to muster enough votes to pass the bill. 他们未能获得足够的选票以通过法案。
