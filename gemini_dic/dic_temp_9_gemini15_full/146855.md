overindulgent:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒənt/ | adj. | 过度放纵的；纵容的 | Excessively lenient or permissive; characterized by excessive indulgence.  |  例句：His overindulgent parenting style led to his son's spoiled behavior.  他的过度溺爱导致儿子被宠坏了。


例句：The overindulgent dessert buffet left many guests feeling ill.  过度丰盛的甜点自助餐让许多客人感到不适。


近义词：permissive, indulgent, lenient, lax | 反义词：strict, austere, disciplined, restrained


词源：over- (表示程度) + indulgent (纵容的，放纵的)  "indulgent" 源自于拉丁语 "indulgere"，意思是“迁就；放纵”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is overindulgent with his children. = 他对他的孩子们过于溺爱。
She is overindulgent in her spending habits. = 她在花钱方面过于放纵。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
She has been overindulgent lately. = 她最近一直过于放纵。
He has been overindulgent with his eating habits. = 他一直以来在饮食习惯上过于放纵。


过去完成时:
