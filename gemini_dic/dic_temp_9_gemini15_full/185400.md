retrospected:/ˌretrəˈspektɪd/| v.|回顾，追溯|To consider or examine (past events, actions, etc.) again; review retrospectively.|例句：Historians have retrospectively analyzed the causes of the war. 历史学家们回顾性地分析了战争的起因。

例句：She retrospectively regretted her hasty decision. 她事后后悔自己仓促的决定。

近义词：reviewed, reconsidered, reexamined

反义词：prospected (展望未来)
