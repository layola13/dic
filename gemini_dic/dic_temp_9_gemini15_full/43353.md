cranberry:/ˈkrænˌbɛri/| n. |蔓越莓，小红莓|A small, tart, red berry used in sauces, jellies, and juices.  Cranberries are native to North America and are known for their high antioxidant content.|例句：I made a cranberry sauce for Thanksgiving.  我做了蔓越莓酱来庆祝感恩节。

例句：Cranberry juice is a popular drink. 蔓越莓汁是一种很受欢迎的饮料。|近义词： lingonberry (similar taste and use) |反义词：None (no direct antonym)|名词
