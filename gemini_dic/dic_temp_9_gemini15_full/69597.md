/fɛm/ | adj. | 女性的；雌性的 | Feminine means relating to or characteristic of women or girls.  It can also refer to the female sex or gender. | Example: She has a very feminine style. 她有一种非常女性化的风格。|近义词：womanly, female, girly | 反义词：masculine, male
