doomster:/ˈduːmstər/| n. |厄运预言者，灾难预言家|A person who habitually predicts disaster or doom.  Someone who constantly focuses on and predicts negative outcomes. They often exaggerate the likelihood and severity of potential problems.|例句：The doomster on the news predicted economic collapse. 新闻中的厄运预言家预测经济崩溃。

例句：He's become a bit of a doomster lately, always talking about the end of the world. 他最近有点像个厄运预言家，总是谈论世界末日。


近义词：pessimist, prophet of doom, Cassandra, naysayer

反义词：optimist, visionary


词性: 名词
