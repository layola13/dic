jackpudding:/ˈdʒækˌpʊdɪŋ/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋，滑稽人物|A jackpudding is a fool or clown, often a buffoonish or ludicrous character in a play or other performance. The term derives from the historical practice of stuffing a pudding with a small, roasted pig, giving it a grotesque appearance.  This image was linked to the clumsy and ridiculous behavior of a fool.|He played the jackpudding in the Shakespearean comedy, falling about comically and making silly jokes.|近义词：fool, buffoon, clown, simpleton|反义词：genius, intellectual, sage
