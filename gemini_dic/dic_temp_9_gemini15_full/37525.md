commonage:/ˈkɒmənɪdʒ/| n. |共有地；公共牧场；共有权|The right of the inhabitants of a village or other community to use a piece of land for grazing animals, cutting wood, etc.  It refers to the land itself and the right to use it. |Example:  The villagers enjoyed the commonage rights granted to them centuries ago.  村民们享受着几个世纪前赋予他们的共有地权利。  The sheep grazed peacefully on the commonage.羊群在共有地上平静地吃草。|近义词：common land, communal land, pasture|反义词：private property, exclusive right|
