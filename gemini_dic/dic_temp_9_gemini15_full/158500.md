plenishing:/ˈplɛnɪʃɪŋ/| v.|补充；再填充；使丰满|The act of replenishing something; making something full or complete again.  It implies restoring something to a previous state or level.|例句：The farmer was busy replenishing the haystacks after the animals had eaten most of it. = 农民忙着在动物吃掉大部分干草后补充干草堆。

例句：The company is replenishing its stock of winter coats in anticipation of colder weather. = 公司正在补充冬季外套的库存，以应对即将到来的寒冷天气。


近义词：restock, refill, replenish, supplement, top up

反义词：deplete, exhaust, empty


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She replenishes her water bottle every morning. = 她每天早上都会补充她的水瓶。
The store replenishes its shelves daily. = 这家商店每天都会补充货架上的商品。


一般过去时:
He replenished the coffee pot after everyone had a cup. = 大家喝完咖啡后，他补充了咖啡壶。
They replenished the dwindling supply of food. = 他们补充了日益减少的食物供应。


现在完成时:
We have replenished our supplies for the camping trip. = 我们已经补充了野营旅行的物资。
She has replenished her savings account. = 她已经补充了她的储蓄账户。


过去完成时:
They had replenished the fuel tanks before starting the long journey. = 他们在开始漫长的旅程之前已经补充了油箱。
The hikers had replenished their water bottles at the stream. = 登山者们已经在溪流边补充了水瓶。


将来时:
We will replenish the flower bed with new plants next spring. = 我们明年春天将用新植物补充花坛。
The company will replenish its inventory by the end of the month. = 公司将在月底前补充库存。


虚拟语气:
If they replenished their stock, they would have more sales. = 如果他们补充库存，他们的销售额就会更多。
I wish I had replenished my gas tank earlier. = 我真希望我早点加满油箱。


被动语态:
The water tanks were replenished by the city workers. = 水箱由城市工人补充。
The shelves are being replenished throughout the day. = 货架全天都在补充商品。


疑问句:
Will you replenish the sugar bowl? = 你会补充糖罐吗？
Have they replenished the stock yet? = 他们已经补充库存了吗？


否定句:
They did not replenish the supplies before leaving. = 他们离开前没有补充物资。
He hasn't replenished the ink cartridge yet. = 他还没有补充墨盒。
