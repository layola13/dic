occurs:/əˈkɜːrz/|/ˈɑːkɜːrz/| v. |发生；出现；存在|To happen; to take place; to be found or present.|The accident occurred at 3pm.事故发生在下午3点。|The problem occurs frequently. 这个问题经常发生。|happen, take place, arise|cease, disappear, vanish|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
This often occurs in the spring. 这经常发生在春天。
The symptom occurs in about 10% of patients.  这种症状大约出现在10%的病人身上。

一般过去时:
The earthquake occurred yesterday. 地震发生在昨天。
The meeting occurred in the afternoon. 会议在下午举行。

现在完成时:
The problem has occurred several times this month.  这个问题这个月已经发生过几次了。
A misunderstanding has occurred between them. 他们之间发生了误解。

过去完成时:
The accident had occurred before the police arrived.  警察到达之前事故已经发生了。
He realized that a mistake had occurred. 他意识到发生了错误。

将来时:
The event will occur next week.  活动将在下周举行。
The eclipse will occur at dawn. 日食将在黎明时分发生。

虚拟语气:
If this should occur, we will have a backup plan. 如果发生这种情况，我们会有一个备用计划。
I wish such a thing would never occur. 我希望这样的事情永远不会发生。

被动语态:
The meeting was said to have occurred in secret.据说会议秘密举行。
It was later discovered that an error had occurred in the calculations.后来发现计算中出现了错误。

疑问句:
When did the incident occur? 事件是什么时候发生的？
Where did the accident occur? 事故发生在哪里？

否定句:
The problem did not occur again. 问题没有再次发生。
The error did not occur during the test.  测试期间没有发生错误。
