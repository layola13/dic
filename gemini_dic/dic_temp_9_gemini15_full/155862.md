photically: /foʊˈtɪkəli/ | adv. | 光学地；以光学方式 |  Relating to or produced by light.  This adverb describes something that is done or happens using or relating to light.  It often appears in scientific or technical contexts.


例句：

The experiment was conducted photically, using lasers to stimulate the cells. = 实验是通过光学方法进行的，使用激光刺激细胞。

The image was created photically, not digitally. = 这张图像是通过光学方式创建的，而不是数字方式。


近义词：optically, luminously

反义词：mechanically, acoustically


词性：副词
