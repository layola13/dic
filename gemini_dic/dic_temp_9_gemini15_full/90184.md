hotspot:/ˈhɑːtspɑːt/ /ˈhɑːtspɑːt/ | n. |热点；焦点；热点地区；无线网络热点 |A hotspot is a place where something important or interesting is happening, or a small area with a high concentration of something.  It can refer to a geographical location, a specific issue, or a wireless network access point. |例句：The city became a hotspot for tourists after the new museum opened.  这座城市在新的博物馆开业后成为了旅游热点。

例句：The coffee shop is a hotspot for local artists to meet and collaborate. 这家咖啡馆是当地艺术家聚会和合作的热点。


近义词：focus, center, focal point, access point |反义词：cold spot, backwater
