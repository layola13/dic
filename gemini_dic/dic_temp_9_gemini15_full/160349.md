pootles:/ˈpuːtəlz/ | v. |闲逛，漫无目的地走动；笨手笨脚地做事 | to move around in a leisurely or aimless way; to do something clumsily or inefficiently |例句：He pootles around the garden all afternoon. 他整个下午都在花园里闲逛。 She pootled about trying to fix the broken chair. 她笨手笨脚地修理那把坏椅子。 |近义词：wander, potter, dawdle, fiddle |反义词：hurry, rush


USAGE->

He pootles around the park every morning. = 他每天早上都在公园里闲逛。
She pootles around the house, tidying up. = 她在家四处走动，整理东西。

被动语态: (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice.)  The passive voice is generally avoided because it sounds unnatural.
