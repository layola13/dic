episcopacy:/ɪˈpɪskəpəsi/| n.|主教制度；主教职位；主教管辖区 | The system of church government in which bishops have authority.  The office or jurisdiction of a bishop. |例句：The early church debated the best form of episcopacy. 早期教会就最佳的主教制度进行了辩论。|例句：His episcopacy was marked by significant reforms. 他的主教任期以重大改革为标志。|近义词：bishopric, diocese, prelacy |反义词：presbyterianism
