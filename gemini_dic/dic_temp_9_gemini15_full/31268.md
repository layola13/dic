chapbook:/ˈtʃæpbʊk/| n. |小册子，诗歌集 |A small book, typically paperback, containing a collection of poems, short stories, or other literary works.  Often self-published or produced in limited editions.|例句：She self-published a chapbook of her poetry. = 她自出版了一本她的诗歌小册子。

例句：The local bookstore featured a chapbook by a rising young author. = 当地书店展售了一位冉冉升起的新秀作者的小册子。


近义词：pamphlet, booklet, broadside |反义词：tome, volume (referring to large books)


词性：名词
