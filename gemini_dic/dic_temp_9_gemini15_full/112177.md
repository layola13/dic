looking-out: /ˈlʊkɪŋ aʊt/ | v. | 向外看；当心；留神 |  Looking out refers to the act of watching or observing something from a position inside, often implying a degree of anticipation or caution.  It can also mean being vigilant or careful. | 例句：He was looking out the window at the rain. 他正在向窗外看着雨。

He's always looking out for his younger brother. 他总是很照顾他的弟弟。


近义词：watching, observing, guarding, heeding | 反义词：ignoring, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are looking out for dangerous animals. = 他们在当心危险的动物。
We look out the window every morning. = 我们每天早上都向窗外看。


一般过去时:
She looked out and saw a beautiful sunset. = 她向外看，看到美丽的日落。
They looked out for each other during the difficult times. = 在困难时期，他们互相照顾。


现在完成时:
I have been looking out for a new job. = 我一直在寻找一份新工作。
We have looked out for signs of trouble. = 我们一直在注意是否有麻烦的迹象。


过去完成时:
They had been looking out for the bus for an hour before it arrived. = 公交车到达之前，他们已经等了一个小时了。
She had looked out the window many times before she saw him. = 在看到他之前，她已经多次向窗外看过。


将来时:
I will be looking out for my friend at the airport. = 我将在机场等候我的朋友。
We will look out for any potential problems. = 我们会注意任何潜在的问题。


虚拟语气:
If I were looking out, I would have seen the accident. = 如果我当时在向外看，我就看到事故了。
I wish I had been looking out more carefully. = 我希望我当时能更仔细地留神。


被动语态:
The house is being looked out for by the security guard. = 安全警卫正在看守房子。
The children were being looked out for by their parents. = 孩子们由他们的父母照看着。


疑问句:
Are you looking out for anything in particular? = 你在特别注意什么吗？
Was he looking out the window when the accident happened? = 事故发生时，他正在向窗外看吗？


否定句:
He wasn't looking out when he crashed into the tree. = 他撞树时没有留神。
They didn't look out for the signs of danger. = 他们没有注意危险的迹象。
