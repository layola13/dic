papain:/pəˈpeɪɪn/| n.|木瓜蛋白酶|A proteolytic enzyme (a protein-digesting enzyme) found in papaya fruit, used in meat tenderizers and as a digestive aid.|例句：Papain is a powerful enzyme that breaks down proteins. 木瓜蛋白酶是一种强大的酶，可以分解蛋白质。  Papain can be used to tenderize meat. 木瓜蛋白酶可以用来嫩化肉类。|近义词：bromelain, ficin|反义词:None
