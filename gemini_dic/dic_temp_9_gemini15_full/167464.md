preventable:/prɪˈvɛntəbl/| adj. | 可预防的，可避免的 |  capable of being prevented or avoided.  | Example:  Many illnesses are preventable through vaccination.  许多疾病可以通过接种疫苗来预防。

Many accidents are preventable if people are careful.  如果人们小心谨慎，许多事故是可以避免的。|近义词：avoidable, avertable |反义词：inevitable, unavoidable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This disease is preventable. = 这种疾病是可以预防的。
Regular exercise is preventable of heart disease. = 定期锻炼可以预防心脏病。

一般过去时:
The accident was preventable. = 这场事故是可以避免的。
That mistake was preventable with more careful planning. = 如果计划更仔细，那个错误是可以避免的。

现在完成时:
Many deaths have been preventable through better public health measures. = 通过更好的公共卫生措施，许多死亡本来是可以避免的。
The damage has been preventable if we had acted sooner. = 如果我们早点采取行动，损害是可以避免的。

过去完成时:
The fire could have been preventable if the alarm had worked properly. = 如果警报器正常工作，这场火灾是可以避免的。
The injury would have been preventable if he had worn a helmet. = 如果他戴着头盔，受伤是可以避免的。


将来时:
Future illnesses will be preventable with advancements in medical technology. = 随着医疗技术的进步，未来的疾病将是可以预防的。
This problem will be preventable if we address the root cause. = 如果我们解决根本原因，这个问题是可以避免的。


虚拟语气:
If we had taken precautions, the accident would have been preventable. = 如果我们采取预防措施，事故是可以避免的。
I wish that the tragedy had been preventable. = 我希望这场悲剧是可以避免的。

被动语态:
Many diseases are being shown to be preventable through lifestyle changes. = 许多疾病正被证明可以通过改变生活方式来预防。
The disaster could have been prevented. = 这场灾难本来是可以避免的。

疑问句:
Is this condition preventable? = 这种情况是可以预防的吗？
Are all accidents preventable? = 所有事故都是可以避免的吗？

否定句:
The damage was not preventable. = 损害是不可避免的。
That outcome was unfortunately not preventable. = 不幸的是，那个结果是不可避免的。
