hoopoe:/ˈhuːpuː/| n. | hoopoe (鸟类) | A hoopoe is a bird with a distinctive long, slender bill, a crested head, and brightly colored plumage.  It is known for its distinctive call and its habit of probing the ground for insects. |  The hoopoe is a beautiful bird with a striking crest. =  hoopoe 是一种有着醒目羽冠的美丽鸟类。
The hoopoe’s call is easily recognizable. = hoopoe 的叫声很容易辨认。| 近义词：upupa (学名)| 反义词：无
