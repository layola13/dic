scatter-gun:/ˈskætəɡʌn/| n. |散弹枪；随意射击|A shotgun that fires many small shot pellets over a wide area.  It is often used figuratively to describe something done haphazardly or without much aim.|例句：He fired a scatter-gun at the flock of birds. 他向那群鸟随意射击。 The government's approach to the problem was a scatter-gun approach, lacking focus. 政府解决这个问题的方法是散弹枪式的，缺乏重点。|近义词：shotgun, random shooting|反义词：precise shooting, targeted approach|名词
