fanfares:/ˈfænˌfɛrz/| n.| 嘹亮的鼓乐声；欢呼声 | A fanfare is a short, loud, and triumphant flourish of trumpets or other brass instruments, typically played to announce the arrival of a person of importance or to mark a special occasion.  It can also refer to a display of triumphant or boastful public acclaim. |例句：The arrival of the king was heralded by a fanfare of trumpets. 国王的到来伴随着嘹亮的喇叭声。

The orchestra began the concert with a spectacular fanfare. 乐队以壮观的号角声开始了音乐会。


近义词：flourish, blare, fanfare,  trumpet call,  jubilation

反义词：silence, hush, quiet
