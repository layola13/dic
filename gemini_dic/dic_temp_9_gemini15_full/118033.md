menaced:/ˈmɛnəst/| vt. |威胁，恐吓|To threaten to harm or damage someone or something.  The word often implies a sense of impending danger or a feeling of being threatened.|例句：The dark clouds menaced the peaceful village.  乌云威胁着宁静的村庄。

The storm menaced the coastal towns. 暴风雨威胁着沿海城镇。|近义词：threaten, intimidate, endanger |反义词：protect, safeguard, secure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He menaces his neighbours with violence. 他以暴力威胁他的邻居。
The rising floodwaters menace the city.  上涨的洪水威胁着这座城市。


一般过去时:
The bully menaced the smaller children.  那个恶霸威胁着那些较小的孩子。
The dark figure menaced her from the shadows.  一个黑暗的身影从阴影中威胁她。


现在完成时:
The ongoing drought has menaced the crops.  持续的干旱已经威胁到了庄稼。
His erratic behavior has menaced his career. 他反复无常的行为已经威胁到了他的事业。


过去完成时:
The pirates had menaced the shipping lanes for months.  海盗们几个月来一直威胁着航线。
The looming storm had menaced the coastal communities for days.  迫在眉睫的风暴几天来一直威胁着沿海社区。


将来时:
The approaching hurricane will menace the island.  即将到来的飓风将威胁该岛屿。
Rising sea levels will menace coastal populations. 海平面上升将威胁沿海居民。


虚拟语气:
If he menaced me again, I would call the police. 如果他再次威胁我，我会报警。
I wish the aggressive dog hadn't menaced the children. 我希望那条凶猛的狗没有威胁到孩子们。


被动语态:
The villagers were menaced by the wild animals. 村民们受到了野生动物的威胁。
The town was menaced by a severe storm.  这个城镇受到了严重风暴的威胁。


疑问句:
Does the situation menace our safety?  这种情况是否威胁到我们的安全？
Did the criminal menace anyone else?  罪犯是否威胁了其他人？


否定句:
The police presence prevented him from menacing the witness. 警方的存在阻止了他威胁证人。
The government's efforts successfully prevented the company from menacing the environment. 政府的努力成功地阻止了该公司威胁环境。
