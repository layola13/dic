quasi-negligent: /ˈkwɑːziˌnɛɡlɪdʒənt/ | adj. | 近乎疏忽的，近似过失的 |  Describing an action or omission that, while not meeting the full legal definition of negligence, shows a lack of reasonable care and could be considered careless or inattentive. It implies a degree of fault, though not necessarily sufficient to establish legal liability.  |  The doctor's quasi-negligent actions, while not technically malpractice, still caused the patient undue suffering.  |  careless, inattentive, remiss | diligent, careful, attentive | 形容词
