plays:/pleɪz/| n. v.|戏剧；游戏；演奏；扮演；发挥作用|

英文解析:
Noun:  A play is a dramatic work intended for performance in a theatre or on radio or television.  It can also refer to a piece of music written to be played on an instrument or a game or sport. As a verb, it means to perform a play, to operate or function, to gamble, or to engage in a sport or game.

例句:
Shakespeare's plays are still performed worldwide. = 莎士比亚的戏剧仍在世界各地演出。

The children were playing in the park. = 孩子们在公园里玩耍。


近义词:(Noun) drama, performance, game, sport,  (Verb) act, perform, operate, function


反义词: (dependent on context)  There isn't a direct antonym,  but depending on the context you might use words like "stops," "ceases," "fails," "ends," or  "inaction."
