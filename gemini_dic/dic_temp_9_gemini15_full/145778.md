overdate:/ˌoʊvərˈdeɪt/ | vt. |过期；(约会)迟到；| to make (something) later than the correct or intended date; to arrive late for a date |例句：The milk has overdated. 这牛奶过期了。  The documents were overdated by a month. 文件过期了一个月。 |近义词：expire, outdate |反义词：predate
