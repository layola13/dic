fortune: /ˈfɔːrtʃuːn/| /ˈfɔːrˌtʃuːn/ | n. |命运；运气；财富；巨款|

英文解析:
Fortune refers to luck, especially in the sense of good luck or unexpected good things happening. It can also refer to a large amount of money or wealth.  The word can also be used to describe one's fate or destiny, the events that happen to a person in their life.

例句:
She had the good fortune to meet the president. = 她很幸运地遇到了总统。

He made his fortune in the tech industry. = 他在科技行业赚了大钱。


近义词:
luck, chance, fate, destiny, wealth, riches

反义词:
misfortune, bad luck, poverty
