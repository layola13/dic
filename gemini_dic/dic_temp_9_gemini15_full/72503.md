fluorines:/ˈflɔːriːnz/| n. |氟（元素）|Fluorines are a chemical element, a pale yellow, highly reactive gas.  Fluorine is the most electronegative element, meaning it readily attracts electrons from other atoms.|The presence of fluorines in the compound significantly increased its reactivity.|近义词:fluoride (离子形式)|反义词:无
