fey:/feɪ/| adj.|古怪的；超自然的；精灵般的；轻浮的；|形容具有超自然或神秘气质的人或事物，也指轻浮或古怪的行为举止。  It can also describe something that is charmingly whimsical or otherworldly. |例句：The fey atmosphere of the forest unnerved her.  森林中超自然的氛围使她感到不安。

The fey creature danced in the moonlight.  那个精灵般的生物在月光下跳舞。


近义词：unworldly, ethereal, whimsical, capricious, ethereal, magical|反义词：mundane, ordinary, earthly, normal|词性：形容词
