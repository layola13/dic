exclusivism:/ɪkˈskluːzɪvɪzəm/| n. |排他主义；独占性；专有权|The belief in the inherent superiority of one's own group or kind and the corresponding desire to exclude others.  It often manifests in social, political, or religious contexts, emphasizing the importance of maintaining boundaries and limiting access to certain privileges or opportunities. |例句：The club's exclusivism kept out many deserving candidates. 这个俱乐部的排他主义挡住了许多有资格的候选人。

例句：Religious exclusivism can lead to intolerance and conflict. 宗教排他主义可能导致不容忍和冲突。


近义词：elitism, sectarianism, separationism |反义词：inclusiveness, universalism, egalitarianism
