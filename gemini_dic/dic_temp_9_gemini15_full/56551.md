domineering: /ˌdɒmɪˈnɪərɪŋ/ /ˌdɑːmɪˈnɪərɪŋ/ | adj. | 专横的；盛气凌人的；支配的 |

英文解析：Having or showing a tendency to dominate others; controlling.  Characterized by a tendency to assert oneself over others.

例句：

Her domineering mother controlled every aspect of her life.  她的专横的母亲控制着她生活的方方面面。

He has a domineering personality that intimidates many people. 他有着盛气凌人的性格，吓倒了许多人。


近义词：bossy, overbearing, controlling, tyrannical, imperious, autocratic

反义词：submissive, meek, yielding, gentle, deferential


词源:  From domineer + -ing.  "Domineer" itself comes from the Old French *dominer, from Latin *domināri,  "to be master," from dominus, "master, lord."  The suffix "-ing" forms an adjective.
