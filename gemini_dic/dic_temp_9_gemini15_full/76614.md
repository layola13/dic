gallon:/ˈɡælən/| n. |加仑|A unit of liquid measure equal to 4 quarts or 8 pints.  It is used primarily in the United States and the United Kingdom.  Different countries have different definitions of the gallon, although the US gallon and the imperial gallon are the most common. |例句：The recipe calls for two gallons of water.  这个食谱需要两加仑水。

例句：He filled the gas tank with five gallons of gasoline. 他加了五加仑汽油。


近义词：quart (smaller unit), liter (metric equivalent)
反义词：None (it's a unit of measurement, not something with a direct opposite)
词性: noun
