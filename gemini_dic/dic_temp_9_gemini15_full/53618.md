discase:/dɪsˈkeɪs/| n.|疾病，病痛|A disease is a disorder of structure or function in a human, animal, or plant, especially one that produces specific signs or symptoms or that affects a specific location and is not simply a direct result of physical injury.|例句：The doctor diagnosed the disease as pneumonia.  医生诊断出这种疾病是肺炎。|例句：Many plant diseases are caused by fungi. 许多植物病害是由真菌引起的。|近义词：illness, sickness, ailment, malady|反义词：health, wellness
