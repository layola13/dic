nonobedience:/ˌnɒnəˈbiːdiəns/ | n. |不服从；违抗 |The refusal to obey or comply with an order, rule, or law.  It often implies a conscious and deliberate act of defiance. |例句：Their nonobedience to the law resulted in severe penalties. 他们的不服从导致了严重的惩罚。 |例句：The teacher punished the student for his nonobedience. 老师因学生的违抗而惩罚了他。|近义词：disobedience, defiance, insubordination |反义词：obedience, compliance, submission |名词
