misspender:/mɪsˈspendər/| n. |挥霍者，浪费者 |A misspender is someone who spends money or resources carelessly or wastefully.  They don't manage their finances responsibly and often end up in debt. |He was a notorious misspender, always buying extravagant things he couldn't afford. 他是一个臭名昭著的挥霍者，总是买他买不起的奢侈品。|近义词：spendthrift, wastrel, profligate |反义词：saver, frugal person


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He misspends his money on frivolous things. = 他把钱浪费在一些琐碎的事情上。
She misspends her time on unproductive activities. = 她把时间浪费在没有成效的活动上。

一般过去时:
He misspent his inheritance in a matter of months. = 他在几个月内就挥霍了他的遗产。
