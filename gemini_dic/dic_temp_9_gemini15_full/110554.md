limestones:/ˈlaɪmˌstoʊnz/| n. |石灰岩|A sedimentary rock composed mainly of calcium carbonate (CaCO3), usually in the form of calcite, and often containing fossils. Limestones are formed from the accumulation of skeletal fragments of marine organisms, such as corals, mollusks, and foraminifera. They are widely used in construction, agriculture, and industry. |例句：The building was constructed from local limestones. 这座建筑是用当地的石灰岩建造的。
The cliffs were made of layers of different coloured limestones.悬崖是由不同颜色的石灰岩层构成的。

近义词：calcium carbonate rock, carbonate rock |反义词：无
