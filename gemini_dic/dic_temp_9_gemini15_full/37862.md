compilation:/ˌkɒmpɪˈleɪʃən/|ˈkɑːmpɪˈleɪʃən/| n. |汇编；编纂；合集；汇编程序|The act of compiling; a collection of things, especially writings, music, or data, gathered together to form a whole.|例句：This anthology is a compilation of her best poems. 这本选集是她最优秀诗歌的汇编。|例句：The software is a compilation of several different programs. 这个软件是几个不同程序的汇编。|近义词：collection, anthology, compendium |反义词：dispersion, scattering


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team is working on a compilation of their best hits. = 团队正在制作他们最热门歌曲的合辑。
This website provides a compilation of useful resources. = 该网站提供了一系列有用的资源汇编。
