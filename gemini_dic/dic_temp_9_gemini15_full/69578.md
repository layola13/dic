felsite:/ˈfɛlsaɪt/| n. |霏细岩|A very fine-grained, usually light-colored igneous rock, often containing small crystals of quartz and feldspar.  It is intermediate in composition between rhyolite and obsidian.  It's often difficult to distinguish from other fine-grained igneous rocks without microscopic examination.|The geologist identified the sample as felsite due to its fine grain size and light color.|近义词：rhyolite, aphanitic igneous rock|反义词：coarse-grained igneous rock
