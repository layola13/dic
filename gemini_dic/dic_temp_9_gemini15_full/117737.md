melanite:/ˈmɛlənaɪt/| n. |黑色钛铁矿|A black, opaque variety of andradite garnet, a calcium-iron silicate mineral.  It's typically found in volcanic rocks and metamorphic rocks.  Its color comes from the presence of iron. |The melanite sample showed a high iron content.  黑色钛铁矿样本显示出高铁含量。|andradite, garnet|  (no clear antonym)|名词
