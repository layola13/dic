fining:/ˈfaɪnɪŋ/| n. v.|罚款；精炼；澄清|The process of making something pure or refined, or imposing a fine.  It can refer to the act of imposing a fine as a punishment, or to the process of purifying a substance, such as wine or metal.|例句：The court imposed a hefty fining for speeding. 法院对他超速行驶处以巨额罚款。 The fining of the wine removed sediment and improved its clarity.  葡萄酒的澄清去除了沉淀物并提高了其清澈度。|近义词：purification, refinement, penalty, sanction|反义词：reward, compensation
