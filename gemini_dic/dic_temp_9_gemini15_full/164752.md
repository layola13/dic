prefecture:/ˈpriːfektʃər/| n.|县；府；管区|A prefecture is a region or division of a country, often a large administrative district.  It is a term used in various countries, particularly in Japan and France, with slightly different meanings depending on the context.|例句：Kyoto is a prefecture in Japan. 京都是日本的一个府。|近义词：province, district, region, county|反义词：N/A
