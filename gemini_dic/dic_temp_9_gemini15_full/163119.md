precolonial: /ˌpriːkəˈləʊniəl/ | adj. |殖民地之前的，殖民化以前的 | Relating to the period of time before a country or region was colonized.  It describes the conditions, cultures, and societies that existed before the arrival and impact of colonizers. |

例句：
The precolonial history of the region is complex and poorly documented. = 该地区的殖民地以前的历史复杂且记录不足。

The archaeologist discovered precolonial artifacts buried beneath the colonial city. = 考古学家发现了埋在殖民城市下面的殖民地时期之前的文物。


近义词：antecolonial, pre-imperial


反义词：postcolonial, colonial


词性：形容词
