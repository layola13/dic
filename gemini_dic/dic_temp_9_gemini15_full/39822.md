conspicuousnesses:/kənˈspɪkjuəsnɪsəz/ | n. |显著性；显眼之处 | The conspicuousnesses of the new building were immediately apparent. 新建筑的显著之处立刻显而易见。 | 近义词：prominences, noticeable features | 反义词：inconspicuousness, obscurity |


英文解析:The plural form of "conspicuousness," referring to the state or quality of being conspicuous; easily seen or noticed; readily visible.  It emphasizes the multiple instances or aspects of something being easily noticeable.


例句：

The conspicuousnesses of her dress and jewelry made her the center of attention at the party.  她的服装和珠宝的显著之处使她成为派对上的焦点。

Several conspicuousnesses in the data suggested a flaw in the experimental design. 数据中的一些显著之处表明实验设计存在缺陷。


词源:

From conspicuous + -ness (suffix denoting a state or quality).  "Conspicuous" itself comes from the Latin "conspicuus," meaning "easily seen," from "conspicere," meaning "to look at carefully," "to observe," composed of "con-" (together, thoroughly) and "specere" (to look, to see).
