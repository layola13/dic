ramequin:/ˈræmɪkɪn/| n.|小烤盘，小砂锅|A small, shallow, ovenproof dish, typically made of ceramic, used for baking individual portions of food, especially soufflés, custards, and gratins.|例句：She baked individual ramekins of chocolate mousse for dessert. 她烤了几个巧克力慕斯小烤盘作为甜点。


例句：The restaurant served its creamy mushroom soup in elegant ramekins. 这家餐厅用精致的小烤盘盛放奶油蘑菇汤。


近义词：ovenproof dish, small baking dish|反义词：large baking pan, roasting pan
