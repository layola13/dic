fumigant:/ˈfjuːmɪɡənt/ | n. |熏蒸剂，杀虫剂 | A substance used for fumigation; a gaseous pesticide.  |Example:  The farmer used a fumigant to control pests in his greenhouse.  这个农民用熏蒸剂来控制温室里的害虫。
The house was treated with a fumigant to eliminate termites. 这栋房子用熏蒸剂处理以消灭白蚁。|近义词：pesticide, insecticide, disinfectant |反义词：None specifically, but terms implying protection against fumigation could be considered antonyms in certain contexts.
