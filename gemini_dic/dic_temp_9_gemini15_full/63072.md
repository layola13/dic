enroot:/ɪnˈruːt/ | vt. |生根；使扎根；固定|To establish or become firmly fixed or embedded; to take root.  |例句：The cutting enrooted successfully in the fertile soil.  扦插在肥沃的土壤中成功生根了。|例句：The new ideas quickly enrooted themselves in the minds of the students.  新的思想迅速在学生的头脑中扎根。|近义词：establish, implant, embed |反义词：uproot, eradicate, remove
