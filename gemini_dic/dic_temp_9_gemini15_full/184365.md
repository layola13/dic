respirometer:/rɛˈspaɪrəˌmiːtər/ | n. |呼吸测量器|An instrument for measuring the rate or volume of respiration.  It is used to study respiration in plants and animals. |The scientists used a respirometer to measure the oxygen consumption of the mice. 科学家们用呼吸测量器测量老鼠的耗氧量。| respirograph, gasometer |  (无明确反义词) | 名词
