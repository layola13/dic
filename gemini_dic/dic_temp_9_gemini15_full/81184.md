grandfathers:/ˈɡrænfɑːðərz/ | n. | 祖父们；外祖父们 | The plural form of "grandfather," referring to multiple paternal or maternal grandfathers.  It is used to describe a group of men who are the fathers of one's parents. | Example: My grandfathers were both skilled carpenters.  我的祖父们都是熟练的木匠。  My wife's grandfathers came from different countries. 我妻子的外祖父们来自不同的国家。|近义词:forefathers (in a broader sense), ancestors|反义词:grandchildren
