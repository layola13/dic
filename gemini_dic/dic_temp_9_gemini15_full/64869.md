esprit:/ˈɛspriː/| n. |精神；才智；气质|A lively intelligence or spirit; the animating spirit of a person, group, or movement.  It often implies a distinctive character or quality. |例句：The team showed great esprit de corps.  团队展现了极强的团队精神。  The artist's work is full of esprit.  这位艺术家的作品充满了精神活力。|近义词：spirit, intelligence, vivacity, flair, panache|反义词：dullness, apathy, listlessness
