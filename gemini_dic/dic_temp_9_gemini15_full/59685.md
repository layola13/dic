echoingly:/ˈɛkoʊɪŋli/ | adv. | 回响地，回荡地，响亮地 |  The word "echoingly" describes something that produces a resounding or echoing effect. It suggests a strong and noticeable resonance or reverberation.  It can refer to sounds, but also to the impact or feeling of something. |例句：His words echoedingly filled the vast hall.  他的话语回荡在巨大的大厅里。

The announcement was made echoingly, ensuring everyone heard it.  公告响亮地宣布，确保每个人都听到了。


近义词：resoundingly, resonantly, reverberantly | 反义词：quietly, softly, faintly | 词性：副词
