redented:/rɪˈdentɪd/ | adj. | 凹进的，缩进的 |  Indented means set back from the margin, especially the first line of a paragraph.  It refers to a space or gap at the beginning of a line of text or other element. | Example: The first paragraph was indented five spaces.  第一个段落缩进了五个空格。 |近义词：recessed, inset | 反义词：protruding, projecting

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The first line is indented. =  第一行是缩进的。
The paragraph is indented. = 段落是缩进的。


一般过去时:
The writer indented the first line. = 作者将第一行缩进了。
The editor indented each paragraph. = 编辑将每个段落都缩进了。


现在完成时:
The document has been indented correctly. =  文档已经正确地缩进了。
He has indented all the paragraphs. = 他已经将所有段落都缩进了。


过去完成时:
She had indented the quote before submitting the paper. = 她提交论文前已经将引语缩进了。
They had indented the headings for better readability. = 为了更好的可读性，他们已经将标题缩进了。


将来时:
I will indent the next paragraph. = 我将缩进下一段。
The software will automatically indent the code. = 该软件将自动缩进代码。


虚拟语气:
If I were writing a letter, I would indent the paragraphs. = 如果我写信，我会将段落缩进。
I wish I had indented the bullet points. = 我希望我已经将项目符号缩进了。


被动语态:
The text was indented using a tab. = 文本使用制表符进行了缩进。
The lines are indented by the program. = 行由程序缩进。


疑问句:
Is the first line indented? = 第一行是缩进的吗？
Are the paragraphs properly indented? = 段落是否正确缩进？


否定句:
The paragraph is not indented. = 段落没有缩进。
The lines are not indented correctly. = 行没有正确缩进。


PHRASE->
Indented margin: 缩进的页边距
Indented paragraph: 缩进的段落
Indented quote: 缩进的引号
Indented list: 缩进的列表
Indented code: 缩进的代码
