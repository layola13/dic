fantoccini:/ˌfæntəˈtʃiːni/| n. |木偶戏；傀儡戏|Small puppets, often manipulated by strings, used in puppet shows.  They are typically made of wood or other lightweight materials.  The term often implies a sense of charm or quaintness.|例句：The children were mesmerized by the colourful fantoccini show. 孩子们被色彩鲜艳的木偶戏迷住了。


例句：The puppeteer skillfully manipulated the fantoccini, bringing them to life. 木偶师熟练地操纵着木偶，使它们栩栩如生。

近义词：puppets, marionettes

反义词：actors (live performers)

词性:名词
