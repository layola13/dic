dissemblingly:/dɪˈsɛmblɪŋli/| adv. |虚伪地；假装地 |In a way that hides one's true feelings or intentions.  It suggests a deliberate attempt to deceive through pretense. |例句：He smiled dissemblingly, hiding his true anger. 他虚伪地笑着，掩盖了他真正的愤怒。

例句：She answered dissemblingly, avoiding the difficult question. 她闪烁其词地回答，避开了那个棘手的问题。


近义词：deceitfully, deceptively, hypocritically, insincerely, falsely


反义词：honestly, truthfully, sincerely, openly, frankly


词性:副词
