molasse:/məˈlæs/| n. |软岩，磨拉石|A soft, loosely consolidated sedimentary rock, typically consisting of clay, silt, and sand, often deposited in a foreland basin.  It is usually poorly cemented and easily eroded.|The Swiss Alps are underlain by thick sequences of molasse. 瑞士阿尔卑斯山下方覆盖着厚厚的磨拉石层。  The molasse deposits are evidence of intense erosion in the nearby mountain ranges. 磨拉石沉积物是附近山脉强烈侵蚀的证据。|conglomerate, shale |hard rock|名词
