rhizostome:/ˈraɪzəstoʊm/| n. |根口动物，根口纲动物 |A rhizostome is a type of jellyfish belonging to the order Rhizostomeae, characterized by their branched oral arms used for feeding. They lack separate tentacles and have a distinctive appearance. | Example:  The diver observed a large rhizostome pulsating gently in the current.  潜水员观察到一个大型根口动物在水流中轻轻地搏动。 |近义词: jellyfish (general term) |反义词: N/A
