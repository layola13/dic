cocultivates:/koʊˈkʌltɪveɪts/| vt.| 共同培养；一起培养 | To grow or cultivate (plants or cells) together in the same environment. This is often done to study the interactions between different organisms or to improve the growth of one organism by the presence of another. |例句：Researchers cocultivated human cells with bacteria to investigate their effects on immune responses. 研究人员将人体细胞与细菌共同培养以研究它们对免疫反应的影响。


例句：The scientists cocultivated plant cells to enhance the production of secondary metabolites. 科学家们共同培养植物细胞以增强次生代谢产物的产生。


近义词：cultivate together, grow together, raise together| 反义词：separate, isolate
