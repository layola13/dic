jacks:/dʒæks/ | n. |千斤顶；插孔；游戏；|a device used to lift heavy objects; a small socket or hole; a children's game.|例句：He used a jack to change the tire. 他用千斤顶换轮胎。
例句：The electrical jack is broken. 电插座坏了。


词源:源自古法语jacque,  最终源于拉丁语jaccus, 指的是一种小型船只或驳船，后来演变为指代支撑或提升重物的工具。


用法说明:


名词用法:

1. 千斤顶:  A mechanical device used to lift heavy objects, such as cars or trucks.
2. 插孔: A small socket or hole, often for electrical connections.
3. 游戏: A children's card game.


例句：

He needed a jack to lift his car. 他需要一个千斤顶来抬起他的车。

The wall socket is also called a jack. 墙上的插座也叫做插孔。


近义词: (取决于具体含义) hoist, lift, elevator (千斤顶); socket, outlet (插孔);  game (游戏)


反义词: (无直接反义词，取决于具体含义)
