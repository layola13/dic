redevelop:/ˌriːdɪˈvɛləp/ | vt. | 再开发；重建；重新发展 | To develop again or differently; to improve or renovate an area or building.  |例句：The city plans to redevelop the waterfront area.  城市计划重建滨水区。

例句：They redeveloped the old factory into a modern apartment complex. 他们把旧工厂改建成了现代化的公寓楼。


近义词：rebuild, renovate, regenerate, refurbish | 反义词：destroy, demolish, raze | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The city redevelops brownfield sites. = 城市对褐地进行再开发。
They redevelop old buildings into modern offices. = 他们将旧建筑改建成现代化的办公室。

一般过去时:
The company redeveloped the software last year. = 公司去年重新开发了该软件。
They redeveloped the park after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，他们重新开发了公园。

现在完成时:
The government has redeveloped many areas of the city. = 政府已经对城市许多地区进行了重建。
They have redeveloped their marketing strategy.  他们已经重新制定了他们的营销策略。

过去完成时:
They had redeveloped the website before the launch. = 他们在网站上线之前已经重新开发了它。
The architect had redeveloped the plans several times. = 建筑师已经修改了几次设计图纸。

将来时:
The council will redevelop the old town square. = 市议会将重建旧城广场。
They will redevelop their product line next year. = 他们明年将重新开发他们的产品线。


虚拟语气:
If they redeveloped the school, it would be much better. = 如果他们重建学校，学校将会好得多。
I wish they would redevelop that derelict building. = 我希望他们能重建那栋废弃的建筑。

被动语态:
The area is being redeveloped. = 该地区正在重新开发。
The old building is to be redeveloped into a hotel. = 这栋旧楼将被改建成一家酒店。

疑问句:
Will the government redevelop the port? = 政府会重建港口吗？
Are they planning to redevelop the shopping centre? = 他们计划重建购物中心吗？

否定句:
They did not redevelop the project. = 他们没有重新开发这个项目。
The company will not redevelop the software this year. = 公司今年不会重新开发该软件。
