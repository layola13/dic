inconsiderateness:/ˌɪnkənˈsɪdərətnəs/| n. |不体贴；粗心；考虑不周 |Lack of consideration for the needs or feelings of others.  It often manifests as thoughtless actions or words that disregard the impact on others. |Example: His inconsiderateness in playing loud music late at night angered his neighbors.  他的粗心大意在深夜播放大声音乐激怒了他的邻居。  His inconsiderateness towards his colleagues made him unpopular. 他对同事的不体贴让他不受欢迎。|近义词：thoughtlessness, disregard, insensitivity, selfishness |反义词：consideration, thoughtfulness, sensitivity, empathy
