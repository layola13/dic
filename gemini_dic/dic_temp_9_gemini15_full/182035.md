remember:/rɪˈmɛmbər/ | vt. | 记得，想起，牢记 | To keep something in your memory; to recall something from the past.  | I remember my childhood. 我记得我的童年。|  I remember to lock the door. 我记得要锁门。| recall, recollect | forget | verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I remember my friend's birthday. = 我记得我朋友的生日。
She remembers the details of the accident. = 她记得事故的细节。


一般过去时:
I remembered to call my mother. = 我记得给妈妈打电话了。
He remembered seeing that movie years ago. = 他记得几年前看过那部电影。


现在完成时:
I have remembered to bring my keys. = 我记得带钥匙了。
She has remembered all the names. = 她记住了所有名字。


过去完成时:
I had remembered to book the tickets. = 我记得已经预订了票。
She had remembered the instructions before starting. = 她开始之前已经记住了说明。


将来时:
I will remember this day forever. = 我会永远记住这一天。
They will remember to bring the presents. = 他们会记得带礼物的。


虚拟语气:
I wish I could remember his name. = 我真希望我能记得他的名字。
If I remembered the address, I would visit her. = 如果我记得地址，我会去看她。


被动语态:
The event will be remembered for years to come. = 这件事将会被人们记住很多年。
His kindness will be remembered by all. = 他的善良将会被所有人铭记。


疑问句:
Do you remember where you parked the car? = 你记得你把车停在哪儿了吗？
Does she remember her childhood home? = 她记得她的童年家吗？


否定句:
I don't remember his phone number. = 我不记得他的电话号码。
She doesn't remember meeting him before. = 她不记得以前见过他。


PHRASE->
remember me to... = 代我向...问好
remember when... = 还记得...的时候吗
remember that... = 记住...
I'll never forget/remember  = 我永远不会忘记
as I remember = 据我记得


词源:
The word "remember" comes from Old English "remynnan," which is a combination of the prefix "re-" (again) and "mynnan" (to be mindful, to think).  It reflects the act of bringing something back to mind.
