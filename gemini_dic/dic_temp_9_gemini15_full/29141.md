caseload:/ˈkeɪsloʊd/| n.|工作量；案件数量|The amount of work or the number of cases a person or organization is responsible for.  It often refers to the number of clients or cases a social worker, lawyer, or doctor handles.|例句：The social worker has a heavy caseload this month.  这个月社工的工作量很大。|例句：The judge's caseload increased significantly after the new law was passed. 新法律通过后，法官的案件数量显著增加。|近义词：workload, burden, volume|反义词：light workload, ease
