pre-book:/ˌpriːˈbʊk/ | vt. | 预订，提前预订 | To reserve or arrange for something in advance.  | 例句：I pre-booked my flight tickets to avoid higher prices. 我提前预定了我的机票以避免更高的价格。

例句：We pre-booked a table at the restaurant for our anniversary dinner. 我们提前预订了周年纪念晚餐的餐桌。


近义词：reserve, book in advance, arrange beforehand | 反义词：cancel, postpone


词性:动词
