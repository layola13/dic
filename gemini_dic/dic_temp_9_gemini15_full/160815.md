possessed: /pəˈzɛst/ | adj., v. | 拥有；被附身；着迷的 |

英文解析: As an adjective, "possessed" means controlled or dominated by a strong emotion or feeling, or inhabited by a spirit or demon. As a verb, it means to have or own something.

例句:

adj. (形容词):
He was possessed by a sudden rage.  = 他被突如其来的愤怒控制住了。

She seemed possessed by a restless energy. = 她似乎被一种不安的能量所支配。


v. (动词):
He possessed a vast collection of stamps. = 他拥有大量的邮票收藏。

They possessed the land for generations. = 他们世世代代拥有这片土地。


近义词: (adj.) obsessed, controlled, haunted; (v.) owned, held, controlled


反义词: (adj.) unpossessed, calm, free; (v.) lacked, relinquished


词性: adjective, verb


USAGE->

将来时:
