knitted:/ˈnɪtɪd/ | adj. | 编织的；针织的 |  Made of knitted fabric; having been created using knitting needles.  | Example:  She wore a knitted sweater. 她穿着一件针织衫。  |近义词: woven, crocheted | 反义词: unknitted, woven (in the sense of a contrasting technique)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The baby is knitted a warm blanket. = 婴儿被织了一条暖和的毯子。
This sweater is knitted from pure wool. = 这件毛衣是用纯羊毛编织的。

一般过去时:
Grandma knitted me a scarf last winter. = 奶奶去年冬天给我织了一条围巾。
She knitted a beautiful dress for her daughter. = 她为女儿织了一件漂亮的连衣裙。

现在完成时:
I have knitted three scarves this month. = 这个月我已经织了三条围巾。
She has knitted many sweaters for charity. = 她已经为慈善机构织了很多毛衣。

过去完成时:
He had knitted the entire blanket before the baby was born. = 宝宝出生前他已经织好了整条毯子。
They had knitted dozens of hats for the homeless shelter. = 他们已经为收容所织了几十顶帽子。

将来时:
I will knit a sweater for my boyfriend. = 我将为我的男朋友织一件毛衣。
She will knit a blanket for her new baby. = 她将为她的新生儿织一条毯子。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would knit a quilt. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会织一床被子。
I wish I could knit as well as my grandmother. = 我希望我能像我奶奶一样擅长织毛衣。

被动语态:
The sweater was knitted by hand. = 这件毛衣是手工编织的。
The blanket is being knitted by a skilled artisan. = 这条毯子正在由一位熟练的工匠编织。

疑问句:
Is this sweater knitted or crocheted? = 这件毛衣是编织的还是钩织的？
Was the hat knitted by your mother? = 这顶帽子是你妈妈织的吗？

否定句:
That scarf wasn't knitted; it was woven. = 那条围巾不是编织的；它是织的。
I haven't knitted anything in years. = 我好几年都没织过东西了。
