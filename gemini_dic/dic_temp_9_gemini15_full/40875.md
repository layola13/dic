cookery:/ˈkʊkəri/ | n. |烹饪；烹调法 |The art or practice of preparing food for eating.  Cookery involves selecting, preparing, and cooking ingredients to create palatable and nutritious meals. It encompasses a wide range of techniques, skills, and knowledge. |例句：Her cookery skills are exceptional; her cakes are always a hit.  她的烹饪技巧很棒；她的蛋糕总是很受欢迎。

例句：The book focuses on the history and techniques of French cookery. 这本书重点介绍法国烹饪的历史和技巧。


近义词：cooking, culinary arts |反义词：None
