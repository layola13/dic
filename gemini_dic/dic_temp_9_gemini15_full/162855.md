precautionary:/prɪˈkɔːʃənəri/| adj.|预防的；警惕的；谨慎的|Taken beforehand to prevent something unpleasant from happening.  Something done as a precaution is done to prevent something bad from happening.|The doctor advised precautionary measures to avoid the spread of infection.  医生建议采取预防措施以避免感染蔓延。|preventive, prophylactic|reckless, careless|形容词


USAGE->

Precautionary measures are necessary to prevent accidents. = 预防措施对于预防事故是必要的。
He takes precautionary steps before every flight. = 他在每次飞行前都采取预防措施。

一般过去时:
They took precautionary steps to ensure their safety. = 他们采取了预防措施以确保自身安全。
Precautionary measures were implemented to protect the environment. = 实施了预防措施来保护环境。

We have taken all necessary precautionary measures. = 我们已经采取了所有必要的预防措施。
