electrooptical: /ˌɪˌlɛktrəʊˈɒptɪkəl/ | adj. | 电光学的 | Relating to or using both electricity and light.  Electro-optical devices convert electrical signals into light signals or vice versa.  | 例句：The electro-optical properties of the crystal were extensively studied.  该晶体的电光特性得到了广泛的研究。

The development of electro-optical technology has revolutionized communication. 电光技术的发展彻底改变了通信方式。


近义词：optoelectronic (often used interchangeably)


反义词：None (no direct antonym, but the opposite concept would be purely mechanical or purely chemical processes)


词性: adjective
