sixths:/sɪksts/| n.|六分之一；六等分之一|A sixth is one of six equal parts of a whole.  It is often used to represent a fraction (1/6).|The cake was cut into sixths, and each person received one sixth.|近义词：one-sixth|反义词：whole


USAGE->
一般现在时:
One sixth of the students are absent today. = 今天六分之一的学生缺席。
He eats one sixth of the pizza. = 他吃掉了六分之一的披萨。

一般过去时:
She gave one sixth of her earnings to charity. = 她把收入的六分之一捐给了慈善机构。
One sixth of the population lived in poverty. = 六分之一的人口生活在贫困中。
