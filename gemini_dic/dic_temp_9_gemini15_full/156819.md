piggery:/ˈpɪɡəri/| n.|猪圈；养猪场；肮脏的地方|A piggery is a place where pigs are kept; it can also refer to a dirty or messy place.  The word carries a connotation of uncleanliness or disorder beyond simply being a pig pen.|例句：The piggery smelled strongly of manure.  = 猪圈里一股浓烈的粪便味。

The old farmhouse was in a terrible state; it was a real piggery. = 老农舍破败不堪，简直就是一个脏乱差的地方。|近义词：pigsty, sty, hog pen|反义词：cleanliness, tidiness
