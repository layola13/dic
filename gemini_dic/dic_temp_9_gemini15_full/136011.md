nonreaction:/ˌnɒnriˈækʃən/| n. |无反应；无动于衷|The lack of any reaction or response to a stimulus or event.  It implies a passive indifference or a failure to respond in any noticeable way.|例句：His nonreaction to the news was unsettling.  他对这个消息的无动于衷令人不安。

例句：The complete nonreaction from the audience was surprising given the powerful performance.  考虑到精彩的演出，观众的完全无动于衷令人惊讶。


近义词：apathy, indifference, unconcern, passivity

反义词：reaction, response, responsiveness


词性：名词
