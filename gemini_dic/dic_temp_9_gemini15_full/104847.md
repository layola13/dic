keelhauling:/ˈkiːlˌhɔːlɪŋ/ | n. |  龙骨下拖行（一种酷刑） | The act of dragging a person under the keel of a ship as a punishment. This involved tying the person to a rope and passing them under the ship's hull.  | Example: Keelhauling was a brutal form of punishment inflicted upon sailors in the past. 龙骨下拖行是过去对水手施加的一种残酷的惩罚方式。 | 近义词：flogging, dragging, punishment | 反义词：reward, praise
