programs:/ˈproʊɡræmz/ (美式)/ˈprəʊɡræmz/ (英式)| n. |程序；节目；规划；项目 |A set of instructions or rules that a computer follows to perform a specific task; a series of actions or events planned to achieve a particular purpose. |例句：The computer is running several programs simultaneously. 这台电脑同时运行多个程序。

例句：The government has launched a new program to combat poverty. 政府启动了一个新的扶贫项目。


近义词：plans, schemes, projects, schedules |反义词：chaos, disorder
