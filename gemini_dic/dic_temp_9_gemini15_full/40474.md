contributing:/kənˈtrɪbjuːtɪŋ/ | v. |贡献的；促成的 |  The act of making a contribution; helping to cause or bring about.  | 例句：His contributing factor to the team's success was undeniable.  他的贡献对团队的成功是不可否认的。
She's contributing to a new book on climate change. 她正在为一本关于气候变化的新书做贡献。 |近义词：giving, donating, assisting, aiding | 反义词：hindering, obstructing, impeding | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is contributing to the charity. = 他正在向慈善机构捐款。
They are contributing articles to the magazine. = 他们正在为杂志撰写文章。

一般过去时:
She contributed generously to the fundraising campaign. = 她慷慨地为筹款活动捐款。
He contributed a chapter to the textbook. = 他为教科书贡献了一章。

现在完成时:
We have contributed significantly to the project's success. = 我们对项目的成功做出了重大贡献。
They have contributed many valuable ideas to the discussion. = 他们为讨论贡献了许多宝贵的意见。

过去完成时:
Before the deadline, she had contributed all her work. = 在截止日期之前，她已经完成了所有工作。
They had contributed a large sum of money before the event started. = 活动开始前，他们已经捐赠了一大笔钱。

将来时:
I will contribute to the discussion tomorrow. = 我明天将参与讨论。
She will contribute a painting to the art exhibition. = 她将为艺术展览捐赠一幅画。

虚拟语气:
If he contributed more, the project would be finished sooner. = 如果他多做些贡献，项目就能更快完成。
I wish he had contributed more to the team. = 我希望他为团队贡献更多。

被动语态:
Much money was contributed to the disaster relief fund. = 大笔资金被捐献给了救灾基金。
A new chapter is being contributed to the book. = 书中正在增加一个新的章节。

疑问句:
Are you contributing to the project? = 你正在为项目做贡献吗？
How much are they contributing? = 他们捐了多少钱？

否定句:
He did not contribute to the discussion. = 他没有参与讨论。
She hasn't contributed anything to the solution. = 她对解决这个问题没有任何贡献。
