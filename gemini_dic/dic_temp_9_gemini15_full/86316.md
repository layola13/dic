hebraize:/ˈhiːbreɪz/| vt.|使希伯来化；用希伯来语表达|To make or become Hebrew in character or language.  The process of adopting Hebrew language, culture, or practices.|例句：The kibbutz attempted to hebraize its members by teaching them Hebrew and immersing them in Jewish culture.  这个集体农庄试图通过教授希伯来语和沉浸在犹太文化中来使它的成员希伯来化。|近义词：Judaize|反义词：de-Hebraize
