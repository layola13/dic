inundator:/ˈɪnʌndeɪtər/| n. | 泛滥者；淹没者；大量涌入者 | One that inundates; a flooder.  The heavy rain acted as an inundator, causing widespread flooding. 大雨如同泛滥者，造成了大范围的洪涝。|近义词：flooder, deluge, overwhelmer |反义词：drainer, dehydrator


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The inundator constantly threatens the low-lying areas. = 泛滥者持续威胁着低洼地区。
He is an inundator of information. = 他是一个信息泛滥者。


一般过去时:
The sudden storm acted as an inundator, destroying many homes. = 突如其来的暴风雨如同泛滥者，摧毁了许多房屋。
The unexpected influx of tourists acted as an inundator for the small town. = 意外涌入的大量游客淹没了这个小镇。


现在完成时:
The river has been an inundator for centuries. = 这条河几个世纪以来一直是泛滥的源头。
The constant influx of emails has been an inundator for his inbox. = 不断涌入的邮件淹没了他的收件箱。


过去完成时:

By the time the rescue team arrived, the flood had already become an inundator. = 当救援队到达时，洪水已经泛滥成灾。


将来时:

The anticipated increase in tourists will be an inundator for the city's infrastructure. = 预计增加的游客数量将使城市的基础设施不堪重负。


虚拟语气:

否定句:
