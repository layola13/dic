knifeedge: /ˈnaɪfˌɛdʒ/ | adj. |刀刃般锋利的；极其危险的；极其微妙的 | Extremely sharp; very precarious or delicate; on the verge of disaster or success. | 例句：The negotiations were on a knife edge.  谈判处于极其微妙的境地。  The climber was precariously balanced on a knife-edge ridge. 登山者危险地平衡在刀刃般锋利的山脊上。 | 近义词：precarious, critical, delicate, perilous | 反义词：safe, secure, stable
