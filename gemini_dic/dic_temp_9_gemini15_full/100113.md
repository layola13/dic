interpretation:/ɪnˌtɜːrprɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 解释；诠释；翻译 | The act of explaining, interpreting, or translating something.  It involves understanding the meaning and expressing it in a different way.  This can refer to verbal or written communication, artistic works, dreams, or even actions. | Example:  Her interpretation of the poem was quite different from mine. 她的诗歌解读与我的大相径庭。  The lawyer's interpretation of the law was crucial to the case. 律师对法律的解释对案件至关重要。|近义词：explanation, explication, translation, rendering |反义词：misunderstanding, misinterpretation
