precombatant:/ˌpriːkəmˈbætənt/| n. |战斗前的；战斗参与者（通常指平民）|A precombatant refers to someone or something that exists or is involved before a combat or battle begins.  This could refer to civilians in a conflict zone, preparations for war, or individuals who will participate in the fighting but haven't engaged yet.|例句：The report detailed the lives of precombatants in the war-torn region.  这份报告详细描述了饱受战争蹂躏地区战斗前平民的生活。 The precombatant training emphasized survival skills. 战斗前的训练强调生存技能。|近义词：civilian (in the context of conflict), non-combatant|反义词：combatant
