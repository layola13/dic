nipper:/ˈnɪpə(r)/| n. v.|钳子；小家伙；偷窃；用力咬；|A nipper is a small tool with two jaws that are used to grip or cut something.  It can also refer to a young person, or the action of pinching or biting. |例句：The mechanic used a pair of nippers to cut the wire.  机械师用钳子剪断了电线。

He's a little nipper, always getting into trouble. 他是个小家伙，总是惹麻烦。|近义词：pliers, pincers, youngster, kid|反义词：adult, grown-up
