dissented:/dɪˈsɛntɪd/| v.|不同意，异议|To disagree with something or someone, especially a decision or an opinion.  It implies a formal or strong disagreement, often within a group or organization.|例句：The judge dissented from the majority opinion. = 法官对多数意见持异议。

例句：Several members of the committee dissented from the final decision. = 委员会的几名成员对最终决定表示异议。


近义词：disagreed, objected, protested, opposed|反义词：agreed, concurred, assented


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dissents from the proposal. = 他反对这项提案。
They dissent on many issues. = 他们在许多问题上意见相左。


一般过去时:
She dissented from the group's decision. = 她对小组的决定表示异议。
The jury dissented on the verdict. = 陪审团对判决意见不一。


现在完成时:
He has dissented from the party line for years. = 他多年来一直反对党的路线。
They have dissented on this matter since the beginning. = 从一开始，他们就在这个问题上意见相左。


过去完成时:
She had dissented from the plan before it was finalized. = 在计划最终确定之前，她就表示反对。
They had dissented many times before reaching a consensus. = 他们在达成共识之前多次表示异议。


将来时:
He will dissent from the vote. = 他将对投票表示反对。
They will likely dissent on the new policy. = 他们很可能会反对这项新政策。


虚拟语气:
If he dissented, the outcome would have been different. = 如果他表示反对，结果就会不同。
I wish he hadn't dissented so strongly. = 我真希望他不要那么强烈地反对。


被动语态:
His views were dissented from by many. = 许多人反对他的观点。
The proposal was dissented from by the committee. = 该提案遭到委员会的反对。


疑问句:
Did anyone dissent from the motion? = 有人反对这项动议吗？
Does he often dissent from the majority opinion? = 他经常反对多数意见吗？


否定句:
He did not dissent from the decision. = 他没有反对这个决定。
They did not dissent on this particular point. = 他们在这一点上没有意见分歧。


PHRASE->
dissent from = 反对…
dissent on = 在…方面持异议
to dissent publicly = 公开表示异议
a dissenting opinion = 异议
dissent and debate = 异议与辩论
