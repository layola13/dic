modeled:/ˈmɑːdəld/ /ˈmɒdəld/| v. |模仿的；造型的；做模型的 |To create a model or representation of something, often as a preliminary step in design or production. It can also refer to the act of being shaped or influenced by something.  |例句：The architect modeled the building in clay before starting construction. 建筑师在开始施工前用粘土制作了建筑模型。
The young girl modeled her behavior after her older sister.  这个小女孩模仿她姐姐的行为。|近义词：emulated, imitated, simulated, patterned|反义词：original, unique


USAGE->

He models his work after famous artists. = 他以著名艺术家为榜样创作作品。
She models clothes for a living. = 她以做模特为生。

一般过去时:
