pejoratives:/ˈpɛdʒərətɪvz/| n.|轻蔑性词语，贬义词 | Words or expressions that have negative connotations and are used to belittle or insult someone or something.  They express contempt or disapproval. |例句：Using racial pejoratives is unacceptable in any context. 使用种族歧视性贬义词在任何情况下都是不可接受的。

例句：He used a pejorative term to describe his political opponent. 他用了一个贬义词来形容他的政治对手。


近义词：derogatory terms, insults, slurs, epithets, disparagements


反义词：compliments, praise, accolades, laudatory terms


词性：noun
