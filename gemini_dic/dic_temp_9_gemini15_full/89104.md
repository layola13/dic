homaged:/ˈhɒmɑːʒd/ | v. |致敬，表达敬意 |To pay homage to someone or something is to show great respect or admiration for them. It often involves a public display of honor or tribute. | The film homaged classic horror movies.这部电影向经典恐怖片致敬。|  honor, tribute, celebrate | disrespect, ignore | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They homage their heroes every year. = 他们每年都向他们的英雄致敬。
We homage the fallen soldiers with a moment of silence. = 我们以默哀的方式向阵亡士兵致敬。


一般过去时:
The artist homaged his mentor in his latest painting. = 艺术家在他最新的绘画作品中向他的导师致敬。
The band homaged their influences during their concert. = 乐队在音乐会上向他们的影响致敬。


现在完成时:
She has homaged many great musicians throughout her career. = 她在整个职业生涯中向许多伟大的音乐家致敬。
The writer has homaged classic literature in his novels. =这位作家在他的小说中向经典文学致敬。


过去完成时:
They had homaged the deceased leader before the ceremony began. = 在仪式开始之前，他们已经向已故领导人致敬。
The sculptor had homaged ancient art forms in his previous works. = 雕塑家在他以前的作品中向古代艺术形式致敬。


将来时:
The museum will homage local artists in its upcoming exhibition. = 博物馆将在即将举行的展览中向当地艺术家致敬。
They will homage the anniversary of the historical event with a special program. = 他们将通过一个特别的节目来纪念这一历史事件的周年纪念日。


虚拟语气:
If I were a better artist, I would homage my influences more skillfully. = 如果我是一个更好的艺术家，我会更巧妙地向我的影响致敬。
I wish I could homage my parents more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地向我的父母致敬。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

The company failed to homage its employees' contributions. = 公司未能向员工的贡献致敬。
