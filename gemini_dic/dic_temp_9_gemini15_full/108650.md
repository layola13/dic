leaflet:/ˈliːflət/ | n. | 传单；小册子 | A leaflet is a printed sheet of paper, usually folded, that gives information or advertising.  |  I picked up a leaflet about the local museum. 我捡起了一张当地博物馆的宣传单。 The company distributed leaflets advertising their new product. 公司分发了宣传他们新产品的传单。 | brochure, pamphlet, flyer |  poster | 名词


USAGE->

He often picks up leaflets on the street. = 他经常在街上捡传单。


一般过去时:
They distributed leaflets at the conference. = 他们在会议上分发了传单。
