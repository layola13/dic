preponderances:/prɪˈpɒndərənsɪz/| n. |优势；优势数量；大多数；支配地位 |The preponderance of evidence suggests he is guilty.  证据的大部分表明他有罪。|近义词：majority, predominance, superiority |反义词：minority, scarcity, deficiency


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The preponderance of opinion favors the proposal. = 大多数意见都赞成这项提案。
The preponderance of evidence points to his guilt. = 大部分证据都指向他的罪行。


一般过去时:
The preponderance of evidence in the case pointed to the defendant's guilt. = 此案中的大部分证据都指向被告有罪。
A preponderance of voters supported the incumbent. = 大多数选民支持现任官员。


现在完成时:
The preponderance of scientific evidence has confirmed the theory. = 大量科学证据已经证实了这个理论。
A preponderance of studies has shown the benefits of exercise. = 大量研究表明了锻炼的好处。


过去完成时:
By the end of the trial, a preponderance of evidence had accumulated against the defendant. = 到审判结束时，已经积累了大量对被告不利的证据。
A preponderance of the votes had already been cast before the polls closed. = 在投票站关闭之前，大部分选票已经投出。


将来时:
A preponderance of the population is expected to vote for the reform. = 预计大部分人口会投票支持改革。
It is predicted that a preponderance of the products will be sold online in the future. = 预计未来大部分产品将在网上销售。


虚拟语气:
If a preponderance of evidence were presented, the court would have to rule in favor of the plaintiff. = 如果有大量证据提交，法院将不得不判决原告胜诉。
If a preponderance of the population supported the initiative, it would likely pass. = 如果大部分人口支持这项倡议，它很可能会通过。


被动语态:
The decision was influenced by the preponderance of public opinion. = 这个决定受到了公众舆论的影响。
The outcome was determined by the preponderance of votes cast. = 结果是由投出的选票数量决定的。


疑问句:
Does a preponderance of the evidence support the hypothesis? = 大部分证据是否支持这个假设？
Is there a preponderance of opinion in favor of the new policy? = 是否有大部分意见支持这项新政策？


否定句:
There was not a preponderance of evidence to support his claim. = 没有足够的证据支持他的说法。
A preponderance of negative reviews did not deter the company from releasing the product. = 大量负面评论并没有阻止公司发布产品。


PHRASE->

a preponderance of power = 强大的力量
a preponderance of the evidence = 大部分证据
show a preponderance = 显示优势
