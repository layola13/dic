idealizers:/ˌaɪdiˈəlaɪzərz/| n. |理想化者，空想家 |Those who idealize tend to focus on the positive aspects of someone or something, often ignoring or downplaying the negative ones. They create an idealized image in their minds that may not reflect reality.  |Example:  Many young fans are idealizers of their favorite pop stars, often unaware of the less glamorous aspects of their lives.  许多年轻粉丝都将他们最喜欢的流行歌星理想化，常常意识不到他们生活中不那么光鲜亮丽的一面。|近义词：romanticists, visionaries |反义词：realists, cynics |名词
