reaves:/riːvz/|v.|夺取，抢夺；剥夺；掠夺|To take something by force; to rob or plunder.  The thieves reaved the village of its valuables.|近义词：rob, plunder, pilfer, despoil|反义词：give, restore, donate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reaves the land of its resources. = 他掠夺土地的资源。
She reaves her neighbors' belongings. = 她抢夺邻居的财物。

一般过去时:
The Vikings reaved the coastal towns. = 维京人洗劫了沿海城镇。
The bandits reaved the travelers of their money. = 强盗抢走了旅行者的钱财。

现在完成时:
The army has reaved the country of its wealth. = 军队已经掠夺了国家的财富。
The corporation has reaved the environment of its natural beauty. = 公司已经破坏了环境的自然美景。（此处reave可以引申为破坏）

过去完成时:
They had reaved the village before the soldiers arrived. = 士兵到达之前，他们已经洗劫了村庄。
The thieves had reaved the bank of its cash before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，小偷已经抢走了银行的现金。

将来时:

The storm will reave the coastline of its beaches. = 暴风雨将侵蚀海岸线的沙滩。（此处reave可以引申为侵蚀）

I wish they hadn't reaved the museum of its artifacts. = 我真希望他们没有掠夺博物馆的文物。

被动语态:
