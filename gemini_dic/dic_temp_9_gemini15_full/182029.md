remeeting:/ˌriːˈmiːtɪŋ/| n. |再次会议；重开会议|A re-meeting is a meeting that is held again, often to discuss something that was not resolved in the first meeting or to revisit a topic.  It implies a previous meeting already took place.|例句：We need a re-meeting to finalize the project details. 我们需要再次开会来敲定项目细节。|例句：The committee scheduled a re-meeting to discuss the unresolved budget issues. 委员会安排了一次再次会议来讨论未解决的预算问题。|近义词：second meeting, follow-up meeting|反义词：initial meeting, first meeting|名词
