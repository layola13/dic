dictionary's:/ˈdɪkʃənɛriz/| n. |字典的；词典的 |The possessive form of "dictionary," indicating ownership or belonging.  It describes something that belongs to or is associated with a dictionary.  |Example:  The dictionary's definition of "serendipity" is quite thorough. 字典对“意外发现”的定义相当详尽。|近义词：lexicon's, glossary's |反义词：N/A
