imaret:/ɪˈmɑːrɛt/| n. |公共厨房；慈善机构；旅馆|A building, typically in the Ottoman Empire, providing a charitable kitchen and often lodging for travelers and the poor.  It frequently included a mosque, a hospital, and other charitable institutions.|The imaret in Edirne provided food and shelter to the needy.|近义词：hospice, almshouse, charitable institution|反义词：None directly applicable.
