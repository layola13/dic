donary:/ˈdoʊnəri/| n. |捐赠；捐款；赠礼| A gift or donation, especially to a charity or religious institution.  A donary is often a significant contribution, intended for a specific purpose or cause. |例句：The museum received a generous donary from a local philanthropist.  博物馆收到一位当地慈善家慷慨的捐赠。|近义词：donation, contribution, gift, offering |反义词：receipt, expenditure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The church receives many donaries. = 教堂收到很多捐款。
She makes regular donaries to the hospital. = 她定期向医院捐款。

一般过去时:
He gave a donary to the orphanage. = 他向孤儿院捐赠了一笔款项。
They received a large donary last year. = 他们去年收到一笔巨额捐款。


现在完成时:
We have received several donaries this month. = 这个月我们已经收到几笔捐款了。
She has made many donaries to various charities. = 她已经向各种慈善机构捐赠了很多款项。


过去完成时:
They had already received the donary before the event started. = 活动开始前他们就已经收到了捐款。
The organization had accumulated many donaries over the years. = 该组织多年来积累了许多捐款。


将来时:
He will make a donary to the university. = 他将向大学捐款。
The foundation will receive a substantial donary next year. = 该基金会明年将收到一笔巨额捐款。


虚拟语气:
If he had more money, he would make a larger donary. = 如果他更有钱，他会捐更多。
I wish I could make a donary to help the victims. = 我希望我能捐款帮助受害者。


被动语态:
The donary was gratefully accepted by the hospital. = 医院欣然接受了捐款。
The scholarship was funded by a generous donary. = 奖学金是由一笔慷慨的捐款资助的。


疑问句:
What was the amount of the donary? = 捐款金额是多少？
Will the donary be enough to cover the costs? = 捐款够支付费用吗？


否定句:
He did not make a donary this year. = 他今年没有捐款。
The organization did not receive the expected donary. = 该组织没有收到预期的捐款。
