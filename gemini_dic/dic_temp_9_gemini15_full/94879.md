in case of: /ɪn keɪs əv/ | preposition | 万一；如果发生…；在…的情况下 | This phrase indicates a contingency or possibility, often used to express precaution or preparation for a potential event.  It introduces a clause describing a situation that might occur, and the action to be taken in response. |

例句：

Please keep this medication in case of an emergency. = 请保存好这种药，以备不时之需。

We should have a backup plan in case of rain. = 万一下雨，我们应该有一个备用计划。


近义词：if, should, in the event of


反义词：certainly, definitely (as these imply certainty, the opposite of a contingency)


词性：介词
