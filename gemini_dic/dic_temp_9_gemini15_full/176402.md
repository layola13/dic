re-hanging:/ˌriːˈhæŋɪŋ/ | n. |重新悬挂；再次悬挂 |The act of hanging something again, after it has been taken down or removed.  This could refer to a picture, a curtain, or any other object that is typically hung. |例句：The re-hanging of the artwork took several hours. =重新悬挂这幅艺术品花了几个小时。

The re-hanging of the curtains improved the room's appearance. =重新悬挂窗帘改善了房间的外观。|近义词：reinstallation, reattachment |反义词：removal, taking down |名词
