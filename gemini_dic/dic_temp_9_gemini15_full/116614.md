matron:/ˈmeɪtrən/| n.|女家长；女管家；护士长；中年妇女|A matron is a woman who is in charge of something, such as a hospital ward or a school dormitory.  It can also refer to an older married woman, often suggesting a respectable and somewhat dignified presence.|The matron of the orphanage was kind but firm.|近义词：housekeeper, warden, head nurse|反义词：young girl, child
