counterbalanced: /ˈkaʊntərbælənst/ | adj. | 平衡的，制衡的 |  Having a counterbalancing effect; offsetting.  The advantages of the new system are counterbalanced by its high cost.  | 近义词：balanced, offset, neutralized | 反义词：unbalanced, imbalanced


例句：

The benefits of the new policy are counterbalanced by its potential drawbacks.  新的政策的好处被其潜在的缺点所抵消。

The wings of the aircraft are carefully designed to be counterbalanced.  飞机的机翼经过精心设计以达到平衡。


词源：

* **counter-**:  prefix meaning "against," "opposite," or "in opposition to."  源自古法语"contre",  最终源于拉丁语"contra".

* **balance**:  from Old French *balence*, from Late Latin *bilanx*, from *bi- "two" + *lanx* "scale-pan".


用法说明：

counterbalanced 主要用作形容词，描述一种状态或系统，其中一种力量、影响或因素被另一种力量、影响或因素抵消或平衡。  它强调的是一种动态的平衡，而不是简单的静态平衡。


PHRASE->

* counterbalanced force: 平衡力
* counterbalanced design: 平衡设计
* counterbalanced system: 平衡系统
* counterbalanced reaction: 平衡反应
* carefully counterbalanced: 精心平衡的
