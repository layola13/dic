disinhibited:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhɪbɪtɪd/| adj. |  抑制释放的；无所顾忌的；行为不受约束的 |  Lacking inhibition; unrestrained.  Characterized by a lack of restraint or inhibition, especially in social situations. | 例句：He felt disinhibited after a few drinks.  他喝了几杯酒后感到无所顾忌。  The disinhibited behavior of the children surprised their teacher. 孩子们不受约束的行为让他们的老师很惊讶。 | 近义词：uninhibited, unrestrained, impulsive, reckless | 反义词：inhibited, restrained, controlled, reserved | 词性:形容词
