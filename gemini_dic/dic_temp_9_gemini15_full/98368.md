instition:/ɪnˈstɪʃən/| n.|制度；机构；习俗|An institution is an established organization or foundation, especially one dedicated to education, public service, or culture, or a long-established custom or practice.|例句：Harvard University is a prestigious institution.  哈佛大学是一所著名的学府。|例句：Marriage is a social institution. 婚姻是一种社会制度。|近义词：establishment, organization, system |反义词：chaos, disorder
