reenrolling: /ˌriːɪnˈroʊlɪŋ/ | v. | 重新注册，重新报名 | The act of enrolling again; registering for a course, program, or membership a second time or more.  | 例句：He is reenrolling in the advanced calculus course next semester. 他下学期将重新注册高等微积分课程。

例句：The university is offering reenrollment opportunities for students who dropped out last year.  大学正在为去年辍学的学生提供重新注册的机会。


近义词：re-registering, re-enlisting, signing up again, reapplying


反义词：dropping out, withdrawing, unregistering


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reenrolls in the class every semester. = 他每个学期都重新注册这门课。
They reenroll their children in the same school. = 他们把他们的孩子重新送回同一所学校上学。

一般过去时:
She reenrolled in the yoga class after a long break. = 她长时间休息后重新注册了瑜伽课。
He reenrolled in college after working for five years. = 他工作五年后重新上大学。

现在完成时:
I have reenrolled in the online course for the second time. = 我第二次重新注册了这门在线课程。
She has reenrolled in the dance academy and is enjoying it. = 她重新注册了舞蹈学院，并且很享受。

过去完成时:
He had reenrolled in the program before he received the scholarship. = 他在获得奖学金之前已经重新注册了该项目。
They had reenrolled in the swimming lessons before the summer started. = 夏天开始之前他们已经重新报了游泳课。

将来时:
I will reenroll in the writing workshop next fall. = 我明年秋天将重新注册写作工作坊。
She will reenroll in the music program after her concert tour. = 她的音乐会巡演结束后，她将重新注册音乐项目。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would reenroll in the painting course. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新注册绘画课程。
I wish I could reenroll in that history class. = 我真希望我能重新选修那门历史课。


被动语态:
He was reenrolled in the program after appealing the decision. = 在对决定提出上诉后，他重新注册了该项目。
Many students were reenrolled in the school after the summer break. = 暑假后许多学生重新回到学校。


疑问句:
Will you be reenrolling in the advanced class next year? = 你明年会重新注册高级班吗？
Are they reenrolling in the same dance studio? = 他们是否重新注册了同一个舞蹈工作室？


否定句:
He did not reenroll in the course this semester. = 这个学期他没有重新注册这门课程。
She will not reenroll in the university after graduating. = 毕业后她不会重新注册大学。
