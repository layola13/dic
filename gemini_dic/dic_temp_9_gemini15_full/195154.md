semidefensively:/ˌsɛmiˈdɪfɛnsɪvli/| adv. |半防守地，略带防守意味地 |Partially or somewhat defensively; in a manner suggesting a slight defensive posture or attitude.  It implies a less overt or aggressive defense than "defensively."|例句：He answered the question semidefensively, avoiding direct eye contact. 他半防守地回答了这个问题，避免直接的眼神接触。


He responded semidefensively to the criticism, offering a weak justification. 他对批评做出了略带防守意味的回应，提供了一个薄弱的理由。


近义词：guardedly, cautiously, hesitantly |反义词：offensively, aggressively, openly


词性：副词
