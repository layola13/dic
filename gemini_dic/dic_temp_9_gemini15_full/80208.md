godmothering:/ˈɡɑːdˌmʌðəriŋ/ | n. | 当教母；担任教母的角色 | The act of being a godmother; the role or responsibilities of a godmother.  This includes providing guidance, support, and a sense of connection to a child, often spiritually or emotionally. | Example:  Her godmothering extended beyond occasional gifts; she actively participated in the child's life.  她当教母不仅仅是偶尔送礼物；她积极参与孩子的日常生活。|近义词:mentoring, guiding, nurturing |反义词:neglecting, abandoning
