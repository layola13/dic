contrafissure:/ˈkɒntrəˌfɪʃər/| n. |反向裂缝；间接裂缝|A fracture in a bone that is some distance from the main fracture.  It is a secondary fracture that occurs in a different location from the primary injury site, often caused by the force of the primary fracture.|例句：The fall resulted in a major fracture of the femur, and a contrafissure in the tibia.  这次跌倒导致股骨严重骨折，胫骨也出现了反向裂缝。|近义词：secondary fracture, indirect fracture|反义词：primary fracture, direct fracture
