restricts:/rɪˈstrɪkts/ | vt. |限制，约束，规定 |To limit the size, amount, or extent of something.  It often implies the imposition of rules or controls. |例句：The new law restricts the sale of alcohol to people over 21.  新的法律限制将酒类销售给21岁以上的人。

The government restricts immigration to control population growth. 政府限制移民以控制人口增长。


近义词：limit, confine, restrain, curb, regulate | 反义词：expand, enlarge, broaden, liberate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The city restricts parking in certain areas. = 城市在某些地区限制停车。
This rule restricts the use of mobile phones. = 这条规定限制了手机的使用。


一般过去时:
The teacher restricted the students' access to the internet. = 老师限制了学生访问互联网。
The company restricted its spending last year. = 公司去年限制了支出。


现在完成时:
The drought has restricted water supplies. = 干旱已经限制了供水。
The regulations have restricted the spread of the disease. = 法规已经限制了疾病的传播。


过去完成时:
The previous administration had restricted trade with that country. = 上届政府已经限制了与那个国家的贸易。
They had restricted access to the building before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经限制了进入大楼的通道。


将来时:
The new policy will restrict the import of certain goods. = 新政策将限制某些商品的进口。
The company will restrict hiring until the market improves. = 公司将在市场好转之前限制招聘。


虚拟语气:
If they restricted spending, they would be in a better financial position. = 如果他们限制开支，他们的财务状况将会更好。
I wish the government restricted the use of plastic bags. = 我希望政府限制使用塑料袋。


被动语态:
Access to the building is restricted. = 进入大楼受到限制。
The sale of fireworks is restricted during the festival. = 节日期间禁止销售烟花。


疑问句:
Does this law restrict the freedom of speech? = 这部法律是否限制言论自由？
What restrictions are there on the use of this software? = 使用此软件有哪些限制？


否定句:
The government does not restrict the press. = 政府不限制新闻界。
This policy does not restrict the growth of small businesses. =这项政策不会限制小型企业的增长。
