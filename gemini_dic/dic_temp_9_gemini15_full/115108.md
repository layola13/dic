mange:/mændʒ/| v. n.| 患皮肤病；管理；吃；啃食 | To suffer from mange; to manage or control; to eat greedily; to gnaw. |例句：The dog has mange. = 这只狗患有皮肤病。 |例句：He manages a large company. = 他管理一家大公司。|近义词：control, direct, govern, eat, gnaw |反义词：neglect, abandon, starve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog manages to find food. = 这只狗设法找到了食物。
He manages the team effectively. = 他有效地管理团队。


一般过去时:
She managed the project successfully. = 她成功地管理了这个项目。
