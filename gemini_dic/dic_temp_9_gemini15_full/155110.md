petrologist:/pɛˈtrɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |岩石学家|A petrologist is a scientist who studies rocks, their origin, composition, structure, and history.  Petrologists use a variety of techniques, including microscopic examination, chemical analysis, and field observations, to understand the processes that form and alter rocks. |The petrologist analyzed the rock samples to determine their age and origin.  岩石学家分析了岩石样本以确定它们的年龄和起源。|geologist, mineralogist |(no clear antonym)|名词
