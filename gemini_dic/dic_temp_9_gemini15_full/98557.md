instinctivity:/ˌɪnstɪŋktɪvɪti/| n. |本能性，天性|The quality or state of being instinctive; the tendency to act on instinct.  It refers to behavior driven by inherent, inborn impulses rather than conscious thought or deliberation.|The bird's instinctivity led it to build a nest without any prior instruction.|近义词：instinct, intuition|反义词：deliberation, rationality|名词
