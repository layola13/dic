semipatriot:/ˈsemiˌpeɪtriət/| adj.|半个爱国者；不彻底的爱国者|A semipatriot is someone who shows only lukewarm or partial patriotism; their loyalty to their country is questionable or incomplete.  They may support some aspects of their nation but not others, or their support may be conditional.|例句：He's considered a semipatriot at best, given his frequent criticism of the government and his reluctance to defend national interests.|近义词：lukewarm patriot, half-hearted patriot|反义词：patriot, nationalist
