resining:/rɪˈzaɪnɪŋ/| v.|辞职；树脂化；|The act of resigning from a position or job; the process of hardening or solidifying with resin.|例句：He's resigning from his job next week. 他下周要辞职。|例句：The artist used a resining process to preserve the painting.  艺术家使用树脂化工艺来保存绘画。|近义词：quitting, departing, hardening|反义词：starting, commencing, softening|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is resigning from his position. = 他正在辞职。
She is resigning her membership. = 她正在辞去会员资格。


一般过去时:
He resigned from his job last year. = 他去年辞去了工作。
She resigned her membership in the club. = 她辞去了俱乐部会员资格。


现在完成时:
He has resigned from his post. = 他已经辞职了。
She has resigned from the committee. = 她已经辞去了委员会的职务。


过去完成时:
He had resigned before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他已经辞职了。
She had resigned her position months ago. = 她几个月前就辞职了。


将来时:
He will resign next month. = 他下个月将辞职。
She will resign if she doesn't get a promotion. = 如果她没有得到晋升，她就会辞职。


虚拟语气:
If he resigned, the company would be in trouble. = 如果他辞职，公司就会陷入困境。
I wish she hadn't resigned so suddenly. = 我真希望她不要那么突然辞职。


被动语态:
His resignation was accepted. = 他的辞职请求被接受了。
The position was resigned by him. = 这个职位被他辞去了。(较少用)


疑问句:
Is he resigning? = 他要辞职吗？
Will she resign soon? = 她很快就会辞职吗？


否定句:
He is not resigning. = 他没有辞职。
She did not resign from her job. = 她没有辞去她的工作。


PHRASE->
resign one's post = 辞职
resign from a job = 辞去工作
resign one's membership = 辞去会员资格
resign oneself to = 听天由命；顺从
resign a position = 辞去职位
