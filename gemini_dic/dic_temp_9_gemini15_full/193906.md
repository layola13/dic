self-drawings: /ˈsɛlf ˈdrɔːɪŋz/ | n. | 自画像；自己画的画 | Drawings that someone makes of themselves.  These can range from simple sketches to highly detailed and realistic portraits. They often offer insights into the artist's self-perception and emotional state. |


例句：

Her self-drawings revealed a deep-seated insecurity.  她的自画像揭示了她根深蒂固的不安全感。

The exhibition featured a series of striking self-drawings by the young artist.  展览展出了一系列这位年轻艺术家令人印象深刻的自画像。


近义词：self-portraits, self-representations


反义词：portraits of others (others' portraits)


词性：名词
