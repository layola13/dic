chairmen:/ˈtʃɛrmən/| n.|主席们；会长们|The plural of chairman, referring to multiple people holding the position of chair.|例句：The chairmen of the various committees met to discuss the budget.  各个委员会的主席们会面讨论预算。

例句：The chairmen announced the results of the election. 主席们宣布了选举结果。


近义词：presidents, heads, leaders|反义词：members, participants
