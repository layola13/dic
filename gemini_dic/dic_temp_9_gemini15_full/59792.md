ecotours:/ˈekoʊˌtʊrz/ | n. |生态旅游 |Eco-tours are journeys to natural areas that aim to minimize environmental impact and benefit local communities.  They often focus on conservation, education, and sustainable tourism practices. |Example: We booked an ecotour to the Amazon rainforest, hoping to see wildlife without harming the ecosystem. 我们预订了亚马逊雨林的生态旅游，希望能看到野生动物而不破坏生态系统。|近义词：nature tours, sustainable tourism, responsible travel |反义词：mass tourism, irresponsible travel
