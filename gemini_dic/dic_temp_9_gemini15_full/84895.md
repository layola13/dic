hankered:/ˈhæŋkərd/| v.|渴望，热切盼望|To have a strong desire or longing for something.  It often implies a persistent or unsatisfied desire.|例句：She hankered after a life of luxury. 她渴望过奢侈的生活。

例句：He hankered for the days of his youth. 他渴望回到年轻的时光。


近义词：longed, yearned, craved, desired|反义词：disliked, despised, abhorred|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hankers after fame. = 他渴望成名。
They hanker after simpler times. = 他们渴望过更简单的日子。

一般过去时:
She hankered after a quiet life in the country. = 她渴望在乡下过平静的生活。
We hankered for a break from our busy schedules. = 我们渴望从忙碌的日程中解脱出来。

现在完成时:
I have hankered after this book for years. = 我多年来一直渴望得到这本书。
They have hankered after a change of scenery. = 他们一直渴望改变一下环境。

过去完成时:
He had hankered after a promotion for months before he finally received one. = 在最终获得晋升之前，他渴望晋升好几个月了。
She had hankered after a new car for years before she finally bought one. = 在最终买车之前，她渴望拥有一辆新车好多年了。

将来时:
I will hanker after this job until I get it. = 我会一直渴望这份工作，直到得到它为止。
We will hanker after peace and quiet once we retire. = 我们退休后将会渴望平静安宁的生活。

虚拟语气:
If I had known how much I would hanker after it, I would have bought the house. = 如果我知道我会如此渴望它，我就会买下这房子了。
I wish I hadn't hankered after those unattainable goals. = 我真希望我没有渴望那些无法实现的目标。

被动语态:
The position was hankered after by many ambitious candidates.  = 许多有抱负的候选人都渴望得到这个职位。
That kind of lifestyle is hankered after by many people. = 许多人都渴望那种生活方式。

疑问句:
What do you hanker after most in life? = 你一生中最渴望的是什么？
Does he still hanker after his lost love? = 他还渴望他失去的爱人吗？

否定句:
I don't hanker after wealth or power. = 我不渴望财富或权力。
They don't hanker after a life of luxury. = 他们不渴望奢侈的生活。


PHRASE->
hanker after something: 渴望某物
hanker for something: 渴望某物
hanker to do something: 渴望做某事
hanker for the past: 怀念过去
hanker after success: 渴望成功
