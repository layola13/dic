lumping:/ˈlʌmpɪŋ/| v.|归类，合并，混为一谈|To group things together without much thought or care; to treat different things as if they were the same. |例句：He's lumping all the different types of apples together. 他把各种不同类型的苹果混为一谈。  例句：Don't lump all musicians into one category. 不要把所有音乐家都归为一类。|近义词：grouping, classifying, amalgamating, combining|反义词：separating, differentiating, distinguishing|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are lumping all the data together. = 他们把所有数据都归类在一起。
He lumps all his problems into one big mess. = 他把所有问题都混成一团糟。

一般过去时:
She lumped all the toys into a single box. = 她把所有的玩具都塞进一个盒子里。
They lumped the expenses together under one heading. = 他们把所有支出都归到一个项目下。

现在完成时:
We have lumped several projects together to save time. = 我们已经把几个项目合并在一起以节省时间。
They have lumped the costs of materials and labor together. = 他们已经把材料和人工的成本合并在一起了。

过去完成时:
He had lumped all the evidence together before drawing a conclusion. = 在得出结论之前，他已经把所有的证据都归纳在一起了。
They had lumped the different tasks together before realizing their mistakes. = 他们把不同的任务混在一起，然后才意识到自己的错误。

将来时:
They will lump all the applications together for review. = 他们将把所有申请放在一起进行审查。
She will lump the ingredients together before baking the cake. = 在烘烤蛋糕之前，她会把配料混合在一起。

虚拟语气:
If they lumped all the tasks together, they would finish sooner. = 如果他们把所有任务都合并在一起，他们会更快完成。
I wish they hadn't lumped the different issues together. = 我真希望他们没有把不同的问题混在一起。

被动语态:
All the complaints were lumped together in a single report. = 所有投诉都被汇总在一个报告中。
The different types of waste were lumped together for disposal. = 不同类型的废物被混合在一起进行处理。

疑问句:
Are you lumping all the costs together? = 你是否把所有成本都加在一起？
Why are they lumping these unrelated items together? = 为什么他们要把这些不相关的物品混在一起？

否定句:
We are not lumping these two problems together. = 我们没有把这两个问题混在一起。
They did not lump the data into separate categories. = 他们没有把数据分成不同的类别。

词源:
The word "lumping" originates from the Old English word "hlūpian," meaning "to leap" or "to jump."  Over time, its meaning shifted to refer to the act of gathering or throwing things together in a careless or haphazard manner, leading to its current usage.
