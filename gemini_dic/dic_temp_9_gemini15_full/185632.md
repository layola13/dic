reverberates:/rɪˈvɜːrbəreɪts/| v.|回响；反响；震动|To continue in a series of echoes; to resonate.  The sound reverberates through the cave. The impact of the news reverberated throughout the community.|近义词：echo, resonate, resound|反义词：silence, quiet


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bell reverberates throughout the valley. = 钟声在山谷中回荡。
The singer's voice reverberates with power. = 歌手的歌声充满力量，产生共鸣。


一般过去时:
The explosion reverberated through the building. = 爆炸声在建筑物中回响。
The news of the victory reverberated across the nation. = 胜利的消息传遍全国，引起强烈反响。


现在完成时:
The impact of his words has reverberated through the years. = 他话语的影响力一直延续至今。
The scandal has reverberated through the political landscape. = 丑闻在政坛上引起了轩然大波。


过去完成时:
The earthquake's tremors had reverberated for miles around. = 地震的震动波及到方圆数英里。
The initial shock had already reverberated through the financial markets. = 最初的冲击已经波及了金融市场。


将来时:
The announcement will reverberate across the globe. = 这一声明将在全球引起反响。
The consequences of this decision will reverberate for generations. = 这个决定的后果将会影响几代人。


虚拟语气:
If the news were true, it would reverberate across the world. = 如果这个消息是真的，它将在全世界引起反响。
I wish my voice could reverberate across the ages. = 我希望我的声音能够流芳百世。


被动语态:
The sound was reverberated throughout the hall. = 声音在整个大厅回响。
The effects of the policy were reverberated in every corner of the country. = 这项政策的影响遍及全国各地。


疑问句:
Does the sound reverberate in this room? = 这个房间的声音会回响吗？
How far will the effects of this decision reverberate? = 这个决定的影响会波及多远？


否定句:
The sound did not reverberate as strongly as expected. = 声音的回响不如预期强烈。
The impact of the speech did not reverberate throughout the audience. = 演讲的影响并没有波及到所有听众。


PHRASE->

reverberate through =  贯穿...；影响...
reverberate across =  传遍...；影响到...
reverberate in =  在...回响；在...中产生共鸣
reverberating effect =  回响效应；持续影响
