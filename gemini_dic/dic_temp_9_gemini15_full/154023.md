performatively:/pərˈfɔːrmətɪvli/| adv.|表演性地；以行动来表达地|In a way that constitutes or is characteristic of a performative utterance.|The speaker's words not only describe an action but also perform it.  For example, saying "I apologize" is performative because the act of apologizing is accomplished through the utterance itself.|He performatively contradicted his earlier statement by immediately acting in a way that directly opposed it. 他通过立即采取与之直接相反的行动，以行动反驳了他之前的声明。|practically, expressively|unperformatively
