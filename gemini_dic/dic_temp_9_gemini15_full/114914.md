manag: /ˈmænɪdʒ/ | v. | 管理；经营；设法做到 | To control, direct, or handle something successfully.  |例句：He manages a large team of employees. 他管理着一个庞大的员工团队。 |近义词：administer, direct, handle, control |反义词：neglect, abandon

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He manages the project effectively. = 他有效地管理着这个项目。
She manages a successful business. = 她经营着一家成功的企业。

一般过去时:
They managed to finish the work on time. = 他们设法按时完成了工作。
He managed the company for ten years. = 他管理这家公司十年了。

现在完成时:
She has managed the team for five years. = 她已经管理这个团队五年了。
They have managed to overcome many challenges. = 他们已经设法克服了许多挑战。

过去完成时:
He had managed to secure funding before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经设法获得了资金。
She had managed the department for years before her promotion. = 在晋升之前，她已经管理该部门多年了。

将来时:
He will manage the new branch next year. = 他明年将管理新分公司。
They will manage to find a solution. = 他们将设法找到解决方案。

虚拟语气:
If I managed the company, I would do things differently. = 如果我管理这家公司，我会采取不同的做法。
I wish I managed my time better. = 我希望我能更好地管理我的时间。

被动语态:
The project is managed by a team of experts. = 这个项目由一个专家团队管理。
The company is well managed. = 这家公司管理良好。

疑问句:
Who manages this department? = 谁管理这个部门？
How do you manage to do so much? = 你是怎么做到这么多事情的？

否定句:
He didn't manage to reach the summit. = 他没有成功到达山顶。
She couldn't manage the workload. = 她无法应付工作量。


PHRASE->
manage to do sth. = 设法做某事
manage without sth. = 没有...也能应付
manage one's time = 管理时间

manage a smile = 强作欢颜
