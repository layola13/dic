It often refers to the artistic or literary depiction of sexual desire and pleasure, or the feeling of sexual excitement itself.  The term can encompass a wide range of expressions, from subtle suggestion to explicit depictions. |例句：The novel is filled with erotic scenes. 这部小说充满了色情场景。

例句：The painting's eroticism is undeniable. 这幅画的性魅力是不可否认的。


近义词：sensuality, sexuality, lust, libido |反义词：chastity, abstinence, purity |词性 n.
