comprises:/kəmˈpraɪzɪz/ | vt. | 包含；构成；组成 | To include or contain; to be made up of.  | The United Kingdom comprises England, Scotland, and Wales.  英国由英格兰、苏格兰和威尔士组成。 | includes, consists of, encompasses |  divides, separates | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team comprises five members. = 这个团队由五名成员组成。
This book comprises ten chapters. = 这本书包含十个章节。


一般过去时:
The collection comprised rare stamps and coins. = 这批收藏品包括稀有的邮票和硬币。
The army comprised soldiers from many countries. = 这支军队由来自许多国家的士兵组成。


现在完成时:
The exhibition has comprised various art forms over the years. = 多年来，展览包含了各种艺术形式。
The project has comprised numerous challenges. = 这个项目包含了许多挑战。


过去完成时:
The agreement had comprised several key points before the final signing. = 在最终签署之前，协议已经包含了几个关键点。
The evidence had comprised several witness statements. = 证据包括几份证人证词。


将来时:
The new curriculum will comprise more practical subjects. = 新的课程将包含更多实践科目。
The team will comprise experts from different fields. = 这个团队将由来自不同领域的专家组成。


虚拟语气:
If the committee comprised only experts, the decision would be better. = 如果委员会只由专家组成，决定会更好。
I wish the package comprised everything I ordered. = 我希望包裹包含我订购的所有东西。


被动语态:
The team is comprised of highly skilled individuals. = 这个团队由高技能人才组成。
The report is comprised of several sections. = 报告由几个部分组成。


疑问句:
What comprises the majority of your income? = 你的收入主要来自什么？
What exactly comprises this mysterious package? = 这个神秘包裹究竟包含什么？


否定句:
The course does not comprise any practical work. = 这门课程不包含任何实践工作。
The deal did not comprise the additional services we requested. = 这笔交易不包含我们要求的额外服务。


PHRASE->
be comprised of = 由...组成
comprise of (非正式用法，但常见)= 由...组成
comprise something of = 包含一些...
comprise in = 包含在...中
comprise a large part of = 构成很大一部分
