propagandic:/ˌprɒpəˈɡændɪk/| adj. |宣传的；煽动的；鼓吹的 | Relating to or characteristic of propaganda; tending to promote a particular doctrine or cause.  |The propagandic nature of the speech was evident in its biased presentation of facts. 这篇演讲的宣传性质在其对事实的有偏见陈述中显而易见。|近义词：propaganda, promotional, persuasive, agitational |反义词：truthful, unbiased, objective
