matriarch:/ˈmeɪtriɑːrk/ | n. |女族长，家长女性 | A woman who is the head of a family or tribe.  |例句：The matriarch ruled her family with a firm but loving hand. 女族长以坚定而慈爱的方式统治着她的家族。 |例句：She was a respected matriarch in her community.  她在她的社区是一位受人尊敬的女族长。|近义词：female head, chief woman, dame |反义词：patriarch


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The matriarch leads the family. = 女族长领导着这个家庭。
She is the matriarch of a large clan. = 她是大家族的女族长。

一般过去时:
The matriarch ruled wisely. = 女族长英明地统治着。

The matriarch has guided the family for many years. = 女族长多年来一直引导着这个家庭。

过去完成时:
