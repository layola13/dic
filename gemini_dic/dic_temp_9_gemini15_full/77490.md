gazelles:/ɡəˈzɛlz/| n.|瞪羚|A gazelle is a small, slender, graceful antelope with long, thin legs and large, expressive eyes.  They are known for their speed and agility.|Example:  The gazelle gracefully leaped over the low bush.  瞪羚优雅地越过了矮灌木丛。The herd of gazelles grazed peacefully in the savanna.  一群瞪羚在热带草原上和平地吃草。|近义词：antelope, deer|反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting slowness or clumsiness could be considered antonyms.|名词
