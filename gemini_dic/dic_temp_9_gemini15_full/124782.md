mstr:/ˈmɑːstər/| n.|大师，硕士，主人|A person who is highly skilled in a particular art, craft, or profession.  Also, a person who owns or controls something.  In academic context, it refers to a postgraduate degree.|例句：He is a master craftsman. 他是一位技艺精湛的工匠。|例句：She earned a master's degree in engineering. 她获得了工程硕士学位。|近义词：expert, proficient, specialist, owner|反义词：novice, beginner, apprentice|名词


USAGE->

He is a master of his craft. = 他是技艺大师。
She masters the new software quickly. = 她很快掌握了新软件。
