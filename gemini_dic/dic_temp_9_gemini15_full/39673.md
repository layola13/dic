conserved:/kənˈsɜːvd/| adj. v. | 保持的；保存的；节约的 | Kept safe from damage or decay; protected.  |  The ancient city's walls are remarkably conserved.  古代城市的城墙保存得非常好。 | preserved, protected, saved | wasted, destroyed, squandered |  adj., v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conserves energy by walking to work. = 他步行上班以节约能源。
They conserve water by taking shorter showers. = 他们通过洗更短的淋浴来节约用水。


一般过去时:
She conserved her strength for the final race. = 她为最后的比赛节省体力。
We conserved food by canning it for the winter. = 我们通过罐装食物来保存食物过冬。


现在完成时:
They have conserved this historical building for future generations. = 他们已经为后代保存了这座历史建筑。
We have conserved a significant amount of money this year. = 今年我们节省了一大笔钱。


过去完成时:
They had conserved enough fuel to complete the journey. = 他们已经节省了足够的燃料来完成旅程。
She had conserved all her energy for the important meeting. = 她已经把所有的精力都留给了重要的会议。


将来时:
We will conserve more water next year. = 明年我们将节约更多用水。
They will conserve the rainforest by preventing deforestation. = 他们将通过阻止森林砍伐来保护雨林。


虚拟语气:
If we conserved more resources, we would have a better environment. = 如果我们节约更多的资源，我们将拥有更好的环境。
I wish we had conserved more energy last year. = 我希望去年我们节省更多的能源。


被动语态:
The forest is being conserved by the government. = 森林正在受到政府的保护。
Energy is conserved through efficient appliances. = 通过高效的电器来节约能源。


疑问句:
How can we conserve more water? = 我们如何才能节约更多用水？
Do you conserve electricity in your home? = 你在家节约用电吗？


否定句:
They did not conserve enough food for the winter. = 他们没有为冬天储存足够的食物。
He doesn't conserve energy; he wastes it. = 他不节约能源；他浪费能源。
