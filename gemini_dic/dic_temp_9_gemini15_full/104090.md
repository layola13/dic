jump-in:/dʒʌmp ˈɪn/| v.|插嘴，介入，加入|To interrupt a conversation or activity; to join or participate suddenly or enthusiastically.|例句：He jumped in with a suggestion. 他插话提了个建议。
例句：Several new members jumped in to help with the project.好几个新成员加入进来帮忙做这个项目。|近义词：interject, butt in, join, participate|反义词:withdraw, abstain, refrain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They jump in to help whenever needed. = 他们需要帮忙的时候就会插手帮忙。
He jumps in conversations without being asked. = 他不请自来地插进谈话中。

一般过去时:
She jumped in the discussion to offer her perspective. = 她插进讨论中，提出自己的观点。
They jumped in to rescue the cat from the tree. = 他们跳进去把猫从树上救下来。

现在完成时:
He has jumped in on several projects this year. = 他今年已经参与了好几个项目。
She has jumped in to help her friends with their business. = 她已经加入帮忙她的朋友们做生意了。

过去完成时:
Before I could explain, he jumped in with a solution. = 在我解释之前，他已经插话提出解决方案了。
They had jumped in to assist the firefighters before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他们已经加入协助消防员了。

将来时:
I will jump in to help if they need extra hands. = 如果他们需要额外的人手，我会加入帮忙。
They will jump in the game at the last minute. = 他们会在最后一分钟加入比赛。

虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have jumped in to help earlier. = 如果我知道的话，我就会早点加入帮忙。
I wish I had jumped in to offer my assistance. = 我真希望我当时能加入帮忙。

被动语态:
The discussion was jumped into by several uninvited guests. = 讨论被几个不速之客打断了。
The project was jumped into enthusiastically by the team. = 团队热情地投入到项目中。

疑问句:
Will you jump in to help us with the task? = 你会来帮忙我们做这个任务吗？
Did he jump in to defend his friend? = 他有插手为他朋友辩护吗？

否定句:
I didn't jump in to the conversation because I didn't know the topic. = 我没有插进谈话，因为我不了解话题。
They didn't jump in to help because they were busy. = 他们没有帮忙，因为他们很忙。
