cultureshocked: /ˈkʌltʃərʃɑːkt/ | adj. | 文化冲击的，文化休克的 | Experiencing a feeling of disorientation, confusion, or anxiety caused by being in an unfamiliar culture.  | 例句：She was cultureshocked by the drastic difference between her hometown and the bustling city. 她被家乡和繁华都市之间的巨大差异而感到文化冲击。

She felt cultureshocked after moving to a country with vastly different customs. 她搬到一个风俗习惯大相径庭的国家后感到文化冲击。


近义词：disoriented, discombobulated, bewildered, overwhelmed (in a cultural context) | 反义词：acclimated, comfortable, at ease
