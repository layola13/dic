non-Spartans: /ˌnɒnˈspɑːrtənz/ | adj. n. | 非斯巴达人；非斯巴达式的 |  Describing those who are not Spartans or who lack the qualities typically associated with Spartans, such as discipline, austerity, and military prowess.  It can be used as an adjective to describe things or people lacking those Spartan qualities, or as a noun to refer to a group of non-Spartans. |


例句：

The Athenian army, composed mostly of non-Spartans, struggled against the Spartan discipline and military skill. = 主要由非斯巴达人组成的雅典军队，在与斯巴达人的纪律和军事技能对抗中苦苦挣扎。

Their non-Spartan lifestyle was a stark contrast to the austere lives of the Spartans. = 他们非斯巴达式的生活方式与斯巴达人简朴的生活形成了鲜明对比。


近义词：non-military, undisciplined, effeminate (in the context of Spartan ideals)


反义词：Spartan, disciplined, austere, militaristic


词性:  adjective, noun
