destinying:  /ˈdɛstɪniɪŋ/ | v. | 注定；预定；使注定 |  The act of determining or deciding something in advance; assigning a fate or destiny to someone or something.  This word is rarely used and is considered archaic or non-standard. It's essentially a less common and more formal way of saying "destining." | 例句：The oracles were busy destinying the fate of kings.  占卜者正忙于预言国王的命运。  The war destinying millions to death.  这场战争注定要致使数百万人死亡。 | 近义词：destining, foreordaining, predetermining | 反义词：leaving to chance, leaving undetermined | 动词
