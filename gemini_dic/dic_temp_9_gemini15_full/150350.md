panier:/pæˈniːeɪ/| n. |篮子，菜篮|A basket, typically one used for carrying food or other goods.  It often has a handle or handles and is usually made of wicker, wood, or other materials.|例句：She carried a wicker panier filled with fresh bread and cheese. 她提着一个装着新鲜面包和奶酪的柳条篮子。|例句：The baker used a large panier to deliver bread to the local shops. 面包师用一个大篮子把面包送到当地商店。|近义词：basket, hamper, creel|反义词:无
