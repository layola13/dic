furbish:/ˈfɜːrbɪʃ/| vt.|擦亮；修整；翻新|To renovate or restore something to its former glory; to brighten or polish something.|例句：He furbished up the old house before selling it. 他在卖掉老房子之前把它翻新了。|例句：She furbished the silver until it gleamed. 她把银器擦亮直到闪闪发光。|近义词：renovate, restore, refurbish|反义词：deteriorate, ruin, neglect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He furbishes his old car every weekend. 他每个周末都修理他的旧车。
She furbishes the silverware before every dinner party. 她在每次晚宴前都擦亮银器。


一般过去时:
They furbished the antique furniture. 他们翻新了古董家具。
He furbished the old painting and it looked brand new. 他修复了那幅旧画，看起来焕然一新。


现在完成时:
She has furbished her kitchen with new appliances. 她用新的电器翻新了她的厨房。
They have furbished the entire house. 他们已经装修了整栋房子。


过去完成时:
He had furbished the car before he took it on a long road trip. 他在长途旅行之前已经修好了车。
They had furbished the living room before the party guests arrived. 客人到达之前，他们已经布置好了客厅。


将来时:
We will furbish the office next month. 我们下个月将翻新办公室。
She will furbish the old chair for her grandmother. 她将为她祖母修理那把旧椅子。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would furbish the entire house. 如果我有钱，我会把整栋房子都翻新。
I wish I could furbish my old bicycle. 我希望我能修理我的旧自行车。


被动语态:
The old house is being furbished by a professional team. 这栋老房子正在由一个专业团队翻修。
The antique clock was furbished and is now working perfectly. 古董钟表经过修复，现在运行良好。


疑问句:
Will you furbish the garden shed this weekend? 你这个周末会修理花园小屋吗？
Are they furbishing the entire building? 他们正在翻新整栋楼吗？


否定句:
He didn't furbish the car, so it still looks old. 他没有修理那辆车，所以它看起来仍然很旧。
They haven't furbished the kitchen yet. 他们还没有装修厨房。
