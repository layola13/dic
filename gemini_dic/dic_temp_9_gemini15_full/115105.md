manganous:/ˈmæŋɡənəs/| adj.|锰的；亚锰的|Containing or relating to manganese in its lower valence state (Mn2+).  It refers to compounds where manganese has a +2 oxidation state.|例句：Manganous oxide is a green powder.  氧化锰是一种绿色粉末。

例句：The solution contained manganous ions. 该溶液含有锰离子。

近义词：divalent manganese (referring to the +2 oxidation state)


反义词：manganic (referring to the +3 or +4 oxidation state)


词性:形容词
