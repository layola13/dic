debatingly:/dɪˈbeɪtɪŋli/| adv.| 争论地；辩论地；犹豫地 |  In a debating manner;  characterized by debate or argument; hesitantly.  The word implies a process of weighing arguments or options, often with some uncertainty. |例句：He looked debatingly at the two options before him. 他犹豫地看了看面前的两个选择。

例句：She answered debatingly, unsure of her response. 她犹豫地回答，不确定自己的回答。


近义词：hesitantly, uncertainly, doubtfully, irresolutely

反义词：decisively, confidently, assuredly


词性：副词
