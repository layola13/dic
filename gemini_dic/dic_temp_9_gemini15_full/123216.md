monoclinic:/ˌmɒnəˈklɪnɪk/ | adj. | 单斜晶系的；单斜晶体的 | Relating to or denoting the monoclinic crystal system, characterized by three unequal axes, two of which are at right angles and the third inclined.  |  The mineral exhibited a monoclinic crystal structure.  这种矿物呈现单斜晶系晶体结构。 |  orthorhombic, triclinic |  cubic, tetragonal |  adj.
