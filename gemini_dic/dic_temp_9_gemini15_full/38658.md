The term often refers to the jarring of the brain within the skull, resulting in a concussion.  |例句：The car accident resulted in a severe concussation, leaving the victim with a concussion.  这次车祸导致了严重的脑震荡，受害者因此患上了脑震荡。|近义词：concussion, jarring, shaking |反义词：stillness, calmness |名词
