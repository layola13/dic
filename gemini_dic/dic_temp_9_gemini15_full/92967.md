idian:/ˈɪdiən/| n. |印度人；印度语|A native or inhabitant of India.  The term can also refer to the Hindi language, though this usage is less common and often considered imprecise.|例句：Many idians emigrated to the UK during the 20th century.  许多印度人在20世纪移民到英国。|近义词：Indian (more common and preferred term)|反义词：None directly applicable.  The antonym would depend on the context (e.g., Pakistani, if contrasting nationalities).
