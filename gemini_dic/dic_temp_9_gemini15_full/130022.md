non-optional:/ˌnɒnˈɒpʃənəl/| adj. |非可选的，必须的|Not able to be omitted or avoided; compulsory.  It emphasizes that something is required and cannot be left out or chosen to be excluded.|The non-optional elements of the contract must be signed.|近义词：mandatory, required, obligatory, compulsory|反义词：optional, elective
