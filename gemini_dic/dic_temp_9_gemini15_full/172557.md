pyrognostics:/ˌpaɪroʊɡˈnɒstɪks/ | n. | 火成岩学 | The study of igneous rocks;  the science of interpreting the formation and characteristics of igneous rocks.  It involves analyzing their mineralogical composition, texture, structure, and geological context to understand the processes that led to their formation, such as volcanic eruptions or intrusive magma emplacement. | Example:  Pyrognostics plays a crucial role in understanding the Earth's history and the dynamics of plate tectonics.  火成岩学在理解地球历史和板块构造动力学方面起着至关重要的作用。| 近义词: igneous petrology, volcanology | 反义词: (no direct antonym, but contrasting fields might include sedimentary petrology or metamorphic petrology)| 名词
