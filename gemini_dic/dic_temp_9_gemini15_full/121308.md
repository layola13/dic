misfocussing: /ˌmɪsˈfoʊkəsɪŋ/ | v. | 错焦，焦点模糊 | The act of failing to focus correctly, either intentionally or unintentionally;  blurring the focus.  This typically refers to a camera lens, but can also be used metaphorically. |


例句：

The photographer was constantly misfocussing his shots, resulting in blurry images.  = 摄影师不断地拍出模糊的照片，因为他的镜头总是对不上焦。

He was misfocussing on minor details and ignoring the bigger picture. = 他把注意力放在细枝末节上，而忽略了大局。


近义词：

blurring, defocusing, unfocussing


反义词：

focussing, sharpening


词性: 动词
