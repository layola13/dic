re-anchors:/ˌriˈæŋkərz/| v. |重新固定；重新锚定|To secure or fasten something again, especially after it has become loose or detached.  The term implies a previous anchoring that has failed or needs reinforcement. |例句：The boat re-anchored in a more sheltered bay after the storm.暴风雨过后，船在更安全的港湾重新抛锚。The surgeon re-anchored the torn ligament. 外科医生重新固定了撕裂的韧带。|近义词：refasten, resecure, reattach |反义词：unmoor, detach
