sanitized:/ˈsænɪtaɪzd/ | adj. | 消毒的；净化了的；删改过的 |  Cleaned and purified, especially to remove harmful bacteria.  This can also refer to the removal of offensive or sensitive content from data or information. | 例句：The hospital staff sanitized the operating room before the surgery. 医院工作人员在手术前对手术室进行了消毒。 The report was sanitized to remove any potentially damaging information.  报告经过删改，以去除任何可能造成损害的信息。 | 近义词：sterilized, disinfected, purged, cleansed | 反义词：contaminated, polluted, uncensored
