parasita:/ˈpærəsaɪt/| n.|寄生虫，寄生植物|A parasite is an organism that lives on or in a host and gets its food from or at the expense of its host.|例句：Tapeworms are parasites that live in the intestines of animals. 绦虫是寄生在动物肠道中的寄生虫。|例句：Mistletoe is a parasitic plant that grows on trees. 槲寄生是一种寄生在树上的植物。|近义词：hanger-on, sponger|反义词：host
