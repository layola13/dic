nonmarkets:/ˈnɒnˌmɑːrkɪts/ | n. |非市场 |Areas or processes outside of traditional market mechanisms, where goods, services, or values are exchanged or determined through non-market means.  These often involve social, political, or regulatory influences. |Example:  Environmental protection is often handled through nonmarkets, with regulations and government initiatives playing a key role. 环境保护通常通过非市场手段来处理，法规和政府举措发挥着关键作用。|近义词：non-market mechanisms, informal economies, social exchanges|反义词：markets, market economies|名词
