ostracoda:/ɒˈstrækədə/| n. |介形类动物|Ostracoda are a class of crustaceans, characterized by their bivalved carapace, which resembles a tiny clam shell. They are found in various aquatic environments, from freshwater to marine habitats.  They play an important role in aquatic food webs and are used as indicators of environmental changes.|Example:  The sediment samples revealed a diverse assemblage of ostracoda species.  沉积物样本显示了种类繁多的介形类动物组合。|近义词:crustacean, bivalve (in terms of shell structure)|反义词:N/A
