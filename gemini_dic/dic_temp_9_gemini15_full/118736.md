metahumans:/ˈmɛtəˌhjumənz/ | n. | 超人类，具有超能力的人 |  A metahuman is a fictional character possessing superhuman abilities, often found in comic books and science fiction. They are typically individuals who have undergone genetic modification, exposure to extraordinary circumstances, or possess innate powers beyond the capabilities of ordinary humans.  | Example:  The metahuman used his telekinetic powers to stop the runaway train.  超人类用他的心灵感应能力阻止了失控的火车。  The city was protected by a team of metahumans with diverse abilities. 这个城市受到一支拥有各种能力的超人类团队的保护。 | 近义词: superhuman, superpowered individual | 反义词: ordinary human, normal person
