hyperbarism:/ˌhaɪpərˈbærɪzəm/| n.|高压环境病；高压症|The condition resulting from exposure to increased ambient pressure, typically underwater diving or in hyperbaric chambers.  It can manifest in various ways depending on the depth and duration of exposure, and the rate of ascent. |例句：Divers can experience hyperbarism if they ascend too quickly from a deep dive.  潜水员如果从深潜中上升得太快，可能会患上高压症。|例句：Treatment for hyperbarism often involves recompression therapy in a hyperbaric chamber. 高压症的治疗通常包括在高压舱内进行再压缩治疗。|近义词：decompression sickness, caisson disease|反义词：N/A
