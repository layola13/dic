immigrating:/ˈɪmɪɡreɪtɪŋ/| v. |移民；迁入|The act of coming to live permanently in a foreign country.|例句：Millions are immigrating to the United States in search of better opportunities. 数百万人在寻求更好机会的驱使下移民到美国。
例句：The family is immigrating to Canada next month.  这个家庭下个月要移民到加拿大。|近义词：migrating, relocating|反义词：emigrating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are immigrating to Australia. = 他们正在移民到澳大利亚。
Many people are immigrating to find better jobs. = 许多人正在移民以寻找更好的工作。

一般过去时:
My grandparents immigrated to this country in 1950. = 我的祖父母在1950年移民到这个国家。
She immigrated to the US after finishing her studies. = 她完成学业后移民到美国。

现在完成时:
They have immigrated to Canada and settled down. = 他们已经移民到加拿大并定居下来。
Millions have immigrated seeking economic opportunities. = 数百万移民寻求经济机会。

过去完成时:
He had immigrated to the UK before the war broke out. = 在战争爆发之前，他已经移民到英国。
They had immigrated several years before I met them. = 在我遇到他们之前，他们已经移民好几年了。

将来时:
We will be immigrating to France next year. = 我们明年将移民到法国。
She will immigrate to New Zealand after she graduates. = 她毕业后将移民到新西兰。

虚拟语气:
If I were younger, I would immigrate to a warmer country. = 如果我还年轻，我会移民到一个气候更温暖的国家。
I wish I could immigrate to Japan. = 我希望我能移民到日本。

被动语态:
Many immigrants are welcomed by the immigrating country.  许多移民受到移民国的欢迎。
Few people are actively discouraged from immigrating.  很少有人被积极劝退移民。

疑问句:
Are you immigrating to a new country? = 你要移民到一个新的国家吗？
When are they immigrating? = 他们什么时候移民？


否定句:
He is not immigrating to the United States. = 他没有移民到美国。
They did not immigrate to Canada last year. = 他们去年没有移民到加拿大。


PHRASE->
immigrating to a new country = 移民到一个新的国家
