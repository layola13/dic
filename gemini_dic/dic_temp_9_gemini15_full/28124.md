capitated:/ˈkæpɪteɪtɪd/| adj.|人均付费的；按人头付费的|Pertaining to a system of payment in which a provider receives a fixed amount of money per patient for a specified period of time, regardless of the number of services provided.  This is often used in healthcare systems.|Example: The clinic operates under a capitated payment system, receiving a fixed fee per patient per month.|近义词：per capita, fixed-fee|反义词：fee-for-service
