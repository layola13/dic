filbert:/ˈfɪlbərt/| n. |榛子|A filbert is a hazelnut, specifically the fruit of the cultivated hazel, Corylus maxima.  It is usually larger and more elongated than other hazelnuts.|例句：We roasted filberts over the campfire. 我们在篝火上烤榛子。
