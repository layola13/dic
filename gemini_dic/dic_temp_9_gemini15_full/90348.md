hovel:/ˈhɒvəl/ /ˈhɑːvəl/| n. |破旧小屋，简陋住所 |a small, poorly built and often dirty house  |例句：They lived in a damp, dilapidated hovel. 他们住在一间潮湿破旧的小屋里。

例句：The family were forced to live in a hovel after losing their home in the fire.  这场火灾后，这家人被迫住在一间破旧的小屋里。


近义词：shack, hut, shanty, cabin


反义词：mansion, palace, villa


词性：名词
