/fɒs/ | n. |化石|A fossil is the remains or impression of a prehistoric organism preserved in petrified form or as a mold or cast in rock.  Fossils provide valuable evidence about the history of life on Earth.| Example:  The museum displayed a remarkable collection of dinosaur fossils.  博物馆展示了一批非凡的恐龙化石。|近义词：remains, relic|反义词：living organism
