plaits:/pleɪts/| n. v.|辫子；编织|A plait is a braid of hair or other flexible material.  It can also refer to the act of braiding.|例句：She wore her hair in two plaits. 她把头发编成两个辫子。

例句：She skillfully plaited the ribbons together. 她熟练地将丝带编在一起。|近义词：braid, tress|反义词：unbraid, unravel|名词，动词
