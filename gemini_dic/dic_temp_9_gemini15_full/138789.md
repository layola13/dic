numismatician:/ˌnuːmɪzˈmeɪʃən/| n. |钱币学家，钱币收藏家|A numismatician is a person who studies or collects coins, medals, paper money, and related items.  They are experts in the history, artistry, and value of these objects.|例句：He's a renowned numismatician with a vast collection of ancient Roman coins. 他是一位著名的钱币学家，拥有大量的古罗马钱币收藏。|例句：The numismatician carefully examined the coin for signs of wear and forgery.  这位钱币学家仔细检查了硬币是否有磨损和伪造的迹象。|近义词：coin collector, medal collector, numismatist|反义词：无
