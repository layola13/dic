interpose:/ˌɪntərˈpoʊz/| n. vt.|插入；介入；(尤指为调解)干预|The act of placing something between two other things; intervention, especially to mediate or settle a dispute. |例句：He interposed himself between the two arguing men. 他介入两个争吵的人之间。
例句：The judge interposed to prevent a fight.  法官介入以阻止一场打斗。|近义词：interject, intervene, mediate|反义词：ignore, neglect|名词，动词
