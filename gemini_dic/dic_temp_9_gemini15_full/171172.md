/ˌsaɪkoʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks/ | n. |心理语言学|The scientific study of the psychological processes involved in language, encompassing perception, understanding, production, and acquisition of language.  It explores the relationship between the mind and language, examining how humans process and use language. | Psycholinguistics explores how we understand and produce language, including how we process sounds, words, sentences, and meaning. | 近义词：linguistic psychology | 反义词：无
