nam:/næm/ | pron.|名字；姓名 | A name is a word or set of words by which a person, animal, place, or thing is known, addressed, or referred to.  | My name is John. 我的名字是约翰。|近义词： appellation, designation |反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What is the name of this flower? = 这朵花的名字是什么？


一般过去时:
What was your name again? = 你叫什么名字来着？
What was the name of the restaurant we went to? = 我们去的餐馆叫什么名字？


现在完成时:
What have you named your pet? = 你给你的宠物取了什么名字？
What has the company been named? = 这家公司叫什么名字？


过去完成时:
What had the baby been named before they changed it? = 在他们改名之前，婴儿叫什么名字？
What had the project been named before the rebranding? = 在重新品牌化之前，项目叫什么名字？


将来时:
What will you name your child? = 你将给你的孩子取什么名字？
What will the new product be named? = 新产品将被命名为什么？


虚拟语气:
If I knew his name, I would tell you. = 如果我知道他的名字，我会告诉你的。
I wish I knew what the name of that song was. = 我真希望我知道那首歌的名字。


被动语态:
The street was named after a famous writer. = 这条街是以一位著名作家的名字命名的。
The new building is being named "The Pinnacle". = 新建筑将被命名为“顶峰”。


疑问句:
What's your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What is the name of the street? = 这条街叫什么名字？


否定句:
I don't know his name. = 我不知道他的名字。
She didn't remember the name of the book. = 她不记得书的名字了。


PHRASE->
in the name of = 以…的名义
to name but a few = 仅举几例
make a name for oneself = 成名
beyond (all) names = 无与伦比的
name and fame = 名望
