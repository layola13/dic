protectorial: /prəˈtɛktəriəl/ | adj. | 保护性的，庇护的 |  Having the nature or function of protecting; protective.  Characterized by or showing protection or guardianship. |

例句：

The protectorial instincts of the mother bear were evident as she shielded her cubs from danger. = 母熊的保护本能很明显，她保护她的幼崽免受危险。

The government implemented protectorial measures to safeguard endangered species. = 政府实施了保护措施以保护濒危物种。


近义词：protective, safeguarding, sheltering, defensive, shielding

反义词：harmful, dangerous, destructive, endangering, unsafe


词性：形容词
