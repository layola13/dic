multiview: /ˈmʌltiˌvjuː/ | adj. | 多视角的，多视图的 | Relating to or using multiple viewpoints or perspectives, often in computer graphics or other visual representations.  This term often describes techniques that render a scene from several different angles simultaneously or combine views from multiple cameras or sensors. | Example:  The multiview rendering system produced a highly realistic 3D model. = 多视角渲染系统产生了高度逼真的3D模型。  The software offers multiview capabilities for enhanced visualization. = 该软件提供多视角功能，以增强可视化效果。|近义词：multiperspective, polyhedral | 反义词：single-view, monocular
