hits:/hɪts/| n. v.|打击；成功；热门歌曲|英文解析：As a noun, "hits" refers to successful blows or impacts, or popular songs or other items. As a verb, it means to strike or impact something forcefully.|例句：The boxer landed several powerful hits. 拳击手击中了几个强有力的拳。  The song was a huge hit. 这首歌非常受欢迎。|近义词：strikes, successes, popular songs|反义词：misses, failures|词性:n. v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hits the ball hard. = 他用力击球。
She hits the books every night. = 她每晚都认真学习。
