covenanter:/ˈkʌvənəntər/| n. |盟约者；契约者 | A person who signs or enters into a covenant; especially, one of the Scottish Presbyterians who in the 17th century bound themselves by a covenant to maintain the Presbyterian religion and the national covenant. |The covenanters fought bravely for their religious beliefs.  盟约者为他们的宗教信仰英勇战斗。|  supporter, subscriber | opponent, traitor | 名词
