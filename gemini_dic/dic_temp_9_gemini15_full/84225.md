half-section: /ˈhæf ˈsɛkʃən/ | n. | 半截；半部分 | A half-section is a portion of something that is exactly half of the whole.  It can refer to a physical object divided in two, or a section of text, a chapter, or a group.  |例句：The baker cut the cake into half-sections for easier serving.  面包师把蛋糕切成两半以便于分发。


The architect presented a half-section drawing to show the interior structure. 建筑师展示了一张剖面图以显示内部结构。|近义词：half, moiety, portion |反义词：whole, entirety

词源:

The word "half-section" is a compound word. "Half" derives from Old English *healf, meaning "half". "Section" comes from the Latin word *sectio, meaning "a cutting" or "division".  Therefore, "half-section" literally means "a half of a cut or division".
