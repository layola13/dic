refiring: /ˌriːˈfaɪərɪŋ/ | n. | 再射击；重新开火；解雇后重新雇用 | The act of firing again, either a weapon or an employee after previously dismissing them.  It can also refer to the process of re-igniting something, such as a furnace. | 例句：The soldier's refiring of his weapon secured their position.  士兵再次开火巩固了他们的阵地。 | 例句：The company's refiring of the employee after a period of unemployment was met with mixed reactions. 公司在一段时间的失业后重新雇用这位员工，引起了褒贬不一的反应。 | 近义词：reignition, re-employment, reinstatement | 反义词：dismissal, termination, layoff |  n.
