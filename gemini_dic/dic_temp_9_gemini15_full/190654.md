saucily:/ˈsɔːsəli/| adj.|轻浮地；调皮地；无礼地|In a bold and slightly impudent or cheeky manner.  Showing or expressing impudence or disrespect. |例句：She saucily retorted, "Mind your own business!" 她轻浮地回击道：“管好你自己的事！”

例句：He saucily winked at the teacher. 他调皮地向老师眨了眨眼。


近义词：cheekily, impudently, pertly, flippantly, brazenly


反义词：respectfully, submissively, timidly, politely


词性：形容词
