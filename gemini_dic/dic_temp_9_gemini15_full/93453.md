illuminize:/ɪˈluːmɪnaɪz/ | vt. |照亮；阐明；启迪|To light up; to make clear or understandable; to enlighten.|例句：The moon illuminated the night. 月亮照亮了夜晚。|例句：The professor's lecture illuminated the complex topic. 教授的讲座阐明了这个复杂的话题。|近义词：light up, clarify, enlighten, elucidate|反义词：obscure, darken, confuse


USAGE->

The sun illuminates the earth. = 太阳照亮地球。
The artist illuminates his ideas through his paintings. = 艺术家通过绘画阐明了他的想法。
