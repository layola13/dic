masticate:/ˈmæstɪkeɪt/| vt.|咀嚼，细嚼慢咽|To chew (food) |She masticated her food thoroughly before swallowing.|近义词：chew, munch, grind|反义词：swallow (without chewing)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He masticates his food carefully. = 他细嚼慢咽地吃东西。
Birds masticate their food with their beaks. = 鸟用喙咀嚼食物。

一般过去时:
She masticated the tough meat for a long time. = 她咀嚼了很久那块韧的肉。
He masticated the gum slowly. = 他慢慢地嚼口香糖。

现在完成时:
I have masticated all the food on my plate. = 我已经把盘子里的所有食物都嚼碎了。
The dog has masticated the bone completely. = 狗已经把骨头完全嚼碎了。

过去完成时:
He had masticated the food before swallowing it. = 他在吞咽之前已经把食物嚼碎了。
She had masticated the gum until it was tasteless. = 她把口香糖嚼到没味了。
