concertino:/ˌkɒnˌtʃɛrˈtiːnoʊ/| n. |协奏曲小品；小协奏曲 |A concertino is a small concerto, typically for a single instrument (or a small group of instruments) with a small orchestra.  It's shorter and less elaborate than a full-scale concerto. | Example: The composer wrote a charming concertino for flute and strings.  这位作曲家为长笛和弦乐器创作了一首动听的协奏曲小品。 |近义词：concerto (but smaller and less complex), solo |反义词：symphony (larger and more complex)
