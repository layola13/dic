computerising: /kəmˈpjuːtəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. |使电脑化；用电脑处理| The process of making something use computers or computer systems.  It involves integrating computer technology into various aspects of a system, process, or organization. |例句：The company is computerising its accounting system. 公司正在将其会计系统电脑化。

例句：They are computerising the library's catalog.  他们正在将图书馆的目录电脑化。


近义词：automate, digitize, digitalize


反义词：manualizing, de-computerizing


词性：动词
