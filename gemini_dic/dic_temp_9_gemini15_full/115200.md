manie:/ˈmeɪni/| n.|狂躁，躁狂症|A mental illness characterized by periods of intense excitement, euphoria, and sometimes delusions.|例句：His manic energy was infectious, driving the project forward at an incredible pace.  他的狂躁能量极具感染力，以令人难以置信的速度推进了项目。|近义词：mania, frenzy,狂热|反义词：depression, calmness, tranquility,平静
