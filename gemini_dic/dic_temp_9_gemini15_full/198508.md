shrinkages:/ˈʃrɪŋkɪdʒɪz/| n.|收缩；缩水；减少|The reduction in size, amount, or value of something.  It often refers to a decrease in the size of materials due to factors like washing, drying, or wear.  It can also refer to a decline in numbers or value in a broader context.|例句：The fabric experienced significant shrinkages after washing. 洗涤后织物严重缩水。|例句：There have been shrinkages in the company's workforce recently. 公司最近员工人数减少了。|近义词：reduction, decrease, diminution, contraction|反义词：expansion, increase, growth|名词
