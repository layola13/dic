purpose-built:/ˈpɜːpəs bɪlt/ | adj. | 特制的；专用建造的；为特定目的而建造的 | Designed and constructed for a specific purpose.  It implies that the building or object is not generic, but created to fulfill a particular need or function efficiently.  | 例句：The factory is purpose-built for the manufacture of microchips. 这家工厂是专门为制造微芯片而建造的。

例句：This purpose-built apartment complex caters specifically to elderly residents. 这座特制公寓大楼专门为老年居民提供服务。


近义词：specially designed, custom-built, tailor-made, dedicated


反义词：general-purpose, multi-purpose, versatile


词性:形容词
