intersperse:/ˌɪntərˈspɜːrs/ | vt. |散布；点缀；插入 | To scatter among or between other things; to insert at intervals. |例句：The teacher interspersed jokes throughout her lecture to keep the students engaged.  老师在讲座中穿插笑话以保持学生的参与度。

例句：Wildflowers were interspersed among the tall grasses. 野花散落在高高的草丛中。


近义词：scatter, distribute, mix, sprinkle | 反义词：concentrate, cluster, group


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He intersperses anecdotes in his speeches. 他在演讲中穿插轶事。
She intersperses her writing with vivid descriptions. 她用生动的描写点缀她的文章。

虚拟语气:

I wish he had interspersed his speech with humor. 我希望他能在演讲中穿插一些幽默。


被动语态:

Pictures are interspersed with the text in the book. 图片穿插在书中的文字中。


疑问句:
How are the activities interspersed throughout the day?  活动在一天中是如何穿插安排的？
Where are the advertisements interspersed in the magazine? 广告在杂志中穿插在哪些地方？


否定句:
He did not intersperse his speech with any jokes. 他在演讲中没有穿插任何笑话。
The writer did not intersperse the story with unnecessary details. 作者没有在故事中穿插不必要的细节。


PHRASE->
intersperse with: 用…点缀；在…中穿插
intersperse among: 散布在…之中
intersperse throughout:  贯穿始终地散布
intersperse between:  散布于…之间
intersperse A with B: 用B点缀A


词源:
inter- (between) + spers- (scatter)  源自拉丁语，inter 表示“在…之间”，spers-  与“散布”相关。
