/ˌkæməˈrælɪstɪks/| n. |照相术；摄影术|The study and practice of photography, especially in its early stages.  Cameralistics encompasses the technical aspects of image creation, including optics, chemistry, and the manipulation of photographic equipment.|The early practitioners of cameralistics were meticulous in their approach to image making.  早期摄影术的实践者们对图像制作非常细致。|photography, photomechanics| (no direct antonym, as it's a field of study)|名词
