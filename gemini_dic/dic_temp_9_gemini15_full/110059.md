licentiously:/laɪˈsɛnʃəsli/| adj.|放荡地；纵欲地；无节制地|Acting or done in a way that shows a lack of moral restraint; disregarding accepted rules or standards of behavior.|The band lived licentiously, indulging in drugs and alcohol.|近义词：debauchedly, dissolutely, lasciviously, libertinely|反义词：chastely, modestly, virtuously


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lives licentiously. = 他生活放荡。
They behave licentiously. = 他们行为放荡。

一般过去时:
She lived licentiously in her youth. = 她年轻时生活放荡。
They partied licentiously all night. = 他们通宵达旦地放荡狂欢。

现在完成时:
He has lived licentiously for many years. = 他放荡地生活了很多年。
They have behaved licentiously throughout their careers. = 他们的职业生涯一直行为放荡。

过去完成时:
He had lived licentiously before he met her. = 在遇见她之前，他生活放荡。

将来时:
He will continue to live licentiously. = 他将继续放荡地生活。
They will likely behave licentiously at the party. = 他们很可能会在派对上行为放荡。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:  (This adjective is rarely used passively.)  The behavior was described as licentious. =  这种行为被描述为放荡的。
The lifestyle was considered licentious. =  这种生活方式被认为是放荡的。

词源:
