hodgepodge:/ˈhɒdʒpɒdʒ/|/ˈhɑːdʒpɑːdʒ/| n. |大杂烩；混杂物；乱七八糟的东西 |A hodgepodge is a mixture of different things that are not well organized or related to each other.  It often implies a lack of coherence or order. |The garage was a hodgepodge of old tools, broken furniture, and discarded toys. 车库里堆满了旧工具、破家具和丢弃的玩具，乱七八糟的。|mishmash, medley, jumble, gallimaufry |order, organization, system |名词
