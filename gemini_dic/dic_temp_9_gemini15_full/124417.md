motorboating:/ˈmoʊtərˌboʊtɪŋ/| n.|摩托艇运动；乘摩托艇旅行|The activity of operating or riding in a motorboat.  It can refer to recreational boating, racing, or other activities involving motorboats.|例句：We went motorboating on the lake yesterday. = 我们昨天在湖上乘摩托艇游玩了。|例句：The annual motorboating race attracted a large crowd. = 每年的摩托艇比赛吸引了大批观众。|近义词：powerboating|反义词：sailing(帆船运动)
