outbribed:/ˌaʊtˈbraɪbd/| v.|贿赂超过；胜过贿赂|To surpass or exceed in bribery; to outdo someone in the use of bribes.  This is not a standard dictionary word; it's a newly coined word formed by combining "out-" (meaning to surpass) and "bribed" (the past participle of "bribe").|例句：The rival company tried to outbribe us, but our offer was ultimately more enticing.  竞争对手试图通过贿赂超过我们，但我们的提议最终更有吸引力。


近义词：outmaneuver, outsmart, outdo (in bribery) |反义词：be outbribed by

词性：动词

He outbribes his competitors regularly. = 他经常贿赂超过他的竞争对手。
The company outbribes its rivals to secure contracts. = 这家公司通过贿赂超过竞争对手来确保合同。
