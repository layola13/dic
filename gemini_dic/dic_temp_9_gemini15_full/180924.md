reigning: /ˈreɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 当权的；统治的；盛行的；占优势的 |  Currently holding power or dominance.  |例句：The reigning champion is undefeated. = 现任冠军保持不败。

例句：The reigning monarch is very popular. = 当今君主非常受欢迎。


近义词：ruling, dominant, prevailing, current, prevalent | 反义词：former, past, deposed, ousted


词源:  From the present participle of reign (v.).  The verb "reign" ultimately derives from the Old French *reigner, from the Latin *regnare, related to rex ("king").
