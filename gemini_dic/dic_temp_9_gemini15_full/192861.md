seasick:/ˈsiːsɪk/ | adj. |晕船的，晕船的；乘船感到不适的 | Suffering from nausea and dizziness caused by the motion of a boat or ship.  | Example:  I get seasick easily, so I always take medication before a boat trip. 我很容易晕船，所以乘船旅行前我总是吃药。  |近义词：nauseous, queasy, unwell | 反义词：well, healthy | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is seasick on the ferry. = 他在渡轮上晕船。
She is always seasick when she travels by boat. = 她乘船旅行时总是晕船。


一般过去时:
I was seasick during the entire voyage. = 在整个航程中我都晕船。
They were seasick after the storm. = 暴风雨过后他们晕船了。


现在完成时:
He has been seasick since the boat left the harbor. = 自从船离开港口他就一直晕船。
She has never been seasick before. = 她以前从未晕船过。


过去完成时:
I had been seasick for hours before we reached land. = 我们到达陆地之前我已经晕船好几个小时了。
They had been seasick all day, and were very relieved to finally reach shore. = 他们晕船了一整天，终于到达岸边时非常轻松。


将来时:
I will be seasick if the sea is rough. = 如果海浪汹涌，我会晕船。
She thinks she will be seasick on the cruise. = 她认为她在邮轮上会晕船。


虚拟语气:
If I weren't so prone to seasickness, I would love to go sailing. = 如果我不那么容易晕船，我会喜欢去航海。
I wish I hadn't eaten so much before the boat trip; I wouldn't be so seasick. = 我希望在乘船旅行前我没有吃那么多；我不会这么晕船。


被动语态:  (Seasick is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Are you seasick? = 你晕船吗？
Is she feeling seasick? = 她感觉晕船吗？


否定句:
I am not seasick. = 我没有晕船。
He wasn't seasick on the calm waters. = 在平静的水域他并没有晕船。


PHRASE->
become seasick = 晕船
feel seasick = 感到晕船
prevent seasickness = 预防晕船
seasickness remedy = 晕船药
suffer from seasickness = 患晕船病
