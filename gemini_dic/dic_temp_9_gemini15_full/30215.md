cellaring:/ˈsɛləriŋ/| n. |酒窖贮藏；储藏（尤指酒）|The process of storing wine in a cellar to age and improve.  It also refers to the storage of other items, such as vegetables, in a cool, dark place.|例句：Proper cellaring is crucial for developing the full potential of fine wines.  良好的酒窖贮藏对于充分发挥优质葡萄酒的潜力至关重要。

例句：We're cellaring these potatoes to keep them fresh through the winter. 我们正在把这些土豆储藏起来，以保持它们在整个冬季的新鲜。|近义词：storage, aging (for wine)|反义词：immediate consumption


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cellars his finest wines for many years. = 他把最好的酒窖藏多年。
The restaurant cellars its vegetables in a cool, dark room. = 这家餐厅在一个阴凉黑暗的房间里储藏蔬菜。
