lessener: /ˈlɛsənər/ | n. | 减少者；减轻者 | A person or thing that lessens something.  Someone or something that makes something smaller, less intense, or less important. | Example: The new law was intended to be a lessener of crime. 这项新法律旨在减少犯罪。  |近义词：reducer, diminisher, mitigator | 反义词：intensifier, magnifier | 名词
