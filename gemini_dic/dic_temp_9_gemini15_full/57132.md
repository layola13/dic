doveling:/ˈdʌvəlɪŋ/| n. | 幼鸽；初出茅庐者 | A young pigeon or dove.  It can also refer to someone who is inexperienced or naive. | Example: The doveling took flight for the first time.  幼鸽第一次展翅飞翔。The young doveling in politics made some naive statements.  这位政坛上的新手发表了一些天真的言论。|近义词：young pigeon, fledgling, novice |反义词：veteran, expert
