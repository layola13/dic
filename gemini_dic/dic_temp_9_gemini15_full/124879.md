muddier:/ˈmʌdiər/| adj. |泥泞的，更浑浊的 |More muddy; containing or resembling mud.  This word describes something that is more covered in mud or has a thicker, mud-like consistency than something else. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something unclear or confused.|例句：The path became muddier after the rain. 雨后小路变得更加泥泞。 The water in the river is muddier than usual. 河水比平时更浑浊。|近义词：muddy, mirey, sludgy, turbid |反义词：clear, clean, transparent
