posts:/poʊsts/ | n. |帖子，柱子，岗位|  A post is a piece of writing, an image, video, etc., that is shared online on a social media platform or website.  It can also refer to a physical upright support, such as a fence post. |例句：She made a post about her vacation on Instagram. = 她在Instagram上发布了她度假的照片。

She put up some new fence posts. = 她竖起了几根新的栅栏柱子。|近义词：entries, messages, articles, columns |反义词：None


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He posts regularly on his blog. = 他定期在他的博客上发帖。
She posts pictures of her cat every day. = 她每天都发她猫的照片。

一般过去时:
I posted a comment on your article yesterday. = 我昨天在你的文章下评论了。
They posted the news on their website last night. = 他们昨晚在他们的网站上发布了新闻。

现在完成时:
She has posted over 100 pictures this month. = 这个月她已经发了超过100张照片。
He has posted several updates about his trip. = 他已经发布了几次关于他旅行的更新。

过去完成时:
I had already posted the update before he called. = 他打电话之前我已经发布了更新。
They had posted the announcement before the meeting started. = 会议开始之前他们已经发布了公告。

将来时:
I will post the photos when I get home. = 我回家后会发布照片。
She will post the results tomorrow. = 她明天会公布结果。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would post more often. = 如果我有时间，我会更频繁地发帖。
I wish I had posted that picture sooner. = 我希望我早点发那张照片。

被动语态:
The news was posted online early this morning. = 新闻今天早上很早就被发布到网上了。
The comments are posted anonymously. = 评论是匿名发布的。

疑问句:
When did you post that? = 你什么时候发的那个？
Have you posted the report yet? = 你已经发布报告了吗？

否定句:
I didn't post anything today. = 我今天什么都没发。
She hasn't posted on social media for weeks. = 她好几个星期都没在社交媒体上发帖了。
