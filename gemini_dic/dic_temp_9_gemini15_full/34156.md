clamatores: /ˌklæməˈtɔːriz/ | n. |鸣禽类，叫禽类 | A group of passerine birds characterized by their vocalizations.  They are a diverse group, and the classification is now largely obsolete, having been replaced by more phylogenetically accurate groupings.  | Example: The clamatores were once considered a suborder, but now their members are distributed across several different lineages.  鸣禽类曾经被认为是一个亚目，但现在它们的成员分布在几个不同的谱系中。| 近义词: passerines (a broader term)| 反义词:  (no direct antonym, as it's a taxonomic grouping)| 名词
