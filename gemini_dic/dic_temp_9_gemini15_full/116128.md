marxist:/ˈmɑːksɪst/| adj. n. |马克思主义的；马克思主义者|relating to or supporting the political and economic theories of Karl Marx and Friedrich Engels, especially as developed after Marx's death;  a person who supports these theories. |例句：His Marxist analysis of capitalism was highly critical.  他的马克思主义资本主义分析非常批判性。

例句：Many prominent intellectuals were self-proclaimed Marxists during the Cold War. 许多著名的知识分子在冷战期间自称为马克思主义者。


近义词：communist, socialist (in some contexts), revolutionary


反义词：capitalist, conservative, libertarian


词性：形容词，名词
