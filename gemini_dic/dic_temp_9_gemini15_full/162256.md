pre-war: /ˌpriːˈwɔːr/ | adj. | 战前的，战前的 | Existing or occurring before a particular war, especially World War I or World War II.  | 例句：Pre-war cars were often less reliable than modern vehicles. 战前的汽车通常不如现代汽车可靠。  | 例句：The pre-war economic system was very different from what we have today. 战前的经济体系与我们今天所拥有的截然不同。 | 近义词：antebellum (especially referring to the American Civil War), pre-conflict | 反义词：post-war, wartime
