horrification:/ˌhɒrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 恐怖；骇人听闻的事 | The act of horrifying or the state of being horrified; something that causes horror. 例句：The description of the accident caused widespread horrification.  事故的描述引起了广泛的恐惧。
