exiguousnesses:/ɪɡˈzɪɡjuəsˌnɛsɪz/| n.|极少；微不足道；少量 |The plural form of "exiguousness," referring to instances or states of being meager or scanty. It denotes multiple occurrences of insufficiency or deficiency.|例句：The exiguousnesses of the funding hampered the research project.  资金的匮乏阻碍了研究项目的进行。The multiple exiguousnesses of the data made it hard to draw strong conclusions. 数据的多重不足使得难以得出强有力的结论。|近义词：scarcities, deficiencies, insufficiencies |反义词：abundances, plentitudes
