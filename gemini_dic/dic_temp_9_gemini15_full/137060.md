nonstories:/ˈnɒnˌstɔːriz/| n.|非新闻，不值得报道的事情，无足轻重的小事|Events or situations that are not considered newsworthy or significant enough to be reported as news.  They often lack the elements of a compelling narrative or public interest.|例句：The daily grind of office life is often filled with nonstories. 日常的办公室生活充满了不值得报道的小事。
The constant bickering between the neighbors is a nonstory to the police. 邻居之间的不断争吵对警察来说是件微不足道的事。|近义词：trivialities, insignificances, trivialities, minutiae|反义词：news, headline, major event|名词
