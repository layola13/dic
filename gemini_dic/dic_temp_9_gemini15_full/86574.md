helicopters:/ˈhɛlɪkɒptərz/| n.|直升机|A helicopter is a type of aircraft that uses rotating blades to generate lift and propulsion.  It can take off and land vertically, and hover in the air.|例句：The helicopters flew over the city. 直升机飞过城市上空。

例句：We took a helicopter tour of the Grand Canyon. 我们乘坐直升机游览了大峡谷。


近义词：rotorcraft, chopper|反义词：fixed-wing aircraft
