penthouse:/ˈpɛntaʊs/ | n. | 顶层公寓；阁楼式公寓 | A penthouse is a luxury apartment located on the top floor of a building, often with additional features like a rooftop terrace or balcony.  |  例句：They bought a stunning penthouse with breathtaking city views. 他们买了一套拥有令人叹为观止城市景观的豪华顶层公寓。

例句：The penthouse suite offered unparalleled luxury and comfort. 这套顶层套房提供了无与伦比的奢华和舒适。


近义词：top-floor apartment, luxury apartment, rooftop apartment | 反义词：basement apartment, ground-floor apartment | 词性:名词
