out-pension:/ˌaʊtˈpɛnʃən/| n.|退休金; 离职金|A pension paid to someone after they have left their job.  It is often a lump sum payment in addition to any ongoing pension.  |例句：He received a generous out-pension when he retired from the company. 他从公司退休时获得了丰厚的离职金。|例句：The out-pension did not cover all of his expected retirement expenses. 这笔离职金并没有涵盖他所有预期的退休支出。|近义词：severance pay, retirement gratuity|反义词：N/A
