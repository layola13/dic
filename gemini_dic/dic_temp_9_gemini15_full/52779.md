dilemma:/dɪˈlɛmə/ | n. |困境，进退两难 | a situation in which a difficult choice has to be made between two or more alternatives, especially equally undesirable ones.  |  He was in a dilemma about whether to tell her the truth or not. 他左右为难，不知道是否该告诉她真相。| predicament, quandary, bind | solution, resolution | 名词
