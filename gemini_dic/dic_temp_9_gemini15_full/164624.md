preexistence: /ˌpriːɪɡˈzɪstəns/ | n. | 预先存在；先有；先在 | The state or fact of existing before something else, especially before birth.  It refers to the belief that a soul existed before its embodiment in a physical body. |例句：The theory of preexistence suggests that our souls existed before birth.  先存说认为我们的灵魂在出生前就存在。

The concept of preexistence is central to some religious beliefs.  先存的概念是某些宗教信仰的核心。


近义词：prior existence, antecedence, anteriority | 反义词：post-existence, subsequent existence | 词性: noun
