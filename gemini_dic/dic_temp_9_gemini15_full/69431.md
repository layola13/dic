feels:/fiːlz/ | v. |感觉；摸起来；认为 | To experience an emotion or sensation; to perceive something through touch; to believe or have an opinion. |例句：I feel happy today. = 我今天很快乐。  The fabric feels soft. = 这布料摸起来很柔软。|近义词： senses, perceives, thinks, believes | 反义词:  numbs, ignores, disbelieves |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I feel happy. = 我感到快乐。
He feels tired. = 他感到疲倦。

一般过去时:
She felt sad yesterday. = 她昨天感到悲伤。
They felt relieved after the exam. = 考试后他们感到轻松。

现在完成时:
I have felt better. = 我感觉好多了。
She has felt lonely since he left. = 自从他离开后，她一直感到孤独。

过去完成时:
I had felt nervous before the speech. = 演讲前我感到紧张。
We had felt hopeful before receiving the news. = 在收到消息之前，我们曾感到充满希望。

将来时:
I will feel better after a good night's sleep. = 睡个好觉后我会感觉好些。
They will feel proud of their achievements. = 他们会为自己的成就感到自豪。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would feel differently. = 如果我是你，我会感觉不一样。
I wish I felt more confident. = 我希望我能更有信心。

被动语态:
The material is felt to be rough. = 这种材料摸起来很粗糙。(被动语态较少用于此词)
His emotions were felt by everyone present. = 在场的人都感受到了他的情绪。(被动语态较少用于此词)

疑问句:
How do you feel today? = 你今天感觉怎么样？
Do you feel comfortable? = 你感觉舒服吗？

否定句:
I don't feel well. = 我感觉不舒服。
He didn't feel appreciated. = 他感觉不被欣赏。


PHRASE->
feel like: 想要；感觉像
feel for: 对...同情；摸索
feel up to: 感觉能够胜任
feel at home: 感到宾至如归
feel free to: 请随意；不必客气
