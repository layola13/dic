impart:/ɪmˈpɑːrt/| vt.|传授；告知；赋予；给予|To communicate (information, knowledge, etc.) to someone; to bestow or confer (a quality, property, etc.)|The teacher imparted her wisdom to her students.  老师把她的智慧传授给学生们。

He imparted the news to his family. 他把这个消息告诉了他的家人。
