outrig:/ˈaʊtrɪɡ/ | n. |支架；平衡木；舷外支架 |A stabilizing support, typically a beam or spar projecting from the side of a boat or aircraft to provide balance.  |例句：The outrigger on the canoe prevented it from capsizing.  独木舟上的平衡木防止它倾覆。

例句：The airplane's outriggers provided additional stability during takeoff. 飞机的舷外支架在起飞时提供了额外的稳定性。


近义词：brace, support, stay | 反义词：None specifically applicable.


词源:  From Middle English *outrigge, from Old Norse útrígr "outside row," from útr "out" + rígr "row, line."  The nautical sense developed from the use of projecting beams to support oars.
