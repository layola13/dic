kibitzes:/kɪˈbɪtsɪz/| v.|在一旁观看；瞎指点；插嘴|To watch or observe (something) especially in a casual or uninvited way; to offer unsolicited advice or criticism.|例句：He kibitzes at the poker game, offering unwanted advice. 他在一旁观看扑克牌游戏，提供不必要的建议。|例句：Don't kibitz while I'm trying to concentrate. 我正努力集中精力，别插嘴。|近义词：watch, observe, interfere, meddle|反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kibitzes every game. = 他每场比赛都瞎指点。
They kibitz from the sidelines. = 他们在一旁瞎指点。

一般过去时:
She kibitzed my attempts at painting. = 她对我的绘画尝试指手画脚。
He kibitzed during the important meeting. = 他在重要的会议上插嘴。

现在完成时:
He has kibitzed all afternoon. = 他整个下午都在瞎指点。
They have kibitzed throughout the entire project. = 他们在整个项目过程中一直在插嘴。

过去完成时:
She had kibitzed before I even started. = 我还没开始她就开始插嘴了。
They had kibitzed the entire process already. = 他们已经对整个过程指手画脚了。

将来时:
He will kibitz at the next game. = 他将在下一场比赛中瞎指点。

虚拟语气:
If he kibitzed less, people would appreciate him more. = 如果他少插嘴，人们会更欣赏他。
I wish he wouldn't kibitz so much. = 我希望他不要总是插嘴。

被动语态:
His work was kibitzed by several colleagues. = 他的工作受到了几位同事的瞎指点。
The plan was kibitzed before it even started. = 这个计划还没开始就被指手画脚了。

疑问句:
Does he often kibitz? = 他经常插嘴吗？

否定句:
He didn't kibitz during the performance. = 他在表演过程中没有插嘴。
They chose not to kibitz on their friend's work. = 他们选择不干涉朋友的工作。


PHRASE->
kibitz on = 对…指手画脚
kibitz at = 在…旁边瞎指点
kibitz about = 闲聊；对…说三道四
kibitz during = 在…期间插嘴

词源:
The word "kibitz" likely originates from Yiddish *kibets*, meaning "a visit," "gossip," or "chat."  The sense of uninvited observation and commentary evolved from this original meaning.
