interpreters:/ɪnˈtɜːprɪtərz/ | n. |口译员，翻译员 | A person who translates spoken or signed language from one language into another. Interpreters work in various settings, such as international conferences, courtrooms, and medical appointments. |Example:  The conference hired several interpreters to facilitate communication between delegates from different countries.  会议聘请了几位翻译来促进来自不同国家的代表之间的沟通。 |近义词: translators, interpreters, conveyers|反义词: N/A

The interpreter interprets the speech for the audience.  口译员为听众口译演讲。
She interprets documents for the legal team. 她为法律团队翻译文件。

一般过去时:
