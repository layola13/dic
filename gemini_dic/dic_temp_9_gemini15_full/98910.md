intensive-cares:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪv kɛrz/ | n. | 集中护理，重症监护 | Intensive care refers to specialized medical care for patients with severe or life-threatening illnesses or injuries, requiring close monitoring and advanced treatment.  It often involves sophisticated equipment and a high nurse-to-patient ratio. |  The patient was moved to intensive care after suffering a heart attack.  这位病人心脏病发作后被转移到重症监护室。 The hospital's intensive care unit is state-of-the-art. 这家医院的重症监护室是先进的。 | critical care, critical medical care |  general ward care |  n.
