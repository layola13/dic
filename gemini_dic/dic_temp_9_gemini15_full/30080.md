cdrom's:/ˈsiːdiːrɒmz/ | n. | 光驱的；光盘的 | The possessive form of "CD-ROM," indicating ownership or relationship.  It refers to something belonging to or associated with a CD-ROM drive or a CD-ROM disc. | Example:  The cdrom's drive is making a strange noise.  光驱发出奇怪的声音。 |近义词：optical disc's, compact disc's |反义词：None.
