caress:/kəˈrɛs/ | vt. n. |爱抚，抚摸 |To gently touch or stroke someone or something.  |例句：She caressed the soft fur of the kitten. 她轻轻抚摸着小猫柔软的毛。|例句：The gentle caress of the breeze felt good on her skin. 微风的轻抚让她感觉很舒服。|近义词：stroke, fondle, pat |反义词：strike, hit, slap


USAGE->
