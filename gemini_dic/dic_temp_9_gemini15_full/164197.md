prediversion:/ˌpriːdɪˈvɜːrʒən/| n. |预先转移，预先改道|The act of diverting something beforehand;  preemptive diversion.  This word is rarely used and may be considered a neologism (newly coined word). It combines "pre-" (before) and "diversion" (the act of diverting).|例句：The prediversion of funds prevented the project from being severely underfunded.  预先转移资金防止了项目严重资金不足。 The prediversion of traffic helped to avoid a major traffic jam. 预先改道交通帮助避免了严重的交通堵塞。|近义词：preemptive measure, advance planning, prior rerouting|反义词：postponement, delay,  unplanned detour|名词
