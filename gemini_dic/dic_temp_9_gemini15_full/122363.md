miz:/mɪz/| n. |米兹（女子姓名）|Miz is a short form of names like Elizabeth or Miriam. It can also be used as a term of address for a woman, similar to "ma'am."|例句：My name is Miz Johnson.|近义词：Madam, Ma'am|反义词：无
