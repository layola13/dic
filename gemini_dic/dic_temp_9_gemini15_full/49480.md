delusive: /dɪˈluːsɪv/ | adj. | 欺骗性的；虚幻的；迷惑的 |  giving a false impression; misleading.  It suggests something is real or true when it is not.  | 例句：His dreams of wealth were delusive. 他的财富梦是虚幻的。

The promises of quick riches proved delusive. 快速致富的承诺被证明是欺骗性的。


近义词： deceptive, illusory, misleading, fallacious | 反义词：real, genuine, true, accurate


词源:  From Middle English *delusyf, from Old French *delussif, from Latin *delūdō ("I deceive, mock, cheat, beguile").  The root is *ludō ("I play"). This reflects the sense of being tricked or played upon by a false impression.
