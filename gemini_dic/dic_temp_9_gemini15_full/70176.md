fibreless:/ˈfaɪbər(lə)s/| adj.|无纤维的；无纤维质的|Lacking fiber or fibers.  The term usually refers to a material or food lacking in dietary fiber.|例句：The fibreless diet led to digestive problems.  无纤维饮食导致消化问题。

例句：This fibreless fabric is very smooth to the touch.  这种无纤维织物摸起来非常光滑。


近义词：fiberless (American spelling)


反义词：fibrous


词性：形容词
