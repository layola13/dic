preagitate: /ˌpriːˈædʒɪteɪt/ | vt. | 预先激动；预先煽动 | To stir up or excite beforehand; to agitate in advance.  |  The politician preagitated the crowd before the rally.  政治家在集会前煽动了人群。The news preagitated her, causing her to lose sleep. 这个消息让她提前感到焦虑，导致她失眠。 |近义词：excite, stir up, incite | 反义词：calm, soothe, pacify | 动词
