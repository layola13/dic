devastators:/ˈdɛvəsteɪtərz/| n.|破坏者，毁灭者|Those who cause widespread destruction or devastation.  People or things that cause great damage or destruction.|The floods were devastating, and the devastators were the relentless rains.|近义词：destroyers, wreckers, ruiners|反义词：rescuers, saviors, protectors|名词

现在完成时:
The locusts have been devastators of the harvest for centuries. = 蝗虫几个世纪以来一直是收成的破坏者。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
