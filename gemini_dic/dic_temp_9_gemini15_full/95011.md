in-your-stocking-feet: /ɪn jɔːr ˈstɒkɪŋ fiːt/ | adj. phrase | 赤脚的，光脚的 | Wearing socks only, without shoes.  |例句：He walked in-your-stocking-feet across the cold floor. 他光着脚丫子走过冰冷的地板。|近义词：barefoot, sockfooted |反义词：shod, wearing shoes


USAGE->
This phrase is primarily adjectival and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, tense variations are not applicable.  It describes a state of being.
