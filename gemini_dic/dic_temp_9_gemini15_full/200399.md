skyrocket:/ˈskaɪˌrɒkɪt/|/ˈskaɪˌrɑːkɪt/| vt. n. |迅速上涨；直线上升；火箭 |To increase rapidly; to rise quickly to a high level.  |例句：House prices skyrocketed after the announcement of the new development. 房价在新开发项目公布后迅速上涨。
The company's profits skyrocketed last year.  该公司去年的利润猛增。|近义词：soar, surge, climb, boom |反义词：plummet, plunge, decrease, decline|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price of oil skyrockets every year. = 石油价格每年都在飙升。
Profits skyrocket when the market improves. = 市场好转时，利润就会猛增。

一般过去时:
The stock price skyrocketed after the good news. = 好消息传出后，股价暴涨。
Sales skyrocketed during the holiday season. = 假日期间销售额激增。

现在完成时:
House prices have skyrocketed in recent years. = 近年来房价已飞涨。
The value of the company has skyrocketed since its inception. = 自公司成立以来，其价值已大幅上升。

过去完成时:
The stock market had skyrocketed before the crash. = 在股市崩盘之前，股市曾大幅上涨。
Inflation had skyrocketed before the government intervened. = 在政府干预之前，通货膨胀已经飙升。

将来时:
Experts predict that energy prices will skyrocket next year. = 专家预测明年能源价格将飙升。
They believe that the demand will skyrocket in the next quarter. = 他们相信下个季度的需求将会激增。

虚拟语气:
If the economy improved, the stock prices would skyrocket. = 如果经济好转，股价就会暴涨。
If the project were successful, its value would skyrocket. = 如果项目成功，其价值将会猛增。

被动语态:
The price was skyrocketed by the sudden demand. = 价格因突然的需求而暴涨。
The sales figures were skyrocketed by the successful marketing campaign. = 成功的营销活动使销售额激增。

疑问句:
Will the share prices skyrocket after the merger? = 合并后股价会暴涨吗？
Did sales skyrocket after the advertising campaign? = 广告宣传后销售额是否激增？

否定句:
The price did not skyrocket as expected. = 价格并未像预期那样飙升。
Sales did not skyrocket despite the marketing efforts. = 尽管进行了营销努力，销售额并没有激增。
