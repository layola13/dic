disrepairs:/ˌdɪsrɪˈpeərz/ | n. | 修理不良；损坏状态 | The house was in a state of disrepair.  房子破败不堪。|近义词：damage, deterioration, dilapidation | 反义词：repair, restoration


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The house shows signs of disrepair. = 这房子显示出失修的迹象。
Disrepair is a common problem in old buildings. = 失修是旧建筑物中的常见问题。


一般过去时:
The bridge fell into disrepair after the flood. = 洪水过后，桥梁破损不堪。
The car was in a state of disrepair after the accident. = 事故后，汽车处于损坏状态。

The neglect has led to significant disrepair. = 忽视导致了严重的损坏。


过去完成时:

将来时:

If we don't act soon, the infrastructure will fall into disrepair. = 如果我们不尽快采取行动，基础设施将会破损。
