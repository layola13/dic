luxurious:/lʌɡˈʒʊəriəs/| adj.|奢侈的，豪华的 | characterized by luxury or extravagance;  lavish.  It suggests a high degree of comfort, elegance, and richness.|例句：They live in a luxurious apartment overlooking Central Park. 他们住在俯瞰中央公园的豪华公寓里。|例句：The hotel offered luxurious amenities, including a spa and a gourmet restaurant. 这家酒店提供豪华的设施，包括水疗中心和美食餐厅。|近义词：opulent, lavish, sumptuous, deluxe|反义词：frugal, austere, modest, simple
