epidermoid:/ˈɛpɪdɜːrmɔɪd/ | n. |表皮样瘤，表皮样细胞的 |  A tumor resembling the epidermis (outer layer of skin) in structure and appearance. It may be benign or malignant. Epidermoid cysts are common benign tumors. | Example: The doctor confirmed the growth was a benign epidermoid cyst and scheduled its removal. 医生确认该肿块为良性表皮样囊肿，并安排了手术切除。|近义词：epithelial tumor, keratinizing cyst|反义词：dermoid cyst (although both are cysts, they have different origins)|名词
