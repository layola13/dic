sentinelling:/ˈsɛntɪnəlɪŋ/ | v. |站岗；放哨；警戒 |The act of acting as a sentinel; keeping watch.  It often implies a solitary or isolated vigil. |例句：The soldier spent the night sentinelling the border.  士兵整夜在边境站岗。

例句：The lonely lighthouse keeper spent his days sentinelling the coast.  孤独的灯塔看守人整天在海岸警戒。


近义词：guarding, watching, patrolling, keeping watch | 反义词：neglecting, ignoring


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sentinels the gate every night. = 他每晚都守卫着大门。
The guards sentinel the perimeter of the base. = 警卫们守护着基地的周边。

一般过去时:
She sentinelled the camp all night. = 她整夜守卫着营地。
The soldiers sentinelled the bridge for hours. = 士兵们在桥上站岗了好几个小时。

现在完成时:

The watchmen have sentinelled the city walls since dawn. = 自黎明以来，守夜人一直在守护着城墙。

过去完成时:

The guards had sentinelled the building all night long before the incident. = 在事件发生之前，警卫们整夜都在守护着那座建筑物。

将来时:
She will sentinel the checkpoint tomorrow. = 她明天将要守卫检查站。

虚拟语气:
