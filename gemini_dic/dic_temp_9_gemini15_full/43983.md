crimed:/kraɪmd/| v.|犯罪，犯过错|To commit a crime or crimes; to be convicted of a crime.  It is often used informally and can imply a less serious offense than "committed a crime."|例句：He crimed out last night.|近义词：offended, transgressed,  committed a crime|反义词:obeyed, complied
