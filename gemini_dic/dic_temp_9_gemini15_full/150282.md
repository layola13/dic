panegyry:/ˈpænədʒəri/| n.|颂词，赞颂|A formal expression of praise, typically one delivered publicly; a eulogy.|The mayor delivered a panegyry at the ceremony, praising the city's founders. 市长在仪式上发表了颂词，赞扬了城市的创建者。| eulogy, encomium, tribute | criticism, condemnation |名词
