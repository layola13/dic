rainiest: /ˈreɪniəst/ | adj. | 最多雨的，雨最大的 |  The superlative form of "rainy," indicating the highest degree of rain or rainfall.  It describes a place, time, or period that experiences the most rain. |


例句：

This has been the rainiest summer on record. = 这是有记录以来雨水最多的一個夏天。

The rainiest region in the country is the northwest. = 该国雨水最多的地区是西北部。


近义词：wettest, most rainy

反义词：driest, sunniest


词性:形容词
