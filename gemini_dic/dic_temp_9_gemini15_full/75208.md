frogfish: /ˈfrɒɡfɪʃ/| n. |蛙鱼|A frogfish is a type of anglerfish characterized by its frog-like appearance,  with a large, flattened body,  and a lure to attract prey.  They are masters of camouflage and ambush predators, often blending seamlessly with their environment.|例句：The frogfish sat motionless on the ocean floor, its lure swaying gently to attract unsuspecting shrimp.  蛙鱼一动不动地坐在海底，它的诱饵轻轻摇摆以吸引毫无戒心的虾。|近义词：anglerfish, angler|反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑fast-moving fish as a contrast)|名词
