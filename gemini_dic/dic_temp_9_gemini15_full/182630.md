reordained:/ˌriːɔːrˈdeɪnd/| v.|重新任命，再次授圣职|To ordain again; to reinstate someone to a clerical office after a period of suspension or removal.  The verb implies a previous ordination that was perhaps revoked or suspended.|例句：The disgraced priest was eventually reordained after completing a period of penance.  这位名誉扫地的牧师在完成一段忏悔期后最终被重新任命为神职人员。
The bishop decided to reordain the priest who had been wrongly dismissed. 主教决定重新任命那位被错误解雇的牧师。|近义词：reappoint, reinstate, re-establish|反义词：defrock, unordain|动词
