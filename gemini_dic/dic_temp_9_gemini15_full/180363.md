refurbishes:/rɪˈfɜːrbɪʃɪz/ | v. | 翻新；修理；重新装修 | To renovate and redecorate (something, especially a building) to improve its appearance or function.  | The company refurbishes old buildings and sells them at a profit. 这家公司翻新旧建筑并以盈利出售。They are refurbishing the kitchen. 他们正在翻修厨房。| renovate, remodel, recondition | destroy, demolish, ruin | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hotel refurbishes its rooms every year. = 这家酒店每年都翻新客房。
He regularly refurbishes his old cars. = 他定期翻新他的旧车。


一般过去时:
They refurbished the entire house last year. = 他们去年翻修了整栋房子。
She refurbished the antique chair. = 她翻新了那把古董椅子。


现在完成时:
The company has refurbished several buildings in the city. = 这家公司已经翻新了城市里的几栋建筑。
We have refurbished our bathroom. = 我们已经翻修了我们的浴室。


过去完成时:
They had refurbished the office before the new employees arrived. = 新员工到达之前，他们已经翻修了办公室。
She had refurbished the car before selling it. = 她卖车之前已经翻修了汽车。


将来时:
We will refurbish the kitchen next month. = 我们下个月将翻修厨房。
The landlord will refurbish the apartment before renting it out. = 房东会在出租公寓前对其进行翻修。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would refurbish my house. = 如果我有钱，我会翻修我的房子。
I wish I could refurbish my old car. = 我真希望我能翻新我的旧车。


被动语态:
The building is being refurbished at the moment. = 这栋建筑正在翻修中。
The kitchen was refurbished last week. = 厨房上周翻修完毕。


疑问句:
Is the hotel being refurbished? = 酒店正在翻修吗？
When will the office be refurbished? = 办公室什么时候翻修？


否定句:
They did not refurbish the car. = 他们没有翻修那辆车。
We will not refurbish the bathroom this year. = 我们今年不会翻修浴室。
