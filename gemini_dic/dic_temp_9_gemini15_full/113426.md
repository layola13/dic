lymphogenic:/ˌlɪmfəˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj.|淋巴发生的；淋巴生成的|Relating to or caused by the lymphatic system.  The term describes something originating in or spreading through the lymphatic system.| Example:  Lymphogenic spread of cancer is a serious concern. 淋巴传播的癌症是一个严重的关注点。 The lymphogenic metastasis of the tumor was rapid.  肿瘤的淋巴转移很快。|近义词：lymphatic|反义词：hematogenous (blood-borne)
