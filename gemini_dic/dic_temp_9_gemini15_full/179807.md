reexcavation:/ˌriːˌɛkskəˈveɪʃən/| n. |重新挖掘；二次挖掘|The act of excavating something a second time, often to further investigate or clarify previous findings.  It implies a previous excavation has already taken place.|例句：The reexcavation of the site revealed new artifacts previously overlooked.  重新挖掘该遗址发现了先前被忽视的新文物。

例句：The team decided on a reexcavation after new evidence emerged.  在出现新的证据后，团队决定重新挖掘。


近义词：re-exploration, reinvestigation | 反义词：initial excavation, first dig | 词性：名词
