crappie:/ˈkræpi/| n.|太阳鱼，一种淡水鱼|A crappie is a freshwater fish of the sunfish family, known for its flaky white meat and popularity as a game fish.  They are often caught using jigs or minnows.|例句：We caught a lot of crappie at the lake yesterday.  昨天我们在湖里钓到了很多太阳鱼。|例句：The crappie were biting well this morning.今天早上太阳鱼很活跃。|近义词：sunfish, papermouth|反义词:  (无直接反义词，可根据语境用其他鱼类代替)|名词
