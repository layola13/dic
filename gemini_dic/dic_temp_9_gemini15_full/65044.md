etcher:/ˈɛtʃər/| n. |蚀刻者；蚀刻机|A person who makes etchings; a machine used in etching.|例句：The etcher carefully applied acid to the plate. 蚀刻者小心地将酸涂在板上。|例句：The new etcher is much faster than the old one. 新的蚀刻机比旧的快得多。|近义词：engraver, carver|反义词：painter (in some contexts)
