herpetotomist:/ˌhɜːrpɛˈtɒtəmɪst/| n. |爬行动物解剖学家|A herpetotomist is a scientist who dissects and studies reptiles and amphibians.  They often work in research labs, universities, or museums. Their work contributes to our understanding of herpetology (the study of reptiles and amphibians).|The herpetotomist meticulously examined the frog's internal organs.|zoologist, anatomist|  |n.
