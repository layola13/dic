interestedness: /ˈɪntərəstɪdnəs/ | n. |兴趣；关注；重要性 | The state or quality of being interesting or of having interest;  the quality of being important or significant.  It often implies a degree of engagement or involvement. | 例句：The interestedness of the topic kept the audience engaged.  这个话题的趣味性让观众保持了参与度。


The interestedness of the political debate surprised many.  政治辩论的重要性让许多人感到惊讶。 | 近义词：engagement, involvement, importance, significance | 反义词：indifference, apathy, unimportance, insignificance |  词性: 名词
