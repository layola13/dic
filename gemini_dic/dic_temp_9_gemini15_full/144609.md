outwent:/aʊtˈwɛnt/| v.|超过，胜过，优于|The team outwent their rivals in the final match.  球队在决赛中超过了他们的对手。| surpass, exceed, excel | be surpassed by, be inferior to | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He often outwents his brother in running. = 他跑步经常胜过他哥哥。
The sales of this product outwent our expectations. =  这款产品的销量超出了我们的预期。


一般过去时:
Yesterday, she outwent her personal best in the 100-meter dash. = 昨天，她在百米短跑中超过了自己的最好成绩。
Our profits last year outwent those of the previous year. = 我们去年的利润超过了前一年。


现在完成时:
The company has outwent its competitors in the market share. = 这家公司在市场份额上已经超过了竞争对手。
She has outwent her initial goals for the project. = 她已经超过了她对这个项目的最初目标。


过去完成时:
By the time the race ended, he had outwent all his opponents. = 到比赛结束时，他已经超过了所有对手。
Before the deadline, they had outwent their projected sales figures. = 在截止日期之前，他们已经超过了预计的销售额。


将来时:
I believe our team will outwent the opposing team in tomorrow's game. = 我相信我们球队会在明天的比赛中胜过对方球队。
With this new strategy, we expect our revenue to outwent the previous year's. = 通过这项新战略，我们预计我们的收入将超过去年。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he would have outwent his rival. = 如果他训练更刻苦，他就会超过他的对手。
If we had invested more, our returns would have outwent our expectations. = 如果我们投资更多，我们的回报就会超过我们的预期。


被动语态:
(Outwent does not typically use the passive voice because it is an intransitive verb, but a similar sentence could be phrased using "surpassed"):  Their performance was surpassed by the newcomers.  = 他们的表现被新人超越了。
His record was outdone by a younger athlete. = 他的纪录被一个更年轻的运动员打破了。


疑问句:
Did he outwent his previous score? = 他是否超过了他之前的分数？
Will our sales outwent the target this quarter? = 我们本季度的销售额会超过目标吗？


否定句:
He did not outwent his opponent in the competition. = 他在比赛中没有超过对手。
Our efforts did not outwent the challenges we faced. = 我们的努力并没有克服我们面临的挑战。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrasal verbs with "outwent."  The verb is fairly straightforward in its usage.  Phrases that convey similar meanings are more common:
Go beyond: 超越
Outperform:  优于，胜过
Surpass expectations: 超出预期
Exceed the limit: 超过限制
Rise above: 超越，克服
