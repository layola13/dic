reappraises:/ˌriːəˈpreɪz/ | vt. |重新评估，重新估价 | To reconsider and revise the value or assessment of something.  |例句：The company reappraises its assets annually.  公司每年都会重新评估其资产。
The committee will reappraise the situation after receiving the new data. 委员会将在收到新数据后重新评估情况。|近义词：reevaluate, reassess, revalue |反义词：accept at face value, take for granted
