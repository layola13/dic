disconnections: /ˌdɪskəˈnekʃənz/ | n. | 断开连接；中断；脱节 | The loss or absence of connection; a break in continuity.  It can refer to physical connections, as well as abstract connections like ideas or relationships. |

例句：

The power outage caused numerous disconnections in the city. = 停电导致城市中许多地方断电。

There were noticeable disconnections in his speech; the ideas didn't flow smoothly. = 他的演讲中存在明显的脱节；观点衔接不流畅。


近义词：separations, interruptions, breaks, disunions, gaps


反义词：connections, links, unions, continuities


词性：名词
