recopy:/ˈriːkɒpi/ | /ˈriːkɑːpi/ | vt. |重新抄写；复制 |To copy something again.  It implies a previous copying action.  The emphasis is on the act of copying again, rather than the creation of a new copy.|例句：Please recopy the document and correct the errors. 请重新抄写这份文件并改正错误。|例句：The student had to recopy his essay because of the numerous mistakes. 因为错误太多，学生不得不重新抄写他的论文。|近义词：copy, duplicate, reproduce |反义词：delete, erase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recopies his notes every week. = 他每周都重新抄写笔记。
She recopies the address on the envelope. = 她把信封上的地址重新抄写了一遍。

一般过去时:
I recopied the entire chapter. = 我重新抄写了整章内容。
They recopied the data after the system crash. = 系统崩溃后，他们重新抄写了数据。

现在完成时:
She has recopied the manuscript three times. = 她已经重新抄写了手稿三次。
He has recopied all the important documents. = 他已经重新抄写了所有重要的文件。

过去完成时:
I had recopied the report before the deadline. = 我在截止日期前已经重新抄写了报告。
They had recopied the files before the power outage. = 停电前他们已经重新抄写了文件。


将来时:

被动语态:

否定句:
