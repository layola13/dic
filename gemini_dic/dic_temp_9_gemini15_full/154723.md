perspirations:/ˌpɜːrspəˈreɪʃənz/| n. |汗水；出汗；流汗|The act or process of perspiring; sweat.  It often refers to the visible moisture on the skin.  The word can also be used more figuratively to refer to strenuous effort or anxiety.|例句：The exertions of the long climb resulted in copious perspirations. 长时间的攀登导致大量出汗。

例句：He wiped the perspirations from his brow. 他擦去了额头上的汗水。


近义词：sweat, sweating, perspiration

反义词：dryness


词性：名词
