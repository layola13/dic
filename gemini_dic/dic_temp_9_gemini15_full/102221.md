itchiness:/ˈɪtʃɪnəs/| n. |瘙痒，发痒|The unpleasant sensation of wanting to scratch the skin. It is caused by various factors such as dry skin, allergies, insect bites, or skin diseases.|例句：The itchiness from the mosquito bite kept me awake all night.  蚊子叮咬引起的瘙痒让我整晚睡不着觉。

The intense itchiness made her scratch until her skin was raw. 剧烈的瘙痒让她抓挠到皮肤破损。|近义词：pruritus, tingling, irritation |反义词:numbness, insensitivity
