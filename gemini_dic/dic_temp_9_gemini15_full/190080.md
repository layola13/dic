sanest:/ˈseɪnɪst/| adj.|最明智的；最理智的；最稳重的 |Most sensible or rational.  It is the superlative form of "sane".|例句：Of all the suggestions, hers was the sanest.  在所有建议中，她的建议是最明智的。|例句：The sanest course of action would be to wait and see. 最明智的做法是等待观望。|近义词：most rational, most sensible, most reasonable, most judicious |反义词：most insane, most irrational, most foolish, most reckless

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is the sanest person I know. = 他是我认识的最理智的人。
She seems sanest when she's quiet. = 她安静的时候看起来最理智。

一般过去时:
His decision was the sanest option at the time. = 他的决定当时是最明智的选择。
That was the sanest thing he ever did. = 那件是他做过的最理智的事。

现在完成时:

This approach has proven to be the sanest. = 这种方法已被证明是最明智的。

将来时:
