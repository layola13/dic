USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and its usage is primarily limited to describing actions performed with official sanction or authorization.  Therefore, the typical verb tense conjugations do not apply in the same way as with action verbs.  The focus is on the manner in which the action was performed, not the action itself.

Example Sentence 1 (Simple Past): The painting was hung commissively in the gallery.  这幅画是被授权挂在画廊里的。

Example Sentence 2 (Present Perfect): The investigation has been conducted commissively, following all proper procedures.  调查是按照所有正当程序授权进行的。
