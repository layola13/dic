ionizings:/ˈaɪənaɪzɪŋz/ | adj. | 电离的；使电离的 |  Capable of ionizing; causing ionization.  The term refers to radiation or other processes that can remove electrons from atoms or molecules, creating ions. | Example: Ionizing radiation, such as X-rays and gamma rays, can damage DNA. 电离辐射，如X射线和伽马射线，会破坏DNA。 The high-energy particles in the accelerator are ionizing. 加速器中的高能粒子具有电离性。 | 近义词：radiant, ionizing | 反义词：non-ionizing
