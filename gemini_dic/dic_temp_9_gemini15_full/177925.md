reckless:/ˈrɛkləs/ | adj. | 鲁莽的，不顾后果的 | adj. characterized by or showing a disregard for danger or consequences.  It implies a lack of caution and a willingness to take unnecessary risks. | 例句：His reckless driving caused the accident.  他的鲁莽驾驶导致了事故。

例句：She showed reckless abandon in spending her money. 她花钱大手大脚，毫不顾忌。


近义词：rash, heedless, careless, imprudent, foolhardy

反义词：cautious, careful, prudent, wary


词源：源自古英语 *rēc-lēas,  由 rēc (care) + -lēas (without)  构成，意为“without care”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is reckless with his money. = 他花钱大手大脚。
She is reckless in her pursuit of adventure. = 她追求冒险，不顾一切。


一般过去时:
He was reckless in his youth. = 他年轻时很鲁莽。
She was reckless with her words, hurting many feelings. = 她说话鲁莽，伤了很多人的心。


现在完成时:
He has been reckless throughout his career. = 他整个职业生涯都鲁莽行事。
She has been reckless with her health, ignoring doctor's advice. = 她一直不注意自己的健康，无视医生的建议。


过去完成时:
He had been reckless in his investments, losing a fortune. = 他投资时非常鲁莽，损失了一大笔钱。
She had been reckless with her time, leaving everything to the last minute. = 她一直很浪费时间，把所有事情都拖到最后一刻。


将来时:
He will be reckless in his actions, regardless of consequences. = 他将鲁莽行事，不顾后果。
She will be reckless with her new-found freedom. = 她将挥霍她新获得的自由。


虚拟语气:
If he were not so reckless, he wouldn't have lost his job. = 如果他不那么鲁莽，就不会丢掉工作了。
If she hadn't been so reckless, she would have avoided the accident. = 如果她不那么鲁莽，就能避免事故了。


被动语态: (形容词不适用被动语态)


疑问句:
Is he reckless in his approach to the project? = 他处理这个项目的方式鲁莽吗？
Was she reckless in her decision-making? = 她的决策鲁莽吗？


否定句:
He is not reckless; he is very careful. = 他并不鲁莽；他非常小心。
She was not reckless; she acted with caution. = 她并不鲁莽；她行事谨慎。
