heaven:/ˈhɛvən/| n. |天堂，天国；极乐世界；天空；至福；极美好的事物|The word "heaven" originates from Old English "heofon," meaning "sky, heavens."  It's related to other Germanic words with similar meanings, and ultimately derives from a Proto-Germanic root.  The concept of heaven as a spiritual realm evolved over time, influenced by religious beliefs.|例句：Many religions believe in heaven as a place of eternal happiness after death. 许多宗教相信死后有天堂，那里充满永恒的幸福。

例句：The sunset painted the heaven with vibrant colors. 夕阳给天空涂上了鲜艳的色彩。|近义词：paradise, celestial sphere, sky, bliss|反义词：hell, purgatory, earth
