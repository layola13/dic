noncommiserations:/ˌnɒnkəmɪˈzɛreɪʃənz/ | n. | 不表示慰问；不同情 |  The expression implies a lack of sympathy or condolence; a refusal to offer comfort or consolation.  It often suggests a deliberate withholding of sympathy, perhaps due to disapproval or anger.  The word is relatively uncommon and carries a strong negative connotation. | Example: "After hearing about his misfortune, all I got was noncommiserations from him." = “在他遭遇不幸后，我从他那里得到的只是冷漠和不表示慰问。” | 近义词: lack of sympathy, coldness, indifference, unconcern | 反义词: sympathy, condolence, compassion, commiseration |
