outsucked:/ˌaʊtˈsʌkt/| v.|被超越，被比下去 |  This word is a slang term, combining "out" (meaning to surpass or exceed) and "sucked" (past tense of "suck," meaning to be bad or inferior). It implies that something or someone has been significantly surpassed in quality or performance.  It carries a strong negative connotation, suggesting a clear defeat or inferiority. | 例句：His performance completely outsucked the previous attempt. 他的表现完全比不上之前的尝试。  Their band totally outsucked the headliners. 他们的乐队完全被压过了头。| 近义词：outperformed, overshadowed, surpassed, eclipsed | 反义词：outdid, excelled, surpassed (in a positive sense) | 动词
