electrocardiogram:/ˌɪˌlɛktroʊˈkɑːrdɪoʊɡræm/| n. |心电图|A recording of the electrical activity of the heart, used to detect abnormalities in heart rhythm and function.|The doctor ordered an electrocardiogram to assess the patient's heart condition.|近义词：ECG, EKG|反义词：无
