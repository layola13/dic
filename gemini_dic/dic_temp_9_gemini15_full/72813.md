folding:/ˈfoʊldɪŋ/| /ˈfəʊldɪŋ/ | v. n. adj. |折叠；褶皱；折叠的；可折叠的 |To bend (something flexible and relatively flat) over on itself so that one part lies on another; a crease or wrinkle formed by bending something.  |例句：He carefully folded the letter and put it in an envelope. 他仔细地折叠信件并把它装进信封里。

例句：The map showed many folds in the terrain. 地图显示地形有很多褶皱。


近义词：creasing, bending, pleating | 反义词：unfolding, straightening

词源:
The word "folding" comes from the Old English word "fealdan," meaning "to fold," "to bend," or "to wrap."  This word has Germanic roots, related to words in other Germanic languages with similar meanings.  The "-ing" suffix indicates the present participle or gerund form of the verb.
