reinvolves:/ˌriːɪnˈvɒlvz/| vt.|重新卷入；再次牵涉|To involve again; to bring into involvement once more.  The word combines "re-" (again) and "involve".|例句：The scandal reinvolved the mayor in a controversy.  丑闻再次将市长卷入争议之中。
The new evidence reinvolved him in the investigation. 新证据使他再次卷入调查。|近义词：reengage, re-enlist|反义词：exclude, remove


USAGE->

He reinvolves himself in the debate. = 他再次参与到这场辩论中。
