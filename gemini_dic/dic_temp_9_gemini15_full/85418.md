harvestings:/ˈhɑːrvestɪŋz/ | n. | 收获；收割；收获物 | The act of harvesting crops or other products.  It refers to the process or the results of gathering a crop.  The plural form emphasizes multiple instances of harvesting or the collective harvested items. |例句：The autumn harvestings were bountiful this year.  今年秋收丰收。

The recent harvestings of apples have been sold to local markets. 最近收获的苹果已经卖给了当地市场。|近义词：gatherings, collections, yields |反义词：planting, sowing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer is harvesting his crops. = 农民正在收割庄稼。
We are harvesting the grapes for wine. = 我们正在采摘酿酒用的葡萄。


一般过去时:
They harvested the wheat last week. = 他们上周收割了小麦。
The farmers harvested a large quantity of corn. = 农民们收割了大量的玉米。


现在完成时:
We have harvested all the tomatoes. = 我们已经收割了所有的西红柿。
They have harvested more rice this year than last year. = 他们今年收割的水稻比去年多。


过去完成时:
They had harvested the rice before the typhoon arrived. = 台风来临之前，他们已经收割了水稻。
The farmers had harvested most of the crops before the heavy rain. = 大雨来临之前，农民们已经收割了大部分庄稼。


将来时:
We will harvest the potatoes next week. = 我们下周将收获土豆。
The farmers will harvest the soybeans in the fall. = 农民们将在秋季收割大豆。


虚拟语气:
If we harvested earlier, we would have a better yield. = 如果我们早点收割，产量就会更好。
I wish we had harvested the apples before the frost. = 我希望我们在霜冻前已经收割了苹果。


被动语态:
The crops were harvested by machines. = 庄稼是用机器收割的。
The wheat is being harvested at the moment. = 小麦正在收割中。


疑问句:
When will the harvestings begin? = 收割什么时候开始？
Have the harvestings been completed? = 收割完成了吗？


否定句:
We haven't harvested the corn yet. = 我们还没有收割玉米。
They didn't harvest the grapes because of the rain. = 因为下雨，他们没有收割葡萄。
