scamped:/skæmpt/| v.|偷偷溜走，逃脱，飞奔|to run or go quickly and playfully; to escape or avoid something quickly and secretly.|例句：The children scampered off to play hide-and-seek. 孩子们飞快地跑开了去玩捉迷藏。|例句：The thief scampered away before the police arrived. 小偷在警察到达之前逃跑了。|近义词：dash, dart, flit, skip, scurry|反义词：crawl, trudge, saunter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scampers around the garden every morning. = 他每天早上都在花园里跑来跑去。
The rabbits scamper through the fields. = 兔子在田野里飞奔。

一般过去时:
The dog scampered away when it saw the cat. = 那条狗看到猫就跑开了。
The children scampered up the hill. = 孩子们飞快地跑上了山。

现在完成时:
The children have scampered all over the house. = 孩子们在房子里跑来跑去。
The mouse has scampered into its hole. = 老鼠已经窜进了洞里。

过去完成时:
The thief had scampered away before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前小偷就已经逃跑了。
The children had scampered off before I could stop them. = 我还没来得及阻止他们，孩子们就已经跑开了。

将来时:
The puppy will scamper across the lawn. = 小狗将会在草坪上跑来跑去。
I think the cat will scamper up the tree. = 我想猫会跑到树上去。

虚拟语气:
If the dog had not been so small, it would have scampered faster. = 如果那条狗不是那么小，它本可以跑得更快。
I wish the children would scamper less noisily. = 我希望孩子们跑的时候能轻一点。

被动语态: (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice.)  This section is therefore omitted.

疑问句:
Did the children scamper away? = 孩子们跑开了吗？
Will the rabbit scamper across the road? = 兔子会跑过马路吗？

否定句:
The children didn't scamper away. = 孩子们没有跑开。
He didn't scamper off. = 他没有偷偷溜走。
