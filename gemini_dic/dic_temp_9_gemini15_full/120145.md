mince:/mɪns/| vt. n.|切碎；剁碎； minced meat(肉馅)|to cut something, especially meat, into very small pieces|The cook minced the garlic before adding it to the sauce. 厨师在将大蒜加入酱汁之前将其切碎。|chop, grind, shred|whole|v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He minces the onions every morning. 他每天早上都切碎洋葱。
She minces the garlic for the pasta sauce. 她把蒜末切碎做意大利面酱。


一般过去时:
I minced the herbs before adding them to the stew. 我把香草切碎后再加到炖菜里。
They minced the meat for the burgers. 他们把肉馅做成汉堡。


现在完成时:
She has minced all the vegetables for the soup. 她已经把所有的蔬菜都切碎做汤了。
He has minced the meat and is ready to start cooking. 他已经把肉切碎了，准备开始做饭了。


过去完成时:
I had minced the garlic before the chef arrived. 在厨师到达之前，我已经把大蒜切碎了。
They had minced the meat before adding the spices. 他们在加入香料之前已经把肉切碎了。


将来时:
I will mince the carrots for the salad tomorrow. 我明天会把胡萝卜切碎做沙拉。
She will mince the chicken for the stir-fry. 她将把鸡肉切碎做成炒菜。


虚拟语气:
If I had a food processor, I wouldn't mince the vegetables by hand. 如果我有食品加工机，我就不会手工切碎蔬菜了。
I wish I had minced the onions more finely. 我真希望我把洋葱切得更碎一些。


被动语态:
The meat was minced by the butcher. 肉是由屠夫切碎的。
The vegetables are being minced for the stew. 蔬菜正在被切碎做炖菜。


疑问句:
