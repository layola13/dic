hypermarkets:/ˈhaɪpəmɑːrkɪts/| n. |大型超市；超级市场 |A hypermarket is a very large self-service store that sells food and household goods.  They are typically located outside of city centers and offer a wide variety of products at competitive prices. |Example:  Many families shop at hypermarkets for their weekly groceries because of the wide selection and low prices.  许多家庭在大型超市购买每周的杂货，因为那里选择多，价格低。


近义词：supermarket, megastore
反义词：boutique, specialty store
