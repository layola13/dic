cope-with:/koʊp wɪð/| v.|应付；处理；克服|To deal successfully with a difficult situation.|例句：She learned to cope with the stress of her job. 她学会了应付工作压力。|例句：He's finding it hard to cope with his wife's death. 他发现很难应对妻子的去世。|近义词：handle, manage, deal with, overcome|反义词：fail, succumb, give in

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I cope with stress by exercising. = 我通过锻炼来应对压力。
We cope with challenges as a team. = 我们作为一个团队来应对挑战。

一般过去时:
She coped with the difficult situation bravely. = 她勇敢地应对了困境。
He coped with the loss of his job by finding a new one. = 他通过找到一份新工作来应对失业。

现在完成时:
I have coped with many difficulties in my life. = 我一生中已经应对了许多困难。
She has coped well with the pressure of her studies. = 她很好地应对了学习压力。

过去完成时:
He had coped with similar problems before. = 他以前曾处理过类似的问题。
They had coped with the crisis successfully. = 他们已经成功地应对了危机。

将来时:
I will cope with whatever comes my way. = 我将应对任何挑战。
We will cope with the project effectively. = 我们将有效地应对这个项目。

虚拟语气:
If I had more support, I would cope with the pressure better. = 如果我得到更多支持，我会更好地应对压力。
I wish I could cope with this situation more easily. = 我希望我能更容易地应付这种情况。

被动语态:
The problem was coped with efficiently. = 这个问题得到了有效的解决。(被动语态较少用于此词组)
The situation is being coped with by the team. = 团队正在处理这种情况。(被动语态较少用于此词组)


疑问句:
How do you cope with your workload? = 你如何应付你的工作量？
Can he cope with the demands of the job? = 他能应付这份工作的要求吗？

否定句:
He couldn't cope with the pressure of the exam. = 他无法承受考试的压力。
She failed to cope with the unexpected changes. = 她没能应对意外的变化。
