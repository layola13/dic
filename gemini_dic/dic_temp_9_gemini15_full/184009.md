reshowing:/ˌriːˈʃoʊɪŋ/| v. |重播；再次展示|The act of showing something again, typically a film, television program, or other performance.|例句：The movie is having a reshowing next week.  下周电影将再次上映。
例句：The museum is reshowing the Van Gogh exhibition. 博物馆正在再次展出梵高的展览。|近义词：replaying, re-screening, re-presenting|反义词：removing, hiding


USAGE->

They reshow the classic movie every Christmas. = 他们每年圣诞节都会重播这部经典电影。
The gallery reshows its most popular exhibits annually. = 画廊每年都会重新展出最受欢迎的展品。
