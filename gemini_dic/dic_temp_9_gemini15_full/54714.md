disowning: /dɪsˈoʊnɪŋ/ | v. | 否认；拒绝承认；放弃；抛弃 |  The act of refusing to acknowledge or accept as one's own; rejecting or abandoning.  |

例句：

He is disowning his past actions. = 他正在否认他过去的行动。

She disowned her son after he was arrested. =  在她儿子被捕后，她抛弃了他。


近义词：renouncing, repudiating, rejecting, abandoning, forsaking


反义词：claiming, acknowledging, accepting, owning


词性：动词

He disowns his family. = 他否认他的家人。
