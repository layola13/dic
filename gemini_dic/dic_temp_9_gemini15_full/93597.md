imbark:/ɪmˈbɑːrk/| v.| 上船；着手；从事 | to go aboard a ship or aircraft; to begin or start something |例句：We embarked on a long journey across the ocean. 我们开始了横跨海洋的长途旅行。  They embarked upon a new business venture. 他们开始了一项新的商业冒险。|近义词：board, commence, begin, start |反义词：disembark, end, finish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He embarks on a new project every year. 他每年都会开始一个新的项目。
They embark on a cruise to the Caribbean.  他们开始了加勒比海的邮轮之旅。


一般过去时:
She embarked on her writing career at a young age. 她年轻时就开始了写作生涯。
We embarked on a challenging hiking trip last summer. 去年夏天我们开始了一次充满挑战的徒步旅行。


现在完成时:
He has embarked on several successful business ventures. 他已经开始了几个成功的商业项目。
They have embarked on a journey of self-discovery. 他们已经开始了自我发现的旅程。


过去完成时:
Before the war, he had embarked on a promising career as a musician.  战争爆发前，他开始了作为音乐家的光明前景的职业生涯。
She had embarked on her travels before receiving the news.  她在收到消息之前就已经开始了她的旅行。


将来时:
They will embark on a world tour next year. 他们明年将开始世界巡演。
She will embark on a new phase of her life after graduation. 毕业后，她将开始人生的新阶段。


虚拟语气:
If I had the chance, I would embark on a solo trip around the world. 如果我有机会，我会开始一次环球个人旅行。
I wish I could embark on a career in medicine. 我希望我能从事医学事业。


被动语态:
The project was embarked on with great enthusiasm.  这个项目充满了热情地开始。
The mission was embarked upon despite the risks.  尽管存在风险，这项任务还是开始了。


疑问句:
When will you embark on your new job? 你什么时候开始你的新工作？
Have they embarked on the journey yet? 他们已经开始旅程了吗？


否定句:
He did not embark on the risky venture. 他没有开始那场冒险的投资。
They decided not to embark on the project. 他们决定不开始这个项目。


PHRASE->

embark on an adventure: 开始一次冒险
