mispaint:/mɪsˈpeɪnt/| v.| 错涂，涂错|To paint something incorrectly or badly.  It implies a mistake in the application of paint, rather than a deliberate artistic choice. |例句：He mispainted the door, leaving streaks of different colors. 他把门涂错了，留下了不同颜色的条纹。

例句：The artist mispainted the background, making the main subject appear out of place.  艺术家把背景涂错了，使得主体显得格格不入。


近义词：paint badly, botch, spoil


反义词：paint well, perfect


词性:动词
