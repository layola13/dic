carbonizable:/ˌkɑːrbənˈaɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可碳化的；可焦化的 | Capable of being converted into carbon or coke.  This typically involves the process of heating organic material in the absence of oxygen, driving off volatile components and leaving behind a carbon-rich residue. | The wood is carbonizable at high temperatures.  这种木材在高温下可碳化。|  coking, charring | non-carbonizable |
