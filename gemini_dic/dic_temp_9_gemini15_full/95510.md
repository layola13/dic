inclines:/ɪnˈklaɪnz/| v. n.|倾斜；倾向；使倾斜 |To lean or slope; to be predisposed to something.|例句：The tower inclines slightly to the south. 塔稍微向南倾斜。|The evidence inclines me to believe his story. 证据使我相信他的话。|近义词：lean, slope, slant, tend, be prone to|反义词：straighten, upright


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tower inclines slightly to the south. = 塔稍微向南倾斜。
He inclines to agree with my opinion. = 他倾向于同意我的意见。


一般过去时:
The old house inclined dangerously to one side. = 老房子危险地向一边倾斜。
She inclined her head in agreement. = 她点头表示同意。


现在完成时:
The road has inclined sharply uphill. = 道路已经陡峭地上坡了。
Recent evidence has inclined scientists to reconsider their theory. = 最近的证据使科学家们倾向于重新考虑他们的理论。


过去完成时:
The tree had inclined so far it almost touched the ground. = 树木已经倾斜到几乎碰到地面了。
After reviewing the data, the committee had inclined towards rejecting the proposal. = 在审查数据后，委员会倾向于否决该提案。


将来时:
The building will incline further with the next storm. = 下一场暴风雨会使建筑物进一步倾斜。
I think the public will incline towards supporting the new policy. = 我认为公众将倾向于支持这项新政策。


虚拟语气:
If the ground inclined less steeply, the climb would be easier. = 如果地面倾斜度小一些，攀登就会更容易。
I wish the building didn't incline so much. = 我希望这座建筑物不要倾斜得那么厉害。


被动语态:
The road was inclined to make the journey difficult. = 道路的倾斜使得旅程变得困难。
The audience was inclined to believe his presentation. = 观众倾向于相信他的陈述。


疑问句:
Does the road incline sharply at this point? = 这段路在这里急剧倾斜吗？
Does she incline towards accepting the job offer? = 她倾向于接受这份工作吗？


否定句:
The tower does not incline noticeably. = 塔没有明显倾斜。
He did not incline to accept their invitation. = 他不愿意接受他们的邀请。


PHRASE->
be inclined to: 倾向于
inclined plane: 斜面
inclined orbit: 倾斜轨道
be strongly inclined to: 非常倾向于
have an inclination to: 有...的倾向
