scutellum:/skuˈtɛləm/| n. |小盾片；(禾本科植物的)盾片|The scutellum is a specialized structure found in the seeds of grasses, acting as a crucial link between the embryo and the endosperm.  It absorbs nutrients from the endosperm to nourish the developing embryo.|  The scutellum of barley is rich in enzymes.|近义词：shield (in a broader sense)|反义词：无
