frescoing:/frɛˈskoʊɪŋ/ | n. | 壁画绘画；湿壁画技法 | The act of painting frescoes; the creation of frescoes.  It refers to the process and the resulting artwork.  | Example: The frescoing of the Sistine Chapel took years.  壁画的创作花费了好几年时间。 |近义词：mural painting, fresco painting |反义词：无 |动名词
