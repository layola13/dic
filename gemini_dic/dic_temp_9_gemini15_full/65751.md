evermore:/ˈɛvərˌmɔːr/| adv.|永远，永久地|Always and forever; for all time to come.|例句：They lived happily evermore. 他们从此过着幸福的生活。|近义词：forever, eternally, always|反义词：never, temporarily


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He loves her evermore. = 他永远爱着她。
We believe in justice evermore. = 我们永远相信正义。


一般过去时:
She dreamt of him evermore. = 她总是梦到他。
They talked about it evermore. = 他们总是谈论这件事。


现在完成时:
I have loved this place evermore. = 我一直深爱着这个地方。
She has cherished those memories evermore. = 她一直珍藏着那些回忆。


过去完成时:
They had known each other evermore. = 他们彼此认识了很久很久。
He had waited for this moment evermore. = 他一直等待着这一刻。


将来时:
We will remember this day evermore. = 我们将永远记住这一天。
They will support each other evermore. = 他们将永远互相支持。


虚拟语气:
If only we could stay together evermore! = 但愿我们能永远在一起！
I wish I could live in peace evermore. = 我希望我能永远平静地生活。


被动语态:
This moment will be remembered evermore. = 这一刻将被永远铭记。
The love they shared was celebrated evermore. = 他们分享的爱将被永远歌颂。


疑问句:

否定句:
Their happiness did not last evermore. = 他们的幸福并没有永远持续下去。
