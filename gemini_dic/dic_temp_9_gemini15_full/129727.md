non-endorsement:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdɔːsmənt/|/ˌnɑːnɛnˈdɔːrsmənt/| n. |不认可，不赞同，未经认可|The statement represents a non-endorsement of the company's actions.  该声明表示不认可该公司的行为。|近义词：disapproval, rejection, condemnation|反义词：endorsement, approval, sanction|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company maintains a policy of non-endorsement for all third-party products. = 公司对所有第三方产品都保持不认可的政策。
This statement signifies a non-endorsement of the controversial proposal. = 这份声明表示不认可有争议的提案。


一般过去时:
Their previous non-endorsement of the project led to its cancellation. = 他们先前对该项目的否定导致其被取消。
The government's non-endorsement of the bill resulted in its failure to pass. = 政府对该法案的不认可导致其未能通过。


现在完成时:
The organization has maintained a consistent policy of non-endorsement regarding political affiliations. = 该组织一直保持着关于政治派别的中立政策。
Their non-endorsement of the new technology has significantly impacted its market adoption. = 他们对这项新技术的否定对其市场采用产生了重大影响。


过去完成时:
The board had already expressed its non-endorsement before the final vote. = 在最终投票之前，董事会就已经表达了他们的不认可。
The committee's non-endorsement of the plan had been anticipated by many. = 许多人已经预料到委员会对该计划的不认可。


将来时:
The committee will likely issue a statement of non-endorsement next week. = 委员会可能在下周发布一份不认可声明。
His non-endorsement of the candidate is expected to influence the election outcome. = 他对候选人的不认可预计会影响选举结果。


虚拟语气:
If the company had shown an endorsement, the project would have succeeded. = 如果公司表示认可，该项目就会成功。(反向表达non-endorsement)
I wish they had issued a clear non-endorsement of the harmful practices. = 我希望他们能明确表示不认可有害行为。


被动语态:
The candidate's views were met with non-endorsement from many influential figures. = 候选人的观点遭到许多有影响力人物的不认可。
The proposal received non-endorsement from the regulatory body. = 该提案未获得监管机构的认可。


疑问句:
Does their non-endorsement constitute a formal rejection? = 他们的不认可是否构成正式拒绝？
Is the lack of a statement an implicit non-endorsement? = 缺乏声明是否意味着默认的不认可？


否定句:
The organization does not offer non-endorsement statements lightly. = 该组织不会轻易发表不认可声明。
Their actions did not imply a non-endorsement of the policy. = 他们的行为并不意味着不认可该政策。
