neckmold:/ˈnekmoʊld/ | n. |颈模，颈部模型|A neckmold is a mold used to shape the neck area of a garment, typically in the creation of custom-made clothing or prosthetics.  It's a three-dimensional form that replicates the shape of a person's neck.| Example: The tailor created a precise neckmold to ensure a perfect fit for the bespoke shirt.  颈部裁缝制作了一个精确的颈模以确保定制衬衫完美贴合。|近义词：neck form, neck cast |反义词：None.
