slinging:/ˈslɪŋɪŋ/| v.| 投掷；悬挂；猛烈抨击 | To throw or hurl something forcefully; to hang or suspend something; to criticize severely. |例句：He was slinging mud at his opponent during the debate. 他在辩论中猛烈抨击他的对手。

例句：The workers were slinging concrete to build the bridge. 工人们正在投掷混凝土来建造桥梁。


近义词：throwing, hurling, flinging, casting,抨击, 猛攻

反义词：catching, receiving, supporting, defending


USAGE->
