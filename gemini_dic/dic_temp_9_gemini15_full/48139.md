decollectivizes:/ˌdiːkəˈlɛktɪvaɪzɪz/| vt.|取消集体化；使非集体化 |To change from a system of collective ownership or management to one of individual ownership or management.  The process of decollectivizing land involves returning it to private ownership. |例句：The government decollectivized agriculture, allowing farmers to own their land. 政府取消了农业集体化，允许农民拥有自己的土地。

例句：The former Soviet Union decollectivized its farms in the 1990s, leading to significant economic changes. 前苏联在20世纪90年代取消了其农场集体化，导致了重大的经济变化。


近义词：privatize, decentralize

反义词：collectivize
