contras:/ˈkɒntrɑːsts/ /ˈkɑːntræsts/| n. v.|对比，对照；使对比；使与…形成对比|The verb "contrast" means to compare two or more things in order to show their differences.  The noun "contrast" refers to a difference between things that are compared.  It can also refer to a striking difference.|例句：The black and white photo showed a stark contrast.黑白照片展现出鲜明的对比。

The artist contrasted the smooth texture of the silk with the rough texture of the burlap.艺术家将丝绸的平滑质地与粗麻布的粗糙质地进行了对比。


近义词：difference, comparison, opposition, disparity|反义词：similarity, likeness, resemblance|名词，动词

The colors contrast beautifully. = 这些颜色对比得很好。
The two approaches contrast sharply. = 这两种方法形成了鲜明的对比。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

We have contrasted the results of the two experiments. = 我们已经对比了两个实验的结果。

过去完成时:

They had contrasted the historical evidence with contemporary accounts. = 他们已经将历史证据与当代记载进行了对比。

虚拟语气:
