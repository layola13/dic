consentingly:/kənˈsɛntɪŋli/| adv. | 同意地；欣然地 | Willingly and readily; with agreement.  |例句：She consentingly signed the contract. 她欣然签署了合同。

例句：He consentingly participated in the experiment. 他欣然参加了实验。


近义词：willingly, readily, gladly, agreeably

反义词：reluctantly, unwillingly, grudgingly


词源：源自consent (v.) + -ingly (adv. suffix)  consent  来自古法语consentir，源自拉丁语 consentire "同意"，由com- "一起" + sentire "感觉，认为"构成。 -ingly 是副词后缀，表示“以…的方式”。
