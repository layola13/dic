revolutionarily: /ˌrevəˈluːʃənɛrili/ | adv. |革命性地，彻底地 | In a revolutionary manner; radically.  This adjective describes something that is dramatically new or innovative, bringing about significant change. It implies a complete or fundamental shift, often in a positive way. | 例句：The new software revolutionarily changed the way we work.  = 这款新软件彻底改变了我们的工作方式。


The company revolutionarily redesigned its product line. = 公司彻底重新设计了其产品线。


近义词：radically, fundamentally, dramatically, drastically, innovatively


反义词：incrementally, gradually, traditionally, conservatively


词性：副词
