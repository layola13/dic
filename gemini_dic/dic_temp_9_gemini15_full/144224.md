outslandered: /ˌaʊtˈslændərd/ | v. | 超越，胜过 | To surpass or excel; to outdo.  This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English. It implies a significant degree of surpassing, often in a competitive context. | 例句：He outslandered all his rivals in the debate. 他在辩论中胜过了所有对手。

|近义词：surpass, excel, outdo, overshadow, eclipse |反义词：be surpassed by, be outdone by |词性：动词
