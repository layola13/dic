individualises: /ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 使个性化；使个别化 | To make something or someone different from others; to distinguish or treat as an individual.  | The teacher individualises her teaching methods to suit the needs of each student. 老师根据每个学生的需要个性化教学方法。  | personalize, customize, differentiate | generalize, standardize | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company individualises its products to appeal to different customer groups. = 公司使其产品个性化以吸引不同的客户群体。
She individualises her approach to each patient. = 她对每位病人的方法都个性化处理。


一般过去时:
The artist individualised each portrait with unique details. = 艺术家用独特的细节使每一幅肖像都个性化。
He individualised his training program to maximize his results. = 他将他的训练计划个性化以最大限度地提高他的成绩。


现在完成时:
The school has individualised its curriculum to better serve students with diverse learning styles. = 学校已经将其课程个性化，以更好地为具有不同学习风格的学生服务。
The company has individualised its marketing strategy for each region. = 公司已经为每个地区个性化了其营销策略。


过去完成时:
The teacher had individualised the lesson plans before the students arrived. = 老师在学生到达之前已经个性化了教学计划。
They had individualised their responses to the questionnaire. = 他们已经个性化地回答了问卷。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
