duds:/dʌdz/| n.|废物；失败之作；无用之人|Things that are useless or unsuccessful.  It often refers to clothes that are worn out or unfashionable.|例句：His new business venture turned out to be a bunch of duds. 他的新生意最终成了失败之作。

例句：Those old clothes are just duds; you should throw them away. 那些旧衣服只是废物；你应该扔掉它们。


近义词：failures, trash, rubbish, junk, worthless things

反义词：successes, treasures, valuables, gems


词性:名词
