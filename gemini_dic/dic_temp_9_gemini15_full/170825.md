pseudoexperimentally:/ˌsuːdoʊˌɛkspɛrɪˈmɛntəli/| adv. |似实验地；伪实验地 |  Adverb describing something done in a manner resembling a scientific experiment but lacking the rigorous controls and methodology of a true experiment. It often implies a lack of randomization, control groups, or other essential elements of experimental design, leading to potentially unreliable or uninterpretable results.  |The study was conducted pseudoexperimentally, leading to questionable conclusions.  这项研究是以伪实验的方式进行的，导致结论值得怀疑。| experimentally, quasi-experimentally | truly experimentally
