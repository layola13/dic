flurries:/ˈflʌriːz/| n.|阵雪，一阵风雪，一阵激动|Short, light bursts of snow, wind, or other activity.|例句：The children were delighted by the snow flurries. 孩子们对阵阵飘落的雪花很高兴。|例句：There were flurries of excitement before the concert. 音乐会开始前一阵激动。|近义词：gusts, showers, bursts|反义词：calm, stillness
