rimbase:/ˈrɪmbeɪs/| n.|轮辋基座，轮辋底座|A rimbase is the base or foundation of a wheel rim, often used in the context of automotive or bicycle wheels. It provides structural support and helps to secure the tire. |例句：The mechanic inspected the rimbase for any signs of damage before mounting the tire. 技工在安装轮胎之前检查了轮辋基座是否有任何损坏迹象。The new bike's rims featured reinforced rimbases for increased durability. 新自行车的轮圈采用了加固的轮辋基座，以提高耐用性。|近义词：rim base, wheel base (in a broader sense)|反义词：无
