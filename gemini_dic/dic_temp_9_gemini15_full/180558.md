regimented:/ˈrɛdʒɪˌmɛntɪd/| adj.|严谨的；刻板的；严格管理的 |Strictly organized or controlled; characterized by strict discipline and adherence to rules.|The regimented life of a soldier contrasts sharply with the freedom of civilian life.  士兵刻板的生活与平民的自由生活形成鲜明对比。|The school's regimented schedule left little room for creativity. 学校严格的课程安排几乎没有给创造力留下空间。|organized, disciplined, structured |chaotic, disorganized, free, spontaneous|


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The army is highly regimented. = 军队纪律严明。
His daily routine is very regimented. = 他的日常生活非常刻板。


一般过去时:
Their lives were highly regimented under the dictatorship. = 在独裁统治下，他们的生活非常刻板。
The factory was regimented and lacked creativity. = 工厂管理严格，缺乏创造力。


现在完成时:
The company has become more regimented since the merger. = 自从合并以来，公司变得更加严格了。
She has felt regimented by her job for years. = 多年来，她一直觉得她的工作很刻板。


过去完成时:
The students had been regimented by years of strict schooling. = 学生们多年严格的学校教育使他们变得循规蹈矩。
Before the revolution, society had been very regimented. = 革命前，社会非常严格。


将来时:
Their lives will be more regimented if they join the military. = 如果他们参军，他们的生活将会更加刻板。
The new rules will make the workplace more regimented. = 新规将使工作场所更加严格。


虚拟语气:
If the system were less regimented, people would be happier. = 如果制度不那么严格，人们会更快乐。
I wish my life weren't so regimented. = 我希望我的生活不要那么刻板。


被动语态:
Their schedules are regimented by the company. = 他们的日程安排由公司严格规定。
His life has been regimented since he started his new job. = 自从他开始新工作以来，他的生活变得很刻板。


疑问句:
Is your lifestyle too regimented? = 你的生活方式太刻板了吗？
Are the rules in this school too regimented? = 这所学校的规章制度太严格了吗？


否定句:
His approach to work is not regimented. = 他处理工作的方式并不刻板。
The company culture is not overly regimented. = 公司文化并非过于严格。
