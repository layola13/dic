oversman:/ˈoʊvərzmæn/| n.|监督人，管理者|A person who supervises or manages others, often in a factory or industrial setting.  The term is somewhat archaic and less commonly used in modern English.|例句：The factory floor was overseen by a strict oversman.  工厂车间由一位严厉的监督人负责。|近义词：supervisor, foreman, manager, superintendent|反义词：subordinate, employee
