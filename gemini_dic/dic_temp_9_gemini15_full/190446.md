sashing:/ˈsæʃɪŋ/| n.| 佩带饰带；腰带；窗帘带；装饰带 |  A sash is a long strip of fabric, often worn as a belt or across the shoulder as a decoration. It can also refer to the cords or tapes used to operate window sashes. |例句：She wore a sash in the school colors. 她系着一条学校代表色的腰带。

The window sashes were adorned with decorative sashing. 窗户的窗扇上装饰着装饰带。|近义词：belt, ribbon, band|反义词：N/A
