pestalozzian:/ˌpɛstəˈlɒziən/| adj.|裴斯泰洛齐的；裴斯泰洛齐教育思想的|Relating to or characteristic of Johann Heinrich Pestalozzi, a Swiss educational reformer, and his educational methods, which emphasized sensory experience, intuitive understanding, and the development of moral character.  His approach focused on holistic child development, integrating physical, intellectual, and emotional growth.|The school adopted a Pestalozzian approach to learning, focusing on hands-on activities and individual attention.  这所学校采用了裴斯泰洛齐式的学习方法，注重实践活动和个性化关注。|近义词：developmental, experiential, holistic|反义词：rote, didactic
