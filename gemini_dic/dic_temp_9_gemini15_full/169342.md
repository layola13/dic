prompters:/ˈprɒmptərz/ | n. |提示者；提词器；提示设备|A prompter is a person or device that gives cues or reminders, especially to actors or speakers.|例句：The prompter whispered the next line to the forgetful actor. 提示员低声地把下一句台词告诉了那个健忘的演员。|近义词：reminder, cue-giver, teleprompter|反义词：obstructor, hinderer


USAGE->

The prompter gives the actors their lines. = 提示员给演员们他们的台词。
The teleprompter displays the speech for the speaker. = 提词器为演讲者显示演讲稿。
