mineral:/ˈmɪnərəl/| n.|矿物，矿物质|A naturally occurring inorganic solid substance having a definite chemical composition and a characteristic crystal structure.|例句：Iron is an important mineral for the human body.  铁是人体重要的矿物质。

例句：The geologist identified several rare minerals in the rock sample. 地质学家在岩石样本中鉴定了几种稀有矿物。


近义词：ore, substance|反义词：organic
