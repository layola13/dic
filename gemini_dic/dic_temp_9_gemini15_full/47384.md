deadbeats:/ˈdedbiːts/| n.|赖账者，游手好闲的人|A deadbeat is someone who avoids paying their debts, or someone who is lazy and irresponsible.  They are often characterized by their unwillingness to work and their reliance on others.|例句：He's a deadbeat dad who never pays child support. 他是个从不付抚养费的赖账爸爸。

例句：The landlord is tired of dealing with deadbeats who don't pay their rent. 房东厌倦了处理那些不付房租的赖账者。|近义词：loafer, idler, freeloader, delinquent|反义词：hard worker, responsible person, reliable person
