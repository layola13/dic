sectarianize:/ˌsɛkˈtɛəriənaɪz/ | vt. | 使宗派化；使分裂；以宗派观点看待 | To make something sectarian; to divide into sects or factions; to interpret or view something through a sectarian lens.  | The politician's speech sectarianized the already tense situation. 政治家的讲话使本已紧张的局势更加宗派化。  |  divide, polarize, fragment | unify, integrate, reconcile | 动词
