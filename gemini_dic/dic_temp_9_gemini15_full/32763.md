chocaholics:/ˌtʃɒkəˈhɒlɪks/ | n. |巧克力瘾君子，巧克力迷 |A person with an excessive craving for chocolate.  Chocaholics often consume large quantities of chocolate despite potential negative consequences for their health or well-being. |例句：Many chocaholics find it difficult to resist the temptation of chocolate. 许多巧克力瘾君子难以抗拒巧克力的诱惑。


例句：She considers herself a chocaholic and eats chocolate every day. 她认为自己是个巧克力迷，每天都吃巧克力。


近义词：chocolate addict, chocoholic (singular) |反义词：None (the opposite would be someone who dislikes chocolate) |词性:名词
