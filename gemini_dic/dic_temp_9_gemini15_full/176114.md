rays:/reɪz/ | n. | 光线；射线；光芒 | Beams of light, especially the sun's light.  A ray is a single line of light, heat, or other electromagnetic radiation. |例句：The sun's rays warmed my face.  太阳的光线温暖了我的脸。

例句：X-rays are used to take pictures of bones. X射线用于拍摄骨骼照片。


近义词：beam, gleam, shaft | 反义词：darkness, shadow | 词性：名词
