kirkman:/ˈkɜːkmən/| n. |柯克曼（姓氏）|Kirkman is a surname of English origin.  It is a locational name, derived from places named Kirkman, which are themselves derived from the Old English elements "kirk" meaning "church" and "mann" meaning "man". Therefore, the name likely originally referred to someone who lived near a church or who worked for a church.
