lenders:/ˈlɛndərz/| n. |贷款人，债权人|People or institutions that lend money.  Lenders assess the creditworthiness of borrowers before approving loans.|例句：Banks are the primary lenders for most home mortgages. 银行是大多数房屋抵押贷款的主要贷款人。

例句：The small business secured a loan from several private lenders. 这家小企业从几家私人贷款机构获得了贷款。


近义词：creditors, moneylenders, financiers


反义词：borrowers


词性：名词
