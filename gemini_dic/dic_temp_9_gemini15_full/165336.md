preinvites:/ˌpriːɪnˈvaɪts/| n. |预邀请，提前邀请 | Invitations sent out in advance of an official invitation, often to gauge interest or manage capacity.  They are not considered binding commitments. | Example:  We sent out preinvites to our email list to see how many people were interested in attending the conference. 我们向我们的邮件列表发送了预邀请，以了解有多少人有兴趣参加会议。|近义词：advance notices, preliminary invitations |反义词：final invitations, official invitations |名词
