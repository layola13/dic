errs:/ɜːrz/| v.|犯错，出错|to make a mistake; to do something wrong|He errs in his judgment. 他判断错误。|近义词：mistake, blunder, slip up|反义词：succeed, excel


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He errs in his judgment. = 他在判断上犯了错误。
She often errs on the side of caution. = 她常常过于谨慎。


一般过去时:
He erred in his calculations. = 他计算错误了。
The company erred in its marketing strategy. = 公司的市场策略错了。


现在完成时:
He has erred in his estimations many times. = 他多次估计错误。
She has erred in her choice of words. = 她选词用错了。


过去完成时:
He had erred in his assumptions. = 他的假设是错误的。
They had erred in their judgment of the situation. = 他们对局势的判断是错误的。


将来时:
He will likely err again if he doesn't learn from his mistakes. = 如果他不从错误中吸取教训，他很可能会再次犯错。
She will err if she continues to ignore the warnings. = 如果她继续忽视警告，她会犯错。


虚拟语气:
If he had not erred, he would have won the game. = 如果他没有犯错，他就会赢得比赛。
I wish I hadn't erred in my decision. = 我真希望我没有在决定上犯错。


被动语态:
Mistakes were erred in the report. = 报告中出现了错误。 (This is an unusual passive construction,  it's more natural to say "Mistakes were made in the report.")
The plan was erred on the side of caution. = 计划过于谨慎。(This is also an unusual passive construction, a better phrasing would be "The plan was overly cautious.")



疑问句:
Does he often err in his work? = 他工作中经常犯错吗？
Where did I err in my reasoning? = 我的推理错在哪里？


否定句:
He does not err easily. = 他不容易犯错。
She didn't err in her judgment this time. = 她这次判断没有错。


PHRASE->
err on the side of caution = 谨慎行事
err in judgment = 判断错误
to err is human = 人非圣贤，孰能无过
err in one's ways = 行为不正
err from the truth = 偏离真相
