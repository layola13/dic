coyotes:/kaɪˈoʊts/| n.|郊狼，土狼|A coyote is a wild dog, typically found in North America.  They are omnivorous, known for their intelligence and adaptability.|例句：We saw a coyote crossing the road last night.  昨晚我们看到一只郊狼穿过马路。|例句：Coyotes are often blamed for livestock losses. 郊狼常被指责造成牲畜损失。|近义词：wild dog, prairie wolf|反义词：domestic dog
