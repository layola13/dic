record-breaker: /ˈrɛkɔːrdˌbreɪkə(r)/ | n. | 创造纪录者，破纪录者 | A person or thing that surpasses a previous record.  |例句：Usain Bolt is a record-breaker in the world of sprinting.  尤塞恩·博尔特是短跑界的纪录创造者。

The new skyscraper is a record-breaker in terms of height. 这座新摩天大楼在高度上创造了纪录。|近义词：record-setter, champion |反义词：record-holder (in the sense of someone who previously held the record)
