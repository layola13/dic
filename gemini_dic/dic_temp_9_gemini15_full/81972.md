grinded:/ˈɡraɪndɪd/| v. |磨碎的；碾碎的；磨钝的 |Ground, past participle of grind.  It describes the state of something after being ground or crushed.  |例句：The coffee beans were finely grinded before brewing.  磨碎咖啡豆后才冲泡。

The spices were grinded into a fine powder. 香料被磨成细粉。


近义词：crushed, pulverized, powdered |反义词：whole, intact
