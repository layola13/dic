out-steps:/ˌaʊtˈstɛps/| v.|超过，胜过；超越；走出；领先|To surpass or exceed; to go beyond the limits of; to step out of or from; to take the lead.|例句：His performance out-stepped all expectations.  他的表现超出了所有人的预期。

例句：The dancer out-stepped the rhythm of the music.  舞者超越了音乐的节奏。|近义词：surpass, exceed, transcend, outdo|反义词：underperform, lag behind, fall short|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outsteps his competitors in sales. = 他在销售方面超过了他的竞争对手。
The company outsteps its rivals in innovation. = 这家公司在创新方面超越了竞争对手。

一般过去时:
She outstepped her previous record. = 她超越了她之前的记录。
The team outstepped their opponents in the final game. = 该队在决赛中击败了对手。

现在完成时:
He has outstepped all expectations in his career. = 他在事业上已经超越了所有人的预期。
The project has outstepped its initial budget. = 该项目已经超出了最初的预算。

过去完成时:
They had outstepped their rivals by a significant margin before the final round. = 在最后一轮之前，他们已经大幅领先对手。
The company had outstepped its competitors in market share before the merger. = 在合并之前，该公司已经超过了竞争对手的市场份额。

将来时:
She will outstep her limitations with hard work. = 通过努力工作，她将克服自己的局限性。
The new technology will outstep the current models in performance. = 新技术在性能方面将超越目前的型号。

虚拟语气:
If he worked harder, he would outstep his colleagues. = 如果他更努力工作，他就会超过他的同事。
I wish I could outstep my current limitations. = 我希望我能克服我目前的局限性。

被动语态:
His achievements were outstepped by no one. = 他的成就无人能及。
The plan was outstepped by unforeseen circumstances. = 计划被不可预见的情况所超越。

疑问句:
Does his work outstep the requirements? = 他的工作是否超过了要求？
Will this technology outstep the existing ones? = 这项技术会超越现有的技术吗？

否定句:
He did not outstep his authority. = 他没有越权。
The plan did not outstep the budget. = 计划没有超过预算。


PHRASE->
outstep the bounds of  = 超出...的界限
outstep oneself = 勉强为之
outstep the competition = 超越竞争对手
outstep the norm = 超出常态
outstep the expectations = 超出预期
