libidinous:/lɪˈbɪdɪnəs/ | adj. |好色的，淫荡的 |  Having or expressing a strong sexual desire. Characterized by lustfulness; lecherous. |例句：His libidinous behavior shocked everyone at the party. 他好色的行为震惊了聚会上所有的人。

例句：The novel features a libidinous protagonist driven by base desires. 这部小说塑造了一个受低级欲望驱使的好色主角。


近义词：lustful, lecherous, lascivious, prurient, salacious | 反义词：chaste, pure, virtuous, demure


词源:  From Latin *libidinōsus, from libido "lust, desire".  The word ultimately relates to the Latin verb *lubēre "to desire, want".
