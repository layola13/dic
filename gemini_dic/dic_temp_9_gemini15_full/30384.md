centifolious:/ˌsɛntɪˈfoʊliəs/| adj. |多瓣的，百叶的|Having many petals; specifically, having more than 100 petals, as a rose.  The term often refers to roses with many petals, though not necessarily exactly 100.|例句：The centifolious rose is known for its abundant petals and strong fragrance.  这朵多瓣玫瑰以其丰富的花瓣和浓郁的香味而闻名。


The centifolious rose bush produced a stunning display of blossoms. 这株多瓣玫瑰花丛开出了令人惊艳的花朵。|近义词：multipetaled, many-petaled |反义词：single-petaled, simple
