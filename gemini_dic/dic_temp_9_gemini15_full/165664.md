premonarchical:/ˌpriːmɒnˈɑːkɪkl/| adj.|君主制以前的；前君主制的|Existing or occurring before the establishment of a monarchy.  The term describes a period or system of governance that predates the existence of a monarchy.  It suggests a state of affairs before a king, queen, or emperor ruled.|例句：The premonarchical society relied on tribal elders for governance. = 君主制以前的社会依靠部落长老来治理。

The premonarchical era saw numerous small kingdoms vying for power. = 君主制以前的时代，许多小王国争夺权力。|近义词：ante-monarchical, pre-regal|反义词：post-monarchical, monarchical
