edentate:/ˈɛdənˌteɪt/ | adj., n. | 无齿的；贫齿类动物 | Lacking teeth;  belonging to an order of mammals characterized by the absence or reduction of teeth.  |例句：The anteater is an edentate mammal.  食蚁兽是一种贫齿类哺乳动物。

The sloth is an example of an edentate. 树懒是贫齿类动物的一个例子。


近义词：toothless (adj.),  (for the animal order) xenarthran


反义词：dentate (adj.)


词性：形容词，名词
