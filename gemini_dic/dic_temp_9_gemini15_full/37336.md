commencer:/kɒmɒ̃ˈseɪ/ /kɑːmənˈsɛr/| v.|开始，着手 |To begin or start something.|The concert will commence at 8 pm. 音乐会将于晚上8点开始。|The project commenced last month.  项目上个月启动。|begin, initiate, start|end, finish, conclude|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He commences his work early every day. 他每天很早就开始工作。
She commences her speech with a joke. 她以一个笑话开始她的演讲。

一般过去时:
The meeting commenced at 2 pm yesterday. 昨天的会议在下午2点开始。
They commenced their journey at dawn. 他们黎明时分开始了他们的旅程。

现在完成时:
The construction has commenced. 建筑工程已经开始。
The project has commenced and is progressing well. 项目已经启动，进展顺利。

过去完成时:
The play had commenced before we arrived. 我们到达之前，戏剧已经开始了。
After the rain had commenced, we went inside.  雨开始下后，我们进去了。

将来时:
The game will commence at 7 pm tomorrow. 明天晚上7点比赛开始。
The festival will commence next week. 节日将于下周开始。

虚拟语气:
If only the work had commenced on time! 要是工作能按时开始就好了！
I wish the meeting had commenced earlier. 我希望会议能早点开始。

被动语态:
The work was commenced last week. 工作上周开始了。
The investigation has been commenced. 调查已经开始。

疑问句:
When will the ceremony commence? 仪式什么时候开始？
Has the construction commenced yet? 建筑工程已经开始了吗？

否定句:
The meeting did not commence on time. 会议没有准时开始。
The work has not yet commenced. 工作还没有开始。
