overnicely:/ˌoʊvərˈnaɪsli/| adv.|过分友善地，过分客气地|excessively or insincerely polite or friendly.  It implies an artificiality or excessive effort in being nice.|例句：He overnicely complimented her dress, making her suspicious. 他过分地夸赞她的裙子，让她起了疑心。

例句：She overnicely agreed to everything he said, which seemed unnatural. 她对他说的一切都过分客气地表示同意，这显得不自然。


近义词：excessively politely, overly friendly, insincerely nice, excessively kind


反义词：rudely, bluntly, frankly, discourteously
