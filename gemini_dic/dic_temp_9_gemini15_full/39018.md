confitent: /ˈkɒnfɪdɒnt/ | adj. | 自信的，确信的 | Confident describes someone who feels sure of their abilities or qualities.  They are self-assured and believe in themselves. |  She is confident about her chances of winning the race. 她对赢得比赛很有信心。 He was confident that he had made the right decision. 他确信自己做出了正确的决定。 | self-assured, assured, sure, positive | insecure, doubtful, hesitant, unsure | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She is confident in her abilities. = 她对自己的能力很有信心。
He is confident he will pass the exam. = 他确信自己会通过考试。


一般过去时:
I was confident I would succeed. = 我确信我会成功。
She felt confident after her presentation. = 演讲后她感觉很自信。


现在完成时:
He has been confident throughout the whole process. = 在整个过程中，他一直都很有信心。
They have become more confident since joining the team. = 加入团队后，他们变得更加自信了。


过去完成时:
I had been confident until I saw the results. = 直到看到结果我才不再那么自信。
She had felt confident before the interview. = 面试前她感觉很自信。


将来时:
I will be confident once I finish my preparations. = 完成准备工作后，我就会很有信心。
They will be confident if they practice more. = 如果他们多练习，就会更有信心。


虚拟语气:
If I were more confident, I would try harder. = 如果我更有自信，我会更加努力。
I wish I were as confident as she is. = 我希望我能像她一样自信。


被动语态:
His confidence was shaken by the criticism. = 他的信心受到了批评的打击。(被动语态在此处较为少见，通常使用主动语态)
Her ability was underestimated, but she remained confident. = 她的能力被低估了，但她仍然很自信。(被动语态在此处较为少见，通常使用主动语态)


疑问句:
Are you confident about your future? = 你对你的未来有信心吗？
Is he confident enough to take on the challenge? = 他有足够的信心来应对挑战吗？


否定句:
I'm not confident I can finish the project on time. = 我不确定自己能否按时完成项目。
She wasn't confident in her abilities at first. = 她一开始对自己的能力没有信心。


PHRASE->
be confident in/about = 对...有信心

look confident = 看起来自信
remain confident = 保持自信
gain confidence = 获得自信
