chigoe:/ˈtʃɪɡoʊ/| n.|  恙虫病, 恙螨|A chigoe is a small, parasitic flea that burrows into the skin of humans and animals, causing intense itching and inflammation.  The disease it causes is called chigoe flea infestation or tungiasis.|例句：The chigoe flea is a common problem in tropical and subtropical regions. 恙螨在热带和亚热带地区很常见。

例句：He developed a severe chigoe infestation after walking barefoot on the beach. 他在沙滩上赤脚行走后，患上了严重的恙虫病。


近义词：tunga flea (同义词，更科学的名称)
反义词：无


词性：名词
