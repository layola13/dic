intifada:/ˌɪntɪˈfɑːdə/| n. |起义，暴动|A Palestinian uprising against Israeli occupation.  The term generally refers to the uprisings that occurred in the late 20th and early 21st centuries. |例句：The first Intifada began in 1987.  第一次起义始于1987年。|例句：The second Intifada, also known as the Al-Aqsa Intifada, was marked by increased violence.  第二次起义，也被称为阿克萨起义，以暴力升级为特征。|近义词：rebellion, uprising, revolt, insurrection|反义词：peace, tranquility, calm|名词
