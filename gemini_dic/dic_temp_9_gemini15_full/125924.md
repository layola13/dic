mushed:/mʌʃt/| adj. v.| 糊状的；捣烂；弄糊涂 |  To make or become soft and pulpy; to crush or mash into a pulp.  |例句：The potatoes were mushed after being boiled for too long.  土豆煮得太久都烂糊了。
The baby mushed his food all over his high chair.  婴儿把食物弄得到处都是。|近义词：mashed, pulped, crushed |反义词：firm, solid


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mushes his food. = 他把食物弄成糊状。
The machine mushes the potatoes. = 机器把土豆捣成泥。

一般过去时:
She mushed the bananas. = 她把香蕉捣烂了。
I mushed the berries for the pie. = 我把浆果捣碎做馅饼。

现在完成时:
He has mushed all the vegetables. = 他已经把所有蔬菜都捣烂了。
The dog has mushed the toy. = 狗已经把玩具弄坏了。

过去完成时:
They had mushed the peas before adding them to the soup. = 他们在把豌豆加到汤里之前就把它们捣碎了。
She had mushed the potatoes until they were completely smooth. = 她把土豆捣成泥直到它们完全光滑。

将来时:
I will mush the carrots for the baby. = 我将为婴儿把胡萝卜捣烂。
They will mush the ingredients together. = 他们将把配料混合在一起。

虚拟语气:
If I mushed the ingredients, it would be easier to mix. = 如果我把配料捣碎，混合起来会更容易。
I wish I hadn't mushed the cake. = 我真希望我没有把蛋糕弄糊了。

被动语态:
The potatoes were mushed by the machine. = 土豆是被机器捣烂的。
The fruit was mushed by the child. = 水果是被孩子弄烂的。

疑问句:
Did you mush the apples? = 你把苹果捣烂了吗？
Has he mushed the vegetables yet? = 他把蔬菜捣烂了吗？

否定句:
I didn't mush the potatoes. = 我没有把土豆捣烂。
She hasn't mushed the bananas yet. = 她还没有把香蕉捣烂。
