francolin:/ˈfræŋkəlɪn/| n.|鹑； फ्रैंकोलिन|A francolin is a type of bird belonging to the pheasant family, characterized by their brown plumage and often found in Africa and Asia.  They are ground-dwelling birds known for their distinctive calls.|例句：Several species of francolin are found in the grasslands of eastern Africa.  几种鹑类生活在东非的草原上。

例句：The hunter spotted a grey francolin hiding in the tall grass. 猎人发现一只灰鹑躲在高高的草丛中。|近义词：partridge, quail|反义词：无|词性:名词
