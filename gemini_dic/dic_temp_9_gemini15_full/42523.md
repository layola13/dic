countermagnet:/ˈkaʊntərˌmæɡnɪt/| n.|反磁铁|A countermagnet is a magnet used to counteract or neutralize the effect of another magnet.|Example:  The device uses a countermagnet to prevent the needle from being deflected by external magnetic fields.  该装置使用反磁铁来防止指针被外部磁场偏转。|近义词： opposing magnet, neutralizing magnet |反义词： magnet (in the context of opposing effect)
