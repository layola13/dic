commemorator:/kəˈmɛməreɪtər/ | n. |纪念者，纪念物 | A person or thing that commemorates something.  A commemorative object. |例句：The war memorial is a powerful commemorator of the fallen soldiers.  这座战争纪念碑是为阵亡士兵而建的强有力的纪念物。

The new museum is a fitting commemorator of the city's history.  这座新博物馆是城市历史的恰当纪念物。|近义词：memorialist, celebrant |反义词：obliterator, destroyer
