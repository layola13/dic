demerge:/dɪˈmɜːrdʒ/| v.|公司分立；(企业)分离|To separate a company into two or more independent companies.  The process is the opposite of a merger.|例句：The parent company decided to demerge its subsidiaries to improve efficiency. 母公司决定将其子公司分立以提高效率。

例句：After years of integration, the corporation is now considering demerging into several smaller, specialized companies.  经过多年的整合，这家公司现在正在考虑将其分拆成几家规模较小、专业化的公司。


近义词：separate, split, divide, unmerge

反义词：merge, combine, consolidate

词性：动词
