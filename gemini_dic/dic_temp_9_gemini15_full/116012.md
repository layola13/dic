marshland:/ˈmɑːrʃlænd/| n. |沼泽地；湿地 |Land that is seasonally or permanently flooded and is characterized by the presence of water-tolerant vegetation. Marshlands are important ecosystems providing habitat for diverse wildlife and offering flood control and water purification benefits. |例句：The birds thrive in the marshland. 这些鸟类在沼泽地里茁壮成长。
例句：The marshland was teeming with life. 沼泽地里充满了生机。|近义词：wetland, swamp, bog, mire |反义词：dryland, upland
