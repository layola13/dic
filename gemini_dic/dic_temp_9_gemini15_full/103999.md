juglone:/ˈdʒuːɡloʊn/ | n. |胡桃醌|A dark-colored naphthoquinone that is a natural allelochemical produced by the walnut tree (Juglans species) and is toxic to many plants.  It inhibits the growth of other plants near walnut trees, a phenomenon known as allelopathy.| Example: Juglone in the soil suppressed the growth of nearby tomatoes.  土壤中的胡桃醌抑制了附近番茄的生长。 |近义词:allelochemical, phytotoxin |反义词:growth promoter
