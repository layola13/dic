long-sufferingly:/lɒŋˈsʌfərɪŋli/| adv.| 忍耐地；长期忍受地 | patiently and for a long time, often despite hardship or provocation. |例句：She long-sufferingly endured his constant complaints. 她忍耐地忍受了他不断的抱怨。

例句：He long-sufferingly waited for the bus, even though it was already an hour late. 他忍耐地等候公共汽车，即使它已经晚了一个小时。


近义词：patiently, tolerantly, enduringly

反义词：impatiently, intolerantly


词性：副词
