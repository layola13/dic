complimented:/ˈkɒmplɪmɛntɪd/ | vt. | 称赞；恭维 | To express praise or admiration for someone or something.  |例句：He complimented her on her beautiful dress. 他称赞她穿的漂亮衣服。 |例句：The teacher complimented the student on their excellent work. 老师称赞学生们优秀的工作。|近义词：praised, lauded, commended | 反义词：criticized, condemned, disparaged | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He compliments his colleagues regularly. = 他经常称赞他的同事。
She compliments her friends on their achievements. = 她经常称赞朋友们的成就。

一般过去时:
He complimented her on her cooking skills. = 他称赞她的厨艺。
They complimented the team on their hard work. = 他们称赞团队的辛勤工作。

现在完成时:
She has complimented me on my presentation several times. = 她已经几次称赞我的演示了。
He has complimented her new haircut. = 他已经称赞她新的发型了。

过去完成时:
I had complimented him on his speech before he left. = 他离开之前我已经称赞了他的演讲。
They had complimented the artist on his masterpiece before the exhibition ended. = 展览结束前，他们已经称赞了这位艺术家的杰作。

将来时:
I will compliment her on her singing voice tomorrow. = 我明天将称赞她的歌声。
He will compliment her on her new job. = 他将祝贺她找到新工作。

虚拟语气:
If I saw her, I would compliment her on her new car. = 如果我看到她，我会称赞她的新车。
I wish I had complimented him on his performance. = 我真希望我当时称赞了他的表演。

被动语态:
His work was complimented by the judges. = 他的作品受到了评委的称赞。
Her efforts were complimented by her manager. = 她的努力得到了经理的赞扬。

疑问句:
Did he compliment you on your work? = 他称赞你的工作了吗？
Has she ever complimented your cooking? = 她曾经称赞过你的厨艺吗？

否定句:
He didn't compliment her on her dress. = 他没有称赞她的衣服。
They didn't compliment the band's performance. = 他们没有称赞乐队的演出。


PHRASE->
compliment someone on something = 称赞某人某事
a sincere compliment = 真诚的赞美
give a compliment = 给予赞美
receive a compliment = 收到赞美
pay a compliment = 奉承
