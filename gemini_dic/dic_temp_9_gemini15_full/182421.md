renewalist: /rɪˈnuːəlɪst/ | n. | 更新主义者，革新者 | A person who advocates or participates in renewal, especially in a specific field such as art, literature, or politics.  They believe in revitalizing existing structures or systems rather than completely replacing them.  | Example:  The renewalist movement in architecture emphasized sustainable building practices. 更新主义者在建筑方面的运动强调可持续的建筑实践。 | 近义词：reformer, innovator, modernizer | 反义词：traditionalist, conservative, reactionary | 名词
