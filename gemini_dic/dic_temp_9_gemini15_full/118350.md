merino:/məˈriːnoʊ/| n.|美利奴羊，美利奴羊毛|Merino refers to a breed of sheep known for its fine, soft wool.  The wool is highly prized for its softness, drape, and warmth.|Example: The sweater is made from 100% merino wool. 这件毛衣由100%美利奴羊毛制成。|近义词：sheep (general term), wool (material)|反义词：无
