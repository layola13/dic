chanukah: /ˈxɑːnəkə/ | n. |光明节|The Jewish festival of lights, commemorating the rededication of the Second Temple in Jerusalem.  It is celebrated for eight nights and days, beginning on the 25th of Kislev according to the Hebrew calendar.  The festival involves the lighting of a special nine-branched candelabrum called a menorah, the recitation of prayers, and the eating of traditional foods like latkes (potato pancakes) and sufganiyot (jelly doughnuts).|  We celebrated Chanukah with family and friends, lighting the menorah each night. 我们和家人朋友一起庆祝光明节，每晚点亮烛台。

The family gathered to play dreidel and eat traditional Chanukah foods.  家人聚在一起玩陀螺并吃传统光明节食物。|近义词：Festival of Lights|反义词：None|名词
