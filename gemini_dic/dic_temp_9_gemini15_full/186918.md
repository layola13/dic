ripe:/raɪp/| adj.|成熟的；时机成熟的|Ready for harvesting or use, often implying a state of fullness or perfection.  It can also refer to something being ready or suitable for a particular purpose or action.|例句：The mangoes are ripe and ready to eat. 芒果成熟了，可以吃了。
The time is ripe for change.  时机成熟了，可以改变了。|近义词：mature, ready, perfect|反义词：unripe, green, immature|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fruit is ripe. = 水果成熟了。
The plan is ripe for implementation. = 计划已成熟，可以实施了。

一般过去时:
The tomatoes were ripe last week. = 西红柿上周成熟了。
The opportunity was ripe for the taking. = 机会已经成熟，可以把握了。

现在完成时:
The apples have ripened. = 苹果已经成熟了。
The conditions have ripened for a breakthrough. = 突破的条件已经成熟了。

过去完成时:
The peaches had ripened before the frost. = 霜冻来临之前桃子已经成熟了。
The situation had ripened to a point of crisis. = 局势已经发展到危机的关头。

将来时:
The grapes will be ripe next month. = 下个月葡萄就成熟了。
The moment will be ripe for a decisive action. =  时机成熟时将采取果断行动。

虚拟语气:
If the conditions were ripe, we would proceed. = 如果条件成熟，我们将继续进行。
I wish the time were ripe for peace talks. = 我希望时机成熟，可以进行和平谈判。

被动语态:
The fruit was ripened by the sun. = 水果被太阳晒熟了。
The plan was considered ripe for approval. = 该计划被认为已经成熟，可以批准。

疑问句:
Are the bananas ripe yet? = 香蕉熟了吗？
Is the time ripe for a new beginning? = 时机成熟可以开启新的篇章了吗？

否定句:
The pears are not ripe yet. = 梨子还没熟。
The situation is not ripe for negotiations. =  形势尚未成熟，不宜谈判。
