rephrases:/ˌriːˈfreɪz/| vt.|改写；重新表达；解释|To express something in different words, often to make it clearer or more effective.|例句：The lawyer rephrased the question to make it easier for the witness to understand. 律师改写了这个问题，以便证人更容易理解。|近义词：reword, paraphrase, restate|反义词：misinterpret


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rephrases his arguments regularly. = 他经常改写他的论点。
She rephrases complex ideas into simpler terms. = 她将复杂的思想改写成更简单的术语。


一般过去时:
The teacher rephrased the difficult sentence. = 老师改写了那个难懂的句子。
He rephrased his answer to avoid confusion. = 他改写了他的答案以避免混淆。


现在完成时:
She has rephrased the entire document. = 她已经改写了整篇文件。
They have rephrased their proposal to address the concerns. = 他们已经改写了他们的提案以解决这些担忧。


过去完成时:
He had rephrased his speech several times before the presentation. = 他在演讲之前已经改写了他的演讲稿好几次了。
She had rephrased the email before sending it to her boss. = 在发送给老板之前，她已经改写了邮件。


将来时:
I will rephrase my comments to be more concise. = 我将改写我的评论以使其更简洁。
She will rephrase the instructions to be clearer. = 她将改写说明以使其更清晰。


虚拟语气:
If I were to rephrase it, I would say this. = 如果我要改写它，我会这么说。
I wish I had rephrased my response more carefully. = 我希望我更仔细地改写我的回应。


被动语态:
The sentence was rephrased to improve clarity. = 句子被改写以提高清晰度。
The instructions will be rephrased for better understanding. = 说明将被改写以更好地理解。


疑问句:
How would you rephrase this sentence? = 你将如何改写这个句子？
Should we rephrase the question to be more specific? = 我们是否应该将问题改写得更具体一些？


否定句:
He didn't rephrase his statement, despite the criticism. = 尽管受到批评，他也没有改写他的声明。
She couldn't rephrase the complex equation in simpler terms. = 她无法将复杂的方程式改写成更简单的术语。
