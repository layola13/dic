jebel:/ˈdʒɛbəl/| n. |山，山脉|A jebel is an Arabic word for a mountain or mountain range, often used in place names in North Africa and the Middle East.  The term is particularly common in geographical contexts referring to mountainous regions.|例句：Jebel Musa is a mountain on the Sinai Peninsula.  摩西山位于西奈半岛。|例句：The Jebel Akhdar is a mountain range in Oman.  绿山山脉位于阿曼。|近义词：mountain, range, peak|反义词：valley, plain
