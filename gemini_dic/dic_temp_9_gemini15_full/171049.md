pseudoturbinal: /ˌsuːdəʊˈtɜːrbɪnəl/ | n. |假鼻甲骨|A pseudoturbinal is a bony projection in the nasal cavity that resembles a true turbinate bone but is not involved in the same way in the functions of warming, humidifying, and filtering air.  It is often smaller and less developed than a true turbinate. Pseudoturbinal bones are found in certain species and can vary in their morphology and presence across different animals. |The study focused on the anatomical variations of the pseudoturbinal bones in different breeds of dogs.  这项研究集中在不同品种犬只的假鼻甲骨的解剖学差异上。|近义词: accessory turbinate,  ethmoturbinal (depending on location)|反义词: true turbinate
