immoveable: /ɪˈmuːvəbl/ | adj. | 不可移动的；坚定不移的 |  Unmovable; fixed; steadfast.  Describes something that cannot be moved or something that is unwavering in its position or determination. |


例句：

The rock was immoveable.  = 这块石头是不可移动的。

His resolve was immoveable in the face of opposition. = 面对反对，他的决心坚定不移。


近义词：fixed, unmovable, steadfast, unshakeable, inflexible


反义词：movable, mobile, flexible, yielding


词性：形容词
