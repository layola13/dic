hypocritely:/hɪˈpɒkrɪtli/| adv. |虚伪地，伪善地 |In a hypocritical manner;  insincerely.  It describes actions or speech that are inconsistent with one's professed beliefs or feelings. |例句：He hypocritely claimed to be a devout Christian while cheating on his taxes. 他虚伪地声称自己是虔诚的基督徒，却逃税。 He spoke hypocritely about the importance of honesty while secretly stealing from his colleagues. 他虚伪地谈论诚实的重要性，却暗中偷窃同事的财物。|近义词：insincerely, deceptively, falsely, sanctimoniously |反义词：honestly, sincerely, genuinely, truthfully |副词
