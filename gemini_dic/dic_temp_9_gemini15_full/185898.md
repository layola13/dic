revolutioneering: /ˌrevəluːʃəˈnɪrɪŋ/ | v. | 革新；彻底改革 |  The act of creating or bringing about a revolution, especially in a specific field or industry.  It implies a radical and transformative change, going beyond mere improvement or innovation.  | 例句：The company is revolutioneering the way we think about transportation.  这家公司正在彻底改变我们对交通运输的看法。


例句：The startup is revolutioneering the healthcare industry with its innovative technology. 这家初创公司正在利用其创新技术彻底改变医疗保健行业。

近义词：revolutionizing, transforming, reforming, innovating

反义词：conserving, maintaining, preserving

词性: 动词
