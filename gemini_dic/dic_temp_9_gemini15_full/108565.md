lcd:/ˌɛlsiːˈdiː/| n. |液晶显示器|Liquid Crystal Display; a flat-panel display or other electronically modulated optical device that uses the light-modulating properties of liquid crystals.  LCDs are used in a wide variety of applications, including computer monitors, televisions, and portable devices. |Example: My new laptop has a high-resolution LCD screen. 我的新笔记本电脑有一个高分辨率的液晶显示屏。|近义词：display, screen, monitor |反义词：CRT (Cathode Ray Tube)|名词
