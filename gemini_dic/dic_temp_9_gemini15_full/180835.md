rehauled:/ˌriːˈhɔːld/| vt.|彻底检修；大修；彻底改革|To overhaul or repair something completely.  It implies a significant and comprehensive process of improvement or restoration.|例句：The old engine was completely rehauled. 这台旧发动机进行了彻底的大修。

例句：The company's marketing strategy was rehauled after a series of poor sales results.  公司在经历了一系列糟糕的销售业绩后，彻底改革了营销策略。


近义词：overhaul, renovate, revamp, reconstruct, refurbish

反义词：destroy, demolish, ruin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rehaul the system every year. = 他们每年都会对系统进行检修。
The mechanic rehauls engines for a living. = 这名机械师靠修理发动机为生。

一般过去时:
We rehauled the old house last summer. = 我们去年夏天对老房子进行了翻修。
The government rehauled the education system. = 政府对教育体系进行了改革。

现在完成时:
They have rehauled the website several times. = 他们已经多次修改了网站。
The factory has rehauled its production line. = 工厂已经对其生产线进行了检修。

过去完成时:
They had rehauled the car before selling it. = 他们在卖车之前已经对它进行了大修。
The company had rehauled its image before the launch. = 公司在发布之前已经重塑了自己的形象。

将来时:
We will rehaul the kitchen next month. = 我们下个月将翻修厨房。
The airline will rehaul its fleet of planes. = 航空公司将对其飞机机队进行大修。

虚拟语气:
If they had rehauled the brakes, the accident might have been avoided. = 如果他们之前对刹车进行了检修，事故可能就不会发生了。
I wish they had rehauled the software earlier. = 我真希望他们早点更新软件。

被动语态:
The engine is being rehauled at the moment. = 发动机正在维修中。
The entire system will be rehauled next year. = 整个系统将于明年进行检修。

疑问句:

否定句:
