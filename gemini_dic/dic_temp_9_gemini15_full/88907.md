hold-down:/ˈhoʊlddaʊn/| n., v.|固定物；抑制；压制；维持；担任（工作）|

英文解析：As a noun, "hold-down" refers to something that holds something else down, preventing it from moving or escaping. As a verb, it means to keep something or someone in place, to suppress something, or to maintain a position or level.

例句：
The straps acted as a hold-down for the cargo during transport.  = 在运输过程中，这些带子充当货物的固定物。

The company implemented new policies to hold down inflation. = 公司实施了新的政策以抑制通货膨胀。


近义词：
Noun: restraint, fastener, clamp, retainer
Verb: restrain, suppress, repress, maintain, retain, secure


反义词：
Noun: release, lift, unfastener
Verb: release, liberate, elevate, increase, relinquish


词性：Noun, Verb
