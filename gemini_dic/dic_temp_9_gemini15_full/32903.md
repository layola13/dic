choo:/tʃuː/| v.| 选择，挑选 | to select or choose something from a number of possibilities |He chose a red car. 他选择了一辆红色的汽车。|select, pick, opt |refuse, reject|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I choose the red one. = 我选择红色的那个。
She chooses her words carefully. = 她仔细地选择措辞。

一般过去时:
They chose to stay home. = 他们选择待在家里。
He chose a different path. = 他选择了一条不同的道路。

现在完成时:
I have chosen my career path. = 我已经选择了我的职业道路。
She has chosen a beautiful dress for the party. = 她已经为聚会选择了一件漂亮的裙子。

过去完成时:
I had chosen my outfit before the party started. = 派对开始前我已经选好了我的衣服。
They had chosen a campsite before nightfall. = 天黑之前他们已经选好了营地。

将来时:
I will choose a gift for her birthday. = 我将选择一件礼物送给她过生日。
She will choose the best option. = 她将选择最佳方案。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would choose differently. = 如果我是你，我会做出不同的选择。
I wish I had chosen a different major. = 我真希望我当初选择不同的专业。

被动语态:
The winner was chosen by a panel of judges. = 获胜者由评审团选出。
The date for the meeting has been chosen. = 会议日期已经确定。

疑问句:
Which one do you choose? = 你选哪个？
What will you choose to study? = 你将选择学习什么？

否定句:
I didn't choose that option. = 我没有选择那个选项。
She didn't choose to participate. = 她没有选择参与。


PHRASE->
choose from = 从...中选择
choose to = 选择做...
choose between = 在...之间选择
can't choose = 无法选择
choose wisely = 明智地选择
