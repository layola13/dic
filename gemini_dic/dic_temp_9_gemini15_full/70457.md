figurehead:/ˈfɪɡərhed/| n.|傀儡；挂名领导；船头像|A figurehead is a person who has a position of authority but no real power.  They are often put in place to give an appearance of leadership or legitimacy.  It can also refer to an ornamental figurehead on the bow of a ship.|例句：The CEO is just a figurehead; the real power lies with the board of directors.  这个首席执行官只是一个傀儡；真正的权力掌握在董事会手中。|例句：The ship's figurehead was carved from wood and painted in vibrant colors. 这艘船的船头像是用木头雕刻而成，并涂上了鲜艳的色彩。|近义词：puppet, frontman, nominal leader|反义词：leader (with real power), authority
