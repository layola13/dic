garners:/ˈɡɑːrnərz/ | n. | 收集；积累；谷仓；粮仓 | To gather and store something, typically grain.  | The farmer garners his harvest each autumn.  农民每年秋天都收集收获的庄稼。 | accumulates, collects, amasses | dissipates, scatters | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He garners information from various sources. = 他从各种来源收集信息。
The company garners a large profit each year. = 这家公司每年赚取巨额利润。

一般过去时:
She garnered much praise for her performance. = 她因其表演而获得许多赞誉。
The museum garnered a significant donation. = 博物馆获得了一笔可观的捐款。

现在完成时:
They have garnered support from many community members. = 他们已经获得了许多社区成员的支持。
The project has garnered significant media attention. = 该项目已获得了大量的媒体关注。

过去完成时:
He had garnered enough evidence to win the case. = 他已收集到足够的证据来赢得官司。
The team had garnered several victories before the final match. = 该队在决赛前已取得数次胜利。

将来时:
She will garner many awards for her work. = 她将因她的工作获得许多奖项。
The company expects to garner significant market share. = 公司预计将获得可观的市场份额。

虚拟语气:
If he garnered more support, he might win the election. = 如果他获得更多支持，他可能会赢得选举。
I wish I had garnered more experience before taking this job. = 我希望我在接受这份工作之前积累了更多经验。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
