nonassertiveness:/ˌnɒnəˈsɜːtɪvnəs/| n. |非坚定；不果断；缺乏自信|The inability or unwillingness to express one's opinions or needs clearly and directly.  It often manifests as passivity, avoidance of conflict, and difficulty in saying "no."|例句：Her nonassertiveness allowed others to take advantage of her.  她的不果断使别人占了她的便宜。

例句：His nonassertiveness in the meeting prevented him from voicing his concerns. 他在会议上的不果断阻止了他表达自己的担忧。


近义词：passivity, submissiveness, meekness, timidity

反义词：assertiveness, decisiveness, confidence, boldness

词性：名词
