The word "counterdistinction" is a combination of "counter-" (meaning "against" or "opposite") and "distinction" (meaning "a difference or contrast").  It's a relatively formal word, often found in academic writing.  The root of "distinction" traces back to the Latin word "distinguere," meaning "to distinguish."
