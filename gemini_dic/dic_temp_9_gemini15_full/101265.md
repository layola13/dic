involucel:/ɪnˈvɒljʊsɛl/ | n. | 小总苞；小总苞片 | A small involucre, especially one subtending a partial umbel or a flower cluster of an umbel-like inflorescence.  An involucel is a whorl of bracts that subtends a secondary umbel in an umbelliferous plant.  | Example: The small, delicate involucels of the plant were barely visible to the naked eye.  小巧精致的小总苞几乎肉眼不可见。 | 近义词: bract, involucre | 反义词:  (无明确反义词)
