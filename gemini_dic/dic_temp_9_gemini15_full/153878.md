percipients:/pərˈsɪpiənts/| n. |感知者，感觉者 |Those who perceive; those capable of sensory experience and understanding.  People who are aware of and responsive to their environment and the things happening around them. |Example: The experiment aimed to study the responses of percipients to different stimuli.  实验旨在研究感知者对不同刺激的反应。

The study focused on the subjective experiences of percipients.  这项研究侧重于感知者的主观体验。|近义词：receivers, observers, experiencers |反义词：unaware, insensitive, unresponsive |名词
