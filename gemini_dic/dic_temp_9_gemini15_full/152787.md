pecks:/peks/| n. v.|啄；少量；啄食；轻吻 |A peck is a quick light kiss.  A peck is also a small amount of something, often dry.  As a verb, it means to strike repeatedly with the beak (as a bird) or to kiss lightly. |例句：The birds were pecking at the seeds.  鸟儿在啄食种子。
The farmer gave his daughter a peck on the cheek. 农民在他的女儿脸上轻轻一吻。He only bought a peck of potatoes. 他只买了一小袋土豆。|近义词：peck at, nibble, kiss (lightly), |反义词：gorge, devour, smack


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hen pecks at the grain. = 母鸡啄食谷物。
He pecks away at his work. = 他埋头工作。


一般过去时:

She pecked him on the cheek. = 她在他脸上轻吻了一下。

She has pecked at her food all day. = 她整天都在少量地吃东西。


过去完成时:

They had pecked at the agreement for weeks. = 他们已经就协议争论了好几个星期。
