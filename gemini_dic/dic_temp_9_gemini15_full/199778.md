sirvente:/sɜːrˈvɛnt/| n.|叙事诗|A sirvente is a type of Occitan lyric poem, typically satirical or political in nature, prevalent in the troubadour tradition of Southern France.  It often features a dialogue or debate, and is characterized by its irregular rhyme scheme and stanza structure. |Example:  The troubadours used the sirvente to express their political opinions and social commentary.  吟游诗人用叙事诗来表达他们的政治观点和社会评论。|近义词：canzone (in some contexts)|反义词：N/A
