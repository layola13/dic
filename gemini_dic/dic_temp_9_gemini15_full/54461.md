disintegrated: /ˌdɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪd/ | v. | 瓦解；崩溃；解体 |

英文解析:To disintegrate means to break down or decompose into smaller parts; to lose cohesion or unity; to crumble or fall apart.  It can refer to both physical and abstract things.

例句:

The old building disintegrated over time. = 这座旧建筑物随着时间的推移而逐渐崩塌。

The empire disintegrated into warring factions. = 这个帝国瓦解成了互相争斗的派系。


近义词: crumble, decay, decompose, fall apart, break down, disband

反义词: coalesce, integrate, unify, consolidate, solidify


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The old car slowly disintegrates. = 旧车慢慢地解体。
The team disintegrates under pressure. = 团队在压力下瓦解。


一般过去时:
The ancient monument disintegrated centuries ago. = 古代纪念碑几个世纪前就崩塌了。
Their relationship disintegrated after a series of arguments. = 他们经过一系列争吵后关系破裂了。


现在完成时:
The evidence has disintegrated over time. = 证据随着时间的推移已经消失了。
The political party has disintegrated since the election. = 自选举以来，这个政党已经瓦解了。


过去完成时:

The empire had disintegrated long before the invasion. = 在入侵很久之前，帝国就已经瓦解了。


将来时:
The ice will disintegrate as the temperature rises. = 随着温度升高，冰会融化。
The company is likely to disintegrate if it doesn't restructure. = 如果公司不进行重组，很可能会瓦解。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:
