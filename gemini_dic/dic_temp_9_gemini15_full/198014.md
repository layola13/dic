shocking: /ˈʃɑːkɪŋ/ /ˈʃɒkɪŋ/ | adj. |令人震惊的，令人吃惊的 | Extremely surprising, upsetting, or disturbing.  |例句：The news of the accident was shocking.  事故的消息令人震惊。

The discovery of the hidden treasure was shocking to everyone.  发现隐藏的宝藏让每个人都感到震惊。


近义词：astonishing, astounding, surprising, startling, alarming, appalling, horrifying


反义词： unsurprising, expected, ordinary, commonplace


USAGE->

The news is shocking. = 这个消息令人震惊。
