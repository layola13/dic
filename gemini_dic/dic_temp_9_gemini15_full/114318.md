maintenance: /ˈmeɪntənəns/ | n. | 保养；维护；维修 | The act of maintaining something in good working order.  It involves preserving, repairing, and improving something to prevent deterioration or failure.  | 例句：Regular maintenance is crucial for the longevity of your car. 定期保养对你的汽车的寿命至关重要。

例句：The building requires extensive maintenance. 这栋建筑需要大规模的维修。


近义词： upkeep, preservation, repair, servicing | 反义词：neglect, deterioration, destruction | 词性: 名词
