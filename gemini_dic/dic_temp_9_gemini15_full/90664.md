humblebee:/ˈhʌmbəlbiː/| n. |大黄蜂|A large, hairy bee, typically black and yellow, that makes a loud buzzing sound.  Humbbees are important pollinators. |例句：The bumblebee buzzed lazily around the flower. 大黄蜂懒洋洋地围绕着花朵嗡嗡作响。|例句：We saw a bumblebee collecting pollen from the roses. 我们看到一只大黄蜂从玫瑰花中采集花粉。|近义词：bumblebee|反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑体型较小的蜜蜂small bee)|名词
