flameproofing: /ˈfleɪmpruːfɪŋ/ | n. adj. | 防火处理；阻燃处理 | The process of treating materials to make them resistant to fire.  It can refer to the process itself or the resulting material. | 例句：The factory implemented strict flameproofing measures for all its textiles. = 工厂对所有纺织品都实施了严格的防火措施。

The curtains were made of flameproofing fabric to prevent fire spreading. = 窗帘采用阻燃织物制成，以防止火势蔓延。


近义词：fireproofing, fire retardant treatment | 反义词：flammable treatment
