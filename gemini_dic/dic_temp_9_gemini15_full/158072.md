plasterboards:/ˈplæstəbɔːrdz/| n.|石膏板，纸面石膏板|A building material consisting of a core of gypsum plaster sandwiched between two layers of cardboard.|例句：The walls are made of plasterboards. 墙壁是用石膏板做的。

The ceiling was finished with plasterboards. 天花板是用石膏板装饰的。|近义词：drywall, wallboard|反义词：无
