lighting:/ˈlaɪtɪŋ/| n.|照明，灯光，闪电|The act of illuminating something or the state of being illuminated; the arrangement or effect of lights in a place. It can also refer to a sudden flash of light, such as lightning.|例句：The lighting in the restaurant was dim and romantic.  餐厅的灯光昏暗而浪漫。|例句：The sudden lighting scared the children.  突然的闪电吓到了孩子们。|近义词：illumination, illumination, luminance|反义词：darkness, shadow
