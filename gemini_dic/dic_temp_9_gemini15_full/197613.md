shepherdia:/ˌʃɛpərˈdiːə/| n. |沙棘属植物|Shepherdia is a genus of shrubs in the family Elaeagnaceae, native to North America.  The genus includes several species known for their edible berries and attractive foliage.  They are often used in landscaping and as a source of food and medicine. |Example: Several species of Shepherdia are cultivated for their nutritious berries.  几种沙棘属植物因其营养丰富的浆果而被种植。|近义词:  (no direct synonyms,  but other berry-producing shrubs could be considered similar)|反义词: (no direct antonyms)|名词
