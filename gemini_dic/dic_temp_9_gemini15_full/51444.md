dethronements:/ˌdiːθroʊnˈmɛnts/| n.|废黜；罢黜；逐出王位|The act of dethroning someone, especially a monarch.  The plural form of "dethronement."|例句：The dethronements of several kings led to years of instability.  几个国王的废黜导致了多年的动荡。

The historical dethronements were often violent and bloody affairs. 历史上多次废黜国王的事件往往暴力血腥。|近义词：ousts, dismissals, removals|反义词：enthronements, installations, coronations|名词
