mizzenmast:/ˈmɪzənˌmæst/| n. | mizzen帆(后)桅 | The mizzenmast is the mast nearest the stern of a three-masted sailing ship.  It is typically smaller than the mainmast and foremast. |例句：The captain ordered the crew to furl the sails on the mizzenmast. 船长命令船员收起后桅上的帆。

例句：The mizzenmast creaked ominously in the rising wind.  后桅在越来越大的风中发出不祥的嘎吱声。


近义词：aftermast (less common)


反义词：foremast, mainmast
