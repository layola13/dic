self-governments:/ˈsɛlf ˈɡʌvənmənts/| n. |自治政府；自我管理|Self-governments are political entities that exercise control over their own affairs without external interference.  自治政府是能够自行管理事务而不受外部干扰的政治实体。|Example: Many indigenous groups advocate for greater self-governments to protect their cultural heritage. 许多土著群体倡导更大的自治权以保护其文化遗产。|近义词：autonomous regions, independent states |反义词：colonies, dependencies
