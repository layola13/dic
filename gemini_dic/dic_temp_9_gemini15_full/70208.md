fibrosis:/faɪˈbroʊsɪs/ | n. |纤维化 |The abnormal formation of fibrous connective tissue, often as a reparative process after tissue injury.  It can occur in various organs and tissues and lead to organ dysfunction.  |例句：Long-term exposure to asbestos can lead to lung fibrosis. 长期接触石棉会导致肺纤维化。|例句：Liver fibrosis is a serious condition that can progress to cirrhosis. 肝纤维化是一种严重的疾病，可能发展为肝硬化。|近义词：scarring, hardening, sclerosis |反义词：tissue regeneration, healing
