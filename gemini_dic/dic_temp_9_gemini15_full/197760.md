shilly-shally:/ˈʃɪli ˈʃæli/| v.|犹豫不决，畏缩不前|To hesitate or delay making a decision; to be indecisive.|例句：Stop shilly-shallying and make up your mind!  别犹豫不决了，做决定吧！

例句：He shilly-shallied for weeks before finally accepting the job. 他犹豫了好几个星期才最终接受了这份工作。


近义词：hesitate, procrastinate, dawdle, vacillate, dither


反义词：decisive, resolute, determined


USAGE->

He shilly-shallies too much. = 他太犹豫不决了。
They shilly-shally over every decision. = 他们每个决定都犹豫不决。

She shilly-shallied before answering my question. = 她回答我的问题前犹豫不决。

PHRASE->
There are not many common phrases directly using "shilly-shally".  The word itself functions as a complete phrase often.  Examples of related phrases could include:

Don't shilly-shally! = 别犹豫不决！
Stop shilly-shallying around! = 别再磨蹭了！
Enough shilly-shallying! = 别再犹豫了！
He's all shilly-shally. = 他总是犹豫不决。
She's been shilly-shallying about it for ages. = 她为此犹豫不决很久了。



词源:
The origin is uncertain, but it's believed to be of imitative origin, perhaps onomatopoeic, suggesting the sound of hesitant speech or action.  The word appeared in the late 16th century.
