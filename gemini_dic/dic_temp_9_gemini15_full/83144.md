gutsiest:/ˈɡʌtsiːst/| adj.|最勇敢的，胆量最大的|The most courageous or bold.  It is the superlative form of "gutsy."  This word implies a willingness to take risks and face danger or opposition with bravery.|例句：Her gutsiest move was quitting her secure job to start her own business. 她最勇敢的举动是辞去稳定的工作，自己创业。

例句：The gutsiest firefighter rushed into the burning building to save the trapped child. 最勇敢的消防员冲进燃烧的建筑物去救被困的孩子。


近义词：bravest, most courageous, most daring, most audacious|反义词：most cowardly, most timid, most faint-hearted|词性：形容词
