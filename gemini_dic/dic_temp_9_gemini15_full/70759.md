finer:/ˈfaɪnər/| adj.|更精细的，更好的，更优秀的 | More refined or delicate; of superior quality.  It implies a higher degree of skill, precision, or elegance. |例句：The finer details of the painting were lost in the reproduction.  这幅画的精细之处在复制品中丢失了。

The finer points of the argument were difficult to grasp.  论证中更精妙之处难以理解。


近义词：better, superior, more refined, more delicate, more subtle |反义词：coarser, rougher, inferior


词源：源自古英语 *fīn,  意思是“纯净的，精致的”，与“fine”同源。 “-er”是比较级后缀。
