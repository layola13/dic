prelegal: /ˌpriːˈliːɡəl/ | adj. |法律之前的；法律预备的；准法律的 | Relating to the period or activities before a legal matter is formally initiated; preparatory to legal action.  | 例句：He undertook prelegal research to strengthen his case. 他进行了法律预备研究以加强他的案情。

例句：The prelegal negotiations failed to resolve the dispute.  法律预备谈判未能解决争议。


近义词： preliminary, preparatory, antecedent

反义词： postlegal, judicial


词性: adjective
