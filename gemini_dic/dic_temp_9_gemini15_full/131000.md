nonattendance: /ˌnɒnəˈtɛndəns/ | n. |缺席，不到场 |The lack of attendance; the state of being absent.  It often refers to a failure to attend something expected or required. |例句：His nonattendance at the meeting raised concerns.  他的缺席会议引起了担忧。

例句：The professor noted the student's frequent nonattendance in his class. 教授注意到这位学生经常旷课。


近义词：absence, default, nonparticipation |反义词：attendance, presence
