cageyness:/ˈkeɪdʒɪnəs/| n. |谨慎；狡猾；隐瞒；遮掩|The quality of being cagey; unwillingness to reveal information.  It suggests a deliberate attempt to conceal something, often for strategic reasons.  This differs from simple shyness or reserve, implying a more calculating and manipulative intent.|The detective noted the cageyness in the suspect's answers, indicating he was hiding something.  侦探注意到嫌疑人回答中的谨慎，表明他隐瞒了什么。|shyness,secretiveness, evasiveness|openness, frankness, candor|名词
