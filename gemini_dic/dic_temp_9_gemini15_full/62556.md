energetically: /ˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪkli/ | adv. |精力充沛地；充满活力地 |In a way that shows energy and enthusiasm.  |例句：She energetically pursued her goals. 她精力充沛地追求自己的目标。

He energetically defended his ideas in the debate. 他在辩论中充满活力地捍卫了自己的观点。


近义词：vigorously, dynamically, powerfully, actively, forcefully

反义词：lazily, lethargically, sluggishly, passively


词源：

* **energy:**  From Late Middle English *energie*, from Old French *energie*, from Late Latin *energia*, from Greek *energeia* "activity, operation," from *energos* "active, working," from *en* "in" + *ergon* "work."

* **-ly:**  A common adverbial suffix in English, indicating manner or quality.
