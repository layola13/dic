pre-books:/ˌpriːˈbʊks/| n. |预订的书；预售的书 |Books ordered or reserved in advance of publication or release.  Pre-books refer to books that are ordered before their official release date, often to guarantee a copy or to receive a special edition or bonus material. | Example:  The pre-books for the new fantasy novel are already exceeding expectations. 新奇幻小说的预订量已超出了预期。|近义词：advance orders, reservations, pre-orders |反义词：back-orders, late orders
