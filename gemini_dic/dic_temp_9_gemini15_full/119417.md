microfinance:/ˈmaɪkroʊfaɪnæns/| n. |小额信贷；微型金融|The provision of financial services to low-income individuals or groups who lack access to traditional banking services.  It aims to empower the poor by providing them with access to credit, savings, insurance, and other financial tools. |例句：Microfinance institutions play a crucial role in poverty reduction.  小额信贷机构在减贫中发挥着至关重要的作用。|近义词：micro lending, small-scale finance|反义词：macrofinance, large-scale finance|名词
