self-disgust:/ˌsɛlf dɪsˈɡʌst/| n.|自我厌恶，反感自己|A feeling of intense dislike or loathing towards oneself.  This can stem from various sources, such as perceived failures, moral shortcomings, or negative self-image.|例句：Overwhelmed by self-disgust, she retreated to her room.  被自我厌恶淹没，她退回了房间。|例句：His self-disgust was palpable after he lied to his friends.  他对朋友撒谎后，自我厌恶之情溢于言表。|近义词：self-loathing, self-contempt, self-abhorrence|反义词：self-love, self-acceptance, self-esteem|名词
