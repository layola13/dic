perambulate:/pəˈræmbjuleɪt/ | v. |四处走动；漫步；巡视 | To walk about or through a place.  |例句：We perambulated the streets of the old town. 我们漫步在古城的大街上。 |例句：The professor perambulated the lecture hall, engaging students in discussion. 教授在演讲厅里走来走去，与学生们进行讨论。|近义词：stroll, walk, wander, ramble |反义词：remain stationary, stay put


USAGE->

They perambulate the park every morning. = 他们每天早晨都在公园里散步。
He perambulates the city streets, observing the daily life. = 他在城市街道上漫步，观察着日常生活。


一般过去时:
We perambulated the countryside last weekend. = 我们上周末在乡下散步。
