fennels:/ˈfɛnəl/| n.|茴香；茴香属植物|The word "fennel" refers to a plant in the genus *Foeniculum*, belonging to the carrot family (Apiaceae). It's known for its aromatic leaves, stalks, and seeds, which are used in cooking and medicine.  The most common type is *Foeniculum vulgare*.|The fennel seeds added a lovely licorice flavor to the bread.|近义词：anise, dill|反义词：无
