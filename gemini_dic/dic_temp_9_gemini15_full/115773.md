mariners:/ˈmærɪnərz/| n.|水手们，海员们|People who work on ships, especially sailors.  Mariners are skilled in navigation and seamanship.|例句：The mariners faced a fierce storm during their voyage. 水手们在航行中遭遇了猛烈的风暴。

例句：Experienced mariners know how to read the signs of the sea.  经验丰富的海员知道如何解读海上的征兆。


近义词：sailors, seamen, seafarers
反义词：None (antonyms are not directly applicable, as it's a collective noun)
词性:noun
