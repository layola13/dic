dirndls:/ˈdɪrndlz/| n.|迪尔德尔裙（复数）|A dirndl is a traditional type of dress worn by women in some parts of Central Europe, particularly Bavaria and Austria.  It typically consists of a bodice, a full skirt, and an apron.|例句：The women at the Oktoberfest were all wearing beautiful dirndls.  慕尼黑啤酒节上的妇女都穿着美丽的迪尔德尔裙。The collection showcased a variety of styles of dirndls.  该系列展示了各种风格的迪尔德尔裙。|近义词：traditional dress, folk costume|反义词：modern dress
