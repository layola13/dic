re-harvested: /ˌriːˈhɑːrvɪstɪd/ | v. | 再收获，二次收获 |  To harvest again; to harvest a crop a second time after an initial harvest.  | Example: The farmer re-harvested the field after the initial crop was damaged by a storm.  农民在第一次收成被暴风雨破坏后再次收割了田地。 | 近义词:  reap again, harvest a second time | 反义词:  leave unharvested


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-harvest the coffee beans every few months. = 他们每隔几个月就会再次收获咖啡豆。
Farmers re-harvest their crops when conditions are favorable. = 当条件有利时，农民会再次收割庄稼。

一般过去时:
The vineyard re-harvested the grapes after the first harvest was deemed insufficient. = 葡萄园在第一次收成被认为不足后再次收割了葡萄。
The orchard re-harvested the apples to maximize yield. = 果园再次收割苹果以最大限度地提高产量。

现在完成时:
They have re-harvested the field twice this year due to an unusually long growing season. = 今年由于生长季节异常长，他们已经两次再次收割了田地。
The farmers have re-harvested their crops, resulting in a surplus. = 农民们再次收割了他们的庄稼，导致了过剩。

过去完成时:
Before the second harvest, they had re-evaluated the soil conditions. = 在第二次收割之前，他们已经重新评估了土壤条件。
The team had re-harvested the data after discovering an error in the initial collection. = 团队在发现初始收集中的错误后重新收集了数据。

将来时:
We will re-harvest the herbs next week. = 我们下周将再次收割香草。
The company will re-harvest the timber in the spring. = 公司将在春天再次收割木材。

虚拟语气:
If we had re-harvested the crop earlier, we would have avoided the losses. = 如果我们早点再次收割庄稼，我们就不会遭受损失。
I wish we had re-harvested the wheat before the frost. = 我真希望我们在霜冻前再次收割了小麦。

被动语态:
The land was re-harvested by the local farmers. = 这块土地被当地农民再次收割。
The data was re-harvested to improve the accuracy of the analysis. = 数据被再次收割以提高分析的准确性。

疑问句:

否定句:

The company will not re-harvest the timber this season. = 公司本季不会再次收割木材。
