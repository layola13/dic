postdepression:/ˌpoʊstdɪˈprɛʃən/| adj.| 后抑郁期的；抑郁症后的 |  Describing the period of time following a major depressive episode, characterized by recovery and potential lingering effects.  It often refers to the challenges and adjustments individuals face after their symptoms have subsided.  | Example:  She experienced significant postdepression fatigue even after completing her therapy. 她即使在完成治疗后也经历了严重的术后疲劳。 |近义词：post-depressive, recovery period | 反义词：acute depression, active depression |
