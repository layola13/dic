shrilled:/ʃrɪld/| v.|尖声叫喊，刺耳地叫|to utter or pronounce with a shrill, high-pitched voice.  |例句：She shrieked in terror when she saw the snake. 她看到蛇后吓得尖叫起来。|例句：The child shrieked with delight at the sight of the toys.  看到玩具，孩子高兴地尖叫起来。|近义词：screamed, shrieked, squeaked|反义词：muttered, whispered|动词
