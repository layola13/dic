extemporises:/ɪkˈstempəraɪz/| v. |即兴创作；临时发挥；无准备地演讲 |To speak or perform without preparation or notes; to improvise.  |He extemporised a speech on the importance of education. 他即兴发表了一篇关于教育重要性的演讲。|improvise, ad-lib |premeditate, plan, rehearse |
