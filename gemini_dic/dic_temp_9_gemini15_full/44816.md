crush:/krʌʃ/| /krʌʃ/| n., vt.|压碎；迷恋；挫败|To press or squeeze something with force so that it breaks, loses its shape, or is reduced to pieces;  A strong feeling of infatuation or love, especially one that is temporary or unrequited; To defeat or overwhelm someone or something completely.|例句：He crushed the can with his foot. 他用脚踩扁了易拉罐。|例句：She has a crush on her classmate. 她迷恋她的同学。|近义词：smash, pulverize, infatuation, crush on|反义词：build, construct, create, indifference
