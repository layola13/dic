outbawl:/aʊtˈbɔːl/| vt.|大声哭喊，大声哭叫|to cry or shout loudly and noisily, especially to express grief or anger. |例句：The child outbawled his anger when his toy broke.  孩子玩具坏了，大声哭喊着表达他的愤怒。|例句：She outbawled her grief at the funeral.  她在葬礼上大声哭诉她的悲伤。|近义词：wail, yell, scream, roar|反义词：whisper, murmur
