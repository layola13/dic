marveller:/ˈmɑːrvələr/ | n. |惊奇者，赞叹者 |One who marvels; one filled with wonder and amazement. |例句：He was a marveller at the beauty of the natural world. 他对自然之美充满了惊奇。|近义词：admirer, wonderer |反义词：critic, cynic |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist marvels at the sunset. = 艺术家对日落感到惊奇。
She marvels at her child's progress. = 她对孩子的进步感到惊奇。

一般过去时:
He marvelled at the size of the building. = 他对建筑物的规模感到惊奇。
They marvelled at the magician's tricks. = 他们对魔术师的戏法感到惊奇。

现在完成时:
I have marvelled at the speed of technological advancement. = 我一直对科技进步的速度感到惊奇。
She has marvelled at the beauty of the Grand Canyon. = 她一直对大峡谷的美景感到惊奇。

过去完成时:

They had marvelled at the singer's voice before the power went out. = 在停电之前，他们曾对歌手的歌声感到惊奇。

将来时:
We will marvel at the new discoveries in space. = 我们将对太空中的新发现感到惊奇。
They will marvel at the architecture of the city. = 他们将对城市的建筑感到惊奇。

虚拟语气:
If I were there, I would marvel at the scenery. = 如果我在那里，我会对风景感到惊奇。
I wish I could marvel at the aurora borealis. = 我希望我能看到北极光并为之惊叹。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
