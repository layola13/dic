rejected:/rɪˈdʒɛktɪd/ | adj. | 被拒绝的；遭到否决的 |  Rejected means refused or not accepted.  It describes something that has been turned down or dismissed. |  Example: His application was rejected. 他的申请被拒绝了。  The proposal was rejected by the committee.  该提案被委员会否决了。 |近义词：refused, declined, spurned, dismissed | 反义词：accepted, approved, embraced


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company rejects unsuitable candidates. = 公司拒绝不合适的候选人。
He rejects all forms of violence. = 他反对一切形式的暴力。


一般过去时:
They rejected my offer. = 他们拒绝了我的提议。
The judge rejected the evidence. = 法官驳回了证据。


现在完成时:
She has rejected several job offers. = 她已经拒绝了好几份工作机会。
The court has rejected the appeal. = 法院驳回了上诉。


过去完成时:
He had rejected the idea before we even discussed it. = 在我们讨论之前，他就已经否定了这个主意。
They had rejected his proposal months ago. = 几个月前他们就已经拒绝了他的提案。


将来时:
They will reject the plan if it's not feasible. = 如果计划不可行，他们将会拒绝。
I think they will reject the application. = 我认为他们会拒绝申请。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would not reject such a good opportunity. = 如果我是你，我不会拒绝这么好的机会。
I wish they hadn't rejected my manuscript. = 我真希望他们没有拒绝我的稿件。


被动语态:
His application was rejected due to lack of experience. = 由于缺乏经验，他的申请被拒绝了。
The proposal was rejected because it was unrealistic. = 该提案因为不切实际而被拒绝了。


疑问句:
Was your application rejected? = 你的申请被拒绝了吗？
Why was the project rejected? = 为什么项目被否决了？


否定句:
They didn't reject my idea; they just needed more time. = 他们并没有拒绝我的想法，他们只是需要更多时间。
The committee didn't reject the proposal outright. = 委员会没有直接拒绝该提案。
