pimpernel:/ˈpɪmpənɛl/| n. |田紫草，小金盏花|A small, low-growing plant with small, bright red, pink, or white flowers.  The common pimpernel (Anagallis arvensis) is known for its flowers closing up in wet weather, hence its nickname "poor man's weatherglass".|The pimpernel closed its petals as the rain began to fall.  田紫草的花瓣在雨开始下的时候合上了。| 近义词：scarlet pimpernel | 反义词：无
