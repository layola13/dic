It's used to measure extremely high power levels, often associated with high-energy physics experiments and powerful lasers.| Example: The laser produced a petawat pulse of energy. 激光产生了一皮瓦的能量脉冲。|近义词：terawatt (less powerful), exawatt (more powerful) |反义词：无
