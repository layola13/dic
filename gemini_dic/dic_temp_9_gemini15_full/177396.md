reawaken:/ˌriːəˈweɪkən/| vt.|重新唤醒；重新激起；使复活|To awaken again; to rouse from sleep or inactivity; to revive or rekindle.|例句：The news reawakened his hopes for a better future.  这个消息重新燃起了他对美好未来的希望。

例句：The music reawakened her childhood memories. 这音乐唤起了她童年的回忆。


近义词：revive, reanimate, rouse, awaken|反义词：quench, extinguish, lull, suppress|动词
