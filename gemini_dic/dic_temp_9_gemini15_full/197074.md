shame:/ʃeɪm/| n. |羞耻，羞愧；耻辱；令人遗憾的事|A feeling of guilt, embarrassment, or disgrace.|例句：He felt a deep shame after his dishonest actions. 他在不诚实的行为之后感到深深的羞愧。|例句：The shame of losing the game was hard to bear. 输掉比赛的耻辱难以承受。|近义词：disgrace, humiliation, guilt, dishonor|反义词：pride, honor, glory


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He feels shame about his mistake. 他为自己的错误感到羞愧。
She experiences shame when she lies. 她撒谎时感到羞愧。

一般过去时:
I felt a deep shame after I lied to my parents. 我对父母撒谎后感到很羞愧。
He showed shame for his actions. 他为自己的行为感到羞愧。


现在完成时:
I have felt shame about this incident for weeks.  我已经为此事件感到羞愧好几个星期了。
She has never experienced such shame in her life. 她一生从未经历过如此的羞耻。


过去完成时:
I had felt shame before I confessed my mistake. 在我承认错误之前，我已经感到羞愧了。
She had never felt such shame before that moment. 在那一刻之前，她从未感到如此羞愧。


将来时:
He will feel shame if he fails the exam. 如果考试不及格，他会感到羞愧。
She will likely feel shame if she's caught cheating.  如果她作弊被抓，她很可能会感到羞愧。


虚拟语气:
I would feel shame if I betrayed my friend. 如果背叛我的朋友，我会感到羞愧。
She wished she hadn't acted in a way that caused shame. 她希望自己没有做出令人羞愧的行为。


被动语态:
His actions were a source of shame to his family. 他的行为给他的家人带来了耻辱。
The scandal brought shame upon the entire organization. 这场丑闻给整个组织带来了耻辱。


疑问句:
Do you feel any shame for your behavior? 你对你的行为感到羞愧吗？
Does he understand the shame he has caused? 他明白他造成的耻辱吗？


否定句:
He showed no shame for his actions. 他对自己的行为毫不羞愧。
She felt no shame in admitting her mistake. 她毫不羞愧地承认了自己的错误。


PHRASE->
put to shame 使相形见绌
a burning shame 非常令人遗憾的事
a source of shame 耻辱的根源
ashamed of oneself 为自己感到羞愧
to feel shame 感到羞愧
