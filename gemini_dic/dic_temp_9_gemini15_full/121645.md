misperform:/ˌmɪspərˈfɔːrm/| v. | 表现不佳；失职；未能达到预期 | To fail to perform a task or duty satisfactorily; to do something badly or inefficiently. |例句：He misperformed his duties, resulting in a significant loss for the company. 他失职，导致公司损失惨重。 |例句：The software misperformed and crashed several times during the presentation.  软件在演示过程中多次出现故障并崩溃。|近义词：fail, underperform, botch, blunder |反义词：excel, succeed, perform well
