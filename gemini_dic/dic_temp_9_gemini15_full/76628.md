gallops:/ˈɡæləps/| v.|飞奔，疾驰|To move very fast, especially on horseback or in a vehicle.|The horse galloped across the field.  马飞奔过田野。|The car galloped down the highway.  汽车飞驰在高速公路上。|canters, charges, dashes|walks, crawls


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gallops his horse every morning. = 他每天早上骑马飞奔。
The deer gallops through the forest. = 鹿在森林里飞奔。

一般过去时:
The knight galloped into battle. = 骑士飞奔进入战场。
She galloped her horse across the finish line. = 她骑着马飞奔过终点线。

现在完成时:
The horse has galloped many miles today. = 这匹马今天已经飞奔了许多英里。
They have galloped across the plains. = 他们已经飞奔过平原。

过去完成时:
The rider had galloped for hours before he stopped. = 骑手在停下来之前已经飞奔了几个小时。
Before the race, the jockey had galloped the horse several times. = 比赛前，骑师已经让马飞奔了几次。

将来时:
The jockeys will gallop their horses in the race. = 骑师们将在比赛中让他们的马飞奔。
He will gallop his horse to victory. = 他将骑着马飞奔去赢得胜利。

虚拟语气:
If I had a horse, I would gallop across the country. = 如果我有一匹马，我就会骑着它飞奔穿越乡村。
I wish I could gallop like the wind. = 我希望我能像风一样飞奔。

被动语态:
The horse was galloped hard by the rider. = 马被骑手用力地驱使着飞奔。
The race was won by the horse that galloped the fastest. = 飞奔最快的马赢得了比赛。

疑问句:
Does he gallop his horse every day? = 他每天都骑马飞奔吗？
Will they gallop the horses before the race? = 他们会在比赛前让马飞奔吗？

否定句:
The horse didn't gallop very fast. = 马跑得并不快。
He doesn't usually gallop his horse so hard. = 他通常不会那么用力地驱使他的马飞奔。
