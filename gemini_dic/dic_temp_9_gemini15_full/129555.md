non-busy:/ˌnɑːnˈbɪzi/| adj. |不忙的，空闲的 |Not busy; free from work or other commitments.  The term is often used informally to describe a period of time or a person's state. |例句：I'm non-busy this afternoon, so I can help you with that project.  今天下午我不忙，所以我可以帮你做那个项目。


例句：The non-busy hours at the library are a great time to study. 图书馆不忙的时候是学习的好时间。|近义词：free, available, unoccupied, idle, at leisure |反义词：busy, occupied, engaged
