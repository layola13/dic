heteronyms:/ˈhɛtərənɪmz/| n. |异形同音词|Words that are spelled the same but have different meanings and sometimes different pronunciations.|Example:  The "lead" (pronounced /led/) in a pencil is different from the verb "lead" (pronounced /liːd/) to guide.  The noun "minute" (a unit of time, /ˈmɪnɪt/) is different from the adjective "minute" (tiny, /maɪˈnuːt/).|近义词：homographs (if considering only spelling)|反义词：无
