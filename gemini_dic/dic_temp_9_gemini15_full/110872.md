lionising: /ˈlaɪənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 崇拜；吹捧；把…奉若神明 | To treat someone as a celebrity.  To praise someone excessively. |例句：The media lionized the athlete after his victory. 媒体在运动员获胜后对他大加赞扬。

例句：The young author was lionized by critics and readers alike.  年轻的作家受到了评论家和读者的一致推崇。


近义词：celebrate, glorify, idolize, extol, deify

反义词：criticize, condemn, denigrate, belittle, ignore


词源：源自lion (狮子)，引申为像对待狮子一样尊敬和崇拜，因为狮子是力量和威严的象征。  The suffix "-ise" (-ize in American English) indicates the act of making something or someone into something else.

一般现在时:
They lionize every successful entrepreneur. = 他们崇拜每一个成功的企业家。
The press lionizes her every move. = 媒体把她的一举一动都视为焦点。


一般过去时:
The town lionized the returning war hero. = 这个小镇的人们把回乡的战争英雄奉若神明。
The critics lionized his latest novel. = 批评家们高度赞扬了他的最新小说。


现在完成时:
The media has lionized him since his groundbreaking discovery. = 自从他取得突破性发现以来，媒体一直把他奉若神明。
Society has lionized this artist for decades. = 社会几十年来一直推崇这位艺术家。


过去完成时:
They had lionized him before he even won the award. = 在他获奖之前，人们就已经把他奉若神明了。
The public had lionized her for her charitable work. = 公众因为她的慈善事业而把她奉若神明。


将来时:
They will lionize her when she wins the Nobel Prize. = 当她获得诺贝尔奖时，人们将会把她奉若神明。
Future generations will lionize him for his contributions to science. = 子孙后代将会因为他对科学的贡献而崇拜他。


虚拟语气:
If he won the lottery, he would be lionized by the press. = 如果他赢得彩票，他将被媒体奉若神明。
I wish I were lionized for my writing. = 我希望我的写作能得到人们的推崇。


被动语态:
He was lionized by the public for his bravery. = 他因其勇敢而受到公众的推崇。
The new technology was lionized by investors. = 投资者对这项新技术大加赞扬。


疑问句:
Will he be lionized for his achievements? = 他会因为他的成就而被人们推崇吗？
Is he being lionized for his charitable work? = 他因为慈善工作而受到推崇吗？


否定句:
They did not lionize him despite his success. = 尽管他成功了，他们并没有崇拜他。
The public did not lionize her work, unfortunately. = 不幸的是，公众并没有推崇她的作品。


PHRASE->
There are not many common idiomatic phrases directly using "lionise". The meaning is usually conveyed directly.  However, phrases that convey similar meanings include:

make a star of someone = 把某人捧成明星
make much of someone = 对某人特别重视
hold someone in high regard = 对某人评价很高
heap praise on someone = 对某人赞不绝口
to be the toast of the town = 成为城里的热门人物
