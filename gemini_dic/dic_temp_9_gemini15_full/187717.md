roos:/ruːz/| n.|袋鼠（复数）|The plural of "roo," a colloquial abbreviation of kangaroo.|Example:  I saw many roos hopping across the outback. 我看见许多袋鼠在内陆跳跃。|近义词：kangaroos|反义词：无


USAGE->
This word is a plural noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.


PHRASE->
There aren't common phrases specifically using "roos" as it's a shortened plural.  Phrases would typically use "kangaroos".  Examples using "kangaroo" instead:

a mob of kangaroos 一群袋鼠
kangaroo court  袋鼠法庭 (unfair trial)

kangaroo paw 袋鼠爪 (a type of flower)
