disavow:/dɪsæˈvaʊ/| vt.|否认；抵赖；与…断绝关系|To deny or repudiate; to refuse to acknowledge or accept.  The statement disavows any connection with the criminal activity. 这份声明否认与犯罪活动有任何联系。|近义词：deny, repudiate, renounce, disclaim|反义词：affirm, acknowledge, admit, endorse|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disavows any knowledge of the incident. 他否认知道此事。
The company disavows responsibility for the accident. 公司否认对事故负责。


一般过去时:
She disavowed her previous statement. 她否认了她之前的声明。
The government disavowed the actions of its agents. 政府否认其特工的行为。


现在完成时:
He has disavowed all connection with the organization. 他已经否认与该组织有任何联系。
The witness has disavowed her testimony. 证人已经否认了她的证词。


过去完成时:
They had disavowed their allegiance to the king. 他们已经否认效忠国王。
The company had disavowed the fraudulent scheme before it was exposed. 公司在欺诈计划被曝光之前就否认了它。


将来时:
He will disavow the contract if the terms are not met. 如果不满足条件，他将否认合同。
The politician will disavow his earlier comments.  这位政客将否认他之前的言论。


虚拟语气:
If he disavowed his past, he might have a chance. 如果他否认他的过去，他或许有机会。
I wish he would disavow those hurtful remarks. 我希望他能否认那些伤人的话。


被动语态:
His involvement in the scandal was disavowed by the company. 公司否认他参与了丑闻。
The statement was disavowed by the spokesperson.  发言人否认了这一说法。


疑问句:
Will the company disavow its previous claims? 公司会否认其之前的声明吗？
Did he disavow his support for the candidate? 他是否否认了对候选人的支持？


否定句:
The suspect did not disavow his guilt. 嫌疑人没有否认他的罪行。
She would not disavow her beliefs, even under pressure. 即使在压力下，她也不会否认自己的信仰。
