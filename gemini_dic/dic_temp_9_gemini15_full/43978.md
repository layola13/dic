crikey:/ˈkraɪki/| int.|哎呀；我的天哪；(澳、英非正式) |An exclamation of surprise, astonishment, or dismay, similar to "wow," "good heavens," or "my goodness."  It is primarily used in Australia, New Zealand, and parts of the United Kingdom. |例句：Crikey! That's a big spider! 哎呀！那是一只大蜘蛛！

例句：Crikey, I didn't expect that! 我的天哪，我没想到！


近义词：wow, gosh, my goodness, good heavens, jeez, blimey


反义词：无


词性：感叹词
