lostness:/ˈlɔːstnəs/ | n. |迷失；迷惘；困惑 |The feeling of being lost or disoriented, both physically and mentally.  It can refer to being unsure of one's location, purpose, or identity. |例句：The dense fog contributed to a feeling of lostness.  浓雾让人感到迷失。

例句：Overwhelmed by grief, she experienced a profound sense of lostness.  悲伤让她感到深深的迷惘。


近义词：disorientation, bewilderment, confusion, perplexity

反义词：orientation, clarity, certainty

词性：名词
