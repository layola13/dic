monodrama:/ˈmɒnəˌdrɑːmə/|/ˈmɑːnəˌdræmə/| n. |独角戏；一人剧 |A monodrama is a dramatic work written for a single performer. It can be a play, a poem, or a song cycle, and often involves a character directly addressing the audience.  |Example:  The actress gave a captivating performance in the monodrama, revealing the character's inner turmoil through her eloquent monologue.  这位女演员在独角戏中奉献了一场引人入胜的表演，通过她雄辩的独白展现了人物内心的动荡。|近义词：solo performance, one-person show, monologue |反义词：play (with multiple characters)
