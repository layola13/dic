hydroscope:/ˈhaɪdrəskoʊp/ | n. | 水下观察镜；潜望镜 | A hydroscope is an instrument for observing underwater objects.  It typically consists of a watertight tube with lenses at both ends, allowing the user to view submerged scenes.  | Example:  The diver used a hydroscope to examine the sunken ship's hull.  潜水员用潜望镜检查沉船的船体。 | 近义词：submarine periscope, underwater viewer | 反义词：None directly applicable.
