phosphated:/ˈfɒsfeɪtɪd/| adj.|磷酸化的；含磷酸的| Treated with phosphoric acid or a phosphate; containing phosphate.  The term often refers to a process where a substance is treated with a phosphate compound to improve its properties, such as corrosion resistance or flame retardancy.|例句：The metal surface was phosphated to prevent rust.  金属表面经过磷酸盐处理以防止生锈。

The engine parts were phosphated for better wear resistance. 引擎部件经过磷酸盐处理以提高耐磨性。|近义词：phosphorylated|反义词：dephosphated
