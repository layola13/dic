noncoking: /ˌnɑːnˈkoʊkɪŋ/ | adj. | 不结焦的；非焦煤的 | Not forming coke when heated.  This term is primarily used in the context of coal and its properties.  Noncoking coal is unsuitable for use in the production of coke, which is a crucial material in the iron and steel industry. | Example:  The power plant uses noncoking coal because it's cheaper and readily available.  这家发电厂使用非焦煤，因为这种煤更便宜且易于获得。|近义词: non-caking,  low-rank | 反义词: coking
