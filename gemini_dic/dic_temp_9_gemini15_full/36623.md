collectiveness:/kəˈlɛktɪvnɪs/| n. |集体性；集体主义；共同性 |The feeling or state of being collective; the practice or principle of giving priority to the group over the individual.  It emphasizes shared goals, responsibilities, and actions. |例句：The project succeeded due to the strong collectiveness of the team.  这个项目的成功归功于团队强大的集体性。
例句：The company's culture prioritizes collectiveness over individual achievement. 这家公司的文化更重视集体成就而非个人成就。|近义词：unity, solidarity, group spirit, community |反义词：individualism, separatism

词源:
The word "collectiveness" is derived from the adjective "collective," which in turn comes from the Latin word "collectivus," meaning "gathered together" or "collected."  The suffix "-ness" adds the sense of a state or quality.
