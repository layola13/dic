postmodernism:/ˌpoʊstˈmɑːdərnɪzəm/ | n. | 后现代主义 | Postmodernism is a late 20th-century movement in the arts, architecture, and criticism that was a departure from modernism.  It is characterized by skepticism towards grand narratives, objective truth, and universal values, embracing instead irony, pastiche, and the deconstruction of established norms.  | Example: The building's design is a striking example of postmodernism, blending classical and futuristic elements.  后现代建筑的设计是后现代主义的突出例子，它融合了古典和未来主义的元素。 | 近义词：poststructuralism, deconstruction | 反义词：modernism, classicism
