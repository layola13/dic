ingraft:/ˈɪnɡræft/| vt.|嫁接；植入；灌输|To insert (tissue, etc.) into a living organism; to implant.|例句：The surgeon skillfully ingrafted the new skin onto the burn victim. 外科医生熟练地将新的皮肤嫁接到烧伤患者身上。
例句：They attempted to ingraft democratic ideals into the authoritarian regime. 他们试图将民主理想灌输到专制政权中。|近义词：graft, implant, insert|反义词：remove, excise, delete|动词
