costumes:/ˈkɒstjuːmz/ /ˈkɑːstuːmz/| n.|服装，戏服|Clothing worn to create a particular look or character, especially for a play, party, or festival.  It can also refer to the style of clothing typical of a particular period or group.|例句：The actors wore elaborate costumes for the Shakespearean play.  演员们在莎士比亚戏剧中穿着华丽的服装。

例句：She chose a beautiful costume for the Halloween party. 她为万圣节派对选择了一套漂亮的服装。


近义词：outfits, garments, apparel, attire|反义词：None (Antonyms are context-dependent;  e.g., if contrasting with everyday clothing, then "everyday clothes" could be considered an antonym)|词性:名词
