/ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrtlɪzaɪz/ | vt. |施肥过多；过度施肥 | To apply more fertilizer to (something) than is needed or beneficial.  This can lead to negative consequences for plant health and the environment. | The farmer overfertilized his crops, resulting in stunted growth and nutrient runoff.  农民给庄稼施肥过多，导致生长迟缓和养分流失。|  overmanure, overfeed | underfertilize | 动词


USAGE->

He overfertilizes his lawn every spring. = 他每年春天都给草坪施肥过多。
They overfertilize their garden with chemical fertilizers. = 他们用化肥给花园施肥过多。

一般过去时:
The gardener overfertilized the roses last year. = 园丁去年给玫瑰施肥过多。
The farmer overfertilized his fields, leading to soil degradation. = 农民给田地施肥过多，导致土壤退化。


现在完成时:

They have overfertilized the land for years, polluting the nearby river. = 他们多年来过度施肥，污染了附近的河流。


过去完成时:
