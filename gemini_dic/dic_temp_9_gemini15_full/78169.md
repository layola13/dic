georgette:/dʒɔːˈʒɛt/| n. |乔其纱|A lightweight, sheer fabric with a slightly crinkled surface, typically made of silk, rayon, or polyester.|例句：The dress is made of delicate georgette. 这件裙子是用轻柔的乔其纱制成的。|近义词：crepe, chiffon|反义词:heavy fabric, thick material

词源:
Named after Georgette de la Plante, a French woman who is credited with popularizing the fabric in the early 20th century.  The exact origins of the fabric itself are less clear, with some attributing it to earlier developments in silk weaving techniques.
