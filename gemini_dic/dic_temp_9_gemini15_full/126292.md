myocarditis:/ˌmaɪ.oʊ.kɑːrˈdaɪ.tɪs/| n. |心肌炎|Inflammation of the heart muscle.  Myocarditis can be caused by viral infections, bacterial infections, autoimmune diseases, or certain medications.  It can range in severity from mild to life-threatening. |例句：The doctor diagnosed him with myocarditis after a series of tests. 医生在进行一系列检查后诊断他患有心肌炎。

例句：  He experienced chest pain and shortness of breath, symptoms consistent with myocarditis. 他经历了胸痛和呼吸短促，这些症状与心肌炎一致。


近义词：cardiomyopathy (if chronic and causing dysfunction), heart inflammation


反义词：无


词性:名词
