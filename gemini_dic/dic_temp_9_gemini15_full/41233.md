corallite:/ˈkɒrəlaɪt/| n. |珊瑚虫杯，珊瑚个体 |A corallite is the calcareous cup or skeleton of a single coral polyp in a coral colony.  It's the individual housing unit within a coral structure. |Example: The reef was composed of numerous corallite structures, each housing a tiny polyp.  这片珊瑚礁由许多珊瑚虫杯构成，每个杯中都住着一个微小的珊瑚虫。  |近义词：coral polyp (though not a direct synonym, as this refers to the living organism),  calyx |反义词：无 |名词

词源:
