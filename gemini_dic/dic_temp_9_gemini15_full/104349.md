juxtaposes:/ˈdʒʌkstəˈpoʊzɪz/| vt.|并置；并列；对比|To place (things) side by side or close together, especially for contrasting effect.|The artist juxtaposes images of war and peace in his paintings.  艺术家在他的画作中并置了战争与和平的图像。|The museum juxtaposes ancient artifacts with modern sculptures. 博物馆将古代文物与现代雕塑并列展出。|近义词：contrast, compare, place side by side|反义词：separate, isolate


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He juxtaposes contrasting ideas in his essays. = 他在他的文章中并置了对比鲜明的观点。
The photographer juxtaposes light and shadow in her work. = 摄影师在作品中并置了光与影。


一般过去时:
She juxtaposed the old and the new in her design. = 她在设计中并置了新旧元素。
The author juxtaposed two very different characters in the novel. = 作者在小说中并置了两个截然不同的角色。


现在完成时:
The artist has juxtaposed various textures in the sculpture. = 艺术家在雕塑中并置了各种不同的纹理。
The curator has juxtaposed paintings from different eras in the exhibition. = 馆长在展览中并置了不同时代的绘画作品。


过去完成时:
He had juxtaposed the two quotes to highlight their differences. = 他之前已经将这两段引言并列，以突出它们的差异。
The writer had juxtaposed the two scenes to create dramatic tension. = 作家已经将这两个场景并置，以营造戏剧性的张力。

虚拟语气:

I wish I had juxtaposed those two arguments more effectively. = 我希望我之前能更有效地将这两个论点并列。


被动语态:

The historical data is juxtaposed with current statistics. = 历史数据与当前统计数据并列。


疑问句:
How does the author juxtapose these seemingly unrelated themes? = 作者是如何将这些看似无关的主题并列的？
Does the artist successfully juxtapose the different styles? = 艺术家是否成功地将不同的风格并置？


否定句:
The writer did not juxtapose the two ideas effectively. = 作家没有有效地将这两个想法并置。
The director did not juxtapose the scenes well enough to create a clear contrast.  导演并没有很好地将这些场景并置以创造明显的对比。
