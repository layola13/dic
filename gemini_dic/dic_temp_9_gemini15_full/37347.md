commendation:/ˌkɒmənˈdeɪʃən/ /ˌkɑːmənˈdeɪʃən/| n. |赞扬；表彰；推荐；嘉奖|The act of praising or recommending someone or something.  It can refer to an official expression of approval or a statement of praise.|The company gave him a commendation for his hard work. 公司因他的辛勤工作而对他进行了表彰。|  He received a commendation for bravery. 他因勇敢而获得嘉奖。| praise, acknowledgment, tribute | criticism, condemnation | 名词

His work receives high commendation. = 他的工作受到高度赞扬。
