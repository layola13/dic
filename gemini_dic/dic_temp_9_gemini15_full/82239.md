ground:/ɡraʊnd/| n. v.|地面，土地；基础；理由；研磨|The solid surface of the earth; the basis or foundation of something; reason or cause; to reduce to powder or small pieces by rubbing or crushing.|例句：The ground was wet with dew.  地面上覆盖着露水。

The company is building a strong foundation for future growth. 这家公司正在为未来的增长打下坚实的基础。


近义词：earth, soil, basis, reason, foundation|反义词：air, sky, heaven, aloft
