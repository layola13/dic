candling:/ˈkændlɪŋ/| n. v.|烛光检验；检查；照亮|The process of inspecting eggs by holding them up to a light source to assess their quality.  Also refers to the act of illuminating something with a candle.|例句：We candled the eggs to check for cracks and blood spots. 我们用烛光检验鸡蛋，检查是否有裂缝和血斑。

The farmer candled each egg before packing them for sale. 农场主在包装鸡蛋出售之前对每个鸡蛋进行了烛光检查。

近义词：inspecting, examining, checking|反义词：obscuring, hiding|名词，动词
