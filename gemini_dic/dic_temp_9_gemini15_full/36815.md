colonialists:/kəˈloʊniəlɪsts/ | n. |殖民主义者 |People who support or practice colonialism, the policy or practice of acquiring full or partial political control over another country, occupying it with settlers, and exploiting it economically. Colonialists often imposed their culture, language, and political systems on the colonized population.  |Example: The colonialists exploited the resources of the colonized land for their own benefit. 殖民主义者为了自身的利益剥削被殖民地的资源。


The legacy of colonialists continues to impact many societies today. 殖民主义者的遗产至今仍在影响许多社会。|近义词：imperialists, conquerors, settlers (in a negative context) |反义词：anti-colonialists, liberationists, independence fighters
