missy:/ˈmɪsi/| n.|小姑娘，小女孩；（尤指）年轻女子|A young girl or woman, often used in a somewhat informal or affectionate way.  It can also be used in a condescending or patronizing way.|例句：The missy skipped down the street happily.  这个小姑娘兴高采烈地沿着街道蹦蹦跳跳。|例句：He addressed the young woman as "missy," which she found slightly patronizing. 他称呼那个年轻女子为“小姑娘”，她觉得有点居高临下。|近义词：girl, young woman, lass, maiden|反义词：old woman, elderly woman|名词
