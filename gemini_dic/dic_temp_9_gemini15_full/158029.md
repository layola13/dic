planticle: /ˈplæntɪkəl/ | n. | 植物小文章；植物小品文 | A short piece of writing about plants, often focusing on a specific aspect or species.  It can be informative, descriptive, or even persuasive in nature.  |  Example:  I read a fascinating planticle about carnivorous plants in the gardening magazine. 我在园艺杂志上读了一篇关于食肉植物的精彩小文章。 | 近义词: plant article, botanical brief, plant note | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑与讨论非植物主题的文章相对)  |  名词
