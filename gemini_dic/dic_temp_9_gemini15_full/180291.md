refried:/riːˈfraɪd/| adj.| 再煎的；油炸再制的 |  Refried means fried a second time.  It's most commonly used to describe beans that have been cooked, mashed, and then fried again, resulting in a creamy texture. | Example:  We had refried beans with our tacos. 我们在吃玉米饼时配了油炸豆。|近义词：reheated, recooked | 反义词：fresh, raw
