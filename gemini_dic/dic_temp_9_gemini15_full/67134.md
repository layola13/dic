exporter:/ɪkˈspɔːrtər/ | n. | 出口商，出口者 | A person or company that sells and ships goods to foreign countries.  |  China is a major exporter of textiles. 中国是主要的纺织品出口国。| importer | importer | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company exports tea to many countries. = 这家公司向许多国家出口茶叶。
He regularly exports his artwork to Europe. = 他定期向欧洲出口他的艺术品。


一般过去时:
They exported a large quantity of rice last year. = 他们去年出口了大量的稻米。
The factory exported machinery to Africa. = 工厂向非洲出口机械。


现在完成时:
We have exported over 1000 tons of steel this year. = 我们今年已经出口了超过1000吨的钢铁。
She has successfully exported her designs to the US market. = 她已经成功地将她的设计出口到美国市场。


过去完成时:
They had exported all the goods before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经出口了所有货物。
The company had exported a significant amount of coffee before the price increase. = 在价格上涨之前，该公司已经出口了大量的咖啡。


将来时:
We will export more products to Asia next year. = 我们明年将向亚洲出口更多产品。
The firm will be exporting its new software to several countries. = 该公司将把其新软件出口到几个国家。


虚拟语气:
If we exported more, we would make more profit. = 如果我们出口更多，我们将获得更多利润。
I wish we had exported more products last year. = 我希望我们去年出口更多产品。


被动语态:
Many goods are exported from this port. = 许多货物从这个港口出口。
The products were exported to various countries around the world. = 产品被出口到世界各地。


疑问句:
What goods does your company export? = 贵公司出口什么商品？
Where are these products exported to? = 这些产品出口到哪里？


否定句:
We don't export weapons. = 我们不出口武器。
The company did not export any goods last month. = 该公司上个月没有出口任何商品。


PHRASE->

agricultural exporter = 农业产品出口商
export market = 出口市场
export price = 出口价格
