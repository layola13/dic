loneliest: /ˈloʊnliəst/ | adj. | 最孤独的，最寂寞的 |  The most extreme degree of loneliness; feeling profoundly alone and isolated.  It is the superlative form of the adjective "lonely." |

例句：

She felt loneliest during the long winter evenings. = 冬天漫长的夜晚，她感到最孤独。

The loneliest place on Earth is not a desert island but a crowded city. = 地球上最孤独的地方不是荒岛，而是一个拥挤的城市。


近义词：most isolated, most desolate, most forlorn

反义词：most sociable, most surrounded, most connected

词性：形容词
