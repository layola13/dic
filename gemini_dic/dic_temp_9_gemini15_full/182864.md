repentant:/rɪˈpɛntənt/| adj. |悔恨的；懊悔的 |Feeling or showing sincere regret and remorse for one's wrongdoing or sin.  |例句：He was repentant for his actions and apologized sincerely. 他为自己的行为感到悔恨并真诚地道歉了。|例句：She looked repentant, her eyes filled with tears. 她看起来很悔恨，眼睛里充满了泪水。|近义词：remorseful, contrite, apologetic |反义词：unrepentant, remorseless, impenitent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is repentant for his mistakes. = 他为自己的错误感到悔恨。
She is repentant and seeks forgiveness. = 她很悔恨并寻求原谅。


一般过去时:
He was repentant after he hurt her feelings. = 他伤害了她的感情后感到悔恨。
They were repentant for their involvement in the crime. = 他们为参与犯罪而感到悔恨。


现在完成时:
He has been repentant since the accident. = 自事故发生以来，他一直很悔恨。
She has remained repentant throughout the trial. = 在整个审判过程中，她一直保持着悔恨的态度。


过去完成时:
He had been repentant for years before finally confessing. = 在最终认罪之前，他已经悔恨多年了。
They had been repentant for their actions long before the consequences. = 在后果出现很久之前，他们就为自己的行为感到悔恨了。


将来时:
He will be repentant when he sees the damage he's caused. = 当他看到自己造成的损害时，他会感到悔恨。
She will likely be repentant once she understands the full implications. = 一旦她理解了全部含义，她很可能会感到悔恨。


虚拟语气:
If he were repentant, he would apologize. = 如果他悔恨的话，他会道歉。
I wish he were more repentant for his actions. = 我希望他对自己的行为更悔恨一些。


被动语态:
His actions were viewed as repentant by many. = 许多人认为他的行为是悔恨的。
His words were interpreted as repentant. = 他的话被解读为悔恨的。


疑问句:

否定句:
He is not repentant for his actions. = 他对自己的行为并不悔恨。
She was not at all repentant for what she had done. = 她对自己所做的事情一点也不悔恨。
