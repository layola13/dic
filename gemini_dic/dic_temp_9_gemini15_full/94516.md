importunity:/ˌɪmpɔːrˈtjuːnɪti/| n. |恳求；强求；纠缠|Persistent and often unreasonable demands or requests.  It suggests a degree of pressure or insistence that may be unwelcome.|例句：He was worn down by the importunity of his creditors. 他被债主的纠缠弄得精疲力尽。|例句：Despite the importunity of her friends, she refused to change her mind. 尽管朋友们苦苦哀求，她还是拒绝改变主意。|近义词：insistence, persistence, nagging, pressure|反义词：acquiescence, compliance, yielding|名词
