maven:/ˈmeɪvən/ | n. |专家；内行；老手 |A maven is an expert or connoisseur, someone highly knowledgeable and skillful in a particular area.  They often have a deep understanding and practical experience, allowing them to offer insightful advice or guidance. |例句：He's a maven of modern art. 他是现代艺术专家。 |例句：She's a maven in the field of organic farming. 她在有机农业领域是专家。 |近义词：expert, authority, connoisseur, specialist |反义词：novice, amateur, beginner
