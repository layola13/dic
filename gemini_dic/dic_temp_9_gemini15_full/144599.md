outweaved:/ˌaʊtˈwiːvd/ | v. | 编织得更好，胜过 | To surpass or exceed in weaving or in something analogous to weaving, such as skill or intricacy.  | The master weaver outweaved all his apprentices.  大师的织造技艺超过了他所有的学徒。 | surpass, excel, outdo |  inferior, less skilled | 动词
