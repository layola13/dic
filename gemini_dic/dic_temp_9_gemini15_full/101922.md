island:/ˈaɪlənd/ | n. |岛屿，岛 | A piece of land surrounded by water.  | Example:  The island is famous for its beautiful beaches.  这个岛屿以其美丽的海滩而闻名。


Example: We spent a week on a tropical island. 我们在一个热带岛屿上度过了一周。|近义词：isle, islet |反义词：mainland, continent |名词
