elohim:/ˈɛləhaɪm/| n. |神；上帝 |The Hebrew word Elohim is a plural noun, but it is often used with singular verbs and refers to God.|例句：In the Bible, Elohim is often used to refer to God.  圣经中，Elohim 常用来指代上帝。|近义词：God, deity, divinity |反义词：none


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It doesn't have tenses or moods.  The usage depends on the context of the sentence.


PHRASE->
Elohim Adonai:  上帝耶和华 (God the Lord)
Elohim Chayim:  永生神 (the Living God)
Elohei Avraham:  亚伯拉罕的神 (God of Abraham)
Elohei Yisrael: 以色列的神 (God of Israel)
Benei Elohim: 神的儿子们 (sons of God)
