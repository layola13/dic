messing about:/ˈmɛsɪŋ əˈbaʊt/| v.|瞎弄；胡闹；消磨时间|To spend time in a relaxed way, often without a specific purpose; to idly play with or handle something; to playfully experiment or fool around.|例句：The children were messing about in the garden.孩子们在花园里瞎闹。 We spent the afternoon messing about on the beach. 我们在海滩上消磨了整个下午。|近义词： fooling around, playing around, larking about, pottering about|反义词：working hard, being productive
