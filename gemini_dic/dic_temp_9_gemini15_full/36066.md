coeditors:/ˌkoʊˈɛdɪtərz/| n. |合编者，共同编辑 |People who edit a publication or work together.  Coeditors share the responsibility of editing a book, journal, or other publication.  They may divide the work among themselves or collaborate closely on all aspects of the editing process.|例句：The coeditors worked collaboratively to ensure the book's consistency and accuracy.  合编者通力合作，确保书籍的一致性和准确性。|近义词：joint editors, collaborating editors |反义词：sole editor, single editor |名词
