ferried:/ˈfɛriːd/| v.|摆渡；运送；渡运|To transport people or goods across a body of water in a ferry.  It also refers to the act of transporting something, often in large quantities, from one place to another.|例句：The ferry ferried passengers across the river. 渡轮把乘客渡过河。

The company ferried supplies to the remote island. 公司把物资运送到偏远的岛屿。


近义词：transported, conveyed, carried|反义词：None directly, but words implying a lack of transportation could be considered antonyms.|动词

The ferry ferries passengers across the lake daily. = 渡轮每天都运送乘客横渡湖泊。
They ferry goods between the two cities. = 他们往返于两个城市之间运送货物。

被动语态:
