shakiness:/ˈʃeɪkinəs/| n. |颤抖；不稳定；摇晃；犹豫 |The state or quality of being shaky; instability or unsteadiness.  It can refer to physical trembling, instability of an object, or uncertainty in one's actions or decisions. |例句：His shakiness betrayed his nervousness. 他的颤抖暴露了他的紧张。

例句：The shakiness of the old bridge worried the drivers.  那座老桥的摇晃让司机们担心。


近义词：trembling, quivering, instability, wavering, uncertainty, hesitation

反义词：steadiness, stability, firmness, confidence, decisiveness

词性:名词
