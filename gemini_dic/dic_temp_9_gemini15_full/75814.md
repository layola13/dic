fumidity:/fjuːˈmɪdəti/ | n. | 闷热；潮湿；潮气 |  The state or quality of being fumid; excessive moisture or humidity, especially in the air, often accompanied by heat and stuffiness.  It implies a feeling of being close, stuffy, and often unpleasant due to excessive moisture and heat.  | 例句：The fumidity of the greenhouse made it difficult to breathe. 温室的闷热让人难以呼吸。

The fumidity in the jungle was oppressive.  丛林里的潮湿让人喘不过气。| 近义词：humidity, mugginess, dampness, stickiness | 反义词：dryness, aridity
