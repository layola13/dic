hi-res: /ˈhaɪrez/ | adj. | 高分辨率的 | High resolution; characterized by a high number of pixels, resulting in a sharp and detailed image or display.  | Example: The hi-res image showed every detail of the landscape.  高分辨率的图像显示了景观的每一个细节。 |近义词：high-resolution, sharp, detailed | 反义词：low-resolution, blurry, pixelated |
