comprehend:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛnd/ /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛnd/| vt.|理解，领会|To understand fully; grasp the meaning of.|例句：I finally comprehended the meaning of the poem. 我终于理解了这首诗的意思。
例句：She struggled to comprehend the complex instructions. 她努力理解这些复杂的指示。|近义词：understand, grasp, apprehend|反义词：misunderstand, misconceive|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I comprehend the situation now. = 我现在理解了情况。
We comprehend the importance of this project. = 我们理解这个项目的重要性。

一般过去时:
I comprehended the lesson after much thought. = 我经过一番思考后理解了这节课。
They comprehended the risks involved. = 他们理解了其中的风险。

现在完成时:
I have comprehended the main points of the lecture. = 我已经理解了讲座的主要内容。
She has comprehended the complexity of the problem. = 她已经理解了问题的复杂性。

过去完成时:
I had comprehended the instructions before starting the task. = 我在开始任务之前已经理解了指示。
They had comprehended the danger before it was too late. = 在为时已晚之前，他们已经理解了危险。

将来时:

We will comprehend the implications of this decision soon. = 我们很快就会理解这个决定的含义。

虚拟语气:

I wish I comprehended the instructions better. = 我希望我更好地理解了指示。

被动语态:
