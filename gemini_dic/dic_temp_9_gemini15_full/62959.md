ennoble:/ɪˈnəʊbl/| vt.|使高贵；提升；封爵|To raise someone to a higher rank or status, often by conferring a title of nobility; to elevate or improve the character or quality of something.|The king ennobled his loyal knight. 国王封他的忠诚骑士为贵族。  He ennobled his family name through his great achievements. 他通过伟大的成就提升了他的家族声誉。|elevate, exalt, dignify|debase, degrade, demean|动词
