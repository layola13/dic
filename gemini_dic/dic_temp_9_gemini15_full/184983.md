retelevised:/ˌriːˈtɛləvaɪzd/| v. | 重播，再次播放 | To broadcast (a television program) again.  This usually refers to a program that was previously broadcast, often at a different time or on a different channel. | Example: The documentary was retelevised last night on PBS.  纪录片昨晚在PBS电视台重播了。 The game was retelevised due to popular demand.  由于观众强烈要求，比赛进行了重播。|近义词：rebroadcast, replay, rerun |反义词：premiere, debut
