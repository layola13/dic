lawn:/lɔːn/| n.|草坪，草地|A piece of land covered with short, mown grass, especially one that is attached to a house.  |例句：We have a beautiful lawn in front of our house. 我们家前面有一个美丽的草坪。|例句：The children were playing frisbee on the lawn. 孩子们在草坪上玩飞盘。|近义词：turf, sward|反义词：desert, wasteland|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lawn needs mowing. = 草坪需要修剪。
They water the lawn every evening. = 他们每天晚上都给草坪浇水。

一般过去时:
We mowed the lawn yesterday. = 我们昨天修剪了草坪。
He played football on the lawn last Sunday. = 他上周日足球在草坪上玩足球。

现在完成时:
They have already cut the lawn. = 他们已经修剪了草坪。
We haven't fertilized the lawn this year. = 我们今年还没给草坪施肥。

过去完成时:
They had already mown the lawn before the party. = 他们在派对之前就已经修剪了草坪。
The children had played on the lawn all afternoon. = 孩子们整个下午都在草坪上玩耍。

将来时:
We will mow the lawn tomorrow. = 我们明天将修剪草坪。
They will plant new grass on the lawn next spring. = 他们明年春天将在草坪上种植新的草坪。

虚拟语气:
If we had a bigger lawn, we could have a swimming pool. = 如果我们有一个更大的草坪，我们就可以有一个游泳池。
I wish we had a greener lawn. = 我希望我们有一个更绿的草坪。

被动语态:

The lawn was damaged by the storm. = 草坪被风暴破坏了。

疑问句:

否定句:
