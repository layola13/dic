overassuredness: /ˌoʊvərəˈʃʊrdnəs/ | n. |过分自信；自负 | Excessive confidence; self-assurance to an excessive degree. It often implies a lack of awareness of potential risks or limitations.  |  His overassuredness led him to make risky investments that ultimately cost him a fortune. 他的过分自信导致他进行了风险投资，最终损失了一大笔钱。 |近义词：conceit, arrogance, presumption, self-importance |反义词：humility, modesty, diffidence, self-doubt |名词
