lycopode:/ˈlɪkəpoʊd/| n. |石松；卷柏；石松科植物|A lycopode refers to any plant belonging to the genus Lycopodium, a group of evergreen, vascular, herbaceous plants belonging to the Lycopodiaceae family.  These plants are often found in moist, shady habitats and are characterized by their small, scale-like leaves and sporangia that produce spores. They are sometimes used in various applications, including medicinal and horticultural purposes.  |Example: The forest floor was carpeted with a thick layer of lycopode.  森林地面覆盖着一层厚厚的石松。|近义词：club moss (常用名称)|反义词：无
