latentness:/ˈleɪtəntnəs/ | n. |潜伏性；潜在性；潜能 |The state of being latent; the condition of being hidden or unexpressed.  Latentness refers to something that exists but is not yet visible, active, or manifest. This could refer to a hidden potential, a dormant characteristic, or an unexpressed feeling or idea.  |Example: The latentness of the virus made it difficult to detect in its early stages.  病毒的潜伏期使得早期难以检测到它。|近义词：dormancy, inactivity, potential, hiddenness |反义词：manifestation, activity, expression, visibility |
