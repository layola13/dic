semantics: /sɪˈmæntiks/ | n. | 语义学 | The study of meaning in language, including the relationships between words, phrases, sentences, and their underlying concepts.  It explores how meaning is created, interpreted, and communicated. | Example:  The semantics of the poem are complex and open to multiple interpretations.  这首诗的语义复杂，有多种解读方式。 | 近义词：significance, meaning, interpretation | 反义词：N/A
