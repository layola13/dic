nonchalantly: /ˈnɒnʃələntli/| adv. | 冷淡地；漫不经心地；无所谓地 |  In a casual and unconcerned manner; indifferently.  | 例句：He nonchalantly tossed the letter into the wastebasket. 他漫不经心地把信扔进了废纸篓。

例句：She nonchalantly shrugged her shoulders. 她漫不经心地耸了耸肩。


近义词：casually, indifferently, unconcernedly, carelessly, apathetically

反义词：concernedly, anxiously, worriedly, earnestly, seriously
