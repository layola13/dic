gromwell:/ˈɡrʌm.wɛl/| n. |琉璃草属植物|A gromwell is any plant belonging to the genus Lithospermum in the borage family.  These plants are known for their rough, hairy leaves and small, usually blue or white, flowers. Some species have seeds that are used medicinally or as a dye. |The field was dotted with patches of gromwell, their tiny blue flowers a striking contrast to the green.  田野点缀着几块琉璃草，它们细小的蓝色花朵与绿色的田野形成鲜明的对比。|近义词：bugloss (某些种类)|反义词：无
