enigmatical: /ˌɛnɪɡˈmætɪkəl/ | adj. | 谜一样的，难以理解的 |  Mysterious and difficult to understand; puzzling. | 例句：Her enigmatic smile hinted at a secret she wouldn't reveal.  她的神秘微笑暗示着一个她不愿透露的秘密。

例句：The professor's enigmatic lecture left the students completely baffled. 教授那令人费解的演讲让学生们完全困惑不解。


近义词：puzzling, mysterious, cryptic, perplexing, obscure


反义词：clear, understandable, explicit, transparent


词性：形容词
