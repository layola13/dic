merriness:/ˈmɛrɪnɪs/ | n. |欢乐，愉快；嬉戏 |The feeling of being merry; gaiety.  It often involves laughter, cheerfulness, and a sense of fun and lightheartedness. |例句：The air was filled with the merriness of children playing.  孩子们嬉戏的欢乐充满了空气。

例句：Their merriness was infectious, and soon everyone was laughing. 他们的欢乐是具有感染力的，很快每个人都笑了。


近义词：gaiety, mirth, cheerfulness, jollity, hilarity |反义词：sadness, gloom, dejection, misery


词源:  From Middle English *merrinesse, from Old French *mérietés, from *mérier "to be merry". Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *marijaz "merry".
