councillor: /ˈkaʊnsələr/ | n. | 议员；顾问 | A member of a council, especially a local government council.  | Example: The councillor promised to address the residents' concerns about the new road construction.  这位议员承诺将解决居民对新道路建设的担忧。 |近义词：council member, representative, advisor | 反义词：constituent (选民，反义词较为宽泛，指与议员相对的选民群体)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The councillor represents the interests of her constituents. = 这位议员代表她选民的利益。
The councillors are currently discussing the new budget. = 议员们目前正在讨论新的预算。

一般过去时:
The councillor voted against the proposed development. = 这位议员投票反对拟议的开发项目。
The councillors met last week to discuss the issue. = 议员们上周会面讨论了这个问题。

现在完成时:
The councillor has served on the council for many years. = 这位议员在议会工作多年了。
The councillors have already approved the plan. = 议员们已经批准了该计划。

过去完成时:
The councillor had already spoken before the mayor arrived. = 市长到达之前，这位议员已经发言了。
The councillors had debated the issue extensively before reaching a decision. = 议员们在做出决定之前已经广泛地讨论了这个问题。

将来时:
The councillor will attend the next council meeting. = 这位议员将参加下一次议会会议。
The councillors will announce their decision next week. = 议员们将在下周宣布他们的决定。

虚拟语气:
If the councillor were to resign, there would be a by-election. = 如果这位议员辞职，将进行补选。
I wish the councillor had listened to our concerns. = 我希望这位议员能听取我们的意见。

被动语态:
The councillor's proposal was rejected by the council. = 议员的提案被议会否决了。
The issue is being considered by the councillors. = 这个问题正在由议员们考虑。

疑问句:
Is the councillor available for a meeting? = 议员有空参加会议吗？
What are the councillors' plans for the future? = 议员们未来的计划是什么？

否定句:
The councillor did not support the motion. = 这位议员不支持这项动议。
The councillors have not yet reached a consensus. = 议员们尚未达成共识。


PHRASE->
city councillor: 市议员
ward councillor: 选区议员
council councillor: 委员会委员 (council在此指代更广泛的委员会，而非特指地方议会)
local councillor: 地方议员
elected councillor: 民选议员
