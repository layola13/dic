like fun:/laɪk fʌn/| adj. phr.|开玩笑地；根本不；一点也不|This phrase is used informally to express disbelief, sarcasm, or strong negation. It implies that something is not true or will not happen.  |例句：He said he'd help, like fun. 他说他会帮忙，我才不信呢。|例句：She said she'd finish the project by Friday, like fun! 她说是周五之前完成项目，我才不信呢！|近义词：not at all, certainly not, absolutely not|反义词：certainly, definitely, absolutely
