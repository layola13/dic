methanol:/ˈmɛθənɔːl/ | n. | 甲醇 | Methanol is a highly flammable, colorless, poisonous liquid alcohol. It is the simplest alcohol, and is used as a solvent, antifreeze, and fuel. | Example:  Methanol is toxic if ingested.  甲醇摄入后有毒。 |近义词：methyl alcohol | 反义词：无
