regears:/ˌriːˈɡɪrz/| v.|重新调整齿轮；重新装备；改变策略|To change the gears of a machine, typically to optimize performance or adapt to a new situation.  It can also refer to a broader strategic shift.|例句：The mechanic had to regear the transmission to handle the increased power. 机械师不得不重新调整变速箱的齿轮以应对增加的动力。

The company decided to regear its marketing strategy after a disappointing sales quarter. 公司在销售季度令人失望后决定改变其营销策略。|近义词：refit, readjust, revamp, retool|反义词：leave as is, maintain status quo|动词
