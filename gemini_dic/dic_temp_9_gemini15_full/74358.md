freight:/freɪt/| n. |货物，运费 |Goods carried by a large vehicle such as a ship, train, or aircraft.  The cost of transporting goods. |The ship carries a large freight of grain. 这艘船装载着大量的谷物。 The freight charges were exorbitant. 运费非常高昂。|cargo, shipment, baggage |passenger | noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The freight train carries tons of coal. = 这列货运列车运载着数吨煤炭。
What is the freight cost for this shipment? = 这批货物的运费是多少？


一般过去时:
The company shipped the freight last week. = 公司上周运送了货物。
How much did the freight cost last month? = 上个月运费是多少？


现在完成时:
They have already paid the freight. = 他们已经支付了运费。
How much freight have we shipped this year? = 今年我们已经运送了多少货物？


过去完成时:
The freight had arrived before the warehouse opened. = 货物在仓库开门之前就已经到达了。
They had already calculated the freight cost before the shipment. = 他们在发货前就已经计算出运费了。


将来时:
We will send the freight tomorrow. = 我们明天将发送货物。
What will the freight charges be? = 运费将是多少？


虚拟语气:
If the freight were cheaper, we would order more. = 如果运费更便宜，我们会多订购一些。
I wish the freight had arrived earlier. = 我希望货物能早点到达。


被动语态:
