pica:/ˈpaɪkə/| n.|排印单位，活字大小，铅字|A pica is a unit of measurement used in printing and typography, typically equal to 1/6 of an inch or 12 points.  It refers to the size of type, the spacing between lines, or the dimensions of a page.|The brochure was designed using a 12-pica column width.|近义词：point, em|反义词：无
