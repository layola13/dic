euxanthin: /ˌjuːzænθɪn/ | n. | 黄连素 | A yellow crystalline glucoside found in various plants, especially in the bark of _genista tinctoria_ (dyer's greenweed) and used as a dye.  It is also a laxative. | Example: Euxanthin, extracted from the dyer's greenweed, was historically used to create yellow dyes. 黄连素，从黄栌提取，历史上被用来制作黄色染料。|近义词:  (as a dye) weld, fustic | 反义词: None specifically, but  compounds producing contrasting colors could be considered antonyms in a dyeing context. |名词
