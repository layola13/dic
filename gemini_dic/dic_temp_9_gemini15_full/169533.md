propene:/ˈproʊpiːn/ | n. |丙烯|Propene is a colorless gas with a faint petroleum-like odor. It's an unsaturated hydrocarbon with the chemical formula C₃H₆, containing a carbon-carbon double bond.  It's a crucial building block in the petrochemical industry, primarily used to produce polypropylene plastic. | Example: The polymerization of propene yields polypropylene, a widely used plastic.  丙烯的聚合反应生成聚丙烯，一种广泛使用的塑料。|近义词:propylene|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与饱和烃类对比，例如丙烷)|名词
