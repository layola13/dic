punishment: /ˈpʌnɪʃmənt/ | n. |惩罚，处罚 | The act of punishing someone for a wrongdoing; a penalty imposed for an offense.  |例句：He received a severe punishment for his crime. 他因为他的罪行受到了严厉的惩罚。

例句：The punishment for speeding is a fine. 超速的惩罚是罚款。


近义词：penalty, chastisement, retribution, discipline | 反义词：reward, recompense, prize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The punishment fits the crime. = 惩罚与罪行相符。
His punishment is imprisonment. = 他的惩罚是监禁。


一般过去时:
The judge handed down a harsh punishment. = 法官做出了严厉的惩罚。
They received punishment for their actions. = 他们因为他们的行为受到了惩罚。


现在完成时:
He has received punishment for his misconduct. = 他因行为不端受到了惩罚。
She has endured much punishment in her life. = 她一生中忍受了很多惩罚。


过去完成时:
He had already suffered punishment for his previous mistakes. = 他已经因为他之前的错误受到了惩罚。
They had never faced such a severe punishment before. = 他们以前从未面临过如此严厉的惩罚。


将来时:
He will face punishment for his actions. = 他将为他的行为受到惩罚。
They will receive the punishment they deserve. = 他们将受到应得的惩罚。


虚拟语气:
If he had confessed, his punishment might have been lighter. = 如果他认罪，他的惩罚可能会轻一些。
I wish I hadn't committed that crime; the punishment is unbearable. = 我真希望我没有犯下那罪行；惩罚难以忍受。


被动语态:
The criminal was given a severe punishment. = 罪犯受到了严厉的惩罚。
The punishment was deemed too harsh by many. = 许多人认为这种惩罚过于严厉。


疑问句:
What is the punishment for theft? = 盗窃的惩罚是什么？
What punishment do you think he deserves? = 你认为他应该受到什么惩罚？


否定句:
He did not receive the punishment he expected. = 他没有受到他预期的惩罚。
There was no punishment for his minor offense. = 他因轻微罪行没有受到惩罚。
