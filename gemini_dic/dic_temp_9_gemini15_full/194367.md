self-preparations:/selfˌprepəˈreɪʃənz/| n.| 自我准备；预先准备 | Preparations made by oneself, without external assistance.  It often refers to preparations for a specific event or task. | 例句：Her self-preparations for the exam included reviewing all her notes and practicing past papers.  她的考试自我准备包括复习所有笔记和练习往年试卷。

例句：The athlete's self-preparations were meticulous, ensuring he was in peak condition for the competition.  这位运动员的自我准备非常细致，确保他以最佳状态参加比赛。


近义词： preparations, prearrangements, advance planning


反义词：improvisation, spontaneity


词性: 名词
