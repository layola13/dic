custom:/ˈkʌstəm/| /ˈkʌstəm/| n., adj., v.| 习惯；风俗；海关；定制；定做 |  a usual way of behaving or doing something; the practice of a particular place or group of people; the department of government that collects taxes on imported goods; to make or alter something according to a customer's special requirements | 例句：It's a local custom to give gifts at New Year. = 新年前送礼物是当地的习俗。  The tailor customized my suit. = 裁缝师为我定制了西装。 | 近义词：habit, tradition, practice, tailor-made | 反义词：standard, usual, ordinary
