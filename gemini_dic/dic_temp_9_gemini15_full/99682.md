interlinks:/ˈɪntərˌlɪŋks/| n. |互联，相互关联|Interlinks refer to connections or relationships between different things, often implying a network or system of interconnected elements.  The term often suggests a complex web of relationships, rather than simple, individual links.|例句：The research highlighted the interlinks between climate change and social inequality.  这项研究强调了气候变化与社会不平等之间的相互关联。


例句：The company's success depends on the strong interlinks between its various departments. 公司的成功取决于其各个部门之间的紧密联系。


近义词：connections, relationships, linkages, interconnections, networks|反义词：disconnections, separations, isolations|名词
