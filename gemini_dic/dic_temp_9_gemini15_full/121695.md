mispracticing:/ˌmɪsˈpræktɪsɪŋ/| v.|不正当执业，渎职，误用|The act of engaging in professional practice in a way that is wrong, illegal, or unethical; often involving negligence, incompetence, or fraudulent behavior. It often refers to professionals like doctors, lawyers, or accountants.|例句：The doctor was accused of mispracticing and losing his license. 这名医生被指控渎职并被吊销执照。

例句：The lawyer was found guilty of mispracticing by using falsified evidence in court. 这名律师因在法庭上使用伪造证据而被判犯有渎职罪。


近义词：malpractice, misconduct, wrongdoing, unprofessional conduct|反义词：professionalism, ethical practice, competent practice
