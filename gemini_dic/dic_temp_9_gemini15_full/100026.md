interpervaded:/ˌɪntər.pərˈveɪd/ | v. | 弥漫；渗透；充满 | To spread throughout and penetrate every part of something.  | The sweet scent of honeysuckle interpervaded the garden.  甜美的金银花香味弥漫在花园里。The feeling of excitement interpervaded the whole team.  兴奋的情绪感染了整个团队。| permeate, saturate, imbue | isolate, exclude | 动词

词源:
