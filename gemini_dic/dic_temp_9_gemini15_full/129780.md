non-feudal: /ˌnɒnˈfjuːdəl/ | adj. | 非封建的 | Not relating to or characteristic of feudalism.  This adjective describes systems, societies, or relationships that are not organized according to the principles of feudalism, which typically involves a hierarchical structure based on land ownership and loyalty. |


例句：

The country adopted a non-feudal system of land ownership after the revolution. = 革命后，这个国家采用了非封建的土地所有制。

His non-feudal approach to management surprised his colleagues. = 他非封建的管理方法令他的同事们感到惊讶。


近义词：anti-feudal, unfeudal (less common)


反义词：feudal


词性: 形容词
