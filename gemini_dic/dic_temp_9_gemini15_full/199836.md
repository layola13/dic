sitcoms:/ˈsɪtkɑːmz/| n. |情景喜剧|Situation comedies, abbreviated as sitcoms, are television or radio comedy shows that typically feature a regular cast of characters and recurring settings.  They often rely on humor derived from everyday situations and character interactions.  Sitcoms usually have a multi-camera setup and a laugh track.|Example:  "Friends" is one of the most popular sitcoms of all time.  “老友记”是历史上最受欢迎的情景喜剧之一。|近义词：comedy series, TV comedy|反义词：drama, tragedy
