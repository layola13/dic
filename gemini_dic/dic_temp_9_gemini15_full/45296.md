culverin:/ˈkʌlvərɪn/| n.|轻型火炮，轻型加农炮|A culverin was a type of long, slender cannon used in the 15th to 17th centuries.  It was characterized by its relatively long barrel and smaller bore compared to other cannons of the time, allowing for a longer range and greater accuracy.  |例句：The army deployed several culverins to bombard the enemy's fortifications.  军队部署了几门轻型火炮来轰击敌人的防御工事。

The ship's culverins were loaded and ready to fire. 这艘船的轻型火炮已经装填完毕，随时可以发射。|近义词：cannon, field gun|反义词：mortar (in terms of projectile trajectory)
