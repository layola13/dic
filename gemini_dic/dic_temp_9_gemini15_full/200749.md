slender: /ˈslɛndər/ | adj. |纤细的；苗条的；微薄的；瘦长的 |

英文解析：Slender means having a thin, delicate, or graceful shape or form.  It can refer to physical build (a slender woman),  objects (a slender tree), or amounts (slender resources).

例句：

She has a slender figure. = 她身材苗条。

The slender spire of the church reached towards the sky. = 教堂细长的尖顶直指天空。


近义词：thin, slim, slight, delicate, frail, lean

反义词：thick, stout, bulky, robust, corpulent


词源:

The word "slender" comes from the Old English word "slēndre," which itself is likely derived from a Proto-Germanic root.  The meaning has remained relatively consistent over time, referring to a thin and graceful form.
