heads-off:/hedzˈɔːf/| adj.|提前的，预先的；已完成的|  Describes something that is done in advance or before something else happens, or that is already completed.  It implies preparation or proactive action.| 例句：The heads-off meeting ensured we were all on the same page before the main presentation.提前的会议确保我们在主要演示之前达成共识。
