outwalk:/ˌaʊtˈwɔːk/| vt.|超过…的步行速度；比…走得远|To walk farther than or faster than.  It implies surpassing someone in a walking competition or exceeding a certain distance.|例句：He outwalked me easily. 他轻而易举地超过了我。

例句：I outwalked my friend on our hike yesterday. 昨天徒步旅行时，我比我的朋友走得更远。


近义词：outspeed, outpace, exceed|反义词：be outwalked by, be surpassed by|动词
