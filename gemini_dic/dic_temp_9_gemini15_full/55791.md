diverticle:/ˈdaɪvɜːrtɪkl/| n.|憩室；小袋|A diverticle is a small pouch or sac that develops from the wall of a tube or organ, such as the intestine or bladder.  Diverticula are often asymptomatic, but can cause pain, bleeding, or infection if they become inflamed or infected (diverticulitis).|Example:  He was diagnosed with a diverticle in his colon. 他被诊断出患有结肠憩室。|近义词：pouch, sac |反义词：N/A
