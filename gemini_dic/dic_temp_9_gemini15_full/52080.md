dialysis:/daɪˈælɪsɪs/| n. |透析；渗析|The process of separating substances, especially the removal of waste products from the blood by a machine.  Dialysis is a life-saving treatment for people with kidney failure.|例句：He requires regular dialysis to stay alive. 他需要定期透析才能活下去。

例句：The scientist used dialysis to separate the proteins from the solution. 科学家用透析法将蛋白质从溶液中分离出来。|近义词：filtration, purification|反义词：synthesis, combination|词性：名词
