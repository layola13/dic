misalphabetizes: /ˌmɪsælˈfæbəˌtaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 拼错字母顺序 | To arrange alphabetically incorrectly.  This word implies a mistake in the ordering, not just a simple misspelling of individual words. | 例句：He misalphabetizes the entries in his index regularly. 他经常把索引中的条目字母顺序排错。

The librarian misalphabetizes the books on the shelf. 图书管理员把书架上的书字母顺序排错了。


近义词：misorder, alphabetize incorrectly, jumble (in alphabetical context) | 反义词：alphabetize correctly, arrange alphabetically
