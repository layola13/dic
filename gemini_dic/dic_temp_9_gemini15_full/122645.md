modifying:/ˈmɒdɪfaɪɪŋ/ /ˈmɑːdɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 修改；更改；修饰 | Modifying means to change something, often in a small way, to improve it or make it more suitable.  It can also refer to adding descriptive words to enhance a noun. |


例句：

He's modifying the design to make it more user-friendly. = 他正在修改设计，使其更方便用户使用。

The artist is modifying the painting with a few extra brushstrokes. = 艺术家正在用几笔额外的笔触修改这幅画。


近义词：altering, changing, adjusting, amending, adapting


反义词：maintaining, preserving, leaving unchanged


词性：动词
