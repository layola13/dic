clerestoried:/ˈklɛrɪstɔːrid/| adj.| 有挑高窗的；带高窗的 | Having clerestory windows.  A clerestory is a high section of wall, typically in a church or other large building, that contains windows placed above the roofline of a lower section.  This allows light to enter the building's interior. | The cathedral's clerestoried nave was bathed in sunlight. 这座大教堂带有高窗的中殿沐浴在阳光中。| 近义词：high-windowed,  windowed | 反义词：windowless
