lodgement:/ˈlɒdʒmənt/ /ˈlɑːdʒmənt/| n. |住所；寄宿；存放；安置；提出（申请、抗议等）|The act of lodging or the state of being lodged;  a temporary place to stay; the act of placing something somewhere; the act of formally presenting something (e.g., a claim, complaint).|例句：He found temporary lodging in a small hotel. 他在一间小旅馆找到了临时住所。

例句：The company lodged a formal complaint about the unfair treatment.  公司就这种不公平待遇提出了正式投诉。


近义词：accommodation, lodging, quarters, residence, submission


反义词：eviction, removal


词性：名词
