recompensating:/ˌrɛkəmˈpɛnseɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 补偿；报答；酬谢 |  The act of giving something to someone in return for something else, usually to make up for a loss or service.  It implies making amends or providing equivalent value. |例句：The company is recompensing its employees for their hard work with a bonus. 公司正在以奖金来补偿员工的辛勤工作。

例句：She recompensed him for his kindness with a heartfelt thank you. 她以衷心的感谢来报答他的好意。


近义词：compensating, rewarding, remunerating, making amends for


反义词：punishing, penalizing


词性：动词
