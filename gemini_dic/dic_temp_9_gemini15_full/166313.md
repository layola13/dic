prerefinements:/ˌpriːrɪˈfaɪnmənts/| n.| 预先改进，初步改进 | Preliminary improvements or refinements made before a final version or product is created.  These are often preparatory steps taken to improve the quality or efficiency of a subsequent process. | Example: The prerefinements to the software included bug fixes and performance enhancements.  预先对软件的改进包括错误修复和性能增强。|近义词：preliminary improvements, initial refinements, preparatory adjustments|反义词：final touches, post-processing
