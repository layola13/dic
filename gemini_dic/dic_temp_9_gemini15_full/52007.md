diagonal:/daɪˈæɡənəl/ | adj., n. | 对角线的；斜线的；对角线 |  An extending from one corner to the opposite corner of a square, rectangle, or other polygon.  A diagonal line or direction. |例句：The diagonal across the square is longer than its sides.  正方形的对角线比它的边长。

The painting features bold diagonal stripes. 这幅画以醒目的斜条纹为特色。


近义词：slanting, oblique, crosswise | 反义词：straight, perpendicular | 词性：形容词，名词
