/dʌnt/| int.|  不，别|A contraction of "don't," used informally to express refusal, disagreement, or objection.  It is primarily used in spoken English and is considered non-standard.|

例句：

"Dunt touch that!" = 别碰那个！

"Dunt you worry." = 别担心。


近义词：don't, nope, no

反义词：yes, okay, alright

词性：interjection (感叹词)
