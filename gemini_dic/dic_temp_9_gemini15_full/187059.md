rived:/raɪvd/| adj.|已剥夺的；被夺去的；被剥夺权利的|Derived from "rive," meaning "to tear or split," "rived" describes something that has been forcefully separated or deprived.  It often implies a violent or forceful removal. |The rived branches lay scattered across the forest floor.  = 被撕裂的树枝散落在森林地面上。 The rived community mourned the loss of their leader. =  被剥夺权利的社区哀悼着他们领导人的逝去。|deprived, bereft,  devested|enriched, endowed, granted|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The strong wind rives the old tree apart. = 强风把老树撕裂了。
This law rives citizens of their basic rights. = 这项法律剥夺了公民的基本权利。


一般过去时:
The storm rived the roof from the house. = 暴风雨把房顶掀掉了。
The earthquake rived the land, creating deep fissures. = 地震撕裂了土地，形成了深深的裂缝。


现在完成时:
The years have rived their relationship beyond repair. = 岁月已将他们的关系撕裂得无法修复。
The constant pressure has rived the company of its profits. =  持续的压力已经使公司蒙受了损失。


过去完成时:
The land had been rived by centuries of erosion. = 这块土地已经被几个世纪的侵蚀所撕裂。
Before the war, the country had been rived by internal conflict. = 在战争之前，这个国家已经被内部冲突撕裂了。


将来时:
The coming storm will rive the coastlines. = 即将来临的风暴将撕裂海岸线。
The new policies may rive the community further apart. = 新的政策可能会进一步撕裂这个社区。


虚拟语气:
If the wind were stronger, it would rive the sail. = 如果风再强一些，它就会撕裂帆。
If I had known the truth, it would have rived my heart. = 如果我知道真相，它会让我心碎。


被动语态:
The land was rived by the earthquake. = 这块土地被地震撕裂了。
His reputation was rived by the scandal. = 他的名誉被丑闻毁了。


疑问句:
Was the ship rived by the waves? = 船是被海浪撕裂的吗？
Has the community been rived by conflict? = 这个社区是被冲突撕裂了吗？


否定句:
The storm did not rive the building. = 暴风雨没有损坏建筑物。
The argument did not rive their friendship. = 争吵并没有破坏他们的友谊。


PHRASE->
rived apart: 撕裂开来
rived with:  充满（负面情绪）
rived from: 源于，来自 (though this is more commonly associated with the verb "derive")
rived by: 被...撕裂
rived asunder: 完全撕裂
