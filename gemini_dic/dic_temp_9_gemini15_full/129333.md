noiselessnesses:/ˈnɔɪzləsnəsəz/ | n. |无声状态，寂静状态 |The plural form of noiselessness, referring to multiple instances or types of silence or absence of noise.  It implies a collection of quiet moments or situations.  The word is relatively uncommon in everyday speech. |Example: The noiselessnesses of the deep forest were broken only by the occasional chirp of a cricket. 深林的寂静状态只被偶尔的蟋蟀鸣叫声打破。|近义词: silences, quietudes, calms |反义词: noises, clamors, uproars |名词
