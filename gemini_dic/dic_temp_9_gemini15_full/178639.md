recrated:/rɪˈkreɪtɪd/ | v. | 再创造，重新创造；娱乐；消遣 | To create something again; to re-make something; to provide recreation or amusement. |例句：The artist recreated the masterpiece using new materials. 艺术家用新材料重新创作了这幅杰作。 The park offers many opportunities for recreation. 公园提供了许多娱乐机会。 |近义词：reproduce, remake, regenerate, entertain |反义词：destroy, ruin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recreates the painting every year. = 他每年都重画这幅画。
They recreate memories through old photos. = 他们通过旧照片重温回忆。

一般过去时:
She recreated the recipe from her grandmother. = 她根据祖母的食谱重新做了一道菜。
