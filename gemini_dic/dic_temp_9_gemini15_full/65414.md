例句：The euphonious music soothed my anxieties.  悦耳的音乐抚慰了我的焦虑。


近义词：melodious, harmonious, musical, sweet-sounding

反义词：cacophonous, dissonant, harsh, jarring


词源:
From Middle French *euphonique, from Latin euphōnicus, from Greek euphōnos “well-sounding,” from eu- “well, good” + phōnē “voice, sound.”
