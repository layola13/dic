outcrafty:/ˌaʊtˈkræfti/| vt.|比…更狡猾，智胜|To surpass in craftiness or cunning; to outwit.|例句：The fox outcrafty the hunter by leading him on a wild goose chase.  狐狸通过引诱猎人进行徒劳无功的追捕而智胜了他。

例句：The clever salesperson outcrafty the customer into buying a more expensive model.  这位精明的销售员巧妙地诱导顾客购买了更昂贵的型号。


近义词：outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit,  overreach


反义词：be outdone, be tricked, be surpassed


词性：动词
