negation:/nɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |否定；否认；取消|The act of denying or contradicting something; the state of being denied or contradicted.  It can also refer to the logical opposite of an assertion. |例句：The negation of his statement was evident in his body language.  他的肢体语言明显否定了他的说法。|例句：The negation of the treaty led to increased tensions between the two countries.  条约的废除导致两国之间的紧张关系加剧。|近义词：denial, refutation, contradiction, repudiation |反义词：affirmation, confirmation, assertion |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He denies the accusation. = 他否认了指控。
She negates the possibility of failure. = 她否定了失败的可能性。


一般过去时:
They negated their previous agreement. = 他们否定了之前的协议。
He denied having seen the suspect. = 他否认见过嫌疑犯。


现在完成时:
She has negated all the evidence. = 她已经否定了所有证据。
They have denied any involvement in the crime. = 他们否认参与了犯罪。


过去完成时:
He had negated the impact of the study. = 他已经否定了这项研究的影响。
She had denied knowing anything about it. = 她否认对此事有任何了解。


将来时:
They will negate the effects of the poison. = 他们将会消除毒药的影响。
He will deny any responsibility for the accident. = 他将否认对事故的任何责任。


虚拟语气:
If he negated his statement, the court would dismiss the case. = 如果他否认他的说法，法院就会驳回此案。
I wish she hadn't negated my efforts. = 我真希望她没有否定我的努力。


被动语态:
His claims were negated by new evidence. = 他的说法被新的证据否定了。
The treaty was negated by the opposing party. = 条约被对方否决了。


疑问句:
Does he negate the findings of the report? = 他是否否认了报告的调查结果？
Does she deny the allegations? = 她是否否认这些指控？


否定句:
He did not negate the importance of the issue. = 他没有否定这个问题的重要性。
She didn't deny her participation in the project. = 她没有否认她参与了这个项目。
