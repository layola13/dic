metaphysic:/ˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪk/| n. |形而上学，形而上学的观点|The branch of philosophy that deals with the first principles of things, including abstract concepts such as being, knowing, identity, time, and space.  It seeks to understand the fundamental nature of reality. |Example:  His lectures delve into complex areas of metaphysics. 他的演讲深入探讨了形而上学领域的复杂问题。|近义词：ontology, cosmology|反义词：physics, empiricism
