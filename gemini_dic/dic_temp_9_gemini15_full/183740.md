reschedule:/ˌriːˈskedʒuːl/| vt.|重新安排时间；改期|To change the planned time of something.|The meeting has been rescheduled for next Tuesday.  会议已重新安排到下周二举行。|We had to reschedule our trip due to the storm. 由于暴风雨，我们不得不改期旅行。|postpone, delay|cancel, keep


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They reschedule the meeting every week. = 他们每周都重新安排会议时间。
We reschedule appointments as needed. = 我们根据需要重新安排预约。

一般过去时:
She rescheduled her flight to avoid the bad weather. = 她重新安排了航班以避开恶劣天气。
He rescheduled the concert due to illness. = 由于生病，他重新安排了音乐会的时间。

现在完成时:
They have rescheduled the conference three times. = 他们已经三次重新安排了会议的时间。
We have rescheduled our vacation plans. = 我们已经重新安排了我们的假期计划。

过去完成时:
They had rescheduled the project deadline before the announcement. = 他们在公告之前已经重新安排了项目的截止日期。
She had rescheduled the interview several times before finally meeting the candidate. = 在最终见到候选人之前，她已经几次重新安排了面试时间。

将来时:
We will reschedule the game if it rains. = 如果下雨，我们将重新安排比赛。
They will reschedule the launch date if necessary. = 如果必要，他们将重新安排发布日期。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would reschedule the appointment. = 如果我是你，我会重新安排预约。
I wish I had rescheduled my trip earlier. = 我希望我早点重新安排我的旅行。


被动语态:
The meeting was rescheduled due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，会议被重新安排了。
The flight has been rescheduled for tomorrow morning. = 航班已被重新安排到明天早上。

疑问句:
Has the meeting been rescheduled? = 会议重新安排了吗？
When will the concert be rescheduled? = 音乐会什么时候重新安排？

否定句:
We did not reschedule the presentation. = 我们没有重新安排演示文稿。
They couldn't reschedule the wedding. = 他们无法重新安排婚礼。
