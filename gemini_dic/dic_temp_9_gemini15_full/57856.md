drest:/drɛst/| adj.|穿着整齐的；打扮好的|dressed 的过去式和过去分词形式，表示穿着衣服的状态或已经打扮好。|例句：She was drest in a beautiful gown. 她穿着美丽的长袍。|近义词：attired, arrayed, clad|反义词：undressed, naked


USAGE->
This word is the past participle of "dress,"  and therefore doesn't have its own tenses. It functions as an adjective.  The tenses are expressed through the verb "dress".

一般现在时:
She dresses elegantly. = 她穿着优雅。
They dress formally for the event. = 他们为这个活动穿着正式。

一般过去时:
She dressed quickly. = 她迅速地穿好衣服。
He dressed the wound carefully. = 他小心地包扎伤口。

现在完成时:
She has dressed the children for school. = 她已经为孩子们准备好上学穿的衣服。
He has dressed for the occasion. = 他已经为这次场合打扮好了。

过去完成时:
She had dressed before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经穿好衣服了。
They had dressed the doll beautifully. = 他们已经把洋娃娃打扮得很漂亮。

将来时:
She will dress warmly for the trip. = 她将为这次旅行穿暖和的衣服。
They will dress up for the party. = 他们将盛装出席派对。

虚拟语气:
If she were to dress more professionally, she might get the job. = 如果她穿得更专业些，她可能会得到这份工作。
I wish I had dressed more comfortably. = 我真希望我穿得更舒服些。

被动语态:
The wound was dressed by the doctor. = 伤口被医生包扎好了。
The children were dressed in matching outfits. = 孩子们穿着配套的衣服。

疑问句:
Is she dressed for the occasion? = 她为这次场合穿好衣服了吗？
Are they dressed appropriately? = 他们穿着得体吗？

否定句:
She was not dressed for the cold weather. = 她没有为寒冷的天气穿衣服。
They are not dressed properly for the meeting. = 他们穿着不适合参加会议。


PHRASE->

dressed for success: 穿着得体以获得成功
well-dressed: 衣着讲究的
simply dressed: 穿着简单的
