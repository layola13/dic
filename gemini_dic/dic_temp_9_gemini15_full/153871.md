perciform:/ˈpɜːrsɪfɔːrm/| adj.|鲈形目的；似鲈形的|Belonging to or characteristic of the Perciformes, the largest order of fishes, including perches, basses, jacks, and many others.  The term often describes fish with spiny dorsal fins and usually a single continuous dorsal fin.|例句：Many commercially important fish species are perciform.  许多重要的商业鱼类都属于鲈形目。

例句：The perciform fish showed a distinctive spiny dorsal fin. 这条鲈形目的鱼展现出独特的棘状背鳍。
