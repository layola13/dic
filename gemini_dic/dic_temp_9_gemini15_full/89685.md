hoosier:/ˈhuːzər/| n. adj.|印第安纳州居民；印第安纳州的；|A Hoosier is a resident of the U.S. state of Indiana. The term can also be used as an adjective to describe things related to Indiana.  The origin of the word is uncertain, but several theories exist.|例句：He's a Hoosier through and through. 他彻头彻尾是个印第安纳州人。  That's a genuine Hoosier pie. 那是地道的印第安纳派。|近义词：Indianan (formal)|反义词：无
