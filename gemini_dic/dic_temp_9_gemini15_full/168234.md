pro-Greek: /ˌproʊˈɡriːk/ | adj. | 亲希腊的；支持希腊的 | Favoring or supporting Greece or the Greeks.  This term usually refers to political or cultural affiliations. | Example:  The pro-Greek sentiment in the region was strong following the war.  这个地区在战争结束后对希腊的同情情绪很强烈。 | 近义词: philhellene,  Greek-supporting | 反义词: anti-Greek


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pro-Greek in his political views. = 他的政治观点是亲希腊的。
She shows pro-Greek sentiments in her writings. = 她的著作表达了亲希腊的感情。


一般过去时:
Many were pro-Greek during the independence movement. = 在独立运动期间，许多人支持希腊。
The newspaper maintained a pro-Greek stance throughout the conflict. = 在整个冲突期间，该报都保持着亲希腊的立场。


现在完成时:
The organization has been pro-Greek for decades. = 这个组织几十年来一直支持希腊。
He has consistently shown himself to be pro-Greek. = 他一直表现出自己是亲希腊的。


过去完成时:
Before the war, the country had been largely pro-Greek. = 在战争之前，这个国家在很大程度上是亲希腊的。
They had already established themselves as pro-Greek advocates. = 他们早已确立了自己作为亲希腊倡导者的地位。


将来时:
He will likely remain pro-Greek throughout his career. = 他可能在他的职业生涯中始终保持亲希腊的立场。
The public opinion will probably shift to become more pro-Greek. = 公众舆论可能会转向更加亲希腊。


虚拟语气:
If he were pro-Greek, he would have supported the initiative. = 如果他是亲希腊的，他会支持这项倡议。
I wish the government were more pro-Greek in its foreign policy. = 我希望政府的外交政策更亲希腊一些。


被动语态:
The candidate was portrayed as being pro-Greek in the media. = 候选人在媒体上被描绘成亲希腊的。
His pro-Greek stance was criticized by some. = 他亲希腊的立场受到了一些人的批评。


疑问句:
Is he pro-Greek or pro-Turkish? = 他是亲希腊的还是亲土耳其的？
Are you pro-Greek in this matter? = 在这件事上你是亲希腊的吗？


否定句:
He is not pro-Greek; he's actually anti-Greek. = 他不是亲希腊的；实际上他是反希腊的。
The article was not explicitly pro-Greek, but rather neutral. = 这篇文章并非明确地亲希腊，而是比较中立。
