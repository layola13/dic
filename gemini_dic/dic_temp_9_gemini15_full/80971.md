gracillent:/ɡrəˈsɪlənt/| adj.|纤细的，瘦弱的，优雅的 | slender and delicate, especially in a graceful way.  The word often implies a pleasing or elegant thinness.|例句：The ballerina possessed a gracillent figure, perfectly suited to her art.  这位芭蕾舞演员拥有纤细优雅的身材，非常适合她的艺术表演。

例句：The gracillent branches of the willow tree swayed gently in the breeze. 柳树纤细的枝条在微风中轻轻摇曳。


近义词：slender, slim, delicate, fragile, dainty, willowy

反义词：stout, robust, thickset, corpulent, bulky


词源：源自拉丁语 *gracilis（纤细的）+ -lent (形容词后缀)
