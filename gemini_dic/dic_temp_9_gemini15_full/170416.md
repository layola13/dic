prowlingly:/ˈpraʊlɪŋli/| adv.|潜行地；偷偷摸摸地；徘徊地|In a stealthy or predatory manner, as if stalking prey.  It suggests a quiet, deliberate movement with a sense of menace or anticipation. |例句：The cat prowled through the undergrowth. 猫在灌木丛中潜行。 The thief prowled the streets looking for an unlocked car. 小偷在街上徘徊寻找没锁的车。|近义词：stealthily, furtively, slinkily, sneakily|反义词：openly, boldly, directly
