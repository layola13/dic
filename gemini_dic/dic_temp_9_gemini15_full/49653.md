demining:/diːˈmaɪnɪŋ/| n.|排雷；清除地雷|The process of removing landmines from an area.  Demining is a dangerous and painstaking process, often requiring specialized equipment and trained personnel.|例句：The army is conducting demining operations in the former war zone.  军队正在前战区进行排雷作业。
例句：The success of the demining program depends heavily on international funding. 排雷项目的成功很大程度上取决于国际资金。|近义词：mine clearance, landmine removal|反义词：mining (埋设地雷)|名词
