insatiateness:/ˌɪnˈseɪʃiətnis/| n. |不知足；贪得无厌；贪婪 |The state or quality of being insatiate; inability to be satisfied.  It often refers to an unquenchable desire or appetite, especially for something non-physical like power or knowledge. |例句：His insatiateness for power led to his downfall. 他的权力欲导致了他的垮台。

例句：Her insatiateness for knowledge drove her to pursue advanced degrees. 她对知识的渴望驱使她去攻读高级学位。


近义词：insatiability, greed, avarice, voracity


反义词：contentment, satisfaction, satiety


词性: 名词
