drippings:/ˈdrɪpɪŋz/| n.|滴下的油脂；肉汁|The juices and fats that drip from meat during cooking.  These flavorful liquids are often used to make gravy or sauces.|例句：The roast chicken drippings were used to make a rich gravy. 烤鸡滴下的油脂被用来制作浓稠的肉汁。

例句：We collected the drippings from the turkey to make stuffing. 我们收集了火鸡滴下的油脂来制作馅料。


近义词：juices, gravy (when made into a sauce)
反义词：无


词性:名词
