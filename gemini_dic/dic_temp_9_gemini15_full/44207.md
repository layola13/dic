croche:/krəˈʃeɪ/|/ˈkrɔːʃeɪ/| n. v.|钩针编织；钩针编织物|The art or process of creating fabric from yarn using a hooked needle.  Crochet is a popular craft used to create a wide variety of items, from clothing and blankets to decorative items.|例句：She learned to crochet from her grandmother. 她从她祖母那里学会了钩针编织。

例句：The scarf was made using delicate crochet stitches. 这条围巾是用精细的钩针针法编织的。


近义词：knitting,needlework|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She crochets every evening. = 她每天晚上都钩针编织。
He crochets beautiful sweaters. = 他钩织漂亮的毛衣。

一般过去时:
I crocheted a blanket last winter. = 我去年冬天钩织了一条毯子。
They crocheted a hat for the baby. = 他们为婴儿钩织了一顶帽子。

现在完成时:
She has crocheted many scarves this year. = 她今年已经钩织了很多围巾。
He has crocheted a beautiful tapestry. = 他已经钩织了一幅美丽的挂毯。

过去完成时:
I had crocheted the entire blanket before she arrived. = 在她到达之前我已经钩织好了整条毯子。
They had crocheted several items for the charity bazaar. = 他们已经为慈善义卖钩织了几件物品。

将来时:

She will crochet a shawl for the winter. = 她将为冬天钩织一条披肩。

虚拟语气:

I wish I could crochet as well as my aunt. = 我希望我能像我阿姨那样擅长钩针编织。
