redrooping:/ˌrɛdˈruːpɪŋ/ | adj. |下垂的，萎靡的 | drooping or wilting, especially due to lack of water or excessive heat;  suggesting sadness or dejection.  |例句：The redrooping flowers needed immediate watering.  垂死的花需要立即浇水。 The redrooping branches suggested the tree was dying.  下垂的树枝表明这棵树快死了。|近义词：drooping, wilting, sagging, pendulous |反义词：erect, upright, perky
