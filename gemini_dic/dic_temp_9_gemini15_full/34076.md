civilised: /ˈsɪvəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 文明的；开化的；有教养的 |

英文解析:  Having a high level of cultural and technological development; showing good manners and respect for others.  Characterized by politeness, refinement, and good manners.


例句:

Many consider the invention of the printing press a significant step in the civilised world.  许多人认为印刷术的发明是文明世界的重要一步。

She behaved in a civilised manner throughout the difficult meeting. 在整个艰难的会议中，她表现得很有礼貌。


近义词: cultured, refined, sophisticated, polite, educated, advanced

反义词: barbaric, savage, uncultured, uncouth, primitive, undeveloped


词性:形容词
