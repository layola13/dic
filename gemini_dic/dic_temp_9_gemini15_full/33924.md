cirrhiferous:/sɪˈrɪfərəs/| adj.| 有卷须的；具卷须的 | Bearing or producing cirrhi (tendrils).  The term describes plants or other organisms that possess cirrhi, which are slender, thread-like appendages used for clinging or climbing. | Example: The cirrhiferous plant climbed the wall using its numerous tendrils.  =  这种有卷须的植物用它众多的卷须攀爬墙壁。|近义词：tendril-bearing,climbing |反义词：acirrhous
