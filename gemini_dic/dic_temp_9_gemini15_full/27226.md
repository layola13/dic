callyciflorous: /ˌkælɪsɪˈflɔːrəs/ | adj. |  花冠类,  合瓣花植物的 | Having the flowers with petals united, forming a corolla.  The term refers to a subclass of dicotyledonous plants characterized by having petals that are fused together to form a corolla. This is in contrast to polypetalous flowers, where the petals are separate. | Example:  Many calyciflorous plants are found in tropical regions.  许多合瓣花植物生长在热带地区。 | 近义词: gamopetalous | 反义词: polypetalous
