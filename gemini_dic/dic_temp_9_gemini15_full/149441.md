pacts:/pækts/| n.|条约，协定|A pact is a formal agreement between two or more individuals or groups.  It often implies a secret or informal agreement.|The two countries signed a pact to cooperate on economic development.  两个国家签署了一项关于经济发展的合作条约。|agreement, treaty, covenant|war, conflict|名词

一般现在时:
The countries maintain a peaceful pact. = 各国保持和平条约。
This pact benefits both sides. = 这项条约对双方都有利。

一般过去时:
They signed a non-aggression pact. = 他们签署了互不侵犯条约。
The pact ended the war. = 该条约结束了战争。

现在完成时:
They have broken the pact. = 他们违背了条约。
We have adhered to the pact. = 我们一直遵守该条约。
