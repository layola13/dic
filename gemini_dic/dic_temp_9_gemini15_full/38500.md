concierges:/kɒnˈsɪəʒz/ | n. | 门房；看门人；礼宾员 | A concierge is a person who works in a hotel or apartment building and provides services to guests or residents, such as making reservations, giving information, and handling mail.  | The hotel concierge helped us book a table at a popular restaurant.  酒店的礼宾员帮我们预订了一家很受欢迎的餐厅的桌子。| receptionist, attendant |  inmate, prisoner (反义词较为宽泛，并非完全对立) | 名词
