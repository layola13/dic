instillation: /ˌɪnstəˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 安装；灌输；注入 | The act of installing something, or the state of being installed; the process of instilling something, such as an idea or feeling.  |例句：The instillation of the new software took several hours. 新软件的安装花费了几个小时。

The instillation of hope in the hearts of the people was crucial. 在人们心中灌输希望至关重要。|近义词：installation, implantation, infusion |反义词：removal, extraction, uninstalling
