renter:/ˈrɛntər/| n. |租客，承租人 |A renter is a person who pays rent to use a property, such as an apartment or house, that they do not own.  |Example: The renter was late with the rent this month.  这个租客这个月房租交晚了。|近义词：tenant, lessee |反义词：landlord, owner |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The renter pays rent monthly. = 租客每月支付房租。
She rents a small apartment downtown. = 她租了一套位于市中心的小公寓。


一般过去时:
He rented a car for his vacation. = 他租了一辆车去度假。
They rented a house by the beach last summer. = 他们去年夏天租了一栋海边的房子。


现在完成时:
We have rented this apartment for five years. = 我们已经租这套公寓五年了。
She has rented several properties in the past. = 她过去租过几处房产。


过去完成时:
They had rented the apartment before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，他们已经租了那套公寓。
He had rented a car, but it broke down. = 他租了一辆车，但它坏了。


将来时:
I will rent a car when I get to the airport. = 我到机场后会租一辆车。
They will rent a bigger house next year. = 他们明年会租一栋更大的房子。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would rent a bigger apartment. = 如果我有更多钱，我会租一套更大的公寓。
I wish I could rent a house in the countryside. = 我希望我能租一栋乡下的房子。


被动语态:
The apartment was rented quickly. = 这套公寓很快就被租出去了。
The car is being rented by a tourist. = 这辆车正被一个游客租用。


疑问句:
Do you rent your apartment or own it? = 你是租公寓还是拥有它？
Who rented this car? = 谁租了这辆车？


否定句:
They don't rent out their property. = 他们不把他们的房产出租。
We didn't rent a car because we used public transportation. = 我们没有租车，因为我们使用了公共交通工具。
