disrobing:/dɪsˈroʊbɪŋ/ | v. | 脱衣；脱掉衣服 | The act of removing clothing.  It often implies a gradual or deliberate removal, sometimes with a sense of unveiling or revealing something underneath. | Example: The dancer began disrobing slowly, revealing a stunning costume beneath.  舞者开始慢慢脱衣，露出了下面一件令人惊艳的服装。  The actor disrobed before applying the stage makeup. 演员在化妆前脱掉了衣服。|近义词：undressing, shedding clothes | 反义词：dressing, clothing
