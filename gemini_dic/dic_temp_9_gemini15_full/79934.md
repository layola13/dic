gnomon:/ˈnoʊmɑːn/| n. |圭表；日晷的指标；标尺；标志|A gnomon is the part of a sundial that casts a shadow to tell the time.  It can also refer to a part of a geometric figure that, when added to another figure, completes a similar larger figure.  In architecture, it can be a projecting part of a building.|例句：The gnomon of the sundial pointed directly at the noon mark. 日晷的圭表正指向正午标记。The gnomon on the sundial cast a long shadow in the late afternoon. 日晷上的圭表在傍晚投下长长的影子。|近义词：index, pointer, marker|反义词：N/A
