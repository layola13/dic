disprover: /dɪsˈpruːvər/ | n. | 反驳者；证明...是错误的人 | A person who disproves something. Someone who demonstrates that something is false or incorrect.  | Example: The scientist presented compelling evidence, silencing his disprovers.  这位科学家提出了令人信服的证据，使他的反对者哑口无言。 |近义词：refuter, contradictor, opponent | 反义词：prover, verifier | 名词

She is a disprover of the theory. = 她是该理论的反对者。


一般过去时:
