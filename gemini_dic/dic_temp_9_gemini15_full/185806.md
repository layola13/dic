revisionists:/rɪˈvɪʒənɪsts/| n.|修正主义者，历史修正主义者|Those who revise accepted historical accounts, often to present a different interpretation or perspective, sometimes with a political agenda.  They may challenge established narratives or reinterpret events to suit their own viewpoints.|Example:  Some revisionists argue that the causes of World War I were more complex than traditionally portrayed.  一些修正主义者认为第一次世界大战的起因比传统描绘的要复杂得多。|近义词：reinterpreters, reinterpreters, revisionists,  revisers|反义词：traditionalists, orthodox, conformists|名词
