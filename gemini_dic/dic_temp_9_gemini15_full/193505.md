selenocentric:/ˌsɛlɪnoʊˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. |以月球为中心的；把月球当作宇宙中心 | Relating to or considered from the perspective of the moon as the center of the universe.  This term is primarily used in a historical context, contrasting with the heliocentric model. | Example:  The ancient Greeks held a selenocentric view of the cosmos, believing the moon to be at the center. = 古希腊人持有以月球为中心的宇宙观，认为月球位于中心。  | 近义词：lunocentric (less common) | 反义词：heliocentric, geocentric | 词性：形容词
