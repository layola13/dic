misinterpretation:/ˌmɪsɪnˌtɜːrprəˈteɪʃən/| n. |误解，曲解 |The misinterpretation of the contract led to a dispute.  合同的误解导致了纠纷。|近义词：misunderstanding, misconstruction |反义词：correct interpretation, accurate understanding |名词
