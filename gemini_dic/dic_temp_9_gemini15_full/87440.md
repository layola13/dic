hesitater:/ˈhezɪteɪtər/| n. |犹豫者，迟疑者 | A person who hesitates or is slow to act or decide.  |例句：The hesitater in the group delayed the decision-making process.  这个小组里的犹豫者延缓了决策过程。|例句：Many hesitater were observed during the survey. 在调查期间观察到许多犹豫者。|近义词：procrastinator, waverer, doubter |反义词：decisiver, go-getter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hesitates before speaking. = 他说话前犹豫了一下。
She hesitates to make a decision. = 她犹豫着要不要做决定。

一般过去时:
He hesitated before answering the question. = 他在回答问题之前犹豫了一下。
She hesitated to commit to the project. = 她犹豫着要不要承诺参与这个项目。

现在完成时:
He has hesitated too long already. = 他已经犹豫太久了。
She has hesitated to tell him the truth. = 她一直犹豫着要不要告诉他真相。

过去完成时:
He had hesitated for a long time before finally agreeing. = 他在最终同意之前犹豫了很久。
She had hesitated to leave her job, fearing the unknown. = 她一直犹豫着要不要辞职，害怕未知的未来。

将来时:
He will hesitate before making such a big decision. = 他会在做出如此重大的决定之前犹豫。
She will hesitate to trust him again. = 她将犹豫是否再次信任他。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't hesitated, he would have succeeded. = 如果他不犹豫的话，他就成功了。
I wish she hadn't hesitated to ask for help. = 我真希望她没有犹豫着去寻求帮助。

被动语态:  (Hesitate is rarely used in the passive voice.  A more natural phrasing would be needed)
The decision was delayed by the hesitation of some members. =  一些成员的犹豫延误了决定。
His progress was hampered by his hesitation to take risks. = 他犹豫冒险阻碍了他的进步。

疑问句:
Does he hesitate to express his opinions? = 他是否犹豫表达自己的意见？
Will she hesitate to accept the offer? = 她会犹豫是否接受这个提议吗？

否定句:
He didn't hesitate to jump into the pool. = 他毫不犹豫地跳进了游泳池。
She didn't hesitate to tell him what she thought. = 她毫不犹豫地告诉他她的想法。


PHRASE->
to hesitate to do something = 犹豫做某事
to hesitate in doing something = 在做某事上犹豫
to hesitate about something = 对某事犹豫
to hesitate over something = 斟酌某事
without hesitation = 毫不犹豫
