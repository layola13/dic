heedfulness: /ˈhidfʊlnəs/ | n. | 注意力；留心；警惕 | The quality of paying attention; careful consideration; attentiveness.  |  例句：Her heedfulness prevented an accident.  她的注意力避免了一场事故。

例句：The driver's heedfulness saved him from a collision. 司机的警惕性使他避免了一次碰撞。


近义词：attentiveness, mindfulness, vigilance, awareness, care | 反义词：negligence, carelessness, inattention, heedlessness


词源:  Derived from the adjective "heedful," which comes from the Old English word "hēdan," meaning "to pay attention to," "to heed."  The suffix "-ness" forms the noun, indicating a state or quality.
