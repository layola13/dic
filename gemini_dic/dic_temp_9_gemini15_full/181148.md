reinforce:/ˌriːɪnˈfɔːrs/| vt.| 加强；增援；补充；巩固 | To strengthen or support, especially with additional material or personnel.  |例句：We need to reinforce the walls before the next storm. 我们需要在下次风暴来临之前加固墙壁。|例句：The army sent reinforcements to the besieged city.  军队向被围困的城市增派了援军。|近义词：strengthen, support, bolster |反义词：weaken, undermine


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reinforce the message daily. = 他们每天都在强化这个信息。
The company reinforces its brand image through advertising. = 公司通过广告强化其品牌形象。

一般过去时:
The teacher reinforced the lesson with examples. = 老师用例子巩固了这个课程。
The government reinforced the border security. = 政府加强了边境安全。

现在完成时:
The new evidence has reinforced the prosecution's case. = 新证据强化了检方的案情。
Recent events have reinforced my belief in the importance of education. = 最近的事件强化了我对教育重要性的信念。

过去完成时:
They had reinforced the bridge before the heavy traffic started. = 他们在交通繁忙开始之前就加固了桥梁。
She had reinforced her arguments with additional data. = 她用额外的数据强化了自己的论点。


将来时:
The engineer will reinforce the structure to withstand the earthquake. = 工程师将加固结构以承受地震。
We will reinforce our position in the market. = 我们将在市场上巩固我们的地位。


被动语态:
The building was reinforced with steel beams. = 这座建筑用钢梁加固了。
The soldiers' morale was reinforced by the victory. = 士兵们的士气因胜利而增强了。


疑问句:
How can we reinforce positive behaviors in children? = 我们如何才能加强孩子们的积极行为？
Will the new policy reinforce or weaken the economy? = 新政策会加强还是削弱经济？


否定句:
The weak foundation could not be reinforced. = 脆弱的地基无法加固。
His arguments did not reinforce his credibility. = 他的论点并没有增强他的可信度。


PHRASE->
reinforce concrete = 钢筋混凝土
reinforce learning = 强化学习
reinforce one's position = 巩固立场
reinforce the idea that = 加强...的想法
reinforce a message = 加强信息


词源:
The word "reinforce" comes from the Old French word "renforcer," which literally means "to strengthen again."  The prefix "re-" indicates repetition or renewal, and "forcer" means "to force" or "to strengthen."
