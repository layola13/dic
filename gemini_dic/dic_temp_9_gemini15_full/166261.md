/ˌpriːrɪˈsiːvərz/ | n. | 预接收器；预接收装置 | A prereceiver is a device or system that prepares or preprocesses data or signals before they are received by a main receiver.  It might filter noise, amplify weak signals, or perform other preprocessing tasks to improve the quality or efficiency of the main receiver. This term is primarily used in technical contexts, particularly in telecommunications and signal processing.  | Example: The advanced prereceiver significantly reduced interference, leading to clearer audio reception.  预接收器显著降低了干扰，从而使音频接收更加清晰。 | 近义词: preprocessor,  frontend  | 反义词:  postprocessor,  main receiver | 名词
