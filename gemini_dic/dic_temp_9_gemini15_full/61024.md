elision:/ɪˈlɪʒən/| n. |省略；省音；（诗歌中）音节省略|The omission of a sound or syllable in a word or phrase, often for ease of pronunciation.  This is a common feature of spoken English.  For example, "gonna" is an elision of "going to".|例句：The elision of the final syllable in "government" is common in casual speech.  政府这个词的最后一个音节在日常口语中经常被省略。|近义词：omission, contraction, abbreviation|反义词：addition, inclusion
