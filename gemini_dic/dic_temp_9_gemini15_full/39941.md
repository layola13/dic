constructibility:/kənˌstrʌktəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可构造性，可建造性 | The feasibility of constructing something; the degree to which something can be built or created.  It considers factors like cost, time, available resources, and technological limitations. |  The constructibility of the bridge was questioned due to the unstable terrain.  桥梁的可建造性由于不稳定的地形而受到质疑。 The high constructibility of the modular building system allowed for rapid construction. 模块化建筑系统的可建造性高，使得施工速度很快。 | feasibility, buildability | impossibility, unfeasibility | 名词
