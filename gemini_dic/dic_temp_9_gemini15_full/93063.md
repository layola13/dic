idolish:/ˈaɪdəlɪʃ/| adj.| 愚蠢的；痴迷的；盲目的 | 英文解析：Idolish describes someone or something that is foolish, infatuated, or blindly devoted. It suggests a lack of judgment or critical thinking.  | 例句：His idolized devotion to the celebrity bordered on the idoolish.  = 他对那个名人的狂热崇拜近乎愚蠢。  His idoolish pursuit of wealth led to his ruin. = 他对财富的盲目追求导致了他的毁灭。| 近义词：foolish, silly, infatuated, blind, uncritical | 反义词：wise, sensible, discerning, critical
