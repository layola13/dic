impassionate:/ɪmˈpæʃənɪt/| adj.| 冷静的；冷静的；缺乏热情的 |  Characterized by a lack of passion or strong emotion; calm; dispassionate.  It suggests a deliberate absence of feeling, often in a situation where strong emotion might be expected. |例句：He gave an impassionate account of the events. 他平静地叙述了事件经过。|例句：Her impassionate delivery made the speech rather dull. 她平静的演讲使得演讲相当乏味。|近义词：calm, unemotional, apathetic, detached, indifferent |反义词：passionate, fervent, enthusiastic, ardent, zealous
