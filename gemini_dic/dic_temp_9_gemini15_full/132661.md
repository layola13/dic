nondisintegrating: /ˌnɒnˌdɪsɪnˈtɪɡreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不崩解的，不瓦解的 | Not breaking apart or falling to pieces.  This adjective describes something that maintains its structure and form over time, resisting disintegration. | Example: The nondisintegrating tablets ensured a consistent release of medication.  不崩解的药片确保了药物的持续释放。 The nondisintegrating concrete structure withstood the earthquake. 不崩解的混凝土结构经受住了地震。|近义词：stable, intact, cohesive | 反义词：disintegrating, crumbling, decaying |
