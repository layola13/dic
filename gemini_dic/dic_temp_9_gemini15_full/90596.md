hulloed:/həˈloʊd/ | v. |大声喊叫；招呼 |To shout or call out loudly; to greet loudly.| He hulloed to his friend across the street. 他大声向街对面的朋友打招呼。|近义词：shouted, called, hailed |反义词：whispered, murmured |动词


USAGE->

She hulloed a greeting from the window. = 她从窗口大声问好。

He has hulloed at me several times today. = 他今天已经几次大声喊我了。
