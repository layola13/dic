launching: /ˈlɔːntʃɪŋ/ | v. |发射；发起；推出；下水 | The act of launching something, such as a rocket, a product, or a boat.  It implies the beginning of a new project or venture. |例句：The company is launching a new marketing campaign next month. = 公司下个月将启动一项新的营销活动。

例句：The rocket launching was delayed due to bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，火箭发射被推迟了。


近义词：starting, initiating, introducing, commencing, unveiling

反义词：ending, concluding, terminating, ceasing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are launching a new satellite. = 他们正在发射一颗新卫星。
The company launches new products every year. = 这家公司每年都会推出新产品。


一般过去时:
We launched the boat last weekend. = 我们上周末下水了船。
They launched a successful fundraising campaign. = 他们发起了一场成功的筹款活动。


现在完成时:
They have launched three successful products this year. = 今年他们已经成功推出了三款产品。
She has launched her own business. = 她已经创办了自己的公司。


过去完成时:
The project had been launched before I joined the team. = 我加入团队之前，项目就已经启动了。
They had launched the product before the competitor. = 他们在竞争对手之前推出了产品。


将来时:
We will launch the new website next week. = 我们下周将上线新网站。
They will be launching a new line of clothing soon. = 他们很快就会推出新的服装系列。


虚拟语气:
If we launched the product earlier, we would have had a bigger market share. = 如果我们早点推出产品，就会有更大的市场份额。
I wish we had launched the campaign sooner. = 我真希望我们早点启动这场活动。


被动语态:
The new product was launched last week. = 新产品上周推出。
The spaceship is being launched tomorrow. = 宇宙飞船将于明天发射。


疑问句:
When will the new game be launched? = 新游戏什么时候发布？
Is the company launching a new marketing strategy? = 公司是否正在启动新的营销策略？


否定句:
They didn't launch the product on time. = 他们没有按时推出产品。
The project hasn't been launched yet. = 项目尚未启动。


PHRASE->
product launching = 产品发布
market launching = 市场发布
project launching = 项目启动
campaign launching = 活动启动
software launching = 软件发布
