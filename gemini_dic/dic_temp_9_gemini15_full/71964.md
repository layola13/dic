flights:/flaɪts/| n.|航班；飞行；飞行次数|Flights are a common way to travel long distances. 航班是长途旅行的常见方式。|近义词：air travel, journeys|反义词：ground transportation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flight departs at 8 a.m. = 航班于上午8点起飞。
Flights to London are often delayed. = 飞往伦敦的航班经常延误。


一般过去时:
The flight arrived late last night. = 航班昨晚到达很晚。
We took several flights during our vacation. = 我们在假期期间乘坐了几次航班。


现在完成时:
I have booked my flights already. = 我已经预订了我的航班。
She has never taken an international flight before. = 她以前从未乘坐过国际航班。


过去完成时:
The plane had already departed before we arrived at the airport. = 我们到达机场之前，飞机已经起飞了。
They had missed their connecting flight because of the traffic. = 由于交通堵塞，他们错过了他们的转机航班。


将来时:
We will take a flight to Paris next month. = 我们下个月将乘坐航班前往巴黎。
The flight will be delayed due to bad weather. = 由于恶劣天气，航班将延误。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would book a first-class flight. = 如果我有更多钱，我会预订头等舱航班。
I wish I had booked my flight earlier. = 我真希望我早点订机票。


被动语态:
The flights were cancelled due to a storm. = 由于暴风雨，航班被取消了。
All flights are being monitored closely. = 所有航班都在密切监控中。


疑问句:
What time is the flight? = 航班几点？
Are there any available flights to Rome? = 有飞往罗马的航班吗？


否定句:
There are no flights available today. = 今天没有航班。
He didn't book his flight in advance. = 他没有提前预订航班。


PHRASE->
book flights = 预订航班
cancel flights = 取消航班
delay flights = 延误航班
missed flights = 错过了航班
connecting flights = 转机航班
