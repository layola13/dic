It suggests impulsive actions or decisions made without sufficient thought. |例句：His noncontemplativeness led him to make rash decisions.  他的不假思索导致他做出草率的决定。  The noncontemplativeness of the response was alarming.  反应的不加思考令人担忧。|近义词：impulsiveness,thoughtlessness,rashness,recklessness|反义词：contemplativeness,reflectiveness,thoughtfulness,deliberation|
