poetesses:/ˌpoʊɪˈtɛsɪz/| n. |女诗人|Female poets; women who write poetry.  The term emphasizes the gender of the poets. |例句：Many talented poetesses emerged during the Victorian era.  许多有才华的女诗人出现在维多利亚时代。

例句：The anthology features works by both poets and poetesses from around the world. 这部选集收录了来自世界各地男女诗人的作品。


近义词：women poets, female bards


反义词：poets (implies both male and female)

词性:名词
