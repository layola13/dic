cancellous:/ˈkænsələs/| adj.|海绵状的；松质的；多孔的 | Spongy; having a lattice-like structure, like that of cancellous bone.  The term refers to a structure with many small spaces or holes. |Example: Cancellous bone is found in the interior of many bones, providing lightweight strength.  海绵状骨存在于许多骨骼的内部，提供轻便的强度。

The cancellous bone structure is crucial for shock absorption.  海绵状骨结构对于减震至关重要。


近义词：spongy, porous, trabecular

反义词：compact, dense, solid


词源:  From Latin *cancellatus, past participle of cancellare "to cross out, make lattice-work," from cancelli "lattice, grating."  The word evolved to describe the lattice-like structure found in cancellous bone.
