memorising: /ˈmeməraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 记忆；记住 | The act of committing something to memory.  | 例句：Memorising vocabulary is crucial for language learning.  记住词汇对语言学习至关重要。

例句：She spent hours memorising the poem for the recital. 她花了数小时记忆这首诗准备朗诵。


近义词：learning, committing to memory, retaining


反义词：forgetting, overlooking


词性：动词 (现在分词形式，动词原形为memorise)


词源:  The word "memorising" comes from the verb "memorise," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "memor," meaning "mindful" or "remembering."  The "-ise" suffix is a common English verb-forming element of French origin.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I memorise new vocabulary every day. = 我每天都记住新的词汇。
He memorises historical dates with ease. = 他很容易记住历史日期。


一般过去时:
I memorised the entire speech last night. = 我昨晚记住了整篇演讲。
She memorised the phone number before she left. = 她在离开之前记住了电话号码。


现在完成时:
I have memorised all the lines for the play. = 我已经记住了戏剧的所有台词。
She has memorised the lyrics to that song. = 她已经记住了那首歌的歌词。


过去完成时:
I had memorised the poem before the competition began. = 在比赛开始之前，我已经记住了这首诗。
They had memorised the map route before their hike. = 在徒步旅行之前，他们已经记住了地图路线。


将来时:
I will memorise the formula for the exam. = 我将记住考试的公式。
She will memorise the script by tomorrow. = 她明天将记住剧本。


虚拟语气:
If I had memorised the material, I would have passed the test. = 如果我记住了材料，我就通过考试了。
I wish I had memorised the directions. = 我真希望我记住了路线。


被动语态:
The poem was memorised by many students. = 这首诗被许多学生记住了。
The speech is being memorised by the candidate. = 演讲正在被候选人记住。


疑问句:
Have you memorised the definitions? = 你记住了定义吗？
Did he memorise the address correctly? = 他正确地记住了地址吗？


否定句:
I couldn't memorise the long numbers. = 我记不住那些长数字。
She didn't memorise the list of ingredients. = 她没有记住配料清单。
