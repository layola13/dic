licorice:/ˈlɪkərɪʃ/| n.|甘草，甘草糖|A plant with a sweet, black root used to flavor candy and medicine.  The root itself, or candy made from it. |例句：The licorice candy was too sweet for me. 这甘草糖对我来说太甜了。|例句：He added a pinch of licorice root to the herbal tea. 他在草药茶里加了一撮甘草根。|近义词：liquorice (British spelling)|反义词：无
