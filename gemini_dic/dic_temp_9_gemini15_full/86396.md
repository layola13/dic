hedging:/ˈhɛdʒɪŋ/| n. v.|对冲；规避风险；含糊其辞|The act of reducing risk by making investments in different areas, or protecting against losses by making a balancing investment.|The company used hedging strategies to protect itself against currency fluctuations.  这家公司使用了对冲策略来保护自己免受汇率波动的影响。|近义词：risk management, mitigation, equivocation|反义词：speculation, gambling


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He hedges his bets in the stock market. 他在股市里分散投资以规避风险。
The politician hedges his answers to avoid controversy.  这位政客含糊其辞地回答问题以避免争议。


一般过去时:
They hedged their investments against inflation last year. 他们去年对冲投资以防通货膨胀。
She hedged her response, unwilling to commit to a definite answer. 她含糊其辞地回答，不愿给出明确的答案。


现在完成时:
We have hedged our risk by investing in multiple sectors. 我们通过投资多个行业来规避风险。
He has hedged his statements to avoid offending anyone. 他含糊其辞以避免冒犯任何人。


过去完成时:
They had hedged their position before the market crash. 他们在市场崩盘前已经对冲了他们的头寸。
She had hedged her comments, leaving room for interpretation. 她先前说话含糊其辞，留下了解释的空间。


将来时:
We will hedge our currency exposure next quarter. 我们将在下一季度对冲我们的货币风险。
He will hedge his bets before making a final decision. 他将在做出最终决定之前分散风险。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would hedge my investments. 如果我是你，我会对冲我的投资。
I wish I had hedged my bets before the stock market dropped. 我真希望股市下跌前我已经分散了风险。


被动语态:
The risks were hedged by diversifying the portfolio. 风险通过多元化投资组合得到规避。
His statements were hedged to avoid making a commitment. 他的说法含糊其辞，以避免做出承诺。


疑问句:
Does the company hedge its currency risk?  这家公司是否对冲其货币风险？
How do you hedge your bets in such a volatile market? 你如何在如此动荡的市场中规避风险？


否定句:
They did not hedge their investment against interest rate hikes. 他们没有对冲他们的投资以防利率上涨。
She didn't hedge her words; she stated her opinion clearly. 她没有含糊其辞；她清楚地表达了自己的观点。
