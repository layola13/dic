exophthalmic:/ˌɛksoʊˈθælmɪk/| adj.|眼球突出(的)，甲状腺突出(的)| pertaining to or characterized by exophthalmos (abnormally prominent eyes).  The term is often associated with hyperthyroidism (overactive thyroid).|例句：Her exophthalmic eyes were a symptom of her Graves' disease.  她的眼球突出是格雷夫斯病的症状。|例句：The patient presented with exophthalmic goiter.  这位病人表现为甲状腺肿大伴眼球突出。|近义词：protruding, bulging, prominent|反义词：sunken, recessed
