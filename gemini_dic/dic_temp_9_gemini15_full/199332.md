similar:/ˈsɪmɪlər/| adj. |相似的，类似的 |Having a resemblance or likeness; alike.  Similar things share characteristics, though they are not identical.  |Example: His ideas are similar to mine. 他的想法与我的相似。

Example:  The two houses are remarkably similar in design. 这两座房子的设计非常相似。


近义词：alike, like, comparable, analogous, resembling

反义词：different, dissimilar, unlike, contrasting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The two paintings are similar in style. = 这两幅画的风格相似。
His approach is similar to hers. = 他的方法和她的一样。

一般过去时:
Their responses were similar to the previous year's. = 他们的回应与去年的相似。
The symptoms were similar to those of the flu. = 症状与流感相似。

现在完成时:
Many studies have shown similar results. = 许多研究都显示了相似的结果。
We have found similar patterns in both datasets. = 我们在这两个数据集中发现了相似的模式。

过去完成时:
The situation had been similar to one encountered previously. = 这种情况与之前遇到的情况相似。
The results had been similar to what they predicted. = 结果与他们的预测相似。

将来时:
The new design will be similar to the previous model. = 新的设计将与之前的型号相似。
Future experiments will likely yield similar outcomes. = 未来的实验可能会产生相似的结果。

虚拟语气:
If the conditions were similar, the outcome might be different. = 如果条件相似，结果可能不同。(注意此例虚拟语气表达的是结果可能不同，而非similar本身的虚拟语气)
It would be helpful if the two approaches were more similar. = 如果这两种方法更相似，那就有帮助了。

被动语态:
The two products are considered similar by many customers. = 这两种产品被许多顾客认为是相似的。
The results were found to be surprisingly similar. = 结果被发现惊人地相似。

疑问句:
Are the two samples similar in composition? = 这两个样本的成分相似吗？
Is this approach similar to the one we used before? = 这种方法与我们以前使用的方法相似吗？

否定句:
The two methods are not similar. = 这两种方法并不相似。
Her opinion is not similar to his. = 她的意见与他的不同。


PHRASE->
similar to = 与...相似
similar in = 在...方面相似
be similar to = 与...相似
quite similar = 非常相似
strikingly similar = 惊人地相似
