discounter:/ˈdɪskaʊntər/| n. |折扣商，打折销售者 |A discounter is a business that sells goods or services at lower prices than its competitors.  They achieve this through lower overhead costs, volume sales, or buying in bulk. |Example:  Many consumers prefer to shop at discounters for everyday household items. 许多消费者更喜欢在折扣店购买日常家用物品。|近义词：retailer, seller, bargain store |反义词：premium retailer, luxury store |名词
