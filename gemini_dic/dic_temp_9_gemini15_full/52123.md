diandrous:/ˈdaɪændrəs/| adj.| 双体的；有两枚雄蕊的 | Having two stamens.  The term is primarily used in botany to describe flowers with two stamens.  |Example: The diandrous flower had two prominent stamens, easily visible to the naked eye.  这朵双体花有两枚醒目的雄蕊，肉眼很容易看到。|近义词: bistaminate |反义词: monandrous, polyandrous
