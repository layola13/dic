mamas:/ˈmɑːməz/| n.|妈妈们，(非正式)母亲们|The plural form of "mama," a term of endearment for mother.  It's often used informally and can refer to multiple mothers or a group of mothers. |例句：The mamas were chatting about their kids at the playground. 妈妈们在操场上聊着他们的孩子。 The mamas all brought snacks for the birthday party.  妈妈们都带了零食来参加生日派对。|近义词：moms, mothers, maternal figures|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "fathers" or "dads".|名词
