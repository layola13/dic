replot:/riːˈplɒt/| vt.|重新绘制；重新构思（情节）|To plan or draw something again, especially a graph or chart, or to replan a story or plot.|The architect had to replot the building's blueprints after discovering an error. 建筑师在发现错误后不得不重新绘制建筑蓝图。|近义词：redraw, replan, revise|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He replots the data every month. 他每月重新绘制数据。
She replots the story to make it more dramatic. 她重新构思故事使其更具戏剧性。

一般过去时:
They replotted the graph to show the new data. 他们重新绘制图表以显示新数据。
The author replotted the novel's ending to improve it. 作者重新构思了小说的结尾以使其更好。

现在完成时:
She has replotted the entire project. 她已经重新规划了整个项目。
He has replotted the course of action. 他已经重新规划了行动方案。

过去完成时:
They had replotted the route before setting off. 他们在出发前已经重新规划了路线。
The team had replotted the strategy after the initial failure. 团队在最初失败后已经重新规划了策略。

将来时:
We will replot the chart next week. 我们下周将重新绘制图表。
The director will replot the final scene of the play. 导演将重新构思戏剧的最后一幕。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would replot the entire graph. 如果我有更多时间，我会重新绘制整个图表。
If he were to replot the story, he would change the ending. 如果他要重新构思故事，他会改变结局。

被动语态:
The graph was replotted to correct the errors. 图表被重新绘制以纠正错误。
The novel's plot was replotted to make it more suspenseful. 小说的情节被重新构思以使其更悬念迭起。

疑问句:
Will you replot the data before the presentation? 你会在演示文稿之前重新绘制数据吗？

否定句:
He did not replot the data; he simply added to it. 他没有重新绘制数据；他只是添加了数据。
They chose not to replot the story for the film adaptation. 他们选择不为电影改编重新构思故事。
