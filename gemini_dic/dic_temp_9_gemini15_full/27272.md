caltrop:/ˈkæltrɒp/| n. |蒺藜；铁蒺藜|A caltrop is a sharp-pointed object, typically made of metal or wood, scattered on the ground to deter cavalry or infantry.  It typically has four points, with three resting on the ground and one pointing upwards.|Example: The soldiers scattered caltrops across the battlefield to slow down the enemy's advance.  士兵们在战场上散布铁蒺藜以减缓敌人的前进速度。|近义词：punji stick, spiked obstacle|反义词：smooth path
