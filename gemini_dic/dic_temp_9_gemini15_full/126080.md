/ˈmʌstɪŋ/| v.|必须做；不得不做 |Musting refers to the act of being obligated or compelled to do something. It's a less common and somewhat informal way of expressing obligation.  It emphasizes the feeling of being forced or pressured to act. |  I'm musting finish this report by tomorrow. = 我必须在明天之前完成这份报告。 He was musting work overtime to meet the deadline. = 他不得不加班才能赶上截止日期。 |must, have to, need to |can, may, needn't |动词
