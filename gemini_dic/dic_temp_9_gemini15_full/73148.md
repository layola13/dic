forayed:/fɔːˈreɪd/| v.|突袭；尝试；涉足|To make a sudden attack or raid; to make a brief initial attempt at something new or unfamiliar. |例句：The army forayed into enemy territory.  军队突袭了敌方领土。

例句：She forayed into the world of finance. 她涉足金融领域。

近义词：raid, attack, venture, attempt, delve

反义词：retreat, withdraw, abandon
