remembrance:/rɪˈmembrəns/| n.|记忆；纪念；追思|The act of remembering someone or something; the state of being remembered; something that serves as a reminder.  It can refer to a specific memory, a general feeling of remembering, or an act of commemoration.|例句：The old photograph brought back many fond remembrances of her childhood. 这张旧照片唤起了许多她童年美好的回忆。|例句：The memorial service was a time of remembrance for the victims. 这次追悼会是为遇难者进行追思的时间。|近义词：memory, recollection, commemoration|反义词：forgetfulness, oblivion
