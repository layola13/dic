shtick:/ʃtɪk/| n. |噱头；拿手好戏；惯用伎俩|A shtick is a person's habitual or characteristic way of behaving or doing something, especially something that is designed to amuse or entertain.  It can also refer to a comedian's routine or a particular act or style of performing. |例句：His stand-up routine relies heavily on observational humor and well-worn shticks.  他的单口相声很大程度上依赖于观察幽默和老套的噱头。

例句：The magician's disappearing act was his signature shtick.  魔术师的消失表演是他的拿手好戏。|近义词：gimmick, routine, trick, act |反义词：seriousness, sincerity
