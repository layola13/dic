islander:/ˈaɪˌlændər/| n. |岛民，岛屿居民|A person who lives on an island.  Islanders often share a unique culture and way of life shaped by their island environment.|例句：The islanders welcomed the tourists with open arms. 岛民们张开双臂欢迎游客。

例句：Many islanders rely on fishing for their livelihood. 许多岛民依靠捕鱼为生。


近义词：islanded person, island dweller |反义词：mainlander (大陆居民)
