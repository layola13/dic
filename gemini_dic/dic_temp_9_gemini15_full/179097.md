redesignation:/ˌriːˌdɪzɪɡˈneɪʃən/| n. |重新指定；改名；重新分类 |The act of assigning something a new designation or purpose.  It involves changing the name, classification, or function of something.  This can apply to a person, place, thing, or even a concept.|例句：The redesignation of the park as a wildlife sanctuary was met with mixed reactions. 公园重新划为野生动物保护区，引起了各种不同的反应。 The company's redesignation of its product line led to increased sales. 公司对其产品线的重新定位导致销售额增长。|近义词：reassignment, reclassification, renaming |反义词：original designation, initial classification
