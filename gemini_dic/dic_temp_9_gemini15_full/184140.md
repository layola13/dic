resisting: /rɪˈzɪstɪŋ/ | v. | 抵抗；反抗；抵制 |  To oppose something or someone.  It implies actively fighting against or refusing to yield to a force, influence, or temptation. |

例句：
The soldiers were resisting the enemy's attack. = 士兵们正在抵抗敌人的进攻。

The government is resisting pressure to raise taxes. = 政府正在抵制加税的压力。


近义词：opposing, defying, withstanding, counteracting

反义词：yielding, surrendering, submitting, complying

词源:
The word "resisting" comes from the Old French word "resistre," meaning "to withstand," which in turn derived from the Latin word "resistere," meaning "to stand against."  The "-ing" suffix indicates the present participle form, denoting an ongoing action.
