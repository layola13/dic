conoidal:/ˈkəʊnɔɪdəl/| adj.|锥形的，圆锥形的 |Having the shape of a cone or resembling a cone.  The term often describes objects that are approximately cone-shaped, not necessarily perfectly geometric cones.  It implies a curved, tapering form. |例句：The volcano had a conoidal shape. 这座火山呈锥形。The conoidal shell spiraled upwards.  锥形贝壳向上盘旋。|近义词：conical, pyramidal, cone-shaped |反义词：cylindrical, cubical
