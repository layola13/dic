dystopianism:/ˌdɪsˈtoʊpiənɪzəm/ | n. |反乌托邦思想；反乌托邦主义 |The philosophical concept or the literary genre that depicts societies characterized by oppressive societal control and dehumanization.  It explores the consequences of flawed social systems and the potential for technology and societal structures to lead to a negative future. | Example: The novel explores themes of dystopianism, depicting a society where individual freedom is suppressed and technology is used for surveillance.  反乌托邦小说探讨了反乌托邦主义的主题，描绘了一个个人自由被压制、技术被用于监控的社会。|近义词：anti-utopianism, negative utopianism |反义词：utopianism
