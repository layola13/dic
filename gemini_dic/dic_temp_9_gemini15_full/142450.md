oscillograph:/ˈɒsɪləɡræf/| n. |示波器|An instrument that displays graphically the variations of a fluctuating electrical quantity, such as voltage or current, as a function of time.  It's used to visually represent electrical signals, allowing for the analysis of their waveforms and characteristics.|The oscillograph showed a clear sinusoidal wave.|近义词：oscilloscope, cathode-ray oscilloscope|反义词:  None readily available.
