combat:/ˈkɒmbæt/ /ˈkɑːmbæt/| n. v.|战斗，格斗；斗争，对抗 | Fighting, especially during a war; a struggle or fight against something.  |例句：The soldiers engaged in fierce combat.  士兵们参与了激烈的战斗。
The government is committed to combating poverty.  政府致力于打击贫困。|近义词：battle, fight, struggle, conflict |反义词：peace, harmony


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He combats injustice. = 他与不公正作斗争。
They combat disease with new medicines. = 他们用新药对抗疾病。


一般过去时:
The army combatted the enemy fiercely. = 军队与敌人进行了激烈的战斗。
She combatted her fear with courage. = 她勇敢地克服了恐惧。


现在完成时:
We have combatted inflation successfully. = 我们成功地抑制了通货膨胀。
They have combatted the virus effectively. = 他们有效地对抗了病毒。


过去完成时:
The soldiers had combatted for days before reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达之前，士兵们已经战斗了几天。
She had combatted illness for months before she finally recovered. = 在她最终康复之前，她已经与疾病斗争了几个月。


将来时:
We will combat climate change with sustainable practices. = 我们将通过可持续实践来应对气候变化。
They will combat the spread of misinformation. = 他们将打击虚假信息的传播。


虚拟语气:
If we combatted prejudice effectively, the world would be a better place. = 如果我们有效地打击偏见，世界将会变得更好。
I wish we could combat poverty more effectively. = 我希望我们能够更有效地打击贫困。


被动语态:
The disease is being combatted by new medical technology. = 这种疾病正在被新的医疗技术所对抗。
Climate change should be combatted by global cooperation. = 气候变化应该通过全球合作来应对。


疑问句:

否定句:

They did not combat the rumors effectively. = 他们没有有效地打击谣言。
