falafels:/ˈfæləfɛlz/ | n. | falafel (复数)；鹰嘴豆泥炸丸子 | Deep-fried balls or patties made from ground chickpeas or fava beans, herbs, and spices.  They are a popular Middle Eastern and Mediterranean food, often served in pita bread with various toppings. | 例句：I ordered a plate of falafels with hummus and tahini. 我点了一盘鹰嘴豆泥炸丸子，配上鹰嘴豆泥和芝麻酱。


例句：The falafels were crispy on the outside and soft on the inside.  这些鹰嘴豆泥炸丸子外面酥脆，里面柔软。


近义词： chickpea patties, bean fritters


反义词：无


词性：名词
