pertinacious: /ˌpɜːrtɪˈneɪʃəs/ | adj. | 顽强的；执着的；坚韧不拔的 |  Persisting stubbornly in an opinion or course of action.  Characterized by persistence; stubbornly tenacious. |

例句：

He was pertinacious in his pursuit of justice, even when faced with overwhelming obstacles.  他追求正义的意志非常坚定，即使面对巨大的阻碍。

Her pertinacious efforts finally paid off when she secured the promotion.  她坚持不懈的努力最终得到了回报，她获得了晋升。


近义词：tenacious, persistent, stubborn, dogged, resolute

反义词：yielding, irresolute, wavering, fickle, compliant


词源：

From Latin *pertinax, from pertināc- (past participle stem of pertināre "to hold to," from per- "thoroughly" + tenere "to hold").  源于拉丁语 *pertinax，来自 pertināc-（pertināre 的过去分词词干，“坚持”，来自 per- “彻底地” + tenere “持有”）。
