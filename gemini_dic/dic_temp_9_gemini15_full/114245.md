mailing:/ˈmeɪlɪŋ/| n.|邮件，邮寄|The act of sending something by mail; the process of preparing and sending items through the postal system.|例句：The mailing of the packages is scheduled for tomorrow. 明天计划邮寄包裹。|例句：She handled the mailing of the invitations for the wedding. 她负责婚礼请柬的邮寄工作。|近义词：postage, dispatch, sending|反义词：receiving, collection


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company mails the newsletters every month. = 公司每月邮寄通讯。
He mails his letters regularly. = 他定期邮寄信件。

一般过去时:
They mailed the package yesterday. = 他们昨天邮寄了包裹。
She mailed the application last week. = 她上周邮寄了申请表。

现在完成时:
I have mailed all the invitations. = 我已经邮寄了所有的请柬。
They have mailed the documents to the correct address. = 他们已将文件邮寄到正确的地址。

过去完成时:
We had mailed the package before the deadline. = 我们在截止日期前已经邮寄了包裹。
She had mailed the letter before she left for vacation. = 她出发度假前已经邮寄了信件。

将来时:
He will mail the documents tomorrow. = 他明天将邮寄文件。
We will mail the gifts next week. = 我们下周将邮寄礼物。

虚拟语气:
If I had known her address, I would have mailed the letter. = 如果我知道她的地址，我就会把信寄出去了。
I wish I had mailed the package earlier. = 我真希望我早点把包裹寄出去。

被动语态:
The letters were mailed on Monday. = 信件在星期一寄出。
The packages are being mailed today. = 包裹今天正在邮寄中。

疑问句:
Have you mailed the package yet? = 你已经邮寄包裹了吗？
When will the invitations be mailed? = 请柬什么时候邮寄？

否定句:
I didn't mail the letter. = 我没有邮寄信件。
She hasn't mailed the package yet. = 她还没有邮寄包裹。


PHRASE->
bulk mailing = 批量邮寄
direct mailing = 直邮
email mailing = 电子邮件邮寄
mailing list = 邮件列表
mailing address = 邮寄地址
