rational:/ˈræʃənəl/| adj.| 理性的，合理的；基于理性的 |  Based on or in accordance with reason or logic.  It implies the ability to think clearly and make sound judgments. | Example:  He made a rational decision after carefully considering all the options. 他经过仔细考虑所有选择后做出了一个理性的决定。  She presented a rational argument for her proposal. 她为她的提案提出了一个合理的论据。|近义词：reasonable, logical, sensible, intellectual |反义词：irrational, illogical, unreasonable, foolish
