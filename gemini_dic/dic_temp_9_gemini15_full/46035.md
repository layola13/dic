cycadaceous:/ˌsaɪkəˈdeɪʃəs/| adj.|苏铁科的，似苏铁的|Of or relating to the Cycadaceae, a family of gymnospermous plants.  Characterized by palm-like appearance and usually having a stout, unbranched trunk with a crown of large, stiff, pinnate leaves.|例句：The cycadaceous plants are ancient and have survived for millions of years.  苏铁科植物古老，已经生存了数百万年。|The cycadaceous flora of the island was remarkable. 岛屿上苏铁科植物的植被非常引人注目。|近义词：palmlike (in appearance)|反义词：angiospermous (referring to flowering plants)
