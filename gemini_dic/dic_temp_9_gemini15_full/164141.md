predisposedness:/ˌpriːdɪˈspoʊzdnəs/| n. |倾向性，易感性，预先倾向|A predisposition is an increased likelihood of developing a particular disease or condition.  It often refers to a genetic or inherent susceptibility, but can also include environmental factors that increase risk.| Example:  He had a predisposition to heart disease due to family history. 他由于家族病史而有患心脏病的倾向。  Her predisposition to anxiety made public speaking difficult. 她易焦虑的倾向使得公开演讲变得困难。|近义词：susceptibility, proneness, tendency, liability|反义词：resistance, immunity, insusceptibility|名词
