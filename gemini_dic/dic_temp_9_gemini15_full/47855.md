deceitfully:/dɪˈsiːtfəli/ | adv. | 欺骗地；狡猾地 |  In a deceitful manner; deceptively.  It describes an action or behavior carried out with the intention to mislead or trick someone. | 例句：He deceitfully obtained the information by posing as a journalist. 他假扮记者，欺骗地获取了信息。

He deceitfully manipulated the evidence to support his claim. 他狡猾地操纵证据来支持他的说法。 | 近义词：fraudulently, deceptively, cunningly, artfully | 反义词：honestly, truthfully, openly, frankly | 词性:副词
