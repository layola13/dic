explained:/ɪkˈspleɪnd/ | v. |解释，说明|To make something clear or easy to understand; to give reasons for something.|The teacher explained the grammar rules to the students.老师向学生解释了语法规则。| clarified, elucidated, illustrated | obfuscated, confused |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He explains the problem clearly. 他清晰地解释了这个问题。
She explains her decision to me. 她向我解释了她的决定。


一般过去时:
He explained the situation to his boss. 他向老板解释了情况。
She explained why she was late. 她解释了她为什么迟到。


现在完成时:
He has explained the project to the team. 他已经向团队解释了这个项目。
She has explained the rules several times. 她已经解释了好几次规则了。


过去完成时:
He had explained the plan before the meeting. 他在会议前已经解释了计划。
She had explained the difficulty to her colleagues. 她已经向同事解释了困难。


将来时:
He will explain the process tomorrow. 他明天会解释这个过程。
She will explain the results next week. 她下周会解释结果。


虚拟语气:
If he explained it better, they would understand. 如果他解释得更好，他们就会理解。
I wish she had explained the instructions more clearly. 我希望她能更清楚地解释说明。


被动语态:
The rules were explained thoroughly. 规则被彻底地解释了。
The problem was explained in detail. 这个问题被详细地解释了。


疑问句:
Can you explain this to me? 你能向我解释一下这个吗？
How did he explain his absence? 他如何解释他的缺席？


否定句:
He didn't explain the reason for his actions. 他没有解释他行为的原因。
She didn't explain herself clearly enough. 她没有足够清晰地解释自己。


PHRASE->
explain away = 搪塞；解释掉
explain oneself = 解释自己的行为或观点
explain in detail = 详细解释
to explain something to someone = 向某人解释某事
It goes without saying that... = 不言而喻
