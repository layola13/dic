dekko:/ˈdɛkəʊ/| n. |匆匆一瞥；查看；察看 |A quick look; a glance.  It's often used informally. |例句：I had a dekko at the newspaper headlines. 我匆匆看了一眼报纸的头条新闻。

例句：Let's have a dekko at what's in the box. 让我们看看盒子里有什么。


近义词：glance, peek, look, view |反义词：ignore, disregard |词性:名词


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, standard verb tense examples are not applicable.  The usage revolves around the noun's meaning of a quick look.

PHRASE->
have a dekko at = 看一看...
take a dekko at = 看一看...
a quick dekko = 匆匆一瞥
a dekko at = 看一眼...
