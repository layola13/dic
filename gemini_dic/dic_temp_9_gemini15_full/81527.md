gravitate:/ˈɡrævɪteɪt/ | vi. |倾向于；被吸引到；受……影响 |To be drawn to something; to tend to move toward something.  |例句：Many young people gravitate towards big cities.  许多年轻人倾向于到大城市发展。|例句：His opinions gravitate towards conservatism. 他的观点倾向于保守主义。|近义词：tend, incline, lean, drift |反义词：avoid, shun, repel


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gravitates towards classical music. = 他喜欢古典音乐。
People gravitate towards those who are kind and compassionate. = 人们倾向于那些善良和富有同情心的人。

一般过去时:
She gravitated towards a career in medicine. = 她选择了医学事业。
The conversation gravitated towards politics. = 谈话转向了政治。

现在完成时:
He has gravitated towards a more spiritual path in life. = 他的人生道路已经转向了更加精神层面。
The company has gravitated towards a more sustainable business model. = 这家公司已经转向了更可持续的商业模式。

过去完成时:
Before the accident, he had gravitated towards risky behavior. = 事故发生前，他一直倾向于冒险行为。
After the scandal, the public had gravitated towards alternative news sources. = 丑闻过后，公众转向了另类的新闻来源。

将来时:
I think she will gravitate towards a leadership role in the future. = 我认为她将来会倾向于担任领导角色。
With the new technology, the market will gravitate towards more efficient solutions. = 随着新技术的出现，市场将会倾向于更高效的解决方案。

虚拟语气:
If he were honest, he would not gravitate towards deception. = 如果他诚实，他就不会倾向于欺骗。
I wish she would gravitate towards healthier lifestyle choices. = 我希望她能转向更健康的生活方式。

被动语态:  (Gravitate is an intransitive verb and does not typically take a passive voice.)

疑问句:
Does he gravitate towards any particular political party? = 他倾向于任何特定的政党吗？
Where do your interests gravitate? = 你的兴趣爱好是什么？


否定句:
He does not gravitate towards conflict. = 他不喜欢冲突。
They didn't gravitate towards the popular opinion. = 他们没有随大流。


PHRASE->
gravitate towards = 倾向于
gravitate to = 被吸引到
gravitate around = 围绕…展开
gravitate together = 聚在一起
gravitate away from = 远离
