closest: /ˈkloʊsɪst/ | adj. | 最近的，最接近的 |  The closest meaning to the word is... 这个词最接近的意思是... | near, nearest, next | farthest, furthest |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The closest store is just around the corner. = 最近的商店就在拐角处。
What is the closest bus stop to your house? =离你家最近的公交车站是哪个？


一般过去时:
The closest he ever got to winning was second place. = 他离获胜最近的一次是获得第二名。
His house was the closest to the fire. = 他的房子离火灾最近。


现在完成时:
That's the closest I've ever come to achieving my goal. = 这是我离实现目标最近的一次了。
Have you found the closest parking spot? = 你找到最近的停车位了吗？


过去完成时:
The closest they had ever been to agreement was before the last meeting. = 他们离达成一致最近的一次是在上次会议之前。
She realized that this was the closest she had been to giving up. = 她意识到这是她离放弃最近的一次。


将来时:
The closest flight to London will be tomorrow morning. = 最近的去伦敦的航班将在明天早上。
What will be the closest public transport to the event? = 最近的公共交通工具到活动地点是什么？


虚拟语气:
If I had a choice, I would choose the closest solution. = 如果我有选择，我会选择最近的解决方案。
I wish the closest hospital was closer. = 我希望最近的医院更近一些。


被动语态:
The closest solution was chosen by the committee. = 最接近的解决方案被委员会选中了。
The closest route to the destination is currently being assessed. = 目前正在评估到达目的地的最近路线。


疑问句:

What is the closest thing to a miracle that you have witnessed? = 你见过的最接近奇迹的事情是什么？


否定句:
That wasn't the closest option. = 那不是最近的选择。
This isn't the closest restaurant to the office. = 这家餐厅不是离办公室最近的。
