reverification:/ˌriːvɛrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |复查；再验证；重新核实 |The act of verifying something again; a second or further check to ensure accuracy or validity.  It often implies a previous verification process that needs further confirmation. |例句：The bank requires reverification of the customer's identity before processing the transaction. = 银行在处理交易之前需要重新核实客户的身份。

The company conducted a reverification of its safety protocols after the accident. = 事故发生后，公司对安全规程进行了复查。

|近义词：recheck, verification, confirmation, validation |反义词：disproof, invalidation |名词
