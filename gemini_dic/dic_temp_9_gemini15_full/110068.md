lichenicolous:/ˌlaɪkənˈɪkəlʌs/| adj.|地衣栖息的，生于地衣上的|Living on or among lichens.  The term describes organisms, typically fungi or other microorganisms, that have a symbiotic or parasitic relationship with lichens.|Example: Many lichenicolous fungi are specialized parasites of specific lichen species.  许多地衣栖息真菌是特定地衣物种的专性寄生虫。|近义词：lichen-inhabiting|反义词：lichen-free
