fura:/ˈfʊrə/| n. |弗拉(一种食物)|Fura is a traditional West African food, typically made from millet or sorghum flour. It's often served as a porridge or a dough-like consistency.|Example:  My grandmother makes the best fura in the village.  我奶奶做的弗拉是村里最好吃的。|近义词：porridge, gruel|反义词：无
