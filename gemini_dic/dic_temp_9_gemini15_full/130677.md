nonaffectation:/ˌnɒnæfɛkˈteɪʃən/ | n. |不矫揉造作，自然，真诚 |The absence of affectation; naturalness; genuineness.  It describes a behavior or manner that is free from artificiality or pretense. |例句：Her nonaffectation made her instantly likable.  她的自然不做作让她立刻就讨人喜欢。

例句：The speaker's nonaffectation made his message more impactful. 演讲者不矫揉造作的方式让他的信息更有影响力。


近义词：naturalness, genuineness, sincerity, authenticity, simplicity

反义词：affectation, artificiality, pretense, posturing, insincerity


词源:  The word is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the noun "affectation," which derives from the Latin word "affectatio," meaning "an attempt to impress, an artificial display."  Therefore, "nonaffectation" literally means "not an affectation."
