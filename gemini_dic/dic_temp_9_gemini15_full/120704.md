misaiming:/ˌmɪsˈeɪmɪŋ/| v. |瞄准错误，失误，射偏 |The act of failing to aim correctly; directing something towards the wrong target.  It implies an intention to aim, but a failure in execution. |例句：The archer's misaiming resulted in a missed shot.  弓箭手的瞄准错误导致射失。

The pilot's misaiming of the missile caused collateral damage. 飞行员导弹瞄准错误导致了附带损害。|近义词：misdirection, misalignment, inaccuracy, erring |反义词：accurate aiming, precision, hitting the mark |词性:动词
