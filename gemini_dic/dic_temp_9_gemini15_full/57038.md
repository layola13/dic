doubling:/ˈdʌblɪŋ/| n. v.|加倍；倍增；复制|The act of making something twice as much or as many;  to become twice as much or as many.  It can refer to the act of making a copy or replicating something.|例句：The company announced a doubling of its profits this year. 今年公司宣布利润翻倍。|例句：The population of the city is doubling every decade. 这个城市的人口每十年翻一番。|近义词：replication, duplication, increase, multiplication|反义词：halving, reduction, decrease


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory is doubling its production. = 工厂正在将其产量翻倍。
He doubles his efforts to finish the project. = 他加倍努力完成项目。


一般过去时:
The price doubled overnight. = 价格一夜之间翻倍了。
They doubled the recipe to feed everyone. = 他们把食谱加倍，好让每个人都能吃饱。


现在完成时:
The company has doubled its market share. = 公司已经将其市场份额翻了一番。
Profits have doubled since last year. = 自去年以来，利润翻了一番。


过去完成时:
They had doubled their investment before the market crashed. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经将投资翻了一番。
The population had doubled in the previous decade. = 在之前的十年里，人口翻了一番。


将来时:
The company will double its workforce next year. = 公司明年将把员工人数翻一番。
We expect sales to double in the next quarter. = 我们预计销售额将在下一季度翻一番。


虚拟语气:
If we doubled our efforts, we might succeed. = 如果我们加倍努力，我们可能会成功。
I wish I had doubled my investment then. = 我真希望当时我把投资翻倍了。


被动语态:
The recipe is doubled to serve more people. = 食谱加倍是为了服务更多的人。
Production will be doubled by next year. = 到明年产量将翻一番。


疑问句:
Will the company double its profits this year? = 公司今年的利润会翻倍吗？
Did the number of participants double? = 参加人数翻倍了吗？


否定句:
The effort didn't double the results. = 这份努力并没有使结果翻倍。
The company did not double its production. = 公司没有将其产量翻倍。

词源:
The word "doubling" comes from the Old English word "dublian," meaning "to make double."  This ultimately derives from the Proto-Germanic root "*dubljan," which also gave rise to similar words in other Germanic languages.
