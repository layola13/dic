predraw:/ˈpriːdrɔː/| vt.| 预先绘制，预先画图 | To draw or sketch something beforehand, often as a preparation for a more finished piece of work.  It implies a preliminary drawing, not necessarily a detailed one. |例句：The architect predrew the building's blueprints before starting construction. 建筑师在开始施工前预先绘制了建筑蓝图。

例句：The artist predrew the composition on a small sketch pad before starting the large canvas.  艺术家在开始创作大幅画布之前，先在小型速写本上预先绘制了构图。


近义词：sketch, outline, draft, pre-sketch


反义词：finalize, complete


词性：动词
