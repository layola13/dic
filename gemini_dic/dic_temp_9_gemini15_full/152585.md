peacebreaker: /ˈpiːsˌbreɪkər/ | n. |破坏和平者，挑起争端者 | A person or thing that disrupts peace or harmony.  |例句：The politician's inflammatory speech made him a peacebreaker.  这位政治家的煽动性言论使他成为一个破坏和平的人。

例句：His constant arguing made him a peacebreaker in the family. 他不断的争吵使他成为家庭中的一个破坏和平的人。


近义词：troublemaker, agitator, discordant element, firebrand |反义词：peacemaker, conciliator


词源: peace (和平) + breaker (破坏者，打破者)  The word is a compound word formed by combining "peace" and "breaker".  "Breaker" here refers to something that breaks or disrupts something else.
