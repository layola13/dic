naphthalenes: /ˈnæfθəliːnz/ | n. |萘；萘丸 | Naphthalene is a white crystalline aromatic hydrocarbon.  It is a volatile solid with a strong, characteristic odor.  It is used as an insecticide and moth repellent, often in the form of mothballs.  | Example:  Mothballs, containing naphthalene, are effective in deterring moths from clothing.  萘丸（含有萘）能有效防止蛾子蛀食衣物。|近义词: mothballs, moth repellent | 反义词: N/A
