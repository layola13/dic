mischarges:/ˈmɪsˌtʃɑːrdʒɪz/ | n. |错误收费，错账 | Incorrect charges; billing errors.  |Example: The customer complained about the mischarges on their bill.  客户抱怨账单上的错误收费。  |近义词：errors, mistakes, overcharges |反义词：correct charges, accurate billing |名词
