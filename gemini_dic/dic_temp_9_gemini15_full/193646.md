self-castigating: /ˌsɛlfˈkæstɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我惩罚的；自我谴责的 |  Describing someone who engages in self-criticism or punishment.  It implies a harsh and often excessive judgment of oneself. | 例句：He was consumed by self-castigating thoughts after the accident. 他事故后被自我谴责的想法吞噬了。

He engaged in self-castigating behavior, refusing to allow himself any enjoyment. 他从事自我惩罚的行为，拒绝让自己享受任何乐趣。


近义词：self-reproaching, self-condemning, self-critical, masochistic (in extreme cases) | 反义词：self-forgiving, self-compassionate, self-accepting | 词性：形容词
