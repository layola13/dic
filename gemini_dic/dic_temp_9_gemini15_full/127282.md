navies:/ˈneɪviz/| n.|海军，舰队|The collective term for a country's navy or the navies of several countries.  It refers to the armed forces of a nation that operate on the sea. |例句：Many navies participated in the international naval exercise.  许多海军参加了这次国际海军演习。
例句：The British Navy is one of the oldest and most respected navies in the world.  英国海军是世界上最古老、最受尊敬的海军之一。|近义词：fleets, armed forces (at sea)|反义词：army, air force|名词
