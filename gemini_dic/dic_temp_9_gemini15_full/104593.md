/ˈkænɪst/ | n. | 康德主义者 | A follower or adherent of the philosophy of Immanuel Kant.  Someone who believes in or advocates Kantian philosophy. | Example: Many prominent thinkers are considered Kantists, deeply influenced by his moral philosophy.  许多杰出的思想家都被认为是康德主义者，深受其道德哲学的影响。 | 近义词：Kantian (adj.) | 反义词：None specifically, but philosophies opposing Kantian ethics might be considered antonyms.  没有具体的反义词，但与康德伦理学相对立的哲学可以被认为是反义词。
