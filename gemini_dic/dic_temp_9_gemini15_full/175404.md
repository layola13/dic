ramble:/ˈræmbəl/| v. n. |闲逛；漫步；漫谈；随意写作 |to walk or go aimlessly; to talk or write in a rambling way |例句：We rambled through the woods for hours. 我们在树林里漫步了几个小时。|例句：He rambled on about his childhood. 他滔滔不绝地谈论起他的童年。|近义词：wander, stroll, meander, roam |反义词：hurry, rush, sprint

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They ramble through the park every Sunday. = 他们每个星期天都在公园里漫步。
He rambles on about politics endlessly. = 他没完没了地漫谈政治。

一般过去时:
We rambled along the coast yesterday. = 昨天我们沿着海岸漫步。
She rambled on about her trip for hours. = 她滔滔不绝地谈论她的旅行好几个小时。

现在完成时:
They have rambled across many countries. = 他们已经漫游过许多国家。
He has rambled on about that topic many times. = 他已经多次就那个话题长篇大论了。

过去完成时:
We had rambled for miles before we found the trail. = 我们走了好几英里才找到小路。
She had rambled on so long that everyone was bored. = 她已经唠叨了很久，以至于每个人都厌倦了。

将来时:
We will ramble through the fields tomorrow. = 我们明天将在田野里漫步。
He will ramble on about his achievements at the party. = 他将在派对上大谈他的成就。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would ramble through the countryside. = 如果我有更多时间，我会在乡间漫步。
I wish he wouldn't ramble on so much during meetings. = 我希望他在会议上不要说得太多。

被动语态:
The paths are often rambled by tourists. = 小路经常被游客践踏。
The story is rambled and difficult to follow. = 故事支离破碎，难以理解。


疑问句:
Do they often ramble in the mountains? = 他们经常在山上漫步吗？
Does he ramble when he's nervous? = 他紧张的时候会滔滔不绝地说吗？


否定句:
They didn't ramble far from the campsite. = 他们没有远离营地太远。
He didn't ramble; he spoke clearly and concisely. = 他没有漫无边际地说话；他说话清晰简洁。
