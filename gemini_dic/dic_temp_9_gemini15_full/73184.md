forboding:/fɔːˈboʊdɪŋ/| /fɔːˈbəʊdɪŋ/ | adj. |预兆的；不祥的；令人不安的 |A feeling of evil to come; a strong feeling that something bad is going to happen.  It suggests a sense of dread or apprehension, often about something unspecified or in the future. |例句：The dark clouds and the stillness in the air created a forboding atmosphere.  阴沉的乌云和空气中的寂静营造出一种不祥的氛围。

The silence after the explosion was forboding. 爆炸后的寂静令人不安。|近义词：ominous, threatening, menacing, sinister, apprehensive, uneasy |反义词：reassuring, comforting, hopeful, optimistic |
