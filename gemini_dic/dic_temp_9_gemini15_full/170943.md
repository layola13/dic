pseudonyms: /ˈsuːdənɪmz/ | n. |假名，笔名| Fictitious names used instead of real ones, often by authors or celebrities to protect their identity or create a separate persona.  | Example: Many authors use pseudonyms to publish works that differ significantly from their established style.  许多作者使用笔名来发表与他们既定风格大相径庭的作品。|近义词：aliases, nom de plumes, pen names |反义词：real names, true identities
