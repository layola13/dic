bustiers:/ˈbʌstiərz/| n.|紧身胸衣；束胸衣|A bustier is a close-fitting, usually strapless, garment that supports and shapes the breasts. It is often made of a firm fabric like satin or lace and can be worn as lingerie or under other clothing.  |例句：She wore a black lace bustier under a sheer blouse. 她穿着一件黑色蕾丝紧身胸衣在透明衬衫里面。|例句：The bustier was beautifully crafted with intricate embroidery. 这件紧身胸衣做工精美，绣工精细。|近义词：corset, bodice, bra|反义词：loose-fitting top
