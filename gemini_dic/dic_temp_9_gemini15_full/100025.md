interpervade:/ˌɪntərˈpeɪveɪd/ | vt. | 弥漫；遍布；渗透 | To spread throughout and penetrate every part of something. | The scent of roses interpervaded the garden. 玫瑰的香味弥漫在花园里。  The spirit of innovation interpervaded the company culture. 创新的精神弥漫于公司文化之中。| permeate, saturate, imbue | isolate, exclude | 动词
