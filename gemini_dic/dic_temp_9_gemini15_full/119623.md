middle-aged:/ˌmɪdlˈeɪdʒd/| adj. |中年(的)|  Referring to the period of life between young adulthood and old age, typically considered to be between 40 and 65 years old.  The exact age range can vary depending on cultural context and individual perception.|例句：Many middle-aged people experience a midlife crisis. 许多中年人经历中年危机。|例句：She's a middle-aged woman with a successful career. 她是一位事业有成的中年妇女。|近义词：adult, grown-up |反义词：young, elderly
