check:/tʃɛk/ | v., n. | 检查；支票；核对 | To examine something to determine its accuracy, quality, or condition; a written order directing a bank to pay money. |例句：Please check your work before submitting it. 请在提交作业前检查一下你的工作。

例句：I'll write you a check for the amount owed. 我会给你开一张支票支付欠款。


近义词：examine, inspect, verify, bill, draft | 反义词：overlook, neglect

词性:动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I check my email every morning. = 我每天早上都查看我的电子邮件。
He checks the oil level in his car regularly. = 他定期检查汽车的油位。

一般过去时:
She checked her phone for messages. = 她查看手机是否有信息。
They checked into the hotel late last night. = 他们昨晚很晚才入住酒店。

现在完成时:
I have checked my answers twice. = 我已经检查了两遍我的答案。
She has checked out of the library. = 她已经从图书馆办理了退借手续。

过去完成时:
He had checked the equipment before starting the experiment. = 他在开始实验前已经检查过设备了。
They had checked the flight schedule many times. = 他们已经多次查看了航班时刻表。

将来时:
I will check the weather forecast before I leave. = 我离开前会查看天气预报。
She will check her bank balance tomorrow. = 她明天会查看她的银行余额。

虚拟语气:
If I had checked the map, I wouldn't have gotten lost. = 如果我查看了地图，我就不会迷路了。
I wish I had checked the price before buying it. = 我真希望在购买之前查看过价格。

被动语态:
The documents were checked thoroughly. = 文件被仔细检查了。
The quality of the product is checked regularly. = 产品质量定期检查。

疑问句:
Did you check the tire pressure? = 你检查了胎压吗？
Have you checked your spelling? = 你检查过你的拼写了吗？

否定句:
I didn't check my email this morning. = 我今天早上没有查看我的电子邮件。
He didn't check the facts before writing the article. = 他在写文章之前没有核实事实。
