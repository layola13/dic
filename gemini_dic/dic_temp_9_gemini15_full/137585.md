nontraversable: /ˌnɒnˈtrævəsəbl/ | adj. | 不可穿越的，无法通过的 |  Incapable of being traversed or crossed; impassable.  This refers to a path, route, or area that cannot be passed through or over.  It implies a significant barrier, not simply difficulty. |


例句：

The mountain pass was nontraversable due to the heavy snowfall. = 由于大雪，山口无法通行。

The dense jungle proved to be nontraversable for the explorers. = 这片茂密的丛林对探险家来说是无法穿越的。


近义词：impassable, inaccessible, impassable, unpassable


反义词：traversable, passable, accessible


词性：形容词
