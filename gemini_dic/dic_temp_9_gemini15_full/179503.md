reeds:/riːdz/| n.|芦苇；簧片|Tall, slender plants with hollow stems growing in wet places, often used for thatching or making musical instruments.|The reeds by the river swayed gently in the breeze. 河边的芦苇在微风中轻轻摇曳。|近义词：bulrushes, rushes|反义词:  无
