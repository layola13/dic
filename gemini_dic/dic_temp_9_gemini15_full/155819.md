phosgene:/ˈfɒzdʒiːn/ /ˈfɑːzʒiːn/| n. |光气|A highly toxic, colorless gas with an odor resembling freshly cut hay or green corn, used in chemical warfare.  It is a carbonyl compound with the formula COCl2.| Example: Phosgene was a weapon of mass destruction used in World War I.| 近义词：carbonyl chloride| 反义词：无
