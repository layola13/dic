delvers:/ˈdɛlvərz/| n.|挖掘者；探究者；钻研者|People who delve, especially into something difficult or obscure.  Those who diligently seek knowledge or information. |例句：The delvers in the ancient ruins unearthed a priceless artifact.  古代遗址的挖掘者们发掘出一件无价的文物。

例句：The delvers into the mysteries of the universe are constantly pushing the boundaries of human knowledge. 钻研宇宙奥秘的人们不断突破人类知识的界限。


近义词：researchers, investigators, explorers, scholars, inquirers

反义词：ignorers, neglecters


词性:名词
