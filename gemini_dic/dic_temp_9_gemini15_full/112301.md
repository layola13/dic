lorded:/ˈlɔːrdɪd/| v. | 统治；支配；炫耀；| To rule or control, often in an arrogant or domineering way; to boast about or act superior.  |He lorded it over his employees, demanding absolute obedience.|近义词：ruled, dominated, tyrannized, swaggered|反义词：served, obeyed, submitted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lords it over his colleagues. = 他对同事颐指气使。
She lords her wealth over everyone. = 她向所有人炫耀她的财富。


一般过去时:
The king lorded over his kingdom for many years. = 国王统治他的王国多年。
He lorded his accomplishments over his friends. = 他向朋友们炫耀他的成就。


现在完成时:
He has lorded over this department for too long. = 他在这个部门专横跋扈太久了。
She has lorded her success over us since she got the promotion. = 自从她升职以来，她就一直对我们炫耀她的成功。


过去完成时:
They had lorded over the village for generations. = 他们世世代代统治着这个村庄。
He had lorded his intelligence over everyone in the room before his mistake was exposed. = 在他的错误被揭露之前，他一直对屋里所有人炫耀他的聪明才智。


将来时:
He will lord it over them once he gets the position. = 一旦他得到这个职位，他就会对他们颐指气使。
She will lord her new car over all her friends. = 她会向所有朋友炫耀她的新车。


虚拟语气:
If he were to lord it over everyone, he would soon lose respect. = 如果他想要对所有人颐指气使，他很快就会失去尊重。
I wish he wouldn't lord his knowledge over us all the time. = 我希望他不总是在我们面前炫耀他的知识。


被动语态:
The people were lorded over by a cruel dictator. = 人民受一个残酷的独裁者统治。
His accomplishments were lorded over everyone. = 他的成就被拿出来炫耀给所有人看。


疑问句:
Does he lord it over his subordinates? = 他对下属颐指气使吗？
Will she lord her new achievements over her colleagues? = 她会向同事炫耀她的新成就吗？


否定句:
He doesn't lord it over his employees. = 他不会对员工颐指气使。
She didn't lord her wealth over anyone. = 她没有向任何人炫耀她的财富。
