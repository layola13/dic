empurple:/ɪmˈpɜːrpəl/ | vt. |使变成紫色；使呈紫红色|To make or become purple or purplish.  The verb often implies a staining or dyeing process, or a figurative coloring. |例句：The sunset empurpled the sky.  日落使天空变成了紫色。  The bruise empurpled his arm.  淤青使他的手臂变成了紫红色。|近义词：purple, dye, stain|反义词：decolorize, bleach
