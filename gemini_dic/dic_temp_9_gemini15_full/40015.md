consumable:/kənˈsjuːməbl/ | adj., n. | 消耗品，可消耗的 | Something that can be used up or consumed.  It can refer to goods that are used up quickly, or to something that is consumed or used during a process. |  例句：Paper and ink are consumables. 纸和墨水是消耗品。

The company's main products are consumables for printers. 该公司主要产品是打印机的耗材。


近义词：expendable, expendable supplies | 反义词：durable, permanent


词性: adjective, noun
