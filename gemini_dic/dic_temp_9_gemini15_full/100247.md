interrupter: /ɪnˈtʌrʌptər/ | n. | 干扰者；中断者；干扰器 | A person or thing that interrupts.  Someone or something that disrupts the continuity or progress of something. |

例句：
The constant buzzing of the fly was a real interrupter during the meeting.  苍蝇不断嗡嗡叫，在会议期间造成了真正的干扰。

The unexpected power outage was a major interrupter to our production schedule.  意外的停电严重打乱了我们的生产计划。


近义词：disruptor, interrupter, disturber,  obstacle

反义词：facilitator, helper, assistant


词源:
From "interrupt" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Interrupt" itself comes from  Latin *interrumpere, from *inter- ("between, among") + rumpere ("to break").
