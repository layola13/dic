precontemplating:/ˌpriːkənˈtɛmpleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先考虑，预想 |  To be in the stage of change before considering changing a behavior.  It's the first stage of the Transtheoretical Model of behavior change.  | 例句：He was precontemplating quitting smoking, but hadn't yet made a decision. 他正在预想戒烟，但还没做出决定。 | 例句：She was precontemplating a career change, exploring different options. 她正在预想职业转型，探索不同的选择。 | 近义词：considering, contemplating, mulling over, pondering | 反义词：acting, doing, implementing
