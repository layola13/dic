naturopathy:/næˈtʃʊrəpəθi/| n. |自然疗法|A system of alternative medicine that emphasizes the use of natural remedies and practices to treat illness and promote wellness.  It often involves techniques such as herbalism, hydrotherapy, and dietary changes. |She believes in naturopathy and avoids conventional medicine whenever possible. 她相信自然疗法，尽可能避免常规药物。Many people are turning to naturopathy to address their health concerns. 许多人正在转向自然疗法来解决他们的健康问题。|holistic medicine, alternative medicine|conventional medicine, allopathy|名词
