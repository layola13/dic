collating:/kəˈleɪtɪŋ/| v.|整理，校对|To gather and combine information from different sources into a single, unified whole; to arrange things in order.|例句：The secretary is collating the data for the report. 秘书正在整理报告的数据。|例句：We need to collate all the research findings before writing the conclusion. 我们需要在撰写结论之前整理所有研究结果。|近义词：assemble, gather, compile, arrange|反义词：disperse, scatter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He collates the data every day. 他每天整理数据。
She collates the documents for the meeting. 她整理会议文件。

一般过去时:
They collated the results of the survey. 他们整理了调查结果。
He collated the information from various sources. 他整理了来自各种来源的信息。

现在完成时:
She has collated all the necessary information. 她已经整理好了所有必要的信息。
We have collated the responses and are ready to analyze them. 我们已经整理好了回复，并准备分析它们。

过去完成时:
They had collated the data before the presentation. 他们在演示文稿之前已经整理好了数据。
She had collated all the relevant documents before the meeting. 她在会议之前已经整理好了所有相关的文件。

将来时:
He will collate the information for the presentation next week. 他下周将整理演示文稿的信息。
They will collate the feedback from the customers. 他们将整理来自客户的反馈。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would collate all the documents. 如果我有更多时间，我会整理所有文件。
It would be helpful if he collated the information more efficiently. 如果他能更有效率地整理信息，那就太好了。

被动语态:
The data is collated by the computer. 数据由计算机整理。
The documents were collated before being sent to the printer. 文件在被送去打印之前被整理好了。

疑问句:

否定句:
He did not collate the data correctly. 他没有正确地整理数据。
