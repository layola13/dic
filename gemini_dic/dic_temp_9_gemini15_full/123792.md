/moʊpɑː/ | n. | 拖把 | A mop is a cleaning implement consisting of a handle with a soft absorbent head, typically made of cotton or synthetic fibers, used for washing floors. | Example:  I need a mop to clean this spilled juice. 我需要一把拖把来擦拭洒出的果汁。 | 近义词：broom, swab | 反义词:  None  | 名词


USAGE->

一般过去时:
