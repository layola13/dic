operations:/ˌɒpəˈreɪʃənz/ /ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. | 操作；手术；行动；业务；运作 |The process of performing a series of actions or tasks to achieve a specific goal.  It can refer to a surgical procedure, a military operation, or the day-to-day running of a business. |例句：The surgical operations were a success. 手术很成功。

The daily operations of the company are running smoothly. 公司的日常运营运转顺利。|近义词：activities, procedures, functions, processes, maneuvers |反义词：inaction, inactivity, idleness


USAGE->

The factory operations are efficient. = 工厂的运作效率很高。
Our daily operations include customer service and product development. = 我们的日常业务包括客户服务和产品开发。
