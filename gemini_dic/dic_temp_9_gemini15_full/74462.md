frankenfood:/ˈfræŋkənˌfuːd/| n. |转基因食品；基因改造食品|Genetically modified food, often used in a negative or critical context, referring to food produced from organisms that have had their DNA altered using genetic engineering techniques.  The term evokes the image of Frankenstein's monster, suggesting an unnatural or dangerous creation.|例句：Many people are wary of frankenfood and prefer organically grown produce. 许多人对转基因食品持谨慎态度，更喜欢有机产品。|例句：The debate surrounding frankenfood and its potential health risks continues.  围绕转基因食品及其潜在健康风险的争论仍在继续。|近义词：GMO food, genetically engineered food|反义词：organic food, naturally grown food|名词
