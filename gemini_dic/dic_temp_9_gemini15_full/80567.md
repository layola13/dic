goody-two-shoes:/ˈɡʊdiˌtuːʃuːz/| n. |假道学的人，伪善者|A person who is excessively or affectedly virtuous or goody-goody; a person who is excessively self-righteous and moralistic.  The term is often used sarcastically or derisively.|例句：He's such a goody-two-shoes, always following the rules.|近义词：saint, angel, prig,  goody-goody|反义词：scoundrel, villain, rogue
