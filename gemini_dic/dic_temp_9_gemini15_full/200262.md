skittering: /ˈskɪtərɪŋ/ | v. |轻快地快速移动；（声音）急速轻微地响 |  to move lightly and quickly; to make a series of light, quick sounds |

The leaves skittered across the pavement.  = 叶子在人行道上快速轻盈地移动。

The mouse skittered across the floor. = 老鼠在地板上快速轻盈地移动。


近义词：scurrying, scampering, darting, flitting

反义词：trudging, lumbering, plodding


词源:  The word "skitter" originates from the Old English word "scaterian," meaning "to scatter" or "to disperse."  Over time, the meaning shifted to emphasize the quick, light movement associated with scattering.
