monte:/ˈmɒnti/ /ˈmɑːnti/ | n. |蒙特；山；(意大利语)山脉|A monte is a mountain, or a range of mountains, especially used in Italian.  The term is also used as part of place names, often indicating a mountainous location.|例句：Monte Carlo is famous for its casino. 摩纳哥以其赌场而闻名。|例句：The climbers ascended Monte Rosa. 登山者攀登了玫瑰峰。|近义词：mountain, peak, hill |反义词：valley, plain
