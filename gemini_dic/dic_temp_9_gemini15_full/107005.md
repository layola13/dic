lactam:/ˈlæktæm/| n. |内酰胺|A lactam is a cyclic amide; that is, it is a molecule containing a nitrogen atom bonded to a carbonyl group (C=O) to form a ring.  The ring size is usually described by the number of atoms in the ring, for example, a β-lactam has a four-membered ring.|Example: β-Lactams, such as penicillin and cephalosporin, are important antibiotics.|近义词: cyclic amide |反义词: N/A
