kinked:/kɪŋkt/| adj.|弯曲的；扭曲的；有毛病的 |Having a sharp bend or twist; not straight or smooth.  It can also refer to something that is malfunctioning or problematic. |例句：The wire was kinked and needed to be replaced.  这根电线弯曲了，需要更换。 The system is kinked and needs fixing. 这个系统出了问题，需要修理。|近义词：bent, twisted, crooked, warped, faulty |反义词：straight, smooth, unbent, flawless
