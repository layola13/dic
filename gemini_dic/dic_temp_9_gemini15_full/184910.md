retaliated: /rɪˈtælieɪtɪd/ | v. | 报复；反击 | To return a harmful action or feeling; to take revenge. |例句：The country retaliated against the attack by launching its own missiles.  这个国家通过发射自己的导弹来报复袭击。

例句：She retaliated by refusing to speak to him. 她通过拒绝和他说话来报复。


近义词：revenge, counterattack, reciprocate (in a negative way), avenge | 反义词：forgive, pardon, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retaliates against injustice. 他反抗不公正。
The company retaliates against unfair competition. 这家公司反击不正当竞争。


一般过去时:
They retaliated swiftly and decisively. 他们迅速而果断地进行了反击。
The government retaliated with new sanctions. 政府实施新的制裁作为报复。


现在完成时:
The victim has retaliated by filing a lawsuit. 受害者通过提起诉讼进行了报复。
She has retaliated against her critics. 她对批评者进行了反击。


过去完成时:
He had retaliated before anyone could stop him.  在他人阻止他之前，他已经进行了报复。
They had retaliated by cutting off trade. 他们通过切断贸易进行了报复。


将来时:
The rebels will retaliate if provoked.  如果被激怒，叛乱分子将会报复。
We will retaliate with a strong response. 我们将以强烈的回应来反击。


虚拟语气:
If he were to retaliate, it would only escalate the conflict. 如果他进行报复，只会加剧冲突。
I wish I hadn’t retaliated so harshly. 我真希望我没有如此严厉地报复。


被动语态:
The attack was retaliated against with fierce determination.  这次袭击受到了坚决的反击。
The unfair treatment was retaliated against with a public protest.  这种不公平的待遇受到了公开抗议的回应。


疑问句:
Did he retaliate against their actions? 他对他们的行为进行报复了吗？
How will they retaliate? 他们将如何报复？


否定句:
He did not retaliate against the insult. 他没有对侮辱进行报复。
The company chose not to retaliate against its competitors.  这家公司选择不反击竞争对手。


PHRASE->
retaliate in kind: 以牙还牙
retaliate against: 对...进行报复
retaliate with: 以...进行报复
retaliatory action: 报复行动
retaliatory strike: 报复性打击
