oversparing:/ˌoʊvərˈsperɪŋ/| adj.|过于节俭的，过度节省的|excessively sparing; overly economical or frugal.  It implies a level of frugality that is excessive or detrimental.|The oversparing use of condiments made the dish bland.  过于节俭的调味料使用使这道菜变得平淡无味。|近义词：stingy, miserly, frugal, thrifty|反义词：lavish, extravagant, prodigal


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, therefore it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  The following examples show its use in different sentence structures.

肯定句:
His oversparing nature prevented him from enjoying life's simple pleasures. 他过于节俭的天性使他无法享受生活中的简单乐趣。
Her oversparing approach to decorating made the house feel cold and uninviting. 她过于节俭的装饰方法使房子显得冷清而不友好。

否定句:
