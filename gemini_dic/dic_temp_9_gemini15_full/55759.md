/daɪˈvɜːrsənəsiz/ | n. |多样性；多种多样 | The word "diversenesses" is not a standard English word. It's an irregular pluralization of "diverseness," which itself is a less common synonym for "diversity."  It suggests multiple instances or types of diversity. However, using "diversities" is grammatically correct and preferred. | The study highlighted the diversenesses of opinions on the proposed policy. (While grammatically questionable, it conveys the idea of multiple differing opinions on the policy). | diversities, varieties, multiplicities | uniformity, homogeneity | 名词
