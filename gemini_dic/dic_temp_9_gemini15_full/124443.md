motormouth:/ˈmoʊtərmɑʊθ/| n. |喋喋不休的人，话匣子|A motormouth is a person who talks incessantly and rapidly.  They are often characterized by their inability to stop talking, even when it's inappropriate or unwanted.|例句：He's such a motormouth; I could barely get a word in edgewise. 他是个话匣子，我几乎插不上话。|例句：The motormouth on the TV kept interrupting the news report. 电视上的那个喋喋不休的人一直在打断新闻报道。|近义词：chatterbox, blabbermouth, talkative person|反义词：quiet person, taciturn person|名词
