permittance:/pəˈmɪtəns/| n. |许可；允许；容许|The permittance of the building plan was granted after a thorough review. 建筑规划许可在经过彻底审查后获得批准。|近义词：permission, allowance, authorization |反义词：prohibition, denial, refusal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government permits the construction of new buildings. = 政府允许建造新建筑。
This software permits users to access multiple files simultaneously. = 此软件允许用户同时访问多个文件。


一般过去时:
They permitted the children to stay up late. = 他们允许孩子们熬夜。
The authorities permitted the demonstration to proceed. = 当局允许示威继续进行。


现在完成时:
The authorities have permitted the use of drones for delivery. = 当局已允许使用无人机进行送货。
We have permitted him access to the confidential information. = 我们已允许他访问机密信息。


过去完成时:
They had permitted the access before the security breach. = 他们曾在安全漏洞发生前允许访问。
The manager had permitted the employee to take a leave of absence. = 经理已允许员工休假。


将来时:
The council will permit the development of the new park. = 理事会将允许开发新的公园。
They will permit the use of public transport free of charge. = 他们将允许免费使用公共交通工具。


虚拟语气:
If they permitted it, we could start the project immediately. = 如果他们允许，我们可以立即启动项目。
I wish they permitted me to attend the meeting. = 我希望他们允许我参加会议。


被动语态:
The application was permitted by the board. = 申请得到了董事会的批准。
Access to the system is permitted only to authorized personnel. = 只有授权人员才能访问系统。


疑问句:
Does the law permit the sale of alcohol to minors? = 法律允许向未成年人出售酒精吗？
Will they permit us to enter the restricted area? = 他们会允许我们进入禁区吗？


否定句:
The regulations do not permit smoking indoors. = 规定不允许在室内吸烟。
They did not permit the use of personal electronics during the exam. = 他们不允许考试期间使用个人电子产品。
