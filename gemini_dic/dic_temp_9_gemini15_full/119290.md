micro-engineering:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈɛndʒɪnɪərɪŋ/| n. |微型工程；微细加工|The branch of engineering dealing with the design and manufacture of very small devices and systems, typically involving techniques and tools from nanotechnology.  It encompasses the creation of microscopic structures and systems with precisely controlled properties and functionalities. |例句：Micro-engineering techniques are used to create advanced medical implants. 微型工程技术被用于制造先进的医疗植入物。

例句：Researchers are exploring new applications of micro-engineering in the field of renewable energy. 研究人员正在探索微型工程在可再生能源领域的新的应用。


近义词：microscale engineering, miniaturization, precision engineering |反义词：macro-engineering, large-scale engineering|名词
