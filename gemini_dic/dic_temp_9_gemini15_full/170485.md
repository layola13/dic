pruritus:/pruˈraɪtəs/| n.|瘙痒症，皮肤瘙痒|An unpleasant sensation on the skin that provokes the urge to scratch. It is a symptom of many dermatological conditions and systemic diseases.  |Example:  The patient presented with intense pruritus, particularly at night.  患者表现出剧烈的瘙痒，尤其是在晚上。|近义词：itching, scratchiness|反义词：anesthesia (absence of sensation), numbness|
