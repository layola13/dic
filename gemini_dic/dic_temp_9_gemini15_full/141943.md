ordonnance:/ˈɔːdnəns/|/ˈɔːrdənəns/| n. |法令；条例；命令；药方|An official order or decree, especially one issued by a government or authority.  It can also refer to a prescription for medicine. |例句：The king issued an ordonnance banning public gatherings. 国王发布了一项禁止公众集会的法令。|例句：The doctor wrote me an ordonnance for antibiotics. 医生给我开了抗生素的处方。|近义词：decree, edict, order, prescription|反义词：cancellation, revocation|名词
