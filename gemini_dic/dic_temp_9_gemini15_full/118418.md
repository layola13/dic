词源:
Merry-go-round is a compound word. "Merry" means cheerful and lively, and "go-round" refers to a circular motion.  Its origins trace back to earlier terms like "roundabout" and variations thereof, reflecting its circular nature. The modern form solidified during the 19th century with the development of the popular amusement ride.
