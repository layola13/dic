flinchingly:/ˈflɪntʃɪŋli/ | adv. |畏缩地；胆怯地；退缩地 |  Describes an action performed in a way that shows fear, nervousness, or reluctance. It suggests a slight recoil or withdrawal from something unpleasant or frightening. | 例句：He flinchingly accepted the award, clearly uncomfortable with the attention. 他畏缩地接受了奖项，显然对这种关注感到不舒服。


He flinchingly avoided eye contact with his boss. 他胆怯地避开了与老板的眼神接触。


近义词：fearfully, nervously, hesitantly, reluctantly | 反义词：boldly, bravely, confidently
