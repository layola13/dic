euthanatizes:/juːθənəˈtaɪzɪz/| vt.|实施安乐死|To kill (a person or animal) by painless means, especially to relieve suffering.|例句：The vet euthanatized the suffering dog. 兽医对这只受苦的狗进行了安乐死。|例句：The family decided to euthanatize their terminally ill grandmother.  这个家庭决定对他们患有绝症的祖母实施安乐死。|近义词：put down, dispatch|反义词：save, preserve|动词
