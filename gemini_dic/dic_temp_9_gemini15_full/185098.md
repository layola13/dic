retighten:/ˌriːˈtaɪtən/| vt.|重新拧紧；再次收紧|To tighten something again.  This often implies that the initial tightening was insufficient or that something has loosened.|例句：We need to retighten the bolts on the wheel. 我们需要重新拧紧车轮上的螺栓。

例句：The strap had loosened, so I had to retighten it. 带子松了，所以我不得不重新把它系紧。


近义词：tighten, secure, fasten

反义词：loosen

词性:动词
