exclusivistic: /ɪkˈskluːsɪvɪstɪk/ | adj. |排外的；唯我独尊的；独占的 |

英文解析:  Characterized by or advocating exclusivity; tending to exclude others.  It implies a belief in the superiority of a particular group or system and a desire to keep others out.

例句:

The exclusivistic policies of the club meant that only a select few were allowed membership.  = 这家俱乐部的排外政策意味着只有少数人被允许成为会员。

His exclusivistic attitude alienated many of his colleagues. = 他唯我独尊的态度疏远了他的许多同事。


近义词: sectarian, elitist, exclusive, discriminatory, provincial

反义词: inclusive, open-minded, egalitarian, democratic


词性: adjective
