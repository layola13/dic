drynurse:/ˈdraɪnɜːrs/| n. |保姆；乳母|A drynurse is a nurse who cares for a baby, especially one who is not breastfed.|例句：She hired a drynurse to care for her newborn baby. 她雇了一个保姆来照顾她的新生儿。|近义词：nanny, nursemaid|反义词：None
