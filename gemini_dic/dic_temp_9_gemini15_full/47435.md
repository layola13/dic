deadworks:/ˈdedwɜːrks/| n.|固定构件，永久性构筑物|The permanent structural parts of a building or other structure, as opposed to the movable or easily removed parts.|例句：The deadworks of the bridge included the piers and abutments.  桥梁的固定构件包括桥墩和桥台。


例句：The contractor is responsible for the installation of all deadworks on the site. 承包商负责现场所有固定构件的安装。


近义词：permanent works, fixed works, substructure


反义词：liveworks, movable parts


词性: 名词
