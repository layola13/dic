hostile:/ˈhɑːstaɪl/| adj.|敌对的；不友好的；敌意的|Unfriendly; showing or feeling opposition or dislike; aggressive.  It can refer to people, environments, or situations.|例句：The villagers were hostile to the strangers. 村民对陌生人怀有敌意。

例句：The hostile environment made survival difficult. 恶劣的环境使生存变得困难。

近义词：aggressive, antagonistic, unfriendly, belligerent, inimical

反义词：friendly, amicable, welcoming, hospitable, cordial
