post-graduate: /ˈpoʊstˌɡrædʒuət/ | adj. n. |研究生；毕业后的|  Relating to or denoting studies undertaken after the completion of a first degree at a university or college.  A post-graduate student is one who is studying for a further degree after having already obtained a first degree. |


例句：

He is a post-graduate student at Oxford University. 他是牛津大学的研究生。

She's pursuing post-graduate studies in engineering. 她正在攻读工程学研究生课程。


近义词：graduate (as a noun), postgraduate (alternative spelling)


反义词： undergraduate


词性: adjective, noun
