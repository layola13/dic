inflating:/ɪnˈfleɪtɪŋ/ | v. |膨胀；充气；夸大；使通货膨胀 |To increase the size or volume of something by filling it with air or gas; to exaggerate something; to cause inflation. |例句：The balloon is inflating rapidly.  气球正在迅速膨胀。

The politician is inflating his achievements.  这位政客正在夸大他的成就。


近义词：expanding, swelling, exaggerating, boosting | 反义词：deflating, shrinking, diminishing


USAGE->
