looping:/ˈluːpɪŋ/ | v. adj. n. |循环的；环状的；反复的 | Repeating a process or sequence repeatedly.  |例句：The program is stuck in a looping sequence.  程序卡在一个循环序列中。

例句：The bird was looping through the air.  鸟儿在空中盘旋。


近义词：cycling, repeating, iterative, recurrent | 反义词：linear, straight, single
