cryptic:/ˈkrɪptɪk/| adj.|神秘的，隐晦的，难以理解的|Having a meaning that is mysterious or obscure.  Cryptic messages or statements are difficult to understand because they are not stated directly. |例句：He gave me a cryptic smile and walked away. 他对我神秘地一笑，然后走开了。|例句：The detective found a cryptic note at the crime scene.  侦探在犯罪现场发现了一张隐晦的字条。|近义词：enigmatic, ambiguous, obscure, puzzling|反义词：clear, explicit, transparent, obvious
