missuses:/ˌmɪsˈjuːzɪz/ | n. |滥用，误用 |The misuse of power led to the downfall of the regime. 滥用权力导致了政权的垮台。|近义词：abuse, misapplication, maltreatment |反义词：proper use, correct application


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He frequently misuses his authority. = 他经常滥用职权。
They misuse the company's resources. = 他们滥用公司的资源。


一般过去时:
She misused the funds for personal gain. = 她挪用公款用于个人利益。
The politician misused his influence to gain votes. = 该政客滥用影响力以争取选票。


现在完成时:
They have misused the data in their report. = 他们在报告中滥用了数据。
He has misused his position to gain an unfair advantage. = 他滥用职务以获得不公平的优势。


过去完成时:
The company had misused its resources before the bankruptcy. = 公司在破产前就滥用了资源。
She had misused her trust in him, and it cost her dearly. = 她曾经错信了他，为此付出了惨痛的代价。


将来时:
He will misuse the information to blackmail his rivals. = 他将滥用信息来敲诈他的竞争对手。
They will misuse the technology for illegal purposes. = 他们会将这项技术用于非法目的。


虚拟语气:
If he misused the funds, he would face serious consequences. = 如果他挪用资金，他将面临严重的后果。
I wish they hadn't misused the opportunity. = 我真希望他们没有错过这次机会。


被动语态:
The software was misused by hackers. = 该软件被黑客滥用。
The company's resources were misused by the management. = 公司的资源被管理层滥用。


疑问句:
How was the equipment misused? = 设备是如何被滥用的？
Were the funds misused intentionally? = 资金是被故意滥用的吗？


否定句:
He did not misuse his position. = 他没有滥用职权。
They did not misuse the information. = 他们没有滥用信息。
