slipcase:/ˈslɪpkeɪs/| n. |护套，书套|A protective cover, usually of cardboard or similar material, that slides over a book or other item.  It often features artwork or design elements matching the item it protects. |The book came in a beautiful slipcase. 这本书装在一个漂亮的护套里。|近义词：case, cover|反义词：None
