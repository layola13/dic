deflectors:/dɪˈflɛktərz/| n. |偏转器，反射器 |A device or structure that deflects something, such as a projectile, light, or sound.  Deflectors are used in various applications to change the direction or path of something. For example, in a car, deflectors might be used to redirect airflow, while in a telescope, they might be used to guide light.|The car's deflectors helped to reduce wind noise. 这车的偏转器有助于减少风噪声。|The telescope uses a series of deflectors to focus the light. 这台望远镜使用一系列偏转器来聚焦光线。|reflectors, diverters| absorbers, concentrators |名词
