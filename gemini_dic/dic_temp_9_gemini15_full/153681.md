peonage:/ˈpiːənɪdʒ/| n. |苦役；半奴隶状态 |A system in which someone is forced to work for another person in order to pay off a debt;  a condition of servitude.  It often involved exploitative labor practices and was historically associated with racial or ethnic discrimination. |Example:  The plantation owners used peonage to maintain a cheap labor force.  种植园主利用苦役维持廉价劳动力。|近义词：servitude, forced labor, indentured servitude |反义词：freedom, liberty |名词
