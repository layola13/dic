polyhedrons:/ˌpɒliˈhiːdrɒnz/ /ˌpɑːliˈhiːdrɑːnz/ | n. |多面体 |A polyhedron is a three-dimensional geometric shape with flat polygonal faces, straight edges, and sharp corners or vertices.  It's a solid figure with many sides. |Example: Cubes, pyramids, and prisms are all examples of polyhedrons.  正方体、棱锥和棱柱都是多面体的例子。|近义词：polyhedra (复数形式) |反义词：sphere (球体)
