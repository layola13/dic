multigeneric: /ˌmʌltiˈdʒɛnərɪk/ | adj. | 多属的；多类型的；多基因的 | Relating to or affecting many genera or types.  This term is primarily used in biological contexts, particularly in taxonomy and genetics.  It indicates a broad range encompassing multiple genera or types. | Example: The multigeneric study investigated the evolutionary relationships across several plant genera.  这项多属研究调查了几个植物属之间的进化关系。 | 近义词:polygeneric, multispecific | 反义词:monogeneric, unispecific
