cassareep:/ˈkæsəriːp/| n.|卡萨里普（一种南美调味料）|A thick, dark brown sauce made from the juice of the cassava root, after it has been fermented. It is used as a seasoning in South American cuisine, particularly in Guyana and other parts of the Caribbean.|The cassareep added a unique smoky flavor to the stew.|近义词：condimento (general seasoning)|反义词：无
