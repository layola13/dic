regarding: /rɪˈɡɑːrdɪŋ/ | prep. | 关于；至于；就…而言 | concerning; with respect to; in reference to |


例句：

He called regarding the job application.  = 他打电话来询问工作申请的事情。

We had a long discussion regarding the future of the company. = 我们就公司的未来进行了长时间的讨论。


近义词：concerning, about, respecting, with regard to, in regard to, as regards, as to, on the subject of


反义词：ignoring, disregarding


词性：介词
