conveners:/kənˈviːnərz/| n. |召集人；组织者|People who convene or call meetings together.  They are responsible for organizing and leading a meeting, conference, or event.|The conveners of the conference were very efficient in their planning.  会议的召集人非常高效地完成了他们的计划。|organizers, coordinators, chairpersons|disruptors, disorganizers|名词
