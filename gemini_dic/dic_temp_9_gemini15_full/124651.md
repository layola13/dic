mouthpiece:/ˈmaʊθpiːs/| n.|发言人；话筒；烟嘴 | A mouthpiece is a person, organization, or thing that speaks or acts on behalf of another.  It can also refer to a part of an instrument, such as a musical instrument or a smoking device. |例句：The government spokesperson acted as a mouthpiece for the ruling party.  政府发言人充当了执政党的代言人。

例句：He put the mouthpiece of the saxophone to his lips. 他把萨克斯管的吹嘴放在嘴唇上。

近义词：spokesperson, spokesman, spokesperson, mouthpiece, organ

反义词：None directly applicable,  but could consider  "silent participant"  as a contextual antonym.

词性:名词

一般现在时:
The company uses social media as a mouthpiece for its marketing campaigns. = 公司利用社交媒体作为其营销活动的代言人。
He is the mouthpiece of the organization. = 他是这个组织的发言人。

一般过去时:
The newspaper served as a mouthpiece for the government's propaganda. = 这家报纸充当了政府宣传的工具。
During the trial, the lawyer acted as the mouthpiece for his client. = 在审判期间，律师充当了其客户的发言人。


现在完成时:
This organization has become a mouthpiece for the far-right. = 这个组织已成为极右翼的代言人。
The media has often been accused of being a mouthpiece for the powerful. = 媒体经常被指责是权势者的代言人。


过去完成时:
Before the revolution, the radio had been a mouthpiece for the dictator's regime. = 在革命之前，广播一直是独裁者政权的喉舌。
The newspaper had long served as a mouthpiece for the ruling class. = 这家报纸长期以来一直是统治阶级的代言人。


将来时:
The new CEO will likely become the mouthpiece for the company's vision. = 新任首席执行官很可能会成为公司愿景的代言人。
They will use their platform as a mouthpiece to spread their message. = 他们将利用他们的平台作为喉舌来传播他们的信息。


虚拟语气:
If he were a mouthpiece for the opposition, he would be more critical. = 如果他是反对派的代言人，他会更批判。
I wish the media wasn't such a mouthpiece for corporate interests. = 我希望媒体不要成为公司利益的喉舌。


被动语态:
The politician's speech was used as a mouthpiece for his party's platform. = 这位政治家的演讲被用作其政党纲领的代言人。
The singer's voice was amplified by the mouthpiece of the microphone. = 歌手的歌声被麦克风的吹嘴放大了。


疑问句:
Is this website a mouthpiece for a particular political party? = 这个网站是不是某个政党的代言人？
Who is the mouthpiece for this campaign? = 这个活动的代言人是谁？


否定句:
The journalist refused to be a mouthpiece for the government. = 记者拒绝成为政府的代言人。
The artist's work was not a mouthpiece for any particular ideology. = 艺术家的作品并非任何特定意识形态的代言人。
