furrier:/ˈfɜːriər/| n. |皮货商；皮毛工人|A furrier is a person who makes or sells fur coats, hats, and other fur products. They may also work with the preparation and processing of furs.|The furrier crafted a beautiful mink coat.|近义词：fur dealer, furrier|反义词：无
