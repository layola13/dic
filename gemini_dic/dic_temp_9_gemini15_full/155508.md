philanthropic:/ˌfɪlənˈθrɒpɪk/| adj.|慈善的；博爱的|Showing or motivated by concern for the welfare of others, especially through charitable giving.|The billionaire's philanthropic endeavors have benefited countless individuals and communities.|近义词：charitable, benevolent, altruistic|反义词：selfish, egoistic, miserly
