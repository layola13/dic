dissymmetrical:/ˌdɪsɪˈmɛtrɪkəl/ | adj. |不对称的，非对称的 | Lacking symmetry; not symmetrical.  It refers to a lack of balance or proportion in the arrangement of parts.  This asymmetry can be in appearance or in function. | 例句：The building's design is deliberately dissymmetrical, creating a visually striking effect. 这座建筑的设计故意不对称，创造了一种视觉冲击力。

The arrangement of leaves on the stem was dissymmetrical. 叶子的茎部排列是不对称的。


近义词：asymmetrical, uneven, unbalanced | 反义词：symmetrical, balanced
