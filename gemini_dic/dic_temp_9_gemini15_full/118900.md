metathorax:/ˈmɛtəˌθɔːræks/| n. |后胸|The metathorax is the third segment of the thorax of an insect, located behind the mesothorax and in front of the abdomen.  It typically bears the hind wings (in winged insects) and the third pair of legs.|例句：The metathorax of a bee is significantly smaller than its mesothorax.|近义词：posterior thorax segment|反义词：prothorax, mesothorax
