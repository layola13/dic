fissigemmation:/ˌfɪsɪdʒɛˈmeɪʃən/ | n. |裂芽生殖；裂殖生殖 |The process of asexual reproduction in which a parent organism divides into two or more daughter organisms of roughly equal size.  This is a type of vegetative propagation found in some plants and lower organisms.  |Example: Fissigemmation is a common method of reproduction in certain algae and fungi.|近义词：budding, fragmentation, binary fission |反义词：sexual reproduction
