fatal:/ˈfeɪtl/ | adj. | 致命的；毁灭性的；重大的；决定性的 | causing death; resulting in death; of crucial importance; decisive |


英文解析：The word "fatal" means causing or capable of causing death.  It can also describe something that has extremely serious consequences or is of decisive importance.


例句：
The accident proved fatal to the driver. = 这起事故导致司机死亡。

His fatal flaw was his arrogance. = 他的致命缺陷是他的傲慢。


近义词：deadly, lethal, mortal, disastrous, critical, decisive


反义词：harmless, innocuous, insignificant, trivial


词性：形容词
