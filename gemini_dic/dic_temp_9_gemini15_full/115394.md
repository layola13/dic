manslaughters:/ˈmænˌslɔːtərz/ | n. | 误杀（复数） | The unlawful killing of a human being without malice aforethought.  This is a plural form of manslaughter, referring to multiple instances of the crime.  | Example: The court heard evidence of multiple manslaughters committed by the reckless driver.  法院听取了证据，证明鲁莽的司机犯下了多起误杀案。 | 近义词：homicides (if unintentional), killings | 反义词： justifiable homicide, accidental death (depending on context)
