effectuation:/ɪˌfektuˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 效果；实现；生效 | The process of making something effective; the act of bringing something about.  It emphasizes learning by doing, experimenting, and adapting to changing circumstances. | 例句：The effectuation of the plan required careful coordination.  计划的实施需要仔细协调。

The successful effectuation of the new strategy led to increased profits. 新战略的成功实施带来了更高的利润。| 近义词：implementation, execution, accomplishment, realization | 反义词：failure, ineffectiveness, nullification
