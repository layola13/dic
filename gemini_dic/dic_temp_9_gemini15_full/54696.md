disorganization: /ˌdɪsˌɔːrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |混乱，紊乱，无组织 | The state or quality of being disorganized; lack of order or system.  It refers to a lack of structure, planning, or efficiency in a system, process, or environment. |例句：The disorganization of the office made it difficult to find anything. 办公室的混乱使得很难找到任何东西。

例句：Her disorganization led to missed deadlines and a stressful work environment.  她的混乱导致错过最后期限和紧张的工作环境。


近义词：chaos, disorder, mess, confusion, turmoil

反义词：organization, order, system, efficiency, structure
