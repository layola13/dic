canales:/kəˈnælez/| n.|运河；水道；沟渠|Canales are artificial waterways constructed for irrigation, navigation, or drainage.  运河是为灌溉、航行或排水而建造的人工水道。|The Panama Canal is a famous example of a canal. 巴拿马运河是运河的著名例子。|channels, waterways|land, dry land|名词
