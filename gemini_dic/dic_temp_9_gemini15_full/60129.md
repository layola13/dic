effective:/ɪˈfektɪv/ | adj. |有效的，生效的，有力的 | Producing a desired result; having a noticeable effect.  |例句：The new marketing campaign was highly effective in boosting sales. 新的营销活动非常有效地促进了销售。 |例句：The medicine proved to be effective in treating the illness. 这药证明对治疗这种疾病有效。|近义词：efficient, successful, productive, potent |反义词：ineffective, useless, fruitless, futile


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new strategy is effective. = 新策略有效。
This method is effective in solving the problem. = 这个方法能有效解决问题。

一般过去时:
The treatment was effective. = 治疗有效。
The medicine was effective in relieving pain. = 这药有效缓解了疼痛。

现在完成时:
The changes have been effective in improving efficiency. = 这些改变已有效提高了效率。
Our efforts have been effective. = 我们的努力卓有成效。

过去完成时:
The previous strategy had been effective until the market changed. = 在市场变化之前，之前的策略一直很有效。
The plan had been effective for many years. = 这个计划已经有效实施多年了。


将来时:
The new system will be effective next month. = 新系统下个月将生效。
We believe the new policy will be effective in reducing crime. = 我们相信新政策将有效降低犯罪率。

虚拟语气:
If the plan were effective, we would see results by now. = 如果计划有效，我们现在应该看到结果了。
It would be effective if we implemented the changes immediately. = 如果我们立即实施这些改变，那将会有效。

被动语态:
The problem was effectively addressed. = 问题得到了有效的解决。
The solution was deemed effective. = 该方案被认为是有效的。

疑问句:
Is this approach effective? = 这种方法有效吗？
Will the new drug be effective against the virus? = 新药对病毒有效吗？

否定句:
The treatment was not effective. = 治疗无效。
The previous attempt was ineffective. = 之前的尝试无效。
