locating: /loʊˈkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 定位；找出；查明 |  The act of finding the location of something or someone.  This is the present participle form of the verb "locate".|

例句：

They are locating the source of the problem. = 他们正在查找问题的根源。

The police are locating the missing child. = 警察正在寻找失踪的孩子。


近义词：finding, discovering, pinpointing, identifying, tracing

反义词：losing, misplacing, obscuring


词性：动词（现在分词）


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are locating the missing files. = 我们正在查找丢失的文件。
They locate new customers every week. = 他们每周都会找到新的客户。

一般过去时:
I located the book on the top shelf. = 我在顶层书架上找到了那本书。
She located her keys in her bag. = 她在她包里找到了钥匙。

现在完成时:
We have located the cause of the delay. = 我们已经找到了延误的原因。
They have located the missing documents. = 他们已经找到了丢失的文件。

过去完成时:
He had located the address before he left. = 他离开之前已经找到了地址。
She had located the error in the code before submitting it. = 她提交代码之前已经找到了代码中的错误。

将来时:
We will locate the package tomorrow. = 我们明天会找到包裹。
They will locate the best solution for the problem. = 他们将找到解决这个问题的最佳方案。

虚拟语气:
If I could locate the information, I would send it to you. = 如果我能找到这些信息，我会发给你。
I wish I could locate my lost phone. = 我希望我能找到我丢失的手机。

被动语态:
The lost dog was located by the rescue team. = 失踪的狗被救援队找到了。
The missing documents were finally located in the archive. = 丢失的文件最终在档案馆里找到了。

疑问句:
Can you locate the information you need? = 你能找到你所需的信息吗？
Have they located the source of the leak yet? = 他们找到泄漏源了吗？

否定句:
I couldn't locate the file on my computer. = 我在我的电脑上找不到这个文件。
They failed to locate the missing person. = 他们没能找到失踪的人。


PHRASE->

locate oneself = 安顿下来

locate a business = 设立企业
locate a resource = 找到资源
be located in = 位于...
