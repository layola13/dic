regularising: /ˈrɛɡjələˌraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使规范化；使正规化；调整 |  Making something more regular or conforming to a standard.  The process involves smoothing out irregularities or inconsistencies. |

例句：

The government is regularising the informal economy. = 政府正在规范非正规经济。

The company is regularising its accounting practices. = 公司正在规范其会计实践。


近义词：standardizing, normalizing, regulating, systematizing

反义词：disorganizing, deranging, disrupting


词源:

The word "regularising" is derived from the verb "regularize" which comes from the  French "régulariser" (to regularize) and ultimately from the Latin "regularis" (regular).  The "-ing" suffix indicates the present participle,  implying an ongoing action or process.
