词源:
The word "pickax" comes from the combination of "pick" (a pointed tool for breaking or digging) and "axe" (a cutting tool).  The "axe" part refers to the shape of the head, resembling a small axe.  The spelling variation "pickaxe" is also common and considered equally acceptable.
