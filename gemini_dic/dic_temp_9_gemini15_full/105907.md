kittys:/ˈkɪtiz/| n. |小猫(复数) |The plural form of "kitty," a term of endearment for a cat.  It can refer to multiple cats, especially young ones. |例句：The kitties were playing with a ball of yarn.  小猫们正在玩线球。
例句：Look at all those adorable kitties! 看那些可爱的小猫！|近义词：cats, kittens |反义词：None (it's a plural form)
