pig-headed: /ˈpɪɡˌhedɪd/ | adj. | 固执的，倔强的 |  Stubborn and refusing to change one's opinion or course of action.  It implies a willful and unreasonable persistence. |

例句：

He was pig-headed and refused to listen to any advice. = 他很固执，拒绝听任何建议。

She's become so pig-headed in her old age. = 她老了以后变得非常固执。


近义词：obstinate, stubborn, willful, mulish, intractable

反义词：yielding, compliant, amenable, flexible


词性:形容词
