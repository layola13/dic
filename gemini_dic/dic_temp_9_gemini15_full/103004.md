/ˈdʒɛfərsənz/| n. |杰斐逊家族；杰斐逊的著作或思想|The word "Jeffersons" refers to the family of Thomas Jefferson, the third President of the United States, or to the body of his writings and political philosophy.  It can also be used more generally to refer to things associated with Jeffersonian ideals, such as democracy and republicanism.|Example:  The Jeffersons were known for their intellectual pursuits and commitment to public service.  杰斐逊家族以其学术追求和对公共服务的奉献而闻名。|近义词：Jeffersonian (adj.)|反义词：无
