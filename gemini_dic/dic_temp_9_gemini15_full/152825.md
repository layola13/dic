peculiar:/pɪˈkjuːliər/ | adj. | 奇特的；古怪的；异常的 |  Strange or odd; unusual; different from what is usual or expected.  | The house had a peculiar smell. 这房子有一种奇怪的气味。 |  strange, odd, unusual, quirky | normal, ordinary, common, typical | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This painting has a peculiar style. = 这幅画的风格很独特。
His behavior seems peculiar today. = 他今天的行为似乎很古怪。

一般过去时:
The situation was peculiar and unsettling. = 情况很奇怪，令人不安。
I noticed a peculiar noise coming from the basement. = 我注意到地下室传来一种奇怪的声音。

现在完成时:
Nothing peculiar has happened recently. = 最近没有什么奇怪的事情发生。
She has always had a peculiar way of speaking. = 她一直有一种奇怪的说话方式。

过去完成时:
They had noticed a peculiar pattern in the data. = 他们已经注意到数据中一种奇怪的模式。
I realized that what I had seen was peculiar and unexplained. = 我意识到我看到的东西很奇怪，无法解释。

将来时:
I predict that the results will be peculiar. = 我预测结果将会很奇怪。
This experiment will reveal some peculiar phenomena. = 这个实验将揭示一些奇特的现象。

虚拟语气:
If the situation were peculiar, we would investigate further. = 如果情况很奇怪，我们会进一步调查。
I wish my neighbor's behavior wasn't so peculiar. = 我希望我邻居的行为不要那么古怪。

被动语态:
A peculiar smell was detected in the room. = 在房间里检测到一种奇怪的气味。
The peculiar case was discussed at length. = 这个奇怪的案例被详细讨论了。

疑问句:
Is there anything peculiar about this situation? = 这种情况有什么奇怪的地方吗？
Do you find anything peculiar in this picture? = 你在这张图片里发现什么奇怪的东西了吗？

否定句:
There is nothing peculiar about his reaction. = 他的反应没有什么奇怪的。
This explanation isn't peculiar at all; it's quite common. = 这个解释一点也不奇怪；它很常见。
