foramen:/fəˈreɪmən/ | n. | 孔，裂孔 | A natural opening or perforation, especially in a bone.  | 例句：The foramen magnum is the large opening at the base of the skull.  颅底大孔是颅底的一个大开口。

The foramen ovale allows blood vessels and nerves to pass through. 卵圆孔允许血管和神经通过。

近义词：aperture, opening, orifice | 反义词：closure, obstruction | 词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin foramen, from forare "to bore, pierce".
