half-reasonable: /ˌhɑːfˈriːznəbl/ | adj. | 半合理的；勉强说得通的 |  Partially reasonable; acceptable to a limited extent, but not entirely satisfactory or logical. It suggests a compromise between reasonableness and unreasonableness. |  His explanation was half-reasonable, but I still had some doubts.  他的解释勉强说得通，但我仍然有一些疑问。  |近义词: partially reasonable, somewhat logical,  plausible (but unconvincing) | 反义词: unreasonable, illogical, absurd
