sharpening: /ˈʃɑːrpənɪŋ/ | n. | 磨尖；使锋利；改进；增强 | The act of making something sharper or more effective.  It can refer to the process of sharpening a tool, improving a skill, or enhancing a quality. | 例句：The sharpening of the knives made the chef's work easier. 磨刀使厨师的工作更容易了。


The sharpening of his skills made him a better musician.  他技艺的提高使他成为一个更好的音乐家。| 近义词：honing, refining, enhancing, improving | 反义词：blunting, dulling, weakening

词源:
The word "sharpening" comes from the Old English word "sċearpian," meaning "to make sharp."  This in turn is related to the Proto-Germanic root "*skerpjan," which also gives rise to words like "sharp" in other Germanic languages.
