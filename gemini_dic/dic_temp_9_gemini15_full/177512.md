rebloom:/ˌriːˈbluːm/| v.|再开花；重新繁荣|To bloom again after a period of dormancy or inactivity.  It refers to the act of a plant flowering again after a rest period, or more figuratively, to a renewed period of success or activity after a decline.|例句：The roses are reblooming beautifully after the recent rain.  最近的雨后，玫瑰花又漂亮地开了。

The company is reblooming after a period of financial difficulty.  这家公司在经历了一段时间的财务困境后重新繁荣起来。|近义词：reflourish, revive, regenerate|反义词：wither, fade, decline|动词
