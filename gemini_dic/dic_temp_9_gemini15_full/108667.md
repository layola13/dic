leafletting:/ˈliːflɛtɪŋ/| n. |散发传单；分发传单|The act of distributing leaflets, typically for advertising or political purposes.  Leafleting is a common form of protest and advertising.|例句：The activists engaged in leafletting to raise awareness about climate change.  活动家们通过散发传单来提高人们对气候变化的认识。|例句：The new restaurant relied heavily on leafletting in the neighborhood to attract customers. 这家新餐馆主要依靠在附近散发传单来吸引顾客。|近义词：handbilling, distributing, circulating|反义词：withholding, suppressing|名词
