card-carrying: /ˈkɑːrdˌkæriɪŋ/ | adj. |  持有会员卡的；正式成员的 | Officially belonging to a group or organization, typically possessing a membership card.  This term often implies a strong commitment or adherence to the group's ideology or principles. |


例句：

He's a card-carrying member of the union. = 他是工会的正式会员。

She's a card-carrying Republican. = 她是共和党的正式党员。


近义词：registered, enrolled, official, committed


反义词：unregistered, unofficial, casual, nominal


词性：形容词
