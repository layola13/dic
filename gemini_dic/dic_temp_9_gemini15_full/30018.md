cayenne:/kaɪˈɛn/| n.|卡宴辣椒；卡宴胡椒|A type of very hot chili pepper, originally from South America, known for its intense heat and vibrant red color.  It's often used in sauces, spices, and as a culinary ingredient.|例句：This curry has a strong cayenne pepper flavor. 这道咖喱有浓烈的卡宴辣椒味。|例句：She added a pinch of cayenne to the stew to give it a kick. 她往炖菜里加了一撮卡宴辣椒以增加辣味。|近义词：chili pepper, chili, red pepper|反义词：sweet pepper
