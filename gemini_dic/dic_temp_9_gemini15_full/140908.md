once-overs:/ˈwʌnsˌoʊvər/| n. |粗略检查，快速查看|A quick, superficial examination or inspection.  It often implies a cursory and not necessarily thorough assessment.|例句：The doctor gave the patient a quick once-over before the examination.医生在检查前快速检查了一下病人。


例句：She gave the apartment a once-over before deciding to rent it. 她快速查看了一下公寓后决定租下它。


近义词：inspection, survey, overview, glance, scan |反义词：detailed examination, thorough investigation
