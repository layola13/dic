lemuroid: /ˈlɛmʊrɔɪd/ | adj. | 狐猴似的；似狐猴的 | Resembling a lemur; characteristic of lemurs.  The term often refers to primates belonging to the suborder Lemuroidea, but can also be used descriptively for animals exhibiting lemur-like characteristics. | 例句：The lemuroid eyes of the nocturnal animal helped it see in the dark.  夜行动物的狐猴似的眼睛帮助它在黑暗中视物。  The newly discovered species exhibits several lemuroid features, including a long tail and forward-facing eyes.  新发现的物种展现出几种狐猴似的特征，包括长尾巴和朝前的眼睛。 | 近义词：lemur-like | 反义词：无  | 词性:形容词
