cybersecurity:/ˌsaɪbərˈsɪkjʊrɪti/| n. |网络安全|The protection of computer systems and networks from the theft of or damage to their hardware, software, or electronic data, as well as from the disruption or misdirection of the services they provide.|例句：Strong cybersecurity measures are crucial for protecting sensitive data.  强大的网络安全措施对于保护敏感数据至关重要。

例句：The company invested heavily in cybersecurity to prevent data breaches. 公司大力投资网络安全以防止数据泄露。


近义词：information security, data security, network security|反义词：vulnerability, insecurity|词性:noun
