reappraisal:/ˌriːəˈpreɪzəl/| n. |重新评估，再评价 |The act of reassessing or reevaluating something.  It involves a fresh look at something already considered, often with new information or a changed perspective. |例句：The company's reappraisal of its marketing strategy led to increased sales. 公司对其营销策略的重新评估导致销售额增长。

例句：After the accident, he underwent a reappraisal of his life priorities. 事故之后，他重新评估了自己的生活优先级。


近义词：reevaluation, reassessment, re-evaluation, review |反义词：initial assessment, first impression |词性:名词
