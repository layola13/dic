parallelize:/ˈpærəlaɪz/| vt.| 并行化 | To divide a task or process into multiple parts that can be executed simultaneously, usually on multiple processors or cores.  This improves efficiency and speed by reducing the overall execution time. |例句：We parallelized the image processing task to significantly reduce processing time. 我们对图像处理任务进行了并行化处理，以显著缩短处理时间。

例句：The researchers parallelized their simulations across multiple computer clusters. 研究人员在多个计算机集群上并行化了他们的模拟。|近义词：concurrent, simultaneous, multithread |反义词：serialize, sequential
