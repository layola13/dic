rubblework:/ˈrʌblˌwɜːrk/| n.|碎石堆砌的建筑；乱石堆砌物|Loose, irregular stonework, often made of rubble or broken stones, used in construction, particularly in walls or foundations.  It is characterized by its uneven appearance and lack of precision.|The old castle walls were constructed of rubblework, a testament to its age and the methods of construction employed centuries ago.|近义词：rough masonry, rough stonework|反义词：precise masonry, fine stonework
