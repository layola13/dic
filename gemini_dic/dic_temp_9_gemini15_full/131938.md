/nɑːnˈkɒntrækts/ | n. |非合同；非契约关系 | Agreements or understandings that do not meet the legal requirements of a formal contract.  These may still have some binding force depending on the circumstances and jurisdiction. | Example:  The noncontractual agreement between the two companies was based on mutual trust. 这两家公司之间的非合同协议是基于相互信任的。 The informal arrangement, while not a formal contract, operated as a de facto noncontract. 虽然不是正式合同，但非正式安排实际上起到了非合同的作用。 |近义词: informal agreement, understanding, arrangement |反义词: contract, agreement (formal) |名词
