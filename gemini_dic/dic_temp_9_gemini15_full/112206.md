loophole:/ˈluːpˌhoʊl/| n. |漏洞；法律漏洞；逃脱途径 |A loophole is a flaw or ambiguity in a system, contract, or law that allows someone to avoid a rule or obligation.  It's often used to describe a way to evade taxes or regulations. |The company found a loophole in the regulations that allowed them to avoid paying millions in taxes.  这家公司找到了法规中的漏洞，从而避免支付数百万美元的税款。|flaw, gap, escape clause | airtight system, perfect system |名词
