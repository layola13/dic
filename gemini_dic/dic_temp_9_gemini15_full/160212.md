ponged:/pɒŋd/ | v. |  砰的一声发出；(球)发出砰的一声 | To make a loud, dull thudding sound, like the sound of a ball hitting something.  The word often implies a sudden and forceful impact. | 例句：The ball ponged against the wall. 球重重地撞在墙上。

The heavy box ponged when it hit the floor.  重箱子落地时发出砰的一声。


近义词：thudded, bumped, banged | 反义词：whispered, rustled
