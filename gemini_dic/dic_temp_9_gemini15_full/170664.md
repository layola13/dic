pseudo-rational:/ˈsuːdoʊˈræʃənəl/ | adj. |似是而非的，伪理性的 | Seemingly rational but actually based on unsound logic or faulty reasoning.  It often involves the appearance of logical reasoning but lacks genuine intellectual rigor.  |例句：His argument, while sounding pseudo-rational, ultimately fell apart under scrutiny.  他的论点虽然听起来似是而非，但在仔细审查下最终还是站不住脚。  |近义词：specious, illogical, fallacious, sophistical |反义词：rational, logical, sound, valid
