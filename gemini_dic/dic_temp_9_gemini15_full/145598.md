overconfidence:/ˌoʊvərˈkɑːnfɪdəns/| n. |过分自信，自负| Excessive confidence; the state of being excessively confident.  It often leads to poor judgment and mistakes because the individual underestimates risks and overestimates their abilities. |Example: His overconfidence led him to underestimate his opponent and lose the match.  他的过分自信让他低估了对手，输掉了比赛。|近义词：arrogance, conceit, presumption, self-assurance (in a negative context)|反义词：humility, modesty, self-doubt
