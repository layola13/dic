intercalation:/ˌɪntər.kəˈleɪʃən/ | n. |插入；夹层；闰日；(层状物质中的)嵌入|The insertion of something between other things.  It can refer to the insertion of a layer of material between other layers, the insertion of a day into the calendar, or the insertion of material into a text or sequence. |例句：The intercalation of a new chapter disrupted the flow of the novel.  插入新章节打断了小说的流畅性。

例句：The geological formation showed evidence of intercalation of different rock strata. 地质构造显示出不同岩层的嵌入证据。


近义词：insertion, introduction, inclusion |反义词：removal, extraction, exclusion |词性：名词
