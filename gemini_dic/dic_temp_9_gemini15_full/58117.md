droppers:/ˈdrɒpərz/| n.|滴管；滴液器；（尤指）点眼药水；|A dropper is a small tube with a rubber bulb at one end, used for dropping liquids.  It is often used for administering medicine, especially eye drops or ear drops.  The term can also refer to someone or something that drops things.|例句：The nurse used a dropper to administer the medication. 护士用滴管给病人服用药物。

The doctor prescribed eye drops, and I need a dropper to use them.  医生开了眼药水，我需要一个滴管来使用它们。


近义词：pipette, eyedropper|反义词：无
