grouted:/ˈɡraʊtɪd/| adj.|已灌浆的；已填缝的|Filled or sealed with grout.  Grouting is the process of filling cracks or gaps with a mortar-like mixture.|例句：The tiles were grouted with a grey cement mixture.  瓷砖用灰色水泥混合物灌浆。The cracks in the wall were neatly grouted. 墙上的裂缝被整齐地灌浆了。|近义词：sealed, filled, cemented|反义词：ungrouted
