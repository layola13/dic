It might mimic the structure or function of a glycan without being a true glycan itself.  The prefix "quasi-" indicates a resemblance or approximation. |  The research focused on the effects of quasi-gly compounds on cellular adhesion.  这项研究侧重于类糖化合物对细胞粘附的影响。|  glycomimetic, glycan analog |  (no direct antonym;  "non-glycan" is a contrasting term)| adj.
