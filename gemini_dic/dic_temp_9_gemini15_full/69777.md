fermentability: /ˌfɜːmɛntəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 发酵性，可发酵性 | The ability of a substance to undergo fermentation.  It refers to the ease with which a substance can be fermented by microorganisms.  This is often assessed by measuring the rate and extent of fermentation. | Example:  The high fermentability of the grape juice contributed to the wine's rich flavor.  高葡萄汁的发酵性促成了葡萄酒浓郁的风味。 The study focused on the fermentability of various sugars. 这项研究的重点是各种糖类的发酵性。| 近义词：fermentability,  (Depending on context)  degradability | 反义词：non-fermentability, resistance to fermentation |
