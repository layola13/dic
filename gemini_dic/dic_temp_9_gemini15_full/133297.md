nonexperimental: /ˌnɑːnɪkˈspɛrɪmɛntəl/ | adj. | 非实验性的 | Not involving or based on experiment; observational.  It describes studies or research methods that do not involve manipulating variables or directly testing a hypothesis through controlled experiments. Instead, they observe and analyze naturally occurring phenomena. | Example:  The study used a nonexperimental design, relying on observational data to draw conclusions.  = 这项研究使用了非实验性设计，依靠观察数据得出结论。


近义词: observational, descriptive, correlational


反义词: experimental, interventional
