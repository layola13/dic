overreach:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃ/ | vt., n. | 权力过伸；越权；超出范围 | To exceed one's authority or limits; to try to do too much.  | 例句：The company's attempt to overreach into new markets resulted in financial losses. 公司试图进军新市场，结果导致了财务损失。

The government's overreach in regulating the internet sparked widespread protests.  政府对互联网的过度管制引发了广泛的抗议。


近义词：exceed, trespass, encroach, overstep


反义词：underreach, restrain, limit


词性：动词，名词
