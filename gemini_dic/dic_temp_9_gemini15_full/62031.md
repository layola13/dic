enaunter:/ɪnˈkaʊntər/| v.|偶然遇见，遭遇，邂逅|to meet someone or something unexpectedly or by chance|例句：I enauntered an old friend at the mall.我在商场偶然遇到一位老朋友。|近义词：meet, encounter, stumble upon|反义词：avoid, shun|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I encounter new challenges every day. = 我每天都遇到新的挑战。
We often encounter difficulties in our work. = 我们在工作中经常遇到困难。

一般过去时:
She encountered a problem with her computer. = 她的电脑出了问题。
They encountered a storm during their journey. = 他们在旅途中遇到了风暴。

现在完成时:
I have encountered many setbacks in my career. = 我在我的职业生涯中遇到过许多挫折。
She has encountered similar situations before. = 她以前遇到过类似的情况。

过去完成时:
He had encountered many obstacles before he achieved success. = 在他取得成功之前，他遇到过许多障碍。
They had encountered this problem many times before. = 他们以前多次遇到过这个问题。

将来时:
We will encounter more difficulties in the future. = 我们将来会遇到更多困难。
They will encounter resistance to their proposal. = 他们的提议将会遇到阻力。

虚拟语气:
If I encountered him again, I would apologize. = 如果我再次遇到他，我会道歉。
I wish I had not encountered such a difficult situation. = 我真希望我没有遇到这么困难的局面。

被动语态:
The problem was encountered by many users. = 这个问题被许多用户遇到。
Many difficulties were encountered during the project. = 在项目过程中遇到了许多困难。

疑问句:
Have you ever encountered this kind of problem? = 你以前遇到过这种问题吗？
What challenges will we encounter in this project? = 我们在这个项目中会遇到什么挑战？

否定句:
I did not encounter any problems during the trip. = 我在旅行期间没有遇到任何问题。
They have not encountered this situation before. = 他们以前没有遇到过这种情况。


PHRASE->
