paintboxes: /ˈpeɪntbɒksɪz/ | n. |颜料盒；水彩盒|A paintbox is a box containing paints, usually watercolors or other paints used for painting.  It is often used by artists and children for painting activities.  The paints are typically arranged in small compartments within the box. |The children eagerly opened their new paintboxes, ready to create masterpieces. 孩子们迫不及待地打开他们新的颜料盒，准备创作杰作。|toolbox, palette |  (No direct antonym, perhaps "empty box") |名词
