/iːz/ | 后缀 |  …语；…式；…人 |  This suffix is added to the name of a nation, people, or language to indicate something relating to that nation, people, or language.  It often forms adjectives or nouns. | Examples: Japanese, Chinese, Portuguese | 近义词:  style, manner, type (depending on context) | 反义词:  N/A


USAGE->
This suffix doesn't have tenses as it's not a verb.  The tense is determined by the word it's attached to.  For example:

一般现在时 (using a word formed with "-ese"):
He speaks Japanese fluently. = 他说日语很流利。
The Chinese restaurant is very popular. = 这家中国餐馆很受欢迎。


一般过去时 (using a word formed with "-ese"):
She studied Chinese for five years. = 她学习中文五年了。
They ate delicious Japanese food. = 他们吃了美味的日本料理。


现在完成时 (using a word formed with "-ese"):
I have learned some basic Portuguese. = 我已经学习了一些基本的葡萄牙语。
They have visited many Japanese gardens. = 他们参观了许多日本花园。


过去完成时 (using a word formed with "-ese"):
She had already mastered Cantonese before moving to Hong Kong. = 在搬到香港之前，她已经掌握了粤语。
He had never tasted authentic Taiwanese food. = 他从未尝过正宗的台湾菜。


将来时 (using a word with "-ese"):
I will learn Japanese next year. = 我明年将学习日语。
She will cook a delicious Chinese meal for us. = 她将为我们做一顿美味的中国菜。


虚拟语气 (using a word with "-ese"):
If I spoke Japanese, I would travel to Japan. = 如果我会说日语，我就会去日本旅行。
I wish I understood Chinese better. = 我希望我能更好地理解中文。


被动语态 (using a word with "-ese"):
This dish is often made with Japanese ingredients. = 这道菜经常用日本食材制作。
The translation was done by a native Chinese speaker. = 翻译是由一位以中文为母语的人完成的。


疑问句 (using a word with "-ese"):
Do you speak Portuguese? = 你会说葡萄牙语吗？
Is that a Chinese painting? = 那是一幅中国画吗？


否定句 (using a word with "-ese"):
I don't understand Cantonese. = 我不懂粤语。
He doesn't like spicy Szechuanese food. = 他不喜欢辛辣的川菜。



PHRASE->
The suffix "-ese" doesn't form phrases independently. Phrases are formed by words *containing* "-ese".  Examples:

Japanese culture = 日本文化
Chinese calligraphy = 中国书法
Portuguese wine = 葡萄牙葡萄酒
Filipino cuisine = 菲律宾菜肴
Cantonese opera = 粤剧
