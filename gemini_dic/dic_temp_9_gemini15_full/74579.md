frayed:/freɪd/| adj.|磨损的；（边缘）蓬乱的；紧张的|Something that is frayed has become worn or damaged at the edges, or is starting to become worn.  It can also describe a person's nerves being worn and strained.|例句：The edges of the carpet were frayed.地毯的边缘磨损了。

The frayed rope was barely holding the bucket.  磨损的绳子勉强吊着水桶。


近义词：worn, ragged, tattered, unraveled,  strained|反义词：intact, unworn, smooth, calm


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rope is frayed. = 绳子磨损了。
His nerves are frayed. = 他很紧张。


一般过去时:
The fabric frayed at the seams. = 布料在接缝处磨损了。
He felt his patience frayed after the long wait. = 长时间的等待让他感到耐心耗尽。


现在完成时:
The edges have frayed significantly. = 边缘已经磨损得很厉害了。
My nerves have frayed after dealing with that difficult client. = 和那个难缠的客户打交道后，我的神经都绷紧了。


过去完成时:
The rope had frayed before it snapped. = 绳子在断裂之前就已经磨损了。
His temper had frayed considerably before the argument erupted. = 争吵爆发之前，他的脾气就已经相当暴躁了。


将来时:
The fabric will fray if it's not handled carefully. = 如果不仔细处理，布料会磨损。
I think my nerves will fray if this continues. = 如果这种情况继续下去，我想我会崩溃的。


虚拟语气:
If the rope hadn't frayed, it wouldn't have broken. = 如果绳子没有磨损，它就不会断了。
If I hadn't been so frayed, I would have handled the situation better. = 如果我没有那么紧张，我会更好地处理这种情况。


被动语态:
The fabric was frayed by the washing machine. = 布料被洗衣机磨损了。
His nerves were frayed by the constant pressure. = 他被持续的压力弄得神经紧张。


疑问句:
Is the rope frayed? = 绳子磨损了吗？
Are your nerves frayed after that stressful day? = 经历了那一天的压力后，你紧张吗？


否定句:
The fabric isn't frayed. = 布料没有磨损。
My nerves aren't frayed at all. = 我一点也不紧张。


PHRASE->
frayed edges: 磨损的边缘
frayed nerves: 紧张的神经
frayed temper: 易怒的脾气
frayed emotions: 紊乱的情绪
frayed patience: 耗尽的耐心

词源:
From Old French *freier, from Frankish *frejan, from Proto-Germanic *frajan (“to eat away, gnaw”).  The meaning shifted from “to eat away” to “to wear away at the edges”.
