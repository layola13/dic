porifera:/pəˈrɪfərə/| n. |多孔动物，海绵 |Sponges are simple multicellular animals belonging to the phylum Porifera.  They are characterized by their porous bodies, through which water flows, and their lack of true tissues and organs.  |例句：The Great Barrier Reef is home to a vast array of porifera, showcasing the diversity of sponge life.  大堡礁是多种多孔动物的家园，展示了海绵生命的丰富多样性。

例句：Researchers are studying the chemical compounds produced by certain porifera for potential medicinal applications. 研究人员正在研究某些多孔动物产生的化合物，以寻找潜在的医药用途。


近义词：sponge (海绵)


反义词：无
