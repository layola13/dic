reportingly:/rɪˈpɔːrtɪŋli/| adv.|据报道；据报告说|The adverb "reportingly" means according to reports or as reported. It indicates that the information being presented comes from a news report, statement, or other form of published information.  It's less formal than "reportedly."|例句：Reportingly, the company is facing financial difficulties.  据报道，该公司正面临财务困难。


例句：Reportingly, the storm caused significant damage to the coastal areas. 据报道，暴风雨对沿海地区造成了重大破坏。


近义词：reportedly, allegedly, supposedly


反义词：confirmed, undeniably, verifiably


词性: 副词
