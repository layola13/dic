packhouse:/ˈpækhaʊs/| n.|包装厂；装箱厂|A building where agricultural products are cleaned, graded, packed, and stored before being shipped to market.  It often involves a significant amount of machinery and labor.|例句：The apples were sent to the packhouse for sorting and packaging. 苹果被送到包装厂进行分拣和包装。|例句：The local packhouse employs many seasonal workers during harvest time. 当地包装厂在收获季节雇佣许多季节性工人。|近义词：packing plant, processing plant, packing shed|反义词：farm, field
