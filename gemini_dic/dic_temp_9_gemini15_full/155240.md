phalaenid:/fəˈliːnɪd/| n. | 蛾科昆虫；夜蛾 | A phalaenid is a moth belonging to the family Noctuidae, commonly known as owlet moths.  They are characterized by their nocturnal habits and often drab coloration.  Many species are agricultural pests.| Example: The farmer found several phalaenid larvae damaging his corn crop.  农民发现几只夜蛾幼虫正在破坏他的玉米作物。|近义词：noctuid moth, owlet moth | 反义词：无 |
