pyrolusite:/ˌpaɪrəˈluːsaɪt/| n. |二氧化锰矿;软锰矿 |A black mineral consisting essentially of manganese dioxide (MnO2), the chief ore of manganese.  It is often found in crystalline masses and is used as a source of manganese and in the manufacture of dry cell batteries.|例句：Pyrolusite is an important ore of manganese and is used in various industries. 二氧化锰矿是重要的锰矿石，用于各行各业。|例句：The battery manufacturer sources its manganese dioxide primarily from pyrolusite deposits. 电池制造商的主要二氧化锰来源是软锰矿矿床。|近义词：manganese dioxide, manganese ore |反义词：无
