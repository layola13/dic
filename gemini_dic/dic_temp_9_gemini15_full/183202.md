represcribes:/ˌriːˈpraɪskraɪbz/| vt.|再次开处方，重新开处方|To prescribe (a medicine or treatment) again.  This usually implies a continuation of a previous prescription, rather than a completely new one.|例句：The doctor represcribed the antibiotics for another week. 医生再次开了抗生素的处方，开了另一个星期的。
The patient needed a represcription for their medication. 病人需要重新开药。|近义词：renew, refill, reissue|反义词：discontinue, withdraw
