hermits:/ˈhɜːrmɪts/| n. |隐士，隐居者 |A hermit is a person who lives alone and avoids other people, often for religious reasons.  They typically live a solitary life, often in a secluded place like a cave or a small cabin. |例句：Many hermits lived in the mountains, far from civilization. 许多隐士居住在远离文明的山区。|例句：He chose to become a hermit after the death of his wife. 他妻子去世后选择成为一名隐士。|近义词：recluse, solitary, loner |反义词：socialite, extrovert
