partlet:/ˈpɑːrtlɪt/ | n. |  褶边领,  围嘴 | A close-fitting linen or other fabric covering for the neck and chest, often worn under a gown or other garment, especially by women in the 14th-16th centuries.  It was typically a piece of linen or other fabric that covered the neck and chest, often decorated with lace or embroidery. | Example: The painting showed a lady in a fine gown, with a delicate partlet peeking out from the neckline.  这幅画上画一位穿着精美长袍的女士，精致的褶边领从领口露出来。 | 近义词: collar,  chemisette | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑open neckline)
