The term is primarily used in zoology and anatomy, often referring to specific segments or structures in certain invertebrate animals.  | Example: The postabdominal appendages of the insect were damaged.  昆虫的腹部后附属物受损了。 | 近义词: posterior, caudal (in some contexts) | 反义词: preabdominal
