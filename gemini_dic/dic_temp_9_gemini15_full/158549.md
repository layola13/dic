plesiosaurus:/ˌpliːsiəˈsɔːrəs/| n. | 蛇颈龙 | An extinct marine reptile of the Jurassic and Cretaceous periods, characterized by a long neck, small head, and four flippers.  | Example: The plesiosaurus was a formidable predator in its time.  蛇颈龙在其时代是可怕的掠食者。 |近义词：none (unique genus)| 反义词：none | 名词

词源:
The word "plesiosaurus" is derived from the Greek words  "plesios" (near to) and "sauros" (lizard), referring to its resemblance to lizards.  The name reflects its physical characteristics which were considered similar, but not identical, to lizards.
