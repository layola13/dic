juddering:/ˈdʒʌdərɪŋ/| n. v.|抖动；震动；颠簸|A shaking or vibrating movement.  The car was juddering violently.|The engine was juddering, indicating a problem.|The ground juddered under the weight of the passing train.|近义词：shaking, trembling, vibrating, rattling|反义词：smooth, steady, still|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine judders constantly. = 发动机一直在抖动。
The old car judders on bumpy roads. = 这辆旧车在颠簸的道路上颠簸。


一般过去时:
The building juddered during the earthquake. = 地震时建筑物震动了。
The train juddered to a halt. = 火车嘎吱一声停了下来。


现在完成时:
The machine has been juddering all morning. = 这台机器整个上午都在抖动。
The car has juddered since I bought it. = 自从我买了这辆车以来，它就一直在抖动。


过去完成时:
The bridge had juddered before it finally collapsed. = 桥在最终倒塌之前就已经在抖动了。
The plane had juddered violently before the pilot landed. = 飞机在飞行员降落之前剧烈抖动过。


将来时:
The train will judder as it goes around the bend. = 火车转弯时会颠簸。
The building will judder if there's another earthquake. = 如果再次发生地震，建筑物将会震动。


虚拟语气:
If the engine were juddering, I would pull over. = 如果发动机在抖动，我会靠边停车。
I wish the train wasn't juddering so much. = 我希望火车不要抖动得这么厉害。


被动语态:
The car was juddered by the rough terrain. = 汽车被崎岖的地形震动了。(很少用被动语态)
The camera was juddered, making the video blurry. = 相机抖动，使视频模糊。(更常见于强调相机本身的抖动)


疑问句:
Is the car juddering again? = 车子又在抖动了？
Why is the engine juddering so much? = 为什么发动机抖动这么厉害？


否定句:
The car doesn't judder anymore. = 车子不再抖动了。
The machine didn't judder during the test. = 机器在测试过程中没有抖动。
