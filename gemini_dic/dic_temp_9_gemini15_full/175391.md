ram:/ræm/| n. v.|公羊；撞击；猛击；强行通过；在内存中存储|A ram is a male sheep.  A computer's RAM is its random-access memory.|例句：The ram charged at the fence.  这只公羊冲向栅栏。  The program was too large to fit in the computer's RAM. 这个程序太大，无法放入计算机的RAM中。|近义词：batter, strike, push, butt|反义词：ewe (母羊)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ram charges at the fence. = 这只公羊冲向栅栏。
He rams the door shut. = 他用力关上门。

一般过去时:
The ram charged at the lion. = 公羊冲向狮子。
They rammed the car into the wall. = 他们把车撞到了墙上。

现在完成时:
He has rammed the data into the computer. = 他已将数据输入电脑。
The workers have rammed the road. = 工人们已经夯实了道路。

过去完成时:
The car had rammed into a tree before it stopped. = 汽车撞到树上后才停下。
They had rammed the message home. = 他们已经反复强调了这个信息。

将来时:
The bull will ram the gate. = 公牛将撞击大门。
They will ram the bill through parliament. = 他们将强行通过议案。

虚拟语气:
If he rammed the car, he would be in trouble. = 如果他撞了车，他就会有麻烦。
I wish he hadn't rammed the door. = 我真希望他没有用力关门。

被动语态:
The data was rammed into the hard drive. = 数据被强行写入硬盘。
The message was rammed home repeatedly. = 这条信息被反复强调。

疑问句:
Did the ram hit the wall? = 公羊撞到墙上了吗？
Will they ram the legislation through? = 他们会强行通过立法吗？

否定句:
The ram did not charge. = 公羊没有冲锋。
He didn't ram the door. = 他没有撞门。
