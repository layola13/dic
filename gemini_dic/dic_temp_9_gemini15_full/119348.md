microchips:/ˈmaɪkroʊtʃɪps/| n. |微芯片，集成电路|A microchip is a small, integrated circuit that contains thousands or millions of transistors and other electronic components on a single piece of semiconductor material.  It is used in a wide variety of electronic devices.|例句：Modern smartphones rely on powerful microchips for their functionality. 现代智能手机依赖强大的微芯片来实现其功能。|例句：The development of microchips revolutionized the computer industry. 微芯片的发展彻底改变了计算机行业。|近义词：integrated circuit, chip, semiconductor|反义词：None
