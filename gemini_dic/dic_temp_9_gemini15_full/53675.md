disciplinable: /dɪˈsɪplɪnəbl/ | adj. | 可管教的，可训练的 | Capable of being disciplined; obedient to discipline.  This adjective describes someone or something that is receptive to discipline and can be readily trained or controlled. |

例句：
The young horse proved to be remarkably disciplinable. = 这匹年轻的马证明了它非常容易调教。

The students in that class are highly disciplinable. = 那个班的学生非常听话。


近义词：trainable, manageable, governable, obedient

反义词：unruly, uncontrollable, defiant, intractable

词性：形容词
