humectants:/ˈhjuːmɛktənts/ | n. |保湿剂 |Substances that attract and retain moisture from the surrounding environment. Humectants are commonly used in cosmetics, food, and pharmaceuticals to prevent dryness and maintain moisture content.  |Example: Glycerin and hyaluronic acid are common humectants used in skincare products. 甘油和透明质酸是护肤品中常用的保湿剂。 |近义词：moisturizers, emollients |反义词：desiccants
