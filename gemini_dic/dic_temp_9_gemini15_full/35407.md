co-opted:/koʊˈɑːptɪd/ | v. | 吸收；收编；利用；篡夺 | To elect or select someone to join a group, especially without their consent or election.  This can also imply subtly influencing or manipulating someone to adopt a particular viewpoint or course of action. |

例句：

The committee co-opted several new members without their prior knowledge. = 委员会在未经他们事先知情的情况下吸收了几名新成员。

The government co-opted the opposition leader by offering him a cabinet position. = 政府通过向反对派领袖提供内阁职位来收买他。


近义词：recruit, enlist, appropriate, usurp

反义词：reject, exclude, expel
