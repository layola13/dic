oversocialize: /ˌoʊvərˈsoʊʃəlˌaɪz/ | v. | 过度社交；使过度社交 | To engage in excessive socializing or to cause someone to engage in excessive socializing.  It implies a level of socializing that is detrimental to one's well-being or other aspects of life. |

例句：

He oversocialized at the party and felt exhausted the next day. 他在派对上过度社交，第二天感到精疲力尽。

She worried that her children were being oversocialized by their overly busy schedule. 她担心孩子们因为过于忙碌的日程安排而过度社交。


近义词：over-network, overstimulate (in the context of social interaction)

反义词：isolate, seclude, withdraw (from social interaction)

词性:动词

词源:

The word "oversocialize" is a relatively modern coinage, formed by adding the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") to the verb "socialize."  "Socialize" itself derives from "social," ultimately from the Latin "socialis," meaning "of or pertaining to society."
