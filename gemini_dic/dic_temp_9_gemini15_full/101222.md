invitar: /ɪnviˈtɑːr/ | v. | 邀请 | To ask (someone) to come to a social event.  | 例句：I invited all my friends to my birthday party. 我邀请了所有朋友来参加我的生日派对。  | 例句：She invited him to dinner. 她邀请他共进晚餐。 | 近义词：ask, request, summon | 反义词：reject, refuse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I invite you to my party. = 我邀请你参加我的派对。
They invite guests to their home. = 他们邀请客人到他们家做客。

一般过去时:
He invited me to the cinema. = 他邀请我去电影院。
We invited them for a drink. = 我们邀请他们喝一杯。

现在完成时:
I have invited everyone to the wedding. = 我已经邀请了所有人参加婚礼。
She has invited her family for Christmas. = 她已经邀请家人过圣诞节。

过去完成时:
They had invited all their relatives before the announcement. = 他们早在宣布之前就邀请了所有亲戚。
He had invited her to the concert, but she couldn't go. = 他曾邀请她去音乐会，但她去不了。

将来时:
I will invite you to my new house next week. = 我下周会邀请你来我的新家。
They will invite the speakers to the conference. = 他们将邀请演讲者参加会议。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would invite everyone. = 如果我有更多时间，我会邀请所有人。
I wish I could invite all of my friends. = 我真希望我能邀请所有朋友。

被动语态:
We were invited to a wedding last weekend. = 上周末我们被邀请参加一场婚礼。
The guests were invited to the ceremony. = 客人们被邀请参加仪式。

疑问句:
Did she invite you to the party? = 她邀请你参加聚会了吗？
Will they invite us to their celebration? = 他们会邀请我们参加他们的庆祝活动吗？

否定句:
I didn't invite him to the meeting. = 我没有邀请他参加会议。
They haven't invited us to their house yet.  = 他们还没邀请我们去他们家。


PHRASE->
invite someone to do something = 邀请某人做某事
invite someone over = 邀请某人过来
invite someone out = 邀请某人出去
a formal invitation = 正式邀请
a warm invitation = 热情的邀请
