pluralisms:/ˈplʊərəlɪzəm/| n. |多元主义；多元论|The acceptance of many different beliefs, ideas, or practices within a society or group.|例句：The country is characterized by its cultural pluralism.  这个国家以其文化多元化而闻名。|例句：Political pluralism is essential for a healthy democracy. 政治多元化对健康的民主至关重要。|近义词：diversity, multiculturalism, inclusiveness|反义词：monism, uniformity, homogeneity
