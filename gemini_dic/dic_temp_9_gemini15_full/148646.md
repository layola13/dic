overthronged: /ˌoʊvərˈθrɒŋd/ | adj. | 挤满的，拥挤不堪的 | Extremely crowded; packed.  The adjective "overthronged" describes a place or situation where there are far too many people, resulting in a feeling of being cramped and overwhelmed. It suggests a level of crowding that goes beyond simply being busy or crowded. |例句：The concert hall was overthronged; people were squeezed together like sardines. 音乐厅里挤满了人；人们像沙丁鱼一样挤在一起。

The narrow streets were overthronged with shoppers during the holiday season. 节日期间，狭窄的街道挤满了购物的人。


近义词： overcrowded, congested, packed, jammed, teeming


反义词：empty, deserted, sparse, uncrowded


词性: adjective
