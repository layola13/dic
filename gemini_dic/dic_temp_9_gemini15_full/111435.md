llano:/ˈlɑːnoʊ/ | n. |广阔平原，大草原 | A llano is a vast, flat, treeless plain, especially one in the southwestern US or South America.  The term is often used in Spanish-speaking regions to describe these types of landscapes. |Example: The cowboys rode across the vast llano, their silhouettes stark against the setting sun.  牛仔们骑马穿过广阔的平原，他们的身影在落日的映衬下显得格外清晰。|近义词：plain, prairie, pampas, steppe |反义词：mountain, hill, highlands |名词
