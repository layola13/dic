rubellas: /ruˈbɛləz/ | n. |风疹| Rubella, also known as German measles, is a mild viral infection.  It is characterized by a rash, fever, and swollen lymph nodes.  While generally mild in adults, rubella can cause serious birth defects if contracted during pregnancy. |The doctor confirmed that the rash was caused by rubella.|近义词：German measles|反义词：无
