protrudes:/proʊˈtruːdz/ | v. |突出，伸出|To extend beyond or above a surface or boundary.|The roof protrudes from the top of the building. 屋顶从建筑物的顶部突出。|近义词：extend, project, jut|反义词：recede, retract


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The branch protrudes from the wall. = 树枝从墙上伸出来。
His teeth protrude slightly. = 他的牙齿略微突出。


一般过去时:
A nail protruded from the tire. = 一个钉子从轮胎里伸出来。
The handle protruded from the bag. =  把手从包里伸出来。


现在完成时:
The rock has protruded from the cliff for centuries. = 这块岩石从悬崖上突出已有数百年了。
Several nails have protruded from the wooden box. =  几个钉子从木箱里伸出来了。


过去完成时:
The wire had protruded before the accident. = 事故发生前，电线就已经突出来了。
The branch had protruded into the road before the car hit it. =  树枝在汽车撞到它之前就已经伸到路上了。


将来时:
The new building will protrude from the skyline. = 新建筑将从天际线上突出。
That tree will protrude from the window soon. = 那棵树很快就会从窗户伸出来。


虚拟语气:
If the rock protruded further, it would be dangerous. = 如果岩石进一步突出，那就危险了。
I wish that nail wouldn't protrude so much. = 我希望那个钉子不要那么突出。


被动语态:
The nail was protruded from the wood. = 钉子从木头里伸了出来。(被动语态表达略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The sign was protruded into the street. = 标志伸到了街上。(被动语态表达略显不自然，主动语态更常用)


疑问句:
Does anything protrude from the package? = 包裹里有什么东西突出来吗？
Does his stomach protrude? = 他的肚子突出吗？


否定句:
Nothing protruded from the box. = 箱子里没有任何东西突出。
His jaw didn't protrude. = 他的下巴没有突出。


PHRASE->
protrude from = 从...突出
protrude into = 突出到...
protrude out = 伸出来
significantly protrude = 显著突出
slightly protrude = 轻微突出
