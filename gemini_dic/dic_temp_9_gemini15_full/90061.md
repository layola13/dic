host:/hoʊst/ /həʊst/| n. v. |主人；主持人；寄主；主办；主持|


英文解析:As a noun, "host" refers to a person who receives or entertains guests.  It can also refer to a living organism on or in which a parasite or commensal organism lives. As a verb, "host" means to act as host or to organize and provide for an event.


例句:

He is a gracious host who always makes his guests feel welcome. 他是一位好客的主人，总是让他的客人感到宾至如归。

The hotel will host a conference next month.  这家酒店下个月将主办一场会议。


近义词: (noun)  master, entertainer,  landlord; (verb) organize,  conduct,  present


反义词: (noun) guest, visitor; (verb) attend, participate


词性: noun, verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hosts a radio show every morning. 他每天早上主持一个广播节目。
She hosts dinner parties every weekend. 她每个周末都举办晚宴。


一般过去时:
They hosted a successful fundraising event. 他们举办了一场成功的筹款活动。
He hosted the Olympic Games. 他主持了奥运会。


现在完成时:
She has hosted numerous international conferences. 她已主持过多次国际会议。
They have hosted exchange students for many years. 他们多年来一直接待交换生。


过去完成时:
The hotel had hosted many famous guests before its closure. 这家酒店在关闭之前接待过许多名客人。
Before the party, she had hosted a rehearsal dinner. 派对之前，她举办了一场彩排晚宴。


将来时:
We will host a Christmas party this year. 今年我们将举办一个圣诞派对。
The university will host a visiting professor next semester. 大学下学期将接待一位访问教授。


虚拟语气:
If he were to host the event, it would be a great success. 如果他来主持这次活动，将会非常成功。
I wish I could host a bigger party. 我真希望我能举办一个更大的派对。


被动语态:
The event was hosted by a well-known celebrity. 这场活动由一位著名的名人主持。
The conference is being hosted in London. 会议在伦敦举行。


疑问句:
Who is hosting the party? 谁在主持聚会？
Will you be hosting the next meeting? 你会主持下一次会议吗？


否定句:
He didn't host the meeting. 他没有主持会议。
They won't host the conference this year. 他们今年不会主办这次会议。
