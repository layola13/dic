herbless:/ˈhɜːrbləs/ | adj. | 无草的；不长草的 | Lacking herbs; without herbs.  The herbless landscape felt barren and desolate.  | 近义词：herbless, barren, desolate, sterile | 反义词：herbaceous, verdant, lush
