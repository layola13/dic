donkeys:/ˈdɒŋkiːz/ | n. |驴；笨蛋 | A donkey is a domesticated hoofed mammal, similar to a horse but smaller and with longer ears.  The term is also used informally as a derogatory term for a stubborn or foolish person. | 例句：The farmer’s donkeys carried heavy loads across the field.  那个农民的驴子驮着沉重的货物穿过田野。 | 例句： Don't be such a donkey!  别那么傻！ |近义词：ass, mule | 反义词： (无直接反义词，但可以根据语境用horse, intelligent等词来表达对比)
