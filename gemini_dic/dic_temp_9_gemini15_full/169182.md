proliferates:/ˈprɒlɪfəreɪts/ /ˈprɑːlɪfəreɪts/ | v. |迅速繁殖；激增；扩散 |To increase rapidly in number; to multiply or spread rapidly.  |例句：Cancer cells proliferate rapidly, making early detection crucial.  癌症细胞迅速增殖，使得早期检测至关重要。

例句：Rumors proliferated throughout the town after the accident. 事故发生后，谣言在镇上迅速传播。

近义词：multiply, increase, spread, expand, mushroom

反义词：decrease, diminish, dwindle, decline, stagnate

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The population proliferates in the city. = 城市人口正在迅速增长。
The weeds proliferate in the garden. = 杂草在花园里迅速蔓延。

一般过去时:
The company's products proliferated in the market. = 公司的产品在市场上迅速增多。
The bacteria proliferated in the warm conditions. = 细菌在温暖的环境中迅速繁殖。

现在完成时:
The number of internet users has proliferated since the invention of smartphones. = 自从智能手机发明以来，互联网用户数量激增。
The rumors have proliferated throughout the country. = 谣言已在全国各地迅速传播。

过去完成时:

The invasive species had proliferated across the entire region before anyone noticed. = 这种入侵物种在任何人注意到之前就已经遍布整个地区。


将来时:
Experts predict that the use of AI will proliferate in the coming years. = 专家预测人工智能的使用将在未来几年迅速普及。
They believe that the problem will proliferate if not addressed immediately. = 他们认为，如果不立即解决这个问题，问题将会迅速恶化。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
