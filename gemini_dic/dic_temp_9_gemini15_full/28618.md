caret's:/ˈkærɪts/| n.|插入符，脱字号|A caret is a symbol (^) used to indicate a place where something should be inserted or corrected, often used in typesetting and proofreading.  It's also used in programming and other contexts to mark a location or perform operations.|Example:  The editor used a caret to show where to add the missing word. 编辑用插入符标明了添加缺失单词的地方。|近义词：insertion mark, indicator|反义词:None.
