overexert:/ˌoʊvərɪˈzɜːrt/| vt.|过度劳累；使过度疲劳|To exert oneself to excess; to strain or fatigue excessively.  |例句：He overexerted himself during the marathon and had to drop out. 他在马拉松比赛中过度劳累，不得不退出比赛。  |例句： Don't overexert yourself trying to finish everything today; take breaks. 不要为了今天完成所有事情而过度劳累；休息一下。|近义词：exhaust, tire, strain, overwork|反义词：rest, relax, conserve energy|动词
