help yourselves to:/hɛlp jɔːrˈsɛlvz tuː/|/ v. phr.|请自便，随便吃/喝|This phrase is an invitation to guests to take something, typically food or drink, freely.  It implies generosity and a welcoming atmosphere.  The "yourselves" indicates that the invitation is extended to multiple people. |例句：Help yourselves to the snacks on the table.  请随意享用桌上的零食。

例句：Help yourselves to the drinks in the fridge. 请自便，冰箱里的饮料随意拿。


近义词：serve yourselves, feel free to take, take as much as you like,  go ahead


反义词：Don't touch, keep away from


词性:动词短语
