desiderative:/dɪˈzaɪərətɪv/| adj.|渴望的；愿望的；想得到的|Expressing or relating to a wish or desire.  It often refers to a grammatical mood or verb form expressing desire or wish.|The desiderative mood is less common in modern English, but it's seen in some older forms of the language and remains in other languages.  He has a desiderative heart, always longing for something more.|近义词：desiring, longing, yearning|反义词：uncaring, apathetic, indifferent|形容词

词源:
