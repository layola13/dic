pyrognostic: /ˌpaɪroʊˈnɒstɪk/ | adj. | 火山的；预示火山爆发的 | Relating to the prediction of volcanic eruptions or the science of such prediction.  The word combines "pyro-" (fire) and "-gnostic" (knowing, predicting). | Example:  The pyrognostic signs, such as increased seismic activity, were carefully monitored.  火山预兆，例如地震活动加剧，受到了仔细监测。| 近义词：volcanological (in the context of prediction), predictive (of volcanic activity) | 反义词：unpredictive (of volcanic activity)
