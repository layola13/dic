process:/ˈprɑːses/ /ˈprəʊses/| n. v.|过程；处理；加工|The process of making cheese is complex. 制造奶酪的过程很复杂。|To process data. 处理数据。|procedure, method, technique|chaos, disorder|n. v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory processes tons of grain every day. = 工厂每天加工数吨谷物。
She processes information very quickly. = 她处理信息的速度很快。


一般过去时:
They processed the film last night. = 他们昨晚冲洗了胶卷。
The application was processed quickly. = 申请很快就被处理了。


现在完成时:
We have processed all the applications. = 我们已经处理完所有申请了。
She has processed the data for the report. = 她已经为报告处理了数据。


过去完成时:
They had processed the order before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经处理好了订单。
The company had processed millions of transactions that year. = 那一年公司已经处理了数百万笔交易。


将来时:
We will process your order within 24 hours. = 我们将在24小时内处理您的订单。
They will process the payment tomorrow. = 他们明天将处理付款。


虚拟语气:
If we processed the data differently, we might get better results. = 如果我们用不同的方法处理数据，可能会得到更好的结果。
I wish I had processed the information more carefully. = 我真希望我当时更仔细地处理了信息。


被动语态:
The application was processed within a week. = 申请在一周内得到了处理。
The data is being processed by a new algorithm. = 数据正在由一种新的算法进行处理。


疑问句:
How do you process your emotions? = 你如何处理你的情绪？
How is this information processed? = 这个信息是如何处理的？


否定句:
They did not process the request in time. = 他们没有及时处理请求。
The computer could not process the data due to a system error. = 由于系统错误，计算机无法处理数据。


PHRASE->
processing power: 处理能力
process improvement: 流程改进
due process: 正当程序
manufacturing process: 制造过程
decision-making process: 决策过程

词源:
From Old French *proces, from Latin processus (“a going forward, advance, progress”), from procedere (“to proceed, advance”), from pro- (“forward”) + cedere (“to go, yield”).
