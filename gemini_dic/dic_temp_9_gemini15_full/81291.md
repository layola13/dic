grants-in-aid:/ɡrænts ɪn eɪd/| n.|拨款，补助金|Financial assistance given by a government, organization, or individual to another entity for a specific purpose, typically without the expectation of repayment.|例句：The university received a substantial grant-in-aid for its research project.  大学收到了对其研究项目的大笔拨款。|例句：The government provides grants-in-aid to support local arts programs. 政府提供拨款以支持地方艺术项目。|近义词：subsidy, grant, funding, subvention|反义词：debt, loan|名词
