kamsin:/ˈkæmsɪn/| n.|  热风，南风 | A hot, dry wind blowing from the desert in Egypt and other parts of North Africa.  It is often associated with sandstorms and extreme heat.  |  The kamsin blew sand into our eyes.  热风吹沙子进了我们的眼睛。  The approaching kamsin caused temperatures to soar.  逼近的南风导致气温飙升。|sirocco, khamsin|  (暂无明确反义词，可考虑gentle breeze)|名词
