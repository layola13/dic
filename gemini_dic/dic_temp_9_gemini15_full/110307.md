词源:
The word "light-footedness" is a compound word.  "Light" means not heavy, and "footed" refers to feet. "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns of state or quality.  Therefore, it literally means "the state of having light feet," which figuratively represents agility and grace in movement.  The word's etymology traces back to the Old English words "lēoht" (light) and "fōt" (foot).
