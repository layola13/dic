interculturally:/ˌɪntərˈkʌltʃərəli/| adv.|跨文化地，在不同文化之间|Adverb describing actions or processes that occur between or involve different cultures.  It emphasizes the interaction and exchange between various cultural groups.|例句：The project aims to promote intercultural understanding.  这个项目旨在促进跨文化理解。

例句：The company encourages intercultural communication among its employees. 这家公司鼓励员工之间进行跨文化交流。


近义词：cross-culturally, transnationally


反义词：monoculturally, within one culture


词性：副词
