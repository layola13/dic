self-tortured: /ˈsɛlf ˈtɔːrtʃərd/ | adj. | 自我折磨的，受自我折磨的 |  Describes someone who inflicts pain or suffering upon themselves, either physically or emotionally.  It suggests a prolonged state of self-inflicted distress. | Example: He was a self-tortured artist, driven by inner demons to create masterpieces at the cost of his own well-being.  他是一位自我折磨的艺术家，被内心的恶魔驱使，以牺牲自己的幸福为代价创作杰作。 |近义词：self-destructive, self-flagellating, tormented, anguished | 反义词：serene, peaceful, content, carefree | 词性：形容词
