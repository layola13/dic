floatation:/floʊˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 浮选；漂浮；浮力 | The process or state of floating.  It can refer to the process of separating materials based on their density, or simply the act of something floating. | 例句：The mineral was separated from the ore by flotation.  矿物通过浮选法从矿石中分离出来。

例句：The buoy maintained its flotation despite the strong waves.  尽管波浪很大，浮标仍然保持漂浮。


近义词：buoyancy, suspension | 反义词：submersion, sinking | 词性:名词
