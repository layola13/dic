precompelled:/ˌpriːkəmˈpɛld/ | adj. | 预先强迫的；被预先迫使的 |  Precompelled describes something that has been forced or compelled beforehand.  It implies a lack of choice or freedom in the action that follows.  | Example:  He felt precompelled to confess, even though he knew it would have negative consequences. 他感到被迫承认，即使他知道这会带来负面后果。 | 近义词：predetermined, compelled, forced, obligated | 反义词：voluntary, optional, free
