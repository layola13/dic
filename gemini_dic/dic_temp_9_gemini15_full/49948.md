dendrologist:/dɛnˈdrɒlədʒɪst/| n. |树木学家，植物学家（专门研究树木的）|A dendrologist is a scientist who studies trees and woody plants.  They are experts in the identification, classification, and biology of trees.  Their work might involve research into tree diseases, forest management, or the historical development of forests.|例句：The dendrologist identified the rare species of oak tree.  树木学家鉴定了这种稀有的橡树品种。

例句：The consulting dendrologist helped the city plan for sustainable forestry practices.  咨询树木学家帮助城市规划可持续的林业实践。|近义词：botanist, arborist|反义词：无
