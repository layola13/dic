intellectualist: /ˌɪntəlɛkˈtʃuəlɪst/ | n. |知识分子；智力主义者 | A person who is highly intellectual and values intellectual pursuits.  Someone who emphasizes intellect and reason over other aspects of life.  They often engage in abstract thought and philosophical discussions. |例句：He considered himself an intellectualist, spending his evenings reading philosophy and debating current events. 他认为自己是个知识分子，晚上花时间阅读哲学书籍并讨论时事。  Many intellectualists gather at the university's philosophy department. 许多知识分子聚集在大学的哲学系。|近义词：intellectual, scholar, academic, highbrow |反义词：ignoramus, philistine, simpleton
