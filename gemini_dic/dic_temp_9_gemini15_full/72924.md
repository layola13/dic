fonding:/ˈfɒndɪŋ/| v.|喜爱，溺爱，珍爱|The act of being fond of someone or something; showing affection or liking.  It often implies a degree of indulgence or over-protectiveness.|例句：She was fonding her new puppy, showering it with cuddles and treats. 她非常喜爱她的新小狗，给了它很多拥抱和零食。|例句：The parents were fonding their only child, perhaps to a fault.  父母非常溺爱他们的独生子，也许有点过头了。|近义词：cherishing, doting, adoring|反义词：despising, neglecting, ignoring|动词
