overshoots:/ˌoʊvərˈʃuːts/ | vt. |超过；越过；射过头；超出预算 |To go beyond or exceed a limit, target, or expectation.  |例句：The plane overshot the runway.  飞机跑过了跑道。

The project overshot its budget by 10%.  项目超支了10%。|近义词：exceed, surpass, go beyond |反义词：undershoot, fall short of


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overshoots the mark consistently. = 他总是超过目标。
The company consistently overshoots its sales targets. = 这家公司总是超过其销售目标。


一般过去时:
The arrow overshot the target. = 箭射过了靶子。
The driver overshot the turn and had to back up. = 司机转弯转过了头，不得不倒车。


现在完成时:
The project has overshot its deadline. = 项目已经超过了截止日期。
We have overshot our budget for the month. = 我们这个月的预算超支了。


过去完成时:
They had overshot their planned expenses. = 他们已经超过了计划的支出。
The plane had overshot the landing strip and crashed. = 飞机跑过了着陆带，坠毁了。


将来时:
The rocket will overshoot its trajectory if the engines fail. = 如果发动机失效，火箭将偏离轨道。
I think we will overshoot our delivery deadline. = 我想我们会超过我们的交货期限。


虚拟语气:
If they had been more careful, they would not have overshot the budget. = 如果他们更小心一点，就不会超支了。
I wish I hadn't overshot my mark in the presentation. = 我真希望我在演示中不要说得太过火。


被动语态:
The target was overshot by the projectile. = 弹丸射过了目标。
The budget was overshot due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，预算超支了。


疑问句:
Did the missile overshoot its target? = 导弹是否射过了目标？
Has the construction project overshot its budget? = 建设项目是否超支了？


否定句:
The pilot did not overshoot the runway. = 飞行员没有跑过跑道。
The archer did not overshoot the target this time. = 弓箭手这次没有射过靶子。


PHRASE->
overshoot the mark: 过度；做得过火
overshoot the runway: 跑出跑道
overshoot the deadline: 超过最后期限
overshoot the target: 超过目标
overshoot the budget: 超支


词源:
over- (表示“超过”) + shoot (射击，投掷)  表示“超过目标射击”引申出“超过”，“越过”等含义。
