outlawing: /ˈaʊtˌlɔːɪŋ/ | v. |宣布…为非法；禁止；取缔 | The act of making something illegal; prohibiting something.  | 例句：The government is considering outlawing the sale of certain weapons. 政府正在考虑禁止销售某些武器。 | 例句：The city council voted to outlaw smoking in public places. 市议会投票决定禁止在公共场所吸烟。 | 近义词：prohibiting, banning, forbidding, making illegal | 反义词：legalizing, permitting, allowing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government outlaws the sale of illegal drugs. = 政府取缔非法药物的销售。
They outlaw discrimination based on race and religion. = 他们禁止基于种族和宗教的歧视。


一般过去时:
The city outlawed smoking in restaurants last year. = 去年，该市禁止在餐馆吸烟。
They outlawed the practice of child labor. = 他们取缔了童工的现象。


现在完成时:
Many countries have outlawed the use of landmines. = 许多国家已经禁止使用地雷。
The organization has been working to outlaw the death penalty. = 该组织一直在努力废除死刑。


过去完成时:
Before the reform, they had outlawed public gatherings. = 在改革之前，他们已经禁止了公众集会。
They had outlawed the use of that chemical long before the accident. = 在事故发生很久之前，他们就已经禁止使用这种化学物质了。


将来时:
The new law will outlaw the production of harmful chemicals. = 新法律将禁止生产有害化学物质。
They will outlaw the practice of bribery in the near future. = 他们将在不久的将来取缔贿赂行为。


虚拟语气:
If they outlawed plastic bags, it would reduce pollution. = 如果他们禁止使用塑料袋，就会减少污染。
I wish they outlawed the use of fireworks. = 我希望他们禁止使用烟花。


被动语态:
The import of certain goods was outlawed. = 某些商品的进口被禁止了。
Smoking is being outlawed in more and more public places. = 越来越多的公共场所正在禁止吸烟。


疑问句:
Will they outlaw the use of this technology? = 他们会禁止使用这项技术吗？
Has the practice been outlawed yet? = 这种做法已经被禁止了吗？


否定句:
They did not outlaw the practice immediately. = 他们并没有立即禁止这种做法。
The government has not yet outlawed the use of that substance. = 政府尚未禁止使用该物质。
