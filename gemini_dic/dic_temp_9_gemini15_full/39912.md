constrain:/kənˈstreɪn/| vt. |限制；约束；克制 |To limit or restrict something or someone.  |例句：The high cost of living constrained our spending.  高昂的生活成本限制了我们的开支。
例句：She felt constrained by her responsibilities. 她感到责任束缚了她。|近义词：restrict, limit, confine, restrain |反义词：liberate, free, release |动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rules constrain our actions. = 规则限制了我们的行动。
Financial difficulties constrain many people's choices. = 经济困难限制了许多人的选择。

一般过去时:
The heavy rain constrained our travel plans. = 大雨限制了我们的旅行计划。
The tight deadline constrained the team's creativity. = 紧张的期限限制了团队的创造力。

现在完成时:
Rising inflation has constrained economic growth. = 持续上涨的通货膨胀已经限制了经济增长。
Regulations have constrained the company's expansion plans. = 法规已经限制了公司的扩张计划。


过去完成时:
The previous government had constrained spending for years. = 前政府多年来一直限制支出。
Lack of funding had constrained the research project. = 资金不足已经限制了研究项目。


将来时:
New laws will constrain the use of pesticides. = 新法律将限制杀虫剂的使用。
Increased competition will constrain profit margins. = 激烈的竞争将限制利润率。


虚拟语气:
If we were not constrained by time, we could have explored further. = 如果我们不受时间限制，我们本可以探索得更远。
If the budget were not constrained, we could fund more projects. = 如果预算不受限制，我们可以资助更多的项目。


被动语态:
Their movements were constrained by the guards. = 他们的行动受到了警卫的限制。
Our options were constrained by the circumstances. = 我们的选择受到了环境的限制。


疑问句:
What constraints are there on this project? = 这个项目有什么限制？
Are our choices constrained by external factors? = 我们的选择受到外部因素的限制吗？


否定句:
The company is not constrained by traditional methods. = 公司不受传统方法的限制。
We are not constrained by budget limitations. = 我们不受预算限制。


PHRASE->
be constrained by = 受…限制
constrained to = 被迫…
feel constrained = 感到受约束
severely constrained = 严格限制
highly constrained = 高度受限
