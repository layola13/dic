holder:/ˈhoʊldər/| n. |持有者；支架；容器|A person or thing that holds something.  It can refer to a person who possesses something, a device that holds something in place, or a container.|例句：He is the holder of the world record. 他是世界纪录的保持者。

例句：The cup holder in my car is broken. 我车上的杯架坏了。


近义词：possessor, owner, container, support


反义词：loser, relinquisher


词性:名词


USAGE->

The holder keeps the trophy safe. = 持有人将奖杯安全地保管着。
She is the current record holder. = 她是目前的纪录保持者。
