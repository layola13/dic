collides:/kəˈlaɪdz/| v.|碰撞；冲突；抵触|To strike or bump against something with force.  It can also refer to ideas or opinions clashing.|例句：The two cars collided at the intersection. 两辆车在十字路口相撞了。|例句：His views on education collided with those of the school board. 他对教育的观点与学校董事会的观点相冲突。|近义词：crash, bump, clash, conflict|反义词：align, harmonize

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The planets collide once every 10 years. = 这几颗行星每十年碰撞一次。
Their opinions often collide. = 他们的观点经常发生冲突。

一般过去时:
The two trains collided last night. = 两列火车昨晚相撞了。
Their plans collided with our schedule. = 他们的计划与我们的时间安排冲突。

现在完成时:
The two companies have collided in the marketplace. = 这两家公司在市场上展开了竞争。
Nothing has collided with my plans so far. = 到目前为止，没有任何事情与我的计划冲突。

过去完成时:
The cars had collided before the police arrived. = 警车到达之前，汽车已经相撞了。
We had collided on several key issues before the negotiation. = 在谈判之前，我们在几个关键问题上发生了冲突。

将来时:
The asteroids will collide in the next few years. = 这些小行星将在未来几年内发生碰撞。
Their ideas will inevitably collide. = 他们的想法势必会发生冲突。

虚拟语气:
If the two objects collided, there would be a big explosion. = 如果这两个物体相撞，将会发生大爆炸。
If our schedules collided, we would have to reschedule the meeting. = 如果我们的时间安排冲突，我们就必须重新安排会议。

被动语态:
The car was collided by a truck. = 这辆车被一辆卡车撞了。(注意：此句式较少用，更常用主动语态)
The project was negatively impacted because deadlines collided. = 项目受到了负面影响，因为最后期限冲突了。

疑问句:
Did the two planes collide? = 两架飞机相撞了吗？
Do their interests ever collide? = 他们的利益是否曾经冲突？

否定句:
The trains did not collide. = 火车没有相撞。
Our goals do not collide with theirs. = 我们的目标与他们的目标并不冲突。


PHRASE->
collide with: 与…碰撞；与…冲突
head-on collision: 正面碰撞
a collision of ideas: 思想的冲突
avoid a collision: 避免碰撞
near collision: 差点相撞
