misarticulated:/ˌmɪsɑːrˈtɪkjuleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 发音不准的，表达不清的 |  Describes speech that is not pronounced clearly or correctly, often due to a speech impediment or neurological condition. It implies an inability to articulate words properly.  | Example: The child misarticulated several words, making it difficult to understand him.  孩子几个词的发音都不准，让人很难听懂他。  | 近义词: slurred, mumbled, indistinct, poorly articulated | 反义词: clearly articulated, well-spoken, enunciated
