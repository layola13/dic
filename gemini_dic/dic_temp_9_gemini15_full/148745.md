overtrust:/ˈoʊvərtrʌst/| v.|过度信任，盲目信任|To place excessive or misplaced trust in someone or something.|例句：He overtrusted his business partner, leading to significant financial losses. 他过度信任他的商业伙伴，导致了巨大的经济损失。|近义词：misplace confidence in,  have blind faith in|反义词：distrust, doubt, be wary of


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overtrust their employees with sensitive information. = 他们过度信任员工，向他们透露敏感信息。
He overtrusts his intuition in making investment decisions. = 他在投资决策中过度依赖直觉。


一般过去时:
She overtrusted him and gave him all her savings. = 她过度信任他，把所有的积蓄都给了他。
The company overtrusted its supplier, resulting in a product recall. = 公司过度信任其供应商，导致产品召回。


现在完成时:
I have overtrusted people in the past and regretted it. = 我过去曾过度信任别人，并为此后悔。
They have overtrusted the market's stability, leading to losses. = 他们过度信任市场的稳定性，导致了损失。


过去完成时:
She had overtrusted him for years before realizing his deception. = 在意识到他的欺骗之前，她已经过度信任他多年了。
The investors had overtrusted the company's financial reports before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，投资者们已经过度信任了该公司的财务报告。


将来时:
He will overtrust his new partner if he is not careful. = 如果他不小心，他将来会过度信任他的新伙伴。
The team will overtrust its analytical model if it doesn't verify the data. = 如果团队不验证数据，它将会过度信任其分析模型。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't overtrusted him, I wouldn't have lost so much money. = 如果我没有过度信任他，我就不会损失这么多钱。
I wish I hadn't overtrusted my instincts in that situation. = 我真希望在那样的情况下我没有过度信任我的直觉。


被动语态:
The company's security system was overtrusted by the hackers. = 公司的安全系统被黑客过度信任了。(被动语态在此处略显生硬，通常不这样使用)
The advice was overtrusted, leading to a bad decision. = 这条建议被过度信任，导致了错误的决定。


疑问句:
Do you think they overtrusted their sources? = 你认为他们是否过度信任了他们的消息来源？
Could the accident have been avoided if they hadn't overtrusted the equipment? = 如果他们没有过度信任设备，事故是否可以避免？


否定句:
We didn't overtrust the data; we carefully checked everything. = 我们没有过度信任数据；我们仔细检查了一切。
He didn't overtrust his colleague; he knew his limitations. = 他没有过度信任他的同事；他知道他的局限性。
