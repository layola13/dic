handbasins: /ˈhændˌbeɪsɪnz/ | n. |盥洗盆；洗脸盆 |A handbasin is a bowl-shaped plumbing fixture, typically installed in a bathroom, used for washing hands and face.  It's usually smaller than a sink. | 例句：The handbasins in the hotel were spotless.  酒店的盥洗盆干干净净。

The new handbasins were installed last week. 上周安装了新的盥洗盆。


近义词：washbasin, lavatory basin, sink (although sinks are generally larger) | 反义词：None  | 词性：名词


词源:

The word "handbasin" is a compound word.  "Hand" refers to its primary use for washing hands, and "basin" refers to the bowl-shaped container.  "Basin" itself originates from the Old French word "bacin," ultimately derived from the Latin word "baccinum," a diminutive of "bacca," meaning "bowl" or "dish."
