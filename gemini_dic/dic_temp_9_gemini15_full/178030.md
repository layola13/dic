reckonings:/ˈrɛkənɪŋz/ | n. |清算；计算；报应；结局 |The act of calculating or computing something; a settling of accounts; a reckoning of consequences; a final accounting or settlement.  |例句：The final reckoning will come sooner or later.  最终的清算迟早会到来。

例句：His reckless spending led to a harsh reckoning with his creditors. 他不计后果的消费导致了他与债权人之间严厉的清算。


近义词：calculation, computation, accounting, settlement, retribution, consequence, judgment


反义词：forgiveness, pardon, acquittal


词源:  From Old English *reccnung, from reccan "to reckon," ultimately of Germanic origin; related to Dutch rekenen, German rechnen.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The accountant is making the reckonings. 会计正在进行结算。
We reckon the project will finish on time. 我们预计项目会按时完成。


一般过去时:
They made their reckonings and paid the bill. 他们算清了账单并付了款。
She reckoned without the delays. 她没有考虑到延误。


现在完成时:
He has made his reckonings with his past. 他已经与他的过去和解了。
We have reckoned the cost of the project. 我们已经计算了项目的成本。


过去完成时:
They had reckoned on a larger profit margin. 他们曾预计有更大的利润率。
She had reckoned without his opposition. 她没有料到他的反对。


将来时:
We will make our reckonings at the end of the month. 我们将在月底算账。
They will reckon the damages soon. 他们很快就会计算损失。


虚拟语气:
If I had reckoned correctly, I would have made more profit. 如果我计算正确的话，我会赚更多钱。
I wish I had reckoned the time needed more carefully. 我希望我当初更仔细地计算所需时间。


被动语态:
The accounts were reckoned by the auditor. 账户由审计员结算。
The damages are being reckoned at the moment. 损失正在计算中。


疑问句:
Have you made your reckonings? 你算清了吗？

否定句:

I haven't yet finished my reckonings. 我还没有完成我的计算。
