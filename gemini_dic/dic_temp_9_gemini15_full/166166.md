preproven:/ˌpriːˈpruːvən/| adj.| 预先证明的；已预先证明的 |  Already proven beforehand; established in advance.  This word is extremely rare and largely considered non-standard. It's a combination of "pre-" and "proven," creating a redundant term since "proven" already implies prior establishment.  |  The preproven theory was later confirmed by empirical evidence.  预先证明的理论后来被实验证实了。|  proven, established, verified | unproven, disproven, refuted | 形容词
