hearty:/ˈhɑːrti/| adj.|丰盛的；热诚的；强壮的；衷心的|Characterized by or exhibiting hearty good cheer; robust and vigorous; plentiful and satisfying.|例句：He gave a hearty laugh. 他爽朗地大笑。|例句：We had a hearty meal. 我们吃了一顿丰盛的饭。|近义词：robust, vigorous, abundant, ample, sincere, warm|反义词：weak, feeble, meager, scanty, insincere, cold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eats a hearty breakfast every morning. = 他每天早上都吃一顿丰盛的早餐。
She gives a hearty welcome to all her guests. = 她热情地欢迎所有客人。

一般过去时:

The farmer gave a hearty handshake.  = 农夫热情地握了握手。


现在完成时:
