musos:/ˈmjuːzoʊz/| n. |音乐迷，乐迷|A muso is an enthusiastic fan and often knowledgeable aficionado of music, especially of a particular genre. They often attend many concerts, possess extensive music collections, and engage in discussions about music.  | Example: He's a real muso; his apartment is filled with vinyl records and concert posters. 他是个真正的乐迷；他的公寓里全是黑胶唱片和音乐会海报。|近义词：music lover, audiophile, fan, enthusiast |反义词：music hater, non-fan
