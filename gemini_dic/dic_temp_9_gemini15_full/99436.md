interest:/ˈɪntrəst/ | n. v. |兴趣，利益；使感兴趣 |

英文解析：Interest as a noun refers to a feeling of wanting to learn more about something or someone, or a feeling of wanting to be involved in something. As a verb, it means to make someone feel interested or curious.  It can also refer to money earned from an investment or the advantage or benefit gained from something.

例句：
He has a strong interest in history. = 他对历史有浓厚的兴趣。

My interest in the project waned after a few weeks. = 我对这个项目的兴趣在几周后减退了。


近义词：hobby, fascination, curiosity, advantage, benefit, profit

反义词：boredom, apathy, disinterest, disadvantage, loss


词性：Noun, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I interest myself in astronomy. = 我对天文学感兴趣。
The investment interests me greatly. = 这项投资非常吸引我。


一般过去时:
The story interested me. = 这个故事让我感兴趣。
The high return interested many investors. = 高回报吸引了许多投资者。


现在完成时:
This painting has interested many art critics. = 这幅画已引起许多艺术评论家的兴趣。
Have you been interested in learning a new language? = 你对学习一门外语感兴趣吗？


过去完成时:
I had already interested myself in the topic before the meeting. = 会议前我已经对这个话题感兴趣了。
They had interested many potential clients with their proposal. = 他们用他们的提案吸引了许多潜在客户。


将来时:
The new policy will interest many businesses. = 新政策将引起许多企业的兴趣。
I will interest myself in that project next week. = 我下周将关注那个项目。


虚拟语气:
If I were interested, I would apply for the job. = 如果我有兴趣，我会申请这份工作。
I wish I had interested myself more in math when I was younger. = 我希望我年轻时对数学更感兴趣一些。


被动语态:
He was interested in the offer. = 他对这个提议感兴趣。
The audience was greatly interested by the speaker's presentation. = 观众对演讲者的演讲非常感兴趣。


疑问句:
Are you interested in this opportunity? = 你对这个机会感兴趣吗？
What interests you most about this job? = 你对这份工作最感兴趣的是什么？


否定句:
I'm not interested in politics. = 我对政治不感兴趣。
The news didn't interest him at all. = 这个消息一点也没引起他的兴趣。
