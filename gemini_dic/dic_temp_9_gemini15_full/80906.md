gowan:/ˈɡoʊwən/| n. |雏菊，山雏菊 |A gowan is a type of daisy, often referring to a wild or field daisy.  The term is primarily used in Scotland and northern England. |例句：The field was carpeted with gowans.  田野里铺满了雏菊。

例句：She picked a gowan and tucked it behind her ear. 她摘了一朵雏菊别在耳后。


近义词：daisy, field daisy, ox-eye daisy


反义词：无


词性: 名词
