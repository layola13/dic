raises:/ˈreɪzɪz/ | vt. | 举起；提高；筹集；抚养；引起 | to lift something to a higher position; to increase the amount or level of something; to collect money for a cause; to bring up and care for a child; to cause something to happen |

例句：
He raises his hand to answer the question. 他举手回答问题。

She raises her voice in anger. 她生气地提高了嗓音。


近义词：lift, elevate, increase, collect, rear, bring up, cause, provoke

反义词：lower, decrease, diminish, suppress


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company raises salaries annually. 公司每年都提高工资。
He raises his fist in protest. 他举起拳头抗议。

一般过去时:
They raised money for charity. 他们为慈善事业筹款。
She raised three children on her own. 她独自抚养了三个孩子。

现在完成时:
They have raised awareness of the issue. 他们已经提高了人们对这个问题的认识。
The government has raised taxes. 政府提高了税收。

过去完成时:
He had raised the alarm before the fire spread. 大火蔓延之前他已经拉响了警报。
She had raised her concerns with her manager. 她已经向她的经理表达了她的担忧。

将来时:
They will raise the flag at dawn. 他们将在黎明时分升旗。
The company will raise prices next month. 公司下个月将提高价格。

虚拟语气:

I wish they raised my salary. 我希望他们能给我涨工资。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

词源:
The word "raise" comes from the Old English word "rǣsan," meaning "to lift up," "to erect," or "to build."  Its evolution reflects its various meanings, stemming from the fundamental concept of lifting or elevating something.
