over-served:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvd/ | adj. | 酒喝多的；过度提供服务的 |  Describes a person who has consumed more alcohol than is advisable or legal, or a situation where a customer has received excessive service.  | Example:  The bartender admitted he over-served the customer, leading to a DUI. 酒保承认他给顾客上了过多的酒，导致酒后驾车。 The restaurant was over-served with negative online reviews after the food poisoning incident.  餐厅在食物中毒事件后收到了过多的负面在线评论。|近义词：intoxicated, inebriated, drunk, overloaded |反义词：sober, under-served
