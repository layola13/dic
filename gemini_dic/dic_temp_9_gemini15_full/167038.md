presuggested:/ˌpriːsəˈdʒɛstɪd/| adj.| 预先建议的，预先提出的 | Previously suggested or proposed.  This adjective describes something that has been suggested beforehand.  |例句：The presuggested itinerary was quite appealing to the tourists. 预先建议的行程对游客很有吸引力。

例句：The presuggested topics for the meeting were all relevant to the company's current goals. 会议预先提出的议题都与公司当前目标相关。


近义词：predetermined, prearranged, proposed, suggested, planned

反义词：unplanned, impromptu, improvised, unexpected


词性: adjective
