Ordovician:/ɔːrˈdoʊviʃən/| adj. n.|奥陶纪的；奥陶纪|The Ordovician Period was a geologic period and system that lasted from 485.4 ± 1.9 to 443.8 ± 1.5 million years ago.|The Ordovician is known for its diverse marine life, including trilobites, brachiopods, and graptolites. 奥陶纪以其多样化的海洋生物而闻名，包括三叶虫、腕足类动物和笔石。|Silurian, Cambrian|Precambrian|adj.
