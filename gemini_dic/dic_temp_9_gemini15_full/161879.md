praeoperculum:/ˌpriːoʊˈpɜːrkjʊləm/| n. |鳃盖前骨|The praeoperculum is a bone in the head of many bony fishes, located anterior to the operculum (the main gill cover). It's part of the opercular series and contributes to the protection and movement of the gills.  It often has a serrated or spiny edge. |例句：The shape and ornamentation of the praeoperculum are important taxonomic characters in many fish families. = 许多鱼类的鳃盖前骨的形状和装饰是重要的分类特征。|例句：Examination of the praeoperculum revealed a small fracture. = 检查鳃盖前骨发现了一个小裂缝。|近义词：preopercle|反义词:|
