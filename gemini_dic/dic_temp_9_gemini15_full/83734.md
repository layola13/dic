hail-from:/heɪlˈfrɑːm/|/ˈheɪl frɒm/| phrasal verb|来自，起源于|This phrase means to originate from or come from a particular place.  It often implies a more permanent or historical connection than simply "come from."|例句：He hails from a small town in Ireland. 他来自爱尔兰的一个小镇。

例句：She hails from a long line of doctors. 她出身于一个医生世家。


近义词：originate from, come from, be from

反义词：无


词性: phrasal verb
