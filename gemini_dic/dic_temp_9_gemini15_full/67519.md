extractives:/ˈɛkstræktɪvz/| n.|提取物，萃取物|Substances extracted from a natural source, such as plants or minerals.  They are often used in various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics.|Example:  Plant extractives are used in many skincare products. 植物提取物被广泛用于许多护肤品中。|近义词：extracts, essences|反义词：synthetics, artificial compounds
