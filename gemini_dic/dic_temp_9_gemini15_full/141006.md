ones:/wʌnz/|pron.|那些；一些；同类事物|The ones I like best are the red ones. 我最喜欢红色的那些。|近义词：some, those|反义词：none


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I like those ones best. = 我最喜欢那些。
These ones are cheaper than those ones. = 这些比那些便宜。


一般过去时:
I chose the blue ones yesterday. = 我昨天选了蓝色的那些。
We bought several ones last week. = 我们上周买了几个。


现在完成时:
I have already eaten three ones. = 我已经吃了三个了。
We have seen those ones before. = 我们以前见过那些。


过去完成时:
I had already finished the ones you gave me. = 我已经完成了你给我的那些。
They had chosen the ones they liked best. = 他们已经选好了他们最喜欢的那些。


将来时:
I will buy the green ones tomorrow. = 我明天会买绿色的那些。
We will discuss these ones at the next meeting. = 我们下次会议将讨论这些。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would buy the expensive ones. = 如果我有更多钱，我会买贵的那些。
I wish I had chosen the bigger ones. = 我希望我选择了更大的那些。


被动语态:
The red ones were chosen first. = 红色那些首先被选中。
These ones are being considered for the project. = 这些正在被考虑用于这个项目。


疑问句:
Which ones do you prefer? = 你更喜欢哪一个？
Are these the ones you ordered? = 这些是你订购的吗？


否定句:
I don't want those ones. = 我不想要那些。
We didn't buy the small ones. = 我们没有买小的那些。
