deciduate:/ˈdɛsɪdʒuˌeɪt/| v.|脱落；(树木)落叶|To shed, as leaves, petals, or horns; to fall off.  The term often refers to the seasonal shedding of leaves by trees and shrubs.|例句：The oak trees deciduate in autumn.  / 橡树在秋天落叶。  The deer deciduate their antlers annually. / 鹿每年都会脱落鹿角。|近义词：shed, cast off, drop|反义词：retain, keep


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The leaves deciduate in the fall. = 叶子在秋天脱落。
The trees deciduate their leaves every year. = 树木每年都会落叶。

一般过去时:
The leaves deciduated quickly after the frost. = 霜冻过后，叶子很快脱落了。
The plant deciduated all its flowers. = 植物落掉了所有的花。

现在完成时:
The oak trees have deciduated their leaves. = 橡树已经落叶了。
The tree has deciduated most of its fruit. = 这棵树已经落掉了大部分果实。

过去完成时:
The leaves had deciduated before the first snow. = 在第一场雪来临之前，叶子就已经落光了。
The plant had deciduated all its seeds. = 植物已经把所有的种子都落掉了。

将来时:
The trees will deciduate their leaves soon. = 树木很快就会落叶了。
The flowers will deciduate in a few days. = 花儿几天后就会凋谢（落瓣）。


虚拟语气:
If the tree deciduated its leaves earlier, it would be better. = 如果这棵树早点落叶就好了。
If the plant had deciduated its seeds sooner, there would be more. = 如果植物早点落种就好了。

被动语态:
The leaves are deciduated by the strong winds. = 叶子被强风吹落了。
The fruit was deciduated by the birds. = 水果被鸟吃掉了（也可以理解为落掉了）。

疑问句:
Do deciduous trees deciduate every year? = 落叶树每年都落叶吗？
When do these plants deciduate? = 这些植物什么时候落叶/落瓣？

否定句:
The tree doesn't deciduate its leaves until late autumn. = 直到深秋这棵树才落叶。
The flowers didn't deciduate even after the storm. = 即使暴风雨过后，花也没有凋谢。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases directly using "deciduate."  The verb is relatively specific in its usage.  Phrases would involve the noun forms of related words (deciduous, etc.) instead.
