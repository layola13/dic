praecipe:/ˈpriːsɪpiː/ | n. | 命令；法令；诉状 | A writ commanding someone to do something, especially a writ ordering someone to appear in court.  This word is primarily used in a legal context, referring to a formal written order or command.  | Example: The praecipe was served on the defendant, ordering him to appear in court. =  诉状送达被告，命令他出庭。|近义词：mandate, writ, order |反义词：request, suggestion |名词
