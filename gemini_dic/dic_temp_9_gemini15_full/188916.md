rutaceous:/ruːˈteɪʃəs/ | adj. |芸香科的；含芸香素的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Rutaceae, a family of flowering plants that includes rue, citrus, and many other aromatic shrubs and trees.  The term often implies the presence of  characteristic oils or other compounds found in these plants. | Example: The rutaceous fragrance filled the air.  芸香科的香味充满了空气。 The essential oil extracted from the rutaceous plant had medicinal properties. 从芸香科植物中提取的精油具有药用特性。|近义词:citrusy (if referring to citrus plants), aromatic |反义词: N/A
