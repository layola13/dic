levee:/ˈlɛvi/| n.| 堤岸；护堤；隆起的河岸 | An embankment built to prevent the overflow of a river.  |例句：The levee protected the town from the flood.  堤岸保护了小镇免受洪水侵害。|例句：The Mississippi River levee is crucial for flood control. 密西西比河堤防对防洪至关重要。|近义词：dike, embankment, dam |反义词：none


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The levee protects the city from flooding. = 这条堤坝保护城市免受洪水侵害。
They maintain the levee regularly. = 他们定期维护堤坝。


一般过去时:
The levee broke during the storm. = 暴风雨期间堤坝决堤了。
