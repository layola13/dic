pores:/pɔːrz/| n.|毛孔，气孔|Tiny openings in a surface, especially of the skin, allowing sweat or other fluids to pass through.  They are also found in plants, allowing gas exchange.|例句：The pores on my face are enlarged due to the hot weather. 我的脸因为天气热毛孔都变大了。|例句：The leaves of the plant have tiny pores that allow for gas exchange. 植物的叶子有微小的气孔，允许气体交换。|近义词：opening, orifice, aperture|反义词：N/A
