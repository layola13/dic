maladroit:/ˌmæləˈdrɔɪt/| adj. |笨拙的；不灵巧的；拙劣的 |Lacking skill or dexterity; clumsy.  The term often implies a lack of grace or finesse in movement or action, as well as a lack of skill or dexterity.  It can also describe actions or behavior that are inept or inappropriate. |例句：His attempts at cooking were maladroit, resulting in a burnt dinner.  他的烹饪尝试很笨拙，导致晚餐烧焦了。

例句：The politician's maladroit handling of the crisis further damaged his reputation.  这位政治家笨拙地处理危机，进一步损害了他的声誉。


近义词：awkward, clumsy, inept, clumsy, gauche, unskillful |反义词：dexterous, skillful, adept, graceful, adroit
