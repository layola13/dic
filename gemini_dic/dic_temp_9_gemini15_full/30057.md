ccing:/ˈsiːsiːɪŋ/ | v. | 抄送；同时发送 |  The act of sending a copy of an email or other communication to someone in addition to the primary recipient.  It's often used to keep someone informed or to request their input. | 例句：I'm ccing my manager on this email to keep them updated. = 我将此邮件抄送给我的经理，以便他们了解最新情况。  I'm ccing you on this email because I need your feedback. = 我将此邮件抄送给你，因为我需要你的反馈。 | 近义词：forwarding, sending a copy | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I cc my boss on all important emails. = 我将所有重要邮件抄送给我老板。
She ccs her team on project updates. = 她将项目更新抄送给她的团队。


一般过去时:
He cced me on the email yesterday. = 他昨天把邮件抄送给了我。
They cced the entire department on the announcement. = 他们将公告抄送给了整个部门。


现在完成时:
I have cced him on several emails this week. = 本周我已经给他抄送了几封邮件。
She has cced the client on all the relevant documents. = 她已经将所有相关文件抄送给了客户。


过去完成时:
He had cced her before the meeting started. = 会议开始前，他已经抄送给了她。
They had cced the stakeholders before releasing the report. = 在发布报告之前，他们已经抄送给了利益相关者。


将来时:
I will cc you on the final draft. = 我会将最终稿抄送给你。
She will cc her supervisor on the proposal. = 她会将提案抄送给她主管。


虚拟语气:
If I needed his input, I would cc him. = 如果我需要他的意见，我会抄送给他。
I wish I had cced her sooner. = 我希望我早点抄送给她。


被动语态:
He was cced on the email without his knowledge. = 他在不知情的情况下被抄送了邮件。
The entire team was cced on the urgent notification. = 整个团队都被抄送了紧急通知。


疑问句:
Did you cc the right people? = 你抄送了正确的人吗？
Should I cc my manager on this email? = 我应该将此邮件抄送给我经理吗？


否定句:
I didn't cc him on the email. = 我没有将邮件抄送给他。
She didn't cc anyone else on the message. = 她没有将信息抄送给其他人。


PHRASE->
cc someone in =  将某人抄送进邮件
cc someone out =  将某人从抄送中移除
blind cc = 暗送抄送 (收件人看不到其他抄送人)
cc list = 抄送列表
reply all (including ccs) = 全部回复（包括抄送）
