self-inspection:/ˌsɛlfɪnˈspɛkʃən/| n. |自我检查，自检|The act of examining oneself or one's own work or conduct.  It involves a critical evaluation of one's own performance, behavior, or condition. |例句：Regular self-inspection is crucial for maintaining high standards of work. 定期进行自我检查对于保持高标准的工作至关重要。

例句：The company encourages self-inspection among its employees to identify areas for improvement. 公司鼓励员工进行自我检查，以找出改进的领域。


近义词：self-examination, introspection, self-assessment |反义词：external audit, oversight
