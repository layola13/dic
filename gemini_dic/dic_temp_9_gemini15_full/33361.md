chums:/tʃʌmz/| n. |好朋友，伙伴 |Close friends; companions.  |例句：He's been chums with Mark since childhood. 他从小就是马克的好朋友。|例句：They're such good chums, always laughing and joking together. 他们是很好的朋友，总是互相嬉笑打闹。|近义词：friends, pals, buddies, mates |反义词：enemies, foes
