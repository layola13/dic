rockeries:/ˈrɒkəriːz/ | n. |假山；岩石园 |A rockery is an artificial arrangement of rocks and plants, often in a garden.  It is designed to create a naturalistic or aesthetically pleasing landscape feature. |例句：The garden featured a beautiful rockery with various succulents and alpine plants. 花园里有一个美丽的假山，种植着各种多肉植物和高山植物。

The park boasts a stunning rockery, complete with a small waterfall. 公园里有一个令人惊叹的假山，还带有一个小瀑布。

近义词：rock garden, stone garden |反义词：flat ground, plain
