maharaja:/məˈhɑːrədʒə/ | n. | 大君，王公 |  A maharaja was a king or prince in India, especially one ruling a large state. The title is often used figuratively to denote someone of great power or importance. | 例句：The maharaja's palace was opulent and grand.  大君的宫殿富丽堂皇。

The maharaja hosted a lavish banquet for his guests. 大君为他的客人举办了一场盛大的宴会。

| 近义词：king, prince, sovereign, potentate | 反义词：subject, commoner |  词性：名词
