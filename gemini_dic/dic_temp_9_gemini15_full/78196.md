geothermal:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈθɜːrməl/| adj. n.|地热的；地热能|Relating to or produced by the internal heat of the earth.  Geothermal energy is a renewable energy source derived from the Earth's internal heat.|例句：Iceland utilizes geothermal energy extensively for heating and electricity generation. 冰岛广泛利用地热能发电和供暖。

例句：The geothermal activity in Yellowstone National Park is evident in its geysers and hot springs. 黄石国家公园的地热活动在其间歇泉和温泉中可见一斑。


近义词：volcanic (in some contexts)


反义词：None directly; antonyms would describe the opposite of heat, such as frigid or icy.


词性: adjective, noun

WORD ORIGIN->
From Greek *gē "earth" + therme "heat".
