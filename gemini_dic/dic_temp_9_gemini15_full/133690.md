nonfreezable: /ˌnɒnˈfriːzəbəl/ | adj. | 不易冻结的，不冻结的 |  Unable to be frozen; resistant to freezing.  This term usually describes substances that do not freeze at typical freezing temperatures. |

例句：

This antifreeze solution is nonfreezable down to -40°C.  = 这种防冻液在零下40摄氏度也不会结冰。

The nonfreezable components of the mixture remained liquid. = 混合物中不易冻结的成分保持液态。


近义词：unfreezable,  frost-resistant (in some contexts)


反义词：freezable, frozen


词性：形容词
