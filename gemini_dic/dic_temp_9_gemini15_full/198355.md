showcases:/ˈʃoʊkeɪsiz/| n. v.|展示，陈列，展出|To display or exhibit something prominently; to serve as a clear example of something.|例句：The museum showcases a stunning collection of Impressionist paintings.  博物馆展出了令人惊叹的印象派绘画收藏。|例句：Her work showcases her talent for innovative design. 她的作品展现了她在创新设计方面的天赋。|近义词：displays, exhibits, presents, demonstrates|反义词：conceals, hides


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The museum showcases ancient artifacts. = 博物馆展出古代文物。
This store showcases local crafts. = 这家商店展示当地的手工艺品。

一般过去时:
The gallery showcased his latest artwork. = 画廊展出了他的最新作品。
The company showcased its new product at the trade show. = 公司在贸易展上展示了它的新产品。

现在完成时:
The artist has showcased his work in numerous galleries. =这位艺术家已经在许多画廊展出过他的作品。
The company has showcased its commitment to sustainability. = 公司已经展示了其对可持续发展的承诺。

过去完成时:
They had showcased their prototype before the investors arrived. = 他们在投资者到达之前已经展示了他们的原型。
The museum had showcased the exhibit for a month before closing it. = 博物馆在关闭展览前已经展出了一个月。

虚拟语气:
If they showcased their products more effectively, they would sell more. = 如果他们更有效地展示他们的产品，他们就能卖出更多。

被动语态:

The new product is being showcased on their website. = 新产品正在他们的网站上展出。
