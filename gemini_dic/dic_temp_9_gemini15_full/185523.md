revalidated:/ˌriːˈvælɪdeɪtɪd/| v. |重新确认；重新验证；使…再次生效|To make something valid or officially accepted again.  This often involves a review or renewal process.|The scientist revalidated his theory with new data.  科学家用新的数据重新验证了他的理论。|近义词：reconfirmed, reaffirmed, renewed, revitalized |反义词：invalidated, nullified
