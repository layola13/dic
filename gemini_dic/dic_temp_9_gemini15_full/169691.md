propositioning:/ˌprɒpəˈzɪʃənɪŋ/ | v. | 提议；求欢；引诱 | The act of making a proposition, especially a sexual proposition.  It can refer to offering a business proposal or suggesting something, but more commonly implies a sexual advance. | Example: He was arrested for propositioning underage girls. 他因向未成年女孩求欢而被捕。  |近义词：soliciting, tempting, proposing | 反义词：rejecting, refusing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He propositions women at the bar every night. = 他每天晚上都在酒吧向女人求欢。
She propositions her boss for a promotion. = 她向老板提议晋升。


一般过去时:
He propositioned her with a business deal. = 他向她提议了一笔生意。
She propositioned him for a date. = 她向他提出约会邀请。


现在完成时:
He has propositioned several women this week. = 本周他已经向好几个女人求欢了。
She has propositioned the company for a merger. = 她已经向公司提议合并。


过去完成时:
He had propositioned her several times before she finally agreed. = 在她最终同意之前，他已经向她求过几次婚了。(此处“求婚”引申为“提出某种请求”)
She had propositioned him for a loan before he got the job. = 在他找到工作之前，她曾向他提出过贷款的要求。


将来时:
He will proposition her again tomorrow. = 他明天还会再次向她求欢。
She will proposition the board for a new budget. = 她将向董事会提议新的预算。


虚拟语气:
If he propositioned her, she would refuse. = 如果他向她求欢，她会拒绝。
If she propositioned the bank for a loan, they might refuse. = 如果她向银行申请贷款，他们可能会拒绝。


被动语态:
She was propositioned by a stranger on the street. = 她在大街上被一个陌生人求欢。
The merger was propositioned by the competitor's company. = 合并是由竞争对手公司提出的。


疑问句:
Was he propositioned by anyone at the party? = 在派对上有人向他求欢吗？
Has the proposal been propositioned to the client? = 这个提案已经向客户提出建议了吗？


否定句:
He did not proposition her. = 他没有向她求欢。
She did not proposition him for a job. = 她没有向他求职。


PHRASE->
proposition a deal:  提出交易
proposition someone for sex: 向某人提出性要求
a business proposition: 商业提案
a tempting proposition: 一个诱人的提议
a dangerous proposition: 一个危险的提议
