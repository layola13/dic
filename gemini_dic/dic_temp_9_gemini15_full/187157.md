roadkills: /ˈroʊdˌkɪlz/ | n. |  路杀动物；被车撞死的动物 | Roadkills refers to animals that have been killed by vehicles on roads.  It is a collective noun, referring to multiple animals. | Example:  The number of roadkills on this highway is alarming. 这条高速公路上被撞死的动物数量令人担忧。


The cleanup crew removed the roadkills from the highway. 清洁队清理了高速公路上的路杀动物。|  近义词：traffic casualties, animal fatalities (in this context) | 反义词：None (as it's a specific noun describing a type of death) | 名词
