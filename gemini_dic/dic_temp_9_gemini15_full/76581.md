gallied:/ˈɡælid/| v.| 驱赶；用轻快小船运送 | to drive or force to move; to transport in a small, light boat. | 例句：The sailors gallied the prisoners to the ship. 水手们把囚犯赶到船上。  The goods were gallied across the lake. 货物是用轻快小船运过湖的。| 近义词：drive, propel, convey | 反义词：  N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They galley the sheep to the pasture. = 他们把羊赶到牧场。
He gallies the tourists to the island. = 他用小船把游客送到岛上。


一般过去时:
The shepherd gallied his flock to safety. = 牧羊人把他的羊群赶到了安全的地方。
They gallied the supplies across the river. = 他们用小船把补给运过了河。


现在完成时:
The farmer has gallied all his cattle into the barn. = 农民已经把所有的牛赶进了牛棚。
They have gallied the injured people to the hospital. = 他们已经把受伤的人送到了医院。


过去完成时:
The captain had gallied his crew to the lifeboats before the ship sank. = 船长在船沉之前已经把船员赶到了救生艇上。
They had gallied the cargo before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经把货物运送完毕。


将来时:
The herder will galley the goats to the higher ground. = 牧人将把山羊赶到较高的地势。
They will galley the timber down the river. = 他们将用船把木材运下河。


虚拟语气:
If we had a boat, we could galley the supplies to the island. = 如果我们有船，我们就能把补给运到岛上。
If he gallied the sheep earlier, they would not have been scattered. = 如果他早点把羊赶走，它们就不会散开了。


被动语态:
The sheep were gallied to the pasture by the shepherd. = 羊被牧羊人赶到了牧场。
The supplies were gallied across the river by the boatmen. = 补给被船夫用船运过了河。


疑问句:

否定句:
