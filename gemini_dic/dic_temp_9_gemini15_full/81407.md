grassing:/ˈɡræsiŋ/| v.|检举；告密；放牧；|The act of informing on someone or something, typically to the authorities.  Also refers to the act of allowing livestock to graze on grass.|例句：He was accused of grassing on his colleagues to the police. 他被指控向警方告发他的同事。

例句：The farmer is grassing his cattle in the field. 农民正在田里放牧他的牛。|近义词：informing, betraying, denouncing, grazing|反义词：protecting, shielding, concealing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is grassing on his friend. = 他正在告发他的朋友。
The farmer grasses his sheep in the meadow. = 农民在草地上放牧他的羊。


一般过去时:
She grassed on the thieves to the police. = 她向警方告发了那些小偷。
They grassed their cattle in the pasture all day. = 他们一整天都在牧场放牧他们的牛。


现在完成时:
He has grassed on several criminals this year. = 他今年已经告发了几个罪犯。
The shepherd has grassed his flock in this field for years. = 牧羊人多年来一直在这个田地里放牧他的羊群。


过去完成时:
They had grassed the horses before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经把马放牧好了。
The farmer had grassed his cows in the best pasture. = 农民已将他的奶牛放在最好的牧场上放牧。


将来时:
