detain:/dɪˈteɪn/| vt.|拘留；耽搁；扣留 |To keep someone in official custody, often for questioning.  To delay or prevent the movement of something or someone. |例句：The police detained him for questioning. 警方拘留他以进行审问。  The bad weather detained the flight. 恶劣天气延误了航班。|近义词：hold, arrest, delay, impede |反义词：release, free, expedite


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police detain suspects for questioning. = 警方拘留嫌疑人进行审问。
They detain the package at customs. = 海关扣留了包裹。

一般过去时:
The police detained him for twenty-four hours. = 警方拘留了他24小时。
The storm detained the travelers. = 风暴耽搁了旅行者。

现在完成时:
They have detained the suspect until further notice. = 他们已将嫌疑人拘留，直至另行通知。
The heavy traffic has detained me for an hour. = 严重的交通堵塞耽误了我一个小时。

过去完成时:
They had detained the evidence before the trial began. = 他们在审判开始前就扣押了证据。
The rain had detained us from reaching our destination. = 雨水耽误了我们到达目的地。

将来时:
The authorities will detain anyone found violating the law. = 当局将拘留任何被发现违反法律的人。
I think the snow will detain us until tomorrow. = 我想雪会让我们耽搁到明天。


被动语态:
He was detained by the police. = 他被警方拘留了。
The shipment was detained at the port. = 货物在港口被扣留了。

疑问句:
Will they detain him for long? = 他们会拘留他很久吗？
Was the mail detained by customs? = 邮件被海关扣留了吗？

否定句:
They did not detain the witness. = 他们没有拘留证人。
The delay did not detain us significantly. = 延误并没有显著耽搁我们。
