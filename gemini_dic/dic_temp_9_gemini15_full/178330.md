reconditioned:/ˌriːˈkən.dɪʃənd/| adj.|翻新的；修复的；再生的|Refers to something that has been repaired, refurbished, or restored to a good working condition.  It implies a more thorough process than simple repair, often involving replacing parts.|例句：The reconditioned engine ran smoothly.  翻新的引擎运转平稳。

例句：This reconditioned car is in excellent condition. 这辆翻新车状况极佳。

近义词：refurbished, renovated, repaired, restored

反义词：broken, damaged, ruined, worn-out

词性：形容词
