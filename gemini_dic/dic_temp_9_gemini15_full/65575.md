evacuating:/ɪˈvækjuˌeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 撤离；疏散；排空 | The act of removing people from a dangerous place.  It implies a planned and organized removal, often in response to a threat or emergency. | 例句：The authorities are evacuating the residents because of the approaching hurricane.  当局正在因为逼近的飓风而疏散居民。

例句：The firefighters were evacuating the burning building. 消防员正在疏散着燃烧的建筑物。


近义词：removing, relocating, clearing, dislodging
反义词：arriving, entering, populating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are evacuating the village. = 他们正在疏散村庄。
The army is evacuating civilians from the war zone. = 军队正在将平民从战区撤离。

一般过去时:
We evacuated our home before the flood. = 我们在洪水来临之前撤离了家园。
The police evacuated the building after the bomb threat. = 警察在炸弹威胁后疏散了大楼。

现在完成时:
They have evacuated all the residents. = 他们已经疏散了所有居民。
The government has evacuated thousands of people from the disaster area. = 政府已经从灾区疏散了数千人。

过去完成时:
They had evacuated the hospital before the earthquake struck. = 地震发生前，他们已经疏散了医院。
The villagers had evacuated their homes before the volcano erupted. = 火山爆发前，村民们已经撤离了家园。

将来时:
We will evacuate if the fire spreads. = 如果火势蔓延，我们将撤离。
The city will evacuate residents in case of a typhoon. = 如果台风来临，城市将疏散居民。

虚拟语气:
If we had evacuated earlier, we would have avoided the danger. = 如果我们早点撤离，我们就不会遇到危险。
I wish we had evacuated the building sooner. = 我希望我们早点撤离那栋楼。

被动语态:
The town was evacuated overnight. = 镇上的人一夜之间被疏散了。
The building is being evacuated right now. = 这栋建筑物正在被疏散。

疑问句:
Are they evacuating the school? = 他们正在疏散学校吗？
Have they evacuated the hospital yet? = 医院已经被疏散了吗？

否定句:
They did not evacuate the building in time. = 他们没有及时疏散大楼。
We could not evacuate because of the heavy snow. = 由于大雪，我们无法撤离。


PHRASE->
evacuate the area = 撤离该地区
evacuate a building = 疏散一栋建筑物
emergency evacuation = 紧急疏散
mass evacuation = 大规模疏散
preemptive evacuation = 预防性疏散


词源:
From Middle French *evacuer, from Latin evacuāre "to empty," from ē- "out" + vacuus "empty."
