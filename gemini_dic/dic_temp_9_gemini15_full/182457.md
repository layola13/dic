/ˌriːˈnoʊtəraɪz/ | v. |重新公证；重新认证|To notarize something again.  This typically involves verifying the authenticity of a document or signature a second time. This is often necessary if there's an issue with the original notarization or if additional requirements arise.|The lawyer had to renotarize the contract after discovering an error. = 律师在发现错误后不得不重新公证合同。|The document needed to be renotarized because the witness' signature was unclear. =  该文件需要重新公证，因为证人的签名不清楚。|recertify, revalidate|invalidate, nullify|动词
