preinitiates:/ˌpriːɪˈnɪʃieɪts/| n.|预备成员，预备启动者| Individuals or groups undergoing preliminary training or preparation for full membership or initiation into a larger group or organization.  They are not yet full members but are in the process of becoming so.  |例句：The preinitiates underwent rigorous training before their formal initiation into the secret society. 预备成员在正式加入秘密社团之前接受了严格的训练。

The preinitiates practiced the rituals repeatedly before the ceremony. 预备成员在仪式前反复练习仪式。


近义词：novices, apprentices, candidates, initiates-in-training|反义词：veterans, members, initiates
