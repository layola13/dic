jingoism:/ˈdʒɪŋɡoʊɪzəm/ | n. | 侵略性爱国主义；沙文主义；盲目爱国 | Extreme nationalism characterized by aggressive foreign policy, a belief in the superiority of one's own country, and a readiness to glorify military power.  It often involves excessive patriotism and a willingness to engage in conflict with other nations. | The government's jingoistic rhetoric fueled tensions with neighboring countries.  政府的沙文主义言论加剧了与邻国的紧张关系。|chauvinism, nationalism, patriotism | pacifism, internationalism | 名词
