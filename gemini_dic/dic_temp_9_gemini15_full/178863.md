red-necks:/ˈrɛdˌnɛks/| n.|乡巴佬；白人穷困阶层|A derogatory term for white people, especially those from the rural Southern United States, perceived as uneducated, unsophisticated, and having racist or conservative views.  The term refers to the often sunburned necks of people who work outdoors.|例句：He was labeled a redneck for his outspoken views on immigration. 他因其关于移民的直言不讳的观点而被贴上了乡巴佬的标签。

例句：Some people find the term "redneck" offensive and prefer more respectful descriptions. 有些人认为“乡巴佬”这个词具有冒犯性，更喜欢使用更尊重的描述。

近义词：hillbilly, yokel, backwoodsman, country bumpkin

反义词：sophisticate, intellectual, urbanite
