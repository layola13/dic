impediment:/ɪmˈpedɪmənt/| n. |障碍；妨碍；口吃|Something that impedes or hinders progress or movement.  It can refer to a physical obstruction, a difficulty, or a speech impediment.|例句：His lack of confidence was a major impediment to his success.他的缺乏自信是他成功的主要障碍。

例句：A severe stutter was a significant impediment to his career as a lawyer.严重的口吃严重阻碍了他的律师生涯。


近义词：obstacle, hindrance, obstruction, barrier, handicap|反义词：aid, assistance, help, advantage


词源：From Middle French *empediment, from Latin impedimentum "hindrance, obstacle," from impedire "to hinder," from in- "in" + ped- "foot" (related to pes "foot").  The original sense was of something that entangled the feet, hence hindering movement.
