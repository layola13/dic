clathrate:/ˈklæθreɪt/ | n. | 包合物，笼形化合物 | A clathrate is a compound in which molecules of one component are physically trapped within the cavities of a crystal lattice formed by molecules of a second component.  The components are not chemically bonded. | Example: Methane clathrates are found in the ocean floor and are a potential source of natural gas. 甲烷包合物存在于海底，是天然气的潜在来源。 |近义词：inclusion compound, cage compound | 反义词：无
