/pɔːrtsəl/ | adj. |待售的；可供出售的 | For sale; available for purchase.  |This house is for sale. 这房子待售。 |The antique clock is for sale. 这座古董钟待售。|  available, on the market | unavailable, not for sale |形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The car is for sale. = 这辆车待售。
The house is for sale for $500,000. = 这栋房子售价50万美元待售。


一般过去时:
The painting was for sale last week. = 这幅画上周待售。
The tickets were for sale at the box office. = 票在售票处出售。


现在完成时:
The item has been for sale for months. = 这件商品已经待售好几个月了。
The tickets have been for sale since Monday. = 票从星期一开始出售。


过去完成时:
The car had been for sale for a year before it finally sold. = 这辆车在最终售出之前已经待售一年了。
The house had been for sale for six months before we bought it. = 我们买下这栋房子之前，它已经待售六个月了。


将来时:
The tickets will be for sale next week. = 票将于下周出售。
The new model will be for sale in the spring. = 新型号将于春季上市。


虚拟语气:
If the car were for sale, I would buy it. = 如果这辆车待售，我会买下它。
I wish that rare stamp were for sale. = 我希望那枚稀有邮票待售。


被动语态:
The products are being advertised as for sale. = 产品正在被广告宣传为待售。
The property is being actively promoted as for sale. = 该房产正在积极推广为待售。


疑问句:
Is the house for sale? = 这房子待售吗？
Are the tickets for sale online? = 票可以在网上购买吗？


否定句:
The item is not for sale. = 这件商品不出售。
That painting is not currently for sale. = 那幅画目前不出售。


PHRASE->
for sale by owner = 个人出售
put up for sale = 出售
up for sale = 待售
on sale = 减价出售 (注意区别于for sale, on sale指降价促销)
for sale or rent = 出售或出租
