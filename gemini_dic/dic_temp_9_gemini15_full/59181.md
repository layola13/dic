dyslexic:/dɪsˈlɛksɪk/ | adj. | 读写困难的；患有诵读困难症的 |  Relating to or characteristic of dyslexia; having dyslexia.  Dyslexic individuals may struggle with reading, writing, and spelling despite having normal intelligence. | Example:  He is a dyslexic student who excels in mathematics. 他是一位在数学方面很优秀，但患有诵读困难症的学生。| 近义词：learning-disabled, reading-impaired | 反义词：N/A
