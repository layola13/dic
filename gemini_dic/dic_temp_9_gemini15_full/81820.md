grew:/ɡruː/ | v. |生长；变得；逐渐增强|To increase in size or amount gradually; to develop or evolve.|例句：The plant grew taller. 这棵植物长高了。
The problem grew more serious. 问题变得越来越严重了。|近义词：increased, developed, expanded |反义词：shrunk, decreased, diminished |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plant grows well in sunlight.  = 植物在阳光下生长良好。
He grows tomatoes in his garden. = 他在花园里种西红柿。


一般过去时:
The tree grew rapidly last year. = 这棵树去年长得很快。
She grew tired of waiting. = 她等得不耐烦了。


现在完成时:
The city has grown significantly in recent years. = 近年来，这座城市发展显著。
My understanding of the subject has grown. = 我对这个主题的理解加深了。


过去完成时:
The company had grown substantially before the merger. = 在合并之前，公司已经有了大幅增长。
They had grown accustomed to the noise. = 他们已经习惯了噪音。


将来时:
The population will grow by 10% next year. = 明年人口将增长10%。
The company expects its profits to grow. = 公司预计其利润将增长。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would grow my own vegetables. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会自己种菜。
I wish I had grown taller. = 我真希望我长得更高。


被动语态:
The crops were grown organically. = 这些作物是有机种植的。
The business was grown from a small startup. = 这家企业是从一个小型的初创公司发展起来的。


疑问句:
How much did the child grow last year? = 孩子去年长了多少？
Does the company still grow its own coffee beans? = 这家公司还在种植自己的咖啡豆吗？


否定句:
The plant didn't grow well in the shade. = 植物在阴凉处长得不好。
The economy didn't grow as expected. = 经济增长没有预期的那样好。


PHRASE->
grow up = 长大成人
grow out of = 脱离；长大后不再…
grow into = 逐渐适应；逐渐变成
grow on somebody = 越来越喜欢
grow old = 变老
