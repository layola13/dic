selfless:/ˈsɛlfslɪs/| adj.|无私的；忘我的|Characterized by unselfish concern for or devotion to the welfare of others;  showing unselfish concern for others.  It implies a disregard for one's own needs or desires in favor of others.|例句：Her selfless dedication to the cause inspired many.  她的忘我奉献鼓舞了很多人。

例句：He made a selfless sacrifice to save his friend. 他为了救朋友做出了无私的牺牲。


近义词：altruistic, unselfish, generous, magnanimous|反义词：selfish, egotistical, self-centered, greedy|词性:形容词
