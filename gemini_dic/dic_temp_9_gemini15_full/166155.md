prepromotions:/ˌpriːprəˈmoʊʃənz/ | n. |预先促销，提前促销活动 | Activities or marketing campaigns designed to generate excitement and anticipation for a future product launch or event.  These promotions often offer early access, exclusive deals, or other incentives to build a customer base before the official release. | Example: The company launched prepromotions for its new phone, offering a discount to those who pre-ordered. 公司为其新手机推出了预售促销活动，为提前预订的用户提供折扣。 |近义词：advance promotions, early bird promotions |反义词：post-launch promotions
