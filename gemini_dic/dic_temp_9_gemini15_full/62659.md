engagement:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt/ | n. | 订婚；约会；参与；雇用；交战 |

英文解析：The act of engaging or the state of being engaged.  This word has multiple meanings depending on context, ranging from a formal commitment like an engagement ring to active participation in something.

例句：

Their engagement was announced last week. = 他们的订婚消息上周公布了。

He showed great engagement in the discussion. = 他在讨论中表现出极大的参与度。


近义词：commitment, involvement, participation, betrothal (for the marriage context), employment (for the work context), battle (for the military context)


反义词：disengagement, detachment, apathy, unemployment (for the work context), peace (for the military context)


词性：名词
