forebears:/ˈfɔːrˌbeərz/| n. |祖先，前辈 |Those who came before in one's ancestry; ancestors.  The term often implies a more distant relationship than "parents" or "grandparents," encompassing a broader lineage. |例句：He researched his forebears and discovered a surprising connection to royalty. 他研究了他的祖先，并发现了一个与皇室令人惊讶的联系。

例句：The family history was meticulously documented, tracing their forebears back several centuries. 家族史被一丝不苟地记录下来，追溯他们的祖先到几个世纪以前。

近义词：ancestors, predecessors, progenitors |反义词：descendants, posterity
