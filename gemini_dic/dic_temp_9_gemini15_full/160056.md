polyurethane:/ˌpɒliˈjʊərɪθeɪn/ (英式)/ˌpɑːliˈjʊrəθeɪn/ (美式)| n. |聚氨酯|A synthetic resin composed of a polymer of a diisocyanate and a polyol.  It is used to make foams, coatings, and elastomers.  |例句：The car seats are made of polyurethane foam. 这汽车座椅是由聚氨酯泡沫制成的。

例句：Polyurethane is a versatile material used in a wide range of applications. 聚氨酯是一种用途广泛的材料，应用范围广泛。


近义词：polyester(聚酯),  plastic(塑料) (in some contexts)


反义词：无


词性:名词
