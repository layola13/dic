counter-influence: /ˌkaʊntərˈɪnflʊəns/ | v. | 反作用力；对抗性影响 | To exert an opposing influence; to counteract or neutralize the effect of something.  | 例句：The government tried to counter-influence the negative media coverage. 政府试图对抗负面的媒体报道。  | 近义词：counteract, offset, neutralize | 反义词：reinforce, support, enhance | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The advertising campaign counter-influences consumer choices. = 广告宣传活动影响着消费者的选择。
Their efforts counter-influence the spread of misinformation. = 他们的努力对抗着虚假信息的传播。


一般过去时:
The speech counter-influenced public opinion. = 这篇演讲改变了公众舆论。
The intervention counter-influenced the negative trend. = 干预行为扭转了负面趋势。


现在完成时:
The new regulations have counter-influenced the market. = 新规已经影响了市场。
Their actions have counter-influenced the initial plan. = 他们的行动已经改变了最初的计划。


过去完成时:
The prior warning had counter-influenced the potential disaster. = 先前的警告已经避免了潜在的灾难。
The previous campaign had counter-influenced the negative perception. = 先前的活动已经改变了负面印象。


将来时:
The upcoming legislation will counter-influence the current situation. = 即将出台的法规将改变现状。
Their strategy will counter-influence the competitor's tactics. = 他们的策略将会对抗竞争对手的策略。


虚拟语气:
If we acted sooner, we could have counter-influenced the crisis. = 如果我们早点采取行动，就能避免这场危机。
I wish the policy had counter-influenced the social unrest. = 我希望这项政策能够平息社会动荡。


被动语态:
The negative effects were counter-influenced by government intervention. = 负面影响受到了政府干预的遏制。
The public perception was counter-influenced by a successful PR campaign. = 公众印象被一次成功的公关活动所改变。


疑问句:
How can we counter-influence the harmful effects of pollution? = 我们如何才能对抗污染的有害影响？
Will this new strategy counter-influence the market trends? = 这一新策略能否改变市场趋势？


否定句:
The measures did not counter-influence the rising prices. = 这些措施没有控制住物价上涨。
Their attempts to counter-influence the narrative failed. = 他们试图改变叙事的尝试失败了。
