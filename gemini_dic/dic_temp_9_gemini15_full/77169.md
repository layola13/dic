gasmen:/ˈɡæzmən/| n.|燃气工人，煤气工人|A gasman is a person who works with or installs gas appliances or maintains gas lines.  They are skilled tradespeople responsible for the safe and efficient use of gas in homes and businesses.|例句：The gasman came to fix the leak in our kitchen. = 燃气工人来修理我们厨房的漏气管道。|例句：We called a gasman to install a new gas stove. = 我们叫了一个燃气工人来安装新的燃气灶。|近义词：gas fitter, gas engineer|反义词：无|名词
