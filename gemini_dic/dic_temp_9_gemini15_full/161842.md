practisant:/ˈpræktɪsænt/| n. |练习者，实习生|A practisant is someone who is engaged in practice, especially in a profession or skill requiring training.  They are usually in the process of learning and developing their abilities.|例句：The practisant diligently worked on her violin technique.  练习者勤奋地练习她的小提琴技巧。 The medical practisant assisted the surgeon during the operation.  实习医生在手术中协助外科医生。|近义词：trainee, apprentice, student, learner|反义词：expert, master, professional
