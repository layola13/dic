re-displayed:/ˌriːdɪˈspleɪd/| v. |重新展示，再次显示 |To display something again after it has been removed or hidden.  It involves a previous display followed by a subsequent one. |例句：The artwork was re-displayed after being restored.  修复后，这件艺术品被重新展出。 The information was re-displayed on the screen after the system crashed. 系统崩溃后，信息重新显示在屏幕上。|近义词：redisplayed, showed again, presented again |反义词：removed, hidden, concealed |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The museum re-displays its collection annually. = 博物馆每年重新展示其收藏品。
They re-display the products in a new arrangement. = 他们以新的排列方式重新展示产品。

一般过去时:
The shop re-displayed the goods after the sale. = 销售结束后，商店重新摆放了商品。
She re-displayed her paintings at the gallery. = 她再次在画廊展出她的画作。

现在完成时:
The company has re-displayed its advertising campaign. = 公司已经重新展示了它的广告宣传活动。

过去完成时:
