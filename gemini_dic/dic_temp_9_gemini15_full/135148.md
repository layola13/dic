nonolfactory:/ˌnɒnəʊlˈfæktəri/| adj.|非嗅觉的|Not relating to or involving the sense of smell.  The word is formed from the prefix "non-" (not) and "olfactory" (relating to the sense of smell).|Example:  The nonolfactory stimuli were processed by other sensory systems.  非嗅觉刺激是由其他感觉系统处理的。|近义词:anosmic (inability to smell),  insensible to odors|反义词:olfactory
