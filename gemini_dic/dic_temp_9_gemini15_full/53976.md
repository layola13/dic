discrete: /dɪˈskriːt/ | adj. | 不连续的；离散的；单独的；谨慎的 |

英文解析:Separate and distinct; individual; unconnected.  Also, marked by careful consideration; prudent; cautious.  The word has two distinct meanings, one relating to separateness and the other to secrecy or caution.

例句:

The data points are discrete, not continuous.  数据点是不连续的，而不是连续的。

He maintained a discrete silence about the affair. 他对那件事保持谨慎的沉默。


近义词: separate, distinct, individual, unconnected, cautious, prudent, reserved

反义词: continuous, connected, indiscrete, careless, imprudent


词性: adjective

词源:
