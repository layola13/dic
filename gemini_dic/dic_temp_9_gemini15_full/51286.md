detachable:/dɪˈtætʃəbəl/ | adj. | 可拆卸的，可分离的 | Capable of being detached or removed.  Something that is detachable can be easily separated from something else. | Example: The wheels on this bike are detachable, making it easier to transport.  这个自行车的轮子是可以拆卸的，便于运输。  The detachable hood provided extra protection from the rain. 可拆卸的引擎盖提供了额外的防雨保护。|近义词：removable, separable | 反义词：permanent, fixed, integral
