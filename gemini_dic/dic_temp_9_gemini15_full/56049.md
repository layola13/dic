doc:/dɒk/ /dɑːk/ | n. | 文件；文档；医生 | A doc is a short form of "document".  It refers to a digital or physical file containing information.  A doctor is also commonly called "doc". | Examples:  I saved the doc to my desktop. (我把文档保存到了我的桌面上。)  The doc said I needed more rest. (医生说我需要多休息。) |近义词：document, file, paper, physician | 反义词：N/A | 名词
