clicker:/ˈklɪkər/| n. |咔嗒器，点击器|A small device that makes a clicking sound when pressed, often used for training animals or as a simple counter.|例句：The dog trainer used a clicker to reward the dog for good behavior.  训犬师用咔嗒器奖励狗的良好行为。|例句：I use a clicker to count the number of times I complete a task. 我用咔嗒器计算我完成任务的次数。|近义词：counter, marker|反义词:None
