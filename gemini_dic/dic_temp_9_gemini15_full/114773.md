malthusianisms:/mælˈθuːʒənɪzəm/| n.|马尔萨斯主义思想；马尔萨斯主义观点|Theories or beliefs based on the principles of Thomas Robert Malthus, particularly concerning population growth exceeding resource availability, leading to poverty and famine.|例句：His views on overpopulation reflect a certain amount of Malthusianism.他的过度人口观点反映出一定程度的马尔萨斯主义。

例句：The essay critiques the inherent limitations of Malthusianism in addressing complex societal problems. 这篇文章批判了马尔萨斯主义在解决复杂社会问题方面的固有局限性。


近义词：population control theories, theories of resource scarcity|反义词：utopianism, cornucopianism|名词
