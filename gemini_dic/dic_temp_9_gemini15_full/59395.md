earst:/ɜːrst/| adv.|首先，最初|Firstly, at first, originally.  The word is archaic and rarely used in modern English. It emphasizes the beginning or priority of something.|例句：Earst, there was nothing but wilderness. = 首先，那里只有荒野。

例句：Earst, they lived in harmony. = 最初，他们生活得很和谐。


近义词：firstly, initially, at first, originally

反义词：finally, lastly, eventually
