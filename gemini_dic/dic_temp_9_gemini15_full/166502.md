presagers:/ˈprɛsɪdʒərz/| n.|预兆，预示者|Something that foreshadows a future event; a person or thing that indicates or foreshadows something.  The dark clouds presaged a storm. |The ominous silence presaged disaster. |近义词：harbingers, forerunners, precursors|反义词：aftermaths, consequences


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dark clouds presage a storm. =乌云预示着暴风雨。
These signs presage a period of economic growth. =这些迹象预示着经济增长时期。

一般过去时:
The falling barometer presaged the hurricane. = 气压计下降预示着飓风。
The unusual bird activity presaged the earthquake. = 异常的鸟类活动预示着地震。

现在完成时:
Recent events have presaged a major political shift. = 最近发生的事件预示着重大的政治转变。
The rising tensions have presaged conflict for months. = 数月来，紧张局势加剧预示着冲突。


过去完成时:
The ominous whispers had presaged the betrayal. = 不祥的低语预示着背叛。
The strange dreams had presaged the accident. = 奇异的梦预示着事故。


将来时:
Experts predict that these trends will presage a new era of technological advancement. =专家预测，这些趋势将预示着一个技术进步的新时代。
Analysts believe the current economic indicators will presage a recession. = 分析师认为目前的经济指标将预示着经济衰退。


虚拟语气:
If only I had heeded the signs that presaged the downfall. = 如果我当时注意到预示着失败的迹象就好了。
If the signs had been correctly interpreted, they would have presaged a different outcome. = 如果这些迹象被正确解读，它们本会预示着不同的结果。


被动语态:
The impending crisis was presaged by several warning signs. = 迫在眉睫的危机已被几个警告信号预示。
The collapse of the company was presaged by a series of poor financial decisions. = 公司的倒闭是由一系列糟糕的财务决策预示的。


疑问句:
What does this unusual weather pattern presage? = 这种异常的天气模式预示着什么？
Do you think these events presage a larger conflict? = 你认为这些事件预示着一场更大的冲突吗？


否定句:
The initial reports did not presage the severity of the damage. = 初步报告并没有预示到损害的严重程度。
The calm before the storm did not presage the ensuing chaos. = 风暴前的平静并没有预示随后而来的混乱。
