enumerated:/ɪˈnjuːməreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 列举的；枚举的 |  Listed or mentioned individually.  | The enumerated list of ingredients was easy to follow.  列出的配料清单很容易遵循。 | listed, itemized, specified | unspecified, unlisted | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher enumerated the rules of the classroom. = 老师列举了教室的规则。
She enumerates the benefits of regular exercise. = 她列举了定期锻炼的好处。

一般过去时:
He enumerated the points of his argument. = 他列举了他论点的要点。
The scientist enumerated the different species of birds. = 科学家列举了不同种类的鸟。


现在完成时:
She has enumerated all the possible solutions. = 她已经列举了所有可能的解决方案。
They have enumerated the items on the shopping list. = 他们已经列举了购物清单上的物品。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, he had enumerated all the potential risks. = 会议之前，他已经列举了所有潜在的风险。
She had enumerated the reasons for her resignation. = 她已经列举了她辞职的原因。


将来时:
He will enumerate the steps involved in the process. = 他将列举该过程中涉及的步骤。
The report will enumerate the causes of the accident. = 报告将列举事故的原因。


虚拟语气:
If she enumerated her concerns, they might be addressed. = 如果她列举了她的担忧，它们可能会得到解决。
I wish he had enumerated the facts more clearly. = 我希望他更清楚地列举了事实。


被动语态:
The items were enumerated in alphabetical order. = 这些项目按字母顺序排列。
The benefits of the program were enumerated in the brochure. = 该计划的好处在宣传册中列出。


疑问句:
Have you enumerated all the possibilities? = 你列举了所有可能性吗？
Were the participants enumerated in the study? = 研究中是否列举了参与者？


否定句:
The report did not enumerate all the relevant factors. = 报告没有列举所有相关的因素。
He failed to enumerate the steps clearly. = 他未能清楚地列举步骤。
