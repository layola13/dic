reconfirms:/ˌriːkənˈfɜːrmz/ | vt. |再次确认；重申；进一步证实 | To confirm something again; to state something emphatically once more.  |例句：The lab tests reconfirmed the initial diagnosis.  实验室的测试再次证实了初步诊断。 |例句：The CEO reconfirmed the company's commitment to sustainability.  首席执行官再次确认了公司对可持续发展的承诺。 |近义词： reaffirm, confirm, verify |反义词：deny, refute
