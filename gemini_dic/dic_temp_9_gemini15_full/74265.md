foxtrots:/ˈfɒkstrɒts/ | n. |狐步舞 |A foxtrot is a smooth, flowing ballroom dance characterized by a slow, graceful rhythm.  It's typically danced to music with a moderate tempo. |例句：They danced a foxtrot flawlessly at the wedding. 他们在婚礼上完美地跳了一支狐步舞。
They learned to foxtrot for their anniversary.  他们为了庆祝结婚纪念日学习跳狐步舞。|近义词： waltz, tango |反义词：None specifically.
