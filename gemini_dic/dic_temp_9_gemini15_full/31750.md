cheetah:/ˈtʃiːtə/| n. |猎豹|A large, slender, spotted cat that is the fastest land animal.  It is known for its speed and agility in hunting prey.|例句：The cheetah is renowned for its incredible speed. 猎豹以其惊人的速度而闻名。|例句：The cheetah chased the gazelle across the savanna. 猎豹在稀树草原上追逐瞪羚。|近义词：leopard (but with key differences in appearance and hunting style)|反义词：slow, sluggish
