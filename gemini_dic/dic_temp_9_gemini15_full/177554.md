rebozo:/rɛˈboʊzoʊ/| n. |披肩，围巾 |A rebozo is a lightweight shawl, often woven, traditionally worn by women in Mexico and other parts of Latin America.  It can be used for various purposes, including warmth, carrying a baby, and as a fashion accessory. | Example: The woman elegantly draped the rebozo around her shoulders.  这位女士优雅地将披肩披在肩上。 The mother used her rebozo to carry her baby.  这位母亲用她的披肩抱着婴儿。 |近义词：shawl, scarf, wrap|反义词：无
