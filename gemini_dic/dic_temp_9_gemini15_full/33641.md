cinem:/ˈsɪnɛm/| n. |电影院；电影业|Cinema refers to the art or practice of filmmaking, the industry associated with it, or a movie theater.  It can also refer to motion pictures in general. |例句：The new cinema has a huge screen. 新电影院有一个巨大的屏幕。|例句：The cinema industry is booming. 电影业正在蓬勃发展。|近义词：movie theater, movie house, film industry |反义词：None.
