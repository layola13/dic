raffle-off:/ˈræfəl ɔːf/| vt.|抽奖赠送，以抽奖方式处理|To distribute (something) by means of a raffle.  This phrasal verb implies that something is given away as a prize in a raffle.|例句：They're raffling off a new car to raise money for charity. = 他们正在进行抽奖活动，奖品是一辆新车，目的是为慈善机构筹款。


例句：The company raffled off several vacation packages to its employees. = 公司向员工抽奖赠送了几套度假套餐。


近义词：give away, distribute by lottery


反义词：keep, retain


词性：动词
