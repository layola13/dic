deoxidization:/ˌdiːˌɒksɪdaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |脱氧；去氧化；还原 |The process of removing oxygen from a substance.  This often involves chemical reactions that reduce the oxidation state of elements within the substance.  For example, rust removal is a form of deoxidization. |例句：The deoxidization process is crucial in the production of high-purity metals.  脱氧过程对于生产高纯度金属至关重要。

The deoxidization of the copper wire improved its conductivity. 铜线的脱氧提高了其导电性。|近义词：reduction, oxygen removal |反义词：oxidation, oxygenation |词性：名词
