limbed:/lɪmd/| adj.|有肢体的；长有…肢体的|Having limbs of a specified kind or number.  The word often describes animals, emphasizing the type or number of limbs they possess.|例句：A four-limbed creature.  一只四肢动物。

A six-limbed insect. 一种六足昆虫。|近义词：legged (often used interchangeably)|反义词：limbless
