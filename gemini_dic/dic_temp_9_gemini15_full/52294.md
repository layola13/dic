dichroous: /daɪˈkroʊəs/ | adj. | 双色的，二色的 | Exhibiting two distinct colors, especially when viewed in different directions or under different lighting conditions.  | Example:  The dichroous gemstone appeared blue when viewed from one angle and green from another. 这块双色宝石从一个角度看是蓝色，从另一个角度看是绿色。 | 近义词:bicolor, two-toned | 反义词:monochromatic, unicolor
