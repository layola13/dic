downmost: /ˈdaʊnmoʊst/ | adj. | 最下面的，最低的 |  Situated farthest down.  This is a superlative adjective, indicating the lowest position in a vertical arrangement.  |例句：The downmost drawer was stuck. 最下面的抽屉卡住了。

The downmost branch of the river is narrowest. 河流最下游的一支最窄。


近义词：lowest, bottommost | 反义词：topmost, uppermost


词性：形容词
