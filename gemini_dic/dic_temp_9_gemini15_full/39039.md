conflicts:/ˈkɒnflɪkts/ /ˈkɑːnflɪkts/| n. v.|冲突，矛盾；抵触|

英文解析:A serious disagreement or argument, typically a protracted one.  A clash between ideas, principles, or interests.  To be incompatible or at variance; clash.

例句:
The two countries are in armed conflict. 这两个国家处于战争状态。

There's a conflict between what he says and what he does.  他的言行不一。


近义词:disagreements, clashes, disputes, controversies, arguments, battles, struggles

反义词:agreement, harmony, accord, concord, peace


词性:Noun, Verb


USAGE->

The project conflicts with my other commitments. 这个项目与我的其他承诺冲突。
Their schedules often conflict. 他们的日程安排经常冲突。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:
