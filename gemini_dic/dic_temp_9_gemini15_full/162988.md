precipitators:/prɪˈsɪpɪteɪtərz/| n.|沉淀器；除尘器|A precipitator is a device used to remove particles from a gas stream, typically by causing them to precipitate out of the gas.  This is often achieved through electrostatic forces (in electrostatic precipitators) or other methods.|Example:  The factory installed new electrostatic precipitators to reduce air pollution. 工厂安装了新的静电沉淀器以减少空气污染。The power plant uses precipitators to remove fly ash from its exhaust gases. 发电厂使用沉淀器去除废气中的飞灰。|近义词：collector, filter, scrubber|反义词：emitter
