quasi-craftily: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈkræftɪli/ | adv. | 近乎狡猾地，似是而非地狡猾 |  Almost craftily; deceptively clever in a subtle way.  It suggests a level of cunning that is not quite fully developed or overt. |


例句：

He quasi-craftily avoided answering the direct question. = 他近乎狡猾地回避了直接的问题。

She manipulated the situation quasi-craftily to her advantage. = 她似是而非地狡猾地操纵了局面以利于自己。


近义词：subtly, slyly, cunningly, artfully, deviously


反义词：openly, honestly, frankly, straightforwardly


词性：副词
