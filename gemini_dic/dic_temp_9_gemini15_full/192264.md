screen-testing:/ˈskriːnˌtɛstɪŋ/ | n. | 屏幕测试，试镜 | The process of evaluating a candidate's suitability for a role through a filmed performance. This often involves reading lines from a script in front of a camera.  |  Example: The actress underwent rigorous screen-testing before landing the lead role.  女演员在获得主角之前进行了严格的屏幕测试。 |  近义词: audition, tryout, casting call | 反义词: N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They screen-test actors for the new movie every week. = 他们每周都会为新电影进行演员筛选测试。
The production company screen-tests hundreds of candidates. = 制片公司对数百名候选人进行筛选测试。


一般过去时:
She screen-tested for the part yesterday. = 她昨天为这个角色进行了试镜。
He successfully screen-tested and got the job. = 他成功通过了试镜并得到了这份工作。


现在完成时:
They have screen-tested several promising actors. = 他们已经测试了几位有潜力的演员。
The director has already screen-tested all the applicants. = 导演已经测试了所有申请者。


过去完成时:

She had screen-tested for several roles before getting this one. = 在得到这个角色之前，她已经为几个角色试镜过。

虚拟语气:
