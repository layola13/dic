regentess:/ˈriːdʒəntəs/| n.|摄政女王，女摄政王|A woman who rules as regent; a female regent.  A regent is a person who rules a country during the minority, absence, or disability of the sovereign.|例句：The regentess ruled wisely and justly during the king's illness.  摄政女王在国王生病期间英明公正地统治着国家。


例句：During the king's long absence, the regentess acted as his substitute. 在国王长期不在期间，摄政女王担任了他的代理人。


近义词：governess, sovereign (in context)|反义词：subject


词性:名词
