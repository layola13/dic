prey on: /preɪ ˈɒn/ | vt. | 捕食；欺骗；折磨 | To hunt and kill for food; to take advantage of someone's weaknesses or vulnerabilities.  | 例句：Lions prey on zebras.  狮子捕食斑马。
                                                                                                     The scammer preyed on the elderly woman's loneliness.  骗子利用了这位老年妇女的孤独。
| 近义词：hunt, exploit, victimize | 反义词：protect, defend, assist | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Predators prey on smaller animals. = 捕食者捕食较小的动物。
The company preys on unsuspecting consumers. = 这家公司欺骗毫无戒心的消费者。

一般过去时:
The wolves preyed on the deer herd. = 狼群捕食鹿群。
He preyed on her kindness and generosity. = 他利用了她的善良和慷慨。

现在完成时:
The hawk has preyed on many mice this week. = 这只鹰本周已经捕食了许多老鼠。
The con artist has preyed on numerous victims. = 这位骗子已经欺骗了许多受害者。

过去完成时:
The fox had preyed on several rabbits before the hunter arrived. = 在猎人到来之前，狐狸已经捕食了几只兔子。
The company had preyed on investors for years before it was exposed. = 这家公司在被揭露之前已经欺骗投资者多年了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
