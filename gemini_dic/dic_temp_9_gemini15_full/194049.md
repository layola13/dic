self-government:/ˈsɛlf ˈɡʌvənmənt/| n. |自治；自我管理；自主权 |The right or power of a group or community to govern itself without external interference.  It often implies a degree of independence from a larger governing body. |例句：Many colonies fought for self-government from the mother country.  许多殖民地为摆脱宗主国的统治而争取自治。


例句：The university enjoys a large degree of self-government in its academic affairs. 这所大学在学术事务上享有很大的自主权。


近义词：autonomy, home rule, self-rule, independence |反义词：external control, subjugation, dependence
