pleader:/ˈpliːdər/| n.|辩护人，请愿者|A person who pleads, especially a lawyer who argues a case in court.  It can also refer to someone who makes an earnest request or appeal.|例句：The skilled pleader won the case for his client.  这位熟练的辩护人赢得了为其客户打赢了官司。|例句：She was a tireless pleader for social justice. 她是一位为社会正义不知疲倦地奔走呼吁的人。|近义词：advocate, lawyer, petitioner|反义词：defendant, opponent|名词
