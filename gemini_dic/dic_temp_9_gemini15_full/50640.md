derision:/dɪˈrɪʒən/| n. |嘲笑；嘲弄；愚弄|The act of deriding or mocking someone or something; contemptuous ridicule.  It implies a feeling of superiority and often involves making fun of someone's flaws or shortcomings. |例句：His attempts at humor were met with derision. 他的幽默尝试遭到了嘲笑。

例句：The politician's proposal was met with widespread derision. 这位政治家的提案遭到了广泛的嘲笑。


近义词：mockery, scorn, ridicule, contempt, jeering

反义词：praise, admiration, approval, respect


词源：源于古法语 *derision，源于拉丁语 *deridere，意思是“嘲笑”，由 *de-（“向下”）和 *ridere（“笑”）构成。
