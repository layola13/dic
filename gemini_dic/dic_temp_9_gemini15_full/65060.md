eternalized:/ɪˈtɜːrnəlaɪzd/|/ˈɪtɜːrnəlaɪzd/| v. |使永恒；使不朽|To make something eternal or immortal; to preserve something in lasting memory or fame.|His heroic deeds were eternalized in song and story.  他的英雄事迹被载入歌史。|The artist's work eternalized the beauty of the landscape. 艺术家的作品使这片风景的美丽永存。|immortalize, immortal, perpetuate|temporary, ephemeral, transient|动词

He eternalizes his memories in a book. = 他把回忆写进书里，使其永存。
