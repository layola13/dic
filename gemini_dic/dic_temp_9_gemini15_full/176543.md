re-screened:/ˌriːˈskriːnd/ | v. | 重新筛选；再次审查 | To screen or examine again.  This implies a previous screening process.  | 例句：The film was re-screened after receiving positive reviews. = 这部电影在收到好评后被重新上映。

The application was re-screened to ensure it met all the requirements. = 为了确保申请满足所有要求，它被重新审查。| 近义词：re-examine, review, reassess | 反义词：accept (without further review), reject
