selenographist:/ˌsɛlɪˈnɒɡrəfɪst/| n.|月面学家|A selenographist is a scientist who studies the surface features of the Moon.  They use various methods, including telescopic observation, satellite imagery, and data from lunar missions, to map, analyze, and understand the Moon's geological history and composition. |The selenographist presented her findings on the newly discovered lunar crater. 月面学家展示了她关于新发现的月球陨石坑的研究结果。| astronomer, lunar geologist | (no direct antonym, perhaps "someone who studies celestial bodies other than the moon")|名词
