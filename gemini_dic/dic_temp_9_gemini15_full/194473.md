self-respect: /ˌsɛlf rɪˈspɛkt/ | n. |自尊，自重 | Self-respect refers to the feeling of having a high regard for oneself, valuing one's own worth, and behaving in ways that align with one's values and beliefs. It involves a sense of self-worth and confidence in one's abilities and character.  |  She showed great self-respect by refusing to compromise her principles. 她拒绝妥协自己的原则，展现了极大的自尊。 He lacked self-respect and allowed others to walk all over him. 他缺乏自尊，让别人随意践踏他。| 近义词：self-esteem, dignity, pride | 反义词：self-loathing, humiliation, degradation | 名词
