hellhound:/ˈhɛlhaʊnd/| n.|地狱猎犬，恶犬|A hellhound is a mythical creature, often depicted as a large, fierce dog associated with hell, the underworld, or death.  It is often portrayed as a demonic or supernatural being, guarding the gates of hell or serving as a messenger of death.  The specific characteristics of a hellhound vary across different cultures and mythologies.|例句：In the legend, Cerberus, the three-headed hellhound, guarded the gates of the Underworld.  在传说中，三头地狱猎犬刻耳柏洛斯守护着冥界之门。

例句：The dark forest was said to be haunted by a hellhound, its howls echoing through the night.  据说黑暗森林里出没着一只地狱猎犬，它的嚎叫声回荡在夜空中。|近义词：demon dog, infernal hound, spectral hound|反义词：guardian angel, celestial being
