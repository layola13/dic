overextrapolation:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈstræpəleɪʃən/| n. |过度外推；过度推断|The act of extending or applying a method, result, or conclusion beyond its logical limits or the range of data on which it is based.  It often leads to inaccurate or misleading predictions.  |例句：Overextrapolation of the initial market success led to a significant financial loss for the company. 过度推断最初的市场成功导致公司遭受了巨大的经济损失。

例句：The scientist's overextrapolation of his research findings to the entire population proved to be flawed. 科学家将其研究结果过度推断到整个人群，结果证明是错误的。


近义词：extrapolation, overgeneralization, overestimation


反义词：interpolation, underestimation


词性：名词
