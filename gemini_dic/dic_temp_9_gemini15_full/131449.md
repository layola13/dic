noncoercively:/ˌnɒnkəʊˈɜːrsɪvli/| adj.|非强制性地，非胁迫性地|Without the use of force or pressure; gently and persuasively.|The company encourages employees to participate in training programs noncoercively.|近义词：voluntarily, persuasively, gently|反义词：coercively, forcefully, compulsorily


词源:

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not"
* **coercive**: adjective meaning "relating to or using force or threats"  (From Latin *coercīvus*, from *coercēre* "to restrain, curb, keep in check")
* **-ly**: adverbial suffix


用法说明:

该词主要用作副词，修饰动词，表示以非强制、非胁迫的方式进行某种行为。强调的是自愿性和温和性。


例句:

The government encouraged citizens to adopt energy-saving measures noncoercively.  = 政府鼓励公民以非强制的方式采取节能措施。

They attempted to resolve the conflict noncoercively through negotiation. = 他们试图通过谈判以非强制的方式解决冲突。


近义词：

voluntarily (自愿地)
persuasively (有说服力地)
gently (温柔地)
peacefully (和平地)


反义词：

coercively (强制地)
forcefully (强制地)
compulsorily (强制性地)
authoritatively (权威地)


注意:  由于 "noncoercively"本身是一个比较正式的词汇，在日常口语中，人们通常会使用更简洁的近义词来表达同样的意思。
