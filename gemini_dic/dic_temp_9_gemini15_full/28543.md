cardiogram:/ˈkɑːrdiəɡræm/|/ˈkɑːrdiaʊɡræm/| n. |心电图 |A cardiogram is a visual record of the heart's electrical activity, typically displayed as a graph showing the changes in voltage over time.  It's used to diagnose various heart conditions. |The doctor ordered a cardiogram to check for irregular heartbeats.  医生开具了心电图检查以排除心律不齐的可能性。|electrocardiogram (ECG), EKG |  |名词
