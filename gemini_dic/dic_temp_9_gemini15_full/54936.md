dispossess:/dɪspəˈzɛs/| vt.|剥夺…的财产；使失去财产；驱逐；赶走|To deprive someone of possession of something, especially property; to evict.|例句：The landlord tried to dispossess the tenants for non-payment of rent. 房东试图因租金未付而将租户驱逐。|例句：They were dispossessed of their land by the invading army. 他们的土地被入侵的军队夺走了。|近义词：evict, deprive, oust, expel|反义词：possess, endow, grant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The landlord dispossesses tenants who fail to pay rent. = 房东会驱逐不付房租的租户。
He dispossesses his enemies of their power. = 他剥夺了敌人的权力。

一般过去时:
The farmer was dispossessed of his land after the drought. = 干旱过后，这位农民失去了他的土地。
The government dispossessed many families of their homes. = 政府剥夺了许多家庭的房屋。

现在完成时:
The court has dispossessed him of his assets. = 法院已经剥夺了他的资产。
They have been dispossessed of their ancestral lands. = 他们祖传的土地已经被剥夺了。


过去完成时:
The family had been dispossessed of their property before the war. = 在战争爆发前，这个家庭已经被剥夺了财产。
He had already been dispossessed of his inheritance. = 他已经被剥夺了继承权。

将来时:
The bank will dispossess the homeowner if the mortgage is not paid. = 如果抵押贷款未偿还，银行将没收房主房产。
They fear they will be dispossessed of their homes. = 他们担心会被赶出家园。

虚拟语气:
If they failed to pay the rent, they would be dispossessed. = 如果他们不付房租，他们就会被赶出去。
I wish I hadn't been dispossessed of my inheritance. = 我真希望我没有被剥夺继承权。

被动语态:
The tenants were dispossessed of their apartment. = 租户被赶出了他们的公寓。
He was dispossessed of his rightful share. = 他被剥夺了他应得的那份。

疑问句:
Will the bank dispossess the family of their house? = 银行会把这个家庭赶出他们的房子吗？
Were they dispossessed of their land unfairly? = 他们是否被不公平地剥夺了土地？

否定句:
The court did not dispossess him of his property. = 法院没有剥夺他的财产。
They were not dispossessed of their belongings. = 他们的财物没有被没收。


PHRASE->
be dispossessed of = 被剥夺…
dispossess someone of something = 剥夺某人某物
dispossessed people = 被剥夺财产的人
dispossess oneself of = 放弃；摆脱
dispossessed class = 被剥夺阶级


词源:
dis- (表示否定或相反) + possess (拥有)  The prefix "dis-" indicates the reversal or negation of the action of the verb "possess".
