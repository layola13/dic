increasers:/ɪnˈkriːsər/| n. |增加者；促进因素|A person or thing that increases something.  An increaser can refer to something that causes an increase in quantity, size, value, or intensity.  It can also refer to a person or thing that promotes or accelerates growth or development.|例句：Fertilizers are increasers of crop yields.  肥料是提高作物产量的促进因素。|例句：The new marketing campaign proved to be a significant increaser of sales. 新的营销活动被证明是销售额显著增长的促进因素。|近义词：augmenter, enhancer, booster, promoter|反义词：decreaser, reducer, diminisher


USAGE->
