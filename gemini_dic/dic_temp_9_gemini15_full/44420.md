cross-platform: /ˌkrɒsˈplætfɔːrm/ | adj. |跨平台的|  Supporting multiple operating systems or platforms.  Software or applications designed to be compatible with different operating systems, such as Windows, macOS, Android, and iOS, are considered cross-platform. This eliminates the need to develop separate versions for each platform. | 例句：This game is cross-platform, so you can play with your friends regardless of what devices they use. 这个游戏是跨平台的，所以无论你的朋友使用什么设备，你都可以和他们一起玩。  Many developers choose cross-platform frameworks to reduce development time and costs. 许多开发者选择跨平台框架以减少开发时间和成本。| 近义词：multi-platform, platform-independent | 反义词：platform-specific, single-platform
