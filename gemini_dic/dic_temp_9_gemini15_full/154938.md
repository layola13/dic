pesterer:/pɛˈstərər/| n. |纠缠者，烦扰者 |A pesterer is someone who persistently bothers or annoys someone else.  They repeatedly make demands or requests, often ignoring the other person's wishes. |例句：He became a real pesterer after he lost his job, constantly asking for money. 他失业后成了个真正的纠缠者，不断地要钱。|近义词：nuisance, annoyance, bother, tormentor |反义词：helper, benefactor


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The child constantly pesters his parents for sweets. =  孩子不断地缠着父母要糖果。
She pesters her colleagues with pointless questions. = 她用毫无意义的问题烦扰她的同事。


一般过去时:
He pestered me with phone calls all day. = 他一整天都用电话骚扰我。
They pestered the waiter for the bill. = 他们不断地催促服务员结账。


现在完成时:
He has pestered me for weeks about this project. = 关于这个项目，他已经烦扰我几周了。
She has pestered the landlord to fix the leaky roof. = 她一直纠缠房东修理漏雨的屋顶。


过去完成时:
I had pestered him for days before he finally agreed. = 我纠缠了他好几天他才终于同意。
She had pestered her boss for a promotion for months. = 她已经纠缠她的老板好几个月要升职了。


将来时:
I know he will pester me again about this. = 我知道他会再次为这事纠缠我。
She will pester him until he gives in. = 她会一直纠缠他直到他屈服。


虚拟语气:
If he pestered me less, I might be more willing to help. = 如果他不那么纠缠我，我可能更愿意帮忙。
I wish he wouldn't pester me so much. = 我希望他不要再这么烦我了。


被动语态:
He was pestered by creditors for months. = 好几个月他都被债主纠缠。
The manager was constantly pestered with complaints. =  经理不断地受到投诉的烦扰。


疑问句:
Is he pestering you again? = 他又在纠缠你吗？
Are they pestering the teacher with questions? = 他们正在用问题烦扰老师吗？


否定句:
Don't pester me while I'm working. = 我工作的时候别烦我。
He didn't pester her about the debt. = 他没有因为债务纠缠她。
