signalist:/ˈsɪɡnəlɪst/ | n. |信号员，信号手 | A person who sends or receives signals, especially in a railway system or on a ship.  | Example: The signalist quickly relayed the urgent message to the approaching train. 信号员迅速地将紧急信息传递给了即将到来的火车。 |近义词：signaller, telegraphist |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The signalist sends signals to the train. = 信号员向火车发送信号。
The signalist monitors the signals constantly. = 信号员持续监控信号。


一般过去时:
The signalist sent a warning signal. = 信号员发送了一个警告信号。
The signalist received a message from the captain. = 信号员收到了船长的信息。


现在完成时:
The signalist has sent hundreds of signals today. = 信号员今天已经发送了数百个信号。
The signalist has worked here for ten years. = 信号员在这里工作了十年。


过去完成时:
The signalist had already sent the signal before the train arrived. = 在火车到达之前，信号员已经发送了信号。
The signalist had never seen such a complex signal system. = 信号员从未见过如此复杂的信号系统。


将来时:
The signalist will send the signal at 3 pm. = 信号员将在下午三点发送信号。
The signalist will be responsible for the new system. = 信号员将负责新的系统。


虚拟语气:
If the signalist had seen the obstruction, he would have stopped the train. = 如果信号员看到障碍物，他就会停车。
I wish the signalist had acted more quickly. = 我希望信号员行动更快。


被动语态:
The signal was sent by the signalist. = 信号是由信号员发送的。
The new system is being monitored by the signalist. = 新系统正在由信号员监控。


疑问句:
Is the signalist on duty? = 信号员值班吗？
What signal did the signalist send? = 信号员发送了什么信号？


否定句:
The signalist did not see the approaching train. = 信号员没有看到即将到来的火车。
The signalist was not able to contact the station. = 信号员无法联系车站。
