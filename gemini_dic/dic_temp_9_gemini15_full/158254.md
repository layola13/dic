play-for-time:/pleɪ fɔːr taɪm/| v. |拖延时间|To deliberately delay something, especially to gain time or avoid a difficult situation. This phrase implies a conscious effort to prolong a situation, often to buy time to think, prepare, or let events unfold favorably.|例句：The government is playing for time, hoping the crisis will resolve itself. 政府在拖延时间，希望危机能自行解决。

例句：She played for time while she searched for a better answer. 她拖延时间，以便寻找更好的答案。


近义词：delay, stall, procrastinate, temporize|反义词：hurry, rush, expedite|词性：动词
