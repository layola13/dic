intervenience:/ˌɪntərˈviːniəns/| n. |干涉；介入；调解|The act of intervening; interference; mediation.  It refers to the action of coming between parties to settle a dispute or to prevent something from happening.  The word suggests a more formal or official intervention than simple interference.|例句：The intervenience of the mediator prevented a violent confrontation. 调解员的介入阻止了一场暴力冲突。
The intervenience of the government was necessary to stabilize the economy. 政府的干预对于稳定经济是必要的。|近义词：intervention, mediation, interference|反义词：non-interference, passivity
