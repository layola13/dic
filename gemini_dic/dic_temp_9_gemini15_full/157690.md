pixeling:/ˈpɪksəliŋ/ | n. v. | 像素化；图像失真 | The process of converting an image into a grid of pixels, often resulting in a loss of detail and a blocky appearance.  This can be done intentionally for artistic effect or unintentionally due to low resolution or compression.  As a verb, it refers to the act of making an image pixelated. | 例句：The old photo was severely pixeling due to poor scanning quality. 这张旧照片由于扫描质量差而严重像素化。  The artist intentionally pixeling the image to create a retro effect.  艺术家故意将图像像素化以创造复古效果。 | 近义词：pixelation, digitization (in some contexts) | 反义词：high-resolution, smooth
