nos:/noʊz/| n.|鼻子，嗅觉|The nose is the part of the face above the mouth and below the forehead, used for breathing and smelling.  It's also a prominent feature on the face, contributing to a person's overall appearance.|例句：He broke his nose in a fight. 他在打架中摔断了鼻子。|例句：The dog followed the scent with its keen nose. 狗用它灵敏的鼻子追踪气味。|近义词：snout, proboscis|反义词：无
