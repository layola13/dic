foretoken:/ˈfɔːrˌtoʊkən/|/ˈfɔːrˌtəʊkən/| n. |预兆，前兆 |A foretoken is an indication or sign of something that is going to happen in the future, especially something bad.  It often suggests a warning or omen. |例句：The dark clouds were a foretoken of the approaching storm.  乌云是暴风雨即将来临的预兆。

The strange noises in the night were a foretoken of trouble. 夜晚的怪声预示着麻烦。|近义词：omen, portent, presage, harbinger |反义词：epilogue, aftermath
