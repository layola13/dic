resealable: /ˌriːˈsiːləbl/ | adj. | 可重新密封的 | Capable of being sealed again after being opened.  This adjective describes containers, bags, or packages that can be closed tightly after opening, preventing the contents from spilling or spoiling. |  例句：This bag is resealable, so you can keep the snacks fresh. 这个袋子是可重新密封的，所以你可以保持零食新鲜。


例句：The resealable container kept the cookies crisp.  这个可重新密封的容器让饼干保持酥脆。


近义词：reclosable, reusable


反义词：non-resealable, disposable


词源:  The word "resealable" is a combination of the prefix "re-" (meaning "again"), the verb "seal" (meaning "to close tightly"), and the suffix "-able" (meaning "capable of being").  Therefore, it literally means "capable of being sealed again."
