pomology:/pɒˈmɒlədʒi/| n. |果树栽培学；果树学|The scientific and practical study of fruit cultivation, including the study of fruit trees, their growth, and their fruits.  It encompasses aspects of horticulture, botany, and plant pathology related to fruit production. |Example:  She studied pomology at university and now runs a successful orchard. 她大学学习果树栽培学，现在经营着一个成功的果园。|近义词：fruit growing, fruit cultivation |反义词：无
