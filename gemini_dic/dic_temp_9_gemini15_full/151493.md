parried:/ˈpærɪd/| vt.| 挡开；避开；巧妙地回答 | To deflect or ward off (a blow, attack, or question) skillfully. |例句：The swordsman parried his opponent's blow.  剑客挡开了对手的攻击。

例句：She skillfully parried the journalist's difficult questions. 她巧妙地回答了记者的刁难问题。|近义词：deflect, ward off, fend off, evade |反义词：accept, confront
