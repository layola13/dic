hitchily:/ˈhɪtʃɪli/| adv.| 结结巴巴地；不顺利地；颠簸地 | In an uneven or jerky manner; with difficulty or hesitation. |例句：The car climbed the hill hitchily.  这辆车颠簸地爬上了山。|例句：He spoke hitchily, as if unsure of what to say. 他结结巴巴地说话，好像不知道说什么。|近义词： jerkily, unevenly, awkwardly |反义词： smoothly, effortlessly
