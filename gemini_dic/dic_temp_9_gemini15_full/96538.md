indomitably:/ɪnˈdɑːmɪtəbli/ | adv. |不屈不挠地；顽强地 |  In an indomitable manner; unshakably; resolutely.  It describes an action performed with unwavering determination and courage, despite facing significant obstacles or adversity. |  She fought indomitably against the disease. 她不屈不挠地与疾病作斗争。 The little plant, indomitably, pushed its way through the crack in the pavement.  小植物不屈不挠地从人行道上的裂缝中生长出来。|近义词：unyieldingly, resolutely, stubbornly, tenaciously |反义词：weakly, submissively, meekly
