fret:/fret/ | v. | 烦躁；焦虑；磨损；(使)烦恼 | To worry or be annoyed; to wear away by rubbing; to cause someone to worry or be annoyed. |例句：He fretted about the exam. 他为考试而焦虑不安。|近义词：worry, annoy, chafe, irritate |反义词：calm, relax, soothe


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He frets over his work. = 他为他的工作而烦恼。
She frets about her children's safety. = 她为孩子们的安全而担忧。


一般过去时:
I fretted all night about the missed deadline. = 我整晚都为错过截止日期而焦虑。
They fretted over the poor weather forecast. = 他们为糟糕的天气预报而担忧。


现在完成时:
She has fretted herself sick over this problem. = 她为这个问题而焦虑得病了。
He has fretted endlessly since he lost his job. = 自从失业以来，他一直非常焦虑。


过去完成时:
I had fretted for days before the important presentation. = 在重要的演示之前，我已经焦虑了好几天。
They had fretted about the trip for weeks, and it turned out fine. = 他们为这次旅行担心了好几个星期，结果一切顺利。


将来时:
I will fret if I don't finish the project on time. = 如果我不能按时完成项目，我会焦虑的。
She will fret about the journey until she arrives safely. = 在她安全到达之前，她会一直为旅途担心。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't have so many deadlines, I wouldn't fret so much. = 如果我没有那么多截止日期，我就不会这么焦虑了。
I wish I didn't fret about everything so much. = 我希望我不必为所有事情都如此担忧。


被动语态:
He was fretted by the constant criticism. = 他被不断的批评弄得很焦虑。
She was fretted by the uncertainty of the future. = 她对未来的不确定性感到焦虑。


疑问句:
Does he fret about his health? = 他担心自己的健康吗？
Are you fretting about the upcoming test? = 你在为即将到来的考试而焦虑吗？


否定句:
Don't fret; everything will be alright. = 别担心；一切都会好起来的。
She didn't fret about the small details. = 她不为那些小细节而烦恼。


PHRASE->
fret over something = 为某事而烦恼
fret about something = 为某事而担忧
fret oneself = 使自己烦恼
fret and fume = 焦躁不安
fret away = 慢慢磨损
