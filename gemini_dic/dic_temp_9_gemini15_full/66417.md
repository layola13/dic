executors:/ɪɡˈzekjʊtərz/ | n. | 执行者；遗嘱执行人 |  People or entities appointed by a will to carry out the testator's instructions. They manage the deceased's estate, paying debts and distributing assets according to the will's terms.  | The executors of her will sold her house to pay off her debts.  她的遗嘱执行人卖掉了她的房子来偿还她的债务。| administrators, trustees |  (no direct antonym, but contextually opposed to beneficiaries) | 名词
