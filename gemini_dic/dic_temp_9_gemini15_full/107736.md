lap's:/læps/ | n. |一圈；(跑道的)一圈；一段旅程；轻拍；拍打声|a single circuit of a track; a short journey; a single stroke or movement of something; a light pat or slap|例句：She completed the race in three laps. 她三圈跑完了比赛。  The cat gave my leg a gentle lap. 猫轻轻地拍了一下我的腿。|近义词：round, circuit, stroke|反义词：N/A


USAGE->

The dog laps up the spilled milk. = 狗舔掉了洒出来的牛奶。
He laps the track every morning. = 他每天早上绕跑道跑一圈。


一般过去时:
She lapped the field in the final lap. = 在最后一圈她超过了所有对手。
The waves lapped gently against the shore. = 海浪轻轻地拍打着海岸。

疑问句:
