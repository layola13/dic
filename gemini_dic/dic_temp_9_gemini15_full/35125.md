cloudinesses:/ˈklaʊdinəsiz/| n.|阴天；阴沉；浑浊；模糊|The plural of cloudiness, referring to multiple instances of cloudy conditions or the state of being cloudy.  It can refer to the atmosphere, a liquid, or even a metaphorical sense of unclearness or confusion.|例句：The frequent cloudinesses of the autumn months made photography difficult. 秋季频繁的阴天使得摄影变得困难。
The cloudinesses in the water prevented us from seeing the bottom of the lake. 水中的浑浊使我们无法看到湖底。|近义词：overcasts, murkinesses, ambiguities|反义词：clearnesses, brightnesses|名词
