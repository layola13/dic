easygoing: /ˈiːziˌɡoʊɪŋ/ | adj. | 随和的；悠闲的；轻松自在的 | Relaxed and tolerant in attitude or manner; easy to get along with.  |例句：He has an easygoing personality and gets along well with everyone. 他性格随和，和每个人相处得都很好。 |  例句：She has an easygoing approach to life and doesn't worry about minor setbacks. 她对生活采取轻松自在的态度，不会为小挫折而担忧。| 近义词：relaxed, laid-back, casual, nonchalant | 反义词： uptight, tense, stressed, rigid
