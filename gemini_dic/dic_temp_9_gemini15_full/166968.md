prestructuring: /ˌpriːˈstrʌktʃərɪŋ/ | n. | 预先构建；预先结构化 | The act or process of structuring something in advance.  It often involves planning and organizing elements before they are actually implemented or built. This can apply to various contexts, from software development to project management to organizational design. | Example:  The prestructuring of the database significantly improved the efficiency of the application. 预先构建数据库显著提高了应用程序的效率。 | 近义词：advance planning, pre-planning, preparation | 反义词：improvisation, ad-hoc arrangement |  名词
