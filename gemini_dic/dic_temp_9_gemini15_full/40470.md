contributed:/kənˈtrɪbjuːtɪd/| v.|贡献，促成|过去分词，表示贡献或促成的动作已经完成。它描述的是一个对结果或整体产生影响的行为。|He contributed significantly to the success of the project. 他对项目的成功做出了重大贡献。|The new law contributed to a decrease in crime. 新法律促使犯罪率下降。|contributed to, added to, helped with|hindered, obstructed, prevented|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He contributes regularly to the charity. 他定期向慈善机构捐款。
She contributes articles to the magazine. 她为杂志撰稿。

一般过去时:
They contributed generously to the fundraising campaign. 他们慷慨地捐款给募捐活动。
He contributed a chapter to the book. 他为这本书贡献了一章。

现在完成时:
She has contributed greatly to the field of medicine. 她对医学领域做出了巨大贡献。
They have contributed many ideas to the discussion. 他们为讨论贡献了许多想法。

过去完成时:
He had contributed to the project before he left the company. 他在离开公司之前就为该项目做出了贡献。
She had contributed significantly to the research before the publication.  在她发表论文之前，她已经对这项研究做出了重大贡献。


将来时:
He will contribute his expertise to the team. 他将把他的专业知识贡献给团队。
She will contribute to the debate next week. 她下周将参与辩论。


虚拟语气:
If he had contributed more, the result would have been better. 如果他贡献更多，结果会更好。
I wish she had contributed her ideas earlier. 我希望她早点提出她的想法。


被动语态:
Much money was contributed to the disaster relief fund.  很多钱被捐献给了救灾基金。
A significant amount was contributed by anonymous donors.  相当数量的捐款来自匿名捐赠者。


疑问句:
How much did you contribute? 你捐了多少钱？
Did you contribute anything to the discussion? 你对讨论有什么贡献吗？


否定句:
He did not contribute to the project. 他没有为这个项目做贡献。
She didn't contribute any money to the cause. 她没有为这项事业捐款。


PHRASE->

contribute to: 促成，导致
contribute something to something: 向…贡献…
contribute one's share: 尽自己的本分
contribute to the discussion: 参加讨论
contribute to a journal: 向期刊投稿
