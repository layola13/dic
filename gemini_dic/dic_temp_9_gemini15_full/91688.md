hyperconcentrations: /ˌhaɪpərˌkɒnsənˈtreɪʃənz/ | n. |高浓度；超浓度 | Extremely high concentrations of a substance.  These concentrations are significantly above normal or typical levels and often imply a potential risk or hazard. |例句：The spill resulted in hyperconcentrations of pollutants in the river.  泄漏导致河流中污染物浓度过高。

The hyperconcentrations of toxins in the air led to respiratory problems for residents. 空气中有毒物质的高浓度导致居民出现呼吸问题。|近义词：high concentrations, excessive concentrations, saturated concentrations |反义词：low concentrations, dilute concentrations, trace amounts
