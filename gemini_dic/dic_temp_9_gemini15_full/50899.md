desertless:/ˈdɛzərtlɪs/| adj.| 无沙漠的，没有沙漠的 | Lacking deserts; without deserts.  The term typically refers to a geographical area or region. | Example: The desertless landscape was surprisingly lush and green.  这片无沙漠的景观出奇地郁郁葱葱。|近义词：devoid of deserts, desert-free |反义词：deserted, arid
