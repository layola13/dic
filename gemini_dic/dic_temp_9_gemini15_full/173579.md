quasi-immediately:/ˈkwɑːziɪˈmiːdiətli/ | adv. |几乎立即，差不多立刻 |Almost immediately; very soon.  The term combines "quasi" (meaning "seemingly," "apparently," or "to a certain extent") and "immediately" (meaning "at once," "without delay"). It suggests a speed that is close to immediate but not precisely instantaneous. There might be a very brief delay. |例句：The system responded quasi-immediately to the command. 系统几乎立即响应了命令。

例句：The lights went out, and quasi-immediately, the backup generator kicked in. 灯灭了，几乎立刻，备用发电机启动了。|近义词：almost immediately, nearly immediately, virtually immediately |反义词：slowly, gradually, eventually
