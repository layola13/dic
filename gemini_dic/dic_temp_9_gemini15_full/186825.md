ring-toss:/ˈrɪŋtɔs/| n. |套圈游戏|A game of skill in which players throw rings at stakes or other objects to score points.  The goal is to get the rings to land around the stakes. It's often played at carnivals, fairs, and festivals. |Example: The children were having fun playing ring-toss at the county fair.  孩子们在县集会上玩套圈游戏玩得很开心。|近义词：ring game, quoits (if using quoits)|反义词：无
