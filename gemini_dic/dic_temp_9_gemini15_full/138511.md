now-and-then:/ˌnaʊ ænd ðɛn/| adv.|偶尔，有时|The adverb "now and then" means occasionally or from time to time. It indicates that something happens irregularly, not frequently or constantly. |例句：I see my old friends now and then.  = 我偶尔会见到我的老朋友们。|例句：We go to the beach now and then during the summer. = 夏天我们偶尔会去海滩。|近义词：occasionally, sometimes, from time to time, infrequently|反义词：always, constantly, frequently, regularly|副词
