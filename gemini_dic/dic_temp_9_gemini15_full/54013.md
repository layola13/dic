discs: /dɪsks/ | n. | 磁盘；圆盘 |  A disc is a flat, circular object.  The word often refers to compact discs (CDs) or digital versatile discs (DVDs), but can also refer to other circular objects. | Example: I put the music disc into the player. 我把音乐光盘放进了播放器。  The potter carefully shaped the clay disc. 陶工仔细地塑造了陶土圆盘。 |近义词：disk, record, plate | 反义词：None
