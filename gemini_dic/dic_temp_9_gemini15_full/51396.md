determinedly:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪndɪdli/| adv. |坚决地；果断地；毅然地 |In a determined manner; resolutely.  The adverb "determinedly" describes an action performed with firmness of purpose and unwavering resolve. It suggests a strong commitment to a goal and a refusal to be deterred by obstacles.|例句：She  determinedly  pushed through the crowd. 她坚决地挤过人群。

She climbed the mountain determinedly, despite the harsh weather. 她不顾恶劣天气，毅然决然地攀登了这座山。|近义词：resolutely, firmly, resolutely, steadfastly |反义词：hesitantly, weakly, reluctantly
