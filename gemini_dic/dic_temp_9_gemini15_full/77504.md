gazillions:/ɡəˈzɪljənz/| n. |极多，无数|An informal term indicating an extremely large, unspecified number.  It's a hyperbole, not a literal quantity.|例句：There are gazillions of stars in the sky. 天上有无数颗星星。 There were gazillions of people at the concert.  演唱会上人山人海。|近义词：millions, billions, countless, innumerable, a lot, heaps, tons|反义词：few, a handful, little, scarce
