deuterogamy:/djuːtəˈrɒɡəmi/ | n. |  次生配偶制，后婚制 |  The practice or condition of having a second marriage, often after the death of a spouse or divorce. It refers specifically to a secondary or subsequent marriage, as opposed to the first marriage (proterogamy). | Example:  The study focused on the societal acceptance of deuterogamy in various cultures.  这项研究关注不同文化对后婚制的社会接受度。 | 近义词： remarriage, second marriage | 反义词：proterogamy (first marriage)
