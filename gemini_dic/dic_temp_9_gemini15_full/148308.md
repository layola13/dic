oversolicitude:/ˌoʊvərˈsɑːləsɪtuːd/| n. |过分关心；过度关怀|Excessive care or attention; anxious concern.  It often implies that the concern is unnecessary or even harmful.|例句：Her oversolicitude for her child bordered on smothering.  她的过度关怀几乎让孩子喘不过气来。

例句：His oversolicitude prevented his daughter from developing independence. 他的过度关怀妨碍了他的女儿独立发展。


近义词：overprotection, excessive concern, solicitude (but less extreme), fussiness


反义词：neglect, indifference, apathy


词性：名词
