fulfill:/fʊlˈfɪl/ | vt. | 履行；实现；满足 | To carry out or bring to completion (a promise, task, or expectation); to satisfy or meet (a need or requirement). |例句：He fulfilled his promise to help me. 他履行了帮助我的承诺。|例句：The new design fulfills all the safety requirements. 新设计满足了所有安全要求。|近义词：achieve, accomplish, satisfy, complete |反义词：fail, neglect, disappoint


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fulfills his duties diligently. = 他勤勤恳恳地履行职责。
She fulfills her potential as a writer. = 她充分发挥了她作为作家的潜能。

一般过去时:
They fulfilled their contract obligations. = 他们履行了合同义务。
The company fulfilled its sales targets. = 公司完成了销售目标。

现在完成时:
We have fulfilled our quota for this month. = 我们已经完成了这个月的配额。
She has fulfilled her lifelong dream of traveling the world. = 她已经实现了她环游世界的终生梦想。

过去完成时:
He had fulfilled his promise before he left. = 他在离开之前已经履行了他的承诺。
They had fulfilled all the requirements before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经完成了所有要求。

将来时:
We will fulfill our commitment to our clients. = 我们将履行对客户的承诺。
She will fulfill her ambition to become a doctor. = 她将实现她成为一名医生的雄心。

虚拟语气:
If only I could fulfill my desires. = 要是我能实现我的愿望就好了。
I wish I could fulfill my parents' expectations. = 我希望我能实现父母的期望。

被动语态:
His requests were fulfilled promptly. = 他的请求得到了及时的满足。
All the conditions of the agreement were fulfilled. = 协议的所有条件都已履行。

疑问句:
Will the project fulfill its objectives? = 这个项目能实现其目标吗？
Does this product fulfill your needs? = 这个产品能满足你的需求吗？

否定句:
He failed to fulfill his responsibilities. = 他未能履行他的职责。
The company did not fulfill its promises. = 公司没有履行承诺。


PHRASE->
