reservation:/ˌrezərˈveɪʃən/| n. |预约，预订；保留；疑虑，顾虑 |The act of reserving something, such as a seat, room, or table, in advance; a feeling of doubt or hesitation. |例句：I have a dinner reservation at eight o'clock. 我八点钟有一个晚餐预订。  I have some reservations about his plan. 我对他计划有一些疑虑。|近义词：booking, reservation, appointment, doubt, hesitation |反义词：confirmation, certainty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I make a reservation for a table every Friday. = 我每周五都预订一张桌子。
He reserves a room at the hotel. = 他在酒店预订了一个房间。

一般过去时:
We made a reservation for the concert last week. = 我们上周预定了音乐会的票。
She reserved a seat on the train. = 她预订了火车上的一个座位。

现在完成时:
I have reserved a flight to Paris. = 我已经预订了去巴黎的航班。
They have made reservations for the entire group. = 他们已经为整个团队预订了。


过去完成时:
Had you made a reservation before you arrived at the restaurant? = 你到达餐厅之前预订了吗？
They had reserved a private room for the meeting. = 他们已经为会议预订了一个私人房间。


将来时:
I will make a reservation for the show tomorrow. = 我明天将预订演出的票。
We will reserve a car for our trip next month. = 我们下个月将为我们的旅行预订一辆车。


虚拟语气:
If I had made a reservation, we wouldn't be standing in line. = 如果我预订了，我们就不会排队了。
I wish I had made a reservation earlier. = 我希望我早点预订。


被动语态:
The table was reserved for a VIP guest. = 这张桌子被预留给一位贵宾。
A room was reserved in my name. = 一个房间以我的名义预订了。


疑问句:
Have you made a reservation yet? = 你预订了吗？
Did they reserve a table for us? = 他们为我们预订桌子了吗？


否定句:
I didn't make a reservation for the restaurant. = 我没有为这家餐厅预订。
They haven't reserved a car yet. = 他们还没预订车。


PHRASE->
make a reservation = 预订
have a reservation = 有预约
cancel a reservation = 取消预约
hold a reservation = 保留预约
reservation system = 预订系统
