semiprogressive:/ˌsɛmiprəˈɡrɛsɪv/| adj.|半进行的，半渐进的|Combining progressive and non-progressive aspects; denoting an action or state that is partly in progress and partly not.|The semiprogressive aspect in linguistics describes verb forms that express an action in progress but with less emphasis on the duration than a fully progressive aspect.  For instance, "I am working on a project" implies a more ongoing and continuous effort than "I work on projects," which is less specific about ongoing action.|近义词：partially progressive, incompletely progressive|反义词：progressive, non-progressive
