flophouse: /ˈflɒphaʊs/ | n. | 廉价旅馆；贫民窟 | A flophouse is a cheap, often rundown hotel or lodging house, typically inhabited by poor people.  It is often associated with a lack of cleanliness and safety. | Example: He spent his last few dollars on a room in a dingy flophouse. 他把仅剩的几美元花在了肮脏的廉价旅馆里。|近义词：dosshouse, cheap hotel, dive | 反义词：luxury hotel, five-star hotel
