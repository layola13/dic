nervy:/ˈnɜːrvi/| adj.|紧张的；胆大的；鲁莽的|Characterized by or exhibiting nervousness; showing a lack of composure; also, showing boldness or audacity.|He gave a nervy performance, but he pulled it off. 他表现得很紧张，但他还是成功了。 She made a nervy attempt to climb the cliff. 她胆大地尝试攀登悬崖。|近义词：nervous, edgy, audacious, reckless|反义词：calm, composed, relaxed
