possum:/ˈpɒsəm/ /ˈpɑːsəm/| n.|负鼠；装睡的人|A possum is a small, nocturnal marsupial mammal with a prehensile tail.  They are known for playing dead when threatened. |例句：The possum scurried up the tree to escape the dog.  这只负鼠窜上树躲避狗。

The possum feigned death to avoid capture. 这只负鼠假装死亡以避免被捕获。|近义词：opossum|反义词：无
