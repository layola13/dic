rearrangeable: /ˌriːəˈreɪndʒəbəl/ | adj. | 可重新排列的，可重新安排的 | Capable of being rearranged or reorganized.  Something that is rearrangeable can have its components or elements shifted, changed, or reordered to create a different configuration or arrangement. |

例句：

The furniture in the room is rearrangeable to suit different needs.  房间里的家具可以根据不同的需求重新摆放。

The software's modules are rearrangeable, allowing for customized workflows. 该软件的模块是可以重新排列的，允许定制工作流程。


近义词：adjustable, adaptable, modifiable, alterable

反义词：fixed, inflexible, immutable, unchangeable

词性：形容词
