half-heathens:/hɑːfˈhiːðənz/| n.|半异教徒；部分未开化的人|A derogatory term referring to people considered only partially converted to Christianity or exhibiting a mixture of Christian and pagan beliefs or practices.  It implies a lack of full commitment or understanding of Christian faith.|The missionaries encountered many half-heathens in the remote villages.|近义词：partially converted, cultural hybrids|反义词：fully devout, orthodox believers
