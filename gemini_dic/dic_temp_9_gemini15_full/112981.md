lulled:/lʌld/ | v. | 使平静；使入睡；使安心；哄骗 | to calm (someone) into a state of peace or quiet; to soothe; to deceive or lull into a false sense of security |例句：The gentle music lulled her to sleep. 轻柔的音乐使她睡着了。

The rhythmic sound of the waves lulled him into a state of tranquility. 海浪有节奏的声音使他平静下来。


近义词：soothed, calmed, tranquilized, pacified | 反义词：agitated, excited, aroused


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The gentle breeze lulls the baby to sleep. = 轻柔的微风哄着婴儿睡觉。
The monotonous drone lulls the audience into boredom. = 单调的嗡嗡声使观众感到厌烦。


一般过去时:
The rocking chair lulled her to sleep. = 摇椅哄她睡着了。
The salesman lulled the customer into a false sense of security. = 销售员哄骗顾客产生虚假的安全感。


现在完成时:
The constant humming of the refrigerator has lulled me into a deep sleep. = 冰箱持续的嗡嗡声已经让我睡得很沉。
The government's promises have lulled the public into complacency. = 政府的承诺已经使公众变得自满。


过去完成时:
The steady rain had lulled the city into a quiet slumber. = 持续的雨水已经使城市陷入宁静的沉睡。
They had been lulled into a false sense of security by the initial successes. = 他们最初的成功使他们产生了虚假的安全感。


将来时:
The soft music will lull the child to sleep. = 轻柔的音乐将会哄孩子睡觉。
The politician's speech will likely lull the voters into complacency. = 这位政治家的演讲可能会使选民变得自满。


虚拟语气:
If the music were softer, it would lull me to sleep more easily. = 如果音乐更轻柔一些，它会更容易让我入睡。
I wish the noise would lull me to sleep instead of keeping me awake. = 我希望噪音能让我睡着，而不是让我醒着。


被动语态:
The baby was lulled to sleep by its mother's gentle singing. = 婴儿被母亲轻柔的歌声哄睡了。
The public was lulled into a false sense of security by the government’s assurances. = 公众被政府的保证哄骗产生了虚假的安全感。


疑问句:
Does the monotonous sound lull you to sleep? = 单调的声音会让你睡着吗？
Will the soothing music lull the baby to sleep? = 舒缓的音乐会哄婴儿睡觉吗？


否定句:
The loud noise did not lull her to sleep. = 嘈杂的噪音并没有让她睡着。
The harsh reality did not lull them into complacency. = 严酷的现实并没有让他们自满。


PHRASE->
be lulled into a false sense of security  = 产生虚假的安全感
lull someone to sleep = 哄某人睡觉
lull the pain = 减轻痛苦
lull the anxiety = 减轻焦虑
lull a crisis = 平息危机


词源:
源自古英语 *lullan，意为“哄睡，使平静”。与 lull 的含义相关的词根在日耳曼语系中很常见，例如古高地德语 lullen 和古诺尔斯语 lulla。  最终源头可能与PIE *(g)leu- “弯曲，柔顺”相关。
