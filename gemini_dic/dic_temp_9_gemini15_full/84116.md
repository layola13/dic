half-light:/ˈhɑːfˌlaɪt/ | n. adj. | 微光，朦胧的光线；半明半暗 |  Dim light; the light of twilight or dawn.  It can also describe a state of indistinctness or uncertainty. |例句：The half-light of dawn revealed the extent of the damage.  拂晓的微光显露出了损害的程度。

The painting was done in the half-light of a winter's afternoon. 这幅画是在冬日下午的朦胧光线下完成的。|近义词：twilight, dusk, dim light, penumbra |反义词：bright light, full light, sunshine
