castrates:/ˈkæstreɪts/| vt.|阉割；使…丧失活力|To castrate is to remove the testicles of a male animal or to surgically remove the ovaries of a female animal.  It can also refer to the act of suppressing or weakening something, rendering it ineffective.|例句：The farmer castrates his young bulls to prevent them from becoming aggressive.  这个农民阉割了他的小公牛以防止它们变得具有攻击性。

The government's policies castrated the growth of the economy. 政府的政策扼杀了经济的增长。|近义词：emasculate, geld, neuter|反义词：enable, empower
