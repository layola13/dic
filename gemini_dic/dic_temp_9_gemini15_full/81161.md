granddad:/ˈɡrændæd/| n. |爷爷；外公|A grandfather; a father's or mother's father.  The word is a blend of "grand" and "dad".|My granddad told me many stories about his childhood.  我的爷爷给我讲了很多关于他童年的故事。|grandfather, grandpa|grandson, grandchild|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My granddad lives in the countryside. = 我的爷爷住在乡下。
Does your granddad still work? = 你的爷爷还工作吗？


一般过去时:
