incongruity: /ɪnˈkɒŋɡruəti/ | n. |不一致；不协调；不适合 |A lack of harmony or agreement; incompatibility.  The state of being incongruous. |例句：The incongruity between his words and his actions was striking.  他的言行不一令人震惊。

例句：There was a certain incongruity in his wearing a tuxedo to a barbecue. 他穿着燕尾服去烧烤，这有点不协调。


近义词：discrepancy, inconsistency, dissonance, disparity, mismatch

反义词：harmony, agreement, consistency, congruity, accord


词源:  From Middle French incongruité, from Latin incongruus "incompatible," from in- "not" + congruus "fitting, suitable," from com- "together" + gruere "to meet, fit."
