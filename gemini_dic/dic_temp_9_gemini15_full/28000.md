canzonet:/ˌkænzəˈnet/| n. |小歌；短歌；小夜曲 |A canzonet is a short, lyrical song, typically of Italian origin, often characterized by a light and melodious character.  It's frequently associated with 16th and 17th-century Italian music. |Example: The composer wrote a beautiful canzonet for his beloved.  这位作曲家为他的爱人创作了一首优美的短歌。  |近义词：song, ballad, air, madrigal |反义词：epic, symphony, opera|名词
