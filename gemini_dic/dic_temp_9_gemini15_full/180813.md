rehangs:/ˌriːˈhæŋz/| v.|重新悬挂，再挂上|To hang something again.|例句：He rehangs the pictures after repainting the walls. 他重新挂上画，墙重新粉刷后。|例句：The shop rehangs its window displays seasonally. 商店按季节重新布置橱窗陈列。|近义词：re-suspend, replace|反义词：remove, take down


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rehangs the paintings every spring. = 他每年春天都重新挂画。
They rehang the curtains after cleaning them. = 他们清洗窗帘后重新挂上。


一般过去时:
She rehung the mirror after fixing the frame. = 她修理好镜框后重新挂上了镜子。
We rehung all the artwork in the new house. = 我们在新房里重新挂上了所有艺术品。

He has rehung the family portraits in the hallway. = 他已经把家族画像重新挂在走廊上了。
