cabbling:/ˈkæblɪŋ/| n.|电缆敷设；电缆；成束电缆|The act of installing or laying cables, or the cables themselves, often in a bundled or organized manner.|例句：The cabbling of the new fiber optic lines took several weeks. 新的光纤线路敷设花了几个星期。|例句：The damaged cabbling caused a power outage. 受损的电缆造成了停电。|近义词：cable laying, wiring, cabling installation|反义词：removal, disconnection
