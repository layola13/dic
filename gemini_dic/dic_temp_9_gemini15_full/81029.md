graduating:/ˈɡrædʒuˌeɪtɪŋ/| adj. v.ing| 毕业的；即将毕业的 | Describing someone who is in the process of completing their studies and about to graduate.  |例句：The graduating class of 2024 is excited for their future.  2024届毕业生对他们的未来感到兴奋。

例句：She is busy with her graduating thesis. 她正忙于她的毕业论文。


近义词：finishing, completing(studies)
反义词：beginning, entering(studies)

词性：形容词(v.ing形式作形容词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The graduating students are preparing for their graduation ceremony.  毕业生们正在为毕业典礼做准备。
He is graduating this semester. 他这个学期毕业。


一般过去时:
The graduating students celebrated their success last night.  毕业生们昨晚庆祝了他们的成功。
Many students were graduating last spring.  去年春天有很多学生毕业。


现在完成时:
She has already finished her graduating project. 她已经完成了她的毕业项目。
They have been graduating students for four years. 他们已经做了四年的毕业生。


过去完成时:
By the time she graduated, she had completed all her required courses.  在她毕业的时候，她已经完成了所有必修课程。
Before he graduated, he had already secured a job offer. 在他毕业之前，他已经获得了一份工作机会。


将来时:
I will be graduating next year. 我明年将毕业。
They will graduate with honors. 他们将以优异成绩毕业。


虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I would have graduated with honors. 如果我学习更努力，我就会以优异成绩毕业。
I wish I were graduating this semester. 我希望这个学期我能毕业。


被动语态:
The graduation ceremony will be attended by many guests. 毕业典礼将有许多客人参加。
The degrees were awarded to the graduating students.  学位授予毕业生。


疑问句:
Are you graduating this year? 你今年毕业吗？
When will they be graduating? 他们什么时候毕业？


否定句:
He is not graduating this year. 他今年不毕业。
She will not be graduating early. 她不会提前毕业。
