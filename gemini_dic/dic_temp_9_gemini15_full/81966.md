grind:/ɡraɪnd/ | v., n. | 磨碎；使…费力；单调乏味的工作 | To crush or pulverize (something) by friction; to work or operate by friction; to reduce to powder or small pieces by friction.  To pursue something with persistent effort.  A monotonous or tiring activity. |例句：He ground the coffee beans before brewing. 他在冲泡咖啡前磨碎了咖啡豆。

The students grinded through their exams. 学生们费力地完成了考试。


近义词：crush, pulverize,  wear down,  drudgery, toil | 反义词：polish, refine,  rest, leisure
