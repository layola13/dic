expropriated:/ɪkˈsproʊprɪeɪtɪd/ | v. | 征用；没收；剥夺 | To take possession of (something, especially property) without the owner's consent, typically with the intention of using it for the benefit of the public or a government.  | The government expropriated the land to build a new highway.  政府征用这块土地修建一条新的高速公路。 | confiscate, seize, commandeer | return, restore, give back |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government expropriates land for public projects. = 政府征用土地用于公共项目。
They often expropriated resources from the local population. = 他们经常从当地居民那里征用资源。


一般过去时:
The company expropriated the farmer's land. = 公司征用了农民的土地。
The dictator expropriated the wealth of his opponents. = 独裁者没收了他的对手的财富。


现在完成时:
The state has expropriated numerous properties in the past decade. = 国家在过去十年中征用了许多房产。
They have expropriated much of the fertile farmland. = 他们已经征用了大部分肥沃的农田。


过去完成时:
The government had already expropriated the building before the protests began. = 在抗议开始之前，政府已经征用了那座建筑物。
The rebels had expropriated the bank's assets before the army arrived. = 在军队到达之前，叛军已经没收了银行的资产。


将来时:
The city will likely expropriate more land for urban renewal. = 城市可能会征用更多土地用于城市更新。
The authorities will expropriate the property for the new railway line. = 当局将为新的铁路线路征用该房产。


虚拟语气:
If the government expropriated that land, there would be widespread opposition. = 如果政府征用那块土地，将会引起广泛的反对。
It would be unjust if they expropriated the family's home. = 如果他们没收了这个家庭的房子，那就太不公正了。


被动语态:
The land was expropriated for the construction of a new airport. = 这块土地被征用用于建造一个新的机场。
Many businesses were expropriated during the revolution. = 许多企业在革命期间被没收了。


疑问句:
Was the property expropriated legally? = 这处房产是否合法被征用？
Will the government expropriate more land in the future? = 政府将来还会征用更多土地吗？


否定句:
The government did not expropriate the land without compensation. = 政府没有无偿征用这块土地。
They would not expropriate private property under normal circumstances. = 在正常情况下，他们不会征用私有财产。
