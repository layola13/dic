sirena:/saɪˈriːnə/ | n. |塞壬，美人鱼 | A siren is a mythical creature, often depicted as a woman with a bird's body or a fish's tail, who lures sailors to their deaths with her enchanting singing.  The term can also refer to a warning device, such as a loud alarm. | Example: The siren's song captivated the sailors, leading them towards the treacherous rocks.  塞壬的歌声迷住了水手们，引诱他们走向危险的礁石。  The fire alarm siren blared, warning everyone to evacuate. 火警警报声大作，警告所有人撤离。 | 近义词：mermaid, temptress, alarm | 反义词： N/A
