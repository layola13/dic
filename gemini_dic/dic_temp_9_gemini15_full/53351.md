disadvantages:/ˌdɪsˈædvənˌtɪdʒɪz/ | n. |缺点，不利条件 |The disadvantages of this plan outweigh its advantages.  这个计划的缺点超过了它的优点。|近义词：drawbacks, shortcomings, downsides |反义词：advantages, benefits


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are the disadvantages of this product? = 这个产品的缺点是什么？
What disadvantages does this job have? = 这份工作有哪些缺点？


一般过去时:
What were the disadvantages of the old system? = 旧制度的缺点是什么？
What disadvantages did they encounter during the project? = 他们在项目过程中遇到了哪些缺点？


现在完成时:
What disadvantages have you discovered so far? = 到目前为止你发现了哪些缺点？
What disadvantages have we overcome? = 我们克服了哪些缺点？


过去完成时:
What disadvantages had they already identified before the meeting? = 会议之前他们已经确定了哪些缺点？
What disadvantages had been overlooked in the initial assessment? = 初始评估中忽略了哪些缺点？


将来时:
What disadvantages will we face in the future? = 我们将来会面临哪些缺点？
What disadvantages might this new technology present? = 这项新技术可能会带来哪些缺点？


虚拟语气:
If we had considered the disadvantages earlier, we would have made a different decision. = 如果我们早点考虑缺点，我们会做出不同的决定。
I wish I had known the disadvantages before I started. = 我希望在我开始之前就知道这些缺点。


被动语态:
The disadvantages of the proposal were carefully considered. = 该提案的缺点受到了仔细考虑。
The disadvantages are being addressed by the team. = 团队正在解决这些缺点。


疑问句:
Are there any disadvantages to this approach? = 这种方法有什么缺点吗？
What are the main disadvantages we need to address? = 我们需要解决的主要缺点是什么？


否定句:
There are no significant disadvantages to this method. = 这种方法没有明显的缺点。
This plan doesn't have many disadvantages. = 这个计划没有很多缺点。


PHRASE->
the disadvantages of = ...的缺点
weigh the disadvantages = 权衡缺点
consider the disadvantages = 考虑缺点
overcome the disadvantages = 克服缺点
address the disadvantages = 解决缺点
