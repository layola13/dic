raise hell:/ˌreɪz ˈhɛl/| v.|兴风作浪，闹事，大吵大闹 |To make a lot of noise and cause a disturbance; to create chaos and trouble.  |例句：The kids raised hell in the supermarket. 孩子们在超市里大吵大闹。|例句：They raised hell when they found out the truth. 他们发现真相后就大吵大闹。|近义词：cause trouble, make a ruckus, kick up a fuss |反义词：behave well, keep quiet, remain calm|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They raise hell every weekend. = 他们每个周末都闹事。
He raises hell whenever he's drunk. = 他每当喝醉酒时就大吵大闹。

一般过去时:
The protesters raised hell outside the government building. = 抗议者在政府大楼外闹事。
She raised hell with her boss about the unfair treatment. = 她向老板大发雷霆，抗议不公平的待遇。

现在完成时:
They've raised hell all night long. = 他们整夜都在闹腾。
He has raised hell several times this month. = 这个月他已经好几次大吵大闹了。

过去完成时:
They had raised hell before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们就已经闹事了。
She had raised hell with her parents before running away. = 她离家出走之前就和父母大吵了一架。

将来时:
They will raise hell if they don't get what they want. = 如果他们得不到想要的东西，他们就会闹事。
He'll raise hell if he finds out. = 如果他发现的话，他会大发雷霆。

虚拟语气:
If they knew the consequences, they wouldn't raise hell. = 如果他们知道后果，他们就不会闹事了。
I wish they wouldn't raise hell all the time. = 我希望他们不要总是闹事。

被动语态:
Hell was raised by the angry mob. = 愤怒的暴民闹事。 (This is less common in this idiom)
The issue was raised and hell was raised in response. = 这个问题被提出，并引发了强烈抗议。


疑问句:
Will they raise hell if we're late? = 如果我们迟到了，他们会闹事吗？
Did they raise hell at the party? = 他们在派对上闹事了吗？


否定句:
They didn't raise hell at the meeting. = 他们在会议上没有闹事。
He didn't raise hell, he just expressed his discontent calmly. = 他没有大吵大闹，只是平静地表达了他的不满。
