nonhumanized:/ˌnɒnˈhjuːmənaɪzd/| adj.|非人化的；未经人道化的|Not made human-like; lacking human qualities or characteristics.  The experiment focused on nonhumanized models to avoid ethical concerns.|例句：The study used nonhumanized mice to test the new drug. 这项研究使用非人源化小鼠来测试新药。

The research avoided nonhumanized approaches, preferring to focus on the human experience. 这项研究避免了非人化的方法，更倾向于关注人类的经验。|近义词：dehumanized, depersonalized, impersonal|反义词：humanized, personalized, humane
