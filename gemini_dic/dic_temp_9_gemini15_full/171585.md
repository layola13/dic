pullovers:/ˈpʊlˌoʊvər/| n.|套头衫，毛衣|A pullover is a sweater or shirt that you pull on over your head, without buttons or a zipper.|例句：I wore a warm pullover to protect myself from the cold. 我穿了一件保暖的套头衫来御寒。|例句：She knitted a beautiful pullover for her grandson. 她为她的孙子织了一件漂亮的套头衫。|近义词：sweater, jumper|反义词：shirt (button-up)|名词
