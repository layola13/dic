genteelly:/dʒɛnˈtiːli/| adj.| 有教养地；优雅地；斯文地 | In a refined or elegant manner; politely.  The word suggests a somewhat affected or artificial refinement. | 例句：She spoke genteelly, choosing her words carefully. 她说话很有教养，仔细斟酌着词语。

例句：He moved genteelly through the crowded room. 他优雅地穿梭于拥挤的房间。


近义词：politely, elegantly, gracefully, refinedly, stylishly

反义词：rudely, coarsely, vulgarly, crudely, unrefinedly

词性:形容词


词源: 源自于古法语 *gentil,  最终源自于拉丁语 *gentilis,  意为"高贵的，有血统的"。  这个词根也和"gentle" (温柔的) 有关。  "-ly" 是副词后缀。
