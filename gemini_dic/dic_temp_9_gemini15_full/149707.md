palaeo:/ˈpæliˌoʊ/| adj.|古的；古代的；旧的 | Relating to or denoting the period before the Holocene Epoch, which is the current geological epoch.  It is often used as a combining form in words relating to ancient times or extinct organisms. |Example: palaeontology is the study of fossils. 古生物学是研究化石的学科。|近义词：ancient, archaic, old |反义词：modern, new, recent |形容词
