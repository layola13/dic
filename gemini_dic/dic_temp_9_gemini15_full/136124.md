nonrefined: /ˌnɒn rɪˈfaɪnd/ | adj. |未精制的；未提纯的；粗糙的 | Not processed or purified; in its natural state.  It refers to substances that haven't undergone processes to remove impurities or improve quality. |例句：The nonrefined sugar has a stronger flavor than the white sugar. 未精制的糖比白糖味道更浓。

The nonrefined oil is rich in natural nutrients. 未精炼的油富含天然营养素。

|近义词：unrefined, crude, raw, natural |反义词：refined, purified, processed | 词性:形容词
