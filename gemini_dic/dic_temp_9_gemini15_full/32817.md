chokeberry: /ˈtʃoʊkˌbɛri/ | n. | 稠李; 黑果;  | A chokeberry is a type of berry from the genus Aronia, known for its tart and astringent flavor.  They are often used in jams, jellies, and other processed foods. | Example:  The tartness of the chokeberry jam was unexpected but delicious.  稠李果酱的酸味出乎意料，但很好吃。  |近义词: aronia berry | 反义词: sweet berry
