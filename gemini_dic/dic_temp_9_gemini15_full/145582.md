overconcentrate:/ˌoʊvərˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪt/| v.|过度集中；过于专注|To focus too intensely or for too long on a single thing, often to the detriment of other things.  It implies a negative consequence from excessive focus.|例句：He overconcentrated on his work and neglected his health. 他过于专注于工作而忽视了自己的健康。


例句：Don't overconcentrate on one aspect; consider the bigger picture. 不要过于专注于一个方面；考虑大局。


近义词：hyperfocus, overfocus


反义词：diversify, generalize


词性:动词
