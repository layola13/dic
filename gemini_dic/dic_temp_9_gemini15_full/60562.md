elasmosaurus:/ɪˌlæzməˈsɔːrəs/| n.|薄板龙|A genus of plesiosaur, a large marine reptile from the Late Cretaceous period, characterized by its long neck and small head.|例句：The elasmosaurus was a formidable predator in its time. 薄板龙在其时代是可怕的掠食者。|例句：Fossils of elasmosaurus have been found in various parts of the world. 在世界各地都发现了薄板龙的化石。|近义词：plesiosaur|反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑体型较小的海洋爬行动物)|名词
