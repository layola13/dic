re-polling:/ˌriːˈpoʊlɪŋ/ | n. |重新投票，再次投票 |The act of conducting a poll again, usually due to irregularities or concerns about the fairness or accuracy of the initial poll.  This often involves repeating the entire voting process. |例句：The re-polling was necessary after allegations of voter fraud were made.  重新投票是在出现选民舞弊指控后所必须的。

例句：Due to technical issues, a re-polling of the online survey was announced. 由于技术问题，宣布对在线调查进行重新投票。


近义词：revoting, second polling, repeat polling |反义词：initial polling, first polling |词性：名词
