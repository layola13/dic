cast:/kæst/| /kɑːst/ |vt. n.| 投掷；铸造；演员阵容；模具 |To throw or fling; to shape (metal, etc.) by pouring into a mold; a group of actors in a play, movie, etc.; a mold. |例句：He cast the fishing line into the lake. 他把鱼线扔进湖里。  The statue was cast in bronze. 这尊雕像用青铜铸造。 |近义词：throw, fling, hurl, mold, form, crew |反义词：receive, collect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He casts a fishing line every morning. = 他每天早上都抛鱼线。
She casts a shadow on the wall. = 她把影子投射到墙上。


一般过去时:
They cast the blame on him. = 他们把责任推到他身上。
The director cast a young actor for the lead role. = 导演为主角挑选了一位年轻演员。


现在完成时:
He has cast his vote. = 他已经投票了。
They have cast aside their old beliefs. = 他们已经抛弃了旧的信仰。


过去完成时:
She had cast doubt on his story. = 她曾对他的故事表示怀疑。
They had already cast their votes before the announcement. = 他们在宣布之前就已经投票了。


将来时:
We will cast our ballots next week. = 我们下周将投票。
The foundry will cast thousands of parts today. = 铸造厂今天将铸造数千个零件。


虚拟语气:
If I were to cast a spell, I would wish for peace. = 如果我要施咒，我会许愿和平。
I wish I had cast a wider net in my job search. = 我希望我在求职时能有更广泛的搜索范围。


被动语态:
The votes were cast by mail. = 这些选票是通过邮寄投票的。
The bronze statue was cast in a local foundry. = 这尊青铜雕像是在当地铸造厂铸造的。


疑问句:
Who was cast in the lead role? = 谁扮演主角？
Where will the play be cast? = 这出戏将在哪里演出？


否定句:
He did not cast a single vote. = 他没有投一票。
They didn't cast any doubt on the theory. = 他们没有对这个理论表示任何怀疑。


PHRASE->
