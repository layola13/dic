digenous:/ˈdɪdʒənəs/ | adj. |土生的；当地特有的；本地的 |Native or indigenous to a particular place; originating or occurring naturally in a particular place; inherent or innate.  |Example:  Kangaroos are indigenous to Australia. 袋鼠是澳大利亚特有的动物。The indigenous population of the island is small.  这个岛屿的原住民数量很少。|近义词：native, aboriginal, endemic |反义词：alien, exotic, foreign
