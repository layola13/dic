nonequestrian:/ˌnɒnˈɛkwɛstriən/| adj.|非马术的，与马术无关的|Not related to horses or horse riding. 例句：His nonequestrian background didn't stop him from enjoying the horse show. =他非马术的背景并没有阻止他欣赏马术表演。|近义词：non-horsey, unhorsey|反义词：equestrian|词性：形容词
