relate:/rɪˈleɪt/ | vt. vi. n. | 叙述；联系；关联；认同 | To tell or describe something; to connect or show a connection between things; to understand and sympathize with someone's feelings or experiences.  | 例句：He related the story of his adventures. 他讲述了他的冒险故事。  |  例句：The two events are closely related. 这两件事密切相关。 | 近义词：connect, associate, link, recount, narrate, empathize | 反义词：disconnect, separate, isolate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He relates his experiences to his friends. = 他向朋友们讲述他的经历。
She relates well to her students. = 她和她的学生相处得很好。


一般过去时:
He related the entire incident to the police. = 他向警方讲述了整件事的经过。
She related how she felt about the situation. = 她讲述了她对这种情况的感受。


现在完成时:
I have related all the important details in my report. = 我已经在我的报告中叙述了所有重要的细节。
She has related her story to many people. = 她已经向许多人讲述了她的故事。


过去完成时:
He had related the news before anyone else did. = 他比任何人都早地讲了这个消息。
She had related her childhood experiences in her memoir. = 她在回忆录中讲述了她童年的经历。


将来时:
He will relate his findings at the conference. = 他将在会议上报告他的发现。
She will relate the story to the children. = 她将把这个故事讲给孩子们听。


虚拟语气:
If he related the truth, people would believe him. = 如果他说实话，人们就会相信他。
I wish she had related the whole story to me. = 我希望她把整个故事都告诉我。


被动语态:
The story was related in great detail. = 这个故事被详细地讲述了。
His experiences were related to the audience with passion. = 他的经历被充满激情地讲述给了听众。


疑问句:
How does she relate to her colleagues? = 她和同事的关系如何？
Does this event relate to the investigation? = 这件事与调查有关吗？


否定句:
He didn't relate the whole story. = 他没有把整个故事都说出来。
She couldn't relate to his feelings. = 她无法理解他的感受。


PHRASE->
relate to: 与...有关；理解
relate to someone: 与某人产生共鸣
in relation to: 关于；涉及
with relation to: 关于；涉及
as it relates to: 就...而言
