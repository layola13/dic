corduroys:/ˈkɔːrdərɔɪz/| n.|灯芯绒裤|Corduroys are a type of fabric with a distinctive raised wale pattern, typically used for trousers.  They are durable and comfortable, often worn in colder weather. |例句：He wore brown corduroys and a tweed jacket. 他穿着棕色的灯芯绒裤和一件花呢夹克。

例句：My favorite pair of corduroys are finally wearing out. 我最喜欢的那条灯芯绒裤终于要穿坏了。


近义词：corduroy trousers,  velveteen trousers (less common)|反义词：None (it's a type of fabric)|词性：n.
