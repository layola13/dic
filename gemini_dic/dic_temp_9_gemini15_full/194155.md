self-integrations: /ˌsɛlfˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃənz/ | n. | 自我整合；内部集成 | The process by which a system or organization integrates its various components or parts.  It refers to the internal consistency and coherence achieved within a system.  This can apply to software systems, organizations, or even individual psychological states. | 例句：The company's success depends on effective self-integrations across its different departments. 公司的成功取决于其不同部门之间有效的内部整合。


The successful project required meticulous self-integrations of the various software modules.  这个成功的项目需要对各种软件模块进行细致的自我整合。| 近义词：internal integration, consolidation, unification | 反义词：fragmentation, disintegration, disunity |  名词
