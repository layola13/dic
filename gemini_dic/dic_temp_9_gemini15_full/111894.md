logout:/ˈlɒɡaʊt/ /ˈlɑːɡaʊt/| vt.|注销，登出|To end a session on a computer system or network.|例句：Please logout before leaving your workstation. 请离开工作站前注销。
例句：I forgot to logout of my email account. 我忘记注销我的电子邮件帐户了。|近义词：sign out, log off|反义词：login, log in|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I logout every night before going to bed. = 我每天晚上睡觉前都会注销。
He logs out of his account after using it. = 他用完账户后会注销。

一般过去时:
I logged out of my computer yesterday. = 我昨天注销了我的电脑。
She logged out of the system after finishing her work. = 她完成工作后注销了系统。

现在完成时:
I have logged out of all my accounts. = 我已经注销了我的所有帐户。
He has not logged out yet. = 他还没有注销。


过去完成时:
I had logged out before the server crashed. = 服务器崩溃之前我已经注销了。
She had logged out before the meeting started. = 会议开始前她就注销了。

将来时:
I will logout when I finish my work. = 我完成工作后会注销。
He will logout before going home. = 他回家前会注销。

虚拟语气:
If I had remembered my password, I would have logged out properly. = 如果我记得我的密码，我就会正确注销。
I wish I had logged out before leaving. = 我希望离开前我已经注销了。


被动语态:
The account was logged out automatically. = 该帐户已自动注销。
The system was logged out after a period of inactivity. = 系统在一段时间不活动后注销。


疑问句:
Have you logged out of your account? = 你注销你的帐户了吗？
Did he logout before leaving the office? = 他离开办公室前注销了吗？


否定句:
I didn't logout properly. = 我没有正确注销。
He did not logout of his account. = 他没有注销他的帐户。


PHRASE->
logout of  = 从...注销
