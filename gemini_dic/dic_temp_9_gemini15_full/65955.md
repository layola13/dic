/ɪkˈstræktərz/ | n. |提取器，萃取器 |A device or process that extracts something.  It can refer to machinery used to extract resources like oil or minerals, or to a method or program used to extract information from data. |Example: The oil company uses powerful extractors to get petroleum from deep underground.  这家石油公司使用强大的提取器从地下深处开采石油。  The software uses sophisticated extractors to pull relevant data from the large database. 该软件使用复杂的提取器从大型数据库中提取相关数据。 |近义词：extractor, remover, separator |反义词：injector, inserter
