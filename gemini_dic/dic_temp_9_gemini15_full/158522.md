pleochroic:/ˌpliːoʊˈkroʊɪk/| adj.|多色的，多向色性的| Exhibiting different colors when viewed in different directions, a property of some crystals.  This is due to the anisotropic absorption of light. | Example: Tourmaline is a pleochroic mineral, showing different colors depending on the viewing angle.  绿宝石是一种多色性矿物，根据观察角度的不同显示出不同的颜色。|近义词：dichroic (if showing two colors), polychroic|反义词：monochroic

Etymology: From Greek *pleon* ("more") + *chroma* ("color").
