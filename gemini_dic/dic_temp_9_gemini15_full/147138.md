overlook:/ˌoʊvərˈlʊk/ | vt. n. | 忽视；俯瞰；眺望；检查；宽恕 | to fail to notice something; to have a view from above; to supervise or oversee; to disregard or pardon | 近义词：ignore, neglect, disregard, oversee, inspect | 反义词：notice, heed, attend to

一般现在时:
