cheekiest: /ˈtʃiːkiɪst/ | adj. | 最无赖的；最淘气的；最厚脸皮的 | Most audacious, impudent, or playful.  It is the superlative form of "cheeky." |例句：He pulled the cheekiest prank on his teacher. = 他捉弄了他老师一个最无赖的恶作剧。


例句：Her cheekiest comment left everyone speechless. = 她最厚脸皮的评论让每个人都哑口无言。


近义词：most impudent, most audacious, naughtiest, most mischievous, most bold


反义词：most respectful, most polite, most timid, most shy

词性:形容词
