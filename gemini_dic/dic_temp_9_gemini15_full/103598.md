joiners:/ˈdʒɔɪnərz/ | n. |木工，细木工；联接物；参加者 |People who join things together, especially wood.  It can also refer to people who join a group or organization. |例句：He's a skilled joiner, capable of creating intricate furniture. 他是一位技艺精湛的木工，能够制作复杂的家具。


The skilled joiners built the entire house from locally sourced timber. 这些技艺精湛的木工用当地采购的木材建造了整栋房子。|近义词：carpenter, cabinetmaker, member |反义词：divider, separator
