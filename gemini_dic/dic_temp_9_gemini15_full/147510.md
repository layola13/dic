overpamper:/ˌoʊvərˈpæmpər/ | vt. |过度溺爱，宠坏 |To spoil or indulge someone excessively.  This word combines "over" (meaning excessively) and "pamper" (meaning to indulge with excessive care, attention, or luxury).  It suggests that the pampering has gone beyond the point of being beneficial and has resulted in negative consequences. | 例句：Parents who overpamper their children often raise spoiled and entitled brats.  父母过度溺爱孩子，往往会养出娇生惯养、自以为是的小孩。


The constant praise overpampered him, making him unable to handle criticism.  不断的赞扬让他过于娇惯，以至于无法承受批评。|近义词：spoil, coddle, indulge excessively |反义词：neglect, ignore, discipline
