finch:/fɪntʃ/ | n. |雀科小鸟 |A small seed-eating bird with a short, stout beak. Finches are known for their colorful plumage and melodious songs. Many species are popular cage birds. |例句：The finch perched on the branch, singing a cheerful tune.  这只雀鸟栖息在树枝上，唱着欢快的歌。

例句：We saw a variety of finches at the bird feeder. 我们在喂鸟器上看到了各种各样的雀鸟。


近义词：bird, songbird |反义词：None


词源：The word "finch" comes from Old English *finc, which is of uncertain origin, possibly related to a Proto-Germanic root.
