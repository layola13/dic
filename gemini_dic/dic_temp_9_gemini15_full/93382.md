illiberally:/ɪˈlɪbərəli/ | adv. | 不自由地；专制地；粗鲁地 | In a manner that is not liberal; in a narrow-minded or intolerant way.  It often implies a restriction of freedoms or rights. | 例句：He ruled illiberally, suppressing dissent and silencing opposition. 他专制统治，压制异见，压制反对派。| 例句：The government acted illiberally by censoring the press. 政府审查新闻，做法专制。| 近义词：intolerantly, dogmatically, tyrannically, oppressively | 反义词：liberally, tolerantly, democratically
