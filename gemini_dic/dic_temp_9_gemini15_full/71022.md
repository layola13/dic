firming:/ˈfɜːrmɪŋ/ | v. | 使结实；使坚固；巩固；加强 |  The process of making something firmer or more solid.  This often refers to tightening or strengthening something, whether physically or metaphorically.  | 例句：The lotion is designed for firming the skin.  这款乳液的设计目的是使皮肤紧致。


The regular exercise program is firming up his muscles.  规律的锻炼计划使他的肌肉更加结实。|近义词：strengthening, solidifying, tightening, consolidating | 反义词：loosening, weakening, softening | 动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cream is firming my skin. = 这款面霜使我的皮肤更紧致。
He is firming his resolve to quit smoking. = 他正在坚定自己戒烟的决心。


一般过去时:
The carpenter firmed up the loose board. = 木匠加固了松动的木板。
The experience firmed her belief in her abilities. = 那次经历增强了她对自身能力的信心。


现在完成时:
The company has firmed up its position in the market. = 公司已经巩固了其在市场上的地位。
She has firmed up her schedule for the week. = 她已经确定了本周的日程安排。


过去完成时:
They had firmed up the agreement before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前敲定了协议。
He had firmed up his plans for the trip before he left. = 他出发前已经把旅行计划确定下来了。


将来时:
The government will firm up its policies on immigration. = 政府将加强其移民政策。
We will firm up the details of the contract next week. = 我们下周将敲定合同的细节。


虚拟语气:
If the government firmed up its stance, the crisis might be averted. = 如果政府立场坚定，危机或许可以避免。
I wish I had firmed up my decision earlier. = 我真希望我早点下定决心。


被动语态:
The agreement was firmed up by both parties. = 协议得到了双方确认。
The foundation of the building is being firmed up. = 建筑的地基正在加固。


疑问句:
Is the foundation firming up properly? = 地基加固得怎么样？
Are you firming up your plans for the conference? = 你在为会议做准备吗？


否定句:
The negotiations did not firm up an agreement. = 谈判没有达成协议。
He didn't firm up his application before the deadline. = 他没有在截止日期前完成申请。


PHRASE->
firm up (plans/ideas):  使计划/想法具体化
firm up (a deal/agreement):  敲定交易/协议
firm up (one's resolve):  坚定决心
firm up (the muscles):  使肌肉结实
firm up (the ground):  夯实地面
