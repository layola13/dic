replate:/riːˈpleɪt/| vt.|重新镀；再镀；重新电镀|To cover (a metal object) again with a layer of metal by electroplating or a similar process.|The engineer decided to replate the old car parts to restore their shine.  工程师决定重新电镀旧车零件以恢复其光泽。|近义词：recoat, refinish|反义词：strip, remove plating
