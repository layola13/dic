extravagancy:/ɪkˈstrævæɡənsi/| n.|奢侈；挥霍；过分；浪费 |Excessive expenditure or luxury.  It suggests a lack of restraint in spending money or using resources. |例句：His extravagant lifestyle led to financial ruin. 他的奢侈生活导致了经济破产。

例句：The extravagance of the wedding was breathtaking. 婚礼的奢华令人叹为观止。


近义词：profligacy, lavishness, exuberance, wastefulness

反义词：frugality, thrift, economy, austerity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His extravagance is shocking. = 他的奢侈令人震惊。
Their extravagance shows their wealth. = 他们的奢侈炫耀着他们的财富。


一般过去时:
Her extravagance surprised everyone. = 她的挥霍使每个人都感到惊讶。
His extravagance cost him dearly. = 他的挥霍让他付出了沉重的代价。


现在完成时:
Their extravagance has drained their savings. = 他们的挥霍已经耗尽了他们的积蓄。
His extravagance has led him into debt. = 他的挥霍使他负债累累。


过去完成时:
Her extravagance had already emptied her bank account. = 她的挥霍已经掏空了她的银行账户。
His extravagance had caused considerable financial problems. = 他的挥霍已经造成了相当大的财务问题。


将来时:
His extravagance will lead to bankruptcy. = 他的挥霍将导致破产。
Their extravagance will ultimately ruin them. = 他们的挥霍最终会毁了他们。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't been so extravagant, he wouldn't be in debt now. = 如果他不那么奢侈，他现在就不会负债了。
I wish I hadn't been so extravagant last month. = 我真希望上个月我不那么奢侈。


被动语态:
His extravagance was criticized by many. = 他的奢侈行为受到许多人的批评。
The extravagance of the event was widely reported. = 活动的奢华被广泛报道。


疑问句:
Is his extravagance justified? = 他的奢侈行为合理吗？
Was the extravagance necessary? = 那样的奢侈有必要吗？


否定句:
His lifestyle is not extravagant. = 他的生活方式并不奢侈。
The party wasn't extravagant at all. = 派对一点也不奢侈。


PHRASE->
an extravagance of = 过度的...；极度的...
extravagance in = 在...方面的奢侈
show one's extravagance = 炫耀一个人的奢侈
live in extravagance = 过奢侈的生活
a display of extravagance = 奢侈的展示


词源:
From Old French *extravagance, from Latin *extravagans, present participle of *extravagari "to wander beyond," from extra "outside, beyond" + vagari "to wander."  The sense development is from "wandering beyond bounds" to "excessive" and then to "extravagant spending."
