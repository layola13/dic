guesstimating:/ˈɡɛstɪˌmeɪtɪŋ/| v. |粗略估计；大概估算 |To make an estimate based on a rough calculation or guess.  It combines "guess" and "estimating."|例句：I'm guesstimating that the project will take about six months. 我粗略估计这个项目大约需要六个月。

例句：We're guesstimating the cost of the renovation based on similar projects. 我们根据类似项目的成本粗略估算了装修费用。


近义词：estimating, approximating, ballparking, roughly calculating | 反义词：precisely calculating, accurately measuring | 词性:动词
