nonnormality:/ˌnɒnˌnɔːrˈmæləti/ | n. | 非正态性，非正态分布 | The state or quality of not being normal; specifically, in statistics, the condition of data not following a normal distribution.  Nonnormality can affect the validity of statistical tests that assume normality. | Example: The nonnormality of the data necessitated the use of a nonparametric test.  非正态的数据需要使用非参数检验。 | 近义词: abnormality, deviation | 反义词: normality
