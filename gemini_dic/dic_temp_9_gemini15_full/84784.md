handsful: /ˈhændsfʊl/ | n. |一把，少量 | A handful refers to the quantity that can be held in one hand.  It can also be used figuratively to mean a small number or amount. |例句：He gave me a handful of coins. 他给了我一把硬币。
She had a handful of problems to deal with. 她有一些问题需要处理。|近义词：small amount, few, several |反义词: a lot, many, numerous |名词


USAGE->

I hold a handful of sand. = 我拿着一把沙子。
She has a handful of cherries. = 她手里拿着一把樱桃。

过去完成时:
