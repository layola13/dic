deities:/ˈdiːətiz/| n.|神，神明|Plural of deity;  supernatural beings, typically worshipped as possessing supernatural powers.  They are often considered to be powerful, immortal, and capable of influencing human affairs.|例句：Many ancient cultures worshipped a pantheon of deities.  许多古代文化崇拜着众神。|例句：The poem celebrates the power of the deities of nature. 这首诗歌颂了自然诸神的威力。|近义词：gods, goddesses, divinities|反义词：mortals, humans
