fostered:/ˈfɒstəd/ /ˈfɑːstərd/| vt.|培养；抚育；鼓励；助长|to care for (a child, typically one from a difficult family background) as a parent, not necessarily legally|例句：She fostered many children over the years. 她这些年来抚养了许多孩子。

例句：The teacher fostered a love of learning in her students. 老师培养了学生们对学习的热爱。


近义词：cultivate, nurture, promote, encourage|反义词：neglect, hinder, discourage|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fosters a positive attitude. 他培养积极的态度。
She fosters creativity in her students. 她培养学生们的创造力。

They fostered a stray cat. 他们收养了一只流浪猫。

现在完成时:
We have fostered many orphaned children. 我们已经收养了许多孤儿。

将来时:
