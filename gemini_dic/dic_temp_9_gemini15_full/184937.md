retards:/rɪˈtɑːrdz/ | n. v. adj. | 阻碍；迟钝者；智力障碍者 | To delay or hinder the progress of something;  a person with intellectual disability; slow or late in development. |例句：The heavy rain retarded the progress of the construction. 大雨阻碍了建筑工程的进展。  The child's development was retarded.  这个孩子的发育迟缓了。| 近义词：delay, hinder, impede, obstruct;  slow, sluggish;  disabled (in the context of intellectual disability) | 反义词：accelerate, expedite, hasten;  advance, progress;  gifted (in the context of intellectual disability)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heavy rain retards the growth of the plants. = 大雨阻碍了植物的生长。
This medicine retards the aging process. = 这种药延缓衰老过程。

一般过去时:
The storm retarded our journey. = 暴风雨延误了我们的行程。
The accident retarded the traffic flow. = 事故阻碍了交通流量。

现在完成时:
The drought has retarded the harvest. = 干旱已经延误了收成。
His illness has retarded his progress in school. = 他的疾病延缓了他的学业进步。

过去完成时:
The previous delays had retarded the project's completion. = 先前的延误已经延缓了项目的完成。
The bad weather had retarded their travel plans. = 恶劣的天气已经延误了他们的旅行计划。

将来时:
The new regulations will retard economic growth. = 新规将阻碍经济增长。
Lack of funding will retard the research project. = 资金不足将延缓研究项目。

虚拟语气:
If the government hadn't intervened, the economic crisis would have retarded the country's development even further. = 如果政府不干预，经济危机本来会进一步阻碍国家的发展。
If only we hadn't waited, the project wouldn't have been retarded. = 如果我们没有等待，项目就不会被延误。

被动语态:
The construction was retarded by the bad weather. = 建筑工程因恶劣天气而延误。
His career was retarded by a series of unfortunate events. = 他的职业生涯因一系列不幸事件而受阻。

疑问句:
What factors retarded the development of this technology? = 哪些因素阻碍了这项技术的发展？
Will the new policy retard economic progress? = 新政策会阻碍经济进步吗？

否定句:
The project wasn't retarded by any unforeseen circumstances. = 该项目没有因任何不可预见的情况而延误。
Nothing retarded the team's progress towards their goal. = 没有什么能阻碍团队朝着目标前进。
