overwhipping: /ˌoʊvərˈhwɪpɪŋ/ | n. |过度抽打；过度搅拌| The act of whipping something excessively, leading to undesirable results.  This can refer to whipping cream, meringue, or even a physical beating.  It often results in a less desirable texture or outcome.| Example:  The overwhipping of the cream resulted in a grainy texture. 过度搅拌奶油导致质地粗糙。 The child received an overwhipping for his misdeeds.  孩子因为他的恶行受到了过度抽打。|近义词: excessive whipping, overbeating |反义词: underwhipping
