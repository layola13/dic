documentation: /ˌdɒkjuːmenˈteɪʃən/ /ˌdɑːkjuːmenˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 文件；文献；记录 | The act of providing documents that give evidence or information; the documents themselves.  It refers to the collection of all documents that provide information about a particular subject or process.  This can include manuals, guides, specifications, reports, etc. | Example:  The software came with comprehensive documentation.  这个软件附带了全面的文档。


The company requires thorough documentation of all expenses. 这家公司要求对所有费用进行彻底的记录。


近义词：record, records, writings, archives


反义词：lack of records, absence of evidence


词性：名词

词源:
The word "documentation" comes from the French word "documenter," meaning "to provide with documents."  The word "document" itself ultimately derives from the Latin word "docere," meaning "to teach."  Thus, documentation literally means the act of providing materials that teach or inform.
