miniaturizing: /ˌmɪnɪətʃəˈraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 小型化；微型化 | The act of making something smaller.  It refers to the process of reducing the size of an object or system while retaining its functionality. |  例句：The company is focused on miniaturizing its products to make them more portable. 公司致力于将其产品小型化，以使其更便携。

例句：Scientists are working on miniaturizing medical devices for minimally invasive surgery. 科学家们正在致力于将医疗设备小型化，用于微创手术。


近义词：downsizing, reducing, shrinking

反义词：enlarging, magnifying, expanding

词性：动词
