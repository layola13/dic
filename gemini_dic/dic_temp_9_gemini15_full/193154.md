sedans:/sɛˈdæn/| n. |轿车|A sedan is a car with two rows of seats and a trunk at the rear.  Sedans are typically smaller than SUVs or minivans and are often considered more fuel-efficient.|例句：He drives a sleek, black sedan. 他开着一辆光滑的黑色轿车。|例句：Many families prefer SUVs to sedans. 许多家庭更喜欢SUV而不是轿车。|近义词：saloon, car|反义词：SUV, minivan, truck
