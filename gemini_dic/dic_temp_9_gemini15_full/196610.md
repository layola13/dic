setigerous: /sɛˈtɪdʒərəs/ | adj. |多刚毛的，具刚毛的 | Having or bearing setae (bristles or hairs).  The term often refers to  annelid worms and other invertebrates possessing numerous bristles. | Example:  The setigerous appendages of the polychaete worm aided its movement through the sediment.  多毛环节动物的刚毛状附属物帮助它在沉积物中移动。|近义词:bristly, hairy, setaceous |反义词:asetigerous, glabrous
