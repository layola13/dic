gristle:/ˈɡrɪzəl/| n.|软骨；肌腱；(肉中的)筋|Tough, relatively inelastic connective tissue; cartilage.  It's often found in meat and can be chewy or unpleasant to eat.|例句：The steak was tough and full of gristle. 这块牛排又老又筋道。|例句：She carefully removed the gristle from the chicken before cooking it.  她在烹饪鸡肉之前小心地去除了鸡肉中的筋。|近义词：cartilage, sinew, tendon|反义词：tender meat
