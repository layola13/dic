chicxulub: /tʃɪkʃuːluːb/ | n. | 奇克苏鲁伯 | The Chicxulub crater is a large impact crater buried underneath the Yucatán Peninsula in Mexico.  It is believed to have been formed by the impactor that caused the Cretaceous–Paleogene extinction event, which wiped out the non-avian dinosaurs.  The name "Chicxulub" comes from the nearby town of Chicxulub Pueblo. | The Chicxulub crater is a significant site for research into the impact event that ended the age of dinosaurs.  | 近义词:  impact crater (撞击坑) | 反义词:  无
