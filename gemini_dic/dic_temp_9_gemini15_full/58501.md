duelist:/ˈdjuːəlɪst/| n. |决斗者，决斗高手|A person who engages in duels.  A duelist is skilled in the art of dueling, often possessing expertise in swordsmanship or other weaponry used in formal combat. |Example: The famous duelist accepted the challenge without hesitation.  这位著名的决斗高手毫不犹豫地接受了挑战。|近义词：fighter, swordsman, combatant |反义词：peacemaker
