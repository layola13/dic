monocarp:/ˈmɒnəˌkɑːrp/|/ˈmɑːnəˌkɑːrp/| n. |单果植物，单果树|A monocarp is a plant that flowers and fruits only once in its lifetime, after which it dies.  This is in contrast to polycarpic plants, which flower and fruit repeatedly throughout their lives.|Example: The agave is a classic example of a monocarp, producing a large flower stalk before dying.|近义词：semelparous plant|反义词：polycarp
