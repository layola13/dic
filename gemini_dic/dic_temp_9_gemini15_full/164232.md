predominator: /prɪˈdɒmɪneɪtə(r)/| n. |支配者；统治者；主要成分| A person or thing that predominates; one that has a dominant influence or role.  |例句：The company's CEO is the clear predominator in all decision-making processes. 这家公司的CEO在所有决策过程中都是明显的支配者。

例句：The scientific community views the Big Bang theory as the predominator in cosmology. 科学界将大爆炸理论视为宇宙学中的主要理论。


近义词：dominant, ruler, leader, major component, main element

反义词：subordinate, follower, minority, minor component
