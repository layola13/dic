shriekingly:/ˈʃriːkɪŋli/| adv. |尖声地，刺耳地，令人毛骨悚然地 |In a shriek; in a way that suggests or resembles a shriek.  It describes a manner or sound that is high-pitched, piercing, and often expressing intense emotion, typically fear, pain, or excitement. |例句：She shriekingly announced the good news. 她尖声宣布了这个好消息。


The children shriekingly laughed at the clown's antics. 孩子们尖声大笑小丑的滑稽动作。


近义词：piercingly, shrilly, stridently |反义词：softly, gently, quietly |词性：副词
