preafflicts:/ˌpriːəˈflɪkts/ | v. | 预先折磨；预先使痛苦 | To afflict beforehand; to cause suffering or distress in advance.  The word is rare and largely found in older texts. | Example: The thought of the upcoming surgery preafflicted him with anxiety.  =  即将到来的手术让他提前感到焦虑。  |近义词:preoccupy,  pre-torment |反义词:soothe, comfort |动词
