redeliveries:/ˌriːdɪˈlɪvəriːz/| n.|再次递送，重新投递|The act of delivering something again, typically after a failed initial delivery.  This often refers to packages or goods that were not successfully delivered the first time.|例句：The number of redeliveries increased significantly during the holiday season.  假日季期间，重新投递的数量显著增加。

例句：Due to the customer's absence, the package required two redeliveries. 由于顾客不在家，包裹需要重新投递两次。


近义词：redeliverals, reshipments, second deliveries|反义词：initial delivery, first delivery|词性:名词
