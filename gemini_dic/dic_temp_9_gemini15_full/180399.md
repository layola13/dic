refuting: /rɪˈfjuːtɪŋ/ | v. | 反驳；驳斥 |  To argue against or disprove something. To show something to be false or incorrect. |


例句：

He refuted the allegations of fraud. = 他驳斥了欺诈的指控。

The scientist refuted the earlier theory with new evidence. = 科学家用新的证据驳斥了早先的理论。


近义词：disproving, contradicting, denying, rebutting


反义词：confirming, supporting, verifying, corroborating


词性：动词
