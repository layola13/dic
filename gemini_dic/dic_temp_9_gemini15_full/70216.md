fibs:/fɪbz/| n. |谎话，谎言 |Untruths or falsehoods; statements that are not true.  |例句：He told a few harmless fibs to avoid hurting her feelings. 他说了几个无伤大雅的小谎来避免伤害她的感情。|例句：Children often tell fibs to get out of trouble. 孩子们经常说谎来逃避麻烦。|近义词：lies, falsehoods, untruths |反义词：truths, facts |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often tells fibs. = 他经常说谎。
She rarely tells fibs. = 她很少说谎。

一般过去时:
He told a fib about his age. = 他关于年龄说了个谎。
She told a fib to her mother. = 她对她母亲说了一个谎。

现在完成时:
He has told many fibs in his life. = 他一生中说了很多谎。
She has never told a fib to me. = 她从未对我撒过谎。

过去完成时:
He had told several fibs before the truth came out. = 在真相大白之前，他已经说了好几个谎。
She had told a fib to cover up her mistake. = 她撒了个谎来掩盖她的错误。

将来时:
He will tell fibs if he gets caught. = 如果他被抓住，他会撒谎。
She will not tell fibs, I believe. = 我相信她不会撒谎。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't told that fib, things would have been different. = 如果他没有说那个谎，事情就会不一样了。
I wish I hadn't told that fib. = 我真希望我没有说过那个谎。

被动语态:
Fibs were told to protect the innocent. = 为了保护无辜者而说了谎。
The fib was quickly uncovered. = 谎言很快就被揭穿了。

疑问句:
Did he tell any fibs? = 他说了谎话吗？
Were there any fibs in his story? = 他的故事里有什么谎话吗？

否定句:
He didn't tell any fibs, honestly. = 老实说，他没说谎。
She never tells fibs, to my knowledge. = 据我所知，她从不说谎。


PHRASE->
