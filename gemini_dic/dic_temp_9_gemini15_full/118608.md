messager:/ˈmɛsɪdʒər/| n. |信使，报信者；信息传递者 |A messager is a person or thing that delivers a message.  It can also refer to a software application or system that transmits messages.|例句：The messenger arrived breathless, delivering urgent news.  信使气喘吁吁地赶到，带来了紧急消息。
The company uses a secure messager for internal communications.  公司使用安全的信使软件进行内部沟通。|近义词：courier, envoy, carrier |反义词：receiver (in some contexts)
