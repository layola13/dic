furious: /ˈfjʊəriəs/ | adj. | 狂怒的，暴怒的 | Extremely angry; enraged. | 例句：He was furious at the injustice. 他对这种不公正感到愤怒。| 例句：She became furious when she saw the damage. 当她看到损坏时，她勃然大怒。|近义词：enraged, enraged, wrathful, indignant, irate |反义词：calm, peaceful, serene, tranquil


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is furious about the delay. = 他对延误感到愤怒。
She is furious with her brother. = 她对她哥哥很生气。


一般过去时:
He was furious when he heard the news. = 他听到这个消息后勃然大怒。
She was furious that they had lied to her. = 她很生气他们对她撒谎。


现在完成时:
I have been furious with him all day. = 我整天都对他很生气。
She has been furious ever since the accident. = 自从那次事故以来，她一直很生气。


过去完成时:
He had been furious before he calmed down. = 他在平静下来之前一直很生气。
She had been furious for weeks before she finally spoke. = 在她最终开口之前，她已经生气了好几个星期。


将来时:
