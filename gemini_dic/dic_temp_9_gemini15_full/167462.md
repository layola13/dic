/prɪˌvɛntəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可预防性；可避免性 | The preventability of the accident is being investigated.  事故的可预防性正在调查中。 | 近义词：avoidability, preventiveness | 反义词：inevitability, unavoidability | 名词

The preventability of many diseases is widely accepted. = 许多疾病的可预防性已被广泛接受。
