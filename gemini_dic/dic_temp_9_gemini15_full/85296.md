The exact powers and responsibilities varied depending on the context. | Example:  The harmost appointed by Sparta ruled the conquered city with an iron fist.  最高执政官严厉地统治着斯巴达征服的城市。|近义词：governor, magistrate, prefect, administrator |反义词：subject, citizen
