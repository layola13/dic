sentimentalising: /ˌsɛntɪmənˈtaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 感伤化；使多愁善感 | To make something sentimental; to excessively emphasize or indulge in sentiment.  | 例句：He sentimentalized his childhood memories, exaggerating the positive aspects and ignoring the hardships. 他将童年记忆感伤化，夸大了积极方面而忽略了苦难。  | 近义词：romanticizing, idealizing, romanticizing, mawkishly rendering | 反义词：objectifying, rationalizing
