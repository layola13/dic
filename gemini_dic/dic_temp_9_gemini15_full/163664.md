predeceasing:/ˌpriːdiˈsiːdɪŋ/| adj.|在前的，先前的，领先的| preceding means coming before something else in time or order.  It describes something that happens or exists before another thing. |例句：The preceding chapter explained the background.  前一章解释了背景。

The preceding week had been very busy.  前一周非常忙碌。


近义词：prior, foregoing, previous, antecedent |反义词：following, subsequent, succeeding
