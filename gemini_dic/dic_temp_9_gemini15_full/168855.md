proexperiment:/ˌproʊɪkˈspɛrɪmənt/| n.|预实验；预备实验|A preliminary experiment conducted to test the feasibility or refine the design of a larger or more complex experiment.  It is often used to identify potential problems or optimize experimental parameters before committing significant resources to the main study.|例句：The team conducted a proexperiment to assess the effectiveness of the new methodology before launching the full-scale trial.  团队在启动全面试验之前进行了一次预实验，以评估新方法的有效性。

例句：We ran a proexperiment to determine the optimal sample size for our main study. 我们进行了一次预实验以确定我们主要研究的最佳样本量。


近义词：pilot study, preliminary trial, test run|反义词：main experiment, final experiment|名词
