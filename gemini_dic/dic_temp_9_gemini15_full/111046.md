lispy:/ˈlɪspi/| adj.|口齿不清的，说话有咝音的|Characterized by a lisp; having difficulty pronouncing the sounds /s/ and /z/.  A lispy voice is one in which these sounds are replaced with /θ/ and /ð/, respectively (as in "th").|例句：He has a lispy voice. 他说话口齿不清。|例句：The child's lispy pronunciation of "sister" was quite endearing.  孩子把“sister”（姐姐）发音成咝音，听起来很可爱。|近义词：slurred, indistinct, sibilant|反义词：clear, articulate
