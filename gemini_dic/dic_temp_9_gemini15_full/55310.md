dissolve:/dɪˈzɒlv/|/dɪˈzɑːlv/| vt. vi. n.|溶解；解散；消失；瓦解|To become or cause to become liquid; to break up or be broken up into constituent parts; to fade away.|例句：The sugar dissolved in the water.  糖溶解在水里了。

The parliament was dissolved by the prime minister.  议会已被首相解散。

近义词：melt, break up, disintegrate, vanish|反义词：solidify, form, coalesce, materialize

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sugar dissolves quickly in hot water. = 糖在热水中快速溶解。
He dissolves the company every year. = 他每年都会解散公司。


一般过去时:
The snow dissolved in the spring sunshine. = 春日的阳光融化了积雪。
The band dissolved after their last tour. = 乐队在最后一次巡演后解散了。


现在完成时:
The ice has dissolved completely. = 冰已经完全融化了。
The tension between the two countries has dissolved. = 两国之间的紧张关系已经缓和了。


过去完成时:
The evidence had dissolved before the police arrived. = 警方到达之前证据已经消失了。
The company had dissolved before I could invest. = 我还没来得及投资公司就倒闭了。


将来时:
The snow will dissolve as the temperature rises. = 随着气温上升，雪会融化。
The government will dissolve parliament next month. = 政府下个月将解散议会。


虚拟语气:
If the problem were to dissolve, we would be happy. = 如果问题能够解决，我们会很高兴。
If the sugar dissolved more quickly, we would have finished earlier.=如果糖溶解得更快，我们就会早点完成。


被动语态:
The salt was dissolved in the water. = 盐溶解在水里了。
The partnership was dissolved amicably. = 合伙关系被友好地解散了。


疑问句:
Does the sugar dissolve easily? = 糖容易溶解吗？
Will the committee dissolve soon? = 委员会很快会解散吗？


否定句:
The ice didn't dissolve quickly enough. = 冰融化得不够快。
The government did not dissolve parliament. = 政府没有解散议会。


PHRASE->
dissolve into laughter = 忍不住大笑起来
dissolve in tears = 泪流满面
dissolve a marriage = 解除婚姻关系
dissolve a partnership = 解散合伙关系
dissolve an organization = 解散组织


词源:
dissolve源自古法语dissoudre和拉丁语dis-（分开）和solvere（松开，解开）。  它体现了溶解过程的本质：物质的成分被分开和松开。
