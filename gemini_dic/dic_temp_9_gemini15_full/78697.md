gigahertz:/ˈɡɪɡəhɜːrts/| n. |千兆赫兹|A gigahertz (GHz) is a unit of frequency equal to one billion hertz (1,000,000,000 Hz).  It's used to measure the clock speed of processors and other electronic devices.  A higher gigahertz rating generally indicates faster processing speed.|The new processor boasts a clock speed of 3.5 GHz.|megahertz, terahertz|kilohertz


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations like "hello".  It's primarily used descriptively.


PHRASE->
clock speed of 3 GHz = 3 GHz 的时钟速度
processor speed of 2.4 GHz = 2.4 GHz 的处理器速度
GHz processor = 千兆赫兹处理器
operating frequency of 5 GHz = 5 GHz 的工作频率
high GHz = 高千兆赫兹
