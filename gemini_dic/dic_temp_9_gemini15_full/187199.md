roast:/roʊst/ | vt. n.| 烤；烘；嘲弄；严厉批评 | to cook (food) by dry heat, typically in an oven or over a fire; to criticize someone or something severely. | 例句：I roasted a chicken for dinner. 我烤了一只鸡当晚餐。  She roasted him mercilessly for his mistake. 她无情地严厉批评了他的错误。| 近义词：bake, grill, criticize, rebuke | 反义词：praise, commend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He roasts coffee beans every morning. = 他每天早上都烘焙咖啡豆。
She roasts vegetables for her side dish. = 她烤蔬菜作为她的配菜。

一般过去时:
They roasted marshmallows around the campfire. = 他们在篝火旁烤棉花糖。
We roasted a pig for the festival. = 我们为节日烤了一头猪。

现在完成时:
I have roasted several chickens this week. = 我这周已经烤了几只鸡了。
She has roasted a turkey for Thanksgiving. = 她为感恩节烤了一只火鸡。

过去完成时:
He had roasted the meat before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前他已经烤好了肉。
They had roasted the nuts for the dessert. = 他们已经为甜点烤好了坚果。


将来时:
We will roast a lamb for the party. = 我们将为派对烤一只羊羔。
She will roast the potatoes with herbs. = 她将用香草烤土豆。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would roast a duck. = 如果我有更多时间，我会烤一只鸭子。
I wish I had roasted the vegetables longer. = 我希望我把蔬菜烤久一点。

被动语态:
The chicken was roasted to perfection. = 鸡肉烤得完美无缺。
The potatoes are being roasted in the oven. = 土豆正在烤箱里烤。

疑问句:
Are you going to roast the turkey today? = 你今天要烤火鸡吗？
Will they roast the marshmallows tonight? = 他们今晚会烤棉花糖吗？

否定句:
I did not roast the vegetables. = 我没有烤蔬菜。
She will not roast the chicken for dinner. = 她不会为晚餐烤鸡。
