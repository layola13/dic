simil: /ˈsɪmɪl/ | adj. | 相似的，类似的 | Similar things are alike in some way, but not necessarily identical.  They share some characteristics but may have differences in other aspects. | The two paintings are similar in style. 这两幅画风格相似。 |近义词：alike, like, comparable, analogous | 反义词：different, dissimilar, unlike


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The two houses are similar in size. = 这两座房子大小相似。
Her dress is similar to mine. = 她的裙子跟我的一样。


一般过去时:
Their reactions were similar to ours. = 他们的反应和我们的相似。
The two events were remarkably similar. = 这两件事非常相似。


现在完成时:
I have found several similar cases. = 我已经找到了几个类似的案例。
We have seen similar problems before. = 我们以前见过类似的问题。


过去完成时:
He had encountered similar difficulties in the past. = 他过去曾遇到过类似的困难。
The situation had been similar to previous years. = 这种情况与往年类似。


将来时:
Future studies will likely reveal similar results. = 未来的研究可能会揭示类似的结果。
The new model will be similar to the old one. = 新型号将与旧型号相似。


虚拟语气:
If the conditions were similar, the outcome would be the same. = 如果条件相似，结果就会相同。
I wish the two proposals were more similar. = 我希望这两个提案更相似一些。


被动语态:
The two approaches are considered similar. = 这两种方法被认为是相似的。
His painting style was described as similar to Picasso's. = 他的绘画风格被描述为与毕加索相似。


疑问句:
Are the two products similar? = 这两种产品相似吗？
In what ways are they similar? = 它们在哪些方面相似？


否定句:
The two plans are not similar. = 这两个计划并不相似。
Their responses were not at all similar. = 他们的反应一点也不相似。

PHRASE->
similar to = 与...相似
similar in = 在...方面相似
strikingly similar = 惊人地相似
bear a close similarity to = 与...非常相似
quite similar = 相当相似
