querulously:/ˈkwɛrəlʊsli/| adj.|抱怨地；牢骚满腹地；不满地 |  Characterized by complaining or grumbling.  It suggests a persistent and often petty complaining, expressing dissatisfaction or annoyance. |例句：He querulously complained about the cold weather. 他抱怨天气寒冷。

例句：She querulously questioned the fairness of the decision. 她不满地质疑了决定的公平性。


近义词：complainingly, grumblingly, peevishly, petulantly, fretfully


反义词：contentedly, cheerfully, happily, agreeably


词源：源自动词querulous (抱怨的；爱发牢骚的),  最终源自拉丁语 *querulus (抱怨的),  与queri (抱怨)相关。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He querulously complains about everything. = 他对所有事情都抱怨连连。
She querulously asks for more money. = 她抱怨地要求更多钱。

一般过去时:
He querulously complained about the long wait. = 他抱怨等待时间太长。
She querulously grumbled about the food. = 她抱怨食物不好吃。

现在完成时:
He has querulously criticized the project for weeks. = 他几周来一直抱怨这个项目。
She has querulously demanded a refund. = 她一直抱怨地要求退款。

过去完成时:
He had querulously opposed the plan before the meeting. = 他在会议前就一直抱怨这个计划。
She had querulously expressed her dissatisfaction earlier. = 她之前就曾抱怨地表达了不满。

将来时:
He will querulously object to any changes. = 他会抱怨任何改变。
She will querulously remind us of our promises. = 她会抱怨地提醒我们承诺。

虚拟语气:
If he were less querulous, people would like him more. = 如果他不那么爱抱怨，人们会更喜欢他。
I wish she wouldn't be so querulous all the time. = 我希望她不要总是那么爱抱怨。

被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)
His complaints were querulously voiced. = 他的抱怨是以抱怨的语气提出的。(较为生硬)
The issues were querulously brought up in the meeting. = 这些问题在会议上被抱怨地提了出来。(较为生硬)

疑问句: (很少直接用querulously构成疑问句)
Is he always so querulous? = 他总是这么爱抱怨吗？
Was her tone querulous during the conversation? = 她谈话时的语气是否抱怨？


否定句:
He didn't complain querulously; he simply stated his concerns. = 他并没有抱怨，只是陈述了他的担忧。
She didn't express her dissatisfaction querulously; she did it calmly. = 她并没有抱怨地表达不满，而是平静地表达。


PHRASE->

querulously object = 抱怨地反对
querulously criticize = 抱怨地批评
querulously express = 抱怨地表达
