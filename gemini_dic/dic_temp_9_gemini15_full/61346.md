embellish:/ɪmˈbɛlɪʃ/ | vt. | 装饰；润色；美化 | To make something more attractive by adding decorative details or features.  |例句：He embellished his story with humorous anecdotes. 他用幽默的轶事来润色他的故事。 |例句：The cake was embellished with fresh berries and chocolate shavings. 蛋糕上装饰着新鲜的浆果和巧克力屑。|近义词：adorn, decorate, ornament, enhance |反义词：simplify, strip, plain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She embellishes her apartment with antique furniture. = 她用古董家具装饰她的公寓。
He embellishes his reports with unnecessary details. = 他在报告中添加了不必要的细节来润色。


一般过去时:
They embellished the Christmas tree with colorful ornaments. = 他们用五彩缤纷的饰品装饰了圣诞树。
She embellished her resume to impress potential employers. = 她美化了自己的简历以打动潜在的雇主。


现在完成时:
She has embellished her garden with a beautiful fountain. = 她已经用一个美丽的喷泉装饰了她的花园。
He has embellished his account of the events to make himself look better. = 他已经美化了他对事件的描述，让自己看起来更好。


过去完成时:
They had embellished the room with flowers before the party. = 他们在派对前用鲜花装饰了房间。
She had embellished her story so much that no one believed her. = 她把故事美化得太过分了，以至于没有人相信她。


将来时:
They will embellish the cake with icing flowers. = 他们将会用糖霜花来装饰蛋糕。
She will embellish her presentation with compelling visuals. = 她将用引人注目的视觉效果来美化她的演示文稿。


虚拟语气:
If I were to embellish this dress, I would add some lace. = 如果我要装饰这件衣服，我会加上一些蕾丝。
I wish I had embellished my essay with more vivid descriptions. = 我希望我用更生动的描述来润色我的文章。


被动语态:
The cake was embellished with intricate designs. = 蛋糕装饰着复杂的图案。
His story was embellished to make it more interesting. = 他的故事被润色得更有趣了。


疑问句:
How will you embellish the wedding cake? = 你将如何装饰婚礼蛋糕？
Will she embellish her speech with personal anecdotes? = 她会在演讲中加入个人轶事来润色吗？


否定句:
He did not embellish his account of the accident. = 他没有美化他对事故的描述。
She chose not to embellish the simple design of the dress. = 她选择不修饰这件衣服简单的设计。
