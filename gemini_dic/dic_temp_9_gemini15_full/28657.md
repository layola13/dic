caries:/ˈkæriːz/| n.|龋齿；蛀牙|Decay of the teeth, caused by bacteria.  The process involves the demineralization of tooth enamel and dentin, leading to cavities.|例句：The dentist found several caries during the examination. 牙医在检查中发现了几个龋齿。

例句：Untreated caries can lead to severe toothache and infection. 未经治疗的龋齿会导致剧烈的牙痛和感染。


近义词：dental decay, tooth decay


反义词：healthy teeth, sound teeth


词性:名词


词源:  From Latin *caries, meaning "rottenness," "decay."  The term reflects the decay process involved in tooth caries.
