sips:/sɪps/| v. n.|啜饮；小口喝|To drink in small quantities.|She sipped her tea slowly. 她慢慢地啜饮着茶。|近义词：gulp, sip, drink|反义词：gorge, guzzle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sips his coffee every morning. = 他每天早上都啜饮咖啡。
She sips the wine delicately. = 她优雅地啜饮着葡萄酒。

一般过去时:
I sipped the hot chocolate slowly. = 我慢慢地啜饮着热巧克力。
He sipped his beer thoughtfully. = 他若有所思地啜饮着啤酒。

现在完成时:
She has sipped all the juice. = 她已经把所有的果汁都喝完了。
He has sipped from the bottle several times. = 他已经从瓶子里喝了好几次了。

过去完成时:
I had sipped the tea before the meeting started. = 会议开始前我已经喝了茶。
She had sipped the wine before she realized how strong it was. = 她喝了酒之后才意识到酒有多烈。

将来时:
I will sip the champagne at the party. = 我将在派对上啜饮香槟。
She will sip her tea while reading a book. = 她将一边看书一边啜饮茶。


虚拟语气:
If I were thirsty, I would sip some water. = 如果我渴了，我会喝点水。
I wish I could sip a refreshing drink right now. = 我真希望现在能喝一杯清爽的饮料。


被动语态:
The wine was sipped slowly by the connoisseur. = 这酒被鉴赏家慢慢地啜饮。
The tea was sipped delicately by the guests. = 茶被客人优雅地啜饮。


疑问句:
Do you sip your coffee black? = 你喝黑咖啡吗？（是否啜饮着喝）
Will he sip the tea or drink it quickly? = 他是啜饮茶还是快速喝掉？


否定句:
She didn't sip her coffee; she gulped it down. = 她没有啜饮咖啡；她是一饮而尽。
He didn't sip the wine; he poured it into a glass and drank it. = 他没有啜饮葡萄酒；他把它倒进杯子里喝掉了。


PHRASE->
sip by sip = 一口一口地
sip on something = 慢慢地啜饮某物
take a sip = 抿一口
sip and savor = 细细品味
sip slowly = 慢慢地啜饮
