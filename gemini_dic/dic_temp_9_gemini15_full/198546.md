shroud:/ʃraʊd/ | n. v. |裹尸布；遮蔽物；掩盖；遮蔽 | A shroud is a piece of cloth used to wrap a dead body for burial.  It can also refer to something that covers or obscures something else.  As a verb, it means to cover or conceal something. |例句：The body was wrapped in a white shroud. 尸体被裹在一块白色的裹尸布里。 The mist shrouded the mountains. 薄雾笼罩着群山。|近义词：sheet, veil, cover, conceal |反义词：reveal, uncover, expose |名词，动词
