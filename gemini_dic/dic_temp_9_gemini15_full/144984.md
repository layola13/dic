overact:/ˈoʊvərækt/ | vt. |夸张表演；反应过度|To act in an exaggerated or unnatural way.  It can also refer to reacting to something with excessive emotion or intensity.|例句：He overacted his role as the villain, making him seem cartoonish. 他扮演反派角色时表演夸张，显得有些滑稽。

例句：She overreacted to the minor criticism, bursting into tears. 她对小小的批评反应过度，哭了起来。|近义词：exaggerate, overemphasize, dramatize |反义词：underact, underplay, restrain
