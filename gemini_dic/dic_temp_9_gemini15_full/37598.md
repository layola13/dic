communicate:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/ | vt. vi. | 传达；沟通；交流 | To share or exchange information, news, or ideas.  | 例句：We communicate daily through email. 我们每天通过电子邮件交流。  | 例句：He struggled to communicate his ideas effectively. 他努力有效地表达他的想法。 |近义词：convey, impart, interact | 反义词：isolate, silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They communicate regularly. = 他们定期沟通。
I communicate with my friends online. = 我通过网络与朋友交流。

一般过去时:
She communicated her concerns to the manager. = 她向经理表达了她的担忧。
We communicated effectively during the meeting. = 我们在会议上进行了有效的沟通。

现在完成时:
He has communicated his decision to the team. = 他已经向团队传达了他的决定。
They have communicated with each other for years. = 他们已经互相联系多年了。

过去完成时:
Before the presentation, she had communicated her key points to her team. = 在演示之前，她已经向她的团队传达了她的要点。
He had communicated his intentions clearly before leaving. = 他离开之前已经清楚地表达了他的意图。

将来时:
I will communicate my findings in the report. = 我将在报告中传达我的发现。
They will communicate the news to the public soon. = 他们很快就会向公众发布这个消息。

虚拟语气:
If I could communicate with animals, I would understand them better. = 如果我能与动物沟通，我会更好地理解它们。
I wish I could communicate more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地沟通。

被动语态:
The message was communicated clearly. = 信息传达得很清楚。
The news was communicated to the employees immediately. = 消息立即传达给了员工。

疑问句:
How do you communicate with your colleagues? = 你如何与同事沟通？
Does he communicate well in English? = 他英语沟通得好吗？

否定句:
He didn't communicate his feelings very well. = 他没有很好地表达他的感受。
They failed to communicate effectively. = 他们未能有效沟通。


PHRASE->
communicate with = 与…沟通
communicate effectively = 有效沟通
communicate clearly = 清晰沟通
communicate a message = 传达信息
means of communication = 沟通方式
