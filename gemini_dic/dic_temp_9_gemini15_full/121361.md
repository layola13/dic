misground:/ˈmɪsɡraʊnd/| adj.| 错误的基础, 不准确的根据 |  Lacking a sound or accurate basis; unfounded.  It refers to something that is based on incorrect or insufficient information, leading to a flawed understanding or conclusion. | 例句：His argument was misgrounded in faulty assumptions.  他的论点基于错误的假设，因此站不住脚。

例句：The company's decision was misgrounded, leading to significant financial losses. 公司的决定是基于错误的判断，导致了巨大的经济损失。


近义词：unfounded, baseless, groundless, fallacious

反义词：well-founded, sound, valid, justified


词性:形容词
