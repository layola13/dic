geocachers:/ˈdʒioʊkæʃərz/ | n. | 参与地理寻宝的人 | People who participate in geocaching.  Geocaching is an outdoor recreational activity, in which participants and teams use a Global Positioning System (GPS) receiver or a mobile device and other navigational techniques to hide and find containers, called geocaches, or caches, anywhere in the world. | Example:  The geocachers excitedly dug up the hidden container.  参与地理寻宝的人兴奋地挖出了隐藏的容器。|近义词: cache hunters, GPS treasure hunters | 反义词:  None specifically applicable.
