noncorrespondent:/ˌnɒnkɒrəˈspɒndənt/| adj.| 不一致的；不相符的；无对应关系的 |Not corresponding; lacking correspondence or agreement.  It implies a lack of matching or similarity between two or more things that are expected to be related or similar. |例句：The two accounts of the incident were noncorrespondent, making it difficult to establish the truth.  两个关于事件的说法不一致，这使得难以确定真相。
The results of the two experiments were noncorrespondent. 两个实验的结果不一致。|近义词：discrepant, inconsistent, incompatible |反义词：correspondent, consistent, compatible
