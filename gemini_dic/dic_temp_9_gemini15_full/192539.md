scudding:/ˈskʌdɪŋ/| v.|飞奔；疾驰；快速移动|To move swiftly, especially in a reckless or uncontrolled manner.  The clouds were scudding across the sky.|近义词：hurrying, dashing, rushing|反义词：crawling, creeping, drifting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The clouds are scudding across the sky. = 云彩在快速飘过天空。
He scuds along the street. = 他沿着街道飞奔。


一般过去时:
The boat scudded before the wind. = 小船在风中疾驰。
She scudded down the hallway. = 她沿着走廊飞奔。


现在完成时:
The storm has scudded away. = 暴风雨已经过去了。
We have scudded through the work. = 我们已经快速完成了工作。


过去完成时:
The ship had scudded under the bridge before the storm hit. = 船在暴风雨来临之前已经快速驶过了桥下。
They had scudded to safety before the landslide. = 山体滑坡发生之前，他们已经快速逃到了安全地带。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
