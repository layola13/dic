journeyings:/ˈdʒɜːrniɪŋz/ | n. | 旅行；旅程；行程 | The plural form of "journeying," referring to multiple acts of traveling or the process of traveling over a period of time.  It emphasizes the ongoing nature of travel or a series of travels. |例句：His journeyings took him across continents. 他的旅行带他横跨各大洲。

例句：The book details the many journeyings of a famous explorer. 这本书详细记述了一位著名探险家的多次旅行。


近义词：travels, voyages, expeditions, wanderings

反义词：None (it's a noun describing an action, not easily paired with an antonym)

词性:Noun
