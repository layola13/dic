coupes:/kuːˈpeɪz/| n.|双门轿跑车|A coupé (plural coupes) is a type of car with two doors and a sloping roofline.  It's typically a sporty or luxurious vehicle. |例句：He bought a new sports coupe. 他买了一辆新的跑车。

例句：Many luxury car brands offer elegant coupes. 许多豪华汽车品牌都提供优雅的双门轿跑车。


近义词：sports car, two-door car |反义词：sedan, station wagon

词源:
The word "coupé" comes from the French word  "couper," meaning "to cut" or "to shorten." This refers to the shortened, or "cut," rear deck of early coupe designs.  The word entered English in the late 19th century.
