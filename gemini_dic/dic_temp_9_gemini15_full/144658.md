outwrestled: /ˌaʊtˈrɛstld/ | v. | 胜过；以摔跤战胜 |  To defeat (someone) in a wrestling match.  This word implies a decisive victory, often suggesting a struggle. | 例句：He outwrestled his opponent in the final round. 他在决赛中战胜了他的对手。  |  The champion outwrestled the challenger with a surprising move. 冠军以一个令人惊讶的动作战胜了挑战者。| overcome, defeat, conquer | lose to, succumb to |  动词

词源:
The word "outwrestled" is formed by adding the prefix "out-" (meaning "to a greater extent than") to the past tense of the verb "wrestle."  "Wrestle" ultimately derives from Old English *wraslian,  related to words meaning "to twist" or "to struggle."
