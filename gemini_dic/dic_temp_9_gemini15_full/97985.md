inoculation:/ˌɪnɒkjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. |接种，预防接种；灌输，接种（思想等）|The act of introducing a vaccine or other substance into the body to produce immunity to a disease; the introduction of new ideas or beliefs.|例句：The doctor recommended inoculation against the flu. 医生建议接种流感疫苗。|例句：The inoculation of young minds with progressive ideas is crucial. 向年轻人的思想灌输进步的理念至关重要。|近义词：vaccination, immunization, implantation|反义词：infection, contamination


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The doctor inoculates patients against various diseases. = 医生为病人接种各种疾病的疫苗。
She inoculates her students with a love of learning. = 她培养学生对学习的热爱。


一般过去时:
They inoculated the entire village against polio. = 他们为整个村庄接种了小儿麻痹症疫苗。
He inoculated his speech with humor.  = 他在演讲中加入了幽默。


现在完成时:
Have you been inoculated against measles? = 你接种过麻疹疫苗了吗？
She has inoculated her writing with a unique style. = 她已经用独特的风格来写她的作品了。


过去完成时:
They had inoculated the animals before the experiment. = 他们在实验前已经给动物接种了疫苗。
The teacher had inoculated the students with a passion for history. = 老师已经培养了学生们对历史的热情。


将来时:
The nurses will inoculate the children tomorrow. = 护士们明天将为孩子们接种疫苗。
He will inoculate his presentation with compelling visuals. = 他将在他的演示文稿中加入引人注目的视觉效果。


虚拟语气:
If they had been inoculated, they would not have contracted the disease. = 如果他们接种了疫苗，就不会感染这种疾病了。
If I were to inoculate my team with confidence, we might succeed. = 如果我能给我的团队灌输信心，我们可能会成功。


被动语态:
The vaccines were inoculated into the patients' arms. = 疫苗注射到病人的手臂里。
New ideas were inoculated into the company culture. = 新思想被灌输到公司文化中。


疑问句:
When were you inoculated against tetanus? = 你什么时候接种的破伤风疫苗？
How can we best inoculate our children against negative influences? = 我们如何才能最好地保护我们的孩子免受负面影响？


否定句:
They did not inoculate the crops against pests. = 他们没有给庄稼接种抗虫病疫苗。
He didn't inoculate his argument with sufficient evidence.  = 他没有用足够的证据来支持他的论点。
