nonmalignant: /ˌnɒnˈmælɪɡnənt/ | adj. |非恶性的，良性的| Not cancerous; not tending to spread or invade other tissues.  A nonmalignant tumor is one that does not spread to other parts of the body. |


例句：

The biopsy revealed the growth to be nonmalignant. = 活检显示生长物为良性。

The doctor reassured the patient that the lump was nonmalignant. = 医生向病人保证肿块是良性的。


近义词：benign, harmless

反义词：malignant, cancerous

词性：形容词
