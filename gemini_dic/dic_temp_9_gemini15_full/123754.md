moorband:/ˈmʊrbænd/| n.|沼泽地带|A moorband is a type of wetland, specifically a type of peat bog or mire characterized by its location on a relatively flat expanse of land, often with a high water table.  These areas are typically dominated by specific types of vegetation adapted to waterlogged conditions.|Example: The rare orchids were found growing in the remote moorband.|近义词：mire, bog, fen, marsh|反义词：dryland, upland
