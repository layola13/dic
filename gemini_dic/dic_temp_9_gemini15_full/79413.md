glinted:/ˈɡlɪntɪd/ | v. |闪光，闪烁 | To shine brightly with reflected light; sparkle.  |例句：The diamond glinted on her finger. 钻石在她手指上闪闪发光。
The sun glinted off the wet pavement. 太阳在潮湿的路面上闪耀。|近义词：sparkled, shimmered, glittered |反义词：dull, dim

The metal glints in the sunlight. = 金属在阳光下闪闪发光。
