infographic:/ˌɪnfəˈɡræfɪk/| n. |信息图表 |A visual representation of information or data, often using a combination of images, charts, and minimal text to communicate complex information quickly and clearly.  Infographics are designed to be easily understood and engaging, making them a popular tool for education, marketing, and communication. | Example:  The infographic clearly illustrated the steps involved in the process.  这个信息图表清晰地阐述了该过程的步骤。|近义词：chart, diagram, graph, illustration |反义词：None
