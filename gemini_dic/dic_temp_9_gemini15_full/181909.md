remaining:/rɪˈmeɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 剩余的，剩下的 |  Remaining means still existing or left after other parts have been used, removed, or destroyed. It describes something that is leftover or not yet used. |  Example:  The remaining students were dismissed early.  剩余的学生提前被解散了。  The remaining food was given to the homeless.  剩下的食物给了无家可归的人。 | 近义词：leftover, residual, extant | 反义词：gone, used, spent |  形容词
