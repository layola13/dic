cavaliero:/ˌkævəˈljɛroʊ/| n. |骑士；武士；绅士|A cavalier is a knight or gentleman, especially one of the medieval period.  The term often implies a chivalrous, gallant, or sometimes arrogant and haughty demeanor. |例句：He acted like a true cavalier, rescuing the damsel in distress. 他像真正的骑士一样，救了陷入困境的少女。

例句：The painting depicted a cavalier in shining armor.  这幅画描绘了一位身穿闪亮盔甲的骑士。


近义词：knight, gentleman, nobleman |反义词：coward, villain, churl


词性：名词
