detrainments:/ˌdiːˈtreɪnmənts/| n.|脱轨；下车；（铁路）卸货 |The act of removing something from a train, or the process of passengers leaving a train.  It can also refer to the negative consequences or setbacks resulting from a disruption or derailment. |例句：The detrainments at the station caused significant delays. 车站的旅客下车过程造成了严重的延误。


例句：The unexpected detrainments of goods disrupted the supply chain. 货物意外的卸车扰乱了供应链。


近义词：disruptions, derailments, setbacks, unloading


反义词：loading, embarkation, boarding
