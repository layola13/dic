monophyletic: /ˌmɒnəʊfaɪˈlɛtɪk/ | adj. | 单系的；单源的 |  Describing a group of organisms that includes a common ancestor and all of its descendants.  A monophyletic group is also called a clade.  It's the opposite of paraphyletic and polyphyletic. |

例句：

The primates are a monophyletic group. = 灵长类动物是一个单系群。

Birds are considered a monophyletic group, descended from a common theropod dinosaur ancestor. = 鸟类被认为是一个单系群，起源于共同的兽脚类恐龙祖先。


近义词：clade, holophyletic


反义词：paraphyletic, polyphyletic


词性: adjective
