d-glucose: /ˌdiːˈɡluːkoʊs/ | n. | D-葡萄糖 | A monosaccharide sugar, the most common and important carbohydrate in living organisms.  It is a key source of energy and a crucial building block in many biological processes.  It is also known as dextrose. |  例句：Glucose is the primary source of energy for most cells. 葡萄糖是大多数细胞的主要能量来源。


例句：Plants produce glucose through photosynthesis. 植物通过光合作用产生葡萄糖。


近义词：dextrose, grape sugar


反义词：None (no direct antonym, but could consider fructose or other sugars as contrasting)


词性: noun
