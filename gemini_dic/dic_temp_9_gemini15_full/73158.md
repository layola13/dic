forbearant: /fɔːrˈbɛərənt/ | adj. | 宽容的；忍耐的；克制的 |  Patient and tolerant; showing forbearance.  |  例句：He was remarkably forbearant, given the provocation.  他考虑到挑衅，表现得非常宽容。

例句：Her forbearant nature allowed her to handle the difficult situation with grace.  她宽容的性格让她优雅地处理了这个棘手的情况。


近义词：patient, tolerant, lenient, understanding, forbearing


反义词：intolerant, impatient, unforgiving, harsh, severe


词性：形容词


词源：源自动词 forbear (忍耐，克制)，加上形容词后缀 -ant。  Forbear itself comes from Old English *forberan,  from for- ("away, completely") + beran ("to bear").  The sense evolution reflects a movement from "to refrain from bearing" to "to bear with patience".
