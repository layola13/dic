reprobes:/rɪˈproʊbz/ | vt. |谴责；非难；反对|To express strong disapproval of; to censure severely.|例句：The committee reproved the member for his misconduct.  委员会因其行为不端而谴责了该成员。|例句：She reproved him for his laziness. 她谴责了他的懒惰。|近义词：rebuke, reprimand, condemn, denounce |反义词：praise, commend, approve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reproves his son for his bad grades. = 他因儿子成绩差而责备他。
The teacher reproves students who cheat on exams. = 老师谴责考试作弊的学生。


一般过去时:
The judge reproved the lawyer for his unprofessional conduct. = 法官因律师行为不专业而谴责了他。
She reproved her friend for spreading rumors. = 她责备她的朋友散布谣言。


现在完成时:
The principal has reproved several students this year for bullying. = 校长今年已因欺凌行为谴责了几名学生。
He has reproved her many times for her carelessness. = 他多次因她的粗心大意而责备她。


过去完成时:
The manager had reproved the employee before he was fired. = 在该员工被解雇之前，经理已对他进行了谴责。
She had reproved him several times, but he continued to misbehave. = 她已经责备了他好几次，但他仍然继续行为不端。


将来时:
The parents will reprove their child if he continues to lie. = 如果孩子继续说谎，父母将会责备他。
The boss will reprove the employee for missing the deadline. = 老板将因错过截止日期而责备员工。


虚拟语气:
If he reproved her more often, perhaps she would behave better. = 如果他更经常地责备她，也许她会表现得更好。
I wish he had reproved his son sooner. = 我希望他早点责备他的儿子。


被动语态:
The student was reproved for disrupting the class. = 该学生因扰乱课堂秩序而受到谴责。
His actions were reproved by the entire community. = 他的行为受到了整个社区的谴责。


疑问句:
Will the teacher reprove him for his tardiness? = 老师会因为他的迟到而责备他吗？
Did the manager reprove the employee for the mistake? = 经理因这个错误而责备了员工吗？


否定句:
The boss did not reprove the employee for his minor mistake. = 老板并没有因为员工的小错误而责备他。
The teacher did not reprove the student, instead she offered help. = 老师没有责备学生，而是提供了帮助。


PHRASE->
reprove someone sharply = 严厉地责备某人
reprove someone for doing something = 因做某事而责备某人
reprove someone's actions = 谴责某人的行为
reprove someone mildly = 轻微地责备某人
reprove someone publicly = 公开谴责某人
