militates:/ˈmɪlɪteɪts/| v.|反对；不利于；妨碍|To speak or act against something; to be an obstacle or a hindrance.|His lack of experience militates against his chances of getting the job.  他的缺乏经验不利于他获得这份工作。|The extreme heat militates against outdoor activities.  酷暑妨碍了户外活动。|近义词：opposes, hinders, obstructs, impedes|反义词：aids, assists, facilitates


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cold weather militates against a picnic. =寒冷的天气不利于野餐。
His arrogance militates against his success. = 他的傲慢妨碍了他的成功。


一般过去时:
The lack of funding militated against the project's completion. = 资金不足阻碍了项目的完成。
Her shyness militated against her making new friends. = 她的害羞妨碍了她结交新朋友。


现在完成时:
His poor health has militated against his career advancement. = 他糟糕的健康状况一直阻碍着他的事业发展。
The ongoing conflict has militated against economic growth. = 持续的冲突一直阻碍着经济增长。


过去完成时:
The bad weather had militated against their plans for a weekend trip. = 恶劣的天气已经破坏了他们周末旅行的计划。
Previous failures had militated against his confidence. = 之前的失败已经动摇了他的信心。


将来时:
The expected storm will militate against any outdoor events. = 预计的暴风雨将不利于任何户外活动。
A lack of resources will militate against the project's timely completion. = 资源匮乏将不利于项目的按时完成。


虚拟语气:
If only the weather hadn't militated against our plans, we would have had a great time. = 如果天气没有妨碍我们的计划，我们会玩得很开心。
If his lack of preparation hadn't militated against him, he would have passed the exam. = 如果他的准备不足没有妨碍他，他就能通过考试。


被动语态:
His application was militated against by his lack of experience. = 他的申请由于缺乏经验而被否决。
The proposal was militated against by the board of directors. = 该提案遭到董事会的反对。


疑问句:
Does his age militate against his chances of getting the job? = 他的年龄是否会影响他获得这份工作的几率？
Does the current economic climate militate against investment? = 当前的经济环境是否不利于投资？


否定句:
The good weather didn't militate against our plans for a barbecue. = 好天气没有妨碍我们烧烤的计划。
His hard work didn't militate against his success. = 他的努力并没有妨碍他的成功。


PHRASE->
milita against something = 反对某事
milita in favor of something = 有利于某事
strongly militate against = 强烈反对
effectively militate against = 有效地阻碍
openly militate against = 公开反对
