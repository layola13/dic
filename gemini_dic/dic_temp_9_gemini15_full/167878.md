princeliest: /ˈprɪnsliːst/ | adj. | 最像王子的；最尊贵的；最华丽的 | Most princely; most noble; most magnificent.  It is the superlative form of "princely".  It suggests an extreme degree of qualities associated with a prince, such as nobility, generosity, magnificence, or elegance. |


The princeliest of gifts was bestowed upon the princess.  = 最尊贵的礼物赠予了公主。


He lived in a princeliest mansion overlooking the sea. = 他住在一座最华丽的海景别墅里。


近义词:  regal, majestic, grand, magnificent, noble


反义词:  common, humble, lowly, mean


词性: adjective
