catastrophic:/ˌkætəˈstrɒfɪk/| adj. |灾难性的，灾害的 | extremely bad or disastrous |The earthquake had catastrophic consequences for the region. 地震给该地区带来了灾难性的后果。|近义词：disastrous, devastating, calamitous |反义词：beneficial, fortunate, positive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The situation is catastrophic. = 情况灾难性。
This policy has catastrophic effects. = 这项政策有灾难性的影响。


一般过去时:
The fire was catastrophic. = 那场火灾是灾难性的。
The consequences of the decision were catastrophic. = 这个决定的后果是灾难性的。


现在完成时:
The failure has been catastrophic for the company. = 这次失败对公司来说是灾难性的。
The environmental damage has been catastrophic. = 环境破坏已经到了灾难性的程度。


过去完成时:
The damage had been catastrophic before the rescue efforts began. = 在救援工作开始之前，破坏已经达到了灾难性的程度。
They realized that the situation had become catastrophic. = 他们意识到情况已经变得灾难性了。


将来时:
The consequences could be catastrophic. = 后果可能不堪设想。
The impact of the storm will be catastrophic. = 暴风雨的影响将是灾难性的。


虚拟语气:
If we don't act now, the consequences would be catastrophic. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，后果将不堪设想。
It would be catastrophic if the dam broke. = 如果大坝决堤，后果将是灾难性的。


被动语态:
The city was devastated by a catastrophic earthquake. = 城市被一场灾难性的地震摧毁。
The project was deemed catastrophic due to mismanagement. = 由于管理不善，该项目被认为是灾难性的。


疑问句:
Is the situation really catastrophic? = 情况真的那么糟糕吗？
Could this decision have catastrophic consequences? = 这个决定可能会有灾难性的后果吗？


否定句:
The situation was not catastrophic, although it was serious. = 情况虽然严重，但并非灾难性。
The results were not as catastrophic as we feared. = 结果并不像我们担心的那样灾难性。
