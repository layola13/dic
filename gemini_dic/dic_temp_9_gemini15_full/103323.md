jiggle:/ˈdʒɪɡəl/| vt. & vi. |轻微摇动；晃动|To move or cause to move slightly and irregularly.  The jelly jiggled on the plate. 果冻在盘子上晃动。|近义词：shake, wiggle, wobble |反义词：stabilize, fix


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He jiggles his keys in his pocket. = 他在他的口袋里轻轻摇晃他的钥匙。
The leaves jiggle in the gentle breeze. = 叶子在微风中轻轻摇动。


一般过去时:
She jiggled the doorknob, but the door remained locked. = 她轻轻地摇动门把手，但门仍然锁着。
The earthquake jiggled the buildings. = 地震使建筑物摇晃。


现在完成时:
The car has jiggled loose from its moorings. = 汽车已经从它的系泊处松动了。
I have jiggled the handle several times, but it won't open. = 我已经摇晃了几次把手，但是它打不开。


过去完成时:
He had jiggled the handle repeatedly before realizing it was broken. = 他反复摇动把手后才意识到它坏了。
The machine had jiggled so much that it finally broke down. = 机器晃动得厉害，最后终于坏了。


将来时:
The wind will jiggle the branches of the tree. = 风会摇动树枝。
I will jiggle the lock until it opens. = 我会一直摇晃锁直到它打开。


虚拟语气:
If I jiggled the handle harder, it might open. = 如果我用力摇晃把手，它可能会打开。
I wish the car hadn't jiggled so much during the bumpy ride. = 我真希望汽车在颠簸的路程中不要晃动得那么厉害。


被动语态:
The loose tooth was jiggled out by the dentist. = 松动的牙齿被牙医摇掉了。
The picture was jiggled out of alignment. = 图片被摇晃得移位了。


疑问句:
Is the loose tile jiggling again? = 那块松动的瓷砖还在晃动吗？
Did you jiggle the handle before you tried the key? = 你在尝试钥匙之前有没有摇动把手？


否定句:
The machine didn't jiggle at all during operation. = 机器在运行过程中根本没有晃动。
He didn't jiggle the handle; he just turned it. = 他没有摇晃把手；他只是转动它。


PHRASE->
jiggle the handle = 摇动把手
jiggle the key in the ignition = 在点火开关处摇晃钥匙
jiggle the loose tooth = 晃动松动的牙齿
jiggle the lever = 摇动杠杆
give it a jiggle = 晃动一下它
