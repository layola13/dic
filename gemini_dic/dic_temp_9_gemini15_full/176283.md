re-description:/ˌriːdɪˈskrɪpʃən/| n. |重新描述，再描述 |A re-description is a new or revised description of something, often offering a different perspective or adding new information to a previous account.  It might involve a more detailed explanation, a correction of inaccuracies, or simply a different way of presenting the same information. |例句：The witness provided a re-description of the suspect after further reflection.  目击者在进一步思考后对嫌疑人进行了重新描述。|近义词：restatement, reformulation, revision, paraphrase |反义词：original description, first account |名词


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, tenses don't apply.  Usage focuses on its role in a sentence.

Example 1: The scientist's re-description of the experiment clarified the methodology. 科学家的实验重新描述阐明了方法论。
Example 2:  Her re-description of the accident contradicted the initial police report. 她对事故的重新描述与最初的警报相矛盾。
