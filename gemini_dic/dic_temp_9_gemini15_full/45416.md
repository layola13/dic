cuppings:/ˈkʌpɪŋz/| n.|品尝(咖啡豆),杯测|The process of evaluating the quality of coffee beans by tasting and smelling brewed samples.  It involves assessing various aspects like aroma, flavor, body, and acidity.|例句：The barista conducted a cuppings to select the best beans for the day.  咖啡师进行了一次杯测，以挑选当天最好的咖啡豆。


例句：Our company holds regular cuppings to ensure consistent quality. 我们公司定期进行杯测以确保质量的一致性。
