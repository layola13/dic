gastroenteritis:/ˌɡæstrəʊˌɛntəˈraɪtɪs/ | n. |胃肠炎 |Inflammation of the stomach and intestines, typically causing vomiting, diarrhea, and abdominal cramps.  It's often caused by viral or bacterial infection. |例句：The gastroenteritis outbreak at the school forced its closure for several days. 学校的胃肠炎疫情迫使学校关闭数天。  |  例句：He suffered from severe gastroenteritis after eating contaminated food. 他吃了受污染的食物后患上了严重的胃肠炎。|近义词：stomach flu, intestinal flu, food poisoning |反义词：None
