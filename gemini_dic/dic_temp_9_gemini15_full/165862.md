preoffering: /ˌpriːˈɔːfəriŋ/ | n. | 预先发行；预售；先行发行 |  A preoffering refers to the activities and processes that occur before an official public offering of securities, such as stocks or bonds.  It involves preparing the necessary documentation, marketing the offering to potential investors, and securing regulatory approvals.  | Example: The company completed the preoffering due diligence before launching its IPO.  这家公司在启动IPO之前完成了预先发行尽职调查。 | 近义词：advance offering, preliminary offering | 反义词：post-offering
