outjumps:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌmps/ | v. |跳出；超过 | to jump further than someone or something else; to surpass or excel | The rabbit outjumps the dog. 兔子跳过了狗。The new model outjumps its predecessor in performance. 新型号在性能上超越了其前代产品。 | surpasses, exceeds, outstrips |  falls short of


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outjumps the fence. = 他跳过了栅栏。
The athlete outjumps his rivals. = 这位运动员跳过了他的对手。


一般过去时:
The kangaroo outjumped the fence. = 袋鼠跳过了栅栏。
She outjumped all her competitors. = 她跳过了所有竞争对手。


现在完成时:
He has outjumped his personal best. = 他跳过了他的个人最好成绩。
The team has outjumped their expectations. = 这个团队的表现超过了他们的预期。


过去完成时:
The horse had outjumped the hurdle before the accident. = 马在事故发生前跳过了障碍物。
They had outjumped their opponents in the previous round. = 他们在上一轮比赛中超过了对手。


将来时:
He will outjump the record. = 他将打破纪录。
She will outjump her previous performance. = 她将超越她之前的表现。


虚拟语气:
If he trained harder, he would outjump everyone. = 如果他训练更努力，他就能跳过所有人。
I wish I could outjump that wall. = 我真希望我能跳过那堵墙。


被动语态:
The record was outjumped by a young athlete. = 这项纪录被一位年轻运动员打破了。
His expectations were outjumped by the team's performance. = 团队的表现超过了他的预期。


疑问句:
Did he outjump the others? = 他跳过了其他人吗？
Can she outjump the high jump bar? = 她能跳过高跳杆吗？


否定句:
He didn't outjump the wall. = 他没有跳过墙。
She couldn't outjump the reigning champion. = 她无法超越卫冕冠军。
