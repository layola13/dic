embouchures:/ˈɑːmbəʃʊr/| n. |吹口方法；吹口；乐器吹口位置|The way in which a musician's lips are positioned to produce sound on a wind instrument.  It is crucial for tone quality and playing technique. |Example:  The oboist carefully adjusted her embouchure to achieve a clear tone.  长笛演奏家仔细调整了他的吹口以获得清晰的音色。|近义词：mouth position, lip position |反义词：无
