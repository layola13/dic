词源:
The word "moisturising" is derived from the word "moisture," which comes from the Old French word "moisteure," meaning "moisture." The suffix "-ising" is a present participle suffix indicating the act of doing something.  Therefore, "moisturising" describes the action of making or keeping something moist.
