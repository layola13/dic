prodemocrats: /ˌproʊˈdɛməkræts/ | n. | 支持民主者，亲民主人士 |  Supporters of democracy; individuals or groups who advocate for democratic principles and systems of government.  They often actively participate in political processes to promote democratic values and reforms. The term is frequently used in contexts where there is a contrast with those who oppose or undermine democratic systems. | Example: The prodemocrats held a large rally to protest the government's actions. 支持民主人士举行大型集会抗议政府行为。 | 近义词：democracy advocates, democrats, pro-democracy activists | 反义词：authoritarians, anti-democrats, pro-autocrats | 名词
