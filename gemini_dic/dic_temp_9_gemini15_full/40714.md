converting:/kənˈvɜːrtɪŋ/ | v. | 转化；转换；改变；兑换 | The act of changing something from one form, state, or system to another.  It can refer to physical changes, data changes, or even changes in belief or opinion. | 例句：They are converting the old factory into apartments. = 他们正在把旧工厂改造成公寓。

They are converting the data from one format to another. = 他们正在将数据从一种格式转换为另一种格式。


近义词：transforming, changing, transmuting, altering | 反义词：maintaining, preserving, conserving | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is converting dollars to euros. = 他正在把美元兑换成欧元。
The factory is converting raw materials into finished products. = 工厂正在将原材料转化为成品。

一般过去时:
She converted her old car into a race car. = 她把她的旧车改造成了一辆赛车。
We converted the attic into a bedroom. = 我们把阁楼改造成了一间卧室。

现在完成时:
They have converted all their documents to digital format. = 他们已经把所有文件转换成数字格式了。
He has converted to a new religion. = 他已经皈依了新的宗教。

过去完成时:
They had converted the building before the new tenants moved in. = 新租户搬进来之前，他们已经改造了那栋建筑。
She had converted her savings into stocks. = 她已经把她的储蓄转换成股票了。


将来时:
We will convert the garage into a home office. = 我们将把车库改造成家庭办公室。
They will convert the old schoolhouse into a museum. = 他们将把旧校舍改造成博物馆。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would convert my notes into a book. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把我的笔记改编成一本书。
I wish I could convert my ideas into reality. = 我希望我能把我的想法变成现实。


被动语态:
The old building is being converted into a hotel. = 旧楼正在被改造成一家酒店。
The data will be converted automatically. = 数据将自动转换。


疑问句:
Are they converting the land into a park? = 他们要把这块地改造成公园吗？
How do you convert Celsius to Fahrenheit? = 你如何将摄氏度转换为华氏度？


否定句:
They did not convert the warehouse into living space. = 他们没有把仓库改造成居住空间。
He hasn't converted to vegetarianism. = 他还没有成为素食主义者。


PHRASE->
convert to: 转变为...
convert into: 转换成...
convert from: 从...转换
data conversion: 数据转换
energy conversion: 能量转换
