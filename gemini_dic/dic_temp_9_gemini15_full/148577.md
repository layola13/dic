overtalked:/ˌoʊvərˈtɔːkt/| v. | 说的太多；喋喋不休 | To talk excessively or for too long; to speak more than is necessary or desirable.  |例句：I overtalked at the meeting and now I feel bad. 我在会议上说了太多话，现在感觉很不好。

例句：He overtalked his welcome at the party. 他在派对上说了太多话，不受欢迎了。


近义词：babbled, chattered, gabbled, rambled, prattled |反义词： remained silent, listened attentively

词源:
The word "overtalked" is formed from the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") and the verb "talked" (the past tense of "talk").  It's a straightforward combination reflecting the meaning of excessive talking.
