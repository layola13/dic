healthy:/ˈhɛlθi/ | adj. |健康的，强健的 |Possessing or enjoying good health; free from illness or injury.  |例句：She leads a healthy lifestyle and exercises regularly. 她过着健康的生活方式，经常锻炼。|例句：Eating a healthy diet is crucial for maintaining good health.  健康的饮食对保持健康至关重要。|近义词：fit, well, robust, vigorous |反义词：unhealthy, sick, ill, diseased


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eats healthy food. = 他吃健康的食物。
She maintains a healthy lifestyle. = 她保持健康的生活方式。

一般过去时:
The doctor said he was healthy. = 医生说他很健康。
She felt healthy after a good night's sleep. = 她在睡了一个好觉后感觉很健康。

现在完成时:
I have been feeling healthy lately. = 我最近一直感觉很健康。
She has maintained a healthy weight for years. = 她多年来一直保持健康的体重。

过去完成时:
He had been healthy until he caught the flu. = 他一直很健康，直到得了流感。
She had maintained a healthy diet before her illness. = 她生病前一直保持健康的饮食。

将来时:
I will be healthy if I continue to exercise. = 如果我继续锻炼，我会很健康。
They will feel healthy after their vacation. = 他们度假后会感觉很健康。

虚拟语气:
If I were healthier, I would run a marathon. = 如果我更健康，我会跑马拉松。
I wish I were healthier. = 我希望我更健康。

被动语态:
(Not typically used in the passive voice because it describes a state of being, not an action performed on something.)  健康这个状态一般不用被动语态。


疑问句:
Is he healthy? = 他健康吗？
Are you feeling healthy today? = 你今天感觉健康吗？

否定句:
He is not healthy. = 他不健康。
She doesn't eat healthy food. = 她不吃健康的食物。

PHRASE->
healthy eating = 健康饮食
healthy lifestyle = 健康的生活方式
healthy heart = 健康的心脏
healthy competition = 良性竞争
stay healthy = 保持健康
