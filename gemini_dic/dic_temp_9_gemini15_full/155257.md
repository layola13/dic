phalansterism:/fəˈlænstərɪzəm/| n. | 傅里叶主义；集体住宅制 | A social and economic theory advocating a system of communal living, typically in large, self-sufficient communities called phalansteries.  It emphasizes cooperation, shared resources, and the abolition of private property, aiming for a utopian society. |  Fourier's phalansterism was a radical departure from traditional social structures.  傅里叶的傅里叶主义是对传统社会结构的彻底背离。 | utopianism, socialism, communism | individualism, capitalism | 名词
