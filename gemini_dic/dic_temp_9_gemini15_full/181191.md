reinjure:/ˌriːˈɪndʒər/| vt.|再次受伤；复发；|To injure again, especially an injury that has already healed.|例句：He reinjured his knee during the game. 他在比赛中再次伤到了膝盖。|例句：The old back injury reinjured itself after he lifted the heavy box. 他搬动重箱子后，旧的背伤复发了。|近义词：re-injure, injure again|反义词：heal, cure|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reinjures his ankle frequently. = 他经常扭伤脚踝。
She reinjures her back lifting heavy objects. = 她搬重物时会再次伤到背。


一般过去时:
He reinjured his shoulder playing tennis. = 他打网球时再次伤到了肩膀。
She reinjured her knee last week. = 她上周再次伤到了膝盖。


现在完成时:
I have reinjured my wrist several times. = 我已经多次扭伤了手腕。
He has reinjured his hamstring during training. = 他在训练中再次拉伤了腿筋。


过去完成时:
He had reinjured his foot before the marathon. = 马拉松比赛之前，他已经再次伤到了脚。
She had reinjured her ankle a month before the accident. = 事故发生前一个月，她已经再次扭伤了脚踝。


将来时:
I fear I will reinjure my knee if I continue running. = 我担心如果继续跑步，我会再次伤到膝盖。
He thinks he will reinjure his back if he doesn't rest. = 他认为如果不好好休息，他会再次伤到背。


虚拟语气:
If I bent down like that, I would reinjure my back. = 如果我那样弯腰，我会再次伤到背。
If he had rested, he wouldn't have reinjured his knee. = 如果他休息了，就不会再次伤到膝盖了。


被动语态:
His ankle was reinjured in the fall. = 他的脚踝在跌倒时再次受伤了。

疑问句:

否定句:
