settees:/sɛˈtiːz/| n.|长沙发，多座位沙发|A settee is a long seat with a back and arms, typically for two or more people.  It's often upholstered and used in living rooms or other seating areas.|The living room has a comfortable settee by the fireplace. 客厅壁炉旁有一张舒适的长沙发。


The antique settee was a family heirloom.  那张古董长沙发是家族传家宝。


近义词：sofa, couch, loveseat|反义词：chair (单人座)

词源:
The word "settee" comes from the French word "settée," meaning "seated," ultimately deriving from the past participle of the verb "seoir," meaning "to sit."  The French word entered English in the 17th century.
