having:/ˈhævɪŋ/ | v. |拥有；进行；持有；从事|  The present participle of "have". It functions as a verb in progressive tenses and as a noun in gerunds.  It can also express possession, experience, or the cause or reason for something. |例句：I am having a great time. = 我玩得很开心。

例句：Having finished my work, I went home. = 完成了工作，我就回家了。


近义词:possessing, experiencing, undertaking |反义词:lacking, missing


词性:Verb (present participle) and Gerund (noun)


USAGE->
现在进行时:
I am having a good time. = 我玩得很开心。
She is having dinner with her friends. = 她正在和朋友们共进晚餐。


现在完成进行时:
I have been having trouble with my computer. = 我一直以来都在为我的电脑而烦恼。
They have been having arguments lately. = 最近他们一直在争吵。


过去进行时 (与其他动词连用):
I was having a shower when the phone rang. = 我正在洗澡时电话响了。
He was having lunch at that time. = 他那时正在吃午饭。


过去完成进行时:
I had been having problems with my car for weeks. = 我已经好几个星期都为我的车而烦恼了。
She had been having doubts about her decision. = 她一直对自己的决定有所怀疑。


将来进行时(与其他动词连用):
I will be having a meeting tomorrow. = 我明天将要开会。
They will be having a party next weekend. = 他们下周末将要举办派对。


现在分词作形容词:
a having a good time=玩得开心的人


现在分词作名词 (动名词):
Having a good time is important. = 玩得开心很重要。
The having of a good time is important. = 玩得开心很重要。
