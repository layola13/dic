quintuplet:/ˈkwɪntʌplɪt/| n. |五胞胎之一；五重奏；五倍数 | A quintuplet is one of five children born at the same birth.  It can also refer to a set of five things, or a musical composition in five parts. | Example: The Dionne quintuplets were famous for their birth and early life.  迪翁五胞胎以他们的出生和早年生活而闻名。  She played a beautiful quintuplet on the piano.她在钢琴上演奏了一首优美的五重奏。|近义词：multiple birth, quins |反义词：single birth
