cocaptaining:/ˌkoʊˈkæptənɪŋ/| v.|共同担任队长；共同队长职务|The act of two or more people sharing the captaincy of a team or organization.  It implies shared responsibility and leadership.|例句：The two most experienced players are cocaptaining the team this season.  本赛季，两位经验最丰富的球员共同担任队长。  例句：They decided on cocaptaining the project to leverage their complementary skills.他们决定共同担任项目负责人，以发挥各自的互补技能。|近义词：joint captaincy, shared leadership|反义词：sole captaincy, individual leadership|动词
