/ˌpriːˌdɛrɪˈveɪʃən/| n. |词源前推导；派生词的构成|The study of prederivation focuses on the processes and stages involved in forming complex words before they are fully derived.  It explores the underlying morphemes and their combinations that lead to the final derived word. For example, prederivation might analyze how "unbreakable" is formed by combining "un-", "break", and "-able".|例句：The linguistic analysis delved into the prederivation of the word, tracing its components back to their Proto-Indo-European roots. = 语言学分析深入研究了这个词的词源前推导，追溯其组成部分到原始印欧语的词根。|近义词：etymological analysis, morphemic analysis|反义词：N/A|名词
