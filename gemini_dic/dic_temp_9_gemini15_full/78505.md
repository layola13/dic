ghazel: /ˈɡeɪzəl/ | n. |  газель (俄语) |  A type of small, graceful antelope, typically with reddish-brown fur and distinctive markings.  It is known for its elegant appearance and swift movements.  | Example: The ghazel gracefully leaped across the rocky terrain.  那只瞪羚优雅地跃过崎岖的地形。 | 近义词: antelope, gazelle (gazelle is often used interchangeably) | 反义词: None specifically, but words implying large or clumsy animals could be considered antonyms. | 名词
