freeze-frames:/friːz freɪmz/| n.|冻结画面，定格画面|A still image from a video or film, capturing a single moment in time.|例句：The director loved the freeze-frame of the actor's surprised expression.  导演很喜欢演员惊讶表情的定格画面。
例句：The video game used freeze-frames to emphasize important plot points.  这款电子游戏使用冻结画面来强调重要的故事情节。|近义词：still, snapshot|反义词：moving image, continuous shot|名词
