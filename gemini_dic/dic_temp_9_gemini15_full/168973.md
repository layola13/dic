profiterole:/ˈprɒfɪtəroʊl/| n. |泡芙；奶油酥；小泡芙|A profiterole is a small, hollow, round pastry shell filled with cream or ice cream.  It is often served with chocolate sauce.|例句：The dessert menu featured a delightful array of profiteroles. 甜点菜单上有各种美味的泡芙。|例句：She decorated the profiteroles with fresh berries and whipped cream. 她用新鲜浆果和鲜奶油装饰泡芙。|近义词：cream puff, chou à la crème|反义词：无|名词
