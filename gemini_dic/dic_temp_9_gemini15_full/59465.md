ease up:/iːz ʌp/| phrasal verb|放松；缓和；减少；放慢|To become less intense or severe; to relax; to reduce one's efforts or speed.|例句：The rain started to ease up in the afternoon. 下午雨开始小了。|例句：Ease up on the pressure, you're working too hard. 放松点压力，你工作太努力了。|近义词：relax, slow down, lessen, moderate|反义词：intensify, accelerate, increase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eases up on the gas pedal. 他松开了油门。
The pain eases up gradually. 疼痛逐渐减轻。


一般过去时:
She eased up on the workload after the deadline. 截止日期后，她减少了工作量。
