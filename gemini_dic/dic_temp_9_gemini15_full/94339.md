impinge-on:/ɪmˈpɪndʒ ɒn/ | vt. | 侵犯；影响；撞击 | To have an effect or impact on something or someone.  | 例句：The noise from the construction site impinged on our ability to concentrate. 建筑工地的噪音影响了我们的注意力。

例句：His actions impinged on her rights. 他的行为侵犯了她的权利。


近义词：affect, impact, influence, encroach upon | 反义词：support, enhance, benefit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud music impinges on my ability to sleep. = 吵闹的音乐影响了我的睡眠。
His opinions impinge on the company's decisions. = 他的意见影响着公司的决策。


一般过去时:
The storm impinged on the coastal villages. = 暴风雨袭击了沿海村庄。
The economic crisis impinged on many families' lives. = 经济危机影响了许多家庭的生活。


现在完成时:
The constant noise has impinged on my sanity. = 持续的噪音已经影响了我的神智。
Her anxieties have impinged on her work performance. = 她的焦虑已经影响了她的工作表现。


过去完成时:
The previous government's policies had impinged on the country's economy. = 前政府的政策已经影响了国家的经济。
The delay had impinged on our travel plans. = 延误已经影响了我们的旅行计划。


将来时:
The new regulations will impinge on small businesses. = 新的法规将影响小型企业。
The rising sea levels will impinge on coastal communities. = 海平面上升将影响沿海社区。


虚拟语气:
If the project impinged on our budget, we would have to reconsider. = 如果这个项目影响了我们的预算，我们就必须重新考虑。
I wish the traffic wouldn't impinge on my commute. = 我希望交通不会影响我的通勤。


被动语态:
Their rights were impinged upon by the new law. = 他们的权利受到了新法律的侵犯。
Our plans were impinged upon by unforeseen circumstances. = 我们的计划受到了不可预见的情况的影响。


疑问句:
Does the new policy impinge on our freedom of speech? = 新政策是否影响了我们的言论自由？
How will the changes impinge on the employees? = 这些变化将如何影响员工？


否定句:
The changes did not impinge on our daily routines. = 这些变化没有影响我们的日常生活。
The decision did not impinge on the overall outcome. = 该决定没有影响最终结果。
