reflect:/rɪˈflɛkt/ | /rɪˈflekt/ | v., n. | 反射；反映；体现 | To throw back (light, heat, sound, etc.) from a surface; to show or be a sign of something.  |例句：The mirror reflected my image.  镜子反射了我的影像。

The lake reflected the blue sky. 湖面映照着蓝天。


近义词：show, indicate, mirror, display | 反义词：refract


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The still water reflects the trees. 静止的水面反射着树木。
His actions reflect his true character. 他的行为反映了他的真实性格。


一般过去时:
The polished surface reflected the light beautifully.  抛光后的表面漂亮地反射了光线。
Her speech reflected her deep concern for the environment. 她的演讲反映了她对环境的深切关注。


现在完成时:
The changes in society have reflected the shift in values. 社会的变化反映了价值观的转变。
His work has reflected his lifelong dedication to art. 他的作品体现了他毕生的艺术奉献。


过去完成时:
The past events had reflected the country's history. 过去的事件反映了国家的历史。
The old photos had reflected the family's happiness in those days. 老照片反映了那个年代家庭的幸福。


将来时:
The new policy will reflect the government's commitment to reform. 新政策将反映政府改革的决心。
The upcoming exhibition will reflect the artist's recent work.  即将举行的展览将反映艺术家最近的作品。


虚拟语气:
If the mirror were cleaner, it would reflect the image more clearly. 如果镜子更干净，它会更清晰地反射影像。
I wish my work reflected my true potential. 我希望我的作品能够反映我的真实潜力。


被动语态:
The image was reflected in the calm water. 影像在平静的水面上反射出来。
His views were reflected in his writings. 他的观点反映在他的著作中。


疑问句:
Does the surface reflect light well? 这个表面反光好吗？
Does this data reflect the real situation?  这些数据能反映真实情况吗？


否定句:
The glass didn't reflect the light properly. 玻璃没有正确地反射光线。
His words didn't reflect his true feelings.  他的话语没有反映他的真实感受。


PHRASE->
reflect on/upon: 仔细思考
reflect badly on:  对…有负面影响
reflect credit on: 给…带来荣誉
be reflected in: 体现在…中
give a true reflection of: 真实地反映
