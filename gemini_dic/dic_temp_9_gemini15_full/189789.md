saluters:/səˈluːtərz/| n. |敬礼者，致敬者 |People who salute, typically soldiers or officials performing a formal greeting or showing respect.  |Example: The saluters stood rigidly at attention.  敬礼者笔直地立正站着。|近义词：greeters, honor guards |反义词：insulters, disregarders |名词

一般现在时:
The soldiers salute their commanding officer every morning.  士兵们每天早晨向他们的指挥官敬礼。
Many people salute the flag during national ceremonies.  许多人在国家仪式上向国旗敬礼。


一般过去时:
The cadets saluted smartly as the general passed by.  当将军走过时，学员们敬了个标准的礼。
He saluted the officer before speaking. 他在说话之前向军官敬礼。
