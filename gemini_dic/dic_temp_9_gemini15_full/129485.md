non-Anglican:/ˌnɒnˈæŋɡlɪkən/| adj. |非英国国教的|Not belonging to or connected with the Anglican Church.  This adjective describes individuals, institutions, or practices that are not affiliated with the Church of England or its related churches within the Anglican Communion. |Example:  The non-Anglican churches in the city held a joint ecumenical service.  这座城市非英国国教的教堂举行了一次联合的宗教仪式。|近义词：Nonconformist, Dissenting|反义词：Anglican
