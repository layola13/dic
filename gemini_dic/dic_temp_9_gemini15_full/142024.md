organizes:/ˈɔːrɡənaɪzɪz/ | v. |组织；安排；筹备 |To arrange or put in order; to establish or create a system or structure.  |She organizes the office supplies.|近义词：arranges, structures, coordinates |反义词：disorganizes, disrupts


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She organizes her day efficiently. = 她有效地安排她的一天。
He organizes his thoughts before speaking. = 他在说话之前整理思绪。


一般过去时:
They organized a fundraising event last year. = 他们去年组织了一场筹款活动。
She organized her closet and got rid of unwanted items. = 她整理了衣橱，扔掉了不需要的东西。


现在完成时:
He has organized several conferences this year. = 他今年已经组织了几次会议。
She has organized her life after the difficult period. = 艰难时期之后，她已经把生活安排好了。


过去完成时:
They had organized everything before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经把一切都安排好了。
She had organized a surprise party for her friend's birthday. = 她已经为朋友的生日准备了一个惊喜派对。


将来时:
We will organize a trip to Europe next summer. = 我们明年夏天将组织一次欧洲之旅。
He will organize the meeting schedule for next week. = 他将安排下周的会议时间表。


虚拟语气:
If she organized her time better, she would be less stressed. = 如果她更好地安排时间，她就不会那么焦虑了。
I wish I had organized my notes before the exam. = 我希望我在考试前已经整理好笔记了。


被动语态:
The event was organized by a professional team. = 这场活动是由一个专业的团队组织的。
The meeting will be organized in the conference room. = 会议将在会议室举行。


疑问句:
Who organized the party? = 谁组织的派对？
How will the project be organized? = 项目将如何组织？


否定句:
He didn't organize the meeting properly. = 他没有妥善地组织会议。
She hasn't organized her thoughts yet. = 她还没有整理好思绪。
