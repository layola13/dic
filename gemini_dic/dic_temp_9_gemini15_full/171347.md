publicising: /ˈpʌblɪsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 宣传；公布；推广 | The act of making something publicly known, especially through advertising or other means.  It emphasizes the act of bringing something to public attention. | 例句：The company is publicising its new product through a series of television commercials. 公司正在通过一系列电视广告宣传其新产品。


例句：They are publicising the event through social media. 他们正在通过社交媒体宣传这个活动。


近义词：advertising, promoting, publicizing, marketing, announcing


反义词：suppressing, concealing, hiding, keeping secret


词性:动词

词源:
The word "publicising" comes from the verb "publicise," which is formed from the noun "public" and the suffix "-ise" (a common suffix used to form verbs meaning "to make something").  The ultimate root is the Latin word "publicus," meaning "public" or "of the people."
