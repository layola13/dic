consolate:/ˈkɒnsəleɪt/ /ˈkɑːnsəleɪt/| vt.|安慰，慰藉|to comfort someone who is feeling sad or disappointed|例句：I tried to consolate her after her dog died. 我试图在她狗死后安慰她。  例句：He consoled his friend with kind words. 他用友好的话语安慰了他的朋友。|近义词：comfort, soothe, solace|反义词：distress, upset


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He consoles his friend daily. = 他每天安慰他的朋友。
She consoles her child after a nightmare. = 她在她孩子做噩梦后安慰她。

一般过去时:
My mother consoled me when I failed the exam. = 我考试不及格时，妈妈安慰了我。
He consoled her with a warm hug. = 他用一个温暖的拥抱安慰了她。

现在完成时:
I have consoled many people during my years as a therapist. = 在我当治疗师的这些年里，我已经安慰过很多人了。
She has consoled him many times over the loss of his father. = 她多次安慰他失去父亲的悲伤。

过去完成时:
He had consoled her before she went on stage. = 在她上台之前，他已经安慰了她。
They had consoled each other after the accident. = 事故后，他们互相安慰。


将来时:
I will consolate her when she hears the news. = 当她听到这个消息时，我会安慰她。
We will consolate him after the funeral. = 葬礼后我们会安慰他。

虚拟语气:
If I could, I would consolate her, but I am too far away. = 如果我能，我会安慰她，但是我太远了。
I wish I could have consoled him sooner. = 我希望我能早点安慰他。

被动语态:
She was consoled by her friends. = 她被她的朋友们安慰了。
He was consoled by the thought of his family. = 他被对家人的思念所安慰。

疑问句:
How can I best consolate him? = 我该如何最好地安慰他？
Will anyone consolate her after the loss? = 失去后会有人安慰她吗？

否定句:
I couldn't consolate her; her grief was too profound. = 我无法安慰她；她的悲伤太深了。
He did not try to consolate her. = 他没有试图安慰她。


PHRASE->
consolate oneself = 自我安慰

offer consolation = 提供安慰
find consolation in sth = 从某事中得到安慰
give consolation = 给予安慰
