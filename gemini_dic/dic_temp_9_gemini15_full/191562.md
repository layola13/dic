scholasticizing:/ˌskɒləˈstaɪzɪŋ/| v.|学院化；使具有经院哲学的特征|The act of applying scholastic methods or principles;  making something conform to scholastic thought or style.  It often implies an overly rigid or pedantic approach to learning or scholarship.|例句：His interpretation of the text was overly scholasticizing, losing sight of the author's original intent.  他的文本解读过于学院化，忽略了作者的本意。

例句：The professor's lectures, while informative, tended towards scholasticizing, neglecting practical application. 教授的讲座虽然内容丰富，但却倾向于学院化，忽略了实际应用。


近义词：intellectualizing, academicizing, formalizing, pedanticizing|反义词：simplifying, popularizing, colloquializing|词性：动词
