mystics:/ˈmɪstɪks/| n.|神秘主义者，神秘家|People who seek direct experience of ultimate reality through contemplation, intuition, or mystical experience. They often believe in a reality beyond the physical world and seek to understand it through spiritual practices.|例句：Many mystics have dedicated their lives to seeking spiritual enlightenment. 许多神秘主义者都致力于寻求精神启蒙。|例句：The writings of the mystics offer profound insights into the nature of consciousness.  神秘主义者的著作提供了对意识本质的深刻见解。|近义词：ascetics, visionaries, seers|反义词：materialists, skeptics
