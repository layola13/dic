glorious:/ˈɡlɔːriəs/| adj. |光荣的；辉煌的；令人愉快的|

英文解析:  Describing something that is extremely good, impressive, or wonderful; filled with glory, honor, or splendor.  It can refer to things that inspire admiration, joy, or a sense of awe.

例句:

The sunset was glorious. = 日落景色壮丽。

We had a glorious time at the beach. = 我们在海滩上玩得很开心。


近义词: magnificent, splendid, superb, wonderful, amazing, excellent

反义词: awful, terrible, dreadful, miserable, shameful


词性: adjective
