preannouncement:/ˌpriːəˈnaʊnsmənt/| n. |预先发布；预告|An announcement made in advance of an official announcement.  It often provides preliminary information or hints about a future event, product, or decision.  The purpose is to generate interest or manage expectations.|例句：The company issued a preannouncement of its strong earnings, boosting investor confidence. 公司提前发布了强劲的盈利预告，增强了投资者的信心。|例句：The preannouncement of the new smartphone generated significant buzz on social media. 新智能手机的预告在社交媒体上引起了很大的轰动。|近义词：advance notice, preliminary announcement, foreshadowing|反义词：postponement, delay, silence|名词
