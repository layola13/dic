porches:/ˈpɔːrtʃɪz/ | n. |门廊；走廊；阳台 | A porch is a covered structure projecting in front of the entrance of a building.  It's often used as a sheltered area for sitting or relaxing. | 例句：The house has a wide porch where we can sit and enjoy the evening breeze. 这栋房子有一个宽敞的门廊，我们可以在那里坐着享受傍晚的微风。


The old Victorian house boasted a beautiful wraparound porch. 那栋古老的维多利亚式房屋拥有一个美丽的环绕式门廊。| 近义词：veranda, gallery, stoop | 反义词：interior, inside
