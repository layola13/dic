help-himself-to:/hɛlp hɪmˈsɛlf tuː/| v. |自取；随便吃（喝）|This phrase describes the action of someone taking something for themselves without asking permission, typically food or drink from a shared supply.  It implies a casual and informal setting.  |例句：He helped himself to another slice of cake. 他又给自己切了一块蛋糕。
He helped himself to a drink from the bar. 他从酒吧拿了一杯酒自饮。|近义词：serve oneself, take, appropriate|反义词：ask for permission, request


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He helps himself to coffee every morning. = 他每天早上都自己倒咖啡喝。
She helps herself to snacks from the kitchen. = 她自己从厨房拿零食吃。


一般过去时:
He helped himself to the last cookie. = 他把最后一块饼干拿走了。
She helped herself to a large helping of pasta. = 她给自己盛了一大盘意大利面。


现在完成时:
He has helped himself to several pieces of fruit. = 他已经吃了好几块水果。
She has helped herself to the wine without asking. = 她没问就自斟自饮了。


过去完成时:
He had helped himself to a second helping before I arrived. = 我到之前他已经添了一次饭了。
She had helped herself to the leftovers before I could get to them. = 我还没来得及吃剩菜，她就已经吃掉了。


将来时:
He will help himself to the buffet. = 他会去自助餐随便吃。
She will help herself to whatever she wants. = 她想吃什么就吃什么。


虚拟语气:
If he were here, he would help himself to some tea. = 如果他在的话，他会自己泡茶喝。
I wish I could help myself to a vacation.  = 我真希望自己能休假。


被动语态:
(This phrase is not typically used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Did he help himself to the cookies? = 他吃饼干了吗？
Will she help herself to more wine? = 她还会再喝点酒吗？


否定句:
He didn't help himself to any dessert. = 他没有吃甜点。
She didn't help herself to the snacks because they were for the guests. = 她没有吃零食，因为那些零食是为客人准备的。


PHRASE->

help oneself to something: 自取某物
help oneself to the buffet: 自助享用
help yourself: 请自便
help yourself to some food: 请随便吃点东西
feel free to help yourself: 请随意
