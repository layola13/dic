extrapolations:/ɪkˈstræpəˈleɪʃənz/| n. |推断；推论；外推法|The process of estimating, beyond the range of known values, the value of a variable on the basis of its relationship with another variable.  It involves extending a trend or pattern observed in a data set to make predictions about future values or values outside the observed range.  Extrapolations are often used in forecasting, prediction, and scientific modeling.|例句：The scientist used extrapolations from the initial data to predict future climate change. 科学家利用最初的数据推断来预测未来的气候变化。

例句：Her conclusions were based on dangerous extrapolations from a small sample size. 她的结论是基于从小样本量得出的危险推断。


近义词：projections, inferences, estimations, predictions|反义词：interpolations|名词
