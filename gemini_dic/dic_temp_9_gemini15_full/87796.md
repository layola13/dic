hibiscuses:/hɪˈbɪskəsɪz/| n. |木槿花（复数）|Hibiscuses are a genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, Malvaceae. They are known for their large, showy flowers in a variety of colors.  The plural form of "hibiscus".|The hibiscuses in her garden were vibrant and beautiful.  她花园里的木槿花色彩鲜艳，美丽动人。

The hibiscuses bloomed all summer long. 整个夏天木槿花都盛开着。|近义词：mallow flowers (泛指), tropical flowers|反义词：无
