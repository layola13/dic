reshifting:/ˌriːˈʃɪftɪŋ/| v.|重新调整，重新分配|The act of shifting something again; readjusting or redistributing something.|例句：The company is reshifting its marketing strategy to target a younger demographic. 公司正在重新调整其营销策略以瞄准更年轻的人群。  例句：The earthquake caused a reshifting of the landmass. 地震导致地块重新移动。|近义词：realigning, readjusting, repositioning|反义词：stabilizing, fixing
