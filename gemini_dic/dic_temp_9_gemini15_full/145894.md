overdid:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪd/| vt.|做得过分了，过分渲染|To do something to an excessive degree; to exaggerate or overdo something.  |例句：I overdid it at the gym and now my muscles are sore. 我在健身房做得过分了，现在我的肌肉酸痛了。|例句：He overdid the description of the accident, making it sound much worse than it actually was. 他对事故的描述过于夸张，听起来比实际情况糟糕得多。|近义词：exaggerated, overemphasized, overacted |反义词：underdid, understated |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overdoes his jokes. = 他把笑话说得太过火了。
She overdoes her makeup. = 她化了过浓的妆。

一般过去时:
I overdid it at the party last night. = 我昨晚在派对上玩得太过了。
They overdid the seasoning in the soup. = 他们把汤里的调料放太多了。

现在完成时:
I've overdone it with the exercise this week. = 我这周运动太多了。
She's overdone the decorations for the Christmas tree. = 她把圣诞树装饰得太过分了。

过去完成时:
He realized he had overdone the praise. = 他意识到他夸奖得太多了。
We discovered we had overdone the sugar in the cake. = 我们发现蛋糕里的糖放太多了。

将来时:
I think I'll overdo it if I eat another slice of cake. = 我想如果再吃一块蛋糕，我会吃撑的。
She's afraid she'll overdo it during the marathon. = 她担心马拉松比赛中会过度疲劳。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't overdone it, I wouldn't be so tired. = 如果我没有玩得太疯，我就不会这么累了。
If she hadn't overdone the makeup, she would have looked more natural. = 如果她没有化那么浓的妆，她看起来会更自然。

被动语态:
The colors in the painting were overdone. = 画中的颜色过于浓艳。
The performance was overdone and lacked subtlety. = 演出过于夸张，缺乏细腻之处。

疑问句:

否定句:
I didn't overdo the exercise; I felt great afterwards. = 我运动并没有过度；之后感觉很好。
They didn't overdo the presentation; it was just right. = 他们的演示并没有做得太过火；刚刚好。
