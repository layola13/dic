/ˈkæptər/| n. |俘获者；捕获者；劫持者|A person or thing that captures someone or something.  The captor held the prisoner captive. 俘获者将囚犯囚禁起来。|近义词：seizer, captor,  abductor |反义词：captive, prisoner


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The captor holds the prisoner. = 俘获者抓住囚犯。
The police captor the thief. = 警察逮捕了小偷。


一般过去时:
The captor held the prisoner for ransom. = 俘获者为了赎金扣押了囚犯。
The hunter captured a large deer. = 猎人捕获了一只大型鹿。


现在完成时:
The police have captured the suspect. = 警方已经逮捕了嫌疑犯。
They have captured the flag.  = 他们已经夺取了旗帜。


过去完成时:
The police had captured the criminal before he could escape. = 警方在他逃脱之前已经逮捕了罪犯。
The army had captured the city before the enemy reinforcements arrived. = 军队在敌军增援到达之前已经占领了这座城市。


将来时:
The army will capture the enemy base. = 军队将攻占敌方基地。
The hunters will capture more animals. = 猎人将会捕获更多动物。


虚拟语气:
If the captor had not been so cruel, the prisoner might have survived. = 如果俘获者不那么残酷，囚犯也许就能活下来。
If they had captured the leader, the rebellion might have ended sooner. = 如果他们抓住了领导人，叛乱可能早就结束了。


被动语态:
The prisoner was captured by the enemy. = 囚犯被敌人俘虏了。
The city was captured after a long siege. = 在经过长时间的围困后，这座城市被攻占了。


疑问句:
Who captured the king? = 谁俘虏了国王？
Who is the captor of the treasure? = 宝藏的俘获者是谁？


否定句:
The police did not capture the criminal. = 警方没有逮捕罪犯。
The hunters did not capture any animals today. = 猎人今天没有捕获任何动物。
