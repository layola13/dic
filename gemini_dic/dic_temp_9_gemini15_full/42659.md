counterstepped:/ˈkaʊntərˌstɛpt/| v.|反制，反击，巧妙地阻止|To take action to prevent or counteract something; to cleverly avoid or overcome an obstacle or opponent's move.|例句：The police counterstepped the protesters' attempts to block the street.  警方反制了抗议者封锁街道的企图。
例句：The boxer counterstepped his opponent's punch and landed a blow of his own. 拳击手巧妙地躲过了对手的拳击并还以一击。|近义词：counteract, thwart, circumvent, preempt|反义词：assist, aid, facilitate

词源:
The word "counterstepped" is a combination of "counter-" (meaning "against" or "opposite") and "stepped" (past tense of "step," referring to a move or action).  It's a relatively modern coinage, formed by analogy with other "counter-" verbs.
