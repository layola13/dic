prett:/prɛt/ | adj. |漂亮的，可爱的，雅致的 | Pretty describes something pleasing to the eye, often delicate and attractive.  It can be used to describe people, objects, or places. |例句：She's a pretty girl. 她是个漂亮的女孩。 The flowers are pretty. 这些花很漂亮。|近义词：beautiful, lovely, attractive, charming |反义词：ugly, unattractive, plain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flowers are pretty. = 这些花很漂亮。
The sunset is pretty tonight. = 今晚的日落很美。

一般过去时:
The dress was pretty. = 这件衣服很漂亮。
The cake looked pretty. = 蛋糕看起来很漂亮。

现在完成时:
The garden has become pretty. = 花园变得漂亮了。
She has always been pretty. = 她一直都很漂亮。

过去完成时:
The house had been pretty before the renovation. = 这房子装修前很漂亮。
The car had looked pretty new. = 这车看起来很新很漂亮。

将来时:
The garden will be pretty in spring. = 春天花园会很漂亮。
The painting will look pretty in the new frame. = 这幅画放在新的画框里会很漂亮。

虚拟语气:
If she were pretty, she would be a model. = 如果她漂亮的话，她就会当模特。
I wish my room were pretty. = 我希望我的房间漂亮些。

被动语态:
The room is made pretty with flowers. = 房间因为鲜花而变得漂亮。
The cake was decorated pretty. = 蛋糕装饰得很漂亮。

疑问句:
Is the dress pretty? = 这件衣服漂亮吗？
Isn't the scenery pretty? = 风景不漂亮吗？

否定句:
The building isn't pretty. = 这栋建筑不漂亮。
The weather isn't pretty today. = 今天天气不好看。
