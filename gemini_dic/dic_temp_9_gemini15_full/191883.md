scolded:/ˈskoʊldɪd/| v.|责骂，训斥|To scold someone means to speak angrily to them because they have done something wrong.|例句：My mother scolded me for breaking the vase.  我的妈妈因为我打破了花瓶而责骂我。|例句：The teacher scolded the student for being late. 老师因为学生迟到而训斥了他。|近义词：rebuke, reprimand, chide, berate|反义词：praise, commend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scolds his children often. = 他经常责骂他的孩子们。
She scolds the dog for barking. = 她责骂狗乱叫。

一般过去时:
My father scolded me for my poor grades. = 我父亲因为我的糟糕成绩而责骂我。
The principal scolded the students for their misbehavior. = 校长因为学生们的行为不端而责骂他们。

现在完成时:
She has scolded him several times this week. = 她这周已经责骂他好几次了。
He has scolded the class for their lack of attention. = 他因为班级缺乏注意力而责骂了全班同学。

过去完成时:
She had scolded him before he even explained. = 他甚至还没解释，她就责骂了他。
The teacher had scolded the students for their disruptive behavior. = 老师因为学生们扰乱课堂的行为而责骂了他们。


将来时:
I will scold him if he does it again. = 如果他再这样做，我会责骂他。
She will scold her son if he fails the exam. = 如果她儿子考试不及格，她会责骂他。


虚拟语气:
I would scold him if I were you. = 如果我是你，我会责骂他。
If she had known, she would have scolded them. = 如果她知道的话，她就会责骂他们。


被动语态:
He was scolded by his boss for his mistake. = 他因为他的错误而被老板责骂。
The children were scolded for making a mess. = 孩子们因为弄乱东西而被责骂。


疑问句:
Were you scolded by your teacher? = 你被老师责骂了吗？
Has he been scolded for his actions? = 他因为他的行为而被责骂了吗？


否定句:
I didn't scold him, I just advised him. = 我没有责骂他，我只是劝告了他。
She didn't scold the children; she simply explained the rules. = 她没有责骂孩子们；她只是解释了规则。


PHRASE->
scold somebody severely = 严厉地责骂某人
scold somebody roundly = 狠狠地责骂某人
scold somebody down = 训斥某人
a scolding lecture = 一顿严厉的训斥
get scolded = 被责骂
