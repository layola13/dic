overplus:/ˈoʊvərplʌs/| n.|剩余；过剩；盈余|An amount that is more than is needed; a surplus.|The overplus of the harvest allowed them to store food for the winter.  收获的剩余部分使他们能够储存过冬的食物。|surplus, excess, superfluity|deficit, shortage, deficiency|名词


USAGE->

There is an overplus of apples this year. = 今年苹果过剩。
The company has an overplus of funds. = 公司资金过剩。
