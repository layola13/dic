intersale:/ˌɪntərˈseɪl/| n. |中间销售；中间转售|The sale of goods between two businesses, not directly to the end consumer.|例句：The company specializes in intersale of electronic components to other manufacturers. 这家公司专门从事电子元件的中间销售给其他制造商。|近义词：resale, secondary sale|反义词：direct sale
