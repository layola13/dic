puranic:/pjʊˈrænɪk/| adj.| 属于普拉那的；源于普拉那的 | Relating to the Puranas, ancient Hindu scriptures.  The adjective describes something that is characteristic of or derived from the Puranas.  | Example:  The lecture focused on puranic mythology. 这场讲座的重点是普拉那神话。  |近义词：mythological (神话的), legendary (传奇的) | 反义词：modern (现代的), contemporary (当代的)
