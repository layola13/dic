rollerballs: /ˈroʊlərˌbɔːlz/ | n. |滚珠笔；滚珠式笔 | A rollerball pen is a type of pen that uses a small ball bearing to transfer ink to the paper.  It is typically less expensive and easier to use than a fountain pen. | Example: I prefer using rollerball pens for their smooth writing experience. 我更喜欢使用滚珠笔，因为它们书写流畅。  |近义词：ballpoint pen, pen |反义词：fountain pen (in terms of mechanism)
