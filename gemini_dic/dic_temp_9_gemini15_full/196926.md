shadowless: /ˈʃædəʊləs/ | adj. | 无影的，没有阴影的 |  Lacking a shadow; free from shadows.  The word describes something that does not cast a shadow, often due to intense light or a specific characteristic. |


例句：

The midday sun cast shadowless light upon the desert.  正午的阳光在沙漠上投下无影的光。

The perfectly smooth surface reflected light so completely it appeared shadowless.  完全光滑的表面完全反射光线，看起来没有阴影。


近义词：shadow-free, unshadowed


反义词：shadowy, shaded


词性：形容词
