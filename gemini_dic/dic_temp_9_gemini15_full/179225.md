redispersal:/ˌriːdɪˈspɜːrsəl/| n. |再分散；重新分散|The act or process of dispersing something again after it has been collected or concentrated.  It refers to the scattering or spreading out of something that was previously gathered or grouped together. |例句：The redispersal of the refugees was a complex and challenging undertaking.  难民的重新安置是一项复杂而具有挑战性的工作。

The redispersal of seeds after a flood helped to restore the forest.  洪水过后种子的重新散播有助于森林的恢复。

|近义词：re-distribution, scattering, dissemination |反义词：concentration, aggregation, collection |名词
