dragonet:/ˈdræɡənɛt/| n. |小型龙鱼；幼龙|A dragonet is a small, often brightly colored fish belonging to the Callionymidae family.  They are known for their elaborate courtship displays and often live in burrows in the sand.|例句：The vibrant colors of the dragonet mesmerized the diver.  这条龙鱼鲜艳的颜色让潜水员着迷。

The tiny dragonet darted amongst the coral.  这条小小的龙鱼在珊瑚丛中穿梭。|近义词：small fish, marine fish|反义词: large fish, whale
