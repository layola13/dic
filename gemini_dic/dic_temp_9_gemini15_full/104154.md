jungle:/ˈdʒʌŋɡəl/ | n. |丛林，密林 |A dense forest, typically tropical, characterized by tangled vegetation, usually with a humid climate.  |例句：The explorers hacked their way through the dense jungle. 探险家们在茂密的丛林中艰难地砍出一条路。|例句：Many rare animals live in the Amazon jungle. 亚马逊丛林里生活着许多珍稀动物。|近义词：forest, wilderness, thickets |反义词：desert, plain
