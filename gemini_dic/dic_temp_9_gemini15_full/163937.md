prediagnoses:/ˌpriːˈdaɪəɡnoʊsiːz/| n. |初步诊断，预先诊断 |Plural of prediagnosis.  It refers to diagnoses made before a definitive diagnosis is established, often based on incomplete information or preliminary tests. These diagnoses are tentative and subject to change. |Example: The prediagnoses suggested a possible infection, but further tests were needed for confirmation.  初步诊断提示可能感染，但需要进一步检查才能确认。|近义词： preliminary diagnoses, initial assessments |反义词： definitive diagnoses, confirmed diagnoses |名词
