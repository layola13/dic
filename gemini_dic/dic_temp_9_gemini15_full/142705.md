otherwhile:/ˈʌðərwaɪl/| adv.|有时，间或|At other times; occasionally; now and then.|例句：Otherwhile he'd be cheerful, otherwhile morose. 有时他会很高兴，有时又很忧郁。|近义词：sometimes, occasionally, at times, now and then|反义词：always, constantly, invariably|副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sometimes works late. = 他有时工作到很晚。
They occasionally meet for coffee. = 他们偶尔会一起喝咖啡。


一般过去时:
She sometimes stayed up late to study. = 她有时熬夜学习。
They occasionally went hiking in the mountains. = 他们偶尔会去山上远足。


现在完成时:
I have sometimes wondered about that. = 我有时会想过这件事。
They have occasionally helped us with the project. = 他们偶尔会帮助我们完成项目。


过去完成时:
He had sometimes felt lonely before he met her. = 在遇到她之前，他有时感到孤独。
They had occasionally visited the museum before moving away. = 在搬走之前，他们偶尔会去参观博物馆。


将来时:
She will sometimes work from home. = 她有时会在家里工作。
They will occasionally need assistance with the task. = 他们偶尔会需要帮助完成这项任务。


虚拟语气:
If I were rich, I would sometimes travel the world. = 如果我有钱，我就会有时周游世界。
I wish I could sometimes escape the city noise. = 我希望有时可以逃离城市的喧嚣。


被动语态:
The rule is sometimes broken. = 这条规则有时会被打破。
The meeting is occasionally postponed. = 会议有时会被推迟。


疑问句:
Do you sometimes feel tired? = 你有时会感到疲倦吗？
Do they occasionally have problems with the software? = 他们偶尔会遇到软件问题吗？


否定句:
He doesn't always work late; he sometimes goes home early. = 他并不总是工作到很晚；他有时会早回家。
They don't constantly need help; they occasionally manage on their own. = 他们并不总是需要帮助；他们偶尔也能自己应付。


PHRASE->
otherwhile...otherwhile... = 时而...时而...
once in a while = 偶尔
every so often = 每隔一段时间
from time to time = 不时
now and again = 时而
