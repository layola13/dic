overhasty:/ˈoʊvərˈheɪsti/ | adj. | 过急的；仓促的 | Too quick or hurried; done without sufficient care or thought. |例句：His overhasty decision led to unforeseen consequences. 他仓促的决定导致了无法预料的后果。

例句：The overhasty repairs to the roof resulted in leaks. 屋顶的仓促修理导致了漏水。

近义词：rash, hasty, precipitate, reckless, impetuous | 反义词：deliberate, careful, cautious, thoughtful

词源:
The word "overhasty" is a combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") and the adjective "hasty" (meaning "done or made too quickly").  "Hasty" itself ultimately derives from the Old English word "hās," meaning "quick" or "swift."
