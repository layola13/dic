demolishment:/dɪˈmɒlɪʃmənt/| n. |拆除；破坏；摧毁|The act or process of demolishing something, especially a building.  It often implies a complete destruction or dismantling.|例句：The demolition of the old factory made way for a new shopping mall.  旧工厂的拆除为新的购物中心腾出了空间。


例句：The building's demolition was completed ahead of schedule. 建筑物的拆除提前完成了。


近义词：destruction, razing, wrecking,teardown


反义词：construction, building, erection, creation
