fervid:/ˈfɜːrvɪd/ | adj. | 热情的；热烈的；狂热的 | Characterized by intense emotion, passion, or enthusiasm.  It often implies an almost excessive or uncontrolled level of feeling. | 例句：He delivered a fervid speech about the importance of environmental protection. 他发表了一篇关于环境保护重要性的热情洋溢的演讲。


例句：Her fervid love for her children was evident in everything she did.  她对孩子的热烈爱意在她所做的一切中都显而易见。


近义词：passionate, ardent, zealous, intense, fervent


反义词：apathetic, indifferent, lukewarm, unenthusiastic


词性：形容词
