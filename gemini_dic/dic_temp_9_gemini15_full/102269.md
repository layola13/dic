itinerary:/aɪˈtɪnəreri/ | n. | 旅行计划；行程安排 | A detailed plan or route of a journey.  It includes the places to be visited, the times of arrival and departure, and other relevant information. | Example:  We need to finalize our itinerary before we leave for our trip. 我们需要在出发旅行之前最终确定我们的行程安排。


|近义词: schedule, plan, route, program |反义词: none |词性: noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The itinerary includes a visit to the Eiffel Tower. = 行程包括参观埃菲尔铁塔。
Our itinerary specifies the meeting times. = 我们的行程表规定了会议时间。


一般过去时:
The itinerary detailed the stops on the bus tour. = 行程详细说明了巴士之旅的停靠站。
We followed the itinerary precisely. = 我们精确地按照行程表进行。


现在完成时:
We have finalized the itinerary for the conference. = 我们已经完成了会议的行程安排。
They have adjusted the itinerary due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，他们已经调整了行程。


过去完成时:
We had already reviewed the itinerary before the meeting. = 会议前我们已经仔细查看了行程。
She had changed the itinerary several times before settling on a final version. = 在最终确定版本之前，她已经多次更改了行程。


将来时:
We will distribute the itinerary to all participants next week. = 我们下周将把行程分发给所有参与者。
The itinerary will be subject to change depending on weather conditions. = 行程可能会根据天气状况而改变。


虚拟语气:
If we had a better itinerary, the trip would have been smoother. = 如果我们有一个更好的行程，这次旅行就会更顺利。
I wish the itinerary had included more free time. = 我希望行程中包含更多自由时间。


被动语态:

疑问句:
