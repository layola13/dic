obliquely:/əˈbliːkli/ | adv. | 斜地；间接地；不坦率地 | In a slanted or indirect way; not straightforwardly.  |例句：He obliquely referred to the scandal, without mentioning it directly. 他间接地提到了丑闻，没有直接提及。|例句：She obliquely hinted at her dissatisfaction with the job. 她含糊地暗示了她对这份工作的 不满。|近义词：indirectly, slantingly, slantwise, at an angle |反义词：directly, straightforwardly, frankly
