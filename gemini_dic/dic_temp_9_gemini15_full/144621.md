outwing:/ˈaʊtwɪŋ/| v.|超越，胜过|To surpass or exceed in performance or ability, especially in a competition or contest.|例句：Our team outwinged the opponents in the final race.  我们的团队在决赛中超越了对手。|例句：The new model outwings its predecessor in terms of speed and efficiency.  新款车型在速度和效率方面都超越了其前代产品。|近义词：outshine, outstrip, surpass, exceed|反义词：underperform, lose to


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outwings his rivals in every competition. = 他在每次比赛中都胜过对手。
The new software outwings the old one in functionality. = 新软件在功能方面胜过旧软件。


一般过去时:
She outwinged her opponent in the chess tournament. = 她在国际象棋锦标赛中击败了对手。
The experienced pilot outwinged the newcomer in the air race. = 经验丰富的飞行员在航空比赛中胜过了新手。


现在完成时:
Our company has outwinged its competitors in the market share. = 我们公司已经超过了竞争对手的市场份额。
The athlete has outwinged all expectations with her performance. = 运动员的表现超出了所有人的预期。


过去完成时:
By the end of the game, the team had outwinged its opponent by ten points. = 到比赛结束时，该队已经领先对手十分。
The inventor had outwinged all previous attempts to solve the problem. = 发明家已经超越了所有之前解决这个问题的尝试。


将来时:
We expect the new technology to outwing the current systems. = 我们预计新技术将超越目前的系统。
They believe their strategy will outwing their rivals' approach. = 他们相信他们的策略将胜过对手的方法。


虚拟语气:
If only he had trained harder, he might have outwinged his opponent. = 如果他当时训练更刻苦，他也许就能战胜对手了。
If the company had invested more, it could have outwinged the competition. = 如果公司投资更多，它就能胜过竞争对手了。


被动语态:
The team was outwinged by a stronger opponent. = 该队被更强大的对手击败了。
The old technology has been outwinged by newer innovations. = 旧技术已被更新的技术所超越。


疑问句:
Will our project outwing the others in terms of innovation? = 我们的项目在创新方面能否胜过其他项目？
Did the new product outwing the existing models in sales? = 新产品在销量方面是否超过了现有型号？


否定句:
The underdog team did not outwing the favorites. = 弱势队伍没有战胜热门队伍。
Our marketing campaign did not outwing the competitor's efforts. = 我们的营销活动没有超过竞争对手的努力。
