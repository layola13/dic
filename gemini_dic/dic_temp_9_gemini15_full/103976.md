jugful: /ˈdʒʌɡfʊl/ | n. | 一壶(的量) | A jugful is the amount that a jug can hold.  It refers to a full jug of something, often a liquid. | Example:  She drank a jugful of lemonade. 她喝了一壶柠檬水。  He poured a jugful of water onto the thirsty plants. 他往口渴的植物上倒了一壶水。|近义词: a jug of, a containerful |反义词: a drop, a little
