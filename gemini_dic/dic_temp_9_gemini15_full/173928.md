quasi-sarcastic: /ˈkwɑːzaɪsɑːrˈkæstɪk/ | adj. |似讽刺的，略带讽刺意味的 |  Appearing to be sarcastic but not fully or explicitly so; subtly or mildly sarcastic. It implies a hint of sarcasm without the full force or overt negativity.  | Example:  His "helpful" suggestions were quasi-sarcastic at best; they seemed more designed to hinder than assist.  他的“有益的”建议充其量只是似讽刺的；它们似乎更旨在阻碍而不是帮助。 | Near synonyms:  dryly humorous, subtly mocking, ironically understated | Antonyms:  genuine, sincere, earnest |
