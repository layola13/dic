death-wish:/ˈdɛθ wɪʃ/| n.| 寻死心理，求死欲望 |A strong desire to die; a subconscious or conscious wish to die.  It often manifests as reckless behavior or self-destructive actions.|例句：He seemed to have a death-wish, constantly engaging in dangerous activities. 他似乎有寻死的心理，不断从事危险活动。

例句：Her impulsive actions suggested a deep-seated death-wish. 她的冲动行为暗示了她内心深处有强烈的求死欲望。|近义词：suicidal tendencies, self-destructive behavior, desire for death|反义词：will to live, survival instinct|词性:名词
