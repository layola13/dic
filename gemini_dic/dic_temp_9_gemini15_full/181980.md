rematches:/ˈriːmætʃɪz/| n.| 重赛；再次比赛 | Rematches are often requested after a close or controversial contest. 重赛通常是在一场势均力敌或有争议的比赛后被要求的。|近义词：replay, second match|反义词：final match

一般现在时:
A rematch is scheduled for next week. = 重赛安排在下周。
The team requests a rematch after their controversial loss. = 他们在有争议的失利后要求重赛。

一般过去时:
They had a rematch last night. = 他们昨晚进行了一场重赛。
The boxers agreed to a rematch after the controversial decision. = 拳击手在有争议的判罚后同意进行重赛。

现在完成时:
There have been several rematches between these two fighters. = 这两位拳手之间已经进行了几场重赛。
The athletes have requested a rematch, citing an unfair advantage to their opponent. = 运动员们要求重赛，理由是他们的对手有不公平的优势。

过去完成时:
They had already agreed to a rematch before the official announcement. = 在官方宣布之前，他们就已经同意进行重赛了。
The coach had considered a rematch even before the final whistle blew. = 即使在终场哨响之前，教练就已经考虑过重赛了。

将来时:

We will see a rematch between these two teams next season. = 下个赛季我们将看到这两支球队之间的重赛。

虚拟语气:
If they lost, they would demand a rematch. = 如果他们输了，他们会要求重赛。
I wish there had been a rematch after that controversial game. = 我希望在那场有争议的比赛之后有一场重赛。
