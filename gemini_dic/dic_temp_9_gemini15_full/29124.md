case:/keɪs/| n. v.|情况；案例；箱子；案件|

英文解析:
As a noun, "case" can refer to a particular instance or example of something, a situation or circumstance, a container, or a legal proceeding.  As a verb, it means to cover or enclose something.

例句:
This is a serious case of negligence. = 这是一个严重的疏忽案例。

The case is made of wood. = 这个箱子是用木头做的。

The lawyer presented her case to the jury. = 律师向陪审团陈述了她的案情。

He carefully cased the building before the robbery.  = 他在抢劫前仔细勘察了大楼。


近义词:instance, example, situation, circumstance, container, lawsuit, affair

反义词:  (Depending on the context)  solution, resolution, non-case


词性: noun, verb
