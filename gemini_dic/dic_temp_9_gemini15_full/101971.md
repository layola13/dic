isobilateral:/ˌaɪsoʊbaɪˈlætərəl/| adj.|等边的；等角的；对称的 |Having two sides that are equal.  The term often refers to structures or shapes exhibiting symmetry, particularly in biology and geometry.  |例句：The butterfly's wings displayed an almost perfect isobilateral pattern. 这只蝴蝶的翅膀展现出几乎完美的等边图案。

例句：The isobilateral leaf structure is a key characteristic of this plant species.  这种植物物种的关键特征是等边的叶结构。


近义词：symmetrical, equivalent, equal-sided


反义词：asymmetrical, unequal, dissimilar


词源:  The word is derived from the Greek prefix "iso-" meaning "equal," "bilateral" meaning "having two sides," and the suffix "-al" indicating an adjective.  Therefore, "isobilateral" literally means "having two equal sides."
