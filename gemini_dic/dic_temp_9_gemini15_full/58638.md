dumbfound:/dʌmˈfaʊnd/ | vt. | 使惊呆；使目瞪口呆 | To make someone speechless with surprise or astonishment.  |例句：The unexpected news dumbfounded her.  =  这意外的消息让她惊呆了。 |例句：He was dumbfounded by the sheer scale of the project. =  项目的巨大规模让他目瞪口呆。|近义词：astonish, amaze, astound, bewilder |反义词：enlighten, clarify


USAGE->

The news dumbfounds me. = 这消息让我惊呆了。
