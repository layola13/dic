flameproofers:/ˈfleɪmpruːfərz/| n.|防火人员;阻燃剂|People or things that make something flameproof.  Flameproofers are crucial in industries where fire hazards are prevalent, ensuring safety by preventing or slowing the spread of flames. |例句：The factory hired specialist flameproofers to treat all the fabric before production. 工厂雇佣了专业的防火人员来处理所有织物，然后再进行生产。

The company developed a new type of flameproofers for use in high-risk environments. 这家公司开发了一种新型阻燃剂，用于高风险环境。|近义词：fire retardants, fire-resistant materials|反义词：flammable materials, accelerants|名词
