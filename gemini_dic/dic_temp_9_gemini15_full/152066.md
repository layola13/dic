paterero:/ˌpætəˈriːroʊ/| n. |小型火炮；轻型火枪|A small cannon, typically used on ships or in fortifications.  It was often smaller and lighter than a culverin or other larger cannons.  The term also sometimes referred to a type of light firearm or musket.|Example: The pirates used patereros to bombard the fort from their small boats. 海盗们用小型火炮从小船上轰击要塞。|近义词: swivel gun, petard,  mortar (in some contexts)|反义词:  heavy artillery, large cannon|名词
