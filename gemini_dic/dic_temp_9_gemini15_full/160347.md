pootle:/ˈpuːtəl/| v.|闲逛；慢慢地走；懒洋洋地走|To travel or move in a leisurely or aimless way.  The word suggests a relaxed and unhurried journey.|例句：We spent the afternoon pootling around the countryside. 我们在乡间悠闲地度过了下午。

例句：The boat pootled gently down the river. 小船缓缓地顺流而下。


近义词：dawdle, meander, amble, stroll, putter


反义词：hurry, rush, dash, speed


词性:动词

They pootle along the coast every Sunday. = 他们每个星期天都在沿海悠闲地散步。
We pootle around the park in the evenings. = 我们晚上在公园里闲逛。

被动语态:  (Pootle is rarely used in the passive voice because it implies a deliberate, leisurely action.)
