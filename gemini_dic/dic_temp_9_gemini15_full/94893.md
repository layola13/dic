in fact:/ɪn fækt/ | adv. |事实上，实际上 |In fact, it's quite simple. 事实上，这很简单。|近义词：actually, really, indeed |反义词：in theory, hypothetically


USAGE->
一般现在时:
In fact, the sun rises in the east. 事实上，太阳从东方升起。
In fact, she works very hard. 事实上，她工作非常努力。


一般过去时:
In fact, he went to the library yesterday. 事实上，他昨天去了图书馆。
In fact, they finished the project early. 事实上，他们提前完成了项目。


现在完成时:
In fact, I have already completed the task. 事实上，我已经完成了任务。
In fact, she has been studying English for five years. 事实上，她已经学习英语五年了。


过去完成时:
In fact, he had already left before I arrived. 事实上，在我到达之前他已经离开了。
In fact, they had finished eating when I called. 事实上，我打电话的时候他们已经吃完了。


将来时:
In fact, we will be going to the beach tomorrow. 事实上，我们明天要去海滩。
In fact, it will be raining later today. 事实上，今天晚些时候会下雨。


虚拟语气:
In fact, I would have gone if I had known. 事实上，如果我知道的话，我就去了。
In fact, it would be better if you apologized. 事实上，如果你道歉会更好。


被动语态:
In fact, the meeting was postponed. 事实上，会议被推迟了。
In fact, the problem is being addressed. 事实上，这个问题正在得到解决。


疑问句:
In fact, is that true? 事实上，那是真的吗？
In fact, is this the right way? 事实上，这是正确的方法吗？


否定句:
In fact, that's not true. 事实上，那不是真的。
In fact, he didn't do it. 事实上，他没做。


PHRASE->
as a matter of fact = 事实上
