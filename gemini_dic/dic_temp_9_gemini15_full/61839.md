emplore:/ɪmˈplɔːr/| vt.|恳求；哀求；乞求|to beg someone earnestly or desperately to do something|例句：I implore you to reconsider your decision. 我恳求你重新考虑你的决定。
例句：She implored him to help her. 她恳求他帮助她。|近义词：beseech, entreat, supplicate|反义词：command, order


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He implores his boss for a raise. 他恳求老板加薪。
They implore the judge for mercy. 他们恳求法官开恩。

一般过去时:
She implored her father to forgive her. 她恳求父亲原谅她。
He implored the gods for help. 他恳求神明帮助。

现在完成时:
I have implored him repeatedly, but he refuses to listen. 我已经反复恳求他，但他拒绝听。
They have implored the government for assistance.  他们已经多次恳求政府援助。

过去完成时:
She had implored him many times before he finally agreed. 在他最终同意之前，她已经多次恳求他。
He had implored his friend for help, but to no avail. 他曾恳求朋友帮忙，但没有结果。

将来时:
I will implore him to change his mind. 我将恳求他改变主意。
They will implore the committee to reconsider. 他们将恳求委员会重新考虑。

虚拟语气:
I would implore you to think carefully before acting. 我会恳求你在行动前仔细考虑。
If I were you, I would implore him to reconsider. 如果我是你，我会恳求他重新考虑。

被动语态:
He was implored to help the needy. 他被恳求去帮助穷人。
The government was implored to take action. 政府被恳求采取行动。

疑问句:
Did he implore you to help? 他恳求你帮忙了吗？
Will they implore the authorities for assistance? 他们会恳求当局援助吗？

否定句:
He did not implore her to stay. 他没有恳求她留下。
They did not implore the police for protection. 他们没有恳求警察保护。


PHRASE->
implore someone to do something: 恳求某人做某事
implore for mercy: 恳求宽恕
implore for help: 恳求帮助
implore forgiveness: 恳求原谅
implore someone's understanding: 恳求某人的理解

词源:
From Old French *implorer (unattested), from Latin implorāre "to call upon," from in- "into" + plōrāre "to weep, cry out."  The sense development is from "to cry out to" to "to beg earnestly."
