capitula: /kəˈpɪtʃuːlə/ | n. | 小头状花序；头状花序 |  Small flower heads, characteristic of the Asteraceae (daisy) family.  They are composed of many individual florets clustered together on a common receptacle.  | Example: The sunflower's large, showy head is actually a composite of many tiny capitula. 向日葵硕大的花序实际上是由许多细小的头状花序组成的。 | 近义词: flower head, inflorescence | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with solitary flowers) 单生花 | 词性: 名词
