malty:/ˈmɔːlti/| adj.|麦芽的；麦芽味的；|Having the flavor or aroma of malt.  The term often refers to the taste of barley malt, but can also describe similar flavors from other grains. |例句：The beer had a rich, malty flavor. 这啤酒有着浓郁的麦芽味。 The bread had a slightly malty aroma.  这面包有一股淡淡的麦芽香。|近义词：brewy, sweet, nutty|反义词:bitter, sharp
