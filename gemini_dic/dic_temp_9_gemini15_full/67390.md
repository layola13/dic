exterminates:/ɪkˈstɜːrmɪneɪts/| vt.|消灭；根除；灭绝|To kill all the members of a group of people or animals.|The government launched a campaign to exterminate rats in the city.  政府发起了一场消灭城市老鼠的运动。|近义词：annihilate, eradicate, eliminate, destroy|反义词：preserve, conserve, protect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government exterminates pests annually. = 政府每年都消灭害虫。
He exterminates cockroaches in his apartment regularly. = 他定期消灭他公寓里的蟑螂。


一般过去时:
The farmer exterminated the weeds in his field. = 农民除掉了田里的杂草。
They exterminated the entire colony of ants. = 他们消灭了整个蚂蚁群。


现在完成时:
The pest control company has exterminated all the termites. = 害虫防治公司已经消灭了所有的白蚁。
She has exterminated all the insects in her garden. = 她已经消灭了她花园里所有的昆虫。


过去完成时:
The authorities had exterminated the disease-carrying insects before the outbreak worsened. = 在疫情恶化之前，当局已经消灭了携带疾病的昆虫。
They had exterminated the rats before they could spread disease. = 他们在老鼠传播疾病之前就消灭了它们。


将来时:
The council will exterminate the stray dogs in the park next week. = 理事会下周将消灭公园里的流浪狗。
We will exterminate the weeds before planting the new crops. = 我们将在种植新作物之前清除杂草。


虚拟语气:
If we had exterminated the pests earlier, we would have avoided this problem. = 如果我们早点消灭害虫，我们就不会遇到这个问题了。
I wish they had exterminated the rats before they infested the house. = 我真希望他们在老鼠侵害房子之前就把它们消灭了。


被动语态:
The pests were exterminated by the new insecticide. = 害虫被新型杀虫剂消灭了。
The rats were exterminated using poison. = 老鼠是用毒药消灭的。


疑问句:
Did they exterminate all the snakes? = 他们消灭了所有的蛇吗？
Will the company exterminate the ants in my house? = 公司会消灭我家里的蚂蚁吗？


否定句:
The farmer did not exterminate the weeds completely. = 农民没有完全清除杂草。
We cannot exterminate all the insects in the world. = 我们不可能消灭世界上所有的昆虫。
