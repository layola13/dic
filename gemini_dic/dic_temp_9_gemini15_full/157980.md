planked:/plæŋkt/| vt. |用木板固定；把…平放在木板上；(烹调)用木板烘烤|To place or secure something with planks.  In cooking, to cook (food) on a plank of wood, often in an oven or on a grill.|例句：They planked the boards across the stream. 他们把木板横放在小溪上。

The salmon was planked and grilled to perfection. 三文鱼用木板烤制，烤得恰到好处。|近义词：secure, fasten, grill|反义词：unfasten, remove
