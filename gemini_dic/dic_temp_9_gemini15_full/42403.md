counterclaimant:/ˌkaʊntəˈkleɪm(ə)nt/| n. |反诉人，反驳方|A person who makes a counterclaim in a lawsuit.  A counterclaim is a claim made by a defendant against a plaintiff in a lawsuit.  |Example: The counterclaimant argued that the plaintiff had breached their contract. 反诉人辩称原告违反了合同。|近义词：defendant (in the context of a counterclaim), respondent|反义词：plaintiff (in the context of the original claim)
