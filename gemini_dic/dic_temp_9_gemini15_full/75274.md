front-end:/ˈfrʌntˌɛnd/ | adj. n. |前端的；前端；前台 |  Referring to the part of a system, especially a computer system, that is closest to the user.  It typically involves the user interface and the client-side of applications. |Example: The front-end of the website is visually appealing and easy to navigate.  网站的前端视觉上很吸引人，而且易于浏览。  The developers focused on improving the front-end performance. 开发人员专注于改进前端性能。|近义词：user interface, client-side,  forefront |反义词：back-end
