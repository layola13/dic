hydrophoby:/haɪˈdrɒfəbi/| n. |恐水症，狂犬病|Fear of water; rabies, a viral disease transmitted through the saliva of infected animals, often causing hydrophobia as a symptom.|例句：The dog bite caused hydrophobia in the victim.  狗咬伤导致受害者患上恐水症。|例句：Rabies, also known as hydrophobia, is a fatal disease if left untreated. 狂犬病，也称为恐水症，如果不治疗是致命疾病。|近义词：rabies, fear of water, aquaphobia|反义词：None

词源:
hydro-  (from Greek hydor) meaning "water"
-phobia (from Greek phobos) meaning "fear"

因此，hydrophobia字面意思是“对水的恐惧”。然而，它更常指狂犬病，因为狂犬病的一个主要症状是对水的恐惧。
