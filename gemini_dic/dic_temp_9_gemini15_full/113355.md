what:/wɒt/ /wʌt/| pron.|什么，什么东西，多么|The word "what" is an interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something. It can refer to things, actions, qualities, or situations.  It can also be used as an exclamation.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？


近义词：which (用于选择),  how (用于方式), why (用于原因)|反义词：nothing, none


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？


一般过去时:
What did you see? = 你看到了什么？
What happened at the party? = 聚会上发生了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you done today? = 你今天做了什么？
What has she accomplished? = 她取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you eaten before the race? = 比赛前你吃了什么？
What had they already discussed? = 他们之前已经讨论了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like? = 天气将会怎样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to expect. = 我真希望知道会发生什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built there? = 那里正在建造什么？


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
He didn't say what he wanted. = 他没有说他想要什么。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What's going on? = 发生了什么事？
What if...? = 如果...会怎么样？
What for? = 为什么？为了什么？
What about...? = ...怎么样？
