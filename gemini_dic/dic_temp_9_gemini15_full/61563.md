emendator: /ɪˈmɛndeɪtər/ | n. | 校订者；修正者 | A person who makes corrections or improvements to a text or other work.  | The emendator carefully reviewed the manuscript, correcting numerous errors in spelling and grammar.  校订者仔细检查了手稿，纠正了许多拼写和语法错误。 | editor, reviser, corrector |  (无明显的反义词，可考虑  "someone who introduces errors") | 名词
