pick-apart:/pɪk əˈpɑːrt/| vt.|仔细检查；挑剔；拆卸；分析；批判|To examine something very carefully and critically, often in order to find faults.  It can also mean to disassemble something.|例句：The critics picked the movie apart, finding fault with every aspect.  评论家们对这部电影进行了仔细的批判，挑出了各个方面的毛病。

例句：She carefully picked the clock apart to see how it worked. 她仔细地拆开了钟表，看看它是如何工作的。


近义词：analyze, dissect, criticize, scrutinize, deconstruct

反义词：assemble, construct, praise, overlook


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They pick the argument apart every day. = 他们每天都仔细推敲这个论点。
He picks apart his work to find errors. = 他仔细检查自己的作品以找出错误。


一般过去时:
The detective picked the crime scene apart. = 侦探仔细检查了犯罪现场。
She picked apart his alibi, finding inconsistencies. = 她仔细分析了他的不在场证明，发现了前后矛盾之处。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:
