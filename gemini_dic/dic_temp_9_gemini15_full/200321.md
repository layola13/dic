skullcap:/ˈskʌlkæp/| n.|头巾；帽状植物|A skullcap is a close-fitting cap, often made of soft material, that covers the head. It can also refer to a type of plant with a helmet-shaped flower.  |例句：She wore a skullcap to keep her head warm. 她戴着头巾保暖。 |例句：The skullcap plant has unique helmet-shaped flowers.  帽状植物有独特的头盔状花朵。|近义词：cap, bonnet, head covering|反义词：None specifically.
