polydactylism:/ˌpɒliˈdæktɪlɪzəm/ | n. |多指（趾）症|The presence of more than the usual number of fingers or toes on hands or feet.  This condition can be inherited or caused by genetic mutations. It varies in severity, from a small extra digit to several extra digits. |Example: The child was born with polydactylism, having an extra finger on each hand.  这个孩子出生时患有多指（趾）症，每只手上都多了一根手指。|近义词：hyperdactyly |反义词：oligodactyly
