penances:/ˈpɛnənsɪz/| n. |苦行；忏悔；赎罪|Acts of self-punishment or religious devotion performed to show sorrow for sin or wrongdoing.  Penances are often undertaken as a way to atone for past actions and seek forgiveness.  They can involve various practices, including fasting, prayer, and charitable work.|例句：He performed various penances to atone for his sins. 他做了各种苦行来赎罪。

例句：The priest assigned her penances to cleanse her soul.  牧师指定她进行苦行以净化灵魂。


近义词：atonement, expiation, penance, self-mortification |反义词：reward, indulgence


词性:名词
