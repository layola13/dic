maran:/ˈmɑːræn/| n.|马兰（一种植物）|Maran is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.  They are commonly known as marigolds, although the term "marigold" also refers to plants in the genus Tagetes.|Example:  The maran flowers bloomed brightly in the garden.  马兰花在花园里盛开。|近义词: marigold (depending on the specific species)|反义词: N/A
