cordoned:/kɔːˈdɔːnd/| v.|包围，设警戒线|To surround (a place) with a cordon, especially to prevent access.|The police cordoned off the area after the explosion.  警察在爆炸后封锁了该区域。|近义词：encircle, surround, isolate, block off|反义词：open, unblock


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The police cordon off the crime scene every day. = 警察每天都封锁犯罪现场。
They cordon the streets during the parade. = 游行期间他们封锁街道。


一般过去时:
The area was cordoned off after the accident. = 事故发生后，该区域被封锁了。
Police cordoned off the building during the investigation. = 调查期间，警方封锁了该建筑物。


现在完成时:
The police have cordoned off the street several times this week. = 本周警方已经几次封锁了这条街。
They have cordoned off the area since the bomb threat. = 自从收到炸弹威胁以来，他们就封锁了该区域。


过去完成时:
The street had been cordoned off before the protesters arrived. = 抗议者到达之前，街道已被封锁。
The police had cordoned off the area hours before the event. = 警方在活动开始前几小时就封锁了该区域。


将来时:
The authorities will cordon off the stadium before the game. = 比赛前，当局将封锁体育场。
They will cordon the streets for the royal visit. = 皇家访问期间，他们将封锁街道。


虚拟语气:
If they had cordoned off the area sooner, fewer people would have been injured. = 如果他们早点封锁该区域，受伤的人就会更少。
It would have been safer if the police had cordoned off the street. = 如果警方封锁街道，就会更安全。


被动语态:
The area was cordoned off by the police. = 该区域被警方封锁了。
The streets are being cordoned off for the marathon. = 街道正在为马拉松比赛而被封锁。


疑问句:
Was the building cordoned off? = 建筑物被封锁了吗？
Have they cordoned off the park yet? = 他们已经封锁公园了吗？


否定句:
The police did not cordon off the entire street. = 警方没有封锁整条街。
They haven't cordoned off the area yet. = 他们还没有封锁该区域。


PHRASE->
cordon off:  封锁
police cordon: 警戒线
security cordon: 安全警戒线
military cordon:  军事警戒线
cordon sanitaire:  卫生警戒线 (通常指为了防止疾病传播而设立的警戒线)
