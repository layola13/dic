minimise:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪz/| vt.|最小化；使减到最少|To reduce something to the smallest possible amount or degree.|We need to minimise the risk of accidents.|近义词：reduce, lessen, decrease|反义词：maximise, increase, enlarge|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They minimise costs to maximise profits. = 他们尽量降低成本以最大化利润。
We minimise our environmental impact. = 我们尽量减少对环境的影响。

一般过去时:
The company minimised its losses during the recession. = 公司在经济衰退期间将损失降到了最低。
She minimised the importance of the problem. = 她淡化了这个问题的重要性。


现在完成时:
We have minimised our carbon footprint significantly. = 我们已经大大减少了碳足迹。
They have minimised the risk of infection. = 他们已经将感染的风险降到了最低。


过去完成时:
He had minimised his involvement in the project. = 他已经尽量减少了自己在项目中的参与。
They had minimised the damage before the emergency services arrived. = 在紧急服务部门到达之前，他们已经尽量减少了损失。


将来时:
We will minimise disruption during the renovations. = 我们将在装修期间尽量减少干扰。
The government will minimise the impact of the new regulations. = 政府将尽量减少新规定的影响。


虚拟语气:
If we minimised our spending, we could save more money. = 如果我们减少开支，就能节省更多钱。
I wish I had minimised the damage. = 我希望我当时能把损失降到最低。


被动语态:
The risks were minimised by careful planning. = 通过仔细的计划，风险被降到了最低。
The impact of the storm was minimised by the emergency services. = 紧急服务部门将风暴的影响降到了最低。


疑问句:
How can we minimise the negative effects? = 我们如何才能将负面影响降到最低？
Can we minimise the cost of the project? = 我们能否降低项目的成本？


否定句:
They did not minimise the seriousness of the situation. = 他们没有淡化局势的严重性。
We cannot minimise the importance of this issue. = 我们不能低估这个问题的重要性。


PHRASE->
minimise risk = 最小化风险
minimise damage = 最小化损失
minimise effort = 尽量减少努力
minimise impact = 尽量减少影响
minimise interference = 尽量减少干扰

词源:
Minimise comes from the Latin word "minimum," meaning "the smallest," combined with the suffix "-ise," which indicates "to make."  Therefore, it literally means "to make the smallest."
