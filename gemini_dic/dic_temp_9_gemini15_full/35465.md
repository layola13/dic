coached:/koʊtʃt/| v. |指导，训练，辅导 |To give instruction or training to; to guide or direct the efforts of.  |例句：The coach coached the team for the upcoming game. 教练指导球队备战即将到来的比赛。 |例句：She coached me through the difficult process. 她指导我完成了这个艰难的过程。|近义词：train, tutor, mentor, guide |反义词：hinder, obstruct


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He coaches the basketball team. = 他执教篮球队。
She coaches her students in public speaking. = 她辅导她的学生进行公开演讲。

一般过去时:
The experienced teacher coached the students on their projects. = 经验丰富的老师指导学生们完成他们的项目。
He coached the young athlete for years. = 他多年来一直指导这位年轻的运动员。

现在完成时:
She has coached numerous athletes to victory. = 她已经指导许多运动员取得了胜利。
He has coached this team for five seasons. = 他执教这支球队已经有五个赛季了。

过去完成时:
The coach had coached the team for many years before he retired.  = 教练在他退休前已经执教这支球队很多年了。
They had coached the children for hours before the performance. = 演出前他们已经指导孩子们几个小时了。

将来时:
He will coach the national team next year. = 他明年将执教国家队。
She will coach the debate team in the upcoming competition. = 她将在即将到来的比赛中指导辩论队。


虚拟语气:
If I had been coached properly, I would have won the race. = 如果我得到正确的指导，我就会赢得比赛。
I wish I had been coached earlier in my career. = 我希望在我的职业生涯早期就能得到指导。

被动语态:
The team was coached by a renowned expert. = 这支球队由一位著名的专家执教。
The students were coached on how to write effective essays. = 学生们接受了如何撰写有效论文的指导。

疑问句:
Who coached the winning team? = 谁执教了获胜的球队？
Have you ever been coached on a specific skill? = 你曾经在某种特定技能上接受过指导吗？

否定句:
He didn't coach the team this year. = 他今年没有执教这支球队。
She wasn't coached properly for the interview. = 她没有得到面试的适当指导。
