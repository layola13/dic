promenaders:/ˌprɒmənˈeɪdəz/| n. |散步者，蹓跶的人|People who are strolling or walking leisurely, especially in a public place.  The word evokes a sense of leisurely and perhaps even stylish walking.|例句：The park was filled with promenaders enjoying the beautiful spring weather. 公园里挤满了享受春日美景的散步者。

The elegant promenaders strolled along the beach at sunset.  优雅的散步者在日落时分沿着海滩漫步。|近义词：strollers, walkers, wanderers|反义词：runners, joggers
