recreated:/ˌriːˈkriːeɪtɪd/ | v. | 再创造；重新创造；重新制作 | To create or make something again; to reproduce something.  | The museum recreated the ancient Roman city using detailed historical research.  博物馆利用详尽的历史研究重新创造了古罗马城市。 They recreated the scene from the movie using CGI. 他们使用电脑特效重现了电影中的场景。 | reproduced, remade, reconstructed | destroyed, demolished


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They recreate historical events for educational purposes. = 他们为了教育目的而重现历史事件。
The artist recreates nature's beauty in her paintings. = 艺术家在她的画作中再现了大自然的美丽。


一般过去时:
The team recreated the experiment to verify the results. = 团队重做了实验以验证结果。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
