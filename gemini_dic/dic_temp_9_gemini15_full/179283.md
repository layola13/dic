rediverted:/ˌriːˈdaɪvɜːtɪd/| v. |重新导向，转移，改道 |To divert something again; to change the course or direction of something a second time.  |例句：The flight was rediverted to a nearby airport due to bad weather.  航班因恶劣天气而改道至附近机场。

例句：The river was rediverted to irrigate the parched fields. 河流被重新改道以灌溉干旱的田地。


近义词：rerouted, redirected, diverted anew, switched


反义词：maintained the original course, continued on the same path


词性:动词
