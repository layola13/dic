nonchalky: /ˌnɑːnˈtʃɔːki/ | adj. | 不粉状的，不白垩的 | Not having the texture or appearance of chalk.  The term describes a substance that lacks the characteristic powdery, white, and often slightly gritty feel of chalk. It can refer to both texture and appearance. | 例句：The soil was nonchalky and rich in organic matter. 这土壤不粉状，且富含有机物。

The paint had a nonchalky finish, giving it a smooth, even surface.  这油漆具有不粉状的表面，使其表面光滑平整。| 近义词：smooth, powdery (in the sense of lacking chalkiness), velvety | 反义词：chalky, powdery (in the sense of resembling chalk)
