egocentrism: /ˈiːɡoʊsɛntrɪzəm/ | n. |自我中心主义，以自我为中心 |The tendency to consider only one's own perspective and to be unable to understand or appreciate the perspectives of others.  It's often associated with a lack of empathy and a preoccupation with one's own needs and desires. |例句：His egocentrism made it difficult for him to understand the impact of his actions on others.  他的自我中心主义使他难以理解他的行为对别人的影响。

例句：The child's egocentrism prevented her from understanding that her actions might upset her friend.  孩子的自我中心主义使她无法理解她的行为可能会让她的朋友不高兴。

近义词：self-centeredness, narcissism, selfishness, self-absorption |反义词：altruism, empathy, consideration, selflessness
