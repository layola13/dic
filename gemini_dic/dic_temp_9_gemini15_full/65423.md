euphorias:/juːˈfɔːriəz/| n. |狂喜，欣快感 |A feeling or state of intense happiness and well-being.|例句：The euphorias of winning the lottery were short-lived. 彩票中奖的狂喜是短暂的。  
例句：She experienced a wave of euphorias after hearing the good news. 听到好消息后，她感到一阵欣喜若狂。|近义词：ecstasy, elation, exhilaration |反义词：depression, despair, misery |名词
