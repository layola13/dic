highlight:/ˈhaɪlaɪt/ | pron.| 突出显示；强调 | to attract attention to or emphasize something | The report highlighted the need for improved safety measures. 这份报告突出了改进安全措施的必要性。 | emphasize, underscore, stress | downplay, ignore | pron.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He highlights the key points in his presentation. 他在他的演示文稿中突出重点。
She highlights the importance of education. 她强调教育的重要性。


一般过去时:
The speaker highlighted the challenges facing the company. 演讲者强调了公司面临的挑战。
They highlighted the risks associated with the project. 他们强调了与该项目相关的风险。


现在完成时:
She has highlighted the need for more funding. 她已经强调了需要更多资金。
The article has highlighted the growing concerns about climate change. 这篇文章突出了人们对气候变化日益增长的担忧。


过去完成时:
He had highlighted the problem in his previous report. 他在之前的报告中已经强调了这个问题。
They had highlighted the potential dangers before we started. 在我们开始之前，他们已经强调了潜在的危险。


将来时:
She will highlight the benefits of the new policy. 她将重点介绍这项新政策的好处。
They will highlight the key findings of the study. 他们将重点介绍这项研究的主要发现。


虚拟语气:
If I were to highlight anything, it would be the importance of teamwork. 如果我要强调任何事情，那就是团队合作的重要性。
I wish I had highlighted the risks more clearly. 我希望我更清楚地强调了风险。


被动语态:
The problem was highlighted in the meeting. 会议上强调了这个问题。
The benefits of exercise are highlighted in many health publications. 许多健康出版物都强调了运动的好处。


疑问句:
What did the article highlight? 这篇文章强调了什么？
What points should I highlight in my presentation? 我应该在我的演示文稿中强调哪些要点？


否定句:
The report did not highlight the negative aspects. 报告没有突出负面方面。
He didn't highlight the importance of the deadline. 他没有强调截止日期的重要性。


PHRASE->
highlight reel: 精彩片段
highlight of the day/week/year: 今日/本周/今年的亮点
highlight the importance of: 强调...的重要性
bring something to highlight: 突出某事
a highlight of: ...的亮点
