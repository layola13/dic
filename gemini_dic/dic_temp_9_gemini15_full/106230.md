knout:/naʊt/| n. v.| 鞭打；皮鞭 |A knout is a whip, typically made of leather thongs, used for flogging.  The word can also refer to the act of flogging with a knout.  Historically, it was a common instrument of punishment in some parts of the world.|例句：The prisoner was punished with a knout. 犯人被鞭打惩罚。|The cruel overseer knouted the slaves. 残酷的监工鞭打了奴隶。|近义词：whip, lash, scourge |反义词：None specifically, but words like "reward" or "praise" suggest the opposite action.
