prehiring: /ˌpriːˈhaɪərɪŋ/ | n. | 预先招聘，提前招聘 | The process of identifying and engaging potential candidates before a formal job opening is announced.  It involves building relationships with potential employees, often through networking and talent pipelines,  to have a pool of qualified candidates ready when a position becomes available.  | Example:  Prehiring allows companies to fill critical roles more quickly and efficiently.  预先招聘使公司能够更快、更有效地填补关键职位。


近义词: proactive recruiting, talent pipelining, early talent acquisition

反义词: reactive recruiting
