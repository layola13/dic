monoceros:/mɒnəˈsɛrɒs/| n. |独角兽|A mythical creature resembling a horse with a single, long horn projecting from its forehead.  It is often depicted as a symbol of purity and grace. |Example: Unicorns are often featured in children's books and fantasy literature. 独角兽经常出现在儿童读物和奇幻文学中。|近义词：mythical creature, fantastical beast|反义词：N/A
