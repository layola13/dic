fauns:/fɔːnz/| n.|森林之神，小妖精|Mythological creatures, typically depicted as having the legs of a goat and the upper body of a man, often associated with woodland and nature.  They are often playful and mischievous. |例句：The fauns danced merrily in the forest glade. 森林里的空地上，森林之神快乐地跳舞。|例句：Ancient myths tell stories of fauns and nymphs inhabiting the woods. 古代神话讲述了居住在森林里的森林之神和仙女的故事。|近义词：satyr, wood sprite|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with more sinister creatures like goblins or ogres.|名词
