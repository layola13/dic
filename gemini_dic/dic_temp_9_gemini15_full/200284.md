skivvying:/ˈskɪviɪŋ/| v.|偷懒；逃避工作；磨洋工|  To avoid work or responsibilities by pretending to be busy or by doing unimportant tasks.  This is often done in a sneaky or underhanded way.|例句：He was caught skivvying off instead of doing his assigned tasks. 他被抓到偷懒而不是做他的任务。

例句：The employees were skivvying around the office, avoiding their actual work. 员工们在办公室里磨洋工，逃避实际工作。


近义词：shirking, malingering, goldbricking, slacking, loafing|反义词：working diligently, toiling, striving
