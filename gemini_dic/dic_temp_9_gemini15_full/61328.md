embassy:/ˈembəsi/| n.|大使馆；使馆|A diplomatic mission in a foreign country, headed by an ambassador.  It represents the interests of its home country.|例句：The American embassy is located in the city center. 美国大使馆位于市中心。

例句：She worked at the British embassy in Paris.她在巴黎的英国大使馆工作。


近义词：diplomatic mission, legation|反义词：无
