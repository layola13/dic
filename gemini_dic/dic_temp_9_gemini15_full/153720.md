pepper:/ˈpɛpər/ | n. |胡椒粉，辣椒；活力，刺激 |A pungent spice obtained from the dried berries of various plants, especially Capsicum species, used to season food.  Also refers to the plant itself and its berries.  Can also be used figuratively to describe something lively or exciting. |例句：I added a pinch of pepper to the soup. 我在汤里加了一撮胡椒粉。

例句：His speech was full of pepper. 他的演讲充满活力。


近义词：spice, chili, paprika | 反义词：bland, tasteless

词性: 名词
