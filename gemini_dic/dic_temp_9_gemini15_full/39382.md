conjunctively:/kənˈdʒʌŋktɪvli/| adv.| 并列地；连接地 | In a conjunctive manner; jointly; together. 例句：The study demonstrated that the two factors acted conjunctively to produce the observed effect.  这项研究表明，这两个因素共同作用产生了观察到的效果。
