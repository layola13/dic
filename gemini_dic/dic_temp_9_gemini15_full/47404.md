deadlatch: /ˈdedlætʃ/ | n. | 门闩；门栓 | A deadlatch is a type of latch that does not have a spring mechanism to automatically return to the locked position.  It requires manual operation to both lock and unlock.  It's often used in conjunction with a separate locking mechanism, such as a key or a knob. | The door had a simple deadlatch, easily opened from the outside.  这扇门只有一个简单的门闩，很容易从外面打开。 The deadlatch was stuck, preventing us from leaving. 门闩卡住了，阻止我们离开。 | bolt, lock | unlocked, open | 名词
