nondecision:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈsɪʒən/| n. |未做决定；未作决定；不做决定|The act or instance of not making a decision; failure to decide.  It often implies a conscious choice to avoid making a decision, rather than simply being unable to decide.|例句：The nondecision to invest in new technology ultimately hurt the company.  未决定投资新技术最终损害了公司。

例句：Her nondecision on the matter left everyone frustrated. 她对这件事的不做决定让每个人都很沮丧。|近义词：inaction, indecision, inaction, postponement|反义词：decision, resolution, determination|词性:名词
