fly-bys:/ˈflaɪˌbaɪz/| n.|飞越；近距离飞掠|A flyby is a quick flight past something, often a spacecraft past a planet or moon.  It usually involves a short period of observation and data collection before the spacecraft continues its journey.|例句：The spacecraft performed a successful flyby of Jupiter, capturing stunning images.  宇宙飞船成功飞越木星，拍摄到了令人惊叹的图像。

例句：NASA is planning several flybys of Mars in the coming years. 美国宇航局计划在未来几年进行几次火星飞越。|近义词：flyover, passage, transit |反义词：landing, orbiting, detailed study |名词
