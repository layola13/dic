iring:/ˈaɪərɪŋ/| v.|熨烫，烫平|To iron something means to press it with a hot iron to remove wrinkles.  |例句：I need to iron my shirt before the meeting. 我需要在开会前熨烫我的衬衫。|近义词：press, smooth|反义词：wrinkle, crease|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She irons her clothes every Sunday. = 她每个星期天都熨烫衣服。
He irons his shirts before work. = 他上班前熨烫衬衫。


一般过去时:
I ironed my dress last night. = 我昨晚熨烫了我的裙子。
She ironed all the linens before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她熨烫了所有的床单。


现在完成时:
I have ironed all the shirts. = 我已经把所有的衬衫都熨烫好了。
She has ironed the tablecloth for the party. = 她已经为派对熨烫了桌布。


过去完成时:
I had ironed the clothes before he came home. = 他回家之前我已经熨烫好衣服了。
She had ironed everything before the party started. = 派对开始之前，她已经把一切都熨烫好了。


将来时:
I will iron the clothes tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上会熨烫衣服。
She will iron her blouse before the interview. = 面试前她会熨烫她的衬衫。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would iron my suit. = 如果我有时间，我会熨烫我的西装。
I wish I had ironed my shirt; now it's wrinkled. = 我真希望我熨烫了我的衬衫；现在它皱巴巴的。


被动语态:
The clothes were ironed by my mother. = 衣服是我妈妈熨烫的。
The shirts are being ironed now. = 衬衫正在被熨烫。


疑问句:
Have you ironed the sheets yet? = 你熨烫床单了吗？
Did she iron the dress for the wedding? = 她为婚礼熨烫了那件裙子吗？


否定句:
I didn't iron my trousers this morning. = 我今天早上没有熨烫我的裤子。
He hasn't ironed his shirt yet. = 他还没熨烫他的衬衫。
