dirt:/dɜːrt/| n.|泥土，污垢，脏东西|Earth or soil, especially when it is dirty or dusty.  It can also refer to something that is morally wrong or unpleasant.|例句：The children were covered in dirt after playing in the garden.孩子们在花园里玩耍后浑身都是泥土。

例句：He told me a lot of dirt about his former boss. 他告诉我很多关于他前任老板的丑闻。|近义词：soil, grime, filth, mud, scandal|反义词：cleanliness, purity
