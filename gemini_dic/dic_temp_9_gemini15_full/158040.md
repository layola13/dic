plantsman:/ˈplæntsˌmæn/| n. |植物学家；精于栽培植物的人|A plantsman is someone with expert knowledge and skill in the cultivation of plants, often focusing on a specific area like a particular type of plant or a gardening style. They possess a deep understanding of plant biology, horticultural techniques, and plant identification.  A plantsman might work in a botanical garden, nursery, or as a private gardener. |He is a renowned plantsman specializing in rare orchids. 他是一位专门研究稀有兰花的著名植物学家。|botanist, horticulturist| (反义词较难直接对应，可考虑 unskilled gardener, amateur gardener等)|名词
