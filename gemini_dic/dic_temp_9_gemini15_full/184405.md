responsibility: /ˌrɪˌspɒnsəˈbɪləti/ /rɪˌspɑːnsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 责任；职责；义务 | The state or fact of being accountable or to blame for something.  It implies a duty to deal with something. |

例句：

He accepted responsibility for the accident. 他承担了事故的责任。

It's her responsibility to ensure the project is completed on time. 确保项目按时完成是她的责任。


近义词：duty, obligation, accountability, charge

反义词：irresponsibility, neglect


词源:

The word "responsibility" comes from the Old French word "responsabilité," which itself is derived from the Latin word "responsabilitas."  "Responsabilitas" is formed from "responsare" (to answer, respond) + "-bilitas" (a suffix indicating capacity or condition).  Therefore, the root meaning points to the capacity or condition of being answerable or accountable.
