powder-puffs:/ˈpaʊdər pʌfs/| n.|粉扑|A soft, fluffy pad, typically made of cotton or synthetic materials, used to apply powder to the face or body.  Powder puffs are designed to be soft and absorbent, helping to evenly distribute powder for a smooth and even finish.|例句：She used a powder puff to apply loose powder to her face. 她用粉扑在脸上扑散粉。


She gently dabbed the powder puff on her cheeks. 她轻轻地用粉扑拍打她的脸颊。|近义词：powder puff, cosmetic puff|反义词:None.
