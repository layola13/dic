rancheria:/rænˈtʃɛriə/| n. |印第安人小村庄，小农场|A rancheria is a small village or settlement, especially one inhabited by Native Americans, often characterized by its small size and relative self-governance.  It is distinct from larger reservations.  The term is particularly associated with California's indigenous populations.|The local rancheria held an annual powwow to celebrate their heritage.|近义词：village, settlement, hamlet|反义词：metropolis, city
