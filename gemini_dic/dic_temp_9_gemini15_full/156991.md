piller:/ˈpɪlər/| n.|柱子，支柱；栋梁|A pillar is a strong upright support, typically a column of stone, wood, or metal, used in architecture or as a symbol of strength and support.  Pillars can also refer to a person or thing that is of primary importance. |例句：The ancient temple was supported by massive stone pillars. 这座古老的寺庙由巨大的石柱支撑着。The pillars of the community are its schools and churches. 社区的支柱是它的学校和教堂。|近义词：column, post, support, mainstay|反义词：weakness, impediment
