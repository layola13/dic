hydropsy:/ˈhaɪdrɒpsi/| n. |水肿，积水|A condition characterized by an abnormal accumulation of fluid in the body tissues, often resulting in swelling.  It can affect various parts of the body and is often a symptom of an underlying medical condition.|例句：The patient presented with hydropsy in his legs, indicating potential heart failure.  患者腿部出现水肿，这表明可能有心脏衰竭。|近义词：edema, dropsy|反义词：dehydration
