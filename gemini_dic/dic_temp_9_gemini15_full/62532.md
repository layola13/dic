endureth:/ɪnˈdʊrz/| v. |忍耐；持续；经受| To continue to exist for a long time; to remain in a particular state or condition; to suffer something painful or difficult with patience.|例句：His courage endured through many hardships.  他的勇气在许多磨难中都坚持了下来。|例句：The empire endured for centuries. 这个帝国持续了几个世纪。|近义词：persist, last, withstand, tolerate|反义词：cease, end, perish
