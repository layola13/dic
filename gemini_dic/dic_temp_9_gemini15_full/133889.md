nongrievousnesses:/ˌnɒnˈɡriːvəsnəsiz/ | n. |非严重性，非痛苦之事 | The absence of serious problems or distress; minor inconveniences.  This word is extremely rare and not commonly used in everyday language. It highlights the lack of significant grievances. |例句：Despite some minor nongrievousnesses, the project was ultimately successful. = 尽管有一些不太严重的问题，但项目最终还是成功了。  The meeting, while not without its minor nongrievousnesses, concluded amicably. = 会议虽然有一些小问题，但还是友好地结束了。|近义词：trifles, trivialities, minor inconveniences |反义词：grievances, hardships, serious problems |名词
