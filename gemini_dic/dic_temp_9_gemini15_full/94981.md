in-the-dumps:/ɪn ðə dʌmps/| adj.|情绪低落，垂头丧气| feeling unhappy and depressed|She's been in the dumps ever since her boyfriend left her.自从她男朋友离开后，她一直闷闷不乐。|近义词：depressed, downcast, dejected|反义词：happy, cheerful, elated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is in the dumps today. = 他今天情绪低落。
They are always in the dumps. = 他们总是情绪低落。

一般过去时:
She was in the dumps after the exam. = 考试后她情绪低落。
We were in the dumps because of the bad news. = 因为坏消息我们情绪低落。

现在完成时:
He has been in the dumps for weeks. = 他已经闷闷不乐好几个星期了。
She has been in the dumps since losing her job. = 自从失业以来，她一直情绪低落。

过去完成时:
I had been in the dumps all day before I received the good news. = 在收到好消息之前，我整天都情绪低落。
They had been in the dumps for months before they finally sought help. = 在他们最终寻求帮助之前，他们已经情绪低落了好几个月。


将来时:
I think she will be in the dumps if she fails the test. = 我认为如果她考试不及格，她会情绪低落。
He will probably be in the dumps after the breakup. = 分手后他可能会情绪低落。

虚拟语气:
If I were in the dumps, I would talk to a friend. = 如果我情绪低落，我会和朋友聊聊。
I wish I weren't in the dumps all the time. = 我希望我不总是情绪低落。

被动语态: (This phrase is not typically used in the passive voice)

否定句:
