corresponds:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɒndz/ | /ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndz/ | v. | 相符；一致；相当于；通信 | To be similar or analogous; to be in agreement or conformity; to communicate by exchanging letters. |例句：His account of the events corresponds with hers.  他的事件描述和她的一致。|例句：I correspond regularly with my pen pal in France. 我定期与我在法国的笔友通信。|近义词：agree, match, equate, conform | 反义词：differ, disagree, conflict


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His description corresponds to the facts. = 他的描述与事实相符。
The two maps correspond closely. = 这两张地图非常吻合。

一般过去时:
The witness's statement corresponded with the evidence. = 目击证人的证词与证据相符。
Our plans corresponded perfectly. = 我们的计划完美地吻合。

现在完成时:
The results have corresponded well with our predictions. = 结果与我们的预测非常吻合。
She has corresponded with her aunt for many years. = 她和她姑妈通信多年了。


过去完成时:
The early accounts corresponded with what we found later. = 早期的记录与我们后来发现的相符。
They had corresponded for months before they finally met. = 他们在最终见面之前已经通信了好几个月。


将来时:
The two datasets will correspond closely, I expect. = 我预计这两个数据集将非常吻合。
I will correspond with the university about my application. = 我将就我的申请与大学进行联系。


虚拟语气:
If the results corresponded to our expectations, we would be very pleased. = 如果结果符合我们的预期，我们会非常高兴。
If only his actions corresponded to his words! = 但愿他的行动与他的话语一致！


被动语态:
The information was found to correspond accurately with the report. = 发现该信息与报告准确相符。
His ideas are often corresponded with by his colleagues. = 他的想法经常得到同事们的认同。


疑问句:
Does his statement correspond with the facts? = 他的陈述与事实相符吗？
Do your findings correspond to the previous research? = 你的发现与之前的研究相符吗？


否定句:
The two versions of the story do not correspond. = 这两个故事版本不相符。
Her account of the incident did not correspond to the police report. = 她对事件的描述与警方报告不符。


PHRASE->
