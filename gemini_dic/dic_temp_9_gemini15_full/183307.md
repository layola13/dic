reproachably: /rɪˈproʊtʃəbli/ | adj. | 应受责备地；无可非议地 |  Deserving of reproach; blameworthy.  |  His actions were reproachably selfish. 他的行为令人谴责地自私。  The company's response to the crisis was reproachably slow. 公司对危机的回应迟缓得令人无法接受。 | blameworthy, culpable, reprehensible | commendable, praiseworthy | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The manager behaves reproachably towards his staff. = 经理对员工的行为应受谴责。
His attitude is reproachably arrogant. = 他的态度令人难以置信地傲慢。


一般过去时:
He acted reproachably in that situation. = 他在那样的情况下行为不当。
The company's response was reproachably inadequate. = 公司的回应令人遗憾地不足。


现在完成时:
They have acted reproachably on many occasions. = 他们多次行为不当。
The government has been reproachably slow to react. = 政府的反应迟缓得令人谴责。


过去完成时:
He had acted reproachably before the incident. = 在事件发生之前，他的行为就已不当。
The company had been reproachably negligent in its safety procedures. = 公司在安全程序方面一直疏忽大意，令人谴责。


将来时:
He will likely act reproachably again. = 他很可能会再次行为不当。
The project will likely be completed reproachably late. = 项目很可能完工严重延误。


虚拟语气:
If he had acted differently, he wouldn't have been reproachably negligent. = 如果他采取不同的行动，他就不会如此令人谴责地失职。
If the company had responded sooner, their actions wouldn't have been reproachably slow. = 如果公司早点回应，他们的行为就不会如此迟缓得令人谴责。


被动语态:
His behavior was considered reproachably selfish. = 他的行为被认为是令人谴责的自私的。
The company's handling of the situation was deemed reproachably inadequate. = 公司处理情况的方式被认为是令人遗憾地不足的。


疑问句:
Was his behavior reproachably careless? = 他的行为是否粗心大意到应受责备？
Is their response to the criticism reproachably dismissive? = 他们对批评的回应是否不屑一顾到应受责备？


否定句:
His actions were not reproachably selfish, but rather misguided. = 他的行为并非令人谴责的自私，而是被误导了。
Her performance, while not perfect, was certainly not reproachably inadequate. = 她的表现虽然不完美，但肯定不至于差到令人谴责。
