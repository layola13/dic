quasi-guaranteed:/ˈkwɑːzaɪˌɡærənˈtiːd/ | adj. | 几乎保证的，近乎肯定的 |  Almost guaranteed; highly probable but not absolutely certain. It implies a strong likelihood but acknowledges a small possibility of failure or exception. |例句：The project is quasi-guaranteed to succeed, given the extensive market research and funding.  这个项目几乎肯定会成功，鉴于广泛的市场调研和资金支持。


例句：While the weather forecast predicts sunshine, it's only quasi-guaranteed; there's still a chance of rain. 虽然天气预报预测阳光明媚，但这只是几乎肯定的；仍然有下雨的可能性。


近义词：highly probable, very likely, almost certain, practically guaranteed


反义词：unlikely, improbable, uncertain, doubtful


词性: adjective
