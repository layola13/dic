estrangedness:/ɪˈstreɪndʒnəs/| n. |疏远；隔阂；不和 |The feeling of being distant or alienated from someone or something; a lack of closeness or affection.  It often refers to a relationship that has become distant or broken. |例句：The estrangedness between the siblings was palpable.  兄弟姐妹之间的疏远是显而易见的。

例句：Years of silence had created a deep estrangedness in their family. 多年的沉默在他们的家庭中造成了深深的隔阂。


近义词：alienation, remoteness, distance, separation, detachment

反义词：closeness, intimacy, familiarity, connection, affection


词性：名词
