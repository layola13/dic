conscription:/kənˈskrɪpʃən/| n. |征兵，强制征募|The compulsory enlistment of people for military service.|例句：Military conscription was abolished in the country after the war.  战后，该国废除了兵役制。|例句：The government announced a new program of national conscription. 政府宣布了一项新的全国征兵计划。|近义词：draft, enlistment, levy|反义词：volunteerism
