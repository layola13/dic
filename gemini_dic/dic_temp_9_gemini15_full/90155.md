hotfooted:/ˈhɒtfʊtɪd/| adj. |匆忙的，飞奔的|Describes someone moving or acting with great speed and urgency, often due to excitement, fear, or a pressing need.|例句：He hotfooted it back to the office when he realized he'd left his presentation behind. 他意识到自己把演示文稿落下了，于是飞奔回办公室。|近义词：hurried, rushed, hasty, speedy|反义词：leisurely, slow, unhurried
