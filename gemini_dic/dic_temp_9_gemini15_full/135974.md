nonradiance:/ˌnɒnˈreɪdiəns/| n. |非辐射，无辐射|The absence of radiance; lack of brightness or luminosity.  It describes something that doesn't emit or reflect light or other forms of radiant energy.  |例句：The deep ocean possesses a remarkable nonradiance, making it difficult to see without artificial light. 深海具有显著的无辐射性，如果没有人工光源很难看清。

The nonradiance of the cave made it feel cold and damp.  洞穴的无辐射性使它感觉寒冷潮湿。|近义词：darkness, dullness, obscurity |反义词：radiance, brightness, luminosity
