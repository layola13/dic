encampments:/ɪnˈkæmpmənts/| n. |营地；宿营 |A temporary settlement, especially of soldiers or nomads.  Encampments often involve tents or other temporary structures and are set up for a specific purpose or duration. |例句：The refugee encampment was overcrowded and lacked basic sanitation. 难民营过度拥挤，缺乏基本的卫生设施。

例句：The army established several encampments along the border.  军队在边境沿线建立了几个营地。


近义词：camps, settlements, bivouacs |反义词：permanent settlements, cities


词源：源自中古英语 encampement，由en- (表示“in”或“into”)和 campement (源自法语 campement，意为“camping”)构成。
