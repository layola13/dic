screenless: /ˈskriːnləs/ | adj. | 无屏幕的 | Lacking a screen; without a screen.  The term describes devices or systems that operate without a traditional display screen.  This often implies interaction through other methods, such as voice control or haptic feedback. | Example:  The new screenless audio player is controlled via voice commands.  新的无屏幕音频播放器通过语音命令控制。


近义词：screen-free, displayless

反义词：screen-equipped, with screen
