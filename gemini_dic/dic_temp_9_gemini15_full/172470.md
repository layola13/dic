pyotr:/ˈpjɔːtr/| n. | 彼特（俄语人名） | Pyotr is a Russian masculine given name, equivalent to Peter in English. It is derived from the Greek name Petros, meaning "stone" or "rock".  The name has a long and rich history, associated with several significant figures throughout Russian history. | Example: Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky was a renowned Russian composer. 彼特·伊里奇·柴可夫斯基是一位著名的俄罗斯作曲家。|近义词: Peter, Petr |反义词:  (无直接反义词，人名无反义词概念)
