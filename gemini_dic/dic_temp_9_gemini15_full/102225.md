itchy:/ˈɪtʃi/ | adj. | 发痒的，令人渴望的 | Having an unpleasant tingling or prickling sensation on the skin that causes a desire to scratch.  It can also refer to a feeling of strong desire or restlessness. | 例句：My skin is itchy after being bitten by a mosquito.  蚊子叮咬后我的皮肤很痒。  I have an itchy feeling to start my own business. 我有一种强烈的渴望要自己创业。 | 近义词：irritated, scratchy, tingling | 反义词：smooth, comfortable


USAGE->

一般现在时:
My skin is itchy. = 我的皮肤很痒。
The wool sweater feels itchy. = 这件羊毛衫摸起来很痒。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

I have felt itchy since I ate that shellfish. = 从我吃了那种贝类海鲜后我就一直觉得痒。

过去完成时:
My skin had been itchy for weeks before I saw the doctor. = 在我看医生之前，我的皮肤已经痒了好几个星期了。
The itching had subsided after I took the medication. = 服药后瘙痒已经减轻了。

虚拟语气:
