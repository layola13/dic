hostlers:/ˈhɒstləz/ | n. |马夫；照看马的人 |A hostler is a person who looks after horses, especially in a stable or inn.  They are responsible for feeding, grooming, and caring for the animals.  The term is now largely archaic. |Example: In the old west, hostlers were essential for keeping stagecoaches running.  在旧西部，马夫对于维持驿站马车运营至关重要。|近义词：groom, stablehand, ostler |反义词：N/A
