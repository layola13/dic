monopolists:/məˈnɒpəlɪsts/| n. |垄断者，独占者 |People or organizations that control the supply of a particular commodity or service to the exclusion of others. They often use their power to set prices artificially high and limit competition. |例句：The government is investigating the actions of the monopolists in the oil industry. 政府正在调查石油行业垄断者的行为。

例句：Monopolists often stifle innovation by suppressing competition. 垄断者经常通过压制竞争来扼杀创新。


近义词：cartel, trust, oligopoly

反义词：competitors, free marketers


词性:名词
