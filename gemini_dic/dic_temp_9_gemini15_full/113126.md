lungi:/ˈlʌŋɡi/| n.| 倫吉（一種印度男子穿的圍裙式長布）|A lungi is a type of garment, traditionally worn by men in South Asia, particularly in India, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, and Malaysia. It's a long piece of cloth, typically wrapped around the waist and tied at the waist.  |Example: He wore a brightly colored lungi to the festival. 他在节日里穿了一条颜色鲜艳的伦吉。|近义词：dhoti (similar garment), sarong (similar garment from Southeast Asia)|反义词：无|名词
