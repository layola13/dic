nonhalogenated: /ˌnɒnˈhælədʒəneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 不含卤素的 | Not containing halogens (fluorine, chlorine, bromine, iodine, or astatine).  These substances are often used in refrigerants, solvents, and fire retardants, but their use is increasingly restricted due to environmental concerns. |  Example:  The company is committed to using only nonhalogenated flame retardants in its products.  这家公司致力于在其产品中仅使用不含卤素的阻燃剂。 | 近义词：halogen-free, halogen-less | 反义词：halogenated
