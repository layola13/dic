hdr:/ˌeɪtʃdiːˈɑːr/|abbr.|高动态范围图像|High Dynamic Range image; a type of image file that contains a wider range of tones and colors than a standard image file, resulting in more realistic and detailed images.|例句：The HDR photo showed incredible detail in both the highlights and shadows. 这张HDR照片在高光和阴影部分都展现了令人难以置信的细节。|近义词：HDR image, high dynamic range photo|反义词：SDR (Standard Dynamic Range) image
