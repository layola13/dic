shows: /ʃoʊz/ | v. n. | 展现；演出；表明；节目；展览 | To reveal, display, or demonstrate something; a television or radio program; an exhibition. | 近义词：displays, demonstrates, exhibits, programs, presentations | 反义词：hides, conceals, obscures


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shows his talent in painting. = 他在绘画方面展现了他的天赋。
The museum shows a variety of ancient artifacts. = 博物馆展出各种各样的古代文物。


一般过去时:
She showed me her new car. = 她给我看了她的新车。
The movie showed a lot of action scenes. = 这部电影有很多动作场面。


现在完成时:
They have shown great courage in the face of adversity. = 面对逆境，他们表现出了极大的勇气。
The company has shown significant growth this year. = 公司今年取得了显著的增长。


过去完成时:
He had shown signs of illness before the trip. = 在旅行之前，他已经表现出生病的迹象。
She had shown me the photographs before I left. = 我离开之前，她已经给我看过照片了。


将来时:
We will show you around the city tomorrow. = 我们明天带你参观这座城市。
The exhibition will show the latest advancements in technology. = 展览会将展示最新的技术进步。


虚拟语气:
If he showed more effort, he would succeed. = 如果他更加努力，他就会成功。
I wish he showed more interest in his studies. = 我希望他对学习更感兴趣。


被动语态:
The results were shown on the screen. = 结果显示在屏幕上。
The painting is shown in many galleries around the world. = 这幅画在世界各地的许多画廊展出。


疑问句:
Does this data show a significant trend? = 这个数据显示出显著的趋势吗？
What does the graph show? = 这个图表显示了什么？


否定句:
The experiment did not show any significant results. = 实验没有显示任何显著的结果。
He didn't show up for the meeting. = 他没有参加会议。


PHRASE->
show off:炫耀
show up:出现；露面
show around:带...参观
put on a show:上演；表演
show business:演艺圈
