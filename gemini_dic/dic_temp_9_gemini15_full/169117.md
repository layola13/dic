proindustrial: /ˌproʊɪnˈdʌstriəl/ | adj. | 前工业化的；工业化之前的 | Relating to the period before the widespread adoption of industrial processes and technologies.  Describes the era, society, or conditions preceding the Industrial Revolution. |

例句：

The proindustrial era saw a significant reliance on agriculture and handcrafts. = 前工业化时代很大程度上依赖农业和手工艺。

Proindustrial farming techniques were labor-intensive but yielded comparatively low output. = 前工业化时期的农业技术劳动密集型，但产量相对较低。


近义词：preindustrial, pre-industrial

反义词：industrial, postindustrial


词性:形容词
