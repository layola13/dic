greatness:/ˈɡreɪtnəs/| n. |伟大；卓越；杰出 |The quality of being great; eminence; distinction.  It refers to the state or quality of being exceptionally good, important, or impressive.  This can apply to achievements, character, or influence. |例句：Her greatness as a writer is undeniable.  她的写作才能是毋庸置疑的。

例句：The greatness of the Roman Empire is still studied today.  罗马帝国的伟大至今仍在被研究。


近义词：excellence, magnificence, eminence, grandeur, splendor

反义词： insignificance, mediocrity, triviality


词性: 名词
