例句：Her half-repentant expression hinted at a deeper guilt she was unwilling to fully confess. 她略带悔恨的表情暗示了她不愿完全承认的更深的罪恶。|近义词：partly remorseful, somewhat contrite|反义词：unrepentant, remorseless
