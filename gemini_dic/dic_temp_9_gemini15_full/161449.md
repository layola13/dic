pot-shotter:/ˈpɑːtʃɒtər/| n.|随意射击者；信口开河者|A person who takes pot shots; someone who makes casual, often ill-informed or critical remarks.|例句：He's a pot-shotter, always criticizing without offering solutions. 他是个信口开河的人，总是批评而不提供解决方案。|近义词：sniper, critic, detractor|反义词：supporter, admirer
