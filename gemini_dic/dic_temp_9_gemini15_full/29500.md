cataracts:/ˈkætərækts/| n.|白内障；瀑布；大洪水|A cataract is a clouding of the eye's lens that causes blurry vision.  It can also refer to a large waterfall or a deluge of water.|例句：The doctor diagnosed him with cataracts in both eyes.医生诊断他双眼患有白内障。|例句：Niagara Falls is a spectacular cataract.尼亚加拉瀑布是一个壮观的瀑布。|近义词：waterfall (for the water sense), clouding (for the eye sense)|反义词：clear vision (for the eye sense)
