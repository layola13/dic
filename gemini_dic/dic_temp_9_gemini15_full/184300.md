resow:/riːˈsoʊ/| vt.| 再播种，重新播种 |To sow (seeds) again.  This usually refers to sowing seeds in the same area after a previous sowing has failed or been harvested.|例句：The farmer had to resow the field after the heavy rain washed away the first planting. = 暴雨冲走了第一次播种后，农民不得不重新播种田地。

例句：We resowed the lawn after the drought killed much of the grass. = 干旱杀死了大部分草后，我们重新播种了草坪。


近义词：replant, sow again, resown

反义词：harvest, reap


词性:动词
