debater:/dɪˈbeɪtər/| n. |辩论者，论辩者 | A person who takes part in a debate.  Someone skilled in debating. |例句：He is a skilled debater, able to argue convincingly on any topic. 他是一位熟练的辩论者，能够令人信服地论证任何话题。

例句：The two debaters presented their arguments with passion and intelligence.  两位辩论者充满激情且智慧地陈述了他们的论点。


近义词：disputant, arguer, contender, discussant
反义词：listener (in the context of a debate), observer


词性：名词
