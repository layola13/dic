sees:/siːz/ | v. |看见；理解；明白 | To perceive with the eyes; to understand or comprehend. |例句：I sees the bird flying high in the sky. 我看见鸟儿在天上高飞。  She sees the problem clearly. 她清楚地看到了问题。|近义词：observes, perceives, understands |反义词：misses, overlooks, misunderstands |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sees the sunrise every morning. 他每天早上都看到日出。
She sees her friends regularly. 她定期见她的朋友们。


一般过去时:
I saw a movie last night. 我昨晚看了一部电影。
They saw the accident happen. 他们目睹了事故的发生。


现在完成时:
I have seen that movie several times. 我看过那部电影好几次了。
She has seen the doctor about her illness. 她去看过医生关于她的病情。


过去完成时:
He had seen the warning signs before the accident. 事故发生前他已经看到警告标志了。
They had seen the movie before it was released in theaters.  他们在电影在影院上映前就看过了。


将来时:
I will see you tomorrow. 我明天见你。
We will see what happens next. 我们将看看接下来会发生什么。


虚拟语气:
If I saw him, I would tell him the truth. 如果我看到他，我会告诉他真相。
I wish I had seen the show. 我真希望我看了那场演出。


被动语态:
The problem is seen by many people. 这个问题被许多人看到。
The movie was seen by millions of people. 这部电影被数百万人观看。


疑问句:
Do you see what I mean? 你明白我的意思吗？
What do you see in the picture? 你在图画里看到了什么？


否定句:
I don't see why you're upset. 我不明白你为什么生气。
She didn't see the car coming. 她没有看到车开来。


PHRASE->
See you later! 再见！
Let's see. 让我们看看。
See for yourself. 自己看。
I see. 我明白了。
See the doctor. 看医生。


词源:
源自古英语 *seon，意思是“看见”。  它与古高地德语 *sehan 和古诺尔斯语 *sjá  同源。
