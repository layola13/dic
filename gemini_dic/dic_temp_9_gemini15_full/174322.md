quick-tempered:/ˈkwɪkˈtɛmpərd/ | adj. | 脾气暴躁的，易怒的 | Easily angered; having a short temper.  A quick-tempered person becomes angry very easily. | 例句：He's known to be quick-tempered, so try to avoid upsetting him. 他以脾气暴躁著称，所以尽量避免惹恼他。

例句：She has a quick temper and often regrets her harsh words afterward. 她脾气火爆，经常事后后悔自己说过的话。


近义词：irritable, choleric, irascible, short-tempered, hot-headed

反义词：patient, even-tempered, calm, placid
