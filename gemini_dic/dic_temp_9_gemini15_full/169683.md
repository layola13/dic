proposes:/prəˈpoʊz/| vt.|建议；提议；求婚|To suggest a plan or course of action; to offer or suggest something for consideration or adoption; to make a proposal of marriage.|例句：He proposed a new marketing strategy. 他提出了一项新的营销策略。|例句：She proposed to him on bended knee. 她单膝跪地向他求婚。|近义词：suggest, offer, recommend, suggest, propose marriage |反义词：oppose, reject, refuse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He proposes a new solution every day. 他每天都提出一个新的解决方案。
She proposes a toast to the happy couple. 她提议为这对幸福的夫妇敬酒。


一般过去时:
They proposed a compromise. 他们提议了一个妥协方案。
He proposed to her last night. 他昨晚向她求婚了。


现在完成时:
She has proposed several amendments to the bill. 她已经对该法案提出了几项修正案。
They have proposed a new plan to reduce pollution. 他们已经提出了一项减少污染的新计划。


过去完成时:
He had proposed a similar idea before. 他之前已经提出过类似的想法。
They had proposed a solution, but it was rejected. 他们曾提出过一个解决方案，但被否决了。


将来时:
She will propose a new budget next week. 她下周将提出一份新的预算。
They will propose changes to the current system. 他们将提议更改现行制度。


虚拟语气:
I would propose that we postpone the meeting. 我建议我们推迟会议。
If he proposed a better solution, we would accept it. 如果他提出更好的解决方案，我们会接受。


被动语态:
A new law was proposed by the government. 一项新的法律由政府提出。
Several solutions were proposed at the meeting. 会议上提出了几个解决方案。


疑问句:
What did he propose? 他提议了什么？
What are you proposing to do? 你打算做什么？


否定句:
He did not propose any changes to the plan. 他没有对计划提出任何修改。
They didn't propose a solution to the problem. 他们没有提出解决这个问题的方案。
