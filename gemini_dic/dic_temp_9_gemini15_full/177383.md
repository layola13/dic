reauthorizations:/ˌriːˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 重新授权；再次授权 | The process of granting authorization again.  It often refers to legislative or governmental processes where existing powers or permissions are renewed or extended.  | Example: The reauthorizations of the farm bill are crucial for agricultural stability.  重新授权农业法案对农业稳定至关重要。  |近义词:renewals, extensions, revalidations|反义词:revocations, cancellations|名词
