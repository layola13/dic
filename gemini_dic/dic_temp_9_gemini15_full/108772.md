leaseholder: /ˈliːzˌhoʊldər/ | n. | 租赁人，承租人 | A person who holds a lease on a property, typically an apartment or a house. They have the right to occupy and use the property for a specified period, paying rent or other fees to the landlord or freeholder.  |  He is the leaseholder of a small apartment in the city. 他是城里一套小公寓的承租人。  She renewed her leaseholder agreement for another year. 她将承租协议续签了一年。| tenant, renter | freeholder, owner |

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The leaseholder pays rent monthly. = 承租人每月支付租金。
He is the leaseholder of this property. = 他是这处房产的承租人。


一般过去时:
The leaseholder left the apartment last month. = 承租人上个月离开了公寓。
She was a leaseholder of this building for five years. = 她曾是这栋大楼的承租人五年。


现在完成时:
The leaseholder has paid all the rent on time. = 承租人已按时支付了所有租金。
He has been a leaseholder here for many years. = 他在这里做了多年的承租人。


过去完成时:
The leaseholder had already paid the rent before the landlord called. = 在房东打电话之前，承租人已经付了房租。
Before moving in, she had checked if she was a leaseholder or a sub-leaseholder. = 在搬进去之前，她已经检查过自己是承租人还是次承租人。


将来时:
The leaseholder will renew the lease next year. = 承租人明年将续租。
He will be a leaseholder of this house for another two years. = 他将再做两年这栋房子的承租人。


虚拟语气:
If he were the leaseholder, he would have made repairs. = 如果他是承租人，他会进行维修。
I wish I were the leaseholder so I could renovate the kitchen. = 我希望我是承租人，这样我就可以装修厨房了。


被动语态:
The lease was signed by the leaseholder yesterday. = 租赁合同昨天由承租人签署。
The apartment is occupied by the leaseholder. = 这套公寓被承租人占用。


疑问句:
Is he the leaseholder of this building? = 他是这栋楼的承租人吗？
Who is the leaseholder of this property? = 这处房产的承租人是谁？


否定句:
He is not the leaseholder; he is only a sub-tenant. = 他不是承租人；他只是一个次承租人。
She wasn't the leaseholder at that time. = 她当时不是承租人。
