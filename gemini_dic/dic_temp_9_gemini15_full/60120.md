effascinate:/ɪˈfæsnˌeɪt/| vt.|使着迷；使入迷；吸引|To attract and hold the attention of someone, by being interesting, beautiful, or exciting.  |例句：The magician's tricks fascinated the children. 魔术师的戏法迷住了孩子们。

例句：Her beauty fascinated him. 她的美貌使他着迷。


近义词：captivate, enchant, enthrall, mesmerize, charm|反义词：bore, repel, disgust|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The art exhibition fascinates many visitors. = 艺术展览吸引了许多游客。
The mystery novel fascinates me. = 这本悬疑小说让我着迷。

一般过去时:
The concert fascinated the audience. = 音乐会迷住了观众。
The story fascinated her greatly. = 这个故事深深地吸引了她。

现在完成时:
The ancient ruins have fascinated historians for centuries. = 古代遗址几个世纪以来一直吸引着历史学家。
This subject has fascinated me since childhood. = 这个主题从孩提时代就吸引着我。


过去完成时:
I had been fascinated by astronomy since I was a child. = 从小我就对天文学很着迷。
She had been fascinated by his stories for years. = 多年来她一直被他的故事所吸引。

将来时:
The new technology will fascinate people all over the world. = 这项新技术将吸引全世界的人们。
I am sure this movie will fascinate you. = 我相信这部电影会让你着迷。

虚拟语气:
If the story were more interesting, it would fascinate me more. = 如果故事更有趣，它会更吸引我。
I wish this book fascinated me more. = 我希望这本书更吸引我一些。

被动语态:
Many people are fascinated by the beauty of nature. = 许多人被大自然的美丽所吸引。
He was fascinated by her intelligence. = 他被她的智慧所吸引。

疑问句:
Does the idea of space travel fascinate you? = 你对太空旅行的想法着迷吗？
What fascinates you about this topic? = 你对这个话题的什么方面着迷？

否定句:
The lecture didn't fascinate the students. = 讲座没有吸引学生。
The film didn't fascinate me at all. = 这部电影一点也没有吸引我。
