plater:/ˈpleɪtər/| n.|盘子；托盘；镀金者|A plater is a person or thing that plates something, often referring to someone who electroplates metal or a tray used to hold plates.  It can also refer to a shallow dish or tray.|例句：He works as a plater in a factory. 他在工厂里做镀金工。  The waiter carried a plater full of plates. 服务员端着一个装满盘子的托盘。|近义词：tray, dish, electroplater|反义词：None specifically.

She plates the food beautifully. = 她把食物摆得很漂亮。
