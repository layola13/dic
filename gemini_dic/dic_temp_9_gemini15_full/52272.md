dicey:/ˈdaɪsi/| adj.| 危险的；不确定的；棘手的 | Risky; uncertain; precarious; likely to fail or go wrong.  |例句：The situation is rather dicey.  情况相当棘手。

例句：The future of the company is looking dicey. 公司的未来看起来岌岌可危。


近义词：risky, precarious, uncertain, problematic, chancy, hazardous

反义词：safe, secure, certain, reliable, stable


词源：源自古英语“dicing”（掷骰子），暗示着不确定性和偶然性，因为骰子的结果是不可预测的。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weather is dicey today. = 今天的天气很糟糕。
This plan seems dicey to me. = 这个计划在我看来很冒险。


一般过去时:
The situation was dicey after the accident. = 事故发生后情况很危险。
The climb was dicey, but we made it. = 攀登很危险，但我们成功了。


现在完成时:
The negotiations have been dicey, but we hope to reach an agreement. = 谈判一直很棘手，但我们希望达成协议。
Things have become quite dicey since the new regulations were introduced. = 自从新的规章制度出台以来，情况变得相当棘手。


过去完成时:
The situation had become dicey before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，情况已经变得很危险。
The deal had seemed dicey from the start. = 这笔交易从一开始就显得不靠谱。


将来时:
The weather forecast predicts dicey conditions tomorrow. = 天气预报预测明天情况不佳。
The next few weeks could be dicey for the business. = 未来几周对这家企业来说可能会很危险。


虚拟语气:
If the situation were less dicey, we would proceed. = 如果情况不那么糟糕，我们会继续进行。
I wish the future were not so dicey. = 我希望未来不要那么不确定。


被动语态:
The plan was deemed dicey by the committee. = 该计划被委员会认为是冒险的。
The entire project is considered dicey by many experts. = 许多专家认为整个项目都存在风险。


疑问句:
Is the situation really that dicey? = 情况真的那么糟糕吗？
Do you think this investment is too dicey? = 你认为这项投资太冒险了吗？


否定句:
The situation is not dicey at all. = 情况一点也不危险。
The results of the experiment were not dicey, they were quite conclusive. = 实验结果并非不确定，而是相当确凿的。
