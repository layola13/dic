peddler's: /ˈpɛdlərz/ | n. | 小贩的，兜售者的 | Belonging to or characteristic of a peddler; relating to the activities of a peddler. |例句：The peddler's cart was laden with colorful trinkets.  小贩的推车装满了五颜六色的饰品。

The peddler's wares were displayed on a blanket. 小贩的商品摊在一张毯子上展示。


近义词：hawker's, vendor's, street seller's | 反义词：N/A


词源:  From "peddler," ultimately from Middle English *pedlere, from Old French *pedler, from a Vulgar Latin word *pediculare, to carry about on foot, related to Latin *pes, pedis, foot.  The "-'s" is a possessive suffix.
