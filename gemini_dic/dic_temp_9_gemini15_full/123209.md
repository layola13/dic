monochronic:/ˈmɒnəˌkroʊnɪk/| adj.|单一时间的；一次只做一件事的|Characterized by the performance of only one task at a time; linear.  Monochronic cultures value punctuality, schedules, and completing one task before beginning another.|例句：He's a monochronic worker; he prefers to focus on one project at a time. 他是一位单一时间的员工；他更喜欢一次专注于一个项目。

例句：Many cultures are monochronic, prioritizing efficiency and order. 许多文化都是单一时间的，优先考虑效率和秩序。


近义词：linear, sequential, orderly, punctual|反义词：polychronic, multitasking
