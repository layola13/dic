hemorrhoidal:/ˌhɛməˈrɔɪdəl/| adj.|痔的；痔疮的| Relating to or affected by hemorrhoids.  Hemorrhoids are swollen veins in the anus and rectum that can cause pain, itching, and bleeding.| Example: He suffered from hemorrhoidal pain. 他患有痔疮疼痛。|近义词：rectal (in relation to location), anal (in relation to location)|反义词：None
