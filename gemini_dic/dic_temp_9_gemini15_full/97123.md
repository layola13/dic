infil:/ˈɪnfɪl/| v.|渗入，渗透；逐渐影响|To gradually penetrate or spread into something.|The enemy soldiers infiltrated the city at night.  敌人夜间潜入城市。|The new ideas slowly infiltrated the company culture. 新思想逐渐渗透到公司文化中。|penetrate, percolate, seep|isolate, exclude|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The water infiltrates the soil. = 水渗入土壤。
He infiltrates the enemy camp. = 他渗透到敌营。


一般过去时:
The enemy infiltrated our defenses. = 敌人渗透了我们的防线。
The rain infiltrated the tent. = 雨水渗透了帐篷。


现在完成时:
Terrorist groups have infiltrated the country. = 恐怖组织已经渗透到这个国家。
The mold has infiltrated the walls. = 霉菌已经渗透到墙壁里。


过去完成时:
The spies had infiltrated the organization before the investigation began. = 间谍在调查开始之前就已经渗透到该组织内部。
The water had infiltrated the basement before we noticed it. = 我们注意到之前，水就已经渗透到地下室了。


将来时:
The enemy will infiltrate our lines. = 敌人将会渗透我们的防线。
The pollutants will infiltrate the groundwater. = 污染物将会渗透到地下水中。


虚拟语气:
If they infiltrated the system, they could steal valuable data. = 如果他们渗透了系统，他们就能窃取宝贵的数据。
I wish the enemy hadn't infiltrated our ranks. = 我真希望敌人没有渗透到我们的队伍中。


被动语态:
The city was infiltrated by enemy spies. = 城市被敌方间谍渗透了。
The data was infiltrated by a hacker. = 数据被黑客入侵了。


疑问句:
Did the enemy infiltrate our base? = 敌人是否渗透了我们的基地？
Has the virus infiltrated the computer system? = 病毒是否已经渗透到电脑系统中了？


否定句:
The enemy did not infiltrate our defenses. = 敌人没有渗透我们的防线。
The water did not infiltrate the building. = 水没有渗透到建筑物里。


PHRASE->

infiltrate the ranks = 渗透到队伍中
infiltrate the enemy lines = 渗透到敌军阵线
infiltrate a group = 渗透到一个群体中
infiltrate the organization = 渗透到组织中
infiltrate the information = 渗透信息
