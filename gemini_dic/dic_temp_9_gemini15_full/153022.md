peek:/piːk/| v. n.|偷看，窥视|To look quickly and secretly at something.  |例句：She peeked through the keyhole. 她从钥匙孔里偷看。

例句：I peeked at the present before my birthday. 我在我生日之前偷看了礼物。


近义词：glance, peep, spy| 反义词：stare, gaze| 词性:动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He peeks at his neighbor's newspaper every morning. 他每天早上都偷看邻居的报纸。
She often peeks into the kitchen to see if the cake is ready. 她经常偷偷溜进厨房看看蛋糕是否烤好了。


一般过去时:
The children peeked into the box to see what was inside. 孩子们偷看了盒子里面有什么。
I peeked through the window and saw them talking. 我透过窗户偷看，看到他们在说话。


现在完成时:
I've peeked at the answers, but I still don't understand the question. 我偷看了答案，但我仍然不明白问题。
She has peeked at the test paper several times during the exam. 考试期间，她几次偷看过试卷。


过去完成时:
He had peeked at the letter before his wife arrived. 他在他妻子到达之前偷看过信。
They had peeked into the room before entering. 他们进入房间之前已经偷看过房间了。


将来时:
I will peek at the recipe before I start cooking. 我会在开始做饭之前偷看食谱。
She will peek through the blinds to see who is at the door. 她会透过百叶窗看看是谁在门口。


虚拟语气:
If I peeked, I would see what was inside. 如果我偷看，我会看到里面是什么。
I wish I hadn't peeked at the ending of the movie. 我真希望我没有偷看电影的结局。


被动语态:
The contents of the letter were peeked at by someone. 信的内容被某人偷看了。
The room was peeked into by a stranger. 房间被一个陌生人偷看了。


疑问句:
Did you peek at my diary? 你偷看我的日记了吗？
Has anyone peeked at my files? 有人偷看过我的文件吗？


否定句:
I didn't peek at your email. 我没有偷看你邮件。
She didn't peek at the answers. 她没有偷看答案。
