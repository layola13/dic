cryptography:/krɪpˈtɒɡrəfi/ /krɪpˈtɑːɡrəfi/| n. | 密码学 | The art or science encompassing the principles and methods of transforming information into an unintelligible form, and then transforming it back into its original form, for the purposes of secure communication in the presence of adversaries.  Cryptography is used to protect sensitive information from unauthorized access and use. | Example: Modern cryptography relies heavily on complex mathematical algorithms. 现代密码学严重依赖复杂的数学算法。


Example:  The company uses strong cryptography to protect its customer data. 这家公司使用强大的密码学来保护其客户数据。


近义词：cryptology, code making


反义词：plaintext, cleartext


词性: 名词
