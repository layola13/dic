over-loved:/ˌoʊvərˈlʌvd/ | adj. |过度宠爱的，溺爱的 | Excessively loved; receiving too much affection or attention, often to the point of spoiling.  |例句：The over-loved child grew up spoiled and entitled.  过度宠爱的孩子长大后变得娇纵自负。

例句：He felt over-loved by his grandmother, who constantly showered him with gifts. 他觉得奶奶太宠他了，奶奶总是给他买很多礼物。


近义词：spoiled, pampered, coddled, indulged


反义词：neglected, ignored, deprived


词性：形容词
