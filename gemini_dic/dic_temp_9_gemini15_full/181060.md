reincreases:/ˌriːɪnˈkriːsɪz/| v.|再次增加，重新增加 |To increase again; to increase something again.  |例句：The company reincreased its marketing budget after seeing positive sales figures. 公司在看到积极的销售数据后再次增加了其营销预算。  |近义词：reinstate, renew, resume|反义词：decrease, reduce|动词
