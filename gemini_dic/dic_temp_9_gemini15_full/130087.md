non-probability:/ˌnɒnˌprɒbəˈbɪləti/| n. |非概率性，非概率事件|The term refers to events or situations where the likelihood of occurrence cannot be quantified using probability calculations.  It often involves subjective judgments, incomplete data, or events that defy standard probabilistic models.  For example, predicting the success of a new business venture is a non-probability problem because it involves many unpredictable factors. |The study focused on the non-probability sampling methods. 这项研究的重点是非概率抽样方法。|近义词：uncertainty, unlikelihood |反义词：probability, certainty
