mixture: /ˈmɪkstʃər/ | n. |混合物，混合体 | A substance consisting of two or more components not chemically bonded.  A combination of different things. |例句：The cake is a mixture of flour, sugar, and eggs.  这个蛋糕是面粉、糖和鸡蛋的混合物。

例句：The artist created a vibrant mixture of colors on the canvas.  艺术家在画布上创造了鲜艳的色彩混合。


近义词：blend, combination, composite, amalgam | 反义词：purity, oneness


词源:  From Middle English *mixtur, from Old French *mixtur, from Latin mixtūra "a mixing," from mixtus, past participle of miscere "to mix."
