shimmered:/ˈʃɪmərd/| v.|闪光；闪烁；微光|To shine with a soft, unsteady light.  The light shimmered on the water.  |The heat shimmered above the asphalt.|The silk shimmered in the candlelight.|近义词：glitter, gleam, glimmer, twinkle|反义词：dull
