cave:/keɪv/| n.|山洞，洞穴|A natural underground space, large enough to enter, typically a hollow in the side of a cliff or hill.|例句：We explored a dark and mysterious cave. 我们探索了一个黑暗而神秘的山洞。|例句：The cave was filled with fascinating rock formations.  这个山洞充满了迷人的岩石构造。|近义词： cavern, grotto|反义词：open space
