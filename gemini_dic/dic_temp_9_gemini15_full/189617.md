salinization:/ˌsælɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |盐碱化；盐化作用|The process by which salts accumulate in the soil, making it unsuitable for agriculture.  This is often caused by irrigation in arid and semi-arid regions, where the water evaporates leaving behind dissolved salts.|例句：The salinization of the land has rendered it infertile.  土地盐碱化使其变得贫瘠。|例句：Excessive irrigation can lead to soil salinization. 过度灌溉会导致土壤盐碱化。|近义词：salination, soil salinization|反义词：desalination
