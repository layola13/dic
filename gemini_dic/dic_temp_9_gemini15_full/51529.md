It implies a previous shortening or cutting off.|例句：The editor decided to detruncate the article, restoring the sections that had been removed. 编辑决定恢复文章被删节的部分。|例句：The historian painstakingly detruncated the ancient manuscript, piecing together fragments to reveal the original text. 历史学家煞费苦心地恢复了古代手稿，将碎片拼凑起来以揭示原文。|近义词：restore, complete, reinstate, lengthen|反义词：truncate, shorten, abridge, curtail|动词
