issues:/ˈɪʃuːz/ | n. | 问题；议题；发行；争论 |  Issues can range from minor inconveniences to major crises.  问题可以从小小的不便到重大的危机不等。|近义词：problems, topics, matters, questions | 反义词：solutions, answers


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company faces many issues this year. = 今年公司面临许多问题。
This project presents several important issues. = 这个项目提出了几个重要的问题。


一般过去时:
The meeting addressed several key issues. = 会议讨论了几个关键问题。
We discussed the issues that had arisen. = 我们讨论了出现的问题。


现在完成时:
They have resolved most of the issues. = 他们已经解决了大部分问题。
We have encountered several unexpected issues. = 我们遇到了一些意想不到的问题。


过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
