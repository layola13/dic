motte:/mɒt/| n. |土墩, 堡垒 |A motte is a raised mound of earth, typically artificial, often forming part of a medieval castle.  It usually served as a fortified refuge, often with a wooden or stone structure built on top. |例句：The Norman castle consisted of a motte and bailey.  这个诺曼城堡由一个土墩和护城河组成。|近义词：mound, hill, rampart |反义词：plain, valley
