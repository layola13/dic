precultivates:/ˌpriːˈkʌltɪveɪts/| vt.| 预先培养；预先种植 |To cultivate (something, especially microorganisms) beforehand.  This typically involves growing cells or organisms in a controlled environment before introducing them to a different or more complex environment.  |例句：The researchers precultivated the bacteria in a nutrient-rich broth before inoculating the experimental plants.  研究人员在接种实验植物之前，在富含营养的肉汤中预先培养了细菌。

例句：The brewery precultivates yeast strains to ensure consistent fermentation. 这家啤酒厂预先培养酵母菌株以确保发酵的一致性。


近义词：prepare, cultivate in advance, pre-grow

反义词：  None directly opposite, but "neglect to cultivate" or "introduce directly" could be considered contextual opposites.

词性:动词
