self-permission:/ˌsɛlf pərˈmɪʃən/ | n. |自我许可，自行允许 |The act of granting oneself permission to do something, often without external authorization.  It implies a degree of autonomy and self-governance. |例句：She gave herself self-permission to take a break from work. 她允许自己休息一下。


例句：He needed to give himself self-permission to pursue his passion. 他需要允许自己去追求自己的热情。


近义词：self-authorization, autonomy, self-approval


反义词：external authorization, requirement, restriction


词性:noun
