embassy:/ˈembəsi/| n.|大使馆；使馆|The official residence or offices of an ambassador and their staff in a foreign country.  It represents the interests of their home country.|例句：The American embassy is located in the city center. 美国大使馆位于市中心。|例句：He visited the British embassy to apply for a visa. 他访问了英国大使馆申请签证。|近义词：legation (较正式，通常指级别较低的外交代表处)|反义词：无

词源:
From Old French *embassie, from Old French ambaxade, from Medieval Latin ambasciata, from Latin ambaxator ("ambassador"), from Greek ἀмбаξάτωρ (ambaxatōr).  The modern spelling reflects the influence of the word "bassador" (ambassador).
