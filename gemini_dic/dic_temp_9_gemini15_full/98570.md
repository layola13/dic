institution:/ˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃən/| n. | 机构；制度；习俗 | A society or organization founded for a religious, educational, social, or similar purpose.  It can also refer to an established law, custom, or practice. | 例句：Harvard University is a prestigious institution.  哈佛大学是一所享有盛誉的机构。 | 例句：The institution of marriage has changed significantly over time.  婚姻制度随着时间的推移发生了显著变化。 |近义词：organization, establishment, system, body |反义词：individual, informality
