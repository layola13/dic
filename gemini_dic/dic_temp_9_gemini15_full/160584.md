porpita:/ˈpɔːrpɪtə/| n. |帆水母，僧帽水母的一种|A small, free-floating marine hydrozoan (Porpita porpita), often mistaken for a jellyfish. It has a flattened, disc-like body with a central, gas-filled float and radiating tentacles.|Example: I saw a beautiful porpita while snorkeling in the tropical waters.|近义词：blue button (though not strictly a synonym, it's a visually similar creature)|反义词：N/A
