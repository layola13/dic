refill:/ˌriːˈfɪl/| vt. n.|再装满；补充；续杯|To fill something again, especially a container that has been emptied.|例句：I need to refill my water bottle. 我需要再装满我的水瓶。|例句：The gas station offers a refill service. 这个加油站提供续杯服务。|近义词：replenish, top up|反义词：empty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refills his coffee cup every morning. = 他每天早上都会续杯咖啡。
She refills the ink cartridge regularly. = 她定期更换墨盒。


一般过去时:
I refilled the bird feeder yesterday. = 我昨天给喂鸟器加满了食物。
They refilled the swimming pool after cleaning it. = 他们清洁游泳池后又加满了水。


现在完成时:
I have refilled the printer with new ink. = 我已经给打印机加了新墨水。
She has refilled her prescription. = 她已经续方了。


过去完成时:
He had refilled the gas tank before the long drive. = 长途驾驶前，他已经加满了油箱。
They had refilled the water bottles before the hike. = 他们远足前已经加满了水瓶。


将来时:
I will refill the sugar bowl tomorrow. = 我明天会把糖罐加满。
She will refill the prescription next week. = 她下周会续方。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would refill the shelves. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把货架补充好。
I wish I had refilled my phone before leaving. = 我真希望离开前给手机充过电。


被动语态:
The water bottle was refilled by the waiter. = 服务员又给水瓶加满了水。
The ink cartridge is being refilled at the moment. = 墨盒正在被灌墨水。


疑问句:
Do you need me to refill your glass? = 你需要我帮你续杯吗？
Will you refill the salt shaker? = 你会把盐瓶加满吗？


否定句:
I didn't refill the ink cartridge. = 我没有换墨盒。
He won't refill the gas tank until tomorrow. = 他直到明天才会加油。


PHRASE->
refill a prescription = 续方
refill a tank = 加满油箱
refill a bottle = 再装满瓶子
refill an order = 补货
refill the supply = 补充库存
