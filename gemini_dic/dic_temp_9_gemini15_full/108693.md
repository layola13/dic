leaking:/ˈliːkɪŋ/ | v. | 泄漏；渗漏 |  To escape or allow escape through a crack or hole.  | Example: The roof is leaking after the storm.  暴风雨后屋顶漏水了。 The pipe is leaking water. 水管漏水了。|近义词：dripping, seeping, oozing | 反义词：sealed, airtight


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The faucet is leaking. = 水龙头漏水了。
The roof leaks during heavy rain. = 大雨时屋顶漏水。

一般过去时:
The pipe leaked last night. = 昨晚水管漏水了。
My car leaked oil on the highway. = 我的车在高速公路上漏油了。


现在完成时:
The tank has leaked all the fuel. = 油箱已经漏光了燃料。
The boat has been leaking since the collision. = 自从碰撞以来，船一直漏水。


过去完成时:
The roof had leaked before we noticed it. = 我们注意到之前屋顶就已经漏水了。
The container had leaked its contents onto the floor. = 容器里的东西已经漏到地板上了。


将来时:
The dam will leak if the water pressure increases. = 如果水压增加，水坝将会漏水。
The bottle will likely leak if you shake it too hard. = 如果你用力摇晃瓶子，它可能会漏水。


虚拟语气:
If the dam were leaking, it would be a disaster. = 如果水坝漏水，那将是一场灾难。
I wish the roof wasn't leaking. = 我希望屋顶不漏水。


被动语态:
The information was leaked to the press. = 信息被泄露给了媒体。
The secret was leaked by an insider. = 秘密被内部人士泄露了。


疑问句:
Is the boat leaking? = 船漏水了吗？
Where is the water leaking from? = 水是从哪里漏出来的？


否定句:
The container doesn't leak. = 容器不漏水。
The roof didn't leak during the light rain. = 小雨时屋顶没有漏水。


PHRASE->
