cannibal:/ˈkænɪbəl/| n. |食人族；食人者|A cannibal is a person who eats human flesh.  The term often refers to tribes or groups known for practicing cannibalism, but it can also describe individuals who engage in this act. |The tribe was known to be cannibals, consuming their enemies after battle.  这个部落以食人为生，他们在战斗后吃掉他们的敌人。|ogre, man-eater|vegetarian, herbivore|名词
