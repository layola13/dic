harked:/hɑːkt/ | v. | 倾听；追溯；追忆 | To listen attentively; to mention or allude to something; to go back to or dwell upon something in the past. |例句：He harked back to his childhood memories. 他追忆起童年的回忆。

例句：The dog harked at the sound of the approaching car.  狗听到驶近的汽车声就吠叫了。(This example uses a slightly different meaning, closer to "listen attentively" in the context of a dog's hearing.)


近义词：listened, recalled, reminisced, alluded

反义词：ignored, disregarded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harks back to his youth often. = 他经常追忆他的青春岁月。
She harks to every word the speaker says. = 她仔细聆听演讲者说的每一个字。


一般过去时:
They harked back to the events of the previous year. = 他们追溯到前一年发生的事情。
He harked to the old tales his grandfather told him. = 他倾听了他祖父告诉他的古老故事。


现在完成时:
She has harked back to that difficult time many times. = 她多次追忆那段艰难的时光。
They have harked to the wisdom of their ancestors for generations. = 世世代代以来，他们一直遵循祖先的智慧。


过去完成时:
He had harked back to his childhood home many times before he finally visited it. = 在他最终回去探望之前，他已经多次追忆起他童年的家。
They had harked to the warnings, but it was too late. = 他们曾听从过警告，但为时已晚。


将来时:
She will hark back to this day for years to come. = 未来几年，她都会回想起这一天。
He will hark to the advice of his mentor. = 他将听从他导师的建议。


虚拟语气:
If he had harked to my advice, he wouldn't be in this mess. = 如果他听从我的建议，他就不会陷入这种困境。
I wish I had harked to my intuition. = 我真希望我当时听了我的直觉。


被动语态:
The past was harked back to frequently in the novel. = 小说中经常追溯过去。 (This is a less common usage in passive voice)
His wisdom was harked to by all who knew him. = 所有认识他的人都听从他的智慧。（This is also a less common passive usage.)


疑问句:
Did he hark back to his experiences in the army? = 他是否追忆起他在军队里的经历？
Will she hark to reason? = 她会听从理智吗？


否定句:
He did not hark to the warnings. = 他没有听从警告。
She did not hark back to her unhappy childhood. = 她没有追忆她不幸的童年。


PHRASE->
hark back to = 追溯到
hark upon = 详述；强调
hark forward to = 向前展望
hark at = 倾听；注意听
hark! = 听着！
