co-worker:/ˈkoʊwɜːrkər/|/ˈkəʊwɜːkə(r)/| n. |同事|A person who works with another person or others, especially in a professional capacity.|例句：I get along well with most of my co-workers. 我和我的大多数同事相处得很好。|例句：He’s a great co-worker and always willing to help.|他是一位很棒的同事，总是乐于助人。|近义词：colleague, teammate|反义词：supervisor, boss


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My co-worker helps me with my work. = 我的同事帮助我工作。
She always communicates well with her co-workers. = 她总是和她同事沟通良好。


一般过去时:
My co-worker helped me with that project. = 我的同事帮我完成了那个项目。
They discussed the project with their co-workers yesterday. = 他们昨天和同事讨论了这个项目。


现在完成时:
I have worked with this co-worker for five years. = 我和这位同事一起工作了五年。
She has collaborated with many co-workers on different projects. = 她与许多同事合作完成过不同的项目。


过去完成时:
I had already spoken to my co-worker before the meeting. = 我在会议之前已经和我的同事谈过了。
They had finished their work before their co-workers arrived. = 在他们的同事到达之前，他们已经完成了工作。


将来时:
I will ask my co-worker for help tomorrow. = 我明天会向我的同事寻求帮助。
They will be collaborating with their co-workers on the new project next week. = 下周他们将与同事合作新项目。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better co-worker, I would be more productive. = 如果我有一个更好的同事，我会更高效。
I wish I had a co-worker who was more organized. = 我希望我有一个更有条理的同事。


被动语态:
The project was completed by my co-worker and me. = 这个项目由我和我的同事完成。
The problem was discussed with my co-workers. = 这个问题与我的同事们讨论过了。


疑问句:
Do you get along with your co-workers? = 你和你的同事相处得好吗？
Have you introduced yourself to your new co-workers? = 你向你的新同事介绍过自己了吗？


否定句:
I don't know my co-workers very well. = 我不太了解我的同事。
She didn't talk to her co-workers about the issue. = 她没有和同事谈论这个问题。
