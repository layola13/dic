parfait:/pɑːrˈfeɪ/|/pɑːrˈfeɪ/| n. |芭菲，圣代|A parfait is a layered dessert typically consisting of ice cream, fruit, and often yogurt or whipped cream.  It's known for its visually appealing layers and refreshing taste.|Example:  I ordered a strawberry parfait for dessert. 我点了一份草莓芭菲作为甜点。  The parfait was a delicious combination of creamy ice cream and fresh berries. 这份芭菲是奶油冰淇淋和新鲜浆果的美味组合。|近义词：sundae, dessert, ice cream sundae|反义词：无
