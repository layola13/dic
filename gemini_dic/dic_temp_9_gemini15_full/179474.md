redwoods:/ˈrɛdˌwʊdz/ | n. |红杉树林，红杉|  Tall evergreen trees of the genus Sequoia, especially Sequoia sempervirens (coast redwood) and Sequoiadendron giganteum (giant sequoia).  Redwoods are known for their immense size and longevity. |例句：We hiked through a magnificent redwood forest. 我们徒步穿越了一片壮丽的红杉林。
例句：The giant sequoias are among the oldest and largest living organisms on Earth. 巨型红杉是地球上最古老和最大的生物之一。|近义词：sequoia, conifer |反义词: None (no direct antonym) |名词
