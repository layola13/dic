excepted:/ɪkˈsɛptɪd/| adj.|被除外的；被豁免的；被排除的|Something that is excepted is excluded or left out from a general statement, rule, or list.  It implies a deliberate action of removing something.  |例句：All students, excepted those with medical notes, must attend the field trip.  所有学生都必须参加实地考察，持有医疗证明的学生除外。

例句：The contract, excepted clause five, is binding.  除了第五条条款外，合同具有约束力。

近义词：excluded, omitted, exempted


反义词：included, incorporated


词性:形容词
