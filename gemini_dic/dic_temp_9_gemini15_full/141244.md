op-eds:/ˈɑːpˌɛdz/| n. |观点文章；专栏文章|Opinion-editorial pieces, typically found in newspapers and magazines, expressing the author's opinion on a current issue.  Op-eds are usually commissioned or invited by the publication, unlike letters to the editor. |Example:  The New York Times published an op-ed about the upcoming election.  纽约时报发表了一篇关于即将到来的选举的观点文章。

|近义词：opinion piece, commentary, editorial (but editorials are typically written by the publication's staff)|反义词：news report, factual article|名词
