diamonds:/ˈdaɪəməndz/| n.|钻石；菱形；金刚石|A diamond is a precious stone consisting of a clear and colorless crystalline form of pure carbon, the hardest naturally occurring substance.|例句：She received a diamond ring as a gift. 她收到一枚钻石戒指作为礼物。|例句：The pattern on the fabric was a series of diamonds.  布料上的图案是一系列菱形。|近义词：gem, jewel|反义词:N/A
