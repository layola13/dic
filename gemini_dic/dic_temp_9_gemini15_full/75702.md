full-blood:/ˈfʊlˌblʌd/| adj. |纯种的，完全的；血统纯正的 |Having parents of the same race or breed.  A full-blooded person is one whose ancestors are all of the same race or ethnicity.  A full-blooded animal is one of unmixed breed. |例句：He is a full-blooded Cherokee. 他是纯种切罗基人。  The horse is a full-blooded thoroughbred. 这匹马是纯种良种马。|近义词：purebred, thoroughbred, unmixed |反义词：mixed-breed, crossbred
