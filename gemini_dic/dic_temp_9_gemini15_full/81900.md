griffith:/ˈɡrɪfɪθ/| n. |格里菲斯（人名）; 格里菲斯峡谷（美国地名）| Griffith is a common surname of Welsh origin.  It refers to a person from a place named Griffith.  The name itself is derived from the Welsh personal name "Gruffydd," meaning "strong lord" or "powerful lord." The name has seen use in various contexts including places and as a personal name.  For example, Griffith Park in Los Angeles is named after Colonel Griffith J.
