lichenism:/ˈlaɪkənɪzəm/| n. |地衣共生|The symbiotic relationship between a fungus and an alga or cyanobacterium, resulting in a lichen.  Lichenism describes the ecological interaction and the resulting organism.  It's a mutually beneficial relationship where the fungus provides structure and protection, while the alga or cyanobacterium photosynthesizes, providing food. |Example:  The study of lichenism revealed the intricate interdependence of fungi and algae.  地衣共生的研究揭示了真菌和藻类之间复杂的相互依赖关系。|近义词：symbiosis, mutualism|反义词：parasitism, antagonism|名词
