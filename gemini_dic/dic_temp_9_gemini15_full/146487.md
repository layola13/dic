overgamble: /ˌoʊvərˈɡæmbəl/ | v. | 赌博过度；沉迷赌博 | To gamble excessively or compulsively.  | He overgambled and lost all his savings. 他赌博过度，输掉了所有积蓄。 | gamble excessively,  excessively gamble | refrain from gambling, stop gambling | v.


USAGE->

He overgambles every weekend. = 他每个周末都过度赌博。
She overgambles to escape her problems. = 她沉迷赌博以逃避她的问题。
