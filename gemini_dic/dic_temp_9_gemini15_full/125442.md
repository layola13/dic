multiples:/ˈmʌltɪplz/| n.|倍数，多个|The word "multiples" refers to numbers that are the product of a given number and any integer. For example, multiples of 3 are 3, 6, 9, 12, and so on.|例句：The multiples of 5 are 5, 10, 15, 20, and so on.  5的倍数是5, 10, 15, 20等等。

The store offers multiples of the same item at a discount. 这家商店提供多件相同商品享受折扣。|近义词：multitude, many, several|反义词：single, one, unit
