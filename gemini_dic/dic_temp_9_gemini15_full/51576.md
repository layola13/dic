deutoplasm:/ˈdjuːtəʊplæzəm/| n. |卵黄；滋养层|The nutritive yolk of an egg, providing nourishment for the developing embryo.  It's a substance rich in lipids and proteins.|The deutoplasm provides the energy and building blocks for the developing embryo until it can obtain nutrients from its environment.|近义词：yolk, vitellus|反义词：germ plasm
