lobsterish: /ˈlɒbstərɪʃ/ | adj. |像龙虾的；粗鲁的；贪婪的 | Resembling a lobster; especially, in color or movement.  Also, characterized by greed or roughness. |例句：The lobsterish red of the sunset painted the sky.  日落时分，天空被龙虾般的红色染红了。

The chef prepared a lobsterish red sauce.  厨师准备了一种龙虾红色的酱汁。


近义词: crustacean-like (if referring to appearance), greedy, grasping, avaricious, rough, rude, boorish |反义词: refined, polite, gentle, generous
