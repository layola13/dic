mummied:/ˈmʌmid/ | v. | 制成木乃伊；用绷带包扎 | To wrap (a body) in cloth bandages, especially as part of the process of mummification.  | Example: The ancient Egyptians mummied their dead. 古代埃及人将他们的死者制成木乃伊。  The surgeon mummied his injured leg with bandages. 外科医生用绷带包扎了他的受伤的腿。|近义词：embalm, wrap | 反义词：unravel, expose
