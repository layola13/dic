sermonises:/ˈsɜːrmənaɪzɪz/| v.|说教；教训；冗长地训斥|To deliver a sermon or preach; to talk in a long, tedious, and moralizing way.|He sermonises endlessly about the importance of hard work. 他没完没了地唠叨努力工作的重要性。|近义词：preach, lecture, moralize|反义词：praise, encourage


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He sermonises every Sunday. = 他每个星期天都讲道。
She sermonises to her children about good manners. = 她对她孩子们说教良好礼貌的重要性。


一般过去时:
The priest sermonised on the importance of forgiveness. = 牧师就宽恕的重要性进行了布道。
My grandfather sermonised to me about the dangers of smoking. = 我爷爷对我讲吸烟的危害。


现在完成时:
He has sermonised on this topic many times before. = 他以前多次就这个话题进行过说教。
She has sermonised to her students about the importance of studying hard. = 她多次告诫她的学生要努力学习的重要性。


过去完成时:
He had sermonised for hours before the congregation finally dispersed. = 他已经讲道数小时，之后会众才最终散去。
She had sermonised about responsibility before her son finally listened. = 在她儿子最终听话之前，她已经就责任问题进行了说教。


将来时:
The pastor will sermonise on the parable of the Good Samaritan. = 牧师将就好撒玛利亚人的比喻进行布道。
He will sermonise again next week about the dangers of social media. = 他下周将再次就社交媒体的危害进行说教。


虚拟语气:
If he sermonised less, people might listen more. = 如果他少说教一些，人们可能会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't sermonise so much. = 我希望他不那么爱说教。


被动语态:
The topic was sermonised upon extensively by the speaker. = 演讲者对该主题进行了广泛的说教。
His views were often sermonised against by his colleagues. = 他的观点经常受到同事们的反对。


疑问句:
Does he often sermonise on political issues? = 他经常就政治问题说教吗？
What will the preacher sermonise about tomorrow? = 传道者明天将就什么进行布道？


否定句:
He doesn't sermonise as much as he used to. = 他不像以前那样经常说教了。
She didn't sermonise to her daughter about her poor grades. = 她没有因为女儿的差成绩而对她进行说教。


PHRASE->
sermonise endlessly  没完没了地说教
sermonise at length  冗长地训斥
sermonise on a topic  就某个话题进行说教
sermonise to someone  对某人说教
sermonise about something 就某事说教
