chinquapin:/ˈtʃɪŋkəpɪn/| n. |栗子；美国山毛榉；美国七叶树|A small, sweet chestnut, especially the nut of the American chinquapin (Castanea pumila).  The term can also refer to the tree itself.  Chinquapins are smaller than typical chestnuts.|例句：We roasted chinquapins over the campfire. = 我们在篝火上烤了栗子。|例句：The chinquapin tree bore a plentiful harvest this year. = 今年山毛榉树结了很多果实。|近义词：chestnut (in some contexts)|反义词：无|名词
