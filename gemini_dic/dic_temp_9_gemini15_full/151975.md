pasts:/pæsts/ | n. |过去，过去的事情 |The pasts of many individuals are filled with both joy and sorrow. 许多人的过去都充满了喜悦和悲伤。|近义词：history, yesteryear |反义词：future


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The past shapes our present. = 过去塑造了我们的现在。
He often talks about his past. = 他经常谈论他的过去。


一般过去时:
We discussed our pasts yesterday. = 我们昨天讨论了我们的过去。
She regretted her past mistakes. = 她后悔她过去的错误。


现在完成时:
I have learned much from my past experiences. = 我从过去的经历中学到了很多。
They have overcome many challenges in their pasts. = 他们克服了他们过去中的许多挑战。


过去完成时:
Before he met her, he had buried his past. = 在遇到她之前，他已经埋葬了他的过去。
By the time she graduated, she had left her troubled past behind. = 到她毕业的时候，她已经把痛苦的过去抛在了脑后。


将来时:
Historians will study the pasts of this era. = 史学家们将研究这个时代的过去。
She believes her future will be brighter than her past. = 她相信她的未来会比她的过去更光明。


虚拟语气:
If I had known my past, I would have made different choices. = 如果我知道我的过去，我会做出不同的选择。
I wish I could change my past. = 我希望我能改变我的过去。


被动语态:
The past is often misrepresented in history books. = 过去常常在历史书中被歪曲。
Our pasts are shaped by the forces beyond our control. = 我们的过去是由我们无法控制的力量塑造的。


疑问句:
What aspects of your past are you most proud of? = 你过去最引以为傲的是什么？

否定句:
His past is not something he often discusses. = 他的过去并不是他经常讨论的东西。
She doesn't dwell on her past mistakes. = 她不会沉湎于过去的错误。
