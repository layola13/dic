connectors:/kəˈnɛktərz/ | n. |连接器，连接词 |Words, phrases, or clauses that connect other parts of a sentence or paragraph.  They show the relationship between ideas. Examples include conjunctions (and, but, or), adverbs (however, therefore, moreover), and prepositions (in, on, at).|例句：The connectors in this essay are well-chosen, making the argument flow smoothly. 这篇文章中的连接词选择得很好，使论证流畅自然。

例句：Using appropriate connectors helps improve the clarity and coherence of your writing. 使用合适的连接词有助于提高写作的清晰度和连贯性。


近义词：linkers, conjunctions, transitions |反义词：disconnectors (not a common word) |词性：名词
