robe:/roʊb/| n.|长袍，浴袍|A long, loose piece of clothing, often worn as a dressing gown or ceremonial garment.|例句：She wore a silk robe to the ceremony. 她穿着丝绸长袍参加仪式。|例句：He put on his robe and slippers before bed. 他睡前穿上了浴袍和拖鞋。|近义词：gown, cloak, dressing gown|反义词：None specifically, depends on context.
