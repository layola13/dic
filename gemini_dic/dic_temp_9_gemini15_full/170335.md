providers:/prəˈvaɪdərz/| n.|提供者，供应者，服务商| A person or company that provides something, especially a service.  |例句：Many internet providers offer high-speed broadband. 许多互联网服务提供商提供高速宽带。|例句：The hospital relies on several providers for medical equipment. 这家医院依靠几家供应商提供医疗设备。|近义词：suppliers, vendors, issuers|反义词：recipients, consumers
