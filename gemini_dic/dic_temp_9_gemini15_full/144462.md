outtalked:/ˌaʊtˈtɔːkt/| v. |说服；辩论中胜过 |To defeat or surpass someone in an argument or discussion by speaking more persuasively or effectively.  |例句：He completely outtalked his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中完全压倒了他的对手。

例句：The experienced negotiator outtalked the younger representative, securing a favorable deal.  经验丰富的谈判代表凭借更强的口才说服了年轻的代表，从而达成了一项有利的协议。


近义词：outspeak, outargue,  surpass, defeat


反义词：be outtalked, be defeated in argument


词性：动词
