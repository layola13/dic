nature: /ˈneɪtʃər/ | n. | 自然；性质；本性 | The inherent character or basic constitution of a person or thing.  |例句：The beauty of nature is breathtaking.  大自然的美丽令人叹为观止。

例句：Human nature is complex and unpredictable. 人性复杂且难以预测。


近义词：environment, essence, character, disposition | 反义词：nurture, artificiality


词源:  The word "nature" comes from the Old French word "nature," which in turn derives from the Latin word "natura," meaning "birth," "natural course of things," or "essential qualities."  The Latin word is related to the verb "nasci," meaning "to be born."
