fooling-around: /ˈfʊlɪŋ əˈraʊnd/ | v. |胡闹，瞎玩，浪费时间 | To spend time idly or playfully, often without a specific purpose or achieving anything significant. It often implies a lack of seriousness or focus. | 例句：They were fooling around in the park all afternoon. 他们整个下午都在公园里瞎玩。

They're fooling around with dangerous chemicals.  他们在玩弄危险的化学品。


近义词：playing around, messing about, goofing off, horsing around | 反义词：working hard, being serious, concentrating


USAGE->

They are fooling around in class. = 他们在课堂上胡闹。
We are fooling around with the new software. = 我们正在玩弄新的软件。

They were fooling around yesterday. = 他们昨天在胡闹。
