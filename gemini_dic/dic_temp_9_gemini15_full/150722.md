paracentric:/ˌpærəˈsɛntrɪk/| adj.|臂外侧的；着丝粒外的| Relating to or denoting the region of a chromosome outside the centromere.  Paracentric inversions, for example, involve chromosome segments that do not include the centromere. | 例句：The researchers observed a paracentric inversion on chromosome 7.  研究人员观察到7号染色体上存在臂外侧倒位。

The study focused on the effects of paracentric inversions on meiosis. 这项研究的重点是臂外侧倒位对减数分裂的影响。| 近义词：extra-centromeric | 反义词：pericentric
