professionals:/prəˈfɛʃənəlz/ | n. | 专业人士，专业人员 | People who are skilled and trained in a particular job or activity.  They typically require specialized education or training. | Example: The project requires experienced professionals in various fields. 这个项目需要各个领域的经验丰富的专业人士。  The conference brought together professionals from around the globe.  这次会议汇集了来自世界各地的专业人士。|近义词：experts, specialists, practitioners |反义词：amateurs, novices, dilettantes |名词
