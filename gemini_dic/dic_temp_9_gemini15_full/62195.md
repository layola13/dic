encoring:/ɛnˈkɔːrɪŋ/| n.|加演；再演；安可|The act of performing something again, especially in response to audience demand.  It often refers to a musical performance repeated at the end of a concert. |例句：The audience demanded an encoring of their favorite song.  观众要求再次演奏他们最喜欢的歌曲。|近义词：repeat performance, encore, rerun |反义词：N/A
