petrology:/pɪˈtrɒlədʒi/ | n. |岩石学|The branch of geology that deals with the origin, composition, structure, and classification of rocks.  |例句：Petrology is a fascinating field of study that helps us understand the Earth's history. 岩石学是一个迷人的研究领域，它帮助我们了解地球的历史。

例句：The petrology of this region reveals a complex geological history involving volcanic activity and sedimentation. 这个区域的岩石学揭示了一个复杂的地球历史，包括火山活动和沉积作用。


近义词：lithology (though lithology focuses more on the description of rocks) | 反义词：无
