leapingly: /ˈliːpɪŋli/ | adv. |飞跃地；跳跃地；迅速地 |  The word "leapingly" is an adverb formed from the verb "leap," meaning to jump or spring a long way. It describes an action performed with leaps or in a manner suggestive of leaping.  It often implies a rapid or sudden movement. | 例句：The deer leapedingly crossed the field.  鹿飞跃般地穿过田野。  The stock prices rose leapingly after the announcement.  公告发布后，股价飞涨。 | 近义词：rapidly, swiftly, suddenly, dramatically | 反义词：slowly, gradually, steadily | 副词
