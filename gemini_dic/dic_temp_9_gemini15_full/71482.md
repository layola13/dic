flammation:/ˌflæˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 炎症 | The process of inflammation is the body's response to injury or infection, characterized by redness, swelling, heat, and pain.  Inflammation is a vital part of the body's healing process, but chronic inflammation can be harmful. | Example:  The doctor diagnosed the swelling in his ankle as inflammation.  医生诊断他脚踝的肿胀为炎症。

The inflammation in her throat made it difficult to swallow. 她喉咙的炎症使她吞咽困难。|  近义词： swelling, redness, irritation | 反义词： healing, resolution
