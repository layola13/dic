noncategorically:/ˌnɒnkætɪˈɡɒrɪkli/ | adv. |非明确地，不确定地 |Not categorically; without making a definite statement or classification. It implies a lack of certainty or a refusal to commit to a specific category or position.  |例句：He responded noncategorically to the question about his involvement. 他含糊其辞地回答了关于他参与的问题。  |近义词：uncertainly, ambiguously, vaguely, equivocally |反义词：categorically, definitely, explicitly, unequivocally
