knelling:/ˈnelɪŋ/| v. | 鸣钟；敲丧钟 | The act of ringing a bell, especially a large bell, slowly and solemnly, often to announce a death or mark a solemn occasion.  | 例句：The bells were knelling for the fallen soldiers. 丧钟为阵亡将士鸣响。  | 例句：The slow knelling of the church bell announced the sad news.  教堂的丧钟缓慢地敲响，宣布了这个悲伤的消息。| 近义词：tolling, ringing, sounding | 反义词：silent, still


USAGE->

The church knells every hour. = 教堂每小时鸣钟。
He knells the bell for the dead. = 他为死者敲响丧钟。

一般过去时:
