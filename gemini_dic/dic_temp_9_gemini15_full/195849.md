senhora: /seˈnjoɾɐ/ | n. | 女士，夫人 | Portuguese word for "madam" or "Mrs.", a polite and formal term of address for a woman.  It's equivalent to "madam" or "Mrs." in English.  It can be used in various contexts, from formal business settings to everyday interactions. | Example:  Boa tarde, senhora.  = Good afternoon, madam.


例句：

A senhora está bem? = Are you alright, madam?

A senhora quer alguma ajuda? = Would you like some help, madam?


近义词：dama, madame (in some contexts)

反义词：senhor (sir, gentleman)


词源:  Derived from the Portuguese word "senhor," meaning "sir" or "gentleman."  The feminine suffix "-a" is added to create the feminine form "senhora."  The root word likely originates from Latin "senior," meaning "elder" or "older."


用法说明：

Senhora is a noun used as a term of address.  It's always used with the formal "você" (you) form in Portuguese. It is generally considered more formal than other terms like "moça" (young woman) or "mulher" (woman).  It shows respect and politeness. There's no specific grammatical tense associated with the word itself since it's a noun.  However, the verb tense in the sentence where it's used will determine the overall tense.


例句使用不同时态：

一般现在时:
A senhora vive aqui? = Do you live here, madam?
A senhora gosta de café? = Do you like coffee, madam?


一般过去时:
A senhora viu o meu carro? = Did you see my car, madam?
A senhora esteve aqui ontem? = Were you here yesterday, madam?


现在完成时:
A senhora já comeu? = Have you already eaten, madam?
A senhora já visitou o Brasil? = Have you visited Brazil, madam?


过去完成时:
A senhora já tinha saído quando cheguei? = Had you already left when I arrived, madam?
A senhora já tinha terminado o trabalho? = Had you already finished the work, madam?


将来时:
A senhora virá amanhã? = Will you come tomorrow, madam?
A senhora viajará para o exterior? = Will you travel abroad, madam?


虚拟语气:
Se a senhora soubesse, me diria. = If you knew, you would tell me, madam.
Eu desejaria que a senhora estivesse aqui. = I wish you were here, madam.


被动语态:
A senhora foi convidada para a festa. = You were invited to the party, madam.
A senhora será informada sobre o resultado. = You will be informed of the result, madam.

否定句:
