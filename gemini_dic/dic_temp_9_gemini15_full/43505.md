crave:/kreɪv/| vt.|渴望，热切地希望|to have a very strong feeling of wanting something:|例句：I crave chocolate. 我渴望巧克力。
例句：She craved excitement and adventure. 她渴望刺激和冒险。|近义词：long for, yearn for, desire|反义词：dislike, abhor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I crave a vacation. = 我渴望假期。
They crave success. = 他们渴望成功。

一般过去时:
He craved attention as a child. = 他小时候渴望关注。
She craved a simpler life. = 她渴望更简单的生活。

现在完成时:
I have craved this book for years. = 我多年来一直渴望这本书。
We have craved change for too long. = 我们渴望改变太久了。

过去完成时:
She had craved freedom before she finally escaped. = 在她最终逃脱之前，她一直渴望自由。
They had craved a better future for their children. = 他们一直渴望为他们的孩子创造一个更美好的未来。


将来时:
I will crave comfort food when I'm feeling down. = 当我心情低落时，我会渴望吃安慰食物。
They will crave knowledge throughout their lives. = 他们一生都会渴望知识。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would crave a quiet life. = 如果我是你，我会渴望平静的生活。
I wish I didn't crave sugar so much. = 我希望我不那么渴望糖。

被动语态:
(Crave is rarely used passively)  This kind of freedom is craved by many. = 许多人渴望这种自由。(被动语态，但较少见)
Peace is craved by all. =  所有人都渴望和平。(被动语态，但较少见)


疑问句:
What do you crave most in life? = 你生活中最渴望什么？
Does he crave recognition for his work? = 他渴望得到对他工作的认可吗？

否定句:
I don't crave fame or fortune. = 我不渴望名利。
They didn't crave power, only peace. = 他们不渴望权力，只渴望和平。


PHRASE->
crave for sth: 渴望某物
crave something sweet: 渴望甜食
crave attention: 渴望关注
crave excitement: 渴望刺激
crave solitude: 渴望独处

词源:
The word "crave" comes from the Old English word "cravian," meaning "to ask earnestly," "to demand," or "to beg."  This in turn is likely related to Proto-Germanic roots suggesting a sense of pressing need.  The modern usage has evolved to focus more on a strong internal desire or yearning.
