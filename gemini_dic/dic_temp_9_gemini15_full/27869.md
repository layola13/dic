canopies:/ˈkænəpiz/| n.|树冠；华盖；天篷|A canopy is a covering, like a roof, that is made of cloth or other material and is supported by poles or other structures. It can provide shade or protection from the elements.  The term can also refer to the leafy covering formed by the branches and leaves of trees. |例句：The trees formed a dense canopy overhead. 树木在头顶形成了茂密的树冠。

The wedding reception was held under a beautiful canopy. 婚礼招待会在美丽的华盖下举行。|近义词：cover, shade, awning|反义词：open space, exposure
