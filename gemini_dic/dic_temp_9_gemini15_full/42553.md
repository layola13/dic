countermyth's:/ˈkaʊntərmɪθz/| n.|反神话；对神话的驳斥|A countermyth is a narrative or belief that directly contradicts or challenges a widely held myth or misconception.  It often aims to correct inaccuracies or biases present in the original myth.  The plural form 'countermyths' refers to multiple such narratives.|例句：Many countermyths have emerged to challenge the traditional narrative of the American Revolution.  许多反神话出现来挑战美国革命的传统叙述。|近义词：refutation, rebuttal, counter-narrative|反义词：myth, legend, fable|名词
