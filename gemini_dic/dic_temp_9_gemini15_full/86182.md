heat:/hiːt/ | /hiːt/| n., v.| 热；加热；激动|

英文解析:Heat can refer to the quality of being hot, the sensation of warmth, or the process of making something hot.  As a verb, it means to make something hot or warm.

例句:
The heat was unbearable.  = 热得让人受不了。

The sun heated the sand. = 太阳晒热了沙子。


近义词: warmth, hotness, fever, excitement

反义词: cold, coolness, chill


词性:Noun, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun heats the earth. = 太阳加热地球。
She heats the milk before she drinks it. = 她喝牛奶之前会先加热。


一般过去时:
