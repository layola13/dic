leaderlessness:/ˈliːdərləsnəs/| n. |缺乏领导；无领导状态|The absence of effective leadership; a state or condition characterized by the lack of a leader or leaders.  This can result in disorganization, inefficiency, and conflict. |例句：The team's leaderlessness led to a series of poor decisions.  团队的无领导状态导致了一系列糟糕的决定。

例句：The company suffered from leaderlessness after the CEO's sudden resignation.  首席执行官突然辞职后，公司陷入了无领导状态。


近义词：lack of leadership, absence of leadership, disorganization, chaos |反义词：strong leadership, good leadership, effective management|名词
