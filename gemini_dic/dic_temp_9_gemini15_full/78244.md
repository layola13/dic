geriatric:/ˌdʒerɪˈætrɪk/| adj.|老年人的；老年病学的|Relating to or characteristic of old age, especially with reference to the medical care of older people.  The term often implies a focus on the medical and social needs of the elderly. |例句：He works at a geriatric hospital. 他在一所老年医院工作。

例句：She's a geriatric nurse specializing in dementia care. 她是一位专门从事痴呆症护理的老年护理人员。


近义词：elderly, senile (in a negative connotation), aged

反义词：youthful, young

词源:
From Greek *γηρoς (gēros) "old man," + -iatric (suffix meaning "pertaining to the treatment of").  The suffix "-iatric" is derived from the Greek word ἰατρικός (iatrikós), meaning "medical" or "relating to healing".
