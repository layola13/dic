gamify:/ˈɡeɪmɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 使游戏化；赋予游戏特性 | To apply game-design elements and game principles in non-game contexts.  This involves incorporating elements such as points, badges, leaderboards, and storytelling to motivate and engage users. | Example: The company gamified its employee training program to increase participation. 公司将员工培训项目游戏化以提高参与度。 | 近义词：ludify, make playful | 反义词：de-gamify,  make serious


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They gamify their marketing campaigns. = 他们将他们的营销活动游戏化。
We gamify the learning process. = 我们将学习过程游戏化。

一般过去时:
The teacher gamified the lesson to make it more engaging. = 老师将这节课游戏化，使其更具吸引力。
The company gamified its onboarding process last year. = 公司去年将入职流程游戏化了。

现在完成时:
They have gamified several of their apps. = 他们已经将几个应用程序游戏化了。
