police-woman's: /pəˈliːswʊmən(z)/ | n. | 女警的；女警所属的 |  The possessive form of "policewoman," indicating ownership or belonging to a policewoman.  It's used to show possession or relationship.  For example,  "the police-woman's badge" refers to the badge belonging to a specific policewoman.  | 例句：The police-woman's report detailed the events of the night. = 女警的报告详细描述了当晚发生的事件。


The police-woman's car was parked outside the station. = 女警的车停在警局外面。


近义词：policewoman's (less formal), female officer's | 反义词：无 | 词性：名词所有格
