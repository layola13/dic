propounder:/prəˈpaʊndər/ | n. | 提出者；倡导者 | One who propounds; one who puts forward a theory, proposition, or plan.  | The propounder of the new theory was a relatively unknown scientist.  这位新理论的提出者是一位相对不知名的科学家。 | advocate, proponent, originator | opponent, critic | 名词

She propounds her ideas with great passion. = 她充满热情地阐述她的观点。
