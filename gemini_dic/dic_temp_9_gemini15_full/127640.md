needlework:/ˈniːdlˌwɜːrk/| n.|针线活，刺绣|The art or activity of creating decorative or practical items using a needle and thread.  It encompasses various techniques like sewing, embroidery, knitting, crocheting, and quilting. |例句：Her needlework is exquisite; she creates the most beautiful tapestries.|近义词：embroidery, sewing, stitchery|反义词：None
