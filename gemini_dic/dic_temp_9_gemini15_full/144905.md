over-speeding:/ˌoʊvərˈspiːdɪŋ/ | v. |超速行驶|The act of driving or traveling faster than the legally permitted or safe speed.|例句：He was fined for over-speeding on the highway. 他因在高速公路上超速而被罚款。|例句：Over-speeding can lead to accidents and injuries. 超速行驶会导致事故和受伤。|近义词：speeding, exceeding the speed limit|反义词：underspeeding, driving slowly
