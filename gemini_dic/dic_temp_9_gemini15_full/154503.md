perpetuate:/pərˈpetʃueɪt/| vt.|使永久存在；使继续下去|To make something continue for a long time.  |例句：His actions perpetuated the myth. 他的行为使这个神话延续了下来。|例句：They perpetuate harmful stereotypes through their advertising. 他们的广告宣传延续了有害的刻板印象。|近义词：continue, maintain, prolong|反义词：end, terminate, abolish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They perpetuate the myth. = 他们使这个神话延续下去。
He perpetuates harmful stereotypes. = 他延续着有害的刻板印象。


一般过去时:
The war perpetuated suffering. = 那场战争使苦难延续下去。
His actions perpetuated the cycle of violence. = 他的行为使暴力循环延续下去。


现在完成时:
They have perpetuated this tradition for centuries. = 他们已经延续这个传统几个世纪了。
We have perpetuated a dangerous misconception. = 我们延续了一个危险的误解。


过去完成时:
The regime had perpetuated its power through fear. = 该政权一直通过恐惧来维持其权力。
They had perpetuated the lie for years. = 他们多年来一直延续着这个谎言。


将来时:
This policy will perpetuate inequality. = 这项政策将使不平等持续下去。
Their actions will perpetuate the problem. = 他们的行为将会使问题持续下去。


虚拟语气:
If they hadn't intervened, the conflict would have perpetuated itself. = 如果他们没有干预，冲突就会自行延续下去。
If we were to perpetuate this system, we would face dire consequences. = 如果我们继续维持这个制度，我们将面临可怕的后果。


被动语态:
The myth was perpetuated by generations of storytelling. = 这个神话被几代人的口头流传延续了下来。
The harmful stereotype is perpetuated in the media. = 有害的刻板印象在媒体中被延续。


疑问句:
Does this policy perpetuate inequality? = 这项政策是否会使不平等持续下去？
Will their actions perpetuate the cycle of violence? = 他们的行为会使暴力循环持续下去吗？


否定句:
We should not perpetuate harmful stereotypes. = 我们不应该延续有害的刻板印象。
They did not intend to perpetuate the misunderstanding. = 他们无意延续误解。


PHRASE->
perpetuate a lie = 延续谎言
perpetuate injustice = 使不公正持续
perpetuate a system = 维持一个制度
perpetuate oneself = 使自己永存
perpetuate the memory of = 使…的记忆永存
