prudentness: /ˈpruːdəntnəs/ | n. |谨慎；审慎；精明 | The quality of being prudent; cautiousness; discretion.  It involves making sensible and careful decisions, showing foresight, and avoiding unnecessary risks. |例句：Her prudentness saved them from financial ruin.  她的谨慎使他们免于经济破产。

例句：The prudentness of his investment strategy ensured a comfortable retirement. 他投资策略的谨慎确保了他舒适的退休生活。


近义词：caution, care, foresight, wisdom, circumspection

反义词：recklessness, carelessness, imprudence, rashness


词性：名词


词源:  Originates from the Latin word "prudens," meaning "foreseeing," "provident," or "skillful."  The suffix "-ness" adds the quality or state of being.
