disappointment:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntmənt/ | n. |失望；令人失望的事物 |The feeling of sadness or frustration when something you hoped for does not happen or is not as good as expected.  It often involves unmet expectations or a failure to achieve a desired outcome. |例句：Her disappointment was evident in her downcast eyes.  她的失望从她低垂的眼睛中显而易见。

例句：The poor quality of the product was a great disappointment.  产品的低劣质量令人非常失望。


近义词：discouragement, letdown, frustration, dissatisfaction |反义词：satisfaction, fulfillment, delight
