nonforbearingly:/ˌnɒnfəˈbɛərɪŋli/| adv. |不宽容地；毫不容忍地 |Not showing forbearance; without leniency or tolerance.  This word is extremely rare and not commonly used in modern English.  It implies a harsh, unrelenting, and unforgiving attitude or action. |例句：He spoke nonforbearingly about the mistakes of his subordinates. 他毫不留情地谈论下属的错误。 He reacted nonforbearingly to her criticism. 他对她的批评毫不容忍。|近义词：unforgivingly, relentlessly, harshly, severely, intolerantly |反义词：forbearingly, tolerantly, leniently, compassionately
