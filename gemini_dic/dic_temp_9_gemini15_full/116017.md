marshmallows:/ˈmɑːʃˌmæloʊz/ | n. |棉花糖 | A confection made of sugar, corn syrup, and gelatin, whipped to a fluffy consistency.  Often flavored and coated in various ways.  | Example:  The children roasted marshmallows over the campfire. 孩子们在篝火上烤棉花糖。 | 近义词: confection, sweet | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑与“苦味食物”对比)
