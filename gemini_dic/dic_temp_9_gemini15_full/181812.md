reliquidates:/ˌriˈlɪkwɪdeɪts/| v.| 再清算，重新清算 | To liquidate again; to settle accounts a second time.  This word is rarely used and is essentially a combination of "re-" (again) and "liquidates".  It implies a second process of settling debts or accounts, often suggesting a more thorough or complete process than the initial liquidation. |例句：The company, after an initial liquidation attempt, was forced to reliquidate its assets to satisfy remaining creditors.  公司在第一次清算尝试失败后，被迫再次清算其资产以满足剩余债权人的要求。


The bankrupt company had to reliquidate its remaining assets. 破产公司不得不再次清算其剩余资产。| 近义词：re-settle, reassess, re-evaluate | 反义词：accumulate, amass
