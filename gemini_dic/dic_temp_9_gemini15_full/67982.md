fact:/fækt/| n. |事实，现实|a thing that is known to be true, to have happened, or to exist.  A fact is something that can be proven to be true.|The fact is, he lied to us. 事实是，他骗了我们。| truth, reality, actuality | fiction, falsehood, lie | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fact remains that he is guilty. = 事实仍然是他有罪。
This fact supports our theory. = 这个事实支持我们的理论。


一般过去时:
The fact emerged during the investigation. = 事实是在调查期间出现的。
That fact was never revealed. =  那个事实从未被揭露。


现在完成时:
The facts have confirmed our suspicions. = 事实已经证实了我们的怀疑。
Many facts have come to light recently. = 最近出现了许多事实。


过去完成时:
The fact had already been established. = 事实已经被确定了。
Before the trial, that fact had been concealed. = 在审判之前，那个事实已被隐瞒。


将来时:
The report will reveal the facts of the case. = 报告将揭示案件的事实。
We will soon know the facts. = 我们很快就会知道事实了。


虚拟语气:
If that fact were true, everything would change. = 如果那个事实是真的，一切都会改变。
I wish that fact were different. = 我希望事实有所不同。


被动语态:
The facts were carefully examined. = 事实被仔细检查了。
The fact is being investigated. = 事实正在调查中。


疑问句:
What are the facts of the matter? =  事情的事实是什么？
What fact supports your claim? =  什么事实支持你的说法？


否定句:
That is not a fact. = 那不是事实。
The report failed to present all the facts. = 报告未能呈现所有事实。
