in-balance:/ˌɪmˈbæləns/| adj.|不平衡的，失衡的|lacking proportion or harmony; not evenly balanced.  The imbalance of power led to conflict.|例句：The economy is suffering from a serious trade imbalance.  经济正在遭受严重的贸易逆差之苦。

The scales are in imbalance.  秤是不平衡的。|近义词：unbalanced, uneven, disproportionate|反义词：balance, equilibrium, harmony|词性：adj.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The budget is in imbalance. = 预算失衡。
The seesaw is in imbalance. = 跷跷板不平衡。


一般过去时:
The team was in imbalance after the key player left. = 关键球员离开后，球队实力失衡。
The ecosystem was in imbalance due to pollution. = 由于污染，生态系统失衡。


现在完成时:
The company's finances have been in imbalance for several years. = 公司的财务多年来一直失衡。
The climate has been in imbalance due to global warming. = 由于全球变暖，气候已经失衡。


过去完成时:
The project had been in imbalance since the initial planning stage. = 该项目自规划初期就一直失衡。
The relationship had been in imbalance for a long time before they separated. = 在他们分开之前，他们的关系已经失衡很久了。


将来时:
The market will be in imbalance if the new regulations are implemented. = 如果实施新的法规，市场将失衡。
The political landscape will be in imbalance after the election. = 选举之后，政治格局将失衡。


虚拟语气:
If the resources were distributed evenly, the system wouldn't be in imbalance. = 如果资源分配均匀，系统就不会失衡。
I wish the workload wasn't in imbalance; it's unfair. = 我希望工作量不要失衡；这不公平。


被动语态:

The ecosystem is considered to be in imbalance by scientists. = 科学家认为生态系统失衡。


疑问句:

否定句:
