mincer:/ˈmɪnsər/ | n. v. |绞肉机；切碎机；（尤指说话）含糊其辞的人 | A machine for mincing meat; someone who speaks vaguely or evasively. |例句：She used a mincer to make the meat filling. 她用绞肉机做肉馅。  He was a mincer, avoiding direct answers. 他是个含糊其辞的人，避免直接回答。|近义词：grinder, chopper, obfuscator |反义词：clear speaker, straightforward person |名词，动词
