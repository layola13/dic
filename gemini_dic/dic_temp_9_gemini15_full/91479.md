hymen:/ˈhaɪmən/| n.|处女膜；（生物）膜|A thin membrane that partially covers the opening of the vagina in some women.  Its presence or absence is not a reliable indicator of virginity.  The hymen can be broken by various activities, not just sexual intercourse.|例句：The hymen is a thin membrane that partially covers the opening of the vagina.  处女膜是部分覆盖阴道开口的薄膜。

The doctor examined the hymen during the routine checkup. 医生在例行检查中检查了处女膜。|近义词：membrane, film|反义词：无
