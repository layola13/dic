hakim:/ˈhækiːm/| n.|哈基姆（阿拉伯语名字），法官，统治者|A hakim is a title meaning "wise one" or "ruler" in Arabic, often used as a personal name or title for a judge or governor.  The word also refers to a doctor or physician, particularly in some parts of the Middle East and South Asia.|Example:  My uncle, Hakim, is a respected judge in the city. 我的叔叔哈基姆是城里一位受人尊敬的法官。|近义词：judge, ruler, governor, physician, doctor|反义词：subject, ruled, subordinate|名词
