noncreditable: /ˌnɒnˈkredɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可记入学分的；不可信的；不值得赞扬的 | Not eligible for academic credit; unworthy of belief or praise.  |


例句：

That course is noncreditable, so it won't count towards your degree. = 那门课不计学分，所以不会算进你的学位。

His claims were deemed noncreditable due to lack of evidence. = 由于缺乏证据，他的说法被认为不可信。


近义词：unacceptable, unworthy, unbelievable, discredited

反义词：creditable, believable, praiseworthy, commendable


词性：形容词
