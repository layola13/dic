help-oneself-to:/ˌhɛlp wʌnˈsɛlf tuː/ | v. | 自取，随便吃/拿 |  To take what one wants or needs, especially food or drink, without asking permission.  | 例句：Help yourself to the sandwiches. = 随便吃三明治吧。  | 例句：Guests are welcome to help themselves to the drinks. = 宾客们可以随意取用饮料。 | 近义词：serve oneself, take freely | 反义词：ask for permission, request


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They help themselves to the buffet. = 他们自取自助餐的食物。
He helps himself to a second helping of dessert. = 他又给自己盛了一份甜点。


一般过去时:
We helped ourselves to the snacks. = 我们自己拿了一些零食。
She helped herself to another cup of coffee. = 她又给自己倒了一杯咖啡。


现在完成时:
They have helped themselves to all the cookies. = 他们已经把所有的饼干都吃掉了。
She has helped herself to the best pieces of cake. = 她已经拿走了最好吃的蛋糕。


过去完成时:
They had helped themselves to the food before the host arrived. = 主人到之前他们就已经自己吃东西了。
He had helped himself to a drink before I could offer him one. = 我还没来得及给他倒饮料，他就自己拿了一杯。


将来时:
They will help themselves to whatever they want. = 他们想吃什么就自己拿什么。
She will help herself to the fruit salad. = 她会自己拿水果沙拉。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would help myself to the ice cream. = 如果我是你，我会自己去拿冰淇淋。
I wish I could help myself to another slice of pizza. = 我真希望我能再吃一片披萨。


被动语态: *(This phrase is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes a self-initiated action.)*


疑问句:
Will you help yourself to more coffee? = 你还要咖啡吗？(This implies an offer, not a direct question about the act of helping oneself)
Would you mind if I helped myself to a cookie? = 我拿块饼干介意吗？


否定句:
They didn't help themselves to the food without asking. = 他们没有不问自取食物。
He didn't help himself to another piece of cake because he was full. = 他因为吃饱了，所以没有再吃一块蛋糕。


PHRASE->
help oneself to the buffet = 自取自助餐
help oneself to drinks = 自取饮料
help oneself to snacks = 自取零食
help oneself to seconds = 再吃一份
help oneself freely = 随意享用
