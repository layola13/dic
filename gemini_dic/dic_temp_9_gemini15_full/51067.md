despair:/dɪˈspɛər/| n.  |绝望，沮丧|The feeling of having lost all hope; a state of hopelessness.  It can refer to a feeling, a state of mind, or a situation causing such a feeling.|例句：After many failed attempts, he fell into despair.  许多次尝试失败后，他陷入了绝望。|例句：Her despair was palpable. 她的绝望显而易见。|近义词：hopelessness, despondency, dejection|反义词：hope, optimism, cheer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He despairs of ever finding a job. = 他绝望地认为自己永远找不到工作。
She despairs over her failing health. = 她为日渐衰弱的健康感到绝望。


一般过去时:
They despaired after the news of the accident. = 事故的消息传来后，他们感到绝望。
He despaired of ever seeing her again. = 他绝望地认为自己再也见不到她了。


现在完成时:
I have despaired many times in my life, but I always find a way to overcome it. = 我一生中多次感到绝望，但我总能找到克服它的方法。
She has despaired of ever finishing the project on time. = 她已经绝望地认为自己永远无法按时完成项目。


过去完成时:
He had despaired of success before he finally achieved his goal. = 在他最终实现目标之前，他已经对成功绝望了。
They had despaired of finding a solution until a breakthrough occurred. = 直到取得突破，他们才对找到解决方案不再绝望。


将来时:
I fear I will despair if this situation continues. = 我担心如果这种情况继续下去，我会绝望。
They will despair if their efforts prove fruitless. = 如果他们的努力证明是徒劳的，他们将会绝望。


虚拟语气:
If I were in your shoes, I would despair. = 如果我是你，我会绝望。
I wish I didn't despair so easily. = 我希望我不那么容易绝望。


被动语态:
(Despair is rarely used in the passive voice.  It is more commonly used as a noun.)  


疑问句:
Does he ever despair? = 他曾经绝望过吗？
Will they despair if the plan fails? = 如果计划失败，他们会绝望吗？


否定句:
She did not despair, even when faced with insurmountable odds. = 即使面对无法克服的困难，她也没有绝望。
He refused to despair despite the challenges. = 尽管面临挑战，他拒绝绝望。


PHRASE->
fall into despair = 陷入绝望
a cause for despair = 绝望的原因
beyond despair = 绝望的
give way to despair = 屈服于绝望
in despair = 绝望地
