cowhiding: /ˈkaʊˌhaɪdɪŋ/ | n. |牛皮革；用牛皮鞭打| The process of tanning cowhide to make leather, or the leather itself.  It can also refer to the act of whipping someone with a cowhide whip. |例句：The craftsman used high-quality cowhiding for the saddle.  这位工匠用优质牛皮革制作马鞍。

The rancher used a cowhiding to discipline his unruly horse.  这位牧场主用牛皮鞭来管教他那匹难以驾驭的马。|近义词：leather, hide, flogging (for the act of whipping)|反义词：None specifically, but  "gentle treatment" could be considered an antonym for the whipping aspect.|名词
