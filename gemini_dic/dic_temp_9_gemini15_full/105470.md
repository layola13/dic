killed:/kɪld/| vt.|杀死，弄死，扼杀|To cause the death of (a person, animal, or other living thing).|例句：The hunter killed a deer.  猎人杀了一只鹿。|例句：The disease killed thousands.  这种疾病夺去了数千人的生命。|近义词：murdered, slaughtered, eliminated|反义词：saved, rescued, preserved


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kills insects. = 他杀虫子。
She kills time by reading. = 她看书消磨时间。

一般过去时:
The police killed the suspect. = 警察击毙了嫌疑犯。
He killed the engine. = 他熄火了。

现在完成时:
The frost has killed the plants. = 霜冻毁掉了这些植物。
She has killed two birds with one stone. = 她一举两得。

过去完成时:
They had killed the enemy before they were captured. = 他们被俘之前已经杀死了敌人。
The news had killed his enthusiasm. = 这个消息让他失去了热情。

将来时:
The storm will kill many people. = 这场暴风雨将夺去许多人的生命。
He will kill the project if he doesn't get the funding. = 如果得不到资金，他会终止这个项目。

虚拟语气:
If he had killed him, he would have been arrested. = 如果他杀了他，他就会被捕。
I wish I hadn't killed the plant. = 我真希望我没有弄死那棵植物。

被动语态:
The criminal was killed in the shootout. = 罪犯在枪战中被击毙。
The project was killed due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，该项目被取消。

疑问句:
Who killed him? = 谁杀了他？
Did he kill anyone? = 他杀过人吗？

否定句:
I didn't kill anyone. = 我没有杀任何人。
He didn't kill the spider. = 他没有杀那只蜘蛛。
