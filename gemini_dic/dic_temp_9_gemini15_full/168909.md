professionalized:/ˌprəˈfɛʃənəlaɪzd/| v.| 使专业化；使职业化 | To make something more professional or to give it the characteristics of a profession.  This often involves formalizing procedures, establishing standards, and increasing the level of expertise required. | Example: The company professionalized its image by redesigning its website and updating its marketing materials.  公司通过重新设计网站和更新营销材料来使其形象专业化。

Example: The army professionalized its training programs to improve the skills of its soldiers.  军队使其训练项目专业化，以提高士兵的技能。


近义词:specialized, formalized, standardized | 反义词:amateurish, unprofessional, casual
