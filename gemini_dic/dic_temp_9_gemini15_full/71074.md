first time:/fɜːrst taɪm/| n.|第一次，首次|It's my first time here. 这是我第一次来这里。|近义词：initial time, maiden voyage|反义词：later time, subsequent time


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This is my first time trying this dish. = 这是我第一次尝试这道菜。
It's his first time driving a car. = 他是第一次开车。


一般过去时:
It was my first time seeing snow. = 那是我第一次看到雪。
It was their first time visiting Europe. = 那是他们第一次访问欧洲。


现在完成时:
This is the first time I have ever been to Paris. = 这是我第一次去巴黎。
It's the first time she has cooked this recipe. = 这是她第一次做这道菜。


过去完成时:
It was the first time I had ever felt so happy. = 那是我第一次感到如此快乐。
That was the first time he had flown on a plane. = 那时是他第一次乘坐飞机。


将来时:
This will be my first time attending such a conference. = 这将是我第一次参加这样的会议。
Next year will be their first time going to college. = 明年将是他们第一次上大学。


虚拟语气:
If this were my first time, I'd be more nervous. = 如果这是我第一次，我会更紧张。
I wish it were my first time so I could experience the excitement again. = 我希望这是我第一次，这样我就能再次体验兴奋感。


被动语态:
The movie's first showing was a success. = 电影的首映很成功。(The movie's first time being shown was a success)
This is the first time the event has been held in this city. = 这是这次活动第一次在这个城市举行。


疑问句:
Is this your first time here? = 这是你第一次来这里吗？
Was that your first time skiing? = 那是不是你第一次滑雪？


否定句:
It wasn't my first time in London. = 这不是我第一次去伦敦。
This isn't their first time dealing with this problem. = 这不是他们第一次处理这个问题。


PHRASE->
for the first time = 第一次
the very first time = 第一次
at the first time = 在第一次
a first-time = 新手的，第一次的
first-time buyer = 首次购房者
