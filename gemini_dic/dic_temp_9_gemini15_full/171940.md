puppeteers:/ˈpʌpɪtɪərz/| n.|木偶表演者；操纵者|A puppeteer is a person who manipulates puppets in a performance.  They are skilled in operating puppets and creating engaging performances.|The puppeteer skillfully manipulated the marionettes, bringing them to life on stage.  木偶表演者熟练地操纵着木偶，让它们在舞台上栩栩如生。| marionetteer, puppeteer, manipulator|  (反义词较抽象，可考虑"audience member"作为相对的，非操纵者)|名词
