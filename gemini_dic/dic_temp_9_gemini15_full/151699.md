partnership:/ˈpɑːtnərʃɪp/| n. |合伙关系；伙伴关系；合作 |A partnership is an association of two or more individuals, groups, or organizations for the purpose of carrying on a business and sharing its profits or losses.  It can refer to a business structure, a collaborative effort, or a general relationship of cooperation. |例句：They formed a partnership to develop new software.他们组建了一个合伙公司来开发新的软件。|例句：The project was a success thanks to the strong partnership between the two teams.  由于两队之间的强有力合作，该项目取得了成功。|近义词：collaboration, alliance, association, joint venture |反义词：competition, rivalry, antagonism
