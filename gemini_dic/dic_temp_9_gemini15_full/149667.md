paisano:/paɪˈzɑːnoʊ/| n.|老乡；同胞；朋友|A paisano is a fellow countryman, especially one from a rural area; it can also be used as a term of friendly address.|例句：He greeted his paisanos warmly upon returning to his village. 他回到村里热情地向他的老乡们打招呼。
例句：After years apart, they reunited like old paisanos. 多年不见，他们像老朋友一样重逢了。|近义词：compatriot, countryman, friend |反义词：stranger, foreigner
