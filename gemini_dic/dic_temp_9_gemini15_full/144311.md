outspread:/ˈaʊtspɹɛd/ | adj. | 展开的；伸开的；广布的 |  Describes something that is spread out or extended to its full width or length. It can refer to physical objects or abstract concepts. |例句：The eagle soared with its wings outspread.  展翅翱翔的老鹰。  The outspread branches of the oak tree provided shade.  橡树伸开的树枝提供了树荫。|近义词：extended, expanded, widespread, sprawling | 反义词：contracted, folded, confined
