self-integrating:/ˌsɛlfˈɪntɪˌɡreɪtɪŋ/| adj.|自整合的，自集成的|  Describing a system or process that integrates itself without external intervention.  This often implies automation and internal consistency.  For example, a self-integrating software system would automatically configure and connect its various components without manual setup. |例句：The new operating system boasts a self-integrating design, simplifying installation and configuration.  新的操作系统拥有自整合设计，简化了安装和配置。


The company developed a self-integrating database system to streamline its operations. 公司开发了一个自整合数据库系统来简化其运营。|近义词：self-contained, autonomous, independent|反义词：requiring external integration, dependent, reliant
