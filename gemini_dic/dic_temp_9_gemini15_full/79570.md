gloomful:/ˈɡluːmfəl/| adj. |忧郁的；阴沉的；令人沮丧的 |Characterized by or expressing gloom; depressing.  The gloomful atmosphere of the old house made me uncomfortable.  |例句：The gloomful weather matched her mood.  阴沉的天气和她糟糕的心情很相配。

例句：The gloomful prophecy foretold a time of great hardship.  那令人沮丧的预言预示着一段艰难的时期。


近义词：depressed, gloomy, melancholy, somber, dreary |反义词：cheerful, bright, sunny, joyful, merry

词源:
The word "gloomful" comes from the Middle English word "glomeful," meaning "dark, gloomy."  The Old English root is "glōm," meaning "twilight," "darkness," or "gloom."  The suffix "-ful" adds the sense of "full of" or "characterized by."
