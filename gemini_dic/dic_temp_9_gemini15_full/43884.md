crestless:/ˈkrɛstləs/| adj.|无冠的，无羽冠的，不显赫的 | Lacking a crest; without a crest.  The term often refers to a bird lacking a crest of feathers, but can also be used metaphorically to describe something lacking distinction or prominence. |例句：The crestless bird looked ordinary among its feathered counterparts. 这只无冠的鸟在其羽毛鲜艳的同类中显得平平无奇。

例句：His crestless ambition led to a life of quiet mediocrity. 他缺乏雄心壮志，过着平庸的生活。


近义词：uncrested, unadorned, undistinguished, humble

反义词：crested, adorned, distinguished, prominent
