fettuccinis:/ˌfɛtəˈtʃiːniz/ | n. |意大利细扁面条|Flat, wide noodles, typically made from egg pasta.  Fettuccine is the singular form.  The plural form, fettuccinis, is sometimes used, particularly in informal settings.|例句：I ordered the fettuccinis with Alfredo sauce. 我点了阿尔弗雷多酱意大利细扁面。

例句：The restaurant is famous for its homemade fettuccinis. 这家餐厅以自制的意大利细扁面而闻名。


近义词：pasta (general term), tagliatelle (similar shape but slightly narrower)
反义词：None (no direct antonym, as it describes a specific type of food)
词性: Noun (plural)
