glandulose:/ɡlændʒuˈloʊs/| adj.|腺状的；具腺毛的|Having the nature or appearance of a gland; glandular.  The term often refers to a plant surface covered with small, glandular hairs or trichomes that secrete substances like oils or resins.|例句：The leaves of the plant were glandulose, indicating the presence of essential oils.  这植物的叶子是腺状的，表明存在精油。The glandulose trichomes on the stem gave it a sticky texture.  茎上的腺毛使它具有粘性。|近义词：glandular, secretory|反义词：glabrous (smooth, hairless)
