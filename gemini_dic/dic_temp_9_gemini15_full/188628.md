ruminated:/ˈruːmɪneɪtɪd/ | v. | 沉思；反复思考；反刍 | To think deeply and at length; to ponder.  It can also refer to the act of chewing cud, as in cows. | 例句：He ruminated on the meaning of life. 他反复思考生命的意义。  She ruminated over her mistakes. 她反复思考自己的错误。 | 近义词：ponder, contemplate, meditate, reflect | 反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ruminates on his future. = 他沉思他的未来。
She ruminates over the problem. = 她反复思考这个问题。


一般过去时:
He ruminated on the events of the day. = 他反复思考当天发生的事情。
She ruminated on her lost love. = 她沉思她失去的爱。


现在完成时:
I have ruminated on this issue for days. = 我已经思考这个问题好几天了。
She has ruminated on the possibilities. = 她已经反复思考各种可能性。


过去完成时:
He had ruminated for hours before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前已经思考了几个小时。
They had ruminated over the proposal before rejecting it. = 他们在拒绝提案之前已经仔细考虑过了。


将来时:
I will ruminate on this matter further. = 我会进一步思考这件事。
She will ruminate on the implications of her actions. = 她将反复思考她行为的影响。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would ruminate more thoroughly. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更仔细地思考。
I wish I had ruminated more before speaking. = 我真希望我在说话之前能够多思考一下。


被动语态:
The problem was ruminated upon by the committee. = 委员会反复思考了这个问题。(较为少见用法)
The implications of the decision are being ruminated. = 正在反复思考这项决定的影响。(较为少见用法)


疑问句:
What are you ruminating on? = 你在思考什么？
Have you ruminated on the consequences? = 你考虑过后果了吗？


否定句:
He did not ruminate on the details. = 他没有仔细思考细节。
She didn't ruminate on the offer before accepting it. = 她接受邀请前没有仔细考虑。


PHRASE->
ruminate on/upon:  反复思考...
ruminate over:  仔细考虑...
ruminate about:  思考...
ruminate the possibilities:  思考各种可能性
ruminate silently:  默默地沉思
