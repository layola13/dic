custodianship:/kʌˈstoʊdiənʃɪp/| n. |监护权；保管；管理 |The legal right to care for someone or something.|The museum has the custodianship of many valuable artifacts.  博物馆拥有许多珍贵文物的保管权。|The school took custodianship of the lost property. 学校保管了遗失的财物。|guardianship, care, keeping|neglect, abandonment|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The museum retains custodianship of the painting. = 博物馆保留着这幅画的保管权。
He exercises custodianship over his younger siblings. = 他对他的弟弟妹妹行使监护权。


一般过去时:
The court awarded custodianship to the grandmother. = 法院判决将监护权授予祖母。
The company relinquished custodianship of the documents. = 公司放弃了这些文件的保管权。


现在完成时:
The family has maintained custodianship of the land for generations. = 这个家族世世代代都拥有这块土地的保管权。
She has held custodianship of the family heirloom for many years. = 她多年来一直保管着家族传家宝。


过去完成时:
Before the trial, the father had lost custodianship of his children. = 在审判之前，父亲已经失去了孩子的监护权。
The organization had previously held custodianship of the archives. = 该组织此前曾保管过这些档案。


将来时:
The judge will decide who will assume custodianship of the child. = 法官将决定谁将承担孩子的监护权。
The library will soon transfer custodianship to a new organization. = 图书馆很快就会将保管权移交给一个新的组织。


虚拟语气:
If the parents were unfit, the court would grant custodianship to a relative. = 如果父母不合格，法院会将监护权授予亲戚。
I wish the museum had better custodianship of its collection. = 我希望博物馆能更好地保管其收藏品。


被动语态:
Custodianship of the property was contested in court. = 该财产的保管权在法庭上受到了争议。
Custodianship was granted to the appointed guardian. = 监护权授予了指定的监护人。


疑问句:
Who has custodianship of the documents? = 谁保管这些文件？
Does the state have custodianship of the historical site? = 国家是否拥有该历史遗址的保管权？


否定句:

The museum does not have custodianship of the artifact.  = 博物馆没有这件文物的保管权。
