immunosuppression:/ˌɪmjuːnoʊsʌˈprɛʃən/ | n. | 免疫抑制 | The suppression of the body's immune system, often as a side effect of drugs or illness.  This can leave the individual vulnerable to infections. | Example:  The patient experienced immunosuppression after receiving a bone marrow transplant.  病人接受骨髓移植后出现了免疫抑制。

The patient's immunosuppression made them susceptible to opportunistic infections. 病人的免疫抑制使他们容易感染机会性感染。


近义词：immune deficiency, immunodeficiency, immunodepression | 反义词：immunostimulation
