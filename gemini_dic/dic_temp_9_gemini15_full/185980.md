rewarranting: /ˌriːˈwɒrəntɪŋ/ | v. | 再担保；重新保证；重新授权 |  The act of warranting again;  giving a new or renewed guarantee or authorization.  It implies a previous warranting that may have expired, been questioned, or needs reinforcement.  The term often appears in legal or financial contexts.

例句：

The bank is considering rewarranting the loan after reviewing the improved financial statements. = 银行在审查了改进后的财务报表后，正在考虑重新担保贷款。

The company decided to rewarrant the product after numerous customer complaints. = 在收到众多客户投诉后，该公司决定重新保证该产品的质量。


近义词：reassuring, guaranteeing, reaffirming, re-authorizing

反义词：disavowing, repudiating, revoking


词性：动词
