overseeds:/ˈoʊvərsiːdz/ | v. | 播撒种子（尤指在现有植被上） | To sow seeds over (existing vegetation), especially to improve or thicken the existing growth.  | Example: The farmer overseeds his pasture with clover to improve the soil.  农民在牧场上播撒三叶草种子以改良土壤。 | 近义词：resow, top-dress, sow | 反义词：harvest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overseeds his lawn every spring. = 他每年春天都在草坪上播撒种子。
They overseed the field with ryegrass. = 他们用黑麦草在田地里补播种子。


一般过去时:
She overseeded the bare patches in her garden. = 她在她花园里光秃秃的地方补播了种子。
The gardener overseeded the lawn after aeration. = 园丁在给草坪松土后播撒了种子。


现在完成时:
