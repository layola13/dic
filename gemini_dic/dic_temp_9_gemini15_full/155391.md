phase:/feɪz/|n.|阶段，时期，相位|A distinct period or stage in a process, series of events, or a person's life.  It can also refer to a particular aspect or stage of development.|例句：The project is in its final phase.  这个项目正处于最后阶段。|例句：He went through a difficult phase after his father's death. 他父亲去世后经历了一段艰难的时期。|近义词：stage, period, step, aspect|反义词：whole, entirety


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is in its planning phase. = 这个项目正处于计划阶段。
This phase requires careful attention to detail. = 这个阶段需要仔细注意细节。


一般过去时:
The first phase of the construction was completed last year. = 建设的第一阶段在去年完成。
We successfully navigated a challenging phase in our business. = 我们成功地度过了我们业务中一个充满挑战的阶段。


现在完成时:
We have completed two phases of the research. = 我们已经完成了研究的两个阶段。
They have passed through many phases of development. = 他们经历了许多发展阶段。


过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, the initial phase had already finished. = 当我到达时，初始阶段已经结束了。
They had already completed the first phase before the funding ran out. = 在资金耗尽之前，他们已经完成了第一阶段。


将来时:
The next phase will begin next month. = 下一阶段将于下个月开始。
We will enter a new phase of negotiations soon. = 我们很快将进入新的谈判阶段。


虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we could complete this phase more quickly. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们可以更快地完成这个阶段。
I wish this difficult phase would end soon. = 我希望这个艰难的阶段尽快结束。


被动语态:
The project's phases are carefully planned and monitored. = 项目的各个阶段都经过仔细的计划和监控。
The different phases were managed by separate teams. = 不同的阶段由不同的团队管理。


疑问句:
What phase of the moon is it tonight? = 今晚是哪个月相？
Which phase of the process are we in right now? = 我们现在处于哪个过程阶段？


否定句:
The project is not yet in its final phase. = 项目尚未进入最后阶段。
We haven't reached that phase of the discussion yet. = 我们还没有到达讨论的这个阶段。


PHRASE->
in phase: 同步的，一致的
out of phase:不同步的，不一致的
transition phase:过渡阶段
initial phase:初始阶段
final phase:最终阶段
