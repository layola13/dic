inch-by-inch: /ɪntʃ baɪ ɪntʃ/ | adj. | 慢慢地，一点一点地 | Gradually, little by little; step by step.  |例句：They advanced inch by inch across the battlefield. 他们在战场上寸步难行地前进。
例句：She painstakingly repaired the damage inch by inch. 她一丝不苟地一点一点地修复了损坏的部分。| 近义词：gradually, slowly, steadily | 反义词：rapidly, quickly, suddenly | 形容词


USAGE->
