remythologizing: /ˌriːmɪθəˈlɒdʒaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |  重新神话化；赋予新的神话意义 | The act of reinterpreting or reimagining existing myths or narratives, often to reflect contemporary concerns or perspectives.  It involves giving new meaning and relevance to old stories. |  例句：The film remythologizes the figure of the superhero, presenting him not as an invincible god but as a flawed and vulnerable human being. 这部电影重新诠释了超级英雄的形象，把他描绘成一个有缺陷且脆弱的人，而不是一个无敌的神。

例句：The author's remythologizing of the national founding myth attempted to address the exclusion of marginalized groups in the traditional narrative.  作者对民族建国神话的重新诠释试图解决传统叙事中边缘群体的缺失问题。


近义词：reimagining, reinterpreting, recasting, updating, revitalizing

反义词：demythologizing, secularizing
