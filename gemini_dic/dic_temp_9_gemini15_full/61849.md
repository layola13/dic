employees:/ɛmˈplɔɪiːz/ | n. | 雇员，员工 | People who work for a company or organization.  | Example: The company employs over 500 employees. 这家公司雇佣了500多名员工。 |近义词:staff, workforce, personnel |反义词:employer, boss


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company employs many employees. = 这家公司雇佣了许多员工。
Our employees work hard. = 我们的员工工作努力。


一般过去时:
The factory employed hundreds of employees last year. = 去年这家工厂雇佣了数百名员工。
They dismissed several employees. = 他们解雇了几名员工。


现在完成时:
The company has employed thousands of employees since its founding. = 自公司成立以来，该公司已雇佣了数千名员工。
We have hired many new employees this year. = 今年我们已经聘用了许多新员工。


过去完成时:
The company had employed over 1000 employees before the merger. = 在合并之前，该公司已雇佣了1000多名员工。
They had already laid off some employees before the announcement. = 他们在宣布之前就已经解雇了一些员工。


将来时:
We will employ more employees next year. = 明年我们将雇佣更多员工。
The company will likely hire additional employees in the spring. = 公司可能会在春天招聘更多员工。


虚拟语气:
If we employed more skilled employees, we would produce more. = 如果我们雇佣更多熟练的员工，我们将生产更多。
I wish we had more employees to help with this project. = 我希望我们有更多员工来帮助这个项目。


被动语态:
Many employees were hired last month. = 上个月雇佣了许多员工。
The employees are being trained on new software. = 员工正在接受新软件的培训。


疑问句:
How many employees does the company have? = 这家公司有多少员工？
Are all the employees satisfied with their jobs? = 所有员工都对他们的工作满意吗？


否定句:
The company does not employ many people. = 这家公司没有雇佣很多人。
They did not hire any new employees this quarter. = 本季度他们没有招聘任何新员工。
