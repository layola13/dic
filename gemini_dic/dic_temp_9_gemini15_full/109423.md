leonitis: /ˌliːəˈnaɪtɪs/ | n. | 狮鬃病 |  A fungal infection of the scalp, characterized by circular patches of hair loss and scaly, inflamed skin.  It is also known as tinea capitis. | Example:  The child was diagnosed with leonitis after experiencing significant hair loss and scalp irritation.  孩子在经历了严重的脱发和头皮刺激后被诊断患有狮鬃病。|近义词：tinea capitis, ringworm (of the scalp)|反义词：无|名词
