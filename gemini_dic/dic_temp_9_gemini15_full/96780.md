inequity:/ɪˈnɛkwɪti/ | n. |不公平；不公正；不平等 | Unfairness or injustice; lack of equity.  It refers to a situation where something is not distributed fairly, often involving a disparity in resources, opportunities, or treatment. |例句：The inequity in wealth distribution is a major social problem.  财富分配的不公正是一个主要的社会问题。

例句：The persistent inequity in access to healthcare is unacceptable. 获得医疗保健机会的持续不平等是不可接受的。


近义词：injustice, unfairness, inequality, disparity

反义词：equity, fairness, justice, equality

词性：名词
