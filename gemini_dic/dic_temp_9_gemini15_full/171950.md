puppily:/ˈpʌpɪli/| adj.|幼犬般地；天真地，幼稚地|  In a way that is characteristic of a puppy; playfully or innocently.  It often implies a slightly naive or immature quality.|例句：She looked at him puppily, her eyes wide with hope. 她天真地望着他，眼睛里充满了希望。


例句：He behaved puppily, begging for another treat. 他像小狗一样撒娇，乞求再给点零食。


近义词：doggishly, childishly, naively, innocently


反义词：maturely, sophisticatedly, world-wearyly


词性：形容词
