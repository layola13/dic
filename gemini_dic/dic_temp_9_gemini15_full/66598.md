exhuming:/ɪɡˈzjuːmɪŋ/| v. |掘出；挖出（尤指尸体）|The act of digging up something, especially a body, from the ground.  It often carries a connotation of investigation or re-examination. |例句：The police are exhuming the body to perform further tests. 警方正在挖掘尸体以进行进一步的检验。

例句：They are exhuming the remains of victims of the war for reburial. 他们正在挖掘战争受害者的遗体以重新安葬。


近义词：unearthing, disinterring |反义词：burying, interring
