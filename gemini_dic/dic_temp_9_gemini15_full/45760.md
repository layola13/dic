cushionet:/ˈkʊʃənɛt/| n.|缓冲垫，减震器|A cushionet is a small cushion or pad used to protect or soften something.  It often provides cushioning or damping.  |例句：The delicate vase was carefully wrapped and cushioned with a soft cushionet.  精美的花瓶被小心地包裹起来，并用柔软的缓冲垫垫好。

例句：The machine's moving parts were fitted with cushionets to reduce noise and vibration.机器的活动部件安装了缓冲垫以减少噪音和振动。


近义词：pad, buffer, damper |反义词：hardener, stiffener


词源:源自于cushion (缓冲垫) + -et (表示小东西的后缀)，表示一个小型的缓冲垫。
