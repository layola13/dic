poseur:/pəˈzɜːr/| n. |装腔作势的人，冒牌货 | A poseur is someone who pretends to be something they are not, especially someone who affects a sophisticated or cultured manner. They try to impress others by imitating the style or behavior of someone they admire, often without genuine understanding or feeling.  |He's such a poseur, always talking about art he doesn't understand. 他是个十足的装腔作势的人，总是谈论他不懂的艺术。|pretender, affected person, phony | genuine person, authentic person


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He poses as an expert. = 他假装自己是专家。
She poses as a sophisticated lady. = 她装作一位精致的女士。


一般过去时:
He posed as a doctor to gain access. = 他假扮医生以获得进入许可。
She posed as a wealthy heiress. = 她假扮成一位富有的继承人。


现在完成时:
He has posed as a different person many times. = 他多次假扮成不同的人。
She has posed as a celebrity for years. = 她多年来一直伪装成名人的身份。


过去完成时:
He had posed as a detective before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，他曾假扮成侦探。
She had posed as a journalist to get the information. = 她曾假扮记者以获取信息。


将来时:
He will pose as a businessman at the conference. = 他将在会议上假扮商人。
She will pose as a student to infiltrate the group. = 她将假扮学生以渗透该组织。


虚拟语气:
If he posed as a rich man, he would attract many friends. = 如果他假扮成富人，他会吸引很多朋友。
If she posed as a famous artist, she might sell more paintings. = 如果她假扮成著名艺术家，可能会卖出更多画作。


被动语态:
He was posed as a hero in the media. = 他在媒体中被塑造成英雄。
She was posed as a victim in the court. = 她被法庭塑造成受害者。


疑问句:
Does he pose as someone else? = 他假扮成其他人吗？
Does she pose as a model? = 她假扮模特吗？


否定句:
He doesn't pose as an intellectual. = 他并不装作知识分子。
She doesn't pose as someone superior. = 她并不装作比别人优越。
