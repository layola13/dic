enrobe:/ɪnˈroʊb/| vt.| 给…穿上长袍；覆盖；笼罩 | To clothe or cover (someone or something) in a robe or something similar.  To envelop or surround. |例句：She enrobes herself in a luxurious silk robe. 她穿着奢华的丝绸长袍。

例句：The mountain was enrobed in mist.  山被雾笼罩着。

近义词：clothe, dress, cover, envelop, shroud

反义词：uncover, undress, reveal

词性:动词
