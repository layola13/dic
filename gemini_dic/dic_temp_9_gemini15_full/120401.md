一般现在时:
The ministers approve the budget annually.  部长们每年批准预算。

虚拟语气:
If the ministers were to agree, the bill would pass. 如果部长们同意，法案就会通过。

被动语态:

疑问句:
Will the ministers support the proposal? 部长们会支持这项提案吗？

否定句:

词源:

From Old French *ministre, from Latin minister "attendant, servant, agent," from minus "less" +  -ter (suffix denoting agent).  The sense evolved from "servant" to "one who serves in a public office."  The word's religious usage ("minister of the gospel") developed later.
