flee:/fliː/| v.|逃跑，逃离|To run away from a place or situation of danger.|例句：The family fled their burning home.  一家人逃离了他们燃烧的房子。|例句：The prisoners fled from the jail. 犯人们从监狱逃跑了。|近义词：escape, run away, abscond|反义词：stay, remain, confront


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flees from danger. = 他逃避危险。
They flee the city every summer. = 他们每年夏天都逃离城市。


一般过去时:
She fled the country during the war. = 战争期间她逃离了那个国家。
The thief fled the scene of the crime. = 小偷逃离了犯罪现场。


现在完成时:
They have fled to the mountains. = 他们已经逃到了山里。
He has fled the country and is now living in exile. = 他已经逃离了国家，现在流亡海外。


过去完成时:
They had fled before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前他们已经逃跑了。
She had fled her abusive husband many years ago. = 多年前她就逃离了虐待她的丈夫。


将来时:
We will flee if the building catches fire. = 如果建筑物着火，我们将逃跑。
They will flee the country if the government falls. = 如果政府垮台，他们将逃离该国。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would flee. = 如果我是你，我会逃跑。
I wish I could flee this terrible situation. = 我真希望我能逃离这种可怕的境地。


被动语态:
The city was fled by many residents after the earthquake. = 地震后许多居民逃离了这座城市。(被动语态用法较少见，常用主动)
The country was fled by thousands of refugees. = 数千名难民逃离了这个国家。(被动语态用法较少见，常用主动)


疑问句:
Did they flee? = 他们逃跑了？
Will you flee if there is a fire? = 如果发生火灾你会逃跑吗？


否定句:
He did not flee the scene. = 他没有逃离现场。
They will not flee, they will fight. = 他们不会逃跑，他们会战斗。


PHRASE->
flee from = 逃离……
flee to = 逃往……
put to flight = 使溃逃；击溃
take flight = 逃走；飞走
flee the scene = 逃离现场
