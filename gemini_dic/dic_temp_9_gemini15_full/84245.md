half-silk:/ˈhælfˌsɪlk/| adj.|半丝的；真丝混纺的|Made of a mixture of silk and another fiber, typically cotton or rayon.  Half-silk fabrics often combine the luxurious feel of silk with the durability and affordability of other materials.|例句：Her elegant blouse was made of half-silk.  她的优雅衬衫是真丝混纺的。|例句：The half-silk scarf felt soft against her skin. 这条真丝混纺围巾摸起来很柔软。|近义词：silk-blend, silk-mix|反义词：pure cotton, all-cotton
