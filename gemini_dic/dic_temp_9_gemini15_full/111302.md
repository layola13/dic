liturate:/ˈlɪtərət/| adj.|有文化的；有读写能力的|Literate describes someone who can read and write.  It implies a basic level of education and understanding of written language.|He is a highly literate individual, capable of insightful analysis.|近义词：educated, learned, scholarly|反义词：illiterate, unlettered


USAGE->

He is literate in both English and French. = 他既懂英语又懂法语。
She is literate and can read complex texts. = 她有文化，能阅读复杂的文本。
