crackshot:/ˈkrækʃɒt/| n. adj.|神枪手；（枪法）精准的|A crackshot is an expert marksman, someone who is exceptionally skilled at shooting.  The adjective describes someone or something exhibiting exceptional accuracy in shooting. |例句：He was a crackshot with a rifle, never missing his target. 他是个神枪手，从未失手。

He's a crackshot; his aim is always true. 他是个神枪手；他的瞄准总是准确无误。|近义词： sharpshooter, marksman, expert shot|反义词：bad shot, inaccurate
