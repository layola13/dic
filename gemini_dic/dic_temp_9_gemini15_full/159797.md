polyglot:/ˈpɒliɡlɒt/ /ˈpɑːliɡlɑːt/| n. adj.|通晓多种语言的人；多种语言的；|A person who knows and is able to use several languages.  A book or text containing passages in several languages.|例句：She's a polyglot who speaks seven languages fluently. 她是一位通晓七种语言的语言天才。

He is a polyglot, capable of switching effortlessly between English, French, and Mandarin. 他是一位通晓多种语言的人，能够轻松地在英语、法语和普通话之间切换。


近义词： multilingual,linguist

反义词：monolingual

词性: noun, adjective
