non-exportation: /ˌnɒnˌɛkspɔːˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 不出口；禁止出口 | The policy of non-exportation aims to protect domestic industries or resources by restricting the sale of certain goods or commodities to foreign markets.  This practice can lead to higher prices for consumers within the country but can also foster self-sufficiency and economic growth within the domestic market. | 近义词： embargo, prohibition, restriction | 反义词： exportation, free trade


例句:

The government imposed a policy of non-exportation on timber to conserve its dwindling forests. = 政府实施了木材不出口政策以保护日益减少的森林。

The non-exportation of certain technologies is often justified on national security grounds. =  出于国家安全考虑，某些技术的禁运常常是合理的。


由于non-exportation本身是名词，没有动词形式，因此不提供动词的各种时态例句。  其他语法结构（如被动语态、虚拟语气等）在non-exportation这个词语本身的应用中也较少见，因此不提供相关例句。
