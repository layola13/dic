nonidentification: /ˌnɒnˌaɪdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 未识别，身份不明 | The lack of identification; the state of not being identified or recognizable.  It often refers to situations where someone's identity is unknown or deliberately concealed. | Example: The police are investigating the nonidentification of the suspect.  警方正在调查嫌疑人的身份不明。  The nonidentification of the body hampered the investigation. 尸体的身份不明阻碍了调查。 | 近义词：anonymity, obscurity, incognito | 反义词：identification, recognition
