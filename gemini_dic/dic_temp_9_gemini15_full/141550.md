oppose:/əˈpoʊz/ | vt. | 反对，抵制 | To disagree with something or someone and try to stop it or them.  |例句：Many people oppose the new law. 很多人反对这项新法律。|例句：The union opposes any attempt to reduce wages. 工会反对任何降低工资的企图。|近义词：resist, object to, protest against |反义词：support, endorse, favor |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He opposes the plan. = 他反对这个计划。
They oppose the government's policies. = 他们反对政府的政策。

一般过去时:
She opposed the marriage. = 她反对这桩婚事。
The workers opposed the factory closure. = 工人们反对工厂关闭。

现在完成时:
We have opposed this idea for years. = 我们多年来一直反对这个主意。
They have opposed the construction of the new highway. = 他们一直反对修建新的高速公路。

过去完成时:
He had opposed the project before it was even started. = 项目启动之前，他就反对过。
She had opposed the changes long before they were implemented. =  很久以前，在她实施这些改变之前，她就反对了。

将来时:
I will oppose any unfair treatment. = 我将反对任何不公平待遇。
They will oppose the bill in parliament. = 他们将在议会中反对该法案。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would oppose the decision. = 如果我是你，我会反对这个决定。
I wish they would oppose the harmful plan. = 我希望他们会反对这个有害的计划。

被动语态:
The proposal was opposed by many people. = 这个提议受到了很多人的反对。
His ideas were opposed by the majority. = 他的想法遭到大多数人的反对。

疑问句:
Do you oppose the new policy? = 你反对这项新政策吗？
Does he oppose the changes? = 他反对这些变化吗？

否定句:
I don't oppose the idea in principle. = 我原则上并不反对这个主意。
She doesn't oppose the plan, but she has some concerns. = 她不反对这个计划，但她有一些担忧。


PHRASE->
