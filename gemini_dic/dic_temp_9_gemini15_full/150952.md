paralyze:/ˈpærəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使瘫痪；使麻痹；使无力 | To make someone or something unable to move or function normally.  | The accident paralyzed him from the waist down.  事故使他从腰部以下瘫痪。| The news paralyzed her with fear.  这个消息使她吓得动弹不得。|近义词：cripple, disable, incapacitate | 反义词：activate, energize, invigorate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm paralyzes the city. = 暴风雨使这座城市瘫痪。
Fear paralyzes him. = 恐惧使他瘫痪。

一般过去时:
The shock paralyzed her. = 震惊使她瘫痪了。
The cold paralyzed his movements. = 寒冷使他的动作僵住了。

现在完成时:
The disease has paralyzed her legs. = 这种疾病使她的腿瘫痪了。
The strike has paralyzed the industry. = 罢工使这个行业瘫痪了。

过去完成时:
The accident had paralyzed him before the rescue arrived. = 在救援到达之前，事故已经使他瘫痪了。
The heavy snow had paralyzed the transportation system. = 大雪已经使交通系统瘫痪了。

将来时:
The upcoming hurricane will paralyze the coastal areas. = 即将来临的飓风将使沿海地区瘫痪。
The power outage will paralyze the city's communication systems. = 停电将使城市的通讯系统瘫痪。

虚拟语气:

否定句:
