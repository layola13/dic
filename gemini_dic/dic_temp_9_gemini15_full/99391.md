interdictory: /ˌɪntərˈdɪktəri/ | adj. | 禁止的；阻止的；妨碍的 | Relating to or having the power of an interdict; prohibitory.  Something that is interdictory forbids or prevents something else. |例句：The interdictory order prevented the shipment of goods.  该禁令阻止了货物的运输。

例句：The judge issued an interdictory injunction against the construction project.  法官发布了针对该建筑项目的禁止令。


近义词：prohibitory, forbidding, restrictive, restraining


反义词：permissive, allowing, enabling


词性：形容词
