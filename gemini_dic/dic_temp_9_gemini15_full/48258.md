decorator:/ˈdɛkəreɪtər/| n. |装饰者；修饰符|A person or thing that decorates something.  In programming, a function or class that modifies or enhances another function or class without altering its core functionality. |例句：She hired a decorator to revamp her living room.  她雇了一位装饰师来重新装修她的客厅。  In Python, decorators are used to add functionality to functions. 在Python中，装饰器用于向函数添加功能。|近义词：ornamenter, embellisher, modifier (in programming)|反义词：despoiler, vandal (in a physical sense)
