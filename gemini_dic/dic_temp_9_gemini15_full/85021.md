harasser:/həˈræsər/| n. |骚扰者，攻击者 |A person who harasses others.  Someone who repeatedly annoys, torments, or frightens someone.|例句：The harasser was eventually arrested and charged with assault.  骚扰者最终被逮捕并被指控犯有袭击罪。 The company is taking steps to protect employees from harassment by colleagues or superiors. 公司正在采取措施保护员工免受同事或上司的骚扰。|近义词：bully, tormentor, persecutor |反义词：protector, helper


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harasses his coworkers daily. 他每天骚扰他的同事。
She harasses her neighbours with loud music. 她用大声的音乐骚扰她的邻居。


一般过去时:
He harassed her relentlessly for weeks. 他连续几周无情地骚扰她。
They harassed the witnesses to prevent them from testifying. 他们骚扰证人以阻止他们作证。


现在完成时:
He has harassed me since I started this job. 自从我开始这份工作以来，他就一直在骚扰我。
She has harassed countless people throughout her career. 在她的职业生涯中，她骚扰过无数的人。


过去完成时:
He had harassed her for months before she finally reported it. 在她最终举报之前，他已经骚扰了她几个月。
They had harassed the victim for years before he finally sought help. 在受害者最终寻求帮助之前，他们已经骚扰了他多年。


将来时:
He will harass her again if given the chance. 如果有机会，他会再次骚扰她。
They will continue to harass their opponents until they concede. 他们将继续骚扰他们的对手，直到他们认输。


虚拟语气:
If he harassed her again, I would call the police. 如果他再次骚扰她，我会报警。
I wish he hadn't harassed her in the first place. 我真希望他一开始就没有骚扰她。


被动语态:
She was harassed by a stranger on the street. 她在街上被一个陌生人骚扰了。
The employees were harassed by their manager. 员工们受到了经理的骚扰。


疑问句:
Who is harassing you? 谁在骚扰你？
Was she harassed by anyone at work? 她有在工作中被任何人骚扰吗？


否定句:
He did not harass her. 他没有骚扰她。
They haven't harassed anyone recently. 他们最近没有骚扰任何人。
