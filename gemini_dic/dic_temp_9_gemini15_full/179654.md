reengrave:/ˌriːɪnˈɡreɪv/| vt.|重新雕刻，再刻|To engrave again; to carve or etch again.|The artist decided to reengrave the plate to correct a minor flaw.  艺术家决定重新雕刻版子以纠正一个小瑕疵。|近义词：re-etch, recarve|反义词：erase, obliterate|动词
