re-drafted:/ˌriːˈdræftɪd/| v. |重新起草；修改；重新制定|To draft something again, typically with revisions or improvements.  It implies a previous draft existed and needed alteration.|The contract was re-drafted to include the new clauses. 合同被重新起草以包含新的条款。 The essay was re-drafted after the professor's feedback.  这篇论文在教授的反馈后被重新起草。|rewritten, revised, amended |unwritten, original


USAGE->

They re-draft the document every year. = 他们每年都重新起草这份文件。
She re-drafts her emails before sending them. = 她发邮件之前都会重新起草。
