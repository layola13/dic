nephalist:/ˈnefəlɪst/| n. |空想家，理想主义者 |A nephalist is someone who is impractical and dreamy, often detached from reality. They are characterized by their focus on ideal worlds and their tendency to ignore or downplay the practical aspects of life.  |Example:  He was a nephalist, more interested in philosophical debates than in making a living. 他是个空想家，对哲学辩论比谋生更感兴趣。 |近义词：dreamer, idealist, visionary, fantasist |反义词：realist, pragmatist
