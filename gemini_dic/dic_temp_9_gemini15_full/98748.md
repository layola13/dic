insusceptible: /ˌɪnsəˈsɛptəbəl/ | adj. | 不易感染的；不受影响的 |  Not susceptible; incapable of being affected.  | The new vaccine renders individuals insusceptible to the virus. 新疫苗使人们不易感染病毒。 |  immune, resistant, proof | susceptible, vulnerable, receptive | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is insusceptible to flattery. = 他不易受奉承的影响。
The plant is insusceptible to disease. = 这种植物不易生病。


一般过去时:
She was insusceptible to his charms. = 她不受他的魅力影响。
The old building was insusceptible to damage from the storm. = 这座旧建筑不受暴风雨的破坏。


现在完成时:
They have remained insusceptible to the propaganda. = 他们一直不受宣传的影响。
The material has proven insusceptible to wear and tear. = 该材料已被证明不易磨损。


过去完成时:
He had been insusceptible to persuasion until that moment. = 直到那一刻，他一直不受劝说。
The fortress had remained insusceptible to attack for centuries. = 这座堡垒几个世纪以来一直坚不可摧。


将来时:
The new technology will make our systems insusceptible to hacking. = 新技术将使我们的系统不易受到黑客攻击。
The crops will likely be insusceptible to frost this year. = 今年的庄稼可能不会受霜冻影响。


虚拟语气:
If he were insusceptible to criticism, he would be a better leader. = 如果他不易受到批评，他会成为一个更好的领导者。
I wish I were insusceptible to stress. = 我希望我不易受压力影响。


被动语态:

The plan was designed to make the system insusceptible to errors. = 该计划旨在使系统不易出错。


疑问句:

否定句:
