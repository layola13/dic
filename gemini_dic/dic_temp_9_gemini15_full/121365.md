misguess:/mɪsˈɡɛs/| vt.|误猜，猜错|To guess incorrectly; to make a wrong guess.|例句：I misguessed the answer to the riddle. 我猜谜语的答案错了。|例句：He misguessed the outcome of the election. 他误判了选举的结果。|近义词：misjudge, mistake, err|反义词：guess correctly, predict accurately
