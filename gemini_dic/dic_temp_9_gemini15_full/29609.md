categorising: /ˈkætəɡəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 分类；归类 | The act of putting things into categories.  This is the present participle form of the verb "categorize". It describes the process of sorting or classifying items into groups based on shared characteristics. | Example:  He was busy categorising the data for his research. 他正忙着为他研究的数据分类。


近义词: classifying, sorting, grouping, arranging, systematizing
反义词:  randomizing, jumbling, mixing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am categorising the books by author. = 我正在按作者对书籍进行分类。
They categorise their expenses meticulously. = 他们一丝不苟地将他们的支出分类。


一般过去时:
She categorised the toys by colour. = 她按颜色对玩具进行了分类。
We categorised the responses into positive and negative. = 我们将回应分为正面和负面。


现在完成时:
I have categorised all the files. = 我已经对所有文件进行了分类。
They have categorised the data into three groups. = 他们已将数据分为三组。


过去完成时:
She had categorised the information before the presentation. = 她曾在演示文稿之前对信息进行了分类。
We had categorised the samples according to size. = 我们已经按照大小对样品进行了分类。

虚拟语气:
