perpetrator:/ˈpɜːrpətreɪtər/ | n. | 犯罪者；作恶者 | The person or entity that commits a crime or harmful act.  The perpetrator of the crime was finally apprehended. 犯罪者最终被逮捕了。 |近义词：offender, culprit, wrongdoer | 反义词：victim


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The perpetrator hides his identity. = 犯罪者隐藏了他的身份。
The perpetrator plans the next crime. = 犯罪者计划下一起犯罪。

一般过去时:
The perpetrator escaped the scene. = 犯罪者逃离了现场。
The police caught the perpetrator red-handed. = 警察当场抓获了犯罪者。

现在完成时:
The perpetrator has left no trace. = 犯罪者没有留下任何痕迹。
The police have finally identified the perpetrator. = 警察最终确定了犯罪者的身份。

过去完成时:
The perpetrator had already fled before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，犯罪者已经逃跑了。
The witnesses had seen the perpetrator run away. = 目击者看到犯罪者跑掉了。

将来时:
The police will find the perpetrator eventually. = 警察最终会找到犯罪者。
The perpetrator will face justice. = 犯罪者将面临审判。

虚拟语气:
If the perpetrator were caught, he would be punished. = 如果犯罪者被抓到，他将会受到惩罚。
I wish the perpetrator had been apprehended sooner. = 我希望犯罪者早点被逮捕。

被动语态:
The crime was committed by a perpetrator unknown to the police. = 犯罪是由警方不认识的犯罪者犯下的。
The perpetrator is being sought by the authorities. = 犯罪者正在被当局追捕。

疑问句:
Who is the perpetrator of this crime? = 这起犯罪的罪犯是谁？
Do you know who the perpetrator is? = 你知道罪犯是谁吗？

否定句:
The police haven't identified the perpetrator yet. = 警方尚未确定犯罪者的身份。
He is not the perpetrator of the crime. = 他不是这起犯罪的罪犯。
