rightsized:/ˈraɪtsaɪzd/| vt.|调整规模，精简，裁员（通常指为了提高效率或降低成本）|To make a company or organization smaller and more efficient by reducing the number of employees or departments.  This often implies a strategic downsizing, rather than simply firing people. |The company rightsized its workforce to improve profitability. 公司精简了员工队伍以提高盈利能力。|downsize, streamline, restructure|expand, increase|动词
