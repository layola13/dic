chamade:/ʃəˈmɑːd/| n. |投降信号；求和信号 |A chamade is a signal, typically a drumbeat or trumpet call, used to indicate a desire to surrender or parley (negotiate) during warfare.  It signifies a willingness to cease fighting and engage in peaceful communication. |Example: The besieged city sent out a chamade, signaling their intention to surrender.  被围困的城市发出了求和信号，表示他们打算投降。|近义词：signal, summons, truce, parley |反义词：attack, assault, defiance
