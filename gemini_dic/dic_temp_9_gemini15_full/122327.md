mix-ups:/ˈmɪksʌps/| n.|混乱，错误，误会|A mix-up is a confusing or muddled situation, often resulting in errors or misunderstandings.  It refers to a state of disorder or confusion caused by a jumble of things.|例句：There was a mix-up with the orders, and some customers received the wrong items.  订单搞错了，一些顾客收到了错误的商品。

There was a mix-up in the schedule, leading to delays.  时间安排混乱，导致延误。|近义词：confusion, muddle, jumble, mess, error|反义词：order, clarity, organization
