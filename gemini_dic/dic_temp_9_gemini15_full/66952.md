experiencing:/ɪkˈspɪəriənsɪŋ/ | v.ing| 经历；体验；感受 | experiencing something means to be involved in it and feeling its effects.  |例句：I am experiencing a lot of stress at work. 我在工作中承受着巨大的压力。

例句：She is experiencing the joys of motherhood. 她正在体验为人母的快乐。


近义词： undergoing, living through, encountering | 反义词：ignoring, avoiding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am experiencing a new culture. = 我正在体验一种新的文化。
They are experiencing technical difficulties. = 他们正在经历技术困难。


一般过去时:
We experienced a power outage last night. = 我们昨晚经历了停电。
He experienced a lot of hardship in his youth. = 他年轻时经历了很多苦难。


现在完成时:
I have experienced many challenges in my life. = 我一生中经历过许多挑战。
She has experienced significant personal growth. = 她已经取得了显著的个人成长。


过去完成时:
They had experienced similar problems before. = 他们以前遇到过类似的问题。
By the time she arrived, he had experienced a complete change of heart. = 等她到达的时候，他已经彻底改变了主意。


将来时:
We will be experiencing warmer temperatures next week. = 下周我们将体验到更高的气温。
They will experience difficulties integrating the new system. = 他们将在整合新系统时遇到困难。


虚拟语气:
If I experienced such joy again, I would never forget it. = 如果我再次体验到这样的快乐，我将永生难忘。
I wish I were experiencing the same sense of adventure as you. = 我希望我能像你一样体验到同样的冒险感。


被动语态:
The new policy is being experienced by many as unfair. = 许多人认为这项新政策是不公平的。
The effects of climate change are being experienced globally. = 全球都在经历气候变化的影响。


疑问句:
Are you experiencing any side effects from the medication? = 你服用药物后有任何副作用吗？
What are you experiencing right now? = 你现在正在经历什么？


否定句:
I am not experiencing any pain. = 我没有任何疼痛。
They are not experiencing any problems with the software. = 他们没有遇到任何软件问题。


PHRASE->
experiencing difficulties = 经历困难
experiencing problems = 遇到问题
experiencing growth = 经历成长
experiencing a crisis = 经历危机
experiencing success = 经历成功
