mispredict:/ˌmɪspɹɪˈdɪkt/ | vt. | 预测错误，误判 | To predict incorrectly; to make a wrong prediction. |例句：The meteorologists mispredicted the severity of the storm. 气象学家们误判了风暴的严重程度。

The analysts mispredicted the market's reaction to the news. 分析师们误判了市场对该消息的反应。


近义词：err in predicting, misjudge, get wrong, forecast incorrectly | 反义词：predict accurately, foresee correctly | 词性:动词
