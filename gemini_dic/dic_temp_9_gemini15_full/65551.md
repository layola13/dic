euthanized:/ˈjuːθənaɪzd/| vt.|安乐死；实施安乐死|To kill (an animal or person) painlessly, especially to relieve suffering.|例句：The veterinarian euthanized the suffering dog.  兽医对这只痛苦的狗进行了安乐死。|例句：The family decided to euthanize their terminally ill pet.  这个家庭决定对他们患有绝症的宠物实施安乐死。|近义词：put down, mercy kill|反义词：heal, cure|动词
