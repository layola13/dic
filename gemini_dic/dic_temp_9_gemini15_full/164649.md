preexpenditure:/ˌpriːɪkˈspɛndɪtʃər/| n. | 预支支出，预付费用 |  Expenditure made in advance of a project or activity.  It represents costs incurred before the main phase of a project begins.  | Example: The preexpenditure on the new building included site surveys and architectural design. 预付新大楼的费用包括场地勘测和建筑设计。|近义词：advance payment, upfront cost, preliminary expense |反义词：post-expenditure, arrears, outstanding payment | 名词
