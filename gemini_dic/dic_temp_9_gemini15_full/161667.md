poured:/pɔːrd/| v.|倾倒，灌注，下（雨）|To cause (a liquid) to flow from a container.  It also refers to heavy rainfall.|例句：She poured the milk into the glass. 她把牛奶倒进玻璃杯里。

例句：The rain poured down all afternoon.  雨整整下了一下午。


近义词：spilled, streamed, cascaded,  deluged

反义词：collected, gathered, reserved


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pours the coffee every morning. = 他每天早上都倒咖啡。
She pours her heart out to her friend. = 她向朋友倾诉衷肠。


一般过去时:
I poured a glass of wine. = 我倒了一杯酒。
The rain poured heavily last night. = 昨晚雨下得很大。


现在完成时:
She has poured all the ingredients into the bowl. = 她已经把所有配料都倒进了碗里。
It has poured with rain all day. =  今天雨下了一整天。


过去完成时:
He had poured the tea before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经倒好了茶。
The rain had poured for hours before it finally stopped. = 雨下了几个小时才最终停下来。


将来时:
I will pour you a cup of tea. = 我会给你倒一杯茶。
They will pour the concrete tomorrow. = 他们明天会浇筑混凝土。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pour myself a drink. = 如果我有更多时间，我会给自己倒一杯酒。
If it hadn't poured all day, we would have gone for a picnic. = 如果今天不是一直在下雨，我们会去野餐的。


被动语态:
The concrete was poured yesterday. = 混凝土是昨天浇筑的。
The milk was poured onto the floor. = 牛奶洒在了地板上。


疑问句:
Did he pour the water? = 他倒水了吗？
Will you pour me some more coffee? = 你再给我倒些咖啡好吗？


否定句:
He didn't pour the milk. = 他没有倒牛奶。
She didn't pour the sauce over the pasta. = 她没有把酱汁浇在意大利面上。


PHRASE->
pour out:倾诉, 倾倒
pour in:涌入, 蜂拥而至
pour over:仔细研究, 细读
pour forth:大量涌出, 倾泻而出
pour oneself a drink:给自己倒一杯酒
