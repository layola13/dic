coin: /kɔɪn/ | n. v. |硬币；铸造；创造 |

英文解析:
As a noun, a coin is a flat, round piece of metal used as money.  As a verb, to coin means to invent a new word or phrase, or to make coins by stamping metal.

例句:
I found a rare old coin in my grandmother's attic. = 我在我祖母的阁楼里发现了一枚稀有的古币。

He dropped a coin into the vending machine. = 他往自动售货机里投了一枚硬币。


The writer coined a new word to describe the phenomenon. =这位作家创造了一个新词来形容这种现象。

The mint coins millions of dollars worth of currency each year. =造币厂每年铸造价值数百万美元的货币。


近义词: (n.)  currency, token, medal; (v.) invent, create, forge


反义词: (n.)  paper money; (v.)  destroy


词性: noun, verb
