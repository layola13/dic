overofficiously:/ˌoʊvərəˈfɪʃəsli/| adj. | 过分殷勤地；多管闲事地 | Excessively eager to offer unwanted help or advice; officious to an excessive degree.  |例句：He overofficiously corrected my grammar. 他过分殷勤地纠正了我的语法。

例句：She overofficiously offered her unsolicited advice. 她过分殷勤地提供了她不需要的建议。


近义词：meddlesomely, officiously, presumptuously, intrusively

反义词：unhelpfully, indifferently, apathetically
