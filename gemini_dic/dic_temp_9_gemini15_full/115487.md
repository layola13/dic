manured:/məˈnʊrd/| v. |施肥；粪肥；|to fertilize (land) with manure|例句：The farmer manured the fields before planting the crops.  农民在播种前给田地施肥。|近义词：fertilized, enriched|反义词：depleted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer manures the fields regularly. = 农民定期给田地施肥。
They manure their garden with compost. = 他们用堆肥给花园施肥。


一般过去时:
He manured the garden last week. = 他上周给花园施肥了。
We manured the vegetable patch before the rain. = 我们在下雨前给菜地施肥了。


现在完成时:
They have manured the fields this spring. = 他们今年春天已经给田地施肥了。
The gardener has manured the rose bushes twice this month. = 园丁这个月已经给玫瑰花丛施肥两次了。


过去完成时:
The plants had been manured before the drought. = 干旱之前，这些植物已经施过肥了。
She had manured the lawn before the party. = 她在派对之前已经给草坪施过肥了。


将来时:
I will manure the garden tomorrow. = 我明天将要给花园施肥。
We will manure the orchard next week. = 我们下周将要给果园施肥。


虚拟语气:
If I had manured the plants, they might have grown better. = 如果我给植物施肥了，它们可能会长得更好。
I wish I had manured the soil before planting. = 我真希望我在播种前给土壤施过肥。


被动语态:
The fields were manured last month. = 田地在上个月施过肥。
The soil is being manured by the farmer. = 土壤正在由农民施肥。


疑问句:
Have you manured the lawn yet? = 你已经给草坪施肥了吗？
When will the fields be manured? = 田地什么时候施肥？


否定句:
He didn't manure the field this year. = 他今年没有给田地施肥。
They haven't manured the garden yet. = 他们还没有给花园施肥。
