nonrationalization: /ˌnɒnˌræʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |非理性化，非合理化 | The act or process of not providing a rational explanation or justification; the absence of rationalization.  It often implies a lack of logical thought or deliberate avoidance of reasoning. | Example: The nonrationalization of their actions led to misunderstandings and conflict.  他们的行为缺乏合理的解释，导致误解和冲突。 | 近义词:irrationality, illogicality, unreasonableness | 反义词:rationalization, justification
