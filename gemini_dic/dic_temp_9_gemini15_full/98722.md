insures:/ɪnˈʃʊərz/ | v. | 保险；确保 | To guarantee something against risk or loss; to make certain.  | 例句：The company insures against fire and theft. = 这家公司承保火灾和盗窃险。 | 例句： We need to insure that the project is completed on time. = 我们需要确保项目按时完成。 | 近义词： guarantees, protects, secures | 反义词：risks, endangers


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He insures his car every year. = 他每年都为他的车投保。
The policy insures against loss of property. = 保单承保财产损失。

一般过去时:
They insured their house before the hurricane season. = 他们在飓风季节来临之前为他们的房子投了保险。
She insured her life for a million dollars. = 她为自己的生命投保了一百万美元。

现在完成时:
He has insured his new car with a reputable company. = 他已经在他新车投保了一家信誉良好的公司。
We have insured our business against potential risks. = 我们已经为我们的业务投保以防范潜在风险。

过去完成时:
They had insured their business before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们已经为他们的企业投保了。
She had insured her jewelry before traveling overseas. = 在出国旅行之前，她已经为自己的珠宝投保了。

将来时:
I will insure my new house next week. = 我下周将为我的新房子投保。
They will insure their shipment against damage. = 他们将为他们的货物投保以防损坏。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would insure my boat. = 如果我有更多的钱，我会为我的船投保。
I wish I had insured my bicycle before it was stolen. = 我真希望我的自行车在被偷之前我已经为它投保了。

被动语态:
The house was insured for a substantial amount. = 这栋房子投保了巨额保额。
The shipment is insured against loss or damage. = 这批货物投保了损失或损坏。

疑问句:
Does your policy insure against flood damage? = 你的保单承保洪水造成的损失吗？
Is the artwork insured for its full value? = 这件艺术品是否按其全额价值投保？

否定句:
He did not insure his motorcycle. = 他没有为他的摩托车投保。
They didn't insure the package before shipping it. = 他们在寄送包裹之前没有为它投保。
