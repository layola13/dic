menage:/ˈmænɑːʒ/ | n. |家庭；管家；管理；(尤指三人或多人间的)同居关系 |A household;  management;  a household, especially one with unconventional relationships, often implying a polyamorous or group living arrangement. |例句：The menage consisted of a husband, wife, and their adopted child. 这个家庭由丈夫、妻子和他们的养子组成。

例句：The menage was known for its unconventional living arrangements. 这个家庭以其非传统的居住安排而闻名。


近义词：household, family,  ménage à trois (for the polyamorous sense) |反义词：None specifically, but concepts like "solitary existence" or "monogamous relationship" could be considered opposites depending on the context. |词性：名词


词源:  The word comes from the Old French word *menage*, meaning "household," "management," or "handling."  This in turn is derived from the Old French verb *menager*, meaning "to manage," "to handle," or "to keep house."
