powered:/ˈpaʊərd/| adj.| 供电的；有动力的；强劲的 |  Supplied with power; driven by a power source; strong and effective. |例句：The car is powered by electricity. 这辆车是电力驱动的。 |例句：His speech was powered by passion. 他的演讲充满了激情。|近义词：energized, driven, strong, forceful |反义词：powerless, weak, feeble


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The computer is powered by a large battery. = 这台电脑由一块大电池供电。
This device is powered by solar energy. = 这个设备由太阳能供电。


一般过去时:
The old factory was powered by steam. = 那家老工厂由蒸汽驱动。
The lights were powered by a generator. = 灯光由发电机供电。


现在完成时:
The city has been powered by renewable energy for years. = 这个城市多年来一直使用可再生能源供电。
My phone has been powered off since yesterday. = 我的手机从昨天开始就关机了。


过去完成时:
The machine had been powered down before the inspection. = 机器在检查之前就已经关闭电源了。
The system had been powered by coal for decades. = 这个系统几十年来一直由煤炭提供动力。


将来时:
The new building will be powered by wind turbines. = 新建筑将由风力涡轮机供电。
The spacecraft will be powered by nuclear energy. = 宇宙飞船将由核能驱动。


虚拟语气:
If the project were powered by sufficient funding, it would succeed. = 如果这个项目有足够的资金支持，它就能成功。
I wish my car were powered by electricity. = 我希望我的车是电力驱动的。


被动语态:
The equipment is powered by a central system. = 设备由中央系统供电。
The entire city was powered by a single power plant. = 整个城市由一座发电厂供电。


疑问句:
Is this device powered by batteries? = 这个设备是用电池供电的吗？
How is the new robot powered? = 新机器人是如何供电的？


否定句:
The machine is not powered by gasoline. = 这台机器不是用汽油驱动的。
The lights were not powered on during the storm. = 暴风雨期间灯光没有打开。


PHRASE->

powered by = 由...提供动力
powered flight = 动力飞行
battery-powered = 电池供电的
nuclear-powered = 核动力的
wind-powered = 风力驱动的
