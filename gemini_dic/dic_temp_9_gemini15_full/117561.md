megadeaths:/ˈmɛɡəˌdɛθs/ | n. |百万死亡人数；大量死亡 | A term used to describe the deaths of a million or more people, often in the context of a disaster, war, or epidemic.  It's a more impactful and concise way of expressing a massive loss of life than simply stating the numerical figure. | Example: The potential megadeaths from a nuclear war are a terrifying prospect. 核战争可能造成的百万死亡人数是一个可怕的前景。 | 近义词:mass casualties, mass mortality, extensive loss of life | 反义词: minimal casualties, survival
