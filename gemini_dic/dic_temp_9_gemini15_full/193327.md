seeth:/siːð/| v.|沸腾；翻腾；怒火中烧|To be in a state of agitation or excitement, often with anger or resentment.  The word often implies a suppressed or simmering anger. |例句：Anger seethed within her.  怒火在她心中翻腾。

例句：The pot of stew was seething on the stove.  炖锅里的肉汤在炉子上沸腾。


近义词：boil, churn, fume, simmer, rage

反义词：calm, tranquil, placid


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water seeths in the pot. = 水在锅里沸腾。
He seeths with resentment. = 他怒火中烧。

一般过去时:
The volcano seethed before erupting. = 火山在喷发前翻腾。
She seethed silently at his remarks. = 她对他的话语默默地感到愤怒。

现在完成时:
The anger has seethed within him for years. = 他多年来一直怒火中烧。
The pot has been seething for an hour. = 锅里的东西已经沸腾了一个小时。

过去完成时:
He had seethed for days before confronting her. = 在与她对质之前，他已经愤怒了好几天。
The pressure cooker had been seething before it exploded. = 高压锅在爆炸前一直在沸腾。

将来时:
The volcano will seethe before its next eruption. = 火山在下一次喷发前会翻腾。
He will seethe with anger if he finds out. = 如果他发现的话，他会怒火中烧。

虚拟语气:
If I were in his shoes, I would seethe with indignation. = 如果我是他，我会气愤不已。
I wish I hadn't seethed at him; I regret my outburst. = 我真希望我没有对他发火；我后悔我的爆发。

被动语态: (Seeth is rarely used passively)  The situation was described as seething with tension. 情势被描述为紧张激烈。
The problem was described as a seething cauldron of discontent.  这个问题被描述为一个不满情绪的沸腾大锅。

疑问句: (Seeth is rarely used in questions)  Is the situation seething with discontent?  情况是否充满了不满？
Does the pot still seethe? 锅还在沸腾吗？


否定句:
The water did not seethe; it was merely warm. = 水没有沸腾；它只是温的。
He didn't seethe outwardly, but his anger was evident. = 他表面上没有生气，但他的愤怒是显而易见的。
