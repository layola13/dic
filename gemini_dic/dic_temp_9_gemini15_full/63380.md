entophyte:/ˈɛntoʊfaɪt/| n. |内生植物，内生菌|A plant or fungus that lives within the tissues of another plant.  Entophytes are often symbiotic, meaning they live in a mutually beneficial relationship with their host. However, some can be parasitic, causing disease. |Example:  Many orchids have mycorrhizal entphytes that assist in nutrient uptake. 许多兰花都具有能帮助吸收养分的菌根内生菌。 |近义词:endophyte (更常用)|反义词:epiphyte (外生植物)
