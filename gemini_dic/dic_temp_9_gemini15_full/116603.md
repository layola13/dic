matrilineally:/ˌmætɹɪˈlaɪnəli/| adv.|母系地，沿母系血统|Tracing kinship through the female line.  Describes a social system where descent and inheritance are traced through the mother's line.|例句：In many indigenous cultures, property is inherited matrilineally.|近义词：through the female line, along the maternal line|反义词：patrilineally
