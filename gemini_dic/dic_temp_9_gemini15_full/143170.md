outclasses:/ˌaʊtˈklæsɪz/| vt.|胜过；超过；比…优秀|To surpass or excel; to be superior to.|例句：His performance outclasses all the others. 他的表现胜过其他人。|例句：Her new design outclasses the previous models. 她的新设计超过了之前的型号。|近义词：surpasses, excels, surpasses, overshadows|反义词：is inferior to, is outdone by|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outclasses his rivals in debate. = 他在辩论中胜过他的对手。
This product outclasses its competitors in terms of quality. =  这款产品在质量方面胜过其竞争对手。

一般过去时:
She outclassed her opponent in the tennis match. = 她在网球比赛中击败了对手。
The new technology outclassed the old system in efficiency. = 新技术在效率方面超过了旧系统。

现在完成时:
He has outclassed all his previous achievements with this new invention. = 他凭借这项新发明超越了他之前的所有成就。
Our team has outclassed the opposition in every game this season. = 我们队在本赛季的每一场比赛中都击败了对手。

过去完成时:
By the end of the race, she had outclassed all the other runners. = 到比赛结束时，她已经超过了所有其他跑步者。
The company had outclassed its competitors in market share by the end of the year. = 到年底，该公司在市场份额上已超过了竞争对手。

将来时:
They will outclass their opponents with their superior skills. = 他们将凭借其高超的技能击败对手。
This new model is expected to outclass all others in terms of performance. = 预计这款新机型在性能方面将超越所有其他机型。

虚拟语气:
If he trained harder, he would outclass all the other athletes. = 如果他训练更刻苦，他就会胜过所有其他运动员。
I wish I could outclass my rival in the upcoming competition. = 我希望我能在这个即将到来的比赛中胜过我的对手。

被动语态:
He was outclassed by the champion in the final round. = 在决赛中，他被冠军击败了。
The old system was outclassed by the new technology. = 旧系统被新技术超越了。

疑问句:
Does this software outclass the others in functionality? = 这款软件在功能上是否胜过其他软件？
Will their new product outclass the existing market leaders? = 他们的新产品能否超越现有的市场领导者？

否定句:
He didn't outclass his opponent in the last game. = 他在上一场比赛中没有胜过对手。
Their strategy did not outclass their competitors' approach. = 他们的策略并没有胜过竞争对手的方法。
