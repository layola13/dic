details:/ˈdiːteɪlz/ | n. |细节，详情 | Details are important in a report. 报告中的细节很重要。|近义词： specifics, particulars |反义词： overview, summary |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The report details the findings of the study. = 这份报告详细说明了研究结果。
He details his experiences in his new book. = 他在新书中详细描述了他的经历。


一般过去时:
She detailed her plans for the upcoming project. = 她详细阐述了她对即将开展的项目的计划。
The witness detailed the events of the accident. = 目击者详细描述了事故经过。


现在完成时:
He has detailed all the necessary steps in the manual. = 他已经在手册中详细说明了所有必要的步骤。
The artist has detailed the textures of the fabric in the painting. = 艺术家在绘画中详细描绘了织物的纹理。


过去完成时:
She had detailed her concerns before the meeting. = 她在会议之前已经详细说明了她的担忧。
They had detailed the budget before presenting it to the board. = 他们在向董事会提交预算之前已经详细说明了预算。


将来时:
He will detail the process in the next chapter. = 他将在下一章详细介绍这个过程。
She will detail her findings in her upcoming presentation. = 她将在即将举行的演示中详细说明她的发现。


虚拟语气:
If he detailed the problem, we could find a solution. = 如果他详细说明了问题，我们就能找到解决方案。
I wish he had detailed his reasons more clearly. = 我希望他更清楚地说明他的理由。


被动语态:
The plan was detailed in the proposal. = 该计划在提案中已详细说明。
The procedure is detailed in the instruction manual. = 该程序在说明书中已详细说明。


疑问句:
Can you detail the steps involved? = 你能详细说明所涉及的步骤吗？
What details should we include in the report? = 我们应该在报告中包含哪些细节？


否定句:
He didn't detail his reasons for leaving. = 他没有详细说明他离开的原因。
The contract doesn't detail the payment terms. = 合同中没有详细说明付款条款。


PHRASE->
