denotate:/ˈdiːnoʊteɪt/ | vt. |表示；代表；象征|To denote means to be a sign of; to indicate; to signify.  It refers to the meaning or significance something represents.  The word implies a clear and established connection between the sign and the thing signified. |The red flag denotes danger.  红旗表示危险。

The symbol "+" denotes addition.  "+"符号表示加法。


近义词: signify, indicate, represent, symbolize, mean

反义词:  None directly opposite, but words suggesting ambiguity or lack of clear meaning could be considered contrasting, such as obfuscate or obscure.


词源:  From Latin *dēnōtāre, from dē- "down, away, from" + notāre "to mark, note."


USAGE->
