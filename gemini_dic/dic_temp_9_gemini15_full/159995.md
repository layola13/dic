polysepalous: /ˌpɒliˈsɛpələs/ | adj. | 多萼的 | Having sepals that are distinct and not fused together.  A polysepalous flower has sepals that are free from each other, unlike a gamosepalous flower where the sepals are fused.  This characteristic is important in plant taxonomy for classifying flowering plants.

例句：
The buttercup displays a polysepalous calyx, with its five distinct sepals clearly visible. = 毛茛具有多萼的花萼，其五个独立的花萼清晰可见。

The polysepalous flower of the rose is a defining characteristic of the genus. = 玫瑰的多萼花是该属的一个决定性特征。


近义词：chorisepalous


反义词：gamosepalous


词源:
From  "poly-" (many) + "sepalous" (having sepals).  "Sepal" itself comes from the Latin "sepalum," a diminutive of "sepes" (hedge), referring to the protective nature of the sepals surrounding the flower bud.
