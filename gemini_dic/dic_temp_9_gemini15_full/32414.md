chilly:/ˈtʃɪli/| adj.|寒冷的，凉爽的，冷淡的 |Slightly cold; causing a feeling of coolness.  It suggests a temperature that is uncomfortably cool but not extremely cold.|例句：The wind made the evening feel chilly.  晚风使夜晚感觉寒冷。|例句：She gave me a chilly reception. 她给了我冷淡的接待。|近义词：cool, crisp, nippy, cold|反义词：warm, hot, balmy
