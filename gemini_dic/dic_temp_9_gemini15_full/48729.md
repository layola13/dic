defiler:/dɪˈfaɪlər/| n. |亵渎者；玷污者 |One who defiles; a polluter.  A defiler is someone who damages or destroys something sacred or pure. They might desecrate a holy place, corrupt a moral code, or ruin someone's reputation. |The defiler of the ancient temple was caught and punished. 亵渎古庙的人被抓获并受到了惩罚。|近义词：polluter, desecrator, corrupter |反义词：purifier, sanctifier
