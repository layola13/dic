misdivide:/ˌmɪs.dɪˈvaɪd/ | vt. |错误地划分，分配不均 |To divide incorrectly or unfairly.  |例句：The inheritance was misdivided among the heirs.  遗产在继承人之间分配不均。

The committee misdivided the funds, allocating too much to one project and too little to others. 委员会错误地分配了资金，给一个项目分配了太多，而给其他项目分配得太少。|近义词：misallocate, distribute wrongly, apportion incorrectly |反义词：divide fairly, distribute equally, apportion justly |动词
