misvalue:/ˌmɪsˈvæljuː/| vt.| 误估；低估；对…估价过低 | To undervalue or misjudge the worth or importance of something or someone.  |例句：The company misvalued its assets, leading to financial difficulties. 公司对资产估价过低，导致了财务困难。

例句：He misvalued her contribution to the project. 他低估了她对项目的贡献。


近义词：undervalue, underestimate, misjudge | 反义词：overvalue, overestimate


词性:动词
