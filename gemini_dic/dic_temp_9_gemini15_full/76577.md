gallican:/ˈɡælɪkən/| adj.|法国教会的；高卢的|Of or relating to the Gallican Church or its rites and practices.  The term often refers to the independent spirit of the French Church in relation to the Papacy. |The Gallican Church maintained a degree of independence from Rome.  法国教会保持了一定程度的罗马独立性。|近义词：French (in a religious context)|反义词：Roman (in a religious context)
