sauntered:/ˈsɔːntərd/| v.|闲逛；漫步|to walk in a slow, relaxed manner, without hurry|He sauntered down the street, enjoying the warm sunshine. 他沿着街道漫步，享受着温暖的阳光。|strolled, ambled, strolled, wandered|hurried, rushed, dashed

They saunter along the beach at sunset. = 他们日落时在海滩上漫步。
