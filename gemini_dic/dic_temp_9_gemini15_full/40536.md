controversy:/ˈkɒntrəvɜːsi/ /ˈkɑːntrəvɜːrsi/ | n. |争议，论战 | A controversy is a public disagreement, debate, or argument, often involving strong opinions on both sides.  It often involves conflicting viewpoints or interpretations of facts. |The new law sparked a heated controversy. 这项新法律引发了激烈的争议。|  dispute, debate, argument, contention | agreement, harmony, accord | 名词


USAGE->

This decision continues to generate controversy. = 这个决定仍在引起争议。


一般过去时:

Their marriage was surrounded by controversy. = 他们的婚姻充满了争议。
