rezoning:/ˌriːˈzoʊnɪŋ/| n. |重新分区，土地重新规划 |The process of changing the zoning regulations for a particular area of land.  This often involves changing the permitted uses of land, such as from residential to commercial. |例句：The city council approved the rezoning of the industrial area to allow for the construction of residential buildings.  市议会批准将工业区重新划分为住宅区，以允许建造住宅楼。

The proposed rezoning will impact traffic flow and local businesses.  拟议的重新分区将影响交通流量和当地企业。


|近义词：redivision, replanning |反义词：zoning (as the opposite action) |名词
