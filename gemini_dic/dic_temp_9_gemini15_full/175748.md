raptness:/ræptnəs/| n. |全神贯注；入迷；着迷 |The state of being completely engrossed or captivated; intense absorption or fascination.  |例句：He listened with raptness to the storyteller's tale. 他全神贯注地听着讲故事的人讲故事。

例句：Her raptness was so complete that she didn't notice me enter the room. 她全神贯注，竟然没注意到我进屋。


近义词：absorption, engrossment, fascination, enthrallment, captivation

反义词：indifference, apathy, boredom, distraction
