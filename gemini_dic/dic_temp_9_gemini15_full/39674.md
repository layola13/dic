conserver:/kənˈsɜːrvər/| n. v.| 保护者；保存者；果酱；蜜饯|  One who conserves; something that is conserved; a preserver. A conserver can refer to a person who actively protects or preserves something, or it can refer to a container or method used for preservation (like a jar of jam). |例句：She is a dedicated conserver of natural resources. 她是一位致力于保护自然资源的保护者。  The old jar contained a delicious homemade conserver.  那个旧罐子里装着美味的自制果酱。|近义词：preserver, protector, keeper |反义词：waster, destroyer, squanderer |名词，动词
