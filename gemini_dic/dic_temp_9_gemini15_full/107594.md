landscapist:/ˈlændskeɪpist/| n. |风景画家，风景设计师|A landscapist is an artist who specializes in painting or drawing landscapes.  They depict natural scenery, often including mountains, valleys, trees, water, and sky.|例句：The renowned landscapist captured the breathtaking beauty of the Scottish Highlands in his latest masterpiece.  这位著名的风景画家在最新的杰作中捕捉到了苏格兰高地的壮丽景色。

例句：Many famous landscapists have been inspired by the dramatic scenery of Yosemite National Park. 许多著名的风景画家都受到优胜美地国家公园的壮丽景色所启发。


近义词：landscape painter, landscape artist, scenic artist|反义词：portrait painter (反义词相对宽泛，并非完全对立)|词性：名词
