portray:/pɔːˈtreɪ/ | vt. | 描绘，描写，扮演 | To represent or describe someone or something in a particular way, especially in a work of art.  | 例句：The painting portrays a young woman in a red dress. 这幅画描绘了一位穿着红裙子的年轻女子。

例句：The actor portrays Hamlet with great intensity.  这位演员以极大的强度扮演哈姆雷特。


近义词：depict, represent, illustrate, depict, characterize

反义词：misrepresent, distort


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He portrays the character convincingly. 他令人信服地扮演这个角色。
The artist portrays nature beautifully.  这位艺术家美丽地描绘了大自然。

一般过去时:
The author portrayed the hero as a flawed but sympathetic figure. 作者将主人公描绘成一个有缺陷但令人同情的形象。
She portrayed the events of that day accurately in her book.  她在书中准确地描绘了那一天的事件。

现在完成时:
The media has portrayed the situation inaccurately. 媒体对局势的报道不准确。
He has portrayed many different roles throughout his career. 他在职业生涯中扮演过许多不同的角色。

过去完成时:
The play had portrayed the conflict between good and evil. 这部戏剧曾描绘过善与恶之间的冲突。
The newspaper had portrayed the politician in a negative light. 报纸曾以负面形象描绘这位政治家。

将来时:
The movie will portray the story of a courageous woman. 这部电影将讲述一位勇敢女人的故事。
The artist will portray the landscape in a realistic style.  这位艺术家将以写实风格描绘风景。

虚拟语气:
If he portrayed the role differently, the play would have been a success. 如果他以不同的方式扮演这个角色，这部戏就会成功。
I wish the painting portrayed more emotion. 我希望这幅画能表达更多的情感。

被动语态:
The character was portrayed as a villain.  这个角色被描绘成一个恶棍。
The events were portrayed unfairly in the news report.  新闻报道不公平地报道了这些事件。

疑问句:
How does the novel portray the protagonist? 这部小说如何刻画主人公？
Does the film accurately portray historical events?  这部电影是否准确地描绘了历史事件？

否定句:
The painting does not portray the subject realistically. 这幅画并没有真实地描绘主题。
The article did not portray the situation objectively. 这篇文章没有客观地描绘局势。


PHRASE->
portray as: 描绘成...
portray oneself as: 将自己描绘成...
portray the role of: 扮演...的角色
