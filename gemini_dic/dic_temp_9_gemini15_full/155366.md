pharming:/ˈfɑːrmɪŋ/ | n. | 网络钓鱼攻击 | A type of cyberattack where malicious code redirects users to fake websites that mimic legitimate ones, usually to steal personal information like usernames, passwords, credit card details, etc.  | Example:  The pharming attack redirected users to a fake banking website to steal their login credentials.  网络钓鱼攻击将用户重定向到模仿合法网站的假网站，以窃取用户名、密码、信用卡详细信息等个人信息。|近义词：spoofing, website hijacking | 反义词：secure website, legitimate website | 名词
