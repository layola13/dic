misappropriates:/ˌmɪsˌæproʊprieɪt/| vt.|挪用，盗用，滥用|To use something (especially money or property) that you have been entrusted with for your own personal gain, often illegally.|The accountant misappropriated company funds for his own gambling habit. 会计挪用公司资金用于自己的赌博习惯。|近义词：embezzle, steal, divert, defraud|反义词：donate, contribute


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misappropriates company funds. 他挪用公司资金。
She misappropriates resources for personal gain. 她挪用资源谋取私利。


一般过去时:
The employee misappropriated the client's money. 员工挪用了客户的钱。
They misappropriated the funds for their own purposes. 他们挪用资金用于自身目的。


现在完成时:
He has misappropriated thousands of dollars. 他已经挪用了数千美元。
The company has discovered that funds have been misappropriated. 公司发现资金已被挪用。


过去完成时:
They had misappropriated the funds before the audit. 他们在审计之前就挪用了资金。
She had misappropriated the money long before anyone noticed. 早在任何人注意到之前，她就挪用了这笔钱。


将来时:
He will misappropriate more funds if he is not stopped. 如果不阻止他，他会挪用更多资金。
They will likely misappropriate the donations if they are not properly monitored. 如果没有妥善监督，他们很可能会挪用捐款。


虚拟语气:
If he misappropriated the funds, he would face serious consequences. 如果他挪用资金，他将面临严重后果。
I wish he hadn't misappropriated the charitable donations. 我真希望他没有挪用慈善捐款。


被动语态:
The funds were misappropriated by a trusted employee. 资金被一位信任的员工挪用。
The evidence shows that company assets were misappropriated. 证据表明公司资产被挪用。


疑问句:
Was the money misappropriated? 钱被挪用了吗？
Did anyone misappropriate the funds? 有人挪用资金了吗？


否定句:
He did not misappropriate the funds; he merely borrowed them. 他没有挪用资金；他只是借用了它们。
The investigation found that no funds were misappropriated. 调查发现没有挪用资金。
