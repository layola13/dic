overpraises:/ˈoʊvərˈpreɪzɪz/| v.|过度赞扬，过分称赞|To praise excessively or beyond what is deserved.  It implies that the praise is not genuine or is disproportionate to the subject's merits.|例句：He overpraised his son's artwork, making the boy feel entitled. 他过分夸奖儿子的画作，让孩子感觉自己理所当然.|近义词：extol, laud, flatter, magnify|反义词：criticize, belittle, denigrate|动词
