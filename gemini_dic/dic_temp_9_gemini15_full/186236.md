rhinocerote:/raɪˈnɑːsərəʊt/ | n. |犀牛 |A large, thick-skinned, plant-eating mammal with one or two horns on its snout.  |The rhinoceros is a critically endangered species. 犀牛是一种极度濒危的物种。|近义词：rhino |反义词：无
