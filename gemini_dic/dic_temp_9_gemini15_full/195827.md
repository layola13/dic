seneca:/ˈsɛnɪkə/| n. |塞内加；塞内加人；塞内加作品|Seneca refers to Lucius Annaeus Seneca, a Roman Stoic philosopher, statesman, dramatist, and orator.  His works, which include philosophical essays, tragedies, and letters, are still studied today.  The term can also refer to a person from the Seneca Nation of Native Americans. |例句：Seneca's writings on Stoicism are highly influential.  塞内加关于斯多葛主义的著作极具影响力。

The Seneca Nation of Indians is known for its rich cultural heritage.  塞内加印第安人的民族以其丰富的文化遗产而闻名。|近义词：Stoic (in the context of the philosopher),  Native American (in the context of the nation)|反义词：无
