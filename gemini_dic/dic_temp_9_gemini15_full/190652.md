sauces: /ˈsɔːsɪz/ | n. |酱汁，调味汁 |A sauce is a liquid, cream, or paste used to flavor or enhance food.  It can be made from a variety of ingredients, including vegetables, fruits, herbs, spices, and dairy products. |例句：The chef prepared a delicious tomato sauce for the pasta.  厨师为意大利面准备了一种美味的番茄酱。|例句：Many different sauces are used in Asian cuisine.  亚洲菜肴中使用了许多不同的酱汁。|近义词：dressing, condiment, relish |反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑dryness, blandness作为相对概念)|名词
