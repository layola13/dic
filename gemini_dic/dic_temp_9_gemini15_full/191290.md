scavenger:/ˈskævɪndʒər/| n. |拾荒者，清道夫，食腐动物 | A scavenger is a person or animal that feeds on discarded or decaying organic matter.  They search for and collect discarded or waste materials. | Example:  Vultures are scavengers that feed on carrion.  秃鹰是吃腐肉的食腐动物。  The homeless man was a scavenger, searching for food in the trash.  那个无家可归的人是个拾荒者，在垃圾桶里寻找食物。|近义词：forager, rummager, scavenger bird, carrion eater|反义词：producer, hunter (in the context of obtaining food)

The birds scavenge for food in the fields. = 鸟儿在田野里觅食。
