overstarch:/ˌoʊvərˈstɑːrtʃ/ | vt. | 浆得太硬；使…过于拘谨 | To starch excessively; to make too stiff or formal. |  The laundress overstarched his shirts, making them uncomfortably stiff. 洗衣女工把他的衬衫浆得太硬了，穿起来很不舒服。  He overstarched his speech, making it sound unnatural. 他讲话过于拘谨，听起来很不自然。 | starch, stiffen | soften, relax | 动词
