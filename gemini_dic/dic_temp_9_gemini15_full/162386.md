preactively:/ˌpriːˈæktɪvli/ | adv. | 预先地；主动地 |  Proactively anticipating and addressing potential problems or issues before they arise.  It emphasizes taking initiative and acting in advance rather than reacting to events. | 例句：The company preactively addressed potential supply chain issues by diversifying its sourcing. 公司通过多元化采购预先解决了潜在的供应链问题。


例句：The team preactively identified and mitigated risks before the project launch.  团队在项目启动前主动识别并减轻了风险。


近义词：proactively, anticipatorily, preventively


反义词：reactively, passively


词性：副词
