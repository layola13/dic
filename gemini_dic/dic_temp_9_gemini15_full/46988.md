das:/dæs/| pron.| 那，那个，那些 |That, those. It refers to something previously mentioned or easily understood from context. |例句：I saw a bird. Das was a robin. 我看到一只鸟。那是一只知更鸟。  |近义词：that, those |反义词：this, these |代词
