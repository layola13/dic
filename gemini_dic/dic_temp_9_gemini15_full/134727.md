nonmeasurable: /ˌnɒnˈmɛʒərəbəl/ | adj. | 不可测量的，无法衡量的 |  Not capable of being measured; immeasurable.  The word combines "non-" (meaning "not") and "measurable" (capable of being measured).  It emphasizes the lack of a quantifiable or objective measurement. |

例句：

The impact of the advertisement on sales was nonmeasurable. 广告对销量的影响是无法衡量的。

The level of his anxiety was nonmeasurable; it seemed infinite. 他的焦虑程度无法衡量；似乎是无限的。


近义词： immeasurable, incalculable, unquantifiable, indefinable

反义词： measurable, quantifiable
