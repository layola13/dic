masoning:/ˈmeɪsənɪŋ/| n.|石工；砌石工程|The art or process of building with stones, bricks, or similar materials.  It involves the skillful arrangement and bonding of these materials to create structures.|例句：The masoning on the old church is remarkably well-preserved.  古老教堂的石工保存得非常好。|例句：The quality of the masoning will determine the building's longevity. 砌石工程的质量将决定建筑物的寿命。|近义词：stonework, bricklaying, brickwork|反义词：destruction, demolition|名词
