maltreat:/mælˈtriːt/| vt.|虐待，粗暴对待|To treat (a person or animal) roughly or cruelly.  |例句：He was accused of maltreating his children. 他被指控虐待他的孩子。|例句：The animals were clearly being maltreated. 这些动物显然受到了虐待。|近义词：abuse, mistreat, ill-treat, ill-use|反义词：cherish, care for, nurture


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They maltreat the animals. = 他们虐待动物。
She maltreats her employees. = 她虐待她的员工。


一般过去时:
He maltreated his dog. = 他虐待了他的狗。
The soldiers maltreated the prisoners. = 士兵虐待了俘虏。


现在完成时:
They have maltreated the refugees. = 他们虐待了难民。
She has maltreated her pet cat. = 她虐待了她的宠物猫。


过去完成时:
The farmer had maltreated his horses for years. = 这位农民多年来一直虐待他的马匹。
They had maltreated the children before the authorities intervened. = 在当局介入之前，他们虐待了孩子们。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:
He did not maltreat his dog. = 他没有虐待他的狗。
