laughingly:/ˈlæfɪŋli/| adv.|笑着地，哈哈地，嬉笑着地|Laughing is used to describe the manner in which something is done, indicating that it was done with laughter. It implies a lighthearted and joyful demeanor.|He laughingly admitted to eating all the cookies. 他笑着承认自己吃了所有的饼干。|smilingly, merrily, jokingly|angrily, seriously, sadly|副词
