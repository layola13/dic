siskin:/ˈsɪskɪn/| n. |柳莺；黄雀|A small, active finch with a distinctive song, often found in wooded areas and gardens.  Siskins are known for their acrobatic feeding habits, often hanging upside down from branches to reach food.|例句：We saw a flock of siskins feeding on the thistle seeds in the garden. 我们看到一群柳莺在花园里吃蓟种子。|例句：The male siskin's plumage is brighter than the female's. 雄性柳莺的羽毛比雌性更鲜艳。|近义词：goldfinch, canary,  finch|反义词：无
