digressing:/daɪˈɡresɪŋ/| v.|离题；枝节；偏离主题| straying from the main topic or subject; rambling.  |例句：He kept digressing from the main point of his speech. 他一直在偏离演讲的主题。
例句：The professor digressed into a long anecdote about his childhood. 教授偏离主题讲起了他童年的一段长长的轶事。|近义词：rambling, straying, wandering, deviating|反义词：focusing, concentrating, sticking to the point|动词
