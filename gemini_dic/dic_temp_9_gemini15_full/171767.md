punches:/ˈpʌntʃɪz/ | n. v. | 拳打；冲压；(饮料的)冲调 |  The act of hitting with a fist; a forceful blow with the fist; also, a hole made by punching;  the act of making a hole with a tool;  a stimulating drink. |

例句:
He threw a few punches at his opponent. = 他朝对手挥了几拳。

The machine punches holes in the metal sheet. = 机器在金属板上冲孔。


近义词: blows, hits, jabs,  perforates,  (for drinks) cocktails

反义词: caresses, soothes


词性: noun, verb


USAGE->

She punches in at 8 am. = 她早上8点打卡上班。
