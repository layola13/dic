nursingly:/ˈnɜːrsɪŋli/| adv.|  护士般地；关怀地；悉心地 |  In a manner characteristic of a nurse; with care and attention.  It implies a gentle and attentive approach, often associated with nurturing or caring for someone. |例句：She nursingly wiped the child's brow. 她像护士一样悉心地擦拭孩子的额头。|例句：He nursingly tended his injured bird. 他细心地照顾他受伤的小鸟。 |近义词：carefully, tenderly, attentively, solicitously |反义词：negligently, carelessly, roughly
