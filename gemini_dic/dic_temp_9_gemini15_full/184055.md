residues:/ˈrezɪduːz/| n.|残留物，剩余物|The remaining substance or substances after a process is complete; something left after other parts have been taken away.  |例句：After the flood, there were residues of mud everywhere. 洪水过后，到处都是泥土残留物。

例句：The chemical reaction left behind harmful residues. 化学反应留下了有害残留物。


近义词：remains, remnants, leftovers, traces

反义词：whole, entirety
