sardonic:/sɑːrˈdɒnɪk/ | adj. | 讽刺的；嘲讽的；挖苦的 |  Characterized by bitter or scornful derision; mocking; cynical.  It suggests a detached, often contemptuous amusement, implying a sense of superiority or disdain.  The sardonic tone often masks a deeper cynicism or pessimism. | 例句：He gave a sardonic smile. = 他冷笑了一下。

He offered a sardonic comment on the politician's promises. = 他对这位政治家的承诺发表了尖刻的评论。


近义词： cynical, sarcastic, sneering, mocking, wry, ironic | 反义词： earnest, sincere, genuine, complimentary
