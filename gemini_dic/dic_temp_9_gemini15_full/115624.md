marcher:/ˈmɑːrtʃər/ | n. | 行军者，游行者 | A marcher is someone who takes part in a march or a procession.  | Example: The marchers chanted slogans as they walked down the street.  游行者们边走边高喊口号。 | 近义词：demonstrator, protester | 反义词：N/A | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The marcher carries a banner. = 行军者扛着横幅。
Many marchers participate in the parade. = 许多游行者参加了游行。

一般过去时:
The marcher walked for miles. = 行军者走了数英里。
The marchers protested against the new law. = 游行者抗议新法律。

现在完成时:
The marcher has completed the marathon. = 行军者已经完成了马拉松。

过去完成时:

The marchers had gathered before the speech began. = 演讲开始前游行者们已经聚集起来了。

将来时:

否定句:
