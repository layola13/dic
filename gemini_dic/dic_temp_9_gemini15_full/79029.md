give-a-hand:/ɡɪv ə hænd/| v. |帮忙，协助 |To offer help or assistance.  |例句：Can you give me a hand with these boxes? 你能帮我搬这些箱子吗？|例句：I'd really appreciate it if you could give me a hand with this project. 如果你能帮我一把这个项目，我会非常感激。|近义词：help, assist, aid |反义词：hinder, obstruct


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I give you a hand whenever you need it. = 我需要的时候都会帮你。
She gives a hand to the homeless people. = 她帮助无家可归的人。


一般过去时:
He gave me a hand with my homework. = 他帮我做了作业。
They gave a hand to the victims of the earthquake. = 他们帮助了地震的受害者。


现在完成时:
I have given a hand to many people in need. = 我已经帮助过很多需要帮助的人。
She has given a hand to her friends many times. = 她多次帮助过她的朋友们。


过去完成时:
He had given a hand to his brother before he left. = 他在他离开之前帮助了他的兄弟。
They had given a hand to the charity before the event started. = 在活动开始之前，他们已经帮助过慈善机构。


将来时:
I will give you a hand with your presentation tomorrow. = 我明天会帮你准备演讲。
She will give a hand to the organization next week. = 她下周会帮助这个组织。


虚拟语气:
If you needed a hand, I would give you one. = 如果你需要帮忙，我会帮你的。
I wish I could have given her a hand with the move. = 我真希望我能帮她搬家。


被动语态:
A hand was given to the injured man. = 受伤的人得到了帮助。
Help was given to the flood victims. = 洪水受害者得到了帮助。(Note:  "give a hand" is less commonly used passively)


疑问句:
Can you give me a hand? = 你能帮我一下吗？
Will you give a hand to this project? = 你会帮忙这个项目吗？


否定句:
I couldn't give him a hand because I was busy. = 我因为忙所以不能帮他。
She didn't give a hand to her colleague. = 她没有帮助她的同事。


PHRASE->
give a hand with = 帮忙...
lend a hand = 帮忙
give someone a helping hand = 给予某人帮助
offer a hand = 提供帮助
a helping hand = 援助之手
