what:/wɒt/|ˌhwɑːt/| pron.|什么，什么东西，什么情况|The word "what" is an interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something. It can refer to things, actions, qualities, or situations.  It is also used in exclamations and relative clauses.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？


近义词：which, which thing


反义词：无


词性：代词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？


一般过去时:
What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么事？


现在完成时:
What have you been doing today? = 你今天一直在做什么？
What has she accomplished this year? = 她今年取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they already discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们已经讨论了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气怎么样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to expect. = 我希望我知道会发生什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built in that area? = 那个地区正在建造什么？


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道该说什么。
I don't understand what you mean. = 我不明白你的意思。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What's new? = 有什么新鲜事吗？
What a shame! = 真可惜！
What if...? = 如果……将会怎样？
What's more... = 更重要的是……
