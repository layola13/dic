pseudoscorpiones:/ˌsuːdəʊˈskɔːpiəˌniz/| n. |伪蝎目|Pseudoscorpiones is an order of arachnids, commonly known as pseudoscorpions or false scorpions.  They are small, typically less than 1 cm in length, and resemble scorpions but lack a tail and stinger. They are found worldwide in various habitats. |Example:  The scientist discovered several species of pseudoscorpiones under loose bark.  科学家在松散的树皮下发现了数种伪蝎。|近义词:false scorpion, book scorpion|反义词: scorpion
