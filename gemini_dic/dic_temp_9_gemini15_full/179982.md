refilled:/ˌriːˈfɪld/ | v. | 再装满；重新填满 | To fill something again after it has been emptied.  |例句：I refilled the water bottle after my run. 我跑步后又把水瓶装满了。|例句：She refilled the coffee pot for the guests. 她为客人重新装满了咖啡壶。|近义词：replenished, topped up |反义词：emptied


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refills his coffee cup every morning. = 他每天早上都重新装满他的咖啡杯。
She refills the bird feeder regularly. = 她定期补充鸟食。

一般过去时:
I refilled the gas tank before the road trip. = 我在公路旅行前加满了油箱。
The waiter refilled our glasses with wine. = 服务员给我们续上了酒。
