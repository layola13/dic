knighting:/ˈnaɪtɪŋ/| v.|授予爵位；封为骑士|The act of making someone a knight; conferring knighthood.  It involves a ceremony where a sword is used to dub the person a knight.|例句：The king performed the knighting ceremony with great solemnity. 国王庄严地举行了授勋仪式。
例句：Sir Lancelot was famously knighting by King Arthur. 兰斯洛特爵士因亚瑟王而闻名。|近义词：dubbing, ennobling|反义词：dethroning, degrading.
