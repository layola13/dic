quasi-enthusiastic:/ˈkwɑːziˌɛnθuːziˈæstɪk/| adj.|似是而非的热情，不热情的，装腔作势的热情 |  Appearing enthusiastic but lacking genuine feeling or conviction; half-hearted.  It suggests a pretense of enthusiasm that doesn't fully ring true. | 例句：He gave a quasi-enthusiastic speech, his heart clearly not in it. 他发表了一个似是而非的热情洋溢的演讲，他的心显然不在里面。


例句：Her quasi-enthusiastic applause felt more like a polite obligation than genuine appreciation.  她似是而非的热烈鼓掌更像是出于礼貌的义务而非发自内心的欣赏。


近义词：apathetic, unenthusiastic, lukewarm, half-hearted, perfunctory


反义词：enthusiastic, passionate, ardent, zealous, fervent


词性：形容词
