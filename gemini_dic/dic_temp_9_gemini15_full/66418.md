executorship:/ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtərʃɪp/| n. |遗嘱执行权；遗产管理；执行人的职务|The role and responsibility of an executor in managing and distributing the assets of a deceased person's estate according to their will.  It involves tasks like paying debts, taxes, and distributing inheritances.|例句：The executorship of my grandmother's will was a lengthy and complex process.  我的祖母遗嘱的执行是一个漫长而复杂的过程。

例句：He was appointed to the executorship of his uncle's estate. 他被任命为叔叔遗产的执行人。


近义词：administration, probate|反义词：无|词性：名词
