commenced:/kəˈmɛnst/| v.|开始，着手 |To begin or start something.|The meeting commenced at 2 p.m.  会议于下午两点开始。|The project commenced last month.  项目上个月启动。|begin, start, initiate|end, finish, conclude|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He commences his work early every day. 他每天很早就开始工作。
She commences her studies next semester. 她下学期开始学习。

一般过去时:
The ceremony commenced with a prayer. 典礼以祈祷开始。
The game commenced at 7:00 PM.  比赛在晚上7点开始。

现在完成时:
They have commenced the renovation of their house. 他们已经开始装修他们的房子。
She has commenced writing her novel. 她已经开始写她的小说了。

过去完成时:
The play had commenced before we arrived. 我们到达之前，戏剧已经开始了。
He had commenced his journey before sunrise. 他在日出前就开始了他的旅程。

将来时:
The concert will commence at 8 o'clock. 音乐会将在八点开始。
Work on the bridge will commence next spring. 桥梁工程将于明年春天开始。

虚拟语气:
If he commenced the project earlier, he would have finished it by now. 如果他早点开始这个项目，他现在就完成了。
I wish I had commenced my studies sooner. 我希望我早点开始学习。

被动语态:
The meeting was commenced with a brief introduction. 会议以简短的介绍开始。
The construction of the building was commenced last year. 这座建筑物的建设始于去年。

疑问句:
When did the meeting commence? 会议什么时候开始？
When will the class commence? 课程什么时候开始？

否定句:
The work did not commence until the next day. 工作直到第二天才开始。
They haven't commenced the project yet. 他们还没有开始这个项目。
