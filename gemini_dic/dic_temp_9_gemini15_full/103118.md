jerkily:/ˈdʒɜːrkɪli/ | adv. | 猛地；急促地；不平稳地 | Moving in a jerky manner; characterized by sudden, uneven movements.  |例句：The car jerked along the bumpy road.  这辆车在颠簸的路上颠簸前行。|例句：The puppet moved jerkily across the stage. 木偶在舞台上猛地移动。|近义词：abruptly, spasmodically, erratically |反义词：smoothly, evenly, steadily


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He walks jerkily. = 他走路很急促。
The machine operates jerkily. = 这台机器运作不稳定。

一般过去时:
The train jerked to a halt. = 火车猛地停了下来。
She moved jerkily across the room. = 她猛地穿过房间。

现在完成时:
The engine has been jerking all day. = 引擎一整天都在猛地抖动。
The car has jerked several times. = 汽车已经猛地抖动了好几次。

过去完成时:
The horse had jerked its head before I could catch it. = 我还没抓住它之前，马已经猛地甩了头。
The needle had jerked wildly before settling down. = 针在稳定下来之前剧烈地抖动。

将来时:
The lift will jerk unexpectedly. = 电梯会意外地猛地一震。
The boat will jerk violently in the rough sea. = 船在汹涌的海面上会剧烈地颠簸。

虚拟语气:
If the car jerked again, I would be very scared. = 如果汽车再次猛地一震，我会非常害怕。
If the train had jerked less, the journey would have been smoother. = 如果火车抖动得少一些，旅程就会更平稳。

被动语态:
The wagon was jerked violently by the horse. = 马车被马猛地拉了一下。
The picture was jerked around by the wind. = 图片被风猛地吹来吹去。

疑问句:
Did the machine jerk? = 机器是不是猛地抖动了？
Does the engine jerk when it starts? = 引擎启动时会猛地抖动吗？

否定句:
The car didn't jerk at all. = 汽车根本没有猛地抖动。
The boat didn't jerk during the calm sea. = 在平静的海面上，船没有颠簸。
