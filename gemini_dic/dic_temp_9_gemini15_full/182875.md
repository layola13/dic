repeopled:/ˌriːˈpɒpəl/|/ˌriːˈpɑːpəl/| vt.|  重新居住；使…重新有人居住 |To repopulate an area or place that has been deserted or emptied of people.  |例句：The town was repeopled after the war.  战后小镇重新有人居住。

例句：After the devastating earthquake, efforts were made to repeople the affected villages.  在毁灭性地震之后，人们努力使受灾村庄重新有人居住。


近义词：repopulate, resettle


反义词：depopulate, evacuate


词性：动词
