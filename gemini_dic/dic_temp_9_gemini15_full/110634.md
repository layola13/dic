limpkin:/ˈlɪmpkɪn/| n. | limpkin（一种涉禽）|A limpkin is a large, long-legged bird of the rail family, known for its distinctive cry and its diet of snails.  They are found in wetlands of the southeastern United States, Central America, and the Caribbean.|The limpkin picked up a snail and smashed it against a rock.|近义词：none  |反义词：none
