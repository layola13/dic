sheeling:/ˈʃiːlɪŋ/| n.|牧羊小屋；临时住所；脱壳；（尤指从谷物中）去壳|A sheeling is a small, temporary dwelling, traditionally made of turf or other readily available materials.  It's often associated with pastoral activities, particularly sheep herding. The term can also refer to the process of removing husks or shells from grain or nuts.|例句：The shepherd lived in a simple sheeling high in the mountains. 牧羊人住在一座简陋的位于高山上的牧羊小屋里。The farmer spent the afternoon sheeling barley. 农民花了整个下午去壳大麦。|近义词：hut, cabin, bothy, temporary dwelling|反义词：permanent dwelling, mansion
