drip-drying: /ˈdrɪpˌdraɪɪŋ/ | n. |滴干；晾干 | The process of allowing wet clothes or other items to dry by dripping water.  |例句：I prefer drip-drying my clothes to using a tumble dryer. 我更喜欢让衣服滴干而不是用烘干机。  |近义词：air-drying,  allowing to dry |反义词：machine drying, tumble drying
