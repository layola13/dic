nonmomentariness:/ˌnɒnˌməʊmənˈtɛəriɪnəs/| n. |非瞬间性，持续性|The state or quality of not being momentary; lastingness; permanence.  It refers to something that is not fleeting or brief but rather endures over time.  The concept often contrasts with ephemerality or transience.|例句：The painting's nonmomentariness was striking; it seemed to transcend time. 这幅画的持久性令人印象深刻；它似乎超越了时间。

The sculpture's nonmomentariness is a testament to the artist's skill.  这尊雕塑的持久性证明了艺术家高超的技艺。|近义词：permanence, durability, lastingness, persistence|反义词：momentariness, ephemerality, transience, brevity|名词
