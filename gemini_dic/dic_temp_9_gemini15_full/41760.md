corrupters:/kəˈrʌptərz/| n. |腐败分子，行贿者|Those who engage in corruption, bribery, or other dishonest practices to gain an advantage. They undermine ethical standards and institutions.|例句：The investigation uncovered a network of corrupters who had infiltrated the government.  调查揭露了一个渗透政府的腐败分子网络。

例句：The corrupters were eventually brought to justice and punished for their crimes. 这些腐败分子最终被绳之以法，并受到惩罚。
