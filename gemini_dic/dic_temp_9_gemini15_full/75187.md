frizzle:/ˈfrɪzəl/ | v. n. | 卷曲；（使）卷起；嘶嘶声 | To make or become curly; a sizzling or crackling sound. |例句：The heat frizzled her hair. 热使她的头发卷曲了。|近义词：curl, crisp, sizzle | 反义词：straighten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The iron frizzles my hair. = 卷发器使我的头发卷曲。
He frizzles his eggs for breakfast. = 他早餐煎蛋时发出嘶嘶声。


一般过去时:
The fire frizzled the leaves. = 大火使树叶卷曲焦黄。
The bacon frizzled in the pan. = 培根在平底锅里发出嘶嘶声。


现在完成时:
The sun has frizzled the paint on the car. = 太阳已使汽车上的油漆卷曲脱落。
The chef has frizzled the onions until golden brown. = 厨师把洋葱煎至金黄酥脆。


过去完成时:
She had frizzled her hair before the party. = 她在派对前已经把头发弄卷了。
The food had frizzled and burned before I got home. = 我回家之前，食物已经煎焦了。


将来时:
I will frizzle my hair for the wedding. = 我将为婚礼把头发弄卷。
The rain will frizzle the radio signal. = 雨会使无线电信号产生嘶嘶声。


虚拟语气:
If I frizzled my hair, I would look more stylish. = 如果我把头发弄卷，我会看起来更时尚。
I wish I hadn't frizzled the cake, it's burnt now. = 我真希望我没有把蛋糕煎焦，现在它烧焦了。


被动语态:
His hair was frizzled by the humidity. = 他的头发被潮湿的空气弄卷了。
The sound was frizzled by static. = 声音被静态干扰弄得嘶嘶作响。


疑问句:
Does the heat frizzle the fabric? = 热会使织物卷曲吗？
Did the frying pan frizzle the butter? = 煎锅使黄油发出嘶嘶声了吗？


否定句:
The curling iron didn't frizzle her hair enough. = 卷发器没有使她的头发卷曲得足够。
The speaker didn't frizzle despite the interference. = 尽管有干扰，扬声器也没有发出嘶嘶声。


PHRASE->
frizzle out:  逐渐消失；逐渐减弱
frizzle up:  卷曲；焦黄
heat frizzle: 热卷曲
frizzle and sizzle:  发出嘶嘶声并卷曲
air frizzle: 风卷曲
