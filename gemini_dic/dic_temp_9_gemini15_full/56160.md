doddle:/ˈdɒdl/| n. v.|轻而易举的事；轻松地做；涂鸦|A doddle is something that is very easy to do.  It can also refer to the act of drawing aimlessly. |例句：The exam was a doddle.  考试易如反掌。  例句：She spent the afternoon doodling in her notebook. 她一下午都在笔记本上涂鸦。|近义词：piece of cake, breeze, cinch|反义词：difficult, hard, challenging


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The test is a doddle for him. = 这场考试对他来说易如反掌。
This task is a doddle. = 这项任务很容易。

一般过去时:
The puzzle was a doddle to solve. = 这个谜题很容易解开。
He doodled a picture on the napkin. = 他在餐巾纸上涂鸦了一幅画。

现在完成时:
I have finished the assignment; it was a doddle. = 我已经完成了作业，它很简单。
She has been doodling all morning. = 她整个上午都在涂鸦。

过去完成时:
I had already finished the work; it was a doddle. = 我已经完成了工作；它很容易。
He had doodled several pictures before the meeting. = 会议前他已经涂鸦了几幅画。

将来时:
The next project will be a doddle. = 下一个项目将会很容易。
I will be doodling during the boring lecture. = 我将在无聊的讲座上涂鸦。

虚拟语气:
If this were a doddle, I'd finish it now. = 如果这很容易，我现在就完成了。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
