entice:/ɪnˈtaɪs/| vt.|引诱；诱惑；吸引|To attract or tempt someone to do something.|The sweet smell of baking bread enticed me into the bakery. 香喷喷的面包味把我吸引进了面包店。|近义词：tempt, lure, attract, allure|反义词：repulse, deter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The advertisement entices consumers to buy the product. = 广告诱使消费者购买该产品。
The beautiful scenery entices tourists to visit the island. = 美丽的景色吸引游客前来参观岛屿。

一般过去时:
The promise of riches enticed many people to join the expedition. = 发财的承诺诱使许多人加入探险队。
The salesman enticed me into buying a new car. = 销售员诱使我买了一辆新车。

现在完成时:
The aroma of coffee has enticed me to the café. = 咖啡的香味把我吸引到了咖啡馆。
The company's marketing campaign has enticed many new customers. = 该公司的营销活动吸引了许多新客户。

过去完成时:
The thought of a warm bed had enticed him to stay inside. = 温暖的床铺的念头诱使他待在屋里。
The glittering jewels had enticed the thief to commit the crime. = 闪闪发光的珠宝诱使小偷犯了罪。

将来时:
The new promotion will entice many employees to work harder. = 新的晋升机会将激励许多员工更加努力工作。
The low prices will entice customers to shop at our store. = 低廉的价格将吸引顾客到我们商店购物。

虚拟语气:
If the offer were more attractive, it would entice more people to apply. = 如果这个提议更诱人，它将吸引更多的人申请。
I wish the prize money would entice more talented artists to participate. = 我希望奖金能够吸引更多有才华的艺术家参与。

被动语态:
Many people were enticed by the promise of a better life. = 许多人被对美好生活的承诺所吸引。
Customers are enticed into buying unnecessary items. = 顾客被诱导购买不必要的物品。

疑问句:
What entices you to this job? = 什么吸引你做这份工作？
What will entice the children to eat their vegetables? = 什么能诱使孩子们吃蔬菜？

否定句:
Nothing could entice her to leave her home. = 什么也无法诱使她离开家。
The low salary did not entice him to accept the position. = 低工资并没有诱使他接受这个职位。
