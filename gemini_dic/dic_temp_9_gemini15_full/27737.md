caniniform:/kəˈnaɪnɪfɔːrm/| adj.|犬齿形的；犬牙状的|Resembling a canine tooth in form or shape; having the shape of a canine tooth.  The term typically refers to a tooth or a structure that resembles a canine tooth in its pointed, conical shape.|例句：The fossil showed a caniniform tooth, suggesting a carnivorous diet.化石显示出一颗犬齿形的牙齿，表明其食肉。The predator's caniniform premolars were particularly sharp. 掠食者的犬齿形前臼齿特别锋利。|近义词：conical, pointed, cuspate|反义词：molariform (臼齿形的)
