self-castigate:/ˌsɛlfˈkæstɪɡeɪt/| v.|自我惩罚；自我责备 |To punish oneself severely, especially as a form of penance or self-discipline.  |例句：He self-castigated for his failures, refusing to forgive himself. 他因自己的失败而惩罚自己，拒绝原谅自己。|例句：She spent hours self-castigating over the argument she had with her friend. 她花了几个小时自责她和朋友的争吵。|近义词：self-reproach, self-criticize, flagellate |反义词：self-indulge, self-praise


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He self-castigates daily for his perceived shortcomings. = 他每天都因自己认为的缺点而自我惩罚。
She self-castigates whenever she makes a mistake. = 她每犯一次错就自我责备。

一般过去时:
He self-castigated after losing the race. = 输掉比赛后，他进行了自我惩罚。
She self-castigated for hours after the argument. = 争吵之后，她自责了好几个小时。

现在完成时:
He has self-castigated relentlessly since the accident. = 自事故发生以来，他一直在不断地惩罚自己。
She has self-castigated for not being more assertive. = 她一直自责自己不够果断。

过去完成时:
He had self-castigated for weeks before finally seeking help. = 在最终寻求帮助之前，他已经自责了好几个星期。
She had self-castigated so much that she was emotionally exhausted. = 她自责得如此厉害以至于身心俱疲。

将来时:
He will self-castigate if he doesn't meet his goals. = 如果达不到目标，他会惩罚自己。
She will likely self-castigate over this mistake for days. = 她可能会因为这个错误而自责好几天。

虚拟语气:
If he had known better, he wouldn't have self-castigated so harshly. = 如果他早知道，他就不会如此严厉地惩罚自己。
I wish I hadn't self-castigated so much over such a minor issue. = 我真希望我没有因为这么小的事情而如此自责。

被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice because it is inherently reflexive.)


疑问句:
Does he self-castigate frequently? = 他经常自我惩罚吗？
Does she self-castigate over minor imperfections? = 她会因为细微的缺点而自责吗？

否定句:
He doesn't self-castigate as much as he used to. = 他不像以前那样经常自我惩罚了。
She doesn't self-castigate; she accepts her mistakes and learns from them. = 她不会自我惩罚；她接受自己的错误并从中学习。
