overstretch:/ˌoʊvərˈstrɛtʃ/| vt. |过度伸展；过度使用；使过度劳累|To stretch or extend beyond the normal or reasonable limits; to overuse or strain.|例句：Don't overstretch yourself by taking on too many projects at once. 不要同时承担太多项目而过度劳累自己。|例句：The company overstretched its resources in trying to expand too quickly. 公司为了快速扩张而过度消耗了资源。|近义词：overextend, strain, tax|反义词：underutilize, conserve
