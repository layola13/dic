scullionly:/ˈskʌləni/| adj.|卑鄙的，下流的，无赖的|The word "scullionly" describes someone who is base, mean, and contemptible; characterized by low morals or behavior. It suggests a lack of integrity and a tendency towards dishonesty or villainy.|例句：He behaved in a most scullionly manner, cheating his friends out of their money. 他行为卑鄙，骗取了朋友的钱。 He was accused of scullionly conduct unbecoming of a gentleman. 他被指控行为卑鄙，有损绅士风度。|近义词：base, mean, vile, scurvy, despicable, contemptible |反义词：noble, honorable, virtuous, upright
