platelet:/ˈpleɪtliːt/| n. |血小板|A small, irregular, colorless body of the blood that is involved in clotting.  Platelets are fragments of large cells called megakaryocytes and are essential for hemostasis. |Example:  Low platelet counts can lead to excessive bleeding. 低血小板计数会导致过度出血。  The doctor checked his platelet count after the accident. 事故后医生检查了他的血小板计数。|近义词：thrombocyte|反义词：无
