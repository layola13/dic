get-there:/ɡɛt ðɛr/| v.|到达，成功，实现目标| to arrive at a destination; to achieve success or a goal. |例句：We finally got there after a long journey. 我们经过漫长的旅程终于到达了目的地。  例句：He worked hard and eventually got there in his career. 他努力工作，最终在事业上取得了成功。|近义词：arrive, succeed, achieve|反义词：fail, miss


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I get there by bus every day. = 我每天乘公共汽车到达那里。
They get there early to secure good seats. = 他们很早就到那里以确保拿到好座位。

一般过去时:
We got there just in time for the show. = 我们刚好赶上演出。
She got there late because of the traffic. = 因为交通堵塞，她迟到了。

现在完成时:
I have got there several times. = 我去过那里好几次了。
They haven't got there yet. = 他们还没到呢。

过去完成时:
We had already got there when he called. = 他打电话的时候我们已经到了。
She had got there before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前她已经到了那里。

将来时:
I will get there by tomorrow evening. = 我明天晚上之前会到达。
They will get there sooner if they take a taxi. = 如果他们打车，他们会更快到达。

虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I would get there faster. = 如果我有车，我会更快到达。
I wish I had got there earlier. = 我真希望我早点到。

被动语态: *(get there doesn't usually take a passive voice)*


疑问句:
Will you get there on time? = 你能准时到达吗？
How did they get there so quickly? = 他们怎么这么快就到了？

否定句:
I didn't get there on time. = 我没有准时到达。
They couldn't get there because of the road closure. = 由于道路封闭，他们无法到达那里。
