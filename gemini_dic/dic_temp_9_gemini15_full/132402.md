nondenials:/ˌnɒn dɪˈnaɪəl z/ | n. |否认的缺乏；未否认；默认 |The absence of denials; a failure to deny something; an implicit admission.  It often implies that a statement or accusation is implicitly accepted due to a lack of denial. |例句：The government's nondenials to the accusations fueled the ongoing controversy.  政府对指控的未否认加剧了持续的争议。|例句：Her silence on the matter was interpreted as a nondenial, effectively confirming the rumors. 她对这件事的沉默被解读为默认，实际上证实了谣言。|近义词：acquiescence, tacit approval, implied consent |反义词：denial, refutation, contradiction |名词
