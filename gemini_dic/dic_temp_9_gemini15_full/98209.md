insectivorous: /ˌɪnˈsɛktɪvərəs/ | adj. | 食虫的；捕食昆虫的 | Relating to or denoting plants or animals that feed on insects.  |例句：Many insectivorous birds help control insect populations. 许多食虫鸟类有助于控制昆虫数量。
The Venus flytrap is an example of an insectivorous plant.捕蝇草是食虫植物的一个例子。|近义词：entomophagous |反义词：herbivorous, carnivorous
