codifying:/ˈkoʊdəˌfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 编纂；将…编成法典；系统化 | To arrange (laws, rules, etc.) into a systematic code; to put into a system or code.  | The government is codifying existing environmental regulations.  政府正在将现有的环境法规编纂成法典。 | systematizing, formalizing |  chaotic, disorganized | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They are codifying the new company policies. = 他们正在将新的公司政策编纂成册。
We codify our data for easier analysis. = 我们将数据编纂成系统，以便更容易分析。


一般过去时:
The committee codified the rules of the game. = 委员会将游戏规则编纂成册。
She codified her research findings into a clear report. = 她将她的研究结果编纂成一份清晰的报告。


现在完成时:
They have codified the ancient laws into a modern legal system. = 他们已将古代法律编纂成现代法律体系。
Scientists have codified the principles of genetics. = 科学家们已经将遗传学的原理系统化了。


过去完成时:
They had codified the laws before the new government took power. = 新政府上台之前，他们已经将法律编纂好了。
The researchers had codified their data before writing the report. = 研究人员在撰写报告之前已经将数据系统化了。
