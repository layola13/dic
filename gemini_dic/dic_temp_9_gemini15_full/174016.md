quasi-traditionally:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ træˈdɪʃənəli/| adj. |准传统地，似是而非地传统地 |Approximating or resembling a tradition; partly traditional but not entirely so.  It suggests something that follows traditional patterns to a significant extent but deviates in some aspects.  |例句：The band's music is quasi-traditionally folk, incorporating modern instruments and influences.乐队的音乐是准民间的，融合了现代乐器和影响。


The company's quasi-traditionally hierarchical structure allows for some flexibility.这家公司准传统的等级结构允许一定的灵活性。|近义词：conventionally, traditionally, somewhat traditionally |反义词：unconventionally, radically, modernly
