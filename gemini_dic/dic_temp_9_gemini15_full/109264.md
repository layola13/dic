lemming:/ˈlɛmɪŋ/| n. |旅鼠|A small rodent of the Arctic and subarctic regions, known for its periodic mass migrations.  Lemmings are known for their sometimes dramatic population fluctuations and mass migrations, often resulting in large numbers of them drowning as they attempt to swim across bodies of water. They are prey for many predators, including foxes, owls, and weasels. |例句：The lemmings' mass migration was a spectacular, if tragic, event. 旅鼠的大规模迁徙是一场壮观的，尽管悲惨的事件。

例句：  Many lemmings died during their attempt to cross the river.  许多旅鼠在试图过河时丧生。
