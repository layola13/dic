porcelanite:/ˌpɔːrsələnˈaɪt/| n. |瓷质岩，瓷石|A hard, flinty, and usually somewhat porous rock that is an intermediate stage between diatomite and chert.  It is generally light-colored and resembles porcelain. |例句：The geologist identified the sample as porcelanite due to its characteristic texture and composition.|近义词：chert, flint|反义词：None specifically, but terms implying softness or non-porosity could be considered opposites.|名词
