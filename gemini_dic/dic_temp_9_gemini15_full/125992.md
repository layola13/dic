muskox:/ˈmʌskoks/| n.|麝牛|A large, shaggy, Arctic mammal with long, curved horns.  Musk oxen are known for their thick coats, which provide insulation in extremely cold climates.|例句：The musk oxen huddled together for warmth against the biting Arctic wind. 麝牛们为了抵御刺骨的北极风而挤在一起取暖。  The researchers observed the musk oxen's migration patterns. 研究人员观察了麝牛的迁徙模式。|近义词：Arctic ox|反义词：无
