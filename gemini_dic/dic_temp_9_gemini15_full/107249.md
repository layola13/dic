/ˌlɛseɪˈfɛərɪzəm/ | n. | 自由放任主义；自由放任政策 | Laissez-faireism refers to the principles and practices of laissez-faire economics, a system characterized by minimal government intervention in the economy.  It emphasizes individual freedom, competition, and the belief that markets will self-regulate.  | The government's laissez-faireism led to rapid economic growth but also increased inequality.  政府的自由放任主义导致了经济的快速增长，但也加剧了不平等。| free market economics, non-interventionism | government regulation, interventionism | 名词
