pretranscribed:/ˌpriːˈtrænskraɪbd/| adj.| 预先转录的 |Previously transcribed; recorded or written down before the main event or process.  This adjective describes something that has been transcribed before its intended use or analysis.  |例句：The pretranscribed interviews saved hours of work. 预先转录的访谈节省了数小时的工作。

例句：The pretranscribed audio allowed for immediate analysis. 预先转录的音频允许立即分析。


近义词：pre-recorded, beforehand transcribed

反义词：post-transcribed, untranscribed

词性：形容词
