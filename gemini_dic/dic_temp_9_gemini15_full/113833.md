maddingly:/ˈmædɪŋli/| adv.|令人恼火地，使人发狂地 |extremely annoyingly; in a way that makes one feel extremely angry and frustrated. |例句：The incessant noise was maddeningly loud.  这持续不断的噪音令人恼火地大声。

例句：He maddeningly refused to answer my questions. 他令人恼火地拒绝回答我的问题。

近义词： infuriatingly, exasperatingly, incredibly annoyingly, frustratingly

反义词：pleasantly, calmly, soothingly
