presubscribe: /ˌpriːsəbˈskraɪb/ | vt. |预先订阅，预订 | To subscribe to something in advance; to arrange for something to be sent or delivered regularly before it is officially available.  |例句：I presubscribed to the magazine to ensure I get the first issue. 我预订了这本杂志以确保我能拿到第一期。

例句：The concert tickets were presubscribed by many fans weeks before the sale opened to the public.  许多歌迷在公开售票前几周就预订了演唱会门票。


近义词：preorder, pre-order, reserve, book in advance


反义词：cancel subscription, unsubscribe


词性：动词
