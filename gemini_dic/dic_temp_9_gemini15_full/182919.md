repetitiousness:/ˌrɛpɪˈtɪʃənəsnəs/| n. |重复性，冗余|The repetitiousness of the lecture made it difficult to stay focused.  演讲的重复性使得很难集中注意力。|近义词：redundancy, reiteration|反义词：originality, novelty|


英文解析：The state or quality of being repetitious; the act of repeating something too often, often to the point of boredom or tediousness.  指重复的状态或性质；过于频繁地重复某事物的行为，通常会让人感到厌烦或乏味。


例句：

The repetitiousness of the music became grating after a while.  音乐的重复性过一段时间后就变得刺耳了。

The repetitiousness in her writing style detracts from the overall impact of her work. 她写作风格的重复性削弱了作品的整体影响。


词源:

From repetitive + -ness.  来自repetitive（重复的）+ -ness（名词后缀）。

Repetitive itself comes from repeat (重复) + -ive (形容词后缀).  repetitive本身来自repeat（重复）+ -ive（形容词后缀）。


注意：  "repetitiousnesses"  本身是一个拼写错误。 正确的词是 "repetitiousness"。  它是一个单数不可数名词，没有复数形式。
