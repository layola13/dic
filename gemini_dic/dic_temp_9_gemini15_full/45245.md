/kʌltˈhɒlɪk/| n. |狂热的栽培者；痴迷于耕作的人 |A cultaholic is someone who is excessively enthusiastic and passionate about cultivating plants, often to an obsessive degree. They may dedicate a significant amount of time, energy, and resources to gardening, farming, or other forms of plant cultivation.  |He's a real cultaholic; his garden is breathtaking, but he spends every waking moment tending to it.|近义词：plant fanatic, gardening enthusiast, horticulture addict |反义词：neglectful gardener, apathetic cultivator|名词
