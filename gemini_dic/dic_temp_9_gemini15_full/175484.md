ramparting:/ræmˈpɑːrtɪŋ/| n.|筑城, 建造城墙, 防御工事的建造|The act of building or constructing ramparts; the process of fortifying a place with ramparts.  It often refers to the structures themselves, collectively. |例句：The ramparting of the city was a massive undertaking, requiring years of labor. 城墙的建造是一项巨大的工程，需要多年的劳动。

The extensive ramparting offered significant protection against siege.  广泛的防御工事提供了针对围攻的重要保护。|近义词：fortification, strengthening, defense, wall-building |反义词：demolishing, destroying, breaching
