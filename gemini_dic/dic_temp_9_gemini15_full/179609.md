reemploys:/ˌriːɪmˈplɔɪz/| vt.|重新雇用，再次雇佣|To employ someone again after they have left their job.|The company reemployed him after a brief period of unemployment.  公司在他短暂失业后又重新雇用了他。|近义词：rehire, reinstate, take back|反义词：fire, dismiss, lay off|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reemploys many former employees. = 公司重新雇用许多以前的员工。
He reemploys his old team every year for the summer project. = 他每年夏天都重新雇用他的老团队来做项目。

一般过去时:
They reemployed her after she had a baby. = 她生完孩子后，他们又重新雇用她了。
The factory reemployed all the workers who were laid off last year. = 工厂重新雇用了去年被解雇的所有工人。

现在完成时:
The company has reemployed over 500 workers this year. = 今年公司已经重新雇用了500多名工人。
She has reemployed her former assistant since the project restarted. = 自从项目重启以来，她已经重新雇用了她以前的助理。

过去完成时:
They had reemployed him before the new project began. = 新项目开始之前，他们已经重新雇用他了。
The manager had reemployed several staff members before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，经理已经重新雇用了几名员工。

将来时:
The company will reemploy him next month. = 公司下个月将重新雇用他。
They will reemploy more staff once the new factory opens. = 新工厂开业后，他们将重新雇用更多员工。

虚拟语气:
If the economy improved, the company would reemploy more workers. = 如果经济好转，公司将会重新雇用更多的工人。
I wish they would reemploy my old friend. = 我希望他们能重新雇用我的老朋友。

被动语态:
He was reemployed by the same company after six months. = 六个月后，他被同一家公司重新雇用。
Many workers were reemployed following the strike. = 罢工结束后，许多工人被重新雇用。

疑问句:
Will the company reemploy the workers who went on strike? = 公司会重新雇用那些罢工的工人吗？
Did they reemploy him after he apologized? = 他道歉后，他们重新雇用他了吗？

否定句:
The company did not reemploy him because of his poor performance. = 由于他的表现不佳，公司没有重新雇用他。
They wouldn't reemploy her even if she begged. = 即使她苦苦哀求，他们也不会重新雇用她。
