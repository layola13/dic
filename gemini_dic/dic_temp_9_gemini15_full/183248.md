reprehend:/ˈrɛprɪhɛnd/ | adj. |应受谴责的，该责备的 | deserving blame or punishment; blameworthy.  Reprehendable actions are those that are considered wrong or unacceptable and deserve criticism or punishment.  |例句：His behavior was reprehendable and he was rightly dismissed from his job. 他的行为应受谴责，他被解雇是理所当然的。


例句：The company's response to the crisis was reprehendable, showing a lack of concern for its employees. 公司对危机的回应是应受谴责的，表现出对员工缺乏关心。


近义词：blameworthy, culpable, censurable, reprehensible


反义词：praiseworthy, commendable, laudable


词性：形容词
