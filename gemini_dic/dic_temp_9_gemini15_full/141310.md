open-source:/ˈoʊpən sɔːrs/| adj.|开源的，开放源代码的|Relating to software or other technology whose source code is made available freely to the public to use, modify, and distribute.|例句：Linux is a popular open-source operating system.  Linux是一个流行的开源操作系统。|例句：Many developers contribute to open-source projects.许多开发者为开源项目做贡献。|近义词：free, public, accessible|反义词：proprietary, closed-source


USAGE->
