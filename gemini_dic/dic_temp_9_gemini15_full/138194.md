nosebleed: /ˈnoʊzˌbliːd/ | n. | 鼻出血 | A nosebleed is a flow of blood from the nose.  This is usually caused by minor trauma to the delicate blood vessels inside the nose, but can also be a symptom of underlying medical conditions. |  I had a nosebleed after I bumped my nose. 我撞到鼻子后流鼻血了。 He suffered a severe nosebleed after the accident. 他事故后流了严重的鼻血。 | hemorrhage, epistaxis |  (no direct antonym, but could use terms like "no bleeding") | 名词
