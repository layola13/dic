forefending: /fɔːrˈfendɪŋ/ | v. | 预防；避免；保护 |  The act of preventing or protecting against something; warding off.  |  例句：The high walls were meant for forefending against invaders.  高墙是为了防御侵略者而建的。

例句：He spent his life forefending against misfortune. 他一生都在避免不幸。


近义词：preventing, averting, warding off, guarding against, shielding from


反义词：allowing, permitting, encouraging, inviting


词性：动词
