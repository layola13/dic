paddy:/ˈpædi/| n.|稻米；水稻；（尤指爱尔兰）乡下人；|Uncooked rice, especially rice in the husk.  The term often refers to rice plants in the field. It can also be a derogatory term for an Irishman.|例句：The paddy fields were lush and green.  水稻田郁郁葱葱。  He called him a paddy, which was offensive. 他称他为“爱尔兰佬”，这是冒犯性的。|近义词：rice (uncooked), rice plant |反义词：无
