howler-monkeys: /ˈhaʊlər ˈmʌŋkiːz/ | n. |吼猴 |Howler monkeys are New World monkeys known for their extremely loud howls, which can be heard for miles. They are arboreal primates found in Central and South America.|  The howler monkeys' howls echoed through the jungle.  吼猴的吼叫声在丛林中回荡。 |  _No direct synonyms or antonyms exist, as it refers to a specific species._ | noun
