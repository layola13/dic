impressing:/ɪmˈprɛsɪŋ/ | adj. |令人印象深刻的，感人的|  causing a feeling of admiration and respect.  It describes something that makes a strong and positive impact on someone. |例句：The sunset was truly impressing.  日落真是令人印象深刻。  The speaker delivered an impressing speech. 演讲者发表了一篇令人印象深刻的演讲。|近义词：striking, remarkable, memorable, moving|反义词：unimpressive, forgettable, dull
