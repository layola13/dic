frigidarium:/frɪdʒɪˈdæriəm/| n. |冷室；冷藏室| A frigidarium is a cold room, especially one in ancient Roman baths used for cooling off after a hot bath or exercise.  It's often associated with the Roman bathing system, where it was a crucial part of the process of cleansing and relaxation.|Example: After a vigorous workout in the caldarium, the bathers would retreat to the frigidarium to cool down.  在热水房剧烈运动后，沐浴者会退到冷室降温。|近义词：cold room, cooling room|反义词：caldarium (hot room)
