commutations:/ˌkɒmjuˈteɪʃənz/| n.|减刑；通勤；交换；变更|The act of commuting or exchanging; the substitution of one thing for another; also, a change or alteration.  It can refer to a reduction in a sentence, the daily travel to and from work, or the exchange of something.  |例句：The governor granted him a commutation of his sentence. 州长减轻了他的刑罚。

例句：The daily commutation between home and office can be tiring.  每天往返于家和办公室的通勤很累人。


近义词：reduction, exchange, alteration, substitution, commute,  travel


反义词：increase (in sentence),  imprisonment (in context of sentence commutation)


词性：名词
