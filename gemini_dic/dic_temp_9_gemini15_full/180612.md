reglazing:/ˌriːˈɡleɪzɪŋ/| n. |重新上釉；重新烧釉；翻新釉面|The process of applying a new glaze to a surface, such as ceramic tiles, cookware, or a bathtub.  It involves removing the old glaze and applying a fresh coat.  This can improve the appearance, durability, and hygiene of the surface.|例句：The bathroom needs reglazing; the tiles are chipped and stained.  浴室需要重新上釉；瓷砖已经破裂和染色了。

The kitchen sink is in dire need of reglazing. 厨房水槽急需重新上釉。


近义词：re-enameling, refinishing|反义词：glazing (initial application) | 名词
