paraffinized:/ˌpærəˈfɪnaɪzd/| adj.|石蜡包埋的；经石蜡处理的| Treated with paraffin wax, typically to preserve biological specimens for microscopic examination.  |例句：The tissue samples were paraffinized before being sectioned for microscopic analysis.  这些组织样本在切片进行显微分析之前进行了石蜡包埋处理。 |例句：The paraffinized sections revealed the presence of cancerous cells.  经石蜡处理的切片显示出癌细胞的存在。|近义词：wax-embedded, preserved|反义词：unembedded, fresh
