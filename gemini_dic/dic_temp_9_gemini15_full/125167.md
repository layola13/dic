multibirth:/ˈmʌltiˌbɜːrθ/ | n. |多胎出生；多胞胎生育 |The birth of more than one offspring at a time, such as twins, triplets, or quadruplets.  This term encompasses pregnancies resulting in multiple babies. | Example: The multibirth was a joyous occasion for the family. 这对家庭来说，多胞胎的出生是一个令人高兴的时刻。|近义词：multiple birth, plural birth |反义词：single birth
