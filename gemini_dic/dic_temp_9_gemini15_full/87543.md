heterography:/ˌhɛtəˈrɒɡrəfi/ | n. |异体字；异体拼写法 | The use of different spellings for the same word or morpheme.  Heterography is often seen in languages with inconsistent spelling conventions or those undergoing orthographic reform. For example, the words "colour" (British English) and "color" (American English) are heterographs. | Example: The existence of "analyse" and "analyze" exemplifies heterography.  异体字“analyse”和“analyze”的存在就是异体拼写法的例子。|近义词：variant spelling, alternative spelling |反义词：homography
