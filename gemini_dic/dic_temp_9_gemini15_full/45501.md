curdling:/ˈkɜːr(d)lɪŋ/ | v. | 凝结；使凝结 | To curdle means to change from a liquid to a solid or semi-solid state, often referring to milk turning sour.  It can also describe a similar process with other liquids or substances. | Example: The milk curdled after sitting out in the sun. 牛奶放在太阳下后凝结了。The fear in his heart curdled into a cold dread. 他内心的恐惧变成了冰冷的恐惧。 | 近义词：coagulate, thicken, clot | 反义词：liquefy, thin
