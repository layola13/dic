possibly:/ˈpɑːsəbli/ /ˈpɒsəbli/ | adv. |可能地，也许 |It means something might happen or be true, but it is not certain.  It expresses a degree of uncertainty or probability. |It might possibly rain later.  =  待会儿可能下雨。|probably, perhaps, conceivably | impossibly, certainly, definitely


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It is possibly the best solution. = 这可能是最好的解决方案。
He possibly knows the answer. = 他可能知道答案。


一般过去时:
It was possibly the coldest winter on record. = 那可能是记录在案的最冷的冬天。
She possibly misunderstood the instructions. = 她可能误解了指示。


现在完成时:
