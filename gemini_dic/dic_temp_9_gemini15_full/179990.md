refilter:/ˌriːˈfɪltər/ | vt. |再次过滤；重新过滤 |To filter again; to filter something a second time or more.  This often implies a more thorough or refined filtering process than the initial one. |例句：We need to refilter the water to remove all impurities. 我们需要再次过滤水以去除所有杂质。  The data was refiltered using a more sophisticated algorithm. 使用更复杂的算法对数据进行了重新过滤。|近义词：reprocess, refine, screen again |反义词：unfilter
