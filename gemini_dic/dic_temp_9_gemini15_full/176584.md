reaccede:/ˌriːækˈsiːd/| v.|重新加入，再次加入，恢复职位|To accede again; to return to a former position or status.  This word implies a previous accession or joining, followed by a departure and subsequent return.|例句：After a brief retirement, the general reacceded to his command.  将军短暂退休后，又重新掌权了。

例句：The former president reacceded to power after the coup failed. 政变失败后，前总统重新掌权。


近义词：reinstate, resume, return to


反义词：resign, relinquish, abdicate


词性：动词
