dialed:/daɪld/| v. |拨打（电话）；调节（收音机等）|To use a telephone to connect to someone; to adjust the controls of a device such as a radio or television. |例句：I dialed his number but he didn't answer. 我拨了他的号码，但他没有接。|例句：She dialed the radio to find a clear station. 她调了收音机以找到一个清晰的电台。|近义词：called, phoned, rang |反义词：hung up, disconnected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I dial my mom every Sunday. = 我每个星期天都给我妈妈打电话。
She dials the radio to a specific frequency. = 她把收音机调到一个特定的频率。


一般过去时:
He dialed 911 after the accident. = 事故发生后，他拨打了911。
They dialed the wrong number. = 他们拨错了号码。


现在完成时:
I have dialed that number several times today. = 我今天已经拨打过那个号码好几次了。
She has dialed in to the conference call. = 她已经加入了电话会议。


过去完成时:
He had dialed the number before realizing it was incorrect. = 他在意识到号码错误之前已经拨打了号码。
They had dialed the wrong extension. = 他们拨错了分机号。


将来时:
I will dial your number as soon as I get home. = 我一到家就拨打你的号码。
She will dial the emergency services if there is a problem. = 如果有问题，她会拨打紧急服务电话。


虚拟语气:
If I had dialed sooner, I might have reached him. = 如果我早点拨打，也许就能联系上他了。
I wish I had dialed the right number. = 我真希望我拨对了号码。


被动语态:
The number was dialed incorrectly. = 号码拨错了。
The radio was dialed to a different station. = 收音机调到了不同的电台。


疑问句:
Have you dialed the number yet? = 你拨打过那个号码了吗？
Which channel did she dial? = 她拨打了哪个频道？


否定句:
I didn't dial the number correctly. = 我没有正确拨打号码。
He didn't dial the emergency services. = 他没有拨打紧急服务电话。


PHRASE->
dial a number: 拨打一个号码
dial in: 拨号接入
speed dial: 快速拨号
rotary dial: 旋转拨号盘
dial-up connection: 拨号连接
