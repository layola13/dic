security: /sɪˈkjʊərɪti/ | n. | 安全；保障；证券；抵押品 | The state of being free from danger or threat.  The protection of a person, place, or thing from loss, damage, or attack. |

例句：

The company has invested heavily in security measures.  这家公司在安保措施上投入巨资。

National security is a top priority for the government.  国家安全是政府的首要任务。


近义词：safety, protection, safeguard, surety

反义词：danger, risk, threat, insecurity


词性：名词

词源:

The word "security" originates from the Old French word "securité," which in turn comes from the Latin word "secūritās."  "Secūritās" is derived from "sēcūrus," meaning "free from care," "safe," or "secure."  The evolution reflects the core meaning of the word: freedom from danger or risk.
