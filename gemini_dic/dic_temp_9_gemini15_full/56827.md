dormitory:/ˈdɔːrmɪtəri/| n. |宿舍，寝室 |A building or a room containing several sleeping rooms, especially for students.  |例句：The university dormitory is quite modern and well-equipped.  大学宿舍相当现代化且设备齐全。|例句：Many students prefer living in a dormitory to renting an apartment. 许多学生更喜欢住在宿舍而不是租公寓。|近义词：residence hall, hostel|反义词：private room, apartment

He lives in the university dormitory. = 他住在大学宿舍。
