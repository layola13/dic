mule:/mjuːl/| n. |骡子；倔强的人；走私者；(尤指)走私毒品的人 |A mule is a hybrid animal, the offspring of a male donkey and a female horse.  It is also used to describe a stubborn person, or someone who smuggles goods, especially drugs. |例句：The farmer used mules to plow his fields. 这个农民用骡子耕地。He was known as a mule for his stubborn refusal to cooperate. 他以顽固拒绝合作而闻名。The police arrested the mule carrying a large quantity of cocaine. 警方逮捕了携带大量可卡因的走私犯。|近义词：hybrid, donkey, stubborn person, smuggler, trafficker |反义词：horse, compliant person
