frenchwomen:/ˈfrɛntʃˌwɪmɪn/| n. |法国女人；法国妇女|The word "frenchwomen" refers to women who are citizens of France or who are of French descent.  It is a plural noun. |The frenchwomen were elegantly dressed. 法国妇女们穿着优雅。  Many frenchwomen are known for their fashion sense. 许多法国妇女以其时尚感而闻名。|French girls, ladies from France|No direct antonym exists, as it's a descriptive noun.
