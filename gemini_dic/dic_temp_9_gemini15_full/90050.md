hospitalization: /ˌhɒspɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌhɑːspɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |住院治疗；住院 | The act of being hospitalized; a period of time spent in a hospital as a patient.  | Example: His hospitalization lasted for two weeks.  他的住院治疗持续了两周。 |近义词：hospital stay, confinement (in hospital) |反义词：discharge, release from hospital |词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Hospitalization is expensive in many countries. = 在许多国家，住院治疗费用昂贵。
My insurance covers hospitalization costs. = 我的保险涵盖住院费用。


一般过去时:
His hospitalization followed a serious accident. = 他在严重事故后住院了。
The hospitalization was necessary to monitor his condition. = 住院治疗是为了监测他的病情而必要的。


现在完成时:
She has had several hospitalizations in the past year. = 她去年经历了几次住院治疗。
The patient has undergone hospitalization for his heart condition. = 这位病人因心脏病住院治疗。


过去完成时:
Before the surgery, he had already experienced a brief hospitalization. = 在手术之前，他已经短暂住院过一次。
They had completed all the pre-hospitalization tests. = 他们已经完成了所有住院前的检查。


将来时:
His hospitalization will likely be several weeks long. = 他很可能要住院几周。
Further hospitalization will be necessary if his condition doesn't improve. = 如果他的病情没有好转，将需要进一步住院治疗。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't needed hospitalization, he would have recovered faster. = 如果他不需要住院治疗，他会恢复得更快。
I wish my hospitalization hadn't been so long. = 我希望我的住院时间不要这么长。


被动语态:

疑问句:
