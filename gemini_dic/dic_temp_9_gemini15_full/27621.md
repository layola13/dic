cancericidal:/ˌkænsərˈɪsaɪdl/| adj.| 杀癌的，抗癌的 |  Capable of destroying or killing cancer cells.  It refers to substances or treatments that have the ability to eliminate cancerous cells. | Example:  The new drug showed promising cancericidal properties in laboratory tests.  这种新药在实验室测试中显示出令人鼓舞的杀癌特性。  | 近义词：anticancer, carcinolytic, cytotoxic (in the context of cancer) | 反义词：carcinogenic
