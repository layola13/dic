schizophyte:/ˈskɪzəˌfaɪt/| n. |裂殖植物，分裂生殖植物|A schizophyte is a plant that reproduces by cell division (binary fission), typically referring to bacteria and cyanobacteria.  These organisms lack a distinct nucleus and other membrane-bound organelles, characteristics that distinguish them from other plants and algae.  They are also known as prokaryotes. |Example:  Cyanobacteria are examples of schizophytes, playing a crucial role in nitrogen fixation in various ecosystems. 蓝细菌是裂殖植物的例子，在各种生态系统中的固氮作用中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词: prokaryote, bacterium, cyanobacterium |反义词: eukaryote
