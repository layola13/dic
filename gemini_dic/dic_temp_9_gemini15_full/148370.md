overspenders:/ˌoʊvərˈspɛndərz/| n.|过度消费的人|People who habitually spend more money than they earn or have budgeted for.  They may struggle with financial management and often accumulate debt. |Example:  The report highlighted the financial difficulties faced by many overspenders.|近义词：big spenders, spendthrifts, profligates|反义词：misers, penny-pinchers, frugal people
