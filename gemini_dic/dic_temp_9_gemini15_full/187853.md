roshi:/ˈroʊʃi/ | n. |禅宗师父；罗汉|A roshi is a highly respected Zen Buddhist master, often an abbot of a Zen monastery.  The term is derived from Japanese and signifies a teacher of profound wisdom and spiritual attainment.|例句：The roshi gave a profound dharma talk on the nature of reality.  禅宗师父就现实的本质做了一次深刻的法义演讲。|近义词：master, teacher, abbot, preceptor|反义词：student, disciple
