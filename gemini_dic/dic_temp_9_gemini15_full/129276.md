nodical:/ˈnɒdɪkəl/| adj.|点头的；表示同意的|Relating to or resembling a nod; expressive of assent.  The nodical gesture showed her agreement.|例句：Her nodical response indicated her agreement.  她的点头表示同意。|近义词：affirmative, approving, consenting|反义词：disapproving, dissenting, negative


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The dog nods its head when it wants food. = 狗想吃东西的时候会点头。
He nods his head in agreement. = 他点头表示同意。


一般过去时:
She nodded her head in understanding. = 她点头表示理解。
He nodded politely to the guest. = 他礼貌地向客人点头。


现在完成时:
She has nodded off during the meeting. = 她会议期间打瞌睡了。
He has nodded in agreement several times. = 他已经几次点头表示同意。


过去完成时:
He had already nodded off before the movie ended. = 电影结束前他已经睡着了。
She had nodded her head before realizing the mistake. = 她点头后才意识到错误。


将来时:
He will nod his head when he hears the answer. = 他听到答案后会点头。
She will probably nod off during the long lecture. = 冗长的讲座中她可能会打瞌睡。


虚拟语气:
If he had nodded, it would have meant agreement. = 如果他点头，就意味着同意。
I wish he had nodded instead of shouting. = 我希望他点头而不是大喊。


被动语态:
His agreement was indicated by a nod. = 他的同意是通过点头表示的。
The signal was conveyed by a nod of the head. = 信号是通过点头传达的。


疑问句:
Did he nod his head? = 他点头了吗？
Does she often nod off during classes? = 她上课经常打瞌睡吗？


否定句:
He didn't nod his head in response. = 他没有点头回应。
She didn't nod off during the speech. = 她演讲期间没有打瞌睡。
