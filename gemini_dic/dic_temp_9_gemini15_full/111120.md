literarily:/ˈlɪtərerəli/ | adv. |  字面上地；确实地；简直 |  In a literal sense, without exaggeration or figurative language.  It emphasizes the exact meaning of words.  |例句：He literally jumped for joy. 他欣喜若狂。（字面意思：他真的跳了起来）
例句：I am literally starving. 我真的饿死了。（字面意思：我在饿到死亡的边缘）| 近义词：literally, actually, truly, really | 反义词：figuratively, metaphorically


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He literally follows the instructions. 他严格按照指示去做。
She literally shines in her role. 她在这角色中光芒四射。

一般过去时:
He literally ran out of the room. 他跑出了房间。

过去完成时:

将来时:

被动语态:

否定句:
