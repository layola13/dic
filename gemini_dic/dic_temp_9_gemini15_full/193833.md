self-developing:/ˌsɛlf dɪˈvɛləpɪŋ/| adj.| 自我发展的；自主发展的 |  Something that is self-developing grows or improves without external help or intervention.  It implies an inherent capacity for growth and progress. | 例句：The self-developing software automatically updates its features.  =  这个自我发展的软件会自动更新其功能。

The self-developing economy shows great potential for growth. = 自主发展的经济显示出巨大的增长潜力。


近义词：autonomous, independent, self-sufficient


反义词：dependent, reliant, externally-driven


词性：形容词
