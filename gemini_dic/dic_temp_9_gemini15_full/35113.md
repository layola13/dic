cloudage:/ˈklaʊdeɪdʒ/ | n. | 云层；大量云；多云天气 | The term "cloudage" refers to a large amount or mass of clouds, often suggesting a significant weather event or atmospheric condition.  It evokes a sense of density and extent.  The word is less common than "cloud cover" or simply "clouds," but it emphasizes the overall effect of the clouds. | Example: The ship vanished into the dense cloudage. 那艘船消失在浓厚的云层中。 The heavy cloudage obscured the mountain peaks.  浓厚的云层遮蔽了山峰。 | 近义词：cloud cover, clouds, overcast | 反义词：clear skies, sunshine
