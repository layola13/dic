rigged:/rɪɡd/| adj., v. |被操纵的；装备的；伪造的；准备好的 |To manipulate or control (a process, system, etc.) dishonestly, typically in order to achieve a desired result.  Also, to equip or furnish something. |例句：The election was rigged.  选举是被操纵的。  The boat was rigged with sails.  船上装有帆。|近义词：manipulated, fixed, controlled, equipped |反义词：fair, honest, legitimate
