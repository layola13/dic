laryngotomy:/ˌlærɪŋˈɡɒtəmi/|/ˌlærɪŋˈɡɑːtəmi/| n. |喉部切开术|Surgical incision into the larynx.  A laryngotomy is a procedure performed to create an opening in the larynx, typically to establish an airway.  This is usually done in emergency situations when the upper airway is obstructed.|Example:  The surgeon performed an emergency laryngotomy to secure the patient's airway.  医生进行了一次紧急喉部切开术以确保病人的呼吸道畅通。|近义词：laryngostomy, tracheotomy (though tracheotomy is below the larynx)|反义词：laryngoplasty (surgical repair)|名词
