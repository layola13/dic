dig-in:/dɪɡ ɪn/| v.| 开始认真地工作或吃饭； 坚守阵地； 挖掘； |to start eating enthusiastically; to start working hard and seriously; to become firmly established in a place or position; to begin digging. |例句：Let's dig in! = 我们开始吃吧！

例句：The soldiers dug in for a long siege. = 士兵们坚守阵地准备长期围困。


近义词：tuck in, get stuck in,  fortify, entrench


反义词：give up, retreat, abandon


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He digs in to his work every day. = 他每天都认真投入工作。
They dig in to their delicious meal. = 他们尽情享用美味佳肴。


一般过去时:
She dug in and finished the project on time. = 她坚持到底，按时完成了项目。
We dug in our heels and refused to compromise. = 我们坚持己见，拒绝妥协。


现在完成时:
They have dug in their heels and are refusing to negotiate. = 他们态度坚决，拒绝谈判。
The team has dug in and is working hard to meet the deadline. = 团队成员齐心协力，努力赶上截止日期。


过去完成时:
The army had dug in before the enemy attacked. = 敌军进攻前，军队已构筑了防御工事。
Before the storm hit, they had dug in for the night. = 暴风雨来临前，他们已做好过夜的准备。


将来时:
We will dig in and fight for what we believe in. = 我们将坚持战斗，为我们所信仰的事业而战。
They will dig in and complete the task, no matter how difficult it is. = 不管有多难，他们都会坚持完成任务。


虚拟语气:
If I had dug in earlier, I would have finished the project sooner. = 如果我早点开始认真工作，我就能早点完成项目。
If they had dug in their heels, the situation might have been different. = 如果他们坚持己见，情况可能会有所不同。


被动语态:
The trenches were dug in by the soldiers. = 战壕是由士兵们挖掘的。
The foundations of the building were dug in deep. = 建筑的地基打得很深。


疑问句:
Will they dig in and fight for their rights? = 他们会坚持战斗，为自己的权利而战吗？
Are they going to dig in and refuse to leave? = 他们会坚持不走吗？


否定句:
He didn't dig in and missed the deadline. = 他没有认真工作，错过了截止日期。
They didn't dig in their heels and compromised easily. = 他们没有坚持己见，轻易妥协了。


PHRASE->
dig in one's heels = 坚持己见
dig in deep = 深入研究/挖掘
dig in for the long haul = 做好长期准备
dig in and fight = 坚持战斗
dig in the garden = 在花园里挖土
