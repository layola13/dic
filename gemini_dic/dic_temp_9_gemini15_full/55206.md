dissertations:/ˌdɪsərˈteɪʃənz/| n. |论文；学位论文|A dissertation is a long piece of writing on a particular subject, especially one that is done for a university degree.  It is usually based on original research and demonstrates the author's expertise and ability to conduct independent scholarly work. |Example:  She completed her doctoral dissertation on the impact of social media on political discourse. 她完成了关于社交媒体对政治话语影响的博士论文。


Example: He is currently writing his dissertation on the history of ancient Rome. 他目前正在撰写关于古罗马历史的论文。|近义词：thesis, treatise, paper|反义词：brief, summary
