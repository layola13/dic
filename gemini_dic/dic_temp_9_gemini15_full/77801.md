generalizer:/ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪzər/ | n. |概括者；推广者；泛化器 | A person or thing that generalizes;  a device or system that performs generalization.  A generalizer in machine learning is a model that learns to map inputs to outputs in a way that generalizes to unseen data. | 例句：The researcher acted as a generalizer, drawing broad conclusions from the specific data.  研究人员扮演了概括者的角色，从具体数据中得出广泛的结论。

The new software is a powerful generalizer, capable of handling a wide range of inputs.  这款新软件是一个强大的泛化器，能够处理各种各样的输入。|近义词：summarizer, abstractor, synthesizer |反义词：specialist, particularizer
