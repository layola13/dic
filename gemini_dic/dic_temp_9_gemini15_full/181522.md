rekindler: /riˈkaɪndlə(r)/ | n. | 复燃者；重新点燃者 | A person or thing that rekindles something, especially a feeling or relationship.  |  例句：The old photograph served as a rekindler of happy memories.  老照片唤起了美好的回忆。 | 近义词：reviver, igniter, re-igniter | 反义词：extinguisher


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The old flame rekindles their passion. = 旧情复燃。
He rekindles the fire in the hearth. = 他重新点燃壁炉里的火。


一般过去时:
The news rekindled her hope. = 这个消息重新燃起了她的希望。
They rekindled their friendship after many years. = 许多年后，他们重拾友谊。


现在完成时:
The trip has rekindled their love for travel. = 这趟旅行重新燃起了他们对旅行的热爱。
She has rekindled her interest in painting. = 她重新燃起了对绘画的兴趣。


过去完成时:
He had rekindled the fire before they arrived. = 他们在到达之前，他已经重新点燃了火。
They had rekindled their romance before the wedding. = 他们在婚礼前重燃爱火。


将来时:
The reunion will rekindle old friendships. = 聚会将会重燃旧日情谊。
I hope this letter will rekindle your interest in the project. = 我希望这封信能重新激起你对这个项目的兴趣。


虚拟语气:
If we rekindled our friendship, it would be wonderful. = 如果我们重拾友谊，那将是美好的。
I wish we could rekindle the magic of our youth. = 我希望我们能够重温青春的激情。


被动语态:
Their love was rekindled by a chance meeting. = 他们的爱情因一次偶然的相遇而重燃。
The fire was rekindled by the wind. = 火被风重新点燃了。


疑问句:

否定句:
He couldn't rekindle the old passion. = 他无法重新燃起旧情。
