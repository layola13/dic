previously:/ˈpriːviəsli/ | adv. |先前地，以前地 |Previously, I worked as a teacher.  以前，我是一名老师。|例句：He had previously served in the army. 他以前在军队服役。  She previously held a position in marketing. 她以前在市场营销部门任职。|近义词：formerly, earlier, before, beforehand |反义词： subsequently, later, afterward


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He previously works at a bank. 他以前在银行工作。(This example is grammatically incorrect in simple present tense.  The adverb "previously" implies a past action.)
They previously live in London. 他们以前住在伦敦。(This example is grammatically incorrect in simple present tense.  The adverb "previously" implies a past action.)

一般过去时:
He previously worked at a bank. 他以前在银行工作。
They previously lived in London. 他们以前住在伦敦。


现在完成时:
He has previously worked at a bank. 他以前曾在银行工作过。
She has previously visited Paris. 她以前去过巴黎。


过去完成时:
He had previously worked at a bank before he moved to New York. 他搬到纽约之前曾在银行工作过。
They had previously lived in London before moving to the countryside. 他们搬到乡下之前住在伦敦。


将来时:
(Previously is rarely used with future tense.  It describes a past action.)
He will previously work at a bank.(Incorrect usage) 他将以前在银行工作。(This is grammatically incorrect and logically nonsensical.)
She will previously visit Paris.(Incorrect usage) 她将以前去巴黎。(This is grammatically incorrect and logically nonsensical.)


虚拟语气:
If he had previously known about the problem, he would have acted differently. 如果他以前知道这个问题，他会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had previously considered the consequences. 我希望我以前考虑过后果。


被动语态:
The matter was previously discussed at the meeting.  这个问题在会议上先前已经讨论过。
The report had previously been submitted to the committee. 报告先前已提交给委员会。


疑问句:
Had you previously met him before the party? 你在聚会之前见过他吗？
Have you previously worked with this software? 你以前用过这个软件吗？


否定句:
He had not previously encountered this problem. 他以前从未遇到过这个问题。
She did not previously know him. 她以前不认识他。
