reword:/ˌriːˈwɜːrd/| vt.|改写，改述|To express something in different words, typically to make it clearer or more concise.  |例句：Could you reword that sentence?  你能改写一下那句话吗？|近义词：rephrase, paraphrase, restate|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be to leave the wording unchanged.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rewords his emails before sending them. = 他在发送邮件之前会改写邮件内容。
She rewords the instructions to make them easier to understand. = 她改写说明以使其更容易理解。

一般过去时:
The editor reworded the article to improve its clarity. = 编辑改写了这篇文章以提高其清晰度。
I reworded my response to be more polite. = 我改写了我的回复使其更礼貌。

现在完成时:
He has reworded the contract several times. = 他已经多次修改了合同。
She has reworded her presentation to make it more engaging. = 她已经修改了她的演示文稿，使其更引人入胜。

过去完成时:
After reviewing the feedback, she had reworded the entire essay. = 在审查反馈后，她已经改写了整篇论文。
He had reworded his speech before delivering it to the audience. = 他在向观众发表演讲之前已经修改了他的演讲稿。

将来时:
I will reword my application letter before submitting it. = 我将在提交申请信之前修改它。
She will reword the email to address the client's concerns. = 她将修改邮件以解决客户的担忧。

虚拟语气:
If I were to reword this, I would make it more concise. = 如果我要改写这个，我会使其更简洁。
I wish I had reworded my email before sending it. = 我希望我在发送电子邮件之前已经改写了它。

被动语态:
The sentence was reworded by the teacher. = 这句话被老师改写了。
The instructions are being reworded to be more user-friendly. = 说明正在被改写以使其更方便用户使用。

疑问句:
Should I reword this paragraph? = 我应该改写这段文字吗？
How would you reword this complex sentence? = 你将如何改写这个复杂的句子？

否定句:
I didn't reword the letter, I just added a few commas. = 我没有改写信，我只是添加了一些逗号。
He refused to reword his statement despite the criticism. = 尽管受到批评，他拒绝改写他的声明。
