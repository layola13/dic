deerberry:/ˈdɪrberi/| n. |越橘属植物；鹿莓|A small, dark-colored berry, typically bluish-black, that grows on low-lying shrubs of the genus *Vaccinium*.  They are often found in acidic, sandy soils and have a tart flavor.  Deerberries are related to blueberries and cranberries.|例句：The deerberries grew abundantly in the shaded areas of the forest.  森林阴凉处长满了鹿莓。

The children enjoyed picking wild deerberries during their hike. 孩子们在远足期间喜欢采摘野生鹿莓。|近义词：huckleberry, blueberry, cranberry|反义词：无
