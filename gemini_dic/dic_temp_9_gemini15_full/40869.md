cooked:/kʊkt/| adj.|煮熟的，烹调过的 | Cooked food is easier to digest.  煮熟的食物更容易消化。|近义词：prepared, done, ready |反义词：raw, uncooked


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The chef cooks delicious meals. = 厨师烹调美味佳肴。
She cooks dinner every evening. = 她每天晚上做饭。


一般过去时:
I cooked pasta for dinner last night. = 我昨晚做了意大利面。
He cooked a big breakfast for us. = 他为我们做了一顿丰盛的早餐。


现在完成时:
I have cooked many different dishes in my life. = 我一生中做过许多不同的菜。
She has cooked this recipe many times. = 她做过很多次这个食谱的菜。


过去完成时:
We had cooked dinner before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，我们已经做好了晚饭。
They had cooked all the food for the party. = 他们已经为派对准备好了所有的食物。


将来时:
I will cook dinner for you tomorrow. = 我明天给你做饭。
She will cook a special meal for his birthday. = 她将为他生日做一顿特别的饭。


虚拟语气:
If I cooked more often, I would be healthier. = 如果我经常做饭，我会更健康。
I wish I had cooked more for the party. = 我真希望我为派对准备了更多的食物。


被动语态:
The chicken was cooked perfectly. = 鸡肉烹调得完美无缺。
The meal is being cooked right now. = 饭菜正在烹调中。


疑问句:
Is the meat cooked yet? = 肉熟了吗？
Have you cooked dinner yet? = 你做晚饭了吗？


否定句:
I didn't cook anything today. = 我今天什么都没做。
The vegetables weren't cooked properly. = 蔬菜没有煮熟。
