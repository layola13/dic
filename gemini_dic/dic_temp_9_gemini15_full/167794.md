primers:/ˈpraɪmər/| n.|引物；初级读本；入门书|A primer is a short introductory book, especially one for teaching children to read, or a substance used to prepare a surface for painting or other processes, or in molecular biology, a short single-stranded DNA sequence used to initiate DNA synthesis.|例句：This book serves as a good primer for learning Python. 这本书是学习Python的好入门书。|近义词：introduction, handbook, textbook, starter|反义词：advanced text, sequel
