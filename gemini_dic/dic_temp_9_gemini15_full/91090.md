huzza:/ˈhʌzə/| int.|欢呼，喝彩|An exclamation of joyous approval or triumph.|例句：The crowd roared "Huzza!" as the champion crossed the finish line. 观众在冠军冲过终点线时大声欢呼“万岁！”|近义词：hurrah, hooray, hip hip hooray|反义词：boo, hiss


USAGE->
This word is an interjection, so it doesn't have tenses like verbs do.  It's used to express excitement or approval.

PHRASE->
There aren't common phrases directly using "huzza" as it's an interjection.  Its usage is standalone.
