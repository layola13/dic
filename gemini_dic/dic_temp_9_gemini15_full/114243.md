mailers:/ˈmeɪlərz/| n. |邮寄者；邮递员；邮寄广告；宣传册|A mailer is a person or thing that mails something.  It can refer to a person who sends mail, a piece of direct mail advertising, or a company that handles mailings.|例句：The company hired several mailers to send out the brochures. 这家公司雇佣了几名邮寄员来分发宣传册。


例句：The political mailers arrived just before the election.  政治宣传册在选举前夕到达。


近义词：sender, advertiser, distributor


反义词：recipient, receiver


词性：名词
