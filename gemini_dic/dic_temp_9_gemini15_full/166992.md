presubmit:/priːsəbˈmɪt/| v.|预提交，预先提交 |To submit (something) before the official deadline or before a formal review.  It implies submitting something for preliminary consideration, feedback, or approval before the final submission. |例句：The author presubmitted the manuscript to his editor for feedback before the official deadline. 作者在正式截止日期前将稿件预先提交给编辑以获得反馈。

例句：The team presubmitted their project proposal to their manager for review. 团队将他们的项目提案预先提交给经理进行审查。


近义词：prefile, submit in advance, forward beforehand

反义词：postsubmit (this word is not commonly used)
