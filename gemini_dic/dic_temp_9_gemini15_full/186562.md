ridge:/rɪdʒ/ | n. | 山脊，脊状物，隆起部分 | A long, narrow, raised strip of land, especially a mountain range.  |例句：The hikers followed the ridge to the summit.  登山者沿着山脊走到山顶。

例句：The roof had a sharp ridge. 屋顶有一个尖锐的脊状物。

近义词：crest, peak, summit, spine | 反义词：valley, hollow, depression | 词性:名词


词源:  Old English *hrycg, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch rug and German Rücken ‘back’.  The meaning ‘mountain ridge’ developed from the earlier sense of ‘back’ or ‘spine’.
