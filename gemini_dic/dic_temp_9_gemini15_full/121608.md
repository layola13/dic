misorganize:/ˌmɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪz/| v.| 组织混乱；安排不当 |To arrange or organize badly or inefficiently; to cause disorder or confusion in.|The project was misorganized, leading to missed deadlines and wasted resources. 项目组织混乱，导致错过最后期限和资源浪费。|disorganize, jumble, clutter|organize, order, systematize|动词
