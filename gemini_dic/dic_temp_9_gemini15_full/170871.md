pseudohypoparathyroidism:/ˌsuːdoʊˌhaɪpoʊˌpærəθaɪˈrɔɪdɪzəm/ | n. | 假性甲状旁腺功能减退症 | A group of genetic disorders characterized by resistance to the effects of parathyroid hormone (PTH), leading to hypocalcemia (low blood calcium) and other symptoms.  It's often associated with Albright hereditary osteodystrophy (AHO).  There are different types, including pseudohypoparathyroidism type 1a (PHP1a) and type 1b (PHP1b), distinguished by genetic variations and clinical features. |  The patient was diagnosed with pseudohypoparathyroidism after exhibiting symptoms of hypocalcemia and characteristic skeletal abnormalities.  这位病人表现出低血钙和特征性骨骼异常症状后被诊断为假性甲状旁腺功能减退症。 |  hypoparathyroidism, Albright hereditary osteodystrophy | hyperparathyroidism |  n.
