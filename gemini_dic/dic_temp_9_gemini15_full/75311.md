frontline:/ˈfrʌntlaɪn/ | n. adj. |前线；最前沿；第一线 |The place or position where fighting or conflict is taking place; the foremost position in any activity or struggle.  |例句：The soldiers fought bravely on the frontline.  士兵们在前线英勇作战。

例句：She's on the frontline of the fight against climate change.  她在对抗气候变化的最前沿。


近义词：forefront, vanguard, leading edge | 反义词：rear, background


词性: 名词，形容词
