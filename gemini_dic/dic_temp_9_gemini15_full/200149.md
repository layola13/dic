skillless:/ˈskɪllɪs/| adj.|缺乏技巧的；笨拙的；无能的|Lacking skill; clumsy; incompetent.  The skillless worker struggled with the complex machinery.|近义词：unskilled, inept, clumsy, awkward |反义词：skilled, adept, proficient, expert


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is skillless at playing the piano. = 他弹钢琴很笨拙。
She is skillless in her approach to problem-solving. = 她解决问题的方法很笨拙。


一般过去时:

The presentation was skillless and poorly organized. = 演示缺乏技巧且组织混乱。
