jaunts:/dʒɔːnts/| n. |短途旅行；轻松愉快的短途出行 | A jaunt is a short journey or excursion, typically one taken for pleasure.  It suggests a lighthearted and enjoyable trip, often implying a relatively short duration and a lack of serious purpose. |例句：We took a jaunt to the coast last weekend.  我们上周末去海岸边短途旅行了一趟。

They planned a jaunt to the mountains for their anniversary. 他们计划去山区庆祝结婚纪念日。|近义词：excursion, outing, trip, excursion |反义词：long journey, arduous expedition
