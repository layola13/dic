depopulators:/ˌdiːˈpɒpjʊleɪtərz/| n.|人口减少者；使人口减少的事物|Someone or something that causes a reduction in population.  The term often implies a negative impact, such as disease, war, or famine.|例句：The series of droughts acted as depopulators in the rural areas.  一系列干旱导致农村地区人口减少。|例句：Overhunting and habitat destruction are major depopulators of wildlife.  过度狩猎和栖息地破坏是野生动物数量减少的主要原因。|近义词：population reducers, depopulants|反义词：population increasers, population boosters|名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The disease acts as a depopulator in this region. = 这场疾病在这个地区导致人口减少。
These policies are depopulators of rural areas. = 这些政策导致农村地区人口减少。

一般过去时:
The war was a major depopulator of the city. = 那场战争导致该城市人口大量减少。
The famine acted as a depopulator in the 19th century. = 19世纪的饥荒导致人口减少。

现在完成时:
Industrialization has been a depopulator of rural communities. = 工业化已经导致农村社区人口减少。
The ongoing conflict has depopulated the region. = 持续的冲突已经使该地区人口减少。

过去完成时:
The plague had already depopulated the village before help arrived. = 在救援到来之前，瘟疫已经使村庄人口减少。
The drought had depopulated the area by the time the rains came. = 等到雨水来的时候，干旱已经使该地区人口减少了。

将来时:
Climate change will be a depopulator of coastal regions. = 气候变化将导致沿海地区人口减少。
Continued deforestation will depopulate this forest. = 持续的森林砍伐将使这片森林的动物数量减少。

虚拟语气:
If the government implemented better policies, it would not be such a depopulator. = 如果政府实施更好的政策，它就不会导致人口如此减少。
If the disease were more easily treated, it wouldn't be such a depopulator. = 如果这种疾病更容易治疗，它就不会导致人口如此减少。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

The new farming techniques are not depopulators of rural areas. = 新的耕作技术并没有导致农村地区人口减少。
