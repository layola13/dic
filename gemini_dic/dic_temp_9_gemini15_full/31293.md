chapiter:/ˈtʃæptər/| n.|章节；篇章|A division of a book or other long work, typically one dealing with a particular aspect of the subject.|例句：The third chapter is the most interesting part of the book. 第三章是这本书最有趣的部分。|近义词：section, division, part|反义词:None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The book has ten chapters. = 这本书有十个章节。
This chapter discusses the history of the topic. = 这一章节讨论了该主题的历史。

一般过去时:
We read the first chapter last night. = 我们昨晚读了第一章。
She finished writing the chapter yesterday. = 她昨天完成了这一章节的写作。

现在完成时:
I have read all the chapters. = 我已经读完了所有章节。
They have completed the first draft of the chapter. = 他们已经完成了章节的第一稿。

过去完成时:
We had already read the first three chapters before the meeting. = 我们在会议之前已经读完了前三章。
She had written most of the chapter before her deadline. = 在截止日期之前她已经写完了大部分章节。

将来时:
I will read the next chapter tomorrow. = 我明天将阅读下一章节。
They will finish the chapter by the end of the week. = 他们将在本周末前完成本章节。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would read the entire chapter. = 如果我有更多时间，我会读完整个章节。
I wish I had understood the previous chapter better. = 我希望我更好地理解了上一章节。

被动语态:
The chapter was written by a famous author. = 这一章节是由一位著名作家撰写的。
The chapter is being edited now. = 这一章节正在被编辑。

疑问句:
How many chapters are there in the book? = 这本书有多少章节？
Which chapter are you reading now? = 你现在读的是哪一章节？

否定句:
The book doesn't have many chapters. = 这本书没有很多章节。
I haven't read that chapter yet. = 我还没读那章节。
