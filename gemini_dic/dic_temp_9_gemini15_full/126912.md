nard:/nɑːrd/| n. |nard, nard oil|香附子；纯香油|Nard is a fragrant plant, and its oil was highly valued in ancient times as a perfume and ointment.  It is mentioned frequently in the Bible and other ancient texts.  The exact species of plant referred to as "nard" is debated, but it is likely a type of spikenard.|The Bible mentions nard as a precious and costly perfume.  The woman anointed Jesus’ feet with a jar of very expensive nard.|近义词：spikenard|反义词：无
