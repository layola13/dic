gratuity:/ɡræˈtʃuːəti/| n. |小费；赏金；赠与物 |A gratuity is a tip given to someone for a service.  It's a voluntary payment showing appreciation. |例句：We left a generous gratuity for the excellent service. 我们为优质服务留下了丰厚的小费。|例句：The company gave him a gratuity for his years of service. 公司为他多年的服务给予了他一笔奖金。|近义词：tip, bonus, gift |反义词：penalty, fine


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The waiter expects a gratuity for his service. = 服务员期待得到服务费。
They usually leave a gratuity at restaurants. = 他们通常在餐馆付小费。


一般过去时:
She gave the driver a gratuity for his help. = 她给了司机一些小费以感谢他的帮助。

Have you ever received a gratuity for your work? = 你有没有因为工作而收到过奖金？
