concertos:/kənˈtʃɜːrtoʊz/| n. |协奏曲|Plural of concerto.  A musical composition for a solo instrument or instruments accompanied by an orchestra, typically in three movements.  Concertos often showcase the virtuosity of the soloist. |Example:  The pianist gave a stunning performance of the Rachmaninoff Piano Concerto No. 2.  钢琴家对拉赫玛尼诺夫第二钢琴协奏曲进行了精彩的演奏。


|Example: Many famous composers, such as Mozart and Beethoven, wrote beautiful concertos.  许多著名的作曲家，如莫扎特和贝多芬，都创作过优美的协奏曲。|近义词：composition, piece (of music)|反义词：N/A|词性：名词
