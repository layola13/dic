raged:/reɪdʒd/| v.|愤怒，狂怒，肆虐|to feel or express great anger; to continue with violence or uncontrolled force.|例句：He raged at the injustice of it all. 他对这一切的不公正感到愤怒。|例句：The storm raged through the night. 暴风雨整夜肆虐。|近义词：furious, angry, furious, tempestuous|反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rages at the slightest provocation. 他一点就着。
The fire rages uncontrollably. 火势无法控制地蔓延。

一般过去时:
She raged at her brother for breaking her favorite vase. 她因为弟弟打破了她最喜欢的花瓶而大发雷霆。
The wind raged all night long.  风整夜狂吹。

现在完成时:
He has raged against the system for years. 他多年来一直反抗体制。
The fire has raged for days, consuming vast areas of forest.  大火已经持续燃烧数日，吞噬了大片森林。

过去完成时:
After he had raged at the referee, he was ejected from the game. 他对裁判大发雷霆后，被逐出了比赛。
The storm had raged for hours before the power went out.  暴风雨肆虐数小时后，电力才中断。

将来时:
If he sees this, he will rage. 如果他看到这个，他会大发雷霆。
The volcano is expected to rage for several more days. 预计火山还会喷发数天。

虚拟语气:
If I were in his shoes, I would rage too. 如果我站在他的位置上，我也会愤怒。
I wish he wouldn't rage so often. 我希望他不那么经常发脾气。


被动语态:
The city was raged by the hurricane.  城市被飓风肆虐。
His reputation was raged against by his enemies.  他的名声遭到敌人的攻击。

疑问句:
Why are you raging so much? 你为什么这么生气？
Is the storm still raging outside? 外面暴风雨还在肆虐吗？

否定句:
He didn't rage at the news. 他对这个消息并没有生气。
The fire didn't rage for long. 火势没有持续很久。


PHRASE->
rage against something:  对...大发雷霆 (He raged against the injustice.)
rage with anger: 怒火中烧 (He raged with anger.)
rage at someone: 对某人大发雷霆 (She raged at her son.)
rage on: 继续肆虐 (The storm raged on all night.)
in a rage: 勃然大怒 (He was in a rage.)
