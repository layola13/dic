preoperates:/ˌpriːˈɑːpəreɪts/| v.|术前准备，手术前操作|To perform actions or procedures before a surgical operation.  This often involves preparing the patient, the surgical site, and the instruments.|例句：The surgical team preoperates the patient by administering anesthesia and monitoring vital signs. = 手术团队通过给予麻醉和监测生命体征来为病人进行术前准备。


例句：The nurses preoperate by sterilizing the instruments and preparing the operating room. = 护士们通过消毒器械和准备手术室来进行术前准备。


近义词：prepare for surgery,  make ready for operation


反义词：postoperates (术后操作)


词性:动词
