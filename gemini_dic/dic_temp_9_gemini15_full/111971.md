lomonite: /ˈlɒmənaɪt/ | n. | 沸石  | Lomonosovite is a rare mineral, a hydrated sodium, calcium, aluminum silicate.  It's a member of the zeolite group.  Its crystal structure is characterized by a framework of interconnected SiO4 and AlO4 tetrahedra, containing cavities occupied by water molecules and cations. | Example: The sample contained traces of lomonosovite, a rare zeolite mineral.  该样本含有微量的方沸石矿物。| 近义词: zeolite (a broader term)| 反义词:  (no direct antonym, as it describes a specific mineral)|  名词
