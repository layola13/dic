preclose:/ˈpriːkloʊz/| v.|预先关闭；提前结束；|To close something before the scheduled or usual time.  This often refers to a business transaction or process being concluded early.|例句：The company decided to preclose the investment fund. 公司决定提前关闭投资基金。
例句：They preclosed the meeting to allow participants to attend another event. 他们提前结束了会议，以便与会者参加另一个活动。|近义词：close early, terminate early, conclude prematurely|反义词：postpone, delay, extend
