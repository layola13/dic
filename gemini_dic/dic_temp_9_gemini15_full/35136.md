cloudscape:/ˈklaʊdskeɪp/| n. |云景，天空景色 |A cloudscape is a view or picture of the sky, especially one that is dramatic or interesting because of the clouds.  It often refers to a landscape where the clouds are a dominant feature. |例句：The sunset created a breathtaking cloudscape of fiery oranges and deep purples. 夕阳创造了一幅令人惊叹的云景，呈现出火红的橙色和深紫色。

The dramatic cloudscape filled the entire canvas.  戏剧性的云景充满了整个画布。|近义词：sky scene, clouds, panorama, vista |反义词：clear sky, open sky
