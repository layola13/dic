rechartered:/ˌriːˈtʃɑːrtərd/ | vt. | 重新特许；重新颁发执照 | To grant a new charter to (a company, organization, etc.); to renew a charter.  | 例句：The university was rechartered in 1990.  = 这所大学于1990年重新获得特许。

The company was rechartered after a period of financial difficulty. = 这家公司在经历了一段时间的财务困难后重新获得了特许。|近义词：reauthorize, renew, reinstate | 反义词：revoke, cancel, abolish | 动词
