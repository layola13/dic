exponentiation:/ˌɛkspəʊnənʃɪˈeɪʃən/| n. | 幂运算；乘方运算 | The process of raising a number to a power.  It involves multiplying a number (the base) by itself a certain number of times (the exponent). For example, 2³ (2 raised to the power of 3) is exponentiation, resulting in 2 * 2 * 2 = 8. | Example:  The calculation of 10² is a simple example of exponentiation. 十的平方是一个简单的幂运算例子。|近义词：involution, power |反义词：root extraction (finding the root of a number)|
