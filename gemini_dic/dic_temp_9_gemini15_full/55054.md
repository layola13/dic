disquisitory:/dɪˈskwɪzɪtɔːri/ | adj. | 详尽探讨的；好辩的；好问的 |  Characterized by or given to disquisition; tending to inquire or discuss at length.  It implies a thorough and detailed examination of a subject, often with a scholarly or academic tone. |  The professor's disquisitory lecture on the history of philosophy lasted for three hours. 教授关于哲学史的长篇大论持续了三个小时。  The disquisitory nature of the debate led to a deeper understanding of the issue.  这场辩论的详尽探讨性质使得人们对这个问题有了更深入的理解。 |  inquisitive, argumentative, analytical |  superficial, concise, brief |  adj.
