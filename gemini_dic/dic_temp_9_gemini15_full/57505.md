dragnet:/ˈdræɡnɛt/| n. |拖网；大规模搜捕；(广播节目)警匪故事片| A dragnet is a large net used for catching fish, or, figuratively, a large-scale investigation or roundup of suspects.  It can also refer to a radio or television program featuring crime stories, often involving police investigations. |例句：The police launched a dragnet to catch the escaped criminal.  警方展开大规模搜捕以捉拿逃犯。

例句：The old radio show "Dragnet" was incredibly popular. 老牌广播节目“警网”非常受欢迎。


近义词：sweep, roundup, manhunt, net


反义词：release, freedom
