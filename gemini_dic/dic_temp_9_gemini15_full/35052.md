closelier:/ˈkloʊzliər/| adv.|更紧密地，更仔细地 |More closely; in a more intimate or detailed manner.  It is a comparative adverb formed from "closely".|例句：He examined the evidence more closely. 他更仔细地检查了证据。
例句：They worked more closely together after the merger. 合并后他们更紧密地合作了。|近义词：more intimately, more attentively, more carefully|反义词：more distantly, more carelessly, more superficially|副词
