Christmastide:/ˈkrɪsməstaɪd/| n. |圣诞节节期|The period of twelve days from Christmas Day to Epiphany, celebrated as a time of festivity and religious observance.  It is also sometimes used more broadly to refer to the Christmas season.|Example:  Many families enjoy carol singing and festive meals throughout Christmastide.  许多家庭在圣诞节期间享受颂歌和节日盛宴。|近义词：Christmas season, Yuletide|反义词：None.
