cogitated:/ˈkɒdʒɪteɪtɪd/| v.| 沉思；仔细考虑 | To think deeply and carefully; to ponder. |例句：He cogitated over the problem for hours before finding a solution. 他花了几个小时仔细思考这个问题，才找到解决方案。|近义词：pondered, contemplated, mused, reflected |反义词：ignored, disregarded


USAGE->

He cogitates on the meaning of life. = 他沉思生命的意义。
She cogitates before making important decisions. = 她做重要决定前会仔细思考。

一般过去时:
I cogitated for days before answering his question. = 我想了好几天才回答他的问题。
They cogitated on the implications of the new law. = 他们仔细考虑了新法律的影响。

现在完成时:

She has cogitated deeply and arrived at a conclusion. = 她经过深思熟虑后得出了结论。

过去完成时:

They had cogitated for hours but still couldn't reach an agreement. = 他们思考了几个小时，但仍然无法达成一致。
