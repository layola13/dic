psalming:/ˈsɑːlmɪŋ/| v.| 吟诵诗篇；唱诗；|The act of reciting or singing psalms.  It often implies a devotional or religious context. |例句：The monks were psalming during the evening service.  僧侣们在晚祷时吟诵诗篇。

例句：The choir was psalming the beautiful hymn. 合唱团正在吟唱优美的圣歌。(Note: While hymns aren't strictly psalms, the action is the same.)


近义词：chanting, singing, reciting, intoning
反义词：None directly opposite, but could consider words suggesting silence or secular music as contrasting.  
词性：verb
