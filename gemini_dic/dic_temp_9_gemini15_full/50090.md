/ˌdɛntɪˈsiːt/ | n. |齿状体，齿状突起 |A denticete is a toothed whale.  Denticete is a term used in zoology to refer to whales that possess teeth, as opposed to baleen whales which have baleen plates for filter-feeding.  They are characterized by their echolocation abilities, used for navigation and hunting.  |Example: Dolphins are a well-known example of a denticete. 海豚是齿鲸的一个众所周知的例子。|近义词：toothed whale |反义词：baleen whale |名词
