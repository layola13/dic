dore:/dɔːr/ | n. |门, 门扇 | A door is a hinged or sliding barrier at the entrance to a building, room, or vehicle. |例句：The door creaked open. 门吱呀一声开了。|例句：He opened the door and stepped inside. 他打开门走了进去。|近义词：entrance, gateway |反义词：window


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The door is open. = 门是开着的。
The doors are always locked at night. = 门晚上总是锁着的。

一般过去时:
I closed the door. = 我关上门了。
The door slammed shut. = 门砰的一声关上了。

现在完成时:
She has painted the door red. = 她把门漆成了红色。
They have replaced the old door. = 他们更换了旧门。

过去完成时:
He had already locked the door before she arrived. = 她到达之前他已经锁上门了。
The door had been broken for weeks. = 门已经坏了几个星期了。

将来时:

I wish the door wasn't so heavy. = 我希望门不要这么重。

被动语态:
The door was opened by the wind. = 门被风吹开了。
The door is being repaired. = 门正在修理中。

否定句:
