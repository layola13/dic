contratenor:/ˈkɒntrəˌtɛnər/| n. |男高音(通常指男童或阉伶)|A male singer with a high voice, typically a boy soprano or a castrato.  The term often refers to a male alto voice type. |例句：The choir featured a beautiful contratenor singing the high harmonies. 合唱团中有一位男高音演唱高音和声，声音非常优美。|例句：He trained for years to develop his powerful contratenor voice. 他训练多年以发展他强大的男高音嗓音。|近义词：alto, tenor (in some contexts)|反义词：bass
