overburdening: /ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrdənɪŋ/ | v. n. | 使负担过重；过多的负担 |

英文解析:The act of placing too much of a burden or demand on someone or something; excessive load or pressure.  It can refer to physical weight, responsibilities, or tasks.

例句:

The company's overburdening workload led to employee burnout.  公司的沉重工作负担导致员工倦怠。

The fragile bridge was overburdening with the weight of the heavy trucks.  脆弱的桥梁不堪重负，承受着重型卡车的重量。


近义词:overloading, overwhelming, overtaxing, taxing

反义词:underloading, relieving, easing


词性: Verb (overburdening) & Noun (overburdening)
