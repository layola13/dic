inundating:/ˈɪnʌndeɪtɪŋ/| v.|淹没；泛滥；充斥|To overwhelm (someone or something) with a large amount of something.|The heavy rain inundated the streets with water. 大雨淹没了街道。  The company was inundated with job applications. 公司收到了大量的求职申请。|overwhelm, flood, deluge|dry up, drain|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river inundates the village every spring. = 每到春天，这条河都会淹没村庄。
She is inundated with work at the moment. = 她现在工作繁忙。


一般过去时:
The flood inundated the entire town. = 洪水淹没了整个城镇。
They were inundated with calls after the announcement. = 公告发布后，他们接到了大量的电话。


现在完成时:
The persistent rain has inundated the fields. = 持续的降雨淹没了田野。
The website has been inundated with traffic since the launch. = 网站自上线以来访问量巨大。


过去完成时:
The basement had been inundated before the pumps could be activated. = 抽水机启动之前，地下室就已经被淹了。
The inbox had been inundated with spam emails before he could check them. = 他查看邮件之前，收件箱已经被垃圾邮件塞满了。


将来时:
The rising tide will inundate the coastal areas. = 上涨的潮水将淹没沿海地区。
If the dam breaks, it will inundate the entire valley. = 如果大坝决堤，它将淹没整个山谷。


虚拟语气:
If the dam were to break, it would inundate the whole town. = 如果大坝决堤，它将会淹没整个城镇。
I wish my inbox wasn't inundated with emails. = 我希望我的收件箱不会被邮件塞满。


被动语态:
The city was inundated by the storm. = 这个城市被暴风雨淹没了。
The helpline was inundated with calls from worried customers. = 热线电话被焦急的顾客的电话打爆了。


疑问句:
Is the area often inundated by floodwaters? = 这个地区经常被洪水淹没吗？
Is the customer service department inundated with complaints? = 客户服务部门是否被投诉淹没了？


否定句:
The town was not inundated by the recent storm. = 这个城镇没有被最近的暴风雨淹没。
The website was not inundated with traffic during the holiday season. = 节日期间网站访问量并没有很大。
