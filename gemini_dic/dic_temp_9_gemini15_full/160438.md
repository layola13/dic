popularising: /ˈpɒpjʊləraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 推广；普及；使大众化 |  The act of making something popular or widely known.  It involves spreading awareness and acceptance of an idea, product, or practice among a broader audience. |

例句：

The scientist is popularising his research findings through public lectures.  = 科学家正在通过公开讲座推广他的研究成果。

The company is popularising its new product through a massive marketing campaign. = 公司正在通过大规模的营销活动推广其新产品。


近义词：promoting, disseminating, spreading, popularizing, making known


反义词：suppressing, obscuring, concealing, keeping secret


词性: 动词

词源:  Comes from "popular" (meaning of or relating to the people) + "-ise" (a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make" or "to become").  Therefore, it literally means "to make popular".
