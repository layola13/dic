exemption:/ɪɡˈzɛmpʃən/| n. |豁免；免除；例外 |The state of being free from an obligation or liability.  A release from a duty or requirement. |例句：He received an exemption from military service. 他获得了免服兵役的资格。

例句：The company claimed exemption from paying the new tax.  该公司声称可以免交新税。


近义词：immunity, freedom, release, dispensation |反义词：obligation, liability, responsibility


词源:  From Middle French *exemption, from Latin *exemptio, from the verb *eximere "to take out, remove", from *ex- "out" + *emere "to take".
