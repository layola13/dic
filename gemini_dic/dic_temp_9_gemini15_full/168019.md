prissiness:/ˈprɪsɪnəs/| n. |矫揉造作，做作；拘谨|Affected primness; excessive precision or formality in manner or behavior.  It suggests an overly careful and sometimes pretentious adherence to social etiquette or propriety.|Her prissiness was evident in her perfectly pressed clothes and clipped speech.  她的矫揉造作在她熨烫得一丝不苟的衣服和剪短的言语中显而易见。|近义词：primness, affectation, pretentiousness|反义词：sloppiness, informality, naturalness
