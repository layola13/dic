reinstil: /ˌriːɪnˈstɪl/ | vt. | 灌输；重新灌输；再次使…充满 | To instill (something) again; to restore or renew (a feeling, quality, etc.) |例句：The teacher tried to reinstil discipline in the unruly class. 老师试图在这个难以管教的班级中重新灌输纪律。

The coach reinstilled confidence in his team after their loss. 教练在球队输球后重新燃起了他们的信心。

近义词：implant, infuse, inculcate | 反义词：eradicate, remove, suppress | 词性: 动词

词源:
The word "reinstil" is formed from the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "anew") and the verb "instil," which comes from the Latin word "instillare," meaning "to drop in," "to instill."  Therefore, "reinstil" literally means "to instill again."
