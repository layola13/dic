reelable: /ˈriːləbl/ | adj. | 可卷绕的，可卷起的 | Capable of being reeled; suitable for reeling.  This word describes something that can be wound onto a reel, typically referring to things like cables, fishing lines, or film. |


例句：

The cable is reelable, making it easy to store and transport.  = 这根电缆可以卷绕，便于储存和运输。

This new type of film is highly reelable, preventing tangles and breakage during use. = 这种新型胶片非常易于卷绕，使用过程中不会发生缠绕和断裂。


近义词：windable, rollable


反义词：unwindable, unrollable


词性：形容词
