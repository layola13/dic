loftiest:/ˈlɒftiest/| adj.|最高的；最崇高的；最傲慢的 |Most elevated in position or rank; highest.  Characterized by or exhibiting loftiness of thought, feeling, or expression. |例句：The loftiest mountain peak in the world is Mount Everest. 世界上最高的山峰是珠穆朗玛峰。

The loftiest ideals of human rights are often difficult to achieve in practice. 人权方面最崇高的理想在实践中往往难以实现。


近义词：highest, tallest, supreme, grandest, noblest |反义词：lowest, humblest, meanest
