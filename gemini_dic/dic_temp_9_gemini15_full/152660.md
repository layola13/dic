peaker:/ˈpiːkər/| n.|扬声器，发言人|a device that produces sound, or a person who speaks publicly|例句：The speaker was damaged.  扬声器坏了。|例句：The speaker gave an inspiring speech. 发言人发表了一篇鼓舞人心的演讲。|近义词：loudspeaker, orator, presenter|反义词：listener, audience


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The speaker produces clear sound. = 扬声器发出清晰的声音。
The speaker often gives presentations. =  这位发言人经常做演示。


一般过去时:
The speaker played music loudly. = 扬声器大声播放音乐。
The speaker delivered a powerful speech yesterday. = 昨天发言人发表了一篇有力的演讲。


现在完成时:
The company has installed new speakers. = 公司已经安装了新的扬声器。
The speaker has already finished his presentation. = 发言人已经完成了他的演讲。


过去完成时:
The music had stopped before the speaker broke. = 扬声器损坏之前音乐已经停止了。
The speaker had already spoken for an hour before the audience began to leave. =  观众开始离开之前，发言人已经讲了一个小时了。


将来时:
The speaker will play at the concert. = 扬声器将在音乐会上播放。
The speaker will address the audience tomorrow. = 发言人明天将向听众发表讲话。


虚拟语气:
If the speaker were working properly, we could hear the music. = 如果扬声器正常工作，我们就能听到音乐。
I wish the speaker had been clearer. = 我希望发言人能更清晰一些。


被动语态:
The sound was amplified by the speaker. = 声音被扬声器放大。
The speech was well received by the speaker. =  演讲受到了听众的好评。(被动语态略有不自然，因为speaker通常是主动发出声音或演讲的)


疑问句:
Is the speaker on? = 扬声器开着吗？
Who is the speaker at the conference? = 会议的发言人是谁？


否定句:
The speaker wasn't working. = 扬声器没有工作。
The speaker didn't speak loudly enough. = 发言人声音不够大。
