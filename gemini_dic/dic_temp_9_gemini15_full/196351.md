serigraph:/ˈsɛrɪɡræf/| n.|丝网版画；丝印画|A serigraph is a fine art print made using the silkscreen printing process.  The process involves pushing ink through a screen with a stencil to create an image on paper or other materials. Serigraphs are known for their vibrant colors and ability to produce detailed images.|例句：The gallery showcased a stunning collection of serigraphs by contemporary artists.  这家画廊展示了当代艺术家令人惊叹的丝网版画收藏。

The museum acquired a rare serigraph by Andy Warhol. 博物馆收藏了一幅安迪·沃霍尔的稀有丝网版画。|近义词：silkscreen print, screen print|反义词：original painting (depending on context)|名词
