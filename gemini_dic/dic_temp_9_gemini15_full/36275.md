cohabiters:/koʊˈhæbɪtərz/| n. |同居者，合住者 |People who live together, especially as an unmarried couple.  |例句：The cohabiters decided to buy a house together. 同居者决定一起买房子。|例句：Many young cohabiters are choosing to delay marriage. 许多年轻的同居者选择推迟结婚。|近义词：live-in partners, roommates (if not romantic)|反义词：separated individuals, estranged couples|名词
