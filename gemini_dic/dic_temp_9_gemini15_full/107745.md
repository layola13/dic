laparotomies:/ˌlæpəˈrɑːtəmiːz/| n. |剖腹手术（复数）|Surgical incisions into the abdominal cavity.  Laparotomies are performed for various reasons, including diagnosis and treatment of abdominal conditions.|例句：The surgeon performed several laparotomies during the week.  本周外科医生进行了数次剖腹手术。|The patient required emergency laparotomies due to internal bleeding. 由于内出血，病人需要紧急剖腹手术。|近义词：abdominal surgeries, celiotomies|反义词：N/A
