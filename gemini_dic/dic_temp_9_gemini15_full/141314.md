open-up:/ˈoʊpən ʌp/|/ˈəʊpən ʌp/| vt. |打开；坦诚；发展；开辟；使…暴露|to make something accessible or available; to become more open and communicative; to develop or expand something.|例句：He opened up the box and took out a present. 他打开盒子拿出了一件礼物。|例句：She opened up to me about her problems. 她向我倾诉了她的烦恼。|近义词：uncover, reveal, disclose, expand, develop|反义词：close, shut, conceal, restrict, limit|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They open up the shop every morning at 8 am. = 他们每天早上8点开门营业。
The company is trying to open up new markets overseas. = 公司正试图开拓海外新市场。


一般过去时:
The surgeon opened up the patient's chest. = 外科医生切开了病人的胸腔。
We opened up the discussion to more participants. = 我们让更多参与者加入了讨论。


现在完成时:
They have opened up a new branch in the city center. = 他们在市中心开设了新分店。
The government has opened up the market to foreign investment. = 政府已向外国投资开放市场。


过去完成时:
They had opened up the business before the economic crisis hit. = 他们在经济危机爆发前就已经开业了。
She had opened up to him about her past before he proposed. = 在他求婚之前，她已经向他吐露了她的过去。


将来时:
We will open up a new chapter in our lives. = 我们将开启人生的新篇章。
The company plans to open up new factories next year. = 公司计划明年开设新工厂。


虚拟语气:
If they opened up the factory, they would create many jobs. = 如果他们开设工厂，他们会创造很多就业机会。
I wish I had opened up to my parents earlier. = 我希望我早点向父母敞开心扉。


被动语态:
The package was opened up by the customs officer. = 包裹被海关官员打开了。
New markets are being opened up by the company's aggressive expansion. = 公司积极扩张正在开拓新的市场。


疑问句:
Will they open up the road for the construction? = 他们会为了建设而开通道路吗？
Have they opened up about their feelings yet? = 他们已经坦诚地表达他们的感受了吗？


否定句:
He didn't open up the letter until yesterday. = 他直到昨天才打开信。
She didn't open up to her friends about her anxieties. = 她没有向朋友倾诉她的焦虑。


PHRASE->
open up a new market = 开拓新市场
open up to someone = 向某人敞开心扉
open up a conversation = 开始谈话
open up a can of worms = 打开潘多拉魔盒
open up shop = 开张营业
