improve:/ɪmˈpruːv/ | vt. |改进，改善，提高 |To make or become better.  |例句：He needs to improve his study habits. 他需要改进他的学习习惯。

例句：The company is always looking for ways to improve its products.  这家公司一直在寻找改进其产品的方法。


近义词：better, enhance, ameliorate, upgrade | 反义词：worsen, deteriorate, decline | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He improves his English every day. = 他每天都在提高英语水平。
They improve their skills through practice. = 他们通过练习来提高技能。


一般过去时:
She improved her performance significantly. = 她的表现有了显著的提高。
The team improved its strategy after the loss. = 比赛失利后，球队改进的策略。


现在完成时:
We have improved our living conditions. = 我们已经改善了我们的生活条件。
They have improved their understanding of the subject. = 他们对这个主题的理解有所提高。


过去完成时:
He had improved his technique before the competition. = 他在比赛前已经提高了他的技术。
She had improved her writing skills by the time she graduated. = 她毕业时已经提高了写作能力。


将来时:
They will improve their efficiency with the new software. = 使用新软件后，他们的效率将会提高。
The project will improve the quality of life in the community. = 该项目将提高社区的生活质量。


虚拟语气:
If he studied harder, he would improve his grades. = 如果他学习更努力，他的成绩就会提高。
I wish I could improve my public speaking skills. = 我希望我能提高我的公开演讲能力。


被动语态:
The situation was improved by the new policy. = 新政策改善了局势。
The product is being improved to meet customer demand. = 正在改进产品以满足客户需求。


疑问句:
How can we improve the system? = 我们如何改进这个系统？
What steps can be taken to improve the situation? = 可以采取什么措施来改善这种情况？


否定句:
He didn't improve his attitude. = 他没有改进他的态度。
The situation hasn't improved much. = 情况并没有多大改善。


PHRASE->
improve on: 改进…
improve oneself: 自我提升
improve one's skills: 提高某人的技能
marked improvement: 明显的改进
steady improvement: 稳步的提高
