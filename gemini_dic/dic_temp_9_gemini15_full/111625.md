localities:/ləʊˈkælɪtiz/| n.|地方，地区，位置|Places, especially when considered in relation to their particular characteristics.|The study examined crime rates in various localities. 这项研究调查了各个地方的犯罪率。|近义词：areas, regions, places, districts|反义词：universality, globality
