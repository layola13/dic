datums:/ˈdeɪtəm/| n.|数据，资料|A datum is a single piece of information.  Data is the plural of datum.  However, datum is rarely used in the singular; data is almost always used, even when referring to a single piece of information.|The datum point on the map showed the location of the treasure.|近义词：data, information, fact|反义词：N/A
