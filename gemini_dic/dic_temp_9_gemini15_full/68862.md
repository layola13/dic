fast-forwarded:/ˌfæstˈfɔːrwəːdəd/| v. adj.|快进；提前；加速|To move quickly through something, skipping over parts;  to advance or progress rapidly.|例句：I fast-forwarded through the boring parts of the movie. 我快进播放了电影中无聊的部分。|例句：His career fast-forwarded after winning the award. 他获奖后事业突飞猛进。|近义词：sped up, accelerated, advanced|反义词：rewound, slowed down, delayed|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We fast-forward the video to the important part. = 我们快进视频到重要部分。
They fast-forward their plans to finish the project earlier. = 他们加快计划以提前完成项目。


一般过去时:
I fast-forwarded the movie to see the ending. = 我快进电影来看结局。
She fast-forwarded through the commercials. = 她快进了广告。


现在完成时:
We have fast-forwarded through many chapters of the book. = 我们已经快进阅读了书的许多章节。
He has fast-forwarded his career significantly in recent years. = 近年来，他事业发展迅速。


过去完成时:

She had fast-forwarded the tape to find her favorite song. = 她快进了磁带找到她最喜欢的歌。
