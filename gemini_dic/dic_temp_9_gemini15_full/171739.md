pumpkin:/ˈpʌmpkɪn/| n. |南瓜；南瓜藤|A large, round orange fruit with thick, hard skin, used as food.  It is also used for decoration, especially at Halloween.|例句：We carved a pumpkin for Halloween. 我们雕刻了一个南瓜来庆祝万圣节。

例句：She used pumpkin puree in her pie recipe. 她用南瓜泥做馅饼。

近义词：gourd, squash

反义词：无
