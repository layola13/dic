porousness:/pɔːˈrɒsnəs/ | /pɔːˈrəʊsnəs/ | n. | 多孔性；透气性；渗透性 | The state of being porous; having many small holes or spaces.  This allows fluids or gases to pass through. | The porousness of the soil allowed for excellent drainage. 这土壤的多孔性使得排水极好。 The high porosity of the sponge makes it ideal for cleaning. 海绵的高孔隙率使其成为清洁的理想选择。 | porosity, permeability | impermeability, density
