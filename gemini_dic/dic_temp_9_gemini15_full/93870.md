immodesties:/ɪmˈmɑːdəstiz/ | n. | 不谦虚；不庄重；放肆；无耻 |  A lack of modesty; immodest behavior or actions.  It often refers to excessive display or boastfulness, or behavior that violates social norms of propriety. | Example: Her immodesties at the party shocked everyone.  她的派对上的不检点行为震惊了所有人。 | 近义词：indecencies, improprieties, indecorums | 反义词：modesty, propriety, decency
