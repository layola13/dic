mynahs:/ˈmaɪnɑːz/| n. |八哥鸟（复数）|A mynah is a type of bird, often kept as a pet because of its ability to mimic human speech. Mynahs are known for their intelligence and ability to learn tricks.|例句：The mynahs in the park were quite noisy. 公园里的八哥鸟很吵闹。  The zookeeper showed us his collection of mynahs. 动物园管理员向我们展示了他收藏的八哥鸟。|近义词：starling, grackle|反义词：无
