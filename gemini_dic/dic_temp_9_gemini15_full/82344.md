grow-out-of:/ɡroʊˈaʊtəv/ | v. |  长大到不再需要；逐渐摆脱；克服 | To become too large for something; to overcome a problem or habit. |例句：He's grown out of his old shoes. 他已经长大了，以前的鞋子穿不下了。  |例句：She's finally grown out of her shyness. 她终于克服了她的害羞。 | 近义词：outgrow, overcome, surmount | 反义词：shrink into, succumb to


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grows out of his clothes quickly. = 他长得很快，衣服很快就穿不下了。
They are growing out of their bad habits. = 他们正在逐渐改掉坏习惯。


一般过去时:
I grew out of my fear of spiders. = 我克服了对蜘蛛的恐惧。
She grew out of that phase quickly. = 她很快地度过了那个阶段。


现在完成时:
He has grown out of that childish behavior. = 他已经改掉了那种幼稚的行为。
She has grown out of her allergy to cats. = 她已经不再对猫过敏了。


过去完成时:
By the time he was ten, he had grown out of needing diapers. = 十岁的时候，他已经不再需要穿尿布了。
She had grown out of her teenage angst before college. = 上大学之前，她已经摆脱了青春期的焦虑。


将来时:
He will grow out of this phase eventually. = 他最终会度过这个阶段的。
She will grow out of her fear of public speaking. = 她将会克服她对公开演讲的恐惧。


虚拟语气:
If he grew out of his shyness, he would be more successful. = 如果他能克服害羞，他会更成功。
I wish I had grown out of my procrastination habit years ago. = 我真希望几年前我就改掉了拖延的习惯。


被动语态:  (grow out of is rarely used passively)


疑问句:
Has he grown out of his allergy yet? = 他已经克服过敏了吗？
Will she grow out of this difficult period? = 她会度过这段艰难时期吗？


否定句:
He hasn't grown out of his bad temper yet. = 他还没改掉坏脾气。
She didn't grow out of her dependence on her parents easily. = 她并没有轻易摆脱对父母的依赖。


PHRASE->
grow out of a habit = 改掉一个习惯
grow out of a problem = 解决一个问题
grow out of a phase = 度过一个阶段
grow out of clothes = 长大到衣服穿不下
grow out of a disease = 从疾病中恢复过来
