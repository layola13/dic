flat-packed:/ˌflætˈpækt/| adj.|扁平包装的，预先组装的|Describes furniture or other items that are sold in a compressed, disassembled state, requiring assembly by the purchaser.  |例句：The new bookcase arrived flat-packed and took an hour to assemble. 新书柜是扁平包装的，组装花了1个小时。|例句：I prefer flat-packed furniture because it's cheaper to ship. 我更喜欢扁平包装的家具，因为运输成本更低。|近义词：ready-to-assemble, knock-down|反义词：fully assembled, pre-assembled|形容词
