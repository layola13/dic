devotion:/dɪˈvoʊʃən/ | n. |奉献，忠诚，热爱 |The act of dedicating oneself to a cause, person, or belief; loyalty; intense love or enthusiasm.  |例句：Her devotion to her family is admirable.  她的对家庭的奉献精神令人钦佩。

例句：His devotion to his work is unparalleled. 他对工作的奉献精神是无与伦比的。


近义词：dedication, commitment, loyalty, love, allegiance |反义词：neglect, indifference, apathy


词源：源自于古法语 *devotion，来自拉丁语 *devotio，意思是“奉献，誓言”。
