floored:/flɔːrd/ | adj. v. | 使震惊；使困惑；铺设地板 |  shocked, amazed, surprised; to cover a floor with a material | The news floored me.  The room was floored with hardwood. | astonished, stunned | calm, composed |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The news floors him every time. = 这个消息每次都让他震惊。
This type of wood floors beautifully. = 这种木材铺地板非常漂亮。

一般过去时:
The comedian's joke floored the audience. = 喜剧演员的笑话让观众大吃一惊。
They floored the entire kitchen with tile. = 他们用瓷砖铺满了整个厨房。

现在完成时:

We have recently floored our living room. = 我们最近铺了客厅的地板。

过去完成时:

They had floored the old house before selling it.  = 他们在卖掉老房子之前铺了地板。
