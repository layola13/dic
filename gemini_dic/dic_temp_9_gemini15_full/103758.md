journal:/ˈdʒɜːrnəl/| n.|期刊；杂志；日记；日志；记录|a periodical publication containing scholarly articles, usually in a specific academic field.|The scientific journal published his groundbreaking research.|近义词：magazine, periodical, diary, log|反义词：None


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He keeps a journal of his daily life. = 他记日记记录日常生活。
She reads several academic journals every month. = 她每个月阅读几本学术期刊。

一般过去时:
I wrote in my journal every night during my trip. = 旅行期间我每天晚上都写日记。
The scientist submitted his findings to a prestigious journal. = 科学家将他的研究成果提交给了一家著名的期刊。


现在完成时:
She has kept a detailed journal of her travels for years. = 她多年来一直详细记录她的旅行。
The company has published its annual journal. = 公司已经出版了它的年度期刊。


过去完成时:
He had already written in his journal before he went to bed. = 他睡觉前已经写好日记了。
The editor had reviewed the manuscript before submitting it to the journal. = 编辑在将稿件提交给期刊之前已经审阅过了。


将来时:
I will start a journal to track my progress. = 我将开始写日记来追踪我的进步。
They will publish the next journal next month. = 他们下个月将出版下一期期刊。


虚拟语气:
If I had kept a journal, I would remember more details. = 如果我记日记的话，我会记得更多细节。
I wish I had kept a better journal of my travels. = 我希望我旅行时能更好地记录日记。


被动语态:
The journal is published quarterly. = 这本期刊每季度出版一次。
His research paper was rejected by the journal. = 他的研究论文被该期刊拒绝了。


疑问句:
Do you keep a personal journal? = 你记私人日记吗？
Which journal published this article? = 哪本期刊发表了这篇文章？


否定句:
He doesn't keep a journal. = 他不记日记。
The article was not accepted by the journal. = 这篇文章没有被期刊接受。
