shuttlecock:/ˈʃʌtəlˌkɒk/ | n. | 羽毛球 | A shuttlecock is a projectile used in the game of badminton. It consists of a light head, traditionally made of cork or plastic, and a skirt of feathers or synthetic material.  The skirt creates air resistance, allowing the shuttlecock to fly in a characteristic trajectory. | Example: The shuttlecock soared high above the net before dropping to the ground.  羽毛球在网上空高高飞起，然后落到地上。|近义词：birdie (informal)|反义词：None
