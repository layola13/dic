ingeniosity:/ˌɪndʒɪniˈɒsəti/ | n. |独创性；巧妙；精巧 |The ability to invent clever new ways of doing things.  It suggests skill and imagination in creating something new or solving a problem. |例句：The engineer showed great ingenuity in solving the complex problem.  工程师在解决这个复杂问题上展现了极大的独创性。

The design of the bridge is a marvel of ingenuity. 这座桥的设计是独创性的奇迹。


近义词：inventiveness, creativity, cleverness, resourcefulness, originality |反义词：stupidity, dullness, unimaginativeness

词源:
源自拉丁语 *ingeniosus，由ingeni-（“天生的能力”、“天才”）和-osus（形容词后缀，表示“富有…的”）构成。  ingeni- 又源于ingenuus（“天生的”、“聪明的”、“高贵的”）。
