deduce:/dɪˈduːs/ | vt. |推断，演绎；推论 | To arrive at (a fact or conclusion) by reasoning; infer.  | 例句：From the evidence, we can deduce that the suspect was at the scene of the crime.  从证据中，我们可以推断嫌疑犯在犯罪现场。

例句： The detective deduced the killer's identity from the clues left behind.  侦探从留下的线索中推断出杀手的身份。


近义词：infer, conclude, infer, gather, surmise | 反义词：assume, guess


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She deduces the answer from the given information. = 她从给定的信息中推断出答案。
He deduces the meaning of the poem. = 他推断出这首诗的意思。


一般过去时:
The police deduced that the thief had escaped through the window. = 警察推断小偷是从窗户逃走的。
Scientists deduced the age of the fossil from its chemical composition. = 科学家根据化石的化学成分推断出它的年龄。


现在完成时:
We have deduced that the project will be delayed. = 我们已经推断出该项目将会延迟。
She has deduced the solution to the puzzle. = 她已经推断出谜题的答案。


过去完成时:
They had deduced the cause of the accident before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，他们已经推断出事故的原因。
The investigators had deduced the murderer's motive before apprehending him. = 调查人员在逮捕凶手之前已经推断出他的作案动机。


将来时:
She will deduce the pattern in the data. = 她将推断出数据中的模式。
Scientists will deduce the origin of the disease. = 科学家将推断出这种疾病的起源。


虚拟语气:
If I had more information, I could deduce the answer. = 如果我有更多信息，我就能推断出答案。
If she had paid closer attention, she might have deduced the truth. = 如果她更仔细地注意，她本可以推断出真相。


被动语态:
The conclusion was deduced from the experimental results. = 这个结论是从实验结果中推断出来的。
The criminal's identity was deduced by the police. = 罪犯的身份被警方推断出来。


疑问句:
Can you deduce the meaning of this sentence? = 你能推断出这句话的意思吗？
What can we deduce from these statistics? = 我们能从这些统计数据中推断出什么？


否定句:
I cannot deduce the solution to this problem. = 我无法推断出这个问题的解决方法。
They were unable to deduce the cause of the fire. = 他们无法推断出火灾的原因。


PHRASE->
deduce from: 从...推断
deduce that: 推断...
deduce the meaning: 推断意思
deduce a conclusion: 推断结论
deduce the cause: 推断原因


词源:
From Latin *dēducere "to lead down, draw off," from dē- "down, away" + dūcere "to lead."  The sense evolved from the idea of "leading down" a line of reasoning to a conclusion.
