homemade:/ˌhoʊmˈmeɪd/| adj.|自制的，家常的|Made at home, rather than commercially produced.|例句：This bread is homemade. 这面包是自制的。|例句：She prefers homemade soup to canned soup. 她更喜欢家常汤而不是罐装汤。|近义词：home-baked, home-cooked, homemade|反义词：store-bought, commercially made


USAGE->

The cake is homemade. = 蛋糕是自制的。
She makes homemade jam. = 她做自制果酱。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
