osteoplastic:/ˌɒstiːəʊˈplæstɪk/| adj.|成骨的；促进骨形成的| Relating to or promoting the formation of bone.  Osteoplastic surgery involves procedures that repair or reconstruct bone. |例句：The surgeon performed an osteoplastic procedure to repair the fractured skull.  外科医生进行了一次成骨手术来修复颅骨骨折。

例句：The osteoplastic material was successfully integrated into the bone defect. 成骨材料成功地整合到了骨缺损中。


近义词：bone-forming, osteogenic


反义词：osteolytic (bone-destroying)


词性：形容词
