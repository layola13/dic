impelled:/ɪmˈpɛld/| vt.|驱使；激励；迫使|To urge or drive forward; to force or compel.|The strong wind impelled the ship forward. 强风推动着船前进。  He felt impelled to confess his crime. 他感到不得不承认自己的罪行。|近义词：urge, drive, compel, force|反义词：deter, restrain, hinder


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The strong wind impels the ship forward. = 强风推动着船前进。
Curiosity impels him to explore the unknown. = 好奇心驱使他探索未知领域。


一般过去时:
The evidence impelled the jury to reach a guilty verdict. = 证据促使陪审团作出有罪判决。
A sense of duty impelled her to help the victims. = 责任感驱使她帮助受害者。


现在完成时:
What has impelled you to make such a drastic decision? = 什么促使你做出如此激烈的决定？
His ambition has impelled him to great heights. = 他的雄心壮志将他推向了更高的境界。


过去完成时:
He had been impelled by a desire for revenge. = 他一直被复仇的欲望驱使着。
Having been impelled by fear, she ran away. = 出于恐惧，她逃跑了。


将来时:
The upcoming election will impel many people to vote. = 即将到来的选举将促使许多人投票。
I believe this new evidence will impel a change in policy. = 我相信这项新的证据将促使政策发生变化。


虚拟语气:
If I were impelled by greed, I would have done it differently. = 如果我被贪婪驱使，我会采取不同的做法。
I wish I hadn't been impelled by such anger. = 我真希望我没有被如此愤怒所驱使。


被动语态:
The ship was impelled by the strong wind. = 船被强风推动着。
He felt impelled to act. = 他感到被迫采取行动。（被动语态中，impelled更常用于这种“感到被迫”的意思）


疑问句:
What impelled you to act so rashly? = 什么促使你如此鲁莽行事？
What factors impelled the company to make such a change? = 哪些因素促使公司做出如此改变？


否定句:
Nothing impelled him to continue the project. = 没有什么驱使他继续这个项目。
He wasn’t impelled by personal gain; he acted for the greater good. = 他并非出于个人利益；他是为了更大的利益而行动的。


PHRASE->
be impelled to do sth. = 被迫做某事
impelled by sth. = 被…驱使
feel impelled to do sth. = 感到不得不做某事
impelled forward = 被向前推进
impelled by a sense of... = 出于…的感觉而被驱使


词源:
From Latin *impellere, from in- ("in, into, on") + pellere ("to drive, push").  The word reflects the idea of driving something "in" or forward.
