pelter:/ˈpɛltər/ | n., v. | 猛击；连续猛击；暴雨；投掷者 |

英文解析:
As a noun, "pelter" refers to someone who pelts (throws things at someone). As a verb, "pelter" means to throw things repeatedly at someone or something. It can also describe a heavy downpour of rain or hail.

例句:
The children were pelters, throwing snowballs at each other. = 孩子们是扔雪球的顽皮鬼，互相扔雪球。

The hail pelted against the windows. = 冰雹猛烈地击打着窗户。


近义词:
Noun: thrower, hurler
Verb: bombard, batter, shower, thrash

反义词:
Noun:  (no direct antonym)
Verb:  gently sprinkle, caress


词性:
Noun, Verb
