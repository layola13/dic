delayed:/dɪˈleɪd/ | adj. | 延迟的，耽搁的 | Delayed means something has been postponed or made later than originally planned. It implies a setback or interruption in a schedule or process. |例句：The flight was delayed due to bad weather.  航班因恶劣天气延误了。|例句：The project is delayed, and the deadline has been pushed back. 项目延误了，截止日期被推迟了。|近义词：postponed, deferred, late, behind schedule |反义词：on time, punctual, expedited


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The train is delayed. = 火车延误了。
The meeting is delayed until tomorrow. = 会议推迟到明天。

一般过去时:
The package was delayed in transit. = 包裹在运输途中延误了。
The concert was delayed because of the rain. = 演唱会因为下雨而延误了。

现在完成时:
The shipment has been delayed for a week. = 货物已经延误了一周了。
They have been delayed by unforeseen circumstances. = 他们因不可预见的情况而延误了。

过去完成时:
The meeting had already been delayed before he arrived. = 他到达之前会议就已经延误了。
We had been delayed by a traffic jam. = 我们因为交通堵塞而延误了。

将来时:
The game will be delayed if it rains. = 如果下雨，比赛将被推迟。
The delivery will be delayed until next week. = 送货将推迟到下周。

虚拟语气:
If the train hadn't been delayed, we would have arrived on time. = 如果火车没有延误，我们就会准时到达。
I wish the project hadn't been delayed. = 我真希望项目没有延误。

被动语态:
The report was delayed by the lack of information. = 报告因缺乏信息而被延误。
The decision was delayed until further notice. = 决定被推迟到另行通知。

疑问句:
Is the flight delayed? = 航班延误了吗？
Was the meeting delayed? = 会议延误了吗？

否定句:
The project wasn't delayed. = 项目没有延误。
The delivery wasn't delayed; it arrived on time. = 送货没有延误；它准时到达了。
