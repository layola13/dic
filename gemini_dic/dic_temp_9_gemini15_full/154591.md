persephone:/pərˈsɛfəniː/| n. |珀耳塞福涅（希腊神话中的冥后）|The Greek goddess of spring and queen of the underworld, daughter of Demeter and wife of Hades. She is often associated with vegetation, especially grains, and her abduction by Hades is a myth explaining the changing seasons.|Example: Persephone's abduction by Hades is a well-known myth in Greek mythology.  珀耳塞福涅被哈迪斯绑架的故事是希腊神话中一个家喻户晓的神话故事。
