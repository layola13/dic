These empires varied greatly in size and power throughout history, but they all shared a common religious and political foundation. |Example: The Abbasid Caliphate was a period of significant intellectual and cultural flourishing in the Islamic world.  哈里发王朝是伊斯兰世界知识和文化繁荣的时期。|近义词：Islamic empires, sultanates (depending on context)|反义词：None directly applicable, but terms like "secular states" could be considered opposites in terms of governance.|名词
