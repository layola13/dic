foretellable: /fɔːrˈtɛləbl/ | adj. | 可预见的，可预测的 | Capable of being foretold or predicted; predictable.  | The outcome of the election seemed foretellable given the recent polls.  选举的结果鉴于最近的民调似乎是可以预见的。| predictable, foreseeable, anticipated | unforeseeable, unpredictable, unexpected | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weather is foretellable today. = 今天的天气是可以预测的。
This outcome is foretellable. = 这个结果是可以预见的。

一般过去时:
The earthquake was not foretellable. = 那次地震是不可预测的。
The stock market crash was largely unforetellable. = 股市崩盘在很大程度上是不可预测的。

现在完成时:
The consequences have been foretellable from the start. = 从一开始结果就可预见。
Many problems have proven to be foretellable. = 许多问题已被证明是可以预见的。

过去完成时:
The failure had been foretellable given the lack of planning. = 鉴于缺乏计划，失败是可以预见的。
It had been foretellable that the project would fail. = 可以预见该项目会失败。

将来时:
The weather will be foretellable tomorrow. = 明天天气是可以预测的。
The results of the experiment will be foretellable. = 实验的结果是可以预测的。

虚拟语气:
If the risks had been foretellable, we would have taken precautions. = 如果风险是可以预见的，我们会采取预防措施。
I wish the outcome had been more foretellable. = 我希望结果更可预测一些。

被动语态:
The future cannot be easily foretellable. = 未来并非易于预测。
The damage was foretellable by experts. = 专家们可以预见这种损害。

疑问句:
Is this situation foretellable? = 这种情况是可以预测的吗？
Are the effects of the policy foretellable? = 这项政策的影响是可以预测的吗？

否定句:
The consequences were not foretellable. = 后果是不可预见的。
The future is not always foretellable. = 未来并非总是可预测的。


PHRASE->

词源:

foretell (动词，预言) + -able (形容词后缀，表示“可…的”)  foretell本身源于古英语 *for- 和 *tellan (告诉)。
