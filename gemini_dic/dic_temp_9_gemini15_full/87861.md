hideout: /ˈhaɪdaʊt/ | n. | 躲藏处；藏身处 | A secret or secluded place used for hiding, especially by criminals or fugitives.  |例句：The police raided the gang's hideout in the abandoned warehouse.  警方突袭了该团伙藏匿在废弃仓库里的藏身处。|例句：The mountain cave served as a perfect hideout for the escaped prisoners. 山洞成了逃犯完美的藏身之处。|近义词：refuge, sanctuary, haven, lair |反义词：open, exposed
