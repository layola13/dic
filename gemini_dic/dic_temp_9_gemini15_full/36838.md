colonizable: /ˈkɑːlənaɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可殖民的，易于被殖民的 | Capable of being colonized; susceptible to colonization by microorganisms or other organisms.  This term often refers to the ability of a surface, environment, or host to support the growth and establishment of a population of microorganisms. | 例句：The wound was colonizable by Staphylococcus aureus.  = 伤口易被金黄色葡萄球菌感染。

The new material proved colonizable by a variety of bacteria. = 这种新材料被证明易于被多种细菌定植。


近义词：infectable, susceptible, inhabitable  | 反义词：sterile, uncolonizable
