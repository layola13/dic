peatlands:/ˈpiːtlændz/| n.|泥炭地；沼泽地|Areas of land that are saturated with water for significant periods, resulting in the accumulation of partially decayed organic matter (peat).  Peatlands are characterized by their unique flora and fauna, and play a vital role in carbon sequestration.|例句：The peatlands of Ireland are a unique and important ecosystem. 爱尔兰的泥炭地是一个独特而重要的生态系统。|例句：Many rare birds breed in the remote peatlands. 许多珍稀鸟类在偏远的泥炭地繁殖。|近义词：bog, mire, moor, marsh, swamp|反义词：desert, dry land|名词
