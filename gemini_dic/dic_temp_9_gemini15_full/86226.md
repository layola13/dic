heathlands:/ˈhiːðləndz/| n.|荒野，荒地；灌木丛生地|Open uncultivated land, typically covered with low-growing shrubs and grasses. Heathlands are characterized by acidic, sandy soils and are often found in coastal areas or on higher ground.|例句：The heathlands stretched out before them, a vast expanse of purple heather.  荒野在他们面前绵延开来，一片广阔的紫色石楠花。

例句：We went for a walk across the heathlands, enjoying the fresh air and the wildflowers. 我们在荒野上散步，享受着新鲜的空气和野花。


近义词：moorland, wasteland, scrubland|反义词：forest, woodland, cultivated land|词性:名词


词源:源自古英语 *hæþ ‘heath’ + land
