faced:/feɪst/| v. adj.|面对；遭遇；面向|To be confronted with or experience (a difficulty, problem, or unpleasant situation).  Also used to describe the direction something is facing.|例句：She faced many challenges in her career. 她职业生涯中面临许多挑战。
He faced the music after his mistake. 他犯错后承担了后果。

The house faced the south. 房子朝南。|近义词：encountered, confronted, met|反义词：avoided, ignored, shirked|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He faces many problems. = 他面临许多问题。
They face a difficult decision. = 他们面临一个艰难的决定。

一般过去时:
She faced her fears bravely. = 她勇敢地面对恐惧。
We faced a storm at sea. = 我们在海上遭遇风暴。

现在完成时:
He has faced numerous obstacles in his life. = 他一生中面临无数障碍。
They have faced similar challenges before. = 他们以前面临过类似的挑战。

过去完成时:
She had faced similar situations in the past. = 她过去曾面临过类似的情况。
We had faced a difficult choice before accepting the job. = 在接受这份工作之前，我们面临过一个艰难的选择。

将来时:
We will face many difficulties in the future. = 未来我们将面临许多困难。
They will face the consequences of their actions. = 他们将承担他们行为的后果。

虚拟语气:
If I faced that situation again, I would handle it differently. = 如果我再次面临这种情况，我会采取不同的处理方式。
I wish I hadn't faced such a difficult choice. = 我真希望我没有面临如此艰难的选择。

被动语态:
The problem was faced by the team. = 这个问题由团队来解决。
The challenge was bravely faced. = 挑战得到了勇敢的应对。

疑问句:
What challenges do you face in your work? = 你在工作中面临哪些挑战？
What difficulties did they face during the project? = 在项目期间他们遇到了哪些困难？

否定句:
He didn't face the music. = 他没有承担后果。
They didn't face the reality of the situation. = 他们没有面对现实。
