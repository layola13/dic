quarantining:/ˈkwɒrənˌtiːnɪŋ/|/ˈkwɔːrənˌtiːnɪŋ/| v. |隔离；检疫 |The act of isolating someone or something to prevent the spread of disease or harmful substance.  It involves separating infected individuals or objects from the healthy population for a specified period.|例句：The city is quarantining all incoming travelers from the affected region.  城市正在对来自受灾地区的所有入境旅客进行隔离。

例句：The suspected contaminated food products are being quarantined. 怀疑受污染的食品正在被隔离。


近义词：isolation, confinement, segregation, quarantine

反义词：release, liberation, freedom

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are quarantining the infected patients. = 他们正在隔离感染的病人。
We quarantine all incoming packages. = 我们对所有入境包裹进行隔离。

一般过去时:
They quarantined the village after the outbreak. = 爆发后，他们隔离了村庄。
The government quarantined the ship to prevent the spread of disease. = 政府隔离了船只以防止疾病传播。

现在完成时:
They have quarantined the affected area for three weeks. = 他们已经对受影响地区进行了三周的隔离。
The authorities have quarantined the suspect for further investigation. = 当局已将嫌疑人隔离以进行进一步调查。

过去完成时:
They had quarantined the building before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，他们已经对大楼进行了隔离。
The doctor had quarantined the patient immediately upon arrival. = 医生在病人到达后立即将其隔离。


将来时:
They will quarantine the city if the virus spreads further. = 如果病毒进一步传播，他们将隔离这座城市。
The hospital will quarantine any suspected cases. = 医院将隔离任何疑似病例。

虚拟语气:
If they quarantined the area sooner, the spread might have been contained. = 如果他们早点隔离该地区，传播可能已被控制。
I wish they had quarantined the infected person earlier. = 我希望他们早点隔离感染者。

被动语态:
The infected patients were quarantined in a separate ward. = 感染的病人被隔离在一个单独的病房里。
The area is being quarantined to prevent further contamination. = 该地区正在被隔离以防止进一步污染。

疑问句:
Are they quarantining the entire neighborhood? = 他们是否正在隔离整个街区？
Was the school quarantined last year? = 学校去年被隔离了吗？

否定句:
They did not quarantine the suspected case immediately. = 他们没有立即隔离疑似病例。
The authorities were unable to quarantine the entire region. = 当局无法隔离整个地区。
