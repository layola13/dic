newspaperman:/ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpərˌmæn/| n. | 报社记者，新闻记者 | A newspaperman is a journalist who works for a newspaper.  They write articles, reports, and other types of news content. | He was a seasoned newspaperman with decades of experience. 他是一位经验丰富的新闻记者，拥有几十年的经验。  The newspaperman quickly filed his story after the event. 事件发生后，这位记者迅速提交了他的报道。 | journalist, reporter, newsman |  (no direct antonym, but could be considered opposite to someone who avoids or dislikes the news)


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The newspaperman writes articles every day. = 报社记者每天写文章。
He reports on local events. = 他报道当地事件。


一般过去时:
The newspaperman wrote a compelling story about the election. = 这位记者写了一篇关于选举的引人入胜的故事。
He interviewed the mayor yesterday. = 他昨天采访了市长。


现在完成时:
The newspaperman has covered many important stories. = 这位记者报道了许多重要的新闻。
He has won several awards for his journalism. = 他因其新闻报道获得了多个奖项。


过去完成时:

He had interviewed the witness several times. = 他已经多次采访了证人。


将来时:
