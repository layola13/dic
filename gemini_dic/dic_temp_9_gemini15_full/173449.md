quasi-excessively: /ˈkwɑːziˌɛksˈɛsɪvli/ | adj. | 准过度地；近乎过度地 |  Nearly excessively; to a degree approaching excess. This word combines "quasi" (meaning "seemingly," "apparently," or "to a certain extent") with "excessively" (meaning "to an excessive degree"). It describes something that is almost excessive but not quite. |例句：He showed a quasi-excessively enthusiastic response to the news. 他对这个消息反应过于热情。 | 近义词：excessively, overly, extremely, immoderately | 反义词：moderately, sparingly, minimally
