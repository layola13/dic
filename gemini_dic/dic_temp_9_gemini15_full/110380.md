The term can also refer to the woody or fibrous nature of certain materials.|The lignite mine was a major source of employment in the region.  褐煤矿是该地区主要的就业来源。|The soil had a distinctly lignite-like texture.  土壤具有明显的褐煤状质地。|近义词：carbonaceous, bituminous (in some contexts)|反义词：anthracitic (referring to coal rank)
