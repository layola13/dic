fibonacci:/ˌfiːbəˈnɑːtʃi/ | /ˌfɪbəˈnætʃi/ | n. |斐波那契数列；斐波那契数|The Fibonacci sequence is a series of numbers where each number is the sum of the two preceding ones, usually starting with 0 and 1.  It has many applications in mathematics, computer science, and nature.|Example: The Fibonacci sequence, 0, 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, ..., appears in the arrangement of leaves on a stem and the spirals of a sunflower.|近义词:Fibonacci numbers, Fibonacci series|反义词:None
