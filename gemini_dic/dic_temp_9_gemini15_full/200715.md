sleepy:/ˈsliːpi/| adj.|困倦的，瞌睡的|Feeling a strong desire to sleep; drowsy.  |例句：I'm feeling sleepy after a long day. 我一天下来感觉很困。|例句：The warm fire made me feel very sleepy.  温暖的炉火让我感到非常困倦。|近义词：drowsy, somnolent, lethargic|反义词：awake, alert, wide-awake|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am sleepy. = 我困了。
He is sleepy. = 他困了。


一般过去时:
I was sleepy yesterday. = 我昨天很困。
She was sleepy after the long flight. = 长途飞行后她很困。


现在完成时:
I have been sleepy all day. = 我今天一直很困。
They have been sleepy since they got sick. = 他们自从生病后就一直很困。


过去完成时:
I had been sleepy all week before I finally slept. = 在我最终睡着之前，我已经困了一周了。
We had been sleepy before the exam. = 考试前我们都很困。


将来时:
I will be sleepy after work. = 我下班后会很困。
They will be sleepy if they stay up late. = 如果他们熬夜，他们会很困。


虚拟语气:
If I weren't so sleepy, I would finish this work. = 如果我不那么困，我会完成这项工作的。
I wish I weren't so sleepy. = 我真希望我不那么困。


被动语态:  (Sleepy is not typically used in the passive voice.)

否定句:
I am not sleepy. = 我不困。
