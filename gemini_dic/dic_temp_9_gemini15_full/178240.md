recompense:/ˈrɛkəmˈpɛns/ | n. | 补偿；报酬 |  Payment made in return for something else; compensation.  It can refer to something given as payment for a service, loss, or injury. |例句：He received a generous recompense for his years of service. 他因为他多年的服务收到了丰厚的报酬。

例句：The company offered recompense to the customers affected by the product recall. 公司向受产品召回影响的客户提供了赔偿。


近义词：compensation, remuneration, repayment, amends, reward

反义词：penalty, punishment


词性: 名词


词源:  From Old French *recompenser, from re- "again" + compenser "to compensate," from Latin compensare "to weigh together," from com- "together" + pensare "to weigh."  The word's development reflects the idea of balancing or making amends for something.
