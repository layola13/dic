perplexers:/pərˈplɛksərz/| n. |令人困惑的事物；难题|Things that cause confusion or bewilderment; puzzles.|The cryptic crossword was full of perplexing clues.  这个隐晦的纵横字谜充满了令人费解的线索。|The instructions were perplexing and difficult to follow.  这些说明令人困惑，难以理解。|近义词：puzzles, enigmas, riddles|反义词：clarifiers, solutions
