podura: /ˈpɒdʒʊrə/ | n. | 胞胚虫 | A springtail; a small, wingless arthropod of the order Collembola, characterized by a forked appendage (furcula) used for jumping.  They are commonly found in soil and leaf litter. |  Example:  The damp soil teemed with podura and other small invertebrates.  潮湿的土壤里挤满了胞胚虫和其他小型无脊椎动物。  | 近义词: springtail, collembolan | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑体型较大的昆虫)  |  n.
