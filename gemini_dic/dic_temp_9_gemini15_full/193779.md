self-defrosting:/ˌsɛlf dɪˈfrɒstɪŋ/ | adj. | 自动除霜的 | Automatically removing frost from a refrigerator or freezer.  This feature eliminates the need for manual defrosting. | Example:  Our new refrigerator is self-defrosting, so we don't have to worry about ice buildup.  我们的新冰箱是自动除霜的，所以我们不用担心冰的堆积。 |近义词：automatic defrosting |反义词：manual defrosting
