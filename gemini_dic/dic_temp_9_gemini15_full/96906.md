inexpiable:/ɪnˈɛkspɪəbl/ | adj. | 不可赎的；无法补偿的；无法弥补的 | Incapable of being atoned for or expiated.  It suggests a wrongdoing so grave that no amount of repentance or amends can make up for it.  | The inexpiable crime haunted him for the rest of his days.  这不可赎的罪行在他余生都困扰着他。|  unforgivable, irredeemable, irreparable | excusable, forgivable, pardonable | 形容词

词源:
