grilse:/ˈɡrɪls/| n. |格陵兰鲑鱼；幼年鲑鱼|A grilse is a salmon in its second year after hatching, typically smaller than an adult salmon and having silvery sides.  They are sexually mature but haven't yet spawned. |Example: The river was teeming with grilse, their silvery flanks flashing in the sun.  这条河里挤满了格陵兰鲑鱼，它们银色的侧面在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：salmon (young salmon), smolt|反义词：adult salmon
