implication:/ˌɪmplɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |暗示；含义；牵连；影响 |The word "implication" refers to something suggested or implied, rather than explicitly stated. It can also refer to the act of implying something, or the consequences or effects of an action or decision. |例句：The implication of his statement was that he disagreed.  他的话暗示了他不同意。

例句：The implication of the new law is that many businesses will have to close. 这项新法律的含义是许多企业将不得不倒闭。


近义词：suggestion, inference, consequence, effect | 反义词：explicit statement, direct expression | 词性：名词
