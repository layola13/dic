equalizing: /ˈiːkwəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使平等；使均衡；使相等 |  The act of making something equal or uniform.  It often involves adjusting or balancing different elements to achieve a state of parity. |

例句：

The equalizer adjusted the sound levels, making the music clearer. = 均衡器调整了音量，使音乐更清晰。

The government implemented policies to equalize income distribution. = 政府实施了政策以平衡收入分配。


近义词：balancing, leveling, standardizing

反义词：differentiating, discriminating, polarizing

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system equalizes the temperature throughout the room. = 系统使房间各处的温度保持一致。
He equalizes the workload among his team members. = 他在团队成员之间平均分配工作量。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
They have equalized the playing field for all participants. = 他们已经为所有参与者创造了公平的竞争环境。
We have equalized the distribution of resources. = 我们已经平衡了资源分配。


过去完成时:
