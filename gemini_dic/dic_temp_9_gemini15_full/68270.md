fakeries:/ˈfeɪkəriːz/| n.|赝品；伪造品；欺骗行为|Things that are fake or counterfeit; imitations intended to deceive.  The exhibition exposed a number of art fakeries.|近义词：counterfeits, forgeries, imitations|反义词：genuines, originals

例句：

The auction house was shocked to discover several fakeries among the supposedly authentic paintings. = 拍卖行震惊地发现几幅据称是真迹的画作中掺杂着赝品。

He was arrested for selling fakeries to unsuspecting tourists. = 他因向毫无戒心的游客出售赝品而被捕。
