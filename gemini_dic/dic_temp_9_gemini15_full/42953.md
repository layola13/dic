coverchief:/ˈkʌvətʃiːf/| n. |头巾，盖头 |A coverchief is a piece of cloth worn on the head, often by women, for warmth, fashion, or religious reasons.  It can also refer to a head covering used to protect the hair or for hygiene purposes. |例句：She wore a brightly colored coverchief to protect her head from the sun. 她戴着一块鲜艳的头巾来保护头部免受阳光照射。


例句：The nurse tied a coverchief around the patient's head to keep it clean. 护士用一块头巾包住病人的头部以保持清洁。

近义词：headscarf, veil, shawl |反义词：None
