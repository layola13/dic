laudator:/ˈlɔːdeɪtər/| n. |赞扬者，称赞者 |A laudator is a person who praises or extols someone or something.  They often express admiration or approval publicly.  |Example:  The laudator delivered a heartfelt speech, praising the recipient's many achievements.  赞扬者发表了一篇衷心的演讲，称赞了获奖者诸多成就。|近义词：praiser, admirer, eulogist |反义词：critic, detractor, censurer |名词
