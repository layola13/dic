nonblinding:/ˌnɒnˈblaɪndɪŋ/| adj. |非盲法的|Not involving blinding or masking, where participants or researchers are unaware of treatment assignments.  This lack of blinding can introduce bias into the results of a study.  |The nonblinding study design might lead to inaccurate conclusions.  非盲法研究设计可能会导致不准确的结论。| blinded, masked |  double-blinded|形容词
