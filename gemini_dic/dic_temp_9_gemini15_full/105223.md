keystroke:/ˈkiːstroʊk/| n. |击键，按键|A single press of a key on a keyboard.  It can refer to the action of pressing a key or the character produced by pressing a key.|例句：Each keystroke was carefully recorded for analysis. 每个按键都被仔细记录下来进行分析。

例句：The software counted the number of keystrokes per minute.  该软件计算每分钟的按键次数。


近义词：key press, key depression |反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The program registers every keystroke. = 程序记录每一次按键。
Each keystroke adds to the document. = 每次按键都会添加到文档中。


一般过去时:
His last keystroke saved the file. = 他最后一次按键保存了文件。
She made a mistake in her third keystroke. = 她在第三次按键时犯了一个错误。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
