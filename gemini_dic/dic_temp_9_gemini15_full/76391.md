gaffe:/ˈɡæf/| n.|失礼；失态；尴尬；|A social blunder; a mistake in behavior or speech that causes embarrassment.  |例句：He made a gaffe when he referred to the Prime Minister by the wrong name. 他把首相的名字叫错了，犯了一个失礼的错误。

例句：Her comment about his weight was a terrible gaffe. 她关于他体重的评论是一个严重的失态。


近义词：blunder, faux pas, indiscretion, mistake, slip|反义词：tact, diplomacy, grace

词性: 名词
