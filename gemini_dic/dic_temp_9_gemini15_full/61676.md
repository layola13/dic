emmetropic:/ˌɛməˈtrɒpɪk/| adj.|正视的，正常视力的|Possessing normal vision; neither hyperopic nor myopic.  An emmetropic eye focuses light rays from distant objects directly onto the retina without the need for refractive correction.|例句：His emmetropic vision allowed him to see clearly without glasses. 他的正视力让他不用眼镜就能看清东西。

例句：The optometrist confirmed that the patient was emmetropic.  验光师确认病人视力正常。

近义词：normal-sighted,  normovisual

反义词：hyperopic (远视的), myopic (近视的)


词源：源自希腊语 emmetros (适度)+ ops (眼睛)


USAGE->
(由于emmetropic是形容词，没有时态变化，以下列举不同句式)


肯定句:
He has emmetropic vision. = 他有正常的视力。
She is emmetropic. = 她是正视的。


否定句:
He does not have emmetropic vision. = 他没有正常的视力。
She is not emmetropic. = 她不是正视的。


疑问句:
Is his vision emmetropic? = 他的视力正常吗？
Is she emmetropic? = 她是正视的吗？


PHRASE->
(由于emmetropic本身就是一个完整的词，没有常用的词组搭配)

emmetropic refraction: 正视屈光
emmetropia: 正视 (名词形式)
emmetropic state: 正视状态
