centers:/ˈsɛntərz/| n.|中心；中央；中心点|The word "centers" is the plural form of the noun "center," referring to multiple points or locations that serve as the middle or most important part of something.  It can refer to geographical locations, organizations, activities, or abstract concepts.|例句：Many business centers are located in downtown areas.  许多商务中心位于市中心地区。|例句：The centers of the circles are aligned.  圆圈的中心点对齐了。|近义词：middles, hubs, cores|反义词：outskirts, peripheries


USAGE->
