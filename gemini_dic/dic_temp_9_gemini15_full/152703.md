pearliest:/ˈpɜːrliɪst/ | adj. | 最早的，最先的 | Earliest means being the first or the most advanced in time or development. It is the superlative form of "early".  |例句：The earliest records of the city date back to the 12th century.  这座城市最早的记录可以追溯到12世纪。

The earliest bird catches the worm.  早起的鸟儿有虫吃。

|近义词：first, foremost, initial, primary |反义词：latest, last, final |词性:形容词
