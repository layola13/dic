democratically:/ˌdɛməˈkrætɪkli/ | adv. |民主地；民主地；以民主方式 | In a manner consistent with the principles of democracy, characterized by fair and equal representation of all members.  | 例句：The country is governed democratically. = 这个国家实行民主统治。

The election was conducted democratically. =  选举以民主的方式进行。


近义词：republicanly, popularly


反义词：autocratically, dictatorially, tyrannically


词源:  Derived from "democracy" (itself from Greek "dēmokratía," meaning "rule by the people"), adding the adverbial suffix "-ically."  The word's evolution reflects the historical development of democratic ideals and practices.
