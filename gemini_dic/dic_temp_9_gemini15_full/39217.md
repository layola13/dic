conglomerates:/kənˈɡlɒmərɪts/| n. |企业集团；联合企业；砾岩|A conglomerate is a large corporation composed of several different companies or business units, often operating in unrelated industries.  It's a type of business structure where a parent company owns a controlling interest in several subsidiaries. |Example: Berkshire Hathaway is a well-known conglomerate, owning companies in insurance, railroads, and energy.  伯克希尔哈撒韦公司是一个众所周知的企业集团，拥有保险、铁路和能源领域的公司。|近义词：multinational, corporation, holding company |反义词：sole proprietorship, partnership|
