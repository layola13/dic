schistose:/ˈʃɪstoʊs/| adj.|片岩的；片状的 |Having a schistose structure; composed of thin layers or plates.  Characterized by cleavage, a property allowing the rock to be split easily into thin, flat sheets.|The metamorphic rock exhibited a distinct schistose texture, easily cleaving into thin layers.  这块变质岩呈现出明显的片状结构，很容易劈成薄层。|foliated, laminated|massive, unfoliated
