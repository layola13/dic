hamming:/ˈhæmɪŋ/| n. |汉明距离，汉明编码|The Hamming distance is a metric for comparing two binary strings.  It is the number of positions at which the corresponding bits are different.|例句：The Hamming distance between 1011101 and 1101100 is 2. 汉明距离在1011101和1101100之间是2。|近义词：error correction code|反义词：无
