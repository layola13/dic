playgroups:/ˈpleɪɡruːps/| n. |游戏小组，托儿所|A playgroup is a group of young children who meet regularly to play together, often under the supervision of an adult.  It's typically for pre-school aged children and provides a social and learning environment. |Example: My daughter attends a playgroup twice a week to socialize with other children. 我的女儿每周去游戏小组两次，以便和其他的孩子交往。  The local church runs a popular playgroup for toddlers. 当地教堂经营着一个很受欢迎的幼儿游戏小组。|近义词：preschool, nursery school, daycare |反义词：None
