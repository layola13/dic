postprocessing:/ˈpoʊstˈprɑːsɛsɪŋ/ | n. | 后期处理，后期制作 | The process of modifying or enhancing a product, image, or data after the initial processing or creation.  It often involves refining, correcting, or adding details to improve the final result.  For example, in image processing, post-processing might involve adjusting brightness, contrast, and color balance. In filmmaking, it refers to the editing, sound mixing, and visual effects added after filming. | Example: The photographer spent hours on post-processing to make the image perfect.  后期摄影师花了数小时进行后期处理以使图像完美。  The film's post-processing took several months.  这部电影的后期制作花了几个月的时间。|近义词：finishing, refinement, enhancement |反义词：preprocessing, initial processing |名词
