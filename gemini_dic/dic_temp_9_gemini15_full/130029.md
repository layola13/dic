non-oxidizing: /ˌnɒnˈɒksɪdaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. |非氧化的；不氧化作用的 | Not causing or involving oxidation.  This adjective describes substances or processes that do not cause or promote oxidation, a chemical reaction involving the loss of electrons or an increase in oxidation state.  |


例句：

The reaction proceeded under non-oxidizing conditions.  = 反应在非氧化条件下进行。

A non-oxidizing atmosphere is crucial for this experiment. =  对这个实验来说，非氧化气氛至关重要。


近义词：reducing (in some contexts)

反义词：oxidizing


词性：形容词
