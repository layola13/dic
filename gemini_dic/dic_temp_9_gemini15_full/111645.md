located:/loʊˈkeɪtɪd/| adj.|位于…的；坐落在…的|situated, placed, positioned in a particular place.  The adjective form of the verb "locate." It describes the position or situation of something.|例句：The library is located near the park. 图书馆位于公园附近。|例句：The treasure was located on a deserted island. 宝藏位于一个荒岛上。|近义词：situated, situated, positioned, placed, sited|反义词：unlocated, unplaced


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory is located in the countryside. = 工厂位于农村。
The restaurant is located on Main Street. = 餐厅位于主街上。

一般过去时:
The hotel was located near the beach. = 酒店位于海滩附近。
The meeting was located in a small conference room. = 会议在一个小会议室举行。

现在完成时:
The missing car has been located. = 失踪的汽车已被找到。
The lost dog has been located near the river. = 走失的狗在河边找到了。

过去完成时:
The evidence had been located before the trial began. = 审判开始前证据已被找到。
The painting had been located in a private collection. = 这幅画之前收藏在一个私人收藏中。

将来时:
The new school will be located in the city center. = 新学校将位于市中心。
The conference will be located at the convention center. = 会议将在会议中心举行。

虚拟语气:
If the treasure were located, we would be rich. = 如果宝藏被找到，我们就富有了。
If the missing documents were located, the investigation could be concluded. = 如果找到丢失的文件，调查就能结束。

被动语态:
The building is being located by the surveyors. = 这栋建筑物正在被测量员定位。
The problem is being located in the software. = 问题正在软件中被定位。

疑问句:
Where is the hospital located? = 医院位于哪里？
Where is the nearest post office located? = 最近的邮局在哪里？

否定句:
The village is not located near a major road. = 这个村庄不靠近主要道路。
The missing person has not yet been located. = 失踪人员尚未找到。
