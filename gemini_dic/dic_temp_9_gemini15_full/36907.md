colorization:/ˌkʌləraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |着色；彩色化 | The process of adding color to a previously black-and-white image or film.  This involves techniques to estimate or infer the original colors, often based on context and similar images. | Example: The colorization of old family photos brought them to life.  老照片的着色使它们栩栩如生。 The colorization of the classic film enhanced its visual appeal. 经典电影的着色增强了其视觉吸引力。 | 近义词：coloring, pigmentation | 反义词：desaturation, monochrome
