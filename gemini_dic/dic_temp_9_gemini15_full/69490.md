felicitate:/fəˈlɪsɪteɪt/| vt.|祝贺，庆贺|To congratulate someone on an achievement or good fortune.  |例句：We felicitated her on her wedding. 我们祝贺她的婚礼。|例句：The team was felicitated for their victory.  团队因胜利而受到祝贺。|近义词：congratulate, commend, celebrate|反义词：condemn, berate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We felicitate him on his success. = 我们祝贺他的成功。
They felicitate the winners every year. = 他们每年都祝贺获胜者。


一般过去时:
The company felicitated its employees on the anniversary. = 公司在周年纪念日祝贺其员工。
I felicitated my friend on his promotion. = 我祝贺我的朋友升职。


现在完成时:
They have felicitated the team on their recent win. = 他们已经祝贺球队最近的胜利。
We have felicitated him many times for his hard work. = 我们多次祝贺他辛勤工作。


过去完成时:
They had felicitated the graduates before the ceremony. = 他们在仪式之前已经祝贺毕业生们。
The school had felicitated the students for their achievements. = 学校已经祝贺学生们取得的成就。


将来时:
We will felicitate her on her graduation next month. = 我们下个月将祝贺她的毕业。
They will felicitate the winners at the awards ceremony. = 他们将在颁奖典礼上祝贺获奖者。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would felicitate him on his new job. = 如果我是你，我会祝贺他找到新工作。
I wish I had felicitated her sooner. = 我希望我早点祝贺她。


被动语态:
He was felicitated by the committee for his contribution. = 他因其贡献而受到委员会的祝贺。
The winners were felicitated at the end of the competition. = 比赛结束后，获奖者受到了祝贺。


疑问句:
Will you felicitate him on his birthday? = 你会在他的生日上祝贺他吗？
Did they felicitate the author on the release of his new book? = 他们有没有在作者新书发布时祝贺他？


否定句:
I did not felicitate him on his success. = 我没有祝贺他的成功。
They didn't felicitate her for her efforts. = 他们没有因为她的努力而祝贺她。


PHRASE->
felicitate someone on something: 祝贺某人某事
felicitate someone for something: 因某事祝贺某人
warmly felicitate: 热烈祝贺
publicly felicitate: 公开祝贺
sincerely felicitate: 真诚祝贺
