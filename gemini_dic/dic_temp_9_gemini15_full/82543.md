guana:/ˈɡwɑːnə/| n. |鬣蜥|A large lizard, typically with spiny crests, found in the Americas.|例句：The guana basked in the sun on a rock.  一只鬣蜥在岩石上晒太阳。|例句： Several species of guana are found in the Caribbean.加勒比海地区发现了数种鬣蜥。|近义词：iguana (often used interchangeably)|反义词：None.
