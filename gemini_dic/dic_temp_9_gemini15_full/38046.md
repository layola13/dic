compos:/ˈkɒmpəʊz/ /kəmˈpoʊz/ | v. | 创作；作曲；构成；使平静；调解 | to create or produce something, especially music, art, or writing; to arrange or form something; to make someone calm or peaceful | 例句：She composed a beautiful melody. 她创作了一首优美的旋律。 He composed himself before speaking. 他在说话前镇定了一下。 | 近义词：create, write, compose, form, settle, calm | 反义词：agitate, disturb, upset


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He composes music every day. = 他每天都作曲。
The elements compose the earth. = 元素构成地球。


一般过去时:
She composed a poem last night. = 她昨晚写了一首诗。
The judge composed the dispute between the two parties. = 法官调解了双方之间的纠纷。


现在完成时:
He has composed several symphonies. = 他已经创作了几首交响乐。
She has composed herself after the shock. = 她经历震惊后已经恢复平静。


过去完成时:

They had composed their differences before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经化解了彼此间的分歧。


将来时:

He will compose himself and deliver the speech. = 他将会镇定下来并发表演讲。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
