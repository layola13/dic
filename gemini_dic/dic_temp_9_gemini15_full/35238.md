clumps:/klʌmps/| n.|块；丛；堆|A clump is a small group of things, especially plants, growing or lying close together.  It can also refer to a shapeless mass.|例句：There was a clump of trees by the river. 河边有一丛树。

There was a clump of earth on his boot. 他的靴子上有一块泥土。|近义词：cluster, bunch, group|反义词：scatter, dispersion
