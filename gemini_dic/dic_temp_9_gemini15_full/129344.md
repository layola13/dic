noisomely:/ˈnɔɪsəmli/| adv. |令人作呕地；恶臭地；令人不快地 |In a noisome manner; offensively; disgustingly.  The decaying fish smelled noisomely.  |The air in the factory smelled noisomely of chemicals. |近义词：offensively, disgustingly, repulsively, nauseatingly |反义词：pleasantly, sweetly, agreeably


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He speaks noisomely about his rivals. = 他恶毒地谈论他的竞争对手。
The garbage smells noisomely in the summer heat. = 夏日的酷暑中，垃圾散发着恶臭。


一般过去时:
She spoke noisomely of her former friends. = 她恶毒地谈论她以前的朋友。
The old house smelled noisomely after the flood. = 洪水过后，老房子里散发着恶臭。
