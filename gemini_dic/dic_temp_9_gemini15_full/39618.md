consenter:/kənˈsɛntər/ | n. | 同意者；赞成者 | A person who consents or agrees to something.  | Example: He was a consenter to the plan, though he didn't actively participate. 他是这项计划的同意者，尽管他没有积极参与。 |近义词:approver, supporter, agreement |反义词:objector, opponent, dissenter

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The consenter signs the document. = 同意者签署文件。
She consents to the terms of the contract. = 她同意合同条款。


一般过去时:
He consented to the marriage. = 他同意了这桩婚事。
The committee consented to the proposal. = 委员会同意了这项提案。


现在完成时:
They have consented to the changes. = 他们已经同意了这些改变。
She has consented to participate in the study. = 她已经同意参与这项研究。


过去完成时:
He had consented to the deal before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经同意了这笔交易。
The board had consented to the merger before the announcement. = 董事会在宣布之前已经同意了合并。


将来时:
They will consent to the request. = 他们将会同意这个请求。
We believe he will consent to our terms. = 我们相信他会同意我们的条件。


虚拟语气:
If he consented, we could proceed. = 如果他同意，我们就可以继续进行。
I wish they consented to our plan. = 我希望他们同意我们的计划。


被动语态:
The terms were consented to by all parties. = 所有各方都同意了这些条款。
His request was consented to by the manager. = 他的请求得到了经理的同意。


疑问句:
Did he consent to the operation? = 他同意手术了吗？
Will they consent to our terms? = 他们会同意我们的条件吗？


否定句:
He did not consent to the search. = 他不同意搜查。
She did not consent to the interview. = 她不同意接受采访。


PHRASE->
give consent: 同意
with one consent: 一致同意
obtain consent: 获得同意
full consent: 完全同意
informed consent: 知情同意
