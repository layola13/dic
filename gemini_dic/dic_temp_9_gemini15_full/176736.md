readabilities: /ˌriːdəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可读性 | The quality of being easy to read and understand.  It refers to how easily a text can be understood by its intended audience. This is often measured by factors such as sentence length, vocabulary complexity, and overall structure. | Example:  The readabilities of the website were improved by simplifying the language and using shorter sentences.  网站的可读性通过简化语言和使用更短的句子得到了提高。|近义词：comprehensibility, understandability, accessibility | 反义词：unintelligibility, obscurity
