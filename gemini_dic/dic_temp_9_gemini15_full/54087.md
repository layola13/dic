disembarks:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbɑːrk/| vt.|下船；下飞机；卸货 |To leave a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.  |例句：The passengers disembarked from the ferry.  乘客们下了渡轮。

例句：The cargo was carefully disembarked at the port. 货物在港口小心地卸下。|近义词：leave, alight, debark |反义词：embark, board


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disembarks the plane every morning. = 他每天早上都下飞机。
The ship disembarks its cargo at the dock. = 这艘船在码头卸货。

一般过去时:
They disembarked the train at the station. = 他们在车站下了火车。
The passengers disembarked quickly and efficiently. = 乘客们迅速而有效地下船了。

现在完成时:
The cruise ship has disembarked all its passengers. = 游轮已经卸下所有乘客了。
He has disembarked from the flight and is now heading home. = 他已经下了飞机，现在正回家。

过去完成时:
The crew had disembarked the lifeboats before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，船员们已经放下救生艇了。
They had disembarked the goods before the warehouse closed. = 仓库关闭之前，他们已经卸下货物了。

将来时:
We will disembark the plane in London. = 我们将在伦敦下飞机。
The ship will disembark its cargo tomorrow morning. = 这艘船将在明天早上卸货。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would disembark at the next stop. = 如果我是你，我会在下一站下车。
I wish I had disembarked earlier to avoid the traffic. = 我希望我早点下车以避免交通堵塞。

被动语态:
The passengers were disembarked safely. = 乘客们安全地下船了。
The goods were disembarked without any damage. = 货物在没有任何损坏的情况下被卸下了。

疑问句:
Where will you disembark? = 你将在哪里下船/下飞机？
When does the ship disembark its passengers? = 船什么时候卸下乘客？

否定句:
He did not disembark at the scheduled time. = 他没有按预定时间下船/下飞机。
The cargo was not disembarked properly. = 货物没有被妥善卸下。


PHRASE->

disembark from a plane = 从飞机下飞机
disembark at a port = 在港口下船
disembark the cargo = 卸货
disembark passengers = 让乘客下船/飞机
