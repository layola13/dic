oversourly: /ˈoʊvərˈsaʊərli/ | adv. | 过酸地，酸得过头地 |  Excessively sour; to an excessive degree of sourness.  |The lemon pie was oversourly tart.  柠檬派酸得过头了。| sourly, tartly | sweetly, mildly |


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't have tenses like verbs.  Its usage is similar to other adverbs of manner.

例句:
She wrinkled her nose and said, "This is oversourly." 她皱着鼻子说：“这太酸了。”

He commented that the kimchi was oversourly fermented. 他评论说泡菜发酵过度，酸过头了。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "oversourly".  The word itself is relatively uncommon.
