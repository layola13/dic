emancipist:/ɪˈmæn.sɪ.pɪst/| n. |废奴主义者；解放论者|A person who advocates for the emancipation of slaves or oppressed people.  They believe in and actively promote the liberation of individuals from slavery, oppression, or other forms of subjugation.|Example:  Many brave emancipists risked their lives fighting for the abolition of slavery in the 19th century.  许多勇敢的废奴主义者冒着生命危险为19世纪废除奴隶制而斗争。|近义词：abolitionist, liberationist|反义词：slaveholder, oppressor|名词
