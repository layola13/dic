occludent:/ˈɒkjʊdənt/| n. |遮挡物，遮蔽物 |Something that blocks or obstructs light, vision, or passage.  An occludent can be anything that prevents something from being seen or passing through.| The mountain range acted as a natural occludent, shielding the valley from the harsh winds.  那座山脉充当了天然的遮挡物，保护山谷免受强风的侵袭。|obstacle, barrier, obstruction|open space, clear path|名词
