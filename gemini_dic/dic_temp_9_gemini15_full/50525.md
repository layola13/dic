depths:/depθs/| n.|深处；深奥；深渊|The depths of the ocean are largely unexplored. 海洋深处在很大程度上未被探索。|近义词：abyss, profundity |反义词：surface, shallows


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The depths of the ocean are vast and mysterious. = 海洋的深处广阔而神秘。
The depths of his knowledge are truly impressive. = 他知识的深度确实令人印象深刻。

一般过去时:
The divers explored the depths of the sunken ship. = 潜水员探索了沉船的深处。
We plumbed the depths of despair after the news. = 在听到这个消息后，我们陷入了绝望的深渊。

现在完成时:
Scientists have mapped the depths of the Mariana Trench. = 科学家们已经绘制了马里亚纳海沟的深度图。
She has plumbed the depths of human emotion in her novel. = 她在小说中探究了人类情感的深处。

过去完成时:
They had already reached the depths of the cave before the lights went out. = 灯灭之前，他们已经到达了洞穴深处。
He had explored the depths of his own psyche before making the decision. = 他在做出决定之前已经探索过自己心灵的深处。

将来时:
We will explore the depths of the Amazon rainforest next year. = 我们明年将探索亚马逊雨林的深处。
Researchers will delve into the depths of the data to find answers. = 研究人员将深入研究数据以寻找答案。

虚拟语气:
If I could explore the depths of space, I would. = 如果我能探索太空深处，我会的。
I wish I could understand the depths of his feelings. = 我希望我能理解他内心深处的感受。

被动语态:

The depths of the mystery were gradually revealed by the investigators. = 这个谜团的深处逐渐被调查人员揭开。

疑问句:

否定句:
