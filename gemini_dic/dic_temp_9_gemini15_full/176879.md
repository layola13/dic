reagent's:/riˈeɪdʒənts/| n. |试剂（复数）|A reagent is a substance or compound that is added to a system to cause a chemical reaction, or added to see if a reaction occurs.|The experiment requires several reagents, including hydrochloric acid and sodium hydroxide.|近义词：chemical, reactant|反义词：product
