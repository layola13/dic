consultatory:/kənˈsʌltəˌtɔːri/| adj. |咨询的；顾问的；劝告的 | Relating to consultation; giving advice or opinions.  It implies a process of seeking and giving advice, often in a professional context. |The report offered consultancy advice on improving efficiency. 这份报告就如何提高效率提供了咨询建议。| advisory, advisory, consultative | non-consultatory
