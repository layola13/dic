longitude:/ˈlɒŋdʒɪtuːd/ /ˈlɑːŋdʒɪtuːd/| n. |经度|The angular distance east or west of the prime meridian, measured in degrees, minutes, and seconds.|The longitude of London is 0 degrees.  伦敦的经度是0度。|The ship's position was determined by its latitude and longitude. 船的位置是由它的纬度和经度确定的。|latitude, meridian|latitude|名词
