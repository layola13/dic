ganged:/ɡæŋd/| v.|成群结队；联合；(使)成组|To come together in a group or gang; to unite or combine into a group.  The word often implies a negative connotation, suggesting a group formed for unlawful or antisocial purposes.|例句：The teenagers ganged up on the smaller boy.  十几岁的孩子们一起欺负那个个子小的男孩。

例句：The workers ganged together to demand better pay. 工人们联合起来要求更高的工资。|近义词：banded, grouped, combined, allied|反义词：separated, dispersed, divided|词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They gang up on him every day. = 他们每天都联合起来对付他。
The birds gang together to find food. = 鸟儿们成群结队地觅食。

一般过去时:
The thieves ganged up to rob the bank. = 窃贼们合伙去抢银行。
The students ganged together to protest the unfair rules. = 学生们联合起来抗议不公平的规定。

现在完成时:
They have ganged up against the new manager. = 他们已经联合起来反对新经理。
The workers have ganged together for many years. = 工人们已经联合了很多年了。

过去完成时:
The rebels had ganged up before the attack. = 反叛者在袭击之前就已经联合起来了。
The criminals had ganged together long before the crime was committed. = 罪犯们在犯罪发生很久之前就已经联合起来了。

将来时:

The protesters will gang together to march on the capital. = 抗议者们将联合起来游行到首都。


虚拟语气:
If they ganged up, they could accomplish more. = 如果他们联合起来，他们就能完成更多的事情。
I wish they hadn't ganged up on me. = 我真希望他们没有联合起来对付我。

被动语态:

The project was ganged together quickly and efficiently. = 这个项目被快速有效地组合到一起。

疑问句:

否定句:

The students didn't gang together to disrupt the class. = 学生们没有联合起来扰乱课堂。
