mesoamerica:/ˌmɛzoʊˈæmərɪkə/| n.|中美洲|Mesoamerica is a historical region and cultural area in North America.  It extends from roughly central Mexico through Belize, Guatemala, Honduras, El Salvador, and into Nicaragua. The term is used to describe the region's shared cultural traits, especially during the Pre-Columbian era.  The region is known for its advanced civilizations, such as the Maya and Aztec, who developed sophisticated writing systems, calendars, mathematics, and art.  |The study of Mesoamerica reveals a fascinating tapestry of interconnected cultures.  |近义词：Central America (but Mesoamerica has a stronger cultural connotation)|反义词：无|名词
