pretabulation: /ˌpriːtæbjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 预先制表；预先列表 | The act or process of preparing a table or list in advance.  It often refers to the creation of tables of data before they are needed for a specific purpose, such as in data processing or statistical analysis. | Example: The pretabulation of survey responses allowed for quicker analysis. 预先制表调查问卷的回复使得分析更快。 | 近义词:  pre-processing, advance preparation | 反义词: post-tabulation
