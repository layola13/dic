marksman:/ˈmɑːrksmən/| n. |神枪手，射手 |A marksman is someone who is skillful in shooting, especially with a rifle or pistol.  They are known for their accuracy and precision. |例句：He is a renowned marksman, capable of hitting targets at incredible distances. 他是一位著名的神枪手，能够在令人难以置信的距离击中目标。

例句：The marksman's steady hand ensured a perfect shot.  神枪手稳住的手确保了完美的一击。


近义词：sharpshooter, rifleman, expert shot |反义词：poor shot, inaccurate shooter |词性:名词

词源:
The word "marksman" comes from the Old English word "marc," meaning "boundary" or "target," combined with "man," referring to a person.  The word evolved to describe someone skilled in hitting a mark or target.
