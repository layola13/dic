freegan:/ˈfriːɡən/| n. |觅食者，反消费主义者 |A freegan is a person who participates in freeganism, a lifestyle that largely rejects consumerism and involves retrieving discarded food and other usable goods.  Freegans aim to reduce their environmental impact and challenge capitalist systems by obtaining resources without spending money. |例句：Many freegans believe that perfectly good food is wasted unnecessarily. 许多觅食者认为完全可以食用的食物被不必要地浪费了。

例句：The freegan community often shares tips and resources on how to find discarded food. 觅食者社群经常分享如何寻找丢弃食物的技巧和资源。


近义词：dumpster diver (俚语), scavenger, reclaimer |反义词：consumer, shopper, spendthrift |词性：名词
