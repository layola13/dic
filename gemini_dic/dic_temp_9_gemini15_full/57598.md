draped:/dreɪpt/| v. |披上，挂上，覆盖|To hang or fall in loose folds.  It can also describe something loosely covering or hanging over something else. |例句：She draped a shawl around her shoulders. 她披了一条披肩在肩上。

例句：The curtains were draped elegantly across the window. 窗帘优雅地垂挂在窗户上。


近义词：hung, covered, festooned |反义词：straightened, taut, arranged neatly |词性:动词


USAGE->
