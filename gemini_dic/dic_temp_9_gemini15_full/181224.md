reinsertion:/ˌriːɪnˈsɜːrʃən/ | n. |重新插入；恢复原状 | The act of inserting something again, or restoring something to its original place or position.  This often implies a previous removal or displacement. |例句：The surgeon performed a reinsertion of the dislocated shoulder. 外科医生进行了脱臼肩膀的复位手术。

例句：The reinsertion of the program into the schedule was met with relief.  该程序重新安排进日程表，让人们松了一口气。


近义词：reinstatement, restoration, reintroduction | 反义词：removal, extraction, deletion | 词性：noun
