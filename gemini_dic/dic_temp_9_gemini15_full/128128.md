netbook:/ˈnɛtbʊk/| n. |小型笔记本电脑|A small, lightweight laptop computer, typically with a smaller screen and less powerful processor than a standard laptop.  Netbooks are designed for portability and basic computing tasks such as web browsing and email. |Example:  She bought a netbook for traveling, as it was lightweight and easy to carry. 她买了一台小型笔记本电脑用于旅行，因为它轻便易携。|近义词：mini-laptop, notebook (less precise)|反义词：desktop computer, workstation|名词
