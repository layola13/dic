scenes:/siːnz/ | n. | 场景；景象；事件 | The word "scenes" refers to a sequence of events, a view, or a setting, often in a play, movie, or book. It can also describe a particular situation or event. | 例句：The movie's opening scenes were breathtaking. 这部电影的开场场景令人叹为观止。

The police are investigating the scenes of the crime. 警方正在调查犯罪现场。


近义词：sights, views, events, episodes | 反义词：None directly applicable.  | 词性：名词
