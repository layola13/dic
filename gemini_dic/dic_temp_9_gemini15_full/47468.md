dealing:/ˈdiːlɪŋ/| n. v.|处理；交易；行为；交往|The act of giving and receiving something, especially in business;  a particular way of behaving or interacting with someone or something.|例句：He's experienced in dealing with difficult customers. 他擅长处理难缠的顾客。
例句：The company is involved in international dealing. 这家公司从事国际贸易。|近义词：handling, transaction, interaction, conduct|反义词：neglect, avoidance


USAGE->
