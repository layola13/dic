overadorns: /ˌoʊvərəˈdɔːrnz/ | vt. |过度装饰；装饰得过分|To decorate excessively; to adorn to an excessive degree.  The room was overadorned with gaudy decorations.  |近义词：overdecorate, embellish excessively, overload|反义词：underdecorate, simplify

词源:
The word "overadorns" is formed by adding the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") to the verb "adorns."  "Adorns" itself comes from the Old French word "adourner," ultimately from the Latin "adornāre," meaning "to adorn" or "to decorate."
