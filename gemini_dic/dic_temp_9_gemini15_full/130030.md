non-parallel:/ˌnɒnˈpærəlel/ | adj. | 不平行的；不相似的；不一致的 | Not parallel; lacking parallelism.  Describes elements that do not maintain a consistent structure or relationship, often in writing or programming. | Example: The two arguments presented were non-parallel, making the conclusion unconvincing. 这两个论点并不平行，因此结论令人难以信服。|近义词：unparallel, disparate, inconsistent |反义词：parallel, consistent, uniform
