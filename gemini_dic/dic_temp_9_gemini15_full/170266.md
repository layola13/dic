proudness:/ˈpraʊdnəs/| n. |骄傲；自豪|The feeling of pride and self-respect; a state of being proud.  It often implies a sense of accomplishment or superiority.|例句：Her proudness in her achievements was evident.  她的成就让她感到非常自豪。

例句：His proudness prevented him from admitting his mistake. 他过于骄傲，不肯承认自己的错误。


近义词：pride, self-esteem, vanity, arrogance

反义词：humility, modesty, shame


词性：名词
