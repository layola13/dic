masterminded:/ˈmɑːstərmʌɪndɪd/ | v. |策划，设计，主导 |  To be the intellectual author or driving force behind a plan, especially one that is complex or ingenious.  It emphasizes the cleverness and skill involved in the planning. | 例句：She masterminded the company's successful marketing campaign. = 她策划了公司成功的营销活动。

The CEO masterminded the company's expansion into new markets. = 首席执行官策划了公司向新市场的扩张。


近义词：planned, devised, conceived, orchestrated, engineered | 反义词：bungled, botched, failed
