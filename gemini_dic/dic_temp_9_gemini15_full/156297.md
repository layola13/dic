phylogenesis:/ˌfaɪləˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/| n. |种系发生；系统发生；系统发育|The evolutionary development and diversification of a species or group of organisms, or of a particular feature of an organism.  It traces the lineage of organisms back through evolutionary history.|例句：The phylogenesis of birds is a complex and fascinating subject. 鸟类的系统发育是一个复杂而迷人的课题。

例句：Understanding the phylogenesis of viruses is crucial for developing effective antiviral strategies. 理解病毒的系统发育对于开发有效的抗病毒策略至关重要。
