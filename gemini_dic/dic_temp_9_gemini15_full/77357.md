gaudygreen:/ˈɡɔːdiˌɡriːn/ | adj. |俗丽的绿色，花哨的绿色 |  Describes a shade of green that is excessively bright, showy, and often considered tasteless. It combines the meaning of "gaudy" (showy and tasteless) with the color "green".  | The gaudygreen dress clashed horribly with her complexion.  那件俗丽的绿色连衣裙与她的肤色极不相称。 | flashy, loud, garish, showy | subtle, muted, understated, tasteful | 形容词
