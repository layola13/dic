phaseomannite:/ˌfeɪzoʊˈmænɪt/| n. |甘露醇|A rare, naturally occurring sugar alcohol found in some plants. It is a stereoisomer of mannitol, differing only in the configuration of one hydroxyl group.  Phaseomannitol is a hexitol, meaning it has six carbon atoms and multiple hydroxyl groups.  It has limited commercial applications. |例句：The research paper detailed the isolation and characterization of phaseomannite from a previously unknown species of legume. = 这篇研究论文详细介绍了从一种以前未知的豆科植物中分离和鉴定甘露醇的过程。|近义词:mannitol, other hexitols|反义词:|  n.
