outshone:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊn/| vt.|胜过，超过，掩盖|To surpass in excellence, brilliance, or achievement.|例句：Her talent outshone all the others.  她的才能胜过其他人。|例句：The sun outshone the clouds. 太阳的光芒盖过了乌云。|近义词：surpass, excel, eclipse, overshadow|反义词：be outdone, be inferior to

USAGE->
一般现在时:
His skills outshine his brother's. = 他的技能胜过他哥哥的。
This painting outshines all the others in the exhibition. = 这幅画胜过展览会上其他的所有画作。

一般过去时:
Her performance outshone all expectations. = 她的表演超出了所有人的预期。
The new design outshone the old one in terms of efficiency. = 新的设计在效率方面胜过旧设计。

现在完成时:
His latest novel has outshone all his previous works. = 他最新的这部小说已经超越了他以前的所有作品。
Their efforts have outshone their rivals. = 他们的努力已经超过了他们的竞争对手。

过去完成时:
By the time the competition ended, her skills had outshone everyone else's. = 到比赛结束时，她的技能已经胜过其他所有人。
The company's performance had outshone its projections for the quarter.  = 公司的业绩已经超过了本季度的预期。

将来时:
The new technology will outshine existing methods. = 新技术将胜过现有的方法。
We believe that our product will outshine the competition. = 我们相信我们的产品将胜过竞争对手。


被动语态:
His achievements were outshone by his brother's.  = 他的成就被弟弟的成就所掩盖。
The beauty of the landscape was outshone by the magnificence of the sunset. = 风景的美景被日落的壮丽所掩盖。


疑问句:
Does his work outshine his colleagues'? = 他的作品是否胜过他的同事们？
Will this strategy outshine the previous one? = 这一策略将会胜过之前的策略吗？


否定句:
His efforts did not outshine his competitors'. = 他的努力并没有胜过他的竞争对手。
The film's special effects did not outshine the story's weakness. = 这部电影的特效并没有掩盖故事情节的薄弱之处。
