portage:/ˈpɔːrtɪdʒ/| n. |搬运；陆路运输；背运；（尤指）运河旁的陆路运河段|The act of carrying boats or goods overland, especially between navigable waterways.|The team completed the arduous portage across the isthmus.  团队完成了穿越地峡的艰苦搬运工作。|carriage, transport|—|n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They portage their canoes around the falls. = 他们把独木舟搬运到瀑布的另一边。
We portage supplies to the remote village. = 我们把物资运送到偏远的村庄。


一般过去时:
The pioneers portaged their belongings across the mountains. = 拓荒者们把他们的行李搬运过山。
He portaged his canoe around the rapids. = 他把独木舟搬运过急流。


现在完成时:
They have portaged all the equipment to the campsite. = 他们已经把所有装备搬运到营地了。
We have portaged several times this week. = 我们这周已经搬运了好几次了。


过去完成时:
They had portaged the canoes before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经把独木舟搬运好了。
She had portaged many miles before she reached the river. = 在到达河边之前，她已经搬运了很多英里。


将来时:
We will portage the supplies tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上会搬运物资。
They will portage their boats around the dam. = 他们将在水坝周围搬运他们的船只。


虚拟语气:
If we had a better trail, we would portage more easily. = 如果我们有一条更好的小路，搬运起来就会更容易。
I wish we could portage the entire load in one go. = 我希望我们能够一次性搬运全部货物。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
They did not portage the heavy equipment. = 他们没有搬运那些重型设备。
We cannot portage the canoe without help. = 没有帮助我们无法搬运独木舟。
