computational:/ˌkɒmpjuˈteɪʃənəl/ /ˌkɑːmpjuˈteɪʃənəl/| adj. |计算的；计算方面的| Relating to or involving computation or computers.  This adjective describes something that is related to or involves the process of computation, often using computers. It can refer to the methods, techniques, or processes involved in computation, or the applications of computation to various fields. |例句：The project requires significant computational resources.  这个项目需要大量的计算资源。

例句：Computational linguistics is a rapidly growing field. 计算语言学是一个快速发展的领域。


近义词：calculative, numerical, digital


反义词：analog, non-digital


词源:  Derived from "computation," which comes from the Latin word "computare," meaning "to compute" or "to reckon."  The suffix "-ational"  transforms the noun into an adjective.
