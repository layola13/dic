equidimensional:/ˌiːkwɪdaɪˈmɛnʃənəl/| adj. |等维的，等度的|Having all dimensions equal.  This term is primarily used in mathematics and geometry, referring to shapes or objects where all sides or dimensions are of the same length or measure. For example, a cube is equidimensional because all its sides are equal.  |例句：A cube is an example of an equidimensional shape. 一个立方体是一个等维形状的例子。


The concept of equidimensionality is also relevant in higher dimensional spaces where all axes have equal extent.  |例句：In this mathematical model, the space is assumed to be equidimensional for simplicity. 在这个数学模型中，为了简便起见，假设空间是等维的。|近义词：isometric, equi-dimensional|反义词：multidimensional (in the context of unequal dimensions), anisotropic
