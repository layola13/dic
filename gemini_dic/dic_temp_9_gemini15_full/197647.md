sheriffs:/ˈʃerɪfs/| n.|警长，治安官|A sheriff is a law enforcement officer, typically in a county, responsible for maintaining peace and order.  They often oversee jails and serve legal documents.|例句：The sheriff's department responded to the emergency call immediately. 警长部门立即回应了紧急呼叫。|例句：The sheriff served the eviction notice to the tenant. 警长向房客送达了驱逐令。|近义词：police chief, constable, marshal|反义词：criminal, offender
