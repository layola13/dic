gaolers:/ˈɡeɪlərz/| n. |监狱看守，看守人 |A gaoler is a person who guards prisoners in a jail.  They are responsible for the security and well-being of the inmates, although their duties and responsibilities vary depending on the specific jail and its security level.  The term is now less common than "jailer" or "prison guard". | The gaolers were strict but fair. 监狱看守很严格但很公平。  The cruel gaoler mistreated the prisoners. 残酷的狱卒虐待囚犯。| jailers, prison guards, wardens |  (No direct antonyms, but could use terms implying freedom or liberation) | 名词
