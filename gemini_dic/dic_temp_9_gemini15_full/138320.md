notepad:/ˈnoʊtpæd/ | n. |记事本，便笺本 |A notepad is a small, usually bound book of paper for writing notes.  It can also refer to a simple text-editing program on a computer. |例句：I jotted down the phone number in my notepad. 我在我的记事本上记下了电话号码。

I used the notepad application to create a simple text file. 我使用记事本应用程序创建了一个简单的文本文件。|近义词：notebook, memo pad,  text editor (for the computer program)|反义词：None
