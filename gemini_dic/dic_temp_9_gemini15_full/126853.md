napkins:/ˈnæpkɪnz/| n.|餐巾纸；餐巾|A small piece of cloth or paper used at meals for wiping the mouth or fingers.  They are often provided in restaurants and homes.|例句：Please pass the napkins. 请把餐巾纸递给我。

例句：She dabbed her mouth with a napkin. 她用餐巾纸擦了擦嘴。


近义词：serviette, tissue, bib|反义词：无
