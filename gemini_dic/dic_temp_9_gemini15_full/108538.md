laze around:/leɪz əˈraʊnd/ | v. |懒洋洋地闲逛，无所事事 | To spend time in a relaxed way, doing very little.  | 例句：I spent the afternoon lazing around in the park. 我在公园里悠闲地度过了下午。

例句：He lazed around all day, watching TV and eating snacks. 他一整天都在懒洋洋地闲逛，看电视吃零食。


近义词：loaf, lounge, relax, idle, do nothing  | 反义词：work hard, hustle, bustle
