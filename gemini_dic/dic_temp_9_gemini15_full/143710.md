outline:/ˈaʊtlaɪn/| n.|概要，轮廓，大纲|A general description or plan giving the essential features of something but not the detail.  It can refer to a drawing showing the outer edges of an object, a summary of a subject, or a plan for a piece of writing.|例句：I made an outline of my essay before I started writing. 我在开始写作之前先做了一个文章提纲。|近义词：summary, sketch, synopsis, framework |反义词：detail, elaboration


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The outline of the mountain is visible. = 山的轮廓可见。
This document provides an outline of the project. =这份文件提供了项目的概要。

一般过去时:
He outlined his plan to the committee. = 他向委员会概述了他的计划。
She outlined the main points of the argument. = 她概述了论点的要点。

现在完成时:
I have outlined the key arguments in my presentation. = 我在我的演示文稿中概述了关键论点。
They have outlined a new strategy for the company. = 他们为公司勾勒了一个新的战略。

过去完成时:
She had outlined the research findings before the meeting. = 会议前她已概述了研究结果。
They had outlined their objectives at the beginning of the project. = 他们在项目开始时就已概述了他们的目标。

将来时:
I will outline the next steps in the process. = 我将概述该流程的后续步骤。
He will outline his vision for the future of the company. = 他将概述他对公司未来的愿景。

虚拟语气:
If I had outlined my ideas more clearly, the meeting would have been more productive. = 如果我更清晰地概述我的想法，会议就会更有成效。
It is essential that he outline his proposal in detail. = 至关重要的是，他必须详细地概述他的提案。

被动语态:
The main points were outlined in the introduction. = 要点在引言中已概述。
The plan will be outlined in the next chapter. = 该计划将在下一章中概述。

疑问句:
Can you outline the key features of the product? = 你能概述一下产品的关键特性吗？
What will be outlined in the next section? = 下一部分将概述什么？

否定句:
The report did not outline the potential risks. = 报告没有概述潜在的风险。
He failed to outline his reasons for the decision. = 他未能概述他做出决定的理由。
