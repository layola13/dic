oscillative:/ˈɒsɪleɪtɪv/| adj. | 振荡的；摇摆的；犹豫不决的 | Relating to or characterized by oscillation; fluctuating or wavering.  | The oscillative motion of the pendulum was mesmerizing.  摆的振荡运动令人着迷。The market showed oscillative behavior in the last quarter.  市场在上一季度表现出震荡行为。 | fluctuating, wavering, unsteady, vibrating | stable, constant, steady


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The pendulum oscillates back and forth. = 钟摆来回摆动。
The needle oscillates slightly. = 指针轻微摆动。

一般过去时:
The stock price oscillated wildly yesterday. = 昨天的股价剧烈波动。
The machine oscillated during the test. = 机器在测试过程中振动。

现在完成时:
The temperature has oscillated between 20 and 30 degrees. = 温度一直在20到30度之间波动。
The fan has oscillated continuously for hours. = 风扇已经连续运转了好几个小时。

过去完成时:
The machine had oscillated violently before it broke down. = 机器在故障前剧烈振动。
The needle had oscillated erratically before settling. = 指针在稳定下来之前不稳定地摆动。

将来时:
The market will oscillate further in the coming weeks. = 未来几周市场将进一步波动。
The device will oscillate at a specific frequency. = 该设备将以特定频率振动。

虚拟语气:
If the machine oscillated less, the results would be more accurate. = 如果机器振动较小，结果将更准确。
I wish the pendulum oscillated more smoothly. = 我希望钟摆摆动更平稳。

被动语态:
The data was oscillated to remove noise. = 数据经过振荡处理以去除噪声。(此处的oscillate使用较少见，通常用其他更合适的动词)
The signal is being oscillated by the circuit. = 信号正在被电路振荡。(此处的oscillate使用较少见，通常用其他更合适的动词)


疑问句:
Does the machine oscillate too much? = 机器振动过多吗？
Does the needle oscillate frequently? = 指针经常摆动吗？

否定句:
The temperature did not oscillate significantly. = 温度没有显著波动。
The pendulum does not oscillate consistently. = 钟摆摆动不稳定。
