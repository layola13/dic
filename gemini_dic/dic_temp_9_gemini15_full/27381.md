came out:/keɪm aʊt/ | v. |公开承认；出版；结果是；显现；(植物)开花；(衣服)褪色 | To publicly declare one's homosexuality; to be published or released; to become known or apparent; to emerge or appear; (of plants) to flower; (of clothes) to fade. |例句：He came out to his family last year. 他去年向家人出柜了。 |She came out with a new novel last month. 她上个月出版了一部新小说。|近义词：revealed, published, emerged, appeared |反义词：concealed, hidden, suppressed
