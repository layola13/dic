meanwhile:/ˈmiːnwaɪl/| adv.|同时，其间|During the time that something else is happening; at the same time.|例句：I'll be at the library; meanwhile, you can start dinner. 我会在图书馆；同时，你可以开始做晚饭。|近义词：simultaneously, concurrently, in the meantime|反义词：afterward, subsequently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are working on the project; meanwhile, they are also planning for the next one. = 他们正在进行这个项目；同时，他们也在为下一个项目做计划。
The children are playing outside; meanwhile, their parents are cleaning the house. = 孩子们在外面玩耍；与此同时，他们的父母正在打扫房子。


一般过去时:
She was studying for the exam; meanwhile, she was also working part-time. = 她正在准备考试；同时，她还在兼职。
He was writing a novel; meanwhile, he was also teaching English. = 他正在写一部小说；与此同时，他还教英语。


现在完成时:
We have finished the report; meanwhile, we have also prepared the presentation. = 我们已经完成了报告；与此同时，我们也准备好了演示文稿。
They have completed the first phase; meanwhile, they have started working on the second. = 他们已经完成了第一阶段；与此同时，他们已经开始着手第二阶段。


过去完成时:
I had finished my homework; meanwhile, I had also watched a movie. = 我已经完成了我的作业；与此同时，我还看了一部电影。
She had prepared the food; meanwhile, he had set the table. = 她已经准备好了食物；与此同时，他已经摆好了桌子。


将来时:
I will be traveling next week; meanwhile, I will keep checking my emails. = 我下周要旅行；与此同时，我会继续查看我的邮件。
They will be launching the product soon; meanwhile, they will continue advertising. = 他们很快就会推出产品；与此同时，他们将继续进行广告宣传。


虚拟语气:
If I were to go on vacation, I would relax on the beach; meanwhile, I would also read a book. = 如果我去度假，我会在海滩上放松；同时，我也会看书。
If she had more time, she would finish her painting; meanwhile, she would also practice her piano. = 如果她有更多的时间，她会完成她的绘画；同时，她也会练习钢琴。


被动语态:
The report is being prepared; meanwhile, the presentation is also being designed. = 报告正在准备中；同时，演示文稿也在设计中。
The house is being renovated; meanwhile, the garden is being landscaped. = 房子正在装修；与此同时，花园正在进行景观设计。


疑问句:
Are you working on your thesis; meanwhile, are you also teaching? = 你正在写你的论文吗；同时，你也教书吗？
Is she studying abroad; meanwhile, is she also working? = 她正在国外学习吗；同时，她也在工作吗？


否定句:
They are not working today; meanwhile, they are not available for meetings. = 他们今天不上班；同时，他们也没有时间参加会议。
She is not attending the conference; meanwhile, she is not submitting her paper. = 她没有参加会议；同时，她也没有提交论文。


PHRASE->
in the meanwhile = 与此同时
for the meanwhile = 暂时
during the meanwhile = 在此期间
meanwhile, however = 然而，与此同时
meanwhile back at the ranch = 然而与此同时（常用语，表示场景转换）
