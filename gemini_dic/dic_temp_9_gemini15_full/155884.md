photoaffinity: /ˌfoʊtoʊəˈfɪnəti/ | n. | 光亲和性 | The specific, high-affinity binding of a photoreactive molecule to a biological macromolecule, often followed by covalent bond formation upon light irradiation.  This technique is used to identify and study the binding sites of ligands on proteins. | Example: Photoaffinity labeling is a powerful technique for studying protein-ligand interactions. 光亲和标记是研究蛋白质-配体相互作用的有力技术。| 近义词：photolabeling, affinity labeling | 反义词：无  | 名词
