raptures:/ˈræptʃərz/| n.|狂喜；欣喜若狂；着迷|Extreme happiness; delight; a feeling of intense joy or pleasure.  The word often suggests an overwhelming, almost ecstatic feeling.|例句：She was filled with raptures at the news of her son's success. 她听到儿子成功的消息后欣喜若狂。


例句：The beauty of the sunset filled her with raptures. 日落的美丽让她欣喜若狂。


近义词：ecstasy, bliss, delight, joy, euphoria


反义词：despair, misery, sorrow, anguish


词性：名词
