puked:/pʊkt/| v.|呕吐，吐出|To vomit or eject the contents of the stomach through the mouth.|例句：He puked all over the floor after drinking too much. 他喝太多酒后把地板都吐脏了。|例句：The smell made her puke.  那种气味让她想吐。|近义词：vomit, throw up, spew|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pukes every time he rides a rollercoaster. = 他每次坐过山车都会吐。
She pukes when she's sick. = 她生病的时候会呕吐。


一般过去时:
I puked after eating that bad seafood. = 吃了那顿坏的海鲜后我吐了。

过去完成时:
