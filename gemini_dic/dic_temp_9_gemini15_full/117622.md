megascope:/ˈmɛɡəskoʊp/| n. |巨型显微镜；大型投影仪|A megascope is a large-scale instrument used for viewing or projecting enlarged images, either microscopic specimens or other visuals.|例句：The scientist used a megascope to examine the intricate details of the fossil.  科学家用巨型显微镜检查化石的复杂细节。|例句：The teacher used a megascope to project images onto the screen for the entire class to see. 老师用大型投影仪将图像投射到屏幕上，以便全班都能看到。|近义词：microscope (for viewing small objects), projector (for projecting images)|反义词：microscope (in the sense of scale)
