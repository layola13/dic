concludent: /kənˈkluːdənt/ | adj. | 结论性的；决定性的；令人信服的 |  Conclusive; decisive; serving to settle a matter.  It refers to something that brings a matter to a definite end or provides sufficient evidence to support a conclusion. |


The evidence presented was concludent, leaving no room for doubt.  =  提供的证据是确凿的，不容置疑。

The judge's decision was concludent, ending the lengthy legal battle. =  法官的判决是决定性的，结束了漫长的法律诉讼。


近义词：conclusive, decisive, definitive, compelling, persuasive

反义词：inconclusive, indecisive, ambiguous, uncertain, questionable


词性：形容词
