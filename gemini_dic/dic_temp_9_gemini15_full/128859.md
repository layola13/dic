nihilists:/ˈnaɪəɪlɪsts/| n. |虚无主义者|A nihilist is a person who believes that life is meaningless and that there are no objective values or moral principles.  Nihilists often reject traditional beliefs and institutions. |Example:  Many of the characters in the novel are depicted as nihilists, rejecting societal norms and embracing self-destruction.|近义词：cynic, pessimist|反义词：optimist, idealist
