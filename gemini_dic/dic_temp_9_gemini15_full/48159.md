decommercializing: /ˌdiːkəˈmɜːrʃəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 去商业化；使非商业化 | The process of removing commercial influences or aspects from something.  This often involves reducing the emphasis on profit maximization and focusing on other values, such as social benefit or environmental sustainability. | 例句：The government is decommercializing the healthcare system, shifting focus from profit to patient care.  政府正在对医疗系统进行去商业化改革，重点从利润转向病人护理。


例句：The university is actively decommercializing its research, prioritizing open access and public benefit over patents and licensing fees. 大学正在积极地对其研究进行去商业化，优先考虑开放获取和公共利益而不是专利和许可费。


近义词：discommercializing, decarbonizing (in the context of removing commercial influence from environmental issues),  demonetizing (if referring to removing financial incentives)


反义词：commercializing, monetizing


词性:动词
