rambled:/ˈræmbəld/| v.| 闲聊；漫步；语无伦次地说 | To talk or write in a confused or rambling manner; to walk or move in a directionless way. | 例句：He rambled on about his childhood. 他絮絮叨叨地谈论他的童年。

例句：She rambled through the fields, enjoying the sunshine. 她漫步在田野里，享受着阳光。


近义词：wander, stray, meander, babble, mutter | 反义词：speak clearly, proceed directly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rambles on endlessly about his work. = 他没完没了地唠叨他的工作。
She rambles through the woods every morning. = 她每天早上都在树林里漫步。

一般过去时:
They rambled through the countryside for hours. = 他们在乡下漫步了几个小时。
He rambled incoherently after the accident. = 事故后他语无伦次地说着话。

现在完成时:
He has rambled all over Europe. = 他走遍了欧洲各地。
She has rambled on about the same topic for too long. = 她对同一个话题唠叨了太久。

过去完成时:
They had rambled for miles before they found shelter. = 他们走了好几英里才找到住所。
He had rambled so much that he was exhausted. = 他已经闲聊了很久，筋疲力尽了。

将来时:
We will ramble along the coast tomorrow. = 我们明天将在海岸边漫步。
She will ramble on about politics again at dinner. = 她晚饭时又会开始长篇大论地谈论政治。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would ramble through the park. = 如果我有时间，我会在公园里漫步。
I wish I hadn't rambled on so much at the meeting. = 我真希望我在会上没有说得太多。

被动语态: (被动语态用得较少)
The story is rambled and confusing.  = 故事讲得混乱不堪，令人费解。
The speech was rambled and difficult to follow. = 演讲语无伦次，难以理解。


疑问句:
Does he often ramble when he speaks? = 他说话时经常语无伦次吗？
Will they ramble through the forest today? = 他们今天会在森林里漫步吗？

否定句:
He didn't ramble; he spoke clearly and concisely. = 他没有语无伦次；他说话清晰简洁。
She didn't ramble aimlessly; she walked with purpose. = 她不是漫无目的地闲逛；她是有目的地行走。


PHRASE->
ramble on: 絮絮叨叨地说
ramble through: 漫步穿过
ramble about: 漫谈；闲聊
ramble in: 偶然闯入
ramble off: 漫步离开
