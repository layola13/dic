prodigals:/ˈprɒdɪɡəlz/ | n. | 挥霍者；败家子 | People who spend money in an extravagant, irresponsible way.  They are often characterized by their wasteful and reckless spending habits. | The prodigal son returned home after squandering his inheritance.  浪子回头金不换。| spendthrift, wastrel, squanderer | thrifty, frugal, economical | 名词
