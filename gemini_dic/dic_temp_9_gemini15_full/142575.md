osteoclasis:/ˌɒstiəʊˈkleɪsɪs/ | n. | 骨折术；骨骼碎裂 | The surgical breaking of a bone to correct a deformity or to relieve pressure.  This procedure is typically used to correct bone fractures, malunions, or other skeletal deformities. | Example: Osteoclasis was performed to correct the severe bowing of the child's leg.  骨折术被用来矫正孩子腿部的严重弯曲。|近义词：bone fracture,  osteotomy (if a more precise break is needed) |反义词：bone fusion, bone grafting
