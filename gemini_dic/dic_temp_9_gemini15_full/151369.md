parlando:/pɑːrˈlændəʊ/|/pɑːrˈlændoʊ/| adj.|朗诵的；谈话式的；|Spoken in a conversational or recitative style, often implying a relatively fast tempo and less emphasis on precise articulation than in a strictly musical context.  It is often used as a direction in musical scores.|例句：The aria was marked "parlando" indicating a conversational style of singing. 这段咏叹调标注为“朗诵式”，表示一种谈话式的歌唱风格。


例句：The composer instructed the singer to deliver the lines in a parlando style, with a natural flow of speech. 作曲家指示歌手以朗诵风格演唱台词，以自然的语言流畅度演唱。


近义词：recitative, conversational, spoken


反义词：cantabile, legato, sung


词性:形容词
