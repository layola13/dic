emptiers:/ˈɛmptɪərz/| n.|清空者；使…空的人或物|A person or thing that empties something.|The emptiers of the waste bins were late today.  今天垃圾桶的清空者迟到了。|近义词：clearers, evacuators|反义词：fillers


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine empties the tank automatically. = 机器自动清空油箱。
He empties the trash every day. = 他每天都倒垃圾。


一般过去时:
She emptied the bottle of wine. = 她喝光了那瓶酒。
They emptied the swimming pool. = 他们把游泳池的水排空了。


现在完成时:
We have emptied all the boxes. = 我们已经清空了所有的箱子。

过去完成时:
They had emptied the shelves before the store opened. = 商店开门前，他们已经清空了货架。
She had emptied the dishwasher before going to bed. = 她睡前已经清空了洗碗机。


将来时:
I will empty the bucket tomorrow. = 我明天会把桶清空。
