liquids:/ˈlɪkwɪdz/ | n. |液体 |Substances that flow freely but are of constant volume, having a consistency like water or oil.  Liquids take the shape of their container. |Example: Water, oil, and mercury are all liquids.  水，油和汞都是液体。

The rain turned the dry dust into sticky liquids. 雨水使干燥的尘土变成了粘稠的液体。


近义词：fluids, solutions | 反义词：solids, gases


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Water is a liquid at room temperature. 水在室温下是液体。
This bottle contains a viscous liquid. 这个瓶子装有一种粘稠的液体。


一般过去时:
