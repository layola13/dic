cyaniding:/ˈsaɪənaɪdɪŋ/| v.|氰化；用氰化物处理|The process of treating something with cyanide, typically for extraction of gold or other metals, or for pest control.  Cyaniding is a highly toxic process and must be handled with extreme caution.|例句：The miners were using cyaniding to extract gold from the ore. 矿工们正在用氰化法从矿石中提取黄金。|例句：Improper cyaniding can lead to environmental contamination. 不当的氰化处理会导致环境污染。|近义词：poisoning (with cyanide), treating with cyanide|反义词：purifying (removing cyanide)
