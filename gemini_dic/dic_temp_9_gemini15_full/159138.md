pod-casting:/ˈpɑːdkæstɪŋ/|/ˈpɒdkɑːstɪŋ/| n. |播客；播客制作|The act of creating and distributing audio programs (podcasts) via the internet.|例句：Many people enjoy pod-casting as a hobby.  许多人喜欢把播客制作作为一种爱好。|例句：She started pod-casting her cooking show last year. 她去年开始制作她的烹饪节目播客。|近义词：audio broadcasting, online broadcasting|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He podcasts every week. 他每周制作播客。
She podcasts about current events. 她制作关于时事的播客。

一般过去时:
They podcasted a special episode last month. 他们上个月制作了一期特别节目播客。
I podcasted my first episode yesterday. 我昨天制作了我的第一期播客。


现在完成时:
We have podcasted over 100 episodes. 我们已经制作了超过100期的播客。
She has podcasted consistently for three years. 她已经持续制作播客三年了。


过去完成时:
They had podcasted several episodes before launching their website. 他们在上线网站之前已经制作了几期播客。
He had podcasted for years before he became famous. 他成名之前已经制作播客很多年了。


将来时:
I will podcast about my trip next week. 我下周将制作关于我旅行的播客。
They will podcast live from the concert. 他们将在音乐会上现场直播播客。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would podcast more often. 如果我有更多时间，我会更频繁地制作播客。
I wish I could podcast more professionally. 我希望我能更专业地制作播客。


被动语态:
The podcast is podcasted weekly.  这个播客每周都会更新。
Many podcasts are podcasted by independent creators. 许多播客是由独立创作者制作的。


疑问句:
Do you podcast regularly? 你定期制作播客吗？
Does she podcast in English? 她用英语制作播客吗？


否定句:
I don't podcast very often. 我不经常制作播客。
They didn't podcast this week. 他们本周没有制作播客。


PHRASE->
podcast episode: 播客节目

podcast listener: 播客听众
