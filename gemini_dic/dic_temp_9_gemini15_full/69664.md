fences:/ˈfɛnsɪz/| n.|栅栏；篱笆；障碍；防御工事|A fence is a structure, typically made of wood, wire, or metal, that is used to enclose an area of land.|The farmer built a fence around his property to keep the animals in.|近义词：barrier, enclosure, railing|反义词：opening


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer builds fences every year. = 农民每年都修建栅栏。
They build high fences around their houses. = 他们在房子周围建了高高的栅栏。

一般过去时:
We built a fence around the garden last week. = 我们上周在花园周围建了一个栅栏。
He repaired the broken fence yesterday. = 他昨天修理了坏掉的栅栏。

现在完成时:
They have built a new fence to keep the dogs in. = 他们已经建了一个新的栅栏来圈住狗。
She has finally finished mending the fence. = 她终于修好了栅栏。

过去完成时:
They had already built the fence before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经建好了栅栏。
We had repaired the fence several times before it finally collapsed. = 在栅栏最终倒塌之前，我们已经修理过好几次了。

将来时:
We will build a new fence next spring. = 我们明年春天将建造一个新的栅栏。
They will need to replace the old fence soon. = 他们很快就要更换旧栅栏了。

虚拟语气:
If we had a fence, the dogs wouldn't escape. = 如果我们有栅栏，狗就不会逃跑了。
I wish we had built a stronger fence. = 我希望我们当初建一个更结实的栅栏。

被动语态:
The fences were built by the local contractor. = 栅栏是由当地承包商建造的。
The old fence is being repaired this week. = 旧栅栏本周正在维修。

疑问句:
Are those fences made of wood? = 那些栅栏是木头做的吗？
How high are the fences around the stadium? = 体育场周围的栅栏有多高？

否定句:
They didn't build a fence around the pool. = 他们没有在游泳池周围建栅栏。
The fences were not high enough to keep the animals out. = 栅栏不够高，无法挡住动物。
