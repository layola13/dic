neuroses:/njuˈroʊsiːz/| n.|神经症；神经官能症|Plural of neurosis.  A relatively mild mental illness that is not caused by organic disease, involving symptoms of stress (such as anxiety, depression, or obsessive behavior) but not a radical loss of touch with reality.  |例句：His neuroses manifested as chronic anxiety and insomnia. 他的神经症表现为慢性焦虑和失眠。


例句：Many different types of neuroses can affect people of all ages. 许多不同类型的神经症会影响各个年龄段的人。


近义词：mental disorder, psychoneurosis, nervous disorder


反义词：normality, sanity, mental health


词性：名词
