retrocopulation:/ˌrɛtroʊkɒpjʊˈleɪʃən/| n.|反向交配|The act of a female insect copulating with a male after having already copulated with another male, often resulting in the second male's sperm displacing the first male's sperm.|例句：Retrocopulation is a common phenomenon in some insect species.  反向交配在某些昆虫物种中很常见。  |  例句：Studies on retrocopulation have revealed complex mechanisms of sperm competition.  关于反向交配的研究揭示了精子竞争的复杂机制。| 近义词：secondary mating, post-copulatory mating| 反义词：primary copulation, initial mating|名词
