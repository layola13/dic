nannied: /ˈnænɪd/ | v. | 受保姆照顾；当保姆 |  Past tense and past participle of "nanny".  It describes the state of being cared for by a nanny or the act of working as a nanny. |


例句：

The children were well nannied while their parents were at work. = 孩子们在父母上班期间得到了很好的照顾。

She nannied for the wealthy family for five years. = 她为那个富裕的家庭做了五年保姆。


近义词：cared for (as a child), looked after, babysat


反义词：None directly, but antonyms could relate to neglect or unsupervised care.

词性: 动词 (过去式和过去分词)
