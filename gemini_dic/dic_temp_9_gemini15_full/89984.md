horsey:/ˈhɔːrsi/| adj.|马的；像马的；爱马的|Having the characteristics of a horse; resembling a horse; fond of horses.  The word often implies a slightly affected or playful manner.|例句：She has a horsey laugh. 她有一个像马叫一样的笑声。


例句：He has a horsey face. 他有一张马脸。


近义词：equine,  horsy (less formal synonym)


反义词：N/A (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with words suggesting lack of horse-related qualities)


词性:形容词
