hoazin:/ˈhɔːzɪn/| n. |麝雉|A tropical bird of the Amazon and Orinoco basins, having a long neck and crest, and partially prehensile claws. It is notable for its unusual diet of leaves, and for its chicks' possessing functional claws on their wings. |The hoazin is a unique bird found in South America.  麝雉是一种在南美洲发现的独特的鸟类。|近义词：stinkbird (informal)|反义词：无
