词源:
The word "non-stimulant" is a compound word.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of," derived from Old French *non, from Latin nōn. "Stimulant" comes from the Latin verb *stimulāre, meaning "to goad, prick, incite," ultimately from the Latin word *stimulus, meaning "goad, point."  Therefore, "non-stimulant" literally means "not stimulating."
