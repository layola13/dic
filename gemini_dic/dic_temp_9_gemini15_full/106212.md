knot:/nɒt/ /nɑːt/| n. v.|结；节；难题；困境|A knot is a fastening made by tying a piece of string, rope, or something similar around itself or another object.  It can also refer to a lump or swelling, a difficult problem or a group of people. |例句：He tied a knot in the rope. 他在绳子上打了个结。

The sailor tied a strong knot to secure the sail. 水手系了一个牢固的结来固定帆。


近义词：node, tie, tangle, difficulty |反义词：untying, solution
