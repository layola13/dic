prorestoration:/ˌproʊˌrɛstəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |  恢复；复兴；重建 | The act or process of restoring something to its former state or condition;  reestablishment or renewal.  It often implies a return to a previous, often idealized, state. | Example: The prorestoration of the old building involved meticulous craftsmanship.  老建筑的修复工程需要精湛的工艺。 The prorestoration of the monarchy was a significant historical event. 君主制的恢复是一个重要的历史事件。 | 近义词：restoration, rehabilitation, renewal, reconstruction | 反义词：destruction, demolition, ruin
