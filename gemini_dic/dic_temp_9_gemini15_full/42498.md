counterinflationary: /ˌkaʊntərɪnˈfleɪʃənəri/ | adj. | 反通货膨胀的 |  An adjective describing policies, measures, or effects that are designed to combat or reduce inflation.  It refers to actions taken to control or lower the rate of inflation in an economy. |

例句：

The government implemented several counterinflationary measures, including raising interest rates.  政府实施了几项反通货膨胀措施，包括提高利率。

The central bank's counterinflationary policy successfully curbed rising prices. 中央银行的反通货膨胀政策成功抑制了物价上涨。


近义词：anti-inflationary


反义词：inflationary


词性:形容词
