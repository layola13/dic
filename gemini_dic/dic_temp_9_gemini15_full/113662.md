machinists:/məˈʃɪnɪsts/| n.|机械师，技工|A machinist is a skilled worker who operates or repairs machinery, especially in factories. They often use precision tools and have a deep understanding of mechanical principles.|例句：The machinists union went on strike demanding better wages. 机械师工会为了争取更高的工资而罢工了。|例句：Experienced machinists are in high demand in the aerospace industry. 航空航天工业对经验丰富的机械师需求量很大。|近义词：mechanic, technician, engineer|反义词：None specifically.
