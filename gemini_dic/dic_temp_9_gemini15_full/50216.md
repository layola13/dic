departures:/dɪˈpɑːrtʃərz/| n.|启程；出发；离开；(飞机等的)班次；(报纸等的)启事栏|The act of departing; the leaving of a place.  It can also refer to a schedule of departures (like flights or trains) or a section in a newspaper or website listing departures.|例句：The departures board showed that my flight was delayed.  (出发信息板显示我的航班延误了。)  There are many departures from this airport daily. (这个机场每天有很多航班起飞。) |近义词：departures, exits, leavings|反义词：arrivals


USAGE->

Many departures happen from this airport every day. = 每天都有很多航班从这个机场起飞。
