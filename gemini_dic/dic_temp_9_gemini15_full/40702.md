convert:/kənˈvɜːrt/| /kənˈvɜːt/ | vt.  |转换；变换；兑换；皈依|To change something from one form, state, or system to another.  This includes changing the nature, purpose, or function of something.  |例句：He converted his garage into a home office. 他把车库改造成家庭办公室。

例句：She converted her British pounds into US dollars. 她把英镑兑换成美元。


近义词：transform, change, alter, translate, switch

反义词：maintain, preserve, retain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He converts his savings into stocks. 他把积蓄转换成股票。
The machine converts raw materials into finished products. 这台机器把原材料转换成成品。

一般过去时:
They converted the old building into a museum. 他们把旧建筑改造成博物馆。
The company converted to a new accounting system. 公司改用了新的会计系统。

现在完成时:
She has converted all her files to a new format. 她已将所有文件转换成新格式。
He has converted to Christianity. 他已经皈依基督教。

过去完成时:
They had converted the barn into living quarters before they moved in. 他们在搬进去之前已将谷仓改造成住所。
The government had converted the old factory into a school. 政府已将旧工厂改造成学校。

将来时:
We will convert the attic into a playroom. 我们将把阁楼改造成游戏室。
They will convert the energy from the sun into electricity. 他们将把太阳能转换成电力。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would convert my old photos into digital format. 如果我有更多时间，我会把我的旧照片转换成数码格式。
I wish I could convert my negative thoughts into positive ones. 我希望我能把我的消极想法转换成积极的想法。

被动语态:
The old factory was converted into a shopping mall. 旧工厂被改造成购物中心。
The currency will be converted automatically. 货币将自动转换。

疑问句:
Can you convert this document to PDF? 你能把这个文档转换成PDF吗？
How do you convert Celsius to Fahrenheit? 你如何将摄氏度转换为华氏度？

否定句:
He didn't convert his savings into property. 他没有把积蓄转换成房产。
The software doesn't convert that file type. 该软件不能转换那种文件类型。


PHRASE->
convert to: 转换成...
convert into: 改造成...
convert from: 从...转换
data conversion: 数据转换
currency conversion: 货币兑换
