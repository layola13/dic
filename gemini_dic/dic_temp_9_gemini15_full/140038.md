oenologists:/ˌiːnəˈlɒdʒɪsts/ | n. | 酿酒师；葡萄酒专家 | Experts in the study of winemaking, including grape growing, fermentation, and wine aging.  They possess extensive knowledge of viticulture, enology, and the sensory evaluation of wine. |  Many oenologists are employed by wineries to oversee the winemaking process. 许多酿酒厂都雇佣酿酒师来监督酿酒过程。  The oenologist carefully analyzed the soil composition before planting the vines. 在种植葡萄藤之前，酿酒师仔细分析了土壤成分。 | viticulturists, winemakers |  (no direct antonym)
