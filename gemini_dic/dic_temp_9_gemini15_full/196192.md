sequence:/ˈsiːkwəns/ | n. | 序列；顺序；连续 | A sequence is a series of events, actions, or things that follow one after another.  It can also refer to a particular order in which things are arranged or happen. | Example: The sequence of events leading to the war is complex.  战争爆发的事件顺序很复杂。 The DNA sequence determines our genetic makeup. DNA序列决定了我们的基因构成。 |近义词：series, progression, order | 反义词：randomness, disorder
