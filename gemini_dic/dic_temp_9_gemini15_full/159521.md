politesse:/pəˈliːtnəs/| n. |礼貌；客气；优雅 |Politeness is essential in social interactions. 礼貌在社交互动中至关重要。|近义词：courtesy, civility, politeness |反义词：rudeness, discourtesy, incivility |名词


英文解析:Politeness is the quality of being polite, having good manners, and showing consideration for others. It involves being respectful, courteous, and considerate in one's interactions with others.  Politeness can be expressed through words, actions, and gestures.  Different cultures have different standards of politeness.


例句:

Her politeness impressed everyone at the meeting.  她的礼貌给会议上的每个人留下了深刻的印象。

He showed great politeness to the elderly lady. 他对那位老太太表现出极大的礼貌。


词源:

The word "politesse" is borrowed from French. It ultimately derives from the Latin word "politus," meaning "polished," "refined," or "cultivated."  This reflects the idea that politeness involves a refined and cultivated manner.
