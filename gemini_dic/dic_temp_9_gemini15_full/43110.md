coxes:/ˈkɒksɪz/ | n. |舵手；教练；（尤指划艇的）掌舵者 | A coxswain, especially in rowing, steers a boat.  |例句：The cox expertly steered the boat through the rough waters.  舵手熟练地驾驶着小船穿过汹涌的水域。|  例句：Our rowing coach, a former Olympic cox, pushed us hard. 我们的划艇教练，一位前奥运会舵手，对我们要求很严格。|近义词： steersman, coach, trainer | 反义词：crew member (in the context of rowing)
