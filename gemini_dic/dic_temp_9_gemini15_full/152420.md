paw-paw: /ˈpɔːpɔː/ | n. |木瓜；美洲山茱萸；(非正式)手；巴掌|

英文解析:Pawpaw can refer to several things. Most commonly, it refers to the fruit of the Asimina triloba tree, a type of tropical fruit known for its custard-like texture.  It can also refer to the tree itself, and less commonly, to other plants in the genus Asimina. In informal use, “paw-paw” is a childish or affectionate term for a hand or paw.

例句:

The pawpaw is a delicious tropical fruit.  = 木瓜是一种美味的热带水果。

I bought some pawpaws at the farmer's market. = 我在农贸市场买了些木瓜。


近义词: (for the fruit) Custard apple (in some contexts),  (for the hand) hand, paw
