downswings:/ˈdaʊnˌswɪŋz/| n.|下降趋势；低迷时期；低谷|A downswing refers to a period of decline or decrease, often in economic activity, but can also apply to other areas like performance or mood.  It implies a gradual or progressive downward movement.|例句：The economy is experiencing a significant downswing.  经济正在经历一个显著的低迷时期。


The company's sales figures show a sharp downswing this quarter. 这家公司的销售数据显示本季度急剧下降。|近义词：decline, downturn, slump, dip, recession|反义词：upswing, upturn, boom, recovery, surge|
