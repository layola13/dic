chorale:/kəˈrɑːl/| n.|合唱团；合唱曲；圣咏|A chorale is a hymn tune, typically one sung by a choir in a church.  It can also refer to the choir itself.  The term often implies a simple, stately melody.|例句：The church choir sang a beautiful chorale. 教堂合唱团唱了一首优美的圣咏。
The composer wrote a new chorale for the Easter service.  作曲家为复活节仪式创作了一首新的圣咏。|近义词：anthem, hymn, choral, motet|反义词：solo, duet
