decompresser:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛsər/ | n. | 解压缩器 | A device or program that decompresses data, typically files or streams that have been compressed using an algorithm such as ZIP, RAR, or gzip.  It reverses the process of compression, restoring the original data. | Example:  The decompressor quickly restored the archive to its original size.  解压缩器迅速将存档恢复到其原始大小。  The software package includes a powerful decompressor for various compressed file formats.  该软件包包含一个功能强大的解压缩器，用于各种压缩文件格式。 |近义词：unpacker, unzipper | 反义词：compressor
