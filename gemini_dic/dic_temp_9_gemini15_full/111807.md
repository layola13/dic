logarithm:/ˈlɒɡərɪðəm/ /ˈlɑːɡərɪðəm/| n. |对数|The logarithm of a number is the exponent to which another fixed value, the base, must be raised to produce that number.  For example, the base 10 logarithm of 100 is 2, because 10 raised to the power of 2 is 100.|The pH of a solution is calculated using the negative logarithm of the hydrogen ion concentration.  溶液的pH值是利用氢离子浓度的负对数计算的。|近义词：antilogarithm (反函数)|反义词：N/A|名词
