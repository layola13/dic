rootedness:/ˈruːtɪdnəs/| n. |根深蒂固；扎根；归属感 |The state or quality of being rooted; the feeling of being firmly established or connected to a place, community, or identity.  It implies a sense of belonging and stability.|例句：Her deep rootedness in her community was evident in her tireless volunteer work.  她的社区归属感深厚，体现在她不辞辛劳的志愿工作中。

例句：The tree's rootedness ensured its survival through the storm. 这棵树扎根很深，确保它在暴风雨中幸存下来。


近义词：belonging, grounding, stability, attachment, connection |反义词：rootlessness, instability, alienation, detachment
