cyanate:/ˈsaɪəneɪt/| n.|氰酸盐|A salt or ester of cyanic acid (HOCN). Cyanates are compounds containing the anion OCN−. They are used in various applications, including the production of plastics, pesticides, and herbicides.|例句：Potassium cyanate is used in some fertilizers. 氰酸钾被用在一些肥料中。
The cyanate ion is a linear molecule. 氰酸根离子是一个线性分子。|近义词：isocyanate (异氰酸酯，结构异构体)|反义词：无
