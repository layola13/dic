regroom:/riːˈɡruːm/| vt.|重新整理，重新打理|To groom again; to groom thoroughly.|例句：He regroomed his horse after the race. 他在赛马后重新整理了他的马匹。|例句：She regroomed her hair before the important meeting. 她在重要会议前重新整理了她的头发。|近义词：restyle, groom, tidy |反义词：mess up, dishevel
