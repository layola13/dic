endeavour:/ɪnˈdevər/ | n., v. |努力；尽力；尝试 |  A serious attempt or effort; to try hard to do or achieve something. |例句：She endeavoured to remain calm despite the chaos. 她尽力保持冷静，尽管周围一片混乱。|近义词：attempt, strive, struggle, try |反义词：neglect, ignore

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He endeavours to improve his skills daily. = 他每天努力提高自己的技能。
They endeavour to meet the deadline. = 他们努力赶上截止日期。

一般过去时:
She endeavoured to help the victims. = 她努力帮助受害者。
We endeavoured to understand the complex issue. = 我们努力理解这个复杂的问题。

现在完成时:
He has endeavoured to solve the problem for weeks. = 他已经努力解决这个问题好几个星期了。
They have endeavoured to reach a compromise. = 他们一直努力达成妥协。

过去完成时:
She had endeavoured to warn them, but it was too late. = 她曾努力警告他们，但为时已晚。
We had endeavoured to finish the project on time, but we failed. = 我们曾努力按时完成项目，但失败了。

将来时:
He will endeavour to finish the work by tomorrow. = 他将努力在明天之前完成工作。
They will endeavour to contact you as soon as possible. = 他们会尽快与您联系。

虚拟语气:
I would endeavour to help if I could. = 如果我能的话，我会尽力帮忙。
If I had endeavoured harder, I might have succeeded. = 如果我更努力的话，也许会成功。

被动语态:
Every effort was endeavoured to be made to rescue the passengers. = 已经尽一切努力去营救乘客。
A solution was endeavoured to be found. = 努力寻找解决方案。


疑问句:
Will you endeavour to complete the task? = 你会努力完成任务吗？
Did they endeavour to understand your perspective? = 他们努力理解你的观点了吗？

否定句:
He did not endeavour to hide his feelings. = 他并没有努力掩饰自己的感情。
They didn't endeavour to contact us. = 他们没有努力联系我们。


PHRASE->
