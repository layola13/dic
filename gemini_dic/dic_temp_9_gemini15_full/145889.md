overdevelopment:/ˌoʊvərdiˈvɛləpmənt/ | n. |过度发展；开发过度 | The excessive development of an area, often leading to negative consequences such as environmental damage, resource depletion, and social disruption.  It refers to a situation where development surpasses the carrying capacity of an area, leading to unsustainable practices. | Example: The overdevelopment of coastal areas has led to habitat loss and increased erosion.  过度开发沿海地区导致栖息地丧失和侵蚀加剧。|近义词：overbuilding, excessive development, unsustainable development | 反义词：underdevelopment, preservation, conservation | 名词
