disallow:/ˌdɪsəˈlaʊ/| vt.|不允许，禁止|To refuse to allow or permit (something); to prohibit.|The rules disallow the use of calculators during the exam. 考试规则不允许使用计算器。|The referee disallowed the goal because of a foul. 裁判因为犯规而取消了这个进球。|permit, allow, approve|allow, permit, authorize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school disallows the use of cell phones. = 学校不允许使用手机。
They disallow smoking in public places. = 他们禁止在公共场所吸烟。


一般过去时:
The judge disallowed the evidence. = 法官驳回了证据。
The teacher disallowed the student's late submission. = 老师不允许学生迟交作业。


现在完成时:
The committee has disallowed the proposal. = 委员会否决了该提案。
They have disallowed any further appeals. = 他们不允许任何进一步的申诉。


过去完成时:
The court had disallowed the previous ruling. = 法院已经否决了先前的裁决。
They had disallowed the entry of unauthorized personnel. = 他们此前已禁止未经授权的人员进入。


将来时:
The government will disallow the import of certain goods. = 政府将禁止进口某些商品。
They will disallow any changes to the contract. = 他们将不允许对合同进行任何更改。


虚拟语气:
If they disallowed the project, we would have to find an alternative. = 如果他们否决了这个项目，我们就必须寻找替代方案。
I wish they disallowed the use of plastic bags. = 我希望他们禁止使用塑料袋。


被动语态:
The application was disallowed due to incomplete information. = 由于信息不完整，申请被拒绝了。
The request was disallowed by the authorities. = 该请求被当局拒绝了。


疑问句:
Does the regulation disallow parking here? = 规定不允许在这里停车吗？
Will they disallow our request for funding? = 他们会否决我们的资金申请吗？


否定句:
The rules do not disallow the use of dictionaries. = 规则并没有禁止使用词典。
They did not disallow his participation in the competition. = 他们没有禁止他参加比赛。
