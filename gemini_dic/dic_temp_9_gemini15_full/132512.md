nondetentions: /ˌnɒnˌdɪˈtenʃənz/ | n. |非拘留；未被拘留 |The absence of detention; the state of not being detained.  This word refers to instances where individuals are not held in custody or confinement.  It's often used in legal or official contexts.|


例句：

The police reported a significant increase in nondetentions this year. = 警方报告今年未被拘留的人数显著增加。

The court's decision resulted in the nondetentions of several suspects. = 法院的判决导致几名嫌疑人未被拘留。


近义词：releases, freedoms, acquittals


反义词：detentions, arrests, imprisonments


词性: 名词
