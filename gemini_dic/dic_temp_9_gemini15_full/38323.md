concedes:/kənˈsiːdz/ | vt. |承认；让步；给予；容许 | To admit that something is true or valid after first denying or resisting it; to grant or yield something to someone. |例句：He conceded that he had made a mistake. 他承认自己犯了一个错误。

例句：The government conceded defeat in the election. 政府承认在选举中失败了。


近义词：admit, acknowledge, grant, yield | 反义词：deny, refute, contest, resist | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He concedes defeat gracefully. 他优雅地承认失败。
She concedes the point to her opponent. 她向她的对手让步。

一般过去时:
The company conceded to the workers' demands. 公司答应了工人的要求。
He conceded the election to his opponent. 他向对手承认竞选失败。

现在完成时:
They have conceded that their plan was flawed. 他们已经承认他们的计划有缺陷。
She has conceded many points during the debate. 在辩论中她已经让步很多。

过去完成时:
He had conceded before the evidence was presented. 在证据提交之前，他已经认输了。
They had conceded the argument before realizing their error. 在意识到他们的错误之前，他们已经放弃了争论。


将来时:
We will concede nothing to our adversaries. 我们不会向我们的对手让步。
He will concede the race if he falls behind. 如果他落后，他会放弃比赛。


虚拟语气:
If he conceded the truth, he would feel better. 如果他承认真相，他会感觉更好。
I wish he would concede the point. 我希望他会让步。

被动语态:
The victory was conceded to the opposing team. 胜利让给了对方球队。
The point was conceded by the speaker. 发言人让步了这一点。

疑问句:
Will he concede the election? 他会承认选举失败吗？
Does she concede the argument? 她承认这个论点吗？


否定句:
He did not concede defeat easily. 他并没有轻易认输。
She would not concede to pressure. 她不会屈服于压力。


PHRASE->
concede defeat: 承认失败
concede a point: 承认一点
concede ground: 让步
concede victory: 承认胜利
concede to pressure: 屈服于压力


词源:
Comes from the Latin word "concedere," meaning "to yield, grant, or give up."  The prefix "con-" means "together," and the root "-cedere" means "to go" or "to yield."  Therefore, the word literally means "to go together" or "to yield together."
