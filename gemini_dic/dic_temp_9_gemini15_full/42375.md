counterbalancer:/ˈkaʊntərˌbælənsər/ | n. |平衡器，制衡物 | A counterbalancer is a device or mechanism that provides counterbalance or equilibrium.  It is used to offset or counteract an opposing force or weight.  Examples include weights in a machine to balance movement, or a political force that opposes another to prevent dominance. |例句：The engineer designed a counterbalancer to stabilize the robotic arm. 工程师设计了一个平衡器来稳定机械臂。

The company's new marketing campaign acts as a counterbalancer to the negative press it received last year. 公司新的市场营销活动起到平衡去年负面新闻的作用。

近义词：counterweight, stabilizer, equilibrium, check

反义词：unbalancer, destabilizer

词性:名词
