innkeeper:/ˈɪnˌkiːpər/ | n. | 旅馆老板；客栈老板 | The innkeeper runs a small hotel in the countryside.  |  hotel owner, landlord |  tenant, guest | 名词


英文解析:A person who owns or manages an inn (a small hotel, especially in the past).  An innkeeper is responsible for the day-to-day operations of the inn, including managing staff, attending to guests' needs, and ensuring the smooth running of the business.


例句:

The innkeeper greeted us warmly and showed us to our room. 旅馆老板热情地迎接我们，并带我们去了房间。

We asked the innkeeper for directions to the nearest town. 我们向旅馆老板询问去最近城镇的路。


近义词:

hotel owner, landlord, publican (if the inn also serves alcohol)


反义词:

guest, tenant (these are the opposite roles)


词性:

名词 (noun)
