maidenhair:/ˈmeɪdnheər/| n.|少女发；铁线蕨|A maidenhair is a type of delicate fern with dark, slender stems and fan-shaped fronds.  The name refers to the fine, hair-like appearance of the stems.|The maidenhair fern thrives in shady, moist environments.  铁线蕨生长在阴暗潮湿的环境中。|fern, adiantum|coarse, thick|noun
