hypocrisies:/hɪˈpɒkrəsiːz/| n.|伪善；虚伪行为|The plural form of hypocrisy, referring to multiple instances of insincere actions or statements that contradict one's stated beliefs or feelings.  It highlights the discrepancy between what someone says and what they do.|例句：His public pronouncements of piety were exposed as mere hypocrisies when his secret life was revealed.  他的公开虔诚声明在他秘密生活被揭露后被证明是虚伪的行为。|近义词：deceptions, duplicities, sham, pretense|反义词：honesty, sincerity, genuineness|名词


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It only takes plural forms.  Therefore, tense, voice, and mood are not applicable.


PHRASE->
act of hypocrisy: 伪善的行为
exposing hypocrisies: 揭露虚伪
political hypocrisies: 政治上的虚伪
social hypocrisies: 社会上的虚伪
moral hypocrisies: 道德上的虚伪
