sesspool:/ˈsɛsˌpuːl/| n.|化粪池；污水坑；污秽之地|A cesspool is an underground container for the storage of sewage and waste.  It is often used figuratively to describe a place or situation that is morally corrupt or unpleasant.|例句：The old building was condemned because its cesspool was overflowing.  这座旧建筑因为化粪池溢出而被责令拆除。


The town's reputation as a cesspool of corruption was hard to shake. 这个城镇充斥腐败的恶名很难消除。|近义词：sewer, septic tank, pit, mire|反义词：cleanliness, purity
