gastromyth:/ˈɡæstrəˌmɪθ/| n.| 胃话，关于胃的虚构故事，(尤指)关于消化系统疾病的民间传说|A gastromyth is a false belief or story, often related to the digestive system, that is commonly believed but lacks scientific basis.  These are often passed down through generations and can be related to food, diet, or digestive health.|Example:  The belief that eating ice cream causes a sore throat is a common gastromyth.|近义词：superstition, myth, fallacy|反义词：fact, truth
