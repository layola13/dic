circumnavigated:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡeɪtɪd/ | v. |环绕航行；绕…航行 | To sail or travel all the way around (something, especially a large body of water).  | Magellan was the first to circumnavigate the globe. 麦哲伦是第一个环球航行的人。|  navigate, travel around |  stay put.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They circumnavigate the globe every year. = 他们每年环绕地球航行。
We circumnavigate the island by boat. = 我们乘船绕岛航行。


一般过去时:
He circumnavigated the lake in his kayak. = 他用皮划艇绕湖航行。
The explorers circumnavigated the continent. = 探险家们环绕了这块大陆航行。


现在完成时:
She has circumnavigated the world twice. = 她已经环游世界两次了。
They have circumnavigated several islands in the Pacific. = 他们已经环绕了太平洋的几个岛屿。


过去完成时:
Before the expedition, they had circumnavigated smaller bodies of water. = 在远征之前，他们已经环绕过较小的水域。
He had circumnavigated the Mediterranean Sea before attempting the Atlantic. = 在尝试横渡大西洋之前，他已经环绕了地中海。


将来时:
We will circumnavigate the coast next summer. = 我们明年夏天将沿着海岸航行。
They will circumnavigate the planet in their newly built sailboat. = 他们将用他们新造的帆船环绕地球航行。


虚拟语气:
If we had a bigger boat, we would circumnavigate the world. = 如果我们有一艘更大的船，我们就会环游世界。
I wish I could circumnavigate the globe someday. = 我希望有一天我能环游世界。


被动语态:
The island was circumnavigated by the research team. = 这个岛屿被研究小组环绕航行了。
The globe has been circumnavigated many times. = 地球已经被环绕航行了很多次。


疑问句:
Has anyone ever circumnavigated the Arctic Ocean solo? = 有人曾经独自环绕北冰洋航行吗？
Will they circumnavigate the southern hemisphere? = 他们会环绕南半球航行吗？


否定句:
They did not circumnavigate the entire continent. = 他们没有环绕整个大陆航行。
We haven't yet circumnavigated the Pacific Ocean. = 我们还没有环绕太平洋航行。


PHRASE->
circumnavigate the globe = 环游世界
circumnavigate the earth = 环绕地球
circumnavigate a problem = 绕过问题
circumnavigate difficulties = 避开困难
circumnavigate the issue = 回避问题


词源:
From Latin *circum- ("around") + navigare ("to sail").  The word combines the prefix "circum-" meaning "around" or "about" with the verb "navigate," meaning "to sail" or "to steer a course."
