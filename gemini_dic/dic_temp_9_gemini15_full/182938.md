rephrasing:/ˌriːˈfreɪzɪŋ/| n. |改述；释义；重新表达|The act of expressing something again or differently; stating something in another way.  It often involves clarifying, simplifying, or improving the original wording. |例句：The teacher suggested a rephrasing of the student's essay to make it clearer.老师建议学生修改作文使其更清晰。|例句：The lawyer's rephrasing of the question avoided a potentially damaging answer. 律师对问题的重新表达避免了一个可能造成损害的答案。|近义词：paraphrasing, restating, reformulating|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be leaving something unchanged or unclear. |名词
