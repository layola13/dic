ravishment:/ˈrævɪʃmənt/| n.|狂喜；强奸；掠夺|The intense pleasure and delight;  the act of raping someone; the violent seizure or plunder of something.|例句：The ravishment of the princess by the dragon was a pivotal moment in the story. 公主被龙抢走是故事中的关键时刻。

例句：The ravishment of the city by the invading army resulted in widespread destruction. 侵略军对城市的掠夺导致了大范围的破坏。


近义词：ecstasy, rapture, violation, plunder, seizure|反义词：disappointment, misery, restoration


词源:源自古法语 ravissement，意为“抢夺，夺取”，最终源于拉丁语 rapere，意为“抢夺，掠夺”。
