processes:/ˈprɑːsɛsɪz/| n.|过程；进程；处理；程序|The word "processes" is the plural form of "process," referring to a series of actions or steps taken in order to achieve a particular end.  It can refer to a natural phenomenon, a manufacturing procedure, a mental activity, or a series of computer operations.|例句：The manufacturing processes are highly automated. 制造过程高度自动化。

The processes of learning and growing are lifelong journeys.学习和成长的过程是终生的旅程。|近义词：procedures, operations, methods, systems|反义词：results, outcomes, conclusions
