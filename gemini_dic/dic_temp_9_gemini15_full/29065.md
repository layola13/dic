USAGE->

一般现在时:
He carves wood every day. = 他每天都雕刻木头。
She carves the turkey for Thanksgiving. = 她为感恩节雕刻火鸡。


一般过去时:
They carved pumpkins for Halloween. = 他们为万圣节雕刻南瓜。
The artist carved a stunning piece of driftwood. = 艺术家雕刻了一件令人惊叹的漂流木作品。


现在完成时:
He has carved many beautiful sculptures. = 他已经雕刻了许多美丽的雕塑。

She had carved the pumpkin before the children arrived. = 孩子们到达之前，她已经雕刻好了南瓜。

They will carve the cake after the wedding ceremony. = 婚礼仪式结束后，他们将切蛋糕。


虚拟语气:
