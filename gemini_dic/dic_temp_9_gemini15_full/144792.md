over-flooding:/ˌoʊvərˈflʌdɪŋ/ | n. | 泛滥，水浸 | The excessive flow of water, beyond normal limits, resulting in inundation.  This can refer to rivers, lakes, or even indoor spaces.  | Example: The over-flooding of the river caused widespread damage to homes and businesses.  河水的泛滥给房屋和企业造成了广泛的破坏。 |近义词: inundation, deluge, overflow | 反义词: drought, dryness
