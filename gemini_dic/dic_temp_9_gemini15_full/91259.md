hydrofluosilicic: /ˌhaɪdroʊˌfluːoʊsɪˈlɪsɪk/ | adj. | 氟硅酸的 |  Relating to or denoting hydrofluosilicic acid or its salts.  Hydrofluosilicic acid is a strong acid formed by the reaction of silicon tetrafluoride with water. Its salts are used in various industrial applications, including water fluoridation. |  The hydrofluosilicic acid solution was carefully added to the water. 氟硅酸溶液小心地加入水中。 The analysis revealed the presence of hydrofluosilicic salts in the sample. 分析显示样品中存在氟硅酸盐。 |  fluorosilicic |  (no direct antonym, but  "non-fluorosilicic" could be used in a comparative sense) | adj.
