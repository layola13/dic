mulishly:/ˈmjuːliʃli/ | adv. | 倔强地；顽固地；固执地 |  Stubbornly and unyieldingly; in a mulish manner.  It implies a stubborn refusal to change one's mind or comply with requests, often in a somewhat obstinate or unreasonable way. |例句：He mulishly refused to admit he was wrong. 他倔强地拒绝承认自己错了。

He mulishly stuck to his original plan despite the obvious flaws.  他固执地坚持他的最初计划，尽管存在明显的缺陷。


近义词：obstinately, stubbornly, doggedly, perversely, inflexibly

反义词：yieldingly, compliantly, readily, willingly


词性：副词
