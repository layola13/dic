diswitted:/dɪsˈwɪtɪd/| adj.|愚笨的；缺乏智力的；头脑迟钝的|Lacking wit or intelligence; foolish; silly.  The diswitted character in the play often made foolish decisions.  这部戏剧中愚笨的角色经常做出愚蠢的决定。|近义词：stupid, foolish, unintelligent, witless|反义词：clever, intelligent, bright, witty|

词源:  The word "diswitted" is a combination of the prefix "dis-" (meaning "lacking" or "without") and "witted" (meaning "having wit," intelligence, or quick understanding).  "Wit" itself has Old English roots,  ultimately deriving from words meaning "to know" or "to think."
