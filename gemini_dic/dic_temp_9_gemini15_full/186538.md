ridded:/ˈrɪdɪd/| v.|摆脱，除去，清除|Past tense of "rid".  It means to have freed or cleared something from something else, often something unwanted or harmful.|例句：He ridded the house of mice. 他把老鼠从房子里清除掉了。
例句：She finally ridded herself of that bad habit. 她最终摆脱了那个坏习惯。|近义词：cleared, freed, purged, eliminated|反义词：burdened, afflicted, infested

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I rid my garden of weeds every week. = 我每周都清除花园里的杂草。
We rid our home of pests regularly. = 我们定期清除家里的害虫。


一般过去时:
He ridded his desk of clutter. = 他清理了他桌上的杂物。
They ridded the ship of unwanted cargo. = 他们清除了船上不需要的货物。


现在完成时:
She has ridded herself of her anxiety. = 她已经摆脱了焦虑。
We have ridded the community of that dangerous gang. = 我们已经清除了社区中那个危险的帮派。


过去完成时:
He had ridded the area of snakes before the picnic. = 野餐前他已经清除了该地区的蛇。
They had ridded the town of the disease before it spread. = 在疾病蔓延之前，他们已经清除掉了城镇的疾病。


将来时:
She will rid her life of negativity. = 她将摆脱消极情绪。
We will rid the country of poverty. = 我们将使国家摆脱贫困。


虚拟语气:
If I could rid myself of this debt, I would be happy. = 如果我能摆脱这笔债务，我会很高兴。
I wish I could rid my mind of these worries. = 我希望我能摆脱这些担忧。


被动语态:
The house was ridded of insects. = 房子里的昆虫被清除了。
The town was ridded of the pollution. = 城镇的污染被清除了。


疑问句:
How did you rid yourself of that problem? = 你是怎么解决那个问题的？
Have you ridded your garden of weeds yet? = 你已经把花园里的杂草清除掉了吗？


否定句:
He couldn't rid himself of the feeling of guilt. = 他无法摆脱内疚感。
They haven't yet ridded the area of all the debris. = 他们还没有清除该地区的所有碎片。
