damme:/ˈdæmi/| int.|该死，妈的|A mild oath expressing anger, frustration, or annoyance.  It's a shortened and slightly less offensive version of "damn me."|例句：Damme, I forgot my keys! 该死，我忘了带钥匙！
例句：Damme if I know what happened.  我他妈的不知道发生了什么。|近义词：damn, blast, heck|反义词:None.


USAGE->
This word is an interjection and doesn't follow standard verb conjugation.  It's used to express emotion, not describe an action. Therefore, grammatical tenses are not applicable.


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases directly using "damme" as it's primarily used as a standalone exclamation.
