frosty:/ˈfrɔːsti/| adj.|寒冷的；严寒的；冷淡的；结霜的|Covered with or characterized by frost; extremely cold.  Also, reserved or unfriendly in manner.|例句：The morning air was frosty.  早晨的空气很寒冷。

The atmosphere in the room was frosty after their argument. 他们争吵后，房间里的气氛很冷淡。

近义词：icy, frigid, cold, unfriendly |反义词：warm, friendly, genial, balmy


词源:
源自古英语 *frōst,  意为“霜”。  "-y" 是一个形容词后缀，表示“具有…性质的”。


USAGE->

The window is frosty this morning. = 今早窗户上结霜了。
The air feels frosty tonight. = 今晚空气感觉很冷。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
