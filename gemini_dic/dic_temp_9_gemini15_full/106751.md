例句：He's become terribly la-di-da since he inherited all that money.自从他继承了所有那些钱后，他变得非常势利眼。


近义词：affected, pretentious, snobbish, posh, supercilious|反义词：down-to-earth, unpretentious, humble


词源:The origin of "la-di-da" is uncertain, but it's likely an onomatopoeic expression mimicking a refined, affected way of speaking.  The phrase might have originated in the late 19th or early 20th century.  Some sources suggest it might be related to earlier expressions of similar meaning, but no definitive etymology has been established.  Its playful, somewhat mocking tone contributes to its continued use.
