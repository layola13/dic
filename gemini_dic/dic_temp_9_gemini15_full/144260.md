outsoared:/ˌaʊtˈsɔːrd/ | vt. |超越，胜过 |To surpass in height or achievement.  |例句：The skyscraper outsoared all the other buildings in the city. 这座摩天大楼超过了城市里所有其他建筑物的高度。|例句：Her success in business outsoared her wildest dreams. 她在商业上的成功超出了她最狂野的梦想。|近义词：surpass, exceed, transcend, outstrip |反义词：underperform, lag behind


USAGE->

The company outsoars its competitors in innovation. = 这家公司在创新方面胜过其竞争对手。

一般过去时:
The stock price outsoared expectations last quarter. = 上个季度股价超出预期。
