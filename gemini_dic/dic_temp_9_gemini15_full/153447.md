pennies:/ˈpɛniz/| n.|便士（penny的复数）|The plural of penny, referring to small amounts of money.  It can also refer to small insignificant things.|例句：I found a few pennies in my pocket. 我在我的口袋里发现了一些便士。

例句：She spent her last few pennies on a bus ticket. 她把最后几个便士花在了公交车票上。


近义词：cents (美式英语)


反义词：无


词性:名词


USAGE->
