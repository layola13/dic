euthanatizing:/ˌjuːθəˈneɪzɪŋ/| v.|安乐死；结束…的生命|The act of killing someone painlessly, especially to end suffering.|例句：The veterinarian euthanatized the suffering animal. 兽医对这只受苦的动物进行了安乐死。|例句：The family considered euthanatizing their terminally ill pet.  这个家庭考虑对他们患有绝症的宠物实施安乐死。|近义词：putting down, mercy killing|反义词：saving, preserving life|动词
