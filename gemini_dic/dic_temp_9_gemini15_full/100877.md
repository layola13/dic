intrusion:/ɪnˈtruːʒən/| n. |入侵；侵扰；干扰 |The unwelcome entry of a person or thing into a place, situation, etc.|例句：The intrusion of the media into her private life was deplorable.媒体对她私生活的侵犯是令人谴责的。


例句：His constant phone calls were a major intrusion on my time. 他不断的电话骚扰严重影响了我的时间。


近义词：invasion, encroachment, interference |反义词：welcome, acceptance


词源：源自于古法语 *intrusion，来自拉丁语 *intrudere，“推入”，由 *in-，“进入” + *trudere，“推”构成。
