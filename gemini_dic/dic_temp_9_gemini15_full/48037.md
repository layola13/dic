deck:/dek/| n. v.|甲板；桥面；一副牌；（建筑物的）层；装饰；打扮；|A deck is the upper outside surface of a ship or boat.  It can also refer to a pack of playing cards, a floor or level in a building, or the act of decorating or dressing something. |例句：The ship's deck was crowded with passengers. 这艘船的甲板上挤满了乘客。
The magician shuffled the deck of cards. 魔术师洗了一副牌。

例句：He decked the room with flowers. 他用鲜花装饰房间。


近义词：platform, floor, terrace, pack (for cards), adorn, decorate |反义词：basement (for floor), undress (for decorate)


词性:名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ship's deck is very spacious. = 这艘船的甲板非常宽敞。

They played cards on the deck. = 他们在甲板上玩牌。

现在完成时:
We have decked the house for the party. = 我们已经为派对装饰了房子。
He has dealt the deck many times. = 他已经发牌很多次了。

过去完成时:

The captain had already inspected the deck. = 船长已经检查过甲板了。
