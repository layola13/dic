raring:/ˈrɛərɪŋ/| adj.|热切渴望的，急切的，兴致勃勃的|Eager or enthusiastic; full of energy and excitement.|例句：He was raring to go on the trip. 他急切地想去旅行。  例句：The children were raring to get started on their project. 孩子们迫不及待地想开始他们的项目。|近义词：eager, enthusiastic, keen, excited|反义词：reluctant, unenthusiastic, apathetic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is raring to start his new job. = 他渴望开始他的新工作。
They are raring to get to the party. = 他们迫不及待地想去参加派对。

一般过去时:
She was raring to tell everyone the good news. = 她渴望告诉每个人这个好消息。
We were raring to get out of the house after being cooped up all day. = 我们被关在家里一整天后，渴望出去。

现在完成时:
They have been raring to go since they heard about the trip. = 从听到旅行的消息以来，他们就一直渴望出发。
I have been raring to try that new restaurant for weeks. = 我几周来一直渴望尝试那家新餐厅。


过去完成时:
He had been raring to see his family after such a long time apart. = 分离这么久后，他渴望见到他的家人。
She had been raring to finish the project before the deadline. = 她渴望在截止日期前完成项目。

将来时:
They will be raring to go once they finish packing. = 一旦他们完成打包，他们就会迫不及待地出发。
I will be raring to relax after the exam. = 考试结束后，我会渴望放松一下。

虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would be raring to learn a new language. = 如果我有时间，我会渴望学习一门外语。
I wish I were raring to go to work tomorrow, but I'm feeling quite unwell. = 我希望明天我渴望去上班，但我感觉不太舒服。

被动语态: (This adjective is rarely used in the passive voice.)  The passive voice doesn't fit the meaning of "raring".


疑问句:
Are you raring to start the game? = 你渴望开始比赛吗？
Is she raring for a challenge? = 她渴望挑战吗？

否定句:
He wasn't raring to go to the dentist. = 他并不渴望去看牙医。
They weren't raring to work overtime. = 他们并不渴望加班。
