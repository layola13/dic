ostensibly:/ɒˈstɛnsəbli/ | adj. |表面上地；似乎；假装地 | Apparently or seemingly, but perhaps not actually.  The adjective implies a degree of deception or concealment. |例句：Ostensibly, the meeting was about budget cuts, but many suspected a hidden agenda.  表面上，会议是关于预算削减的，但许多人怀疑另有隐情。

例句：The company ostensibly supports environmental protection, but its actions suggest otherwise.  这家公司表面上支持环境保护，但其行动表明并非如此。


近义词：apparently, seemingly, superficially, outwardly, nominally


反义词：actually, truly, really, genuinely


词性：形容词
