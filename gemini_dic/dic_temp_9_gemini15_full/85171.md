hare-brained:/ˈhɛrˌbreɪnd/| adj.|头脑简单的，愚蠢的，异想天开的|Foolish; impractical; impulsive and ill-considered.|例句：That hare-brained scheme is doomed to failure.  那个异想天开的计划注定要失败。

例句：He came up with a hare-brained idea to solve the problem. 他想出了一个愚蠢的主意来解决这个问题。


近义词：impractical, foolish, silly, absurd, crazy, reckless


反义词：sensible, practical, wise, prudent, cautious


词性：形容词

词源:
The term "hare-brained" combines "hare," referring to the perceived flighty and unpredictable nature of hares, and "brained," implying a lack of intelligence or sound judgment.  The association likely arose from observing the erratic movements of hares.
