cluttering:/ˈklʌtərɪŋ/| v.|杂乱，弄乱 |The act of making a place or thing untidy.  It refers to the process of filling a space with too many things, making it disordered and difficult to navigate. |例句：He spent the afternoon decluttering his garage. 他花了整个下午整理车库。

例句：The overflowing drawers were cluttering the bedroom.  塞满的抽屉使卧室杂乱不堪。

近义词：messing, jumbling, littering, overcrowding

反义词：organizing, tidying, clearing, streamlining


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is cluttering the room with toys. = 他正用玩具把房间弄乱。
They clutter the kitchen with dirty dishes. = 他们把脏盘子弄得厨房乱糟糟的。

一般过去时:
She cluttered the desk with papers. = 她把桌子弄得乱七八糟的。
We cluttered the living room with furniture. = 我们把客厅塞满了家具，显得乱糟糟的。


现在完成时:
They have cluttered the attic with old boxes. = 他们已经把阁楼塞满了旧箱子。
He has cluttered his mind with useless information. = 他已经把自己的头脑塞满了无用的信息。


过去完成时:
She had cluttered the shelves with knick-knacks before the guests arrived. = 客人来之前，她已经把架子塞满了小摆设。
They had cluttered the garden with discarded tools. = 他们已经把花园里塞满了废弃的工具。


将来时:
They will clutter the hallway with their coats. = 他们将会把他们的外套堆放在走廊里，弄得乱糟糟的。
He will clutter the table with books. = 他将会把桌子堆满书。


虚拟语气:
If he didn't clutter his workspace, he would be more productive. = 如果他不把他的工作空间弄乱，他会更高效。
I wish I hadn't cluttered my suitcase with so many unnecessary items. = 我真希望我没有把我的手提箱塞满那么多不必要的东西。


被动语态:
The room was cluttered with old furniture. = 房间里堆满了旧家具，显得杂乱无章。
The table is cluttered with paperwork. = 桌子上堆满了文件，乱七八糟的。


疑问句:
Is the garage cluttered with junk? = 车库里堆满了垃圾吗？
Are they cluttering the space with unnecessary items? = 他们是否正在用不必要的东西弄乱空间？


否定句:
He doesn't clutter his desk. = 他不把他的桌子弄乱。
They didn't clutter the house with decorations. = 他们没有用装饰品把房子弄得乱七八糟。
