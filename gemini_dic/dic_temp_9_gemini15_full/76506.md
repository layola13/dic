gale:/ɡeɪl/ | n. | 大风，强风 | A strong wind, often stormy.  |例句：A gale was blowing, making it difficult to walk.  一阵大风吹来，使得行走困难。 |例句：The gale tore the roof off the barn.  大风把谷仓的屋顶掀掉了。|近义词：storm, tempest, squall |反义词：calm, breeze
