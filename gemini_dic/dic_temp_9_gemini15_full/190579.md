satisfier: /ˈsætɪsfaɪər/ | n. | 令人满意的事物；满足者 | A person or thing that satisfies.  Something that fulfills a need or desire. | Example: The new software is a satisfier for our team's needs.  新软件满足了我们团队的需求。 |近义词：fulfiller, gratification, satisfaction | 反义词：dissatisfier, disappointment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new car is a satisfier for him. = 这辆新车让他很满意。
This job is a satisfier for her ambition. = 这份工作满足了她的雄心。


一般过去时:
That vacation was a great satisfier. = 那次假期非常令人满意。
The promotion was a satisfier for his hard work. = 晋升是对他的辛勤工作的奖赏。


现在完成时:
This project has been a satisfier for the whole team. = 这个项目让整个团队都很满意。
Finding a solution has been a satisfier for many months of hard work. = 找到解决方案是对数月辛勤工作的回报。


过去完成时:
Completing the report had been a satisfier before the deadline. = 在截止日期前完成报告令人满意。
Winning the award had been a satisfier after years of dedication. = 在多年的奉献之后获得奖项令人满意。


将来时:
Buying a house will be a satisfier for their family. = 买房子将使他们的家庭感到满意。
The new policy will be a satisfier for many customers. = 新政策将令许多顾客满意。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, buying that car would be a satisfier. = 如果我有更多的钱，买那辆车会让我很满意。
If they finished the project, it would be a satisfier. = 如果他们完成了项目，那将令人满意。


被动语态:
The problem was satisified by a new solution. = 一个新的解决方案解决了这个问题。(被动语态中，satisfier 作名词使用，句子主语是解决问题的方案)
His needs were satisified by his new job. = 他的新工作满足了他的需求。(被动语态中，satisfier 作名词使用，句子主语是工作)


疑问句:
Is this new design a satisfier for you? = 你觉得这个新设计令人满意吗？
What is the best satisfier for your stress? = 什么是减压的最佳方法？


否定句:
That explanation was not a satisfier. = 那个解释令人不满意。
The result was not a satisfier for the company. = 结果令公司不满意。


PHRASE->
a satisfier of needs  = 满足需求的事物
a primary satisfier = 首要的满足者
a potential satisfier = 潜在的满足者
a temporary satisfier = 暂时的满足者
a great satisfier = 极大的满足


词源:
Derived from the verb "satisfy," meaning "to fulfill the desires or needs of."  The suffix "-er" is added to form a noun denoting the agent or thing that satisfies.  Therefore, a satisfier is something that satisfies.
