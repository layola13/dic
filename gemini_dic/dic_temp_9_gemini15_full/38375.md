concepting:/ˈkɒnsɛptɪŋ/| v.|概念化；构思；创意构想 |The process of developing and refining ideas and concepts, often in a creative or strategic context.  It involves brainstorming, sketching, and prototyping to arrive at a viable solution or product.| 例句：The team spent a week concepting new marketing campaigns. =团队花了一周时间构思新的营销活动。
The architect spent months concepting the design of the new museum. = 建筑师花了几个月的时间构思新博物馆的设计。|近义词：ideation, brainstorming, conceptualization|反义词：implementation, execution


USAGE->

We are concepting a new logo for the company. = 我们正在为公司构思一个新的标志。
They concept new products every quarter. = 他们每个季度都会构思新产品。


一般过去时:
