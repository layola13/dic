insurance:/ɪnˈʃʊərəns/| n. |保险，保险业|A contract, represented by a policy, in which an individual or entity receives financial protection or reimbursement against losses from an insurance company.  The company pools clients' risks to make payments more manageable. |例句：He purchased car insurance to protect himself from financial losses in case of an accident. 他购买了汽车保险，以防万一发生事故造成经济损失。|例句：The company offers a wide range of insurance products, including health, life, and home insurance.  这家公司提供各种保险产品，包括健康保险、人寿保险和房屋保险。|近义词：assurance, protection, security |反义词：risk, hazard
