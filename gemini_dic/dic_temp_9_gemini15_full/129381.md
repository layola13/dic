nomarchy:/ˈnoʊmɑːrki/ | n. |氏族统治；部落统治 |Government by a tribe or clan; rule by the elders of a tribe or clan.  Nomarchy refers to a system of government where power is held by a tribal or clan leadership, often based on hereditary or traditional authority.  This form of governance is typically found in less developed societies or historical contexts.  |Example: The remote village operated under a strict nomarchy, with decisions made by the council of elders.  = 偏远的村庄实行严格的氏族统治，由长老会做出决定。|近义词：tribal rule, clan rule, gerontocracy |反义词：democracy, republic
