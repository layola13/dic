kerfuffle:/ˈkɜːrfʌl/| n. |喧嚣；混乱；骚动 |A kerfuffle is a commotion or fuss, typically a minor one. It describes a noisy or disorganized situation, often involving a lot of excited talk and argument.  |例句：There was a bit of a kerfuffle when the celebrity arrived.  名流到来时引起了一点小骚动。|例句：The announcement caused quite a kerfuffle in the office.  这个公告在办公室里引起了一阵骚动。|近义词：commotion, fuss, ado, to-do, rumpus |反义词：calm, peace, quiet, tranquility |名词
