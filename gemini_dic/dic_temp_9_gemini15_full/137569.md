nontransparency: /ˌnɒn trænsˈpærənsi/ | n. |不透明性，缺乏透明度 |The lack of transparency in the government's dealings caused public distrust.  政府事务的缺乏透明度导致了公众的不信任。| opacity, obscurity | transparency | 名词


英文解析: Nontransparency refers to the quality or state of being nontransparent;  lacking transparency;  not allowing light to pass through;  or not being open or easily understood.  It often implies a lack of openness, honesty, or clarity, especially in processes, decisions, or communication.


例句:

The nontransparency of the company's financial records raised concerns among investors. 公司财务记录的不透明性引起了投资者的担忧。

The nontransparency of the selection process led to accusations of favoritism.  选拔过程的不透明性导致了徇私舞弊的指控。


近义词:

opacity, obscurity, opaqueness, lack of openness, secrecy, concealment


反义词:

transparency, openness, clarity, lucidity, frankness
