caul:/kɔːl/| n.|网状膜；羊膜；内脏膜|A caul is a thin membrane that sometimes covers the head of a baby at birth.  It can also refer to a similar membrane covering the intestines of an animal.  Historically, it was considered to have magical or protective properties.|例句：The baby was born with a caul over its head. = 这个婴儿出生时头上盖着一层羊膜。|近义词：membrane, veil|反义词：None
