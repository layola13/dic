lunchbox:/ˈlʌntʃbɒks/| n. |饭盒；午餐盒|A container used to carry lunch.|例句：I packed my lunchbox with a sandwich and an apple. 我用三明治和苹果装满了我的饭盒。|例句：She forgot her lunchbox at home. 她把饭盒忘在家里了。|近义词：lunch pail, tiffin carrier|反义词:None
