sitfast:/ˈsɪtfæst/| n. |固执; 顽固; 坚持;（马鞍等）卡住|A sitfast is a stubborn adherence to a position or belief;  it can also refer to something, like a saddle, that is stuck fast.|例句：His sitfast on the issue of taxation prevented any compromise. 他在税收问题上的顽固立场阻碍了任何妥协。


The horse's saddle developed a sitfast, making it difficult to remove. 马鞍卡住了，很难取下来。|近义词：obstinacy, persistence, tenacity, adhesion|反义词：yielding, flexibility, compliance|名词
