cholera:/ˈkɒlərə/ /ˈkɑːlərə/| n. |霍乱 |A severe infectious disease caused by a bacterium (Vibrio cholerae) that causes severe diarrhea and dehydration.  It is often transmitted through contaminated water or food.|The cholera outbreak was quickly contained thanks to the rapid response of health officials.|近义词：dysentery, infectious diarrhea|反义词：health, wellness
