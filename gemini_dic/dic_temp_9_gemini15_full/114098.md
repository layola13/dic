magnify:/ˈmæɡnɪfaɪ/ | vt. |放大；夸大；赞美 | To increase the size or apparent size of something.  |例句：The microscope magnifies the image hundreds of times. 显微镜将图像放大数百倍。

例句：He magnified the importance of his contribution to the project. 他夸大了他对项目贡献的重要性。

近义词：amplify, enlarge, exaggerate | 反义词：diminish, reduce, minimize | 词性：动词

词源:
Magnify comes from the Latin word "magnificare," which is a combination of "magnus" (great, large) and "facere" (to make).  Therefore, it literally means "to make great" or "to make large."
