rediscussing:/ˌriːdɪˈskʌsɪŋ/| v.|重新讨论，再次讨论 |  The act of discussing something again; revisiting a topic previously discussed.  |例句：They are rediscussing the budget proposals. 他们正在重新讨论预算提案。|例句：We need to rediscuss the terms of the contract. 我们需要重新讨论合同条款。|近义词：reconsidering, reviewing, reexamining |反义词：concluding, finalizing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are rediscussing the project's timeline. = 他们正在重新讨论项目的期限。
We rediscuss our goals every quarter. = 我们每个季度都会重新讨论我们的目标。


一般过去时:
They rediscussed the proposal after the meeting. = 会议后他们重新讨论了提案。
We rediscussed the issue until we reached an agreement. = 我们反复讨论这个问题，直到达成一致。


现在完成时:
They have rediscussed the matter several times. = 他们已经多次重新讨论了此事。
We have rediscussed the contract's clauses to ensure clarity. = 我们已经重新讨论了合同条款以确保清晰。


过去完成时:
They had rediscussed the strategy before making a final decision. = 他们在做出最终决定之前已经重新讨论了战略。
We had rediscussed the problem before presenting the solution. = 我们在提出解决方案之前已经重新讨论了这个问题。


将来时:
They will rediscuss the contract next week. = 他们下周将重新讨论合同。
We will rediscuss the budget after the new data is available. = 新数据可用后，我们将重新讨论预算。


虚拟语气:
If we had time, we would rediscuss the details. = 如果我们有时间，我们会重新讨论细节。
It would be helpful if they rediscussed the plan. = 如果他们重新讨论一下计划，将会有所帮助。


被动语态:
The proposal is being rediscussed by the committee. = 提案正在由委员会重新讨论。
The budget has been rediscussed many times. = 预算已经被多次重新讨论。


疑问句:
Are they rediscussing the merger now? = 他们现在正在重新讨论兼并吗？
Will the team rediscuss the proposal before submitting it? = 团队在提交提案之前会重新讨论吗？


否定句:
They are not rediscussing the issue at this time. = 他们目前没有重新讨论这个问题。
We did not rediscuss the decision after the vote. = 我们投票后没有重新讨论决定。
