from:/frɒm/ /frəm/ | prep. | 从；来自；由于；从…起 |  Indicates origin, source, or starting point. It can refer to place, time, cause, or material.  | 例句：I'm from China. = 我来自中国。

The package arrived from London. =包裹是从伦敦寄来的。


近义词：since, out of, beginning with | 反义词：to, towards


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He comes from a small town. = 他来自一个小镇。
The river flows from the mountains. = 这条河从山上流下来。


一般过去时:
She came from a wealthy family. = 她出身于富裕家庭。
The letter arrived from abroad. = 这封信是从国外寄来的。


现在完成时:
We have traveled from many countries. = 我们去过很多国家旅行。
She has been absent from work for a week. = 她已经缺勤一周了。


过去完成时:
I had already left from the house before he arrived. = 他到之前我已经离开家了。
They had heard about the news from several sources. = 他们已经从多个渠道听说过这个消息了。


将来时:
They will travel from London to Paris. = 他们将从伦敦前往巴黎。
He will graduate from college next year. = 他明年将大学毕业。


虚拟语气:
If I came from a wealthy family, I would have many opportunities. = 如果我出身于富裕家庭，我将拥有许多机会。
I wish I came from a more supportive background. = 我希望我来自一个更有支持性的背景。


被动语态:
The information was obtained from reliable sources. = 信息是从可靠的来源获得的。
The decision was made from a careful consideration. = 这一决定是经过仔细考虑做出的。


疑问句:
Where are you from? = 你是哪里人？
From where did the news originate? = 这个消息是从哪里起源的？


否定句:
I didn't receive a response from them. = 我没有收到他们的回复。
He is not from this area. = 他不是这个地区的人。


PHRASE->
from now on = 从现在开始
from time to time = 时不时地
far from = 远非；决非
from scratch = 从头开始
different from = 与…不同
