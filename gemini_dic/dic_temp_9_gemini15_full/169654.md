proportionable: /prəˈpɔːʃənəbl/ | adj. | 成比例的；相称的 |  Having a suitable or proper proportion; commensurate.  It refers to something that is balanced, harmonious, or in the right ratio to other things.  | 例句：The punishment should be proportionable to the crime.  惩罚应该与罪行成正比。

The window size is proportionable to the room's dimensions. 窗户尺寸与房间尺寸成比例。


近义词：commensurate, proportionate, balanced, symmetrical | 反义词：disproportionate, unbalanced, asymmetrical | 词性：形容词


词源:  The word "proportionable" derives from the Old French "proporcionable," ultimately stemming from the Latin "proportio," meaning "proportion."  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or suitability.
