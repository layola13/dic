goofed:/ɡuːft/| v.|搞砸了，犯错|To make a mistake; to do something wrong or foolish.|例句：I completely goofed on the test. 我考试完全搞砸了。|例句：He goofed and missed the deadline. 他犯错错过了最后期限。|近义词：messed up, blundered, screwed up|反义词：succeeded, excelled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I goof up sometimes. = 我有时会犯错。
He goofs off during class. = 他上课时偷懒。

一般过去时:
I goofed on the exam. = 我考试考砸了。
She goofed the recipe. = 她把食谱搞错了。

现在完成时:
I have goofed up several times. = 我已经犯过几次错误了。
They have goofed on the project. = 他们把项目搞砸了。

过去完成时:
I had goofed before realizing my mistake. = 我在意识到错误之前就已经犯错了。
She had goofed the directions, so we got lost. = 她把方向搞错了，所以我们迷路了。

将来时:
I will goof up if I'm not careful. = 如果我不小心，我会犯错。
He will goof off again if given the chance. = 如果有机会，他还会偷懒。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't goofed, I would have passed. = 如果我没有犯错，我就及格了。
I wish I hadn't goofed on the presentation. = 我真希望我没有在演示中犯错。

被动语态:  *Goofed is rarely used in the passive voice.*

疑问句:
Did you goof on the assignment? = 你作业做砸了吗？
What did I goof? = 我哪里做错了？


否定句:
I didn't goof this time. = 我这次没犯错。
She didn't goof on the interview. = 她面试没有搞砸。


PHRASE->
goof around = 闲逛；胡闹
goof off = 偷懒；磨蹭
goof up = 搞砸；弄糟
goof it up = 搞砸了
to goof something up = 把某事搞砸了
