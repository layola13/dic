nlp:/ˌɛn.ɛlˈpiː/ | n. |自然语言处理|Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a branch of artificial intelligence (AI) that deals with the interaction between computers and human languages.  It focuses on enabling computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language. This includes tasks such as text translation, sentiment analysis, and question answering. |Example:  NLP techniques are used in spam filters to identify unwanted emails.  自然语言处理技术用于垃圾邮件过滤器以识别不需要的电子邮件。|近义词：computational linguistics, language processing |反义词：无
