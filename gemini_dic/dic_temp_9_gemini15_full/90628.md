humanistic:/ˌhjuːməˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 人文主义的；人道主义的 | Relating to humanism; characterized by an emphasis on human interests and values.  It often involves a focus on human potential and agency, critical thinking, and ethical considerations. |


例句：

The humanistic approach to education emphasizes critical thinking and creativity. = 人文主义的教育方法强调批判性思维和创造力。

His humanistic values led him to dedicate his life to helping others. = 他的人文主义价值观促使他将一生奉献于帮助他人。


近义词：humanitarian, compassionate, empathetic

反义词：inhuman, inhumane, dehumanizing


词性：形容词
