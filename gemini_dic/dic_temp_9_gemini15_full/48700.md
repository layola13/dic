defibrillates:/dɪˈfɪbrɪleɪts/| v. | 除颤；电击除颤 | To administer a defibrillating shock to (someone) to restore a normal heartbeat.  | Example: The paramedics defibrillated the patient after he went into cardiac arrest.  急救人员在他心脏骤停后对他进行了电击除颤。 |近义词：resuscitate, revive | 反义词：None specifically, but words implying cessation of heart function could be considered antonyms. | 动词
