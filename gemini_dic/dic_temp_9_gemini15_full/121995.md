misspelled:/ˌmɪsˈpɛld/ | adj. | 拼写错误的 | Incorrectly spelled.  The word was misspelled in the document.  | 近义词：wrongly spelled, incorrectly written | 反义词：correctly spelled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The word is misspelled. = 这个单词拼写错误。
This sentence contains a misspelled word. = 这句话包含一个拼写错误的单词。

一般过去时:
The student misspelled the word "definitely". = 学生把“definitely”这个词拼错了。
I misspelled your name on the form. = 我在表格上把你的名字拼错了。

现在完成时:
I have misspelled several words in this essay. = 我在这篇文章中拼错了几个单词。
She has misspelled the same word twice. = 她把同一个词拼错了两次。

过去完成时:
I realized I had misspelled his name after I sent the email. = 我发邮件后才意识到我把他名字拼错了。
