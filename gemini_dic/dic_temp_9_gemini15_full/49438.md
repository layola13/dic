delphically:/ˈdɛlfɪkli/| adj.|神谕般地；含糊其辞地；模棱两可地|  resembling or characteristic of the pronouncements of the Delphic oracle; ambiguous, mysterious, or enigmatic.  |例句：He answered my question delphically, leaving me more confused than before. 他含糊其辞地回答了我的问题，让我更加困惑。

例句：The politician's response was delphically vague, avoiding a direct answer.  这位政治家的回应含糊其辞，避免了直接回答。


近义词：enigmatically, ambiguously, obscurely, cryptically

反义词：clearly, explicitly, directly, transparently

词性：形容词
