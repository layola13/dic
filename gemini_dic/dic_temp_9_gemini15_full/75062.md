frightfullest:/ˈfraɪtfʊləst/| adj.|最可怕的，最令人恐惧的|The superlative form of "frightful," indicating the highest degree of fearfulness or awfulness. It emphasizes the extreme nature of something frightening.|例句：The frightfullest storm I've ever seen ravaged the coastline. 我见过的最可怕的风暴摧毁了海岸线。 The frightfullest creature imaginable emerged from the shadows.  你能想象到的最可怕的生物从阴影中出现了。|近义词：terrifying, horrifying, dreadful, appalling|反义词：delightful, pleasant, charming, comforting|词性:形容词
