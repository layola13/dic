impower:/ɪmˈpaʊər/| vt.|授权，赋予权力，使增强能力|To give someone more control over their own life and the power to do things.|The workshop empowered women to start their own businesses.  这个研讨会授权女性创业。|The new software empowers users to manage their data more efficiently.  新软件使⽤户能够更⾼效地管理数据。|enable, authorize, strengthen|weaken, disable, disempower|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company empowers its employees to take initiative. = 公司授权员工主动承担责任。
This program empowers students to learn independently. = 这项计划使学生能够独立学习。

一般过去时:
The manager empowered his team to make decisions. = 经理授权他的团队做决定。
The training empowered the participants to overcome challenges. = 培训使参与者能够克服挑战。

现在完成时:
The new policy has empowered women in the workplace. = 新政策已经赋予了职场女性权力。
This experience has empowered me to pursue my dreams. = 这段经历使我有能力追求我的梦想。

过去完成时:
She had empowered herself with knowledge before starting her business. = 在创业之前，她已经通过知识提升了自己的能力。
They had empowered the community to solve its own problems. = 他们已经授权社区解决自身问题。

将来时:
The government will empower local communities to manage their resources. = 政府将授权地方社区管理其资源。
This technology will empower individuals to connect globally. = 这项技术将使个人能够在全球范围内互联。

虚拟语气:
If we empowered our employees, they would be more productive. = 如果我们授权我们的员工，他们会更有生产力。
I wish I had empowered myself to speak up sooner. = 我真希望我早点有勇气说出自己的想法。

被动语态:
