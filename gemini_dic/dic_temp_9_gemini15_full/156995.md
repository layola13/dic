pillions:/ˈpɪljən/| n.|后座乘客；（摩托车）后座|A pillion is a passenger seat on a motorcycle or other two-wheeled vehicle, or a person who sits on such a seat.  The word can also refer to a passenger riding on the back of a horse.|例句：She rode pillion on his motorcycle. 她坐在他的摩托车后座上。|例句：Two pillions were on the back of the motorbike. 摩托车后座上有两个乘客。|近义词：passenger (on a motorcycle), rider (on a horse)|反义词：driver, rider (in the sense of the person controlling the vehicle)
