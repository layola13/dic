monumentless: /ˈmɒnjʊməntləs/ | adj. | 无纪念碑的；缺乏纪念意义的 | Lacking monuments or memorials; not commemorated by a monument.  The landscape felt monumentless, devoid of any markers of past events or significant figures.  | 近义词:unmarked, unremembered, insignificant | 反义词: monumental, memorable


例句：

The city, despite its long history, felt monumentless; no statues or buildings marked its past glories.  =这座城市尽管历史悠久，却感觉缺乏纪念意义；没有任何雕像或建筑物标志着它过去的辉煌。

The plain stretched before them, a monumentless expanse of grass and scrub. = 平原展现在他们面前，一片没有纪念碑的广阔的草地和灌木丛。
