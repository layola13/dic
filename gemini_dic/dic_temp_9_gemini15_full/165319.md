preinvested: /ˌpriːɪnˈvɛstɪd/ | adj. | 预先投资的；已预先投资的 |  Already invested beforehand;  having been invested in advance.  This term describes a situation where resources or capital have been committed before a specific project or event. | 例句：The preinvested funds were crucial to the project's early success. = 预先投资的资金对项目的早期成功至关重要。

The company's preinvested capital allowed them to quickly expand into new markets. = 公司预先投资的资金使他们能够迅速拓展到新的市场。


近义词：prefunded, advanced, upfront

反义词：post-invested, delayed


词性：形容词
