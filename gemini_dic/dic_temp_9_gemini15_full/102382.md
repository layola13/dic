jabbed:/dʒæbd/ | vt. | 刺，戳，猛击 | To poke or thrust quickly and forcefully.  | 例句：He jabbed the needle into my arm. 他用针猛刺我的手臂。 | 例句：The boxer jabbed his opponent in the ribs. 拳击手猛击对手的肋部。| 近义词：poke, prod, stab, pierce | 反义词：pull, caress


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He jabs the button repeatedly. = 他反复戳按按钮。
She jabs at the dough with a fork. = 她用叉子戳面团。

一般过去时:
He jabbed his finger at the map. = 他用手指指着地图。
The child jabbed the toy at his friend. = 孩子用玩具戳他的朋友。

现在完成时:
She has jabbed herself with a needle. = 她不小心用针扎到自己了。
They have jabbed at the problem for hours without a solution. = 他们几个小时都在试图解决这个问题，但没有找到解决方法。

过去完成时:
He had jabbed the key into the lock several times before it opened. = 他在锁打开之前已经多次把钥匙插进锁孔了。
The thief had jabbed a knife into the victim's back. = 小偷用刀刺了受害者的背部。

将来时:
He will jab the ball forward to the teammate. = 他会把球向前捅给队友。
She will jab at the computer screen in frustration. = 她会沮丧地猛戳电脑屏幕。

虚拟语气:
If he had jabbed harder, he might have won the fight. = 如果他当时刺得更猛，他可能就赢了比赛。
I wish I hadn't jabbed him so hard. = 我真希望我没有那么用力地戳他。

被动语态:
The meat was jabbed with a fork to tenderize it. = 肉被叉子戳过以使其变嫩。
The painting was accidentally jabbed with a brush, damaging it. = 画不小心被画笔戳了一下，弄坏了。

疑问句:
Did he jab you on purpose? = 他是故意戳你的吗？
What did he jab at? = 他戳的是什么？

否定句:
He didn't jab the needle in hard enough. = 他没有用力地扎针。
She didn't mean to jab him with her elbow. = 她不是故意用肘部戳他的。


PHRASE->
jab at something = 猛戳某物
jab someone with something = 用某物戳某人
jab someone in the ribs = 戳某人的肋部
a jab to the face = 一记脸部重拳
a jab at someone (figurative) = 对某人的讽刺/挖苦
