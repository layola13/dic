murmuringly:/ˈmɜːrmərɪŋli/| adv. |低声地，喃喃地说 |adverbial form of murmuring, indicating a low, indistinct, or almost inaudible manner of speaking.|The old woman spoke murmuringly, her words barely audible. 老妇人低声细语，几乎听不见她的声音。|近义词：softly, quietly, under one's breath|反义词：loudly, boisterously


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He speaks murmuringly to himself. = 他低声自语。
She always answers murmuringly. = 她总是低声回答。
