premediate:/ˌpriːˈmɛdieɪt/| vt.|预谋；预先考虑；预先计划|To plan or consider beforehand; to premeditate.|例句：He premeditated the crime for weeks before carrying it out. 他在实施犯罪前预谋了几周。|例句：She premeditated her response to the difficult question. 她预先考虑了对那个难题的回答。|近义词：plan, plot, scheme, contemplate|反义词：improvise, act impulsively


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He premeditates his speeches carefully. = 他仔细地预先构思他的演讲。
They premediate their moves in the chess game. = 他们在国际象棋游戏中预先计划他们的行动。


一般过去时:
She premeditated her revenge for months. = 她预谋报复了好几个月。
The robber premeditated the heist for weeks. = 强盗预谋抢劫了好几个星期。


现在完成时:
He has premeditated this action for a long time. = 他为此行动预谋已久。
They have premeditated their escape route. = 他们已经预先计划好了逃跑路线。


过去完成时:
She had premeditated the murder before the victim’s arrival. = 在受害者到达之前，她就预谋了谋杀。
The conspirators had premeditated their coup for years. = 阴谋者们已经密谋策划政变多年了。


将来时:
They will premediate their next move carefully. = 他们将仔细地预先计划他们的下一步行动。
He will premediate his arguments before the debate. = 他将在辩论前预先构思他的论点。


虚拟语气:
If he had premeditated his actions, he might have been more successful. = 如果他事先计划好了他的行动，他可能会更成功。
I wish I had premeditated my response to his accusations. = 我真希望我事先考虑好了对他指控的回应。


被动语态:
The crime was premeditated. = 这起犯罪是预谋的。
The plan was premeditated over several meetings. = 该计划是在几次会议上预先计划好的。


疑问句:
Was the attack premeditated? = 袭击是预谋的吗？
Did they premediate their actions? = 他们的行动是预谋的吗？


否定句:
The accident was not premeditated. = 事故并非预谋。
He did not premediate his actions; they were impulsive. = 他没有预谋他的行为；它们是冲动的。
