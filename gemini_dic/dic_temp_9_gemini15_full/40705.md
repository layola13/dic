converter:/kənˈvɜːrtər/| n. |转换器，变换器|A device or program that changes something from one form to another.  For example, a device that changes alternating current to direct current, or a computer program that changes data from one format to another.|例句：The AC/DC converter is essential for powering the device.  这个交流/直流转换器对于为设备供电至关重要。

例句：The video converter changed the format of the movie file so that it could be played on my phone.  视频转换器更改了电影文件的格式，以便我的手机可以播放。


近义词：transformer, transducer, translator


反义词：None (no direct antonym, context-dependent)

词源:
The word "converter" comes from the verb "convert," meaning "to change something into a different form or function."  "Convert" itself derives from the Latin word "convertire,"  which is a combination of "com-" (together) and "vertere" (to turn).  Therefore, the original meaning implied a turning or changing together.
