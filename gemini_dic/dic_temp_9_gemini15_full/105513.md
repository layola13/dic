kilojoule:/ˈkɪləˌdʒuːl/| n. |千焦耳|A kilojoule (kJ) is a unit of energy equal to one thousand joules.  It's commonly used to measure the energy content of food and the energy produced or consumed in various processes. |The energy bar contains 200 kilojoules. 这能量棒含有200千焦耳。  This machine generates 1000 kilojoules of heat per hour. 这台机器每小时产生1000千焦耳的热量。|joule, calorie|  (no direct antonym, but smaller units like joule could be considered a relative antonym)|名词
