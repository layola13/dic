leg-pull:/ˈlɛɡpʊl/| n. |开玩笑，捉弄，戏弄 |A leg-pull is a joke or a trick intended to tease or deceive someone. It's a playful, usually harmless, attempt to make someone believe something untrue.  |例句：He told me he'd won the lottery; it was just a leg-pull. 他告诉我他中了彩票；那只是个玩笑。


例句：Don't worry, it was just a leg-pull. 别担心，那只是个玩笑。


近义词：joke, prank, tease, hoax, spoof


反义词：serious statement, truth, earnest


词性：名词
