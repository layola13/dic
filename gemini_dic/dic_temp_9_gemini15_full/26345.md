buzzphrase:/ˈbʌzˌfreɪz/ | n. |流行语，时髦语 |A buzzphrase is a word or phrase that is very popular for a short time, often used in a particular field or context.  It is often considered trendy or fashionable but can quickly become outdated.  |Example: "Synergy" was a popular buzzphrase in the business world for a while.  |近义词：catchphrase, cliché, fad word |反义词：archaic term, obsolete term
