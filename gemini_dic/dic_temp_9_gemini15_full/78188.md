geostationary:/ˌdʒioʊˈsteɪʃənəri/ | adj. | 地球同步的；对地静止的 |  Relating to or denoting a satellite that orbits the earth at the same rate as the earth rotates, so that it appears stationary relative to a point on the earth's surface.  | Example: Geostationary satellites are used for communication and weather forecasting.  地球同步卫星用于通信和天气预报。|近义词：geosynchronous|反义词：geocentric
