cookouts:/ˈkʊkˌaʊts/| n. |户外烧烤；野餐|An outdoor gathering where food is cooked and eaten, typically involving grilling.|例句：We're having a cookout at the park this weekend.  我们这个周末在公园举办烧烤派对。|例句：The family enjoyed a cookout on the beach.  这家人在海滩上享受了一次烧烤。|近义词：barbecue, picnic, grill|反义词：indoor party, formal dinner
