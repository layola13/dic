insole:/ˈɪnˌsoʊl/| n.|鞋垫|A removable or fixed inner sole of a shoe or boot, typically made of leather, fabric, or foam, providing cushioning, support, or insulation.|Example:  He replaced the worn-out insoles in his hiking boots. 他换掉了徒步鞋里磨损的鞋垫。|近义词：inner sole, footbed|反义词：outsole
