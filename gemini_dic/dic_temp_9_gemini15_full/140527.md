oleic:/ˈoʊliɪk/| adj.|油酸的；油状的|relating to or derived from oleic acid, an unsaturated fatty acid found in many oils and fats.  Oleic acid is the most common fatty acid in nature.|Example: Olive oil has a high oleic acid content. 橄榄油含有高含量的油酸。 The oleic acid in the avocado provides healthy fats. 牛油果中的油酸提供健康的脂肪。|近义词：fatty, oily|反义词：saturated (in the context of fatty acids)
