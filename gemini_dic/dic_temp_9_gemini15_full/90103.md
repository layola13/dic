hot-and-bothered: /ˌhɑːt ænd ˈbɑːðərd/ | adj. |又热又烦躁的，焦虑不安的 | Feeling hot and bothered describes a state of being both physically hot and emotionally agitated or stressed.  It often implies a feeling of being overwhelmed or uncomfortable. |例句：The stuffy room left me feeling hot and bothered.  闷热的房间让我感到又热又烦躁。

The humidity made her hot and bothered and irritable. 潮湿的天气让她又热又烦躁，易怒。


近义词：overwhelmed, stressed, agitated, flustered, irritated, uncomfortable


反义词：calm, relaxed, cool, composed


词性:形容词
