preeners:/ˈpriːnərz/| n. |爱打扮的人；自我陶醉的人|A preener is someone who spends a lot of time on their appearance and is often vain or self-absorbed. They might meticulously groom themselves and pay close attention to their clothing and style.  |例句：The preeners in the room all stared at themselves in the mirror.  房间里那些爱打扮的人都在镜子里看着自己。
