caulk:/kɔːk/| v. |填塞，堵住|to fill or close (a gap or crack) with a sealant, especially to make it waterproof or airtight. |例句：He caulked the seams of the boat to prevent leaks. 他用填缝剂填塞船的缝隙以防止漏水。|例句：We need to caulk the cracks in the window frame to stop the draft.我们需要堵住窗框上的裂缝以阻止冷风的进入。|近义词：seal, plug, stop up |反义词：open, unseal


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He caulks the cracks in the boat every year. = 他每年都用填缝剂填塞船上的裂缝。
The plumber caulks the pipes to prevent leaks. = 水管工用填缝剂堵住管道以防止漏水。

一般过去时:
She caulked the window frame before painting. = 她粉刷之前先用填缝剂堵住了窗框。
They caulked the bathtub to prevent water damage. = 他们用填缝剂堵住浴缸以防止水损坏。

现在完成时:
He has caulked all the gaps in the deck. = 他已经堵塞了甲板上的所有缝隙。

过去完成时:
