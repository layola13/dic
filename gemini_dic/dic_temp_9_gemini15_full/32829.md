choking-up:/ˈtʃoʊkɪŋ ʌp/ | v. |哽咽；声音哽塞；情绪激动得说不出话 | To become unable to speak because of strong emotion, such as sadness or anger, often accompanied by tears.  | Example:  He choked up when he talked about his late mother. 他谈到他已故的母亲时哽咽了。She choked up during the awards ceremony. 她在颁奖典礼上哽咽了。| 近义词：welling up, tearing up, getting choked, becoming emotional | 反义词：remaining calm, staying composed
