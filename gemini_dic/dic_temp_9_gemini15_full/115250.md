manipulate:/məˈnɪpjuleɪt/ | vt. |操纵；控制；操作；巧妙地处理 | to control or influence (a person or situation) cleverly or unscrupulously; to handle or operate (a mechanism, process, etc.) | 例句：He manipulated the evidence to support his theory. 他操纵证据以支持他的理论。

He skillfully manipulated the controls of the aircraft. 他熟练地操作飞机的控制装置。


近义词：control, influence, manage, operate, handle | 反义词：leave alone, ignore


USAGE->
