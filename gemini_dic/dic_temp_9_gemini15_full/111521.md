loathe:/loʊð/| vt.|厌恶，憎恨|To feel intense dislike or disgust for.  It implies a strong feeling of aversion and revulsion.|I loathe the smell of cabbage. 我讨厌卷心菜的味道。|He loathes his job. 他痛恨他的工作。|abhor, detest, despise|adore, love, like|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I loathe public speaking. = 我讨厌公开演讲。
She loathes dishonesty. = 她厌恶不诚实。

一般过去时:
He loathed the way she treated him. = 他讨厌她对待他的方式。
They loathed the bitter taste of the medicine. = 他们讨厌那药的苦味。

现在完成时:
I have loathed this task since it began. = 从这项任务开始以来，我就一直讨厌它。
She has loathed him ever since their argument. = 从他们争吵以来，她就一直讨厌他。

过去完成时:
He had loathed the idea for years before finally agreeing to it. = 在最终同意之前，他已经讨厌这个主意很多年了。
She had loathed the thought of going to the party before she met the host. = 在她遇到主人之前，她就一直讨厌去参加派对的想法。

将来时:
I will loathe this job until the day I retire. = 直到我退休的那一天，我都会讨厌这份工作。
She will loathe having to go to that meeting. = 她将会讨厌不得不去参加那个会议。

虚拟语气:
I would loathe to be in his shoes. = 我不愿意处于他的境地。
If I had known how much I would loathe the job, I wouldn't have taken it. = 如果我知道我会如此讨厌这份工作，我不会接受的。

被动语态:
The task was loathed by everyone. = 这项任务被每个人都讨厌。
His behavior is loathed by his colleagues. = 他的行为受到同事们的厌恶。

疑问句:
Do you loathe the sound of nails on a chalkboard? = 你讨厌指甲刮黑板的声音吗？
Does she loathe spiders? = 她讨厌蜘蛛吗？

否定句:
I don't loathe all vegetables, just some. = 我并非讨厌所有的蔬菜，只是讨厌其中一些。
He didn't loathe her at first, but his feelings changed. = 他一开始并不讨厌她，但他的感觉变了。


PHRASE->
loathe to do something: 不愿做某事
loathe the thought of: 讨厌想到...
loathe the idea of: 讨厌...的想法
to loathe and despise: 极其厌恶
cannot loathe enough: 再怎么讨厌也不为过
