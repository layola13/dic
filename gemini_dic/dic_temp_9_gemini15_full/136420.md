nonretiring: /ˌnɒn.rɪˈtaɪə.rɪŋ/ | adj. | 不退休的；继续工作的 |  Not retiring; continuing to work. This word describes someone who chooses to continue working past the typical retirement age or who does not intend to retire.  | 例句：He's a nonretiring professor, still teaching at 75. 他是一位不退休的教授，75岁还在教书。


The company encourages nonretiring employees to share their expertise with younger colleagues. 公司鼓励不退休的员工与年轻同事分享他们的专业知识。


近义词：working, employed, active, still employed | 反义词：retired, pensioned
