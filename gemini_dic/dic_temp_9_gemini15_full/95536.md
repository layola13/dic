inclusivity: /ˌɪnˌkluːˈsɪvɪti/ | n. | 包容性 | The quality of being inclusive; the practice or policy of including people who might otherwise be excluded.  It encompasses the acceptance and respect of differences in people's backgrounds, abilities, perspectives, and identities.  | Example: The company prides itself on its inclusivity, employing people from diverse backgrounds and offering flexible working arrangements.  公司以其包容性而自豪，雇佣来自不同背景的人，并提供灵活的工作安排。 | 近义词：tolerance, acceptance, breadth, comprehensiveness | 反义词：exclusion, discrimination, narrow-mindedness, bigotry |  名词
