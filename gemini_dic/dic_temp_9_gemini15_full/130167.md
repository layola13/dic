non-reusable: /ˌnɒnriˈjuːzəbl/ | adj. |不可重复使用的，一次性的|  Not able to be used again; disposable.  The term emphasizes the inability to reuse the item after a single use, unlike something that can be cleaned or repaired for further use. | Example:  The plastic cups are non-reusable and should be discarded after use.  一次性塑料杯用完后应丢弃。


The plastic bag is non-reusable; please recycle it.  这个塑料袋是不可重复使用的；请回收它。


近义词: disposable, single-use, one-time

反义词: reusable, recyclable, refillable


词源:  The word is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the adjective "reusable," which is derived from the verb "reuse" (to use again).  "Reuse" itself is composed of the prefix "re-" (meaning "again") and the verb "use."  Therefore, the word's etymology traces back to the Old English word "ūs," meaning "use."
