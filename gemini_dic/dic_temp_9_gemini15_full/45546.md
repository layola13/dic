curiously:/ˈkjʊəriəsli/ | adv. | 好奇地；奇怪地 | In a curious manner; strangely.  It expresses the manner in which something is done or happens, indicating a sense of wonder, strangeness, or unexpectedness. | 例句：Curiously, the dog started barking at the empty chair.  = 好奇的是，那条狗开始对着空椅子吠叫。


例句：He looked curiously at the strange object. = 他好奇地看了看那个奇怪的物体。


近义词：strangely, oddly, remarkably, unexpectedly


反义词：expectedly, normally, predictably


词性: 副词
