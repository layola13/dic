sinistral:/ˈsɪnɪstrəl/| adj.|左边的；左手的；逆时针方向的；（动物）左旋的|Left-handed; occurring on the left side; counterclockwise; (of a shell) having the aperture on the left side when held with the apex pointing upwards.  The term often implies a deviation from the norm or a preference for the left side.|例句：He is sinistral, preferring to use his left hand. 他是左撇子，更喜欢用左手。

例句：The snail shell spirals in a sinistral direction. 蜗牛壳以逆时针方向旋转。|近义词：left-handed, leftward, counterclockwise|反义词：dextral, right-handed, clockwise|词性:形容词
