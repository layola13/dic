colorizer:/ˈkʌləraɪzər/ | n. |着色器，彩色化工具 |A colorizer is a tool or software that adds color to images or videos, typically those that were originally black and white or grayscale.  It can also refer to a person who adds color to images. |Example:  The film was remastered using a sophisticated colorizer to enhance its visual appeal.  这部电影使用复杂的着色器重新制作，以增强其视觉吸引力。  The artist acted as a colorizer, breathing life into the monochrome sketch.艺术家充当着色器，为单色素描注入了生命。|近义词：color enhancer, tint, hue adjuster |反义词：desaturator, grayscale converter |名词
