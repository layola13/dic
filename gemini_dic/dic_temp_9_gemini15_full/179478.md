redying:/ˈriːdaɪɪŋ/| v.|重新染色，再染色|To dye (something) again.  This typically refers to fabrics or materials that have been dyed previously and are being dyed a different color or the same color to refresh the color.|例句：She's re-dyeing her hair a darker shade. 她正把头发染成更深的颜色。

例句：The factory is re-dyeing the fabric to meet the new order specifications. 工厂正在重新染色织物以满足新的订单规格。


近义词：recoloring, recoloring, dyeing again|反义词：bleaching, fading
