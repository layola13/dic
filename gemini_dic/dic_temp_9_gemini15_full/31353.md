characterized:/ˈkærəktəraɪzd/|/ˈkærəktəraɪzd/| v. |描绘…的特性；以…为特征 |To describe the qualities or features of someone or something.  |The city is characterized by its vibrant nightlife.  这座城市以其充满活力的夜生活为特色。|described, defined, distinguished|indistinct, amorphous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His work is characterized by its meticulous detail. = 他的作品以其细致入微的细节为特色。
The region is characterized by its harsh climate. = 该地区以其恶劣的气候为特征。


一般过去时:
The meeting was characterized by heated debate. = 会议的特点是激烈的辩论。
The novel was characterized by its complex characters. = 这部小说的特点是人物复杂。


现在完成时:
His career has been characterized by consistent success. = 他的职业生涯一直以持续的成功为特征。
The 20th century has been characterized by rapid technological advancement. = 20 世纪的特点是科技的飞速发展。


过去完成时:
The previous decade had been characterized by economic instability. = 前十年一直以经济不稳定为特征。
Her childhood had been characterized by a lack of parental support.  = 她的童年时期缺乏父母的支持。


将来时:
The next decade will likely be characterized by increased automation. = 未来十年很可能以自动化程度的提高为特征。
The project will be characterized by its innovative approach. = 该项目将以其创新的方法为特色。


虚拟语气:
If the disease were characterized by fever, it would be easier to diagnose. = 如果这种疾病的特点是发烧，那么诊断起来就会更容易。
If his work were characterized by greater clarity, it would be better received. = 如果他的作品更清晰明了，就会得到更好的评价。


被动语态:
The painting is characterized by its bold use of color. = 这幅画的特点是大胆的色彩运用。
The era is characterized by its social upheaval. = 这个时代以其社会动荡为特征。


疑问句:
How is this type of cancer characterized? = 这种癌症有什么特征？
How is the new software characterized by its users? = 新软件的用户如何评价其特点？


否定句:
The play was not characterized by originality. = 这出戏并不以原创性为特色。
The relationship wasn't characterized by mutual respect. = 这段关系并非以相互尊重为特征。


PHRASE->
be characterized by = 以……为特征
characterized as = 被认为是……
strongly characterized = 强烈地表现出……的特征
uniquely characterized = 独一无二地表现出……的特征
well characterized = 特征鲜明
