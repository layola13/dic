cryosat:/ˈkraɪoʊsæt/| n. |冰冻卫星|A satellite designed to measure the thickness of ice sheets and sea ice using radar altimetry. Cryosat is a specific name for a series of Earth observation satellites developed by the European Space Agency (ESA) to monitor the polar ice caps and sea ice. |Cryosat-2 is providing valuable data on the changes in the Earth's ice sheets and sea ice.  冰冻卫星-2正在提供关于地球冰盖和海冰变化的宝贵数据。|近义词:ice-monitoring satellite, polar-observing satellite |反义词: N/A
