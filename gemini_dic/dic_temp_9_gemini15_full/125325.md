multilamellar: /ˌmʌltiˈlæmələr/ | adj. | 多层板状的，多层膜的 | Having or composed of many layers.  This term is typically used to describe structures with multiple concentric layers, often referring to lipid bilayers in biological contexts.  | Example:  The liposome exhibited a multilamellar structure under electron microscopy.  电子显微镜下，脂质体呈现多层结构。 |近义词:  stratified, layered, multilayered | 反义词: unilamellar (单层膜的)


词源：

* **multi-**:  Prefix meaning "many" or "multiple."  源自拉丁语 "multus," 意思是“许多的”。
* **lamellar**: Adjective derived from "lamella," meaning a thin plate or layer. 源自拉丁语 "lamella," 指薄片或薄层。  This, in turn, is a diminutive of "lama," meaning a thin plate or scale.


用法说明：

该词主要用于科学和技术领域，特别是描述生物膜的结构，例如脂质体（liposomes）和细胞膜（cell membranes）。 它也可能用于描述其他材料的层状结构。  它通常不作名词使用。


例句：

1. The multilamellar vesicles were prepared using a thin-film hydration method. 使用薄膜水化法制备多层囊泡。

2.  Multilamellar structures are frequently observed in biological systems such as cell membranes. 多层结构经常在生物系统中观察到，例如细胞膜。

3. The image shows a cross-section of the multilamellar material. 图片显示了多层材料的横截面。

4. The researchers analyzed the phase behavior of the multilamellar lipid system. 研究人员分析了多层脂质系统的相行为。

5. The formation of multilamellar structures depends on several factors, including lipid composition and hydration. 多层结构的形成取决于几个因素，包括脂质组成和水合作用。


需要注意的是，由于该词较为专业，在日常生活中并不常用。
