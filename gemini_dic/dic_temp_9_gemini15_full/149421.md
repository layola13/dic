packhorse:/ˈpækˌhɔːrs/| n. |驮畜，驮马|A horse used to carry packs or burdens.  It is typically a sturdy, strong horse capable of carrying heavy loads over difficult terrain.|例句：The packhorse struggled up the steep mountain path, its burden swaying precariously. 驮马艰难地沿着陡峭的山路向上攀登，它的负重摇摇晃晃地。|  The miners relied on packhorses to transport their supplies. 矿工们依靠驮马运输他们的物资。|近义词：burden-bearer, pack animal|反义词：rider (in the sense of a horse not carrying a load)|词性:名词
