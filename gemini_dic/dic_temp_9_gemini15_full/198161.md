shortage:/ˈʃɔːrtɪdʒ/| n. |短缺，不足|A lack or deficiency of something.|The city is experiencing a water shortage due to the drought.  这个城市由于干旱而经历着缺水。|There is a shortage of skilled workers in the tech industry. 科技行业熟练工人短缺。|abundance, deficiency, scarcity|surplus, excess|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A shortage of food exists in many developing countries. = 许多发展中国家都存在粮食短缺。
There is a shortage of qualified teachers. = 合格教师短缺。

一般过去时:
A severe energy shortage occurred last winter. = 去年冬天发生了严重的能源短缺。
There was a shortage of qualified applicants for the job. = 这个职位合格的申请者不足。

现在完成时:
We have experienced several shortages of essential supplies this year. = 今年我们经历了几次必需品的短缺。
The company has had a shortage of skilled labor for months. = 这家公司几个月来一直缺乏熟练劳动力。

过去完成时:
Before the storm, there had been a shortage of bottled water. = 暴风雨来临之前，瓶装水就已短缺。
They had already experienced a shortage of funds before the project even started. = 项目开始之前，他们就已经资金短缺了。


将来时:
There will be a shortage of resources in the future if we don't act now. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，将来将会资源短缺。
Experts predict a severe housing shortage next year. = 专家预测明年将出现严重的住房短缺。


虚拟语气:
If there were no shortage of funds, we could finish the project on time. = 如果资金不短缺，我们就能按时完成项目。
I wish there were no shortage of affordable housing in this city. = 我希望这个城市没有经济适用房短缺的问题。


被动语态:
The shortage of qualified nurses was widely reported in the media. = 护士短缺的问题在媒体上被广泛报道。
The problem of the shortage of materials was being addressed by the management. = 管理层正在解决材料短缺的问题。


疑问句:
Is there a shortage of parking spaces in this area? = 这个地区停车位短缺吗？
What kind of shortage are we facing right now? = 我们现在面临什么类型的短缺？


否定句:
There is no shortage of willing volunteers for the charity event. = 慈善活动并不缺乏志愿者。
We don't anticipate a shortage of supplies for the upcoming event. = 我们预计即将举行的活动不会出现物资短缺。
