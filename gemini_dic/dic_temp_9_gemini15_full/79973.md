go-between:/ˈɡoʊˌbɪtwiːn/ | n. |中间人，媒介，调停人 | A person who acts as a link or intermediary between people who cannot or do not wish to communicate directly.  |例句：He acted as a go-between for the warring factions. 他充当了交战各派之间的中间人。 |例句：The lawyer served as a go-between in the divorce negotiations. 律师在离婚谈判中充当了中间人。|近义词：intermediary, mediator, negotiator, agent |反义词：direct communication, principal
