larking:/ˈlɑːkɪŋ/| n.|嬉戏；玩闹；寻乐|Engaging in frivolous or playful activities;  often mischievous or slightly irresponsible behavior.|例句：The children were larking around in the park.孩子们在公园里嬉戏玩耍。|例句：They spent the afternoon larking about on the beach.他们下午在海滩上嬉戏玩闹。|近义词：frolicking, playing, fooling around|反义词：working, toiling, being serious


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are larking about in the garden. = 他们在花园里嬉戏玩闹。
We are larking around the office. = 我们在办公室里四处嬉戏。

一般过去时:
They were larking around in the street. = 他们在大街上嬉戏玩闹。

现在完成时:

We have larked around enough for one day. = 我们今天已经玩够了。

过去完成时:

We had larked around so much that we were exhausted. = 我们玩得太疯了，累坏了。
