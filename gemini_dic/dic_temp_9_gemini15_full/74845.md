frequent:/ˈfriːkwənt/| adj.|频繁的，常见的|occurring or appearing many times at short intervals|The frequent interruptions disrupted the meeting. 频繁的打断扰乱了会议。|He makes frequent visits to his parents. 他经常去看望父母。|近义词：common, usual, regular, ordinary|反义词：rare, infrequent, occasional


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He frequently visits his grandmother. = 他经常去看望他的祖母。
They frequent this restaurant. = 他们经常光顾这家餐馆。

一般过去时:
She frequently called him last week. = 上周她经常给他打电话。
They frequented that bar every Friday. = 他们每个星期五都经常去那家酒吧。

现在完成时:
I have frequently warned him about the dangers. = 我已经多次警告过他危险。
They have frequented this cafe for years. = 他们光顾这家咖啡馆已经很多年了。

过去完成时:
He had frequently complained about the noise before moving. = 在搬家之前，他经常抱怨噪音。
She had frequented that bookstore since childhood. = 她从小就经常光顾那家书店。

将来时:
I will frequently check my email. = 我将经常检查我的电子邮件。
They will frequent the gym more often next year. = 他们明年将更频繁地去健身房。

虚拟语气:
If I frequented that gym, I might get fitter. = 如果我经常去那个健身房，我可能会更健康。
I wish I had frequented this museum sooner. = 我真希望我早点来这个博物馆。

被动语态:
This cafe is frequently visited by tourists. = 这家咖啡馆经常被游客光顾。
The park is frequently used by local residents. = 这个公园经常被当地居民使用。

疑问句:
Do you frequently travel abroad? = 你经常出国旅行吗？
How frequently do they meet? = 他们多久见面一次？

否定句:
He doesn't frequent bars anymore. = 他不再经常去酒吧了。
She doesn't frequently use her car. = 她不经常使用她的车。
