goofiest:/ˈɡuːfiɪst/| adj. |最愚蠢的，最滑稽的 | Most foolish or silly; characterized by foolishness or silliness.  It is the superlative form of "goofy".|例句：He wore the goofiest hat I've ever seen. 他戴着我见过的最傻的帽子。

例句：That's the goofiest thing I've ever heard!  那是听过最蠢的事情了！


近义词：silliest, most absurd, most ridiculous, most foolish

反义词：most sensible, most serious, most intelligent


词性：形容词
