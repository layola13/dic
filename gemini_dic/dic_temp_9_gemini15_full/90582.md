hulks:/hʌlks/ | n. |破旧的船；庞然大物；强壮的人 |A hulk is a large, heavy, and usually old and decaying ship.  It can also refer to something very large and unwieldy, or a very strong and large person. |例句：The hulk of the abandoned ship lay rotting on the beach.  那艘废弃的船的残骸躺在海滩上腐烂。 The building was a massive hulk of concrete and steel.  这栋建筑是混凝土和钢铁的庞大巨物。|近义词：wreck, ruin, behemoth, giant |反义词：small, dainty, delicate
