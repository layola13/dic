sanctioneer:/ˌsæŋkʃəˈnɪər/| n. | 制裁者；批准者 | One who imposes sanctions or gives official approval.  A person or entity that enforces sanctions or approves actions. |The sanctioneer imposed strict trade restrictions on the offending nation. 制裁者对违规国家实施了严格的贸易限制。|近义词：enforcer, approver |反义词：supporter (in context of sanctions), opponent (in context of approval)
