repulse:/rɪˈpʌls/| vt. n.|击退；使厌恶；令人反感|to drive back (an attack, an attacker, etc.) by force; to cause (someone) to feel disgust or aversion|The army successfully repulsed the enemy attack.  军队成功击退了敌人的进攻。

The smell repulsed me.  这气味让我作呕。|repel, drive back, ward off|attract, appeal to|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The strong fortress repels all attacks. = 这座坚固的堡垒击退了所有的攻击。
The smell of garbage repels many people. = 垃圾的气味让很多人反感。


一般过去时:
The soldiers repulsed the enemy's advance. = 士兵们击退了敌人的进攻。
Her rudeness repulsed him. = 她的粗鲁让他反感。


现在完成时:
Our defenses have repulsed every attack. = 我们的防御工事已经击退了所有的攻击。
His arrogance has repulsed many potential friends. = 他的傲慢已经让许多潜在的朋友反感。


过去完成时:
The city had repulsed several sieges before finally falling. = 在最终沦陷之前，这座城市已经击退了几次围攻。
She had been repulsed by his advances before, so she ignored him this time. = 她以前就对他示好感到反感，所以这次她无视了他。

I believe that his charm will repulse her. = 我相信他的魅力会让她反感。


虚拟语气:
