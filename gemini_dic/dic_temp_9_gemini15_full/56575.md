dominus:/ˈdɒmɪnəs/|/ˈdɑːmɪnəs/| n. |主人，统治者，支配者 |A dominus is a master or ruler, often implying a position of authority and control over others.  The term often carries connotations of ownership and power, sometimes even suggesting harshness or tyranny.  It is derived from Latin and has historical and legal significance.|例句：The Roman dominus held absolute power over his slaves. 罗马的主人对他的奴隶拥有绝对权力。|例句：He acted as dominus over his family's business affairs. 他掌控着家族企业的业务。|近义词：master, lord, ruler, sovereign, potentate |反义词：slave, servant, subject
