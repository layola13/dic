shears:/ʃɪrz/| n. v.|大剪刀；剪；修剪|Large scissors used for shearing wool from sheep or cutting other materials; to cut with shears; to cut something short.  |例句：The farmer used shears to trim the hedges. = 农民用大剪刀修剪树篱。
The tailor shears the cloth precisely. =裁缝精确地剪裁布料。|近义词：scissors, clippers, shears|反义词：sew, mend


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He shears the sheep every spring. = 他每年春天都剪羊毛。
She shears the fabric with precision. = 她精确地剪裁布料。


一般过去时:
The barber sheared my hair too short. = 理发师把我的头发剪得太短了。
They sheared the fleece from the sheep. = 他们剪下了羊的羊毛。


现在完成时:
I have sheared all the wool from the sheep. = 我已经把羊身上的羊毛都剪完了。
She has sheared the fabric to the desired length. = 她已将织物剪裁到所需的长度。


过去完成时:

They had sheared all the wool before the festival began. = 节日开始前，他们已经剪完了所有的羊毛。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
