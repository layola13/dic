penny-farthing:/ˈpeniˌfɑːrθɪŋ/| n. |高轮车|A penny-farthing bicycle, also known as a high-wheel bicycle or a high-wheeler, is a type of bicycle with a large front wheel and a small rear wheel.  The name comes from the fact that the large front wheel was typically about the size of a farthing (a small British coin), and the smaller rear wheel was about the size of a penny. This design was popular in the late 19th century.|Example: The penny-farthing was a popular mode of transportation in the Victorian era.  The museum showcased a beautifully restored penny-farthing bicycle.|近义词：high-wheel bicycle, high-wheeler|反义词：safety bicycle (with equal-sized wheels)
