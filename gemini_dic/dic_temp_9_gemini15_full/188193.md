rowage:/ˈroʊɪdʒ/ | n. | 浆; 船桨推进 |  Rowage refers to the act or process of propelling a boat or ship using oars.  It can also refer to the distance covered by rowing. | Example: The rowage was arduous, but they finally reached the shore. 浆行过程很艰难，但他们最终到达了岸边。|近义词：oaring, propulsion | 反义词：None directly applicable.
