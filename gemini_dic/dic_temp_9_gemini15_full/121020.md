miscommunication:/ˌmɪskəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |误解，沟通不畅|The failure to communicate effectively, resulting in misunderstanding.  This can stem from unclear language, differing interpretations, or a breakdown in the communication process.|例句：The project failed due to miscommunication between the design and engineering teams.  项目由于设计和工程团队之间的沟通不畅而失败。


例句：Miscommunication led to a significant delay in the product launch.  沟通不畅导致产品发布严重延误。


近义词：misunderstanding, misinterpretation, confusion, lack of communication|反义词：clear communication, understanding, agreement|词性:名词
