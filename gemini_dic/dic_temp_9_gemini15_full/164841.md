preforgave:/ˌpriːfɔːˈɡeɪv/| v.|预先原谅；提前宽恕|This word is not a standard English word. It's a combination of "pre-" (before) and "forgave" (past tense of forgive).  It implies forgiving someone before an offense has even occurred, or before the full extent of the offense is known. This usage is uncommon and might be considered non-standard or informal.  The meaning is derived from its constituent parts. |例句：She preforgave him, knowing his impulsive nature. = 她预先原谅了他，知道他冲动易怒的性格。  He preforgave her potential mistakes, believing in her capacity for growth. = 他预先原谅了她可能犯的错误，相信她有成长能力。|近义词：pardon in advance, anticipate forgiveness|反义词：prejudge, condemn beforehand


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I forgive you. = 我原谅你。
We forgive our friends' mistakes. = 我们原谅朋友们的错误。

一般过去时:
She forgave him for his lies. = 她原谅了他说谎。
They forgave their son for his bad grades. = 他们原谅了他们儿子不好的成绩。

现在完成时:
I have forgiven him many times. = 我已经原谅他很多次了。
She has forgiven her parents for their past mistakes. = 她已经原谅了她父母过去的错误。

过去完成时:
I had forgiven her before she apologized. = 我在她道歉之前就原谅了她。
They had forgiven him before they knew the whole story. = 在他们了解事情的全貌之前就原谅了他。

将来时:
I will forgive you if you apologize. = 如果你道歉我会原谅你。
She will forgive him, eventually. = 她最终会原谅他的。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would forgive him. = 如果我是你，我会原谅他。
I wish I had forgiven her sooner. = 我希望我早点原谅她。

被动语态:
He was forgiven by his wife. = 他被妻子原谅了。
Their mistakes were forgiven by their teacher. = 他们的错误被老师原谅了。

疑问句:
Will you forgive me? = 你会原谅我吗？
Have they forgiven her yet? = 他们已经原谅她了吗？

否定句:
I won't forgive you this time. = 这次我不会原谅你。
She didn't forgive him easily. = 她没有轻易原谅他。
