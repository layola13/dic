laocoön:/ˈleɪəˌkɒn/| n. |拉奥孔|A Trojan priest who, in Greek mythology, warned against bringing the Trojan Horse into Troy.  He and his sons were killed by sea serpents sent by Poseidon or Apollo.  The name is also used to refer to a famous Hellenistic sculpture depicting this scene. |例句：The Laocoön group is a masterpiece of Hellenistic sculpture.  拉奥孔群雕是希腊化时期雕塑的杰作。The story of Laocoön serves as a cautionary tale. 拉奥孔的故事是一个警示故事。|近义词：priest (in the context of his role), victim|反义词：N/A
