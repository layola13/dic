oversleeps:/ˈoʊvərˈsliːpz/ | v. |睡过头；睡懒觉| To sleep longer than intended or necessary.  |例句：I oversleep every Sunday and miss breakfast.  我每个星期天都睡过头，错过了早餐。
I overslept this morning and was late for work.  我今天早上睡过头了，上班迟到了。|近义词：sleep in, lie in |反义词：wake up early


USAGE->
