regrouped:/ˌriːˈɡruːpt/| v. adj.|重新组合，重新分组；重整旗鼓| To gather again in a new formation or group; to reorganize.|例句：The soldiers regrouped after the initial attack.  士兵在最初的攻击后重新集结。|例句：The team regrouped and developed a new strategy.  团队重新组合并制定了新的策略。|近义词：reorganize, reorganize, reform, rally|反义词：scatter, disperse|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team regroups after every quarter. = 团队在每一节之后都会重新组合。
They regroup regularly to discuss strategy. = 他们定期重新组合以讨论策略。

一般过去时:
The army regrouped after the battle. = 军队在战斗后重新集结。
The students regrouped into smaller groups for the activity. = 学生们为活动重新分成更小的组。

现在完成时:
The company has regrouped and is now more profitable. = 公司已经重整旗鼓，现在盈利更多了。
We have regrouped our resources to focus on this project. = 我们已经重新分配了资源以专注于这个项目。

过去完成时:
They had regrouped before the enemy launched a counterattack. = 敌军发动反攻之前，他们已经重新集结。
The company had regrouped its marketing strategy after the product launch failed. = 产品发布失败后，公司已经重新调整了其营销策略。


将来时:
The team will regroup after the game. = 比赛结束后，团队将重新集结。
We will regroup and discuss the next steps. = 我们将重新组合并讨论接下来的步骤。

虚拟语气:
If they had regrouped sooner, they might have won the battle. = 如果他们早点重新集结，他们可能就赢得了战斗。
If we regrouped our forces, we could achieve more. = 如果我们重整旗鼓，我们可以取得更多成就。

被动语态:
The troops were regrouped under the cover of darkness. = 部队在黑暗的掩护下重新集结。
The resources were regrouped to meet the urgent needs. = 资源被重新分配以满足紧急需求。

疑问句:

否定句:
The team did not regroup after the game. = 球队在比赛后没有重新集结。
They didn't regroup effectively after the setback. = 他们在挫折后没有有效地重新组合。
