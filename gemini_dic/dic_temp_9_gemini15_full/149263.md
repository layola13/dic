oysterling:/ˈɔɪstərlɪŋ/| n.|小牡蛎；牡蛎苗|A young oyster.  Oysterlings are typically smaller than adult oysters and are often raised in aquaculture settings before being released into the wild or harvested.|例句：The oysterlings were carefully placed in the oyster farm.  这些牡蛎苗被小心地放在牡蛎养殖场里。|例句：The researchers studied the growth rate of the oysterlings in different water temperatures. 研究人员研究了不同水温下牡蛎苗的生长速度。|近义词：oyster spat, juvenile oyster|反义词：adult oyster
