shaer:/ʃɛər/| n. |份额，股份；共享；(动物的)毛；| a portion or part of something, especially one that has been divided among several people; the act of sharing something;  the fur or hair of an animal |例句：I have a share in the company. 我在这家公司有股份。|近义词：portion, part, quota |反义词：whole, entirety |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shares his toys with his brother. = 他和弟弟分享他的玩具。
They share a flat. = 他们合住一套公寓。

一般过去时:
She shared her experiences with us. = 她和我们分享了她的经历。
We shared a pizza. = 我们一起吃了一块披萨。

现在完成时:
I have shared my ideas with the team. = 我已经和团队分享了我的想法。
They have shared many happy moments together. = 他们一起分享了许多快乐的时光。

过去完成时:
He had shared his secret with her before the party. = 他在派对前已经把秘密告诉了她。
They had shared a room during their trip. = 他们在旅行期间合住一个房间。

将来时:
We will share the cost equally. = 我们将平均分担费用。
She will share her knowledge with the students. = 她将把她的知识与学生分享。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would share my story with you. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把我的故事告诉你。
I wish I could share your happiness. = 我希望我能分享你的快乐。

被动语态:
The profits are shared equally among the partners. = 利润在合伙人之间平均分配。
The information was shared with the public. = 信息已与公众分享。

疑问句:
Do you want to share your food with me? = 你想和我分享你的食物吗？
Will you share your thoughts on this matter? = 你愿意分享你对这件事的看法吗？

否定句:
He doesn't share his feelings easily. = 他不容易分享他的感受。
They didn't share the secret with anyone else. = 他们没有把秘密告诉任何人。



PHRASE->
share price = 股票价格

share of voice = 声音份额（营销领域）
social sharing = 社交分享
