graphed:/ɡræft/ | v. | 绘制图表；用图表表示 | To represent (data, etc.) in the form of a graph.  | The data was graphed to show the trend. 数据被绘制成图表以显示趋势。  | plotted, charted | uncharted | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They graph the data every day. = 他们每天绘制数据图表。
We graph the results of the experiment. = 我们绘制实验结果图表。

一般过去时:
She graphed the sales figures last week. = 她上周绘制了销售数据图表。
He graphed the points on the coordinate plane. = 他在坐标平面上绘制了这些点。

现在完成时:
I have graphed all the necessary data. = 我已经绘制了所有必要的数据图表。
They have graphed the results and presented them. = 他们已经绘制了结果图表并进行了展示。
