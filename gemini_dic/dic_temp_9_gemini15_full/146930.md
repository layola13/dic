overintensification:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈtɛnsɪfɪkeɪʃən/ | n. |过度强化，过度密集 |The excessive strengthening or concentration of something, often to a detrimental effect.  For example, overintensification of agricultural practices can lead to soil degradation. |The overintensification of farming led to soil erosion and water pollution.  过度集约化农业导致土壤侵蚀和水污染。  | intensification, exaggeration | moderation, underdevelopment | 名词
