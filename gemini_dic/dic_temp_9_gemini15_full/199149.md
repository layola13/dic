silicate:/ˈsɪlɪkeɪt/| n. |硅酸盐|A salt of silicic acid, containing silicon and oxygen, and usually one or more metals. Silicates are the most abundant minerals in the Earth's crust.  |例句：Many rocks are composed primarily of silicate minerals. 许多岩石主要由硅酸盐矿物组成。|近义词：siliceous|反义词：无
