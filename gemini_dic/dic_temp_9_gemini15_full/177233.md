reasoning:/ˈriːzənɪŋ/| n.|推理；论证；理由|The process of thinking about something in a logical way in order to form a conclusion or judgment.  It involves using logic and evidence to support a claim or solve a problem. |例句：His reasoning was flawed because he ignored crucial evidence. 他的推理有缺陷，因为他忽略了关键证据。

例句：The judge carefully considered the reasoning behind the defendant's plea.  法官仔细考虑了被告申辩背后的理由。


近义词：inference, deduction, argumentation, logic, rationale

反义词：illogic, nonsense, absurdity
