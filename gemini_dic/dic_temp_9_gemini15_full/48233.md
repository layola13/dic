decontaminate:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪt/| vt.|净化；消毒；去污|To remove contaminants from something, making it clean and safe.  The process involves removing or neutralizing harmful substances such as bacteria, viruses, chemicals, or radiation.|例句：The team worked tirelessly to decontaminate the area after the chemical spill.  团队不懈努力，在化学品泄漏后对该区域进行了净化。
The hospital staff followed strict protocols to decontaminate surgical instruments. 医院工作人员遵循严格的规程对外科器械进行消毒。|近义词：purify, sterilize, cleanse|反义词：contaminate
