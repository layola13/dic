gleeing:/ˈɡliːɪŋ/| adj.|洋溢着快乐的；兴高采烈的|Full of glee; merry; joyful.  It describes a state of great happiness and excitement.|例句：Her face was gleaming with delight after receiving the good news.  她收到好消息后脸上洋溢着喜悦。
