roughleg:/ˈrʌfleɡ/| n.|粗腿雕（鹰的一种）|A rough-legged hawk is a bird of prey with feathered legs.  They are known for their distinctive feathered legs, which are noticeably more feathered than those of other hawks.  They are found in the northern hemisphere. |Example:  The roughleg hawk circled high above the snowy field.  粗腿雕在雪地高空盘旋。  The photographer captured a stunning image of a roughleg in flight. 摄影师捕捉到一张粗腿雕飞行时的精彩照片。|近义词： hawk, buzzard|反义词：无
