necessitated:/nəˈsɛsɪteɪtɪd/| v.| 使成为必要；需要；迫使 | to make something necessary | The urgency of the situation necessitated immediate action.  紧急情况需要立即采取行动。| required, demanded, compelled | optional, unnecessary | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project necessitates careful planning. = 这个项目需要仔细的计划。
This situation necessitates a change in our strategy. = 这种情况需要我们改变策略。


一般过去时:
The storm necessitated the cancellation of the flight. = 暴风雨迫使航班取消。
The increase in demand necessitated hiring more staff. = 需求增加迫使我们雇佣更多员工。


现在完成时:
Recent events have necessitated a review of our security protocols. = 最近发生的事件迫使我们审查安全协议。
The crisis has necessitated a major shift in government policy. = 危机迫使政府政策发生重大转变。


过去完成时:
The previous drought had necessitated water rationing. = 先前的干旱已经迫使人们实行限水。
The delay had necessitated a complete rescheduling of the conference. = 延误迫使会议完全重新安排。


将来时:
The new regulations will necessitate a complete overhaul of our systems. = 新规将需要我们彻底检修系统。
Future growth will necessitate further investment in infrastructure. = 未来的增长将需要进一步投资基础设施。


虚拟语气:
If the budget were larger, it wouldn't necessitate such drastic cuts. = 如果预算更大，就不会需要如此大幅度的削减。
If the situation were different, it would not necessitate such a response. = 如果情况不同，就不会需要这样的回应。


被动语态:
Immediate action was necessitated by the fire. = 大火迫使人们立即采取行动。
Further investigation is necessitated by the conflicting evidence. = 互相矛盾的证据需要进一步调查。


疑问句:
Does the situation necessitate immediate action? = 情况需要立即采取行动吗？
Does this problem necessitate a new approach? = 这个问题需要一种新的方法来解决吗？


否定句:
The situation did not necessitate any drastic measures. = 这种情况不需要采取任何严厉措施。
The project does not necessitate the purchase of new equipment. = 这个项目不需要购买新设备。


PHRASE->
be necessitated by = 由…引起；因…而需要
necessitated by circumstances = 由于环境所迫
necessitated by events = 由于事件所迫
necessitated by the situation = 由于形势所迫
necessitated by the need to = 由于需要…而必须
