overanimation: /ˌoʊvərænɪˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度动画；动画过度 | Excessive animation;  the state of having too much animation or movement, often making something appear unnatural or distracting.  It can refer to overly detailed or busy animation, or animation that draws undue attention to itself rather than serving the narrative. |  The film suffered from overanimation, with distracting elements competing for the viewer's attention.  The scene was marred by overanimation; the characters' jerky movements were unrealistic. |  excessive movement, hyperactivity | underanimation, stillness | 名词
