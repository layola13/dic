porthole:/ˈpɔːrtˌhoʊl/| n. |舷窗，船舱窗 |A porthole is a small, round window in the side of a ship or aircraft.  |The porthole offered a glimpse of the stormy sea.  舷窗可以看到暴风雨的海。  The captain peered through the porthole. 船长透过舷窗凝视着。|window, scuttle | (无明确反义词，可考虑closed window)|名词

词源:
From Middle English *portehole, from port (meaning "a harbor or gate") + hole.  The word evolved from the idea of a "gate-shaped hole" in a ship's side.
