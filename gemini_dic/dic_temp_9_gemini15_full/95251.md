incandescents:/ˌɪnkænˈdɛsnt(s)/| n.|白炽的；发光的；发出白炽光的物体|Emitting light as a result of being heated.  Incandescents refer to objects that glow with intense heat, such as a light bulb filament. The plural form, "incandescents," refers to multiple such objects.|The incandescent lights in the old theater created a warm, inviting atmosphere.  老剧院里的白炽灯营造出温暖舒适的氛围。|glowing, luminous, radiant|dark, dull, opaque|名词
