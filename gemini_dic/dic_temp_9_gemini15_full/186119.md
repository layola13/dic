rhachis:/ˈreɪkɪs/| n. |轴，主干；（植物的）花轴，叶轴；（动物的）脊柱|The main stem or axis of a plant or animal.  It refers to the central supporting structure of something. In botany, it specifically refers to the main axis of a plant, such as the stem of a flower or the rachis of a fern frond. In zoology, it commonly refers to the spine. |例句：The rhachis of the fern was strong and sturdy.  蕨类的叶轴强壮而坚固。
The doctor examined the patient's rhachis for any signs of injury.医生检查了病人的脊柱是否有任何受伤迹象。|近义词：axis, spine, stem, backbone|反义词：branch (in botany context)
