pyroantimonate: /ˌpaɪroʊænˈtɪməneɪt/ | n. | 焦锑酸盐 | Pyroantimonate is a salt of pyroantimonic acid (H₄Sb₂O₇).  It's commonly used in analytical chemistry, notably for the detection of potassium ions. The potassium pyroantimonate test is a precipitation reaction where the addition of potassium pyroantimonate solution to a solution containing potassium ions results in the formation of a crystalline precipitate of potassium pyroantimonate. | Example: Potassium pyroantimonate is used to detect the presence of potassium ions in a solution. 钾焦锑酸盐用于检测溶液中钾离子的存在。 | 近义词: potassium pyroantimonate (more specific) | 反义词:  N/A (no direct antonym)
