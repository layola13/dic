quartette:/kwɑːˈtɛt/|/ˈkwɔːrtet/| n. |四重奏；四重唱；四人一组 |A quartette is a musical composition for four instruments or voices, or a group of four performers.  It can also refer to a group of four people. |Example: The string quartette played a beautiful piece by Mozart.  弦乐四重奏演奏了一首莫扎特的优美乐曲。 The singing quartette harmonized perfectly.  这个四重唱的和声非常完美。|近义词： quartet, quadruplet |反义词：solo, duet, trio
