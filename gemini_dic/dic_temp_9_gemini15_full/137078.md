nonstructurally:/ˌnɒnstrʌkˈtʃərəli/ | adv. |非结构地，无组织地 | In a way that is not based on or organized around a structure or framework.  It emphasizes the lack of a defined or systematic arrangement.  | The data was collected nonstructurally, making analysis difficult. 数据是非结构化收集的，这使得分析变得困难。 | systematically, randomly, haphazardly | structurally, systematically, methodically | 副词
