romeo:/ˈroʊmioʊ/| n. |罗密欧|A male given name, also the name of the male protagonist in Shakespeare's play "Romeo and Juliet".  Romeo is a passionate and impulsive young man, caught in a tragic love affair.|Example:  My friend's name is Romeo. 我的朋友名叫罗密欧。|近义词：None (as a proper noun)|反义词：None (as a proper noun)|名词


USAGE->
This word, being a proper noun, doesn't conjugate through tenses.  The usage is limited to its role as a name.


PHRASE->
Romeo and Juliet: 罗密欧与朱丽叶 (Shakespeare's play)
Romeo Montague: 罗密欧·蒙太古 (character's full name)
star-crossed lovers:  命运多舛的情侣 (referring to Romeo and Juliet)
a Romeo:  一个罗密欧式的人物 (a romantic, passionate, perhaps impulsive man)
true love: 真爱 (often associated with Romeo and Juliet's relationship)
