comeback:/ˈkʌmbæk/| n., v.|卷土重来；东山再起；反驳；回应|

英文解析:A comeback is a return to a previous level of success or popularity, or a successful return after a period of absence or decline.  It can also refer to a witty or effective reply.

例句:
After a long illness, she made a remarkable comeback to the stage.  一场大病之后，她令人瞩目地重返舞台。

His sharp comeback silenced his critics. 他尖锐的反驳使批评者哑口无言。


近义词:return, revival, recovery, retort, reply

反义词:failure, defeat, downfall


词性: noun, verb
