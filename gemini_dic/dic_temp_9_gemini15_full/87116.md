herbaged:/ˈhɜːrbeɪdʒd/| adj.|草木茂盛的；长满草的|Covered with or consisting of herbs or grass.|The hillside was herbaged and vibrant with wildflowers.|近义词：grassy, verdant, leafy|反义词：barren, desolate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The field is herbaged. = 田野长满了草。
The meadow is herbaged with wildflowers. = 草地长满了野花。


一般过去时:
The field was herbaged last spring. = 去年春天田野长满了草。
The area was herbaged after the rain. = 雨后该地区长满了草。


现在完成时:
The field has been herbaged since the rain. = 雨后田野一直长满了草。
The hillside has become herbaged this year. = 山坡今年长满了草。
