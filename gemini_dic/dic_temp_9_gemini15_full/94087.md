impatience:/ɪmˈpeɪʃəns/| n. | 不耐烦，急躁 | a feeling of annoyance caused by someone's inability to accept delay or waiting | Her impatience was evident in her restless movements. 她焦躁不安的动作显露出了她的不耐烦。|近义词：irritability, restlessness, anxiety |反义词：patience, calmness, forbearance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His impatience shows in his constant fidgeting. = 他总是坐立不安，显示了他的不耐烦。
I understand her impatience with the slow service. = 我理解她对缓慢的服务感到不耐烦。


一般过去时:
His impatience led him to make a rash decision. = 他的急躁导致他做出了草率的决定。
Their impatience with the negotiations caused a breakdown in talks. = 他们对谈判的不耐烦导致了谈判破裂。
