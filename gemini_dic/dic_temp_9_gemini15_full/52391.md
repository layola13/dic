didactics:/daɪˈdæktɪks/ | n. |教学法；教学论 |The study and practice of teaching.  Didactics explores effective teaching methods, curriculum design, and the overall process of learning and instruction.  It often involves research into how students learn best and how teachers can adapt their methods accordingly. |例句：The course focused on the didactics of teaching English as a second language. 这门课程侧重于英语作为第二语言教学法。

例句：Modern didactics emphasizes active learning and student engagement. 现代教学法强调主动学习和学生参与。


近义词：pedagogy, teaching methodology | 反义词：None directly applicable.
