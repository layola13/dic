ecosystem:/ˈiːkoʊsɪstəm/ | n. | 生态系统 | A biological community of interacting organisms and their physical environment.  An ecosystem includes all living things (plants, animals, bacteria, fungi) in a specific area, along with the nonliving parts of that environment (air, water, soil, sunlight) interacting together. | Example: The Amazon rainforest is a complex ecosystem with incredible biodiversity. 亚马逊雨林是一个生物多样性极高的复杂生态系统。  The coral reef ecosystem is threatened by pollution and climate change. 珊瑚礁生态系统受到污染和气候变化的威胁。 |近义词：environment, habitat, biosphere | 反义词：N/A
