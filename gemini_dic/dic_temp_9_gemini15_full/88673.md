hoax:/hoʊks/| n., vt.| 骗局；恶作剧；戏弄 | A hoax is a deliberate deception, intended to trick or mislead someone, often for amusement or malicious purposes.  It can be a practical joke, a false story, or a fabricated event. |例句：The moon landing was once claimed to be a hoax.  月球着陆曾经被宣称是一个骗局。|例句：He played a hoax on his friends by pretending to be a famous actor. 他假扮成著名演员来戏弄他的朋友。|近义词：trick, deception, fraud, prank |反义词：truth, reality, genuineness
