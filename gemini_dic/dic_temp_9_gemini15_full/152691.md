pearl-like: /ˈpɜːrllaɪk/ | adj. | 像珍珠的，珍珠似的 | Resembling a pearl in appearance; having the smooth, lustrous quality of a pearl.  |例句：Her skin had a pearl-like sheen. 她的皮肤有着珍珠般的光泽。

The necklace featured pearl-like beads. 那条项链饰有珍珠般的珠子。|近义词：pearly, lustrous, iridescent, pearly white |反义词：dull, matte, rough

词源:

The word "pearl-like" is a compound adjective formed from the noun "pearl" and the suffix "-like," which indicates resemblance.  The word "pearl" itself has roots in Old French and ultimately in Latin.
