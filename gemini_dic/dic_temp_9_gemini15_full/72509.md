fluosilicate:/ˌfluːoʊˈsɪlɪkeɪt/ | n. |氟硅酸盐|A salt of fluorosilicic acid (H₂SiF₆). Fluorosilicates are commonly used in various applications, including water fluoridation, as a pesticide, and in some ceramic and metallurgical processes.  They are generally considered to be relatively non-toxic at low concentrations, but high concentrations can be harmful.|例句：Sodium fluorosilicate is often used in municipal water treatment to prevent tooth decay.  氟硅酸钠常用于市政供水处理以预防龋齿。
The industrial application of fluorosilicates requires careful handling due to potential health risks.  氟硅酸盐的工业应用需要小心处理，因为存在潜在的健康风险。|近义词：fluoride compound|反义词：无
