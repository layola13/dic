/ˌnɒnˌfɔːrˈkloʊʒərz/ | n. | 非法拍|  Properties that have not gone through the foreclosure process.  These properties are still owned by the original borrower and have not been repossessed by the lender.  | 例句：The bank is focusing on working with borrowers to avoid nonforeclosures.  银行正专注于与借款人合作，以避免非法拍。


The number of nonforeclosures has increased recently due to government intervention.  由于政府干预，最近非法拍的数量有所增加。| 近义词：non-repossessions | 反义词：foreclosures
