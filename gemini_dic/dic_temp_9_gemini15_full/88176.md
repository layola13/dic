hillwalker:/ˈhɪlˌwɔːkər/| n. |徒步旅行者，登山者|A person who walks in hills, especially as a leisure activity.| Example:  He's an experienced hillwalker and knows the trails well. 他是一位经验丰富的徒步旅行者，并且熟悉小路。  She's a keen hillwalker and enjoys the challenge of the mountains. 她是一位热衷于徒步旅行的人，并且享受山区的挑战。|近义词：hiker, mountaineer (for more challenging terrain), rambler|反义词：sedentary person
