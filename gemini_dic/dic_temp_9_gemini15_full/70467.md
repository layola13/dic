figurist:/ˈfɪɡjʊrɪst/| n. |造型师，雕塑家，数字专家|A person skilled in creating or interpreting figures, especially in art or mathematics. The choreographer worked closely with the figurist to ensure the dancers’ movements were precise and expressive.  编舞师与造型师密切合作，以确保舞者动作的精准和表达力。
