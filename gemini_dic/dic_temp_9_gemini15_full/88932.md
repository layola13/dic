holdfast: /ˈhoʊldˌfæst/ | n. |固着器；附着器；抓手| A holdfast is an organ, structure, or device that firmly attaches something to a surface or substrate. It is commonly used to describe the root-like structures of certain organisms, such as algae and fungi, that anchor them to rocks, wood, or other surfaces. It can also refer to a mechanical device used for securing something in place.  | 例句：The limpet uses its powerful holdfast to cling to rocks in the intertidal zone.  海螺用它强大的附着器紧紧地吸附在潮间带的岩石上。

The climber secured the rope to the holdfast on the cliff face. 登山者将绳索固定在悬崖峭壁上的抓手上。| 近义词：attachment, anchor, grip, fastener | 反义词：release, detachment
