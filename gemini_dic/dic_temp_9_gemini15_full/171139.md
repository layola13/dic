psychoanalytical: /ˌsaɪkoʊˌænəˈlɪtɪkəl/ | adj. | 心理分析的；精神分析的 | Relating to or characteristic of psychoanalysis.  Psychoanalysis is a system of psychological theory and therapy that aims to treat mental disorders by investigating the interaction of conscious and unconscious elements in the mind and bringing repressed fears and conflicts into the conscious mind by techniques such as dream interpretation and free association. | Example: His psychoanalytical approach to understanding human behavior was groundbreaking.  他的心理分析方法对理解人类行为具有开创性意义。 | 近义词: psychodynamic, Freudian | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与行为主义或认知疗法等相对)
