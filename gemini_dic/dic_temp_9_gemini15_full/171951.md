pupping:/ˈpʌpɪŋ/| n. v.|幼犬的诞生；(动物)产崽；|The act of a female animal giving birth to puppies; the period of time when a female animal is giving birth to puppies.  Also refers to the process of young animals emerging from their mother.|例句：The dog is pupping; we expect six puppies. 这只狗正在生产，我们预计会有六只小狗。

The mother cat was pupping throughout the night. 母猫整夜都在生产。|近义词：whelping, birthing, giving birth|反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog is pupping right now.  这只狗现在正在生产。
She pups every year in the spring. 她每年春天都生小狗。

一般过去时:
The bitch pupped last night. 母犬昨晚生产了。
My cat pupped three kittens. 我的猫生了三只小猫。

现在完成时:
She has pupped three times this year. 她今年已经生了三次小狗了。
The dog has just pupped. 这只狗刚刚生完小狗。

过去完成时:
The vet had already arrived before the dog pupped.  兽医在狗生产之前就已经到了。
She had pupped twice before she died. 她死前已经生了两次小狗了。

将来时:
The dog will pup next month. 这只狗下个月要生产了。
We expect her to pup sometime in the spring. 我们预计她会在春天某个时候生产。


虚拟语气:
If she pupped today, we would be very happy. 如果她今天生产，我们会非常高兴。
I wish the dog would pup soon. 我希望这只狗尽快生产。


被动语态:
The puppies were pupped in a warm, safe place. 小狗在一个温暖安全的地方出生。 (This is a less common usage, as "pupped" is usually active.)


疑问句:
Is the dog pupping now? 狗现在正在生产吗？
When will the cat pup? 猫什么时候生产？


否定句:
The dog isn't pupping yet. 狗还没生产。
She didn't pup last year. 她去年没有生产。
