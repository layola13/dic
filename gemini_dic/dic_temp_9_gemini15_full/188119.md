roundup:/ˈraʊndʌp/ | n., vt. | 综述；总结；围捕；收集 | To gather or bring together; a summary or review of events. | The police conducted a roundup of suspected criminals.  警方对涉嫌犯罪的人进行了围捕。 | The newspaper published a roundup of the week's news. 这家报纸发表了本周新闻的综述。 | summary, compilation, collection | dispersal, scattering | noun, verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer rounds up his cattle every evening. = 农夫每天晚上都把他的牛赶到一起。
She rounds up the key points of the meeting in her report. = 她在报告中总结了会议的要点。


一般过去时:
The police rounded up the suspects after the robbery. = 抢劫案发生后，警方逮捕了嫌疑人。
He rounded up all the stray dogs in the neighborhood. = 他把附近所有流浪狗都抓了起来。


现在完成时:
They have rounded up all the necessary documents for the application. = 他们已经收集齐了申请所需的所有文件。
She has rounded up a good team for the project. = 她为这个项目组建了一支优秀的团队。


过去完成时:
The soldiers had rounded up the prisoners before the attack. = 士兵们在袭击前已经抓住了俘虏。
The reporter had rounded up all the facts before writing the article. = 记者在撰写文章之前已经收集了所有事实。


将来时:
The company will round up its financial results at the end of the year. = 公司将在年底总结财务业绩。
They will round up the sheep before nightfall. = 他们会在夜幕降临前把羊赶拢。


虚拟语气:
If the police had rounded up the criminals sooner, the damage would have been less. = 如果警方早点逮捕罪犯，损失就会少一些。
I wish I had rounded up the information earlier. = 我希望我早点收集好信息。


被动语态:
The suspects were rounded up by the police. = 嫌疑人被警方逮捕了。
The stray animals were rounded up and taken to the shelter. = 流浪动物被收容起来送往收容所。


疑问句:
Will they round up the cattle before the storm? = 暴风雨来临之前他们会把牛赶到一起吗？
Have you rounded up all the necessary materials? = 你已经收集好所有必要的材料了吗？


否定句:
They did not round up all the sheep. = 他们没有把所有的羊都赶拢。
The company hasn't yet rounded up its annual report. = 公司还没有完成年度报告的总结。
