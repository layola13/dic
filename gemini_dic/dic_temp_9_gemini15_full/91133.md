hybridists:/ˈhaɪbrɪdɪsts/| n.|杂交育种者；杂交专家|People who specialize in creating hybrids, especially in plants or animals.  They are involved in the science and art of cross-breeding different species or varieties to produce offspring with desirable traits.|例句：The hybridists carefully selected parent plants to create a new rose with larger blooms and a stronger fragrance. 杂交育种者精心挑选亲本植物，培育出花朵更大、香味更浓郁的新型玫瑰。|近义词：breeders, geneticists|反义词：purebred breeders (in a contrasting sense)
