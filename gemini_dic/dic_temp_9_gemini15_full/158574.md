pleuritic:/pluːˈraɪtɪk/| adj.|胸膜炎的；胸膜的；|Relating to or characteristic of pleurisy.  The pain is sharp, stabbing, and worsens with deep breaths or coughing.|例句：He experienced pleuritic chest pain. 他经历了胸膜炎引起的胸痛。

例句：The doctor diagnosed her condition as pleuritic. 医生诊断她的病情为胸膜炎。
