monander:/ˈmɒnændə(r)/| n. |单蕊植物|A monander is a plant with only one stamen (the male reproductive organ in a flower).  It refers to a flower having a single stamen.  The term is primarily used in botanical classification and description.|  The botanist carefully examined the flower, noting that it was a monander. = 植物学家仔细检查了花朵，注意到它是一种单蕊植物。 The classification of this species as a monander is based on its unique floral structure. = 将该物种分类为单蕊植物是基于其独特的floral结构。|近义词：uni-staminate |反义词：polyander
