monoculture:/ˈmɒnəʊkʌltʃə(r)/|/ˈmɑːnəʊkʌltʃər/| n. |单一栽培；单一文化；单一作物种植|The practice of growing or raising a single crop or livestock species, often on a large scale.  This can lead to increased efficiency in the short term but also makes the system vulnerable to pests, diseases, and market fluctuations.|例句：The reliance on monoculture farming has depleted the soil nutrients.  单一作物种植导致土壤养分枯竭。|例句：The homogeneity of the monoculture threatened the biodiversity of the region. 单一文化的同质性威胁到该地区的生物多样性。|近义词：single-crop farming, uniformity, homogeneity|反义词：polyculture, biodiversity, diversity|名词
