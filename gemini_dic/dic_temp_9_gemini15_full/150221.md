pandas:/ˈpændəz/| n.|熊猫；（复数）熊猫|Pandas are large black and white bears native to China.  熊猫是大熊猫，原产于中国。|近义词：giant panda|反义词：无


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Pandas eat bamboo. = 熊猫吃竹子。
Pandas live in mountainous regions. = 熊猫生活在山区。


一般过去时:
Pandas roamed freely in the past. = 熊猫过去自由漫游。
The zoo acquired two pandas last year. = 动物园去年引进了两只熊猫。


现在完成时:
Scientists have studied pandas for decades. = 科学家们几十年来一直在研究熊猫。
Conservation efforts have saved pandas from extinction. = 保护工作拯救了熊猫免于灭绝。


过去完成时:
Before the conservation efforts, pandas had faced severe threats. = 在保护工作开展之前，熊猫面临着严重的威胁。
The population had declined significantly before the protection programs started. = 在保护项目启动之前，熊猫的数量已经大幅下降。


将来时:
Scientists will continue to monitor panda populations. = 科学家们将继续监测熊猫种群数量。
We will see more pandas in zoos in the future. = 未来我们将看到更多熊猫在动物园里。


虚拟语气:
If pandas were not protected, they would be extinct. = 如果熊猫没有得到保护，它们就会灭绝。
I wish pandas were easier to see in the wild. = 我希望在野外更容易看到熊猫。


被动语态:
Pandas are loved by people worldwide. = 熊猫受到全世界人民的喜爱。
New habitats are being created for pandas. = 正在为熊猫创造新的栖息地。


疑问句:
Are pandas endangered? = 熊猫濒临灭绝吗？

否定句:

Pandas do not eat meat. = 熊猫不吃肉。
