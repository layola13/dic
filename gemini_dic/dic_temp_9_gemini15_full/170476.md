pruniferous:/pruːˈnɪfərəs/| adj.|结果实的，多果实的|Bearing or producing fruit, especially plums or similar stone fruit.  The word refers to plants that produce fruits with a stone or pit, such as plums, cherries, peaches, etc.|例句：The pruniferous orchard was heavy with the weight of ripening plums.  这个结果实的果园里挂满了成熟的李子。

例句：Many pruniferous trees were planted along the roadside.  路边种了许多结果实的树。


近义词：fruit-bearing, fruitful, productive|反义词：barren, sterile, unproductive|词性：形容词
