perils:/ˈperəlz/ | n. |危险，风险，危害 | Dangers or threats; a source of danger.  | Example: The perils of mountain climbing are well known. 登山运动的危险众所周知。  The perils of drug abuse are severe. 滥用毒品的危害是严重的。|近义词：dangers, hazards, risks, threats |反义词：safety, security


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The perils of this journey are underestimated. = 这段旅程的危险被低估了。
Many perils threaten our planet. = 许多危险威胁着我们的星球。


一般过去时:
The sailors faced many perils at sea. = 水手们在海上面临许多危险。
He narrowly escaped the perils of the storm. = 他险些逃脱了暴风雨的危险。


现在完成时:
We have overcome many perils on our adventure. = 在我们的冒险中，我们克服了许多危险。
She has faced numerous perils in her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她面临着无数的危险。


过去完成时:
They had encountered many perils before reaching their destination. = 他们在到达目的地之前已经遇到过许多危险。
We had underestimated the perils of the expedition. = 我们低估了探险的危险。


将来时:
The expedition will face many perils ahead. = 探险队将面临许多未来的危险。
Future generations will face new perils related to climate change. = 未来几代人将面临与气候变化相关的新的危险。


虚拟语气:
If we had known the perils, we wouldn't have gone. = 如果我们知道这些危险，我们就不会去了。
I wish we hadn't underestimated the perils involved. = 我真希望我们没有低估所涉及的危险。


被动语态:
The explorers were warned of the perils. = 探险家们被警告了危险。
The dangers were overlooked; the perils were not fully appreciated. = 危险被忽视了；危险没有得到充分的重视。


疑问句:

否定句:

There were no apparent perils on the surface. = 表面上没有明显的危险。
