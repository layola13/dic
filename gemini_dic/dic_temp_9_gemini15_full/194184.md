self-issued:/ˌselfˈɪʃuːd/| adj.| 自发的；自行颁发的；自己签发的 |  Something that is self-issued is created or authorized by the person or entity involved, without external approval or intervention. It implies independence and autonomy in the process. | 例句：He obtained a self-issued certificate to prove his skills. 他获得了一张自发的证书来证明他的技能。

He presented a self-issued permit for the construction. 他出示了一份自己签发的建筑许可证。


近义词：self-authorized, independently issued, self-certified | 反义词：officially issued, externally approved, authorized


词性:形容词
