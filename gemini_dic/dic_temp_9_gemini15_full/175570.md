random:/ˈrændəm/| adj.|随机的，任意的，杂乱的|Occurring without a pattern or plan; haphazard.|The selection of participants was completely random.  参与者的选择完全是随机的。|The books were arranged randomly on the shelves. 书籍杂乱地摆放在书架上。|systematic, deliberate|ordered, planned|形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The computer generates random numbers. = 电脑生成随机数。
He makes random choices. = 他做出了随机的选择。


一般过去时:
She randomly picked a name from the hat. = 她从帽子里随机抽取了一个名字。
They chose a random sample for the survey. = 他们为调查选择了一个随机样本。


现在完成时:
We have selected random participants for the study. = 我们已经为这项研究选择了随机参与者。
They have made several random attempts to solve the problem. = 他们已经尝试了几次随机方法来解决这个问题。


过去完成时:
They had already made a random selection before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就已经做了随机选择。
She had randomly assigned tasks to the team members. = 她已经随机地将任务分配给了团队成员。


将来时:
The system will generate random passwords. = 系统将生成随机密码。
I will randomly select a winner from the entries. = 我将从参赛作品中随机抽取一位获胜者。


虚拟语气:
If I chose a random number, it might be lucky. = 如果我选择一个随机数，它可能很幸运。
I wish I had chosen a random path instead. = 我真希望我选择了另一条随机的路径。


被动语态:
Random numbers were generated by the computer. = 随机数是由电脑生成的。
The questions were randomly selected from the pool. = 问题是从题库中随机选择的。


疑问句:
Is this a random selection? = 这是一个随机选择吗？
Are the results truly random? = 结果真的是随机的吗？


否定句:
The choices were not random; they were carefully planned. = 选择并非随机；它们是经过精心策划的。
The arrangement of the furniture was not random; it was intentional. = 家具的摆放并非随意，是有意的。
