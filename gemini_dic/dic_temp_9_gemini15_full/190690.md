saurel:/ˈsɔːrəl/| n. |鲯鳅|A saurel is a type of small, silvery fish found in warm waters, often used as food.  They are known for their fast swimming ability and schooling behavior.  They are sometimes referred to as scad or jack mackerel, though these terms can also apply to related but distinct species. |The saurel is a popular fish in many coastal cuisines.|近义词：scad, jack mackerel|反义词：无
