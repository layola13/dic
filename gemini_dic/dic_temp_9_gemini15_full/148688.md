overtint:/ˈoʊvərtɪnt/ | v. |过分着色，涂抹过浓|To tint (something) excessively or to an unnatural degree.  This usually refers to coloring something, like hair or a photograph, beyond what is considered natural or aesthetically pleasing. |例句：She overtinted her hair, making it look unnatural. 她把头发染得太过了，看起来不自然。

例句：The photographer overtinted the sunset picture, losing the subtle gradations of color. 摄影师把日落照片的色彩涂抹得太浓了，失去了微妙的色彩渐变。|近义词：oversaturate, overdye, overcolor |反义词：undercolor, undertint
