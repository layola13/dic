patentee:/ˌpætənˈtiː/| n. |专利权人，专利拥有者 | A person or company who holds a patent.  A patentee is granted exclusive rights to make, use, and sell an invention for a set period. | Example: The patentee successfully defended their patent against infringement.  专利权人成功地捍卫了他们的专利免受侵犯。  The patentee is entitled to royalties on sales of the patented product. 专利权人有权获得专利产品的销售提成。|近义词：patent holder, inventor (sometimes, but not always)|反义词：patent infringer

They have successfully defended their position as the patentee. = 他们已成功捍卫了其作为专利权人的地位。


过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
