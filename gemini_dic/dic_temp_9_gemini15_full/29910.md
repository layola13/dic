cautions:/ˈkɔːʃənz/| n.|警告，注意事项|Warnings or things to be careful about.  They are usually given to prevent harm or errors.|例句：The instructions include cautions about the use of the machine. 说明书中包含关于机器使用的警告。|例句：He offered cautions to the young driver about speeding. 他告诫年轻司机不要超速。|近义词：warnings, advisories, alerts|反义词：encouragement, assurances


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cautions against risky behavior. = 他告诫不要冒险。
The manual cautions users to unplug the device before cleaning. = 手册告诫用户清洁设备前先拔掉电源。

一般过去时:
She cautioned me about the dangers of the hike. = 她警告我远足的危险。
The doctor cautioned him to avoid strenuous activity. = 医生告诫他避免剧烈运动。

现在完成时:
They have cautioned us repeatedly about the risks. = 他们反复警告过我们风险。
The authorities have cautioned against travel to the affected region. = 当局已警告不要前往受灾地区。

过去完成时:
The teacher had cautioned the students about the upcoming exam. = 老师之前已经警告学生即将到来的考试。
She had cautioned him not to trust strangers. = 她之前已告诫他不要相信陌生人。

将来时:
He will caution his son about the dangers of drugs. = 他将告诫他的儿子毒品的危害。
The police will caution drivers about icy road conditions. = 警方将提醒司机注意结冰路况。

虚拟语气:
If I had cautioned him earlier, he might not have made that mistake. = 如果我早点警告他，他可能就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I had cautioned her about the slippery floor. = 我真希望我警告过她地板很滑。

被动语态:
Drivers were cautioned about the speed limit. = 司机们受到了关于速度限制的警告。
The public was cautioned to remain vigilant. = 公众被告诫要保持警惕。

疑问句:
Did he caution you about anything specific? = 他有没有特别警告你什么？
What cautions were given before the experiment? = 实验前提出了什么警告？

否定句:
He didn't caution me about the potential hazards. = 他没有警告我潜在的危险。
They didn't caution the children about playing near the road. = 他们没有告诫孩子们不要在路边玩耍。
