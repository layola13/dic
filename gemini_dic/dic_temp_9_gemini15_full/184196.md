resocializing: /ˌriːˈsoʊʃəlˌaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 再社会化；重新融入社会 | The process of helping someone who has been isolated or marginalized to reintegrate into society.  It often involves addressing social skills deficits, providing support networks, and facilitating access to resources and opportunities. |  例句：The prison's resocializing program aims to help inmates successfully transition back into the community.  监狱的再社会化项目旨在帮助囚犯成功过渡回社会。

The government is investing heavily in resocializing programs for former gang members. 政府正在大力投资针对前帮派成员的再社会化项目。


近义词：reintegrating, rehabilitating, reforming | 反义词：isolating, marginalizing | 动词
