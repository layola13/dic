scrappiest: /ˈskræpiəst/ | adj. | 最破烂的；最不整洁的；最不入流的 | Most disorganized, messy, or lacking in quality.  It is the superlative form of "scrappy". | 例句：The scrappiest dog in the shelter eventually found a loving home.  收容所里最不入流的狗最终找到了一个充满爱的家。


The scrappiest manuscript I've ever seen needed a lot of editing. 我见过的最糟糕的手稿需要大量的编辑。

近义词：ragged, dilapidated, messy, unkempt, disheveled,  shabby,  rundown

反义词：neat, tidy, clean, orderly, pristine, immaculate


词源:  Derived from "scrappy," which itself comes from "scrap," meaning a small piece or fragment.  The sense of being disorganized or lacking in quality developed from the idea of something being made up of disparate, haphazard scraps.
