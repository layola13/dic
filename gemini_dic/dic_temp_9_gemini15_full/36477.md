coleaders:/ˈkoʊˌliːdərz/| n.|共同领导者，联合领导|Two individuals or groups working together in a leadership capacity.  They share responsibility and authority.|例句：The project was successful due to the effective collaboration of the coleaders.  这个项目的成功归功于共同领导者的有效合作。
The two coleaders presented a unified front to the board.  两位共同领导者在董事会面前展现了统一战线。|近义词：co-chairs, joint leaders|反义词：sole leader, dictator
