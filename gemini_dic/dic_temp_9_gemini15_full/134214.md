nonindividuality:/ˌnɒnˌɪndɪvɪdʒuˈælɪti/ | n. |非个体性，缺乏个性 |The lack of distinct or individual characteristics; the state of being indistinguishable from others.  It often implies a lack of originality, uniqueness, or personal expression. |Example: The mass production of identical houses led to a sense of nonindividuality in the neighborhood.  这个街区里成批生产的相同房屋导致了缺乏个性的感觉。|近义词：uniformity, homogeneity, sameness, conformity |反义词：individuality, uniqueness, originality, distinctiveness |
