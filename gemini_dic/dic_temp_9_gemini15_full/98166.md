inscribe:/ɪnˈskraɪb/ | vt. |刻写；题写；铭刻；登记|To write or carve words or symbols on something, especially as a permanent record.|例句：They inscribed their names on the monument.  他们把名字刻在纪念碑上。|例句：The medal was inscribed with the winner's name. 这枚奖牌上刻着获胜者的名字。|近义词：carve, engrave, etch |反义词：erase, delete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He inscribes his name in the yearbook every year. 他每年都在年鉴上签名。
She inscribes inspirational quotes on her journal.  她在日记本上写下励志名言。


一般过去时:
The artist inscribed a dedication on the painting. 艺术家在画上题写了题词。
They inscribed the date of their wedding on the ring. 他们在戒指上刻上了结婚日期。


现在完成时:
The students have inscribed their names on the class roster. 学生们已经在班级花名册上登记了姓名。
She has inscribed a heartfelt message in the birthday card. 她已在生日贺卡上写下了一条衷心的信息。


过去完成时:
They had inscribed the names of the fallen soldiers before the ceremony. 他们在仪式之前已经刻上了阵亡士兵的名字。
She had inscribed a poem in the book before giving it as a gift.  她在送书之前已经在书里题了一首诗。


将来时:
He will inscribe his daughter's name on the family tree. 他将把女儿的名字刻在族谱上。
The committee will inscribe the winning essay on a plaque. 委员会将在牌匾上刻上获奖论文。


虚拟语气:
If I had a time machine, I would inscribe a message to my future self. 如果我有一台时光机，我会给自己未来的自己写一条信息。
I wish I could inscribe a message on the stars. 我希望我能把信息刻在星星上。


被动语态:
His name was inscribed on the list of attendees. 他的名字被刻在了出席者名单上。
The inscription was inscribed by a master calligrapher. 这段铭文是由一位书法大师题写的。


疑问句:
Will they inscribe the names of all the participants? 他们会刻上所有参与者的名字吗？
Where did they inscribe the important dates? 他们在哪里刻上了重要的日期？


否定句:
He did not inscribe his name on the document. 他没有在文件上签名。
They did not inscribe the correct information on the trophy. 他们没有在奖杯上刻写正确的信息。
