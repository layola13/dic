reverted:/rɪˈvɜːrtɪd/| v.|恢复，回复，逆转|To return to a previous state, practice, or belief.|The system reverted to its default settings after the power outage. 系统在停电后恢复到默认设置。|近义词：return, revert back, relapse|反义词：progress, advance, develop


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reverts to his childhood habits when he's stressed. 他在压力大的时候会回到童年的习惯。
The company reverts to its old methods every few years. 这家公司每隔几年就会回到旧方法。


一般过去时:
The river reverted to its original course after the flood. 洪水过后，河水恢复了原来的河道。
She reverted to her maiden name after the divorce. 离婚后她恢复了娘家姓。


现在完成时:
The government has reverted to its old policies. 政府已经恢复了旧政策。
He has reverted to type, acting exactly as he did before. 他又故态复萌了，行为和以前一模一样。


过去完成时:
The building had reverted to its original state after the renovation. 翻新后，建筑物恢复了原来的状态。
Before the meeting, the team had reverted to their previous plan. 会议之前，团队已经恢复了之前的计划。


将来时:
They will revert to their previous strategy if the current one fails. 如果目前的策略失败，他们将恢复到以前的策略。
The software will automatically revert to the previous version if an error occurs. 如果发生错误，软件将自动恢复到之前的版本。


虚拟语气:
If they reverted to their original plan, they might succeed. 如果他们恢复到最初的计划，他们可能会成功。
I wish he hadn't reverted to his old bad habits. 我真希望他没有恢复他以前那些坏习惯。


被动语态:
The decision was reverted by the higher management. 这个决定被上层管理人员否决了。
The changes were reverted after they were found to be causing problems. 这些更改在发现它们导致问题后被撤销了。


疑问句:
Did the system revert to its default settings? 系统是否恢复到默认设置？
Will they revert to their previous plan? 他们会恢复到以前的计划吗？


否定句:
The situation did not revert to normal. 情况没有恢复正常。
He refused to revert to his old ways. 他拒绝恢复他的老习惯。


PHRASE->
revert to: 回到，恢复到
revert back to: 回到，恢复到（强调回到之前的状态）
revert the changes: 撤销更改
revert a file: 恢复文件
revert to the default settings: 恢复默认设置
