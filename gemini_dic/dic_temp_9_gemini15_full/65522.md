eurypterus:/jʊəriˈptɛrəs/| n.|鲎类（尤指广翅鲎）|A genus of extinct eurypterids, a group of aquatic arthropods that lived during the Paleozoic Era.  They were often large, with a body resembling that of a scorpion. |Example: The fossil record shows a wide variety of eurypterids, including the genus Eurypterus.  化石记录显示各种各样的广翅鲎，包括广翅鲎属。|近义词:sea scorpion (informal)|反义词:None.
