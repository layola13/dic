reciprocate:/rɪˈsɪprəkeɪt/| vt. | 回报；互换；互相给予 | To give, feel, or do something in return; to respond to an action or feeling with a similar action or feeling. |例句：I appreciate your kindness, and I want to reciprocate by helping you with your project. 我感谢你的好意，我想通过帮助你完成你的项目来回报你。|例句：The two countries reciprocated each other's diplomatic gestures. 这两个国家互相回应对方的外交姿态。|近义词：return, repay, exchange, mirror |反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reciprocate favors. = 他们互相帮忙。
She reciprocates his feelings. = 她回应了他的感情。

一般过去时:
He reciprocated her gift with a bouquet of flowers. = 他用一束鲜花回赠了她礼物。
The company reciprocated the employee's loyalty with a promotion. = 公司通过晋升来回报员工的忠诚。

现在完成时:
We have reciprocated their hospitality many times. = 我们多次回馈了他们的热情款待。
She has reciprocated his love with equal passion. = 她以同样的热情回应了他的爱。

过去完成时:
They had reciprocated the kindness shown to them. = 他们已经报答了对他们的好意。
He had reciprocated the insult with a sharp retort. = 他已经用尖刻的反驳来回应侮辱。

将来时:
I will reciprocate your generosity at a later date. = 我将在以后的日子里回报你的慷慨。
They will reciprocate the invitation and visit us soon. = 他们将回访并很快来看望我们。

虚拟语气:
If he were to apologize, I would reciprocate. = 如果他道歉，我会回应他。
I wish he would reciprocate my affection. = 我希望他能回应我的感情。

被动语态:
His kindness was reciprocated by many. = 他的好意得到了许多人的回报。
Their efforts were reciprocated with equal enthusiasm. = 他们的努力得到了同样热情的回应。

疑问句:
Will you reciprocate the offer? = 你会接受这个提议吗？（此处reciprocate指“接受”，引申自“回报”）
Does she reciprocate his feelings? = 她回应了他的感情吗？

否定句:
He didn't reciprocate my invitation. = 他没有回应我的邀请。
She failed to reciprocate his love. = 她没能回应他的爱。


PHRASE->
reciprocate feelings = 回报感情
reciprocate a gesture = 回报姿态
reciprocate an invitation = 回应邀请
reciprocate the kindness = 回报善意
reciprocate the favor = 互相帮忙
