percussion: /pərˈkʌʃən/ | n. |打击乐；撞击声；冲击 |The striking of one object against another to produce sound; the sound so produced.  Also, instruments that produce sound in this way. |例句：The percussion section of the orchestra played a lively rhythm. 乐队的打击乐部分演奏了欢快的节奏。

例句：The doctor used percussion to examine the patient's chest. 医生用叩诊检查病人的胸部。


近义词：striking, impact, beating | 反义词：silence


词源:  From Middle French *percussion, from Latin *percussio, from percutere "to strike through," from per- "through" + quatere "to shake."
