inextinguishable: /ˌɪnɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl/ | adj. | 不可熄灭的；无法扑灭的；永久的 | Incapable of being extinguished or put out; unquenchable.  The word implies a persistent and powerful force that cannot be suppressed or eliminated. |


例句：

His inextinguishable spirit inspired everyone around him.  他的不屈不挠的精神鼓舞了周围的每一个人。

The fire, fueled by oil, was inextinguishable.  由石油助燃的大火，是无法扑灭的。


近义词：unquenchable, unyielding, enduring, perpetual, everlasting

反义词：extinguishable, quenchable, suppressable


词性：形容词


词源：

* **in-:**  prefix meaning "not" or "without"
* **extinguish:** verb meaning "to put out (a fire, light, etc.)"  From Latin *extinguere*  (ex- + *stinguere* "to quench")
* **-able:** suffix meaning "capable of being"
