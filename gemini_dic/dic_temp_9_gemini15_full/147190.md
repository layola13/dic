overly:/ˈoʊvərli/ | adj. | 过度的；极其的 | excessively; to a degree exceeding what is normal or desirable.  |例句：He was overly critical of her work. 他对她工作的批评过于苛刻。|例句：She's overly concerned about her health. 她对自己的健康过于担忧。|近义词：excessively, excessively, unduly, too, extremely |反义词：moderately, insufficiently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is overly cautious. = 他过于谨慎。
The dress is overly ornate. = 这件衣服过于华丽。


一般过去时:
She was overly emotional yesterday. = 她昨天情绪过于激动。
They were overly optimistic about the project. = 他们对这个项目过于乐观。


现在完成时:
I have been overly stressed lately. = 我最近压力过大。
He has overly indulged in sweets. = 他吃糖吃得太多了。


过去完成时:
She had been overly tired before the race. = 比赛前她已经过于疲惫了。
They had been overly confident in their abilities. = 他们对自己的能力过于自信。


将来时:
He will be overly busy next week. = 他下周会非常忙碌。
The party will be overly crowded. = 派对将会过于拥挤。


虚拟语气:
If I were not overly sensitive, I wouldn't be so upset. = 如果我不那么敏感，我就不会这么沮丧。
I wish I hadn't been overly critical of his performance. = 我真希望我没有对他表现出过多的批评。


被动语态:
The problem was overly simplified. = 问题被过于简化了。
The plan was overly ambitious. = 计划过于雄心勃勃。


疑问句:
Is his reaction overly dramatic? = 他的反应是不是太夸张了？
Are you overly worried about the exam? = 你是不是对考试过于担忧？


否定句:
The response wasn't overly enthusiastic. = 反响并不热烈。
The film wasn't overly violent. = 这部电影并不暴力。


PHRASE->
overly sensitive = 过度敏感
overly cautious = 过于谨慎
overly ambitious = 过于雄心勃勃
overly critical = 过于挑剔
overly complicated = 过于复杂

词源:
The word "overly" is formed by combining the adverb "over" (meaning "to an excessive degree") with the suffix "-ly" (which forms adverbs from adjectives).  The meaning therefore directly reflects the combination of its constituent parts.
