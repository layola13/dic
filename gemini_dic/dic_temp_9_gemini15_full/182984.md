repleaded:/riˈpliːdɪd/| v.|再次辩护，重新答辩|To plead again; to answer a charge or accusation a second time.  This word is the past tense and past participle of "replead."|例句：The lawyer repleaded his client's case after new evidence emerged. 律师在出现新的证据后再次为他的当事人辩护。

例句：After the judge rejected his initial plea, the defendant repleaded, presenting new witnesses.  在法官驳回其最初的申辩后，被告再次申辩，并提供了新的证人。


近义词：re-argue, re-assert, reiterate

反义词：withdraw, concede, admit

词性：动词
