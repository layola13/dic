eschara:/ˈɛskərə/| n.|焦痂，坏死组织|A dry, dark scab or slough formed on the surface of a burn or ulcer.  It is composed of dead tissue and often adheres firmly to the underlying tissue.|例句：The burn wound was covered with a thick eschara.  烧伤伤口覆盖着一层厚厚的焦痂。|例句：Removal of the eschara was necessary to promote healing.  去除焦痂对于促进愈合是必要的。|近义词：scab, slough, crust|反义词：healthy tissue, live tissue|名词
