rotundly:/ˈraʊndli/| adv.|圆润地；响亮地；充分地；坚决地 |  The word "rotundly" describes something done in a full, rounded, or sonorous manner. It can refer to a physical characteristic (like a rotund voice), a manner of speaking (a rotund declaration), or a general sense of completeness or fullness.  |例句：He rotundly declared his innocence. 他响亮地宣称自己无罪。

He laughed rotundly. 他圆润地笑了。


近义词：fully, completely, emphatically, resonantly, roundly


反义词：weakly, faintly, hesitantly, timidly


词性:副词
