codded:/ˈkɒdɪd/ | adj. | 编码的；用密码写的 | Coded refers to information that has been converted into a code, often for the purpose of secrecy or efficient storage and transmission.  It implies a systematic transformation of data into a different format. | Example: The message was coded to prevent unauthorized access.  这条消息经过编码以防止未经授权的访问。 The software uses a coded algorithm to encrypt user data. 该软件使用编码算法来加密用户数据。 | 近义词：encrypted, encoded, ciphered | 反义词：decoded, uncoded, plain
