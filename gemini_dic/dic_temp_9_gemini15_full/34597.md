cleats:/kliːts/| n.|鞋钉；卡环；(船的)卡子|Metal or plastic projections on the sole of a shoe or boot, providing grip.  Also, a device used to fasten something securely.|例句：The football player's cleats dug into the muddy field. 足球运动员的鞋钉深深地陷入了泥泞的球场。

例句：The cleats on my hiking boots helped me maintain my footing on the slippery rocks. 我登山鞋上的鞋钉帮助我在湿滑的岩石上保持平衡。


近义词：spikes, studs, crampons|反义词：smooth soles|词性：名词
