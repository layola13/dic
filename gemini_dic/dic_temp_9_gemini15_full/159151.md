podcasting:/ˈpɑːdkæstɪŋ/| n. |播客制作；播客节目制作|The act of creating and distributing audio or video programs, typically as a series, over the internet for download or streaming.  Podcasting involves recording, editing, and uploading episodes, often on a regular schedule. |例句：Many people enjoy podcasting as a creative outlet and way to share their thoughts and ideas. 许多人喜欢播客制作，因为它是一种创造性的途径，可以分享他们的想法和观点。

例句：She started podcasting about her travel experiences and quickly gained a large following. 她开始制作关于她旅行经历的播客，并迅速获得了大量的追随者。


近义词：webcasting, broadcasting (in a more general sense)
反义词：None directly applicable.  The opposite would be *not* creating podcasts.
