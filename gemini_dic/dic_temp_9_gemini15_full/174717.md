quota:/ˈkwəʊtə/| n. |配额，限额|A quota is a limited quantity of something that is officially allowed.  It is often used in the context of imports, exports, or production.| 例句：The factory reached its production quota for the month.  这家工厂达到了本月的生产配额。| 例句：The government imposed quotas on the import of textiles. 政府对纺织品的进口实行配额限制。|近义词：allocation, limit, restriction |反义词：unlimited, unrestricted |名词
