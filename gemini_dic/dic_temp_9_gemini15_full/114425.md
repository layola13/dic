make the rounds:/meɪk ðə raʊndz/ | phrasal verb | 传播；巡回；四处走动 | To circulate or be circulated; to go from place to place; to visit a series of places.  |例句：The rumor made the rounds quickly.  谣言迅速传开了。

The news made the rounds at the office.  这个消息在办公室里传开了。


近义词：circulate, spread, go around, travel, visit | 反义词：stay put, remain stationary


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gossip makes the rounds every day. = 流言蜚语每天都在传播。
The manager makes the rounds, checking on his staff. = 经理四处巡视，查看他的员工。


一般过去时:
The joke made the rounds at the party. = 这个笑话在派对上广为流传。
The inspector made the rounds of the building. = 检查员巡视了整栋建筑。


现在完成时:
The story has made the rounds in the media. = 这个故事已经在媒体上广为流传。
The salesman has made the rounds of all the major clients. = 销售员已经拜访了所有主要的客户。


过去完成时:
Before the scandal broke, the rumors had made the rounds for weeks. = 在丑闻爆发之前，谣言已经流传了好几个星期。
By the time the meeting ended, the news had made the rounds. = 到会议结束时，消息已经传开了。


将来时:
This new policy will make the rounds soon. = 这项新政策很快就会传开。
The CEO will make the rounds to greet the new employees. = 首席执行官将会四处走动，问候新员工。


虚拟语气:
If the news made the rounds earlier, we could have prepared better. = 如果消息早点传开，我们就能更好地准备。
I wish the information had made the rounds sooner. = 我希望信息能早点传开。


被动语态:
The proposal was made the rounds by the committee. = 这个提案被委员会传阅了。
The announcement is being made the rounds via email. = 公告正在通过电子邮件传播。


疑问句:
Has the invitation made the rounds yet? = 请柬已经发放了吗？
Will this information make the rounds among the competitors? = 这个信息会在竞争对手之间传播吗？


否定句:
The memo didn't make the rounds as quickly as we'd hoped. = 备忘录并没有像我们希望的那样迅速传开。
The story didn't make the rounds outside of our department. = 这个故事并没有传到我们部门以外。


PHRASE->
make the rounds of (places): 巡视……
make the rounds with (something): 传播……
make the rounds to (someone):  拜访……
make the rounds in (a place): 在……流传
do the rounds:  四处走动，传播


词源:
The phrase "make the rounds" originates from the practice of rounds in various contexts:  military patrols, bar visits, and social gatherings where individuals would visit several locations in a set sequence.  The "rounds" refers to this cyclical path, and "make" signifies the action of completing this circuit.
