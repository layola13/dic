dedicates:/ˈdɛdɪkeɪts/| vt.| 献身；致力于；题献给 | To devote (something) to a particular purpose or cause; to formally dedicate something. |例句：He dedicates his life to helping the poor. 他致力于帮助穷人。|例句：The book is dedicated to his wife.  这本书献给他妻子。|近义词：devotes, consecrates, commits |反义词：neglects, abandons


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dedicates his free time to painting. = 他把空闲时间都用来画画。
She dedicates her life to environmental protection. = 她一生致力于环境保护。


一般过去时:
They dedicated the new building to the city's founder. = 他们把新大楼献给了城市创始人。
He dedicated his first novel to his parents. = 他把他的第一部小说献给了他的父母。


现在完成时:
She has dedicated many years to researching this disease. = 她已经多年致力于研究这种疾病。
He has dedicated his career to improving education. = 他毕生致力于改善教育。


过去完成时:
They had dedicated the park to the memory of the fallen soldiers before the ceremony. = 在仪式之前，他们已将公园献给阵亡士兵的纪念。
She had dedicated her youth to the cause of social justice before becoming a lawyer. = 在成为律师之前，她已将青春献给了社会正义事业。


将来时:
He will dedicate his next book to his daughter. = 他将把他的下一本书献给他女儿。
The company will dedicate its resources to developing new technology. = 公司将致力于研发新技术。


虚拟语气:
If he were to dedicate himself fully, he could achieve great things. = 如果他全力以赴，他就能取得巨大的成就。
I wish I had dedicated more time to my studies. = 我真希望我当时能多花些时间学习。


被动语态:
The statue was dedicated to the memory of the victims. = 这座雕像是为了纪念受害者而设立的。
The new hospital will be dedicated next month. = 新医院将于下个月落成。


疑问句:
To whom will you dedicate your next project? = 你将把你的下一个项目献给谁？
What cause will she dedicate her life to? = 她将把一生奉献给什么事业？


否定句:
He did not dedicate enough time to the project. = 他没有为这个项目投入足够的时间。
She refused to dedicate herself entirely to her work. = 她拒绝完全投入工作。


PHRASE->
dedicate oneself to = 献身于
dedicate time to = 投入时间于
dedicate a song to = 将歌曲献给
dedicate resources to = 将资源用于
be dedicated to = 致力于
