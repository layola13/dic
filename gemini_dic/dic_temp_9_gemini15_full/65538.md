euthanasia:/ˌjuːθəˈneɪʒə/| n. |安乐死；无痛处死|The act of intentionally ending a life to relieve pain and suffering;  typically applied to cases of incurable and unbearable suffering.  It is a controversial topic with strong ethical and legal considerations.|例句：The debate surrounding euthanasia is complex and emotionally charged.  安乐死引发的争论复杂且充满情感。|例句：  Active euthanasia involves a direct action to end a life, while passive euthanasia involves withholding treatment.|主动安乐死指的是直接结束生命，而被动安乐死指的是不进行治疗。|近义词：mercy killing, assisted suicide|反义词：life support, treatment
