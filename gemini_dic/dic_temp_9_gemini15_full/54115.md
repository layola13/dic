disempower:/ˌdɪsɪmˈpaʊər/| vt.|使失去权力；使无力；削弱|To deprive of power or authority.|The new regulations disempowered local authorities. 新规削弱了地方政府的权力。|近义词：weaken, undermine, cripple|反义词：empower, strengthen


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They disempower the workers through unfair labor practices. 他们通过不公平的劳动行为使工人失去权力。
The policy disempowers marginalized communities. 这项政策使边缘化社区失去了权力。


一般过去时:
The dictator disempowered his opponents by silencing them. 独裁者通过压制反对者来削弱他们的力量。
The company's restructuring disempowered many middle managers. 公司的重组使许多中层管理人员失去了权力。


现在完成时:
Years of oppression have disempowered the people. 多年的压迫已经使人民失去了权力。
The government's actions have disempowered many citizens. 政府的行为已经使许多公民失去了权力。


过去完成时:
They had disempowered the union before the strike even began. 他们在罢工开始之前就已经削弱了工会的势力。
The previous administration had disempowered the environmental protection agency. 前一届政府已经削弱了环境保护机构的权力。


将来时:
The new law will disempower small businesses. 新法律将削弱小型企业的实力。
The proposed changes will disempower the judiciary. 提出的修改将削弱司法机构的权力。


虚拟语气:
If they passed that law, it would disempower the entire industry. 如果他们通过那项法律，它将使整个行业失去权力。
I wish the government wouldn't disempower its citizens in this way. 我希望政府不要以这种方式使公民失去权力。


被动语态:
Local communities were disempowered by the central government's decision. 地方社区因中央政府的决定而失去了权力。
The workers felt disempowered by the management's lack of transparency. 工人们感到管理层的缺乏透明度使他们失去了权力。


疑问句:
Does this policy disempower certain groups of people?这项政策是否使某些人群失去权力？
How did the new regulations disempower the local council? 新规是如何削弱地方议会的权力的？


否定句:
The reforms did not disempower the workers; instead, they empowered them. 改革并没有使工人失去权力；相反，它赋予了他们权力。
The government aims to not disempower but rather to empower its citizens. 政府的目标不是使公民失去权力，而是赋予他们权力。
