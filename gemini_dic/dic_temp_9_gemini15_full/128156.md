nettlebird: /ˈnetlbɜːrd/ | n. | 雀鸟 | A small, insectivorous bird of the family Meliphagidae, found in Australia and New Guinea.  They are known for their distinctive songs and their habit of foraging among stinging nettles.  | Example: The nettlebird's song was a cheerful melody echoing through the eucalyptus grove.  这只刺鸟的歌声是一首欢快的旋律，回荡在桉树林中。|近义词: honeyeater (a broader term)|反义词:  None directly applicable.
