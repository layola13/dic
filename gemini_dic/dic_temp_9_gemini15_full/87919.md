hierography:/ˌhaɪəˈrɒɡrəfi/| n. |象形文字；圣书体|The system of writing using pictorial symbols, especially that of ancient Egypt.  Hieroglyphs are a complex system of writing that uses pictures and symbols to represent words, sounds, and ideas.  The decipherment of hieroglyphs was a major achievement in the field of archaeology and linguistics.|The Rosetta Stone, containing inscriptions in hieroglyphs, Greek, and Demotic script, was crucial in deciphering Egyptian hieroglyphs.|近义词：hieroglyphics, pictography|反义词：alphabet (as a contrasting writing system)|名词
