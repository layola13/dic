outran:/ˌaʊtˈræn/| vt.|超过，跑赢，胜过|To run faster than and overtake (someone or something).  It implies exceeding someone or something in speed or performance.|例句：He outran all the other runners in the marathon. 他在马拉松比赛中跑赢了所有其他选手。
例句：The rabbit outran the fox. 兔子跑赢了狐狸。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outpace, outstrip|反义词：be outrun, lagged behind|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outruns his opponents in every race. = 他在每场比赛中都超过了他的对手。
They outrun their competitors in innovation. = 他们在创新方面超过了竞争对手。


一般过去时:
The hare outran the tortoise. = 兔子跑赢了乌龟。
She outran her personal best time. = 她跑出了个人最好成绩。


现在完成时:
He has outrun his previous best time by a full minute. = 他比之前的最好成绩快了一分钟。
The company has outrun its rivals in market share. = 公司在市场份额方面超过了竞争对手。


过去完成时:
By the time they reached the finish line, she had outrun everyone else. = 当他们到达终点线时，她已经超过了其他人。
He had outrun his expectations for the project. = 他超出了对项目的预期。


将来时:
I think I will outrun him in the next race. = 我想在下一场比赛中我会超过他。
The company anticipates it will outrun its competitors in sales growth next year. = 公司预计明年在销售增长方面将超过竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If I had trained harder, I would have outrun him. = 如果我训练更努力，我就能超过他了。
If she had focused more, she would have outrun the deadline. = 如果她更专注，她就能赶上截止日期了。


被动语态:
He was outrun by a younger, faster competitor. = 他被一个更年轻、更快的竞争对手超过了。
The plan was outrun by unforeseen circumstances. = 计划被不可预见的情况打乱了。


疑问句:
Did he outrun his opponent in the race? = 他在比赛中超过了他的对手吗？
Will the team outrun their rivals in the championship? = 该队会在锦标赛中超过他们的对手吗？


否定句:
He didn't outrun his opponent in the final sprint. = 他在最后的冲刺中没有超过他的对手。
The company failed to outrun its competitors in the new market. = 公司未能在这个新市场上超过竞争对手。


PHRASE->
outrun one's expectations 超出预期
outrun the competition 超过竞争对手
outrun the deadline 超过最后期限
outrun inflation 抵御通货膨胀
outrun the storm 逃脱风暴
