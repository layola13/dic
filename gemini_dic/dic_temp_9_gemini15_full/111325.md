live-high-on-the-hog: /ˌlɪv ˈhaɪ ɒn ðə hɒɡ/ | phrasal verb |过着奢侈的生活；挥金如土| To live luxuriously and extravagantly; to enjoy a life of great comfort and plenty.  |例句：They've been living high on the hog ever since they won the lottery.  他们自从赢得彩票后就过着奢侈的生活。


例句：After years of scrimping and saving, they finally decided to live high on the hog for a while.  在多年的省吃俭用之后，他们终于决定过一段时间奢侈的生活。


近义词：live in the lap of luxury, live it up, roll in money, wallow in luxury


反义词：scrimp and save, live frugally, be penny-pinching


词性: phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They live high on the hog. = 他们过着奢侈的生活。
We live high on the hog this week because of a bonus. = 我们这周因为奖金而过着奢侈的生活。


一般过去时:
They lived high on the hog last year. = 他们去年过着奢侈的生活。
She lived high on the hog during her vacation. = 她在度假期间过着奢侈的生活。


现在完成时:
They have lived high on the hog for years. = 他们多年来一直过着奢侈的生活。
We have lived high on the hog since we inherited the money. = 自从我们继承了这笔钱后，我们就过着奢侈的生活。


过去完成时:
They had lived high on the hog before the economic downturn. = 经济衰退之前，他们过着奢侈的生活。
She had lived high on the hog before she lost her job. = 在她失业之前，她过着奢侈的生活。


将来时:
They will live high on the hog once they retire. = 他们退休后将过着奢侈的生活。
We will live high on the hog next month when we get paid. = 下个月领到薪水后，我们将过着奢侈的生活。


虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would live high on the hog. = 如果我中了彩票，我会过着奢侈的生活。
I wish I could live high on the hog, but I have to budget my money. = 我希望我能过着奢侈的生活，但我必须控制我的预算。


被动语态: (This phrase is not typically used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Do they live high on the hog? = 他们过着奢侈的生活吗？
Did they live high on the hog last summer? = 他们去年夏天过着奢侈的生活吗？


否定句:
They don't live high on the hog. = 他们不过着奢侈的生活。
They didn't live high on the hog when they were younger. = 他们年轻的时候不过着奢侈的生活。


PHRASE->
live high on the hog for a while

词源:  The phrase's origin is unclear, but it likely emerged in the 19th century.  The image of a hog, often associated with plenty and abundance, is contrasted with the verb "live," suggesting a life of indulgence and luxury.  "High" emphasizes the elevated level of comfort and extravagance.
