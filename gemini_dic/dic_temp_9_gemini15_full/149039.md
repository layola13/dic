owllike:/ˈaʊlˌlaɪk/| adj.|像猫头鹰的；有猫头鹰特点的；深思熟虑的|Resembling an owl in appearance or characteristics, often implying wisdom, solemnity, or nocturnal habits.  The word often suggests a quiet, watchful, and perhaps slightly mysterious or wise demeanor.|例句：He had an owllike gaze, his eyes fixed intently on the chessboard. 他有着猫头鹰般凝视，眼睛专注地盯着棋盘。

例句：The owllike silence of the forest was broken only by the chirping of crickets.  森林里猫头鹰般的寂静只被蟋蟀的鸣叫声打破。


近义词：sagacious, wise, thoughtful, solemn, watchful
