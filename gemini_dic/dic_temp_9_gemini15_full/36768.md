collusions:/kəˈluʒənz/ | n. |共谋；勾结；串通 | Secret or illegal cooperation between people or groups, especially in order to deceive or cheat others.  |Example: The police uncovered a series of collusions between the corrupt officials and the organized crime.  警方揭露了一系列腐败官员与有组织犯罪之间的勾结行为。


The judge found evidence of collusion between the two companies to fix prices.  法官发现了这两家公司串通定价的证据。|近义词：conspiracy, connivance, intrigue, plot |反义词：cooperation (in a legitimate context), honesty, transparency |名词

词源:
