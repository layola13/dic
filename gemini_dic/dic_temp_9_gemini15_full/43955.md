cribriform:/ˈkrɪbrɪfɔːrm/ | adj. |筛状的，有许多小孔的 |  Having many small holes or perforations, resembling a sieve.  The term is often used to describe anatomical structures. |例句：The cribriform plate of the ethmoid bone allows olfactory nerves to pass through.  筛状板允许嗅觉神经通过。

The cribriform fascia of the abdomen helps support the abdominal organs. 腹部的筛状筋膜有助于支撑腹腔器官。|近义词：perforated, sieve-like, porous |反义词：imperforate, solid, dense |词性：形容词
