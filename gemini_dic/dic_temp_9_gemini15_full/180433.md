regalvanizing: /ˌriːˈɡæl.vən.aɪ.zɪŋ/ | v. | 再镀锌；重新激励；复兴 | The process of applying a new layer of zinc to a previously galvanized surface to restore its protective coating and prevent corrosion.  It also refers to the act of renewing or reinvigorating something, giving it new life or purpose. | Example: The old bridge underwent regalvanizing to extend its lifespan.  桥梁进行了再镀锌处理以延长其使用寿命。 The company's new marketing campaign is an attempt at regalvanizing its brand image.  公司的新的市场营销活动试图重振其品牌形象。|近义词：rejuvenate, revitalize, refresh | 反义词：deteriorate, decay
