praiseworthiness:/ˈpreɪzwɜːðɪnəs/ | n. |值得称赞，可赞扬性 |The quality or state of being praiseworthy; deserving of praise.  It refers to something that is commendable, admirable, or excellent and warrants praise. |例句：The praiseworthiness of her actions was undeniable.  她的行为值得称赞，这是不可否认的。


The dedication shown by the rescue workers deserves immense praiseworthiness.  救援人员展现的奉献精神值得高度赞扬。|近义词：commendableness, merit, excellence, admirability |反义词：blameworthiness, reprehensibility, undesirability
