ointment:/ˈɔɪntmənt/ | n. |药膏，软膏 |A thick substance, typically greasy, that is applied to the skin to heal wounds or relieve soreness.  |Example: The doctor prescribed an antibiotic ointment for the infection. 医生开了抗生素药膏来治疗感染。

Example:  She applied a soothing ointment to her burn. 她把舒缓的药膏涂在烧伤处。


近义词：balm, salve, cream


反义词：None (No direct antonym, as it describes a type of substance)
