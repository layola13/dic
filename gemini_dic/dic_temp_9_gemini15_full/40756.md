conviction:/kənˈvɪkʃən/| n.|信念；定罪；确信|A strong belief or opinion.  The act of finding someone guilty of a crime.  A firm persuasion. |例句：His conviction that he was right was unshakeable. 他的确信自己是正确的信念坚定不移。

例句：The jury delivered a conviction after hearing all the evidence. 陪审团在听取所有证据后做出了定罪判决。


近义词：belief, certainty, faith, judgment, sentence|反义词：doubt, uncertainty, acquittal
