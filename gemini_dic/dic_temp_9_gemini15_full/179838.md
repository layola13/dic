reexpelling:/ˌriːɪkˈspɛlɪŋ/ | v. | 重新驱逐，再次驱逐 | The act of expelling someone or something again.  It implies a previous expulsion and a renewed action of removal. |  The school is considering reexpelling the student for repeated violations of the code of conduct. 学校正在考虑因该学生反复违反行为准则而再次将其开除。 The government announced the reexpelling of several diplomats for espionage.  政府宣布再次驱逐几名从事间谍活动的官员。 | expelling, ejecting | admitting, welcoming | 动词


USAGE->

They reexpel disruptive students. = 他们再次驱逐扰乱秩序的学生。
The company reexpels unqualified employees. = 公司再次驱逐不合格的员工。


一般过去时:
