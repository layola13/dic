nonsenses:/ˈnɒnsɛnsɪz/ | n. |胡说八道，废话 | Unreasonable or illogical talk, ideas, or actions.  It often refers to something silly or absurd. | Example:  He was talking complete nonsense. 他在说完全是胡说八道。  |近义词：rubbish, drivel, balderdash, absurdity |反义词：sense, logic, reason


USAGE->

This statement makes no sense; it's pure nonsense. = 这句话毫无道理，纯粹是胡说八道。
His ideas are nonsense and impractical. = 他的想法是胡说八道，不切实际的。
