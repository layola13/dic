reoffend:/ˌriːˈɒfɛnd/ | v. |再次犯罪，重犯|To commit a crime again after having been convicted of a previous crime.  |例句：He was released from prison, but he reoffended within six months. 他获释出狱，但在六个月内再次犯罪。|例句：The high recidivism rate shows that many offenders reoffend after serving their sentences.  高比例的累犯率表明许多罪犯服刑后会再次犯罪。|近义词：relapse, recommit, repeat the offense |反义词：reform, rehabilitate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reoffends frequently. = 他经常再次犯罪。
She reoffends after every parole. = 她每次假释后都会再次犯罪。

一般过去时:
He reoffended after only a few weeks. = 他仅仅几周后就再次犯罪了。
She reoffended and was sent back to prison. = 她再次犯罪，被送回了监狱。

现在完成时:
He has reoffended several times since his release. = 自从他获释以来，他已经多次再次犯罪。
She has not reoffended since her last conviction. = 自从她上次定罪以来，她就再也没有犯过罪。

过去完成时:
He had reoffended before the police even knew about his release. = 警察甚至不知道他获释之前，他就已经再次犯罪了。
She had reoffended multiple times before they finally caught her. = 在他们最终抓住她之前，她已经多次再次犯罪了。

将来时:
He will likely reoffend if he doesn't get help. = 如果他不得到帮助，他很可能会再次犯罪。
She will probably reoffend unless she addresses the root causes of her criminal behavior. = 除非她解决犯罪行为的根本原因，否则她很可能会再次犯罪。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't reoffended, he would be a free man now. = 如果他没有再次犯罪，他现在就是一个自由人了。
If she had not reoffended, she might have had a different life. = 如果她没有再次犯罪，她可能会有不同的生活。

被动语态:
It is feared that he will be reoffended against. = 人们担心他会再次成为犯罪的受害者。(被动语态在此处用以表达“再次成为犯罪目标”)
It is reported that many criminals are reoffended. = 据报道，许多罪犯再次犯罪。(被动语态更强调行为本身)

疑问句:
Will he reoffend? = 他会再次犯罪吗？
Has she ever reoffended? = 她曾经再次犯罪吗？

否定句:
He did not reoffend after his release. = 他获释后没有再次犯罪。
She has never reoffended in her life. = 她一生从未再次犯罪。

reoffending rates = 再次犯罪率
