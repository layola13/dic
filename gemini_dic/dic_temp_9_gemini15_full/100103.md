interpouring: /ˌɪntərˈpɔːrɪŋ/ | v. | 互相倾注；相互渗透 | The act of pouring something into or among multiple things, implying a mutual exchange or blending.  It suggests a reciprocal action where several entities contribute to and receive from each other.  This word is less common and often found in specialized contexts, particularly where liquids or abstract concepts are involved. | Example: The interpouring of cultures led to a rich and diverse society.  文化间的相互融合造就了一个丰富多彩的社会。The interpouring of ideas during the brainstorming session resulted in innovative solutions. 头脑风暴期间思想的相互碰撞产生了创新的解决方案。 | 近义词: intermingling, blending, merging, mixing | 反义词: separating, segregating, isolating | 动词
