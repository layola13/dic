postural:/ˈpɒstʃərəl/ /ˈpɔːstʃərəl/| adj.|姿势的；体位的；与姿势有关的|relating to posture or body position.  The term often describes something that affects or is related to posture.|例句：Poor postural habits can lead to back pain.  不良的姿势习惯会导致背痛。

例句：The therapist assessed his postural alignment.  治疗师评估了他的姿势排列。


近义词：postural, attitudinal (侧重态度和姿势), positional (侧重位置)
反义词：None (没有明显的反义词，只能通过反面描述，例如：unaligned, misaligned)
词性: adjective
