non-Bolsheviks:/ˌnɑːnˈbɒlʃəvɪks/ | n. |非布尔什维克分子|Those who opposed or were not aligned with the Bolsheviks, the majority faction of the Russian Social Democratic Labour Party that seized power in the October Revolution.  They represented a diverse range of political viewpoints, including Mensheviks, Socialist Revolutionaries, and other opposition groups.|The non-Bolsheviks were initially surprised by the speed and decisiveness of the Bolshevik seizure of power.|近义词：anti-Bolsheviks, counter-revolutionaries |反义词：Bolsheviks
