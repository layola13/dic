documentable:/ˌdɒkjʊˈmɛntəbl/ /ˌdɑːkjʊˈmɛntəbl/| adj.| 可证明的；有文件的；可记录的 |  capable of being documented or proved with evidence; supported by documentation.  |The claim is documentable; we have records to support it.  这项说法是可以证明的；我们有记录可以支持它。| provable, verifiable, demonstrable | unprovable, unverifiable, undemonstrable | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence is documentable. = 证据是可以证明的。
This fact is easily documentable. = 这个事实很容易证明。

一般过去时:
The claim was documentable with historical records. = 该说法可以通过历史记录来证明。
The transaction was documentable through bank statements. = 这笔交易可以通过银行对账单来证明。

现在完成时:
The company has documentable proof of its financial success. = 公司有可以证明其财务成功的证据。
We have documentable evidence to support our findings. = 我们有文件证据来支持我们的发现。

过去完成时:
They had documentable evidence of the crime before the trial. = 审判前他们已有可以证明犯罪的证据。
The researchers had documentable results from their experiment. = 研究人员的实验有可记录的结果。

将来时:
The project will be documentable through regular progress reports. = 该项目将通过定期进度报告进行记录。
We will ensure that all aspects of the process are fully documentable. = 我们将确保流程的所有方面都完全可以记录。

虚拟语气:
If the information were documentable, we could use it in the report. = 如果信息可以证明，我们就可以在报告中使用它。
If the evidence had been documentable, the case would have been different. = 如果证据是可以证明的，案件的结果就会有所不同。

被动语态:
The incident was documentable by witnesses. = 这一事件可以由证人证明。
The findings were fully documentable and readily available. = 调查结果是可以完全证明的，并且很容易获得。

疑问句:
Is the information documentable? = 这些信息是可以证明的吗？
Are the allegations documentable? = 这些指控是可以证明的吗？

否定句:
The rumor was not documentable. = 这个谣言是无法证明的。
The accusations were not documentable, lacking sufficient evidence. = 这些指控是无法证明的，缺乏足够的证据。


PHRASE->
fully documentable = 完全可证明的
well-documentable = 有充分证据证明的
easily documentable = 易于证明的
thoroughly documentable = 彻底可证明的
properly documentable = 正确可证明的
