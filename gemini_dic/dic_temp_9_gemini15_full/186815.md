rinds:/raɪndz/| n.|果皮；外皮；猪皮|The tough outer covering of certain fruits and vegetables, or the skin of a pig.  It often refers to the peel of citrus fruits like oranges and lemons. |例句：He carefully peeled the rinds from the oranges before juicing them. 他在榨汁之前仔细地剥掉了橘子的皮。

例句：The chef used orange rinds to add flavor to the cake.  厨师用橘子皮来增加蛋糕的风味。


近义词：peel, skin, crust, husk|反义词：flesh, pulp|词性：名词
