leared:/lɜːrnd/|/lɜːrnt/| v. |学习，得知|Learned is the past participle of the verb "learn". It can function as an adjective or part of a verb phrase.  As an adjective, it means having acquired knowledge or skill through study or experience. As a verb, it is used in perfect tenses and passive voice constructions.|

He has leared a lot about history. 他学到了很多历史知识。

She had leared to play the piano before she was ten. 她十岁之前就会弹钢琴了。


近义词：studied, acquired, mastered, grasped
反义词：unlearned, ignorant, untaught
