ignores:/ɪɡˈnɔːrz/ | vt. |忽视；不理；不顾 |To refuse to take notice of or acknowledge; disregard. |例句：He ignores all the warnings. 他无视所有的警告。|例句：She ignores his calls. 她不接他的电话。|近义词：disregards, overlooks, neglects |反义词：heeds, attends to, considers


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ignores my advice. = 他不听我的劝告。
They ignore the traffic rules. = 他们无视交通规则。


一般过去时:
She ignored his pleas for help. = 她无视了他求救的请求。
I ignored the ringing telephone. = 我没理会响铃的电话。


现在完成时:
He has ignored my emails for weeks. = 他几周来一直不回复我的邮件。
They have ignored the problem for too long. = 他们对这个问题的忽视时间太长了。


过去完成时:
She had ignored the warning signs before the accident. = 事故发生前，她忽视了警告标志。
They had ignored the evidence for years. = 他们多年来一直忽视证据。


将来时:
He will ignore the criticism again. = 他还会再次无视批评。
They will ignore the consequences of their actions. = 他们将无视自己行为的后果。


虚拟语气:
If he ignored the evidence, it would be a serious mistake. = 如果他忽视了证据，那将是一个严重的错误。
I wish she hadn't ignored my advice. = 我真希望她没有忽视我的建议。


被动语态:
My warnings were ignored. = 我的警告被忽视了。
His pleas for help were ignored. = 他求救的请求被忽视了。


疑问句:
Does he ignore your feelings? = 他是否忽视你的感受？
Why do they ignore the facts? = 为什么他们忽视事实？


否定句:
He doesn't ignore his responsibilities. = 他没有忽视自己的责任。
She doesn't ignore the needs of her children. = 她没有忽视孩子的需求。


PHRASE->
ignore completely = 完全忽视
ignore the fact that = 忽略…的事实
safely ignore = 可以忽略
deliberately ignore =故意忽视
cannot ignore = 不能忽视
