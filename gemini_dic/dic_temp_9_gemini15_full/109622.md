lettering:/ˈlɛtərɪŋ/| n.|字母设计；刻字；题字 |The art or process of drawing or painting letters, especially in a decorative or stylized way.  It refers to the style, arrangement, and overall aesthetic of written or printed letters. |例句：The lettering on the monument was beautifully carved.  纪念碑上的题字雕刻得非常精美。

The elegant lettering on the invitation made it extra special. 请柬上优雅的书法使它显得格外特别。
