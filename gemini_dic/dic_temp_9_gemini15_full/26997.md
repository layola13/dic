calculate:/ˈkælkjuleɪt/ | vt. |计算，估算，推测 |To determine or ascertain by mathematical methods; compute.  To make a calculation or computation. To reckon; estimate. |例句：I need to calculate the total cost. 我需要计算总成本。

例句：She calculated the risk before making the decision.  她在做决定之前权衡了风险。


近义词：compute, reckon, estimate, figure, assess | 反义词：guess (in the sense of a rough estimate without calculation)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He calculates the area of the rectangle. 他计算矩形的面积。
We calculate the project's budget every year. 我们每年都会计算项目的预算。


一般过去时:
She calculated the answer incorrectly. 她计算答案算错了。
They calculated the distance between two cities. 他们计算了两个城市之间的距离。


现在完成时:
I have calculated the results of the experiment. 我已经计算出实验的结果。
She has calculated the total expenses for the trip. 她已经计算出这次旅行的总支出。


过去完成时:
He had calculated the trajectory before launching the rocket. 他在发射火箭之前已经计算出了轨迹。
They had calculated the profit margin before the meeting. 他们在会议之前就已经计算出了利润率。


将来时:
I will calculate the statistics tomorrow. 我明天会计算统计数据。
We will calculate the impact of the change on the environment. 我们将计算这一变化对环境的影响。


虚拟语气:
If I had calculated correctly, I wouldn't have made that mistake. 如果我计算正确的话，我就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I had calculated the time more accurately. 我真希望我当时更准确地计算时间。


被动语态:
The total cost was calculated by the accountant. 总成本由会计计算。
The trajectory of the projectile is calculated by the computer. 弹丸的轨迹由计算机计算。


疑问句:
How do you calculate the area of a triangle? 你如何计算三角形的面积？
Have you calculated the final score yet? 你已经计算出最终分数了吗？


否定句:
I didn't calculate the shipping costs. 我没有计算运费。
They couldn't calculate the complex equation. 他们无法计算这个复杂的方程式。



PHRASE->
calculate on:  依赖，指望 (rely on)
calculate with:  考虑到，把...考虑进去 (take into account)
calculate something out: 计算出 (compute)
calculate something up:  把...加起来 (add up)
carefully calculate: 仔细计算
