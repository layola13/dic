self-help:/ˈsɛlf hɛlp/| n.|自助；自我帮助|  Self-help refers to any activity that helps one improve their life without the direct help of professionals. This includes various methods like reading self-help books, attending workshops, practicing mindfulness, or engaging in self-reflection. |She found solace in self-help books after her divorce. 她离婚后在自助书籍中找到了慰藉。|self-improvement, self-reliance|dependence, helplessness|名词
