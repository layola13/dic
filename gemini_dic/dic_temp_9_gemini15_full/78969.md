girlishly:/ˈɡɜːrlɪʃli/| adv.|女孩子般地，娇滴滴地 |In a way that is characteristic of a girl; in a childish or naive way.  |例句：She giggled girlishly. 她娇滴滴地咯咯笑。
She laughed girlishly at the joke. 她听到笑话后像个小女孩一样咯咯地笑。|近义词：childishly, coquettishly, demurely|反义词：maturely, mannishly,  severely

He speaks girlishly when nervous. = 他紧张的时候说话像个小女孩。
