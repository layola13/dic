/sɪst/| n. |囊肿；(植物的)囊状体；(矿物)晶洞|A cyst is a sac-like structure that contains fluid or semisolid material.  It can occur in various parts of the body and may be benign or cancerous.  Cysts can be formed from various tissues and often require medical attention depending on their location and characteristics. |例句：The doctor discovered a cyst on her ovary during the ultrasound. 医生在超声波检查中发现了她卵巢上的囊肿。|例句：The geologist found a beautiful geode, a type of cyst, inside the rock. 地质学家在岩石内发现了一个美丽的晶洞，一种囊状体。|近义词：tumor, nodule, vesicle |反义词：None specifically.
