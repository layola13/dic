oklahomans:/ˌoʊkləˈhoʊmən z/| n. |俄克拉荷马州人|People who live in the state of Oklahoma.  The term refers to the residents of Oklahoma, collectively or individually.|例句：Many Oklahomans are proud of their state's history.  许多俄克拉荷马人都为他们州的历史感到自豪。

例句：The Oklahomans I met were incredibly friendly and welcoming. 我遇到的俄克拉荷马人都非常友好和热情。

近义词：Okies (informal and sometimes considered derogatory)


反义词：无
