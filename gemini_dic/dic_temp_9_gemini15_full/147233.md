overmedicates:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛdɪkeɪts/| vt.|过度用药|To give someone or something more medicine than is needed or recommended.  This often implies a negative consequence, such as harmful side effects or ineffective treatment.|例句：The doctor warned against overmedicating the patient, as it could lead to serious complications. 医生警告不要给病人过度用药，因为这可能导致严重的并发症。

The hospital is being investigated for overmedicating elderly patients to control their behavior. 医院正在接受调查，原因是过度给老年病人用药以控制他们的行为。|近义词：overdose, medicate excessively|反义词：undermedicate, treat minimally|动词
