pockets: /ˈpɒkɪts/ /ˈpɑːkɪts/ | n. | 口袋；少量；隐秘处 | A pocket is a small bag sewn into clothing or a similar container.  It can also refer to a small, secluded area or a small amount of something. |例句：He put his hands in his pockets. 他把手插进口袋里。

My coat has two deep pockets. 我的外套有两个深口袋。


近义词： pouch, receptacle, compartment | 反义词： N/A
