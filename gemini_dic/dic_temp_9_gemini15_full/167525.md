previsiting: /ˌpriːˈvɪzɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 预先访问的；事先探访的 |  Describing an action or visit that takes place before a main or more significant visit. It implies a preliminary or preparatory visit. | 例句：The previsiting team assessed the site's suitability before the main construction crew arrived. 预先访问小组在主要施工队到达之前评估了场地的适用性。


The previsiting inspection revealed several potential problems. 事先检查发现了几个潜在问题。


近义词：advance, preliminary, prior, antecedent | 反义词：postvisiting (not a common word), subsequent


词性:形容词
