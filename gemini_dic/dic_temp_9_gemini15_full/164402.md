preenclosed:/ˌpriːɪnˈkloʊzd/| adj.|预先封闭的，预先附上的|Pre-enclosed means something was enclosed or included beforehand, often referring to documents or items sent with a letter or package.|The pre-enclosed form requires your signature. 预先附上的表格需要你的签名。 |近义词：included, enclosed, pre-inserted |反义词：unattached, separate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The document is pre-enclosed with the package.  = 文件预先附在包裹里。
The instructions are pre-enclosed in the box. = 说明书预先装在盒子里。


一般过去时:
The contract was pre-enclosed with the offer letter. = 合同预先附在要约函中。
The warranty was pre-enclosed with the product. = 保修单预先附在产品中。


现在完成时:
They have pre-enclosed all the necessary documents. = 他们已经预先附上了所有必要的文档。
We have pre-enclosed the payment details with the invoice. = 我们已经预先在发票中附上了付款详情。


过去完成时:

She had pre-enclosed the application form in the envelope. = 她已经预先把申请表装进信封里了。
