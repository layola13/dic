deltoid:/ˈdɛltɔɪd/| n. |三角形的；三角肌|A deltoid is a muscle of the shoulder, shaped like the Greek letter delta (Δ).  It's responsible for abduction, flexion, extension, and medial and lateral rotation of the arm. It is also a geometric shape resembling a triangle. |例句：The deltoid muscle is crucial for lifting heavy objects. 三角肌对于举重物至关重要。

The patient suffered a tear in his right deltoid muscle. 病人右三角肌撕裂。


近义词：trapezius (referring to a similar shoulder muscle), triangular|反义词：None (no direct antonym)|词性:名词
