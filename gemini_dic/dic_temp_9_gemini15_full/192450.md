scrubbing:/ˈskrʌbɪŋ/| v.|擦洗；用力擦洗|The act of cleaning something by rubbing it hard, especially with a brush and water or detergent.  It implies a thorough and vigorous cleaning. |例句：She spent the morning scrubbing the kitchen floor. 她花了整个上午擦洗厨房地板。

例句：He scrubbed at the stain until it finally disappeared. 他用力擦洗污渍直到它最终消失。


近义词：cleaning, scouring, scouring, scrubbing up, rubbing


反义词：soiling, dirtying

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I scrub the dishes every night. = 我每天晚上都洗碗。
She scrubs the bathtub weekly. = 她每周都擦洗浴缸。


一般过去时:
He scrubbed the car yesterday. = 他昨天洗车了。
They scrubbed the deck after the party. = 他们聚会后擦洗了甲板。


现在完成时:
I have scrubbed the floor twice today. = 我今天已经擦过两次地板了。
She has scrubbed the grout clean. = 她已经把填缝剂擦干净了。


过去完成时:
He had scrubbed the pots before he left for work. = 他上班前已经洗过锅了。
She had scrubbed the walls before painting them. = 她粉刷墙壁前已经擦洗过墙壁了。


将来时:
I will scrub the bathroom tomorrow. = 我明天将擦洗浴室。
They will scrub the boat before the race. = 比赛前他们将擦洗船只。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would scrub the entire house. = 如果我有时间，我会把整个房子都擦洗干净。
I wish I had scrubbed the counters before the guests arrived. = 我真希望客人们来之前我把台面擦洗干净。


被动语态:
The floors were scrubbed clean. = 地板被擦洗干净了。
The car is being scrubbed right now. = 车子正在被擦洗。


疑问句:
Do you scrub your floors regularly? = 你经常擦洗地板吗？
Have you scrubbed the oven yet? = 你已经擦洗烤箱了吗？


否定句:
He didn't scrub the dishes. = 他没有洗碗。
She doesn't scrub the bathtub often. = 她不经常擦洗浴缸。


PHRASE->
scrub up:  (v.) to wash and clean oneself thoroughly, especially before surgery or an operation.  (擦洗干净；彻底清洗)
scrub out: (v.) to remove something by scrubbing. (擦掉；洗掉)
scrub brush: (n.) a brush used for scrubbing. (刷子)
give something a good scrub: (v.) to clean something thoroughly by scrubbing. (彻底擦洗某物)
power scrubbing: (n.) using a power tool to scrub. (动力擦洗)
