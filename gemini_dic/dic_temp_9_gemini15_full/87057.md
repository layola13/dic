heptagons:/ˈheptəɡɑːnz/| n. |七边形|A heptagon is a polygon with seven sides and seven angles.  Heptagons can be regular (all sides and angles are equal) or irregular (sides and angles are unequal).|The artist used heptagons in the mosaic design.  艺术家在马赛克设计中使用了七边形。|polygon, septagon|—|
