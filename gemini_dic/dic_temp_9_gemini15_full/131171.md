nonbreathing:/ˌnɒnˈbriːðɪŋ/ | adj. |不呼吸的，无呼吸的 | Not breathing; lacking breath.  The term often implies a temporary cessation of breathing, rather than permanent death. | Example: The baby was nonbreathing for a few seconds after birth, but then started crying.  婴儿出生后几秒钟没有呼吸，但随后开始哭泣。|近义词：apneic, breathless, asphyxiated | 反义词：breathing, alive
