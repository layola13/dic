nonsymmetry:/ˌnɒnˈsɪmətri/ | adj. | 非对称性；不对称 | Lack of symmetry; the quality or state of not being symmetrical.  It describes a situation or object that lacks balance or proportion in its parts. | Example: The nonsymmetry of the building's design was intentional, creating a visually striking effect.  建筑设计的不对称性是故意的，创造了一种视觉冲击力。|近义词：asymmetry, imbalance, disproportion |反义词：symmetry, balance, proportion
