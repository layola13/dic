continuo:/kənˈtɪnjuoʊ/|/ˈkɒntɪnjuːoʊ/| adj.|连续的，持续的|Continuous means happening or existing without interruption.|The continuo part in the Baroque music provided a harmonic foundation. 巴洛克音乐中的通奏低音部分提供了和声基础。|constant, uninterrupted, unceasing|intermittent, discontinuous, sporadic|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain continues throughout the day. 雨持续下了整整一天。
He continues to work hard despite the difficulties.  尽管困难重重，他仍然努力工作。


一般过去时:
The music continued for several hours. 音乐持续了几个小时。
She continued to smile despite the bad news. 尽管听到坏消息，她仍然保持微笑。


现在完成时:
It has continued to rain all week.  整个星期都在下雨。
They have continued their research for many years. 他们已经进行了多年的研究。


过去完成时:
The rain had continued for days before the flood.  洪水来临之前，雨已经下了好几天了。
He had continued his studies even after he got injured. 他即使受伤后也继续学习。


将来时:
The concert will continue after the intermission. 音乐会在中场休息后继续。
She will continue to support her family. 她将继续养家糊口。


虚拟语气:
If it continued to rain, the game would be canceled. 如果继续下雨，比赛就会取消。
I wish the happiness continued forever.  我希望幸福永远持续下去。


被动语态:
The project will be continued next year.  该项目将于明年继续进行。
The meeting was continued after a short break. 会议在短暂休息后继续进行。


疑问句:
Does the pain continue? 疼痛持续吗？
Will the show continue? 节目会继续吗？


否定句:

He did not continue his education. 他没有继续他的学业。
