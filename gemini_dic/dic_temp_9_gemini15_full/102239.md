itemizes: /ˈaɪtəmaɪzɪz/ | vt. |列举；逐条说明 | To list or mention (things) individually.  | The report itemizes the company's assets.  这份报告逐条列明了公司的资产。 | enumerate, detail, specify | generalize, summarize | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He itemizes his expenses each month. = 他每个月都逐条列出他的开支。
The teacher itemizes the homework assignments for the week. = 老师逐条列出了本周的家庭作业。

一般过去时:
She itemized the groceries she bought. = 她列出了她买的杂货清单。
The accountant itemized all the deductions on the tax form. = 会计在税表上逐条列出了所有扣除项目。

现在完成时:
He has itemized his charitable donations for the tax return. = 他已将他的慈善捐款列入报税单中。
She has itemized all the changes made to the document. = 她已经列出了对文档所做的所有更改。

过去完成时:
The lawyer had itemized the evidence before presenting his case. = 律师在陈述案情之前已将证据逐条列出。
They had itemized the project's costs before submitting the proposal. = 他们在提交提案之前已列出了项目的成本。

将来时:
She will itemize the expenses for her business trip. = 她将列出她的商务旅行费用。
The manager will itemize the tasks for the team members. = 经理将把任务逐条列给团队成员。

虚拟语气:
If I were to itemize my achievements, it would take hours. = 如果我要列举我的成就，那将需要数小时。
I wish I had itemized my receipts more carefully. = 我真希望我更仔细地列出我的收据。

被动语态:
The costs were itemized in the final report. = 成本在最终报告中被逐条列出。
The changes will be itemized in the next update. = 这些更改将在下一次更新中逐条列出。

疑问句:
Does the contract itemize all the services provided? = 合同是否列明了所有提供的服务？
Have they itemized the risks involved in the project? = 他们是否已列出该项目中涉及的风险？

否定句:
The report didn't itemize the individual contributions. = 报告没有列出个人捐款。
He didn't itemize his expenses, so it was difficult to track his spending. = 他没有逐条列出他的开支，因此很难追踪他的支出。
