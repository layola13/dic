chugger:/ˈtʃʌɡər/| n. v.|  募捐者；（引擎）发出隆隆声；猛喝；|A chugger is a person who solicits donations, often aggressively or persistently, especially for a charity.  The word can also describe the sound of an engine running roughly or the act of drinking something quickly and noisily.|例句：The chugger stopped me on the street and asked for a donation.  这个募捐者在街上拦住我，向我要捐款。|近义词：solicitor, fundraiser, beggar |反义词：donor
