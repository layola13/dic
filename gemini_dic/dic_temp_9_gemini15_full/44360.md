cross-checked:/krɒsˈtʃekt/| v.|交叉核对，互相核实|To verify information or data by comparing it with another source or method.  This ensures accuracy and reduces the risk of errors.|例句：I cross-checked the figures with the original data to ensure accuracy. 我用原始数据交叉核对数据以确保准确性。


例句：The team cross-checked their findings before submitting the report.  团队在提交报告之前互相核实了他们的发现。


近义词：verified, confirmed, validated, double-checked


反义词：unverified, unconfirmed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cross-check the information daily. = 他们每天交叉核对信息。
We cross-check all the data before publishing. = 我们在发布之前交叉核对所有数据。

一般过去时:
He cross-checked the addresses on the list. = 他交叉核对列表上的地址。
She cross-checked the spelling of every name. = 她交叉核对每个名字的拼写。

现在完成时:
They have cross-checked the results multiple times. = 他们已经多次交叉核对结果。
I have cross-checked the information with three different sources. = 我已经用三个不同的来源交叉核对信息。

过去完成时:
They had cross-checked the information before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前已经交叉核对信息。
She had cross-checked the figures before presenting her findings. = 她在展示她的发现之前已经交叉核对数据。

将来时:
We will cross-check the data again tomorrow. = 我们明天将再次交叉核对数据。
He will cross-check the information with the database. = 他将用数据库交叉核对信息。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would cross-check every detail. = 如果我有时间，我会交叉核对每一个细节。
If they had cross-checked the information, they wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果他们交叉核对信息，他们就不会犯那个错误。

被动语态:
The data was cross-checked by two independent analysts. = 数据由两位独立分析师交叉核对。
The information has been cross-checked thoroughly. = 信息已经过彻底的交叉核对。

疑问句:
Have you cross-checked the figures yet? = 你已经交叉核对数据了吗？
Did they cross-check the addresses before sending the letters? = 他们在寄信之前交叉核对地址了吗？

否定句:
They didn't cross-check the information carefully enough. = 他们没有认真地交叉核对信息。
I didn't cross-check the numbers before submitting the report. = 我在提交报告之前没有交叉核对数字。
