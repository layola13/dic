decivilizes: /ˌdiːˈsɪvəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使野蛮化；使不文明；使粗俗化 | To deprive of the characteristics of civilization; to make less cultured or refined; to revert to a primitive or savage state. | 例句：The war decivilized the once-peaceful nation.  = 战争使这个曾经和平的国家野蛮化了。

The corrupt government's policies decivilized the society. = 腐败政府的政策使社会变得粗俗不堪。


近义词：barbarize, brutalize, savage, coarsen, dehumanize | 反义词：civilize, refine, cultivate, humanize | 词性：动词
