censurers:/ˈsɛnzərz/| n. |批评者；责难者；审查者 |Those who express strong disapproval of someone or something.  People who criticize severely. Those who examine something officially to ensure it meets standards. |例句：The censurers of the government's policies were numerous and vocal. 政府政策的批评者众多且声音洪亮。
The film faced heavy criticism from religious censurers. 这部电影受到了宗教审查者的强烈批评。|近义词：critics, condemners, reviewers, monitors |反义词：praisers, approvers
