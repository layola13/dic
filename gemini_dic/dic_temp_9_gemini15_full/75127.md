frisket:/ˈfrɪskɪt/| n.|盖板；遮板；(印刷术)衬纸|A frisket is a piece of paper or other material used to protect parts of a printing surface from ink or paint.  In printing, it's a thin sheet of paper cut to allow certain areas to be printed while masking others.|Example: The printer carefully positioned the frisket to prevent ink from bleeding onto the unwanted areas of the design.  印刷工人小心地放置盖板以防止墨水渗到设计不需要的区域。|近义词：mask, stencil, screen|反义词：N/A|名词
