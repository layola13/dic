peaces:/piːsɪz/| n.|和平（复数）;平静;和解;碎片|The plural form of "peace," referring to instances or periods of peace, or to the separate parts of something broken.  It can also refer to agreements that bring an end to conflict.|例句：The peace talks brought several peaces to the war-torn region. 和谈为饱受战争蹂躏的地区带来了几段和平时期。

例句：After the storm, the broken vase lay in several small peaces. 暴风雨过后，破碎的花瓶散落成几块碎片。|近义词：calm, tranquility, harmony, agreements, fragments|反义词：conflict, war, chaos, discord, whole
