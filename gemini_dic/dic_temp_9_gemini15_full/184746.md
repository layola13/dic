resume:/rɪˈzuːm/| n. v.|简历；摘要；恢复；重新开始|A brief account of a person's education, qualifications, and previous experience, typically sent with a job application.|例句：I've attached my resume to the email.  我已将我的简历附加到电子邮件中。|例句：The doctor ordered a resume of the treatment. 医生下令恢复治疗。|近义词：curriculum vitae (CV), summary, abstract, reinitiate, recommence|反义词：terminate, discontinue, end


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He resumes his work after a long vacation. = 他在长假后恢复工作。
She resumes her studies after a break. = 她休息后恢复学业。


一般过去时:
He resumed his seat after the intermission. = 中场休息后他回到座位上。
She resumed her writing after a long interruption. = 她经过长时间中断后重新开始写作。


现在完成时:
He has resumed his position as CEO. = 他已经恢复了首席执行官的职位。
She has resumed her training after an injury. = 她在受伤后恢复了训练。


过去完成时:
He had resumed his work before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经恢复了工作。
She had resumed her studies before the new semester began. = 新学期开始前她已经恢复了学业。


将来时:
He will resume his duties next week. = 他下周将恢复职责。
She will resume her painting after the exhibition. = 展览结束后她将恢复绘画。


虚拟语气:
If he were to resume his position, the company would improve. = 如果他恢复职位，公司将会好转。
I wish she would resume her singing career. = 我希望她能恢复她的歌唱事业。


被动语态:
His position was resumed by a new employee. = 他的职位由一位新员工接任。
The project was resumed after a delay. = 项目在延误后重新启动。


疑问句:
When will he resume his job? = 他什么时候恢复工作？
Has she resumed her studies yet? = 她恢复学业了吗？


否定句:
He did not resume his work until next month. = 他直到下个月才恢复工作。
She has not yet resumed her training. = 她还没有恢复训练。
