enaliosaur:/ɪˈneɪliəsɔːr/| n. |鳍龙 |An enaliosaur is a marine reptile from the Mesozoic Era, characterized by its flipper-like limbs and adaptations for aquatic life.  They were diverse group including plesiosaurs, ichthyosaurs, and placodonts.  | Example:  The fossil discovered in the limestone quarry was identified as a new species of enaliosaur.  该石灰石采石场发现的化石被鉴定为一种新的鳍龙物种。  |近义词: marine reptile, Mesozoic reptile |反义词: terrestrial reptile
