calculate: /ˈkælkjuleɪt/ | vt. |计算，估算，预测 | To determine or ascertain by mathematical methods; compute.  |例句：I need to calculate the total cost of the project. 我需要计算项目的总成本。|例句：She calculated that she would arrive by 6 pm. 她计算出她会在下午6点到达。|近义词：compute, reckon, estimate |反义词：guess (in the sense of lacking precision)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He calculates the area of the circle. 他计算圆的面积。
We calculate the expenses every month. 我们每月计算支出。

一般过去时:
They calculated the distance incorrectly. 他们计算距离错误了。
She calculated the risk before making the decision.  她在做决定之前计算了风险。

现在完成时:
I have calculated the final score. 我已经计算出最终分数。
He has calculated the probabilities for the experiment. 他已经计算了实验的概率。

过去完成时:
She had calculated the trajectory before launching the rocket. 在发射火箭之前，她已经计算好了轨迹。
They had calculated the budget before the meeting. 他们在会议之前已经计算好了预算。

将来时:
We will calculate the results tomorrow. 我们明天将计算结果。
They will calculate the impact of the policy. 他们将计算该政策的影响。

虚拟语气:
If I could calculate the future, I would be a millionaire. 如果我能预测未来，我会成为百万富翁。
I wish I could calculate the odds of winning the lottery. 我真希望我能计算中彩票的几率。

被动语态:
The area was calculated by the engineer. 这个面积是由工程师计算的。
The total cost will be calculated later. 总成本稍后将计算。

疑问句:
How do you calculate the volume of a cube? 你如何计算立方体的体积？

否定句:

词源:
The word "calculate" comes from the late Middle English word "calculaten," which itself derives from the Latin word "calculare," meaning "to reckon, compute."  The Latin word is ultimately related to the Latin word "calculus," meaning "small stone," referring to the use of stones for counting.
