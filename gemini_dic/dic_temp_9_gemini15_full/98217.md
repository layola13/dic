insecurely:/ˌɪnsɪˈkjʊrli/| adv. | 不安全地；缺乏自信地 | In a way that shows a lack of confidence or self-assurance; in an unstable or unreliable manner. |例句：He spoke insecurely, avoiding eye contact. 他说话吞吞吐吐，不敢直视对方。

例句：The ladder was insecurely fixed to the wall. 梯子不牢固地固定在墙上。
