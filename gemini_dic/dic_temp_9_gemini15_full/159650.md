polony:/ˈpɒləni/| n. |波洛尼亚香肠，肉冻|A type of sausage, typically made from cured pork, often served cold.  It is sometimes also made with beef.  The texture can vary from firm to soft, depending on the recipe.|例句：We had polony sandwiches for lunch. = 午餐我们吃了波洛尼亚香肠三明治。
He bought a large polony for the picnic. = 他为野餐买了一大块波洛尼亚香肠。|近义词：sausage, bologna (US), headcheese|反义词：None specifically, but terms like "fresh meat" or "unprocessed meat" could be considered opposites in the context of preparation.
