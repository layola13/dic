in:/ɪn/| prep.|在…里面，在…期间，穿着|表示位置、时间、状态、方式等。|例句：The book is in the box. 这本书在盒子里。|近义词：inside, within, during |反义词：out, outside, beyond


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat is in the house. = 猫在房子里。
She is in the garden. = 她在花园里。


一般过去时:
He was in London last week. = 他上周在伦敦。
They were in the library studying. = 他们在图书馆学习。


现在完成时:
I have been in this city for ten years. = 我在这个城市住了十年了。
She has been in the hospital since Monday. = 她从星期一开始就在医院里。


过去完成时:
The movie had already started when I got in. = 我进去的时候电影已经开始了。
They had been in the country for a month before they returned. = 他们回国之前已经在乡下待了一个月。


将来时:
I will be in Paris next month. = 我下个月会在巴黎。
The meeting will be in the conference room. = 会议将在会议室举行。


虚拟语气:
If I were in your shoes, I would do differently. = 如果我是你，我会做得不一样。
I wish I were in a tropical paradise right now. = 我真希望我现在在一个热带天堂。


被动语态:
The documents were in the safe. = 文件在保险箱里。
The key was in the drawer. = 钥匙在抽屉里。


疑问句:
Is the dog in the car? = 狗在车里吗？
Are they in the office? = 他们在办公室吗？


否定句:
The key is not in the lock. = 钥匙不在锁孔里。
He wasn't in the mood to talk. = 他没有心情说话。


PHRASE->
in time = 及时
in fact = 事实上
in order to = 为了
in addition = 此外
in case = 以防万一
