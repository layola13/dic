leet:/liːt/ | n. | 骇客行话，黑客语 |  A slang term primarily used by hackers and other computer enthusiasts to represent letters and numbers using similar-looking symbols. It's a form of coded language used to obfuscate or disguise text, often for humorous effect or to circumvent censorship.  | Example:  "The password was written in leetspeak."  "密码是用黑客语写的。" | 近义词：hacker slang, internet slang | 反义词：plain text, standard language |  n.
