USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ratio of debt to equity is 2:1. = 债务与权益的比率是2:1。
What is the student-teacher ratio at this school? = 这所学校的师生比例是多少？


一般过去时:
The ratio of wins to losses was surprisingly high last season. = 上赛季胜负比例出乎意料地高。
The gold-to-silver ratio fluctuated throughout the day. = 黄金白银比率全天都在波动。


现在完成时:
The company has maintained a healthy profit-to-revenue ratio. = 公司一直保持着健康的利润收入比。
The ratio of renewable to non-renewable energy sources has improved significantly. = 可再生能源与不可再生能源的比例有了显著提高。


过去完成时:
Before the merger, the debt-to-equity ratio had been much lower. = 在合并之前，债务与权益比率要低得多。
By the end of the year, the student-teacher ratio had decreased. = 到年底，师生比例有所下降。


将来时:
The government predicts that the ratio of elderly to young people will increase. = 政府预测老年人与年轻人的比例将会增加。
We expect the win-loss ratio to improve next season. = 我们预计下赛季胜负比会提高。


虚拟语气:
If the debt-to-equity ratio were lower, the company would be less risky. = 如果债务与权益比率更低，公司风险就会更小。
I wish the student-teacher ratio were smaller. = 我希望师生比例更小一些。


被动语态:
The ratio of applicants to available positions is being carefully considered. = 申请者与职位空缺的比例正在被仔细考虑。
The ideal ratio of ingredients was determined by the chef. = 厨师确定了理想的配料比例。


疑问句:
What is the price-to-earnings ratio of this stock? = 这只股票的市盈率是多少？
What is the optimal water-cement ratio for this concrete mix? = 这种混凝土混合物的最佳水灰比是多少？


否定句:
The ratio of success to failure was not favorable. = 成功与失败的比例并不理想。
The company did not maintain a consistent profit-to-revenue ratio. = 公司没有保持稳定的利润收入比。
