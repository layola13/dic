charterers:/ˈtʃɑːrtərərz/| n.|租船人，包租人|People or companies that lease or rent a vessel, aircraft, or other means of transport for a specific period or journey.  They are responsible for the operational costs and management during the charter period.|例句：The charterers were responsible for loading the cargo onto the ship.  租船人负责将货物装上船。|例句：The airline and the charterers agreed upon the terms of the flight lease. 航空公司和包机人就航班租赁条款达成一致。|近义词：lessees, renters, hirers|反义词：owners, lessors
