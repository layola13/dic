prorogation:/ˌprɒrəˈɡeɪʃən/|/ˌproʊroʊˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |延期；延长；休会 |The act of prolonging something, especially the extension of a session of parliament or other assembly.  It can also refer to the postponement of something. |例句：The prorogation of parliament caused widespread controversy.  议会休会引起了广泛争议。

例句：The court ordered a prorogation of the trial until next week. 法院下令将审判延期至下周。


近义词：postponement, adjournment, extension, deferment |反义词：conclusion, termination, ending |词性：名词
