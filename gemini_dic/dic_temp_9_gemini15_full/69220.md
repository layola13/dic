fearless:/ˈfɪərləs/| adj.| 无畏的，大胆的 |  Without fear; brave.  The adjective fearless describes someone who is not afraid of anything, or who shows no fear in a dangerous or difficult situation. |  例句：The fearless firefighter rushed into the burning building.  无畏的消防员冲进了燃烧的建筑物。

例句：She showed a fearless determination to succeed. 她表现出一种无畏的决心去成功。


近义词：intrepid, courageous, bold, dauntless, valiant

反义词：timid, cowardly, fearful, apprehensive


词性：形容词
