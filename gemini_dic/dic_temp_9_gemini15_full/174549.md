quintuple:/ˈkwɪntəpəl/| adj., v., n.|五倍的；五重奏；使成五倍|To make or become five times as great or numerous.  It can function as an adjective, verb, or noun.|

例句：

The company's profits quintupled last year. = 公司的利润去年增长了五倍。

He quintupled the recipe to feed the crowd. = 他将食谱的用量增加了五倍以供众人食用。


近义词：fivefold, multiply by five

反义词：reduce to one-fifth


词性：adj., v., n.
