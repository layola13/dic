died:/daɪd/| v.|死了，去世了|To cease to live; to expire.|例句：My grandfather died last year.  我的祖父去年去世了。
例句：The plant died because it didn't get enough water. 这株植物因为缺水而死了。|近义词：passed away, perished, expired|反义词：born, lived


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dies every day in the game. 他在游戏中每天都死。
They die for their country. 他们为国捐躯。

一般过去时:
He died yesterday. 他昨天去世了。
The dog died peacefully in its sleep. 那条狗安详地睡梦中去世了。

现在完成时:
He has died. 他已经去世了。
Many people have died in this war.  这场战争中许多人丧生。

过去完成时:
He had died before the doctor arrived.  医生到达之前他已经去世了。
She had died before I could say goodbye. 我还没来得及道别她就去世了。

将来时:
He will die soon. 他很快就会死。
If he continues to drink like that, he will die early. 如果他继续那样喝酒，他会早死的。


虚拟语气:
If I died tomorrow, I would have no regrets. 如果我明天死了，我将毫无遗憾。
I wish I had died before I saw this happen. 我真希望在我看到这件事发生之前就死了。


被动语态:
(Not applicable in a natural way.  "Died" is an intransitive verb and cannot be passively voiced.)


疑问句:
Did he die? 他死了吗？
When did she die? 她什么时候死的？


否定句:
He didn't die. 他没死。
The soldier didn't die in vain.  那个士兵的牺牲并非徒劳。


PHRASE->
die down:逐渐平息
die out:灭绝；消失
die hard:顽固不化
die of:死于（疾病等）
die from:死于（外因）
