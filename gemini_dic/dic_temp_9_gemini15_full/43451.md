crash's:/kræʃz/| n.|坠毁；碰撞；失败|The sound of the crash woke me up. 坠毁的声音把我吵醒了。|近义词：collision, accident, failure|反义词：success, triumph


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plane crashes every year. = 飞机每年都会坠毁。
The market crashes frequently. = 市场经常崩盘。


一般过去时:
The car crashed into a tree. = 汽车撞到了一棵树上。
The stock market crashed in 1929. = 股市在1929年崩盘。


现在完成时:
The economy has crashed several times. = 经济已经几次崩溃了。
The server has crashed again. = 服务器又崩溃了。


过去完成时:
The system had crashed before I could save my work. = 系统在我保存工作之前就崩溃了。
The company had crashed before the merger. = 公司在合并之前就倒闭了。


将来时:
The stock market will crash if interest rates rise. = 如果利率上升，股市将会崩盘。
The plane will crash if the engine fails. = 如果引擎失效，飞机将会坠毁。


虚拟语气:
If the brakes had worked, the car wouldn't have crashed. = 如果刹车有效，汽车就不会坠毁了。
If I had known the road was icy, I wouldn't have crashed. = 如果我知道路面结冰，我就不会出车祸了。


被动语态:
The car was crashed by a drunk driver. = 汽车是被一个醉酒的司机撞坏的。
The system was crashed by a virus. = 系统是被病毒搞垮的。


疑问句:
Did the car crash? = 车祸了吗？
Has the server crashed? = 服务器崩溃了吗？


否定句:
The plane didn't crash. = 飞机没有坠毁。
The computer didn't crash. = 电脑没有死机。
