geothermic:/ˌdʒioʊˈθɜːrmɪk/| adj.|地热的；由地热产生的| Relating to or produced by the internal heat of the earth.  Geothermal energy is harnessed from underground reservoirs of hot water or steam.|例句：The geothermal energy plant uses steam from underground to generate electricity. 地热发电厂利用地下蒸汽发电。|例句： Iceland utilizes its abundant geothermal resources for heating and electricity generation. 冰岛利用其丰富的地下热资源用于供暖和发电。|近义词：volcanic, subterranean|反义词：surface, external
