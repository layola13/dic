screen:/skriːn/| n. |屏幕；遮蔽物；筛选；银幕|A flat surface, typically made of glass, on which images are displayed, as on a television or computer.  It can also refer to a protective barrier or a process of selection or filtering.|例句：The screen on my phone is cracked. 我的手机屏幕裂了。

The security screen kept out the intruders. 安全屏挡住了入侵者。|近义词：display, monitor, filter, sieve|反义词：reality (in the sense of escaping reality)|词性:名词
