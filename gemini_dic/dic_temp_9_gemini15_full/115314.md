mannerliness:/ˈmænəlinəs/| n. |礼貌；优雅；得体 |The quality of being polite and well-behaved; propriety of manners.  It refers to the refined and courteous behavior exhibited by someone.  |例句：Her mannerliness impressed everyone at the dinner party.  她的礼貌给晚宴上的每个人留下了深刻的印象。

例句：The children were taught good mannerliness from a young age.孩子们从小就被教导要有良好的礼貌。


近义词：courtesy, politeness, civility, grace, refinement

反义词：rudeness, discourtesy, incivility, boorishness, vulgarity

词性：名词
