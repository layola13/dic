pauses:/ˈpɔːzɪz/ | n. |暂停；停顿；间歇 |The pauses in his speech were deliberate. 他讲话时的停顿是故意的。|The music had long pauses between the movements.乐曲乐章之间有很长的停顿。|近义词：breaks, interruptions, rests |反义词：continuations, uninterrupted flow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pauses frequently during his lectures. 他在讲课过程中经常停顿。
She pauses to consider her next move. 她停下来思考下一步行动。


一般过去时:
He paused before answering the question. 他在回答问题之前停顿了一下。
The music paused abruptly. 音乐突然停止了。


现在完成时:
She has paused several times throughout her speech.她在演讲中已经停顿了好几次了。
He has paused for breath. 他已经停下来喘口气了。


过去完成时:
He had paused several times before he finally spoke. 他在最终开口说话之前已经停顿了好几次了。
The rain had paused for a while before starting again. 雨停了一会儿又下起来了。


将来时:
He will pause to take a photograph. 他将停下来拍照。
She will pause before making a decision. 她会在做决定之前停顿一下。


虚拟语气:
If he paused for a moment, he might have seen the answer. 如果他停顿一下，他可能会看到答案。
I wish he would pause and think before he speaks. 我希望他在说话之前能停下来思考一下。


被动语态:
The performance was paused due to technical difficulties. 演出因技术问题而暂停。
The recording was paused accidentally. 录音意外暂停了。


疑问句:
Does he pause often during his presentations? 他在演讲中经常停顿吗？
Will she pause before crossing the street? 她过马路前会停顿一下吗？


否定句:
He didn't pause at all during his presentation. 他在演讲过程中根本没有停顿。
She doesn't pause to consider the consequences. 她不会停下来考虑后果。


PHRASE->
brief pauses: 短暂停顿
long pauses: 长暂停
awkward pauses: 尴尬的停顿
pregnant pauses: 富有深意的停顿
strategic pauses: 策略性停顿
