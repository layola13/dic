monitored:/ˈmɒnɪtərd/|/ˈmɑːnɪtərd/| v. |监控，监测，监督|To watch, observe, listen to, or check (something) regularly in order to prevent, control, or improve something.|The police monitored the suspect's movements. |警察监控了嫌疑人的行动。|observed, tracked, supervised|ignored, neglected|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We monitor the server's performance daily. = 我们每天监控服务器的性能。
They monitor air quality in the city. = 他们监测城市空气质量。

一般过去时:
The doctor monitored the patient's vital signs. = 医生监测了病人的生命体征。
The company monitored employee productivity last quarter. = 公司上个季度监控了员工的生产力。

现在完成时:
We have monitored the project's progress closely. = 我们一直密切关注项目的进展。
They have monitored the situation for several weeks. = 他们已经监测了这个情况好几个星期了。

过去完成时:
They had monitored the system for months before the failure. = 系统故障之前，他们已经监控了几个月。
The scientists had monitored the weather patterns for years. = 科学家们多年来一直在监测天气模式。

将来时:
We will monitor the experiment's results carefully. = 我们将仔细监测实验结果。
They will monitor the patient's recovery closely. = 他们将密切监测病人的恢复情况。

虚拟语气:
If we had monitored the situation earlier, we could have prevented the crisis. = 如果我们早点监控情况，就能避免这场危机。
It is essential that the data be monitored regularly. = 定期监测数据至关重要。

被动语态:
The suspect's calls were monitored by the police. = 嫌疑人的电话被警方监听了。
The patient's heart rate is being monitored continuously. = 病人的心率正在持续监测中。

疑问句:
Are they monitoring the traffic flow? = 他们正在监测交通流量吗？
Was the equipment monitored properly? = 设备是否被正确监控？

否定句:
They did not monitor the system adequately. = 他们没有充分监控系统。
The company failed to monitor the risks involved. = 公司未能监控所涉及的风险。
