forefather:/ˈfɔːrfɑːðər/| n. |祖先，祖宗 |A forefather is a direct ancestor, usually several generations removed.  It often implies a significant historical or familial connection.| Example:  He traced his lineage back to a forefather who fought in the Revolutionary War. 他追溯了他的血统到一位在独立战争中战斗过的祖先。|近义词：ancestor, progenitor |反义词：descendant, offspring
