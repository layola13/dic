postponable: /pəʊstˈpəʊnəbl/ | adj. | 可延期的，可推迟的 | Capable of being postponed or delayed.  Something that is postponable can be put off to a later time. |

例句：

The meeting is postponable; we can reschedule it for next week.  会议可以延期；我们可以把它改到下周。

The deadline for the project is postponable, but only by a few days. 项目的截止日期是可以延期的，但只能延期几天。


近义词：deferrable, delayable, adjustable

反义词：unavoidable, unavoidable, immediate


词性：形容词
