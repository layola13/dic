dishonest:/dɪsˈɒnɪst/ | adj. | 不诚实的，欺诈的 | Not honest; characterized by a lack of honesty or integrity; deceitful.  |例句：He was accused of dishonest behavior in his business dealings. 他被指控在其商业交易中行为不端。

例句：The dishonest employee stole money from the company.  这位不诚实的员工偷了公司的钱。

近义词：fraudulent, deceitful, untruthful, deceptive, unscrupulous, shady

反义词：honest, truthful, upright, trustworthy, virtuous, sincere


USAGE->

词源:
dishonest is formed from the prefix "dis-" (meaning "not" or "opposite of") and the adjective "honest".  "Honest" traces back to Old English "honeste" meaning "honor," related to "honour" and ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*hanz-"  meaning "honor, grace".
