heteronymic:/ˌhɛtəˈnɪmɪk/ | adj. |  同形异义的；(词语)具有不同发音的 |  Describing words that are spelled the same but have different pronunciations and meanings.  These words are also known as homographs with different pronunciations. | Example:  The word "lead" can be pronounced /lɛd/ (meaning to guide) or /liːd/ (meaning a heavy metal).  The word "present" can be pronounced /ˈprɛzənt/ (meaning a gift) or /prɪˈzɛnt/ (meaning to give).| 近义词：homographic (but emphasizes the same spelling aspect more strongly)| 反义词：homophone (same pronunciation, different spelling)
