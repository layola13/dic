charmer:/ˈtʃɑːrmər/| n. |有魅力的人，讨人喜欢的人|a person who is charming and attractive|He's a real charmer; everyone loves him. 他真是个有魅力的人；每个人都喜欢他。|近义词：sweetheart, darling, captivating person|反义词：repellent, unpleasant person

USAGE->

He charms everyone he meets. = 他迷住了他遇到的每一个人。
She charms her way into every situation. = 她用她的魅力应对各种情况。


一般过去时:
