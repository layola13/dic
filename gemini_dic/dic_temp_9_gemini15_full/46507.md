daisi:/ˈdeɪzi/| n.|雏菊|Daisy is a common wildflower with white petals and a yellow center. 雏菊是一种常见的野花，花瓣白色，中心黄色。|近义词：day's eye, ox-eye daisy|反义词:无


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Daisies bloom in spring. = 雏菊在春天盛开。
She picks daisies every morning. = 她每天早上摘雏菊。


一般过去时:
The children picked daisies in the field. = 孩子们在田野里摘雏菊。
He gave her a bouquet of daisies. = 他送给她一束雏菊。


现在完成时:
I have planted many daisies in my garden. = 我在我的花园里种了许多雏菊。

将来时:
