ionizable:/aɪˈaɪznəbəl/ | adj. | 可电离的，可离子化的 | Able to be ionized; capable of forming ions.  This refers to a substance's ability to lose or gain electrons, thereby becoming an ion.  Ionizable substances often conduct electricity when dissolved in a solution. |  The salt is highly ionizable in water, leading to a conductive solution.  盐在水中高度可电离，从而形成导电溶液。


The strong acid is completely ionizable.  这种强酸是完全可电离的。|  近义词：ionisable (British spelling), dissociable | 反义词：nonionizable, undissociable |  形容词
