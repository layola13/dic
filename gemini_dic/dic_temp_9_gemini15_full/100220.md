interrogational:/ˌɪntərəˈɡeɪʃənəl/| adj. |疑问的，讯问的|Relating to or of the nature of a question; questioning.|The interrogational style of the essay made it engaging.|近义词：inquisitive, questioning|反义词：declarative, affirmative|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Is this your book? = 这是你的书吗？
Are they coming to the party? = 他们要来参加聚会吗？


一般过去时:
Did you go to school yesterday? = 你昨天去上学了吗？
Were they at home last night? = 他们昨晚在家吗？


现在完成时:
Have you finished your work? = 你完成你的工作了吗？
Has she ever been to Europe? = 她去过欧洲吗？


过去完成时:
Had you seen him before? = 你以前见过他吗？
Had they left before the rain started? = 雨开始下之前他们离开了嗎？


将来时:
Will you be at home tomorrow? = 你明天在家吗？
Will it rain tomorrow? = 明天会下雨吗？


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would ask him. = 如果我是你，我会问他。
If she had studied harder, she would have passed. = 如果她学习更努力，她就会及格了。


被动语态:
Was the report written by him? = 报告是他写的吗？
Are the questions being answered correctly? = 问题被正确回答了吗？


疑问句:
How old are you? = 你几岁了？
Where do you live? = 你住在哪里？


否定句:
This is not an interrogational sentence. = 这不是一个疑问句。
That wasn't an interrogative question. = 那不是一个疑问句。


PHRASE->
interrogational sentence = 疑问句
interrogational pronoun = 疑问代词
interrogational adverb = 疑问副词
interrogational mood = 疑问语气
interrogational word = 疑问词
