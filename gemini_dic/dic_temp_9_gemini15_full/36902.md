colorism:/ˈkʌlərɪzəm/| n. |肤色歧视；色素歧视|The prejudice or discrimination based on the social construct of skin color.  It often manifests as preferential treatment for individuals with lighter skin tones and discrimination against those with darker skin tones, even within the same racial or ethnic group. |例句：Colorism is a pervasive issue in many societies, leading to disparities in education, employment, and social status. 肤色歧视在许多社会中是一个普遍存在的问题，导致教育、就业和社会地位方面的差距。

例句：The model's success was partly attributed to her light skin tone, highlighting the insidious nature of colorism in the fashion industry. 模特的成功部分归因于她较浅的肤色，突显了时尚行业中肤色歧视的阴险本质。


近义词：racial discrimination, skin-tone bias, shadeism


反义词：colorblindness (in the sense of not judging based on skin color), racial equality


词性:名词
