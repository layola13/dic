idiopathic:/ˌɪdiəˈpæθɪk/| adj.|特发性的，原因不明的|Of unknown cause; self-originated.|The cause of his illness is idiopathic.  他的病因不明。|近义词：essential, primary, spontaneous|反义词：symptomatic, secondary


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The doctor says her condition is idiopathic. = 医生说她的病情是特发性的。
This type of rash is often idiopathic. = 这种皮疹通常是特发性的。

一般过去时:
The researchers found that the disease was idiopathic in many cases. = 研究人员发现，在许多情况下，这种疾病是特发性的。
Her doctor initially believed her symptoms were idiopathic. = 她的医生最初认为她的症状是特发性的。


现在完成时:
Scientists have yet to determine the cause of many idiopathic disorders. = 科学家们尚未确定许多特发性疾病的原因。
We have ruled out several causes, but the illness remains idiopathic. = 我们已经排除了几个原因，但疾病仍然是特发性的。


过去完成时:
Before the advanced testing, the condition had been considered idiopathic. = 在进行先进检测之前，这种情况被认为是特发性的。
Doctors had initially diagnosed the condition as idiopathic, but further investigation revealed a cause. = 医生最初将这种情况诊断为特发性疾病，但进一步调查揭示了原因。


将来时:
Further research will hopefully clarify whether the condition is truly idiopathic. = 进一步的研究有望阐明这种情况是否真的是特发性的。
We will continue to monitor the patient to see if the idiopathic nature of the condition persists. = 我们将继续监测病人，看看这种情况的特发性是否持续存在。


虚拟语气:
If the condition were idiopathic, treatment would focus on symptom management. = 如果这种情况是特发性的，治疗将侧重于症状管理。
I wish we knew the cause; it's frustrating when a disease is idiopathic. = 我希望我们知道原因；当一种疾病是特发性疾病时，这令人沮丧。


被动语态:
Many cases of this disorder are classified as idiopathic. = 许多这种疾病的病例被归类为特发性疾病。
The origin of the disease is believed to be idiopathic, but more research is needed. = 这种疾病的起源被认为是特发性的，但需要进一步研究。


疑问句:
Is this type of headache idiopathic? = 这种类型的头痛是特发性的吗？
Could the underlying cause of her fatigue be idiopathic? = 她疲劳的根本原因可能是特发性的吗？


否定句:
The disease is not idiopathic; its cause has been identified. = 这种疾病不是特发性的；它的病因已被确定。
The symptoms were initially thought to be idiopathic, but later testing revealed a viral infection. = 症状最初被认为是特发性的，但后来的测试显示是病毒感染。
