overuse:/ˈoʊvərjuːs/ | n. v. |过度使用，滥用 |The excessive use of something to the point of inefficiency or damage.  |例句：The overuse of antibiotics has led to the development of resistant bacteria.  抗生素的过度使用导致了耐药细菌的产生。

例句：The overuse of fertilizer can damage the soil.  化肥的过度使用会破坏土壤。


近义词：misuse, abuse, exploitation |反义词：underuse, sparing use |词性: noun, verb
