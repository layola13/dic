reprover:/rɪˈpruːvər/| n. |责备者；批评者；训诫者 |A reprover is a person who reproves or criticizes someone.  They often point out faults or mistakes with the intention of correcting behavior. |例句：The teacher acted as a reprover, pointing out the student's grammatical errors.  老师扮演着责备者的角色，指出了学生的语法错误。

The stern reprover delivered a harsh lecture on the importance of honesty.  严厉的责备者就诚实的重要性发表了一篇严厉的演讲。


近义词：critic, censor, admonisher, chastiser |反义词：praiser, flatterer |名词
