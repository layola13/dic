clean-and-jerking:/ˌkliːn ænd ˈdʒɜːrkɪŋ/ | n. |挺举(举重项目) |The clean and jerk is a weightlifting movement consisting of two distinct parts: the clean and the jerk.  It involves lifting a barbell from the ground to the shoulders (clean) and then from the shoulders overhead (jerk).|近义词：weightlifting, overhead lift |反义词：N/A
