ram-raiding:/ˈræmˌreɪdɪŋ/| n. | 撞店抢劫 | The act of driving a vehicle through the front of a building, typically a shop or store, to steal goods.  This is a violent and destructive form of robbery. | Example: The police are investigating a recent ram-raiding incident at the jewelry store on Main Street.  警方正在调查最近发生在主街珠宝店的撞店抢劫案。 | 近义词: smash-and-grab, burglary, robbery | 反义词: N/A | 名词
