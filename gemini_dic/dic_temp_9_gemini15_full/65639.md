evangelizing: /ɪˈvændʒəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 传教；布道；宣扬 | The act of spreading the Christian gospel or a particular religious belief.  It involves actively sharing one's faith with others, often through preaching, teaching, or missionary work. |

例句：

The missionaries devoted their lives to evangelizing in remote villages.  传教士们将毕生精力奉献给了在偏远村庄的传教工作。

He was criticized for his aggressive style of evangelizing on social media. 他因在社交媒体上咄咄逼人的传教风格而受到批评。


近义词：preaching, proselytizing, propagating, missionary work

反义词：suppressing (beliefs), silencing (voices), secularizing
