salpingitis:/ˌsælpiŋˈaɪtɪs/| n. |输卵管炎|Inflammation of the fallopian tubes.  This condition is often caused by infection, typically sexually transmitted infections (STIs) such as chlamydia or gonorrhea, or pelvic inflammatory disease (PID). Symptoms can include pelvic pain, abnormal vaginal discharge, fever, and irregular menstrual bleeding.  Diagnosis involves physical examination, pelvic ultrasound, and sometimes laparoscopy. Treatment usually involves antibiotics to combat the infection.|The patient presented with salpingitis and was treated with intravenous antibiotics.|近义词：tubal inflammation,  PID (pelvic inflammatory disease) (partially)|反义词：None directly, but a state of healthy fallopian tubes could be considered an antonym.|名词
