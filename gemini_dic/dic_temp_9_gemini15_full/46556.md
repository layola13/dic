dallies:/ˈdæliz/| v.| 虚度光阴；磨蹭；玩弄；闲逛 | To waste time; to spend time idly; to trifle; to flirt. |例句：He dallied over his breakfast, completely forgetting about the meeting. 他慢慢悠悠地吃着早餐，完全忘记了会议。

He dallied with the idea of quitting his job but ultimately decided to stay. 他想过辞职，但最终还是决定留下。


近义词：linger, procrastinate, trifle, toy, flirt |反义词：hurry, rush
