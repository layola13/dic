foreclosed:/ˌfɔːrˈkloʊzd/|/ˈfɔːrˌkləʊzd/| adj. v. |止赎的；被取消抵押品赎回权的；取消抵押品赎回权|

英文解析:  Foreclosed refers to a property that has been seized by a lender because the borrower failed to make the mortgage payments.  It can also describe the act of seizing the property.  The term implies the legal process of foreclosure has been completed.

例句:
The bank foreclosed on his house after he fell behind on his mortgage payments. =  在他拖欠抵押贷款后，银行取消了他的房屋抵押赎回权。

The property was foreclosed last year. = 这处房产去年被取消了抵押品赎回权。


近义词: repossessed, seized


反义词: redeemed


词性: Adjective (adj.) and Verb (v.)
