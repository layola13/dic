homers:/ˈhoʊmərz/ | n. |本垒打；荷马史诗；(Homer的复数) |A home run in baseball;  referring to the epic poems attributed to Homer; the plural of Homer. |例句：He hit three homers in the game. 他在这场比赛中打出了三个本垒打。


例句：The professor lectured on the themes of Homer's epics. 教授讲授了荷马史诗的主题。


近义词：grand slam (棒球), home run (棒球)


反义词：strikeout (棒球)


词性：名词
