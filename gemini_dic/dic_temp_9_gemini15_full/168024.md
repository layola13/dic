pristinely:/ˈprɪstinli/| adv. | 纯净地；完美地；崭新地 | In a pristine or unspoiled condition; perfectly clean, fresh, or new.  | 例句：The mountain lake was pristinely clear.  山间湖水清澈见底。

例句：The newly renovated house looked pristinely clean.  刚装修好的房子看起来干干净净。


近义词：perfectly, immaculately, flawlessly, cleanly

反义词：dirtily, messily, sloppily, roughly


词源：源自于名词 pristine (/ˈprɪstiːn/),  意为“原始的；纯净的”，最终来源于拉丁语“pristinus”，意为“最古老的；原始的”。
