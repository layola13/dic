grant-maintained:/ˈɡrɑːnt meɪnˈteɪnd/| adj.|特许维持的；政府拨款维持的|  Referring to schools in England and Wales that received funding directly from the government rather than from a local education authority.  These schools were independent of local council control but still publicly funded. They existed from 1988 to 1998.|例句：Many parents favored grant-maintained schools because they offered more autonomy.|近义词：independently funded, government-funded|反义词：locally controlled, council-maintained
