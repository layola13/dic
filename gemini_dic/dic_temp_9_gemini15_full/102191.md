italianesque:/ˌɪtæliəˈnesk/| adj.|意大利风格的；仿意大利式的|Resembling or suggestive of Italian style, especially in architecture or art.  The term often implies a romanticized or idealized version of Italian styles, rather than a strictly accurate representation.|例句：The restaurant had an italianesque ambiance with exposed brick and wrought-iron details. 这家餐厅有着意大利风格的氛围，裸露的砖块和锻铁细节。  The villa possessed an italianesque design, with its arched windows and terracotta roof. 这座别墅采用意大利风格的设计，有着拱形窗户和赤陶屋顶。|近义词：Italianate, Italian-style|反义词：non-Italian, plain
