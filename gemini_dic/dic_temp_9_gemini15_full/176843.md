ready-mixed:/ˈredi mɪkst/| adj.|预拌的，预混合的|Already mixed and ready for use.  This term typically refers to materials like concrete or mortar that are supplied pre-mixed, eliminating the need for on-site mixing.|例句：We used ready-mixed concrete for the foundation. 我们用预拌混凝土浇筑地基。|例句：The ready-mixed paint made the project much faster. 预拌油漆使工程进展得更快。|近义词：pre-mixed, pre-blended |反义词：homemade, on-site mixed
