ordeals:/ˈɔːrdiːlz/| n.|严酷的考验；磨难|A severely trying experience.  An ordeal is a difficult or painful experience, often a test of one's endurance, courage, or faith.|例句：The long journey was a real ordeal. 这趟漫长的旅程真是苦不堪言。|例句：She endured the ordeal with remarkable courage. 她以非凡的勇气忍受了这场磨难。|近义词：trial, tribulation, hardship, test|反义词：pleasure, ease, comfort|名词
