chickpea:/ˈtʃɪkˌpiː/| n.|鹰嘴豆，鸡豆|A chickpea is a legume, a type of edible seed from the plant Cicer arietinum. They are a good source of protein and fiber, and are used in many cuisines around the world.  They are also known as garbanzo beans.|例句：I love to add chickpeas to my salads.  我喜欢在我的沙拉里加鹰嘴豆。

例句：Hummus is made from mashed chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic.  鹰嘴豆泥是由鹰嘴豆泥、芝麻酱、柠檬汁和大蒜制成的。


近义词：garbanzo bean, ceci (Italian)
反义词：无
词性:名词
