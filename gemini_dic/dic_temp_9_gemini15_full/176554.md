re-shift:/ˌriːˈʃɪft/| vt.|重新调整，转移，改组|To shift again or anew; to change the position, arrangement, or distribution of something.|例句：We need to re-shift the furniture to make more space. 我们需要重新调整家具以腾出更多空间。
例句：The company decided to re-shift its marketing strategy. 公司决定重新调整其营销策略。|近义词：rearrange, reorganize, reposition |反义词：leave unchanged, maintain the status quo.
