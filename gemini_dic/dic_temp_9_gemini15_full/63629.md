eolian:/ˈiːliən/| adj.|风成的，风积的；伊奥利亚的| relating to or caused by the wind, especially in the geological context.  Eolian processes shape landscapes through wind erosion and deposition.  The term also refers to the Aeolian Islands, a volcanic archipelago off the coast of Sicily.|例句：The sand dunes are a striking example of eolian landforms.  这些沙丘是风成地貌的突出例子。

The eolian deposits covered the ancient ruins. 风积物覆盖了古代遗址。|近义词：wind-formed, wind-blown|反义词：aquatic, fluvial (referring to water-related processes)
