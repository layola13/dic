remating:/riˈmeɪtɪŋ/| v.|再次交配|The act of mating again, typically after a period of time or after a previous mating has failed to produce offspring.|例句：The female bird engaged in remating after her first clutch of eggs failed to hatch.  这只母鸟在第一窝蛋孵化失败后再次交配。

例句：Successful remating is crucial for the continuation of the species in this endangered animal.  对于这种濒危动物来说，成功的再次交配对于物种的延续至关重要。
