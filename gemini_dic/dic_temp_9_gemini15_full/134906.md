nonmuscular: /ˌnɒnˈmʌskjʊlə(r)/ | adj. |非肌肉的；非肌性的| Not involving or relating to muscles.  This adjective describes something that is not related to or caused by muscles.  It often contrasts with muscular actions or structures. |例句：The movement was nonmuscular, likely caused by a neurological issue.  非肌肉运动，可能是神经系统问题造成的。

The nonmuscular tremors were a symptom of the disease.  非肌性震颤是该疾病的症状。

近义词：non-muscular, unmuscular (less common) |反义词：muscular

词源:  The word is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the adjective "muscular" (relating to muscles). "Muscular" originates from the Latin word "musculus," meaning "little mouse," a reference to the shape of certain muscles.
