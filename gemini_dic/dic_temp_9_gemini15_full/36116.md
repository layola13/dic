coercions:/kəʊˈɜːʃənz/ | n. | 强迫；胁迫；压制 | The act of compelling someone to do something by using force or threats.  Coercions often involve the use of power or authority to influence a person's actions against their will.  They can range from subtle pressures to overt violence. |例句：The confession was obtained under duress and through coercions.  这次供词是在胁迫和强迫下获得的。


The government's coercions led to widespread protests. 政府的压制导致了大规模抗议。 |近义词：compulsions, intimidations, duress, pressures |反义词：persuasion, encouragement, freedom
