re-purifying:/ˌriːˈpjʊərɪfaɪɪŋ/| v. |再纯化，重新纯化 |The process of purifying something again to remove further impurities.  This typically involves repeating a purification process, such as filtration, distillation, or chromatography. |例句：The chemist spent hours re-purifying the sample to achieve the desired purity level.  化学家花了数小时重新纯化样品以达到所需的纯度水平。

例句：The pharmaceutical company employs a rigorous re-purifying process to ensure the safety and efficacy of its drugs. 制药公司采用严格的再纯化工艺来确保其药物的安全性和有效性。


近义词：repurification, refining, further purifying, secondary purification

反义词：contaminating, polluting


词性：动词
