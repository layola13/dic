porphyria:/pɔːˈfɪəriə/| n. |紫质；紫红病；卟啉症 |A rare genetic disorder characterized by the accumulation of porphyrins in the body, often leading to skin sensitivity to light, neurological problems, and disfigurement.  It is caused by defects in enzymes involved in heme synthesis. |The patient's symptoms were consistent with acute porphyria.  这位病人的症状与急性紫质症相符。| Porphyrinopathy,  hematoporphyria |healthy, normal|名词
