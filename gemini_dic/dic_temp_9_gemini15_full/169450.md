proof-read:/ˈpruːfˌriːd/ | vt. |校对，校阅 | To carefully examine a text for errors in spelling, grammar, punctuation, and style before it is published or printed.  | 例句：I need to proofread this essay before submitting it.  我需要在提交论文之前校对一下。

例句：The editor proofread the manuscript carefully before sending it to the printer. 编辑在送交印刷厂之前仔细校对稿件。


近义词：edit, revise, check, correct | 反义词：None (The opposite would be to leave errors uncorrected) | 词性:动词
