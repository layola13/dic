eluders: /ɪˈluːdərz/ | n. | 逃避者；躲避者 | Those who successfully evade capture or detection;  people or things that are difficult to find, understand, or define.  | 例句：The eluders of justice remained at large.  正义的逃避者仍然逍遥法外。

The elusive thief proved to be a master eluder, constantly slipping through the police's grasp.  那个难以捉摸的小偷证明自己是个逃避高手，不断地从警方的掌握中溜走。 | 近义词：evaders, avoiders, escapers | 反义词：captives, prisoners
