preseminal:/ˌpriːˈsɛmɪnəl/| adj.|  射精前的；精液前的 | Relating to or occurring before the emission of semen.  The term often refers to the pre-ejaculatory fluid. |例句：Preseminal fluid, also known as pre-cum, can contain sperm in some men.  射精前的液体，也称为前精液，在某些男性中可能含有精子。

例句：The study examined the composition of preseminal fluid and its role in fertility.  这项研究检查了射精前液体的成分及其在生育中的作用。


近义词：pre-ejaculatory, ante-ejaculatory | 反义词：post-ejaculatory  | 词性:形容词
