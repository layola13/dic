dumbshow:/ˈdʌmʃoʊ/ | n. |哑剧；默剧 | A performance using gestures and mime instead of speech.  It is a form of theatrical entertainment where the actors convey a story or message through physical actions and facial expressions without speaking.  | Example: The silent film era saw the rise of elaborate dumbshows in place of dialogue.  哑剧时代见证了代替对话的精心设计的哑剧的兴起。 | 近义词：mime, pantomime | 反义词：spoken drama, dialogue
