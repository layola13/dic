champ:/tʃæmp/| n. v.|冠军，优胜者；大力咀嚼|A champion, a person who wins a competition; to chew vigorously.|例句：He's the world champ in boxing. 他是世界拳击冠军。|例句：The dog champed at the bit.  狗用力地啃着嚼子。|近义词：champion, victor,  winner|反义词：loser,  defeated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He champs at the bit. = 他按捺不住。
She champs her food. = 她狼吞虎咽地吃东西。

一般过去时:
He champed at the bit during the race. = 比赛期间他按捺不住。
She champed her food quickly. = 她很快地狼吞虎咽地吃完了食物。

He has champed at the bit all morning. = 他整个上午都按捺不住了。
She has champed through a whole bag of chips. = 她已经吃掉了一整袋薯片。

过去完成时:
