lumbar:/ˈlʌmbər/| adj. n.|腰部的，腰椎的|Relating to the loins or lumbar region of the body.  The lumbar region is the lower part of the back, between the ribs and the pelvis.|例句：He experienced severe lumbar pain after lifting the heavy box. 他搬动重箱子后，腰部剧烈疼痛。  The doctor examined his lumbar spine for any abnormalities. 医生检查了他的腰椎是否有异常。|近义词：spinal, dorsal (in part), lower back|反义词：cervical (opposite end of spine)
