machiavellianly:/ˌmækɪəˈvɛliənli/| adv.|  狡猾地；权谋地；精于算计地 |  In a way that is characterized by cunning, deceit, and a lack of moral principles in order to achieve political power;  in a manner suggestive of Niccolò Machiavelli's political philosophy. | 例句：He manipulated the situation machiavellianly to gain an advantage. 他狡猾地操纵局势以获得优势。  The CEO machiavellianly used the merger to eliminate his rivals.  首席执行官精于算计地利用并购来消除竞争对手。| 近义词：cunningly, craftily, deviously, shrewdly, artfully | 反义词：honestly, ingenuously, frankly, openly
