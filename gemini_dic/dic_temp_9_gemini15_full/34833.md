clingingness: /ˈklɪŋɪŋnəs/ | n. |依恋；粘人；依赖性 | The quality or state of being clinging; an excessive dependence on or attachment to someone or something.  It often implies a neediness and reluctance to be independent. |例句：Her clingingness made it difficult for him to maintain a healthy relationship.  她的依恋让他难以维持健康的关系。

例句：The child's clingingness to its mother was understandable after the accident.  孩子在事故后对母亲的依恋是可以理解的。


近义词：dependence, dependency, attachment, possessiveness, neediness


反义词：independence, self-reliance, autonomy
