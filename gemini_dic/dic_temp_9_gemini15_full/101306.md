inward:/ˈɪnwərd/ | adj., adv. | 向内的；内心的；内向的 | Directed or moving toward the inside; relating to or affecting a person's inner self or thoughts. | 例句：The door swung inward. 门向内打开。  She felt an inward sense of peace. 她感到内心的平静。| 近义词：inner, internal, interior | 反义词：outward, external


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The arrow points inward. = 箭头指向内部。
He always looks inward for solutions. = 他总是向内寻求解决办法。

一般过去时:
The pressure pushed the air inward. = 压力将空气推向内部。
She turned inward, lost in thought. = 她转向内心，陷入沉思。

现在完成时:
The company has turned inward, focusing on cost-cutting. = 公司已经转向内部，专注于削减成本。
My focus has shifted inward, prioritizing personal growth. = 我的注意力已经转向内心，优先考虑个人成长。


过去完成时:
The wound had already started to heal inward. = 伤口已经开始向内愈合。
She had long since turned inward, avoiding social interactions. = 她很久以前就变得内向，避免社交互动。


将来时:
The economy will likely turn inward in the coming years. = 未来几年经济可能会转向内向。
He will likely turn inward to find strength. = 他可能会转向内心寻找力量。


虚拟语气:
If the door opened inward, it would be easier to enter. = 如果门向内开，进入会更容易。
If she had turned inward sooner, she might have avoided the conflict. = 如果她早点转向内心，她可能会避免冲突。


被动语态:
The wound was treated to promote inward healing. = 伤口经过处理以促进向内愈合。
His thoughts were turned inward by the quiet atmosphere. = 安静的气氛使他的思绪转向内心。


疑问句:
Does the door open inward or outward? = 门是向内开还是向外开？
Is she naturally inward or extroverted? = 她天生内向还是外向？


否定句:
The pressure did not push the air inward. = 压力并没有将空气推向内部。
He did not turn inward to confront his problems. = 他没有转向内心去面对他的问题。
