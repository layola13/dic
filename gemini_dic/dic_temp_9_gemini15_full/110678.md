The surname itself originates from the place name, Lindesay, in Scotland.  The name's meaning is uncertain, but some scholars suggest it could be derived from elements meaning "lime tree" and "island" or "meadow".|例句：My friend's name is Lindsey. 我的朋友名叫林赛。
