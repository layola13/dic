self-pitifully:/ˈsɛlf ˈpɪtɪfli/| adj. |自怜地，可怜兮兮地 |Expressing or characterized by self-pity.  It describes an action or behavior that shows excessive self-pity.|She spoke self-pitifully about her misfortunes. 她可怜兮兮地谈论着她的不幸。 He looked self-pitifully at the spilled milk. 他可怜巴巴地看着洒了的牛奶。|近义词：pathetically, miserably, dolefully |反义词：cheerfully, bravely, stoically
