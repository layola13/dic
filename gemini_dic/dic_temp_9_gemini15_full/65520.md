eurypteroid:/ˌjʊərɪpˈtɛrɔɪd/| n. |广翅鲎类，板足鲎类|A eurypterid is an extinct aquatic arthropod belonging to the order Eurypterida.  They were among the largest arthropods ever to have lived, with some species reaching lengths of over 2 meters.  They are often referred to as "sea scorpions," although they are not true scorpions.|Example: The fossil record shows a diverse range of eurypteroid species inhabiting various ancient aquatic environments.  广翅鲎化石记录显示，各种各样的广翅鲎物种栖息在各种古代水生环境中。|近义词:sea scorpion (informal)|反义词:None
