septets:/ˈsɛptɛts/ | n. |七重奏；七行诗节 | A septet is a group of seven things, especially a musical composition for seven instruments or voices, or a stanza of seven lines in a poem.  |Example: The composer wrote a beautiful septet for clarinet, oboe, bassoon, horn, violin, viola, and cello.  作曲家为单簧管、双簧管、巴松管、圆号、小提琴、中提琴和大提琴创作了一首美丽的七重奏。  |近义词:heptad, septuplet |反义词:None
