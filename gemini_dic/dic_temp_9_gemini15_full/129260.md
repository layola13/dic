nodder:/ˈnɒdə(r)/|/ˈnɑːdər/| n. |点头的人；频频点头的人|A person who nods frequently, often implying agreement or understanding, or someone who falls asleep frequently.  It can also refer to something that nods or sways. |例句：He was a constant nodder during the meeting, seemingly agreeing with everything. 他在会议上不停地点头，似乎同意一切。

The old wooden sign was a nodder in the wind.  旧木头招牌在风中摇晃。|近义词：nodder, assenter, acquiescer |反义词：obstinate, resistant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nods his head in agreement. = 他点头表示同意。
She nods frequently during the lecture. = 她在讲座期间频频点头。


一般过去时:
He nodded his head in agreement yesterday. = 他昨天点头表示同意。
She nodded off during the boring movie. = 她在那部无聊的电影中睡着了。


现在完成时:
He has nodded off several times today. = 他今天已经好几次打瞌睡了。
She has nodded in agreement to all my proposals. = 她已经同意了我所有的提议。


过去完成时:
He had nodded off before the speaker finished his presentation. = 演讲者完成演讲之前他就睡着了。
She had nodded her head in understanding before he explained further. = 他还没进一步解释，她就点头表示理解了。


将来时:
He will nod off if the meeting goes on much longer. = 如果会议再继续下去，他会睡着的。
She will nod her head in approval once she sees the results. = 一旦她看到结果，她就会点头表示赞同。


虚拟语气:
If he were more interested, he would nod less frequently. = 如果他对这件事更感兴趣，他点头就不会那么频繁了。
If she had nodded her head, I wouldn't have been so worried. = 如果她当时点头了，我就不会那么担心了。


被动语态:
The proposal was nodded through by the committee. = 该提案已获得委员会批准。(点头通过)
His words were nodded at but not really understood. = 他的话虽然得到了点头回应，但并没有真正理解。


疑问句:
Did he nod in agreement? = 他点头表示同意了吗？
Does she often nod off during lectures? = 她经常在课堂上打瞌睡吗？


否定句:
He didn't nod his head during the entire presentation. = 在整个演示过程中，他都没有点头。
She didn't nod off, she was fully alert. = 她没有打瞌睡，她很清醒。


PHRASE->
frequent nodder = 频频点头的人
constant nodder = 不停点头的人
sleepy nodder = 打瞌睡的人
a nodder of assent = 表示同意的点头者
to nod one's head = 点头
