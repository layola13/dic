redonned:/ˌriːˈdɒnd/| v.|重新穿上，再次穿上|To put on again; to don again.|He redonned his coat before going out. 他出门前又穿上了外套。She redonned her uniform after the ceremony. 典礼结束后她又穿上了制服。|近义词：redress, re-clothe|反义词：doff, remove

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He redons his coat every morning. 他每天早上都穿上外套。
She redons her uniform for special occasions. 她在特殊场合会穿上制服。


一般过去时:
He redonned his jacket after the rain stopped. 雨停后他又穿上了夹克。
She redonned her shoes after cleaning them. 她擦干净鞋子后又穿上了。

He has redonned his winter coat as the weather turned cold. 天气变冷后，他重新穿上了冬衣。
