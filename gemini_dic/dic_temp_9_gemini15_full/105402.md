kiddyish:/ˈkɪdiʃ/| adj.|幼稚的，孩子气的|Characterized by or suggestive of a child; childish.  It often implies something is excessively or inappropriately childish, lacking maturity or sophistication.|例句：The movie's plot was rather kiddyish and predictable.  这部电影的情节相当幼稚且可预测。

例句：Her kiddyish behavior was embarrassing at the formal dinner.  她在正式晚宴上的孩子气行为令人尴尬。


近义词：childish, immature, infantile, puerile


反义词：mature, adult, sophisticated, grown-up
