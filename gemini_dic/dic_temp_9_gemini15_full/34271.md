They are often found in theaters and other performance venues. | Example: The opera singer hired claqueurs to ensure a positive reception for her performance.  这位歌剧演员雇佣了鼓掌者来确保她的演出获得好评。|近义词：applauder, flatterer, yes-man | 反义词：critic, detractor
