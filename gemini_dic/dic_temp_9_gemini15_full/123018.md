monasticism:/məˈnæstɪsɪzəm/| n. |隐修主义；隐居生活；僧侣制度 |The practice of living the life of a monk or nun, typically characterized by seclusion from the world, devotion to prayer and religious observance, and often poverty and chastity.  |例句：Many people find solace and spiritual fulfillment in monasticism. 许多人从隐修主义中找到了慰藉和精神上的满足。|例句：The decline of monasticism in Europe was a gradual process.欧洲隐修主义的衰落是一个循序渐进的过程。|近义词：asceticism, seclusion, religious retirement |反义词：worldly life, secularism
