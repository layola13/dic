huddlingly: /ˈhʌdliŋli/ | adv. | 瑟缩地；挤作一团地 | In a huddled manner; close together and in a slightly disorderly way.  | 例句：The sheep huddled together huddlingly against the biting wind.  羊群瑟缩着挤作一团，抵御刺骨的寒风。

The children huddlingly gathered around the fire, seeking warmth and comfort. 孩子们挤作一团围坐在火堆旁，寻求温暖和安慰。


近义词：crowdedly, closely, compactly

反义词：sprawlingly, scatteredly, loosely


词性：副词
