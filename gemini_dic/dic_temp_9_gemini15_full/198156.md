shops:/ʃɒps/ /ʃɑːps/| n.|商店；店铺；车间|Places where goods are sold to the public.  It can also refer to workshops or factories.|例句：There are many shops in the city center. 市中心有很多商店。

例句：The shoe shop is closed today. 鞋店今天关门了。


近义词：stores, boutiques, outlets|反义词：None specifically, but "factory" could be considered an antonym in the context of production.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shops at the mall every Saturday. 他每个星期六都在商场购物。
They shop online for the best deals. 他们为了寻找最优惠的价格在网上购物。


一般过去时:
She shopped for groceries yesterday. 她昨天买了杂货。
We shopped until we dropped! 我们逛街逛到精疲力尽！


现在完成时:
I have shopped at this store many times. 我在这家店买过很多次东西了。
They have shopped around for the best price. 他们为了找到最好的价格四处比较过价格。


过去完成时:
She had shopped for hours before she found what she wanted. 她找了几个小时才找到她想要的东西。
They had shopped at the market before the storm hit. 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经在市场上买过东西了。


将来时:
I will shop for a new dress tomorrow. 我明天要去买一件新衣服。
We will shop online to avoid the crowds. 我们将在网上购物以避开人群。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would shop at a more expensive store. 如果我有更多钱，我会去一家更贵的商店购物。
I wish I could shop for clothes all day. 我希望我能整天买衣服。


被动语态:
The goods are shopped for by the store manager. 商品由店长采购。
The items are shopped from various suppliers. 这些商品是从不同的供应商那里采购的。


疑问句:
Where do you usually shop? 你通常在哪里购物？
What kind of shops do you like? 你喜欢什么样的商店？


否定句:
He doesn't shop often. 他不经常购物。
She didn't shop at all last week. 她上周根本没购物。
