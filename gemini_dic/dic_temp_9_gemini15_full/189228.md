词源:

From Middle English *sacristie, from Old French sacristie, from Late Latin sacristia, from Latin sacer "sacred" + -ista "place".  The word ultimately derives from the Latin word "sacer," meaning "sacred," indicating the sacred nature of the objects housed within.
