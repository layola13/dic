phantasied:/ˈfæntəsaɪzd/| v.|幻想，梦想，凭空想象|To have imagined or pictured something unreal or improbable; to create something in the mind.  This often involves vivid and detailed imagery and may be influenced by emotions or desires. |例句：She phantasied about winning the lottery and buying a tropical island. 她幻想中彩票并买下一个热带岛屿。|例句：He phantasied a world where everyone lived in peace and harmony. 他幻想出一个世界，那里每个人都和平和谐地生活。|近义词：imagined, dreamed, fantasized, daydreamed |反义词：realized, experienced
