overtrusted: /ˌoʊvərˈtrʌstɪd/ | adj. | 过分信任的，被过度信任的 |  Describes a state where excessive trust has been placed in something or someone, often leading to negative consequences.  It implies a misplaced or naive level of trust. |  例句：He felt overtrusted and exploited by his former business partner. 他觉得被以前的生意伙伴过度信任并利用了。

The company's overtrusted security system was easily breached. 公司过度信任的安保系统很容易被攻破。


近义词：gullible, naive, credulous, unsuspecting | 反义词：suspicious, wary, distrustful, skeptical | 词性：形容词
