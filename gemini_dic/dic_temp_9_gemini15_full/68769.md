farro:/ˈfɑːroʊ/| n. |法罗小麦，远古小麦|An ancient grain, a type of wheat with a nutty flavor and chewy texture. It's a good source of protein and fiber.  |例句：I made a delicious salad with farro, roasted vegetables, and feta cheese. 我用法罗小麦、烤蔬菜和菲达奶酪做了一份美味的沙拉。|例句：Farro is a popular ingredient in many healthy recipes. 法罗小麦是许多健康食谱中的一种受欢迎的食材。|近义词：wheat, barley, spelt |反义词:  None specifically, but might be contrasted with refined grains. |名词
