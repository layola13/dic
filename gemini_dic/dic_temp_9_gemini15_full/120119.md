mimicry:/ˈmɪmɪkri/| n.|模仿；拟态；模仿行为|The act of imitating someone or something, especially in order to entertain or ridicule.  It can also refer to the close resemblance of one organism to another, usually for protection. |例句：Her mimicry of the teacher's voice was hilarious.  她的老师声音模仿得非常滑稽。

例句：The viceroy butterfly's mimicry of the monarch butterfly protects it from predators.  副王蝶模仿帝王蝶的外表来保护自己免受捕食者的攻击。

近义词：imitation, simulation, impersonation, copy

反义词：originality, innovation


USAGE->

She mimics famous actors. = 她模仿著名的演员。
