crazier:/ˈkreɪziər/| adj.|更疯狂的，更古怪的 | More insane; more eccentric.  It implies a higher degree of craziness than "crazy".|例句：Her ideas are crazier than ever before.她的想法比以往任何时候都更疯狂了。

例句：The crazier the better! 越疯狂越好！


近义词：more insane, more mad, more wild, more eccentric
反义词：saner, more rational, more sensible

词性:形容词

词源:源自古英语"crasian"，意思是"破碎的，不完整的"，后来演变成"疯狂的"。
