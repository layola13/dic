downswing:/ˈdaʊnswɪŋ/| n. |下降；衰退；低迷期 | A downswing is a period of decline or decrease, often in economic activity or a particular trend. It can refer to a downward movement in a cycle or a literal downward swing of something. |例句：The economy is experiencing a significant downswing.  经济正在经历严重的衰退。

The downswing of the golf club sent the ball soaring. 高尔夫球杆的下挥动作让球飞了起来。|近义词：decline, downturn, slump, recession |反义词：upswing, upturn, boom, recovery |名词
