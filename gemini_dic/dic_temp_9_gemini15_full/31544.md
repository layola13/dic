chartist:/ˈtʃɑːrtɪst/| n. |图表分析者；图表主义者；（19世纪英国的）宪章运动支持者 |A chartist is someone who analyzes charts to predict price movements in financial markets, or historically, a supporter of the Chartist movement.  |Example:  He's a skilled chartist, often correctly predicting market trends. 他是一位技艺高超的图表分析者，经常能正确预测市场趋势。 |近义词：technical analyst, market analyst |反义词：fundamentalist (in finance)
