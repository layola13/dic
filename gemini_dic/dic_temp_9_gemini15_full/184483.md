restamp:/riːˈstæmp/| vt.|重新盖章；重新贴邮票|To stamp again; to affix a stamp again.|例句：The post office will restamp your letter if the postage is insufficient. 邮局会帮你重新盖章，如果邮资不足的话。|例句：I need to restamp this package before I send it. 我需要重新贴邮票在这个包裹上，在我寄出之前。|近义词：re-stamp, re-mark|反义词：remove stamp


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He restamps envelopes every day. = 他每天都重新盖章信封。
They restamp the packages before shipping. = 他们在发货前重新盖章包裹。

一般过去时:
She restamped the letter and sent it again. = 她重新盖了信的邮票并再次寄出。
The clerk restamped the documents. = 职员重新盖章了文件。

现在完成时:
He has restamped all the documents. = 他已经重新盖章了所有文件。
They have restamped the parcels for reshipment. = 他们已经为重新装运而重新盖章包裹。

过去完成时:
She had restamped the package before she realized it was damaged. = 她在她意识到包裹损坏之前已经重新盖章了它。
They had restamped the letters before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经重新盖章了信件。

将来时:

They will restamp the returned goods. = 他们将重新盖章退回的货物。

虚拟语气:

I wish I had restamped the package before sending it. = 我希望我在寄出包裹之前已经重新盖了邮票。
