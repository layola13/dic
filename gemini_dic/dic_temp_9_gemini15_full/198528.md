shrood:/ʃruːd/| n.|裹尸布；遮盖物；掩盖|A shroud is a piece of cloth used to wrap a dead body before burial.  It can also refer to something that covers or obscures something else. |The body was laid out, shrouded in a simple white cloth.  尸体被摆放好，裹着一块简单的白布。|The fog shrouded the valley in mist. 雾笼罩着山谷。|veil, covering, pall|open, uncovered|名词


USAGE->

The shroud covers the body. = 裹尸布盖住了尸体。
A thick fog shrouds the mountain. = 厚厚的雾遮盖着山峰。
