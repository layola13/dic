labeling:/ˈleɪbəlɪŋ/ | n. |标签，标记，贴标签 |The act of attaching labels to something; the process of assigning labels to data or items.  It can also refer to the labels themselves. |例句：The labeling of the products is crucial for consumers to understand their contents. 产品的标签对于消费者理解其内容至关重要。

例句：Careful labeling of chemicals is essential for safety.  仔细标记化学品对安全至关重要。


近义词：tagging, marking, identification, classification |反义词：unlabeling, unmarked


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are labeling the boxes. = 他们正在贴箱子标签。
She labels all her belongings. = 她给她的所有物品贴标签。

一般过去时:
He labeled the jars yesterday. = 他昨天贴了罐子的标签。

过去完成时:

将来时:
