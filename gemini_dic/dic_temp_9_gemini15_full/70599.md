film-going: /ˈfɪlmˌɡoʊɪŋ/ | n. | 电影观看；去看电影的习惯 | The act of going to see films, often considered as a regular activity or habit.  It can also refer to the overall experience of attending a movie theater. | 例句：His film-going habits are quite eclectic.  他的电影观看习惯相当多样化。


例句：The decline in film-going is a concern for the industry. 电影观看人数的下降令业界担忧。


近义词：moviegoing, cinema-going


反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑staying home (待在家)


词性:名词
