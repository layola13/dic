hauberk:/ˈhɔːbɜːrk/ | n. |锁子甲 |A hauberk is a type of medieval chainmail shirt, typically worn as body armor. It was often made of interconnected metal rings and provided protection against slashing and piercing weapons.  |Example: The knight donned his hauberk before charging into battle.  骑士穿上锁子甲后冲入战场。|近义词：chainmail, mail shirt |反义词：None (antonyms don't readily apply to a piece of armor)
