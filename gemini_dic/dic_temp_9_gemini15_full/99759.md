/ˌɪntərˈmiːdieɪt/ | adj. | 中间的；中级的；中等的 | Intermediate means situated or occurring in the middle of a series or process, or having a level of difficulty between beginner and advanced.  It can refer to a position, level, or stage that is neither the highest nor the lowest. |  This is an intermediate level course. 这是一门中级课程。 She achieved an intermediate result. 她取得了中等的结果。|近义词：medium, middle, average | 反义词：beginner, advanced, extreme
