repudiatory: /rɪˈpjuːdieɪtəri/ | adj. | 否定的；拒绝的；否认的 | Relating to or characterized by repudiation; denying the validity or authenticity of something.  | The repudiatory clause in the contract allowed either party to withdraw. 合同中的否认条款允许任何一方撤回。  The court found the repudiatory conduct of the defendant to be unacceptable. 法院认定被告的否认行为不可接受。|  repudiative, disavowing | accepting, affirming | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company repudiates its responsibility. = 公司否认其责任。
He repudiates the allegations against him. = 他否认针对他的指控。

一般过去时:
She repudiated the offer. = 她拒绝了提议。
The government repudiated the treaty. = 政府否定了条约。

现在完成时:
They have repudiated their earlier claims. = 他们已经否认了他们之前的声明。
He has repudiated all responsibility for the accident. = 他已经否认对事故的所有责任。

过去完成时:
She had repudiated the agreement before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，她已经否定了协议。
The company had repudiated its debt obligations. = 公司此前已经否认其债务义务。


将来时:
They will repudiate the contract if the conditions are not met. = 如果不满足条件，他们将否认合同。
He will repudiate the accusations made against him. = 他将否认对他提出的指控。

虚拟语气:
If he repudiated the contract, he would face legal consequences. = 如果他否认合同，他将面临法律后果。
I wish he hadn't repudiated our offer. = 我真希望他不要拒绝我们的提议。

被动语态:
The contract was repudiated by the buyer. = 合同被买方否认了。
The agreement was repudiated by both parties. = 该协议被双方否认。

疑问句:
Did he repudiate the agreement? = 他是否否认了协议？
Will the company repudiate its debt? = 公司是否会否认其债务？

否定句:
He did not repudiate his promise. = 他没有否认他的承诺。
The company did not repudiate the contract. = 公司没有否认合同。
