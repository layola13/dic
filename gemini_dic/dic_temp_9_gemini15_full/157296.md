pintle:/ˈpɪntəl/| n.|销轴；梢轴；枢轴；阀杆|A pintle is a short, strong pin or rod that forms a pivot or axis, often used in a hinge or similar mechanism.  It can also refer to the vertical pin in a rudder or the stem of a valve.|例句：The door swung easily on its pintle. 门在其销轴上轻松地旋转。

The rudder's pintle was badly corroded.  舵的梢轴严重腐蚀了。|近义词：pin, bolt, pivot, rod|反义词：N/A
