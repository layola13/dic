nonabsolutistic: /ˌnɒnˌæbsəˈluːtɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非绝对主义的 | Not based on absolute principles; relativistic.  It suggests a perspective that acknowledges multiple viewpoints and avoids asserting universal truths.  |


例句：

His nonabsolutistic approach to morality allowed him to understand diverse cultural practices.  他的非绝对主义道德观使他能够理解不同的文化习俗。


The artist's nonabsolutistic style embraced ambiguity and open interpretation.  这位艺术家的非绝对主义风格包含了模糊性和开放式的解读。


近义词：relativistic, contextual, pluralistic, flexible

反义词：absolutist, dogmatic, rigid, inflexible

词性:形容词
