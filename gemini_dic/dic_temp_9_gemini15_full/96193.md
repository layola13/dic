indeterminates:/ˌɪndɪˈtɜːrmɪnəts/| n.|不确定因素，未定量|Things that are not yet known or decided; variables whose values are unknown or unspecified.  They are often used in mathematics and statistics to represent unknown quantities.|例句：The model includes several indeterminates representing the effects of external factors.  这个模型包含几个不确定因素，代表外部因素的影响。  The presence of indeterminates makes it difficult to predict the outcome. 不确定因素的存在使得预测结果变得困难。|近义词：unknowns, variables, uncertainties|反义词：knowns, constants, certainties|名词
