feathered:/ˈfɛðərd/ | adj. | 长羽毛的；有羽毛的 | Covered with feathers.  |例句：The feathered bird sang a beautiful song.  长着羽毛的鸟儿唱着动听的歌。|例句：A feathered arrow flew through the air.  一支带羽毛的箭飞过空中。|近义词：plumed, winged |反义词：featherless, bare
