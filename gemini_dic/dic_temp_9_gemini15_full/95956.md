inculcations:/ˌɪnkʌlˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |灌输；谆谆教诲；反复强调 |The repeated inculcations of moral principles shaped his character.  反复强调道德原则塑造了他的性格。|近义词：indoctrination, instruction, teaching |反义词：neglect, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher inculcates good manners in her students. = 老师向她的学生灌输良好的礼仪。
His parents inculcate the importance of hard work. = 他的父母反复强调努力工作的重要性。


一般过去时:
The school inculcated discipline in its students. = 学校向学生灌输了纪律。
They inculcated a love of learning in their children. = 他们从小就培养孩子们热爱学习。


现在完成时:
They have inculcated a strong sense of responsibility in their children. = 他们已经培养了孩子们强烈的责任感。
The government has inculcated a spirit of patriotism. = 政府已经灌输了爱国主义精神。


过去完成时:
She had inculcated in him a deep respect for his elders. = 她从小就让他对长辈怀有深深的敬意。
The church had inculcated a strong belief in God. = 教堂已经灌输了对上帝的坚定信仰。


将来时:
The program will inculcate new values in the community. = 这个项目将向社区灌输新的价值观。
They will inculcate a sense of self-reliance in their students. = 他们将培养学生的自立精神。


虚拟语气:
If they inculcated better values, society would be improved. = 如果他们灌输更好的价值观，社会就会得到改善。
I wish they had inculcated more discipline in their children. = 我希望他们从小就对孩子进行更多的纪律教育。


被动语态:
Good manners were inculcated in the children from a young age. = 孩子们从小就被灌输良好的礼仪。
Patriotism is inculcated through education. = 爱国主义是通过教育灌输的。


疑问句:
What values are being inculcated in today's youth? = 当今的年轻人被灌输了什么价值观？
What methods are used to inculcate discipline? = 使用什么方法来灌输纪律？


否定句:
The school did not inculcate critical thinking skills. = 学校没有灌输批判性思维能力。
They failed to inculcate a sense of responsibility. = 他们未能培养责任感。


PHRASE->
inculcate values = 灌输价值观
inculcate beliefs = 灌输信仰
inculcate principles = 灌输原则
inculcate habits = 灌输习惯
inculcate knowledge = 灌输知识

词源:
源自拉丁语 *inculcare，意为“踩踏，压实”。引申为反复强调，最终灌输某种思想或观念。
