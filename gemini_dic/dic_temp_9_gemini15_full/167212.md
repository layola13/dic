pretenseful: /prɪˈtɛnsfəl/ | adj. | 假装的；虚伪的；做作的 | Characterized by pretense or affectation; insincere.  It suggests an excessive or ostentatious display of pretense. |例句：Her pretenseful concern for the poor was easily seen through.  她对穷人的虚情假意很容易被看穿。

例句：His pretenseful humility hid a deep-seated ambition. 他虚伪的谦逊掩盖了他根深蒂固的野心。


近义词：affected, artificial, insincere, hypocritical, ostentatious

反义词：genuine, sincere, authentic, natural, unpretentious


词源:  Derived from "pretense" + "-ful" (suffix indicating full of or characterized by). "Pretense" itself comes from the Old French "pretense," meaning "intention, purpose," ultimately from the Latin "praetendere," meaning "to stretch forth, allege, pretend."
