hijack:/ˈhaɪdʒæk/| vt. n.|劫持；劫机；篡夺|To seize control of (a vehicle, especially an aircraft) by force.|The terrorists hijacked the plane and demanded a ransom. 恐怖分子劫持了飞机并索要赎金。|近义词：seize, commandeer, pirate|反义词：release, surrender


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hijack the conversation constantly. = 他们不断地劫持谈话。
He hijacks every meeting with his own agenda. = 他总是带着自己的议程来劫持每一次会议。


一般过去时:
The pirates hijacked the ship. = 海盗劫持了那艘船。
She hijacked the project and took all the credit. = 她篡夺了项目并窃取了所有的功劳。


现在完成时:
They have hijacked several vehicles this year. = 今年他们已经劫持了几辆车。
Hackers have hijacked our website. = 黑客已经劫持了我们的网站。


过去完成时:
The plane had been hijacked before the authorities were alerted. = 在当局接到警报之前，飞机已经被劫持了。
The rebels had hijacked the radio station to broadcast their demands. = 叛乱分子已经劫持了广播电台来广播他们的要求。


将来时:
Terrorists will likely hijack more planes in the future. = 恐怖分子将来可能会劫持更多飞机。
They will hijack the discussion and prevent us from reaching a decision. = 他们会劫持讨论，阻止我们达成决定。


虚拟语气:
If they hijacked the train, what would happen? = 如果他们劫持了火车，会发生什么？
I wish they hadn't hijacked my idea. = 我真希望他们没有剽窃我的主意。


被动语态:
The plane was hijacked by terrorists. = 这架飞机被恐怖分子劫持了。
The meeting was hijacked by a disruptive participant. = 会议被一个捣乱的参与者打断了。


疑问句:
Was the car hijacked? = 车子是被劫持的吗？
Has the server been hijacked? = 服务器被劫持了吗？


否定句:
The police did not allow the hijackers to escape. = 警方不允许劫机者逃跑。
He did not hijack the election; he won fairly. = 他没有操纵选举；他是公平获胜的。
