disturbing: /dɪˈstɜːrbɪŋ/ | adj. |令人不安的，烦扰的 | causing anxiety, worry, or unease.  |The disturbing news left her speechless.  令人不安的消息让她说不出话来。  The film contained disturbing scenes of violence. 这部电影包含令人不安的暴力场景。| unsettling, worrying, upsetting | calming, reassuring, comforting | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The news is disturbing. = 这个消息令人不安。
That noise is disturbing me. = 那个噪音扰乱了我。

一般过去时:
The movie was disturbing. = 那部电影令人不安。
The news report was quite disturbing. = 新闻报道相当令人不安。

现在完成时:
The events have been disturbing. = 这些事件一直令人不安。
This situation has been disturbing me for weeks. =  这种情况已经困扰我几周了。

过去完成时:
I had been disturbed by the strange noises. = 我一直被奇怪的噪音打扰。
They had been disturbed by the news. = 他们一直被这个消息困扰着。

将来时:
The results will be disturbing. = 结果将令人不安。
I think the announcement will be disturbing to many people. = 我认为这个公告会让很多人不安。

虚拟语气:
It would be disturbing if that were true. = 如果那是真的，那将是令人不安的。
I wish that news wasn't so disturbing. = 我希望那个消息不要那么令人不安。

被动语态:
She was disturbed by the phone call. = 她被电话吵到了。
He was disturbed by the loud music. = 他被吵闹的音乐打扰了。

疑问句:
Is this news disturbing to you? = 这个消息让你不安吗？
Is the thought of that disturbing to you? = 想到那件事让你不安吗？

否定句:
The situation wasn't disturbing at all. = 情况一点也不令人不安。
The film wasn't disturbing, it was quite funny. = 这部电影并不令人不安，它相当有趣。
