encumberment: /ɪnˈkʌmbərmənt/ | n. | 妨碍；阻碍；累赘 | The encumberment of unnecessary regulations hindered the project's progress.  不必要的规章制度的阻碍妨碍了项目的进展。|近义词：burden, hindrance, impediment, obstacle |反义词：assistance, help, facilitation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The excessive paperwork encumbers the workflow. = 过多的文书工作妨碍了工作流程。
This heavy backpack encumbers my movement. = 这个沉重的背包妨碍了我的行动。


一般过去时:
The old laws encumbered the development of the economy. = 旧的法律阻碍了经济的发展。
His numerous commitments encumbered his ability to focus. = 他众多的承诺妨碍了他集中精力的能力。


现在完成时:
Regulations have encumbered the growth of small businesses. = 法规已经阻碍了小型企业的发展。
The debt has encumbered the family's financial situation. = 债务已经拖累了家庭的财务状况。


过去完成时:
The outdated technology had encumbered the company's efficiency. = 过时的技术已经影响了公司的效率。
Previous mistakes had encumbered their ability to succeed. = 之前的错误已经妨碍了他们的成功。


将来时:
New taxes will encumber the already struggling industry. = 新税收将进一步拖累已经举步维艰的行业。
Increased regulations will encumber the development of renewable energy. = 越来越多的监管将会阻碍可再生能源的发展。


虚拟语气:
If the project weren't encumbered by bureaucracy, it would be completed sooner. = 如果这个项目没有官僚主义的阻碍，它会更快完成。
I wish my life weren't encumbered by so much debt. = 我希望我的生活没有这么多债务的拖累。


被动语态:
The project was encumbered by delays. = 该项目因延误而受阻。
Their progress was encumbered by unforeseen circumstances. = 他们的进展受到了不可预见情况的阻碍。


疑问句:
Is the business encumbered by excessive debt? = 这家企业是否背负了过多的债务？
Are our efforts encumbered by a lack of resources? = 我们的努力是否因为缺乏资源而受阻？


否定句:
The company isn't encumbered by any significant financial problems. = 这家公司没有面临任何重大的财务问题。
The team wasn't encumbered by the technical difficulties. = 团队没有被技术难题所阻碍。


PHRASE->
encumberment of rights = 权利的限制
financial encumberment = 财务负担
encumberment on property = 房产上的负担
legal encumberment = 法律上的障碍
encumberment of land = 土地上的负担


词源:
From Old French *encombrement, from encombrer "to encumber," from *in- + combrier "to heap," related to combre "heap."  Ultimately from a Germanic root.
