goods:/ɡʊdz/| n.|货物；商品；财产|Goods are items that are bought and sold.  Goods can refer to merchandise, commodities, or possessions.|例句：The store sells a wide variety of goods. 这家商店出售各种各样的商品。|例句：He lost all his goods in the fire. 他在火灾中失去了所有财产。|近义词：merchandise, wares, commodities, possessions|反义词：services, intangibles


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The goods are delivered today. = 货物今天送达。
These goods are expensive. = 这些商品很贵。

一般过去时:
The goods were shipped last week. = 货物上周发货了。
We bought many goods at the market. = 我们在市场上买了许多商品。

现在完成时:
The goods have been sold out. = 货物已售罄。
We have received the goods. = 我们已经收到了货物。

过去完成时:
The goods had already been dispatched before we called. = 我们打电话之前，货物已经发出去了。
They had not received the goods by the deadline. = 截止日期之前他们还没有收到货物。


将来时:
The goods will arrive tomorrow. = 货物明天到达。
We will order more goods next week. = 我们下周将订购更多商品。

虚拟语气:
If the goods were cheaper, I would buy more. = 如果商品更便宜，我会买更多。
I wish the goods were of better quality. = 我希望商品质量更好。

被动语态:
The goods are being inspected. = 货物正在被检查。
The goods were damaged during transportation. = 货物在运输途中受损。

疑问句:
What goods are you selling? = 你卖什么商品？
Where are the goods located? = 货物在哪里？

否定句:
The goods are not ready yet. = 货物还没准备好。
We did not receive the goods on time. = 我们没有按时收到货物。
