USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is kind-hearted. = 他心地善良。
She is warm-hearted towards children. = 她对孩子们很热情。


一般过去时:
He was kind-hearted in his youth. = 他年轻时心地善良。
They were warm-hearted and welcoming. = 他们热情好客。


现在完成时:
She has always been kind-hearted. = 她一直心地善良。
He has shown himself to be warm-hearted and supportive. = 他表现出热情和支持。


过去完成时:
I had never known him to be so kind-hearted before. = 我以前从未知道他如此心地善良。
They had always been warm-hearted to their neighbors. = 他们一直对邻居很热情。


将来时:

疑问句:
