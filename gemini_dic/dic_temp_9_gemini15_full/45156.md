cudgels:/ˈkʌdʒəlz/| n.|棍棒；警棍|A cudgel is a short, thick stick, often used as a weapon.  It can also refer to a club or staff.|例句：He threatened them with a cudgel. 他用棍棒威胁他们。


例句：The angry mob wielded cudgels against the soldiers. 愤怒的暴民挥舞着棍棒攻击士兵。


近义词：club, stick, staff, bludgeon|反义词：None specifically, but implies the opposite of a refined or delicate instrument.


词源：源自古法语 *coutel,  最终源自拉丁语 *cultellus（小刀）。尽管词义演变，但都体现了短而粗的工具的含义。
