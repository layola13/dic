insp:/ɪnsp/| v.|激励，鼓舞|to encourage or motivate someone to do something|The coach inspired his team to victory.  教练激励他的团队取得胜利。|motivate, encourage, stimulate|discourage, depress|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He inspires his students every day. 他每天都激励他的学生。
The painting inspires awe and wonder. 这幅画激发了敬畏和惊奇。


一般过去时:
Her speech inspired me to pursue my dreams. 她的演讲激励我去追求我的梦想。
The music inspired a sense of peace. 音乐激发了一种平静感。


现在完成时:
His words have inspired countless people. 他的话激励了无数的人。
This book has inspired me to write my own novel. 这本书激励我写我自己的小说。


过去完成时:
She had been inspired by her grandmother's life story. 她一直受到她祖母的人生故事的启发。
They had already been inspired to take action. 他们已经被激励采取行动了。


将来时:
This new technology will inspire a new wave of innovation. 这项新技术将激发新一轮的创新。
The upcoming exhibition will inspire many artists.  即将到来的展览将激励许多艺术家。


虚拟语气:
If I had been inspired, I would have finished the project earlier. 如果我受到激励，我会更早完成这个项目。
I wish I had been inspired to try harder. 我希望我曾经有动力更加努力。


被动语态:
The team was inspired by the coach's pep talk. 球队受到了教练的鼓舞性讲话的激励。
Many artists have been inspired by nature. 许多艺术家都受到大自然的启发。


疑问句:
What inspires you to work so hard? 什么激励你如此努力工作？
What inspired this amazing painting?  是什么激发了这幅令人惊叹的画作？


否定句:
Nothing inspired him to continue.  没有什么激励他继续下去。
The film didn't inspire me at all. 这部电影一点也没有激励我。
