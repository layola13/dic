face up to: /feɪs ʌp tuː/ | phrasal verb |勇敢面对；承担责任；承认| To confront or accept (a difficult or unpleasant situation or task). It implies acknowledging a problem and dealing with it directly, rather than avoiding it. |例句：She finally faced up to her responsibilities. 她最终承担起了自己的责任。| He needs to face up to the fact that he made a mistake. 他需要承认自己犯了错误。|近义词：confront, acknowledge, accept, deal with |反义词：avoid, ignore, deny, shirk
