for fun:/fɔːr fʌn/| adj. phr. |为了娱乐;为了消遣|  Used to describe something done for enjoyment or amusement, not for any serious purpose.  It indicates an activity is undertaken purely for pleasure.|例句：I painted this picture just for fun. 我只是为了消遣才画这幅画。|例句：We went to the beach for fun. 我们去海滩玩耍。|近义词：for amusement, for entertainment, as a hobby|反义词：seriously, professionally


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We play games for fun every weekend. = 我们每个周末都玩游戏消遣。
They sing karaoke for fun after work. = 他们下班后唱卡拉OK娱乐。


一般过去时:
She baked a cake for fun yesterday. = 她昨天为了好玩烤了一个蛋糕。
He went fishing for fun last Sunday. = 他上周日去钓鱼玩乐。


现在完成时:
I have painted several pictures for fun this year. = 今年我已经画了几幅画来消遣。
They have gone hiking for fun many times. = 他们已经多次去远足消遣了。


过去完成时:
She had already finished her work, so she decided to read for fun. = 她已经完成了工作，所以她决定读些东西来消遣。
They had played video games for fun before going to the party. = 他们去参加派对之前玩过电子游戏来消遣。


将来时:
We will go to the cinema for fun next week. = 我们下周将去看电影娱乐。
They will try rock climbing for fun this summer. = 他们今年夏天将尝试攀岩来消遣。


虚拟语气:
If I had more free time, I would play the guitar for fun. = 如果我有更多空闲时间，我会弹吉他来消遣。
I wish I could travel the world for fun. = 我希望我能周游世界来消遣。


被动语态:
The game was played for fun, not for competition. = 这场比赛是为了娱乐而不是为了竞争而进行的。
The movie was made for fun, not to make a profit. = 这部电影是为了娱乐而制作的，而不是为了赚钱。


疑问句:
Do you do anything for fun in your free time? = 你在空闲时间做些什么消遣？
What do you usually do for fun on weekends? = 你周末通常做什么消遣？


否定句:
He didn't go to the concert for fun; he went to support his friend. = 他去参加音乐会不是为了消遣；他是去支持他的朋友。
She didn't study hard for fun; she studied to pass her exams. = 她学习努力不是为了消遣；是为了通过考试。
