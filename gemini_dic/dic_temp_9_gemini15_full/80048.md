goaf:/ɡoʊf/| n. |采空区，矿井采空区 |A goaf is a void or cavity left in a mine after the extraction of ore or coal.  It's essentially the space left behind after mining operations have removed the valuable material.  Goafs can pose significant safety hazards due to their instability and potential for collapse. |The abandoned goaf was slowly filling with water.  采空区正在慢慢地被水灌满。| cavity, void,  excavation | solid ground |
