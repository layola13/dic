fraternizations:/ˌfrætərnaɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n.|兄弟情谊；友谊；（尤指士兵与敌人的）亲密来往|The act of fraternizing, especially between soldiers of opposing armies or between officers and enlisted men.  It often implies inappropriate familiarity or intimacy, potentially violating regulations or code of conduct.|例句：The fraternizations between the soldiers and the local villagers caused concern among the officers. 士兵与当地村民的亲密来往引起了军官们的担忧。

例句：The increased fraternizations between the two companies led to improved collaboration. 两家公司之间日益密切的交往促进了合作。


近义词：camaraderie, fellowship, association, bonding|反义词：estrangement, alienation, antagonism, hostility
