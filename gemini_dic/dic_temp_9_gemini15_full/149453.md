paddle:/ˈpædəl/| n. v.|桨；短桨；划桨；拍打|A paddle is a short oar used to propel a small boat.  It can also refer to the act of propelling a boat with a paddle or to a flat, broad instrument used for stirring, mixing, or hitting something.|例句：He used a paddle to steer the canoe through the rapids. 他用桨在激流中操控独木舟。

She paddled the kayak across the lake. 她划着皮划艇穿过湖面。|近义词：oar, bat, spatula|反义词:sail(in the context of boat propulsion)|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I paddle my kayak every weekend. = 我每个周末都划皮划艇。
They paddle the boat across the river. = 他们划船过河。

一般过去时:
We paddled the canoe down the stream. = 我们划着独木舟顺流而下。
He paddled hard to reach the shore. = 他奋力划桨到达岸边。


现在完成时:
She has paddled across the English Channel. = 她已经划过英吉利海峡。
They have paddled many miles today. = 今天他们已经划了许多英里。


过去完成时:
We had paddled for hours before we reached the island. = 我们划了几个小时才到达岛屿。
They had paddled against the current all morning. = 他们整个上午都在逆流而上划桨。


将来时:
I will paddle the boat to the island tomorrow. = 我明天将划船去岛上。
We will paddle down the river next weekend. = 我们下周末将顺流而下划桨。


虚拟语气:
If I had a paddle, I would go kayaking. = 如果我有一支桨，我就会去划皮划艇。
I wish I could paddle a dragon boat. = 我真希望我能划龙舟。


被动语态:
The canoe was paddled expertly across the lake. = 那艘独木舟熟练地划过了湖面。
The kayak is being paddled by a young girl. = 皮划艇正由一个小女孩划着。


疑问句:
Do you know how to paddle a surfboard? = 你知道怎么划冲浪板吗？
Can you paddle the canoe alone? = 你能独自划独木舟吗？


否定句:
I cannot paddle the boat in this strong wind. = 我在这种强风中无法划船。
She didn't paddle very far. = 她没有划得很远。
