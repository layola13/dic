pouffe:/puːf/| n. |矮胖的软垫坐具|A pouffe is a soft, upholstered seat without arms or back, often round or square.  It is typically smaller and lower to the ground than a chair or sofa and serves as extra seating or as a decorative element in a room. |The living room is furnished with a comfortable sofa, two armchairs, and a colorful pouffe.|近义词：ottoman, footstool|反义词：无
