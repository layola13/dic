phytophagous:/ˈfaɪtəʊfæɡəs/| adj.|植食性的；食草的|Feeding on plants; herbivorous.  Phytophagous insects are a major component of many ecosystems.|例句：Many insects are phytophagous, feeding on leaves, stems, or other plant parts. 许多昆虫是植食性的，以叶子、茎或其他植物为食。

例句：The phytophagous larvae of the butterfly caused significant damage to the farmer's crops. 蝴蝶的植食性幼虫给农民的庄稼造成了严重的损失。


近义词：herbivorous, plant-eating

反义词：carnivorous, zoophagous


词源:

* **phyto-**: from Greek *phutón* "plant"
* **-phagous**: from Greek *phagein* "to eat"
