reconvince:/ˌriːkənˈvɪns/| vt.| 再说服；重新使信服 | To persuade someone again or to change their mind again after they had already been persuaded.  |例句：I had to reconvince him that the plan was safe. 我不得不再次让他相信这个计划是安全的。
例句：After the initial rejection, the sales team had to reconvince the client. 在最初被拒绝后，销售团队不得不再次说服客户。|近义词：reassure, persuade, convince |反义词：dissuade, discourage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reconvinces his boss every week. 他每周都重新说服他的老板。
They reconvince the customer with new data. 他们用新的数据重新说服客户。

一般过去时:
She reconvinced her parents to let her go to the party. 她再次说服了父母让她去参加派对。
The lawyer reconvinced the jury of his client's innocence. 律师再次让陪审团相信他的当事人是无罪的。

现在完成时:
He has reconvinced me that this is the right decision. 他已经再次让我相信这是正确的决定。
They have reconvinced themselves of the project's feasibility. 他们已经再次相信了这个项目的可行性。

过去完成时:
She had reconvinced herself that she could succeed. 她已经再次相信自己能够成功。
The team had reconvinced the investors after presenting new findings. 在展示新的发现后，团队已经再次说服了投资者。

将来时:
I will reconvince him once I have all the evidence. 一旦我有所有证据，我就会再次说服他。
They will reconvince the public with a new marketing campaign. 他们将通过新的营销活动再次说服公众。

虚拟语气:
If I could reconvince him, I would. 如果我能再次说服他，我会的。
If only I could reconvince her to stay. 但愿我能再次说服她留下。

被动语态:
He was reconvinced by the new evidence. 他被新的证据再次说服了。
The plan was reconvinced to the board members. 该计划再次被说服了董事会成员。

疑问句:
Can you reconvince him to change his mind? 你能再次说服他改变主意吗？
Will they be reconvinced by the new proposal? 他们会被新的提案再次说服吗？

否定句:
I could not reconvince her of my innocence. 我无法再次让她相信我的清白。
They failed to reconvince the shareholders. 他们未能再次说服股东。
