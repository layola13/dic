ebonist:/ˈɛbənɪst/| n. |乌木工匠，制乌木器物者 |A person who works with ebony wood, often creating furniture or other decorative objects.  Ebonists are skilled craftspeople who specialize in the intricate work required to shape and finish ebony, a very hard and dense wood. |The ebonist meticulously carved the intricate details of the chess pieces.  乌木工匠一丝不苟地雕刻着棋子上的复杂细节。|craftsman, artisan |(近义词缺乏精确的反义词，可考虑与从事其他木材工作的工匠对比)|名词
