clink:/klɪŋk/| v. n.|叮当声，碰响|The sound of two hard objects hitting each other; to make this sound.|例句：The glasses clinked as we toasted. 当我们举杯祝酒时，酒杯发出清脆的碰撞声。The coins clinked in his pocket. 硬币在他口袋里叮当作响。|近义词：chink, tinkle, jingle|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The glasses clink. = 杯子发出叮当声。
The coins clink in his pocket. = 硬币在他口袋里叮当作响。

The bells clinked merrily. = 铃铛愉快地叮当作响。
