nonet:/ˈnɒnɛt/| n. |九重奏；九人一组|A nonet is a musical composition written for nine instruments or voices.  It can also refer to a group of nine people.|Example: The composer wrote a beautiful nonet for strings and woodwinds.  这位作曲家为弦乐器和木管乐器创作了一首优美的九重奏。|近义词：ensemble (if referring to a group), octet (for eight), dectet (for ten)|反义词：solo, duet, trio (referring to smaller ensembles)
