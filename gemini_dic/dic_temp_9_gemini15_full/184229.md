resolutest:/ˈrɛzəluːtɪst/| adj.| 最坚决的；最果断的 | Most resolute; firmest in purpose.  This word is the superlative form of "resolute," meaning showing firm determination. It emphasizes the highest degree of determination and firmness of purpose. |例句：The general's resolutest decision saved the army from utter defeat.  将军最坚决的决定使军队免于彻底失败。 |近义词：most determined, most unwavering, most steadfast |反义词：most irresolute, most wavering, most vacillating
