hopper:/ˈhɒpər/| n. |漏斗；料斗；跳跃者|A hopper is a container with a sloping bottom and an opening at the bottom, used for feeding materials into a machine or process.  It can also refer to a person or animal that hops. |例句：The grain hopper was nearly full. 谷仓的料斗几乎满了。

The grasshopper is a type of hopper. 蚱蜢是一种跳跃者。|近义词：funnel, chute, feeder |反义词：N/A
