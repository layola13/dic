e-reader:/ˈiːriːdər/| n. |电子阅读器|An electronic device used for reading e-books and other digital publications.  It typically has a screen that resembles paper and is designed for comfortable reading.| I bought a new e-reader so I can download and read books on the go. 我买了一个新的电子阅读器，这样我就可以随时下载和阅读书籍了。|digital reader, ebook reader|physical book, paperback|名词
