sigil:/ˈsɪdʒəl/| n. |符咒；印记；象征|A sigil is a symbol used in magic or ritual to represent a specific intention or entity.  It's often created through a process of reducing words or phrases to their essential symbolic form.  Sigils can be used for various purposes, from protection to manifestation.|Example: The magician inscribed a powerful sigil on the amulet for protection.  符咒师在护身符上刻上了一个强大的符咒以求保护。|近义词：symbol, emblem, talisman, charm|反义词：None specifically, but its *effect* might be countered by a contrasting sigil or ritual.
