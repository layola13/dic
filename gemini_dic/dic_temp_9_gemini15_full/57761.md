dreamlessly: /ˈdriːmləslɪ/ | adj. | 无梦地；沉睡地；无意识地 | Without dreams; in a state of deep, unconscious sleep.  The adjective describes a state of sleep devoid of dreams.  It can also imply a lack of imagination or aspiration. |

例句：

He slept dreamlessly all night.  他整夜睡得很沉，没有做梦。

She lived a dreamlessly existence, devoid of ambition. 她过着毫无梦想的生活，缺乏雄心壮志。


近义词：dreamless, unthinkingly, unconsciously

反义词：dreamfully, vividly (referring to dreaming)


词性：形容词
