fabber:/ˈfæbər/| n.|吹牛者，骗子|A fabber is someone who habitually exaggerates or tells lies, often to impress others.  They fabricate stories or achievements.|例句：He's a notorious fabber; don't believe a word he says. 他是个臭名昭著的吹牛者；别相信他说的任何话。|近义词：liar, prevaricator, fibber, exaggerator|反义词：truth-teller, honest person|名词
