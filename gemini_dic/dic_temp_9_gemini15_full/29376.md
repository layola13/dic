casually:/ˈkæʒuəli/ | adv. | 随意地，漫不经心地 | In a relaxed and informal way, without ceremony or formality.  |例句：He casually mentioned that he'd won the lottery. 他漫不经心地提到他中了彩票。
例句：She casually tossed her keys onto the table. 她随意地把钥匙扔在桌子上。|近义词：informally, nonchalantly, carelessly |反义词：formally, ceremoniously, deliberately
