multidisciplines: /ˌmʌltiˈdɪsəplɪnz/ | n. | 多学科 |  The study or application of knowledge from multiple disciplines.  It involves integrating expertise and approaches from different fields to address complex problems.


例句：

The project requires a multidisciplines approach, bringing together engineers, designers, and social scientists.  这个项目需要多学科的方法，汇集工程师、设计师和社会科学家。

This research initiative fosters collaboration across multidisciplines to tackle climate change.  这项研究倡议促进跨学科合作以应对气候变化。


近义词：interdisciplinary, cross-disciplinary

反义词：单学科 (No direct single-word antonym, but "single-discipline" or "unidisciplinary" can be used to contrast)


词性：名词
