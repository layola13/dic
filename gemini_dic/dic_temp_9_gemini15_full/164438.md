preenjoyment:/ˌpriːɪnˈdʒɔɪmənt/| n. | 预先享乐；提前享受；先期享受 | The anticipation and excitement felt before an enjoyable experience.  It refers to the pleasure derived from looking forward to something pleasurable.  |例句：The preenjoyment of the upcoming vacation was almost as good as the vacation itself.  即将到来的假期的预先享受几乎和假期本身一样美好。

The preenjoyment of a delicious meal heightened the experience.  美味佳肴的预先享受增强了用餐体验。

|近义词：anticipation, expectation, foretaste |反义词：disappointment, disillusionment |名词
