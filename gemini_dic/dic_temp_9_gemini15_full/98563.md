institute:/ˈɪnstɪˌtjuːt/ | n., vt. |协会；学院；制度；建立；制定 |

英文解析:As a noun, an institute is an organization founded for a specific purpose, often academic or professional.  As a verb, it means to establish or create something, such as a system or policy.

例句:
The research institute published groundbreaking findings on climate change. = 这个研究所发表了关于气候变化的突破性研究成果。

The government instituted a new policy to address unemployment. = 政府实施了一项新的政策来解决失业问题。


近义词: (noun) organization, academy, foundation, society; (verb) establish, found, implement, enact

反义词: (verb) abolish, dismantle, repeal, terminate


词性: noun, verb
