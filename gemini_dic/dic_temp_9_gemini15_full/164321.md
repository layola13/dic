preeffectively:/priːɪˈfektɪvli/| adv. |预先有效地；提前生效地 |  Describes something that is effective or produces results before the expected or intended time. It implies a proactive approach leading to early success or impact. |The new marketing campaign preeffectively boosted sales before the official launch.|近义词:proactively, preemptively, beforehand |反义词:ineffectively, belatedly, after the fact
