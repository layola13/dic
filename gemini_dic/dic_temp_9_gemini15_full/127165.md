naturalises: /ˈnætʃrəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使归化；使入籍；使自然化 | To grant someone citizenship in a country; to make something natural or normal. |

例句：

He naturalised in Canada after living there for five years. 他在加拿大住了五年后入籍加拿大。

The government is working to naturalise the process of obtaining a visa. 政府正在努力使获得签证的过程变得更自然化。


近义词：acclimatize, assimilate, integrate

反义词：denaturalize, expatriate


词性：动词
