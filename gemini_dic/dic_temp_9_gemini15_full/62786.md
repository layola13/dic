engrieve:/ɪnˈɡriːv/| vt.|铭刻，深刻地铭记|to impress deeply (a feeling, etc.) on the mind or memory; to carve or engrave|The experience was deeply engrained in my memory. 那次经历深深地铭刻在我的记忆中。 He engraved his name on the ring. 他在戒指上刻上了他的名字。|近义词：inscribe, etch, imprint|反义词：erase, forget


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He engraves beautiful designs on wood. 他在木头上雕刻美丽的图案。
She engraves her feelings in her diary. 她把她的感情铭刻在日记里。
