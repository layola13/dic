nonrepellency:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈpɛlənsi/ | n. |非排斥性，不相斥性 |The property of not repelling or causing aversion.  It often refers to the lack of mutual repulsion between particles or entities,  allowing for interaction or coexistence.  |例句：The nonrepellency of the magnetic poles allowed them to attract. 磁极的非排斥性使它们能够相互吸引。


The study demonstrated the nonrepellency of the two chemical compounds, leading to their successful combination. 这项研究证明了这两种化合物的非排斥性，从而导致了它们的成功结合。|近义词：compatibility, attraction |反义词：repulsion, aversion
