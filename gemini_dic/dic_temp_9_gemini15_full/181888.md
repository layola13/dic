remade:/ˌriːˈmeɪd/| v. |重拍；重制；重新制作|To make something again, often with improvements or changes.  It implies a previous version existed.|例句：The movie was remade with a bigger budget and a new cast. 这部电影用更大的预算和新的演员阵容重新制作了。

例句：They remade the old kitchen, installing new cabinets and appliances.他们重新装修了旧厨房，安装了新的橱柜和电器。


近义词：recreate, redo, revise, refashion |反义词：destroy, demolish
