ciliform:/ˈsɪlɪfɔːrm/| adj.|纤毛状的，毛状的|Resembling or shaped like cilia (hair-like projections).  The term is often used to describe structures that are covered in fine, hair-like processes.|例句：The surface of the leaf had a ciliform texture. 叶子的表面具有纤毛状的纹理。The ciliform appendages on the insect's body aided in its movement.  昆虫身体上的纤毛状附属物有助于它的移动。|近义词：ciliated, hair-like, fimbriated|反义词：smooth, glabrous
