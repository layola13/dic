confectioner:/kənˈfɛkʃənər/| n. |糖果师；糕点师 |A confectioner is a person who makes or sells confectionery, such as candy, chocolates, and cakes.  They are skilled in the art of sugar work and often create elaborate and decorative sweets. |The confectioner created a stunning wedding cake.  这位糕点师制作了一个令人惊艳的婚礼蛋糕。| baker, pastry chef | butcher, grocer |
