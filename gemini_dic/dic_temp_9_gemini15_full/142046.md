organophosphate:/ˌɔːrɡænəˈfɒsfeɪt/ | n. |有机磷酸酯|Organophosphates are a class of chemical compounds containing phosphorus bonded to oxygen and at least one alkyl or aryl group. They are used in a wide range of applications, including insecticides, herbicides, and nerve agents.  Their toxicity arises from their ability to inhibit acetylcholinesterase, a vital enzyme in the nervous system.|例句：Many organophosphates are highly toxic to insects and other invertebrates.  许多有机磷酸酯对昆虫和其他无脊椎动物具有剧毒。|例句：The use of organophosphates as pesticides has been controversial due to their potential harm to human health and the environment.  由于其对人类健康和环境的潜在危害，有机磷酸酯作为杀虫剂的使用一直备受争议。|近义词：phosphorus ester|反义词：无
