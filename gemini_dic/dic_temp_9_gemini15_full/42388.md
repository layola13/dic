counterbond: /ˈkaʊntərbɒnd/ | n. | 反担保；对保；反证券 | A counterbond is a bond issued to guarantee the performance of another bond or obligation.  It acts as a secondary guarantee, providing additional security to the investor in case the primary bond defaults.  It's essentially a form of insurance for the original bond. | Example: The company issued a counterbond to secure the loan.  这家公司发行了一份反担保债券来担保贷款。  | 近义词：guarantee, surety, security | 反义词：risk, liability |  名词
