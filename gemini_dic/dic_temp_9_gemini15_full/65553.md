euthanizing:/ˈjuːθənˌaɪzɪŋ/| v.|安乐死；结束…的生命 |The act of killing an animal or person painlessly, especially to relieve suffering.  |例句：The vet had to euthanize the dog because of its incurable illness.  兽医不得不对这条狗实施安乐死，因为它患有无法治愈的疾病。
例句：The family decided to euthanize their beloved pet after a long battle with cancer.  在与癌症长期斗争后，这个家庭决定对他们心爱的宠物实施安乐死。|近义词：putting down, mercy killing|反义词：saving, healing|动词
