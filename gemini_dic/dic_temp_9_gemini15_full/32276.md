chidingly:/ˈtʃaɪdɪŋli/ | adv. | 责备地；斥责地；训斥地 | In a way that expresses disapproval or scolding.  It implies a gentle but firm rebuke, often with a hint of disappointment. |  She looked at him chidingly. 她责备地看了他一眼。 He spoke chidingly about her laziness. 他责备她懒惰。 | reproachfully, reprovingly, admonishingly | approvingly, commendably | 副词
