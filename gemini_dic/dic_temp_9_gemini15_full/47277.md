de-cultural:/ˌdiːˈkʌltʃərəl/ | adj. | 脱文化的；去文化化的；非文化的 |  Depriving something of its cultural aspects or characteristics; removing cultural influences.  | Example: The de-culturalization of indigenous populations through forced assimilation is a historical tragedy.  脱文化：通过强制同化剥夺土著居民文化特征的历史悲剧。 | 近义词：deculturated, uncultured,  | 反义词：cultural, cultured
