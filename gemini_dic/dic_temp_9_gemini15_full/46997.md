dashboards:/ˈdæʃˌbɔːrdz/ | n. | 仪表盘；控制面板 | A dashboard is a visual display of the most important information related to a specific process or system.  It provides a quick overview of key performance indicators (KPIs) and other relevant data, allowing users to monitor performance, identify trends, and make informed decisions. | Example: The car's dashboard showed the speed, fuel level, and engine temperature.  汽车仪表盘显示速度、油量和发动机温度。 The marketing dashboard displays website traffic, conversion rates, and sales figures. 市场营销控制面板显示网站流量、转化率和销售数据。 | 近义词: control panel, instrument panel, overview | 反义词:  detailed report (反义词并非完全对立，而是指呈现信息的方式不同) | 名词
