The government's repacifying efforts in the region, following the recent uprising, were met with mixed success.  = 政府在最近起义后对该地区的再次安抚努力取得了喜忧参半的成功。

After the initial apology, further repacifying gestures were needed to mend the damaged relationship. = 在最初的道歉之后，还需要进一步的安抚姿态来修复受损的关系。


近义词：reassuring, calming, soothing, appeasing, reconciling

反义词：aggravating, inflaming, provoking, inciting


词源:

re- (prefix indicating repetition or again) + pacify (verb meaning to bring peace or quiet to; to quell)
