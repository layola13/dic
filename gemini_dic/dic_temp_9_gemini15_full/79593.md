gloria:/ˈɡlɔːriə/| n. |光荣；荣耀；赞美；盛名 |Glory refers to great praise and honor received for achievement or distinction. It can also denote a state of great happiness or joy.  |例句：He achieved glory on the battlefield. 他在战场上获得了荣誉。

例句：The athlete basked in the glory of her victory.  这位运动员沉浸在胜利的喜悦中。

近义词：honor, fame, renown, praise

反义词：shame, disgrace, dishonor, infamy

词性：名词
