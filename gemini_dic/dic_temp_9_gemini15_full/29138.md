casein:/ˈkeɪsiːn/| n. |酪蛋白|A complex mixture of phosphoproteins (αs1, αs2, β, κ) found in the milk of mammals. It is the main protein in milk and is used in many foods and industrial products.  |Example:  Casein is a major component of cheese. 酪蛋白是奶酪的主要成分之一。

Casein is used in adhesives. 酪蛋白被用作粘合剂。|近义词：milk protein, phosphoprotein |反义词：无
