prochurch:/ˈproʊˌtʃɜːrtʃ/| adj.| 亲教会的；支持教会的；拥护教会的 |  Supporting or favoring the church or religious institutions.  The term often implies a strong, sometimes overly enthusiastic, support for established religious organizations and their doctrines.  It can carry a negative connotation, suggesting a lack of critical thinking or an overly rigid adherence to traditional beliefs. | 例句：His prochurch views led him to actively campaign against same-sex marriage. 他的亲教会观点使他积极参与反对同性婚姻的运动。

His prochurch stance was evident in his generous donations to the cathedral. 他对大教堂的慷慨捐款表明了他拥护教会的立场。


近义词：pro-religious, church-supporting, devout, pious | 反义词：anti-church, secular, irreligious | 词性：形容词
