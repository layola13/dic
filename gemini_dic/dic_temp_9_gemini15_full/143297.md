outdressing:/ˌaʊtˈdrɛsɪŋ/| v. |穿着比…更讲究；打扮得比…更出色|To surpass in the elegance or quality of one's dress.  It implies exceeding someone else in the stylishness and impressiveness of one's attire.|She completely outdressed everyone at the gala.|近义词：outdo, surpass, eclipse|反义词：underdress
