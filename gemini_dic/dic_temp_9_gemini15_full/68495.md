famoused:/ˈfeɪməst/| adj.|著名的，出名的|The word "famoused" is not a standard English word. It is an incorrect form often used informally.  The correct word is "famous".|例句：He is a famous actor. 他是一位著名的演员。|近义词：renowned, celebrated, well-known, illustrious|反义词：obscure, unknown, anonymous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is famous for his paintings. = 他以他的绘画而闻名。
She is famous worldwide. = 她世界闻名。


一般过去时:
He was famous in his time. = 他在他那个时代很有名。
The band was famously known for their unique sound. = 这个乐队以其独特的音色而闻名。


现在完成时:
She has become famous overnight. = 她一夜成名。
He has remained famous for decades. = 他几十年来一直很有名。


过去完成时:
The author had already become famous before the publication of his first novel. = 在他的第一部小说出版之前，这位作家就已经成名了。
The painting had been famous for centuries. = 这幅画已经闻名数百年了。


将来时:
He will be famous one day. = 他总有一天会出名。
This new technology will become famous in the near future. = 这项新技术将在不久的将来闻名。


虚拟语气:
If he were famous, he would have a lot of fans. = 如果他出名的话，他会有很多粉丝。
I wish I were famous. = 我希望我能出名。


被动语态:
He was famously known as a great scientist. = 他作为一位伟大的科学家而闻名。
The city is known for its beautiful architecture. = 这个城市以其美丽的建筑而闻名。


疑问句:
Is he famous? = 他有名吗？
Are you famous? = 你有名吗？


否定句:
He is not famous. = 他不出名。
She wasn't famous then. = 她那时不出名。
