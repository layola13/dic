interannually: /ˌɪntərˈænjuəli/ | adv. |年度间的，隔年地| Occurring or calculated once a year, but not necessarily at the same time each year.  It emphasizes the interval between years rather than the specific time within each year. |例句：The company reports its interannually profits.该公司每年报告其利润。(注意，此处利润报告并非固定在每年的同一时间)|近义词：annually, yearly |反义词：daily, monthly, weekly
