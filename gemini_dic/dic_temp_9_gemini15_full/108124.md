latin-american: /ˌlætn əˈmɛrɪkən/ | adj. n. | 拉丁美洲的；拉丁美洲人 | Relating to Latin America, its people, or its culture.  The term encompasses a diverse range of countries and cultures in the Americas where Spanish, Portuguese, or French are the dominant languages. |  例句：He's studying Latin-American history. 他正在学习拉丁美洲历史。

例句：Many Latin-American countries are experiencing rapid economic growth. 许多拉丁美洲国家正在经历快速的经济增长。


近义词：Hispanic (often used interchangeably but not always accurate), Ibero-American


反义词：None directly opposite, but terms referring to other regions could be considered contrasting (e.g., North American, European).


词性：形容词，名词
