reddish:/ˈrɛdɪʃ/| adj.|微红的，略带红色的|Slightly red; having a reddish color.  The word implies a less intense shade of red than "red" itself.|例句：The sunset painted the sky in reddish hues.  日落给天空涂上了微红的色彩。

例句：His cheeks were reddish from the cold.  他的脸颊冻得微红。


近义词：ruddy, pinkish, rose-colored|反义词：pale, colorless


词源:  Derived from the Old English word "rēad" meaning "red," plus the suffix "-ish," which indicates a slight or somewhat degree of the quality described by the base word.  This suffix is common in English to form adjectives indicating an approximation or resemblance to a particular quality.
