saturators:/ˈsætʃəreɪtərz/| n.|饱和器；使饱和者|A saturator is a device or substance that saturates something, typically a gas or liquid, with another substance.  It's often used in industrial processes to ensure a uniform mixture or concentration.|例句：The air saturator in the paint booth ensures even distribution of paint particles.  油漆喷漆室中的空气饱和器确保油漆颗粒均匀分布。

The chemical saturator added fragrance to the air. 化学饱和器向空气中添加了香味。|近义词：impregnator, infuser|反义词：depletor, reducer|名词
