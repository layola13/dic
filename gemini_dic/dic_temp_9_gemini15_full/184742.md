resulting: /rɪˈzʌltɪŋ/ | adj. | 导致的；结果的；由…引起的 | Arising as a consequence or effect; caused by something.  |例句：The resulting damage was extensive.  = 造成的损害是巨大的。

例句：The resulting changes to the landscape were dramatic. = 对景观的相应改变是巨大的。


近义词：consequent, resultant, ensuing, subsequent | 反义词：preceding, prior, antecedent | 词性:形容词


USAGE->

The resulting solution is clear and colorless. = 得到的溶液是清澈无色的。
