photographometer:/ˌfoʊtoʊˈɡræfəˌmiːtər/| n. |摄影测距仪|A photographometer is an instrument used in surveying and mapping to measure distances and angles by taking photographs from known locations. It combines photographic techniques with geometrical principles to determine the three-dimensional coordinates of points in the landscape.|Example: The surveyor used a photographometer to create a detailed map of the area.  摄影测量员用摄影测距仪绘制了该区域的详细地图。|近义词:photogrammetric camera, stereophotogrammeter|反义词:None
