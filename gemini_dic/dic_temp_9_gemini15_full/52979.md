dining-room:/ˈdaɪnɪŋ ruːm/| n. |餐厅，饭厅|A room in a house or apartment where meals are eaten.|例句：We had a lovely dinner in the dining-room. 我们在餐厅吃了一顿美味的晚餐。|近义词：eating room, dining hall|反义词:kitchen
