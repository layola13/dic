half-decades:/ˈhæf ˈdɛkeɪdz/| n. |五年；半个十年|A half-decade is a period of five years. It's a less formal way of saying "five years".|The project spanned two half-decades.|近义词：five years, quinquennium|反义词：decade


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A half-decade represents five years. = 半个十年代表五年。
Two half-decades make a decade. = 两个五年构成一个十年。

一般过去时:
The project lasted two half-decades. = 该项目持续了两个五年。
We worked on that project for a half-decade. = 我们在这个项目上工作了五年。

现在完成时:
We have completed the project after a half-decade of hard work. = 在经过五年的努力之后，我们完成了这个项目。
It has been a half-decade since the last major update. = 自上次重大更新以来已经五年了。

过去完成时:
By the end of the half-decade, they had achieved a lot. = 在五年结束时，他们已经取得了很多成就。
They had worked for almost a half-decade before they saw any results. = 在看到任何结果之前，他们已经工作了将近五年。


将来时:
In a half-decade, we will see significant progress. = 五年后，我们将看到显著的进步。
The company plans to launch the new product in a half-decade. = 公司计划在五年后推出新产品。

虚拟语气:
If we had another half-decade, we could achieve more. = 如果我们再有五年时间，我们可以取得更多成就。
I wish we had started the project a half-decade earlier. = 我希望我们早五年开始这个项目。

被动语态:
The task was completed within a half-decade. = 任务在五年内完成。
Significant changes were observed after a half-decade. = 五年后观察到重大变化。

疑问句:
How many half-decades are there in a century? = 一个世纪有多少个五年？
Did the project take more than a half-decade? = 这个项目是否花了五年以上的时间？

否定句:
The project did not take a full decade, only a half-decade. = 该项目没有花十年，只花了五年。
