self-terminative:/ˌsɛlfˈtɜːrmɪnətɪv/| adj.|自终止的；自我结束的|Automatically ending or ceasing;  inherently limited in duration or extent.  Something that is self-terminative will end by itself without external intervention. |例句：The program is self-terminative; it will shut down automatically after completion.  该程序是自终止的；完成后它会自动关闭。

The self-terminative nature of the reaction prevented a catastrophic explosion.  反应的自终止特性防止了灾难性爆炸。


近义词：self-limiting, automatically ending, inherently limited, self-contained


反义词：continuous, ongoing, perpetual, unending


词性：形容词
