nonbelief:/ˈnɒn.bɪˈliːf/| n. |不信，怀疑；无神论|The absence or denial of belief; disbelief.  It often refers to a lack of belief in a particular religion or supernatural phenomena, but can also encompass broader skepticism towards established ideas or claims.  |例句：His nonbelief in God stemmed from a childhood experience.  他的不信神源于童年的一次经历。

例句：The article explores the rise of nonbelief in modern society. 这篇文章探讨了现代社会中不信现象的兴起。


近义词：disbelief, skepticism, incredulity, atheism (in the context of religious belief)

反义词：belief, faith, credence


词性:名词
