repartotion:/ˌriːpɑːrˈtɪʃən/| n. |重新分配，再分配 |The act of dividing or distributing something again, especially after it has been previously divided or distributed.  It often implies a more equitable or fair redistribution than the initial distribution.  |例句：The repartotion of land after the war led to significant social changes. 战后土地的重新分配导致了重大的社会变革。
例句：The repartotion of resources is crucial for ensuring fairness and equity. 资源的重新分配对于确保公平与公正至关重要。|近义词：redistribution, reallocation, reallotment |反义词：concentration, monopolization |名词
