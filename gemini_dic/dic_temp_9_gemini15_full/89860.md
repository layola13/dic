hornyhead:/ˈhɔːrnɪhed/| n.|角头鱼，河豚的一种|A hornyhead is a type of fish, specifically referring to several species within the genus _Sebastes_, often found in the waters of the Pacific Ocean.  They are characterized by their bony head and often spiny fins.|Example: The fisherman caught a large hornyhead chub during his trip to the coast.  渔夫在去海岸的旅行中钓到了一条大型的角头鱼.|近义词：rockfish (a broader term)|反义词：无
