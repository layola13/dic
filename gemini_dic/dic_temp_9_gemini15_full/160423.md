poppies:/ˈpɒpiz/ /ˈpɑːpiz/ | n. |罂粟花 | Poppies are flowering plants with typically bright-red petals.  They are known for their delicate beauty and are also the source of opium. |The field was ablaze with poppies.  那片田野里盛开着鲜红的罂粟花。|  Many poppies bloomed after the recent rain.  最近雨后，许多罂粟花都开了。|  flower, blossom |weed (in certain contexts) |
