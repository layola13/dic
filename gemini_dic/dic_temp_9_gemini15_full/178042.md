reclaiming:/rɪˈkleɪmɪŋ/ | v. | 索回；开垦；改造；再利用 |  The act of taking back something that was lost or taken away; to recover or regain possession of something. It can also refer to improving or developing land, or to reuse materials. |例句：

The government is reclaiming land from the sea to build a new airport. = 政府正在填海造地以建造一座新的机场。

She is reclaiming her life after a difficult divorce. = 离婚后她正在重新掌控自己的生活。


近义词：recover, regain, redeem, rehabilitate, recycle | 反义词：abandon, relinquish, forfeit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reclaiming their stolen property. = 他们正在追讨被盗的财产。
Farmers are reclaiming the marshland for agriculture. = 农民们正在开垦沼泽地用于农业。


一般过去时:
He reclaimed his lost wallet. = 他找回了丢失的钱包。
The company reclaimed its market share. = 公司收回了它的市场份额。


现在完成时:
She has reclaimed her independence. = 她已经重新获得了独立。
They have reclaimed much of the polluted land. = 他们已经修复了大部分被污染的土地。


过去完成时:
He had reclaimed his inheritance before his father's death. = 在他父亲去世之前，他已经取回了他的遗产。
The city had reclaimed the abandoned buildings. = 该城市已经修复了废弃的建筑物。


将来时:
We will reclaim the cost of the repairs. = 我们将收回维修费用。
The government will reclaim the land for public use. = 政府将收回这块土地用于公共用途。


虚拟语气:
If she reclaimed her confidence, she would be a better leader. = 如果她找回自信，她会成为一个更好的领导者。
I wish he had reclaimed his lost youth. = 我希望他能够找回失去的青春。


被动语态:
The land is being reclaimed for housing development. = 这块土地正在被开垦用于住宅开发。
The stolen goods were reclaimed by the police. = 被盗的货物被警方追回。


疑问句:
Can we reclaim the tax we overpaid? = 我们能追回多交的税款吗？
Will the environment be reclaimed after the disaster? = 灾难过后环境会得到恢复吗？


否定句:
He did not reclaim his luggage at the airport. = 他没有在机场取回他的行李。
They could not reclaim the lost data. = 他们无法找回丢失的数据。
