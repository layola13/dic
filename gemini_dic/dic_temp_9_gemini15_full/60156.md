effeminate:/ɪˈfɛmɪnət/ | adj. | 柔弱的；女人气的；娘娘腔的 | Characterized by excessive softness, delicacy, or weakness; unmanly.  It often implies a lack of traditionally masculine traits and can be used in a derogatory way.  | 例句：He was criticized for his effeminate mannerisms. 他因其女人气的举止而受到批评。

例句：His voice was strangely effeminate for a man of his size and build. 他的声音对于一个如此高大强壮的男人来说，奇怪地柔弱。


近义词：womanly, unmanly, girlish, delicate, weak | 反义词：masculine, manly, strong, robust, virile


词源:  From Middle French *effeminé, from Latin *effeminātus, past participle of effemināre "to make womanly, to weaken," from ex- "out, from" + femina "woman."
