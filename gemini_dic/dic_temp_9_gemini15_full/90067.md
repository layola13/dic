hostaging:/ˈhɑːstɪdʒɪŋ/| n.|挟持；扣押；为人质|The act of holding someone hostage.  It involves seizing and detaining a person against their will, typically to force someone to comply with a demand.|例句：The bank robbery ended with the hostaging of several customers.  银行抢劫以挟持几名顾客而告终。|例句：The terrorists' hostaging of the diplomats sparked international outrage. 恐怖分子挟持外交官的行为激起了国际公愤。|近义词：kidnapping, seizure, abduction|反义词：release, liberation|名词
