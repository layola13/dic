pragmatism: /ˈpræɡməˌtɪzəm/ | n. | 实用主义；实用性 |

英文解析:Pragmatism is a philosophical approach that emphasizes practical consequences and real-world applications over abstract theories or ideals.  It prioritizes what works in practice over purely theoretical considerations.  Pragmatists believe that truth and meaning are derived from their practical consequences and usefulness.

例句:

His approach to problem-solving is characterized by pragmatism; he focuses on finding solutions that work, regardless of theoretical elegance. = 他解决问题的方法以实用主义为特征；他专注于寻找有效的解决方案，而不考虑理论上的优雅性。

The company's pragmatism in its marketing strategy led to increased sales and a stronger market share. = 公司在其营销策略中的实用主义带来了销售额的增长和更强的市场份额。


近义词:practicality, utilitarianism, empiricism

反义词:idealism, dogmatism, purism


词性:noun
