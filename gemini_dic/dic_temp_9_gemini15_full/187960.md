rots:/rɒts/| v.|腐烂；堕落；衰败 |To decay or decompose, especially of organic matter.|例句：The fruit rots quickly in this heat.  这水果在这炎热的天气下很快腐烂。|例句：The empire rotted from within.  这个帝国从内部腐烂衰败。|近义词：decay, decompose, putrefy|反义词：flourish, thrive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The apple rots quickly in the sun. = 苹果在阳光下很快腐烂。
The system rots slowly but surely. = 这个系统缓慢但肯定地走向腐朽。

一般过去时:
The meat rotted in the fridge. = 肉在冰箱里腐烂了。
The building rotted over the years. = 这栋建筑物年久失修，腐朽了。

现在完成时:
The wood has rotted away. = 木头已经腐烂了。
The moral fabric of society has rotted. = 社会的道德结构已经腐败了。

过去完成时:
The fruit had rotted before we could eat it. = 水果在我们能吃之前就已经腐烂了。
The empire had rotted before the invasion. = 在入侵之前，帝国就已经腐朽了。

将来时:
The food will rot if you leave it out. = 如果你把食物放在外面，它会腐烂。
The system will rot without reform. = 没有改革，这个系统将会腐朽。

虚拟语气:
If the food hadn't been refrigerated, it would have rotted. = 如果食物没有冷藏，它就会腐烂。
If the government had acted sooner, the system might not have rotted so badly. = 如果政府早点采取行动，系统可能不会腐朽得这么厉害。

被动语态:
The vegetables were rotted by the frost. = 蔬菜被霜冻腐烂了。
The reputation of the company was rotted by scandal. = 该公司的声誉因丑闻而受损。

疑问句:
Is the wood rotting? = 木头在腐烂吗？
Has the system begun to rot? = 这个系统开始腐烂了吗？

否定句:
The fruit didn't rot because it was refrigerated. = 水果没有腐烂，因为它被冷藏了。
The building didn't rot because it was well-maintained. = 这栋建筑没有腐烂，因为它维护得很好。


PHRASE->
rot away = 腐烂殆尽
rot down = 腐烂
rot out = 腐烂掉
rotten to the core = 腐烂到底；彻底腐败
rot through = 从内部腐烂
