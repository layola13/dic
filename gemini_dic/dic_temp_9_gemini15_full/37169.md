come up with: /kʌm ˈʌp wɪð/ | phrasal verb | 想出；提出；提供 | To produce or suggest an idea or plan.  |例句：I need to come up with a solution to this problem. 我需要想出一个解决这个问题的办法。


例句：The team came up with a creative marketing campaign.  团队想出了一个有创意的营销方案。


近义词：devise, invent, create, formulate, suggest, propose


反义词：reject, abandon, discard


词性：phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They come up with new ideas every week. = 他们每周都会想出新的主意。
We come up with a plan for the future. = 我们想出了一个未来的计划。

一般过去时:
She came up with a brilliant solution. = 她想出了一个绝妙的办法。
He came up with an excuse for his lateness. = 他为他的迟到找了个借口。

现在完成时:
They have come up with three different proposals. = 他们已经提出了三个不同的方案。
We have come up with a solution to the problem. = 我们已经想出了解决这个问题的办法。

过去完成时:
By the deadline, they had come up with a working prototype. = 在截止日期之前，他们已经想出了一个可用的原型。
She had come up with a plan before the meeting. = 她在会议前就已经想好了一个计划。

将来时:
We will come up with a decision by the end of the week. = 我们将在本周末前做出决定。
They will come up with a response to the complaint soon. = 他们很快就会对投诉做出回应。

虚拟语气:
If only I could come up with a better way! = 但愿我能想出一个更好的办法！
I wish I had come up with that idea earlier. = 我真希望我早点想到那个主意。

被动语态:  (This phrasal verb is rarely used passively)  The solution was eventually come up with by the team. = 这个方案最终由团队想了出来。(不常用)
A new strategy needs to be come up with. = 需要想出一个新的策略。(不常用)


疑问句:
Can you come up with a better idea? = 你能想出一个更好的主意吗？
Have they come up with a solution yet? = 他们想出解决方案了吗？

否定句:
We couldn't come up with a suitable name. = 我们想不出一个合适的名称。
They didn't come up with anything useful. = 他们什么有用的东西都没想出来。


PHRASE->
come up with a plan = 制定计划
come up with an excuse = 找借口
come up with a solution = 想出解决方案
come up with an idea = 想出一个主意
come up with a suggestion = 提建议
