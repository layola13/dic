hardheaded:/ˌhɑːrdˈhedɪd/| adj.|固执的，顽固的；倔强的；头脑简单的 | Having or showing a stubborn and unyielding attitude;  unwilling to change one's opinion or course of action.  It can also imply a lack of intelligence or sensitivity. |例句：He's hardheaded and refuses to listen to reason. 他很固执，不肯听劝。

例句：Despite the evidence, she remained hardheaded in her belief.  尽管有证据，她仍然固执地相信自己的想法。


近义词：stubborn, obstinate, inflexible, pigheaded, bullheaded

反义词：yielding, flexible, compliant, amenable, open-minded

词性：形容词
