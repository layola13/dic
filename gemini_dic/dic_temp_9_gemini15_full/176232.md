re-caging:/ˌriːˈkeɪdʒɪŋ/| n.|重新关笼；再次囚禁|The act of putting something or someone back into a cage.  This can refer to animals, people, or even abstract concepts.  It implies a previous confinement and a return to that state.|例句：The escaped parrot required re-caging.  这只逃脱的鹦鹉需要重新关进笼子。

例句：The repeated re-caging of the lion caused it significant stress.  反复将狮子重新关进笼子给它带来了很大的压力。|近义词：re-confinement, re-imprisonment|反义词：release, liberation|词性：名词
