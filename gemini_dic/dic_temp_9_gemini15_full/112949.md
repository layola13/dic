lugubrious:/luːˈɡuːbriəs/| adj.|忧郁的；悲伤的；愁眉苦脸的|Characterized by or expressing sorrow or grief; mournful.|例句：He delivered a lugubrious account of his misfortunes. 他悲痛地讲述了他的不幸遭遇。|例句：The lugubrious music perfectly matched the somber mood of the film. 忧郁的音乐与电影阴沉的气氛完美地融合在一起。|近义词：mournful, melancholy, doleful, gloomy|反义词：cheerful, joyful, merry, upbeat
