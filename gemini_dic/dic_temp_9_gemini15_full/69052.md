faulcon:/ˈfɔːlkən/| n. |猎鹰|A falcon is a bird of prey of the family Falconidae, typically having long pointed wings and a short tail.|例句：The falcon soared high above the mountains.  猎鹰在群山之上翱翔。|近义词：hawk, kestrel|反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting birds of prey that are not falcons, like sparrow or pigeon, could be considered antonyms in a broad sense.|名词
