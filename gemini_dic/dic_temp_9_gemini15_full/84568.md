hammerman:/ˈhæmərˌmæn/| n. |锤击工，铁匠|A hammerman is a person who works with a hammer, typically a skilled craftsman such as a blacksmith or someone who uses a hammer in construction or other trades.  The term is less common now, often replaced by more specific job titles.|Example: The hammerman skillfully shaped the hot iron into a horseshoe.  锤击工熟练地将热铁锻造成马蹄铁。|近义词：blacksmith, metalworker,  craftsman|反义词：None specifically, but terms suggesting desk work or non-manual labor could be considered opposites.
