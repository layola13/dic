defero:/ˈdɛfəroʊ/| vt.| 顺从；服从；听从；提交；推迟 |To yield to; submit to; obey; to put off; postpone|例句：He deferred to his father's wishes. 他顺从了他父亲的意愿。|例句：The decision has been deferred until next week.  决定推迟到下周。|近义词：submit, comply, obey, postpone, adjourn |反义词：defy, disobey, resist, expedite


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He defers to his superior's judgment. = 他服从上司的判断。
They defer all difficult decisions to their manager. = 他们把所有困难的决定都推迟到他们的经理处理。


一般过去时:
She deferred to her husband's opinion on the matter. = 在这件事上，她听从了她丈夫的意见。
The meeting was deferred due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，会议被推迟了。


现在完成时:
He has deferred his trip several times. = 他已经几次推迟了他的旅行。
The committee has deferred a decision on the proposal. = 委员会已经推迟了对该提案的决定。


过去完成时:
They had deferred the project until funding was secured. = 他们已经推迟了该项目，直到资金到位。
She had deferred making a decision until she had more information. = 她一直推迟做决定，直到获得更多信息。


将来时:
We will defer the discussion until after the holidays. = 我们将把讨论推迟到假期之后。
The court will defer the sentencing until next month. = 法院将把判刑推迟到下个月。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would defer to his expertise. = 如果我是你，我会听从他的专业意见。
I wish I had deferred the purchase until the price dropped. = 我真希望我把购买推迟到价格下降的时候。


被动语态:
The decision was deferred until further notice. = 决定被推迟，直到另行通知。
The project has been deferred due to budget constraints. = 项目由于预算限制而被推迟。


疑问句:
Will the game be deferred because of the weather? = 比赛会因为天气而推迟吗？
Should we defer making a decision until we have more data? = 我们应该推迟做决定直到我们有更多数据吗？


否定句:
He did not defer to the pressure from his colleagues. = 他没有屈服于同事的压力。
The company will not defer the deadline. = 公司不会推迟截止日期。


PHRASE->
defer to someone's judgment = 听从某人的判断
defer payment = 推迟付款
defer consideration = 推迟考虑
defer an action = 推迟行动
defer the question = 推迟问题
