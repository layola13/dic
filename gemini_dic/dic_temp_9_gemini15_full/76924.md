gaoler:/ˈdʒeɪlə(r)/| n. |监狱长，看守|A gaoler is a person who is responsible for guarding prisoners in a jail.  This term is now largely archaic, with "jailer" being more common. |The gaoler threw the key into the cell.  监狱长把钥匙扔进了牢房。|jailer, keeper, warden|freeer, liberator

He checks on the prisoners regularly. = 他定期检查囚犯。
