nonreception:/ˌnɒnriˈsɛpʃən/| n.|无接收；未接收到|The lack of reception or the failure to receive something, such as a signal, message, or idea.  It often implies a failure in the process of receiving, rather than a simple absence.|例句：The radio experienced nonreception in the mountainous area.  收音机在山区接收不到信号。

The nonreception of the email led to a missed deadline.  邮件未收到导致错过截止日期。|近义词：lack of reception, failure to receive, non-receipt|反义词：reception, receipt
