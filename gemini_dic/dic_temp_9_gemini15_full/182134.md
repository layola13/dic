remittance:/rɪˈmɪtəns/| n. |汇款；汇款额；汇付；债务偿还|The act of sending money, especially to a person or organization in a different place.  It can refer to the money sent itself, or the process of sending it.|例句：The remittance arrived safely. 汇款安全到达了。|例句：She sent a remittance to her family in the countryside. 她给乡下的家人汇了款。|近义词：transmission, transfer, payment|反义词：receipt (in the sense of receiving money)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He regularly sends remittances to his parents. = 他定期给他父母汇款。
The company processes many remittances each day. = 公司每天处理许多汇款。


一般过去时:
They sent a remittance last week. = 他们上周汇了一笔款。
I received a remittance from my uncle. = 我收到了叔叔寄来的汇款。


现在完成时:
She has sent several remittances this year. = 她今年已经汇了几笔款。
Have you received the remittance yet? = 你收到汇款了吗？


过去完成时:
They had sent the remittance before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经汇款了。
He had not received the remittance when he called. = 他打电话时还没有收到汇款。


将来时:
I will send the remittance tomorrow. = 我明天会汇款。
She will receive the remittance next week. = 她下周会收到汇款。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would send a larger remittance. = 如果我有更多钱，我会汇更多款。
I wish I could send a remittance to help them. = 我希望我能汇款帮助他们。


被动语态:
The remittance was processed quickly. = 汇款很快就被处理了。
The remittance is being sent now. = 汇款正在发送中。


疑问句:
When will the remittance arrive? = 汇款什么时候到达？
How much was the remittance? = 汇款金额是多少？


否定句:
He did not send the remittance on time. = 他没有按时汇款。
The remittance has not yet been received. = 汇款尚未收到。
