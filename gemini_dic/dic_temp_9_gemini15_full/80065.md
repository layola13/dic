goalpost: /ˈɡoʊlˌpoʊst/ | n. |球门柱；目标；标杆 | The upright posts at each end of a playing field, between which the ball must be kicked or driven to score.  Goalposts are also used metaphorically to represent objectives or targets. |例句：The football team scored a goal by kicking the ball between the goalposts.  足球队将球踢进球门柱之间，进了一球。

The company set ambitious goalposts for its new product launch.  这家公司为新产品的发布设定了雄心勃勃的目标。|近义词：target, objective, aim |反义词：failure, setback
