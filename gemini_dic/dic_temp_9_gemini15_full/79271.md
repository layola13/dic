glaswegian:/ɡlæsˈweɪdʒən/| adj. n.|格拉斯哥的；格拉斯哥人|Relating to or characteristic of Glasgow, Scotland.  A person from Glasgow.|例句：He has a strong Glaswegian accent. 他有浓重的格拉斯哥口音。

例句：She's a Glaswegian, born and raised in the city.  她是格拉斯哥人，在那个城市土生土长。

近义词：Glaswegian (as a noun) can be loosely replaced by "citizen of Glasgow" or "native of Glasgow".  There is no direct antonym.


词性:形容词，名词
