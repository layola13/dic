defensing:/dɪˈfɛnsɪŋ/| v.| 防御；辩护 | The act of defending or protecting something or someone.  It often implies active resistance or argumentation. | 例句：The army was busy defensing the border.  军队正忙于保卫边境。 | 例句：He spent hours defensing his actions in court. 他在法庭上花了数小时为自己辩护。 | 近义词：defending, protecting, shielding, advocating | 反义词：attacking, assaulting, surrendering
