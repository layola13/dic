lickety:/ˈlɪkɪti/| adj.|飞快的，迅速的|extremely fast; swiftly.  The word is chiefly used in the phrase "lickety-split".|例句：He ran lickety-split to catch the bus. 他飞快地跑着去赶公共汽车。  例句：The car sped lickety-split down the highway.  那辆车飞快地沿着高速公路行驶。|近义词：rapid, quick, speedy, fast |反义词：slow, sluggish
