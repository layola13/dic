groundnut:/ˈɡraʊndnʌt/| n.|花生|A peanut; the edible seed of a plant in the legume family, typically grown in warm climates.  Groundnuts are rich in protein and oil and are used in various foods and products.|例句：We roasted some groundnuts for a snack. 我们烤了一些花生作为零食。|例句：Peanut butter is made from groundnuts. 花生酱是由花生制成的。|近义词：peanut, goober (US informal)|反义词：无
