reaping:/ˈriːpɪŋ/| n. | 收割；收获；结果 | The act of harvesting a crop; the gathering of a harvest; the consequences or results of one's actions. |例句：The farmer is busy with the reaping of the wheat.  农民正忙着收割小麦。|例句：Reaping the rewards of hard work is a satisfying experience.  收获辛勤劳动的成果是一种令人满足的体验。|近义词：harvesting, gathering, collecting |反义词：sowing, planting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is reaping the benefits of his hard work. = 他正在享受他努力工作的成果。
Farmers are reaping the corn in the fields. = 农民们正在田里收割玉米。


一般过去时:
They reaped a bountiful harvest last year. = 他们去年获得了丰收。
The company reaped huge profits from the new product. = 公司从新产品中获得了巨额利润。


现在完成时:
She has reaped the rewards of her dedication. = 她已经收获了她奉献的回报。
We have reaped what we sowed. = 我们已经收获了我们所播种的。


过去完成时:

By the end of the year, she had reaped the benefits of her investment. = 到年底，她已经收获了投资的回报。


将来时:
They will reap the consequences of their actions. = 他们将承担他们行为的后果。
We will reap a rich harvest if the weather cooperates. = 如果天气配合的话，我们将获得丰收。


虚拟语气:
If he had worked harder, he would have reaped greater rewards. = 如果他更努力工作，他就会获得更大的回报。
I wish I had reaped the benefits of that opportunity. = 我真希望我抓住了那个机会带来的好处。


被动语态:

疑问句:
