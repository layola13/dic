purveyed:/pərˈveɪd/| vt.|供应，提供，兜售|To supply or provide (something, especially goods or services); to offer for sale.|The restaurant purveyed fresh seafood.这家餐馆供应新鲜海鲜。The company purveyed its products to a wide range of customers. 这家公司向广大客户供应其产品。|近义词：supply, provide, furnish, vend|反义词：withhold, deny


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company purveys high-quality products. = 这家公司供应高质量的产品。
He purveys his services to many clients. = 他向许多客户提供他的服务。

一般过去时:
The store purveyed only locally sourced produce. = 这家商店只供应当地生产的产品。
They purveyed false information to the public. = 他们向公众散布虚假信息。

现在完成时:
They have purveyed substandard goods for years. = 他们多年来一直供应劣质商品。
She has purveyed her expertise to numerous projects. = 她已将她的专业知识用于许多项目。

过去完成时:
The vendor had purveyed counterfeit goods before being caught. = 该摊贩在被抓获之前曾兜售假冒商品。
The government had purveyed misleading statistics. = 政府曾发布误导性统计数据。

将来时:
The festival will purvey a diverse range of foods. = 节日将提供种类繁多的食物。
The company will purvey its new software globally. = 公司将向全球销售其新软件。

虚拟语气:
If the restaurant purveyed vegetarian options, more people would dine there. = 如果这家餐馆提供素食选择，更多的人会去那里吃饭。
I wish they purveyed organic produce. = 我希望他们供应有机农产品。

被动语态:
Substandard goods were purveyed to unsuspecting consumers. = 劣质商品被供应给了毫无戒心的消费者。
False information was purveyed by the media. = 虚假信息被媒体传播。

疑问句:

否定句:

The restaurant did not purvey any vegan dishes. = 这家餐馆没有提供任何素食菜肴。
