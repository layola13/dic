mortiser:/ˈmɔːrtɪsər/ | n., v. |榫眼机；凿榫眼的人；凿榫眼 | A mortiser is a tool or machine used to cut mortises (rectangular holes) in wood, typically for joining pieces of wood using tenons.  As a verb, it means to cut a mortise in wood. | The carpenter used a mortiser to create precise holes for the joints.  |  tenoner, jointer |  (no direct antonym, but the opposite action would be removing a mortise) | n., v.
