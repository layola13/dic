It often implies a significant connection,  perhaps bridging a geographical gap or providing redundancy. | Example: The new interconnector will significantly improve the reliability of the electricity supply. 新的互联器将大大提高电力供应的可靠性。


Example: The high-voltage interconnector enables the transfer of electricity between countries. 高压互联器能够实现国家间的电力传输。|近义词：connector, link, bridge, tie |反义词：separator, divider, isolator |名词
