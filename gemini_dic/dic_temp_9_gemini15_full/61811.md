emphyteusis: /ɛmˈfaɪtuːsɪs/ | n. | 永佃权；长期租赁权 |  A long-term lease of land, typically inheritable and often involving the payment of a fixed rent or fee.  It grants the lessee (emphyteuta) extensive rights over the land, almost akin to ownership, but the ultimate ownership remains with the lessor (dominus).| The farmer held an emphyteusis on the land, allowing him to cultivate it for generations.  这个农民拥有这块土地的永佃权，允许他世世代代耕种它。| 近义词：long-term lease, usufruct | 反义词：freehold, ownership | 名词
