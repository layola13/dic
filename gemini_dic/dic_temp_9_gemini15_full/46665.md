damourite:/ˈdæməraɪt/| n. | 水云母 | A hydrous potassium aluminum silicate mineral, a variety of muscovite with a high iron content.  It is typically pearly white or silvery-gray in color and has a pearly luster. Damourite is often found in metamorphic rocks and is a common alteration product of feldspars. | Example: The metamorphic rock sample contained significant amounts of damourite, indicating alteration of the original feldspar.  这个变质岩样本含有大量的达摩石，表明原始长石发生了蚀变。| 近义词: sericite, muscovite | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, as it's a specific type of mineral)| 名词
