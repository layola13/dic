misremembering:/ˌmɪsrɪˈmɛmbərɪŋ/| v. |记错，误记|The act of remembering something incorrectly; a false or inaccurate recollection.  It involves recalling something differently from how it actually happened. |例句：I’m misremembering the details of that conversation. 我记错了那次谈话的细节。

例句：He's misremembering the events of that night. 他记错了那天晚上的事情。


近义词：forgetting (in the sense of inaccurate recall),  confusing, distorting (memory)

反义词：remembering accurately, recalling correctly


词性:动词
