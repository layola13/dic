densen:/ˈdɛnsən/| adj.|浓密的；密集的；稠密的|Dense refers to something that is closely packed together or has a high density.  It can describe physical objects or abstract concepts.|例句：The forest was dense with trees, making it difficult to see through. 这片森林树木茂密，难以看穿。|例句：The air was dense with smoke after the fire. 火灾过后，空气中弥漫着浓烟。|近义词：thick, crowded, compact, concentrated|反义词：sparse, thin, scattered, dilute|形容词
