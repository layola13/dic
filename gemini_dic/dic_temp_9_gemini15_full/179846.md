reexperimenting:/ˌriːɪkˈspɛrɪmɛntɪŋ/ | v. | 重新实验；反复实验 |  The act of performing an experiment again, often with modifications or to verify previous results.  It implies a process of repeated testing and refinement. |例句：Scientists are reexperimenting with the new drug to confirm its efficacy.  科学家们正在重新实验这种新药以确认其疗效。

例句：The researchers are reexperimenting to rule out any potential confounding variables. 研究人员正在重新实验以排除任何潜在的混杂变量。


近义词：rerunning, repeating, retesting | 反义词：concluding, finishing
