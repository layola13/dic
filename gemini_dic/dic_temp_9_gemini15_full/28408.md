carbonations:/ˌkɑːrbəˈneɪʃənz/| n. |碳酸化；碳化作用|The process of adding carbon dioxide to a liquid, typically water or a beverage, to make it fizzy.  It involves dissolving carbon dioxide gas under pressure. Carbonation can also refer to the process of converting a substance into a carbonate. |例句：The carbonation in this soda is excellent. 这瓶苏打的碳酸气很足。

例句：The geologist studied the carbonation of rocks over time. 地质学家研究了岩石随时间的碳酸化过程。


近义词：fizzing, effervescence |反义词：decarbonation


词性：名词
