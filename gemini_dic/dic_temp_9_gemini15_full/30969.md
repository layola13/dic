chalcanthite:/ˈkæl.kæn.θaɪt/| n. |蓝铜矿|A hydrous copper sulfate mineral,  CuSO₄·5H₂O,  occurring as a secondary mineral in copper deposits. It's usually blue in color and has a vitreous luster.  It's also known as blue vitriol.|The chalcanthite sample showed a characteristic blue color and glassy appearance.|近义词：blue vitriol|反义词：无
