ratchet:/ˈræʧɪt/| n., v.|棘轮；步进机构；(使)步进前进|A ratchet is a mechanism that allows continuous rotation in one direction but prevents it in the other.  It's often used in tools and machinery to ensure that movement only occurs in a desired direction. As a verb, it means to increase or improve something gradually, step by step.|例句：The ratchet on the wrench prevented the bolt from loosening.  棘轮扳手上的棘轮防止螺栓松动。The company is slowly ratcheting up production. 公司正在逐步提高产量。|近义词：pawl, pawl and ratchet, click, increment|反义词：decrement, decrease
