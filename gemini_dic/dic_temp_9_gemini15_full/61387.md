emblazon:/ɪmˈbleɪzən/| vt.|装饰；显著地显示；以显著的方式展现|to decorate (something) conspicuously; to display prominently; to depict (a coat of arms) on a shield or banner|The castle's walls were emblazoned with the royal crest.|城堡的墙壁上饰有皇家纹章。
The company logo was emblazoned across the side of the truck.|公司标志醒目地印在卡车的侧面。| adorn, decorate, display, illustrate|obscure, conceal, hide|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist emblazons the shield with a dragon. = 艺术家在盾牌上绘上了一条龙。
She emblazons her message on social media. = 她在社交媒体上显著地表达了她的信息。

一般过去时:
They emblazoned the banner with the team's colors. = 他们用球队的颜色装饰了旗帜。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

She had emblazoned her point across before the meeting ended. = 会议结束前她已经清晰地表达了她的观点。

词源:
From Old French *enblazoner "to paint a coat of arms", from Old French blason "coat of arms", from Old High German *blason, from Proto-Germanic *blasōn.  The prefix *en- is of Germanic origin.
