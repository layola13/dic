cataractal:/ˌkætəˈræktəl/| adj.|白内障的；瀑布似的；洪水般的| Relating to or resembling a cataract; characterized by a sudden, overwhelming rush or flood.  The term often describes something resembling a waterfall in its force or appearance, or related to the eye condition cataracts.|例句：The cataractal lens clouded his vision. 白内障晶状体使他的视力模糊。 The cataractal rush of water swept away the bridge. 洪水般的水流冲垮了桥梁。|近义词：cascading, torrential, overwhelming|反义词：calm, gentle, gradual
