The deservedness of the punishment is questionable.  这种惩罚是否应得是有疑问的。|近义词：merit, entitlement, worthiness |反义词：unworthiness, undeservedness

词源:

The word "deservedness" is derived from the adjective "deserved," which itself comes from the verb "deserve."  "Deserve" ultimately traces back to Old French *deservir,  meaning "to serve," "to merit," or "to be worthy of."  The suffix "-ness" is a common English suffix forming abstract nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
