restrengthening: /ˌriːˈstrɛŋθənɪŋ/ | v. | 再增强；重新加强 | The act of making something stronger again.  It implies a previous weakening or decline that is being reversed.  |例句：The government is focusing on restrengthening the economy after the recent recession.  政府正在关注在最近的经济衰退后重新加强经济。

The company's restrengthening of its marketing strategy led to increased sales. 公司加强其营销策略导致销售额增加。|近义词：reinforcing, strengthening, fortifying |反义词：weakening, undermining


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is restrengthening its national defense. = 政府正在加强其国防力量。
They are restrengthening their relationship after a period of conflict. = 他们正在努力修复一段时期冲突后的关系。

一般过去时:
The company restrengthened its brand image through a new advertising campaign. = 公司通过新的广告宣传活动重新加强了其品牌形象。
He restrengthened his commitment to his fitness goals after a setback. = 他在遇到挫折后重新加强了他对健身目标的承诺。


现在完成时:
The team has restrengthened its position at the top of the league. = 该队已经巩固了其在联赛榜首的地位。
She has restrengthened her resolve to finish the marathon. = 她已经下定决心完成马拉松比赛。


过去完成时:
They had restrengthened the bridge before the heavy rains came. = 他们在暴雨来临之前加固了桥梁。
The doctor had restrengthened the patient's bones before the surgery. = 医生在手术前已经加强了病人的骨骼。


将来时:
The company will restrengthen its supply chain to prevent future disruptions. = 公司将加强其供应链，以防止未来的中断。
We will restrengthen our security measures to prevent future breaches. = 我们将加强我们的安全措施，以防止未来的漏洞。


虚拟语气:
If they restrengthened their defenses, they would be more secure. = 如果他们加强防御，他们会更安全。
I wish they had restrengthened the building's foundation earlier. = 我希望他们早点加固建筑的地基。


被动语态:
The economy is being restrengthened by government initiatives. = 经济正在通过政府措施得到加强。
The bridge was restrengthened using new materials. = 桥梁使用了新材料进行了加固。


疑问句:
Is the government restrengthening its commitment to environmental protection? = 政府是否正在加强其对环境保护的承诺？
Are they restrengthening their efforts to find a solution? = 他们是否正在加倍努力寻找解决方案？


否定句:
The company did not restrengthen its marketing team after the sales decline. = 公司在销售额下降后并没有加强其营销团队。
He failed to restrengthen his resolve to quit smoking. = 他未能下定决心戒烟。

词源:
re- (again) + strengthen (to make stronger)
