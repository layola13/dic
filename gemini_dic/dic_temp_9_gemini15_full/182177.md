remodifying: /ˌriːˈmɒdəfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 再修改；重新修改 |  The act of modifying something that has already been modified.  It implies a second or further alteration, refinement, or adjustment. | 例句：The team is remodifying the software to address the newly discovered bugs. = 团队正在重新修改软件以解决新发现的错误。

The artist is remodifying the sculpture, adding finer details. = 艺术家正在重新修改雕塑，添加更精细的细节。

近义词：revising, altering, amending, readjusting | 反义词：finalizing, completing

词源:

The word "remodifying" is formed by combining the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "anew") with the verb "modifying," which comes from the Latin word "modificare" (to shape, form, or alter).  Therefore, "remodifying" literally means "to modify again."
