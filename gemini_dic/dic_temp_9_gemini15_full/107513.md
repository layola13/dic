landamman: /ˈlændæmæn/ | n. |瑞士某些州的行政长官|

英文解析:The title of a chief magistrate or governor in certain Swiss cantons, particularly those in the German-speaking part of the country.  Historically, the landamman held significant political and judicial power. The exact powers varied depending on the specific canton and period.

例句:
The landamman presided over the cantonal council. = 州长主持州议会。

The power of the landamman was gradually reduced over time. = 州长的权力随着时间的推移逐渐减弱。


近义词:governor, magistrate, chief executive


反义词:subject, citizen (in the context of relative power)


词性:noun
