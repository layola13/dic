hatching: /ˈhætʃɪŋ/ | n. v. |孵化；起草；策划|

英文解析:
The noun "hatching" refers to the process of young animals emerging from eggs.  As a verb, "hatching" means to cause something to emerge from an egg or to plan or create something.

例句:
The hatching of the eggs was a joyous occasion for the family.  = 这些蛋的孵化对这个家庭来说是一个令人高兴的时刻。

We watched the baby birds hatching from their shells. = 我们看着小鸟从蛋壳里孵出来。


近义词:
incubation (n.),  brooding (n. & v.), plotting (v.), planning (v.)

反义词:
There isn't a direct antonym, but words suggesting the opposite of the hatching process could include:  death (of the embryo), failure (to hatch).
