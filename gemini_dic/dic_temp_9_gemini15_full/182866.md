repented:/rɪˈpɛntɪd/ | v. | 后悔；忏悔 | To feel regret or remorse for a past action; to show sorrow for sin. |例句：He repented his hasty decision. 他后悔自己草率的决定。|例句：She repented her sins and asked for forgiveness. 她悔过自己的罪行并请求宽恕。|近义词：regretted, atoned,  contrite |反义词：unrepentant, unapologetic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repents his actions daily. = 他每天都为自己的行为悔恨。
She repents her sins regularly. = 她定期忏悔自己的罪过。


一般过去时:
He repented his mistakes. = 他后悔自己的错误。
She repented her harsh words. = 她后悔自己说了那些刻薄的话。


现在完成时:
He has repented his wrongdoing. = 他已经悔过自己的恶行。
She has repented for her lies. = 她已经为自己的谎言忏悔了。


过去完成时:
He had repented his decision before it was too late. = 在为时已晚之前，他已经后悔了自己的决定。
She had repented her actions before she spoke. = 在她说话之前，她已经后悔了自己的行为。


将来时:
He will repent his actions one day. = 他总有一天会后悔自己的行为。
She will repent her decisions eventually. = 她最终会后悔自己的决定。


虚拟语气:
If he had repented earlier, things might have been different. = 如果他早点悔过，情况可能就不一样了。
I wish she had repented her cruel words. = 我真希望她能为她残酷的话语忏悔。


被动语态:
His actions were later repented. = 他的行为后来受到了悔恨。
Her mistakes were deeply repented. = 她的错误受到了深深的悔恨。


疑问句:
Has he repented his sins? = 他忏悔了自己的罪过吗？
Did she repent her lies? = 她为自己的谎言忏悔了吗？


否定句:
He did not repent his actions. = 他没有为自己的行为后悔。
She did not repent her mistakes. = 她没有为自己的错误后悔。


PHRASE->

repent at leisure = 从容不迫地后悔

deeply repent = 深深悔恨
