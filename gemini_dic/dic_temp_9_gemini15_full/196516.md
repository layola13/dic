servilize:/ˈsɜːrvəlaɪz/| vt.| 使奴役；使卑躬屈膝 | To make someone servile or submissive; to reduce to or treat as a slave.  |例句：The oppressive regime attempted to servilize the population through fear and intimidation.  压迫性政权试图通过恐惧和恐吓来奴役人民。

例句：Years of poverty and hardship had servilized him, robbing him of his dignity and self-respect.  多年的贫困和苦难使他卑躬屈膝，夺走了他的尊严和自尊。


近义词：enslave, subjugate, subdue, humiliate

反义词：empower, liberate, emancipate


词性：动词
