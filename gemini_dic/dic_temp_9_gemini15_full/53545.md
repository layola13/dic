disavows:/dɪs əˈvaʊz/ | vt. |否认；与…断绝关系；拒绝承认|To deny any connection with; renounce; repudiate.  |例句：The company disavows any responsibility for the accident. 公司否认对事故负有任何责任。|例句：He publicly disavowed his former associates. 他公开与他以前的同伙断绝关系。|近义词：deny, repudiate, renounce, disclaim |反义词：acknowledge, admit, endorse

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disavows his past actions. 他否认他过去的行动。
The politician disavows any connection to the scandal.  这位政治家否认与丑闻有任何关联。


一般过去时:
She disavowed the statements made by her spokesperson. 她否认了她的发言人发表的声明。
The government disavowed the actions of its agents. 政府否认其特工的行动。


现在完成时:
The company has disavowed responsibility for the environmental damage. 公司已经否认对环境破坏的责任。
He has disavowed his earlier beliefs. 他已经否认了他早先的信仰。


过去完成时:
Before the trial, he had disavowed all knowledge of the crime. 在审判之前，他已否认对犯罪有任何了解。
They had disavowed their allegiance to the king. 他们已经否认对国王效忠。


将来时:
She will disavow the agreement if the conditions are not met. 如果不满足条件，她将否认该协议。
The company will disavow any liability for damages. 公司将否认任何赔偿责任。


虚拟语气:
If he disavowed his involvement, the investigation would end. 如果他否认参与其中，调查就会结束。
I wish he had disavowed his association with that group. 我希望他早点否认与那个团体的联系。


被动语态:
His claims were disavowed by the authorities. 他的说法被当局否认了。
The treaty was disavowed by the opposing party.  条约被对方否认了。


疑问句:
Will the government disavow the report? 政府会否认这份报告吗？
Does he disavow the allegations against him? 他是否否认针对他的指控？


否定句:
The witness did not disavow her testimony. 证人没有否认她的证词。
He would not disavow his loyalty to his country. 他不会否认他对国家的忠诚。


PHRASE->

disavow knowledge  否认了解
