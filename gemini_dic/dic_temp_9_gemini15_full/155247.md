phalangid:/fəˈlænɡɪd/ | n. | 盲蛛；长脚蜘蛛 | A phalangid is an arachnid belonging to the order Opiliones, commonly known as harvestmen or daddy longlegs.  They are characterized by their long legs and generally small bodies. Unlike spiders, they lack venom glands and silk-producing spinnerets. | Example:  The phalangid scuttled across the damp leaf litter.  这只盲蛛快速地穿过潮湿的落叶层。 | 近义词：harvestman, daddy longlegs | 反义词：spider (in terms of venom and silk production)
