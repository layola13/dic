frees-up: /friːz ʌp/ | vt. |腾出（时间、空间等）；使解脱；释放；使自由 | To make (something) available for use; to release or liberate (someone or something). |例句：Finishing that project frees up my schedule for the weekend. 完成那个项目使我周末的日程空了出来。


例句：The new software frees up valuable time for the employees. 新软件为员工腾出了宝贵的时间。


近义词：release, liberate, free, clear, vacate


反义词：restrict, tie up, occupy, engage


词性: 动词
