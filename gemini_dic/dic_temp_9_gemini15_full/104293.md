justification:/ˌdʒʌstɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 辩解；理由；证明；正当理由 | The act of showing something to be right or reasonable.  It can refer to a reason, explanation, or excuse for an action or belief. |例句：His justification for his actions was unconvincing. 他的行为解释令人难以信服。

例句：The government offered no justification for its decision. 政府没有为其决定提供任何理由。


近义词：reason, explanation, rationale, excuse, vindication | 反义词：refutation, condemnation, invalidation | 词性：名词


词源:  From Middle French *iustification, from Latin iustificatio, from iustificare "to justify," from iustus "just" + facere "to make."  The word's history reflects its core meaning: making something just or right.
