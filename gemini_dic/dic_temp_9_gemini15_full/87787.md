hibernation:/ˌhaɪbərˈneɪʃən/| n. |冬眠；蛰伏|The state or period of winter sleep in which some animals pass the winter.  It is characterized by a slowing of metabolism, a decrease in body temperature, and a suspension of most bodily functions. |例句：Bears enter hibernation in the late autumn. 熊在深秋进入冬眠。

例句：Many animals use hibernation to survive harsh winters. 许多动物利用冬眠来度过严酷的冬天。


近义词：dormancy, lethargy, torpor|反义词：activity, wakefulness|词性:名词
