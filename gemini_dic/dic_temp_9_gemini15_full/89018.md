hollows:/ˈhɒləʊz/ /ˈhɑːloʊz/ | n. v. |凹地；空洞；使凹陷；使空虚 |

英文解析：
As a noun, "hollows" refers to a low-lying area, often concave in shape, such as a valley or a depression in the ground.  It can also refer to a cavity or empty space within something. As a verb, "hollows" means to make something hollow or empty, or to become hollow or empty.


例句：
The wind howled through the hollows of the mountains.  = 风在山谷中呼啸。

The tree's trunk was hollowed out by decay. = 树干被腐烂掏空了。


近义词：
(n.) cavity, depression, valley, pit, recess
(v.) excavate, empty, deplete


反义词：

(v.) fill, replenish
