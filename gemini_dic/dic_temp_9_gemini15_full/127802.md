neighs:/neɪz/| v.|马嘶叫|The sound a horse makes.|The horse neighs loudly when it sees its owner.|近义词：whinnies|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The horse neighs every morning. = 马每天早上都嘶叫。
He neighs when he is excited. = 他兴奋时会嘶叫。


一般过去时:
The horse neighed loudly last night. = 昨夜马大声嘶叫。
The stallion neighed to attract a mare. = 为了吸引母马，种马嘶叫。


现在完成时:
The horse has neighed several times today. = 马今天已经嘶叫了好几次。
The foal has never neighed before. = 这小马驹以前从未嘶叫过。


过去完成时:
The horse had already neighed before I arrived. = 我到达之前，马已经嘶叫过了。
They had heard the horse neigh many times before. = 他们以前就多次听到马嘶叫。


将来时:
The horse will neigh when it hears the bell. = 马听到铃声就会嘶叫。
I think the horse will neigh soon. = 我想马很快就会嘶叫。


虚拟语气:
If the horse neighed, I would know it was nearby. = 如果马嘶叫，我就知道它就在附近。
I wish the horse would neigh; it would make me feel better. = 我希望马能嘶叫；那会让我感觉更好。


被动语态:
(Neighs is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Does the horse neigh often? = 这匹马经常嘶叫吗？
Why did the horse neigh? = 马为什么嘶叫？


否定句:
The horse didn't neigh last night. = 昨夜马没有嘶叫。
The foal hasn't neighed yet. = 小马驹还没嘶叫呢。


PHRASE->
