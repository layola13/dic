secondary:/ˈsekəndəri/| adj.|次要的；中等的；二级的|Of second importance or rank; subordinate.  It refers to something that comes after the primary or main thing.  It can also refer to the second stage in a process or system, such as secondary education.|例句：His secondary concern was the cost of the project.他的次要考虑是项目的成本。

例句：Secondary education typically includes high school.中等教育通常包括高中。|近义词：subsidiary, subordinate, supplementary|反义词：primary, principal, main|词性：adj.
