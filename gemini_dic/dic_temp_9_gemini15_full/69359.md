federation:/ˌfedəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |联邦，联合会，同盟 | A group of states or nations united under a central government; a union of organizations.  |Example: The United States is a federation of fifty states. 美国是一个由五十个州组成的联邦。 |Example: The International Cycling Federation governs professional cycling worldwide. 国际自行车联盟管理着全世界的职业自行车运动。|近义词：confederation, league, alliance |反义词：separation, division
