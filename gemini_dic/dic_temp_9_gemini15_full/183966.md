reshaped:/ˌriːˈʃeɪpt/| v.|重塑；改变形状；改造|To give a new shape or form to something.  It implies a significant alteration, often improving or refining the original form. |例句：The company reshaped its marketing strategy after a period of declining sales. 公司在销售额下降一段时间后重塑了其营销策略。

例句：The sculptor reshaped the clay into a graceful bird. 雕塑家将粘土重塑成一只优雅的鸟。


近义词：reform, remodel, restructure, transform, revamp

反义词：destroy, ruin, demolish
