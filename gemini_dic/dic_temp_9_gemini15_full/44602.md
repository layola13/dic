crowd-pleasers: /ˈkraʊdˌpliːzərz/ | n. |讨好大众之物；深受欢迎的事物 | Things or people that are guaranteed to be popular with a large number of people.  They are designed or intended to appeal to a wide audience and evoke positive responses. |例句：The band played a set of crowd-pleasers, including many of their greatest hits. 乐队演奏了一套深受大众喜爱的歌曲，其中包括他们许多最伟大的热门歌曲。

The comedian's jokes were all crowd-pleasers, getting laughs from everyone in the audience.  这位喜剧演员的笑话都很讨喜，让观众席上的每个人都哈哈大笑。 |近义词：hits, favorites, popular attractions, sure-fire successes |反义词：flops, failures, unpopular choices
