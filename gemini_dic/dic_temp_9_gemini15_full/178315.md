recondemnations:/ˌriːkɒndɛmˈneɪʃənz/ | n. | 再谴责；再次谴责的行为 | The act of condemning something again, often after a previous condemnation.  It implies a renewed or intensified expression of disapproval. | Example: The recondemnations of the war crimes were met with mixed reactions.  再次谴责战争罪行引起了各种不同的反应。 The recondemnations of his actions highlighted the severity of his offenses.  再次谴责他的行为突显了他罪行的严重性。 | 近义词：reprobation, denunciation, censure | 反义词：endorsement, approval, praise | 名词
