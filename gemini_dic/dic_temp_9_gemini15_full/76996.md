gardenist:/ˈɡɑːrdənɪst/ | n. | 园艺师，园丁 | A person who works in or is skilled in gardening.  | Example: The gardenist carefully pruned the roses.  这位园艺师仔细修剪了玫瑰花。 |近义词：gardener, horticulturist | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gardenist plants flowers every spring. = 园艺师每年春天都种花。
He tends the garden every morning. = 他每天早上都照料花园。

一般过去时:
The gardenist designed a beautiful garden last year. = 园艺师去年设计了一个漂亮的花园。
She pruned the bushes yesterday. = 她昨天修剪了灌木。

现在完成时:
The gardenist has planted hundreds of trees. = 园艺师已经种了几百棵树。
He has already finished the weeding. = 他已经除完了草。

过去完成时:
The gardenist had prepared the soil before planting the seeds. = 园艺师在播种前已经准备好了土壤。
She had already watered the plants before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，她已经浇过水了。

将来时:
The gardenist will plant new vegetables next month. = 园艺师下个月将种植新的蔬菜。
He will prune the trees in the autumn. = 他将在秋季修剪树木。

虚拟语气:
If the gardenist had more time, he would create a vegetable patch. = 如果园艺师有更多的时间，他会创建一个菜园。
I wish the gardenist could help me with my garden. = 我希望园艺师能帮我打理我的花园。

被动语态:
