optometrists:/ˌɒptˈɒmɪtrɪsts/ | n. |验光师，视光师 | Healthcare professionals who examine eyes, diagnose vision problems, and prescribe corrective lenses.  They also manage certain eye diseases. | Example: The optometrist checked my vision and prescribed new glasses.  验光师检查了我的视力并开了新的眼镜。  |近义词:optician (sometimes used interchangeably, but optometrists have more extensive training and can diagnose and treat eye diseases)|反义词: N/A
