hibernations:/ˌhaɪbərˈneɪʃənz/ | n. |冬眠；蛰伏 |The act or process of hibernating; a period of hibernation.  Hibernations are characterized by a state of inactivity and slowed metabolism.  |例句：Bears are known for their long hibernations during winter. 熊以其在冬季长时间冬眠而闻名。

例句：The snake spent the cold months in a state of hibernation.  这条蛇在寒冷的几个月里处于冬眠状态。


近义词：dormancy, torpor, lethargy

反义词：activity, wakefulness


词性：名词
