gephyrean:/dʒɪˈfɪəriən/| n. |叶须动物|A gephyrean is a marine invertebrate belonging to the phylum Gephyrea, characterized by a long, often worm-like body and a proboscis used for feeding.  They are found in various marine habitats, from shallow waters to deep-sea trenches.  Gephyreans are typically benthic, meaning they live on or in the seafloor.| Example:  The deep-sea expedition discovered several new species of gephyrean worms. 深海探险队发现了几个新的叶须动物物种。  |近义词:  (lacks close synonyms due to its specific taxonomic classification)|反义词: (lacks direct antonyms)| noun
