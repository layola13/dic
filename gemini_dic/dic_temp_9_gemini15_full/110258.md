lifetime:/ˈlaɪftaɪm/| n.|一生，寿命，使用寿命|The entire period of a person's or thing's existence.|例句：He lived a long and happy lifetime. 他过着漫长而幸福的一生。|例句：The lifetime of the battery is about two years.  这个电池的寿命大约是两年。|近义词： lifespan, duration |反义词:moment, instant
