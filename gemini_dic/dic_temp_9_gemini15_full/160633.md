porte:/pɔːrt/|/pɔːrt/| n. |门，入口，港口|A porte is a door or gate, especially a large or impressive one.  It can also refer to a harbor or port.  |例句：The heavy oak porte creaked open.  这扇沉重的橡木门吱呀一声开了。|例句：The ship sailed into the busy port. 这艘船驶入了繁忙的港口。|近义词：door, gate, entrance, harbor|反义词：exit, outlet
