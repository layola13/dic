globalisations: /ˌɡloʊbəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 全球化现象 (复数) | The process of interaction and integration among people, companies, and governments worldwide.  This process is driven by international trade and investment and aided by information technology.  It has effects on the environment, culture, political systems, economic development and human physical well-being. |例句：The rapid advancements in technology have accelerated globalisations in recent decades. 近年来科技的快速发展加速了全球化进程。

The increasing globalisations of markets has led to greater competition among businesses. 市场日益全球化导致企业间的竞争更加激烈。|近义词：globalization (单数), internationalization, worldwide integration |反义词：localization, isolation, protectionism
