reanimate:/ˌriˈænɪmeɪt/| vt.|使复苏；使复活；使重新活跃|To restore life to; revive.  To give new life, vigor, or activity to.|例句：The doctor tried to reanimate the patient after the accident.医生试图在事故后使病人复苏。

例句：The community project reanimated the local economy. 社区项目使当地经济重新活跃起来。


近义词：revive, revitalize, resuscitate|反义词：kill, destroy, extinguish|动词
