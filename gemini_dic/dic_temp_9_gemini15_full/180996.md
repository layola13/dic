reimportune:/ˌriːɪmˈpɔːrtuːn/| vt.|恳求；再三请求；纠缠|To ask someone repeatedly and urgently to do something.|例句：He continued to reimportune his friend for a loan. 他继续向朋友恳求贷款。|例句：She reimportuned him until he finally agreed. 她一再恳求他，直到他最终同意。|近义词：beseech, implore, entreat, importune|反义词：refuse, deny


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reimportunes his boss for a raise. = 他反复向老板请求加薪。
She reimportunes her parents for permission. = 她反复向父母请求许可。

一般过去时:
He reimportuned his friend for a loan, but he refused. = 他反复向朋友请求贷款，但对方拒绝了。
She reimportuned the manager until she got the job. = 她反复恳求经理，直到她得到这份工作。

现在完成时:
He has reimportuned me for help several times. = 他已经多次恳求我帮忙。
She has reimportuned her parents for a car, but they still haven't agreed. = 她已经多次恳求父母给她买一辆车，但他们仍然没有同意。

过去完成时:
He had reimportuned her for days before she finally relented. = 他已经恳求了她好几天，她才最终让步。
They had reimportuned the committee for funding before they received a positive response. = 他们在得到积极回应之前，已经多次向委员会恳求资金。

将来时:

She will reimportune her friends for support. = 她将再次恳求朋友的支持。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
