catwalks:/ˈkætˌwɔːks/ | n. | 栈道；天桥；（时装表演的）伸展台 | A raised, narrow walkway, typically one that is elevated above the ground or floor.  In fashion, it refers to the runway where models present clothing. |  例句：The workers used the catwalk to access the top of the building. 工人们用栈道到达建筑物的顶部。  The models strutted down the catwalk in their dazzling new designs. 模特们穿着他们令人眼花缭乱的新款服装在伸展台上昂首阔步。 | 近义词：runway, walkway, gangway | 反义词：ground, floor
