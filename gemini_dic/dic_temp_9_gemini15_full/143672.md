outlasting: /ˌaʊtˈlæstɪŋ/ | v. | 持久超过；比…更持久 |  to last longer than (someone or something) |

The old oak tree outlasted all the other trees in the forest.  = 那棵古老的橡树比森林里其他所有的树都活得长。

The company's innovative design outlasted its competitors' products. = 该公司的创新设计比竞争对手的产品更持久。


近义词:  surpassing, surviving, enduring, exceeding

反义词:  predeceasing, failing, perishing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The quality of this product outlasts its price. = 这个产品的质量超过了它的价格。
His determination outlasts his physical limitations. = 他的决心超过了他的身体局限性。


一般过去时:
The Roman Empire outlasted many other ancient civilizations. = 罗马帝国比许多其他古代文明存续时间更长。
Her love for her family outlasted all hardships. = 她对家人的爱战胜了所有的苦难。


现在完成时:
This technology has outlasted many of its predecessors. = 这项技术已经比许多它的前辈存在的时间更长。
The building has outlasted numerous storms. = 这座建筑已经经受住了无数的风暴。


过去完成时:
The ancient ruins had outlasted centuries of neglect. = 古代遗迹已经经历了几个世纪的忽视。
The old bridge had outlasted several major floods. = 那座老桥已经经受住了几次大的洪水。


将来时:
This material will outlast any other on the market. = 这种材料将比市场上任何其他材料都更耐用。
We believe our strategy will outlast the current economic downturn. = 我们相信我们的战略将能够度过当前的经济低迷时期。


虚拟语气:
If only his health had outlasted his ambition. = 如果他的健康能够比他的雄心更持久就好了。
If the bridge had outlasted the storm, there would have been no casualties. = 如果桥梁能够经受住风暴，就不会有伤亡了。


被动语态:
The original design was outlasted by newer models. = 原来的设计被更新的型号超越了。
His legacy will be outlasted by the impact of his actions. = 他的遗产将被他的行为的影响所超越。


疑问句:
Will this battery outlast the others? = 这块电池的续航能力会比其他的长吗？
Does this material outlast its competitors? = 这种材料的耐用性比竞争对手的产品好吗？


否定句:
His patience did not outlast the ordeal. = 他的耐心没能熬过这场磨难。
The old car didn't outlast the warranty period. = 那辆旧车没有撑过保修期。
