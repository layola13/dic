nazifying: /ˈnɑːzɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 纳粹化；使…纳粹化 | The process of imposing Nazi ideology, practices, or policies on something or someone.  It involves making something conform to Nazi principles, often through forceful or coercive means. | 例句：The regime was accused of nazifying the education system.  这个政权被指控纳粹化了教育体系。

The government's policies are seen by some as a form of nazifying the media.  一些人认为政府的政策是对媒体的一种纳粹化。


近义词：fascistizing, totalitarianizing | 反义词：democratizing, liberalizing


词性：动词
