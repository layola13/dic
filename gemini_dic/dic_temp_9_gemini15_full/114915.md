manage:/ˈmænɪdʒ/ | vt. & n. | 管理；经营；设法对付 | To control or be in charge of something, especially a business, organization, or group of people.  | 例句：She manages a team of ten people. 她管理着一个十人的团队。 | 例句：He managed to finish the project on time. 他设法按时完成了项目。 | 近义词：administer, direct, handle, cope with | 反义词：fail, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She manages the company effectively. = 她有效地管理着公司。
He manages to solve the problem. = 他设法解决了这个问题。


一般过去时:
They managed the project successfully last year. = 他们去年成功地管理了这个项目。
She managed to reach the summit despite the bad weather. = 尽管天气恶劣，她还是设法到达了山顶。


现在完成时:
She has managed the department for five years. = 她已经管理这个部门五年了。
He has managed to overcome many challenges in his life. = 他一生中克服了许多挑战。


过去完成时:
They had managed to complete the report before the deadline. = 他们已在截止日期前完成了报告。
She had managed to save enough money for a down payment. = 她已攒够了首付的钱。


将来时:
We will manage the crisis effectively. = 我们将有效地管理这场危机。
He will manage to find a solution. = 他将设法找到一个解决方案。


虚拟语气:
If I managed the company, I would implement new strategies. = 如果我管理这家公司，我会实施新的战略。
I wish I had managed to finish the marathon. = 我真希望我已经完成了马拉松比赛。


被动语态:
The company is managed by a young CEO. = 这家公司由一位年轻的首席执行官管理。
The problem was managed efficiently. = 这个问题得到了有效的处理。


疑问句:
Do you manage your time well? = 你时间管理得好吗？
How do you manage to do so much in one day? = 你一天怎么能做这么多事？


否定句:
He didn't manage to finish the race. = 他没能完成比赛。
She doesn't manage her stress very well. = 她不太擅长控制压力。


PHRASE->
manage to do something = 设法做某事
manage without something = 没有…也能应付
manage one's time = 管理时间
manage expectations = 管理预期
manage a team = 管理一个团队
