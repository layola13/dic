goad:/ɡoʊd/| n., vt.|刺激；驱使；激励；尖锐的刺激物|A goad is something that encourages or urges someone to do something. It can be a physical object or an idea.  As a verb, it means to encourage or urge someone to do something. |例句：He used a goad to urge the oxen forward. 他用尖棒驱赶牛前进。The constant criticism goaded him into action.  持续的批评刺激他采取行动。|近义词：spur, prod, incite, stimulate|反义词：discourage, dissuade, deter

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He goads his employees to work harder. = 他激励他的员工更努力工作。
The coach goads the team to victory. = 教练激励球队取得胜利。


一般过去时:
The farmer goaded the mule with a stick. = 农夫用棍子驱赶骡子。
Her taunts goaded him into a fight. = 她的嘲讽激怒了他，让他大打出手。


现在完成时:
Years of hardship have goaded her into becoming a successful entrepreneur. = 多年的艰辛激励她成为一名成功的企业家。
The constant pressure has goaded him into making a rash decision. = 持续的压力迫使他做出了草率的决定。


过去完成时:
He had been goaded into making a confession by the relentless interrogation. = 他在无情的审讯下被激怒而招供。
She had been goaded by her friends into skydiving. = 她被朋友怂恿去跳伞。


将来时:
I will goad him into finishing his project. = 我将激励他完成他的项目。
The advertisement will goad consumers into buying the product. = 广告将刺激消费者购买该产品。


虚拟语气:
If I were to goad him, he might finally finish the report. = 如果我去激励他，他也许最终会完成报告。
I wish I hadn't goaded him into such a risky endeavor. = 我真希望我没有怂恿他去做这么冒险的事情。


被动语态:
He was goaded into a confession. = 他被激怒而招供。
The team was goaded into greater effort by their coach. = 球队被他们的教练激励着付出更大的努力。


疑问句:
What goads him to work so hard? = 是什么激励他如此努力工作？
Does public opinion goad the government into action? = 公众舆论是否会促使政府采取行动？


否定句:
He didn't goad me into doing anything. = 他没有怂恿我做任何事。
Nothing could goad her into revealing her secrets. = 什么也无法诱使她泄露她的秘密。


PHRASE->
goad someone into doing something = 刺激某人做某事
goad someone on = 鼓励某人继续
goad someone to action = 激励某人采取行动
goad someone to anger = 激怒某人
a sharp goad = 一根尖锐的刺激物


词源:
The word "goad" comes from the Old English word "gad," meaning a pointed stick used to urge on cattle.  This in turn has roots in Proto-Germanic.  The verb sense developed later from the noun's usage.
