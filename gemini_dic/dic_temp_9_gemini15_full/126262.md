myelitis:/ˌmaɪəˈlaɪtɪs/| n. |脊髓炎|Inflammation of the spinal cord.  This can be caused by various infections (viral, bacterial, fungal), autoimmune disorders, or other conditions. Symptoms can vary widely depending on the cause and the area of the spinal cord affected, but often include pain, muscle weakness, numbness, and paralysis.|Example:  The doctor suspects myelitis as the cause of his sudden paralysis.  医生怀疑脊髓炎是导致他突然瘫痪的原因。  The patient presented with symptoms consistent with transverse myelitis. 病人出现的症状与横贯性脊髓炎一致。|近义词：spinal cord inflammation|反义词：N/A
