hydrofluoric:/ˌhaɪdrəˈflʊərɪk/| adj.|氢氟酸的；含氢氟酸的 |  Relating to or denoting hydrofluoric acid.  Hydrofluoric acid is a highly corrosive, colorless solution of hydrogen fluoride (HF) in water. It is used in various industrial processes, including glass etching, metal cleaning, and the production of certain chemicals.  | Example: Hydrofluoric acid is a dangerous chemical and must be handled with extreme care. 氢氟酸是一种危险的化学物质，必须小心处理。 |近义词:fluoridic|反义词:None.

词源:
The word "hydrofluoric" is a combination of "hydro-" (from Greek  ὕδωρ "hydor" meaning "water") and "fluoric" (from "fluorine," ultimately from Latin "fluere" meaning "to flow").  It describes an acid containing fluorine and derived from it.
