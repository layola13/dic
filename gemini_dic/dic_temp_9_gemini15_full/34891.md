clippingly:/ˈklɪpɪŋli/| adv.|剪切地；急促地；简短而生硬地|In a short, sharp, and sometimes abrupt manner, often suggesting impatience or disdain.  It implies a quick, clipped way of speaking, lacking warmth or fluency.|例句：She answered clippingly, "No." 她简短而生硬地回答说：“不。”

例句：He spoke clippingly, dismissing the suggestion with a wave of his hand. 他说话简短而生硬，挥挥手就否决了这个建议。


近义词：curtly, abruptly, sharply, briefly, tersely|反义词：lengthily, expansively, verbosely, eloquently|词性:副词
