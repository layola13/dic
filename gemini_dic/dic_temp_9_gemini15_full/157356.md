piperazine: /ˌpaɪpəˈzeɪn/ | n. |哌嗪|A heterocyclic organic compound with two nitrogen atoms in a six-membered ring. It is a colorless crystalline solid with a slight amine odor. Piperazine is used in medicine as an anthelmintic (to treat parasitic worm infections) and as a building block in the synthesis of other organic compounds.  | Example: Piperazine is effective against intestinal parasites. 哌嗪对肠道寄生虫有效。|近义词:  (No direct synonyms, as it's a specific chemical compound) |反义词: (No direct antonyms)|名词
