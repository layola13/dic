punisher:/ˈpʌnɪʃər/| n. |惩罚者，报应者|A person or thing that punishes; someone who inflicts punishment, especially severely.  It can also refer to something that causes suffering or hardship.|例句：The punisher brought swift justice to the criminals.  惩罚者迅速地将罪犯绳之以法。|例句：The harsh winter was a punisher for the homeless.  严酷的冬天对无家可归的人来说是一场灾难。|近义词：avenger, chastiser, disciplinarian |反义词：rewarder, savior, protector |名词
