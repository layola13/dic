immobility:/ˌɪmˈoʊbɪləti/| n. |不动；静止；不活动 |The state of being unable to move.  It can refer to a physical inability to move, or a metaphorical inability to progress or change. |例句：His immobility was caused by a severe stroke.他的不动是严重中风造成的。|例句：The political immobility of the country led to widespread frustration.国家的政治僵局导致了广泛的失望。|近义词：stillness, stagnation, inertia, paralysis |反义词：mobility, movement, activity, dynamism
