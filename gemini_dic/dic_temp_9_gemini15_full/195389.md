semimonarchic: /ˌsɛmiˈmɒnɑːkɪk/ | adj. | 半君主制的；半君主国的 | Relating to or characteristic of a semimonarchy; a system of government combining elements of monarchy and other forms of government, often republic or democracy.  | The country transitioned from a fully monarchic system to a more semimonarchic one, granting the parliament significant power.  这个国家从完全君主制过渡到半君主制，赋予议会很大的权力。 | near-monarchic, quasi-monarchic | fully democratic, fully republican |  形容词
