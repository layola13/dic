gravest:/ˈɡreɪvɪst/| adj.|最严重的；最严肃的；最庄重的|Most serious or severe; most solemn or dignified.|例句：This is the gravest crisis the country has faced in decades.  这个国家几十年来面临着最严重的危机。

例句：He spoke with the gravest of expressions. 他以最严肃的表情说话。


近义词：most serious, most critical, most solemn, most important


反义词：least serious, least important, trivial, frivolous


词性：形容词
