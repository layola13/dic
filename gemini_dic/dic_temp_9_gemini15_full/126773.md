nanobots:/ˈnænəʊbɒts/ | n. |纳米机器人 | Microscopic robots, typically measuring between 1 and 100 nanometers, designed to perform specific tasks at a molecular or cellular level.  These robots are currently largely theoretical, with limited real-world applications, but hold immense potential in medicine, manufacturing, and environmental remediation. | Example: Scientists are developing nanobots to deliver drugs directly to cancer cells.  科学家们正在研发将药物直接输送到癌细胞的纳米机器人。


| Example: The potential applications of nanobots in environmental cleanup are vast. 纳米机器人在环境清理方面的潜在应用是巨大的。|  近义词:microrobots, nanomachines | 反义词:macroscopic robots, large-scale machines |  词性:名词
