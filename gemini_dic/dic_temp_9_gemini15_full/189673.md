salmonellas:/ˌsælməˈnɛləz/| n. |沙门氏菌属|The plural of Salmonella, a genus of rod-shaped, Gram-negative, facultative anaerobic bacteria.  They are a common cause of food poisoning. |例句：Contaminated food can contain salmonellas, leading to food poisoning.|近义词：bacteria (general term), pathogens|反义词：无
