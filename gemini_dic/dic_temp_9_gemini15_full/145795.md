The term implies a surpassing of typical decadence, reaching a level of extreme corruption or degradation.|The overdecadences of the late Roman Empire led to its eventual collapse.  过度颓废的罗马帝国最终导致了它的崩溃。 The novel depicts a society consumed by overdecadences and moral bankruptcy. 这部小说描绘了一个被过度颓废和道德破产吞噬的社会。|近义词：extreme corruption, utter depravity, moral disintegration |反义词：moral integrity, virtue, righteousness
