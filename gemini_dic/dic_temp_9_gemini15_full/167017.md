presubstitute: /ˌpriːˈsʌbstɪtjuːt/ | vt. |预先替代；预先取代| To substitute beforehand; to replace something in advance.  The word combines "pre-" (before) and "substitute".|例句：They presubstituted the damaged parts before the launch. 他们在发射前预先更换了损坏的部件。


例句：The team presubstituted their star player to give him rest.  球队预先换下他们的明星球员以让他休息。


近义词：preplace, replace beforehand, anticipate


反义词：postpone substitution, leave as is


词性:动词
