maltobiose:/mælˈtoʊbaɪoʊs/| n. |麦芽糖|A disaccharide composed of two glucose units linked by an α(1→4) glycosidic bond. It is a product of the hydrolysis of starch and is a component of many polysaccharides. Maltobiose is an important intermediate in the digestion of starch and other carbohydrates.|例句：Maltobiose is a reducing sugar that can be further hydrolyzed into two glucose molecules. 麦芽糖是一种还原糖，可以进一步水解成两个葡萄糖分子。|近义词：maltose (although maltose is a more common term)|反义词:|
