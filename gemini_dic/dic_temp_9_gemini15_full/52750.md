dilapidator: /dɪˈlæpɪdeɪtər/ | n. | 使破败者；使荒废者 | One who dilapidates; someone who causes something to fall into disrepair or ruin.  | The dilapidator of the old mansion left it in a state of utter disrepair.  使那栋旧宅变得破败不堪的人把它留在了完全失修的状态下。|近义词：ruiner, destroyer, wrecker | 反义词：restorer, preserver, renovator | 名词
