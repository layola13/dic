monkism:/ˈmʌŋkɪzəm/| n. |僧侣生活；隐修主义|The practice of living the life of a monk, characterized by religious devotion, celibacy, and often seclusion.  It emphasizes spiritual discipline, meditation, and detachment from worldly affairs.|Example:  He embraced monkism after a period of deep spiritual searching. 他在经历了一段深刻的精神探索后，皈依了僧侣生活。|近义词：monasticism, asceticism|反义词：hedonism, secularism
