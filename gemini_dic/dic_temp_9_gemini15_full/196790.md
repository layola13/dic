sextet:/ˈsɛksɛt/| n. |六重奏；六行诗；六个一组的事物 |A sextet is a musical composition for six performers, or a poem or stanza of six lines.  It can also refer to a group of six things. |Example: The string sextet played a beautiful piece.  弦乐六重奏演奏了一首优美的乐曲。 The poem's final sextet beautifully resolved the narrative. 这首诗的最后六行诗句完美地解决了叙事问题。|近义词：ensemble (for musical context), stanza (for poetic context)|反义词：无
