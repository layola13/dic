flow-diagram:/ˈfloʊˌdaɪəˌɡræm/| n. |流程图，流程图|A flow diagram is a type of diagram that represents a workflow or process.  It shows the steps in a process and how they are connected. Flow diagrams are used in many fields, such as software engineering, business process management, and manufacturing. They help to visualize and understand complex processes. |Example:  The software engineer created a flow diagram to illustrate the program's logic.  软件工程师创建了一个流程图来说明程序的逻辑。|近义词： flowchart, process chart, workflow diagram |反义词：N/A
