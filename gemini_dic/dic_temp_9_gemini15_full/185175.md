retoric:/ˈrɛtərɪk/| n. |修辞学；雄辩术；华丽的辞藻 |The art of effective or persuasive speaking or writing, especially the use of figures of speech and other compositional techniques.  |His speech was full of rhetoric but lacked substance. 他的演讲充满了华丽辞藻，却缺乏实质内容。| eloquence, oratory, discourse | inarticulate, plain speaking |名词

虚拟语气:
If he had used less rhetoric, his argument would have been more persuasive. 如果他少用一些华丽辞藻，他的论点就会更有说服力。
