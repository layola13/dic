crescendos:/krɛˈʃɛndoʊz/| n.|渐强；高潮|A gradual increase in loudness, force, or intensity.|例句：The music built to a crescendo of thrilling intensity. 音乐逐渐增强，达到令人兴奋的高潮。
The tension in the room reached a crescendo. 房间里的紧张气氛达到了高潮。|近义词：climax, peak, culmination|反义词：diminuendo, decrease
