psychopomp:/ˈsaɪkəpɒmp/|/ˈsaɪkoʊpɑːmp/| n. |引魂者，冥府使者|A psychopomp is a being, spirit, or deity whose function is to guide souls of the dead to the afterlife.  They often appear in myths and folklore, acting as intermediaries between the world of the living and the world of the dead.  The term is often used in literature and art to represent this role.|Example: In Greek mythology, Hermes acted as a psychopomp, guiding souls to Hades.  希腊神话中，赫尔墨斯充当引魂者的角色，引导亡灵前往冥府。|近义词：soul guide, spirit guide, ferryman, conductor of souls|反义词：N/A
