haplessly:/ˈhæpləsli/| adv.|不幸地，倒霉地，无助地 | Unluckily; in an unfortunate manner; without luck or success.  |例句：He haplessly stumbled and fell into the mud. 他不幸地绊倒摔进了泥里。

例句：She haplessly watched her dreams crumble before her eyes. 她无助地看着她的梦想在她眼前破灭。


近义词：unluckily, unfortunately, unhappily, wretchedly

反义词：luckily, fortunately, happily, successfully

词性:副词
