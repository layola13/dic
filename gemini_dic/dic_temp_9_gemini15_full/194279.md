self-opener:/ˈsɛlf ˈoʊpənər/| n. |自动开启器，自开式装置 | A device or mechanism that opens automatically without manual intervention.  It can refer to various things, from a self-opening can to a self-opening umbrella. | Example: The new can of paint is a self-opener, making it much easier to use.  这个新油漆罐是自开式的，使用起来方便多了。The self-opener on the gate malfunctioned and we had to open it manually.  门的自动开启装置失灵了，我们不得不手动打开它。|近义词：automatic opener, self-activating device |反义词： manually operated device
