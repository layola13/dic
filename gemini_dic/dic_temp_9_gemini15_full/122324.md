mix-it:/mɪks ɪt/| v. |混合它，调配它 | To combine or blend ingredients or elements.  This is a phrasal verb, meaning it functions as a verb but includes a particle ("it").  It's informal and commonly used in instructions or recipes. | Example: Mix it well before serving.  混合好后再食用。|近义词：blend, combine, mingle |反义词：separate, divide


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mix it every morning. = 他们每天早上都混合它。
He mixes it with care. = 他小心地混合它。


一般过去时:
She mixed it quickly. = 她很快地混合了它。
We mixed it according to the recipe. = 我们按照食谱混合了它。


现在完成时:
I have mixed it twice already. = 我已经混合了两次了。
They have mixed it thoroughly. = 他们已经彻底混合了它。


过去完成时:
He had mixed it before adding the sugar. = 他在加糖之前已经混合好了。
She had mixed it improperly. = 她混合的不正确。


将来时:
I will mix it later. = 我一会儿再混合它。
They will mix it tomorrow. = 他们明天会混合它。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would mix it properly. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会把它好好混合。
I wish I had mixed it better. = 我希望我混合得更好。


被动语态:
It is mixed by the machine. = 它是由机器混合的。

疑问句:
Do you mix it with water? = 你是用水混合的吗？

否定句:

He didn't mix it correctly. = 他没有正确地混合它。
