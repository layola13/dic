empathising: /ˈɛmpəθaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 移情，感同身受 |  The act of understanding and sharing the feelings of another. It involves stepping into someone else's shoes and experiencing the world from their perspective.  | Example:  Empathising with the grieving family, she offered her condolences.  (她设身处地为悲伤的家庭着想，表达了慰问。)  The therapist emphasized the importance of empathising with her patients. (治疗师强调了与病人感同身受的重要性。) | 近义词:  sympathizing, understanding, relating to | 反义词:  being indifferent, being uncaring, being apathetic | 动词
