词源：

* **semi-**:  From Latin *semi*, meaning "half," "partly," or "partially."
* **satiric**: From "satire," ultimately derived from the Latin *satura*, meaning "a medley," "a mixed dish," later referring to a literary genre using humor, irony, exaggeration, or ridicule to expose and criticize folly or vice.
