crushers:/ˈkrʌʃərz/| n.|压碎机；粉碎机|Machines used to crush or pulverize materials, such as rocks, minerals, or other solid substances.|例句：The quarry uses powerful crushers to break down the rocks into smaller pieces.  采石场使用强大的压碎机将岩石破碎成更小的碎片。
例句：These crushers are designed to handle large volumes of material efficiently. 这些压碎机设计用于高效处理大量物料。|近义词：breakers, pulverizers, grinders|反义词：assemblers, constructors
