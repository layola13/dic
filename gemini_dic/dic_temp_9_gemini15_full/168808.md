product:/ˈprɒdʌkt/ /ˈprɑːdʌkt/ | n. | 产品，产物，结果 | A product is something that is made or grown to be sold.  It can also refer to the result of a process or action. | Example:  This factory produces many different products. 这个工厂生产许多不同的产品。

The new software is the product of years of research.  这个新软件是多年研究的成果。|近义词：item, commodity, output, result |反义词：input, raw material


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory produces many cars. = 工厂生产许多汽车。
This company sells a wide range of products. = 这家公司销售各种各样的产品。


一般过去时:
They produced a new type of phone last year. = 他们去年生产了一种新型手机。
The experiment produced unexpected results. = 实验产生了意想不到的结果。


现在完成时:
They have produced over 1000 units this month. = 这个月他们已经生产了超过1000个单位。
We have developed many successful products. = 我们已经开发了许多成功的产品。


过去完成时:
They had produced several prototypes before the final product was launched. = 在最终产品推出之前，他们已经生产了几个原型。
The company had already invested heavily in the product before it failed. = 在产品失败之前，公司已经对其进行了巨额投资。


将来时:
We will produce a new model next year. = 我们明年将生产一个新型号。
They will launch their new product next month. = 他们下个月将推出他们的新产品。


虚拟语气:
If they had better resources, they could produce a higher quality product. = 如果他们有更好的资源，他们就能生产出更高质量的产品。
I wish we had produced more products last quarter. = 我希望我们上季度生产更多产品。


被动语态:
Many products are made in China. = 许多产品在中国制造。
The product was designed by a team of engineers. = 该产品是由一个工程师团队设计的。


疑问句:
What products does this company manufacture? = 这家公司生产什么产品？
What is the final product of this process? = 这个过程的最终产品是什么？


否定句:
They did not produce enough products to meet demand. = 他们生产的产品不足以满足需求。
This company does not produce any environmentally harmful products. = 这家公司不生产任何对环境有害的产品。
