outread:/ˌaʊtˈriːd/| v. |大声朗读，超过…的阅读量|to read aloud; to surpass in reading|例句：The teacher asked the students to outread the passage.老师要求学生们大声朗读这段文章。 |例句：He outread all his classmates in the reading competition.他在阅读比赛中超过了所有同学。|近义词：read aloud, surpass|反义词：whisper, mumble

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outreads his brother every day. 他每天都比他弟弟读得多。
She outreads her classmates in the library.她在图书馆里比她的同学们阅读量大。

一般过去时:
He outread his brother yesterday. 他昨天比他弟弟读得多。
She outread all her classmates in the competition.她在比赛中超过了所有同学的阅读量。

现在完成时:
He has outread his brother this month. 这个月他比他弟弟读得多。
She has outread all her goals for the year.她已经超额完成了她今年的阅读目标。

过去完成时:
He had outread his brother by the end of the year.到年底他已经超过了他弟弟的阅读量。
She had outread everyone else before the contest concluded.比赛结束前，她已经超过了其他所有人。

将来时:
He will outread his brother by the end of the semester.到学期末他将超过他弟弟的阅读量。
She will outread him easily in the coming competition.在接下来的比赛中她将轻松地超过他。

虚拟语气:
If he studied harder, he would outread everyone.如果他学习更努力，他会超过所有人。
I wish I could outread all the books in the library.我希望我能读完图书馆里所有的书。


被动语态:
His reading speed was outread by his sister.他的阅读速度被他姐姐超过了。(This is less common and sounds unnatural)
The book was outread by many students last year.这本书去年被许多学生读完。


疑问句:
Does he outread his sister every day?他每天都比他姐姐读得多吗？
Can she outread all her classmates?她能超过所有同学的阅读量吗？


否定句:
He doesn't outread his brother.他不比他弟弟读得多。
She couldn't outread him in the last competition.在上次比赛中她没能超过他。


PHRASE->

outread one's peers 超过同龄人
