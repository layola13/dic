flota:/ˈfloʊtɑː/|/ˈflɒtə/| n. |舰队，船队 |A flota is a fleet of ships, especially a large group of warships or merchant ships sailing together.  |例句：The Spanish Armada was a powerful flota. 西班牙无敌舰队是一个强大的舰队。
The fishing flota returned with a bountiful catch. 捕鱼船队满载而归。|近义词：fleet, armada |反义词:single ship, individual vessel |名词
