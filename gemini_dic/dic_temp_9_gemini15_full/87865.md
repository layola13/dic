hides:/haɪdz/| v.|隐藏；遮盖；掩饰|To conceal or cover something from view; to keep something secret.|例句：The cat hides under the bed. 猫藏在床底下。

例句：He hides his true feelings. 他掩饰自己的真实感情。|近义词：conceal, cover, mask, secrete|反义词：reveal, expose, show


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hides his toys every day. 他每天都藏起他的玩具。
She hides her feelings well. 她很擅长隐藏自己的感情。


一般过去时:
The thief hid the money in a box. 小偷把钱藏在一个盒子里。
They hid from the rain under a tree. 他们在树下躲雨。


现在完成时:
He has hidden the evidence. 他已经藏匿了证据。
She has hidden her feelings for years. 她已经隐藏了自己的感情多年了。


过去完成时:
They had hidden the treasure before the storm. 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经藏好了宝藏。
He had hidden his disappointment well. 他已经很好地掩饰了自己的失望。


将来时:
I will hide the present until his birthday. 我会把礼物藏起来直到他的生日。
They will hide the truth from the police. 他们会向警方隐瞒真相。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would hide my valuables. 如果我是你，我会把我的贵重物品藏起来。
I wish I had hidden the letter. 我真希望我把信藏起来了。


被动语态:
The truth was hidden from the public. 真相被隐瞒了公众。
The clues were hidden in plain sight. 线索隐藏在显眼的地方。


疑问句:
Where did you hide the key? 你把钥匙藏在哪里了？
Does he hide his feelings from everyone? 他是否对所有人隐瞒了他的感受？


否定句:
I did not hide anything from the police. 我没有向警方隐瞒任何事情。
She doesn't hide her opinions. 她从不隐瞒自己的意见。


PHRASE->
