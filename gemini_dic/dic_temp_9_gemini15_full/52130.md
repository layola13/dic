dianthus:/daɪˈænθəs/| n. |石竹属植物；康乃馨| Dianthus is a genus of about 300 species of flowering plants in the family Caryophyllaceae.  They are native to Europe and Asia, and many are popular garden flowers.  The genus is characterized by its showy flowers, often with fringed petals.
