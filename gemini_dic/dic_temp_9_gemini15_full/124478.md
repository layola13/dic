mouldable: /ˈmoʊldəbəl/ | adj. | 可塑的；易于影响的 | Easily shaped or molded;  capable of being influenced or changed.  | 例句：The clay is very mouldable, perfect for sculpting.  这粘土非常易于塑形，非常适合雕刻。

The young minds are mouldable and receptive to new ideas. 年轻的心灵易于接受新思想。| 近义词：malleable, pliable, ductile, tractable | 反义词：inflexible, rigid, intractable, unyielding |

词源:
The word "mouldable" comes from the verb "to mould" (or "to mold" in American English), meaning to shape or form something.  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or possibility.  The ultimate origin traces back to Old English *mōldan, meaning "to shape, form".
