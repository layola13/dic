draining:/ˈdreɪnɪŋ/| v. adj. n.| 排水；耗尽；令人疲惫的；|  To remove liquid from something; to make something empty or dry;  to exhaust or deplete someone's energy or resources. |例句：The plumber is draining the bathtub. (水管工正在放掉浴缸里的水。)  The long journey was draining. (漫长的旅程令人精疲力尽。)近义词：emptying, exhausting, depleting, tiring|反义词：filling, replenishing, energizing|动词，形容词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sink is draining slowly. = 水槽排水很慢。
He is draining the swamp. = 他正在排干沼泽。

一般过去时:
She drained the pasta. = 她把意面里的水沥干了。
They drained the pool yesterday. = 他们昨天排空了游泳池。

现在完成时:
I have drained the battery of my phone. = 我已经耗尽了手机的电量。
We have drained the lake for maintenance. = 我们已经为了维护而排干了湖水。

过去完成时:
He had drained the tank before he left. = 他离开前已经排空了油箱。
They had drained their savings on the trip. = 他们把积蓄都花在了这次旅行上。

将来时:
We will drain the pool tomorrow. = 我们明天将排空游泳池。
They will drain the batteries soon. = 他们很快就会耗尽电池电量。


虚拟语气:
If I drained the tank, I could start the car. = 如果我把油箱排空，我就能启动汽车了。
If I had drained the swamp, the mosquitoes wouldn't have been so bad. = 如果我把沼泽排干，蚊子就不会那么多了。

被动语态:
The field was drained last week. = 这个田地是上周排干的。
The batteries are being drained quickly. = 电池正在快速耗尽。


疑问句:
Is the bathtub draining? = 浴缸在排水吗？
Are you feeling drained after work? = 下班后你感觉很累吗？


否定句:
The water isn't draining properly. = 水没有正常排水。
The work didn't drain me as much as I expected. = 这项工作并没有像我预期的那样让我感到疲惫。
