gaselier:/ˌɡæzəˈliər/| n. |枝形吊灯，气体灯具|A gaselier is a type of chandelier that uses gas, typically a decorative lighting fixture with multiple arms and lights.  It is often elaborate and ornate, and was popular during the 19th century.|例句：The grand ballroom was illuminated by a magnificent gaselier.  宏伟的舞厅被一个华丽的枝形吊灯照亮了。The antique gaselier cast a warm glow over the dining room. 古董枝形吊灯在餐厅投下温暖的光芒。|近义词：chandelier, gaslight fixture|反义词：table lamp, floor lamp|名词
