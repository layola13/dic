piece-work:/ˈpiːsˌwɜːrk/ | n. |计件工作；按件付酬的工作|Work paid for according to the number of units produced.|The seamstress earned a good living through piece-work.|近义词：task work, contract work|反义词：salary, wage


USAGE->

They perform piece-work to supplement their income. = 他们做计件工作来补充收入。
