rebottles:/ˌriːˈbɒtəlz/| vt.|重新装瓶；再次装瓶|To put something back into bottles.  This usually refers to liquids, such as drinks or sauces, that have been previously bottled.|例句：The factory rebottles returned bottles of soda. 工厂重新装瓶退回的苏打水瓶。

例句：We rebottled the leftover wine into smaller containers. 我们把剩下的葡萄酒重新装入较小的容器中。


近义词：repackage, refill


反义词：unbottle, empty


词性:动词
