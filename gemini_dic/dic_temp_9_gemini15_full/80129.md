gobsmack:/ˈɡɒbsmæk/| vt.|目瞪口呆；震惊；使大吃一惊 |To be extremely surprised or shocked; to leave someone speechless with astonishment.  |例句：I was gobsmacked when I heard the news. 我听到这个消息后惊呆了。
例句：The magician's trick gobsmacked the audience. 魔术师的戏法让观众大吃一惊。|近义词：astonish, amaze, astound, stun|反义词：bore, disappoint
