discolorate:/dɪsˈkʌləreɪt/| vt.|使变色，使褪色|To change the color of something, especially in an undesirable way.  The verb implies a change from a natural or original color to something less attractive or appealing.  It often suggests a fading, staining, or tarnishing process.|例句：The sun discolorated the curtains. 阳光使窗帘褪色了。

The acid rain discolorated the stone statues. 酸雨使石像褪色了。


近义词：discolor, stain, fade, tarnish, decolorize|反义词：brighten, color, enhance

虚拟语气:
