caregiving:/ˈkɛərɡɪvɪŋ/ | n. |照料；护理；照顾 | The act of providing care for someone who needs assistance, typically an elderly person, a child, or someone with a disability.  It encompasses a wide range of tasks, from personal care to emotional support. | Example:  The family is facing challenges with the intensive caregiving required for their grandmother's Alzheimer's disease.  家庭正面临着祖母阿尔茨海默病所需的密集护理带来的挑战。|近义词：care, nursing, tending, support |反义词：neglect, abandonment |名词
