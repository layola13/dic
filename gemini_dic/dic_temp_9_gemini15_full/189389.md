sagos:/ˈseɪɡoʊz/| n. |西米；西米淀粉|Starchy pith from the trunk of several tropical palm trees, used as food.  Sagos are a source of carbohydrates and are often used in desserts and other dishes.|Example: The dessert was made with sagos and coconut milk. 这道甜点是用西米和椰奶做的。|近义词：tapioca (similar starch)|反义词：无
