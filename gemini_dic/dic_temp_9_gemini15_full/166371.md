prereporting: /ˌpriːrɪˈpɔːrtɪŋ/ | n. | 预先报告；预先汇报 | The act of reporting something before it officially happens or becomes known.  It often involves providing advance notice or preliminary information. | Example:  Prereporting of potential safety hazards is crucial for preventing accidents. 预先报告潜在的安全隐患对于预防事故至关重要。 |近义词：advance reporting, preliminary reporting, early warning |反义词：post-reporting, retrospective reporting | 名词
