dispersing: /dɪˈspɜːrsɪŋ/ | v. | 散布；驱散；分散 |  The act of spreading something over a wide area; causing something to spread out and become less concentrated.  |

例句：

The wind is dispersing the clouds. = 风正在驱散云彩。

The police are dispersing the crowd. = 警察正在驱散人群。


近义词：scattering, spreading, diffusing, distributing

反义词：concentrating, gathering, assembling

词性：动词
