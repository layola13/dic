professor:/prəˈfɛsər/ | n. |教授，导师 | A professor is a teacher of the highest rank in a university or college.  They usually conduct research and publish scholarly work in addition to teaching. | Professor Smith is renowned for his research on ancient civilizations. 史密斯教授以其对古代文明的研究而闻名。 He is a professor of English literature. 他是英语文学教授。| lecturer, instructor, teacher | student, pupil |名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The professor teaches history.  教授教历史。
The professor lectures on Shakespeare. 教授讲授莎士比亚。


一般过去时:
The professor taught a fascinating class last semester. 上个学期教授讲授了一堂精彩的课程。
The professor lectured on the French Revolution. 教授讲授了法国大革命。


现在完成时:
The professor has published several books on the subject. 教授已经出版了几本关于这个主题的书。
The professor has taught at this university for twenty years. 教授在这所大学任教20年了。


过去完成时:
The professor had finished grading the exams before the students arrived. 学生到达之前，教授已经批改完试卷了。
The professor had lectured on this topic many times before. 教授之前已经多次讲授过这个主题。


将来时:
The professor will teach a new course next semester. 下学期教授将教授一门新课程。
The professor will be lecturing at the conference. 教授将在会议上作报告。


虚拟语气:
If the professor were here, he would answer your question. 如果教授在这里，他会回答你的问题。

The lecture was given by the professor.  讲座是由教授所作。
The research was conducted by the professor and his team. 这项研究是由教授和他的团队进行的。


疑问句:

否定句:
