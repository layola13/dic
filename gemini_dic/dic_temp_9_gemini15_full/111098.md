litchis:/ˈliːtʃiːz/| n.|荔枝|The word "litchis" is the plural form of "litchi," referring to the small, round fruit of the Litchi chinensis tree, known for its sweet, fragrant flesh.  It is native to Southeast Asia.|例句：I bought some delicious litchis at the market. 我在市场上买了一些美味的荔枝。  The litchis were so sweet and juicy. 这些荔枝又甜又多汁。|近义词：lychees|反义词:None.
