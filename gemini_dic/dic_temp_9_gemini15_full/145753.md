overcultivates:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌltɪveɪts/| vt.|过度耕作|To cultivate excessively, often leading to soil degradation and depletion of nutrients.  This practice can harm the land's long-term productivity and negatively impact the environment.|例句：The farmer overcultivates his fields, leading to soil erosion and reduced crop yields.  农民过度耕作他的田地，导致水土流失和作物减产。

The continuous overcultivation of the land has resulted in a significant decline in soil fertility.  土地持续过度耕作导致土壤肥力显著下降。


近义词：overfarm, exhaust, deplete|反义词：fallow, conserve,  rotate crops|动词
