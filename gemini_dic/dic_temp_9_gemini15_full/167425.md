prevails:/prɪˈveɪlz/| v.|流行，盛行；获胜，占优势|to be generally accepted or practiced; to triumph or be victorious|例句：That view prevails among young people.  这种观点在年轻人中很流行。|例句：Justice will prevail in the end. 公正最终会战胜一切。|近义词：dominates, predominates, triumphs|反义词：fails, loses


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The custom prevails in many cultures. = 这种习俗在许多文化中盛行。
His opinion prevails in this matter. = 在这件事上，他的意见占主导地位。


一般过去时:
That fashion prevailed in the 1920s. = 那种时尚在20世纪20年代盛行。
The Allied forces prevailed in World War II. = 盟军在二战中获胜。


现在完成时:
This idea has prevailed for centuries. = 这个想法已经流行了几个世纪。
Despite the setbacks, their determination has prevailed. = 尽管遭遇挫折，他们的决心最终战胜了一切。


过去完成时:
Before the reforms, a different system had prevailed. = 改革之前，实行的是另一种制度。
The old beliefs had prevailed until the scientific revolution.  在科学革命之前，旧的信仰一直占主导地位。


将来时:
Hopefully, peace will prevail in the future. = 但愿和平将来会到来。
I believe that reason will prevail. = 我相信理性最终会获胜。


虚拟语气:
If justice prevailed, everyone would be treated fairly. = 如果公正能够实现，每个人都会得到公平对待。
I wish understanding would prevail over hatred. = 我希望理解能够战胜仇恨。


被动语态:
The opinion was prevailed upon by the majority. =  大多数人的意见占了上风。(此用法较少见，通常用主动语态)
The decision was not easily prevailed upon. = 这个决定不容易被改变。(此用法较少见，通常用主动语态)


疑问句:
Does that idea still prevail in your country? = 那个想法在你们国家仍然流行吗？
Will fairness prevail in this case? = 在此案中，公平会获胜吗？


否定句:
His arguments did not prevail. = 他的论点没有说服力。
The old ways of thinking no longer prevail. = 旧的思维方式不再流行。


PHRASE->
prevail upon/on = 说服；劝说
prevail against = 战胜；克服
prevail over = 战胜；胜过
prevail with = 对...有效；对...有影响
to prevail oneself of = 利用；使用
