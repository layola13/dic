overdepressed: /ˌoʊvər dɪˈprɛst/ | adj. | 极度沮丧的，过度抑郁的 | Extremely depressed; suffering from a severe or prolonged state of depression.  This word isn't a standard dictionary entry; it's a combination of "over-" (meaning excessively) and "depressed." | 例句：He felt overdepressed after losing his job and his girlfriend in the same week. 他在同一周失去工作和女朋友后感到极度沮丧。


例句：She became overdepressed after the death of her pet. 她宠物死后变得极度沮丧。


近义词：extremely depressed, profoundly sad, deeply despondent, severely melancholic


反义词：ecstatic, elated, joyful, cheerful


词性：形容词
