resonating: /ˈrezəneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 共鸣，引起共鸣；回响 |  Producing a strong feeling of sympathy or agreement; echoing or reverberating.  |


例句：

His speech resonated with the audience. = 他的演讲引起了听众的共鸣。

The singer's voice resonated throughout the hall. = 歌手的歌声在整个大厅回响。


近义词：echoing, reverberating, vibrating, striking a chord with


反义词：failing to connect with, leaving indifferent


USAGE->

The music resonates with the listeners. = 音乐与听众产生共鸣。
Her ideas resonate deeply with many people. = 她的想法与许多人产生深刻的共鸣。
