quizmasters:/ˈkwɪzmɑːstərz/| n. | 测验主持人；问答比赛主持人 | People who host or present quiz shows or competitions. They typically ask questions and judge answers.  | Example: The quizmasters kept the energy high throughout the competition.  测验主持人让比赛始终保持高昂的热情。|近义词：question masters, quiz hosts |反义词：contestants, players
