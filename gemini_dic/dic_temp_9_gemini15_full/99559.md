interhybridize:/ˌɪntərˈhaɪbrɪdaɪz/| vt.| 杂交；种间杂交 | To hybridize (plants or animals of different species or varieties) with each other.  The process involves combining the genetic material of two different species or varieties to create offspring with traits from both parents. |例句：Researchers are attempting to interhybridize different strains of rice to produce a more drought-resistant crop.  研究人员正在尝试杂交不同品种的水稻，以培育出更耐旱的作物。

例句：The interhybridization of these two bird species resulted in offspring with unique plumage. 这两种鸟类的杂交产生了具有独特羽毛的后代。


近义词：crossbreed, hybridize

反义词：inbreed (purebreed)


词性：动词
