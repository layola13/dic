nondependences:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈpɛndənsiz/ | n. |非依赖性，相互独立性 | The lack of dependence or reliance on something or someone else.  Nondependences in software design refer to the ability of modules to function independently of each other.  This promotes modularity, reusability, and easier maintenance. |Example: The project emphasizes nondependences between different teams to improve efficiency and avoid bottlenecks.  这个项目强调不同团队之间的相互独立性，以提高效率并避免瓶颈。


Example:  The design of the system promotes nondependences between its various components, making it easier to update individual parts without affecting others. 系统的设计促进了其各个组件之间的相互独立性，使得更容易更新各个部分而不会影响其他部分。


近义词: independence, autonomy, self-sufficiency


反义词: dependence, reliance, interdependency


词性: noun
