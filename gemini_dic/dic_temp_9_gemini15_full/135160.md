nonoperating:/ˌnɑːnˈɑːpəreɪtɪŋ/| adj. |非经营性的，非营业的 |Not related to the primary business operations of a company.  It refers to activities or income that are not directly involved in the core business functions. |Example: Nonoperating income includes interest earned on investments and gains from asset sales.|近义词：incidental, ancillary, extraneous |反义词：operating, core
