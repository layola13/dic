redelete:/ˌriːdɪˈliːt/|v.|重新删除|To delete something again, often after a previous deletion attempt has failed or been undone.  It implies a more thorough or repeated deletion process.|例句：I had to redelete the file because the first attempt didn't work. 我不得不重新删除该文件，因为第一次尝试没有成功。


例句：The system administrator had to redelete the corrupted data to ensure system stability. 系统管理员不得不重新删除损坏的数据以确保系统稳定性。|近义词：delete again, remove completely, purge|反义词：restore, recover, reinstate|动词
