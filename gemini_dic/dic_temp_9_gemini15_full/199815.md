sisters:/ˈsɪstərz/| n.|姐妹们，女儿们|The word "sisters" refers to female siblings, or, in a figurative sense, women who are closely connected or share a common cause.  It can also refer to nuns belonging to a particular order.|例句：My sisters and I are very close. 我和我的姐妹们关系非常亲密。

例句：The sisters of charity work tirelessly to help the poor.  慈爱修女们不知疲倦地帮助穷人。|近义词：female siblings, kindred spirits|反义词：brothers
