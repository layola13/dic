halter:/ˈhɔːltər/| n.| 缰绳；套索；马勒；(女式)细带紧身衣 | A halter is a rope or strap used to lead or control an animal, especially a horse. It can also refer to a type of sleeveless garment that ties around the neck. |例句：The cowboy used a halter to control his horse.  牛仔用缰绳控制他的马。

The halter top showed off her shoulders beautifully. 这件细带紧身衣衬托出她美丽的肩膀。|近义词：bridle, reins, halter top|反义词：leash(for smaller animals)
