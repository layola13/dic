run-up:/ˈrʌnʌp/| n. |准备阶段；预备时期；增长；上升|The period of time leading up to an important event.  It can also refer to an increase in something, such as prices or activity.|例句：The run-up to the election was filled with heated debates.  选举前的准备阶段充满了激烈的辩论。

例句：There was a significant run-up in house prices last year. 去年房价大幅上涨。|近义词：preparation, buildup, increase, rise|反义词：decline, decrease, downfall


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The run-up to the festival is always exciting. = 节日的准备阶段总是令人兴奋。
The company is experiencing a run-up in sales. = 公司正在经历销售额的增长。


一般过去时:
The run-up to the concert was chaotic. = 音乐会的准备阶段很混乱。
There was a sharp run-up in inflation last month. = 上个月通货膨胀急剧上升。


现在完成时:
There has been a significant run-up in oil prices this year. = 今年油价已经大幅上涨。
We have completed all the preparations in the run-up to the project. = 我们已经完成了项目准备阶段的所有准备工作。


过去完成时:
By the run-up to the deadline, they had finished most of the work. = 在截止日期前的准备阶段，他们已经完成了大部分工作。
The run-up to the war had seen increased tensions between the countries. = 在战争爆发前的准备阶段，各国之间的紧张关系加剧了。


将来时:
The run-up to the Olympics will be very busy. = 奥运会的准备阶段将会非常忙碌。
We anticipate a run-up in demand for our product next quarter. = 我们预计下个季度对我们产品的需求将会增长。


虚拟语气:
If there were a longer run-up to the project, we could have done a better job. = 如果项目有更长的准备阶段，我们本来可以做得更好。
I wish there had been a smoother run-up to the launch. = 我希望发布前的准备阶段更顺利一些。


被动语态:
The preparations in the run-up to the event were meticulously planned. = 活动前的准备工作是精心策划的。
The increase in sales was attributed to the successful marketing run-up. = 销售额的增长归功于成功的市场推广准备阶段。


疑问句:
What was the run-up to the crisis like? = 危机的准备阶段是什么样的？
How long is the run-up to the next election? = 下一次选举的准备阶段有多长？


否定句:
There wasn't a significant run-up in share prices before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，股价并没有大幅上涨。
The team didn't have enough time in the run-up to the competition. = 团队在比赛前的准备阶段没有足够的时间。


PHRASE->
in the run-up to = 在...之前
the run-up to the election = 选举前的准备阶段
a run-up in prices = 价格上涨
a significant run-up = 大幅增长
a steady run-up = 稳定增长
