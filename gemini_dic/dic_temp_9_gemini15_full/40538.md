controversial:/ˌkɒntrəˈvɜːʃəl/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈvɜːrʃəl/ | adj. |有争议的，引起争议的 | causing a lot of disagreement or argument  | The politician's proposal was highly controversial, sparking heated debates among the public.  这位政治家的提案极具争议，引发了公众的激烈辩论。| debatable, disputed, contentious | uncontroversial, agreeable, undisputed |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new law is controversial. = 这项新法律是有争议的。
His opinions are always controversial. = 他的观点总是引起争议。


一般过去时:
The film was highly controversial when it was released. = 这部电影上映时极具争议。
The decision proved to be controversial. = 这个决定证明是有争议的。


现在完成时:
The issue has remained controversial for years. = 这个问题多年来一直备受争议。
The book has become increasingly controversial. = 这本书变得越来越有争议。


过去完成时:
The plan had already been controversial before the meeting. = 在会议之前，这个计划就已经饱受争议了。
The decision had proved controversial even before it was announced. = 这个决定甚至在宣布之前就已经引起了争议。


将来时:
The new policy will likely be controversial. = 新政策可能会引起争议。
The upcoming election will be very controversial. = 即将到来的选举将非常有争议。


虚拟语气:
If the proposal were less controversial, it would pass easily. = 如果提案不那么有争议，它很容易通过。
I wish the topic weren't so controversial. = 我希望这个话题不要那么有争议。


被动语态:
The policy was deemed controversial by many. = 许多人认为这项政策是有争议的。
The new technology is being described as controversial by some experts. = 一些专家将这项新技术描述为有争议的。


疑问句:
Is the new tax law controversial? = 新的税法有争议吗？
Are his ideas really so controversial? = 他的想法真的那么有争议吗？


否定句:
The decision wasn't controversial at all. = 这个决定一点也不具有争议性。
The book is not particularly controversial. = 这本书并没有特别有争议。


PHRASE->
