pric: /prɪs/ | n. | 价格，价钱 | The price of the house is too high. 这房子的价格太高了。|近义词：cost, value, charge | 反义词：free, worthless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price of gas is high. =  汽油价格很高。
What is the price of this book? = 这本书多少钱？


一般过去时:
The price of gold was lower last year. = 去年黄金价格较低。
What was the price of that car? = 那辆车多少钱？


现在完成时:
The price of oil has risen sharply. = 油价已大幅上涨。
How much has the price increased? = 价格上涨了多少？


过去完成时:
The price had already fallen before we bought it. = 我们买之前价格已经下跌了。
The price had been higher than we expected. = 价格比我们预期的要高。


将来时:
The price will likely increase next year. = 明年价格可能会上涨。
What will the price be tomorrow? = 明天的价格是多少？


虚拟语气:
If the price were lower, I would buy it. = 如果价格低一些，我会买它。
I wish the price was cheaper. = 我希望价格更便宜。


被动语态:
The price is determined by supply and demand. = 价格由供求关系决定。
The price was set by the government. = 价格由政府设定。


疑问句:
What is the price? = 价格是多少？
How much is the price? = 价格是多少？


否定句:
The price is not reasonable. = 价格不合理。
The price isn't affordable for me. = 这个价格我买不起。



PHRASE->
price tag = 价格标签
price war = 价格战
fixed price = 固定价格
list price = 标价
competitive price = 竞争价格
