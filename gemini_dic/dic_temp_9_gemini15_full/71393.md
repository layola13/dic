flagrant:/ˈfleɪɡrənt/| adj.| 臭名昭著的；公然的；明目张胆的|  Clearly or obviously wrong or illegal; scandalous.  It implies a shocking disregard for rules or morality. | 例句：His flagrant disregard for the law led to his arrest.  他的公然违法导致了他的逮捕。


例句：The judge criticized the flagrant abuse of power by the police officer.  法官批评了警官公然滥用职权的行为。


近义词：blatant, obvious, glaring, scandalous, notorious


反义词：subtle, discreet, hidden, concealed


词性:形容词
