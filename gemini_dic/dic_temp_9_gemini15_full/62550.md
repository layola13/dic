enemies:/ˈɛnəmiz/ | n. |敌人，仇敌 |People who are hostile or unfriendly towards another or others.  They may be individuals, groups, or even nations engaged in conflict or opposition.|例句：He made many enemies during his career. 他在他的职业生涯中树敌无数。|例句：The two countries were enemies for many years. 这两个国家多年来一直是敌人。|近义词：foes, adversaries, opponents |反义词：friends, allies

一般过去时:
