fogeyisms:/ˈfoʊɡiˌɪzəm/| n. |守旧观念；老朽的思想；老掉牙的说法 |Old-fashioned or outdated ideas, opinions, or expressions.  Fogeyisms often reflect a resistance to change and a clinging to traditional ways of thinking. They can be amusing, irritating, or even offensive depending on the context. | His speech was filled with fogeyisms about the importance of hard work and traditional values.  他的演讲充满了关于努力工作和传统价值观重要性的老朽观念。


|近义词：old-fashioned ideas, outdated notions, anachronisms, traditionalisms |反义词：modern ideas, progressive thinking, avant-garde concepts |名词
