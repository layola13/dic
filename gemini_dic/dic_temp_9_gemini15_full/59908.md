edematose:/ˈɛdəmətoʊs/| adj.|水肿的，浮肿的|Affected by or characterized by edema (an abnormal accumulation of fluid in the intercellular tissue spaces of the body).  The condition often presents as swelling. |例句：The patient presented with edematous legs and ankles.  病人出现腿踝水肿。

例句：His edematose face indicated a serious underlying condition. 他浮肿的脸表明存在严重的潜在疾病。


近义词：swollen, puffy, turgid

反义词：dehydrated, desiccated


词源:  From  edema (itself from Greek οἴδημα (oídēma) "swelling") + -ose (suffix indicating "full of" or "characterized by").
