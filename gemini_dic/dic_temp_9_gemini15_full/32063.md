cherub:/ˈtʃɛrəb/| n. |小天使，胖乎乎的孩子|A cherub is a winged, usually chubby, baby-like figure often depicted in art as an attendant of God.  The word is also used to describe a beautiful, innocent-looking child.|例句：The painting depicts cherubs playing among the clouds. 这幅画描绘了在云层中玩耍的小天使。|例句：Her cherubic face lit up with joy. 她胖乎乎的脸上充满了喜悦。|近义词：angel, cupid, infant|反义词：demon, devil
