implantation:/ˌɪmplænˈteɪʃən/| n. |植入；着床；嵌入|The process of implanting something, especially a fertilized ovum in the uterus or an artificial device in the body.  It refers to the process by which a fertilized egg attaches to the uterine wall and begins to develop.  This is crucial for pregnancy. In other contexts, it can refer to the insertion of a medical device or other object. |例句：The successful implantation of the embryo is crucial for a healthy pregnancy.  成功的胚胎着床对健康的怀孕至关重要。

The dentist performed an implantation of a dental implant. 牙医进行了牙齿种植手术。|近义词：insertion, embedding, engrafting|反义词：removal, extraction, expulsion|名词
