jacobite:/ˈdʒeɪkəbaɪt/| n. adj.|雅各布派支持者；拥护斯图亚特王朝的；|A Jacobite is a supporter of the Jacobite cause, which aimed to restore the House of Stuart to the British throne after the Glorious Revolution of 1688.  The term often refers to supporters of James II and his descendants. |Example:  Many Jacobites fought in the Jacobite risings. 许多雅各布派成员参加了雅各布派起义。The Jacobite cause was ultimately unsuccessful. 雅各布派事业最终失败了。|近义词：Stuart supporter, royalist|反义词：Whig, Hanoverian|名词，形容词
