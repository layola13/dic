nonsufferable:/ˈnɑːnsʌfərəbl/ | adj. |难以忍受的，无法容忍的 |  Intolerable; impossible to bear.  The adjective "nonsufferable" describes something or someone that is extremely unpleasant and difficult to endure. It implies a high degree of annoyance or displeasure. |例句：His constant complaining was nonsufferable.  他不断的抱怨令人难以忍受。

His arrogance made him nonsufferable to his colleagues. 他的傲慢使他同事无法忍受。|近义词：intolerable, unbearable, unendurable, insufferable |反义词：bearable, tolerable, endurable
