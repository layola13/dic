decommissioning:/ˌdiːkəˈmɪʃənɪŋ/| n. |退役；除役；报废；停止使用|The process of taking out of service a facility, system, or piece of equipment.  This often involves dismantling, disposal, and environmental remediation. |例句：The decommissioning of the nuclear power plant will take several years. 核电站的退役将需要数年时间。

例句：The decommissioning of the old oil rig was a complex and costly undertaking.  旧油井平台的退役是一项复杂且昂贵的任务。


近义词：dismantling, retirement, phasing out, closure|反义词：commissioning, activation, startup|词性：名词
