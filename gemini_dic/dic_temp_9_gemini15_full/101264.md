involved:/ɪnˈvɒlvd/ | adj. | 涉及到的；卷入的；复杂的 |  Engaged or participating in something; complicated or intricate. | 例句：He was involved in the accident. 他卷入了那场事故。

He is involved in a complex legal battle. 他卷入了一场复杂的法律纠纷。


近义词： implicated, embroiled, complicated, complex | 反义词： uninvolved, detached, simple, straightforward | 词性：形容词


involved:/ɪnˈvɒlv/ | v. | 使卷入；包含；涉及 | To include or contain (something) as a part; to connect or associate (someone) in something. | 例句：The project involved a lot of hard work.  这个项目需要很多努力。

The scandal involved several prominent politicians. 这场丑闻牵涉到几位著名的政治家。


近义词： include, encompass, engage, implicate | 反义词： exclude, omit, detach, disengage | 词性：动词
