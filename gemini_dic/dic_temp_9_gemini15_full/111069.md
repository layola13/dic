lister:/ˈlɪstər/| n. |李斯特（姓氏）; 清洁工，消毒剂制造商 |A Lister is a person with the surname Lister.  It can also refer to someone who cleans or disinfects, or a manufacturer of disinfectants, especially in reference to Joseph Lister's pioneering work in antiseptic surgery.  |例句：Many famous people have the surname Lister. 许多名人姓李斯特。

The Lister company produces high-quality disinfectants. 李斯特公司生产高质量的消毒剂。|近义词：cleaner, disinfector, sanitizer|反义词：polluter (in the context of a disinfector)
