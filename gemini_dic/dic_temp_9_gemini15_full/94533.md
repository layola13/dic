impositions:/ˌɪmpəˈzɪʃənz/| n.|强加；负担；义务|The act of imposing something, especially a burden, tax, or duty, on someone.  It can refer to something imposed unfairly or unwelcomely.|例句：The new regulations felt like an imposition on our freedom. 新规章让我们感觉自由受到了限制。

例句：The extra work was a considerable imposition on her already busy schedule.额外的工作对她本来就很忙碌的日程造成了相当大的负担。


近义词：burden, obligation, requirement, constraint

反义词：relief, freedom, liberation


词性:名词
