rewagered:/riːˈweɪdʒərd/| v. |再次下注，重新下赌注 |To place a bet again; to re-engage in gambling.  This word is not common and is formed by adding the prefix "re-" to "wagered," the past participle of "wager."|例句：He rewagered his winnings on the next race. 他把赢来的钱又押在了下一场比赛上。

He rewagered after losing his initial bet, hoping for a comeback. 他在输掉第一注后又重新下注，希望能翻盘。


近义词：rebet, restake,  re-gamble | 反义词：cash out, quit gambling

词性: 动词
