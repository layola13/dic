largos:/ˈlɑːrɡoʊz/| n.|慢板（音乐术语）|Largo is a musical tempo marking indicating a slow, broad, and stately tempo.  It is often used for expressive and majestic pieces.|例句：The adagio movement was followed by a stately largo.|近义词：adagio, grave|反义词：presto, allegro

USAGE->
This word is primarily a musical term and doesn't have typical verb conjugations or other grammatical usages like "what".  Therefore, standard tense conjugations (present, past, future, etc.) and passive/active voice are not applicable.  The word functions as a noun.


PHRASE->
Largo maestoso:  A majestic and slow tempo.
Largo cantabile: A slow and singing tempo.
Largo espressivo: A slow and expressive tempo.
Tempo largo:  Simply indicating the tempo is largo.
Play it largo: An instruction to perform a piece of music at a largo tempo.
