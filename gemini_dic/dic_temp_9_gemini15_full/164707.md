preextracting: /ˌpriːɪkˈstræktɪŋ/ | v. | 预先提取；提前提取 |  The act of extracting something before it is normally extracted.  This is a newly coined word, not found in standard dictionaries, formed by combining "pre-" (before) and "extracting". It implies taking something out earlier than usual or expected. | 例句：The company implemented a preextracting process to optimize their workflow. 公司实施了预先提取流程以优化其工作流程。  The researchers conducted preextracting of data to speed up the analysis. 研究人员进行了数据预先提取以加快分析速度。 | 近义词：pre-removal, advance extraction, prior extraction | 反义词：post-extraction, late extraction
