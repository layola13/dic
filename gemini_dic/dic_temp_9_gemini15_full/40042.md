consummates:/ˈkɒnsəmeɪts/| /ˈkɑːnsəmeɪts/| vt.|完成；使完美；达到顶点；完婚|To complete or make perfect.  To bring to a state of completion or perfection.  Also, to celebrate a marriage by sexual intercourse.|例句：The deal was consummated with a handshake. 这笔交易以握手的方式完成。

例句：Their love was consummated on their wedding night. 他们的爱情在他们的新婚之夜达到高潮（性关系）。


近义词：complete, perfect, finalize, achieve, fulfill, climax

反义词：ruin, destroy, spoil, incomplete, imperfect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He consummates the deal every year. 他每年都完成这笔交易。
She consummates her work with precision. 她以精确的态度完成她的工作。

一般过去时:
They consummated their marriage last month. 他们上个月完婚了。
He consummated the sale of his house. 他完成了他的房屋出售。

现在完成时:
She has consummated her plans for the business. 她已经完成了她的商业计划。
They have consummated their relationship. 他们已经完成了他们的关系（通常指性关系）。

过去完成时:
He had consummated the agreement before the deadline. 他在截止日期前完成了协议。
She had consummated her revenge before he could escape.  在她逃跑之前，她已经报了仇。

将来时:
They will consummate their marriage in the spring. 他们将在春天完婚。
He will consummate his ambition with hard work. 他将通过努力工作来实现他的雄心壮志。

虚拟语气:
If he had worked harder, he would have consummated his project on time. 如果他更努力工作，他就能按时完成他的项目。
If she had been more decisive, she would have consummated the sale. 如果她更果断一些，她就能完成这笔交易。

被动语态:
The deal was consummated quickly and efficiently. 这笔交易迅速而高效地完成了。

疑问句:
