highway:/ˈhaɪweɪ/| n. |高速公路，公路 |A highway is a main road, especially one connecting towns or cities.  It is typically designed for high-speed traffic and often has multiple lanes. |例句：We drove on the highway for hours. 我们在高速公路上开了好几个小时。 |例句：The new highway will improve transportation in the region. 新建的高速公路将改善该地区的交通状况。|近义词：expressway, freeway, motorway, thoroughfare |反义词：back road, byway, lane
