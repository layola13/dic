noncomposer:/ˌnɒnˈkɒmˈpəʊzər/| n. |非作曲家 |A person who does not compose music.  Someone who is not a composer. | Example:  He's a talented musician, but a noncomposer. 他是一位才华横溢的音乐家，但不是作曲家。|近义词：non-composer, amateur musician (if referring to someone who plays but doesn't compose)|反义词：composer|名词
