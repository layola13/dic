recreate:/ˌriːkriˈeɪt/ | vt. |再创造；重新创造；重现；娱乐；消遣 |To make or do something again, or to create something similar to something that already exists.  It can also refer to  reliving past experiences or creating a feeling of something from the past.|例句：The museum aims to recreate the atmosphere of a Victorian street.  博物馆旨在重现维多利亚时期街道的氛围。|例句：We recreated the scene from the movie using LEGOs. 我们用乐高积木重现了电影中的场景。|近义词：reproduce, reconstruct, revive, re-enact |反义词：destroy, demolish, obliterate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They recreate historical events for their documentaries. = 他们为纪录片重现历史事件。
She recreates delicious meals from her grandmother's recipes. = 她根据祖母的食谱重新制作美味佳肴。

一般过去时:
The artist recreated the famous painting. = 艺术家复制了这幅名画。
He recreated his childhood memories in his autobiography. = 他在他的自传中重现了他的童年记忆。

现在完成时:
They have recreated the lost city with amazing accuracy. = 他们以惊人的精确度重建了失落的城市。
She has recreated the feeling of a summer evening in her painting. = 她用画笔重现了夏夜的感觉。

过去完成时:
The archaeologists had recreated the ancient civilization's way of life. = 考古学家们已经复原了古代文明的生活方式。
Before the presentation, they had recreated the crucial data analysis. = 在演示之前，他们已经重新进行了关键的数据分析。

将来时:
The team will recreate the experiment to verify the results. = 该团队将重现实验以验证结果。
We will recreate the magical atmosphere of the festival next year. = 我们明年将重现节日的神奇氛围。

虚拟语气:
If I had the resources, I would recreate the entire garden. = 如果我有资源，我会重建整个花园。
It would be wonderful if we could recreate that special moment. = 如果我们能重现那一刻，那将是多么美好。

被动语态:
The historical scene was recreated using computer graphics. = 历史场景是用电脑图形重现的。
The lost manuscript is being recreated by a team of scholars. = 一组学者正在重建失落的稿件。

疑问句:
Can we recreate the original conditions of the experiment? = 我们能重现实验的原始条件吗？
How can we recreate the success of last year's project? = 我们如何才能重现去年项目的成功？

否定句:
They couldn't recreate the exact flavor of the original recipe. = 他们无法复制原始食谱的确切味道。
He did not recreate the painting accurately. = 他没有准确地复制这幅画。
