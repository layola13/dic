renascency:/rɪˈnæsənsi/ | n. |再生；复兴；复活 | The rebirth or revival of something, especially of art, learning, or culture.  It often refers to a period of renewed vigor and creativity following a period of decline. |Example: The city experienced a renascency after the devastating earthquake, with new buildings and thriving businesses.  这座城市在地震后经历了复兴，出现了新的建筑和蓬勃发展的企业。|近义词：revival, resurgence, regeneration |反义词：decline, decay, degeneration |名词
