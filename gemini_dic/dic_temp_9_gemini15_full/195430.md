seminervously:/ˌsɛmiˈnɜːrvəsli/| adv.|半紧张地，有点紧张地|Slightly nervously; halfway between nervous and calm.  It combines "semi-" (half, partly) with "nervously."|例句：He spoke seminervously, his voice trembling slightly. 他有点紧张地说话，声音微微颤抖。

例句：She approached the stage seminervously, but once she started playing, her confidence grew. 她有点紧张地走上舞台，但一旦开始演奏，她的自信心就增强了。


近义词：nervously, anxiously, uneasily, hesitantly

反义词：calmly, confidently, assuredly

词性:副词
