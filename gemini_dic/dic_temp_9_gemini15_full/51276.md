desynchronized:/ˌdiːˈsɪŋkrənaɪzd/| adj.|不同步的，不同期的|Out of synchronization; not occurring at the same time or rate.  The term often refers to a disruption or lack of coordination between normally synchronized systems or processes.|例句：The two clocks are desynchronized; one is five minutes ahead of the other. 这两块钟表不同步；一个快了五分钟。
The dancers' movements were desynchronized, resulting in a chaotic performance. 舞蹈演员的动作不同步，导致演出混乱。|近义词：out of sync, uncoordinated, asynchronous|反义词：synchronized, coordinated, synchronous|
