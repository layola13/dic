overtrained:/ˌoʊvərˈtreɪnd/| adj.|过度训练的，训练过度的|  Describes a person or animal whose training has been excessive, leading to negative consequences such as fatigue, injury, or decreased performance.  This condition often arises from pushing oneself too hard without sufficient rest or recovery. |例句：The athlete was overtrained and had to withdraw from the competition. 运动员训练过度，不得不退出比赛。

例句：He felt exhausted and sluggish; clear signs that he was overtrained. 他感到筋疲力尽且行动迟缓；这些都是他训练过度的明显迹象。


近义词：exhausted, fatigued, burned out, worn out

反义词：undertrained, rested, recovered

词性：形容词
