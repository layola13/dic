marc:/mɑːrk/| n. |马克（男子名）；商标；分数；痕迹|A Marc is a masculine given name, often a short form of Marcellus.  It can also refer to a trademark or a mark or score. |例句：My name is Marc. = 我的名字是马克。  The company has registered its logo as a Marc. = 公司已将其标志注册为商标。|近义词：Mark (name), brand, trace|反义词：N/A

The brand marks the quality of the product. = 品牌标志着产品的质量。
