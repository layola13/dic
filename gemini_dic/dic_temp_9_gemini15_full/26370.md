by-hook-or-crook:/baɪ hʊk ɔːr krʊk/ |adv.|不择手段，无论如何 |By any means necessary, regardless of legality or morality.  It emphasizes the determination to achieve a goal, even if it requires unconventional or dishonest methods.|例句：He'll get what he wants by hook or by crook. 他会不择手段地得到他想要的东西。|近义词：at all costs, at any price, by any means necessary |反义词：honestly, ethically, legitimately
