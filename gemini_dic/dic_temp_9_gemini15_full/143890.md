outpost:/ˈaʊtpəʊst/ | n. |前哨；前哨站；偏远地区 | A remote military or trading station, typically one situated in difficult or dangerous territory.  An outpost is a small settlement or base located far from a main area of population or activity, often established for military, trading, or exploratory purposes.  |例句：The soldiers manned a lonely outpost in the desert.  士兵们驻守着沙漠中一个偏僻的前哨站。

The trading outpost was attacked by bandits.  这个贸易前哨站遭到土匪袭击。


近义词：station, base, settlement, station, frontier post | 反义词：center, metropolis, capital
