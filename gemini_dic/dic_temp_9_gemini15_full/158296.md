playfellow:/ˈpleɪˌfeloʊ/| n. |玩伴，游戏伙伴 |A playfellow is a child who plays with other children.  It often implies a close friendship built around shared play activities.|例句：He and his playfellow built a magnificent sandcastle on the beach. 他和他的玩伴在沙滩上建了一座宏伟的沙堡。|例句：My childhood playfellow and I still keep in touch. 我童年的玩伴和我至今仍然保持联系。|近义词：companion, friend, chum|反义词：enemy, rival


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is my playfellow. = 他是我的玩伴。
They are always playfellows. = 他们总是玩伴。

一般过去时:
She was his playfellow in kindergarten. = 她是他幼儿园的玩伴。
We were playfellows for many years. = 我们是多年的玩伴。

现在完成时:
I have been her playfellow since we were little. = 从小我就一直是她的玩伴。
They have been best playfellows throughout their childhood. = 他们从小就是最好的玩伴。

过去完成时:
He had been my playfellow before we moved away. = 在我们搬家之前，他一直是我的玩伴。
They had been playfellows for five years before the argument. = 争吵之前，他们已经做了五年的玩伴。

将来时:
She will be my playfellow at the summer camp. = 她将在夏令营里成为我的玩伴。
We will be playfellows for many years to come. = 我们将来还会做很多年的玩伴。

虚拟语气:
If only I had a playfellow like him. = 要是我有个像他一样的玩伴就好了。
I wish I had had a playfellow when I was younger. = 我真希望我年轻的时候有个玩伴。

被动语态:
(This word is rarely used passively)  The children were often seen as playfellows by their parents. = 孩子们经常被父母视为玩伴。（较少用）
He was considered a good playfellow by everyone. = 他被大家认为是个好玩伴。（较少用）

疑问句:
Who is your playfellow? = 谁是你的玩伴？
Were they playfellows in school? = 他们在学校是玩伴吗？

否定句:
He wasn't my playfellow. = 他不是我的玩伴。
She was never his playfellow. = 她从来都不是他的玩伴。
