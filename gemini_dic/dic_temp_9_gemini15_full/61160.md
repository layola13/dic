elsewhither:/ˌɛlsˈhwɛðər/| adv.|到别处；向别处|The word "elsewhither" is an archaic adverb meaning "to another place" or "somewhere else."  It's rarely used in modern English.  It combines "else" (meaning "other" or "additional") and "whither" (an archaic word meaning "to where" or "where to").|例句：He went elsewhither to seek his fortune. 他到别处去寻找他的财富。


They wandered elsewhither, seeking shelter from the storm. 他们四处流浪，寻找避风的场所。|近义词：elsewhere, somewhere else, to another place|反义词：here, nowhere, this place|词性:副词
