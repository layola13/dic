reviler:/rɪˈvaɪlər/ | n. |辱骂者，斥责者 | A person who reviles; someone who uses harsh or abusive language.  |例句：He became a reviler of the government after his business failed. 他生意失败后成了政府的辱骂者。|例句：The crowd turned into a mob of revilers, shouting insults at the politician. 人群变成了辱骂者的暴民，向政治家大声喊叫侮辱性的话语。|近义词：insulter, abuser, critic, slanderer |反义词：praiser, flatterer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reviles his opponents at every opportunity. = 他抓住一切机会辱骂他的对手。
She reviles the injustice of the system. = 她痛斥这个制度的不公正。


一般过去时:
They reviled the dictator for his cruelty. = 他们辱骂独裁者的残酷。
The protesters reviled the government's policies. = 抗议者痛斥政府的政策。


现在完成时:
He has reviled his critics for years. = 多年来他一直辱骂他的批评者。
She has reviled the corruption in the city. = 她一直痛斥这个城市的腐败。


过去完成时:
They had reviled him for his actions before he even spoke. = 在他说话之前，他们就因为他的行为而辱骂他了。
She had reviled the company's practices for months before filing a lawsuit. = 在提起诉讼之前，她已经数月谴责公司的行为。


将来时:
He will revile his enemies until his dying day. = 他将一直到死都会辱骂他的敌人。
They will revile the new law if it passes. = 如果这项新法律通过，他们将会痛斥它。


虚拟语气:
If he were to revile me, I would ignore him. = 如果他辱骂我，我会无视他。
I wish they hadn't reviled her so mercilessly. = 我真希望他们没有那么无情地辱骂她。


被动语态:
The politician was reviled by the public for his lies. = 这位政治家因说谎而遭到公众的痛斥。
His ideas were reviled by the scientific community. = 他的想法被科学界斥责。


疑问句:
Why does he revile everyone he meets? = 他为什么辱骂他遇到的每一个人？
Who is reviling the judge? = 谁在辱骂法官？


否定句:
He does not revile his friends. = 他不辱骂他的朋友。
She did not revile the innocent bystander. = 她没有辱骂无辜的路人。


PHRASE->
revile against = 反对...而辱骂
revile somebody's name = 辱骂某人的名字
revile somebody to their face = 当面辱骂某人
to be reviled by = 被...所辱骂
revile and scorn = 辱骂和蔑视
