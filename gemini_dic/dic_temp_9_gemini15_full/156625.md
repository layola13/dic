pictogram:/ˈpɪktoʊɡræm/| n. | 象形文字；象形图 | A pictogram is a pictorial symbol representing a word or phrase.  It uses images to communicate information. Pictograms are often used in signs, icons, and early writing systems. | Example: The road sign used a pictogram of a winding road to warn drivers.  这个路标用弯曲道路的象形图来警告司机。 |近义词：ideogram, symbol, icon |反义词：alphabet (as a contrasting writing system) |名词
