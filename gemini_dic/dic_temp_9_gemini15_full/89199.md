homesick:/ˈhoʊmsɪk/| adj.|想家的，思乡的|Feeling a longing for home and family while away from them.|例句：She felt homesick after only a week at college.  她上大学才一个星期就感到想家了。
例句：Being so far from home, he was terribly homesick.  离家这么远，他非常想家。|近义词：longing for home, yearning for home|反义词：content, at ease


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is homesick for his family. = 他想念他的家人。
She feels homesick whenever she's away from home. = 每次离开家，她都感到想家。


一般过去时:
I was homesick during my first year of university. = 我大学一年级的时候很想家。
They were homesick after spending several months abroad. = 在国外待了几个月后，他们开始想家了。


现在完成时:
He has been homesick since he left home. = 自从他离开家后，他就一直想家。
She has never felt so homesick before. = 她以前从未如此想家。


过去完成时:
I had been homesick for weeks before I finally called my mom. = 在我最终打电话给我妈妈之前，我已经想家好几个星期了。
They had been homesick all summer before returning home. = 他们在夏天一直想家，直到回家才好。


将来时:
I will be homesick when I go to college next year. = 明年我去上大学的时候会想家的。
She thinks she will be homesick if she moves to a new city. = 她认为如果搬到新城市，她会想家的。


虚拟语气:
If I were closer to home, I wouldn't be so homesick. = 如果我离家近一些，我就不会这么想家了。
I wish I weren't so homesick. = 我真希望我不这么想家。


被动语态:  (Homesick is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Are you homesick? = 你想家了吗？
Is he homesick for his parents? = 他想念他的父母吗？


否定句:
I'm not homesick at all. = 我一点也不想家。
She wasn't homesick during her trip. = 在她的旅行中，她没有想家。


PHRASE->
feel homesick = 感到想家
become homesick = 开始想家
get homesick = 开始想家
overcome homesickness = 克服思乡病
suffer from homesickness = 遭受思乡之苦
