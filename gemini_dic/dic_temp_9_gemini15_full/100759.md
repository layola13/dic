intraregional: /ˌɪntrəˈriːdʒənəl/ | adj. |区域内的；地区内的| Relating to or occurring within a particular region.  |例句：Intraregional trade is increasing among Southeast Asian nations. 东南亚国家之间的区域内贸易正在增长。

例句：The study focused on intraregional migration patterns within the country. 这项研究侧重于该国境内的区域内迁移模式。


近义词：regional, local, internal

反义词：interregional, international
