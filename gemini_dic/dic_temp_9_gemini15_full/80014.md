go-round:/ˈɡoʊraʊnd/| n. |旋转；循环；流传；聚会|A go-round is a rotating movement or a cycle of events. It can also refer to a social gathering or a rumour spreading.  |例句：The merry-go-round is a popular ride for children.  旋转木马是孩子们喜欢的游乐设施。

There's been a go-round of flu in our office. 我们办公室流感盛行。|近义词：rotation, cycle, gathering, rumour|反义词：stillness, stagnation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The carousel makes a slow go-round. = 旋转木马缓慢旋转。
The rumour does the go-round in the office. = 谣言在办公室里流传。


一般过去时:
The earth made a complete go-round the sun. = 地球绕太阳完成了一次完整的公转。
