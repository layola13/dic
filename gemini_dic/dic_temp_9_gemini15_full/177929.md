rechoreograph: /ˌriːˈkɔːriəˌɡræf/ | vt. |重新编排（舞蹈）|To create a new choreography for an existing dance or piece of music.|The choreographer decided to rechoreograph the ballet, updating it for a modern audience.|近义词：revise, rearrange |反义词：compose (in the original sense)
