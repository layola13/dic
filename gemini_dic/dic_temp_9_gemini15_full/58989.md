duty-free:/ˈdjuːtiːfriː/ | adj. | 免税的 |  Free from any duty or tax, especially on goods imported or exported.  Goods sold at a duty-free shop are not subject to the usual import taxes. | Example: I bought some perfume at the duty-free shop in the airport. 我在机场的免税店买了一些香水。 |近义词: tax-free, exempt | 反义词: taxable, dutiable
