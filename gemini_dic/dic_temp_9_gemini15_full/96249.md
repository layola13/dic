indicatively:/ɪnˈdɪkətɪvli/ | adv. | 暗示地；象征性地；指示性地 | In a way that indicates or suggests something.  It implies something without stating it directly. |例句：He spoke indicatively of his displeasure. 他含蓄地表达了他的不满。

例句：The rising tide indicatively foreshadowed the coming storm.  上涨的潮水暗示着即将到来的风暴。


近义词：suggestively, implicitly, indirectly, symbolically


反义词：explicitly, directly, openly


词性：副词
