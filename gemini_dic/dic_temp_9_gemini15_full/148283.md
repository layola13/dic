oversoaping: /ˌoʊvərˈsoʊpɪŋ/ | n. |过度使用肥皂；肥皂用量过多 |The act of using too much soap, often resulting in dryness or irritation.  |例句：Oversoaping can strip your skin of its natural oils, leading to dryness and irritation. 过度使用肥皂会剥夺皮肤的天然油脂，导致干燥和刺激。

例句：The oversoaping of the delicate fabrics caused them to fade. 过度使用肥皂使精细的织物褪色。


近义词：excessive soaping, overlathering

反义词：undersoaping (less common)

词性：名词
