calorifere:/ˈkæləriˌfɛr/| n. |暖气片，暖气设备 |A calorifere is a heating apparatus, typically a radiator or similar device, used to warm a room or building.  |例句：The old house was heated by a large, cast-iron calorifere in the center of each room. 这栋老房子是用每个房间中央一个大型铸铁暖气片来取暖的。|近义词：radiator, heater, convector |反义词：air conditioner, cooler
