enlarge:/ɪnˈlɑːrdʒ/| vt. |扩大，放大，详述 |To make or become larger; to increase in size; to expand upon something in detail. |例句：The company plans to enlarge its factory. 公司计划扩大其工厂。

例句：He enlarged on his travel experiences. 他详细讲述了他的旅行经历。

近义词：expand, magnify, amplify, extend | 反义词：reduce, shrink, diminish, contract | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They enlarge the image. = 他们放大图像。
We enlarge our business scope. = 我们扩大业务范围。

一般过去时:
She enlarged the photo. = 她放大了照片。
He enlarged upon the subject during the lecture. = 他在讲座中详细阐述了这个主题。

现在完成时:
They have enlarged the garden. = 他们已经扩大了花园。
We have enlarged our understanding of the problem. = 我们已经加深了对这个问题的理解。

过去完成时:
They had enlarged the house before they moved in. = 他们在搬进去之前已经扩建了房子。
She had enlarged on the topic in her previous speech. = 她之前在演讲中已经详细阐述了这个话题。

将来时:
We will enlarge the picture. = 我们将会放大图片。
They will enlarge their market share. = 他们将会扩大他们的市场份额。

虚拟语气:
If we enlarged the sample size, the results would be more reliable. = 如果我们扩大样本量，结果会更可靠。
I wish I had enlarged the font size in my presentation. = 我希望我在演示文稿中把字体调大一些。

被动语态:
The photo was enlarged. = 照片被放大了。
The plan was enlarged to include more details. = 计划被扩大以包含更多细节。

疑问句:

否定句:
