hypothesises:/haɪˈpɒθəsaɪziz/ | /haɪˈpɑːθəsaɪziz/ | v. | 假设，提出假设 | To form or test a hypothesis.  It is the third-person singular present tense form of the verb "hypothesize".|

The scientist hypothesizes that climate change is affecting the polar bear population.  = 科学家假设气候变化正在影响北极熊的数量。

He carefully hypothesizes about the origins of the universe. = 他仔细地推测宇宙的起源。


近义词: theorizes, postulates, suggests, speculates | 反义词: confirms, proves


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She hypothesizes that the experiment will be successful. = 她假设实验将会成功。
The researcher hypothesizes a link between diet and disease. = 研究人员假设饮食和疾病之间存在联系。

一般过去时:
They hypothesized that the results were due to chance. = 他们假设结果是由于偶然造成的。
The team hypothesized a new model for explaining the phenomenon. = 该团队假设了一个解释这种现象的新模型。

现在完成时:
Scientists have hypothesized about the existence of dark matter for decades. = 科学家们几十年来一直在假设暗物质的存在。
She has hypothesized several explanations for the anomaly. = 她已经提出了几种解释这种异常现象的解释。

过去完成时:
They had hypothesized that the reaction would be exothermic, but it wasn't. = 他们曾假设反应会放热，但事实并非如此。
Before conducting the experiment, they had hypothesized a specific outcome. = 在进行实验之前，他们已经假设了一个特定的结果。

将来时:
We will hypothesize about the future implications of this discovery. = 我们将推测这一发现对未来的影响。
The researchers will hypothesize different scenarios to test their theory. = 研究人员将假设不同的情景来检验他们的理论。

虚拟语气:
If we hypothesized a different scenario, the results might be different. = 如果我们假设不同的情景，结果可能会有所不同。
I wish I had hypothesized a more comprehensive model. = 我希望我当时假设了一个更全面的模型。

被动语态:
It was hypothesized that the disease was caused by a virus. = 人们假设这种疾病是由病毒引起的。
A new theory was hypothesized to explain the phenomenon. = 一个新的理论被假设出来以解释这种现象。

疑问句:
What does the author hypothesize in this paper? = 作者在这篇论文中假设了什么？
How would you hypothesize about the cause of this problem? = 你会如何假设这个问题的原因？

否定句:
The study did not hypothesize any causal relationship between the two variables. = 这项研究没有假设这两个变量之间有任何因果关系。
He did not hypothesize a connection between the two events. = 他没有假设这两个事件之间有联系。


PHRASE->

working hypothesis = 工作假设
null hypothesis = 零假设
alternative hypothesis = 备择假设
