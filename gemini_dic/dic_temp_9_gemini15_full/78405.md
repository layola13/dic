get-hooked:/ɡɛt ˈhʊkt/|/v.|上瘾，迷上|to become strongly interested in something or someone, often to the point of addiction.|例句：I got hooked on that TV series. 我迷上了那个电视剧。|  He got hooked on gambling and lost all his money. 他沉迷于赌博，输光了所有的钱。|近义词：addicted, captivated, enthralled|反义词：disinterested, uninterested


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gets hooked on video games easily. = 他很容易沉迷于电子游戏。
She gets hooked on new trends quickly. = 她很快就会迷上新的潮流。


一般过去时:
I got hooked on coffee during college. = 我大学期间染上了咖啡瘾。
They got hooked on that new band's music. = 他们迷上了那个新乐队的音乐。


现在完成时:
I have gotten hooked on this podcast. = 我迷上了这个播客。
She has gotten hooked on online shopping. = 她沉迷于网购。


过去完成时:
By the time the movie ended, I had gotten hooked. = 电影结束时，我已经迷上了。
Before I knew it, I had gotten hooked on social media. = 不知不觉中，我已经沉迷于社交媒体了。


将来时:
I think I will get hooked on this new book. = 我想我会迷上这本新书的。
He predicts he will get hooked on the sport soon. = 他预测他很快就会迷上这项运动。


虚拟语气:
If I watched that show, I think I would get hooked. = 如果我看那个节目，我想我会迷上的。
I wish I hadn't gotten hooked on that game. = 我真希望我没有沉迷于那个游戏。


被动语态:
(This phrase is rarely used passively)


疑问句:
Did you get hooked on that new show? = 你迷上那个新节目了吗？
Will he get hooked on the game? = 他会迷上这个游戏吗？


否定句:
I didn't get hooked on that book. = 我没有迷上那本书。
She didn't get hooked on the series, despite trying. = 尽管尝试了，她也没有迷上这个系列。


PHRASE->
get hooked on sth = 迷上某物
get hooked on sb = 迷上某人
be hooked on sth = 对某物上瘾
become hooked on sth = 开始迷上某物
remain hooked on sth = 持续迷恋某物
