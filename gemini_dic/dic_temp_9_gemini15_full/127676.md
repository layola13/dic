/nɛɡ/ | adj. |否定的，消极的 |  Negative means expressing or implying denial, refusal, or opposition.  It can also describe something unfavorable or undesirable. | The answer was negative.  答案是否定的。|  He had a negative attitude. 他态度消极。| positive, affirmative | positive, affirmative | adjective


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The test result is negative. = 测试结果是阴性。
His response to the proposal is negative. = 他对这个提议的回应是否定的。


一般过去时:
The doctor gave a negative diagnosis. = 医生给出了否定的诊断。
Her reaction to the news was negative. = 她对这个消息的反应是否定的。


现在完成时:
They have received a negative response to their application. = 他们收到了申请被拒的回复。
She has had a negative experience with that company. = 她与那家公司有过一次负面的经历。


过去完成时:
The test had already returned a negative result. = 测试已经返回了阴性结果。
Before the meeting, he had a negative feeling about the project. = 会议之前，他对这个项目感觉很消极。


将来时:
The outcome of the negotiation is likely to be negative. = 谈判的结果很可能是不利的。
I predict a negative impact on the environment. = 我预测会对环境产生负面影响。


虚拟语气:

I wish the feedback wasn't so negative. = 我希望反馈不要这么消极。


被动语态:
The proposal was negatively received by the committee. = 这个提案受到了委员会的负面评价。
His efforts were negatively impacted by the unexpected delay. = 他付出的努力受到了意外延误的负面影响。


疑问句:
