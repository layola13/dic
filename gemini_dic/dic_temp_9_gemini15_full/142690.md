other:/ˈʌðər/| pron., adj., n.|其他的；另一个；另一些|

英文解析：The word "other" refers to a person or thing that is different from one already mentioned or known about.  It can function as a pronoun, adjective, or noun, and often implies a contrast or alternative.

例句：

I have two brothers. One is a doctor, and the other is a teacher. 我有两个兄弟。一个是医生，另一个是老师。

There are other ways to solve this problem.  解决这个问题还有其他方法。


近义词：another, different, else, additional

反义词：same, identical


词性：pronoun, adjective, noun
