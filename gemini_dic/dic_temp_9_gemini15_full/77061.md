garnish:/ˈɡɑːrnɪʃ/| vt. n.|装饰；点缀；(菜肴的)装饰物|To decorate or embellish, especially food.|The chef garnished the dish with fresh herbs.  厨师用新鲜香草装饰这道菜。|decorate, adorn, embellish|plain, undecorated|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He garnishes his salads with croutons. = 他用面包丁装饰他的沙拉。
The restaurant garnishes all its dishes with parsley. = 这家餐厅用欧芹装饰所有菜肴。


一般过去时:
She garnished the cake with chocolate shavings. = 她用巧克力屑装饰蛋糕。
They garnished the cocktails with cherries. = 他们用樱桃装饰鸡尾酒。


现在完成时:
I have garnished the soup with a swirl of cream. = 我已经用一圈奶油装饰了汤。
She has garnished the plate beautifully with vegetables. = 她已经用蔬菜漂亮地装饰了盘子。


过去完成时:
He had garnished the turkey with cranberries before roasting it. = 他在烤火鸡之前已经用蔓越莓装饰了它。
They had garnished the ice cream with sprinkles before serving it. = 他们上冰淇淋之前已经用彩色糖粒装饰了它。


将来时:
I will garnish the pizza with fresh basil. = 我将用新鲜罗勒装饰比萨饼。
She will garnish the seafood platter with lemon wedges. = 她将用柠檬角装饰海鲜拼盘。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would garnish the cupcakes more elaborately. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更精心装饰纸杯蛋糕。
I wish I had garnished the steak with a peppercorn sauce. = 我希望我用胡椒酱装饰牛排。


被动语态:
The plate was garnished with edible flowers. = 盘子用食用花装饰。
The cake is garnished with whipped cream. = 蛋糕用鲜奶油装饰。


疑问句:
How did you garnish the fish? = 你是怎么装饰鱼的？
Will you garnish the drinks with lime? = 你会用青柠装饰饮料吗？


否定句:
I didn't garnish the pasta because I ran out of parsley. = 我没有装饰意大利面，因为我欧芹用完了。
She didn't garnish the pie, leaving it plain. = 她没有装饰馅饼，让它保持原样。


PHRASE->
garnish with = 用...装饰
