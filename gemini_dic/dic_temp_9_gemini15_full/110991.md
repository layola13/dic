liquidizing: /ˈlɪkwɪdaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 液化；使…变成液体；清算；变现 |  The process of turning something into a liquid; also, the act of converting assets into cash. |

例句：

The blender is liquidizing the fruit. = 搅拌机正在把水果搅成液体。

The company is liquidizing its assets to pay off its debts. = 公司正在变卖资产以偿还债务。


近义词：liquefying, dissolving, converting to cash

反义词：solidifying, crystallizing


词源:

From "liquid" + "-ize" (to make or become).  The suffix "-ize" is of Greek origin, meaning "to make" or "to become."  The word ultimately derives from the Latin word "liquidus," meaning "liquid," or "flowing like water."
