localisable: /ˈloʊkəlaɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 可本地化的 |  Able to be localized; adaptable to different languages and cultural contexts. This refers to software, content, or other materials that can be easily modified to suit the specific needs of a particular region or language.  | 例句：The software is designed to be fully localisable, allowing for easy translation into multiple languages.  该软件设计为完全可本地化的，便于翻译成多种语言。

The application's interface is highly localisable, accommodating various date and currency formats. 该应用程序的界面高度可本地化，可适应各种日期和货币格式。

近义词：adaptable, customizable, translatable | 反义词：unadaptable, inflexible
