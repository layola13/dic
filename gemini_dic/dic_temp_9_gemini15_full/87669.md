hexagons:/ˈhɛksəɡɑːnz/ | n. |六边形 |A hexagon is a polygon with six sides and six angles. Hexagons can be regular, meaning all sides and angles are equal, or irregular.  They are found in nature and in many man-made structures. |例句：Honeycombs are made of many hexagons.蜂巢是由许多六边形组成的。

The floor tiles were arranged in a pattern of hexagons.地板瓷砖排列成六边形图案。|近义词：six-sided polygon |反义词：无
