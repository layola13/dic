jeroboams:/ˈdʒɛrəboʊmz/ | n. |大瓶酒（通常指香槟酒）|A jeroboam is a large bottle of wine, typically champagne, holding the equivalent of four standard bottles (or 3 liters). It's named after Jeroboam I, the first king of Israel, known for his lavish lifestyle and abundant wine supplies.  | The restaurant offered a jeroboam of their finest champagne to celebrate the anniversary. 这家餐厅提供了一瓶他们最好的香槟酒来庆祝周年纪念。| magnum, rehoboam | small bottle, standard bottle | 名词
