jabbingly:/ˈdʒæbɪŋli/| adv.|尖刻地；刺痛地；猛地；突然地 |In a manner that is sharp, sudden, and often unpleasant.  It suggests a forceful, pointed, and sometimes aggressive action or utterance.|例句：He jabbingly criticized her performance. 他尖刻地批评了她的表现。

例句：She jabbingly pointed out my mistake. 她猛地指出我的错误。


近义词：sharply, pointedly, abruptly, suddenly, aggressively


反义词：gently, softly, kindly, mildly


词性:副词
