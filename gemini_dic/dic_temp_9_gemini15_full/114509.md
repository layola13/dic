maladministration:/ˌmælædmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən/ | n. |  maladministration | 管理不善；行政失职 |  Maladministration refers to the mismanagement or mishandling of affairs, typically in a government or organization. It involves incompetence, negligence, or corruption in the execution of duties and responsibilities.  This can lead to inefficiency, waste of resources, and a failure to meet objectives. | The report highlighted instances of maladministration within the local council, leading to a public inquiry.  该报告强调了地方议会内部管理不善的实例，导致了公众调查。 | mismanagement, misrule, incompetence | good governance, efficient administration
