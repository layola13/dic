conked:/kɒŋkt/ | v. |昏倒；撞击；停止工作；|to suddenly lose consciousness; to hit something forcefully; to stop functioning|

The old man conked out on the sofa. | 老人家在沙发上昏过去了。

He conked his head on the low-hanging beam. | 他把头撞到了低垂的横梁上。

The engine conked out halfway up the hill. | 发动机在半山腰熄火了。


近义词：faint, collapse, hit, crash, fail

反义词：wake up, revive, function, work
