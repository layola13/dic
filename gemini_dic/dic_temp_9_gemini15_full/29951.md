caveman:/ˈkeɪvmæn/ | n. |穴居人，原始人 |A caveman is a person who lived in a cave, especially during the Stone Age.  They are often depicted as being uncivilized and primitive. |例句：The movie portrays cavemen as strong but simple-minded. 这部电影把穴居人描绘成强壮但头脑简单的。
例句：Archaeologists discovered ancient cave paintings made by cavemen. 考古学家发现了穴居人创作的古代洞穴壁画。|近义词：prehistoric man, troglodyte |反义词：modern man, civilized person |词性:名词
