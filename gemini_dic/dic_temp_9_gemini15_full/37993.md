complicit:/kəmˈplɪsɪt/| adj.|同谋的；共犯的；参与的|Involved with others in an activity that is unlawful or morally wrong.  It implies knowledge of and participation in a wrongdoing, even if not directly responsible for it. |例句：He was found complicit in the cover-up. 他被发现参与了掩盖事件。

例句：The evidence suggests she was complicit in the robbery.  证据表明她参与了抢劫案。


近义词：accomplice, involved, implicated, accessory|反义词：innocent, guiltless, blameless|词性：形容词
