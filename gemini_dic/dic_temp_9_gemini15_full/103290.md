jibbed:/dʒɪbd/| v.| 突然停止；畏缩；拒绝；（帆船）突然失去动力|To stop suddenly and refuse to go on; to shy away from; to refuse to do something; (of a ship's sail) to become becalmed or to lose its effectiveness. |例句：The horse jibbed at the fence.  这匹马在篱笆前突然停住。

The engine jibbed and then sputtered to a stop. 发动机突然停了，然后发出噼啪声停止了。


近义词：refuse, balk, shy, flinch| 反义词：proceed, continue, comply| 词性:动词
