homeschooled:/ˈhoʊmˌskuːld/| adj.|在家接受教育的|Educated at home rather than at a school.  This typically involves parents or guardians providing the instruction.|例句：Many families choose to homeschool their children for religious or philosophical reasons. 许多家庭选择让孩子在家接受教育，原因是宗教或哲学上的考虑。|例句：She was homeschooled until she was twelve, then enrolled in a public high school. 她在家接受教育直到十二岁，然后进入公立高中。|近义词：home-educated, privately educated|反义词：school-educated
