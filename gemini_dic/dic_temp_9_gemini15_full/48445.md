deed:/diːd/ | n. |行为；事实；契约；功绩 | An action or an act; something that is done.  A legal document that proves ownership of property. An accomplishment; something noteworthy that has been done. |例句：He did a good deed by helping the old woman cross the street. 他帮助老太太过马路做了一件好事。 The deed to the house is in my name. 房屋所有权证书是我的名字。 Her heroic deeds saved many lives. 她的英勇事迹挽救了许多人的生命。|近义词：act, action, feat, accomplishment |反义词：inaction, idleness
