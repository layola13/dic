rescore:/riːˈskɔːr/| vt.|重新评分，重新计分|To score (something) again, often to correct an error or improve accuracy.|例句：The teacher decided to rescore the exams after discovering a grading error.老师发现评分错误后决定重新评分。  The athlete requested a rescore of their performance. 运动员要求重新计算他们的成绩。|近义词：regrade, reassess|反义词：score (initial scoring)
