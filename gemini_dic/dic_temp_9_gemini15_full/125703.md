munging:/ˈmʌŋɪŋ/| v.|篡改；修改；揉捏；捣碎 |The act of manipulating or altering data, often in a clumsy or haphazard way.  It often implies a lack of elegance or efficiency. |例句：The data was munged beyond recognition after the system crash.  系统崩溃后，数据被篡改得面目全非。

例句：He was accused of munging the financial reports to hide the losses. 他被指控篡改财务报告以掩盖损失。


近义词：altering, modifying, manipulating, distorting, corrupting, tweaking


反义词：preserving, maintaining, protecting, restoring


词性：动词
