meridians:/məˈrɪdiən/| n.|子午线；经线；最高点；（中医的）经络|Lines of longitude on a globe or map, running from the North Pole to the South Pole. In traditional Chinese medicine, meridians are channels through which vital energy (qi) flows.|例句：The ship crossed the meridian at noon.  这艘船在中午穿过子午线。

例句：Acupuncture stimulates specific points along the body's meridians. 针灸刺激身体经络上的特定穴位。


近义词：longitude line, channel (in TCM context)
反义词：None specifically, but depending on context, might be parallel.

词性：名词
