grittier:/ˈɡrɪtɪər/| adj. |更坚韧的；更粗糙的；更真实的；更严酷的 |More gritty; having or showing more grit.  It suggests a greater degree of roughness, harshness, or realism than "gritty." |例句：The new documentary offered a grittier portrayal of urban life than previous versions.  新的纪录片比以前的版本更真实地描绘了城市生活。

例句：The grittier sections of the city are often overlooked. 城市中比较粗糙的区域常常被忽视。


近义词：rougher, tougher, harsher, more realistic, more raw

反义词：smoother, gentler, kinder, more polished, more idealistic
