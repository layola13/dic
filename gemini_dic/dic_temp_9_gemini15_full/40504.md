contriver:/kənˈtraɪvər/| n. |策划者；发明者；图谋者 |A contriver is someone who devises plans, especially intricate or ingenious ones, often with a sense of scheming or manipulation.  They are skilled at creating or inventing things, but this skill can be used for good or ill. |例句：The contriver of the elaborate hoax was never caught.  (这个精心策划的骗局的策划者从未被抓住。)

The clever contriver managed to solve the complex puzzle. (这位聪明的发明者成功地解决了这个复杂的难题。) |近义词：schemer, plotter, inventor, deviser |反义词：follower, conformist


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He contrives elaborate schemes to achieve his goals. = 他策划精心设计的计划来实现他的目标。
She contrives new ways to save money. = 她想出新的省钱方法。

一般过去时:
They contrived a plan to escape the prison. = 他们策划了一个越狱计划。
He contrived a story to explain his lateness. = 他编造了一个故事来解释他的迟到。

现在完成时:
She has contrived to get herself into a difficult situation. = 她让自己陷入困境了。
He has contrived a remarkable solution to the problem. = 他想出了一个非凡的解决方案。

过去完成时:
They had contrived a false alibi before the trial. = 他们在审判前编造了一个虚假的不在场证明。
She had contrived to meet him secretly before he left. = 在他离开之前，她已经设法秘密地见了他。

将来时:
We will contrive a way to overcome this obstacle. = 我们将想办法克服这个障碍。
They will contrive to finish the project on time. = 他们将设法按时完成项目。


虚拟语气:
If he contrived a better plan, he might succeed. = 如果他策划一个更好的计划，他可能会成功。
I wish I had contrived a more effective strategy. = 我希望我制定了一个更有效的策略。

被动语态:
A clever solution was contrived by the engineers. = 工程师们想出了一个巧妙的解决方案。
The escape was cleverly contrived by the prisoners. = 囚犯们巧妙地策划了越狱。

疑问句:
How did they contrive to do that? = 他们是怎么做到的？
Who contrived this complicated mechanism? = 谁设计了这个复杂的机制？

否定句:
They did not contrive to deceive anyone. = 他们没有设法欺骗任何人。
He could not contrive a believable excuse. = 他编不出一个可信的借口。


PHRASE->
