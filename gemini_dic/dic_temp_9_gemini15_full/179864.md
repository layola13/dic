reexportations:/ˌriːˌɛkspɔːrˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 再出口，转口 | The act of re-exporting goods; the goods re-exported.  It refers to the process of importing goods into a country and then exporting them to another country, often with some added value or processing. | Example:  The country relies heavily on reexportations of manufactured goods. 这个国家严重依赖制成品的再出口。  The increase in reexportations boosted the nation's economy. 再出口的增加推动了该国的经济发展。 | 近义词：re-exports, transshipments | 反义词：importations
