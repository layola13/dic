scrums:/skrʌmz/| n.|争吵；混乱；橄榄球赛中的争球|A scrum is a confused struggle or a disorganized mass of people or things.  In rugby, it's a method of restarting play.|例句：The protesters engaged in a violent scrum in the street. 抗议者在街上发生了激烈的争吵。|例句：The checkout was a complete scrum of shoppers. 收银台挤满了购物者，一片混乱。|近义词：melee, brawl, ruck, scrimmage|反义词：order, calm, peace
