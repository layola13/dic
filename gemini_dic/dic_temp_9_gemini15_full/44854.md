crustless:/ˈkrʌstləs/| adj.|无外皮的，无硬皮的|Lacking a crust.  Often used to describe food, particularly bread or pies, without their usual outer crust.|例句：I prefer crustless sandwiches because I don't like the crust. 我更喜欢无皮三明治，因为我不喜欢硬皮。


例句：She baked a crustless quiche for the party. 她为派对烤了一个无皮蛋挞。


近义词：uncrusted, crust-free


反义词：crusted
