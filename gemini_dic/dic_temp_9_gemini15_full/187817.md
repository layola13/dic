rosebuds:/ˈroʊzbʌdz/ | n. |玫瑰花蕾，含苞待放的玫瑰 | Rosebuds are the unopened flower buds of a rose plant.  They are often used in perfumes and as a decorative element.  |例句：The rosebuds in the garden were just beginning to open. 花园里的玫瑰花蕾才刚刚开始绽放。

例句：She carefully arranged the rosebuds in a vase. 她小心地将玫瑰花蕾插在花瓶里。


近义词：rose flower buds, unopened roses | 反义词：bloomed roses, full-blown roses | 词性：名词

词源：
The word "rosebuds" is a compound word.  "Rose" comes from the Old French "rose," ultimately derived from the Latin "rosa."  "Bud" comes from the Middle English "budde,"  possibly of Germanic origin.  Thus, "rosebuds" literally means the buds of a rose plant.
