jerkin:/ˈdʒɜːrkɪn/ | n. |短上衣，马甲 |A jerkin is a close-fitting, sleeveless jacket, typically made of leather or other sturdy material.  Historically, it was worn as a piece of armor or as a practical outer garment.  Modern usage often refers to a similar style of short jacket, often without sleeves. |例句：He wore a leather jerkin over his shirt. 他在衬衫外面穿着一件皮革短上衣。

例句：The knight donned his chainmail jerkin before battle.  骑士在战斗前穿上了他的锁子甲短上衣。


近义词：jacket, waistcoat, vest |反义词：coat (in the sense of a long coat)


词性：名词
