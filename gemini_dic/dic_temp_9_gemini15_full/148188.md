overshadower: /ˌoʊvərˈʃædəʊər/ | n. |  overshadowing的人或事物，使黯然失色者 | Something or someone that overshadows;  a person or thing that makes another seem less important. | The towering skyscrapers overshadowed the smaller buildings. 摩天大楼使较小的建筑物相形见绌。|近义词：rival, competitor, eclipse | 反义词：supporter, enhancer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tall trees overshadow the small house. = 高大的树木遮蔽了小房子。
His great success overshadows his past failures. = 他巨大的成功掩盖了他过去的失败。


一般过去时:
The storm overshadowed the picnic. = 暴风雨使野餐黯然失色。
Her beauty overshadowed everyone else at the party. = 她的美貌使派对上其他人都相形见绌。


现在完成时:
Recent events have overshadowed the earlier news. = 最近发生的事件使之前的新闻黯然失色。
His achievements have overshadowed his earlier mistakes. = 他的成就已掩盖了他早期的错误。


过去完成时:
The scandal had overshadowed the politician's career. = 丑闻已经损害了这位政治家的职业生涯。
The bad weather had overshadowed our planned trip. = 恶劣的天气已经使我们计划中的旅行蒙上阴影。


将来时:
The new stadium will overshadow the old one. = 新体育场将使旧体育场相形见绌。
The upcoming election will overshadow all other news. = 即将到来的选举将使所有其他新闻黯然失色。


虚拟语气:
If only his accomplishments weren't so great, they wouldn't overshadow his flaws. = 如果他的成就不是那么伟大，就不会掩盖他的缺陷。
I wish my success didn't overshadow my friends' achievements. = 我希望我的成功不会掩盖我朋友的成就。


被动语态:
The smaller shops were overshadowed by the large department store. = 小型商店被大型百货公司比了下去。
His talent was overshadowed by his lack of confidence. = 他的才能被他的缺乏自信所掩盖。


疑问句:
Does his fame overshadow his talent? = 他的名气是否掩盖了他的才能？
What overshadowed your happiness today? = 什么事情让你今天不开心？


否定句:
His kindness did not overshadow his mistakes. = 他的善良并没有掩盖他的错误。
The small details did not overshadow the overall success of the project. = 小细节并没有掩盖项目的整体成功。


PHRASE->
overshadowed by = 被...遮蔽; 被...比下去
be overshadowed = 被比下去；被掩盖
overshadowing presence = 令人印象深刻的存在
overshadowing influence = 影响力巨大
overshadowing event = 举足轻重的事件
