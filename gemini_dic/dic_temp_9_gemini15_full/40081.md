containable:/kənˈteɪnəbl/ | adj. | 可控制的，可包含的 | Able to be contained; capable of being controlled or limited.  | The fire was thankfully containable with the quick response of the firefighters.  这场火灾由于消防员的快速反应而幸运地得到了控制。  | controllable, manageable, restrainable | uncontainable, uncontrollable, irrepressible | 形容词

USAGE->

The situation is containable with proper measures. = 采取适当措施，局势是可以控制的。
This container is containable enough for the liquid. = 这个容器足够盛放这种液体。

一般过去时:
The riot was containable before it escalated further. = 暴乱在进一步升级之前得到了控制。
