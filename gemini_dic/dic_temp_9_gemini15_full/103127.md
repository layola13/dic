jerkins:/ˈdʒɜːrkɪnz/| n.|短上衣，马甲|A jerkin is a close-fitting, sleeveless jacket, often made of leather or similar material.  Historically, it was worn by men as a piece of outerwear.  Modern usage often refers to a similar style of garment, sometimes with sleeves.|例句：He wore a leather jerkin over his shirt. 他在衬衫外面穿着一件皮短上衣。

例句：The historical reenactor donned a jerkin and breeches for the event.  历史再现者为了活动穿上了短上衣和马裤。


近义词：jacket, waistcoat, vest|反义词：None specifically, but could be considered the opposite of a long coat.|词性:名词
