recoupe:/rɪˈkuːp/| vt.|补偿, 挽回, 收回|To recover (something lost or spent); regain|The company hopes to recoup its losses through increased sales. 公司希望通过增加销售额来弥补损失。|近义词：recover, regain, retrieve|反义词：lose, forfeit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recoups his losses through diligent work. 他通过勤奋工作来弥补损失。
We recoup the cost of the trip by renting out our apartment. 我们通过出租公寓来收回旅行的费用。


一般过去时:
The farmer recouped his losses after a good harvest.  农夫在丰收后弥补了损失。
She recouped her strength after a long illness. 她大病初愈后恢复了体力。


现在完成时:
They have recouped most of their investment. 他们已经收回了大部分投资。
The company has recouped its initial investment. 公司已经收回了最初的投资。


过去完成时:
He had recouped his losses before the end of the year. 他在年底前已经弥补了损失。
By the time the project ended, they had recouped all their expenses. 项目结束后，他们已经收回了所有费用。


将来时:
We will recoup the costs of the repairs with insurance money. 我们将用保险金来弥补修理费用。
The business expects to recoup its investment within five years. 这家企业预计在五年内收回投资。


虚拟语气:
If we worked harder, we could recoup our losses more quickly. 如果我们更努力工作，就能更快地弥补损失。
If they had acted sooner, they might have recouped their investment. 如果他们早点采取行动，也许就能收回投资。


被动语态:
The losses were recouped by increased sales. 损失通过增加销售额得到了弥补。
The initial investment will be recouped within five years. 初始投资将在五年内收回。


疑问句:
How will we recoup the expenses? 我们将如何弥补开支？
Can we recoup the losses from last year? 我们能弥补去年的损失吗？


否定句:
They could not recoup their investment in the failing business. 他们无法收回在失败企业中的投资。
He failed to recoup his losses from the bad deal. 他未能弥补那笔糟糕交易带来的损失。
