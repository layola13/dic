miseries:/ˈmɪzəriːz/| n. |苦难；不幸；痛苦 |The plural form of "misery," referring to multiple instances of suffering, unhappiness, or distress.  It encompasses a range of negative experiences, from physical pain to emotional anguish. |例句：He recounted the miseries of his childhood. 他叙述了他童年时代的苦难。

例句：The refugees endured unimaginable miseries during their escape. 难民在逃亡过程中忍受着难以想象的苦难。


近义词：sufferings, woes, hardships, troubles, afflictions |反义词：joys, happinesses, delights, blessings


词性: 名词 (复数)
