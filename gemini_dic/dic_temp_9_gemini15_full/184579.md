restoration:/ˌrɛstəˈreɪʃən/| n. |修复；恢复；重建；复原 |The act of restoring something to its former state or condition; the process of repairing or renewing something old, damaged, or decayed.  It can refer to physical objects, environments, or even social structures. |例句：The restoration of the old church took many years.  古老教堂的修复工程耗时多年。

例句：The restoration of the monarchy marked a turning point in history. 君主制的恢复标志着历史的转折点。


近义词：repair, renewal, rehabilitation, refurbishment, recovery |反义词：destruction, demolition, ruin
