destiny:/ˈdɛstɪni/ | n. |命运，天命，注定 | Inevitable fate; a predetermined course of events.  It often implies a power beyond human control shaping one's life. | Example: It was her destiny to become a great artist.  这是她注定要成为一位伟大的艺术家的命运。

Example:  He believed his destiny was to travel the world. 他相信他的命运是环游世界。


近义词：fate, fortune, lot, doom | 反义词：chance, accident, coincidence | 词性：名词
