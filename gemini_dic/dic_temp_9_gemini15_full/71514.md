flannelettes:/ˌflænəˈlɛts/| n. |法兰绒布；法兰绒睡衣| A type of soft, warm cotton fabric with a napped surface.  It's often used for pajamas, shirts, and bedding. | Example:  She bought a new flannelette nightgown for the winter. 她买了一件新的法兰绒睡衣过冬。  The baby's blanket is made of soft flannelette. 婴儿的毯子是用柔软的法兰绒做的。|近义词：flannel|反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting opposite textures like silk or linen could be considered contrastive.
