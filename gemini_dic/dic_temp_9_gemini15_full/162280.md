preaccess:/ˌpriːˈækses/| n. |预先访问；提前访问|Access granted before the official or scheduled time.  It often implies special permission or privilege.|例句：The researchers were granted preaccess to the data for preliminary analysis. 研究人员被允许提前访问数据以进行初步分析。

例句：The VIPs enjoyed preaccess to the exhibition before it opened to the public.  贵宾们在展览向公众开放之前就享有了提前参观的权利。


近义词：prior access, advance access, early access


反义词：postaccess (not a common word), delayed access


词性:名词
