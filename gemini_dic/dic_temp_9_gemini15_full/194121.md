self-impulsion:/ˌsɛlfɪmˈpʌlʃən/| n. |自我驱动；自主性；自动推进|The act or process of propelling oneself; the ability to motivate oneself.  It implies internal motivation rather than external pressure.|例句：Her self-impulsion led her to achieve great things despite facing many obstacles.  她的自我驱动使她在面对许多障碍时仍然取得了巨大的成就。

例句：The clockwork toy demonstrated remarkable self-impulsion, moving across the floor without any external assistance.  这个发条玩具展现了非凡的自我驱动能力，无需任何外部辅助就能在地板上移动。


近义词：self-motivation, initiative, drive, autonomy


反义词：passivity, inertia, dependence


词性:名词
