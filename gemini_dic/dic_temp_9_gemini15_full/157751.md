placative:/ˈplæskətɪv/| adj.|抚慰的；缓和的；安慰的 |Something intended to soothe or appease.  It often refers to words or actions designed to calm or pacify someone who is angry or upset.  |例句：He offered a placative smile to the angry customer. 他对愤怒的顾客露出了一个抚慰的笑容。

例句：Her placative words calmed the crying child. 她安慰的话语使哭泣的孩子平静下来。

近义词：soothing, appeasing, conciliatory, mollifying

反义词：provocative, inflammatory, irritating, antagonizing

词性:形容词
