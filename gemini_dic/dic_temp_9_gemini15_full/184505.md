restationed:/ˌriːˈsteɪʃənd/| v. |重新驻扎；重新安置 | To move (troops, equipment, etc.) to a new location, typically for military or strategic reasons.  |例句：The troops were restationed to a more secure base.  部队被重新部署到一个更安全的基地。
The company restationed its employees to a new office building. 公司将员工重新安置到新的办公楼。|近义词：relocate, reposition, redeploy |反义词：withdraw, remove
