siphoned:/ˈsaɪfənd/| v.|虹吸；抽取；窃取|To draw liquid from a container or place by means of a siphon.  Also, to steal or transfer money or other assets fraudulently.|例句：They siphoned the water from the pool.他们用虹吸管把泳池里的水抽干了。

例句：The embezzler siphoned millions of dollars from the company account. 那个贪污犯从公司账户中窃取了数百万美元。

近义词：drain, draw off, transfer, pilfer, embezzle

反义词：fill, replenish, restore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He siphons gas from his neighbor's car. 他从邻居的车里偷汽油。
They siphon water from the river to irrigate their fields. 他们从河里抽水灌溉田地。


一般过去时:
She siphoned the money out of the account secretly. 她秘密地把钱从账户中转走。
The thieves siphoned petrol from the tanker. 小偷从油罐车里偷油。


现在完成时:
They have siphoned off all the funds from the project. 他们已经把项目的所有资金都转移走了。
He has siphoned petrol from my car several times. 他已经好几次从我的车里偷汽油了。


过去完成时:
The accountant had siphoned off the company's money for years before anyone noticed. 会计在被人发现之前已经挪用公司资金多年了。
Before the police arrived, they had already siphoned the gas from the truck. 在警察到达之前，他们已经从卡车上偷走了汽油。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
