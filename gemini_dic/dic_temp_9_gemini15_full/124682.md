movers-and-shakers: /ˈmuːvərz ænd ˈʃeɪkərz/ | n. |举足轻重的人物，有影响力的人物 |  Influential people who have a significant impact on a particular field or society.  They are the people who initiate change and shape events. | The movers and shakers of the tech industry gathered at the conference. 科技行业的举足轻重的人物聚集在这次会议上。 |  influential figures, key players, bigwigs | insignificant people, nonentities | 名词
