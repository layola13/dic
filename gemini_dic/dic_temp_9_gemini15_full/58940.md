dust-up:/ˈdʌst ʌp/| n.|小争吵，小冲突，一阵喧嚣|A brief but intense quarrel or commotion.  It often implies a minor, relatively insignificant conflict.|例句：There was a bit of a dust-up in the pub last night. 昨晚酒吧里发生了一点小争吵。

例句：The dust-up over the new policy quickly subsided. 关于新政策的争吵很快就平息了。


近义词：altercation, squabble, brawl, fuss, commotion|反义词：peace, harmony, tranquility|词性:名词
