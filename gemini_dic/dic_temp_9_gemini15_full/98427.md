inspecting: /ɪnˈspɛktɪŋ/ | v. | 检查；视察；检阅 |  The act of examining something carefully to check its condition, quality, or the presence of something.  | 例句：The engineer is inspecting the bridge for structural damage. 工程师正在检查桥梁是否有结构性损坏。

例句：The teacher is inspecting the students' homework. 老师正在检查学生的作业。


近义词：examining, reviewing, surveying, checking | 反义词：ignoring, overlooking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is inspecting the car for any defects. 他正在检查汽车是否有任何缺陷。
They are inspecting the factory for safety violations. 他们正在检查工厂是否有安全违规行为。

一般过去时:
She inspected the painting carefully before purchasing it.  她在购买这幅画之前仔细检查了它。
The police inspected the crime scene thoroughly. 警察彻底检查了犯罪现场。

现在完成时:
They have inspected all the equipment. 他们已经检查了所有设备。
The authorities have inspected the building for fire hazards. 当局已经检查了该建筑物的火灾隐患。

过去完成时:
The mechanic had inspected the engine before the race. 技工在比赛前检查了发动机。
After the accident, investigators had inspected the wreckage. 事故发生后，调查人员检查了残骸。

将来时:
We will inspect the goods upon arrival. 货物到达后我们将进行检查。
The team will inspect the site tomorrow.  团队明天将视察现场。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would inspect the contract carefully before signing. 如果我是你，我会在签署合同之前仔细检查一下。
It's crucial that he inspect the data thoroughly. 至关重要的是，他必须彻底检查数据。

被动语态:
The building is being inspected for safety.  该建筑物正在接受安全检查。
The documents were inspected by the authorities. 文件已被当局检查。

疑问句:
Are you inspecting the samples now? 你现在正在检查样品吗？
Will they be inspecting the factory next week? 他们下周会检查工厂吗？

否定句:
He did not inspect the goods properly. 他没有正确检查货物。
They haven't inspected the bridge yet. 他们还没有检查桥梁。
