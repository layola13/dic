peckish:/ˈpɛkɪʃ/| adj.|  有点饿的；易怒的；挑剔的 | Feeling slightly hungry; irritable; picky. | Example: I'm feeling a bit peckish;  let's have a snack.  我有点饿了，咱们吃点零食吧。  She was peckish about the details of the contract. 她对合同的细节很挑剔。| 近义词：hungry, irritable, fussy,  | 反义词：full, satisfied, complacent

She is peckish about her food choices. 她对食物的选择很挑剔。
