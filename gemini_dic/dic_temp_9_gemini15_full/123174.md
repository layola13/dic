monks:/mʌŋks/| n.|僧侣，和尚|Members of a religious community, especially Buddhist or Christian monks who live a secluded life, often in a monastery and under vows of poverty, chastity, and obedience.|例句：Many monks live a life of prayer and meditation. 许多僧侣过着祈祷和冥想的生活。|例句：The monks chanted prayers throughout the night.和尚整夜都在诵经。|近义词：friars, mendicants, religious|反义词：laypeople, secular


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Monks meditate daily. = 僧侣每天冥想。
The monks chant prayers. = 僧侣们诵经。

一般过去时:
The monks lived a simple life. = 僧侣们过着简朴的生活。

现在完成时:
The monastery has housed monks for centuries. = 这座寺院已经容纳僧侣几个世纪了。

过去完成时:
