rechosen: /ˌriːˈtʃoʊzən/ | adj. | 再度被选中的，重新选择的 |  Having been chosen again.  This word is the past participle of the verb "rechoose," which itself is a less common variant of "re-elect" or "choose again." It implies a previous selection and a subsequent reaffirmation of that choice. | Example: The rechosen leader addressed the nation.  再次被选中的领导人向全国发表讲话。 |近义词: re-elected, selected again, chosen anew | 反义词: rejected, dismissed, deselected | 词性: 形容词
