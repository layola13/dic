/ˌpriːɪkˈspɛnsɪz/ | n. | 预付费用，前期费用 | Expenses paid before a project or venture begins.  These costs are often incurred before any revenue is generated. | Example:  Preexpenses for the new marketing campaign included website development and advertising material creation.  = 新营销活动的预付费用包括网站开发和广告材料制作。


The company covered all preexpenses related to the launch of the new product. = 公司承担了新产品发布的所有预付费用。| 近义词：advance payments, upfront costs, preliminary expenses | 反义词：post-expenses, subsequent costs
