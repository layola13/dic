liberation:/ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən/| n. |解放；释放；解放运动 |The act of setting someone free from imprisonment, slavery, or oppression.  It can also refer to the process of freeing oneself from something that restricts or confines. |例句：The liberation of the prisoners was a joyous occasion.  囚犯的获释是一个令人高兴的时刻。

The liberation of women from traditional gender roles is a long-standing struggle.  妇女从传统性别角色中解放出来是一场长期的斗争。|近义词：release, emancipation, freeing |反义词：imprisonment, enslavement, oppression |名词
