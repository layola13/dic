pecking:/ˈpɛkɪŋ/| v.|啄，啄食；轻敲；|to strike or hit with the beak or a pointed object; to tap lightly and repeatedly.|The birds were pecking at the seeds.  鸟儿们在啄食种子。


The chickens were pecking the grain. 鸡在啄食谷物。


近义词：picking, tapping, prodding| 反义词：None


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He pecks at his food. 他没胃口地吃东西。
The bird pecks the branch. 鸟啄树枝。


一般过去时:
She pecked at the keyboard. 她快速地敲打键盘。
The woodpecker pecked a hole in the tree.  啄木鸟在树上啄了个洞。


现在完成时:
The hen has pecked all the grain.母鸡已经啄食了所有的谷物。
He has been pecking away at the problem all day. 他整天都在努力解决这个问题。


过去完成时:
The bird had pecked at the fruit before I arrived.在我到达之前，鸟已经啄食了水果。
She had pecked out the entire letter before the power went out.停电前，她已经打完了整封信。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

PHRASE->

peck order: 啄食顺序；等级制度
peck away at: 努力地做；一点一点地做
peck and dab: 这里戳戳那里点点
peck at:  少量地吃；轻敲；不认真地做
peck blind:  瞎啄；乱做


词源:

From Middle English *peken, from Old English *piccian, of imitative origin.  Its meaning developed from the action of a bird's beak to a more general sense of striking or tapping.
