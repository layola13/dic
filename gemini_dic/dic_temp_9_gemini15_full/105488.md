kiln:/kɪln/| n. |窑，焙炉|A kiln is a furnace or oven for burning, baking, or drying something, especially bricks or pottery.|例句：The potter carefully placed the clay pots in the kiln for firing. 陶工小心翼翼地将陶罐放入窑中烧制。  The bricks were fired in a large, industrial kiln. 这些砖是在一个大型工业窑中烧制的。|近义词：oven, furnace|反义词：无
