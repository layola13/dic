sesame:/ˈsɛsəmi/| n. |芝麻；芝麻酱；芝麻油 |A small, oily seed that is used to make sesame oil and tahini.  Sesame seeds are also used as a topping for bread and other foods. |例句：The bread was sprinkled with sesame seeds. 这面包撒了芝麻。

例句：I love the taste of sesame oil in Asian cuisine. 我喜欢亚洲菜肴中芝麻油的味道。


近义词：None (no direct synonym, but other seeds or oil could be considered substitutes depending on context).|反义词：None |词性:Noun
