pleaders:/ˈpliːdərz/| n. |恳求者；辩护律师；代祷者|Those who plead, especially in a court of law.  Pleaders advocate for their clients, presenting arguments and evidence to support their case.  The term can also refer to those who earnestly request or implore something.|例句：The skilled pleaders presented compelling arguments to the judge. 熟练的辩护律师向法官提出了令人信服的论点。

The passionate pleaders urged the council to reconsider its decision.  充满激情的恳求者敦促委员会重新考虑其决定。|近义词：advocates, lawyers, supplicants, petitioners|反义词：opponents, adversaries, defendants (in some contexts)
