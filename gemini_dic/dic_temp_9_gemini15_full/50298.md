deplanes:/dɪˈpleɪnz/| v.|下飞机；乘客离开飞机|To disembark from an airplane.|The passengers deplaned quickly and efficiently after the flight landed.  乘客在飞机降落后迅速有效地下了飞机。|disembark, alight|board, embark|动词
