shellacing: /ʃɛˈlækiŋ/ | n. v. | 涂漆；压制；彻底击败 |

英文解析:Shellacking refers to the act of coating something with shellac, a resinous substance secreted by insects.  Figuratively, it means to decisively defeat or overwhelm someone or something.  As a noun, it's the act of shellacking; as a verb, it's the action of doing so.

例句:

The antique furniture was given a beautiful sheen after shellacking. = 古董家具涂上清漆后呈现出美丽的亮泽。

The team suffered a shellacking in the final game, losing by a score of 50-10. = 该队在决赛中惨败，以50比10的比分输掉了比赛。


近义词: (verb)  coating, varnishing, defeating decisively, overwhelming, trouncing; (noun)  coating, defeat, drubbing


反义词: (verb)  repairing, restoring, losing to; (noun) victory, triumph


词性: noun, verb
