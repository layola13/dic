overidealize: /ˌoʊvərˈaɪdiəlˌaɪz/ | vt. | 过分理想化；理想化过头 | To have an unrealistic or excessively positive view of someone or something.  | 例句：He overidealized his childhood, forgetting the hardships he faced. 他过分理想化了他的童年，忘记了他所面临的困难。

He overidealizes his relationship, ignoring the flaws. 他对自己的关系过于理想化，忽略了其中的缺点。


近义词：romanticize, idealize excessively,  put on a pedestal


反义词：be realistic about, be pragmatic about,  see clearly


词性：动词
