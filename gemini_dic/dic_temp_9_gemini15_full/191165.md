scaredy:/ˈskɛrdi/| n.|胆小鬼，懦夫|A scaredy-cat is someone who is easily frightened.|例句：He's such a scaredy-cat; he wouldn't even go near the spider.|近义词：coward, chicken,fraidy-cat|反义词:brave, courageous.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a scaredy-cat. = 他是个胆小鬼。
She is scaredy about the dark. = 她害怕黑暗。


一般过去时:
He was a scaredy-cat when he was a child. = 他小时候是个胆小鬼。
She was scaredy of the loud noise. = 她害怕巨大的噪音。


现在完成时:
He has always been a scaredy-cat. = 他一直是个胆小鬼。
She has never gotten over being scaredy of dogs. = 她一直克服不了对狗的恐惧。


过去完成时:
He had been a scaredy-cat all his life. = 他一辈子都是个胆小鬼。
She had always been scaredy of heights before that experience. =在那次经历之前，她一直害怕高空。


将来时:
He will probably remain a scaredy-cat. = 他可能仍然是个胆小鬼。
She will likely still be scaredy of thunderstorms. = 她很可能仍然害怕雷雨。
