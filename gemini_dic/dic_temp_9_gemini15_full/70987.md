firestorm:/ˈfaɪəˌstɔːrm/|/ˈfaɪərˌstɔːrm/| n. |火风暴；猛烈批评；强烈抗议|A firestorm is a rapidly spreading and intensely hot wildfire, often accompanied by strong winds and intense heat.  It can also refer to a sudden and intense outburst of public anger or criticism.  |例句：The forest fire quickly escalated into a devastating firestorm. 这场森林火灾迅速升级为一场毁灭性的火风暴。

The controversial decision sparked a firestorm of protest.  这一有争议的决定引发了强烈抗议。|近义词：wildfire, conflagration, inferno, uproar, outcry |反义词：calm, peace, quiet
