pavise:/ˈpeɪvɪs/| n.|盾牌；防护物|A large shield, typically used in the 14th and 15th centuries, often carried by a soldier to protect himself and others.  It was often large enough to cover multiple men.|Example: The pavise shielded the archers from enemy fire.  这面盾牌保护弓箭手免受敌人的攻击。|近义词：shield, buckler,  protection|反义词：exposure, vulnerability|名词
