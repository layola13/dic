engrave:/ɪnˈɡreɪv/ | vt. |雕刻，铭刻 | To cut or carve words, designs, etc. into a hard surface.  |  The artist engraved his name on the base of the sculpture. 艺术家将他的名字刻在雕塑的底座上。 | carve, etch, inscribe | erase, obliterate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He engraves beautiful designs onto wood. 他在木头上雕刻美丽的图案。
The artist engraves intricate patterns onto metal. 艺术家在金属上雕刻复杂的图案。

一般过去时:
They engraved their wedding date on their rings. 他们把结婚日期刻在戒指上。
She engraved a special message on the locket. 她把一条特别的信息刻在锁匙牌上。

现在完成时:
He has engraved hundreds of plates in his career. 他在他的职业生涯中已经雕刻了数百块版。
The company has engraved thousands of trophies this year.  公司今年已经雕刻了数千个奖杯。

过去完成时:
Before the ceremony, they had engraved the names on the plaques. 典礼之前，他们已经把名字刻在了牌匾上。
She had engraved a message on the silver heart before giving it as a gift.  送礼物之前，她在银质心形饰品上刻了一条信息。


将来时:
We will engrave your custom design on the ring. 我们会在戒指上刻上您定制的设计。
The company will engrave the logo on all the products. 公司将在所有产品上刻上标志。

虚拟语气:
If I had the tools, I would engrave a message on the stone. 如果我有工具，我会在石头上刻字。
I wish I could engrave my own jewelry. 我希望我能自己刻珠宝。

被动语态:
The names were engraved onto the tombstone. 名字被刻在了墓碑上。
The trophy is being engraved with the winner's name. 奖杯正在刻上获胜者的名字。

疑问句:
Can you engrave a picture on this watch? 你能在这块手表上刻画图案吗？
Will they engrave the date on the back of the watch?  他们会在手表的背面刻上日期吗？

否定句:
I cannot engrave that design; it's too intricate. 我不能刻那个图案；它太复杂了。
They did not engrave the inscription correctly. 他们没有正确地刻上铭文。
