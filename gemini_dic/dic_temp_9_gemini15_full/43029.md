cowerer:/ˈkaʊərər/ | n. |畏缩者，胆小鬼 | A person who habitually cowers; someone who shows fear and timidity.  |The cowerer hid behind the sofa during the thunderstorm.  这个畏缩者在雷雨期间躲在沙发后面。|近义词：coward, poltroon, craven |反义词：brave, courageous, bold | 名词
