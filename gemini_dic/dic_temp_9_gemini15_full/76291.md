gabber:/ˈɡæbər/| n. |喋喋不休的人；（尤指）饶舌乐迷| A gabber is someone who talks a lot, especially someone who talks incessantly and rapidly.  The term is also used to refer to a fan of gabber music, a fast-paced hardcore electronic music genre originating in the Netherlands.|例句：He's a real gabber; he never stops talking. 他是个真正的喋喋不休的人；他从不停下说话。

例句：The concert was packed with gabbers, all bouncing to the frenetic beats. 音乐会上挤满了饶舌乐迷，所有人都随着狂热的节奏跳动。

近义词：chatterbox, blabbermouth, talker, (for music fan) hardcore fan

反义词：quiet person, listener
