It is used as a synonym for Christmas, often appearing in Christmas carols and greetings.  |例句：We sang carols of Noel on Christmas Eve.  我们在平安夜唱了圣诞颂歌。  例句：The church bells rang out a joyful Noel. 教堂的钟声响起了欢乐的圣诞节。|近义词：Christmas, Yuletide |反义词：None  |名词
