sloppiest: /ˈslɒpiɪst/ | adj. | 最邋遢的，最马虎的 | Most careless, untidy, or negligent.  It is the superlative form of "sloppy".|例句：His sloppiest work was still better than anyone else's. 他的最粗糙的作品仍然比其他任何人的都好。

例句：The sloppiest handwriting I've ever seen made the letter nearly illegible. 我见过的最潦草的字迹使这封信几乎无法辨认。


近义词：most careless, most untidy, most negligent, laziest, most haphazard


反义词：most meticulous, most careful, most tidy, most organized


词性：形容词
