ravingly:/ˈreɪvɪŋli/| adv.|狂热地，极度地，令人惊叹地 |extremely enthusiastically or intensely; in a way that is wildly enthusiastic or excited.  |例句：He raved about the concert, describing it as ravingly good. 他对这场音乐会赞不绝口，称其好得令人难以置信。  |例句：The critics raved about the performance; it was ravingly brilliant. 评论家们对这场演出赞不绝口；它精彩绝伦。|近义词：enthusiastically, passionately, intensely, wildly |反义词：indifferently, apathetically, calmly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He raves about his new car ravingly. = 他对他的新车赞不绝口。
She raves about the food ravingly. = 她对食物赞不绝口。

一般过去时:
He raved about the movie ravingly yesterday. = 昨天他对这部电影赞不绝口。
She raved about the book ravingly last night. = 昨晚她对这本书赞不绝口。


现在完成时:
He has raved about that band ravingly for years. = 多年来他一直对那个乐队赞不绝口。
She has raved about this restaurant ravingly since she discovered it. = 自从她发现这家餐馆以来，她就一直对它赞不绝口。


过去完成时:
He had raved about the play ravingly before we even saw it. = 我们还没看演出之前，他就已经对它赞不绝口了。
She had raved about the artist ravingly before the exhibition. = 展览之前，她就对这位艺术家赞不绝口了。


将来时:
He will rave about the trip ravingly when he gets back. = 他回来后会对这次旅行赞不绝口。
She will rave about the concert ravingly tomorrow. = 她明天会对这场音乐会赞不绝口。


虚拟语气:
If he saw the show, he would rave about it ravingly. = 如果他看了演出，他会对它赞不绝口。
If she tasted the wine, she would rave about it ravingly. = 如果她尝了酒，她会对它赞不绝口。


被动语态: (This adverb is rarely used in passive voice, it's more naturally used to describe an active enthusiastic expression)
The concert was described ravingly by the critics. = 音乐会受到了评论家的极力赞扬。(This is less natural than the active voice)
The food was spoken of ravingly by the diners. = 食客们对食物赞不绝口。(This is less natural than the active voice)


疑问句: (Not naturally used in questions)
Does he rave about everything ravingly? = 他对所有事情都赞不绝口吗？ (awkward)
Does she describe everything ravingly? = 她对所有事情都赞不绝口吗？ (awkward)


否定句:
He didn't rave about the film ravingly. = 他并没有对这部电影赞不绝口。
She didn't rave about the performance ravingly. = 她并没有对这场演出赞不绝口。
