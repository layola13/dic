clearheaded: /ˌklɪrˈhedɪd/ | adj. |头脑清醒的；思路清晰的 | Having a clear and rational mind; not confused or muddled.  |例句：Despite the chaos, she remained clearheaded and made sound decisions. 尽管一片混乱，她仍然头脑清醒，做出了正确的决定。 |近义词：rational, lucid, sane, composed |反义词：confused, muddled, bewildered, disoriented


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is clearheaded in his approach to problem-solving. = 他解决问题的方法很清晰。
She remains clearheaded even under pressure. = 即使在压力下，她仍然保持头脑清醒。


一般过去时:
He was clearheaded enough to avoid the trap. = 他头脑清醒，避免了陷阱。
She was clearheaded about her goals. = 她对自己的目标很清楚。


现在完成时:
He has remained clearheaded throughout the crisis. = 在整个危机中，他一直保持头脑清醒。
She has always been clearheaded in her judgments. = 她一直以来判断都很清晰。


过去完成时:
He had been clearheaded before the accident, but afterwards he was disoriented. = 事故发生前他头脑清醒，但之后他迷失了方向。
She had been clearheaded about the risks involved. = 她对所涉及的风险十分清楚。


将来时:
He will be clearheaded enough to make the right decision. = 他将保持头脑清醒，做出正确的决定。
She will remain clearheaded even in the face of adversity. = 她即使面对逆境也能保持头脑清醒。


虚拟语气:
If he were clearheaded, he wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果他头脑清醒，就不会犯那个错误了。
If she had been clearheaded, she would have seen the danger. = 如果她头脑清醒，就能看到危险了。


被动语态:  (This adjective is rarely used in passive voice)
His thinking was described as clearheaded and logical. = 他的思维被形容为清晰而逻辑。
The plan was considered clearheaded and practical. = 这个计划被认为是清晰而实用的。


疑问句:
Is he clearheaded enough to handle this situation? = 他头脑清醒到足以应付这种情况吗？
Was she clearheaded when she made that decision? = 她做那个决定的时候头脑清醒吗？


否定句:
He wasn't clearheaded enough to understand the instructions. = 他头脑不够清醒，无法理解这些指示。
She wasn't clearheaded at the time of the accident. = 她出事的时候头脑不清醒。
