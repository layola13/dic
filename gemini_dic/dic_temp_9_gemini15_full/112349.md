lorikeets:/ˈlɔːrɪkiːts/| n. |吸蜜鹦鹉|Small to medium-sized parrots found in Australia, New Guinea, and surrounding islands, known for their brightly colored plumage and long, brush-tipped tongues used for feeding on nectar, pollen, and soft fruits.|Example:  The lorikeets were a vibrant splash of color amongst the green foliage.  吸蜜鹦鹉在绿叶丛中构成了一幅鲜艳的色彩画面。|近义词：parrots, honeyeaters|反义词：None.
