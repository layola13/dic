evolutions:/ˌɛvəˈluːʃənz/ | n. |进化；发展；演变 |The gradual development of something, especially from a simple to a more complex form.  It refers to the process of change over time, often involving a series of gradual steps.| 例句：The evolutions of the human brain are a fascinating subject. 人类大脑的进化是一个令人着迷的话题。

The evolutions in technology have made our lives easier. 技术的进步使我们的生活更加便利。


近义词：developments, advancements, transformations, progressions

反义词：regressions, deteriorations, declines
