photosynthesizing:/ˌfoʊtoʊˈsɪnθəsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |光合作用的；进行光合作用的 | The process by which green plants and some other organisms use sunlight to synthesize foods from carbon dioxide and water.  Photosynthesis in plants generally involves the green pigment chlorophyll and generates oxygen as a byproduct. | 例句：The plants are photosynthesizing in the sunlight. 植物在阳光下进行光合作用。  The leaves are actively photosynthesizing, producing oxygen. 叶子正在活跃地进行光合作用，产生氧气。| 近义词：photosynthesizing, producing food through photosynthesis | 反义词：respiring (in the context of opposite metabolic processes)
