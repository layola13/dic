sippling:/ˈsɪplɪŋ/| n.|细细地啜饮；小口喝|The act of sipping something slowly and delicately.  It often implies a small amount of liquid being consumed in a refined manner.|例句：She enjoyed the sippling of her fine wine. 她享受着细细品尝美酒的乐趣。
例句：The child was happily sippling their juice.  孩子正快乐地细细啜饮果汁。|近义词：sipping, drinking, tasting|反义词：gulping, chugging|名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sips his tea every morning. = 他每天早上都细细品茶。
She sips her coffee slowly. = 她慢慢地啜饮咖啡。

一般过去时:
He sipped his wine throughout the dinner. = 他在整个晚餐期间都小口喝着葡萄酒。
She sipped the tea carefully. = 她小心翼翼地啜着茶。

现在完成时:
I have sipped this tea several times. = 我已经啜饮了好几次这种茶了。
She has sipped all of her water. = 她已经把所有的水都喝完了。

过去完成时:
He had sipped the entire glass before I arrived. = 我到之前他已经喝完了整杯酒。
She had sipped the soup slowly before speaking. = 她慢慢地喝完汤后才开始说话。

将来时:
I will sip my cocktail at the party. = 我将在派对上啜饮我的鸡尾酒。
He will sip his drink carefully. = 他将仔细地啜饮他的饮料。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would sip my coffee leisurely. = 如果我有更多时间，我会悠闲地啜饮咖啡。
I wish I could sip this delicious wine all day. = 我希望我能够一整天都啜饮这种美味的葡萄酒。

被动语态: (This verb doesn't typically use the passive voice naturally)
The wine was being slowly sipped by the guest. =  客人在慢慢地啜饮葡萄酒。（不太自然）
The drink was sipped delicately. =  饮料被细细地啜饮。（不太自然）


疑问句:
Are you sipping your tea? = 你在喝茶吗？
Will you sip the wine with me? = 你愿意和我一起喝这杯酒吗？


否定句:
He did not sip his coffee this morning. = 他今天早上没有喝咖啡。
She doesn't sip her drinks quickly. = 她喝饮料不快。

PHRASE->
sip by sip = 一口一口地

sip quietly = 安静地啜饮
