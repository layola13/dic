regrede:/rɪˈɡrɛd/ | v. | 后退；倒退；退化 | To move or go back to an earlier or less advanced state; to revert.  |例句：The company regressed to its old, inefficient methods. 公司又回到了它以前低效的方法。|例句：His health regressed after a period of improvement. 他的健康状况在好转了一段时间后又恶化了。|近义词：revert, decline, deteriorate |反义词：progress, advance, improve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He regresses under pressure. = 他在压力下退缩。
The economy is regressing. = 经济正在倒退。


一般过去时:
The patient regressed after the surgery. = 患者手术后病情恶化了。
The team regressed in the second half of the game. = 球队在下半场表现退步了。


现在完成时:
The country has regressed significantly since the coup. = 自政变以来，这个国家已经严重倒退了。
His behavior has regressed to that of a child. = 他的行为已经退化到像个孩子一样了。


过去完成时:
They had regressed to their old habits by the end of the year. = 到年底他们又回到了老习惯。
The project had regressed significantly due to funding cuts. = 由于资金削减，该项目已经严重倒退。


将来时:
The situation will regress further without intervention. = 如果不干预，情况将会进一步恶化。
We predict the market will regress next quarter. = 我们预测市场在下个季度将会回落。


虚拟语气:
If he had not regressed, he would have won the race. = 如果他没有退步，他就会赢得比赛。
I wish he had not regressed in his studies. = 我希望他的学习没有退步。


被动语态:
The project was regressed to an earlier version. = 项目被回退到早期版本。
The symptoms were regressed by the new medication. = 新药使症状减轻了。


疑问句:
Has the disease regressed? = 疾病是否好转了？
Did his skills regress after the injury? = 他受伤后技术是否退步了？


否定句:
The symptoms did not regress despite treatment. = 尽管接受了治疗，症状并没有好转。
The company refused to regress to its old methods. = 公司拒绝回到它以前的方法。


PHRASE->
regress to the mean  = 回归平均值
regress towards the mean =  向平均值回归
regress on =  对...进行回归分析
regress against =  对...进行回归分析 (与regress on 同义)
regressive taxation =  累退税制
