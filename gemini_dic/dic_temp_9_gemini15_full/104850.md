keeling-over: /ˈkiːlɪŋ ˈoʊvər/ | v. |倾覆；突然倒下；突然死亡 | To fall over suddenly; to capsize; to die suddenly.  |例句：The strong winds caused the small boat to keel over.  强风导致小船倾覆。

The old tree keeled over in the storm.  那棵老树在暴风雨中倒塌了。


近义词：capsize, overturn, collapse, topple, succumb, expire |反义词：to right oneself, to stand upright, to survive
