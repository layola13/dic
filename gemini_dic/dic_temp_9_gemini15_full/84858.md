hanged-on:/hæŋt ˈɒn/|/ˈhæŋd ɑːn/| v. |坚持；依靠；附着；|To continue doing something despite difficulties or to rely on someone or something for support.  It can also mean to cling to something physically. |例句：He hanged on to the rope for dear life. 他紧紧抓住绳子，拼命保命。

例句：Despite the setbacks, she hanged on to her dream. 尽管遭遇挫折，她仍然坚持自己的梦想。


近义词：persist, persevere, cling, depend, rely


反义词：give up, abandon, let go


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hangs on to his beliefs. = 他坚持自己的信仰。
She hangs on to every word he says. = 她认真听着他说的每一个字。


一般过去时:
He hanged on to the branch until help arrived. = 他抓住树枝直到有人来帮忙。
They hanged on to their hope even in the face of adversity. = 即使面对逆境，他们仍然坚持希望。


现在完成时:
She has hanged on to that old coat for years. = 她已经留着那件旧外套好几年了。
He has hanged on to his job despite the company's difficulties. = 尽管公司遇到困难，他仍然保住了工作。


过去完成时:
They had hanged on to the idea for months before finally giving up. = 他们在放弃之前，已经坚持这个想法好几个月了。
She had hanged on to the hope of reconciliation, even after the breakup. = 即使分手后，她仍然抱有和好的希望。


将来时:
I will hang on to this information until the right time. = 我会保留这个信息直到合适的时候。
They will hang on to their seats for as long as the event lasts. = 他们会一直坐在那里，直到活动结束。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the truth earlier, I would have hanged on to my money. = 如果我早点知道真相，我就不会把钱花掉了。(这里hang on to表示"保留")
I wish I had hanged on to that opportunity. = 我真希望我当时抓住了那个机会。


被动语态:  The painting was hanged on to the wall. = 这幅画被挂在了墙上。(这里hang on to表示"挂着")  被动语态很少用于此义。


疑问句:
Will you hang on to my bag while I go to the restroom? = 我去洗手间的时候，你能帮我拿着包吗？
Did he hang on to his promise? = 他遵守诺言了吗？


否定句:
He didn't hang on to his anger for long. = 他并没有生气太久。
She didn't hang on to the idea of moving to London. = 她放弃了搬到伦敦的想法。


PHRASE->
hang on a minute = 等一下
hang on to something = 抓住某物；坚持某事
hang on in there = 坚持下去
hang on tight = 抓紧
hang on to your hat = （口语）准备吃惊
