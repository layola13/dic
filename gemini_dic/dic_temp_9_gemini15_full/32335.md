childbed:/ˈtʃaɪldbed/| n. |产褥期；分娩后的床位|The period of time immediately following childbirth, during which a woman recovers physically and emotionally.  It also refers to the bed in which a woman lies after giving birth.|例句：She spent a week in childbed after the birth of her twins. 她生下双胞胎后在产褥期住了一个星期。|例句：The nurse carefully prepared the childbed for the new mother. 护士仔细地为这位新妈妈准备了产床。|近义词：postpartum period, confinement|反义词：无
