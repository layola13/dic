inedited:/ˌɪnˈedɪtɪd/ | adj. | 未编辑的；未发表的 | Unedited material is in its original form, without any changes or corrections having been made.  It is often used to describe manuscripts, recordings, or other works that have not been prepared for publication or distribution. | Example: The author released an inedited version of the manuscript to a select group of critics.  作者向一小部分评论家发布了未经编辑的手稿。 | 近义词：unedited, raw, unpolished | 反义词：edited, published, polished
