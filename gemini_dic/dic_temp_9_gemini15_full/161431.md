postviral: /ˌpoʊstvɑɪrəl/ | adj. |病毒后的，病毒感染后的|Relating to or occurring after a viral infection.  It often refers to the lingering symptoms or complications that can persist after the initial viral infection has subsided. |例句：Many people experience postviral fatigue for weeks after recovering from the flu. 许多人在流感痊愈后几周内都会经历病毒后疲劳。

例句：She suffered from postviral neurological symptoms following a bout of chickenpox. 她患水痘后出现病毒后神经系统症状。


近义词：postinfectious, post-infection


反义词：previral (not commonly used)


词性：形容词
