erected:/ɪˈrektɪd/| v.|竖立；建造；使勃起|To set upright; to construct or build; to cause (an organ) to become erect.|例句：They erected a statue in the park.他们在公园里竖立了一座雕像。They erected a new building last year.他们去年建了一栋新楼。|近义词：constructed, built, raised, established|反义词：demolished, destroyed, razed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He erects buildings for a living. = 他以建造建筑物为生。
The company erects communication towers across the country. = 这家公司在全国各地建造通讯塔。

一般过去时:
They erected a monument to honor the fallen soldiers. = 他们竖立了一座纪念碑来纪念阵亡的士兵。
The workers erected the scaffolding before starting the repairs. = 工人们在开始修理之前搭建了脚手架。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

The city will erect a new stadium for the upcoming games. = 该城市将为即将到来的比赛建造一个新的体育场。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
