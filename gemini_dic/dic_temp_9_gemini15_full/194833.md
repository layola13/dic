semen: /ˈsiːmən/ | n. |精液|Semen is the fluid containing spermatozoa that is ejaculated by the male during sexual intercourse. It is a complex mixture of fluids from various glands, including the seminal vesicles, prostate gland, and bulbourethral glands, and contains millions of sperm cells.|Example:  The analysis of the semen sample revealed the presence of sperm.  精液样本分析显示存在精子。|近义词：sperm (less formal), seminal fluid|反义词：无
