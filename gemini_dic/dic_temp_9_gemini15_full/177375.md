reauthenticate:/ˌriːɔːˈθɛntɪkeɪt/| vt.|重新验证，重新认证|To verify or authenticate something again.  This typically involves a second or repeated process of confirming identity or validity.|例句：You will need to reauthenticate your account after 30 days of inactivity.  你需要在帐户闲置30天后重新验证你的帐户。

例句：The system prompted me to reauthenticate using two-factor authentication. 系统提示我使用双因素身份验证进行重新验证。


近义词：reverify, revalidate, confirm again|反义词：deauthorize, invalidate


词性：动词
