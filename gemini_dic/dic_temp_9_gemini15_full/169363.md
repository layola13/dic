promulgate:/ˈprɒmʌlɡeɪt/| vt.|颁布；公布；宣传|To make (a law, belief, etc.) widely known; to disseminate or promote (an idea or cause)|The government will promulgate new environmental regulations next month.  政府下个月将颁布新的环境法规。|The company worked hard to promulgate its new product. 公司努力宣传其新产品。|disseminate, propagate, broadcast|suppress, conceal, hide|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government promulgates new laws regularly. = 政府定期颁布新法律。
She promulgates her ideas through her writing. = 她通过写作传播她的想法。


一般过去时:
The king promulgated a new decree. = 国王颁布了一道新的法令。
They promulgated the news throughout the city. = 他们在全城传播了这个消息。


现在完成时:
The organization has promulgated its mission statement globally. = 该组织已在全球范围内公布其使命宣言。
They have promulgated their findings in several peer-reviewed journals. = 他们在几家同行评审期刊上发表了他们的研究结果。


过去完成时:
Before the war, they had promulgated a peace treaty. = 在战争爆发前，他们已经公布了一项和平条约。
The company had promulgated its new marketing strategy before the product launch. = 公司在产品发布之前已经公布了新的营销策略。


将来时:
The president will promulgate the new economic policy next week. = 总统下周将颁布新的经济政策。
They will promulgate their research results at the conference. = 他们将在会议上公布他们的研究结果。


虚拟语气:
If the government promulgated stricter laws, pollution would decrease. = 如果政府颁布更严格的法律，污染就会减少。
I wish they would promulgate their findings more widely. = 我希望他们能更广泛地传播他们的研究结果。


被动语态:
The new regulations were promulgated last year. = 新规是去年颁布的。
The company's vision statement is being promulgated through various channels. = 公司的愿景声明正在通过各种渠道传播。


疑问句:
When will the court promulgate its decision? = 法院何时会公布其判决？
How effectively are the new guidelines being promulgated? = 新指南的传播效果如何？


否定句:
The government did not promulgate the bill. = 政府没有颁布该法案。
They failed to promulgate the information to the public effectively. = 他们未能有效地向公众传播信息。


PHRASE->

promulgate a law = 颁布法律
promulgate a doctrine = 传播教义
promulgate an idea = 传播思想
promulgate a policy = 公布政策
promulgate a decision = 公布决定
