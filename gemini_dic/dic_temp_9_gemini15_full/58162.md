drouthy:/ˈdraʊθi/| adj.|口渴的；渴望的|Thirsty; eager or desirous.  The word implies a strong, almost desperate thirst, often extending beyond a simple physical need for water to a yearning for something else.|例句：The long trek across the desert left them drouthy and exhausted. 长途跋涉穿过沙漠使他们又渴又累。

例句：She was drouthy for knowledge, devouring books day and night. 她渴望知识，日夜阅读书籍。


近义词：thirsty, parched, arid (referring to a place), eager, yearning


反义词：quenched, satisfied, satiated


词性：形容词
