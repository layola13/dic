crosswalk:/ˈkrɒsˌwɔːk/ /ˈkrɑːsˌwɔːk/| n. |人行横道，斑马线 |A marked pedestrian crossing on a road.  It is usually marked with white lines or stripes. |例句：Pedestrians should use the crosswalk to cross the street safely. 行人应该使用人行横道安全过街。

例句：The children waited patiently at the crosswalk before crossing the busy street. 孩子们在繁忙的街道上耐心等待人行横道过马路。


近义词：pedestrian crossing, zebra crossing | 反义词：None  | 词性：名词

词源:
The word "crosswalk" is a compound word.  "Cross" refers to the act of moving from one side to another, and "walk" refers to the pedestrian movement.  The combination indicates a designated area for pedestrians to cross a road.
