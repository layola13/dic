lumpish:/ˈlʌmpɪʃ/| adj.|笨拙的；迟钝的；呆板的|Characterized by clumsiness or lack of grace; slow-moving and unresponsive.  It can also describe something that is dull or uninspired.|例句：He moved in a lumpish, awkward manner. 他笨拙地、不灵活地移动着。

例句：The presentation was lumpish and lacked any real dynamism.  这次演示很呆板，缺乏活力。


近义词：clumsy, awkward, sluggish, slow, dull, heavy, lethargic|反义词：graceful, nimble, agile, quick, lively, bright, energetic|词性:形容词
