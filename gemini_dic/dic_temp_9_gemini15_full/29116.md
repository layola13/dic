cascades:/kæˈskeɪdz/ | n. v. | 瀑布；层叠；大量涌现 | a series of waterfalls, one flowing into another; something that resembles this; to fall or flow like a cascade | 例句：Water cascaded down the mountainside.  水从山坡上倾泻而下。  The news cascaded across the internet.  新闻在互联网上迅速传播。 | 近义词：waterfall, torrent, deluge | 反义词：trickle, dribble


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Water cascades down the mountain. = 水从山上倾泻而下。
The children cascade into the playground. = 孩子们蜂拥进入操场。


一般过去时:
The waterfall cascaded beautifully yesterday. = 瀑布昨天景色优美地倾泻而下。
The information cascaded across the news channels. = 信息在新闻频道上迅速传播。


现在完成时:
The rain has cascaded down the streets all day. = 雨水一整天都倾泻在大街上。
Problems have cascaded upon the company since the merger. = 自从合并以来，问题接踵而至。


过去完成时:
The river had cascaded over the rocks before the dam was built. = 在大坝建成之前，河水已经翻越了岩石。
The news of the scandal had cascaded through the company before the meeting. = 丑闻的消息在会议之前就已经在公司内部迅速传播。


将来时:
The water will cascade down the newly built dam. = 水将从新建的大坝上倾泻而下。
We expect that new orders will cascade in after the product launch. = 我们预计产品发布后将有大量新订单涌入。


虚拟语气:
If the dam broke, the water would cascade down the valley. = 如果大坝决堤，水就会倾泻而下。
If only the problems hadn’t cascaded, the project would have been successful. = 要是没有出现这么多问题，项目本来会成功的。


被动语态:
The rocks were cascaded by the powerful waterfall. =  岩石被强大的瀑布冲刷。
The information was cascaded through email to all employees. = 信息通过电子邮件发送给了所有员工。


疑问句:
Does the water cascade all year round? = 水一年四季都倾泻而下吗？
Will the new features cascade across all our platforms? = 新功能会覆盖我们所有的平台吗？


否定句:
The water did not cascade down the dry riverbed. = 水没有从干涸的河床上倾泻而下。
The information didn't cascade quickly enough to prevent the crisis. = 信息传播的速度不够快，无法阻止危机。


PHRASE->

a cascade of flowers = 一串花
a cascade of light = 一帘光
a cascade of emotions = 一涌而出的情绪
a cascade of data = 大量数据
