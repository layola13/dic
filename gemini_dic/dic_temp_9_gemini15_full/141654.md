optimized:/ˈɒptɪmaɪzd/ /ˈɑːptɪmaɪzd/ | adj. | 优化的；最佳化的 |  Made as effective, functional, or efficient as possible.  This usually involves improving something by making small changes. | 例句：The software has been optimized for speed.  软件已被优化以提高速度。

例句：The algorithm has been optimized to reduce processing time. 算法已被优化以减少处理时间。


近义词：improved, refined, enhanced, perfected | 反义词：unoptimized, suboptimal, inefficient

词源:
The word "optimized" comes from the verb "optimize," which itself is derived from the word "optimum" (Latin for "best").  The suffix "-ize" is added to create a verb meaning "to make optimum."  Therefore, "optimized" means "made to be the best or most effective."
