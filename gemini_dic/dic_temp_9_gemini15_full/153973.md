perfectionists:/pərˈfɛkʃənɪsts/| n. |完美主义者|People with a strong desire to achieve flawlessness; those who have extremely high standards and are often critical of themselves and others for not meeting those standards.  They may struggle with procrastination and dissatisfaction due to their inability to achieve their idealized goals.|He's a perfectionist, constantly striving for flawlessness in his work, often to the detriment of his own well-being. 他是一个完美主义者，不断追求工作上的完美，常常以牺牲自己的幸福为代价。|近义词：idealists, high-achievers|反义词：slovenly, negligent|名词
