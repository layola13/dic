cotyledonary:/ˌkɒtɪˈliːdənəri/| adj.| 子叶的；双子叶的 | Relating to or characteristic of cotyledons.  Cotyledons are the embryonic leaves in seed plants.  The term often implies the presence of two cotyledons, as in dicotyledonous plants. | Example: The cotyledonary leaves of the bean plant provide initial nourishment for the seedling.  豆类植物的子叶为幼苗提供最初的营养。|近义词：embryonic, seminal |反义词：mature, developed
