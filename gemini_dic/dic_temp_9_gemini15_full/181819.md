relisher:/ˈrɛlɪʃər/ | n. |  爱好者；品味者；享受者 | One who relishes something; one who takes great pleasure in something.  |  He's a true relish of fine wines. 他是个真正的美酒爱好者。  |  enthusiast, connoisseur, aficionado |  indifferent, apathetic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He relishes the challenge. = 他喜欢这个挑战。
She relishes her work. = 她喜欢她的工作。

一般过去时:
They relished the delicious meal. = 他们享受了美味的一餐。
He relished the opportunity to travel. = 他珍惜旅行的机会。

现在完成时:
I have relished every moment of this trip. = 我享受了这次旅行的每一刻。
She has relished the success of her project. = 她为她项目的成功而感到高兴。

过去完成时:
They had relished the quiet evening before the storm broke. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们享受了宁静的夜晚。
She had relished the anticipation of the party. = 她享受了派对之前的期待。

将来时:
We will relish the fruits of our labor. = 我们将享受我们劳动的果实。
He will relish the opportunity to meet the author. = 他将珍惜有机会见到这位作者。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would relish the opportunity to paint. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会享受绘画的机会。
I wish I could relish the quiet moments more often. = 我希望我能更经常地享受宁静的时光。

被动语态:
The opportunity was relished by all the participants. = 所有参与者都珍惜这个机会。

疑问句:

否定句:
