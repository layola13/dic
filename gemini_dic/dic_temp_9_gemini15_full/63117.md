ensile:/ˈɛnsaɪl/| adj.|青贮的；用作青贮饲料的|Relating to or denoting the process of preserving green fodder (such as grass or clover) in airtight conditions to prevent decay.  Ensilage is the preserved fodder itself.|The farmer uses an ensile system to preserve his corn for winter feed.|近义词：preserved, stored|反义词：fresh, unpreserved
