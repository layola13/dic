ends:/ɛndz/| n. v.|结尾；终点；目的；结束；终止|The ends of the rope were frayed.绳子的两端磨损了。  The meeting came to an abrupt end.会议突然结束了。|近义词：finish, conclusion, termination|反义词：beginning, start, commencement|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project ends next week. = 项目下周结束。
This road ends at the river. = 这条路在河边结束。


一般过去时:
The movie ended at midnight. = 电影在午夜结束。
The party ended early. = 聚会早早结束了。


现在完成时:
The meeting has ended. = 会议已经结束了。
The rain has finally ended. = 雨终于停了。


过去完成时:
The game had ended before we arrived. = 我们到达之前比赛已经结束了。
She had ended her speech before the questions began. = 她演讲结束后才开始提问。


将来时:
The concert will end at 10 pm. = 音乐会将在晚上10点结束。
The semester will end in June. = 学期将在六月结束。


虚拟语气:
If the war ended tomorrow, many people would be relieved. = 如果战争明天结束，许多人都会松一口气。
I wish this difficult period would end soon. = 我希望这段艰难时期能尽快结束。


被动语态:
The meeting was ended prematurely. = 会议提前结束了。
The project will be ended by the end of the year. = 项目将在年底结束。


疑问句:
When does the show end? = 节目几点结束？
Where does this path end? = 这条路通向哪里？


否定句:
The game didn't end until late. = 比赛直到很晚才结束。
The meeting hasn't ended yet. = 会议还没结束。


PHRASE->
make ends meet = 收支平衡
come to an end = 结束
bring something to an end = 结束某事
put an end to something = 结束某事
on the ends of the earth = 在地球的尽头


词源:
The word "ends" originates from the Old English word "endan," meaning "to finish, to end."  This, in turn, has roots in Proto-Germanic and further back into Proto-Indo-European.  The noun form developed naturally from the verb's meaning.
