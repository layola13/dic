misbestow:/ˌmɪsbɪˈstoʊ/| vt.|错置；滥用；误用|To bestow wrongly or inappropriately; to misuse or misapply.|例句：He misbestowed his talents on trivial pursuits. 他把自己的才能浪费在琐碎的事情上。 He misbestowed the company's funds. 他滥用了公司的资金。|近义词：misapply, misuse, misdirect, squander|反义词：bestow appropriately, utilize effectively, apply correctly|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They misbestow their time on useless activities. = 他们把时间浪费在无用的活动上。
He misbestows his energy on unimportant tasks. = 他把精力浪费在不重要的事情上。

一般过去时:
She misbestowed her inheritance on a failing business. = 她把遗产错误地投资到一家即将倒闭的企业。
The manager misbestowed the company's resources. = 经理滥用了公司的资源。

现在完成时:
They have misbestowed their efforts on a hopeless project. = 他们把精力浪费在一个毫无希望的项目上。
He has misbestowed his trust in the wrong person. = 他把信任错付给了错误的人。

过去完成时:
She realized she had misbestowed her affection on an unworthy person. = 她意识到她把感情错付给了不值得的人。
The government had misbestowed billions of dollars on ineffective programs. = 政府已将数十亿美元错误地用于无效的项目。


将来时:
He will misbestow his opportunity if he doesn't focus. = 如果他不集中精力，他会错过这次机会。(这句例句略微调整，因为misbestow本身不直接表达"错过机会"，而是指错误地使用机会)
They will misbestow their savings on unwise investments. = 他们会把积蓄浪费在不明智的投资上。

虚拟语气:
If he had not misbestowed his funds, he would be wealthy now. = 如果他没有滥用资金，他现在就会富有。
If she hadn't misbestowed her time, she would have finished the project. = 如果她没有浪费时间，她就能完成项目了。

被动语态:
The resources were misbestowed by the incompetent manager. = 资源被无能的经理滥用了。
His talents were misbestowed on a worthless cause. = 他的才能被浪费在一个毫无价值的事业上。

疑问句:
Were the funds misbestowed? = 资金是不是被滥用了？
Have the resources been misbestowed? = 资源是否被误用了？

否定句:
They did not misbestow their trust in their friends. = 他们没有错信他们的朋友。
He did not misbestow his time; he used it wisely. = 他没有浪费时间；他明智地利用了时间。
