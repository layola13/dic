dyes:/daɪz/| n.|染料；染色剂|Substances used to color fabrics, hair, etc.  They are available in a wide range of colors and are used in various industries, from textiles to food.|例句：The artist used vibrant dyes to create a stunning tapestry.  艺术家用鲜艳的染料创作了一幅令人惊叹的挂毯。

例句：She added food dyes to the cake batter to make it pink. 她往蛋糕糊里加了食用色素，让它变成粉红色。|近义词：colorants, pigments|反义词：bleaches|词性:名词

词源:
The word "dye" comes from the Old English word "dyggan," meaning "to dye."  This is related to the Proto-Germanic word "*deugan," with the same meaning, ultimately tracing back to Proto-Indo-European roots connected to concepts of soaking or steeping.
