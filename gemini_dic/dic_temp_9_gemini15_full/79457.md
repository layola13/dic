glitziest: /ˈɡlɪtsiːst/ | adj. | 最闪耀的；最华丽的 | Most glamorous or showy.  The word describes something that is extremely dazzling and ostentatious, often in a superficial way. It is the superlative form of "glitzy." |例句：The glitziest hotel in the city hosted the awards ceremony.  这座城市最豪华的酒店举办了颁奖典礼。


The glitziest fashion show of the year showcased the latest designs. 今年最华丽的时装秀展示了最新的设计。


近义词：most glamorous, most dazzling, most showy, most flamboyant, most spectacular


反义词：dullest, plainest, most understated, most simple


词源:  Derived from "glitzy," which itself is a relatively recent informal word, likely emerging in the mid-20th century.  "Glitzy" is formed from the word "glitter," which ultimately comes from Old English "gliten" meaning "to shine."  The "-iest" suffix creates the superlative form, indicating the highest degree.
