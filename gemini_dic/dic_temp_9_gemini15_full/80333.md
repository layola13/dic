goldfield:/ˈɡoʊldfiːld/| n.|金矿；金矿区|A goldfield is an area of land where gold is found and mined.  It often refers to a region known for its gold mining activity, potentially including towns and infrastructure built around the mining operations.|例句：The California Gold Rush transformed the goldfields into bustling towns. 加州淘金热将金矿区变成了繁华的城镇。

The Klondike Gold Rush led to the establishment of numerous settlements in the Yukon goldfields. 克朗代克淘金热导致育空金矿区建立了许多定居点。|近义词：gold mine, mining area|反义词：无
