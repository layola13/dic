dudgeon:/ˈdʌdʒən/| n.|愤慨；怒气|A feeling of resentment or indignation.|He left in a fit of dudgeon. 他怒气冲冲地离开了。|近义词：anger, resentment, indignation, offense|反义词：calmness, composure, placidity


USAGE->

She expresses her dudgeon openly. = 她公开表达她的愤怒。
