hieroglyphed:/ˈhaɪəroʊɡlɪft/| adj.| 象形文字书写的；用象形文字装饰的 | Covered or decorated with hieroglyphs; written in hieroglyphs.  |例句：The ancient Egyptian tomb was beautifully hieroglyphed.  古埃及的陵墓上刻着美丽的象形文字。
The papyrus scroll was hieroglyphed with intricate scenes of daily life.  莎草纸卷轴上用复杂的象形文字描绘了日常生活场景。|近义词：inscribed, decorated, embellished |反义词：plain, undecorated
