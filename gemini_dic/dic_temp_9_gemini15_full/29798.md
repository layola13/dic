cauda: /ˈkɔːdə/ | n. | 尾巴；尾部 | The hindmost part of an animal's body, typically extending beyond the trunk and ending in a tuft of hair or a brush.  It refers to a tail-like appendage on various organisms, structures, or things.  | Example: The dog wagged its cauda happily.  这条狗高兴地摇着尾巴。  |近义词: tail, appendage | 反义词: head, front
