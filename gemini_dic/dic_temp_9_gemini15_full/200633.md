slay:/sleɪ/| vt. n. | 杀害；彻底击败；惊艳；大获成功 | To kill (someone) violently.  To overcome or defeat decisively. To impress greatly; to be exceptionally stylish or successful. | 例句：The knight slayed the dragon.  = 骑士杀死了巨龙。

The team slayed their opponents. = 这支队伍彻底击败了他们的对手。


Her outfit was a slay. = 她的服装非常惊艳。

近义词:kill, defeat, vanquish, impress, wow | 反义词: fail, lose, disappoint
