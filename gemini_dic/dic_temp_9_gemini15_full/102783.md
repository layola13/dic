japing:/ˈdʒeɪpɪŋ/| v.|开玩笑，打趣|To speak or act playfully or teasingly; to jest.|例句：He was japing with his friends about the game. 他在和朋友们开玩笑，谈论比赛。  例句：Stop japing and tell me what's wrong.  别闹了，告诉我出了什么事。|近义词：joking, jesting, teasing|反义词：serious, solemn


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He japs with his colleagues every day. = 他每天都和同事开玩笑。
They are japing about the upcoming party. = 他们正在开玩笑谈论即将到来的派对。


一般过去时:
She japed about his new haircut. = 她取笑他的新发型。
We japed around until it was time to leave. = 我们一直打闹到该离开的时候。
