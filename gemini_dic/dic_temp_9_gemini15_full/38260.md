comradeships:/ˈkɒmˌrædʃɪps/| n.|同志情谊；友情；伙伴关系|The feeling of friendship and loyalty that exists between comrades.  It often implies a shared experience, goal, or struggle. |例句：Their comradeships forged in the heat of battle lasted a lifetime. 他们在激烈的战斗中建立的同志情谊持续了一生。

例句：The team developed strong comradeships through their collaborative work on the project. 通过在项目中的合作，团队建立了牢固的同志情谊。


近义词：friendship, camaraderie, fellowship, bond

反义词：estrangement, animosity, hostility


词性:名词
