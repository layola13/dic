do-it-yourself:/ˌduː ɪt jʊrˈsɛlf/| adj. n.|自己动手做的；自己动手；DIY|Describes something that is made or repaired by a person themselves, rather than by a professional.  It can also refer to the activity of doing things yourself.|例句：He's a real do-it-yourself enthusiast. 他是一个真正的DIY爱好者。


例句：She prefers do-it-yourself projects to hiring contractors. 她更喜欢自己动手做项目而不是雇佣承包商。


近义词：homemade, self-made, handmade


反义词：professionally made, commercially made


词性：adjective, noun
