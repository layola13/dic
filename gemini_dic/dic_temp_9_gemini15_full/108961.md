leers:/lɪər/| v.|斜视；色迷迷地看|To look at someone in a sexually suggestive way.  It implies a lascivious or contemptuous gaze.|例句：He leered at the women walking by. 他色迷迷地看着走过的女人们。
例句：She leered at him from across the room. 她从房间的另一边斜眼看着他。|近义词：ogle, leer at, gawk at|反义词：stare respectfully, gaze admiringly|动词
