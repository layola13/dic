irradiates:/ɪˈreɪdieɪts/| vt.|照射；辐射；使充满活力|To expose to radiation; to emit rays of light, heat, or other radiation; to spread or diffuse (something, typically something beneficial) throughout an area.|例句：The sun irradiates the earth with light and heat. 太阳以光和热照射地球。|例句：His confidence irradiates the whole room.他的自信感染了整个房间。|近义词：radiate, illuminate, beam, shine|反义词：obscure, darken
