hazarding:/ˈhæzərdɪŋ/| v.|冒险；尝试；猜测 | To take a risk; to attempt something dangerous or uncertain; to make a guess or conjecture. |例句：He was hazarding a guess at the murderer's identity. 他在猜测凶手的身份。|例句：She was hazarding her life by climbing the mountain in such bad weather. 她冒着生命危险在如此恶劣的天气里登山。|近义词：risking, venturing, speculating, guessing |反义词：avoiding, shunning, ensuring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is hazarding a guess. = 他在冒险猜测。
They are hazarding a trip across the ocean in a small boat. = 他们冒险乘坐小船横渡大洋。


一般过去时:
She hazarded a comment about the situation. = 她对这种情况做出了冒险的评论。
He hazarded a climb up the treacherous cliff. = 他冒险攀登险峻的悬崖。


现在完成时:
They have hazarded a lot in their business ventures. = 他们在商业冒险中投入了很多。
She has hazarded several attempts to solve the puzzle. = 她已经尝试了几次解决这个难题。


过去完成时:
He had hazarded a guess before seeing the evidence. = 他在看到证据之前就冒险猜测了。
They had hazarded their entire savings on the project. = 他们已经把全部积蓄都押在了这个项目上。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If I were to hazard a guess, I'd say it's likely to rain. = 如果我要冒险猜测的话，我会说可能会下雨。
I wish I hadn't hazarded so much money on that investment. = 我真希望我没有在那项投资上冒这么大的风险。


被动语态:
A guess was hazarded at the solution. = 对解决方案进行了一次冒险的猜测。
The expedition was hazarded despite the warnings. = 尽管有警告，探险队仍然冒险前往。


疑问句:

否定句:

They did not hazard a response to the criticism. = 他们没有冒险回应批评。
