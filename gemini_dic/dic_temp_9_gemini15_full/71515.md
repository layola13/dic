flanneling:/ˈflænəlɪŋ/| n. |用法兰绒布擦拭；虚情假意地奉承|The act of using flannel to clean or polish something; insincere flattery or smooth talk.|例句：The car needed a good flanneling. 这辆车需要好好擦拭一下。 He was accused of flanneling the boss to get a promotion. 他被指控虚情假意地奉承老板以获得晋升。|近义词： polishing, buttering up,  coaxing |反义词：criticizing,  confronting, honesty
