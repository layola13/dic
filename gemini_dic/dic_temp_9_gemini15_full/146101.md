overelaborating: /ˌoʊvərɪˈlæbəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 过分详细阐述；过于精雕细琢 |  To explain or describe something in excessive detail; to make something excessively complex or ornate.  |  例句：He was overelaborating the plot of the novel, losing the audience in unnecessary details. 他对小说的情节解释得过于详细，以至于听众迷失在不必要的细节中。  The architect overelaborated the design, resulting in a building that was both costly and impractical. 建筑师的设计过于复杂，导致建筑既昂贵又不实用。 | 近义词：overexplaining, overcomplicating, embellishing excessively,  | 反义词：simplifying, streamlining, abbreviating |  动词
