gawkers:/ˈɡɔːkərz/| n.| 盯视者；看热闹的人 | People who stare openly and often rudely at something or someone.  | Example: The gawkers lined the street, eager to catch a glimpse of the celebrity.  看热闹的人们排满了街道，渴望瞥见那位名人一眼。|近义词：onlookers, rubberneckers, starers |反义词：participants, involved persons
