nondiscoverable: /ˌnɒn.dɪˈskʌvərəbl/ | adj. |不可发现的，无法查明的|  Unable to be discovered or found; hidden or concealed.  This term often implies a deliberate effort to keep something hidden,  rather than simply being unknown. |

例句：

The location of the hidden treasure remained nondiscoverable, despite years of searching.  隐藏宝藏的位置尽管经过多年的搜寻，仍然无法找到。


The witness's identity was kept nondiscoverable to protect their safety. 为保护证人的安全，其身份被保密。


近义词：undiscoverable, hidden, concealed, secret, unknown


反义词：discoverable, known, obvious, apparent


词性:形容词
