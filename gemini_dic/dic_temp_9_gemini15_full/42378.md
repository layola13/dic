counterbid:/ˈkaʊntərbɪd/| n. |反价，还价 | A counterbid is a bid made in response to a previous bid, typically offering a higher price or better terms.  It's an offer made to surpass a previous offer in an auction or negotiation. | Example:  The initial bid was $10,000, but a competitor quickly made a counterbid of $12,000.  首次出价为1万美元，但一位竞争者迅速还价1.2万美元。 |近义词：counteroffer,  rebid |反义词：initial bid, opening bid |名词
