/ˈleɪtriə/| n. |崇拜；敬礼；至尊的敬拜|The word "latria" refers to the supreme worship due only to God.  It is a theological term distinguishing the worship given to God from the veneration given to saints or other religious figures.  Latria involves complete adoration, submission, and reverence, exceeding any other form of religious devotion.|Example:  The Catholic Church reserves latria exclusively for God. 天主教把至高无上的崇拜只保留给上帝。|近义词：adoration, veneration, homage|反义词：contempt, disrespect, disregard|名词
