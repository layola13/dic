evaluated:/ɪˈvæljuˌeɪtɪd/ | v. | 评估；评价；估价 | To form an idea of the amount, number, or value of; assess.  | The committee evaluated the proposals and selected the best one.  委员会评估了这些提案并选择了最佳方案。 | assessed, appraised, judged | underestimated, overestimated | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They evaluate the performance of each employee annually. = 他们每年评估每位员工的绩效。
The teacher evaluates students' work based on several criteria. = 老师根据几个标准来评估学生的作业。


一般过去时:
The scientist evaluated the data from the experiment. = 科学家评估了实验数据。
We evaluated the risks before making the investment. = 我们在投资之前评估了风险。


现在完成时:
The board has evaluated the company's financial situation. = 董事会已经评估了公司的财务状况。
She has evaluated several different options. = 她已经评估了几个不同的选择。


过去完成时:
They had evaluated the project's feasibility before starting. = 他们在开始之前已经评估了项目的可行性。
After he had evaluated all the evidence, he reached a conclusion. = 在他评估了所有证据之后，他得出了结论。


将来时:
The manager will evaluate the employee's progress next month. = 经理下个月将评估员工的进度。
We will evaluate the results of the survey after it's completed. = 我们将在调查结束后评估结果。


虚拟语气:
If they evaluated the situation more carefully, they would have avoided the mistake. = 如果他们更仔细地评估情况，他们本来可以避免这个错误。
I wish they had evaluated the risks before proceeding. = 我希望他们在继续之前已经评估了风险。


被动语态:
The proposal was evaluated by the committee last week. = 该提案上周由委员会进行了评估。
The results of the study are being evaluated at the moment. = 目前正在评估这项研究的结果。


疑问句:
How will the teacher evaluate our final project? = 老师将如何评估我们的最终项目？
Have you evaluated the potential benefits of this approach? = 你评估过这种方法的潜在好处吗？


否定句:
The company did not evaluate the long-term consequences. = 公司没有评估长远后果。
They haven't evaluated the impact on the environment yet. = 他们还没有评估对环境的影响。


PHRASE->
evaluate the situation = 评估形势
