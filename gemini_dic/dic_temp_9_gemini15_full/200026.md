skeletology:/ˌskɛləˈtɒlədʒi/| n. |骨骼学|The scientific study of skeletons, including their structure, development, function, and evolution.  It encompasses aspects of anatomy, physiology, paleontology, and anthropology.|例句：Skeletology helps us understand how animals moved and functioned in their environments. 骨骼学帮助我们了解动物如何在环境中活动和运作。

例句：The professor's lecture on skeletology focused on the skeletal adaptations of birds for flight. 教授关于骨骼学的讲座重点介绍了鸟类为飞行而进行的骨骼适应。


近义词：osteology (more focused on bones themselves)


反义词：无


词性：名词
