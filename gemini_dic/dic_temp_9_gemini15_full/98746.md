insusceptibility:/ˌɪnsʌsɛptəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不易感染性；免疫力；抗性 |The state or quality of being insusceptible;  immunity to or resistance against something, especially disease.  It refers to a lack of susceptibility or vulnerability. |Example: The insusceptibility of certain individuals to common colds has always intrigued scientists.  某些人对普通感冒的免疫力一直让科学家们着迷。|近义词：immunity, resistance, invulnerability |反义词：susceptibility, vulnerability
