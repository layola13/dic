cigarette:/ˌsɪɡəˈrɛt/| n. |香烟，纸烟 |A small cylinder of finely cut tobacco rolled in paper for smoking.  |例句：He lit a cigarette and took a deep drag. 他点燃了一支香烟，深深地吸了一口。|例句：Smoking cigarettes is harmful to your health. 吸烟有害健康。|近义词：smoke, cigar (but cigar is larger and typically not paper-wrapped)|反义词:None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He smokes cigarettes every day. = 他每天都抽烟。
She lights a cigarette after dinner. = 她晚饭后点燃一支香烟。

一般过去时:
He smoked a cigarette before the meeting. = 他在会议前抽了一支烟。
She lit a cigarette and sat down. = 她点燃了一支香烟然后坐了下来。

现在完成时:
I have smoked cigarettes for ten years. = 我抽烟十年了。
She has quit smoking cigarettes. = 她已经戒烟了。

过去完成时:
He had smoked a cigarette before he went to bed. = 他睡觉前抽了一支烟。
She had finished her cigarette before he arrived. = 他到达之前，她已经抽完烟了。

将来时:
I will smoke a cigarette after the presentation. = 演示结束后我将抽支烟。
He will probably quit smoking cigarettes soon. = 他可能会很快戒烟。

虚拟语气:
If I didn't smoke cigarettes, I would be healthier. = 如果我不抽烟，我会更健康。
I wish I could quit smoking cigarettes. = 我希望我能戒烟。

被动语态:
Cigarettes are sold everywhere. = 香烟到处都有卖。
The cigarettes were confiscated by the police. = 香烟被警察没收了。

疑问句:
Do you smoke cigarettes? = 你抽烟吗？
Have you ever tried smoking cigarettes? = 你曾经尝试过抽烟吗？

否定句:
He doesn't smoke cigarettes anymore. = 他不再抽烟了。
She didn't smoke cigarettes in public. = 她不在公共场合抽烟。
