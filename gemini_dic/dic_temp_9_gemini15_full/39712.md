consigner:/kənˈsaɪnər/| n. |托运人，寄货人 |A person or company that sends goods to another for sale or storage.  |例句：The consigner is responsible for ensuring the goods are properly packaged.  托运人有责任确保货物包装妥当。|例句：The consigner shipped the goods via express delivery.  寄货人通过快递运输货物。|近义词：shipper, sender |反义词：consignee


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The consigner ships goods regularly. = 托运人定期发货。
He consigns his artwork to a gallery. = 他将他的艺术品寄售给画廊。

一般过去时:
The consigner shipped the package last week. = 托运人上周寄送了包裹。
She consigned her paintings to an auction house. = 她将她的画作委托给拍卖行。

现在完成时:
The consigner has shipped all the goods. = 托运人已将所有货物发出。
He has consigned numerous artworks over the years. = 多年来，他已委托了许多艺术品。

过去完成时:
The consigner had already consigned the goods before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，托运人已经寄送了货物。
She had consigned her collection before the market crashed. = 在市场崩盘之前，她已经委托了她的收藏品。

将来时:
The consigner will ship the order tomorrow. = 托运人明天将发货。
He will consign his latest sculptures next month. = 他下个月将寄售他最新的雕塑作品。

虚拟语气:
If the consigner were more careful, there wouldn't be so many damaged goods. = 如果托运人更仔细一些，就不会有这么多损坏的货物了。
I wish the consigner had insured the shipment. = 我希望托运人已经为货物投保了。

被动语态:
The goods were consigned to a warehouse. = 货物被寄送到仓库。
The shipment was consigned by a reputable company. = 货物是由一家信誉良好的公司托运的。

疑问句:
Who is the consigner of this package? = 这个包裹的托运人是谁？
Has the consigner provided all the necessary documentation? = 托运人是否提供了所有必要的单据？

否定句:
The consigner did not provide tracking information. = 托运人没有提供追踪信息。
He didn't consign the goods to the correct address. = 他没有将货物寄送到正确的地址。
