olive:/ˈɒlɪv/ /ˈɑːlɪv/| n. adj.|橄榄；橄榄色|An olive is the fruit of the olive tree, used to produce olive oil.  The color olive is a dull yellowish-green.|例句：We had olives and bread for a snack. 我们零食吃了橄榄和面包。

The walls were painted a lovely shade of olive. 墙壁漆上了可爱的橄榄色。|近义词：oil, green|反义词:bright, vivid.
