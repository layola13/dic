cardiology:/ˌkɑːrdiˈɒlədʒi/ /ˌkɑːrdiˈɑːlədʒi/ | n. |心脏病学 |The branch of medicine dealing with the structure, function, and diseases of the heart.  It encompasses the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of heart conditions. | Example:  He specialized in cardiology after medical school. 他医学院毕业后专攻心脏病学。|近义词：heart medicine, cardiovascular medicine |反义词：无
