colonialisms: /kəˈloʊniəlɪzəm/ | n. |殖民主义|The policies and practices of a powerful nation in extending its political and economic control over a less powerful territory or people, often involving exploitation and cultural domination.  Colonialisms refers to the system and ideology of colonialism, encompassing multiple instances and forms across history.  |例句：The legacy of colonialism continues to affect many countries today. 殖民主义的遗产至今仍在影响许多国家。  The effects of colonialism are still visible in many parts of the world. 殖民主义的影响在世界许多地方仍然可见。|近义词：imperialism, dominion, subjugation |反义词：independence, liberation, self-governance|名词
