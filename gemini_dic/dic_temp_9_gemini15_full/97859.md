inletting:/ɪnˈlɛtɪŋ/| n. |嵌套；嵌入；镶嵌|The act of inserting or fitting something into a recess or opening, especially in woodwork or metalwork.  It often involves creating a recess to receive the inserted piece. |例句：The intricate inletting of the wood panels showed the craftsman's skill.  木板精细的嵌套工艺展现了工匠的技艺。

The inletting of the metal piece required precise measurements. 金属件的嵌入需要精确的测量。


近义词：insertion, embedding, mortising, fitting|反义词：removal, extraction|词性:n.
