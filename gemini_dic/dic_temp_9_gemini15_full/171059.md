pseuds:/sjuːdz/| n.|假冒者，伪君子|People who pretend to be something they are not, especially intellectuals or artists.|例句：He considered the literary critics mere pseuds, detached from the real world. 他认为那些文学评论家不过是一群脱离现实的假冒文人。 He dismissed them as pretentious pseuds. 他把他们斥为矫揉造作的伪君子。|近义词：poseur, phony, charlatan, imposter|反义词：genuine, authentic, sincere
