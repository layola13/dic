exsanguine:/ɛkˈsæŋɡwɪn/| adj.| 贫血的；失血过多的；苍白的 | Extremely pale, as from loss of blood; bloodless.  The adjective "exsanguine" describes a state of extreme paleness, often due to a significant loss of blood. It can also be used figuratively to describe something drained of vitality or energy. | Example: The accident victim was exsanguine and barely conscious.  事故受害者脸色苍白，几乎不省人事。 The once vibrant city now felt exsanguine, devoid of its former energy.  曾经充满活力的城市现在感觉贫血，失去了昔日的活力。| 近义词：pale, bloodless, ashen, wan | 反义词：ruddy, flushed, vibrant, full of life |  adj.
