join's:/dʒɔɪnz/| n. |加入，连接；关节|The word "join's" is a contraction of "join is" or "join has". It's not a standard dictionary entry and its usage depends entirely on the context.  It functions grammatically as a verb phrase. |例句：He join's the club next week. = 他下周加入俱乐部。(join is)  The team join's the competition tomorrow. =  队伍明天参加比赛。(join has) |近义词：connects, unites, links|反义词：separates, divides, disconnects
