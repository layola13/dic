frantic:/ˈfrænɪk/| adj.| 狂乱的；极度不安的；忙乱的 |extremely agitated;wild with excitement or fear; showing or feeling extreme anxiety. |例句：She was frantic with worry when her son didn't come home.  她儿子没回家，她急得快要疯了。|例句：The frantic search for the missing child lasted for hours.  对失踪儿童的疯狂搜寻持续了好几个小时。|近义词：frenzied, desperate, hysterical |反义词：calm, composed, tranquil


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is frantic about the upcoming exam. = 他对即将到来的考试感到非常焦虑。
She is frantic with worry about her sick mother. = 她因为她生病的母亲而非常担心。


一般过去时:
He was frantic when he missed his flight. = 他错过航班时非常着急。
She was frantic trying to finish the report before the deadline. = 她为了赶在截止日期前完成报告而手忙脚乱。


现在完成时:
I have been frantic all day trying to find my keys. = 我今天一整天都在焦急地寻找我的钥匙。
She has been frantic ever since she lost her job. = 自从失业以来，她一直非常焦虑。


过去完成时:
He had been frantic all night waiting for news. = 他整夜焦急地等待消息。
She had been frantic before she received the good news. = 在收到好消息之前，她一直非常焦虑。


将来时:
I will be frantic if I don't finish this project on time. = 如果我不能按时完成这个项目，我会非常焦虑。
She will be frantic if she misses the train. = 如果她错过火车，她会非常着急。


虚拟语气:
If I were in her shoes, I would be frantic. = 如果我处在她那种境地，我会非常焦虑。
I wish I wasn't so frantic about this presentation. = 我希望我不必对这次演讲如此焦虑。


被动语态:
The audience was driven frantic by the speaker's passionate speech.  听众被演讲者充满激情的演讲弄得激动不已。
The lost dog made the family frantic.  走失的狗让全家都非常焦虑。


疑问句:
Are you frantic about the test? = 你对考试感到焦虑吗？
Is she frantic because of the delay? = 她因为延误而感到焦虑吗？


否定句:
He wasn't frantic; he was just a little nervous. = 他并不慌张；他只是有点紧张。
She wasn't frantic; she handled the situation calmly. = 她并不慌张；她冷静地处理了这种情况。
