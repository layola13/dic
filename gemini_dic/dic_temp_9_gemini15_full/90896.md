hunter-gatherer:/ˈhʌntər ˈɡæðərər/| n. |狩猎采集者| A hunter-gatherer is a member of a nomadic group of people who live chiefly by hunting, fishing, and gathering plants.  They do not practice agriculture. | Example:  Many anthropologists study the lifestyles of hunter-gatherer societies to understand early human history.  许多人类学家研究狩猎采集社会的生活方式，以了解早期人类历史。|近义词：forager, gatherer, nomadic people |反义词：farmer, agriculturalist
