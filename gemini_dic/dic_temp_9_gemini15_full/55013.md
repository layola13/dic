dispute:/dɪˈspjuːt/| n./v.|争论，争议；争执；质疑 | A dispute is a disagreement or argument.  It can refer to a debate, a quarrel, or a legal conflict. |例句：The two countries are involved in a bitter territorial dispute.  这两个国家卷入了一场激烈的领土争端。

There was a dispute over the ownership of the land.  关于土地所有权存在争议。


近义词：argument, disagreement, contention, quarrel, conflict | 反义词：agreement, accord, harmony, consensus | 词性: Noun & Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They dispute the findings of the report. = 他们对报告的调查结果提出异议。
He frequently disputes with his neighbors. = 他经常和邻居争吵。


一般过去时:
The two companies disputed the contract terms. = 这两家公司就合同条款发生了争议。
We disputed the bill at the restaurant. = 我们对餐馆的账单提出异议。


现在完成时:
They have disputed the results for weeks. = 他们已经就结果争论了几个星期。
She has never disputed my authority before. = 她以前从未质疑过我的权威。


过去完成时:
They had disputed the matter before the judge arrived. = 在法官到来之前，他们已经就此事争论过了。
He had disputed the claim several times. = 他已经多次驳斥了这一说法。


将来时:
They will dispute the decision in court. = 他们将在法庭上对该决定提出异议。
We will likely dispute the charges on our credit card. = 我们可能会对信用卡上的收费提出异议。


虚拟语气:
If they disputed the contract, they would lose the deal. = 如果他们对合同有异议，他们将会失去这笔交易。
I wish they hadn't disputed my ideas so vehemently. = 我真希望他们不要那么激烈地反对我的想法。


被动语态:
The results were disputed by many experts. = 许多专家对结果提出了质疑。
The contract is being disputed in arbitration. = 该合同正在仲裁中被争议。


疑问句:
Are they disputing the terms of the agreement? = 他们正在就协议条款进行争论吗？
Do you dispute the evidence presented? = 你是否对所提供的证据提出异议？


否定句:
They did not dispute the evidence presented. = 他们没有对所提供的证据提出异议。
He doesn't dispute the facts. = 他并不否认事实。
