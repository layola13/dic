cover-ground:/ˈkʌvər ɡraʊnd/| v.|覆盖地面；走遍；涵盖；|To cover a lot of ground means to accomplish a great deal or to discuss a wide range of topics in a short amount of time. It can also refer to literally covering a large area of land.|例句：We covered a lot of ground in our meeting today. 今天的会议我们讨论了很多内容。|例句：The hikers covered a lot of ground before sunset.  徒步旅行者在日落前走了很长一段路。|近义词：traverse, encompass, span|反义词：neglect, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cover a lot of ground in their research. = 他们的研究涵盖了很多方面。
We cover a wide range of topics in this course. = 本课程涵盖了广泛的主题。

一般过去时:
The army covered much ground in a single day. = 军队一天内行进了很长的距离。
We covered all the important points in the presentation. = 我们在演示文稿中涵盖了所有重点。

现在完成时:
They have covered considerable ground in their negotiations. = 他们在谈判中取得了相当大的进展。
We have covered a lot of material this semester. = 这个学期我们学习了很多内容。

过去完成时:
They had covered a great deal of ground before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经走了很长一段路。
We had covered the basics before moving on to advanced topics. = 在学习高级主题之前，我们已经学习了基础知识。

将来时:
We will cover a lot of ground on our trip next week. = 我们下周旅行将走很长的路。
They will cover all the relevant issues in their report. = 他们将在报告中涵盖所有相关问题。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we could cover more ground. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们可以取得更大的进展。
I wish we could cover all the topics we planned. = 我希望我们能够涵盖我们计划的所有主题。

被动语态:
Much ground was covered in the discussion. = 讨论中涵盖了很多内容。
A wide area was covered by the search party. = 搜索队搜寻了大片区域。

疑问句:

否定句:
We didn't cover enough ground in the meeting. = 会议上我们讨论的内容不够多。
They didn't cover all the aspects of the problem. = 他们没有涵盖问题的各个方面。
