ectoderm:/ˈɛktoʊdɜːrm/| n. |外胚层|The outermost layer of cells or tissue of a developing embryo in most animals; it differentiates to form the epidermis, the nervous system, and sense organs.|例句：The ectoderm gives rise to the skin and nervous system. 外胚层发育形成皮肤和神经系统。|近义词：germ layer|反义词：endoderm, mesoderm|名词
