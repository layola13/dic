kiwikiwi:/kiːˈwiːkiːwiː/| n. |几维鸟；猕猴桃|A small, flightless bird native to New Zealand, known for its brown, hair-like feathers and long beak.  The name is also used for the fruit of a vine, also native to New Zealand.|例句：The kiwi is a national symbol of New Zealand.  几维鸟是新西兰的国鸟。
The kiwi fruit is delicious.  猕猴桃很好吃。|近义词：kiwi fruit (for the fruit)|反义词：无|名词
