hectic:/ˈhɛktɪk/| adj.|繁忙的；慌乱的；兴奋的 |Characterized by intense activity, confusion, or excitement; frenetic.  It suggests a state of being overwhelmed by a large number of tasks or events.|例句：It's been a hectic week at work.  = 这周工作非常忙碌。|例句：The atmosphere in the office was hectic before the deadline. = 截止日期前办公室气氛很慌乱。|近义词：frantic, frenetic, frantic, rushed, busy |反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil, relaxed
