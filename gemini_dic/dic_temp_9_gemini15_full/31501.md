charms:/tʃɑːmz/| n. v.|魅力；符咒；迷住；使陶醉|The word "charms" can refer to a captivating quality or qualities that fascinate or delight.  It can also refer to an object believed to have magical powers. As a verb, it means to delight or fascinate.|例句：Her beauty charms everyone.  她的美貌迷住了所有人。

The ancient amulet charms away evil spirits. 这古老的护身符驱除邪灵。|近义词：allure, appeal, enchant, fascinate|反义词：repulse, repel
