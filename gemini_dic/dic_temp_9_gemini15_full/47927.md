decern:/dɪˈsɜːrn/ | vt. | 分辨；识别；看出 | To perceive or recognize something.  To distinguish mentally; discriminate. | 例句：He was able to discern a subtle difference in the two wines. 他能够辨别出两种葡萄酒之间细微的差别。

例句：It was difficult to discern the figures in the mist. 在薄雾中很难辨认出那些身影。


近义词：distinguish, discriminate, perceive, identify, recognize | 反义词：confuse, mistake, misinterpret | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He discerns the truth behind the lies. = 他辨明了谎言背后的真相。
She discerns subtle nuances in the music. = 她能辨别出音乐中细微的差别。


一般过去时:
The detective discerned a clue in the room. = 侦探在房间里发现了一条线索。
I discerned a pattern in the data. = 我在数据中发现了一种模式。


现在完成时:
We have discerned a shift in public opinion. = 我们已经觉察到民意发生了转变。
She has discerned the weaknesses in her opponent's strategy. = 她已经看出了对手策略中的弱点。


过去完成时:
He had discerned the fraud before the authorities. = 他在当局之前就发现了欺诈行为。
They had discerned the danger lurking in the shadows. = 他们已经觉察到潜伏在阴影中的危险。


将来时:
We will discern the results of the experiment soon. = 我们很快就会知道实验的结果。
Scientists will discern new patterns in the data. = 科学家们将能辨别出数据中的新模式。


虚拟语气:
If I could discern the future, I would be a rich man. = 如果我能预知未来，我就成为富翁了。
I wish I could discern his true intentions. = 我真希望我能看穿他的真实意图。


被动语态:
The truth was discerned by the jury. = 法官们辨明了真相。
The subtle differences were easily discerned by experts. = 专家们很容易就能辨别出细微的差别。


疑问句:
Can you discern the difference between these two paintings? = 你能分辨出这两幅画的区别吗？
Could you discern any patterns in the data? = 你能从数据中看出什么规律吗？


否定句:
I couldn't discern any flaws in his argument. = 我看不出他论点中的任何缺陷。
They failed to discern the hidden meaning. = 他们没能看出隐藏的含义。



PHRASE->
discern the truth = 辨明真相
discern a pattern = 发现规律
discern the difference = 辨别差异

词源:
源自古法语 *descerner，来自拉丁语 discernere，意为“区分，分离”。  dis- 表示“分开”，cernere 表示“筛分，区分”。
