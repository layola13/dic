preinhabiting:/ˌpriːɪnˈhæbɪtɪŋ/| adj.| 先住的；先前居住的 | Existing or living in a place before someone or something else arrives or is introduced.  |The preinhabiting species were largely unaffected by the introduction of the new plant. 先居住的物种在引进新品种植物后基本上没有受到影响。 |近义词：prior, antecedent, preceding |反义词： subsequent, following
