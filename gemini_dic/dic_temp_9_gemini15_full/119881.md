militants:/ˈmɪlɪtənts/| n.|激进分子，好战分子|People who are actively involved in political or social conflict, often using aggressive or violent methods.  They are characterized by their strong commitment to a cause and their willingness to engage in forceful action to achieve their goals.|例句：The militants launched a surprise attack on the government building. 激进分子对政府大楼发动了突然袭击。|例句：The peace talks were disrupted by the actions of the militants. 和谈因激进分子的行为而中断。|近义词：activists, radicals, insurgents, guerrillas|反义词：pacifists, peacemakers
