fidel:/ˈfɪdɛl/| n. adj.|菲德尔（男子名）；忠诚的；真实的；|A name of Spanish origin, meaning "faithful" or "trustworthy".  It is also used as an adjective meaning faithful or true. |例句：His name is Fidel. 他的名字叫菲德尔。The fidel account of the event was crucial.  对事件的忠实描述至关重要。|近义词：loyal, true, faithful, trustworthy|反义词：disloyal, unfaithful, treacherous|名词，形容词
