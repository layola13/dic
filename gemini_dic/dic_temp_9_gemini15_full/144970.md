overacceleration: /ˌoʊvəræksɛləˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 超速加速；过度加速 | The rapid increase of speed beyond a safe or recommended limit.  This term often refers to a situation where a vehicle or system accelerates faster than intended or allowed, potentially leading to dangerous consequences. | 例句：The driver was cited for overacceleration and reckless driving. 司机因超速驾驶和鲁莽驾驶而被处以罚款。  The sudden overacceleration of the engine caused a catastrophic failure. 发动机的突然过度加速导致了灾难性的故障。 | 近义词：excessive acceleration, rapid acceleration, speeding | 反义词：deceleration, slowing down |  名词
