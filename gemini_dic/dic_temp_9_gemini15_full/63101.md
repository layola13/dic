enshrinement: /ɪnˈʃraɪnmənt/ | n. | 奉为神圣；珍藏；铭记 | The act of enshrining; the state of being enshrined.  The act of placing something in a shrine or other sacred place; the preservation of something as sacred or important. |例句：The museum's enshrinement of the artist's work ensures its legacy.  博物馆对这位艺术家作品的珍藏确保了他的遗产永存。


The enshrinement of human rights in the constitution is a crucial step towards justice.宪法中对人权的奉为神圣是迈向正义的关键一步。|近义词：consecration, sanctification, preservation, commemoration |反义词：desecration, profanation, neglect, forgetting |词性 n.
