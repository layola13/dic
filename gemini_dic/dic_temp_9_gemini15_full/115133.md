mangonel:/ˈmæŋɡənɛl/| n. |投石机|A mangonel is a type of ancient siege engine used to hurl large projectiles, such as stones or flaming projectiles, at fortifications.  It typically consisted of a long arm that swung around a pivot, powered by a twisted rope or similar mechanism. |The mangonel was a fearsome weapon in medieval warfare.|trebuchet, catapult| (no direct antonym)|名词
