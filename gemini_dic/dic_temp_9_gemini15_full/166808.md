presper:/ˈprɒspər/| v.|繁荣，兴旺，成功 |to succeed and be successful, especially in financial terms.| The business is prospering under its new management. 这家公司在新的管理下蓬勃发展。 |近义词：flourish, thrive, succeed |反义词：fail, decline, struggle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prospers in his new career. 他在他的新事业中很成功。
The company prospers despite the economic downturn.  尽管经济低迷，公司依然兴旺发达。


一般过去时:
The town prospered after the discovery of gold. 在发现金矿后，小镇繁荣起来。
Their business prospered for many years. 他们的生意兴隆了很多年。


现在完成时:
The company has prospered since it changed its strategy. 自从改变策略以来，公司就一直兴旺发达。
They have prospered greatly in recent years. 近年来他们非常成功。


过去完成时:
The kingdom had prospered for centuries before the war. 在战争爆发前，这个王国已经繁荣了几个世纪。
The family had prospered until the father's business failed. 直到父亲的生意失败，这个家庭才一直很富裕。


将来时:
With hard work, they will prosper. 只要努力工作，他们就会成功。
The city is expected to prosper in the coming years. 预计这座城市在未来几年会繁荣起来。


虚拟语气:
If they had worked harder, they would have prospered. 如果他们更加努力工作，他们就会成功。
I wish I could prosper as much as my brother. 我希望我能像我哥哥一样成功。


被动语态:
The region was prospered by the new trade route. 新的贸易路线使该地区繁荣起来。(被动语态用法较少见，通常用主动语态)
The city is being prospered by tourism. 旅游业正在使这座城市繁荣起来。(被动语态用法较少见，通常用主动语态)


疑问句:
Will the business prosper in the long run? 这家企业从长远来看会兴旺吗？
How did the family prosper? 这个家庭是如何兴旺发达的？


否定句:
The business did not prosper under his leadership. 在他的领导下，这家企业并没有兴旺发达。
The farm did not prosper because of the drought. 由于干旱，农场没有兴旺发达。
