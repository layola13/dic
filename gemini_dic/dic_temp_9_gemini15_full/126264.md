myelogenic:/ˌmaɪələˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 骨髓生成的；髓源性的 | Relating to or originating in bone marrow.  Myelogenic refers to something that is produced or derived from bone marrow, typically concerning blood cells. | Example: Myelogenic leukemia is a cancer of the blood-forming cells in the bone marrow. 骨髓性白血病是骨髓中造血细胞的癌症。|近义词：medullary (in some contexts)|反义词：non-myelogenous
