shrubless:/ˈʃrʌbləs/| adj.|无灌木的，缺少灌木的|Lacking shrubs; without shrubs.  The shrubless landscape was stark and exposed.|近义词：bush-free, treeless|反义词：shrubby, wooded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The desert is shrubless. =沙漠里没有灌木。
This area remains shrubless despite replanting efforts. = 尽管进行了补种，这个地区仍然缺乏灌木。


一般过去时:
The hillside was shrubless before the recent planting project. = 在最近的植树项目之前，山坡上没有灌木。
The land appeared shrubless after the wildfire. = 野火过后，这片土地看起来没有灌木了。


现在完成时:
The drought has left the area shrubless. = 干旱使该地区缺乏灌木。
Years of overgrazing have rendered the pasture shrubless. = 多年的过度放牧使牧场失去了灌木。


过去完成时:
The land had been shrubless for decades before the conservation initiative. = 在保护计划启动之前，这片土地几十年来一直缺乏灌木。
Before the fire, the area wasn’t shrubless. = 在火灾发生之前，这个地区并非没有灌木。


将来时:
Without intervention, the hillside will remain shrubless. = 如果不进行干预，山坡将仍然缺乏灌木。
The area will likely be shrubless for many years to come. = 未来许多年，该地区可能会缺乏灌木。


虚拟语气:
If we didn't take action, the area would be shrubless. = 如果我们不采取行动，这个地区将缺乏灌木。
I wish the landscape wasn’t so shrubless. = 我希望这片景观不要如此缺乏灌木。


被动语态:
The area was rendered shrubless by the severe drought. = 严重的干旱使该地区缺乏灌木。
The land has been left shrubless by years of neglect. = 多年的忽视使这片土地缺乏灌木。


疑问句:
Is the park shrubless? = 公园里没有灌木吗？
Was the slope shrubless before the planting? = 种植之前，山坡上没有灌木吗？


否定句:
The garden is not shrubless; it's full of roses. = 花园里不是没有灌木，它种满了玫瑰。
The area wasn't completely shrubless; a few bushes survived. = 该地区并非完全没有灌木；一些灌木幸存了下来。
