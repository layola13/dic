/ˈhɪstəriːz/ | n. |历史(复数)|The plural form of "history," referring to multiple historical accounts, periods, or subjects.  It can also refer to a person's past experiences or a record of past events.| Examples:  The histories of different countries often intertwine.  (不同国家的历史往往交织在一起。) The histories of those involved in the accident are quite different. (事故中涉及人员的经历大相径庭。) |近义词： annals, chronicles, records |反义词：future, present
