dirge:/ˈdɜːrdʒ/| n. |挽歌，哀歌|A dirge is a mournful song, especially one intended to accompany a funeral procession or express sorrow for the dead.  It often features slow tempos, minor keys, and somber lyrics. |例句：The funeral procession was accompanied by a slow, mournful dirge.  葬礼队伍伴随着一首缓慢哀伤的挽歌。

The choir sang a dirge for the fallen soldiers. 合唱团为阵亡士兵唱了一首挽歌。|近义词：lament, elegy, requiem |反义词：celebration, anthem
