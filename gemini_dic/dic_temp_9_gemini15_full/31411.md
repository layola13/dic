chargrill:/ˈtʃɑːrɡrɪl/| vt. |炭火烤，烧烤|To cook food on a chargrill.  Chargrilling involves cooking food directly over hot coals or a very hot grill, resulting in a characteristic charred or smoky flavour and often visible grill marks.|例句：I chargrilled the steak until it was perfectly medium-rare. 我把牛排炭烤到刚好五分熟。|例句：We chargrilled some vegetables for a delicious side dish. 我们炭烤了一些蔬菜做配菜。|近义词：grill, barbecue, broil|反义词：boil, steam
