nonpreventively:/ˌnɒnprɪˈvɛntɪvli/ | adv. | 预防性地，非预防性地 |  Not done to prevent something.  This adverb indicates an action or process that is not intended to prevent a particular outcome or event. It implies that the action may even contribute to or fail to mitigate the undesirable outcome. | Example: The disease spread nonpreventively through the community.  疾病在社区中无预防性地传播。 The damage was done nonpreventively, as safety measures were not in place. 损害是非预防性造成的，因为没有采取安全措施。 | 近义词: unintentionally, inadvertently, accidentally | 反义词: preventively, proactively
