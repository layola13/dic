citizenship:/ˈsɪtɪzənʃɪp/| n. |公民身份；公民资格；公民权利和义务 |The state or quality of being a citizen; the rights and duties of a citizen.  It refers to the legal status of being a citizen of a particular country, which often grants certain rights and responsibilities. |He applied for US citizenship. 他申请了美国公民身份。|She gained citizenship through marriage. 她通过婚姻获得了公民身份。|nationality, residency |statelessness, alienage |名词
