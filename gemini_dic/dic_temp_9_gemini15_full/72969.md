fool's:/fʊlz/| n.|傻瓜的；愚人的|The term "fool's" is a possessive form of the noun "fool," indicating belonging to or associated with a fool.  It's often used adjectivally to describe something characteristic of a fool, something foolish, or something designed to trick a fool.|例句：He fell for the fool's gold. 他上了假金子的当。

例句：That's a fool's errand.  那是徒劳无功的差事。|近义词：silly's, unwise's, foolish's|反义词：wise's, clever's, intelligent's|名词
