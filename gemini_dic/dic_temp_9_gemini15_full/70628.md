filose:/ˈfaɪloʊs/| adj. |丝状的，线状的|Threadlike; resembling a thread.  The term refers to structures that are long, slender, and filamentous.|例句：The diatom exhibited filose pseudopodia extending from its cell body.  硅藻在其细胞体上延伸出丝状伪足。

例句：The filose projections on the surface of the fungus were barely visible to the naked eye. 真菌表面的丝状突起肉眼几乎不可见。


近义词：threadlike, filamentous, filiform


反义词：thick, stout, robust


词性：形容词
