metathesis:/mɪˈtæθɪsɪs/| n. | 换位，变音 | The transposition of sounds or letters in a word or phrase.  It's a linguistic phenomenon where sounds or letters change places within a word or across words.  For example, "ask" becoming "aks" is a metathesis.  | Example: The word "comfortable" sometimes becomes "comfterble" through metathesis.  舒适有时会被误读成comfterble，这就是一个换位现象的例子。|近义词：transposition, permutation |反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be maintaining the original order of sounds or letters. |名词
