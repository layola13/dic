floreted: /flɔːˈretɪd/ | adj. | 开花的；饰以花的；| Adorned with flowers or flower-like patterns.  The adjective "floreted" describes something decorated with flowers or floral designs. It suggests a decorative and often ornate style. |例句：The  floreted gown was breathtaking.  = 这件饰有花朵图案的长袍令人惊叹。

The antique brooch was floreted with tiny pearls and gemstones. = 这枚古董胸针装饰着细小的珍珠和宝石，如同花朵般。

近义词：flowered, floral, ornate, embellished

反义词：plain, undecorated, simple

词性: adjective
