overspending: /ˌoʊvərˈspɛndɪŋ/ | n. | 超支，过度消费 | The act of spending more money than one has budgeted or planned.  It often leads to debt and financial difficulties. |例句：Their overspending on luxury items led to significant debt. 他们购买奢侈品过度消费导致了巨额债务。


例句：The company's overspending on marketing campaigns resulted in a loss. 公司在市场营销活动上的过度支出导致了亏损。


近义词：excessive spending, extravagance, profligacy | 反义词：underspending, frugality, thrift
