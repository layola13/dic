deteriorated:/dɪˈtɪəriəreɪtɪd/ | v. |恶化；变坏；衰退 |To become worse; to decline in quality, value, or strength. |例句：The building has deteriorated over the years. 这座建筑物多年来已经破败不堪了。

The weather deteriorated rapidly in the afternoon.  下午天气迅速恶化。

近义词：worsen, decline, degenerate, decay |反义词：improve, enhance, ameliorate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The situation deteriorates slowly. = 情况正在缓慢恶化。
His health deteriorates gradually. = 他的健康状况逐渐恶化。

一般过去时:
The relationship deteriorated after the argument. = 争吵后，关系恶化了。
The quality of the product deteriorated over time. = 产品质量随着时间的推移而下降。

现在完成时:
The building has deteriorated significantly since the last inspection. = 自上次检查以来，建筑物已严重恶化。
His condition has deteriorated despite medical treatment. = 尽管接受了治疗，他的病情仍然恶化了。

过去完成时:
The bridge had deteriorated before the accident. = 事故发生前，桥梁就已经老化了。
By the time we arrived, the food had deteriorated. = 我们到达时，食物已经变质了。

将来时:
The situation will deteriorate further without intervention. = 如果不干预，情况将进一步恶化。
We predict that the economy will deteriorate next year. = 我们预测明年经济将恶化。

虚拟语气:
If the weather deteriorated, we would postpone the trip. = 如果天气恶化，我们将推迟旅行。
I wish the situation hadn't deteriorated so quickly. = 我真希望情况没有恶化得这么快。

被动语态:
The building was deteriorated by years of neglect. = 这座建筑物由于多年的忽视而破败不堪。
The project was deteriorated by a lack of funding. = 该项目由于缺乏资金而失败。

疑问句:
Has the patient's condition deteriorated? = 病人的病情恶化了吗？
Did the quality of the food deteriorate? = 食物质量下降了吗？

否定句:
The situation did not deteriorate as quickly as expected. = 情况并没有像预期的那样迅速恶化。
The product did not deteriorate during transportation. = 产品在运输过程中没有变质。
