enter:/ˈɛntər/ | /ˈɛntə(r)/| vt. vi. n.|进入，参加，输入，登记|to go or come into a place; to become involved in something; to type information into a computer or machine; to write one's name on a list|例句：Please enter your password. 请输入您的密码。

例句：He entered the room quietly. 他悄悄地走进了房间。


近义词：go into, join, input, register | 反义词：exit, leave, depart


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enters the building every morning. 他每天早上都进入大楼。
They enter the competition every year. 他们每年都参加比赛。


一般过去时:
She entered the university last year. 她去年进了大学。
I entered my name on the list. 我在名单上登记了我的名字。


现在完成时:
He has entered the race. 他已经报名参加了比赛。
She has entered all the data. 她已经输入了所有数据。


过去完成时:
They had entered the house before the storm hit. 暴风雨来临之前他们已经进了屋。
He had already entered the competition before I knew. 在我知情之前他已经报名参加了比赛。


将来时:
I will enter the contest next week. 我下周将参加比赛。
She will enter the data into the system. 她将把数据输入系统。


虚拟语气:
If I entered the lottery, I might win. 如果我买彩票，我可能会中奖。
I wish I had entered the competition. 我真希望我参加了那次比赛。


被动语态:
The data was entered into the database. 数据被输入数据库。
His name was entered on the guest list. 他的名字被记在了宾客名单上。


疑问句:
Did you enter the building? 你进大楼了吗？
Where do they enter the race? 他们在哪里参加比赛？


否定句:
He didn't enter the room. 他没有进入房间。
She didn't enter the competition. 她没有参加比赛。



PHRASE->
enter into: 开始进行（谈判、协议等）
enter upon: 开始（一段时期、工作等）
enter a plea: 提出答辩
to enter a contest: 参加比赛
to enter data: 输入数据
