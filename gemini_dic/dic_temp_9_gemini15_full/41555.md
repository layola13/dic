corporal:/ˈkɔːrpərəl/ | adj. | 肉体的；身体的；物质的；军体的 | Relating to the body; physical; bodily.  Also relating to a corporal (a rank in the military).|例句：The corporal punishment was considered cruel and unusual.  体罚被认为是残忍和不寻常的。  例句：He suffered corporal injuries in the accident. 他在事故中遭受了身体伤害。|近义词：bodily, physical, material |反义词：spiritual, mental, immaterial
