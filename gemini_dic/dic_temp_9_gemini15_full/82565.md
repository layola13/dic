guaranteers:/ˌɡærənˈtɪrz/ | n. | 保证人；担保人 |  A person or thing that guarantees something.  Those who provide a guarantee, promising to answer for the debt or default of another. | 例句：The bank requires two guarantors for the loan.  银行要求贷款要有两个担保人。  The company's reputation as a guarantor of quality is well-established. 这家公司作为质量保证者的声誉已久负盛名。 | 近义词：guarantor, surety, sponsor | 反义词：debtor, defaulter | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company guarantees all its products. = 公司保证其所有产品。
He guarantees his work. = 他保证他的工作。


一般过去时:
They guaranteed the loan. = 他们担保了贷款。

过去完成时:
