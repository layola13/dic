cooperatives:/koʊˈɑːpərətɪvz/| n.|合作社；合作企业|Organizations owned and operated by their members, who share in the profits or benefits.  These organizations often focus on economic, social, or environmental goals.|例句：Many farmers belong to agricultural cooperatives to get better prices for their crops. 许多农民属于农业合作社，以获得更好的农作物价格。|例句：The housing cooperative provides affordable homes for its members. 这个住房合作社为其成员提供经济适用房。|近义词：associations, partnerships, collectives|反义词：corporations (in the sense of being member-owned and operated)|词性:名词
