humiliates:/ˈhjuːmɪlieɪts/| vt.|使丢脸；使蒙羞；羞辱|To cause someone to feel ashamed and humiliated.|His public criticism humiliated her.|近义词：mortify, shame, disgrace|反义词：honor, exalt


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He humiliates his employees regularly. = 他经常羞辱他的员工。
The constant bullying humiliates the child. = 持续的欺凌使孩子蒙羞。
