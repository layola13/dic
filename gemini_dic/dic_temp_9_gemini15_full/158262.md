play-on:/ˈpleɪ ɒn/| n. |双关语，文字游戏|A play on words is a humorous use of a word or phrase that has two meanings, or of words that have similar sounds but different meanings.  It relies on the use of words with multiple meanings or similar-sounding words to create a humorous or interesting effect.|例句：His joke was a play on words, using "bank" to refer to both a financial institution and the side of a river.  他的笑话是一个双关语，使用了“bank”这个词，既指金融机构，也指河岸。|近义词：pun, wordplay, witticism|反义词：literal meaning, straightforward statement|名词
