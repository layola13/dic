keffiyehs:/kɛˈfiːjɑːz/| n. |头巾（复数）|A keffiyeh is a traditional Arab headdress, typically a square scarf of checkered or patterned cotton. It's often worn folded in a triangle and held in place with an agal (a black cord).  The keffiyeh is a symbol of Arab identity and Palestinian nationalism.|The Palestinian keffiyeh is often black and white checkered.|近义词：headscarf, scarf |反义词：无
