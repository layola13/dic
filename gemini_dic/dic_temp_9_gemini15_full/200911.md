slipperily:/ˈslɪpərili/| adv.|滑溜地；偷偷摸摸地；狡猾地 |In a slippery manner; smoothly and secretly; in a deceitful or evasive way.  |例句：He slipperily avoided answering the question. 他偷偷摸摸地回避了这个问题。|例句：The thief moved slipperily through the shadows. 小偷在阴影中鬼鬼祟祟地移动。|近义词：smoothly, secretly, deviously, cunningly, slyly |反义词：directly, openly, honestly

词源:
