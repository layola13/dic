incidental-to:/ˌɪnsɪˈdentəl tuː/| adj.|附带的；与…有关的；伴随发生的| relating to or connected with something else, but of less importance.  It describes something that happens in addition to something else, often unexpectedly or unintentionally. |例句：The incidental music added to the film's atmosphere.  (附带的音乐增加了电影的氛围。) The incidental costs of the project were surprisingly high. (项目的附带成本出奇的高。) |近义词：contingent, ancillary, subsidiary, collateral |反义词：essential, primary, principal, main
