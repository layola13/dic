evacuations:/ˌiːvækjuˈeɪʃənz/ | n. |疏散；撤离 | The act of evacuating people from a dangerous place.  Evacuations are often necessary during natural disasters or emergencies. | 例句：The emergency evacuations were carried out swiftly and efficiently.紧急疏散迅速而有效地进行了。

例句：Mass evacuations were ordered due to the approaching hurricane. 由于即将到来的飓风，下令进行了大规模疏散。


近义词：removal, exodus, withdrawal | 反义词：arrival, influx
