millipede:/ˈmɪlɪpiːd/| n. |千足虫，马陆|A millipede is a type of arthropod with a long body divided into many segments, each with two pairs of legs.  They are herbivores and are often found in damp environments. |例句：I found a millipede under a rock in the garden. 我在花园里的一块石头下发现了一条千足虫。

例句：The millipede curled into a tight coil when disturbed.  当受到干扰时，千足虫蜷缩成一个紧密的螺旋状。


近义词：centipede (although millipedes have two pairs of legs per segment, while centipedes have one)


反义词：无


词性：名词
