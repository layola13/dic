playpens:/ˈpleɪpɛnz/| n.|游戏围栏，婴儿活动围栏|A playpen is a safe, enclosed area for babies and young children to play in.  It is typically made of fabric, plastic, or mesh and often folds up for easy storage.|例句：My daughter loves playing in her new playpen. 我的女儿喜欢在她新的游戏围栏里玩耍。

The baby giggled happily within the confines of his brightly colored playpen.  婴儿在他色彩鲜艳的游戏围栏里高兴地咯咯地笑。|近义词：play yard, crib (partially), safety enclosure|反义词：open space, playground (in terms of confinement)
