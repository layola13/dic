flint:/flɪnt/| n. |燧石，火石；坚决的人；|a hard, grey or black stone that produces sparks when struck against steel and was formerly used to make tools and weapons|The flint was struck against steel to create a spark.  燧石敲击钢铁以产生火花。

The ancient tools were made of flint. 这些古代工具是用燧石制成的。|gravel, stone|soft, malleable|n.
