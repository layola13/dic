careens:/kəˈriːnz/| v.| 摇晃地行进；蹒跚而行；倾斜；急速奔驰 | To move swiftly and recklessly, often in an uncontrolled manner; to lean or sway to one side.  |例句：The car careened around the corner, narrowly missing a pedestrian.  那辆车摇摇晃晃地转过弯，险些撞到一个行人。
The ship careened wildly in the storm.  船在暴风雨中剧烈摇晃。|近义词：swerve, lurch, tilt, veer |反义词：proceed steadily, move smoothly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The drunk driver careens down the street. = 那个醉酒的司机摇摇晃晃地沿着街道行驶。
The old ship careens gently in the harbor. = 那艘老船在港口轻轻地摇晃。

一般过去时:
The bus careened violently and crashed into a tree. = 公共汽车剧烈摇晃，撞上了一棵树。
The rollercoaster careened around the track. = 过山车在轨道上摇晃着飞驰。

现在完成时:
The car has careened off the road several times. = 这辆车已经几次冲出道路了。
The boat has careened dangerously close to the rocks. = 船已经危险地靠近岩石了。

过去完成时:
The plane had careened before crashing into the mountain. = 飞机在坠毁撞向山峰之前就已经摇晃了。
The horse had careened wildly before the rider could regain control. = 在骑手重新掌控之前，马已经剧烈地摇晃了。

将来时:
The runaway train will careen off the tracks. = 失控的火车将冲出轨道。
I think the car will careen into that ditch if you don't slow down. = 如果你不减速，我认为车会冲进那个沟里。

虚拟语气:
If he had not braked, the car would have careened into the wall. = 如果他没有刹车，车就会撞到墙上。
If the ship had not been so sturdy, it would have careened and capsized. = 如果船没有那么坚固，它就会摇晃并倾覆。


被动语态:
The car was careened into the wall by the impact. = 汽车由于撞击而撞到了墙上。(此用法较少见，通常用主动语态)
The ship was careened onto the rocks by the strong waves.  (此用法较少见，通常用主动语态)


疑问句:
Did the car careen into the lamppost? = 车子撞到了路灯杆上吗？
Will the boat careen in these rough seas? = 在这波涛汹涌的海面上，船会摇晃吗？


否定句:
The car did not careen; it stopped smoothly. = 车子没有摇晃；它平稳地停了下来。
The ship did not careen despite the strong winds. = 尽管风很大，船却没有摇晃。
