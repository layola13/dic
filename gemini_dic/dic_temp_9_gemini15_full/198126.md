shopfloor:/ˈʃɒpflɔːr/| n. |车间；工厂底层；生产现场 |The shopfloor is where the actual manufacturing takes place. 车间是实际生产发生的地方。|近义词：factory floor, workshop, production floor |反义词：office, management


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shopfloor operates 24/7. = 车间24/7不间断运行。
Management monitors the shopfloor productivity. = 管理层监控车间生产力。
