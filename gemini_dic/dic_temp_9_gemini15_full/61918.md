empyrosis:/ɛmˈpaɪroʊsɪs/ | n. |世界末日；大火；宇宙大火|A complete conflagration or destruction by fire; especially : a cosmic conflagration.  The word often evokes a sense of catastrophic, world-ending fire.|The ancient Greeks believed in an empyrosis, a cyclical destruction of the world by fire.  希腊人相信世界末日是周期性的火灾。|近义词：conflagration, cataclysm, apocalypse |反义词：creation, genesis
