crotchety:/ˈkrɑːtʃəti/| adj.|脾气坏的，古怪的，易怒的|easily irritated or annoyed; grumpy; whimsical.|例句：My grandfather is a crotchety old man who complains about everything.  我的祖父是个脾气坏的老头，什么事都抱怨。

例句：She became crotchety in her old age, often snapping at her family. 她上了年纪后变得脾气暴躁，经常对家人发脾气。


近义词：grumpy, irritable, grumpy, cantankerous, ill-tempered, bad-tempered,testy, peevish, сварливый (Russian for grumpy)


反义词：good-natured, amiable, pleasant, cheerful, agreeable, affable, genial


词源：The word "crotchety" ultimately derives from "crochet," referring to a hook.  The connection lies in the association of a hooked stick (a crutch or crochet hook) with something twisted or bent, reflecting a similarly twisted or inflexible personality.  The word evolved through various stages, connecting "crooked" or "twisted" to temperament.
