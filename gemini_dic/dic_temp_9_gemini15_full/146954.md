overinvests:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɛsts/| v.|过度投资|To invest more money, time, or effort into something than is wise or necessary.  This often implies a disproportionate allocation of resources leading to potential negative consequences.|例句：He overinvested in the failing startup, losing most of his savings. 他过度投资于这家即将倒闭的创业公司，损失了他大部分的积蓄。
例句：The company overinvested in marketing, neglecting crucial aspects of product development. 这家公司在市场营销上投入过多，忽略了产品开发的关键方面。|近义词：overspend, overcommit, squander|反义词：underinvest, underfund
