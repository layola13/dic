interred:/ɪnˈtɜːrd/| vt.|埋葬，安葬|To place (a dead body) in a grave or tomb.  The act of burying a dead body. |例句：They interred the body in a quiet cemetery. 他们在宁静的墓地里埋葬了遗体。

例句：The ancient pharaoh was interred with great ceremony.  这位古代法老以盛大的仪式下葬。


近义词：bury, entomb, commit to the earth, inhume|反义词：exhume, disinter|动词


词源:

From Old French *enterrer, from Latin *interrare, from inter "between, among" + terra "earth".  The meaning evolved from "to put into the earth" to specifically "to bury a corpse".
