dramatis personae: /ˈdræmətɪs pərˈsoʊniː/ | n. | 剧中人物；演员表 | The dramatis personae of Shakespeare's Hamlet include Hamlet, Ophelia, and Claudius.  莎士比亚的《哈姆雷特》中的剧中人物包括哈姆雷特、奥菲莉娅和克劳狄斯。| 近义词：cast, characters | 反义词：audience, spectators


USAGE->
This word is a noun phrase and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, typical verb tenses don't apply.  Its usage is primarily descriptive.

Example 1: The program began by listing the dramatis personae. = 节目一开始就列出了剧中人物。
Example 2:  The novel's dramatis personae are richly drawn and compelling. = 这部小说的剧中人物刻画得非常生动逼真，引人入胜。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "dramatis personae."  The phrase itself is a fixed expression.
