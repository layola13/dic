malediction:/ˌmælɪˈdɪkʃən/| n.|诅咒；恶言；咒骂|The utterance of a curse; a wish of evil upon someone.  It can range from a simple verbal denunciation to a formal, ritualistic curse.|例句：The witch cast a malediction upon the village.  女巫对村庄下了诅咒。|例句：His words were filled with malediction and hatred. 他的话充满了诅咒和仇恨。|近义词：curse, imprecation, execration|反义词：benediction, blessing
