channels:/ˈtʃænəlz/ | n. |频道；渠道；途径 | A channel is a route or means of communication or distribution.  It can refer to a television or radio station, a waterway, or a method of conveying information or goods. | Examples:  The news channel reported on the recent events. (新闻频道报道了最近发生的事件。)  They used multiple channels to distribute their product. (他们使用多种渠道分销他们的产品。) |近义词：pathways, conduits, routes, mediums |反义词：blockage, obstruction
