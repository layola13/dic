They are often insincere and obsequious.|例句：He's nothing but a lickspittle, always agreeing with the boss, regardless of what he thinks. 他不过是个拍马屁的，总是同意老板的话，不管他心里怎么想。|例句：The manager surrounded himself with lickspittles who never offered any constructive criticism. 经理身边围绕着一群拍马屁的人，他们从不提出任何建设性的批评。|近义词：flatterer, sycophant, yes-man, toady|反义词：critic, opponent, dissenter|名词
