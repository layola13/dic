convince:/kənˈvɪns/ | vt. | 说服；使相信 | To persuade someone or oneself to believe something or to do something. | 例句：I tried to convince him to stay, but he refused. 我试图说服他留下，但他拒绝了。
                                                                                                    | 例句：She was convinced of her innocence. 她确信自己是无辜的。 |近义词：persuade, satisfy, assure |反义词：dissuade, dishearten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He convinces his audience with his eloquent speech. = 他以其雄辩的演讲说服了他的听众。
I convince myself that everything will be alright. = 我说服自己一切都会好起来的。


一般过去时:
The evidence convinced the jury of his guilt. = 证据使陪审团相信了他的罪行。
She convinced her parents to let her go to the concert. = 她说服了父母让她去听音乐会。


现在完成时:
He has convinced me of the importance of hard work. = 他让我相信努力工作的重要性。
They have convinced themselves that they are right. = 他们已经说服自己他们是正确的。


过去完成时:
I had convinced myself that I would fail before I even started. = 我甚至在开始之前就说服自己我会失败。
She had convinced him to invest in her company before the big announcement. = 在重大宣布之前，她已经说服他投资她的公司。


将来时:
I will convince him to change his mind. = 我会说服他改变主意。
We will convince the board to approve the project. = 我们将说服董事会批准该项目。


虚拟语气:
If I could convince him, I would. = 如果我能说服他，我会的。
I wish I could convince her to see my point of view. = 我希望我能说服她理解我的观点。


被动语态:
The jury was convinced by the evidence. = 陪审团被证据说服了。
He was convinced that he had made the right decision. = 他确信自己做出了正确的决定。


疑问句:
Can you convince her to come with us? = 你能说服她跟我们一起去吗？
Will you be convinced by my explanation? = 你会相信我的解释吗？


否定句:
I couldn't convince him to change his mind. = 我无法说服他改变主意。
She wasn't convinced by his arguments. = 她不被他的论点说服。


PHRASE->
convince someone of something = 使某人相信某事
convince someone to do something = 说服某人做某事
be convinced that = 确信
convinced of = 深信
convince oneself = 自我安慰
