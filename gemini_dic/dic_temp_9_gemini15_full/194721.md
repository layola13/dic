self-worth: /ˈsɛlf wɜːrθ/ | n. | 自我价值，自我认同 |  A feeling of one's own value or worth as a person; self-esteem.  | Example:  Building self-worth is crucial for mental well-being. 建立自我价值对心理健康至关重要。 |近义词: self-esteem, self-respect, confidence | 反义词: self-doubt, low self-esteem, insecurity | 名词
