overconcerned:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsɜːrnd/ | adj. | 过分担心的；过于关切的 | Excessively concerned or worried; showing too much concern.  |例句：She's overconcerned about her son's future. 她对儿子的未来过于担心。 |例句：He was overconcerned with the details and missed the big picture. 他过于关注细节，而忽略了大局。|近义词：anxious, worried, apprehensive, solicitous |反义词：indifferent, unconcerned, apathetic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is overconcerned about his health. 他对自己的健康过于担心。
They are overconcerned with appearances. 他们过于在意外表。


一般过去时:
She was overconcerned about her exam results. 她对考试成绩过于担心。
He was overconcerned that he might fail. 他过于担心自己可能会失败。


现在完成时:
I have been overconcerned about this project for weeks. 我几周来一直对这个项目过于担心。
They have been overconcerned about their children's safety. 他们一直对孩子们的安全过于担心。


过去完成时:
She had been overconcerned about the trip before she left.  在她出发前，她对这次旅行过于担心。
They had been overconcerned about the potential risks. 他们曾对潜在的风险过于担忧。


将来时:
I will be overconcerned until I hear from him. 在收到他的消息之前，我会一直过于担心。
She will be overconcerned if he doesn't call. 如果他不打电话，她会过于担心。


虚拟语气:
If I weren't overconcerned, I would enjoy the party more. 如果我不那么担心，我会更享受这个派对。
I wish I weren't so overconcerned about everything. 我希望自己不要对所有事情都这么担心。


被动语态:
His concerns were considered overconcerned by his friends. 他的担忧被他的朋友们认为是过于担忧的。
The situation was seen as overconcerned by the media.  媒体认为这种情况被夸大了。


疑问句:
Is he overconcerned about his job security? 他是否对自己的工作保障过于担心？

否定句:
He is not overconcerned about the outcome. 他并不担心结果。
