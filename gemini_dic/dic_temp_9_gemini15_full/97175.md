infirmities:/ɪnˈfɜːrmɪtiz/ | n. | 虚弱；疾病；衰老的迹象 |  Weaknesses or failings of character or body; illnesses.  Infirmities often refer to the physical weaknesses associated with old age or illness, but can also refer to moral or intellectual weaknesses. |

例句：

His age and infirmities prevented him from travelling. 他的年龄和疾病使他无法旅行。

The old woman suffered from various infirmities.  这位老妇人患有各种疾病。


近义词：ailments, weaknesses, defects, failings

反义词：strength, health, vigor


词性：名词
