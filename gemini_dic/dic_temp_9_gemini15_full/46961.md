darted:/ˈdɑːrtɪd/ | v. | 快速移动；疾驰；突然移动 | To move suddenly and quickly.  |例句：The rabbit darted into the bushes. 兔子窜进了灌木丛。 The car darted across the intersection. 车子飞快地穿过十字路口。|近义词：sped, shot, zipped, flashed|反义词：crept, crawled, inched


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He darts across the street every morning. = 他每天早上都快速穿过街道。
The bird darts from branch to branch. = 鸟儿在树枝间飞来飞去。

一般过去时:
She darted out of the room. = 她猛地冲出了房间。
The thief darted down the alley. = 小偷飞快地跑进了小巷。

现在完成时:
The squirrel has darted up the tree. = 松鼠已经窜上了树。
He has darted in and out of the office all day. = 他一整天都在办公室里跑来跑去。

过去完成时:
The cat had darted away before I could catch it. = 我还没来得及抓住它，猫就跑掉了。
She had darted through the crowd to reach the stage. = 她穿过人群冲到舞台上。

将来时:
The train will dart through the tunnel. = 火车将快速穿过隧道。
He will dart across the road to avoid the traffic. = 他会快速穿过马路以避开车辆。

虚拟语气:
If I had seen him, I would have darted to his side. = 如果我看到他，我就会立刻跑到他身边。
I wish I had darted out of the way sooner. = 我真希望我早点躲开。

被动语态:  (被动语态较少用，因为dart通常是主动行为)
The ball was darted across the field. = 球被快速地扔过了球场。(比较生硬)
The message was darted to the headquarters. = 消息被迅速传递到总部。(比较生硬)

疑问句:
Did the mouse dart into the hole? = 老鼠窜进洞里了吗？
Will the deer dart across the road? = 鹿会穿过马路吗？

否定句:
He didn't dart away when he saw me. = 他看到我时没有跑开。
The bird didn't dart from branch to branch. = 那只鸟没有在树枝间飞来飞去。


PHRASE->
dart a glance  = 快速地瞥一眼
dart a look  = 快速地看一眼
dart between = 在...之间快速穿梭
dart in and out = 跑来跑去
dart forward = 向前冲刺
