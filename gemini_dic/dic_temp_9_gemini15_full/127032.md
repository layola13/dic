nasopalatine:/ˌneɪzoʊˈpælətaɪn/| adj.|鼻腭的|Relating to the nasal cavity and the palate.  It describes anatomical structures or processes involving both the nasal cavity and the palate (the roof of the mouth).|例句：The nasopalatine nerve supplies sensation to the anterior palate. 鼻腭神经支配前腭的感觉。|The nasopalatine artery supplies blood to the anterior palate. 鼻腭动脉向前腭供血。|近义词：None directly equivalent, but  related terms could include intranasal, palatal.|反义词：None directly opposite.|形容词
