replant:/riːˈplænt/ | vt. |重新种植；再植|To plant something again, especially after it has been removed or destroyed.  |例句：We need to replant the trees that were damaged in the storm. 我们需要重新种植在暴风雨中受损的树木。  |例句：The farmer replanted his crops after the flood. 洪水过后，农民重新种植了他的庄稼。|近义词：replant, replace, renew |反义词：uproot, remove


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They replant the flowers every spring. = 他们每年春天都重新种植花卉。
The gardener replants the herbs regularly. = 园丁定期重新种植香草。


一般过去时:
She replanted the rose bushes after the frost. = 霜冻过后，她重新种植了玫瑰灌木。
The council replanted the trees along the street last year. = 去年市议会重新种植了街道两旁的树木。


现在完成时:
They have replanted the entire orchard. = 他们已经重新种植了整个果园。
The farmers have replanted their rice fields after the drought. = 干旱过后，农民们已经重新种植了他们的稻田。


过去完成时:
They had replanted the damaged crops before the rain came. = 雨来之前，他们已经重新种植了受损的庄稼。
The villagers had replanted the forest after the wildfire. = 村民们在野火之后重新种植了森林。


将来时:
We will replant the garden next month. = 我们下个月将重新种植花园。
The park authorities will replant the trees in the autumn. = 公园管理部门将在秋季重新种植树木。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would replant the whole field. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们会重新种植整个田地。
I wish we could replant the damaged forest quickly. = 我希望我们能够迅速地重新种植受损的森林。


被动语态:
The trees were replanted after the hurricane. = 飓风过后，树木被重新种植了。
The damaged area will be replanted with native species. = 受损区域将种植本地物种。


疑问句:
Will they replant the lawn this year? = 他们今年会重新种植草坪吗？
Have they replanted the shrubs yet? = 他们已经重新种植灌木了吗？


否定句:
They didn't replant the vegetables this season. = 他们本季没有重新种植蔬菜。
He will not replant the damaged plants.  = 他不会重新种植受损的植物。
