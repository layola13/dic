lay-open: /leɪˈoʊpən/ | vt. |摊开；暴露；揭露 | To make something open or accessible; to reveal or expose something. |例句：The surgeon laid open the patient's abdomen. 外科医生剖开了病人的腹部。


The investigation laid open the company's corrupt practices.  调查揭露了公司的腐败行为。|近义词：reveal, expose, uncover, display |反义词：conceal, hide, cover, obscure |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lays open the wound to examine it. = 他打开伤口检查。
She lays open her heart to her friend. = 她向朋友敞开心扉。


一般过去时:
The detective laid open the criminal's plan. = 侦探揭露了罪犯的计划。
They laid open the box to reveal the treasure inside. = 他们打开盒子，露出了里面的宝藏。


现在完成时:
The artist has laid open the complexities of human nature in his paintings. = 艺术家在他的画作中揭示了人性的复杂性。
The scandal has laid open the weaknesses of the government. = 丑闻暴露了政府的弱点。


过去完成时:
The historian had laid open the secrets of the ancient civilization. = 历史学家已经揭开了古代文明的秘密。
They had laid open the truth, despite the risks. = 尽管冒着风险，他们还是揭露了真相。


将来时:
The scientist will lay open the mysteries of the universe. = 科学家将揭开宇宙的奥秘。
We will lay open the box carefully. = 我们将小心地打开盒子。


虚拟语气:
If he laid open his feelings, she would understand. = 如果他坦露自己的感情，她就会理解。
I wish he would lay open the truth to us. = 我希望他会向我们说出真相。


被动语态:
The wound was laid open by the surgeon. = 伤口被外科医生剖开了。
The truth was laid open by the investigation. = 真相被调查揭露了。


疑问句:
Will they lay open the evidence to the public? = 他们会向公众公开证据吗？
Did the author lay open the characters' motivations in the novel? = 作者在小说中揭示了人物的动机吗？


否定句:
He didn't lay open his true intentions. = 他没有公开他的真实意图。
They didn't lay open the full extent of the damage. = 他们没有完全公开损害的程度。


PHRASE->
lay open to: 使容易遭受…；使暴露于…
lay open the facts: 公开事实
lay open one's heart: 敞开心扉
lay open a wound: 打开伤口
lay open the secrets: 揭开秘密
