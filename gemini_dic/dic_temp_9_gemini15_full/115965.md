marrowbones:/ˈmærəʊboʊnz/ | n. |骨髓；骨头；（尤指）牛骨髓 | Marrowbones are the long bones from animals, especially cattle, containing marrow.  They are often used in soups and stews for flavor and richness. | The chef used marrowbones to add depth of flavor to the soup.  厨师用牛骨髓来增加汤的鲜味。  |  bones,  limbs |  (无明确反义词，可考虑反义词为soft tissues) | 名词
