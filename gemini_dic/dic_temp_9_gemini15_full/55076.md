disrepair:/ˌdɪsrɪˈpɛər/ | n. |破损，损坏，失修 | A state of being damaged or neglected; the condition of something that is broken or not working properly.  |The house was in a state of disrepair.  这房子破败不堪。|The old bridge was falling into disrepair. 那座旧桥正在逐渐损坏。|近义词：damage, deterioration, dilapidation |反义词：repair, good condition, maintenance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The building is in disrepair. = 这栋建筑物破败不堪。
The car remains in disrepair. = 这辆车仍然处于失修状态。


一般过去时:
The roof was in disrepair after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，屋顶破损了。
The equipment fell into disrepair. = 设备失修了。


现在完成时:
The bridge has fallen into disrepair. = 这座桥已经破损了。
The house has been in disrepair for years. = 这房子已经破败多年了。


过去完成时:
The car had been in disrepair for months before it was finally fixed. = 这辆车在最终修理之前已经坏了好几个月了。
The infrastructure had fallen into disrepair due to neglect. = 由于疏忽，基础设施已经破败不堪。


将来时:
The building will be in disrepair if it isn't maintained. = 如果不维护，这栋建筑物将会破败不堪。
The road will likely fall into disrepair without regular repairs. = 这条路如果不定期维修，很可能会损坏。


虚拟语气:
If the bridge wasn't in disrepair, we could use it. = 如果桥没有损坏，我们就可以使用它。
I wish the house wasn't in such a state of disrepair. = 我希望这房子不要这么破败。


被动语态:
The house was left in disrepair. = 这房子被丢弃，处于破败状态。
The park's facilities are being allowed to fall into disrepair. = 公园的设施正在被任由损坏。


疑问句:
Is the car in disrepair? = 这辆车坏了吗？
Is the building in a state of disrepair? = 这栋建筑物是否破败不堪？


否定句:
The equipment is not in disrepair. = 设备没有损坏。
The car is not in a state of disrepair. = 这辆车没有损坏。
