gardenful: /ˈɡɑːrdənfl/ | adj. | 花园似的；充满花园气息的 |  Full of gardens; resembling a garden.  This word is not common and is often considered informal or nonce word (a word coined for a specific occasion). It suggests abundance and beauty associated with gardens.  |  The gardenful scene was breathtaking.  = 花园般的景色令人叹为观止。 The cottage was surrounded by a gardenful array of flowers. = 小屋周围环绕着花园般盛开的鲜花。|  flowery, lush, blooming | barren, desolate |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The garden is beautiful. = 花园很漂亮。
He plants flowers in the garden. = 他在花园里种花。


一般过去时:
She visited a beautiful garden yesterday. = 她昨天参观了一个美丽的公园。
They gardened all afternoon. = 他们整个下午都在园艺。


现在完成时:
I have gardened for many years. = 我已经园艺多年了。
We have planted many flowers in the garden this year. = 今年我们在花园里种了很多花。


过去完成时:
They had already finished gardening before it rained. = 下雨之前他们已经完成了园艺工作。
She had never seen such a beautiful garden before. = 她以前从未见过如此美丽的公园。


将来时:
We will visit the botanical garden tomorrow. = 我们明天将参观植物园。
He will plant more roses in his garden next spring. = 明年春天他将在他的花园里种植更多的玫瑰。


虚拟语气:
If I had a garden, I would plant roses. = 如果我有一个花园，我会种玫瑰。
I wish I had a gardenful of flowers. = 我希望我有一个充满鲜花的花园。


被动语态:

疑问句:

Is the garden open to the public? = 花园对公众开放吗？


否定句:
