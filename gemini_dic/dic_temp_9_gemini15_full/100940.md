inundant:/ˈɪnʌndænt/| adj.| 泛滥的；淹没的；大量的 | overflowing; flooded; abundant | The inundant river threatened to flood the town. 泛滥的河流威胁着要淹没小镇。  The inundant paperwork kept him busy all day.  大量的文书工作让他忙了一整天。|近义词：overwhelming, abundant, overflowing |反义词：scarce, lacking, deficient


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The river inundates the low-lying areas every spring. = 春天河水都会淹没低洼地区。
Inundant rain causes significant damage to crops. = 大雨对庄稼造成了严重的损害。


一般过去时:
The heavy rain inundated the village. = 大雨淹没了村庄。
The sudden influx of tourists inundated the small town. = 游客的突然涌入淹没了这个小镇。


现在完成时:
The area has been inundated by floodwaters several times this year. = 今年该地区已经被洪水淹没过几次了。
The market has been inundated with cheap imports. = 市场已经被廉价进口商品充斥了。


过去完成时:
The basement had been inundated before the emergency services arrived. = 紧急服务人员到达之前，地下室已经被淹没了。
The company had been inundated with applications for the job. = 公司已经被大量的求职申请淹没了。


将来时:
The forecast predicts that the coastal areas will be inundated by the storm. = 预报预测沿海地区将被风暴淹没。
If the dam breaks, the valley will be inundated. = 如果大坝决堤，山谷将被淹没。


虚拟语气:
If the dam were to break, the entire valley would be inundated. = 如果大坝决堤，整个山谷都将被淹没。
I wish the city weren't inundated with traffic every day. = 我希望这座城市每天不要被交通堵塞。


被动语态:
The fields were inundated by the rising river. = 田地被上涨的河水淹没了。
The town was inundated with tourists during the summer. = 夏季期间，小镇被游客挤满了。


疑问句:
Was the village inundated by the storm? = 村庄被暴风雨淹没了吗？
Is the market inundated with similar products? = 市场充斥着类似的产品吗？


否定句:
The farmland was not inundated this year. = 今年农田没有被淹没。
The shop was not inundated with customers despite the sale. = 尽管打折，商店也没有被顾客挤满。

inundant praise = 铺天盖地的赞扬
