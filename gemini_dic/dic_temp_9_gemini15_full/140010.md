oecumenical:/ˌiːkjuˈmenɪkəl/| adj.|普世的；世界性的；大公的| relating to or representing the whole Christian world or the whole of Christendom.  The word implies a spirit of unity among different Christian denominations.|例句：The oecumenical council aimed to foster unity among the various Christian churches.  这次普世会议旨在促进不同基督教教会之间的团结。

例句：The oecumenical movement seeks to overcome divisions within Christianity. 普世运动旨在克服基督教内部的分裂。


近义词：universal, ecumenical, catholic (in the sense of universal)


反义词：sectarian, divisive, parochial


词性：形容词
