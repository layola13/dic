halitosis:/ˌhæliˈtoʊsɪs/| n. |口臭 |Bad breath; a condition characterized by unpleasant-smelling breath.  Halitosis can be caused by various factors, including poor oral hygiene, gum disease, certain foods, and medical conditions. | Example:  He suffered from halitosis, which made social interaction difficult. 他患有口臭，这使得社交变得困难。 |近义词：bad breath, foul breath, fetid breath |反义词：sweet breath, fresh breath
