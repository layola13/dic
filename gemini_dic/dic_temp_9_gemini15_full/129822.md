non-Hebraic: /ˌnɑːn hɪˈbreɪɪk/ | adj. | 非希伯来语的 | Not relating to or derived from Hebrew.  This adjective describes something that is not connected to the Hebrew language, culture, or people.  It can refer to languages, texts, customs, or any other aspect. |

例句：

The museum houses a collection of non-Hebraic artifacts from around the world. = 博物馆收藏了来自世界各地的非希伯来语系文物。

This research focuses on non-Hebraic influences on medieval European literature. = 这项研究侧重于中世纪欧洲文学中非希伯来语的影响。


近义词：non-Jewish (in some contexts), foreign (depending on context), non-Israelite


反义词：Hebraic, Jewish (in some contexts)


词性：形容词
