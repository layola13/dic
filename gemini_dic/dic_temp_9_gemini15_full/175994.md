rattoon:/ræˈtuːn/| n.| 甘蔗芽；分蘖；从根部长出的幼苗 | A rattoon is a shoot that grows from the base of a sugarcane plant after harvesting.  It's also used to describe the process of growing sugarcane from these shoots.  | Example: The farmers carefully nurtured the rattoons to ensure a successful harvest.  甘蔗农小心翼翼地培育甘蔗芽以确保丰收。|近义词：sprout, sucker, shoot |反义词： (无明确反义词，可考虑与成熟甘蔗相对) mature cane
