hoods:/hʊdz/ | n. |  兜帽；引擎盖；（尤指城市贫民区的）街区 |  A hood is a covering for the head and neck, often attached to a coat or jacket.  It can also refer to the metal covering over the engine of a car, or a neighborhood, especially a poor or dangerous one. |例句：He pulled his hood up over his head to keep warm. 他把兜帽戴在头上保暖。  The mechanic raised the hood of the car to check the engine. 修理工掀起了汽车的引擎盖检查发动机。|近义词：cap, cowl, bonnet (for car hood), neighborhood, district |反义词：open (for hood of a car)
