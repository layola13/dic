reinventions:/ˌriːɪnˈvɛnʃənz/| n.|再发明；革新；重新创造 |The act of inventing something again or anew; a new version or form of something that already exists.|例句：The company's reinvention involved a complete overhaul of its marketing strategy. 公司的革新包括对其营销策略的彻底改革。|例句：His reinvention as a successful entrepreneur was remarkable. 他作为一名成功企业家的重新塑造令人瞩目。|近义词：renovations, transformations, innovations|反义词：destruction, demolition
