sassafras:/ˈsæsəfræs/| n.|北美檫木，檫木树，檫木根，檫木油|A tree of the laurel family, native to eastern North America, having aromatic bark and roots, used medicinally and as a flavoring. |The sassafras tree is known for its fragrant leaves and roots.  北美檫木以其芬芳的叶子和根而闻名。|The sassafras oil is used as a flavoring agent in root beer.  檫木油被用作沙士汽水的调味剂。|近义词：spicebush, camphor tree |反义词：无
