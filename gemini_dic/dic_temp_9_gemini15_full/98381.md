insofar:/ˌɪnsoʊˈfɑːr/ | adv. | 就…而言；在…范围内 | To the extent or degree that; as far as.  |  例句：Insofar as it is possible, we will help.  就我们力所能及的范围而言，我们会提供帮助。


例句：The plan is good insofar as it addresses the main problem.  就其解决了主要问题而言，这个计划是好的。


近义词：to the extent that, to the degree that


反义词：completely, entirely, totally (in contexts where insofar implies limitation)


词性: 副词
