neoplatonic:/ˌniːoʊˈplætɒnɪk/ | adj. | 新柏拉图主义的 | Relating to Neoplatonism, a system of philosophy that developed from Plato's teachings in the 3rd century CE and emphasized the existence of a transcendent One or Good from which all things emanate.  It combined elements of Plato's philosophy with other mystical and religious ideas.  |  The Neoplatonic school of thought greatly influenced early Christian theology.  新柏拉图主义的思想体系极大地影响了早期基督教神学。 |  Platonic, mystical |  materialistic |  形容词
