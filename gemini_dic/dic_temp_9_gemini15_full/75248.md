from hand to mouth: /frɒm hænd tu maʊθ/ | adj. |勉强糊口，入不敷出 | barely having enough money to survive; living from day to day without savings.  |例句：Many families in this region live from hand to mouth.  这个地区的许多家庭都勉强糊口。

例句：The recent economic downturn has left many people living from hand to mouth. 近期经济衰退导致许多人入不敷出。


近义词： impoverished, destitute, penniless, impecunious

反义词： affluent, wealthy, prosperous, well-off

词性：形容词
