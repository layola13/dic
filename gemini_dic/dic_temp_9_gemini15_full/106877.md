The city's streets formed a labyrinthine, labyrinthal network, making it difficult to find our way.  城市的街道构成了一个迷宫般的网络，很难找到方向。

The plot of the novel was labyrinthal, with numerous twists and turns that kept the reader guessing. 这部小说的情节错综复杂，充满了无数的曲折，让读者不断猜测。
