quinone:/ˈkwɪnoʊn/| n. |醌；苯醌|A class of organic compounds containing a cyclic conjugated diketone functional group.  Quinones are important in biological systems, acting as electron carriers in respiration and photosynthesis. They are also used in various industrial applications, such as dyes and pigments.|Example: Ubiquinone, also known as coenzyme Q10, is a vital quinone involved in cellular respiration.  泛醌，也称为辅酶Q10，是一种参与细胞呼吸的重要醌。|近义词：benzoquinone, naphthoquinone|反义词：hydroquinone
