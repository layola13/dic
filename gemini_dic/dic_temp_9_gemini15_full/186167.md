rheometer:/raɪˈɒmɪtər/ | n. | 流变仪 | An instrument used to measure the flow and deformation characteristics of materials, especially fluids.  It measures viscosity, elasticity, and other rheological properties. | Example: The rheometer was used to determine the viscosity of the polymer solution.  流变仪被用来测定聚合物溶液的粘度。 |近义词：viscometer (for viscosity measurements only) | 反义词：无
