she:/ʃiː/| pron.|她|She is a kind woman. 她是一位善良的女人。|近义词：her|反义词：he, him


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She sings beautifully. = 她唱歌很美。

She works hard every day. = 她每天努力工作。


一般过去时:
She went to the park yesterday. = 她昨天去了公园。

She cooked dinner for us. = 她为我们做了晚饭。


现在完成时:
She has finished her homework. = 她已经完成了作业。

She has lived here for ten years. = 她在这里住了十年了。


过去完成时:
She had already left when I arrived. = 我到达时她已经离开了。

She had studied English before she went to college. = 她上大学前学过英语。


将来时:
She will travel to Europe next year. = 她明年将去欧洲旅行。

She will be a doctor one day. = 她将来会成为一名医生。


虚拟语气:
If she were here, she would help us. = 如果她在，她会帮助我们。

I wish she were more careful. = 我希望她更细心一点。


被动语态:
She was praised for her work. = 她因工作受到表扬。

She is loved by everyone. = 她受大家的喜爱。


疑问句:
Is she coming to the party? = 她来参加聚会吗？

Who is she? = 她是谁？


否定句:
She is not happy today. = 她今天不开心。

She didn't go to school yesterday. = 她昨天没去上学。



PHRASE->
It's she. = 是她。
She herself did it. = 她自己做的。
She is in charge. = 她负责。
As for her... = 关于她…
Let her be. = 让她去吧。
