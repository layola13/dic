contractee:/ˌkɒntrækˈtiː/| n. |合同签订者，承包人|A party to a contract, typically the one who receives services or goods.|The contractee was satisfied with the quality of the work.  承包人对工程质量感到满意。|近义词：contractor (in some contexts), client, recipient |反义词：contractor (in some contexts)
