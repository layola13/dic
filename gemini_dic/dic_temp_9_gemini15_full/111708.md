locksteps:/ˈlɒkˌstɛps/ | n. |齐步走；步调一致；整齐划一 |The act of moving in unison, in a coordinated and synchronized manner, often in a line or formation.  It implies a lack of individuality or independent action. |例句：The soldiers marched in lockstep. 士兵们齐步前进。

The team worked in lockstep to achieve their goal.  团队齐心协力地实现了目标。|近义词：unison, synchrony, uniformity, conformity |反义词：disarray, chaos, discord, independence
