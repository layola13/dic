expostulating:/ɪkˈspɒstʃʊleɪtɪŋ/| v.| 规劝；强烈抗议；争辩；抱怨 |To reason earnestly with someone in an attempt to dissuade them from a course of action.  To express strong disapproval or protest. | 例句：He spent the entire evening expostulating with his son about the importance of studying. 他整个晚上都在苦口婆心地劝说儿子学习的重要性。


He was expostulating with the manager about the poor service. 他正在向经理强烈抗议糟糕的服务。


近义词：remonstrate, protest, object, argue, plead | 反义词：acquiesce, agree, comply


USAGE->

He expostulates with his friend about his reckless driving. = 他劝说他的朋友不要鲁莽驾驶。
She expostulates with her children about the mess they made. = 她责备她的孩子们弄乱了东西。
