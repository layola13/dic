cremating: /krɪˈmeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 火化 | The act of burning a dead body to ashes.  Cremating is a common practice in many cultures as a way to dispose of the deceased.  | The family decided to cremate their grandmother after her passing.  家人决定在她去世后火化他们的祖母。 |  incinerating, burning | burying, burying, entombment | 动词
