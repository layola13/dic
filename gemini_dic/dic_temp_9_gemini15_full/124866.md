mucus:/ˈmjuːkəs/| n.|粘液，黏膜分泌物|a slippery secretion produced by mucous membranes and glands, chiefly composed of glycoproteins, cells, and inorganic salts.|例句：The mucus in my nose is thick and yellow. 我的鼻涕又浓又黄。|例句：Excessive mucus production can cause a cough.  黏液分泌过多会导致咳嗽。|近义词：phlegm, slime|反义词：dryness
