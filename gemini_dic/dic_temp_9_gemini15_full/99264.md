intercondensers:/ˌɪntərˈkɒndɛnsərz/| n. |互联电容器|A device consisting of two or more condensers connected together, often to increase capacitance or improve performance in a circuit.|例句：The circuit uses intercondensers to smooth the fluctuating voltage.  该电路使用互联电容器来平滑波动电压。
The design of the intercondensers is crucial for optimal performance. 互联电容器的设计对于最佳性能至关重要。|近义词：capacitors (in some contexts)|反义词：N/A
