chunky:/ˈtʃʌŋki/ | adj. | 矮胖的；粗大的；厚实的；块状的 |  Describing something that is short and thick, or consisting of large pieces.  |例句：He wore chunky sweaters. 他穿着厚实的毛衣。

例句：The soup had chunky vegetables. 这汤里有块状的蔬菜。


近义词：stocky, stout, thick, bulky, solid | 反义词：slender, thin, delicate, slight


词源:  The word "chunky" is derived from "chunk," meaning a thick, solid piece of something.  "Chunk" itself likely originates from the Old Norse word "kank," meaning "a lump or mass."  The suffix "-y" adds an adjectival quality, indicating abundance or possession of the characteristic.
