huskily:/ˈhʌskɪli/| adj.|沙哑地，嘶哑地| sounding hoarse or rough, as if from a sore throat or from shouting.|He spoke huskily, his voice barely a whisper. 他沙哑地说着话，声音几乎是耳语。|She whispered huskily into his ear. 她在他耳边沙哑地耳语。|近义词：hoarsely, raspyly|反义词：clearly, smoothly
