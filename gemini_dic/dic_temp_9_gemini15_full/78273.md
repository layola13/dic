germanize:/ˈdʒɜːrmənaɪz/| vt.|使德意志化；使具有德国特征|To make something German in character or style.  This can refer to language, culture, or political systems.|例句：The occupying forces attempted to germanize the conquered territories. 占领军试图使被征服的领土德意志化。|例句：He germanized the names of the streets in his historical novel. 他在他的历史小说中将街道名称德意志化了。|近义词：germanify|反义词：de-germanize
