repatches:/riːˈpætʃɪz/| v.|重新修补；再次修补 | To repair something again, especially after it has been damaged again.  |例句：The mechanic repatched the tire after it was punctured a second time.  机械师在轮胎第二次被刺破后又重新修补了它。

例句：After the heavy rain, the farmer had to repatch the roof of his barn. 大雨过后，农民不得不重新修补谷仓的屋顶。


近义词：repair, mend, fix


反义词：destroy, damage, ruin


词性：动词
