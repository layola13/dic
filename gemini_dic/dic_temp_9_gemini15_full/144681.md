outyelps:/ˌaʊtˈjɛlps/| v. |大声喊叫过…|To yell or shout louder than (someone or something).  This is a relatively uncommon word, formed by combining "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) with "yelps." It implies a contest or comparison of loudness.|例句：The child outyelped the dog, drowning out its barks with her piercing cries. 孩子比狗叫得更大声，她的尖锐哭声盖过了狗吠声。


例句：The singer outyelped her backup singers, creating a powerful and dynamic performance.  歌手的喊叫声盖过了伴唱，创造了强劲而充满活力的表演。


近义词：outshout, outscream, overpower (in terms of volume)


反义词：whisper, murmur, mumble


词性:动词
