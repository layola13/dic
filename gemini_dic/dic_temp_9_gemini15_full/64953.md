esthete:/ˈɛsθiːt/| n.|唯美主义者；审美家|A person who has or affect a great love for and appreciation of beauty, especially in art.|例句：He was an esthete, spending hours admiring the subtle nuances of the painting. 他是个唯美主义者，花费数小时欣赏绘画的细微差别。

例句：The esthete's apartment was a testament to his refined taste.  这位唯美主义者的公寓是他高雅品味的证明。


近义词：aesthete, connoisseur, dilettante|反义词： philistine, barbarian, vulgarian|词性:名词


词源:  From French *esthète*, from Greek *αἰσθητικός* (aisthētikós), from *αἴσθησις* (aísthēsis) "perception, sensation".  Ultimately derived from the Greek verb *αἰσθάνομαι* (aisthánoma), "I perceive."
