nonpensioner: /ˌnɒnˈpɛnʃənər/ | n. | 非领取养老金者 | A person who does not receive a pension.  This term typically refers to someone who is not yet eligible for a pension, or who has chosen not to receive one. | Example:  Many nonpensioners rely on savings and investments for their retirement income.  许多非领取养老金者依靠储蓄和投资来获得退休收入。|近义词: non-recipient of pension, non-pensioner |反义词: pensioner
