prerequisite:/ˌpriːˈrɛkwɪzɪt/| n. |先决条件，必备条件 | a thing that is required as a prior condition for something else to happen or exist.  |Example: A good understanding of algebra is a prerequisite for this course.  学习这门课程的前提是良好的代数基础。
|近义词：requirement, precondition, necessity |反义词：optional, unnecessary |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is the prerequisite for this job? = 这份工作的先决条件是什么？
What are the prerequisites for university admission? = 大学入学的先决条件是什么？


一般过去时:
What was the prerequisite for the previous job? = 上份工作的先决条件是什么？

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
