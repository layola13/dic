resultantly:/rɪˈzʌltəntli/ | adv. | 结果地，因此 | As a consequence; therefore.  It describes a consequence or effect resulting from a previous action or event. |  The rain was heavy; resultantly, the game was postponed.  雨下得很大；因此，比赛被推迟了。 The experiment failed; resultantly, we had to start over. 实验失败了；因此，我们不得不重新开始。 | consequently, therefore, hence, thus, accordingly |  incidentally, coincidentally | 副词
