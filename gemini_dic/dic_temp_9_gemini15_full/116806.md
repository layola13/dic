maws:/mɔːz/| n.|嘴巴，胃口，吞噬力|The jaws or mouth of a voracious animal.  Often used figuratively to describe a large opening or a capacity for consuming large quantities.|例句：The shark opened its maws to reveal rows of razor-sharp teeth.  鲨鱼张开大嘴，露出排排锋利的牙齿。|例句：The city's maws consumed everything in its path. 城市像个巨兽一样吞噬着它所到之处的一切。|近义词：jaws, mouth, gullet|反义词：N/A
