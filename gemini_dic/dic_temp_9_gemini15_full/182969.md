replantation:/ˌriːplænˈteɪʃən/| n. |再种植；再植；再植手术|The act of planting something again, especially a tooth or other body part that has been removed or lost.  It often refers to surgical procedures. |例句：The surgeon performed a successful replantation of the severed finger.  外科医生成功地进行了断指再植手术。

例句：The replantation of the trees after the storm helped restore the landscape.  暴风雨过后重新种植树木有助于恢复景观。


近义词：replanting, transplantation, regrafting


反义词：removal, extraction, excision


词性:名词
