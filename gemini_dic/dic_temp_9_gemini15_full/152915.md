pedestrian:/pəˈdɛstriən/| n. |行人，步行者| a person walking, especially in a town or city.  |例句：The pedestrian was hit by a car.  这位行人被车撞了。|例句：Pedestrians should use the crosswalk. 行人应该使用人行横道。|近义词：walker, stroller |反义词：driver, motorist |名词
