fellest:/ˈfɛləst/| adj.| 最残酷的，最邪恶的 |  The superlative form of "fell," meaning cruel or deadly. It describes something that is the most cruel or wicked.  This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English. | Example: The fellest tyrant the kingdom had ever known ruled with an iron fist.  = 这个王国有史以来最残酷的暴君铁腕统治。| 近义词: most cruel, most wicked, most savage | 反义词: most kind, most merciful, most benevolent | 形容词
