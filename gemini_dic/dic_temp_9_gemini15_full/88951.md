hole-up: /ˈhoʊl ʌp/ | v. | 躲藏；隐匿；藏身 | To hide or conceal oneself, typically in a secluded or protected place, often to avoid danger or the law. |例句：The robbers holed up in an abandoned warehouse after the bank robbery. 抢劫犯在银行抢劫后躲藏在一间废弃的仓库里。
The fugitive holed up in the mountains for weeks, evading capture.  逃犯在山上躲藏了几周，躲避抓捕。|近义词：hide, conceal, secrete, shelter |反义词：reveal, expose, show
