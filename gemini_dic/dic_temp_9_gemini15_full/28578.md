careered:/kəˈrɪrd/| v.| 猛冲；飞奔；（事业）迅速发展 | to move or proceed rapidly and often recklessly; to have a rapid and successful career | 例句：The car careened around the corner.  = 汽车猛拐过弯。 The young lawyer careened through the ranks of the firm. = 这位年轻的律师在律师事务所迅速升迁。|近义词：sped, dashed, raced, zoomed | 反义词：crawled, inched, crept


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He careens down the hill every day. = 他每天都飞奔下山。
The company careens towards success. = 公司正朝着成功飞速发展。

一般过去时:
The horse careened wildly across the field. = 马在田野里狂奔。
Her career careened to new heights after that award. = 获得那个奖项后，她的事业达到了新的高度。

现在完成时:
The car has careened off the road several times. = 这辆车已经几次冲出公路了。
He has careened through life, achieving great things but also facing many challenges. = 他的人生经历跌宕起伏，取得了巨大的成就，但也面临着许多挑战。

过去完成时:
The runaway train had careened into a ditch before rescuers arrived. = 失控的火车在救援人员到达之前冲进了一个沟里。
Before the scandal, her career had careened towards unprecedented success. = 在丑闻爆发之前，她的事业正朝着前所未有的成功发展。

将来时:
The boat will careen dangerously close to the rocks. = 船将危险地靠近岩石。
If she continues working hard, her career will careen to greater heights. = 如果她继续努力工作，她的事业将会攀登更高的顶峰。

虚拟语气:
If he had not careened so recklessly, he might not have had the accident. = 如果他不那么鲁莽地飞奔，他可能就不会发生事故了。
If my career had careened in a different direction, I might be a different person today. = 如果我的职业生涯朝着不同的方向发展，我今天可能就是另一个人了。

被动语态: (此词较少用于被动语态)
The car was careened into a wall. = 汽车撞上了墙。(强调汽车是被撞的，而不是主动猛冲)
The project was careened towards completion with the help of additional staff. = 在额外员工的帮助下，项目迅速推进。(更常见的表达会使用其他动词)


疑问句:
Did the car careen off the road? = 汽车冲出公路了吗？
Will his career careen towards greatness? = 他的事业会走向伟大吗？

否定句:
The car didn't careen off the road. = 汽车没有冲出公路。
His career didn't careen towards success as he had hoped. = 他的事业并没有像他希望的那样走向成功。


PHRASE->
careen out of control = 失控
careen down the road = 沿着道路飞驰
careen into something = 猛撞到某物上
careen towards failure = 朝着失败的方向发展
careen through life = 经历跌宕起伏的人生
