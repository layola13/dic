polymath:/ˈpɒliˌmæθ/ /ˈpɑːliˌmæθ/| n. |博学者，通才 | A person of wide-ranging knowledge or learning.  Someone who excels in many different fields of study. | Leonardo da Vinci was a renowned polymath, excelling in art, science, and engineering.  列奥纳多·达芬奇是一位著名的博学者，在艺术、科学和工程方面都非常出色。 | genius, expert, virtuoso | specialist, novice | 名词
