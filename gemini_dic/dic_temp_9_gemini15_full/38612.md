concretes:/kənˈkriːts/| v.|具体化，凝固，使凝固|To make (something) concrete or solid; to give definite form to something abstract.|例句：The architect concretes his ideas into a detailed blueprint. 建筑师将他的想法具体化为详细的蓝图。|近义词：solidify, harden, materialize|反义词：abstract, generalize
