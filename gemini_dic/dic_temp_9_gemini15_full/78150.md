geophysics:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈfɪzɪks/ | n. |地球物理学|The science that deals with the physical processes and properties of the Earth and its surrounding space environment.  It encompasses a wide range of topics, including seismology, geodesy, geochronology, geomagnetism, and more. |Geophysics helps us understand earthquakes, volcanoes, and the Earth's magnetic field. |近义词：geological science, earth science |反义词：无
