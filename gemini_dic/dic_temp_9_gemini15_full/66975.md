experimenting:/ɪkˈsperɪmɛntɪŋ/| v.|进行实验；尝试|The act of trying out new things or methods; conducting experiments.|例句：Scientists are experimenting with a new drug to treat cancer. 科学家们正在实验一种治疗癌症的新药。
例句：She's experimenting with different hair styles.  她在尝试不同的发型。|近义词：trying, testing, exploring|反义词：proving, confirming


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are experimenting with new teaching methods. = 他们在尝试新的教学方法。
We are experimenting with different recipes for the cake. = 我们正在尝试不同的蛋糕食谱。

一般过去时:
The scientists experimented on animals. = 科学家们对动物进行了实验。
She experimented with various colors before choosing one. = 她尝试了各种颜色才选定了一种。

现在完成时:
They have been experimenting with this technology for years. = 他们多年来一直在试验这项技术。
Scientists have experimented extensively with gene editing. = 科学家们已经广泛地进行了基因编辑实验。

过去完成时:
They had experimented with several approaches before finding a solution. = 在找到解决方案之前，他们已经尝试了几种方法。
The researchers had experimented extensively with the drug before human trials. = 在人体试验之前，研究人员已经对该药物进行了广泛的实验。

将来时:
They will be experimenting with new materials next year. = 他们明年将尝试新的材料。
We will experiment with different marketing strategies. = 我们将尝试不同的营销策略。

虚拟语气:
If they experimented more, they might find a better solution. = 如果他们多做些实验，可能会找到更好的解决方案。
I wish I had experimented with more colors in my painting. = 我希望我在绘画中尝试更多颜色。

被动语态:
New techniques are being experimented with in the laboratory. = 新技术正在实验室里进行实验。
The drug was experimented on mice before human trials. = 在人体试验之前，这种药物曾在老鼠身上进行过实验。

疑问句:
Are they experimenting with a new vaccine? = 他们正在试验新的疫苗吗？
What are you experimenting with in the kitchen? = 你在厨房里尝试什么？

否定句:
They are not experimenting with dangerous chemicals. = 他们没有使用危险化学品进行实验。
She did not experiment with the new software. = 她没有尝试使用新的软件。


PHRASE->
experiment on/with: 对…进行实验
experiment with:  尝试；试验
carry out an experiment: 进行实验
conduct an experiment: 实施实验
scientific experiment: 科学实验
