polyserositis:/ˌpɒliˌsɛroʊˈsaɪtɪs/| n. |多浆膜炎|Inflammation of several serous membranes (the membranes lining the chest, abdomen, and other body cavities).  It's often a symptom of other diseases, rather than a disease itself.  Examples of conditions that can cause polyserositis include lupus, rheumatoid arthritis, and infections.|The patient presented with polyserositis, indicating a possible autoimmune disorder.|近义词：serositis (单浆膜炎),  pleuritis (胸膜炎), peritonitis (腹膜炎)|反义词：无
