deputations:/ˌdepjʊˈteɪʃənz/ | n. |代表团；代表派遣 | A deputation is a group of people sent to represent others, especially to speak to someone in authority.  A deputation can also refer to the act of sending such a group. | 例句：A deputation of workers went to see the manager to discuss their grievances. 工人们派了一个代表团去见经理，讨论他们的不满。  The company received a deputation from the local residents protesting the new factory. 公司接到了当地居民的代表团，抗议新建工厂。 | 近义词：delegation, mission, embassy | 反义词：individual, lone representative |  词性：名词
