cartwheeled:/ˈkɑːrtˌwiːld/ | v. |侧手翻，翻筋斗 | To move or cause to move by turning repeatedly end over end, like a cartwheel.  |例句：The gymnast cartwheeled across the floor with grace. 体操运动员优雅地在木地板上翻着筋斗。

例句：The child cartwheeled with unrestrained joy.  孩子兴高采烈地翻着筋斗。


近义词：somersaulted, tumbled, rolled


反义词：None (antonyms are difficult to define for this verb as it describes a specific action)


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cartwheels across the field. 他在田野里翻筋斗。
She cartwheels effortlessly. 她轻松地翻着筋斗。

一般过去时:
He cartwheeled across the field. 他在田野里翻了个筋斗。
She cartwheeled effortlessly. 她轻松地翻了个筋斗。

现在完成时:
He has cartwheeled across the field many times. 他在田野里翻过很多次筋斗。
She has cartwheeled since she was a child. 她从孩提时代就开始翻筋斗。

过去完成时:
He had cartwheeled before he fell. 他摔倒之前翻过筋斗。
She had cartwheeled many times that day. 那天她已经翻过很多次筋斗了。

词源:
The word "cartwheel" is a compound word.  "Cart" refers to a type of two-wheeled vehicle, and "wheel" refers to a circular rotating object. The action of cartwheeling visually resembles a cart rotating on its wheels. The verb form emerged from the noun's descriptive action.
