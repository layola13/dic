commingle:/kəˈmɪŋɡəl/ | vt. |混合；混杂；融合 | To mix or blend together.  |例句：The two rivers commingle before flowing into the sea.  两条河流在流入大海之前汇合了。

例句：The different cultures commingled in the vibrant city.  不同的文化在这个充满活力的城市中融合在一起。


近义词：blend, mix, merge, intermingle, fuse | 反义词：separate, segregate, divide
