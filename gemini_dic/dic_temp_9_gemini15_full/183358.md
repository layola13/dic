reproducers:/rɪˈpruːdəsərz/| n.|繁殖者；复制器；复制设备|A reproducer is something or someone that reproduces something else, whether it's offspring or copies of something.|例句：Many animals are efficient reproducers. 许多动物都是高效的繁殖者。  The company invested in new reproducers to increase output. 公司投资了新的复制设备以提高产量。|近义词：duplicators, copiers, breeders|反义词：destroyers, obliterators


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine reproduces documents quickly. = 这台机器快速复制文件。
These plants reproduce easily from cuttings. = 这些植物很容易从插枝繁殖。


一般过去时:
The artist reproduced the painting perfectly. = 艺术家完美地复制了这幅画。
