nonfelicitously:/ˌnɒnˈfɛlɪsɪtlɪ/| adj. | 不幸地；不适当地；不巧地 |  In a way that is not appropriate or fortunate; awkwardly or inappropriately.  The word combines "non-" (not) and "felicitous" (appropriate, well-chosen). It describes actions, words, or situations that are clumsy, ill-timed, or otherwise undesirable. | The comedian's joke fell nonfelicitously flat.  喜剧演员的笑话不幸地完全失败了。  |The timing of his announcement was nonfelicitous. 他宣布的时机很不巧。| awkwardly, inappropriately, unfortunately, unsuitably | felicitously, aptly, suitably, appropriately  | adj.
