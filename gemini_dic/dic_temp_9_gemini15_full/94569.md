impounders:/ɪmˈpaʊndərz/| n. |扣押者；没收者；充公者 |Those who impound something, typically vehicles or property.  They are often officials or authorities with the legal power to seize and hold something. | The impounders towed away the illegally parked cars. 扣押者拖走了违章停放的汽车。 |seizers, confiscators|  --- |  n.
