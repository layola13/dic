neologist:/niːˈɒlədʒɪst/| n. |新词创造者；新词语使用者| A neologist is a person who coins or uses newly invented words or expressions.  They are often involved in the creation and popularization of neologisms. | Example:  The neologist introduced a new term into the scientific lexicon.  这位新词创造者在科学词汇中引进了一个新词。 |近义词：word coiner, innovator (in language)|反义词：None specifically, but perhaps someone who strictly adheres to traditional language.
