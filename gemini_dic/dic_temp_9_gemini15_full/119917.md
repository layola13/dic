milk:/mɪlk/| n.|牛奶|A white liquid produced by the mammary glands of female mammals, used as food by humans and other animals.  |例句：I drink a glass of milk every morning. 我每天早上喝一杯牛奶。|例句：The baby is crying because it wants milk. 宝宝哭了，因为它想要喝奶。|近义词：breast milk, cream|反义词：无
