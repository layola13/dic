equate:/ɪˈkweɪt/| vt.| 等同；使相等；认为…相等 | To consider or treat as equal or the same.  |例句：Don't equate success with money. 不要把成功等同于金钱。
例句：Many people equate happiness with wealth. 许多人把幸福等同于财富。|近义词：equal, identify, parallel, liken |反义词：differentiate, distinguish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He equates hard work with success. = 他把努力工作与成功等同起来。
They equate freedom with democracy. = 他们把自由与民主等同起来。


一般过去时:
She equated his silence with agreement. = 她把他的沉默理解为同意。
The judge equated the two offenses. = 法官将这两项罪行等同起来。


现在完成时:
Scientists have equated this phenomenon with global warming. = 科学家们已经将这种现象与全球变暖联系起来。
We have equated knowledge with power for centuries. = 我们几个世纪以来都把知识与权力等同起来。


过去完成时:
They had equated the two theories before discovering their differences. = 他们在发现两者的不同之前，曾把这两个理论等同起来。
She had equated his anger with concern until he spoke. = 直到他说话，她才明白他的愤怒不是关心。


将来时:
He will equate his father's legacy with his own achievements.  = 他将把父亲的遗产与他自己的成就等同起来。
Future historians will likely equate this era with rapid technological advancement. = 未来的历史学家可能会将这个时代与快速的科技进步等同起来。


虚拟语气:
If you equate hard work with intelligence, you might miss out on other talents. = 如果你把努力工作与智力等同起来，你可能会错过其他天赋。
I wish I hadn't equated his kindness with weakness. = 我真希望我没有把他的善良与软弱等同起来。


被动语态:
Success is often equated with wealth in our society. = 在我们的社会中，成功往往与财富等同起来。
His actions were equated with treason by the government. = 他的行为被政府认定为叛国罪。


疑问句:
Do you equate happiness with material possessions? = 你是否把幸福与物质财富等同起来？
Does she equate artistic merit with commercial success? = 她是否把艺术价值与商业成功等同起来？


否定句:
You shouldn't equate intelligence with academic achievement. = 你不应该把智力与学业成就等同起来。
He does not equate loyalty with blind obedience. = 他不把忠诚等同于盲从。


PHRASE->
equate A with B = 把A与B等同起来
equate to = 等于
equate something to something = 把某事物比作某事物
be equated with = 与……等同
equate oneself with = 自诩为
