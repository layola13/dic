relabel:/ˌriːˈleɪbəl/| vt.|重新贴标签；重新命名|To change the label on something; to give something a new name or label.|例句：We need to relabel these boxes before shipping them.  我们需要在发货前重新贴上这些箱子的标签。|例句：The company decided to relabel its product to attract a younger market. 公司决定重新命名其产品以吸引更年轻的市场。|近义词：rename, retitle, tag anew|反义词：retain original label
