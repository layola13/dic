muddlement:/ˈmʌdlmənt/| n. |混乱；糊涂；困境 |A state of confusion and disorder; a muddled situation.  |例句：The report was a muddle of conflicting facts and figures. 这份报告充斥着相互矛盾的事实和数据，一片混乱。

例句：His explanation only added to the general muddlement.  他的解释只会加剧整体的混乱。


近义词：confusion, chaos, disorder, mess, jumble

反义词：clarity, order, organization, coherence
