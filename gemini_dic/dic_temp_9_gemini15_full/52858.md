dimeter:/ˈdaɪmɪtər/| n. |双音步；二音步诗|A dimeter is a line of verse consisting of two metrical feet.  It is a relatively short line in poetry, often used for effect or to create a sense of brevity.  |Example:  The short line, "Bright star," is a dimeter in some poetic forms.  短行“明亮的星星”在某些诗歌形式中是双音步。|近义词：short line (in poetry)|反义词：hexameter, pentameter|名词
