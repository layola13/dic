practicably:/ˈpræktɪkəbli/ | adv. |实际上地，可行地 |In a practical way; feasibly.  It means something is possible to do or achieve in a practical sense, considering the resources and limitations involved. |例句：Practicably, we can only finish the project by next month.  实际上，我们只能在下个月完成这个项目。
例句：It's practically impossible to complete the task in such a short time. 在这么短的时间内完成这项任务实际上是不可能的。|近义词：feasibly, realistically, viably |反义词：impractically, unrealistically, impossibly
