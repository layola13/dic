overwatching:/ˌoʊvərˈwɒtʃɪŋ/| n. |过度监视，严密监视|The act of watching something or someone too closely or for too long; excessive surveillance.|例句：The overwatching of employees led to resentment and decreased productivity.  员工的过度监视导致了怨恨和生产力下降。


例句：Constant overwatching by the security cameras made the employees feel uncomfortable.  保安摄像头的持续监控让员工感到不舒服。


近义词：surveillance, monitoring, scrutiny, observation


反义词：neglect, ignoring
