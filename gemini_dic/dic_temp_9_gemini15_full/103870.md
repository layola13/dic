jubbeh:/ˈdʒʊbeɪ/| n.|长袍，长衫|A long, loose-fitting robe, typically worn in the Middle East and Central Asia.  It is often made of heavy fabric and can be richly decorated.|例句：He wore a long jubbeh, the dark fabric heavy with embroidery. 他穿着一件长长的长袍，深色的布料上绣满了刺绣。


例句：The jubbeh was a symbol of his status and wealth. 这件长袍象征着他的地位和财富。


近义词：robe, gown, caftan|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with shorter garments like shirts or jackets.|词性:名词
