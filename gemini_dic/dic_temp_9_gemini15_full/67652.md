extroversion:/ˌɛkstrəˈvɜːrʒən/| n. |外向性；性格外向|The tendency to be sociable and to seek out the company of others.  Extroversion is a personality trait characterized by sociability, assertiveness, and emotional expressiveness.  It is often contrasted with introversion.|例句：Her extroversion made her a natural leader.  她的外向性格使她成为一个天生的领导者。

例句：His extroversion was evident in his energetic and engaging personality. 他外向的性格在他的精力充沛和引人入胜的个性中显而易见。


近义词：outgoingness, sociability, gregariousness|反义词：introversion, shyness, reserve|名词
