overstrident:/ˌoʊvərˈstraɪdənt/ | adj. |过于尖锐的；过于刺耳的；过于强硬的 | Excessively loud, harsh, or forceful.  Characterized by excessive stridency; jarringly loud or harsh. |例句：His overstrident tone alienated many listeners.  他的声音过于尖锐，疏远了许多听众。

例句：The overstrident music made it difficult to concentrate. 过于刺耳的音乐让人难以集中精力。

近义词：shrill, harsh, raucous, strident, grating, jarring, loud, forceful, aggressive

反义词：gentle, soft, subdued, quiet, mild, mellow


词源:  The word "overstrident" is a combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") and the adjective "strident," which comes from the Latin word "stridere," meaning "to creak" or "to make a harsh sound."  Therefore, "overstrident" literally means "excessively harsh-sounding."


用法说明:

overstrident主要用作形容词，修饰声音、语气或行为。它强调声音或语调的刺耳和令人不快，以及行为的过于强硬和咄咄逼人。  它通常暗示着一种不愉快的、令人反感的品质。
