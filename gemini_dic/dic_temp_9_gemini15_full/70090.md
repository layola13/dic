feature:/ˈfiːtʃər/ | n. | 特征，特点，特写 | A distinctive attribute or aspect of something.  | The car's key feature is its fuel efficiency. 这款车的关键特征是燃油效率高。 The magazine featured an article about the election. 这本杂志刊登了一篇关于选举的文章。 | characteristic, trait, attribute | defect, flaw | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
This product features a new design. = 这个产品具有一个新的设计。
The article features interviews with several experts. = 这篇文章以对几位专家的采访为特色。


一般过去时:
The film featured a famous actor. = 这部电影主演了一位著名的演员。
The newspaper featured a story about the local election. = 报纸刊登了一篇关于地方选举的报道。


现在完成时:
The company has featured this product in all its advertising. = 公司在其所有广告中都重点宣传了这款产品。
Many magazines have featured articles about sustainable living. = 许多杂志都刊登过关于可持续生活的文章。


过去完成时:
They had featured that song in their previous concert. = 他们在之前的演唱会上已经演绎过那首歌了。
The museum had featured this exhibit for several months. = 这个博物馆已经展出这个展览好几个月了。


将来时:
The next issue will feature an interview with the president. = 下期杂志将刊登对总统的采访。
The conference will feature speakers from around the world. = 会议将邀请来自世界各地的演讲者。


虚拟语气:
If the movie featured better actors, it would be more successful. = 如果这部电影有更好的演员，它会更成功。
I wish the article featured more data. = 我希望这篇文章能提供更多的数据。


被动语态:
This problem is featured in the research paper. = 这个问题在研究论文中有所体现。
The new product will be featured in the upcoming trade show. = 新产品将在即将举行的贸易展览会上展出。


疑问句:
What features does the software have? = 这个软件有什么功能？
What features are most important to you? = 你认为哪些功能最重要？


否定句:
The presentation did not feature any new information. = 这次演示没有提供任何新的信息。
The menu doesn't feature any vegetarian options. = 菜单上没有任何素食选择。


PHRASE->

key feature: 关键特征
main feature: 主要特征
special feature: 特殊功能
featured product: 主打产品
feature film: 故事片
