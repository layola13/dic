over-attached: /ˌoʊvərəˈtætʃt/ | adj. |过度依恋的，依赖性过强的 | Excessively attached or dependent; showing an unhealthy level of dependence on someone or something.  |例句：He's over-attached to his mother and struggles to make independent decisions. 他过度依赖母亲，难以做出独立的决定。|  例句：She became over-attached to her phone, checking it constantly throughout the day. 她对手机过度依赖，整天不停地查看手机。| 近义词：dependent, clingy, possessive, needy | 反义词：independent, detached, self-reliant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is over-attached to his job. = 他过度依赖他的工作。
She is over-attached to her boyfriend. = 她过度依赖她的男朋友。

一般过去时:
He was over-attached to his childhood toys. = 他以前过度依赖他的童年玩具。
She was over-attached to her old habits. = 她以前过度依赖她的旧习惯。

现在完成时:

She has been over-attached to her social media accounts. = 她一直过度依赖她的社交媒体账号。

过去完成时:

She had become over-attached to the idea of success before she failed. = 在她失败之前，她对成功的想法已经过度依赖了。

将来时:

She will probably be over-attached to her new project. = 她可能会对她的新项目过度依赖。


虚拟语气:
