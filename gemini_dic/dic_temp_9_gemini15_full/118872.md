metasomatism:/ˌmɛtəˈsoʊmətɪzəm/| n. |交代作用，变质交代作用 |The alteration of the chemical composition of a rock by the action of fluids, gases, or solutions. This process typically occurs at high temperatures and pressures, often associated with magmatic or hydrothermal activity.  |例句：The metasomatism resulted in the formation of skarn deposits. 交代作用导致了矽卡岩矿床的形成。
The extensive metasomatism altered the original rock's mineralogy. 广泛的交代作用改变了原岩的矿物学特征。|近义词：alteration, replacement,  metasomatic replacement |反义词：unchanged, unaltered
