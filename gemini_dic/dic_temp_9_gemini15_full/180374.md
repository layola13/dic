refusals:/rɪˈfjuːzəlz/ | n. | 拒绝；谢绝 | The act of refusing or declining something.  Refusals can range from polite declines to outright rejections.  |  例句：His refusals to cooperate hampered the investigation. 他的拒绝合作妨碍了调查。

例句：Despite numerous refusals, she persisted in her efforts. 尽管屡遭拒绝，她仍然坚持不懈。


近义词：rejections, denials, declinations | 反义词：acceptances, approvals


词源:  Derived from the verb "refuse," ultimately from the Latin "refusare," meaning "to refuse, reject."  The suffix "-als" creates the plural noun form.
