gooses:/ɡuːsɪz/| n.|鹅(复数)|The plural form of "goose".  It refers to more than one goose.  The word is irregular, as the singular is "goose".|例句：Several gooses were swimming in the pond.  几只鹅在池塘里游泳。

例句：I saw a flock of gooses flying south. 我看到一群鹅向南飞。


近义词：geese (更常用的复数形式)| 反义词：无


词性:名词
