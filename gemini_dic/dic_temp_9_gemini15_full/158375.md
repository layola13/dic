pleadingnesses:/ˈpliːdɪŋnəsɪz/| n. |恳求；哀求；请求；哀怜|The word "pleadingnesses" is the plural form of "pleadingness," which itself is not a common word.  It refers to multiple instances of pleading or the quality of being pleading. It emphasizes the repeated or numerous nature of acts of pleading or the intensity of a pleading nature. The word is quite uncommon and sounds somewhat awkward in most contexts;  simpler alternatives usually exist.|例句：The repeated pleadingnesses of the child finally wore down the parent's resistance.  孩子的反复恳求最终消磨了父母的抵抗力。The multiple pleadingnesses in his letter revealed his desperation. 他信中多次恳求显示了他的绝望。|近义词：supplications, entreaties, pleas, petitions, importunities|反义词：rejections, refusals, denials
