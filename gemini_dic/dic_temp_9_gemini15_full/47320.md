de-objectification:/ˌdiː ɔːbˈdʒɛktɪfɪkeɪʃən/| n. |去客体化，摆脱物化 |The process of reversing or counteracting objectification;  treating someone as a person with inherent worth and dignity, rather than as a mere object or commodity.  It involves challenging and dismantling systems and structures that perpetuate objectification. |例句：The feminist movement has actively promoted de-objectification in media representations of women. 女权运动积极倡导媒体中女性形象的去客体化。 |例句：De-objectification requires a shift in societal attitudes and a recognition of individual agency. 去客体化需要社会态度的转变以及对个人能动性的认可。|近义词：humanization, empowerment, personification |反义词：objectification, commodification, dehumanization |名词
