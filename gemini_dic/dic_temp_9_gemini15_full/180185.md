reformingly:/rɪˈfɔːrmɪŋli/ | adv. | 变革地；改革地 | In a manner that promotes or effects reform.  This word describes the way something is done, emphasizing a focus on improvement or change.  It suggests a process of making something better. | 例句：The government acted reformingly to address the economic crisis.  政府采取改革措施来解决经济危机。 The company is reformingly restructuring its operations to improve efficiency. 这家公司正在改革性地重组其运营以提高效率。 | 近义词：revolutionarily, progressively, innovatively | 反义词：conservatively, traditionally, regressively | 副词
