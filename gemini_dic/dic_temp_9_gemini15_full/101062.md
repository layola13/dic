inventible:/ɪnˈvɛntəbl/ | adj. | 不可发明的；无法创造的 |  Inventible describes something that cannot be invented or created.  It implies an impossibility, often due to inherent limitations or laws of nature. | The perpetual motion machine remains inventible, defying the laws of thermodynamics.  永动机仍然是不可发明的，它违背了热力学定律。 |  uncreatable, impossible  | feasible, possible |  adj.
