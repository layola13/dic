glutes:/ɡluːts/| n.|臀肌，臀部肌肉|The glutes are a group of three muscles in the buttocks: the gluteus maximus, gluteus medius, and gluteus minimus.  They are responsible for hip extension, abduction, and external rotation.  Strong glutes are important for many activities, including walking, running, jumping, and climbing.|例句：She strengthened her glutes by doing squats and lunges. 她通过深蹲和弓步来增强臀部肌肉。|例句：Strong glutes are crucial for maintaining good posture and preventing injuries. 强壮的臀部肌肉对于保持良好的姿势和预防受伤至关重要。|近义词：buttocks, hips, rear|反义词：无
