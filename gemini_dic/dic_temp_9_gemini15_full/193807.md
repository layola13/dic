self-deprivation: /ˌsɛlfˌdɛprɪˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 自我剥夺，自我克制 | The act of denying oneself something, often a pleasure or a necessity.  This can be done for various reasons, including religious practices, self-discipline, or as a form of punishment. | 例句：His self-deprivation extended to foregoing even basic comforts.  他的自我剥夺甚至延伸到放弃基本舒适。

His years of self-deprivation finally led to a nervous breakdown. 他多年的自我克制最终导致了精神崩溃。


近义词：abstinence, self-denial, asceticism, self-discipline | 反义词：indulgence, self-indulgence | 词性：名词
