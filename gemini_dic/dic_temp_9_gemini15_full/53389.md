disagreements:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːmənts/| n. |分歧，意见不合| Noun.  A disagreement is a situation in which people have different opinions or beliefs and do not agree. It often involves a conflict or clash of ideas, perspectives, or interests.  The plural form indicates multiple instances of such differences. |例句：Their disagreements over the project delayed its completion. 他们对项目的意见分歧导致项目延期完成。

例句：The family is plagued by constant disagreements. 这个家庭饱受持续不断的争吵困扰。


近义词：differences, disputes, conflicts, quarrels, contentions|反义词：agreement, accord, harmony, consensus|词性：名词
