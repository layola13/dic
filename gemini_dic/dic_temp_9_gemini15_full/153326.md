pendicle:/ˈpɛndɪkl/| n. |小枝；细柄；花梗 |A small stalk or stem, especially one supporting a flower or fruit.  It is often used in botany to describe the small stems supporting individual flowers in an inflorescence. |Example: The delicate pendicles held the tiny blossoms aloft.  这些纤细的花梗托举着娇小的花朵。|近义词：pedicel, stalk, stem |反义词：无
