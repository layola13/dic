keens:/kiːn/| adj.|热切的；渴望的；敏锐的|Having or showing eagerness or enthusiasm.  Keen interest or perception.|例句：She has a keen interest in art. 她对艺术有着浓厚的兴趣。

例句：His keen eyes noticed the detail others missed. 他敏锐的眼睛注意到了其他人错过的细节。

近义词：eager, enthusiastic, ardent, sharp, acute

反义词：indifferent, apathetic, dull, obtuse

词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is keen to learn. = 他渴望学习。
They are keen on sports. = 他们热衷于运动。

一般过去时:
She was keen to help. = 她渴望帮忙。
We were keen on that idea. = 我们对那个主意很感兴趣。

现在完成时:
He has been keen on photography for years. = 他多年来一直热衷于摄影。
They have been keen to finish the project. = 他们一直渴望完成这个项目。

过去完成时:
She had been keen to go to the concert, but she was ill. = 她本来很想去看音乐会，但她病了。
They had been keen on the plan, but it fell through. = 他们之前很热衷于这个计划，但它最终失败了。

将来时:
He will be keen to see the results. = 他将渴望看到结果。
They will be keen on trying the new restaurant. = 他们将渴望尝试这家新餐厅。

虚拟语气:
If I were keen, I would have tried harder. = 如果我渴望的话，我会更加努力。
I wish I were keen on learning a new language. = 我希望自己渴望学习一门外语。

被动语态: (keen本身不常用于被动语态)


疑问句:
Are you keen on going out tonight? = 你今晚想去出去吗？
Is he keen to join the club? = 他渴望加入俱乐部吗？

否定句:
He is not keen on spicy food. = 他不喜欢辛辣食物。
She wasn't keen to go to the party. = 她不太想去参加派对。
