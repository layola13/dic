mis-started:/ˌmɪsˈstɑːrtɪd/ | adj. | 出师不利；开头不顺利的 |  Describes something that began badly or unsuccessfully.  It implies a flawed or problematic beginning that negatively impacts the overall outcome. | The project mis-started due to poor planning.  这个项目由于计划不周而一开始就进展不顺利。|  poorly begun, badly begun | successfully started, well-started | 形容词
