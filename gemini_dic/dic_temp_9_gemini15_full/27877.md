cans:/kæn z/| n.|罐头；容器|A can is a metal container, typically cylindrical, used for storing food or drink.  It can also refer to the contents within the container. |例句：I bought a can of soda. 我买了一听苏打水。|例句：He opened the can and poured the soup into a bowl. 他打开罐头并将汤倒进碗里。|近义词：tin, container|反义词：bottle (for liquids)


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He cans peaches every summer. = 他每年夏天都做桃子罐头。
She cans tomatoes for the winter. = 她为冬天储存罐装西红柿。


一般过去时:
They canned the fruit last week. = 他们上周把水果做成了罐头。
My grandmother canned pickles for years. = 我祖母腌制泡菜很多年了。


现在完成时:
We have canned all the vegetables. = 我们已经把所有的蔬菜都做成罐头了。
She has canned many jars of jam this year. = 她今年已经做了很多罐果酱。


过去完成时:

He had canned several hundred jars of soup before he retired. = 他退休前已经做了几百罐汤。


将来时:
