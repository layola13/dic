remastery:/rɪˈmɑːstəri/| n. | 重获掌握；再次掌握；精通 | The act of mastering something again, often after a period of neglect or loss of skill.  It implies a higher level of skill than before. | Example: After years of inactivity, her remastery of the piano was truly impressive.  = 多年不练后，她再次掌握钢琴演奏技巧，令人印象深刻。|近义词：reacquisition, regaining, relearning |反义词：loss of mastery, unfamiliarity
