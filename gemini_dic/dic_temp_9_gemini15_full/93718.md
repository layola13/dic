immanation:/ɪmænˈeɪʃən/| n. |内在化；内在性；神在世间|The inherent presence of the divine within creation.  This refers to the belief that God is not separate from the world but is present within all things.  It contrasts with transcendence, where God is seen as existing outside of and separate from creation. |Example:  The concept of immanation is central to pantheistic and panentheistic philosophies. 内在化概念是泛神论和泛神论哲学的核心。|近义词：inherence, indwelling |反义词：transcendence
