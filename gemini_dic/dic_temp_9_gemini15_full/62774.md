engrave:/ɪnˈɡreɪv/| vt.|雕刻，铭刻|To cut or carve words, designs, or patterns into a hard surface.  |例句：He engraved his initials on the ring. 他把自己的姓名首字母刻在戒指上。
例句：The artist engraved a beautiful landscape onto the wood block. 艺术家在木板上雕刻了一幅美丽的风景画。|近义词：carve, etch, inscribe|反义词：erase, obliterate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He engraves beautiful designs on jewelry. = 他在珠宝上雕刻精美的图案。
They engrave names on trophies. = 他们把名字刻在奖杯上。

一般过去时:
She engraved a message on the locket. = 她把信息刻在了锁匙坠上。
The craftsman engraved the family crest onto the silverware. = 工匠把家族纹章刻在了银器上。

现在完成时:
He has engraved hundreds of pieces of jewelry. = 他已经雕刻了数百件珠宝。
They have engraved a plaque to commemorate the event. = 他们已经刻了一块牌匾来纪念这个事件。

过去完成时:
She had engraved the invitation before the party. = 派对前她已经刻好了请柬。
The artist had engraved the portrait before the frame arrived. = 画框送来之前，艺术家已经刻好了肖像。

将来时:
He will engrave a special message on the watch. = 他会在手表上刻上特别的信息。
They will engrave the company logo on all the products. = 他们会在所有产品上刻上公司标志。

虚拟语气:
If I had the tools, I would engrave my name on the stone. = 如果我有工具，我会把我的名字刻在石头上。
I wish I could engrave this beautiful design. = 我真希望我能雕刻这个美丽的图案。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
