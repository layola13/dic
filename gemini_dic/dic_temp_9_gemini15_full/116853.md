maximizations:/ˌmæksɪmɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 最大化；最大值；极大化 | The act of maximizing something, or the state of being maximized.  It refers to the process of making something as large or as great as possible.  This term is often used in mathematics, economics, and computer science. | 例句：The company's primary goal is the maximization of profits.  = 公司的首要目标是利润最大化。

The design prioritizes the maximization of space. =  设计优先考虑空间最大化。


近义词：optimization, amplification, enhancement | 反义词：minimization
