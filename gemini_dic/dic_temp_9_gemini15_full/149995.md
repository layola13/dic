palmipedes:/ˈpælɪpɪdiːz/| n. |蹼足类动物；水禽|Web-footed birds; birds having feet with the toes connected by a membrane.  The term is often used to refer to waterfowl such as ducks, geese, and swans. |例句：Many palmipedes are migratory birds.  许多蹼足类动物是候鸟。|例句：Palmipedes are well adapted to aquatic environments. 蹼足类动物非常适应水生环境。|近义词：waterfowl, aquatic birds|反义词：None directly applicable.  (Could consider birds with non-webbed feet, but no single antonym exists.)|名词
