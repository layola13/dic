mewling:/ˈmjuːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 哭泣的；柔弱的；无力的 |  Making a weak, crying sound, typically of a baby or young animal.  Describing something weak, feeble, or pathetic. | 例句：The mewling infant cried incessantly.  嗷嗷待哺的婴儿不停地哭泣。  The mewling kitten sought its mother.  柔弱的小猫寻找它的母亲。 | 近义词：whimpering, crying, wailing, feeble, weak | 反义词：strong, powerful, robust
