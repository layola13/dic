sackclothed:/ˈsækˌkloʊðd/| adj.|穿着粗布麻衣的；穿着苦修服的|Wearing sackcloth; dressed in rough, coarse material, often associated with mourning, penance, or humility.  It implies a state of sorrow, repentance, or poverty.|The sackclothed mourners walked silently behind the coffin.|近义词：penitent, mournful, humble|反义词：opulent, luxurious, richly dressed
