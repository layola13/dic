euthanising:/ˈjuːθənˌaɪzɪŋ/| v.|安乐死；仁慈地杀死|The act of killing a person or animal suffering from an incurable and painful disease, to relieve suffering.|例句：The vet had to euthanise the dog after it was involved in a serious accident. 兽医不得不对这只在严重事故中受伤的狗实施安乐死。例句：The debate surrounding euthanasia is complex and emotionally charged.  围绕安乐死的辩论复杂且充满情绪。|近义词：putting down, mercy killing|反义词：saving, preserving, treating
