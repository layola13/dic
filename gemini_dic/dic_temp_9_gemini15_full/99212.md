intercluster: /ˌɪntərˈklʌstər/ | n. | 集群间 |  Relating to or occurring between clusters; specifically, in computing, referring to communication or interaction between different clusters of computers or data.  | Example:  The intercluster communication protocol ensured data consistency across the distributed system. 集群间通信协议确保了分布式系统的数据一致性。


|近义词: inter-cluster (same meaning, less common)|反义词: intracluster (within a cluster)|词性:noun
