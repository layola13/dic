loudly:/ˈlaʊdli/| adv.|大声地，喧闹地|The adverb "loudly" means in a loud manner; with much noise. It modifies a verb, describing how an action is performed. |例句：He spoke loudly so everyone could hear him. 他大声说话以便每个人都能听到他。

例句：The children laughed loudly during the show. 孩子们在演出期间大声笑了起来。

近义词：noisily, boisterously, clamorously

反义词：quietly, softly, silently

词性：副词
