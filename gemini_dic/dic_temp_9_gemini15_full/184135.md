resistibility: /rɪˌzɪstəˈbɪləti/ | n. |抵抗力；耐受力；抗性| The ability to withstand or resist something.  The property of a material that determines its ability to resist a particular force, process, or substance. | 例句：The resistibility of the material to corrosion was tested.  该材料的耐腐蚀性进行了测试。

The resistibility of this bridge to earthquakes is a major concern. 这座桥梁的抗震能力是一个主要问题。


近义词：resistance, endurance, durability, resilience | 反义词：vulnerability, susceptibility


词源:  Derived from the verb "resist" (from Latin *resistere, "to stand against"), plus the suffix "-ibility," indicating capacity or capability.
