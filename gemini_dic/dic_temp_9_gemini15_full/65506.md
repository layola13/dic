eurosceptical:/ˌjʊərəʊˈskeptɪkəl/| adj.|怀疑欧洲一体化的；欧洲怀疑论的 |  Relating to or characterized by skepticism or opposition to the European Union or European integration.  This term describes individuals, groups, or political movements that question the benefits of EU membership or integration and may advocate for greater national sovereignty. | Example:  The rise of eurosceptical parties in several European countries has led to increased political instability.  欧洲几个国家疑欧党的崛起导致了政治动荡加剧。 | 近义词：anti-EU, pro-national sovereignty | 反义词：pro-European, pro-integration |
