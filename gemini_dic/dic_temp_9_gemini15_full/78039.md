genuflector:/ˈdʒɛnjuˌflɛktər/| n. | 跪拜者；跪凳 | A person who genuflects; a kneeling cushion or stool.  | Example: The priest was a devout genuflector, performing the ritual flawlessly.  牧师是一位虔诚的跪拜者，完美地进行了仪式。 |近义词:kneeler, prostrater |反义词: stander
