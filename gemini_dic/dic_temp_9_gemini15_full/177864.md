recertifications:/ˌriːsɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n.|再认证；重新认证 |The process of obtaining a certification again after it has expired or been revoked.  It often involves demonstrating continued competency or meeting updated requirements. |例句：My professional license requires annual recertifications to maintain its validity. 我的专业执照需要每年重新认证才能保持有效性。

例句：The company implemented a new system for employee recertifications to ensure ongoing compliance with safety regulations.  公司实施了新的员工再认证制度，以确保持续遵守安全法规。


近义词：renewals, re-registrations

反义词：initial certifications, first-time certifications

词性:名词
