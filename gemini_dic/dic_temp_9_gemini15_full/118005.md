memorised:/ˈmeməraɪzd/ | v. |记住；熟记；背诵 |To have committed something to memory.  |例句：I memorised the poem for the competition. 我为了比赛背诵了这首诗。|例句：She had memorised all the capital cities. 她已经记住了所有首都城市。|近义词：learned, committed to memory, mastered |反义词：forgot, forgotten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I memorise new vocabulary every day. = 我每天都记住新的词汇。
He memorises phone numbers easily. = 他很容易记住电话号码。

一般过去时:
I memorised the speech last night. = 我昨晚背诵了演讲稿。
She memorised the entire play. = 她记住了整部戏剧。

现在完成时:
I have memorised all the important dates. = 我已经记住了所有重要的日期。
She has memorised the lyrics to that song. = 她已经记住了那首歌的歌词。

过去完成时:
I had memorised the script before the audition. = 我在试镜前已经背熟了剧本。
They had memorised the instructions carefully. = 他们已经仔细地记住了指示。

将来时:
I will memorise the formula for the exam. = 我将为考试背诵这个公式。
She will memorise the route before the journey. = 她会在出行前记住路线。

虚拟语气:
If I had memorised the answers, I would have passed the test. = 如果我记住了答案，我就通过考试了。
I wish I had memorised the poem earlier. = 我真希望我早点把这首诗背下来。

被动语态:
The lines were memorised by the actors. = 演员们把台词背下来了。
The speech needs to be memorised before tomorrow. = 演讲稿需要在明天之前背熟。

疑问句:
Have you memorised the poem yet? = 你已经把诗背下来了吗？
Did he memorise the address correctly? = 他正确地记住了地址吗？

否定句:
I couldn't memorise the long list of names. = 我记不住那长长的名字列表。
She didn't memorise the directions properly. = 她没有正确地记住路线。


PHRASE->
memorise something by heart = 全部记住某事

memorise a phone number = 记住电话号码
