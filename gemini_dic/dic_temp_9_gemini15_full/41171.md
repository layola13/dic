copycatted:/ˈkɒpikætɪd/| v. |模仿，抄袭|The verb "copycatted" is the past tense and past participle of "copycat," meaning to imitate someone or something, often in a servile or unoriginal way.  It implies a deliberate act of copying, often without acknowledgement or permission. |Example: He copycatted the design from a competitor's product. 他抄袭了竞争对手的产品设计。|近义词：imitated, plagiarized, duplicated, copied|反义词：originated, innovated
