investure:/ɪnˈvɛstʃər/| n. |授职；授予；赋予；财产转让|The act of investing someone with an office, rank, or dignity; the ceremony or act of conferring a title or dignity; the thing conferred.  It can also refer to the formal bestowal of property or rights.|例句：The investiture of the new king was a magnificent ceremony. 新国王的加冕典礼非常盛大。|例句：The investiture of the land to the new owner was finalized. 土地所有权正式转让给了新主人。|近义词：conferment, installation, induction, grant|反义词：removal, dismissal, divestment
