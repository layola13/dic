everywhere:/ˈɛvriˌwɛr/| adv.|到处，各处|Everywhere means in or to all places. It indicates a lack of restriction in location.  |例句：She searched everywhere for her lost keys. 她到处寻找她丢失的钥匙。
例句：The bird sings everywhere.  鸟儿到处唱歌。|近义词：anywhere, all over, globally|反义词:nowhere


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have verb conjugations.  It modifies verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs.

PHRASE->
