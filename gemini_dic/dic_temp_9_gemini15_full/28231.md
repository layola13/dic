capstan:/ˈkæpstən/| n. |绞盘；卷扬机 | A capstan is a vertical rotating drum used for hoisting or hauling heavy loads by winding a rope around it.  It is often found on ships and in other applications requiring heavy lifting or pulling. |例句：The sailors used the capstan to raise the anchor. 水手们用绞盘起锚。
例句：The construction crew employed a powerful capstan to lift the heavy beams. 建筑队用强大的绞盘吊起沉重的梁木。|近义词：windlass, winch |反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑与“手动提升装置”对比)|名词
