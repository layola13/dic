shh: /ʃ/ | int. | 嘘；安静 |  An interjection used to urge silence.  |  Shh, the baby's sleeping.  嘘，宝宝睡着了。 |  hush, mum |  loud, noisy |  感叹词


USAGE->
This word is an interjection and doesn't follow typical verb tenses.  It's always used in the imperative form.


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases directly using "shh"  as it's a single-word interjection.  However, it can be combined with other words to create phrases like: "Shh, be quiet!" or "Shh, it's a secret!".
