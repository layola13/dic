neothalamus: /ˌniːoʊˈθæləməs/ | n. | 新丘脑 | The neothalamus is the phylogenetically newer part of the thalamus, involved in higher-order sensory processing and integration with the cerebral cortex. It is distinct from the paleothalamus.  |  The neothalamus plays a crucial role in relaying sensory information to the cortex, enabling complex cognitive functions. | 近义词:  thalamus (a broader term) | 反义词: paleothalamus
