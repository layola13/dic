fingerprintings: /ˈfɪŋɡərˌprɪntɪŋz/ | n. | 指纹采集；指纹识别 | The process of taking and recording fingerprints.  This term often refers to the act of collecting fingerprints for identification purposes, either manually or using automated systems. |例句：The police conducted fingerprintings at the crime scene.  警方在犯罪现场进行了指纹采集。

The fingerprintings were analyzed to identify the suspect. 指纹被分析以识别嫌疑人。


近义词：fingerprinting (singular), dactyloscopy | 反义词：None (no direct antonym)
