词源:  The word "humanitarianism" is derived from the word "humanitarian," which comes from the French word "humanitaire," ultimately derived from the Latin word "humanus" meaning "human, humane."  The suffix "-ism" denotes a system, doctrine, or practice.  Therefore, humanitarianism signifies the system of principles and practices based on humaneness and compassion.
