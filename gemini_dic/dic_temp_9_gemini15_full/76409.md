gages:/ˈɡeɪdʒɪz/ | n. |抵押品；保证；测量仪器 |  Pledges, especially as security for a loan; a device for measuring something.  The word often refers to something given as security for a debt or obligation, or a means of measuring something, such as pressure or flow. |例句：He used his house as gages for the loan. 他用他的房子作为贷款的抵押品。The engineer used a pressure gage to monitor the system. 工程师用压力计监测系统。|近义词：pledge, security, instrument, gauge |反义词：release, unsecured
