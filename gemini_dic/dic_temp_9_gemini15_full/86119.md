heartburning: /ˈhɑːrtˌbɜːrnɪŋ/ | adj. | 心痛的；令人心痛的；令人苦恼的 |  A feeling of intense sadness, disappointment, or anger; causing or characterized by this feeling.  | 例句：The news of his death caused heartburning among his friends.  他的死讯令他的朋友们心痛不已。

例句：Her heartburning over the lost opportunity kept her awake at night.  失去机会的懊悔让她整夜睡不着觉。


近义词：anguish, distress, sorrow, grief, agony | 反义词：joy, happiness, delight, elation


词源:  The word "heartburning" is a compound word.  "Heart" refers to the emotional center, and "burning" describes an intense feeling.  The combination suggests a feeling of intense emotional pain or distress.  It's a descriptive term rather than having a direct etymological root in a single older word.
