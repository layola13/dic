photoregulation: /ˌfoʊtoʊˌreɡjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 光调节，光控 | The process by which light regulates biological processes.  This involves the interaction of light with photoreceptors and subsequent signaling pathways that affect gene expression, enzyme activity, and other cellular functions. |  The photoregulation of flowering in plants is crucial for their survival and reproduction. 植物开花的光调节对其生存和繁殖至关重要。|  light control, photosensitivity |  darkness, light insensitivity | 名词
