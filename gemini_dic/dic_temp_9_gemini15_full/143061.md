outbought:/ˌaʊtˈbɔːt/ | v. |超过...买入；比...买得多 | To buy more of something than someone else.  |例句：He outbought his rivals in the auction, securing the prized painting. 他在拍卖会上比他的竞争对手买得多，获得了那幅珍贵的画作。


例句：The company outbought its competitors in the acquisition of key resources. 这家公司在收购关键资源方面超过了竞争对手。


近义词：outbid, surpass, exceed


反义词：underbuy


词性：动词
