pervaders:/pərˈveɪdərz/| n.| 弥漫者；渗透者；遍布者 | Those who or that which pervades.  Something that spreads throughout an area or pervades a situation. | 例句：The pervader of the foul odor was eventually identified as a broken sewer pipe.  臭味的来源最终被确定为破裂的下水道管道。

The pervasive influence of social media is a pervader of modern life. 社交媒体的普遍影响是现代生活的弥漫者。| 近义词：spreaders, infiltrators, permeators | 反义词：restrictors, isolators, separators | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The smell pervades the entire house. = 气味弥漫整个房子。
Social media pervades our daily lives. = 社交媒体充斥着我们的日常生活。

一般过去时:
The aroma of coffee pervaded the room. = 咖啡的香气弥漫了整个房间。
Fear pervaded the city after the earthquake. = 地震后，恐惧笼罩了这座城市。

现在完成时:
The news has pervaded every corner of the globe. = 这个消息已经传遍了全球各地。
Doubt has pervaded his mind since the incident. = 自从那件事发生后，怀疑就一直萦绕在他的心头。

过去完成时:
A sense of unease had pervaded the atmosphere before the announcement. = 在宣布消息之前，一种不安的情绪就已经弥漫在空气中了。
Suspicion had pervaded the team before the truth came to light. = 在真相大白之前，怀疑已经弥漫在团队之中了。

将来时:
The scent of spring will pervade the air. = 春天的气息将会弥漫在空气中。
Optimism will pervade the country after the election. = 选后，乐观情绪将弥漫全国。

虚拟语气:
If only peace pervaded the world. = 但愿和平能遍及全世界。
I wish happiness pervaded every heart. = 我希望幸福能充满每个人的心房。

被动语态:
The town was pervaded by a sense of calm. = 城镇被一种平静的气氛所笼罩。
The air was pervaded by the sweet fragrance of flowers. = 空气中弥漫着鲜花的甜香。

疑问句:
What pervades the atmosphere in this room? = 这个房间里的气氛是什么？
Does negativity pervade their relationship? = 负面情绪是否弥漫在他们的关系中？

否定句:
The joy did not pervade the celebration. = 喜悦并未弥漫在庆祝活动中。
Silence did not pervade the room; there was a lot of chatter. =  房间里并不寂静；有很多闲聊。
