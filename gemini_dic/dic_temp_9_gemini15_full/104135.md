junction:/ˈdʒʌŋkʃən/ | n. |交叉点；连接点；接合处 |A junction is a place where two or more things, such as roads, lines, or streams, meet or join.  It can also refer to the point of joining or connection itself. |例句：The accident occurred at the junction of two major highways. 这起事故发生在两条主要高速公路的交叉路口。

The train will stop at the next junction. 火车将在下一个连接点停靠。


近义词：intersection, confluence, meeting point, connection |反义词：separation, divergence, division |词性：名词
