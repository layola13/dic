outworded: /ˌaʊtˈwɜːrdɪd/ | adj. | 被说服的；被辩驳的；被言辞击败的 |  Describes someone who has been defeated in an argument or debate through the superior use of words.  It implies a complete and decisive defeat, not just a slight disadvantage. |  He felt completely outworded by her eloquent and persuasive argument. 他觉得她的雄辩和有说服力的论证完全让他无话可说。|近义词：defeated, bested, out-argued | 反义词：out-spoken, victorious
