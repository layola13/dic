outwrestle:/ˌaʊtˈrɛsəl/| vt.|  战胜（尤指摔跤）|To defeat someone in wrestling, especially by superior skill or strength. |例句：He outwrestled his opponent in the final match. 他在决赛中战胜了他的对手。|例句：The experienced wrestler easily outwrestled the newcomer.  经验丰富的摔跤运动员轻松战胜了新手。|近义词：defeat, overcome, surpass|反义词：be defeated by, lose to|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outwrestles his opponents regularly. = 他经常战胜他的对手。
She outwrestles most of the women in her weight class. =  她在她体重级别的比赛中战胜大多数女性。


一般过去时:
He outwrestled his rival in the championship bout. = 他在冠军争夺战中战胜了他的对手。
The underdog outwrestled the champion, shocking the crowd. =  黑马战胜了冠军，震惊了观众。


现在完成时:
He has outwrestled many opponents throughout his career. = 他在他整个职业生涯中战胜了许多对手。
She has outwrestled all challengers this season. = 本赛季她战胜了所有挑战者。


过去完成时:
He had outwrestled his brother countless times before their final match. = 在他们的最后一场比赛之前，他已经无数次战胜了他的兄弟。
She had outwrestled him once before, but this time was different. = 她之前已经战胜过他一次，但这次不同。


将来时:

She will likely outwrestle her competitor given her superior technique. = 鉴于她高超的技术，她很可能会战胜她的竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he might have outwrestled his opponent. = 如果他训练更努力，他可能已经战胜了他的对手。
If she were stronger, she could outwrestle anyone. = 如果她更强壮，她就能战胜任何人。


被动语态:

The champion was unexpectedly outwrestled in the first round. = 冠军在第一轮比赛中出人意料地被击败了。


疑问句:

否定句:
