photo-shoot:/ˈfoʊtoʊʃuːt/ | n. | 照片拍摄，写真拍摄 | A planned photography session, often for professional purposes, such as for a model, product, or event.  | Example: The model had a photo-shoot for a new clothing line.  模特为新的服装系列进行了一次照片拍摄。 |近义词: photography session, photoshoot, picture taking |反义词: N/A
