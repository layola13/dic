meteoroscope:/ˈmiːtiərəˌskoʊp/| n. |流星观测仪，流星探测器 |A meteoroscope is an instrument used to detect and observe meteors.  It often involves sophisticated technology to track the trajectory and characteristics of meteors. |The meteoroscope detected a meteor shower far beyond the range of the naked eye.  流星观测仪探测到肉眼无法看到的流星雨。|近义词：meteor detector, meteor tracker|反义词：无
