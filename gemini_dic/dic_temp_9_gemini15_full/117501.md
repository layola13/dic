meece: /miːs/ | n. | 懦弱的人；胆小鬼 | A meech is a person who is timid, cowardly, or lacking in courage.  The term is chiefly used in informal contexts and carries a strong connotation of contempt. | He's such a meech; he wouldn't even stand up to the bully. 他真是个懦夫，他甚至不敢对抗那个恶霸。 | 近义词：coward, poltroon, craven | 反义词：brave, courageous, valiant | 名词
