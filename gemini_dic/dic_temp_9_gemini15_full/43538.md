crawls:/krɔːlz/| v.|爬行，缓慢移动|To move on the hands and knees, or by dragging the body along the ground.|例句：The baby crawls across the floor.  婴儿在地上爬行。|例句：The traffic crawled along at a snail's pace. 交通缓慢地蠕动前进。|近义词：creep, slither, inch|反义词：run, sprint


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The snake crawls through the grass. = 蛇在草丛中爬行。
The car crawls along the congested highway. = 汽车在拥堵的高速公路上缓慢行驶。

一般过去时:
The insect crawled into the hole. = 昆虫爬进了洞里。
He crawled under the table to hide. = 他爬到桌子底下躲起来。

现在完成时:
The baby has crawled across the room. = 婴儿已经爬过了房间。
The snail has crawled up the wall. = 蜗牛已经爬上了墙。

过去完成时:
The thief had crawled away before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，小偷已经爬走了。
She had crawled to safety before the building collapsed. = 建筑物倒塌之前，她已经爬到了安全的地方。

将来时:
The caterpillar will crawl onto the leaf. = 毛毛虫将会爬到叶子上。
I think he will crawl back to me eventually. = 我认为他最终会回到我身边。


虚拟语气:
If I were a snake, I would crawl through the tunnels. = 如果我是一条蛇，我会爬过隧道。
If the baby could crawl, it would explore the room. = 如果婴儿会爬，它就会探索房间。

被动语态:
(Not applicable in a natural way.  The verb "crawl" is rarely used passively.)


疑问句:
Does the spider crawl on the ceiling? = 蜘蛛在 天花板上爬吗？
Why does the traffic crawl during rush hour? = 为什么在高峰时段交通会缓慢？


否定句:
The baby doesn't crawl yet. = 婴儿还不会爬。
The car didn't crawl, it sped away. = 汽车没有缓慢行驶，而是飞速驶离。
