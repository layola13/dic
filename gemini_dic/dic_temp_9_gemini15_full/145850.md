overdependent: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪpɛndənt/ | adj. |过度依赖的，依赖性过强的 | Excessively reliant on someone or something else.  Characterized by an unhealthy level of dependence. | 例句：He's become overdependent on his parents for financial support. 他变得过度依赖父母的经济支持。 | 近义词：dependent, reliant, reliant on | 反义词：independent, self-reliant, autonomous

词源:

* **over-**:  Prefix indicating "too much," "excessive," or "beyond."  It comes from Old English *ofer, *of, meaning "over," "above," "beyond."

* **dependent**:  Adjective meaning "relying on someone or something else for support." It originates from the Old French *dependent, from *pendre, meaning "to hang," implying a state of being hung or suspended from something else.  The "de-" prefix adds emphasis on "from" or "away from" self-sufficiency.


用法说明:

The word "overdependent" describes a state of reliance that is excessive or unhealthy.  It suggests a level of dependence that hinders independence and self-sufficiency.  The person or entity described as "overdependent" may struggle to function without the support they rely on.  It often implies a negative connotation, suggesting a lack of self-reliance or a potential for problems stemming from the dependence.


例句：

* She's become overdependent on her medication. 她变得过度依赖药物。

* The company is overdependent on a single supplier.  这家公司过度依赖单一供应商。


近义词：

* excessively dependent
* heavily reliant
* unduly reliant


反义词:

* independent
* self-reliant
* autonomous
* self-sufficient


词性:  Adjective
