reassigning:/ˌriːˈsaɪnɪŋ/| v.|重新分配，重新指派|The act of assigning something again; giving a new task or responsibility.|例句：The manager is reassigning tasks to improve team efficiency.  经理正在重新分配任务以提高团队效率。|近义词：reallotting, reallocating, redistributing|反义词：assigning (in the sense of initial assignment)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reassign projects regularly. = 他们定期重新分配项目。
The company reassigns employees based on need. = 公司根据需要重新分配员工。


一般过去时:
He reassigned the case to a different detective. = 他将此案重新分配给另一位侦探。
She reassigned the resources to the most urgent project. = 她将资源重新分配给了最紧急的项目。


现在完成时:
They have reassigned all the staff to new departments. = 他们已经将所有员工重新分配到新的部门。
The company has reassigned its marketing budget. = 公司已经重新分配了其市场营销预算。


过去完成时:
They had reassigned the funds before the audit. = 审计之前，他们已经重新分配了资金。
The manager had reassigned the tasks before leaving for vacation. = 经理在休假前已经重新分配了任务。


将来时:
They will reassign the responsibilities next week. = 他们下周将重新分配责任。
The team will reassign the roles after the project review. = 项目审查后，团队将重新分配角色。


虚拟语气:
If they reassigned the resources, the project would be completed on time. = 如果他们重新分配资源，项目就能按时完成。
I wish they had reassigned the task to someone more experienced. = 我希望他们把任务重新分配给更有经验的人。


被动语态:
The tasks were reassigned to the new team members. = 任务被重新分配给了新的团队成员。
The budget is being reassigned to different departments. = 预算正在被重新分配到不同的部门。


疑问句:
Will the tasks be reassigned? = 任务会被重新分配吗？
Have the resources been reassigned yet? = 资源已经被重新分配了吗？


否定句:
They did not reassign the project. = 他们没有重新分配项目。
The manager will not reassign the staff without proper justification. = 经理不会无正当理由重新分配员工。


PHRASE->

reassign resources =重新分配资源
