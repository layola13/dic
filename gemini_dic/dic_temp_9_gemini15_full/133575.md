nonflakiest: /ˌnɒnˈfleɪkiɪst/ | adj. | 最不片状的，最不脱落的 |  This word is the superlative form of "nonflaky," meaning not tending to flake or peel. It describes something that is exceptionally resistant to flaking or peeling.  The word combines the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with "flakiest" (the superlative form of "flaky").

The nonflakiest paint we tested lasted for over five years without peeling. 我们测试过的最不易脱落的油漆持续使用了五年多而没有剥落。

This nonflakiest sunscreen provides excellent protection without leaving a chalky residue. 这款最不易脱落的防晒霜提供了极好的保护，而且不会留下粉状残留物。


近义词：most durable, most solid, least flaky,  most stable

反义词：flakiest, most peeling,  least durable
