evangelists:/ɪˈvændʒəlɪsts/ | n. | 福音传道者；拥护者 |  A person who actively seeks to convert others to their cause or belief, especially in religion.  An evangelist is someone who zealously promotes a particular cause or idea.  In a religious context, they are preachers who spread the Gospel. | Example 1:  Many evangelists traveled the country, preaching their message of faith.  许多福音传道者走遍全国各地，宣讲他们的信仰信息。 | Example 2:  She's an evangelist for environmental protection, tirelessly promoting sustainable practices. 她是一位环保主义者，不遗余力地推广可持续发展实践。 | 近义词：preacher, missionary, advocate, proponent | 反义词：skeptic, opponent, critic |  名词
