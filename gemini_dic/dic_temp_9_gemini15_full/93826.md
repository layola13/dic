immigratory: /ˈɪmɪɡrətɔːri/ | adj. | 移民的；移居的 | Relating to immigration or immigrants.  The word describes something connected to the process of people coming to live permanently in a foreign country. |

例句：

The immigratory policies of the country are quite strict.  = 这个国家的移民政策非常严格。

The immigratory experience can be both challenging and rewarding. = 移民经历既充满挑战，也令人欣慰。


近义词：migration-related, immigrant-related

反义词：emigratory (relating to emigration)


词性：形容词
