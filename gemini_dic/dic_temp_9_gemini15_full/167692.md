prideful: /ˈpraɪdfəl/ | adj. | 骄傲的；自负的 | Characterized by or feeling pride, especially excessive pride; arrogant.  |例句：His prideful demeanor alienated many of his colleagues.  他的骄傲态度疏远了他的许多同事。

例句：She was prideful of her accomplishments, but not boastful. 她为自己的成就感到骄傲，但并不夸耀。


近义词：proud, arrogant, haughty, vain, conceited | 反义词：humble, modest, unassuming


词源:  The word "prideful" comes from the Old English word "prūde," meaning "proud."  The suffix "-ful" was added later to create the adjective form, indicating "full of pride."
