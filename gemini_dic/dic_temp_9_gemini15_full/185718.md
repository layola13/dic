revertible:/rɪˈvɜːrtəbl/| adj.| 可逆的；可撤销的；可回复的 | Capable of being reversed or changed back to a previous state or condition.  Something that is revertible can be undone or returned to its original form. |例句：The process is revertible, so any mistakes can be easily corrected. 这个过程是可逆的，所以任何错误都可以很容易地纠正。

The decision is revertible; the company can change its mind.  这个决定是可以改变的；公司可以改变主意。

|近义词：reversible, reversible, returnable |反义词：irreversible, permanent, irreversible
