nonstereotypical:/ˌnɒnˌstɛəriəˈtɪpɪkəl/ | adj. |非刻板印象的，不符合刻板印象的 | Not conforming to or representing a stereotype.  This adjective describes something or someone that doesn't fit into a commonly held, often oversimplified, belief about a particular group of people or thing. | 例句：The film offered a nonstereotypical portrayal of a working-class family. 这部电影对工薪阶层家庭的刻画并非刻板印象。
