词源:
The word "cancelli" is the plural of the Italian word "cancello," meaning "gate" or "barrier."  It ultimately derives from Latin "cancellus," a diminutive of "cancer," meaning "lattice," referring to the crisscrossed structure often seen in gates and railings.
