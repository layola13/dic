/ˈpɪktʃər/| n. |图画，照片，景象 |A picture is worth a thousand words. 一图胜千言。| image, photograph, illustration | abstract concept | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The picture hangs on the wall. = 图片挂在墙上。
This picture depicts a beautiful landscape. = 这幅图画描绘了一幅美丽的风景。


一般过去时:
I took a picture of the sunset. = 我拍了一张日落的照片。
She painted a picture of her family. = 她画了一幅她家人的画像。


现在完成时:
He has drawn many pictures this year. = 他今年画了很多画。
They have already taken pictures with the celebrity. = 他们已经和名人合影了。


过去完成时:
She had already painted the picture before the exhibition. = 展览开始之前，她已经画好了画。
They had taken many pictures of the event before the power went out. = 电力中断之前，他们已经拍了很多活动的图片。


将来时:
I will take a picture with my new camera. = 我将用我的新相机拍照。
We will paint a picture of our ideal future. = 我们将描绘我们理想的未来蓝图。


虚拟语气:
If I had a camera, I would take a picture. = 如果我有一台相机，我会拍张照片。
I wish I had taken a picture of the scenery. = 我真希望我拍下了那景色。


被动语态:
The picture was painted by a famous artist. = 这幅画是一位著名艺术家画的。
The pictures are being developed now. = 照片正在冲洗。


疑问句:
Is this the picture you took? = 这是你拍的照片吗？
What is depicted in the picture? = 图画中描绘了什么？


否定句:
I didn't take a picture. = 我没有拍照。
The picture doesn't show much detail. = 这张照片没有显示很多细节。
