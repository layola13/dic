jingo:/ˈdʒɪŋɡoʊ/ | n. adj. | 沙文主义者；侵略主义者；爱国心极端者 |

英文解析：A jingo is a person who is excessively patriotic and believes in the aggressive use of military force.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting blind patriotism and a willingness to go to war without considering the consequences.  As an adjective, "jingoistic" describes such behavior or attitudes.

例句：

He's a bit of a jingo, always boasting about his country's military might. = 他有点沙文主义倾向，总是吹嘘自己国家的军事实力。

The jingoistic rhetoric of the politician inflamed tensions between the two nations. = 这位政客的沙文主义言论加剧了两国之间的紧张关系。


近义词：chauvinist, nationalist, patriot (in a negative context), warmonger

反义词：pacifist, internationalist, cosmopolitan


词性：名词，形容词
