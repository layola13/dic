overshoe:/ˈoʊvərʃuː/ | n. |套鞋，胶鞋 |A covering for a shoe, typically made of rubber or plastic, worn to protect shoes from mud or water. Overshoes are often used in wet or snowy weather. |例句：I wore my overshoes to keep my feet dry in the rain. 我穿着套鞋以防雨天淋湿脚。  例句：She put on her overshoes before going outside in the snow.  下雪前她穿上了套鞋。 |近义词：galoshes, overboots |反义词：None |名词
