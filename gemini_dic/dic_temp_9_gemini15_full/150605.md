papermaking:/ˈpeɪpərˌmeɪkɪŋ/| n. |造纸术；造纸业|The process of making paper from plant fibers.  It involves various steps, from pulping the raw material to drying and finishing the paper sheets. |例句：The invention of papermaking revolutionized communication.  造纸术的发明彻底改变了交流方式。|例句： Ancient Chinese were pioneers in papermaking techniques. 古代中国人是造纸技术的先驱。|近义词：paper production, paper manufacture|反义词：无
