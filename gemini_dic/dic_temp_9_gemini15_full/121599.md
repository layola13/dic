misopinion:/ˌmɪs.əˈpɪn.jən/| n. |误解，错误的观点 |A misopinion is a wrong or inaccurate opinion; a misunderstanding.  It implies a belief that is not based on facts or correct information.  | Example: His misopinion about the situation led to a serious mistake.  他的对局势的误解导致了一个严重的错误。|近义词：misconception, misjudgment, fallacy |反义词：correct opinion, accurate understanding, truth


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense forms are not applicable.  The usage focuses on its function within a sentence.


Example 1:  The widespread misopinion that vaccines cause autism has been debunked by numerous scientific studies.  关于疫苗会导致自闭症的广泛误解已经被许多科学研究驳斥了。

Example 2:  Her misopinion regarding the company's financial health cost her a significant investment. 她对公司财务状况的误解让她损失了一笔可观的投资。

There are no common multi-word phrases directly involving "misopinion".  The word is used independently.
