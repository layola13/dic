cultivation:/ˌkʌltiˈveɪʃən/ | n. |耕作；栽培；培养；陶冶 |The act of cultivating or improving something, especially land, crops, or skills.  It encompasses the processes of preparing land for planting, nurturing plants, and harvesting crops, as well as the broader development of skills, knowledge, or qualities. |例句：The cultivation of rice requires careful water management.  水稻的种植需要细致的水分管理。

例句：Years of dedicated cultivation have made him a master musician.  多年的刻苦磨练使他成为一位大师级的音乐家。


近义词：development, improvement, refinement, training, nurture

反义词：neglect, destruction, abandonment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer cultivates his land every spring. = 农民每年春天都耕种他的土地。
She cultivates a positive attitude. = 她培养积极的态度。


一般过去时:
They cultivated a large vegetable garden last year. = 他们去年种植了一个大型菜园。
He cultivated a friendship with his colleague. = 他和同事培养了友谊。


现在完成时:
We have cultivated a strong relationship with our clients. = 我们已经和客户建立了牢固的关系。
She has cultivated her singing voice for many years. = 她多年来一直培养她的歌喉。


过去完成时:
They had cultivated the land before the rainy season. = 他们雨季前已经耕种了土地。
He had cultivated a reputation for honesty. = 他已经树立了诚实的声誉。


将来时:
We will cultivate more organic crops next year. = 我们明年将种植更多有机作物。
She will cultivate her public speaking skills.  = 她将培养她的公共演讲技巧。


虚拟语气:
If we cultivated more patience, we would be less stressed. = 如果我们培养更多的耐心，我们会减少压力。
I wish I had cultivated better study habits in my youth. = 我希望我年轻时培养了更好的学习习惯。


被动语态:
The land was cultivated by the local farmers. = 这块土地由当地农民耕种。
His talent was cultivated by his teacher. = 他的天赋由他的老师培养。


疑问句:
Do you cultivate any vegetables in your garden? = 你在你的花园里种植蔬菜吗？
How do you cultivate a sense of calm? = 你如何培养平静感？


否定句:
They did not cultivate the fields this year. = 他们今年没有耕种田地。
She does not cultivate relationships easily. = 她不容易培养人际关系。

词源:
From Middle English *cultivacioun, from Old French cultivaison, from cultiver "to cultivate," from Latin coltivāre, from colere "to cultivate, till, inhabit," related to cultus "cultivation, worship."  The root ultimately connects to concepts of "dwelling" and "care."
