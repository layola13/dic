It can refer to physical purity (absence of contaminants), moral purity (absence of sin or vice), or conceptual purity (absence of ambiguity or contradiction). | Example: The high purities of the materials ensured the success of the experiment.  高纯度的材料确保了实验的成功。 The purities of her intentions were never in doubt.  她的动机纯洁无瑕，从未令人怀疑。 |近义词：cleanliness, purity, innocence | 反义词：impurity, contamination, corruption | 名词
