declinometer:/ˌdɛklɪˈnɒmɪtər/ | n. | 磁偏计，倾角计 | An instrument used to measure the magnetic declination (the angle between magnetic north and true north) or the dip angle of the Earth's magnetic field.  It can also be used to measure the inclination of a plane or other surface. | Example: The surveyor used a declinometer to determine the magnetic declination at the site.  测量员使用磁偏计确定该地点的磁偏角。 |近义词：inclinometer, dipmeter | 反义词：None
