impropriate:/ɪmˈproʊprieɪt/| adj. | 不合适的；不恰当的 |  Impropriate refers to something that is not suitable or proper for a particular situation, time, or place. It suggests a lack of good judgment or consideration for social norms or context. | Example:  His comments were inappropriate for a formal dinner.  他的言论在正式晚宴上是不合适的。


His clothing was inappropriate for the occasion.他的穿着不适合这个场合。|近义词：unsuitable, improper, unfitting, unbecoming |反义词：appropriate, suitable, proper, fitting
