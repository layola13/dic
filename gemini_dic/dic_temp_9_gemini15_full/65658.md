evasible:/ɪˈveɪzəbəl/| adj.| 可逃避的；可规避的；难以捉摸的 | capable of being evaded or escaped; elusive.  The question of responsibility is evasible. The problem is evasible only by someone with extraordinary skill. |近义词：avoidable, escapable, elusive |反义词：inevitable, unavoidable, inescapable


例句：

The tax laws are so complex that many people find them evasible.  税法如此复杂，以至于许多人发现它们是可以逃避的。

His evasive answers made it difficult to get to the truth. 他闪烁其词的回答使得很难找到真相。


词源:

源自动词evade (逃避，规避) + 后缀-ible (可…的)。  Evade 最终源自拉丁语 *evadere，由 *e- (离开) + *vadere (走) 构成。


用法说明:

Evasive 通常用来描述行为或答案，而 evasible 通常用来描述情况或问题。  Easible 强调的是某种东西能够被逃避或规避的可能性。
