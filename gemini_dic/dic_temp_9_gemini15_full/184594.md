restorers:/rɪˈstɔːrərz/| n. |修复者，修复人员|People who restore something, such as art, buildings, or ecosystems.  They bring something back to its original condition or a former state of excellence.|The restorers painstakingly cleaned the ancient frescoes.  修复人员一丝不苟地清洁着古老的壁画。The museum hired a team of expert restorers to work on the damaged sculpture. 博物馆聘请了一支专家修复团队来修复受损的雕塑。|repairers, renovators, rehabilitators|destroyers, demolishers, vandals|名词
