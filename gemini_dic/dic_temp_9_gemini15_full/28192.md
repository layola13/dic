capriccio:/kəˈprɪtʃoʊ/| n. |奇想，随想曲；异想天开；反复无常 | A capriccio is a musical composition, typically for a solo instrument, characterized by its whimsical, improvisatory nature and often unexpected shifts in mood and tempo. It can also refer to a piece of art or writing that exhibits a similar spirit of spontaneity and caprice.  | Example:  The pianist played a delightful capriccio, full of unexpected turns and flourishes.  钢琴家演奏了一首令人愉快的随想曲，充满了意想不到的转折和华丽的装饰。  The artist's latest painting is a true capriccio, a whirlwind of color and form. 艺术家的最新画作是一幅真正的奇想之作，色彩和形式交织成旋风。 | 近义词：fantasy, whim, improvisation, caprice | 反义词：orderliness, predictability, regularity | 名词
