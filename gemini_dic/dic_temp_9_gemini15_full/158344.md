playsuit:/ˈpleɪsuːt/| n.|连体衣，连身裤|A playsuit is a one-piece garment, typically for women, that combines a top and shorts or trousers.|例句：She wore a stylish playsuit to the party. 她穿着时髦的连体衣去参加派对。
例句：The playsuit was made of a lightweight cotton fabric. 这套连体衣是用轻便的棉布制成的。|近义词：jumpsuit, romper|反义词：separate top and bottom
