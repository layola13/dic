novice:/ˈnɑːvɪs/| n. |新手，初学者 |A novice is a person who is new to a job or activity.  They lack experience and skill. |例句：He's a novice at skiing, so he's taking lessons. 他是滑雪新手，所以正在上滑雪课。
He's a novice in the field of finance. 他是金融领域的新手。|近义词：beginner, amateur, tyro |反义词：expert, veteran, professional
