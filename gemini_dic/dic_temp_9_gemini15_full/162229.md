pre-restoration:/ˌpriːˌrɛstəˈreɪʃən/ | adj. |修复前的，重建前的|Existing or occurring before a restoration or rebuilding.  Describes something that existed or happened before a process of restoring something to its former state. |例句：The pre-restoration photographs showed the building's dilapidated condition. 修复前的照片显示了建筑物破败不堪的状态。 The pre-restoration documents revealed the building's original design. 修复前的文件揭示了建筑物的原始设计。|近义词：prior to restoration, before restoration |反义词：post-restoration, after restoration |形容词
