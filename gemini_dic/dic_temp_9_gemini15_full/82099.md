groceries:/ˈɡroʊsəriz/ | n. | 食品杂货 | Food and household supplies sold in a grocery store.  The term often refers to the act of purchasing these items as well. | Example: I need to go to the grocery store to buy groceries for the week.  我需要去杂货店买一周的食品杂货。

I bought groceries online for the first time. 我第一次在网上买了杂货。


近义词：foodstuffs, provisions, supplies | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He buys groceries every Saturday. 他每周六都买杂货。
We get our groceries delivered. 我们让杂货送货上门。


一般过去时:
She bought groceries yesterday. 她昨天买了杂货。
They went to the grocery store to get groceries. 他们去杂货店买杂货。

We had already bought groceries before the storm hit.  暴风雨来临之前，我们已经买好了杂货。

将来时:
