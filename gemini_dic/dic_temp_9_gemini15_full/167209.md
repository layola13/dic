pretends: /prɪˈtɛndz/ | v. |假装，伪装 | To behave as if something is true when it is not; to make believe. |例句：He pretends to be a doctor. 他假装是医生。|例句：She pretended she didn't see me. 她假装没看见我。|近义词：feign, simulate, affect |反义词：be honest, be genuine


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pretends to be happy. = 他假装很快乐。
They pretend they don't know. = 他们假装不知道。

一般过去时:
She pretended she hadn't seen the letter. = 她假装没看见那封信。
He pretended to be asleep. = 他假装睡着了。

现在完成时:
He has pretended to be ill all week. = 他整个星期都在装病。
She has pretended not to hear me. = 她一直假装没听见我说话。

过去完成时:
He had pretended to be working when his boss came in. = 老板进来的时候，他假装在工作。
She had pretended she didn't know him before. = 她以前假装不认识他。

将来时:
I will pretend I didn't see you. = 我会假装没看见你。
They will pretend to be busy. = 他们会假装很忙。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would pretend to be calm. = 如果我是你，我会假装冷静。
I wish I could pretend I didn't care. = 我真希望我能假装不在乎。

被动语态:
His illness was pretended.  = 他的病是装出来的。(较少用被动)
It was pretended that nothing had happened. = 人们假装什么事也没发生。(强调动作而非施动者)


疑问句:
Does he pretend to be rich? = 他假装有钱吗？
Are they pretending to be friends? = 他们在假装是朋友吗？

否定句:
He doesn't pretend to be perfect. = 他不假装完美。
They didn't pretend to know the answer. = 他们没有假装知道答案。


PHRASE->
pretend to be = 假装是...
pretend to do something = 假装做某事
pretend sleep = 假装睡觉
pretend ignorance = 假装不知情
pretend not to see = 假装没看见
