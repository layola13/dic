senor:/seɪˈnɔːr/|/ˈsɛnɔːr/| |n.|先生；老爷|Spanish title of respect for a man, equivalent to "Mr." or "Sir".  It is often used in Spanish-speaking countries to address or refer to a man, especially an older one, as a sign of politeness or respect.|例句：Señor Garcia, may I help you? 加西亚先生，我能帮您吗？|例句：The senor was very kind to us.  这位先生对我们很和蔼。|近义词：mister, sir, gentleman|反义词： señora (女士)
