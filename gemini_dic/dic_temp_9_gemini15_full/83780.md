haired:/hɛrd/ | adj. | 有…毛发的；有…头发的 | Having hair of a specified type or color.  | He is a dark-haired man. 他是个黑发男人。  She has long, blonde haired children. 她有长着金发的孩子。|  dark-haired, light-haired, red-haired | bald, hairless |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat is short-haired. = 这只猫是短毛的。
He is brown-haired. = 他是棕发的。


一般过去时:
The dog was long-haired. = 这只狗是长毛的。
She was red-haired when she was young. = 她年轻的时候是红发的。


现在完成时:
He has always been light-haired. = 他一直都是浅发的。
The horse has become grey-haired. = 这匹马已经变成灰色的毛了。


过去完成时:
The puppy had been long-haired before it was cut. = 小狗在剪毛之前是长毛的。
She had been blonde-haired in her youth. = 她年轻时是金发的。


将来时:
The kitten will be long-haired soon. = 小猫很快就会长出长毛了。
He will likely be grey-haired by the time he's 50. = 到他50岁的时候，他很可能就头发花白了。


虚拟语气:
If he were dark-haired, he would look different. = 如果他是黑发的，他会看起来不一样。
I wish my dog were short-haired; it's easier to groom. = 我希望我的狗是短毛的；这样更容易打理。


被动语态:
The doll is described as having long, curly haired. = 这个娃娃被描述为有着长长的卷发。
The sheep were shorn, leaving them short-haired. = 羊被剪了毛，变成了短毛的。


疑问句:
Is she light-haired or dark-haired? = 她是浅发还是深发？
Are those long-haired cats? = 那些是长毛猫吗？


否定句:
He is not red-haired; he is brown-haired. = 他不是红发的；他是棕发的。
The cat is not short-haired; it's medium-haired. = 这只猫不是短毛的；它是中毛的。
