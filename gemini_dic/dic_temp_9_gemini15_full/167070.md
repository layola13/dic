presupervise:/ˌpriːˈsuːpərvaɪz/| v.|预先监督；提前监督|To supervise beforehand or in advance; to oversee preparations or preliminary stages.  This word is a relatively rare and newly coined term, not widely found in standard dictionaries. It's formed by combining "pre-" (meaning before) and "supervise".|例句：The project manager will presupervise the team's initial research to ensure its alignment with project goals. 项目经理将预先监督团队的初步研究，以确保其与项目目标一致。

例句：The safety officer will presupervise the construction site setup to prevent accidents. 安全员将预先监督施工现场的搭建，以防止事故发生。


近义词：pre-inspect, pre-check, oversee (preparations), review (plans)
反义词：post-supervise, monitor (after the fact)
词性:动词
