nicotinization:/ˌnɪkətaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |尼古丁化；尼古丁中毒|The process of becoming saturated or affected by nicotine.  This can refer to the physiological effects of nicotine on the body, or the process of adding nicotine to something. |例句：The study investigated the effects of nicotinization on the brain. 这项研究调查了尼古丁对大脑的影响。

例句：The cigarettes underwent a process of nicotinization to control their strength. 这些香烟经过尼古丁化处理以控制其强度。

近义词：nicotinisation (British spelling) | 反义词：denicotinization (removal of nicotine) | 词性：名词
