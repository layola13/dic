oxytoluene:/ˌɒksɪˈtɒljʊin/| n. |邻甲基苯酚，邻甲酚|A cresol isomer, a methylphenol with the methyl group located ortho to the hydroxyl group. It's a colorless to pale yellow liquid with a characteristic phenolic odor. It is used as a disinfectant and antiseptic, also as an intermediate in the production of other chemicals. |Example: Oxytoluene is a powerful disinfectant used in various cleaning products.|近义词: cresol, methylphenol |反义词:  (No direct antonym, but compounds without the hydroxyl group could be considered contrasting)|名词
