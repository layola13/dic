cyberattacks:/ˈsaɪbərəˌtæks/| n.|网络攻击|Attacks on computer systems and networks, often with malicious intent.  These can range from simple denial-of-service attacks to sophisticated data breaches and malware infections. |例句：The company suffered a major cyberattack that compromised its customer data.  这家公司遭受了一次重大的网络攻击，导致客户数据泄露。|例句：  Governments are investing heavily in cybersecurity to protect against cyberattacks.  各国政府正在大力投资网络安全以防范网络攻击。|近义词：hack attacks, online attacks, digital attacks |反义词：cybersecurity, network protection
