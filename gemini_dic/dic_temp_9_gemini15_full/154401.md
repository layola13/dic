permeators:/ˈpɜːrmieɪtərz/ | n. |渗透物；渗透剂 | Materials or devices that allow substances to pass through them, often selectively.  Permeators are used in various applications, such as membrane separation processes. | Example:  Reverse osmosis membranes utilize specialized permeators to separate water from dissolved salts and impurities. 反渗透膜利用特殊的渗透剂将水与溶解的盐分和杂质分离。|近义词：filters, separators |反义词：barriers, blockades
