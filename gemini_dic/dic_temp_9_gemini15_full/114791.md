maltreatment:/ˌmælˈtriːtmənt/ | n. |虐待；虐待行为 | The cruel and inhumane treatment of a person or animal.  It encompasses physical, emotional, and sexual abuse. |例句：The children suffered years of maltreatment at the hands of their parents.  孩子们多年来遭受父母的虐待。

例句：Animal maltreatment is a serious crime and should be punished severely. 动物虐待是严重的罪行，应该受到严厉的惩罚。


近义词：abuse, ill-treatment, mistreatment, cruelty, oppression | 反义词：kindness, care, compassion, tenderness


词源:  The word "maltreatment" is derived from the prefix "mal-" (meaning "bad" or "ill") and the noun "treatment."  The original meaning simply implied bad or improper treatment, but it has evolved to specifically refer to cruel and inhumane treatment.
