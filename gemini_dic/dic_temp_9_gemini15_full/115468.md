manufactured:/ˌmænjuˈfæktʃərd/| adj. |人造的；制造的；人工的|Made or produced industrially, especially on a large scale.  This refers to items created through a process of manufacturing, implying a level of industrial production rather than handcrafted creation.|例句：Many of the toys are manufactured in China. 许多玩具都是在中国制造的。
例句：This fabric has a manufactured feel. 这块布料给人一种人造的感觉。|近义词：produced, fabricated, artificial, synthetic|反义词：natural, handmade, organic|词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory manufactures cars. = 工厂生产汽车。
They manufacture high-quality products. = 他们生产高质量的产品。

一般过去时:
The company manufactured toys for children. = 这家公司生产儿童玩具。
They manufactured a new type of engine. = 他们制造了一种新型发动机。

现在完成时:
They have manufactured over 100,000 units this year. = 今年他们已经生产了超过10万台设备。
The company has manufactured many successful products. = 这家公司已经生产了许多成功的产品。

过去完成时:
They had manufactured all the parts before the assembly began. = 在组装开始之前，他们已经制造好了所有零件。
The factory had manufactured millions of items before closing. = 工厂在关闭之前已经生产了数百万件商品。

将来时:
The factory will manufacture more electric cars next year. = 工厂明年将生产更多电动汽车。
They will manufacture a new model of smartphone. = 他们将生产一款新型智能手机。

虚拟语气:
If they manufactured more efficiently, they would be more profitable. = 如果他们生产效率更高，他们就会更有利润。
I wish they manufactured a smaller version of this product. = 我希望他们能生产这款产品的较小版本。

被动语态:
Thousands of cars are manufactured daily. = 每天生产数千辆汽车。
The product is manufactured using advanced technology. = 该产品采用先进技术制造。

疑问句:
Where are these products manufactured? = 这些产品在哪里制造？

否定句:

They did not manufacture enough products to meet the demand. = 他们生产的产品数量不足以满足需求。
