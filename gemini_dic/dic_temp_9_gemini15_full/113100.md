luncheons:/ˈlʌntʃənz/ | n. |午餐会；午宴 | A luncheon is a formal lunch, often a social or business event.  |例句：The company held a luncheon to celebrate its anniversary. 公司举行了一场午餐会庆祝周年纪念。|例句：She was invited to a luncheon at the ambassador's residence. 她被邀请参加大使官邸的午餐会。|近义词：lunch, banquet, feast |反义词：dinner (in the context of a formal evening meal)

The company holds luncheons every quarter. = 公司每个季度都举行午餐会。
We usually have a quick luncheon at our desks. = 我们通常在办公桌前快速享用午餐。
