regarder:/ʁəɡaʁde/| v.|注视，观看，看待|To look at someone or something; to consider or think about something.|例句：He regarded her with suspicion. 他怀疑地看着她。|例句：We need to regard this problem seriously. 我们需要认真对待这个问题。|近义词：look at, watch, observe, consider|反义词：ignore, neglect

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I regard him as a friend. = 我把他当作朋友。
She regards her work seriously. = 她认真对待她的工作。


一般过去时:
They regarded the news with disbelief. = 他们难以置信地看着这条新闻。
He regarded the painting with admiration. = 他欣赏地观赏这幅画。


现在完成时:
I have regarded him highly ever since. = 从那以后我一直很尊重他。
She has regarded the situation with concern. = 她一直担忧地看着局势。


过去完成时:
They had regarded him as a hero before the scandal. = 丑闻爆发前，他们一直把他视为英雄。
She had regarded the offer with skepticism before accepting it. = 在接受提议之前，她一直抱着怀疑的态度。


将来时:
I will regard your advice carefully. = 我会仔细考虑你的建议。
They will regard the matter with attention. = 他们会认真对待这件事。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would regard the situation differently. = 如果我是你，我会以不同的方式看待这种情况。
I wish I had regarded his warning more seriously. = 我真希望我当时更认真地对待他的警告。


被动语态:
The painting is regarded as a masterpiece. = 这幅画被认为是杰作。
His actions were regarded with disapproval. = 他的行为受到了谴责。


疑问句:
How do you regard his proposal? = 你如何看待他的提议？
What do they regard as success? = 他们把什么看作成功？


否定句:
I don't regard him as a threat. = 我不认为他是一个威胁。
She doesn't regard her job as important. = 她不认为她的工作很重要。


PHRASE->
regarder avec (with):  用……的眼神看
regarder fixement (fixedly): 凝视
regarder attentivement (attentively): 专注地看
regarder de haut (from above):居高临下地看
regarder autour de soi (around oneself):环顾四周
