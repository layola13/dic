cardigans:/ˈkɑːrdɪɡənz/| n. |羊毛衫，开襟衫|A cardigan is a knitted or crocheted jacket, typically made of wool, that opens down the front.  It's usually worn over other clothing. |例句：She wore a cozy cardigan over her blouse. 她穿着毛衣在衬衫外面。|例句：My grandmother knitted me a beautiful cardigan for Christmas. 我奶奶为我织了一件漂亮的羊毛衫作为圣诞礼物。|近义词：sweater, jumper, jacket|反义词：None specifically, but terms like "coat" might be considered opposites in terms of warmth and formality.
