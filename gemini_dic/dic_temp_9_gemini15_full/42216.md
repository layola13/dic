counseled:/ˈkaʊnsəld/ | v. | 劝告；建议；咨询；辅导 | To give advice to (someone); to provide guidance or support to (someone), often in a professional capacity.  |  The therapist counseled the patient on coping with anxiety.  治疗师就如何应对焦虑向病人进行了咨询。 | advised, guided, consulted | ignored, disregarded | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He counsels clients on financial matters. 他就财务问题向客户提供咨询。
She counsels students struggling with academic pressure. 她为那些在学业压力下挣扎的学生提供辅导。


一般过去时:
The lawyer counseled his client to plead guilty. 律师建议他的当事人认罪。
My mentor counseled me on my career path. 我的导师就我的职业道路给了我建议。


现在完成时:
She has counseled hundreds of people throughout her career.  在她职业生涯中，她已经为数百人提供过咨询。
The school has counseled many students facing difficult family situations. 学校已经为许多面临困难家庭状况的学生提供了咨询。


过去完成时:
He had counseled her extensively before she made her decision.  在她做出决定之前，他已经对她进行了广泛的咨询。
The teacher had counseled the student about his poor behavior before the incident.  在事件发生之前，老师已经就他的不良行为对学生进行了劝导。


将来时:
The doctor will counsel the patient about the risks of the surgery. 医生将就手术风险对病人进行咨询。
They will counsel the couple on improving their communication. 他们将就如何改善沟通向这对夫妇提供建议。


虚拟语气:
If I had counseled him earlier, he might not have made that mistake. 如果我早点劝告他，他可能就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I had counseled her better when she was struggling. 我希望在她挣扎的时候，我能更好地指导她。


被动语态:
The patient was counseled on the importance of regular exercise. 病人接受了关于定期锻炼重要性的咨询。
The students were counseled by the school psychologist about their issues. 学生们就他们的问题接受了学校心理咨询师的辅导。


疑问句:
Did the counselor counsel you about your depression? 咨询师是否就你的抑郁症对你进行了咨询？
Has he been counseled on the proper use of the equipment? 他是否接受过关于设备正确使用方法的指导？


否定句:
The teacher didn't counsel the student about his disruptive behavior. 老师没有就他的扰乱行为对学生进行劝导。
They did not counsel her on the potential consequences. 他们没有就潜在的后果对她进行劝告。


PHRASE->

counsel against:  劝阻...
counsel someone to do something:  劝某人做某事
counsel with someone:  与某人商议
seek counsel:  寻求建议
give counsel: 提供建议
