preferences: /ˈprɛfərənsɪz/ | n. |偏好，喜爱，优先选择 | Preferences are the things that a person likes best.  They reflect personal tastes and choices. This can range from favorite foods to preferred work styles. |  My preferences are for spicy food and classical music. 我的偏好是辛辣食物和古典音乐。 I have a strong preference for working independently. 我强烈偏好独立工作。 | predilections, inclinations, likings | dislikes, aversions | 名词
