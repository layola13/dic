rebus:/ˈriːbʌs/ | n. |谜语；字谜|A rebus is a puzzle where the answer is represented by a combination of pictures, symbols, or letters.  It often involves a play on words or a clever use of visual elements.|Example: A picture of a sun and a picture of a flower might be a rebus for "sunflower".|近义词：enigma, riddle, puzzle |反义词：answer, solution
