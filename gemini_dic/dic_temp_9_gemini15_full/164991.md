prehazard:/ˈpriːˌhæzərd/| n. |潜在危害，预警危险|A prehazard is a potential danger or risk that has not yet occurred but could occur in the future. It is a warning sign of a possible hazard.  Prehazards are often identified through risk assessments or hazard identification processes. |Example:  The cracked pavement was a prehazard, indicating a potential tripping hazard.  破损的人行道是一个潜在的危险，预示着可能存在的绊倒危险。|近义词：potential hazard, warning sign, risk factor|反义词：safe, secure, harmless|名词
