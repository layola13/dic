peritreme:/ˈpɛrɪtriːm/| n. |周缘; 周围结构 |A peritreme is the raised rim or margin around the opening of a structure, especially the opening of a certain type of fossil shell or the mouth of a volcano.  It often refers to the raised edge surrounding the opening of a brachiopod shell.  |例句：The peritreme of the brachiopod fossil was exceptionally well-preserved.  腕足类化石的周缘保存得非常好。|例句：The geologist carefully examined the peritreme of the volcanic crater. 地质学家仔细检查了火山坑的边缘。|近义词：rim, margin, border, edge |反义词：center, interior
