second-class:/ˈsɛkənd klæs/| adj.|二等的；二流的；劣等的|Of inferior quality, rank, or status.  It implies something is not as good as the best or standard.|例句：The hotel offered second-class accommodations. 这家酒店提供二等住宿。

例句：He received a second-class degree. 他获得了二等学位。


近义词：inferior, substandard, low-grade, mediocre

反义词：first-class, superior, high-grade, excellent


词源：源自"second" (第二的) + "class" (等级，类别).  It originally referred to a literal second class of travel, but has broadened to encompass a more general sense of inferiority.
