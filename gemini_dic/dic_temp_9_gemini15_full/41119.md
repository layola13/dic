coprolite:/ˈkɒprəlaɪt/| n. |粪化石| Fossilized excrement or dung.  Coprolites provide valuable information about the diet and health of extinct animals.  They are often found in sedimentary rocks and can range in size and shape depending on the animal that produced them. |The analysis of coprolites revealed the presence of undigested plant material, indicating a herbivorous diet.|近义词：fossilized dung, fossilized feces |反义词：None
