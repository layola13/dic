comets:/ˈkɒmɪts/ /ˈkɑːmɪts/| n.|彗星|A celestial body moving about the sun, usually in a highly eccentric orbit, that consists of a central mass surrounded by a nebulous envelope and often a tail.|例句：Halley's Comet is a famous periodic comet.  哈雷彗星是一颗著名的周期彗星。
例句：The comet streaked across the night sky. 这颗彗星划过夜空。|近义词：meteor|反义词:无
