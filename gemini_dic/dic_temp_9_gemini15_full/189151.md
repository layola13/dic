sack:/sæk/| n., vt.| 袋子；解雇；洗劫；大量；|A sack is a large bag made of strong material, often used for carrying things.  As a verb, it means to dismiss someone from their job, or to plunder a place. |例句：He carried the potatoes in a large sack. 他用一个大麻袋装着土豆。|例句：The manager sacked him for being late. 经理因为他的迟到而解雇了他。|近义词：bag, dismiss, fire, plunder, loot |反义词：hire, employ, keep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sacks potatoes into the truck. = 他把土豆装进卡车。
She sacks the groceries. = 她把杂货装进袋子里。


一般过去时:
They sacked the city. = 他们洗劫了这座城市。
The company sacked several employees. = 公司解雇了几名员工。


现在完成时:
He has sacked three employees this month. = 这个月他已经解雇了三名员工。
She has sacked all her old clothes. = 她已经把所有旧衣服都扔掉了。(此处sack指丢弃，非解雇)


过去完成时:
They had sacked the village before the army arrived. = 军队到达之前，他们已经洗劫了村庄。
The company had sacked him before he even knew about the merger. = 在他甚至知道合并之前，公司就已经解雇了他。


将来时:
They will sack the building and look for clues. = 他们将会搜查这栋建筑物并寻找线索。
The boss will probably sack him if he continues to be late. = 如果他继续迟到，老板可能会解雇他。


虚拟语气:
If he were to be sacked, he would lose his health insurance. = 如果他被解雇，他将失去医疗保险。
I wish they hadn't sacked her; she was a valuable employee. = 我真希望他们没有解雇她；她是一位宝贵的员工。


被动语态:
The city was sacked by the invaders. = 这座城市被入侵者洗劫了。
He was sacked for misconduct. = 他因行为不端而被解雇。


疑问句:
Will they sack him? = 他们会解雇他吗？
Have they sacked the entire department? = 他们解雇了整个部门吗？


否定句:
They did not sack him. = 他们没有解雇他。
The company won't sack anyone without a good reason. = 公司不会无缘无故解雇任何人。


PHRASE->

sleep in the sack = 睡在睡袋里
