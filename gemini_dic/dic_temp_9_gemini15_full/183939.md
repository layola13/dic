reservoirs:/ˈrezəvwɑːrz/| n. |水库；蓄水池；储藏库；知识的宝库|A reservoir is a large artificial lake used for storing water, especially for supplying a town or city.|The city relies on several reservoirs for its water supply.  这个城市依靠几个水库供水。|The new reservoir will provide water for thousands of people.  新的水库将为成千上万的人提供水。|lake, tank, pond|desert, drought


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The reservoir holds a vast amount of water. = 这个水库蓄积了大量的清水。
Many reservoirs supply drinking water to the region. = 许多水库为该地区提供饮用水。


一般过去时:
The old reservoir leaked last winter. = 去年冬天，旧水库漏水了。
They built a new reservoir to address water shortages. = 他们建造了一个新的水库来解决缺水问题。


现在完成时:
The reservoir has overflowed several times this year. = 今年这个水库已经溢出好几次了。
We have relied on this reservoir for decades. = 几十年来，我们一直依靠这个水库。


过去完成时:
The dam had collapsed before anyone could react. = 大坝倒塌之前，没有人能够做出反应。（此处reservoir 可隐含在dam中）
The reservoir had been full just a few days before the drought began. = 干旱开始前的几天，水库还是满的。


将来时:
They will build a new reservoir next year. = 他们明年将建造一个新的水库。
The reservoir will likely be full by spring. = 到春天，水库很可能会装满。


虚拟语气:
If the reservoir were to fail, the city would be in crisis. = 如果水库发生故障，城市将面临危机。
I wish the reservoir had more water. = 我希望水库有更多的水。


被动语态:
The reservoir is being cleaned regularly. = 水库定期进行清洗。
The water level in the reservoir is being monitored closely. = 水库的水位正受到密切监控。


疑问句:
Is the reservoir safe? = 水库安全吗？
How much water does the reservoir contain? = 水库有多少水？


否定句:
The reservoir is not full. = 水库没有装满。
They did not fill the reservoir completely. = 他们没有完全灌满水库。
