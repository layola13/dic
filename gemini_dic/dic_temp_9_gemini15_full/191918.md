scone:/skɒn/|n.|烤饼|A small, slightly sweet quick bread, typically baked until golden brown and often served with clotted cream and jam.  Scones can be made with a variety of ingredients, including butter, milk, and flour. They are a popular breakfast or afternoon tea treat, especially in Britain.|例句：I enjoyed a delicious scone with clotted cream and strawberry jam for afternoon tea. 我享用了一块美味的烤饼，配上凝脂奶油和草莓酱作为下午茶。|例句：The bakery sells a variety of scones, including plain, fruit, and cheese scones. 这家面包店出售各种烤饼，包括原味、水果和奶酪烤饼。|近义词：biscuit (in some contexts)|反义词：无
