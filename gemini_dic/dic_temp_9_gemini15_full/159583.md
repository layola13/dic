pollen:/ˈpɒlən/ /ˈpɑːlən/| n. |花粉|Fine powdery substance, typically yellow, consisting of microscopic grains discharged from the male part of a flower or from a male cone, serving to fertilize the female part.  It is a key element in plant reproduction, carried by wind, insects, or other means.|Example: The air was thick with pollen, causing allergies for many people.  空气中充满了花粉，导致许多人过敏。

Example: Bees collect pollen from flowers to make honey. 蜜蜂采集花朵的花粉酿造蜂蜜。


近义词：spores(孢子) (in some contexts)

反义词：None directly;  Antonyms would relate to the absence of pollen or its effect, such as sterile (不育的).

词性:名词
