redeveloping:/ˌriːdɪˈvɛləpɪŋ/| v. |再开发；重建；重新发展|The act of developing something again, often after a period of decline or neglect.  This involves improving or transforming an area or asset. |例句：The city is redeveloping the waterfront area.  城市正在重新开发滨水区。

例句：They are redeveloping the old factory into a modern apartment complex. 他们正在将旧工厂改建成现代化的公寓楼。


近义词：reconstructing, renovating, regenerating, revitalizing

反义词：abandoning, neglecting, demolishing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are redeveloping the downtown area. = 他们正在重建市中心区域。
The company is redeveloping its marketing strategy. = 公司正在重新制定其营销策略。


一般过去时:
The government redeveloped the port. = 政府重建了港口。
They redeveloped the old building into a museum. = 他们把旧建筑改建成了博物馆。


现在完成时:
They have redeveloped several areas of the city. = 他们已经重建了城市的几个区域。
The company has redeveloped its software to improve its efficiency. = 公司已经重新开发了其软件以提高效率。


过去完成时:
They had redeveloped the site before the new tenants moved in. = 新租户搬进来之前，他们已经重新开发了该场地。
The city had redeveloped the park before the summer festival. = 夏季节庆之前，城市已经重新开发了公园。


将来时:
They will redevelop the abandoned mine. = 他们将重新开发废弃的矿山。
The city will redevelop the old train station into a shopping mall. = 城市将把旧火车站改建成购物中心。


虚拟语气:
If they redeveloped the area, it would attract more tourists. = 如果他们重新开发这个区域，将会吸引更多游客。
I wish they had redeveloped the school sooner. = 我希望他们早点重建学校。


被动语态:
The area is being redeveloped. = 该区域正在被重新开发。
The old building is being redeveloped into a hotel. = 旧建筑正在被改建成酒店。


疑问句:
Are they redeveloping the park? = 他们正在重新开发公园吗？
Is the company redeveloping its products? = 公司正在重新开发其产品吗？


否定句:
They are not redeveloping the beach. = 他们没有重新开发海滩。
The company did not redevelop its website last year. = 公司去年没有重新开发其网站。
