make-work:/ˈmeɪkˌwɜːrk/| n. adj.|虚假工作；敷衍塞责的工作；|Work that is contrived or unnecessary, often designed to keep people busy without achieving anything meaningful.  It can also refer to a job or task that is created artificially to provide employment.|例句：The new policy led to a lot of make-work projects that didn't actually improve efficiency.  这项新政策导致了许多实际上并未提高效率的虚假项目。|近义词：busywork, pointless work, time-wasting task,  artificial employment |反义词：productive work, meaningful work, gainful employment|名词，形容词
