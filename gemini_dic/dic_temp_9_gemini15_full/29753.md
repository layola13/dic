catpipe:/ˈkætpaɪp/| n.|猫管，排水管|A catpipe is a small pipe, often made of clay, used to drain water or sewage.  It's typically found in older buildings or in specific plumbing systems.  The term likely derives from its resemblance to a cat's tail or the way it might be hidden and inconspicuous.|例句：The old house's drainage system relied on a network of catpipes. 这栋老房子的排水系统依靠的是一套猫管网络。The plumber replaced the broken catpipe under the sink. 水管工更换了水槽下破裂的猫管。|近义词：drainpipe, sewer pipe|反义词：N/A|名词
