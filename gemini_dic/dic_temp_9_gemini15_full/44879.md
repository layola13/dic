crybabies: /ˈkraɪˌbeɪbiz/ | n. |爱哭鬼，爱哭的人 | A crybaby is a person, especially a child, who cries a lot, often for trivial reasons.  They are often perceived as overly sensitive or lacking resilience. | Example:  He's such a crybaby; he cries every time he loses a game. 他真是个爱哭鬼；每次输了比赛都哭。  Stop being such a crybaby and face your problems. 别再像个爱哭鬼了，勇敢面对你的问题。| 近义词: whiner, complainer, weakling | 反义词: stoic, tough, resilient |  名词
