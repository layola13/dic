regenerated:/ˌriːˈdʒɛnəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 再生的；更新的；改造的 |  Having been regenerated; renewed or restored.  The regenerated forest is thriving.  | 近义词：renewed, revitalized, reborn | 反义词：destroyed, ruined, decayed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The city regenerates its downtown area. = 这个城市正在更新改造其市中心区域。
This plant regenerates itself through cuttings. = 这植物通过扦插繁殖自身。


一般过去时:
The community regenerated after the disaster. = 社区在灾难后重建了。
The factory regenerated its production line. = 工厂改造了它的生产线。


现在完成时:
The town has regenerated significantly in recent years. = 近年来，这个小镇已经得到了显著的改造。
The forest has regenerated itself after the fire. = 森林在大火之后已经自我更新了。


过去完成时:
The city had regenerated its infrastructure before the new businesses arrived. = 新企业入驻之前，这座城市已经更新了其基础设施。
They had regenerated the old building into a modern apartment complex. = 他们已将旧建筑改造成现代化公寓大楼。


将来时:
The government will regenerate the neglected areas of the city. = 政府将改造城市中被忽视的地区。
The company will regenerate its brand image. = 公司将重塑其品牌形象。


虚拟语气:
