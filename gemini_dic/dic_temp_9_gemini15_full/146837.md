overinclines: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈklaɪnz/ | v. | 倾向过度，过于偏向 | To lean or bend excessively; to be excessively inclined towards something.  It implies a degree of imbalance or disproportion. | The tower overinclines to the west.  塔向西过度倾斜。| The government overinclines towards deregulation. 政府过度倾向于放松管制。|近义词：lean excessively, tilt excessively, favor excessively | 反义词：stand straight, be balanced, be impartial
