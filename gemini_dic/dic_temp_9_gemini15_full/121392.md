mishits:/ˈmɪʃɪts/| n.|失误；击不中；脱靶|A mishit is a shot, hit, or attempt that does not achieve its intended result, often in sports or games.  It implies a lack of accuracy or precision.|例句：The golfer's mishits cost him the tournament. 高尔夫球手的失误让他输掉了比赛。

The batter's mishit resulted in an easy out.  击球手的击球失误导致轻松出局。|近义词：miss, mis-hit, error, blunder, fault|反义词：bullseye, hit, success
