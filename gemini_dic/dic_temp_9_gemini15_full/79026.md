give:/ɡɪv/| vt.|给予，赠送，提供|To present something to someone; to transfer possession of something.  It can also mean to express or deliver something, such as an opinion or performance.|例句：He gave me a book. 他给了我一本。

例句：She gave a speech at the conference.她在会议上做了一个演讲。|近义词：offer, donate, present, bestow|反义词：receive, take, withhold|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I give you my word. = 我向你保证。
They give generously to charity. = 他们慷慨地捐款给慈善机构。

一般过去时:
She gave him a second chance. = 她给了他第二次机会。
He gave a detailed explanation. = 他做了一个详细的解释。

现在完成时:
I have given him all the information he needs. = 我已经给了他所有他需要的信息。
They have given up smoking. = 他们已经戒烟了。

过去完成时:
She had given him a warning before. = 她之前已经警告过他了。
They had given their best effort. = 他们已经尽了最大的努力。

将来时:
I will give you a call later. = 我稍后会给你打电话。
They will give a presentation tomorrow. = 他们明天将做一个演示。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would give it to you. = 如果我有钱，我会给你。
I wish I could give you more help. = 我希望我能给你更多帮助。

被动语态:
The prize was given to the best student. = 奖品颁给了最好的学生。
The information was given to the police. = 信息已经提供给了警方。

疑问句:
What did you give him? = 你给了他什么？
Will you give me a ride? = 你能载我一程吗？

否定句:
I didn't give him my address. = 我没有给他我的地址。
They didn't give a satisfactory answer. = 他们没有给出令人满意的答案。
