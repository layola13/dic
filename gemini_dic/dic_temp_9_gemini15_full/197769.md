shimmed:/ʃɪmd/| v. |用垫片调整；加垫片|To adjust or support something by placing shims beneath or beside it.  This is often done to level something or fill a gap.|例句：The mechanic shimmed the engine block to align it properly. = 机械师用垫片调整发动机缸体以使其正确对齐。

例句：We shimmed the door to stop it from sticking. = 我们用垫片调整门以防止它卡住。|近义词：adjust, level, align |反义词：misalign, unlevel
