elut: /ˈɛljuːt/ | vt. | 洗脱；溶出 | To wash out; to extract (a substance) by washing or dissolving.  |The researchers eluted the proteins from the chromatography column. 研究人员从层析柱中洗脱蛋白质。|  近义词：extract, leach, wash out | 反义词：retain, absorb
