respraying:/ˌriːˈspreɪɪŋ/| v. |重新喷涂，再喷漆 |The act of spraying again; applying a new coat of paint or other substance by spraying.|例句：The car needs respraying after the accident. 事故后，汽车需要重新喷漆。

例句：The walls are being resprayed to freshen up the room.  墙壁正在重新喷涂以使房间焕然一新。


近义词：repainting, recoating


反义词：stripping (removing paint)


词性：动词
