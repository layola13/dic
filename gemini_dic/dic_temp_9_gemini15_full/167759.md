primally:/ˈpraɪməli/| adv.|主要地，根本地|primarily means mainly or essentially. It emphasizes the most important aspect or cause of something.  |Example: The project failed primarily due to lack of funding.  这个项目主要由于缺乏资金而失败了。|近义词：mainly, chiefly, essentially, principally|反义词：secondarily, incidentally, marginally


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The success is primarily due to hard work. = 成功主要归功于努力工作。
We primarily use this software for data analysis. = 我们主要使用这个软件进行数据分析。


一般过去时:
They were primarily concerned with safety. = 他们主要关心安全问题。
The decision was primarily based on cost. = 该决定主要基于成本考虑。


现在完成时:
We have primarily focused on improving customer service. = 我们主要致力于改进客户服务。
They have primarily relied on traditional methods. = 他们主要依赖传统方法。


过去完成时:
He had primarily focused on his career before starting a family. = 在组建家庭之前，他主要专注于他的事业。
The problem had primarily stemmed from a misunderstanding. = 问题主要源于误解。


将来时:
We will primarily use renewable energy sources. = 我们将主要使用可再生能源。
The company will primarily target young adults. = 公司将主要目标客户群定位为年轻人。


虚拟语气:
If we were primarily concerned with profits, we would have made different choices. = 如果我们主要关心利润，我们会做出不同的选择。
I wish we had primarily focused on quality control. = 我希望我们主要关注质量控制。


被动语态:
The decision was primarily influenced by external factors. = 该决定主要受外部因素影响。
The problem is primarily caused by human error. = 这个问题主要由人为错误造成。


疑问句:
Is the problem primarily caused by software issues? = 这个问题主要由软件问题引起吗？
What are the primarily factors contributing to this phenomenon? = 导致这种现象的主要因素是什么？


否定句:
The failure was not primarily due to negligence. = 失败并非主要由于疏忽。
It wasn't primarily about money, but about principle. = 这主要不是关于钱，而是关于原则。
