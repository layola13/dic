overdistant: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪstənt/ | adj. |过于遥远的，关系疏远的 | excessively distant; far beyond a reasonable or acceptable distance, either physically or emotionally.  |例句：The two cousins, once close, had become overdistant after a bitter quarrel.  两个表兄弟，曾经很亲近，在一次激烈的争吵后变得疏远了。

例句：The island was overdistant for a day trip.  这个岛屿距离太远，不适合一日游。


近义词：remote, far-off, estranged, alienated, detached

反义词：close, near, intimate, connected


词性：形容词
