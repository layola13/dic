insulating: /ˈɪnsəleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 隔热的，绝缘的 |  Preventing the passage of heat, electricity, or sound.  |  The house is well-insulating, keeping it warm in winter and cool in summer.  这房子隔热性能好，冬天保暖，夏天凉爽。 |  isolating, non-conductive | conductive, permeable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The blanket is insulating the baby from the cold. = 这条毯子使婴儿免受寒冷。
This material insulates against heat. = 这种材料具有隔热作用。


一般过去时:
They insulated the wires to prevent a short circuit. = 他们给电线包了绝缘层以防止短路。
The workers insulated the attic to improve energy efficiency. = 工人们给阁楼加了隔热层以提高能源效率。


现在完成时:
We have insulated the walls of the house to make it more energy-efficient. = 我们已经给房子的墙壁加了隔热层，以提高能源效率。

过去完成时:
