drummer:/ˈdrʌmər/ | n. |鼓手|A person who plays the drums. Drummers are essential to many musical genres, providing the rhythm and beat.|例句：The drummer's powerful beat kept the audience energized.  那个鼓手的强劲节奏让观众充满活力。|The band's drummer left unexpectedly last week.乐队的鼓手上周突然离开了。|近义词：percussionist, timpanist |反义词：N/A
