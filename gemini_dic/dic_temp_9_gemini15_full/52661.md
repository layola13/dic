digitises:/ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪzɪz/| vt.|数字化；将…转换成数字形式|To convert information or data into a digital format, typically from an analog format.|例句：The museum is digitizing its entire collection of photographs.  博物馆正在将其所有照片收藏数字化。|例句：We need to digitize our filing system to improve efficiency. 我们需要将我们的文件系统数字化以提高效率。|近义词：convert, transform|反义词：analogize
