overinhibiting: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈhɪbɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |过度抑制的，抑制过度的|  Describes a state or action where inhibition is excessive or beyond what is necessary or healthy.  It suggests a suppression of natural impulses or behaviors to an unhealthy degree. |


例句：

His overinhibiting nature prevented him from expressing his true feelings.  他的过度抑制的天性阻止了他表达真实感受。

The medication caused overinhibiting of his motor functions.  这种药物导致了他的运动功能过度抑制。


近义词：suppressed, repressed, constricted, stifled, overcontrolled


反义词：uninhibited, unrestrained, free, expressive


词性：形容词
