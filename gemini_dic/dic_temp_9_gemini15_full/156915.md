pilcher:/ˈpɪltʃər/| n. |小水桶；（尤指旧时）盛牛奶的桶；（尤指）陶器水罐|A pilcher is a small pail, especially one formerly used for carrying milk, often made of earthenware.|例句：The milkmaid carried her pilcher carefully.  挤奶女工小心翼翼地提着她的水桶。|例句：The antique pilcher was a beautiful example of pottery.  这件古董水罐是陶器的精美范例。|近义词：pail, bucket, jug|反义词：large container, cistern|名词
