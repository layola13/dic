preferably: /ˈprɛfərəbli/ | adv. | 更好地，最好是，更可取地 |  This adverb indicates a preference for one option over others, suggesting that something is desirable or more suitable but not necessarily mandatory. It implies a degree of flexibility. | 例句：I'd like to finish the project by Friday, preferably sooner.  = 我想在星期五之前完成这个项目，最好是更早一些。  | 例句：We should use a natural material, preferably wood. = 我们应该使用天然材料，最好是木材。 | 近义词：ideally, optimally, if possible | 反义词：unpreferably (not a common word, the opposite meaning is usually conveyed through context)
