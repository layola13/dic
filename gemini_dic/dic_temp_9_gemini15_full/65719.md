eventognathi:/ˌɛvɛntoʊˈnæθaɪ/| n. |颚面畸形|A congenital malformation characterized by the absence of the mandible (lower jaw) and/or maxilla (upper jaw).  It's a rare and severe birth defect.| 例句：The infant was diagnosed with eventognathi at birth, requiring immediate surgical intervention. = 婴儿出生时被诊断出患有颚面畸形，需要立即进行手术干预。|近义词：agnathia, micrognathia (if partial)|反义词：None (it describes a deficiency)
