prebronze:/ˈpriːbrɒnz/| adj.| 前青铜时代 |  Relating to the period before the Bronze Age, typically characterized by the use of copper and advanced stone tools.  This term is primarily used in archaeology and refers to a transitional period between the Neolithic and the Bronze Age. |  The prebronze artifacts found at the site suggest a long history of human settlement in the region. 前青铜时代在该遗址发现的人工制品表明该地区人类定居的历史悠久。| 近义词：prehistoric, Neolithic | 反义词：Bronze Age
