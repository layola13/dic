gyrfalcon:/ˈdʒɜːrfɔːlkən/| n. |矛隼|A large, white falcon, found in arctic regions, known for its speed and hunting prowess.  It is the largest falcon species.|The gyrfalcon is a magnificent bird of prey, capable of reaching speeds of over 200 mph during a stoop.|近义词：falcon, bird of prey|反义词：None specifically, but smaller falcons could be considered opposites in terms of size.
