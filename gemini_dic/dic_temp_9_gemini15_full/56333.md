dogmatizer:/ˈdɒɡmətaɪzər/ | n. | 教条主义者，武断者 | One who dogmatizes; one who asserts opinions as if they were dogmas, without regard to evidence or argument.  A person who is dogmatic; a person who insists on their own opinions without considering other viewpoints. | Example: He's a dogmatizer who refuses to listen to any opposing views. 他是一个教条主义者，拒绝听取任何反对意见。 |近义词：dogmatist, authoritarian, dictator, absolutist | 反义词：open-minded person, skeptic, relativist
