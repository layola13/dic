nonpaying: /ˌnɒnˈpeɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 不付钱的，欠费的 | Not paying; failing to pay.  |例句：The nonpaying tenant was finally evicted.  =  那个不付房租的房客最终被赶了出去。

The company is struggling with a high percentage of nonpaying customers. = 这家公司正在努力应对高比例的欠费客户。


近义词：unpaid, delinquent, owing, in arrears | 反义词：paying, solvent

词源:
The word "nonpaying" is a compound word formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the present participle "paying" (derived from the verb "to pay").  It indicates a state of not engaging in the act of payment.
