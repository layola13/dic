lanyard:/ˈlænjərd/| n. |系绳；挂绳；拉带|A lanyard is a cord, string, or strap worn around the neck or wrist to hold an object, such as keys, an ID card, or a whistle.  It is often made of material such as nylon or woven fabric. |Example:  I keep my keys on a lanyard so I don't lose them. 我把钥匙挂在系绳上，这样就不会丢了。  She wore a lanyard with her company ID. 她戴着印有公司ID的挂绳。|近义词：cord, strap, tether|反义词：无
