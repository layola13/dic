firefighting:/ˈfaɪərˌfaɪtɪŋ/| n.|消防，灭火工作|The act of extinguishing fires.  It involves the use of specialized equipment and techniques to control and suppress fires.|例句：The firefighting team worked tirelessly to contain the blaze. 消防队夜以继日地控制大火。

例句：Effective firefighting requires proper training and coordination.有效的灭火需要适当的训练和协调。|近义词：fire suppression, fire control|反义词：arson, fire starting|名词
