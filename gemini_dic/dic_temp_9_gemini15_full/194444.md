self-renewing: /ˌsɛlf rɪˈnuːɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我更新的，自我复制的 | Able to regenerate or replace itself.  This describes a system, process, or organism that can repair and replace its own components or parts, maintaining its structure and function over time.  |例句：Stem cells have the remarkable ability to be self-renewing, constantly producing new cells.  干细胞具有自我更新的非凡能力，不断产生新的细胞。


The self-renewing nature of the rainforest allows it to recover from disturbances. 雨林的自我更新特性使其能够从干扰中恢复过来。


近义词：regenerative, self-replicating, self-sustaining | 反义词：degenerative, depleting
