cubism:/ˈkjuːbɪzəm/| n. |立体主义|A style of painting and sculpture developed in the early 20th century, characterized by the representation of natural forms by means of geometric shapes, especially cubes.|例句：Picasso was a pioneer of Cubism.毕加索是立体主义的先驱。|近义词：abstraction, modernism|反义词：realism, representational art
