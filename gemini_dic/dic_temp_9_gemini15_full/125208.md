multicoated:/ˌmʌltiˈkoʊtɪd/ | adj. | 多层镀膜的 | Having multiple layers of coating, typically on a lens or other optical surface to reduce reflection and improve transmission.  This enhances image quality by minimizing glare and improving contrast. | Example:  My new camera lens is multicoated to reduce flare and ghosting. = 我的新相机镜头是多层镀膜的，以减少眩光和鬼影。  The multicoated eyeglasses reduced glare significantly. = 多层镀膜眼镜显著减少了眩光。| 近义词：coated, layered | 反义词：uncoated, single-coated
