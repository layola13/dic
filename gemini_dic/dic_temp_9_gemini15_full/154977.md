peta:/ˈpɛtə/| n.|拍它(数量词，10的15次方)|A peta is a unit prefix in the metric system denoting a factor of 10<sup>15</sup> (a quadrillion or one thousand trillion).  It is used to express very large quantities.|Example: A petabyte is equal to 10<sup>15</sup> bytes.  一个拍字节等于10<sup>15</sup>字节。|近义词:None (unique prefix)|反义词:None (no opposite prefix)

USAGE->
This word is a unit prefix and doesn't have verb conjugations or typical grammatical usage like a regular word.  Its usage is limited to expressing large quantities.


PHRASE->
petabyte (PB):  拍字节
petaflop: 拍浮点运算
petameter: 拍米
petawatt: 拍瓦特
petaherz: 拍赫兹
