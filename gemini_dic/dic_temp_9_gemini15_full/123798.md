moper:/ˈmoʊpər/ | n. | 闷闷不乐的人，意志消沉的人 | A moper is someone who is habitually gloomy or depressed. They often withdraw socially and exhibit a lack of energy or enthusiasm.  | Example: He's become a real moper since losing his job.  自从失去工作后，他变得非常消沉。|近义词：gloomer, pessimist, downer | 反义词：optimist, enthusiast | 名词
