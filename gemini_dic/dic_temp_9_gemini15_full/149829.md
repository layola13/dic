paleontologists:/ˌpeɪliɒnˈtɒlədʒɪsts/|/n.|古生物学家|Scientists who study fossils to learn about ancient life.  They analyze fossils to understand the evolution, behavior, and environment of extinct organisms.|例句：Paleontologists have discovered a new species of dinosaur in the Gobi Desert.  古生物学家在戈壁沙漠发现了一种新的恐龙物种。|例句：The paleontologists carefully excavated the fossilized bones. 古生物学家仔细挖掘了化石骨骼。|近义词：fossil experts, paleozoologists (if focusing on animals), paleobotanists (if focusing on plants)|反义词：N/A
