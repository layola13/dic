self-dedications:/ˌsɛlfˌdɛdɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n.|自我奉献；献身精神 |The act of dedicating oneself to a cause, purpose, or person.  It implies a strong commitment and often involves significant sacrifice.|例句：Her self-dedications to her work are admirable.  她的工作奉献精神令人钦佩。

例句：His years of self-dedications to environmental protection have made a significant impact. 他多年来致力于环境保护，取得了显著成效。|近义词：devotion, commitment, dedication, sacrifice |反义词：selfishness, self-interest, apathy|名词
