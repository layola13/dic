fruited:/ˈfruːtɪd/| adj.|多果实的；结果实的|Bearing or producing fruit; abounding in fruit.|例句：The fruited plain stretched before us.  果实累累的平原展现在我们面前。|近义词：fruitful, productive|反义词：barren, sterile|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The orchard is fruited this year. = 今年果园果实累累。
This tree is fruited heavily. = 这棵树果实累累。


一般过去时:
The trees fruited well last summer. = 去年夏天树木果实丰收。
The vineyard fruited abundantly last season. = 上个季节葡萄园果实丰收。


现在完成时:
The apple trees have fruited exceptionally well this year. = 苹果树今年结果特别好。
The farmer has fruited his land for many years.  = 这个农民多年来一直在耕种他的土地并使其结果实。


过去完成时:
The trees had fruited before the frost arrived. = 霜冻来临之前树木已经结果了。
The orchard had fruited poorly the previous year. = 果园前一年收成不好。


将来时:
The trees will fruit well next year, if the weather is good. = 如果天气好，明年树木会结果良好。
We expect the trees to fruit abundantly next spring. = 我们预计明年春天树木会大量结果。


虚拟语气:
If the trees fruited more, we would have more apples. = 如果树木结果更多，我们就会有更多苹果。
I wish the trees fruited better this year. = 我希望今年树木结果更好。


被动语态:
The land was fruited by the diligent farmer. = 这片土地被勤劳的农民耕种并使其结果。
The orchard was fruited heavily last autumn. = 去年秋天果园果实累累。


疑问句:
Does this tree fruit every year? = 这棵树每年都结果吗？
Have the apple trees fruited yet? = 苹果树结果了吗？


否定句:
The trees did not fruit well this year. = 今年树木结果不好。
The land hasn't fruited well in recent years. = 近年来这块土地收成不好。


PHRASE->
