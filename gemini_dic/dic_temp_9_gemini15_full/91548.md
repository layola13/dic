hyper-excited: /ˌhaɪpərɪkˈsaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 极度兴奋的，过度兴奋的 | Extremely excited; overly enthusiastic. |例句：The children were hyper-excited about the upcoming trip to the zoo. 孩子们对即将到来的动物园之旅兴奋不已。

The team was hyper-excited after winning the championship.  球队在赢得冠军后非常兴奋。|近义词：overexcited, exhilarated, thrilled, ecstatic |反义词：calm, apathetic, indifferent, lethargic | 词性:形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is hyper-excited about his new job. = 他对他的新工作非常兴奋。
They are hyper-excited to see their favorite band. = 他们非常兴奋地去看他们最喜欢的乐队。


一般过去时:
She was hyper-excited after receiving the good news. = 她收到好消息后非常兴奋。
We were hyper-excited about the surprise party. = 我们对惊喜派对非常兴奋。


现在完成时:

I have been hyper-excited ever since I heard the announcement. = 从我听到宣布的那一刻起，我就一直非常兴奋。


过去完成时:

She had been hyper-excited about the possibility of winning. = 她一直对赢得比赛的可能性感到非常兴奋。
