parietes:/pəˈraɪɪtiːz/| n.|壁，侧壁，(器官的)壁 |The word "parietes" refers to the walls or boundaries of a cavity or container, especially in anatomy and architecture.  It often describes the walls of a body cavity, such as the chest or abdomen. |例句：The parietes of the abdomen protect the internal organs. 腹壁保护着内脏器官。

The parietes of the thorax enclose the lungs and heart. 胸壁包围着肺和心脏。

|近义词：walls, sides, boundaries|反义词：interior, center|名词
