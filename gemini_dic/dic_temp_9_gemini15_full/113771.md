macropus:/ˈmækrəpʌs/| n.|大袋鼠属|Macropus is a genus of marsupials in the family Macropodidae, commonly known as kangaroos.  The genus includes a variety of species, ranging in size and appearance.  They are characterized by their large hind legs, powerful tails, and relatively small forelimbs. |The red kangaroo (Macropus rufus) is the largest extant marsupial.|近义词：kangaroo (泛指)|反义词：无
