participates:/pɑːrˈtɪsɪpeɪts/|/pɑːrˈtɪsɪpeɪts/|v.|参与，参加|To take part in something.|The students actively participates in class discussions. 学生积极参与课堂讨论。|He participates in the marathon every year. 他每年都参加马拉松比赛。|joins, takes part in, engages in|withdraws, refrains from, abstains from|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He participates in many community events. 他参与许多社区活动。
She participates in the school's debate team. 她参加学校的辩论队。

一般过去时:
They participated in a fundraising walk last weekend. 他们上周末参加了一次筹款步行活动。
He participated in the science fair. 他参加了科学展览会。

现在完成时:
She has participated in several international conferences. 她已经参加过几次国际会议。
He has participated in volunteer work for many years. 他多年来一直参与志愿者工作。

过去完成时:
Before the project started, she had participated in similar initiatives. 在项目启动之前，她已经参与过类似的倡议。
They had participated in the planning stage before the actual construction began. 在实际施工开始之前，他们已经参与了规划阶段。

将来时:
He will participate in the upcoming chess tournament. 他将参加即将到来的象棋锦标赛。
She will participate in the online meeting tomorrow. 她明天将参加在线会议。

虚拟语气:
If he participated, the outcome might have been different. 如果他参与了，结果可能会有所不同。
I wish I had participated in the trip. 我希望我参加了那次旅行。

被动语态:
Many people were participated in the survey. 许多人参与了调查。(注意：participate本身不常用于被动语态，此句略显不自然，更自然的表达是：Many people participated in the survey.)
The project was completed with the participation of many volunteers.  该项目在许多志愿者的参与下完成。

疑问句:
Will you participate in the project? 你会参与这个项目吗？
Did she participate in the competition? 她参加了比赛吗？

否定句:
He did not participate in the meeting. 他没有参加会议。
She chose not to participate in the discussion. 她选择不参与讨论。


PHRASE->
participate in = 参与...
fully participate = 全力参与
actively participate = 积极参与
participate actively in = 积极参与...
participate enthusiastically = 热情参与
