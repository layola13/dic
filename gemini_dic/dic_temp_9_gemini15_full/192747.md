seafarer:/ˈsiːˌfɛərər/| n. |海员，水手 | A person who travels by sea, especially as a member of a ship's crew.  |Example:  Many seafarers were lost at sea during the storm. 许多海员在暴风雨中葬身大海。  |近义词：sailor, mariner, seaman |反义词：landlubber
