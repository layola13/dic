pedophile:/ˈpedəfaɪl/| n. |恋童癖者|A person who is sexually attracted to prepubescent children.  This term is often used to describe individuals who have acted on their attractions, but it can also refer to those who experience these attractions without acting on them. However, it's crucial to understand that the mere existence of such attractions is considered a serious problem requiring intervention.|例句：He was arrested and charged as a pedophile after numerous victims came forward. 他在许多受害者站出来后被逮捕并指控为恋童癖者。|近义词：child molester, child abuser|反义词：N/A
