penalized:/ˈpiːnəlaɪzd/ | v. |处罚；惩罚 | To subject to a penalty or punishment.  |例句：He was penalized for his late submission. 他因为提交晚了而受到处罚。 |例句：The team was penalized five yards for holding.  这个队因为抱人犯规被罚五码。|近义词：punished, sanctioned, fined |反义词：rewarded, commended


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team is penalized for every foul. =  球队因每次犯规而受罚。
They penalize late submissions. = 他们会惩罚迟交的作业。


一般过去时:
The driver was penalized for speeding. = 司机因超速而受罚。
She was penalized for breaking the rules. = 她因为违反规定而受到惩罚。


现在完成时:
The player has been penalized several times this season. = 这个球员本赛季已经被罚了好几次了。
He has been penalized for his misconduct. = 他因行为不端而受到处罚。


过去完成时:
The athlete had been penalized before the final race. = 运动员在决赛前就已经受罚了。
They had been penalized for their previous infringements. = 他们因为之前的违规行为而受到了惩罚。


将来时:
The company will be penalized if they violate the law. = 如果公司违反法律，将会受到处罚。
He will be penalized for his actions. = 他将为他的行为付出代价。


虚拟语气:
If he had followed the rules, he wouldn't have been penalized. = 如果他遵守规则，就不会受到处罚。
I wish I hadn't been penalized for that mistake. = 我真希望我没有因为那个错误而受到惩罚。


被动语态:
The project was penalized due to the delay. = 项目因延误而受到惩罚。
The team was penalized for their unsportsmanlike conduct. = 球队因不公平竞争行为而受到处罚。


疑问句:
Was he penalized for the incident? = 他因为那件事受罚了吗？
Will they be penalized for the breach of contract? = 他们会因为违反合同而受到处罚吗？


否定句:
He wasn't penalized for his minor mistake. = 他因为那个小错误没有受到惩罚。
The company wasn't penalized this time. = 公司这次没有受到处罚。


PHRASE->
be penalized for = 因为…而受罚
heavily penalized = 受到严厉处罚
severely penalized = 受到严重处罚
penalized points = 罚分
penalized by = 被…处罚
