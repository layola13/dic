polemist:/ˈpɒləmɪst/ /ˈpɑːləmɪst/ | n. |论战者；辩论家 |A polemicist is a person skilled in or given to polemics; a controversialist.  They are often involved in debates and arguments,  presenting their views forcefully and sometimes aggressively. | Example:  The renowned polemicist delivered a scathing critique of the government's policies.  这位著名的论战者对政府的政策进行了严厉的批评。|近义词：debater, controversialist, arguer, disputant |反义词：peacemaker, conciliator
