replies:/rɪˈplaɪz/ | n. | 回复，回答 | 复数名词，指对问题、请求或评论的回应。  | 例句：The replies to my email were all positive.  我的邮件回复都是积极的。 | 例句：She received numerous replies to her advertisement. 她收到很多对她的广告的回复。 | 近义词：answers, responses, retorts | 反义词：questions, inquiries


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He replies to emails promptly. = 他及时回复邮件。
They reply with a smile. = 他们笑着回应。

一般过去时:
She replied to my letter yesterday. = 她昨天回复了我的信。
He replied that he couldn't come. = 他回复说他不能来。

现在完成时:
I have replied to all your questions. = 我已经回答了你的所有问题。
They have not yet replied to our request. = 他们还没有回复我们的请求。

过去完成时:
He had already replied before I called. = 我打电话之前他已经回复了。
She had replied angrily to his accusations. = 她愤怒地回应了他的指责。

将来时:
I will reply to your email tomorrow. = 我明天会回复你的邮件。
He will reply as soon as possible. = 他会尽快回复。

虚拟语气:
If he replied honestly, we would believe him. = 如果他诚实地回答，我们会相信他。
I wish he had replied sooner. = 我希望他早点回复。

被动语态:
The question was replied to immediately. = 这个问题立即得到了回复。
All emails will be replied to within 24 hours. = 所有邮件将在24小时内得到回复。

疑问句:
Did he reply to your message? = 他回复你的信息了吗？
How did she reply to the criticism? = 她如何回应批评？

否定句:
He didn't reply to my email. = 他没有回复我的邮件。
She hasn't replied yet. = 她还没回复。


PHRASE->
in reply to = 作为对……的回应
reply in kind = 以牙还牙
reply to = 回复
make a reply = 作出回应
a quick reply = 快速回复
