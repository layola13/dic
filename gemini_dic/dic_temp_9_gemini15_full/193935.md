self-enhancement:/ˌsɛlfɪnˈhɑːnsmənt/| n. |自我提升，自我完善|The act of improving or enhancing one's self-image, abilities, or status.  It often involves behaviors designed to make oneself feel better or more competent.|例句：He engaged in self-enhancement activities like taking up a new hobby and joining a gym. 他从事自我提升活动，例如学习新的爱好和加入健身房。
例句：Her constant boasting is a clear sign of self-enhancement. 她不断的吹嘘是自我提升的明显迹象。|近义词：self-improvement, self-development, betterment|反义词：self-deprecation, self-abasement|名词
