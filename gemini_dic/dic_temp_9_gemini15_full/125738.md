muntjac:/ˈmʌntʃæk/| n. |麂; 吠鹿|A muntjac is a small deer with short antlers and prominent canine teeth.  They are found in Asia.|例句：The researchers observed a group of Reeves' muntjacs in the forest. 研究人员在森林里观察到一群赤麂。|例句：The barking deer, also known as the muntjac, is known for its distinctive call.  吠鹿，也叫麂，以其独特的叫声而闻名。|近义词：barking deer, Reeve's muntjac|反义词:|无
