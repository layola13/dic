simpled:/ˈsɪmpəld/| adj.|简化的；简单的；单纯的|Simplified means made less complex or complicated; reduced to its essential elements.  It often implies a process of making something easier to understand or use. |例句：The instructions were simplified for beginners.  (说明书为初学者简化了。)

例句：He offered a simplified explanation of the complex theory. (他提供了一个对复杂理论的简化解释。)


近义词：simplified, basic, elementary, uncomplicated

反义词：complex, complicated, intricate, elaborate

词性：形容词
