condyloid:/ˈkɒndɪlɔɪd/|/ˈkɑːndɪlɔɪd/| adj.|髁状的；枕状的| Relating to or resembling a condyle (a rounded articular projection on a bone).  A condyloid joint is a type of synovial joint that allows movement in two planes (biaxial).| The condyloid joint of the jaw allows for up-and-down and side-to-side movement.|近义词：articular,  knobbed|反义词：planar, flat
