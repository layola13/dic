jaunted:/ˈdʒɔːntɪd/ | v. |短途旅行；轻松愉快地旅行 | to make a short journey, especially for pleasure | They jaunted off to the coast for the weekend.  他们周末轻松地去了海边旅行。|近义词：excursioned, tripped, journeyed | 反义词：stayed, remained


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He jauntedly jaunts around the city every Sunday. = 他每个星期天都兴高采烈地在城里四处闲逛。
We often jaunt to the nearby park after work. = 我们下班后经常去附近的公园散步。

一般过去时:
She jaunted through the countryside on her bicycle. = 她骑着自行车在乡间轻松地旅行。
They jaunted to the seaside for a day trip. = 他们去海边进行了一日游。

现在完成时:
We have jaunted to many different places this year. = 今年我们已经去过许多不同的的地方。
They have jaunted all over Europe on their backpacking trip. = 他们在背包旅行中走遍了欧洲各地。

过去完成时:
They had jaunted to the mountains before the snow fell. = 下雪之前他们去过山区。
We had jaunted across the country before settling down. = 我们在定居之前横跨了全国各地。

将来时:
We will jaunt to the lake next weekend. = 我们下周末将去湖边。
They will jaunt to Paris for a romantic getaway. = 他们将去巴黎进行浪漫的短途旅行。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would jaunt to the beach. = 如果我有更多时间，我会去海滩。
I wish I could jaunt to Italy next year. = 我希望明年能去意大利。

被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice because it implies an active, deliberate action.)


疑问句:
Did they jaunt to the museum yesterday? = 他们昨天去博物馆了吗？
Will you jaunt to the mountains with us? = 你会和我们一起去山里吗？

否定句:
They didn't jaunt to the party last night. = 他们昨晚没去参加派对。
We will not jaunt anywhere this weekend. = 这个周末我们哪儿也不去。
