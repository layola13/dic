cicero:/ˈsɪsərəʊ/| n. |西塞罗；雄辩家|Marcus Tullius Cicero (106-43 BC), a Roman statesman, lawyer, writer, and philosopher.  He is considered one of the greatest orators and prose stylists of Roman antiquity. His writings had a profound influence on the development of Latin prose and Western rhetoric. |Example: Cicero's speeches are still studied today for their rhetorical skill.  西塞罗的演讲至今仍因其修辞技巧而被研究。|近义词：orator, statesman, writer|反义词：N/A|名词
