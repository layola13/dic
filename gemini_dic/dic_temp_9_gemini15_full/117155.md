meaningless:/ˈmiːnɪŋləs/| adj.|毫无意义的，无目的的|lacking meaning, purpose, or significance.|The movie was utterly meaningless and a waste of time. 这部电影完全毫无意义，浪费时间。|近义词：pointless, senseless, futile|反义词：meaningful, purposeful, significant

USAGE->
一般现在时:
This conversation is meaningless.  = 这段对话毫无意义。
His actions seem meaningless to me. = 他的行为在我看来毫无意义。

一般过去时:
Her efforts proved meaningless in the end. = 她的努力最终证明是毫无意义的。
The meeting was largely meaningless. = 会议大多毫无意义。

现在完成时:
All his hard work has been meaningless. = 他所有的努力都白费了。
My attempts to help have proved meaningless. = 我试图帮忙的尝试已经证明是徒劳的。

过去完成时:
I realized my previous efforts had been meaningless. = 我意识到我之前的努力毫无意义。

将来时:

Further protests will be meaningless without a leader. = 没有领导人的进一步抗议将毫无意义。
