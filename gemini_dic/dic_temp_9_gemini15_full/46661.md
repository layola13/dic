damon:/ˈdeɪmən/ | n. |达蒙（男子名） |Damon is a masculine given name of Greek origin.  It is a variation of the name Damion.  The name is associated with the mythical figure Damon, known for his loyalty and friendship with Pythias.  |My friend Damon is a talented musician.  我的朋友达蒙是一位才华横溢的音乐家。|近义词：Damian,Damien|反义词：无
