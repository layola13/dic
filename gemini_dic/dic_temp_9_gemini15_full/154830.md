perus: /ˈpɛrʊs/ | v. | 仔细检查；细读；审视 | To examine or consider with great care.  |例句：I perussed the contract before signing it. 我在签合同之前仔细阅读了它。|近义词：examine, scrutinize, study, review |反义词：skim, glance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He peruses the newspaper every morning. = 他每天早上仔细阅读报纸。
She peruses the documents for any errors. = 她仔细检查文件是否有任何错误。

一般过去时:
I perused the book for hours. = 我仔细阅读了这本书好几个小时。
They perused the map before starting their journey. = 他们出发前仔细研究了地图。

现在完成时:
He has perused all the relevant documents. = 他已经仔细阅读了所有相关的文件。
She has perused the report and found several inconsistencies. = 她仔细检查了报告，发现了好几个不一致的地方。

过去完成时:
I had perused the manuscript before submitting it. = 我在提交手稿之前已经仔细阅读过它了。
They had perused the evidence before reaching a verdict. = 他们在做出判决之前已经仔细审查过证据了。
