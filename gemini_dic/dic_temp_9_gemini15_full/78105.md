geolocation:/ˌdʒioʊˌloʊˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |地理位置定位，地理位置信息|The determination of the precise location of a person or thing on Earth.  This often involves using GPS, IP address, or other technologies. |例句：The app uses geolocation to show you nearby restaurants. 这个应用程序使用地理位置定位来显示你附近的餐馆。

例句：Our geolocation data indicates you are currently in London. 我们的地理位置数据表明您目前在伦敦。


近义词：geotagging, location tracking, positioning |反义词：None (lack of geolocation is the antonym concept)|词性：名词
