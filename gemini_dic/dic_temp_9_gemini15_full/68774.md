/ˈfɛri/ | n. |渡船；摆渡；渡口 | A ferry is a boat or ship used to carry passengers and sometimes vehicles across a body of water, especially a shorter distance.  | The ferry to the island runs every hour. 这艘去岛上的渡船每小时一班。  |  boat, ship, barge |  airplane, train (for long distances) | 名词

The ferry runs every hour. = 渡船每小时运行一次。
Ferries transport many people daily. = 渡轮每天运送很多人。
