inorganized: /ˌɪnˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/ | adj. | 无组织的，杂乱无章的 | Lacking organization or order; chaotic.  | The inorganized files made it difficult to find the necessary documents.  无组织的文件使得查找必要的文档变得困难。 | organized, orderly, systematic | organized, orderly, systematic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The room is inorganized. = 这个房间很杂乱。
His thoughts are inorganized. = 他的思想很混乱。

一般过去时:
The project was inorganized from the start. = 这个项目从一开始就组织得很混乱。
The presentation was inorganized and unclear. = 演示文稿组织混乱且不清楚。


现在完成时:
The team has been inorganized throughout the project. = 团队在整个项目中都缺乏组织性。
Everything has become inorganized since the move. = 自从搬家后，一切都变得杂乱无章了。


过去完成时:
The meeting had been inorganized, leading to wasted time. = 会议组织混乱，导致浪费时间。
They realized the plan had been inorganized after it failed. = 计划失败后，他们意识到计划的组织性很差。


将来时:

The exhibition will likely be inorganized without more volunteers. = 没有更多的志愿者，展览很可能会组织混乱。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:
