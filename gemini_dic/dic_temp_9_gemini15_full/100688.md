intracapsular:/ˌɪntrəˈkæpsjʊlər/| adj.|囊内(的);关节囊内的|Within a capsule or joint capsule.  The term is often used in medicine to describe something occurring within the joint capsule, particularly in the context of hip or shoulder fractures.|例句：The fracture was classified as intracapsular, meaning it occurred within the hip joint capsule.  该骨折被归类为囊内骨折，这意味着它发生在髋关节囊内。|例句：Intracapsular hip fractures often require surgical intervention. 关节囊内髋部骨折通常需要手术干预。|近义词：intra-articular|反义词：extracapsular
