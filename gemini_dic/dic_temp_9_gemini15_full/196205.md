sequestering: /sɪˈkwɛstərɪŋ/ | v. |隔离；扣押；查封；(尤指二氧化碳)封存| The act of isolating, confining, or setting apart;  especially the removal or storage of something, such as carbon dioxide, to prevent its release into the atmosphere. |例句：The company is sequestering funds to cover potential lawsuits. 这家公司正在隔离资金以支付潜在的诉讼费用。|例句：Scientists are exploring methods of sequestering carbon dioxide to mitigate climate change. 科学家们正在探索封存二氧化碳以减轻气候变化的方法。|近义词：confining, isolating, impounding, sequestrating|反义词：releasing, liberating, freeing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They sequester funds for emergencies. = 他们为紧急情况隔离资金。
The government sequesters land for conservation. = 政府为保护而扣押土地。


一般过去时:
The court sequestered the assets. = 法院查封了资产。
The scientists sequestered the samples. = 科学家们隔离了样本。


现在完成时:
They have sequestered enough money for the project. = 他们已经为该项目隔离了足够的资金。
The company has sequestered all its profits this year. = 公司今年已经封存了所有利润。


过去完成时:
They had sequestered the evidence before the trial. = 他们在审判前就已隔离了证据。
The forest had sequestered a significant amount of carbon. = 这片森林已经封存了大量的碳。


将来时:
They will sequester the witnesses. = 他们将隔离证人。
The city will sequester the contaminated soil. = 该城市将隔离受污染的土壤。


虚拟语气:
If they sequestered the funds, they could avoid bankruptcy. = 如果他们隔离资金，他们就可以避免破产。
I wish they had sequestered the dangerous chemicals. = 我希望他们已经隔离了危险的化学品。


被动语态:
The assets were sequestered by the court. = 资产被法院查封了。
Carbon dioxide is being sequestered by plants. = 二氧化碳正在被植物封存。


疑问句:
Were the documents sequestered? = 文件被隔离了吗？
Is the land being sequestered for development? = 这块土地是否被隔离用于开发？


否定句:
They did not sequester the funds. = 他们没有隔离资金。
The government did not sequester the property. = 政府没有查封该房产。
