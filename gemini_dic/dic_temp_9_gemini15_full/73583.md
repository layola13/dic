foretaste:/ˈfɔːrteɪst/|/ˈfɔːrˌteɪst/| n.| 预尝；预兆；先验|An anticipatory experience or taste of something to come; a premonition or foreshadowing.|例句：The small sample was a foretaste of the delicious meal to come.  这小样品预示了即将到来美味佳肴。

例句：The successful pilot project gave them a foretaste of what they could achieve.  成功的试点项目让他们预尝到了他们能够取得的成就。


近义词：preview, anticipation, premonition, harbinger


反义词：aftertaste, consequence


词性：名词
