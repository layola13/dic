oversureness's: /ˌoʊvərˈʃʊrnəsɪz/ | n. | 过分自信的；自负的 | The state of being excessively confident; overconfidence. 词源:

The word is formed by combining "over" (meaning excessive or too much) with "sureness" (the state of being sure or confident).  "Sureness" itself derives from the adjective "sure," ultimately from Old French "seur" and Latin "secūrus."
