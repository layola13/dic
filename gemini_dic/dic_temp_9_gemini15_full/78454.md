gets:/ɡɛts/ | v. | 得到；获得；理解；抓住 | To receive or obtain something. To understand or grasp something. |例句：He gets a lot of mail. 他收到很多邮件。|例句：I don't get it. 我不明白。|近义词：receives, obtains, understands, grasps |反义词：loses, misses, fails to understand


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gets a new car every year. = 他每年都买一辆新车。
She gets up early every morning. = 她每天早上起得很早。

一般过去时:
I got a letter from my friend yesterday. = 昨天我收到朋友的一封信。
They got lost in the forest. = 他们在森林里迷路了。

现在完成时:
She has gotten much better at playing the piano. = 她弹钢琴技术进步了很多。
He has gotten a new job. = 他找到了一份新工作。

过去完成时:
Before I left, I had gotten everything ready. = 我离开之前，我已经把一切都准备好了。
They had gotten married before they moved to the city. = 他们在搬到城市之前就已经结婚了。

将来时:
I will get the report to you tomorrow. = 我明天把报告给你。
She will get a promotion soon. = 她很快就会升职。

虚拟语气:
If I got a million dollars, I would travel the world. = 如果我得到一百万美元，我会周游世界。
I wish I got better grades. = 我希望我的成绩更好。

被动语态:
The problem was gotten solved quickly. = 问题很快得到了解决。
The message was gotten across successfully. = 信息成功地传达出去了。

疑问句:
Does he get paid weekly? = 他每周领工资吗？
What does she get for her birthday? = 她生日得到什么礼物？

否定句:
He doesn't get enough sleep. = 他睡眠不足。
I didn't get the joke. = 我没明白那个笑话。
