fathom:/ˈfæðəm/| n. v.| 1. 寻; 理解; 2. 长度单位（六英尺）| 1. A fathom is a unit of length equal to six feet (approximately 1.8 meters).  It was originally used to measure water depth by stretching out one's arms.  2. To fathom something means to understand it, especially something difficult or mysterious. |例句：The diver used a rope marked in fathoms to measure the depth.  潜水员用标有英寻的绳子测量深度。

The mystery of the disappearance remained unfathomed for years.  失踪的谜团多年来一直未解。

近义词：comprehend, understand, grasp, plumb| 反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret| 词性:n. v.
