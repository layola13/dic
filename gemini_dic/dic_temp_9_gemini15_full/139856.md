octroi:/ˈɒktrɔɪ/ | n. | 关税；税捐；特许 | A tax or duty levied on goods entering a town or district.  Historically, it often referred to a tax imposed at the gates of a city. |  The octroi on imported goods significantly increased their price.  进口商品的关税大大提高了它们的价格。|  tax, duty, toll, impost |  subsidy, exemption  | 名词
