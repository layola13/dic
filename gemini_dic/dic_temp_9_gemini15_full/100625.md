intinction:/ɪnˈtɪŋkʃən/ | n. | 浸渍；蘸；浸染 | The act of dipping something into a liquid, especially for religious purposes (e.g., dipping bread into wine during communion).  | Example: The intinction of the bread in the wine is a central part of the Mass.  浸面包于圣酒是弥撒仪式的核心部分。|  immersion, dipping |  (暂无明确反义词，可考虑dryness, absence of immersion) | 名词
