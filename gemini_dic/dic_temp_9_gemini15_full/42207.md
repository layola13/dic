councilmen:/ˈkaʊnsəlmən/| n.|市议员；委员会成员|A councilman is a member of a council, especially a municipal council.|例句：The councilmen voted to approve the new budget. 市议员投票批准了新的预算。|例句：The city councilmen are responsible for making decisions about local issues. 市议员负责就当地问题做出决策。|近义词：councilor, alderman|反义词：citizen (in the context of not being a member of the council)
