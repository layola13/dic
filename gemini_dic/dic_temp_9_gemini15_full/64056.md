epitomises:/ɪˈpɪtəmaɪzɪz/| v. | 作为…的典型; 完美地体现; 充分代表 | To be a perfect example of something; to embody or represent something in a concentrated or typical form. | His work epitomises the spirit of the age.他的作品完美地体现了那个时代的精神。| exemplifies, embodies, represents, symbolizes | differs from, contrasts with | 动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
This painting epitomises the artist's style. = 这幅画是这位艺术家风格的典型代表。
Her success epitomises hard work and dedication. = 她的成功是勤奋和奉献的完美体现。

一般过去时:
The building epitomised Victorian architecture. = 这座建筑是维多利亚时期建筑的典范。
His actions epitomised the company's values. = 他的行为是公司价值观的完美体现。
