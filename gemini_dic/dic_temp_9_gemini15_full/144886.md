over-rule:/ˌoʊvərˈruːl/| vt.|否决；推翻；凌驾于…之上|To reject or override a decision, ruling, or opinion.  It implies a higher authority rejecting a lower one.|The judge overruled the objection.  法官否决了异议。|The appeal court overruled the lower court's decision. 上诉法院推翻了下级法院的判决。|sustain, supersede|uphold, approve|
