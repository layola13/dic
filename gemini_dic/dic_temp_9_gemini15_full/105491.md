kiln:/kɪln/| n. |窑，焙炉|A kiln is a thermally insulated chamber, often made of brick or clay, used for firing pottery, bricks, or other ceramic materials at high temperatures.  It provides a controlled environment for the heat treatment of materials, facilitating chemical and physical changes. |例句：The potter carefully loaded the kiln with his latest creations. 陶工小心翼翼地将他的最新作品装进窑里。

The ancient Egyptians used kilns to fire their pottery. 古埃及人用窑来烧制陶器。|近义词：oven, furnace|反义词：N/A
