memorize:/ˈmeməraɪz/| vt.|记住，熟记|To commit to memory; learn by heart.|例句：I need to memorize this poem for tomorrow's class. 我需要为明天的课背诵这首诗。|例句：She memorized all the capital cities of Europe. 她记住了所有欧洲国家的首都。|近义词：learn, commit to memory, learn by heart|反义词：forget, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I memorize new vocabulary every day. = 我每天都记住新的词汇。
She memorizes the script before each performance. = 她在每次演出前都会记住剧本。

一般过去时:
I memorized the entire speech last night. = 我昨晚记住了整篇演讲。
He memorized the phone number. = 他记住了电话号码。

现在完成时:
I have memorized all the irregular verbs. = 我已经记住了所有不规则动词。
She has memorized the lines for the play. = 她已经记住了戏剧的台词。

过去完成时:
I had memorized the poem before the recital. = 在朗诵会之前我已经记住了这首诗。
They had memorized the instructions carefully. = 他们已经仔细地记住了指示。

将来时:
I will memorize the formula for the exam. = 我将为考试记住这个公式。
She will memorize the song before the audition. = 她将在试镜前记住这首歌。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would memorize the entire book. = 如果我有更多时间，我会记住整本书。
I wish I could memorize more easily. = 我希望我能更容易地记住东西。

被动语态:
The poem was memorized by all the students. = 这首诗被所有学生记住了。
The lines are memorized by the actors before the show. = 台词在演出前由演员们记住。

疑问句:
Do you memorize things easily? = 你容易记住东西吗？
Can you memorize this long number? = 你能记住这个长号码吗？

否定句:
I can't memorize this complex equation. = 我记不住这个复杂的方程式。
He didn't memorize the lecture notes. = 他没有记住讲课笔记。
