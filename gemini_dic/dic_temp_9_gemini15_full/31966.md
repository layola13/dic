chemise:/ʃəˈmiːz/| n.| 衬衣；女衬衫；睡衣 | A chemise is a woman's undergarment, typically a loose-fitting shirt or smock.  It can also refer to a simple, loose-fitting dress or blouse. |例句：She wore a simple cotton chemise under her dress. 她穿着一件简单的棉质衬衣在裙子里面。

She wore a long, flowing chemise to bed. 她穿着一条飘逸的长睡衣睡觉。|近义词：undershirt, blouse, smock|反义词：None specifically, but terms like "jacket" or "coat" represent contrasting outerwear.
