desaturate:/ˈdiːsætʃəreɪt/| vt.|使褪色；使减饱和度；降低饱和度|To reduce the intensity of a color by adding gray or another neutral color; to remove saturation from.|The photo was desaturated to create a more muted effect. 这张照片经过减饱和度处理，营造出一种更柔和的效果。|近义词：fade, dull, tone down|反义词：saturate
