recluses:/ˈrɛkluːsɪz/| n.|隐士，隐居者|People who live alone and avoid contact with society. They often choose a solitary life to pursue personal interests or spiritual growth, or to escape the pressures of social interaction.|例句：Many hermits are recluses who dedicate their lives to religious practices. 许多隐士是将一生奉献给宗教实践的隐士。|例句：The old woman lived as a recluse, rarely leaving her small cottage.  这位老妇人过着隐居的生活，很少离开她的小屋。|近义词：hermit, loner, solitary|反义词：socialite, extrovert
