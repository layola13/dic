elaborate:/ɪˈlæbəreɪt/ | adj., v. | 精细的；详尽的；精心制作的；详细阐述 |  to explain something in great detail;  to develop or produce something in a detailed and careful way. | 近义词：detailed, complex, intricate, expand on | 反义词：simple, plain, basic


elaborate (adj.):
The elaborate costumes were the highlight of the parade. = 精美的服装是游行的高潮。

The scientist presented an elaborate theory. = 科学家提出了一个复杂的理论。


elaborate (v.):
He elaborated on his reasons for leaving. = 他详细解释了他离开的原因。

She elaborated a plan to improve the company's profits. = 她制定了一个提高公司利润的计划。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He elaborates his points clearly. = 他清楚地阐述了他的观点。
They elaborate on the details of the project. = 他们详细说明了项目的细节。


一般过去时:
She elaborated a detailed proposal. = 她制定了一个详细的提案。
He elaborated his ideas during the meeting. = 他在会议上详细阐述了他的想法。


现在完成时:
They have elaborated a comprehensive strategy. = 他们已经制定了一个全面的战略。
She has elaborated on the subject in several articles. = 她已经在几篇文章中详细阐述了这个主题。


过去完成时:
He had elaborated his argument before the debate. = 他在辩论之前已经详细阐述了他的论点。
They had elaborated a plan to escape. = 他们已经制定了一个逃跑计划。


将来时:
She will elaborate further on the topic tomorrow. = 她明天将进一步详细阐述这个话题。
He will elaborate his design in the next presentation. = 他将在下一次演示中详细阐述他的设计。


虚拟语气:
If he elaborated his ideas more clearly, people would understand better. = 如果他更清楚地阐述他的想法，人们就会更好地理解。
I wish he had elaborated on the consequences. = 我希望他详细说明了后果。


被动语态:
The plan was elaborated by a team of experts. = 这个计划是由一个专家团队制定的。
The details will be elaborated in the following chapters. = 详情将在接下来的章节中详细说明。


疑问句:
Will you elaborate on your proposal? = 你会详细说明你的提案吗？
Can you elaborate on that point? = 你能详细说明那一点吗？


否定句:
He did not elaborate further on the subject. = 他没有进一步详细阐述这个主题。
She refused to elaborate on the details of the incident. = 她拒绝详细说明事件的细节。


PHRASE->
elaborate on = 详细阐述
elaborate design = 精致的设计
elaborate system = 复杂的系统
elaborate plan = 周密的计划
elaborate scheme = 复杂的计划


词源:
From Latin *elaboratus, past participle of elaborare "to work out, perfect, finish," from e- "out" + laborare "to work."
