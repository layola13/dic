civilizes: /ˈsɪvəlaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 使文明；使开化；教化 | To bring (a people, place, etc.) to a state of civilization or culture; to refine; cultivate. | 例句：Education civilizes people and improves their quality of life. 教育使人文明，提高了他们的生活质量。  | 例句：The Roman Empire helped to civilize much of Europe. 罗马帝国帮助开化了欧洲大部分地区。| 近义词：cultivate, refine, enlighten, educate | 反义词：barbarize, brutalize, debase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government civilizes the rural areas through education and infrastructure development. = 政府通过教育和基础设施建设使农村地区文明化。
She civilizes her children by teaching them manners and respect. = 她通过教导孩子们礼貌和尊重来使他们文明。

一般过去时:
The ancient Greeks civilized many parts of the Mediterranean world. = 古希腊人使地中海世界许多地区文明化。
The missionaries attempted to civilize the indigenous population. = 传教士试图使当地居民文明化。

现在完成时:
Education has civilized many societies throughout history. = 教育在历史上使许多社会文明化。
They have civilized the wild lands by building homes and schools. = 他们通过建造房屋和学校使荒地文明化。

过去完成时:
By the time the explorers arrived, the local people had been partially civilized by traders. = 当探险家到达时，当地居民已经被商人部分地文明化了。
Before the arrival of the Europeans, the indigenous population had already civilized to a certain extent. = 在欧洲人到来之前，当地居民已经到一定程度文明化了。

将来时:
The new program will civilize the remote villages by providing access to technology and information. = 新项目将通过提供技术和信息访问来使偏远村庄文明化。
We hope that future generations will civilize the planet further. = 我们希望后代将进一步使地球文明化。

虚拟语气:
If we invested more in education, we could civilize more communities. = 如果我们更多地投资于教育，我们就能使更多社区文明化。
I wish the world were more civilized and less violent. = 我希望世界更加文明，暴力更少。

被动语态:
The remote tribes were civilized by contact with the outside world. = 偏远部落通过与外界的接触而文明化。
The city was civilized through centuries of cultural development. = 这个城市通过几个世纪的文化发展而文明化。

疑问句:
How can we civilize the most impoverished regions of the world? = 我们如何才能使世界上最贫困的地区文明化？
What measures are being taken to civilize the current generation? = 正在采取什么措施来使当代人文明化？

否定句:
The brutal regime did not civilize the people; it oppressed them. = 残暴的政权并没有使人民文明化；它压迫了他们。
Their actions did not civilize the area; they only caused more damage. = 他们的行为并没有使该地区文明化；他们只造成了更大的破坏。
