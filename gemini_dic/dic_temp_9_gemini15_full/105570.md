kind-hearted:/kaɪndˈhɑːtɪd/ | adj. |好心的，仁慈的 |Having or showing kindness and compassion.  Kind-hearted people are generous and empathetic towards others, often showing concern for their well-being. |She is a kind-hearted woman who always helps those in need. 她是一位好心的女人，总是帮助那些需要帮助的人。|近义词：benevolent, compassionate, charitable, merciful |反义词：cruel, heartless, unkind, ruthless


USAGE->

She is always kind-hearted to strangers. 她总是对陌生人很友善。
