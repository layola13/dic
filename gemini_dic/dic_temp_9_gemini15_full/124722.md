mozarabic:/ˌmoʊzəˈræbɪk/| adj.|摩萨拉伯的；摩尔人基督教徒的| Relating to the Mozarabs, the Christians of Muslim Spain who lived under Muslim rule and preserved their own language and culture.  The term also refers to their language, a Romance language with significant Arabic influence. | The Mozarabic liturgy was a distinctive feature of the Visigothic Church in Spain.  摩萨拉伯礼拜仪式是西班牙西哥特教会的一个显著特征。|近义词：Hispano-Arabic, Visigothic (in some contexts)|反义词：none
