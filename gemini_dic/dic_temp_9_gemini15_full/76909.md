ganoidian:/ˈɡænɔɪdiən/| adj.|硬鳞鱼类的；硬鳞的|Of or relating to ganoid scales.  Ganoid scales are rhombic (diamond-shaped), thick, bony scales that are characteristic of certain fish.  They are covered with a hard, enamel-like substance called ganoin.|例句：The fossil shows the ganoidian scales of an extinct fish. = 这化石显示了一种已灭绝鱼类的硬鳞。

The study focused on the phylogenetic relationships of ganoidian fishes. = 这项研究侧重于硬鳞鱼类的系统发育关系。|近义词：rhomboidal (referring to scale shape)|反义词：cycloid (referring to scale type)|形容词
