jitters:/ˈdʒɪtərz/| n.|紧张不安，焦虑|A feeling of nervousness or anxiety, especially before an important event.|例句：She had the jitters before her big presentation. 她在她重要的演示之前感到紧张不安。|例句：He got the jitters before the interview. 他在面试前感到紧张不安。|近义词：anxiety, nervousness, apprehension, unease|反义词：calmness, composure, serenity
