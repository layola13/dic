dogging:/ˈdɒɡɪŋ/ | v. | 纠缠；紧跟；持续不断地做；(尤指)持续不断地批评 | To follow or pursue persistently; to continue doing something persistently, especially criticizing persistently. |例句：The paparazzi were dogging the celebrity's every move.  狗仔队寸步不离地跟踪着这位名人的每一个举动。


例句：He kept dogging her with questions about her past. 他不断地用关于她过去的问题纠缠她。


近义词：tailing, pursuing, harassing, hounding, shadowing


反义词：ignoring, neglecting, abandoning


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dogs her footsteps everywhere she goes. 他走到哪里都紧跟着她。
They are dogging the opposition party with relentless criticism. 他们持续不断地批评反对党。

一般过去时:
The detectives dogged the suspect for days.  侦探们跟踪了嫌疑犯好几天。
She doggedly pursued her dream of becoming a doctor. 她顽强地追求成为一名医生的梦想。(Note: This uses the related adjective "doggedly")

现在完成时:
The media have dogged his career since the scandal broke. 自丑闻爆发以来，媒体一直对他穷追不舍。
They have doggedly defended their position despite the mounting evidence. 尽管证据越来越多，他们还是顽强地捍卫自己的立场。(Note: This uses the related adverb "doggedly")

过去完成时:
The police had dogged the criminal for months before finally apprehending him.  警方在最终逮捕罪犯之前已经跟踪了他几个月。
She had doggedly worked towards her goal for years before finally achieving it. 在最终实现目标之前，她多年来一直顽强地努力。(Note: This uses the related adverb "doggedly")

将来时:
They will dog his every step until they get the information they need.  他们会寸步不离地跟踪他，直到得到他们需要的信息。
The reporters will doggedly pursue the story until they uncover the truth.  记者们会坚持不懈地追查真相，直到揭露真相。


虚拟语气:
If they dogged him more effectively, they might have caught him sooner. 如果他们更有效地跟踪他，他们可能早就抓到他了。
I wish I hadn't dogged him so relentlessly; it caused unnecessary stress. 我真希望我没有这么无情地纠缠他；这造成了不必要的压力。


被动语态:
The celebrity felt dogged by the press constantly.  这位名人感到自己 постоянно受到媒体的纠缠。
His reputation was dogged by rumors of corruption.  他的名声一直受到腐败传闻的困扰。


疑问句:
Is the journalist dogging the politician's movements?  记者是否正在跟踪这位政治家的行踪？
Are they dogging the suspect's every move?  他们是否正在紧追不舍地跟踪嫌疑人的每一个举动？


否定句:
The police did not dog the suspect closely enough.  警方对嫌疑人的跟踪不够紧密。
They didn't dog the story as hard as they should have. 他们没有像他们应该的那样努力追查这个故事。

词源:
The word "dogging" comes from the Old English word "doggan," meaning "to follow persistently like a dog."  This reflects the persistent nature of a dog's pursuit.
