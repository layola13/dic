salute:/səˈluːt/| vt. n.|敬礼；致敬；欢迎；庆祝 |to greet someone in a formal way, especially by raising one's hand to one's head;  to express approval or admiration for someone or something. |例句：The soldiers saluted the flag. 士兵们向国旗敬礼。 The crowd saluted the winning team.  人群向获胜的队伍致敬。|近义词：greet, hail, honor, celebrate|反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He salutes the flag every morning. = 他每天早上都向国旗敬礼。
She salutes her colleagues with a friendly smile. = 她用友好的微笑向同事们致意。

一般过去时:
The soldiers saluted their commanding officer. = 士兵们向他们的指挥官敬礼。
He saluted her with a bow.  = 他鞠躬向她致敬。


现在完成时:
The team has saluted their fans with a victory lap. = 球队以庆祝胜利的一圈绕场跑来向球迷们致敬。
She has saluted the efforts of the volunteers. = 她对志愿者的努力表示敬意。

过去完成时:
He had saluted the general before he spoke. = 他在发言前已向将军敬礼。
They had saluted the fallen soldiers before the ceremony. = 仪式前，他们已向阵亡将士敬礼。

将来时:
The soldiers will salute the new president. = 士兵们将向新总统敬礼。
We will salute their courage and sacrifice. = 我们将向他们的勇气和牺牲致敬。

虚拟语气:
If I were to meet the queen, I would salute her. = 如果我遇到女王，我会向她敬礼。
I wish I could salute the heroes of this nation. = 我希望我能向这个国家的英雄们致敬。

被动语态:
The flag is saluted daily by the soldiers. = 国旗每天都被士兵们敬礼。
Their achievements were saluted by the entire community. = 他们的成就受到了整个社区的赞扬。

疑问句:
Did he salute the officer? = 他向军官敬礼了吗？
Will they salute the flag? = 他们会向国旗敬礼吗？

否定句:
He didn't salute the flag. = 他没有向国旗敬礼。
They did not salute the winner. = 他们没有向获胜者致敬。
