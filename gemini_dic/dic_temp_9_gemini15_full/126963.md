narrowboat:/ˈnærəʊboʊt/|/ˈnæroʊboʊt/| n. |窄船；狭窄运河船 |A narrowboat is a long, narrow boat traditionally used on canals, often with a distinctive shape and living quarters.  They are typically used for leisure and tourism. |Example: We spent a week cruising the canals on a narrowboat. 我们在一艘窄船上沿着运河航行了整整一周。|近义词：canal boat, barge (less precise)|反义词：wide boat (this is not a common term)
