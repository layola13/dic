nondeductions: /ˌnɒnˈdʌkʃənz/ | n. | 非扣除额，不可扣除项目 | Items that cannot be deducted from taxable income.  These are expenses or losses that are not allowed to reduce the amount of tax owed. | Example:  Medical expenses exceeding a certain limit are often nondeductions. 医疗费用超过一定限额通常是不可扣除的。  |近义词：nonallowable deductions, disallowed expenses | 反义词：deductions
