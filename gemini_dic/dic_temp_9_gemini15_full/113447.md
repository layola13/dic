lynchpin:/ˈlɪntʃpɪn/| n. |关键人物；关键部分；核心要素 |A lynchpin is a person or thing vital to the success of an enterprise or project.  It is the essential element that holds everything together.  The word originates from the pin used to secure a wheel to an axle, highlighting its crucial role in preventing collapse.| The CEO is the lynchpin of this company; without him, the entire operation would crumble.  The new software is the lynchpin of our marketing strategy.| 近义词：keystone, cornerstone, pivotal point | 反义词：unimportant part, peripheral element |
