kryptonite:/ˈkrɪptənaɪt/| n. |氪石（虚构的元素，能削弱超人）|A fictional element from DC Comics' Superman stories that weakens Superman.  It is typically depicted as a green crystalline substance. |Example: Superman's weakness is kryptonite. 超人的弱点是氪石。|近义词：weakness (in the context of Superman)|反义词：strength (in the context of Superman)
