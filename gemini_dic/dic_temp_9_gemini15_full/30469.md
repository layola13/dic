centrifuges:/ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒɪz/| n. |离心机|A centrifuge is a machine that rotates at high speed to separate substances of different densities.  It is commonly used in laboratories to separate blood components, isolate DNA, and perform various other analytical procedures.|例句：The laboratory uses high-speed centrifuges to separate blood cells. 实验室使用高速离心机分离血细胞。

例句：Scientists employed a centrifuge to isolate the DNA from the bacterial sample.科学家们用离心机从细菌样本中分离DNA。|近义词：separator, rotator|反义词:None.
