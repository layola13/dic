self-adulation:/ˌsɛlfˌædʒʊˈleɪʃən/| n. |自我吹捧，自吹自擂 |The act of praising oneself excessively; self-congratulation.  It often implies a lack of genuine accomplishment or an inflated sense of self-importance. |例句：His self-adulation was tiresome; his achievements were modest at best.  他的自我吹捧令人厌烦；他的成就充其量也只是平平无奇。

例句：Her constant self-adulation made her seem arrogant and out of touch. 她不断地自我吹捧，让她显得傲慢且脱离实际。


近义词：narcissism, conceit, vanity, self-praise, egotism

反义词：self-deprecation, humility, modesty


词性: 名词
