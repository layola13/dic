repping:/ˈrɛpɪŋ/| v.|代表；支持；炫耀；说唱|Representing or supporting something or someone; showing off; rapping.  |例句：He's repping his college at the debate. 他在辩论中代表他的大学。|近义词：representing, supporting, showcasing|反义词：opposing, denouncing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am repping my favorite band at the concert. = 我在音乐会上代表我最喜欢的乐队。
They are repping their new product at the trade show. = 他们在贸易展上展示他们的新产品。


一般过去时:
She repped her hometown proudly at the Olympics. = 她自豪地在奥运会上代表她的家乡。
He repped his company at the business conference. = 他在商务会议上代表他的公司。


现在完成时:
I have been repping this brand for five years. = 我已经代表这个品牌五年了。
They have repped several different charities this year. = 他们今年已经代表了几家不同的慈善机构。


过去完成时:
She had repped that company before starting her own business. = 在她自己创业之前，她曾代表过那家公司。
They had repped the product at several smaller events before the big launch. = 在大型发布会之前，他们已经在几个较小的活动中宣传过该产品。


将来时:
I will be repping my school at the upcoming science fair. = 我将在即将到来的科学展览会上代表我的学校。
They will rep their new designs at next year's fashion week. = 他们将在明年的时装周上展示他们的新设计。


虚拟语气:
If I were repping a major corporation, I'd have more resources. = 如果我代表一家大型公司，我就会有更多资源。
I wish I were repping a cause I truly believed in. = 我希望我代表的是我真正相信的事业。


被动语态:
The product is being repped by several influencers online. = 该产品正在由几名网络红人推广。
The company was repped well at the investor meeting. = 该公司在投资者会议上的表现很好。


疑问句:
Who is repping your company at the conference? = 谁在会议上代表你的公司？
What brands are you repping this season? = 这个季度你推广哪些品牌？


否定句:
I am not repping any political parties. = 我不代表任何政党。
They didn't rep the product effectively. = 他们没有有效地推广该产品。


PHRASE->
repping hard = 努力地代表/支持
repping it =  努力地做某事，通常指说唱
repping for = 代表…
repping out = 大力宣传
repping the brand = 推广品牌
