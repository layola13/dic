drawn-out: /drɔːnˈaʊt/ | adj. |冗长的；拖延的；缓慢的 |  Prolonged; extended in time; slow.  It implies something that takes longer than necessary or is tedious because of its length. |例句：The meeting was a drawn-out affair, lasting well into the evening.  会议冗长乏味，一直持续到晚上。

The negotiation process was incredibly drawn-out, taking months to reach an agreement.  谈判过程极其冗长，耗时数月才达成协议。


近义词：lengthy, prolonged, protracted, tedious, slow, tiresome | 反义词：brief, short, concise, succinct


词性:形容词
