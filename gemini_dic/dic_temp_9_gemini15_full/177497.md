rebleaches:/ˌriːˈbliːtʃɪz/ | v. | 再漂白；重新漂白 | To bleach again; to subject to bleaching a second time.  | The dress was so stained, she had to rebleach it. 这件裙子污渍太多，她不得不重新漂白它。 | bleach, whiten | dye, stain | verb
