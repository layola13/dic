mundic:/ˈmʌndɪk/| n.|黄铁矿；硫铁矿|Iron pyrite, a mineral composed of iron disulfide (FeS2), often mistaken for gold due to its brassy yellow color. It is a common mineral found in igneous, metamorphic, and sedimentary rocks.|例句：The miners mistook the mundic for gold, leading to much disappointment. 矿工们把黄铁矿误认为是黄金，导致了很大的失望。|近义词：iron pyrite, pyrite, fool's gold|反义词：gold
