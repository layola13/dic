gainsay:/ɡeɪnˈseɪ/| vt.|反驳；否认；违背|To speak against or oppose (someone or something); to deny or contradict.|例句：No one can gainsay the truth of his words. 没有人能反驳他话语的真实性。|例句： I will not gainsay your decision. 我不会反对你的决定。|近义词：contradict, deny, oppose, refute|反义词：affirm, confirm, support, endorse|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gainsays everything I say. = 他反驳我说的每一句话。
They gainsay the validity of the agreement. = 他们否认协议的有效性。

一般过去时:
She gainsaid his accusations. = 她否认了他的指控。
The witness gainsaid the defendant's claims. = 证人反驳了被告的说法。

现在完成时:
No one has gainsaid the evidence presented. = 没有人反驳所提出的证据。
They have gainsaid their earlier promises. = 他们违背了他们早先的承诺。

过去完成时:
He had gainsaid the prophecy before it came true. = 预言成真之前，他就已经反驳了它。
They had gainsaid their obligations to the community. = 他们已经违背了他们对社区的义务。

将来时:
She will gainsay his authority. = 她将反对他的权威。
They will gainsay the findings of the report. = 他们将反驳报告的调查结果。

虚拟语气:
If she gainsaid the evidence, she would be in trouble. = 如果她反驳证据，她就会有麻烦。
I wish he hadn't gainsaid my advice. = 我真希望他没有反驳我的建议。

被动语态:
His claims were gainsaid by several witnesses. = 他的说法被几位证人反驳了。
The decision was gainsaid by the opposition party. = 这个决定被反对党否决了。

疑问句:
Does anyone gainsay the facts? = 有人反驳事实吗？
Would you gainsay the opinion of the experts? = 你会反驳专家的意见吗？

否定句:
I will not gainsay your authority. = 我不会反对你的权威。
They did not gainsay the importance of the issue. = 他们没有否认这个问题的重要性。
