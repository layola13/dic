reposts:/ˈriːpoʊsts/ | n. |转发（帖子）|The act of sharing or republishing a post, typically on social media.  It involves taking existing content and sharing it with one's own audience. | Example: The article received many reposts on Twitter. 这篇文章在推特上被转发了很多次。|近义词：shares, retweets, reshares |反义词：deletes, removes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reposts interesting articles on his blog. 他在他的博客上转发有趣的文章。
She reposts funny memes daily. 她每天都转发搞笑的迷因。

一般过去时:
I reposted your tweet yesterday. 我昨天转发了你的推文。
They reposted the announcement on their Facebook page. 他们在他们的脸书页面上转发了公告。

现在完成时:
She has reposted several times today. 她今天已经转发了好几次了。
He has reposted all of his friend's posts. 他已经转发了他所有朋友的帖子。

过去完成时:
I had reposted the article before I saw your comment. 在我看你的评论之前，我已经转发了这篇文章。
She had reposted the video numerous times before it went viral. 这段视频爆红之前，她已经转发了很多次了。

将来时:
I will repost your picture tomorrow. 我明天会转发你的照片。
He will repost the news on his Instagram account. 他会在他的Instagram账号上转发这条新闻。

虚拟语气:
If I had seen that post earlier, I would have reposted it. 如果我早点看到那篇文章，我就转发了。
I wish I had reposted that inspiring quote. 我希望我转发了那句鼓舞人心的名言。

被动语态:
The post was reposted thousands of times. 这篇文章被转发了数千次。
The image is being reposted across multiple platforms. 这张图片正在多个平台上被转发。

疑问句:
Did you repost my story? 你转发了我的故事吗？
Have they reposted the announcement yet? 他们转发公告了吗？

否定句:
I didn't repost the video because of the copyright. 我没有转发视频，因为版权问题。
She hasn't reposted anything today. 她今天什么都没转发。


PHRASE->
repost a comment 转发评论
repost a photo 转发照片
repost a video 转发视频
repost an article 转发文章
repost on social media 在社交媒体上转发
