compounding:/ˈkɒmpaʊndɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːmpaʊndɪŋ/| n. v.|复合，混合；构成；加重|The process of combining two or more things to form a single entity.  This can refer to the creation of new words (in linguistics), the mixing of substances (in chemistry), or the accumulation of interest (in finance).|例句：The compounding of interest made his investment grow rapidly.  利滚利使得他的投资迅速增长。

例句：The chemist achieved a successful compounding of the two chemicals. 化学家成功地将两种化学物质混合在一起。


近义词：combination, mixture, synthesis, aggregation|反义词：separation, decomposition, simplification|词性：n. v.
