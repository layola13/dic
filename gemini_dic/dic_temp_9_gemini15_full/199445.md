simulcasts:/ˈsɪmjʊlˌkæsts/| v.|同时广播，同步播放|To broadcast (a program) simultaneously on multiple channels or platforms.  |例句：Many TV stations simulcast the presidential debate. 许多电视台同时播放总统辩论。|例句：The concert was simulcast live to cinemas around the world. 音乐会在全球各地的电影院同步直播。|近义词：broadcast simultaneously, transmit concurrently|反义词：delay, stagger


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The radio station simulcasts the news every hour. =  广播电台每小时同时播放新闻。
They simulcast the game on television and online. = 他们在电视和网络上同时播放比赛。

The network simulcast the special event across all its channels. =  该网络在其所有频道上同时播放了特别节目。
We simulcast the conference to a global audience. = 我们将会议同步直播给全球观众。
