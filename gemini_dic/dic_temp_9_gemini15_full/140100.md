off-licensed:/ˈɒfˌlaɪsənst/ | adj. | 持有脱售许可证的；非持牌销售的 | Having a license to sell alcohol for consumption off the premises where it is sold.  This typically refers to shops or stores that are licensed to sell alcohol for takeaway consumption, rather than for on-premises consumption (like a pub or bar). | Example:  The off-licensed shop sold a wide variety of beers, wines, and spirits. 这家持有脱售许可证的商店出售各种各样的啤酒、葡萄酒和烈酒。|近义词：licensed to sell takeaway alcohol, retail alcohol license holder | 反义词：on-licensed
