rejail:/ˌriːˈdʒeɪl/| vt.|再次监禁，重新入狱|To put (someone) back into jail.  This typically implies a return to jail after a previous release, often due to a parole violation or a new offense.|例句：He was rejailed for violating his parole. 他因为违反假释而再次入狱。|例句：After a brief period of freedom, he was rejailed for another crime. 在短暂的自由之后，他因另一项罪行再次入狱。|近义词：reimprison, incarcerate again|反义词：release, free, liberate|动词
