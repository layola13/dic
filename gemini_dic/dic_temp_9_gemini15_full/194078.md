self-humiliations:/ˌsɛlfˌhjuːmɪliˈeɪʃənz/ | n. | 自我羞辱；自贬身价 | Acts of humbling oneself, often publicly or intentionally.  These acts can stem from feelings of guilt, shame, or a desire for self-punishment, or they can be a manipulative tactic. | Example: His public self-humiliations were a spectacle to behold.  他公开的自我羞辱是一场奇观。  His constant self-humiliations before his boss were pathetic. 他在他老板面前不断地自我贬低，令人同情。|近义词：self-abasement, self-degradation, mortification |反义词：self-aggrandizement, self-exaltation
