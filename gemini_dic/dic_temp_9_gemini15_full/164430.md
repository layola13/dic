preengage:/ˌpriːɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/| vt.|预先占用；预先约定；预先承诺|To arrange or secure (something) in advance; to commit oneself to something beforehand.  It implies a prior commitment or arrangement that prevents something else from happening.|例句：The company preengaged all the available hotel rooms for the conference. 公司预先订好了会议所需的所有酒店房间。

例句：She preengaged herself to help with the project, so she couldn't attend the party. 她预先承诺要帮忙做这个项目，所以不能参加派对。|近义词：reserve, book, commit, allot|反义词：leave free, release, cancel|动词
