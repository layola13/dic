inhaled:/ɪnˈheɪld/| v.|吸入，吸气|To breathe in (air, smoke, gas, etc.).|例句：He inhaled deeply before speaking. 他在说话前深吸了一口气。
例句：The firefighters inhaled toxic fumes. 消防员吸入了有毒烟雾。|近义词：breathe in, aspirate|反义词：exhaled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He inhales deeply before each dive. 他每次潜水前都深吸一口气。
She inhales the fresh mountain air. 她吸入新鲜的山间空气。


一般过去时:
He inhaled the sweet scent of roses. 他吸入了玫瑰花的甜香。
She inhaled sharply, surprised by the sudden noise. 她猛地吸了一口气，被突如其来的噪音吓了一跳。


现在完成时:
He has inhaled too much smoke and needs medical attention. 他吸入了过多的烟雾，需要医疗救助。
She has inhaled the scent of lavender all day. 她一整天都在闻薰衣草的香味。


过去完成时:
He had inhaled the poison gas before anyone noticed.  在他人注意到之前，他已经吸入了毒气。
She had inhaled deeply to calm herself before the performance. 演出前她深吸了一口气来让自己平静下来。


将来时:
He will inhale the anesthetic before the surgery. 手术前他会吸入麻醉剂。
She will inhale the sweet fragrance of the flowers. 她将吸入鲜花的芬芳。


虚拟语气:
If he had inhaled less smoke, he wouldn't be so ill. 如果他吸入的烟少一些，他就不会这么难受了。
If she inhaled deeply, she could relax better. 如果她深吸一口气，就能更好地放松。


被动语态:
The toxic fumes were inhaled by several workers.  几种有毒烟雾被几名工人吸入。
The sweet scent was inhaled by everyone in the room.  房间里每个人都闻到了甜美的香味。


疑问句:
Did he inhale the entire contents of the bottle? 他吸入了瓶子里的全部内容物吗？
Has she ever inhaled helium to change her voice? 她曾经吸入氦气来改变自己的声音吗？


否定句:
He did not inhale the smoke. 他没有吸入烟雾。
She did not inhale any harmful substances. 她没有吸入任何有害物质。


PHRASE->
inhale deeply 深呼吸

forcefully inhale 强吸
inhale and exhale 吸气呼气
inhale deeply and slowly 深而慢地吸气
