oppressing:/əˈprɛsɪŋ/| adj. |压迫的；令人压抑的|  The act of oppressing; subjecting to harsh and authoritarian treatment.  It describes something that weighs heavily on the mind or spirit, causing a feeling of discomfort or anxiety. |例句：The oppressive heat made it difficult to work.  闷热的天气让人难以工作。

例句：The oppressive regime silenced all dissent.  压迫的政权压制了所有的异见。


近义词：suppressive, tyrannical, burdensome, suffocating |反义词：liberating, freeing, uplifting, encouraging |词性：形容词
