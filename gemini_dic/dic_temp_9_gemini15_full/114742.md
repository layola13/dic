malobservation:/ˌmælˌɒbzərˈveɪʃən/| n. |误观察，错误观察 |The act of observing incorrectly or misinterpreting what is observed. It involves a mistake in perception, interpretation, or recording of observations.  This can be due to various factors, including biases, limitations of the senses, or faulty equipment. | Example: The scientist's malobservation of the data led to an incorrect conclusion. 科学家对数据的误观察导致了错误的结论。|近义词：misobservation, misperception, error in observation |反义词：accurate observation, correct observation |名词
