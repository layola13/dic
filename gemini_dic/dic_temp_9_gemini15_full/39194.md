congenious:/kənˈdʒiːniəs/ | adj. | 具有天赋的；合适的；协调的；一致的 |  Showing an aptitude for a particular activity or skill; having a natural talent or ability.  Also, describing things that are suitable or fitting; working well together; harmonious. | 例句：He possessed a congenious aptitude for music. 他天生具有音乐天赋。The two ideas are congenious and complement each other. 这两个想法很契合，彼此互补。| 近义词：talented, gifted, apt, appropriate, suitable, compatible, harmonious | 反义词：inapt, unsuitable, incompatible, discordant |  词性：形容词
