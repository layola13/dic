outfeed:/ˈaʊtfiːd/| n. |输出，出料；输出装置|The process or mechanism of conveying material from a machine.  It often refers to the mechanism that removes finished products from a machine, such as a saw, planer, or conveyor belt. |例句：The outfeed table on the planer was too small to accommodate the large boards.  刨床的出料台太小，无法容纳大型木板。
The automatic outfeed system greatly improved efficiency.  自动出料系统极大地提高了效率。|近义词：output, discharge, delivery, ejection |反义词：input, intake, feed
