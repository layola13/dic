laying: /ˈleɪɪŋ/ | v. | 放置；产卵；铺设；制定 |  The act of putting something down or into a position; the process of a bird producing eggs; to put something down in position, often in layers; to create or establish a plan or system. |


例句：
She is laying the table for dinner. = 她在摆放晚餐的餐具。

The hen is laying eggs. = 母鸡正在下蛋。


近义词：placing, setting, producing, establishing


反义词：removing, lifting, destroying, dismantling


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is laying bricks. = 他正在砌砖。
The farmer is laying the groundwork for the new crop. = 农民正在为新作物打基础。


一般过去时:
She laid the book on the table. = 她把书放在桌子上。
They laid the foundation for the building. = 他们为建筑物奠定了基础。


现在完成时:
The hen has laid ten eggs this week. = 母鸡这周下了十个蛋。
They have laid out a detailed plan for the project. = 他们为这个项目制定了一个详细的计划。


过去完成时:
She had laid the groundwork for her presentation before the conference. = 会议前她已经为她的演讲做了准备工作。
The birds had laid their eggs before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，鸟儿们已经产下了卵。


将来时:
He will be laying the carpet tomorrow. = 他明天将铺设地毯。
They will lay the blame on someone else. = 他们会把责任推到别人身上。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would lay out a more detailed budget. = 如果我有更多时间，我会制定更详细的预算。
I wish I had laid the foundation earlier. = 我希望我早点打好基础。


被动语态:
The foundation stone was laid by the mayor. = 基石由市长奠定。
The plans for the new road are being laid. = 新路的计划正在制定中。


疑问句:

否定句:

They didn't lay the groundwork for their argument. = 他们没有为他们的论点打下基础。
