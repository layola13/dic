diplomatic:/ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk/| adj.|外交的；有外交手腕的；圆滑的 | Relating to diplomacy; skilled in dealing with sensitive matters or people.  |例句：The situation requires a diplomatic approach.  这种情况需要采取外交手段。
The ambassador delivered a diplomatic response to the criticism. 大使对批评作出了外交辞令的回应。|近义词：tactful, skillful, subtle |反义词：blunt, abrupt, undiplomatic
