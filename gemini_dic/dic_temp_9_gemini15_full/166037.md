preplacements:/ˌpriːˈpleɪsmənts/| n.| 预先安置；预先配置；提前部署 |The act of placing something in position beforehand; prior arrangement or deployment of personnel or resources.|例句：The preplacements of security personnel ensured a smooth event. 预先部署安保人员确保了活动的顺利进行。
例句：The preplacements of the equipment were crucial for the experiment's success. 设备的预先配置对于实验的成功至关重要。|近义词：prearrangements, preparations, deployments|反义词：postplacements, afterthoughts|名词
