seeder:/ˈsiːdər/| n. |播种者；种子；引发者|A seeder is a person or thing that plants seeds, or something that initiates or starts something.|例句：The farmer is a hardworking seeder.  这个农民是个勤劳的播种者。|例句：The new policy acted as a seeder for economic growth. 这项新政策成为经济增长的催化剂。|近义词：sower, planter, initiator|反义词：harvester, destroyer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer seeds the field every spring. = 农民每年春天都在田里播种。
She seeds the flowerbeds with colorful blooms. = 她用五颜六色的花朵播种花坛。

一般过去时:
He seeded the lawn with new grass seed. = 他用新的草籽播种草坪。
They seeded the clouds to induce rain. = 他们播撒云层以诱导降雨。

现在完成时:
The company has seeded the market with its new product. = 公司已经用其新产品打开了市场。
We have seeded the database with initial data. = 我们已经用初始数据填充了数据库。

过去完成时:
Before the planting season, they had seeded the entire field. = 在种植季节之前，他们已经播种了整个田地。
She had seeded the tomatoes before the frost hit. = 霜冻来临之前，她已经播种了西红柿。

将来时:
They will seed the rice paddies next week. = 他们下周将播种稻田。
The scientists will seed the storm clouds to control the rainfall. = 科学家们将播种暴风雨云层以控制降雨量。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would seed the garden with organic vegetables. = 如果我是你，我会在花园里播种有机蔬菜。
I wish I had seeded the sunflowers earlier in the spring. = 我真希望春天早点播种向日葵。

被动语态:
The field was seeded with wheat last autumn. = 秋天小麦播种于这块田地。
The database is being seeded with customer information. = 数据库正在用客户信息填充。

疑问句:
Have you seeded the lawn yet? = 你播种草坪了吗？
When will the farmers seed the corn? = 农民们什么时候播种玉米？

否定句:
He didn't seed the garden this year. = 他今年没有播种花园。
They haven't seeded the field because of the drought. = 由于干旱，他们还没有播种田地。
