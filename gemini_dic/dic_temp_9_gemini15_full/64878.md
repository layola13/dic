esquisse:/ɛˈskɪs/| n. |素描，草图，梗概 | A preliminary sketch or rough draft of a design or work of art.  It's a quick, often incomplete, representation of an idea. |例句：The architect presented an esquisse of the proposed building. 建筑师展示了拟建大楼的草图。|The writer created an esquisse of the novel before starting the full draft. 作者在开始完整草稿之前创作了小说的梗概。 |近义词：sketch, draft, outline, preliminary study |反义词：finished work, completed product
