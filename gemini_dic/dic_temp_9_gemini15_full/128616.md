nicked:/nɪkt/ | v. | 偷窃；划伤；碰撞；轻微损伤 | To steal something; to make a small cut or scratch on a surface; to lightly collide with something.  |例句：He nicked a chocolate bar from the shop. 他从商店偷了一块巧克力。

The car was nicked during the night.  汽车在夜间被偷了。


例句：The branch nicked my arm as I fell. 我摔倒时树枝划伤了我的胳膊。

My car was nicked by a passing truck. 我的车被一辆经过的卡车轻微碰撞了。


近义词：steal, rob, scratch, graze, bump | 反义词：give, donate, repair, heal
