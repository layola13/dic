policies:/ˈpɑːləsiz/ /ˈpɒləsiz/ | n. |政策；策略；方针 | Principles or rules that govern conduct within an organization or field of activity.  Policies are often intended to achieve certain goals or objectives. |例句：The company's policies on employee benefits are very generous. 这家公司关于员工福利的政策非常优厚。

例句：Government policies often have a significant impact on the economy. 政府的政策通常对经济有重大影响。


近义词：strategies, guidelines, rules, regulations, principles | 反义词：chaos, disorder


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government implements new policies annually. = 政府每年实施新的政策。
Our company follows strict ethical policies. = 我们公司遵循严格的道德政策。


一般过去时:
They changed their return policies last year. = 他们去年改变了退货政策。
The school adopted new disciplinary policies. = 学校采用了新的纪律政策。


现在完成时:
The company has revised its privacy policies several times. = 公司已经多次修改了其隐私政策。
The government has implemented many new environmental policies. = 政府已经实施了许多新的环保政策。


过去完成时:
Before the incident, they hadn't established clear safety policies. = 事故发生前，他们还没有制定明确的安全政策。
They had already announced the new economic policies before the election. = 他们在选举前就已经宣布了新的经济政策。


将来时:
The bank will announce new lending policies next month. = 银行下个月将宣布新的贷款政策。
They will likely adopt stricter security policies in the future. = 他们将来可能会采取更严格的安全政策。


虚拟语气:
If they had better policies, the situation would be different. = 如果他们有更好的政策，情况就会不同。
I wish the company had clearer policies on this matter. = 我希望公司对这件事有更清晰的政策。


被动语态:
New policies are being developed by the committee. = 委员会正在制定新的政策。
The old policies were replaced with more effective ones. = 旧的政策被更有效的政策所取代。


疑问句:
What are the company's policies on sick leave? = 公司关于病假的政策是什么？
What policies are in place to address this issue? = 为了解决这个问题，有哪些政策？


否定句:
The company does not have a clear policy on this issue. = 公司对这个问题没有明确的政策。
They did not follow the established policies. = 他们没有遵守既定的政策。
