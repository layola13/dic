photogenically:/ˌfoʊtoʊˈdʒɛnɪkli/| adv.|上镜地，照相好看地| appearing attractive in photographs.  The adjective "photogenic" describes someone or something that photographs well, and the adverb "photogenically" modifies a verb to indicate how that action is performed in relation to photography. |例句：She posed photogenically for the camera. 她摆出上镜的姿势拍照。

She smiled photogenically for the family portrait. 她对着家庭照露出了上镜的笑容。


近义词：attractively (in photographs),picturesquely


反义词：unflatteringly (in photographs)


词性：副词
