midtown:/ˈmɪdˌtaʊn/| n. |市中心；城镇中部|The central area of a town or city, typically between the downtown core and the suburbs.  Midtown often refers to a more upscale or residential area compared to the downtown business district. |例句：She lives in midtown Manhattan. 她住在曼哈顿中城。|例句：We decided to meet in midtown for lunch. 我们决定在市中心吃午饭。|近义词：city center, downtown (sometimes), central area|反义词：suburbs, outskirts
