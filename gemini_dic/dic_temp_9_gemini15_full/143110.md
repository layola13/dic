outbulge:/ˈaʊtˌbʌldʒ/| v.|凸出，鼓出|To bulge outward; protrude.  The word combines "out" (meaning outward) and "bulge" (meaning to swell or protrude).|例句：The muscles in his arms outbulged as he lifted the heavy weight.  他的胳膊肌肉在他举起重物时鼓了起来。

The old sack outbulged with potatoes.  旧麻袋鼓鼓囊囊地装着土豆。|近义词：protrude, project, swell, distend|反义词：concave, indent, recede|
