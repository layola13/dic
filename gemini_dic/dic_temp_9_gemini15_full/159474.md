polices:/pəˈliːsɪz/ | n. |警察（复数）,政策（复数） |The plural form of "police".  It refers to either multiple police officers or multiple policies.  |例句：The polices responded quickly to the emergency. 警察迅速响应了紧急情况。


例句：The company's polices on data privacy are very strict.  这家公司关于数据隐私的政策非常严格。|近义词：officers (for police), regulations, rules (for policies) |反义词：暂无(取决于语境) |名词
