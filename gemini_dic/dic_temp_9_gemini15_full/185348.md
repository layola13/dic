retroact:/ˈrɛtroʊækt/| v.|追溯生效，反向作用|To give retroactive effect to (a law, decision, etc.); to operate or be effective from a date earlier than its enactment or implementation.|例句：The new law will retroact to January 1st.  新法律将追溯到1月1日生效。|例句：The company decided to retroact the pay raise to the beginning of the year. 公司决定将加薪追溯到年初。|近义词：backdate, apply retrospectively|反义词：proact, preact


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new rule retroacts to last month. = 新规追溯到上个月。
This law retroacts to the beginning of the year. = 这项法律追溯到年初。

一般过去时:
The court retroacted its decision. = 法院撤销了其决定。
The company retroacted the salary increase. = 公司追溯实施了加薪。

现在完成时:
The government has retroacted the tax changes. = 政府已追溯实施了税收变更。
They have retroacted the penalty to the previous quarter. = 他们已将处罚追溯到上一季度。

过去完成时:
The judge had retroacted the sentence before the appeal. = 法官在上诉前已撤销了判决。
The board had retroacted the policy after much debate. = 董事会在经过大量辩论后已追溯修改了政策。

将来时:
The amendment will retroact to the date of the original law. = 修正案将追溯到原法律的日期。
The new regulation will retroact to the start of the fiscal year. = 新规将追溯到财政年度开始。


虚拟语气:
If the judge retroacted the ruling, the defendant would be released. = 如果法官撤销裁决，被告将被释放。
If the policy retroacted, many people would be compensated. = 如果政策追溯生效，许多人将获得赔偿。


被动语态:
The decision was retroacted by the higher court. = 该决定被上级法院撤销。
The payment was retroacted to the previous month. = 支付追溯到上个月。


疑问句:
Does this law retroact to previous years? = 这部法律是否追溯到往年？
Will the new policy retroact to existing contracts? = 新政策是否会追溯到现有合同？


否定句:
The court did not retroact its decision. = 法院没有撤销其决定。
The changes will not retroact to earlier transactions. = 这些更改不会追溯到之前的交易。


PHRASE->
retroact to = 追溯到
retroact upon = 反向作用于
retroactively apply = 追溯适用
retroactive effect = 追溯效力
retroactive legislation = 追溯立法
