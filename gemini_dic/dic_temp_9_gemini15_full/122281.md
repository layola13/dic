mitochondrial:/ˌmaɪtəʊˈkɒndrɪəl/ | adj. | 线粒体的 | Relating to or characteristic of mitochondria.  Mitochondria are organelles found in most eukaryotic cells, and are often referred to as the "powerhouses" of the cell because they generate most of the cell's supply of adenosine triphosphate (ATP), used as a source of chemical energy. | Example:  Mitochondrial DNA is inherited maternally.  线粒体DNA是母系遗传的。 |近义词: mitochondrial-related |反义词:nuclear (in the context of genetics)
