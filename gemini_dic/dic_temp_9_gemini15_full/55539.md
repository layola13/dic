distributively:/dɪˈstrɪbjʊtɪvli/ | adv. | 分配地；分别地；个别地 | In a distributive manner; separately to each member of a group.  | The prizes were distributed distributively among the winners.奖品被分别发放给获胜者。 | collectively, jointly |  concentratedly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have tense conjugations like verbs. It describes *how* an action is performed.


Example Sentences:

The tasks were assigned distributively to each team member. 任务被分别分配给每个团队成员。

The teacher distributed the worksheets distributively to each student. 老师将工作表分别发放给每位学生。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "distributively".  The word itself is used to modify verbs describing distribution.
