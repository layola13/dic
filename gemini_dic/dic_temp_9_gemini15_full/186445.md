riboflavins:/ˌraɪbəʊˈfleɪvɪnz/| n. |核黄素（复数）|Riboflavins are a group of water-soluble vitamins, also known as vitamin B2. They play a crucial role in various metabolic processes, including energy production and the maintenance of healthy skin, eyes, and mucous membranes.  Riboflavin deficiency can lead to various health problems.|Example:  A deficiency in riboflavins can cause cheilosis and glossitis. 核黄素缺乏会导致唇炎和舌炎。|近义词：vitamin B2|反义词：无
