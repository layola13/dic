on-the-air: /ˌɑːn ði ˈɛr/ | adj. | 正在播出的；直播的 | Being broadcast live on radio or television.  |例句：The interview was on-the-air for an hour.  这次采访直播了一个小时。

The concert is on-the-air now. 音乐会正在直播。|近义词：live, broadcasting, live on air |反义词：off-the-air, recorded, pre-recorded |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The show is on-the-air right now. = 节目现在正在播出。
The radio station is on-the-air 24/7. = 这个广播电台全天候播出。

一般过去时:
The news was on-the-air when the power went out. = 停电时新闻正在播出。
The president's speech was on-the-air yesterday. = 总统的演讲昨天播出了。

现在完成时:
The program has been on-the-air for ten years. = 这个节目已经播出了十年了。
They have been on-the-air since 6pm. = 他们从下午六点就开始播出了。

过去完成时:
The show had already been on-the-air for five minutes when I tuned in. = 我收听的时候，节目已经播出了五分钟了。
The interview had been on-the-air before the technical difficulties began. = 在出现技术问题之前，采访已经播出了。

将来时:
The debate will be on-the-air tomorrow night. = 辩论将在明晚播出。
The concert will be on-the-air next week. = 音乐会将在下周播出。

虚拟语气:
If the show were on-the-air, I would watch it. = 如果节目正在播出，我会看的。
I wish the interview were on-the-air right now so I could hear it. = 我希望采访现在正在播出，这样我就能听到了。

被动语态:
The announcement was being on-the-air when the phone rang. = 电话响的时候，公告正在播出。
The program is being on-the-air at this moment. = 节目此刻正在播出。

疑问句:
Is the game on-the-air yet? = 比赛开始播出了吗？
Was the movie on-the-air last night? = 昨晚电影有播出吗？

否定句:
The program wasn't on-the-air yesterday. = 节目昨天没有播出。
The radio show isn't on-the-air at this time. = 广播节目现在没有播出。
