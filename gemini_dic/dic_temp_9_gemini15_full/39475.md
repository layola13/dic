connivers:/kəˈnɪvərz/| n. |密谋者，共谋者 | A conniver is a person who secretly plans with others to do something immoral or illegal. They are involved in conspiracies and schemes.  |Example: The connivers were finally caught and brought to justice after years of evading the law.  密谋者最终被抓获并绳之以法，此前他们逃避法律多年。|近义词：conspirator, plotter, schemer |反义词：honest person, upright person |名词
