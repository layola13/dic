nonstatements:/ˈnɒnˌsteɪtmənts/ | n. |非陈述句，非声明 |Things that are not statements; utterances that do not assert a proposition.  They can include questions, commands, exclamations, or other forms of speech that do not express a belief or opinion. |Example:  "Go away!" is a nonstatement; it's a command.  "What time is it?" is a nonstatement; it's a question.|近义词：interrogatives, imperatives, exclamations|反义词：statements, declarations
