overslowly:/ˌoʊvərˈsloʊli/ | adv. | 过慢地，迟缓地 |  The word "overslowly" is an adverb formed by adding the prefix "over-" to the adverb "slowly." It intensifies the meaning of "slowly," indicating an excessive or unnecessarily slow speed.  It implies a degree of slowness beyond what is considered normal or acceptable. | Example: He walked overslowly, almost as if he were afraid to reach his destination. 他走得很慢，几乎好像害怕到达目的地。 | 近义词：excessively slowly, very slowly, painstakingly slowly | 反义词：quickly, rapidly, swiftly |  词性: 副词
