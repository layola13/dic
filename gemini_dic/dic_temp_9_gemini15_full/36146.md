coexistence:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪstəns/| n. |共存，共处|The state or fact of two or more things existing together at the same time or in the same place.  It often implies a relationship between the things, whether harmonious or not.|例句：The coexistence of different cultures enriches society.  不同文化的共存丰富了社会。|例句：The coexistence of lions and zebras in the same national park is a delicate balance.  狮子和斑马在同一个国家公园里的共存是一种微妙的平衡。|近义词：cohabitation, symbiosis, concurrence|反义词：exclusion, incompatibility, antagonism|名词
