pro's:/proʊz/| n. |优点，好处 |The pros of working from home include flexibility and comfort.  在家工作的优点包括灵活性和舒适性。|近义词：advantages, benefits, pluses |反义词：cons, drawbacks, disadvantages |名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The pros outweigh the cons in this situation. = 在这种情况下，优点超过缺点。
What are the pros of this new technology? = 这项新技术的优点是什么？


一般过去时:
The pros of the previous plan were clear. = 之前计划的优点很明显。
We discussed the pros and cons before making a decision. = 我们在做决定之前讨论了利弊。


现在完成时:
The project has shown many pros since its launch. = 自从启动以来，该项目已经展现出许多优点。
We have weighed the pros and cons carefully. = 我们仔细权衡了利弊。


过去完成时:
They had already considered the pros and cons before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就已经考虑了利弊。
I had not fully understood the pros of the offer before accepting it. = 在接受之前，我并没有完全理解这个提议的优点。


将来时:
The new policy will bring many pros to the company. = 新政策将给公司带来许多好处。
We will analyze the pros and cons before proceeding. = 我们将在继续之前分析利弊。


虚拟语气:
If the pros were more evident, we would have invested. = 如果优点更明显，我们就会投资。
I wish the pros of this option were clearer. = 我希望这个选项的优点更清晰。


被动语态:
The pros of the new system were highlighted in the presentation. = 新系统的优点在演示文稿中得到了强调。
The pros and cons are being debated by the committee. = 委员会正在辩论利弊。


疑问句:
What are the pros of this approach? = 这种方法的优点是什么？
Are there any other pros we should consider? = 我们还应该考虑其他优点吗？


否定句:
There are no significant pros to this plan. = 这个计划没有明显的优点。
The plan didn't show many pros. = 该计划没有显示出许多优点。
