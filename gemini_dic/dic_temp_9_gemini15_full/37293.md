commandingly:/kəˈmændɪŋli/| adv. | 命令地；权威地；引人注目地 | In a commanding manner; with authority or influence.  It suggests a strong, confident, and often forceful presence or action. |例句：He spoke commandingly, silencing the room. 他命令式地说话，让房间安静下来。

例句：She stood commandingly on the stage, captivating the audience. 她站在舞台上，姿态威严，迷住了观众。


近义词：authoritatively, forcefully, impressively, powerfully, dominantly

反义词：submissively, timidly, weakly, hesitantly

词性：副词
