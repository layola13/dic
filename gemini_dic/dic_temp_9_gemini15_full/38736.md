conditions:/kənˈdɪʃənz/| n.|条件，状况，环境|The conditions of the contract are very strict.  合同的条件非常严格。|近义词：terms, circumstances, state|反义词：unconditional


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The conditions are favorable for growth. = 生长条件有利。
What conditions are required for the experiment? = 实验需要什么条件？


一般过去时:
The conditions were harsh during the winter. = 冬天的条件很恶劣。
What conditions did they face during their journey? = 他们在旅途中面临什么情况？


现在完成时:
Conditions have improved significantly since the last inspection. = 自上次检查以来，情况已大大改善。
Have the conditions changed since you last visited? = 自你上次来访以来，情况有变化吗？


过去完成时:
The conditions had deteriorated before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，情况已经恶化。
They had faced difficult conditions before reaching their destination. = 他们在到达目的地之前面临过艰难的条件。


将来时:
Conditions will likely improve next year. = 明年情况可能会好转。
What conditions will we encounter on our trip? = 我们在旅途中会遇到什么情况？


虚拟语气:
If the conditions were different, we might have succeeded. = 如果条件不同，我们可能会成功。
I wish the conditions were better for the plants to grow. = 我希望植物生长的条件更好。


被动语态:
The experiment was conducted under controlled conditions. = 实验在受控条件下进行。
The project was delayed due to unfavorable conditions. = 项目因不利条件而延误。


疑问句:
What are the conditions for entry? = 入场条件是什么？
Under what conditions will the reaction occur? = 在什么条件下反应会发生？


否定句:
The conditions were not ideal for planting. = 种植条件并不理想。
The contract does not specify the exact conditions. = 合同没有具体说明条件。


PHRASE->
on condition that = 如果…
under the conditions = 在…条件下
working conditions = 工作条件
living conditions = 生活条件
necessary conditions = 必要条件
