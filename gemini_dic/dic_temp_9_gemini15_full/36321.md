cohost:/ˈkoʊhoʊst/ | n. |联合主持人，共同主持人 | A person who shares hosting duties with another person or people.  | Example:  She's the cohost of a popular morning radio show. 她是一位热门早间广播节目的联合主持人。 |近义词：joint host, co-presenter |反义词：sole host, single host


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cohosts a podcast with his friend. = 他和朋友一起主持一个播客。
They cohost a weekly online show. = 他们共同主持一个每周的在线节目。

一般过去时:
She cohosted the awards ceremony last night. = 她昨晚联合主持了颁奖典礼。
We cohosted a fundraiser for the local library. = 我们共同主持了当地图书馆的筹款活动。

现在完成时:
He has cohosted numerous events over the years. = 多年来，他已联合主持过无数活动。
They have cohosted several successful television programs. = 他们共同主持过几档成功的电视节目。

过去完成时:
Before the show, they had cohosted a smaller event together. = 演出之前，他们一起联合主持过一个较小的活动。
She had cohosted many similar events before this one. = 在这次活动之前，她已经主持过许多类似的活动。

将来时:
They will cohost a webinar next month. = 他们下个月将联合主持一个网络研讨会。
He will cohost a cooking show on a new network. = 他将在一个新的网络上联合主持一个烹饪节目。

虚拟语气:
If he were available, he would cohost the event. = 如果他可以的话，他会联合主持这个活动。
I wish I could cohost that popular podcast. = 我真希望我能联合主持那个受欢迎的播客。

被动语态:
The event was cohosted by two well-known celebrities. = 该活动由两位知名人士联合主持。
The conference will be cohosted by several leading organizations. = 会议将由几家领先的组织联合主持。

疑问句:
Who will cohost the upcoming debate? = 谁将联合主持即将到来的辩论？
Have they ever cohosted a show together before? = 他们以前一起主持过节目吗？

否定句:
He did not cohost the program last year. = 他去年没有联合主持这个节目。
She will not cohost the event due to a scheduling conflict. = 由于时间冲突，她将不会联合主持该活动。
