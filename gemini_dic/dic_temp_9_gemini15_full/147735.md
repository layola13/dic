overprocessed:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːsɛst/| adj. |过度加工的，加工过度的| excessively processed; subjected to too much processing, often resulting in a loss of nutritional value or desirable qualities.  |例句：The overprocessed food lacked flavor and nutritional value.  过度加工的食物缺乏风味和营养价值。

例句：This overprocessed cheese contains too much salt and preservatives. 这加工过度的奶酪含有过多的盐和防腐剂。


近义词：highly processed, excessively refined, artificially processed

反义词：unprocessed, raw, natural, minimally processed
