doorbelled: /ˈdɔːrbɛld/ | v. | 按门铃 |  To ring someone's doorbell. This is a relatively informal verb formed from the noun "doorbell".  It implies a brief and simple act of ringing the doorbell. |


例句：

I doorbelled at their house but nobody answered.  = 我按了他们家的门铃，但没有人回应。

She doorbelled before entering the house. = 她按了门铃之后才进屋。


近义词：rang the doorbell, pressed the doorbell button, buzzed

反义词：无直接反义词，可以考虑一些表示不按门铃的动作，例如：sneaked in (偷偷溜进去)


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He doorbells every morning before work. = 他每天早上上班前都按门铃。
They doorbell their neighbours frequently. = 他们经常按邻居家的门铃。

一般过去时:
I doorbelled at 8 o'clock last night. = 我昨晚八点钟按了门铃。
She doorbelled, but no one came to the door. = 她按了门铃，但没有人来开门。

现在完成时:
Have you doorbelled at their house yet? = 你已经按过他们家的门铃了吗？
I have doorbelled several times without a response. = 我已经按了好几次门铃了，都没有回应。

过去完成时:
He had doorbelled before they even knew he was there. = 在他们知道他来了之前，他已经按过门铃了。
She had doorbelled twice before giving up. = 她按了两次门铃后放弃了。

将来时:
I will doorbell them first thing tomorrow morning. = 我明天一大早就会去按他们的门铃。
They will doorbell again if they don't answer the phone. = 如果他们不接电话，他们会再次按门铃。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would doorbell their house again. = 如果我是你，我会再去按他们家的门铃。
I wish I had doorbelled earlier. = 我真希望我早点按门铃。

被动语态:
The doorbell was doorbelled repeatedly. (This is awkward and unnatural.  Passive voice is rarely used with this verb.) = 门铃被反复按响。（这个句子很别扭，不自然。这个动词很少用被动语态。）
The house was doorbelled by the delivery man. (Slightly more natural but still unusual.) = 送货员按了那家的门铃。（稍微自然一些，但仍然不常见。）


疑问句:
Did you doorbell before entering? = 你进门前按门铃了吗？
Will you doorbell my neighbour for me? = 你能帮我按一下我邻居的门铃吗？

否定句:
I didn't doorbell because I had a key. = 我没有按门铃，因为我有钥匙。
They didn't doorbell, they just knocked. = 他们没有按门铃，只是敲门。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "doorbelled".  The verb is too specific.
