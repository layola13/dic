lucernarian:/ˌluːsɜːˈneəriən/| n. |萤火虫；发光体|A lucernarian is a luminous organism, often referring to a type of jellyfish or a firefly.  It can also refer to something that resembles or emits light like a firefly.|例句：The deep-sea lucernarians illuminated the dark abyss. 深海萤火虫照亮了黑暗的深渊。 The twinkling lights of the city resembled a vast colony of lucernarians. 城市闪烁的灯光如同一个巨大的萤火虫群落。|近义词：firefly, glowworm, phosphorescent organism |反义词：dark, non-luminous
