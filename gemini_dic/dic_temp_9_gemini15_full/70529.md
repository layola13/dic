filiation:/ˌfɪliˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 子女关系；隶属关系；血统；亲子鉴定 | The state of being a son or daughter; relationship between parent and child.  It can also refer to the act of establishing or determining parentage. | Example:  The court ordered a filiation test to determine paternity. 法院下令进行亲子鉴定以确定父子关系。  The filiation of the young man to the wealthy family was never questioned.  这个年轻人与富裕家庭的亲属关系从未受到质疑。 | 近义词：parentage, kinship, lineage, descent | 反义词：dissociation, disaffiliation
