/ˌnɒnsoʊpɒrɪfɪks/| n. |非催眠剂；兴奋剂|Substances or activities that prevent sleep or drowsiness.  They are the opposite of soporifics, which induce sleep.|Examples:  Coffee and tea are common nonsoporifics.  A lively conversation can act as a nonsoporific. |近义词：stimulant, energizer |反义词：soporific, sedative |名词
