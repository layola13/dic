confiscator: /ˈkɒnfɪskeɪtər/ | /ˈkɑːnfɪskeɪtər/ | n. |没收者；充公者 |A person or entity that confiscates property.  Someone or something that seizes and takes possession of something, usually by legal authority. | The government confiscator seized the illegal drugs.  政府没收者查抄了非法毒品。|  seizer, appropriator |restorer, returner |名词
