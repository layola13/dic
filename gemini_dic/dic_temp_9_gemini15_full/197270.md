sharpener:/ˈʃɑːrpənər/| n.|卷笔刀；磨刀器|A tool used for sharpening pencils, knives, or other pointed objects.|例句：I need a sharpener to sharpen this pencil. 我需要一个卷笔刀来削这支铅笔。|近义词：pencil sharpener, knife sharpener|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 duller, blunt)

词源:
The word "sharpener" is derived from the verb "sharpen" + the suffix "-er," which indicates an agent or instrument.  "Sharpen" itself has Old English roots, related to words meaning "to make pointed or keen."
