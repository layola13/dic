hams:/hæmz/| n.|火腿；业余无线电爱好者；拙劣的表演|Ham refers to the cured meat from a pig's leg or shoulder.  It can also refer to an amateur radio operator, often implying a less skilled or experienced one, or to something done in a clumsy or amateurish way, especially a performance.|例句：We had ham and eggs for breakfast.  我们早餐吃了火腿和鸡蛋。|近义词：pork, bacon (for the meat); amateur, novice (for the radio operator)|反义词：professional (for the performance)
