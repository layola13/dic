modernizable: /ˌmɒdənaɪˈzeɪbəl/ /ˌmɑːdərnˈaɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 可现代化的；能够现代化的 | Capable of being modernized; suitable for modernization.  The word implies that something can be updated to conform to modern standards, technology, or practices. |

例句：

The old factory is modernizable with significant investment. = 这家旧工厂可以通过大量的投资进行现代化改造。

The software is fully modernizable to the latest version. = 该软件可以完全升级到最新版本。


近义词：updatable, improvable, upgradeable

反义词：obsolete, antiquated, outdated


词性：形容词
