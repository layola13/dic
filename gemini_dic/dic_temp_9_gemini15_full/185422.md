retry:/riːˈtraɪ/| vt.|重试，再试|To try again; to attempt something again after a previous failure.|例句：I failed the first time, so I will retry. 我第一次失败了，所以我将重试。|例句：After the computer crashed, I had to retry the program. 电脑崩溃后，我不得不重试程序。|近义词：reattempt, try again|反义词：give up, abandon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retries the exam every year. = 他每年都重考。
They retry the experiment until they get the result. = 他们反复实验直到得到结果。


一般过去时:
She retried the password several times. = 她重试了几次密码。
He retried sending the email after the first attempt failed. = 第一次尝试失败后，他又重发了邮件。


现在完成时:
I have retried connecting to the server many times today. = 我今天已经多次尝试连接服务器了。
They have retried the experiment with different parameters. = 他们已经用不同的参数重复实验了。


过去完成时:
He had retried the code before asking for help. = 他在寻求帮助之前已经重试过代码了。
She had retried calling him several times before giving up. = 她放弃之前已经多次尝试给他打电话了。


将来时:
I will retry the login tomorrow. = 我明天将重新登录。
They will retry the experiment next week. = 他们下周将重做实验。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would retry the task. = 如果我是你，我会重试这个任务。
I wish I had retried sending the email sooner. = 我真希望我早点重发邮件。


被动语态:
The program was retried by the technician. = 程序由技术人员重试。
The experiment will be retried under different conditions. = 实验将在不同的条件下重做。


疑问句:
Will you retry the operation? = 你会重试操作吗？
Should we retry the download? = 我们应该重试下载吗？


否定句:
He did not retry the test. = 他没有重考。
They will not retry the experiment. = 他们不会重做实验。
