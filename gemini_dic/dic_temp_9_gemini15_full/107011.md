lactate:/ˈlækteɪt/| n. v.|乳酸；产生乳酸|Lactate refers to either lactic acid or the salt of lactic acid.  As a noun, it describes the acid produced in muscle tissue during strenuous exercise. As a verb, it means to produce or secrete lactic acid. |例句：The muscles lactate during intense exercise. 剧烈运动时肌肉会产生乳酸。|例句：The body naturally lactates to produce energy. 身体自然产生乳酸来产生能量。|近义词：lactic acid|反义词：无|名词，动词
