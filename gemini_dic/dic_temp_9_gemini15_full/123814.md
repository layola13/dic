mops:/mɒps/| n. v.|拖把；用拖把拖｜a tool for cleaning floors, typically consisting of a handle and a mop head; to clean (a floor, etc.) with a mop |例句：She mopped the spilled milk. 她擦掉了洒出的牛奶。|近义词：swab, sponge|反义词：mess,soil


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mops the floor every day. = 他每天都拖地。
She mops up the spilled juice. = 她擦掉了洒出的果汁。


一般过去时:
I mopped the kitchen floor yesterday. = 我昨天拖了厨房的地。
They mopped up the water after the rain. = 雨后他们擦干了水。


现在完成时:
I have mopped the floor twice today. = 我今天已经拖过两次地了。
She has mopped up all the spills. = 她已经擦干了所有洒出的东西。


过去完成时:
I had mopped the floor before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前我已经拖过地了。
She had mopped up the mess before her mother came home. = 她母亲回家之前已经清理了烂摊子。


将来时:
I will mop the floor later. = 我一会儿会拖地。
He will mop up the spill before it dries. = 他会在污渍干掉之前把它擦掉。


虚拟语气:
If I had a mop, I would mop the floor. = 如果我有拖把，我会拖地的。
I wish I had mopped the floor earlier. = 我真希望我早点拖地。


被动语态:
The floor is mopped every day. = 地板每天都被拖。
The spill was mopped up quickly. = 洒出的东西很快就被擦干净了。


疑问句:
Do you mop the floor regularly? = 你经常拖地吗？
Have you mopped the kitchen yet? = 你已经拖厨房的地了吗？


否定句:
I didn't mop the floor today. = 我今天没有拖地。
She doesn't mop the floor often. = 她不经常拖地。


PHRASE->
mop up = 擦干；清理
mop and bucket = 拖把和水桶
string mop = 绳拖把
wet mop = 湿拖把
floor mop = 地拖


词源:
The word "mop" ultimately derives from the Middle Dutch word "moppen," meaning "to rub" or "to wipe."  This reflects the action of using a mop to clean a surface.
