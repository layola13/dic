pouch:/paʊtʃ/| n.|小袋，钱包，育儿袋|A small bag, typically made of flexible material and often used for carrying small items.  It can also refer to a pocket-like structure in an animal's body, such as a kangaroo's pouch.|例句：She kept her money in a small leather pouch. 她把钱放在一个小皮袋里。

例句：The kangaroo kept its baby safe in its pouch. 袋鼠把它的宝宝安全地放在育儿袋里。

近义词：bag, sack, pocket, satchel|反义词：None|词性:名词
