refrozen: /ˌriːˈfroʊzən/ | adj. | 再次冷冻的；重新冷冻的 |  Refrozen describes something that has been frozen once and then frozen again.  This often implies a loss of quality compared to food frozen only once. | Example: The refrozen vegetables tasted less fresh.  再次冷冻的蔬菜尝起来不那么新鲜了。The refrozen meat was tough and dry.  再次冷冻的肉又硬又干。|近义词：re-frozen, repeatedly frozen|反义词：fresh, newly frozen|形容词
