ignoble:/ɪɡˈnoʊbl/| adj. |卑鄙的；不光彩的；低贱的 |lacking nobility in character or purpose; dishonorable.  |例句：His ignoble actions led to his downfall. 他的卑鄙行为导致了他的垮台。

例句：The ignoble lie was designed to manipulate public opinion.  这个卑鄙的谎言是为了操纵公众舆论。


近义词：base, mean, dishonorable, shameful, undignified

反义词：noble, honorable, glorious, dignified


词源：来自古法语 *ignoble，源自拉丁语 *in- + *nobilis，“不+高贵的”。
