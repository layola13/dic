jugs:/dʒʌɡz/| n.|罐，壶|A jug is a container, typically made of ceramic or glass, with a handle and a lip, used for holding and pouring liquids.  It is usually larger than a cup but smaller than a large pot.|例句：She poured the juice from the jug into glasses. 她把果汁从罐子里倒进杯子里。

例句：He filled the jug with water from the well. 他用井水灌满了罐子。


近义词：pitcher, carafe, ewer|反义词：None specifically.  Antonyms would relate to the opposite of the *contents* of a jug, not the jug itself. |词性:名词
