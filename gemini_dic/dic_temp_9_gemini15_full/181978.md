rematch:/ˈriːmætʃ/ | n. v. | 重赛；再赛；再次较量 | A rematch is a contest or game that is played again, often because the first contest was not conclusive or because one of the participants wants a second chance.  It can also refer to a renewed confrontation or conflict. |例句：They demanded a rematch after the controversial ending of the first game.  他们要求在第一场比赛有争议的结局后进行重赛。

例句：The boxer requested a rematch to prove his worth. 拳击手要求重赛以证明自己的实力。


近义词：replay, repeat, second match | 反义词：final match, conclusive match | 词性: 名词和动词
