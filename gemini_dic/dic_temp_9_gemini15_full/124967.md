mugfuls:/ˈmʌɡfʊlz/| n. |满满几杯；许多杯|A mugful is the amount that a mug can hold.  The plural, "mugfuls," refers to multiple mug-sized quantities of something.  It often implies a large quantity.|例句：She drank several mugfuls of tea to warm up. 她喝了几大杯茶来暖和身子。


例句：He gulped down mugfuls of coffee throughout the morning.  他整个上午猛灌了好几大杯咖啡。

近义词：cups, quantities

反义词：drops, sips

词性:名词
