megavolt:/ˈmɛɡəˌvoʊlt/| n. |兆伏特|A megavolt (MV) is a unit of electrical potential difference or electromotive force equal to one million volts.  It's used to measure very high voltages, typically in power transmission and high-energy physics.|例句：The power lines carry electricity at megavolt levels.  输电线以兆伏特的电压输送电力。The particle accelerator operates at several megavolts. 粒子加速器以几兆伏特的电压运行。|近义词：million volts|反义词：millivolt, microvolt
