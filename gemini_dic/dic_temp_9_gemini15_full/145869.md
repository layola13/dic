overdescribes:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskraɪbz/ | vt. | 过度描述；详述得过于冗长 | To describe something in excessive detail, often to the point of being tedious or unnecessary.  | The author overdescribes the setting, slowing the pace of the narrative. 作者对场景的描写过于冗长，减慢了叙事的节奏。|  elaborates, details excessively, paints a picture (in too much detail) | understates, summarizes, simplifies | 动词
