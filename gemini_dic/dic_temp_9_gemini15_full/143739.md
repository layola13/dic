outluster: /aʊtˈlʌstər/ | vt. |胜过，超过（在光泽或光彩方面）| To surpass in luster or brilliance. |The polished silver outlustred all the other tableware.  抛光的银器比其他所有餐具都更有光泽。|近义词：outshine, surpass, excel |反义词：underperform, be eclipsed


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The diamond outlustres all other jewels.  这颗钻石的光泽胜过所有其他珠宝。
His new car outlustres mine. 他的新车比我的更有光泽。


一般过去时:
Her performance outlustred all expectations.  她的表演超出了所有人的预期（在光彩方面）。
The gold necklace outlustred the silver one. 金项链比银项链更有光泽。


现在完成时:
The sun has outlustred the moon tonight. 今晚太阳的光芒盖过了月亮。
His achievements have outlustred his previous work. 他的成就已超过了他以前的作品（在辉煌方面）。


过去完成时:
The painting had outlustred all other works in the exhibition.  这幅画的光彩在展览会上胜过了所有其他作品。
The renovated house had outlustred its neighbors. 翻修后的房子比邻居的房子更有光泽。


将来时:
The new technology will outluster the old methods. 新技术将胜过旧方法（在效率或效果方面）。
The upcoming movie is expected to outluster its predecessors.  即将上映的电影有望超过之前的作品（在票房或口碑方面）。


虚拟语气:
If only my writing could outluster his!  如果我的写作也能超过他该多好！
I wish my presentation could have outlustred the others.  我希望我的演示能比其他演示更精彩。


被动语态:
The old building was outlustred by the new skyscraper. 旧建筑的光彩被新的摩天大楼盖过了。
Her work was outlustred by the innovative designs of her competitor. 她的作品的光彩被竞争对手的创新设计所掩盖。


疑问句:
Does the silk outluster the satin? 丝绸的光泽是否胜过缎子？
Will this new design outluster the previous models?  这个新设计会比之前的型号更出色吗？


否定句:
The cheaper fabric did not outluster the more expensive one.  较便宜的织物的光泽不如较昂贵的织物。
His efforts did not outluster those of his colleagues.  他的努力没有超过他的同事们。
