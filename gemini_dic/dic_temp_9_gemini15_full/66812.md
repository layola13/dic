expansing:/ɪkˈspændɪŋ/ | adj. |膨胀的；扩张的；扩展的 | Expanding  means becoming larger or more extensive. It describes something that is growing in size, scope, or influence.  | The expanding universe is a concept in cosmology.  宇宙的膨胀是一个宇宙学概念。 |The company is experiencing an expanding market share. 公司正在经历市场份额的扩大。| contracting, shrinking | constricting, shrinking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The balloon is expanding. = 气球正在膨胀。
The company is expanding its operations. = 公司正在扩大业务。

一般过去时:
The universe expanded rapidly after the Big Bang. = 大爆炸后宇宙迅速膨胀。
The city expanded its borders. = 城市扩大了它的边界。

现在完成时:
The population has expanded significantly in recent years. = 近年来人口显著增长。
The company has expanded into new markets. = 公司已拓展到新的市场。

过去完成时:
The economy had expanded before the crisis hit. = 危机爆发前经济已经扩张。
The empire had expanded its territory through conquest. = 帝国通过征服扩张了其领土。

将来时:
The city will expand outwards in the next decade. = 未来十年，这座城市将向外扩张。
The business will expand its product line. = 这家企业将扩大其产品线。

虚拟语气:
If the economy expanded, more jobs would be created. = 如果经济扩张，就会创造更多的就业机会。
I wish my knowledge of this subject expanded. = 我希望我对这个主题的了解能够更广泛。

被动语态:
The bridge was expanded to accommodate more traffic. = 桥梁进行了扩建以容纳更多的交通流量。
The business's reach was expanded through effective marketing. = 通过有效的营销，该企业的覆盖范围得到了扩大。

疑问句:
Is the universe still expanding? = 宇宙还在膨胀吗？
Is the company planning to expand its workforce? = 公司计划扩大员工队伍吗？

否定句:
The metal did not expand when heated. = 金属受热时没有膨胀。
The business did not expand into that market. = 这家企业没有进入那个市场。
