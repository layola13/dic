self-evaluating: /ˌsɛlfɪˈvæljueɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我评价的，自我评估的 |  Describing something or someone capable of assessing their own performance, qualities, or progress.  It implies an inherent mechanism or capacity for internal critique and improvement. |

例句：

The self-evaluating software automatically grades the student's answers.  = 自我评估软件会自动批改学生的答案。

This self-evaluating system allows employees to identify their strengths and weaknesses. = 这个自我评估系统允许员工识别自己的优势和劣势。


近义词：self-assessing, introspective, reflective


反义词：uncritical, unreflective, externally evaluated


词性：形容词
