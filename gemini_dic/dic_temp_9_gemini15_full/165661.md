It typically functions as an adjective or adverb.  | Example:  The "quickly running" dog chased the ball. (Here, "quickly running" is a premodifying phrase acting as an adjective modifying "dog"). | 近义词: modifying, qualifying, descriptive | 反义词: postmodifying
