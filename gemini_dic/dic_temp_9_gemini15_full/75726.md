full-timer:/ˈfʊlˌtaɪmər/| n. |全职员工| A person who works full-time.  A full-timer is someone employed to work the standard number of hours per week for their job, as opposed to a part-timer.|Example:  She's a full-timer at the company, working 40 hours a week. 她公司里是全职员工，每周工作40小时。|近义词：full-time employee, regular employee|反义词：part-timer

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a full-timer at the bank. = 他是银行的全职员工。
She works as a full-timer. = 她做全职工作。

He was a full-timer before he started his own business. = 在他创业之前，他是一名全职员工。
