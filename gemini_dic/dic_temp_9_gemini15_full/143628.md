outjourneys:/ˈaʊtˌdʒɜːrniz/ | n. |外出旅行；远行 |A long journey away from home, often involving exploration or adventure.  The word is not a standard dictionary word; it's a combination of "out" and "journeys," creating a sense of journeys that are outward-bound and extensive.|例句：Their outjourneys took them to remote corners of the globe.他们的远行带他们去了全球偏远的角落。


Their outjourneys were meticulously planned, encompassing both historical sites and natural wonders.他们的远行经过精心策划，涵盖了历史遗迹和自然奇观。|近义词：expeditions, explorations, travels, voyages |反义词：homecomings, returns
