procidence:/ˈprɑːvɪdəns/| n. |天意；神意；预见；远见；节俭|Providence, in its broadest sense, refers to the protective care of God or of a superior power.  It can also denote foresight, prudence, or providential care.  The word often implies a benevolent intervention in human affairs.|例句：The farmer attributed his successful harvest to providence.  农民把丰收归功于天意。


例句：Her providence in saving money allowed her to buy a house.她的节俭让她买了一套房子。


近义词：foresight, prudence, foresight,  divine intervention


反义词：negligence, recklessness, improvidence


词性：名词
