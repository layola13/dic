nourish: /ˈnɜːrɪʃ/ | vt. |滋养；培养；鼓励；|to provide with the food or other substances necessary for growth, health, and good condition|例句：A healthy diet helps nourish the body. 健康的饮食有助于滋养身体。|近义词：feed, nurture, foster |反义词：starve, deplete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer nourishes his crops with fertilizer. = 农民用肥料滋养他的庄稼。
She nourishes her plants regularly. = 她定期给植物浇水施肥。


一般过去时:
He nourished his body with plenty of fruits and vegetables. = 他用大量的水果和蔬菜来滋养他的身体。
The teacher nourished her students' creativity. = 老师培养了学生们的创造力。


现在完成时:
They have nourished their garden with compost for years. = 他们多年来一直用堆肥来滋养他们的花园。
The company has nourished its employees' talent through training programs. = 公司通过培训项目培养了员工的才能。


过去完成时:
She had nourished her hope for many years before it finally came true. = 在她的希望最终实现之前，她已经默默地守护了多年。
The soil had been nourished by the previous year's crops. = 土壤已经被前一年的庄稼养分滋养过了。


将来时:
We will nourish our bodies with healthy food. = 我们将用健康的食物来滋养我们的身体。
The government will nourish the economy with new policies. = 政府将用新的政策来发展经济。


虚拟语气:
If I had nourished my body better, I wouldn't be so weak now. = 如果我以前更好地保养身体，现在就不会这么虚弱了。
If she had nourished her talent, she would have become a great artist. = 如果她以前好好培养自己的天赋，她就会成为一位伟大的艺术家。


被动语态:
The plants were nourished by the rain. = 植物被雨水滋润了。
The economy is being nourished by government investment. = 经济正在得到政府投资的扶持。


疑问句:
How do you nourish your skin? = 你如何保养你的皮肤？
What methods are used to nourish the soil? = 使用什么方法来滋养土壤？


否定句:
He didn't nourish his body properly. = 他没有好好保养身体。
The company failed to nourish its employees' morale. = 公司未能提升员工的士气。
