outpunching:/ˌaʊtˈpʌntʃɪŋ/| v.| 超过…的拳击能力；以更强的拳击能力击败 | To defeat someone by hitting them harder and more effectively than they hit you.  |例句：The smaller boxer surprised everyone by outpunching his much larger opponent.  更小的拳击手以比他大得多的对手更强的拳击能力击败了他，这令所有人大吃一惊。

例句：He consistently outpunched his rivals throughout the match.  在整场比赛中，他始终以更强的拳击能力击败他的对手。


近义词：outbox, overwhelm (in boxing), surpass (in punching power)
反义词：be outpunched by, lose to (in boxing)
词性:动词
