cellist:/ˈtʃɛlɪst/| n. |大提琴演奏家|A cellist is a musician who plays the cello.  Cellists are known for their ability to produce a wide range of tones and emotions on the instrument. They often perform in orchestras, chamber groups, and as soloists.|例句：Yo-Yo Ma is a world-renowned cellist.  (马友友是一位世界著名的琵琶演奏家。)  The orchestra's principal cellist gave a stunning solo performance.
