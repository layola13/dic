forepast:/ˈfɔːrˌpɑːst/| adj.|过去的，往昔的|  Of or belonging to a time before the present; past.  The word is archaic and rarely used in modern English. It emphasizes a time significantly further in the past than simply "past".|例句：In the forepast ages, civilizations rose and fell.  在过去的年代里，文明兴衰更迭。

The forepast glories of the empire are now just legends. 帝国过去的辉煌如今只是传说。

近义词：bygone, past, ancient, former


反义词：future, upcoming


词性:形容词
