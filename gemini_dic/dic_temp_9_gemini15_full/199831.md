sitar:/ˈsɪtɑːr/| n. |西塔琴|A sitar is a plucked stringed instrument from the Indian subcontinent, with a long neck and a distinctive sound.  It's known for its use in Hindustani classical music.|例句：The musician played a beautiful raga on the sitar.  演奏家用西塔琴演奏了一段优美的拉格.|近义词：tanpura (a related Indian instrument)|反义词：None|名词
