in the end:/ɪn ði ɛnd/|adv.|最终，最后|Ultimately; finally.  It emphasizes the final outcome after a period of time or a series of events.|例句：In the end, he decided to accept the job.  最终，他决定接受这份工作。
例句：After many setbacks, they succeeded in the end. 经过多次挫折，他们最终成功了。|近义词：finally, eventually, ultimately|反义词：initially, at first
