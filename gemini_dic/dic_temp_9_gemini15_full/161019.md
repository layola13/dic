postcardinal:/ˌpoʊstˈkɑːrdɪnəl/ | adj. |后基数的，次基数的 | Relating to or denoting numbers that are greater than the cardinal numbers.  These numbers typically represent ordinal positions beyond the basic cardinal count.  For example, while "third" is an ordinal number, a post-cardinal number might describe the third-from-the-last element in a series. |The research paper discusses postcardinal numbers in set theory.  | 近义词：transfinite (in certain contexts) | 反义词：cardinal (in the context of number classification)
