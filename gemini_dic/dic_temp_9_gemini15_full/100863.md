introversion:/ˈɪntrəvɜːrʒən/| n. |内向；内倾性 |The state of being introverted; a personality trait characterized by a preference for solitary activities and limited social interaction. Introversion is often contrasted with extroversion.  |Example: Her introversion made it difficult for her to network at conferences.  她的内向使她在会议上难以拓展人脉。|近义词：shyness, reserve, reticence |反义词：extroversion, sociability |名词
