obtainer: /əbˈteɪnər/ | n. | 获得者，取得者 | A person who obtains something.  Someone who gets or acquires something. | Example: The obtainer of the award was visibly thrilled.  获得奖项的人显然非常激动。 |近义词: recipient, acquirer, receiver | 反义词: loser, relinquisher


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The obtainer collects the prize. = 获得者领取奖品。
He is a successful obtainer of contracts. = 他是成功的合同获得者。


一般过去时:
The obtainer quickly signed the documents. = 获得者迅速签署了文件。
She was the obtainer of the scholarship. = 她获得了奖学金。


现在完成时:
He has been a consistent obtainer of top marks. = 他一直以来都是取得最高分数的人。
The company has successfully become an obtainer of government grants. = 这家公司成功获得了政府拨款。


过去完成时:
The obtainer had already secured the resources before the deadline. = 获得者在截止日期前就已经获得了资源。
Before the announcement, she had been a consistent obtainer of positive feedback. = 在宣布结果之前，她一直获得积极的反馈。


将来时:
The obtainer will receive the notification soon. = 获得者很快就会收到通知。
They will be the obtainer of the prestigious award. = 他们将获得这个著名的奖项。


虚拟语气:
If he were the obtainer, he would donate it to charity. = 如果他是获得者，他会把它捐给慈善机构。
I wish I were the obtainer of that lottery prize. = 我希望我是那个彩票奖的获得者。


被动语态:
The prize was obtained by a lucky obtainer. = 奖品被一个幸运的获得者获得。
The contract was finally obtained by a seasoned obtainer. = 合同最终被一个经验丰富的获得者获得。


疑问句:
Who is the obtainer of the first prize? = 一等奖的获得者是谁？
Is she a reliable obtainer of information? = 她是一个可靠的信息获得者吗？


否定句:
He is not a typical obtainer of high scores. = 他不是一个典型的获得高分的人。
The company was not the obtainer of the contract. = 这家公司没有获得合同。
