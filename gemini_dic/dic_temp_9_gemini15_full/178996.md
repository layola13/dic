redefines:/ˌriːˈdifaɪnz/| vt.|重新定义，改写定义|To redefine something means to define it again, often in a new or different way.  It implies a change or revision to the existing definition.| The company redefined its brand image to appeal to a younger audience. 公司重新定义了其品牌形象以吸引更年轻的受众。  The discovery redefined our understanding of the universe.  这一发现重新定义了我们对宇宙的理解。|redefine, reinterpret, re-evaluate|conform, accept|动词
