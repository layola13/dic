rectangled:/ˈrɛktæŋɡld/| adj.|矩形的，成矩形的|Having the shape of a rectangle.  A rectangled object has four sides, with opposite sides being equal and parallel, and four right angles.|例句：The garden is perfectly rectangled. 花园是完美的矩形。

The building has a rectangled footprint. 这座建筑的平面图是矩形的。


近义词：rectangular, square (in the case of a square, which is a special type of rectangle)


反义词：circular, round, oval
