parentless:/ˈpɛrəntləs/| adj.|无父母的，孤儿的|Lacking parents; orphaned. The adjective describes a person or animal without parents, often implying a state of vulnerability or hardship.|例句：The parentless child was taken in by a loving family.  无父母的孩子被一个慈爱的家庭收养了。|例句：The parentless kitten was found wandering the streets.  这只无父母的小猫被发现流落在街头。|近义词：orphaned, fatherless, motherless|反义词：parented
