The word "parelle" is not a standard English word.  The correct spelling is **parallel**.  The following analysis is for the word "parallel".


parallel:/ˈpærəlel/| adj., n.|平行的；类似的；相似的事物|The two roads run parallel to each other. 这两条路彼此平行。|近义词：concurrent, simultaneous, analogous|反义词：divergent, intersecting


英文解析:

* **As an adjective:**  Meaning "extending in the same direction, equidistant at all points," or "similar or analogous; comparable."

* **As a noun:** Meaning "a line, plane, surface, or the like, that is everywhere equidistant from another," or "something that is parallel or analogous to something else."


例句:

* **Adjective:**
The two rivers flow parallel for many miles.  这两条河平行流淌了许多英里。

The findings of the two studies are largely parallel. 这两项研究的结果在很大程度上是相似的。


* **Noun:**
We drew a parallel between the two situations. 我们在这两种情况下进行了类比。

There is no parallel to this achievement in history. 历史上没有与之相比的成就。
