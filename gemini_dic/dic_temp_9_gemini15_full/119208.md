/miː/|pron.|什么，什么东西|It refers to something unknown or unspecified.  It can be used in questions, exclamations, and relative clauses.|What is your name?|近义词：which, what kind of|反义词:N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？


一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you done today? = 你今天做了什么？
What has she accomplished? = 她取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you eaten before you felt sick? = 你在感到恶心之前吃了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始之前他们讨论了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like? = 天气会怎么样？


虚拟语气:
I wish I knew what to do. = 我真希望我知道该做什么。
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了些什么？
What is being done to help the victims? = 正在采取什么措施来帮助受害者？


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道该说什么。
I didn't understand what he meant. = 我不明白他的意思。


PHRASE->
what about = ...怎么样？
what if = 如果...怎么办？
what's up = 怎么了？/你好吗？
no matter what = 无论如何
what for = 为什么
