knapweed:/ˈnæpwiːd/| n. |蓝盆花，糙苏|A plant of the genus Centaurea, typically having purple flower heads.  Many knapweed species are considered invasive weeds.|The field was overgrown with knapweed.|近义词：Centaurea (genus name),  (depending on specific species) thistle, weed|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with desirable cultivated plants.
