heterothermic:/ˌhɛtərəˈθɜːrmɪk/| adj.|异温的；变温的|Having variable body temperature.  The body temperature fluctuates with the environmental temperature.  This is in contrast to homeothermic animals, which maintain a constant internal body temperature.  |例句：Many reptiles are heterothermic, meaning their body temperature changes depending on the surrounding environment.  许多爬行动物是变温动物，这意味着它们的体温会根据周围环境的变化而变化。


例句：Bats are an example of heterothermic mammals, exhibiting torpor in cold conditions. 蝙蝠是异温哺乳动物的一个例子，在寒冷的环境中会表现出蛰伏状态。


近义词：poikilothermic (more precise term for animals)|反义词：homeothermic


词性：形容词
