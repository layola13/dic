imbraid:/ɪmˈbreɪd/| v.|编织；装饰（尤指用彩色线）|To decorate (something, especially cloth) with interwoven threads of a different color.|The artist imbraided the tapestry with intricate patterns. 艺术家用复杂的图案编织了挂毯。|近义词：embroider, weave, decorate|反义词：strip, remove


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She imbraids beautiful patterns into her quilts. = 她在她做的被子上绣制美丽的图案。
He imbraids the fabric with gold thread. = 他用金线在织物上绣花。


一般过去时:
My grandmother imbraided many intricate designs into her clothing. = 我祖母在她衣服上绣了很多复杂的图案。
They imbraided the edges of the blanket with colorful yarn. = 他们用彩色的纱线绣了毯子的边缘。
