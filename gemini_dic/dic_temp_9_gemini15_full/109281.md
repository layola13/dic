lemonade:/ˌlɛməˈneɪd/| n. |柠檬水|A sweet drink made from lemon juice, water, and sugar.  It is often served chilled. |例句：I made a pitcher of lemonade for the picnic. 我为野餐做了一大壶柠檬水。|例句：The lemonade was so refreshing on a hot day.  炎热的天气里，柠檬水真是令人耳目一新。|近义词：lemon squash, juice |反义词：无

词源:
The word "lemonade" comes from the French word "limonade," which in turn is derived from "limon," meaning "lemon."  The word entered English in the 17th century.
