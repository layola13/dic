impair:/ɪmˈpɛər/| vt.|损害；削弱 |To weaken or damage something, especially a person's body or mind.  |例句：Excessive alcohol consumption can impair your judgment.  过量饮酒会损害你的判断力。|例句：The storm impaired communications.  暴风雨中断了通讯。|近义词：weaken, damage, harm, deteriorate|反义词：improve, enhance, strengthen


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud music impairs my concentration. = 嘈杂的音乐影响我的注意力。
Smoking impairs lung function. = 吸烟会损害肺功能。

一般过去时:
The accident impaired his vision. = 那次事故损害了他的视力。
The heavy rain impaired the road conditions. = 大雨恶化了路况。

现在完成时:
Years of neglect have impaired the building's structure. = 多年的忽视损坏了建筑物的结构。
Stress has impaired her ability to sleep well. = 压力已经损害了她好好睡觉的能力。

过去完成时:
The previous injury had impaired his athletic performance. = 先前的伤势已经影响了他的运动成绩。
The long drought had impaired the crops' growth. = 长期的干旱已经损害了农作物的生长。

将来时:
Excessive use of this medication will impair your liver function. = 过量使用这种药物会损害你的肝功能。
Lack of sleep will impair your cognitive abilities. = 睡眠不足会损害你的认知能力。

虚拟语气:
If he continued to drink so much, it would impair his health. = 如果他继续喝这么多酒，那将损害他的健康。
I wish the noise wouldn't impair my ability to concentrate. = 我希望噪音不会影响我的注意力。

被动语态:
His hearing was impaired by the explosion. = 他的听力受到了爆炸的损害。
Their performance was impaired by technical difficulties. = 他们的表现受到了技术问题的阻碍。

疑问句:
Does smoking impair your health? = 吸烟会损害你的健康吗？
Will this medicine impair my driving ability? = 这药会影响我的驾驶能力吗？

否定句:
The medication did not impair his cognitive function. = 这种药物没有损害他的认知功能。
The weather did not impair our travel plans. = 天气没有影响我们的旅行计划。


PHRASE->
impair judgment = 损害判断力
impair function = 损害功能
impair vision = 损害视力
impair hearing = 损害听力
impair performance = 损害表现


词源:
From Old French *empeirer, from empeirer "to worsen," from em- "in, into" + peirer "to make worse," from Vulgar Latin *pejorare, from Latin peior "worse."
