reissues:/ˌriːˈɪʃuːz/| n. v.|再版；重新发行|The act of issuing something again, such as a book, record, or software.  It can also refer to the items themselves that are re-issued.|例句：The band announced the reissues of their early albums. 乐队宣布了他们早期专辑的再版。

The company is planning to reissue the popular game next year. 公司计划明年重新发行这款热门游戏。|近义词：reprints, re-releases|反义词：first edition, original release|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reissues old albums every year. = 公司每年都会重新发行旧专辑。
He reissues his books frequently. = 他经常再版他的书。

一般过去时:
They reissued the classic novel last month. = 他们上个月重新发行了这部经典小说。
The record label reissued the singer's first album. = 唱片公司重新发行了这位歌手的首张专辑。

现在完成时:
They have reissued several of their most popular titles. = 他们已经重新发行了几部最受欢迎的书。
The artist has reissued her debut album with bonus tracks. =这位艺术家已经重新发行她的首张专辑，并增加了额外曲目。

过去完成时:
Before the concert, they had reissued his greatest hits album. = 在音乐会之前，他们已经重新发行了他的精选集。
The publisher had reissued the book several times before it became a bestseller. = 出版商在该书成为畅销书之前已经再版了好几次。

将来时:
They will reissue the movie soundtrack next year. = 他们明年将重新发行这部电影的原声带。
The band will reissue their early demos as a limited edition box set. = 乐队将把他们早期的演示作为限量版套装重新发行。

虚拟语气:
If they reissued the album, it would sell well. = 如果他们重新发行这张专辑，它将会卖得很好。
I wish they would reissue that particular song. = 我希望他们能重新发行那首歌。

被动语态:
The book was reissued with a new cover. = 这本书重新发行时换了新的封面。
The album is being reissued with bonus tracks. = 这张专辑正在重新发行，并增加了额外的曲目。

疑问句:

否定句:
They did not reissue the album. = 他们没有重新发行这张专辑。
