fleecing:/ˈfliːsɪŋ/| n. v.|欺诈；剥削；剪羊毛|The act of defrauding someone of money or property, often by trickery or deception; also, the act of shearing a sheep.  |例句：He accused the company of fleecing customers with exorbitant prices. 他指控该公司以过高的价格欺诈顾客。

例句：The farmer was fleecing his sheep in preparation for winter. 这位农民正在为冬天准备而剪羊毛。


近义词：swindling, cheating, defrauding, robbing, shearing

反义词：benefiting, assisting, helping
