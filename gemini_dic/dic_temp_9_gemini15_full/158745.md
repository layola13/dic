plow:/plaʊ/| vt. n.|耕地，犁地；辛勤工作|to turn over and break up the soil of (land) with a plow; to work hard and steadily|The farmer plowed the field before planting.  农民在播种前耕种了田地。|They plowed through their work to meet the deadline. 他们辛勤工作以赶上最后期限。|cultivate, till|idle, rest|动词
