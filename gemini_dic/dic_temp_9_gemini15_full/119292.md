micro-environment:/ˌmaɪkroʊɪnˈvaɪrənmənt/| n. |微环境，局部环境|The immediate surroundings of something or someone, especially as it affects their development or survival.  It refers to a smaller, localized environment within a larger environment.  For example, the soil around a plant's roots is its micro-environment.|Example: The micro-environment within the rainforest canopy is significantly different from the forest floor. 雨林树冠内的微环境与森林地面有很大不同。  The bacteria's micro-environment within the human gut influences its behavior. 人体肠道内细菌的微环境影响着它的行为。|近义词：local environment, immediate surroundings, niche|反义词：macro-environment, global environment|名词
