improving: /ɪmˈpruːvɪŋ/ | v. |改进；改善；提高 |  The act of making something better or more satisfactory.  It often implies a gradual process of enhancement. |例句：He is improving his English skills by reading extensively. 他通过广泛阅读来提高英语技能。

例句：The company is constantly improving its products to meet customer demands. 公司不断改进产品以满足客户需求。


近义词：betterment, enhancement, amelioration | 反义词：worsening, deteriorating

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are improving their communication skills. = 他们在提高他们的沟通技巧。
The weather is improving slowly. = 天气正在慢慢好转。


一般过去时:
She improved her performance significantly. = 她显著地提高了她的表现。
The situation improved after the intervention. = 干预后情况有所好转。


现在完成时:
They have improved their productivity dramatically. = 他们极大地提高了生产力。
The team has improved its performance over the past year. = 团队在过去一年中提高了它的表现。


过去完成时:
He had improved his technique before the competition. = 他在比赛前已经提高了他的技术。
The economy had improved before the recent crisis. = 在最近的危机之前，经济有所好转。


将来时:
We will improve our service in the near future. = 我们将在不久的将来改进我们的服务。
The technology will improve significantly in the coming years. = 未来几年这项技术将得到显著改进。


虚拟语气:
If we improved our marketing strategy, we would attract more customers. = 如果我们改进我们的营销策略，我们将吸引更多客户。
I wish I had improved my study habits earlier. = 我希望我早点改掉我的学习习惯。


被动语态:
The system is being improved to enhance security. = 该系统正在改进以增强安全性。
The design was improved based on customer feedback. =  设计根据客户反馈得到了改进。


疑问句:
How can we improve this process? = 我们如何改进这个流程？
Are you improving your health through exercise? = 你通过锻炼来改善你的健康吗？


否定句:
The situation isn't improving. = 情况没有好转。
He didn't improve his grades despite his efforts. = 尽管他努力了，他的成绩却没有提高。



PHRASE->
improving health = 改善健康
improving skills = 提升技能
improving quality = 提高质量
improving efficiency = 提高效率
improving performance = 提升表现
