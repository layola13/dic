paralegals:/ˈpærəliːɡəlz/ | n. | 法律助理，律师助理 |  Paralegals assist lawyers with various legal tasks. 法律助理协助律师处理各种法律事务。 | 近义词：legal assistant, law clerk | 反义词：lawyer (in terms of qualification and responsibility)

词源:
The word "paralegal" is a blend of "para" (meaning "beside" or "alongside") and "legal."  It indicates someone working alongside a lawyer, performing legal tasks but not having the full qualifications of a lawyer.
