sandstones:/ˈsændstoʊnz/| n. |砂岩|A sedimentary rock composed mainly of sand-sized minerals or rock fragments.  Sandstones are clastic sedimentary rocks, meaning they are composed of fragments of other rocks. They are typically composed of quartz and feldspar, but can also contain other minerals and rock fragments.| Example: The ancient castle was built from strong sandstones. 这座古老的城堡是用坚固的砂岩建造的。|近义词：sandrock|反义词：无
