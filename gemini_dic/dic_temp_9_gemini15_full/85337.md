harpsichord:/ˈhɑːrpɪsɪkɔːrd/| n. |羽管键琴|A harpsichord is a stringed keyboard instrument.  It produces sound when plectra (small pieces of quill, plastic, or leather) pluck the strings. Unlike the piano, the harpsichord does not have hammers striking the strings, resulting in a different timbre and dynamic range.  It was a very popular instrument in the Baroque period.|The harpsichord was the dominant keyboard instrument in the Baroque era.|近义词：clavichord, spinet|反义词：piano (in terms of sound production mechanism)
