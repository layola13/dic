chromatin:/ˈkroʊmətɪn/ | n. | 染色质 |  The complex of DNA and proteins that makes up chromosomes.  Chromatin is found in the nucleus of eukaryotic cells and is responsible for packaging and organizing the genome.  Its structure varies depending on the cell cycle stage and gene expression activity. | Example:  The structure of chromatin changes during cell division. 染色质的结构在细胞分裂过程中会发生变化。  The researchers studied the effects of histone modifications on chromatin structure. 研究人员研究了组蛋白修饰对染色质结构的影响。 |近义词:chromosome (in some contexts), nucleoprotein | 反义词:  None directly; concepts like "uncondensed DNA" are related but not true antonyms.
