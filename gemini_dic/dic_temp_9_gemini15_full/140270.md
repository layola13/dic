offstage:/ˌɒfˈsteɪdʒ/ /ˌɑːfˈsteɪdʒ/| adj.|幕后的；台下的；不在舞台上的|Away from the stage; out of sight of the audience.  The term is commonly used in the context of theatre, but can also be applied more broadly to situations where something is happening outside of the main focus or view.|例句：The actors waited offstage until their cue.  演员们在后台等待他们的提示。

例句：The orchestra played offstage during the scene.  乐队在场景表演期间在后台演奏。


近义词：backstage, behind the scenes, out of sight|反义词：onstage, in view
