chor:/kɔːr/| n.|合唱团；合唱队|A choir is a group of singers, especially one that performs choral music.  |Example: The church choir sang beautifully at the wedding. 教堂合唱团在婚礼上唱得非常优美。|近义词：chorus, ensemble|反义词：soloist
