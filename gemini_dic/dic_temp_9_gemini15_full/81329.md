graphene:/ˈɡræfiːn/| n. |石墨烯|A single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a hexagonal lattice.  It is known for its exceptional strength, conductivity, and flexibility. |Example: Graphene is a wonder material with potential applications in many fields. 石墨烯是一种具有巨大潜力的神奇材料，在许多领域都有应用前景。|近义词：carbon sheet (less precise)|反义词：无
