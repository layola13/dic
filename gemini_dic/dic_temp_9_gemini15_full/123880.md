morbid:/ˈmɔːrbɪd/| adj.|病态的；令人毛骨悚然的；死亡的；阴郁的 |Characterized by or appealing to an abnormal and unhealthy interest in disturbing and unpleasant subjects, especially death and disease.  It often suggests a fascination with the gruesome or macabre. |例句：He has a morbid fascination with serial killers. 他对连环杀手有着病态的迷恋。

例句：Her morbid curiosity led her to investigate the abandoned house.  她病态的好奇心驱使她去调查那座废弃的房子。


近义词：gruesome, gruesome, macabre, ghastly, unhealthy, sick

反义词：healthy, wholesome, cheerful, optimistic
