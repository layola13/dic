ophiuroidea:/oʊˈfjʊrɔɪdiə/| n. |蛇尾纲动物|Ophiuroidea is a class of echinoderms, characterized by their slender, snake-like arms, which are sharply distinct from the central disc. They are commonly known as brittle stars or serpent stars. They are found in various marine habitats worldwide.| Example:  Many species of ophiuroidea inhabit the deep sea. 许多蛇尾纲动物栖息在深海。|近义词: brittle stars, serpent stars |反义词: None
