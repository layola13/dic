response:/rɪˈspɒns/ /rɪˈspɑːns/| n. v.|回应；反应；答复|An answer or reaction.|The customer's response to the advertisement was overwhelming. 客户对广告的反应极其热烈。|answer, reply, reaction|indifference, silence|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He responds to emails promptly. 他迅速回复邮件。
The plant responds well to sunlight.  这植物对阳光反应良好。


一般过去时:
She responded to my question politely. 她礼貌地回答了我的问题。
The government responded quickly to the crisis. 政府迅速回应了这场危机。


现在完成时:
Have you responded to their offer yet? 你已经回复他们的提议了吗？
The company has responded positively to the market changes. 公司对市场变化做出了积极的回应。


过去完成时:
He had already responded before I could ask.  在我开口之前他已经回应了。
They had responded to the emergency call promptly. 他们迅速回应了紧急呼叫。


将来时:
I will respond to your email tomorrow. 我明天会回复你的邮件。
The company will respond to the customer's complaint soon. 公司很快会回复客户的投诉。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have responded differently. 如果我知道的话，我会给出不同的回应。
I wish I had responded more thoughtfully. 我希望我当时能更周全地回应。


被动语态:
The email was responded to immediately.  邮件得到了立即回复。
The proposal was poorly responded to.  这个提案的反响很差。


疑问句:
How did he respond to your request? 他如何回应你的请求？
What is the expected response time? 预计的响应时间是多少？


否定句:
He didn't respond to my call. 他没有回应我的电话。
She failed to respond to the criticism. 她没有回应批评。


PHRASE->

make a response  做出回应
