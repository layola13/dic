repayer:/rɪˈpeɪər/| v.|偿还，赔偿；修理，修复|to pay back (money) that one owes; to restore something to good condition|例句：I need to repay my student loan. = 我需要偿还我的学生贷款。

例句：The mechanic repaired my car engine. = 技工修理了我的汽车发动机。

近义词：refund, reimburse, mend, fix |反义词：borrow, damage, break


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repays his debts on time. = 他按时偿还债务。
She repays her kindness with loyalty. = 她以忠诚来报答她的恩情。


一般过去时:
They repaid the favor promptly. = 他们迅速地报答了这份好意。
He repaid the money he owed. = 他还清了他欠的钱。


现在完成时:
I have repaid all my loans. = 我已经还清了所有的贷款。
She has repaid the debt to her family. = 她已经还清了对家人的债务。


过去完成时:
They had repaid the loan before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已还清了贷款。
He had repaid the money he borrowed from his friend. = 他已经还清了他从朋友那里借的钱。


将来时:
I will repay you next week. = 我下周会还你钱。
She will repay the kindness shown to her. = 她会报答对她表示的善意。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would repay you immediately. = 如果我有钱，我会立即还你。
I wish I could repay you for your help. = 我希望我能报答你的帮助。


被动语态:
The debt will be repaid in installments. = 债务将分期偿还。
The damage to the car was repaid by the insurance company. = 汽车的损坏由保险公司赔偿。


疑问句:
When will you repay the loan? = 你什么时候还贷款？
Has he repaid the money yet? = 他已经还钱了吗？


否定句:
He didn't repay the money he borrowed. = 他没有偿还他借的钱。
She hasn't repaid the debt yet. = 她还没有偿还债务。

PHRASE->
repay a debt = 还债
repay a loan = 还贷款
repay a favor = 报答恩情
repay kindness = 报答善意
repay the cost = 偿还成本
