modestly:/ˈmɒdəstli/ /ˈmɑːdəstli/ | adv. | 适度地；谦虚地；谨慎地 | In a modest manner; without excessive claims or pretensions.  | 例句：He modestly accepted the award. 他谦虚地接受了奖项。

例句：She modestly described her achievements. 她谦逊地描述了自己的成就。


近义词：humbly, unassumingly, discreetly, sparingly

反义词：boastfully, arrogantly, ostentatiously, extravagantly


词源:  From Middle English *modestly, from Old French modestement, from Latin modestus (see modest).
