resyntheses:/ˌriːˈsɪnθɪsiːz/| n.| 再合成；重新合成 |The process of combining elements or components to create something new, often based on previous synthesis.  It implies a reworking or refinement of a previous synthesis.  | Example: The resynthesis of the historical data revealed new trends.  再合成历史数据揭示了新的趋势。 |近义词：reconstruction, recomposition, re-creation|反义词：analysis, decomposition|名词
