词源:
The word "evincible" comes from the Latin word "invincibilis," which is a combination of the prefix "in-" (not) and "vincibilis" (conquerable).  Therefore, "invincibilis" literally means "not conquerable."  The English word "evincible" is a less common variant, likely influenced by the related word "vincible".
