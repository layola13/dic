misallot:/ˌmɪsˈælɒt/| vt.|分配错误，错配|To allocate incorrectly or unfairly; to distribute wrongly.|例句：The company misallotted funds to the project, resulting in a budget shortfall. 公司错误地分配了项目的资金，导致预算不足。
例句：The teacher misallotted the students to different groups, causing confusion.老师把学生分到了不同的组，造成了混乱。|近义词：misappropriate, misapply, misdistribute|反义词：allocate properly, distribute fairly|动词
