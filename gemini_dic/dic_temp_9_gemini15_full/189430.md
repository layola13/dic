sailboats:/ˈseɪlboʊts/ | n. | 帆船 | A boat propelled by sails.  Sailboats are popular for recreational use and racing. |例句：The sailboats glided gracefully across the calm water.  那些帆船优雅地滑过平静的水面。

例句：We saw many colorful sailboats during our trip to the coast.  我们在去海边的旅行中看到了许多色彩鲜艳的帆船。


近义词：sailing vessels, yachts (for larger, more luxurious sailboats) | 反义词：motorboats, powerboats


词性: 名词
