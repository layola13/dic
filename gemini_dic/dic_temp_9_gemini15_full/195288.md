semiheretics:/ˌsɛmiˈhɛrɪtɪks/| n.|半异端分子；持部分异端学说者 |Individuals who hold some, but not all, of the beliefs considered heretical within a particular religious or ideological system.  They may deviate from orthodox doctrine in certain aspects but not to the extent of being considered full-fledged heretics.  |The semiheretics were tolerated within the church, as long as they didn't openly preach their dissenting views.|近义词：schismatic, dissenter|反义词：orthodox believer
