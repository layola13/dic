mandatories:/ˈmæn.dəˌtɔːriz/| n.|强制性事物，义务；授权书|Things that are mandatory are compulsory; they must be done.  The term can also refer to documents granting authority.|例句：Wearing seatbelts is mandatory in most countries. = 在大多数国家，系安全带是强制性的。
The UN mandate for peacekeeping operations in the region was renewed. = 联合国在该地区的维和行动授权得到延期。|近义词：requirements, obligations, directives|反义词：optionals, electives


USAGE->
一般现在时:
These regulations are mandatory. = 这些规章是强制性的。
The rules are mandatory for all participants. = 这些规则对所有参与者都是强制性的。

一般过去时:
Attendance at the meeting was mandatory. = 参加会议是强制性的。
The governor issued a mandatory evacuation order. = 州长发布了强制疏散令。

现在完成时:
The company has made safety training mandatory for all employees. = 公司已将安全培训列为所有员工的强制性培训。
Several new laws have been made mandatory this year. = 今年已经实施了几项新的强制性法律。

过去完成时:
The judge had made it mandatory for the defendant to appear in court. = 法官已强制被告出庭。
Before the accident, helmet use hadn't been made mandatory. = 事故发生前，头盔使用并非强制性的。

将来时:
The new law will make it mandatory to install smoke detectors in all homes. = 新法律将强制要求所有房屋安装烟雾探测器。
Next year, the government will make wearing masks mandatory in public places. = 明年，政府将强制要求在公共场所佩戴口罩。

虚拟语气:
If it were mandatory, more people would participate. = 如果它是强制性的，更多的人会参与。
I wish it were mandatory to recycle in our city. = 我希望在我们城市强制回收利用。

被动语态:
The new rules were made mandatory last month. = 新规上个月已强制实施。
Many safety procedures are mandated by the government. = 许多安全程序都是政府强制规定的。

疑问句:
Is attendance at the training session mandatory? = 培训课程的出席是强制性的吗？
Are vaccinations mandatory for school children? = 学校儿童的疫苗接种是强制性的吗？

否定句:
Helmet use is not mandatory in this area. = 在这个区域，头盔使用并非强制性的。
The dress code is not mandatory for the event. = 此活动的着装规范并非强制性的。
