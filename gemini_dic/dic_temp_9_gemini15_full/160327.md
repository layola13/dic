pooping:/ˈpuːpɪŋ/| v.| 正在大便；拉屎 |The act of defecating.  It's a vulgar term for the process of eliminating solid waste from the body. |例句：The baby is pooping right now.  = 婴儿现在正在大便。
The dog is pooping in the garden. = 狗正在花园里大便。|近义词：defecating, stooling, excreting|反义词：urinating

He is pooping. = 他正在大便。
The dog poops in the garden every morning. = 这只狗每天早上都在花园里大便。

The baby pooped all over the floor. = 婴儿把大便拉得到处都是。
She pooped three times yesterday. = 她昨天拉了三次屎。

He had already pooped before we got home. = 我们到家之前他已经拉过大便了。
The child had pooped on the carpet before we noticed. = 我们注意到之前，孩子已经在大地毯上拉了大便。
