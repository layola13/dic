firmware:/ˈfɜːrmˌweər/ | n. |固件|Permanent software embedded into the memory of hardware devices.  It provides low-level control for the device's operations and is essential for its basic functionality. Unlike application software, firmware cannot be easily modified or deleted by the user.  It's typically updated through a process involving specialized tools or software. |Example:  The router's firmware needs updating to fix a security vulnerability. 路由器的固件需要更新才能修复安全漏洞。  The printer's firmware controls its print functions. 打印机的固件控制其打印功能。|近义词：embedded software, microcode|反义词：application software, software
