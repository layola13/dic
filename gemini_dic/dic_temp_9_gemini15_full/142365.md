orthophony:/ˈɔːrθɒfəni/ | n. | 正确发音；清晰发音 |The clear and correct pronunciation of words; the science or art of correct pronunciation.  It refers to the proper articulation and enunciation of speech sounds.  | Example: The speech therapist worked to improve the child's orthophony.  语音治疗师致力于改善孩子的清晰发音。 | 近义词: clear articulation, correct pronunciation, euphony | 反义词: dysphonia, slurred speech
