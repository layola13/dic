follow-the-leader:/ˈfɒləʊ ðə ˈliːdə(r)/| n. |模仿领袖者游戏；亦步亦趋；盲目效仿|A game where children copy the actions of a leader.  It can also refer to the act of blindly following someone else's actions or decisions without independent thought.|例句：The children played follow-the-leader in the park.孩子们在公园里玩模仿领袖者游戏。

例句：The company's follow-the-leader strategy led to its downfall. 公司的盲目效仿策略导致了它的垮台。|近义词：mimicry, imitation, emulation|反义词：independent thinking, originality, innovation|名词

一般过去时:
