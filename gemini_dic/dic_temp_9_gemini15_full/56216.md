dodson:/ˈdɒdsən/| n. |多德森（姓氏）|Dodson is a surname of English origin.  It is believed to be a topographic name for someone who lived near a type of enclosure or dwelling.  The name is derived from the Old English elements *dodde*, meaning "a tuft of grass," and *hūs*, meaning "house." Therefore, it could be interpreted as "house by the tuft of grass."|例句：The Dodson family has a long history in this town. 多德森家族在这个镇上有悠久的历史。|近义词：无直接近义词，因其为姓氏|反义词：无|名词
