rowback:/ˈroʊbæk/| v.|倒退；撤回；收回（声明等）|To reverse or retract something previously done or said, especially a decision or statement.  It often implies a return to a previous state or position.|例句：The company had to row back on its controversial advertising campaign after public outcry. 公司在公众抗议后不得不撤回其有争议的广告宣传活动。


例句：The government rowed back on its plans to increase taxes. 政府撤回了增税计划。


近义词：reverse, retract, rescind, revoke, withdraw


反义词：advance, proceed, confirm, uphold


词性：动词
