ratifies:/ˈrædəfaɪz/ | vt. |批准；认可；使正式生效| To give formal consent to (a treaty, contract, or agreement), making it officially valid.|The Senate will ratify the treaty next week. 参议院下周将批准该条约。|The government ratified the new trade agreement. 政府批准了新的贸易协定。|confirm, approve, sanction |reject, disapprove, annul|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The Senate ratifies the treaty. = 参议院批准该条约。
He ratifies the contract. = 他批准了合同。

一般过去时:
The president ratified the agreement. = 总统批准了该协议。
They ratified the decision. = 他们批准了这个决定。

现在完成时:
The treaty has been ratified. = 该条约已被批准。
They have ratified the amendments. = 他们已经批准了这些修正案。

过去完成时:
The committee had ratified the proposal before the meeting. = 委员会在会议前已批准了该提案。
She had ratified the contract before leaving the office. = 她离开办公室前已批准了合同。

将来时:
The council will ratify the new law next month. = 理事会下个月将批准这项新法律。
They will ratify the changes soon. = 他们很快就会批准这些修改。

虚拟语气:
If the government ratified the bill, it would become law. = 如果政府批准该法案，它就会成为法律。
I wish the court would ratify the decision quickly. = 我希望法院能迅速批准这项决定。

被动语态:
The agreement was ratified by the parliament. = 该协议已由议会批准。
The treaty is being ratified by the Senate. = 该条约正在由参议院批准。

疑问句:
Has the treaty been ratified yet? = 该条约已经批准了吗？
Will the agreement be ratified soon? = 该协议很快就会被批准吗？

否定句:
The court did not ratify the appeal. = 法院没有批准上诉。
The council will not ratify the proposal. = 理事会将不会批准该提案。


PHRASE->
ratify a treaty = 批准条约
ratify an agreement = 批准协议
ratify a decision = 批准决定
ratify an amendment = 批准修正案
ratify a nomination = 批准提名


词源:
来自拉丁语 *ratificāre，由 *ratus（已决定的，批准的）和 -ficāre（使……）构成。
