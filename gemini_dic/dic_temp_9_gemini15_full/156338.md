physicological:/ˌfɪzɪkəlˈɒdʒɪkəl/| adj.|物理生理学的；物理的和生理的结合的 |Physicological refers to the combined aspects of physical and physiological processes. It describes something relating to both the physical structure and the biological functions of a living organism.  The term is relatively uncommon and often replaced by more specific terminology depending on the context.|例句：The study explored the physicological effects of prolonged space travel on astronauts.  这项研究探讨了长时间太空旅行对宇航员的物理生理影响。

The research investigated the physicological mechanisms underlying muscle growth. 这项研究调查了肌肉生长背后的物理生理机制。|近义词：biophysical, physiological, physical|反义词：psychological (emphasizing mental aspects), abstract (lacking physical or physiological aspects)
