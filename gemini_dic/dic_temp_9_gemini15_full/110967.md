liquescent: /lɪˈkwɛsnt/ | adj. | 液化的；易液化的 | Becoming or tending to become liquid; easily melted or dissolved.  |例句：The liquescent substance flowed easily.  这种易液化的物质流动自如。

The wax became liquescent under the heat of the sun.  蜡在阳光的照射下变得易液化。


近义词：fluid, melting, liquefiable | 反义词：solid, solidified, hard


词源:  From Latin *liquescere "to become liquid," from liquidus "liquid."
