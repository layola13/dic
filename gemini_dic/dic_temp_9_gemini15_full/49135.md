deists:/ˈdiːɪsts/| n.| 泛神论者，信奉自然神论者 | A deist is someone who believes in God but denies supernatural revelation and accepts only reason and observation in determining the nature of God. Deists typically believe God created the universe but does not intervene in its affairs. | Example:  Many of the Founding Fathers of the United States were deists.  许多美国开国元勋都是泛神论者。


Example:  Voltaire, a prominent Enlightenment figure, was a well-known deist. 伏尔泰，一位著名的启蒙思想家，是一位著名的泛神论者。


近义词：naturalist, rationalist |反义词：theist, atheist, supernaturalist |词性:名词
