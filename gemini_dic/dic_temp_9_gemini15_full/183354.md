reproduce:/ˌriːprəˈduːs/| vt. n.|复制，繁殖，再现|To make or create a copy of something; to create offspring.|例句：The artist reproduced the painting perfectly.  艺术家完美地复制了这幅画。|例句： Rabbits reproduce quickly. 兔子繁殖迅速。|近义词：duplicate, replicate, copy, breed, generate|反义词：destroy, annihilate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reproduce the experiment carefully. = 他们仔细地重复了实验。
The plants reproduce through seeds. = 植物通过种子繁殖。


一般过去时:
She reproduced the painting in her own style. = 她以自己的风格复制了这幅画。
The company reproduced the faulty product. = 公司重做了有缺陷的产品。


现在完成时:
Scientists have reproduced the results multiple times. = 科学家们已经多次重复了实验结果。
The artist has reproduced many famous masterpieces. =这位艺术家复制了许多著名的杰作。


过去完成时:
They had reproduced the data before submitting the report. = 他们在提交报告之前已经复制了数据。
The birds had reproduced successfully that season. = 那些鸟类在那一年繁殖成功了。


将来时:
The company will reproduce the brochure next week. = 公司下周将重印宣传册。
We will reproduce the experiment with different conditions. = 我们将在不同的条件下重复实验。


虚拟语气:
If we had the right equipment, we could reproduce the experiment accurately. = 如果我们有合适的设备，我们就能精确地重复实验。
If the plants had more sunlight, they would reproduce more prolifically. = 如果植物有更多的阳光，它们就能更旺盛地繁殖。


被动语态:
The image was reproduced in high resolution. = 图像以高分辨率复制。
The experiment will be reproduced by another team. = 实验将由另一个团队重复。


疑问句:
How do these animals reproduce? = 这些动物是如何繁殖的？
Can this software reproduce the original file? = 这个软件能够复制原始文件吗？


否定句:
They could not reproduce the results. = 他们无法复制结果。
The plants did not reproduce well in that environment. = 植物在那种环境下繁殖不良。
