protovirus:/ˈproʊtoʊˌvaɪrəs/ | n. |原病毒；祖先病毒 | A hypothetical virus that is considered to be the ancestor of all other viruses.  It is a theoretical construct, not a specific identified virus.  Scientists use this concept to understand the evolution and origins of viruses. | Example: The study proposes a protovirus as the common ancestor of all retroviruses.  这项研究提出了一种原病毒作为所有逆转录病毒的共同祖先。| 近义词：ancestral virus, precursor virus | 反义词：N/A
