pyramidologist:/ˌpɪrəˈmɪdɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |金字塔学家，研究金字塔的专家|A pyramidologist is a person who studies pyramids, their construction, purpose, and significance.  They often combine archaeological, historical, and sometimes pseudoscientific approaches to their research. |The pyramidologist presented his findings on the ventilation shafts in the Great Pyramid. 金字塔学家介绍了他关于大金字塔通风井的研究成果。|archaeologist, Egyptologist | (No direct antonym, but could be considered the opposite of someone who actively dismisses pyramid studies)|名词
