retardative:/rɪˈtɑːdətiv/| adj.|阻碍的；延缓的；抑制的|Having the effect of retarding or delaying; hindering progress or development.  The drug has a retardative effect on the growth of tumors.|例句：The heavy rain had a retardative effect on the construction project.  大雨对建筑工程产生了阻碍作用。

例句：The economic recession had a retardative impact on the company's growth. 经济衰退对公司增长产生了抑制作用。

近义词：inhibitory, delaying, hindering, impeding, slowing

反义词：accelerative, promoting, stimulating, advancing
