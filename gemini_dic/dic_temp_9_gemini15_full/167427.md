prevalency:/ˈprɛvələnsɪ/| n. |流行；盛行；普遍 |The prevalency of a disease refers to how widespread it is in a particular population.  It indicates the proportion of individuals within a group who have the condition at a specific time.  The study showed a high prevalency of smoking among young adults.  |近义词：incidence, prevalence, frequency |反义词：rarity, scarcity |名词
