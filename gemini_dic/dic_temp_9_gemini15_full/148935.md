overwork:/ˈoʊvərwɜːrk/ | v. n. |过度工作；使过度劳累 | to work excessively hard; to exhaust through excessive work |例句：He's been overworking lately and is completely exhausted. 他最近一直在过度工作，已经筋疲力尽了。

例句：The manager overworked his employees to meet the deadline.  经理让员工过度工作以赶上最后期限。


近义词：overexert, strain, tire out | 反义词：rest, relax

词源:
The word "overwork" is a combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") and the noun "work".  It developed naturally from the common use of the verb "to work" and the intensifying prefix.  The meaning is straightforward—doing too much work.
