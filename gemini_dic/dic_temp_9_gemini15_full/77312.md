gather:/ˈɡæðər/| vt. vi. n.|收集；聚集；聚集；集合|to bring together; to collect; to assemble; to come together|例句：The children gathered around the storyteller. 孩子们围在讲故事的人身边。

例句：The clouds gathered before the storm.暴风雨前乌云密布。

近义词：collect, assemble, accumulate, congregate|反义词：scatter, disperse, dissipate|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They gather information daily. = 他们每天收集信息。
Birds gather in large flocks before migration. = 鸟类在迁徙前会成群结队聚集。


一般过去时:
We gathered wildflowers in the meadow. = 我们在草地上采摘野花。
The crowd gathered to watch the parade. = 人群聚集起来观看游行。


现在完成时:
She has gathered a lot of evidence for the case. = 她已经为这个案子收集了很多证据。
The storm has gathered strength. = 风暴已经增强了。


过去完成时:
They had gathered all the necessary materials before starting the project. = 他们开始项目之前已经收集了所有必要的材料。
The villagers had gathered to discuss the problem. = 村民们已经聚集起来讨论这个问题。


将来时:
We will gather for a family reunion next month. = 我们下个月将举行家族聚会。
The troops will gather at dawn. = 部队将在黎明时分集结。


虚拟语气:
If they gathered more evidence, they would win the case. = 如果他们收集更多证据，他们就会赢得官司。
I wish I had gathered more information before making a decision. = 我希望在做决定之前我已经收集了更多信息。


被动语态:
The data was gathered by the research team. = 数据是由研究团队收集的。
The crops were gathered in the autumn. = 收获在秋季进行。


疑问句:
Where do the birds gather in winter? = 冬天鸟类在哪里聚集？
Will you gather at the library? = 你会在图书馆集合吗？


否定句:
They didn't gather enough support for their proposal. = 他们没有为他们的提案收集到足够的支持。
The farmer didn't gather his crops on time. = 农民没有及时收割庄稼。
