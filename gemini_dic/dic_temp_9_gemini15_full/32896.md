chondrostei:/kɒndrəˈsteɪi/| n. |软骨鱼纲|A subclass of fish characterized by a cartilaginous skeleton, including sturgeons and paddlefish.  They are ancient fish, with some lineages dating back to the Paleozoic era.  They are distinguished from other cartilaginous fish (like sharks and rays) by several features, including the presence of a bony ganoid scale covering. |例句：Chondrostei are an ancient group of fishes that have survived for millions of years. 软骨鱼纲是一种古老的鱼类，已经生存了数百万年。|例句：The sturgeon, a member of the Chondrostei, is prized for its caviar. 鲟鱼，属于软骨鱼纲，以其鱼子酱而闻名。|近义词：暂无完全近义词，可根据语境用“ancient fish”或“cartilaginous fish”等替代|反义词：暂无|名词
