practicing:/ˈpræktɪsɪŋ/ | v. |练习，实践|The act of doing something repeatedly to improve one's skill or ability.  It implies a continuous and ongoing effort to master a skill.|例句：I'm practicing my piano skills every day. = 我每天都在练习我的钢琴技巧。

例句：She's been practicing her English speaking for months. = 她已经练习说英语好几个月了。


近义词：rehearsing, training, exercising, drilling

反义词：neglecting, ignoring


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am practicing the piano. = 我正在练习钢琴。
He practices yoga every morning. = 他每天早上练习瑜伽。


一般过去时:
I practiced the guitar yesterday. = 我昨天练习了吉他。
She practiced her speech before the presentation. = 她在大演讲前练习了自己的演讲。


现在完成时:
I have been practicing for hours. = 我已经练习了好几个小时了。
They have practiced diligently for the competition. = 他们为比赛勤奋地练习了。


过去完成时:
I had practiced the song many times before the concert. = 在音乐会之前我已经练习过很多遍这首歌了。
She had practiced her lines before the audition. = 试镜前，她已经练习过台词了。


将来时:
I will be practicing my presentation tomorrow. = 我明天将练习我的演示文稿。
They will practice their dance routine next week. = 他们下周将练习他们的舞蹈套路。


虚拟语气:
If I practiced more, I would be better. = 如果我练习更多，我会更好。
I wish I had practiced harder for the exam. = 我希望我为考试更努力地练习了。


被动语态:  (Practicing doesn't typically use passive voice, as it's an active process.)  A more appropriate example using a related verb would be:

The song was rehearsed many times. = 这首歌排练了很多次。
The skill is being practiced by many students. = 许多学生正在练习这项技能。


疑问句:
Are you practicing your speech? = 你在练习你的演讲吗？
Is he practicing for the marathon? = 他在为马拉松做练习吗？


否定句:
I am not practicing today. = 我今天不练习。
They were not practicing when I arrived. = 我到达时他们没有在练习。


PHRASE->
practicing law = 从事法律工作
practicing medicine = 从事医疗工作
practicing yoga = 练习瑜伽
practicing calligraphy = 练习书法
practicing mindfulness = 练习正念
