irretractile: /ˌɪrɪˈtræktɪl/ | adj. | 不可收回的，不可缩回的 | Incapable of being retracted or withdrawn.  The term often refers to anatomical structures that cannot be pulled back or drawn in. | Example: The snake's fangs were irretractile, meaning they remained permanently exposed.  这条蛇的毒牙是不可收回的，这意味着它们永久地暴露在外。  | 近义词: fixed, immovable, nonretractable | 反义词: retractable, withdrawable | 词性: adjective
