dera:/ˈdɛrə/| n.|脱轨；岔轨；偏离正轨|The derailment of the train caused significant delays. 火车脱轨导致了严重的延误。|近义词：derailment, divergence |反义词：alignment, conformity


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The train derails frequently on this track. 这条铁轨上的火车经常脱轨。
The project is derailing from its original plan.  这个项目正在偏离最初的计划。


一般过去时:
The car derailed on a sharp curve. 汽车在一个急转弯处脱轨了。
The negotiations derailed due to a lack of trust.  由于缺乏信任，谈判破裂了。


现在完成时:
The economy has derailed since the pandemic. 自疫情以来，经济已经脱轨了。
His career has derailed after the scandal. 丑闻之后，他的事业就毁了。


过去完成时:
The train had derailed before the emergency services arrived.  救援人员到达之前，火车就已经脱轨了。
The project had derailed long before the deadline. 项目早就偏离轨道了，远在截止日期之前。


将来时:
The project will derail if we don't address the issues. 如果我们不解决这些问题，项目将会失败。
The talks will likely derail unless both sides compromise.  除非双方妥协，否则谈判很可能会破裂。


虚拟语气:
If the train had not derailed, we would have arrived on time. 如果火车没有脱轨，我们就会准时到达。
If the negotiations hadn't derailed, we would have reached an agreement. 如果谈判没有破裂，我们就能达成协议。


被动语态:
The train was derailed by a landslide. 火车被山体滑坡导致脱轨。
The project was derailed by unforeseen circumstances. 项目因不可预见的情况而搁浅。


疑问句:
Was the train derailed? 火车脱轨了吗？
Has the project derailed? 项目偏离轨道了吗？


否定句:
The train did not derail. 火车没有脱轨。
The project has not derailed yet. 项目还没有脱轨。
