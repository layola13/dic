competencies:/ˈkɑːmpɪtənsiz/ | n. |能力；胜任力；技能 |The ability to do something successfully or efficiently.  Competencies are often specific skills, knowledge, or behaviors that contribute to successful performance in a particular job or role. |例句：The job requires a range of competencies, including communication and problem-solving skills. 这份工作需要一系列的能力，包括沟通和解决问题的能力。

例句：He demonstrated strong competencies in leadership and teamwork during his previous role.  他在之前的角色中展现了强大的领导力和团队合作能力。


近义词：abilities, skills, aptitudes, capabilities |反义词：incompetence, incapability, inadequacy |词性:名词
