sapper:/ˈsæpər/| n. |工兵；破坏者|A sapper is a soldier trained in military engineering, especially in the construction and demolition of fortifications and the laying of mines.  They are crucial for battlefield mobility and defense.  Sappers are also skilled in breaching obstacles and creating pathways for advancing troops.|例句：The sappers worked tirelessly to clear the minefield. 工兵们夜以继日地清除地雷。|例句：The enemy sappers attempted to breach the fortifications. 敌军工兵试图突破防御工事。|近义词：engineer, miner, demolitionist|反义词：N/A
