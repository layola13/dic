cross-staff:/ˈkrɒsˌstɑːf/| n.| 十字测角仪 |A cross-staff is an ancient astronomical and surveying instrument used to measure angles, particularly altitudes of celestial bodies. It consists of a staff with a perpendicular cross-piece that slides along the staff.  By aligning the cross-piece with the horizon and the object being measured, the angle can be determined.| Example: The navigator used a cross-staff to determine the ship's latitude.  导航员使用十字测角仪确定船的纬度。|近义词：astrolabe, quadrant|反义词：None|名词
