monomaniac:/ˌmɒnəʊˈmeɪniæk/| n. |偏执狂；着魔的人|A monomaniac is a person who is obsessed with a single thing or idea to the exclusion of all others.  They are often characterized by intense focus and disregard for other aspects of life. |例句：He became a monomaniac about collecting stamps, neglecting his family and career. 他对集邮着了迷，忽略了家庭和事业。|例句：Her monomaniacal pursuit of wealth led to her isolation and unhappiness. 她对财富的偏执追求导致了她孤立和不幸。|近义词：obsessive, fanatic, maniac|反义词：balanced, well-rounded
