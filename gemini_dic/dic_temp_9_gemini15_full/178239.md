recompensed:/ˌriˈkɑːmpɛnst/|/ˈriːkɒmpɛnst/| vt.| 补偿；报答；酬谢 | To give someone something, especially money, in return for something they have done or suffered.  |例句：The company recompensed the workers for their overtime. 公司补偿了工人们的加班费。

例句：He was handsomely recompensed for his services. 他因其服务而获得了丰厚的报酬。


近义词：compensate, remunerate, reward, repay

反义词：deprive, cheat

词性：动词
