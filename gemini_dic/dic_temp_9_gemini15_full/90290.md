housemanship:/ˈhaʊsmənʃɪp/| n. |实习期，住院医师实习期|The period of time a newly qualified doctor spends working in a hospital to gain experience.  It is a period of supervised practice, typically lasting one year.  The term is primarily used in the UK and some other Commonwealth countries.|Example: After graduating medical school, she began her housemanship at a busy city hospital.  = 毕业后，她开始在一家繁忙的城市医院进行实习。


He completed his housemanship and then specialized in cardiology. = 他完成了实习期，然后专攻心脏病学。|近义词：internship, residency (in US), probationary period|反义词：consultancy, senior physician position
