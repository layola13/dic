nudges:/nʌdʒɪz/| n. v.|轻推；怂恿；暗示|A nudge is a gentle push, or a subtle suggestion or encouragement.  It can be a physical action or a more indirect influence.|例句：She gave him a nudge in the ribs. 她轻轻地推了推他的肋骨。
The marketing campaign used subtle nudges to encourage consumers to buy the product.  市场营销活动利用微妙的暗示来鼓励消费者购买产品。|近义词：prod, prompt, encourage, hint|反义词：ignore, neglect, discourage


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He nudges his friend. = 他轻轻推了推他的朋友。
She nudges me to try the new dessert. = 她怂恿我尝试新的甜点。


一般过去时:
He nudged his friend to get his attention. = 他轻轻推了推他的朋友以引起他的注意。
She nudged the box across the floor. = 她轻轻地把盒子推过地板。


现在完成时:
I have nudged him several times, but he still hasn't responded. = 我已经多次暗示他了，但他仍然没有回应。
She has nudged her way to the front of the line. = 她已经设法挤到了队伍的前面。


过去完成时:
He had nudged the door open before entering the room. = 他在进房间之前已经轻轻地推开了门。
She had nudged her friend to apply for the job weeks ago. = 几周前她就已暗示她的朋友去申请这份工作。


将来时:
I will nudge him again tomorrow. = 我明天还会再暗示他一下。
She will nudge the cart down the aisle. = 她将把购物车轻轻地推过过道。


虚拟语气:
If I nudged him harder, he might react. = 如果我更用力地推他一下，他可能会做出反应。
I wish I had nudged her to go to college. = 我真希望我当时已经暗示她去上大学。


被动语态:
He was nudged by his colleague to speak up. = 他被同事怂恿着开口说话。
The box was nudged off the table. = 箱子被轻轻地推下了桌子。


疑问句:
Did he nudge you? = 他推了你吗？
Who nudged the table? = 谁推了桌子？


否定句:
He didn't nudge her. = 他没有推她。
I didn't mean to nudge you. = 我不是故意推你的。


PHRASE->
give someone a nudge =  轻轻推某人一下
a gentle nudge =  轻轻一推
a nudge in the right direction =  一些正确的引导
a little nudge =  一点点鼓励
get a nudge =  得到一个暗示
