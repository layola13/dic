jimmying:/ˈdʒɪmiɪŋ/| n. v.|撬锁；偷偷进入；篡改|The act of picking a lock or forcing a way into something, often illegally.  It can also refer to tampering with or manipulating something. |例句：The police investigated the jimmying of the back door.  警方调查了后门的撬锁事件。

例句：He was caught jimmying the vending machine. 他被抓到在撬自动售货机。


近义词：burglary, breaking and entering, tampering, forcing


反义词：locking, securing, protecting


词性:动词及名词


词源:源自jimmy,  a slang term for a crowbar or a lock pick. The word's origins are uncertain, but possibly from a proper noun or a dialectal variant.  Its use to describe the act of picking locks became popular in the late 19th and early 20th centuries.
