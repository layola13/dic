operable:/ˈɒpərəbl/ /ˈɑːpərəbl/| adj.| 可操作的；可手术的；可使用的 | Capable of being operated on or used;  amenable to surgical treatment.  | 例句：The tumor is operable.  肿瘤是可手术的。The machine is operable after the repair. 修理后机器可以使用了。|近义词：functional, workable, manageable |反义词：inoperable, unusable, dysfunctional

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine is operable. = 这台机器可以使用。
This software is operable on all platforms. = 这个软件在所有平台上都可以运行。

一般过去时:
The car was operable yesterday. = 昨天这辆车可以使用。
The equipment was operable until the power outage. = 直到停电之前，设备都是可以使用的。

现在完成时:
The system has been operable since the upgrade. = 系统自升级以来一直可以使用。
The factory has been operable for over a decade. = 这家工厂已经运行了十多年了。

过去完成时:
The engine had been operable until the last trip. = 直到上次旅行之前，发动机一直都能使用。
The bridge had been operable for many years before it collapsed. = 那座桥在倒塌之前已经使用了多年。

将来时:
The new feature will be operable next month. = 新功能将于下个月可以使用。
The hospital will be operable by the end of the year. = 医院将于年底投入使用。

虚拟语气:
If the machine were operable, we could finish the project. = 如果机器可以使用，我们就能完成项目。
I wish the software were operable on my old computer. = 我希望这个软件在我的旧电脑上也能运行。

被动语态:
The problem is being addressed so the system will be operable soon. = 问题正在解决中，系统很快就能使用了。
The equipment is being checked to ensure it is operable. = 设备正在检查中，以确保其可以使用。

疑问句:
Is the equipment operable? = 设备可以使用吗？
Is the tumor operable? = 肿瘤可手术吗？

否定句:
The machine is not operable at the moment. = 目前机器无法使用。
The bridge is no longer operable due to the damage. = 由于损坏，桥梁已无法通行。


PHRASE->
