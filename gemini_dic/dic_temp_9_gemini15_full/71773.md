fleas:/fliːz/ | n. |跳蚤 |Small, wingless insects that are parasites on mammals and birds, feeding on their blood.  They are known for their ability to jump and can transmit diseases. |Example:  The dog was covered in fleas. 这只狗身上长满了跳蚤。  The cat had a bad case of fleas.  这只猫得了严重的跳蚤病。|近义词：parasites, insects |反义词: N/A
