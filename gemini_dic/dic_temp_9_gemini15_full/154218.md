peripheral: /pəˈrɪfərəl/ | adj. | 周边的；外围的；次要的 | Relating to or situated on the edge or periphery of something.  Peripheral things are not of central importance. | The peripheral vision is less sharp than the central vision. 周围视野不如中心视野清晰。 The issues raised were peripheral to the main argument. 提出的问题与主要论点无关。 |近义词：marginal, secondary, outlying |反义词：central, main, essential |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The peripheral devices are connected to the computer. = 外围设备连接到电脑上。
This issue is peripheral to our main concerns. = 这件事与我们的主要关注点无关。

一般过去时:
The peripheral vision was blurry after the accident. = 事故后周边视野模糊。
He considered the topic peripheral to the discussion. = 他认为这个话题与讨论无关。

现在完成时:
We have connected all the peripheral devices to the network. = 我们已经将所有外围设备连接到网络。
The company has made peripheral changes to its strategy. = 公司对其战略做了一些细微的调整。

过去完成时:
They had already installed all the peripheral equipment before the launch. = 他们在发布之前已经安装好所有外围设备。
He had dismissed the arguments as peripheral to the main point. = 他已经将这些论点视为与要点无关。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If the peripheral devices were faulty, the computer wouldn't work. = 如果外围设备出现故障，电脑将无法工作。
I wish the peripheral issues had not complicated matters. = 我希望周边问题没有使事情复杂化。

被动语态:
The peripheral devices are regularly checked by the technician. = 外围设备定期由技术人员检查。
The project's peripheral aspects were largely ignored. = 该项目的次要方面在很大程度上被忽视了。

疑问句:

否定句:
The problem isn't peripheral; it's a major issue. = 问题并非次要的；这是一个主要问题。
