D-Day:/ˈdiː deɪ/| n. |诺曼底登陆日；决定性的一天；重要日子 |The day (June 6, 1944) in World War II on which Allied forces landed in Normandy, France.  It is also used more generally to refer to any crucial day or date. |例句：D-Day was a pivotal moment in World War II. 诺曼底登陆日是二战中的一个关键时刻。

例句：The deadline is approaching; this is our D-Day.  截止日期临近；这是我们决定性的一天。

近义词：critical day, crucial day, decisive day

反义词：insignificant day, unimportant day


词性:名词
