overruled:/ˌoʊvərˈruːld/ | vt. |推翻，驳回，否决 | To reject or invalidate (a decision, ruling, etc.) by means of superior authority.  |例句：The judge overruled the objection. = 法官驳回了异议。
The higher court overruled the lower court's decision. = 上级法院推翻了下级法院的判决。 |近义词：reject, overturn, invalidate, veto |反义词：uphold, sustain, approve
