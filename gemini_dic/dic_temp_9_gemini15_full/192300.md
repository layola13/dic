screwier:/ˈskruːiər/| adj.|更古怪的，更不正常的，更离奇的 | More eccentric, strange, or unusual than usual.  It is the comparative form of "screwy".|例句：The plot of the movie got screwier and screwier as it went on.  这部电影的情节越来越古怪。


例句：His behavior became increasingly screwier after he lost his job. 他失业后行为变得越来越古怪。


近义词：weirder, stranger, crazier, more bizarre, more eccentric


反义词：normal, sane, rational, ordinary, conventional


词源:源于"screw"（螺丝钉），引申为“扭曲的”、“不正常的”。  The original meaning of "screw" relates to twisting or turning something, leading to the figurative sense of something being twisted or abnormal.
