non-supportive:/ˌnɒn səˈpɔːrtɪv/| adj. |不支持的，不给予帮助的| Not providing support or encouragement; unhelpful.  This adjective describes something or someone that lacks support, either emotionally, practically, or materially.  |例句：His family was non-supportive of his artistic ambitions. 他的家人不支持他的艺术抱负。

例句：The non-supportive soil made it difficult to grow crops.  这片贫瘠的土壤使得种植庄稼变得困难。


近义词：unhelpful, unsupportive, unhelpful, discouraging, antagonistic


反义词：supportive, encouraging, helpful, assisting


词性:形容词
