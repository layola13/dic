mysticisms:/ˈmɪstɪsɪzəm/| n.|神秘主义；神秘论|The study or practice of mysticism; the belief in the possibility of direct communion with God or ultimate reality through intuition and mystical experience, often involving esoteric practices and beliefs.|例句：The book explores the various forms of mysticism found in different religions. 这本书探讨了不同宗教中各种形式的神秘主义。|例句：Her paintings reflect her deep interest in mysticism and spirituality.她的绘画反映了她对神秘主义和灵性的浓厚兴趣。|近义词：esotericism, occultism|反义词：rationalism, materialism|名词
