putrefaction:/ˌpjuːtrɪˈfækʃən/| n. |腐烂；腐败；分解 | The decomposition of organic matter, especially of proteins, by microorganisms, resulting in foul-smelling products.  This process involves the breakdown of complex organic molecules into simpler ones, often accompanied by the production of gases and unpleasant odors. |例句：The putrefaction of the dead animal attracted many flies.  死动物的腐烂吸引了许多苍蝇。

The putrefaction of the food made it inedible.  食物腐烂得不能吃了。|近义词：decay, decomposition, rot, spoilage |反义词：preservation, conservation
