It often refers to a statistical concept or a property of a system that remains constant despite changes in other factors. | Example: The nonvariance of the sample mean under certain conditions is a key result in statistics.  这个例子说明了在某些条件下样本均值的不变性是统计学中的一个关键结果。|近义词：constancy, invariance, stability |反义词：variance, variability, fluctuation |
