descendible:/dɪˈsɛndəbəl/| adj.| 可继承的；可传下来的 | Able to be inherited or passed down; capable of being descended from.  The inheritance is descendible to the heir's children.  |近义词：heritable, transferable |反义词：uninheritable, inalienable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This property is descendible to my son. = 这处房产可以继承给我的儿子。
The title is descendible through the male line. = 这个头衔可以通过男性血统继承。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:
