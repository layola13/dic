predevour: /ˌpriːdɪˈvaʊər/ | v. | 预先吞食；抢先吃掉 | To eat or consume something before others have a chance.  This word is a relatively rare and somewhat informal neologism, formed by combining "pre-" (before) and "devour".  It implies a hasty or greedy consumption. |例句：The children predevoured all the cookies before the party even started. 孩子们在派对开始之前就抢先吃掉了所有的饼干。

The hungry wolves predevoured the carcass before the scavengers arrived. 饥饿的狼群在食腐动物到达之前就抢先吃掉了尸体。| 近义词：gobble, gulp, consume, wolf down | 反义词：save, conserve, preserve | 动词
