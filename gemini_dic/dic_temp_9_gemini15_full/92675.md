ice-cold: /ˈaɪs kold/ | adj. | 冰冷的，极其寒冷的 | Extremely cold;  characterized by a temperature close to or below the freezing point of water.  It can also be used figuratively to describe something extremely unpleasant or unfriendly. | 例句：The ice-cold water refreshed me after my run.  冰冷的水让我跑步后感觉很清爽。

The manager gave me an ice-cold stare. 经理给了我一个冰冷的眼神。|近义词：freezing, frigid, icy, glacial |反义词：boiling hot, scorching, lukewarm
