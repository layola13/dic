groupon:/ˈɡruːpɒn/| n. |团购网站，团购券 |A Groupon is a website that offers deals on various goods and services.  These deals are typically offered at a significant discount if a certain number of people purchase them.  |Example: I bought a Groupon for a massage at a local spa. 我在当地一家水疗中心买了一张团购按摩券。|近义词：deal, coupon, discount |反义词：full price, premium
