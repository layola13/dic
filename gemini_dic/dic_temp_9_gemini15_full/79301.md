glazier:/ˈɡleɪzər/| n. |玻璃工，装玻璃工人|A glazier is a person who cuts, installs, and repairs glass, typically in windows and doors.|例句：The glazier replaced the broken window pane. 玻璃工更换了破损的窗格。 The glazier carefully measured the glass before cutting it. 玻璃工在切割玻璃之前仔细测量了尺寸。|近义词：glass fitter, window fitter|反义词：无
