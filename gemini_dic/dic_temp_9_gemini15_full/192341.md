scrimmager:/ˈskrɪmədʒər/| n.|混战者；争吵者；（美式橄榄球）争球者|A scrimmager is someone who engages in a scrimmage, a confused fight or struggle.  In American football, it refers specifically to a player involved in the scrimmage line. |例句：The two teams engaged in a fierce scrimmager for the ball.  两队为了球展开了激烈的争夺。The quarterback was protected by his offensive scrimmager. 四分卫受到进攻线球员的保护。|近义词：brawler, scrapper, scuffler, wrestler (in the context of a fight)|反义词：peacemaker, mediator
