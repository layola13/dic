flanges:/ˈflændʒɪz/| n.|法兰，凸缘|A flange is a projecting rim, collar, or rib on an object, typically used to strengthen or support it, or to connect it to another object.  Flanges are commonly used in piping systems, machinery, and other applications where joining or reinforcing is needed.|例句：The plumber carefully aligned the flanges before tightening the bolts. 水管工在拧紧螺栓之前仔细对准了法兰。


The large flanges on the tank provided added stability.  油箱上的大型法兰提供了额外的稳定性。|近义词：rim, collar, rib, edge|反义词：None specifically, but words implying a lack of a projecting rim could be considered antonyms in context.|名词
