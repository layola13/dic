kinest:/kaɪˈnɛst/ | adj. |动觉的；运动感觉的 | Relating to or denoting the sense of movement and position of the body.  |Example: Kinesthetic learning involves using the body to learn. 动觉学习包括使用身体来学习。|近义词：sensomotor, proprioceptive | 反义词：static
