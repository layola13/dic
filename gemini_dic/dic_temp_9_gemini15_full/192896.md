seating:/ˈsiːtɪŋ/| n.|座位；座次安排；就座的行为|The arrangement of seats in a place, or the act of providing or occupying seats.  It can also refer to the number of seats available.|例句：The seating in the theater was uncomfortable. 剧院的座位很不舒服。

The restaurant's seating capacity is 100 people. 这家餐厅的座位数是100人。|近义词：accommodation, placement, arrangement|反义词：standing
