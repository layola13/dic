frenular:/ˈfrɛnjələr/| adj.|系带的； freio的| Relating to a frenulum.  A frenulum is a small fold of tissue that restricts movement. The term is often used in the context of anatomy, particularly referring to the frenulum of the tongue or the frenulum of the prepuce (foreskin).| Example: The child underwent a frenular release to correct tongue-tie. 这个孩子接受了系带切断术来矫正舌系带过短。|近义词：ligamentous (in some contexts)|反义词：unrestricted
