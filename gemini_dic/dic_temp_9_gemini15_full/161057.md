postcontracts:/ˌpoʊstˈkɒntrækts/ | n. | 售后合同；合同签订后的协议 | Agreements or arrangements made after the signing of a main contract, often dealing with details or adjustments.  These often involve additional work, changes to the original scope, or resolving disputes. | Example: The postcontracts clarified the payment schedule for the extra work.  售后合同明确了额外工作的付款时间表。|近义词：supplementary agreements, addenda, amendments | 反义词：precontracts (preliminary agreements)
