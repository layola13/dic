nonguests:/ˈnɑːnɡɛsts/ | n. |非宾客，非受邀者 |People who are not invited or expected guests.  This word is formed by adding the prefix "non-" to the noun "guests". It implies those excluded from a particular event or gathering. | Example: The security guards checked the identification of all nonguests attempting to enter the private party.  安保人员检查了所有试图进入私人派对的非宾客的身份。|近义词：uninvited guests, outsiders, strangers |反义词：guests, invitees |名词
