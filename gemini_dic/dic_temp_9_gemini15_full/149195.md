oxygenator:/ˈɒksɪdʒəneɪtə(r)/| n.| 氧合器；充氧器 | A device that increases the oxygen content of a liquid, typically blood.  It is used in various medical applications, particularly in heart-lung machines during open-heart surgery. | Example: The heart-lung machine's oxygenator ensured the patient received sufficient oxygen during the operation.  = 心肺机的氧合器确保了患者在手术过程中获得足够的氧气。 The fish tank oxygenator kept the water properly aerated. = 鱼缸充氧器使水保持了适当的充氧状态。|近义词：aerator, oxygen enrichment device |反义词：deoxygenator
