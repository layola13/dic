rephrasing: /ˌriˈfreɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 改述；重新表达；释义 | To express something in different words, often to make it clearer or more effective.  |例句：The teacher asked me to rephrase my answer to make it more concise. 老师让我重新表达我的答案，使其更简洁。

例句：I need to rephrase this sentence; it's too ambiguous. 我需要改写这句话；它太含糊了。


近义词：paraphrasing, restating, reformulating, rewording | 反义词：None  |词性：动词
