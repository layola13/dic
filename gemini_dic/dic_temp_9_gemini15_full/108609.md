leadened:/ˈlɛdn̩d/| adj.|迟缓的；沉重的；无生气的|Heavy, sluggish, or spiritless.  The leadened atmosphere in the room reflected the gravity of the situation.|近义词：heavy, sluggish, dull, spiritless, lifeless|反义词：light, lively, energetic, brisk


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heavy rain leadens the atmosphere. = 大雨使气氛沉闷。
His movements are leadened by age. = 他的行动因年龄而迟缓。


一般过去时:
The news leadened her spirits. = 这个消息使她情绪低落。
The long journey leadened their pace. = 漫长的旅程使他们的步伐缓慢。


现在完成时:
The constant worry has leadened his steps. = 持续的担忧使他的步伐沉重。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

I wish my steps weren't so leadened by fatigue. = 我希望我的步伐不要因为疲劳而如此沉重。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
