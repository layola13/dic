grubs:/ɡrʌbz/| n.|蛴螬；幼虫；贪吃的人；食物|Larvae of insects, especially beetles; also used informally to refer to unpleasant or unattractive people, or to food, especially simple food. |例句：The gardener found grubs in the soil. 园丁在土壤中发现了蛴螬。

例句：The children ate their grubs with gusto. 孩子们津津有味地吃着他们的食物。


近义词：larvae, worms, maggots, caterpillars


反义词：adult (insects)


词性：名词
