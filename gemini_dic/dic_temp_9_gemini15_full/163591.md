precurricular:/ˌpriːkəˈrɪkjʊlər/| adj. |课程前的，课程预备的| Relating to activities or learning that takes place before formal schooling begins.  It often refers to activities designed to prepare children for the demands of formal education.|Example:  Precurricular programs often focus on developing a child's social and emotional skills. 课程预备项目通常侧重于培养孩子的社会和情感能力。|近义词: preparatory, preliminary, antecedent|反义词:postcurricular
