charioteer:/ˈtʃæriətiər/| n. |御者，驾车者 |A charioteer is a person who drives a chariot.  The word evokes images of ancient warfare or sporting events. |Example: The skilled charioteer expertly guided his horses around the tight turn.  熟练的御者熟练地引导他的马匹绕过急转弯。  The charioteer's courage was evident as he raced through the dangerous course. 御者在危险的赛道上飞驰，勇气显而易见。|近义词：driver, coachman |反义词：passenger
