precontrolled:/ˌpriːkənˈtrəʊld/ | adj. | 预先控制的，预先调节的 |  Precontrolled refers to something that has been controlled or regulated in advance. It implies a system or process designed to manage or limit something before it happens.  | Example: The precontrolled environment of the laboratory ensured accurate results.  实验室预先控制的环境确保了结果的准确性。 |近义词：regulated, predetermined, pre-set | 反义词：uncontrolled, unregulated, spontaneous | 词性：形容词
