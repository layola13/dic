complementers:/ˈkɒmplɪmentərz/| n. |补语成分|Words or phrases that complete the meaning of a verb or an adjective.  They provide essential information about the subject or object of the sentence.  They can be nouns, pronouns, adjectives, adverbs, or prepositional phrases.|The verb "is" needs a complementer to complete its meaning.  For example, in "The flower is red," "red" is the complementer.|近义词：predicate complements, object complements|反义词：None (it's a grammatical term, not a word with inherent antonyms)|名词
