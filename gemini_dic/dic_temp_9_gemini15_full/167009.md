presubscriptions:/ˌpriːˈskrɪpʃənz/| n.|预先处方，预先订购|Pre-subscriptions refer to subscriptions or orders placed before a product or service is officially released or available.  They often involve securing a place in line or guaranteeing access to something in high demand. | Example:  The pre-subscriptions for the new video game sold out within hours.  新的电子游戏的预购在几小时内售罄。|近义词：advance orders, pre-orders|反义词：post-subscriptions (a term not commonly used)
