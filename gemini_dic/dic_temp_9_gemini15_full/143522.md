outgrew:/ˌaʊtˈɡruː/| v.|超过，比…长得快，发展得超过|To grow beyond or surpass in size, extent, or importance.  |例句：He outgrew his childhood fears. 他克服了童年的恐惧。|例句：The company outgrew its original office space. 公司发展壮大了，原来的办公场所不够用了。|近义词：surpass, exceed, transcend|反义词：be dwarfed by, be surpassed by|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The child outgrows his clothes quickly. = 孩子很快就会长大把衣服穿小了。
She outgrows her anxieties with time. = 她随着时间的推移克服了焦虑。

一般过去时:
I outgrew my old shoes last year. = 我去年把旧鞋子穿小了。
The small business outgrew its initial market. = 这家小企业发展壮大了，超出了最初的市场规模。

现在完成时:
He has outgrown his shyness. = 他已经克服了害羞。
The city has outgrown its infrastructure. = 这个城市发展太快，基础设施跟不上了。

过去完成时:
By the time he was 18, he had outgrown his childish habits. = 到18岁的时候，他已经改掉了幼稚的习惯。
The company had outgrown its original business plan before it even launched. = 公司还没开始运营，就已经发展壮大了，最初的商业计划已经跟不上了。

将来时:
She will outgrow her need for constant reassurance. = 她将来会不再需要别人不断地安慰她。
The organization will likely outgrow its current headquarters soon. = 组织很快就会发展壮大，目前的总部可能不够用了。

虚拟语气:
If he had focused more on his studies, he might have outgrown his academic difficulties sooner. = 如果他更专注于学习，他可能早就会克服学习上的困难。
I wish I had outgrown my procrastination habits years ago. = 我真希望几年前就能改掉拖延的坏习惯。

被动语态:  (outgrow doesn't typically use passive voice.  The growth is inherent to the subject.)


疑问句:
Will she outgrow this phase quickly? = 她会很快度过这个阶段吗？
Has the company outgrown its limitations? = 公司已经克服了它的局限性了吗？


否定句:
He hasn't outgrown his fear of heights yet. = 他还没克服恐高症。
The project hasn't outgrown its original budget. = 这个项目还没超出最初的预算。


PHRASE->
outgrow one's clothes = 穿小了衣服
outgrow one's shoes = 穿小了鞋子
outgrow a problem = 克服问题
outgrow a habit = 改掉习惯
outgrow a phase = 度过某一阶段
