countersuing: /ˌkaʊntərˈsuːɪŋ/ | v. | 反诉，反控 | To file a lawsuit against someone who has already sued you.  It's a retaliatory legal action. | 例句：After being sued for breach of contract, the company countersued, claiming the other party had failed to fulfill their obligations.  在被控违反合同后，该公司提起反诉，声称对方未履行其义务。


例句：The defendant countersued the plaintiff for defamation.  被告以诽谤罪对原告提起反诉。


近义词：filing a counterclaim, bringing a countersuit | 反义词：settling, withdrawing a lawsuit


USAGE->
