reflexives:/rɪˈflɛksɪvz/| n.|反身代词；反射性动作|Reflexives are pronouns that refer back to the subject of the clause. They indicate that the subject performs the action on itself.  Examples include myself, yourself, himself, herself, itself, ourselves, yourselves, and themselves.|例句：He hurt himself playing football. 他踢足球时伤到了自己。She looked at herself in the mirror. 她照镜子看着自己。|近义词：self-referential pronouns|反义词：objective pronouns


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I wash myself every morning. = 我每天早上都洗漱。
She often praises herself. = 她经常表扬自己。


一般过去时:
He blamed himself for the accident. = 他为那次事故责备自己。
They enjoyed themselves at the party. = 他们在派对上玩得很开心。


现在完成时:
She has hurt herself badly. = 她伤得很严重。
We have helped ourselves to the food. = 我们自己拿了食物。


过去完成时:
He had already prepared himself for the interview. = 他已经准备好接受面试了。
They had exhausted themselves with work. = 他们工作把自己累垮了。


将来时:
I will treat myself to a nice dinner. = 我将请自己吃一顿丰盛的晚餐。
They will dress themselves before going out. = 他们出门前会自己穿衣服。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would look after myself better. = 如果我是你，我会更好地照顾自己。
I wish I had expressed myself more clearly. = 我希望我表达得更清楚一些。


被动语态:
The problem solved itself eventually. = 这个问题最终自己解决了。(注意：被动语态中reflexive pronoun的使用较为特殊，通常表示事物自行发生作用)
The door closed itself with a bang. = 门砰的一声自己关上了。(注意：被动语态中reflexive pronoun的使用较为特殊，通常表示事物自行发生作用)


疑问句:
Did you hurt yourself? = 你伤到自己了吗？
Can she manage herself? = 她能自己应付吗？


否定句:
He didn't help himself to any more food. = 他没有再吃东西。
She didn't express herself very well. = 她表达得不太好。


PHRASE->
by oneself = 独自地
in itself = 本身
of itself = 自行地
for oneself = 为自己
to oneself = 自言自语
