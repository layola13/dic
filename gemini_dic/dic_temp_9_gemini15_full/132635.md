nondisciplinary: /ˌnɒnˈdɪsɪplɪnəri/ /ˌnɑːnˈdɪsɪplɪnɛri/ | adj. | 非纪律性的；非处罚性的 | Not relating to or involving punishment or disciplinary action.  This adjective describes actions, processes, or situations that are not related to formal discipline or punishment. |


例句：

The company's response to the employee's mistake was nondisciplinary, focusing instead on retraining and support.  公司的回应员工的错误是非处罚性的，而是侧重于再培训和支持。

The nondisciplinary meeting aimed to address concerns and find solutions collaboratively.  这次非纪律性会议旨在共同解决问题和寻找解决方案。


近义词：informal, unofficial, nonpunitive


反义词：disciplinary, punitive, corrective


词性: adjective
