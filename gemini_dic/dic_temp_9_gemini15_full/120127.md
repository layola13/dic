min:/mɪn/ | n. |分钟；极小量 | a unit of time equal to 60 seconds |例句：The meeting lasted for 30 min. 会议持续了30分钟。|例句：I only need a min to finish this. 我只需要一分钟就能完成这个。|近义词：minute |反义词:hour, age


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A minute is 60 seconds.  = 一分钟是60秒。
How many minutes are there in an hour? = 一小时有多少分钟？

一般过去时:
The meeting lasted ten minutes. = 会议持续了十分钟。
He spent five minutes on the phone. = 他在电话上花了五分钟。

现在完成时:
We have waited for five minutes. = 我们已经等了五分钟。
She has spent many minutes on this problem. = 她已经在这个问题上花了许多分钟。

过去完成时:
They had waited for twenty minutes before the bus arrived. = 公共汽车到达之前，他们已经等了二十分钟。
I had already spent an hour on this assignment, but I still had five minutes left to complete it. = 我已经在作业上花了整整一个小时，但是我还剩五分钟可以完成。


将来时:
I will call you in five minutes. = 五分钟后我给你打电话。
The meeting will start in ten minutes. = 会议将在十分钟后开始。

虚拟语气:
If I had another minute, I would finish this email. = 如果我还有多一分钟，我会完成这封电子邮件。
I wish I had had a few more minutes to prepare. = 我希望我有几分钟的时间来准备。

被动语态:
Many minutes were spent preparing this presentation. = 准备这个演示文稿花了许多时间。
Five more minutes are needed to complete the task. = 完成这项任务还需要五分钟。

疑问句:
How many minutes are left? = 还剩多少分钟？
How long will the meeting take, in minutes? = 会议需要多长时间，以分钟为单位？

否定句:
I don't have a minute to spare. = 我没有多余的时间。
He didn't spend more than five minutes on the homework. = 他在作业上花费的时间不超过五分钟。
