eskar:/ˈɛskɑːr/| n.|冰碛垄，冰川垄|A long, narrow, winding ridge of glacial drift; an esker is formed by the deposition of sediment from meltwater streams flowing within or beneath a glacier.|例句：The esker snaked across the landscape, a testament to the power of ancient glaciers. 冰碛垄蜿蜒穿过这片土地，这是古代冰川力量的证明。  The hikers followed the esker, using it as a natural pathway.  徒步旅行者沿着冰碛垄走，把它当作一条天然小路。|近义词：ridge, mound, embankment|反义词：valley, depression
