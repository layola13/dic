law:/lɔː/| /lɑː/ | n. |法律；法规；规律；法则 |The body of rules and principles that govern the conduct of individuals and communities within a society, enforced through established institutions.  Laws are designed to maintain order, protect rights, and resolve disputes. |Example:  The law prohibits driving under the influence of alcohol.  法律禁止酒后驾车。

The government is considering new laws to protect the environment. 政府正在考虑新的环保法。|近义词：rule, regulation, principle, statute |反义词：chaos, disorder, anarchy

The law requires drivers to wear seatbelts. = 法律要求司机系安全带。
This law applies to all citizens. = 这条法律适用于所有公民。
