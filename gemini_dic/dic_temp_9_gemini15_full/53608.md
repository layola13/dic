discard:/dɪˈskɑːrd/| vt. n.|丢弃；抛弃；放弃|To throw something away because it is useless or unwanted.  |例句：He discarded the old newspapers. 他扔掉了旧报纸。|例句：The company decided to discard its outdated technology. 公司决定放弃其过时的技术。|近义词：throw away, get rid of, abandon, reject|反义词：keep, retain, preserve|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He discards old clothes every year. = 他每年都丢弃旧衣服。
They discard broken tools immediately. = 他们立即丢弃损坏的工具。


一般过去时:
She discarded the letter without reading it. = 她没有阅读就扔掉了信。
We discarded the faulty equipment. = 我们丢弃了有故障的设备。


现在完成时:
They have discarded all the evidence. = 他们已经销毁了所有证据。
I have discarded my old habits. = 我已经改掉了我的坏习惯。


过去完成时:
He had discarded his old beliefs before he met her. = 他在她之前已经放弃了他的旧信仰。
The company had discarded the project by the end of the year. = 公司在年底前已经放弃了该项目。


将来时:
They will discard the outdated machines next month. = 他们下个月将丢弃过时的机器。
She will discard the broken vase. = 她将丢弃破碎的花瓶。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would discard that idea. = 如果我是你，我会放弃那个想法。
I wish I had discarded that old car sooner. = 我真希望我早点把那辆旧车扔掉。


被动语态:
The old furniture was discarded. = 旧家具被丢弃了。
The proposal was discarded by the committee. = 该提案被委员会否决了。


疑问句:
Will you discard the broken toys? = 你会丢弃坏掉的玩具吗？
Did they discard the confidential documents? = 他们丢弃了那些机密文件了吗？


否定句:
He didn't discard the letter. = 他没有丢弃那封信。
They did not discard their old traditions. = 他们没有丢弃他们的古老传统。


PHRASE->

discard a plan = 放弃计划

discard a hypothesis = 丢弃一个假设
