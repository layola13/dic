naturalism:/ˈnætʃrəlɪzəm/| n. |自然主义；自然观|A literary movement that attempts to depict life as it really is, without idealization or romanticism.  It emphasizes realism, objectivity, and often focuses on the effects of heredity and environment on human character. |Example:  The novel is a prime example of naturalism, portraying the harsh realities of poverty and social injustice. 这部小说是自然主义的典型例子，描绘了贫困和社会不公的残酷现实。

The play's characters are depicted with stark naturalism, devoid of sentimentality.  这出戏的人物刻画得非常自然主义，没有一丝感伤。|realism, verisimilitude|idealism, romanticism|名词
