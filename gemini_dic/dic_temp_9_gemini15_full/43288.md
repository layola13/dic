craftswoman:/ˈkræftsˌwʊmən/| n. |女工匠，女手艺人 | A craftswoman is a woman skilled in a particular craft, especially one involving making things by hand.  She possesses expertise and artistry in her chosen craft. |例句：She is a highly skilled craftswoman, renowned for her intricate pottery. 她是一位技艺精湛的女工匠，以其精美的陶器而闻名。|例句：The craftswoman meticulously painted each detail on the miniature dollhouse.  这位女工匠一丝不苟地在微型娃娃屋上描绘每一个细节。|近义词：artisan, artist, craftsperson |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with unskilled worker.

词源:
craft (n.) + woman (n.)  "craft"源自古英语 *cræft，意为“力量，技巧，技能”，最终源自日耳曼语系。 "woman" 源自古英语 *wīfmann，意为“女人”。  因此，craftswoman 指的是掌握某种工艺技能的女性。
