cavorts:/kəˈvɔːrts/| v.|嬉戏；跳跃；欢闹|To move in a lively and playful way; to frolic.|例句：The children cavorted joyfully in the park.孩子们在公园里快乐地嬉戏。 The horses cavorted in the field. 马儿在田野里欢腾跳跃。|近义词：frolic, romp, gambol, prance|反义词：be still, be quiet


USAGE->

The puppies cavort in the garden every afternoon. = 小狗每天下午都在花园里嬉戏。
They cavort around the maypole during the festival. = 节日期间，他们在五月柱周围欢快地跳跃。

The horses had cavorted through the meadow before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，马儿们已经在草地上嬉戏玩耍了。

否定句:

词源:
源自古法语 *cavorter，意思是“跳跃，踢腿”。  最终可能源于拉丁语 *caballare，意思是“骑马”。
