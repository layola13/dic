gum-up:/ˈɡʌmʌp/| vt. |使堵塞；使混乱；使失效|To obstruct or block something, causing it to malfunction or cease to function properly.  It can also refer to causing disorganization or confusion.|例句：The heavy rain gummed up the traffic. 大雨堵塞了交通。

The new software gummed up the whole system. 新软件导致整个系统瘫痪。|近义词：clog, jam, obstruct, mess up |反义词：unclog, clear, fix, streamline


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The constant interruptions gum up my work. = 不断的打断影响了我的工作。
Heavy snow gums up the roads every winter. = 每到冬天，大雪都会堵塞道路。


一般过去时:
