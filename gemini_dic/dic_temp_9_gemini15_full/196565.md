sesshin:/ˈsɛʃɪn/| n.|禅修;静坐|A period of intensive Zen Buddhist meditation, typically lasting several days and involving periods of sitting meditation (zazen) and walking meditation (kinhin).  It's a practice designed to cultivate mindfulness and deepen one's understanding of Zen principles.|例句：He participated in a seven-day sesshin at a mountain monastery. 他参加了在山区寺院进行的为期七天的禅修。|近义词：retreat, meditation intensive|反义词：distraction, frivolity|名词
