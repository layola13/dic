particularised: /pəˈtɪkjʊləraɪzd/ | v. | 使具体化；详细说明 | To give details or specifics about something; to make something more specific or detailed. | The report particularised the challenges faced by small businesses.  这份报告详细说明了小型企业面临的挑战。|  specified, detailed, elaborated, delineated | generalized, vague, abstract | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He particularises his needs in the order. = 他在订单中详细说明了他的需求。
She particularises the instructions for the task. = 她详细说明了这项任务的指示。


一般过去时:
The lawyer particularised the terms of the contract. = 律师详细说明了合同条款。
The witness particularised the events of that night. = 目击者详细说明了那天晚上的事件。


现在完成时:
He has particularised his concerns to the management. = 他已向管理层详细说明了他的担忧。
She has particularised the steps required for the project. = 她已经详细说明了项目所需的步骤。


过去完成时:
The historian had particularised the causes of the war in his previous book. = 这位历史学家在他之前的书中详细阐述了战争的起因。
They had particularised their objections before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经详细说明了他们的反对意见。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If he particularised the problem, we could solve it. = 如果他详细说明了这个问题，我们就能解决它。
I wish he had particularised his request more clearly. = 我希望他更清楚地说明了他的请求。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
