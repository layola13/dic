miscataloging:/ˌmɪsˈkætəlɒɡɪŋ/ | n. | 错误分类，错分 | The act of incorrectly classifying or categorizing something.  This can refer to items in a library, museum, database, or any other system of organization.  | Example:  The miscataloging of the ancient artifacts led to years of confusion and research delays. = 古代文物的错误分类导致了多年的混乱和研究延误。 The museum's miscataloging of its collection resulted in many items being difficult to locate. = 博物馆对其馆藏的错误分类导致许多物品难以找到。 |近义词：misclassification, misfiling,  incorrect categorization | 反义词：accurate cataloging, proper classification | 名词
