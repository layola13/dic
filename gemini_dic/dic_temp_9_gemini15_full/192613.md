scuppered:/ˈskʌpəd/ | vt. |使失败；破坏；毁掉 | To scupper something means to ruin or destroy it completely, often unexpectedly.  It can refer to plans, hopes, or projects. | The sudden storm scuppered our plans for a picnic.  暴风雨突然来袭，打乱了我们的野餐计划。 The new regulations scuppered the company's expansion plans. 新规章制度破坏了公司的扩张计划。 | ruin, wreck, destroy, thwart | succeed, achieve, accomplish | 动词
