heroine:/ˈhɛroʊɪn/|/ˈhɪərəʊɪn/| n. |女英雄；女主角；海洛因|A heroine is a woman who is admired for her courage, outstanding achievements, or noble qualities.  The word can also refer to the female lead in a story, or, unfortunately, to the illegal drug. |例句：She is a heroine in my eyes for saving the child from the fire. 她救孩子于火海，在我眼中是个女英雄。

She played the heroine in the Shakespearean play. 她在这部莎士比亚戏剧中扮演女主角。


近义词：hero, leading lady, protagonist |反义词：villain, antagonist |词性：名词
