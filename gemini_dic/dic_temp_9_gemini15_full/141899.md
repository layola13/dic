orderedness:/ˈɔːdədnəs/ | n. | 有序性，整齐，规律性 | The state or quality of being ordered; arrangement or systematization.  It refers to the degree to which elements are arranged in a structured and predictable way. This can apply to physical objects, abstract concepts, or sequences of events. | The orderedness of the library's collection was impressive. 图书馆藏书的整齐有序令人印象深刻。 The lack of orderedness in his notes made it difficult to understand his ideas. 他笔记的杂乱无章使得难以理解他的想法。 | organization, system, regularity, structure | chaos, disorder, randomness, disarray | 名词
