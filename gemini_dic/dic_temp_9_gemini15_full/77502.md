gazillion:/ˈɡæzɪljən/| n. |极大数量，无数 |An informal term for an extremely large, unspecified number.  It doesn't represent a real number. |例句：I've told you a gazillion times to clean your room!  我已经告诉你无数次了，把你的房间打扫干净！|近义词：a million, a billion, tons of, heaps of, countless |反义词：few, little, a handful

USAGE->
一般现在时:
There are a gazillion stars in the sky. = 天上有无数颗星星。
He has a gazillion things to do today. = 他今天有无数的事情要做。

一般过去时:
She spent a gazillion dollars on her wedding. = 她在她婚礼上花了巨额的钱。
They ate a gazillion cookies at the party. = 他们在派对上吃了无数的饼干。
