luckies:/ˈlʌkiːz/| n. |幸运儿，幸运的东西|Lucky items or people.  The word is the plural form of "lucky".|例句：The luckies won the lottery. 幸运儿们中了彩票。
例句：She considers her children her luckies. 她认为她的孩子们是她人生中的幸运儿。|近义词：fortunates, the favored|反义词：unfortunates, the unlucky
