counter-marque:/ˈkaʊntərmɑːrk/| n.|反证标记；副本；对证|A counter-marque is a duplicate or copy of a document, especially one used for verification or as a counter-signature.  It can also refer to a mark placed on something to show it has been inspected or approved.|例句：The shipment required a counter-marque for customs clearance. 这批货物需要一份反证标记才能办理海关清关。

例句：The museum director used a counter-marque to verify the authenticity of the painting. 博物馆馆长用反证标记来验证这幅画的真伪。


近义词：duplicate, copy, counterpart, verification|反义词：original
