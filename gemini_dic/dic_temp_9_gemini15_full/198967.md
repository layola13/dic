sighted:/ˈsaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 有视力的；被看见的；瞄准的 | Having or using sight; seen; aimed.  |  例句：The sighted man could easily navigate the dark room.  (这个有视力的人很容易就能在黑暗的房间里行走。)  The bird was sighted near the lake. (那只鸟在湖边被看见了。) | 近义词：observed, spotted, perceived, visible | 反义词：blind, unseen, invisible


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is sighted. = 他有视力。
The ship is sighted from the shore. = 从岸上可以看到那艘船。


一般过去时:
She was sighted yesterday. = 昨天有人看见了她。
The plane was sighted over the mountains. = 飞机在山区上空被发现。


现在完成时:
The bird has been sighted several times this week. = 这周这只鸟已经被看见好几次了。
Have you ever sighted a whale? = 你曾经见过鲸鱼吗？


过去完成时:
The ship had been sighted before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，那艘船就已经被发现了。
They had sighted the land before nightfall. = 他们天黑前就看到了陆地。


将来时:
The comet will be sighted next month. = 下个月就能看到这颗彗星。
We will have sighted the mountain by sunset. = 我们日落前就能看到那座山。


虚拟语气:
If I were sighted, I could drive. = 如果我有视力，我就能开车。
If the bird had been sighted earlier, we could have taken better photos. = 如果早点看到那只鸟，我们就能拍到更好的照片。


被动语态:
The plane was sighted by the radar. = 飞机被雷达发现了。
The missing child was sighted near the park. = 失踪的孩子在公园附近被发现。


疑问句:
Was the suspect sighted near the bank? = 嫌疑犯在银行附近被看见了吗？
Have you ever sighted a shooting star? = 你曾经见过流星吗？


否定句:
The ship was not sighted during the storm. = 暴风雨期间没有看到那艘船。
He has not yet been sighted. = 他还没被发现。


PHRASE->
well-sighted: 视力好的
short-sighted: 近视的
long-sighted: 远视的
sighted in: 瞄准
sighted on: 瞄准
