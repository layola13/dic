redingote:/ˌrɛdɪŋˈɡoʊt/ | n. |  女式长外套；骑马服 | A long, usually loose-fitting woman's coat, often reaching to the ankles, and sometimes worn for riding.  It typically features a close-fitting waist and long sleeves.  The style originated in the 18th and 19th centuries. | Example:  She wore a stylish redingote for the horse show. 她穿着时髦的骑马服参加马术表演。 |近义词：overcoat, wrap coat, riding habit | 反义词： None specifically, but  terms like "short jacket" or "dress" could be considered antonyms in terms of length and style. | 名词
