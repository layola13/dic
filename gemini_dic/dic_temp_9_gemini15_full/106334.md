kogin:/ˈkoʊɡɪn/| n. |刺子绣，一种日本传统刺绣 |Kogin is a type of Japanese embroidery characterized by geometric patterns and simple stitches, often using only black thread on white fabric. It originated in the Tsugaru region of Aomori Prefecture.|The finished kogin piece is a beautiful example of traditional Japanese craftsmanship.  这件完成的刺子绣作品是日本传统工艺的精美典范。|近义词：embroidery, needlework |反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑与其相对的装饰风格，如：elaborate embroidery)
