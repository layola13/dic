nanochemist:/ˈnænəʊˌkɛmɪst/ | n. |纳米化学家 | A scientist who specializes in nanochemistry, the branch of chemistry dealing with materials at the nanoscale (typically 1-100 nanometers).  Nanochemists work with atoms and molecules to create new materials and devices with unique properties. | Example:  The nanochemist carefully synthesized nanoparticles for use in targeted drug delivery.  纳米化学家仔细合成了用于靶向药物递送的纳米粒子。 | 近义词: nanoscientist, materials scientist | 反义词: N/A
