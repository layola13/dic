shrublet:/ˈʃrʌblət/| n. |小灌木；矮灌木|A small shrub; a diminutive shrub.  It is often used to describe a young or underdeveloped shrub. |例句：The gardener carefully pruned the shrublets to encourage bushier growth. 园丁仔细修剪小灌木以促进其生长得更茂盛。
The hillside was dotted with various shrublets, creating a tapestry of green and color. 山坡上点缀着各种矮灌木，构成了一幅绿色和彩色的图画。|近义词：small shrub, dwarf shrub, bushlet |反义词：large tree, tall plant
