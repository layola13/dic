disbeliefs:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːfs/| n. |怀疑；不相信；不信|The plural form of "disbelief," indicating multiple instances of not believing something.  It refers to a collection of instances of doubt or lack of faith in something.|例句：His disbeliefs about the government's claims were widespread among his friends.  他的朋友们广泛怀疑政府的说法。|近义词：doubts, suspicions, incredulity|反义词：beliefs, convictions, acceptances|名词
