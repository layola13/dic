longitudes:/ˈlɒŋdʒɪtjuːdz/ | n. |经度；经线 |The distances east or west of the prime meridian, measured in degrees.  Longitudes are used to locate points on the Earth's surface.  |例句：The ship's position was determined by its latitude and longitude. 这艘船的位置是由它的纬度和经度确定的。
The map shows the longitudes and latitudes of major cities. 这张地图显示了主要城市的经度和纬度。|近义词：meridians (as a system)|反义词：latitudes
