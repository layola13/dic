proleg:/ˈproʊlɛɡ/| n.|腹足，伪足|A proleg is a fleshy, unjointed appendage on the abdomen of some insect larvae, such as caterpillars, used for locomotion and gripping.  They are often referred to as false legs, as opposed to true legs, which are jointed. |例句：Caterpillars use their prolegs to cling to leaves and branches.  毛毛虫用它们的腹足抓住树叶和树枝。

例句：The prolegs of the inchworm help it to move along.  尺蠖的腹足帮助它移动。


近义词：false leg, abdominal appendage|反义词：true leg
