outroot:/ˌaʊtˈruːt/| vt.|根除；铲除；连根拔起|To remove completely; to eradicate.|例句：The gardener outrooted the weeds from the flower bed. 园丁将花坛里的杂草连根拔起。|例句：They tried to outroot corruption within the organization. 他们试图根除组织内部的腐败。|近义词：eradicate, exterminate, eliminate|反义词：plant, cultivate, foster|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They outroot weeds regularly. = 他们定期清除杂草。
The farmer outroots diseased plants. = 农民将患病的植物连根拔起。

一般过去时:
He outrooted the old tree. = 他把那棵老树连根拔起了。
We outrooted the problem at its source. = 我们从根源上解决了这个问题。

现在完成时:
They have outrooted most of the invasive species. = 他们已经根除了大部分入侵物种。
The government has outrooted the criminal gang. = 政府已经消灭了这个犯罪团伙。

过去完成时:
They had outrooted the infection before it spread. = 他们在感染蔓延之前就将其根除了。
She had outrooted all her bad habits by the time she turned thirty. = 到她三十岁的时候，她已经改掉了所有的坏习惯。

将来时:
We will outroot poverty in our community. = 我们将在我们的社区中根除贫困。
They will outroot the source of the pollution. = 他们将清除污染源。

虚拟语气:
If we outrooted corruption, the country would prosper. = 如果我们铲除了腐败，国家就会繁荣。
I wish they had outrooted the rumors before they spread. = 我希望他们在谣言传播之前就将其根除了。

被动语态:
The weeds were outrooted by the gardener. = 杂草被园丁拔除了。
The problem was outrooted by a new strategy. = 这个问题通过一项新策略得到了解决。

疑问句:
Did they outroot the problem successfully? = 他们成功地解决了问题了吗？
Have they outrooted all the weeds yet? = 他们已经把所有的杂草都拔了吗？

否定句:
They didn't outroot the problem completely. = 他们没有完全解决这个问题。
We couldn't outroot the deeply entrenched prejudice. = 我们无法根除根深蒂固的偏见。
