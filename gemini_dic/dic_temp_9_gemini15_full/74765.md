freeze-dried:/ˈfriːzˌdraɪd/| adj.|冷冻干燥的|Freeze-dried food is food that has had its moisture removed by freezing and then sublimation. This process preserves the food and makes it lightweight and easy to store.|例句：Freeze-dried coffee is convenient for camping trips. 冷冻干燥咖啡方便野营旅行。例句：Many hikers carry freeze-dried meals for their expeditions. 许多徒步旅行者携带冷冻干燥的食品进行探险。|近义词：dehydrated, lyophilized|反义词：fresh, wet|
