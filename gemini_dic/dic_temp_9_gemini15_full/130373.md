non-usable: /ˌnɒnˈjuːzəbl/ | adj. |不可用的，不能使用的|  Not able to be used; unfit for use.  This typically refers to something that is either broken, damaged, or otherwise unsuitable for its intended purpose.  It can also refer to something that is legally or practically impossible to use.|

例句：

The old equipment is non-usable. = 这台旧设备无法使用了。

The software became non-usable after the recent update. =  最近的更新后，软件变得无法使用了。


近义词：unusable, unusable, inoperable, defunct, ineffective,  useless

反义词：usable, functional, operable, effective


词性: 形容词
