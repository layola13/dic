levitation: /ˌlɛvɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 悬浮；漂浮 | The act or process of rising and remaining suspended in the air, without physical support.  It often implies a magical or supernatural effect, but can also refer to scientific phenomena.  | Example: The magician performed a breathtaking levitation act, making the assistant float in mid-air.  魔术师表演了一个令人叹为观止的悬浮魔术，让助手漂浮在空中。

|近义词: suspension, floating, hovering |反义词: grounding, settling
