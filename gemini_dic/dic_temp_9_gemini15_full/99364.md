interdependent:/ˌɪntərˈdɪpɛndənt/| adj. |相互依赖的，互相依存的 |Mutually dependent;  characterized by reciprocal dependence.  Two or more things are interdependent if they rely on each other.  This relationship can be between individuals, groups, countries, or even systems. |例句：The economies of many countries are interdependent.  许多国家的经济是相互依赖的。

例句：The success of the project is interdependent on the cooperation of all team members.  项目的成功取决于所有团队成员的合作。


近义词：mutually dependent, reciprocal, interconnected, interrelated

反义词：independent, autonomous, self-sufficient


词性：形容词
