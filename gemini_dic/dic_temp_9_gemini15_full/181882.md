relume:/rɪˈluːm/| vt.|重新点燃；使复燃 |To light or ignite again; to rekindle.  |例句：He relumed the dying embers in the fireplace. 他重新点燃了壁炉里将熄的余烬。 |例句：The news relumed her hopes for a better future.  这个消息重新燃起了她对美好未来的希望。|近义词：reignite, rekindle, revive |反义词：extinguish, quench
