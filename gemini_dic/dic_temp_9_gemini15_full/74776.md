freezes:/friːzɪz/| v.|冻结；凝固；使僵硬|To change from a liquid to a solid state because of cold.  It can also refer to a sudden stop or halt.|例句：The water freezes at 0 degrees Celsius.  水在零摄氏度结冰。

The project froze due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，项目被搁置了。|近义词：congeal, solidify, harden|反义词：melt, thaw


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water freezes at 0°C. = 水在0°C结冰。
He always freezes up during interviews. = 他在面试时总是紧张得说不出话来。

一般过去时:
The lake froze over last night. = 湖昨天晚上结冰了。
My computer froze while I was working. = 我工作时电脑死机了。

现在完成时:
The pipes have frozen because of the cold weather. = 由于寒冷的天气，管道已经冻住了。
It has frozen solid. = 它已经完全冻住了。

过去完成时:
The river had frozen before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，河水已经结冰了。
The food had frozen solid in the freezer. = 食物在冰箱里已经冻得硬邦邦的了。

将来时:
The forecast says it will freeze tonight. = 预报说今晚会结冰。
I think the milk will freeze if we leave it outside. = 我想如果我们把牛奶放在外面，它会冻住的。

虚拟语气:
If it froze tonight, we'd have to cancel the picnic. = 如果今晚结冰，我们就得取消野餐。
I wish the argument had frozen before it got so heated. = 我希望争论在变得如此激烈之前就停止了。

被动语态:
The meat was frozen for preservation. = 肉被冷冻起来保存。
The account was frozen due to suspicious activity. = 由于可疑活动，账户被冻结了。

疑问句:
Does water freeze at 0°C? = 水在0°C结冰吗？
Does the ground freeze in your area during winter? = 你们那儿冬天地面会结冰吗？

否定句:
The ice cream didn't freeze properly. = 冰激凌没有冻好。
The pond didn't freeze over this winter. = 这个池塘今年冬天没有结冰。



PHRASE->
freeze over = 完全结冰
freeze out = 排挤；使…受冷落
freeze up = 冻结；（因紧张）说不出话来
frozen food = 冷冻食品
deep freeze = 深度冷冻
