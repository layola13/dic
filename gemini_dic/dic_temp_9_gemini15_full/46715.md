dances:/dænsiz/ | v. |舞蹈；跳舞 |To move rhythmically to music, typically following a set of steps.  It can also refer to the act of moving in a lively and energetic way.|例句：The children dances joyfully to the music. 孩子们随着音乐快乐地跳舞。 |例句：The leaves danced in the wind. 叶子在风中飞舞。|近义词：moves, sways, twirls |反义词：stands still, remains motionless

USAGE->
一般现在时:
She dances every Saturday. = 她每个星期六都跳舞。
He dances with grace and elegance. = 他跳舞优雅而富有魅力。

一般过去时:
They danced all night at the wedding. = 他们在婚礼上跳了一整夜的舞。
She danced beautifully at the prom. = 她在舞会上跳得很美。

现在完成时:
I have danced many times in my life. = 我一生中跳过很多次舞。
She has never danced before. = 她以前从未跳过舞。

过去完成时:
We had danced for hours before we got tired. = 我们跳了几个小时的舞才感到疲倦。
They had already danced when I arrived. = 我到的时候他们已经在跳舞了。

将来时:
I will dance at the party tonight. = 我今晚将在派对上跳舞。
We will dance until dawn. = 我们将跳到黎明。


虚拟语气:
If I could dance, I would join the competition. = 如果我会跳舞，我会参加比赛。
I wish I could dance like her. = 我希望我能像她一样跳舞。

被动语态:
The dance was danced beautifully. = 这支舞跳得很美。（此处被动语态略显不自然，通常不用被动）
The waltz is danced by many couples. = 华尔兹舞由许多情侣跳。（此处被动语态更常用）


疑问句:
Do you dance? = 你跳舞吗？
Can she dance the tango? = 她会跳探戈舞吗？

否定句:
I don't dance very well. = 我跳舞不太好。
He didn't dance at the party. = 他没有在派对上跳舞。
