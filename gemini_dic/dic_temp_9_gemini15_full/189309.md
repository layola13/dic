safaris:/səˈfɑːriːz/ | n. | 旅行；狩猎旅行；探险 | A safari is an adventurous journey, typically to observe wildlife in their natural habitat, often in Africa.  It can involve camping, jeep rides, and guided tours. | We went on a safari in Kenya and saw lions, elephants, and giraffes. = 我们在肯尼亚进行了一次狩猎旅行，看到了狮子、大象和长颈鹿。  They booked a luxury safari to Tanzania. = 他们预订了一次去坦桑尼亚的豪华狩猎旅行。 | expedition, excursion, hunting trip | sedentary lifestyle, homebody
