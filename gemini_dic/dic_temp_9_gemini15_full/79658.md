glossology:/ɡlɒˈsɒlədʒi/ | n. |词源学，词义学 |The scientific study of the structure and history of vocabulary and word meanings.  It explores how words change over time, their etymologies, and their relationships to each other. |Example: Glossology helps us understand the evolution of language and the subtle shifts in word meaning. 词源学帮助我们理解语言的演变和词义的细微变化。|近义词: lexicology, semantics |反义词: N/A
