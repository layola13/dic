odometers: /əˈdɑːmɪtərz/ | n. |里程表；里程计 | A device that measures the distance traveled by a vehicle.  Odometers are typically found in cars, bicycles, and other vehicles to track mileage.  | Example:  The odometer showed that we had driven 500 miles.  里程表显示我们已经行驶了500英里。


The odometer reading is inaccurate after the accident. 事故后里程表读数不准。|近义词：mileage indicator, distance recorder |反义词：None |名词
