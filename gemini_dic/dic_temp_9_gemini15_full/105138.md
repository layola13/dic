ketch:/kɛtʃ/| n.|双桅帆船|A ketch is a sailing vessel with two masts, where the mizzen mast (the after mast) is shorter than the main mast and is stepped aft of the rudder post.  Ketches are often used for recreational sailing or smaller commercial purposes.|例句：He sailed his ketch across the Atlantic. 他驾驶他的双桅帆船横渡了大西洋。  The old ketch was a beautiful sight in the harbor. 那艘老式的双桅帆船在港口里是一道美丽的风景线。|近义词：yawl, schooner|反义词：monohull (in terms of hull design, not necessarily mast configuration)|名词
