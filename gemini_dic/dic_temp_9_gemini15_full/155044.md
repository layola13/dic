petitioner:/pəˈtɪʃənər/| n. |请愿人，起诉人 |A petitioner is a person who makes a formal request to an authority, typically through a written petition.  They are often involved in legal proceedings or seeking a change in policy or law. |例句：The petitioner argued for the right to appeal the decision. 请愿人辩称有权对该决定提出上诉。

例句：The petitioner filed a lawsuit against the company. 起诉人对该公司提起诉讼。


近义词：applicant, supplicant, claimant
反义词：respondent, defendant


词性:名词
