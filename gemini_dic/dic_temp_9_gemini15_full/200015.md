skein:/skeɪn/| n.|线团；一股；一系列；一串|A skein is a length of yarn or thread wound into a loose coil.  It can also refer to a tangled or complicated mass of something, or a series or sequence of events.|例句：The bird's nest was made of twigs and a skein of hair.  鸟巢是用树枝和一股头发做的。
