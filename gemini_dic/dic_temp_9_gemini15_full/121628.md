misparaphrases: /ˌmɪsˈpærəfreɪzɪz/ | n. | 误释，曲解 |  The act of misrepresenting or incorrectly summarizing someone else's words or ideas. It involves altering the original meaning, often unintentionally. | 例句：His misparaphrases of the author's arguments led to a misunderstanding of the central thesis.  他的对作者论点的误释导致了对中心论题的误解。


例句：The student's misparaphrases of the text resulted in a lower grade on the essay. 学生对课文的曲解导致论文分数降低。


近义词：misinterpretations, misrepresentations, distortions, paraphrases (incorrect ones)


反义词：accurate paraphrases, correct interpretations, faithful summaries


词性:名词
