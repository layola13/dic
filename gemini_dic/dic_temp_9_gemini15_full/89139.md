homecomings: /ˈhoʊmˌkʌmɪŋz/ | n. | 返乡；返校；回归 | The act of returning home, especially after a long absence; also, a celebration or event marking such a return, often associated with schools or colleges.  | Example: The annual homecoming game was a highlight of the school year.  一年一度的返校节比赛是学校一年中最精彩的活动。  |近义词：return, arrival, reunion | 反义词：departure, leaving
