franchisee:/ˌfrænˈtʃaɪziː/| n. |特许经营者，加盟商 |A franchisee is a person or business that is granted the right to sell or distribute a company's goods or services under the company's brand name.  They typically pay a fee and royalties to the franchisor in exchange for the right to use the brand and business model. |Example:  He's a franchisee of a popular fast-food chain. 他是某家受欢迎快餐连锁店的加盟商。|近义词：licensee, concessionaire |反义词：franchisor
