gavelling:/ˈɡævəlɪŋ/| n. |用木槌敲击；（议会等）休会；|The act of repeatedly striking a gavel, often to call a meeting to order or adjourn it.  It can also refer to the sound produced by this action.|例句：The gavelling of the hammer signaled the end of the debate.  木槌的敲击声标志着辩论的结束。The constant gavelling was distracting.  不断的敲槌声令人分心。|近义词：hammering, rapping, knocking|反义词：silence, quiet
