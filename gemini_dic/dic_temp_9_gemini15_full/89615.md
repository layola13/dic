hooey:/ˈhuːiː/| n. |胡说，废话，无稽之谈 |Nonsense; something untrue or foolish.  It's often used informally to express disbelief or dismissal of something said.|例句：That's a lot of hooey!  = 那些都是胡说八道！


That's pure hooey; don't believe a word of it. = 那是纯粹的胡说八道；别相信一个字。|近义词：balderdash, bunkum, rubbish, nonsense |反义词：truth, fact, reality |名词
