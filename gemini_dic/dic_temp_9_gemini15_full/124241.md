mossies:/ˈmɒsiz/| n. |蚊子（复数）|Mosquitoes,  small flying insects that bite and suck blood.  The word is a plural form of "mossie", a colloquial or informal term for mosquito.|例句：There were lots of mossies buzzing around the campfire.  营火周围有很多蚊子嗡嗡作响。


There were so many mossies in the swamp, we couldn't stay outside. 沼泽里的蚊子太多了，我们无法待在外面。|近义词：mosquitoes, gnats (if small)|反义词：无
