punning:/ˈpʌnɪŋ/| n. |双关语；玩弄文字游戏|The use of words that have two or more meanings to create a humorous or interesting effect.  It involves exploiting multiple meanings of a term, or of similar-sounding terms, for an intended humorous or rhetorical effect.|例句：His speech was full of puns. 他的演讲充满了双关语。

例句：The comedian's act relied heavily on puns.  这位喜剧演员的表演很大程度上依赖于双关语。


近义词：wordplay, double entendre, witticism|反义词：literalness, straightforwardness|词性：名词
