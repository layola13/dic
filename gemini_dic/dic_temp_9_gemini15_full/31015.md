challenged:/ˈtʃæləndʒd/ | adj. | 受到挑战的；有挑战性的 |  Presenting a challenge; difficult.  | Example: The team faced a challenged situation.  团队面临着一个充满挑战的局面。  The course is designed for challenged learners. 这门课程是为有学习困难的学生设计的。|近义词：difficult, demanding, taxing | 反义词：easy, simple, straightforward


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The task challenges him. = 这个任务对他来说是一个挑战。
The mountain challenges experienced climbers. = 这座山对经验丰富的登山者来说也是一个挑战。

一般过去时:
The athlete challenged the rules. = 运动员对规则提出了质疑。
The problem challenged our assumptions. = 这个问题挑战了我们的假设。

现在完成时:
She has challenged the status quo. = 她已经挑战了现状。
They have challenged the authority. = 他们已经挑战了权威。

过去完成时:
He had challenged the decision before. = 他之前就对这个决定提出过质疑。
We had challenged the system for years. = 我们多年来一直在挑战这个制度。

将来时:
They will challenge the results of the election. = 他们将质疑选举结果。
I will challenge him to a race. = 我将向他挑战赛跑。

虚拟语气:
If I were challenged, I would rise to the occasion. = 如果我面临挑战，我会迎难而上。
I wish I hadn't challenged him so aggressively. = 我真希望我没有那么咄咄逼人地挑战他。

被动语态:
His authority was challenged. = 他的权威受到了挑战。
The team was challenged by the difficult task. = 团队受到了这项艰巨任务的挑战。

疑问句:
Was the decision challenged? = 这个决定受到了质疑吗？
Are you challenged by this problem? = 你被这个问题难倒了吗？

否定句:
He didn't challenge my opinion. = 他没有质疑我的意见。
The students weren't challenged by the easy assignment. = 学生们没有被简单的作业难倒。
