care-for:/kerˈfɔːr/|/ˈkɛərfɔːr/| |vt.| |关心；照顾；喜欢|To feel concern or affection for someone or something; to provide care or attention to; to like or be fond of.|例句：She cares for her elderly parents. 她照顾她年迈的父母。|例句：I don't care for spicy food. 我不喜欢辛辣的食物。|近义词：look after, tend, cherish, like|反义词：neglect, despise, hate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I care for my pets very much. = 我非常关心我的宠物。
He cares for his sick mother every day. = 他每天都照顾他生病的母亲。


一般过去时:
She cared for her grandmother until her passing. = 她照顾她祖母直到她去世。
They cared for the abandoned kittens. = 他们照顾那些被遗弃的小猫。


现在完成时:
I have cared for many stray animals over the years. = 多年来，我已经照顾过许多流浪动物。
She has cared for her ailing husband with great patience. = 她以极大的耐心照顾她生病的丈夫。


过去完成时:
We had cared for the injured bird before releasing it back into the wild. = 我们在把受伤的小鸟放回野外之前已经照顾过它了。
They had cared for the children for many years before adopting them. = 在收养他们之前，他们已经照顾了这些孩子很多年了。


将来时:
I will care for my aging parents as long as they need me. = 我会在他们需要我的时候一直照顾我年迈的父母。
He will care for the plants while we are on vacation. = 我们度假期间他会照顾这些植物。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would care for more animals. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会照顾更多的动物。
I wish I could care for all the homeless animals in the world. = 我希望我能照顾世界上所有无家可归的动物。


被动语态:
The children are well cared for in the orphanage. = 孩子们在孤儿院里得到很好的照顾。
The injured soldier was carefully cared for by the medical team. = 受伤的士兵受到了医疗队的精心照顾。


疑问句:
Do you care for a cup of tea? = 你想喝杯茶吗？
Does he care for his new job? = 他喜欢他的新工作吗？（这里care for表示喜欢）


否定句:
I don't care for that kind of music. = 我不喜欢那种音乐。
She doesn't care for him anymore. = 她不再喜欢他了。


PHRASE->
care for nothing: 对什么都不在乎
care for oneself: 自理
care for others: 关心他人
take care of: 照顾
care about: 关心
