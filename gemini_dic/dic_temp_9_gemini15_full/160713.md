portrait: /ˈpɔː(r)treɪt/ | n. |肖像，画像 |A portrait is a painting, drawing, photograph, or other artistic representation of a person, especially one depicting the face.  It can also refer to a description of someone's character or personality. |例句：The artist painted a stunning portrait of the queen.  艺术家画了一幅女王的精彩肖像。

The writer created a vivid portrait of the protagonist. 作者创作了一个生动的主人公形象。|近义词：likeness, image, depiction |反义词：N/A

词源:
The word "portrait" comes from the Old French word "portraicture," which itself derives from the Late Latin word "protrahere," meaning "to draw forth" or "to portray."  This ultimately stems from the Latin prefix "pro-" (forth) and the verb "trahere" (to draw).
