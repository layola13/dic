graspingly:/ˈɡræspɪŋli/| adv.|贪婪地，渴望地，急切地 |  Describes an action done in a greedy or eager manner, often implying a strong desire to obtain something.  It suggests a forceful or almost desperate attempt to seize or acquire. | 例句：He grasped at the opportunity, eagerly seeking to advance his career. 他抓住这个机会，渴望提升自己的事业。

He looked graspingly at the pile of gold coins. 他贪婪地盯着那一堆金币。


近义词：avidly, greedily, covetously, eagerly, ravenously

反义词：generously, selflessly, reluctantly, indifferently


词性：副词
