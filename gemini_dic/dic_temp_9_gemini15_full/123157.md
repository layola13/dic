monkeying:/ˈmʌŋkiŋ/| v.|胡闹，瞎弄，摆弄|To playfully or idly handle or manipulate something; to act in a mischievous or silly way.|例句：The children were monkeying around in the playground.孩子们在操场上胡闹。|例句：He spent the afternoon monkeying with the old radio. 他下午一直在摆弄那台旧收音机。|近义词：fooling around, messing around, playing around|反义词：being serious, working diligently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is monkeying around with the computer. = 他正在摆弄电脑。
They are constantly monkeying around in class. = 他们在课堂上总是胡闹。


一般过去时:
She was monkeying around with the camera. = 她当时正在摆弄相机。
The boys were monkeying around near the river. = 男孩子们在河边胡闹。


现在完成时:
They have been monkeying around all afternoon. = 他们整个下午都在胡闹。
I have been monkeying with this engine for hours. = 我已经摆弄这个引擎好几个小时了。


过去完成时:
He had been monkeying around with the wires before the fire started. = 起火之前他一直在摆弄电线。
They had been monkeying around all day, so they were very tired. = 他们玩了一整天，所以非常疲倦。


将来时:
They will be monkeying around at the party. = 他们将在派对上胡闹。
He will be monkeying with the car engine tomorrow. = 他明天将要摆弄汽车引擎。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't been monkeying around, he wouldn't have broken the vase. = 如果他没有胡闹，他就不会打破花瓶了。
I wish they wouldn't monkey around so much. = 我希望他们不要总是胡闹。


被动语态:
The equipment was monkeyed around with, rendering it useless. = 设备被人摆弄过，变得无法使用了。(很少用被动语态)
The situation was monkeyed up by his careless actions. = 他的粗心行为把情况搞糟了。(很少用被动语态，通常用主动语态)


疑问句:
Were they monkeying around when the accident happened? = 事故发生时他们在胡闹吗？
Is he monkeying around again? = 他又在胡闹了吗？


否定句:
He wasn't monkeying around; he was genuinely trying to fix it. = 他不是在胡闹；他是在认真地尝试修理它。
They didn't monkey around with the evidence. = 他们没有胡乱摆弄证据。



PHRASE->
monkey around with: 胡乱摆弄

make a monkey out of someone: 愚弄某人
monkey see, monkey do: 有样学样
go ape: 变得疯狂或激动
