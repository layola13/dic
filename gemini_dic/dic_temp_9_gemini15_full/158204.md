platterful:/ˈplætərˌful/| n.|满满一盘，一大盘|A platterful is a quantity that fills a platter.  It implies a large amount of something, typically food.|例句：She brought out a platterful of delicious cookies. 她端出一大盘美味的饼干。  There was a platterful of roast chicken on the table. 桌子上有一大盘烤鸡。|近义词：plateful, heaping helping, large quantity|反义词：少量，少许
