expander:/ɪkˈspændər/ | n. |膨胀器，扩张器 |A device or substance that causes something to expand or enlarge.  It can refer to a physical object used to increase the size or volume of something, or a metaphorical process of expansion or growth. |例句：The metal expander is used to widen the bridge's support beams. 金属膨胀器用于加宽桥梁的支撑梁。


The company's new marketing campaign acted as an expander, increasing brand awareness. 公司的新营销活动起到了扩张作用，提高了品牌知名度。|近义词：dilator, stretcher, enlarger |反义词：compressor, constrictor
