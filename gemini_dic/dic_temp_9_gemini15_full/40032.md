consuming: /kənˈsuːmɪŋ/ | adj. v. | 消耗的；费时的；吞噬的 |  requiring or using a lot of time, energy, or resources;  to eat or drink something. | 近义词：exhausting, demanding, devouring,  | 反义词：producing, generating, sparing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This task is consuming a lot of my time. = 这项任务占据了我很多时间。
The fire is consuming the forest rapidly. = 大火正在迅速吞噬森林。


一般过去时:
The project consumed all of our resources. = 这个项目耗尽了我们所有的资源。
She consumed a whole pizza in one sitting. = 她一口气吃掉了一个披萨。


现在完成时:
The journey has consumed much of our energy. = 这段旅程已经消耗了我们大量的精力。
He has consumed three cups of coffee this morning. = 他今天早上已经喝了三杯咖啡。


过去完成时:
They had consumed all the food before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经吃光了所有的食物。
The fire had consumed most of the building by the time firefighters arrived. = 当消防员到达时，大火已经吞噬了大部分建筑物。


将来时:
This work will consume all my free time. = 这项工作将会占用我所有空闲时间。
I predict this new game will consume many hours of my life. = 我预测这款新游戏将会吞噬我很多时间。


虚拟语气:
If the fire continued burning, it would consume the whole city. = 如果大火继续燃烧，它将会吞噬整个城市。
I wish I hadn't consumed so much cake; I feel sick now. = 我真希望我没有吃那么多蛋糕，我现在感觉不舒服。


被动语态:
Most of the forest was consumed by the fire. = 大部分森林被大火吞噬了。
A large amount of energy is consumed by the factory each day. = 工厂每天消耗大量的能量。


疑问句:
How much time is consumed by this process? = 这个过程消耗了多少时间？
What resources are being consumed by the new project? = 新项目正在消耗哪些资源？


否定句:
The machine doesn't consume much energy. = 这台机器不怎么费电。
He didn't consume any alcohol at the party. = 他在聚会上没有饮酒。


PHRASE->
time-consuming = 费时的
resource-consuming = 资源消耗大的
energy-consuming = 能耗高的
fuel-consuming = 耗油的
emotionally consuming = 情绪消耗大的
