corrosivenesses:/kəˈroʊsɪvnəsɪz/| n.|腐蚀性(复数)|The plural form of corrosiveness, referring to multiple instances or types of corrosive properties.  It describes the degree or instances of the ability of a substance to damage or destroy other materials by chemical action.|例句：The different corrosivenesses of the various acids were tested.|近义词：erosivenesses, destructivenesses|反义词：protectivenesses, preservativenesses
