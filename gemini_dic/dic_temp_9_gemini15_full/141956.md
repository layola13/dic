oregano:/ɔˈrɛɡənəʊ/| n. |牛至|A fragrant herb of the mint family, used in cooking, especially in Italian and Mediterranean cuisine.  It has small, oval leaves and produces small purple or white flowers.|例句：I added some oregano to the tomato sauce. 我在番茄酱里加了一些牛至。|例句：The pizza was sprinkled with oregano and Parmesan cheese. 比萨撒上了牛至和帕尔玛干酪。|近义词：marjoram, thyme|反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑与缺乏香气的香料作对比)|名词
