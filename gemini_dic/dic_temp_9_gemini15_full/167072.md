presupervises:/ˌpriːˈsuːpərvaɪzɪz/ | v. | 预先监督；提前监督 | To supervise or oversee something before it officially begins or is implemented.  This word is a relatively uncommon neologism, formed by combining "pre-" (before) and "supervises". Therefore, a direct translation might not perfectly capture the nuance. | 例句：The project manager presupervises the initial design phase to ensure alignment with overall goals. = 项目经理预先监督初始设计阶段，以确保与总体目标一致。

The committee presupervises the selection process to avoid any bias. = 委员会预先监督甄选过程，以避免任何偏见。


近义词：pre-inspect, pre-check, review beforehand, oversee in advance


反义词：post-supervise, monitor subsequently
