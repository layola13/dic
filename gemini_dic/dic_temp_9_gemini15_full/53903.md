discounted:/ˈdɪskaʊntɪd/| adj.| 打折的；不予考虑的 |Reduced in price.  Also,  dismissed as unimportant or untrue. |例句：The store is having a sale with many discounted items.  这家商店正在打折销售许多商品。

The proposal was discounted as unrealistic.  这个提议被认为不切实际而被否决了。|近义词：reduced, marked down,  rejected, dismissed |反义词：full-priced,  unreduced,  considered, accepted |词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store discounts many items during the holiday season. = 节日期间，这家商店许多商品都打折。
These arguments are discounted by experts. = 这些论点被专家们否定了。


一般过去时:
They discounted the price by 20%. = 他们把价格降低了20%。
He discounted her warnings as mere exaggeration. = 他认为她的警告只是夸大其词而轻视了。


现在完成时:
The company has discounted its products to boost sales. = 公司已对其产品进行打折以促进销售。
The evidence has been discounted as unreliable.  证据因不可靠而被驳回。


过去完成时:
They had discounted the possibility of rain before the picnic. = 在野餐之前，他们已经排除了下雨的可能性。
The judge had discounted the witness's testimony due to inconsistencies. = 由于证词前后矛盾，法官驳回了证人的证词。


将来时:
They will discount the tickets for students. = 他们将为学生打折。
The jury will likely discount the defendant's alibi.  陪审团很可能会驳回被告的不在场证明。


虚拟语气:
If they discounted the price, I would buy it. = 如果他们降价，我就买。
If the court discounted the evidence, the case would be lost. = 如果法院驳回证据，案件就会败诉。


被动语态:
The prices were discounted significantly. = 价格被大幅度降低了。
His claims were discounted by the investigation. = 他的说法被调查驳回了。


疑问句:
Are these items discounted? = 这些商品打折了吗？
Were her concerns discounted by the management? = 管理层是否忽视了她的担忧？


否定句:
The prices are not discounted. = 价格没有打折。
Her explanation wasn't discounted immediately. = 她的解释并没有立即被否定。



PHRASE->
heavily discounted: 大幅折扣
discounted price: 折扣价
deeply discounted: 深度折扣
discounted cash flow (DCF): 现金流量折现
discounted rate: 折现率
