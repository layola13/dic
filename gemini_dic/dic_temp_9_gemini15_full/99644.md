interlayered: /ˌɪntərˈleɪərd/ | adj. | 交错的；层叠的；夹层的 |  Placed or occurring between layers of something else.  The word describes a structure where different materials or elements are arranged in alternating layers.  | 例句：The cake had layers of sponge and jam interlayered with cream.  这个蛋糕有海绵蛋糕和果酱层，夹层是奶油。

The geological formations were interlayered with sandstone and shale.  这些地质构造是由砂岩和页岩互相层叠而成的。


近义词：stratified, layered, interspersed, interwoven | 反义词：unlayered, homogeneous, uniform | 词性：形容词
