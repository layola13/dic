canoeists:/kəˈnuːɪsts/| n. |独木舟运动员，皮划艇运动员 |People who use canoes or kayaks for recreation or sport.  They often participate in races or expeditions.|例句：The canoeists paddled swiftly across the lake. 独木舟运动员快速划过湖面。

例句：Experienced canoeists know how to handle strong currents.  经验丰富的独木舟运动员知道如何应对强流。


近义词：kayakers, paddlers
反义词：无
