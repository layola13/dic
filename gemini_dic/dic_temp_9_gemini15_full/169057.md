programma:/prəˈɡræmə/| n. |程序，规划，节目 | A set of instructions or rules that a computer follows to perform a specific task; a plan of action; a broadcast show. | Example: The computer program crashed. (电脑程序崩溃了。)  The political party released its election program. (政党发布了竞选纲领。) |近义词：program, schedule, plan, scheme |反义词：chaos, disorder
