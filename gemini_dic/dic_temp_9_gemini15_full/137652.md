nonunderstandingly: /ˌnɒnˌʌndəˈstændɪŋli/ | adv. | 不解地；茫然地 |  In a way that shows a lack of understanding.  It describes an action or state performed without comprehension. | 例句：He stared nonunderstandingly at the complex equation. 他茫然地盯着复杂的方程式。

She nodded nonunderstandingly, clearly not grasping the explanation. 她不解地点了点头，显然没有理解解释。| 近义词：uncomprehendingly, blankly, cluelessly | 反义词：understandingly, knowingly, intelligently | 词性：副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  It describes how an action is performed.  Therefore, tense, voice, mood, etc. are irrelevant to the word itself.  The tense, voice, etc. will be determined by the verb it modifies.


例句1：He looked nonunderstandingly at the instructions. 他不解地看着说明书。
例句2：She responded nonunderstandingly to his question. 她对他的问题茫然地回答。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "nonunderstandingly".  Its usage is generally standalone to modify a verb.
