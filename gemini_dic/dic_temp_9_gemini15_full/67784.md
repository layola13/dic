eyeline:/ˈaɪlaɪn/| n.|视线，眼线|The line of sight; the direction in which one is looking.  Also, a line drawn along the eyelid, typically with makeup.|例句：The eyeline of the painting is directed towards the central figure. 这幅画的视线指向中央的人物。

例句：She applied a bold eyeline to enhance her eyes. 她画了一条粗粗的眼线来突出她的眼睛。
