quasi-commonly:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈkɑːmənli/ | adv. | 准普遍地，近乎普遍地 |  Describes something that is almost but not quite common; frequently occurring but not to the extent of being truly common. It suggests a level of prevalence that is significant but not universal. | The study showed that this particular gene mutation is quasi-commonly found in the population.  这项研究表明这种特殊的基因突变在人群中几乎普遍存在。| commonly, frequently, often | rarely, exceptionally, unusually | 副词
