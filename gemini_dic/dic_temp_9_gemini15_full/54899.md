disporting:/dɪˈspɔːrtɪŋ/ | v. |嬉戏；玩耍；消遣 |  To engage in playful activity; to amuse oneself; to frolic.  The word often implies a carefree and lively enjoyment. | 例句：The children were disporting themselves in the park. 孩子们在公园里嬉戏。

The lambs were disporting in the green meadow.  小羊们在绿色的草地上嬉戏。


近义词：playing, frolicking, sporting, amusing oneself, recreating | 反义词：toiling, working, laboring


词源:  From Middle English *disporten, from Old French desporter "to carry away, divert," from des- "away" + porter "to carry." The modern sense of playful activity developed later.
