finials:/ˈfɪniəlz/| n.|尖顶装饰; 顶饰 |Ornamental structures at the top of a building, furniture, or other object.  They often serve a decorative purpose but can also have a structural role.|The church spire was crowned with elaborate finials. 这座教堂的尖顶上装饰着精美的尖顶装饰。  The bedposts were topped with elegant finials. 床柱顶部装饰着优雅的顶饰。|近义词：crowns, crests, ornaments|反义词：bases, foundations|名词
