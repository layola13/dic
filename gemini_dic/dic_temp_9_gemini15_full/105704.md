kinky:/ˈkɪŋki/| adj.|奇特的，古怪的；性行为反常的|  Describes something unusual, strange, or unconventional, often in a playfully exciting way.  It can also refer to unusual or unconventional sexual practices. |例句：He has a kinky sense of humor. 他有一种古怪的幽默感。

例句：Their sexual practices are considered kinky by many. 他们的性行为被许多人认为是反常的。


近义词：quirky, eccentric, unconventional, bizarre (for the unusual sense);  perverse, deviant (for the sexual sense)

反义词：conventional, normal, orthodox, straight (for the sexual sense)
