monophonically:/ˌmɒnəˈfɒnɪkli/| adj. | 单音的，单音地 |  Producing or relating to sound with only one pitch or tone; lacking variation in pitch.  It describes a sound or musical piece that uses only one note or tone without any melodic variation. | Example: The drone of the airplane engine sounded monophonically.  飞机引擎的轰鸣声听起来单调乏味。| 近义词：monotonously, unvaried | 反义词：polyphonically, harmoniously
