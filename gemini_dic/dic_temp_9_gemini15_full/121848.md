misreporters:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːrtərz/ | n. |错误报道者，歪曲事实者 |Those who inaccurately or deceptively report information, often with malicious intent. They may intentionally distort facts, omit crucial details, or present biased narratives. |例句：The misreporters twisted the politician's words to create a negative image.  错误报道者歪曲了这位政治家的言辞，以制造负面形象。
