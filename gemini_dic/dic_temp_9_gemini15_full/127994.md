nephelite:/ˈnefəlaɪt/| n. |霞石|A rock-forming mineral consisting of a hydrated aluminosilicate of sodium and potassium. It is a feldspathoid mineral and is often found in igneous rocks that are poor in silica. Nepheline is used in the production of ceramics and glass.|例句：Nepheline syenite is a common rock type containing nephelite. 霞石正长岩是一种常见的含有霞石的岩石类型。|近义词：feldspathoid|反义词：N/A
