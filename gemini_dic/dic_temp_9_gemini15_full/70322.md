field-test:/ˈfiːldˌtɛst/ | v. |现场测试，实地测试 | To test something (a product, a theory, etc.) in real-world conditions, rather than in a laboratory or controlled environment.  | Example: The new software was field-tested in several different offices before its official release.  新的软件在正式发布之前在几个不同的办公室进行了现场测试。 |近义词：trial, test, experiment | 反义词：simulation, laboratory test


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We field-test the new car every day. = 我们每天都对新车进行实地测试。
They field-test the new software in various settings. = 他们在各种环境中测试新软件。

一般过去时:
The engineers field-tested the prototype last week. = 工程师们上周对原型进行了现场测试。
We field-tested the new product in three different cities. = 我们在三个不同的城市对新产品进行了现场测试。

现在完成时:
They have field-tested the device in extreme conditions. = 他们已经在极端条件下对该设备进行了现场测试。
We have field-tested the software across multiple platforms. = 我们已经在多个平台上测试了该软件。

过去完成时:
They had field-tested the equipment before the expedition. = 他们在探险之前已经对设备进行了现场测试。
The company had field-tested the product for several months. = 公司已经对该产品进行了几个月的现场测试。

将来时:
We will field-test the new model next month. = 我们下个月将对新车型进行现场测试。
They will field-test the application in real-world scenarios. = 他们将在真实场景中测试该应用程序。

虚拟语气:
If we field-tested it properly, we wouldn't have these problems. = 如果我们进行了适当的现场测试，就不会出现这些问题。
It would be better if we field-tested the software before launch. = 如果我们在发布之前对软件进行现场测试会更好。

被动语态:
The product was field-tested by a team of experts. = 该产品由专家团队进行了现场测试。
The new system is being field-tested at several locations. = 新系统正在多个地点进行现场测试。

疑问句:
Have they field-tested the new design yet? = 他们已经对新设计进行现场测试了吗？
Will the software be field-tested before release? = 该软件会在发布前进行现场测试吗？

否定句:
They did not field-test the product adequately. = 他们没有充分地对产品进行现场测试。
We haven't field-tested the new features yet. = 我们还没有测试新的功能。
