sequester:/sɪˈkwɛstər/| v. n.|隔离；扣押；没收；使隔离；隐居|To isolate or hide away; to confiscate or seize;  to set apart; to remove or withdraw (oneself) from society.|例句：The jury was sequestered during the trial. 陪审团在审判期间被隔离。  The government sequestered the assets of the suspected terrorist. 政府没收了嫌疑恐怖分子的资产。|近义词：isolate, confine, confiscate, impound|反义词：release, free, integrate
