semihostilely:/ˌsɛmiˈhɑːstaɪlli/ | adv. | 半敌对地，略带敌意地 | In a manner that is partly hostile or unfriendly; showing some hostility but not openly aggressive. | 例句：He regarded the new proposal semihostilely. 他对新的提议持半敌对态度。

例句：She looked at him semihostilely, her eyes narrowed. 她眯起眼睛，半敌对地望着他。


近义词：unfriendly, antagonistically, resentfully, suspiciously


反义词：amicably, cordially, friendly, warmly


词性：副词
