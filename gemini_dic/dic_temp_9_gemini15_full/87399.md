herringbone:/ˈhɛrɪŋboʊn/| adj. n.|人字形；鱼骨形|A herringbone pattern is one in which parallel diagonal lines intersect each other to create a V-shaped pattern resembling the bones of a herring.  It is used to describe a variety of things, including brickwork, fabrics, and floor patterns.|The road had a herringbone pattern.|近义词：chevron, zigzag|反义词：straight, parallel|形容词，名词
