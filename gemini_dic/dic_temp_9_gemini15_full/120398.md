ministerialist: /ˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəlɪst/ | adj. n. | 部长的；大臣的；政府官员的；(政治)内阁主义者 |

英文解析:
Relating to or characteristic of a minister or ministers of a government.  As a noun, it refers to a supporter of ministerial government or the policy of a minister.  The term often carries connotations of supporting the government's policies, sometimes unquestioningly.

例句:
The ministerialist approach to policy-making prioritized efficiency over public consultation.  部长主义的政策制定方法优先考虑效率而不是公众咨询。

The prime minister faced strong opposition from non-ministerialist members of parliament. 首相面临来自议会非部长派成员的强烈反对。


近义词: governmental, pro-government, pro-establishment

反义词: oppositional, rebellious, anti-establishment


词性: adjective, noun
