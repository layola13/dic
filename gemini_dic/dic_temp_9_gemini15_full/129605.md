non-concur:/ˌnɒn kənˈkɜːr/|v.|不同意，不一致|To disagree; to not concur.|The board members non-concurred on the proposed budget. 董事会成员不同意拟议的预算。|concur, agree|agree, concur|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They non-concur on the best course of action. = 他们对最佳行动方案意见不一。
We non-concur with your assessment of the situation. = 我们不同意你对局势的评估。

一般过去时:
The committee non-concurred with the CEO's recommendation. = 委员会不同意首席执行官的建议。
He non-concurred with the findings of the report. = 他不同意报告的调查结果。

现在完成时:
They have non-concurred on several key issues. = 他们在几个关键问题上意见不一。
We have non-concurred with their decision to postpone the project. = 我们不同意他们推迟项目的决定。

过去完成时:
The shareholders had non-concurred on the merger proposal before the vote. = 在投票之前，股东们对合并提案意见不一。
She had non-concurred with his analysis of the data. = 她不同意他对数据的分析。

将来时:
They will non-concur on the final decision, I suspect. = 我怀疑他们最终的决定会意见不一。
We will non-concur with any attempt to censor the report. = 我们将不同意任何试图审查该报告的企图。

虚拟语气:
If they non-concurred, we would have to find a compromise. = 如果他们不同意，我们就必须找到一个妥协方案。
I wish the committee had non-concurred sooner; it would have saved time. = 我希望委员会早点表示不同意；这将节省时间。

被动语态:
The proposal was non-concurred with by the majority of the board. = 该提案未获得董事会大多数成员的同意。
His opinion was non-concurred with by his colleagues. = 他的意见未得到同事们的认同。

疑问句:
Do you non-concur with the proposed solution? = 你不同意拟议的解决方案吗？
Would they non-concur if we presented a different proposal? = 如果我们提出不同的方案，他们会不同意吗？

否定句:
They did not non-concur with the plan; they actively supported it. = 他们并没有不同意这个计划；他们积极支持它。
We do not non-concur with every aspect of the report, but we support its overall conclusions. = 我们并非不同意报告的每个方面，但我们支持其总体结论。
