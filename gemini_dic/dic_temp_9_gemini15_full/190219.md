sapindaceous:/ˌsæpɪnˈdeɪʃəs/| adj.| 远志科的；无患子科的 |Belonging to or characteristic of the Sapindaceae, a family of mostly tropical trees and shrubs that includes maples, horse chestnuts, and lychees.|例句：Many sapindaceous plants produce edible fruits. 许多远志科植物会产生可食用的果实。|近义词：maple-like (if referring specifically to maple characteristics)|反义词：无|词性：形容词
