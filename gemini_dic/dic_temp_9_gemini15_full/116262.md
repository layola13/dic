massacring:/ˈmæsəkərɪŋ/| v.| 大屠杀；残杀 |The act of killing a large number of people indiscriminately and violently.  It often implies a brutal and merciless slaughter.| 例句：The soldiers were accused of massacring civilians.  士兵被指控屠杀平民。 | 例句：The historical record details the horrifying massacre of the indigenous population.  历史记录详细描述了对原住民的可怕大屠杀。| 近义词：slaughtering, butchering, slaughtering, exterminating | 反义词：saving, rescuing, protecting
