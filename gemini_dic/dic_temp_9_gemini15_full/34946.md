clocked:/klɒkt/| /klɑːkt/ | vt. |记录时间；计时；达到（速度）；发现；突然意识到|To record the time of something; to measure the speed of something; to achieve a particular speed; to discover or notice something unexpectedly; to suddenly realize something.|例句：The police clocked the car speeding at 100 mph.  警察测得那辆车超速行驶，时速达100英里。

The runner clocked a new personal best.  这位跑步者创造了个人最好成绩。

近义词：timed, registered, recorded, spotted, noticed| 反义词：missed, overlooked|  动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police clock the speed of cars. = 警察测量汽车的速度。
He clocks in at 8 am every day. = 他每天早上8点打卡上班。


一般过去时:
They clocked the thief leaving the scene. = 他们发现窃贼离开了现场。
The athlete clocked a time of under 10 seconds. = 运动员的成绩低于10秒。


现在完成时:
I have clocked over 10,000 miles this year. = 今年我已经行驶了一万多英里。
She has clocked many hours of practice. = 她已经练习了很多个小时。


过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, they had clocked the suspect. = 当我们到达时，他们已经发现了嫌疑人。
He had clocked his fastest time ever in the race. = 他在比赛中跑出了有史以来最快的速度。


将来时:
We will clock the progress of the project regularly. = 我们将定期记录项目的进度。
I will clock my running time using a fitness app. = 我将使用健身应用程序记录我的跑步时间。


虚拟语气:
If I had clocked his speed earlier, I could have stopped him. = 如果我早点发现他的速度，我就能阻止他了。
It would be helpful if we clocked the time spent on each task. = 如果我们能记录下完成每个任务所花费的时间，那就太好了。


被动语态:
The time was clocked accurately. = 时间被精确地记录下来了。
His speed was clocked at 90 mph. = 他的速度被测定为每小时90英里。


疑问句:
Did the referee clock the time correctly? = 裁判记录的时间准确吗？
How long have you clocked in for today? = 你今天打卡上班多久了？


否定句:
The system didn't clock his attendance. = 系统没有记录他的考勤。
The police didn't clock the driver speeding. = 警察没有发现司机超速。


PHRASE->

clock speed = 时钟速度
