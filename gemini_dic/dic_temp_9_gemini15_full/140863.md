on-the-march:/ˌɒn ðə mɑːtʃ/| adj. |正在行进中；正在进展中|正在行进的；正在进行的；发展中的|The army is on the march.  军队正在行进。|The project is on the march.  项目正在进展中。|advancing, progressing, developing|stationary, at a standstill, inactive|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The soldiers are on the march. = 士兵们正在行军。
The reforms are on the march. = 改革正在进行中。

一般过去时:
The troops were on the march all day. = 部队一整天都在行军。
The negotiations were on the march until a breakthrough was reached. = 直到取得突破，谈判才有所进展。

现在完成时:
The movement has been on the march for decades. =  这场运动已经进行了几十年。
The technology has been on the march since the invention of the internet. = 自从互联网发明以来，这项技术一直在发展。

过去完成时:
The army had been on the march for three days before they reached the city. = 部队行军三天后才到达城市。
The project had been on the march for months before it was suddenly cancelled. = 项目进展了几个月后突然被取消了。

将来时:
The protesters will be on the march next week. = 抗议者下周将举行游行。
The company's new product will be on the march soon. = 公司的新产品很快就会上市。

虚拟语气:
If the army were on the march, we would have to evacuate. = 如果军队正在行军，我们就必须撤离。
If the plan were on the march, we would have seen some results by now. = 如果计划正在进行，我们现在应该看到一些结果了。

被动语态:
It was announced that the reforms were on the march. = 据宣布，改革正在进行中。
The progress of the project was said to be on the march. = 项目的进展据说正在顺利进行。

疑问句:
Is the construction project on the march? = 建设项目进展顺利吗？
Are the negotiations on the march? = 谈判进展顺利吗？

否定句:
The peace talks were not on the march. = 和谈没有取得进展。
The economy was not on the march during that period. = 那段时间经济没有发展。
