occupy:/ˈɑːkjupaɪ/| vt.|占据，占用，使忙碌|To take control of a place, typically with force; to fill or use up (time or space).|例句：The army occupied the city.  军队占领了这座城市。|例句：The meeting occupied the entire afternoon.  会议占用整个下午。|近义词：seize,  hold,  engage,  absorb|反义词：vacate, abandon, leave


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The soldiers occupy the building. = 士兵们占据了那座建筑物。
This work occupies most of my time. = 这项工作占据了我大部分时间。

一般过去时:
The rebels occupied the capital. = 反叛者占领了首都。
The task occupied him for hours. = 这项任务让他忙了好几个小时。


现在完成时:
The army has occupied the territory for years. = 军队已经占领了这块领土多年了。
She has occupied herself with painting lately. = 她最近一直忙于绘画。


过去完成时:
They had occupied the house before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前他们已经占据了那所房子。
He had occupied himself with reading all morning. = 他整个上午都在读书。


将来时:
The enemy will occupy the town. = 敌人将会占领小镇。
I will occupy myself with gardening this weekend. = 这个周末我将忙于园艺。


虚拟语气:
If the enemy occupied the city, we would have to retreat. = 如果敌人占领了城市，我们就必须撤退。
I wish I could occupy my time more productively. = 我希望我能更有效地利用我的时间。


被动语态:
The city was occupied by the enemy. = 城市被敌人占领了。
His time was occupied by endless meetings. = 他时间被没完没了的会议占据了。


疑问句:
Does this project occupy much of your time? = 这个项目占据你很多时间吗？
What occupies your thoughts these days? = 这些天你在想什么？


否定句:
The rebels did not occupy the city. = 反叛者没有占领这座城市。
This game doesn't occupy much space on my phone. = 这个游戏在我的手机上不占用太多空间。


PHRASE->
occupy oneself with = 忙于...
occupy a position = 担任职位
occupy a space = 占据空间
occupy a seat = 占据一个座位
occupy the land = 占据土地
