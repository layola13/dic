lorikeet:/ˈlɔːrɪkiːt/| n. |吸蜜鹦鹉|A small, colorful parrot from Australia, New Guinea, and surrounding islands, known for its brush-tipped tongue used to feed on nectar and pollen.| 例句：The lorikeet perched on the flowering eucalyptus tree, its vibrant plumage a splash of color. 这只吸蜜鹦鹉栖息在开花的桉树上，它鲜艳的羽毛是一抹亮色。

The lorikeet's diet consists mainly of nectar and pollen. 吸蜜鹦鹉的食物主要由花蜜和花粉组成。|近义词：parakeet, honeyeater |反义词：None
