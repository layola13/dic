reflexologies:/ˌriːflekˈsɒlədʒiz/| n. |反射疗法；反射学|The study and practice of reflexology, a complementary therapy that involves applying pressure to specific points on the feet, hands, or ears to stimulate corresponding areas of the body.  Reflexology is based on the principle that these points are connected to different organs and systems in the body.  |例句：She believes in the benefits of reflexologies to relieve stress. 她相信反射疗法可以缓解压力。

例句： Many people find reflexologies helpful for managing pain and improving overall well-being.许多人发现反射疗法有助于缓解疼痛和改善整体健康状况。


近义词：massage therapy, acupressure |反义词：无
