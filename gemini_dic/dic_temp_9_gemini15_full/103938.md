judgy:/ˈdʒʌdʒi/| adj.|爱评头论足的；爱批判的；爱指手画脚的 |Someone who is judgy is quick to criticize or judge others, often in a negative or unfair way. They tend to make assumptions and pass judgment without sufficient information.  |He's so judgy; he always has something negative to say about everyone. 他太爱评头论足了；他对每个人总是有负面评价。|critical, judgmental, censorious |accepting, tolerant, understanding |形容词
