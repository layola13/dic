selves:/sɛlvz/| pron.|自身，自己们|The reflexive pronoun "selves" is used as the object of a verb or preposition when the subject and object are the same.  It is always used with a plural personal pronoun (ourselves, yourselves, themselves).|They hurt themselves playing football.  他们踢足球时弄伤了自己。|ourselves, yourselves, themselves|other, others


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We praise ourselves for our achievements. = 我们为自己的成就感到自豪。
They often blame themselves for their mistakes. = 他们经常责备自己犯的错误。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If they were to blame themselves, it would not help. = 如果他们责备自己，也不会有什么帮助。
I wish we had better prepared ourselves for the exam. = 我希望我们为考试做了更好的准备。


被动语态:
The team was praised for having excelled themselves. = 该团队因表现出色而受到赞扬。(注意这里self不是被动语态的主语)
The children were left to amuse themselves. = 孩子们被留下来自己玩耍。(注意这里self不是被动语态的主语)


疑问句:
Did they hurt themselves? = 他们伤到自己了吗？
Have you prepared yourselves for the presentation? = 你们为演示做好了准备吗？


否定句:

We didn't blame ourselves for the failure. = 我们没有为失败责备自己。
