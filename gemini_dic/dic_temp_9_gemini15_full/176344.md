re-examine:/ˌriːɪɡˈzæmɪn/| vt.|重新检查；再审查|To examine again or more carefully.  It implies a more thorough or critical examination than the initial one.  |例句：The judge decided to re-examine the evidence.  法官决定重新审查证据。
The committee will re-examine the proposal next week. 委员会下周将重新审查该提案。|近义词：review, reconsider, reassess |反义词：overlook, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-examine the data regularly. = 他们定期重新检查数据。
We re-examine our strategies annually. = 我们每年重新审查我们的战略。

一般过去时:
The doctor re-examined the patient's X-rays. = 医生重新检查了病人的X光片。
The police re-examined the crime scene. = 警方重新检查了犯罪现场。

现在完成时:
She has re-examined her life choices. = 她已经重新审视了她的人生选择。
The scientists have re-examined the results of the experiment. = 科学家们已经重新检查了实验结果。

过去完成时:
He had re-examined the contract before signing it. = 他在签署合同之前已经重新审查了它。
They had re-examined the evidence several times. = 他们已经多次重新审查了证据。

将来时:
We will re-examine the budget next month. = 我们下个月将重新审查预算。
The court will re-examine the witness's testimony. = 法院将重新审查证人的证词。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would re-examine my options. = 如果我是你，我会重新考虑我的选择。
It's essential that we re-examine our assumptions. = 我们必须重新审查我们的假设，这是至关重要的。

被动语态:
The case will be re-examined by the higher court. = 此案将由高级法院重新审查。
The evidence was re-examined by the experts. = 证据由专家重新审查。

疑问句:
Will the committee re-examine the proposal? = 委员会会重新审查该提案吗？
Should we re-examine our approach to the problem? = 我们应该重新审视我们解决这个问题的方法吗？

否定句:
They did not re-examine the evidence. = 他们没有重新审查证据。
The judge refused to re-examine the witness. = 法官拒绝重新审查证人。
