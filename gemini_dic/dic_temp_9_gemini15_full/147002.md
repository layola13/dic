overlabors: /ˌoʊvərˈleɪbər/ | vt. | 过度劳累；过于详细地阐述 | To work too hard; to expend excessive effort on something; to elaborate excessively.  |例句：He overlabors every detail, making the project take far longer than necessary. 他对每一个细节都过于仔细，导致项目花费的时间比必要的长得多。

例句：She overlabored her explanation, confusing rather than clarifying the issue. 她对她的解释过于冗长，反而使问题更加混乱。


近义词：overwork, overburden, overexert,  overdo


反义词：underwork, neglect


词性：动词
