macrobiotic:/ˌmækroʊbaɪˈɒtɪk/ | adj. | 宏观生物学的；宏观的；长寿的；(饮食) 宏量营养素的 | Relating to or denoting a diet consisting mainly of unprocessed whole grains, vegetables, and legumes, and avoiding meat, dairy products, and refined foods, believed to promote health and longevity.  | Example: She follows a macrobiotic diet to improve her health. 她遵循宏量营养素饮食来改善健康。 | 近义词：holistic, health-promoting | 反义词：unhealthy, processed-food-based
