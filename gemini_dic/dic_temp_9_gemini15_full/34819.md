clinches:/klɪntʃɪz/| vt. n.| 确定；解决；抓住；使牢固；咬紧；(拳击)击倒 | To firmly grasp or hold something.  To settle or conclude something decisively. |例句：He clinched the deal with a firm handshake. 他通过一次坚定的握手确定了这笔交易。

例句：The boxer clinched his victory with a knockout punch. 拳击手以一记击倒拳确定了他的胜利。


近义词：secure, fasten, settle, conclude, grasp, grip

反义词：loosen, release, unfasten, leave unsettled


词性:动词, 名词
