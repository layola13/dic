primed:/praɪmd/ | adj. |准备好；预先准备好的；启动的；预先刺激的 |  Prepared or ready for action or use;  stimulated beforehand.  |例句：The athletes were primed and ready for the race. 运动员们已准备好参加比赛。The pump is primed and ready to operate. 水泵已启动并准备运行。|近义词：ready, prepared, set, activated |反义词：unprepared, unready, inactive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine is primed and ready to start. = 引擎已启动并准备启动。
The team is primed for success. = 团队已准备好迎接成功。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
