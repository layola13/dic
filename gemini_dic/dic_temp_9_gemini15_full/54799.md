dispenser:/dɪˈspɛnsər/ | n. |分配器；自动售货机 |A dispenser is a device or container that releases a substance, such as liquid, powder, or tickets, in a controlled manner.  |例句：The soap dispenser in the bathroom is empty.  浴室里的肥皂分配器空了。|例句：The vending machine is a type of dispenser that sells snacks and drinks. 自动售货机是一种出售零食和饮料的分配器。|近义词：distributor, vendor, apparatus |反义词：receiver, collector
