flatbeds: /ˈflætˌbɛdz/ | n. |平板车，平板货车 | A flatbed is a vehicle with a flat, open platform used for carrying goods.  It lacks sides or a roof. | Example: The construction company used a flatbed to transport the large beams to the building site.  这家建筑公司用平板车将大型梁运送到建筑工地。  The farmer used a flatbed to haul his harvest. 农民用平板车运送他的收成。|近义词：truck (with a flatbed), lorry (with a flatbed), trailer |反义词： enclosed truck, van
