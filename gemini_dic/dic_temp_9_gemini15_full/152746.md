peaty:/ˈpiːti/| adj.|泥炭的；泥煤似的；|Containing or resembling peat.  The adjective describes something that has the characteristics of peat, a dark brown soil-like material consisting of partially decayed vegetable matter.  It often implies a dark, damp, and somewhat spongy quality.|例句：The soil was peaty and rich in organic matter. 这片土壤是泥炭土，富含有机质。The peaty smell hung heavy in the air. 泥炭味弥漫在空气中。|近义词：boggy, marshy, mucky|反义词：dry, sandy
