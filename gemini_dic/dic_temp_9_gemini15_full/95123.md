inapathy:/ɪˈnæpəθi/| n. |冷漠，无情，缺乏同情心 |Apathy is a lack of feeling, emotion, or interest. It's a state of indifference or emotional numbness.  People experiencing apathy may show little or no reaction to events that would normally evoke strong feelings. |例句：His apathy towards his studies was concerning. 他对学习的冷漠令人担忧。

例句：The politician's speech was met with apathy from the crowd. 政治家的演讲受到了人群的冷漠回应。


近义词：indifference, unconcern, insensitivity, detachment |反义词：empathy, compassion, passion, enthusiasm |词性: 名词
