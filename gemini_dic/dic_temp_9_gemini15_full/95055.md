inadequacies:/ˌɪnæˈdɛkwəsiːz/| n.|不足之处，缺陷| shortcomings, deficiencies, defects; the state or quality of being inadequate.  The project failed due to numerous inadequacies in planning and execution.  该项目因规划和执行中的诸多不足而失败。|近义词：shortcomings, deficiencies, flaws|反义词：sufficiency, adequacy
