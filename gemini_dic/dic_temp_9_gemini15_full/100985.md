invalidates:/ɪnˈvælɪdeɪts/ | vt. | 使无效；使作废；驳倒 | To make something invalid or ineffective; to prove something wrong or false. |例句：The new evidence invalidates his previous claim. 新证据使他之前的声明无效。|例句：The judge invalidated the contract because of a technicality. 由于一个技术细节，法官宣布合同无效。|近义词：nullifies, cancels, annuls, refutes |反义词：validates, confirms, supports


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new law invalidates previous regulations. = 新法律使之前的规章无效。
This evidence invalidates his alibi. = 这证据推翻了他的不在场证明。


一般过去时:
The court invalidated the contract. = 法院宣布合同无效。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
