lamps:/læmpz/| n.|灯，灯具|A lamp is a device that produces light, typically using electricity.|例句：The lamps in the living room are quite dim. 客厅的灯很暗。|例句：He switched on the lamps before entering the room. 他进房间前打开了灯。|近义词：light, luminaire|反义词：darkness
