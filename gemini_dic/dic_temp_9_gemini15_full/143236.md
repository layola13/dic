outdazzle:/ˌaʊtˈdæzəl/| vt.|胜过…的光彩；使…黯然失色|To surpass in brilliance or splendor; to outshine.|例句：Her beauty outdazzled everyone in the room.  她的美貌使屋里每个人都黯然失色。|例句：The fireworks display outdazzled the previous year's show. 今年的烟火表演比去年的更加精彩。|近义词：outshine, eclipse, overshadow|反义词：be outshone, be eclipsed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The singer outdazzles the audience with her performance. = 歌手以她的表演征服了观众。
His charisma outdazzles all his colleagues. = 他的魅力胜过所有同事。


一般过去时:
The diamond necklace outdazzled all the other jewelry. = 那条钻石项链的光彩胜过所有其他珠宝。

现在完成时:

The performance has outdazzled expectations. = 表演已经超出了预期。
