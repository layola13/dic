cauliculus:/kɔːˈlɪkjʊləs/ | n. |小茎；小花梗；小柄 | A small stem or stalk, especially the short stem supporting a flower or fruit.  The term is often used in botany to describe small, stem-like structures.  | 例句：The cauliculus of the flower was barely visible.  这朵花的小花梗几乎看不见。

The cauliculus connected the seed to the plant. 小茎连接着种子和植株。| 近义词：pedicel, stalk, stemlet | 反义词：无
