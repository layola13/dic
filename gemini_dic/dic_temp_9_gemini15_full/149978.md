palmer:/ˈpɑːlmər/| n.|朝圣者；棕榈树种植者；（尤指）手相家|A palmer is a pilgrim who has visited the Holy Land and carries a palm branch as a symbol of their journey.  The word can also refer to someone who cultivates palm trees or a person who practices palmistry (reading palms).|例句：Many palmers returned from the Holy Land with tales of their pilgrimage. 许多朝圣者从圣地回来讲述他们的朝圣故事。

例句：The old palmer read my palm and predicted a long and prosperous life. 那位老手相家看了我的掌纹，预言我将拥有漫长而富足的人生。


近义词：pilgrim, devotee, palm reader|反义词：N/A|词性：名词
