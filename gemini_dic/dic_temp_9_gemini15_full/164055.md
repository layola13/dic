prediscontent:/ˌpriːdɪsˈkɒntɛnt/| adj.|不满的，不满足的|Feeling or showing discontent before it is openly expressed;  preexisting discontent.  It suggests an underlying dissatisfaction that hasn't yet fully manifested. |例句：His prediscontent with his job eventually led him to quit.  他的工作不满最终导致他辞职了。

例句：There was a prediscontent among the workers even before the pay cuts were announced.  即使在减薪公告发布之前，工人们也已经心怀不满。


近义词：dissatisfied, disgruntled, discontented, resentful

反义词：content, satisfied, pleased


词性:形容词
