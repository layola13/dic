dropsies:/ˈdrɒpsiz/| n.|颤抖，手抖|A trembling or shaking of the hands, often associated with weakness or disease.|例句：The old woman suffered from the dropsies, making it difficult for her to hold a cup.  这位老妇人患有手抖，以至于很难端起杯子。|近义词：tremors, shakes, palsy|反义词：steadiness, firmness|名词
