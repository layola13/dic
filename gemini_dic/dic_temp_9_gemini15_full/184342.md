respectful: /rɪˈspektfəl/ | adj. |恭敬的；尊敬的；有礼貌的 | Showing or feeling respect.  It implies a polite and considerate attitude towards others, acknowledging their worth and feelings. |例句：He was always respectful to his elders. 他总是对长辈很尊敬。

例句：She showed respectful behavior towards her teacher. 她对老师表现得很尊敬。


近义词：courteous, polite, deferential, considerate | 反义词：disrespectful, rude, impolite, discourteous


词源:  The word "respectful" derives from the Old French "respectueux," ultimately from the Latin "respectus," meaning "regard, consideration." The suffix "-ful" indicates "full of" or "characterized by."
