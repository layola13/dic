preconsultations: /ˌpriːkɒnsəlˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 预先咨询；事先磋商 |  Plural form of "preconsultation," referring to consultations that take place before a main event, decision, or action.  These consultations aim to gather information, opinions, or feedback to inform subsequent decisions. | Example: The preconsultations with stakeholders were crucial in shaping the final project proposal.  预先与利益相关者进行磋商对于最终项目提案的形成至关重要。  | Near synonyms: preliminary discussions, advance consultations, prior consultations | Antonyms: post-consultations, follow-up consultations | noun
