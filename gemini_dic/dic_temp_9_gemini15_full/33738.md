circuiter:/sɜːˈkjuːtər/ | n. |巡回人员，巡回赛选手，电路设计师 |A person who travels from place to place, especially one who regularly visits a circuit of places for work or competition;  someone who designs or works with electronic circuits. |例句：He's a circuiter, constantly traveling between different conferences. 他是一个巡回人员，经常往返于不同的会议之间。  She's a top circuiter on the professional gaming circuit. 她是职业电竞巡回赛上的一名顶级选手。|近义词：traveler, itinerant, competitor, circuit designer |反义词：resident, homebody
