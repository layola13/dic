nonfriend: /ˈnɑːnfrɛnd/ | n. |非朋友，不相熟的人 |A person who is not a friend; someone you have a neutral or slightly negative relationship with.  This term often implies a prior acquaintance or interaction, distinguishing it from simply a stranger. |Example:  He's not exactly a friend, more of a nonfriend. 他算不上朋友，更像个不相熟的人。|近义词：acquaintance, casual contact, someone you know|反义词：friend, close friend, confidant
