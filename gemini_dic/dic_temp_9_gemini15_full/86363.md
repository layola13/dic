hectored:/ˈhɛktərd/| v.|训斥；恐吓；威吓|To bully or intimidate someone; to treat someone in a bossy or arrogant manner.|例句：The teacher hectored the students into silence.老师训斥学生们保持安静。例句：He constantly hectored his wife about her spending habits. 他不断地训斥他妻子的花钱习惯。|近义词：bully, intimidate, browbeat, domineer|反义词：encourage, support, soothe, comfort


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hectors his employees daily. = 他每天都训斥他的员工。
She hectors her children about their chores. = 她训斥她的孩子们去做家务。

一般过去时:
The manager hectored the team after the project failure. = 项目失败后，经理训斥了团队。
He hectored me into agreeing to his plan. = 他恐吓我同意他的计划。

现在完成时:
He has hectored his colleagues for years. = 他多年来一直恐吓他的同事。
She has constantly hectored me about my weight. = 她一直不断地恐吓我关于我的体重。

过去完成时:
He had hectored her into submission before she finally agreed. = 在她最终同意之前，他已经恐吓她屈服了。
They had hectored the witness into changing his testimony. = 他们已经恐吓证人改变他的证词。

将来时:
He will hector you into doing what he wants. = 他会恐吓你做他想要的事情。
She will likely hector him about his messy room. = 她很可能会因为他的凌乱房间而训斥他。

虚拟语气:
If he hectored me again, I would leave. = 如果他再次恐吓我，我会离开。
I wish he wouldn't hector me so much. = 我希望他不要再这么训斥我了。

被动语态:
The employees were hectored into working overtime. = 员工们被恐吓加班。
He felt hectored by his boss's constant criticism. = 他感觉被老板不断的批评所恐吓。

疑问句:
Does he often hector his children? = 他经常训斥他的孩子吗？
Was she hectored into signing the contract? = 她是被恐吓签署合同的吗？

否定句:
He didn't hector me; he simply explained the situation. = 他没有训斥我；他只是解释了情况。
She doesn't hector her students, she encourages them. = 她不训斥她的学生，她鼓励他们。


PHRASE->
hector someone into doing something = 恐吓某人做某事
constantly hector = 不断地训斥
be hectored by = 被…恐吓
hectoring tone = 训斥的语气
hectoring voice = 训斥的声音


词源:
The verb "hector" comes from the name Hector, a Trojan prince in Homer's Iliad, known for his bravery and belligerence.  Over time, the name became associated with bullying and domineering behavior, leading to the verb's current meaning.
