scored:/skɔːrd/ | vt. n. |得分；获得；刻痕；划痕 | to obtain a particular mark or grade in a test, exam, or competition; to achieve a particular amount of points in a game or competition;  to make a series of marks or scratches on a surface. | 例句：He scored 90% on the exam. 他考试得了90分。  She scored the winning goal. 她射入了制胜一球。 |近义词：obtained, achieved, gained, marked | 反义词：failed, missed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scores high marks in all subjects. = 他所有科目都考得很高。
She scores well in the competition. = 她在比赛中表现出色。


一般过去时:
They scored two goals in the first half. = 他们在上半场进了两个球。
He scored a perfect 100 on the test. = 他考试得了满分100分。


现在完成时:
The team has scored three goals so far. = 球队目前已经进了三个球。
She has scored consistently high marks throughout the semester. = 她整个学期都保持着高分。


过去完成时:
By the end of the game, they had scored five goals. = 到比赛结束时，他们已经进了五个球。
Before the final exam, she had scored poorly on most quizzes. = 期末考前，她大部分小测都考得很差。


将来时:
I think he will score well on the upcoming exam. = 我认为他即将到来的考试会考得好。
They hope to score at least ten goals in the match. = 他们希望在这场比赛中至少进十个球。


虚拟语气:
If he had studied harder, he would have scored higher. = 如果他学习更努力，他本可以考得更高。
I wish I had scored better on the test. = 我希望我在考试中考得更好。


被动语态:
The goal was scored by the captain. = 这个球是由队长进的。
Many points were scored during the last few minutes of the game. = 比赛最后几分钟进了很多分。


疑问句:
How many points did they score? = 他们得了多少分？
Did she score high enough to pass the exam? = 她得分够高通过考试了吗？


否定句:
He didn't score well on the math test. = 他数学考试考得不好。
The team failed to score any goals in the second half. = 球队下半场一个球都没进。
