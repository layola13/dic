launches:/lɔːntʃɪz/ | /ˈlɔːntʃɪz/ | vt. n. |发射；启动；推出；开办 | To propel (something, such as a rocket or spacecraft) into the air or space.  To introduce (a new product or service) to the market. To start or begin something. | 例句：The company launched a new marketing campaign. 公司启动了一场新的营销活动。


例句：NASA launched the satellite into orbit. 美国宇航局将卫星送入轨道。


近义词：initiate, start, begin, introduce, unveil


反义词：conclude, end, finish, terminate


词性：动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company launches new products every year. = 公司每年都会推出新产品。
She launches into a long story about her trip. = 她开始滔滔不绝地讲述她的旅行经历。


一般过去时:
They launched a rocket into space last week. = 他们上周发射了一枚火箭进入太空。
He launched his business with a small loan. = 他用一笔小额贷款启动了他的事业。


现在完成时:
They have launched three successful products this year. = 今年他们已经成功推出了三款产品。
She has launched several successful startups. = 她已经成功启动了几家初创公司。


过去完成时:
The project had been launched before he joined the team. = 他加入团队之前，项目就已经启动了。
The company had launched the product before the competitor. = 公司在竞争对手之前推出了该产品。


将来时:
We will launch the new website next month. = 我们下个月将推出新的网站。
They will launch a new advertising campaign in the spring. = 他们将在春天推出新的广告宣传活动。


虚拟语气:
If we had launched the product earlier, we would have had more market share. = 如果我们早点推出产品，就会有更大的市场份额。
I wish I had launched my business sooner. = 我真希望我早点创业。


被动语态:
The rocket was launched successfully. = 火箭成功发射了。
The new product line will be launched in the fall. = 新产品线将于秋季推出。


疑问句:
When will the new game be launched? = 新游戏什么时候推出？
Have they launched the new website yet? = 他们已经推出新网站了吗？


否定句:
The project was not launched on time. = 项目没有按时启动。
They did not launch the product as planned. = 他们没有按照计划推出产品。


PHRASE->
launch a product = 推出产品
launch a campaign = 发起一场运动
launch an attack = 发动攻击
launch an investigation = 开始调查
launch a missile = 发射导弹
