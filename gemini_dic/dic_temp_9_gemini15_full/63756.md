epicentres: /ˈɛpɪˌsɛntərz/ | n. |震中；中心 | The points on the earth's surface vertically above the focus of an earthquake, from which seismic waves radiate outwards.  Epicentres are often used metaphorically to refer to the central point of an activity or problem. |例句：The epicentre of the earthquake was located in a sparsely populated area. 地震震中位于人口稀疏地区。

The epicentre of the political unrest was the capital city. 政治动荡的中心是首都。|近义词：center, focus, core |反义词：periphery, outskirts
