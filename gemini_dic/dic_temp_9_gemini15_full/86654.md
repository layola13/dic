hellene:/ˈhɛliːn/| n. |希腊人；希腊语使用者|A person of Greek origin or a speaker of the Greek language.  The term can refer to both ancient and modern Greeks.  It emphasizes the cultural and linguistic aspects of Greek identity.|Example:  Many Hellenes migrated to America in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. 许多希腊人在19世纪末和20世纪初移民到美国。|近义词：Greek|反义词：None directly applicable.
