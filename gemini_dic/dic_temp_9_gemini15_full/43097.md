cowwheat:/ˈkaʊwiːt/| n. |小麦, 粗麦粉 | Cowwheat is a type of plant that belongs to the genus Melampyrum.  It is characterized by its small, tubular flowers and its parasitic nature, often growing on the roots of other plants.  |例句：The cowwheat grew profusely in the meadow.  = 牛膝菊在草地上大量生长。 |近义词：wheat, grain |反义词:  N/A
