meddlesomeness:/ˈmɛdəlsəmnəs/| n. | 爱管闲事；干涉；多事；好为人师|The act of interfering in other people's affairs or business, often in a way that is unwelcome or annoying.  It implies excessive curiosity and a tendency to involve oneself in matters that do not concern one.|例句：Her meddlesomeness caused a great deal of trouble in the neighborhood.  她的爱管闲事在附近造成了很多麻烦。

例句：His constant meddlesomeness made him unpopular with his colleagues. 他不断地爱管闲事，使他在同事中不受欢迎。


近义词：interference, intervention, intrusion, officiousness

反义词：noninterference, unconcern, indifference

词性：名词
