quickener:/ˈkwɪkənər/| n.|加速器；促进剂|Something that quickens or hastens a process or action.  It can refer to a person, thing, or substance that speeds up something else.|The new fertilizer acted as a quickener for plant growth.|近义词：accelerator, catalyst, stimulant|反义词：retardant, inhibitor, decelerator
