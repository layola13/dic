conjugative:/ˈkɒndʒʊɡətɪv/ /ˈkɑːndʒʊɡeɪtɪv/ | adj. |结合的；接合的；共轭的 | Relating to or denoting bacterial conjugation.  Conjugative elements are usually plasmids or transposons that can transfer themselves to another bacterium.  This process is crucial for the spread of antibiotic resistance and other genetic traits. | Example: The study focused on the conjugative transfer of antibiotic resistance genes between bacterial strains.  这项研究集中在细菌菌株之间抗生素抗性基因的接合转移。 |近义词：transferable, transmissible |反义词：non-conjugative
