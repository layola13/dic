gringa:/ˈɡriŋɡɑː/| n.|老美女人；外国女人（尤指在拉丁美洲的美国或加拿大女人）|A gringa is a term, primarily used in Latin American Spanish, referring to a woman from the United States or Canada.  It can sometimes carry a connotation of being a foreigner who is unfamiliar with local customs or culture, and occasionally, a negative or derogatory connotation. However, in many contexts, it is a neutral term simply referring to a female foreigner.|Example:  "The gringa ordered her coffee with extra sugar."  “那个美国女人要加糖的咖啡。”|近义词：foreigner, expat (in some contexts)|反义词：local
