hippopotami:/ˌhɪpəˈpɑːtəmaɪ/| n. |河马(复数)|The plural of hippopotamus.  It refers to more than one hippopotamus.  Hippopotami are large, semi-aquatic mammals native to Africa. They are known for their barrel-shaped bodies, short legs, and large mouths. |例句：Several hippopotami were seen wallowing in the river.  几只河马被看到在河里打滚。|例句：The herd of hippopotami grazed peacefully on the riverbank.一群河马在河岸上平静地吃草。|近义词：hippos (informal)|反义词:无
