hunker down:/ˈhʌŋkər daʊn/| v.| 蜷缩；躲藏；坚持到底；安顿下来 | To crouch or settle down low to the ground, often for protection or warmth; to persevere through a difficult situation. | 例句：We hunkered down in the basement during the storm. = 我们在暴风雨期间躲在地下室里。  They decided to hunker down and weather the economic crisis. = 他们决定坚持下去，渡过经济危机。|近义词：crouch, squat, persevere, endure |反义词：stand up, leave, give up


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hunker down every night by the fireplace. = 他们每晚都蜷缩在壁炉旁。
We hunker down and focus on our work during busy seasons. = 在繁忙的季节里，我们都会埋头苦干。


一般过去时:
The hikers hunkered down for the night under a rocky overhang. = 登山者在岩石突出的地方过夜。
She hunkered down and finished writing her report. = 她坚持到底，完成了她的报告。


现在完成时:
We have hunkered down for the winter. = 我们已经为冬天做好了准备（安顿下来）。
They have hunkered down and fought through many challenges. = 他们坚持不懈，克服了许多挑战。


过去完成时:
They had hunkered down before the tornado hit. = 龙卷风来临之前，他们已经躲起来了。
We had hunkered down for several hours when the power came back on. = 断电后我们躲藏了好几个小时，电力才恢复。


将来时:
We will hunker down and wait for the storm to pass. = 我们将躲起来，等待暴风雨过去。
They will hunker down in their mountain cabin for the winter months. = 他们将在冬季的几个月里待在山上的小屋里。


虚拟语气:
If we had hunkered down sooner, we would have been safer. = 如果我们早点躲起来，我们会更安全。
I wish we had hunkered down and saved more money last year. = 我真希望我们去年能省下更多的钱。


被动语态:  (Hunker down is generally not used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Will they hunker down during the hurricane? = 飓风期间他们会躲起来吗？
Did they hunker down when the shooting started? = 开枪的时候他们躲起来了没有？


否定句:
They didn't hunker down during the earthquake. = 地震期间他们没有躲起来。
We won't hunker down unless the situation becomes dangerous. = 除非情况变得危险，否则我们不会躲起来。
