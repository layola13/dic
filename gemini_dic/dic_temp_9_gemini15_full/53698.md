disclaimer:/dɪsˈkleɪmər/ | n. |免责声明，否认 | A statement that denies responsibility for something.  It is often used to limit liability or avoid legal responsibility. | Example: The website contains a disclaimer stating that it is not responsible for the accuracy of the information provided.  网站上有一个免责声明，声明其不对所提供信息的准确性负责。|近义词：denial, repudiation, disavowal | 反义词：affirmation, endorsement, guarantee
