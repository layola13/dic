costermongered:/ˈkɒstərmʌŋɡərd/| v.| 叫卖；兜售（尤指水果蔬菜）|To hawk goods, especially fruit and vegetables, in the street.  The word implies a somewhat aggressive or insistent manner of selling.|例句：The costermonger shouted his wares loudly in the market.  那个小贩在大声叫卖他的商品。

例句：He costermongered his apples with great enthusiasm. 他热情地叫卖着他的苹果。


近义词：hawked, peddled, vend, retailed


反义词：donated, gifted


词性:动词
