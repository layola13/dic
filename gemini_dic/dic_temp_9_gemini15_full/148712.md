overtorture: /ˌoʊvərˈtɔːrtʃər/ | v. |过度折磨，虐待 | To torture excessively or beyond what is necessary or justifiable.  It implies a degree of cruelty and unnecessary suffering beyond simple torture. |例句：The prisoners were overtortured for information, resulting in severe physical and psychological trauma. 囚犯们为了获取信息而遭受了过度折磨，导致了严重的身心创伤。  The dictator was known for overtorturing his political opponents.  独裁者以过度折磨其政治对手而闻名。|近义词：excessively torture, cruelly torment, barbarously abuse |反义词：nurture, care for
