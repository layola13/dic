disentrail:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtreɪl/| vt.|取出内脏；去除内脏|To remove the entrails from (an animal).  The act of removing the entrails from something, especially an animal carcass.|例句：The butcher disentrailed the pig before preparing it for sale.屠夫在准备出售猪肉之前取出了猪的内脏。

例句：After the hunt, they disentrailed the deer and prepared it for cooking.打猎后，他们取出鹿的内脏并准备烹饪。
