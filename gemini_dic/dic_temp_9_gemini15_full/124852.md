mucopolysaccharidosis:/ˌmjuːkoʊˌpɒlɪˌsækəraɪˈdoʊsɪs/| n. |粘多糖沉积症|A group of genetic disorders that affect the body's ability to break down complex sugars called glycosaminoglycans (GAGs).  The buildup of these sugars in various tissues and organs causes a range of symptoms depending on the specific type of MPS. |例句：Mucopolysaccharidosis type I, also known as Hurler syndrome, is a severe form of the disease. 粘多糖沉积症I型，也称为赫勒综合征，是该病的严重形式。|近义词：lysosomal storage disease|反义词：N/A
