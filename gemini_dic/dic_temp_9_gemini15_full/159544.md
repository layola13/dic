politicizing: /ˌpɒləˈtɪsaɪzɪŋ/ | /ˌpɑːləˈtɪsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使政治化；赋予政治意义 | To give a political character to (something that is not inherently political); to make political.  | 例句：The issue of climate change is increasingly being politicized.  气候变化问题正日益被政治化。

例句：They accused the media of politicizing the tragedy. 他们指责媒体将这场悲剧政治化了。


近义词：politicize, ideologize

反义词：depoliticize, depersonalize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are politicizing the debate. = 他们在使这场辩论政治化。
The government politicizes every issue. = 政府将每一个问题都政治化。


一般过去时:
The media politicized the accident. = 媒体将这场事故政治化了。

虚拟语气:
