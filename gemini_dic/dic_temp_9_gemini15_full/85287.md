harmonizations:/ˌhɑːrmənaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |协调；和谐；统一 |The process of bringing different elements into agreement or conformity; the state of being in harmony.  It often refers to the process of aligning different systems, standards, or practices.  This can apply to various fields, including music, economics, and international relations. |例句：The harmonizations of the different musical instruments created a beautiful sound. 各种乐器的协调演奏创造出美妙的声音。

The harmonizations of international trade regulations are essential for global economic stability. 国际贸易规则的协调对于全球经济稳定至关重要。


近义词：coordination, unification, integration, alignment |反义词：discord, conflict, disharmony
