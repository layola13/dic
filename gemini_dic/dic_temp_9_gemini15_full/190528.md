satiation:/ˌsæʃiˈeɪʃən/| n. |饱足；满足；厌腻|The state of being completely satisfied; fullness.  It can refer to a feeling of fullness after eating, or a feeling of having enough of something.  This can also extend to a feeling of boredom or weariness due to an overabundance.|例句：After the large meal, he experienced a feeling of satiation.  大餐之后，他感到饱足。  The constant repetition of the same task led to satiation and a lack of motivation.  重复同样的工作导致了厌倦和缺乏动力。|近义词：satisfaction, repletion, fullness, surfeit|反义词：hunger, deprivation, want, dissatisfaction
