compile:/kəmˈpaɪl/| vt.|编译，汇编|To collect and combine (different pieces of written material) into a book or other publication; to translate (a program written in a high-level programming language) into machine code.|例句：She compiled a report on the accident. 她编写了一份关于事故的报告。
例句：The compiler translated the source code into executable code. 编译器将源代码翻译成可执行代码。|近义词：collect, assemble, translate|反义词：disassemble, decompile


USAGE->

She compiles a list of tasks before starting work. = 她开始工作前会列一个任务清单。
