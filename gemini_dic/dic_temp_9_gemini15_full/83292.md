gynandria:/ˌdʒaɪˈnændriə/| n.|雌雄同体（植物）|A gynandrium is a structure in certain orchids where the stamens and pistil are fused together into a single column.  This structure is characteristic of many orchid species and plays a crucial role in pollination. | Example:  The orchid displayed a prominent gynandrium, a key feature for its identification.  这株兰花显示出一个突出的雌雄蕊合体，这是其鉴定的一个关键特征。|近义词：hermaphrodite (in botany)|反义词：unisexual flower (单性花)
