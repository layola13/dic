presutural: /ˌpriːˈsuːtʃərəl/ | adj. |  suture之前的；缝合线之前的 |  Relating to the area or structure situated before or in front of a suture, particularly in the context of anatomy, especially of the skull.  The term often refers to the bones or structures located anterior to a cranial suture. | Example: The presutural bone fragments were carefully examined.  缝合线之前的骨骼碎片被仔细检查。 The presutural region of the skull showed signs of trauma.  颅骨缝合线之前的区域显示出创伤迹象。 | 近义词:  ante-suture, pre-suture | 反义词: postsutural
