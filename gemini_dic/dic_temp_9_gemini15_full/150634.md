papillote:/ˌpæpɪˈlɒt/|/pæpɪˈloʊt/| n. |法式料理纸袋包; 纸袋烹调法|A cooking method in which food is sealed in parchment paper or foil and baked.  This method helps to retain moisture and flavor.|Example:  We cooked the salmon en papillote for a delicious and healthy meal. 我们用纸袋包法烹饪鲑鱼，做了一顿美味又健康的晚餐。|近义词:en papillote (French phrase)|反义词: N/A
