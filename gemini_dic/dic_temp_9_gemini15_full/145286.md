overbrace:/ˈoʊvərbreɪs/| n. |括号，上标括号|A symbol (typically a horizontal line with a label above) used in mathematical notation to group or indicate a part of an expression. It is used to show which terms are being grouped together or to clarify the meaning of a complex expression.|  Example:  The expression 2 + 2 + 2 can be written as 3⋅2  or  3×2 or  with an overbrace: 2 + 2 + 2.  This is three twos added together.|近义词：bracket, parenthesis|反义词:None.
