lists:/lɪsts/| n.|列表，清单|A list is a series of items written or printed one below the other.  It can be a shopping list, a to-do list, or a list of names.|例句：I made a list of things to buy at the grocery store. 我列了一个在杂货店要买的东西清单。|例句：The teacher called out the names on the attendance list. 老师大声念出点名册上的名字。|近义词：catalogue, inventory, schedule|反义词：disorder, chaos|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher lists the students' names. =老师列出学生的名字。
She lists her favorite movies. =她列出了她最喜欢的电影。

一般过去时:
He listed all the ingredients. =他列出了所有配料。
They listed their accomplishments on their resumes. =他们在简历上列出了他们的成就。

现在完成时:
She has listed all the items for sale. =她已经列出了所有待售物品。
We have listed our house for sale. =我们已经把房子挂牌出售了。

过去完成时:
He had listed the tasks before the meeting. =他在会议前列出了任务。
They had listed the pros and cons before making a decision. =他们在做决定之前列出了利弊。

将来时:
I will list the participants tomorrow. =我明天将列出参与者名单。
She will list the reasons for her resignation. =她将列出她辞职的理由。

虚拟语气:
If I listed all my problems, it would take all day. =如果我列出我所有的问题，那将花费一整天。
I wish I had listed the steps more clearly. =我希望我更清楚地列出了步骤。

被动语态:
The names were listed alphabetically. =名字按字母顺序排列。
The items will be listed on the website. =这些物品将在网站上列出。

疑问句:
What items are listed on the menu? =菜单上列出了哪些项目？
Are all the participants listed correctly? =所有参与者是否都正确列出？

否定句:
He didn't list all the necessary information. =他没有列出所有必要的信息。
They haven't listed the price of the product yet. =他们还没有列出产品的价格。


PHRASE->
a shopping list = 购物清单
a to-do list = 待办事项清单
a list of names = 名单
a prioritized list = 优先级列表
a waiting list = 等待名单
