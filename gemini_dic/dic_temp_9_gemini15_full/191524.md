schmucks:/ʃmʌks/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A schmuck is a foolish or contemptible person.  It's a Yiddish term considered highly informal and often offensive.|例句：Don't be such a schmuck!  别那么傻！

例句：He acted like a total schmuck. 他表现得像个十足的傻瓜。


近义词：fool, idiot, moron, jerk, dope

反义词：genius, intellect, wise person


词性:名词
