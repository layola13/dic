contractor:/kənˈtræktər/ | n. |承包商，承建商 | A person or company that agrees to do work or provide goods at a fixed price.  |例句：The construction contractor finished the building ahead of schedule.  这家建筑承包商提前完成了大楼的建设。 |例句： We hired a contractor to renovate our kitchen. 我们雇了一家承包商来装修我们的厨房。|近义词：builder, supplier, vendor |反义词：employee, client


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The contractor builds houses. = 承包商建造房屋。
The company contracts with many clients. = 这家公司与许多客户签订合同。


一般过去时:
The contractor built a bridge last year. = 承包商去年建了一座桥。
They contracted with a local firm. = 他们与当地的一家公司签订了合同。


现在完成时:
The contractor has completed several projects. = 承包商已经完成了几个项目。
The firm has contracted for the new highway. = 该公司已经承包了新的高速公路。


过去完成时:
The contractor had finished the work before the deadline. = 承包商在截止日期前完成了工作。
The company had contracted with them for years. = 这家公司已经和他们合作多年了。


将来时:
The contractor will start the project next month. = 承包商下个月将开始该项目。
They will contract with a new supplier. = 他们将与一个新的供应商签订合同。


虚拟语气:
If the contractor were available, we would hire him. = 如果承包商有空，我们会雇佣他。
I wish the company had contracted with us earlier. = 我希望该公司早些时候就与我们签订合同。


被动语态:
The house was built by a local contractor. = 这房子是由当地的一家承包商建造的。
The contract was signed last week. = 合同是上周签署的。


疑问句:
Who is the contractor for this project? = 这个项目的承包商是谁？
Has the company contracted with anyone yet? = 公司已经与任何人签订合同了吗？


否定句:
The contractor did not finish on time. = 承包商没有按时完成。
The company has not contracted with them before. = 这家公司以前没有与他们签订过合同。


PHRASE->
general contractor: 总承包商
independent contractor:  独立承包商
construction contractor: 建筑承包商
government contractor:  政府承包商
contract contractor: 合同承包商
