reprehend:/ˌrɛprɪˈhɛnd/| vt.|责备；谴责；批评|To reprimand or rebuke; to express disapproval of.|The teacher reprimanded the student for talking in class.  老师因学生在课堂上讲话而责备了他。|He was reprimanded for his careless mistake. 他因粗心大意而受到责备。|rebuke, reproach, chide, admonish|praise, commend, applaud|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reprimands his children often. 他经常训斥他的孩子。
She reprimands her employees for being late. 她因员工迟到而责备他们。

一般过去时:
My boss reprimanded me for my mistake. 我的老板因我的错误而责备我。
The teacher reprimanded the students for their disruptive behavior. 老师因学生扰乱课堂秩序而责备他们。

现在完成时:
He has reprimanded his son several times this week.  本周他已经训斥了他的儿子好几次了。
She has never reprimanded me before. 她以前从未责备过我。

过去完成时:
The principal had reprimanded the student before calling his parents. 校长在打电话给学生的家长之前已经责备过他了。
They had reprimanded him several times, but he continued to misbehave. 他们已经训斥了他好几次，但他仍然继续不守规矩。

将来时:
I will reprimand him if he does it again. 如果他再这样做，我会责备他。
She will reprimand the students who are late. 她会责备迟到的学生。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would reprimand him. 如果我是你，我会责备他。
I wish I had reprimanded him sooner. 我希望我早点责备他。

被动语态:
He was reprimanded for his behavior. 他因他的行为受到责备。
The students were reprimanded for cheating on the exam. 学生们因考试作弊而受到责备。

疑问句:
Will you reprimand him for his actions? 你会因为他的行为而责备他吗？
Has she ever been reprimanded by her boss? 她曾经被老板责备过吗？

否定句:
I did not reprimand him, I simply warned him. 我没有责备他，我只是警告了他。
She will not reprimand him unless he repeats the mistake. 除非他犯同样的错误，否则她不会责备他。
