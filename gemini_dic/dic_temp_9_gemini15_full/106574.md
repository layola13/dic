ksar:/ksɑːr/| n.| 堡垒，要塞 | A ksar is a fortified village or settlement, typically found in North Africa, particularly in Morocco and Algeria.  They are often built of earth and stone, and characterized by their defensive walls and tightly packed buildings.  | Example: The ancient ksar of Ait Benhaddou is a UNESCO World Heritage site.  古代艾本哈杜堡垒是联合国教科文组织世界遗产地。|近义词：fortress, citadel, stronghold |反义词：open settlement, undefended village
