nightmares:/ˈnaɪtmerz/| n.|噩梦|A nightmare is a frightening or unpleasant dream.  Nightmares often involve feelings of fear, helplessness, or anxiety. They can be vivid and disturbing, and may leave a person feeling anxious or unsettled even after waking up.|例句：I had a nightmare last night about being chased by a monster. = 我昨晚做了一个噩梦，梦见被怪物追赶。

I woke up screaming after a particularly terrifying nightmare. = 经历了一个特别可怕的噩梦后，我尖叫着醒来。|近义词：bad dream, horrific dream, frightening dream|反义词：pleasant dream, sweet dream
