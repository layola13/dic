fileted:/fɪˈleɪtɪd/| adj.|去骨的；剔除骨头的|Having had the bones removed.  This usually refers to fish or meat.|例句：The fish was fileted and ready to cook.  这条鱼已经去骨，可以烹饪了。  The chicken breast was perfectly fileted. 鸡胸肉去骨很完美。|近义词：boneless, deboned|反义词：boned
