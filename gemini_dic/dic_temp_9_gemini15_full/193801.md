self-depraved: /ˌsɛlf dɪˈpreɪvd/ | adj. | 自我堕落的；道德败坏的 |  Describes someone who has corrupted their own morals or character through their own actions. It implies a deliberate and conscious act of self-destruction or moral degradation.  | Example: He led a self-depraved life, indulging in excessive drinking and drug use. 他过着自我堕落的生活，沉迷于酗酒和吸毒。 | 近义词：self-corrupt, morally bankrupt, degenerate, depraved | 反义词：virtuous, moral, upright, righteous |
