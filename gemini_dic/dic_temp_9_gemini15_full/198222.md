shorteners:/ˈʃɔːrtənərz/ | n. | 缩短器，缩写器 | Devices or services that shorten URLs or other long pieces of text.  They are widely used online to make links more manageable and aesthetically pleasing.  Examples include bit.ly, tinyurl.com, and the built-in shortening functions of some social media platforms. | Example:  Many social media platforms use URL shorteners to make links easier to share. 许多社交媒体平台使用网址缩短器来使链接更容易分享。| 近义词：URL shorteners, link shorteners | 反义词：expanders (in the context of text) | 名词
