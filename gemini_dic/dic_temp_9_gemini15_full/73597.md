forethoughtful: /ˈfɔːrθɔːtfl/ | adj. |  深思熟虑的；有先见之明的 |  Showing careful consideration of the likely consequences of actions; having foresight.  | 例句：Her forethoughtful planning ensured the event ran smoothly.  她的深思熟虑的计划确保了活动的顺利进行。


例句：The forethoughtful investor diversified his portfolio.  这位有先见之明的投资者使他的投资组合多样化。


近义词：considerate, thoughtful, provident, farsighted


反义词：inconsiderate, thoughtless, improvident, shortsighted


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is forethoughtful in his investments. = 他在投资方面深思熟虑。
She is always forethoughtful about her future plans. = 她总是对未来的计划深思熟虑。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
He has been forethoughtful in his career choices. = 他在职业选择方面一直深思熟虑。
She has been forethoughtful in saving for retirement. = 她一直为退休储蓄而深思熟虑。


过去完成时:
He had been forethoughtful in securing the contract. = 他在确保合同方面已经深思熟虑。
She had been forethoughtful in booking the tickets in advance. = 她已经很有先见之明地提前预订了车票。

被动语态:  (This adjective is rarely used in the passive voice)  The project was planned in a forethoughtful manner. = 这个项目以深思熟虑的方式进行规划。

否定句:

She was not forethoughtful in her spending habits. = 她在消费习惯上不够深思熟虑。

词源:  The word "forethoughtful" combines "forethought" (prior planning or consideration) with the suffix "-ful" (meaning "full of" or "characterized by").  Forethought itself comes from "fore-" (meaning "before" or "in advance") and "thought".
