palaeontology:/ˌpeɪliɒnˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. |古生物学|The scientific study of life that existed prior to, and sometimes including, the start of the Holocene Epoch (roughly 11,700 years before present).  It includes the study of fossils to determine organisms' evolution and interactions with each other and their environments. |例句：Palaeontology provides crucial insights into the history of life on Earth. 古生物学为地球生命史提供了至关重要的见解。
例句：The discovery of a new dinosaur fossil has excited the field of palaeontology.  发现新的恐龙化石让古生物学界兴奋不已。|近义词：paleontology|反义词：无
