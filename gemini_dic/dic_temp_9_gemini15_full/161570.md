potter:/ˈpɒtər/ /ˈpɑːtər/| n., v.| 陶工；笨手笨脚的人；忙乱；(尤指) 闲逛 |  A potter is a person who makes pots and other articles of clay. To potter means to do things in a leisurely and unhurried way, often involving small tasks. |例句：He's a skilled potter, known for his beautiful ceramic pieces. 他是一位技艺精湛的陶工，以其精美的陶瓷作品而闻名。|近义词：craftsman (n.),  idle (v.)|反义词：expert (n., adj.),  industrious (adj.)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He potters around in the garden every afternoon. 他每天下午都在花园里闲逛。
She potters with her plants, making sure they get enough water. 她忙着照料她的植物，确保它们有足够的水分。

一般过去时:
He pottered around the house all day yesterday. 他昨天一整天都在家里闲逛。
They pottered about in the kitchen, preparing for the party. 他们在厨房里忙忙碌碌地准备派对。


现在完成时:
He has pottered away most of the afternoon. 他已经闲逛了大部分下午。
She has been pottering around the house all day. 她一整天都在家里忙忙碌碌。


过去完成时:
He had pottered around the garden for hours before it started to rain. 下雨之前，他在花园里闲逛了几个小时。
They had pottered about for a while before finally deciding what to do. 他们闲逛了一会儿，才最终决定做什么。


将来时:
I will potter around the house this weekend. 我这个周末将在家里闲逛。
She will potter in the garden all afternoon tomorrow. 她明天下午将在花园里闲逛。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would potter around more often. 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常地闲逛。
I wish I could potter around in the countryside all day. 我希望我能整天在乡下闲逛。


被动语态:  (The verb "potter" is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Does he often potter around the house? 他经常在家里闲逛吗？
What are you pottering about with? 你在忙些什么？


否定句:
He doesn't potter around much anymore. 他不再经常闲逛了。
She didn't potter about in the garden this morning. 她今天早上没有在花园里闲逛。


PHRASE->
potter about/around:  闲逛，忙忙碌碌
potter away:  消磨时间，虚度光阴
potter with:  摆弄，修补
a potter's wheel: 陶轮
potter's clay: 陶土
