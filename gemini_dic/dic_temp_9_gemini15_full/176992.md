ream:/riːm/ | n. | 一刀纸(500张)；大量 | A ream is a quantity of paper, usually 500 sheets.  It can also refer to a large quantity of something. | Example: I bought a ream of printer paper. 我买了一刀打印纸。  He wrote a ream of articles last month. 他上个月写了大量的文章。 |近义词：quantity, batch, pile | 反义词：少量, few
