licking:/ˈlɪkɪŋ/| v.|舔；轻拍；轻拂|The act of passing the tongue over a surface.  It can also refer to a quick, light blow or touch.|例句：The cat was licking its paw.  这只猫正在舔它的爪子。

例句：The waves were licking the shore.  波浪轻拍着海岸。


近义词：lapping, dabbing, stroking|反义词：N/A


USAGE->

He is licking the ice cream. = 他正在舔冰淇淋。
The dog is licking its bowl. = 狗正在舔它的碗。

一般过去时:
She licked the stamp. = 她舔了邮票。

过去完成时:

The cat had licked itself clean before I arrived. = 我到的时候，猫已经把自己舔干净了。
