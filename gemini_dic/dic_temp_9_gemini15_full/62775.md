engraved:/ɪnˈɡreɪvd/ | vt. | 雕刻的；铭刻的 | To cut or carve (a design, inscription, etc.) into a hard surface.  | 例句：The artist engraved the lovers' names into the silver locket. 艺术家将情侣的名字雕刻在银锁上。

例句：The inscription was beautifully engraved on the gold ring.  铭文精美地刻在金戒指上。


近义词：carve, etch, inscribe | 反义词：erase, obliterate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He engraves beautiful designs on wood. = 他在木头上雕刻精美的图案。
The company engraves trophies for various events. = 这家公司为各种活动雕刻奖杯。

一般过去时:
