epiphyllous:/ˌɛpɪˈfɪləs/| adj.|着生于叶上的；叶生的|Growing on a leaf.  The term refers to structures, such as flowers or other plant organs, that arise from the surface of a leaf rather than from the stem or branch. |Example: The epiphyllous flowers of this species are particularly striking.  这种植物叶生的花朵格外引人注目。|近义词：leaf-borne|反义词：cauline (stem-borne)
