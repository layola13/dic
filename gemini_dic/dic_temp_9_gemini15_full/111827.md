loggerhead:/ˈlɒɡəhed/ /ˈlɑːɡərˌhed/| n. |笨蛋；傻瓜；棱皮龟|A loggerhead is a stupid or stubborn person, or a type of sea turtle.  The word's use referring to a person is now largely informal and somewhat dated.  As a noun referring to the turtle, it is a common and accepted term. |Example: He was a real loggerhead, refusing to listen to reason. 他真是个笨蛋，不肯听劝。

Example: The loggerhead sea turtle is an endangered species. 棱皮龟是一种濒危物种。|近义词(人):fool, idiot, dunce |反义词(人):clever, intelligent, bright |词性:n.
