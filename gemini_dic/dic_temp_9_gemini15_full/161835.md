/ˈpræktɪsɪz/| n.|练习；实践|The act of doing something repeatedly to improve one's skill or knowledge.  It refers to the process of repeated performance or application to gain proficiency.|例句：He needs more practice on his pronunciation. 他需要更多练习他的发音。|例句：The teacher emphasized the importance of regular practice. 老师强调了定期练习的重要性。|近义词：drill, exercise, training|反义词：neglect, idleness|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His daily practices improve his skills. = 他每天的练习提高了他的技能。
Regular practices are essential for success. = 定期练习对于成功至关重要。

一般过去时:
Her practices last week were very intensive. = 她上周的练习非常紧张。
We had several practices before the game. = 我们比赛前进行了几次练习。

现在完成时:
I have completed all my practices for the week. = 我已经完成了本周所有的练习。
She has had extensive practices in public speaking. = 她有大量的公开演讲练习经验。

过去完成时:
They had finished their practices before the competition began. = 比赛开始前他们已经完成了练习。
We had practiced many times before the performance. = 表演前我们练习了很多次。

将来时:
We will have practices every day next week. = 下周我们每天都会有练习。
They will practice their dance routine tomorrow. = 他们明天将练习他们的舞蹈套路。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would practice more. = 如果我有更多时间，我会练习更多。
I wish I had practiced harder for the exam. = 我希望我为考试练习更努力一些。

被动语态:
The exercises were practiced diligently. = 这些练习被认真地练习了。
The piano piece is being practiced by the student. = 这首钢琴曲正在被学生练习。

疑问句:
What kind of practices are you doing? = 你在做什么样的练习？
How often do you have practices? = 你多久练习一次？

否定句:
He doesn't practice regularly. = 他不定期练习。
They didn't practice enough for the concert. = 他们为音乐会练习得不够。
