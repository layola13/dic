japonism:/ˈdʒæpənɪzəm/| n. |日本主义；日式风格|The stylistic influence of Japanese art and design on Western art, particularly in the late 19th and early 20th centuries.  It's characterized by asymmetry, naturalism, and a focus on the beauty of the everyday. |例句：The painting shows a clear influence of Japonism, with its asymmetrical composition and muted colours. 这幅画明显受到日本主义的影响，其构图不对称，色彩柔和。|近义词：orientalism(更广义的东方主义)|反义词：无|

词源:
The word "Japonism" is a relatively modern coinage, derived from the French word "Japonisme," which itself is derived from "Japon," the French word for Japan.  Its emergence reflects the growing interest in Japanese art and culture in the West during the late 19th century.
