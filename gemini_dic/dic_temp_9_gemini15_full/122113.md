mistook:/mɪˈstuːk/| v.|误认为，弄错|To incorrectly identify or understand something or someone.  It is the past tense of the verb "mistake".|例句：I mistook her for her sister. 我把她误认为是她姐姐了。

例句：I mistook the date and missed the deadline. 我弄错了日期，错过了截止日期。


近义词：misunderstood, misidentified, confused|反义词：understood, correctly identified


词性:动词
