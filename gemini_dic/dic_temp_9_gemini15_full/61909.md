empyesis:/ɛmˈpaɪsɪs/| n. |脓肿；蓄脓|The accumulation of pus in a body cavity.  Empyema is a collection of pus within a body cavity, especially the pleural space.  |例句：The patient developed an empyema after undergoing lung surgery.  患者肺部手术后出现了脓胸。|例句：The physician drained the empyema to relieve the patient's symptoms. 医生排出了脓液以缓解病人的症状。|近义词：suppuration, pyemia |反义词：absence of pus, sterility
