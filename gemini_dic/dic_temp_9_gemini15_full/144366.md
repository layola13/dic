/ˌaʊtˈstɛpt/| v.|超过；超越；越权|To go beyond the limits of (something); to exceed or surpass.  It also implies exceeding appropriate boundaries or authority.|例句：He outstepped his authority by releasing the information to the press. 他越权向媒体发布了信息。

例句：The athlete outstepped his competitors, winning the race by a significant margin.  这位运动员超越了他的竞争对手，以显著的优势赢得了比赛。


近义词：surpass, exceed, transcend, overstep


反义词：understep, underperform, remain within limits


词性:动词
