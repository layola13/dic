cense:/ˈsɛns/| n.|香,熏香|A substance that produces a fragrant smoke when burned, used in religious ceremonies or for scenting a room.  |Example: The priest burned incense to cense the temple.  祭司焚香以熏香寺庙。|近义词：incense, perfume|反义词：None.
