lockage:/ˈlɒkɪdʒ/ /ˈlɑːkɪdʒ/| n. |锁闸；船闸；阻塞；阻碍|The act or process of locking or being locked; the state of being locked.  It can also refer to the water level difference between two locks on a canal.  Or, the obstruction of something, like a blockage.|例句：The boat passed through the canal lockage.  船通过运河船闸。

The heavy snow caused a blockage in the pipes, resulting in a water supply blockage. 大雪导致管道堵塞，造成供水中断。|近义词：blockage, obstruction, impediment, damming|反义词：unlocking, opening, clearance
