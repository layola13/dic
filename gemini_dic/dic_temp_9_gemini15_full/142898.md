out-passes:/ˌaʊtˈpæsɪz/| v. |超越，超过，发出(球)|To surpass or exceed;  in sports, to pass the ball beyond an opponent's reach.|例句：The striker out-passed the defender and scored.  前锋超越后卫并进球了。|例句：The quarterback out-passed his opponent, gaining crucial yardage. 四分卫传球超过对手，获得了关键码数。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outmaneuver|反义词：underperform, fail|动词
