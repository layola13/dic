词源:  From Latin *faunus ("a Roman woodland deity, a kind of satyr"), from *faveō ("I favor, I am kind to"). The word's evolution reflects the association of Faunus with the animals of the forest.  The adjective form then emerged to describe aspects relating to the animal kingdom.
