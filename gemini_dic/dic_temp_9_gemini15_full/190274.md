sapucaia:/ˌsæpʊˈkaɪə/| n. |巴西栗|The sapucaia is a large South American tree (Lecythis pisonis) of the Lecythidaceae family, known for its distinctive fruit, which resembles a large, woody urn containing numerous seeds.  The tree is also called the monkey pot tree. The seeds, which are edible, are sometimes called Brazil nuts, though this name is more commonly associated with the seeds of Bertholletia excelsa.|The sapucaia tree provides shade and habitat for wildlife.  Many birds and animals rely on its fruits for food.|近义词：monkey pot tree|反义词:无
