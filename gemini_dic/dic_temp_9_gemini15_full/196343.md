serie: /ˈsɪəriːz/ | n. | 系列，连续剧 | A set or sequence of similar things, especially one appearing or occurring in succession.  A television or radio program that is broadcast in a series of episodes. | 例句：He collected a whole series of stamps. 他收集了一整套邮票。  The new television series is very popular.  这部新的电视剧非常受欢迎。 | 近义词：sequence, succession, string, set | 反义词：single, individual
