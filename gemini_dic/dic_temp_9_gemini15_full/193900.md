self-donation:/ˌsɛlfdoʊˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 自我捐赠；无偿献血 | The act of donating something, typically blood or organs, to oneself for later use. This is often used in the context of autologous blood donation where a person donates blood for their own future use in a planned surgery.  | Example:  Self-donation of blood is a common practice before elective surgeries to reduce the risk of transfusion reactions. 自我输血在择期手术前是一种常见的做法，以减少输血反应的风险。|近义词：autologous donation, pre-donation |反义词：altruistic donation (donation to others)
