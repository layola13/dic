/ˈlɪliz/| n.|百合花；百合属植物|Lilies are beautiful flowers known for their elegant appearance and fragrant scent. 百合花以其优雅的外观和芬芳的气味而闻名。|近义词：tulips, roses, daffodils|反义词：无
