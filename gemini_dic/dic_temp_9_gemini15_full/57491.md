dragbar: /ˈdræɡbɑːr/ | n. | 拖动条，拖动杆 | A rectangular bar or handle in a graphical user interface (GUI) that allows the user to drag and change the position or size of a window, object, or other element.  It's often used to resize or reposition elements on a screen. | Example:  The user dragged the dragbar at the bottom of the window to increase its height. 用户拖动窗口底部的拖动条来增加它的高度。 | 近义词：slider, handle, control | 反义词：无
