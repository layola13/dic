desecrating:/ˈdɛsɪkreɪtɪŋ/| v.|亵渎，玷污，破坏圣物|To violate the sacredness of something; to treat with disrespect.|例句：They were accused of desecrating the tomb of a saint.  他们被指控亵渎圣徒的坟墓。

例句：The vandals desecrated the war memorial. 那些破坏分子亵渎了战争纪念碑。


近义词：profane, violate, defile, pollute, contaminate|反义词：revere, sanctify, consecrate|动词
