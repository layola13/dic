gardenless:/ˈɡɑːrdənles/| adj.|无花园的，没有花园的|Lacking a garden.  The term describes a place, building, or property that does not have a garden.|例句：Their new apartment is rather small and gardenless. = 他们的新公寓很小，而且没有花园。

例句：The gardenless house felt stark and incomplete. =  这座没有花园的房子显得简陋而不完整。


近义词：gardened, ungardened

反义词：garden-filled, with a garden


词性：形容词
