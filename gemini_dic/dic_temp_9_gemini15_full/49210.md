delectable:/dɪˈlɛktəbəl/| adj.|令人愉快的；美味的；可口的|Pleasing to the senses, especially the taste; delicious.  The word often implies a refined or sophisticated pleasure.|例句：The delectable aroma of freshly baked bread filled the kitchen.  新鲜烘焙的面包的美味香气充满了厨房。

例句：We enjoyed a delectable meal at the restaurant.我们在餐厅享用了一顿美味佳肴。


近义词：delicious, delightful, appetizing, palatable, savory

反义词：disgusting, unappetizing, repulsive, distasteful

词性：形容词
