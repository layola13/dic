This term often refers to the lack of release of harmful substances into the environment. | Example: The company boasts of its commitment to nonemission technologies. 这家公司吹嘘其致力于无排放技术。 The new car engine promises near-zero nonemission. 新的汽车引擎承诺几乎零排放。|近义词：emission-free, zero-emission |反义词：emission, pollution
