governed:/ˈɡʌvənd/| v.|统治；管理；支配；影响|To control and direct the affairs of; rule.|The country is governed by a president and a parliament. 这个国家由总统和议会统治。|近义词：rule, control, regulate, administer|反义词：ungovernable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The king governs the country. = 国王统治着这个国家。
The rules govern our behavior. = 规则规范着我们的行为。


一般过去时:
The emperor governed wisely. = 皇帝统治得很明智。
The committee governed the decision-making process. = 委员会控制了决策过程。


现在完成时:
The queen has governed for fifty years. = 女王已经统治了五十年。
The law has governed this practice for decades. = 这项法律几十年来一直管辖着这种做法。


过去完成时:
They had governed the territory for centuries. = 他们统治这片领土已有几个世纪了。
The old regulations had governed the industry until recently. = 直到最近，旧的规章制度才管理着这个行业。


将来时:
She will govern with fairness and justice. = 她将以公平公正的方式进行统治。
New laws will govern the use of this technology. = 新法律将规范这项技术的应用。


虚拟语气:
If he governed differently, the country would prosper. = 如果他治理方式不同，国家就会繁荣。
I wish the country were governed more efficiently. = 我希望这个国家能够更加有效地治理。


被动语态:
The country is governed by a democratic system. = 这个国家由民主制度管理。
The company's operations are governed by strict regulations. = 该公司的运营受严格的规章制度约束。


疑问句:
Who governs this region? = 谁统治这个地区？
What principles govern the selection process? = 什么原则决定了选择过程？


否定句:
The city was not well governed. = 这个城市管理得不好。
The rules did not govern this particular situation. = 这些规则并不适用于这种情况。


PHRASE->
self-governed  = 自治的
well-governed = 治理良好的
poorly governed = 治理不善的
misgoverned = 施政不力的
govern oneself = 自我约束


词源:
源自古法语 *gouvernen，源自拉丁语 gubernare，意为“to steer, direct, govern”。
