scuta:/ˈskjuːtɑː/|/ˈskuːtɑː/| n. |角质盾;鳞甲|A scutum is a horny plate or scale, especially one of those forming the carapace of a turtle or the armor of a crocodile.  The word is often used in the plural, scuta.|例句：The turtle's carapace is composed of numerous scuta.  这只乌龟的甲壳由许多角质盾组成。

The large scuta on the crocodile's back provided excellent protection.  鳄鱼背上巨大的角质盾提供了极好的保护。|近义词：scale, plate, shield|反义词：无
