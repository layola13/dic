chummed:/tʃʌmd/ | v. |  成为朋友；变得友好 | To become friendly or familiar with someone; to make friends with someone.  |  The new students quickly chummed with each other. 新生们很快就彼此熟悉了。 |  Befriended, befriending, bonded |  estranged, alienated | 动词
