epicureanism:/ˌɛpɪˈkjuəriənɪzəm/| n. |享乐主义；伊壁鸠鲁哲学|The philosophy of Epicurus, emphasizing pleasure as the highest good, but defining pleasure as the absence of pain and mental disturbance, and advocating a simple life.  It's often mistakenly associated with simple hedonism.|Example:  His lifestyle is a clear example of epicureanism; he prioritizes comfort and enjoyment above all else.  他的人生方式是享乐主义的典型例子；他把舒适和享受置于一切之上。|近义词：hedonism, sensualism|反义词：asceticism, puritanism|名词
