cackler:/ˈkæklər/| n. |咯咯叫的人；母鸡；爱说闲话的人 | A cackler is a person who cackles, laughs loudly and shrilly, often in a somewhat unpleasant way.  It can also refer to a hen that cackles, or a person who gossips excessively. |例句：The old woman was a notorious cackler, spreading gossip throughout the village.  那个老妇人是个臭名昭著的嚼舌头，在村里到处散布流言蜚语。

例句：The farmer's cackler laid a dozen eggs this morning.  今天早上，农夫家的母鸡下了12个蛋。


近义词：gossiper, chatterbox, hen, blabbermouth|反义词：quiet person, reserved person
