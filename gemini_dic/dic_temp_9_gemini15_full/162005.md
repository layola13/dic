prawner:/ˈprɔːnər/| n. |对虾渔民；捕虾者；(非正式)爱吃对虾的人|A person who catches prawns.  The term can also be used informally to refer to someone who enjoys eating prawns. |例句：He's a prawner from the coast. 他是沿海的一个捕虾人。  She's a real prawner; she orders prawns every time we go out to eat. 她真是个爱吃虾的人；每次我们出去吃饭她都点虾。|近义词：shrimper, fisherman (in a broader sense) |反义词：None specifically  |词性：名词
