halo:/ˈheɪloʊ/ | n. |光环；晕圈；光轮 | A halo is a ring of light appearing around the sun or moon. It can also refer to a metaphorical ring of glory or prestige surrounding someone or something.  |  A halo of light surrounded the angel.  天使周围环绕着一圈光环。  The athlete was surrounded by a halo of success.  这位运动员被成功的荣光所环绕。| nimbus, aureole | darkness, shadow | 名词
