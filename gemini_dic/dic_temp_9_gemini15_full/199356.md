simmering:/ˈsɪmərɪŋ/| adj. v.| 煨，炖；即将爆发；酝酿 |  Describes something that is slowly boiling or cooking gently just below the boiling point.  It can also describe a situation or feeling that is gradually intensifying and about to reach a critical point. |例句：The sauce was simmering gently on the stove.  酱汁在炉子上轻轻地炖着。

The tension in the room was simmering.  房间里的紧张气氛正在酝酿。|近义词：stewing, brewing, bubbling, smoldering |反义词：boiling, exploding, calm, peaceful


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The soup simmers gently. = 汤慢慢地炖着。
She simmers with resentment. = 她心中充满了怨恨。


一般过去时:
The chef simmered the vegetables for an hour. = 厨师将蔬菜炖了一个小时。
He simmered in anger all afternoon. = 他整个下午都怒火中烧。


现在完成时:
The stew has been simmering for three hours. = 这炖菜已经炖了三个小时了。
She has been simmering with rage since the argument. = 自从那次争吵以来，她就一直怒火中烧。


过去完成时:
The sauce had simmered for too long and was reduced. = 酱汁炖的时间太长，变得浓稠了。
He had simmered his anger for days before finally exploding. = 他压抑怒火多日后终于爆发了。


将来时:
I will simmer the sauce until it thickens. = 我会把酱汁炖到浓稠为止。
The situation will simmer for a while before it erupts. = 局势会在爆发前持续酝酿一段时间。


虚拟语气:
If I simmered the vegetables longer, they would be softer. = 如果我把蔬菜炖久一点，它们就会更软。
If the anger simmered down, the conversation could have been more productive. = 如果怒气能够平息下来，谈话本可以更有成效。


被动语态:
The vegetables were simmered until tender. = 蔬菜被炖至软烂。
The conflict was simmering, unnoticed by many. = 冲突正在酝酿，许多人没有注意到。


疑问句:
Is the sauce simmering properly? = 酱汁炖得合适吗？
Are tensions simmering beneath the surface? = 表面之下是否暗流涌动？


否定句:
The soup wasn't simmering; it was boiling. = 汤不是在炖，而是在沸腾。
He didn't simmer his feelings; he expressed them openly. = 他没有压抑自己的感情，而是公开表达了它们。
