self-dissociated: /ˌsɛlf dɪˈsoʊʃieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 自我解离的，自我分离的 |  Describing a state where a person experiences a detachment or separation from their own thoughts, feelings, memories, or sense of self. This is often associated with dissociative disorders.  | Example:  The trauma victim exhibited self-dissociated behaviors, such as depersonalization and derealization.  自我解离的行为，例如人格解体和现实解体。| 近义词: detached, disassociated, estranged (from oneself) | 反义词: integrated, connected, grounded
