lease:/liːs/ | n. |租赁；租约；租赁物 | A lease is a contract granting use or occupation of property during a specified time for a specified payment.  It details the terms of the agreement between a landlord and a tenant.  | Example: We signed a lease for a one-year apartment rental. 我们签了一份为期一年的公寓租赁合同。|近义词：rental, tenancy, letting |反义词：purchase, ownership


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He leases a car every year. = 他每年租一辆车。
The company leases office space in the city center. = 公司在市中心租赁办公场所。

一般过去时:
They leased the property for five years. = 他们租赁了该物业五年。
She leased a small shop on Main Street. = 她在主街上租了一家小店。

现在完成时:
We have leased this building for ten years. = 我们已经租赁这座建筑十年了。
The family has leased a summer cottage by the lake. = 这家人在湖边租了一间夏季别墅。

过去完成时:
They had leased the apartment before they moved in. = 他们在搬进去之前已经租好了公寓。
The business had leased the equipment for several months. = 该企业已经租赁了该设备数月。

将来时:
We will lease a new car next month. = 我们下个月将租赁一辆新车。
They will lease a larger office when the company expands. = 当公司扩张时，他们将租赁更大的办公室。

虚拟语气:
If I had enough money, I would lease a yacht. = 如果我有足够的钱，我会租一艘游艇。
I wish I could lease a beachfront property. = 我希望我能租到海滨房产。

被动语态:
The apartment was leased quickly. = 这套公寓很快就租出去了。
The equipment is being leased to several different companies. = 设备正在租赁给几家不同的公司。

疑问句:
How long will you lease the car for? = 你要租多久的车？
Does the lease include utilities? = 租约包括水电费吗？

否定句:
They did not lease the house because of the high rent. = 由于租金高，他们没有租那所房子。
We will not lease a car unless it's fuel-efficient. = 除非汽车省油，否则我们不会租车。


PHRASE->
lease agreement = 租赁协议
lease term = 租赁期限
lease option = 租赁期权
break a lease = 违反租赁合同
enter into a lease = 签订租赁合同
