outeaten: /ˌaʊtˈiːtən/ | v. | 被吃光；被吃掉；被比下去 |  Past participle of "outeat".  It describes a situation where something has been completely consumed or surpassed in a competition involving eating.  |

The food was outeaten by the hungry guests.  = 饥饿的客人把食物吃光了。

His appetite was outeaten by the massive buffet. = 他被丰盛的自助餐吃垮了。(他的胃口被自助餐的巨大食量比下去了)


近义词：consumed, devoured, surpassed, outdone


反义词：leftover, remaining, untouched
