mateships:/ˈmeɪtʃɪps/| n. |友谊；伙伴关系；同志情谊|A close friendship or companionship, especially between men, often characterized by loyalty, mutual respect, and shared experiences.  It often implies a strong bond and a sense of belonging.|例句：The mateship between the two soldiers was evident in their unwavering support for each other.  这两名士兵之间的友谊在他们对彼此坚定不移的支持中显而易见。

例句：Their long and arduous journey forged a deep mateship between them. 漫长而艰辛的旅程在他们之间铸就了深厚的友谊。


近义词：friendship, camaraderie, companionship, bond|反义词：estrangement, animosity, antagonism|词性：名词
