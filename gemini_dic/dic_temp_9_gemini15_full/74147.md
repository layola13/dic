founders:/ˈfaʊndərz/| n. |创始人，建立者 |People who establish or create an organization, company, or institution.  They are responsible for the initial development and often play a significant role in its ongoing success. |The founders of Google were Larry Page and Sergey Brin. 谷歌的创始人分别是拉里·佩奇和谢尔盖·布林。|establishers, originators, initiators |destroyers, dismantlers
