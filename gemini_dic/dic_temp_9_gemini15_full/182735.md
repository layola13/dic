repaneling:/ˌriːˈpænlɪŋ/| n. |重新安装面板；更换面板 |The act of replacing or reinstalling panels, such as on a wall, door, or vehicle.  This often involves removing old panels and installing new ones. |Example: The kitchen requires extensive repaneling due to water damage. 厨房因水浸需要大规模更换面板。The old car needed repaneling in several areas after the accident. 事故后，旧车需要在几个区域重新安装面板。|近义词：relining, refitting, renovation |反义词：paneling (as a completed state)
