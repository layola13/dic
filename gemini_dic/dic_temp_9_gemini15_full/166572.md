prescribes:/prɪˈskraɪbz/| vt.|开处方；规定；指示；命令 |To order or recommend the use of a medicine, treatment, or course of action.|例句：The doctor prescribed antibiotics for her infection. 医生为她的感染开了抗生素。|例句：The rules prescribe that all members must attend. 规定所有成员都必须出席。|近义词：order, dictate, recommend |反义词：prohibit, forbid


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor prescribes medication for his patients. = 医生为他的病人开药。
She prescribes regular exercise to stay healthy. = 她规定定期锻炼以保持健康。


一般过去时:
The doctor prescribed me some painkillers. = 医生给我开了些止痛药。
He prescribed a strict diet for his client. = 他为他的客户制定了严格的饮食计划。


现在完成时:
The doctor has prescribed this medicine many times. = 这位医生已经多次开过这种药。
She has prescribed yoga as a stress-relief method. = 她已经将瑜伽作为一种减压方法推荐了。


过去完成时:
The doctor had prescribed the same medicine before. = 医生之前开过同样的药。
They had prescribed a course of action before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经制定了一套行动方案。


将来时:
The doctor will prescribe a stronger medication if necessary. = 如果需要，医生会开更强的药。
She will prescribe a detailed plan for the project. = 她将为该项目制定一个详细的计划。


虚拟语气:
If I were the doctor, I would prescribe a different treatment. = 如果我是医生，我会开不同的治疗方案。
If she had listened, she wouldn't have ignored the prescribed medication. = 如果她听了话，她就不会忽略规定的药物。


被动语态:
Medication was prescribed to the patient. = 药物被开给了病人。
A strict diet was prescribed by the nutritionist. = 营养师制定了严格的饮食计划。


疑问句:
What did the doctor prescribe for your condition? = 医生为你开的什么药？
What actions does the company prescribe for this situation? = 公司为这种情况规定了哪些行动？


否定句:
The doctor did not prescribe any medication. = 医生没有开任何药。
The regulations do not prescribe a specific procedure. = 该规章制度没有规定具体的程序。


PHRASE->
prescribe medication  开药
prescribe treatment 开具治疗方案
prescribe a course of action 制定行动方案
prescribe limits 设定限制
prescribe rules 制定规则
