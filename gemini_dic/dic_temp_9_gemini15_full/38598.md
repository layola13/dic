concourses:/ˈkɒŋkɔːrsɪz/ | n. |大厅；广场；汇合处 | A large open area, typically in a public building such as a train station or airport, where people can meet, gather, or wait.  | Example: The passengers waited in the concourse before boarding their flights.  旅客在候机大厅等候登机。The spacious concourse was filled with people. 宽敞的大厅里挤满了人。 |近义词：lobby, hall, atrium | 反义词：private room, secluded area
