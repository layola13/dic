propagulum:/ˈprɒpəɡjʊləm/| n. |繁殖体，繁殖单位|A propagulum is any part of a plant or animal that can detach and develop into a new individual.  This includes seeds, spores, buds, cuttings, tubers, runners, and other vegetative structures. It refers to a structure that is capable of independent growth and development into a new organism.|Example: The dandelion spreads easily because each seed head contains numerous propagules.|近义词: propagule, offspring, scion|反义词: N/A
