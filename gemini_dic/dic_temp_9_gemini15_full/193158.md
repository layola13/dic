sedated:/ˈsɛdeɪtɪd/| adj.|镇静的；平静的；被镇静剂麻醉的|Being calmed or soothed, often as a result of medication.  It can refer to a person's mental state or a physical state induced by drugs.|例句：The patient was sedated before the surgery.  手术前病人被镇静了。

The child, sedated by the medication, slept peacefully. 服药后，孩子平静地睡着了。|近义词：tranquilized, calmed, soothed, subdued|反义词：agitated, excited, stimulated|词性:形容词
