noninducible:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdjuːsɪbl/| adj. |不可诱导的，非诱导性的 |Not capable of being induced or stimulated.  This term is often used in biology and genetics to describe genes or processes that are not activated or increased in expression in response to a specific stimulus.  |Example: The noninducible nature of the gene made it difficult to study its regulation.  这个基因的不可诱导性使得对其调控的研究变得困难。 |近义词：uninducible, constitutive (in the context of gene expression)|反义词：inducible
