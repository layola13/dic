minisummits:/ˈmɪniˌsʌmɪts/| n. |小型峰会，迷你峰会 |A minisummit is a smaller, less formal meeting than a full summit, often focusing on a specific subset of issues or involving a smaller group of participants.  It might be held in preparation for a larger summit or as a follow-up to address specific action points. | Example: The two countries held a minisummit to discuss trade relations before the larger G20 summit.  两个国家在更大的G20峰会之前举行了一次小型峰会来讨论贸易关系。|近义词：small summit, informal meeting, preparatory meeting |反义词：full summit, major conference |名词
