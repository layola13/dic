encryptions:/ɪnˈkrɪpʃənz/ | n. |加密（行为或方法）|The process of converting information or data into a code, especially to prevent unauthorized access.  It involves transforming readable data into an unreadable format, known as ciphertext.  This process requires a decryption key to revert the ciphertext back to its original form.|Example:  The company uses strong encryptions to protect sensitive customer data. 这家公司使用强大的加密技术来保护敏感的客户数据。|近义词：codings, ciphers, encipherments |反义词：decryptions
