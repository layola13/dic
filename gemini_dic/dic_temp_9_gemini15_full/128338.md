neutral-zones: /ˈnjuːtrəl zoʊnz/ | n. | 中立区，非军事区 | An area where opposing forces agree not to fight, often established during a conflict or to separate conflicting parties.  It can also refer to areas of political or ideological neutrality. | Example: The demilitarized zone between North and South Korea is a well-known neutral zone.  朝鲜和韩国之间的非军事区是一个著名的中立区。  |近义词：buffer zone, demilitarized zone, neutral territory | 反义词：battlefield, combat zone |
