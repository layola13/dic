dmx:/dɛmˈɛks/ | n. | 数字化多路复用 | Digital Multiplexing, a communication protocol used for lighting control in entertainment venues. It allows for precise control of multiple lighting fixtures from a single console.  | Example: The stage lighting was controlled via DMX. 舞台灯光通过DMX控制。 |近义词: lighting control protocol | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑analog lighting control) | 名词
