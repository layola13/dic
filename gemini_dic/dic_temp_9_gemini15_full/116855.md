maximized:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzd/| v.|最大化；使最大限度地发挥作用|To increase or make as great as possible.  To make the most of something.|例句：The company maximized its profits by cutting costs. 公司通过削减成本使利润最大化。

例句：We need to maximize efficiency in the workplace. 我们需要最大限度地提高工作效率。


近义词：optimize, amplify, enhance

反义词：minimize, reduce, lessen
