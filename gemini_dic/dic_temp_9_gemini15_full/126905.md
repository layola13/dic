narcotically:/nɑːrˈkɒtɪkli/| adj. |麻醉地；麻醉性地；昏睡地 |Having the characteristics of or resembling a narcotic; causing or characterized by stupor or lethargy.  It describes a state induced by narcotics, characterized by drowsiness, insensibility, or a lack of responsiveness.|The patient was narcotically drowsy after the surgery. 手术后病人处于麻醉性昏睡状态。 The music had a narcotically soothing effect. 这音乐有令人昏昏欲睡的舒缓效果。|近义词：drowsily, lethargically, stuporously |反义词：alertly, keenly, vibrantly
