preventive:/prɪˈvɛntɪv/| adj.|预防性的，预防的|Relating to or characterized by the prevention of something, especially disease.|例句：Preventive measures were taken to avoid the spread of the virus.  采取了预防措施以避免病毒传播。|近义词：prophylactic, precautionary, anticipatory|反义词：curative, remedial
