pedestrianate:/pəˈdɛstrɪəneɪt/| vt.|使……行人化；使……适合步行；使……变得平淡无奇|To make something pedestrian or commonplace; to deprive something of its excitement or interest.|例句：The new design pedestrianated the once-vibrant city square, making it less appealing to visitors.  新的设计使曾经充满活力的城市广场变得平淡无奇，使其对游客的吸引力降低了。|例句：They pedestrianated the route by adding more sidewalks and crosswalks. 他们通过增加更多的人行道和人行横道使路线更适合步行。|近义词：banalize, trivialize,  commonplace|反义词：excite, enliven, invigorate|动词
