prescriptibility:/prɪˌskrɪptəˈbɪləti/| n. |可开处方性；可规定性|The capacity or quality of being able to be prescribed; the state of being subject to prescription or regulation.  This term often refers to the legal or medical ability to prescribe a medication or treatment.  It can also refer to the possibility of something being prescribed or stipulated.|The prescriptibility of this new drug is still under review by the FDA.|近义词：prescribability, regulation |反义词：unprescribability, illegality
