inhibitory: /ɪnˈhɪbɪtəri/ | adj. | 抑制性的，禁止的 | Having the power or function of inhibiting or restraining.  Something that is inhibitory prevents or slows down a process or action. |


例句：

The drug has inhibitory effects on the growth of cancer cells.  这种药物对癌细胞的生长有抑制作用。

The teacher's stern gaze had an inhibitory effect on the noisy students. 老师严厉的目光对吵闹的学生起到了抑制作用。


近义词：restrictive, suppressive, repressive, deterrent


反义词：stimulatory, excitatory, promoting


词源:  From inhibit + -ory.  "Inhibit" comes from Latin *inhibere, from in- "in, into" + habēre "to have, hold".  The suffix "-ory" indicates something that has the function or quality of.
