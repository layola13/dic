dataset:/ˈdeɪtəsɛt/ | n. |数据集 |A dataset is a collection of data, often organized in a tabular format with rows and columns. It can be used for analysis, machine learning, or other data processing tasks.  | Example: The researchers analyzed a large dataset of customer purchases.  研究人员分析了一个大型的客户购买数据集。 |近义词：data collection, data sample |反义词：N/A
