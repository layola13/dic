hereabouts:/ˈhɪrəbaʊts/| adv.|附近，大约在这个地方 |This word indicates a location that is near the speaker's current location or a time that is approximately the present time.  It is often used informally. |例句：He lives hereabouts. 他住在附近。

例句：The accident happened hereabouts last week. 事故大约发生在上周这个地方附近。


近义词：nearby, around here, in this vicinity, thereabouts

反义词：afar, far away, distant


词性:副词
