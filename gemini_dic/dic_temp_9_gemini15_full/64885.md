essaying:/ˈeseɪɪŋ/| v.|尝试；试图；写作；阐述|Attempting or trying to do something; writing or composing an essay;  explaining or describing something.|例句：He is essaying a new role in the play. 他正在尝试话剧中的一个新角色。

例句：She is essaying a complex argument in her paper.  她在论文中阐述了一个复杂的论点。


近义词：trying, attempting, endeavoring, writing, expounding


反义词：neglecting, ignoring, avoiding


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is essaying a new painting technique. = 他正在尝试一种新的绘画技巧。
She essays a new role every year. = 她每年都尝试一个新的角色。

一般过去时:
They essayed a daring escape. = 他们试图进行一次大胆的逃脱。
He essayed a humorous tone in his speech. = 他在演讲中采用了幽默的语气。

现在完成时:
She has essayed many different styles of writing. = 她尝试过许多不同的写作风格。
He has essayed to explain the complex theory. = 他试图解释这个复杂的理论。

过去完成时:
They had essayed several solutions before finding the right one. = 他们尝试过几种解决方案才找到正确的。
She had essayed a career in acting before becoming a writer. = 在成为作家之前，她曾尝试过演艺事业。

将来时:

He will essay a detailed description of the event. = 他将对事件进行详细的描述。

虚拟语气:
If he essayed a different strategy, he might succeed. = 如果他尝试不同的策略，他可能会成功。
I wish she had essayed a more persuasive argument. = 我希望她能提出更有说服力的论点。

被动语态:

否定句:
