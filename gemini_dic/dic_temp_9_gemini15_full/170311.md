proverbially:/prɒˈvɜːbɪəli/| adj.|谚语般地；众所周知地；习惯性地|  It means something is widely known or commonly said, often referring to a proverb or saying. It suggests a common experience or truth.|例句：He is proverbially slow. 他出了名的慢。

例句：The task proved proverbially difficult. 这项任务正如俗话所说那样难。


近义词：commonly, famously, notoriously, typically, usually

反义词：unusually, exceptionally, rarely


词性: adjective
