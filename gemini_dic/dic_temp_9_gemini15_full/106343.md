koi:/kɔɪ/| n.|锦鲤，鲤鱼|A type of ornamental carp, often bred for its vibrant colors and patterns.  Koi are popular in ponds and water gardens.|例句：He has a beautiful pond filled with koi. 他有一个装满锦鲤的漂亮池塘。|例句：The koi swam gracefully in the clear water. 锦鲤优雅地在清澈的水中游动。|近义词：carp (鲤鱼), goldfish (金鱼)|反义词:  N/A
