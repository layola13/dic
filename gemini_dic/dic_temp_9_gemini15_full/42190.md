It is defined as the amount of charge that passes a given point in a circuit when a current of one ampere flows for one second. | Example: The capacitor stores a charge of 10 coulombs.  这个电容器储存了10库仑的电荷。|近义词:None (as it's a specific unit)|反义词:None (as it's a unit, not a concept with opposites)|名词
