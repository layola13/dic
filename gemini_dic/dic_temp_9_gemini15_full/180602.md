registries:/ˈrɛdʒɪstriːz/| n.|注册表；登记簿；注册处|A registry is an official record of something, such as births, deaths, marriages, or property ownership.  It can also refer to a database or system for storing and managing information.  In computing, a registry is a central database that stores configuration settings and other information for the operating system and applications.|例句：The hospital maintains detailed registries of all patients. 医院保存所有病人的详细登记簿。

The national registry of births and deaths is an important resource for researchers. 全国出生和死亡登记处是研究人员的重要资源。
