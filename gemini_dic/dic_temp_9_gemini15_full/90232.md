house:/haʊs/| n.|房子，住宅|A building for human habitation, especially one that consists of a ground floor and one or more upper storeys.|例句：We bought a new house last year. 我们去年买了一栋新房子。|例句：The house is painted white. 这房子漆成白色的。|近义词：home, dwelling, residence|反义词：outdoors


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The house stands on a hill. = 这房子坐落在山上。
They build houses for a living. = 他们以盖房子为生。

一般过去时:
We visited an old house in the countryside. = 我们参观了乡下的一座老房子。
He built his house with his own hands. = 他亲手建造了他的房子。

现在完成时:
They have just bought a new house. = 他们刚刚买了一栋新房子。
We have lived in this house for ten years. = 我们在这栋房子里住了十年了。

过去完成时:
They had already sold the house before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他们已经卖掉了房子。
She had lived in that house for twenty years before moving. = 她搬家之前在那栋房子里住了二十年。

将来时:
We will build a new house next year. = 我们明年将建造一栋新房子。
They will move into their new house next month. = 他们下个月将搬进他们的新房子。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would buy a bigger house. = 如果我有钱，我会买一栋更大的房子。
I wish I had a house by the sea. = 我希望我有一栋海边的房子。

被动语态:
The house was built in 1950. = 这房子建于1950年。
The houses are being renovated. = 这些房子正在被翻新。

疑问句:
Is this your house? = 这是你的房子吗？
Whose house is that? = 那是什么人的房子？

否定句:
That's not my house. = 那不是我的房子。
They don't own a house yet. = 他们还没拥有房子。
