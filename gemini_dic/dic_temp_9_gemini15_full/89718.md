hopefulness:/ˈhoʊpfəl.nəs/ | n. |希望；乐观；有希望 |The feeling or state of being hopeful; optimism.  It often involves a positive expectation about the future. |例句：Her hopefulness was infectious, spreading to everyone around her.  她的乐观感染了周围所有人。

例句：Despite the challenges, a sense of hopefulness remained. 尽管面临挑战，仍然存在希望。


近义词：optimism, expectancy, confidence, encouragement |反义词：despair, pessimism, hopelessness, dejection |词性：名词
