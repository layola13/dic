screens:/skriːnz/ | n. |屏幕；遮蔽物；筛选；检查 | A flat surface on which images are shown, especially one forming part of a computer or television.  A barrier or partition that shields something. The process of examining something carefully to separate good parts from bad parts. |例句：The screens in the cinema were huge. 电影院里的屏幕很大。 The trees formed a green screen against the sun. 树木形成了一道绿色的屏障遮挡阳光。  They screen applicants carefully before hiring them. 他们在雇用之前会仔细筛选申请人。 |近义词：displays, monitors, filters, sieves |反义词：openings, exposures
