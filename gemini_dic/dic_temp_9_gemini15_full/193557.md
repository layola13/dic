self-affirmations:/ˌsɛlfˌæfərˈmeɪʃənz/| n. |自我肯定，积极肯定句 |Positive statements repeated to oneself to build self-esteem and confidence.  Self-affirmations are used to counteract negative self-talk and promote a more positive self-image. They can focus on various aspects of one's life, such as personal qualities, achievements, or goals. | Example: "I am capable," "I am strong," and "I am worthy of love and happiness" are examples of self-affirmations.  I use positive self-affirmations every morning to boost my confidence before work. 我每天早上都使用积极的自我肯定来增强工作前的信心。|近义词：positive self-talk, self-encouragement |反义词：negative self-talk, self-criticism
