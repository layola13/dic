dunam:/ˈduːnæm/| n.| 公顷；顿南（面积单位）|A dunam is a unit of area, primarily used in Israel and other parts of the Middle East.  It is equal to 1000 square meters, or approximately 0.247 acres.|例句：The farmer owns a dunam of land planted with olive trees.  这位农民拥有种植橄榄树的一公顷土地。The new building will cover at least five dunams. 新建筑至少将覆盖五公顷土地。|近义词：hectare, acre|反义词：无
