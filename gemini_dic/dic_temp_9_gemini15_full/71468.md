flamers: /ˈfleɪmər/ | n. | 恶意攻击者；煽动者 | A person who posts inflammatory or abusive messages online, especially in online forums or games.  | Example: The online forum was overrun with flamers who attacked anyone with a differing opinion.  在线论坛充斥着恶意攻击者，他们攻击任何持有不同意见的人。 |近义词：troll, cyberbully, hater |反义词：peacemaker, mediator


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flamers other users constantly. = 他不断地恶意攻击其他用户。
She often flamers people on social media. = 她经常在社交媒体上恶意攻击他人。

一般过去时:
They flamed the newcomer mercilessly. = 他们无情地攻击了新手。
He flamed her for her political views. = 他因为她的政治观点而攻击她。

现在完成时:
They have flamed many people in this forum. = 他们在该论坛上已经攻击了许多人。
She has been flamed repeatedly for her comments. = 她因为自己的评论而被反复攻击。

过去完成时:
They had flamed each other before the moderator intervened. = 在版主介入之前，他们就已经互相攻击了。
He had flamed several users before his account was banned. = 在他的账户被封禁之前，他已经攻击了几位用户。

将来时:

She will likely flame him for his controversial post. = 她很可能会因为他的争议性帖子而攻击他。

虚拟语气:
If he flamed me again, I would report him. = 如果他再次攻击我，我会举报他。
I wish he hadn't flamed her so harshly. = 我希望他不要对她如此严厉地攻击。
