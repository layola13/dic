dispatches:/ˈdɪspætʃɪz/| n. |新闻报道；急件；派遣；|The act of sending someone or something somewhere, or something that is sent.  It can also refer to news reports, often sent quickly.|例句：The newspaper published dispatches from the war zone.  报纸刊登了来自战区的新闻报道。|例句：The general sent a dispatch ordering the troops to advance.将军发出了命令部队前进的急件。|近义词：reports, messages, dispatches, dispatches, transmissions|反义词：receipts, arrivals

He dispatches the letters every day. = 他每天都寄信。
She dispatches her work efficiently. = 她有效率地完成工作。
