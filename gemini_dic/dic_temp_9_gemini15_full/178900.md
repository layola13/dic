redbush:/ˈrɛdˌbʊʃ/| n.|南非红灌木茶，红茶树|A type of tea made from the leaves of the *Aspalathus linearis* plant, native to South Africa. It is naturally caffeine-free and has a sweet, slightly earthy flavor.|Example: I prefer redbush tea to black tea because it's caffeine-free.  我喜欢红灌木茶胜过红茶，因为它不含咖啡因。|近义词:rooibos tea|反义词: black tea, caffeinated tea|名词
