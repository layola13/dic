choo-chooed:/tʃuːtʃuːd/| v.|模仿火车的声音；发出“ chug-chug ”声|To make the sound of a train.  It is an onomatopoeic verb, imitating the sound of a steam train.|例句：The little boy choo-chooed as he pushed his toy train across the floor. 小男孩推着他的玩具火车在地板上跑，发出“ chug-chug ”的声音。|例句：The old steam engine choo-chooed loudly as it pulled into the station.  老式蒸汽机车进站时发出巨大的“ chug-chug ”声。|近义词：chugged, puffed|反义词：None.
