quasi-financial:/ˈkwɑːzi faɪˈnænʃəl/| adj.|准金融的，似金融的|Seeming or being almost but not quite financial;  resembling or having some characteristics of financial activities but lacking full financial attributes.|The company engaged in quasi-financial activities, such as factoring and leasing, in addition to its core business.|近义词：parafiscal, semi-financial|反义词：purely financial
