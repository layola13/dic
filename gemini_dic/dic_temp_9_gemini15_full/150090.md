paludina:/pæljuˈdaɪnə/| n. |河螺；椎实螺属 | A genus of freshwater snails, belonging to the family Viviparidae.  They are typically found in slow-moving or still waters and have a distinctive spiral shell. |Example:  The pond was teeming with paludinas of various sizes. 池塘里挤满了各种大小的河螺。  |  近义词：Viviparus (同科不同属的螺类)| 反义词：无
