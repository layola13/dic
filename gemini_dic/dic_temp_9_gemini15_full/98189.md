inseams:/ˈɪnsiːmz/| n.|内缝，裤裆|The inseam is the length of the inside leg of a garment, measured from the crotch to the bottom hem.  It's a crucial measurement for proper fit.|The inseam of my jeans is too short. 我的牛仔裤内缝太短了。|The inseam of these trousers is perfect. 这条裤子的内缝很合身。|hemline, leg length|outseam
