cracker:/ˈkrækər/| n. |饼干；爆竹；(俚)高手；破解者 |A cracker is a thin, crisp biscuit or cookie.  It can also refer to a firework that makes a cracking sound,  a skilled person (often in a computer context), or someone who breaks into computer systems.|例句：She enjoyed a cracker with her tea. 她用茶点心吃了一块饼干。|例句：The children loved setting off crackers on Guy Fawkes Night.孩子们喜欢在盖伊·福克斯之夜燃放爆竹。|近义词：biscuit, cookie, firework, hacker|反义词： (根据语境而定，没有绝对的反义词)
