oaf:/oʊf/ | n. |笨拙的人，粗鲁的人 |A clumsy, insensitive, or stupid person.  |He's such an oaf; he tripped over the rug and spilled his drink. 他真是个笨蛋，他绊倒了地毯，把饮料洒了。|clod, lout, boor |refined person, gentle person|名词

That oaf keeps bothering me. = 那个笨蛋一直烦我。
