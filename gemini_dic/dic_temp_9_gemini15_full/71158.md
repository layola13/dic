fission:/ˈfɪʒən/| n. |裂变；分裂；分割|The splitting of an atomic nucleus into two or more fragments, releasing a large amount of energy.  Nuclear fission is used in nuclear power plants. |例句：Nuclear fission releases enormous amounts of energy. 核裂变释放出巨大的能量。|例句：The cell undergoes fission to reproduce. 细胞进行裂变以繁殖。|近义词：splitting, division, cleavage |反义词：fusion
