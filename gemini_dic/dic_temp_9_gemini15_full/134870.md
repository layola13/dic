nonmonist: /ˈnɒnmɒnɪst/ | adj. | 非一元论者；非一元论的 |  Relating to or characterized by nonmonism; denying the ultimate reality of a single principle or substance.  A nonmonist believes that reality is composed of multiple fundamental substances or principles, rather than a single unifying one. | Example:  The philosopher presented a nonmonist view of consciousness, arguing that it arises from the interaction of multiple brain regions.  The theory of nonmonist causality is a complex one. | 近义词: pluralist, dualist,  | 反义词: monist
