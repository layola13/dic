rilievo:/rɪˈljɛvoʊ/| n. |浮雕；凸雕| Relief sculpture; the projection of figures from a background.  The term describes the projection of sculpted figures from a background surface.  It can range from low relief (bas-relief), where figures project slightly, to high relief (alto-relievo), where figures project significantly. |例句：The ancient Roman sarcophagus was adorned with intricate rilievo depicting scenes of mythology. 古罗马石棺上装饰着描绘神话场景的复杂浮雕。

The church's facade featured a stunning rilievo of the Virgin Mary. 教堂的正面装饰着圣母玛利亚的精美浮雕。


近义词：relief, embossing |反义词：intaglio (凹雕) |词性：名词
