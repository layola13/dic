gallopers:/ˈɡæləpərz/| n. |疾驰的人；快马；|快速奔跑的马或骑手。  This word refers to people or horses moving at a gallop.  |例句：The galloping horses were a magnificent sight. 奔腾的骏马是一道壮丽的景象。
The children pretended to be galloping knights. 孩子们假装自己是疾驰的骑士。|近义词：riders, racers, steeds|反义词：walkers, creepers
