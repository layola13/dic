derogately:/dɪˈrɒɡətli/ | adv. |轻蔑地；贬低地；毁谤地 |  To speak or act in a derogatory manner; to belittle or disparage.  It implies a disrespectful and contemptuous attitude towards someone or something. | Example: He spoke derogately about his opponent's qualifications. 他轻蔑地谈论了他对手的资格。|近义词：disparagingly, contemptuously, scornfully, disdainfully | 反义词：respectfully, admiringly, approvingly
