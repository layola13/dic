repercussively:/ˌriːpərˈkʌsɪvli/ | adv. | 反响地；间接地；连锁反应地 |  In a way that produces repercussions; indirectly; consequentially.  The word describes actions or events that have widespread and often unforeseen consequences, spreading outwards like ripples in a pond. | Example: The initial economic downturn repercussively affected numerous industries, leading to widespread unemployment.  最初的经济衰退间接地影响了许多行业，导致了大范围的失业。|近义词：indirectly, consequentially, secondarily | 反义词：directly, immediately
