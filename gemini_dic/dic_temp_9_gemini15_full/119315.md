microbenthos:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈbɛnθɒs/| n. |底栖微生物|Microscopic organisms living on or in the bottom sediments of aquatic environments.  They play a crucial role in nutrient cycling and decomposition. |Example: The study investigated the diversity of microbenthos in the coastal sediments. 这项研究调查了沿海沉积物中底栖微生物的多样性。|近义词：benthic microorganism, bottom-dwelling microorganism |反义词：plankton (浮游生物)|名词
