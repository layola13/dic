openability: /ˌoʊpənəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可开启性，易开启程度 | The capacity or ease with which something can be opened.  This refers to the degree to which something is easily opened,  considering factors like design, mechanism, and resistance.  | 例句：The openability of the new packaging was significantly improved after the redesign. 新包装经过重新设计后，其可开启性得到了显著提高。

The poor openability of the jar frustrated many customers.  这罐子的开启困难让许多顾客感到沮丧。


近义词：accessibility, ease of opening | 反义词：difficulty of opening, inaccessibility
