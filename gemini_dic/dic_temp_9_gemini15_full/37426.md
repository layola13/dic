commigration:/ˌkɒmɪˈɡreɪʃən/ | /ˌkɑːmɪˈɡreɪʃən/ | n. |共同迁移；集体迁移 | The movement of a group of people or animals from one region to another, often for a specific purpose, such as finding better resources or escaping adverse conditions.  | Example: The commigration of birds to warmer climates in the winter is a remarkable natural phenomenon. 冬季鸟类向温暖气候地区的共同迁移是一种非凡的自然现象。 |近义词：migration, movement, relocation |反义词：immigration (in the context of people moving into a region), settlement (in the context of establishing a permanent residence)
