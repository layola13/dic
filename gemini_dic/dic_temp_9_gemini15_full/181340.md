reintroduction:/ˌriːˌɪntrəˈdʌkʃən/| n. |重新引入；再介绍；复兴|The act of introducing something again; the process of bringing a species back from the brink of extinction or into a new area.  It can also refer to reintroducing a person or topic. |例句：The reintroduction of wolves to Yellowstone National Park had a significant impact on the ecosystem.  黄石国家公园重新引入狼对生态系统产生了重大影响。

The professor's reintroduction of the topic clarified any confusion. 教授对主题的重新介绍澄清了任何混淆。|近义词：reinstatement, restoration, re-establishment|反义词：removal, elimination, extinction|名词
