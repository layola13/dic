cubicle:/ˈkjuːbɪkl/| n. |小隔间，小房间 |A cubicle is a small, enclosed area, often partitioned off from a larger space, typically used as a workspace or office.  It provides privacy and separation for individuals within a shared environment. |例句：He works in a tiny cubicle in a large office building. 他在一个大型办公楼里狭小的隔间工作。  |例句：The library offers study cubicles for students needing quiet space. 图书馆为需要安静空间的学生提供学习隔间。|近义词：compartment, stall, booth |反义词：open space, large room
