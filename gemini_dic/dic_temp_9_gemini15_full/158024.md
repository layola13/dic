planted:/ˈplæntɪd/ | vt. |种植；栽种；安置；埋藏 |To put (a seed, plant, etc.) in the ground to grow. To place or set firmly.  |例句：The farmer planted corn in the field.  农民在田里种玉米。

He planted a bomb under the car. 他在车底下埋了一颗炸弹。|近义词：sowed, set, embedded |反义词：uprooted, removed, harvested


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They plant trees every year. = 他们每年都种树。
She plants flowers in her garden. = 她在她花园里种花。

一般过去时:
We planted a new rose bush yesterday. = 我们昨天种了一棵新的玫瑰树。
He planted the seeds carefully. = 他小心翼翼地播种。

现在完成时:
They have planted hundreds of trees this year. = 他们今年已经种了几百棵树。
She has planted a vegetable garden. = 她已经种了一个菜园。

过去完成时:
They had planted the crops before the rain started. = 雨开始下之前他们已经种好了庄稼。
We had planted the seeds a week before they sprouted. = 我们在一周前播种，种子才发芽。

将来时:
I will plant tomatoes in my garden next spring. = 我明年春天会在我的花园里种西红柿。
They will plant more trees in the park. = 他们将在公园里种更多的树。

虚拟语气:
If I had planted the seeds earlier, they would have grown better. = 如果我早点播种，它们会长得更好。
I wish I had planted more flowers. = 我希望我种更多的花。

被动语态:
The trees were planted by the volunteers. = 这些树是由志愿者种植的。
The bomb was planted near the building. = 炸弹被安放在建筑物附近。

疑问句:
When did they plant the flowers? = 他们什么时候种的花？
Have you planted your seeds yet? = 你播种了吗？


否定句:
He didn't plant anything in the garden this year. = 他今年没在花园里种任何东西。
They haven't planted the trees yet. = 他们还没种树。


PHRASE->
planted a seed = 播下种子 (比喻意义：埋下想法)
planted evidence = 栽赃证据
planted a kiss = 亲吻
deeply planted = 根深蒂固的
planted firmly = 牢牢地栽种
