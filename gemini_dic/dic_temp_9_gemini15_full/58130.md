dropt:/drɒpt/| /drɑːpt/ | v. |滴下；落下；放弃；终止|To fall or let something fall in drops; to let something fall; to discontinue or abandon something. |例句：The rain dropt from the eaves. 雨水从屋檐滴落。  The price of oil dropt sharply. 油价暴跌。|近义词：fall, drop, plunge, relinquish, abandon |反义词：raise, lift, retain, continue


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drops his keys every day. = 他每天都把钥匙掉在地上。
The temperature drops significantly at night. = 夜晚气温显著下降。

一般过去时:
She dropped the glass and it broke. = 她把玻璃杯掉了，摔碎了。
He dropped out of college last year. = 他去年退学了。

现在完成时:
The company has dropped its prices. = 公司已经降价了。
I have dropped my plans to travel abroad. = 我已经放弃了出国旅行的计划。


过去完成时:
They had dropped the ball before the game started. = 比赛开始前他们已经失误了。
She had dropped her studies to take care of her family. = 为了照顾家人，她已经放弃了学业。


将来时:
The government will drop the new tax plan. = 政府将放弃新的税收计划。
I will drop you off at the station. = 我会在车站把你放下。


虚拟语气:
If I dropped the course, I would have more free time. = 如果我放弃这门课，我就会有更多空闲时间。
I wish I had dropped that project earlier. = 我真希望我早点放弃那个项目。


被动语态:
The ball was dropped by the player. = 球被球员掉了。
The case was dropped by the police. = 警方放弃了此案。


疑问句:
Did you drop the package? = 你把包裹弄掉了吗？
Will they drop the charges? = 他们会撤销指控吗？


否定句:
He didn't drop the subject during the meeting. = 他在会议上没有放弃这个话题。
The rain didn't drop all day. = 一整天都没有下雨。



PHRASE->
drop by:顺便拜访
drop off:放下；让…下车
drop out:退出；辍学
drop in:顺便拜访
let it drop:别再提了
