dressmaker:/ˈdrɛsmˌeɪkər/| n. |女装裁缝，女裁缝师 |A dressmaker is a person, typically a woman, who makes or alters dresses and other garments, often custom-made for clients.  They typically design, sew, and fit clothing. |The dressmaker created a stunning gown for the wedding.| seamstress, tailor |  (反义词无直接对应词，可根据语境使用如: factory worker, mass producer)| 名词
