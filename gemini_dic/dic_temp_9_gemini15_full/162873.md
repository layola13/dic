precedes:/prɪˈsiːdz/| v. |领先，在…之前，先于|To come or exist before something else in time or order.|The introduction precedes the main body of the text.  引言在正文之前。|The accident preceded his resignation. 事故发生在他辞职之前。|follows, antecedes |succeeds|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The appetizer precedes the main course. 前菜在主菜之前上。
Success precedes failure. 成功先于失败。


一般过去时:
The storm preceded the flood. 暴风雨发生在洪水之前。
The announcement preceded the actual event. 公告先于实际事件发生。


现在完成时:
Many challenges have preceded this victory.  许多挑战先于这场胜利。
No significant progress has preceded this breakthrough.  在这项突破之前没有取得任何重大进展。


过去完成时:
He had preceded me to the meeting. 他在我之前到达了会议现场。
The rain had preceded the arrival of the storm. 雨水先于暴风雨到来。


将来时:
The concert will precede the fireworks display. 音乐会将在烟火表演之前举行。
The meeting will precede the dinner. 会议将在晚餐之前举行。


虚拟语气:
If only success preceded failure. 如果成功总是在失败之前就好了。
If the warning had preceded the accident, it might have been avoided. 如果警告先于事故发生，事故或许可以避免。


被动语态:
The speech was preceded by a moment of silence. 演讲之前有一刻的沉默。
The parade was preceded by a military band.  游行队伍前面是由一支军乐队开道。


疑问句:
What preceded the big bang? 大爆炸之前是什么？
Does the introduction precede the conclusion?  引言在结论之前吗？


否定句:
The result did not precede the effort. 结果并非在努力之前。
The conclusion did not precede the evidence. 结论并非在证据之前。
