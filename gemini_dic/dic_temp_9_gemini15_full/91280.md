hydrographic:/ˌhaɪdrəˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|水文的，水道的|Relating to the surveying and charting of bodies of water, such as oceans, lakes, and rivers, including their depths, currents, and other physical features.|The hydrographic survey revealed a previously unknown underwater canyon. 水文测量揭示了一个以前未知的水下峡谷。|近义词：hydrological, nautical|反义词:terrestrial
