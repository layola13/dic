haunches: /ˈhɔːntʃɪz/ | n. | 臀部；后腿 | The fleshy part of an animal's hindquarters, especially the part between the hips and thighs.  It can also refer to the corresponding part of the human body. |例句：The lion crouched low to the ground, its haunches tensed.  狮子伏低身子，臀部绷紧。

The dog rested its haunches against the tree. 这只狗把臀部靠在树上。


近义词：hindquarters, buttocks, rump | 反义词：forequarters


词源:  From Old French hanche, from Vulgar Latin *hanca ("hip").  Ultimately related to Latin anca ("hip").
