lightweights:/ˈlaɪtweɪts/| n.|轻量级选手；轻量级的东西；能力不足的人|A lightweight is someone or something that is not strong or important.  It can refer to a boxer in a lower weight class, something that is not heavy, or someone lacking in strength, skill, or importance.|例句：He's a lightweight in the boxing world. 他是拳击界的一个轻量级选手。

例句：The new software is a lightweight application, requiring minimal system resources. 这款新软件是一个轻量级应用程序，只需要最少的系统资源。

近义词：weakling, novice, amateur, insignificant

反义词：heavyweight, strongman, expert, significant

词性:名词
