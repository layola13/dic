childish:/ˈtʃaɪldɪʃ/| adj.|幼稚的，孩子气的|Characterized by or appropriate to a child; immature.  It can refer to behavior, thoughts, or objects.  The term often carries a negative connotation, implying a lack of maturity or responsibility.|例句：His childish behavior at the meeting embarrassed everyone. 他在会议上的幼稚行为让每个人都尴尬。

例句：That's a childish excuse; you should take responsibility for your actions. 那是幼稚的借口；你应该对你的行为负责。


近义词：immature, infantile, puerile, juvenile|反义词：mature, adult, grown-up|词性:形容词
