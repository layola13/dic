codependent:/ˌkoʊdɪˈpɛndənt/ | adj. | 互相依赖的；共生的；依赖成瘾的 |  Characterized by or exhibiting codependency; relating to or characterized by a relationship in which one person is controlled or manipulated by another who is addicted to substances or exhibits other dysfunctional behaviors.  Codependency often involves a person neglecting their own needs to care for someone else's, leading to a variety of emotional and psychological issues. | Example:  Their relationship was clearly codependent; she constantly excused his bad behavior and ignored her own needs.  = 他们的关系显然是互相依赖的；她总是为他的不良行为找借口，并且忽略了自己的需求。| 近义词: interdependent, mutually dependent, reliant | 反义词: independent, self-reliant
