nurser:/ˈnɜːrsər/ | n. |托儿所；育婴室；护士；保育员| A place where babies and young children are cared for while their parents are at work or otherwise occupied;  a person who cares for babies and young children.  |例句：She works as a nurser in a daycare center. 她在一个日托中心当保育员。|例句：The hospital has a special nurser for premature babies. 这家医院有一个专门为早产儿服务的育婴室。|近义词：daycare, creche, nursery, childminder |反义词：N/A
