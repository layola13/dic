gorgeously:/ˈɡɔːrdʒəsli/| adj.|华丽地；漂亮地；极好地|Beautifully; splendidly; in a magnificent manner.  The word implies a high degree of beauty or splendor.|例句：The ballroom was gorgeously decorated for the wedding.  婚礼的舞厅装饰得华丽无比。

例句：She was gorgeously dressed in a silk gown. 她穿着丝绸长袍，打扮得非常漂亮。|近义词：splendidly, magnificently, beautifully, exquisitely|反义词：plainly, poorly, simply, meagerly

词源:
