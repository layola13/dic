realms: /relmz/ | n. |领域，王国，范围 | A realm is a kingdom or a particular area of activity, interest, or knowledge.  It can refer to a specific geographical region ruled by a monarch, or a metaphorical area of experience or expertise.  | 例句：The novel explores the realms of fantasy and science fiction. 这部小说探索了奇幻和科幻的领域。

The discovery opened up new realms of scientific inquiry.  这一发现开辟了科学研究的新领域。|近义词：kingdom, domain, sphere, field, area |反义词：point, specific, single  |词性：名词
