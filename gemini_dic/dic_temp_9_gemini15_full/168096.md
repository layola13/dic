prizeman:/ˈpraɪzmən/| n. |获奖者，得奖人 | A prizeman is someone who has won a prize, especially in a competition or examination.  | Example: He was a prizeman at Oxford. 他是牛津大学的获奖者。 |近义词：winner, laureate |反义词：loser
