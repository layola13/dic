pharisaical:/ˌfærɪˈseɪɪkəl/| adj. |伪善的；假装虔诚的 |  Characterized by hypocrisy; outwardly pious but inwardly wicked.  It refers to the behavior of the Pharisees, a Jewish sect known for their strict adherence to religious law but also for their perceived hypocrisy. |例句：His pharisaical pronouncements on morality were undermined by his own scandalous behavior. 他的关于道德的伪善言论被他自己的丑闻行为所破坏。


例句：The pharisaical nature of his charity work became apparent when it was revealed he was using the money for personal gain. 他慈善工作的伪善本质在他被揭露将钱用于个人利益时变得明显。


近义词：hypocritical, sanctimonious, self-righteous, pietistic


反义词：sincere, genuine, honest, humble


词源:  Derived from "Pharisee," a member of an ancient Jewish sect known for their strict observance of religious law.  The term acquired its negative connotation due to their perceived hypocrisy.
