deergrass:/ˈdɪrˌɡræs/| n.|鹿草|A type of grass, often found in wetland areas, that is a preferred food source for deer.  It is characterized by its height, dense growth, and nutritious qualities for herbivores. |Example: The deer grazed contentedly in the meadow, their favorite deergrass swaying gently in the breeze.  鹿在草地上心满意足地吃草，它们最喜欢的鹿草在微风中轻轻摇曳。|近义词：wild grass, forage, browse|反义词：无
