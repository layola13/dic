media's:/ˈmiːdiəz/| n.|媒体的，大众传播媒介的|The possessive form of "media," referring to something belonging to or associated with the media.  It indicates ownership, origin, or relationship. |例句：The media's coverage of the election was biased. 媒体对这次选举的报道有偏见。|例句：The media's influence on public opinion is undeniable. 媒体对公众舆论的影响是不可否认的。|近义词：press', 'news outlets', 'broadcasting'|反义词：None specifically, but words implying absence of media influence could be considered antonyms in context.|名词性物主代词
