last:/lɑːst/| v.|持续；维持|To continue for a specified period of time.|The meeting lasted for two hours.|会议持续了两个小时。|continue, endure, persist|end, finish|verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The meeting lasts two hours. = 会议持续两个小时。
This offer lasts until the end of the month. = 此优惠有效期至月底。


一般过去时:
The movie lasted three hours. = 电影持续了三个小时。
The rain lasted all day. = 雨下了一整天。


现在完成时:
The project has lasted longer than expected. = 项目持续的时间比预期的长。
How long has this pain lasted? = 这种疼痛持续了多久？


过去完成时:
The drought had lasted for months before the rain came. = 在下雨之前，干旱已经持续了好几个月。
He had lasted only a few rounds in the boxing match. = 他在拳击比赛中只坚持了几轮。


将来时:
The concert will last for about two and a half hours. = 音乐会将持续大约两个半小时。
I hope the good weather will last. = 我希望好天气能持续下去。


虚拟语气:
If the power lasted longer, we could have finished the work. = 如果电力持续时间更长，我们就能完成工作了。
I wish the happiness lasted forever. = 我希望幸福永远持续下去。


被动语态:
The meeting was expected to last for an hour. = 预计会议将持续一小时。
The supplies were expected to last until the end of the month. = 预计物资可以持续到月底。


疑问句:
How long did the game last? = 比赛持续了多久？
How long will the battery last? = 电池能用多久？


否定句:
The performance didn't last very long. = 表演持续的时间不长。
The food didn't last the whole week. = 食物没有持续一整周。
