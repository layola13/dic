deal:/diːl/| n., v.|协议；交易；份额；处理；给予；分配|An agreement or arrangement between people or groups, especially in business; to give, distribute, or hand out something; to cope with or manage a difficult situation.|例句：We made a deal to buy the house. 我们达成协议买下这栋房子。  They dealt the cards. 他们分发牌。|近义词：agreement, transaction, bargain, handle, manage |反义词：failure, disagreement
