indignities:/ɪnˈdɪɡnɪtiz/| n. |侮辱；轻蔑；不尊敬|An indignity is an act that shows a lack of respect for someone and causes them to feel ashamed or angry.  It is often a public humiliation or an offense to one's dignity. |例句：He suffered many indignities at the hands of his captors. 他遭受了俘虏的多次侮辱。

例句：The constant indignities she faced at work finally led her to quit.  她在工作中不断遭受的侮辱最终导致她辞职。


近义词：insults, affronts, humiliations, slights, abuses|反义词：honors, respects, accolades, compliments|词性:名词
