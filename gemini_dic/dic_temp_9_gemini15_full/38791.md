conductions:/kənˈdʌkʃənz/ | n. | 传导；传导作用 | The process by which heat or electricity is directly transmitted through a substance when there is a difference of temperature or of electrical potential between adjoining regions, without movement of the material.  | Example:  The conduction of heat through the metal was rapid. 金属导热很快。 The poor conduction of electricity in this material caused a malfunction.  这种材料的导电性差导致了故障。 |近义词：transmission, transfer | 反义词：insulation
