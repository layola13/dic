protocol:/ˈproʊtəkɒl/| n. /ˈprəʊtəkɒl/ |名词|协议；规程；礼仪|A protocol is a set of rules or standards for data transmission, communication, or behavior.  It defines the format and order of messages exchanged between systems or entities.|The company uses a strict security protocol to protect sensitive data.|近义词：agreement, convention, procedure, etiquette|反义词：chaos, disorder
