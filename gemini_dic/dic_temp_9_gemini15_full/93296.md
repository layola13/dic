ilks:/ɪlks/| n.|种类，类型|The word "ilks" refers to kinds or types, often implying a similar or related group.  It's an archaic word, rarely used in modern English.  It often suggests a somewhat derogatory or dismissive tone.|例句：The same old ilk of politicians are always promising change.  同样的政客老一套，总是承诺改变。


例句：He belongs to that ilk of people who never finish what they start. 他属于那种永远无法完成他们开始的事情的人。


近义词：kind, sort, type, variety, class, category


反义词：individual, unique, singular


词性：名词
