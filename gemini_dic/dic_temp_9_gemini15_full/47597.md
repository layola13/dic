debaters:/dɪˈbeɪtərz/| n. |辩论者，争论者 |People who engage in debates.  They are skilled in arguing and presenting their viewpoints. |例句：The debaters presented compelling arguments on both sides of the issue. 辩论者们就该问题的双方都提出了令人信服的论点。

例句：The team of debaters won the national championship.  这个辩论队赢得了全国冠军。


近义词：arguers, disputants, contenders

反义词：listeners (in the context of a debate), observers


词性: 名词
