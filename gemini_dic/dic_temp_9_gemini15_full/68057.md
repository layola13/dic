facultative:/ˈfækəlˌteɪtɪv/| adj.|兼性的；可选择的；任择的|Capable of existing under more than one set of conditions; not obligate.  For example, a facultative anaerobe can survive with or without oxygen.  In other contexts, it means optional or discretionary.|

例句：

Many bacteria are facultative anaerobes, meaning they can survive in both aerobic and anaerobic environments.  许多细菌是兼性厌氧菌，这意味着它们可以在有氧和无氧环境中生存。

The college offers a range of facultative courses alongside its compulsory subjects. 这所大学除了必修课外，还提供一系列选修课。


近义词：optional, discretionary, contingent, elective

反义词：obligatory, mandatory, required


词源:  From Latin *facultātīvus, from facultās "power, ability, opportunity," from facilis "easy."  The original sense implied something that was easily done or possible under various conditions.
