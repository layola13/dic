drawerfuls:/ˈdrɔːrfulz/| n. |抽屉满的量；许多|A drawerful refers to the amount that fills a drawer.  It is used to express a large quantity of something.  The plural form, "drawerfuls," indicates multiple drawers' worth.|例句：She baked drawerfuls of cookies for the bake sale. 她为义卖烘焙了满满几抽屉的饼干。


例句：The antique shop was filled with drawerfuls of old buttons. 这家古董店里装满了满满几抽屉的旧纽扣。|近义词：many, lots of, heaps of, a great deal of|反义词：a few, a little, scarcely any|词性:名词
