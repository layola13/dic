previews:/ˈpriːvjuːz/| n.| 预览；预告；预展 | A preview is a short presentation or demonstration of something that is going to be shown or released later, such as a movie, a play, or a product.  It gives people a chance to see what it is like before the official release. |例句：The movie studio released a preview of the upcoming superhero film. 电影公司发布了即将上映的超级英雄电影的预告片。

例句：The magazine included a preview of the new fashion collection. 这本杂志刊登了新款时装系列的预告。


近义词：sneak peek, trailer, foretaste, advance screening |反义词：finale, conclusion


USAGE->

The website previews the new software features. = 网站预览了新的软件功能。
This magazine previews the latest fashion trends. = 这本杂志预告了最新的时尚潮流。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:

虚拟语气:
