pseudo-Turkish:/ˈsuːdoʊˈtɜːrkɪʃ/|/ˌsjuːdəʊˈtɜːkɪʃ/| adj. |伪土耳其语的；貌似土耳其语的 |Apparently or falsely Turkish in origin or character; resembling Turkish language or culture but not genuinely so.  It often refers to words or phrases that appear to be Turkish but are not authentically derived from the Turkish language.  |例句：The restaurant's menu featured several pseudo-Turkish dishes that were actually of Greek origin. 这家餐馆的菜单上有几道伪土耳其菜，实际上是希腊菜。

例句：The name, while sounding pseudo-Turkish, actually has a Latin root.  这个名字虽然听起来像是伪土耳其语，但实际上源于拉丁语。


近义词：false, sham, bogus, spurious, affected


反义词：authentic, genuine, real, true


词性：形容词
