misappellation:/ˌmɪsˌæpəˈleɪʃən/| n. |误称；误用名称 |The incorrect or inappropriate use of a name or term.  It often implies a misunderstanding or a deliberate attempt to use a wrong label.|例句：The use of "Eskimo" is now considered a misappellation.  使用“爱斯基摩人”现在被认为是误称。


例句：His misappellation of the historical event led to confusion among the audience. 他对这一历史事件的误称导致听众感到困惑。


近义词：misnaming, misnomer, wrong designation


反义词：correct appellation, accurate naming


词性: 名词
