melody:/ˈmɛlədi/ | n. |旋律，曲调 |A melody is a sequence of musical notes that is perceived as a single, coherent unit. It is often the most memorable part of a piece of music.  |The melody of the song was hauntingly beautiful. 这首歌的旋律令人难忘地优美。|tune, air, theme |noise, dissonance |名词
