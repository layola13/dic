isothere: /ˈaɪsəðɛr/ | adj. | 等温线上的；等温的 |  Describes a line on a map connecting points of equal temperature.  It refers to a condition or process that maintains a constant temperature. |例句：The isotherm map showed a significant temperature gradient across the region. 等温线图显示该地区存在显著的温度梯度。


The isotherms on this chart illustrate the changing temperatures across the country. 这张图表上的等温线说明了全国气温的变化。


近义词：isothermal | 反义词：N/A


词源:  The word "isothere" is a combination of the Greek prefix "iso-" (meaning "equal") and the Greek word "therme" (meaning "heat" or "temperature").  Therefore, it literally means "equal heat" or "equal temperature".
