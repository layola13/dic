reconsigning:/ˌriːˈkɒnsaɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 再托运；重新装运；改发 | The act of sending goods or shipments to a different destination than originally intended.  It involves changing the consignee or the delivery address after the shipment has already been initiated. | Example: The manufacturer had to reconsign the shipment to a new address after the customer changed their location. = 制造商不得不将货物改发到一个新的地址，因为客户更换了地址。 |近义词：reship, redirect, reroute | 反义词：N/A
