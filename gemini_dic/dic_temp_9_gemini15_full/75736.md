fullery:/ˈfʊləri/| n.| 整理厂；漂洗厂；绒布厂 | A fullery is a workshop or factory where cloth is fulled or cleaned.  It's a historical term referring to places where woolen cloth was processed, often involving fulling (a process of cleansing and thickening cloth) and sometimes dyeing. | The old fullery was abandoned decades ago, its machinery rusted and forgotten. = 那家旧的整理厂被废弃了几十年，其机器生锈且被遗忘了。|  The village's economy once revolved around its thriving fullery. = 村庄的经济曾经围绕着它繁荣的整理厂而运转。|  mill, scouring works|  (no direct antonym) | 名词
