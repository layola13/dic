schmoozes:/ʃmuːz/ | v. | 逢迎；奉承；闲聊 | To engage in prolonged, often insincere conversation, typically for social or professional gain.  |  He schmoozed with the executives, hoping to secure a lucrative deal. 他逢迎那些主管，希望能达成一笔有利可图的交易。 |  network, socialize, flatter |  ignore, snub, alienate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He schmoozes with clients every week. = 他每周都和客户闲聊。
She schmoozes her way to the top. = 她靠逢迎拍马爬到了顶峰。


一般过去时:
He schmoozed the investors last night. = 他昨晚逢迎了那些投资者。
She schmoozed with the celebrities at the party. = 她在派对上跟那些名流闲聊。


现在完成时:
He has schmoozed his way into a powerful position. = 他靠逢迎拍马已经获得了有权势的地位。
She has schmoozed with many important people in her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她已经和许多重要人物闲聊过。


过去完成时:
He had schmoozed the board before presenting his proposal. = 在提交提案之前，他已经逢迎了董事会。
She had schmoozed the director for weeks before getting the role. = 在得到这个角色之前，她已经奉承导演好几个星期了。


将来时:
He will schmooze with the potential buyers at the trade show. = 他将在贸易展上与潜在买家闲聊。
She will schmooze the committee to get their support. = 她将奉承委员会以获得他们的支持。


虚拟语气:
If he schmoozed more, he might get the contract. = 如果他多逢迎一些，他可能会得到合同。
I wish I hadn't schmoozed with that guy; he's unreliable. = 我真希望我没有跟那个人闲聊；他不靠谱。


被动语态:
He was schmoozed by the sales team into signing the contract. = 他被销售团队逢迎得签了合同。
The new employee was schmoozed by the senior staff. = 新员工受到了高级员工的奉承。


疑问句:
Does he often schmooze with his colleagues? = 他经常和同事闲聊吗？
Will she schmooze the potential investor tomorrow? = 她明天会逢迎潜在投资者吗？


否定句:
He doesn't schmooze; he prefers direct communication. = 他不逢迎；他更喜欢直接沟通。
She didn't schmooze her way to success; she earned it. = 她不是靠逢迎拍马获得成功的；她是靠自己挣来的。


PHRASE->
schmooze with someone = 和某人闲聊
schmooze one's way to something = 靠逢迎获得某物
schmooze up to someone = 讨好某人
be schmoozing = 正在闲聊
a schmooze session = 闲聊时间
