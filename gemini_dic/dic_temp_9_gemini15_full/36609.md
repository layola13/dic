collected:/kəˈlɛktɪd/ | adj. v. | 收集的；被收集的；沉着的 | gathered together; composed; calm and self-possessed | The collected works of Shakespeare are extensive.  莎士比亚的全集篇幅浩大。|  He remained collected during the crisis.  他在危机期间保持镇静。| gathered, amassed, assembled | scattered, agitated, flustered | 形容词，过去分词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He collects stamps. = 他收集邮票。
She collects antique dolls. = 她收集古董娃娃。


一般过去时:
They collected donations for charity. = 他们为慈善机构募捐。
He collected his things and left. = 他收拾好东西离开了。


现在完成时:
I have collected all the necessary documents. = 我已经收集了所有必要的文档。
She has collected a large number of rare books. = 她已经收集了大量的珍本书。


过去完成时:
He had collected enough evidence before he went to court. = 他在去法院之前已经收集了足够的证据。
They had collected the data for months before they analyzed it. = 他们在分析数据之前已经收集了几个月的数据。


将来时:
We will collect the children from school. = 我们将去学校接孩子们。
They will collect the garbage tomorrow morning. = 他们明天早上将收集垃圾。


虚拟语气:
If I collected all the stamps, I would have a complete set. = 如果我收集了所有的邮票，我就会有一套完整的邮票。
I wish I had collected more information before making the decision. = 我希望在做决定之前我已经收集了更多信息。


被动语态:
The data was collected over a period of five years. = 数据是在五年内收集的。
The donations are collected by volunteers. = 捐款由志愿者收集。


疑问句:
Have you collected all the signatures? = 你收集齐所有签名了吗？
What did they collect at the event? = 他们在活动中收集了什么？


否定句:
He didn’t collect his mail yesterday. = 他昨天没有收邮件。
They haven’t collected enough money yet. = 他们还没有筹集到足够的钱。
