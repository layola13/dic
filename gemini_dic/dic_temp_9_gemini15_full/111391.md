lividity:/lɪˈvɪdəti/| n.|青紫；死后尸斑；愤怒；暴怒|The discoloration of the skin caused by poor circulation or death.  It can also refer to extreme anger.|例句：The lividity on his face indicated a lack of blood circulation. 他脸上青紫表明血液循环不良。

例句：His lividity was evident in his clenched fists and raised voice. 他紧握的拳头和提高的声音表明了他极度的愤怒。

近义词：discoloration, cyanosis, fury, rage, wrath|反义词：health, calmness, serenity, tranquility|词性：名词
