over-breed:/ˌoʊvərˈbriːd/ | vt. |过度繁殖；使过度繁殖| To breed (animals or plants) to an excessive degree, often resulting in undesirable genetic consequences.  This can lead to health problems, decreased fertility, and increased susceptibility to diseases. |例句：The irresponsible breeding of certain dog breeds has led to an increase in genetic disorders.  过度繁殖某些犬种导致遗传疾病增多。|例句：Farmers should avoid over-breeding their livestock to maintain the health and productivity of their herds. 农民应避免牲畜过度繁殖，以保持其畜群的健康和生产力。|近义词：overpopulate, excessively breed |反义词：control breeding, limit breeding
