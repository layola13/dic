routiner:/ruːˈtiːnər/| n. |惯例者；按部就班者 |A routiner is someone who follows a routine or set of procedures, often to the point of being inflexible or unimaginative. They may be perceived as predictable and lacking spontaneity.  | He's such a routiner; he does the same thing every day. 他是一个惯例者；他每天都做同样的事情。|  routine follower, stickler | innovator, maverick | 名词
