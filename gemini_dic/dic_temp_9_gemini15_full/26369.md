by hook or by crook:/baɪ hʊk ɔːr baɪ krʊk/| phrase | 不择手段；无论如何 |  This idiom means by any means necessary, regardless of legality or morality. It implies a determination to achieve something, even if it requires using dishonest or underhanded methods. |例句：She was determined to get the job, by hook or by crook. 她下定决心要得到这份工作，不择手段。

例句：They'll find a way to get the money, by hook or by crook. 他们会想方设法弄到钱，无论如何。


近义词：at all costs, no matter what, by any means necessary

反义词：honestly, ethically, legitimately

词性：phrase
