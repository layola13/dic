capacitive:/kəˈpæsɪtɪv/ | adj. | 电容的，容性的 | Relating to or exhibiting capacitance; storing electrical energy in an electric field.  | Example: The capacitive reactance of the circuit was high.  该电路的容抗很高。 The capacitive touchscreen responded quickly to my touch. 电容式触摸屏对我的触摸反应迅速。 |近义词：condenser,  |反义词：resistive (in the context of touchscreens)
