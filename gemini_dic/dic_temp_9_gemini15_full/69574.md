felonwort:/ˈfɛlənˌwɔːrt/| n. |毒芹, 水毒芹|A poisonous plant, particularly *Cicuta maculata*,  also known as spotted water hemlock,  known for its highly toxic nature.  It is a member of the carrot family (Apiaceae) and is responsible for numerous fatalities due to accidental ingestion.|Example:  The farmer warned his children to stay away from the felonwort growing near the creek.  农民警告他的孩子们远离小溪边生长的毒芹。|近义词：water hemlock, poison hemlock, spotted hemlock|反义词：无|词性：名词
