overheaped:/ˌoʊvərˈhiːpt/| adj.|堆积如山的，过多的| excessively piled or heaped; overloaded.  The word suggests an abundance that is almost excessive or overwhelming.|例句：The table was overheaped with gifts.  桌子堆满了礼物。 The garden was overheaped with blooming flowers. 花园里鲜花盛开，堆积如山。|近义词：overloaded, piled high, overflowing, abundant|反义词：sparse, meager, scanty
