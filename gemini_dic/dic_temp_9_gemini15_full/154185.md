periodontist: /ˌpɪəriəˈdɒntɪst/ | n. |牙周病学家| A dentist specializing in the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of diseases and conditions of the supporting and surrounding tissues of the teeth (periodontal tissues).  They focus on the gums, bone, and ligaments that hold teeth in place. | My periodontist recommended a deep cleaning to address my gum inflammation. = 我的牙周病学家建议进行深层清洁以解决我的牙龈炎症。  I need to see a periodontist for my receding gums. = 我需要去看牙周病学家治疗我的牙龈萎缩。 | dentist, gum specialist |  (no direct antonym, but perhaps a general dentist could be considered a contrasting term)| noun
