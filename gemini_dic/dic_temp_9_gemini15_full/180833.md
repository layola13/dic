rehashing:/ˌriːˈhæʃɪŋ/| v.|重新讨论；重新处理；炒冷饭|To discuss or present (something) again, typically without significant changes or additions.  It often implies a lack of originality or freshness. |例句：The committee spent hours rehashing the same old arguments.  委员会花了几个小时重新讨论那些老掉牙的论点。


例句：He's constantly rehashing the same tired jokes. 他一直在重复那些老掉牙的笑话。


近义词：reiterate, recapitulate, recycle, revamp (if implying improvement)


反义词：innovate, originate, create


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rehash the same ideas every meeting. = 他们每次会议都重复同样的想法。
He rehashes old news stories for his blog. = 他为他的博客重新加工旧新闻故事。

一般过去时:
The teacher rehashed the previous lesson. = 老师复习了上一课的内容。
She rehashed the plot of the movie for her friend. = 她为朋友复述了电影的情节。

现在完成时:
They have rehashed this proposal several times. = 他们已经多次修改这个提案了。
He has rehashed the same information in his report. = 他在他的报告中重复了同样的信息。

过去完成时:
They had rehashed the arguments before the final decision. = 在做出最终决定之前，他们已经反复争论过了。
She had rehashed her presentation several times before delivering it. = 在做演讲之前，她已经修改了她的演示文稿好几次。

将来时:
They will rehash the contract details next week. = 他们下周将重新讨论合同细节。
He will rehash his old research for the conference. = 他将为会议重新整理他的旧研究成果。


虚拟语气:
If they rehashed the plan, it might work better. = 如果他们重新策划这个计划，它可能会更好。
I wish they hadn't rehashed the same old arguments. = 我真希望他们没有再重复那些老掉牙的论点。

被动语态:
The ideas were rehashed endlessly. = 这些想法被没完没了地重复。
The story has been rehashed countless times. = 这个故事已经被无数次地翻炒了。

疑问句:
Are they rehashing the old debate again? = 他们又在翻炒旧话题了吗？
Will the report rehash the findings of the previous study? = 这份报告会重新整理先前研究的结果吗？

否定句:
They didn't rehash the details of the agreement. = 他们没有详细重述协议的内容。
He will not rehash the same points in his speech. = 他不会在他的演讲中重复同样的观点。
