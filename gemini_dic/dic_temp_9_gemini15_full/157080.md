pin-cushion: /ˈpɪnkʊʃən/ | n. |针插,针垫| A small cushion, usually stuffed with something soft, into which needles are stuck for safekeeping.  It is often used by tailors, seamstresses, and other people who work with needles. |例句：She carefully placed the needles back in the pin-cushion after finishing her sewing.  她缝完衣服后小心地把针插回针垫里。

例句：The pincushion sat on her sewing table, a colorful addition to her workspace. 针垫放在她的缝纫桌上，为她的工作空间增添了一抹色彩。
