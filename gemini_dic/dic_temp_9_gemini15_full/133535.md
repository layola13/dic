英文解析:

Nonfigurativeness refers to the quality of being literal or factual, lacking figurative language or metaphorical expression. It emphasizes directness and avoids embellishment through literary devices such as similes, metaphors, or personification.  A text with high nonfigurativeness prioritizes conveying information clearly and concisely without artistic embellishment.


例句:

The scientist's report was characterized by its nonfigurativeness;  every statement was intended to be a straightforward representation of the data. = 科学家的报告以其非比喻性为特征；每个陈述都是对数据的直接表达。

The stark nonfigurativeness of the news report contrasted sharply with the flowery language of the editorial. = 新闻报道的简洁直接与社论的华丽辞藻形成了鲜明对比。
