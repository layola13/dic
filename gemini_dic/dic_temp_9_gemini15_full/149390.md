pack:/pæk/ |/pæk/| n./v.|包裹；背包；一群；捆；包装；塞满；挤满| A collection of things fastened or wrapped together; to put things into a container; to fill tightly. |例句：I packed my suitcase for the trip. 我为旅行打包了我的手提箱。

He packed the boxes carefully. 他仔细地打包了箱子。|近义词：bundle, package, group, crowd |反义词：unpack, scatter, disperse|名词/动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He packs his lunch every day. 他每天都打包午餐。
She packs her clothes neatly. 她把衣服叠得很整齐。


一般过去时:
They packed their bags and left. 他们收拾好行李就离开了。
I packed the presents beautifully. 我把礼物包装得很漂亮。


现在完成时:
Have you packed your things yet? 你收拾好东西了吗？
She has packed all the boxes. 她已经打包好所有箱子了。


过去完成时:
They had packed their bags before the announcement. 他们在公告之前就已经打包好行李了。
He had packed his suitcase the night before. 他前一天晚上就把手提箱打包好了。


将来时:
I will pack my bags tomorrow morning. 我明天早上会打包行李。
We will pack the food for the picnic. 我们将打包野餐的食物。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pack more carefully. 如果我有更多时间，我会更仔细地打包。
I wish I had packed a warmer coat. 我真希望我带了一件更暖和的大衣。


被动语态:
The suitcases were packed by the hotel staff. 行李箱由酒店员工打包。
The boxes are being packed now. 箱子现在正在打包。


疑问句:
Have you packed everything? 你都打包好了吗？
What did you pack for the journey? 你带了什么东西去旅行？


否定句:
I didn't pack enough clothes. 我没带够衣服。
He hasn't packed his lunch yet. 他还没打包午餐。


PHRASE->
pack a punch:  有很大影响力
pack it in:  停止；放弃
pack a bag:  打包行李
pack one's bags:  收拾行李
pack a room:  挤满房间


词源:
The word "pack" originates from the Old Norse word "pakki," meaning "bundle" or "package."  This word entered Middle English around the 14th century.  Its evolution reflects the long history of humans using bundles and containers for transport and storage.
