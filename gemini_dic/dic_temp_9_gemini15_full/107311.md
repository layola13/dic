lambaste: /læmˈbeɪst/ | vt. |痛斥；严厉批评；痛打 | To criticize (someone or something) harshly; to beat severely. |例句：The critics lambasted the new film. 批评家痛斥这部新电影。

例句：The coach lambasted the team for their poor performance. 教练因球队糟糕的表现而痛斥他们。


近义词：criticize, rebuke, reprimand, castigate, denounce, excoriate, berate

反义词：praise, commend, applaud

He lambastes his employees for minor mistakes. 他因员工的小错误而痛斥他们。
