cyst:/sɪst/ | n. |囊肿，胞囊 |A cyst is an abnormal, fluid-filled sac or cavity in the body.  Cysts can occur in various parts of the body and may be benign or malignant. They can be caused by infection, inflammation, or other factors.  |例句：The doctor discovered a cyst on her ovary during the ultrasound. 医生在超声波检查中发现了她卵巢上的一个囊肿。

The dentist found a small cyst on his gum. 牙医在他牙龈上发现了一个小囊肿。


近义词：tumor, nodule, abscess |反义词：None (no direct antonym) |词性:名词
