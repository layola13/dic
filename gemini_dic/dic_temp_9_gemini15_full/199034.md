signatures:/ˈsɪɡnətʃərz/| n.|签名，签字；特征，标志；信号；(音乐)乐段；|英文解析:A signature is a person's name written in their own handwriting, typically as a way to authenticate a document or other item.  It can also refer to a distinctive characteristic or feature of something.  In music, it refers to a repeated melodic or rhythmic pattern.|例句：He signed the contract with his signature. 他用他的签名签署了合同。The artist's signature style is immediately recognizable.这位艺术家的标志性风格很容易辨认。|近义词：autograph, mark, sign, symbol|反义词：illegible mark, facelessness
