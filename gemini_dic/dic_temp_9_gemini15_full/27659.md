candidating:/ˈkændɪdeɪtɪŋ/| v. | 竞选；角逐；成为候选人 |The act of running for an elected office or position.  It involves actively campaigning and seeking votes or support. | Example: She's candidating for mayor. 她正在竞选市长。  He is candidating for the student council president. 他正在竞选学生会主席。|近义词：running for office, campaigning, vying for, seeking nomination |反义词：abdicating, resigning, withdrawing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is candidating for the Senate this year. 他今年正在竞选参议员。
They are candidating for different positions within the company. 他们正在竞选公司内的不同职位。

一般过去时:
She candidated for class president last year. 她去年竞选班长。
He candidated for the scholarship but didn't win. 他申请了奖学金但没有获得。

现在完成时:
He has candidated for several political offices in his career. 他在他的职业生涯中竞选过多个政治职位。
She has candidated for the board of directors three times. 她已经三次竞选董事会成员。

过去完成时:
Before the election, he had candidated for several local positions. 在选举之前，他曾竞选过几个地方职位。
They had candidated for the same position before, but this time, one of them withdrew. 他们之前曾竞选过同一个职位，但这次其中一人退出了。

将来时:
She will be candidating for the school board next year. 她明年将竞选学校董事会。
They will candidating for the upcoming leadership elections. 他们将参加即将到来的领导力选举。


虚拟语气:
If he candidated, he would have a good chance of winning. 如果他竞选，他将有很大的获胜机会。
I wish I had candidated for that position. 我真希望我当时竞选了那个职位。

被动语态:
No one was candidating for the position. 没有人竞选这个职位。(被动语态用得较少，因为candidating 通常是主动行为)
The position was being candidated for by several people. 这个职位正在被几个人竞选。(被动语态用得较少，因为candidating 通常是主动行为)


疑问句:
Is she candidating for re-election? 她正在竞选连任吗？
Who is candidating for the open seat? 谁正在竞选空缺的席位？


否定句:
He is not candidating for any office this year. 他今年没有竞选任何职位。
She decided not to candidate for the position. 她决定不竞选这个职位。
