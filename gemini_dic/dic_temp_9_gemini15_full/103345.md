jillaroos:/ˌdʒɪləˈruːz/| n.|女牛仔;从事牧牛工作的妇女|Australian English: A woman who works as a stockperson, typically on a cattle station.  The term is a blend of "Jill" and "aroos" (a shortened form of "aroosers," meaning rouseabouts or general hands).|例句：The jillaroos on the station are highly skilled horse riders and cattle handlers.  牧场上的女牛仔们都是非常熟练的骑手和牲畜管理员。


The jillaroos demonstrated their expertise in mustering cattle. 这些女牛仔们展示了她们在牧牛方面的专业技能。|近义词：cowgirl, stockwoman|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with terms implying desk work or city life.
