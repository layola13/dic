dissatisfied:/ˌdɪsˈsætɪsfaɪd/ | adj. | 不满意的，不满足的 | Feeling or expressing unhappiness or dissatisfaction.  It implies a lack of fulfillment or contentment with something.  | 例句：He was dissatisfied with his job and decided to quit. 他对自己的工作不满意，决定辞职了。

He expressed his dissatisfaction with the poor service. 他表达了他对糟糕的服务的不满。


近义词：discontented, unhappy, displeased, frustrated, disgruntled | 反义词：satisfied, content, pleased, happy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am dissatisfied with the result. = 我对结果不满意。
They are dissatisfied with the current situation. = 他们对现状不满。


一般过去时:
I was dissatisfied with my performance. = 我对自己的表现不满意。
She was dissatisfied with the quality of the product. = 她对产品的质量不满意。


现在完成时:
I have been dissatisfied with this job for months. = 我对这份工作已经不满好几个月了。
They have been dissatisfied with the management's decisions. = 他们一直对管理层的决定不满。


过去完成时:
I had been dissatisfied with my life for years before I made a change. = 在我做出改变之前，我已经对我的生活不满多年了。
She had been dissatisfied with her relationship for a long time. = 她对她的感情不满已经很久了。


将来时:
I will be dissatisfied if the project is not completed on time. = 如果项目没有按时完成，我会不满意的。
They will be dissatisfied if their concerns are ignored. = 如果他们的担忧被忽视，他们将会不满意的。


虚拟语气:
I would be dissatisfied if I didn't get a promotion. = 如果我没有得到晋升，我会不满意的。
She would have been dissatisfied if the event hadn't been successful. = 如果活动不成功，她会感到不满意的。


被动语态:
His complaints showed that he was dissatisfied. = 他的抱怨表明他感到不满。 (Note:  "Dissatisfied" is rarely used passively.)


疑问句:
Are you dissatisfied with your current job? = 你对目前的工作不满意吗？
Is he dissatisfied with the outcome of the negotiations? = 他对谈判的结果不满意吗？


否定句:
I am not dissatisfied with the service; in fact, I'm quite happy with it. = 我对这项服务并不感到不满，事实上，我很满意。
They were not dissatisfied with the changes that were made. = 他们对所做的改变并不感到不满。


PHRASE->
feel dissatisfied = 感到不满
express dissatisfaction = 表达不满
become dissatisfied = 变得不满
show dissatisfaction = 表现出不满
a source of dissatisfaction = 不满的根源
