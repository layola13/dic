replanning:/ˌriːˈplænɪŋ/ | n. |重新规划，重新计划 | The act of planning something again, often after a previous plan has failed or been deemed insufficient.  It involves reviewing existing plans, identifying shortcomings, and developing improved strategies. | 例句：The company's replanning of its marketing strategy resulted in increased sales. 公司重新规划营销策略，导致销售额增长。

The city council is undertaking a major replanning of the downtown area. 市议会正在对市中心区域进行大规模的重新规划。


近义词：restructuring, revision, redesign | 反义词：implementation, execution
