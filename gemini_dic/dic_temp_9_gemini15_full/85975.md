headscarf:/ˈhɛdskɑːrf/| n. |头巾，围巾 |A headscarf is a piece of cloth worn on the head, often by women, for warmth, fashion, or religious reasons.  |Example: She wore a silk headscarf to protect her hair from the sun. 她戴着丝绸头巾以保护头发免受阳光照射。  She tied a headscarf around her head. 她把头巾围在头上。|近义词：scarf, veil, kerchief |反义词：无
