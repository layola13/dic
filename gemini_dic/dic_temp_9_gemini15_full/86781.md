helvite:/ˈhɛlvaɪt/| n. |含锰硅铍酸盐矿物|Helvite is a manganese silicate mineral with the chemical formula (Mn,Fe,Zn)4Be3Si3O12S.  It is usually found in granular masses or crystals, often associated with other beryllium minerals. Its color varies from yellowish-brown to brownish-red, and it's characterized by its resinous luster and good cleavage.  |The helvite sample displayed a distinct yellowish-brown hue and exhibited excellent cleavage.|近义词：None (unique mineral)|反义词：None (no direct antonym)|
