mis-fielding: /ˌmɪsˈfiːldɪŋ/ | n. | 失误接球 | The act of failing to catch or control a ball that has been hit or thrown, especially in baseball or cricket.  | Example:  The team's poor mis-fielding led to their defeat.  这个队的糟糕接球导致了他们的失败。 |近义词:  error, fumble, botch | 反义词:  good fielding, clean catch |
