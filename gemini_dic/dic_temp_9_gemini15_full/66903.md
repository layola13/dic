expeditionary:/ˌɛkspəˈdɪʃənəri/| adj.|远征的；探险的；考察的|Relating to or characteristic of an expedition.  It often implies a military or scientific undertaking of exploration or conquest.|The expeditionary force landed on the beach.  远征军登陆海滩。|The expeditionary team made significant discoveries. 考察队取得了重大发现。|exploratory, investigative|peaceful, domestic
