haricots:/ˈhærɪkɒts/| n.| 菜豆；四季豆 | French word for beans, especially kidney beans or green beans.  The plural form is used even when referring to a single serving.  | Example:  I made a delicious stew with haricots. 我用菜豆做了一道美味的炖菜。|近义词：beans, kidney beans, green beans|反义词：N/A
