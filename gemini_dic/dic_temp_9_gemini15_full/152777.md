pecked:/pekt/| vt.|啄，轻啄；匆匆写；|To strike or pick at with a beak. To make (something) by pecking. To write or type quickly and carelessly.|例句：The birds pecked at the seeds. 鸟儿啄食种子。The student pecked out the essay at the last minute. 学生在最后一刻匆匆写完论文。|近义词：tap, peck at, strike, jab|反义词：polish, refine


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hen pecks at the grain. = 母鸡啄食谷物。
He pecks at his keyboard furiously. = 他疯狂地敲打键盘。


一般过去时:
The woodpecker pecked a hole in the tree. = 啄木鸟在树上啄了一个洞。
She pecked out a quick email before the meeting. = 她在会议前匆匆写了一封邮件。


现在完成时:
The chickens have pecked all the seeds. = 鸡已经啄食了所有的种子。
He has pecked out the entire report in one sitting. = 他一口气打完了整份报告。


过去完成时:
The bird had pecked at the fruit before I arrived. = 我到之前，鸟已经啄食了水果。
She had pecked out a draft of the letter before the deadline. = 截止日期前，她已经打出了信件的草稿。


将来时:
The chick will peck at its food soon. = 小鸡很快就会啄食食物了。
He will peck out a response to the email later. = 他稍后会回邮件。


虚拟语气:
If the bird pecked at the window, I would be annoyed. = 如果鸟啄窗户，我会很生气。
If she had pecked at her keyboard more carefully, she wouldn't have made so many mistakes. = 如果她更仔细地打字，她就不会犯这么多错误。


被动语态:
The seeds were pecked by the birds. = 种子被鸟啄食了。
The email was quickly pecked out by the assistant. = 邮件被助理匆匆打了出来。


疑问句:
Did the chicken peck at the corn? = 鸡啄玉米了吗？
Has he already pecked out the letter? = 他已经打出信了吗？


否定句:
The bird didn't peck at the fruit. = 鸟没有啄食水果。
She didn't peck out the report on time. = 她没有按时打出报告。


PHRASE->
peck away at:  持续地做（通常指枯燥的工作）
peck at: 轻啄；少量地吃
peck and order:  随意地啄食
peck something out: 匆匆地打字或写字
peck order: 啄序；等级制度
