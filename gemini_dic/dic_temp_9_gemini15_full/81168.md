grandee:/ɡrænˈdiː/| n. |显贵，名流 |A person of high rank or influence, especially in Spain or Latin America.  A grandee is someone of high social standing and power, often holding a hereditary title.  |例句：The grandees of the court were all present at the ceremony.  宫廷显贵们都出席了典礼。

The Spanish grandees held significant power during the reign of the Catholic Monarchs.  西班牙的显贵们在 天主教君主统治时期拥有很大的权力。

近义词：noble, aristocrat, magnate, dignitary |反义词：commoner, peasant
