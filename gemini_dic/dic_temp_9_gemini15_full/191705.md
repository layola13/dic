schweitzerkäse: /ˈʃvaɪtsɐˌkɛːzə/ | n. |瑞士奶酪| Swiss cheese; a type of cheese originating from Switzerland, characterized by its holes.  It's typically made from cow's milk, though other milks can be used.  The holes are caused by the production of carbon dioxide during the aging process by Propionibacterium bacteria.| Example:  I bought a block of schweitzerkäse for sandwiches. 我买了一块瑞士奶酪做三明治。  The restaurant served a delicious schweitzerkäse fondue.  这家餐馆供应美味的瑞士奶酪火锅。|近义词：Swiss cheese |反义词：None (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with cheeses lacking holes)|名词
