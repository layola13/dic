proceed:/prəˈsiːd/| v.|继续，进行，着手|To continue doing something that has already been started, or to continue to be done.|例句：The meeting proceeded smoothly. 会议顺利进行。|例句：Despite the rain, they proceeded with their picnic. 尽管下雨，他们还是继续野餐。|近义词：continue, advance, go on|反义词：stop, cease, halt


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He proceeds with caution. = 他谨慎地继续。
They proceed to the next item on the agenda. = 他们继续进行议程上的下一项。

一般过去时:
She proceeded to tell me the whole story. = 她接着把整个故事都告诉我了。
The construction proceeded slowly due to bad weather. = 由于天气不好，施工进展缓慢。

现在完成时:
We have proceeded with the plan as scheduled. = 我们已按计划执行了该计划。
They have proceeded to the next stage of the project. = 他们已经进入项目的下一阶段。

过去完成时:
After he had proceeded a few steps, he tripped. = 他走了几步后绊倒了。
They had proceeded with the experiment before realizing the flaw in the design. = 他们在意识到设计缺陷之前已经进行了实验。

将来时:
We will proceed with the investigation tomorrow. = 我们明天将继续调查。
The project will proceed according to the timeline. = 项目将按时间表进行。

虚拟语气:
If we proceeded carefully, we would succeed. = 如果我们谨慎行事，我们就会成功。
I wish we had proceeded differently. = 我希望我们采取不同的做法。

被动语态:
The plan was proceeded with efficiently. = 该计划得到了有效执行。
The investigation is being proceeded with by the police. = 警方正在进行调查。

疑问句:
How shall we proceed? = 我们该如何继续？
When will the trial proceed? = 审判何时开始？

否定句:
They did not proceed with the sale. = 他们没有继续出售。
The meeting did not proceed as planned. = 会议没有按计划进行。


PHRASE->
proceed with = 继续进行...
proceed against = 对...提起诉讼
proceed to = 接着做...
proceed from = 源于...
proceed upon = 基于...
