haemorrhaging:/ˈhɛməˌrɪdʒɪŋ/| v.| 大出血；大量流失 | The rapid loss of blood, typically from a ruptured blood vessel.  It can also refer to a rapid loss of something valuable, like money or talent. |例句：The patient was haemorrhaging profusely after the accident. 事故后病人大量出血。|例句：The company is haemorrhaging money due to poor management.  公司由于管理不善而大量亏损。|近义词：bleeding, losing heavily, hemorrhaging (American spelling)|反义词：healing, recovery, stabilizing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is haemorrhaging from a deep wound. = 他从深层伤口大量出血。
The company is haemorrhaging funds. = 公司正在大量亏损资金。


一般过去时:
She haemorrhaged heavily after childbirth. = 她分娩后大量出血。
The business haemorrhaged capital during the recession. = 在经济衰退期间，这家企业损失了大量资金。


现在完成时:
He has haemorrhaged a significant amount of blood. = 他已经流失了大量的血液。
The project has haemorrhaged resources. = 这个项目已经流失了大量的资源。


过去完成时:
They had haemorrhaged most of their savings before they sought help. = 在他们寻求帮助之前，他们已经损失了大部分积蓄。
The government had haemorrhaged public trust before the election. = 在选举之前，政府已经失去了大部分公众信任。


将来时:
He will haemorrhage if he doesn't receive immediate medical attention. = 如果他不立即接受医疗救治，他会大量出血。
The startup will haemorrhage cash if it doesn't secure further funding. = 如果这家初创公司无法获得进一步的资金，它将面临资金大量流失。


虚拟语气:
If he had received immediate treatment, he wouldn't have haemorrhaged so much. = 如果他立即接受治疗，他就不会流失这么多血。
If they had managed their finances better, the company wouldn't have haemorrhaged so much money. = 如果他们能更好地管理财务，公司就不会损失那么多钱。


被动语态:
Blood was haemorrhaged from the wound. = 血液从伤口流出。
Millions of dollars were haemorrhaged by the failing company. = 数百万美元被这家即将倒闭的公司损失了。


疑问句:
Is he haemorrhaging internally? = 他是不是内出血？
Is the project haemorrhaging money faster than anticipated? = 这个项目的资金流失速度是否比预期的更快？


否定句:
He isn't haemorrhaging at present. = 他目前没有出血。
The government isn't haemorrhaging support. = 政府的支持并没有减少。
