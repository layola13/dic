preachment:/ˈpriːtʃmənt/| n. |说教；冗长的训斥|A long and tedious moral lecture or sermon.  It often implies a feeling of being lectured or scolded unnecessarily.  |例句：His preachment on the evils of gambling went on for an hour.  他的关于赌博罪恶的说教持续了一个小时。

例句：The teacher's preachment about the importance of studying bored the students. 老师关于学习重要性的说教让学生们感到厌烦。


近义词：sermon, lecture, diatribe, harangue, admonition


反义词：praise, commendation, encouragement


词性：名词
