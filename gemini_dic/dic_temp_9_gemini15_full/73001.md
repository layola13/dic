footprints:/ˈfʊtˌprɪnts/ | n. |脚印；足迹；影响 |Marks left by a foot or shoe; the impact or lasting effect of something.|The footprints in the sand showed where the dog had run. 沙滩上的脚印显示了狗跑过的地方。  The conference left its footprints on the future of the company.  会议对公司的未来留下了影响。|traces, tracks, impressions|None|
