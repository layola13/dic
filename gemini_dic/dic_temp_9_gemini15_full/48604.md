defection:/dɪˈfɛkʃən/| n. |叛逃；变节；缺陷；不足|The act of abandoning a cause, allegiance, or group.  It can also refer to a defect or flaw. |例句：His defection to the opposition party shocked everyone.  他的叛逃到反对党震惊了所有人。

例句：The engineer discovered a major defection in the bridge's design.工程师发现桥梁设计存在一个主要缺陷。

近义词：desertion, betrayal, apostasy, flaw, defect |反义词：loyalty, fidelity, allegiance, perfection
