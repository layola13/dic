percolator:/ˈpɜːrkəleɪtər/|/ˈpɝːkəˌleɪtɚ/| n. |渗透器；咖啡壶 |A percolator is a device used to make coffee by passing hot water through ground coffee beans.  It typically involves a pot with a perforated basket or tube where the coffee grounds are placed, and hot water is circulated through the grounds to extract the coffee flavor. |例句：She brewed a pot of coffee in her old-fashioned percolator. 她用她那老式的渗透式咖啡壶煮了一壶咖啡。|例句：The percolator whistled, signaling that the coffee was ready. 咖啡壶发出汽笛声，表示咖啡已经煮好了。|近义词：coffee maker, coffee pot |反义词：N/A
