haemolysis:/ˌhiːˈmɒləsɪs/ | /ˌhiːˈmɑːləsɪs/ | n. |溶血|The destruction of red blood cells and the release of hemoglobin into the surrounding fluid.  This process can be caused by various factors, including infections, autoimmune diseases, and certain medications.  |The patient's blood test showed signs of haemolysis, indicating a potential problem with their red blood cells. 患者的血液检查显示溶血迹象，表明其红细胞可能存在问题。|  intravascular haemolysis, extravascular haemolysis |  none (antonym is the absence of haemolysis)| 名词
