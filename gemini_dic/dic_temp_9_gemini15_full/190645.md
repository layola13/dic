saucepan:/ˈsɔːspən/| n. |小煎锅，炖锅 |A small pan with a long handle, used for cooking sauces and other foods on the stovetop.  |例句：I used the saucepan to make a delicious tomato sauce. 我用这个小煎锅做了一道美味的番茄酱。  |例句：She carefully simmered the soup in the saucepan. 她小心地在小煎锅里慢炖汤。|近义词：pan, pot |反义词: None
