preallotting: /ˌpriːˈælɒtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先分配，预先拨给 |  The act of allocating or assigning something in advance.  It implies a prior determination or reservation of resources or items before they are actually needed or used. |

例句：

The company is preallotting resources for the new project. = 公司正在为新项目预先分配资源。

The government preallotted funds for disaster relief. = 政府预先拨款用于救灾。


近义词：preassigning, allocating in advance, reserving beforehand

反义词：post-allocating, assigning afterwards


词性：动词
