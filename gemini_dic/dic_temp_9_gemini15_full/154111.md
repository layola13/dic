periganglionic: /ˌpɛriˌɡæŋɡliˈɑːnɪk/ | adj. |节前神经节的，节前神经元的 | Pertaining to or situated near a ganglion, especially a nerve ganglion.  This term is primarily used in anatomy and physiology, specifically in the context of the autonomic nervous system.  It refers to the nerve fibers that extend from the central nervous system to the autonomic ganglia. |  The periganglionic fibers release acetylcholine onto the postganglionic neurons.  节前神经纤维释放乙酰胆碱到节后神经元上。 | 近义词：preganglionic (though technically distinct, often used interchangeably in less precise contexts) | 反义词：postganglionic
