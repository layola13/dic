fluidize:/ˈfluːɪdaɪz/| vt. |使流化；使液化；使易流动|To convert (a solid material, such as a powder) into a fluid-like state by the passage of a gas or liquid through it.  This process is often used in chemical engineering to improve mixing, heat transfer, or mass transfer.|例句：The catalyst was fluidized by passing air upward through the bed. = 通过向上通过床层的气流使催化剂流化。

The powder was fluidized to improve its flow properties. = 粉末被流化以改善其流动性能。

近义词：liquefy, loosen, mobilize |反义词：solidify, compact, harden|动词
