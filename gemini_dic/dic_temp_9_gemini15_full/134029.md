nonhunting: /ˌnɑːnˈhʌntɪŋ/ | adj. | 不狩猎的；非狩猎的 | Not involving or related to hunting.  This adjective describes something or someone that is not engaged in or associated with the activity of hunting.  It emphasizes the absence of hunting. |

例句：

The nonhunting areas are protected. = 不狩猎的区域受到保护。

Nonhunting regulations are in place to protect wildlife. =  为了保护野生动物，已经制定了禁止狩猎的法规。


近义词：unhunting (less common), non-venatorial


反义词：hunting


词性：形容词
