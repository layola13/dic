self-service:/ˈsɛlfˈsɜːrvɪs/|/ˈsɛlfˈsɜːrvɪs/| n. |自助服务；自助式|  A system or process in which customers serve themselves without the assistance of staff.  This can refer to various settings, such as grocery stores, gas stations, or online platforms.| 例句：Many supermarkets now offer self-service checkouts. 许多超市现在提供自助结账服务。| 例句：The airport has self-service kiosks for checking in. 机场设有自助值机亭。|近义词：self-help, automated service |反义词：full-service, attended service |名词
