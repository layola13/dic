revamp:/ˌriːˈvæmp/| vt.|彻底改造，改进|To renovate or improve something, typically by making major changes.|例句：The company decided to revamp its image after a series of scandals. 公司在一系列丑闻后决定彻底改造其形象。|例句：They revamped the old building, giving it a modern look. 他们彻底改造了旧建筑，使之焕然一新。|近义词：renovate, overhaul, modernize, update|反义词：deteriorate, ruin, damage|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They revamp their marketing strategy every year. = 他们每年都会改进他们的营销策略。
We revamp the website regularly to improve user experience. = 我们定期改进网站以改善用户体验。


一般过去时:
The designer revamped the dress, adding some lace. = 设计师重新设计了这件裙子，加了一些蕾丝。
She revamped her resume before applying for the job. = 她申请工作之前修改了她的简历。


现在完成时:
They have revamped their product line to better meet customer needs. = 他们已经改进他们的产品线以更好地满足客户需求。
The company has revamped its website several times this year. = 公司今年已经几次更新了他们的网站。


过去完成时:
They had revamped the system before the new data arrived. = 新数据到达之前，他们已经改造了系统。
She had revamped her presentation before giving it. = 她演讲之前已经修改了她的演示文稿。


将来时:
We will revamp the kitchen next month. = 我们下个月将改造厨房。
The government will revamp the healthcare system. = 政府将改革医疗系统。


虚拟语气:
If they revamped the software, it would be more user-friendly. = 如果他们改进软件，它将会更用户友好。
I wish they had revamped the website sooner. = 我希望他们早点改进网站。


被动语态:
The old building is being revamped. = 旧建筑正在被改造。
The software will be revamped next year. = 软件明年将被改进。


疑问句:

Have they revamped the marketing strategy yet? = 他们已经改进营销策略了吗？


否定句:
They didn't revamp the website on time. = 他们没有按时改进网站。
She hasn't revamped her portfolio yet. = 她还没有修改她的作品集。
