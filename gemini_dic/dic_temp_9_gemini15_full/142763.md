ottomanism:/ˈɒtəmənɪzəm/ | n. |奥斯曼主义；奥图曼帝国主义|The political ideology and practice of the Ottoman Empire, aiming to unify and expand its territories and influence under a shared Ottoman identity.  It often involved the suppression of other cultures and religions within the empire.|Example:  The rise of Ottomanism in the 19th century led to significant reforms and attempts at modernization, but also increased tensions with nationalist movements.奥斯曼主义在19世纪的兴起导致了重大的改革和现代化尝试，但也加剧了与民族主义运动的紧张关系。|近义词：Pan-Ottomanism, Ottoman imperialism|反义词：Nationalism (within the Ottoman Empire),  Secessionism|名词
