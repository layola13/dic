scenting:/ˈsɛntɪŋ/| v.|嗅闻；察觉；散发香味 |The act of smelling or detecting smells; also, the act of emitting a scent.|例句：The dog was scenting the trail.  这只狗正在追踪气味。The air was scenting of roses. 空气中散发着玫瑰的香味。|近义词：sniffing, smelling, detecting|反义词： ignoring, disregarding

She scents the flowers. 她闻着花。
