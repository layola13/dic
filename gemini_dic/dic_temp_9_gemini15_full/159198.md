podunk:/ˈpɑːdʌŋk/| n. |偏僻小镇；穷乡僻壤|A podunk is a small, insignificant, and often isolated town, typically characterized by its lack of cultural or economic importance.  The term often carries a slightly derogatory connotation, suggesting backwardness or lack of sophistication.|例句：He grew up in a podunk town in the middle of nowhere. 他在一个偏僻的小镇长大。|近义词：backwater, hick town, one-horse town |反义词：metropolis, big city
