overtimes:/ˈoʊvərtaɪmz/| n.|加班时间；超时工作|Extra time worked beyond one's normal working hours.  It often refers to the hours worked and can also refer to the pay received for those hours.|例句：He worked many overtimes to finish the project. 他加班很多时间来完成项目。|例句：She received extra pay for her overtimes. 她因为加班获得了额外报酬。|近义词：extra hours, overtime pay, extra shifts|反义词：regular hours, standard working hours|名词
