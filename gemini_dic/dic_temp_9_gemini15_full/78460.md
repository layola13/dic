getting:/ˈɡɛtɪŋ/ | v. | 获得；正在；变得 |  The word "getting" is the present participle of the verb "to get." It has various meanings depending on the context, often implying a process or a gradual change.  | 例句：I'm getting ready for the party. = 我正在为派对做准备。 | 近义词：obtaining, acquiring, becoming | 反义词：losing, giving up


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am getting better. = 我正在好转。
He is getting old. = 他正在变老。


一般过去时:
I was getting tired. = 我当时越来越累了。
She was getting ready for bed. = 她当时正在准备睡觉。


现在完成时:
I have been getting headaches lately. = 我最近一直头痛。
They have been getting along well. = 他们相处得很好。


过去完成时:
I had been getting ready for hours. = 我已经准备了好几个小时了。
She had been getting increasingly anxious. = 她越来越焦虑了。


将来时:
I will be getting a new car next month. = 我下个月将买一辆新车。
We will be getting married in the spring. = 我们将在春天结婚。


虚拟语气:
If I were getting paid more, I would be happier. = 如果我的工资更高，我会更快乐。
I wish I were getting enough sleep. = 我希望我能睡够觉。


被动语态:
The work is getting done slowly. = 工作进展缓慢。
The house is getting painted. = 房子正在粉刷。


疑问句:
Are you getting enough exercise? = 你有足够的运动吗？
Is he getting the help he needs? = 他得到了他需要的帮助吗？


否定句:
I am not getting anywhere with this project. = 我在这个项目上毫无进展。
She is not getting the support she needs. = 她没有得到她需要的支持。


PHRASE->

getting started: 开始
getting old: 变老
