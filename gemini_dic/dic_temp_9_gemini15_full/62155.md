enclosing: /ɪnˈkloʊzɪŋ/ | v. adj. | 包围；封闭；附上；包含 | To surround or shut in on all sides; to include or contain within; to put something inside an envelope or package. |

例句：

The police enclosed the crime scene. = 警察封锁了犯罪现场。

The letter enclosed a check. = 信中附有一张支票。


近义词：surrounding, enclosing, containing, including, encompassing

反义词：opening, exposing, excluding


词性:动词和形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He encloses the garden with a fence. = 他用篱笆围住花园。
The package encloses a valuable gift. = 包裹里装着一件贵重的礼物。

一般过去时:
She enclosed the document in the envelope. = 她把文件装进信封。
They enclosed the area with barbed wire. = 他们用铁丝网围住了该区域。

现在完成时:
He has enclosed the property with a wall. = 他已经用墙把房产围起来了。
She has enclosed all the necessary documents. = 她已经附上了所有必要的证件。

过去完成时:
They had enclosed the park before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经封锁了公园。
He had enclosed the money in a separate envelope. = 他已经把钱装在一个单独的信封里了。

将来时:
She will enclose the photographs with her letter. = 她会把照片和信一起寄出。
They will enclose the area for safety reasons. = 出于安全原因，他们将会封锁该区域。

虚拟语气:
If I had enclosed the instructions, he would have understood. = 如果我附上说明书的话，他就会明白了。
I wish I had enclosed my passport in my luggage. = 我真希望我把护照装进行李里了。

被动语态:
The area was enclosed by a high fence. = 该区域被高高的篱笆围了起来。
The documents were enclosed with the application form. = 文件已与申请表一起附上。

疑问句:
Did you enclose the cheque with your application? = 你在申请书里附上支票了吗？
Is the area enclosed by a wall? = 该区域是否被墙围起来？

否定句:
He did not enclose the necessary documents. = 他没有附上必要的证件。
The package did not enclose anything valuable. = 包裹里没有任何贵重物品。


PHRASE->
enclosing document  = 附带文件
enclosing material = 附带材料
enclosing letter = 附信
enclosing area = 围合区域
enclosing wall = 围墙


词源:
The word "enclosing" comes from the Old French word "enclos," meaning "enclosed, surrounded."  This ultimately derives from the Latin prefix "in-" (in, into) and the past participle of "claudere" (to shut, close).
