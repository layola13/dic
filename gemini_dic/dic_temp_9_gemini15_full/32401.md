chilliness:/ˈtʃɪlɪnəs/| n. |寒冷；凉意；冷淡|A feeling or state of being cold; coldness.  It can also refer to a lack of warmth in interpersonal relationships.|例句：The chilliness in the air made me put on my jacket.  空气中的寒冷让我穿上了夹克。

例句：There was a noticeable chilliness in her voice as she spoke to him. 她对他说话时，声音中明显带着冷淡。|近义词：coldness, coolness, frigidity|反义词：warmth, heat, cordiality
