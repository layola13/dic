excommunicated:/ˌɛksˈkɒmjʊneɪtɪd/ | v. |逐出教籍，开除教籍 | to officially exclude someone from a religious community, especially the Christian Church.  |The priest excommunicated him for heresy.  牧师因其异端邪说而将其逐出教籍。 |近义词：banish, oust, expel |反义词：reinstate, welcome


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The church excommunicates members who violate its rules. 教会开除违反规章制度的成员。
He excommunicates anyone who disagrees with him. 他开除任何与他意见相左的人。


一般过去时:
The bishop excommunicated the heretic. 主教开除了那个异教徒。
They were excommunicated for their beliefs. 他们因为信仰而被逐出教籍。


现在完成时:
The council has excommunicated several prominent figures. 理事会已经开除了几位知名人士。
She has been excommunicated for years. 她已经被逐出教籍多年了。


过去完成时:
He had been excommunicated before he even realized his mistake.  在他意识到自己的错误之前，他已经遭到逐出教籍了。
The court had already excommunicated him by the time the appeal was made.  在上诉的时候，法院已经将他逐出教籍了。


将来时:
The church will excommunicate him if he continues to preach his unorthodox views. 如果他继续宣扬他的非正统观点，教会将会将他逐出教籍。
They will be excommunicated if they refuse to repent. 如果他们拒绝悔改，他们将会被逐出教籍。


被动语态:
He was excommunicated for his controversial teachings. 他因其有争议的教义而被逐出教籍。
Several members were excommunicated last year. 去年有几名成员被逐出教籍。


疑问句:
Was he excommunicated for his actions? 他因为他的行为而被逐出教籍了吗？
Will she be excommunicated for her beliefs? 她会因为她的信仰而被逐出教籍吗？


否定句:
The church did not excommunicate him despite his offenses.  尽管他犯了罪，教会也没有将他逐出教籍。
They were not excommunicated, only reprimanded. 他们没有被逐出教籍，只是受到了训斥。



PHRASE->

There are no common idiomatic phrases directly using "excommunicated".  However, related phrases could include:


cast out (逐出):  The devil cast out the demons. 魔鬼赶走了恶魔。(This is a metaphorical use, similar in concept)
thrown out (赶出去):  He was thrown out of the meeting. 他被赶出了会议。(This is a more general expulsion)
ostracized (被排斥):  She was ostracized by her community. 她被她的社区排斥了。(This refers to social exclusion)
blacklisted (被列入黑名单): He was blacklisted after the scandal. 丑闻之后，他被列入了黑名单。(This implies official exclusion from a group or opportunity)
under censure (受到谴责): The politician was under censure for his remarks. 这位政治家因为他的言论而受到谴责。(This is a milder form of exclusion, but signifies disapproval)
