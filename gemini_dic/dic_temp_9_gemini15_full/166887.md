prestamped:/ˌpriːˈstæmpt/| adj. |预先盖印的；预付邮资的 |Already having a stamp affixed; having postage prepaid.  |例句：I sent the letter using a prestamped envelope. 我用一个预付邮资的信封寄了这封信。


例句：The postcards were prestamped for convenience. 为了方便，明信片是预先盖好邮票的。


近义词：prepaid, stamped in advance

反义词：unstamped, unpaid
