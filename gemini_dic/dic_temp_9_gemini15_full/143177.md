outclimbs:/ˌaʊtˈklaɪmz/| vt.|超越，攀登得更高|To surpass in climbing; to climb higher than.|例句：The experienced climber outclimbed all the others and reached the summit first.  经验丰富的登山者超越了其他人，第一个到达了山顶。|例句：The ambitious young entrepreneur outclimbed his competitors in the fiercely competitive market. 野心勃勃的年轻企业家在竞争激烈的市场上超越了他的竞争对手。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip|反义词：underclimb (not a common word, implies failing to climb as high)|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outclimbs his rivals in every competition. = 他在每次比赛中都超越他的对手。
The mountain goat outclimbs the sheep with ease. = 山羊轻松地超越了绵羊。


一般过去时:
She outclimbed her brother to the top of the tree. = 她爬到树顶，超过了她哥哥。
The team outclimbed their previous best time on the rock face. = 团队在岩壁上的攀登时间超过了之前的最好成绩。


现在完成时:
He has outclimbed every other climber in the region. = 他已经超越了该地区所有其他登山者。
The expedition has outclimbed all expectations, reaching a new altitude record. = 探险队已经超越了所有预期，达到了新的海拔记录。


过去完成时:
They had outclimbed the difficult section before nightfall. = 他们在夜幕降临前已经攀越了那段艰难的路程。
The climbers had outclimbed their previous record by several hundred feet. = 登山者们已经比之前的记录高出几百英尺。


将来时:
She will outclimb her opponents in the upcoming climbing competition. = 她将在即将到来的攀岩比赛中超越对手。
The team believes they will outclimb the current record holder. = 该团队相信他们将超越目前的纪录保持者。


虚拟语气:
If only I could outclimb this mountain, I would feel so accomplished. = 如果我能攀登这座山，我会感到非常有成就感。
I wish I had the strength to outclimb the sheer cliff face. = 我希望我有足够的力量去攀登陡峭的悬崖。


被动语态:
The record was outclimbed by a young climber last year. = 去年这个记录被一位年轻的登山者超越了。
The previous best time on the route has been outclimbed. = 路线的先前最佳时间已被超越。


疑问句:
Can he outclimb the challenging cliff? = 他能攀登那座充满挑战的悬崖吗？
Will they be able to outclimb the experienced team? = 他们能够超越经验丰富的团队吗？


否定句:
He couldn't outclimb the experienced climber. = 他无法超越经验丰富的登山者。
They failed to outclimb the difficult section of the mountain. = 他们未能攀越山区的那段险峻路程。


PHRASE->
outclimb the competition = 超越竞争对手
outclimb the mountain = 攀登高峰/超越困难
outclimb one's fears = 战胜恐惧
outclimb expectations = 超出预期
outclimb the odds = 克服困难
