roarings:/ˈrɔːrɪŋz/| n.|轰鸣声，咆哮声|The loud, deep sounds made by something like an animal or engine.|例句：The roarings of the lion echoed through the jungle.  狮子的咆哮声在丛林中回荡。|例句：The roarings of the motorcycle were deafening.  摩托车的轰鸣声震耳欲聋。|近义词：bellowing, rumbling, thundering|反义词：whispering, murmuring|名词
