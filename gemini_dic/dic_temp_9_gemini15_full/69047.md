词源:
The word "fauchion" comes from the Old French word "fauscion,"  which in turn likely derives from a Vulgar Latin word, possibly related to the Latin word "falx" meaning "sickle" or "hooked blade".  The evolution reflects the curved shape characteristic of the weapon.
