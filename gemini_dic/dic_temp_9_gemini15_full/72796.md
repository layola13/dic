foists:/fɔɪst/| vt.|强迫接受；硬塞；欺骗性地推销|To force (something unwelcome or objectionable) on someone.|例句：He foisted the unwanted task onto his assistant. 他把那件不愿意做的任务强加给了他的助手。|例句：They tried to foist substandard goods on unsuspecting customers. 他们试图将劣质商品卖给毫无戒心的顾客。|近义词：impose, thrust, palm off|反义词：offer willingly, present graciously


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He foists his opinions on everyone. = 他把他的意见强加于每个人。
They foist fake products onto the market. = 他们把假货推销到市场上。

一般过去时:
She foisted the responsibility onto me. = 她把责任推卸给了我。
The salesman foisted a faulty washing machine on the customer. = 销售员把一台有故障的洗衣机卖给了顾客。

现在完成时:
He has foisted his beliefs on his children for years. = 多年来，他一直把他的信仰强加给他的孩子们。
They have foisted this problem onto us without warning. = 他们毫无预兆地把这个问题强加给了我们。

过去完成时:
She had foisted the blame onto her colleague before the meeting. = 会议之前，她已经把责任推卸给了她的同事。
They had foisted a damaged car on the buyer before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前就把一辆损坏的汽车卖给了买家。

将来时:
He will foist his ideas on the committee. = 他将会把他的想法强加于委员会。
They will attempt to foist their agenda on the public. = 他们将会试图把他们的议程强加于公众。

虚拟语气:
If he foisted the responsibility on me, I would refuse. = 如果他把责任推卸给我，我会拒绝。
I wish he hadn't foisted that old car on me. = 我真希望他没把那辆旧车卖给我。

被动语态:
The responsibility was foisted upon the new employee. = 责任被强加给了新员工。
The fake paintings were foisted upon the art collector. = 那些假画被卖给了艺术收藏家。

疑问句:
Was the responsibility foisted on you unfairly? = 责任是不是被不公平地强加于你了？
Are they trying to foist these substandard products on consumers? = 他们试图把这些劣质产品卖给消费者吗？

否定句:
He didn't foist the blame on anyone else. = 他没有把责任推卸给任何人。
They didn't try to foist their opinions on us. = 他们没有试图把他们的意见强加于我们。


PHRASE->
foist something on/upon someone = 把某事强加于某人
foist oneself on someone = 向某人献殷勤（通常是不受欢迎的）
foist a lie on someone = 向某人撒谎
foist a fake on someone = 向某人兜售赝品
foist a responsibility on someone = 把责任推卸给某人
