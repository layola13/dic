ptarmigan:/ˈtɑːrmɪɡən/| n. |雷鸟|A type of bird found in arctic and mountainous regions, characterized by its feathered feet and seasonal changes in plumage.  Ptarmigans are ground-dwelling birds that blend well with their surroundings for camouflage.  They are primarily herbivores, feeding on various plants and berries. |例句：The ptarmigan's white winter plumage provides excellent camouflage in the snow. 雷鸟白色的冬羽在雪地里提供了极好的伪装。

The ptarmigan's diet consists mainly of berries and shoots in the summer.  雷鸟的饮食在夏天主要由浆果和嫩芽组成。|近义词：grouse (a broader category)|反义词：None specifically, but birds of different habitats could be considered contrasting.|词性:名词
