reharmonizes:/ˌriːˈhɑːrmənaɪzɪz/ | v. | 再和声化；重新编配和弦 | To change the harmony of a musical piece, typically by adding or substituting chords.  It involves altering the underlying chord progression to create a new harmonic context while maintaining the melody. |例句：The jazz musician reharmonized the standard ballad, giving it a fresh, modern sound.  爵士乐音乐家重新编配了这首标准的抒情歌，赋予了它一种清新现代的声音。

The composer reharmonized the piece to create a more dramatic effect. 作曲家重新编配了这首乐曲，以营造更戏剧性的效果。


近义词：rearrange, re-orchestrate, re-voice | 反义词：leave unchanged, maintain original harmony | 动词
