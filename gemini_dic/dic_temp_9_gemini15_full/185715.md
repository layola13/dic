It can also refer to a biological phenomenon where an organism exhibits traits of a more distant ancestor.  In law, it refers to a person who has the right to regain ownership of property under certain conditions. |例句：The software reverters quickly fixed the bug.  软件复原者迅速修复了这个错误。|例句：Genetic reverters showed characteristics of their great-grandparents. 遗传返祖现象显示了其曾祖父母的特征。|近义词：restorers, revertants,  reversers|反义词：progressors, developers
