partially:/ˈpɑːrʃəli/ /ˈpɑːrʃəli/| adj. |部分地，不完全地|Partially means to some extent, but not completely. It indicates incompleteness or imperfection.  The word implies that something is only done or achieved in part.|


例句：

The project is only partially completed.  = 项目只完成了一部分。

The window was partially open. = 窗户半开着。


近义词：partly, incompletely, in part


反义词：completely, totally, entirely, fully


词性:形容词
