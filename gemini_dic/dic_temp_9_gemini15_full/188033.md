roughhouse: /ˈrʌfˌhaʊs/ | v. n. | 粗暴地玩耍；打闹；喧闹；粗制滥造 |

英文解析:To engage in boisterous and often rough play; to create something in a careless and hasty manner.  As a noun, it refers to boisterous play or a place of such activity.


例句:

动词用法：

The children were roughhousing in the backyard. = 孩子们在后院打闹。

They roughhoused until they were exhausted. = 他们打闹直到筋疲力尽。


名词用法：

There was a lot of roughhousing going on at the party. = 派对上有很多打闹。

The film set was a scene of constant roughhousing. = 电影片场一直都在喧闹。


近义词:  tumble, wrestle, brawl (v.), horseplay,  rough-and-tumble (n.)

反义词:  gentle play, refined behavior, meticulous work


词性: Verb, Noun
