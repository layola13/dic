preconfirm:/ˌpriːkənˈfɜːrm/| vt.|预先确认，事先确认|To confirm something in advance.  It implies a double-checking process before final confirmation.  This word is often used in business and formal contexts.|例句：We need to preconfirm the flight bookings before the deadline. 我们需要在截止日期前预先确认航班预订。

例句：The hotel asked us to preconfirm our reservation by email.  酒店要求我们通过电子邮件预先确认我们的预订。


近义词：confirm beforehand, verify in advance, check in advance |反义词：cancel, deny


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They preconfirm all bookings before sending out tickets. = 他们在发出机票之前预先确认所有预订。
We preconfirm the meeting time with all attendees. = 我们与所有与会者预先确认会议时间。


一般过去时:
The client preconfirmed the contract details yesterday. = 客户昨天预先确认了合同细节。
She preconfirmed her attendance at the conference last week. = 她上周预先确认了参加会议。


现在完成时:
They have preconfirmed the shipment details. = 他们已经预先确认了发货详情。
We have preconfirmed the hotel reservation for our trip. = 我们已经预先确认了我们旅行的酒店预订。


过去完成时:
He had preconfirmed his appointment before leaving for the meeting. = 他在去开会之前已经预先确认了他的预约。
They had preconfirmed the speaker's availability before announcing the event. = 他们在宣布活动之前已经预先确认了演讲人的时间安排。


将来时:
They will preconfirm the delivery date with the supplier. = 他们将与供应商预先确认交货日期。
We will preconfirm the guest list before sending out invitations. = 我们将在发出邀请函之前预先确认宾客名单。


虚拟语气:
If we preconfirmed the order earlier, we wouldn't have this problem now. = 如果我们早点预先确认订单，现在就不会有这个问题了。
I wish we had preconfirmed the venue before booking the caterers. = 我希望我们在预订餐饮服务之前已经预先确认了场地。


被动语态:
The reservation was preconfirmed by the customer service. = 预订已由客户服务部门预先确认。
The delivery date has been preconfirmed by the logistics team. = 交货日期已由物流团队预先确认。


疑问句:
Has the shipment been preconfirmed? = 货物是否已预先确认？
Did they preconfirm the meeting location? = 他们是否预先确认了会议地点？


否定句:
They did not preconfirm the reservation. = 他们没有预先确认预订。
We haven't preconfirmed the flight yet. = 我们还没有预先确认航班。
