collectivistically:/ˌkəˌlɛktɪˈvɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 集体主义地；集体地 | In a collectivist manner; emphasizing the importance of the group over the individual.  | 例句：The community functioned collectivistically, prioritizing shared needs over individual desires.  这个社区以集体主义的方式运作，优先考虑共享需求而非个人愿望。


例句：The company's decisions were made collectivistically, with input from all employees. 公司的决策是集体做出的，所有员工都参与了。


近义词：communally, cooperatively, collaboratively


反义词：individualistically, egoistically, selfishly


词性：副词
