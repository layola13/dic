puissance:/pwiˈsɑːns/| n. |力量；威力；实力|Power; might; strength; capability.  It refers to the inherent ability or capacity to exert influence or control.  It can denote military strength, political power, or personal ability.|例句：The puissance of the Roman Empire was unmatched in its time. 罗马帝国的强大在当时是无与伦比的。

The puissance of his argument convinced the jury. 他论证的力量说服了陪审团。


近义词：power, might, strength, potency, force, capability


反义词：weakness, impotence, feebleness


词性：名词
