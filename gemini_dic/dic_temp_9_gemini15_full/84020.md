half-drunk: /ˌhɑːfˈdrʌŋk/ | adj. | 半醉的，喝了一半的 | Partially intoxicated; containing half the expected amount of liquid. |

例句：

He was half-drunk and singing loudly. 他半醉了，大声唱歌。

The glass was half-drunk, indicating someone had started but not finished their drink.  酒杯喝了一半，说明有人开始喝但没有喝完。


近义词：tipsy, slightly intoxicated, half-empty (referring to a container)


反义词：sober, completely drunk, full (referring to a container)


词源:

half- + drunk.  "Half" is from Old English *healf,  meaning "half". "Drunk" is the past participle of "drink", tracing back to Old English *drincan.
