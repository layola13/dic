self-sought: /ˌselfˈsɔːt/ | adj. | 自寻的；自行寻求的 |  Something that is self-sought is found or achieved through one's own efforts, without external help or guidance.  It emphasizes the independent and proactive nature of the acquisition or discovery. |


例句：

His self-sought exile was a way to escape the pressures of his family.  他的自我放逐是为了逃避家庭压力。

She found solace in self-sought solitude after the breakup. 分手后，她在自寻的孤独中找到了慰藉。


近义词：self-acquired, independently obtained, self-discovered


反义词：imposed, externally driven, given


词性：形容词
