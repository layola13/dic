megatons:/ˈmɛɡətɑːnz/| n. |百万吨（TNT当量）|A megaton is a unit of explosive power equal to one million tons of TNT.  It's commonly used to measure the explosive yield of nuclear weapons.|The bomb had a yield of 10 megatons. 这枚炸弹的当量为10百万吨。|kilotons,  gigatons|milligrams, grams|名词


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't have verb, adjective, or adverb forms in typical usage.  Therefore, grammatical tenses and voices are not applicable in the same way as with verbs.  The word itself describes a quantity, and its use in sentences follows standard noun phrasing.


例句1：The explosion was equivalent to several megatons of TNT.  爆炸相当于几百万吨TNT当量。

例句2：Scientists measured the impact of the asteroid as equivalent to hundreds of megatons. 科学家测量到小行星的撞击相当于数百兆吨TNT当量。
