It suggests a similarity that is superficial or ultimately untrue. |The painting bore a striking missemblance to the original, but closer inspection revealed it to be a forgery. 这幅画与原作惊人地相似，但仔细检查后发现它是赝品。|近义词：semblance, similitude, likeness, resemblance |反义词：dissimilarity, difference, contrast |名词
