recount:/rɪˈkaʊnt/| vt.|复述，叙述，详细讲述|To tell or describe something again, especially in detail.|He recounted his adventures in the Amazon. 他详细讲述了他亚马逊河的冒险经历。|relate, narrate, describe|forget, omit|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recounts his childhood memories often. = 他经常讲述他童年的回忆。
She recounts the story with great enthusiasm. = 她充满热情地讲述了这个故事。

一般过去时:
He recounted the events of the day to his wife. = 他向妻子复述了当天发生的事情。
She recounted her trip to Paris to her friends. = 她向朋友们讲述了她去巴黎的旅行。

现在完成时:
He has recounted this story many times. = 他已经多次讲述过这个故事。
She has recounted the details of the accident to the police. = 她已经向警方详细讲述了事故的经过。

过去完成时:
He had recounted the story before anyone else could. = 他在其他人之前就讲述了这个故事。
She had recounted the events leading up to the incident. = 她已经讲述了导致该事件发生的经过。

将来时:
He will recount his experiences in his upcoming book. = 他将在即将出版的书中讲述他的经历。
She will recount the details of the meeting to her boss. = 她将向老板详细汇报会议内容。

虚拟语气:
If he recounted the truth, people might believe him. = 如果他讲述真相，人们可能会相信他。
I wish she had recounted everything honestly. = 我希望她能诚实地讲述一切。

被动语态:
The story was recounted by several witnesses. = 这个故事被几位证人讲述过。
The events of the war were recounted in the history book. = 战争的事件记载在这本历史书中。

疑问句:
Will he recount the whole story? = 他会把整个故事都讲出来吗？
Did she recount the details accurately? = 她是否准确地复述了细节？

否定句:
He did not recount the entire incident. = 他没有讲述整个事件。
She refused to recount what she had seen. = 她拒绝讲述她所看到的一切。


PHRASE->
recount the events = 叙述事件
recount a story = 讲述一个故事
recount an experience = 讲述一次经历
recount the details = 详细讲述
recount in detail = 详细叙述
