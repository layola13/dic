mecca:/ˈmɛkə/| n. |麦加，圣地，中心 |A city in western Saudi Arabia; the holiest city of Islam.  It is the birthplace of the Prophet Muhammad and the religious center of the Muslim world. Mecca is a significant pilgrimage site, and millions of Muslims travel there each year to perform the Hajj. | Example: Millions of Muslims make a pilgrimage to Mecca each year.  数百万穆斯林每年都去麦加朝圣。|近义词：holy city, center, hub |反义词：periphery, outskirts
