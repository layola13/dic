rheumatismal:/ruːˈmætɪz(ə)m(əl)/| adj.|风湿性的，类风湿的|Relating to or characteristic of rheumatism.  The term often describes conditions involving inflammation of the joints, muscles, or connective tissues.|例句：He suffered from rheumatismal pain in his knees. 他膝盖患有风湿性疼痛。

例句：The doctor diagnosed her condition as rheumatismal arthritis. 医生诊断她的病情为风湿性关节炎。


近义词：rheumatic, arthritic

反义词：None directly opposite, but words suggesting absence of rheumatic conditions could be used (e.g., healthy, sound).


词性：形容词
