choristic: /kəˈrɪstɪk/ | adj. | 异位的；异常的；分离的 | Relating to or characterized by chorism, the separate development of parts or organs of an organism.  It often refers to a condition in which tissues or organs develop in an abnormal location. | 例句：The tumor showed choristic features, indicating its development from misplaced embryonic tissue.  肿瘤表现出异位特征，表明它是由错位的胚胎组织发育而成的。|近义词：ectopic, aberrant, misplaced | 反义词：normal, typical, in situ
