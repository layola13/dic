defiance:/dɪˈfaɪəns/| n.|蔑视；反抗；违抗|The act or an instance of defying;  bold resistance to authority or an opposing force.  It implies a conscious and often openly expressed refusal to obey or comply.|例句：His defiance of the law led to his arrest.  他的蔑视法律的行为导致了他的逮捕。

例句：The protesters showed their defiance of the government by marching in the streets.  抗议者们通过在街上游行来表达他们对政府的反抗。


近义词：resistance, opposition, rebellion, insubordination|反义词：obedience, compliance, submission|词性：名词
