preconferring:/ˌpriːkənˈfɜːrɪŋ/ | v. | 预先协商；预先商议 |  The act of conferring beforehand; discussing or arranging something in advance.  | Example:  Preconferring with the stakeholders ensured a smooth project launch.  预先与利益相关者协商确保项目顺利启动。 |近义词：preconsultation, prior discussion, advance planning | 反义词：post-conference, last-minute arrangement | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They preconfer regularly before major decisions. = 他们在重大决策之前定期预先协商。
We preconfer on project timelines every Monday. = 我们每周一预先协商项目时间表。


一般过去时:
The team preconferred about the budget last week. = 团队上周预先协商了预算。
They preconferred extensively before signing the contract. = 他们在签署合同之前进行了广泛的预先协商。


现在完成时:
We have preconferred with all relevant parties. = 我们已经与所有相关方进行了预先协商。
They have preconferred on the merger details for months. = 他们已经就合并细节进行了几个月的预先协商。


过去完成时:
They had preconferred with the client before presenting the proposal. = 他们在提交提案之前已经与客户进行了预先协商。
The committee had preconferred on the amendments prior to the meeting. = 委员会在会议之前已经预先协商了修正案。


将来时:
We will preconfer with the investors next week. = 我们下周将与投资者进行预先协商。
They will preconfer about the marketing strategy before the launch. = 他们将在发布之前预先协商营销策略。


虚拟语气:
If we had preconferred, we might have avoided the conflict. = 如果我们事先协商过，我们本可以避免冲突。
I wish we had preconferred before making such a hasty decision. = 我希望我们在做出如此仓促的决定之前能够预先协商。


被动语态:
The agenda was preconferred upon by the board members. = 议程已由董事会成员预先协商。
The details were preconferred before the final announcement. = 细节在最终宣布之前已预先协商。


疑问句:
Did they preconfer before implementing the changes? = 他们在实施更改之前是否进行了预先协商？
Have you preconferred with your colleagues about this project? = 你是否已经与你的同事就这个项目进行了预先协商？


否定句:
They did not preconfer before making the decision. = 他们在做决定之前没有进行预先协商。
We have not yet preconferred with the supplier about the delivery date. = 我们尚未与供应商就交货日期进行预先协商。
