rol:/rəʊl/ | n. | 角色；作用；卷；滚动 |  A role is a part played by an actor or a character in a play, film, or story.  It can also refer to a function or position someone or something has.  | 例句：He played the role of Hamlet. 他扮演哈姆雷特的角色。|近义词：part, function, position, duty |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The actor plays a crucial role in the movie. = 这位演员在电影中扮演着至关重要的角色。
This machine plays a vital role in the production line. = 这台机器在生产线上发挥着至关重要的作用。

一般过去时:
She played the leading role in the school play. = 她在那部学校戏剧中扮演了主角。
The wheel played a key role in the development of transportation. = 轮子在交通运输的发展中扮演了关键角色。

现在完成时:
He has played many important roles in his career. = 他在他的职业生涯中扮演过许多重要的角色。
This technology has played a significant role in improving our lives. = 这项技术在改善我们的生活方面发挥了重要作用。

过去完成时:
They had already assigned the roles before the rehearsal started. = 彩排开始之前他们已经分配好角色了。
The invention of the printing press had played a transformative role in the spread of knowledge. = 印刷术的发明在知识传播中发挥了变革性的作用。


将来时:
She will play the role of a detective in the upcoming film. = 她将在即将上映的电影中扮演一名侦探的角色。
AI is predicted to play a more significant role in our daily lives in the future. = 人工智能预计将在未来在我们的日常生活中发挥更重要的作用。


虚拟语气:
If he were to play that role, he would be perfect. = 如果他来扮演那个角色，他会非常完美。
If the company had played a different role, the outcome might have been different. = 如果公司扮演不同的角色，结果可能会有所不同。


被动语态:
The role was played exceptionally well by the actor. = 这个角色被演员出色地诠释了。
A significant role was played by the government in resolving the crisis. = 政府在解决危机中发挥了重要作用。


疑问句:
What role does she play in the company? = 她在公司扮演什么角色？
What role did the environment play in the disaster? = 环境在灾难中扮演了什么角色？


否定句:
He didn't play a significant role in the project. = 他在这个项目中没有扮演重要的角色。
The weather did not play a major role in our decision. = 天气对我们的决定没有起到主要作用。


PHRASE->

have a role to play = 有作用
in a leading role = 在主角位置
a supporting role = 配角
