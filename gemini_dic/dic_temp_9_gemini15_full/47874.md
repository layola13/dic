decelerates:/dɪˈsɛləreɪts/ | v. |减速；使减速 | To reduce the speed of something.  The verb decelerates describes the process of slowing down. It implies a gradual decrease in speed, rather than a sudden stop. |例句：The car decelerates as it approaches the red light.  这辆车在接近红灯时减速了。

The train decelerated smoothly before entering the station. 火车在进站前平稳地减速了。


近义词：slow down, reduce speed, brake


反义词：accelerate, speed up

词源:
The word "decelerates" comes from the Latin prefix "de-" (meaning "down" or "away") and the word "celeritas" (meaning "speed").  The suffix "-ate" indicates a verb. Therefore, "decelerate" literally means "to decrease speed."
