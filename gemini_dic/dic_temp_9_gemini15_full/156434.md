phytoplankton:/ˈfaɪtoʊˌplæŋktən/| n. |浮游植物|Microscopic plants that live in water and are the base of many aquatic food chains.  They are crucial for producing oxygen and forming the base of the marine food web.|Example: Phytoplankton blooms can be seen from space as they color the ocean surface.|近义词：microscopic algae, plant plankton|反义词：zooplankton
