enquiringly: /ɪnˈkwaɪərɪŋli/ | adv. |  探询地；询问地；好奇地 |  In a way that shows curiosity or a desire for information.  | 例句：He looked at me enquiringly. 他好奇地望着我。

She asked enquiringly about his family. 她探询地询问了他的家人。


近义词： inquisitively, curiously, questioningly | 反义词： unconcernedly, indifferently


词性：副词
