producing: /prəˈduːsɪŋ/ | v. | 生产；制造；创作；引起；导致 |  The act of bringing something into existence; creating or making something.  It can refer to physical objects, works of art, or even consequences. |

例句：

The factory is producing cars at a rapid rate. = 工厂正在以很快的速度生产汽车。

This artist is producing a stunning new series of paintings. =这位艺术家正在创作一系列令人惊叹的新画作。


近义词：manufacturing, creating, generating, yielding, causing

反义词：consuming, destroying, ending


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are producing new software. = 他们正在开发新的软件。
The company produces high-quality goods. = 这家公司生产高质量的商品。

一般过去时:
She produced a convincing argument. = 她提出了一个令人信服的论点。
The play was produced on Broadway. = 这部戏在百老汇上演。

现在完成时:
We have produced three reports this month. = 这个月我们已经完成了三份报告。
They have produced a lot of innovative ideas. = 他们已经提出了很多创新的想法。

过去完成时:
The company had produced thousands of units before the factory closed. = 工厂关闭前，该公司已经生产了数千台设备。
He had produced evidence to support his claim. = 他已拿出证据来支持他的说法。

将来时:
We will produce a detailed analysis next week. = 我们下周将进行详细的分析。
The company will produce more electric vehicles in the future. =该公司未来将生产更多电动汽车。


被动语态:
The film was produced by a famous director. = 这部电影是由一位著名导演制作的。
Many products are produced overseas. = 许多产品是在海外生产的。


疑问句:
What are they producing in the lab? = 他们在实验室里生产什么？
Is the company still producing that model? = 公司还在生产那个型号吗？


否定句:
The factory isn't producing enough goods to meet demand. = 工厂生产的商品不足以满足需求。
He didn't produce any evidence to support his theory. = 他没有拿出任何证据来支持他的理论。
