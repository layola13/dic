inflamed:/ɪnˈfleɪmd/ | adj. | 发炎的，红肿的，激动的 |  Describes a body part that is swollen and red because of infection or injury.  It can also describe a situation that is highly charged emotionally. |


例句：
The cut on his knee was inflamed and painful. = 他膝盖上的伤口发炎且疼痛。

His speech inflamed the already tense situation. = 他的讲话激化了本已紧张的局势。


近义词：irritated, swollen, red, angry, aggravated

反义词：uninflamed, calm, soothed


词源:
From Old French *enflamer, from en- + flamer "to flame," from Latin flamma "flame."  The sense development is from the visual similarity of inflamed tissue to flame.
