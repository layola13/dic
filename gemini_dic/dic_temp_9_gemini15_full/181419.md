reiteration:/ˌriːɪtəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 重复，反复；重申 | The act of repeating something; the repetition of something.  It can refer to the act of stating something again or the instance of something being stated again. | Example: The reiteration of the company's mission statement emphasized its core values. 公司使命宣言的重申强调了其核心价值观。

The judge's reiteration of the verdict left no room for doubt.  法官对判决的重申消除了任何疑虑。|近义词：repetition, reiteration, restatement, repetition |反义词：omission, silence


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The teacher reiterates the instructions. = 老师反复强调指示。
He reiterates his point every meeting. = 他每次会议都重申他的观点。


一般过去时:
She reiterated her concerns to the manager. = 她向经理重申了她的担忧。
The speaker reiterated the key findings of the report. = 演讲者重申了报告的主要发现。


现在完成时:
The government has reiterated its commitment to reform. = 政府已重申其改革承诺。
They have reiterated their support for the project. = 他们已重申对该项目的支持。


过去完成时:
He had reiterated his position several times before the vote. = 在投票之前，他已经多次重申了他的立场。
She had reiterated the warning, but they ignored it. = 她已经重申了警告，但他们忽略了。


将来时:
The president will reiterate his policy at the press conference. = 总统将在新闻发布会上重申他的政策。
They will reiterate their demands during the negotiations. = 他们在谈判期间将重申他们的要求。


虚拟语气:
If he reiterated his apology, she might forgive him. = 如果他再次道歉，她可能会原谅他。
I wish he had reiterated his offer. = 我希望他当时重申了他的提议。


被动语态:
The instructions were reiterated by the supervisor. = 指示由主管反复强调。
The company's policy has been reiterated in the new handbook. = 公司政策在新手册中得到了重申。


疑问句:
Will the speaker reiterate his main points? = 演讲者会重申他的要点吗？
Has the government reiterated its stance on the issue? = 政府是否已重申其对该问题的立场？


否定句:
He did not reiterate his request. = 他没有重申他的请求。
The witness did not reiterate her testimony. = 证人没有重申她的证词。



PHRASE->

a reiteration of = 对…的重申
constant reiteration = 不断的重复
without reiteration = 不加重复
needless reiteration = 不必要的重复
repeated reiteration = 反复的重复


词源:
reiterate comes from the Latin word "reiterare," which means "to repeat again."  The prefix "re-" means "again," and "iterare" means "to repeat."
