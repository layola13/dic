developeruniversity:/dɪˈvɛləpərjuːnɪˈvɜːrsɪti/| n. |开发者大学|A developer university is an educational program or initiative offered by a technology company or organization to train and upskill developers in specific technologies or skills.  It typically involves courses, workshops, mentorship, and community building opportunities.  These programs aim to improve the developer ecosystem and create a skilled workforce. |Example: Google's Developer University provides extensive training to software developers worldwide. 谷歌的开发者大学为全球软件开发者提供广泛的培训。|近义词:developer training program, coding bootcamp, tech academy|反义词:None
