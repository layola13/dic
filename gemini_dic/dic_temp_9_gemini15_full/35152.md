cloverleaf:/ˈkloʊvərˌlif/| n. |苜蓿叶形交叉路口；三叶草形|A cloverleaf is a type of highway interchange designed to allow traffic to flow smoothly between two intersecting highways without the need for traffic signals or stop signs.  It's characterized by its clover-like shape, with curving ramps connecting the different roadways. |The cloverleaf interchange allowed drivers to easily switch between the north and south highways.|近义词：interchange, roundabout (in some contexts)|反义词：None specifically, but a simple intersection could be considered an antonym in terms of complexity.
