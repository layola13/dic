intermesenteric:/ˌɪntərˌmɛsəˈtɛrɪk/| adj.|肠系膜间的| Situated between the mesenteries.  The term refers to the anatomical location between the layers of the mesentery, a double layer of peritoneum that connects the intestines to the abdominal wall. | Example: The intermesenteric lymph nodes are crucial for immune function in the intestines.  肠系膜间的淋巴结对肠道的免疫功能至关重要。|近义词：mesenteric (related, but not precisely the same)|反义词：extramesenteric (outside the mesentery)|形容词
