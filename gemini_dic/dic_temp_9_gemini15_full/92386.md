hypocrites:/ˈhɪpəkrɪts/| n. |伪君子，伪善者 |A hypocrite is a person who pretends to have virtues, moral or religious beliefs, principles, etc., that they do not actually possess.  They often act in a way that contradicts their claimed beliefs. |例句：He's a hypocrite; he preaches about honesty but cheats on his taxes. 他是个伪君子；他宣扬诚实，却逃税。|例句：She claims to be a devout Christian, but her actions show her to be a hypocrite. 她自称虔诚的基督徒，但她的行为表明她是个伪君子。|近义词：pretender, deceiver, phony, fraud |反义词：sincere, genuine, honest |名词
