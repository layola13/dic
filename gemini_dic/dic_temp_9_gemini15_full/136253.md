nonrepeater:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈpiː.tə(r)/| n. |非重复者；不重复的装置| A nonrepeater is something that does not repeat or a device that prevents repetition.  This term is often used in technical contexts, such as telecommunications or computing. For example, a nonrepeater cable transmits signals without amplification or regeneration.  |Example: The high-quality nonrepeater cable ensured a clear signal transmission across the long distance.  高品质的无中继电缆确保了长距离清晰的信号传输。|近义词：non-repeating device, single-pass system |反义词：repeater, amplifier
