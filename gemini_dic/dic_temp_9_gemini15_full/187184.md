roamers:/ˈroʊmərz/| n. |漫游者，流浪者 | People or animals that roam freely over a wide area.  They are often characterized by their nomadic lifestyle and lack of a fixed abode. |例句：The wild horses are roamers of the vast plains.  这些野马是广阔平原上的漫游者。

The digital roamers enjoyed the flexibility of their work. 这些数字漫游者享受着工作的灵活 性。|近义词：wanderers, nomads, travelers, drifters |反义词：settlers, residents
