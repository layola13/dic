night-schools:/ˈnaɪtˌskuːlz/ | n. |夜校 |Night schools are educational institutions that offer classes in the evening for students who cannot attend during the day. They provide a flexible learning opportunity for working adults and others with daytime commitments.  |Many adults attend night schools to improve their job skills or pursue further education.|近义词：evening classes, adult education programs |反义词：day schools
