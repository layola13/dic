ecumenisms:/ɪˈkjuːmənɪzəm/| n. |促进基督教各宗派联合的运动；普世主义；（尤指宗教方面的）世界主义|The pursuit of unity among different Christian denominations or churches.  It emphasizes cooperation and understanding between various religious groups, often aiming to overcome historical divisions and promote common goals.  The term can also refer to broader movements promoting unity and understanding among different faiths or worldviews.|例句：The ecumenisms movement gained momentum in the 20th century.  促进基督教各宗派联合的运动在20世纪获得了发展。|近义词：interfaith dialogue, religious unity, universalism|反义词：sectarianism, denominationalism, schism
