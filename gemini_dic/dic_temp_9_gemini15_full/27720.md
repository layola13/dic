canella:/kəˈnɛlə/| n. |香料树,肉桂|A small evergreen tree of the genus *Canella*, native to the West Indies, with aromatic bark and leaves used as a spice.  The bark is sometimes used as a substitute for cinnamon. |Example:  The canella tree's bark is prized for its spicy aroma. 香料树的树皮以其辛辣的香气而闻名。|近义词：cinnamon (though not a perfect substitute)|反义词：无
