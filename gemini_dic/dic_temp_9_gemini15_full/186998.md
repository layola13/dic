risottos:/rɪˈzɒtoʊz/ | n. |意大利烩饭（复数） | Risottos are a type of rice dish originating from Italy, characterized by its creamy texture and the incorporation of various ingredients.  They are typically made with Arborio rice, which has a high starch content, contributing to the creamy consistency. |  We ordered three different risottos: mushroom, seafood, and asparagus. 我们点了三种不同的烩饭：蘑菇、海鲜和芦笋。 The restaurant is famous for its delicious saffron risottos. 这家餐馆以其美味的番红花烩饭而闻名。 |  paellas, pastas |  (无明确反义词，可考虑与清淡的米饭做对比) | 名词
