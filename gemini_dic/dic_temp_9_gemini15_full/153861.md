percher:/ˈpɜːrtʃər/| n. v.|栖息；（鸟）停歇；|to settle or rest, especially on a branch or other elevated place; to place or put something somewhere high up.|The bird perched on the branch.  这只鸟栖息在树枝上。

The artist perched the painting on the easel. 艺术家把画架在画架上。| roost, alight | fall, drop | n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird perches on the branch every morning.  这只鸟每天早上都栖息在树枝上。
He perches himself on the edge of the table. 他把自己栖息在桌子的边缘。


一般过去时:
The eagle perched on a high cliff.  鹰栖息在一座高高的悬崖上。
She perched her bag on the shelf. 她把包放在架子上。


现在完成时:
The sparrow has perched on the wire all day.  麻雀整天都栖息在电线上。
He has perched his hat on his head. 他把帽子戴在了头上。


过去完成时:
The robin had perched there before the storm began.  在暴风雨开始之前，知更鸟就栖息在那里了。
She had perched the vase precariously on the mantelpiece. 她把花瓶不稳定地放在壁炉台上。


将来时:
The owl will perch on the rooftop tonight.  猫头鹰今晚将栖息在屋顶上。
I will perch myself comfortably on the sofa. 我将舒服地坐在沙发上。


虚拟语气:
If the bird perched any higher, it would be in danger. 如果鸟再高一些，它就会有危险。
I wish I could perch on that cloud and watch the world below. 我希望我能坐在那朵云上，俯瞰下面的世界。


被动语态:
The painting was perched on the wall.  画被挂在墙上。
The hat was perched jauntily on his head. 帽子俏皮地戴在他的头上。


疑问句:
Where does the bird perch? 鸟在哪里栖息？
Where did you perch your belongings? 你把你的东西放在哪里？


否定句:
The bird didn't perch on the branch today.  鸟今天没有栖息在树枝上。
He didn't perch the book on the shelf. 他没有把书放在架子上。
