ruler: /ˈruːlər/ | n. |尺子，统治者 | A ruler is an instrument used for measuring length or drawing straight lines; a person who rules or governs.  |例句：She used a ruler to draw a straight line. 她用尺子画了一条直线。|例句：Queen Elizabeth II was a ruler of the United Kingdom.  伊丽莎白二世女王是英国的统治者。|近义词：straight edge, measuring stick, sovereign, monarch |反义词：subject, ruled
