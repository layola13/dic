literary:/ˈlɪtərəri/| adj.|文学的；书面的；文雅的| relating to literature or its study; suggesting an elegant or cultured style.|The novel is a work of literary merit. 这部小说具有文学价值。  The professor delivered a literary lecture. 教授做了一个文学讲座。|近义词：scholarly, literary, intellectual|反义词:unliterary, popular

She reads literary journals. 她阅读文学期刊。
