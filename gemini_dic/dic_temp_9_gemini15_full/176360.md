re-fencing:/ˌriːˈfɛnsɪŋ/| n. |重新围栏；重新修建栅栏 |The act of replacing or repairing existing fences.  It involves removing old fencing materials and installing new ones. This might be due to damage, deterioration, or a desire for a different style of fence. |例句：The old fence was rotten, so we needed re-fencing.  旧栅栏烂了，所以我们需要重新围栏。

The farm requires extensive re-fencing after the storm. 暴风雨过后，农场需要大规模地重新围栏。|近义词：fence repair, fence replacement |反义词：fence removal
