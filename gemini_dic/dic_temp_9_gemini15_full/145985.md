overdog:/ˈoʊvərdɔːɡ/ | n. | 优势者；胜利者；压迫者 | A person or group who holds a dominant or superior position, often at the expense of others.  This term often implies an unfair or unjust advantage.  It is frequently used in a social or political context. | Example: The overdogs of society often ignore the plight of the underdogs. 社会的优势者常常忽视弱势群体的困境。 The company's success made them the overdogs in the market.  公司的成功使他们成为市场上的优势者。| 近义词：winner, victor, dominant group, oppressor | 反义词：underdog, loser, oppressed group | 名词
