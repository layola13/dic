miscellanies:/ˌmɪsɪˈleɪniz/| n.|各种杂项；杂集；文集|A collection of diverse things or writings.|The book contained miscellanies on various subjects, from history to poetry.|近义词：assortment, collection, medley|反义词：uniformity, homogeneity
