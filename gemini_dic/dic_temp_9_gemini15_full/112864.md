lucrative: /ˈluːkrətɪv/ | adj. | 有利可图的，赚钱的 | Profitable; producing wealth.  |例句：The business proved to be highly lucrative. 这项业务证明非常有利可图。

例句：That investment turned out to be incredibly lucrative. 那项投资结果非常有利可图。


近义词：profitable, rewarding, gainful, remunerative, advantageous

反义词：unprofitable, unrewarding, losing, uneconomical
