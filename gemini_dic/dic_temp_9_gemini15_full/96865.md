inexhaustedly:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzɔːstɪdli/ | adv. |不知疲倦地；无穷无尽地 |Continuously and tirelessly; without showing signs of exhaustion.  |例句：He worked inexhaustedly on the project, fueled by his passion. 他不知疲倦地致力于这个项目，动力源于他的热情。

例句：The ocean's resources seem inexhaustedly abundant, but they are not. 海洋资源看似取之不尽，用之不竭，但事实并非如此。


近义词：tirelessly, endlessly, incessantly, perpetually, continuously


反义词：wearily, exhaustedly, languidly, listlessly


词性：副词
