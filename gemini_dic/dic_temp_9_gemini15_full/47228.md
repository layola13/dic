dazing: /ˈdeɪzɪŋ/ | adj. |令人眩晕的；令人震惊的；令人吃惊的 | causing a feeling of being unable to think clearly; astonishing or overwhelming. |例句：The news was absolutely dazzling.  这个消息真是令人震惊。

The dazzling light hurt my eyes.  耀眼的光线刺痛了我的眼睛。


近义词：stunning, breathtaking, overwhelming, astonishing, amazing, shocking |反义词：unimpressive, underwhelming, ordinary, commonplace


USAGE->

The bright light is dazzling. = 耀眼的光线令人目眩。
The beauty of the scene is dazzling. = 景色的美丽令人惊叹。
