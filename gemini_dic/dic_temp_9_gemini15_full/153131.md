pelagian:/pɛˈleɪdʒɪən/| adj.|深海的；远洋的；大洋的 |Relating to or living in the open ocean, far from land.  The term often refers to organisms that live in the pelagic zone, the water column of the ocean. |例句：Pelagic fish are those that live in the open ocean, not near the coast. 深海鱼类是指生活在公海而不是靠近海岸的鱼类。

例句：The study focused on the distribution of pelagic zooplankton in the Pacific Ocean. 这项研究的重点是大西洋中远洋浮游动物的分布。


近义词：oceanic, pelagic zone, open-ocean


反义词：littoral, neritic, coastal


词性:形容词
