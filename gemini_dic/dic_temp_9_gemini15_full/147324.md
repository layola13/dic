overmorrow:/ˈoʊvərmɔːroʊ/ | n. | 后天 | The day after tomorrow.  It refers to the day following the next day.  | Example: I will visit my grandparents the overmorrow. 我后天将去拜访我的祖父母。|近义词：the day after tomorrow |反义词：yesterday, today
