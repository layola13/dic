reconstruct:/ˌriːkənˈstrʌkt/| vt.|重建，改造，复原|To build or create something again, often in a different way or from damaged parts.  It involves putting something back together, often after destruction or damage, or creating a model or representation based on available evidence.|例句：The city is being reconstructed after the earthquake. 地震后，这座城市正在重建。

例句： Historians tried to reconstruct the events leading up to the war. 历史学家试图重建导致战争发生的事件。


近义词：rebuild, restore, reform, remake |反义词：destroy, demolish, raze

词性:动词
