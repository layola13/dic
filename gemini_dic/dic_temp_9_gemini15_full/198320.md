shovelhead:/ˈʃʌvəlhed/| n.|铲斗头；（尤指）哈雷戴维森发动机|A type of V-twin engine, particularly associated with Harley-Davidson motorcycles, characterized by its distinctive cylinder head shape resembling a shovel.  The term can also refer to a person who is considered to be foolish or naive.|例句：His Harley-Davidson boasted a powerful shovelhead engine.他的哈雷戴维森摩托车拥有一个强劲的铲斗头发动机。|例句：Don't be such a shovelhead; think before you act.别那么傻；行动前先想想。|近义词：panhead (for engine type), fool, simpleton|反义词：genius, mastermind
