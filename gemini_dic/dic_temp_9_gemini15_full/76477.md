galactically:/ɡəˈlæktɪkli/| adv.|银河系地；极其地；非常地 | Extremely; on a galactic scale.  The term implies a vastness or scale comparable to a galaxy.  It can be used literally to refer to something happening within or relating to a galaxy, or figuratively to emphasize the immense size or extent of something. | 例句：The empire expanded galactically.  = 这个帝国呈星系般地扩张。

The new technology is galactically advanced. = 这项新技术极其先进。| 近义词：immensely, enormously, vastly, incredibly | 反义词：minimally, insignificantly
