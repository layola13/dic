melodica:/məˈlɒdɪkə/|/ˈmɛlədɪkə/| n. |口风琴；旋律卡|A melodica is a free-reed wind instrument that is played by blowing air through a mouthpiece and pressing keys to produce different notes.  It's often used in music education and sometimes in popular music.|Example: My son is learning to play the melodica in his music class. 我的儿子正在音乐课上学吹口风琴。|近义词：keyboard instrument, harmonica (less accurate)|反义词：None.
