counteracts:/ˈkaʊntərækts/| v.|对抗，抵消，中和 |To act against something in order to reduce its effect.  |例句：The drug counteracts the effects of the poison.  这种药物能抵消毒药的作用。|例句：Regular exercise counteracts the effects of a poor diet.  规律的运动可以抵消不良饮食的影响。|近义词：neutralizes, offsets, combats|反义词：reinforces, strengthens, enhances


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The medicine counteracts the virus. = 药物对抗病毒。
This policy counteracts inflation. = 这项政策对抗通货膨胀。

一般过去时:
The antidote counteracted the poison. = 解毒剂抵消了毒药的作用。
He took steps to counteract the negative effects. = 他采取措施来抵消负面影响。

现在完成时:
The government has counteracted the economic crisis. = 政府已经抵消了经济危机。
She has counteracted many challenges in her life. = 她已经克服了她生命中的许多挑战。

过去完成时:
They had counteracted the threat before it escalated. = 他们在威胁升级之前就将其化解了。
He had counteracted the rumors before they spread widely. = 他在谣言广泛传播之前就将其制止了。

将来时:
We will counteract any attempts to sabotage the project.  我们将对抗任何破坏项目的企图。
The new legislation will counteract discrimination. = 新法案将对抗歧视。


被动语态:
The effects of the drug are counteracted by the antidote. = 药物的作用被解毒剂所抵消。
The negative impact was counteracted by positive measures. = 负面影响被积极措施所抵消。

疑问句:
Does this treatment counteract the symptoms? = 这种治疗方法是否能对抗症状？
How can we counteract the spread of misinformation? = 我们如何才能对抗虚假信息的传播？

否定句:
The medication did not counteract the allergic reaction. = 药物没有对抗过敏反应。
Their efforts did not counteract the damage. = 他们的努力没有抵消损失。


PHRASE->
counteract the effects of = 抵消…的影响
counteract the influence of = 抵消…的影响
counteract the spread of = 抵消…的蔓延
counteract negative effects = 抵消负面影响
counteract a trend = 逆转一种趋势
