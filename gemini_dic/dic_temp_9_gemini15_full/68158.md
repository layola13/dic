fails:/feɪlz/| v.|失败；不及格；衰竭 |to be unsuccessful in achieving or doing something.  It can also refer to a mechanical or system failure. |例句：He fails to understand the instructions. 他不明白这些指示。

例句：The engine failed during the race.  赛车引擎在比赛中失灵了。|近义词：miscarry,  fall short,  break down |反义词：succeed,  triumph,  function

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fails to meet his deadlines. 他总是完不成最后期限。
She fails to see the problem. 她没有看到问题所在。


一般过去时:
The experiment failed. 实验失败了。
My attempt to climb the mountain failed. 我试图攀登那座山失败了。


现在完成时:
He has failed three times. 他已经失败了三次。
The project has failed to meet its goals.  这个项目未能达到其目标。


过去完成时:
The plan had failed before it even started.  计划在开始之前就已经失败了。
They had failed to warn us of the danger. 他们未能警告我们危险。


将来时:
The mission will likely fail without more funding.  没有更多资金，这项任务很可能会失败。
I fear the negotiations will fail. 我担心谈判会失败。


虚拟语气:
If he had studied harder, he wouldn't have failed the exam. 如果他学习更努力，他就不会考试不及格。
I wish I hadn't failed to tell him the truth. 我真希望我没有忘记告诉他真相。


被动语态:
The plan was failed by poor management.  计划因管理不善而失败。
The project was failed due to lack of resources.  项目由于缺乏资源而失败。


疑问句:
Did the operation fail? 手术失败了吗？
Why did your attempt fail? 你的尝试为什么失败了？


否定句:
The system did not fail. 系统没有故障。
I will not fail you. 我不会让你失望的。


PHRASE->
fail to: 未能
fail in: 在...方面失败
fail safe: 安全失效
fail miserably: 惨败
let sb down (fail sb): 使某人失望
