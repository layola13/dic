dynamism: /ˈdaɪnəmɪzəm/ | n. |活力；动力；动势 | The quality of being dynamic; characterized by constant change, activity, or progress.  It refers to the force that produces movement or change. |例句：The dynamism of the city was evident in its bustling streets and vibrant nightlife.  这座城市的活力在它繁华的街道和充满活力的夜生活中显而易见。

The company's success is due to its dynamism and innovative spirit.  公司的成功归功于其活力和创新精神。


近义词：energy, vigor, vitality, force, momentum, drive

反义词：stagnation, inertia, lethargy, passivity, stillness
