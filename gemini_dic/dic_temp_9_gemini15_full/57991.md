drivellers:/ˈdrɪvələrz/| n.|喋喋不休的人，傻瓜，笨蛋|People who talk foolishly and at excessive length; foolish or stupid people.  |例句：He dismissed them as mere drivellers. 他把他们斥为一群喋喋不休的傻瓜。

例句：The drivellers in the pub were arguing about nothing. 酒吧里那些喋喋不休的家伙在为一些鸡毛蒜皮的事争吵。


近义词：babblers, chatterboxes, simpletons, fools, idiots

反义词：intellectuals, geniuses, wise men, sages

词性:名词
