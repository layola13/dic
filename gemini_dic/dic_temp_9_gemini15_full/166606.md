presell: /ˌpriːˈsɛl/ | vt. |预售，先行销售| To sell (a product or service) before it is available for general sale, often to generate interest or secure early adopters.  | 例句：The company chose to presell the new phone to gauge demand.  公司选择预售这款新手机以评估需求。

例句：They successfully pre-sold 10,000 units before the official launch. 他们在正式发布前成功预售了10000台。


近义词：advance sale, presale, early sale


反义词：post-sale, clearance sale


词性：动词
