chunking:/ˈtʃʌŋkɪŋ/| n.| 分块；组块；整体处理 | The process of grouping individual items into larger units, often for easier processing or understanding.  This is commonly used in cognitive psychology, linguistics, and computer science. | Example:  Chunking information into manageable sections helps improve memory recall.  分块信息成易于管理的部分有助于提高记忆力。|近义词：grouping, segmentation, clustering|反义词：atomization, fragmentation|名词
