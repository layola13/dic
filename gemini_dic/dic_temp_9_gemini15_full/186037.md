rewood:/ˈriːwʊd/| n. |再生木材；再造木材|Reclaimed or recycled wood, often used in construction or furniture making.  It is a sustainable alternative to newly harvested wood.| Example: The park benches were made from rewood, giving old lumber a new life. 公园的长椅是用再生木材做的，赋予了旧木材新的生命。|近义词：recycled wood, reclaimed wood, salvaged wood|反义词：new wood, virgin timber|名词
