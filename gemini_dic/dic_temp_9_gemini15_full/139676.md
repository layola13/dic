occurrences:/əˈkʌrənsɪz/ | n. |事件；发生；出现 | Instances of something happening; the fact of something happening.  |例句：The police are investigating the occurrences of vandalism in the area.  警方正在调查该地区发生的破坏事件。

例句：There have been several occurrences of this problem in the past month.  过去一个月里这个问题已经出现过好几次了。

近义词：events, incidents, happenings, instances | 反义词：absence, non-occurrence | 词性：名词
