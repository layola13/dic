pandect:/ˈpændɛkt/| n.|法典；大全；概要|A complete or systematic collection or compendium, especially of laws.  A pandect is a comprehensive digest or systematization of a body of law, often arranged systematically.  |例句：The Justinian Code is a famous example of a pandect.  查士丁尼法典是一个著名的法典例子。

例句：He attempted to create a pandect of all known scientific theories. 他试图创建一个包含所有已知科学理论的大全。


近义词：digest, compendium, synopsis, summary |反义词：fragment, excerpt, detail
