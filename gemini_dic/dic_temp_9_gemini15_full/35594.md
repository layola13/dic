coarsen:/ˈkoʊzən/| vt.|使粗糙；使粗俗；使粗劣|To make or become rough or coarse in texture, appearance, or manner.  The process of making something less refined, smoother, or delicate.|例句：The sandpaper coarsened the wood. 砂纸使木材变粗糙了。

例句：His language coarsened over the years. 他的语言随着岁月的流逝变得粗俗了。


近义词：roughen, worsen, degrade, debase|反义词：refine, smooth, polish, elevate


词性：动词
