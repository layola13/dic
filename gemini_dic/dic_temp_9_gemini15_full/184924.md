/riːˈteɪps/ | v. | 重新录音；重新录像 | To record something again, typically audio or video.  | He retaped the interview because of background noise. 他因为背景噪音而重新录了采访。 | rerecord, re-record |  | v.


USAGE->

He retapes the audio every week. = 他每周都重新录音。
She retapes her lectures for online students. = 她为网上学生重新录制讲座。
