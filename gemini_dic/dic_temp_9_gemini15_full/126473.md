mythologically:/ˌmɪθəˈlɑːdʒɪkli/ | adv. |神话般地，从神话角度 |Adverb describing something in a way that relates to myths or mythology. It suggests a narrative style or interpretation influenced by mythical themes, characters, or events.| Example: The story was mythologically rich, filled with gods, monsters, and epic quests.  这个故事充满了神话色彩，充满了神灵、怪物和史诗般的探险。|近义词：mythically, fabulously, legendarily |反义词：realistically, factually
