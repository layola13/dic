revictualling: /ˌriːˈvɪktʃuəlɪŋ/ | n. |给…补给食物和物资|The act of supplying a ship, army, or other group with food and other necessary provisions.  This term is especially relevant to seafaring and military contexts.  It implies a resupply of essential items beyond just food, encompassing things needed for continued operation. | Example: The ship put into port for revictualling and repairs. 这艘船进港进行补给和修理。|近义词: provisioning, supplying, replenishing |反义词: depleting, exhausting
