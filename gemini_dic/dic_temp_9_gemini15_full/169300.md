promisor:/ˈprɑːmɪsɔːr/|/ˈprɒmɪsə(r)/| n. |承诺人，保证人 |A promisor is a person who makes a promise.  They are bound by the terms of the promise under contract law. |Example: The promisor failed to fulfill their obligations under the contract.  承诺人未能履行合同规定的义务。|近义词：guarantor, obligor |反义词：promisee


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The promisor guarantees the quality of the product. = 承诺人保证产品的质量。
He is the promisor in this agreement. = 他是这份协议中的承诺人。

一般过去时:
The promisor promised to deliver the goods on time. = 承诺人承诺按时交付货物。

现在完成时:
