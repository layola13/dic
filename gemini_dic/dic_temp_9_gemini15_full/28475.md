carcasses:/ˈkɑːrkəsɪz/ | n. |尸体；残骸；（动物的）躯体 |The dead bodies of animals, especially those of large animals that have been killed recently;  a dead body, especially of a large animal.  |例句：The carcasses of sheep littered the hillside after the storm.暴风雨过后，绵羊的尸体散落在山坡上。

例句：Vultures circled the decaying carcasses in the desert.秃鹫在沙漠中盘旋着腐烂的尸体。


近义词：corpses, remains, bodies |反义词：living beings, the living
