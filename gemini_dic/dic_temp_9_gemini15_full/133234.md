nonexclusives:/ˌnɒnɪkˈskluːsɪvz/ | adj. |非排他的；兼有的|  Not limited to one thing or person; including or allowing for more than one.  The term often refers to agreements or relationships that don't preclude other similar agreements or relationships. |例句：The contract is nonexclusive, allowing us to work with other suppliers. 这份合同是非排他的，允许我们与其他供应商合作。

例句：This research grant allows for nonexclusive licensing of the technology. 这项研究经费允许对该技术进行非排他性许可。


近义词：inclusive, open-ended, multiple, shared | 反义词：exclusive, sole, single, limited


词性：形容词
