intracranial:/ˌɪntrəˈkreɪniəl/| adj.|颅内的；脑内的|Relating to the inside of the cranium (the skull).  It refers to structures, processes, or conditions within the skull, especially those affecting the brain.|例句：The patient experienced intracranial bleeding after the accident.  车祸后病人出现了颅内出血。

例句：She suffered from an intracranial hemorrhage. 她遭受了颅内出血。


近义词：intracerebral (if specifically referring to the brain)


反义词：extracranial (outside the skull)


词性：形容词
