scariest: /ˈskɛəriəst/ | adj. |最可怕的，最吓人的 | Most frightening or terrifying.  |例句：That was the scariest movie I've ever seen.  那是我看过的最恐怖的电影。

例句：The scariest moment was when the lights went out.  最可怕的时刻是灯灭的时候。


近义词：most terrifying, most frightening, most dreadful | 反义词：safest, most comforting, most reassuring | 词性：形容词


USAGE->

The clown is the scariest thing in the circus. = 小丑是马戏团里最可怕的东西。
That haunted house is scariest at night. = 那座鬼屋晚上最可怕。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
