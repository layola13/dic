hautein:/ˈoʊteɪn/| adj.|傲慢的，自大的|Haughty describes someone who behaves in an arrogant and superior manner, typically because they believe themselves to be better than other people.  It suggests a disdainful attitude and a lack of consideration for others' feelings.|例句：She treated the shop assistants with a haughty disdain. 她对店员表现出傲慢的轻蔑。

例句：His haughty manner alienated many of his colleagues. 他傲慢的态度疏远了他许多同事。


近义词：arrogant, supercilious, disdainful, condescending, imperious

反义词：humble, modest, unassuming, deferential
