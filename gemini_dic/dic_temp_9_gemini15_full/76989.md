gardener:/ˈɡɑːrdənər/|/ˈɡɑːrdnər/ | n. | 园丁，园艺工人 | A gardener is a person who cultivates and maintains gardens.  They plant, care for, and harvest plants, flowers, and vegetables. |  He is a skilled gardener with a beautiful rose garden. 他是一位技艺精湛的园丁，拥有一个美丽的玫瑰园。
The gardener pruned the roses this morning. 园丁今天早上修剪了玫瑰花。|  horticulturalist, cultivator |  (无明显反义词，可考虑：neglectful person, destroyer of gardens) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gardener tends the flowers every day. = 园丁每天都照顾花朵。
He waters the plants twice a week. = 他每周浇两次水。

一般过去时:
The gardener planted many new trees last spring. = 园丁去年春天种了很多新树。
She pruned the bushes yesterday. = 她昨天修剪了灌木丛。

现在完成时:
The gardener has already harvested the tomatoes. = 园丁已经收割了西红柿。
He has redesigned the garden several times. = 他已经多次重新设计了花园。

过去完成时:
The gardener had finished weeding before the rain started. = 下雨之前，园丁已经除完了草。
She had planted all the seeds before the frost arrived. = 霜冻来临之前，她已经种下了所有的种子。

将来时:
The gardener will plant tulips next autumn. = 园丁将在下一个秋天种植郁金香。
He will fertilize the lawn next week. = 他下周将给草坪施肥。

虚拟语气:
If the gardener had watered the plants, they wouldn't have died. = 如果园丁浇了水，植物就不会死了。
I wish the gardener had pruned the trees earlier. = 我希望园丁早点修剪树木。

被动语态:
The roses were planted by the gardener last year. = 这些玫瑰是园丁去年种的。
The lawn is being mowed by the gardener now. = 草坪现在正在被园丁割草。

疑问句:
Is the gardener available today? = 园丁今天有空吗？
What kind of plants does the gardener grow? = 园丁种植什么类型的植物？

否定句:
The gardener didn't water the plants yesterday. = 园丁昨天没有浇水。
He doesn't use chemical fertilizers. = 他不用化肥。
