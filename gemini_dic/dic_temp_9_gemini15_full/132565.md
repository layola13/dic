nondictation: /ˌnɒnˌdɪkˈteɪʃən/ | n. |非听写；非口述 | The act or process of not dictating;  something that is not dictated.  It emphasizes the absence of dictation. | 例句：The essay was completed through nondictation, showcasing the student's independent writing skills. 这篇文章是通过非听写完成的，展现了学生的独立写作能力。


The use of "nondictation" is relatively limited. It's primarily used in contexts where the emphasis is on the absence of dictation, contrasting it with methods involving dictating text.  There aren't many direct synonyms or antonyms.  The opposite concept would be "dictation" itself or perhaps "oral transcription".
