politicky:/pəˈlɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 政治上地；从政治角度 | In a political manner; relating to politics. |例句：The situation was handled politically rather than practically.  情况是政治性而非实际性地处理的。 |例句：The issue is politically sensitive.  这个问题在政治上很敏感。|近义词： politically,  diplomatically |反义词：impolitically, non-politically
