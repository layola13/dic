outgazing:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪzɪŋ/ | v. |凝视，眺望 | To look out at something with a fixed gaze, often with wonder or contemplation.  |例句：He spent hours outgazing at the vast ocean, lost in thought. 他花了好几个小时凝视着浩瀚的海洋，陷入沉思。|近义词：gaze, stare, contemplate |反义词：ignore, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outgazes the sunset every evening. = 他每天晚上都凝视着日落。
She outgazes at the stars from her balcony. = 她从阳台上凝视着星星。

一般过去时:
They outgazed the mountain range for a long time. = 他们长时间地凝视着山脉。
He outgazed the passing ships from the shore. = 他从岸边凝视着来往的船只。

现在完成时:
I have outgazed the city lights many times. = 我多次凝视着城市灯火。
She has outgazed the ocean until the sun rose. = 她一直凝视着大海直到太阳升起。

过去完成时:
They had outgazed the storm clouds before it began to rain. = 下雨之前，他们凝视着乌云。
She had outgazed the painting for hours before noticing a detail. = 在注意到细节之前，她已经凝视这幅画几个小时了。

将来时:
We will outgaze the aurora borealis tonight. = 今晚我们将凝视北极光。
He will outgaze the cityscape from his new apartment. = 他将从他的新公寓凝视城市景观。

虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would outgaze the stars all night. = 如果我有时间，我会整夜凝视星星。
I wish I could outgaze the sunset from a tropical beach. = 我希望我能从热带海滩凝视日落。

被动语态:  (The verb "outgaze" is rarely used passively.)

疑问句:
Do you often outgaze the landscape? = 你经常凝视风景吗？
Will they outgaze the moon tonight? = 今晚他们会凝视月亮吗？

否定句:
He didn't outgaze the scenery; he was too busy. = 他没有凝视风景；他太忙了。
She wouldn't outgaze the painting for too long. = 她不会长时间凝视那幅画。


PHRASE->

outgazing the vastness = 凝视广阔无垠
outgazing into the distance = 凝视远方
outgazing at the sea = 凝视着大海
outgazing in wonder = 惊奇地凝视
