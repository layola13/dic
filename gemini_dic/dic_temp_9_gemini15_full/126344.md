myrcia:/ˈmɜːrsiə/| n. |桃金娘属植物|A genus of flowering plants in the myrtle family, Myrtaceae.  Many species are shrubs or small trees with fragrant leaves and attractive flowers, often used ornamentally or for their essential oils.  |例句：Many species of *Myrcia* are native to the tropical Americas.  许多桃金娘属植物原产于美洲热带地区。


例句：The essential oil extracted from certain *Myrcia* species is used in perfumes. 从某些桃金娘属植物中提取的精油用于香水。
