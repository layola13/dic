hump:/hʌmp/| n. v.|驼峰；隆起；努力；(非正式)性交|

英文解析:
As a noun, "hump" refers to a rounded protuberance on the back of an animal, like a camel's hump, or a similar raised shape on a surface.  It can also refer to a period of intense effort or difficulty. In informal contexts, it is slang for sexual intercourse. As a verb, "hump" means to carry something heavy or to move something with effort.  It can also be used informally as a synonym for sexual intercourse.

例句:
The camel carried its heavy load on its hump.  = 骆驼背上驮着沉重的货物。

The road had a steep hump in the middle. = 这条路中间有一个陡峭的隆起。

We had to hump all the boxes up the stairs. = 我们不得不费力地把所有箱子搬上楼梯。
