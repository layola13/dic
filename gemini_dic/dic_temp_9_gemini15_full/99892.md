internally:/ɪnˈtɜːrnəli/| adv.|内部地，内在地|From within; in the inner part or parts.|The company is restructuring internally. 公司正在进行内部重组。|externally, outwardly|  


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine runs internally. = 引擎在内部运转。
He thinks internally before speaking. = 他说话前会在内心思考。


一般过去时:
The problem was dealt with internally. = 这个问题在内部得到了解决。
They communicated internally about the project. = 他们就项目进行了内部沟通。


现在完成时:
The company has improved its processes internally. = 公司已经改进其内部流程。
We have resolved the issue internally. = 我们已在内部解决了这个问题。


过去完成时:
They had already discussed the matter internally. = 他们之前已经在内部讨论过此事。
The team had completed the project internally before the deadline. = 团队在截止日期之前已在内部完成了项目。


将来时:
We will address the concerns internally. = 我们将在内部处理这些担忧。
They will investigate the matter internally. = 他们将在内部调查此事。


虚拟语气:
If we handled this internally, it would be more efficient. = 如果我们内部处理此事，效率会更高。
It would be better if they communicated internally first. = 如果他们先进行内部沟通会更好。


被动语态:
The decision was made internally. = 这个决定是在内部做出的。
The issue is being handled internally. = 这个问题正在内部处理中。


疑问句:
Is the problem being addressed internally? = 这个问题是否正在内部得到解决？
Was the decision made internally or externally? = 这个决定是在内部还是外部做出的？


否定句:
The problem wasn't handled internally. = 这个问题没有在内部得到解决。
They didn't communicate internally about the changes. = 他们没有就这些变化进行内部沟通。


PHRASE->
