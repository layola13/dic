shoot-out:/ˈʃuːtˌaʊt/| n.|枪战，激烈的争斗|A shoot-out is a gun battle, typically between law enforcement and criminals, or between rival gangs. It often implies a prolonged and intense exchange of gunfire.|Example: The police were involved in a shoot-out with armed robbers.|近义词：gunfight, firefight|反义词：peace, negotiation
