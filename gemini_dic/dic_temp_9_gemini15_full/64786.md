esling:/ˈɛzlɪŋ/| n. |吊索，吊带|A sling is a loop of rope, chain, or other strong material used to lift or support something heavy.  It is often used in construction, rigging, and other industrial applications. |例句：The workers used a sling to lift the heavy beam into place. 工人们用吊索将沉重的横梁吊放到位。|近义词：hoist, cradle, strap|反义词：N/A
