outnumbering: /ˌaʊtˈnʌmbəriŋ/ | v.ing | 超过；多于 |  The present participle of the verb "outnumber".  It describes a situation where one group or quantity exceeds another in number. |例句：The protesters outnumbered the police. 抗议者的人数超过了警察。

The invading army was vastly outnumbered by the defenders. 入侵军队的人数远远少于防守方。


近义词：surpassing, exceeding, surpassing in number, predominating


反义词：being outnumbered, being outweighed


词性：动名词
