reprofiling:/ˌriːˈproʊfaɪlɪŋ/ | n. |重新设计轮廓；重新设定形象；改进外形 | The act of changing the shape or profile of something, often to improve its performance or appearance.  This can apply to physical objects, like roads or products, or to abstract concepts like a company's image. |例句：The road needs re-profiling to improve safety. 这条路需要重新设计轮廓以提高安全性。

The company underwent a major re-profiling to attract younger customers. 这家公司进行了重大形象改造以吸引年轻客户。|近义词：redesign, reshape, remodel, rebranding |反义词：maintain the status quo, leave unchanged |名词
