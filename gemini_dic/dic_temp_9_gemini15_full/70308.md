fiducial:/ˈfɪdʒuəl/| adj. n.| 可信赖的；基准的；信标；基准点 |Inspiring trust; of or serving as a guide or landmark.  A point, line, or plane used as a reference in measurement or calculation. |例句：The results are based on a fiducial marker. 结果基于一个基准标记。

The new CEO inspired fiducial confidence in the company's future. 新任首席执行官激发了人们对公司未来的信任。


近义词：trustworthy, reliable, dependable, benchmark, reference point

反义词：untrustworthy, unreliable, questionable


词性: adjective, noun
