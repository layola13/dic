self-idolizing: /ˌsɛlf ˈaɪdəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 自我崇拜；自我陶醉 | The act of excessively admiring oneself and one's accomplishments, often to the point of narcissism.  It involves a preoccupation with one's own image and a tendency to inflate one's self-importance. | 例句：His self-idolizing behavior alienated many of his colleagues. 他自我陶醉的行为疏远了他的许多同事。

His constant self-idolizing posts on social media are tiresome. 他在社交媒体上不断发布自我吹嘘的帖子令人厌烦。


近义词：narcissism, self-obsession, vanity, egotism, self-admiration


反义词：self-deprecation, humility, modesty


词性：动词
