rhythms:/ˈrɪðəmz/ | n. | 节奏；韵律；规律性 | The natural, regular patterns of sound, movement, or events.  Rhythms are fundamental to music, poetry, and even daily life. For example, the rhythm of the heartbeat, the rhythm of the tides, or the rhythm of daily routines. |例句：The music had a strong, driving rhythm. 这音乐节奏强劲有力。


例句：The poet uses rhythm and rhyme to create a pleasing effect. 这位诗人运用节奏和韵律创造出一种悦耳的效果。

近义词：beat, cadence, tempo, meter | 反义词：chaos, disorder, irregularity


词性：名词
