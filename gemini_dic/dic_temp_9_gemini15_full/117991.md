memorialization: /ˌməmɔːriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |纪念，纪念活动，不朽化 |The act of commemorating or memorializing someone or something; the process of creating a memorial or lasting record.  It involves preserving the memory of a person, event, or idea through various means, such as monuments, writings, or ceremonies. | Example: The memorialization of the victims of the war took many forms, including a national monument and numerous individual memorials.  战争受害者的纪念活动采取多种形式，包括一座国家纪念碑和许多个人纪念碑。|近义词：commemoration, remembrance, perpetuation |反义词：oblivion, forgetting
