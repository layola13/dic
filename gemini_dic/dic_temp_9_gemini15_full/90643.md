humanizer: /ˈhjuːmənˌaɪzər/ | n. | 使人性化的人或事物 | Something or someone that makes something or someone more human or humane; a device or process that makes something easier for humans to use or understand.  | The new software is a humanizer, making complex data accessible to everyone. 新软件使复杂的数据更容易被所有人理解，它是一个人性化工具。|近义词：humaniser, personalizer, empathizer |反义词：dehumanizer, depersonalizer
