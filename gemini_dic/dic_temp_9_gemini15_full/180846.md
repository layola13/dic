rehearsals:/rɪˈhɜːrsəlz/ | n. | 排练；预演 |  The act of practicing or repeating something, especially a play, concert, or other performance, in preparation for a public performance.  |  The band had several rehearsals before their concert. 乐队在音乐会之前进行了多次排练。 The actors were running through their lines during rehearsals. 演员们在排练中练习台词。 | practices, run-throughs |  (无明确反义词，可考虑"performance"作为对比词，表示最终的演出而非排练) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The band has rehearsals every Tuesday. = 乐队每周二都有排练。
We have rehearsals for the play next week. = 我们下周要为戏剧排练。

一般过去时:
They had rehearsals all day yesterday. = 他们昨天一整天都在排练。
The orchestra had a long rehearsal before the concert. = 乐队在音乐会之前进行了一次长时间的排练。

现在完成时:
We have had many rehearsals this month. = 这个月我们已经排练了很多次了。
They have had several successful rehearsals. = 他们已经进行了几次成功的排练。

过去完成时:
They had already had several rehearsals before the opening night. = 在首映之夜之前，他们已经进行了几次排练。
We had finished all our rehearsals by Friday. = 我们在星期五之前完成了所有的排练。


将来时:
We will have rehearsals next week. = 我们下周将进行排练。
The actors will have a final rehearsal tomorrow. = 演员们明天将进行最后一次排练。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would have more rehearsals. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们将会有更多的排练。
I wish we had more time for rehearsals. = 我希望我们有更多的时间来排练。

被动语态:
The play's rehearsals were postponed. = 该剧的排练被推迟了。
All the rehearsals are being carefully monitored. = 所有排练都在被仔细监控。

疑问句:
When are the rehearsals for the concert? = 音乐会的排练是什么时候？
How many rehearsals do they plan to have? = 他们计划进行多少次排练？

否定句:
We don't have rehearsals on weekends. = 我们周末不排练。
They didn't have enough time for rehearsals. = 他们没有足够的时间来排练。
