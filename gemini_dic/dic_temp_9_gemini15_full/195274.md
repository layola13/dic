semigeometric:/ˌsɛmiˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 半几何的；部分几何的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a system or process that combines geometric and non-geometric elements or properties. It suggests a blend of geometric precision and organic or irregular features.  | Example: The semigeometric design incorporated both straight lines and flowing curves.  半几何设计结合了直线和流畅的曲线。 | 近义词：partially geometric, quasi-geometric | 反义词：purely geometric, completely non-geometric |  形容词
