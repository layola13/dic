iridescents:/ˌɪrɪˈdɛsnt/| adj. |彩虹色的，闪光的，变彩的 |Showing luminous colours that seem to change when seen from different angles.  The iridescence is caused by the diffraction of light. |例句：The soap bubble showed iridescent colours in the sunlight.  阳光下肥皂泡呈现彩虹般的颜色。

The oil slick on the water had an iridescent sheen. 水面上的油膜呈现出彩虹般的光泽。


近义词：shimmering, lustrous, opalescent |反义词：dull, matte


Etymology:

The word "iridescent" originates from the Latin word "iris," meaning "rainbow,"  combined with the suffix "-escent," indicating a gradual or increasing state.  Therefore, "iridescent" literally means "becoming like a rainbow" or "showing rainbow-like colours."
