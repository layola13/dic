The noncollapsible umbrella survived the storm. 这把不可折叠的伞挺过了暴风雨。|近义词：unfoldable, rigid, inflexible |反义词：collapsible, foldable, flexible

词源:
The word "noncollapsible" is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the adjective "collapsible" (capable of being collapsed or folded).  "Collapsible" itself comes from the verb "collapse," which ultimately derives from the Old French "collapser" (to fall together).
