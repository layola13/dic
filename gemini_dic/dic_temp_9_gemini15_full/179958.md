referrer:/rɪˈfɜːrər/| n.|推荐人；引荐人；来源；引用者|A referrer is a person who refers someone or something to another person or place.  It can also refer to the source of a website visit, such as a link from another website.|Example:  The referrer for this job opening was my former professor. 这份工作的推荐人是我的前教授。

The referrer of the article was a reputable journal. 这篇文章的引用来源是一家信誉良好的期刊。|近义词：referer, recommender, source|反义词：N/A
