proceedings:/prəˈsiːdɪŋz/ | n. |会议记录；诉讼程序；进程；行动 |The official record of a meeting, a legal case, or a series of events.  It can refer to the events themselves or the written account of them. |例句：The court proceedings were long and complex. 法庭诉讼程序冗长而复杂。

例句：The minutes of the meeting are the official proceedings. 会议记录是正式的会议记录。


近义词：transactions, minutes, events, actions, processes

反义词：conclusion, termination, end
