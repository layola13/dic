pipe down:/paɪp daʊn/| v.|安静点，别吵了|To become quiet; to stop making noise.|例句：Pipe down, you noisy children!  安静点，你们这些吵闹的孩子！|例句：The teacher told the students to pipe down. 老师让学生们安静下来。|近义词：be quiet, shut up, hush|反义词：shout, yell


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They pipe down when the teacher enters. = 老师进来时，他们安静下来。
He pipes down after being scolded. = 他被责骂后安静下来。

一般过去时:
The crowd piped down after the announcement. = 公告之后人群安静下来。
She piped down as soon as her mother came in. = 她母亲一进来她就安静下来了。

现在完成时:
The children have piped down at last. = 孩子们终于安静下来了。
They have piped down after a long noisy play. = 他们玩闹很久后终于安静下来了。

过去完成时:
The audience had piped down before the performance began. = 演出开始前观众已经安静下来了。
They had piped down when the principal walked in. = 校长走进来时他们已经安静下来了。

将来时:
They will pipe down once the movie starts. = 电影一开演他们就会安静下来。
He will pipe down when he gets tired. = 他累了就会安静下来。

虚拟语气:
If they would only pipe down, we could concentrate. = 如果他们能安静下来，我们就能集中注意力了。
I wish they would pipe down; I can't hear myself think. = 我希望他们能安静下来；我根本听不见自己在想什么。

被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used passively.)


疑问句:
Will they pipe down soon? = 他们很快会安静下来吗？
Can you make them pipe down? = 你能让他们安静下来吗？

否定句:
They didn't pipe down until late at night. = 他们直到深夜才安静下来。
The students wouldn't pipe down despite the teacher's requests. = 尽管老师要求，学生们也不安静。
