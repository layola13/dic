radioisotopic:/ˌreɪdiouˌaɪsəˈtɒpɪk/| adj.|放射性同位素的；用放射性同位素进行的|Relating to or using radioisotopes.  Radioisotopic methods are commonly used in medical imaging and various scientific research fields.|例句：Radioisotopic dating techniques are used to determine the age of ancient artifacts. 放射性同位素测年技术被用来确定古代文物的年代。

例句：The study employed radioisotopic labeling to track the movement of molecules within cells. 该研究采用放射性同位素标记来追踪分子在细胞内的运动。


近义词：radioactive (less specific)


反义词：non-radioactive, stable isotopic
