gymnosperms:/ˈdʒɪmnɒspɜːmz/| n. |裸子植物|Plants whose seeds are not enclosed in an ovary; they are usually borne on cones.  Gymnosperms include conifers, cycads, and ginkgoes. They are characterized by their naked seeds, which are not protected by a fruit. |Example: Conifers, such as pines and spruces, are examples of gymnosperms.  松树和云杉等针叶树是裸子植物的例子。|近义词:conifers (in a narrower sense)|反义词:angiosperms
