goiter:/ˈɡɔɪtər/ | n. |甲状腺肿 |An enlargement of the thyroid gland in the neck.  It can be caused by iodine deficiency, inflammation, or tumors. |She developed a goiter after years of living in an iodine-deficient region. 她多年居住在缺碘地区后患上了甲状腺肿。|thyroid enlargement, swelling of the thyroid |normal thyroid size |名词
