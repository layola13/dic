five-and-dime: /ˌfaɪv ænd daɪm/ | n. |五美分和一角钱商店；廉价商店 | A five-and-dime store is a type of retail store that historically sold a wide variety of goods, mostly for five and ten cents (hence the name).  These stores were known for their low prices and wide selection of items, appealing to a broad range of customers. They often featured counters with various items like notions, cosmetics, toys, and candy. The term is now largely nostalgic, as few such stores remain. | Example: My grandmother loved to shop at the five-and-dime for her household supplies.  奶奶喜欢去五美分和一角钱商店购买家用物品。| 近义词：variety store, dollar store, general store | 反义词：high-end store, luxury boutique |  n.
