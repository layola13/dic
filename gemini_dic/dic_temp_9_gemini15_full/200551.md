slateful: /ˈsleɪtfl/ | adj. | 有状态的；状态化的 |  Having or involving internal state that persists between function calls.  A slateful function's output depends not only on its input but also on its internal state.  This contrasts with stateless functions, where the output depends solely on the input. |


例句：

The counter is a slateful object because it remembers its previous value. = 计数器是一个有状态的对象，因为它记住它之前的数值。

A slateful database system maintains information about previous interactions. = 有状态的数据库系统会维护有关先前交互的信息。


近义词：stateful, state-dependent


反义词：stateless


词源：  The word is a compound of "state" and "ful,"  reflecting the inherent state maintained by the function or system. "State" in this context refers to the internal memory or condition of a system, while "-ful" is a suffix indicating "full of" or "characterized by."
