sifting:/ˈsɪftɪŋ/| n. v.| 筛分；细查；筛选 |The process of separating finer particles from coarser ones by passing through a sieve or mesh.  The act of carefully examining something in order to find important information. |例句：The baker is sifting the flour. 面包师正在筛面粉。
The police are sifting through the evidence. 警方正在仔细审查证据。|近义词：screening, filtering, winnowing, scrutinizing |反义词：lumping, combining, merging |动词
