raincheck:/ˈreɪntʃɛk/ | n. |延期；改期 |A raincheck is an offer to do something at a later time, especially if plans are canceled due to bad weather or other unforeseen circumstances.  It's often used in the context of social events or activities.|例句：I'm so sorry I can't make it to the party tonight, can I get a raincheck?  今晚我不能参加派对了，可以改天吗？


例句：The picnic was canceled due to rain, but we'll take a raincheck and have it next weekend.  野餐因为下雨取消了，但我们下周末会改期举行。|近义词：postponement, reschedule |反义词：cancellation


词性: 名词
