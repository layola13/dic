insecurity:/ˌɪn.sɪˈkjʊrɪti/ | n. | 不安全感，不安，不稳定 | A feeling of lacking confidence or assurance.  It can refer to a lack of confidence in oneself, one's abilities, or one's future, or it can refer to a lack of safety or protection. |  例句：Her insecurity stemmed from a difficult childhood.  她的不安全感源于不幸的童年。

例句：The increasing crime rate has led to a feeling of insecurity among residents.  犯罪率的上升导致居民感到不安。


近义词：anxiety, nervousness, uncertainty, doubt, apprehension | 反义词：confidence, assurance, security, stability | 词性:noun
