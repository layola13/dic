conflate:/kənˈfleɪt/| vt.|合并，混淆|To combine (two or more sets of things) into one.|The author conflates several different historical events into a single narrative. 作者将几个不同的历史事件合并成一个单一的叙事。|近义词：combine, merge, amalgamate, blend|反义词：separate, distinguish, differentiate

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conflates the two issues in his speech. = 他在演讲中混淆了这两个问题。
They conflate facts and opinions in their reporting. = 他们在报道中混淆了事实和观点。

一般过去时:
She conflated the two characters in her novel. = 她的小说中混淆了这两个角色。
The media conflated the two separate incidents. = 媒体将这两个独立事件混淆了。
