redbird:/ˈrɛdbɜːrd/|/ˈrɛdˌbɜːrd/| n. |红雀；红鸟|A redbird is a type of bird with red plumage.  The term can refer to various species depending on region, most commonly the Northern Cardinal in North America.  |Example: The male Northern Cardinal is a vibrant redbird with a distinctive crest.|近义词：cardinal (北美红雀),  red bird|反义词：无
