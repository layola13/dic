carlot:/ˈkɑːrlɑːt/| n. |汽车销售场，停车场|A car lot is an outdoor area where used or new cars are displayed and sold.  It's often associated with car dealerships. |例句：He found a good deal on a used car at the local car lot. 他在当地的一家汽车销售场找到了一辆二手车，价格很划算。|例句：The car lot was full of shiny new vehicles. 汽车销售场里停满了闪闪发光的新车。|近义词：car dealership, used car lot, auto sales lot|反义词：无
