misthreading: /ˌmɪsˈθredɪŋ/ | v. | 线穿错，穿线错误 | The act of incorrectly threading something, typically a needle or machine.  This often results in a malfunction or poor outcome.  | 例句：The seamstress spent an hour correcting her misthreading.  缝纫工花了一个小时纠正她的穿线错误。


The constant misthreading of the sewing machine frustrated the tailor. 缝纫机的反复穿线错误让裁缝很沮丧。 | 近义词：improper threading, incorrect threading, faulty threading | 反义词：proper threading, correct threading | 动词
