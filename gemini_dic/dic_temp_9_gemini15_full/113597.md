macer:/ˈmeɪsər/| v.|捣碎，浸软|To soften or disintegrate (a substance) by steeping or soaking in liquid, often as a preparatory step in a process.  |例句：The cook macerated the fruit overnight to soften it for the jam. 厨师将水果浸泡了一夜，以使其变软方便制作果酱。|例句：The herbalist macerated the herbs in alcohol to extract their medicinal properties. 草药师将草药浸泡在酒精中以提取其药性。|近义词：soak, steep, soften|反义词：harden, solidify
