sightlessly:/ˈsaɪtləsli/| adj.| 盲目地；失明地；不见得；没有看见地 |Without sight; blindly.  It implies a lack of vision, either literally or figuratively. |例句：He stumbled sightlessly through the dark room. 他在黑暗的房间里盲目地摸索着。

例句：She walked sightlessly down the street, unaware of the danger. 她漫无目的地走在街上，没有意识到危险。


近义词：blindly, unseeingly, unknowingly

反义词：sightedly, seeingly, knowingly

词性：形容词
