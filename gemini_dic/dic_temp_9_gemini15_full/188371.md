rubidine:/ˈruːbɪdaɪn/| n.| 铷红素| Rubidine is a red pigment formerly obtained from madder root, now largely replaced by synthetic dyes. It's a natural dye historically used to color textiles.  |例句：The ancient tapestry was dyed with rubidine, a vibrant red pigment derived from madder.  古代挂毯是用茜草根提取的鲜红色素——铷红素染色的。|近义词：madder red, alizarin (referring to the chemical compound)|反义词：无|名词
