encroachment:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃmənt/ | n. | 侵犯；侵占；蚕食 | The gradual encroachment of the desert on fertile land.  沙漠逐渐侵占肥沃的土地。 |近义词：intrusion, infringement, invasion | 反义词：withdrawal, retreat


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company's expansion encroaches on our territory. = 公司的扩张侵犯了我们的领地。
His constant complaining encroaches on our time. = 他不断的抱怨侵占了我们的时间。


一般过去时:
The sea encroached upon the coastline after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，海水侵蚀了海岸线。
The forest encroachment on the village was significant. = 森林侵占村庄的情况非常严重。


现在完成时:
The government has enacted laws to prevent further encroachment on natural habitats. = 政府已经制定法律来防止进一步侵占自然栖息地。
The development has significantly encroached on the local wildlife. = 开发项目严重侵占了当地野生动物的生存空间。


过去完成时:
Before the dam was built, the river had already encroached upon the farmland. = 在水坝建成之前，河流已经侵占了农田。
They had never experienced such a rapid encroachment of the weeds into their garden. = 他们从未经历过杂草如此迅速地侵入他们的花园。


将来时:
The rising sea levels will encroach on coastal communities in the future. = 未来，海平面上升将侵占沿海社区。
Unless action is taken, the desert will further encroach on the oasis. = 除非采取行动，否则沙漠将进一步侵占绿洲。


虚拟语气:
If the government didn't intervene, the development would encroach on the protected area. = 如果政府不干预，开发项目将会侵占保护区。
I wish the noise from the construction wouldn't encroach on my peace and quiet. = 我希望施工噪音不会扰乱我的宁静。


被动语态:
The neighboring property was encroached upon by the new construction. = 新建筑侵占了邻近的房产。
The park was slowly encroached upon by urban sprawl. = 公园正慢慢地被城市蔓延所侵占。


疑问句:
Does the new road encroach on any protected wetlands? = 新路是否侵占了任何受保护的湿地？
How can we prevent further encroachment on our resources? = 我们如何才能防止进一步侵占我们的资源？


否定句:
The new building does not encroach on public land. = 新建筑没有侵占公共土地。
The expansion plans do not encroach on neighboring properties. = 扩张计划不会侵犯邻近的房产。
