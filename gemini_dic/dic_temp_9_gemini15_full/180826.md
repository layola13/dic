reharvest:/ˌriːˈhɑːrvɪst/| vt.|再收割，二次收割|To harvest again; to harvest a second crop from the same land.|例句：We plan to reharvest the field next month. 我们计划下个月再次收割这块田地。  例句：The farmer reharvested the soybeans after a late-season rain.  农民在季末雨后再次收割了大豆。|近义词：reap, gather, harvest again|反义词：plant, sow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reharvest the wheat every year. = 他们每年都再次收割小麦。
Farmers reharvest the rice after the first harvest. = 农民在第一次收割后再次收割稻米。

一般过去时:
The farmers reharvested the corn last week. = 农民上周再次收割了玉米。
They reharvested the grapes for a second wine. = 他们再次收割葡萄用来酿造第二种葡萄酒。

现在完成时:
We have reharvested the apples twice this season. = 我们这个季节已经两次再次收割苹果了。
The farm has reharvested the barley for animal feed. = 该农场已经再次收割大麦作为动物饲料。

过去完成时:
They had reharvested the land before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经再次收割了土地。
The company had reharvested the timber after the initial cut. = 公司在第一次砍伐后再次收割了木材。

将来时:
We will reharvest the strawberries next week. = 我们下周将再次收割草莓。
The team will reharvest the data for a more detailed analysis. = 团队将再次收集数据进行更详细的分析。

虚拟语气:
If we reharvested the crop now, we would lose some yield. = 如果我们现在再次收割庄稼，我们将损失一些产量。
I wish we had reharvested the potatoes earlier. = 我希望我们早点再次收割土豆。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
