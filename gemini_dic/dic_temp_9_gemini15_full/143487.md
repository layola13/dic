outgive:/aʊtˈɡɪv/| v.|超过，胜过；给予过多 |to surpass or exceed in giving; to give more than is necessary or expected. |例句：The company outgave its competitors in charitable donations.  这家公司在慈善捐款方面超过了竞争对手。 |例句：He outgave himself in an attempt to impress her. 他为了给她留下印象，给予过多。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo |反义词：undergive (非标准词), skimp


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company outgives its competitors in charity. = 这家公司在慈善事业上超过了竞争对手。
He usually outgives his budget for gifts. = 他通常在礼物预算上超支。


一般过去时:
Last year, they outgave their previous record. = 去年，他们超过了之前的记录。
She outgave her promise to donate. = 她捐赠的超过了她承诺的数额。


现在完成时:
They have outgiven their initial pledge. = 他们已经超过了最初的承诺。

过去完成时:
By the end of the year, they had outgiven their target. = 到年底，他们已经超过了目标。
He had outgiven his financial means to help his family. = 他为了帮助家人，已经超过了他的经济能力。

虚拟语气:
