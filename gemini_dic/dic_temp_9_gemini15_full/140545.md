olfaction:/ɒlˈfækʃən/ | n. |嗅觉；嗅觉器官|The sense of smell; the act or process of smelling.  It involves the detection and interpretation of odours by specialized sensory receptors in the nose. |例句：Her olfaction was remarkably acute, allowing her to identify the subtle scent of jasmine.她的嗅觉非常灵敏，能够辨别出茉莉花的微妙香味。

例句：The loss of olfaction is a common symptom of COVID-19. 嗅觉丧失是新冠肺炎的常见症状。


近义词：smell, sense of smell

反义词：anosmia (loss of smell)

词性：名词


词源:  From Latin *olfactionem, from olfacere "to smell," from olere "to smell" + facere "to make, do."
