reconstructional:/ˌriːkənˈstrʌkʃənəl/ | adj. |重建的；再造的 | Relating to or involving reconstruction.  The word describes something that pertains to the act of reconstructing or rebuilding something, often after damage or destruction. It emphasizes the process of putting something back together. |例句：The reconstructional efforts after the earthquake were extensive. 地震后的重建工作非常广泛。
