fellows:/ˈfɛloʊz/ | n. |伙伴；家伙；院士；研究员 |A fellow is a man, or sometimes a boy.  It can also refer to a member of a learned society or a postgraduate student. |例句：He's a good fellow. 他是个好人。

The fellows in the lab are working on a new project. 实验室里的研究员们正在做一个新的项目。|近义词：companions, guys, members, associates |反义词：enemies, adversaries, opponents |名词
