denotement: /dɪˈnoʊtmənt/ | n. |指示;含义;指称|The literal meaning of a word or phrase, as opposed to its connotations or associated meanings.  It refers to the direct, explicit meaning of a sign, symbol, or word, independent of its context or cultural associations.|Example: The denotation of "home" is a place where one lives, but its connotation might be warmth and comfort.|近义词：signification, meaning, sense |反义词：connotation
