collectivization:/ˌkəˌlɛktɪvaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |集体化；集体经营|The process of transforming private property into collective property, typically under state control.  This often involves the consolidation of land and resources into larger, collectively owned and operated units.  It's a significant process with wide-ranging economic and social consequences.|例句：The forced collectivization of agriculture in the Soviet Union led to widespread famine.  苏联强制实行农业集体化导致了大范围的饥荒。|例句：The collectivization of the fishing industry resulted in increased efficiency but also reduced individual autonomy.  渔业的集体化提高了效率，但也降低了个体自主性。|近义词：nationalization, socialization, communization |反义词：privatization, individualization|名词
