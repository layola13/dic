grimacers:/ɡrɪˈmeɪsər/| n. |做鬼脸的人，扮怪相的人 | A person who makes grimaces.  Grimacers often express displeasure, pain, or disgust through facial expressions. |例句：The children were grimacers during the medicine-taking process. 孩子们吃药时都在做鬼脸。|近义词： contortionists,  pranksters (if the grimaces are playful) |反义词：  smilers
