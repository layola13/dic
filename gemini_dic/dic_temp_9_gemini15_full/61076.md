ellipsoids:/ɪˈlɪpsɔɪdz/| n. |椭球体 |A three-dimensional geometrical shape that is a generalization of an ellipse;  all plane sections through the center are ellipses.  It's a quadric surface that is a stretched or compressed version of a sphere.| The Earth is not a perfect sphere, but rather an oblate ellipsoid. 地球不是一个完美的球体，而是一个扁球体。 The data points were fitted to an ellipsoid. 数据点拟合到一个椭球体上。|sphere, spheroid |  |  n.
