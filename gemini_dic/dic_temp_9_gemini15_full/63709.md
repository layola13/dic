ephemeral:/ɪˈfɛmərəl/| adj. |短暂的，转瞬即逝的|lasting for a very short time|The beauty of the cherry blossoms is ephemeral.  樱花的美丽是短暂的。| transient, fleeting, short-lived | permanent, everlasting, enduring |形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The beauty of a summer day is ephemeral.  夏日的美景是短暂的。
His fame is ephemeral; he will soon be forgotten. 他的名声是短暂的；他很快就会被遗忘。


一般过去时:
The feeling of happiness was ephemeral; it vanished quickly.  幸福的感觉是短暂的；它很快消失了。
Her excitement over the gift was ephemeral; she quickly lost interest. 她对礼物的兴奋是短暂的；她很快就失去了兴趣。


现在完成时:
Many things in life have proven to be ephemeral.  生活中许多事情已被证明是短暂的。
All my youthful dreams have proven ephemeral. 我所有的青春梦想都证明是短暂的。


过去完成时:
Before I knew it, the meeting had been ephemeral.  在我意识到之前，会议就已经结束了（短暂的）。
The joy of the moment had been ephemeral, quickly replaced by anxiety. 那份喜悦转瞬即逝，很快就被焦虑所取代。


将来时:
The success of this project will likely be ephemeral.  这个项目的成功很可能是短暂的。
His popularity will be ephemeral, unless he continues to work hard. 他的名气将会是短暂的，除非他继续努力工作。


虚拟语气:
If only happiness weren't so ephemeral!  要是快乐不是那么短暂就好了！
I wish my time in Paris hadn't been so ephemeral. 我希望我在巴黎的时间不要那么短暂。


被动语态:

The effects of the medication are often described as ephemeral. 这种药物的效果通常被描述为短暂的。


疑问句:

否定句:

His influence on the community was not ephemeral; it continues to this day. 他对社区的影响并非短暂的；它持续至今。
