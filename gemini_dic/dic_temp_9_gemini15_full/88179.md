hilock:/ˈhɪlək/| n. |小丘；土堆 |A hillock is a small hill or mound.  It is a gently rising piece of land, smaller than a mountain or even a large hill. |例句：We climbed the hillock for a better view. 我们爬上了小丘，以便更好地观赏景色。
The sheep grazed peacefully on the grassy hillock. 羊群平静地吃着小山坡上的青草。|近义词：knoll, mound, hummock |反义词：plain, valley
