inequal:/ɪnˈiːkwəl/| adj.|不平等的，不均等的|Unequal means not equal; different in quantity, degree, or value.  It often refers to a lack of fairness or justice in the distribution of resources or opportunities.|例句：The unequal distribution of wealth is a major social problem. 财富分配不均是主要的社会问题。|例句：The two teams were fairly unequal in terms of skill. 这两支球队在技术方面相当不均等。|近义词：unfair, uneven, disparate, unequalled|反义词：equal, even, equitable, fair
