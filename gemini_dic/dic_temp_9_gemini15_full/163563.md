precrediting:/ˌpriːˈkrɛdɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先记入贷方；预先贷记 |  To credit an account before a transaction or event occurs. This is often done to ensure sufficient funds are available or to reflect an anticipated transaction.  | Example: The company precredited my account with the expected bonus payment. 公司预先将我预期的奖金支付记入我的账户。  | 近义词：advance payment, prepaying | 反义词：post-crediting, debiting | 动词
