overkilling:/ˌoʊvərˈkɪlɪŋ/| n.|过度杀戮；杀伤过剩；矫枉过正|The act of doing more than is necessary, often to an excessive or counterproductive degree.  It can refer to excessive force, effort, or preparation. |例句：The security measures were overkill; a simple lock would have sufficed.  安全措施太过分了；简单的锁就足够了。

例句：They overkilled the presentation with too many charts and graphs. 他们的演示文稿使用了过多的图表，矫枉过正了。|近义词：excess, overdoing, overdoing it, going overboard, superfluity|反义词：underdoing, insufficiency, deficiency
