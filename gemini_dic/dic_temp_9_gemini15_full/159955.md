polyplastic: /ˌpɒliˈplæstɪk/ | adj. | 多塑料的；多种塑料的 | Made of or relating to several different types of plastic.  This term describes materials or products composed of multiple kinds of plastics, often blended for specific properties.  |  例句：The new car dashboard is polyplastic, combining different plastics for strength, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness.  新型汽车仪表盘是多塑料的，结合了不同类型的塑料以增强强度、柔韧性和成本效益。

The company uses a polyplastic approach to create a lighter and more durable product. 这家公司采用多塑料方法来创造更轻、更耐用的产品。| 近义词：multiplastic (less common) | 反义词：monoplastic (made of a single type of plastic) |  形容词
