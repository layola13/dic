dipperfuls:/ˈdɪpərfʊlz/| n. |满满一勺，几勺|The word "dipperful" refers to the amount that a dipper can hold.  It's often used informally to describe a quantity of something, especially a semi-liquid or granular substance, that fills a dipper.  The plural "dipperfuls" indicates multiple dipper-sized amounts.|例句：She served several dipperfuls of ice cream to the children. 她给孩子们盛了几勺冰淇淋。

She ate three dipperfuls of chili. 她吃了三勺辣椒。
