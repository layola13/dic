godmother:/ˈɡɑːdmʌðər/ | n. | 教母，女教父 | A woman who acts as a sponsor at a baptism and is considered a spiritual guide and protector.  A godmother is a woman who has undertaken a special responsibility for a child's spiritual and moral development, often playing a significant role in their life. |  She is a kind godmother to her nieces and nephews. 她是她侄女侄子们慈爱的教母。|  sponsor, guardian |  (无明确反义词，可考虑anti-mentor等非正式说法) | 名词
