novation:/noʊˈveɪʃən/| n. |合同变更；创新；更新|The substitution of a new contract for an old one, with the consent of all parties involved.  This involves the termination of the original contract and the creation of a new one, often with modified terms.  It differs from assignment or novation because it replaces the original contract entirely.|Example: The parties agreed to a novation of the contract, changing the payment schedule.  = 各方同意对合同进行变更，更改付款时间表。


Example: The company introduced a novation in its software, adding new features. = 公司在其软件中引入了创新，增加了新功能。|近义词：substitution, replacement, alteration, innovation|反义词：termination, cancellation, rescission|名词
