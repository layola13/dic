get-a-buzz-on:/ɡɛt ə bʌz ˈɑːn/ | phrasal verb | 兴奋起来；喝醉；陶醉 | To become intoxicated or excited; to get high.  This phrase is informal and primarily used in spoken English. | 例句：He went to the party to get a buzz on. = 他去参加派对是为了让自己兴奋起来。

They got a buzz on after drinking several beers. = 他们喝了几瓶啤酒后喝醉了。


近义词：get high, get drunk, get intoxicated, get wasted, get plastered


反义词：sober up, become calm, become composed


词性: phrasal verb
