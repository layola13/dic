housewifery:/ˈhaʊswɪfrɪ/| n. |家务管理；家政；家庭主妇的职责|The art or practice of managing a household, especially the tasks traditionally done by women.  It encompasses cooking, cleaning, childcare, and other domestic duties.|例句：She excelled at housewifery, keeping a spotless home and raising a happy family. 她擅长家务管理，家里干干净净，还养育了一个幸福的家庭。|例句：Many women found their identity solely through housewifery in the past.  过去许多女性仅仅通过家务管理来定义自己的身份。|近义词：homemaking, domesticity, household management|反义词：career, profession
