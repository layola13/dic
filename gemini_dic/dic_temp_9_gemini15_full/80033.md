go-to-town:/ˈɡoʊ tuː taʊn/ | phrasal verb |尽情享受；兴高采烈地做；卖力地做 |To do something with great energy and enthusiasm; to engage in an activity with unrestrained enjoyment.  |例句：Let's go-to-town on this project and finish it before the deadline.  让我们全力以赴完成这个项目，并在截止日期前完成。


例句：They went to town decorating the Christmas tree. 他们兴高采烈地装饰圣诞树。


近义词：go all out, make a big effort,  indulge in, revel in


反义词：hold back, restrain oneself, take it easy


词性：phrasal verb


USAGE->
(由于go-to-town本身是一个短语动词，没有不同时态的变形，因此不适用传统语法时态分析。)


PHRASE->
go to town on something:  全力以赴做某事
go to town with something:  尽情挥霍某物
go to town decorating:  兴高采烈地装饰
go to town shopping:  疯狂购物
go to town celebrating:  尽情庆祝
