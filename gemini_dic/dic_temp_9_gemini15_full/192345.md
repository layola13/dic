scrimp:/skrɪmp/| v. | 吝啬；节省；勉强维持 | To use or spend as little as possible; to be frugal or stingy. |  He scrimped and saved to buy a house. 他省吃俭用买了一套房子。  They scrimped on food to save money for a vacation. 他们为了省钱去度假而在食物上节省开支。 | save, economize, pinch | squander, splurge, lavish


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He scrimps on everything. 他对所有东西都吝啬。
She scrimps to make ends meet. 她省吃俭用以维持生计。

一般过去时:
They scrimped and saved for years. 他们省吃俭用多年。
We scrimped on our holiday to afford the new car. 我们在假期上节省开支以买得起新车。

现在完成时:
She has scrimped and saved all her life. 她一生都省吃俭用。
He has scrimped so much he has almost no money left. 他省得太厉害了，几乎没钱剩下了。

过去完成时:
They had scrimped and saved before they bought the house. 他们在买房子之前已经省吃俭用了。
We had scrimped for months to pay for the trip. 我们为了支付旅行费用已经省了好几个月了。

将来时:
We will scrimp and save for our children's education. 我们将为孩子的教育省吃俭用。
They will have to scrimp to afford the new apartment. 他们将不得不省吃俭用才能负担得起新的公寓。

虚拟语气:
If I scrimped a little more, I could afford that car. 如果我再省一点，我就能买得起那辆车了。
I wish I hadn't scrimped on the ingredients; the cake is terrible. 我真希望我没有在食材上节省；蛋糕太难吃了。

被动语态:
(Scrimp is rarely used in the passive voice.)  省吃俭用很少用被动语态。
Money should not be scrimped on essential things.  不应该在必需品上省钱。

疑问句:
Do you scrimp on anything? 你会在任何事情上省钱吗？
Does he usually scrimp on his meals? 他通常会在饭食上省钱吗？


否定句:
We don't scrimp on quality. 我们不会在质量上省钱。
They didn't scrimp on the decorations for the party. 他们没有在派对的装饰上省钱。


PHRASE->
scrimp and save: 省吃俭用
scrimp on: 在...上节省
scrimp and scrape: 勉强维持生计
to scrimp and save money: 省吃俭用攒钱
scrimping and saving: 省吃俭用
