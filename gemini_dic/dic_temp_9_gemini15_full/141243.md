op-ed:/ˈɑːpˌɛd/ | n. | 专栏文章；评论文章 | A signed opinion piece, usually on a topical issue, published on the editorial or opinion page of a newspaper or magazine.  Op-eds are often written by experts, public figures, or commentators, offering their perspectives on current events. | Example:  The New York Times published an op-ed on the rising cost of healthcare.  纽约时报发表了一篇关于医疗保健成本上升的评论文章。 | 近义词：column, commentary, editorial (but often less formal than an editorial) | 反义词：news report, factual article


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The newspaper publishes several op-eds each day. = 这家报纸每天发表几篇评论文章。
She writes regular op-eds for a national magazine. = 她定期为一家全国性杂志撰写评论文章。

一般过去时:
The politician wrote a powerful op-ed about the new law. = 这位政治家撰写了一篇关于这项新法律的有力评论文章。
I read an interesting op-ed on climate change yesterday. = 我昨天读了一篇关于气候变化的有趣的评论文章。


现在完成时:
He has written many op-eds on economic policy. = 他撰写过许多关于经济政策的评论文章。
The author has published several op-eds in major publications. =  这位作者已在主要出版物上发表了几篇评论文章。


过去完成时:
Before the election, she had written several op-eds criticizing the incumbent. = 在选举前，她写过几篇评论文章批评现任官员。
They had already read the op-ed before the meeting began. = 会议开始前他们已经读过那篇评论文章了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If I were a journalist, I would write an op-ed on this issue. = 如果我是一名记者，我会就这个问题撰写一篇评论文章。
I wish I had written an op-ed about that important topic. = 我希望我曾就那个重要话题写过一篇评论文章。


被动语态:
The op-ed was widely shared on social media. = 这篇评论文章在社交媒体上被广泛分享。
Many op-eds are written by experts in their fields. =  许多评论文章是由各自领域的专家撰写的。


疑问句:
Have you read today's op-eds? = 你读过今天的评论文章了吗？
When will the op-ed be published? = 这篇评论文章什么时候发表？


否定句:
