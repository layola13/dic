quasi-responsive:/ˈkwɑːzaɪrɪˈspɑːnsɪv/| adj.|准响应的，似反应的|Approaching but not fully achieving responsiveness; showing a partial or incomplete response.|The system exhibited quasi-responsive behavior, reacting to some stimuli but not others.|近义词：partially responsive, semi-responsive|反义词：unresponsive, unresponsive


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like "What is your name?".  Instead, we use it to describe something.

Example sentences illustrating its use:

The quasi-responsive nature of the market made predictions difficult.  =市场的准响应特性使得预测变得困难。

His quasi-responsive attitude indicated a lack of genuine engagement. = 他似是而非的态度表明缺乏真正的参与。


PHRASE->
There aren't common phrases directly using "quasi-responsive".  Phrases would involve using it to describe a noun or system.  For example:  "a quasi-responsive system", "a quasi-responsive market".
