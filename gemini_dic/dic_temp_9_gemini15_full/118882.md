metastatic:/ˌmɛtəˈstætɪk/| adj.|转移性的，转移的|Relating to the transfer of disease from one organ or part of the body to another not directly connected with it.|The cancer had metastasized to her lungs.|近义词：spreading, disseminated|反义词：localized, contained


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cancer is metastatic. = 癌症是转移性的。
This type of tumor rarely metastasizes. = 这种类型的肿瘤很少转移。


一般过去时:
The doctor confirmed that the cancer had metastasized. = 医生证实癌症已经转移。
The disease metastasized quickly. = 疾病迅速转移。


现在完成时:
The cancer has metastasized to the bones. = 癌症已经转移到骨骼。
The tumor has not yet metastasized. = 肿瘤尚未转移。


过去完成时:
By the time she was diagnosed, the cancer had already metastasized. = 当她被诊断出来时，癌症已经转移了。
He had metastasized to several organs before the symptoms appeared. = 在症状出现之前，他已经转移到多个器官了。


将来时:
Doctors fear the cancer will metastasize. = 医生担心癌症会转移。
The treatment aims to prevent the cancer from metastasizing. = 治疗的目标是防止癌症转移。


虚拟语气:
If the cancer hadn't metastasized, she might still be alive. = 如果癌症没有转移，她可能还活着。
It would be devastating if the disease metastasized further. = 如果疾病进一步转移，那将是毁灭性的。


被动语态:
It was discovered that the cancer had metastasized. = 发现癌症已经转移。
The spread of the disease is being closely monitored to see if it has metastasized. = 正在密切监测疾病的传播，以查看它是否已转移。


疑问句:
Has the cancer metastasized? = 癌症转移了吗？
To what organs has the tumor metastasized? = 肿瘤转移到哪些器官了？


否定句:
The cancer has not metastasized yet. = 癌症还没有转移。
The tests showed that the cancer had not metastasized. = 测试显示癌症没有转移。



PHRASE->
metastatic cancer = 转移性癌症
metastatic disease = 转移性疾病

metastatic lesion = 转移性病灶
metastatic potential = 转移潜能


词源:
From Late Latin *metastaticus, from Greek μεταστατικός (metastatikós), from μεταστῆναι (metastênai) "to transfer," from μετα- (meta-) "beyond, after" + ἵστημι (hístēmi) "I stand, place."  The word reflects the movement of the disease "beyond" its initial location.
