excluded:/ɪkˈskluːdɪd/| adj.|被排除的；被除外的|Excluded means kept out or prevented from entering or participating.  It implies a deliberate act of omission or rejection. |Example:  The injured player was excluded from the competition. 受伤的球员被排除在比赛之外。 The data excluded from the analysis were deemed unreliable.  从分析中排除的数据被认为不可靠。|近义词：omitted, excepted, barred, prohibited|反义词：included, admitted, accepted|形容词
