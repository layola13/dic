remits:/rɪˈmɪts/| vt. n.|汇款；赦免；委任；提交；(权利等的)移交|to send money to someone, especially by post:  The company remits the money to my bank account every month. 公司每月将钱汇到我的银行账户。|近义词：transmit, forward, transfer, pardon|反义词：retain, withhold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company remits payments to its employees every Friday. = 公司每周五向员工支付工资。
He regularly remits money to his family in his home country. = 他定期向国内的家人汇款。


一般过去时:
She remitted the payment on time. = 她按时汇款。
The governor remitted the prisoner's sentence. = 州长赦免了囚犯的刑期。


现在完成时:
They have remitted all the outstanding debts. = 他们已经偿还了所有未清债务。
He has remitted the funds via wire transfer. = 他已通过电汇汇款。


过去完成时:
She had remitted the funds before the deadline. = 她在截止日期前已汇款。
The court had remitted the case to a lower court. = 法院已将此案发回下级法院。


将来时:
The company will remit the bonus to our accounts next week. = 公司下周将奖金汇入我们的账户。
He will remit the money as soon as he receives it. = 他一收到钱就会汇款。


虚拟语气:
If he remitted the money on time, there wouldn't be any problem. = 如果他按时汇款，就不会有任何问题。
I wish I had remitted the payment earlier. = 我希望我早点汇款。


被动语态:
The payment was remitted to the wrong account. = 款项汇入了错误的账户。
The sentence was remitted by the governor. = 刑期被州长赦免了。


疑问句:
Has the payment been remitted yet? = 款项已经汇出了吗？
Will the money be remitted this week? = 这周会汇款吗？


否定句:
He did not remit the money as promised. = 他没有按照承诺汇款。
The court refused to remit the sentence. = 法院拒绝赦免刑期。


PHRASE->
remit payment = 汇款
remit a debt = 偿还债务
remit a crime = 赦免罪行
remit a case = 将案件发回下级法院
remit to = 汇款至
