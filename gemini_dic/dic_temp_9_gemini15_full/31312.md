chapped:/tʃæpt/| adj.|干裂的，裂开的|Having cracks in the surface, especially from dryness or cold.  Often used to describe skin, lips, or hands.|例句：My lips are chapped from the cold wind. 我的嘴唇被冷风吹裂了。

例句：The gardener's hands were chapped and rough from years of working in the soil. 园丁的手因多年在土壤中工作而干裂粗糙。


近义词：cracked, fissured, dry, sore

反义词：smooth, moisturized, supple


词性：形容词
