grainy:/ˈɡreɪni/| adj.|颗粒状的，粗糙的，模糊的|Having the appearance or texture of grains; lacking sharpness or clarity, especially in an image.|The old photograph was grainy and difficult to see. 这张旧照片颗粒感很强，很难看清。The grainy texture of the sand was rough against my skin. 沙子的粗糙质感摩擦着我的皮肤。|近义词：coarse, rough, blurry, indistinct|反义词：smooth, clear, sharp


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The image is grainy. = 图片颗粒感很强。
The texture of the bread is grainy. = 面包的质地很粗糙。

一般过去时:
The film was grainy. = 胶片颗粒感很强。
