lovebird:/ˈlʌvˌbɜːrd/| n. |爱情鸟，情侣|A small parrot, often kept as a pet, known for its strong pair bonds and affectionate behavior.  Many species exhibit lifelong pair bonding.|例句：They gave us a pair of lovebirds as a wedding gift.他们送给我们一对爱情鸟作为结婚礼物。

例句：The lovebirds were inseparable, always cuddling and preening each other. 这对爱情鸟形影不离，总是依偎在一起互相梳理羽毛。|近义词：parakeet, small parrot|反义词:None.
