gathered:/ˈɡæðərd/| v.| 收集；聚集；(使)皱起 | To bring together; to collect; to assemble; to wrinkle. |例句：She gathered her belongings and left. 她收拾好东西离开了。|例句：The crowd gathered to hear the speaker. 人群聚集起来听演讲者讲话。|近义词：collected, assembled, amassed, congregated |反义词：scattered, dispersed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gathers wildflowers every weekend. = 他每个周末都采集野花。
They gather at the park every evening. = 他们每天晚上都在公园里集合。


一般过去时:
She gathered all the evidence for the case. = 她收集了此案的所有证据。
The students gathered in the auditorium. = 学生们聚集在大礼堂。


现在完成时:
We have gathered enough information to make a decision. = 我们已经收集到足够的信息来做出决定。
The clouds have gathered, it's going to rain. =乌云密布，要下雨了。


过去完成时:
They had gathered all the necessary materials before starting the project. = 他们开始项目之前已经收集了所有必要的材料。
She had gathered her courage before speaking to the boss. = 在跟老板说话之前，她已经鼓起了勇气。


将来时:
We will gather for dinner at seven o'clock. = 我们七点钟一起吃晚饭。
The troops will gather at dawn. = 部队将在黎明时分集合。


虚拟语气:
If I had gathered more information, I would have made a better decision. = 如果我收集了更多信息，我就会做出更好的决定。
If they had gathered earlier, they wouldn't have missed the train. = 如果他们早点集合，就不会错过火车了。


被动语态:
The evidence was gathered by the police. = 证据是由警方收集的。
The crops were gathered in the autumn. = 收获是在秋天进行的。


疑问句:
Where did they gather last night? = 他们昨晚在哪里集合？
Have you gathered all the necessary ingredients? = 你已经收集所有必要的材料了吗？


否定句:
He didn't gather enough support for his proposal. = 他没有为他的提案获得足够的支持。
They didn't gather at the usual time. = 他们没有在通常的时间集合。
