load's: /loʊdz/ | n. |  负载；大量；许多 |  A large amount or quantity of something.  It is often used informally. |例句：There's a load's of washing to do.  = 有好多衣服要洗。


例句：He's got loads of money. = 他有很多钱。|近义词：lots of, a lot of, plenty of, heaps of |反义词：a little, a few, hardly any, scarce

USAGE->
一般现在时:
There's loads of work to do. = 有很多工作要做。
There are loads of apples in the basket. = 篮子里有很多苹果。

一般过去时:
There were loads of people at the concert. = 音乐会上有很多的人。
There was loads of food left over. = 剩下很多食物。

现在完成时:
There have been loads of changes since I last visited. = 自从我上次来访以来发生了很多变化。
We've had loads of fun this summer. = 今年夏天我们玩得很开心。

过去完成时:
There had been loads of rain before the drought. = 干旱之前下了很多雨。
They had loads of problems to deal with before the project started. = 项目开始前他们有很多问题要处理。

将来时:
There will be loads of people at the party. = 聚会上将会有很多人。
We'll have loads of time to relax next week. = 下周我们将有很多时间放松。

虚拟语气:
If there were loads of money, we could solve the problem. = 如果有很多钱，我们就能解决这个问题。
I wish there were loads of time for this project. = 我希望这个项目有很多时间。

被动语态:
Loads of work needs to be done. = 需要做很多工作。
Loads of questions were asked during the meeting. = 会议期间提出了很多问题。

疑问句:
Are there loads of seats available? = 还有很多座位吗？
Do you have loads of homework tonight? = 你今晚有很多作业吗？

否定句:
There aren't loads of apples left. = 苹果没剩下多少了。
He doesn't have loads of friends. = 他没有很多朋友。
