reedbuck:/ˈriːdbʌk/| n. |苇羚|A small to medium-sized antelope found in Africa, known for its reddish-brown coat and its habitat in reed beds and grasslands.  Reedbucks are generally shy and elusive animals. |Example: We spotted a reedbuck grazing near the riverbank. 我们在河岸边看到一只苇羚在吃草。|近义词：antelope, gazelle |反义词:  (no direct antonym)|名词
