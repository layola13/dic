doe:/doʊ/| n.|母鹿，雌兔，母山羊|A female deer, rabbit, or goat.|The doe grazed peacefully in the meadow.  母鹿在草地上平静地吃草。|The doe gave birth to twins.  母鹿生了一对双胞胎。|hind, ewe (for goat)| buck (for deer), ram (for goat)|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The doe eats grass. = 母鹿吃草。
The doe runs swiftly. = 母鹿跑得很快。


一般过去时:
The doe ate the leaves. = 母鹿吃了树叶。
The hunter saw a doe. = 猎人看到一只母鹿。


现在完成时:
The doe has grazed in this field all day. = 母鹿整天都在这个田里吃草。
The farmer has seen many does this year. = 今年农民看到了许多母鹿。


过去完成时:
The doe had already run away before the hunter arrived. = 在猎人到达之前，母鹿已经跑开了。
The dogs had chased the doe into the woods. = 狗们已经把母鹿追进了树林。


将来时:
The doe will give birth next spring. = 母鹿将在明年春天分娩。
The hunters will look for does tomorrow. = 猎人明天将寻找母鹿。


虚拟语气:
If the doe were not so quick, the hunter would have caught her. = 如果母鹿不那么快，猎人就会抓住它了。
I wish the doe had not been injured. = 我希望母鹿没有受伤。


被动语态:
The doe was seen by the hikers. = 母鹿被徒步旅行者看到了。
The doe is being watched by the wildlife photographer. = 母鹿正在被野生动物摄影师观察。


疑问句:
Is that a doe? = 那是一只母鹿吗？
Did you see the doe? = 你看到母鹿了吗？


否定句:
That is not a doe; it's a fawn. = 那不是母鹿；那是小鹿。
I did not see a doe today. = 我今天没有看到母鹿。
