excusals:/ɪkˈskjuːzəl/| n. |辩解；借口；道歉 |Plural of excuse.  A statement offered as a reason for a fault or offence.  It often implies an attempt to justify an unacceptable action or behavior. |例句：His excusals for his lateness were unconvincing.  他的迟到理由令人难以信服。

例句：Her excusals were numerous but none were truly valid. 她的辩解很多，但没有一个是真正有效的。


近义词：apologies, justifications, explanations, defenses


反义词：admissions, confessions, acceptances


词性：名词
