newsworthiest: /ˈnjuːzˌwɜːrðiɪst/ | adj. | 最值得报道的，最重要的新闻 | Most worthy of reporting; of greatest news value.  The adjective is the superlative form of "newsworthy." It implies that something is more significant or interesting than other news items and thus warrants the most attention. |


例句：

The earthquake was the newsworthiest event of the week.  = 这场地震是本周最值得报道的事件。

The president's speech was considered the newsworthiest story by major news outlets. = 总统的演讲被主要新闻媒体认为是最重要的新闻。


近义词：most important, most significant, most compelling, top news


反义词：insignificant, unimportant, trivial, inconsequential


词性：形容词
