conceptualized:/kənˈsɛptʃuəlaɪzd/| vt.|概念化，设想|To form a concept or idea of something; to imagine or visualize something.|例句：The architect conceptualized a stunning new building design. 建筑师构想了一个令人惊叹的新建筑设计。|例句：She conceptualized the project as a series of interconnected modules. 她将这个项目概念化为一系列相互关联的模块。|近义词：envisioned, visualized, imagined|反义词：materialized, realized


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conceptualizes new marketing strategies every week. = 他每周都会构想新的营销策略。
The artist conceptualizes his work through intense meditation. = 这位艺术家通过冥想来构想他的作品。

一般过去时:
They conceptualized the plan during a brainstorming session. = 他们在头脑风暴会议期间构想了这个计划。
The writer conceptualized the novel's plot over several months. = 作家花了几个月的时间构思了这部小说的情节。

现在完成时:
She has conceptualized several award-winning designs. = 她已经构想出几个屡获殊荣的设计。
The team has conceptualized a new approach to the problem. = 该团队已经构想出了解决这个问题的新方法。

过去完成时:
They had conceptualized the project before seeking funding. = 他们在寻求资金之前就已经构思好了这个项目。
The artist had conceptualized the painting's composition before beginning to paint. = 艺术家在开始绘画之前就已经构思好了绘画的构图。

将来时:
They will conceptualize a new product line next quarter. = 他们将在下个季度构想一个新的产品线。
The company will conceptualize a new marketing campaign for the upcoming holiday season. = 公司将为即将到来的假日季节构想一个新的营销活动。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would conceptualize a more detailed plan. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会构想一个更详细的计划。
I wish I could conceptualize a solution to this complex problem. = 我希望我能构想出一个解决这个复杂问题的方案。

被动语态:
The project was conceptualized by a team of experts. = 这个项目是由一个专家团队构想的。
A new business model was conceptualized to improve efficiency. = 为了提高效率，一个新的商业模式被构想出来。

疑问句:

否定句:
