perdurable:/pərˈdʒʊərəbl/ | adj. | 持久的；永久的；耐久的 | Lasting for a very long time; enduring.  The word emphasizes the ability to withstand the passage of time and decay. |  The pyramids are a testament to the durable nature of some ancient building techniques.  埃及金字塔证明了一些古代建筑技术的持久性。
