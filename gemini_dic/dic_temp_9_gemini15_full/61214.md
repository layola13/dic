elysian:/ɪˈlɪʒən/| adj.|极乐的；天堂似的；幸福的 | Relating to or characteristic of Elysium; blissful; delightful.  Elysian fields or Elysium is a paradise in Greek mythology. The word evokes a sense of idyllic beauty, peace, and happiness, often associated with landscapes and experiences that are exceptionally pleasant and serene. |例句：The sunset painted the sky in elysian hues.  日落给天空涂上了极乐般的色彩。

The island was described as an elysian paradise.  这个岛屿被描述成一个极乐的乐园。


近义词：heavenly, blissful, paradisiacal, utopian |反义词：hellish, infernal, nightmarish
