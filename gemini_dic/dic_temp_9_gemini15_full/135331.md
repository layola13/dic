nonpejoratively:/ˌnɒnˈpɛdʒəreɪtɪvli/ | adv. |非贬义地，非轻蔑地 | Without intending to cause offense or express contempt.  It's used to clarify that a word or phrase is being used neutrally, not to insult or belittle. | Example:  "He referred to the situation as 'chaotic,' nonpejoratively, simply describing the events as they unfolded." 他将情况称为“混乱的”，并非贬义，只是在描述事件的展开情况。| 近义词：neutrally, objectively, dispassionately | 反义词：pejoratively, contemptuously, disparagingly
