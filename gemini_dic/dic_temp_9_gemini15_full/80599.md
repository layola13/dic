googs:/ɡuːɡz/| n.| 谷歌（非正式说法）|  A slang term for Google, the popular search engine.  |Example: I googled it and found the answer.  我谷歌了一下找到了答案。|近义词：Google (formal)|反义词：无


USAGE->
