meanor:/mɪˈniər/| n. |举止；行为；风度|A person's appearance and behavior; their demeanor.  It often reflects their inner feelings or character. Demeanor can be positive or negative, formal or informal.  |例句：His demeanor was calm and confident despite the pressure. 他尽管承受着压力，但举止依然镇定自信。


例句：Her friendly demeanor put everyone at ease. 她友好的举止使每个人都感到轻松自在。


近义词：bearing, conduct, manner, mien, deportment|反义词：None directly opposite, but could be contrasted with terms like:  aggressiveness (if demeanor is meek),  disrespect (if demeanor is respectful) |名词
