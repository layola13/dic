millionairess:/ˌmɪljəˈnɛːrɛs/| n. |女百万富翁|A woman who has assets worth one million or more.  This word is formed by adding the feminine suffix "-ess" to "millionaire".|She is a successful businesswoman and a self-made millionairess.|近义词：wealthy woman, rich woman|反义词：pauper, beggar
