invariability: /ˌɪnˌveriəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不变性；稳定性 | The state or quality of being invariable; unchangingness.  It refers to the characteristic of something that remains constant or does not change. |例句：The invariability of the laws of physics is a fundamental principle. 物理定律的不变性是一个基本原则。

The invariability of her routine surprised everyone.  她例行的活动一成不变让每个人都感到惊讶。|近义词：constancy, stability, uniformity, permanence |反义词：variability, changeability, fluctuation, instability |词性: 名词
