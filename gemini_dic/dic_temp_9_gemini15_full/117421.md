medieval:/ˌmediˈiːvəl/ | adj. | 中世纪的；中古的 | Relating to the Middle Ages in Europe (roughly from the 5th to the 15th century).  The term often evokes images of castles, knights, and feudal society. | Example: The castle is a magnificent example of medieval architecture.  这座城堡是中世纪建筑的壮丽典范。|近义词：mediaeval,  ancient (in a broad sense) | 反义词：modern, contemporary
