sealers:/ˈsiːlərz/| n.| 密封剂；密封者；海豹捕猎者 | Substances used to seal or make something airtight or waterproof.  People or things that seal something. In historical context, it can refer to hunters of seals.|例句：The plumber used a special sealer to repair the leak. 水管工用一种特殊的密封剂来修理漏水的地方。

The company employs several skilled sealers for their high-quality products. 这家公司雇佣了几位熟练的密封工人来生产高质量的产品。

近义词：sealing agents, caulkers, sealants | 反义词：openers, unsealers | 词性：名词
