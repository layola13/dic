rechallenges:/ˌriːˈtʃæləndʒɪz/ | v. |再次挑战，重新挑战 | To challenge again; to issue a new challenge.  |例句：The athlete rechallenged his opponent after the controversial decision. 运动员在有争议的判决后再次向对手发起挑战。
例句：The scientist rechallenged the existing theory with new evidence. 科学家用新的证据再次挑战了现有的理论。|近义词：re-contest, challenge anew |反义词：accept, concede
