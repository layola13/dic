salvation:/sælˈveɪʃən/| n. |救赎；拯救；解救 |The act of saving or being saved from sin, error, or evil.  It often refers to spiritual salvation, but can also apply to rescue from physical danger or other difficult situations. |例句：His faith offered him salvation from despair.他的信仰给了他摆脱绝望的救赎。

例句：The life raft provided salvation from the stormy sea.救生筏使他们从暴风雨的大海中得救。


近义词：redemption, deliverance, rescue, liberation |反义词：damnation, ruin, destruction |词性:n.
