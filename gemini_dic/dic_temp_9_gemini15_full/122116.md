mistrained:/mɪsˈtreɪnd/| adj.|训练错误的，训练不当的|Incorrectly or improperly trained.  The term implies a flaw in the training process itself, leading to inadequate or harmful results.  It suggests a deficiency in the method, technique, or guidance used during training.|例句：The mistrained dog continued to bite despite extensive obedience classes.  这条训练错误的狗即使经过大量的服从性训练，仍然继续咬人。

The mistrained soldiers were ill-prepared for combat.  这些训练不当的士兵对战斗毫无准备。|近义词：poorly trained, inadequately trained, ill-trained, improperly trained|反义词：well-trained, properly trained, expertly trained|

词源:  The word is a combination of the prefix "mis-" (meaning "badly" or "wrongly") and the past participle of the verb "train."  The past participle "trained" indicates a completed action of training.
