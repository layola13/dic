nonnervously: /ˌnɒnˈnɜːvəsli/ | adv. | 不紧张地，平静地 |  The word "nonnervously" is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the adverb "nervously". It describes an action or state performed or experienced without nervousness or anxiety.  | Example: He spoke nonnervously to the large crowd, showing no signs of stage fright. 他平静地对着一大群人讲话，没有表现出任何怯场迹象。 | 近义词：calmly, serenely, composedly, peacefully | 反义词：nervously, anxiously, uneasily, tensely
