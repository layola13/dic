inter-pollinated: /ˌɪntərˈpɑːləneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 异花授粉的 |  Pollinated by pollen from a different plant of the same species.  This process promotes genetic diversity within a plant population. | Example:  Many fruit trees are inter-pollinated, requiring the presence of multiple trees for successful fruit production.  许多果树是异花授粉的，需要多种树木才能成功结果。 | 近义词: cross-pollinated | 反义词: self-pollinated
