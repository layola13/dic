redrove:/ˌriːˈdroʊv/| v.|重新驾驶；再开动|To drive again or repeatedly.  It implies a return journey or repeated trips.|例句：He redrove the route to make sure he hadn't missed anything. 他重新开车走了一遍路线，以确保自己没有错过任何东西。
例句：The mechanic redrove the engine to test its performance. 技师重新启动发动机以测试其性能。|近义词：re-drive, drive again, travel again|反义词：stop, park
