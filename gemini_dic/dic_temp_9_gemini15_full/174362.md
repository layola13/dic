quicksilvery: /ˈkwɪksɪlvəri/ | adj. | 活泼的；变化迅速的；闪烁的；轻快的 |  Characterized by a quick, lively, and changeable nature, like quicksilver (mercury).  It suggests a sparkling, fleeting, and often unpredictable quality. |例句：His quicksilvery wit kept the conversation lively. 他机智敏捷，使谈话生动有趣。


例句：The quicksilvery movements of the dancer captivated the audience. 舞者的轻快灵巧的动作迷住了观众。


近义词： mercurial, volatile, flighty, agile, sprightly | 反义词： sluggish, slow, steady, ponderous, lethargic | 词性：形容词


词源:  The word derives from "quicksilver," an archaic name for mercury.  "Quick" means lively or fast, reflecting mercury's rapid movement, and "silver" refers to its silvery appearance.  The "-y" suffix transforms the noun into an adjective, indicating a quality or characteristic.
