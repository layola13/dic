prerefined:/ˌpriːrɪˈfaɪnd/| adj.|预先精制的，预先提炼的 |  Already refined before a particular process or use.  It implies a higher level of refinement than simply "refined." |例句：The prerefined oil was ready for immediate use in the manufacturing process. 预先精制的油可以直接用于制造过程。

The prerefined sugar was used to make a delicate dessert. 预先精制的糖被用来制作精致的甜点。|近义词：preprocessed, purified, pre-treated |反义词：unrefined, crude, raw |词性: adjective
