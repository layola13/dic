cherry-picked:/ˈtʃɛri pɪkt/| adj.|精选的，挑选出来的，经过挑选的|Selecting only the best or most favorable examples, often in a way that is misleading or dishonest.  This implies a biased selection, omitting less favorable data. |例句：The company's report cherry-picked data to exaggerate its success. 这家公司的报告挑选了数据来夸大其成功。

例句：He cherry-picked the best arguments to support his position. 他挑选了最好的论据来支持他的立场。


近义词：selected, handpicked, chosen, biased, skewed, manipulated

反义词：random, unbiased, representative, inclusive


词性：形容词
