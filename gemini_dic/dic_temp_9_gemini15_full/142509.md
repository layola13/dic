ospreys:/ˈɒspreɪz/ | n. |鱼鹰（复数）|A large bird of prey with a white head and brown back, which catches fish.  It is also known as the sea hawk or fish hawk.  |例句：Osprey nests are often built on tall trees near water. 鱼鹰的巢穴通常建在靠近水边的参天大树上。

例句：We saw several ospreys diving for fish in the lake. 我们看到几只鱼鹰在湖里捕鱼。


近义词：fish hawk, sea hawk


反义词：无


词性：名词
