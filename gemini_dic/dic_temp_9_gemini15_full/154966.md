pestle:/ˈpɛstl/| n.|研杵，杵；捣碎|A pestle is a tool used to grind substances in a mortar.  It is typically made of hard material such as wood, stone, or metal, and is used by hand. |例句：She used a pestle and mortar to grind the spices. 她用研钵和研杵研磨香料。

例句：The cook crushed the garlic with a pestle.  厨师用研杵捣碎了大蒜。


近义词：mortar (when used together), grinder|反义词：None


词源：源自古法语 *pestel，最终源自拉丁语 *pistillum，“杵”，与“pistillus”（小杵）有关。
