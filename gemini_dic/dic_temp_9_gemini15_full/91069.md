hutches:/ˈhʌtʃɪz/| n.|兔笼；食槽；(尤指成排的)储物柜|A hutch is a cage for rabbits or other small animals, or a cupboard or container for storing things, often in rows.  |例句：The rabbits are kept in clean hutches.  兔子被关在干净的兔笼里。

例句：The kitchen has built-in hutches for storing dishes. 厨房有内置的储物柜用来存放餐具。


近义词：cage, coop, cupboard, cabinet|反义词：None

词源:
The word "hutch" ultimately derives from the Old Norse word *húsa, meaning "house" or "dwelling".  Over time, its meaning narrowed to refer specifically to a rabbit's dwelling and later to other types of storage containers.
