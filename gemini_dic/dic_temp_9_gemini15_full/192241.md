scream: /skriːm/ | v., n. | 尖叫；喊叫 | To utter a loud, sharp cry, especially one expressing pain, fear, or excitement.  A loud, sharp cry. | 近义词：shout, yell, shriek | 反义词：whisper, murmur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He screams at the top of his lungs. = 他大声尖叫。
The baby screams when it's hungry. = 宝宝饿了就哭闹。

一般过去时:
She screamed in terror when she saw the snake. = 她看到蛇时吓得尖叫起来。
The crowd screamed with excitement. = 人群兴奋地尖叫。

现在完成时:
The child has screamed all afternoon. = 这孩子整个下午都在尖叫。
I have never screamed so loud in my life. = 我一生中从未如此大声尖叫过。

过去完成时:
He had screamed for help before he fainted. = 他昏倒之前曾大声呼救。
She realized she had screamed when she saw the blood. = 她看到血后才意识到自己尖叫了。

将来时:
They will scream if they see a mouse. = 如果他们看到老鼠，他们会尖叫。
I think the audience will scream when the star appears. = 我想当明星出现时观众会尖叫。

虚拟语气:
If I saw a spider, I would scream. = 如果我看到蜘蛛，我会尖叫。
I wish I hadn't screamed so loudly. = 我真希望自己没有那么大声尖叫。

被动语态:
A scream was heard in the night. = 夜里听到一声尖叫。
Her name was screamed from the rooftops. = 她的名字被从屋顶上喊叫出来。

疑问句:
Did you scream when you fell? = 你摔倒的时候尖叫了吗？
Why did they scream? = 他们为什么尖叫？

否定句:
He didn't scream when he was hurt. = 他受伤时没有尖叫。
She didn't scream, but she was terrified. = 她没有尖叫，但她很害怕。


PHRASE->
scream at sb: 对某人大喊大叫
scream with laughter: 哈哈大笑
scream blue murder: 大声呼救，大声抱怨
let out a scream: 发出一阵尖叫
scream for joy: 欣喜若狂地尖叫
