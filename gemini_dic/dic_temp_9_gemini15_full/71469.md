flames:/fleɪmz/| n.|火焰，烈火|Burning gases emitting visible light and heat.|The campfire flames danced in the night breeze.  篝火在夜风中摇曳。 The building was engulfed in flames.  建筑物被大火吞噬。|近义词：fires, blaze|反义词：cold, ice


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The fire flames brightly. = 火焰明亮地燃烧着。
The candles flames flicker gently. = 蜡烛的火焰轻轻地闪烁。


一般过去时:
The flames leaped high into the air. = 火焰窜向高空。
The flames died down slowly. = 火焰慢慢熄灭了。


现在完成时:
The flames have consumed the entire forest. = 火焰已经吞噬了整个森林。
The flames have spread rapidly. = 火焰迅速蔓延。


过去完成时:
The flames had already spread before the firefighters arrived. = 消防员到达之前，火焰已经蔓延开来。
The flames had almost completely died down by morning. = 到早上，火焰几乎完全熄灭了。


将来时:
The flames will likely spread to the nearby houses. = 火焰很可能会蔓延到附近的房屋。
The flames will be extinguished soon. = 火焰很快就会被扑灭。


虚拟语气:
If the flames were higher, we could see them from here. = 如果火焰更高，我们就能从这里看到它们。
I wish the flames were not so intense. = 我希望火焰不要那么强烈。


被动语态:
The building was consumed by the flames. = 建筑物被火焰吞噬。
The flames were quickly extinguished by the firefighters. = 火焰被消防员迅速扑灭。


疑问句:
Are the flames getting bigger? = 火焰越来越大了吗？
Do you see the flames? = 你看到火焰了吗？


否定句:
The flames didn't reach the house. = 火焰没有到达房子。
There were no flames visible at that moment. = 那时没有看到火焰。
