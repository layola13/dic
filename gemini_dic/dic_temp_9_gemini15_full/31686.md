chaunt:/tʃɑːnt/ | v. |吟唱，诵读 | To sing or recite in a monotonous tone, often in a ritualistic or repetitive manner.  | The monks chanted prayers throughout the night.  僧侣整夜都在吟诵祈祷文。|The crowd chanted the team's name.  人群高呼着球队的名字。| sing, recite, intone | shout, whisper


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They chant prayers every morning. = 他们每天早上都吟诵祈祷文。
The fans chant the team's name during the game. = 球迷在比赛期间高呼球队的名字。

一般过去时:
The choir chanted a beautiful hymn. = 合唱团吟唱了一首优美的圣歌。
The protesters chanted slogans in the street. = 抗议者在街上高喊口号。

现在完成时:
They have chanted this mantra for years. = 他们多年来一直吟诵这句咒语。
The monks have chanted their prayers for centuries. = 僧侣们几个世纪以来一直在吟诵他们的祈祷文。

过去完成时:
They had chanted the verses before the ceremony began. = 仪式开始前他们已经吟诵了经文。
The audience had chanted the singer's name before she even appeared on stage. = 歌手甚至还没上台，观众就已经高呼她的名字了。

将来时:
The choir will chant a Gregorian chant at the service. = 合唱团将在仪式上吟唱一首格里高利圣咏。
We will chant our support for the team throughout the match. = 我们将在整场比赛中高呼我们对球队的支持。

虚拟语气:
If they chanted louder, they might be heard. = 如果他们喊得更大声，或许就能被听到。
I wish they would chant more enthusiastically. = 我希望他们能更热情地吟唱。

被动语态:
The hymn was chanted beautifully by the choir. = 圣歌被合唱团优美地吟唱。
The slogans were chanted by the protesters throughout the demonstration. = 口号在整个示威活动中都被抗议者高喊。

疑问句:
What did they chant during the procession? = 他们在游行期间吟唱了什么？
Will they chant the national anthem before the game? = 他们会在比赛前吟唱国歌吗？

否定句:
They did not chant during the quiet moment. = 他们在安静的时刻没有吟唱。
The crowd didn't chant the opposing team's name. = 人群没有高呼对方队名的。


PHRASE->
chant down =  压制；使沉默
chant along =  跟着一起唱
chant a spell =  念咒语
chant in unison =  齐声吟唱
chant for =  为...而高呼


词源:
From Old French *chanter, from Latin cantare "to sing."  The sense shifted from general singing to a specific type of repetitive, often ritualistic singing.
