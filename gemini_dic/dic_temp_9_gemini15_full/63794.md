epidemically:/ˌɛpɪˈdɛmɪkli/ | adv. | 流行病般地；像流行病一样地 |  Describes something spreading rapidly and widely, like an epidemic.  It often implies a negative connotation, suggesting a rapid and uncontrolled spread of something undesirable. | Example: The rumor spread epidemically throughout the school.  谣言像瘟疫一样在学校里传播开来。 | 近义词：rapidly, swiftly, widely, extensively | 反义词：slowly, gradually, sparsely

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The disease spreads epidemically in this region. = 这种疾病在这个地区迅速蔓延。
Rumors spread epidemically among the students. = 谣言在学生中迅速传播。


一般过去时:
