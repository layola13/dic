lowe:/loʊ/| vt.|降低；减低；贬低；使谦卑|to make something lower or less in amount, value, quality, or degree; to humble or degrade someone.|例句：The company lowered its prices to attract more customers. 公司降低了价格以吸引更多顾客。
例句：He lowered his head in shame. 他羞愧地低下了头。|近义词：reduce, decrease, diminish, humble|反义词：raise, increase, elevate, exalt|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lowers the volume of the TV. = 他把电视音量调低了。
They lower their expectations. = 他们降低了他们的期望。

一般过去时:
She lowered the flag. = 她降下了旗帜。
The government lowered taxes. = 政府降低了税收。

现在完成时:
He has lowered his standards. = 他降低了他的标准。
They have lowered the price of the product. = 他们已经降低了产品的价格。

过去完成时:
She had lowered her voice before speaking. = 她说话前已经压低了声音。
The company had lowered its profits last quarter. = 公司上个季度利润已经下降了。

将来时:
They will lower the interest rate. = 他们将会降低利率。
He will lower the boom on them. = 他将严厉惩罚他们。

虚拟语气:
If they lowered the prices, we would buy more. = 如果他们降低价格，我们会买更多。
I wish they lowered the rent. = 我希望他们能降低房租。

被动语态:
The prices were lowered significantly. = 价格被大幅降低了。
The flag was lowered at sunset. = 旗帜在日落时被降下。

疑问句:
Will they lower the bridge? = 他们会降低桥梁高度吗？
Did they lower the voting age? = 他们是否降低了投票年龄？

否定句:
They did not lower the speed limit. = 他们没有降低速度限制。
He will not lower himself to that level. = 他不会自贬到那种程度。

词源:
The word "lowe" ultimately derives from Old English *lūgian, meaning "to bend, bow, incline."  This root also gave rise to other words related to bending or lowering, showing a consistent semantic trajectory over time.
