prototype:/ˈproʊtoʊtaɪp/| n. |原型，雏形，样品 |A prototype is an original model of something from which other forms are developed.  It is a first, typical or preliminary model of something, from which other forms are developed or copied.|例句：The company unveiled a prototype of its new electric car. 这家公司展示了其新型电动汽车的原型车。|例句： Engineers are working on a prototype for a new type of aircraft engine. 工程师们正在研发一种新型飞机发动机的原型。|近义词：model, sample, example, archetype |反义词：final product, finished product
