词源:
The word "photostater" is a blend of "photo" (from "photography," referring to the use of light in the copying process) and "stater" (a shortened form of "stat," short for "statim," meaning "immediately").  This reflects the speed and immediacy of photocopying.  It's a relatively straightforward compound word.
