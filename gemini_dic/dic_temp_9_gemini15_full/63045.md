enriched:/ɪnˈrɪtʃt/| adj.|丰富的；增强的；改进的|Made richer or better in quality; improved.  The enriched soil produced a bountiful harvest.|近义词：enhanced, improved, amplified|反义词：impoverished, depleted, weakened|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The program enriches the lives of many students. = 这项计划丰富了许多学生的生活。
This experience enriches my understanding of the world. = 这段经历丰富了我对世界的理解。


一般过去时:
The artist enriched the painting with vibrant colors. = 艺术家用鲜艳的色彩丰富了这幅画。
The teacher enriched the lesson with interesting anecdotes. = 老师用有趣的故事丰富了这节课。


现在完成时:
The new technology has enriched our communication methods. = 新技术丰富了我们的沟通方式。
Their travels have enriched their perspectives on life. = 他们的旅行丰富了他们对生活的看法。


过去完成时:

He had enriched his vocabulary by reading extensively. = 他通过大量阅读丰富了他的词汇量。


将来时:

This investment will enrich the company's product line. = 这项投资将丰富公司的产品线。


虚拟语气:

I wish I had enriched my life with more experiences. = 我希望我的生活有更多体验。


被动语态:

疑问句:

The lack of interaction didn't enrich the online learning experience. = 缺乏互动并没有丰富在线学习体验。
