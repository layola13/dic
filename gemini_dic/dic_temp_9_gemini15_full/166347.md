preremittances: /ˌpriːrɪˈmɪtənsɪz/ | n. | 预付汇款；预先汇款 |  Payments sent in advance of goods or services.  Pre-remittances are often used in international trade to ensure that the recipient will receive payment before releasing goods or providing services. This mitigates risk for the payer. | Example: The importer arranged preremittances to secure the timely delivery of the crucial components.  进口商安排预付汇款以确保及时交付关键部件。|近义词：advance payment, prepayment, upfront payment |反义词：postpayment, arrears |名词
