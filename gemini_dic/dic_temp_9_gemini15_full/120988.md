miscite:/ˈmɪsaɪt/| vt.| 错引，误引 | To quote (a passage, book, author, etc.) incorrectly or inaccurately.  |例句：The student miscited the source in his essay.  学生在他的论文中错误引用了来源。

例句：He miscited a statistic in his presentation, leading to confusion. 他在他的演示文稿中错误引用了一个统计数据，导致了混乱。


近义词：misquote, misrepresent, paraphrase incorrectly |反义词：cite correctly, accurately quote
