noodled:/ˈnuːdəld/| v.|随意弹奏（尤指吉他）；漫无目的地思考；闲逛；随意地做某事|to play a musical instrument, especially a guitar, in a casual or improvisational way; to think or daydream idly; to wander aimlessly; to do something casually or without much planning|例句：He noodled around on the guitar for a while. 他随意弹了一会儿吉他。

例句：I spent the afternoon noodling around the city. 我在城里闲逛了一下午。


近义词：improvise, strum, dawdle, wander, fiddle


反义词：plan, organize, focus, concentrate


词性：动词

USAGE->

He noodles around on his guitar every evening. = 他每天晚上都随意弹吉他。
She noodles around on her phone during meetings. = 她在会议期间随意玩手机。
