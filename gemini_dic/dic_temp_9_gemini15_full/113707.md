macro-economics: /ˌmækroʊˌiːkəˈnɑːmɪks/ | n. |宏观经济学 | The branch of economics that studies the behavior and performance of an economy as a whole.  It focuses on aggregate indicators such as inflation, unemployment, and economic growth. | Example:  Macroeconomics examines the overall performance of the national economy. 宏观经济学研究的是国民经济的整体运行状况。 | 近义词: aggregate economics | 反义词: microeconomics

Macroeconomics studies inflation and unemployment. = 宏观经济学研究通货膨胀和失业。
The government analyzes macroeconomic indicators. = 政府分析宏观经济指标。
