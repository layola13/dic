postsigns: /ˈpoʊstsaɪnz/ | n. |告示牌，路标|  Signs posted in a public place to give information or instructions.  These signs are usually fixed to posts or other supports.| 例句：The postsigns clearly indicated the direction to the beach.  告示牌清楚地指明了去海滩的方向。| 近义词：signs, notices, billboards, markers | 反义词：None (no direct antonym) | 名词
