school:/skuːl/| n. |学校；学院；学派；鱼群；一群|A place where children go to learn.  It can also refer to a particular type of institution (e.g., medical school), a group of fish, or a group of people with similar ideas.|例句：My children go to a good school. 我的孩子们在一所好学校上学。|例句：She attended law school after graduating from university. 她大学毕业后上了法学院。|近义词：academy, college, institute, seminary|反义词：home

He goes to school by bus. = 他乘公共汽车去上学。

一般过去时:
I went to school in London. = 我在伦敦上学。
We studied hard at school. = 我们在学校努力学习。

现在完成时:
She has attended this school for five years. = 她已经在这所学校上了五年学了。
They have finished their school projects. = 他们已经完成了学校的项目。

过去完成时:
I had already finished my homework before school started. = 在学校上课之前我已经完成了作业。
She had never been to such a big school before. = 她以前从未去过这么大的学校。

将来时:
He will go to school tomorrow. = 他明天要去上学。
We will have a school trip next week. = 我们下周将有一次学校旅行。

虚拟语气:
If I were at school, I would see my friends. = 如果我在学校，我会看到我的朋友们。
I wish I were still at school. = 我真希望我还在上学。

被动语态:
The school was built in 1950. = 这所学校建于1950年。
The students are taught by experienced teachers. = 学生们由经验丰富的老师教学。

疑问句:
What school do you go to? = 你上哪所学校？
What time does school finish? = 学校几点放学？

否定句:

She didn't attend school yesterday. = 她昨天没去上学。
