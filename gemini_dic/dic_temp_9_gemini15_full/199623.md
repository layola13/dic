sinneress:/ˈsɪnəs/| n.| 女罪人，罪孽深重的女人 | A sinneress is a woman who has committed sins, often implying a significant or habitual transgression against moral or religious principles.  The term often carries a stronger connotation of wickedness or depravity than simply "sinner".|例句：She was branded a sinneress by the puritanical society.|近义词：wicked woman, evil woman, transgressor, offender|反义词：saint, angel, virtuous woman
