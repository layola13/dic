diametral:/daɪˈæmɪtrəl/| adj.|直径的；对径的；完全相反的；截然不同的|completely opposite in nature or character;  relating to or along a diameter.  |例句：Their views are diametrically opposed. 他们的观点截然相反。

例句：The diametrically opposed opinions on this issue made a compromise nearly impossible.  在这个问题上截然相反的意见使得妥协几乎不可能。


近义词：opposite, contrary, antithetical

反义词：identical, similar, alike

词性：形容词
