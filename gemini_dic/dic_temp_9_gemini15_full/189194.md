sacred:/ˈseɪkrɪd/| adj.|神圣的，庄严的，不可侵犯的|Consecrated to or belonging to a god; extremely holy.  Something sacred is considered to be holy and deserving of respect. It often carries religious or spiritual significance, and should not be treated lightly or disrespected.|例句：The temple is a sacred place.  这座寺庙是神圣的地方。

例句：The ancient forest is considered sacred by the indigenous people.  这片古老的森林被当地土著居民视为神圣之地。


近义词：holy, consecrated, hallowed, divine, inviolable|反义词：profane, unholy, secular, common, ordinary
