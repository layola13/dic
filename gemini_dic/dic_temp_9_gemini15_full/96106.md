indefinites:/ɪnˈdɛfɪnɪts/| adj. |不确定的；无限的；无期限的 |  Not clearly defined or specified; vague or uncertain.  Indefinites refers to things that are not clearly defined or specific. It can apply to nouns (like "a cat" vs. "my cat"), pronouns (like "someone" vs. "John"), or articles (like "a" and "an").  | Example: The indefinite nature of the future is both exciting and daunting. 未来不确定的性质既令人兴奋又令人畏惧。 | 近义词：vague, uncertain, unspecified, ambiguous | 反义词：definite, specific, precise, certain |  adj.
