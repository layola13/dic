/poʊ/ | pron. | 啥，什么 |  This is a very informal contraction of "what". It is used in spoken English, primarily in questions and exclamations, to express inquiry, surprise, or disbelief.  It is not considered grammatically correct for formal writing. |  Po you doing? = 你在干嘛？  Po's the matter? = 怎么了？ |  what, huh |  nothing (in the sense of a contrast)
