overtalks:/ˈoʊvərˌtɔːks/| v.|喋喋不休地谈论；冗长地谈论|To talk excessively or for too long; to talk over someone.|例句：He overtalks at meetings, making it hard for others to contribute. 他在会议上喋喋不休，使其他人难以发言。|例句：She overtalks during presentations, losing the audience's attention. 她演示时说得太多，失去了观众的注意力。|近义词：ramble, drone on, chatter excessively|反义词：be concise, be brief, keep silent
