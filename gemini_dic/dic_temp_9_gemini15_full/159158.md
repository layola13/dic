podge:/pɑːdʒ/| n.| 混杂物，杂烩；笨拙的人 |A confused or disordered mass; a clumsy or awkward person.  |例句：The artist created a podge of colors on the canvas.  艺术家在画布上创造了一团杂乱的色彩。|例句：He’s a bit of a podge, always tripping over his own feet. 他有点笨手笨脚，总是绊倒自己。|近义词：mess, jumble, mishmash, klutz |反义词：order, neatness, dexterity
