schematized:/ˈskiːmətaɪzd/ | adj. |  图解的；模式化的；纲要化的 |  represented in the form of a schema or diagram; showing the essential features of something in a simplified or generalized form.  | 例句：The data was schematized to highlight the key trends. 数据被模式化以突出主要趋势。

The lecture was heavily schematized, focusing on the main points rather than details.  讲座高度模式化，侧重于要点而非细节。


近义词：diagrammed, outlined, summarized, simplified | 反义词：detailed, elaborate, complex | 词性：形容词
