permeability:/ˌpɜːrmiəˈbɪləti/| n.|渗透性，透气性，磁导率|The ability of a material to allow liquids or gases to pass through it.  It can also refer to the ability of a material to allow magnetic fields to pass through it.|例句：The permeability of the soil affects the rate of water drainage.  土壤的渗透性影响水的排水速度。

例句：The high permeability of this fabric makes it ideal for sportswear.  这种面料的高透气性使其成为运动服的理想选择。


近义词：perviousness, porosity, transmissibility|反义词：impermeability, impenetrability|名词
