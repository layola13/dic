clucking:/ˈklʌkɪŋ/| v. |咯咯叫声；絮絮叨叨地责备|The sound made by a hen.  It can also refer to a repetitive, disapproving muttering.|例句：The hens were clucking in the yard.  母鸡们在院子里咯咯叫。

The old woman clucked her tongue disapprovingly. 老妇人啧啧地表示不满。|近义词：cackling, chattering, murmuring|反义词：silence, quiet


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hens are clucking loudly. = 母鸡们大声地咯咯叫。
He is constantly clucking about his neighbor's dog. = 他不断地唠叨他邻居的狗。


一般过去时:
The chickens clucked all night. = 鸡整夜都在咯咯叫。
She clucked her disapproval at his messy room. = 她对他凌乱的房间啧啧表示不满。


现在完成时:
The hens have been clucking all morning. = 母鸡们整个上午都在咯咯叫。
She has clucked about this problem for weeks. = 她已经为此问题唠叨了好几周了。


过去完成时:
The hens had been clucking before the fox appeared. = 在狐狸出现之前，母鸡们一直在咯咯叫。
She had clucked at him repeatedly before he finally cleaned his room. = 在他最终打扫房间之前，她已经多次对他唠叨了。


将来时:
The hens will cluck when they see their chicks. = 母鸡们看到小鸡时会咯咯叫。
He will probably cluck about it again tomorrow. = 他明天可能会再次为此唠叨。


虚拟语气:
If the hens clucked, I would know they found food. = 如果母鸡咯咯叫，我就知道它们找到食物了。
If she had clucked less, he might have cleaned his room sooner. = 如果她少唠叨一些，他可能早就打扫房间了。


被动语态:
The clucking sound was heard from the coop. = 从鸡舍里听到了咯咯叫的声音。(很少用被动语态)
His complaints were constantly clucked about by his neighbors. = 他的抱怨不断地被他的邻居们唠叨。(很少用被动语态，更常用主动)


疑问句:
Are the hens clucking? = 母鸡在咯咯叫吗？
Is he constantly clucking about trivial matters? = 他是否不断地为琐事唠叨？


否定句:
The hens weren't clucking this morning. = 今早母鸡没叫。
She didn't cluck at him this time. = 这次她没有对他唠叨。


PHRASE->
