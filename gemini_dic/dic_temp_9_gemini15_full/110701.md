linearized:/ˈlɪniəraɪzd/ | adj. | 线性的；线性化的 |  Made linear;  transformed into a linear form.  The process simplifies complex relationships by approximating them with straight lines or linear equations. This is often used in mathematical modeling and simulations to make complex systems easier to analyze and understand.  | 例句：The linearized model accurately predicts the system's behavior near the operating point.  线性化模型准确地预测了系统在工作点附近的行为。

The linearized version of the equation is much simpler to solve.  该方程的线性化版本更容易求解。


近义词：simplified, streamlined, approximated | 反义词：nonlinear, complex, convoluted | 词性：形容词
