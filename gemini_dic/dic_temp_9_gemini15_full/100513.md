interview:/ˈɪntərvjuː/ | n. vt.|面试；采访；会见 | A formal meeting in which someone is asked questions to assess their suitability for a job or other position, or to collect information for a news report.  |例句：I have a job interview tomorrow.  我明天有一个工作面试。

例句：The journalist conducted an interview with the president.  记者对总统进行了采访。


近义词：audition, interrogation, consultation | 反义词：N/A


词性:Noun, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He interviews candidates every Tuesday. 他每周二都会面试候选人。
The reporter interviews celebrities for her show. 记者为她的节目采访名人。


一般过去时:
She interviewed for a marketing position last week. 她上周面试了一个市场营销职位。
They interviewed several witnesses to the accident. 他们采访了几位事故目击者。


现在完成时:
I have interviewed over fifty applicants this month. 这个月我已经面试了五十多名申请人。
The police have interviewed all the suspects. 警方已经采访了所有嫌疑人。


过去完成时:
They had interviewed all the potential candidates before making a decision. 他们在做出决定之前已经面试了所有潜在的候选人。
The journalist had interviewed the author several times before writing the article. 记者在撰写文章之前已经采访过几次作者。


将来时:
I will interview for a new job next week. 我下周将参加一个新工作的面试。
The company will interview all shortlisted applicants. 公司将面试所有入围的申请人。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would thoroughly prepare for the interview. 如果我是你，我会认真准备面试。
I wish I had known more about the company before the interview. 我希望在面试之前对公司了解更多。


被动语态:
The applicants were interviewed by the hiring manager. 申请人由招聘经理进行面试。
The witnesses were interviewed by the police. 目击者接受了警方的问话。


疑问句:
When will you be interviewed? 你什么时候面试？
Have you ever been interviewed for a job before? 你以前面试过工作吗？


否定句:
They did not interview any unqualified applicants. 他们没有面试任何不合格的申请人。
I have not yet been interviewed for the position. 我还没有就这个职位接受面试。


PHRASE->
job interview: 工作面试
interview for a job: 面试一份工作
conduct an interview: 进行面试
panel interview: 小组面试
prepare for an interview: 准备面试
