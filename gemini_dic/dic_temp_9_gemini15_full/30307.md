cenozoic:/ˌsiːnəˈzoʊɪk/| n. |新生代|The Cenozoic Era is the current geological era, spanning from 66 million years ago to the present day. It is characterized by the diversification of mammals, birds, and flowering plants.  |The Cenozoic Era saw the rise of humans. 新生代见证了人类的崛起。| Mesozoic, Paleozoic |Precambrian|名词
