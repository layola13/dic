metate:/ˈmɛteɪt/ | n. |研磨石，磨盘 | A metate is a flat, usually slightly concave, stone used for grinding grains, seeds, and other materials.  It is typically used with a mano (a hand stone).  | Example:  The ancient civilization used metates to grind corn into flour.  古代文明用研磨石将玉米磨成粉。|近义词：grinding stone, quern |反义词：None (no direct opposite) |名词
