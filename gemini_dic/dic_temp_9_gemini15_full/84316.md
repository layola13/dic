half-winged:/ˌhæfˈwɪŋd/| adj.| 半翅的；(昆虫)前翅退化的 |Having only one pair of fully developed wings, or having wings that are reduced or vestigial.  This term is primarily used in entomology to describe insects with incomplete wing development.  |Example:  The insect specimen displayed a half-winged morphology.  这只昆虫标本显示出半翅形态。


The half-winged fly struggled to take flight.  这只半翅的苍蝇努力地想飞起来。|近义词：partially winged, incompletely winged |反义词：full-winged, fully winged
