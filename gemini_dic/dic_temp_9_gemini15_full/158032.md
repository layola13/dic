planting:/ˈplæntɪŋ/| n. |种植；栽种|The act of putting plants or seeds into the ground to grow.  Planting trees helps the environment. 种树有益于环境。|sowing, cultivation|harvesting|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer is planting corn. = 农民正在种玉米。
They plant flowers every spring. = 他们每年春天都种花。

一般过去时:
We planted a tree in the garden yesterday. = 昨天我们在花园里种了一棵树。
She planted seeds in the pots last week. = 她上周在花盆里播种了。

现在完成时:
They have planted all the seedlings. = 他们已经种好了所有的幼苗。
We have planted a lot of trees this year. = 今年我们种了很多树。

过去完成时:
The gardener had planted the roses before the rain started. = 下雨之前，园丁已经种好了玫瑰。
They had planted the vegetables before the frost came. = 霜冻来临之前，他们已经种好了蔬菜。

将来时:
We will plant more flowers next year. = 明年我们将种植更多鲜花。
They will be planting rice in the paddy fields next month. = 下个月他们将在稻田里种植水稻。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would be planting a vegetable garden. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会种一个菜园。
I wish I had planted more tomatoes. = 我真希望我种了更多的西红柿。

被动语态:
The seeds were planted carefully by the farmer. = 农民小心翼翼地播种。
The trees are being planted along the roadside. = 树木正在路边种植。

疑问句:
When are you planting the potatoes? = 你什么时候种土豆？
What kind of plants are they planting? = 他们在种什么植物？

否定句:
They didn't plant anything this year. = 今年他们什么也没种。
We are not planting sunflowers this season. = 这个季节我们不种向日葵。
