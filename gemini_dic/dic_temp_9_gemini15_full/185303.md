retrenched:/rɪˈtrentʃt/| v. adj.|紧缩开支；裁员；减少；退却 | To reduce costs or spending, often by laying off employees.  It can also mean to withdraw or retreat. |例句：The company retrenched heavily during the recession.  公司在经济衰退期间大幅裁员。|近义词：cut back, reduce, downsize, economize |反义词：expand, increase, grow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company retrenches every year to improve profitability. = 公司每年都进行成本紧缩以提高盈利能力。
They retrench their spending on luxury items. = 他们减少在奢侈品上的开支。


一般过去时:
The government retrenched its spending on social programs. = 政府削减了社会福利项目的支出。
The army retrenched from its forward positions. = 军队从前沿阵地撤退。


现在完成时:
The company has retrenched significantly since the merger. = 自从合并以来，公司已经大幅度裁员/紧缩开支了。
They have retrenched their operations in several countries. = 他们已经收缩了在几个国家的业务。


过去完成时:
The company had retrenched before the economic crisis hit. = 在经济危机爆发前，公司已经进行了裁员/紧缩开支。
The soldiers had retrenched to a defensive position. = 士兵们已经退守到防御阵地。


将来时:
The company will retrench if profits continue to fall. = 如果利润继续下降，公司将进行裁员/紧缩开支。
The general said they would retrench if the enemy attacked. = 将军说如果敌人攻击，他们将会撤退。


虚拟语气:
If the company hadn't retrenched, it would have gone bankrupt. = 如果公司没有进行裁员/紧缩开支，它就会破产。
If the army hadn't retrenched, they would have been surrounded. = 如果军队没有撤退，他们就会被包围。


被动语态:
Many employees were retrenched during the restructuring. = 在重组期间，许多员工被裁员。
The company's operations were retrenched in response to market changes. = 为了应对市场变化，公司的业务进行了收缩。


疑问句:
Will the company retrench again this year? = 公司今年还会裁员/紧缩开支吗？
Did the army retrench after the battle? = 战后军队是否撤退了？


否定句:
The company did not retrench despite the losses. = 尽管亏损，公司并没有裁员/紧缩开支。
The general refused to retrench even under heavy pressure. = 将军即使在巨大的压力下也拒绝撤退。


PHRASE->
retrenchment policy = 紧缩政策
retrenchment measures = 紧缩措施
retrenchment strategy = 紧缩战略
retrenchment program = 紧缩计划
retrenchment of spending = 开支紧缩


词源:
The word "retrench" comes from the Old French word "retrancher," meaning "to cut off" or "to cut back."  This, in turn, is derived from the Latin word "retrāns," meaning "back again" and "caedere," meaning "to cut."  Thus, the word literally means "to cut back again."
