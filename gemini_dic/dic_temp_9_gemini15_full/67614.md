extravert:/ˈekstrəvɜːrt/ | n. adj. | 外向的人；外向的 | An extravert is a person who is outgoing and sociable; someone who is energized and gains energy from interacting with others.  An extraverted person is characterized by sociability, talkativeness, and assertiveness. | Example:  She's a real extravert and loves to be the center of attention. 她是个十足的外向的人，喜欢成为关注的中心。  He's an extravert; he thrives in social situations. 他是个外向的人；他在社交场合很活跃。| 近义词： extrovert, outgoing, sociable | 反义词： introvert, shy, reserved | 名词，形容词
