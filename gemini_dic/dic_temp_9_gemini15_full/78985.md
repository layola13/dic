girth:/ɡɜːrθ/| n. |周长，腰围，粗度 |The measurement around something, especially the distance around a person's waist or the body of an animal.  |例句：The girth of the tree trunk was impressive. 这棵树的树干周长令人印象深刻。

例句：He measured the horse's girth before saddling it. 他在给马鞍前测量了马的腰围。


近义词：circumference, perimeter, waist, thickness |反义词：narrowness, thinness |词性: 名词
