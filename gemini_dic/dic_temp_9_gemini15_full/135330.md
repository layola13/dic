nonpejorative: /ˌnɒnˈpɛdʒəreɪtɪv/ | adj. |非贬义的，不贬低的 | Not expressing or suggesting disapproval.  This adjective describes words, phrases, or actions that are not intended to be insulting or offensive.  It implies neutrality or even a positive connotation, in contrast to pejorative terms.


例句：

The term "alternative" is used in a nonpejorative sense in this context. = 在此语境下，“另类”一词并非贬义。


He used a nonpejorative description of the situation, avoiding any inflammatory language. = 他对情况的描述并非贬义，避免了任何煽动性语言。


近义词：uncritical, complimentary, favorable, positive, respectful


反义词：pejorative, derogatory, disparaging, insulting, offensive


词性：形容词
