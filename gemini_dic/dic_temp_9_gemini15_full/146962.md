overirrigating:/ˌoʊvərˈɪrɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ | v. |过度灌溉|The act of watering plants excessively, leading to potential harm.  It can cause root rot, nutrient leaching, and hinder plant growth. |例句：Overirrigating your lawn can lead to fungal diseases. 过度灌溉你的草坪会导致真菌病害。|例句：The farmer's overirrigating resulted in a poor harvest. 农民过度灌溉导致收成歉收。|近义词：waterlogging, inundating |反义词： underwatering, dehydrating
