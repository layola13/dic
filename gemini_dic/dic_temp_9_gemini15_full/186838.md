ringette:/rɪŋˈɛt/| n. |冰球式环球赛|A team sport played on ice, similar to ice hockey, but using a ring instead of a puck.  Players use a stick to pass and shoot a rubber ring into the opponent's net.  It's a fast-paced and skillful game often played by women and girls.|The Canadian national ringette team won the gold medal.  加拿大国家环球赛队获得了金牌。|近义词：ice hockey (but with key differences)|反义词：无
