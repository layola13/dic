cavalry:/ˈkævəlri/| n.|骑兵，骑兵部队|Mounted soldiers; a branch of the army that fights on horseback.  The term can also refer to armored units using tanks and other vehicles.|The cavalry charged across the field. 骑兵冲过田野。|The enemy cavalry was routed.敌人的骑兵被打败了。|infantry, horsemen|infantry, foot soldiers
