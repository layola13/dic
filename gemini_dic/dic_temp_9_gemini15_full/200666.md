sledgehammering: /ˈslɛdʒˌhæmərɪŋ/ | v. |  猛击；粗暴地处理；强力打击 |  The act of hitting something repeatedly with a sledgehammer, often implying forceful and destructive action. It can also refer to a forceful and clumsy approach to a problem or task. |


例句：

He was sledgehammering the rock with all his might. = 他用尽全力猛砸那块石头。

They sledgehammered the problem, ignoring the more nuanced solutions. = 他们粗暴地处理这个问题，忽略了更细致的解决方案。


近义词：battering, smashing, pulverizing, bludgeoning,  thrashing


反义词：  delicately handling, gently approaching, subtly resolving


词性：动词
