redoubling:/riːˈdaʊblɪŋ/| n. |加倍；重复；再加一层|The act of doubling or repeating something;  intensifying or increasing something.|例句：The company is redoubling its efforts to improve customer service. 公司正在加倍努力以改善客户服务。|例句：The redoubling of the troops resulted in a decisive victory.  军队兵力的加倍最终导致了决定性的胜利。|近义词：intensification, augmentation, repetition|反义词：halving, reduction, diminishing|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is redoubling its marketing efforts. = 公司正在加倍其营销努力。
He is redoubling his training regime. = 他正在加倍他的训练计划。

一般过去时:
They redoubled their efforts after the setback. = 他们在遭遇挫折后加倍努力。
She redoubled her attempts to contact him. = 她加倍努力尝试联系他。

现在完成时:
We have redoubled our commitment to environmental protection. = 我们已经加倍致力于环境保护。
They have redoubled their security measures. = 他们已经加倍了安全措施。

过去完成时:
He had redoubled his efforts before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前加倍努力。
She had redoubled her study habits before the exam. = 她在考试前加倍努力学习。

将来时:
We will redouble our efforts to find a solution. = 我们将加倍努力寻找解决方案。
They will redouble their vigilance in the coming weeks. = 他们将在未来几周加倍警惕。

虚拟语气:
If we redoubled our efforts, we might succeed. = 如果我们加倍努力，我们可能会成功。
I wish I had redoubled my efforts earlier. = 我真希望我早点加倍努力。

被动语态:
Efforts are being redoubled to contain the spread of the virus. = 正在加倍努力以遏制病毒的传播。
Security measures were redoubled after the incident. = 事件发生后，安全措施得到了加强。

疑问句:
Are they redoubling their efforts to win the contract? = 他们是否正在加倍努力以赢得合同？
Will the government redouble its investment in education? = 政府是否会增加对教育的投资？

否定句:
They did not redouble their efforts, and consequently failed. = 他们没有加倍努力，结果失败了。
The company has not redoubled its commitment to sustainability. = 公司没有加倍致力于可持续发展。
