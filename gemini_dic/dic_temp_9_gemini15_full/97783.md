The word implies a negative outcome or potential for negative consequences resulting from poor judgment.|His injudicious remarks offended many people. 他不谨慎的言论得罪了许多人。|近义词：unwise, imprudent, ill-advised, foolish, reckless |反义词：wise, prudent, judicious, sensible
