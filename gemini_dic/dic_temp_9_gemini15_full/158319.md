playlets:/ˈpleɪlɛts/| n. |短剧；小戏剧 |A playlet is a short play, typically one that is less complex or serious than a full-length play.  It often focuses on a single incident or theme and may be comedic or dramatic. |例句：The students put on a series of charming playlets for the school festival. 学生们为学校节日上演了一系列迷人的短剧。

The children enjoyed the playlets based on their favourite fairy tales. 孩子们喜欢根据他们最喜欢的童话故事改编的短剧。|近义词：short play, skit, one-act play |反义词：full-length play, epic drama
