posteriori:/ˌpɒstɪəriˈɔːraɪ/| adj.| 后验的；事后的 |  Relating to or denoting reasoning or knowledge that proceeds from observations or experiences to the deduction of probable causes.  It contrasts with a priori knowledge, which is independent of experience.  | Example:  The posteriori argument is based on the observed data. 后验论证是基于观察到的数据。  | 近义词：empirical, a posteriori | 反义词：a priori
