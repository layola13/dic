rehydrate:/ˌriːˈhaɪdreɪt/| vt.|使再水合；使重新水化；补充水分|To restore water to (something), especially to restore the water content of a dehydrated substance or organism.|例句：The doctor recommended that I rehydrate with an electrolyte solution after my run.  医生建议我在跑步后用电解质溶液来补充水分。

例句：I need to rehydrate my dried mushrooms before adding them to the soup. 我需要在把干蘑菇加入汤中之前先把它重新水化。


近义词：replenish, rewet, moisten|反义词：dehydrate, dry out|动词
