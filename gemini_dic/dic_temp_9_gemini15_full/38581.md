concordance:/kənˈkɔːrdəns/| n. |索引；和谐；一致|A concordance is an alphabetical index of the principal words in a book or text, showing every instance of their occurrence and its context.  It's also used to describe agreement or harmony. |例句：The concordance to Shakespeare's works is a valuable research tool.  莎士比亚作品的索引是一个宝贵的科研工具。

例句：There was a remarkable concordance of views among the panel members.  小组成员之间观点出奇地一致。|近义词：index, harmony, agreement, accord |反义词：discord, disagreement, conflict |词性:n.
