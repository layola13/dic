reingratiate:/ˌriːɪnˈɡreɪʃieɪt/ | vt. |重新获得好感；讨好；重新取得…的欢心 | To regain the favor or goodwill of someone.  |例句：He tried to reingratiate himself with his boss after his mistake. 他试图在他犯错后重新获得老板的好感。 |例句：She sent a gift to reingratiate herself with her estranged friend. 她送了一份礼物来讨好她疏远的的朋友。|近义词：reconcile, conciliate, ingratiate |反义词：alienate, estrange
