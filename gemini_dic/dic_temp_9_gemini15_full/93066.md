idolist:/ˈaɪdəlɪst/| n. |偶像崇拜者；理想主义者|A person who excessively admires or worships an idol or idols; someone who is guided by ideals rather than practical considerations. |例句：He's an idolist, completely devoted to the pop star. 他是个偶像崇拜者，完全迷恋于那位流行歌星。|近义词：fanatic, idealist, devotee |反义词：realist, pragmatist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He idolizes that famous actor. = 他崇拜那位著名的演员。
She idolizes her older sister. = 她崇拜她姐姐。


一般过去时:
They idolized the rock band in their youth. = 他们年轻时崇拜那个摇滚乐队。
He idolized his grandfather. = 他崇拜他的祖父。


现在完成时:
I have idolized this musician for years. = 我多年来一直崇拜这位音乐家。
She has idolized that athlete since she was a child. = 从小她就崇拜那位运动员。


过去完成时:
He had idolized that painter before he even met him. = 他甚至在遇到那位画家之前就崇拜他了。
They had idolized the singer for many years before her sudden death. = 在她突然去世之前，他们已经崇拜这位歌手很多年了。


将来时:
I will continue to idolize this author. = 我将继续崇拜这位作家。
She will likely idolize her favorite artist for many years to come. = 她很可能会在未来很多年继续崇拜她最喜欢的艺术家。


虚拟语气:
If I were to idolize anyone, it would be him. = 如果我要崇拜任何人，那将是他。
I wish I hadn't idolized that person so blindly. = 我真希望我没有那么盲目地崇拜那个人。


被动语态:
That singer is idolized by millions. = 那位歌手被数百万人崇拜。
The athlete was idolized for his incredible talent. = 那位运动员因其令人难以置信的天赋而受到崇拜。


疑问句:
Who do you idolize? = 你崇拜谁？
Does he idolize anyone in particular? = 他特别崇拜谁吗？


否定句:
I don't idolize anyone. = 我不崇拜任何人。
She didn't idolize celebrities. = 她不崇拜名人。


PHRASE->
blind idolization = 盲目崇拜
celebrity idolization = 名人崇拜
teenage idolization = 青少年崇拜
excessive idolization = 过度崇拜
healthy idolization = 健康的崇拜
