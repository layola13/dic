housecoats: /ˈhaʊskoʊts/ | n. |家居服，睡衣 | A loose, comfortable garment worn at home, typically by women.  It's often made of soft material like cotton or flannel.  |  She slipped into her favorite housecoat after a long day. 她长长的一天后换上了她最喜欢的家居服。  My grandmother always wore a floral housecoat around the house. 我祖母总是在家里穿着印花家居服。 | dressing gown, robe |  (反义词较难定义，因为与其相对的是正式的服装，而非一个具体的词)  | 名词
