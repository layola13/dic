caws:/kɔːz/| n. v.|乌鸦叫声；大声叫喊|The sound a crow makes; to utter a harsh cry.|例句：The crows cawed loudly in the morning.  清晨乌鸦大声啼叫。|例句：He cawed his displeasure at the news. 他大声叫喊表示对这个消息的不满。|近义词：croak, squawk|反义词：whisper, murmur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The crows caw every morning. = 乌鸦每天早上都在叫。
They caw loudly to attract attention. = 他们大声叫喊以吸引注意。

一般过去时:
The crow cawed loudly last night. = 乌鸦昨晚大声叫唤。
He cawed in protest. = 他大声抗议。

现在完成时:
The crows have cawed all day. = 乌鸦叫了一整天了。
They have cawed their disapproval. = 他们已经大声表示反对。

过去完成时:
The crows had cawed before the sun rose. = 太阳升起之前乌鸦就已经叫了。
He had cawed several times before anyone noticed. = 在任何人注意到之前，他已经叫喊了好几次了。

将来时:
The crows will caw at dawn tomorrow. = 明天黎明乌鸦会叫。
He will caw if he doesn't get his way. = 如果他不遂心愿，他会大声叫喊。

虚拟语气:
If the crows cawed louder, we would have heard them. = 如果乌鸦叫得更响亮，我们就会听到它们。
I wish the crows wouldn't caw so early. = 我希望乌鸦不要那么早叫。

被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used passively)  这个动词很少用被动语态。
The sound was described as a caw. = 这种声音被描述为乌鸦叫声。

疑问句:
Do the crows caw at night? = 乌鸦晚上叫吗？
Did he caw when he saw the accident? = 他看到事故时叫喊了吗？

否定句:
The crows didn't caw this morning. = 今天早上乌鸦没叫。
He didn't caw a single word. = 他一句话也没喊。
