diplomatically:/ˌdɪpləˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 外交地；策略地；圆滑地 | In a way that avoids causing offense or disagreement; tactfully.  | 例句：He handled the difficult situation diplomatically. 他外交地处理了这个棘手的情况。

例句：She diplomatically steered the conversation away from the controversial topic. 她巧妙地将谈话从有争议的话题上转移开了。


近义词：tactfully, skillfully, subtly, cleverly, delicately

反义词：bluntly, frankly, abruptly, rudely


词源:  Derived from "diplomacy," which comes from the French word "diplomatie," ultimately from the Greek "diploma" (a document conferring privileges or authority). The "-ically" suffix denotes "in the manner of."  The evolution reflects the historical association of diplomacy with official documents and the art of negotiating skillfully.
