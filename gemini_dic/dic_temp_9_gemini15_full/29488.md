cataplexy:/ˈkætəplɛksi/| n. |猝倒症，肌张力丧失症 |A sudden, brief loss of voluntary muscle tone, often triggered by strong emotions such as laughter, anger, or surprise.  It is a symptom of narcolepsy. | 例句：He experienced a sudden cataplexy while watching a funny movie. 他看一部搞笑电影时突然发生猝倒。  She suffers from cataplexy, a hallmark symptom of her narcolepsy. 她患有猝倒症，这是她患嗜睡症的典型症状。| 近义词：muscle weakness, paralysis (partial)| 反义词：muscle strength, muscle tension| 名词
