/mɑː/ | pron. | 那；什么；什么东西 |  "Ma" is a pronoun used informally, chiefly in the speech of children, as a shortened form of "mamma" or "mama." It refers to one's mother.  |  "Ma, can I have some candy?"  妈妈，我能吃点糖吗？ | mamma, mom, mother |  father, dad | pronoun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？


一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say to you? = 她对你说了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you been doing? = 你一直在做什么？
What has he accomplished? = 他取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you eaten before you felt sick? = 你在感到不舒服之前吃了什么？
What had she done before she arrived? = 她到达之前做了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will we have for dinner tonight? = 我们今晚吃什么？


虚拟语气:
If I knew what to do, I would do it. = 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。
I wish I knew what to expect. = 我真希望知道会发生什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built on that lot? = 那块地正在建什么？


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
She didn't say what she wanted. = 她没说她想要什么。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What's going on? = 发生了什么事？
What's the matter? = 怎么了？
What about...? = ……怎么样？
What if...? = 如果……将会怎样？
