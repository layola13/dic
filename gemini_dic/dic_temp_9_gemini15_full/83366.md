gyrolepis:/ˌdʒaɪ.roʊˈlɛpɪs/| n. |旋鳞鱼属|A genus of extinct, primarily freshwater bony fish from the Late Triassic period, characterized by their unique, spirally arranged scales.  They are considered to be closely related to modern-day bowfin (Amia calva).|例句：The fossil discovery of Gyrolepis provided valuable insights into the evolution of early bony fish. 旋鳞鱼属的化石发现为了解早期硬骨鱼的进化提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词：暂无精确近义词，因其为一属名|反义词：暂无|名词
