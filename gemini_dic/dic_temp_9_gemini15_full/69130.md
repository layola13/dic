favoring:/ˈfeɪvərɪŋ/ | adj. |偏袒的，有利于的，偏爱的 | showing preference or partiality; giving advantage to.  |例句：The judge's decision was seen as favoring the wealthy defendant.  法官的判决被视为偏袒富有的被告。

The new policy is favoring small businesses. 新政策有利于小型企业。|近义词：supporting, preferring, benefiting, promoting |反义词：opposing, hindering, disadvantaging


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is favoring large corporations. = 政府偏袒大公司。
He is favoring his son over his daughter. = 他偏袒儿子胜过女儿。


一般过去时:
The judge favored the prosecution in his ruling. = 法官在判决中偏袒了检方。
The weather favored us on our trip. = 天气在我们旅行中对我们有利。


现在完成时:
Recent changes have favored the development of renewable energy. = 最近的变化有利于可再生能源的发展。
The evidence has favored the defendant's claims. = 证据有利于被告的陈述。


过去完成时:
The previous administration had favored deregulation. = 上届政府曾偏袒放松管制。
The historical context had favored the rise of the empire. = 历史背景有利于帝国的崛起。


将来时:
Future policies will likely favor environmental protection. = 未来的政策可能会偏向环境保护。
The forecast favors sunny weather for the weekend. = 预报周末天气晴朗。


虚拟语气:
If the court favored the plaintiff, the case would be over. = 如果法院偏袒原告，案件就会结束。
I wish the committee favored my proposal. = 我希望委员会支持我的提议。


被动语态:
Small businesses are favored by the new tax cuts. = 小型企业受益于新的减税政策。
The decision was clearly favored by the influential lobbyist. = 这个决定显然受到了有影响力的游说者的偏袒。


疑问句:
Does the evidence favor the defense? = 证据是否对被告有利？
Does the current economic situation favor investment? = 当前的经济形势是否利于投资？


否定句:
The referee did not favor either team. = 裁判没有偏袒任何一方。
The outcome did not favor our team. = 结果对我们队不利。


PHRASE->
in favor of: 支持，赞成
show favor to: 偏袒，厚待
find favor with: 受…的喜爱，得到…的欢心
out of favor: 失宠，不受欢迎
with favor:  赞许地，亲切地
