glad-hands:/ɡlædˈhændz/| v.|热情地与某人握手，逢迎；巴结|To greet someone enthusiastically and effusively, often insincerely, usually by shaking hands repeatedly.|例句：The politician glad-handed his way through the crowd, shaking everyone's hand.  这位政客热情地穿过人群，和每个人握手。

例句：He glad-handed the potential investors, hoping to secure funding for his project. 他热情地接待潜在投资者，希望能为他的项目获得资金。


近义词：fawn over, ingratiate oneself with,  butter up| 反义词：ignore, snub, slight| 词性:动词
