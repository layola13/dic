panegyric:/pænəˈdʒaɪrɪk/| n.|赞辞，颂词 | A formal speech or piece of writing praising someone or something.  | Example:  The panegyric delivered at the funeral was both moving and insightful.  |近义词：eulogy, encomium, tribute |反义词：diatribe, invective, condemnation |名词

He delivers a panegyric every year. = 他每年都发表一篇颂词。
The newspaper publishes many panegyrics. = 这家报纸发表了许多颂词。
