skates:/skeɪts/| n. v.|溜冰鞋；冰鞋；旱冰鞋；(用溜冰鞋)滑冰|  A skate is a type of footwear with blades attached to the bottom, used for gliding on ice or other smooth surfaces.  The verb form refers to the act of gliding on skates.|例句：She bought a new pair of ice skates. 她买了一双新的冰鞋。

例句：He skates every Saturday at the ice rink. 他每个星期六都在溜冰场滑冰。
