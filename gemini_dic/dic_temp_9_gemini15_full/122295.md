mitres:/ˈmaɪtərz/| n.|主教冠, 尖顶帽|A mitre is a tall, pointed hat worn by bishops and other high-ranking clergy in some Christian churches.  It is a symbol of their authority and office.|The bishop wore his mitre during the solemn ceremony.  主教在庄严的仪式上戴着主教冠。|bishop's hat, tiara|  (无明显反义词)|名词
