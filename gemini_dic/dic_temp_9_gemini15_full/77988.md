gentility:/dʒɛnˈtɪləti/ | n. | 高雅；优雅；上流社会；有教养 |  The quality or state of being genteel; refinement of manner or style; elegance.  It can also refer to people of high social standing or birth. |例句：Her movements were marked by a quiet gentility.  她的举止优雅而娴静。


例句：The old house exuded an air of faded gentility. 这栋老房子散发着褪色的优雅气息。


近义词：refinement, elegance, politeness, grace, aristocracy


反义词：vulgarity, crudeness, coarseness, boorishness


词源：源自中古英语 *gentilitee，源自古法语 *gentilesse，最终源自拉丁语 *gentilis，“属於氏族或家族的”，与拉丁语 *gens，“氏族，家族”相关。
