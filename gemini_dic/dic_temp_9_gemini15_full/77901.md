genie:/ˈdʒiːniː/| n. |精灵，妖怪 |A genie is a supernatural being, often depicted in folklore as having magical powers and being able to grant wishes.  Genies are frequently confined within objects, such as lamps or bottles, until released by someone. |Example:  The genie in the lamp granted Aladdin three wishes.  这个灯里的精灵给了阿拉丁三个愿望。|近义词：spirit, demon, jinn |反义词：human, mortal
