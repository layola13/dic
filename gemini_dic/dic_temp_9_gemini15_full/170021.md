protector: /prəˈtɛktər/ | n. | 保护者，保护物 | A person or thing that protects someone or something.  |例句：The mother bear acted as a protector for her cubs. 母熊充当她幼崽的保护者。

例句：He wore a face protector during the sanding process.他在打磨过程中戴着面罩。


近义词：guardian, shield, defender, safeguard | 反义词：attacker, destroyer, harm


词源:  The word "protector" comes from the Old French word "protectore," which itself derives from the Latin word "protegere," meaning "to cover," "to protect," or "to defend."  The suffix "-or" indicates the agent or doer of the action.
