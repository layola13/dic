gyrus:/ˈdʒaɪrəs/| n.|脑回，回旋|A gyrus (plural gyri) is a ridge or fold in the cerebral cortex of the brain.  These folds increase the surface area of the brain, allowing for more complex cognitive functions.|例句：The intricate gyri and sulci of the brain are visible in a detailed MRI scan.  脑部复杂的脑回和脑沟在详细的核磁共振扫描中清晰可见。|近义词：convolution, fold, ridge|反义词：sulcus
