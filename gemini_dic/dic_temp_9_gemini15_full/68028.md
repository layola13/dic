factoring-in: /ˈfæktərɪŋ ɪn/ | v. | 考虑，把…计算在内 | To include something as a factor in your calculations or considerations.  |例句：We need to factor in the cost of transportation when calculating the total expenses. 我们在计算总支出时需要考虑运输成本。

例句：We need to factor in potential delays when planning the project schedule. 我们在计划项目进度时需要考虑到潜在的延误。


近义词：considering, taking into account, allowing for, incorporating


反义词：ignoring, disregarding, neglecting


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
We factor in the weather when planning outdoor events. = 我们在规划户外活动时会考虑天气。
They factor in customer feedback when developing new products. = 他们在开发新产品时会考虑客户反馈。


一般过去时:
We factored in the inflation rate when setting the prices. = 我们在设定价格时考虑了通货膨胀率。
She factored in her travel time when scheduling her appointments. = 她安排约会时考虑了她的出行时间。


现在完成时:
We have factored in all the relevant costs. = 我们已经考虑了所有相关的成本。
They have factored in the risks involved. = 他们已经考虑了所涉及的风险。


过去完成时:
We had factored in the possibility of rain, so we had a backup plan. = 我们已经考虑到了下雨的可能性，所以我们有备用计划。
They had factored in the previous year's sales figures. = 他们已经考虑了去年的销售数据。


将来时:
We will factor in the new tax laws when preparing our next budget. = 我们将在编制下一个预算时考虑新的税法。
They will factor in the market trends when making their investment decisions. = 他们在做出投资决策时将考虑市场趋势。


虚拟语气:
If we factored in all the variables, we might get a more accurate result. = 如果我们考虑所有变量，我们可能会得到更准确的结果。
I wish we had factored in the potential for traffic congestion. = 我希望我们当时考虑到了交通拥堵的可能性。


被动语态:
The unforeseen expenses were not factored in. = 未预料到的支出没有被考虑在内。
All possible scenarios need to be factored in. = 所有可能的情况都需要被考虑在内。


疑问句:
Have you factored in the cost of materials? = 你考虑了材料成本吗？
Did they factor in the time needed for testing? = 他们考虑了测试所需的时间吗？


否定句:
They didn't factor in the possibility of a strike. = 他们没有考虑到罢工的可能性。
We didn't factor in the extra travel time. = 我们没有考虑到额外的出行时间。
