nondominant:/ˌnɑːnˈdɑːmɪnənt/| adj. |非优势的，非主导的 | Not dominant; not the most powerful or influential.  This term is often used to describe the hand, eye, or foot that is not the dominant one. |Example: He's left-handed, so his right hand is his nondominant hand. 他是左撇子，所以他的右手是非优势手。


His nondominant eye wasn't as sharp as his dominant eye.他的非优势眼不如优势眼锐利。|近义词：subordinate, secondary, less important|反义词：dominant, major, primary
