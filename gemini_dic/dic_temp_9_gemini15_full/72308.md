flourished:/ˈflʌrɪʃt/ | v. |繁荣；兴旺；挥舞；炫耀 | to grow or develop successfully; to wave something around; to show off |例句：The business flourished under his leadership.  这家企业在他的领导下蓬勃发展。 |例句：He flourished his sword and charged at the enemy. 他挥舞着剑冲向敌人。 |近义词：thrive, prosper, succeed, wave |反义词：decline, fail, wither


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The economy flourishes in this region. = 这个地区的经济很繁荣。
The artist flourishes his brush. = 艺术家挥舞着画笔。


一般过去时:
The company flourished during the 1990s. = 这家公司在20世纪90年代蓬勃发展。
She flourished the letter triumphantly. = 她得意地挥舞着信。


现在完成时:
The town has flourished since the new road was built. = 自从修建了新路以来，这个城镇已经繁荣起来。
He has flourished his expertise in many fields. = 他在许多领域都炫耀过他的专业知识。


过去完成时:
The empire had flourished for centuries before its decline. = 这个帝国在衰落之前已经繁荣了几个世纪。
Before the presentation, she had flourished the notes. = 演示之前，她已经挥舞过笔记。


将来时:
The company will flourish if it adapts to the changing market. = 如果公司适应不断变化的市场，它将会蓬勃发展。
He will flourish his new skills at the job fair. = 他将在招聘会上展示他的新技能。


虚拟语气:
If they had invested wisely, their business would have flourished. = 如果他们投资明智的话，他们的生意就会兴旺发达。
If I were you, I would flourish my talents more. = 如果我是你，我会更多地展现我的才能。


被动语态:
The city was flourished by the arrival of new industries. = 这个城市因新兴产业的到来而繁荣起来。(虽然flourish本身较少用被动语态)
His achievements were flourished by the media. = 他的成就被媒体大肆宣扬。


疑问句:
Did the plant flourish in the new soil? = 植物在新土壤中生长茂盛吗？
Will your business flourish in the future? = 你的企业将来会兴旺发达吗？


否定句:
The project didn't flourish due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，该项目没有成功。
The flowers didn't flourish in the shade. = 花在阴凉处没有茂盛生长。


PHRASE->
flourish and thrive: 蓬勃发展
flourish dramatically: 显著发展
flourish under: 在...的领导下兴旺
flourish in: 在...中兴旺
a flourishing business: 一个兴旺的企业
