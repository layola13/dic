reinfluenced:/ˌriːˈɪnflʊənst/| v.|再影响，重新影响 |To influence again or anew; to exert an influence on something or someone a second time.|The new evidence reinfluenced the jury's decision. 新证据再次影响了陪审团的决定。|近义词：reimpact,reinforce|反义词：counteract,negate
