This results in a material that is harder and more durable than the original, but still retains some porosity.  It's commonly used in ceramics and glass manufacturing.|The semivitrification of the clay increased its strength and water resistance. 半瓷化处理提高了粘土的强度和防水性。|vitrification, sintering|complete vitrification, complete melting|名词
