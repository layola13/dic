popularise:/ˈpɒpjʊləraɪz/ /ˈpɑːpjʊləraɪz/| vt.|普及；推广；使流行|To make (something) popular or widely known.|The government launched a campaign to popularise the new technology. 政府发起了一场推广这项新技术的运动。|近义词：promote, disseminate, spread|反义词：suppress, obscure|动词
