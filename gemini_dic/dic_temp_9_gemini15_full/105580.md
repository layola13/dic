kindle:/ˈkɪndl/| vt. n.|点燃；激发；煽动；点着；火种|to start a fire; to arouse or excite a feeling or emotion; a small fire or flame;  a device for reading e-books|例句：He kindled a fire in the fireplace. 他在壁炉里生了火。|近义词：ignite, ignite, spark, inspire, arouse|反义词：extinguish, quench, suppress
