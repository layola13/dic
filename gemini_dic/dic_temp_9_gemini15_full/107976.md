lashed:/læʃt/| v.|鞭打；猛击；捆绑；猛烈抨击；(风、雨等)猛烈冲击| to strike or beat with a whip or similar object; to fasten or secure something tightly; to criticize severely; to be hit by something with great force (e.g., wind, rain)|例句：The angry farmer lashed out at the trespassers.  愤怒的农民痛斥那些闯入者。

例句：The wind lashed the rain against the windows. 风雨猛烈地拍打着窗户。


近义词：whipped, struck, beat, bound, criticized, slammed

反义词：soothed, calmed, gently treated
