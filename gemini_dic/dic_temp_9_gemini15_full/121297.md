misfiring:/ˈmɪsˌfaɪrɪŋ/| n. |失火，故障，失效|The failure of something to function correctly, especially an engine or firearm.  It often implies a partial or intermittent failure rather than a complete breakdown. |例句：The engine was experiencing misfiring, causing it to lose power. 引擎出现失火现象，导致动力下降。

The car's misfiring was traced to a faulty spark plug. 汽车的失火故障被追溯到一个有故障的火花塞。|近义词：malfunction, failure, breakdown|反义词：functioning properly, working correctly|名词
