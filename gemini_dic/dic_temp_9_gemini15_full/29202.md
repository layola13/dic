cashmir: /ˈkæʃmɪr/| n. |开司米；羊绒；卡什米尔羊毛|  A soft, luxurious type of wool made from the undercoat of the Cashmere goat. It is known for its warmth, softness, and high price. |例句：That sweater is made of pure cashmere. 那件毛衣是纯羊绒的。

例句：The scarf is incredibly soft, a testament to its cashmere composition. 这条围巾非常柔软，证明了它的羊绒成分。


近义词：pashmina, merino wool| 反义词：cotton, linen| 词性：名词

词源:
The word "cashmere" ultimately derives from the region of Kashmir in the Himalayas, where the Cashmere goat, the source of the fiber, originates.  The word's journey through languages reflects this origin, with variations in spelling and pronunciation arising from different linguistic influences.
