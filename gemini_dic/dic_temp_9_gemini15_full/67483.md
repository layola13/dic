extra-curricular: /ˌɛkstrə kəˈrɪkjʊlər/ | adj. |课外的，业余的 |  Beyond the regular curriculum or course of study.  Activities that are not part of the regular school or college program. |


例句：He participates in many extra-curricular activities, such as playing football and joining the debate club. 他参与许多课外活动，例如踢足球和参加辩论社。

例句：Extra-curricular activities can help students develop valuable life skills. 课外活动可以帮助学生培养宝贵的生存技能。


近义词：non-academic, supplemental, outside


反义词：curricular, academic, required


词性：形容词
