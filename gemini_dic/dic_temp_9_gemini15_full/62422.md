endomorphs:/ˈɛndoʊmɔːrfz/| n. |内胚体型的人；内胚型；内胚叶| A person with a body type characterized by a soft, round physique, often associated with a tendency to gain weight easily.  Endomorphs are typically described as having a larger bone structure, more body fat, and less muscle mass compared to other somatotypes.|Example:  He was identified as an endomorph due to his stocky build and propensity for weight gain. 他因其矮胖的体格和容易增重的倾向而被认定为内胚型。|近义词：mesomorph (with qualifications), ectomorph (in contrast)|反义词：ectomorph
