configurational:/ˌkɒnfɪɡjʊˈreɪʃənəl/| adj.|构型的；构造的；组态的 | Relating to or denoting configuration.  The term often refers to the three-dimensional arrangement of atoms in a molecule, especially in relation to stereoisomerism.  It can also refer to the arrangement of components in a system or device. |例句：The configurational isomers of a molecule differ in the spatial arrangement of their atoms.  该分子的构型异构体在其原子的空间排列上有所不同。
