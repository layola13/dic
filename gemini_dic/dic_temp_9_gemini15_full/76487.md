galago:/ɡəˈlɑːɡoʊ/| n. |丛猴，瘦猴|A galago is a small primate belonging to the family Galagidae, known for its large eyes, long hind legs, and nocturnal habits.  They are also called bushbabies.|Example:  The researchers observed the galago's remarkable leaping ability.  研究人员观察了丛猴非凡的跳跃能力。|近义词：bushbaby|反义词：无
