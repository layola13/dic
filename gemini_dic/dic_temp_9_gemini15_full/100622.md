intimidator: /ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪtər/ | n. | 恐吓者，威胁者 | A person who intimidates others; someone who uses threats or violence to make others fearful or submissive.  | Example: The school bully was a known intimidator, making life miserable for many students.  学校里的恶霸是个出了名的恐吓者，让很多学生的生活苦不堪言。  |近义词: bully, threatener, menace, terrorizer | 反义词: protector, comforter, supporter | 名词
