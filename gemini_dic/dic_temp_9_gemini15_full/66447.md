exemplified:/ɪɡˈzɛmplɪfaɪd/ | v. (过去式和过去分词) | 举例说明；作为…的例子 | To serve as a typical example of; illustrate.  | His work exemplified the best qualities of the era. 他的作品是那个时代最佳品质的典范。 | illustrated, demonstrated, shown | contradicted, refuted


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He exemplifies patience. = 他是耐心的典范。
The painting exemplifies the artist's skill. = 这幅画是艺术家技艺的典范。


一般过去时:
Her actions exemplified her kindness. = 她的行为体现了她的善良。
The project exemplified their dedication. = 这个项目体现了他们的奉献精神。


现在完成时:
His career has exemplified success. = 他的事业是成功的典范。
Their efforts have exemplified teamwork. = 他们的努力体现了团队合作精神。


过去完成时:
His previous work had exemplified his talent. = 他以前的作品已经充分体现了他的才能。
The historical events had exemplified the dangers of war. = 历史事件已经充分说明了战争的危险性。


将来时:
The upcoming exhibition will exemplify modern art. = 即将来临的展览将是现代艺术的典范。
Her future actions will exemplify her commitment. = 她未来的行动将体现她的承诺。


虚拟语气:
If he were to exemplify patience, he would be a better leader. = 如果他能成为耐心的典范，他会成为更好的领导者。
I wish my work exemplified more creativity. = 我希望我的作品能体现出更多的创造力。


被动语态:
Patience is exemplified by his actions. = 耐心在他的行为中得到了体现。
Teamwork is exemplified by their collaborative efforts. = 团队合作精神体现在他们合作的努力中。


疑问句:
Does this case exemplify the problem? = 这个案例能说明这个问题吗？
What exemplifies the company's values? = 什么能体现公司的价值观？


否定句:
His behavior did not exemplify professionalism. = 他的行为没有体现出专业精神。
The experiment did not exemplify the theory. = 实验没有证明这个理论。



PHRASE->
to exemplify something: 举例说明某事
to be exemplified by: 以…为例
a perfect example of: 一个完美的例子
to serve as an example of: 作为…的例子
to stand as an example of:  作为…的例子
