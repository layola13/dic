matchlock:/ˈmætʃlɒk/| n. |火绳枪锁, 火门枪机 | A mechanism in a firearm that uses a burning match to ignite the gunpowder.  It is an early type of firearm lock. | Example: The soldier carefully adjusted the matchlock of his musket before firing.  士兵在开火前仔细调整了他的火绳枪的火门枪机。|近义词：firelock (archaic)|反义词：None specifically.  (Modern firing mechanisms are functionally different)|词性:名词
