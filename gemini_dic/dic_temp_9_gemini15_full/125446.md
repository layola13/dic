multiplexed:/ˈmʌltɪplekst/ | adj. |多路复用的；多工的 | Combining or using multiple signals or data streams over a single communication channel.  The term implies that several signals share the same physical medium, often through a process of time division or frequency division. | Example:  The fiber optic cable uses multiplexed signals to transmit vast amounts of data simultaneously.  光纤电缆利用多路复用信号同时传输大量数据。

The company's network is multiplexed to handle high traffic volume. 该公司的网络采用了多路复用技术来处理高流量。


近义词：multi-channel, combined, integrated | 反义词：single-channel, uncombined, separate | 词性：adjective

词源:
From "multiplex,"  combining "multi-" (many) and "plex" (fold, weave, plait).  The suffix "-ed" indicates the past participle or adjectival form.  The root ultimately derives from Latin "plectere" (to braid, to weave), reflecting the intertwining nature of multiplexed signals.
