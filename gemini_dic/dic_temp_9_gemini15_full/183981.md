reshingle:/ˈriːʃɪŋɡəl/| vt.|重新盖屋顶(用瓦片)|To cover a roof again with shingles.  This involves removing old shingles and installing new ones.|例句：We need to reshingle the roof before winter arrives. 我们需要在冬天到来之前重新铺盖屋顶。
例句：The old shingles were damaged, so we had to reshingle the entire roof. 旧瓦片损坏，所以我们不得不重新铺盖整个屋顶。|近义词：re-roof, reroof|反义词：remove shingles, leave unshingled|动词
