reprosecute:/ˌriːˈprɒsɪkjuːt/| vt.|再次起诉，重新起诉|To prosecute (someone) again for the same offense.|The case was dismissed, but the prosecutor decided to reprosecute.|近义词：reindict, retry|反义词：acquit, dismiss

一般现在时:
The prosecutor reprosecutes the defendant for the same crime. = 检察官再次起诉被告犯有同一罪行。
They reprosecute cases that were previously dismissed. = 他们重新起诉之前被驳回的案件。


一般过去时:
The court reprosecuted the suspect after new evidence emerged. = 在出现新的证据后，法院再次起诉了嫌疑人。
They reprosecuted him for perjury. = 他们因伪证罪再次起诉了他。


现在完成时:
The authorities have reprosecuted him on several occasions. = 当局已经多次对他提起公诉。

过去完成时:

It had been reprosecuted twice before the final verdict. = 在最终判决之前，它已经被重新起诉了两次。
