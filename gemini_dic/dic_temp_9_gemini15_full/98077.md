inquires:/ɪnˈkwaɪrz/| vt.|询问；打听；查问|To seek information about.  It often implies a more formal or polite way of asking than simply asking a question.|例句：He inquired about the price of the tickets. 他询问了票价。|例句：She inquired politely if there were any vacancies.|她礼貌地询问是否有空缺。|近义词：ask, question, investigate|反义词：answer, respond


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He inquires about the weather every morning. = 他每天早上都询问天气情况。
She inquires about the availability of the product. = 她询问产品的供应情况。


一般过去时:
They inquired about the lost package yesterday. = 他们昨天询问了丢失的包裹。
I inquired at the information desk for directions. = 我在问讯处询问了方向。


现在完成时:
He has inquired about the job several times. = 他已经多次询问过这份工作。
She has inquired after your health. = 她问候过你的健康。


过去完成时:
I had inquired about the flight before booking my ticket. = 我在预订机票之前已经询问过航班信息。
They had inquired at the hotel before arriving. = 他们到达之前已经向酒店询问过。


将来时:
She will inquire about the possibility of a refund. = 她将询问退款的可能性。
He will inquire into the matter thoroughly. = 他将彻底调查此事。


虚拟语气:
If he inquired politely, they would help him. = 如果他礼貌地询问，他们会帮助他。
I wish I had inquired about the deadline earlier. = 我真希望我早点询问截止日期。


被动语态:
The matter is being inquired into by the police. = 警方正在调查此事。
Information was inquired about by several customers. = 几位顾客询问了信息。


疑问句:
Did you inquire about the job opening? = 你询问过职位空缺吗？
Will you inquire about the delivery time? = 你会询问交货时间吗？


否定句:
He did not inquire about the details. = 他没有询问细节。
They didn't inquire whether it was necessary. = 他们没有询问是否有必要。


PHRASE->
inquire after:  询问（某人的健康状况）
inquire into: 调查
inquire of: 向…询问
inquire about: 询问…
make inquiries:  进行询问
