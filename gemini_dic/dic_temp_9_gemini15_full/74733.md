freest:/friːst/| adj.|最自由的，最不受约束的|Most free; having the greatest amount of freedom.  It is the superlative form of the adjective "free".|例句：She felt the freest she had ever felt in her life. 她感到是她一生中最自由的时刻。

例句：The birds are freest in the vast sky.  鸟儿在广阔的天空中最为自由。


近义词：most liberated, most unconstrained, most independent|反义词：most restricted, most constrained, most enslaved|词性：形容词
