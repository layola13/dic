carbazole:/ˈkɑːrbəzɔːl/| n. |咔唑|A heterocyclic organic compound consisting of a benzene ring fused to a pyrrole ring. It is a colorless crystalline solid, used as an intermediate in the synthesis of dyes and other organic compounds.  |Carbazole is used in the production of various dyes and pharmaceuticals.|近义词：None (unique chemical structure)|反义词：None (no direct antonym)|名词
