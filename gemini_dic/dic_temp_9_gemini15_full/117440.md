medita:/ˈmɛdɪteɪt/| v.|沉思，冥想|To think deeply and quietly, especially about spiritual matters.|例句：I often meditate to clear my mind. 我经常冥想以理清思绪。She meditated for an hour each morning. 她每天早上冥想一个小时。|近义词：contemplate, contemplate, reflect, ponder|反义词：act, rush


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I meditate every morning. = 我每天早上冥想。
He meditates to reduce stress. = 他冥想以减轻压力。

一般过去时:
I meditated yesterday for an hour. = 我昨天冥想了一个小时。
She meditated before the exam. = 考试前她进行了冥想。

现在完成时:
I have meditated for years. = 我已经冥想多年了。
She has meditated daily since last year. = 从去年开始她就每天冥想。

过去完成时:
I had meditated before I went to sleep. = 我睡觉前冥想过了。
She had meditated for a week before she felt calmer. = 她冥想了一周后才感到平静。

将来时:
I will meditate tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上会冥想。
He will meditate before the interview. = 他会在面试前冥想。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would meditate. = 如果我有时间，我会冥想。
I wish I could meditate more often. = 我希望我能更经常地冥想。

被动语态:
Meditation is practiced by many people. = 冥想被许多人练习。
The benefits of meditation are widely discussed. = 冥想的益处被广泛讨论。

疑问句:
Do you meditate regularly? = 你经常冥想吗？
Does he meditate before sleep? = 他睡觉前冥想吗？

否定句:
I don't meditate very often. = 我不经常冥想。
He didn't meditate yesterday. = 他昨天没有冥想。
