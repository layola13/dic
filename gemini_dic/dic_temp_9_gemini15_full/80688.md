gorgers:/ˈɡɔːrdʒərz/| n. |贪吃者；暴食者 |A gorger is someone who eats excessively or greedily.  They are characterized by their large appetites and tendency to overindulge in food. |例句：The gorgers at the buffet left little for the latecomers.  宴会上的贪吃者们几乎把东西都吃光了，留给晚来的人很少了。|近义词：glutton, gourmand, overeater |反义词：dieter, abstemious person


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gorges himself on pizza every Friday. = 他每个星期五都狂吃披萨。
They gorge on sweets after dinner. = 他们晚饭后暴饮暴食甜食。


一般过去时:
She gorged herself at the wedding feast. = 她在婚礼宴会上一顿猛吃。

过去完成时:

They had gorged on snacks, so they weren't very hungry for dinner. = 他们已经吃了很多零食，所以晚饭不是很饿。
