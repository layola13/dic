hand:/hænd/| n. vt.|手，援助，递交|The word "hand" has a rich history, originating from Proto-Germanic *handuz, ultimately deriving from Proto-Indo-European *ghend-.  It refers to the body part used for grasping, manipulating objects, and gesturing. As a verb, it means to give or pass something to someone.|例句：She offered a helping hand. 她提供帮助。|例句：Please hand me the book. 请把书递给我。|近义词：arm,  aid, assist|反义词：foot, leg


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I hand him the letter. = 我把信递给他。
She hands out flyers. = 她分发传单。


一般过去时:
He handed me the keys. = 他把钥匙交给了我。
They handed in their assignments. = 他们交上了作业。


现在完成时:
I have handed in my report. = 我已经提交了我的报告。
She has handed out all the gifts. = 她已经分发了所有的礼物。


过去完成时:
He had handed the package before leaving. = 他在离开之前已经递交了包裹。
They had handed over the evidence to the police. = 他们已经把证据移交给警方了。


将来时:
I will hand you the document tomorrow. = 我明天会把文件交给你。
She will hand in her resignation next week. = 她下周会递交辞呈。


虚拟语气:
If I had a hand in it, I would confess. = 如果我参与其中，我会承认的。
I wish I had a hand to help. = 我真希望我能帮上忙。


被动语态:
The letter was handed to him. = 信交给了他。
The flyers were handed out quickly. = 传单很快就被分发完了。


疑问句:
Can you hand me that tool? = 你能把那个工具递给我吗？
Who handed you the package? = 谁把包裹交给了你？


否定句:
I didn't hand him the money. = 我没有把钱给他。
She didn't hand in her homework. = 她没有交作业。
