quoting:/ˈkwoʊtɪŋ/ | v. |引用，引述|The act of repeating or citing a passage or expression from a book, speech, or other source.|例句：He quoting extensively from Shakespeare in his speech. 他在演讲中大量引用莎士比亚的作品。|例句：The article quoting several experts on the subject. 这篇文章引用了几位该领域的专家。|近义词：citing, repeating, paraphrasing|反义词：originating, creating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is quoting the Bible. = 他正在引用圣经。
She quotes Shakespeare frequently. = 她经常引用莎士比亚的作品。


一般过去时:
They quoted the statistics incorrectly. = 他们错误地引用了统计数据。
The professor quoted a famous philosopher. = 教授引用了一位著名的哲学家。


现在完成时:
I have quoted this passage before. = 我以前引用过这段话。
She has quoted that author in several essays. = 她在几篇论文中都引用过那位作者。


过去完成时:
He had quoted the same poem in his previous lecture. = 他在之前的讲座中引用过同一首诗。
They had quoted the wrong figures in their report. = 他们在报告中引用了错误的数据。


将来时:
I will quote a relevant example. = 我将引用一个相关的例子。
She will quote from the Declaration of Independence. = 她将引用《独立宣言》中的内容。


虚拟语气:
If I were to quote him directly, I would need his permission. = 如果我要直接引用他的话，我需要他的许可。
I wish I had quoted the source correctly. = 我希望我正确地引用了来源。


被动语态:
This verse was quoted frequently in the sermon. = 这节经文在布道中被频繁引用。
His words were quoted out of context. = 他的话被断章取义地引用了。


疑问句:
Where did you quote that from? = 你从哪里引用那句话的？
Who is quoted in this article? = 这篇文章引用了谁？


否定句:
He did not quote any sources. = 他没有引用任何来源。
She didn't quote the author directly. = 她没有直接引用作者的话。


PHRASE->
quote somebody directly = 直接引用某人
quote something verbatim = 原封不动地引用某事
quote out of context = 断章取义
a direct quote = 直接引语
quote unquote = 引述
