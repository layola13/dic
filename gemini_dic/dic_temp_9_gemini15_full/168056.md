privatising: /ˈpraɪvətaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 私有化 | The act of transferring ownership of a business, industry, or service from a government to a private company or individual.  It involves the sale of state-owned assets to private entities.  This process is often undertaken to increase efficiency, reduce government spending, and promote competition. |  The government is privatising the railway system. 政府正在将铁路系统私有化。The privatisation of the postal service led to increased efficiency. 邮政服务的私有化提高了效率。| nationalising, socialising | nationalisation, socialisation |

The government is privatising several state-owned companies. = 政府正在将几家国有公司私有化。
They are privatising the energy sector. = 他们正在将能源部门私有化。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:
