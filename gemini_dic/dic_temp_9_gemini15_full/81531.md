gravitation:/ˌɡrævɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |万有引力，重力，吸引力 |The force of attraction by which terrestrial bodies tend to fall toward the center of the earth.  It is also the force that causes astronomical bodies to orbit one another.|例句：Newton's law of gravitation explains the movement of planets around the sun. 牛顿的万有引力定律解释了行星绕太阳运行的现象。

例句：The gravitation pull of the moon affects the tides. 月球的万有引力影响着潮汐。


近义词：attraction, pull, gravity |反义词：repulsion
