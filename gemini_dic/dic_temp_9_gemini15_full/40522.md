controlled:/kənˈtroʊld/ | adj. | 受控制的；受约束的；有节制的 |  adj.  Meaning:  Subject to control; regulated; restrained.  It describes something or someone that is managed, directed, or limited in its actions or behavior. |例句：The experiment was carefully controlled to ensure accurate results.  实验得到了仔细的控制以确保结果准确。

The children were well-behaved and controlled during the school trip. 孩子们在学校旅行期间表现良好且行为得体。|近义词：regulated, managed, restrained, governed |反义词：uncontrolled, unrestrained, wild, free


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The temperature is controlled by a thermostat. = 温度由恒温器控制。
The company carefully controls its expenses. = 公司仔细控制其开支。

一般过去时:
The fire was quickly controlled by firefighters. = 大火很快被消防员控制住了。

现在完成时:
They have controlled the spread of the disease. = 他们已经控制住了疾病的蔓延。
The situation has been controlled effectively. = 局势已被有效控制。

过去完成时:

The police had controlled the crowd before the riot broke out. = 在暴乱爆发前，警方控制住了人群。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
