perception: /pərˈsɛpʃən/ | n. |感知，感觉；理解，看法 |The process of becoming aware of something through the senses.  It also refers to an awareness of something through sight, hearing, touch, taste, or smell, or a way of understanding or interpreting something. |例句：His perception of the situation was quite different from mine. 他的看法与我的大相径庭。

例句：Her acute perception allowed her to notice the subtle change in his expression. 她敏锐的感知力让她注意到他表情的细微变化。


近义词：understanding, apprehension, awareness, insight, cognizance | 反义词：misunderstanding, misinterpretation, ignorance


词源:  From Old French *percepcion, from Latin perceptio "a receiving, seizing, taking possession of," from percipere "to receive, seize, perceive," from per- "thoroughly" + capere "to take."
