polentas:/pəˈlɛntɑːz/| n. |玉米粥（意大利菜）；粗玉米粉|Polenta is a dish made from cornmeal. It's a staple food in Northern Italy.|例句：We had polenta with sausage for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了香肠玉米粥。

例句：The restaurant is famous for its creamy polenta. 这家餐馆以其奶油玉米粥而闻名。


近义词：cornmeal mush, grits|反义词：无


词源:  The word "polenta" originates from the Italian word for "porridge," ultimately deriving from the Latin word "puls," meaning "porridge" or "pap."  The Italian word evolved over time, reflecting the specific use of cornmeal in the dish.

用法说明:

Polenta is primarily a noun.  It can be used in both singular and plural forms (polenta/polentas).
