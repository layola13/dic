They may blindly follow the actions and beliefs of their hero, regardless of their flaws or negative consequences. |Example:  The singer's hero-worshippers lined up for hours to get a glimpse of him.  这位歌手的狂热粉丝们排了好几个小时的队只为能看他一眼。  |近义词:admirers, idolaters, devotees, fanatics |反义词:critics, skeptics, cynics |名词
