reunifies:/ˌriːˈjuːnɪfaɪz/| vt.|使重新统一；使重归于好 | To bring together again; to restore unity to.  The verb "reunify" implies the prior existence of a unity that has been broken or separated.  It often refers to political entities or groups of people. |例句：The country was reunified after decades of civil war.  这个国家在几十年的内战后重新统一了。

The reunification process was long and difficult, but ultimately successful.  重新统一的过程漫长而艰难，但最终成功了。

|近义词：unite, consolidate, reunite, reconcile |反义词：divide, separate, fragment |动词
