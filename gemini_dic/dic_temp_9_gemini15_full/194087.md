self-ignorance:/ˌsɛlfˈɪɡnərəns/| n. |自我无知；对自身缺乏了解|Lack of awareness or understanding of one's own character, motives, or feelings.  It often involves a disconnect between one's self-perception and reality.  This can manifest in various ways, such as being unaware of one's own biases, limitations, or impact on others.|  He acted impulsively, a clear sign of his self-ignorance. 他冲动行事，这清楚地表明了他对自己的无知。

His self-ignorance led him to believe he was always right.  他的自我无知使他认为自己总是对的。|近义词：self-deception, unawareness, obliviousness |反义词：self-awareness, self-knowledge
