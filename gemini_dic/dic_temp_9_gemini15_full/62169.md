encodes:/ɪnˈkoʊdz/ | vt. |编码，加密，译码|To convert information or data into a code, especially for transmission or storage.  It also means to express something in a particular way or form.|例句：The message was encoded to prevent unauthorized access.  这条消息经过编码以防止未经授权的访问。

The software encodes the video before uploading it to the server. 该软件在将视频上传到服务器之前对其进行编码。


近义词：cipher, encrypt, translate | 反义词：decode, decrypt | 词性：动词
