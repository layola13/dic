cinchonism:/ˈsɪŋkənɪzəm/| n.| 金鸡纳霜中毒 |A group of symptoms caused by an overdose of quinine or other cinchona alkaloids.  These symptoms can include tinnitus (ringing in the ears), hearing loss, headache, dizziness, visual disturbances, nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea. In severe cases, cinchonism can lead to cardiovascular collapse and death.| 例句：The patient presented with symptoms consistent with cinchonism, including tinnitus and visual disturbances.  病人表现出与金鸡纳霜中毒一致的症状，包括耳鸣和视力障碍。


The patient experienced severe cinchonism after taking a large dose of quinine. 病人在服用大量奎宁后出现了严重的金鸡纳霜中毒。


近义词：quinism (less common)| 反义词：无


词源:  The term "cinchonism" is derived from the genus *Cinchona*, a group of trees from which quinine and other cinchona alkaloids are extracted.  These alkaloids have been used for centuries to treat malaria. The suffix "-ism" indicates a state, condition, or doctrine.  因此，cinchonism 指的是由金鸡纳树碱类物质引起的疾病状态。
