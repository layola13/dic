forestless:/ˈfɔːrɪstləs/ | adj. | 无森林的，没有树林的 | Lacking forests or trees.  The term describes a landscape or area devoid of trees and the characteristic features of a forest ecosystem. | Example: The landscape was stark and forestless, stretching as far as the eye could see.  这片景色荒凉而无森林，一眼望不到边。  The once-lush valley is now barren and forestless due to deforestation.  曾经郁郁葱葱的山谷现在由于森林砍伐而变得贫瘠且无树木。 | 近义词：treeless, woodlandless, woodless | 反义词：forested, wooded
