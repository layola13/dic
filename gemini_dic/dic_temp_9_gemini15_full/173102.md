quasar:/ˈkweɪzɑːr/|/ˈkwɑːzɑːr/| n. |类星体|An extremely distant and luminous celestial object, emitting exceptionally large amounts of energy, believed to be powered by a supermassive black hole at the center of a young galaxy.|例句：Quasars are among the most distant and luminous objects in the universe. 类星体是宇宙中最遥远和最明亮的天体之一。

例句：The intense energy output of quasars is still not fully understood. 类星体巨大的能量输出至今仍未完全被理解。|近义词：active galactic nucleus (AGN)|反义词：无
