deducting:/dɪˈdʌktɪŋ/| v. |扣除；减去；推断 |The act of subtracting or taking away; inferring or concluding from evidence.  |Example: The company is deducting taxes from my paycheck. 公司正在从我的工资中扣税。


Example:  By deducting the expenses, we can calculate the net profit. 通过扣除费用，我们可以计算净利润。


近义词：subtracting, subtracting from,  withdrawing,  inferring


反义词：adding, including


词性：动词 (present participle of deduct)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are deducting the cost of the materials from the final bill. 他们正在从最终账单中扣除材料成本。
The software automatically deducts sales tax. 该软件会自动扣除销售税。


一般过去时:
They deducted the expenses from the total revenue. 他们从总收入中扣除了支出。
The accountant deducted the wrong amount. 会计扣错了金额。


现在完成时:
We have deducted all applicable expenses. 我们已经扣除了所有适用的费用。
The bank has deducted the overdraft fee from my account. 银行已经从我的账户中扣除了透支费用。


过去完成时:
They had deducted the service charge before I received the invoice. 我收到发票之前，他们已经扣除了服务费。
The company had deducted the bonus from her salary. 公司已经从她的工资中扣除了奖金。


将来时:
They will deduct the shipping fee from the total amount. 他们将从总金额中扣除运费。
The system will automatically deduct the discount. 系统将自动扣除折扣。


虚拟语气:
If they deducted the full amount, we would have no profit. 如果他们扣除全部金额，我们将没有利润。
I wish they had deducted the correct amount. 我希望他们扣除了正确的金额。


被动语态:
The tax is deducted automatically. 税款会自动扣除。
The discount will be deducted from the price. 折扣将从价格中扣除。


疑问句:
Is the tax deducted from the gross income? 税款是从总收入中扣除的吗？
Are any other fees deducted?  还有其他费用被扣除吗？


否定句:
The company did not deduct the correct amount. 公司没有扣除正确的金额。
They will not deduct the shipping fee. 他们不会扣除运费。



PHRASE->
deduct from: 从...中扣除

deduct points: 扣分
