re-freshes:/rɪˈfreʃɪz/ | v. |使恢复精力；使更新；使想起；|To make someone or something feel less tired or more energetic; to make something seem new again; to remind someone of something.|例句：The cool breeze refreshes me.  凉爽的微风使我感到神清气爽。
The new coat of paint refreshes the old house. 新涂的油漆使老房子焕然一新。|近义词：invigorate, revitalize, renew|反义词：exhaust, tire, deplete|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cool breeze refreshes me every day. = 凉爽的微风每天都使我感到神清气爽。
This coffee refreshes my mind. = 这杯咖啡使我头脑清醒。


一般过去时:
The rain refreshed the parched land. = 雨水使干旱的土地恢复了生机。
The vacation refreshed her spirit. = 假期使她精神焕发。


现在完成时:
The long sleep has refreshed me. = 长时间的睡眠使我恢复了精力。
This news has refreshed my memory of that event. = 这条新闻使我想起了那件事。


过去完成时:
The rest had refreshed him before the next race. = 在下一场比赛之前，休息使他恢复了体力。
She had refreshed her makeup before the meeting. = 会议之前，她补过妆。


将来时:
A short break will refresh us. = 短暂的休息会让我们恢复精力。
The new software will refresh the user interface. = 新软件将更新用户界面。


虚拟语气:
If I had a cup of tea, it would refresh me. = 如果我喝杯茶，我会感到精神焕发。
I wish the cold water could refresh me more. = 我希望冷水能让我更精神一些。


被动语态:
I was refreshed by the cool air. = 我被凉爽的空气使精神焕发。
The website is refreshed automatically every hour. = 网站每小时自动刷新。


疑问句:
Does this drink refresh you? = 这饮料能让你提神吗？
Will this new design refresh the brand image? = 这种新的设计会更新品牌形象吗？


否定句:
The long journey didn't refresh me. = 漫长的旅程并没有使我恢复精力。
The old system hasn't been refreshed in years. = 老系统多年来没有更新过。
