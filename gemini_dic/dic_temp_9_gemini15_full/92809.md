iconoclasm:/ˈaɪkənˌɒklæzəm/ | n. |偶像破坏;反传统;对传统习俗或信仰的攻击 |The deliberate destruction of religious images.  Iconoclasm is the action of attacking or rejecting cherished beliefs and institutions. It often involves challenging established norms and authorities.  | Example: The Reformation was a period of intense iconoclasm, with many religious images being destroyed.  宗教改革时期是激烈的偶像破坏时期，许多宗教形象被毁坏。  |近义词：heresy, rebellion, subversion |反义词：conformity, tradition, orthodoxy |名词
