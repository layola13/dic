endoscopes:/ˈɛndəskoʊps/| n. |内窥镜（复数）|A medical instrument consisting of a long, thin, flexible tube with a light and a camera at the end, used for examining the inside of the body.  It is used to visualize internal organs and tissues without the need for major surgery.|例句：The doctor used endoscopes to examine the patient's stomach.医生用内窥镜检查了病人的胃。|  The surgeon inserted the endoscopes through a small incision. 外科医生通过一个小切口插入内窥镜。|近义词：endoscope (单数)|反义词：无
