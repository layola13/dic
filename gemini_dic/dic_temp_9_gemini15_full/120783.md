misappropriated:/ˌmɪsˌəˌproʊpriˌeɪtɪd/ | v. | 挪用；盗用；滥用 | To use something (especially money or property) that is not yours for your own benefit, especially in a dishonest or illegal way. |  The company discovered that funds had been misappropriated by a senior manager.  这家公司发现一名高级经理挪用了资金。

The CEO misappropriated company funds for personal gain. 首席执行官挪用公司资金用于个人利益。


近义词：embezzle, steal, defraud, divert | 反义词：donate, contribute, return


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misappropriates company funds. 他挪用公司资金。
She misappropriates resources for personal use. 她挪用资源用于个人用途。


一般过去时:
They misappropriated the charitable donations. 他们挪用了慈善捐款。
The accountant misappropriated millions of dollars. 会计挪用了数百万美元。


现在完成时:
He has misappropriated funds for years. 他多年来一直挪用资金。
The government has misappropriated taxpayer money. 政府挪用了纳税人的钱。


过去完成时:
They had misappropriated the money before the audit. 他们在审计之前就已经挪用了这笔钱。
The thief had misappropriated the jewels before the police arrived. 小偷在警察到达之前就已经盗取了珠宝。


将来时:
He will misappropriate the funds if given the opportunity. 如果有机会，他会挪用这笔资金。
They will misappropriate more resources if not stopped. 如果不阻止他们，他们会挪用更多资源。


虚拟语气:
If he had not misappropriated the funds, the company would not have gone bankrupt. 如果他没有挪用资金，公司就不会破产。
I wish they hadn’t misappropriated the donations. 我真希望他们没有挪用捐款。


被动语态:
The funds were misappropriated by a trusted employee. 这笔资金被一位信任的员工挪用。
Millions of dollars were misappropriated by the corrupt officials. 数百万美元被腐败官员挪用。


疑问句:
Were the funds misappropriated? 资金被挪用了吗？
Has anyone misappropriated the company's assets? 有人挪用公司资产了吗？


否定句:
He did not misappropriated the money. 他没有挪用这笔钱。
The company claims that no funds were misappropriated. 公司声称没有挪用任何资金。


PHRASE->
misappropriation of funds 资金挪用
misappropriation of assets 资产挪用
misappropriation of property 财产挪用
misappropriation of power 滥用权力
misappropriation of resources 资源挪用

词源:
mis- (错误地) + appropriate (拨款；恰当的)  The word "appropriate" originally meant "to set aside for a particular purpose,"  and the prefix "mis" adds the sense of wrongdoing or improper use.
