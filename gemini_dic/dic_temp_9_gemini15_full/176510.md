re-projects:/ˌriːˈprɑːdʒɛkts/| vt.|再策划，重新规划|To plan or design something again, often with modifications or improvements.|The architect re-projected the building to incorporate more natural light. 建筑师重新设计了建筑，以融入更多的自然光。|近义词：replan, redesign, rethink|反义词：abandon, scrap


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-project the marketing strategy every quarter.  他们每季度都会重新规划营销策略。
The company re-projects its financial goals annually. 公司每年都会重新规划其财务目标。


一般过去时:
The team re-projected the timeline after encountering unforeseen delays.  团队在遇到不可预见的延误后重新规划了时间表。
She re-projected her presentation after receiving feedback.  她在收到反馈后重新设计了她的演示文稿。


现在完成时:
They have re-projected the budget several times this year.  他们今年已经几次重新规划了预算。
We have re-projected the project scope to better meet client needs. 我们已经重新规划了项目范围，以更好地满足客户需求。


过去完成时:
They had re-projected the sales figures before the final report was submitted.  在提交最终报告之前，他们已经重新规划了销售数据。
The director had re-projected the film's ending before its release.  导演在电影发行前已经重新设计了电影的结尾。


将来时:
We will re-project the design next week after considering the new data.  下周我们将重新规划设计，以考虑新的数据。
The government will re-project the infrastructure plan based on economic forecasts. 政府将根据经济预测重新规划基础设施计划。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would re-project the entire project. 如果我们有更多的时间，我们会重新规划整个项目。
It would be wise to re-project the budget if unexpected costs arise. 如果出现意外成本，重新规划预算将是明智之举。


被动语态:
The project was re-projected after the initial design was deemed unsatisfactory.  在最初的设计被认为不令人满意后，项目被重新规划了。
The schedule is being re-projected to accommodate the new team member.  时间表正在被重新规划以适应新的团队成员。


疑问句:
Will the team re-project the project before the deadline?  团队会在截止日期前重新规划项目吗？
Have they re-projected the sales targets for the next fiscal year? 他们是否已经为下一个财政年度重新规划了销售目标？


否定句:
They did not re-project the budget despite the rising costs.  尽管成本上升，他们并没有重新规划预算。
The company will not re-project the product launch date. 公司不会重新规划产品发布日期。
