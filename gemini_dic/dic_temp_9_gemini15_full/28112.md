capitalizer:/ˈkæpɪtəlaɪzər/ | n. | 首字母大写器，大写转换器 | A capitalizer is a tool or program that automatically converts the first letter of each word in a text to uppercase.  It is often used in text processing and programming to format text appropriately. | Example:  The software includes a built-in capitalizer to ensure consistent formatting.  这个软件包含一个内置的首字母大写器来确保一致的格式。 |近义词：uppercase converter, case changer |反义词：lowercase converter
