chipotles:/ˌtʃɪpəˈtoʊlz/| n.|墨西哥熏辣椒|Dried, smoked jalapeño peppers.  Chipotles have a smoky, slightly sweet, and spicy flavor. They are often used in sauces, stews, and rubs for meats.|例句：The chili contained chipotles for a deep smoky flavor. 这道辣椒使用了墨西哥熏辣椒，使其具有浓郁的烟熏味。

例句：I added some chipotles to the barbecue sauce for extra heat. 我在烧烤酱里加了一些墨西哥熏辣椒，以增加辣味。


近义词：smoked jalapeños, smoked peppers|反义词：fresh jalapeños (in terms of preparation)
