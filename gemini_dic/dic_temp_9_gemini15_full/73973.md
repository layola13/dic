fortissimo:/fɔːrˈtɪsɪməʊ/| adj. adv.| 最强音；极强地 |  Very loud; with maximum force.  It is a musical direction indicating the loudest possible volume.  | 例句：The orchestra swelled to a fortissimo.  乐队声音增强到最强音。  The pianist played the final chord with fortissimo.  钢琴家以最强音演奏了最后一个和弦。 | 近义词：fortississimo, very loud, thunderous | 反义词：pianissimo, very quiet, soft
