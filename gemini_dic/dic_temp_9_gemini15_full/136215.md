nonremediable: /ˌnɒnriˈmiːdjəbl/ | adj. | 不可补救的，无法弥补的 | incapable of being remedied or corrected; incurable.  | The damage to the antique vase was nonremediable.  这件古董花瓶的损坏是无法弥补的。 | irremediable, incurable, irreparable | remediable, curable


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, and therefore doesn't have verb conjugations.  Its usage is limited to descriptive sentences.

例句1: The damage caused by the flood was nonremediable.  洪水造成的损害是不可弥补的。
例句2: His reputation suffered a nonremediable blow after the scandal.  丑闻之后，他的名誉遭受了无法挽回的打击。


PHRASE->
This word is not typically used in common phrases.  It's primarily used as a standalone adjective.
