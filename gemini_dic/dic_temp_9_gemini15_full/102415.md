jack's:/dʒæks/| n.|杰克的；杰克所有物|Jack's possessive form, indicating ownership or belonging to someone named Jack.  It's a possessive noun formed by adding an apostrophe and 's' to the proper noun "Jack".|例句：That's Jack's car.那是杰克的车。

例句：Jack's house is very big.杰克的房子很大。


近义词：Jack's belongings, the property of Jack|反义词:None|词性:Noun (possessive)


USAGE->
This word is a possessive form and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It doesn't have tenses.  Its usage is always to show possession.


PHRASE->
Jack's place = 杰克的家
Jack's idea = 杰克的主意
Jack's book = 杰克的书
It's Jack's turn = 轮到杰克了
That's Jack's fault = 那是杰克的错
