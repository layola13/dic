nonpossessiveness:/ˌnɒn.pəˈzɛsɪvnəs/ | n. | 非占有性；不占有；无私 | The lack of a possessive attitude or behavior; unselfishness.  It refers to a state of being free from possessiveness, characterized by generosity and lack of desire to control or own things or people. | Example:  Her nonpossessiveness allowed her partner to pursue his own interests without feeling constrained.  她的不占有性让她的伴侣能够追求自己的兴趣，而不会感到受约束。|近义词：unselfishness, generosity, detachment, liberality | 反义词：possessiveness, selfishness, greed, jealousy | 名词
