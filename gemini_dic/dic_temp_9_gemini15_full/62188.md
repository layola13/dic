encompasses:/ɪnˈkʌmpəsɪz/| vt.|包含，包括，围绕|To include or contain (something) within its scope; surround or enclose.|The course encompasses a wide range of topics. 这门课程包含了广泛的主题。|近义词：include, comprise, embrace|反义词：exclude, omit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project encompasses several key initiatives.  = 这个项目包含几个关键举措。
Her responsibilities encompass all aspects of marketing. = 她的职责涵盖了市场营销的各个方面。

一般过去时:
The study encompassed a large sample of participants. = 这项研究包含大量的参与者。
The ancient city encompassed a vast area. = 古城占地辽阔。

现在完成时:
The new law has encompassed many previously unregulated activities. = 新法已经涵盖了许多以前不受管制的活动。
Our research has encompassed various methodologies. = 我们的研究涵盖了各种方法。

过去完成时:
By the time the meeting concluded, the discussion had encompassed several important issues. = 会议结束时，讨论已经涵盖了几个重要议题。
The investigation had encompassed all potential suspects. = 调查已经涵盖了所有可能的嫌疑人。

将来时:
The revised curriculum will encompass more practical skills. = 修订后的课程将包含更多实践技能。
The next phase of the project will encompass the development of a new product. = 项目的下一阶段将包括新产品的开发。

虚拟语气:
If the plan encompassed all potential risks, it would be more successful. = 如果该计划包含所有潜在风险，它将会更成功。
I wish the report encompassed more detailed analysis. = 我希望报告包含更详细的分析。

被动语态:
Many aspects of the problem were encompassed in the report. = 报告中包含了这个问题的许多方面。
The entire city was encompassed by a thick fog. = 整座城市被浓雾笼罩。

疑问句:
Does the agreement encompass all the necessary clauses? = 这份协议是否包含所有必要的条款？
What areas does the research encompass? = 这项研究涵盖哪些领域？

否定句:
The initial proposal did not encompass the long-term implications. = 初步方案没有包含长期影响。
The contract does not encompass liability for unforeseen circumstances. = 合同不包含对不可预见情况的责任。


PHRASE->

encompass all aspects of = 涵盖…的各个方面
to be encompassed by = 被…所包围
encompass the entire  = 包含全部
