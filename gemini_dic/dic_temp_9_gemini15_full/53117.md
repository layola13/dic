diplex:/ˈdaɪplɛks/ | n. |双工器；双工系统|A device or system that allows simultaneous transmission and reception of signals over a single communication channel.  It is commonly used in telecommunications and radio frequency systems.|例句：The diplexer allowed the radio to transmit and receive on the same frequency.  双工器允许无线电在同一频率上传输和接收信号。|近义词：duplexer |反义词：simplex
