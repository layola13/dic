portmote:/pɔːrˈmoʊt/|  n. |促进，推进；推广|A portmote is a port town's assembly or court, particularly in medieval England, responsible for managing port affairs and regulating trade.  The term has evolved to encompass any action that promotes or advances something.|The new marketing campaign will portmote the company's new product.|近义词：promote, advance, further|反义词：hinder, impede, obstruct|名词
