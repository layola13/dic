half-angry:/ˈhæfˈæŋɡri/| adj.|半怒的，有点生气的|Partially angry; showing some anger but not fully enraged.  It suggests a state of mild irritation or annoyance rather than intense fury.|例句：He gave me a half-angry look when I was late. 他因为我迟到而给了我一个有点生气的眼神。

例句：She felt half-angry and half-sad about the situation.  她对这种情况感到又有点生气又有点悲伤。|近义词：irritated, annoyed, disgruntled, peeved|反义词：calm, serene, peaceful, happy
