invalidly:/ɪnˈvælɪdli/| adv.|无效地，不合法地 | done in a way that is not legally or officially acceptable.  The contract was invalidly drawn up, rendering it unenforceable.|The marriage was declared invalidly contracted due to the prior existing marriage of one party. |有效地，合法地 | legally, validly | no direct antonym, but the opposite would be described by phrases like "validly," "legally," or "correctly."
