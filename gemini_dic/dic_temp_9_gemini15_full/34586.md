clears:/klɪərz/ | vt.  |清除，使干净；澄清，阐明；（嗓子）清了清；变晴朗 |to make something clean or free from something unwanted; to make something easier to understand; to clear one's throat; to become clear (weather)|例句：He cleared his throat before speaking. 他在说话前清了清嗓子。

The sun cleared the clouds. 太阳驱散了乌云。|近义词：cleanse, purify, elucidate, clarify |反义词：cloud, obscure, confuse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He clears his desk every evening. 他每天晚上都清理他的桌子。
She clears the table after dinner. 晚餐后她收拾桌子。


一般过去时:
They cleared the snow from the driveway. 他们清除了车道上的积雪。
The judge cleared him of all charges. 法官宣告他所有指控不成立。


现在完成时:
He has cleared his debts. 他已经还清了债务。
She has cleared up the misunderstanding. 她已经消除了误解。


过去完成时:
They had cleared the room before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前他们已经清扫了房间。
He had cleared the path before we started hiking. 我们开始徒步旅行之前，他已经清理了道路。


将来时:
We will clear the garden this weekend. 我们这个周末将清理花园。
The storm will clear by morning.  暴风雨将在早晨过去。


虚拟语气:
If I cleared my schedule, I could go with you. 如果我腾出时间，我就能和你一起去。
I wish I could clear up this confusion. 我希望我能消除这种混乱。


被动语态:
The table was cleared quickly. 桌子很快就被收拾干净了。
The misunderstanding was cleared up by the manager. 误会被经理解释清楚了。


疑问句:
Does he clear his throat often? 他经常清嗓子吗？
Will the weather clear up tomorrow? 明天天气会放晴吗？


否定句:
He didn't clear the dishes after dinner. 晚餐后他没有收拾盘子。
The explanation didn't clear up my doubts. 解释并没有消除我的疑虑。


PHRASE->
clear out: 清理；离开
clear up:  清理；（天气）放晴；解决
clear away:  清除；收拾
make clear:  讲清楚；阐明
clear the air:  消除误会；缓和气氛
