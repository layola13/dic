non-executive:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪv/| adj.|非执行的；非经营管理的|Not involved in the day-to-day running of a company or organization.|The board comprises both executive and non-executive directors.|近义词：independent, external|反义词：executive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a non-executive director. = 他是一位非执行董事。
The committee has several non-executive members. = 委员会有一些非执行成员。

一般过去时:
She was a non-executive chairman for five years. = 她担任了五年非执行主席。
The company appointed a non-executive director last month. = 公司上个月任命了一位非执行董事。

现在完成时:
They have appointed many non-executive directors this year. = 他们今年任命了许多非执行董事。
The board has had a strong focus on non-executive input. = 董事会一直非常重视非执行董事的意见。

过去完成时:
The company had already appointed a non-executive director before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，公司已经任命了一位非执行董事。
She had served as a non-executive director for several companies before retiring. = 在退休之前，她曾担任过几家公司的非执行董事。


将来时:

The company anticipates having more non-executive representation in the future. = 公司预计未来将有更多的非执行董事代表。


虚拟语气:
If they had a non-executive director, the situation might have been different. = 如果他们有一位非执行董事，情况可能会有所不同。
It would be beneficial to have more non-executive input on the board. = 董事会拥有更多非执行董事的意见将大有裨益。

被动语态:
