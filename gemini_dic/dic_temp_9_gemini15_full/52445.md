diecian:/ˌdaɪɪˈsæn/| adj.| 十六的，十几个的 |  The word "diecian" is a Spanish adjective meaning "sixteen" or, more generally, "of sixteen". It's used to describe something related to the number sixteen.  For example,  "dieciséis" is the Spanish word for the number sixteen itself (as a noun).   The "-ian" suffix indicates an adjective form.  | 例句：Tengo diecian años. = I am sixteen years old.  El libro tiene diecian capítulos. = The book has sixteen chapters. | 近义词：sixteen (English), seize (French) | 反义词：un (Spanish for "one", indicating the opposite of sixteen) |  形容词
