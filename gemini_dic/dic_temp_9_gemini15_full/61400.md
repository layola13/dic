embleming:/ˈɛmbləmɪŋ/ | v. | 象征；代表；赋予象征意义 | To represent something symbolically; to serve as an emblem for something.  | The artist skillfully emblemed the nation's struggles in his painting.  艺术家在他的画中巧妙地象征了国家的奋斗。 | symbolize, represent, typify |  (无明确反义词，可根据语境选择如：misrepresent, distort) | 动词
