eternalizes: /ɪˈtɜːrnəlaɪzɪz/ | vt. |使永恒；使不朽| To make something eternal or immortal; to preserve something in memory or history for a very long time.  |例句：The monument eternalizes the memory of the fallen soldiers.  这座纪念碑使阵亡士兵的记忆永垂不朽。


The artist's work eternalizes the beauty of the natural world.  这位艺术家的作品使自然世界的美丽永存。|近义词：immortalize, perpetuate, enshrine |反义词：obliterate, destroy, forget
