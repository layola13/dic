princes:/ˈprɪnsɪz/| n.|王子们，公主们|The plural form of "prince" or "princess," referring to multiple princes or princesses.|例句：The princes and princesses attended the royal ball. 王子和公主们参加了皇家舞会。|近义词：royal offspring, heirs|反义词：commoners
