It is often used in physics, optics, and materials science to describe the shape of objects or particles that are not perfectly spherical.  |Example: The nonsphericality of the nanoparticles significantly affected their optical properties.  非球形纳米粒子的形状显著影响了它们的光学特性。|近义词：asphericity, deviation from sphericity |反义词：sphericity
