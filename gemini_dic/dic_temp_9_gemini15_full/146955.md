overinvolve:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɒlv/ | vt. |过度参与；过分牵涉|To involve oneself too much in something; to become excessively concerned or engaged in an activity or situation.  | 例句：He tends to overinvolve himself in his children's lives, which can be detrimental to their independence. 他倾向于过度参与孩子的生活，这可能会损害他们的独立性。

He overinvolved himself in the office politics, leading to unnecessary stress. 他过分参与办公室政治，导致不必要的压力。| 近义词：interfere, meddle, intrude | 反义词：uninvolve, detach, ignore | 词性:动词
