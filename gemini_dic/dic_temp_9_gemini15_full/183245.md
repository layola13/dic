repressurized:/ˌriːˈprɛʃəraɪzd/| adj.| 再加压的，重新增压的 |  Describes something that has had its pressure increased again after a previous decrease or loss of pressure.  It implies a return to a previous pressure level or a new higher pressure level. |例句：The submarine was repressurized after a leak was repaired. =  潜艇在修复泄漏后重新增压了。The scuba tank needed to be repressurized before the next dive. = 潜水气瓶在下次潜水前需要重新增压。|近义词：recharged, re-pressurized |反义词：depressurized, decompressed |形容词
