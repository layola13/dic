candleholder: /ˈkændəlˌhoʊldər/ | n. |烛台|A device used to hold a candle.  It can be made from various materials and in many styles.|例句：She placed a flickering candle in the elegant candleholder. 她把跳动的蜡烛放在精致的烛台上。

例句：The antique candleholder added a touch of elegance to the dining table.  这支古董烛台为餐桌增添了一丝优雅。


近义词：candlestick, candelabra (for multiple candles)
反义词：无
