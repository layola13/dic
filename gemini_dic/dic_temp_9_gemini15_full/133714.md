nonfrosted:/ˌnɑːnˈfrɒstɪd/| adj. |未结霜的；无霜的 | Not covered with frost.  This term is primarily used to describe surfaces, particularly food items, that lack a layer of frost or ice crystals. | Example: The nonfrosted berries were easier to handle.  未结霜的浆果更容易处理。  The cake was nonfrosted, awaiting its final decoration. 蛋糕没有上糖霜，等待最后的装饰。 |近义词：frostless, unfrosted |反义词：frosted, iced


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "What is/was/will be nonfrosted?".  Its usage is descriptive.  Therefore, tense-based conjugations are not applicable.  The grammatical structures below demonstrate how "nonfrosted" functions in a sentence:


肯定句:
The nonfrosted window was clear. = 未结霜的窗户很清晰。
The nonfrosted cake was simple yet elegant. = 没有上霜的蛋糕简单而优雅。


否定句:
The window wasn't nonfrosted; it was covered in ice. = 窗户没有未结霜；它覆盖着冰。
The cake wasn't nonfrosted; it had a thick layer of frosting. = 蛋糕没有没上霜；它有一层厚厚的糖霜。


疑问句:
Was the windshield nonfrosted? =挡风玻璃没结霜吗？
Is the pie nonfrosted? =派没上霜吗？


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "nonfrosted".  Its usage is generally standalone as a descriptive adjective.  However, you can use it with various nouns:

nonfrosted berries = 未结霜的浆果
nonfrosted cake = 未上霜的蛋糕
nonfrosted glass = 未结霜的玻璃
nonfrosted window = 未结霜的窗户
