phenology:/fɪˈnɒlədʒi/ | /fɪˈnɑːlədʒi/| n. |物候学|The study of the timing of natural events in plant and animal life cycles, especially in relation to climate and seasonal changes.  It examines the recurring biological events in nature and how they relate to climate and other factors.  |Example: The study of phenology helps us understand the impact of climate change on ecosystems. 物候学的研究帮助我们了解气候变化对生态系统的影响。  |近义词:seasonal study, ecological timing, biological calendar |反义词:None.
