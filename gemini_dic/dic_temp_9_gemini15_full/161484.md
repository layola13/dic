potch:/pɒtʃ/| vt.|  偷猎（动物），腌渍（食物）| to hunt or catch (animals) illegally; to preserve (food) in a liquid, typically brine or vinegar |例句：They were caught potching deer in the royal forest. 他们在皇家森林里偷猎鹿被抓住了。|近义词：poach, pilfer, pilferage |反义词：protect, conserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He potches fish illegally. = 他非法捕捞鱼类。
She potches eggs for breakfast. = 她腌制鸡蛋做早餐。


一般过去时:
The poachers potched salmon from the river. = 偷猎者从河里偷猎鲑鱼。
My grandmother potched the onions before adding them to the stew. = 我祖母在把洋葱加入炖菜之前先腌制了它们。

They have potched several deer this season. = 他们这个季度已经偷猎了几只鹿。
She has potched the pears in cider for a week. = 她用苹果酒腌制梨子已经一个星期了。
