de-automates: /ˌdiːˈɔːtəmeɪts/ | v. | 使自动化程度降低；使去自动化 | To reduce the level of automation in a process or system.  The word is a combination of "de-" (meaning to remove or reverse) and "automates" (the verb form of "automate"). It implies a shift away from automated processes towards more manual or human-involved operations. | 例句：The company decided to de-automate some of its manufacturing processes to improve quality control. = 公司决定使其一些制造流程去自动化以提高质量控制。


The factory's de-automation of the packaging line led to increased labor costs. = 工厂包装生产线的去自动化导致劳动力成本增加。| 近义词：demechanize, manualize | 反义词：automate, mechanize |  动词
