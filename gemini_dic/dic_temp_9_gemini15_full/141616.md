opter:/ˈɒptər/| v.|选择，挑选|To make a choice or decision.|The company opted to invest in renewable energy.  这家公司选择投资可再生能源。|She opted for the vegetarian option. 她选择了素食。|choose, select, elect|reject, refuse


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He opts for the cheaper option. 他选择更便宜的选择。
They opt to stay home. 他们选择待在家里。

一般过去时:
She opted out of the competition. 她退出比赛。
We opted to walk instead of driving. 我们选择步行而不是开车。

现在完成时:
They have opted to relocate. 他们已经选择搬迁。
I have opted for a simpler life. 我选择了更简单的生活。

过去完成时:
He had opted to leave before the meeting ended. 他在会议结束前选择了离开。
She had opted for a different career path. 她之前选择了不同的职业道路。

将来时:
They will opt for a more sustainable solution. 他们将选择一个更可持续的解决方案。
We will opt to travel by train. 我们将选择乘坐火车旅行。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would opt for the red dress. 如果我是你，我会选择那件红裙子。
I wish I had opted for a different job. 我希望我选择了不同的工作。

被动语态:
The project was opted out of due to budget cuts.  由于预算削减，该项目被取消了。(Note: Passive voice is less common with "opt")
The location was opted for because of its accessibility.  这个地点之所以被选中是因为其便利性。(Note: Passive voice is less common with "opt")

疑问句:
Will they opt for peace or war? 他们会选择和平还是战争？
Did he opt to participate? 他选择参与了吗？

否定句:
I did not opt to go. 我没有选择去。
They opted not to attend the conference. 他们选择不参加会议。


PHRASE->

opt in: 选择加入

opt for: 选择
opt to: 选择
to opt against: 选择反对
