depicture:/dɪˈpɪktʃər/ | vt. | 描绘；描述；刻画 | To represent or portray something in words or pictures.  | 例句：The novel vividly depicts life in 19th-century London. 这部小说生动地描绘了19世纪伦敦的生活。 | 例句：The painting depicts a serene landscape. 这幅画描绘了一幅宁静的风景。 | 近义词：portray, represent, describe, illustrate | 反义词：misrepresent, obscure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist depicts the scene with great skill. = 艺术家以高超的技巧描绘了这个场景。
She depicts her childhood memories in her novel. = 她的小说描绘了她童年的记忆。

一般过去时:
The painter depicted the sunset in vibrant colors. = 画家用鲜艳的色彩描绘了日落。
The writer depicted the characters with great depth. = 作家对人物进行了深入的刻画。

现在完成时:
The author has depicted the struggles of the working class. = 作者已经描绘了工人阶级的奋斗历程。
The photographer has beautifully depicted the beauty of nature. = 摄影师已经漂亮地描绘了大自然的美丽。

过去完成时:
He had depicted the events leading up to the war in his book. = 他在他的书中描绘了导致战争发生的事件。
The artist had depicted the landscape in a realistic style. = 艺术家以写实风格描绘了这片风景。

将来时:
The film will depict the true story of a brave soldier. = 这部电影将描绘一位勇敢士兵的真实故事。
The artist will depict the bustling city scene in his next painting. = 艺术家将在他的下一幅画中描绘热闹的城市景象。

虚拟语气:
If I could paint, I would depict the beauty of the mountains. = 如果我会画画，我会描绘山脉的美丽。
I wish I could depict the emotions I felt at that moment. = 我希望我能描绘出我当时的情感。

被动语态:
The scene was depicted with remarkable detail. = 这个场景被描绘得非常细致。
The historical event was depicted inaccurately in the movie. = 这部电影不准确地描绘了这一历史事件。

疑问句:
How does the author depict the main character? = 作者是如何刻画主人公的？
Does the painting depict a realistic or abstract scene? = 这幅画描绘的是现实的还是抽象的场景？

否定句:
The painting does not depict a happy scene. = 这幅画没有描绘快乐的场景。
The novel does not accurately depict the historical period. = 这部小说没有准确地描绘那个历史时期。
