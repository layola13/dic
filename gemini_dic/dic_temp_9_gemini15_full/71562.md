词源:
The word "flashdrive" is a blend of "flash memory" and "drive".  Flash memory refers to the type of non-volatile memory used in the device, while "drive" refers to its function as a storage device.  The term emerged in the late 1990s and early 2000s with the popularization of USB flash drives.
