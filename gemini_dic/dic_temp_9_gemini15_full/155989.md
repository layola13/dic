photoheliometer:/ˌfoʊtoʊhɪliˈɒmɪtər/| n. |太阳光度计|An instrument for measuring the intensity of solar radiation.  It's used to measure the brightness of the sun's surface and can help in studying solar activity and sunspots.| The photoheliometer readings showed a significant increase in solar radiation during the recent solar flare.|近义词：solar radiometer, actinometer|反义词:None.
