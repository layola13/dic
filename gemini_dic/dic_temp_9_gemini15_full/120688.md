misadventure:/ˌmɪsˈædˌvɛntʃər/ | n. | 不幸事故；灾难；坏运气 | An unfortunate accident or unlucky event.  |例句：His trip to the mountains ended in misadventure when he fell and broke his leg.  他的登山旅行以不幸事故告终，因为他摔断了腿。

例句：The company's latest project was a misadventure, resulting in significant financial losses.  公司的最新项目是一场灾难，导致了巨大的经济损失。


近义词：accident, mishap, misfortune, calamity | 反义词：good fortune, success, triumph | 词性：名词

词源:
The word "misadventure" combines the prefix "mis-" (meaning "bad" or "wrong") with the noun "adventure."  Therefore, it literally means a "bad" or "unsuccessful" adventure.  The word's origins trace back to Middle English, evolving from Old French and ultimately from Latin roots.
