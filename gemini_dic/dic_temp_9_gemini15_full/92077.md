hyperrational: /ˌhaɪpərˈræʃənəl/ | adj. | 超理性的；过度理性的 | Excessively rational; going beyond what is reasonable or practical in pursuit of logic and efficiency.  It implies a flaw in rationality, often leading to counterproductive or undesirable outcomes.  | The hyperrational CEO ignored the emotional needs of his employees, leading to high turnover.  |  excessively rational, overly logical | emotional, irrational, illogical |
