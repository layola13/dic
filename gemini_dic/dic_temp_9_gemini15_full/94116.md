impeccancy:/ɪmˈpekənsi/| n. |无瑕疵；完美无缺|The state or quality of being impeccable; faultlessness.  It refers to the condition of being free from fault or flaw; absolute perfection.|例句：The impeccancy of her performance was truly remarkable.  她的表演完美无瑕，令人惊叹。

例句：His work showed an impeccancy of detail rarely seen. 他的作品展现了少见的细节完美。


近义词：perfection, flawlessness, faultlessness, immaculateness

反义词：flaw, imperfection, defect, blemish

词性：名词
