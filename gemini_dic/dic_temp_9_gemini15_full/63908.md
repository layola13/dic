epipharynx:/ˌɛpɪˈfærɪŋks/| n. | 咽上部 | The epipharynx is the superior part of the pharynx, located above the soft palate. It is involved in respiration and swallowing.  It's a relatively small area compared to the rest of the pharynx. | The epipharynx's function in respiration and swallowing is crucial for maintaining health. | 近义词：nasopharynx (部分重叠功能)| 反义词：无
