directs:/dəˈrɛkts/ | vt. |指导，指挥，导演；指示，管理 | To guide or control the movement or course of (something); to manage or oversee (a project, organization, etc.). |例句：The director directs the actors in the film.  导演指导电影里的演员。 |例句：She directs the marketing team. 她领导营销团队。|近义词：leads, guides, manages, supervises |反义词：follows, obeys


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher directs the students' attention to the board. = 老师引导学生们注意黑板。
He directs the company's operations. = 他指导公司的运营。


一般过去时:
The general directed the troops into battle. = 将军指挥军队投入战斗。
She directed me to the nearest station. = 她指示我到最近的车站。


现在完成时:
She has directed several successful films. = 她执导过几部成功的电影。
The project manager has directed the team effectively. = 项目经理有效地领导了团队。


过去完成时:
He had directed the orchestra for many years before retiring. = 他退休前指挥管弦乐队多年。
They had directed all resources towards the new project. = 他们之前已将所有资源都投入到新项目中。


将来时:
She will direct the play next season. = 她将在下个季度执导这部戏。
He will direct the construction of the new building. = 他将指导新建大楼的建设。


虚拟语气:
If he directed the project, it would be finished on time. = 如果他来指导这个项目，它就能按时完成。
I wish she had directed me more clearly. = 我希望她能更清晰地指导我。


被动语态:
The actors were directed by a famous director. = 演员们由一位著名导演指导。
The project was directed with great efficiency. = 项目被高效地管理。


疑问句:
Who directs the company? = 谁领导这家公司？
Does she direct the choir? = 她指挥合唱团吗？


否定句:
He doesn't direct the traffic. = 他不指挥交通。
The manager didn't direct the staff properly. = 经理没有正确地指导员工。
