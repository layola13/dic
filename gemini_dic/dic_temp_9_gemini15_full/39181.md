congelation:/kɒnˌdʒɛˈleɪʃən/ | n. |凝固；冻结；凝结 |The process of changing from a liquid to a solid state, especially by freezing.  It also refers to the solidification of a substance, often due to a decrease in temperature. |例句：The rapid congelation of the lava created a unique rock formation.  快速凝固的熔岩形成了独特的岩石构造。

The congelation of the blood in the wound slowed the bleeding.  伤口血液的凝固减缓了出血。

近义词：freezing, solidification, coagulation |反义词：melting, thawing, liquefaction
