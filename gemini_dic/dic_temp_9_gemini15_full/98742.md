insurrectionism:/ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃənɪzəm/| n. |叛乱主义；暴动主义|The belief in or the practice of insurrection.  It often refers to a political ideology advocating for the overthrow of a government through violent means.  The term can also describe the general state of unrest and rebellion within a society.|例句：The government cracked down hard on any hint of insurrectionism after the failed coup.|近义词：rebellion, sedition, revolution |反义词：obedience, compliance, submission
