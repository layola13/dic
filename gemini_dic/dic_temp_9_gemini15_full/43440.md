crapshoots:/ˈkræpʃuːts/| n.|碰运气的事；投机行为；|a situation or activity that is completely dependent on chance; a gamble.|例句：Investing in that startup was a crapshoot.  投资那家初创公司是一场赌博。

例句：The whole project felt like a crapshoot from the beginning.  从一开始，整个项目就感觉像是在碰运气。


近义词：gamble, speculation, long shot, risk


反义词：certainty, sure thing


词性:名词
