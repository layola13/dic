guv:/ɡʌv/| n.|老兄，老板，长官|A shortened form of "governor," used informally as a term of address for a man, often implying authority or respect.  It can also be used playfully or ironically.|例句：Hey guv, got a light?  老兄，有火吗？|近义词：boss, governor, chief, mate|反义词:subordinate
