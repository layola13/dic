distension:/dɪˈstɛnʃən/| n. |膨胀；扩张；紧张；冲突 |The stretching or dilation of a bodily organ or part.  It can also refer to a state of tension or strained relations. |例句：The distension of her abdomen was a symptom of her illness.  她的腹部膨胀是她生病的一个症状。

例句：The political distension between the two countries led to increased military spending.  两国之间的政治紧张导致军事支出增加。

近义词：inflation, swelling, expansion, tension, strain

反义词：contraction, compression, relaxation
