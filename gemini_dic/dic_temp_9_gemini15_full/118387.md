mermaids:/ˈmɜːrmeɪdz/| n. |美人鱼|Mythical sea creatures with the upper body of a woman and the lower body of a fish.  They are often depicted as enchanting and alluring, but also dangerous and unpredictable. |例句：Many children believe in mermaids and their magical powers. 许多孩子相信美人鱼及其神奇的力量。

例句：The story was about a prince who fell in love with a mermaid. 这个故事是关于一个爱上美人鱼的王子。


近义词：siren (in the sense of a dangerous, alluring creature)


反义词：None (as it's a mythical creature)
