plinths:/ˈplɪnθs/| n. |基座，底座 |A plinth is a heavy, flat block, typically square or rectangular, used as a base for a column, statue, or other object.  |例句：The ancient statue stood proudly on its marble plinth.  古老的雕像骄傲地矗立在它的理石基座上。

The sculptor carefully positioned the bronze sculpture on its plinth. 雕塑家小心地将青铜雕塑放在它的基座上。|近义词：base, pedestal, stand |反义词：top, summit
