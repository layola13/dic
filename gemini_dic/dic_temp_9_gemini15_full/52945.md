diners:/ˈdaɪnərz/| n. |用餐者；餐厅顾客；（尤指）小餐馆|People who are eating a meal, especially at a small restaurant.  The word often implies a casual or informal dining experience.|例句：The diners enjoyed the delicious food and friendly service. 用餐者享受了美味的食物和友好的服务。|例句：Many diners flocked to the new seafood restaurant. 许多顾客涌向这家新开的海鲜餐厅。|近义词：patrons, customers, eaters|反义词：chefs, cooks
