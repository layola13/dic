embodied:/ɪmˈbɑːdid/ | adj. | 体现的；具体化的；包含的 |  To be embodied means to be given a physical or concrete form; to be expressed or represented in a particular way. It suggests that something abstract or intangible is made real or tangible.  | The sculpture embodied the artist's vision.  这尊雕塑体现了艺术家的构思。 | The new law embodies the government's commitment to environmental protection. 这项新法律体现了政府对环境保护的承诺。 | intangible, incarnate, personified | disembodied, abstract, theoretical | adj.
