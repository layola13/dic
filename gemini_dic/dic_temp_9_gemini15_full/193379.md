seignioral: /seɪˈnjɔːriəl/ | adj. | 庄园主的；封建领主的；具有庄园主气派的 | Relating to or characteristic of a seigneur or seigniory; having the characteristics of a feudal lord or his estate.  The term often implies an antiquated system of feudal power and land ownership. |


例句：

The seignioral system in Canada was abolished in the 19th century.  加拿大的庄园制度在19世纪被废除了。

The old manor house retained a distinct seignioral air.  这座古老的庄园住宅保留着明显的庄园主气派。


近义词：feudal, manorial, aristocratic


反义词：democratic, egalitarian


词源：From Old French *seigneurial, from seigneur "lord," from Vulgar Latin *senior "elder, older," from Latin senior.


用法说明：

seignioral 主要用作形容词，描述与封建领主、庄园及其相关制度有关的事物或特征。它通常带有历史的、过时的意味，暗示着一种等级制度和权力不平等。  在现代语境中，它更多地用于描述建筑风格、社会结构或制度的某些特征，而非直接指代现存的庄园主或领地。


PHRASE->

There are no common phrases directly using "seignioral".  The word itself is relatively uncommon in modern English.
