servings:/ˈsɜːrvɪŋz/| n.|份；份量；供应量|The number of portions a recipe yields or the amount of food served at one time.  It can also refer to the act of serving food.|例句：This recipe makes four servings. 这个食谱可以做四份。

例句：The restaurant offers generous servings of their pasta dishes. 这家餐馆供应分量很足的意大利面食。


近义词：portions, helpings, allotments

反义词：None directly opposite, but "small portions" could be considered a relative antonym.

词性:名词
