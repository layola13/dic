discourse:/ˈdɪsˌkɔːrs/| n. v.|话语；论述；谈话；演讲|

英文解析:Communication of thought or feeling.  It can refer to spoken or written communication, and often implies a more formal or extended discussion than simply conversation.  It can also refer to the way language is used in a particular context, often with social or political implications.


例句:

The professor's discourse on the history of philosophy was fascinating.  教授关于哲学史的论述非常引人入胜。

His public discourse was often marked by inflammatory rhetoric. 他的公开演讲常常带有煽动性言辞。


近义词:conversation, discussion, speech, lecture, address, communication, talk, sermon


反义词:silence, muteness


词性:Noun and Verb
