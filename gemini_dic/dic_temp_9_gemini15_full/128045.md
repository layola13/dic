nerf:/nɜːrf/| n. vt. |削弱；使无效；使减弱；(玩具枪)软弹枪 |to reduce the effectiveness of something; a toy gun that shoots foam darts|  The new law nerfs the power of the police. 新法律削弱了警方的权力。  They nerfed the game's difficulty. 他们降低了游戏的难度。 |近义词：weaken, diminish, impair |反义词：strengthen, enhance, amplify |名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nerfs the enemy's attack. = 他削弱了敌人的攻击。
The company nerfs the price of its product. = 公司降低了其产品的价格。


一般过去时:
They nerfed the overpowered weapon. = 他们削弱了过于强大的武器。
The update nerfed the character's abilities. = 更新削弱了角色的能力。


现在完成时:
The developers have nerfed several bugs. = 开发人员已经修复了几个错误。(此处nerf指修复，引申义)
They have nerfed the game's economy. = 他们已经削弱了游戏的经济系统。


过去完成时:
They had nerfed the boss before the players arrived. = 在玩家到达之前，他们已经削弱了Boss。
The company had nerfed the marketing campaign before it launched. = 公司在营销活动启动之前就已经削弱了它。


将来时:
They will nerf the most powerful spells. = 他们将会削弱最强大的法术。
The government will nerf the tax incentives. = 政府将会削弱税收优惠。


虚拟语气:
If they nerfed the character, the game would be more balanced. = 如果他们削弱这个角色，游戏就会更平衡。
I wish they had nerfed the overpowered weapon earlier. = 我希望他们早点削弱那个过于强大的武器。


被动语态:
The character's abilities were nerfed in the last update. = 角色的能力在上次更新中被削弱了。
The game's economy was nerfed to prevent inflation. = 游戏的经济系统被削弱以防止通货膨胀。


疑问句:
Will they nerf the new hero? = 他们会削弱新英雄吗？
Have they nerfed the game's difficulty yet? = 他们已经降低游戏的难度了吗？


否定句:
They didn't nerf the boss enough. = 他们没有足够削弱Boss。
The update didn't nerf the overpowered weapon. = 更新没有削弱过于强大的武器。


PHRASE->
nerf the economy = 削弱经济
nerf the power = 削弱权力
nerf the character = 削弱角色
nerf the game = 削弱游戏
nerf the effect = 削弱效果
