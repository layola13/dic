preponing:/prɪˈpoʊnɪŋ/| n. |提前；预先安排|The act of moving something to an earlier time or position.  It often refers to scheduling or prioritizing tasks or events earlier than originally planned.|例句：The preponing of the meeting caused some inconvenience.  会议提前举行造成了一些不便。|近义词：advancement, acceleration, prioritization|反义词：postponement, delay
