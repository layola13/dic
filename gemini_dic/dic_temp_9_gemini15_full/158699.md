plotting:/ˈplɒtɪŋ/| v.|策划；密谋；绘图；标绘|The act of making or developing a plan, especially a secret one; the act of marking on a chart or map.  It can also refer to the act of creating a plot in a story.|例句：They were plotting to overthrow the government. 他们正密谋推翻政府。|例句：The navigator carefully plotted the ship's course on the chart. 导航员仔细地在海图上标绘船的航线。|近义词：scheming, planning, plotting out, charting|反义词：unplanning, improvising
