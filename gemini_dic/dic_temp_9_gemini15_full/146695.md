overheated:/ˌoʊvərˈhiːtɪd/| adj.|过热的，过度的|Excessively heated; too hot.  The term can also be used figuratively to describe something that is excessively intense or passionate.|The engine overheated and stalled. 发动机过热并熄火了。The debate overheated quickly, with insults flying between participants.  这场辩论迅速升温，参与者之间互相辱骂。|近义词：superheated, scorching, fervent|反义词：cool, underheated


USAGE->

The engine overheats frequently. = 发动机经常过热。
This room overheats in the summer. = 这个房间夏天太热了。

The computer overheated and shut down. = 电脑过热并关机了。
