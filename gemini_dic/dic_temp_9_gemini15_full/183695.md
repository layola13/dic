reroutes:/ˌriːˈraʊts/| vt.|重新规划路线，改道|To change the route or course of something, typically traffic or a journey.|例句：The traffic was rerouted due to the accident. 交通事故导致交通改道。|例句：The airline rerouted the flight to avoid the storm. 航空公司为了避开风暴而改变了航班路线。|近义词：redirect, divert, deviate|反义词：maintain the original route
