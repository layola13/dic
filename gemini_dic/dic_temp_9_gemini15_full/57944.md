drinks:/drɪŋks/| n.|饮料；酒水|Liquid substances intended for drinking, often alcoholic beverages.|例句：The bar offers a wide variety of drinks. 这家酒吧提供各种各样的饮料。
例句：He ordered a round of drinks for everyone. 他为每个人点了一轮酒。|近义词：beverages, refreshments|反义词：food, solids


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drinks coffee every morning. = 他每天早上喝咖啡。
She drinks tea with milk. = 她喝加牛奶的茶。

一般过去时:
I drank too much wine last night. = 我昨晚喝了太多酒。
They drank all the beer. = 他们喝光了所有的啤酒。

现在完成时:
I have drunk three glasses of water. = 我已经喝了三杯水。
She has drunk all the juice. = 她已经喝完了所有的果汁。

过去完成时:
He had drunk the entire bottle before I arrived. = 我到之前他已经喝完了一整瓶。
They had drunk several cocktails. = 他们已经喝了几杯鸡尾酒。

将来时:
I will drink more water tomorrow. = 我明天会多喝水。
We will drink champagne at the celebration. = 我们将在庆祝活动上喝香槟。

虚拟语气:
If I were thirsty, I would drink some water. = 如果我渴了，我会喝些水。
I wish I had drunk less wine last night. = 我希望昨晚少喝点酒。

被动语态:
The drinks were served quickly. = 饮料很快就上来了。
The water is being drunk quickly. = 水很快就被喝光了。

疑问句:
What drinks are you having? = 你要喝什么饮料？
Do you drink alcohol? = 你喝酒吗？

否定句:
I don't drink coffee. = 我不喝咖啡。
He didn't drink anything at all. = 他什么也没喝。


PHRASE->

mixed drinks = 鸡尾酒
