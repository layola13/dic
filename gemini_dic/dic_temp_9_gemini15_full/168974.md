profiteroles:/ˌprɒfɪˈtɛərlz/ | n. |泡芙；小油炸面团 |Small, hollow, cream-filled pastries.  They are typically made from choux pastry, which is a light and airy dough made with flour, butter, eggs, and water.  The pastry is piped into small balls, baked until golden brown and crisp, and then filled with a variety of sweet or savory fillings, such as cream, custard, ice cream, or cheese. | The profiteroles were a delightful end to the meal.  那些泡芙是这顿饭令人愉快的结尾。|  cream puffs, éclairs |  (no direct antonym) | 名词
