coriaceous:/ˌkɔːriˈeɪʃəs/| adj. |革质的；坚韧的；似皮革的 |Having a tough, leathery texture.  The word refers to a material that is tough, thick, and somewhat rigid, like leather.  It often describes plant parts or animal skin. |例句：The leaves of the plant were coriaceous and difficult to tear.  这植物的叶子很坚韧，很难撕裂。

The old leather boots were coriaceous and showed signs of wear. 这双旧皮靴很坚硬，并显示出磨损的迹象。|近义词：leathery, tough, thick, rigid |反义词：soft, delicate, tender
