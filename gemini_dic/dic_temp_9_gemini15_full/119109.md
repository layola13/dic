metromaniac: /ˌmɛtrəˈmeɪniæk/ | n. | 大都市狂热者，大城市迷 | A person who is excessively enthusiastic about or obsessed with metropolitan areas and their culture.  They often idealize city life and may feel alienated or uncomfortable in rural or suburban settings. | Example:  He's a complete metromaniac; he wouldn't dream of living anywhere outside of Manhattan. 他是个彻头彻尾的大都市狂热者；他做梦也不会想到住在曼哈顿以外的地方。| 近义词：urbanite, city-lover | 反义词：ruralist, suburbanite | 名词
