rambler:/ˈræmblər/| n. v.|闲逛者；漫步者；攀缘植物；（尤指）长篇累牍的说话者；|A rambler is a person who walks for pleasure, especially in the countryside.  It can also refer to a rambling rose, a type of climbing rose, or someone who talks at length and in a disorganized way.|例句：He's a keen rambler and enjoys exploring the local countryside. 他是一位热衷远足的人，喜欢探索当地乡村。  She's a bit of a rambler when she gets started on a topic. 她一旦开始谈论一个话题，就会滔滔不绝。|近义词：wanderer, stroller, rambler rose, proser|反义词：stay-at-home, concise speaker|名词，动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rambles through the woods every Sunday. = 他每个星期天都在树林里漫步。
She rambles on and on about her trip. = 她没完没了地谈论她的旅行。

一般过去时:
They rambled across the fields. = 他们漫步穿过田野。
He rambled for hours about his childhood. = 他滔滔不绝地谈论了他童年的事情好几个小时。

现在完成时:
We have rambled through most of the park. = 我们已经漫步穿过大部分公园了。
She has rambled on for so long, I've lost track of what she's saying. = 她已经说了这么久了，我都不知道她在说什么了。

过去完成时:
They had rambled for miles before they found a place to rest. = 他们在找到休息的地方之前已经走了几英里了。
He had rambled so much that everyone was tired of listening. = 他说得太多了，以至于大家都听烦了。

将来时:
We will ramble along the coast tomorrow. = 我们明天将在海岸边漫步。
She will ramble on about politics all evening. = 她整个晚上都会没完没了地谈论政治。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would ramble through the mountains. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会在山里漫步。
If he hadn't rambled so much, we would have finished the meeting earlier. = 如果他没说那么多，我们就能早点结束会议了。

被动语态:
The path is often rambled on by tourists. = 这条路经常被游客走。
The topic was rambled over extensively in the meeting. = 会议上对这个话题进行了广泛的讨论。


疑问句:
Do you often ramble in the countryside? = 你经常在乡下漫步吗？
Does he often ramble on about his work? = 他经常没完没了地谈论他的工作吗？

否定句:
They did not ramble far from the campsite. = 他们没有远离营地太远。
She did not ramble on, instead she spoke concisely. = 她没有长篇大论，而是简洁地说了。
