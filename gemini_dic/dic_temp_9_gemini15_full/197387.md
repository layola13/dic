sheathers:/ˈʃiːðərz/| n.|鞘，剑鞘；保护套|A sheath is a close-fitting covering, often for a blade or other object.  The plural "sheathers" refers to multiple sheaths or, less commonly, to people who make or use sheaths.|例句：The swords were carefully placed back in their sheathers.  宝剑被小心地放回了剑鞘。

The antique shop displayed a variety of sheathers for different types of knives. 古董店展示了各种类型的刀鞘。|近义词：cases, scabbards, coverings|反义词：None directly applicable.
