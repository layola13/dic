infiniteness:/ɪnˈfɪnɪtnəs/ | n. |无限性，无穷性 |The state or quality of being infinite; boundlessness.  It refers to something without limits or end, either in space, time, or quantity. |例句：The infiniteness of the universe is a concept that has captivated philosophers and scientists for centuries.  宇宙的无限性是一个几个世纪以来一直吸引着哲学家和科学家们的概念。

例句：The infiniteness of possibilities can be both exciting and overwhelming.  无限的可能性既令人兴奋又令人不知所措。


近义词：boundlessness,limitlessness, immensity, eternity

反义词：finiteness, limitation, restriction


词性：名词
