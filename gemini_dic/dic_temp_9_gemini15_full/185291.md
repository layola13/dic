retreated:/rɪˈtriːtɪd/ | v. |撤退，退却，退缩 | to move back or away from a place or situation, especially because of danger or fear; to withdraw from something previously held, such as a position or claim. |例句：The army retreated after suffering heavy losses.  军队在遭受重大损失后撤退了。

The soldiers retreated to their base.士兵们退回到了他们的基地。


近义词：withdraw, recede, fall back, pull back | 反义词：advance, proceed, progress, approach | 词性: Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army retreats when faced with superior forces.  军队在面对强大的敌人时撤退。
He retreats into his shell when he's upset. 他生气时会把自己封闭起来。


一般过去时:
The soldiers retreated from the battlefield. 士兵们从战场上撤退了。
She retreated from the argument. 她退出了争论。


现在完成时:
The enemy has retreated across the border. 敌人已经越过边境撤退了。
They have retreated from their earlier position. 他们已经放弃了他们之前的立场。


过去完成时:
The troops had retreated before the enemy arrived.  敌军到达之前，部队已经撤退了。
She had retreated into silence after the harsh criticism. 遭到严厉批评后，她沉默不语。


将来时:
The army will retreat if they are outnumbered. 如果敌众我寡，军队将撤退。
He will retreat from public life after the scandal. 丑闻过后，他将退出公众生活。


虚拟语气:
If the enemy attacked, we would retreat. 如果敌人进攻，我们将撤退。
I wish I had retreated sooner. 我真希望我早点撤退。


被动语态:
The city was retreated from by the invading army.  入侵的军队从城市撤退了。(被动语态较少用在此词上)
The position was retreated from after the heavy losses. 遭受重大损失后，这个阵地被放弃了。(被动语态较少用在此词上)


疑问句:

否定句:
The army did not retreat. 军队没有撤退。
He refused to retreat from his position. 他拒绝放弃他的立场。
