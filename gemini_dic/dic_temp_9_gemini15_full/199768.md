sirocco:/sɪˈrɒkoʊ/ | n. |西洛可风，热风 |A sirocco is a hot, dry wind that blows from North Africa across the Mediterranean Sea to southern Europe.  It is often associated with dust and sand, and can cause significant discomfort and even health problems. | Example: The sirocco brought with it a wave of oppressive heat and sand. 西洛可风带来了一股令人窒息的热浪和沙尘。 |近义词：hot wind, desert wind | 反义词：cold wind, refreshing breeze |名词
