globus:/ˈgloʊbəs/| n.|地球仪；球体；球状物|A globe is a spherical representation of the Earth, showing its geographical features.  It can also refer to a sphere or ball-like object.|例句：The teacher used a globus to show the students the location of different countries.老师用地球仪向学生展示不同国家的地理位置。

例句：The architect designed a building with a globus-shaped dome. 建筑师设计了一座带有球形圆顶的建筑物。


近义词：sphere, orb, ball|反义词：flat, plane


词源:  From Latin *globus, meaning "sphere, ball".  The word's journey through language reflects its ancient association with round objects and the celestial sphere.
