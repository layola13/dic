liberticide:/ˈlɪbərtiˌsaɪd/| n. | 摧毁自由；压制自由；剥夺自由的行为 | The systematic destruction of liberty; the suppression of freedom.  It often refers to the deliberate and often violent actions taken to eliminate or severely curtail individual or collective freedoms.  | Example: The government's actions were seen as an act of liberticide, silencing dissent and suppressing basic human rights.  政府的行为被视为摧毁自由的行为，压制异见和基本人权。|近义词：tyranny, oppression, repression, suppression |反义词：liberation, emancipation, enfranchisement |名词
