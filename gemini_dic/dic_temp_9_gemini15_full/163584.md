precultural: /ˌpriːˈkʌltʃərəl/ | adj. | 前文化的，文化之前的 |  Relating to the period or state before the development of a particular culture or civilization; existing before a culture has developed.  Describes aspects of human behavior, social organization, or belief systems that existed before the emergence of distinct cultures. |


例句：

The archaeologist studied precultural tools found in the cave. = 考古学家研究了在洞穴中发现的史前工具。

Some scholars believe that certain aspects of human language may have precultural origins. = 一些学者认为人类语言的某些方面可能起源于文化出现之前。


近义词：protocultural, pre-civilizational, primordial


反义词：cultural, post-cultural


词性：形容词
