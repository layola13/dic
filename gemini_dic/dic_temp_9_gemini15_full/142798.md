our:/ˈaʊər/ | pron. | 我们的 |  Our possessive pronoun, indicating belonging to or association with the speaker and one or more other people. It functions as a determiner before nouns and as a pronoun on its own.  | Example: Our house is quite small. 我们的房子很小。 We painted our bedroom blue. 我们把卧室漆成了蓝色。|近义词:  belonging to us | 反义词: their, your, his, her, its


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Our team wins most games. = 我们的团队赢得了大部分比赛。
Our dog barks at strangers. = 我们的狗对陌生人狂吠。


一般过去时:
Our vacation was amazing. = 我们的假期太棒了。
Our friends visited us last week. = 我们的朋友上周来看我们了。


现在完成时:
Our company has grown significantly. = 我们的公司有了显著的增长。
We have finished our project. = 我们已经完成了我们的项目。


过去完成时:
Our work had already been completed before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，我们的工作已经完成了。
We had lost our keys before we found them. = 我们在找到钥匙之前已经把钥匙弄丢了。


将来时:
Our trip will start next month. = 我们的旅行将在下个月开始。
We will celebrate our anniversary next week. = 我们下周将庆祝我们的周年纪念日。


虚拟语气:
If our plan were successful, we would be very happy. = 如果我们的计划成功了，我们会非常高兴。
I wish our team had won the game. = 我希望我们的团队赢得了比赛。


被动语态:
Our proposal was rejected. = 我们的提案被拒绝了。
Our car is being repaired. = 我们的车正在修理中。


疑问句:
Is our presentation ready? = 我们的演示文稿准备好了吗？
Are our supplies sufficient? = 我们的物资充足吗？


否定句:
Our efforts were not in vain. = 我们的努力并非徒劳。
Our plans did not work out. = 我们的计划没有成功。
