feigner:/ˈfeɪnə(r)/| vt.|伪装，假装；捏造|to pretend to have or feel something; to invent something false|He feigned illness to avoid work. 他假装生病以逃避工作。|She feigned surprise when she received the gift. 她收到礼物时假装很惊讶。|simulate, pretend, affect|be genuine, be truthful|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He feigns ignorance. = 他假装不知道。
She feigns interest in his work. = 她假装对他的工作感兴趣。

一般过去时:
He feigned sleep to avoid the conversation. = 他假装睡觉以避免谈话。
She feigned a headache to get out of the meeting. = 她假装头痛以逃避会议。

现在完成时:
He has feigned illness several times this year. = 他今年已经几次假装生病了。
She has feigned indifference to his advances. = 她一直假装对他示好漠不关心。

过去完成时:
He had feigned surprise when he saw the gift. = 他看到礼物时假装很惊讶。
She had feigned ignorance throughout the investigation. = 在整个调查过程中，她一直假装不知情。

将来时:
He will feign illness to avoid the upcoming exam. = 他将假装生病以逃避即将到来的考试。
She will feign disinterest in the project to avoid being assigned to it. = 她将假装对该项目不感兴趣以避免被分配到该项目。

虚拟语气:
If he feigned illness, he would avoid the punishment. = 如果他假装生病，他就能避免惩罚。
If she feigned ignorance, she would escape questioning. = 如果她假装不知情，她就能避免被盘问。

被动语态:
Illness was feigned by him to skip the meeting. = 他假装生病以逃避会议。（被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用）
Indifference was feigned by her to reject his proposal. = 她假装冷漠来拒绝他的求婚。（被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用）

疑问句:
Did he feign illness to escape responsibility? = 他是否假装生病以逃避责任？
Does she feign interest in the conversation? = 她是否对谈话表现出兴趣？

否定句:
He did not feign illness; he was truly sick. = 他并没有假装生病；他真的病了。
She did not feign surprise; she was genuinely shocked. = 她并没有假装惊讶；她确实很震惊。
