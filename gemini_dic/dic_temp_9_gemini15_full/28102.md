capitalism: /ˈkæpɪtəlɪzəm/ | n. |资本主义 |An economic and political system in which a country's trade and industry are controlled by private owners for profit, rather than by the state.  |例句：The rise of capitalism led to significant economic growth but also increased inequality. 资本主义的兴起导致了显著的经济增长，但也加剧了不平等。|例句：Many argue that pure capitalism leads to exploitation of workers.  许多人认为纯粹的资本主义会导致对工人的剥削。|近义词：free market economy, market economy |反义词：socialism, communism |名词
