cardinalist:/ˈkɑːrdɪnəlɪst/| n. |枢机主教的支持者；红衣主教的支持者|A cardinalist is a supporter of a cardinal or the cardinal system within a church, often the Roman Catholic Church.  The term often implies strong allegiance and advocacy for the cardinal's policies or views.  |Example:  Many cardinalists rallied behind Cardinal Richelieu during his time as chief minister. 许多枢机主教的支持者在其担任首席大臣期间都支持黎塞留红衣主教。|近义词：supporter, adherent, advocate |反义词：opponent, adversary
