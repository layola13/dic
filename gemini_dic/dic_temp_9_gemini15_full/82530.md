guacho:/ˈɡwatʃoʊ/| n.| 高乔人（南美的一种牛仔）|A gaucho is a South American cowboy, particularly from Argentina, Uruguay, and southern Brazil. They are known for their horsemanship and skills in cattle herding.  |Example: The gaucho skillfully lassoed the wild horse.  高乔人熟练地套住了野马。 |近义词：cowboy, gaucheron (French), llanero (Venezuelan)|反义词：N/A
