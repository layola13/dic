hyperscrupulous:/ˌhaɪpərˈskruːpjʊləs/| adj. |过分谨慎的；过于细心的；吹毛求疵的 |Extremely careful and precise; excessively conscientious.  It implies a level of caution that is excessive and potentially counterproductive.|The hyperscrupulous accountant checked every entry three times, delaying the audit by a week.|近义词：meticulous, painstaking, scrupulous, fastidious|反义词：careless, negligent, lax
