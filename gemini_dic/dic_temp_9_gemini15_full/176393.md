re-granting: /ˌriːˈɡræntɪŋ/ | v. | 再授予，重新授予 | The act of granting something again, especially a right, privilege, or license.  It often implies a previous grant that has been revoked, expired, or needs renewal. |例句：The university is considering re-granting the research funding after reviewing the revised proposal. 大学正在考虑在审查修改后的提案后重新拨款研究经费。

The government is re-granting licenses to those who previously had them revoked due to technicalities. 政府正在向那些由于技术原因而被吊销执照的人重新颁发执照。


近义词：reissuing, renewing, reinstating | 反义词：revoking, cancelling, rescinding | 词性：动词
