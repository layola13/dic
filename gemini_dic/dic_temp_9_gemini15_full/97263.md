一般现在时:
He inflicts pain on others. 他使别人痛苦。
The government inflicts new taxes on its citizens. 政府向公民征收新税。

一般过去时:
The accident inflicted serious injuries on the driver. 事故使司机受了重伤。
The war inflicted immense suffering on the population. 战争给人民带来了巨大的痛苦。

现在完成时:
The drought has inflicted severe damage on the crops. 干旱对庄稼造成了严重的损害。
Years of neglect have inflicted irreparable damage on the building. 多年的忽视使建筑物遭受了无法弥补的破坏。

过去完成时:
Before the storm hit, it had inflicted minor damage to some houses. 在暴风雨来临之前，它已经对一些房屋造成了一些轻微的损坏。
They had inflicted a heavy defeat on their opponents. 他们已经给对手带来了惨重的失败。

将来时:
The new policy will inflict hardship on low-income families. 新政策将使低收入家庭陷入困境。
The coming storm is expected to inflict significant damage on the city.  即将到来的风暴预计会给城市造成重大损失。

虚拟语气:
If the company continued this practice, it would inflict serious damage to its reputation. 如果公司继续这种做法，将会严重损害其声誉。
I wish they hadn't inflicted such pain on me. 我希望他们没有给我带来如此痛苦。

被动语态:
Heavy damage was inflicted on the city by the hurricane.  飓风给城市造成了巨大的破坏。
Great suffering was inflicted upon the people by the famine. 饥荒给人民带来了巨大的痛苦。

疑问句:
What kind of damage did the fire inflict? 火灾造成了什么样的损失？
What suffering does this policy inflict on the poor? 这一政策给穷人带来了什么痛苦？

否定句:
The rain didn't inflict any significant damage to the crops. 雨水没有对庄稼造成任何重大损失。
The treatment didn't inflict any side effects on him.  治疗没有给他带来任何副作用。
