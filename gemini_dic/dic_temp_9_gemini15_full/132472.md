nonderivative:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈrɪvətɪv/| adj. |非衍生的；原创的；独立的 |Not derived from something else; original.  It emphasizes the independent creation or existence of something, without being based on or copied from a pre-existing source. |The artist's work is entirely nonderivative, showcasing a unique style.  这位艺术家的作品完全是原创的，展示了一种独特的风格。|original, independent, unique, innovative |derivative, copied, imitative, unoriginal
