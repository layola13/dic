ladle:/ˈleɪdl/| n. v.|勺子；盛（汤等）|A ladle is a large deep spoon with a long handle, used for serving soup, stew, or other liquids.|例句：She ladled the soup into bowls. 她用勺子把汤盛进碗里。|近义词：spoon, dipper|反义词:N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ladles the soup carefully. = 他小心地舀汤。
She ladles out the gravy. = 她舀出肉汁。


一般过去时:
The chef ladled the stew into serving dishes. = 厨师把炖菜舀进盛菜的盘子里。
He ladled a generous portion onto my plate. = 他在我的盘子里舀了一大份。


现在完成时:
She has ladled out all the soup. = 她已经把所有的汤都舀出来了。
They have ladled the sauce over the pasta. = 他们已经把酱汁淋在意大利面上。


过去完成时:
He had ladled the broth before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经舀好了肉汤。
She had ladled out several servings of pudding. = 她已经舀了几份布丁。


将来时:
I will ladle the punch into glasses. = 我将把宾治酒舀进杯子里。
She will ladle the chili into bowls for everyone. = 她将把辣椒舀进碗里给每个人。


虚拟语气:
If I had a ladle, I would ladle the soup myself. = 如果我有一个勺子，我会自己舀汤。
I wish I had ladled more gravy onto my mashed potatoes. = 我真希望我把更多的肉汁浇在我的土豆泥上。


被动语态:
The soup was ladled into bowls by the waitress. = 汤由女服务员舀进碗里。
The gravy was ladled generously over the roast beef. = 肉汁慷慨地浇在烤牛肉上。


疑问句:
Will you ladle the soup for me? = 你会帮我舀汤吗？
Did he ladle the sauce evenly over the chicken? = 他是否均匀地把酱汁浇在鸡肉上？


否定句:
I didn't ladle enough sauce onto my pasta. = 我没有在我的意大利面上浇足够的酱汁。
She doesn't ladle the soup very carefully. = 她舀汤不太小心。
