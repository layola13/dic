reversibles:/rɪˈvɜːrsəbəlz/ | adj. | 可逆的；可翻转的 | Capable of being reversed or turned inside out.  Something that is reversible can be changed back to its original state or condition, or can be turned inside out. | Example: These jackets are reversible, so you can wear them two different ways. 这件夹克是可翻转的，所以你可以两种不同的方式穿着它。|近义词：invertible, reversible, returnable | 反义词：irreversible, permanent
