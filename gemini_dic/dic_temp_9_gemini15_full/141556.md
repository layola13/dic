opposes:/əˈpoʊzɪz/| v. |反对，抵制|To be against something; to disagree with something and try to stop it.  |例句：He opposes the new law. 他反对这项新法律。|例句：Many people oppose the war. 许多人反对战争。|近义词：resist, object to, protest against, fight against|反义词：support, endorse, favor, advocate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He opposes the government's policies. = 他反对政府的政策。
She opposes the construction of the new highway. = 她反对修建新的高速公路。


一般过去时:
They opposed the changes to the company's structure. = 他们反对公司结构的变动。
He opposed his father's decision to sell the farm. = 他反对父亲卖掉农场的决定。


现在完成时:
She has opposed this project since its inception. = 自从这个项目开始以来，她就一直反对它。
They have opposed the bill for many years. = 他们多年来一直反对这项法案。


过去完成时:
He had opposed the plan before it was even presented. = 在计划提出之前，他就已经反对了。
They had opposed the merger for months before it finally collapsed. = 在合并最终失败之前，他们已经反对了几个月。


将来时:
We will oppose any attempt to reduce funding for education. = 我们将反对任何削减教育经费的企图。
He believes he will oppose the candidate in the upcoming election. = 他相信他将在即将到来的选举中反对这位候选人。


虚拟语气:
If he opposed the bill, he would have made a statement. = 如果他反对该法案，他会发表声明。
I wish he opposed the development plans. = 我希望他反对发展计划。


被动语态:
The proposal was opposed by many members of the committee. = 该提案遭到委员会许多成员的反对。
The new policy is being opposed by several interest groups. = 新政策遭到几个利益集团的反对。


疑问句:
Does he oppose the new regulations? = 他反对新的规章制度吗？
Do they oppose the government's decision? = 他们反对政府的决定吗？


否定句:
He does not oppose the idea. = 他并不反对这个主意。
They did not oppose the plan at first. = 他们起初并不反对这个计划。


PHRASE->
oppose forcefully = 强烈反对
oppose vehemently = 猛烈反对
oppose strongly = 强烈反对
oppose openly = 公开反对
oppose silently = 暗中反对
