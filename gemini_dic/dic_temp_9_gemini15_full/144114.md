outscooping:/ˌaʊtˈskuːpɪŋ/| v.|超过，胜过（尤指在新闻报道方面）|To surpass or exceed someone, especially in obtaining or reporting news.  It implies a competitive aspect, often in journalism or similar fields where getting information first is crucial.|例句：The rival newspaper completely outscooped them on the story.  竞争对手的报纸在报道这条新闻上完全超过了他们。

例句：Our team outscooped the competition by getting the exclusive interview.  我们的团队通过获得独家采访而胜过了竞争对手。


近义词：outdo, surpass, outstrip, beat, scoop


反义词：be scooped by, be outdone by, be surpassed by


词性:动词
