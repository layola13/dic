defoliating:/diːˈfoʊliˌeɪtɪŋ/| v. |落叶的；使落叶的 |The act of removing leaves from plants, trees, or other vegetation. This can be a natural process or caused by environmental factors, disease, or human intervention.  |例句：The trees were defoliating in autumn. 秋天树叶开始脱落。The herbicide caused extensive defoliating of the crops. 除草剂导致农作物大量落叶。|近义词：leaf-shedding,  defoliation (n.)|反义词：leafing,  foliation (n.)
