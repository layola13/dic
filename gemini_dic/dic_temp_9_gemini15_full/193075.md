sectarianly: /sekˈtɛəriənli/ | adv. |宗派地，狭隘地|In a sectarian manner; showing prejudice or favoritism based on religious or political affiliations.  It implies narrow-mindedness and intolerance towards those outside one's own group.|例句：The issue was debated sectarianly, with little regard for the broader implications.|近义词：parochially, narrowly, bigotedly |反义词：broadly, inclusively, impartially
