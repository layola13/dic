kaolins:/ˈkeɪəʊlɪnz/| n. |高岭土（复数）|A naturally occurring soft white clay, a hydrated aluminum silicate, used in the manufacture of porcelain, paper, and other products.  It is named after the hill of Kao-ling in China where it was first discovered.|例句：The ceramic artist used high-quality kaolins in her sculptures.  这位陶瓷艺术家在她的雕塑中使用了优质的高岭土。

The porcelain factory sources its kaolins from several different mines. 这家瓷器厂从几个不同的矿山采购高岭土。|近义词：clay, china clay|反义词：无
