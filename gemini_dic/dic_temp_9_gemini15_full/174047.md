quasi-vitally:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈvaɪtəli/| adj.|准重要的；似重要的；近乎重要的 |  Describes something that is almost or nearly vital, essential, or crucial, but not completely so. It suggests a degree of importance that falls short of absolute necessity. |例句：The timely delivery of the components was quasi-vitally important to the project's success. 及时交付组件对项目的成功至关重要。

The quasi-vitally important research could lead to a breakthrough. 这项近乎重要的研究可能会带来突破。|近义词：essential, crucial, important, vital, critical|反义词：unimportant, trivial, inconsequential
