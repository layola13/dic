denials:/dɪˈnaɪəl/| n.|否认；拒绝；否定|The act of denying something; a statement that something is not true or not the case.  It can also refer to the refusal of a request or demand. |例句：His denials of guilt were unconvincing. 他否认有罪的说法令人难以信服。

例句：The insurance company issued a denial of her claim. 保险公司拒绝了她的索赔。


近义词：refusals, rejections, contradictions, disavowals

反义词：admissions, confirmations, acceptances


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His denials are frequent. = 他的否认很频繁。
Their denials of responsibility are unacceptable. = 他们否认责任的说法是不可接受的。


一般过去时:
His denials were unconvincing. = 他的否认令人难以信服。
The court rejected his denials. = 法院驳回了他的否认。


现在完成时:
The government has issued several denials of the allegations. = 政府已经多次否认这些指控。
She has consistently offered denials of any wrongdoing. = 她一直否认有任何不当行为。


过去完成时:
Before the trial, he had made several denials of involvement. = 在审判之前，他曾多次否认参与其中。
They had repeatedly issued denials, but nobody believed them. = 他们曾反复否认，但没有人相信他们。


将来时:
He will likely issue further denials. = 他可能会进一步否认。
The company will continue to issue denials until the evidence is irrefutable. = 在证据无可辩驳之前，该公司将继续否认。


虚拟语气:
If his denials were true, he wouldn't be in this situation. = 如果他的否认是真的，他不会身处这种情况。
I wish his denials were more credible. = 我希望他的否认更有说服力。


被动语态:
His denials were dismissed by the judge. = 他的否认被法官驳回。
The accusations were met with immediate denials. = 指控遭到了立即的否认。


疑问句:
What were his denials based on? = 他的否认是基于什么的？
Are his repeated denials believable? = 他反复的否认可信吗？


否定句:
His denials were not credible. = 他的否认不可信。
There were no denials of his culpability. = 没有否认他的罪责。


PHRASE->

in denial = 否认现实
