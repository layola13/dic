quizzing:/ˈkwɪzɪŋ/| v.|测验；问问题；盘问|The act of asking questions to test someone's knowledge or understanding, or the act of being tested in this way.  It can also imply a less formal or more playful testing than a formal examination.|例句：The teacher is quizzing the students on their vocabulary.老师正在测验学生们的词汇量。

例句：He was quizzing her about her whereabouts last night. 他在盘问她昨晚的行踪。


近义词：testing, questioning, interrogating|反义词：instructing, teaching


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is quizzing the students on their grammar. 他正在考学生们的语法。
She quizzes her children on their times tables every night. 她每天晚上都会考她的孩子们乘法表。


一般过去时:
The professor quizzed the students on the material from the previous lecture. 教授考了学生们上一节课的内容。
They quizzed me about my trip to Europe. 他们问我关于我去欧洲旅行的事。


现在完成时:
The teacher has quizzed the class on three chapters already. 老师已经考了班级三个章节了。
I have been quizzing myself on the material for the exam. 我一直在自测考试内容。


过去完成时:
She had quizzed him thoroughly before he went on stage. 在他上台之前，她已经彻底盘问了他。
We had quizzed the witnesses before the police arrived. 在警察到达之前，我们已经盘问了证人。


将来时:
He will quiz the students on the new material next week. 他下周将考学生们新内容。
The instructor will be quizzing us on the upcoming exam. 讲师将考我们即将到来的考试。


虚拟语气:
If I were better prepared, I wouldn't be so nervous about the quizzing. 如果我准备得更好，我就不会对测验那么紧张了。
I wish I had been quizzed more often during the semester. 我希望学期中能更经常地被测验。


被动语态:
The students were quizzed on the assigned reading. 学生们被考了指定的阅读材料。
The suspect is being quizzed by the police. 嫌疑人正在接受警方的盘问。


疑问句:
Are you quizzing me? 你在考我吗？
Will you be quizzing us tomorrow? 你明天会考我们吗？


否定句:
He wasn't quizzing the students at that moment. 那时候他并没有在考学生们。
She doesn't quiz her students very often. 她不经常考她的学生。
