hijacked:/ˈhaɪdʒækt/| v.|劫持；篡夺；盗用|To seize (a vehicle, especially an aircraft or ship) by force, typically with a weapon, and demand ransom or other concessions.  To take control of something, typically illegally or by force.|例句：The plane was hijacked by terrorists. 这架飞机被恐怖分子劫持了。

The meeting was hijacked by a disruptive participant.  会议被一个捣乱的参与者打乱了。|近义词：seize, commandeer, capture, usurp|反义词：release, relinquish, surrender


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The terrorists hijack planes. = 恐怖分子劫持飞机。
He often hijacks the conversation. = 他经常打断谈话。


一般过去时:
They hijacked the bus yesterday. = 他们昨天劫持了公共汽车。
She hijacked the project's direction. = 她篡夺了项目的领导权。


现在完成时:
They have hijacked several vehicles this year. = 今年他们已经劫持了几辆车。
He has hijacked the company's resources for his own gain. = 他盗用了公司的资源来谋取私利。


过去完成时:
The passengers had already been hijacked before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，乘客已经被劫持了。
The hackers had hijacked the website before security measures were implemented. = 黑客在安全措施实施之前就已入侵了网站。


将来时:
They will hijack the train tomorrow. = 他们明天将劫持火车。
She will hijack the discussion at the meeting. = 她将在会议上打断讨论。


虚拟语气:
If they hijacked the plane, they would demand a ransom. = 如果他们劫持了飞机，他们会勒索赎金。
I wish they hadn't hijacked the conversation. = 我希望他们没有打断谈话。


被动语态:
The plane was hijacked. = 飞机被劫持了。
The meeting was hijacked by a rival company. = 会议被竞争对手公司所操纵。


疑问句:
Was the car hijacked? = 车子被劫持了吗？
Has the system been hijacked? = 系统被劫持了吗？


否定句:
They didn't hijack the train. = 他们没有劫持火车。
The project wasn't hijacked by anyone. = 项目没有被任何人篡夺。
