over-drives:/ˌoʊvərˈdraɪvz/ | v. |超速行驶；使超负荷运转；过度操纵|To drive a vehicle faster than the speed limit or at a speed considered unsafe.  To force something to operate beyond its normal capacity, often leading to damage or failure. To exert excessive control or influence over something.|例句：He over-drives his car frequently, risking accidents. 他经常超速驾驶，冒险发生事故。

He over-drives his employees, leading to burnout. 他过度压榨员工，导致他们筋疲力尽。|近义词：speed, accelerate, overload, overwork|反义词：underdrive, slow down, underwork
