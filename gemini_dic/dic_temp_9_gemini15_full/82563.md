guaranteeing:/ˌɡærənˈtiːɪŋ/| v. |保证；担保|The act of making something certain or sure to happen.  It implies a promise or assurance of a particular outcome or quality.  The word often involves a promise to compensate for failure.|例句：The company is guaranteeing a full refund if you are not satisfied. 公司保证如果您不满意，将全额退款。
The warranty guarantees the product for one year. 保修单保证产品一年保修。


近义词：ensuring, assuring, securing, promising|反义词：jeopardizing, risking, endangering

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is guaranteeing the success of the project. 他保证项目成功。
The bank guarantees the loan. 银行担保贷款。


一般过去时:
They guaranteed the quality of their products. 他们保证了产品的质量。
She guaranteed her friend's honesty. 她担保她朋友的诚实。


现在完成时:
We have guaranteed the safety of our customers. 我们已经保证了客户的安全。
The government has guaranteed the payment. 政府已担保付款。


过去完成时:
They had guaranteed the delivery before the deadline. 他们在截止日期前保证了交付。
She had guaranteed the outcome of the experiment. 她已保证实验的结果。


将来时:
We will guarantee the timely completion of the project. 我们将保证项目的及时完成。
The company will guarantee your satisfaction. 公司将保证您的满意。


虚拟语气:
If they guaranteed the loan, we would invest. 如果他们担保贷款，我们将投资。
I wish they had guaranteed the results. 我希望他们当时保证了结果。


被动语态:
The product is guaranteed for one year. 产品保修一年。
The success of the project was guaranteed by careful planning. 项目的成功是由周密的计划保证的。


疑问句:
Does the warranty guarantee repairs? 保修单是否保证维修？
Are you guaranteeing the delivery date? 你保证交货日期吗？


否定句:
They did not guarantee the outcome. 他们没有保证结果。
The company does not guarantee against loss. 公司不保证免受损失。


PHRASE->
guarantee against: 保证免受…
guarantee of: …的保证
guarantee to: 向…保证
lifetime guarantee: 终身保修
money-back guarantee: 退款保证

词源:
The word "guaranteeing" originates from the Old French word "garantir," which means "to guarantee, to warrant."  This ultimately derives from the Germanic root "wær" meaning "to be aware, to watch over," suggesting the protective and assuring nature of a guarantee.
