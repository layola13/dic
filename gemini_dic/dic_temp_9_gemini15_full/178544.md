reconvinces: /ˌriːkənˈvɪnsɪz/ | vt. | 再说服；再次使确信 | To persuade someone again or to reinforce a previous conviction.  | The lawyer reconvinced the jury of the defendant's innocence. 律师再次使陪审团相信被告的清白。 | persuade, convince, reassure | doubt, disbelieve, unconvince | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reconvinces his client of the benefits of the deal. = 他再次使他的客户相信这笔交易的好处。
She reconvinces her daughter to go to college. = 她再次说服女儿上大学。


一般过去时:
The teacher reconvinced the student to finish the assignment. = 老师再次说服学生完成作业。
The salesman reconvinced the customer to buy the product. = 销售员再次说服顾客购买产品。


现在完成时:
He has reconvinced himself that he made the right decision. = 他已经再次确信自己做出了正确的决定。
She has reconvinced her family of her commitment to her career. = 她已经再次使她的家人相信她对事业的投入。


过去完成时:
I had reconvinced myself that giving up was not an option. = 我已经再次确信放弃不是一个选择。
They had reconvinced the board of their plan's viability. = 他们已经再次使董事会相信他们的计划的可行性。


将来时:
I will reconvince him that this is the best course of action. = 我将再次让他相信这是最好的行动方案。
She will reconvince the investors of the company’s potential. = 她将再次使投资者相信公司的潜力。


虚拟语气:
If I could only reconvince him of the truth. = 如果我能再次让他相信真相就好了。
I wish I could reconvince her to stay. = 我希望我能再次说服她留下。


被动语态:
He was reconvinced by the new evidence. = 他被新的证据再次说服了。
The jury was reconvinced by the witness's testimony. = 陪审团被证人的证词再次说服了。


疑问句:
Can we reconvince them to reconsider? = 我们能再次说服他们重新考虑吗？
Will he be reconvinced by our arguments? = 他会被我们的论点再次说服吗？


否定句:
I couldn't reconvince her to change her mind. = 我无法再次说服她改变主意。
They were not reconvinced by the explanation. = 他们没有被解释再次说服。



PHRASE->
reconvince someone of something: 使某人再次相信某事
reconvince someone to do something: 使某人再次做某事
be reconvinced: 被再次说服
reconvince oneself:  再次使自己确信
try to reconvince: 试图再次说服
