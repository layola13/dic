mismates:/ˈmɪsmeɪts/| n.| 不相配的夫妇；不合适的配对 | A mismated couple is a couple who are not well-suited to each other.  This term often implies incompatibility in personality, interests, or goals, leading to unhappiness or conflict. It can refer to romantic relationships, business partnerships, or any pairing where the elements involved clash. | Example: Their marriage was a clear case of mismates; they had nothing in common and constantly argued.  他们的婚姻明显是一对不相配的例子；他们没有任何共同点，经常争吵。|近义词：incompatible pair, ill-matched couple, unsuitable partners |反义词：perfect match, ideal partners, well-suited couple |名词

词源:

The word "mismates" is a compound word formed from the prefix "mis-" (meaning "wrongly" or "badly") and the noun "mates" (meaning "companions" or "partners").  Therefore, "mismates" literally means "wrongly matched" or "badly matched" partners.
