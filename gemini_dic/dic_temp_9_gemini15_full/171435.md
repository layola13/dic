pudu:/ˈpuːduː/| n.|普度鹿|A small deer native to southern Chile and southwestern Argentina.  Pudu are the smallest deer species in the world, known for their shy nature and reddish-brown coat.| 例句：The pudu is critically endangered due to habitat loss and hunting. 普度鹿由于栖息地丧失和狩猎而濒临灭绝。


The pudu's delicate hooves allow it to navigate the dense undergrowth of its native habitat. 普度鹿纤细的蹄子使它能够在原生栖息地的茂密灌木丛中穿行。
