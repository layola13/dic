divani:/dɪˈvɑːni/ | n. | 长沙发；贵妃椅 | A divan is a long, upholstered couch or sofa, often without arms or with low arms.  It can also refer to a type of daybed. | Example:  She relaxed on the plush divan after a long day. 她长舒一口气，在柔软的长沙发上放松下来。 | 近义词：sofa, couch, settee, daybed | 反义词：chair (单人座)
