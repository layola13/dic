remediating: /rɪˈmiːdieɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 补救；矫正；改进 | To correct or improve something that is wrong or unsatisfactory.  | The company is remediating the environmental damage caused by its factory. 公司正在补救其工厂造成的环境破坏。 | 近义词：rectify, correct, repair, amend | 反义词：worsen, exacerbate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are remediating the contaminated soil. = 他们正在修复受污染的土壤。
The government remediates environmental problems. = 政府正在解决环境问题。

一般过去时:
The engineers remediated the faulty wiring. = 工程师修复了有故障的线路。
We remediated the situation by apologizing. = 我们通过道歉来弥补局面。

现在完成时:
They have remediated the leaks in the roof. = 他们已经修补了屋顶的漏洞。
The school has remediated the learning gaps of its students. = 学校已经弥补了学生的学习差距。

过去完成时:
They had remediated the damage before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经修复了损坏。
The company had remediated the safety issues before the accident. = 事故发生前，公司已经解决了安全问题。

将来时:
We will remediate the problem as soon as possible. = 我们将尽快解决这个问题。
The contractor will remediate the asbestos in the building. = 承包商将清除建筑物中的石棉。

虚拟语气:
If they had remediated the issue earlier, the damage would have been less severe. = 如果他们早点解决这个问题，损失就不会那么严重了。
It is essential that the contaminated site be remediated immediately. = 必须立即修复受污染的地点。

被动语态:
The polluted river is being remediated by the local authorities. = 当地政府正在治理受污染的河流。
The damaged bridge was remediated quickly and efficiently. = 受损的桥梁得到了迅速而有效的修复。

疑问句:
Are they remediating the situation effectively? = 他们正在有效地解决这个问题吗？
How will the company remediate the environmental damage? = 公司将如何弥补环境破坏？

否定句:
The company did not remediate the problem adequately. = 公司没有充分解决这个问题。
They failed to remediate the situation before it worsened. = 他们在情况恶化之前没有解决问题。
