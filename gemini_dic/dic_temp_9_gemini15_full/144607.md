outweighs:/ˌaʊtˈweɪz/| vt.|超过；比…重要；胜过 |To be greater than something else in weight, importance, or value.  |例句：The benefits of the project outweigh the costs.  这个项目的益处超过了成本。 |例句：Her desire for independence outweighs her fear of failure. 她对独立的渴望超过了她对失败的恐惧。|近义词：exceed, surpass, eclipse |反义词：underweigh
