confutant:/kənˈfjuːtənt/| n. |驳斥者，反驳者|A person who refutes or opposes an argument or opinion.  Someone who presents a counter-argument.|The confutant presented compelling evidence to discredit the defendant's testimony.  驳斥者提出了令人信服的证据来驳斥被告的证词。|近义词：opponent, refuter, contradictor |反义词：supporter, proponent
