fibrocartilage:/ˌfaɪbroʊˈkɑːrtɪlədʒ/| n. |纤维软骨|A type of connective tissue that is found in areas of the body that require both strength and flexibility, such as the intervertebral discs and menisci of the knee. It is composed of a mixture of collagen fibers and cartilage cells (chondrocytes).|The intervertebral discs are made of fibrocartilage, allowing for flexibility and support of the spine.|近义词：cartilage|反义词：bone
