paraboloidal:/ˌpærəˈbɔɪdəl/| adj.|抛物面形的，抛物线形的|Of or relating to a paraboloid.  A paraboloid is a quadric surface that has exactly one axis of symmetry and is generated by rotating a parabola around its axis.
