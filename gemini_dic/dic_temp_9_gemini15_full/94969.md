in-that:/ɪn ðæt/| conj.|因为，由于|This conjunction introduces a clause that explains the reason or cause for something stated in the main clause.  It emphasizes the reason or cause. It is similar to "because" or "since," but often implies a more specific or important reason.|例句：He was successful in that he worked very hard. = 他成功了，因为他非常努力。|近义词：because, since, as, inasmuch as|反义词：although, despite, notwithstanding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is happy in that he has a loving family. = 他很快乐，因为他有一个充满爱的家庭。
She is successful in that she is very dedicated to her work. = 她很成功，因为她非常专注于自己的工作。


一般过去时:
They were relieved in that the storm passed without causing damage. = 他们很欣慰，因为暴风雨过去了并没有造成损失。
He was disappointed in that he didn't get the job. = 他很失望，因为他没有得到那份工作。


现在完成时:
We have been fortunate in that we have avoided serious illness. = 我们很幸运，因为我们避免了严重的疾病。
She has progressed well in that she has mastered a new skill. = 她进步很快，因为她掌握了一项新技能。


过去完成时:
They had been prepared in that they had studied the material thoroughly. = 他们已经做好了准备，因为他们已经彻底学习了材料。
I had been successful in that I had completed all my assignments on time. = 我很成功，因为我按时完成了所有作业。


将来时:
He will be confident in that he has prepared well for the exam. = 他会很有信心，因为他已经为考试做好了充分的准备。
She will be happy in that she will finally see her family. = 她会很快乐，因为她终于可以见到家人了。


虚拟语气:
It would be better if we acted in that it is the right thing to do. = 如果我们这么做是正确的，那就更好。
I would have been grateful if you had informed me in that it was urgent. = 如果你当时告诉我这件事很紧急，我会很感激。


被动语态:
The plan was successful in that it was carefully executed. = 该计划很成功，因为它被小心地执行了。
The problem was solved in that a comprehensive solution was implemented. = 问题解决了，因为实施了一个全面的解决方案。


疑问句:
Is he happy in that he achieved his goal? = 他实现目标后快乐吗？
Was she satisfied in that the project was completed on time? = 项目按时完成，她满意吗？


否定句:
He is not happy in that his plans failed. = 他不快乐，因为他的计划失败了。
She was not successful in that her efforts were insufficient. = 她不成功，因为她的努力不足。
