fraternize:/ˈfrætərnaɪz/| v.|友善相处；亲密交往；与敌军士兵私下友好相处|To associate or be friendly with, especially in a way that is considered improper, such as with the enemy during wartime.  |例句：The soldiers were court-martialed for fraternizing with the enemy.  士兵因与敌人私下友好相处而被军事法庭审判。


例句：It's important to fraternize with your colleagues to build a strong team.  与同事友好相处对建立强大的团队至关重要。|近义词：associate, socialize, mingle|反义词：isolate, estrange, alienate|动词
