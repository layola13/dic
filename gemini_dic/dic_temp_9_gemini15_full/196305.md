sergeant-major:/ˌsɑːrdʒənt ˈmeɪdʒər/| n. |准尉，上士 | A non-commissioned officer of the highest rank in a company or battery.  A sergeant-major is responsible for the administration and discipline of the soldiers under his command.  They often act as an advisor to the commanding officer. | Example: The sergeant-major inspected the troops before the parade. 准尉在阅兵前检阅了部队。  The sergeant-major ensured all the equipment was accounted for. 准尉确保所有设备都已清点完毕。 |近义词：master sergeant, first sergeant |反义词：private, recruit
