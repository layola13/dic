reconceptualization: /ˌriːkənˌsɛptʃuəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |重新概念化；重新构想 | The process of changing or reforming a concept, idea, or system of thought.  It involves a fundamental shift in understanding, often challenging existing paradigms and perspectives.  |  例句：The reconceptualization of the curriculum involved a complete overhaul of teaching methods.  重新概念化课程包括对教学方法的彻底改革。

The reconceptualization of gender roles has led to greater equality. 性别角色的重新概念化带来了更大的平等。


近义词：rethinking, reinterpretation, reformulation, reimagining


反义词：reinforcement (of existing concepts),  stagnation (of thought)


词性：名词
