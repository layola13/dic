heartwoods: /ˈhɑːrtwʊdz/ | n. |心材；(木材的)中心部分 | The dark, inner wood of a tree, usually harder and more resistant to decay than the surrounding sapwood.  It's the mature, central wood of a tree, formed from older xylem cells that have become non-functional for water transport.  Heartwood is often darker in color than sapwood due to the accumulation of extractives. |例句：The heartwood of this oak is incredibly durable. 这棵橡树的心材非常耐用。

The heartwood of the mahogany was prized for its rich color and strength.  桃花心木的心材因其丰富的颜色和强度而备受珍视。


近义词：duramen | 反义词：sapwood
