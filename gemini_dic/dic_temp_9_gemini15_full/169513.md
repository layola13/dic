propatriotism:/ˌproʊˈpeɪtriəˌtɪzəm/| n. |爱国主义，强烈的爱国主义|Propatriotism refers to an excessive or aggressive form of patriotism, often characterized by blind loyalty, xenophobia, and a disregard for dissenting opinions. It can manifest in the glorification of one's nation and the denigration of others.  |例句：The government's propatriotism campaign led to increased tensions with neighboring countries.  政府的爱国主义宣传导致与邻国的紧张关系加剧。

例句：His propatriotism bordered on nationalism, causing him to reject any criticism of his country. 他的爱国主义近乎民族主义，导致他拒绝任何对其国家的批评。|近义词：nationalism, jingoism, chauvinism |反义词：cosmopolitanism, internationalism |词性: noun
