rulers:/ˈruːlərz/ | n. |统治者；尺子 | People or things that govern or control.  A straight-edged instrument used for measuring or drawing straight lines. |例句：The ancient rulers of Egypt built magnificent pyramids.  埃及的古代统治者建造了宏伟的金字塔。

The architect used a ruler to draw a precise plan. 建筑师用尺子画了一个精确的图纸。|近义词：leaders, governors, monarchs, measuring sticks |反义词：subjects, ruled,  (for measuring sticks)  measuring tape
