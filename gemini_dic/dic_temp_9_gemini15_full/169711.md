proprietary-schools:/prəˈpraɪəˌteriː skuːlz/ | n. | 专有学校，私立学校 | Proprietary schools are privately owned and operated, often charging tuition fees.  They are distinct from public schools, which are funded by the government.  | Example: Many parents choose to send their children to proprietary schools for a more specialized education.  许多家长选择送孩子去私立学校接受更专业的教育。 | 近义词：private schools, independent schools | 反义词：public schools
