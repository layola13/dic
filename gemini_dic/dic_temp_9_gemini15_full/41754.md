corrupt:/kəˈrʌpt/| adj., v.|腐败的；贪污的；败坏的 | to make someone or something morally bad; to change something good into something bad | 近义词：decayed, dishonest, depraved | 反义词：honest, virtuous, pure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mayor is corrupt. = 市长很腐败。
This system is corrupt and needs reform. = 这个制度腐败且需要改革。


一般过去时:
The scandal corrupted his reputation. = 丑闻败坏了他的名声。
The judge was corrupted by bribes. = 法官被贿赂收买了。


现在完成时:
Power has corrupted him. = 权力腐蚀了他。
Years of neglect have corrupted the building's structure. = 多年的忽视已经破坏了建筑物的结构。


过去完成时:
The empire had been corrupted from within. = 这个帝国已经被内部腐蚀了。
They had corrupted the data before the analysis. = 他们在分析之前就篡改了数据。


将来时:
Absolute power will corrupt absolutely. = 绝对的权力将导致绝对的腐败。
This new policy may corrupt the existing system. = 这项新政策可能会破坏现有的制度。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't been corrupted, he would have been a great leader. = 如果他没有被腐蚀，他将成为一位伟大的领导者。
I wish the system weren't so corrupt. = 我希望这个制度不要这么腐败。


被动语态:
The politician was corrupted by big money. = 这个政客被巨款收买了。
The data was corrupted during the transmission. = 数据在传输过程中被破坏了。


疑问句:
Is the government corrupt? = 政府腐败吗？
Have they corrupted the evidence? = 他们篡改了证据吗？


否定句:
The judge was not corrupt. = 法官并不腐败。
They did not corrupt the election results. = 他们没有篡改选举结果。
