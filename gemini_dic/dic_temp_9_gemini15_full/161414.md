postumus: /ˈpɒstjʊməs/ | adj. | 死后的，遗腹的 |  Occurring, done, or published after someone's death.  | 例句：His posthumous works were published years after his death.  他的遗作在他死后多年才出版。

例句：The award was given posthumously to the brave soldier.  这个奖项追授给了这位勇敢的士兵。(死后追授)


近义词：post-mortem (有时), after-death


反义词：ante-mortem, pre-mortem


词性：形容词
