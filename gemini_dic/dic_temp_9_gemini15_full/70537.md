filibusterous: /ˌfɪlɪˈbʌstərəs/ | adj. | 冗长乏味的；阻挠议事的；故意拖延的 | Relating to or characteristic of a filibuster; characterized by prolonged speechmaking to delay legislative action.  | The filibusterous debate went on for hours, delaying the crucial vote.  冗长的辩论持续了数小时，延误了关键投票。 |  obstructionist, delaying, protracted | efficient, expeditious |  形容词

词源:
From "filibuster," ultimately from Dutch *vrijbuiter ("freebooter").  The term originally referred to adventurers or privateers, and later to those who obstruct legislative processes.
