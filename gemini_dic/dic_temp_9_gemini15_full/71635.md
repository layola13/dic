flatmates:/ˈflætmeɪts/| n.|室友|People who share a flat (apartment) together.|My flatmates and I are throwing a party this weekend.我的室友和我这周末要开派对。|roommates, housemates|enemies, strangers|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My flatmates often cook dinner together. = 我的室友经常一起做饭。
They usually clean the flat on weekends. = 他们通常周末打扫公寓。


一般过去时:
My flatmates watched a movie last night. = 我的室友昨晚看了一部电影。
We had a big argument about the rent last month. = 上个月我们因为房租吵了一架。


现在完成时:
My flatmates have lived together for two years. = 我的室友已经一起住了两年了。
They have painted the living room recently. = 他们最近粉刷了客厅。


过去完成时:
I had already moved out before my flatmates found a new roommate. = 在我的室友找到新的室友之前，我已经搬走了。
They had finished cleaning before I got home. = 我回家之前他们已经打扫完了。


将来时:
My flatmates will move out next month. = 我的室友下个月要搬出去。
We will have a farewell party for them. = 我们会为他们举办一个告别派对。


虚拟语气:
If my flatmates were cleaner, I would be happier. = 如果我的室友更爱干净，我会更开心。
I wish my flatmates would stop making so much noise. = 我希望我的室友能停止制造这么大的噪音。


被动语态:
The flat was cleaned by my flatmates. = 公寓被我的室友打扫干净了。
The rent is usually paid by one of my flatmates. = 房租通常由我的一个室友支付。


疑问句:
Do your flatmates pay their share of the bills? = 你的室友支付他们应付的账单吗？
Are your flatmates easy to live with? = 你的室友容易相处吗？


否定句:
My flatmates don't always get along. = 我的室友并不总是相处融洽。
They didn't invite me to their party. = 他们没有邀请我参加他们的派对。

find flatmates = 寻找室友
