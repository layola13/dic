munching:/ˈmʌntʃɪŋ/ | n. | 咀嚼声；用力咀嚼 | The act of chewing something noisily.  |例句：The sound of munching apples filled the air. 苹果的咀嚼声充满了空气。

例句：I could hear the quiet munching coming from the other room. 我能听到从另一个房间传来的轻轻咀嚼声。


近义词：chewing, chomping, gnawing | 反义词：None (It's an action, not easily expressed with an antonym) | 词性:名词 (gerund)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog is munching on a bone. = 狗正在啃骨头。
He munches his food very quickly. = 他吃东西很快。


一般过去时:
She munched popcorn during the movie. = 她在看电影的时候吃爆米花。
They munched on sandwiches for lunch. = 他们午餐吃三明治。


现在完成时:
He has munched through half the bag of chips. = 他已经吃掉了半袋薯片。
They have been munching on snacks all afternoon. = 他们整个下午都在吃零食。


过去完成时:
The children had munched all the cookies before their parents came home. = 孩子们在父母回家之前已经把所有的饼干都吃掉了。
She had munched a whole apple before the meeting started. = 会议开始前她已经吃掉了一个苹果。


将来时:
I will be munching on some trail mix during my hike. = 我徒步旅行时会吃一些混合坚果。
They will munch on pretzels while watching the game. = 他们看比赛时会吃椒盐脆饼。


虚拟语气:
If I had some chips, I would be munching right now. = 如果我有薯片，我现在就会吃。
I wish I could be munching on a chocolate bar. = 我真希望我能吃一块巧克力棒。


被动语态:  (Not typically used in passive voice, as it's an active process)
The apples were being munched on by the children. = 苹果正被孩子们吃。 (Sounds unnatural)


疑问句:
Are you munching on anything? = 你在吃什么吗？
What are they munching? = 他们在吃什么？


否定句:
He wasn't munching on anything. = 他什么也没吃。
She doesn't munch loudly. = 她咀嚼时声音不大。


PHRASE->
munching on:  正在吃…
munching through: 吃掉… (全部)
munching sound: 咀嚼声
happily munching: 快乐地咀嚼
quietly munching: 安静地咀嚼
