neatens:/ˈniːtənz/| v.|使整洁；使整齐|To make something tidy or orderly.|She neatens her desk before leaving work every day. 她每天下班前都会整理她的桌子。|近义词：tidys, arranges, organizes|反义词：messes, disorganizes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He neatens his room every Saturday. = 他每个星期六都整理他的房间。
She neatens her hair before going out. = 她出门前会整理她的头发。

一般过去时:
I neatened the kitchen after dinner. = 晚饭后我整理了厨房。
He neatly neatened his tie. = 他整齐地整理了他的领带。


现在完成时:

They have neatened up the garden after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，他们已经整理了花园。


过去完成时:
