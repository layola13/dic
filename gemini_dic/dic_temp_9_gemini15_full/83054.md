gurglingly:/ˈɡɜːrɡlɪŋli/| adv.|咕噜咕噜地，汩汩地|In a gurgling manner; with a sound like that of water flowing rapidly through a narrow passage.|例句：The water gurgled down the drain gurglingly. 水咕噜咕噜地流进了下水道。

The stream gurgled gurglingly over the smooth stones. 小溪咕噜咕噜地流过光滑的石头。


近义词：bubblingly, murmuringly|反义词：silently, quietly


PHRASE->
gurglingly flow: 汩汩地流淌
gurglingly sound: 咕噜咕噜的声音
gurglingly bubbling: 咕噜咕噜地冒泡
gurglingly stream: 汩汩流淌的小溪
gurglingly voice: 咕噜咕噜的声音（嗓音）
