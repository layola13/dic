cryptal:/ˈkrɪptəl/| n. |密码，加密货币|A cryptal is a type of cryptocurrency; a digital currency designed to work as a medium of exchange through a computer network that is not reliant on any central authority, instead using cryptography to secure and verify transactions as well as to control the creation of new units of the currency.|Example: Bitcoin is a well-known cryptal.|近义词：cryptocurrency, digital currency|反义词：fiat currency
