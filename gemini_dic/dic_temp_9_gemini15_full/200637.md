slayers:/ˈsleɪərz/| n.|杀手，屠杀者，消灭者|Those who slay, especially habitually or professionally.  The term often refers to those who kill mythical creatures or monsters.  It can also be used figuratively to describe someone who excels at something and defeats opponents. |例句：The brave slayers ventured into the dark forest to defeat the dragon.  勇敢的杀手冒险进入黑暗森林去打败巨龙。

例句：She is a slayer of boredom with her endless supply of fascinating stories. 她是一个无聊的杀手，她有无尽的精彩故事。


近义词：killers, destroyers, vanquishers, exterminators, assassins

反义词：victims, saviors, protectors


词源:  Derived from the verb "slay," meaning to kill violently. The suffix "-ers" denotes a person who performs the action.  The word's origins trace back to Old English "slean," meaning "to strike, hit, kill."
