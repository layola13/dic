hype-up: /haɪp ʌp/ | vt. | 使兴奋；使激动；大肆宣传 | To increase excitement or enthusiasm for something, often excessively; to promote something aggressively.  |例句：The media hyped up the upcoming election. 媒体大肆宣传即将到来的选举。

The band hyped up the crowd before their performance.乐队在演出前将观众的情绪调动起来。

近义词：excite, energize, promote, publicize, build up | 反义词：depress, calm, downplay,  undermine | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hype up every new product launch. = 他们为每次新产品发布造势。
The company hypes up its latest innovation. = 公司大力宣传其最新的创新成果。


一般过去时:
The campaign hyped up the candidate's popularity. = 该竞选活动提升了候选人的知名度。
They hyped up the concert to attract a larger audience. = 他们为演唱会造势以吸引更多观众。


现在完成时:
The marketing team has hyped up the new movie release. = 市场营销团队已经为新电影的上映造势了。
They have hyped up the event for weeks. = 他们已经为此活动宣传了好几个星期了。


过去完成时:
They had hyped up the product before the official release. = 他们在正式发布之前就为产品造势了。
The media had hyped up the story, creating unrealistic expectations. = 媒体对这个故事进行了大肆宣传，造成了不切实际的期望。


将来时:
They will hype up the festival to boost attendance. = 他们将为节日造势以提高出席率。
The organizers will hype up the event through social media. = 组织者将通过社交媒体为活动造势。


虚拟语气:
If they hyped up the product more, they would sell more. = 如果他们更多地为产品造势，他们就能卖出更多产品。
I wish they hadn't hyped up the event so much; it was disappointing. = 我真希望他们没有对这次活动进行如此大肆的宣传；它令人失望。


被动语态:
The event was hyped up extensively by the organizers. = 活动受到了组织者的大肆宣传。
The new game is being hyped up by gamers online. = 新游戏正在被游戏玩家在网上炒作。


疑问句:
Is the new phone being hyped up too much? = 新手机是不是被过度宣传了？
Are they going to hype up the next concert as much? = 他们会像上次演唱会那样进行宣传吗？


否定句:
They didn't hype up the product enough. = 他们对产品的宣传不够。
The company chose not to hype up the new feature. = 公司选择不为新功能进行宣传。
