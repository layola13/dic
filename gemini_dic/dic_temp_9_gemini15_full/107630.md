langoustines:/ˈlæŋɡəstiːnz/| n. |龙虾|A langoustine is a type of marine crustacean similar to a prawn or shrimp, but larger and with a more robust shell.  They are highly prized for their delicate flavor and are often considered a delicacy. |例句：The restaurant is famous for its delicious langoustines. 这家餐厅以其美味的龙虾而闻名。

例句：We ordered a platter of grilled langoustines for our appetizer. 我们点了一盘烤龙虾作为开胃菜。


近义词：prawns, shrimps, scampi (though scampi often refers to a prepared dish)

反义词：None (no direct antonym exists)
