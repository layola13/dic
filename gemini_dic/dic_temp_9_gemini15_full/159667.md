polyacoustic: /ˌpɒliəˈkuːstɪk/ | adj. | 多声学的；多声的 | Relating to or characterized by multiple sounds or acoustic sources.  It describes a situation or system involving many different sounds, often overlapping or interacting.  The term is often used in fields like acoustics, music, and audio engineering. |

例句：

The concert hall's polyacoustic design created a rich and immersive soundscape. = 音乐厅的多声学设计创造了一个丰富而身临其境的音景。

The recording engineer struggled to balance the polyacoustic mix of the live performance. = 录音工程师努力平衡现场表演的多声学混音。


近义词：multi-acoustic, multiphonic,  multisonous (less common)

反义词：monaural, single-source (these describe a single sound source)

词性：形容词


词源:

* **poly-**:  From Greek πολύς (polús), meaning "many," "much," or "several."

* **acoustic**: From Greek ἀκουστικός (akoustikós), meaning "of or relating to hearing," derived from ἀκούω (akoúō), meaning "I hear."
