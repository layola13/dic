junker:/ˈdʒʌŋkər/ | n. |破烂货，旧车，废物，（尤指）老旧的汽车|A junker is something old, broken-down, and often worthless, especially an old car.  It can also refer to a person considered to be worthless or undesirable. |例句：He drives a rusty old junker. 他开着一辆生锈的老破车。


例句：That old car is a real junker; it's barely running. 那辆旧车真是个破烂货；它几乎开不动了。


近义词：wreck, clunker, heap, derelict


反义词：new car, gem, treasure


词性:名词
