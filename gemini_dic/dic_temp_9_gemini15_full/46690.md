damsel:/ˈdæmzəl/| n.|少女；贵妇；（尤指）待救的女子|A young woman, especially a young woman of noble birth or one regarded with admiration.|例句：The damsel was rescued from the burning building.  这位少女从燃烧的建筑物中获救了。|例句：The knight rescued the fair damsel from the dragon.  骑士从龙爪下救出了美丽的少女。|近义词：maiden, young woman, lady|反义词：old woman
