recounter:/rɪˈkaʊntər/| vt.|复述，叙述|To recount something means to tell someone about it, often in detail. It implies a narrative or storytelling aspect, focusing on the sequence of events.|He recounted his adventures in the Amazon rainforest.|近义词：narrate, relate, describe|反义词：conceal, hide


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recounts his childhood memories every time we meet. = 每次我们见面，他都会讲述他的童年回忆。
She recounts the events of that day with vivid detail. = 她生动地描述了那天发生的事情。


一般过去时:
He recounted the story of his escape from the burning building. = 他讲述了他从燃烧的建筑物中逃生的故事。
She recounted her travels to her friends. = 她向朋友们讲述了她的旅行经历。


现在完成时:
He has recounted the story many times. = 他已经多次讲述了这个故事。
She has recounted her experiences in the war to several audiences. = 她已经向多个听众讲述了她在战争中的经历。


过去完成时:
He had recounted the entire incident before the police arrived. = 在警察到来之前，他已经讲述了整个事件。
She had recounted the story so many times that she knew it by heart. = 她已经多次讲述了这个故事，以至于她已经烂熟于心了。


将来时:
He will recount his experiences at the conference tomorrow. = 他将在明天讲述他在会议上的经历。
She will recount the details of the accident to the insurance company. = 她将向保险公司详细讲述事故的经过。


虚拟语气:
If he recounted the truth, people might believe him. = 如果他讲述真相，人们可能会相信他。
I wish she had recounted the whole story to me. = 我希望她能把整个故事都告诉我。


被动语态:
The story was recounted by several witnesses. = 这个故事被几个证人复述了。
The events of the battle were recounted in detail in the history book. = 这本书详细地记述了战斗的经过。


疑问句:
Will he recount the details of the meeting? = 他会详细讲述会议内容吗？
Did she recount everything that happened? = 她是否讲述了发生的一切？


否定句:
He didn't recount the whole truth. = 他没有讲述全部真相。
She refused to recount the events of that night. = 她拒绝讲述那天晚上发生的事情。


PHRASE->
recount the events = 叙述事件
recount a story = 讲述一个故事
recount experiences = 讲述经历
recount details = 详细叙述
recount the history = 叙述历史
