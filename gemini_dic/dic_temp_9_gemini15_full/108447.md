laxest: /ˈlæksɪst/ | adj. | 最松懈的，最不严格的 | Most relaxed or careless; least strict.  The laxest security measures allowed intruders easy access.  | 近义词：loosest, most lenient, most permissive | 反义词：strictest, tightest, most rigorous |  形容词


例句：

The laxest security measures allowed intruders easy access. 最松懈的安全措施使得入侵者很容易进入。

His laxest approach to deadlines often resulted in missed opportunities. 他对截止日期最松懈的态度常常导致错失良机。
