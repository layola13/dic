overprescribing:/ˌoʊvərˈprɛˌskraɪbɪŋ/ | v. |过度处方，滥用处方 |The act of prescribing medication more often or in higher doses than is medically necessary or appropriate. This can lead to various negative consequences, including adverse drug reactions, increased healthcare costs, and the development of antibiotic resistance.  | Example: The doctor was criticized for overprescribing antibiotics to his patients.  医生因给病人过度开抗生素而受到批评。|近义词: excessive prescribing,  polypharmacy (if referring to multiple medications)|反义词: underprescribing, appropriate prescribing |动词
