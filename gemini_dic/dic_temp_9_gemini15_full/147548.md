overpedals: /ˌoʊvərˈpɛdəlz/ | v. |用力过猛踩踏板(通常指自行车或汽车的踏板)| To press or operate a pedal (typically of a bicycle or car) too forcefully or excessively.  |例句：He overpedaled on the steep hill and lost his balance. 他在陡峭的山坡上用力过猛踩踏板，失去了平衡。 He overpedaled coming into the final turn, costing him valuable seconds. 他在进入最后一个弯道时用力过猛踩踏板，浪费了宝贵的时间。|近义词：overdo, overuse |反义词：underpedal (罕用)
