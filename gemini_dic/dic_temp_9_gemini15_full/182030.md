/ˌriːˈmiːts/| v. |再次相遇，重逢|To meet again after a period of time.|They remeet after many years of separation. 他们多年分离后再次重逢。|meet again, reunite|separate, part|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-meet every year for a reunion. = 他们每年都聚会重逢。
We re-meet at the same cafe every Tuesday. = 我们每周二都在同一家咖啡馆重逢。


一般过去时:
They re-met after a decade of silence. = 他们十年沉默之后再次重逢。
We re-met unexpectedly at the airport. = 我们在机场意外地再次相遇。


现在完成时:
They have re-met several times since the graduation. = 毕业后他们已经重逢好几次了。
We have re-met only once since that fateful day. = 从那一天起，我们只重逢过一次。


过去完成时:
They had re-met before the conference began. = 在会议开始之前，他们已经重逢了。
We had re-met briefly at a friend's wedding. = 我们曾在朋友的婚礼上短暂地重逢过。


将来时:
They will re-meet next year at the anniversary celebration. = 他们明年将在周年庆典上再次重逢。
We will re-meet soon, I promise. = 我保证我们很快就会再见。


虚拟语气:
If they re-met, it would be a joyous occasion. = 如果他们重逢，那将是一件令人高兴的事。
I wish we could re-meet under better circumstances. = 我希望我们能在更好的情况下重逢。


被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice,  a more natural phrasing would be needed.)


疑问句:
Will they re-meet at the reunion? = 他们会在聚会上重逢吗？
Have they re-met since their quarrel? = 他们吵架后重逢了吗？


否定句:
They did not re-meet for another ten years. = 他们十年后才再次重逢。
We have not re-met since college. = 自从大学毕业后我们就没有再见面了。


PHRASE->
re-meet with friends = 与朋友再次会面
re-meet after years = 多年后再次重逢
re-meet by chance = 偶然再次相遇
re-meet in a new city = 在一座新的城市里再次相遇
re-meet at the old place = 在老地方再次重逢
