physio-psychologic: /ˌfɪzioʊˌsaɪkəˈlɑːdʒɪk/ | adj. |身心(生理心理)的| Relating to both the physical and mental aspects of a person or condition.  This term combines "physio-" (relating to the body) and "-psychologic" (relating to the mind). It emphasizes the interconnectedness of physical and psychological processes. |例句：The patient's condition requires a physio-psychologic approach, addressing both physical symptoms and emotional distress.  这位病人的病情需要身心结合的治疗方法，既要解决生理症状，也要解决情绪困扰。


The physio-psychologic effects of stress can be significant.  压力的身心影响可能是巨大的。


近义词：biopsychosocial, psychosomatic, holistic


反义词：None (as it describes a combination, not a single aspect)


词性：形容词
