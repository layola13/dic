resprayed:/riːˈspreɪd/ | v. | 再喷涂，重新喷漆 | To spray paint or coat something again.  This typically involves applying a new layer of paint or coating over a previously existing one.  | 例句：The car was resprayed after the accident. 事故后，汽车被重新喷漆了。

例句：The artist resprayed the mural to correct a mistake. 艺术家重新喷绘壁画以纠正错误。


近义词：repaint, recoat, reapply

反义词：None (The antonym would describe the opposite action, such as removing paint, which doesn't have a single direct opposite word.)

词性：verb
