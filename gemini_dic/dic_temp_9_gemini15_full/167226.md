The term often implies advanced technological augmentation or other enhancements beyond typical human limitations.  |  例句：The preterhuman strength of the cyborg allowed it to lift the heavy object with ease.  这个机器人的超人力量使它能够轻松举起重物。

例句：The athlete's preterhuman endurance allowed him to finish the marathon despite severe injuries.  这位运动员超人的耐力使他尽管受了重伤，还是完成了马拉松比赛。
