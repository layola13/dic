charities:/ˈtʃærətiz/| n.|慈善机构；慈善事业|Organizations set up to provide help to those in need, often raising money through donations and fundraising.|例句：Many charities are working to alleviate poverty in developing countries.  许多慈善机构正在努力减轻发展中国家的贫困。|例句：The charity auction raised a significant amount of money for the hospital. 慈善拍卖为医院筹集了大量资金。|近义词：benevolence, philanthropy|反义词：selfishness, greed|名词
