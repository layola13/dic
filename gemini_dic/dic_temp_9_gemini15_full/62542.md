endyma: /ˈɛndɪmə/ | n. | 脑室膜；脉络膜 | The endyma is a membrane lining the ventricles of the brain and the central canal of the spinal cord. It is composed of ependymal cells, which are involved in the production and circulation of cerebrospinal fluid.  | The endyma plays a crucial role in maintaining the homeostasis of the central nervous system. | 近义词：ependyma | 反义词：无
