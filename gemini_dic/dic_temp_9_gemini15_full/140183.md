offers:/ˈɔːfərz/ | vt. n. | 提供；出价；报价 | To present or propose something for acceptance or rejection;  a proposal to do something or give something. |例句：The company offers a wide range of services. 这家公司提供各种各样的服务。

The store offers a discount for students. 这家商店为学生提供折扣。


近义词：provides, presents, suggests, proposes | 反义词：demands, refuses

词性:动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He offers his help to everyone. = 他向每个人提供帮助。
The company offers competitive salaries. = 这家公司提供有竞争力的薪水。


一般过去时:
She offered me a job. = 她给我提供了一份工作。
They offered a reasonable price for the car. = 他们为这辆车提供了一个合理的价格。


现在完成时:
I have offered my assistance several times. = 我已经多次提供过我的帮助。
They have offered no explanation for their actions. = 他们没有解释他们的行为。


过去完成时:
He had offered to help before I even asked. = 我还没开口他就主动提出帮忙了。
She had offered a substantial sum of money, but it was still refused. = 她已经提供了一大笔钱，但仍然被拒绝了。


将来时:
I will offer you a ride home. = 我会送你回家。
The company will offer a new product next month. = 公司下个月将推出新产品。


虚拟语气:
If they offered me the job, I would accept it. = 如果他们给我这份工作，我会接受。
I wish they had offered me more money. = 我希望他们能给我更多钱。


被动语态:
The job was offered to me. = 这份工作提供给了我。
A discount is offered to senior citizens. = 对老年人提供折扣。


疑问句:
What are you offering? = 你提供什么？
What did they offer in return? = 他们作为回报提供了什么？


否定句:
He didn't offer any help. = 他没有提供任何帮助。
They offered no explanation. = 他们没有解释。


PHRASE->
offer a solution = 提供解决方案
offer a suggestion = 提供建议
offer an apology = 道歉
offer a price = 出价
offer a reward = 提供奖励
