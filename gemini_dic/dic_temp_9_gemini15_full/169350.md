prompts:/prɒmpt/|ˈprɑːmpt| n. v.|提示；促使；提示词；脚本；|a word, phrase, or question that encourages someone to speak or act;  a sign or signal to do something; a set of instructions or information given to a computer program|例句：The teacher gave us prompts to help us write our essays. 老师给了我们一些提示来帮助我们写论文。|例句：The actor forgot his lines, so the prompter whispered them to him. 演员忘了台词，所以提示员低声告诉他。|近义词：cue, suggestion, stimulus, reminder|反义词：hindrance, obstruction, deterrent|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The program prompts the user for their name. = 程序提示用户输入姓名。
The question prompts a lively discussion. = 这个问题引发了热烈的讨论。

一般过去时:
The alarm prompted me to wake up. = 警报促使我醒来。
The evidence prompted further investigation. = 证据促使进一步调查。

现在完成时:
Recent events have prompted a change in policy. = 最近发生的事件促使政策发生了变化。
His success has prompted many to follow in his footsteps. = 他的成功促使许多人效仿他。

过去完成时:
The earlier problems had prompted a thorough review of the system. = 早期的问题促使对系统进行了彻底的审查。
Her past experiences had prompted her to seek help. = 她过去的经历促使她寻求帮助。

将来时:
The upcoming election will prompt many debates. = 即将到来的选举将引发许多辩论。
The new findings will prompt further research. = 新的发现将促使进一步的研究。

虚拟语气:
If the prompt were clearer, I would understand better. = 如果提示更清晰，我会更好地理解。
I wish the prompt had been more specific. = 我希望提示更具体一些。

被动语态:
The user is prompted to enter a password. = 用户被提示输入密码。
The system is prompted by the user input. = 系统由用户输入来提示。

疑问句:
What prompts you to say that? = 什么促使你说那样的话？
What prompts this sudden change of plans? = 什么促使你突然改变计划？

否定句:
Nothing prompted him to act. = 没有什么促使他采取行动。
The situation did not prompt any immediate response. = 这种情况没有引起任何即时的反应。


PHRASE->
command prompt = 命令提示符
prompt response = 及时响应
prompt payment = 及时付款
user prompt = 用户提示
system prompt = 系统提示
