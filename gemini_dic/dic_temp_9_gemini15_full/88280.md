hinterland:/ˈhɪntərlænd/| n. |内地；边远地区；腹地 |The area lying beyond a coastal region or a river's banks.  It often refers to a region that is less developed or less accessible than the more urbanized areas. |例句：The hinterland of the city is largely agricultural.  城市的腹地主要是农业区。|例句：The mountainous hinterland offered little in the way of easy transportation.  多山的内地交通不便。|近义词：backcountry, backwoods, interior |反义词：metropolis, urban center
