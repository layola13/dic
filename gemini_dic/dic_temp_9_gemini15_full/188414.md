rucks:/rʌks/| n.|背包，旅行包|A rucksack is a large bag carried on the back, typically used for carrying supplies on hikes or travels.  |例句：I packed my clothes and essentials into my rucksack before setting off on the trek. 我把衣服和必需品装进我的背包，然后开始徒步旅行。
例句：She carried a heavy rucksack filled with books and camping gear. 她背着一个沉重的背包，里面装满了书籍和露营装备。|近义词：backpack, knapsack|反义词：handbag, purse
