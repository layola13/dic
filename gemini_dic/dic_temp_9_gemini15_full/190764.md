saving:/ˈseɪvɪŋ/| n. |储蓄；节省；挽救|The act of preserving something from loss or destruction, or the money saved.  It can also refer to the amount of money saved. |例句：He is saving money for a new car. 他正在存钱买一辆新车。

例句：The firefighters worked tirelessly to effect the saving of the trapped miners. 消防队员们不遗余力地营救被困矿工。

近义词：economy, preservation, conservation |反义词：spending, waste, loss|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is saving money for a down payment. = 他正在攒钱付首付。
They are saving energy by using LED lights. = 他们通过使用LED灯来节约能源。

一般过去时:
She saved a lot of money last year. = 她去年存了很多钱。
They saved the company from bankruptcy. = 他们将公司从破产边缘拯救了回来。

现在完成时:
I have saved enough money for a vacation. = 我已经存够了去度假的钱。
We have saved several endangered species from extinction. = 我们已经拯救了几种濒临灭绝的物种。

过去完成时:
He had saved a considerable sum before he retired. = 他退休前已经存了一笔可观的款项。
They had saved the child from drowning. = 他们已经将孩子从溺水中救了出来。

将来时:
She will save a portion of her salary each month. = 她每个月都会存一部分工资。
We will save the planet by reducing carbon emissions. = 我们将通过减少碳排放来拯救地球。

虚拟语气:
If I had saved more money, I could have bought a house. = 如果我存了更多的钱，我就能买房子了。
I wish I had saved more for retirement. = 我真希望我为退休存更多钱。

被动语态:
Millions of lives were saved by the invention of penicillin. = 青霉素的发明挽救了数百万人的生命。
The environment is being saved through conservation efforts. = 通过保护工作正在拯救环境。

疑问句:
How much money are you saving each month? = 你每个月存多少钱？
Are they saving enough for their future? = 他们为未来存够了吗？

否定句:
He isn't saving enough money for his education. = 他为他的教育没有存够钱。
They didn't save the old building from demolition. = 他们没有把那座旧楼从拆迁中拯救出来。
