non-thermal:/ˌnɒnˈθɜːrməl/| adj. |非热性的，非热量的|Not involving or producing heat.  This adjective describes processes, effects, or phenomena that do not involve heat transfer or temperature changes.  For example, non-thermal plasma processing doesn't rely on high temperatures to achieve its effects.|Example: Non-thermal plasma technology is used in various industrial applications. 非热等离子体技术用于各种工业应用。|近义词：athermal, athermal|反义词：thermal
