capitate:/kæˈpɪteɪt/|/kəˈpætɪt/| adj.| 头状的；有头状体的；|Having a head or head-like structure;  resembling a head.  The term often describes structures in botany and zoology where small units are clustered together to form a rounded mass. |例句：The flower has a capitate inflorescence. 这朵花具有头状花序。

The plant's seeds are arranged in a capitate manner.  这种植物的种子呈头状排列。

|近义词：headed, globular, round-headed|反义词：caudate (having a tail)|词性：形容词
