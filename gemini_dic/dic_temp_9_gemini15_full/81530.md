gravitating: /ˈɡrævɪteɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 趋向；被吸引；受…影响 | Moving towards or being drawn to something.  |例句：People tend to gravitate towards those who share their interests. 人们往往倾向于与那些有着共同兴趣的人交往。

例句：The conversation gravitated towards politics.  谈话转向了政治。


近义词：tending, inclining, attracting | 反义词：repelling, avoiding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gravitates towards the beach during summer. 他夏天喜欢去海滩。
The discussion gravitates towards the central issue. 讨论正趋向核心问题。


一般过去时:
The crowd gravitated towards the stage. 人群向舞台聚集。
She gravitated towards a career in medicine. 她选择了医学作为自己的职业。


现在完成时:
They have gravitated towards a more sustainable lifestyle. 他们已经转向更可持续的生活方式。
The company has gravitated towards a more efficient model.  这家公司已经转向更有效的模式。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, the focus had gravitated towards the budget. 会议之前，焦点已经转向预算。
By the end of the evening, the conversation had gravitated towards personal matters.  到晚上结束时，谈话已经转向了私事。


将来时:
I think the trend will gravitate towards smaller, more eco-friendly vehicles. 我认为这一趋势将转向更小、更环保的车辆。
The company expects its clients to gravitate towards its new products. 公司预计客户将会转向其新产品。


虚拟语气:
If he were honest, he would not gravitate towards deceit. 如果他诚实，他就不会趋向于欺骗。
I wish my son would gravitate towards more constructive activities. 我希望我的儿子能转向更有建设性的活动。


被动语态:
The debate was gravitated towards by the media. 媒体将辩论的焦点引导了过去。
The attention was gravitated towards the new celebrity. 注意力被吸引到这位新名人的身上。


疑问句:
Why do you gravitate towards that particular style of music? 你为什么偏爱那种类型的音乐？
Where do most of the tourists gravitate? 大多数游客都去哪里？


否定句:
He does not gravitate towards conflict. 他不喜欢冲突。
She doesn't gravitate towards the drama. 她不喜欢戏剧。


PHRASE->
gravitate towards: 趋向于，倾向于
gravitate to:  趋向于，倾向于
gravitate around:  围绕…运转
gravitate in:  聚集在
gravitate out:  移出


词源:
The word "gravitating" comes from the verb "gravitate," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "gravitas," meaning "weight" or "heaviness."  The scientific concept of gravity, and its metaphorical extension to attraction or tendency, shaped the modern meaning of the word.
