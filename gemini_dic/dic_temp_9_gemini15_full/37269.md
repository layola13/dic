comity:/ˈkɒməti/ /ˈkɑːməti/| n. |礼让；友好；互敬；社会和谐；(国家间的)友好关系|Courtesy and consideration shown to others; mutual respect.  It often refers to the willingness of independent entities (such as nations or states within a nation) to cooperate and accommodate each other. |例句：International comity requires nations to respect each other's sovereignty. 国际礼让要求各国尊重彼此的主权。

例句：The comity of the Senate allowed for a swift passage of the bill.  参议院的友好合作使得法案迅速通过。


近义词：courtesy, civility, harmony, cooperation, concord


反义词：hostility, discord, antagonism, friction, conflict


词性:名词
