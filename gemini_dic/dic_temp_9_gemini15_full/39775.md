consommé:/ˈkɒnsɒmeɪ/| n. |清汤|A consommé is a rich, clear broth, typically made from meat, poultry, or fish stock, that has been clarified to remove all fat and impurities, resulting in a transparent and flavorful liquid. It is often served as a first course in a formal meal.  |The chef prepared a delicate chicken consommé.  厨师准备了一份精致的鸡清汤。 |broth, bouillon|thick soup|名词
