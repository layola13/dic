cascara:/kæˈskɑːrə/| n. |卡斯卡拉（泻药树皮）|Dried bark of the cascara sagrada tree, used as a laxative.  It is known for its purgative properties and is often used to treat constipation.|例句：The doctor recommended cascara sagrada to relieve her constipation.  医生建议她服用卡斯卡拉 sagrada 来缓解便秘。|例句：She brewed a tea from cascara bark to help with her bowel movements. 她用卡斯卡拉树皮泡茶来帮助排便。|近义词：laxative, purgative |反义词：constipant (causing constipation)
