copulas:/ˈkɒpjʊləz/ | /ˈkɑːpjʊləz/ | n. |系动词| Linking verbs; verbs that connect a subject to a predicate, typically expressing a state of being, rather than an action.  They often link a subject with a subject complement (a word or phrase that describes or identifies the subject). Examples include "be," "seem," "become," "appear," and "remain." |


The sentence "The sky is blue" uses the copula "is" to link the subject "sky" with the subject complement "blue."


The child seems happy. 孩子看起来很开心。

The weather became cold. 天气变冷了。


近义词：linking verbs, predicative verbs | 反义词：action verbs


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a doctor. 他是一名医生。
She seems tired. 她看起来很累。


一般过去时:
They were students. 他们是学生。
The book appeared interesting. 这本书看起来很有趣。


现在完成时:
She has become a successful writer. 她已经成为一名成功的作家。
He has remained silent. 他一直保持沉默。


过去完成时:
The situation had become critical. 局势已经变得危急。
They had been friends for years. 他们已经是多年的朋友了。


将来时:
She will be a teacher. 她将成为一名老师。
The project will seem challenging.  这个项目看起来很有挑战性。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would reconsider. 如果我是你，我会重新考虑。
It would seem unfair to punish him.  惩罚他似乎是不公平的。


被动语态:
The problem was considered serious.  这个问题被认为很严重。
He was seen as a leader. 他被视为领导者。


疑问句:
Is he happy? 他开心吗？
Are they ready?  他们准备好了吗？


否定句:
She is not a singer. 她不是歌手。
The answer was not correct. 答案是不正确的。
