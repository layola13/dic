powerlifting:/ˈpaʊərlɪftɪŋ/| n. |力量举重|The sport of powerlifting involves three lifts: the squat, the bench press, and the deadlift.  Powerlifting is a strength sport that focuses on maximizing the amount of weight lifted in three specific exercises.|例句：He's been training hard for the powerlifting competition. 他一直在为力量举重比赛刻苦训练。

例句：She achieved a personal best in powerlifting today. 她今天在力量举重比赛中取得了个人最佳成绩。|近义词：weightlifting (更广义的重量训练)|反义词：aerobics (有氧运动), endurance training (耐力训练)|词性:名词
