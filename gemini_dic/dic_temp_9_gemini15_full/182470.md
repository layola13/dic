renotifies:/ˌriːˈnoʊtɪfaɪz/| vt.|再次通知，重新通知|To notify again; to give notice a second time.|The company renotified all customers of the price increase. 公司再次通知所有顾客价格上涨。|近义词：re-notify, inform again, remind|反义词：ignore, disregard|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He renotifies his clients of any changes. 他会再次通知他的客户任何变化。
The system renotifies users of important updates. 系统会再次通知用户重要的更新。


一般过去时:
She renotified the team about the meeting time. 她再次通知团队会议时间。
They renotified the participants of the event cancellation. 他们再次通知参与者活动取消。


现在完成时:
He has renotified me about the deadline. 他已经再次通知我截止日期。
The bank has renotified all account holders of the new policy. 银行已经再次通知所有账户持有人新的政策。


过去完成时:
They had renotified the supplier before the shipment. 他们在发货前再次通知了供应商。
She had renotified her boss about the delay before the meeting. 她在会议前再次通知了她的老板关于延误的事情。


将来时:
I will renotify you when the results are available. 结果出来后我会再次通知你。
He will renotify her if there are any further issues. 如果有任何进一步的问题，他会再次通知她。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would renotify him immediately. 如果我是你，我会立即再次通知他。
It is important that he renotify the authorities promptly.  重要的是他应立即再次通知当局。


被动语态:
The customers were renotified of the product recall. 客户再次被通知产品召回。
All employees will be renotified of the new schedule. 所有员工都将被再次通知新的时间表。


疑问句:
Will you renotify me if there are any problems?  如果有什么问题，你会再次通知我吗？
Has he renotified his clients about the delay? 他是否再次通知他的客户关于延误？


否定句:
I did not renotify him about the change in plans. 我没有再次通知他计划的改变。
They will not renotify us unless there is a major issue. 除非出现重大问题，否则他们不会再次通知我们。
