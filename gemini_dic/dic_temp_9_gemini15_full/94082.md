impasting:/ɪmˈpeɪstɪŋ/| n. |调色，涂抹（颜料）|The act of mixing and applying paint or pigment to a surface, often in a thick or textured manner.  It often refers to a technique in painting where colors are blended directly on the canvas, creating a rich, textured surface.|Example:  The impasting of the paint created a striking three-dimensional effect.  调色的颜料创造出令人惊叹的三维效果。|近义词：painting, applying, daubing, smearing|反义词：smoothing, thinning (of paint)|名词
