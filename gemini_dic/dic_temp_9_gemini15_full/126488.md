mytilotoxine:/ˌmaɪtɪloʊˈtɒksɪn/ | n. |贻贝毒素|A lipophilic toxin produced by certain species of dinoflagellates and accumulated in the tissues of shellfish, particularly mussels (Mytilus), causing paralytic shellfish poisoning (PSP) in humans if consumed.|The consumption of mussels containing mytilotoxine can lead to severe illness, even death.|近义词：saxitoxin (a related PSP toxin) |反义词：无
