demilitarised:/ˌdiːˈmɪlɪtəraɪzd/| adj.|非军事化的，解除武装的|  Free from military forces or equipment; having been rendered non-military.  The term implies a deliberate action to remove military presence or capability.| 例句：The demilitarised zone between the two countries remains tense.  两国之间的非军事区仍然紧张。

例句：The island was demilitarised after the war ended.  战争结束后，该岛屿被非军事化。


近义词：disarmed, neutral, unarmed, pacifist

反义词：militarised, armed, fortified


词性:形容词
