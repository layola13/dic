laniard:/ˈlænɪərd/| n.|系索；挂绳；腕带|A lanyard is a cord, strap, or similar length of material worn around the neck or wrist to hold an object, such as an ID card or whistle.  It's often made of a strong material like nylon or leather.| Example: He wore a lanyard around his neck holding his employee ID. 他脖子上挂着一条系索，系着他的员工证。|近义词：cord, strap, tether|反义词：N/A
