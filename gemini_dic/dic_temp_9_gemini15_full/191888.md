scolds:/skoʊldz/| vt.|训斥，责骂|To rebuke or reprimand severely.  |例句：The teacher scolded the student for being late. 老师因学生迟到而训斥了他。|例句：My mother scolded me for breaking the vase.  妈妈因为我打破花瓶而责骂我。|近义词：rebuke, reprimand, chide, berate, upbraid|反义词：praise, commend, compliment|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scolds his children frequently. = 他经常训斥他的孩子。
She scolds the dog for barking. = 她训斥狗乱叫。

一般过去时:
The teacher scolded him for his bad behavior. = 老师因为他的不良行为而训斥了他。
My father scolded me yesterday for my mistakes. = 昨天我父亲因为我的错误而责备了我。

现在完成时:
She has scolded him many times for his laziness. = 她已经多次因为他的懒惰而训斥他了。
He has scolded me for being late again. = 他因为我又迟到而责备了我。


过去完成时:
He had scolded her before she even started to explain. =  在她开始解释之前，他就已经训斥了她。
The manager had scolded the employee before the meeting. = 会议前，经理已经训斥了那个员工。


将来时:
I think he will scold you if you don't finish your work. = 如果你不完成工作，我想他会责备你的。
She will scold him if he breaks the rules again. = 如果他再次违反规定，她会训斥他的。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't scold him so harshly. = 如果我是你，我不会这么严厉地训斥他。
I wish I hadn't scolded her so severely. = 我真希望我没有那么严厉地训斥她。


被动语态:
He was scolded by his boss for his mistake. = 他因为自己的错误而被老板训斥了。
The children were scolded for making a mess. = 孩子们因为弄乱东西而被责骂了。


疑问句:
Did your parents scold you? = 你父母训斥你了吗？
Will the teacher scold us for being noisy? = 老师会因为我们吵闹而训斥我们吗？


否定句:
He didn't scold me this time. = 这次他没有训斥我。
She doesn't usually scold her children. = 她通常不会训斥她的孩子。


PHRASE->
