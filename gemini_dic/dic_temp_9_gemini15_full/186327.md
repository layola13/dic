rhopalium:/roʊˈpeɪliəm/| n. |平衡器，感觉器|A sensory structure found in certain jellyfish (Scyphozoa) and siphonophores, typically containing statocysts for balance and possibly other sensory receptors for light, chemicals, or currents.|The rhopalia of the box jellyfish allow it to detect prey and navigate its environment.|近义词：statocyst (部分功能)|反义词:|无
