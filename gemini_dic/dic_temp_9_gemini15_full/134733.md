nonmechanical: /ˌnɒn.məˈkænɪkl/ | adj. |非机械的；非力学的|Not involving or relating to machinery or mechanics.  The term emphasizes the absence of mechanical processes or devices.  It often highlights the use of other methods, such as manual, biological, or digital processes. |例句：The new system uses a nonmechanical method of controlling temperature. 新系统采用非机械的温度控制方法。

The process is entirely nonmechanical, relying instead on biological processes. 这个过程完全是非机械的，而是依靠生物过程。


近义词：manual, non-automatic, hand-operated,  biological (in certain contexts)


反义词：mechanical, automated, automatic, motorized
