half-conquered: /ˈhæf ˈkɒŋkəd/ | adj. | 半征服的，部分征服的 | Partially conquered;  under partial control.  The adjective describes a situation where a significant portion of a territory or population has been subjugated, but not completely. | 例句：The rebels held onto a few mountain strongholds, leaving the kingdom in a half-conquered state.  = 叛军占据着几座山区要塞，使王国处于半征服状态。

The half-conquered province remained restless under the emperor's rule. = 在皇帝的统治下，这个半征服的省份仍然不安分。


近义词：partially subdued, partly conquered, under subjugation (in part) | 反义词：fully conquered, completely subjugated, liberated, free


词性：形容词
