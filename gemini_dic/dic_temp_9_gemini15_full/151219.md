pare:/pɛər/| vt.|削皮，剥皮，修剪|To remove the skin or outer covering of something, especially fruit or vegetables.  Also, to trim or reduce something.|例句：I need to pare the apples before making the pie. = 我需要在做馅饼之前削苹果皮。

例句：He pared the edges of the photograph to make it fit the frame. = 他修剪了照片的边缘以使其适合相框。


近义词：peel, skin, trim, cut

反义词：add, increase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pares the potatoes every morning. = 他每天早上削土豆皮。
She pares her nails carefully. = 她仔细地修剪指甲。


一般过去时:
I pared the carrots for the stew. = 我为炖菜削了胡萝卜皮。
The gardener pared back the overgrown bushes. = 园丁修剪了过度生长的灌木丛。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

The editor had pared the manuscript down to 500 words. = 编辑已经将手稿压缩到 500 字。
