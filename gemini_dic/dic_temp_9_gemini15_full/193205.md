seducement:/səˈduːsmənt/| n.|诱惑；引诱；煽动|The act or process of seducing; the state of being seduced.  It often implies a tempting or alluring quality that leads to persuasion or enticement, sometimes with negative connotations of manipulation or deception. |例句：His charm and wit were his main instruments of seduction. 他的魅力和机智是他主要的诱惑手段。

例句：The seduction of power led him down a dark path. 权力的诱惑将他引向了一条黑暗的道路。


近义词：temptation, enticement, allurement, lure, blandishment

反义词：repulsion, deterrence, resistance
