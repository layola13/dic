hitchhiker:/ˈhɪtʃˌhaɪkər/| n. |搭便车的人|A person who travels by getting free rides in vehicles, especially by standing at the roadside and thumbing for cars to stop.|He was a hitchhiker, traveling across the country with nothing but a backpack. 他是一个搭便车的人，只带着一个背包横跨全国旅行。|free rider, traveler|car owner, driver|名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hitchhikes across the country every summer. 他每年夏天都搭便车穿越全国。
She hitchhikes to work when her car is broken. 当她的车坏了的时候，她搭便车去上班。

一般过去时:
They hitchhiked to the festival last weekend. 他们上周末搭便车去了节日。
He hitchhiked from London to Paris. 他从伦敦搭便车到巴黎。

现在完成时:
I have hitchhiked across three continents. 我已经搭便车穿越了三大洲。
She has never hitchhiked before. 她以前从未搭便车。

过去完成时:
They had hitchhiked for days before they reached the city. 他们在到达城市之前搭便车走了好几天。
He had never hitchhiked before that trip. 在那次旅行之前他从未搭便车。

将来时:
We will hitchhike to the beach next month. 我们下个月将搭便车去海滩。
She will hitchhike to her grandmother's house. 她将搭便车去她祖母家。

虚拟语气:
If I were brave enough, I would hitchhike around the world. 如果我足够勇敢，我会搭便车环游世界。
I wish I had hitchhiked to that concert instead of driving. 我真希望我当时是搭便车去那个音乐会而不是开车。

被动语态:
Many miles were hitchhiked by him during his summer vacation. 他在暑假搭便车走了很多英里。
The distance can be easily hitchhiked in less than a day. 这段距离很容易在不到一天的时间内搭便车走完。

疑问句:
Have you ever hitchhiked before? 你以前搭过便车吗？
Does he often hitchhike? 他经常搭便车吗？

否定句:
I have never hitchhiked in my life. 我一生中从未搭过便车。
They didn't hitchhike to the game. 他们没有搭便车去看比赛。
