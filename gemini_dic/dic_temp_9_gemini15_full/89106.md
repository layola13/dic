homages:/ˈhɒmɪdʒɪz/| n. |敬意；致敬；效仿|Expressions of respect and admiration, often shown publicly.  It can refer to a work of art, music, literature, etc., created as a tribute to someone or something.  |例句：The film is a homage to classic Hollywood musicals. 这部电影是对经典好莱坞歌舞片的致敬。

The architect's design was a clear homage to Frank Lloyd Wright.这位建筑师的设计明显是对弗兰克·劳埃德·赖特的效仿。


近义词：tribute, tribute, acknowledgment, deference, respect


反义词：disrespect, disregard, scorn, contempt


词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist pays homage to his mentor. =这位艺术家向他的导师致敬。
Many films pay homage to classic cinema. =许多电影都向经典电影致敬。


一般过去时:
The band paid homage to their influences during their concert. =乐队在音乐会上向他们的影响致敬。
The sculpture was a homage to the fallen soldiers. =这座雕塑是为了纪念阵亡士兵而创作的。


现在完成时:
The museum has received many homages to its founder. =博物馆收到了许多对其创始人的敬意。
She has paid homage to her roots in her latest novel. =她在她最新的小说中向她的根源致敬。


过去完成时:
The play had already paid homage to Shakespeare before the director's changes. =在导演修改之前，这部戏剧就已经向莎士比亚致敬了。
The artist had already completed his homage to the master painter. =这位艺术家已经完成了对这位大师画家的致敬作品。


将来时:
The orchestra will pay homage to Beethoven next season. =乐团将在下个季度向贝多芬致敬。
The exhibition will feature several homages to modern art movements. =展览将展出几件向现代艺术运动致敬的作品。


虚拟语气:
If I were a better artist, I would create a homage to my favorite musician. =如果我是一位更好的艺术家，我会创作一件向我最喜欢的音乐家致敬的作品。
I wish I could pay a fitting homage to his legacy. =我希望我能对他留下的遗产表达恰当的敬意。


被动语态:
The late composer was paid a moving homage at the memorial concert. =在纪念音乐会上，已故作曲家受到了感人的致敬。
Many homages are given to the victims of the war each year. =每年都有许多人向战争受害者致敬。


疑问句:
What homage will the writer give in her next book? =这位作家将在她的下一本书中表达什么敬意？
Is this painting a direct homage to another artist's work? =这幅画是对另一位艺术家作品的直接致敬吗？


否定句:
The film did not pay homage to any particular director. =这部电影没有向任何特定的导演致敬。
The artist refused to create a homage to the controversial figure. =这位艺术家拒绝创作一件向有争议的人物致敬的作品。


PHRASE->
a heartfelt homage = 一份衷心的敬意
a subtle homage = 一种含蓄的敬意
a musical homage = 一次音乐上的致敬
an artistic homage = 一次艺术上的致敬
a literary homage =一次文学上的致敬

词源:
源自古法语 homage, 源自拉丁语 hominium  “manhood, service”。最初指的是封建制度下臣民对领主的效忠和服从。 后来引申为对人的尊敬和敬仰，以及向经典作品致敬。
