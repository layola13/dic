petrify:/ˈpɛtrɪfaɪ/ | vt.  |使石化；使惊呆；使僵化 | To turn into stone; to paralyze with fear or astonishment; to make rigid or inflexible. |例句：The sight of the accident petrified me.  目睹事故让我惊呆了。 |例句：Fear petrified her, making her unable to move. 恐惧使她僵住，无法动弹。|近义词：terrify, horrify, paralyze |反义词：enliven, animate, excite |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The intense heat petrifies the wood. = 剧烈的热量使木材石化。
The news petrifies him. = 这个消息让他惊呆了。


一般过去时:
The ancient city was petrified by a volcanic eruption. = 古城被火山爆发石化了。
The sudden announcement petrified the audience. = 突然的宣布使观众惊呆了。


现在完成时:
The fear has petrified her into inaction. = 恐惧使她僵住了，无法行动。
The experience has petrified him; he rarely speaks about it. = 那次经历让他惊恐不已，他很少提及此事。


过去完成时:
The villagers had been petrified by the stories of the ghost. = 村民们已经被鬼故事吓坏了。
He had been petrified by the sight of the accident, unable to react. = 他被事故的景象吓呆了，无法做出反应。


将来时:
The scientist believes that the experiment will petrify the organic matter. = 科学家认为实验将使有机物石化。
I think the upcoming test will petrify many students. = 我认为即将到来的考试会让许多学生紧张害怕。


虚拟语气:
If the lava had reached the town, it would have petrified everything in its path. = 如果熔岩到达城镇，它就会把沿途的一切都石化。
If I had seen that snake, I would have been petrified. = 如果我看到那条蛇，我会被吓呆的。


被动语态:
The ancient remains were petrified over millions of years. = 古代遗迹经过数百万年的时间变成了化石。
The audience was petrified by the unexpected twist in the play.  观众被戏剧中出乎意料的情节发展吓呆了。


疑问句:
Will the intense cold petrify the water? = 剧烈的寒冷会使水结冰吗？（此处petrify指结冰，并非完全符合词义，但可用于此种语境）
Does the thought of public speaking petrify you? = 想到公开演讲是否让你害怕？


否定句:
The heat did not petrify the metal. = 热量并没有使金属石化。
The announcement did not petrify her; she remained calm. = 宣布的消息并没有让她害怕，她依然保持冷静。

petrify someone's blood =  使某人血液凝固(比喻)
