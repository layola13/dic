slosh:/slɒʃ/ /slɑːʃ/| v. n.|晃动；泼洒；溅；(液体)冲击声|To move or cause to move with a splashing sound.  The verb often describes the movement of liquid, but can also be used figuratively.|例句：The water sloshed around in the bucket.  水在桶里晃动。
The waves sloshed against the boat. 海浪拍打着船。|近义词：splash, swash, gurgle|反义词：drip, trickle

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water sloshes in the bottle. = 水在瓶子里晃动。
He sloshes the paint onto the canvas. = 他把颜料泼到画布上。

一般过去时:
The boat sloshed violently in the storm. = 船在暴风雨中剧烈晃动。
She sloshed the leftover soup down the drain. = 她把剩下的汤倒进下水道。

现在完成时:
The rain has sloshed all over the floor. = 雨水溅得到处都是。
The children have sloshed mud all over the carpet. = 孩子们把泥巴溅得到处都是。


过去完成时:
The bucket had sloshed over before I could stop it. = 在我阻止之前，桶里的水已经洒出来了。
They had sloshed the wine all over the tablecloth. = 他们把葡萄酒洒得到处都是。

将来时:
The waves will slosh over the deck in the high tide. = 在涨潮时，海浪会拍打甲板。
I think the milk will slosh if you carry it like that. = 如果你那样拿牛奶，我想牛奶会洒出来。

虚拟语气:
If I had held the cup tighter, the coffee wouldn't have sloshed. = 如果我当时紧紧抓住杯子，咖啡就不会洒了。
I wish the soup hadn't sloshed all over my shirt. = 我真希望汤没有洒在我的衬衫上。

被动语态:
The paint was sloshed carelessly onto the wall. = 油漆被随意地泼到墙上。
The liquid was sloshed from the container. = 液体从容器中溅了出来。

疑问句:
Did the water slosh out of the bottle? = 水从瓶子里洒出来了吗？
Will the drink slosh if I shake the bottle? = 如果我摇晃瓶子，饮料会洒出来吗？

否定句:
The liquid didn't slosh out of the container. = 液体没有从容器中溅出来。
He didn't slosh the water on purpose. = 他不是故意把水泼出来的。


PHRASE->
slosh around:  四处晃动
slosh over: 溢出，洒出
slosh about:  乱泼，乱洒
sloshing sound: 泼洒声
a slosh of something: 一大口，一大滩
