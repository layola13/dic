moralizing:/ˈmɔːrəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |说教；道德说教 |  The act of giving moral lessons or lectures, often in a tedious or preachy manner.  It often implies a judgmental or condescending tone. |例句：His constant moralizing about my lifestyle is getting on my nerves. 他不断地对我的人生方式说教，让我很烦。

例句：She's always moralizing about the importance of honesty. 她总是说教诚实的重要性。


近义词：preaching, lecturing, sermonizing, reproving | 反义词：tolerating, accepting, understanding | 词性:动词
