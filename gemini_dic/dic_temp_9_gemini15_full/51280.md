desynonymization:/ˌdiːsɪˈnɒnɪmaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |去同义词化，消除同义词|The process of removing synonyms from a text or dataset to reduce redundancy and improve clarity.  It aims to represent information using only one term for each concept, eliminating ambiguity caused by multiple synonymous expressions.|例句：The desynonymization of the database significantly improved the accuracy of the search results.  数据库的去同义词化显著提高了搜索结果的准确性。|近义词：standardization, normalization|反义词：synonymization|名词
