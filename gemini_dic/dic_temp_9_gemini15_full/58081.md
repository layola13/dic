droop:/druːp/| vi. n.|下垂；萎靡；沮丧|to bend or hang downwards, or to become weaker or less successful|The flowers drooped in the heat.  花朵在热浪中下垂。|The branches drooped under the weight of the snow. 树枝在雪的重量下下垂。|近义词：sag, slump, wilt|反义词：straighten, perk up|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The leaves droop in the autumn. = 秋天树叶下垂。
His shoulders droop with exhaustion. = 他疲惫地垂下了肩膀。

一般过去时:
The flowers drooped after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，花朵都垂下来了。
Her head drooped in sadness. = 她悲伤地低下了头。

现在完成时:
The plant has drooped since the drought began. = 自从干旱开始以来，植物就一直枯萎下垂。
His spirits have drooped considerably lately. = 最近他的精神状态相当低落。

过去完成时:
The branches had drooped for days before they finally broke. = 树枝在断裂之前已经下垂了好几天了。
He had drooped with fatigue after the long journey. = 长途旅行后，他疲惫不堪地萎靡不振。

将来时:
The flowers will droop if we don't water them. = 如果我们不浇水，花朵就会枯萎下垂。
His energy will droop as the day goes on. = 随着一天的过去，他的精力会下降。

虚拟语气:
If the flowers were watered, they wouldn't droop. = 如果给花浇水，它们就不会下垂了。
If he hadn't been so tired, he wouldn't have drooped so much. = 如果他不那么累，他就不会那么萎靡了。

被动语态:
The plants were drooped by the strong wind. = 植物被强风吹得下垂了。(This usage is less common.)
The leaves are drooped by the lack of sunlight. = 叶子因缺乏阳光而下垂。(This usage is less common.)

疑问句:
Are the flowers drooping? = 花朵下垂了吗？
Is he drooping with illness? = 他是不是病得萎靡不振？

否定句:
The branches didn't droop under the weight of the fruit. = 树枝没有被果实的重量压弯。
His spirits didn't droop despite the setbacks. = 尽管遭遇挫折，他的精神并没有萎靡。
