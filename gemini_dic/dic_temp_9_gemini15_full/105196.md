keyed-in: /kiːd ɪn/ | v. | 键入，输入 | To enter data into a computer or other electronic device using a keyboard.  | Example: I keyed in my password and accessed my account. 我输入了我的密码并访问了我的帐户。 |近义词：input, enter, type | 反义词：output, retrieve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He keys in the data every morning. 他每天早上都输入数据。
She keys in her address on the form. 她在表格上输入她的地址。

一般过去时:
I keyed in the wrong password yesterday. 昨天我输入了错误的密码。
They keyed in all the customer information. 他们输入了所有客户信息。

现在完成时:
She has keyed in all the necessary information. 她已经输入了所有必要的信息。
We have keyed in the data and are ready to analyze it. 我们已经输入了数据，并准备分析它。

过去完成时:
He had keyed in the report before the deadline. 他在截止日期前已经输入了报告。
They had keyed in the addresses before sending the emails. 他们在发送电子邮件之前已经输入了地址。

将来时:
I will key in the data as soon as I receive it. 我一收到数据就会输入。
She will key in the information later this afternoon. 她今天下午晚些时候会输入信息。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would key in the data myself. 如果我有时间，我会自己输入数据。
It's important that she key in the correct information. 重要的是她要输入正确的信息。

被动语态:
The data was keyed in by the assistant. 数据是由助理输入的。
The information is being keyed in right now. 信息正在被输入。

疑问句:
Have you keyed in all the details? 你已经输入所有细节了吗？
Did he key in the correct address? 他输入正确的地址了吗？

否定句:
I didn't key in the correct information. 我没有输入正确的信息。
She hasn't keyed in the data yet. 她还没有输入数据。
