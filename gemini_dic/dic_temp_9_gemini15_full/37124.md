combustive:/kəmˈbʌstɪv/| adj.|易燃的；易爆的；激烈的|easily ignited or burned; tending to cause combustion; characterized by or tending to cause sudden outbursts of anger or violence.  |例句：The old building contained combustive materials, posing a significant fire risk. 这座旧建筑物含有易燃材料，存在重大的火灾风险。

例句：His combustive personality often led to arguments and conflicts. 他易怒的性格经常导致争吵和冲突。|近义词：flammable, inflammable, explosive, volatile|反义词：non-combustible, fire-resistant, stable

词源:  From Latin *combūrere "to burn completely," from com- "completely" + *ūrere "to burn." The suffix "-ive" indicates a tendency or capacity.  The word evolved through Old French and Middle English.
