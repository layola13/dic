incinerations:/ɪnˌsɪnəˈreɪʃənz/| n. |焚烧；火葬 |The act of incinerating something; the process of burning something completely.  It often refers to the disposal of waste or bodies. |例句：The city is considering new regulations on incinerations to reduce air pollution.  这个城市正在考虑关于焚烧的新规以减少空气污染。

例句：The funeral included a traditional cremation and incinerations of the deceased's personal belongings. 葬礼包括传统的火化和死者个人物品的焚烧。

近义词：cremation, burning, combustion |反义词：preservation, conservation
