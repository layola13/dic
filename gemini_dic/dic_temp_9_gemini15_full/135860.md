nonprotection:/ˌnɒnprəˈtɛkʃən/ | n. | 非保护；缺乏保护 | The lack of protection or defense against something.  It refers to a state or condition where something is not protected or shielded from harm, damage, or risk. | Example: The nonprotection of endangered species led to their rapid decline.  非保护濒危物种导致它们迅速减少。 The company's nonprotection of its intellectual property resulted in significant losses. 公司对其知识产权的缺乏保护导致了巨大的损失。 | 近义词：lack of protection, vulnerability, exposure | 反义词：protection, safeguard, defense | 名词
