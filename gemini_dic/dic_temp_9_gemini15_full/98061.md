inquests:/ˈɪŋkwɛsts/| n.|死因调查；调查；探究|An inquest is a judicial inquiry into the circumstances of a death, especially one that is sudden, violent, or of unknown cause.  It aims to determine the cause and manner of death.  Inquests are typically conducted by a coroner or medical examiner.|例句：The inquest into the death of the young man revealed that he died from a drug overdose. 死因调查显示这位年轻男子死于药物过量。

The coroner held an inquest to determine the cause of death. 验尸官进行了死因调查以确定死因。|近义词：inquiry, investigation, inquiry, examination|反义词：conclusion, determination(in the sense of a definitive answer)|名词
