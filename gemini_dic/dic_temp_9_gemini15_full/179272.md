redistribution:/ˌriːdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən/ | n. | 再分配，重新分配 | The act or process of distributing something again, especially wealth, income, or resources, in a different way.  It often implies a more equitable or fairer distribution than existed previously.  | Example: The government announced a policy of wealth redistribution to reduce inequality.  政府宣布了一项财富再分配政策以减少不平等。

The government's redistribution of land led to increased agricultural productivity.政府对土地的重新分配导致农业生产力提高。 | 近义词：reallocation, realignment, apportionment | 反义词：concentration, monopolization | 名词
