experimenters:/ɪkˈsperɪmɛntərz/| n. |实验者，试验者 |Those who conduct experiments; people involved in experimentation.  They are actively engaged in designing, performing, and analyzing experiments. |The experimenters carefully documented their findings. 实验者仔细记录了他们的发现。|researchers, investigators, scientists|observers, participants (in a broader sense)|名词
