passing-through: /ˈpæsɪŋ θruː/ | adj. |路过的；途经的；短暂停留的 |  Describes something or someone that is temporarily in a place while traveling to another.  It emphasizes the transient nature of the presence. | 例句：He's just passing-through on his way to California. 他只是路过加州的途中。  The birds are passing-through on their migration.  这些鸟在迁徙途中经过这里。|近义词：transient, temporary, in transit |反义词：permanent, resident, settled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are passing through the town. = 他们正在经过这个城镇。
The train is passing through many stations. = 火车正在经过许多车站。


一般过去时:
We passed through the forest. = 我们穿过了森林。
The army passed through the village. = 军队经过了村庄。


现在完成时:
We have passed through many difficulties. = 我们已经克服了许多困难。(Here, "pass through" means to experience and overcome.)
She has passed through several countries during her travels. = 在她的旅行中，她已经经过了几个国家。


过去完成时:
They had passed through the checkpoint before the alarm sounded. = 警报响起之前，他们已经通过了检查站。
The ship had passed through the storm before reaching calm waters. = 船在到达平静水域之前已经度过了风暴。


将来时:
We will pass through the mountains tomorrow. = 我们明天将穿越山脉。
The comet will pass through the solar system. = 这颗彗星将穿过太阳系。


虚拟语气:
If we passed through that town, we would see the old church. = 如果我们经过那个城镇，我们会看到那座古老的教堂。
I wish we had passed through the city center instead of taking the highway. = 我希望我们走的是市中心而不是高速公路。


被动语态:
The bill was passed through parliament. = 该法案已通过议会。
The goods are passed through customs. = 货物正在通过海关。


疑问句:
Did they pass through the security check? = 他们通过了安全检查吗？
Will the pipeline pass through this environmentally sensitive area? = 管道会穿过这个环境敏感区吗？


否定句:
The proposal did not pass through the committee. = 该提案未获委员会通过。
We didn't pass through the village; we went around it. = 我们没有经过村庄，我们绕过了它。
