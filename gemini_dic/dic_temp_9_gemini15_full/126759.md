nanites:/ˈnænˌaɪts/| n.|纳米机器人，微型机器人|Extremely small robots, typically microscopic, used in nanotechnology.  They are often depicted in science fiction as being able to perform complex tasks at a molecular level.|例句：The scientists are developing nanites to repair damaged tissues. 科学家们正在研制用于修复受损组织的纳米机器人。

例句：In the science fiction novel, the protagonist used nanites to fight off a deadly virus. 在这部科幻小说中，主人公利用纳米机器人对抗致命病毒。
