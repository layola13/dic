loses:/ˈluːzɪz/| v.|失去；输掉；松脱|To be deprived of or cease to possess (something).  To fail to win (a game, contest, etc.). To become loose or detached.|例句：He loses his temper easily. 他很容易发脾气。


例句：The team loses the game.  球队输掉了比赛。


近义词：miss, forfeit, drop, shed


反义词：wins, gains, keeps, retains


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He loses his keys every week. = 他每周都丢钥匙。
She loses her patience easily. = 她很容易失去耐心。


一般过去时:
I lost my wallet yesterday. = 我昨天丢了钱包。
They lost the match last night. = 他们昨晚输掉了比赛。


现在完成时:
She has lost her job. = 她丢了工作。
He has lost a lot of weight recently. = 他最近瘦了很多。


过去完成时:
I had lost my hope before he arrived. = 他来之前我就已经失去了希望。
They had lost all their money by the end of the year. = 到年底他们已经输光了所有的钱。


将来时:
I will lose my mind if this keeps happening. = 如果这种情况持续发生，我会疯掉的。
We will lose the election if we don't work harder. = 如果我们不更加努力，我们将输掉选举。


虚拟语气:
If I lost my job, I don't know what I would do. = 如果我丢了工作，我不知道我会做什么。
I wish I hadn't lost my keys. = 我真希望我没有丢了钥匙。


被动语态:
The game was lost by the home team. = 主队输掉了比赛。(比赛被主队输掉了)
The battle was lost due to lack of supplies. = 由于缺乏补给，战役以失败告终。(战役由于缺乏补给而输掉了)


疑问句:
Does he often lose his way? = 他经常迷路吗？
Do you lose your temper easily? = 你容易发脾气吗？


否定句:
He doesn't lose his cool easily. 他不容易失去冷静。
She didn't lose hope even after many failures. = 即使经历多次失败，她也没有失去希望。


PHRASE->
lose oneself in = 沉浸于...
lose one's way = 迷路
lose touch with = 与...失去联系
lose one's head = 慌张；失去理智
lose face = 丢脸
