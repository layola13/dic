ignobly:/ɪɡˈnoʊbli/ | adj. | 不光彩地；卑鄙地；可耻地 |  Shamefully dishonorable; meanly.  It suggests a lack of honor or glory, often implying a sense of disgrace or degradation. The act itself might not be inherently evil, but the manner in which it's carried out or the motivations behind it are dishonorable. | 例句：He died ignominiously in a drunken brawl. 他死于一场醉酒斗殴中，死得很不光彩。

He ignominiously surrendered without a fight. 他不战而降，非常可耻。


近义词：disgracefully, shamefully, dishonorably, meanly, basely | 反义词：gloriously, honorably, nobly, splendidly
