perpetuations:/ˌpɜːrpɛtʃuˈeɪʃənz/| n.|  持续；永久化；沿袭 | The perpetuation of these myths is harmful.  这些神话的持续流传是有害的。|近义词：continuation, preservation, maintenance |反义词：cessation, termination, extinction| 名词


英文解析：The act or process of perpetuating something; the continuation or prolongation of something.  It often refers to the continuation of something undesirable, such as a harmful practice or belief.


例句：

The perpetuation of poverty in this region is a complex problem requiring multifaceted solutions.  这个地区贫困的持续是一个复杂的问题，需要多方面的解决方案。

The museum is dedicated to the perpetuation of local art and culture. 博物馆致力于当地艺术和文化的传承。


词源：

From Middle English *perpetuacioun, from Old French perpetuacion, from Latin perpetuatio, from perpetuus ("continuous, perpetual").
