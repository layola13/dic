endangering:/ɪnˈdeɪndʒərɪŋ/| v. |危及；使遭受危险|  The act of putting something or someone in danger.  It is the present participle form of the verb "endanger".|

例句：

Deforestation is endangering many species of animals.  森林砍伐正在危及许多动物物种。

Pollution from the factory is endangering the health of local residents.  工厂的污染正在危及当地居民的健康。


近义词：threatening, jeopardizing, imperiling, risking

反义词：protecting, safeguarding, securing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The factory is endangering the local water supply. 工厂正在危及当地的水源供应。
Hunting is endangering the tiger population. 狩猎正在危及老虎的数量。


一般过去时:
The storm endangered the lives of many people. 暴风雨危及了许多人的生命。
His reckless driving endangered the safety of others. 他鲁莽的驾驶危及了其他人的安全。


现在完成时:
Human activity has endangered many plant and animal species. 人类活动已经危及了许多动植物物种。
The prolonged drought has endangered the crops. 持续的干旱已经危及了庄稼。


过去完成时:
They had endangered the mission by revealing their location. 他们暴露了自己的位置，从而危及了任务。
The company had endangered its reputation through unethical practices. 公司通过不道德的行为危及了自己的声誉。


将来时:
Climate change will endanger coastal communities. 气候变化将危及沿海社区。
Continued pollution will endanger the ecosystem. 持续的污染将危及生态系统。


虚拟语气:
If we continue to pollute the environment, we will be endangering future generations. 如果我们继续污染环境，我们将危及子孙后代。
I wish we hadn't endangered the project with our poor planning. 我真希望我们没有因为糟糕的计划而危及这个项目。


被动语态:
Many species are being endangered by habitat loss. 许多物种正因栖息地丧失而濒临灭绝。
The project was endangered by a lack of funding. 该项目因缺乏资金而面临危险。


疑问句:
Is the new dam endangering the local wildlife? 新建的大坝是否危及当地的野生动物？
Are our actions endangering the planet? 我们的行为是否正在危及地球？


否定句:
The government is not endangering the environment. 政府没有危及环境。
We did not endanger the children during the evacuation. 我们在疏散过程中没有危及儿童的安全。


PHRASE->

endangering species 危及物种
endangering public health 危及公共卫生
endangering the environment 危及环境
endangering national security 危及国家安全
endangering the peace 危及和平


词源:

The word "endangering" comes from the verb "endanger," which is formed from the prefix "en-" (meaning "to put into a state of") and the noun "danger."  The suffix "-ing" indicates the present participle form, signifying an ongoing action.
