reprised:/rɪˈpraɪzd/| v.|重演；重播；重新使用|To repeat a performance, especially a musical or theatrical one, or to repeat something from the past.  It can also mean to reuse something. |例句：The orchestra reprised their most popular piece.  乐团重奏了他们最受欢迎的作品。

The company reprised its successful marketing campaign. 公司重新使用了他们成功的营销活动。|近义词：repeated, reiterated, restaged, replayed |反义词：omitted, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reprises his role as Hamlet. 他重演哈姆雷特这个角色。
The singer reprises the chorus. 歌手重唱了合唱部分。


一般过去时:
She reprised her award-winning performance. 她重演了她获奖的表演。
The band reprised a song from their first album. 乐队重演了他们第一张专辑里的一首歌。


现在完成时:
They have reprised several of their old hits. 他们已经重演了几首他们的老歌。
The director has reprised the same theme in his new film. 导演在他的新电影中再次使用了同样的主题。


过去完成时:
He had reprised the role many times before. 他以前多次扮演过这个角色。
The play had reprised its successful run in London. 这部戏在伦敦已经成功上演过多次了。


将来时:
They will reprise the play next season. 他们将在下个季度重新上演这部戏。
