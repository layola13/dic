estimating:/ˈɛstɪmeɪtɪŋ/| v.|估计；估算；评价|To form an approximate judgment or opinion regarding the worth, amount, size, weight, etc., of something.|The contractor is estimating the cost of the new building. 承包商正在估算新建筑的成本。  We are estimating that the project will take about six months. 我们估计这个项目大约需要六个月。|calculating, appraising, assessing|underestimating, overestimating|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is estimating the project costs. 他正在估算项目成本。
They estimate the completion date to be next month. 他们估计完工日期是下个月。


一般过去时:
She estimated the value of the painting to be around $10,000. 她估计这幅画的价值大约为1万美元。
The engineer estimated the bridge's load-bearing capacity. 工程师估算了桥梁的承载能力。


现在完成时:
They have estimated the damage caused by the storm. 他们已经估算了风暴造成的损失。
We have estimated the number of attendees to be around 500. 我们估计参加者人数大约为500人。


过去完成时:
They had estimated the budget before the project started. 项目开始前，他们已经估算了预算。
She had estimated the time required for the journey. 她已经估算了旅程所需的时间。


将来时:
He will estimate the repair costs tomorrow. 他明天将估算修理费用。
They will estimate the impact of the new policy on the market. 他们将评估新政策对市场的影响。


虚拟语气:
If I had estimated the time correctly, I would have arrived on time. 如果我正确地估计了时间，我就会准时到达。
I wish I had estimated the difficulty of the task more accurately. 我希望我更准确地估计了任务的难度。


被动语态:
The cost of the project was estimated to be $5 million. 该项目的成本估计为500万美元。
The damage was estimated at over $100,000. 损失估计超过10万美元。


疑问句:
How do you estimate the time needed? 你如何估计所需时间？
How did they estimate the population of the city? 他们是如何估计城市人口的？


否定句:
He did not estimate the risks accurately. 他没有准确地估计风险。
They didn't estimate the project would take so long. 他们没有估计到项目会花费这么长时间。
