nirvana:/nɪrˈvɑːnə/| n. |涅槃；极乐；解脱|A state of perfect peace and happiness, often associated with Buddhism;  a state free from suffering, desire, and the cycle of rebirth.  In popular culture, it can also refer to a state of blissful euphoria.|例句：After years of searching, he finally found nirvana in meditation.  多年寻找后，他最终在冥想中找到了涅槃。|例句：The music transported her to a state of nirvana.  音乐把她带入了一种涅槃的境界。|近义词：bliss, enlightenment, ecstasy, serenity|反义词：suffering, misery, torment, anguish|名词
