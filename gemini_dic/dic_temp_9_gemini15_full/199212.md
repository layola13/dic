silkiness:/ˈsɪlkiːnəs/| n. |丝滑；柔滑；光滑|The smooth, soft, and glossy quality of silk; a similar quality in something else.  It refers to a pleasant, smooth texture, often associated with silk but applicable to other materials or substances.|例句：The silkiness of her hair was remarkable.  她的头发非常丝滑。|例句：The cream gave my skin a lovely silkiness.  这款面霜让我的皮肤变得非常柔滑。|近义词：smoothness, softness, slipperiness|反义词：roughness, coarseness, harshness
