gabardine:/ˈɡæbərdiːn/ | n. adj. |斜纹布；斜纹呢；防水衣 |A strong, closely woven fabric, typically made of cotton, wool, or synthetic fibers, with a diagonal twill weave. It's known for its water-resistant properties and is often used in outerwear. |例句：Her gabardine raincoat kept her dry in the downpour.  她的斜纹布雨衣让她在倾盆大雨中保持干燥。 |例句：The suit was made of a fine quality gabardine. 这套西装是用优质斜纹布制作的。|近义词：twill, serge|反义词：暂无
