occasionally:/əˈkeɪʒənəli/ | adv. | 偶尔地，间或地 |  Sometimes, but not often or regularly.  |例句：I occasionally go to the gym. = 我偶尔去健身房。

例句：We occasionally have dinner at that restaurant. = 我们偶尔在那家餐厅吃饭。


近义词：sometimes, now and then, from time to time, infrequently, sporadically

反义词：always, constantly, regularly, frequently


词源:  Comes from the word "occasion," meaning a particular time or circumstance. The suffix "-ally" transforms it into an adverb, indicating manner or frequency.  The word's roots trace back to the Latin word "occasio," meaning "a falling, a chance, an opportunity."


USAGE->

They occasionally visit their grandparents. = 他们偶尔去看望他们的祖父母。

被动语态:
