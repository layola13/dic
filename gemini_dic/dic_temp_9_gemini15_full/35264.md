cluster:/ˈklʌstər/ | n. v. |丛;群;簇;聚集;群集 |A group of things of the same kind that are close together.  |例句：A cluster of grapes hung from the vine.  一串葡萄挂在藤上。

例句：The stars clustered together in the night sky.  星星在夜空中聚集在一起。


近义词：group, bunch, clump, aggregation | 反义词：scatter, disperse | 词性:名词，动词
