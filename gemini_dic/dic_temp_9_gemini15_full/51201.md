destine:/ˈdɛstɪn/|/ˈdɛstɪn/| vt. |注定；预定；指定；使具有特定用途|to be certain to happen, or to cause something to be certain to happen; to intend or plan something for a particular purpose.|The company is destined for great things. 这家公司注定会取得巨大的成就。|She was destined to become a famous actress. 她注定要成为一名著名的女演员。|近义词：intend, appoint, ordain |反义词：avoid, prevent


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He destines his fortune to charity. 他把他的财产捐献给慈善机构。
They destine this land for a new park. 他们将这块土地指定为新的公园。

一般过去时:
She destined her son for a military career. 她为儿子安排了从军的事业。
The king destined the throne for his eldest son. 国王将王位指定给他的长子。

现在完成时:
The project has been destined for success from the start. 这个项目从一开始就注定要成功。
They have destined their lives to helping others. 他们把一生都奉献给了帮助他人。

过去完成时:
It had been destined that they would meet again. 他们注定要再次相遇。
He had destined the painting for his daughter's wedding. 他把这幅画预定给了他女儿的婚礼。

将来时:
The company will be destined for bankruptcy if it continues to lose money. 如果公司继续亏损，它将注定要破产。
They will destine their savings for their children's education. 他们将把积蓄用于孩子的教育。

虚拟语气:
If he had studied harder, he might have been destined for a better future. 如果他学习更努力，他可能会拥有更好的未来。
I wish I had been destined for a life of adventure. 我希望我注定要过冒险的生活。

被动语态:
The land was destined for development. 这块土地被指定用于开发。
He was destined to inherit the family business. 他注定要继承家族企业。

疑问句:
Is this package destined for overseas? 这个包裹是发往国外的吗？
What is this tool destined for? 这个工具是用来做什么的？

否定句:
This plan was not destined to succeed. 这个计划注定不会成功。
They did not destine the money for that purpose. 他们没有把钱用于那个目的。
