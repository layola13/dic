inland:/ˈɪnlænd/| adj. n. | 内地的；内陆的；国内的 | Situated or occurring in the interior of a country, rather than on the coast.  |例句：The inland cities are less affected by the hurricane. 内陆城市受飓风的影响较小。
例句：They took an inland route to avoid the coastal traffic. 他们选择内陆路线以避免沿海交通拥堵。

近义词：domestic, interior, internal | 反义词：coastal, offshore, maritime
