entryway:/ˈɛntriweɪ/| n. |入口，门厅，过道 |An entryway is the area at the entrance to a building or room. It is often a short hallway or passage leading to other rooms.  It can also refer to the entrance itself. |例句：The entryway to the house was quite grand, with a large oak door and marble flooring.  这栋房子的入口非常宏伟，有着一扇巨大的橡木门和大理石地板。

The entryway was cluttered with coats and shoes.  门厅里堆满了外套和鞋子。

|近义词：entrance, doorway, vestibule, foyer |反义词：exit, departure
