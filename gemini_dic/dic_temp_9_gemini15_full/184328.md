respecter:/rɪˈspɛktər/| n. |尊重者，尊敬者 |A respecter is someone who shows respect to others.  They value the rights, feelings, and opinions of others.  They often demonstrate politeness, consideration, and deference in their interactions.|例句：He is a respecter of tradition and always follows established customs. 他是一个尊重传统的人，总是遵循既定的习俗。|近义词：admirer, venerator, honorer |反义词：insulter, abuser, despiser |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He respects his elders. = 他尊敬他的长辈。
She respects the law. = 她遵守法律。

一般过去时:
They respected his wishes. = 他们尊重了他的愿望。
We respected her privacy. = 我们尊重了她的隐私。

现在完成时:
I have always respected her opinions. = 我一直很尊重她的意见。
He has respected his commitments. = 他一直信守承诺。

过去完成时:
She had respected his authority before the incident. = 在事件发生前，她一直尊重他的权威。
We had respected their decision until recently. = 直到最近，我们一直尊重他们的决定。

将来时:
They will respect the outcome of the election. = 他们将会尊重选举的结果。
I will respect your decision, whatever it may be. = 无论你的决定是什么，我都会尊重。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would respect his judgment. = 如果我是你，我会尊重他的判断。
I wish they respected my feelings. = 我希望他们尊重我的感受。

被动语态:
His work is respected worldwide. = 他的作品在全世界受到尊重。
Her opinions are respected by her colleagues. = 她的意见受到同事们的尊重。

疑问句:
Do you respect your teachers? = 你尊重你的老师吗？
Does he respect other people's beliefs? = 他尊重别人的信仰吗？

否定句:
They didn't respect the rules. = 他们没有遵守规则。
She doesn't respect people who lie. = 她不尊重说谎的人。
