electroplated:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌpleɪtɪd/| adj.| 电镀的；镀层的 | Covered with a thin layer of metal deposited by electrolysis.  Electroplating is a process that uses an electric current to coat a metal object with a thin layer of another metal. This process enhances the object's appearance, durability, and corrosion resistance.| The jewelry is electroplated with gold. 这件首饰是镀金的。|近义词：plated, coated |反义词：unplated
