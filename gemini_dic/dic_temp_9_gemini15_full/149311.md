/pæks/ | n. | 包装；包裹；一包；(尤指)纸盒包装 | A container, typically made of cardboard, used for packaging goods.  | Example:  He carried a six-pack of beer in a brown paper pac. 他提着一包六听装啤酒，用的是棕色牛皮纸包装。|近义词：package, bundle, container | 反义词：unpack, unwrap | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory packs thousands of boxes every day. = 工厂每天包装数千个盒子。
This company packs its products carefully. = 这家公司仔细包装其产品。

一般过去时:
She packed her suitcase for the trip. = 她为旅行打包行李。
They packed the fragile items with great care. = 他们非常小心地包装易碎物品。

现在完成时:
I have packed all my things. = 我已经打包好所有东西了。
They have packed the order and it's ready for shipping. = 他们已经打包好订单，准备发货了。

过去完成时:
He had packed his bag before the alarm went off. = 警报响起前他已经打包好行李了。
We had packed the lunches before the children woke up. = 孩子们醒来之前，我们已经准备好午餐了。

将来时:
I will pack my bags tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上会打包行李。
They will pack the goods and ship them next week. = 他们下周会打包货物并发货。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pack more carefully. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更仔细地打包。
I wish I had packed a warmer coat. = 我真希望我带了件更暖和的外套。

被动语态:
The boxes are packed by machine. = 箱子是由机器包装的。
The goods were packed securely for transport. = 货物为运输而被安全地包装。

疑问句:
Are the gifts packed yet? = 礼物包装好了吗？
Have they packed the food for the picnic? = 他们为野餐打包食物了吗？

否定句:
I haven't packed my lunch yet. = 我还没打包午餐。
They didn't pack enough food for the journey. = 他们为旅途准备的食物不够多。


PHRASE->
pack a punch:  有影响力; 震撼力 (Example:  His speech really packed a punch. 他的演讲真的很有影响力。)
pack it in:  停止；放弃 (Example:  Let's pack it in for the day.  我们今天就到此为止吧。)
pack one's bags:  打包行李 (Example: It's time to pack your bags and leave. 是时候收拾行李离开了。)
pack a lunch:  准备午餐便当 (Example:  Remember to pack a lunch for school. 记得为上学准备午餐。)
pack a suitcase:  打包行李箱 (Example: He carefully packed his suitcase for the trip. 他仔细地为旅行打包行李箱。)
