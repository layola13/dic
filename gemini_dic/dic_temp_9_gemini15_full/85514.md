hasting:/ˈheɪstɪŋ/| n. |匆忙；急速；急速行动|The act of hastening; speed; urgency.  It often implies a lack of deliberation or carefulness.|例句：His hastening to the airport caused him to miss his flight. 他匆忙赶往机场导致错过了航班。


例句：The hastening of the project's deadline caused considerable stress.  项目截止日期的提前导致了相当大的压力。|近义词：hurry, rush, speed, acceleration |反义词：delay, procrastination, slowness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hastens his steps. = 他加快脚步。
She hastens to complete the task. = 她赶紧完成任务。

一般过去时:
He hastened his pace. = 他加快了步伐。
They hastened to help the victim. = 他们赶紧去帮助受害者。

现在完成时:
He has hastened through his work. = 他仓促完成了他的工作。
She has hastened to inform us of the news. = 她赶紧通知我们这个消息。

过去完成时:
He had hastened to the scene before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他已经匆忙赶到了现场。
They had hastened to finish the project before the deadline. = 他们已经匆忙赶在截止日期前完成了项目。

将来时:
He will hasten to catch the train. = 他将赶紧赶火车。
She will hasten to respond to your email. = 她将很快回复你的邮件。

虚拟语气:
If he hastened, he would have caught the bus. = 如果他快点，他就能赶上公共汽车。
I wish I had hastened to help her. = 我真希望我当时赶紧去帮她。

被动语态:
The process was hastened by the new technology. = 新技术加快了这个过程。
The decision was hastened by the urgency of the situation. = 情况紧急，所以决定被加快了。

疑问句:
Why did he hasten his departure? = 他为什么匆忙离开？
Will they hasten the construction? = 他们会加快建设速度吗？

否定句:
He did not hasten his pace. = 他没有加快步伐。
They did not hasten to answer the questions. = 他们没有急于回答问题。
