doublespeak:/ˈdʌbəlspiːk/| n. |双重言语；含糊其词；官方谎言|Language deliberately used to disguise, distort, or mislead. It often involves euphemisms, jargon, and ambiguity to obscure the truth or present a false impression.  |Example: The government's doublespeak about the war made it difficult to understand the true situation. 政府关于战争的双重说法使得难以了解真实情况。|近义词：euphemism, jargon, obfuscation, circumlocution|反义词：frankness, transparency, clarity
