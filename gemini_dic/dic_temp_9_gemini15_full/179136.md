redia:/ˈriːdiə/| n. | 胞蚴 | A larval stage in the life cycle of certain parasitic flatworms (trematodes), particularly those of the family Digenea.  It is characterized by its elongated, sac-like body containing developing cercariae. | Example: The redia develops within the snail intermediate host and gives rise to numerous cercariae.  胞蚴在蜗牛中间宿主体内发育，并产生许多尾蚴。 | 近义词: cercaria (although a different stage) | 反义词:  None directly applicable.  | 词性:noun
