pastils:/ˈpæstɪlz/| n. |锭剂；药丸；小药片|A pastil is a small, medicated lozenge or tablet, often used to relieve sore throats or coughs.  They are typically hard and dissolve slowly in the mouth.|Example:  She sucked on a menthol pastil to soothe her sore throat. 她含了一颗薄荷锭剂来缓解喉咙痛。|近义词：lozenge, tablet, troche|反义词：liquid medicine|名词
