quasi-emotional:/ˈkwɑːziˌɪmoʊʃənəl/| adj. |似情感的，准情感的 | Seemingly or partly emotional; having some but not all the characteristics of emotion.  It suggests an appearance or semblance of emotion, but lacks the full depth or authenticity of genuine feeling.  The term often implies a superficial or artificial display of emotion. | Example: His quasi-emotional speech lacked genuine conviction.  他的似情感的演讲缺乏真正的说服力。|近义词：pseudo-emotional, affected, simulated |反义词：genuine, authentic, heartfelt
