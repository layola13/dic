re-harvesting: /ˌriːˈhɑːrvɪstɪŋ/ | v. | 再收获；二次收获 | The act of harvesting a crop or resource a second time, or repeatedly.  It often implies a sustainable or cyclical process, as opposed to a single harvest.  |例句：The farmer is re-harvesting the rainwater for irrigation. 这位农民正在再次收集雨水用于灌溉。

The community is exploring re-harvesting greywater for non-potable uses. 社区正在探索将灰水再利用于非饮用水用途。


近义词：secondary harvesting, repeated harvesting,  reclaiming (in some contexts) | 反义词：initial harvesting, first harvest, depletion (in some contexts) | 词性: verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-harvest the rainwater every year. = 他们每年都再次收集雨水。
Farmers re-harvest the fields after the initial harvest. = 农民在第一次收割后会再次收割田地。


一般过去时:
We re-harvested the crops last week. = 我们上周再次收割了庄稼。

虚拟语气:
