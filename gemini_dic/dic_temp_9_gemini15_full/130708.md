nonalcoholics:/ˌnɒnˌælkəˈhɒlɪks/| n. |不喝酒的人；戒酒者|People who do not drink alcoholic beverages.  This term often refers to individuals who abstain from alcohol for various reasons, including personal choice, health concerns, religious beliefs, or recovery from alcohol addiction.|The support group is designed for nonalcoholics and their families.|近义词：teetotalers, abstainers|反义词：alcoholics
