cozied:/ˈkoʊziːd/| v. |使舒适；使温暖；使温馨；变得舒适|To make or become comfortable and cozy.  The verb "cozy" is less common than its adjective form.|例句：She cozied up to the fire. 她靠近火取暖。

She cozied up to her boyfriend on the couch.  她依偎在她男朋友身边坐在沙发上。


近义词： snuggled, nestled, warmed, comforted |反义词： chilled, uncomfortable, exposed |词性：动词
