improvidentially:/ˌɪmˌprɒvɪˈdɛnʃəli/ | adv. | 不顾后果地，轻率地，欠考虑地 | Acting without foresight or prudence; rashly.  |  例句：He improvidentially invested all his savings in a risky venture. 他不顾后果地把所有积蓄都投资到一个风险很大的项目中。  |  例句：They improvidentially decided to cross the river without checking the depth. 他们轻率地决定过河，而没有检查水深。 | 近义词：recklessly, rashly, heedlessly, imprudently | 反义词：prudently, cautiously, wisely, carefully |
