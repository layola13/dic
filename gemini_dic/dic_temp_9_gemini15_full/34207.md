clandestinely:/ˌklændˈɛstɪnli/| adv.|秘密地，偷偷地|done secretly or privately, especially because the action is illegal or improper.  It implies concealment and often suggests a sense of wrongdoing or deception.|例句：They met clandestinely in a back alley. 他们在后巷秘密会面。

例句：The government clandestinely supported the rebels. 政府秘密支持叛军。


近义词：secretly, secretly, furtively, stealthily, covertly


反义词：openly, publicly, overtly


词性：副词
