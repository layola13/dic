perestroika:/ˌpɛrəˈstrɔɪkə/| n. |改革，重组|The policy of political and economic reforms implemented in the Soviet Union in the late 1980s.  It aimed to restructure the Soviet system without abandoning communism.  The reforms ultimately failed, contributing to the collapse of the Soviet Union.| The Gorbachev era was marked by perestroika and glasnost.  戈尔巴乔夫时代以改革和公开性为标志。| restructuring, reorganization, reform | stagnation, repression | 名词
