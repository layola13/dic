emendation:/ˌɛmənˈdeɪʃən/| n. | 修正；校订；改进 | The act of correcting or improving a text or other work.  It often involves making small changes to improve accuracy, clarity, or style.  | Example: The emendation of the manuscript took several months.  校订这份手稿花了几个月时间。 |近义词：correction, improvement, revision | 反义词：deterioration, worsening
