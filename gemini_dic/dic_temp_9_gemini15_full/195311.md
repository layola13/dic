semijudicial:/ˌsɛmiːdʒuˈdɪʃəl/| adj.|半司法性质的，准司法的|Relating to or having the characteristics of both judicial and non-judicial processes.  It suggests a process that shares some features with a formal court but lacks its full authority or formality.|例句：The disciplinary committee held a semi-judicial hearing to determine the student's fate.  该纪律委员会举行了一次准司法听证会来决定学生的命运。 The arbitration process is often described as semi-judicial. 仲裁过程通常被描述为半司法性质的。|近义词：quasi-judicial, quasi-legal|反义词：judicial, fully legal
