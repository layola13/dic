slipthrift:/ˈslɪpθrɪft/| n. adj.|挥霍者；浪费者；浪费的；不经济的|A slipthrift is a person who spends money carelessly and wastefully.  They are often irresponsible with their finances and tend to squander their resources.  The word can also be used as an adjective to describe something that is wasteful or uneconomical.|例句：He was a notorious slipthrift, always buying expensive gadgets he didn't need. 他是个臭名昭著的挥霍者，总是买些他不需要的昂贵的小玩意儿。


The old house was falling apart; it had been allowed to slipthrift for years. 这栋老房子快要倒塌了；多年来它一直无人打理，任其荒废。|近义词：spendthrift, prodigal, wasteful, improvident|反义词：thrifty, frugal, economical, saving
