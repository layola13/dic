daddock:/ˈdædək/| n.|大黄花菜，黄花菜|A type of daylily, specifically *Hemerocallis fulva*, known for its edible flowers and sometimes its medicinal properties.  It is also sometimes used to refer to other daylilies.|例句：The daddocks were in full bloom, their bright orange flowers a striking contrast to the green field.  大黄花菜盛开，鲜艳的橘色花朵与绿色的田野形成鲜明的对比。|近义词：daylily, tiger lily (in some contexts)|反义词：无|名词
