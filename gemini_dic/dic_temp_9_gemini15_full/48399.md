dedicated: /ˈdɛdɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 专注的；献身的；敬业的 | Devoted to a cause, task, or purpose; showing dedication.  |例句：He is a dedicated teacher who works tirelessly for his students. 他是一位尽职尽责的老师，为学生们辛勤工作。|近义词：committed, devoted, loyal, steadfast |反义词：apathetic, indifferent, lazy, careless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is dedicated to his work. = 他致力于他的工作。
She is dedicated to helping others. = 她致力于帮助他人。

一般过去时:
He was dedicated to his family. = 他以前非常忠于他的家庭。
They were dedicated to finding a solution. = 他们致力于寻找解决方案。

现在完成时:
She has been dedicated to this project for years. = 她多年来一直致力于这个项目。
He has dedicated his life to charity work. = 他一生致力于慈善事业。

过去完成时:
They had been dedicated to the cause long before I joined. = 在我加入之前，他们就长期致力于这项事业了。
She had dedicated many years to research before achieving her breakthrough. = 在取得突破之前，她已将许多年献给了研究。

将来时:
He will be dedicated to his new role. = 他将全身心地投入他的新角色。
They will remain dedicated to their goals. = 他们将继续致力于实现他们的目标。

虚拟语气:
If he were more dedicated, he would succeed. = 如果他更专注一些，他就会成功。
I wish I were as dedicated as she is. = 我希望我像她一样专注。

被动语态:
The project was dedicated to the memory of his father. = 该项目是为了纪念他的父亲而设立的。 (Note:  Passive voice is less common with "dedicated" in this sense)
His life was dedicated to the pursuit of knowledge. = 他的一生都致力于追求知识。(Note:  Passive voice is less common with "dedicated" in this sense)

疑问句:
Is he dedicated enough to finish the project on time? = 他足够专注于按时完成项目吗？
Are they truly dedicated to improving the company? = 他们真的致力于改善公司吗？

否定句:
He is not dedicated enough to the task. = 他对这项任务不够专注。
She wasn't dedicated to her studies, so she failed. = 她学习不够努力，所以她考试不及格。


PHRASE->

highly dedicated = 高度敬业的
a dedicated effort = 尽心尽力的努力
