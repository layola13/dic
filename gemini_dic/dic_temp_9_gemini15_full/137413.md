nonterrestrial:/ˌnɒntəˈrestriəl/| adj. |非地球的；非陆地的|Not of, on, or relating to the Earth.  The term typically refers to things existing or occurring outside the Earth's atmosphere or surface, often in space. |例句：The search for nonterrestrial life is a major focus of modern astronomy.  寻找地外生命是现代天文学的主要焦点。

例句：Scientists are increasingly interested in the possibility of nonterrestrial origins for certain meteorites. 科学家们越来越关注某些陨石可能起源于地球以外的可能性。


近义词：extraterrestrial, extraplanetary


反义词：terrestrial


词性:形容词
