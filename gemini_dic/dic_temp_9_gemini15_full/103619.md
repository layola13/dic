jointure:/ˈdʒɔɪntʃər/| n. |嫁妆；(尤指寡妇的)终身保有权|A jointure is a settlement of property made by a husband to his wife, to be enjoyed by her during her lifetime, especially in the event of his death.|例句：The jointure provided her with a comfortable income after her husband's death.  她的丈夫去世后，嫁妆为她提供了舒适的收入。|近义词：dowry, settlement|反义词：none
