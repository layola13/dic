journalize:/ˈdʒɜːrnəlaɪz/ | vt. | 载入日记；将…记入日记；使…成为期刊 | To record (a transaction) in a journal.  |  例句：We need to journalize all the transactions before the end of the month. 我们需要在月底之前将所有交易记入日记。 | 例句：The company journalizes all its financial transactions daily. 这家公司每天将所有财务交易记入日记。| 近义词：record, enter, log | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He journalizes his daily expenses. = 他记录他每天的支出。
She journalizes every meeting she attends. = 她记录她参加的每一次会议。

一般过去时:
They journalized the sale yesterday. = 他们昨天将销售额记入日记。
He journalized all the transactions at the end of the day. = 他在一天结束时将所有交易记入日记。

现在完成时:
She has journalized all the entries for this month. = 她已经记入了本月的所有条目。
We have journalized the major financial events of the year. = 我们已经记入了今年的主要财务事件。

过去完成时:
They had journalized the transactions before the audit. = 审计之前，他们已经将交易记入日记。
She had journalized all the receipts before leaving for the trip. = 出发旅行之前，她已经将所有收据记入日记。

将来时:
He will journalize the entries tomorrow. = 他明天将记入这些条目。
We will journalize the transactions as soon as we receive the confirmation. = 一旦我们收到确认，我们将记入这些交易。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would journalize all the details. = 如果我有时间，我会将所有细节都记入日记。
It would be helpful if we journalized every step of the process. = 如果我们记录下过程中的每一步，将会很有帮助。

被动语态:
The transactions were journalized accurately. = 交易被准确地记入日记。
All the entries are being journalized by the accounting team. = 所有条目正由会计团队记入日记。

疑问句:
Have you journalized the recent purchase? = 你记入了最近的购买了吗？
Will the transactions be journalized today? = 交易今天会被记入日记吗？

否定句:
He did not journalize the small expenses. = 他没有记入小额支出。
They haven't journalized the transaction yet. = 他们还没有记入交易。


PHRASE->
