drumbeaters:/ˈdrʌmˌbiːtərz/ | n. |鼓手；宣传者；煽动者 |People who beat drums, often metaphorically referring to those who promote something enthusiastically, sometimes excessively.  They may be actively involved in promoting a cause or idea, or they might simply be seen as creating hype. |The drumbeaters for the new product kept the media buzzing with excitement.  新产品的宣传者们不断地让媒体兴奋不已。


The relentless drumbeaters for the war finally convinced the public.  鼓吹战争的人最终说服了公众。|propagandists, advocates, promoters|critics, opponents, doubters|名词
