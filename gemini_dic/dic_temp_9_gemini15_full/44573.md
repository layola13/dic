crouches:/kraʊtʃɪz/| v.|蹲下；蜷伏；|To lower the body close to the ground by bending the legs.  It often implies a secretive or cautious posture.|例句：The cat crouches before pouncing on its prey. 猫在猛扑猎物之前蹲下。|例句：She crouched behind the bushes, trying to remain unseen. 她蹲在灌木丛后面，试图不被发现。|近义词：squat, stoop, bend|反义词：stand, rise


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He crouches behind the tree. = 他蹲在树后。
The cat crouches and waits for a mouse. = 猫蹲下等待老鼠。

一般过去时:
She crouched down to examine the flower. = 她蹲下检查花朵。
The thief crouched in the shadows. = 小偷躲在阴影里蹲着。

现在完成时:
He has crouched in that position for hours. = 他已经保持那个姿势蹲了几个小时了。
The dog has crouched by the fire all evening. = 这只狗整个晚上都蜷缩在火炉旁。

过去完成时:
She had crouched there for a long time before he noticed her. = 他注意到她之前，她已经在那里蹲了很久了。
The bird had crouched low to the ground to avoid being seen. = 鸟儿为了不被发现而低低地蹲在地上。

将来时:
He will crouch down to tie his shoelaces. = 他将蹲下系鞋带。
They will crouch low to avoid being spotted. = 他们将蹲得很低以避免被发现。

虚拟语气:
If I were a cat, I would crouch and pounce. = 如果我是一只猫，我会蹲下然后猛扑。
I wish I could crouch unseen like a ninja. = 我希望我能像忍者一样不被发现地蹲下。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The figure was crouched low to the ground. = 那个人影低低地蹲在地上。(强调状态)


疑问句:
Is he crouching behind the wall? = 他是否躲在墙后蹲着？
Why is the cat crouching? = 猫为什么蹲着？


否定句:
He doesn't crouch when he sleeps. = 他睡觉的时候不蹲着。
She didn't crouch down to pick up the pen. = 她没有蹲下捡钢笔。


PHRASE->
crouch down = 蹲下
crouch low = 蹲得很低
crouch in fear = 害怕地蹲下
crouch in the shadows = 躲在阴影里蹲着
crouch over = 弯腰驼背
