preop:/ˈpriːɑːp/| adj.|术前，手术前的|relating to the period before a surgical operation.  |例句：The preop assessment included a thorough review of the patient's medical history. 术前评估包括对患者病史的全面回顾。|例句：The patient was given preop medication to relax them before surgery.  病人被给予术前药物以在手术前放松。|近义词：anteoperative, preoperative|反义词：postop, postoperative
