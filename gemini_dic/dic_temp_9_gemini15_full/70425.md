fights:/faɪts/| n. v.|打架；战斗；斗争；搏斗|The word "fights" can be a noun or a verb. As a noun, it refers to a physical or metaphorical struggle or conflict. As a verb, it means to engage in a physical or metaphorical struggle.|例句：The two boys had a fight.  两个男孩打了一架。|例句：She fights for what she believes in. 她为自己的信仰而斗争。|近义词：battles, combats, struggles, brawls|反义词：agreements, peace, harmony|动词
