glozer:/ˈɡloʊzər/| n. |骗子，欺诈者|A glozer is a person who is skilled at deception and trickery, often for personal gain. They may use charm, flattery, or intimidation to manipulate others.|例句：He was a smooth-talking glozer who swindled many people out of their money. 他是个油嘴滑舌的骗子，骗走了许多人的钱。|近义词：swindler, con artist, cheat, sharper|反义词：honest person, truthful person|名词
