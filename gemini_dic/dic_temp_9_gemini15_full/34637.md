clementine:/ˈklɛmənˌtiːn/| n. |小柑橘，柑橘小果|A clementine is a small, sweet citrus fruit, a hybrid of a mandarin orange and a sweet orange.  It is usually seedless and easy to peel.|例句：I love the sweet taste of clementines. 我喜欢柑橘的甜味。

例句：Clementines are a popular snack during the winter months.  柑橘在冬季月份是很受欢迎的零食。


近义词：mandarin, satsuma, tangerine|反义词：无|词性：名词
