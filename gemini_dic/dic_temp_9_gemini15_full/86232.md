heathy:/ˈhelθi/| adj.|健康的，强健的，有益健康的 |Healthy refers to a state of being free from illness or injury, or to something that promotes good health.  It can describe a person, an animal, a plant, or even a lifestyle.|例句：She leads a healthy lifestyle. 她过着健康的生活方式。|例句：Eating fruits and vegetables is healthy. 吃水果和蔬菜有益健康。|近义词：well, fit, robust, wholesome|反义词：unhealthy, sick, diseased, unwell
