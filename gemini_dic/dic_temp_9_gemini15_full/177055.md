reanointed:/ˌriːəˈnɔɪntɪd/| v. |再次涂油；重新膏立；再次任命|  Past participle of "reanoint".  It implies a renewed or repeated anointing, often in a ceremonial or religious context, signifying a restoration of power, authority, or sacred status.  The act of anointing typically involves applying oil to someone as a symbol of consecration or investiture. |例句：The newly reanointed king addressed his loyal subjects.  重新膏立的国王向他的忠实臣民发表讲话。

The exiled prince was reanointed after the revolution. 流亡王子在革命后被重新膏立。| 近义词：reappoint, reinstate, re-establish | 反义词：dethrone, depose
