demand:/dɪˈmænd/ | n., vt. | 需求；要求；需要 |  The act of demanding something; a strong request; something that is required or wanted.  |例句：The demand for skilled workers is high.  熟练工人的需求量很大。

例句：He demanded an explanation. 他要求一个解释。


近义词：request, requirement, need, claim | 反义词：supply, offer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company demands high standards of its employees. = 公司要求员工达到高标准。
Customers demand better quality products. = 客户要求更高质量的产品。


一般过去时:
She demanded a refund for the faulty goods. = 她要求退还有缺陷商品的款项。
They demanded to see the manager. = 他们要求见经理。


现在完成时:
The government has demanded an immediate response. = 政府已要求立即作出回应。
They have demanded their rights for years. = 他们多年来一直在争取他们的权利。


过去完成时:
He had demanded an apology before he would speak to her again. = 他要求道歉后才愿意再和她说话。
The workers had demanded a pay raise for months. = 工人们几个月来一直要求加薪。


将来时:
The union will demand better working conditions. = 工会将要求改善工作条件。
They will demand a thorough investigation. = 他们将要求进行彻底调查。


虚拟语气:
If they demanded a higher salary, they might get it. = 如果他们要求更高的薪水，他们可能会得到。
I wish they hadn't demanded so much. = 我真希望他们不要要求那么多。


被动语态:
A higher price was demanded for the rare painting. = 这幅珍稀画作被索要更高的价格。
More resources are demanded by the project. = 该项目需要更多资源。


疑问句:
What are the demands of this job? = 这份工作的要求是什么？
What did they demand from you? = 他们向你索要什么？


否定句:
The company didn't demand overtime work. = 公司没有要求加班。
He didn't demand an answer immediately. = 他没有立即要求答复。


PHRASE->
in demand:  广受欢迎的；需求量大的
on demand:  按要求；随时供应
meet the demand:  满足需求
demand for: 对…的需求
supply and demand: 供求关系

词源:
The word "demand" comes from the Old French word "demander," meaning "to ask," "to request," or "to require."  This in turn derives from the Latin word "demandare," which means "to entrust," "to commit," or "to hand over."  The sense of "to ask forcefully" developed later.
