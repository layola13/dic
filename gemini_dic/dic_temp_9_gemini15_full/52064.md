dialogize:/ˈdaɪəlɒdʒaɪz/| vt.|使对话化；使戏剧化|To represent or express something in the form of a dialogue or conversation; to make something more dramatic or conversational.|例句：The author dialogized the historical events in his novel, creating a more engaging narrative.  作者在他的小说中将历史事件对话化，创造了一个更引人入胜的叙事。|例句：The film dialogized the complex political issues, making them more accessible to the audience.  这部电影将复杂的政治问题对话化，使观众更容易理解。|近义词：dramatize, converse,  |反义词：monologuize, summarize
