pervaded:/pərˈveɪd/| v.|弥漫，遍布，充满|To spread throughout and be perceived in every part of.|The aroma of freshly baked bread pervaded the kitchen. 新鲜烘焙的面包香味弥漫在整个厨房。|近义词：penetrate, permeate, saturate|反义词：lack, absent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sweet scent pervades the air. = 甜美的香味弥漫在空气中。
Fear pervades the hearts of many. = 恐惧弥漫在许多人的心中。

一般过去时:
A sense of unease pervaded the room. = 一种不安的感觉弥漫在房间里。
Silence pervaded the deserted streets. = 寂静笼罩着空荡荡的街道。

现在完成时:
Disappointment has pervaded her life. = 失望已经充满了她的生活。
Doubt has pervaded the minds of many. = 怀疑已经充满了许多人的心中。

过去完成时:
A feeling of dread had pervaded the city before the earthquake. = 地震之前，一种恐惧感已经弥漫了整个城市。
An air of mystery had pervaded the old house. = 一种神秘的气氛笼罩着那座老房子。

将来时:
A wave of panic will pervade the crowd. = 一阵恐慌将会席卷人群。
The news will pervade the entire nation. = 这个消息将会传遍全国。

虚拟语气:
If only peace pervaded the world. = 但愿和平能够遍布世界。
If joy pervaded my heart, I would be happy. = 如果快乐充满我的内心，我会很幸福。

被动语态:
The entire city was pervaded by a sense of fear. = 整个城市都被恐惧笼罩着。
The atmosphere was pervaded by a feeling of excitement. = 气氛充满了兴奋感。

疑问句:

Does the smell of smoke pervade the building? = 烟味是否弥漫在建筑物中？

否定句:
The feeling of hope did not pervade the meeting. = 希望的感觉并没有弥漫在会议中。
Calm did not pervade the situation. = 平静并没有弥漫在这种情况中。
