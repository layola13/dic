giver:/ˈɡɪvər/ | n. |给予者，赠送者 | A person or thing that gives something.  |  He is a generous giver to charity. 他是一位慷慨的慈善捐赠者。 | donor, benefactor, contributor | receiver, taker | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The giver distributes the gifts fairly. = 给予者公平地分发礼物。
She is a kind giver to the needy. = 她是一位乐于助人的施与者。


一般过去时:
The anonymous giver left a large donation. = 匿名捐赠者留下了一笔巨额捐款。
He was a generous giver in his youth. = 他年轻时是一位慷慨的给予者。


现在完成时:
She has been a consistent giver to the church. = 她一直是教会的忠实捐赠者。
The organization has received many gifts from various givers. = 该组织已收到来自各种捐赠者的许多礼物。


过去完成时:
The giver had already left before I could thank him. = 我还没来得及感谢他，给予者就已经离开了。
They had been generous givers for many years before retiring. = 他们在退休前多年来一直都是慷慨的捐赠者。


将来时:
The company will be a major giver to the new hospital. = 公司将成为新医院的主要捐赠者。
He will continue to be a giver to those in need. = 他将继续帮助需要帮助的人。


虚拟语气:
If only I knew the giver's identity! = 要是我知道捐赠者的身份就好了！
I wish the giver had left a note. = 我希望给予者留张便条。


被动语态:
The gifts were distributed by the giver. = 礼物由给予者分发。
The money was donated by a generous giver. = 钱是由一位慷慨的捐赠者捐赠的。


疑问句:
Who is the giver of this present? = 这份礼物是谁送的？
Do you know who the giver was? = 你知道给予者是谁吗？


否定句:
He wasn't the giver of that gift. = 那份礼物不是他送的。
She is not a known giver to this charity. = 她并不是这个慈善机构的知名捐赠者。
