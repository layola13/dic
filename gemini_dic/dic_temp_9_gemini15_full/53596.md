disc: /dɪsk/ | n. | 磁盘；圆盘；唱片 | A flat, circular object.  It can refer to a phonograph record, a compact disc, a DVD, or a similar data storage medium.  Also, it can refer to a circular anatomical structure like an intervertebral disc. |例句：He played the old vinyl disc on the record player. 他在唱机上播放了那张旧黑胶唱片。

例句：The doctor explained the damage to the intervertebral disc in his spine. 医生解释了他脊椎间盘的损伤。


近义词：record, CD, DVD, plate


反义词：无


词性：名词

词源:
The word "disc" comes from the Latin word "discus," meaning "a quoit" or "a flat circular plate."  The meaning evolved to encompass various flat circular objects, including the modern technological uses.
