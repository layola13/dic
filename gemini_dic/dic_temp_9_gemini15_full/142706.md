otherwhiles:/ˈʌðə(r)waɪlz/| adv. |有时，间或|Sometimes; occasionally.  The word suggests a less frequent occurrence than "often" but more frequent than "rarely." It carries a slightly archaic or literary feel.|例句：Otherwhiles he would sit and read, otherwhiles he would wander in the garden. 有时他会坐着看书，有时他会漫步在花园里。


例句：Otherwhiles the sun shone brightly, otherwhiles it was hidden behind clouds. 有时阳光灿烂，有时却被云层遮挡。


近义词：occasionally, sometimes, at times, now and then, from time to time


反义词：always, constantly, perpetually, invariably


词性：副词
