restituto: /rɛstɪˈtuːtoʊ/ | adj. | 恢复的；归还的；赔偿的 | Restored; given back; compensated.  It refers to something that has been returned to its original state or owner, or to compensation given for a loss or injury. | The restituto painting was finally returned to the museum.  归还的画作最终被送回了博物馆。| 近义词：restored, returned, compensated | 反义词：lost, stolen, uncompensated |  形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The painting is restituto. = 这幅画被修复了。
The stolen goods are restituto to their owner. = 被盗的物品已归还给主人。

一般过去时:
The damaged building was restituto. = 受损的建筑物已修复。
The money was restituto to the victim. = 钱已赔偿给受害者。

现在完成时:
The artifacts have been restituto to their original location. = 文物已被送回原址。
Justice has been restituto in this case. = 在此案中正义得到了伸张。

过去完成时:
The property had been restituto before the trial. = 该房产在审判前已被归还。
Compensation had been restituto to all the affected parties. = 所有受影响的各方都已获得赔偿。

将来时:
The stolen car will be restituto to its rightful owner. = 被盗的汽车将归还给其合法所有者。
The damages will be restituto by the insurance company. = 保险公司将赔偿损失。


虚拟语气:
If the painting were restituto, the museum would be overjoyed. = 如果这幅画被修复，博物馆将欣喜若狂。
I wish the stolen jewels were restituto. = 我希望失窃的珠宝能够找回。

被动语态:
The stolen goods are being restituto. = 被盗的物品正在被归还。
Compensation is being restituto to the families of the victims. = 正在向受害者家属赔偿。

疑问句:
Is the damaged property restituto? = 受损的财产是否已修复？
Has the money been restituto? = 钱是否已经赔偿？

否定句:
The stolen items were not restituto. = 被盗物品未被归还。
The damages have not been restituto yet. = 损失尚未得到赔偿。


PHRASE->
restituto ad integrum:  (Latin) completely restored to its original state. 完全恢复到原来的状态。
restituto in specie: (Latin)  restoration in kind.  实物赔偿。
restituto in value: (Latin) restoration in value.  价值赔偿。
restituto to its former glory: 恢复昔日的辉煌。
restituto to its original condition: 恢复到原来的状态。
