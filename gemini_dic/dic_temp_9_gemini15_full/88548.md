hit-up:/hɪt ʌp/| vt.|拜访；请求；索要；攻击|To visit someone unexpectedly or informally; to ask someone for something; to attack someone or something.|例句：I'm going to hit up my friend for some money. 我要去向我的朋友要一些钱。
例句：Let's hit up that new bar downtown.咱们去市中心那家新开的酒吧看看吧。|近义词：visit, ask, request, attack|反义词：ignore, avoid, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I hit up my friend every week. = 我每周都去看望我的朋友。
They hit up the boss for a raise. = 他们向老板要求加薪。


一般过去时:
I hit up Sarah last night. = 我昨晚拜访了萨拉。
He hit up the bank for a loan. = 他向银行申请贷款。


现在完成时:
I have hit up my parents for money several times. = 我已经多次向父母要钱了。
She has hit up every store in the mall. = 她已经逛遍了商场里的每家商店。


过去完成时:
I had hit up all my friends before I found a solution. = 在找到解决方案之前，我已经拜访了所有朋友。
They had hit up the manager before complaining to HR. = 在向人力资源部门投诉之前，他们已经找过经理了。


将来时:
I will hit up my brother for help tomorrow. = 我明天将向我哥哥求助。
We will hit up that new restaurant next week. = 我们下周将去那家新餐厅吃饭。


虚拟语气:
If I needed money, I would hit up my family. = 如果我需要钱，我会向我的家人求助。
I wish I hadn't hit up that guy for a loan. = 我真希望我没有向那个人借钱。


被动语态:
The store was hit up by robbers last night. = 昨晚那家商店被抢劫了。 (This usage is less common for the "visit" sense)
The manager was hit up repeatedly for extra funds. = 经理反复被要求提供额外资金。


疑问句:
Are you going to hit up your parents for a car? = 你打算向你父母要一辆车吗？
Did he hit up that new club last night? = 他昨晚去了那家新开的俱乐部吗？


否定句:
I didn't hit up my friends for help this time. = 这次我没有向朋友求助。
They won't hit up the boss again for a raise so soon. = 他们不会这么快再次向老板要求加薪。


PHRASE->
hit up for a favor: 求人帮忙
hit up for money: 讨钱
hit up for a date: 约人约会
hit up a party: 参加派对
hit up a place: 去某个地方
