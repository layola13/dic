describe: /dɪˈskraɪb/ | vt. |描述，形容|To give an account in words of (someone or something), including all the relevant characteristics or features.|例句：Please describe the suspect to the police. 请向警方描述嫌疑人的特征。|例句：She described her childhood as happy and carefree. 她将她的童年描述成快乐而无忧无虑的。|近义词：portray, depict, delineate, recount|反义词：misrepresent, distort|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He describes the scenery vividly. = 他生动地描述了风景。
She describes her feelings in her diary. = 她在日记里描述了自己的感受。

一般过去时:
The witness described the accident accurately. = 目击者准确地描述了事故经过。
He described his journey in detail. = 他详细地描述了他的旅程。

现在完成时:
She has described the process clearly. = 她已经清晰地描述了这个过程。
They have described the situation as critical. = 他们已将情况描述为危急。

过去完成时:
He had described the painting before she arrived. = 她到达之前，他已经描述了这幅画。
They had described their plan to the boss. = 他们已经向老板描述了他们的计划。

将来时:
I will describe the experiment in my report. = 我将在我的报告中描述这个实验。
She will describe the new product to the customers. = 她将向顾客描述新产品。

虚拟语气:
If I could describe it, I would. = 如果我能描述它，我会的。
I wish I could describe the feeling better. = 我希望我能更好地描述这种感觉。

被动语态:
The event was described in the newspaper. = 这件事在报纸上被描述了。
The painting is described as a masterpiece. = 这幅画被描述为杰作。

疑问句:
Can you describe what you saw? = 你能描述你所看到的东西吗？
How would you describe your personality? = 你会如何形容你的个性？

否定句:
I cannot describe the beauty of the sunset. = 我无法描述日落的美丽。
He didn't describe the incident properly. = 他没有恰当地描述这起事件。



PHRASE->
describe in detail = 详细描述
describe as = 将…描述为
describe the scene = 描述场景
give a description of = 对…进行描述
vivid description = 生动的描述
