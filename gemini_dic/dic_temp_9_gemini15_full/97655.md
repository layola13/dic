inhibit:/ɪnˈhɪbɪt/ | vt. |抑制；禁止；阻止 | To restrain, hinder, or prevent (an action, process, or person).  |例句：The drug inhibits the growth of tumors.  这种药物抑制肿瘤的生长。

例句：Fear inhibited him from speaking out.  恐惧阻止了他大声说话。


近义词：suppress, restrain, restrict, impede, check | 反义词：stimulate, encourage, promote, facilitate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The medication inhibits the virus's replication. = 这种药物抑制病毒的复制。
Cold weather inhibits plant growth. =寒冷的天气抑制植物生长。


一般过去时:
The heavy rain inhibited our outdoor plans. = 大雨打乱了我们的户外计划。
The strict rules inhibited creativity. = 严格的规定抑制了创造力。


现在完成时:
Fear has inhibited her from pursuing her dreams. = 恐惧一直阻止她追求梦想。
Regulations have inhibited the development of the industry. = 法规已经抑制了该行业的发展。


过去完成时:
Previous failures had inhibited his confidence. = 之前的失败已经打击了他的信心。
The lack of funding had inhibited the project's progress. = 资金不足已经阻碍了项目的进展。


将来时:
New laws will inhibit the spread of misinformation. = 新法律将抑制虚假信息的传播。
Increased security measures will inhibit unauthorized access. = 加强的安全措施将阻止未经授权的访问。


虚拟语气:
If the government didn't inhibit free speech, we would have a more vibrant society. = 如果政府不压制言论自由，我们将拥有一个更加充满活力的社会。
I wish they wouldn't inhibit my creativity. =我希望他们不要压制我的创造力。


被动语态:
His progress was inhibited by a lack of resources. = 他的进步由于缺乏资源而受到了阻碍。
The project was inhibited by unforeseen complications. = 该项目因不可预见的问题而受阻。


疑问句:
Does this chemical inhibit bacterial growth? =这种化学物质是否抑制细菌生长？
What factors inhibit economic growth? =哪些因素抑制经济增长？


否定句:
The lack of evidence didn't inhibit the investigation. = 缺乏证据并没有阻止调查。
Nothing inhibited his determination to succeed. = 没有什么能阻止他成功的决心。


PHRASE->
inhibit the growth of = 抑制…的增长
inhibit enzyme activity = 抑制酶活性
inhibit gene expression = 抑制基因表达
inhibit cell proliferation = 抑制细胞增殖
inhibit the spread of = 抑制…的传播

词源:
From Latin *inhibitus, past participle of inhibere "to hold back, restrain," from in- "in" + habēre "to have, hold."
