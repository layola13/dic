shoelaces:/ˈʃuːleɪsɪz/ | n. |鞋带 |Shoe laces are the strings used to fasten shoes.  They are typically made of cotton, nylon, or other strong materials. |例句：My shoelaces are untied. = 我的鞋带开了。

My shoelaces came undone during the race. = 比赛中我的鞋带松开了。


近义词：bootlaces (for boots) | 反义词：无
