provides:/prəˈvaɪdz/ | vt. | 提供，供应 | To make something available; to supply or furnish.  | 例句：The company provides excellent training for its employees.  这家公司为其员工提供优秀的培训。

The government provides healthcare services to its citizens. 政府为其公民提供医疗服务。|近义词：supplies, furnishes, offers, gives | 反义词：withholds, denies, deprives


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school provides free lunches to all students. = 学校为所有学生提供免费午餐。
This job provides a good salary and benefits. = 这份工作提供丰厚的薪水和福利。

一般过去时:
The hotel provided us with clean towels. = 旅馆为我们提供了干净的毛巾。
She provided all the necessary information for the report. = 她为报告提供了所有必要的信息。

现在完成时:
They have provided significant support to the project. = 他们为该项目提供了重要的支持。
The charity has provided shelter for hundreds of homeless people. = 该慈善机构已为数百名无家可归者提供了住所。

过去完成时:
He had provided a detailed explanation before the meeting. = 他在会议前提供了详细的解释。
They had provided all the equipment needed for the experiment. = 他们已经提供了实验所需的所有设备。

将来时:
The company will provide further updates next week. = 公司将在下周提供进一步的更新。
We will provide transportation to the airport. = 我们将提供前往机场的交通工具。

虚拟语气:
If they provided more funding, we could finish the project earlier. = 如果他们提供更多资金，我们就能更早完成项目。
I wish they provided better customer service. = 我希望他们提供更好的客户服务。

被动语态:
Food and water were provided to the victims of the disaster. = 灾难受害者得到了食物和水的供应。
Training will be provided by the company. = 培训将由公司提供。

疑问句:
Does this plan provide enough resources? = 这个计划提供足够的资源吗？
What benefits does this position provide? = 这个职位提供什么福利？

否定句:
The company does not provide health insurance. = 公司不提供医疗保险。
He didn't provide any evidence to support his claim. = 他没有提供任何证据来支持他的说法。


PHRASE->
provide for:  to support financially  经济上供养
provide against: to protect from; guard against 预防；防范
provide with: to supply or furnish with  提供…给…
provide oneself with:  to get something for oneself 自己准备…
provided that: on condition that; if  如果；假如
