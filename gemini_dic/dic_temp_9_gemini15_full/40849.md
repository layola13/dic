cooed:/kuːd/| vt.|咕咕叫；轻声地说；温柔地说话|To make soft, gentle sounds like a dove; to speak in a soft, gentle voice.  |例句：The baby cooed contentedly in its mother's arms.  婴儿在母亲的怀抱中舒服地咕咕叫。
The lovers cooed sweet nothings to each other. 恋人们低声说着情话。|近义词：murmur, purr, murmur, croon|反义词：shout, yell, scream|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dove coos softly.  鸽子轻轻地咕咕叫。
She coos to her baby. 她对她宝宝轻声细语。


一般过去时:
The baby cooed all night. 婴儿整晚都在咕咕叫。
He cooed sweet words in her ear. 他在她耳边轻声细语。


现在完成时:
The bird has cooed since dawn.  这只鸟从黎明开始就一直咕咕叫。
She has cooed to her baby all afternoon. 她一下午都在对她的宝宝轻声细语。


过去完成时:
The baby had cooed itself to sleep. 婴儿已经自己咕咕叫着睡着了。
Before the argument, they had cooed and cuddled. 争吵之前，他们一直卿卿我我。


将来时:
The birds will coo at sunrise. 鸟儿将在日出时咕咕叫。
I think he will coo when he sees his grandchild. 我想他会在他看到他的孙子时轻声细语。


虚拟语气:
If she cooed to him, he might calm down. 如果她对他轻声细语，他可能会平静下来。
I wish I could coo like a dove. 我希望我能像鸽子一样咕咕叫。


被动语态:
Sweet nothings were cooed throughout the evening.  整个晚上都在低声说着情话。
(Note:  Passive voice is less common with this verb.)


疑问句:
Does the baby coo often? 婴儿经常咕咕叫吗？
Did he coo to you? 他对你轻声细语了吗？


否定句:
The baby didn't coo today. 婴儿今天没有咕咕叫。
She didn't coo to him, she shouted. 她没有对他轻声细语，她大喊大叫。


PHRASE->
coo over:  to express great affection or admiration for someone or something.  (对某人或某事表达极大的喜爱或赞赏)
coo something to someone: to whisper something lovingly to someone. (温柔地对某人耳语)
coo and bill:  to kiss and cuddle affectionately. (亲吻并依偎)
coo your approval:  to express approval quietly and gently. (轻声细语地表示赞同)
coo with delight:  to express delight with soft sounds. (用轻柔的声音表达喜悦)
