reckless-of:/ˈrɛkləs əv/ | adj. | 鲁莽的，不顾后果的（关于…） |  Describes someone's behavior that shows a lack of care for the consequences of their actions, specifically regarding a particular thing or situation.  | He was reckless of other people's feelings. 他不顾及别人的感受。| careless, heedless, rash | cautious, careful, prudent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is reckless of the dangers involved. = 他不顾及所涉及的危险。
She is reckless of her own safety. = 她不顾自己的安全。


一般过去时:
He was reckless of the consequences. = 他不顾后果。
They were reckless of the law. = 他们不顾法律。


现在完成时:
He has been reckless of his health for years. = 多年来他一直不顾自己的健康。
She has been reckless of her finances. = 她一直对自己的财务不负责任。


过去完成时:
He had been reckless of his promises in the past. = 他过去常常不顾自己的承诺。
They had been reckless of the risks involved. = 他们过去一直不顾风险。


将来时:
He will be reckless of the rules. = 他将不顾规则。
She will be reckless of the warnings. = 她将不顾警告。


虚拟语气:
If he were reckless of the rules, he would get into trouble. = 如果他不顾规则，他会惹上麻烦。
I wish he weren't so reckless of his health. = 我希望他不那么不顾自己的健康。


被动语态:
His actions were considered reckless of the public's safety. = 他的行为被认为不顾公众安全。
The decision was deemed reckless of the environmental impact. = 该决定被认为不顾及环境影响。


疑问句:
Is he reckless of the danger? = 他不顾危险吗？
Are they reckless of the consequences? = 他们不顾后果吗？


否定句:
He is not reckless of his responsibilities. = 他没有不顾他的责任。
She is not reckless of others' opinions. = 她并不鲁莽地不顾及别人的意见。
