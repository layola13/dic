nontotalitarian:/ˌnɒntəʊˌtælɪˈteəriən/| adj. |非极权主义的；非极权的|Not characterized by or relating to a totalitarian system of government.  It implies a system where individual freedoms and rights are respected, and power is not concentrated in the hands of a single authority. |The country is slowly transitioning from a totalitarian regime to a nontotalitarian one, with increasing political freedoms and civil liberties being granted to its citizens.|近义词：democratic, liberal, free, open|反义词：totalitarian, authoritarian, dictatorial
