encroacher:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃər/| n. |侵占者；侵略者| A person who encroaches on the rights or possessions of another.  Someone who gradually invades or intrudes upon something.|The encroacher was ordered to remove his illegally built fence.  侵占者被命令拆除他非法建造的栅栏。| trespasser, invader, intruder |  defender, protector | 名词

一般现在时:
The encroacher constantly tries to expand their territory. = 侵占者不断试图扩张他们的领地。
He encroaches on his neighbor's property every year. = 他每年都侵占邻居的财产。


一般过去时:
The farmer complained that the encroacher had damaged his crops. = 农民抱怨侵占者损坏了他的庄稼。
They found out that someone had encroached on their land. = 他们发现有人侵占了他们的土地。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:
We fear that the encroachers will claim even more land. = 我们担心侵占者将占据更多的土地。
The authorities will act against any further encroachment. = 当局将对任何进一步的侵占采取行动。


虚拟语气:

疑问句:
