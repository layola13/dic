constraints:/kənˈstreɪnts/ | n. |限制，约束；束缚 |Limitations or restrictions on something.  Constraints can be physical, financial, legal, or social.  They limit what is possible or allowed. |例句：The project was completed despite numerous time constraints.  这个项目尽管有很多时间限制，但还是完成了。|例句：Financial constraints prevented them from buying a new house.  经济上的限制阻止了他们购买新房。|近义词：restrictions, limitations, restraints, boundaries |反义词：freedom, liberty, openness
