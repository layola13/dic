hired:/ˈhaɪərd/ | vt. |雇佣，聘用|To employ someone to do a job for payment.  |例句：The company hired ten new employees last month. 公司上个月雇佣了十名新员工。|例句：They hired a contractor to renovate their kitchen. 他们雇了一名承包商来装修他们的厨房。|近义词：employed, engaged, recruited |反义词：fired, dismissed, laid off


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company hires many interns every summer. = 公司每年夏天都会雇佣很多实习生。
She hires a cleaner once a week. = 她每周雇佣一次清洁工。


一般过去时:
They hired a new marketing manager. = 他们雇佣了一位新的市场经理。
I hired a car for the weekend. = 我周末租了一辆车。


现在完成时:
We have hired three new staff members this year. = 今年我们已经雇佣了三名新员工。
She has hired a lawyer to represent her. = 她已经聘请了一位律师来代表她。


过去完成时:
They had hired the caterer before the event. = 他们在那次活动之前就已经雇佣了餐饮承办商。
The company had hired him before I joined. = 我加入公司之前，公司就已经雇佣了他。


将来时:
We will hire more people next year. = 我们明年将雇佣更多的人。
The firm will hire a consultant to assess the situation. = 公司将聘请一名顾问来评估情况。


虚拟语气:
If they hired more staff, the workload would be reduced. = 如果他们雇佣更多员工，工作量就会减少。
I wish they had hired someone more experienced. = 我希望他们当初雇佣一个更有经验的人。


被动语态:
Two new teachers were hired by the school. = 学校聘请了两位新老师。
A new CEO is being hired by the company. = 公司正在招聘一位新的首席执行官。


疑问句:
Did they hire anyone for the position? = 他们有没有为这个职位招聘任何人？
Will the company hire more developers? = 公司会雇佣更多开发者吗？


否定句:
They didn't hire anyone for the summer internship program. = 他们没有为暑期实习项目招聘任何人。
The company has not hired any new employees recently. = 公司最近没有雇佣任何新员工。


PHRASE->
be hired for = 被雇用从事……
hire out = 出租
hire on = 受雇
hire someone to do something = 雇佣某人做某事
for hire = 可出租的/可雇用的
