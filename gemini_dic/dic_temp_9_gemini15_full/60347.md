egotism:/ˈiːɡəʊtɪzəm/ | n. | 自我中心；自大；自负 | The excessive talk about oneself and one's achievements.  A characteristic of people who are preoccupied with themselves and their own interests, often to the exclusion of others. | 例句：His egotism prevented him from seeing other people's points of view. 他的自负使他无法理解别人的观点。

His constant boasting about his accomplishments reveals his deep-seated egotism. 他不断吹嘘自己的成就，暴露了他根深蒂固的自负。


近义词：narcissism, self-centeredness, conceit, vanity, self-importance | 反义词：humility, modesty, selflessness
