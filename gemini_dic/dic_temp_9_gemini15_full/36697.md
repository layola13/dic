collingual:  /ˈkɒlɪŋɡwəl/ | adj. | 通俗的，口语化的 | Colloquial refers to language that is used in ordinary conversation rather than formal writing. It is informal and may include slang, idioms, and contractions.  | Example: "He used a lot of colloquialisms in his speech, making it more engaging to the audience."  他演讲中使用了许多口语词，使演讲更吸引人。 | 近义词：informal, vernacular, conversational | 反义词：formal, literary
