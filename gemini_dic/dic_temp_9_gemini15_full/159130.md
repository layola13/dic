pococurantism:/ˌpɒkəʊˈkjuərənˌtɪzəm/ | n. |漠不关心；无所谓主义 |The philosophical attitude of indifference to pleasure or pain, good or evil, success or failure.  It emphasizes a detached and unconcerned approach to life's events. |例句：His pococurantism was evident in his lack of reaction to both praise and criticism. 他的漠不关心在他对赞扬和批评的缺乏反应中显而易见。

例句：Her pococurantism towards her career surprised her colleagues. 她对职业生涯的漠不关心让她的同事们感到惊讶。|近义词：apathy, indifference, unconcern, nonchalance |反义词：concern, care, passion, enthusiasm |词性：名词
