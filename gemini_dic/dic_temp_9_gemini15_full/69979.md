festschriften:/ˈfɛstsˌkrɪftən/| n.|纪念文集|A festschrift is a collection of scholarly papers dedicated to a person, usually a scholar, on the occasion of a significant event such as a birthday, retirement, or a major career milestone.  It's often published as a book and contributed to by colleagues, students, and friends.|This festschrift honors Professor Smith's decades of contributions to the field of linguistics.  这本纪念文集是为了纪念史密斯教授几十年来对语言学领域的贡献。|纪念刊，献礼文集|None|None
