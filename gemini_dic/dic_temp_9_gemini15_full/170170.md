protopian:/proʊˈtoʊpiən/| adj. |乌托邦式的，原始的，最初的 | Relating to or characteristic of a protoype or early stage of development; often used to describe societies or systems that are idealized but not yet fully realized.  It suggests a stage before a fully developed utopia, often with imperfections or unresolved issues. | Example: The protopian society envisioned in the novel struggled with issues of inequality despite its lofty goals.  |近义词：prototypical, incipient, nascent, embryonic |反义词：dystopian, fully realized
