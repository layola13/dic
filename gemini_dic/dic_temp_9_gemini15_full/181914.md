remakes:/ˈriːmeɪks/ | n. |重拍；翻拍作品 |A remake is a new version of a movie, television show, or other work, often with a different cast and sometimes updated for a modern audience.  It aims to retell the same story or present the same concept with potentially altered elements. |例句：The new remake of the classic film is generating a lot of buzz.  新的经典电影重拍版正在引起很多关注。

例句：The television series received a successful remake after many years. 这部电视剧在多年后获得了成功的翻拍。


近义词：re-adaptation, re-imagining, revision |反义词：original, first version |词性：名词
