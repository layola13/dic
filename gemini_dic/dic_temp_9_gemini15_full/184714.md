resubmitting:/ˌriːsəbˈmɪtɪŋ/| v. |重新提交，再次提交 |The act of submitting something again, typically after it has been rejected or requires further review.  |例句：He is resubmitting his application after addressing the reviewer's comments. 他在处理了评审人员的意见后重新提交了他的申请。|例句：The company is resubmitting the proposal with the necessary revisions. 公司正在提交修改后的提案。|近义词：re-submitting, submitting again, reapplying|反义词：withdrawing, rejecting


USAGE->

They resubmit their work every week. = 他们每周都重新提交他们的工作。
We resubmit the documents if necessary. = 如果有必要，我们会重新提交文件。
