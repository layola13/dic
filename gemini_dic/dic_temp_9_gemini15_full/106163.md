knock-around:/ˌnɒk əˈraʊnd/| /ˌnɑːk əˈraʊnd/| vt. n. adj.|四处闲逛；粗暴对待；经久耐用的；|英文解析：As a verb, it means to treat roughly or carelessly. As a noun, it refers to a rough or informal treatment or a period of such treatment. As an adjective, it describes something that is durable and able to withstand rough handling.|例句：The old car has been knocked around a bit, but it still runs. 这辆旧车被粗暴对待过一些，但它仍然能跑。  They knocked him around pretty badly. 他们狠狠地揍了他一顿。|近义词：rough up, mishandle, beat up|反义词：pamper, cherish, protect|词性：verb, noun, adjective
