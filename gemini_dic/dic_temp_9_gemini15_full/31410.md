charging:/ˈtʃɑːrdʒɪŋ/| v. n. |充电；收费；指控；猛冲|The act of loading something with energy, typically electricity, or the act of demanding payment, or the act of accusing someone of a crime, or the act of moving forward forcefully.|例句：My phone is charging. 我的手机正在充电。  The hotel charges $100 a night. 这家酒店每晚收费100美元。|近义词：loading, billing, accusing, rushing|反义词：discharging, crediting, acquitting, receding|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is charging his phone. = 他正在给手机充电。
The hotel charges a high price for rooms. = 这家酒店的房间价格很高。


一般过去时:
She charged her electric car yesterday. = 她昨天给她的电动汽车充电了。
They charged me too much for the repair. = 他们收了我太多的修理费。
