irokos:/ˈɪrəkoʊs/| n. |伊罗科树，非洲楝|A large African hardwood tree (Chlorophora excelsa) valued for its durability and resistance to decay.  It is often used in construction, furniture making, and veneer.|The iroko wood is highly prized for its strength and water resistance.|近义词：African teak, odum|反义词：无
