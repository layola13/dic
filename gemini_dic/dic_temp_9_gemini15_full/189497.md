salaamed:/səˈlɑːmd/| vt.| 行礼；向…行注目礼 | To greet someone, especially an important person, by lowering the head and body. This is a common greeting in some cultures.  |例句：He salaamed respectfully to the king. 他恭敬地向国王行礼。 |近义词：bowed, prostrated, greeted |反义词：insulted, ignored


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He salaams to his elders daily. = 他每天向长辈行礼。
She salaams respectfully to her teachers. = 她恭敬地向老师行礼。


一般过去时:
He salaamed to the dignitary. = 他向贵宾行礼。
They salaamed deeply as a sign of respect. = 他们深深地鞠躬以示敬意。


现在完成时:
I have salaamed to the Sheikh several times. = 我已经向谢赫行过几次礼了。
She has never salaamed to anyone in her life. = 她一生从未向任何人行过礼。


过去完成时:
He had salaamed before the emperor spoke. = 在皇帝说话之前，他已经行过礼了。
They had salaamed to the leader before entering the meeting. = 他们在进入会议之前已向领导行过礼。


将来时:
I will salaam to the President when I meet him. = 我见到总统时会向他行礼。
They will salaam to the visiting dignitaries. = 他们将向来访的贵宾行礼。


虚拟语气:
If I were to meet the Queen, I would salaam. = 如果我见到女王，我会行礼。
I wish I had salaamed to the Dalai Lama when I had the chance. = 我真希望我有机会向达赖喇嘛行礼。


被动语态:
The dignitary was salaamed to by the crowd. = 贵宾受到了人群的敬礼。
The visiting official was salaamed to with great respect. = 来访官员受到了非常尊重的敬礼。


疑问句:
Did he salaam to the judge? = 他向法官行礼了吗？
Will you salaam to the imam? = 你会向伊玛目行礼吗？


否定句:
He did not salaam to the stranger. = 他没有向陌生人行礼。
She didn't salaam to the emperor. = 她没有向皇帝行礼。


PHRASE->
There are limited common phrases directly using "salaamed".  The verb itself is quite specific.  Related phrases would focus on the act of bowing or showing respect, such as:

* give a salaam: 行个礼
* deep salaam: 深深的鞠躬
* respectful salaam: 恭敬的行礼
* a curt salaam:  一个简短的鞠躬
* a formal salaam: 一个正式的鞠躬
