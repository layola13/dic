gourmet:/ˈɡʊrmeɪ/| n.|美食家；美味佳肴|A gourmet is a person who enjoys and is knowledgeable about good food and drink.  A gourmet meal is a high-quality and delicious meal.|例句：He's a gourmet who appreciates fine wines and cheeses. 他是一位美食家，欣赏优质葡萄酒和奶酪。

例句：The restaurant offered a gourmet menu with seasonal ingredients. 这家餐厅提供了一份采用当季食材的美食菜单。


近义词：connoisseur, epicure|反义词：glutton (in the sense of being indiscriminate about food)|词性:名词
