mishmosh:/ˈmɪʃmɒʃ/| n. |混杂，大杂烩，杂乱无章 |A mishmash is a disorganized collection of different things. It implies a lack of uniformity or coherence.  |例句：The room was a mishmash of furniture and belongings.  房间里堆满了杂七杂八的家具和物品。

The presentation was a mishmash of unrelated ideas.  这次演示是一堆毫无关联的想法的大杂烩。|近义词：hodgepodge, jumble, medley, mixture |反义词：order, organization, uniformity
