musmon:/ˈmʌzmɒn/| n.|欧洲盘羊|European mouflon, a wild sheep (Ovis musimon) inhabiting Corsica, Sardinia, and Cyprus.  It is characterized by its short, curved horns and reddish-brown coat. The musmon is an ancestor of many domestic sheep breeds.|The musmon population in Corsica is thriving.|近义词：mouflon, wild sheep|反义词：domestic sheep
