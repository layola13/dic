powwow: /ˈpaʊˌwaʊ/ | n., v. | 议事；集会；讨论；闲聊 |

英文解析:A powwow is a meeting or conference, especially among Native Americans.  It can also refer to a casual or informal gathering for discussion or socializing. As a verb, it means to have a powwow; to discuss something informally.


例句:
n.  The tribe held a powwow to discuss the upcoming harvest.  部落举行了一次议事来讨论即将到来的收成。

n.  Let's have a powwow about the project later.  我们稍后再就这个项目讨论一下吧。


v.  We need to powwow before making a final decision.  在做出最终决定之前，我们需要讨论一下。

v.  They powwowed for hours, trying to solve the problem.  他们讨论了好几个小时，试图解决这个问题。


近义词:conference, meeting, gathering, consultation, discussion, chat


反义词:silence, solitude, separation


词性:
名词 (n.)
动词 (v.)
