give-the-hook:/ɡɪv ðə hʊk/| phrasal verb|解雇；抛弃；甩掉|To dismiss someone from their job or to abandon or reject someone or something.|The company gave him the hook after only six months. 公司在仅仅六个月后就解雇了他。  He gave her the hook after she lied to him. 他在她对他撒谎后甩了她。|fire, sack, dismiss, dump, abandon|keep, retain, employ, cherish|


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They give employees the hook for poor performance.  他们因为表现不佳而解雇员工。
The manager gives the project the hook due to insufficient funding. 经理由于资金不足而放弃了这个项目。


一般过去时:
The band gave their manager the hook after a series of unsuccessful tours. 乐队在经历了一系列不成功的巡演后解雇了他们的经理。
She gave her old car the hook and bought a new one. 她把她的旧车处理掉了，买了一辆新的。


现在完成时:
The company has given several employees the hook this year due to restructuring. 今年公司因为重组已经解雇了几名员工。
He has given his old habits the hook and started a healthier lifestyle. 他已经改掉了他的旧习惯，开始过一种更健康的生活方式。


过去完成时:
They had given the project the hook before realizing its potential. 他们在意识到它的潜力之前就已经放弃了这个项目。
She had given him the hook long before he even knew it was coming. 在他甚至不知道之前，她就很久以前就甩掉他了。


将来时:
They will give him the hook if he doesn't improve his performance. 如果他不改进他的表现，他们将会解雇他。
I will give this old furniture the hook and buy something new. 我将会把这些旧家具处理掉，买一些新的。


虚拟语气:
If he continued to make mistakes, they would give him the hook. 如果他继续犯错，他们就会解雇他。
If I had known it was a bad idea, I would have given the whole plan the hook. 如果我知道这是一个坏主意，我就会放弃整个计划。


被动语态:
He was given the hook due to his unprofessional conduct. 他因为不专业的行为而被解雇。
The project was given the hook after a thorough review. 在彻底审查之后，该项目被放弃了。


疑问句:
Will they give him the hook? 他们会解雇他吗？
Is she going to give her old apartment the hook? 她要处理掉她的旧公寓吗？


否定句:
They didn't give him the hook, even though he made many mistakes. 尽管他犯了很多错误，他们也没有解雇他。
She didn't give the idea the hook, despite some initial reservations. 尽管一开始有些顾虑，她并没有放弃这个主意。


PHRASE->
