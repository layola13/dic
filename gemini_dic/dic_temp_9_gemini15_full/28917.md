carrie:/ˈkæri/| n. v. |凯丽（女子名）；携带，搬运 |A woman's name; to convey or transport something. |例句：Carrie is a popular name. 凯丽是一个流行的名字。  Carrie carried the heavy box. 凯丽搬运了那个沉重的箱子。|近义词：carry, bear, convey|反义词：leave, abandon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She carries her bag to school every day. = 她每天都把书包带到学校。
He carries a lot of responsibility. = 他承担了很多责任。


一般过去时:
I carried the groceries inside. = 我把杂货搬进去了。
She carried her baby in her arms. = 她把婴儿抱在怀里。


现在完成时:
I have carried this heavy box all day. = 我整天都在搬这个沉重的箱子。
She has carried the torch for the cause for many years. = 多年来她一直为这项事业奔走呼号。


过去完成时:
He had carried the message before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他已经传达了这个消息。
She had carried out her plan successfully. = 她已经成功地完成了她的计划。


将来时:
I will carry your luggage to the car. = 我会把你的行李搬到车上去。
They will carry out the experiment next week. = 他们下周将进行实验。


虚拟语气:
If I were stronger, I would carry that heavy table. = 如果我更强壮一些，我会搬动那张沉重的桌子。
I wish I could carry on without feeling sad. = 我希望我能继续生活而不会感到悲伤。


被动语态:
The heavy box was carried by John. = 那个沉重的箱子是由约翰搬运的。
The message was carried by a messenger. = 这条消息是由信使传达的。


疑问句:
Can you carry this for me? = 你能帮我拿这个吗？
What are you carrying? = 你在拿什么？


否定句:
I cannot carry this heavy suitcase. = 我搬不动这个沉重的行李箱。
They didn't carry out the order. = 他们没有执行命令。


PHRASE->
carry on = 继续
carry out = 执行，实施
carry through = 完成，贯彻
carry weight = 有分量，有影响力
carry the day = 获得成功
