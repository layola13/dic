hoaxers:/ˈhoʊksərz/ | n. |恶作剧者，骗子 |Those who perpetrate hoaxes, creating false or misleading information to deceive others.  They often aim to cause confusion, fear, or amusement.  The motivations behind their actions can vary widely. | Example: The hoaxers spread false news about a nonexistent alien invasion.  恶作剧者散布了关于不存在的 外星人入侵的假新闻。

The hoaxers created a convincing fake story about a celebrity's death. 这些恶作剧者编造了一个令人信服的关于名人去世的假故事。|近义词：tricksters, deceivers, pranksters, con artists |反义词：truth-tellers, honest people |名词
