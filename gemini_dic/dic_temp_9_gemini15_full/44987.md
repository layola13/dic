crystallize:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪz/ | v. | 使结晶；明确表达；(思想、计划等)成形 | To form crystals; to become definite or clear; to solidify into a definite form or shape. | The sugar solution crystallized into beautiful rock candy. 糖溶液结晶成美丽的冰糖。  His ideas crystallized into a concrete plan. 他的想法变成了一个具体的计划。 | solidify, solidify, coalesce | liquefy, blur | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The solution crystallizes slowly. = 溶液慢慢结晶。
He crystallizes his thoughts before speaking. = 他在说话之前会理清自己的思路。


一般过去时:
The sugar crystallized overnight. = 糖在夜间结晶了。
She crystallized her argument in a powerful speech. = 她在一个强有力的演讲中明确表达了自己的论点。


现在完成时:
The project has finally crystallized. = 项目终于成形了。
His vision has crystallized over the years. = 他的视野多年来日趋清晰。


过去完成时:
The plan had crystallized before the meeting. = 会议之前计划已经成形了。
They had crystallized their differences before negotiations. = 他们在谈判前已经明确了彼此的分歧。


将来时:
The mixture will crystallize in a few hours. = 混合物将在几小时内结晶。
His ideas will crystallize as he researches further. = 随着他的进一步研究，他的想法将会更加清晰。


虚拟语气:
If the conditions were right, the liquid would crystallize. = 如果条件合适，液体就会结晶。
If he had crystallized his thoughts earlier, he would have presented better. = 如果他早点理清思路，他的陈述就会更好。


被动语态:
The sugar was crystallized by lowering the temperature. = 通过降低温度使糖结晶。
The plan was crystallized after many discussions. = 经过多次讨论后，计划最终成形。


疑问句:
Does the solution crystallize easily? = 溶液容易结晶吗？
Have his thoughts crystallized yet? = 他的想法已经清晰了吗？


否定句:
The solution didn't crystallize. = 溶液没有结晶。
His ideas didn't crystallize into a plan. = 他的想法并没有形成一个计划。
