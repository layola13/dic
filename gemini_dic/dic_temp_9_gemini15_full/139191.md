objection:/əbˈdʒɛkʃən/| n.|反对，异议|The expression of disapproval or opposition to something.|例句：I have no objection to your proposal. 我对你的提议没有异议。|例句：He raised several strong objections to the plan. 他对这个计划提出了几项强烈的反对意见。|近义词：protest, disapproval, dissent, opposition|反义词：approval, acceptance, consent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His objection to the plan is well-founded. = 他对这项计划的反对是有道理的。
There is no objection from my side. = 我这边没有异议。


一般过去时:
Her objection was based on a misunderstanding. = 她的反对是基于误解。
They voiced their objections loudly. = 他们大声地表达了他们的反对意见。


现在完成时:
He has raised several objections to the new policy. = 他已经对这项新政策提出了几项反对意见。
No objections have been received so far. = 到目前为止，还没有收到任何反对意见。


过去完成时:
They had already voiced their objections before the meeting began. = 会议开始前他们就已经表达了他们的反对意见。
She had no objection to his leaving. = 她对他离开没有异议。


将来时:
I anticipate no objections to the project. = 我预计这个项目不会遇到任何反对。
We will address any objections that arise. = 我们将处理出现的任何反对意见。


虚拟语气:
If there were no objections, we would proceed. = 如果没有异议，我们将继续进行。
I wish there were fewer objections to my ideas. = 我希望我的想法少一些反对意见。


被动语态:
His objection was overruled by the judge. = 他的反对意见被法官驳回了。
The proposal was met with strong objections. = 该提案遭到了强烈的反对。


疑问句:
Do you have any objections to this plan? = 你对这个计划有什么异议吗？
Are there any further objections? = 还有其他的反对意见吗？


否定句:
I have no objection to your suggestion. = 我对你的建议没有异议。
There were no objections from the audience. = 观众没有异议。


PHRASE->
raise an objection = 提出异议
have an objection = 有异议
lodge an objection = 提出异议
no objection = 没有异议
overrule an objection = 驳回异议
