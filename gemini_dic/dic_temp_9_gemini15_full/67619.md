extract:/ˈɛkstrækt/| vt. n.|提取，摘录，榨取|to remove or take out something from something else;  a passage or section taken from a book, article, etc.|例句：The dentist extracted my tooth.  牙医拔掉了我的牙。|例句：I've extracted the key information from the report. 我从报告中提取了关键信息。|近义词：remove, take out, excerpt|反义词：insert, add
