naturism:/ˈneɪtʃərɪzəm/ | n. | 自然主义；天体主义 | The practice of living in accordance with nature;  a social movement advocating nudity, especially in the context of outdoor recreation.  It encompasses a wide range of practices, from sunbathing nude to living in close harmony with the natural world.  |例句：Some people practice naturism as a way to reconnect with nature and their bodies.  一些人将自然主义作为一种与自然和自身重新连接的方式。|例句：The naturism movement has faced legal and social challenges in many countries. 自然主义运动在许多国家都面临着法律和社会挑战。|近义词：nudism, natural living |反义词：artificiality, unnaturalness
