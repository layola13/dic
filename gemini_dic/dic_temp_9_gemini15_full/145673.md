overcooking: /ˌoʊvərˈkʊkɪŋ/ | n. | 过度烹调，煮过头 | The act of cooking something for too long, resulting in it being dry, tough, or otherwise undesirable.  | Example: The overcooking of the chicken made it dry and tasteless.  过度烹调的鸡肉使其干巴巴且无味。 |近义词：overdone, burning | 反义词：undercooking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Overcooking ruins many dishes. = 过度烹调毁了许多菜肴。
He overcooks his vegetables every time. = 他每次都把蔬菜煮过头。

一般过去时:
I overcooked the pasta last night. = 我昨晚把意大利面煮过头了。
She overcooked the steak, making it tough. = 她把牛排煮过头了，导致它很老。

I have overcooked the fish several times this week. = 这周我已经好几次把鱼煮过头了。

过去完成时:
