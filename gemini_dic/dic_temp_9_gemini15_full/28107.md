capitalists:/ˈkæpɪtəlɪsts/ | n. |资本家；资本主义者 |People who own capital and use it to produce goods and services for profit.  They are often associated with the capitalist economic system, characterized by private ownership of the means of production and the pursuit of profit. |例句：The capitalists invested heavily in the new technology. 资本家们大量投资于新技术。|例句：Some critics argue that capitalists exploit workers. 一些批评家认为资本家剥削工人。|近义词：business owners, entrepreneurs, investors |反义词：proletariat, socialists, communists |名词
