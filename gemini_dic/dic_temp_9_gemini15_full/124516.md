mount up:/maʊnt ʌp/ | v. | 累积；增加；骑上；攀登 | To increase gradually in amount, number, or size.  Also, to ride a horse or other animal. | 例句：The bills are mounting up. = 账单越积越多。  The soldiers mounted up their horses and rode into battle. = 士兵们骑上马冲进了战场。| 近义词：accumulate, increase, ascend, climb | 反义词：decrease, diminish, descend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The debt mounts up every month. = 债务每个月都在增加。
His anger mounts up slowly over time. = 他的怒气慢慢积累起来。

一般过去时:
The tension mounted up before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，紧张气氛越来越高涨。
The troops mounted up their horses and charged. = 部队骑上马冲锋了。

现在完成时:
The problems have mounted up since the accident. = 自事故发生以来，问题越来越多。
The evidence has mounted up against the suspect. = 对嫌疑人的证据越来越多。

过去完成时:
The pressure had mounted up before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，压力已经越来越大。
The snow had mounted up to several feet. = 雪已经积了几英尺厚。

将来时:
The cost will mount up if we don't act soon. = 如果我们不尽快采取行动，成本将会增加。
The workload will mount up if we don't hire more staff. = 如果我们不雇佣更多员工，工作量将会增加。

虚拟语气:
If we continued this way, our debts would mount up quickly. = 如果我们继续这样下去，我们的债务会迅速增加。
If he hadn't worked so hard, his frustration would have mounted up. = 如果他不那么努力工作，他的挫败感会越来越大。


被动语态:
The evidence is being mounted up against him. = 证据正在被积累起来对他不利。
The troops were mounted up and ready to march. = 部队已经骑上马，准备行军。


疑问句:
How quickly will the interest mount up on the loan? = 贷款利息将以多快的速度累积？
Is the pressure mounting up on the team? = 团队的压力是否越来越大？


否定句:
The numbers didn't mount up as quickly as expected. = 数字的增长速度没有预期的那么快。
The evidence did not mount up against the defendant. = 没有足够的证据证明被告有罪。
