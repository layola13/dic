citation:/saɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 引用；引证；传唤；嘉奖 | A citation is a quotation from a book, article, or other source, usually given to support a point or to provide evidence.  It can also refer to a summons to appear in court or an award or honor. | Example: The professor asked for citations to support the student's claims. 教授要求学生提供证据来支持其说法。  The police officer issued a citation for speeding. 警官开出了超速罚单。 | 近义词：quotation, reference, mention, summons, award | 反义词：omission


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The article cites several studies. = 这篇文章引用了几项研究。
She cites her sources carefully. = 她仔细地引用了她的来源。

一般过去时:
He cited the relevant laws in his argument. = 他在他的论证中引用了相关的法律。
The judge cited the defendant for contempt of court. = 法官以藐视法庭为由传唤了被告。

现在完成时:
They have cited this research extensively in their work. = 他们在他们的工作中广泛引用了这项研究。
She has received numerous citations for her bravery. = 她因勇敢而获得了许多嘉奖。

过去完成时:
The lawyer had cited several precedents before presenting his case. = 律师在陈述案情之前已经引用了几个先例。
The author had cited that source in her previous book. = 作者在之前的书中引用过那个来源。

将来时:
He will cite this article in his upcoming publication. = 他将在即将出版的著作中引用这篇文章。
The court will cite him for violating the law. = 法院将以违反法律为由传唤他。

虚拟语气:
If he had cited his sources properly, the paper would have been accepted. = 如果他正确地引用了他的来源，论文就会被接受。
I wish I had cited more recent research in my thesis. = 我希望我在我的论文中引用更多最新的研究。

被动语态:
The study was cited numerous times in scientific journals. = 这项研究在科学期刊中被多次引用。
He was cited for his outstanding contributions to the field. = 他因对该领域的杰出贡献而获得表彰。

疑问句:
Did the author cite any primary sources? = 作者是否引用了任何主要来源？
Has the study been cited widely? = 这项研究是否被广泛引用？

否定句:
The article did not cite any credible sources. = 这篇文章没有引用任何可信的来源。
The student failed to cite her sources correctly. = 学生未能正确引用她的来源。


PHRASE->
in-text citation =  正文引用
bibliographic citation =  参考文献
legal citation = 法律引用
citation needed =  需要引用（通常用于质疑信息来源）
citation index = 引用索引
