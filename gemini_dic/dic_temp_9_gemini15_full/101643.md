irrefrangible: /ˌɪrɪˈfrændʒəbəl/ | adj. | 不可摧毁的；不可抗拒的；坚不可摧的 | Incapable of being broken or violated; unbreakable.  This word describes something that is extremely strong and resistant to any force that attempts to break or destroy it. It can be used in both literal and figurative senses. | Example: The irrefrangible bond between the two friends was tested by time and distance, but never broken.  两个朋友之间牢不可破的友谊经受了时间和距离的考验，却从未破裂。 |近义词：unbreakable, indestructible, invincible, immutable |反义词：breakable, fragile, vulnerable
