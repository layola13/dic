recognizance:/ˈrekəɡnɪzəns/| n.| 保释；保证；认定；承认 | A formal undertaking, usually given to a court, to appear at a certain time and place or to do some other specified act.  It's a type of surety bond, where the person promises to fulfill a legal obligation, often to appear in court. Failure to meet the obligation results in forfeiture of the recognizance.  | 例句：He was released on his own recognizance. 他被无保释放。

例句：The court accepted his recognizance to appear in court next week. 法院接受了他下周出庭的保证。


近义词：bail, surety, bond, undertaking, acknowledgment

反义词：conviction, imprisonment, incarceration


词性：名词
