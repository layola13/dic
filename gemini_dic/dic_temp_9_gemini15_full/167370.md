pretty:/ˈprɪti/| adj.|漂亮的，美丽的，可爱的，相当的 |  Attractive in appearance; pleasing.  It can also mean "to a fairly significant degree"  |例句：She's a pretty girl. = 她是一个漂亮的女孩。  The flowers are pretty. = 这些花很漂亮。 |近义词：beautiful, lovely, handsome, attractive, fairly, quite |反义词：ugly, unattractive, plain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flowers are pretty. = 这些花很漂亮。
This dress is pretty. = 这件裙子很漂亮。


一般过去时:
The cake was pretty good. = 蛋糕相当好吃。
The movie was pretty boring. = 电影相当无聊。


现在完成时:
Things have been pretty hectic lately. = 最近事情相当繁忙。
She has been pretty successful in her career. = 她在事业上相当成功。


过去完成时:
I had been pretty tired before the vacation. = 假期前我已经相当疲惫了。
We had been pretty sure we would win. = 我们之前相当肯定我们会赢。


将来时:
The weather will be pretty cold tomorrow. = 明天天气会相当寒冷。
The party will be pretty exciting. = 派对将会相当令人兴奋。


虚拟语气:
If the weather were pretty, we would go for a picnic. = 如果天气不错，我们会去野餐。
I wish the situation were pretty clear. = 我希望情况相当明朗。


被动语态:
The house is pretty well-maintained. = 这房子保养得相当好。
The problem is pretty easily solved. = 这个问题很容易解决。


疑问句:
Isn't she pretty? = 她不是很漂亮吗？
Is the view pretty from here? = 从这里看风景很漂亮吗？


否定句:
The painting isn't pretty. = 这幅画不漂亮。
The results aren't pretty. = 结果不太好。


PHRASE->
pretty good = 相当好
pretty much = 几乎，差不多
pretty soon = 马上，很快
pretty well = 相当好，几乎
pretty close = 非常接近
