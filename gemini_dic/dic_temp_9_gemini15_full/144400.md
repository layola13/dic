outstrips:/ˌaʊtˈstrɪps/| vt.|超过，胜过 |To surpass or exceed.  |例句：Her success outstrips all expectations.  她的成功超出了所有人的预期。|例句：His ambition outstrips his ability. 他的雄心壮志超过了他的能力。|近义词：surpass, exceed, excel, transcend|反义词：fall short of, lag behind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The demand outstrips the supply. = 需求超过供应。
His talent outstrips that of his peers. = 他的天赋超过了他的同龄人。

一般过去时:
Last year's sales outstripped those of the previous year. = 去年销售额超过了前一年。
The team outstripped their rivals in the final match. = 该队在决赛中击败了他们的对手。

现在完成时:
The company's growth has outstripped expectations. = 公司的增长已经超过了预期。
Technological advancement has outstripped our ability to control it. = 技术进步已经超过了我们控制它的能力。

过去完成时:
By the time the rescue team arrived, the floodwaters had outstripped the barriers. = 当救援队到达时，洪水已经超过了障碍物。
He had outstripped his opponents by a considerable margin before the final lap. = 在最后一圈之前，他已经大幅领先他的对手。

将来时:
The population is expected to outstrip the available resources. = 预计人口将超过可用资源。
Demand for renewable energy will outstrip supply in the coming years. = 未来几年，对可再生能源的需求将超过供应。

虚拟语气:
If the demand outstripped the supply, prices would rise. = 如果需求超过供应，价格就会上涨。
I wish my skills outstripped my ambitions. = 我希望我的技能超过我的雄心壮志。

被动语态:
The company's growth was outstripped by its competitors. = 公司的增长被竞争对手超过了。
The old record was outstripped by the new one. = 旧纪录被新纪录超过了。

疑问句:
Does the company's revenue outstrip its expenses? = 公司的收入是否超过了支出？
Will technological progress outstrip our ethical considerations? = 技术进步是否会超过我们的伦理考量？

否定句:
His performance didn't outstrip expectations. = 他的表现没有超出预期。
The supply did not outstrip the demand. = 供应没有超过需求。
