peeling:/ˈpiːlɪŋ/| n. v.|削皮；果皮；剥落|The act of removing the skin or outer layer of something, or the skin or outer layer that has been removed.  |例句：The peeling of potatoes is a tedious task. 削土豆皮是一件乏味的工作。

例句：The old paint was peeling off the wall. 旧油漆正在从墙上剥落。|近义词：skinning, paring, shedding, exfoliation|反义词：application, sticking, adhering|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He peels potatoes every day. 他每天削土豆皮。
She peels an orange. 她剥橘子。


一般过去时:
I peeled the apples yesterday. 我昨天削了苹果皮。
They peeled the bananas before eating them. 他们在吃香蕉之前剥了皮。


现在完成时:
I have peeled all the carrots. 我已经把所有胡萝卜都削皮了。
She has peeled the garlic for the soup. 她已经为汤剥好了蒜。


过去完成时:
He had peeled the onions before he started cooking. 他在开始做饭之前已经把洋葱剥好了。
They had peeled the oranges before the party started.  派对开始前他们已经剥好了橘子。


将来时:
I will peel the potatoes for dinner. 我将为晚餐削土豆皮。
She will peel the fruit tomorrow. 她明天会削水果皮。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would peel all the apples. 如果我有时间，我会把所有苹果都削皮。
I wish I had peeled the potatoes earlier. 我真希望我早点把土豆削了皮。


被动语态:
The potatoes were peeled by my sister. 土豆是我姐姐削的皮。

疑问句:
Are you peeling the garlic? 你在剥蒜吗？
Have you peeled the carrots yet? 你已经把胡萝卜削皮了吗？


否定句:
