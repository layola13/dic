reinvolving: /ˌriːɪnˈvɒlvɪŋ/ | v. |  重新卷入；再次牵涉 |  The act of involving someone or something again; bringing someone or something back into a situation or process.  |  例句：The ongoing investigation is reinvolving several witnesses. (持续的调查正在重新牵涉到几位证人。)

例句：The scandal reinvolved the former mayor in a new controversy. (这起丑闻使前任市长再次卷入新的争议。)


近义词：re-engaging, reintroducing, re-including

反义词：excluding, removing, disengaging

词性：动词
