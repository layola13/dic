ofc:/ɒfˈkoːs/| adj. adv.|当然，自然|Of course, it's a great idea.  当然，这是一个好主意。|近义词：certainly, surely, naturally|反义词：no way, absolutely not


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I will, of course, help you. = 我当然会帮助你。
Of course, I understand your concerns. = 当然，我理解你的担忧。


一般过去时:
He said, of course, he would come. = 他当然说他会来。
She of course agreed to the proposal. = 她当然同意了这个提议。


现在完成时:
I have, of course, finished the project. = 我当然已经完成了项目。
She has, of course, already heard the news. = 她当然已经听到这个消息了。


过去完成时:
He had, of course, already left before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，他当然已经离开了。
They had of course anticipated this problem. = 他们当然已经预料到这个问题了。


将来时:
We will, of course, be there on time. = 我们当然会准时到那里。
It will, of course, be a challenging task. = 这当然会是一项具有挑战性的任务。


虚拟语气:
I would, of course, help if I could. = 如果我能的话，我当然会帮忙。
If I were you, I would, of course, accept the offer. = 如果我是你，我当然会接受这个提议。


被动语态:
The problem will, of course, be solved soon. = 这个问题当然很快就会解决。
The invitation was, of course, accepted. = 邀请当然被接受了。


疑问句:
Would you, of course, like some tea? = 你当然想要一些茶吗？
Will it of course rain tomorrow? = 明天当然会下雨吗？


否定句:
It is not, of course, always easy. = 这当然并不总是容易的。
He did not, of course, mean to offend anyone. = 他当然不是有意冒犯任何人。


PHRASE->
