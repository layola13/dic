perusing:/pəˈruːzɪŋ/| v.|仔细阅读；审阅；浏览|To read (something), typically in a thorough or careful way.|例句：I spent the afternoon perusing old photographs. 我花了一下午时间仔细翻阅旧照片。|例句：She perused the contract before signing it. 她在签署合同之前仔细阅读了合同。|近义词：reading, studying, examining, scanning|反义词：skimming, glancing


USAGE->

He peruses the newspaper every morning. = 他每天早上都仔细阅读报纸。
She peruses the menu carefully before ordering. = 她点菜前仔细研究菜单。
