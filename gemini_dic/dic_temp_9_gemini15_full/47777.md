decantate:/dɪˈkænteɪt/| vt.|倾析；慢慢倒出(液体)|To carefully pour a liquid from one container to another, leaving sediment behind.  The process is typically done slowly to avoid disturbing the sediment.|例句：We decantated the wine to remove the sediment. 我们倾析葡萄酒以去除沉淀物。

例句：The chemist carefully decantated the solution into a clean beaker. 化学家小心地将溶液倾析到一个干净的烧杯中。


近义词：pour, strain, filter|反义词：None specifically, but actions that mix or agitate would be the opposite.|词性:动词
