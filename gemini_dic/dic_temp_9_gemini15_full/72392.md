fluctuation:/ˌflʌkʃuˈeɪʃən/| n. |波动；起伏；变动 |A fluctuation is a change in a price, amount, level, or intensity.  It refers to the variation or oscillation between different levels or values over time.  |例句：The stock market experienced significant fluctuations throughout the day. 股市全天经历了剧烈的波动。

例句：There has been a fluctuation in the water level of the river due to recent rainfall.  由于最近的降雨，河流水位发生了波动。


近义词：variation, oscillation, change, shift, swing |反义词：stability, constancy, steadiness |词性: 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price fluctuates daily. = 价格每天都在波动。
The temperature fluctuates throughout the year. = 温度全年都在波动。

一般过去时:
The stock price fluctuated wildly yesterday. = 股票价格昨天剧烈波动。
The water level fluctuated during the storm. = 暴风雨期间水位波动。

现在完成时:
The value of the currency has fluctuated considerably this year. = 今年货币价值波动相当大。
The market has fluctuated since the announcement. = 自从公告发布以来，市场一直在波动。

过去完成时:
The economy had fluctuated for months before the crisis. = 危机爆发前，经济已经波动了好几个月。
The temperature had fluctuated dramatically before stabilizing. = 温度在稳定下来之前剧烈波动。

将来时:
The price is expected to fluctuate in the coming weeks. = 价格预计在未来几周内波动。
The weather is likely to fluctuate throughout the week. = 本周天气可能会波动。

虚拟语气:
If the exchange rate fluctuated less, businesses would be more stable. = 如果汇率波动较小，企业将更加稳定。
I wish the prices didn't fluctuate so much. = 我希望价格不要波动这么大。

被动语态:
The data was carefully analyzed to identify any fluctuations. = 对数据进行了仔细分析，以识别任何波动。
The results were affected by fluctuations in the power supply. = 结果受电源波动影响。

疑问句:
Do you think the prices will fluctuate next month? = 你认为下个月价格会波动吗？
How much do you expect the exchange rate to fluctuate? = 你预计汇率波动多少？

否定句:
The quality of the product does not fluctuate. = 产品质量不会波动。
The signal did not fluctuate during the experiment. = 实验期间信号没有波动。
