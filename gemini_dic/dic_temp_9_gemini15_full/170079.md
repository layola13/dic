protestingly:/prəˈtestɪŋli/| adv.|抗议地，反对地|In a way that shows protest or disapproval.  It describes the manner in which an action is performed, indicating dissent or objection.|例句：He spoke protestingly, his voice trembling with anger. 他抗议地说，声音因愤怒而颤抖。 He shook his head protestingly. 他抗议地摇了摇头。|近义词：objectingly, resentfully, rebelliously|反义词：submissively, willingly, agreeably


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It doesn't have tenses.  Its usage is always adverbial, modifying a verb.


PHRASE->
There are no common set phrases specifically using "protestingly".  The word is used to describe the manner of an action within a phrase or sentence.  Examples:
protestingly refused: 抗议般地拒绝
spoke protestingly: 抗议地说
acted protestingly: 抗议般地行动
shook his head protestingly: 抗议地摇了摇头
left protestingly: 抗议地离开
