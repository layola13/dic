iable: /ˈaɪəbl/ | adj. | 可靠的；可信赖的 | Capable of being relied on; dependable.  | The new bridge is iable to withstand heavy traffic. 新桥能够承受大量的交通流量。 | reliable, trustworthy, dependable | unreliable, untrustworthy, undependable | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system is liable to errors.  = 这个系统容易出错。
This product is liable for damage. = 此产品承担损坏责任。

一般过去时:
The company was liable for the accident. = 公司对事故负有责任。
He was liable to sudden anger. = 他容易突然发怒。


现在完成时:
The company has been liable for all damages. = 公司一直对所有损失负责。
He has been liable to fits of depression. = 他一直容易陷入抑郁。


过去完成时:
The driver had been liable for the accident. = 司机对事故负有责任。
She had been liable to faint. = 她容易晕倒。


将来时:
The project will be liable to delays. = 项目可能会延迟。
He will be liable to prosecution. = 他将面临起诉。


虚拟语气:
If the product were liable to defects, we would recall it. = 如果产品有缺陷，我们会召回它。
I wish the car were not liable to breakdowns. = 我希望这辆车不会经常出故障。


被动语态:
The company was found liable for negligence. = 公司被发现有过失责任。
The driver was held liable for the damage. = 司机被判承担损坏责任。


疑问句:
Is the company liable for the damages? = 公司对损失负有责任吗？
Is he liable to punishment? = 他会受到惩罚吗？


否定句:
The witness was not liable to be cross-examined. = 证人无需接受盘问。
The company is not liable for any indirect losses. = 公司对任何间接损失不负责任。



PHRASE->
liable to = 易于…；有…倾向的
liable for = 对…负有责任的
strictly liable = 严格责任
jointly and severally liable = 共同和个别责任
vicariously liable = 替代责任
