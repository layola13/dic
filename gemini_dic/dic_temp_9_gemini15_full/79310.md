gleam:/ɡliːm/| n., v.|微光，闪光；闪耀，闪光|A faint, soft light; to shine softly.|The diamond gleamed on her finger.  钻石在她手指上闪闪发光。|The polished wood gleamed in the sunlight.  抛光的木头在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：shine, shimmer, glitter|反义词：dullness, darkness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The star gleams brightly in the night sky. = 星星在夜空中闪耀。
The polished surface gleams under the lamp. = 抛光后的表面在灯下闪闪发光。
