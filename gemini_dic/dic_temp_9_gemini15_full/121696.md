mispractise:/ˌmɪsˈpræktɪs/| n. v.|渎职；误诊；不正当行为 | Improper or illegal practice, especially in a professional context.  It can refer to negligent or unethical conduct by professionals such as doctors, lawyers, or teachers. |例句：The doctor was accused of medical mispractise after a patient died during surgery.  医生在一名病人手术中死亡后被指控犯有医疗渎职罪。


The lawyer's mispractise cost his client a significant amount of money.  律师的渎职行为使他的当事人损失了一大笔钱。|近义词：malpractice, misconduct, negligence, wrongdoing |反义词：proper practice, ethical conduct, due diligence
