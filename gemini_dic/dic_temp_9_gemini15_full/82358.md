growlers:/ˈɡroʊlər/| n.|咕噜声；低沉的抱怨声；（装啤酒的）大桶；（小型）鲨鱼|A growler is a large container, typically a jug or a small keg, used for carrying beer. It can also refer to a low, rumbling sound like a growl, or a small shark.|例句：He bought a growler of his favorite IPA from the brewery. 他从酿酒厂买了一大桶他最喜欢的印度淡啤酒。|例句：The growler of the engine was unsettling. 发动机的低沉轰鸣声令人不安。|近义词：rumbler, grumbler, keg|反义词：whisper, murmur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine growls loudly. = 发动机发出巨大的轰鸣声。
The bear growls at the intruder. = 熊对着入侵者咆哮。

一般过去时:
The dog growled at the postman. = 狗对着邮递员咆哮。
My stomach growled because I was hungry. = 我肚子饿了，咕咕叫。

现在完成时:
The machine has been growling all morning. = 这台机器整个上午都在发出轰鸣声。
He has growled at me several times today. = 他今天已经对我咆哮了好几次。


过去完成时:
The engine had been growling for hours before it finally broke down. = 发动机在最终抛锚前已经轰鸣了好几个小时。
She had growled at him earlier, so he avoided her. = 她之前对他咆哮过，所以他躲着她。

将来时:
The lion will growl if you get too close. = 如果你靠得太近，狮子会咆哮。
The old car will probably growl when you start it. = 你启动这辆旧车时，它可能会发出隆隆声。

虚拟语气:
If I were a bear, I would growl at anyone who disturbed me. = 如果我是一只熊，我会对任何打扰我的人咆哮。
I wish the old car didn't growl so much. = 我希望这辆旧车不要发出这么大的隆隆声。

被动语态:
The noise was growled by the animal. = 动物发出了咆哮声。(This usage is less common)
The song was described as growling bass. = 这首歌被描述为低沉的贝斯声。

疑问句:
Does the engine growl when it's cold? = 发动机在冷的时候会发出隆隆声吗？
Why did the dog growl at the child? = 为什么狗对着孩子咆哮？

否定句:
The cat didn't growl at the stranger. = 猫没有对着陌生人咆哮。
The engine didn't growl; it just sputtered and died. = 发动机没有发出隆隆声；它只是发出噼里啪啦的声音然后熄火了。
