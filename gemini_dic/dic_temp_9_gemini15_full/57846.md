词源:
The word "dressing-gown" is a compound word.  "Dressing" refers to the act of getting dressed, and "gown" refers to a long loose robe.  It evolved naturally from the practice of wearing a loose robe while getting dressed or undressed, hence the name.  The term has been in use since the 18th century.
