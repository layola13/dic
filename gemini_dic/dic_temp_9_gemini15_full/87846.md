hideaway:/ˈhaɪdəweɪ/| n. |隐蔽处；秘密地点；隐居处 |A hideaway is a secluded or hidden place, often used for relaxation or escape.  It can be a small, private space or a more remote location. |例句：Their mountain cabin served as their perfect hideaway from the city's hustle and bustle.  他们的山间小屋是他们远离城市喧嚣的完美隐蔽处。

例句：The artist found inspiration in his secluded hideaway by the sea.  这位艺术家在海边的隐蔽处找到了灵感。


近义词：retreat, sanctuary, refuge, haven |反义词：public place, open space |词性:名词
