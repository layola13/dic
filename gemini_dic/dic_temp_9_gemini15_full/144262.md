outsoars:/ˌaʊtˈsɔrz/| vt.|超越，胜过|To surpass or exceed in height, extent, or power.|例句：The mountain outsoars all the surrounding hills. 这座山比周围所有的山都高。|例句：Her ambition outsoars her capabilities. 她的雄心壮志超过了她的能力。|近义词：surpass, exceed, transcend|反义词：underperform, fall short of
