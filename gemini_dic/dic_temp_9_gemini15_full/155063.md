petrifaction:/ˌpɛtrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |石化；化石；惊呆|The process of being turned into stone; the state of being petrified; something that has been turned to stone.  It can also refer to a state of shock or amazement.|例句：The petrifaction of the wood was a result of long exposure to minerals. 木头的石化是长期暴露于矿物质的结果。

The petrifaction of the audience was complete after the shocking announcement. 令人震惊的公告之后，观众完全惊呆了。|近义词：fossilization, mineralization, stupefaction|反义词：revival, animation
