lockjaw: /ˈlɒkdʒɔː/ /ˈlɑːkdʒɔː/ | n. | 破伤风 |  A serious disease caused by bacteria, characterized by painful muscle spasms, especially in the jaw.  It is often fatal if untreated. |  The tetanus bacteria caused a severe case of lockjaw.  破伤风细菌导致了严重的破伤风。  The child developed lockjaw after stepping on a rusty nail.  孩子踩到生锈的钉子后得了破伤风。| tetanus, trismus |  (no direct antonym, but 'absence of lockjaw' or 'healthy jaw muscles' could be considered the opposite)| 名词
