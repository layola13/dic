radiality:/ˌreɪdiˈælɪti/| n. |辐射性；辐射状排列；径向性|The quality or state of being radial; arrangement in a radial manner;  the property of something exhibiting radial symmetry.  It refers to the arrangement of parts around a central point, like spokes on a wheel.|例句：The flower exhibits a striking radiality in its petal arrangement. 这朵花的花瓣排列展现出惊人的辐射状。 The radiality of the design is evident in its symmetrical pattern.  该设计的径向性在其对称图案中显而易见。|近义词：radial symmetry, radiation,  arrangement|反义词：bilateral symmetry
