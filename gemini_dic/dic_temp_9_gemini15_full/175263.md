rainbow:/ˈreɪnboʊ/| n. |彩虹|A meteorological phenomenon that is caused by reflection, refraction and dispersion of light in water droplets resulting in a spectrum of light appearing in the sky. It is typically seen after or during rainfall.|例句：A beautiful rainbow arched across the sky.  一道美丽的彩虹横跨天空。|例句：We saw a double rainbow after the storm.  暴风雨过后我们看到了一道双彩虹。|近义词：arc, spectrum |反义词：无
