contentednesses:/kənˈtɛntɪdnɪsɪz/ | n. |满足感(复数) |The state of being content; multiple instances or types of contentment.  It refers to various forms or aspects of satisfaction and happiness. |例句：His contentednesses were numerous: a loving family, a successful career, and good health.  他的满足感很多：一个充满爱的家庭，一份成功的事业和良好的健康。

The various contentednesses he experienced throughout his life led to a profound sense of peace. 他一生中体验到的各种满足感让他感到一种深刻的平静。|近义词：satisfactions, gratifications, pleasures |反义词：dissatisfactions, discontents, miseries |名词
