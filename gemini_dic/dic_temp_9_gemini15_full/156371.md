physiologies:/ˌfɪziˈɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 生理学(复数) | The study of the functions of living organisms and their parts.  It encompasses the mechanical, physical, and biochemical functions of humans, animals, and plants. | Example:  The professor's lectures covered various aspects of animal physiologies.  教授的讲座涵盖了动物生理学的各个方面。  |近义词：biological functions, bodily processes |反义词：None directly applicable.  | 名词
