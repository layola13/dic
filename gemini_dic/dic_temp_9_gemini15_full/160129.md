pompadour:/ˈpɒmpədɔːr/ /ˈpɑːmpədɔːr/| n. adj.|蓬巴杜式发型；华丽的；夸张的|A pompadour is a hairstyle where the hair is combed up high from the forehead.  The term often evokes a sense of elegance and extravagance. It derives its name from Madame de Pompadour, a prominent and fashionable figure in 18th-century France, who popularized the style.|例句：He sported a classic pompadour. 他留着经典的蓬巴杜式发型。

His room was decorated in a rather pompadour style.  他的房间装饰得相当华丽。|近义词：bouffant, upsweep|反义词：simple, understated
