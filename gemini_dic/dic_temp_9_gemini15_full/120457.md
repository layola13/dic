minored:/ˈmaɪnərd/| v.|辅修，少量存在|to study a subject as a secondary subject at college or university |例句：She minored in art history while majoring in English. 她主修英语，辅修艺术史。|近义词：supplement, subsidiary |反义词:major in


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He minors in music. = 他辅修音乐。
She minors in psychology. = 她辅修心理学。


一般过去时:
I minored in history in college. = 我大学辅修历史。
He minored in physics during his undergraduate studies. = 他本科期间辅修物理。


现在完成时:
She has minored in two subjects besides her major. = 除了主修之外，她还辅修了两门课程。
He has minored in mathematics and now plans to pursue a career in finance. = 他辅修了数学，现在计划从事金融行业。


过去完成时:
Before graduating, she had minored in both art and music. = 毕业前，她辅修了艺术和音乐。
By the time he finished his degree, he had minored in three different subjects. = 到他完成学位时，他已经辅修了三门不同的科目。


将来时:
I will minor in computer science next semester. = 下学期我将辅修计算机科学。
She will minor in French to enhance her language skills. = 她将辅修法语来提高她的语言技能。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would have minored in a foreign language. = 如果我有更多时间，我就会辅修一门外语。
I wish I had minored in business when I was in college. = 我真希望我在大学时辅修过商业。


被动语态:
A minor subject was required for graduation. = 毕业需要辅修一门科目。
This subject is not often minored in. = 这门课很少有人辅修。


疑问句:
What did you minor in at university? = 你大学辅修什么？
What will you be minoring in next year? = 你明年要辅修什么？


否定句:
He did not minor in any subject other than his major. = 他除了主修科目外，没有辅修任何科目。
She decided not to minor in art because of her busy schedule. = 由于时间安排紧张，她决定不辅修艺术。


PHRASE->

minor in something = 辅修某科目
a minor role/part =次要角色/部分
a minor detail =次要细节
minor in age =未成年
minor in significance =微不足道
