pyrotechnics:/ˌpaɪroʊˈtɛknɪks/| n. |烟火技术；烟火表演|The art and technique of making and using fireworks.  Pyrotechnics involves the use of combustible materials to create visual effects, such as explosions, flames, and sparks.|例句：The fireworks display was a spectacular example of pyrotechnics.  这场烟火表演是烟火技术的精彩范例。

The local festival featured a dazzling pyrotechnics show. 当地节日以一场炫目的烟火表演为特色。|近义词：fireworks, pyrotechny|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with something like "quiet celebration" or "simple lighting"
