mustache:/ˈmʌstæʃ/| n. |胡子（唇上髭须）|A mustache is the hair that grows on a man's upper lip.  It is a facial hair feature. |He has a magnificent handlebar mustache. 他留着令人印象深刻的八字胡。|beard, whiskers|clean-shaven
