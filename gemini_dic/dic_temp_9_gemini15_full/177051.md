reannouncements:/ˌriːəˈnaʊnsmənts/| n.|重新发布公告，再次宣布|The act of announcing something again; a repeated announcement.  It often implies a previous announcement was insufficient, unclear, or needed further emphasis.|例句：The company issued reannouncements to clarify the details of the merger. 公司发布了重新公告以澄清合并的细节。
The reannouncements of the policy changes were met with mixed reactions.  政策变更的再次宣布受到了褒贬不一的回应。|近义词：reiterations, reminders, repetitions|反义词：withdrawals, cancellations|名词
