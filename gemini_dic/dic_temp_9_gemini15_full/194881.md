semi-diurnal:/ˌsɛmiˈdaɪɜːrnəl/| adj.|半日两次的；一天两次的|Occurring twice a day, especially referring to tides.  The term is commonly used in describing tidal patterns where two high tides and two low tides occur each day.|例句：The semi-diurnal tides are a regular feature of the coastline.  = 半日潮是海岸线的一个常见特征。

例句：Many coastal areas experience semi-diurnal tidal patterns. = 许多沿海地区经历半日潮汐模式。


近义词：twice-daily|反义词：diurnal (daily),  a-diurnal (once a day)


词性:形容词
