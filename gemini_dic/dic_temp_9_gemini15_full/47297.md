de-icers:/diːˈaɪsərz/| n.|除冰剂，融冰剂|Substances used to melt ice and snow, typically applied to roads, runways, and sidewalks.  They often contain salts like sodium chloride or calcium chloride, or other chemicals.|例句：The city uses de-icers on the roads during winter storms.  城市在冬季暴风雪期间会在道路上使用除冰剂。|例句：Applying de-icers too early can be ineffective and wasteful.  过早使用除冰剂可能无效且浪费。|近义词：ice melters, de-icing agents|反义词：ice-forming substances|名词
