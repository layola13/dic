flicky:/ˈflɪki/| adj.|轻率的；反复无常的；不稳定的；（尤指动作）快速的，灵巧的 |Flicking describes something quick, light, and often repeated. It can refer to a movement or a person's behaviour.  |例句：He gave a flicky wave of his hand. 他快速地挥了挥手。  The flickering candlelight cast dancing shadows on the wall. 闪烁的烛光在墙上投下舞动的阴影。|近义词：nimble, agile, erratic, capricious, volatile|反义词：steady, stable, constant, deliberate, slow
