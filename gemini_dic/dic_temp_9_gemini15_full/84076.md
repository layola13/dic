half-hungered:/ˈhæfˌhʌŋɡərd/| adj. |半饥饿的，饥肠辘辘的 |Partially starved; feeling a persistent, moderate hunger.|例句：The half-hungered children eagerly devoured the meager meal. 半饥饿的孩子们急切地吃掉了那顿可怜的饭。 The long journey left them half-hungered and exhausted. 漫长的旅程使他们又饿又累。|近义词：hungry, famished, peckish |反义词：full, satiated
