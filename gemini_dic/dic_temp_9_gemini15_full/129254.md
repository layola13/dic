nod-out:/nɑːdˈaʊt/| v. |打瞌睡；睡着；昏昏欲睡|To fall asleep, especially unintentionally and briefly.  It often implies a lack of energy or exhaustion.|

例句：

I nearly nod out during the boring lecture. = 我在枯燥的讲座中差点睡着。

He nodded out on the sofa after a long day of work. = 他工作了一整天后在沙发上睡着了。


近义词：doze off, drift off, fall asleep, slump

反义词：stay awake, be alert
