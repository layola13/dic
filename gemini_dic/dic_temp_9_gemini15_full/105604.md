kinds:/kaɪndz/| n.|种类，类型，性质|Kinds refers to a number of things or people sharing similar characteristics.  It implies a diversity within a category.|There are many kinds of apples.|types, sorts, varieties, categories|uniqueness, individuality


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There are many kinds of birds in this park. = 这个公园里有许多种鸟类。
What kinds of music do you like? = 你喜欢什么类型的音乐？

一般过去时:
There were several kinds of cakes at the party. = 派对上有好几种蛋糕。
What kinds of games did they play? = 他们玩了什么类型的游戏？

现在完成时:
We have seen many kinds of animals on this trip. = 在这次旅行中，我们已经看到了许多种动物。
What kinds of books have you read recently? = 你最近读了什么类型的书？

过去完成时:
They had already tried several kinds of solutions before they found the right one. = 在找到正确答案之前，他们已经尝试过几种解决方案了。
What kinds of cars had she owned before this one? = 在买这辆车之前，她拥有过哪些类型的车？

将来时:
We will explore many kinds of marine life during our dive. = 在潜水期间，我们将探索许多种海洋生物。
What kinds of jobs will be available in the future? = 未来将会有哪些工作机会？

虚拟语气:
If there were more kinds of vegetables available, I would eat healthier. = 如果有更多种类的蔬菜可供选择，我会吃得更健康。
I wish there were more kinds of entertainment options in this town. = 我希望这个城镇有更多种类的娱乐选择。

被动语态:
Many kinds of flowers are grown in this garden. = 这个花园里种植着许多种花。
Different kinds of fabrics are used to make this dress. = 这件衣服是用不同种类的面料制作的。

疑问句:
What kinds of food do you prefer? = 你更喜欢哪种食物？
What kinds of problems are you facing? = 你面临着哪些问题？

否定句:
There weren't many kinds of fruits in the shop. = 商店里水果种类不多。
I don't like many kinds of spicy food. = 我不喜欢许多种辛辣食物。
