realizable: /ˈriːəlaɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 可实现的，可完成的 | capable of being achieved or attained; feasible.  |例句：The project, while ambitious, is still realizable with sufficient funding.  这个项目虽然雄心勃勃，但只要有足够的资金，仍然是可以实现的。

例句：Her dreams of becoming a doctor are entirely realizable with hard work and dedication.  她成为一名医生的梦想只要努力和奉献就能完全实现。


近义词：achievable, attainable, feasible, practicable

反义词：impossible, unattainable, infeasible, impractical

词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The goal is realizable. = 目标是可以实现的。
The plan is realizable with more resources. =  有了更多资源，这个计划是可以实现的。

一般过去时:
The project was realizable before the budget cuts. = 在预算削减之前，这个项目是可以实现的。
The task was not realizable given the time constraints. =  考虑到时间限制，这项任务是无法实现的。

现在完成时:
The dream has become realizable thanks to her hard work. = 多亏了她的努力，梦想已经变得可以实现了。
The goal has proven realizable after several attempts. =  经过几次尝试后，目标已被证明是可以实现的。

过去完成时:
The plan had been realizable, but the unforeseen circumstances made it impossible. =  这个计划本来是可以实现的，但不可预见的情况使其变得不可能。
They had realized that the objective was no longer realizable. = 他们意识到目标已经无法实现了。

将来时:
The project will be realizable once we secure funding. = 一旦我们获得资金，项目将能够实现。
The innovation will make the previously unrealizable goal realizable. = 这项创新将使以前无法实现的目标变得可以实现。

虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, the project would be realizable. = 如果我们有更多资源，项目是可以实现的。
If only the conditions were better, the goal would be realizable. =  如果条件更好一些，目标是可以实现的。

被动语态:
The target is considered realizable by the team. =  团队认为目标是可以实现的。
The project's feasibility is being assessed to determine if it is realizable. =  正在评估项目的可行性，以确定它是否可行。

疑问句:
Is the deadline realizable? = 截止日期可以实现吗？
Is the proposed solution realizable within the given timeframe? = 在给定的时间范围内，提出的解决方案可行吗？

否定句:
The goal is not realizable without additional funding. =  没有额外资金，目标是无法实现的。
The project proved to be not realizable due to unforeseen technical difficulties. =  由于不可预见的技术难题，该项目证明是无法实现的。

词源:  The word "realizable" derives from the verb "realize," which in turn comes from the Old French word "realiser," meaning "to make real."  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or possibility.
