prelicensing: /ˌpriːˈlaɪsənsiŋ/ | n. | 预先许可；预先授权 | The process of obtaining a license or permission before an activity begins.  It often involves meeting specific requirements and undergoing approvals before the actual licensing process. | Example:  The prelicensing phase for the new drug involved extensive clinical trials.  预先许可新药的阶段包括广泛的临床试验。| 近义词：prior authorization, advance approval | 反义词：post-licensing
