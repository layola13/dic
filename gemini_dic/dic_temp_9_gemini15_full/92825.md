iconological:/ˌaɪkənəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. |图像学的；图像符号学的 | Relating to iconology, the study of images and their symbolic meaning.  Iconological analysis goes beyond simply identifying what is depicted in an image to interpret its deeper cultural and historical significance. | Example: The painting's iconological interpretation reveals a hidden critique of the ruling class.  这幅画的图像学解读揭示了对统治阶级的隐秘批判。 |近义词：semiotic, symbolic |反义词：literal, realistic
