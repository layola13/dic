hoist:/hɔɪst/| /hɔɪst/| vt. n.|提升；吊起；升起|To lift or raise something heavy, typically using a machine.  The word often implies a vertical movement.|例句：The workers hoisted the heavy beam into place using a crane. 工人们用起重机将沉重的梁吊到适当位置。
例句：The flag was hoisted at sunrise.  国旗在日出时升起。|raise, lift, elevate|lower, drop|动词，名词


USAGE->

They hoist the sails. = 他们升起帆。
He hoists the flag every morning. = 他每天早上升国旗。

一般过去时:
The crane hoisted the container onto the ship. = 起重机把集装箱吊上了船。

将来时:
