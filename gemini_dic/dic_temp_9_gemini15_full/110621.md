limpets:/ˈlɪmpɪts/| n. |帽贝；石鳖|A limpet is a small shellfish with a conical shell that clings tightly to rocks.  They are found in intertidal zones and are known for their strong adhesion. |例句：The limpets clung tenaciously to the slippery rocks. 帽贝顽强地依附在光滑的岩石上。


例句：We found many limpets on the rocky shore during our low tide walk.  我们在退潮时在岩石海岸上发现了许多帽贝。


近义词：none (no direct synonym, but other shellfish could be considered similar)|反义词：none|词性：名词
