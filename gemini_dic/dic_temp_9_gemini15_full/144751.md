over-chilled: /ˌoʊvərˈtʃɪld/ | adj. | 过度冷却的，冷过头了的 | excessively chilled; colder than intended or necessary. | The soup was over-chilled and lacked flavor. 这汤冷过头了，而且缺乏味道。|近义词：super-cooled, excessively cold | 反义词：overheated, lukewarm


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It's used to describe something that is too cold.  There's no specific tense conjugation needed.

Example Sentences:

The wine was over-chilled, making it less enjoyable.  这葡萄酒冷过头了，喝起来不太舒服。

My drink was over-chilled; I had to let it sit for a while to warm up. 我的饮料冷过头了；我不得不让它坐一会儿暖和起来。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases specifically using "over-chilled".  The term is generally used descriptively.
