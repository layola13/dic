linguistic:/lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk/ | adj. |语言学的；语言的 | Relating to linguistics or language.  |Linguistic analysis is crucial to understanding the nuances of a text.|近义词：philological|反义词：non-linguistic


例句：

The professor gave a fascinating lecture on linguistic theory.  教授做了一个关于语言学理论的精彩讲座。

Her linguistic skills allowed her to easily translate the document.  她的语言技能使她能够轻松地翻译该文件。


USAGE->

This word is primarily an adjective, and therefore doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It does not have tenses or aspects.  The grammatical function is determined by its use in a sentence.  Examples showing its use in different sentence structures are given below.

Declarative Sentence:
Linguistic diversity is important. 语言多样性很重要。
The linguistic structure of the sentence is complex. 句子的语言结构很复杂。

Interrogative Sentence:
Is this a linguistic phenomenon? 这是一个语言现象吗？
Are there any linguistic differences between the two dialects? 这两种方言之间是否存在任何语言差异？

Exclamatory Sentence:
What a fascinating linguistic discovery! 多么令人着迷的语言学发现！
That's a truly impressive linguistic achievement! 那真是一个令人印象深刻的语言成就！
