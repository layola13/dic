nomocracy:/noʊˈmɑːkrəsi/ | n. | 法治；法律统治 | A system of government in which the rule of law is supreme and all individuals and institutions are subject to it.  It emphasizes the importance of established laws and legal procedures in governing society, rather than the arbitrary will of rulers or the influence of special interests. | Example:  The country transitioned from a dictatorship to a nomocracy, where the rule of law was paramount.  这个国家从独裁统治转变为法治社会，法律至上。 | 近义词：rule of law, legalism | 反义词：tyranny, autocracy
