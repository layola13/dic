deploy:/dɪˈplɔɪ/ | vt. | 部署；展开；运用 | To put (troops, equipment, etc.) into position for military action. To bring into effective action. To make use of something, especially in a planned and effective way. | 例句：The army deployed its troops along the border.  军队沿边境部署了部队。

The company deployed a new marketing strategy. 公司部署了一项新的营销策略。


近义词：implement, utilize, utilize,  unfold,  em ploy | 反义词：withdraw,  retreat, recall
