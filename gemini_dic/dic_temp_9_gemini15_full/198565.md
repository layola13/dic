shrugs: /ʃrʌɡz/ | v. |耸肩| To raise and lower your shoulders, usually to express that you do not know or care about something.  It often implies indifference, lack of knowledge, or resignation.|

例句：

He shrugged and walked away. 他耸了耸肩，走开了。

She shrugged her shoulders in response to my question. 她对我的问题耸了耸肩作为回应。


近义词：shrugged


反义词：N/A (No direct antonym, as it's an action expressing a lack of something)


词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shrugs his shoulders when he doesn't understand. = 他不明白的时候会耸耸肩。
She shrugs off criticism easily. = 她很容易就忽略批评。

一般过去时:
He shrugged his shoulders in response. = 他耸了耸肩作为回应。
She shrugged off the insult. = 她对侮辱置之不理。

现在完成时:
He has shrugged off many challenges in his life. = 他一生中克服了许多挑战。
She has shrugged off the suggestion. = 她对这个建议置之不理。

过去完成时:
He had shrugged off the warning before the accident. = 事故发生前，他忽视了警告。
She had shrugged off her responsibilities. = 她之前一直推卸责任。

将来时:
He will shrug off the pressure. = 他会摆脱压力。
She will shrug off the negative comments. = 她会无视负面评论。

虚拟语气:
If he understood, he wouldn't shrug his shoulders. = 如果他理解了，他就不会耸肩。
I wish she wouldn't shrug off my concerns. = 我希望她不要忽视我的担忧。

被动语态:  (Shrug is rarely used passively)  N/A

疑问句:
Does he always shrug when he's unsure? = 他不确定的时候总是耸肩吗？
Why did she shrug off my help? = 她为什么拒绝我的帮助？

否定句:
He didn't shrug; he answered directly. = 他没有耸肩；他直接回答了。
She didn't shrug off the opportunity. = 她没有错过这个机会。


PHRASE->
shrug something off:  to ignore or treat something as unimportant  忽略，不理会
shrug it off:  to ignore something  忽略某事
a shrug of the shoulders: 耸肩的动作
give a shrug: 耸肩
shrug something aside: to dismiss something as unimportant 把...置之不理
