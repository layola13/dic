revie:/ˈrivi/| n.|复习，回顾|A review is a critical assessment or evaluation of something.|例句：I wrote a review of the new restaurant. 我写了一篇关于这家新餐厅的评论。|近义词：examination, survey, appraisal|反义词:originality, innovation.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reviews his notes before the exam. = 他考试前复习笔记。
She reviews the project plan regularly. = 她定期审查项目计划。


一般过去时:
They reviewed the film last night. = 他们昨晚观看了这部电影。
I reviewed my work before submitting it. = 我在提交作业之前检查了我的工作。


现在完成时:
She has reviewed all the applications. = 她已经审查了所有申请。
He has reviewed the contract thoroughly. = 他已经仔细审查了合同。


过去完成时:
They had reviewed the data before presenting their findings. = 他们在展示研究结果之前已经审查了数据。
She had reviewed the book twice before writing her essay. = 她在写论文之前已经复习了两遍这本书。


将来时:
We will review the proposal next week. = 我们下周将审查该提案。
He will review the entire process again. = 他将再次审查整个过程。


虚拟语气:
If I had reviewed my notes, I would have done better on the test. = 如果我复习了笔记，考试成绩会更好。
I wish I had reviewed the material more thoroughly. = 我希望我更彻底地复习了资料。


被动语态:
The article was reviewed by three experts. = 这篇文章由三位专家评审。
The proposal will be reviewed by the committee. = 该提案将由委员会审查。


疑问句:
Have you reviewed the document yet? = 你已经审查过文件了吗？
Will they review the decision? = 他们会复习决定吗？


否定句:
He didn't review his notes before the test. = 他考试前没有复习笔记。
They haven't reviewed the evidence carefully. = 他们没有仔细审查证据。


PHRASE->
book review = 书评
movie review = 影评
peer review = 同行评审
review of literature = 文献综述
under review = 正在审查中
