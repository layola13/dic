disbursers:/dɪsˈbɜːrsərz/ | n. | 支付者；支出者 | Those who disburse funds or money.  People or organizations responsible for paying out money. | The company's disbursers ensured all employees received their salaries on time.  这家公司的支付人员确保所有员工都能按时收到工资。| payers, distributors, dispensers | receivers, collectors | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government disburses funds to various departments. = 政府向各部门拨款。
She disburses the company's petty cash. = 她负责公司零用现金的支出。

一般过去时:
They disbursed the money among the shareholders. = 他们将钱分给股东们。
The bank disbursed a large loan to the developer. = 银行向开发商发放了一笔巨额贷款。

现在完成时:
The organization has disbursed millions of dollars in aid. = 该组织已拨款数百万美元用于援助。
We have disbursed all the funds allocated for the project. = 我们已经支付了项目分配的所有资金。

过去完成时:
The company had disbursed all its profits before the audit. = 公司在审计之前已支付了所有利润。
They had disbursed the emergency funds before the disaster struck. = 灾难发生前，他们已经支付了紧急资金。

将来时:
The government will disburse additional funds for infrastructure projects. = 政府将拨出更多资金用于基础设施项目。
They will disburse the bonuses to employees next month. = 他们将在下个月向员工发放奖金。

虚拟语气:
If the bank disbursed the loan, we could start construction. = 如果银行发放贷款，我们就可以开始建设了。
I wish they had disbursed the funds earlier. = 我希望他们早点拨款。

被动语态:
The funds were disbursed according to the budget. = 资金按照预算拨付。
The salaries are disbursed monthly. = 工资每月发放。

疑问句:
When will the grants be disbursed? = 拨款什么时候发放？
How are the funds disbursed? = 资金是如何分配的？

否定句:
The company did not disburse the dividends this year. = 公司今年没有分红。
They haven't yet disbursed the scholarship money. = 他们还没有发放奖学金。
