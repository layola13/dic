carapace:/ˈkærəpeɪs/| n. |甲壳，硬壳|A carapace is a shell covering the back of an animal, such as a turtle or crab.  It provides protection from predators and the elements. |例句：The turtle retracted into its carapace when it sensed danger.  乌龟感受到危险时缩进了它的甲壳里。  The crab's carapace was a mottled brown and green.  螃蟹的甲壳是斑驳的棕色和绿色。|近义词：shell, shell, covering, armour|反义词：soft skin, exposed skin
