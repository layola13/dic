caprivi:/kæˈpriviː/| n. |卡普里维地带| A long, narrow strip of land in southern Africa, belonging to Namibia, extending eastward between Angola and Zambia.  It is named after Georg Leo von Caprivi, the German Chancellor who negotiated its acquisition.  The Caprivi Strip is characterized by its diverse geography, including the Okavango River Delta. |Example: The Caprivi Strip is known for its unique wildlife and diverse ecosystems. 卡普里维地带以其独特的野生动物和多样化的生态系统而闻名。|近义词：None (as a specific geographical location)|反义词：None|名词
