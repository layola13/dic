egomanias:/ˌiːɡoʊˈmeɪniəz/ | n. |自我中心狂, 自我陶醉 |  An excessive preoccupation with oneself and one's own importance; an extreme form of egotism or narcissism.  It often manifests as a disregard for others' feelings and needs. |例句：His egomanias prevented him from seeing the flaws in his plan. 他的自我中心蒙蔽了他，让他看不到计划中的缺陷。
His egomanias made him a difficult person to work with. 他的自我中心让他难以相处。|近义词：narcissism, egotism, self-obsession, conceit |反义词：selflessness, humility, modesty
