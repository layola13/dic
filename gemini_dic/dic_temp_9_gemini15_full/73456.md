forensic: /fəˈrɛnzɪk/ | adj. | 法医的；有关法庭审判的 | Relating to or denoting the application of scientific methods and techniques to the investigation of crime.  | Example:  Forensic evidence proved crucial in solving the case.  法医证据对破案至关重要。  The forensic examination revealed traces of explosives. 法医检查发现了爆炸物的痕迹。|近义词：legal, judicial, evidentiary |反义词：illegitimate, unofficial
