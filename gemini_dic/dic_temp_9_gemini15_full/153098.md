pegging:/ˈpɛɡɪŋ/| v. n.|  固定；标记；估价；（非正式）性交|

英文解析:
As a verb, "pegging" can refer to several actions, depending on the context.  It can mean to fix something firmly in place, to mark or label something, to estimate the value of something, or, informally, to engage in a specific sexual act. As a noun, it refers to the act of fixing something firmly in place, or the act of attaching a price to something. The informal sexual meaning is also used as a noun.

例句:

He pegged the tent to the ground. 他把帐篷固定在地上。

The price of oil is pegged to the dollar. 油价与美元挂钩。


They pegged the cost of the project at $1 million. 他们估算该项目的成本为100万美元。


She pegged him as a liar. 她认为他是个骗子。


(Informal) They were pegging all night. (他们整晚都在发生性关系。)


近义词:
fix, fasten, attach, label, estimate, assess, evaluate, (informal) screw


反义词:
unfasten, detach, remove, underestimate, overestimate


词性:
Verb, Noun
