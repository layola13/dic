ostracodermi:/ˌɒstrækəˈdɜːrmi/ | n. | 盔甲鱼类 | Ostracodermi refers to an extinct group of jawless, armored fish that lived during the Paleozoic Era.  They are considered the earliest known vertebrates.  They possessed bony plates covering their bodies, and lacked paired fins. |  The ostracoderms were an incredibly diverse group, showcasing a variety of body forms and lifestyles. | 近义词:  Agnatha (although this term includes other jawless fish) | 反义词:  Gnathostomata (jawed vertebrates)
