monsignore:/ˈmɒnsiˈnjɔːreɪ/|/ˈmɑːnsiˈnjɔːr/| n. |尊称：主教，教士|A title of honor given to certain clergymen, especially in the Catholic Church, ranking below a bishop.  It is often a courtesy title, not necessarily indicating a specific ecclesiastical office.|The monsignore delivered a powerful sermon.  这位主教发表了一篇意义深远的布道。|近义词：monsignor, reverend, priest|反义词：layperson
