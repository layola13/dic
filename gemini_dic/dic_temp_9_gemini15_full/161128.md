postern:/ˈpɒstən/| n. |后门，侧门，隐蔽通道 |A postern is a back door or a side entrance, often secret or hidden.  It is typically smaller and less conspicuous than the main entrance. |例句：The spy escaped through a postern in the castle wall.  间谍从城堡墙上的侧门逃脱了。 |例句：They used a postern to sneak into the garden. 他们用侧门偷偷溜进了花园。|近义词：back door, side door, secret entrance |反义词：main entrance, front door
