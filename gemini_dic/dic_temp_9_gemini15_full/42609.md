counterquestions:/ˈkaʊntərkwestʃənz/| n. |反问，反诘|Questions asked in response to a previous question, often to challenge or clarify the original question.|The interviewee responded to the interviewer's direct questions with several counterquestions about the company culture.  面试者用几个关于公司文化的问题来回应面试官的直接提问。|近义词：rebuttals, rejoinders|反义词：direct questions
