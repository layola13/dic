primogenitureship:/ˌpraɪməˈdʒɛnɪtʃərʃɪp/ | n. |长子继承权；长子继承制度 |The right of the eldest son to inherit his father's entire estate.  This system often excluded daughters and younger sons from inheriting property. |The eldest son inherited the family estate by primogenitureship.|近义词：inheritance, succession |反义词：equal inheritance, equitable distribution
