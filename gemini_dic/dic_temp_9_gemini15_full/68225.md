fairings:/ˈferɪŋz/ | n. |整流罩，蒙皮，护罩 |Protective coverings, typically aerodynamically shaped, used to streamline an object, often a vehicle or aircraft.  They improve efficiency by reducing drag. Fairings are often removable for access to the components they enclose. | Example: The motorcycle's fairings were damaged in the accident.  摩托车的整流罩在事故中受损了。 The rocket's nose fairing separated successfully after launch. 火箭的鼻锥整流罩在发射后成功分离。 |近义词：casing, shroud, cover |反义词：exposure
