corbeil:/ˈkɔːrbeɪl/| n. |柳条篮；盛物篮；(建筑) 筐形装饰|A corbeil is a basket, typically made of wicker, used for carrying or storing things.  In architecture, it refers to a basket-shaped ornamental feature.|例句：The baker carried a corbeil of freshly baked bread. 面包师提着一个盛满新鲜烘焙面包的柳条篮。

The architect incorporated corbeils into the design of the building's facade. 建筑师将筐形装饰融入建筑物立面的设计中。|近义词：basket, hamper, container|反义词：无

词源:
The word "corbeil" comes from the Old French word "corbeil," meaning "basket."  This ultimately derives from a Vulgar Latin word, possibly related to the Latin word "corbis," also meaning "basket."
