lanthorn: /ˈlæntən/ | n. | 灯笼 | A portable light, typically enclosed in a case with a transparent covering.  |  Example:  The children carried paper lanthorns during the Halloween parade. 孩子们在万圣节游行中拿着纸灯笼。 | 近义词：lantern, lamp | 反义词：darkness

词源:
