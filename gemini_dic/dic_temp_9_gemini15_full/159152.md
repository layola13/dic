podcasts:/ˈpɑːdkæsts/| n. |播客；(广播节目的)系列音频文件|A podcast is a series of digital audio files that can be downloaded from the Internet.  They often cover a specific topic or theme and are available for subscription.|例句：I listen to podcasts while I commute to work. 我通勤时听播客。|例句：Many podcasts offer insightful commentary on current events. 许多播客对时事提供有见地的评论。|近义词：audio programs, online radio shows|反义词：N/A|名词
