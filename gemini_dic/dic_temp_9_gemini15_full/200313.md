skulker:/ˈskʌlkər/| n. |潜行者，躲躲藏藏的人 | A skulker is someone who avoids their duties or responsibilities, often by hiding or being secretive. They are typically characterized by their unwillingness to participate or their tendency to shirk their obligations. | Example: The skulker hid in the shadows, hoping to avoid being caught.  潜行者躲在阴影里，希望能避免被抓住。 He was a skulker at work, always finding excuses to avoid doing his share. 他是个工作上的逃兵，总是找借口逃避自己的份内工作。|近义词： shirker, slacker, malingerer |反义词：hard worker, diligent, dedicated | 名词
