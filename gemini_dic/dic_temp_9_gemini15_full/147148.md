overlord:/ˈoʊvərlɔːrd/| n. |统治者，最高统治者，霸主| A supreme ruler, especially one who rules over several others.|The overlord demanded tribute from his vassal states.  这位统治者向他的属国索取贡品。|The evil overlord plotted to conquer the kingdom.  邪恶的霸主密谋征服王国。|近义词：sovereign, ruler, master|反义词：subject, vassal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The overlord rules the land with an iron fist. = 这位统治者铁腕统治这片土地。
He is the overlord of many kingdoms. = 他是许多王国的统治者。


一般过去时:
The overlord conquered many lands. = 这位统治者征服了许多土地。
The evil overlord ruled with fear. = 邪恶的统治者凭借恐惧统治。


现在完成时:
The overlord has consolidated his power. = 这位统治者巩固了他的权力。
The overlord has oppressed his people for years.  这位统治者多年来一直压迫他的子民。


过去完成时:
Before the rebellion, the overlord had enjoyed absolute power. = 在叛乱之前，这位统治者享有绝对权力。
The people had suffered under the overlord's tyrannical rule for decades. = 几十年来，人民在统治者的暴政下饱受苦难。


将来时:
The overlord will continue his reign of terror. = 这位统治者将继续他的恐怖统治。
The neighboring kingdoms fear what the overlord will do next. = 邻国担心这位统治者下一步会做什么。


虚拟语气:
If he were the overlord, he would rule justly. = 如果他是统治者，他会公正地统治。
I wish the overlord were more merciful. = 我希望统治者更仁慈一些。


被动语态:
