dexterity:/dekˈstɛrəti/| n. |灵巧；熟练；敏捷|Skill in performing tasks, especially with the hands.  It refers to the ability to use one's hands or body with skill and ease.  This can range from fine motor skills like playing a musical instrument to larger movements like dancing or sports.|

例句：

The surgeon's dexterity was evident in the precision of the operation. = 外科医生的灵巧在手术的精确性中显而易见。

His dexterity with a paintbrush was truly remarkable. = 他使用画笔的技巧确实令人惊叹。


近义词：skill, adroitness, deftness, agility, nimbleness

反义词：clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptitude
