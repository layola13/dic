presteaming:/prɪˈstiːmɪŋ/| n.|预蒸, 预先蒸煮|The process of steaming food before another cooking method, often to improve texture or reduce cooking time.|例句：Presteaming the vegetables before roasting helps to retain their nutrients and color. 预先蒸蔬菜再烘烤有助于保留其营养和颜色。|近义词：pre-cooking, parboiling|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chef presteams the vegetables before sautéing them. = 厨师在翻炒蔬菜之前先将它们蒸一下。
They presteam the dumplings to make them easier to cook. = 他们先蒸饺子，使它们更容易煮熟。

She presteamed the broccoli for five minutes. = 她把西兰花蒸了五分钟。
We presteamed the buns to soften them. = 我们把包子蒸了一下，使它们变软。
