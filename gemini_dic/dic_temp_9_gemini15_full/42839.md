coursework:/ˈkɔːrswɜːrk/| n. |课程作业，课程论文|Work done by students as part of a course of study.  It often involves essays, projects, or other assignments. |例句：My coursework this semester is particularly demanding.  这个学期的课程作业特别繁重。|例句：She received top marks for her coursework.  她的课程作业得了最高分。|近义词：assignments, projects, studies|反义词：examination, exam (referring to the assessment method, not the work itself)
