cms:/ˌsiːˈɛmˈɛs/ | n. |内容管理系统|Content Management System; a computer program that allows publishing, editing, and modifying content, like text, images, or videos, on a website or in an application.|Example: Our website uses a powerful CMS to manage all our articles and blog posts.  我们的网站使用强大的内容管理系统来管理我们所有的文章和博客帖子。|近义词：web content management system, website management system|反义词：无
