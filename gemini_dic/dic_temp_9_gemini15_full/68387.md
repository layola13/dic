follower: /ˈfɑːləʊər/ | n. | 追随者，跟随者 |  A person who follows another person or thing; a disciple or adherent.  | 例句：She has thousands of followers on Instagram.  她Instagram上有数千名粉丝。

例句：He is a follower of Gandhi's philosophy. 他是甘地哲学的追随者。


近义词：admirer, disciple, adherent, fan, subscriber

反义词：leader, innovator, pioneer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He follows his favorite band on tour. 他跟随他最喜欢的乐队巡演。
She follows the latest fashion trends. 她追随最新的时尚潮流。


一般过去时:
They followed the map to find their way. 他们跟着地图找路。
Millions followed the royal wedding on television. 数百万人在电视上观看了皇室婚礼。


现在完成时:
I have followed this blog for years. 我关注这个博客很多年了。
She has followed his career with great interest. 她一直怀着极大的兴趣关注着他的职业生涯。


过去完成时:
We had followed the instructions carefully, but still failed. 我们已经仔细按照说明做了，但还是失败了。
They had followed the trail for miles before realizing they were lost. 他们在意识到自己迷路之前沿着小路走了几英里。


将来时:
I will follow your advice. 我会听从你的建议。
They will follow the rules strictly. 他们将严格遵守规则。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would follow his example. 如果我是你，我会效仿他的榜样。
I wish I had followed my heart instead of my head. 我真希望我当时听了我的心而不是我的头脑。


被动语态:
The instructions were followed carefully. 说明书被仔细地遵守了。
His advice was followed by many. 他的建议被许多人采纳。


疑问句:
Who are you following on Twitter? 你在推特上关注谁？
How many people are following your blog? 有多少人在关注你的博客？


否定句:
I don't follow politics. 我不关注政治。
He didn't follow the instructions properly. 他没有正确地按照说明操作。
