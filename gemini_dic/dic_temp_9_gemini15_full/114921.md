managed:/ˈmænɪdʒd/| v.|管理的；设法做到的| to control or be in charge of something, or to succeed in doing something difficult |例句：She managed the project successfully. = 她成功地管理了这个项目。|例句：He managed to finish the marathon despite the pain. = 尽管很痛苦，他还是设法完成了马拉松比赛。|近义词：handled, controlled, succeeded, accomplished |反义词：failed, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She manages a team of ten people. = 她管理着一个十人团队。
He manages to get to work on time every day. = 他每天都能按时上班。


一般过去时:
They managed the company for many years. = 他们管理这家公司多年了。
She managed to solve the problem quickly. = 她设法很快解决了这个问题。


现在完成时:
We have managed to reduce costs significantly. = 我们已经成功地大幅降低了成本。
He has managed the department since 2010. = 自2010年以来，他一直管理着这个部门。


过去完成时:
They had managed to complete the project before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前设法完成了项目。
She had managed the team for five years before she was promoted. = 她升职前管理这个团队五年了。


将来时:
We will manage to finish the work by tomorrow. = 我们明天将设法完成这项工作。
They will manage the new branch office next year. = 他们明年将管理新的分公司。


虚拟语气:
If I had managed my time better, I would have finished the assignment. = 如果我更好地管理时间，我就完成了作业。
I wish I had managed to contact him before he left. = 我真希望在他离开之前设法联系上他。


被动语态:
The project was managed efficiently. = 这个项目管理得很有效率。
The company is managed by a board of directors. = 公司由董事会管理。


疑问句:
How do you manage to do so much in a day? = 你一天怎么能做这么多事？
Who manages the accounts department? = 谁管理会计部？


否定句:
I couldn't manage to open the door. = 我打不开门。
They didn't manage to meet their sales targets. = 他们没有达到销售目标。
