muttered:/ˈmʌtərd/| v. |低声说，咕哝 | to speak in a low, indistinct tone |He muttered something under his breath. 他低声说了些什么。|近义词：murmur, grumble, mumbled |反义词：shout, yell


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mutters to himself every morning. 他每天早上都自言自语。
She mutters complaints about her job. 她小声抱怨她的工作。


一般过去时:
He muttered a response I couldn't understand. 他咕哝了一句我听不懂的话。
She muttered apologies after bumping into me. 她撞到我后小声道歉。


现在完成时:
He has muttered his way through the entire meeting. 他在整个会议上都在低声抱怨。
She has muttered about the unfairness of it all. 她一直在小声嘀咕这件事的不公平。


过去完成时:
He had muttered his concerns to his friend before the meeting. 会议前，他已经向朋友抱怨了他的担忧。
She had muttered a prayer before entering the church. 她进入教堂前低声祈祷。


将来时:
He will mutter his dissent during the vote. 他将在投票期间低声表达他的异议。
She will mutter her disapproval under her breath. 她会小声表达她的不满。


虚拟语气:
If he had not muttered his concerns, the problem would have remained unresolved. 如果他不曾抱怨他的担忧，问题将得不到解决。
I wish she hadn't muttered those unkind words. 我希望她没有说那些刻薄的话。


被动语态:
His concerns were muttered in the meeting. 他的担忧在会议上被低声表达出来。
The answer was muttered so quietly that I couldn't hear it. 答案说得太小声，我听不见。


疑问句:
Was he muttering something about the weather? 他是不是在小声嘀咕天气？
What was she muttering about? 她究竟在咕哝些什么？


否定句:
He didn't mutter a single word. 他一句话也没说。
She didn't mutter any complaints this time. 她这次没有抱怨。


PHRASE->
mutter to oneself 自言自语
mutter under one's breath 低声咕哝
mutter a complaint 小声抱怨
mutter an apology 低声道歉
mutter something unintelligible 咕哝一些听不懂的话
