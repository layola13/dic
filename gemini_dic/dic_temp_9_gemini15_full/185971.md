rewarmed:/ˌriːˈwɔːrmd/| adj.| 再加热的；重新加热的 | Heated again after having been cooked.  The word implies that the food was previously cooked and is now being reheated. | 例句：I had some rewarmed leftovers for lunch. = 我午餐吃了一些重新加热的剩菜。

例句：The rewarmed soup tasted just as good as when it was freshly made. = 这重新加热的汤尝起来和刚做的一样好。


近义词：heated, reheated | 反义词：freshly cooked, newly made | 词性：形容词
