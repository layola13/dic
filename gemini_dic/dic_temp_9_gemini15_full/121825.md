misrelates:/ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪts/| v. |错误地联系；曲解；误解|To connect or interpret incorrectly; to misunderstand the relationship between things. |例句：He misrelates the events, causing confusion. 他曲解了事件，造成了混乱。 |例句：The article misrelates the cause and effect. 这篇文章误解了因果关系。|近义词：misinterpret, misconstrue, misrepresent|反义词：understand, correctly relate
