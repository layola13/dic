redstreak: /ˈrɛdstriːk/ | n. |红条纹，红纹病| A red streak, usually referring to a streak of red color on something, or a plant disease characterized by red streaks on leaves and stems.  It can also refer to a fast, red vehicle or something moving quickly. |例句：The sunset painted a vibrant redstreak across the sky.  日落时分，一道鲜艳的红霞划过天空。

The runner, a blur of red, was a true redstreak on the track.  那个穿着红色的跑步者，在跑道上像一道红色的闪电。|近义词：red stripe, flash, streak|反义词：pale streak, slow movement
