noose:/nuːs/| n.| 绳套；困境；圈套 | A loop of rope or wire used for hanging someone or something.  It can also refer to a difficult or dangerous situation. | Example: The cowboy tightened the noose around the calf's neck. 牛仔把绳套勒紧在小牛脖子上。

The criminal was caught in a noose of lies. 罪犯被谎言的圈套困住了。|近义词：loop, snare, trap |反义词：freedom, release
