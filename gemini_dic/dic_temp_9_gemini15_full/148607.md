overtenaciously: /ˌoʊvərˌtɛneɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 过于顽强地；过于坚持地 | Excessively tenacious; too persistent.  |  He overtenaciously pursued the lawsuit, even after being advised against it. 他过于顽强地追究这场诉讼，即使在被建议放弃之后也是如此。 |近义词：obstinately, doggedly, stubbornly | 反义词：easily, readily, casually
