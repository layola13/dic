purpose:/ˈpɜːrpəs/| n. |目的；意图；用途；效果|The intended aim or objective of a course of action.|The purpose of the meeting is to discuss the new project.  这次会议的目的是讨论新项目。|The main purpose of this book is to educate. 这本书的主要目的是教育。|aim, goal, intention, objective|meaninglessness, pointlessness|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is the purpose of your visit? = 你来访的目的何在？
What purpose does this machine serve? = 这台机器有什么用途？


一般过去时:
What was the purpose of his actions? = 他行为的目的何在？
What purpose did the experiment serve? = 实验有什么作用？


现在完成时:
What purpose has this research served? = 这项研究起了什么作用？
What purpose has your trip achieved? = 你的旅行达到了什么目的？


过去完成时:
What purpose had they hoped to achieve? = 他们希望达到什么目的？
What purpose had the meeting served before it was adjourned? = 会议在休会前达成了什么目的？


将来时:
What purpose will this meeting serve? = 这次会议将起到什么作用？
What purpose will your visit achieve? = 你的访问将实现什么目的？


虚拟语气:
If I knew the purpose, I would tell you. = 如果我知道目的，我会告诉你的。
I wish I knew the purpose of his actions. = 我真希望知道他行为的目的。


被动语态:
The purpose of the study was misunderstood. = 研究的目的被误解了。
The purpose will be explained later. = 目的稍后会解释。


疑问句:
What is the purpose of life? = 人生目的是什么？
What is the purpose of this exercise? = 这个练习的目的是什么？


否定句:
The meeting lacked purpose. = 会议缺乏目的性。
His actions had no clear purpose. = 他的行为没有明确的目的。
