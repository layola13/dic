rompish:/ˈrɒmpɪʃ/| adj.|嬉戏的；顽皮的；活泼的；轻率的 |Playful and boisterous; characterized by or given to romping.  It often implies a carefree and slightly mischievous energy.|例句：The children were in a rompish mood, running and laughing in the park. 孩子们兴高采烈地玩耍，在公园里跑来跑去，欢声笑语。|例句：Her rompish behavior at the formal dinner surprised everyone.  她在正式晚宴上的嬉戏行为让每个人都感到惊讶。|近义词：frisky, playful, mischievous, exuberant |反义词：sedate, subdued, demure, serious
