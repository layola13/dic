skitter:/ˈskɪtər/| v.|轻快地移动，疾走，掠过 |to move quickly and lightly, especially over a surface; to move in a jerky or erratic manner. |例句：The mouse skittered across the floor.  老鼠轻快地跑过地板。
The child skittered on the ice. 孩子在冰上快速滑行。|近义词：scurry, dart, flit, scamper |反义词：trudge, lumber, crawl


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mouse skitters across the floor. = 老鼠轻快地跑过地板。
Leaves skitter across the pavement. = 叶子在人行道上快速飘动。

一般过去时:
The rabbit skittered into the bushes. = 兔子飞快地窜进了灌木丛。
The ball skittered across the court. = 球在球场上快速滚动。

现在完成时:
The rain has skittered across the window. = 雨水已经打在窗户上了。
The children have skittered down the hill. = 孩子们已经从山上快速滑下来了。

过去完成时:
The bird had skittered away before I could see it clearly. = 我还没看清楚，鸟就飞走了。
The thief had skittered out of sight before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，小偷已经溜之大吉了。

将来时:
The leaves will skitter in the autumn wind. = 秋风中树叶会飘动。
The cat will skitter up the tree if it sees a dog. = 如果看到狗，猫会迅速爬上树。

虚拟语气:
If the ball skittered faster, it might have scored a goal. = 如果球滚得更快，可能就进了球。
If the mouse skittered out of the way, it would have avoided the trap. = 如果老鼠迅速躲开，就能躲过陷阱。

被动语态:
The ball was skittered across the field by the wind. = 球被风吹过球场。
The seeds were skittered across the garden by the wind. = 种子被风吹散在花园里。

疑问句:
Did the mouse skitter across the room? = 老鼠是否跑过了房间？
Will the leaves skitter in the wind tomorrow? = 明天树叶会在风中飘动吗？

否定句:
The mouse didn't skitter across the floor; it walked slowly. = 老鼠没有跑过地板；它慢慢地走着。
The leaves didn't skitter because it was a calm day. = 叶子没有飘动，因为天气平静。
