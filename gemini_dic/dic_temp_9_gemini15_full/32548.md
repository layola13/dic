chipped-in:/tʃɪpt ˈɪn/ | v. | 凑钱；捐款；插话；参与 | To contribute a share of money or effort towards a common goal; to interrupt a conversation with a remark; to participate. | 例句：Several colleagues chipped in to buy their boss a retirement gift. = 几个同事凑钱给他们的老板买了一份退休礼物。 They chipped in with ideas during the brainstorming session. = 他们在头脑风暴会议中提出了很多想法。  | 近义词：contribute, donate, participate, interject | 反义词：withdraw, abstain


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He chips in a few dollars every week for the charity. = 他每周为慈善机构捐几美元。
They chip in with suggestions during meetings. = 他们在会议上插话提建议。

一般过去时:
We chipped in to buy a present for Sarah's birthday. = 我们凑钱给莎拉买了份生日礼物。
He chipped in with a joke to lighten the mood. = 他插话讲了个笑话来活跃气氛。

现在完成时:
She has chipped in generously to the fundraising campaign. = 她慷慨地为筹款活动捐款。
They have chipped in on several projects this year. = 他们今年参与了几个项目。

过去完成时:
Before the project started, everyone had chipped in their initial ideas. = 在项目开始之前，每个人都贡献了最初的想法。
They had chipped in enough money to cover the expenses by the end of the month. = 到月底他们已经凑够了钱来支付费用。

将来时:
We will chip in for a new coffee machine for the office. = 我们将共同购买一台新的办公室咖啡机。
They will chip in their expertise to help solve the problem. = 他们将贡献他们的专业知识来帮助解决这个问题。

虚拟语气:
If everyone chipped in, we could afford the trip. = 如果每个人都捐款，我们就能负担得起这次旅行。
I wish they had chipped in earlier; we would have finished sooner. = 我希望他们早点参与；我们会早点完成。

被动语态:
Money was chipped in by the employees to help the needy. = 员工们捐款来帮助需要帮助的人。
Ideas were chipped in by the team members during the discussion. = 在讨论中，团队成员们纷纷提出了想法。

疑问句:
Will you chip in for the team dinner? = 你会为团队晚餐出钱吗？
Did anyone chip in during the presentation? = 有人在演示过程中插话吗？

否定句:
He didn't chip in at all for the party. = 他根本没有为聚会出钱。
She didn't chip in her opinion during the meeting. = 她在会议上没有发表自己的意见。


PHRASE->

chip in some money = 捐一些钱
chip in and help = 帮忙
chip in to (a cause) = 为（事业）捐款
