nondefendant:/ˌnɒnˈdɛfɛndənt/| n. |非被告人，非被控方|A person or entity not involved in a lawsuit as a defendant.  This term is often used in legal contexts to refer to someone or something that is not being sued or accused. |例句：The nondefendant witness testified to seeing the accident.  非被告人证人作证说看到了事故。

The judge ruled that the nondefendant company was not liable for damages.  法官裁定非被告公司无需承担损害赔偿责任。|近义词：bystander, nonparty|反义词：defendant
