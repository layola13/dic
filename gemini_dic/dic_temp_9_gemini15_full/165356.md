prejudger: /prɪˈdʒʌdʒər/ | n. | 预断者，偏见者 | One who forms prejudices or makes premature judgments.  A person who makes judgments before having sufficient knowledge or understanding. | Example: He was a known prejudger, quick to condemn without investigation. 他是一个出了名的预断者，总是迅速谴责而不调查。 | 近义词: bigot, bias, discriminator | 反义词: open-minded person, impartial judge
