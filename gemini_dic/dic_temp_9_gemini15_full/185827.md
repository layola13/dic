revitalize:/ˌriˈvaɪtəlaɪz/| vt.|使复兴；使恢复活力；使重新充满活力|To give new life or vigor to something; to renew or refresh.|The city is revitalizing its downtown area with new shops and restaurants.  城市正在通过新的商店和餐馆来复兴其市中心地区。|The new manager revitalized the company's marketing strategy. 新经理使公司的营销策略焕发了活力。|reanimate, rejuvenate, refresh|kill, destroy, stagnate|动词
