plaud:/plɔːd/| v.| 鼓掌，赞扬 | to clap one's hands in applause; to express strong approval of someone or something |例句：The audience loudly plauded the performance. 观众热烈鼓掌。 The critics plauded the author's new book. 批评家们称赞作者的新书。 |近义词：applaud, commend, praise |反义词：boo, hiss, criticize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plauds every performance. = 他为每次演出鼓掌。
They applaud the speaker. = 他们为演讲者鼓掌。


一般过去时:
The crowd plauded enthusiastically. = 人群热烈鼓掌。
She plauded his courage. = 她赞扬了他的勇气。


现在完成时:
The audience has plauded the singer many times. = 观众多次为这位歌手鼓掌。
We have plauded their efforts. = 我们赞扬了他们的努力。


过去完成时:
They had plauded before the curtain fell. = 在幕布落下之前，他们已经鼓掌了。
She had plauded his work before the meeting. = 会议之前，她已经赞扬了他的工作。


将来时:
The audience will applaud the performance. = 观众将会为演出鼓掌。
They will applaud his decision. = 他们将会赞扬他的决定。


虚拟语气:
If they knew how to express their feelings, they would applaud. = 如果他们知道如何表达自己的感受，他们就会鼓掌。
I wish they would applaud the innovation. = 我希望他们能为这项创新鼓掌。


被动语态:
His work was plauded by many. = 他的作品受到了许多人的赞扬。
The performance was plauded by the critics. = 演出受到了评论家的好评。


疑问句:
Did the audience applaud the show? = 观众对这场演出鼓掌了吗？
Will they applaud my proposal? = 他们会赞同我的提议吗？


否定句:
The audience did not applaud the performance. = 观众没有为演出鼓掌。
They did not applaud his actions. = 他们不赞同他的行为。
