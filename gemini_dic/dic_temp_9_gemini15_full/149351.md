pachydermata:/ˌpækiˈdɜːrmətə/| n. |厚皮类动物；厚皮目动物|A now-obsolete order of mammals characterized by thick skin, including elephants, rhinoceroses, and hippopotamuses.  The term is now largely considered outdated in scientific taxonomy.|例句：The pachydermata were once considered a single order, but modern classification separates them into different groups. 厚皮类动物曾被认为是一个单一的目，但现代分类法将它们分成不同的类群。|近义词：thick-skinned animals, ungulates (partially)|反义词：N/A|
