plume:/pluːm/| n. v.| 羽毛；烟 plume；（感情等的）爆发；| a long, soft feather;  a cloud of smoke, dust, or gas; a sudden outburst of emotion|The bird spread its plumes. 这只鸟张开了羽毛。|A plume of smoke rose from the burning building. 一股浓烟从燃烧的建筑物中升起。|近义词：feather, cloud, burst|反义词：N/A

The bird displays its plumes beautifully. = 这只鸟漂亮地展示着它的羽毛。
Smoke plumes rise from the volcano. = 火山喷发出烟柱。
