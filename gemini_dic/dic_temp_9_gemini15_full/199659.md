sinuated:/ˈsɪnjuˌeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 蜿蜒的；弯曲的 | Having many curves and bends; winding.  | The sinuated river flowed through the valley.  蜿蜒的河流流经山谷。 The road sinuated through the mountains. 这条路蜿蜒穿过群山。 | winding, meandering, curving, tortuous | straight, direct |  形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The river sinuously winds through the valley. = 河水蜿蜒地流过山谷。
The path sinuates gently upward. = 小路缓缓向上蜿蜒。


一般过去时:
The road sinuated sharply around the cliff. = 道路在悬崖边急剧弯曲。
The snake sinuated its way through the grass. = 蛇蜿蜒穿过草丛。


现在完成时:
The river has sinuated its way to the sea. = 河水已经蜿蜒流向大海。
The path has sinuated through the forest for miles. = 这条小路已经蜿蜒穿过森林数英里。


过去完成时:
The road had sinuated for miles before reaching the village. = 这条路在到达村庄之前已经蜿蜒了几英里。
The river had sinuated through the mountains for centuries. = 这条河已经蜿蜒穿过群山数百年了。


将来时:
The new road will sinuate along the coastline. = 新路将沿着海岸线蜿蜒延伸。
The path will sinuate up the hill. = 小路将蜿蜒上山。


虚拟语气:
If the river sinuated less, the journey would be shorter. = 如果河水蜿蜒程度较小，旅程就会更短。
I wish the road didn't sinuate so much. = 我希望这条路不要这么蜿蜒。


被动语态:
The route was sinuated to avoid the steep slopes. = 路线蜿蜒是为了避开陡坡。
The course of the river has been sinuated by erosion. = 河流的走向因侵蚀而变得蜿蜒。


疑问句:
Does the road sinuate much further? = 这条路还会蜿蜒延伸很远吗？
How much does the path sinuate before reaching the summit? = 小路在到达山顶之前蜿蜒了多少？


否定句:
The road doesn't sinuate; it's perfectly straight. = 这条路不蜿蜒；它是笔直的。
The river doesn't sinuate as much as it used to. = 河流不像以前那样蜿蜒了。

词源:
From Latin *sinuātus, past participle of sinuāre "to bend, curve," from sinus "bend, curve, bay."  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Indo-European root *seu- "to swell, be convex."
