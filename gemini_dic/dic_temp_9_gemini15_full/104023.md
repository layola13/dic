jujube:/ˈdʒuːdʒuːb/ | n. |枣；红枣 | A small, sweet fruit that is usually red or brown and has a hard skin.  It is also known as a Chinese date. |例句：I love the sweet taste of fresh jujubes. 我喜欢新鲜枣子的甜味。|例句：We made a delicious pie filled with jujubes and apples. 我们做了一个美味的派，里面加了枣子和苹果。|近义词：date, Chinese date |反义词：无
