reargue:/ˌriːˈɑːrɡjuː/| vt.|再争论；重新辩论|To argue again or in a different way; to present arguments again, often with modifications or additions.|例句：He reargues the case for a higher minimum wage. 他再次论证提高最低工资的理由。|例句：The lawyer reargues the case before the appeals court. 律师在高等法院再次辩论此案。|近义词：reiterate, restate, rehash|反义词：concede, accept
