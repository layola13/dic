carburet:/ˈkɑːrbjʊreɪt/ | vt. |使碳化；使富含碳；（尤指）使（燃料）增碳 | To combine or treat (a substance) with carbon.  Specifically, to increase the carbon content of (a fuel), especially to enrich a fuel mixture with gasoline vapor. | 例句：The process carburetes the iron, making it harder and stronger.  这个过程使铁碳化，使其更坚硬更结实。

The engine was carburetted to improve its performance at high altitudes.  该发动机经过增碳处理以提高其在高海拔地区的性能。| 近义词：carbonize, enrich (fuel) | 反义词：decarbonize
