loganberries:/ˈloʊɡənˌbɛriːz/ | n. |罗甘莓 | A hybrid berry, a cross between a blackberry and a raspberry.  Loganberries are dark red to purplish-black, larger than raspberries, and have a tart flavor. They are often used in jams, pies, and other desserts. | Example:  The farmer's market had a beautiful display of fresh loganberries.  农贸市场摆放着漂亮的鲜罗甘莓。

The pie was made with homemade loganberry filling.  这个馅饼是用自制的罗甘莓馅做的。|近义词：blackberries, raspberries |反义词：无
