pyrophanous: /ˌpaɪrəˈfeɪnəs/ | adj. |透光的，透明的|  Able to be seen through; translucent.  The word suggests a quality of glowing or fiery translucence, hinting at light passing through something that might otherwise be opaque.  It's not simply transparent, but suggests a warm, luminous translucence. | The stained-glass window was pyrophanous, glowing with inner light.  The pyrophanous quality of the opal was mesmerizing. | 近义词：translucent, diaphanous | 反义词：opaque, impermeable
