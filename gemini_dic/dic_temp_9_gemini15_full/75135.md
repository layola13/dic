friskingly:/ˈfrɪskɪŋli/| adv.|活泼地；轻快地；嬉戏地|In a frisky or playful manner; lively and cheerful.|例句：The children played friskingly in the park.孩子们在公园里活泼地玩耍。

例句：The puppy ran friskingly around the garden. 小狗在花园里轻快地跑来跑去。

近义词：playfully, merrily, cheerfully, sprightly

反义词：sluggishly, lethargically, sullenly

词性：副词
