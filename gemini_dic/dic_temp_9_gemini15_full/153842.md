perceptions: /pərˈsɛpʃənz/ | n. |感知，感觉，理解 |The way in which something is perceived can be significantly influenced by individual experiences and cultural backgrounds.  感知某事物的方式会受到个人经历和文化背景的显著影响。| impressions, understandings, views | misinterpretations, misunderstandings |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His perceptions of the situation are quite accurate. = 他对局势的感知相当准确。
Their perceptions often differ based on their backgrounds. = 他们的感知往往因背景不同而差异很大。


一般过去时:
Her perceptions changed drastically after the accident. = 事故后她的感知发生了巨大变化。
His perceptions of his childhood were often romanticized. = 他对童年岁月的感知往往是浪漫化的。


现在完成时:
My perceptions of the city have evolved since I moved here. = 自从我搬到这里以来，我对这个城市的感知已经发生了变化。
Her perceptions have been shaped by years of experience. = 她多年的经验塑造了她的感知。


过去完成时:
By then, his perceptions of the company had soured. = 到那时，他对公司的感知已经恶化了。
Before the meeting, their perceptions of the project were quite optimistic. = 会议之前，他们对项目的感知相当乐观。


将来时:
His perceptions will likely change as he learns more. = 随着他了解更多，他的感知可能会改变。
Our perceptions of technology will continue to evolve. = 我们对技术的感知将继续发展。


虚拟语气:
If only his perceptions were more accurate. = 但愿他的感知更准确些。
I wish my perceptions of the situation were clearer. = 我希望我对局势的感知更清晰。


被动语态:

Their perceptions did not align with the reality of the situation. = 他们的感知与实际情况并不相符。
