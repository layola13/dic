/ˌriːˈfɪŋɡərz/| n. |重新编号；重新编制索引|The process of renumbering or re-indexing something, often documents or files, to create a new, consistent order or system.  This might involve changing numerical labels, page numbers, or other identifiers.  |例句：The librarian initiated a refingers of the entire library catalog after the recent acquisition of new books. 图书管理员在最近购入新书后启动了整个图书馆目录的重新编制索引。


例句：The software automatically performed a refingers of the database after the data migration.  软件在数据迁移后自动对数据库进行了重新编号。


近义词：reindex, renumber, reorganize|反义词：disorganize, scramble|名词
