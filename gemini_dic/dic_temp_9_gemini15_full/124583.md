mousakas: /muːzəˈkɑːs/ | n. |穆萨卡 | A Greek casserole dish consisting of layers of eggplant or potatoes, minced meat, and a creamy béchamel sauce, typically baked in the oven.  It's a popular dish in Greece and other parts of the Mediterranean. | Example:  My grandmother's mousakas is legendary; it's the best I've ever tasted.  我的祖母做的穆萨卡是传奇的；这是我吃过的最好吃的。|近义词: casserole, gratin |反义词:  (no direct antonym, but dishes without layers could be considered opposite)|名词
