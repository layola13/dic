renumbers:/ˌriːˈnʌmbərz/| vt.|重新编号；改编号码|To assign new numbers to something, typically in a sequence.|The librarian renumbered all the books after the reorganization. 图书管理员在重新整理后对所有书籍重新编号。|近义词：re-number, relabel|反义词：number (without re-)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He renumbers the files every week. = 他每周都重新编号文件。
The system automatically renumbers the pages. = 系统自动重新编号页面。

现在完成时:
We have renumbered the invoices for better tracking. = 我们已经重新编号了发票以便更好地跟踪。
