corner-stone:/ˈkɔːrnərstəʊn/| n. |基石，奠基石，基础 |A cornerstone is a stone that forms the base of a building, often placed ceremonially at the start of construction.  Figuratively, it represents something fundamental or essential upon which something else is built.|例句：Honesty is a cornerstone of any successful relationship.  诚实是任何成功关系的基石。|例句：The new library is a cornerstone of the community's cultural development. 新图书馆是社区文化发展的基石。|近义词：foundation, base, bedrock, fundamental |反义词：superstructure, top, apex
