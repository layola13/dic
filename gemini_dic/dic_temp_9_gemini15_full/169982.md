protandrous:/proˈtændrəs/| adj.|雄性先熟的，雌雄异熟的|Referring to a species in which the individual organisms are initially male and later become female. This sequential hermaphroditism is a reproductive strategy observed in various species, allowing them to maximize reproductive success depending on their size and age.  |Example:  Clownfish are protandrous, meaning a male can transition to a female if the dominant female dies.|近义词：protogynous (反义词)|反义词: protogynous
