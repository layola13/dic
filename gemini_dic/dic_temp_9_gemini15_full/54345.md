dishful: /ˈdɪʃfʊl/ | n. | 满盘的；一盘的 | A dishful is the amount that fills a dish.  It refers to a quantity of something contained within a dish. The specific amount varies depending on the size of the dish. |  例句：She prepared a dishful of delicious strawberries. 她准备了一盘美味的草莓。

She served a dishful of steaming rice. 她盛了一盘热气腾腾的米饭。


近义词：plateful, bowlful,  helping (depending on context) | 反义词：无  | 词性：名词
