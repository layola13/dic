outblushing: /ˌaʊtˈblʌʃɪŋ/ | v. |胜过…的红晕；使…相形见绌 |  To surpass in blushing; to exceed in redness or color.  This word is quite rare and often found in older literature. It implies a surpassing of blush, not just a blush itself. It suggests a degree of intensity or a competition in redness.


例句：

The sunset outblushed even the most vibrant roses in the garden.  = 夕阳的红晕甚至胜过了花园里最鲜艳的玫瑰。

Her cheeks outblushed the crimson of her dress. = 她的脸颊比她那件深红色的衣服还要红。


近义词：outshine, outrival, surpass, exceed

反义词：pale beside, be outdone by

词性：动词
