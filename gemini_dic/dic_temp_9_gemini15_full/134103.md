It describes something that is not intrinsically a part of something else, or not existing within something else. |The theory suggests that meaning is nonimmanently derived, existing outside the text itself.  = 该理论认为意义并非内在地派生，而是存在于文本之外。

The concept of justice is nonimmanently applied to various situations. = 正义的概念并非内在地适用于各种情况。
