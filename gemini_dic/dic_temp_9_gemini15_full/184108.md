resign:/rɪˈzaɪn/| vt. vi.|辞职；放弃；顺从；委托；|to give up a job or position by formally telling your employer that you are leaving|例句：He resigned from his post as CEO. 他辞去了CEO的职务。
例句：She resigned herself to her fate. 她认命了。

近义词：quit, relinquish, abdicate | 反义词：begin, commence, start


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resigns from his job every year. 他每年都辞职。
She resigns herself to her fate. 她认命了。

一般过去时:
He resigned from his job last week. 他上周辞职了。
She resigned herself to the inevitable. 她接受了不可避免的事实。

现在完成时:
He has resigned from his position. 他已经辞去了职务。
She has resigned herself to living alone. 她已经认命独自生活了。

过去完成时:
He had resigned before the scandal broke. 丑闻爆发之前他已经辞职了。
She had resigned herself to failure before even trying. 她甚至还没尝试就认命会失败。

将来时:
He will resign next month. 他下个月将辞职。
She will resign herself to whatever happens. 她将顺从任何发生的事情。

虚拟语气:
If he resigned, the company would be in trouble. 如果他辞职，公司就会陷入困境。
I wish I had resigned sooner. 我真希望我早点辞职。

被动语态:
His resignation was accepted. 他的辞职请求被接受了。
She was resigned to her fate by the cruel circumstances.  残酷的境遇使她认命了。

疑问句:
Will he resign? 他会辞职吗？
Has she resigned herself to the situation? 她对现状认命了吗？

否定句:
He did not resign from his job. 他没有辞职。
She did not resign herself to defeat. 她没有认输。
