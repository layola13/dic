canker:/ˈkæŋkər/ | n. v. |溃疡；腐蚀；恶化 |A canker is a sore or ulcer, especially in the mouth.  It can also refer to something that causes decay or corruption. As a verb, it means to become affected by canker or to cause decay. |例句：He suffered from a painful canker sore on his tongue. 他舌头上长了一个疼痛的溃疡。 The scandal acted as a canker on the reputation of the company. 这场丑闻腐蚀了公司的名誉。|近义词：ulcer, sore, lesion, blight, rot |反义词：healing, recovery, health
