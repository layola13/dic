outwasting:/ˌaʊtˈweɪstɪŋ/| v.|超过…的消耗；比…更浪费；| Exceeding in waste; wasting more than.|例句：His lavish spending was outwasting even his considerable fortune. 他挥霍无度的消费甚至超过了他相当可观的财富。

例句：The company's outdated processes were outwasting their competitors, leading to significant losses.  公司的过时流程比竞争对手更浪费，导致了巨大的损失。


近义词：exceeding, surpassing, outspending, outdoing (in waste)
反义词：conserving, saving, economizing


词性：动词
