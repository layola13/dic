canaanitish:/ˌkeɪnəˈnaɪtɪʃ/| adj.|迦南的；迦南人的；迦南语的|Of or relating to Canaan, its people, or their language.  The term often refers to the ancient inhabitants of Canaan, a region in the Levant corresponding roughly to modern-day Israel, Palestine, Lebanon, and parts of Jordan and Syria.|例句：The Canaanitish civilization flourished for centuries before the arrival of the Israelites.  迦南文明在以色列人到来之前繁荣了几个世纪。

The archaeological evidence suggests a rich Canaanitish culture. 考古证据表明存在着丰富的迦南文化。|近义词：Canaanite (n.), Phoenician (sometimes used interchangeably, but with nuances)|反义词：无
