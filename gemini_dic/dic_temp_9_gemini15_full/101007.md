invaluable:/ˈɪnvæljʊəbl/| adj.| 无价的，非常宝贵的 |extremely useful or helpful; of great value.  |例句：His advice proved invaluable in resolving the crisis. 他的建议在解决危机中被证明是无价的。

例句：The experience gained from this project is invaluable. 从这个项目中获得的经验是无价的。


近义词：priceless, priceless, indispensable, essential


反义词：worthless, valueless


词源: in- (not) + valuable (having value)  The prefix "in-" in this context doesn't mean "not," but rather "in" or "into," emphasizing the depth of value.  It signifies that something is so valuable it goes beyond simple monetary worth.
