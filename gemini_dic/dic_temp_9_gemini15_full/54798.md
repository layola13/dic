dispensed:/dɪˈspɛnst/| vt.| 分配；分发；施予；免除 | To distribute or give out something, especially in shares or portions; to grant or bestow something, or to excuse someone from something. |例句：The pharmacist dispensed the medication. 药剂师分发了药物。

The teacher dispensed wisdom to her students.老师传授知识给她的学生们。


近义词：distribute, allot, administer, grant, bestow | 反义词：retain, withhold, keep

词性:动词
