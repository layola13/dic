sedimental:/ˌsɛdɪˈmɛntəl/| adj.|沉积的；沉淀的；沉渣的| Relating to or denoting sediment or sediments.  Sediment is material that has settled at the bottom of a liquid.  The adjective describes things related to this process or the resulting material.|例句：The sedimentary rocks contained fossils of ancient marine life.  这些沉积岩包含古代海洋生物的化石。

例句：The river carries a large amount of sediment to the sea. 这条河携带大量泥沙入海。


近义词：deposited, layered, stratified|反义词：unstratified, igneous, metamorphic


词性:形容词
