pneumonia:/njuːˈmoʊniə/| n. |肺炎|Inflammation of the lungs caused by infection, usually bacterial or viral.  Symptoms can include cough, fever, chills, and shortness of breath.  Pneumonia can range in severity from mild to life-threatening.|Example: The doctor diagnosed him with pneumonia and prescribed antibiotics.|近义词：lung infection, pneumonitis|反义词：None specifically, but "healthy lungs" could be considered an antonym.
