mardi-grases:/ˌmɑːdiˈɡrɑːsɪz/| n. | 狂欢节 (复数) | Mardi Gras is a festival celebrated in many places, most famously in New Orleans, Louisiana. It's a time of parades, parties, and costumes, typically held before Ash Wednesday. The plural form, "mardi-grases," is used to refer to multiple instances of the Mardi Gras celebration, or perhaps to emphasize the numerous events and festivities associated with it.  | Example: The New Orleans mardi-grases are world-renowned for their vibrant atmosphere. = 新奥尔良的狂欢节以其热烈的氛围而闻名世界。  The upcoming mardi-grases promise to be spectacular. = 即将来临的狂欢节有望非常壮观。 | 近义词：carnival, festival | 反义词：None (Not applicable to a noun representing a specific event) |名词
