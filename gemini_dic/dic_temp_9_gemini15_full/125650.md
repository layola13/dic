mummified:/ˈmʌmɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 木乃伊化的；干枯的 |  Describes something that has been preserved, especially a body, in a way that prevents decay, similar to the process used to create mummies.  | The mummified remains were remarkably well-preserved.  木乃伊化的遗体保存得非常好。 | embalmed, preserved | decayed, rotting |
