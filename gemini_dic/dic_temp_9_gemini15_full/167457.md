prevenience:/prɪˈviːniəns/| n. |预防；先见之明；预先采取的措施 |The word "prevenience" is an obsolete word, meaning the act of preventing something or the state of being prevented. It suggests foresight and preemptive action to avoid a negative outcome.  It is rarely used in modern English. |例句：The king's prevenience in fortifying the border prevented a disastrous invasion. 国王预先加固边境，避免了一场灾难性的入侵。  |近义词：prevention, precaution, foresight, anticipation |反义词：negligence, carelessness, oversight |名词
