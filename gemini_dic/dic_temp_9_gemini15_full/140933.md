one-and-all:/ˌwʌn ænd ˈɔːl/| pron.|每一个；所有的人；全体|This phrase means everyone without exception.  It's used to emphasize inclusivity and to address a group collectively. |Example: The invitation was extended to one and all.  邀请函发给了所有人。|近义词：everyone, everybody, all|反义词：no one, nobody


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The invitation is for one and all. = 邀请函是给所有人的。
One and all are welcome to the party. = 每个人都欢迎参加派对。


一般过去时:
The announcement was made to one and all. =  公告向所有人宣布了。
One and all celebrated the victory. = 每个人都庆祝了胜利。


现在完成时:
The news has reached one and all. = 消息已经传达给所有人了。
One and all have been informed of the changes. = 所有的人都已经被告知了这些改变。


过去完成时:
The decision had been explained to one and all. =  这个决定已经向所有人解释过了。
One and all had already left before the rain started. = 在下雨之前，所有人都已经离开了。


将来时:
The results will be shared with one and all. = 结果将与所有人分享。
One and all will be invited to the ceremony. = 所有的人都会被邀请参加仪式。


虚拟语气:
If only one and all could understand. =  如果所有人都能理解就好了。
I wish one and all were here to witness this. = 我希望所有的人都来见证这件事。


被动语态:
The message was relayed to one and all. =  消息被转达给了所有人。
The opportunity was offered to one and all. = 机会提供给了所有人。


疑问句:
Will the offer be available to one and all? = 这个优惠对所有人都有效吗？
Are one and all invited to the meeting? = 所有的人都邀请参加会议吗？


否定句:
The information was not given to one and all. = 信息没有告诉所有人。
Not one and all agreed with the proposal. =  并非所有人都同意这项提案。
