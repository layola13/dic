fracases:/frəˈkæsɪz/| n.|  fiascoes, 闹剧；失败；混乱 |A fracas is a noisy quarrel or fight.  It implies a chaotic and often public disturbance.  The word often suggests a degree of violence or at least a significant disruption. |例句：The party ended in a fracas after too much alcohol was consumed.  派对在喝了太多酒后以一场闹剧告终。

例句：A fracas erupted between rival supporters after the football match. 足球比赛结束后，敌对的支持者之间爆发了冲突。


近义词：brawl, melee, disturbance, commotion, uproar

反义词：peace, calm, tranquility, order


词性：名词
