ensley:/ˈɛnzli/| n. |恩斯利（女子名）|Ensley is a feminine given name of English origin.  It is believed to be derived from the Old English elements "æn" meaning "one" and "lēah" meaning "wood" or "clearing," thus possibly meaning "one's clearing in the wood."  It's a relatively modern name gaining popularity in recent decades.|Example: My daughter's name is Ensley.  我的女儿名叫恩斯利。|近义词:  Ashley, Ainsley|反义词:  (无)|名词
