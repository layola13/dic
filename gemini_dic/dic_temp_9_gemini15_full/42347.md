counteractors: /ˌkaʊntərˈæktərz/ | n. |对抗物；反作用物；对抗者 |Those who work against something, or things that counteract something.  Counteractors can be individuals, groups, or even opposing forces or processes. |例句：The government's new policies faced strong counteractors from various interest groups.  政府的新政策受到了来自各个利益集团的强烈反对。

例句：The effects of the drug were counteracted by a number of physiological counteractors within the body.  药物的作用被体内的一些生理对抗物抵消了。


近义词：opponents, antagonists, adversaries, counteragents

反义词：collaborators, allies, supporters

词性: noun
