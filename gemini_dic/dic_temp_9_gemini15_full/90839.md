hundredth-places:/ˈhʌndrɪθ pleɪsɪz/| n. |百分位数；小数点后第二位|The hundredth places refer to the second digit after the decimal point in a number.  For example, in the number 3.14159, the '1' is in the tenths place, and the '4' is in the hundredth place.|近义词：second decimal place|反义词：无
