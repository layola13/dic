dying-out: /ˈdaɪɪŋ aʊt/ | adj. | 逐渐消失的；濒临灭绝的 | Gradually ceasing to exist; becoming extinct. |  The dying-out of the old traditions is a sad thing. 老传统逐渐消失是一件令人悲伤的事情。 The species is in danger of dying out.  这个物种面临灭绝的危险。 | 近义词：extinct, disappearing, fading, vanishing | 反义词：flourishing, thriving, booming


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The old craft is dying out slowly. = 这门古老的手艺正在慢慢消失。
Many languages are dying out around the world. = 世界各地许多语言正在消亡。


一般过去时:
The local dialect died out many years ago. = 当地方言在很多年前就消失了。
That particular style of painting died out in the 19th century. = 那种特殊的绘画风格在19世纪就消失了。


现在完成时:
Several species have died out in the last decade. = 在过去的十年里，已经有几种物种灭绝了。
Many traditional skills have died out due to modernization. = 许多传统技能由于现代化而消失了。


过去完成时:
The old customs had died out before I was born. = 我出生之前，那些旧习俗就已经消失了。
That type of bird had died out long before the arrival of Europeans. = 那种鸟类早在欧洲人到来之前就灭绝了。


将来时:
If we don't act now, this species will die out within the next century. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，这个物种将在下一个世纪灭绝。
Without conservation efforts, many plants will die out. = 如果没有保护工作，许多植物将会灭绝。


虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, the species might not have died out. = 如果我们早点采取行动，这个物种可能就不会灭绝了。
I wish that those old songs hadn't died out. = 我真希望那些老歌没有消失。


被动语态:
The ancient language was slowly dying out. = 古代语言正在慢慢消亡。
The tradition is being allowed to die out. = 这种传统正在被允许消亡。


疑问句:
Is this type of flower dying out? = 这种花快灭绝了吗？
Are these customs dying out in your community? = 这些习俗在你的社区中正在消失吗？


否定句:
The tradition is not dying out yet. = 这种传统还没有消失。
This type of tree isn't dying out in this region. = 这种树在这个地区并没有灭绝。


PHRASE->
die out gradually = 逐渐消失

on the verge of dying out = 濒临灭绝
slowly dying out = 慢慢消失
likely to die out = 很可能消失
