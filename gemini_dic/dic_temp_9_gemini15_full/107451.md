lampooning:/læmˈpuːnɪŋ/| v.| 讽刺；挖苦；嘲弄 | The act of publicly criticizing someone or something by using ridicule, irony, or sarcasm.  It often involves humor, but with the intent to expose flaws or weaknesses. | 例句：The magazine lampooned the politician's recent gaffe. 这本杂志讽刺了这位政治家最近的失误。

例句：His speech was a lampooning of the government's policies.  他的演讲是对政府政策的嘲讽。


近义词：satirizing, caricaturing, mocking, ridiculing, parodying

反义词：praising, commending, flattering

词性：动词
