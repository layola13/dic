fusion: /ˈfjuːʒən/ | n. |融合；熔合；混合；聚变| The process or result of combining different things into a single, unified entity.  It can refer to the merging of different ideas, cultures, styles, or substances. In physics, it refers to nuclear fusion. |例句：The fusion of two companies created a powerful new entity.  两家公司的合并创造了一个强大的新实体。

例句：Nuclear fusion is a promising source of clean energy. 核聚变是清洁能源的有希望的来源。

近义词：combination, amalgamation, synthesis, blending, mixture

反义词：separation, division, segregation, fission
