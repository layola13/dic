servility:/sɜːˈvɪləti/ | n. | 卑躬屈膝；奴性；奴颜婢膝 | The state or quality of being servile; excessive willingness to serve or please others.  It often implies a lack of self-respect and independence. | 例句：His servility to the boss was embarrassing to watch.  他的对老板的卑躬屈膝令人尴尬。

His servility prevented him from expressing his true opinions. 他的奴性使他无法表达自己的真实想法。


近义词：subservience, obsequiousness, fawning, sycophancy | 反义词：independence, assertiveness, defiance, autonomy
