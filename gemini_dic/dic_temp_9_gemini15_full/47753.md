decalog:/ˈdɛkəˌlɒɡ/ | n. |十诫；十戒律 |The Decalogue refers to the Ten Commandments given by God to Moses, as described in the Book of Exodus in the Bible.  It serves as a fundamental set of moral and religious laws.  The term can also refer to any set of ten principles or rules. |Example:  The Decalogue is a cornerstone of Judeo-Christian ethics.  十诫是犹太教和基督教伦理学的基石。  The company adopted a decalogue of best business practices. 公司采用了十项最佳商业实践准则。|近义词：ten commandments, precepts, principles |反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be terms representing lawlessness or immorality.
