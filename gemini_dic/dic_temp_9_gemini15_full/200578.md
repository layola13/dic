slaughterable: /ˈslɔːtərəbl/ | adj. | 可屠宰的，适于屠宰的 | Capable of being slaughtered; fit for slaughter.  This usually refers to animals raised for meat, implying they are of suitable age and condition for processing. |


例句：

The pigs are now slaughterable and ready for market. = 猪现在可以屠宰了，准备上市。

The farmer assessed his cattle to determine which were slaughterable. = 农民评估了他的牛群以确定哪些可以屠宰。


近义词：fit for slaughter, suitable for slaughter, butcherable


反义词：unfit for slaughter, too young to slaughter


词性:形容词
