closets:/ˈklɒzɪts/ /ˈklɑːzɪts/| n. |壁橱；储藏室|A closet is a small room or cupboard used for storing clothes or other things.  It's typically built into a wall.|例句：I hung my coat in the closet.我把外套挂在壁橱里了。

例句：She keeps all her old photographs in the closet. 她把所有旧照片都放在壁橱里。


近义词：wardrobe, cupboard, locker|反义词：open, exposed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The closet holds all my winter clothes. = 这个壁橱装着我所有的冬衣。
Does the closet have enough space for all your shoes? = 这个壁橱有足够的空间放你所有的鞋子吗？


一般过去时:
I found my lost earring in the closet. = 我在壁橱里找到了我丢失的耳环。
