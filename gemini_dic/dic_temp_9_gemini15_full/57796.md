dredges:/ˈdrɛdʒɪz/| n. v.|挖掘机；疏浚；挖掘；拖网捕捞|A dredge is a machine used for digging or removing things from the bottom of a body of water, such as a river or harbor.  It can also refer to the act of digging or dragging a net through water to catch fish or shellfish. |例句：The harbor was dredged to allow larger ships to enter. 港口进行了疏浚以允许更大的船只进入。

The fishermen used a dredge to harvest scallops from the seabed. 渔民用挖掘机从海底收获扇贝。|近义词：excavate, dredge up (figurative)|反义词：fill, bury|
