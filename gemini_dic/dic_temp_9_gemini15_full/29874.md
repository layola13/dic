causey:/ˈkɔːzi/| n.|铺砌的小路，鹅卵石路|A causey is a raised paved path, typically one made of stones.  It is often found in rural areas or alongside waterways.|例句：The causey was worn smooth by centuries of foot traffic.  这条鹅卵石路被几个世纪的行人磨得光滑。

例句：We walked along the causey, enjoying the scenery. 我们沿着鹅卵石路走着，欣赏着风景。|近义词：path, road, track|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with  unpaved track or muddy path.|名词
