rehouse: /ˌriːˈhaʊs/ | vt. |重新安置；搬迁安置 | To provide a new home or accommodation for (someone or something).  |例句：The council is working to rehouse the families affected by the fire. 市议会正在努力为受火灾影响的家庭重新安置住房。

例句：The company plans to rehouse its employees in a more modern building. 公司计划将员工搬迁到一栋更现代化的建筑物中。


近义词：relocate, resettle, accommodate


反义词：evict, displace (in the sense of forcing someone to leave their home)


词性：动词
