packing:/ˈpækɪŋ/| n. v.|包装；打包；填充物|The act of putting things into containers for transport or storage; the materials used for this.  It can also refer to the process of filling something tightly. |例句：The packing of the boxes was completed before the shipment. 箱子的包装在发货前完成了。

例句：He spent the afternoon packing his suitcase for the trip. 他花了一下午时间打包去旅行的行李。

近义词：packaging, bundling, wrapping|反义词：unpacking, unwrapping|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He packs his lunch every day. = 他每天都打包午餐。
They pack the goods carefully. = 他们仔细地包装货物。

一般过去时:
She packed her bags last night. = 她昨晚收拾了行李。
We packed the boxes tightly. = 我们把箱子紧紧地打包了。

现在完成时:
I have packed all my belongings. = 我已经打包好了所有我的东西。
They have packed the fragile items with extra care. = 他们已经非常小心地包装了易碎物品。

过去完成时:
He had packed his suitcase before the taxi arrived. = 出租车到达之前，他已经打包好了行李箱。
She had packed all the presents before the party started. = 派对开始前，她已经把所有礼物都打包好了。

将来时:
I will pack my bags tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上会打包行李。
We will pack the order for shipment next week. = 我们下周会打包订单以便发货。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pack more carefully. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更仔细地打包。
I wish I had packed a warmer jacket. = 我真希望我打包了一件更暖和的外套。

被动语态:
The boxes were packed yesterday. = 箱子昨天被打包了。
The goods are being packed for shipment. = 货物正在打包准备发货。

疑问句:
Have you packed your clothes yet? = 你已经打包好衣服了吗？
Are they packing the furniture carefully? = 他们正在仔细地包装家具吗？

否定句:
He didn't pack his lunch today. = 他今天没有打包午餐。
She hasn't packed her suitcase yet. = 她还没打包行李箱。
