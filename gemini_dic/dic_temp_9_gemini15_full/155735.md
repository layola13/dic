phonemization:/ˌfoʊnəmaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |音素化，语音化 |The process of representing speech sounds with phonemes.  It involves identifying and transcribing the individual sounds of a word or utterance using a phonetic alphabet. This is crucial in linguistics for analyzing pronunciation variations and creating dictionaries and language-learning materials.  | Example: The phonemization of the word "though" in Received Pronunciation would be /ðɔː/. |近义词：phonetic transcription, sound segmentation |反义词：orthography (spelling)
