phycomycetes:/ˌfaɪkəˈmaɪsiːts/| n. |藻状菌纲|A class of fungi characterized by their typically aquatic or terrestrial habitats, coenocytic hyphae (lacking septa), and the production of motile zoospores.  They are often found in moist environments and play important roles in decomposition and nutrient cycling. |例句：The study focused on the diversity of phycomycetes in the soil samples. 这项研究集中在土壤样本中藻状菌纲的多样性。|近义词：water molds, oomycetes (although oomycetes are technically not true fungi)|反义词：N/A
