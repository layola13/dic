overpotency:/ˌoʊvərˈpoʊtənsi/| n. |效力过强，药效过强|The excessive potency or strength of something, especially a drug or medicine.  It refers to a situation where the effect of a substance is stronger than intended or expected, potentially leading to adverse effects.|例句：The overpotency of the medication caused unexpected side effects.  药物效力过强导致了意想不到的副作用。|近义词：hyperpotency, excessive potency, overdose|反义词：underpotency, weak effect
