pictorials:/pɪkˈtɔːriəl/| n.|图画；图解；画报|A collection of pictures, especially one that tells a story or illustrates a book or article.  It can also refer to a publication consisting primarily of pictures.|例句：The magazine featured stunning pictorials of the Amazon rainforest. 这本杂志刊登了亚马逊雨林的精彩图片。|例句：The children's book used vibrant pictorials to engage young readers. 这本儿童读物使用了鲜艳的图画来吸引年轻读者。|近义词：illustrations, photographs, images|反义词：text, narrative (without images)
