hollander:/ˈhɒləndə(r)/| n. |荷兰人；（旧时）一种漂洗羊毛的机器|A Hollander is a person from the Netherlands.  It also refers to a machine formerly used in the process of fulling or cleansing wool. |例句：He is a Hollander, born and raised in Amsterdam. 他是荷兰人，在阿姆斯特丹土生土长。  The old mill housed a massive Hollander for processing wool. 那座旧磨坊里有一台巨大的荷兰式洗毛机。|近义词：Dutchman (for person), wool-washing machine|反义词：无
