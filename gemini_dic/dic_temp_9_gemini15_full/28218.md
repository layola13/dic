capsaicin: /kæpˈseɪsɪn/ | n. | 辣椒素 |  A pungent compound found in chili peppers that produces a burning sensation when it comes into contact with mucous membranes. It is responsible for the "heat" in chili peppers and is used in various products, including pain relievers and pepper sprays. | Example: The capsaicin in jalapeños makes them spicy.  辣椒中的辣椒素使它们辛辣。 |近义词: chili extract, pungent compound |反义词:  (no direct antonym, but could be described as the opposite of a "cooling" or "soothing" substance) | 词性：名词
