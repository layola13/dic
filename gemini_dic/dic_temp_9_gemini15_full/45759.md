cushioned:/ˈkʊʃənd/ | adj. | 减震的；缓冲的；舒适的 | Provided with cushions; softened or mitigated.  |例句：The car's suspension system is well cushioned, providing a smooth ride. 这辆车的悬挂系统减震效果很好，行驶起来很平稳。
The fall was cushioned by the soft grass.  柔软的草地缓冲了跌落的力量。|近义词：padded, buffered, softened, protected |反义词：unprotected, harsh, jarring
