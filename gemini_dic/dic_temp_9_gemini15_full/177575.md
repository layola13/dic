rebrewing:/ˌriːˈbruːɪŋ/| v.|再酿造；重新酿造|The act of brewing again, often referring to brewing the same beer recipe or style.|例句：The brewery is rebrewing their popular IPA after a successful launch. 这家啤酒厂在成功推出后正在重新酿造他们受欢迎的IPA。例句：They decided to rebrew the batch after discovering a flaw in the original process. 他们在发现原始工艺中的缺陷后决定重新酿造这批啤酒。|近义词：remaking, brewing again |反义词：discarding, destroying
