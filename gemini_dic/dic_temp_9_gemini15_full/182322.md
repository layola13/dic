renamed:/ˌriːˈneɪmd/| v.|重新命名，改名|To give a new name to (something).|The company renamed its product to improve its image. 公司为了提升形象而重新命名了其产品。|  |  |verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rename the street every year. = 他们每年都给这条街改名。
The company renames its products frequently. = 这家公司经常更改其产品名称。


一般过去时:
The park was renamed after a famous writer. = 公园以一位著名作家的名字重新命名。
He renamed his dog after his favorite superhero. = 他以他最喜欢的超级英雄的名字重新命名了他的狗。


现在完成时:
They have renamed the building several times. = 他们已经多次更改了建筑物的名称。
The town has been renamed since the war. = 自战争以来，这个城镇已经改名了。


过去完成时:
They had renamed the project before the launch. = 他们在项目启动之前就已将其改名。
The book had been renamed by the publisher before it was released. = 这本书在发行前已被出版商改名。


将来时:
They will rename the stadium next year. = 他们明年将重新命名体育馆。
The project will be renamed after the completion of the feasibility study. = 项目的可行性研究完成后将被重新命名。


虚拟语气:
If they renamed the product, it might sell better. = 如果他们重新命名该产品，它可能会卖得更好。
I wish they had renamed the street years ago. = 我希望他们几年前就给这条街改名了。


被动语态:
The street was renamed last month. = 这条街在上个月改名了。
The software is being renamed to match the new branding. = 该软件正在改名以匹配新的品牌形象。


疑问句:
Will the company be renamed soon? = 公司很快就会改名吗？
Has the product been renamed yet? = 产品已经改名了吗？


否定句:
They did not rename the building. = 他们没有给建筑物改名。
The project has not been renamed yet. = 项目尚未改名。


PHRASE->
be renamed = 被重新命名
get renamed = 被重新命名
have been renamed = 已经被重新命名
newly renamed = 新近重新命名的
renamed as = 重新命名为
