perpetrated:/ˈpɜːrpətreɪtɪd/| v.|犯下（罪行）；实施（坏事）|to carry out or commit (a harmful, illegal, or immoral act)|The terrorists perpetrated a series of bombings. 恐怖分子犯下了一系列爆炸案。|近义词：commit, execute, inflict|反义词：prevent, stop, avert


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He perpetrates crimes regularly. = 他经常犯下罪行。
They perpetrate fraud on a large scale. = 他们大规模地实施诈骗。

一般过去时:
The government perpetrated a lie to the public. = 政府对公众说谎。
He perpetrated a cruel joke on his friend. = 他对他朋友开了个残酷的玩笑。

现在完成时:
They have perpetrated many atrocities. = 他们犯下了许多暴行。
She has perpetrated a hoax on social media. = 她曾在社交媒体上制造了一个恶作剧。

过去完成时:
The war had been perpetrated by a power-hungry dictator. = 那场战争是由一个权力欲熏心的独裁者发动的。
Before the scandal broke, they had already perpetrated many illegal acts. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他们已经犯下了许多违法行为。

将来时:
He will perpetrate more violence if he's not stopped. = 如果不阻止他，他会实施更多暴力行为。
They will perpetrate a significant environmental damage if this project goes ahead. = 如果这个项目继续进行，他们将造成重大的环境破坏。

虚拟语气:
If he had not perpetrated that crime, he would be free now. = 如果他没有犯下那项罪行，他现在就自由了。
I wish they had not perpetrated such a terrible act. = 我真希望他们没有做出如此可怕的行为。

被动语态:
Many crimes were perpetrated during the war. = 战争期间犯下了许多罪行。
The fraud was perpetrated by a group of insiders. = 这起诈骗是由一群内部人员实施的。

疑问句:
Who perpetrated this terrible act? = 谁犯下了这件可怕的罪行？
What kind of crime was perpetrated? = 犯了什么罪？

否定句:
They did not perpetrate any violence. = 他们没有实施任何暴力行为。
The company denied perpetrating any illegal activities. = 公司否认从事任何非法活动。
