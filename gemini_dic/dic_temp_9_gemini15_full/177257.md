reassert:/ˌriːəˈsɜːrt/ | vt. | 再次声明；重申；重新确立；恢复；坚定地主张 | To state or declare something again with emphasis, especially in order to correct or contradict something previously said or implied.  |例句：The government reasserted its commitment to economic reform. 政府再次申明其致力于经济改革。

例句：He reasserted his authority over the team. 他重新确立了对团队的权威。


近义词：reaffirm, restate, reinstate, reaffirm, re-establish | 反义词：retract, deny, abandon, relinquish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reasserts his innocence every chance he gets. 他抓住每一个机会重申自己的清白。
They reassert their dominance in the market regularly. 他们定期重申他们在市场上的主导地位。


一般过去时:
She reasserted her position after a period of uncertainty.  一段时间的犹豫不决之后，她重申了自己的立场。
The company reasserted its market share through aggressive marketing. 公司通过积极的市场营销重新确立了其市场份额。


现在完成时:
He has reasserted his control over the situation. 他已经重新控制了局面。
The team has reasserted its winning streak.  这支队伍已经再次取得连胜。


过去完成时:
They had reasserted their independence before the negotiations began. 在谈判开始之前，他们已经重新确立了独立性。
She had reasserted her authority before the meeting started. 会议开始前，她已经重申了自己的权威。

虚拟语气:
