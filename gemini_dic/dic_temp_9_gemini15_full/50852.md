desegregated: /ˌdiːˈsɛɡrɪɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. |  取消种族隔离的；去除种族隔离的 |  The adjective "desegregated" describes a state or situation where racial segregation has been abolished or eliminated. It implies the integration of previously separated racial groups, particularly in areas like education, housing, and public facilities.  | Example:  The school was desegregated in the 1960s.  这所学校在20世纪60年代取消了种族隔离。  | 近义词：integrated, mixed, unified | 反义词：segregated, separated, discriminatory | 词性: adjective
