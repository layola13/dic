geologists:/dʒiˈɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. |地质学家 |Scientists who study the Earth's physical structure and substance, its history, and the processes that act upon it. Geologists analyze rocks, minerals, and fossils to understand the Earth's formation, evolution, and resources.  | Example: Geologists are working to understand the causes of the recent earthquake. 地质学家们正在努力了解最近地震的成因。  Many geologists believe that the continents were once joined together. 许多地质学家认为大陆曾经连在一起。|近义词：geophysicists, earth scientists |反义词：N/A
