elevenfold: /ɪˈlɛvənfoʊld/ | adj. | 十一倍的；十一重 |  Elevenfold means eleven times as great or as much. It is used to describe an increase or multiplication by a factor of eleven.  | The company's profits increased elevenfold this year.  今年公司利润增长了十一倍。 |  tenfold, twelvefold |  onefold (反义并非完全对立，而是指单一倍数)
