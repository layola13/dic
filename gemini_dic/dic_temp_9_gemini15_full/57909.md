driftwood:/ˈdrɪftwʊd/| n. |漂流木，冲积木 | Pieces of wood that have been carried by water and deposited on a riverbank or seashore.  Driftwood is often weathered and bleached by the sun and sea. | 例句：The beach was littered with driftwood after the storm.  暴风雨过后，海滩上散落着许多漂流木。

The children built a raft out of driftwood they found on the shore.  孩子们用在岸边找到的漂流木造了一艘木筏。


近义词：flotsam, jetsam | 反义词：None (no direct antonym)
