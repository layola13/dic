episodic:/ˌɛpɪˈsɑːdɪk/| adj.|情节的；片段的；间歇性的|occurring in separate episodes; intermittent;  relating to or consisting of a series of separate events.  |例句：The show has an episodic structure, with each episode focusing on a different case. 这部剧采用情节剧结构，每一集都关注一个不同的案例。

例句：He suffers from episodic migraines. 他患有间歇性偏头痛。


近义词：intermittent, sporadic, serial|反义词：continuous, constant, uninterrupted|词性：形容词
