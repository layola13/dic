filature:/ˈfɪljətʃər/| n.|丝厂；缫丝厂；丝线|A filature is a factory where silk is processed and spun into thread.  It involves the unwinding of cocoons, cleaning, and spinning the silk filaments into continuous threads.  |例句：The ancient filature still stands as a testament to the region's silk-producing history.  这座古老的丝厂仍然屹立着，证明了该地区养蚕业的历史。|近义词：silk mill, silk factory|反义词：无
