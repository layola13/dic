fischer:/ˈfɪʃər/ | n. | 费舍尔（姓氏）；渔夫；捕鱼者 |  A fisher is a person who catches fish, either for a living or as a hobby.  The word can also be a surname. |例句：My grandfather was a fisher who spent his life on the sea.  我的祖父是一位渔夫，一生都在海上度过。

例句：The family name Fischer is quite common in Germany. 费舍尔这个姓氏在德国很常见。

近义词：fisherman, angler

反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fishes every weekend. = 他每个周末钓鱼。
The fisher catches salmon in the river. = 渔夫在河里捕捞鲑鱼。


一般过去时:
He fished all day yesterday. = 他昨天钓了一整天鱼。
The fisher caught a large tuna last week. = 渔夫上周钓到了一条大金枪鱼。

过去完成时:

将来时:
