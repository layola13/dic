paya:/ˈpaɪə/|n.| 酬劳，报酬；报应；报答| payment; reward; retribution|The paya for his work was generous.  他的报酬很丰厚。|  reward, recompense, compensation | punishment, penalty | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company pays its employees well. = 公司给员工的薪水很高。
He pays his bills on time. = 他按时付账单。


一般过去时:
She paid for the groceries with a credit card. = 她用信用卡付了杂货款。
They paid a visit to their grandparents. = 他们去拜访了他们的祖父母。


现在完成时:
I have paid all my debts. = 我已经还清了所有的债务。
Have you paid your taxes yet? = 你交税了吗？


过去完成时:
He had paid the rent before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前付了房租。
She had paid her dues to the organization. = 她已经向组织缴纳了会费。


将来时:
I will pay you back tomorrow. = 我明天还你钱。
They will pay for their mistakes. = 他们将为他们的错误付出代价。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would pay for the trip. = 如果我有更多钱，我会支付这趟旅行的费用。
I wish I could pay off my student loan quickly. = 我希望我能尽快还清我的学生贷款。


被动语态:
The workers are paid weekly. = 工人们每周领工资。
The bill was paid in full. = 账单已全额支付。


疑问句:
How much did you pay for that car? = 你那辆车花了多少钱？
Will you pay by cash or card? = 你用现金还是卡支付？


否定句:
He didn't pay attention to the warning. = 他没有注意警告。
I won't pay for something I didn't order. = 我不会为我没有点的东西付钱。
