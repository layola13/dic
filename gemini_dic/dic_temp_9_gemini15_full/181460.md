rejoicement:/rɪˈdʒɔɪsmənt/| n. |欢庆；喜悦；庆祝活动 |The feeling of great joy;  a celebration or festive gathering.  It often involves expressions of happiness and festivity. |例句：There was much rejoicing at the news of the victory.  胜利的消息带来了许多欢庆。

例句：The wedding was a time of great rejoicing for the whole family.  婚礼对全家来说是一段充满喜悦的时光。


近义词：celebration, festivity, jubilation, merriment

反义词：sorrow, grief, mourning, sadness

词性：名词
