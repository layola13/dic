forcingly:/ˈfɔːrsɪŋli/ | adv. | 强制地；强迫地 |  In a forceful or compelling manner; with force.  It implies a lack of choice or consent. |例句：He forcingly pushed his way through the crowd. 他强行挤过人群。|例句：She forcingly opened the door, ignoring the doorknob. 她用力打开门，没管门把手。|近义词：compulsorily, forcefully, forcibly, imperatively |反义词：voluntarily, willingly, gently
