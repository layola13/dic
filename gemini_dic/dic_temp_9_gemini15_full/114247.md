mailings:/ˈmeɪlɪŋz/| n.|邮寄；邮件发送；邮递件|The act of sending something by mail, or the items sent.  It often refers to a batch or series of items sent at once, such as marketing materials.|例句：The marketing team prepared thousands of mailings for the new product launch. 市场团队为新产品发布准备了数千份邮寄材料。

例句：Our company's direct mailings have seen a significant increase in response rates this quarter. 我们公司直邮的回复率本季度显著提高。


近义词：postings, sendings, distributions|反义词：None specifically; antonyms would depend on the context (e.g., if referring to digital vs. physical mail, the antonym might be "emails").|词性：名词
