overextraction:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈstrækʃən/| n. |过度开采，过度提取|The act of extracting something, such as a resource, to an excessive or unsustainable degree, often leading to depletion or damage.  This can refer to natural resources like groundwater, minerals, or timber, or even to overusing something less tangible like data or labor.|例句：The overextraction of groundwater has led to severe water shortages in the region.  地区地下水的过度开采导致了严重的水资源短缺。|例句：Overextraction of timber from the forest has resulted in deforestation and habitat loss. 森林中木材的过度开采导致了森林砍伐和栖息地丧失。|近义词：overexploitation, depletion, exhaustion|反义词：conservation, preservation, sustainable use|名词
