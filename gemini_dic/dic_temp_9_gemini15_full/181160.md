reinfusion:/ˌriːɪnˈfjuːʒən/| n. |再输液，再灌注|The process of reintroducing a fluid, especially blood or plasma, into the circulatory system.  It's often used in medical contexts to describe the reintroduction of previously removed or processed fluids. |例句：The surgeon decided on reinfusion of the patient's own blood to minimize the risk of transfusion reactions.  医生决定对病人进行自体血液再输液，以最大限度地降低输血反应的风险。

例句：After the surgery, reinfusion of the collected blood helped to reduce the need for blood transfusions. 手术后，收集血液的再输注有助于减少对输血的需求。|近义词：reintroduction, reinjection |反义词：removal, extraction
