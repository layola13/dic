hoorays:/hʊˈreɪz/| n. |欢呼声，喝彩声 |A series of shouts of "Hooray!" expressing approval, celebration, or joy.  |例句：The crowd erupted in hoorays as the team scored the winning goal.  人群在球队攻入制胜球时爆发出阵阵欢呼声。
The audience responded with enthusiastic hoorays. 观众报以热烈的欢呼。|近义词：cheers, shouts of joy, acclamations |反义词：boos, hisses, groans |名词
