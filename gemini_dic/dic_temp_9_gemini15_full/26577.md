caboodles:/kəˈbuːdəlz/| n. |一大堆，许多 |A large number or quantity of something; a lot.  The word is informal and often used humorously. |例句：I have a caboodles of things to do today.  = 我今天有一大堆事情要做。

例句：There were caboodles of people at the concert. = 音乐会上人山人海。


近义词：a lot, lots, heaps, tons, a bunch, a slew


反义词：a few, a little, scarcely any, hardly any


词性：名词
