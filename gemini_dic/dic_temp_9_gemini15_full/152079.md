paternality: /ˌpætərˈnælɪti/ | n. | 父性；父权；父亲身份 | The state or fact of being a father; fatherhood.  It can also refer to the social role and responsibilities of a father. |  例句：The paternity test confirmed he was the father. 亲子鉴定证实他就是父亲。

例句：He questioned his own paternality after seeing how his son behaved.  在看到儿子的行为后，他开始质疑自己作为父亲的角色。


近义词：fatherhood, paternity test, parentage


反义词：maternity (母性)


词性: 名词
