season:/ˈsiːzn/| n.|季节，时期，一段时间|A period of the year characterized by particular weather patterns; a period or stage in a process or cycle.|例句：Spring is my favorite season. 春天是我最喜欢的季节。

例句：The growing season for tomatoes is relatively short.  西红柿的生长期比较短。


近义词：period, time, phase|反义词：N/A|词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The seasons change throughout the year. =一年四季变化。
Summer is the hottest season. = 夏季是最热的季节。


一般过去时:
Last winter was very harsh. = 去年冬天非常寒冷。
We enjoyed a wonderful summer season last year. = 去年我们度过了一个美好的夏季。


现在完成时:
We have experienced four distinct seasons this year. = 今年我们经历了四个不同的季节。
The farmers have already harvested their crops for the season. = 农民们已经收割了本季的庄稼。


过去完成时:
By the time autumn arrived, the leaves had changed color. = 秋天到来的时候，树叶已经变色了。
They had finished their work before the off-season began. = 他们在淡季开始之前完成了工作。


将来时:
Next spring, we will plant new flowers. = 明年春天，我们将种植新的花卉。
The next season will bring many changes. = 下个季度将会带来很多变化。


虚拟语气:
If it were spring, we could go for a hike. = 如果是春天，我们可以去远足。
I wish it were summer already. = 我希望现在已经是夏天了。


被动语态:
The crops are affected by the changing seasons. = 庄稼受到季节变化的影响。
The schedule for the next season has not yet been released. = 下个季度的日程安排尚未公布。


疑问句:
What is your favorite season? = 你最喜欢的季节是什么？
Which season do you prefer? = 你更喜欢哪个季节？


否定句:
This year, we didn't have a real winter. = 今年，我们没有真正的冬天。
The summer season wasn't as hot as usual. = 夏季不像往常那样热。
