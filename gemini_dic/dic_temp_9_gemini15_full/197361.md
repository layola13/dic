sheafs:/ʃiːvz/| n.|捆，束；(谷物等的)禾束|A sheaf is a collection of things, typically stalks of grain, bound together.  It often refers to a number of things tied or gathered together.|例句：The farmer gathered the sheafs of wheat into bundles.  农夫把麦捆捆成捆。

例句：Several sheafs of papers were stacked on the desk.  几捆文件堆在桌子上。


近义词：bundle, bunch, cluster|反义词：散乱(没有直接的反义词，需用描述性词语表达)|词性:名词
