repunish:/ˌriːˈpʌnɪʃ/| vt.|再次惩罚；重复惩罚|To punish again or repeatedly.  This word is not commonly used and is considered somewhat archaic or informal.|例句：He was repunished for his continued disobedience. 他因为持续的违抗而再次受到惩罚。|例句：The judge decided to repunish the offender for his repeated crimes. 法官决定再次惩罚这个罪犯，因为他犯下了多次罪行。|近义词：reprimand, chastise, penalize|反义词：reward, pardon
