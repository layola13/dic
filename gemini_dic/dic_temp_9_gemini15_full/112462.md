loudspeakers:/ˈlaʊdˌspiːkərz/ | n. |扬声器，扩音器 | An electronic device that converts electrical signals into sound waves, making sounds louder.  Loudspeakers are used in a wide variety of applications, from home stereo systems to public address systems. |  The loudspeakers at the concert were incredibly powerful.  演唱会的扬声器非常强大。

The loudspeakers in the stadium blasted out the national anthem. 体育场里的扬声器播放着国歌。|近义词：speakers, audio speakers |反义词：earphones, headphones
