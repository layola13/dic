saves:/seɪvz/| vt. n.|节省；挽救；保存|to keep something safe from harm or loss; to rescue someone or something from danger; to store information|例句：He saves money every month. 他每个月都存钱。

例句：The firefighters saved the cat from the burning building. 消防员从燃烧的建筑物中救出了猫。


近义词：preserve, conserve, rescue, store|反义词：waste, squander, destroy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He saves money each month. = 他每个月存钱。
She saves all her emails. = 她保存所有邮件。


一般过去时:
They saved the drowning child. = 他们救了那个溺水的孩子。
I saved the document before closing the program. = 我在关闭程序之前保存了文档。


现在完成时:
She has saved enough money for a down payment. = 她已经攒够了首付的钱。
We have saved several endangered species from extinction. = 我们已经挽救了几个濒临灭绝的物种。


过去完成时:
He had saved a considerable sum before he retired. = 他退休前已经攒了一大笔钱。
They had saved the best cake for the end of the party. = 他们把最好的蛋糕留到派对结束时吃。


将来时:
I will save you a seat. = 我会给你留个座位。
We will save the planet if we act now. = 如果我们现在行动，我们将拯救地球。


虚拟语气:
If I had saved more money, I could have bought a house. = 如果我存的钱更多，我就能买房子了。
I wish I had saved that photo. = 我真希望我保存了那张照片。


被动语态:
The city was saved from disaster by the timely intervention. = 及时的干预挽救了这座城市免遭灾难。
Many lives were saved by the heroic efforts of the rescuers. = 救援人员的英勇努力挽救了许多生命。


疑问句:
Does he save any money? = 他存钱吗？
Did you save the file? = 你保存文件了吗？


否定句:
He doesn't save money. = 他不存钱。
I didn't save the document. = 我没有保存文档。
