emollient:/ɪˈmɑːliənt/| n. adj.|润肤剂；柔软剂；使柔软的；缓和的|A substance that softens or soothes the skin.  It can also refer to something that soothes or softens feelings or situations.|例句：The lotion is an emollient that helps to soothe dry skin. 这款乳液是一种润肤剂，有助于舒缓干燥的皮肤。

例句：His words were an emollient to her anger.  他的话语缓和了她的怒气。|近义词：softener, balm, lubricant, soothing agent|反义词：irritant, astringent, harsh
