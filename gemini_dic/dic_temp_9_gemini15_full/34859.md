clinographic:/ˌklɪnəˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|斜绘的；斜描的|Relating to or denoting a clinograph or clinographic projection.  A clinographic projection is a perspective drawing showing an object as it would appear if viewed from a slightly inclined angle.|The clinographic drawing clearly showed the slope of the land.  斜绘图清晰地显示了地面的坡度。|近义词：oblique, perspective|反义词：orthographic
