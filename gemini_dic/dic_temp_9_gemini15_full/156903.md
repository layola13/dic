pilaster:/ˈpɪlæstər/| n.|壁柱，半柱|A pilaster is a rectangular column, usually built into a wall, projecting slightly from the surface.  It is often used as a decorative element to give the impression of strength and architectural grandeur.  It functions similar to a column but is not freestanding.|The pilasters on the building's facade added a sense of elegance.  这栋建筑立面的壁柱增添了一种优雅感。 The ancient Roman temple featured impressive pilasters supporting the entablature.  这座古罗马神庙以其支撑着檐口的壮观壁柱为特色。|column, pillar|freestanding column
