couplings:/ˈkʌplɪŋz/| n.|联轴器；联接；耦合|A coupling is a device that connects two shafts together, allowing them to rotate as one.  It can also refer to the act of connecting two things, or the state of being connected. |例句：The engineer inspected the couplings on the train's wheels. 工程师检查了火车车轮上的联轴器。

例句：The successful coupling of the two companies created a powerful synergy. 两家公司的成功合并产生了强大的协同效应。


近义词：connection, joint, linkage, coupling device

反义词：disconnection, separation, disjunction

词性：名词
