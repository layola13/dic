hooping:/ˈhuːpɪŋ/| n. v.|呼啦圈；箍；大声喊叫；(使)成环状 | a circular frame, typically of wood or metal, used in various ways, especially as a toy or in gymnastics; the act of making something into a hoop shape; a loud shout or cry |例句：The children were playing with a hoop. 孩子们正在玩呼啦圈。

She hooped the fabric around the frame. 她把织物箍在框架上。|近义词：circle, ring, band, shout, yell |反义词：quiet, whisper
