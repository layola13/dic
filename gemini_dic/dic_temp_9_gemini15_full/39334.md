conjecturers:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərərz/| n.|推测者，猜测者|Those who make conjectures or hypotheses; people who speculate or guess.  They often form opinions based on incomplete information or evidence.|例句：The conjecturers debated the meaning of the ancient inscription. 推测者们就古代铭文的含义展开了辩论。

例句：The panel of conjecturers offered several competing theories about the cause of the disaster.  专家小组提出了关于灾难原因的几种相互竞争的理论。


近义词：speculators, guessers, theorizers, hypothesizers|反义词：knowers, verifiers, confirmers|名词
