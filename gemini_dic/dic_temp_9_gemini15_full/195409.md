seminar:/ˈsɛmɪnɑːr/| n. |研讨会，讲习班 |A seminar is a small group meeting for discussion and instruction, typically on a specific academic topic.  It often involves presentations by experts and interactive discussions among participants. |例句：The professor led a fascinating seminar on the history of ancient Rome. 教授主持了一个关于古罗马历史的精彩研讨会。

例句：We attended a seminar on effective communication skills. 我们参加了一个关于有效沟通技巧的研讨会。


近义词：workshop, conference, lecture series, colloquium |反义词：N/A |词性:名词
