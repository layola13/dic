primigenius:/praɪˈmɪdʒənɪəs/| adj.|原始的，最初的|Of or relating to the first or earliest stage of something; original; primitive.  The term often refers to the ancestral species from which others are derived. |例句：The primigenius aurochs is considered the ancestor of modern cattle.  原始野牛被认为是现代牛的祖先。

例句：The primigenius form of the language is barely recognizable today.  这种语言的原始形式如今几乎无法辨认。


近义词：original, primordial, primeval, ancestral

反义词：modern, derived, recent, secondary

词性：形容词
