dialoguize:/ˈdaɪəlɒɡaɪz/| vt.|使…对话化；将…改编成对话形式|To convert or adapt something (such as a novel or screenplay) into a dialogue format.  This often involves creating conversational exchanges between characters to represent events or ideas.|例句：The author decided to dialoguize his novel for a stage adaptation. 作者决定将他的小说改编成舞台剧的对话形式。

例句：The professor dialoguized the complex scientific concepts to make them more accessible to students. 教授将复杂的科学概念转化成对话形式，以便学生更容易理解。


近义词：dramatize, adapt, script


反义词：summarize, condense


词性:动词
