ethnographic:/ˌeθnəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 人种学的；民族志的 | Relating to the scientific description of peoples and cultures.  Ethnographic research involves immersing oneself in a culture to understand its beliefs, practices, and social structures. |  Example: The ethnographic study revealed fascinating insights into the tribe's social hierarchy.  这项人种学研究揭示了关于该部落社会等级制度的迷人见解。|近义词：anthropological, cultural, sociocultural | 反义词：N/A
