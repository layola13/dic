nonauthors:/ˈnɒnɔːθərz/| n. |非作者们，未署名作者 |People who have not been credited as authors of a work.  This term is often used in academic contexts to refer to individuals who contributed to a project but are not listed as authors. |Example: The nonauthors of the study played a crucial role in data collection.  非作者们在这个研究中扮演了关键的数据收集角色。|近义词:uncredited contributors, anonymous contributors|反义词:authors, credited writers|名词
