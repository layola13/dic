proofs:/pruːfs/| n. |证据；证明；校样|Evidence or arguments that prove something is true.  A set of pages printed to check for errors before final printing.|例句：The police have enough proofs to convict him. 警方有足够的证据定他的罪。

例句：The author received the proofs of his new book. 作者收到了新书的校样。

近义词：evidence, verification, demonstration, corroboration | 反义词：disproof, refutation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence proofs his innocence. = 证据证明了他的清白。
This document proofs the contract's validity. = 这份文件证明了合同的有效性。


一般过去时:
The lawyer presented proofs that supported his client's claim. = 律师出示了支持其当事人主张的证据。
The experiment proofs the theory's accuracy. = 实验验证了理论的准确性。


现在完成时:
Scientists have proofs that climate change is real. = 科学家已经证明了气候变化是真实存在的。
They have proofs the suspect was at the scene. = 他们已经证明嫌疑犯在现场。


过去完成时:
They had proofs that the crime had been committed. = 他们已有证据证明该罪行已被犯下。
The investigators had proofs the alibi was false. = 调查人员已经证明不在场证明是假的。


将来时:
The upcoming trial will present further proofs. = 即将到来的审判将提供进一步的证据。
We will need more proofs to support our hypothesis. = 我们将需要更多证据来支持我们的假设。


虚拟语气:
If we had proofs of his guilt, we would arrest him. = 如果我们有他犯罪的证据，我们会逮捕他。
If the proofs were stronger, the judge would convict him.  = 如果证据更充分，法官就会判他罪。


被动语态:
His innocence was proofs by the witness's testimony. = 他的清白由证人的证词证明。
The theory was proofs by numerous experiments. = 该理论已被多次实验所证实。


疑问句:
What proofs do you have to support your argument? = 你有什么证据来支持你的论点？
What proofs were presented in court? = 法庭上提交了什么证据？


否定句:
There were no proofs to suggest his involvement. = 没有证据表明他参与其中。
The defense lacked sufficient proofs to win the case. = 防御方缺乏足够的证据来赢得案件。
