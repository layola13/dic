demergers:/dɪˈmɜːrdʒərz/| n. |公司分拆；业务剥离|The process of separating a company into two or more independent entities.  This is often done to improve efficiency, focus on core businesses, or unlock shareholder value.  Demergers are the opposite of mergers. |例句：The company announced a demerger of its retail and manufacturing divisions.  这家公司宣布将其零售和制造部门分拆。

例句：The demerger resulted in two separate, publicly traded companies.  这次分拆导致了两家独立上市公司。


近义词：spin-off, divestiture, separation

反义词：merger, amalgamation, consolidation

词性：名词
