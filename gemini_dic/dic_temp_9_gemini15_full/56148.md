documenters:/ˌdɒkjuˈmɛntərz/| n. |记录者，文献编纂者 |Those who create documents or records.  They compile, organize, and preserve information in various formats. |Example: The documenters meticulously recorded every detail of the experiment.  记录者一丝不苟地记录了实验的每一个细节。 The team of documenters worked tirelessly to archive the historical manuscripts.  文献编纂团队夜以继日地整理历史手稿。|近义词：archivists, recorders, chroniclers |反义词：destroyers, obliterators
