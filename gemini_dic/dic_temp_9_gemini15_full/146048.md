/ˌoʊvərˈdrʌŋk/ | adj. | 醉醺醺的，喝醉了的 | Extremely drunk; intoxicated to a high degree.  | Example: He was overdrunk and unable to drive home safely. 他喝醉了，无法安全开车回家。 |近义词:  very drunk, inebriated, intoxicated, plastered, sloshed | 反义词: sober,  teetotal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is overdrunk and needs help getting home. 他喝醉了，需要帮助回家。
She is overdrunk and can barely stand. 她喝醉了，几乎站不稳。


一般过去时:
He was overdrunk last night and missed his flight. 他昨晚喝醉了，错过了航班。
They were overdrunk at the party and caused a scene. 他们在派对上喝醉了，并制造了一场混乱。

He has been overdrunk several times this month.  这个月他已经喝醉好几次了。

将来时:

被动语态: (This adjective is rarely used in the passive voice.  The state of being overdrunk is active.)
