creeds:/kriːdz/| n.|信条；教义；信念|A creed is a set of beliefs or aims which guide someone's actions.|Example: The company's creed is to provide excellent customer service. 这家公司的信条是提供优质的客户服务。|近义词：doctrine, tenet, dogma, belief|反义词：heresy, unbelief
