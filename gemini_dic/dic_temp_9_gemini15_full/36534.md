collaborationist: /kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃənɪst/ | n. | 勾结者；通敌者 | A person who collaborates with an enemy, especially during wartime.  This term often carries a strong negative connotation, implying betrayal and treason.  | Example:  The collaborationist was executed for aiding the enemy.  这个通敌者因帮助敌人而被处死。|近义词：traitor, quisling, collaborator | 反义词：patriot, loyalist
