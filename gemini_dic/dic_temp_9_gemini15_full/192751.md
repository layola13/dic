一般现在时:
The seafloor supports a diverse ecosystem. 海底支撑着多种多样的生态系统。
Scientists study the seafloor to understand plate tectonics. 科学家们研究海底以了解板块构造。

Divers explored the seafloor near the shipwreck. 潜水员探索了沉船附近的海底。

现在完成时:
Researchers have mapped a large portion of the seafloor. 研究人员已经绘制了很大一部分海底地图。
They have discovered new species living on the seafloor. 他们在海底发现了新的物种。

过去完成时:
