meaningful:/ˈmiːnɪŋfəl/ | adj. | 有意义的，意味深长的 |  Having a purpose or importance; significant.  |  Her work is meaningful to her.  她的工作对她很有意义。 | significant, important, purposeful | meaningless, insignificant, trivial | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This job is meaningful to me. = 这份工作对我来说很有意义。
His words are meaningful and inspiring. = 他说的话很有意义也很鼓舞人心。


一般过去时:
The meeting was meaningful and productive. = 会议很有意义且富有成效。
The experience was meaningful for his personal growth. = 这段经历对他个人成长很有意义。


现在完成时:
My life has become more meaningful since I started volunteering. = 自从我开始做志愿者以来，我的生活变得更有意义。
Her efforts have made a meaningful contribution to society. = 她的努力为社会做出了有意义的贡献。


过去完成时:
I realized that my previous efforts had been meaningful after all. = 我后来才意识到我之前的努力毕竟是有意义的。
They understood that their past sacrifices had been meaningful for the future. = 他们明白他们过去的牺牲对未来是有意义的。


将来时:
This project will be meaningful for the community. = 这个项目对社区将大有裨益。
His future work will be more meaningful than his previous work. = 他未来的工作将比他以前的工作更有意义。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pursue a more meaningful career. = 如果我有更多时间，我会追求更有意义的事业。
I wish my life were more meaningful. = 我希望我的生活更有意义。


被动语态:
The results of the study were found to be meaningful. = 研究结果被认为是有意义的。
His contribution was deemed meaningful by the community. = 他的贡献被社区认为是有意义的。


疑问句:
Is this work meaningful to you? = 这份工作对你来说有意义吗？
Is there anything meaningful you want to share? = 你有什么有意义的事情想分享吗？


否定句:
His life felt meaningless and empty. = 他的生活感觉毫无意义且空虚。
The decision was not meaningful in the long run. = 从长远来看，这个决定毫无意义。
