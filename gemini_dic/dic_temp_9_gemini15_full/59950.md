edible:/ˈɛdəbl/ | adj. | 可食用的，食用的 |  capable of being eaten; fit for human consumption.  | Example:  Many wild berries are edible, but some are poisonous.  许多野生浆果是可食用的，但有些是有毒的。

The mushrooms we found were edible and tasted delicious. 我们找到的蘑菇是可食用的，而且味道鲜美。|近义词：eatable, palatable | 反义词：inedible, poisonous

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The berries are edible. = 这些浆果是可食用的。
This cake is edible, but not very tasty. = 这个蛋糕可以吃，但不太好吃。

一般过去时:
The fruit was edible when we picked it. = 我们采摘的时候，水果是可食用的。
That bread was edible, but a little stale. = 那面包能吃，但有点干硬。

现在完成时:
These apples have been edible for a week.  = 这些苹果已经可以吃一周了。
We have found some edible mushrooms in the forest. = 我们在森林里找到了一些可食用的蘑菇。


过去完成时:
The food had been edible, but it was spoiled after sitting out. = 食物本来是可以吃的，但放在外面后就变质了。
The cake had been edible yesterday, but it's gone bad now. = 蛋糕昨天是可以吃的，但现在坏了。


将来时:
The bread will be edible tomorrow. = 面包明天就可以吃了。
Those fruits will be edible in a few days. = 那些水果几天后就可以吃了。


虚拟语气:
If the food were edible, I would eat it. = 如果食物能吃，我就吃了。
If those berries were edible, we could make a pie. = 如果那些浆果能吃，我们可以做个馅饼。


被动语态:
The food is considered edible. = 这食物被认为是可食用的。
The mushrooms were deemed edible by the expert. = 蘑菇被专家认定为可食用。


疑问句:
Is this mushroom edible? = 这个蘑菇能吃吗？
Are these berries edible? = 这些浆果能吃吗？


否定句:
The meat is not edible. = 这肉不能吃。
That food is inedible because it's spoiled. = 那食物不能吃，因为它变质了。


PHRASE->
edible plants = 可食用植物
edible flowers = 可食用花卉
edible insects = 可食用昆虫
edible seaweed = 可食用海藻
edible fungi = 可食用真菌


词源:
From Middle English *edible, from Old French *edible, from Latin edibilis, from edere "to eat".
