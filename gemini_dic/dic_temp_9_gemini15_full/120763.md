misapplying:/ˌmɪsæˈplaɪɪŋ/| v. |误用；错用 |To use something incorrectly or for the wrong purpose.  |例句：He was accused of misapplying company funds. 他被指控挪用公司资金。 |例句：The technician misapplied the adhesive, resulting in a weak bond. 技术人员粘合剂使用错误，导致粘合力弱。|近义词：misusing, abusing, mishandling |反义词：applying correctly, using properly
