refractories:/rɪˈfræktəri/| n. |耐火材料，难熔物质|Materials that are resistant to high temperatures and chemical attack, typically used in furnaces and kilns.  Examples include bricks, ceramics, and certain metals.|The furnace lining is made of high-quality refractories to withstand the intense heat.|近义词：firebricks, heat-resistant materials|反义词：fusible materials
