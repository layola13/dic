interreflect:/ˌɪntərɪˈflɛkt/ | vt. |相互反射；互相影响 |To reflect repeatedly from one surface to another.  This often refers to light, sound, or even ideas bouncing back and forth. |例句：The mirrors in the room interreflected the light, creating a dazzling effect.  房间里的镜子相互反射光线，创造出令人眼花缭乱的效果。

The polished surfaces of the building interreflected the sunlight, illuminating the surrounding area. 建筑物的抛光表面反射阳光，照亮了周围区域。


近义词：reflect, rebound, reverberate | 反义词：absorb, dissipate | 词性：动词
