plugging-up: /ˈplʌɡɪŋ ʌp/ | v. |堵塞；阻塞；填满|To obstruct or fill a hole, pipe, or other opening.  This usually implies a gradual filling or blocking. |例句：The drain is plugging-up with hair. = 下水道被头发堵住了。

例句：The heavy rain is plugging-up the streets with mud. = 大雨把街道堵满了泥浆。


近义词：blocking, clogging, obstructing, filling, stopping up

反义词：unclogging, clearing, opening


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pipes are plugging up slowly. = 水管正在慢慢堵塞。
He is plugging up the holes in the wall. = 他正在堵墙上的洞。
