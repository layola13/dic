mixableness: /ˌmɪksəˈbɪlnəs/ | n. | 可混合性，可混合度 | The ability or capacity of something to be mixed or combined with other things.  It refers to the ease and effectiveness with which different substances or elements can be blended together. This term is often used in contexts involving materials science, chemistry, and cooking. | Example: The mixableness of the paint determined its suitability for the project.  油漆的可混合性决定了它是否适合这个项目。 The high mixableness of the ingredients made the cake batter easy to prepare.  配料的高度可混合性使得蛋糕糊很容易准备。 | 近义词: compatibility, blendability | 反义词: incompatibility, immiscibility
