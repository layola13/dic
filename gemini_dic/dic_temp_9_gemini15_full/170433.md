proximations:/ˌprɒksɪˈmeɪʃənz/ | n. | 近似值，近似法 | Approximations are values that are close to the true value but not exactly the same.  They are often used when an exact value is difficult or impossible to obtain. |例句：The approximations used in the calculation were accurate enough for practical purposes. 计算中使用的近似值对于实际用途来说足够精确。

The scientist's approximations led to a groundbreaking discovery. 科学家的近似计算导致了一项突破性发现。|近义词：estimates, estimations, approximations, calculations |反义词：exact values, precise measurements
