daylight:/ˈdeɪlaɪt/| n.|日光，白昼|The period of time between sunrise and sunset, when the sun is above the horizon. It refers to the natural light of the day.|例句：We went for a walk in the daylight. 我们在日光下散步。|例句：The daylight hours are shorter in winter. 冬天白昼时间较短。|近义词：sunlight, daytime|反义词：darkness, night


USAGE->

There is enough daylight to finish the work. = 白天有足够的光线完成工作。
