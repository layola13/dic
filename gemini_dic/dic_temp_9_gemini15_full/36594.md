collative:/ˈkɒlətɪv/| adj.|  校勘的；对照的；收集的 | Relating to the collation or comparison of texts or other materials to establish their authenticity or correct errors.  The process of comparing different versions of a text to identify variations and create a definitive version. |例句：The collative work involved comparing dozens of manuscripts. 校勘工作涉及比较数十份手稿。


例句：The scholar spent years on the collative edition of the ancient text.  这位学者花了数年时间校勘古代文本。


近义词：comparative, comparative,  confrontative


反义词：disparate, uncompared


词性:形容词
