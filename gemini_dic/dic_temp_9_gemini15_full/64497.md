erodes:/ɪˈroʊdz/ | vt. |侵蚀，腐蚀，削弱 |To gradually destroy something by chemical or physical processes, or to weaken something over time.|例句：The wind and rain have eroded the stone carvings. 风雨侵蚀了石雕。

The constant criticism eroded her confidence.  持续的批评削弱了她的信心。|近义词：corrode, wear away, undermine |反义词：build up, strengthen, enhance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river erodes the banks slowly. = 河水缓慢地侵蚀着河岸。
Acid rain erodes the statues in the park. = 酸雨腐蚀着公园里的雕像。

一般过去时:
The storm eroded the coastline. = 暴风雨侵蚀了海岸线。
Years of neglect eroded the building's structure. = 多年的忽视破坏了建筑物的结构。

现在完成时:
The constant pressure has eroded his patience. = 持续的压力已经磨损了他的耐心。
Years of sun and rain have eroded the paintwork. = 多年的阳光和雨水已经侵蚀了油漆。

过去完成时:
The foundations had eroded before the building collapsed. = 建筑物倒塌之前，地基就已经被侵蚀了。
The prolonged drought had eroded the topsoil. = 长期的干旱已经侵蚀了表层土壤。

将来时:
The sea will erode the cliffs over time. = 海水会随着时间的推移侵蚀悬崖。
Continued pollution will erode the quality of the water. = 持续的污染会降低水质。

虚拟语气:
If the river eroded any further, the bridge would collapse. = 如果河水进一步侵蚀，桥就会倒塌。
I wish the harsh weather hadn't eroded the paint so badly. = 我希望恶劣的天气没有那么严重地侵蚀油漆。

被动语态:
The coastline is being eroded by the sea. = 海岸线正在被海水侵蚀。
The building's foundations were eroded by the flood. = 建筑的地基被洪水侵蚀了。

疑问句:

否定句:
The river doesn't erode the rocks very quickly. = 河水并没有很快地侵蚀岩石。
The protective coating prevents the metal from eroding. = 保护涂层防止金属腐蚀。
