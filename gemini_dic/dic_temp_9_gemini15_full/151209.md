pardon:/ˈpɑːdn/|/ˈpɑːrdən/| n./vt.|原谅；宽恕；赦免|to excuse (someone) for a fault or offense; to forgive|例句：I beg your pardon. 我ขอโทษครับ/ค่ะ。(泰国语)  I beg your pardon, I didn't hear you. 对不起，我没听到你说什么。|近义词：forgive, excuse, remit|反义词：condemn, punish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I pardon your rudeness. = 我原谅你的粗鲁。
He pardons his friend's mistakes. = 他原谅朋友的错误。


一般过去时:
She pardoned him for his transgression. = 她原谅了他的过错。
The judge pardoned the criminal. = 法官赦免了罪犯。


现在完成时:
I have pardoned him many times. = 我已经原谅他很多次了。
She has pardoned his offenses in the past. = 她过去已经原谅过他的罪行。


过去完成时:
The king had pardoned the rebels before the war ended. = 国王在战争结束前赦免了叛乱者。
They had pardoned the thief before he confessed. = 他们在小偷坦白之前就赦免了他。


将来时:
The president will pardon the convicted man. = 总统将赦免这个被定罪的人。
I will pardon you this time, but don't do it again. = 这次我原谅你，但不要再犯了。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would pardon him. = 如果我是你，我会原谅他。
I wish I had pardoned her sooner. = 我希望我早点原谅她。


被动语态:
His crimes were pardoned by the emperor. = 他的罪行被皇帝赦免了。
The prisoner was pardoned after serving ten years. = 那名囚犯服刑十年后被赦免了。


疑问句:
Will you pardon me for interrupting? = 你能原谅我打断你吗？
Do you pardon my ignorance? = 你能原谅我的无知吗？


否定句:
I cannot pardon such behavior. = 我不能原谅这种行为。
The court will not pardon him. = 法院不会赦免他。


PHRASE->
beg your pardon = 请原谅
pardon me = 对不起
I beg your pardon = 对不起(更正式)
ask for pardon = 请求原谅
grant pardon = 赦免


词源:
From Old French *pardoner, from Vulgar Latin *perdonare, from Latin per- ("through," "thoroughly") + donare ("to give").  The sense development is from "to give completely" to "to forgive".
