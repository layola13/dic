/piːdʒiː/| n. |页，页码|A page is one side of a sheet of paper in a book, magazine, or document.  |例句：This book has 300 pages. 这本书有300页。 |例句：Turn to page 10. 翻到第10页。|近义词：leaf, sheet|反义词：volume (as a whole)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The book has many pages. = 这本书有很多页。
This page contains important information. = 这页包含重要信息。


一般过去时:
The printer printed 100 pages. = 打印机打印了100页。
I read five pages last night. = 我昨晚读了五页。


现在完成时:
I have read all the pages. = 我已经读完了所有页。
She has printed several pages already. = 她已经打印了几页了。


过去完成时:
I had already read that page before you asked. = 在你问之前我已经读过那一页了。
They had finished all the pages before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前完成了所有页面。


将来时:
I will print ten pages tomorrow. = 我明天将打印十页。
We will read the next page soon. = 我们很快就会读下一页。


虚拟语气:
If this page were clearer, I would understand it better. = 如果这一页更清晰，我会更容易理解。
I wish this page were less confusing. = 我希望这一页不要那么令人困惑。


被动语态:
The page was printed yesterday. = 这一页是昨天打印的。
The pages are being bound into a book. = 这些页面正在被装订成一本书。


疑问句:
How many pages are there in this book? = 这本书有多少页？
Which page are we on? = 我们现在读到哪一页了？


否定句:
This book doesn't have many pages. = 这本书没有很多页。
That page is not relevant to our discussion. = 那页与我们的讨论无关。
