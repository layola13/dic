proportioning:/prəˈpɔːʃənɪŋ/| v.|比例分配；使成比例；调配|The act of adjusting or determining the correct proportions of different ingredients or components in a mixture or system.  It involves carefully measuring and combining elements to achieve a desired balance or outcome.|例句：The baker carefully proportioning the flour, sugar, and eggs for the cake. 面包师仔细地按比例分配面粉、糖和鸡蛋来做蛋糕。

例句：The architect is proportioning the windows and doors to maintain visual harmony. 建筑师正在调整窗户和门窗的比例以保持视觉和谐。


近义词：balancing, adjusting, regulating, distributing


反义词：disproportioning, misproportioning


词性：动词
