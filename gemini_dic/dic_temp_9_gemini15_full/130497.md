英文解析：The plural noun "nonacceptances" refers to instances or cases of something not being accepted, approved, or acknowledged.  It often implies a formal rejection, particularly in contexts involving business transactions, legal matters, or official processes.


例句：

The bank reported a high volume of nonacceptances of checks due to insufficient funds. = 由于资金不足，银行报告了大量的支票拒付。

Several nonacceptances of the proposed amendments delayed the legislative process. = 对拟议修正案的几次拒收延误了立法进程。
