nonreflective: /ˌnɒn.rɪˈflɛk.tɪv/ | adj. | 不反光的；无反射的 | Not reflecting light or sound.  Something that is nonreflective does not reflect light or sound back towards its source. |

例句：

The nonreflective surface of the monitor reduced eye strain.  = 监视器的无反光表面减少了眼睛疲劳。

The artist used a nonreflective black paint for the background. = 艺术家使用无反光的黑色颜料作为背景。


近义词： matte, dull, flat


反义词： reflective, glossy, shiny


词性：形容词


词源：

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of"
* **reflective**:  adjective derived from "reflect," meaning to throw back light, heat, sound, etc.


USAGE->

(Nonreflective is an adjective, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like "What" in the example.  The usage examples will show how it's used in different sentence structures.)

肯定句:

The screen has a nonreflective coating. = 屏幕具有不反光的涂层。

This material is highly nonreflective. =  这种材料高度不反光。


否定句:

The surface is not nonreflective; it's quite glossy. = 表面不是不反光的；它相当有光泽。

That paint isn't particularly nonreflective. = 那涂料不是特别不反光。


疑问句:

Is the material nonreflective? =  这种材料不反光吗？

Are these sunglasses nonreflective? = 这些太阳镜不反光吗？
