reannotate:/ˌriːˈænəteɪt/| vt.|重新注释，重新标注|To annotate something again, often with corrections or additions.  This typically involves adding or changing existing annotations.|例句：The researchers had to reannotate the data after discovering errors in the initial labeling. 研究人员在发现初始标记错误后不得不重新标注数据。|例句：We need to reannotate the images to improve the accuracy of the model. 我们需要重新标注图像以提高模型的准确性。|近义词：relabel, revise, amend|反义词：remove annotations, delete annotations|动词
