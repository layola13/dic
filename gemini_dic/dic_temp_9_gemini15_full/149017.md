/ˈaʊəl/ | n. | 鸮; 猫头鹰 | A bird of prey with large forward-facing eyes, a hooked beak, and typically a nocturnal habit.  Owls are known for their silent flight and excellent hearing. |  The owl hooted in the night.  这只猫头鹰在夜里发出叫声。|  nightjar, hawk |  sparrow, robin | 名词
