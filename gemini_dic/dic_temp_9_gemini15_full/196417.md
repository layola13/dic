serpentinic: /ˌsɜːrpənˈtɪnɪk/| adj. |蜿蜒的；蛇形的；狡猾的；诡计多端的|  relating to or resembling a serpent; winding or twisting like a serpent.  The word suggests a sinuous, curving, and potentially deceptive quality. |

例句：
The serpentinic path wound its way through the dense forest. = 蜿蜒的小路蜿蜒穿过茂密的森林。

The river followed a serpentinic course through the valley. = 河流蜿蜒穿过山谷。


近义词：winding, meandering, tortuous, sinuous, devious, tricky

反义词：straight, direct, straightforward, honest


词源:
From serpentine +‎ -ic.  Serpentine itself comes from the Latin *serpentīnus, from serpens "serpent".  The suffix "-ic" forms adjectives.  Thus, the word's origin clearly links it to the image of a snake and its characteristic movement.
