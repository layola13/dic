megametre:/ˈmɛɡəmˌiːtər/ | n. |兆米，百万米 | A megametre (Mm) is a unit of length equal to one million metres (10⁶ m).  It is a multiple of the base unit of length in the International System of Units (SI).  | The distance between two cities might be measured in megametres.  | 近义词：million metres | 反义词：millimetre, centimetre, metre
