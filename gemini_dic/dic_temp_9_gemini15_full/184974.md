retelegraph:/ˌriːˈtɛləɡræf/| vt. | 再电报；重发电报 | To send a telegram again; to retransmit a telegram.  |例句：They had to retelegraph the message because the first one was unclear. 他们不得不重发电报，因为第一份电报不清楚。

例句：The urgent message was retelegraphed immediately after the error was detected.  紧急信息在错误被发现后立即被重发。


近义词：resend, retransmit | 反义词：receive,  first send


词性：动词
