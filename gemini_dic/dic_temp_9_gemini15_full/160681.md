portion:/ˈpɔːrʃən/| n. |一部分，份额 | a part or share of a whole | The lion's portion of the profits went to the CEO.  狮子的那份利润都给了CEO。| a section, part, share | whole, all |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He receives a large portion of the inheritance. 他继承了一大笔遗产。
A significant portion of the budget is allocated to research. 预算中很大一部分用于研究。


一般过去时:
She ate a small portion of the cake. 她吃了一小块蛋糕。
A large portion of the city was destroyed by the earthquake. 地震摧毁了该市很大一部分地区。


现在完成时:
We have already consumed a large portion of the food. 我们已经吃掉了大部分食物。
A considerable portion of the project has been completed. 项目的相当一部分已经完成。


过去完成时:
They had already finished a large portion of their work before the deadline. 他们在截止日期之前已经完成了大部分工作。
By the time I arrived, a significant portion of the meeting had already taken place. 我到达时，会议已经进行了一大部分。


将来时:
I will only eat a small portion of the pizza. 我只吃一小块披萨。
A substantial portion of the funds will be used for infrastructure improvements. 大部分资金将用于基础设施建设。


虚拟语气:
If I had a larger portion, I would be satisfied. 如果我分量更大一些，我会满意。
I wish I had received a larger portion of the inheritance. 我希望我继承到更多的遗产。


被动语态:
A small portion of the land was allocated for the park. 一小部分土地被分配给了公园。
A significant portion of the profits will be distributed among the shareholders.  大部分利润将分配给股东。


疑问句:
What portion of the pie did you eat? 你吃了多少馅饼？
What portion of the budget is allocated to marketing? 预算的多少比例分配给了市场营销？


否定句:
He didn't eat a large portion of the meal. 他没有吃很多饭。
I didn't receive a significant portion of the credit for the project. 我在这个项目中没有得到很大的功劳。


PHRASE->
a large portion of  大部分
a small portion of  一小部分
a significant portion of  相当一部分
a considerable portion of 相当大的一部分
