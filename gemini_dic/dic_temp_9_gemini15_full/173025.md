quarrelingly:/ˈkwɒrəlɪŋli/| adv.|争吵地，好斗地|In a manner characterized by quarreling or argumentativeness.  It describes an action or behavior done in a contentious or disputatious way.|例句：They spoke quarrelingly, their voices rising with each accusation.  他们争吵着说话，每一声指责都提高了音量。


例句：He glared at me quarrelingly before storming off. 他怒视着我，然后怒气冲冲地走了。


近义词：contentiously, argumentatively, belligerently, aggressively


反义词：peacefully, amicably, harmoniously


词性：副词

词源:
The word "quarrelingly" is derived from the word "quarrel," which comes from the Old French word "querelle," meaning "complaint, dispute."  The suffix "-ingly" is an adverbial suffix, indicating manner or way.  Therefore, "quarrelingly" literally means "in a manner of quarreling."
