prosecutes:/ˈprɒsɪkjuːts/ |/ˈprɑːsɪkjuːts/| v. |起诉；检举；进行；继续 |To institute legal proceedings against (a person or organization) for a criminal offense.  |The district attorney prosecutes criminals in court. |地区检察官在法庭上起诉罪犯。|indict, charge, sue|acquit, dismiss|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government prosecutes those who break the law. = 政府起诉违法者。
The company prosecutes its business aggressively. = 这家公司积极地开展业务。

一般过去时:
The police prosecuted the case diligently. = 警方认真地调查了此案。
They prosecuted him for tax evasion. = 他们以逃税罪起诉他。

现在完成时:
The authorities have prosecuted several high-profile cases this year. = 今年当局已起诉了几起引人注目的案件。
They have prosecuted this project with great determination. = 他们以极大的决心推进这个项目。

过去完成时:
The lawyer had prosecuted many similar cases before. = 这位律师以前处理过许多类似的案件。
Before the trial, they had prosecuted their argument vigorously. = 在审判之前，他们曾大力辩论。

将来时:
The prosecutor will prosecute the defendant next week. = 检察官下周将起诉被告。
They will prosecute their research further. = 他们将进一步推进他们的研究。

虚拟语气:
If they had sufficient evidence, they would prosecute. = 如果他们有足够的证据，他们就会起诉。
I wish they would prosecute the case more quickly. = 我希望他们能更快地审理此案。

被动语态:
The case was prosecuted by the state attorney. = 此案由州检察官起诉。
The project is being prosecuted efficiently. = 该项目正在高效地推进。

疑问句:
Will the police prosecute this crime? = 警方会起诉这起犯罪吗？
Is the company prosecuting its expansion plans? = 这家公司正在推进它的扩张计划吗？

否定句:
The police did not prosecute the case due to lack of evidence. = 由于缺乏证据，警方没有起诉此案。
The company is not prosecuting its claim against the supplier. = 公司没有对供应商提起诉讼。
