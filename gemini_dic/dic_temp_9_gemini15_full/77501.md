gazetting:/ˈɡæzɪtɪŋ/| v.| 公布；登录；列入官方公告|The act of officially recording or publishing something, typically in a gazette or official publication.  It often refers to the process of including something in a list, register, or official record.|例句：The new law was gazetted yesterday. = 新法昨天已正式公布。|例句：The names of the new appointees were gazetted in the government gazette. = 新任命的名单已在政府公报上公布。|近义词：publishing, announcing, proclaiming, registering|反义词：suppressing, concealing, withholding
