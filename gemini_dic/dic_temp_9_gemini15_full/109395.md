lentils:/ˈlɛntlz/| n. |扁豆，小扁豆 |Lentils are small, flat, lens-shaped seeds that are a type of legume. They are a popular food source, high in protein and fiber.  They are available in various colors, including brown, green, red, and yellow. |例句：I added lentils to the soup to make it more filling.我把扁豆加到汤里让它更饱腹。

例句：Lentils are a good source of plant-based protein. 扁豆是植物性蛋白质的良好来源。|近义词：pulses, legumes |反义词:无
