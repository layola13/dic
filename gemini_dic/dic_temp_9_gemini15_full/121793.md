misrecording:/ˌmɪsrɪˈkɔːrdɪŋ/ | n. | 误录，错误记录 | The act of recording something incorrectly; an instance of this.  It refers to an inaccurate or flawed recording of audio, video, or data. | Example: The misrecording of the witness's testimony led to a mistrial.  误录证人的证词导致审判无效。  The misrecording on the tape made it impossible to understand the conversation. 磁带上的误录使得无法理解谈话内容。 | 近义词：error, mistake, inaccuracy, misrepresentation | 反义词：accurate recording, correct transcription | 名词
