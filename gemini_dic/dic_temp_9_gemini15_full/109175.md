legitimising: /ləˈdʒɪtɪmaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使合法化；使正当化 | To make something seem right or acceptable.  | The government is trying to legitimise its actions. 政府试图使其行为合法化。 | justify, validate, authorize | illegitimatize, invalidate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are legitimising their claim to the land. = 他们正在使他们对这块土地的索赔合法化。
The company is legitimising its practices by obtaining certifications. = 公司通过获得认证来使其做法合法化。

一般过去时:
The court legitimised the marriage. = 法院使这桩婚姻合法化。
He legitimised his actions by citing precedent. = 他通过引用先例使他的行为合法化。

现在完成时:
They have legitimised their position through years of hard work. = 他们通过多年的努力使自己的地位合法化。
The reforms have legitimised the government's rule. = 改革使政府的统治合法化。

过去完成时:
The dictator had legitimised his power through propaganda. = 独裁者通过宣传使他的权力合法化。
The treaty had legitimised the country's independence. = 条约使该国的独立合法化。

将来时:
The new law will legitimise same-sex marriage. = 新法律将使同性婚姻合法化。
The upcoming election will legitimise the new government. = 即将举行的选举将使新政府合法化。

虚拟语气:
If they had evidence, they could legitimise their claim. = 如果他们有证据，他们就能使他们的索赔合法化。
I wish the government would legitimise the use of medical cannabis. = 我希望政府能使医用大麻合法化。

被动语态:
His actions were legitimised by the court. = 他的行为得到了法院的合法化。
The new policy is being legitimised through public debate. = 新政策正在通过公开辩论而合法化。

疑问句:
How can we legitimise our actions in the eyes of the public? = 我们如何才能在公众眼中使我们的行为合法化？
Does this evidence legitimise their claims? = 这证据能使他们的说法合法化吗？

否定句:
The regime failed to legitimise its rule. = 该政权未能使它的统治合法化。
They could not legitimise their actions after the scandal. = 丑闻发生后，他们无法使他们的行为合法化。


PHRASE->
legitimise power = 使权力合法化
legitimise actions = 使行为合法化
legitimise a claim = 使索赔合法化
legitimise a regime = 使政权合法化
legitimise the use of = 使...的使用合法化

词源:  From Latin *legitimare ("to declare legitimate"), from lex, legis ("law") + -timare (suffix forming verbs from nouns).
