glareous: /ˈɡlɛəriəs/ | adj. |耀眼的，炫目的，华丽的 |  Extremely bright; dazzling.  The word suggests an intense, sometimes overwhelming brightness, often with a slightly unpleasant or harsh connotation. It can also describe something ostentatiously showy or flamboyant. |例句：The glareous sunlight reflected off the snow, making it difficult to see. 耀眼的阳光反射在雪地上，让人难以看清。 The singer's glareous costume was a distraction from her mediocre performance. 歌手的炫目服装分散了人们对她平庸表演的注意力。|近义词：dazzling, blinding, radiant, flamboyant, showy |反义词：dull, dim, subdued, muted
