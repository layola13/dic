chlorinate:/ˈklɔːrɪneɪt/|/ˈklɔːrɪneɪt/| vt.|氯化；用氯处理|To treat (a substance) with chlorine, especially to purify water or to bleach.  The process involves adding chlorine to a substance to kill bacteria, viruses, or other harmful microorganisms. It is also used in industrial processes to bleach fabrics or paper. |例句：The city chlorinates its water supply to make it safe to drink.  这个城市对自来水进行氯化处理，以确保饮用安全。

The swimming pool water is chlorinated regularly to prevent the growth of bacteria. 游泳池水定期进行氯化处理，以防止细菌滋生。


近义词：disinfect, sterilize|反义词：dechlorinate
