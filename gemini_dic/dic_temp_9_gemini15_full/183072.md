repollings:/ˌriːˈpəʊlɪŋz/| n. |再次投票；反复投票|The act of conducting multiple polls on the same issue.  It often implies a lack of decisiveness or a need for further clarification.  Repollings are common in close elections or when there are concerns about irregularities in the initial voting process.|Example: The repollings in the disputed constituency dragged on for weeks.  再次投票在有争议的选区持续了好几个星期。The numerous repollings failed to resolve the contentious issue.  多次重新投票未能解决有争议的问题。|近义词：repeated polls, revotes, further votes|反义词：single poll, final vote|名词
