disengages:/ˌdɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/ | vt. |脱离；解开；使脱离；使脱离接触|To separate or disconnect (something) from something else; to become free or detached.|The train disengaged from the engine. 火车与机车脱钩了。|He disengaged himself from the argument. 他退出了争论。|近义词：detach, disconnect, separate, release |反义词：engage, connect, attach


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disengages the gears smoothly. 他平稳地挂空挡。
The mechanism disengages automatically. 机制会自动脱离。


一般过去时:
She disengaged the safety catch. 她解开了保险装置。
The soldiers disengaged from the enemy. 士兵们与敌人脱离接触。


现在完成时:
The company has disengaged itself from the project. 公司已脱离该项目。
He has disengaged from the stressful situation. 他已摆脱了压力大的局面。


过去完成时:
The pilot had disengaged the autopilot before landing. 飞行员在降落前已关闭了自动驾驶仪。
They had disengaged the coupling before moving the machinery. 他们在移动机器之前已经解开了联轴器。


将来时:
The engineer will disengage the power supply. 工程师将切断电源。
The team will disengage from the negotiation. 团队将退出谈判。


虚拟语气:
If he disengaged the clutch, the car would stop. 如果他踩下离合器，车就会停下来。
I wish I could disengage myself from this problem. 我真希望我能摆脱这个问题。


被动语态:
The coupling was disengaged by the operator. 联轴器由操作员解开。
The safety mechanism is disengaged when the button is pressed. 按下按钮时，安全机制会自动脱离。


疑问句:
Did they disengage the brakes? 他们是否解开了刹车？
Has he disengaged the machine yet? 他是否已经使机器脱离运转？


否定句:
The machine didn't disengage properly. 机器没有正确地脱离。
He couldn't disengage himself from his work. 他无法从工作中脱身。
