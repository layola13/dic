nest-egg:/ˈnestˌɛɡ/| n.|储蓄；养老金；积蓄|A sum of money or other assets saved for the future, especially for retirement.  It refers to the way birds save food and materials to build a nest for their young, implying a careful saving for future use.|例句：She’s been diligently building a nest-egg for her retirement. 她一直在努力为退休攒钱。|例句：He used his nest-egg to start his own business. 他用自己的积蓄创办了自己的企业。|近义词：savings, reserve, fund|反义词：debt, liability


USAGE->

He has a substantial nest-egg. = 他有一笔可观的积蓄。
She is building a nest-egg for her children's education. = 她正在为孩子的教育攒钱。
