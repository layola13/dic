overintellectualization: /ˌoʊvərɪntɛləktʃuəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度思考；过度智力化 | The act of overthinking or analyzing something to an excessive degree, often to the point of hindering understanding or action.  It involves interpreting situations or issues with unnecessary complexity or sophistication. | Example: His overintellectualization of the simple instructions led to confusion and delays.  他的过度思考简单的指令导致了混乱和延误。| 近义词: overanalysis, overthinking, overcomplication | 反义词: simplification, simplification, straightforwardness |  名词
