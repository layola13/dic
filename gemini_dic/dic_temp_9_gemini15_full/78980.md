giro:/ˈdʒɪroʊ/ | n. | 汇款；票据；循环；旋转 | A giro is a system of transferring money between bank accounts, often used in international transactions. It can also refer to a type of postal check or a circular movement. | Example:  He sent the money via giro. 他通过汇款方式汇款。 |近义词：transfer, remittance, check |反义词：N/A
