carolers:/ˈkærələrz/| n.|颂歌演唱者；报佳音者 |People who sing carols, especially at Christmas.  |例句：The carolers sang beautiful Christmas carols in the town square. 颂歌演唱者在城镇广场唱着优美的圣诞颂歌。
例句：We heard carolers singing outside our window on Christmas Eve. 圣诞夜我们听到窗外有报佳音的人在唱歌。|近义词：singers, carol singers, choir |反义词：None
