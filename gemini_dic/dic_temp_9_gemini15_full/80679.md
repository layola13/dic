It implies a greedy or gluttonous approach, characterized by rapidity and lack of restraint.  |  The starving man gorgedly devoured the entire loaf of bread.  饥饿的人狼吞虎咽地吃掉了整条面包。 He gorgedly spent his inheritance on luxury goods. 他挥霍无度地把遗产花在了奢侈品上。 | gluttonously, ravenously, voraciously, lavishly | sparingly, frugally, modestly | 副词
