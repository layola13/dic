self-responsibilities:/ˌsɛlf rɪˌspɒnsəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 自我责任 | Self-responsibilities refer to the obligations and duties an individual has towards themselves and their own well-being, encompassing physical, mental, and emotional aspects.  These responsibilities often include taking care of one's health, managing personal finances, and making responsible choices. | Example:  Taking care of your physical and mental health is a crucial self-responsibility.  照顾你的身心健康是一项至关重要的自我责任。  | 近义词: personal responsibilities, self-care duties, personal obligations | 反义词: irresponsibility, negligence |  名词
