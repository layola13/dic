sad:/sæd/ | adj. | 悲伤的，难过的 | Feeling or showing sorrow; unhappy.  |例句：She felt sad after her friend moved away. 她朋友搬走后感到很难过。|例句：The sad news made everyone cry.  这个悲伤的消息让每个人都哭了。|近义词：unhappy, sorrowful, miserable, dejected |反义词：happy, joyful, cheerful, merry


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is sad because he lost his job. = 他因为失业而感到悲伤。
She is always sad when it rains. = 下雨的时候她总是很悲伤。

一般过去时:
I was sad when my dog died. = 我的狗死了，我很伤心。
They were sad to hear the bad news. = 他们听到这个坏消息感到很难过。

现在完成时:
I have been sad all week. = 我整个星期都很悲伤。
She has felt sad ever since her grandmother passed away. = 从她祖母去世后，她就一直感到悲伤。

过去完成时:
I had been sad for days before I finally told someone. = 在我最终告诉别人之前，我已经悲伤了好几天了。
They had been sad about the situation for months. = 几个月来，他们一直为这种情况感到悲伤。

将来时:
I will be sad to see you go. = 我将很难过看到你离开。
She will probably be sad if she doesn't get the job. = 如果她没有得到这份工作，她可能会很难过。

虚拟语气:
I wish I weren't so sad. = 我希望我不那么悲伤。
If I had known, I wouldn't have been so sad. = 如果我知道的话，我就不会那么悲伤了。

被动语态:  (Sad is rarely used in the passive voice.  It describes a state of being, not an action performed upon someone.)  N/A

疑问句:
Are you sad? = 你难过吗？
Is he sad about the test results? = 他对考试结果难过吗？

否定句:
I am not sad. = 我不难过。
She wasn't sad at all. = 她一点也不难过。
