pre-booked:/ˌpriːˈbʊkt/| adj.| 预定的，预订好的 | Already booked in advance.  Pre-booked implies that a reservation or booking has been made earlier. |例句：I have a pre-booked appointment with the doctor at 3 pm.  我下午三点和医生有预约。

例句：We have pre-booked our flights and accommodation for the trip to Italy. 我们已经预订了去意大利旅行的机票和住宿。


近义词：reserved, booked, prearranged, scheduled


反义词：unbooked, unscheduled, impromptu


词性：形容词
