eclecticism:/ɪˌklɛktɪsɪzəm/ | n. |兼容并蓄；折衷主义；采众家之长 |The practice of deriving ideas, style, or taste from a broad and diverse range of sources.  Eclecticism in art, for example, involves the combination of different styles and techniques. | Her interior design showed a delightful eclecticism, mixing antique furniture with modern art.  她的室内设计展现了令人愉悦的兼容并蓄，将古董家具与现代艺术融为一体。| syn: syncretism, fusion,  amalgamation | ant:  purity, uniformity, homogeneity
