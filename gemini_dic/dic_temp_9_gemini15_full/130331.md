non-threatening: /ˌnɒnθˈretnɪŋ/ | adj. | 不具威胁性的，无害的 | Not causing fear or intimidation; harmless.  |  例句：The dog seemed non-threatening, wagging its tail happily. 这只狗看起来很温顺，高兴地摇着尾巴。

例句：The teacher created a non-threatening classroom environment to encourage participation. 老师创造了一个轻松的课堂环境来鼓励参与。


近义词：harmless, innocuous, safe, benign


反义词：threatening, menacing, intimidating, dangerous


词性：形容词
