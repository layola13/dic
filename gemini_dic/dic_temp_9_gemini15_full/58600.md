dullards:/ˈdʌlərdz/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A dullard is a stupid or unintelligent person.  The term implies a lack of wit, understanding, or mental sharpness.|例句：He's such a dullard; he can't even follow simple instructions. 他真是个笨蛋，连简单的指示都听不懂。


例句：They considered him a dullard for not understanding the obvious solution. 他们认为他不理解显而易见的解决方案，是个笨蛋。


近义词：fool, idiot, imbecile, simpleton


反义词：genius, intellectual, mastermind, bright spark


词性:名词
