reissuing:/ˌriːˈɪʃuːɪŋ/| v.|重新发行，再版|The act of issuing something again, typically after a period of time or due to some error or demand.|例句：The company is reissuing the popular novel with a new cover. 公司正在重新发行这本畅销小说，并配上了新的封面。例句：Due to printing errors, the bank is reissuing all the affected credit cards. 由于印刷错误，银行正在重新发行所有受影响的信用卡。|近义词：republishing, reprinting, re-releasing|反义词：withdrawing, canceling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reissuing the album next month. = 他们下个月将重新发行这张专辑。
The company reissues faulty products regularly. = 公司定期重新发行有缺陷的产品。

一般过去时:
The government reissued the travel advisory. = 政府重新发布了旅行建议。
They reissued the bonds with a higher interest rate. = 他们以更高的利率重新发行了债券。

现在完成时:
The bank has reissued my debit card. = 银行已经重新发行了我的借记卡。
They have reissued the statement to clarify the information. = 他们已经重新发布了声明以澄清信息。

过去完成时:
The publisher had reissued the book before the author's death. = 出版商在作者去世前已经重新发行了这本书。
Before the scandal, the company had reissued the product several times. = 在丑闻爆发之前，该公司已经多次重新发行该产品。

将来时:
The museum will reissue the catalogue next year. = 博物馆明年将重新发行目录。
They will reissue the software with bug fixes. = 他们将重新发行带有错误修复程序的软件。

虚拟语气:
If they reissued the tickets, I would buy them. = 如果他们重新发行门票，我会买的。
I wish they had reissued the limited edition. = 我希望他们能重新发行限量版。

被动语态:
The album is being reissued with bonus tracks. = 这张专辑正在重新发行，并附带额外曲目。
The faulty products were reissued after repairs. = 在维修后，有缺陷的产品被重新发行。

疑问句:
Will the tickets be reissued? = 门票会重新发行吗？
Are they reissuing the book in paperback? = 他们会以平装本的形式重新发行这本书吗？

否定句:
The company did not reissue the defective product. = 公司没有重新发行有缺陷的产品。
They won't reissue the album this year. = 他们今年不会重新发行这张专辑。
