puberty:/ˈpjuːbərti/ | n. |青春期，发育期 |The period of life in which sexual maturity is reached.  This is characterized by rapid physical growth and development, including the appearance of secondary sexual characteristics and the onset of reproductive capability. |例句：She went through puberty early. 她早熟。

例句：The changes of puberty can be challenging for teenagers. 青春期的变化对青少年来说可能具有挑战性。


近义词：adolescence, adolescence, coming of age | 反义词：childhood

词源:
From Middle French puberté, from Latin pubertas "age of manhood, maturity," from pubes "adult," from PIE *peu- "boy,"  *peus- "girl, child".  The original meaning relates to the growth of hair (pubes) associated with sexual maturity.
