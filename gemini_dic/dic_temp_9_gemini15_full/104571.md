kane:/keɪn/| n. |凯恩；手杖；凶手|A cane is a walking stick.  Kane is a common surname. In some contexts, "kane" might refer to a killer or murderer.|例句：He used his cane to help him walk. 他用拐杖帮助他行走。

The name Kane is of Irish origin. |近义词：stick, staff (for cane as a walking stick); murderer, killer (for kane as a person)|反义词： (No direct antonyms, depends on the context.  For "cane" as a walking stick, perhaps "crutches" if referring to a different assistive device.)
