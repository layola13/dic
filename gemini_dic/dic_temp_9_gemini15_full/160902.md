post-fine:/ˌpoʊstˈfaɪn/| adj.|罚款后，罚款之后的|  Describes something that occurs or is related to the period after a fine has been imposed.  It implies a consequence or condition that follows the payment or imposition of a fine. | Example: Post-fine restrictions were placed on his driving license. 罚款后，他的驾照受到了限制。|近义词：post-penalty, after-fine |反义词：pre-fine, before-fine
