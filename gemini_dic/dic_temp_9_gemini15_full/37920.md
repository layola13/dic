complete:/kəmˈpliːt/ | adj. v. |完整的；完成 | complete  means to make something whole or perfect, or to finish something.  It can also describe something that is entire and lacking nothing. |例句：The project is now complete.  这个项目现在完成了。  |例句：She gave a complete account of the incident. 她对事件作了详尽的叙述。|近义词：finished, entire, whole, perfect |反义词：incomplete, unfinished, partial


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team completes the task efficiently. = 团队高效地完成了任务。
He completes his homework every day. = 他每天都完成作业。

一般过去时:
They completed the marathon in record time. = 他们以创纪录的时间完成了马拉松比赛。
She completed her degree last year. = 她去年完成了学业。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

It is essential that he complete the assignment on time. = 他务必按时完成作业。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
