micraster:/ˈmaɪkræstər/| n. |微星，微型星状体|A genus of extinct irregular sea urchins characterized by a small, star-shaped mouth and a flattened test (shell).  They are common fossils found in Cretaceous-age rocks.|The micraster fossils found in the chalk cliffs are remarkably well-preserved.|近义词：sea urchin (general term)|反义词：无
