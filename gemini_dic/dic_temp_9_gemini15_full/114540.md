malapropism:/ˈmæləˌprɒpɪzəm/| n. | 字词误用；滑稽的用词错误 | The humorous misuse of a word in place of a similar-sounding one, often with unintentionally amusing effect.  A malapropism is a type of speech error. | Example:  "He's a real <i>ignoramus</i>" (instead of "ignorant")  他真是个无知的人（误用ignoramus代替ignorant）。

| 近义词：misuse, blunder, solecism | 反义词：accuracy, precision |
