mourner:/ˈmɔːrnər/| n. |哀悼者，送葬者 |A mourner is a person who is grieving the death of someone.  Mourners often attend funerals and memorial services to express their sorrow and pay their respects.|例句：Many mourners attended the funeral to pay their last respects. 许多哀悼者参加葬礼以表达最后的敬意。


The mourners wept openly at the graveside. 哀悼者在坟墓旁公开哭泣。|近义词：grievers, bereaved, those in mourning |反义词：celebrant
