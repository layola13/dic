irreligiously:/ˌɪrɪˈlɪdʒəsli/| adv.|不虔诚地，不敬神地|In a manner showing a lack of respect for religion or religious practices.  It implies a disregard for religious beliefs or norms, often through actions or behavior.|例句：He spoke irreligiously about the sanctity of the church. 他不敬地谈论教堂的圣洁。|例句：They lived irreligiously, ignoring all religious observances. 他们过着不虔诚的生活，忽略所有宗教仪式。|近义词：unreligiously, impiously, profanely, sacrilegiously|反义词：piously, devoutly, religiously|副词
