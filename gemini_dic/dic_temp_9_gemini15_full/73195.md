force-feed:/fɔːrsˈfiːd/ | vt. | 强迫喂食，强行灌输 | To make someone eat or drink something against their will, or to make someone accept an idea or information forcefully. | 例句：The farmer had to force-feed the sick calf.  = 农民不得不强迫喂食生病的小牛。

The government tried to force-feed the public with propaganda. = 政府试图强行向公众灌输宣传。


近义词：ram down someone's throat, stuff, cram


反义词：allow, permit, encourage


词性:动词
