regimens:/ˈredʒɪmənz/| n. |规章；制度；养生法；疗养法 | A regimen is a systematic plan or course of action, especially one imposed in the interest of health or as part of a treatment.  It often involves a strict routine or set of rules. |  The doctor prescribed a strict regimen of diet and exercise.  医生开具了严格的饮食和运动疗法。

The patient followed the regimen meticulously. 病人一丝不苟地遵循着治疗方案。| regimen, program, system, plan | chaos, disorder | 名词

词源:

The word "regimen" comes from the Latin word "regimen," meaning "rule," "government," or "management."  This reflects its core meaning of a systematic and controlled plan.
