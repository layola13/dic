contrarious:/kənˈtreəriəs/| adj.| 反抗的；固执的；与…相反的 |  Difficult or impossible to deal with; contrary.  Characterized by opposition or contradiction. |例句：He was contrarious and refused to cooperate. 他很固执，拒绝合作。

例句：Her contrarious nature made it difficult to work with her.  她固执的性格使得和她一起工作很困难。


近义词：obstinate, stubborn, perverse, refractory, contrary


反义词：obedient, compliant, docile, amenable


词性：形容词
