fireproofed:/ˈfaɪərpruːft/| adj. | 防火的；防火处理过的 | Treated to resist the damaging effects of fire.  The building was fireproofed to meet safety regulations. 这座建筑物经过防火处理以符合安全条例。|近义词：fire-resistant, flameproof |反义词：flammable, combustible


USAGE->

The factory fireproofs its buildings annually. = 工厂每年都会对其建筑物进行防火处理。
This material is fireproofed to a high standard. = 这种材料的防火处理达到了很高的标准。
