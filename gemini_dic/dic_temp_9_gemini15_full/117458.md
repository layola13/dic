Mediterranean:/ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən/| adj. n.|地中海的；地中海地区；地中海气候|Of or relating to the Mediterranean Sea or the countries bordering it.  The word refers to both the sea and the region surrounding it, characterized by its warm, dry summers and mild, wet winters.  |例句：The Mediterranean climate is ideal for growing olives and grapes. 地中海气候非常适合种植橄榄和葡萄。

例句：We took a cruise in the Mediterranean Sea. 我们在地中海乘船游览。
