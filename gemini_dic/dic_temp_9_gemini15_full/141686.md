opulence:/ˈɒpjʊləns/| /ˈoʊpjələns/ | n. |富裕；奢华；丰富 | Great wealth or luxuriousness.  It refers to a display of wealth and abundance, often in a lavish or extravagant manner.  The term can describe material possessions, but also extends to things like artistic richness or abundance of natural resources. |The opulence of the palace was breathtaking.  皇宫的奢华令人叹为观止。

The king's court was known for its opulence and extravagance. 国王的宫廷以其奢华和铺张浪费而闻名。


近义词：luxury, richness, affluence, magnificence, splendor

反义词：poverty, scarcity, deprivation, austerity
