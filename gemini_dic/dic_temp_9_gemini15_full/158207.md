platting:/ˈplætɪŋ/ | v. | 绘制…的图；分区；编排；策划 | To make a plat of; to divide into plots or lots, as land; to plan or scheme. | The surveyor is platting the new subdivision.  测量员正在绘制新的细分图。  They are platting the land for future development. 他们正在规划土地以供未来开发。 | plotting, designing, scheming |  unplatting (反义词不常用，需结合语境) | 动词


USAGE->

They plat the land for new houses. = 他们正在规划土地用于建造新房。
The architect is platting the design of the building. = 建筑师正在绘制建筑物的设计图。


一般过去时:
The developers platted the entire area last year. = 开发商去年规划了整个区域。
