reinvigorator:/ˌriːɪnˈvɪɡəreɪtər/ | n. |恢复活力者；使精力恢复的人或事物 | Someone or something that reinvigorates.  A reinvigorator can be a person, a substance, an experience, or an idea that restores energy, enthusiasm, or strength. | Example:  The mountain air acted as a reinvigorator, filling me with renewed energy.  山间空气像是一个恢复活力的源泉，让我精力充沛。|近义词：stimulant, tonic, energizer |反义词：depressant, drain, depleter |名词
