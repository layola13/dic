paralegal:/pærəˈliːɡəl/| n. |法律助理，准律师|A paralegal is a person qualified to assist a lawyer, but who is not yet, or never will be, a fully qualified lawyer.  They typically perform tasks such as legal research, drafting documents, and managing cases under the supervision of an attorney. |Example:  She worked as a paralegal for five years before applying to law school. 她做了五年法律助理，之后申请了法学院。


Example: The paralegal prepared the necessary documents for the court hearing.  法律助理为庭审准备了必要的文书。|近义词：legal assistant, law clerk|反义词：lawyer (in the sense of fully qualified)
