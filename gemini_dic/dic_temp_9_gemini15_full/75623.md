fuggy:/ˈfʌɡi/| adj.|闷热的，潮湿的，模糊的 |  Describes weather that is hot and humid, or something that is unclear or indistinct.  |例句：The air was fuggy and oppressive.  空气闷热而压抑。
The photograph was rather fuggy. 照片相当模糊。|近义词：muggy, humid, hazy, blurry |反义词：crisp, clear, dry
