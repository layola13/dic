fraughter:/ˈfrɔːtər/| adj.|充满…的；装满…的；充满…的|Full of or laden with something specified.  The word often implies an overwhelming or excessive amount.|例句：The air was fraught with tension.  空气中充满了紧张气氛。
The journey was fraught with danger. 这趟旅程充满了危险。|近义词：laden, burdened, filled, charged|反义词：empty, devoid, lacking
