critiquing:/krɪˈtiːkiŋ/| v.|评论；批判；评论性地评价|The act of evaluating and judging something, often in a detailed and analytical way, to identify its strengths and weaknesses.|例句：The professor spent the semester critiquing student papers. 教授花了整个学期来评论学生的论文。

例句：She offered a thoughtful critiquing of the film's plot and character development. 她对电影的情节和人物发展进行了深入的评论。


近义词：analyzing, evaluating, reviewing, assessing, judging

反义词：praising, applauding, endorsing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He critiques the latest political developments weekly. 他每周评论最新的政治动态。
She critiques her own work ruthlessly. 她无情地批判自己的作品。


一般过去时:
The art critic critiqued the painter's latest exhibition.  艺术评论家评论了这位画家最新的展览。
They critiqued the proposal's feasibility. 他们评论了该提案的可行性。


现在完成时:
He has critiqued numerous films throughout his career. 他在他的职业生涯中评论了许多电影。
She has critiqued the government's handling of the crisis. 她已经评论了政府处理危机的措施。


过去完成时:
The editor had critiqued the manuscript before submitting it for publication. 编辑在提交稿件出版之前已经评论了它。
They had critiqued the design several times before finalizing it. 他们在最终确定设计之前已经评论了几次。


将来时:
She will critique the student's presentation tomorrow. 她明天将评论学生的演示文稿。
He will critique the new restaurant's menu next week. 他下周将评论这家新餐馆的菜单。


虚拟语气:
If I were to critique his performance, I would focus on his timing. 如果我要评论他的表演，我会关注他的时间把握。
I wish I had critiqued that project more thoroughly. 我希望我当时更彻底地评论了那个项目。


被动语态:
The book was critiqued by several literary scholars. 这本书被几位文学学者评论过。
The performance was harshly critiqued by the audience.  这场演出受到了观众的严厉批评。


疑问句:
Will you critique my essay before I submit it? 你会在我在提交论文之前评论一下吗？
Has anyone critiqued this policy yet? 有人评论过这项政策吗？


否定句:
I did not critique his work publicly. 我没有公开评论他的作品。
She hasn't yet critiqued the latest report. 她还没有评论最新的报告。
