curule:/ˈkjʊruːl/| adj.|弯脚的；曲线的；（古罗马）执政官用的|Of or relating to a curule chair;  specifically denoting the official chair of a Roman magistrate.  The term often implies high rank or authority. |例句：The curule chair, a symbol of Roman authority, was highly coveted by magistrates.  古罗马执政官的弯脚椅是官员们梦寐以求的象征。|近义词：magisterial, official, authoritative|反义词：unofficial, subordinate
