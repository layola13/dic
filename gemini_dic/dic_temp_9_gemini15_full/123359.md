monologuing:/ˈmɒnəlɒɡɪŋ/|/ˈmɑːnəlɒɡɪŋ/| v.|独白；自言自语|The act of delivering a monologue; speaking to oneself or as if to oneself.|例句：The actor was superb in his monologuing, captivating the audience with his emotional delivery.  演员的独白表演非常出色，其饱满的情感打动了观众。

例句：She sat alone, monologuing about her day, oblivious to those around her. 她独自一人坐着，自言自语地讲述着自己的一天，对周围的人视而不见。


近义词：soliloquizing, soliloquy (n.)


反义词：dialogue, conversation, interaction


词性:动词
