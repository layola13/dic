singles:/ˈsɪŋɡəlz/| n.|单数；单张；单身人士；单打比赛|A single is a thing or person considered individually, or one of a set of things or people.  It can also refer to a person who is not married or in a relationship.|例句：He bought a single ticket to the concert. 他买了一张演唱会的门票。


例句：She's been a single parent since her husband died. 她丈夫去世后她就成了单亲妈妈。


近义词：individuals, units, bachelors (for unmarried men), spinsters (for unmarried women)


反义词：multiples, couples, married people


词性:名词
