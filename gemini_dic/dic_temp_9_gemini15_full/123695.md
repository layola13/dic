moonblind:/ˈmuːnˌblaɪnd/| adj.|月盲症的；夜盲症的|A condition affecting the eyes, particularly in horses, causing night blindness or temporary blindness in bright light.  It is often associated with inflammation of the eye.|例句：The horse was diagnosed with moonblind and was unable to see clearly at night. 这匹马被诊断患有月盲症，晚上看不清东西。

The farmer noticed his horse was suffering from moonblind after several nights of stumbling.  农夫注意到他的马在连续几个晚上绊倒后患上了月盲症。|近义词：night blindness, temporary blindness|反义词：perfect vision, excellent eyesight
