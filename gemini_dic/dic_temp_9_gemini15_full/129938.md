non-Lutheran: /ˌnɒnˈluːθərən/ | adj. |非路德教的|Not belonging to or connected with the Lutheran Church.  |例句：The non-Lutheran churches in the area held a joint service.  该地区非路德教教堂举行了一次联合礼拜。

The school has a diverse student body, including many non-Lutheran students. 这所学校的学生来自不同的背景，包括许多非路德教学生。


近义词：non-Protestant (in some contexts), other denominations


反义词：Lutheran


词源:  The word is a compound of "non-" (meaning "not") and "Lutheran" (referring to Martin Luther and the Protestant denomination he founded).  The term "Lutheran" derives from Martin Luther, the 16th-century German theologian who initiated the Protestant Reformation.
