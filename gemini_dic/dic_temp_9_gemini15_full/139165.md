obit:/ˈoʊbɪt/| n. |讣告，死亡通知|A short biography of a deceased person, especially one published in a newspaper.|The newspaper published an obit about the famous actor.  报纸刊登了关于这位著名演员的讣告。| eulogy, necrology |  life story | 名词
