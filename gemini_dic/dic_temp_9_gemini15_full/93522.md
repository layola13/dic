imageries:/ˈɪmɪdʒəriːz/| n. |意象；图像；想象；意象群|The plural of "imagery," referring to the use of descriptive language to create vivid mental images.  It encompasses visual, auditory, tactile, olfactory, and gustatory sensations evoked by language.  Imagery is a powerful literary device used to engage the reader's senses and emotions.|例句：The poet's imagery vividly depicted the bustling marketplace. 这位诗人的意象生动地描绘了熙攘的市场。

The novel is rich in imagery, transporting the reader to another time and place. 这部小说充满了意象，将读者带到另一个时空。|近义词：images, figures, representations, visualizations|反义词：abstraction, vagueness
