remount:/ˌriːˈmaʊnt/| vt. n.|重新安装；再骑上；重新装备|To mount again; to equip or supply again.|例句：The soldiers remounted their horses after a brief rest.  士兵们短暂休息后又骑上了马。|例句：The army was remounted with new artillery. 这支军队重新装备了新的火炮。|近义词：reinstall, refit, re-equip|反义词：dismount, dismantle
