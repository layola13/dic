cystotomy:/sɪˈstɒtəmi/ | n. |膀胱切开术|Surgical incision into the urinary bladder.  The procedure is performed to access the bladder for various reasons, such as removing stones, draining urine, or performing biopsies.|Example: The veterinarian performed a cystotomy to remove the bladder stone from the dog.  兽医进行了膀胱切开术以去除狗的膀胱结石。  The patient underwent a cystotomy to relieve urinary retention. 病人接受了膀胱切开术以缓解尿潴留。|近义词：cystostomy (膀胱造口术), vesicotomy (膀胱切开术 -  a more general term)|反义词：cystorrhaphy (膀胱缝合术)
