leeringly:/ˈlɪəriŋli/ | adv. | 淫笑着，色迷迷地 |  In a leering manner; with a suggestive or lascivious gaze.  It describes a way of looking at someone that is sexually suggestive and often disrespectful. |例句：He looked at her leeringly. 他色迷迷地望着她。

He leered leeringly at the young woman. 他色迷迷地盯着那个年轻女子。


近义词：lustfully, lasciviously, suggestively, slyly


反义词：innocently, chastely, respectfully


词性：副词
