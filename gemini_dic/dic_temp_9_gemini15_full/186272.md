rhizophaga:/ˌraɪzˈɒfəɡə/| n. |根食菌属|A genus of fungi that are parasitic on plant roots.  These fungi are often associated with root diseases and can cause significant damage to crops.| Example:  *Rhizophaga* species are known to infect various plant species, causing root rot and reduced yield. 根食菌属已知会感染多种植物物种，导致根腐病和减产。|近义词: (genus of root-infecting fungi, needs specific species for true synonyms)|反义词: (no direct antonym, but could consider beneficial mycorrhizal fungi)|名词
