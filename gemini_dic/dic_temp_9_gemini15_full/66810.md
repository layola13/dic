expansible: /ɪkˈspæn.sə.bəl/ | adj. | 可扩展的，可膨胀的 | Capable of being expanded or enlarged.  Something that is expansible can be made bigger or wider.  It often refers to physical objects that can increase in size, but can also refer to abstract concepts like ideas or systems. |


例句：

The expansible suitcase is perfect for travelers who need to pack a lot. = 这个可扩展的行李箱非常适合需要打包很多东西的旅行者。

The company's expansible business model allows it to adapt to changing market conditions. = 公司的可扩展业务模式使其能够适应不断变化的市场环境。


近义词：expandable, extensible, dilatable


反义词：non-expansible, inflexible, rigid


词源:

From "expand" + "-able".  "Expand" itself comes from the Latin word *expandere*, meaning "to spread out".
