clevis:/ˈklɛvɪs/| n. |U形销，U形环，叉头|A U-shaped metal device with a pin through the ends of the arms, used to connect two parts of a machine or structure.|The clevis pin secures the clevis to the other component.|近义词：link, shackle|反义词：无


例句：

The clevis is used to connect the two parts of the linkage system.  = U形环用于连接连杆系统的两个部分。

The farmer used a clevis to attach the plow to the tractor. = 农民用U形环将犁连接到拖拉机上。
