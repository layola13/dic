preadmission: /ˌpriːædmɪʃən/ | n. |入学前准备，入学前的审核|  The process of evaluating an applicant before they are officially admitted to a school, college, or program.  It often involves reviewing applications, transcripts, and other supporting documents.|例句：The university's preadmission process is rigorous and involves multiple steps. 大学的入学前审核过程非常严格，需要多个步骤。

例句：  He completed all the necessary paperwork for preadmission to the medical school. 他完成了医学院入学前所有必要的文书工作。


近义词：pre-enrollment, preliminary admission, prior admission

反义词：post-admission


词性：名词
