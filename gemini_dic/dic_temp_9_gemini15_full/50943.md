design:/dɪˈzaɪn/ | n., vt. |设计，图案；计划，企图 | to plan and make something, or to plan something that will happen | 近义词：plan, scheme, project | 反义词：disorganize, chaos


英文解析:
As a noun, "design" refers to a plan or drawing produced to show the look and function or workings of a building, garment, or other object before it is made.  It can also refer to the way in which something is planned and made. As a verb, "design" means to create or invent something, typically a detailed plan or drawing.  It implies careful consideration and intentionality in the creation process.


例句:
The design of the new building is modern and functional. = 新建筑的设计现代而实用。

The architect presented several different designs for the bridge. = 建筑师展示了几种不同的桥梁设计。


He designed a new system for tracking inventory. = 他设计了一个新的库存追踪系统。

She carefully designed the marketing campaign. = 她仔细设计了营销活动。
