nonorganics:/ˌnɒnɔːrˈɡænɪks/| n.|无机物|Substances that are not derived from living organisms;  they lack carbon-based structures typically found in organic compounds.|例句：Rocks and minerals are examples of nonorganics.  岩石和矿物是无机物的例子。|近义词：inorganic compounds, mineral substances|反义词：organics, organic compounds
