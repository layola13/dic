remiss:/rɪˈmɪs/| adj.| 疏忽的；懈怠的；失职的 | Failing to fulfill one's duty or obligation; negligent.  It implies a lack of care or attention that results in a failure to do something that should have been done. |例句：It was remiss of me to forget to send you the invitation.  我忘了给你寄请柬，真是我的疏忽。

例句：The police were remiss in their duty to protect the public. 警方没有尽到保护公众的责任，失职了。


近义词：negligent, careless, neglectful, derelict, lax


反义词：diligent, careful, attentive, meticulous


词性：形容词
