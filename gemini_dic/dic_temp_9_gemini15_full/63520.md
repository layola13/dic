enunciated:/ɪˈnʌnsieɪtɪd/ | v. |清晰地发音，明确表达 |To pronounce words clearly and distinctly.  |The speaker enunciated each word carefully so that everyone could understand.  演讲者仔细地清晰地发音每个单词，以便每个人都能理解。|articulated, pronounced, expressed|mumbled, slurred|动词

词源:
