redeclaring:/ˌriːdɪˈklɛərɪŋ/| v. |重新声明，再次声明 |The act of stating something again, often with more emphasis or clarity, or to correct a previous statement.  It implies a previous declaration existed. |例句：The company is redeclaring its commitment to sustainability after recent criticism. 公司在最近受到批评后，再次声明其对可持续发展的承诺。

例句：After the initial confusion, the speaker redeclared his position on the treaty.  在最初的混乱之后，发言人再次声明了他对条约的立场。


近义词：restating, reaffirming, reiterating, reasserting |反义词：retracting, denying, disclaiming |词性：动词
