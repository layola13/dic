recollecting:/ˌrɛkəˈlɛktɪŋ/| v. |回忆，想起|The act of remembering something from the past; recalling.  It implies a more deliberate and effortful process of searching one's memory than simply remembering.|例句：I spent the afternoon recollecting childhood memories. 我花了整个下午回忆童年往事。

例句：She was recollecting the details of the accident. 她正在回忆事故的细节。


近义词：remembering, recalling, reminiscing | 反义词：forgetting, overlooking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I recollect my childhood often. = 我经常回忆我的童年。
He recollects the names of all his classmates. = 他记得所有同学的名字。


一般过去时:
I recollected the event vividly. = 我清晰地回忆起那件事。
She recollected the address after a moment's thought. = 她思考了一会儿后记起了地址。


现在完成时:
I have recollected many happy moments from my trip. = 我已经回忆起旅行中许多快乐的时光。
She has recollected enough information to write the report. = 她已经回忆起足够的信息来写报告了。


过去完成时:
I had recollected the password before logging in. = 我登录之前已经记起了密码。
They had recollected the evidence before the trial. = 审判前他们已经回忆起了证据。


将来时:
I will recollect this day for years to come. = 未来几年我都会回忆起今天。
She will recollect the information when she needs it. = 她需要的时候会回忆起这些信息。


虚拟语气:
If I could recollect the details, I would tell you. = 如果我能回忆起细节，我会告诉你的。
I wish I could recollect where I put my keys. = 我真希望我能想起我把钥匙放在哪里了。


被动语态:
The details of the incident were recollected by the witness. = 事故的细节被证人回忆起来了。
The lost memories were slowly recollected over time.  = 丢失的记忆随着时间的推移慢慢被回忆起来了。


疑问句:
Can you recollect what happened yesterday? = 你能回忆起昨天发生的事情吗？
Do you recollect the name of the book? = 你还记得那本书的名字吗？


否定句:
I cannot recollect his name. = 我记不起他的名字。
She didn't recollect seeing him that day. = 她不记得那天见过他。


PHRASE->
