nonperspective: /ˌnɒn pərˈspektɪv/ | adj. | 非透视的；非透视法的 | Lacking perspective; not representing objects in three dimensions as they appear to the eye.  It often refers to a style of art or representation that does not use linear perspective, resulting in a flattened, less realistic image. | Example: The painting had a nonperspective style, with figures and objects arranged in a seemingly flat plane.  这幅画采用了非透视风格，人物和物体排列在一个看似平面的平面上。|近义词：flat, nonrepresentational, abstract|反义词：perspective, realistic, representational |


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective, and thus doesn't have verb conjugations.  The usage below shows how it functions in different sentence structures.

肯定句:
The artist employed a nonperspective technique in the mural. = 艺术家在这幅壁画中使用了非透视技术。
His nonperspective drawings were surprisingly effective. = 他的非透视绘画作品出奇地有效。


否定句:
The painting wasn't a nonperspective work; it used clear linear perspective. = 这幅画并非非透视作品；它使用了清晰的线性透视。
The design wasn't nonperspective; it was meticulously rendered in three dimensions. = 这个设计并非非透视的；它是经过精心渲染的三维设计。


疑问句:
Is this a nonperspective painting? = 这是一幅非透视画吗？
Are these nonperspective designs? = 这些是非透视设计吗？


PHRASE->
There are no common multi-word phrases directly using "nonperspective".  However, phrases describing the effect of nonperspective art are common:

flat perspective
lack of depth
two-dimensional representation
non-realistic portrayal
stylized depiction
