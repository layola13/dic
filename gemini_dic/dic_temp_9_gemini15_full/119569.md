microtrabecula: /ˌmaɪkroʊtræˈbɛkjʊlə/ | n. | 微梁 | A tiny, delicate, supporting structure within a cell, forming a three-dimensional network.  These structures are involved in maintaining cell shape and intracellular transport.  Their exact function and composition remain areas of ongoing research. | Example:  The study investigated the role of microtrabeculae in maintaining the structural integrity of neurons.  这项研究调查了微梁在维持神经元结构完整性中的作用。 | 近义词: microfilament (though not a perfect synonym, as microtrabeculae are a more complex, three-dimensional structure) | 反义词: N/A (no direct antonym exists)
