photosynthesizer: /ˌfoʊtoʊˈsɪnθəsaɪzər/ | n. | 光合作用器，光合生物 | An organism, or a device that performs photosynthesis.  A photosynthesizer converts light energy into chemical energy in the form of organic compounds. | Example: Plants are natural photosynthesizers, converting sunlight into energy. 植物是天然的光合作用器，将阳光转化为能量。  The artificial photosynthesizer in the lab is still under development. 实验室里的人工光合作用器仍在研发中。|近义词：photosynthetic organism |反义词：N/A
