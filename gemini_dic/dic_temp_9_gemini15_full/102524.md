jadding:/ˈdʒædɪŋ/| n.| 喧闹；吵闹；争吵 | A state of noisy disturbance or quarrel.  The word is primarily used in Scotland and some northern parts of England. It lacks widespread usage in standard English.  |例句：The jadding in the pub spilled out into the street. 酒吧里的喧闹吵嚷蔓延到街上。 |近义词：commotion, uproar, tumult, row, brawl |反义词：calm, peace, quiet, tranquility |名词
