foliolate:/ˈfoʊliəleɪt/ | adj. |小叶的；多小叶的 | Having leaflets; divided into leaflets.  A foliolate leaf is a compound leaf divided into several leaflets. | Example: The plant has a trifoliolate leaf, meaning it has three leaflets.  这植物有三小叶的叶子，意思是它有三片小叶。  The clover has palmately foliolate leaves.  酢浆草有掌状复叶。|近义词：leafleted |反义词：unifoliate
