caroused:/kəˈraʊzd/| v.|狂欢，痛饮|To drink and celebrate in a noisy, lively way.|例句：They caroused late into the night, celebrating their victory.  他们狂欢到深夜，庆祝他们的胜利。|近义词：revel, celebrate, binge, drink|反义词：fast, abstain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They carouse every weekend. = 他们每个周末都狂欢。
He carouses with his friends at the pub. = 他和朋友们在酒吧里狂欢。


一般过去时:
We caroused until dawn. = 我们狂欢到黎明。
They caroused all night long. = 他们整夜狂欢。


现在完成时:
They have caroused many times this year. = 今年他们已经狂欢过很多次了。
She has never caroused so wildly before. = 她以前从未如此疯狂地狂欢过。


过去完成时:
They had caroused the night before the important meeting. = 他们在重要的会议前一天晚上狂欢了。
We had caroused so much that we were completely exhausted. = 我们狂欢得太多了，完全筋疲力尽了。

被动语态:  (被动语态很少用于carouse)
