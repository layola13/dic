hailing:/ˈheɪlɪŋ/| v.|欢呼；下冰雹；来自；招呼|To greet or call out to someone.  To come from a particular place. To shower down (as of hail).|例句：They were hailing the champion. 他们正在欢呼冠军。 The storm was hailing fiercely.暴风雨下着冰雹。  He hails from a small town in Ireland. 他来自爱尔兰的一个小镇。|近义词：greeting, shouting, originating, coming from|反义词：ignoring, neglecting
