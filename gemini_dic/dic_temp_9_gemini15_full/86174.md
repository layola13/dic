heartswelling: /ˈhɑːrtswɛlɪŋ/ | adj. |令人心潮澎湃的，令人感动的|

英文解析:  Describes a feeling of overwhelming emotion, typically joy, happiness, or love, that fills one's heart. It suggests a profound and moving experience.

例句:

The reunion with her long-lost brother caused a heartswelling moment of joy.  = 与她失散多年的兄弟重逢带来了令人心潮澎湃的喜悦时刻。

The orchestra's performance was a heartswelling experience, leaving the audience in tears. = 管弦乐队的演奏是一次令人心潮澎湃的体验，让观众感动落泪。


近义词: moving, touching, heartwarming, overwhelming, deeply affecting

反义词: unmoving, cold, indifferent, unfeeling

词性: adjective
