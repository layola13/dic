USAGE->

一般现在时:
The enemy flanks our position. = 敌人侧翼攻击我们的阵地。
The mountain flanks the valley. = 山脉环绕着山谷。


一般过去时:
The general flanked the enemy's army. = 将军迂回敌军侧翼。
The runner flanked his opponent. = 赛跑者超过了他的对手。


现在完成时:
They have flanked the opposition successfully. = 他们成功地绕过了反对派。
The new road has flanked the old town. = 新路绕过了旧城。


过去完成时:
The army had flanked the enemy before the main attack. = 大部队攻击之前，军队已经迂回至敌军侧翼。
The team had flanked the other team by scoring a goal. = 球队通过进球超过了对方。


将来时:
We will flank the enemy's defenses. = 我们将绕过敌人的防御工事。
The new building will flank the park. = 新建筑将位于公园一侧。


虚拟语气:
If we had flanked them earlier, we would have won. = 如果我们早点绕过他们，我们就赢了。
I wish we had flanked the enemy's position. = 我希望我们已经绕过敌人的阵地。


被动语态:
Their position was flanked by the enemy. = 他们的阵地被敌人侧翼攻击了。
The city was flanked by mountains. = 城市被群山环绕。


疑问句:
Were the flanks of the army protected? = 军队的侧翼受到保护了吗？
How many flanks does a building have? = 一座建筑有多少个侧面？


否定句:
The army did not flank the enemy effectively. = 这支军队没有有效地迂回敌军侧翼。
The building does not flank the river. = 这座建筑没有位于河边。
