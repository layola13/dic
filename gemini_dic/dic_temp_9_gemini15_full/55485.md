distractful: /dɪˈstræktfəl/ | adj. | 使人分心的；令人厌烦的 | tending to distract or divert attention; annoyingly distracting. |  The distracting music made it hard to concentrate on my work. 令人分心的音乐使我难以集中精力工作。 | distracting, diverting, bothersome | focusing, engaging, captivating |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The noisy environment is distractful.  吵闹的环境令人分心。
That movie is distractful to children. 那部电影对孩子们来说很分散注意力。


一般过去时:
The party was distractful last night. 昨晚的派对很吵闹，让人无法专心。
The loud music was distractful yesterday. 昨天的音乐太吵了，让人分心。


现在完成时:
The constant interruptions have been distractful.  不断的打断让人很分心。
The project has been distractful; I haven't finished it yet.  这个项目太费神了，我还没完成。


过去完成时:
The previous meeting had been distractful, delaying our decision.  之前的会议很混乱，延误了我们的决定。
The children had been distractful all morning, making it hard to teach. 孩子们整个上午都很不专心，难以教学。

虚拟语气:

I wish the classroom weren't so distractful. 我希望教室不要那么吵闹。

否定句:
