shape-shifter:/ˈʃeɪpˌʃɪftər/| n. |变形者，易容者 | A shape-shifter is a person, animal, or creature that can change its shape or appearance.  This term often appears in fantasy and science fiction contexts, referring to beings with magical or supernatural abilities. |Example: The ancient myth tells of a shape-shifter who could transform into any animal.  古代神话讲述了一个能够变成任何动物的变形者。The villain in the movie was a master shape-shifter, constantly deceiving his enemies. 电影里的反派是一个变形大师，不断欺骗他的敌人。|近义词：metamorph, transformer, illusionist |反义词：constant, unchanging
