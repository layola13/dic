salinous:/ˈseɪləɪnəs/| adj.|含盐的，咸的|Salty; containing or resembling salt.|The salinous water of the Dead Sea is known for its high mineral content. 死海含盐量高的水以其丰富的矿物质含量而闻名。|近义词：salty, briny, saline|反义词：fresh, unsalted


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective, so there's no conjugation like verbs.  The usage depends on the context of the sentence.


PHRASE->

moderately salinous 中等盐度的
