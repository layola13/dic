plough:/plaʊ/| n./v.|犁；耕地；费力地前进|A plough is a farm tool used to turn over the soil before planting.  It can also refer to the act of plowing or the furrow created by plowing. The word can also be used figuratively to describe making progress through something difficult.|例句：The farmer used a plough to prepare the field for planting.  这个农民用犁耕地准备播种。
The ship ploughed through the stormy seas. 这艘船奋力驶过暴风雨的海面。|近义词：plow, till, furrow|反义词：harvest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ploughs the field every spring. = 他每年春天都耕地。
The farmer ploughs his land regularly. = 这位农民定期耕种他的土地。

一般过去时:
They ploughed the field yesterday. = 他们昨天耕了地。
The farmer ploughed the land before planting the seeds. = 农民在播种之前耕了地。


现在完成时:
He has ploughed the entire field. = 他已经耕完了整块地。
The farmer has ploughed the land three times this season. = 这个农民本季已经耕地三次了。


过去完成时:
They had ploughed the field before the rain started. = 下雨之前他们已经耕过地了。
The farmer had ploughed the land before the sun rose. = 农民在日出之前已经耕过地了。


将来时:
We will plough the field next week. = 我们下周将耕地。
The farmer will plough the land after the rain stops. = 雨停后农民将耕地。


虚拟语气:
If I had a plough, I would plough the field myself. = 如果我有一台犁，我会自己耕地。
I wish I could plough the field more quickly. = 我希望我能更快地耕地。


被动语态:
The field was ploughed last week. = 这块地在上周被耕了。
The land is being ploughed by the tractor. = 土地正在被拖拉机耕种。


疑问句:
Have you ploughed the field yet? = 你已经耕地了吗？
Will they plough the field tomorrow? = 他们明天会耕地吗？


否定句:
He did not plough the field this year. = 他今年没有耕地。
They haven't ploughed the land yet. = 他们还没耕地。


PHRASE->
plough through: 费力地完成
plough back: 再投资
plough ahead: 继续努力
plough the ground: 耕地
plough one's way: 艰难地前进


词源:
The word "plough" comes from Old English *plōh, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *plūgō.  Cognates appear in other Germanic languages (e.g., German Pflug, Dutch ploeg).  The word's history reflects the importance of agriculture in early societies.
