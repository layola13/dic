misogamy:/mɪˈsɒɡəmi/ | n. | 厌恶结婚；婚姻恐惧症 |  A hatred of marriage; aversion to marriage.  It is often used to describe a deep-seated aversion, not simply a preference for remaining single.  | Example: His misogamy stemmed from a traumatic childhood experience involving his parents' bitter divorce.  他的厌恶结婚源于童年时期父母痛苦的离婚经历。 | 近义词：marriage aversion, gamophobia | 反义词：philogamy
