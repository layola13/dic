laemodipod:/leɪˈmɒdɪpɒd/| n. |桨足类动物|A laemodipod is a small crustacean belonging to the order Amphipoda, characterized by its laterally compressed body and the presence of well-developed pereiopods (legs) used for swimming and walking.  They are typically found in marine and freshwater environments.|Example: The researchers discovered a new species of laemodipod in the deep-sea trench.|近义词: amphipod|反义词: N/A
