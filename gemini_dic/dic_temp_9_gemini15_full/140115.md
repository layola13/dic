off-roader:/ˌɒfˈrəʊdə(r)/|/ˈɑːfˈroʊdər/| n. |越野车；越野爱好者 |A vehicle designed for driving on rough terrain, typically with four-wheel drive.  Someone who enjoys driving off-road vehicles.|例句：He bought a powerful off-roader to tackle the challenging mountain trails. 他买了一辆强劲的越野车来应对具有挑战性的山路。

例句：She's a keen off-roader and spends her weekends exploring remote tracks. 她是一位狂热的越野爱好者，周末都花在探索偏远小路上。

近义词：four-wheel-drive, SUV, jeep, all-terrain vehicle (ATV)|反义词：sedan, saloon car
