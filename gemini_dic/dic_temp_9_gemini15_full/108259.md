launching-pad: /ˈlɔːntʃɪŋ pæd/ | n. | 发射台；起点 | A launching pad is a structure used to launch rockets and spacecraft. It provides a stable platform for the vehicle and the necessary infrastructure for fueling and control. The term is also used metaphorically to refer to a starting point for a project, career, or any significant undertaking.  | Example: The rocket blasted off from its launching pad. 火箭从发射台上发射升空了。 The new technology served as a launching pad for the company's expansion.  这项新技术成为公司扩张的跳板。 | 近义词：platform, base, springboard | 反义词：conclusion, endpoint
