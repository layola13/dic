词源:
From Latin *penicillātus, past participle of penicillāre "to paint with a brush," from penicillus "paintbrush," diminutive of penis "tail," referring to the shape of a brush.  The connection to penicillin is indirect, referring to the brush-like appearance of the Penicillium mold.
