eviscerated:/ɪˈvɪsərˌeɪtɪd/| v. adj.|掏空内脏的；使失去重要部分的；严厉批评的 |To remove the internal organs of (a body or animal).  Figuratively, to remove the essential content of something, leaving it empty or ineffective; to severely criticize.|例句：The surgeon eviscerated the body to perform an autopsy.  外科医生解剖尸体以进行尸检。The critic eviscerated the author's new novel in his review. 评论家在他的评论中严厉批评了作者的新小说。|近义词： disembowel, gut,  criticize severely, demolish |反义词：reinforce, strengthen,  praise
