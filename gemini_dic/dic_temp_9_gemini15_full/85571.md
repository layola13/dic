haters:/ˈheɪtərz/| n.|讨厌者，仇恨者|People who express strong dislike or hatred towards someone or something.  They often express their negative feelings publicly, sometimes through online platforms.|例句：The band has a lot of haters online, but they also have many devoted fans. 乐队在网上有很多讨厌者，但他们也有许多忠实的粉丝。  例句：He ignored the haters and focused on his work. 他无视那些讨厌者，专注于自己的工作。|近义词：detractors, critics, enemies|反义词：supporters, admirers, fans|名词
