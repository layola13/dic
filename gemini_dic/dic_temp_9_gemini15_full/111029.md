lish:/lɪʃ/| v.| 梳理；整理；使整齐 |to make (something, especially hair or wool) smooth and neat by combing or brushing|例句：She lished her hair before going out. 她出门前梳理了头发。|近义词：comb, brush, straighten |反义词:tangle, mess up


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lishes his beard every morning. = 他每天早上都梳理胡须。
She lishes her dog's fur regularly. = 她定期梳理她狗狗的毛发。

一般过去时:
I lished my hair before the party. = 我在聚会前梳理了头发。
He lished the wool before spinning it. = 他在纺纱之前梳理了羊毛。

现在完成时:
She has lished her hair, and she looks much neater. = 她已经梳理了头发，看起来整洁多了。
They have lished all the sheep. = 他们已经把所有的羊都梳理过了。

过去完成时:
I had lished my hair before I went to bed. = 我睡觉前已经梳理了头发。
She had lished the yarn before she started knitting. = 在她开始编织之前，她已经梳理好了纱线。

将来时:
