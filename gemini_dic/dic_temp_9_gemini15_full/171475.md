puffy:/ˈpʌfi/| adj. | 肿胀的；蓬松的；膨胀的 | Swollen, bloated, or inflated;  soft and light, like a cloud.  It describes something that is abnormally large or full of air or liquid. |例句：Her eyes were puffy from crying.  她的眼睛哭肿了。

The pillow is so puffy and comfortable. 这个枕头又蓬松又舒服。


近义词：swollen, bloated, inflated, plump, rounded

反义词：flat, deflated, sunken, thin


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The bread is puffy. = 面包蓬松。
His cheeks are puffy. = 他的脸颊肿胀。


一般过去时:

The clouds looked puffy in the evening sky. = 晚霞中的云朵看起来蓬松。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

The cake had puffed up too much and spilled over. = 蛋糕膨胀得太多，溢出来了。
