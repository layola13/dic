dope:/doʊp/| n., adj., v.|兴奋剂；傻瓜；很棒的；使兴奋；麻醉|

英文解析：As a noun, "dope" can refer to an illegal drug, especially a narcotic.  It can also mean a stupid person or a fool.  As an adjective, it means excellent or amazing (slang). As a verb, it means to drug someone or something.  Its origins are complex, possibly related to Dutch words meaning "sauce" or "stuff."

名词 (stupid person):

形容词 (excellent):
That concert was dope! = 那场演唱会太棒了！

形容词 (amazing):
Her new shoes are totally dope. = 她的新鞋子太酷了！


动词 (to drug):

动词 (to drug):
