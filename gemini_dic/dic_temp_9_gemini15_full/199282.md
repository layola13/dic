silverfish:/ˈsɪlvərfɪʃ/| n. |银鱼，衣鱼|A small, wingless insect that is silvery-grey in color and often found in damp places, feeding on paper, starch, and other organic matter.  They are considered pests.| Example: I found a silverfish in my old book. 我在旧书里发现了一只衣鱼。|近义词：fishmoth, bristletail|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑体型较大的昆虫)|名词
