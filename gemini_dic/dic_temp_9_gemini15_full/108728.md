leap-years:/ˈliːp jɪrz/| n.|闰年|A leap year is a year, occurring once every four years, which has 366 days including February 29th.  This is done to keep the calendar year synchronized with the solar year.|例句：2024 is a leap year. 2024年是闰年。|The next leap year is 2028.下一个闰年是2028年。|近义词：intercalary year|反义词：common year|名词
