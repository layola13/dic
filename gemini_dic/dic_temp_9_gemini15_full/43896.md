criticism:/ˈkrɪtɪsɪzəm/| n. | 批评；批判；评论 | The act of expressing disapproval of someone or something; a critical comment or judgment.  |  His criticism of the government's policies was widely reported.  他的政府政策批评被广泛报道。 The film received mixed criticism. 这部电影收到了褒贬不一的评论。 | praise, review, analysis |  approval, praise, commendation | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
His criticism is always constructive. = 他的批评总是建设性的。
Their criticism focuses on the lack of detail. = 他们的批评集中在细节的缺乏上。

一般过去时:
The play received harsh criticism after its opening night. = 该剧首映夜后受到了严厉批评。
Her criticism stung him deeply. = 她的批评深深刺痛了他。


现在完成时:
The author has received much criticism for his latest novel. = 作者因其最新小说而受到诸多批评。
The company has faced intense criticism for its environmental record. = 这家公司因其环保记录而面临强烈批评。

过去完成时:
The project had already faced considerable criticism before it even began. = 该项目甚至在开始之前就已经面临相当大的批评。
They had anticipated criticism, but it was still worse than expected. = 他们预料到了批评，但实际情况比预期的还要糟糕。


将来时:
The new policy will undoubtedly attract criticism. = 新政策无疑会招致批评。
He anticipates criticism of his ideas. = 他预料到人们会批评他的想法。


虚拟语气:
If his criticism were more constructive, it would be more helpful. = 如果他的批评更有建设性，那就更有帮助了。
I wish his criticism hadn't been so harsh. = 我希望他的批评不要那么严厉。


被动语态:
The book was subjected to intense criticism. = 这本书受到了激烈的批评。
His work has been widely criticised for its lack of originality. = 他的作品因缺乏原创性而受到广泛批评。


疑问句:
What is your criticism of this proposal? = 你对这个提议有什么批评意见？
What kind of criticism are you expecting? = 你期待什么样的批评？


否定句:
There was no criticism of his performance. = 对他的表现没有批评。
She offered no criticism of his work. = 她没有批评他的作品。
