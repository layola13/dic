overprogramme: /ˌoʊvərˈproʊɡræm/ | v. |  超负荷编程；过度安排；节目编排过多 | To overload a computer program with data or instructions, or to schedule too many events or activities.  | The system crashed because they overprogrammed the server. 系统崩溃是因为他们服务器程序超负荷了。  They overprogrammed the day with meetings, leaving no time for actual work. 他们安排了一天满满的会议，没有时间做实际工作。 | overload, overschedule | streamline, simplify | v.
