PHRASE->
pleural effusion (胸腔积液):  a buildup of fluid in the pleural space, often associated with pleurisies.
pleuritic chest pain (胸膜性胸痛): sharp, stabbing chest pain that worsens with deep breaths or coughs, characteristic of pleurisies.
acute pleurisies (急性胸膜炎): a sudden onset of pleurisies.
dry pleurisies (干性胸膜炎): a form of pleurisies without significant fluid buildup.
bacterial pleurisies (细菌性胸膜炎): pleurisies caused by bacterial infection.
