lipases:/ˈlaɪpeɪsiz/| n.|脂肪酶|Lipases are enzymes that break down fats (lipids) into fatty acids and glycerol.  They play a crucial role in digestion and metabolism.|例句：Pancreatic lipases are essential for the digestion of dietary fats. 胰腺脂肪酶对消化膳食脂肪至关重要。|例句：Deficiency in lipases can lead to malabsorption of fats. 脂肪酶缺乏会导致脂肪吸收不良。|近义词：esterases|反义词：无
