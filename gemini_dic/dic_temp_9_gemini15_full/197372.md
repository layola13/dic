shearing: /ʃɪərɪŋ/ | n. v. | 剪羊毛；剪切；修剪 |

英文解析:The act of cutting or clipping, especially the removal of wool from a sheep.  It can also refer to the process of cutting or trimming something else, such as fabric or metal.

例句:
The farmer is shearing his sheep. = 农夫正在剪他的羊毛。

The tailor is shearing the fabric. = 裁缝正在剪裁布料。


近义词: clipping, trimming, shearing, cutting


反义词:growing, lengthening


词性: Noun and Verb
