memoirs:/ˈmɛmwɑːrz/| n. |回忆录；个人回忆；自传|A memoir is an account of the author's personal experiences and reflections, often focusing on a specific period or theme in their life. It differs from an autobiography in its focus on specific events and themes rather than a comprehensive life story.|Example: Her memoirs detail her childhood in rural France and her later years working as a journalist.  她的回忆录详细描述了她童年在法国农村的经历以及后来作为记者的岁月。|近义词：autobiography, reminiscences, recollections|反义词：fiction, fabrication
