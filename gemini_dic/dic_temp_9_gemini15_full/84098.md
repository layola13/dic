half-intellectual: /ˌhæfɪnˈtɛləktʃuəl/ | adj. | 半吊子知识分子；自以为是的知识分子 |  Someone who possesses some intellectual knowledge or pretensions but lacks genuine depth or understanding.  They may display superficial knowledge or engage in intellectual posturing without true intellectual rigor. |


例句：

He's a bit of a half-intellectual, quoting obscure philosophers but failing to grasp their core arguments. = 他有点半吊子知识分子，引用晦涩的哲学家，但却抓不住他们的核心论点。

She enjoys the air of sophistication that comes with being perceived as a half-intellectual, even if her knowledge is largely superficial. = 她喜欢被认为是半吊子知识分子带来的那种精致感，即使她的知识大多是肤浅的。


近义词：pseudo-intellectual, dilettante, armchair intellectual, superficial


反义词：intellectual, scholar, expert, learned


词性: adjective
