grain:/ɡreɪn/| n. |谷物；颗粒；纹理 |  A single seed of a cereal plant, such as wheat, barley, or rice.  Also refers to the seeds collectively, or to the small hard particles of various substances.  It can also describe the texture or pattern of a surface. |例句：The farmer harvested a bountiful grain harvest this year.  今年农民收获了丰盛的谷物。|例句：The wood has a beautiful, fine grain. 这木头纹理漂亮细腻。|近义词：seed, kernel, particle, texture |反义词：powder (in the context of particle size)
