reascended: /ˌriːəˈsɛndɪd/ | v. |重新上升；再次攀登 | To ascend again; to climb or go up again. |例句：After the storm subsided, the hot air balloon reascended gracefully.  暴风雨过后，热气球优雅地再次升空。

The climbers reascended the mountain to retrieve their lost equipment. 登山者们重新攀登了山峰以取回丢失的装备。


近义词：re-ascent,climb again,go up again | 反义词：descend, fall, drop | 词性：动词
