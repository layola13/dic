de-deputizes: /ˌdiːˈdɛpjʊtaɪzɪz/ | v. |解除副职；撤销代理职务 |To remove someone from a deputy or acting position.  The verb is formed by adding the prefix "de-" (meaning "to remove" or "reverse") to the verb "deputizes," which means "to act as a deputy or substitute."  This word is relatively rare and often found in formal contexts. |例句：The sheriff de-deputized the volunteer after discovering his criminal record.  警长发现志愿者有犯罪记录后，撤销了他的代理警员职位。 The governor de-deputized the acting attorney general after the scandal broke.  丑闻爆发后，州长撤销了代理司法部长职务。 |近义词：remove, dismiss, revoke, rescind |反义词：appoint, deputize, authorize
