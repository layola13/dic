counteracted:/ˈkaʊntərækt/| v.|对抗；抵消；阻止|To act against (something) so as to reduce its force or neutralize it.  |例句：The government took measures to counteract the effects of the economic crisis. 政府采取措施来对抗经济危机的影響。|例句：The drug counteracted the effects of the poison.  这种药物抵消了毒药的作用。|近义词：oppose, neutralize, offset, counteract |反义词：reinforce, strengthen, support|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The medication counteracts the allergic reaction. = 药物对抗过敏反应。
He counteracts negativity with optimism. = 他以乐观对抗消极情绪。


一般过去时:
She counteracted the rumor by releasing a statement. = 她发表声明来反驳谣言。
The team counteracted their opponent's strategy. = 该团队对抗了对手的策略。


现在完成时:
They have counteracted the spread of misinformation. = 他们已经阻止了虚假信息的传播。
We have counteracted the negative impact of the storm. = 我们已经抵消了风暴的负面影响。


过去完成时:
She had counteracted the effects of the stress before the presentation. = 在演示之前，她已经抵消了压力的影响。
He had counteracted the initial setback with determination. = 他已经以决心克服了最初的挫折。


将来时:
We will counteract the potential risks with careful planning. = 我们将通过周密的计划来应对潜在的风险。
The company will counteract the competitor's new product. = 公司将对抗竞争对手的新产品。


虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, we could have counteracted the damage. = 如果我们早点采取行动，我们就能减轻损失。
If they counteracted the problem, everything would be fine. = 如果他们解决了这个问题，一切都会好起来的。


被动语态:
The effects of the poison were counteracted by the antidote. = 毒药的作用被解毒剂抵消了。
The negative trends were counteracted by government intervention. = 负面趋势被政府干预所抵消。


疑问句:
How can we counteract the negative effects of climate change? = 我们如何才能抵消气候变化的负面影响？
What measures will be taken to counteract inflation? = 将采取什么措施来对抗通货膨胀？


否定句:
The treatment did not counteract the disease. = 治疗没有对抗疾病。
Their efforts did not counteract the damage caused by the flood. = 他们的努力没有抵消洪水造成的损失。


PHRASE->
counteract the effects of = 抵消...的影响
counteract a trend = 逆转趋势
counteract the impact of = 抵消...的影响
counteract negative feelings = 克服负面情绪
counteract each other = 相互抵消
