sealifts:/ˈsiːlɪfts/| n.| 海上运输；海运；海运物资 | The act of transporting goods or people by sea, especially large-scale operations such as the movement of troops or supplies during wartime.  It refers to a large-scale operation to move people or goods by sea. | 例句：The sealift of troops to the island was a complex and dangerous operation. 海上向岛屿运送军队是一项复杂而危险的行动。

The massive sealift supported the humanitarian aid effort.  大规模的海上运输行动支援了人道主义援助工作。|近义词：maritime transport, sea transport, shipment |反义词：land transport, airlift
