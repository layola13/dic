donor:/ˈdoʊnər/| n. |捐赠者，捐助者，献血者|A person or organization that makes a donation of money, goods, or organs.  A donor can be an individual, a corporation, or a charitable foundation.  They contribute to a cause, an organization, or an individual in need. |Example: He is a generous donor to the hospital. 他是医院慷慨的捐赠者。  The blood donor program needs more volunteers. 献血计划需要更多志愿者。|近义词： contributor, giver, benefactor |反义词： recipient, receiver

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The donor gives generously to charity. = 捐赠者慷慨地捐赠给慈善机构。
She donates blood regularly. = 她定期献血。

一般过去时:
He donated a large sum of money to the university. = 他向大学捐赠了一大笔钱。
The company donated computers to the school. = 公司向学校捐赠了电脑。

现在完成时:
They have donated over $1 million to the cause. = 他们已经向该事业捐赠了超过100万美元。
She has been a loyal donor for many years. = 她多年来一直是忠实的捐赠者。

过去完成时:
He had already donated before the appeal was launched. = 在发起呼吁之前，他已经捐款了。
The organization had received donations from various sources. = 该组织已收到来自各种来源的捐款。

将来时:
She will donate her time to the project next year. = 她明年将把时间捐献给这个项目。
They will donate their old clothes to charity. = 他们将把旧衣服捐给慈善机构。

虚拟语气:
If I were a wealthy donor, I would support this research. = 如果我是一个富有的捐赠者，我会支持这项研究。
I wish I could be a donor, but I don't have the means. = 我希望我能成为捐赠者，但我没有能力。

被动语态:
The hospital received a large donation from an anonymous donor. = 医院收到一位匿名捐赠者的巨额捐款。
The funds were donated to various environmental charities. = 资金被捐赠给各种环保慈善机构。

疑问句:
Who is the main donor for this project? = 这个项目的捐助者是谁？
Are you a regular blood donor? = 你是定期献血者吗？

否定句:
He did not donate to the campaign. = 他没有向竞选捐款。
She has not yet decided whether to become a donor. = 她尚未决定是否成为捐赠者。
