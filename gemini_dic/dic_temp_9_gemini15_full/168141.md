pro-Belgian: /ˌproʊˈbɛldʒən/ | adj. | 亲比利时的；支持比利时的 |  Supporting or favoring Belgium or its people.  This term is used to describe individuals, policies, or movements that express positive sentiment towards Belgium.  It can refer to political stances, cultural appreciation, or economic alliances.


例句：

He holds pro-Belgian views on trade agreements. = 他对贸易协定持有亲比利时的观点。

The pro-Belgian lobby group advocated for closer ties with the EU. = 亲比利时的游说团体倡导与欧盟建立更紧密的联系。


近义词：Belgian-friendly, pro-Belgian-friendly


反义词：anti-Belgian


词性：形容词
