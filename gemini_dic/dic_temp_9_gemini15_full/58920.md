/dʌst/ | n. | 灰尘，尘土 | Dust is fine particles of dirt or earth.  It often accumulates on surfaces. | The furniture was covered in a thick layer of dust.  家具上覆盖着一层厚厚的灰尘。|dirt, grime, powder |cleanliness, purity| 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Dust settles on everything. = 灰尘落在任何东西上。
The wind blows dust across the desert. = 风把尘土吹过沙漠。

一般过去时:
He dusted the furniture. = 他擦拭了家具。
She dusted the shelves yesterday. = 她昨天擦了架子上的灰尘。

现在完成时:
We have dusted the entire house. = 我们已经打扫了整栋房子。
They have not dusted the attic yet. = 他们还没有打扫阁楼。


过去完成时:
I had dusted the room before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前我已经打扫了房间。
She had already dusted the table when he spilled the drink. = 他洒了饮料的时候，她已经擦过桌子了。

将来时:
I will dust the living room tomorrow. = 我明天将打扫客厅。
They will dust the car before the show. = 他们会在演出前擦拭汽车。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would dust the shelves. = 如果我有时间，我会擦拭架子。
I wish I had dusted the room earlier. = 我真希望我早点打扫房间。

被动语态:
The furniture is dusted regularly. = 家具定期打扫。
The room was dusted before the party. = 派对前房间被打扫了。


疑问句:
Is there dust on the floor? = 地上有灰尘吗？
Has the car been dusted? = 汽车被擦拭了吗？


否定句:
There is no dust in this room. = 这个房间里没有灰尘。
He didn't dust the table. = 他没有擦桌子。


PHRASE->
dust bunny = 灰尘球

dust storm = 沙尘暴
a speck of dust = 一粒灰尘
to bite the dust = 失败；死亡
