lumpsucker:/ˈlʌmpsʌkər/| n. |隆头鱼，圆头鱼 |A lumpsucker is a type of marine fish known for its round, plump body and sucker-like pelvic fins.  They are typically found in cold, northern waters. |例句：The lumpsucker, with its distinctive suction cup, clings to rocks in the strong currents. = 隆头鱼凭借其独特的吸盘，在强劲的水流中紧紧依附在岩石上。

例句：Divers observed several lumpsuckers attached to kelp forests. = 潜水员观察到几条隆头鱼附着在海带林上。


近义词：sea squirt (指其吸附能力),  cyclopteridae (科属名)
反义词：无
词性：名词
