electric blankets:/ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈblæŋkɪts/| n.|电热毯|An electric blanket is a blanket containing electric heating elements that is used to warm a bed.  It is usually controlled by a thermostat to regulate the temperature.|例句：I turned on the electric blanket to warm the bed before going to sleep. 我睡觉前打开电热毯暖床。|例句：The electric blanket is too hot; I need to turn down the temperature.  电热毯太热了；我需要调低温度。|近义词：heated blanket|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a regular blanket.|名词
