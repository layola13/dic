grills:/ɡrɪlz/ | n., v. | 烤架；烤；严厉盘问 |

英文解析:
Noun: A grill is a device used for cooking food over hot coals or gas, typically consisting of a metal grate over a heat source.  It can also refer to a framework of bars, typically metal, used for protection or decoration.

Verb: To grill means to cook food on a grill. It can also mean to question someone intensely and aggressively.

例句:
Noun: We grilled some burgers on the barbecue. = 我们在烧烤架上烤了一些汉堡包。

Noun: The windows were protected by strong security grills. = 窗户被坚固的安全格栅保护着。


Verb:  He grilled the steak until it was perfectly cooked. = 他把牛排烤到刚好熟。

Verb: The police grilled the suspect for hours. = 警方严厉盘问了嫌疑犯数小时。
