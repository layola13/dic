fishcake:/ˈfɪʃkeɪk/| n. |鱼饼|A fishcake is a patty or cake made from fish flesh, often combined with potato, breadcrumbs, and spices, then fried or baked.  It's a common dish in many parts of the world, with variations in ingredients and preparation methods. |例句：I bought some fishcakes from the fishmonger for dinner. 我从鱼贩那里买了些鱼饼当晚餐。

例句：The fishcakes were delicious, crispy on the outside and soft on the inside.  这些鱼饼很好吃，外酥里嫩。


近义词：fish patty, fish fritter|反义词：无
