non-appreciation:/ˌnɒnəˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən/| n. |不欣赏；不赞赏；缺乏感激|The lack of recognition or valuing of something or someone.  It can refer to a failure to understand or appreciate the value or significance of something, or a lack of gratitude for something received.  |例句：His non-appreciation of her efforts led to a breakdown in their relationship.  他的不欣赏她的努力导致了他们关系的破裂。
His non-appreciation for classical music was evident in his bored expression. 他对古典音乐的漠不关心在他的无聊表情中显而易见。|近义词：disregard, undervaluation, ingratitude, unthankfulness |反义词：appreciation, gratitude, recognition, valuing |名词
