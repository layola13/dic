nonthermoplastics:/ˌnɒnθɜːrməʊˈplæstɪks/| n.|热固性塑料|Materials that, once molded, cannot be remelted and reshaped without chemical decomposition.  These plastics undergo irreversible chemical changes during the curing process, forming a rigid, cross-linked structure.|Example: Epoxy resins are examples of nonthermoplastics used in adhesives and coatings. 环氧树脂是热固性塑料的例子，用于粘合剂和涂料中。|近义词：thermosets, thermosetting plastics|反义词：thermoplastics
