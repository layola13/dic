outbelched:/ˌaʊtˈbɛltʃt/| v. |大声地呕吐出来；大声地喷出| to expel (something) forcefully from the mouth or lungs, often with a loud noise.  The word combines "out" (away from the inside) and "belch" (to eructate or expel gas from the stomach).|例句：The volcano outbelched a plume of ash and smoke.  火山喷出了大量的火山灰和烟雾。


The whale outbelched a torrent of water.  鲸鱼喷出了一股水流。|近义词：vomit, eject, spew, expel|反义词：inhale, swallow, absorb|动词
