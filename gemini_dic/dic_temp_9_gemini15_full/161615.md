poulp:/pulp/| n.|章鱼；果肉；纸浆|A mollusk with a soft body, eight arms, and usually a sucker on each arm.|例句：The octopus is also known as a poulp. 章鱼也被称为章鱼。|例句：The fruit pulp is sweet and juicy. 水果果肉甜美多汁。|近义词：octopus, squid, mush|反义词：shell, whole fruit
