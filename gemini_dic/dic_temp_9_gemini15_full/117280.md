mechanizers:/ˈmɛkənaɪzərz/| n.|机械化者；机械制造者|People or things that mechanize something.  Those who introduce or utilize machinery in a process or system. |例句：The mechanizers of the factory implemented new robotic arms to increase production. 工厂的机械化者们引进了新的机械臂以提高产量。


例句：The early mechanizers of agriculture revolutionized farming practices.  农业早期的机械化者们彻底改变了农业耕作方式。


近义词：automaters, industrialists, innovators


反义词：manual laborers, traditionalists


词性：名词
