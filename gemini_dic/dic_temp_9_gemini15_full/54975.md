disproportionate: /ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːʃənət/ | adj. |不成比例的，不相称的 |  Not proportionate; out of proportion.  It implies a lack of balance or fairness, often suggesting an unfair or excessive emphasis on one aspect compared to others. | 例句：The punishment was disproportionate to the crime.  惩罚与罪行不成比例。

The company's profits were disproportionate to its size. 这家公司的利润与其规模不成比例。


近义词：unbalanced, uneven, unequal, excessive, undue, immoderate | 反义词：proportionate, balanced, equal, fair


词源：dis- (否定前缀) + proportionate (成比例的)  Proportionate itself comes from the Latin "proportio," meaning "proportion," which is composed of "pro" (for, in favor of) and "portio" (part, share).

虚拟语气:
