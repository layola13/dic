menacer:/məˈneɪsər/ | n. |威胁者，危险人物 | A person or thing that causes or is likely to cause danger, trouble, or harm.  |例句：The escaped convict was a menacer to the community.  逃犯对社区构成威胁。 |例句：That snarling dog is a real menacer. 那条咆哮的狗真是个危险人物。|近义词：threat, danger, hazard, menace |反义词：protector, safeguard, benefactor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bully menaces his classmates daily. = 那个恶霸每天都威胁他的同学们。
That wild animal menaces the village. = 那只野生动物威胁着村庄。


一般过去时:
The storm menaced the coastal towns. = 暴风雨威胁着沿海城镇。
The thief menaced the shopkeeper with a knife. = 小偷用刀威胁店主。


现在完成时:
The ongoing drought has menaced the farmers' crops. = 持续的干旱已经威胁到农民的庄稼。
His reckless behavior has menaced his career. = 他鲁莽的行为已经威胁到他的事业。


过去完成时:
The looming war had menaced the nation for years. = 迫在眉睫的战争多年来一直威胁着国家。
The old bridge had menaced travelers for decades before collapsing. = 那座旧桥在倒塌前几十年来一直威胁着旅行者。


将来时:
The approaching hurricane will menace the island. = 即将来临的飓风将威胁该岛屿。
Rising sea levels will menace coastal cities. = 海平面上升将威胁沿海城市。


虚拟语气:
If the government hadn't acted, the crisis would have menaced the entire country. = 如果政府没有采取行动，这场危机就会威胁到全国。
I wish that the disease didn't menace so many people. = 我希望这种疾病不会威胁到这么多人。


被动语态:
The city was menaced by the rising floodwaters. = 城市受到上涨的洪水威胁。
The villagers were menaced by the wild animals. = 村民们受到野生动物的威胁。


疑问句:
Does the unstable situation menace the peace talks? = 不稳定的局势是否威胁着和平谈判？
What menaces the survival of the species? = 什么威胁着该物种的生存？


否定句:
The situation doesn't menace us at present. = 目前这种情况并不威胁我们。
The new security measures will ensure that the building isn't menaced. = 新的安全措施将确保该建筑不会受到威胁。
