intactness:/ɪnˈtæktnəs/ | n. |完整性；完好无损的状态 |The intactness of the ancient artifact was remarkable.  古代文物的完整性令人惊叹。|近义词：completeness, wholeness, preservation |反义词：damage, destruction, breakage


英文解析:The state of being complete or undamaged; the condition of being untouched or unimpaired.  完整或未损坏的状态；未受触动或未受损害的状态。


例句:

The intactness of the ecosystem is crucial for biodiversity. 生态系统的完整性对生物多样性至关重要。

The surgeon was pleased with the intactness of the patient's nerve endings after the operation.  手术后，外科医生对患者神经末梢的完整性感到满意。
