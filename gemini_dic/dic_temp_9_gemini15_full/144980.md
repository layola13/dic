overachieve: /ˌoʊvərəˈtʃiːv/ | v. | 超额完成；超出预期 | To achieve more than was expected or considered possible.  |例句：He overachieved in his studies, earning a scholarship to a top university. 他在学业上取得了超出预期的成就，获得了顶尖大学的奖学金。

例句：The team overachieved this season, winning more games than anyone predicted. 这个赛季球队超额完成了目标，赢得了比任何人预测的都多的比赛。


近义词：excel, surpass, exceed, outperform | 反义词：underachieve, fail, underperform
