electroplaters:/ɪˌlɛkˌtroʊˈpleɪtərz/| n. |电镀工；电镀厂|People or businesses that perform electroplating, a process of depositing a thin layer of metal onto another metal object using an electric current.  Electroplaters use various techniques and chemicals to achieve different finishes and enhance the properties of the base metal. |The electroplaters finished the job ahead of schedule. 电镀工提前完成了工作。 The company hired several experienced electroplaters to increase production.  这家公司聘请了几名经验丰富的电镀工来提高产量。|metal finishers, platers| (无明确反义词，可考虑与不做电镀的行业对比，例如：fabricators, machinists)|名词
