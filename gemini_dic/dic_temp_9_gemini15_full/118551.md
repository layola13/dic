mesonotum:/ˌmɛzəˈnoʊtəm/| n. |中胸背板|The mesonotum is the dorsal sclerite of the mesothorax, the middle segment of the thorax of an insect.  It's a hard, chitinous plate that forms the upper surface of the mesothorax, often bearing wings and legs.  The mesonotum is crucial for insect locomotion and flight.|The dragonfly's powerful mesonotum supports its strong flight muscles.|近义词:mesothorax (partially), notum|反义词:sternum
