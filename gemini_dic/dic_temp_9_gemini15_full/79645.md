glossitis:/ɡlɒˈsaɪtɪs/ | n. |舌炎|Inflammation of the tongue.  It can be caused by various factors, including infections, nutritional deficiencies, allergies, and trauma. Symptoms may include pain, swelling, redness, and changes in the tongue's appearance. |The doctor diagnosed her with glossitis after examining her inflamed tongue.|近义词：tongue inflammation|反义词：healthy tongue
