colonelship:/ˈkɜːnəlʃɪp/| n. |上校军衔；上校职位|The rank and position of a colonel in the army.  It refers to the status, authority, and responsibilities associated with being a colonel.  The term can also sometimes refer to the period of time someone holds this rank.|例句：He achieved the colonelship after many years of dedicated service. 他在多年的奉献服务后获得了上校军衔。

例句：The responsibilities of the colonelship include commanding a regiment. 上校的职责包括指挥一个团。


近义词：command, rank (in this specific context)


反义词：privateship (a less formal and less precise antonym, implying a lower rank)


词性:名词
