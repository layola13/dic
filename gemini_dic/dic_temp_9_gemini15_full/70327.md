field-workers:/ˈfiːldˌwɜːrkərz/ | n. | 田间工人；外勤人员 | People who work outdoors, often in agriculture or related fields.  They are involved in practical tasks rather than office-based work.  | Example: The field-workers harvested the wheat before the storm. 田间工人暴风雨前收割了小麦。 The field-workers collected data on the local bird population. 外勤人员收集了当地鸟类种群的数据。 |近义词：field hands, outdoor workers, researchers (in some contexts) |反义词：office workers, desk workers |名词
