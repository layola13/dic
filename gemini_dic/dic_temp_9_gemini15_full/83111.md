gustavo:/ɡʊˈstɑːvoʊ/|/ˈɡʌstɑːvoʊ/| Proper Noun |古斯塔沃（男子名） | A masculine given name of Spanish origin, ultimately derived from the Germanic name "Wulfgar".  It combines elements meaning "wolf" and "spear". |My friend Gustavo is a talented musician.  我的朋友古斯塔沃是一位才华横溢的音乐家。


The name Gustavo is of Germanic origin, ultimately deriving from the name Wulfgar.  Wulfgar is composed of two Old High German elements: *wulf* meaning "wolf" and *gar* meaning "spear".  Therefore, the name Gustavo literally translates to "wolf spear".  The name's popularity spread through its adoption in Spain and other Spanish-speaking countries.
