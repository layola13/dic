propelling: /prəˈpɛlɪŋ/ | v. | 推进；驱动；激励 | Driving forward; causing to move; motivating. |


例句：

The engine propels the car forward. = 发动机推动汽车前进。

His ambition propelled him to great success. = 他的雄心壮志驱使他获得了巨大的成功。


近义词：driving, pushing, motivating, impelling

反义词：restraining, hindering, impeding


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The wind propels the sailboat across the lake. = 风推动帆船穿过湖面。
The rocket propels itself into space. = 火箭自身推进进入太空。


一般过去时:
The strong current propelled the raft downstream. = 强劲的水流把木筏冲向下游。
The film propelled her to stardom. = 这部电影使她成名。


现在完成时:
The company's new marketing strategy has propelled its growth. = 公司新的营销策略推动了它的增长。
His dedication has propelled him to the top of his field. = 他的奉献精神促使他成为该领域的佼佼者。


过去完成时:
The accumulated pressure had propelled the situation to a crisis. = 累积的压力已将局势推向了危机。
Years of hard work had propelled him to his current position. = 多年的辛勤工作使他达到了现在的职位。


将来时:
The new technology will propel the industry forward. = 新技术将推动该行业向前发展。
This invention will propel us into a new era of space exploration. = 这项发明将把我们带入一个新的太空探索时代。


虚拟语气:
If the engine were more powerful, it would propel the car faster. = 如果发动机更强劲，它就能推动汽车更快地行驶。
If only his passion had propelled him earlier, he would have achieved more. = 如果他的热情早点推动他，他会取得更大的成就。


被动语态:
The boat was propelled by the wind. = 船被风推动。
The spaceship is propelled by powerful rockets. = 宇宙飞船由强大的火箭推进。


疑问句:
What propels the ship? = 什么推动船只？
What is propelling this rapid economic growth? = 什么在推动这种迅速的经济增长？


否定句:
The weak engine couldn't propel the car uphill. = 弱小的发动机无法推动汽车上坡。
His lack of motivation didn't propel him to finish the project. = 他缺乏动力，没有推动他完成项目。


PHRASE->

propel forward = 推动前进
propel oneself = 自我驱动
propel change = 推动变革
propel growth = 推动增长
propel innovation = 推动创新


词源:

From Old French *propeler, from Latin propellere, from pro- ("forward") + pellere ("to drive, push").
