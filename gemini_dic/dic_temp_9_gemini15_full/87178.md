herders:/ˈhɜːrdərz/| n.|牧羊人，牧民|People who herd livestock, such as sheep, cattle, or goats.  They are responsible for the care and management of the animals, often moving them from place to place to find pasture.|Example: The herders led their flocks to greener pastures. 牧羊人带领他们的羊群到更绿的牧场。 The nomadic herders moved their yaks across the vast plains. 游牧民族的牧民们将他们的牦牛赶过广阔的平原。|近义词：shepherds, herdsmen, stockmen|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with farmers who cultivate land.
