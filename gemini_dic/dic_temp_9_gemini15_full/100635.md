intolerable:/ɪnˈtɑːlərebl/|/ɪnˈtɒlərəbl/| adj.|无法忍受的，不能容忍的|extremely unpleasant; unacceptable.  The pain was intolerable.  The heat was intolerable.|例句：The noise was intolerable; I had to leave.  这噪音太大了，我不得不离开。

例句：The conditions in the prison were intolerable.  监狱里的条件令人无法忍受。


近义词：unbearable, insufferable, unbearable, unacceptable

反义词：tolerable, bearable, acceptable

词性：形容词
