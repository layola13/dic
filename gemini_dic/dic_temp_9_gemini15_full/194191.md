self-kindness:/ˈsɛlf ˈkaɪndnəs/| n. |善待自己，自我慈悲|The act of being kind and compassionate towards oneself, especially during difficult times.  It involves treating oneself with the same understanding, care, and acceptance one would offer a close friend.  This is in contrast to self-criticism and harsh self-judgment.|例句：Practicing self-kindness can help reduce stress and improve mental well-being.  练习善待自己可以帮助减轻压力，改善心理健康。

例句：She showed herself great self-kindness after the breakup, allowing herself time to heal.  分手后，她善待自己，给自己时间疗伤。|近义词：self-compassion, self-acceptance, self-love|反义词：self-criticism, self-hate, self-flagellation|名词
