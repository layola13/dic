crafts:/kræfts/ | n. |工艺，手艺；手工艺品 | The skillful activity of making things by hand.  It involves dexterity and creativity, often using traditional techniques and materials. |例句：She is skilled in various crafts, such as knitting and pottery. 她擅长各种工艺，例如编织和陶艺。

例句：The museum showcases local crafts from the region. 这个博物馆展出了该地区的当地工艺品。


近义词：handicrafts, trades, arts | 反义词：mass production, industrial manufacturing | 词性: 名词

词源:  The word "craft" comes from the Old English word *cræft*, meaning "strength," "power," "skill," and "art."  It's related to the German *Kraft* (strength, power).  The meaning evolved over time to its modern sense of skillful work, especially with the hands.
