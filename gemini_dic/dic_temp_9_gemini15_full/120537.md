mir: /mɪr/ | n. | 沼泽；泥潭；困境 | A mir is a bog or marsh, often implying a difficult or dangerous place to traverse.  It can also be used metaphorically to describe a difficult situation or predicament. |  例句：He got stuck in the mir and had to be rescued. 他陷入了泥潭，不得不被营救。 | 近义词：bog, marsh, swamp, quagmire | 反义词：dry land, solid ground | 名词
