pastoral:/ˈpæstərəl/| adj.|田园的；牧歌的；乡村的| relating to shepherds or rural life; idyllically simple;  characterized by or suggesting the tranquility of rural life.  |例句：The painting depicts a pastoral scene of sheep grazing in a green meadow. 这幅画描绘了羊群在绿草地上吃草的田园景色。 |例句：She enjoys the pastoral simplicity of life in the countryside. 她喜欢乡村生活的田园般质朴。|近义词：rural, rustic, idyllic, bucolic|反义词：urban, metropolitan, industrial
