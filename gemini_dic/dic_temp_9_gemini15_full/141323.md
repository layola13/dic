opener:/ˈoʊpnər/ | n. | 开场白；开瓶器；启动器；先锋 | A person or thing that opens something or begins something.  It can refer to the first act, remark, or event that initiates something.  Also, a device used to open something, such as a bottle. |例句：His joke was a great opener for the speech. 他的笑话是演讲的一个很好的开场白。
The waiter brought a bottle opener. 服务员拿来了一个开瓶器。


近义词：initiator, starter, beginning, introduction,  (for bottle) corkscrew
反义词：closer, conclusion, ending
