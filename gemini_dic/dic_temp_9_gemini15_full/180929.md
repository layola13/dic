reignition:/ˌriːɪɡˈnɪʃən/| n. |重新点燃；复燃；再次点火|The act of igniting something again; the process of restarting a fire or engine after it has been extinguished or stopped.  It can also refer to the renewed interest or activity in something.|例句：The pilot attempted reignition of the engine after the emergency landing.  飞行员在紧急着陆后试图重新启动发动机。

例句：The reignition of the debate on climate change is crucial.  关于气候变化的辩论重新燃起至关重要。

近义词：rekindling, restarting, relighting

反义词：extinction, cessation, termination
