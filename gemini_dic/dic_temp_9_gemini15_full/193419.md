seize:/siːz/| vt. |抓住；夺取；控制；没收；（疾病）突然发作|to take hold of something suddenly and forcefully; to take control of a place or situation suddenly and forcefully; to take something away from someone, especially by legal authority; (of a disease or feeling) to suddenly affect someone|例句：The police seized the illegal drugs. 警方查获了这些非法毒品。

例句：The rebels seized control of the capital.  叛军夺取了首都的控制权。

近义词：grab, snatch, capture, confiscate, commandeer |反义词：release, relinquish, liberate
