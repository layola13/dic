flakes:/fleɪks/| n.|薄片；雪花；片状物|Small, flat, thin pieces of something, often produced by breaking or separating a larger mass.  For example, flakes of snow, flakes of paint, or flakes of skin. |例句：Snowflakes fell softly on the ground.  雪花轻轻地飘落在地上。
The paint was peeling off in flakes. 油漆正成片状脱落。|近义词：bits, pieces, scales|反义词：lump, mass
