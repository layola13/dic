raceway:/ˈreɪsweɪ/| n. |赛道；沟槽；线槽 | A raceway is a channel or groove designed for something to move or be contained within, especially in the context of racing, electrical wiring, or water.  It can refer to a track for racing, a trough for cables, or a channel for water. | Example: The race car sped around the raceway. 赛车在赛道上飞驰。  The electrician ran the wires through the raceway. 电工将电线穿过线槽。 |近义词：track, channel, conduit, gutter |反义词：None specifically, depends on context (e.g., obstacle if referring to a race track).
