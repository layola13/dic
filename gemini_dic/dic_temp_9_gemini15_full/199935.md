sizzler:/ˈsɪzələr/| n.|滋滋作响的东西；极好的人或事物；非常热的东西|A sizzler is something that makes a sizzling sound, often referring to food cooking. It can also refer to something or someone exceptionally good or exciting, or something extremely hot, like weather.|例句：The steak was a real sizzler on the grill.  烤架上的牛排滋滋作响，真香。

The new restaurant is a real sizzler; it's always packed. 这家新餐厅很棒，总是挤满了人。


近义词：crackerjack, hotshot, scorcher|反义词：dud, flop, disappointment
