houseboats:/ˈhaʊsboʊts/| n.|水上房屋，船屋|A houseboat is a boat that has been designed or modified to be used as a dwelling, typically moored in a marina or on a waterway.|例句：They live on a houseboat in the canals of Amsterdam. 他们在阿姆斯特丹的运河上住在一艘船屋里。  例句：Many tourists enjoy renting houseboats for a unique vacation experience. 许多游客喜欢租用船屋来获得独特的度假体验。|近义词：floating home, house barge|反义词：land house, terrestrial dwelling
