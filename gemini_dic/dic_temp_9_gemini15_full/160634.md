ported:/ˈpɔːrtɪd/ | adj. | 搬运的；携带的；运输的 |  Transferred or conveyed; carried.  It often refers to something that has been moved or transported, especially in a specific manner or to a specific location. | 例句：The ported goods arrived safely. 运输的货物安全到达了。  The ported data is now available for analysis.  已导入的数据现可供分析。|近义词：transferred, conveyed, transported, carried | 反义词：stationary, immobile


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company ports data to a secure server. = 公司将数据传输到安全的服务器。
They port the application to different operating systems. = 他们将应用程序移植到不同的操作系统。


一般过去时:
He ported the wine from France. = 他从法国运来了葡萄酒。
The company ported the software to the new platform. = 公司将软件移植到新的平台。


现在完成时:
We have ported all the necessary files. = 我们已经传输了所有必要的文档。
They have ported the game to mobile devices. = 他们已将游戏移植到移动设备。


过去完成时:

She had ported the documents to the cloud before the deadline. = 她在截止日期之前已将文件上传到云端。
