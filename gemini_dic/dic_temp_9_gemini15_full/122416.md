mo's:/moʊz/| n.|莫氏；莫斯（姓氏所有格）|Mo's is a possessive form of the surname "Mo," indicating something belonging to someone named Mo.  It can also refer to a place named Mo's.| 例句：This is Mo's house. 这是莫的家。|近义词：Mo's possession, Mo's property|反义词：无


USAGE->
(This word, being a possessive form, doesn't have verb conjugations or other grammatical tenses like "what" would.  The usage is determined by the noun it modifies)

Mo's car is red. = 莫的车是红色的。
Mo's dog is barking. = 莫的狗在叫。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:
Mo's assignment had already been submitted. = 莫的作业已经提交了。

将来时:
