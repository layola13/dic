pilots:/ˈpaɪləts/| n. v.|飞行员；领航员；试点；试验|A pilot is a person who flies an aircraft.  Pilots require extensive training and skill.  The word can also refer to a person who steers a ship or boat, or to a test run or trial of something new.|例句：The pilots landed the plane safely despite the storm.  飞行员们尽管暴风雨，还是安全地降落了飞机。

The company is running pilot programs for their new software. 公司正在为他们的新软件运行试点程序。|近义词：aviator, airman, navigator, test, trial|反义词：passenger, observer|名词, 动词

He pilots the plane skillfully. 他熟练地驾驶飞机。

一般过去时:

The experienced pilot piloted the aircraft safely to the destination. 经验丰富的飞行员安全地将飞机驾驶到目的地。


现在完成时:
He has piloted many different types of aircraft. 他已经驾驶过许多不同类型的飞机。

过去完成时:
They had piloted the plane for hours before the engine failed. 他们的飞机引擎故障前已经飞行数小时了。
Before the incident, he had piloted the helicopter safely for years. 事故发生前，他安全地驾驶直升机多年。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
