dosh:/dɒʃ/| n. |钱，现金|Informal British English term for money, especially cash.  It is a slang term and should not be used in formal settings.|例句：He flashed his dosh around to impress everyone. 他炫耀他的钱来给所有人留下深刻印象。


例句：I need some dosh to buy a new phone. 我需要一些钱买一部新手机。


近义词：money, cash, loot, readies, funds|反义词：debt, poverty


词性：名词
