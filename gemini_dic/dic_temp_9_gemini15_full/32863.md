The term's origin and usage are complex and highly controversial due to its historical association with negative stereotypes and gang culture. While some within the community reclaim the term, it is generally considered offensive when used by outsiders.  The term's meaning and connotations vary greatly depending on context and speaker.|Example: The term "cholo" has a complicated history and should be used with caution.
