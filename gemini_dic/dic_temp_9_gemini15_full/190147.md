sanitises:/ˈsænɪtaɪzɪz/| v.|消毒；净化|To make something clean and free from bacteria or other germs.|例句：The nurse sanitises her hands before examining the patient. 护士在检查病人之前先消毒了双手。  The kitchen needs to be thoroughly sanitised. 厨房需要彻底消毒。|近义词：sterilize, disinfect, clean|反义词：contaminate, pollute|动词


USAGE->
