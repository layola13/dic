shauri:/ˈʃaʊri/| n.| 咨询，商议，问题，难题 | Swahili for "consult, discuss, problem, matter".  It refers to a process of seeking advice, deliberation, or a situation requiring attention or resolution. | Example:  Tulifanya shauri kuhusu safari. = We had a discussion about the journey. |近义词：consultation, discussion, deliberation, issue, problem |反义词：decision, resolution (in the sense of a solved problem)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee is holding a shauri. = 委员会正在举行一次商议。
We are having a shauri about the budget. = 我们正在商讨预算。


一般过去时:
They had a shauri yesterday. = 他们昨天进行了一次商议。
We held a shauri to solve the problem. = 我们举行了一次商议来解决这个问题。


现在完成时:
They have had many shauri this week. = 他们本周已经进行了许多商议。
We have finished the shauri. = 我们已经完成了商议。


过去完成时:
They had already had a shauri before the meeting. = 会议之前他们已经进行过商议了。
The issue had been a shauri for months. = 这个问题已经是一个难题好几个月了。


将来时:
We will have a shauri tomorrow. = 我们明天将进行一次商议。
They will hold a shauri next week. = 他们下周将举行一次商议。


虚拟语气:
If we had had a shauri earlier, we could have avoided the problem. = 如果我们早点商议的话，就能避免这个问题了。
I wish we had a shauri before making the decision. = 我希望我们在做决定之前进行过商议。


被动语态:
The problem is being discussed in a shauri. = 这个问题正在商议中。
A shauri was held to address the concerns. = 举行了一次商议来解决这些担忧。


疑问句:
What is the shauri? = 问题是什么？
Do you have a shauri? = 你有问题吗？


否定句:
We did not have a shauri about that. = 我们没有就此进行商议。
There was no shauri about the plan. = 关于这个计划没有商议。



PHRASE->
kufanya shauri = to have a discussion/consultation
kutoa shauri = to give advice/consultation
kupata shauri = to get advice/consultation
shauri la haraka = urgent matter
shauri kubwa = big problem
