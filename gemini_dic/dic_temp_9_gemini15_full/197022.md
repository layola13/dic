shalelike:/ˈʃeɪlˌlaɪk/| adj.|页岩状的，似页岩的|Resembling shale in appearance or texture.  It is often used to describe rocks or other materials that have a similar layered, fissile, or finely grained structure to shale.|The sedimentary rock formation exhibited a shalelike structure, easily splitting into thin layers.  页岩状的沉积岩层易于分裂成薄层。|The clay deposit had a shalelike consistency.  粘土矿床具有页岩状的稠度。|claylike, fissile|granitic, massive
