priapism:/ˈpraɪəpɪzəm/| n. |阴茎持续勃起|A persistent, usually painful erection of the penis that is not associated with sexual arousal.  This condition can be caused by various factors, including injury, certain medications, blood disorders, and neurological conditions.  Untreated priapism can lead to permanent erectile dysfunction. |The patient presented with priapism lasting for over four hours.  这位病人出现了持续四个多小时的阴茎持续勃起。
