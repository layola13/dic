dipyridine:/ˌdaɪˈpɪrɪdiːn/| n. |二吡啶|Dipyridine refers to any of several organic compounds containing two pyridine rings.  These rings can be connected in various ways, leading to different isomers and properties.  They are often used as ligands in coordination chemistry. |Example:  The synthesis of novel dipyridine ligands for catalysis is an active area of research. 二吡啶用于催化的新型配体的合成是一个活跃的研究领域。 |近义词:bipyridine (often used interchangeably, but dipyridine is a broader term)|反义词:|无
