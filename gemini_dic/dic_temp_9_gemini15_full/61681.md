词源:
The name "Emmys" is a shortened form of "Primetime Emmy Awards," named after the statuette given to the winners, which is shaped like a winged woman holding an atom.  The name's origin is not definitively established but is possibly a reference to the television industry's early days and the use of the word "Emmy" as a nickname within the industry.
