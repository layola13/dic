factory:/ˈfæktəri/ | n. | 工厂，制造厂 | A building or group of buildings where goods are manufactured or assembled chiefly by machine.  A factory is a place where things are made on a large scale, often using machinery. | Example:  The car factory employs thousands of workers. 这家汽车厂雇佣了数千名工人。

Example:  Many textile factories are located in this region.  这个地区有很多纺织厂。


近义词：plant, mill, works, manufactory
反义词：None (no direct opposite)


词性: 名词
