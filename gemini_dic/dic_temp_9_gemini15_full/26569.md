cabling:/ˈkeɪblɪŋ/| n.|布线；电缆敷设|The process of installing and connecting cables.  It encompasses the design, routing, and installation of cables for various purposes, such as data transmission, power distribution, and telecommunications. |例句：The cabling in this building is outdated and needs replacing. 这栋楼里的布线已经过时，需要更换。

例句：Proper cabling is crucial for a stable network connection. 正确的布线对于稳定的网络连接至关重要。


近义词：wiring, wiring installation, cable installation|反义词：None
