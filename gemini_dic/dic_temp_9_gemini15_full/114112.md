magnums:/ˈmæɡnəm/| n.|大瓶酒；杰作；大型手枪|A magnum is a large bottle of wine, typically holding 1.5 liters. It can also refer to a large-caliber handgun or a work of exceptional quality or size.|例句：He brought a magnum of champagne to the party. 他带了一瓶香槟酒去参加派对。  The director's latest film is considered a magnum opus.  这位导演的最新电影被认为是杰作。|近义词：bottle (for wine), masterpiece, classic|反义词：miniature, small-scale work
