praisement:/ˈpreɪzmənt/| n. |赞扬，称赞 |The act of praising or extolling; commendation.  It is less common than "praise".|例句：Her praisement of his work was heartfelt. = 她对他的作品给予了衷心的赞扬。


例句：The praisement he received boosted his confidence. = 他收到的赞扬增强了他的信心。


近义词：praise, commendation, acclaim, applause |反义词：criticism, condemnation, censure |名词
