invertase:/ˈɪnvɜːrteɪs/| n. |转化酶，蔗糖酶|An enzyme that catalyzes the hydrolysis of sucrose into glucose and fructose.  It's widely used in the food industry.|例句：Invertase is added to candies to create a creamy texture. 转化酶添加到糖果中以产生奶油状质地。|例句：The yeast produces invertase, which breaks down sucrose into simpler sugars.|酵母菌产生转化酶，它将蔗糖分解成更简单的糖。|近义词：sucrase|反义词：无
