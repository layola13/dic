nurse: /nɜːrs/| n. v.|护士，保育员；照料，护理|A person trained to care for the sick or injured, especially in a hospital.|例句：She works as a nurse in a children's hospital. 她在一所儿童医院当护士。|例句：The mother carefully nursed her sick child. 母亲细心地照料她生病的孩子。|近义词：attendant, caregiver, caretaker|反义词：patient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She nurses the baby. = 她护理婴儿。
The nurse cares for the patients. = 护士照顾病人。


一般过去时:
She nursed her sick mother. = 她护理她生病的母亲。
The nurse cared for the injured man. = 护士照顾受伤的男子。


现在完成时:
She has nursed many patients. = 她护理过许多病人。
The nurse has cared for hundreds of patients. = 护士已经照顾了数百名病人。


过去完成时:
She had nursed her grandmother for years. = 她护理她祖母多年了。
The nurse had already cared for him before the doctor arrived. = 在医生到来之前，护士已经照顾过他了。


将来时:
She will nurse the newborn baby. = 她将护理新生儿。
The nurse will care for the new patients tomorrow. = 护士明天将照顾新病人。


虚拟语气:
If I were a nurse, I would help more people. = 如果我是护士，我会帮助更多的人。
If she had nursed him properly, he might not have died. = 如果她当时护理得当，他可能不会死。


被动语态:
The baby was nursed by his mother. = 婴儿由他母亲护理。
The patients are cared for by the nurses. = 病人由护士照料。


疑问句:
Does she nurse the baby often? = 她经常护理婴儿吗？
Does the nurse care for the patients well? = 护士照顾病人好吗？


否定句:
She doesn't nurse patients anymore. = 她不再护理病人了。
The nurse didn't care for that patient. = 护士没有照顾那位病人。
