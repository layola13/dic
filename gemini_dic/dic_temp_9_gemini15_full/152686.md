pear-shaped:/ˈpɛrʃeɪpt/| adj.|梨形的，像梨子的；不成比例的，混乱的 |Having the shape of a pear;  wide at the bottom and narrow at the top.  Also used figuratively to describe something that is disproportionate or chaotic. |例句：The vase was pear-shaped, wider at the base than at the neck.  那个花瓶是梨形的，底部比瓶颈宽。|例句：The project became pear-shaped when the funding was unexpectedly cut.  当资金意外被削减时，项目变得一团糟。|近义词：pyriform,  (figuratively) chaotic, disorganized |反义词：cylindrical, symmetrical, orderly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The vase is pear-shaped. = 这个花瓶是梨形的。
Her body is pear-shaped. = 她的身材是梨形的。


一般过去时:
The cake was pear-shaped after I dropped it. = 我把蛋糕掉下来后，它就变得不成形了。
The negotiations became pear-shaped due to a misunderstanding. = 由于误解，谈判变得一团糟。


现在完成时:
The economy has become pear-shaped since the pandemic. = 自从疫情以来，经济已经变得一片混乱。
The situation has gone pear-shaped due to poor planning. = 由于计划不周，情况已经变得一团糟。


过去完成时:
The company had already become pear-shaped before the new CEO arrived. = 在新任CEO到来之前，公司就已经陷入混乱了。
The project had gone pear-shaped by the time we realised the mistake. = 等我们意识到错误的时候，项目已经失败了。


将来时:
If we don't act now, the situation will become pear-shaped. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，情况将会变得一团糟。
The market might go pear-shaped if interest rates rise too sharply. = 如果利率上升过快，市场可能会崩溃。


虚拟语气:
If the project hadn't been so badly managed, it wouldn't have gone pear-shaped. = 如果项目管理得不是那么糟糕，它就不会失败了。
I wish the negotiations hadn't gone pear-shaped. = 我真希望谈判没有失败。


被动语态:
The project was found to be pear-shaped after the audit. = 审计之后，发现该项目一团糟。
The situation was made pear-shaped by a series of unfortunate events. = 一系列不幸的事件导致情况变得一团糟。


疑问句:
Is the design pear-shaped? = 这个设计不成比例吗？
Has the deal gone pear-shaped? = 这笔交易失败了吗？


否定句:
The apple wasn't pear-shaped; it was perfectly round. = 苹果不是梨形的；它是完美的圆形。
The meeting didn't go pear-shaped; it was actually quite productive. = 会议并没有失败；它实际上相当富有成效。
