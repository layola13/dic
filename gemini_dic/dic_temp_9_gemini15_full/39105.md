confrontations:/ˌkɒnfrʌnˈteɪʃənz/| n. |对抗；冲突；对质 |  A confrontation is a hostile or aggressive meeting or situation between opposing parties.  It often involves a direct and forceful encounter, potentially leading to conflict or violence.  |例句：The two leaders had a tense confrontation during the debate.  两位领导人在辩论中发生了一次紧张的对峙。

The protesters had a violent confrontation with the police. 抗议者与警察发生了暴力冲突。


近义词：clashes, conflicts, encounters, disputes, showdowns |反义词：agreements, compromises, resolutions, peaceful settlements |词性:名词
