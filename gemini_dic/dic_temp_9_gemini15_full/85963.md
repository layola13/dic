headquarters'/ˈhedˌkwɔːrtərz/| n. |总部；司令部 |The main administrative building of a large organization.  The term often refers to the central offices from which an organization is managed and controlled. |例句：The company's headquarters is located in New York City.  这家公司的总部位于纽约市。|例句：The army headquarters issued a statement denying the reports.  军队总部发表声明否认这些报道。|近义词：main office, central office, command center |反义词：branch office, field office
