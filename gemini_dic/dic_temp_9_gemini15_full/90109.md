hot-button:/ˈhɑːt ˌbʌtən/ | adj. | 容易引起强烈反应的话题；敏感话题 |  a subject, issue, or idea that is likely to cause a strong emotional reaction, especially one of anger or controversy.  | Example: Abortion is a hot-button issue in many countries.  堕胎在许多国家都是一个敏感话题。  |近义词:controversial, sensitive, inflammatory | 反义词:uncontroversial, inconsequential


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The immigration debate is a hot-button issue. = 移民问题是一个容易引起强烈反应的话题。
This topic remains a hot-button issue in the media. = 这个话题在媒体上仍然是一个敏感话题。


一般过去时:
The election campaign focused on several hot-button issues. = 选举活动集中在几个容易引起强烈反应的问题上。
Gun control was a hot-button issue during the last presidential campaign. = 在上一届总统竞选中，枪支管制是一个敏感话题。


现在完成时:
The debate on climate change has become a major hot-button issue. = 关于气候变化的辩论已经成为一个主要争议话题。
This issue has remained a hot-button topic for decades. =  几十年来，这个问题一直是一个敏感话题。


过去完成时:
Before the debate, they had already identified several hot-button topics. = 在辩论之前，他们已经确定了几个容易引起强烈反应的话题。
The politician had avoided discussing the hot-button issue until the final debate. =这位政治家直到最后一场辩论才避免讨论这个敏感话题。


将来时:
This issue will likely remain a hot-button topic for years to come. =  这个问题很可能会在未来几年内仍然是一个敏感话题。
We predict that healthcare will be a major hot-button issue in the next election. = 我们预测医疗保健将在下一次选举中成为一个主要争议话题。


虚拟语气:
If it were not for the hot-button nature of the topic, it would be easier to discuss. = 如果这个话题不是如此敏感，讨论起来会更容易。
I wish they would avoid discussing such hot-button issues in public. = 我希望他们能避免在公共场合讨论这些敏感话题。


被动语态:
The topic was treated as a hot-button issue by the media. = 这个话题被媒体视为一个敏感话题。
Several hot-button issues were raised during the meeting. = 会议期间提出了一些敏感话题。


疑问句:
Is this really a hot-button issue for the public? = 对公众来说，这真的是一个敏感话题吗？
What are some of the most prominent hot-button issues of our time? = 我们这个时代最突出的敏感话题有哪些？


否定句:
The speaker tried to avoid discussing hot-button topics. = 演讲者试图避免讨论敏感话题。
This issue is not a hot-button topic in this region. =  在这个地区，这个问题不是一个敏感话题。
