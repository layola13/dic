ennuyee:/ˌɑːnwiˈeɪ/ | n. |厌倦的人；感到厌倦的人 | A person who is bored or weary.  An ennuyee is someone who is bored and dissatisfied, often because of a lack of excitement or stimulation in their life. They may feel apathetic and listless. |Example: He became an ennuyee after retiring, finding his days monotonous and lacking purpose. 他退休后变得百无聊赖，发现自己的日子单调乏味，缺乏目标。|近义词：bore, weary, jaded, listless |反义词：enthusiast, energetic, excited |名词
