pseudoscience:/ˈsjuːdoʊˌsaɪəns/ | n. |伪科学 |  A collection of beliefs or practices mistakenly regarded as being based on scientific method.  Pseudoscience often lacks empirical support, fails to be falsifiable, or relies on anecdotal evidence. | Example: Astrology is often cited as an example of pseudoscience.  占星术经常被引用为伪科学的例子。|近义词：quackery, charlatanism |反义词：science, fact
