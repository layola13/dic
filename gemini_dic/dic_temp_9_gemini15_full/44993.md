crystallogenic:/ˌkrɪstəlɵˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj.|成晶的；结晶的；晶体生成的| Relating to or causing the formation of crystals.  The process involves the arrangement of atoms or molecules into a highly ordered, repeating three-dimensional structure.  | Example:  The crystallogenic conditions in this cave have resulted in the formation of spectacular calcite crystals.  这种洞穴中的成晶条件导致了壮观的方解石晶体的形成。 The slow cooling of the magma facilitated crystallogenic processes.岩浆缓慢冷却促进了成晶过程。|近义词：crystallizing, crystallizable|反义词：amorphous (无定形的)
