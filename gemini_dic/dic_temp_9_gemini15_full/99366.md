interdepending:/ˌɪntərˈdɪpɛndɪŋ/| adj.|相互依赖的，互相依存的|Existing or functioning as a whole so that each part is dependent on the others.|The success of the project depends on the interdepending efforts of all team members.  这个项目的成功取决于所有团队成员相互依赖的努力。|近义词：mutually dependent, interdependent, reciprocal|反义词：independent, autonomous|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The economies of these two countries are interdepending. 这两个国家的经济是相互依赖的。
Their fates are interdepending. 他们的命运是相互依存的。

一般过去时:
The two companies were interdepending on each other for survival. 这两家公司为了生存而相互依赖。
Their success was interdepending on teamwork. 他们的成功取决于团队合作。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
