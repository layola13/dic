jigsawing:/ˈdʒɪɡsɔːɪŋ/ | v. | 拼图；拼凑；细致地组装 | The act of assembling a jigsaw puzzle, or of fitting disparate pieces together to form a whole.  It can also imply a complex or intricate process of putting things together. |例句：He spent hours jigsawing the intricate puzzle. 他花了几个小时拼这个复杂的拼图。

The children were jigsawing the picture of a farm. 孩子们正在拼农场图案的拼图。


近义词：assembling, fitting together, piecing together, constructing, putting together | 反义词：disassembling, separating, dismantling, taking apart | 词性：动词
