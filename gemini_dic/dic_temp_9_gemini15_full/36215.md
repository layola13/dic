cogitator:/ˈkɑːdʒɪteɪtər/| n. |思考者，沉思者 |A cogitator is a person who thinks deeply and carefully; one given to contemplation.  They are often engaged in abstract thought or problem-solving. |Example:  He was a deep cogitator, often lost in thought for hours. 他是一位沉思者，经常沉思数小时。  The renowned philosopher was a prolific cogitator, constantly generating new ideas.  这位著名的哲学家是一位多产的思想家，不断产生新的想法。|近义词：thinker, philosopher, ponderer, contemplator |反义词：doer, actor, pragmatist |名词

This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, tense conjugation examples are not applicable.  The word itself describes a state of being, not an action.
