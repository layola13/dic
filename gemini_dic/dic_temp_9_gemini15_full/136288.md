nonrepresentable: /ˌnɒnˌreprɪˈzentəbəl/ | adj. | 不可表示的，不可表达的 |  Unable to be represented or expressed, especially in a particular system or form.  This term often arises in mathematics, computer science, and other fields dealing with formal systems.  It implies a limitation of the system in question, not necessarily an inherent property of the thing being represented. |


例句：

Some irrational numbers are nonrepresentable as finite decimals.  一些无理数不能表示为有限小数。

The chaotic behavior of the system makes its long-term state nonrepresentable by any simple model. 系统的混沌行为使得其长期状态无法用任何简单的模型来表示。


近义词：inexpressible, indescribable, unrepresentatable, unmodellable


反义词：representable, expressible, describable


词性:  adjective
