It suggests a collection of wise acts, judgments, or insights.  |例句：The ancient texts were filled with sapiencies that guided generations. 古代文本充满了指导几代人的智慧。  The council's decisions reflected the combined sapiencies of its members. 理事会的决定反映了其成员的集体智慧。 |近义词：wisdoms, intelligences, insights, sagacities |反义词：foolishness, ignorance, stupidity |名词
