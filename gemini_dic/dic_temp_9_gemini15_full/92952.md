ideography:/ˌaɪdiˈɒɡrəfi/|/ˌaɪdiˈɑːɡrəfi/| n. |表意文字；表意文字法 |The system of writing that uses symbols to represent ideas or concepts rather than sounds.  Ideography contrasts with phonetic writing systems, which use symbols to represent sounds.  |Example:  Ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs are a well-known example of ideography.  古代埃及象形文字是表意文字的一个著名例子。  Many modern symbols, such as the plus sign (+), are also considered ideographic. 许多现代符号，例如加号（+），也被认为是表意文字。|近义词：pictography, logography|反义词：phonetics, alphabet
