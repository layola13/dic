pseudocartilaginous: /ˌsuːdoʊkɑːrtɪˈlædʒɪnəs/ | adj. | 假软骨的；类似软骨的 |  Resembling cartilage in appearance or consistency but not of true cartilaginous origin.  The term indicates a tissue that mimics the properties of cartilage but lacks the cellular and structural characteristics of true cartilage.  It may be composed of fibrous connective tissue, or other materials. |
