hyperlinked:/ˈhaɪpərˌlɪŋkt/| adj.| 超链接的 | Having a hyperlink; containing or using hyperlinks. A hyperlinked document allows users to jump to other parts of the document or to other documents by clicking on highlighted words or phrases. | Example:  The word "Google" is hyperlinked to the Google homepage.  这个词“谷歌”是一个指向谷歌主页的超链接。 |近义词：linked, connected |反义词：unlinked, disconnected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The website is hyperlinked to the company's blog. = 网站与公司的博客超链接。
This document contains many hyperlinked references. = 这份文档包含许多超链接的参考文献。
