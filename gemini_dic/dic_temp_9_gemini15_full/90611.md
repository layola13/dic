humane:/hjuːˈmeɪn/ | adj. | 人道的；仁慈的；慈悲的 |  Characterized by kindness, compassion, and consideration for others, especially those less fortunate or vulnerable.  It implies a concern for the well-being and dignity of all living beings. | 例句：The humane treatment of animals is essential.  = 善待动物至关重要。

The organization works to promote humane policies. = 该组织致力于推广人道政策。


近义词：compassionate, merciful, benevolent, kind, gentle | 反义词：cruel, inhumane, brutal, savage, merciless


词源：源于拉丁语 *humanus，意为“人类的”，最终源于拉丁语 *homo，意为“人”。  The word evolved to describe qualities associated with being human in the best sense: kindness, compassion, and respect for others.
