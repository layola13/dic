nonvassal: /ˈnɒnvæsəl/ | adj. | 非附庸的，非臣属的 |  Not a vassal; not subject to feudal obligations.  This word describes someone or something that is not under the control or authority of a feudal lord.  It implies independence and freedom from vassalage. |

例句：

The newly independent city-state declared itself nonvassal to the empire. = 新独立的城邦宣布自己不受帝国的统治。

The neighbouring kingdom, having won its freedom, enjoyed a nonvassal status. = 邻国赢得自由后，享有非附庸国的地位。


近义词：independent, free, autonomous, sovereign

反义词：vassal, subject, subordinate, dependent


词性：形容词
