premerit:/ˈpriːmɛrɪt/| n. |优先权，优先考虑的事项 |A premerit is a right or privilege that is given to someone before others. It is something that is considered to be more important than other things.  |例句：The project's premerit was its potential to generate significant revenue.  这个项目的优先权在于其产生巨额收入的潜力。|近义词：priority, precedence, preference |反义词：postponement, deferral
