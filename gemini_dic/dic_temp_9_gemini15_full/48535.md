/dɪˈfeɪsmənts/| n. |毁损；污损；破坏|The act of defacing something; the state of being defaced.  It involves damaging or spoiling the surface or appearance of something, often intentionally. |例句：The building was marred by numerous defacements, including graffiti and vandalism. 这座建筑物被许多污损破坏了，包括涂鸦和破坏行为。

例句：The defacements on the ancient statue were deeply regrettable. 古老雕像上的毁损令人深感遗憾。

近义词：damage, destruction, marring, disfigurement, vandalism

反义词：preservation, restoration, beautification, improvement


词性: 名词
