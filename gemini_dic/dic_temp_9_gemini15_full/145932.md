/ˌoʊvərˈdɪsəplɪnɪŋ/ | n. |过度管教；过度约束 | The act of disciplining someone excessively, often leading to negative consequences such as resentment, anxiety, or rebellion.  It often involves imposing rules and restrictions beyond what is necessary or developmentally appropriate. | Example: Overdisciplining children can stifle their creativity and independence.  过度管教孩子可能会扼杀他们的创造力和独立性。  |近义词: excessive discipline, strictness, overregulation |反义词: permissiveness, leniency, neglect |  名词
