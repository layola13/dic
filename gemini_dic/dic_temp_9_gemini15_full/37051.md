comatous:/ˈkɒmətoʊs/| adj.|昏迷的，昏睡的|In a state of coma; unconscious and unresponsive.|The patient remained comatose for several days after the accident.  病人事故后昏迷了好几天。|近义词：unconscious, insensible, comatose|反义词：conscious, awake, alert


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is comatose. = 他处于昏迷状态。
The patient remains comatose. = 病人仍然处于昏迷状态。


一般过去时:
She was comatose after the surgery. = 她手术后处于昏迷状态。
He became comatose suddenly. = 他突然昏迷了。


现在完成时:
He has been comatose for three days. = 他昏迷三天了。
She has remained comatose since the accident. = 自事故发生以来，她一直处于昏迷状态。


过去完成时:
He had been comatose for hours before the doctors arrived. = 在医生到达之前，他已经昏迷了几个小时。
She had already been comatose when the ambulance arrived. = 救护车到达时，她已经昏迷了。


将来时:
He will likely remain comatose for several more days. = 他很可能还会昏迷几天。
The doctors hope he will come out of his comatose state soon. = 医生希望他很快能从昏迷状态中苏醒过来。


虚拟语气:
If he were comatose, he wouldn't respond to stimuli. = 如果他处于昏迷状态，他就不会对刺激做出反应。
I wish he weren't comatose. = 我希望他不要处于昏迷状态。


被动语态:
He was found comatose in his apartment. = 他被发现昏迷在他的公寓里。
The patient is being kept comatose with medication. = 病人正在通过药物维持昏迷状态。(This is less common, as it implies an active keeping of the patient in a coma)


疑问句:
Is the patient still comatose? = 病人还在昏迷吗？
Was he comatose when you found him? = 你发现他时，他处于昏迷状态吗？


否定句:
He is not comatose; he's just sleeping. = 他没有昏迷，他只是在睡觉。
She was not comatose after the minor car accident. = 她那次轻微的车祸后没有昏迷。


PHRASE->
fall into a comatose state = 陷入昏迷状态
remain comatose = 持续昏迷
induced comatose = 诱导性昏迷
emerge from a comatose state = 从昏迷状态中苏醒
deep comatose = 深度昏迷
