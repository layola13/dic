one-offs:/ˈwʌnɒfs/| n.|一次性事物，特例|a single, unique event or item, not part of a regular series or pattern.  Something made or done only once.|例句：The concert was a one-off event, not part of a tour. 这场音乐会是一次性的活动，不是巡演的一部分。


例句：This prototype is a one-off; we don't plan to mass-produce it. 这个原型机是独一无二的；我们不打算批量生产它。


近义词：single instance, unique item, exception, rarity


反义词：regular, recurring, standard, typical


词性:名词
