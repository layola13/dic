candies:/ˈkændiz/| n.|糖果，甜食|Candies are sweet treats made of sugar, often with added flavorings and colors.  They come in various shapes, sizes, and flavors.|例句：The children were delighted with their bag of candies.孩子们对他们那一袋糖果非常高兴。|例句：She bought a variety of candies for the party. 她为派对买了很多种糖果。|近义词：sweets, confections, treats|反义词：None directly applicable.
