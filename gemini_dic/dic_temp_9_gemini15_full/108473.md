lay-off:/ˈleɪ ɔːf/| n. vt.|解雇；裁员；停工；暂时解雇|The act of temporarily or permanently dismissing employees from their jobs, often due to economic downturn or restructuring.|The company announced a lay-off of 100 employees.  这家公司宣布裁员100人。
The factory had a lay-off due to the shortage of raw materials.  由于原材料短缺，工厂停工了。|layoff, dismissal, redundancy|hiring, employment|vt.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company lays off workers every year. = 公司每年都会裁员。
He lays off his anger on his family. = 他把气撒在家人身上。


一般过去时:
They laid off several workers last month. = 他们上个月解雇了几名工人。
The factory laid off production due to a power outage. = 工厂因停电而停产。


现在完成时:
The company has laid off hundreds of employees this year. = 公司今年已裁员数百人。
They have laid off their worries by going on vacation. = 他们通过度假排解了忧虑。


过去完成时:
The company had laid off many workers before the merger. = 在合并之前，公司已经裁员很多。
They had laid off their plans for expansion due to the economic crisis. = 由于经济危机，他们搁置了扩张计划。


将来时:
The company will lay off more workers if sales don't improve. = 如果销售额没有改善，公司将裁员更多。
They will lay off their responsibilities to someone else.  他们会把责任推卸给别人。


虚拟语气:
If the economy worsened, they would lay off half the staff. = 如果经济恶化，他们会裁员一半。
If I had known earlier, I wouldn’t have laid off my friend so quickly. = 如果我早知道，我不会这么快解雇我的朋友。


被动语态:
Many workers were laid off during the recession. = 在经济衰退期间，许多工人被解雇了。
The project was laid off due to lack of funding. = 该项目由于缺乏资金而被搁置。


疑问句:
Were any employees laid off last quarter? = 上个季度有员工被裁员吗？
Will the construction be laid off for the winter? = 冬季建筑工程会停工吗？


否定句:
The company didn't lay off any employees this year. = 公司今年没有裁员。
They didn’t lay off their ambitions to become successful. = 他们并没有放弃成功的雄心壮志。


PHRASE->
lay off someone = 解雇某人
lay off work = 停工
lay off (doing) something = 停止(做)某事
lay off the booze = 少喝酒
lay off on someone = 责备某人
