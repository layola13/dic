respect:/rɪˈspekt/| n., vt.|尊敬，尊重；重视；遵守|

英文解析：The feeling of deep admiration for someone or something elicited by their abilities, qualities, or achievements;  to treat someone or something with consideration and politeness.  It can refer to a feeling of admiration and regard, or the act of showing that feeling.

例句：
I have great respect for her work ethic. = 我非常尊重她的职业道德。

He showed no respect for the law. = 他对法律毫无尊重。


近义词：esteem, regard, admiration, deference, honor

反义词：disrespect, contempt, scorn, disregard


词性：名词和动词
