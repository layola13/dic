self-recriminations:/ˌsɛlfˌrɛkrɪmɪˈneɪʃənz/| n. |自我谴责；自责 |The act of blaming oneself; harsh self-criticism.  It often involves feelings of guilt, shame, and regret. |例句：Overwhelmed by self-recriminations, she couldn't sleep.  她被自我谴责所压垮，无法入睡。

例句：His self-recriminations over the accident haunted him for years. 他对事故的自我谴责多年来一直困扰着他。


近义词：self-condemnation, self-reproach, self-blame, remorse, regret

反义词：self-justification, self-congratulation


词性:名词
