parties:/ˈpɑːrtiːz/ | n. | 聚会，党派，参与者 |  A group of people gathered together, usually for social reasons; a political organization; people involved in a situation or event. |  例句：We're having a party to celebrate my birthday. = 我们要举办一个派对来庆祝我的生日。  | 例句：The two main political parties are debating the new bill. = 两个主要的政党正在辩论这项新法案。 | 近义词：celebration, gathering, faction, group | 反义词：solitude, individual
