ruin:/ˈruːɪn/| n./v.|毁灭；废墟；使破产|The noun "ruin" refers to the state of being destroyed or severely damaged, or the remains of something destroyed.  As a verb, it means to destroy or severely damage something.|例句：The earthquake left the city in ruins. 地震使这座城市成为废墟。 The scandal ruined his career. 这场丑闻毁了他的事业。|近义词：destruction, devastation, wreck, spoil|反义词：build, construct, repair, restore|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain ruins the picnic. = 雨毁了野餐。
He ruins his health by smoking. = 他抽烟毁了自己的健康。


一般过去时:
The storm ruined the crops. = 暴风雨毁了庄稼。
The war ruined the country's economy. = 战争毁了该国的经济。


现在完成时:
The heavy rain has ruined the garden. = 大雨毁了花园。
Years of neglect have ruined the building. = 多年的忽视毁了这座建筑。


过去完成时:
The flood had ruined the village before help arrived. = 在救援到来之前，洪水已经毁了村庄。
He had ruined his reputation by his actions. = 他因自己的行为毁了自己的名声。


将来时:
The coming storm will ruin the harvest. = 即将来临的风暴将毁掉收成。
If you continue this way, you will ruin your life. = 如果你继续这样下去，你会毁掉你的生活。


虚拟语气:
If I had been more careful, I wouldn't have ruined my chances. = 如果我更小心一点，我就不会毁掉我的机会了。
I wish I hadn't ruined the surprise. = 我真希望我没有破坏惊喜。


被动语态:
The castle was ruined by fire. = 城堡被大火毁了。
His career was ruined by the scandal. = 他的事业被丑闻毁了。


疑问句:
What ruined the party? = 什么毁了聚会？
Will the bad weather ruin the trip? = 糟糕的天气会毁掉这次旅行吗？


否定句:
The rain didn't ruin the picnic. = 雨没有毁了野餐。
The criticism didn't ruin her confidence. = 批评并没有毁掉她的自信。
