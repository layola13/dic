reiterately:/rɪˈɪtərətli/ | adv. |反复地，再三地 | repeatedly; again and again;  The manager reiterated the company's policy repeatedly. |近义词：repeatedly, again and again, over and over |反义词：once, singly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reiterates his point repeatedly. = 他反复强调他的观点。
She reiterates the instructions to the new employees. = 她向新员工反复说明指示。


一般过去时:
The teacher reiterated the rules to the students. = 老师向学生们重申了规则。
The politician reiterated his campaign promises. = 这位政治家重申了他的竞选承诺。


现在完成时:
He has reiterated his commitment to the project. = 他已经重申了他对项目的承诺。
She has reiterated her concerns about the safety issue. = 她已经多次表达了她对安全问题的担忧。


过去完成时:
They had reiterated their support for the cause before the final vote. = 在最终投票之前，他们已经重申了对这项事业的支持。
The company had reiterated its warning about the potential risks. = 公司已经重申了关于潜在风险的警告。


将来时:
He will reiterate his position in the upcoming debate. = 他将在即将到来的辩论中重申他的立场。
She will reiterate her request for more funding. = 她将再次请求更多的资金。


虚拟语气:
If he reiterated his apology, she might forgive him. = 如果他再次道歉，她可能会原谅他。
I wish he had reiterated his offer to help. = 我希望他再次提出帮忙的建议。


被动语态:
The company's policy was reiterated by the manager. = 公司的政策由经理反复强调。
The instructions were reiterated to the employees several times. = 指示被反复向员工说明了好几次。


疑问句:
Will he reiterate his statement tomorrow? = 他明天会重申他的声明吗？
Has she reiterated her concerns to the management? = 她是否已向管理层重申了她的担忧？


否定句:
He did not reiterate his request for a raise. = 他没有再次要求加薪。
She did not reiterate the importance of punctuality. = 她没有再次强调准时的重要性。
