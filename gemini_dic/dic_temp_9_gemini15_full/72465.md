flunk out:/flʌŋk aʊt/| v.|因不及格而被开除|To fail a course or courses and be dismissed from a school or college.|He flunked out of college his first year. 他大学一年级就因不及格被开除。|fail, wash out|graduate, succeed|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He flunks out of college every semester. 他每个学期都因不及格被开除大学。
Students who flunk out often feel discouraged.  不及格而被开除的学生常常感到沮丧。


一般过去时:
She flunked out of university last year. 她去年大学不及格被开除。
John flunked out of his math class.  约翰数学课不及格被开除。


现在完成时:
He has flunked out of two different colleges. 他已经被两所不同的大学开除。
Many students have flunked out due to poor time management. 许多学生因为时间管理不善而被开除。


过去完成时:
By the time he reached his senior year, he had flunked out of three schools.  到他读大四的时候，他已经从三所学校被开除。
She had flunked out twice before she finally graduated. 在她最终毕业之前，她已经被开除过两次了。


将来时:
If he doesn't improve his grades, he will flunk out next semester. 如果他不提高成绩，下学期他就会被开除。
I fear that she will flunk out if she continues to skip classes. 我担心如果她继续逃课，她会被开除。


虚拟语气:
If he had studied harder, he wouldn't have flunked out. 如果他学习更努力，他就不会被开除。
I wish I hadn't flunked out of that important class. 我真希望我没有在那门重要的课上不及格而被开除。


被动语态:
He was flunked out of the program for poor attendance. 由于出勤率低，他被该项目开除。
Many students are flunked out each year for academic reasons. 每年都有许多学生因学业原因而被开除。


疑问句:
Will he flunk out this semester? 他这个学期会被开除吗？
Has she ever flunked out of a course? 她曾经因为某门课不及格而被开除吗？


否定句:
He didn't flunk out of college; he graduated. 他没有被大学开除；他毕业了。
She didn't flunk out despite her initial struggles. 尽管她一开始很挣扎，但她并没有被开除。


PHRASE->
flunk out of school:  从学校被开除
flunk out of college: 从大学被开除
flunk out of a program: 从某个项目中被开除
flunk out of university: 从大学被开除
barely flunk out: 勉强逃过被开除
