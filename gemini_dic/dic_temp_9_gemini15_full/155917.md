photocopier:/ˈfoʊtoʊˌkɑːpiər/| n. |复印机|A machine that makes copies of documents, photographs, etc.  |例句：The photocopier is jammed again.  复印机又卡纸了。|例句：We need a new photocopier for the office. 我们办公室需要一台新的复印机。|近义词：copier, duplicator |反义词：None
