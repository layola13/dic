halting:/ˈhɔːltɪŋ/|/ˈhɒltɪŋ/| adj.| 犹豫的；蹒跚的；结结巴巴的；中断的 | Hesitant or unsteady; characterized by stops and starts.  |例句：His speech was halting and uncertain.  他的讲话犹豫不决，不确定。

例句：The progress of the project was halting due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，项目的进展缓慢而中断。


近义词：hesitant, faltering, stammering, stuttering, interrupted, unsteady


反义词：fluent, smooth, continuous, steady


词性：形容词
