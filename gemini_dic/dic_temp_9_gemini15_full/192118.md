scowled:/skaʊld/| v.|皱眉，怒视|To draw down or contract the brows in a frown, typically to express displeasure or anger.|He scowled at the rude customer. 他怒视着那个粗鲁的顾客。|She scowled in disapproval. 她不悦地皱起了眉头。|frowned, glared|smiled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scowls at me every time he sees me. = 他每次看到我都对我皱眉。
They scowl when they are angry. = 他们生气时会皱眉。

一般过去时:
He scowled at the mess. = 他对这烂摊子皱起了眉头。
She scowled when she heard the news. = 她听到这个消息时皱起了眉头。

现在完成时:
He has scowled at me all day. = 他整天都对我皱眉。
She has scowled since she received the letter. = 自从收到这封信后，她就一直愁眉苦脸。

过去完成时:
He had scowled before he spoke. = 他在说话之前就已皱起了眉头。
She had scowled all afternoon before finally bursting into tears. = 她闷闷不乐地过了整个下午，最后终于哭了起来。

将来时:
He will scowl if you don't listen to him. = 如果你不听他的话，他会皱眉。
She will scowl if you interrupt her. = 如果你打断她，她会皱眉。

虚拟语气:
If he knew the truth, he would scowl. = 如果他知道真相，他会皱眉。
If she had known, she would have scowled. = 如果她知道的话，她就会皱眉了。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
His actions were scowled at by everyone. = 他的行为遭到所有人的怒视。
The idea was scowled upon by the committee. = 委员会对这个想法表示反对。

疑问句:
Does he often scowl? = 他经常皱眉吗？
Why is she scowling? = 她为什么皱眉？

否定句:
He didn't scowl at all. = 他一点也不皱眉。
She didn't scowl, she smiled. = 她没有皱眉，她笑了。


PHRASE->
scowl at someone: 对某人皱眉
scowl with anger: 愤怒地皱眉
scowl in disapproval: 不赞成地皱眉
scowl furiously: 怒目而视
scowl darkly: 阴沉地皱眉
