exchangeability:/ɪkˌtʃeɪnʒəˈbɪləti/| n. |可交换性，互换性 |The property of being interchangeable or substitutable.  This concept is crucial in various fields like economics (e.g., perfect substitutes), statistics (e.g., exchangeable random variables), and linguistics (e.g., synonymous words).| Example: The high exchangeability of the parts makes repairs easy.  高互换性的零件使得维修很容易。 The exchangeability of the two variables simplifies the statistical analysis. 这两个变量的可交换性简化了统计分析。|近义词：interchangeability, substitutability |反义词：uniqueness, distinctness |名词
