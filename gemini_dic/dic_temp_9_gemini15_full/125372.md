multimessenger:/ˌmʌltiˈmɛsɪndʒər/| n. |多信使|A multimessenger astronomy observation combines data from different types of astronomical messengers, such as gravitational waves, electromagnetic radiation (light), neutrinos, and cosmic rays, to provide a more complete understanding of astronomical events.  |Example: The detection of gravitational waves alongside electromagnetic radiation from a kilonova marked a breakthrough in multimessenger astronomy.  多信使天文学中引力波和来自千新星的电磁辐射的探测标志着一次突破。|近义词：multi-wavelength astronomy (in a narrower sense)|反义词：single-messenger astronomy|名词
