nonsurrealistic: /ˌnɒnsɜːrɪˈælɪstɪk/ | adj. |非超现实主义的，现实的|Not characterized by or relating to Surrealism; realistic or ordinary.  The painting, while imaginative, lacked the dreamlike quality of Surrealist works; it was simply nonsurrealistic. |近义词：realistic, ordinary, conventional |反义词：surrealistic, fantastical
