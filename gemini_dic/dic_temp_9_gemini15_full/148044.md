/ˌoʊvərˈruːlərz/ | n. | 否决者；具有否决权的人 | A person or thing that overrules; someone or something that has the power to override or cancel a decision.  | The board of directors acted as overrulers, rejecting the CEO's initial proposal.  董事会充当否决者，否决了首席执行官的最初提案。|  vetoer, arbitrator |  supporter, approver | 名词

一般现在时:
The committee overrules the objections. = 委员会否决了反对意见。

一般过去时:
The judge overruled the objection. = 法官驳回了异议。
The president overruled the veto. = 总统否决了否决权。

现在完成时:
The higher court has overruled the lower court's decision. = 上级法院已推翻了下级法院的判决。
They have overruled my proposal several times. = 他们已经几次否决了我的提议。

过去完成时:
The Supreme Court had already overruled the previous ruling. = 最高法院已经推翻了之前的裁决。
She had overruled his objections before he could even finish speaking. = 在他甚至还没说完话之前，她就驳回了他的异议。

将来时:
The court will likely overrule the initial verdict. = 法院很可能会推翻最初的判决。
I believe the committee will overrule our suggestion. = 我相信委员会会否决我们的建议。

虚拟语气:

疑问句:
