clip:/klɪp/| n., v.|夹子；剪辑；片段；别住；剪；修剪|A small device used to fasten things together, a short section of film, audio, or video, or the act of cutting something.|例句：I used a paper clip to hold the papers together. 我用一个回形针把纸张夹在一起。  He clipped the article from the newspaper. 他从报纸上剪下了这篇文章。|近义词：fastener, attachment, excerpt, snippet, trim|反义词：unfasten, join


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He clips his toenails every week. 他每周修剪脚趾甲。
She clips coupons from the newspaper. 她从报纸上剪下优惠券。


一般过去时:
I clipped the rose bush yesterday. 我昨天修剪了玫瑰花丛。
They clipped the video to make it shorter. 他们剪辑了视频使其更短。


现在完成时:
She has clipped all the coupons this week. 她本周已经剪完了所有的优惠券。
He has clipped the hedges several times this year. 他今年已经修剪了几次树篱。


过去完成时:
