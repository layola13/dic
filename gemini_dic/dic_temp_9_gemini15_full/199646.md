sinopsis:/sɪˈnɒpsɪs/| n. |概要，梗概，提要 | A synopsis is a brief summary or general survey of something.  It often presents the main points of a longer work, such as a book, play, or film. | Example: The synopsis of the novel intrigued me enough to read the entire book.  这部小说的梗概足够吸引我读完整本书。|近义词：summary, abstract, précis, outline |反义词：detail, full account
