Someone who holds that a particular authority (such as the Pope, a religious text, or a political ideology) is incapable of error. | Example:  He was an infallibilist, convinced of the absolute truth of his religious leader's pronouncements. 他是一个绝对正确论者，确信其宗教领袖的声明是绝对真理的。 |近义词：dogmatist, absolutist | 反义词：skeptic, relativist | 名词
