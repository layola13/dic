churchgoer:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃɡəʊər/ | n. | 教堂常客，经常去教堂的人 | A churchgoer is someone who regularly attends church services.  | Example: He's a devout churchgoer, attending mass every Sunday. 他是一位虔诚的教堂常客，每个星期天都参加弥撒。 |近义词:church attendee, worshiper |反义词:Non-churchgoer
