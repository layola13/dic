reenable:/ˌriːɪˈneɪbl/| vt.|重新启用，重新使可用|To enable something again after it has been disabled.|The administrator reenabled the user account after verifying their identity. 管理员在验证用户身份后重新启用了用户帐户。  The system automatically reenabled the firewall after the update. 系统在更新后自动重新启用了防火墙。|enable, activate|disable, deactivate|动词
