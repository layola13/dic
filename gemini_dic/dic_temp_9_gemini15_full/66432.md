exegetist: /ˈɛksɪdʒɪtɪst/ | n. | 解经家，释经学者 | A person who studies and interprets biblical texts.  An exegetist carefully examines the text, considering its historical context, literary style, and intended meaning. |  The exegetist presented a compelling new interpretation of the Gospel of John.  解经家提出了对约翰福音的一种引人注目的新解释。| biblical scholar, interpreter, commentator |  (无明显的反义词，可以考虑anti-exegete, 但该词并非常用)| 名词
