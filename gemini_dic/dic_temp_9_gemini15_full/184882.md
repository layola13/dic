retailers:/rɪˈteɪlərz/| n. |零售商，零售业者| Businesses that sell goods to the public in relatively small quantities for direct consumption.  Retailers operate in various formats, from small independent stores to large multinational chains. They play a crucial role in the distribution of goods from manufacturers to consumers.| Example: Many retailers are offering online shopping options now. 许多零售商现在都提供网上购物选择。  Many retailers are struggling with rising costs. 许多零售商都在努力应对不断上涨的成本。|近义词：vendors, merchants, dealers|反义词：wholesalers, manufacturers|名词
