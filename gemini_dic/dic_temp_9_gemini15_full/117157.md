mean:/miːn/ | vt.  adj. n.|意思是；意图；平均数；卑鄙的；吝啬的 |to express or represent something such as an idea, thought, or fact; having a particular meaning or intention;  the average of a set of numbers;  unkind or unpleasant; unwilling to spend money |例句：What does this word mean?  这个词是什么意思？|The mean temperature for July was 25 degrees. 七月份的平均气温是25度。|近义词：signify, intend, average, unkind, stingy|反义词：generous, kind, pleasant


meant:/ment/ | v. (mean的过去式和过去分词)|意思是；意图；打算 |past tense and past participle of mean|例句：I meant to call you, but I forgot. 我本来打算打电话给你，但我忘了。|He meant well, but his actions were misguided. 他的出发点是好的，但他的行为却误导了人。|近义词：intended, proposed, aimed|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What does this word mean? = 这个词是什么意思？
He means well, but he's clumsy. = 他心肠好，但是他很笨拙。


一般过去时:
What did he mean by that? = 他那是什么意思？
She meant to help, but she made things worse. = 她本想帮忙，但却使事情变得更糟。


现在完成时:
I have meant to write to her for weeks. = 我几周来一直想给她写信。
What has he meant to do all this time? = 这段时间他一直想做什么？


过去完成时:
I had meant to go, but I was too tired. = 我本来打算去的，但我太累了。
She had meant to tell him the truth, but she couldn't bring herself to do it. = 她本想告诉他真相，但她无法鼓起勇气。


将来时:
I will mean to call you later. = 我打算晚点给你打电话。
What will this decision mean for the future? = 这个决定对未来意味着什么？


虚拟语气:
If I had meant to hurt you, I would have apologized. = 如果我故意伤害你，我会道歉的。
I wish I had meant to be more careful. = 我真希望我当时能更小心点。


被动语态:
The word is meant to be ironic. = 这个词的本意是讽刺的。
His actions were meant to be a surprise. = 他的行为本意是想制造一个惊喜。


疑问句:
What do you mean by that remark? = 你那句话是什么意思？
What does this symbol mean? = 这个符号是什么意思？


否定句:
I didn't mean to offend you. = 我不是故意冒犯你的。
He didn't mean any harm. = 他并没有恶意。


PHRASE->
