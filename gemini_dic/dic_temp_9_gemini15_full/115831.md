marketer:/ˈmɑːrkɪtər/ | n. | 市场营销人员，营销者 | A person who works in marketing, planning and executing strategies to promote and sell products or services.  | Example:  A successful marketer needs to understand consumer behavior. 一位成功的营销人员需要了解消费者行为。  The marketer developed a creative campaign to reach a new audience.  这位营销人员制定了一个有创意的活动来接触新的受众。 |近义词：salesperson, advertiser, promoter | 反义词：consumer, buyer


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The marketer analyzes market trends. = 营销人员分析市场趋势。
She develops marketing strategies. = 她制定营销策略。


一般过去时:
The marketer presented the campaign results. = 营销人员展示了活动结果。
He researched the target audience. = 他研究了目标受众。


现在完成时:
The company has hired a new marketer. = 公司已经聘请了一位新的营销人员。
They have launched a successful marketing campaign. = 他们已经发起了一个成功的营销活动。


过去完成时:
The marketer had already created the presentation before the meeting. = 在会议之前，营销人员已经制作好了演示文稿。
The team had completed the market research. = 团队已经完成了市场调研。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
