ferreter:/fɛrɪˈtər/| n. |雪貂；(尤指)养来捉老鼠的雪貂|A ferret is a small, slender, carnivorous mammal belonging to the weasel family, often kept as pets or used to hunt rabbits or rats.  |例句：The farmer used a ferret to hunt rabbits in his burrow.  这个农民用雪貂在洞穴里猎兔。

例句：My aunt keeps a ferret as a pet, it's very playful. 我的阿姨养了一只雪貂作为宠物，它非常顽皮。|近义词：polecat, weasel |反义词：None  |词性:名词
