dissuades:/dɪˈsweɪdz/ | vt. |劝阻；阻止；使泄气 | To persuade (someone) not to take a particular course of action.  |例句：Her parents tried to dissuade her from marrying him.  她的父母试图劝她不要嫁给他。

例句：The doctor dissuaded him from smoking. 医生劝他戒烟。


近义词：deter, discourage, prevent, inhibit | 反义词：encourage, incite, persuade


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dissuades his son from playing video games excessively. 他劝儿子不要过度玩电子游戏。
The government dissuades citizens from traveling abroad during the pandemic. 政府劝告公民疫情期间不要出国旅行。


一般过去时:
She dissuaded me from quitting my job. 她劝我不要辞职。
The heavy rain dissuaded us from going for a picnic. 大雨劝退了我们去野餐的计划。


现在完成时:
Nothing has dissuaded him from pursuing his dream.  没有什么能阻止他追求梦想。
The recent scandals have dissuaded many people from investing in that company. 最近的丑闻已经劝退了许多人投资那家公司。


过去完成时:
He had dissuaded her from going, but she went anyway. 他曾劝她别去，但她还是去了。
They had dissuaded him from entering the competition, but he still participated. 他们曾劝他不要参加比赛，但他还是参加了。


将来时:
I hope to dissuade him from making such a rash decision. 我希望劝他不要做出如此草率的决定。
The new policy will dissuade many people from buying that product. 新政策将劝退许多人购买该产品。


虚拟语气:
If I could dissuade him, I would. 如果我能劝住他，我会的。
I wish I could dissuade her from leaving. 我真希望我能劝她不要离开。


被动语态:
He was dissuaded from applying for the position. 他被劝退了，没有申请那个职位。
Many people were dissuaded by the high price.  许多人被高价劝退了。


疑问句:
What can I do to dissuade him? 我能做些什么来劝退他？
Will anything dissuade him from his goal? 有什么能阻止他实现目标吗？


否定句:
Nothing could dissuade her from her purpose.  没有什么能阻止她实现目标。
He couldn't be dissuaded from his decision. 他无法被劝退改变主意。



PHRASE->
dissuade someone from doing something: 劝某人不要做某事
dissuade someone from a course of action: 劝某人不要采取某种行动
be dissuaded from: 被劝退…
dissuade someone against something: 劝某人反对某事
try to dissuade: 试图劝退


词源:
dissuade源自拉丁语“dis-” (表示否定) + “suadere” (意思是劝说，说服)。  因此，dissuade字面意思是“劝退”。
