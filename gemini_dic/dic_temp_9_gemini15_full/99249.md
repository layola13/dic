intercompany: /ˌɪntərˈkʌmpəni/ | adj. |公司间的，公司内部的 | Relating to transactions or activities between different companies within the same corporate group.  |例句：The intercompany transfer of funds was recorded accurately. 公司间的资金转移被准确记录。 |例句：Intercompany agreements are crucial for maintaining financial transparency. 公司间的协议对于维持财务透明度至关重要。|近义词：intragroup, intracompany |反义词：external, third-party
