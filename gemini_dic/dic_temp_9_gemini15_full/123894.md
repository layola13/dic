mordacious:/mɔːrˈdeɪʃəs/| adj.| 尖刻的；讽刺的；爱咬的；|  biting or sarcastic; inclined to bite.  It refers to a sharp, cutting, and often witty remark.  It can also describe something that bites or is inclined to bite. |例句：His mordacious wit made him a feared opponent in debates.  他的尖刻的才智使他在辩论中成为一个令人畏惧的对手。

His mordacious satire cut to the heart of the matter. 他的辛辣讽刺直击要害。


近义词：sarcastic, biting, caustic, acerbic, scathing, trenchant|反义词：mild, gentle, kind, complimentary
