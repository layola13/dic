intentional:/ɪnˈtɛnʃənəl/ | adj. | 故意的；有意的；蓄意的 | Done on purpose; deliberate.  |例句：His actions were intentional, not accidental. 他的行为是有意的，并非意外。

例句：The damage to the car was clearly intentional. 车辆损坏显然是故意的。


近义词：deliberate, willful, premeditated, designed | 反义词：accidental, unintentional, involuntary


USAGE->

词源:
The word "intentional" comes from the Latin word "intendere," meaning "to stretch out, aim, or direct."  The prefix "in-" adds the sense of "in" or "toward," resulting in a meaning of "to direct toward a goal or purpose."  The addition of "-tional" creates the adjectival form.
