congenital:/kənˈdʒɛnɪtəl/| adj.| 先天的，天生的 | existing at or dating from birth; innate.  |例句：He was born with a congenital heart defect. 他天生患有心脏缺陷。

例句：The child's congenital blindness prevented her from seeing the world around her.  孩子的先天性失明使她无法看到周围的世界。


近义词：innate, inborn, hereditary, natural |反义词：acquired, learned


词源:  From Latin *congenitalis, from con- "together" + gignere "to beget".  The word reflects the idea of something being present "from birth."
