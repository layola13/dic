interlibelling: /ˌɪntərˈlɪbəlɪŋ/ | n. | 图书馆之间互借 |  The act of libraries lending materials to each other. This often involves a network or system facilitating the process.  | Example:  The success of the interlibelling program hinges on efficient communication between participating libraries.  图书馆互借计划的成功取决于参与图书馆之间有效的沟通。 | 近义词: interlibrary loan (ILL) | 反义词:  N/A
