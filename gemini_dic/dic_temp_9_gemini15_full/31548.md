chartography:/kɑːˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. |地图绘制学，制图学 |The science or art of making maps.  It involves the process of creating maps, charts, and other visual representations of geographical areas. |例句：Cartography is a complex field requiring both artistic skill and scientific knowledge. 地图绘制学是一个复杂的领域，需要艺术技巧和科学知识。  |例句：Advanced cartography techniques allow for the creation of highly detailed and accurate maps. 先进的制图技术允许创建高度详细和准确的地图。 |近义词：mapmaking, mapping |反义词：None.
