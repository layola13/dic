scansionist:/ˈskænʃənɪst/ | n. | 音律分析者，格律学家 | A person who practices scansion, the analysis of metrical patterns in poetry.  They study the rhythmic structure of verse by identifying stressed and unstressed syllables. |  The scansionist meticulously analyzed the poem's iambic pentameter.  音律分析者一丝不苟地分析了这首诗的五音步抑扬格。 |  prosodist, metrist |  (无明确反义词，可考虑non-analyst) | 名词
