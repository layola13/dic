discoverer: /dɪˈskʌvərər/ | n. | 发现者，探险家 |  A person who discovers something, especially a place, a scientific principle, or a work of art.  | Example: Columbus is often considered the discoverer of America, though this view is now widely disputed. 哥伦布通常被认为是美洲的发现者，尽管这种观点现在受到广泛质疑。  |近义词: explorer, inventor, pioneer | 反义词:  concealer (隐瞒者，并非完全反义，但语义上形成对比)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The discoverer presents their findings. = 发现者展示他们的发现。
He is a discoverer of new species. = 他是一位新物种的发现者。

一般过去时:
The discoverer announced his discovery. = 发现者宣布了他的发现。
She was a discoverer of ancient ruins. = 她是一位古代遗址的发现者。

现在完成时:
The team has discovered a new planet. = 团队已经发现了一颗新的行星。（此处主语是team，discoverer 作定语修饰）
Many discoverers have contributed to our understanding of the universe. = 许多发现者都为我们对宇宙的理解做出了贡献。

过去完成时:
Before the expedition, the discoverer had studied the maps carefully. = 在探险之前，发现者已经仔细研究了地图。
They had already met the discoverer of the lost city. = 他们已经见过了那个失落之城的发现者。

将来时:
The future discoverer will be honored. = 未来的发现者将受到表彰。
We will celebrate the discoverer's achievements. = 我们将庆祝发现者的成就。

虚拟语气:
If he were the discoverer, he would be famous. = 如果他是发现者，他就会很有名。
I wish I were the discoverer of that treasure. = 我希望我是那个宝藏的发现者。

被动语态:
The new element was discovered by a young scientist. (此处主语是element，discoverer 未直接出现) = 新元素是由一位年轻科学家发现的。
Many important discoveries have been made by dedicated discoverers. = 许多重要的发现都是由献身的发现者做出的。

疑问句:
Who was the discoverer of penicillin? = 青霉素的发现者是谁？
Is he really the discoverer of this new technology? = 他真的是这项新技术的发现者吗？

否定句:
He is not the discoverer of that theory. = 他不是那个理论的发现者。
She was not the first discoverer of that species. = 她不是那个物种的第一个发现者。


PHRASE->
the discoverer of... = ……的发现者
a renowned discoverer = 知名的发现者
a tireless discoverer = 不懈的发现者
a great discoverer = 伟大的发现者
the early discoverers = 早期的发现者
