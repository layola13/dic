outflamed:/ˌaʊtˈfleɪmd/| v.|胜过，超过(火焰)；超过(在强度或影响力上)|To surpass in flame; to exceed in intensity or effect.|例句：The bonfire outflamed all the others on the beach.  篝火比海滩上其他篝火都旺。

例句：Her anger outflamed his attempts to calm her. 她的怒火超过了他试图平息她的努力。


近义词：surpass, exceed, outshine, overshadow|反义词：be outdone, be surpassed


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun outflames the moon in brightness. 太阳的光芒胜过月亮。
His passion outflames his reason. 他的热情超过了他的理智。


一般过去时:
The volcano outflamed all previous eruptions. 这座火山喷发的强度超过了以往所有的喷发。
Her performance outflamed all expectations. 她的表演超出了所有人的预期。


现在完成时:
The artist's latest work has outflamed all his previous creations.  这位艺术家最新的作品已经超越了他以前的所有作品。
His influence has outflamed that of his predecessors. 他的影响力已经超过了他的前辈们。


过去完成时:
The scandal had outflamed any other in recent memory. 这场丑闻的激烈程度已经超过了人们近期记忆中的任何其他丑闻。
The rumor had outflamed the truth. 谣言已经超过了真相的影响力。


将来时:
The new technology will outflame the old methods. 新技术将超过旧方法。
His ambition will outflame his ability. 他的野心将超过他的能力。


虚拟语气:
If his courage outflamed his fear, he would have succeeded. 如果他的勇气超过了他的恐惧，他就会成功。
If her talent outflamed her rivals', she would win the contest. 如果她的天赋超过了她的竞争对手，她就会赢得比赛。


被动语态:
His work was outflamed by the critics' negative reviews. 他的作品被评论家的负面评价所掩盖。
The small fire was outflamed by the larger blaze. 小火被更大的火焰所吞没。


疑问句:
Will his determination outflame his obstacles? 他的决心能否克服障碍？
Does his influence outflame that of his rivals? 他的影响力是否超过了他的竞争对手？


否定句:
His efforts did not outflame the challenges he faced. 他的努力没有克服他面临的挑战。
Her kindness did not outflame the cruelty she witnessed. 她的善良未能超越她目睹的残酷。
