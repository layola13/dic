nonindictment: /ˌnɒnɪnˈdaɪtmənt/ | n. | 不起诉 | The decision not to indict someone;  a situation where charges are not filed.  It often implies a lack of sufficient evidence to proceed with a prosecution. | 例句：The nonindictment of the police officer sparked widespread protests.  = 警官的不起诉引发了大规模抗议。


The grand jury's nonindictment was a major setback for the prosecution. = 大陪审团的不起诉对检方来说是一个重大挫折。| 近义词：lack of indictment, dismissal of charges | 反义词：indictment, prosecution
