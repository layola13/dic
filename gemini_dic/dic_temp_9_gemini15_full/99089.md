interbreed:/ˌɪntərˈbriːd/| v.|杂交，混种|To breed (animals or plants of different varieties or species) together.|例句：Many dog breeds have been interbred to create new varieties.  许多犬种已经杂交以创造新的品种。

例句：The farmer interbred his prize-winning sheep with a different breed to improve the flock's wool quality.  这位农民将他获奖的羊与不同品种的羊杂交，以提高羊群的羊毛质量。


近义词：crossbreed, hybridize|反义词：inbreed, purebreed|词性:动词
