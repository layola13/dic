pupillarity:/ˌpjuːpɪˈlærɪti/ | n. |瞳孔性质；瞳孔状态；(尤指)瞳孔放大 | The state or quality of being a pupil; the condition of the pupil of the eye, especially its dilation.  The term is rarely used in modern English and often refers to the dilation of the pupil. |The doctor noted the pupillarity of his eyes, indicating a possible neurological issue.  医生注意到了他瞳孔的状态，这表明可能存在神经系统问题。 | dilation, mydriasis | constriction, miosis | 名词
