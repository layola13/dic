hebraic:/hɪˈbreɪɪk/| adj.|希伯来语的；希伯来人的；|Of or relating to the Hebrew language or people.  The term often implies a connection to Jewish culture and traditions.|例句：He is fluent in Hebraic literature. 他精通希伯来语文学。

例句：The Hebraic calendar is lunar. 希伯来历是阴历。


近义词：Jewish, Israelite


反义词：None specifically, but words implying non-Jewish or non-Israelite cultures could be considered antonyms in context.


词性:形容词
