interinfluencing: /ˌɪntərˈɪnfluənsɪŋ/ | v. |相互影响 |The act or process of two or more things influencing each other mutually.  It emphasizes the reciprocal nature of the influence, unlike simply "influencing." | Example: The economies of these two countries are interinfluencing, with growth in one affecting the other.  这些国家的经济相互影响，一个国家的增长会影响另一个国家。 |近义词: mutually influencing, reciprocally affecting |反义词:  unrelated, independent
