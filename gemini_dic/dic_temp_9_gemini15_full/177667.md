recalculate:/ˌriːˈkælkjuleɪt/ | vt. |重新计算，再计算 | To calculate something again, often because you found a mistake in the first calculation.  | Example: We need to recalculate the budget after the unexpected expenses. 我们需要在意外开支后重新计算预算。  | 近义词：recompute, recalibrate | 反义词：estimate (if the recalculation is more precise)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We recalculate the figures every month. = 我们每个月都会重新计算这些数字。
They recalculate the route based on traffic conditions. = 他们根据交通状况重新计算路线。


一般过去时:
The engineer recalculated the stress on the bridge. = 工程师重新计算了桥梁的压力。
She recalculated her taxes after finding an error. = 她发现错误后重新计算了税款。


现在完成时:
They have recalculated the project's cost several times. = 他们已经多次重新计算了项目的成本。
I have recalculated the answer and found a mistake in my previous calculation. = 我已经重新计算了答案，并在之前的计算中发现了一个错误。


过去完成时:
After reviewing the data, they had recalculated the probabilities. = 在审查数据后，他们重新计算了概率。
We had recalculated the travel time before leaving. = 我们出发前已经重新计算了旅行时间。


将来时:
We will recalculate the results once we receive the updated data. = 一旦我们收到更新的数据，我们将重新计算结果。
The team will recalculate the project timeline after the delay. = 团队将在延误后重新计算项目时间表。


虚拟语气:
If we had recalculated the figures earlier, we would have avoided the problem. = 如果我们早点重新计算这些数字，我们就不会遇到这个问题。
I wish I had recalculated the recipe before baking the cake. = 我真希望我在烘焙蛋糕之前重新计算过食谱。


被动语态:
The budget was recalculated by the finance department. = 预算由财务部门重新计算。
The figures will be recalculated next week. = 数字将在下周重新计算。


疑问句:
Should we recalculate the total cost? = 我们应该重新计算总成本吗？
Will the software automatically recalculate the values? = 该软件会自动重新计算值吗？


否定句:
They did not recalculate the scores, even after the protest. = 即使抗议之后，他们也没有重新计算分数。
We cannot recalculate the results without the original data. = 没有原始数据，我们就无法重新计算结果。
