polypharmacy:/ˌpɒliˈfɑːrməsi/ /ˌpɑːliˈfɑːrməsi/| n. |多药治疗；多种药物联用|The simultaneous use of multiple drugs by a single patient, which can increase the risk of adverse drug events.  This is often seen in elderly patients with multiple chronic conditions.|例句：The elderly patient's polypharmacy contributed to her confusion and falls.|近义词：multiple medication use, concurrent medication use|反义词：monotherapy
