fueling:/ˈfjuːlɪŋ/| v.|供给燃料；激励；促进|Providing fuel or energy to something;  stimulating or encouraging something.|例句：The fire was fueled by strong winds. 大火被强风助长。|例句：His ambition fueled his relentless work. 他的雄心壮志激励着他不断工作。|近义词：powering, energizing, stimulating, driving|反义词：depleting, hindering, suppressing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fuels his car with gasoline. = 他用汽油给他的车加油。
The engine is fueled by electricity. =  发动机由电力驱动。

一般过去时:
She fueled the campfire with wood. = 她用木柴给营火加柴。
The news fueled public anger. = 这个消息激起了公众的愤怒。

现在完成时:
They have fueled the debate with new evidence. = 他们用新的证据加剧了这场辩论。
The company has been fueled by innovation. = 这家公司一直以来都靠创新发展。

过去完成时:
The protests had been fueled by years of discontent. = 多年的不满情绪已经激起了这些抗议活动。
The rumor had fueled speculation about a merger. = 谣言激起了关于合并的猜测。

将来时:
We will fuel the rocket with liquid hydrogen. = 我们将用液态氢为火箭提供燃料。
This new policy will fuel economic growth. = 这项新政策将促进经济增长。

虚拟语气:
If we fueled the engine properly, it would run smoothly. = 如果我们正确地为发动机加油，它就会平稳运行。
If the media hadn't fueled the controversy, it wouldn't have escalated. = 如果媒体没有煽动争议，它就不会升级。

被动语态:
The car was fueled by the mechanic. = 这辆车是由机械师加油的。
The fire was fueled by the strong winds. = 大火是被强风助长的。

疑问句:
What fuels your passion for art? = 什么激发了你对艺术的热情？
How is this machine fueled? = 这台机器是用什么燃料的？


否定句:
The lack of investment didn't fuel economic growth. = 投资不足并没有促进经济增长。
They didn't fuel the debate with false information. = 他们没有用虚假信息来煽动辩论。


PHRASE->
fuel the fire = 火上浇油
fuel a debate = 激化辩论
fuel speculation = 激起猜测
fuel growth = 促进增长
fuel the economy = 刺激经济
