codependency:/ˌkoʊdɪˈpɛndənsi/| n.|共依赖，互相依赖|The excessive emotional or psychological reliance on a partner, parent, child, or friend.  It is characterized by a preoccupation with the other person's needs and behaviors, often at the expense of one's own.  Codependency can manifest in various ways, including enabling unhealthy behaviors, neglecting personal needs, and experiencing low self-esteem.|例句：Her codependency on her mother prevented her from developing a healthy sense of self.  她的依附性人格障碍让她无法形成健康的自我认知。

例句：His codependency with his addicted brother led him to enable his destructive behavior. 他与吸毒兄弟之间的共依赖导致他纵容了兄弟的破坏性行为。


近义词：interdependence, dependence, reliance|反义词：independence, self-reliance|名词
