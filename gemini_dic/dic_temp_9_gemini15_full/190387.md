sarkiest:/ˈsɑːrkiɪst/| adj. |最刻薄的，最讽刺的 | Most sarcastic;  characterized by the most cutting or bitter sarcasm.  It implies a high degree of intended mockery or insult. |例句：He delivered the sarkiest comment I'd ever heard. 他说出了我听过的最刻薄的话。

例句：Her sarkiest remarks left everyone feeling uncomfortable. 她最刻薄的言论让每个人都感到不舒服。|近义词：most sarcastic, most cutting, most biting, most cynical |反义词：kindest, most gentle, most complimentary
