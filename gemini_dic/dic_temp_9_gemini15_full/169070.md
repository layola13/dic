programmes:/ˈprəʊɡræmz/ /ˈproʊɡræmz/ | n. | 节目；规划；程序 | A set of related activities with a specific purpose, or a plan of action.  It can also refer to a broadcast on television or radio. | 例句：The television programmes are excellent this week. = 这周的电视节目很棒。

The government announced its new programmes for economic growth. = 政府宣布了其新的经济增长计划。


近义词：plans, schemes, schedules, broadcasts, shows | 反义词：chaos, disorder
