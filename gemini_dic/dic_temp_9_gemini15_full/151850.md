passings:/ˈpæsɪŋz/| n.|逝世，去世；经过，通过；(时间的)流逝|The noun "passings" refers to instances of death or the act of dying. It can also refer to the act of something going by or being passed, or the passage of time.  |例句：The passings of her loved ones left her heartbroken.  她的亲人的去世让她心碎。

The passing of the seasons is a natural cycle.  季节的更迭是自然循环。


近义词：deaths, deceases, departures, transitions, goings-by|反义词：births, arrivals|词性：名词
