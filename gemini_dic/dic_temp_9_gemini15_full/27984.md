canvases:/ˈkænvəsiːz/ | n. |画布；帆布|The plural form of "canvas," referring to pieces of strong cloth used for painting or other purposes.  It can also refer to the surface upon which a painting is created.|


例句：The artist displayed her canvases at the gallery.  =艺术家在画廊展出了她的画布。

The ship's canvases were billowing in the wind. = 船帆在风中鼓动。


近义词：cloths, surfaces


反义词：无


词性：名词


词源：源自古法语"canevas",  最终源自拉丁语"cannabis" (大麻)，因为早期帆布和画布经常用大麻纤维制成。


USAGE->

The artist uses several canvases. = 艺术家使用几块画布。
