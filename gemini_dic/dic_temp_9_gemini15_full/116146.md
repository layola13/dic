masala:/məˈsɑːlə/| n. |玛萨拉（印度香料混合物）|A blend of ground spices commonly used in Indian cooking.  The exact composition varies regionally and according to individual preference, but often includes cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, and others. |This curry has a delicious masala blend.|近义词：spice blend, spice mix|反义词：None
