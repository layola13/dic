effluviate:/ɪˈfluːvieɪt/| v.|散发，流出，发出（气味等）|to flow out gradually; to emanate or exhale (as an odor)|The swamp effluviated a noxious odor.  沼泽散发出一股恶臭。|The flowers effluviated a sweet fragrance.  花朵散发着甜蜜的芬芳。|emanate, exude, emit|absorb, retain


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The flowers effluviate a sweet scent. = 花朵散发着甜美的香味。
The factory effluviates noxious fumes. = 工厂排放出有害烟雾。

一般过去时:
The volcano effluviated ash and smoke. = 火山喷发出火山灰和烟雾。
The wound effluviated pus. = 伤口流出脓液。

现在完成时:
The landfill has effluviated methane for years. = 垃圾填埋场多年来一直排放甲烷。
The decaying matter has effluviated a foul smell. = 腐烂的物质散发出一股恶臭。

过去完成时:
The pipe had effluviated gas for hours before the leak was detected. = 管道泄漏前几个小时一直在排放气体。
The swamp had effluviated a miasma for days. = 沼泽几天来一直散发着瘴气。

将来时:
The broken sewer pipe will effluviate sewage into the street. = 破裂的下水道管道将把污水排放到街道上。
The damaged container will effluviate its contents. = 受损的容器将排出其内容物。


虚拟语气:
If the pipes were leaking, they would effluviate water. = 如果管道泄漏，它们就会流出水。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

The new system prevents the tank from effluviating any liquid. = 新系统防止油箱泄漏任何液体。
