redistributive:/ˌriːdɪˈstrɪbjuːtɪv/ | adj. | 再分配的；重新分配的 | Relating to or characterized by the redistribution of something, especially wealth or income.  This adjective describes systems, policies, or actions that aim to shift resources from one group to another, often with the goal of greater equality or fairness. |

例句：

The government implemented redistributive policies to reduce income inequality.  政府实施了再分配政策以减少收入不平等。

The proposed tax reform has significant redistributive effects.  拟议的税制改革具有重大的再分配效应。


近义词：reallocating, reapportioning, sharing


反义词：concentrative, centralizing


词性：形容词
