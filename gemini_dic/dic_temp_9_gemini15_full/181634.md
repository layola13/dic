relaxedly:/rɪˈlæksɪdli/ | adv. |轻松地，悠闲地 |  The adverb "relaxedly" describes an action performed in a calm and unhurried manner, suggesting a state of ease and freedom from tension. It implies a lack of stress or anxiety. | 例句：He leaned back relaxedly in his chair. 他轻松地靠在椅子上。

She smiled relaxedly and nodded. 她轻松地微笑着点了点头。| 近义词：casually, calmly, easily, leisurely | 反义词：tensely, anxiously, nervously, hurriedly
