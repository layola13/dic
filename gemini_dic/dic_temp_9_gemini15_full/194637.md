self-torturing: /ˈsɛlf ˈtɔːrtʃərɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我折磨的，自我摧残的 |  Describing someone who inflicts pain or suffering upon themselves, often mentally or emotionally. This can manifest in various ways, from self-harm to obsessive negative thinking. | 例句：He engaged in self-torturing behavior, constantly criticizing himself and dwelling on past mistakes. 他从事自我折磨的行为，不断地批评自己并沉溺于过去的错误中。

His self-torturing guilt prevented him from moving on.  他的自我折磨式的内疚感使他无法继续前进。


近义词：self-destructive, masochistic, self-flagellating | 反义词：self-loving, self-compassionate
