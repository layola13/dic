coach:/koʊtʃ/|ˈkəʊtʃ/| n./v.|教练；轿车；辅导；训练|

英文解析:
Noun: A coach is a person who trains an athlete or a team. It can also refer to a type of comfortable passenger vehicle, typically a large, luxurious car or bus. As a verb, it means to teach or instruct someone, often in a particular skill or subject.

例句:
He is a basketball coach. 他是一位篮球教练。

She hired a life coach. 她雇了一位人生教练。


近义词:
trainer, instructor, tutor, mentor, carriage, bus


反义词:
player (in the context of sports), student (in the context of teaching)


词性:
Noun and Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He coaches the football team. 他执教足球队。
She coaches swimming lessons. 她教游泳课。

一般过去时:
He coached the team to victory. 他带领球队取得了胜利。
She coached me on my presentation. 她指导我做演讲。

现在完成时:
He has coached many successful athletes. 他已经指导过许多成功的运动员。
She has coached this team for five years. 她执教这支球队五年了。

过去完成时:
He had coached the team for ten years before he retired. 他退休前执教这支球队十年了。
She had coached him extensively before the competition.  比赛前她对他进行了大量的指导。


将来时:
He will coach the national team next year. 他明年将执教国家队。
She will coach him on his public speaking skills. 她将指导他的演讲技巧。


虚拟语气:
If he coached the team differently, they might win. 如果他换一种方式执教球队，他们可能会赢。
If she had coached me earlier, I would have done better. 如果她早点指导我，我会做得更好。

被动语态:
The team was coached by a famous athlete. 这支球队由一位著名的运动员执教。
He was coached intensively for the marathon. 他为马拉松进行了强化训练。


疑问句:
Does he coach any other sports? 他还执教其他运动项目吗？
Who coached the winning team? 谁执教的冠军队？


否定句:
He doesn't coach professional teams. 他不执教职业队。
She didn't coach him enough for the exam. 她没有足够地指导他准备考试。
