self-evaluations:/ˌsɛlf ɪˌvæljuˈeɪʃənz/| n.|自我评价，自我评估|The act of assessing one's own abilities, performance, or character.  It often involves reflecting on strengths, weaknesses, and areas for improvement. |例句：My self-evaluations always highlight areas where I can improve my time management skills. 我的自我评价总是突出我可以在时间管理方面改进的领域。

例句：Annual self-evaluations are mandatory for all employees. 所有员工都必须进行年度自我评价。


近义词：self-assessments, appraisals, critiques (of oneself)


反义词：external evaluations, peer reviews


词性:名词
