mud:/mʌd/| n.|泥，泥浆|soft, wet earth; a mixture of earth and water.|The children were covered in mud after playing outside. 孩子们在外面玩耍后浑身是泥。|dirt, mire, clay|clean, dry, pavement|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The mud is very thick today. = 今天的泥很厚。
This mud sticks to your shoes. = 这泥巴粘在你的鞋子上。


一般过去时:
The car got stuck in the mud. = 车陷在泥里了。
We walked through the muddy field. = 我们走过了泥泞的田野。


现在完成时:
The rain has turned the ground into mud. = 雨水把地面变成了泥地。
They have cleaned all the mud off their boots. = 他们已经把靴子上的泥巴都擦干净了。


过去完成时:
The road had become impassable because of the mud. = 因为泥泞，道路已无法通行。
They had walked for hours through the deep mud. = 他们在深泥里走了好几个小时。


将来时:
The forecast predicts that it will be muddy tomorrow. = 预报说明天会很泥泞。
We will need boots if it gets muddy. = 如果变泥泞了，我们需要穿靴子。


虚拟语气:
If it hadn't been for the mud, we would have arrived earlier. = 要不是因为泥泞，我们本来会早点到的。
I wish the mud wasn't so deep. = 我希望泥巴不要这么深。


被动语态:
The car was covered in mud. = 车子被泥土覆盖了。
The path is often muddied by passing vehicles. = 小路经常被过往车辆弄脏。


疑问句:
Is this mud drying up? = 这泥巴干了吗？
Why is there so much mud here? = 为什么这里有这么多泥巴？


否定句:
The ground wasn't muddy. = 地面不泥泞。
The shoes weren't covered in mud. = 鞋子没有沾上泥巴。
