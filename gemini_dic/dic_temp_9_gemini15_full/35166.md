clowning:/ˈklaʊnɪŋ/| n. |滑稽表演；小丑行为 |The act of clowning around; behaving in a playful and silly manner, often to entertain or amuse.  It can also refer to the profession of a clown. |例句：The children were clowning around in the playground.孩子们在操场上做着滑稽表演。  The circus featured a talented clowning troupe.马戏团有一个才华横溢的小丑表演团。|近义词：buffoonery, tomfoolery, horseplay |反义词：seriousness, solemnity
