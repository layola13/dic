nuance:/ˈnuːɑːns/| n. |细微差别；细微之处；微妙之处 |A subtle difference in or shade of meaning, expression, or sound.  It refers to a subtle distinction or variation in meaning, tone, or expression that is often difficult to articulate but significantly impacts the overall message. |例句：The nuances of the poem were lost in translation.  这首诗的细微之处在翻译中丢失了。

The painting captured the nuances of light and shadow.  这幅画捕捉到了光影的细微之处。


近义词：subtlety, gradation, shade, refinement |反义词：obviousness, crudeness, bluntness
