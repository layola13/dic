non-universal:/ˌnɑːnjuːnɪˈvɜːrsəl/| adj. |非普遍的，非通用的|Not applicable or true in all cases; not universal.  It implies a limitation or exception to a general rule or principle. |例句：The findings of this study are non-universal and may not apply to other populations.  这项研究的结果并非普遍适用，可能不适用于其他人群。

例句：This method, while effective in some contexts, is non-universal and shouldn't be considered a panacea.  这种方法虽然在某些情况下有效，但并非普遍适用，不应被视为万能药。


近义词：limited, restricted, specific, particular, exceptional


反义词：universal, general, common, widespread, ubiquitous
