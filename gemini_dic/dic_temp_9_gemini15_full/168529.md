procambium:/ˈproʊkæmbiəm/| n. |原形成层| A meristematic tissue in plants that gives rise to the vascular tissues (xylem and phloem).  It's a primary meristem that develops into the vascular bundles of stems and roots. |Example: The procambium differentiates into primary xylem and phloem. 原形成层分化成初生木质部和韧皮部。|近义词：primary meristem, vascular cambium (in a more mature context)|反义词：无|名词
