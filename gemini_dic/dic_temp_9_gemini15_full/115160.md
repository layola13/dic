manhunt:/ˈmænˌhʌnt/| n.|大规模搜捕；追捕|The organized search for a person, especially a criminal.  A manhunt is a large-scale search for a person who has committed a crime or is otherwise wanted by authorities. It often involves multiple law enforcement agencies and significant resources.|例句：The police launched a massive manhunt after the escape of the notorious killer.警方在臭名昭著的杀手越狱后发起了大规模搜捕。

例句：The manhunt for the escaped prisoner lasted for several days. 对逃犯的搜捕持续了几天。|近义词：hunt, pursuit, search, chase|反义词：release, freedom
