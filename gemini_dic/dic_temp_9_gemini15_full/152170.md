patriarchal:/ˌpeɪtriˈɑːrkl/| adj.| 父权制的；家长统治的 | Relating to or characteristic of a patriarchal society.  A patriarchal society is one in which men hold primary power and predominate in roles of political leadership, moral authority, social privilege, and control of property.  |例句：Many cultures around the world have historically been patriarchal.  许多世界各地的文化历史上都是父权制的。

例句：The patriarchal system often disadvantages women. 父权制常常使妇女处于不利地位。


近义词：paternalistic, male-dominated

反义词：matriarchal, egalitarian

词性:形容词
