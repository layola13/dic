patters:/ˈpætərz/| v.|发出连续轻微声响，轻拍|to make a series of light, quick movements or sounds|The rain pattered against the window. 雨点轻轻地敲打着窗户。The child's feet pattered down the hallway. 孩子的小脚在走廊上轻轻地走着。|近义词：tap, drum, pitter-patter|反义词：boom, crash

He patters his feet impatiently. = 他不耐烦地轻轻跺着脚。
