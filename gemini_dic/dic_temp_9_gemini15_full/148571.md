overtaking:/ˌoʊvərˈteɪkɪŋ/|/ˈoʊvəˌteɪkɪŋ/| n. v.| 超车；赶上；超越 | The act of overtaking or the state of being overtaken.  Overtaking can refer to surpassing someone or something in a race, competition, or progress.  It can also describe surpassing in quantity or amount. |

例句：
The car overtook me on a blind bend.  这辆车在盲弯处超了我。

The company's growth has overtaken that of its competitors.  这家公司的增长速度超过了其竞争对手。


近义词：surpassing, surpassing, outstripping, outpacing, passing


反义词：being overtaken, lagging behind


词性：
名词(n.):  The act of overtaking.  超车行为
动词(v.): To overtake; to surpass. 超车；超越


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overtakes other cars frequently. 他经常超车。
The company overtakes its competitors in sales. 这家公司在销售额上超过了竞争对手。

一般过去时:
She overtook the runner in the final stretch. 她在最后一段路程超越了那个跑步者。
The storm overtook the ship at sea. 暴风雨在海上袭击了那艘船。

现在完成时:
They have overtaken us in the race. 他们在比赛中超越了我们。
The demand for the product has overtaken the supply. 对该产品的需求已经超过了供应。

过去完成时:
The police had overtaken the suspects before they reached the border.  警方在嫌疑犯到达边境前就已将他们逮捕。
The project had overtaken its budget before completion. 项目在完成前已超出预算。

将来时:
We will overtake them if we continue at this pace. 如果我们继续保持这个速度，我们将超越他们。
The new technology will soon overtake the old. 新技术很快就会取代旧技术。

虚拟语气:
If he had tried harder, he might have overtaken his rival. 如果他更努力一点，他可能就超越了他的对手。
I wish I had overtaken him in the race. 我真希望我在比赛中超越了他。

被动语态:
He was overtaken by a faster car. 他被一辆更快的车超车了。
The country was overtaken by economic crisis. 这个国家被经济危机席卷了。

疑问句:
Will we be overtaken by the other team? 我们会被另一队超越吗？
Has the company been overtaken by its competitors? 这家公司被竞争对手超越了吗？

否定句:
We didn't overtake them in the race. 我们在比赛中没有超越他们。
The company failed to overtake its competitors in the market. 这家公司未能超越其在市场上的竞争对手。


PHRASE->
overtake someone by surprise  出其不意地超过某人
overtake an opportunity 抓住机会
overtake the situation 控制局势
overtake a crisis 克服危机
overtake the market 占领市场
