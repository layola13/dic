repair:/rɪˈpɛər/ | vt. n. | 修理，修复 | To restore something damaged to a good condition. | 例句：The mechanic repaired my car. = 机械师修理了我的车。  | 例句：The repair of the bridge took several months. = 修理这座桥花了几个月的时间。 |近义词：mend, fix, restore | 反义词：damage, break, destroy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repairs computers for a living. = 他以修理电脑为生。
She regularly repairs her own clothes. = 她经常自己修理衣服。

一般过去时:
They repaired the roof after the storm. = 暴风雨后他们修理了屋顶。
The technician repaired the faulty appliance. = 技术人员修理了有故障的电器。

现在完成时:
He has repaired several cars this week. = 他这周已经修理了几辆车。
She has finally repaired the broken vase. = 她终于修好了那个破花瓶。

过去完成时:
They had repaired the engine before the race. = 他们在比赛前已经修理好了发动机。
The building had been repaired several times before it collapsed. = 那栋建筑在倒塌之前已经修理过好几次了。

将来时:
The engineer will repair the damaged pipeline tomorrow. = 工程师明天将修理损坏的管道。
We will repair the fence next weekend. = 我们下周末将修理围栏。

虚拟语气:
If I had the tools, I would repair the bike myself. = 如果我有工具，我会自己修理自行车。
It would be helpful if someone could repair the leaking faucet. = 如果有人能修理漏水的龙头就好了。

被动语态:
The car is being repaired at the moment. = 汽车正在修理中。
The bridge was repaired last year. = 那座桥去年被修理了。

疑问句:
Can you repair this broken chair? = 你能修理这把坏椅子吗？
Who is going to repair the washing machine? = 谁来修理洗衣机？

否定句:
He couldn't repair the television. = 他无法修理电视。
They didn't repair the damage immediately. = 他们没有立即修理损坏的地方。


PHRASE->
under repair = 正在修理中
in need of repair = 需要修理
beyond repair = 无法修理
repair shop = 修理店
carry out repairs = 进行修理
