dynamization:/ˌdaɪnəmaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |活力化；赋能；动力化 |The process of making something more dynamic or energetic; the act of imparting dynamism.  It can refer to the process of converting something into a dynamic state, or the infusion of energy or vitality into a system or process. | Example: The dynamization of the company's marketing strategy led to a significant increase in sales. 公司营销策略的活力化带来了销售额的显著增长。|近义词：energization, revitalization, activation |反义词：deactivation, stagnation, inertia
