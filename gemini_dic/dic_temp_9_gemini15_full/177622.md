rebukes:/rɪˈbjuːks/ | vt. | 斥责；训斥；谴责 | To express sharp disapproval or criticism of (someone) because of their behaviour or actions.  |例句：The teacher rebuked the student for his disruptive behavior. 老师因学生的扰乱行为而斥责了他。

例句：My boss rebuked me for missing the deadline. 我的老板因为我错过了截止日期而训斥了我。


近义词：reprimand, reproach, scold, chastise, admonish | 反义词：praise, commend, applaud | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rebukes his son for lying. 他斥责儿子说谎。
She rebukes her employees for their mistakes. 她斥责她的员工犯错。


一般过去时:
The judge rebuked the lawyer for his unprofessional conduct.  法官因律师的非专业行为而斥责了他。
My father rebuked me harshly for breaking the vase. 我父亲因为打破花瓶而严厉地斥责了我。


现在完成时:
The principal has rebuked several students this week for cheating. 本周校长因作弊已经斥责了几名学生。
She has repeatedly rebuked him for his careless behavior. 她一再斥责他粗心大意的行为。


过去完成时:
The manager had already rebuked the team before the presentation.  经理在演示之前已经斥责了团队。
He had rebuked her several times, but she continued to ignore his warnings. 他已经斥责了她好几次，但她继续无视他的警告。


将来时:
The teacher will rebuke the students if they continue to be disruptive. 如果学生继续扰乱秩序，老师将会斥责他们。
I am sure my parents will rebuke me for coming home late. 我确信我父母会因为我回家太晚而斥责我。


虚拟语气:
If he had known the consequences, he wouldn't have done it and wouldn't have been rebuked. 如果他知道后果，他不会那样做，也不会被斥责。
I wish I hadn't made that mistake; I wouldn't have been rebuked. 我真希望我没有犯那个错误；我就不会被斥责了。


被动语态:
The student was rebuked for his rudeness. 学生因其粗鲁无礼而受到斥责。
He was rebuked publicly for his actions. 他因自己的行为而受到公开斥责。


疑问句:
Will the teacher rebuke him for his lateness? 老师会因为他的迟到而斥责他吗？
Has he ever been rebuked by his supervisor? 他有没有被他的主管斥责过？


否定句:
The teacher did not rebuke the student for his honest mistake. 老师没有因为学生的诚实错误而斥责他。
She didn't rebuke him, but she clearly disapproved. 她没有斥责他，但她显然表示反对。

词源:
The word "rebuke" comes from the Old French word "rebuker," which means "to repulse, refuse."  This in turn derives from the Old French prefix "re-" (again) and "buker" (to push back, resist).  The meaning evolved from simply pushing back to expressing strong disapproval.
