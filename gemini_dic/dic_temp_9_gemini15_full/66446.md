exemplification:/ɪɡˌzemplifiˈkeɪʃən/| n. |例证；例示；典范|The act of exemplifying or serving as an example; an instance that serves as an example.  It involves providing concrete examples to illustrate or clarify a point, concept, or argument. |例句：His paintings are a perfect exemplification of modern art. 他的画作是现代艺术的完美例证。  The teacher used several case studies as exemplification of the theory. 老师用几个案例来举例说明这个理论。|近义词：illustration, instance, example, demonstration|反义词：refutation, contradiction|名词
