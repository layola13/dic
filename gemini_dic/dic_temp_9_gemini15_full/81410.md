grassless:/ˈɡræsləs/| adj.|无草的，没有草地的|Lacking grass; devoid of grass.  The desert landscape was largely grassless.|例句：The overgrazed pasture was almost grassless.  这片过度放牧的牧场几乎没有草了。

例句：The scorched earth was completely grassless after the wildfire.  野火过后，烧焦的土地完全没有草了。

反义词：grassy, verdant, lush
