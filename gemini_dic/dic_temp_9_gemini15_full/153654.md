pentremite: /ˈpɛntrəmaɪt/| n. |海百合类化石|A pentremite is a type of extinct marine animal belonging to the crinoid class, characterized by its cup-shaped body and a stem.  They are now found only as fossils. |Example: The limestone quarry yielded numerous pentremite fossils, indicating a thriving ancient marine environment.  = 石灰石采石场发现了许多海百合类化石，表明这里曾经存在着繁荣的古代海洋环境。|近义词: crinoid (broadly), fossil echinoderm |反义词: none |名词
