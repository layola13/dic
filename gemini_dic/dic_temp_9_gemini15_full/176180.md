re-advertise:/ˌriːˈædvərtaɪz/ | vt. |再次刊登广告；重新做广告 |To advertise something again, typically after it has not been successful the first time.  |例句：We had to re-advertise the position after failing to find a suitable candidate. 我们因为没有找到合适的候选人，不得不重新刊登招聘广告。|例句：The company decided to re-advertise their product with a new marketing strategy. 公司决定采用新的营销策略来重新为其产品做广告。|近义词：readvertise, advertise again |反义词：remove advertisement, cancel advertisement


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-advertise the product every year. = 他们每年都重新为该产品做广告。
We re-advertise the job opening if we don't get enough applicants. = 如果申请人不够多，我们会重新发布招聘信息。


一般过去时:
They re-advertised the concert after the first show was canceled. = 因为第一次演出取消了，他们重新为演唱会做了广告。
The company re-advertised the position with a higher salary. = 公司提高了薪水后重新发布了职位信息。


现在完成时:
They have re-advertised the position three times this month. = 这个月他们已经三次重新发布了这个职位信息。
We have re-advertised the product on social media. = 我们已经在社交媒体上重新为产品做了广告。


过去完成时:
They had re-advertised the property before we saw the listing. = 我们看到房产信息之前，他们已经重新为该房产做了广告。
The store had re-advertised the sale before the weekend rush. = 在周末高峰到来之前，商店已经重新为促销活动做了广告。


将来时:
They will re-advertise the job opening next week. = 他们下周将重新发布招聘信息。
The company will re-advertise the product in the fall. = 公司将在秋季重新为产品做广告。


虚拟语气:
If they had re-advertised sooner, they might have found a better candidate. = 如果他们早点重新发布广告，或许就能找到更好的候选人。
I wish they had re-advertised the concert in a different venue. = 我希望他们能在不同的场地重新为演唱会做广告。


被动语态:
The position was re-advertised after several unsuccessful interviews. = 经过几次不成功的面试后，该职位重新发布了招聘信息。
The product is being re-advertised with a new slogan. = 该产品正在使用新的广告语重新做广告。


疑问句:
Will they re-advertise the product after the negative reviews? = 负面评价之后，他们还会重新为产品做广告吗？
Have they re-advertised the position yet? = 他们已经重新发布职位信息了吗？


否定句:
They did not re-advertise the property, despite the low number of applicants. = 尽管申请人数很少，他们也没有重新为房产做广告。
The company will not re-advertise the product until next year. = 公司直到明年才会重新为产品做广告。
