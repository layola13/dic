词源:

The adverb "flaringly" derives from the adjective "flaring," which itself comes from the verb "flare."  "Flare" ultimately originates from the Old English word "flæra," meaning "a blaze or flame."  The evolution reflects a shift in meaning from a literal flame to a more figurative sense of showy display or brightness.
