quadriplegic:/kwɑːdrəˈplɛdʒɪk/| n. adj.|四肢瘫痪的；四肢麻痹的|A person affected by quadriplegia; suffering from paralysis of all four limbs.  A quadriplegic person can be described as suffering from paralysis of all four limbs. |例句：He was a quadriplegic after the car accident.  他车祸后成了四肢瘫痪。

例句：The rehabilitation center provides care for quadriplegic patients. 康复中心为四肢瘫痪患者提供护理。


近义词：tetraplegic

反义词：able-bodied


词性:  noun, adjective
