draying: /ˈdreɪɪŋ/ | v. | 拖运；搬运 | The act of hauling or carrying, typically heavy loads, often using a dray.  It often implies the use of a horse-drawn cart. | 例句：The farmer was draying hay to the barn.  农民正在用车运草料到谷仓。 | 例句：The heavy equipment was being drayed to the construction site.  重型设备正在被运送到建筑工地。| 近义词：hauling, carting, transporting | 反义词：delivering (in the sense of bringing something to a destination, rather than the process of moving it)

词源:
From Middle English *drayen, from Old North French *draier, from Vulgar Latin *trahere ("to draw, pull").  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Indo-European root *treh₂- ("to draw, pull").  The sense development reflects the historical use of horse-drawn carts for transportation.
