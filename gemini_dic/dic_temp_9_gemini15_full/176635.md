reaccredited:/ˌriːˈækrɛdɪtɪd/| adj.|重新认证的，再次认可的|Reaccredited refers to something or someone that has been accredited again, after a previous accreditation has been reviewed or has expired.  This typically applies to institutions, organizations, or individuals who have met the required standards for a second time. |Example: The university's engineering program was reaccredited after a rigorous review process.  该大学的工程项目在严格的审查流程后获得了重新认证。|近义词：recertified, revalidated, renewed|反义词：deaccredited, unaccredited
