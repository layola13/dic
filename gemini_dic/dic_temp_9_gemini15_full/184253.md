resolving:/rɪˈzɑːlvɪŋ/ | v. | 解决；决定；使坚定 | To find an answer or solution to (a problem, question, etc.); to firmly decide to do something; to make something (e.g., an image) clear or distinct. | 例句：They are resolving the conflict peacefully. 他们正在和平解决冲突。  The committee resolved to take immediate action. 委员会决定立即采取行动。 | 近义词：solving, settling, determining, deciding | 反义词：ignoring, neglecting, delaying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is resolving the issue with his neighbor. = 他正在解决与邻居的纠纷。
The council is resolving to improve public transport. = 理事会决定改善公共交通。


一般过去时:
She resolved the mystery of the missing painting. = 她解决了失踪画作的谜团。
They resolved to climb Mount Everest. = 他们决定攀登珠穆朗玛峰。


现在完成时:
They have resolved the technical difficulties. = 他们已经解决了技术难题。
We have resolved to support the charity. = 我们已经决定支持这家慈善机构。


过去完成时:
He had resolved to quit smoking before he met his wife. = 在遇见妻子之前，他已经决定戒烟。
They had resolved the conflict before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经解决了冲突。


将来时:
We will resolve the problem as soon as possible. = 我们将尽快解决这个问题。
She will resolve to be more organized next year. = 她明年将下定决心变得更有条理。


虚拟语气:
If I had the tools, I would resolve the issue quickly. = 如果我有工具，我会很快解决这个问题。
I wish I could resolve the conflict between them. = 我希望我能解决他们之间的冲突。


被动语态:
The dispute was resolved amicably. = 纠纷已得到友好解决。
The problem is being resolved by the engineers. = 工程师们正在解决这个问题。


疑问句:
How will they resolve this crisis? = 他们将如何解决这场危机？
Can we resolve this matter today? = 我们今天能解决这个问题吗？


否定句:
They could not resolve the differences between them. = 他们无法解决他们之间的分歧。
The committee failed to resolve the issue. = 委员会未能解决这个问题。


PHRASE->
resolve to do something = 决心做某事
resolve a conflict = 解决冲突
resolve a problem = 解决问题
resolve an issue = 解决问题
resolve a dispute = 解决纠纷


词源:
The word "resolving" comes from the Latin word "resolvere," which means "to loosen, untie, dissolve."  This reflects the sense of untying a knot or breaking down a complex problem into manageable parts.  The prefix "re-" emphasizes the repetition or completion of the action.
