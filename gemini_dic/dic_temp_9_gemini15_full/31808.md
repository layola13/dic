checkpoints:/ˈtʃɛkˌpɔɪnts/| n.|检查站；关卡；关键点；检查点|Points where progress is checked or controlled;  critical points in a process or system.|例句：The police set up checkpoints on the highway.  警察在高速公路上设置了检查站。
