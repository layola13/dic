nonfraternal: /ˌnɒnfrəˈtɜːrnl/ | adj. | 非兄弟的；非手足的 | Not related by blood or having the characteristics of siblings.  This term typically refers to twins who are not identical (dizygotic). | Example:  The twins, though raised together, were nonfraternal, possessing distinct genetic makeup.  = 虽然这对双胞胎一起长大，但他们是非同卵双胞胎，拥有不同的基因构成。 | 近义词： dizygotic, unlike, dissimilar | 反义词： fraternal, identical, similar
