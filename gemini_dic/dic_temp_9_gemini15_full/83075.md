gurring:/ˈɡɜːrɪŋ/| v.|咕噜咕噜地叫；发出轻柔的摩擦声|The sound of a cat purring or water flowing smoothly.|例句：The kitten was gurring contentedly on my lap. 小猫舒服地在我腿上咕噜咕噜叫。 The stream gurred its way down the mountain. 小溪哗哗地流下山。|近义词：purring, murmuring, rumbling|反义词：screaming, roaring
