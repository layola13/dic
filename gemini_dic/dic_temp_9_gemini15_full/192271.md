screenings:/ˈskriːnɪŋz/| n.| 筛选；放映；检查 | The act of showing films or other visual media.  It can also refer to the process of examining or testing something, such as for disease. | 例句：The film screenings were sold out. 电影放映场次售罄了。  The hospital offers free cancer screenings. 医院提供免费的癌症筛查。|近义词：showings, examinations, tests |反义词：N/A
