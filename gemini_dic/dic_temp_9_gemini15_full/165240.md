preinspection: /ˌpriːɪnˈspɛkʃən/ | n. | 预检；预验；事前检查 |  A preinspection is an examination or inspection carried out before the main inspection or event. It aims to identify potential problems or issues beforehand, allowing for corrective actions to be taken before the formal inspection.  | Example: The preinspection of the aircraft revealed a minor fault in the engine.  飞机的预检发现发动机存在一个小故障。  |近义词： preliminary inspection, advance check, prior examination | 反义词： post-inspection, final inspection | 名词
