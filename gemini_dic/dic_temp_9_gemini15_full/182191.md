remolding: /ˌriːˈmoʊldɪŋ/ | n. | 重塑；改造；重建 | The act of reshaping or restructuring something.  It often refers to a process of changing the form or structure of something, often to improve it or give it a new purpose.  |例句：The remodeling of the old house took six months.  老房子的改造花了六个月的时间。

The company is undergoing a major remodeling of its business strategy.  这家公司正在对其商业战略进行重大改造。


近义词：restructuring, renovation, rebuilding, transformation | 反义词：destruction, demolition


词性：名词
