dreamy:/ˈdriːmi/ | adj. |梦幻般的，沉思的，朦胧的 |  Evoking or suggestive of dreams; pleasantly unreal or fanciful.  Characterized by or suggestive of a dreamy state; lost in thought. |例句：The dreamy music lulled her to sleep. 梦幻般的音乐使她入睡了。
The artist's dreamy landscapes captivated the viewers. 艺术家梦幻般的风景画吸引了观众。

近义词：fantastical, ethereal, idyllic, visionary, romantic | 反义词：realistic, harsh, stark, practical
