propagation:/ˌprɒpəˈɡeɪʃən/ | /ˌprɑːpəˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. |传播；繁殖；蔓延|The spreading of something (e.g., a wave, a signal, or a plant) from its origin to a wider area.  It can refer to the transmission of a signal, the reproduction of plants, or the spread of an idea or disease.|例句：The propagation of sound waves through the air is essential for hearing.声音通过空气传播对于听觉至关重要。

The propagation of this invasive weed is a serious concern.这种入侵性杂草的蔓延是一个严重的问题。

近义词：dissemination, spread, proliferation, transmission | 反义词：suppression, extinction, eradication |  词性：名词
