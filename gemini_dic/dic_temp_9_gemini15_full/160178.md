ponder-over: /ˈpɒndər ˈoʊvər/ | v. | 仔细考虑，深思熟虑 | To think carefully and deeply about something. |例句：I've been pondering over your proposal for days. 我已经考虑你的提案好几天了。  |近义词：consider, contemplate, reflect on, mull over |反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ponders over the problem every day. 他每天都在思考这个问题。
They ponder over the meaning of life. 他们思考着生命的意义。

一般过去时:
She pondered over her decision for a long time. 她长时间地思考她的决定。
We pondered over the implications of the news. 我们仔细考虑了这个消息的含义。

现在完成时:
I have been pondering over this issue for weeks. 我已经思考这个问题好几个星期了。
They have pondered over the best course of action. 他们已经仔细考虑了最佳行动方案。

过去完成时:
He had pondered over the question for hours before he answered. 在回答问题之前，他已经思考了这个问题几个小时。
She had pondered over the consequences before making the choice.  在她做出选择之前，她已经仔细考虑了后果。


将来时:
I will ponder over your suggestion before I make a decision. 我会在做决定之前仔细考虑你的建议。
They will ponder over the data before drawing conclusions. 他们会在得出结论之前仔细思考数据。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would ponder over the matter more thoroughly. 如果我有更多的时间，我会更仔细地考虑这件事。
I wish I had pondered over the contract more carefully. 我希望我当时更仔细地考虑了合同。


被动语态:
The problem was pondered over by the committee for many meetings. 这个问题在委员会的多次会议上被仔细考虑。
The proposal has been pondered over by the board of directors.  董事会已经仔细考虑了这个提案。


疑问句:
Have you pondered over the risks involved? 你考虑过其中涉及的风险吗？
Will they ponder over the alternatives? 他们会考虑其他方案吗？


否定句:
He didn't ponder over the details enough. 他没有充分考虑细节。
They didn't ponder over the implications of their actions. 他们没有考虑他们行为的含义。
