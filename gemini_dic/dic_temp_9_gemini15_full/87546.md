heterology:/ˌhɛtəˈrɒlədʒi/| n. |异质性；他律性；不相容性 |The study of the inherent limitations of systems of classification and the impossibility of fully describing any system without reference to something outside of the system.  It explores the inherent contradictions and paradoxes within systems of knowledge and representation.  Heterlogy highlights the ways in which systems inevitably fail to capture their objects fully. | Example:  The inherent heterology of language means that any attempt to define a word perfectly will always fall short.  语言的内在异质性意味着任何试图完美定义一个词的尝试都将失败。|近义词：inconsistency, paradox, contradiction |反义词：homogeneity, consistency, coherence |名词
