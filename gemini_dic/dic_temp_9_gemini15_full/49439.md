delphin:/ˈdɛlfɪn/| n. |海豚|A marine mammal belonging to the family Delphinidae, characterized by their streamlined bodies, intelligence, and social behavior.  They are known for their playful nature and acrobatic displays. | Example: Dolphins are highly intelligent creatures known for their complex communication and social structures. 海豚是高度聪明的生物，以其复杂的沟通和社会结构而闻名。|近义词：dolphin (same meaning)|反义词：N/A
