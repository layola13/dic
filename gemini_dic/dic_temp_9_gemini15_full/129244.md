nocturnal:/nɒkˈtɜːnəl/ | adj. |夜行的；夜间的 |  Active at night.  |例句：Owls are nocturnal birds. 猫头鹰是夜行动物。
例句：Many nocturnal animals have excellent night vision.  许多夜行动物具有极好的夜视能力。|近义词：nightly, nighttime |反义词：diurnal
