evened:/ˈiːvənd/| vt.| 使平坦；使均匀；使相等 | To make something level, smooth, or uniform; to make things equal. |例句：He evened the piles of papers. 他把几堆文件弄平了。 |例句：She evened out the discrepancies in the accounts. 她消除了账目上的差异。|近义词：leveled, smoothed, balanced |反义词：unevened, roughened, unbalanced


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He evens the playing field for everyone. 他为每个人创造了公平的竞争环境。
She evens the workload among her team members. 她平均分配团队成员的工作量。


一般过去时:
The carpenter evened the surface of the wood. 木匠把木头的表面弄平了。
The referee evened the score by awarding a penalty. 裁判判罚点球，使比分持平。


现在完成时:
They have evened out the differences between the two groups. 他们已经消除了两组之间的差异。
She has evened the distribution of resources. 她已经平均分配了资源。


过去完成时:
He had evened the accounts before submitting the report. 他在提交报告之前已经核实了账目。
They had evened the playing field before the game started. 比赛开始前，他们已经创造了公平的竞争环境。


将来时:
The construction crew will even the ground before building the house. 建筑队将在建房前将地面平整。
She will even out the differences in opinion. 她将消除意见分歧。


虚拟语气:
If he evened the score, we would win. 如果他扳平比分，我们就能赢。
If she had evened the workload earlier, she wouldn't be so stressed now. 如果她早点平均分配工作量，现在就不会这么紧张了。


被动语态:
The field was evened for the game. 比赛场地被平整了。
The discrepancies in the data were evened out by the statistician. 数据中的差异由统计员进行了平衡。


疑问句:
Have they evened the playing field yet? 他们已经把场地弄平了吗？
Did she even out the discrepancies? 她是否消除了差异？


否定句:
He didn't even the surface properly. 他没有把表面弄平整。
The committee didn't even out the voting power. 委员会没有平均分配投票权。
