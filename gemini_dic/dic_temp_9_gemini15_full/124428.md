motorcycling:/ˈmoʊtərsaɪklɪŋ/ | n. | 摩托车运动；骑摩托车 | The activity of riding a motorcycle.  It encompasses various aspects, from leisurely rides to competitive racing. | Example: He enjoys motorcycling on weekends. 他周末喜欢骑摩托车。 |近义词:motorcycling, motorbike riding | 反义词: N/A

The race was won by a skilled motorcyclist. = 比赛由一名技术娴熟的摩托车手赢得。 (This focuses on the race and the motorcyclist)
