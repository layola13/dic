cachexia:/kæˈɛksiə/| n. |恶病质，消瘦|A condition of wasting away of the body, typically associated with chronic disease or malnutrition.  It is characterized by extreme weight loss, muscle atrophy, fatigue, and weakness. |Example:  The cancer patient suffered from severe cachexia, losing significant weight and muscle mass.  癌症患者患有严重的恶病质，体重和肌肉大量减少。|近义词：wasting, emaciation, atrophy|反义词：robustness, health, well-being
