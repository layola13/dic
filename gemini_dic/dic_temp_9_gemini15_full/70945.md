firefighters:/ˈfaɪərˌfaɪtərz/| n.|消防员|People who fight fires, typically as members of a fire department. They are trained to extinguish fires, rescue people from burning buildings, and provide emergency medical assistance.|例句：The firefighters bravely battled the blaze and saved several lives. 消防员英勇地与大火搏斗，挽救了几条生命。
The firefighters arrived quickly and efficiently controlled the situation. 消防员迅速到达并有效控制了局面。|近义词：firemen, fire crews|反义词:N/A
