entrancement:/ɪnˈtrænsmənt/| n. |迷恋；着迷；入迷状态|The state of being entranced; fascination; captivation.  It implies a state of being completely absorbed or captivated by something, often to the point of being unaware of one's surroundings. |例句：Her entrancement with the painting was evident in her prolonged gaze.  她长时间凝视油画，可见她对其着了迷。

例句：The magician's performance held the audience in a state of complete entrancement. 魔术师的表演让观众完全入了迷。


近义词：captivation, fascination, enchantment, absorption, mesmerism|反义词：indifference, apathy, boredom|词性：名词
