clearways:/ˈklɪəweɪz/| n.| 清洁路段，畅通路段 | Sections of road where traffic is allowed to flow freely, often with restrictions on parking or stopping.  These are designed to improve traffic flow, particularly during peak hours. | Example:  The city council is planning to implement more clearways to reduce congestion.  市议会正计划实施更多畅通路段以缓解交通拥堵。|近义词：freeway (部分情况下), traffic lanes, open road|反义词：bottleneck, traffic jam
