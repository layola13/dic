predetermination:/ˌpriːdɪˌtɜːrmɪˈneɪʃən/| n.|预先决定；预定；宿命论|The act of determining something beforehand; a decision made in advance.  It can also refer to the belief that events are predetermined by fate or destiny.|例句：The predetermination of the winner was evident from the biased judging.  赢家的预先决定从有偏见的评判中显而易见。

例句：His strong belief in predetermination led him to accept whatever happened as fate. 他坚信预定论，因此接受任何发生的事情都是命运。


近义词：predestination, foreordination, prearrangement


反义词：chance, accident, spontaneity


词性：名词
