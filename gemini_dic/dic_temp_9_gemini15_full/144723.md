ovenbird: /ˈʌvənˌbɜːrd/ | n. | 灶鸟 | A small, brownish New World warbler (genus *Seiurus*) that nests on the ground and has a distinctive song resembling the sound of an oven being heated.  |  The ovenbird is known for its distinctive song.  The ovenbird builds its nest on the ground, often under a log or rock. | 近义词：warbler (a more general term) | 反义词：无
