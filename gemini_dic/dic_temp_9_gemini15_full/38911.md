confessingly: /kənˈfɛsɪŋli/ | adv. |坦白地；承认地|In a manner that suggests confession or admission of guilt or fault.  It implies a reluctant or apologetic acknowledgement.|例句：Confessingly, I ate the last cookie. = 坦白说，我吃了最后一块饼干。

例句：He confessingly admitted his mistake to his boss. = 他坦白地向老板承认了他的错误。


近义词：frankly, openly, honestly, candidly


反义词：secretively, deceptively, hypocritically


词性: 副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and does not have verb conjugations like "what".  It modifies verbs, describing how an action is performed.  Therefore, tense considerations don't apply to the word itself, but rather to the verb it modifies.


PHRASE->
There are no common set phrases specifically using "confessingly". Its usage is generally stand-alone to modify a verb within a sentence.
