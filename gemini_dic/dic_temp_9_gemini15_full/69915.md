It can also refer to a cylindrical or ring-shaped device used in various applications, such as a metal band around a handle or a tool for shaping wood. |例句：The teacher used a ferule to discipline the unruly student. 老师用教鞭惩罚那个不守规矩的学生。

The printer used a ferule to smooth the paper. 印刷工用压印滚筒压平纸张。|近义词：rod, cane, paddle, ruler|反义词：reward, praise
