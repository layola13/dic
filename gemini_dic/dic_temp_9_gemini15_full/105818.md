kistful:/ˈkɪstfəl/| adj.|渴望的；怀旧的；忧郁的 |Feeling a longing for something lost or gone; melancholic; nostalgic. |例句：She felt a wistful longing for her childhood home. 她渴望回到童年的家。(怀旧的)

例句：He cast a wistful glance back at the disappearing coastline. 他依依不舍地回头看了一眼逐渐消失的海岸线。(忧郁的)


近义词：nostalgic, yearning, pensive, melancholic, dreamy

反义词：cheerful, joyful, happy, content


词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He feels wistful about his past. = 他对过去感到怀旧。
She often looks wistful when she talks about her childhood. = 她谈到童年时，常常露出渴望的神情。


一般过去时:
He felt wistful as he watched the sunset. = 他看着日落，感到一丝忧伤。
She looked wistfully at the old photograph. = 她怀旧地望着那张旧照片。


现在完成时:
She has felt wistful for many years about her lost love. = 多年来，她一直对失去的爱人感到怀旧。
He has been wistful about his college days ever since he graduated. = 自从毕业后，他就一直对大学时光感到怀旧。


过去完成时:
He had felt wistful about his missed opportunity before he made the decision. = 在他做出决定之前，他已经对错过的机会感到惋惜。
She had been wistful about her old friends before she moved to the city. = 在她搬到城市之前，她一直对老朋友们感到怀旧。


将来时:
I think I will feel wistful when I leave this place. = 我想离开这里的时候我会感到怀旧。
They will likely feel wistful about their time abroad after they return home. = 回国后，他们很可能会怀念他们在国外的时光。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I wouldn't have left; I would have felt wistful about staying. = 如果我知道，我不会离开的；我会怀念留下的时光。
I wish I could have felt more wistful about that moment. = 我希望我当时能对那一刻怀念更多一些。


被动语态:
His past experiences were often described as wistful. = 他过去的经历常常被形容为怀旧的。
The song was perceived as wistful by many listeners. = 这首歌被许多听众认为是忧郁的。


疑问句:
Does this painting evoke a wistful feeling in you? = 这幅画在你心中唤起了一种渴望的感觉吗？
Is there anything that makes you feel wistful? = 有什么东西让你感到怀旧吗？


否定句:
He didn't feel wistful; he was angry. = 他并没有感到怀旧；他很生气。
She wasn't wistful about her departure; she was excited. = 她并没有对她的离开感到怀旧；她很兴奋。
