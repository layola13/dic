gestour:/dʒɛˈstʊər/| n. |姿态，姿势；手势|A gesture is a movement of the body, especially of the hands and arms, that expresses an idea or meaning.  Gestures are often used to emphasize speech or to communicate without words.  For example, a thumbs-up gesture typically signifies approval. |例句：He made a gesture of dismissal. 他做了一个打发人的手势。|例句：Her gestures were eloquent and expressive. 她的手势生动而富有表现力。|近义词：posture, motion, sign, signal |反义词：stillness, immobility|名词
