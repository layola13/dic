nitwits:/ˈnɪtwɪts/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A nitwit is a foolish or stupid person.  The word is informal and often used humorously or dismissively.|例句：Don't be such a nitwit!  别那么傻！

例句：He acted like a complete nitwit in the meeting. 他在会议上表现得像个十足的傻瓜。


近义词：fool, idiot, moron, dunce, simpleton

反义词：genius, intellect, sage, mastermind


词性:名词
