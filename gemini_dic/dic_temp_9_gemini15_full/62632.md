enforcing:/ɪnˈfɔːrsɪŋ/| v.|实施；执行；强制；强迫|The act of putting something into effect; compelling observance of a law or rule.  It involves making sure a law, rule, or agreement is followed.|例句：The police are enforcing the new traffic laws. = 警方正在执行新的交通法规。

例句：The school is enforcing a strict dress code. = 学校正在执行严格的着装规定。


近义词：implementing, executing, upholding, enforcing, compelling

反义词：neglecting, ignoring, disregarding, abandoning


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They enforce the rules strictly. = 他们严格执行规章制度。
The government enforces environmental regulations. = 政府执行环境法规。


一般过去时:
The court enforced the contract. = 法院强制执行了合同。
The teacher enforced the classroom rules. = 老师强制执行了课堂规则。


现在完成时:
They have enforced the new policy since last year. = 他们从去年开始就一直在执行这项新政策。
The company has enforced stricter safety measures. = 公司已经实施了更严格的安全措施。


过去完成时:
The police had enforced a curfew before the riot. = 暴乱前警方已经实施了宵禁。
They had enforced the law for many years before it was repealed. = 在这项法律被废除之前，他们已经执行了多年。


将来时:
They will enforce the law more strictly next year. = 他们明年将更严格地执行这项法律。
The government will enforce new taxes. = 政府将征收新税。


虚拟语气:
If they enforced the rules properly, there would be fewer accidents. = 如果他们正确地执行规则，事故就会减少。
I wish they enforced the parking regulations more effectively. = 我希望他们能更有效地执行停车规定。


被动语态:
The law was enforced by the police. = 这项法律由警方执行。
The new rules are being enforced throughout the company. = 新规正在公司范围内实施。


疑问句:
Are they enforcing the speed limits? = 他们正在执行限速吗？
How will the new regulations be enforced? = 新规将如何执行？


否定句:
They didn't enforce the contract properly. = 他们没有正确执行合同。
The school didn't enforce the dress code consistently. = 学校没有始终如一地执行着装规范。
