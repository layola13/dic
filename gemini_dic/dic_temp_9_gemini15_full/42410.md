countercomplaint: /ˈkaʊntər kəmˈpleɪnt/ | n. |反诉，反控告 | A countercomplaint is a formal response to a complaint, where the person or entity being accused makes their own accusations against the original complainant.  It's essentially a lawsuit filed in response to a lawsuit. | Example: After being sued for breach of contract, the company filed a countercomplaint alleging fraud on the part of the plaintiff.  反诉：在被控违反合同后，该公司提起反诉，指控原告欺诈。

| Example:  The defendant filed a countercomplaint accusing the plaintiff of defamation. 被告提出反诉，指控原告诽谤。|近义词： countersuit,  recrimination |反义词：complaint (原告状)|词性:noun
