despair:/dɪˈspɛər/| n. |绝望，失望|The feeling of utter hopelessness and loss of courage.  It often involves a loss of hope and a feeling of being overwhelmed by problems.|例句：He fell into despair after losing his job. 他失业后陷入了绝望。|例句：Her despair was palpable as she watched her business crumble. 她看着自己的生意垮掉，绝望之情溢于言表。|近义词：despondency, hopelessness, dejection|反义词：hope, optimism, confidence|名词
