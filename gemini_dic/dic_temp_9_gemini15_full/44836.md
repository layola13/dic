crustaceous:/krʌˈsteɪʃəs/ | adj. | 甲壳类的；似甲壳的 | Relating to or characteristic of crustaceans.  The word describes something having a hard shell or rind, similar to that of a crustacean. | The crustaceous exoskeleton protects the crab from predators.  甲壳状的外骨骼保护螃蟹免受捕食者的侵害。 The old bread was hard and crustaceous.  旧面包又硬又像甲壳。|  shelled, crusted | soft, smooth |
