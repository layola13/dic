dioxides:/ˈdaɪɒksaɪdz/| n. |二氧化物 |Dioxides are chemical compounds containing two oxygen atoms.  They can be formed by various elements and have diverse properties and applications. For example, carbon dioxide (CO2) is a greenhouse gas, while silicon dioxide (SiO2) is the main component of sand.  |Example:  Many industrial processes produce harmful dioxides as byproducts.  许多工业过程产生有害的二氧化物作为副产品。

The release of sulfur dioxides into the atmosphere contributes to acid rain. 二氧化硫的排放导致酸雨。|近义词：oxides|反义词：无|名词
