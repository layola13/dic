impertinence:/ɪmˈpɜːtɪnəns/ | n. | 冒失；无礼；放肆 |  The quality of being impertinent; lack of respect or courtesy; insolence.  It often involves being rude or disrespectful, especially to someone in authority. | Example: Her impertinence towards the judge was shocking.  她的无礼态度震惊了法官。  The children's impertinence was finally dealt with by their parents. 孩子们的放肆最终被他们的父母处理了。 | 近义词：insolence, rudeness, disrespect, impudence, audacity | 反义词：respect, courtesy, politeness, deference
