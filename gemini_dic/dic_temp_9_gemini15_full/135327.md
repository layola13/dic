nonpedagogically: /ˌnɒnˌpedəˈɡɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 非教学地，非教育性地 |  In a manner that is not related to or suitable for teaching.  It implies a lack of pedagogical approach, method, or intention. |


例句：

The information was presented nonpedagogically, making it difficult for the audience to understand.  = 信息的呈现方式是非教学性的，使得听众难以理解。

He approached the subject nonpedagogically, focusing solely on the technical aspects. = 他以非教学的方式处理这个主题，仅仅关注技术层面。


近义词：unpedagogically, un-teacherly, didactically (in a negative sense), informally


反义词：pedagogically, educationally, instructively, didactically (in a positive sense)


词性：副词
