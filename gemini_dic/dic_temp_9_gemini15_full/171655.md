pulseless:/ˈpʌlsləs/| adj.|无脉搏的，没有脉搏的|Lacking a pulse; without a heartbeat.  The term describes a state where no pulse can be detected in a person or animal.|例句：The paramedics found the victim pulseless and unresponsive.  急救人员发现受害者没有脉搏且没有反应。

例句：After several minutes of CPR, the patient remained pulseless.  经过几分钟的心肺复苏后，病人仍然没有脉搏。


近义词：lifeless, apulse, pulseless, without a pulse

反义词：alive, with a pulse
