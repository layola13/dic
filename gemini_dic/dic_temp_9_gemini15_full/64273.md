equisetaceous:/ˌɛkwɪˈsɛteɪʃəs/| adj.|木贼属的；木贼状的| Relating to or characteristic of the genus *Equisetum* (horsetails).  The term often describes plants with jointed, hollow stems and whorled leaves, resembling horsetails. | The equisetaceous plants thrived in the marshy area.  木贼属植物在沼泽地带茁壮成长。|  jointed, rush-like|  flowering, leafy| adj.
