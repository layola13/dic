mandio: /mænˈdiːoʊ/ | n. |木薯|A starchy tuberous root, *Manihot esculenta*, cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions for its edible roots and stems.  It is a major source of carbohydrates in many parts of the world and is also known as cassava.| 例句：Cassava, also known as mandioca, is a staple food in many countries. 木薯，也称为木薯，是许多国家的 staple food。 |近义词：cassava, yuca |反义词：无
