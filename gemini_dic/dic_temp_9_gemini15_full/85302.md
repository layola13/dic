harnessers: /ˈhɑːrnɪsər/ | n. | 驾驭者；利用者；治理者 | A person or thing that harnesses something, typically power or resources.  Those who utilize or control a force or system for a specific purpose. | Example:  The skilled harnessers of wind energy are crucial to the transition to renewable power. 熟练驾驭风能的人对于向可再生能源转型至关重要。


近义词: controllers, exploiters, managers, users

反义词: neglecters, wasters


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company harnessers its resources efficiently. = 这家公司有效地利用其资源。
He harnesses the power of social media to promote his business. = 他利用社交媒体的力量来推广他的业务。


一般过去时:
They harnessed the river's power to generate electricity. = 他们利用河水的能量发电。
She harnessed her creativity to solve the problem. = 她发挥她的创造力来解决这个问题。


现在完成时:
We have harnessed solar energy for years. = 我们多年来一直在利用太阳能。
The scientists have harnessed the power of nanotechnology for medical applications. = 科学家们已经将纳米技术的能量用于医疗应用。


过去完成时:
They had harnessed the horses before the journey began. = 在旅程开始之前，他们已经套好了马。
The engineers had harnessed the water power to irrigate the fields. = 工程师们已经利用水力灌溉田地。


将来时:
We will harness the power of artificial intelligence to improve efficiency. = 我们将利用人工智能的力量来提高效率。
They will harness the wind to generate clean energy. = 他们将利用风力发电。


虚拟语气:
If we harnessed renewable resources, we would reduce carbon emissions. = 如果我们利用可再生资源，我们将减少碳排放。
I wish we could harness the power of the ocean currents. = 我希望我们能够利用洋流的力量。


被动语态:
The river's power was harnessed to irrigate the fields. = 河水的能量被用来灌溉田地。
The potential of geothermal energy is being harnessed for electricity generation. = 地热能的潜力正在被利用来发电。


疑问句:
How can we harness the power of the sun? = 我们如何利用太阳的能量？
Have they harnessed the potential of this new technology? = 他们是否利用了这项新技术的潜力？


否定句:
They did not harness the full potential of the project. = 他们没有充分利用该项目的潜力。
We haven't harnessed the power of big data yet. = 我们还没有利用大数据的力量。
