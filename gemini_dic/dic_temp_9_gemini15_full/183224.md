representer:/ˌrɛprɪˈsɛntər/| n. |代表；代理人；表现者|A representer is someone or something that represents something else.  This can be a person acting on behalf of another, a symbol standing for an idea, or a mathematical function used in machine learning.  |例句：He acted as a representer for the company at the conference. 他在会议上担任公司代表。The cross is a representer of Christianity. 十字架是基督教的象征。|近义词：representative, agent, delegate, symbol|反义词：represented (past participle of represent, indicating the opposite action)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist's work represents her inner world. = 艺术家的作品代表了她内心的世界。
This symbol represents danger. = 这个符号代表危险。


一般过去时:
He represented his country at the Olympics. = 他代表他的国家参加奥运会。
She represented the views of her constituents. = 她代表了她选民的观点。


现在完成时:
She has represented this company for ten years. = 她已经代表这家公司十年了。
They have successfully represented their case in court. = 他们在法庭上成功地代理了他们的案子。


过去完成时:
He had represented the company before the merger. = 在合并之前，他曾代表过该公司。
She had already represented herself in court once. = 她之前已经在法庭上为自己辩护过一次。


将来时:
She will represent the firm at the upcoming trade show. = 她将在即将到来的贸易展会上代表公司。
He will represent his class in the debate. = 他将在辩论中代表他的班级。


虚拟语气:
If he were to represent us, we would have a stronger case. = 如果他来代表我们，我们的案子会更有力。
I wish I could represent my country at the games. = 我希望我能代表我的国家参加比赛。


被动语态:
The company was well represented at the meeting. = 公司在会议上有很好的代表。
His interests were represented by his lawyer. = 他的利益由他的律师代表。


疑问句:
Who will represent our interests? = 谁来代表我们的利益？
Does this image represent hope or despair? = 这幅图像代表希望还是绝望？


否定句:
He did not represent the company’s views accurately. = 他没有准确地代表公司的观点。
This painting does not represent reality. = 这幅画不代表现实。
