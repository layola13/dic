eosaurus:/ˌiːoʊˈsɔːrəs/| n. |始祖鸟类|A hypothetical early reptile or reptilian ancestor, often depicted as a small, lizard-like creature.  The term is now largely outdated and is not used in scientific literature, as the understanding of early reptile evolution has advanced significantly.|  例句：The eosaurus is a concept from older paleontological theories, now superseded by more accurate models of early reptile evolution.  始祖鸟类是旧的古生物学理论中的一个概念，现在已被更准确的早期爬行动物进化模型所取代。|近义词：proto-reptile (though more precise) |反义词：N/A
