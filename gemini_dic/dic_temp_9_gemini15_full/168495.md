problem:/ˈprɑːbləm/ /ˈprɒbləm/| n.|问题，难题，麻烦|a matter or situation regarded as unwelcome or harmful and needing to be dealt with and overcome.|The main problem is lack of funding.  主要问题是资金不足。|We have a serious problem with our supplier. 我们与供应商之间存在严重问题。|issue, difficulty, challenge|solution, answer|名词
