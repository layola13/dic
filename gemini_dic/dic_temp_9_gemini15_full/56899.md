dost:/dʌst/| v.| 你做，你拥有| Archaic second-person singular present tense of "do".  It is a contraction of "doest," which is itself a contraction of "doth."  It's almost exclusively found in older texts, particularly literature from the Shakespearean era. | Example: Thou dost know what I mean. = 你知道我的意思。 | 近义词:doest, doth (archaic)| 反义词:None (Due to archaic nature and meaning)|动词


USAGE->
This word is archaic and has no modern tense conjugations.  Its usage is limited to historical contexts.  It is the second person singular present tense of "to do".
