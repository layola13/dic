panned:/pænd/ | vt. n. |严厉批评；彻底失败；淘洗；寻找（金矿等）|to criticize severely; to be a complete failure; to wash (e.g., gravel) to separate valuable minerals; to search for (gold, etc.) |例句：The critics panned the movie.  评论家们严厉批评了这部电影。|The project was completely panned.  这个项目彻底失败了.|近义词：criticized, condemned, slammed, denounced, failed |反义词：praised, acclaimed, lauded
