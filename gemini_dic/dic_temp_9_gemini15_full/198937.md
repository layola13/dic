sift:/sɪft/| vt. n.|筛选；筛分；细查|to separate finer particles from coarser ones by passing through a sieve or other device with holes; to examine carefully in order to separate out what is wanted or needed.|例句：She sifted the flour before adding it to the cake batter. 她把面粉过筛后才加入蛋糕糊中。

例句：The police carefully sifted through the evidence. 警方仔细地审查了证据。

近义词：screen, filter, examine, scrutinize|反义词：combine, mix, aggregate|动词
