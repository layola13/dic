gloweringly:/ˈɡlaʊərɪŋli/| adv.|怒视地，怒狠狠地，凶狠地|In a manner that shows anger or displeasure; fiercely. It describes an action or state performed with a fierce, angry stare.|例句：He glowered at me gloweringly. 他怒狠狠地瞪着我。


例句：She looked at him gloweringly, her lips pressed together in a thin line. 她怒视着 er,嘴唇紧紧地抿成一条线。


近义词：angrily, fiercely, menacingly, wrathfully


反义词：kindly, gently, lovingly, amiably


词源:  Derived from the verb "glower," which comes from the Middle English word "glowren," meaning "to stare angrily."  The suffix "-ingly" indicates a manner or way of doing something.
