reinserted:/ˌriːɪnˈsɜːrtɪd/ | v. |重新插入；重新嵌入 |  To insert something again into its original or a similar place.  |例句：The surgeon reinserted the catheter after it became dislodged.  外科医生在导管脱落后重新插入了它。

例句：The administrator reinserted the deleted file into the system. 管理员将已删除的文件重新插入系统。


近义词：reinsert, replace, reintroduce, restore


反义词：remove, delete, extract


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reinserts the SIM card into his phone. = 他把SIM卡重新插入手机。
She reinserts the missing paragraph into the essay. = 她将缺失的段落重新插入文章。

一般过去时:
They reinserted the image into the presentation. = 他们将图像重新插入演示文稿。
He reinserted the CD into the player. = 他将CD重新放入了播放器。

现在完成时:
I have reinserted the data into the spreadsheet. = 我已将数据重新插入电子表格。
She has reinserted the quote into her speech. = 她已将引语重新插入她的演讲。

过去完成时:
They had reinserted the plug before the power came back on. = 电力恢复之前，他们已经重新插入了插头。
She had reinserted the battery before trying again. = 她尝试之前已经重新插入了电池。

将来时:
He will reinsert the screws once the glue is dry. = 胶水干了之后，他会重新插入螺丝。
They will reinsert the document into the folder. = 他们会将文档重新插入文件夹。

虚拟语气:

否定句:
