flatline:/ˈflætlaɪn/| v. n. adj.| 平线；（心电图）直线；停滞不前；|  To become completely inactive or unresponsive; to show a flat line on a heart monitor, indicating cardiac arrest; to stagnate or cease to progress. |例句：His heart flatlined during the surgery. 手术期间他的心跳停止了。The project has been flatlining for months. 这个项目已经停滞了几个月了。|近义词：stagnate, cease, stop, level off |反义词：improve, progress, surge, rise
