medicate:/ˈmɛdɪkeɪt/| vt.|给…服药；治疗|To treat (a disease or injury) with medicine.  It also means to give medicine to (someone).|例句：The doctor medicated the patient for the pain. 医生给病人服药止痛。|例句：She medicated herself with over-the-counter drugs. 她用非处方药自己治疗。|近义词：treat, dose, drug|反义词：neglect, ignore

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor medicates patients daily. = 医生每天给病人服药。
He medicates his anxiety with exercise. = 他通过运动来缓解焦虑。


一般过去时:
She medicated her son's fever with ibuprofen. = 她用布洛芬给儿子退烧。
They medicated the wound to prevent infection. = 他们对伤口进行药物处理以防止感染。


现在完成时:
He has medicated himself for years. = 他多年来一直自己服药。
The hospital has medicated hundreds of patients this week. = 这家医院本周已经治疗了数百名病人。


过去完成时:
She had medicated the dog before leaving for work. = 她上班前已经给狗服过药了。
They had medicated the injured soldier before transporting him. = 他们在转移受伤士兵之前已经给他服过药了。


将来时:
The nurse will medicate the patient after the surgery. = 手术后护士会给病人服药。
I will medicate my headache with aspirin. = 我会用阿司匹林治疗我的头痛。


虚拟语气:
If I were sick, I would medicate myself. = 如果我生病了，我会自己服药。
If I had known it was so serious, I would have medicated him sooner. = 如果我知道情况如此严重，我会早点给他服药。


被动语态:
The patient was medicated three times a day. = 病人一天服药三次。
The wound was medicated thoroughly. = 伤口得到了彻底的药物处理。


疑问句:
Did the doctor medicate the patient? = 医生给病人服药了吗？
How often should I medicate my child? = 我应该多久给孩子服药一次？


否定句:
The doctor did not medicate the patient with that drug. = 医生没有用那种药给病人服药。
I will not medicate myself without a doctor's prescription. = 我不会在没有医生处方的情况下自己服药。


PHRASE->
self-medicate = 自己服药
medicate against = 预防性用药
medicate with = 用…药物治疗
medicate for = 治疗…的药物
