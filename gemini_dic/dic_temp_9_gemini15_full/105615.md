kinesipathy: /ˌkaɪnɛˈsɪpəθi/ | n. | 运动疗法；动觉疗法 | The study and treatment of diseases through movement and physical activity.  Kinesipathy utilizes various techniques to improve physical function, reduce pain, and enhance overall well-being. It's a holistic approach that considers the interconnectedness of the mind, body, and spirit.  | Example:  The patient underwent kinesipathy sessions to improve their range of motion after surgery.  病人术后接受了运动疗法来改善他们的活动范围。| 近义词: physiotherapy, physical therapy, movement therapy | 反义词: inactivity, immobilization | 名词
