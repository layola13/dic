depressurization:/ˌdiːˌprɛʃəraɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |减压；降压；泄压|The process of reducing pressure, especially in a container or enclosed space.  It often refers to the loss of pressure in a system, such as an aircraft cabin or a pressure vessel.|例句：The rapid depressurization of the aircraft cabin caused oxygen masks to deploy.  飞机客舱的快速减压导致氧气面罩弹出。


例句：The engineers are investigating the cause of the depressurization in the underwater habitat.工程师们正在调查水下栖息地减压的原因。


近义词：pressure reduction, pressure drop, decompression|反义词：pressurization


词性：名词
