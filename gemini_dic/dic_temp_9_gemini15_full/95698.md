inconform:/ɪnˈkɑːnfɔːrm/| adj. |不一致的；不符合的；不服从的 | Not conforming; not agreeing or according; disobedient.  |The geological strata showed an unconformity, indicating a period of erosion or non-deposition. 地质岩层显示出不整合，表明存在一段侵蚀或不沉积时期。 |近义词：discordant, nonconformist, deviant |反义词：conforming, compliant, obedient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The data does not conform to the expected pattern. = 数据与预期模式不符。
His behaviour doesn't conform to company regulations. = 他的行为不符合公司规定。

一般过去时:
The results did not conform to the hypothesis. = 结果与假设不符。
The building did not conform to building codes. = 这座建筑物不符合建筑规范。

现在完成时:
The evidence has not conformed to our initial predictions. = 证据与我们最初的预测不符。
Their actions have not conformed to the established norms. = 他们的行为不符合既定的规范。

过去完成时:
The previous findings had not conformed to the new data. = 先前的发现与新数据不符。
His earlier statements had not conformed to his recent testimony.  = 他早前的陈述与他最近的证词不符。

将来时:
The new software will not conform to the old system. = 新软件将与旧系统不兼容。
We expect the final product will conform to our specifications. = 我们预计最终产品将符合我们的规格。

虚拟语气:
If the data conformed to the model, we would have accepted the theory. = 如果数据符合模型，我们将接受该理论。
It is essential that the design conform to safety standards. = 设计必须符合安全标准，这是至关重要的。

被动语态:
The report was found to not conform to the required format. = 发现报告不符合要求的格式。
The building plans were rejected because they did not conform to regulations. = 建筑图纸被拒绝，因为它们不符合规定。

疑问句:
Does this proposal conform to our company's policy? = 这个提案符合我们公司的政策吗？
Does the new design conform to the existing standards? = 新设计符合现有标准吗？

否定句:
The product does not conform to safety standards. = 该产品不符合安全标准。
His actions did not conform to the law. = 他的行为不合法。
