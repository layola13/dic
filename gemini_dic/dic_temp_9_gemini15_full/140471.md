old-maids:/oʊldˈmeɪdz/| n.| 老姑娘；剩女 | A woman who is unmarried, especially one considered past the usual age for marriage.  The term is often used in a derogatory or humorous way. | Example:  She was labeled an old maid by her gossipy neighbors. 她被她爱说闲话的邻居贴上了老姑娘的标签。|近义词：spinster, unmarried woman|反义词：bride, newlywed
