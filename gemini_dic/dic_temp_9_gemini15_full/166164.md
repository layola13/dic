preprove:/ˌpriːˈpruːv/| v.|预先证明；预先检验|To prove something beforehand; to test something in advance to ensure its validity or effectiveness.|例句：The engineers preproved the software before launching it to the public.  工程师在向公众发布软件之前预先测试了它。|例句：The chef preproved the recipe to ensure it tasted perfect before serving it to the guests. 厨师在为客人上菜之前预先试验了食谱，以确保味道完美。|近义词：pretest, verify, check|反义词：disprove, invalidate
