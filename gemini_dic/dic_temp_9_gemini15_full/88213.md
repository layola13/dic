hindenburg:/ˈhɪndənˌbɜːrɡ/ | n. |兴登堡号飞艇；兴登堡 | A large German passenger airship that caught fire and was destroyed in 1937.  The name is also often used to refer to the disaster itself. | Example: The Hindenburg disaster shocked the world.  兴登堡号灾难震惊了全世界。The Hindenburg was a symbol of German engineering prowess before its tragic end. 兴登堡号在其悲惨结局之前是德国工程实力的象征。|近义词：airship (飞艇), zeppelin (齐柏林飞艇) |反义词：无 |名词
