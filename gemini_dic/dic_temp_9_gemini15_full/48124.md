decoction:/dɪˈkɒkʃən/| n.|煎剂；浓缩液|A decoction is a liquid preparation made by boiling a substance in water.  It's often used in herbal medicine and cooking.|例句：She prepared a decoction of herbs to soothe her cough. 她准备了一种草药煎剂来缓解咳嗽。|近义词：infusion, extract|反义词：None specifically.
