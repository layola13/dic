democratist:/ˈdɛməkrætɪst/| n. |民主主义者，拥护民主者 |A person who supports or believes in democracy.  Someone who advocates for democratic principles and practices. |例句：Many democratists  believe in the power of the people to shape their own government. 许多民主主义者相信人民有能力塑造自己的政府。

例句：The democratists  organized a peaceful protest to demand fair elections. 民主主义者组织了一场和平抗议，要求进行公平的选举。


近义词：democrat, proponent of democracy, champion of democracy |反义词：autocrat, authoritarian, dictator |词性：名词
