nodding:/ˈnɒdɪŋ/|/ˈnɑːdɪŋ/| v. |点头；(表示同意、理解等) |The act of moving one's head up and down, typically to indicate agreement, understanding, or acknowledgment.  It can also express drowsiness or sleepiness. |例句：She nodded her head in agreement. 她点头表示同意。

例句：He nodded off during the lecture. 他在讲座上打瞌睡了。


近义词：bowing (轻微的点头), inclining (倾斜头部), shaking (摇头表示不同意)


反义词：shaking (摇头)


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nods his head frequently. = 他经常点头。
She nods in agreement. = 她点头表示同意。


一般过去时:
He nodded his head in understanding. = 他点头表示理解。
She nodded off during the movie. = 她看电影时睡着了。


现在完成时:
He has nodded off several times today. = 他今天已经好几次打瞌睡了。
She has nodded her agreement to the proposal. = 她已经点头同意了这个提案。


过去完成时:
He had nodded off before the speaker finished. = 演讲者还没讲完他就睡着了。
She had nodded in agreement before realizing the implications. = 她点头同意之前没有意识到其中的含义。


将来时:
He will nod when he understands. = 他明白了就会点头。
She will nod off if the meeting goes on too long. = 如果会议持续太久，她会打瞌睡。


虚拟语气:
If he understood, he would nod. = 如果他明白了，他会点头。
If she had known, she would have nodded. = 如果她知道，她就会点头。


被动语态:
(Nodding is rarely used in the passive voice because it's an action done by the subject, not to the subject.)


疑问句:
Did he nod his head? = 他点头了吗？
Is she nodding off? = 她要睡着了吗？


否定句:
He didn't nod his head. = 他没有点头。
She didn't nod in agreement. = 她没有点头表示同意。


PHRASE->
nod off = 打瞌睡
nod in agreement = 点头同意
nod one's head = 点头
give a nod = 点头示意
a slight nod = 轻微的点头
