ideograms:/ˈaɪdiəˌɡræmz/| n. |表意文字；语素文字|Pictures or symbols that represent words or ideas, rather than sounds.  They are used in writing systems such as Chinese and Japanese.|Example: Chinese characters are a classic example of ideograms, where each character often represents a word or concept.  中文汉字是表意文字的典型例子，每个汉字通常代表一个词或概念。|近义词：logograms,pictograms|反义词：alphabets (phonetic writing systems)|名词
