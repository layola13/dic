inadaptation:/ˌɪnˌædəpˈteɪʃən/| n. |不适应；不适应性；未能适应|The inability to adapt or adjust to a new environment, situation, or change. It often refers to a lack of fit between an organism and its environment, or a failure to adjust to changing circumstances.  |Example: The plant showed signs of inadaptation to the arid climate.  这植物显示出不适应干旱气候的迹象。The student's inadaptation to college life led to poor academic performance.  学生不适应大学生活导致学业成绩差。|近义词：maladaptation, unsuitability, incompatibility |反义词：adaptation, adjustment, suitability
