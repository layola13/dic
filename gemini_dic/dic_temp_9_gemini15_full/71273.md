fixate:/ˈfɪkseɪt/| vt.  | 使固定；使注视；使迷恋 | To direct one's attention or gaze intently on something; to become preoccupied or obsessed with something. |例句：He fixated on the small detail, ignoring the bigger picture. 他专注于一个小细节，忽略了更大的图景。|例句：She fixated on her weight, constantly worrying about it. 她对自己的体重很执着，不断为此担心。|近义词：focus on, concentrate on, obsess over |反义词：ignore, neglect, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fixates on his work. = 他专注于他的工作。
She fixates on every little flaw. = 她对每一个小瑕疵都耿耿于怀。

一般过去时:
He fixated on the painting for hours. = 他对着那幅画看了好几个小时。
She fixated on the problem until she solved it. = 她一直专注于这个问题直到解决它。

现在完成时:
I have fixated on this project for weeks. = 我已经在这个项目上专注了好几个星期了。
They have fixated on finding a solution. = 他们一直致力于寻找一个解决方案。

过去完成时:
He had fixated on the idea for years before he finally acted on it. = 在他最终付诸行动之前，他已经对这个想法痴迷多年了。
She had fixated on her goal since childhood. = 自孩提时代起，她就一直专注于她的目标。

将来时:
I will fixate on my studies until the exam. = 我会一直专注于我的学习直到考试。
He will fixate on the details of the contract. = 他将会专注于合同的细节。

虚拟语气:
If I didn't fixate on small things, I would be less stressed. = 如果我不那么执着于小事，我会少一些压力。
I wish I hadn't fixated on that mistake. = 我希望我没有那么执着于那个错误。

被动语态:
His attention was fixated on the speaker. = 他的注意力集中在演讲者身上。
The camera was fixated on the main character. = 相机对准了主角。


疑问句:
What are you fixated on? = 你在专注什么？
Why is she fixated on him? = 她为什么那么迷恋他？

否定句:
He didn't fixate on the negative aspects. = 他没有专注于消极方面。
She didn't fixate on the details. = 她没有专注于细节。


PHRASE->
fixate on = 专注于
