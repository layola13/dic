The Heisenberg Uncertainty Principle states that it is impossible to simultaneously know both the position and momentum of a particle. 海森堡不确定性原理指出，不可能同时知道粒子的位置和动量。
Scientists apply Heisenberg's principles to various quantum phenomena. 科学家将海森堡的原理应用于各种量子现象。
