excellency:/ˈɛksələnsi/| n. |阁下；卓越；优秀|A title of respect used in addressing or referring to high-ranking officials, especially heads of state or ambassadors.  It shows great respect and politeness. |例句：Her Excellency, the Ambassador, addressed the gathering.  尊敬的X大使阁下向与会者致辞。  例句：The excellency of her work was immediately apparent. 她作品的卓越之处立刻显而易见。|近义词：eminence, highness, lordship|反义词：mediocrity, inferiority
