modular:/ˈmɒdʒələr/ /ˈmɑːdʒələr/| adj.| 模块化的；模块式的 | Consisting of or capable of being broken down into separate, interchangeable modules.  A modular system is designed to allow flexibility and easy customization. |例句：The software uses a modular design, allowing for easy upgrades. 这个软件采用模块化设计，便于升级。

例句：This furniture is modular, so you can rearrange it to fit your space. 这套家具是模块化的，你可以根据你的空间重新安排它。


近义词：unitary, sectional, component-based | 反义词： monolithic, integrated
