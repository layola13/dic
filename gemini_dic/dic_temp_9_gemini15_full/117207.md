measuring-up: /ˈmɛʒərɪŋ ʌp/ | v. | 达标；符合标准；胜任；测量 |  To assess someone or something's suitability or quality against a particular standard or expectation.  | 例句：He's measuring up the candidates for the job. 他正在评估求职者的资格。

例句：The new employee is measuring up well to the expectations of the company. 新员工很好地达到了公司的期望。


近义词：assessing, evaluating, judging, appraising, gauging

反义词：falling short, failing, not meeting expectations


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is measuring up the fabric for the curtains. 他正在测量窗帘的布料。
They are measuring up the potential risks of the project. 他们正在评估该项目的潜在风险。


一般过去时:
She measured up the ingredients for the cake. 她量好了蛋糕的配料。
The committee measured up the applicants' qualifications. 委员会评估了申请人的资格。


现在完成时:
We have measured up the room for new furniture. 我们已经测量了房间的尺寸以便添置新家具。
They have measured up to the challenge and succeeded.  他们经受住了挑战并成功了。


过去完成时:
The architect had measured up the site before beginning the design. 建筑师在开始设计之前已经测量过场地了。
Before the competition, she had measured up against her rivals' performance. 比赛前，她已经评估过对手的表现。


将来时:
They will measure up the land for the new building. 他们将测量新建筑的土地。
The team will measure up the effectiveness of the new strategy.  团队将评估新策略的有效性。


虚拟语气:
If he measured up to the standards, he would get the promotion. 如果他达到标准，他就会得到晋升。
I wish I had measured up better in the interview.  我希望我在面试中表现得更好。


被动语态:
The candidates were measured up by the hiring manager. 求职者由招聘经理进行了评估。
The building's dimensions were measured up by a surveyor. 建筑物的尺寸由测量员进行了测量。


疑问句:
Are they measuring up the candidates fairly? 他们是否公平地评估了候选人？
Have you measured up the ingredients accurately? 你是否精确地测量了配料？


否定句:
He didn't measure up to the expectations of his boss. 他没有达到老板的期望。
The product didn't measure up to the quality standards. 产品达不到质量标准。



PHRASE->
