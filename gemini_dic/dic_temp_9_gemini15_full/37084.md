combination:/ˌkɒmbɪˈneɪʃən/ /ˌkɑːmbəˈneɪʃən/| n. |组合，联合；搭配；[化学]化合|A combination of factors led to the accident.  多种因素导致了这起事故。|The new drug is a combination of two existing medicines.这种新药是两种现有药物的组合。|近义词：union, blend, mixture |反义词：separation, division


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This recipe requires a combination of sweet and sour flavors. = 这个食谱需要甜酸味的组合。
The painting is a unique combination of colors and textures. = 这幅画是颜色和纹理的独特组合。

一般过去时:
The winning team showed a perfect combination of skill and teamwork. = 获胜的团队展现了技巧和团队合作的完美结合。
Her outfit was a stylish combination of classic and modern elements. = 她的服装是经典和现代元素的时尚组合。

现在完成时:
Scientists have achieved a remarkable combination of efficiency and sustainability. = 科学家们已经取得了效率和可持续性的显著结合。
The company has developed a new product through a combination of innovation and research. = 公司通过创新和研究的结合开发了一种新产品。

过去完成时:
Before the presentation, they had already finalized the combination of slides and data. = 在演示之前，他们已经完成了幻灯片和数据的组合。
By the end of the project, they had successfully implemented a combination of old and new technologies. = 到项目结束时，他们已经成功地实施了新旧技术的结合。

将来时:
The architect will use a combination of wood and steel in the building design. = 建筑师将在建筑设计中使用木材和钢铁的组合。
The chef will create a unique dish with a combination of exotic spices and local ingredients. = 厨师将用异国香料和当地食材的组合创造一道独特的菜肴。

虚拟语气:
If we had a better combination of resources, we could achieve more. = 如果我们有更好的资源组合，我们可以取得更多成就。
I wish I had known about this combination of ingredients earlier. = 我真希望我早点知道这种配料的组合。

被动语态:
The winning strategy was a carefully planned combination of offensive and defensive moves. = 获胜的策略是经过精心策划的攻防结合。
The final product is a result of a complex combination of processes. = 最终产品是复杂工艺组合的结果。

疑问句:

否定句:
