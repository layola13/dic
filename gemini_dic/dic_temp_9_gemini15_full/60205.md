effloresce:/ˌɛfləˈrɛs/| v.| 盐析；风化；开花；(感情等)逐渐显露 |To lose water of crystallization; to crumble and fall to powder.  Also, to bloom or blossom; to flourish. Figuratively, to gradually become evident or manifest. |The salt effloresced on the surface of the rocks.  盐分在岩石表面析出。|The crystals effloresced in the dry air. 晶体在干燥的空气中风化。|近义词：blossom, bloom, flourish, appear |反义词：wither, fade
