intercostal:/ˌɪntərˈkɒstəl/| adj.|肋间的，肋骨间的|Situated between the ribs.  The term refers to the space, muscles, nerves, and blood vessels located between adjacent ribs.|例句：The patient experienced sharp pain in the intercostal region.  病人肋间区域感到剧烈疼痛。  The doctor examined the intercostal muscles for any abnormalities. 医生检查了肋间肌是否有异常。|近义词：interrib|反义词：intracostal (within the ribs)
