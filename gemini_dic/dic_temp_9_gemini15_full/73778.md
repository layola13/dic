formality:/fɔːˈmæləti/| n. |正式；礼节；拘谨；程式 |The act of being formal; adherence to convention or propriety.  Formality refers to the adherence to rules, conventions, or protocols, often in social or professional settings. It can also describe the quality of being formal, stiff, or lacking in informality.  |例句：The occasion demanded a certain level of formality.  这次场合需要一定的正式程度。

The meeting was characterized by excessive formality.  会议的特点是过于正式。


近义词：ceremony, protocol, convention, stiffness, rigidity |反义词：informality, casualness, informality


词源:  From Middle French formalité, from Latin formalitas, from formalis "relating to form," from forma "form, shape."
