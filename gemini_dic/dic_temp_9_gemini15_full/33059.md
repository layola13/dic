chowdering: /ˈtʃaʊdərɪŋ/ | v. |  烹制浓汤；闲聊；喧闹 |  The act of making chowder or a similar thick soup; also, informal, noisy talk or commotion. | 例句：He spent the afternoon chowdering with his friends. 他下午和朋友们一起闲聊。 | 近义词：chatting, gossiping, brewing (in the sense of creating a commotion) | 反义词：quiet, silent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are chowdering up a delicious clam chowder. = 他们在烹制美味的蛤蜊浓汤。
We often chowder about the latest gossip. = 我们经常闲聊最新的八卦。


一般过去时:
She chowdered a hearty vegetable soup for dinner. = 她为晚餐烹制了一锅丰盛的蔬菜汤。
They chowdered away for hours about their childhood memories. = 他们就童年回忆聊了好几个小时。


现在完成时:
He has chowdered up a storm in the kitchen. = 他在厨房里忙得一团糟。(指烹饪过程很混乱)
They have chowdered all afternoon about the upcoming election. = 他们整个下午都在闲聊即将到来的选举。


过去完成时:
She had chowdered a pot of stew before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经煮好了一锅炖菜。
They had chowdered excitedly about the news before anyone else heard it. = 在其他人听到这个消息之前，他们就已经兴奋地讨论过了。


将来时:
We will chowder up a big batch of seafood chowder for the party. = 我们将为派对准备一大锅海鲜浓汤。
They will probably chowder about this for weeks. = 他们可能会就此事闲聊几周。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would chowder a proper lobster bisque. = 如果我有更多时间，我会煮一道像样的龙虾浓汤。
I wish they wouldn't chowder so loudly. = 我希望他们不要这么大声地闲聊。


被动语态:
A delicious clam chowder was chowdered for the picnic. = 为野餐准备了一锅美味的蛤蜊浓汤。(被动语态使用较少见)
The matter was chowdered over by the committee for days. = 委员会几天来一直在讨论此事。(被动语态使用较少见，更倾向于用discuss)


疑问句:
Are they chowdering up anything special for tonight's dinner? = 他们今晚的晚餐在烹制什么特别的菜吗？
Were they chowdering about anything important? = 他们在讨论什么重要的事情吗？


否定句:
They didn't chowder up anything interesting for the potluck. = 他们为聚餐没有准备任何有趣的东西。
We didn't chowder much about personal matters. = 我们没有过多地讨论私人事情。

chowder about: 闲聊关于...
