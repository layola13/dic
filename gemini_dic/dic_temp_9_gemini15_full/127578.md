nectarines:/ˈnɛktəriːnz/| n.|油桃|A nectarine is a fruit that is similar to a peach, but has smooth skin instead of fuzzy skin.  It is sweet and juicy, and is often eaten fresh or used in desserts.|例句：I bought some nectarines at the farmer's market. 我在农贸市场买了一些油桃。|例句：Nectarines are a delicious summer fruit. 油桃是一种美味的夏季水果。|近义词：peach, plum|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with fruits lacking similar sweetness or texture.
