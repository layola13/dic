divesting: /dɪˈvɛstɪŋ/ | v. | 剥夺；脱去；放弃；出售（资产） | The act of divesting oneself of something, typically an asset or responsibility.  It implies a deliberate and often strategic removal or disposal. |


例句：

The company is divesting itself of its non-core businesses.  = 这家公司正在剥离其非核心业务。

The government is divesting from fossil fuels. = 政府正在放弃化石燃料。


近义词：selling, disposing of, relinquishing, shedding, alienating


反义词：acquiring, investing in, retaining, accumulating


词源：

From divest + -ing.  Divest itself comes from the Old French *desvestir, from des- (un-) + vestir (to clothe). The original sense was thus "to unclothe," which evolved to a more general sense of "to deprive" or "to rid oneself of."

She is divesting herself of her responsibilities. = 她正在卸下自己的责任。

一般过去时:
They divested their holdings in the stock market. = 他们抛售了他们在股市的股票。
He divested himself of his power. = 他放弃了他的权力。

现在完成时:
The government has divested itself of its stake in the company. = 政府已经剥离了它在公司中的股份。
She has divested herself of all her worldly possessions. = 她已经放弃了她所有的世俗财产。

过去完成时:
They had divested their property before the crisis hit. = 危机来临之前，他们已经处理掉了他们的房产。
The company had divested its unprofitable divisions before the merger. = 在合并之前，公司已经剥离了其无利可图的部门。

将来时:

They will divest their holdings in the oil industry. = 他们将剥离他们在石油行业的投资。


被动语态:
The assets are being divested. = 资产正在被剥离。
The company's holdings were divested. = 公司的股份被抛售了。


疑问句:
Is the company divesting its assets? = 公司正在剥离其资产吗？
Are they divesting from that project? = 他们正在撤出那个项目吗？


否定句:
The company is not divesting its profitable divisions. = 公司没有剥离其盈利部门。
He did not divest himself of his responsibilities. = 他没有放弃他的责任。
