replanned:/ˌriːˈplænd/| v. |重新规划，重新计划 |To plan something again, often after a previous plan has failed or changed.  It implies a revision or alteration of an existing plan. |例句：They replanned the project after discovering a crucial flaw in the original design. 他们发现原始设计中存在一个关键缺陷后，重新规划了项目。

例句：The city council replanned the traffic system to alleviate congestion. 市议会重新规划了交通系统以缓解拥堵。


近义词：revise, redesign, restructure, rethink

反义词：implement (if referring to sticking to the original plan)


词性：动词
