overspeed:/ˌoʊvərˈspiːd/ | v. |超速行驶，速度过快 | To exceed the permitted or safe speed. |例句：He oversped on the highway and got a ticket. 他在高速公路上超速行驶并被罚款。

例句：The engine was overspeeding and could break down. 发动机转速过高，可能会坏掉。

近义词：speed excessively, exceed the speed limit | 反义词：underspeed, creep
