disavowable: /dɪs.əˈvaʊ.ə.bəl/ | adj. | 可否认的，可拒绝承认的 |  capable of being disavowed;  that can be denied or repudiated.  |  The claim was entirely disavowable, based on flimsy evidence.  这说法完全可以否认，因为它基于薄弱的证据。 The forged signature was easily disavowable.  伪造的签名很容易被否认。| 近义词：deniable, refutable, repudiable | 反义词： undeniable, irrefutable, incontrovertible

He disavows any knowledge of the crime. = 他否认对犯罪有任何了解。
