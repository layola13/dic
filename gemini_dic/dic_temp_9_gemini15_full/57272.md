downpours:/ˈdaʊnpɔːrz/| n.|倾盆大雨，暴雨|Heavy rain falling very quickly in large amounts.|例句：The downpours flooded the streets. 倾盆大雨淹没了街道。|例句：We had to postpone the picnic because of the sudden downpours. 因为突然的暴雨，我们不得不推迟野餐。|近义词：torrential rain, heavy rain, deluge|反义词：drought, dry spell|名词
