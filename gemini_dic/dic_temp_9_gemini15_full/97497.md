ingestible:/ɪnˈdʒɛstəbəl/| adj.| 可食用的，可吞咽的 | Capable of being swallowed or digested; suitable for eating.  | Example: The small pills were easily ingestible.  这些小药丸很容易吞咽。  The cake was delicious and ingestible. 这蛋糕美味可口且易于吞咽。|近义词：edible, digestible | 反义词：inedible, indigestible
