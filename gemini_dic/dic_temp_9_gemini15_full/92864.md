idahoan:/ˌaɪdəˈhoʊən/| n. adj.|爱达荷州人；爱达荷州的|A person who lives in or comes from Idaho.  An adjective referring to something related to Idaho.|例句：He's an Idahoan, born and raised in Boise. 他是爱达荷州人，在博伊西长大。

例句：Idahoan potatoes are famous for their quality. 爱达荷州的土豆以其品质而闻名。
