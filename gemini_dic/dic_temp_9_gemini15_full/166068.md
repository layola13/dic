preponder:/prɪˈpɒndər/| v.|  preponderate |压倒；超过；比…更重要|To be greater in number, weight, influence, or importance.|The evidence preponderates in favor of the defendant. 证据压倒性地有利于被告。| outweigh, surpass, exceed | be outweighed by, be less than |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence preponderates in favor of the defendant. = 证据压倒性地有利于被告。
His influence preponderates in this matter. = 在这件事上，他的影响力占主导地位。


一般过去时:
The scales preponderated on one side. = 天平向一边倾斜。
His opinion preponderated in the decision. = 他的意见在决定中占主导地位。


现在完成时:
The negative aspects have preponderated throughout the project. = 在整个项目中，负面因素占据主导地位。
Public opinion has preponderated in their favor.  = 公众舆论压倒性地支持他们。


过去完成时:
By the end of the meeting, his arguments had preponderated. = 到会议结束时，他的论点已占据上风。
Before the vote, the opposition's arguments had preponderated. = 在投票之前，反对派的论点占据主导地位。


将来时:
It is likely that his arguments will preponderate in the debate. = 他的论点很可能在辩论中占据主导地位。
We predict that the positive aspects will preponderate in the long term. = 我们预测从长远来看，积极方面将占据主导地位。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence preponderated in his favor, he would be acquitted. = 如果证据压倒性地对他有利，他就会被无罪释放。
If the benefits preponderated over the risks, we would proceed with the plan. = 如果好处大于风险，我们将继续执行计划。


被动语态:
His opinion was preponderated in the final decision. = 他的意见在最终决定中占据主导地位。(被动语态略显不自然，很少用)
The decision was influenced by the factors that preponderated. = 该决定受到了占据主导地位的因素的影响。(被动语态略显不自然，很少用)


疑问句:
Does the evidence preponderate in favor of the prosecution or the defense? = 证据是否压倒性地有利于检方还是辩方？
Which factor preponderates in this complex situation? = 在这种复杂的情况下，哪个因素占主导地位？


否定句:
The arguments did not preponderate in favor of the proposal. = 论点并没有压倒性地支持该提案。
His influence did not preponderate in the decision-making process. = 他的影响力在决策过程中并没有占据主导地位。


PHRASE->
preponderate in favor of:  压倒性地有利于…
preponderate in number:  数量上占优势
preponderate in influence: 影响力占主导地位
preponderate in importance:  重要性超过…
preponderating factor:  主导因素
