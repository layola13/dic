phenomena:/fəˈnɒmɪnə/ | /fəˈnɑːmɪnə/ | n. |现象；奇观 | Phenomena are observable events or occurrences.  They can be natural occurrences like weather patterns or social occurrences like trends.  The plural of phenomenon. |The aurora borealis is a spectacular natural phenomenon.  极光是一种壮丽的自然现象。

The recent increase in online shopping is a significant social phenomenon.  最近网上购物的增加是一个重要的社会现象。|近义词： occurrences, events, incidents |反义词：  normality, regularity
