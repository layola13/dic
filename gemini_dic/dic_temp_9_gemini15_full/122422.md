moaned:/moʊnd/| v.|呻吟，抱怨，悲叹 |to make a low, long sound, usually because of pain, unhappiness, or discomfort.  It can also mean to complain in a whining way.|例句：The injured man moaned in pain. 受伤的男人痛苦地呻吟着。

The child moaned about having to go to school.  孩子抱怨要去上学。|近义词：groaned, whined, complained |反义词：shouted, cheered


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He moans every time he has to work. = 他每次不得不工作的时候都会抱怨。
She moans about the weather constantly. = 她不断地抱怨天气。

一般过去时:
He moaned softly in his sleep. = 他在睡梦中轻轻地呻吟。
She moaned about the unfair treatment she received. = 她抱怨她受到的不公平待遇。

现在完成时:
He has moaned about his job all day. = 他整天都在抱怨他的工作。
She has moaned about the noise from the neighbors for weeks. = 她已经抱怨邻居的噪音好几个星期了。

过去完成时:
He had moaned about the project for days before he finally quit. = 在他最终辞职之前，他已经抱怨这个项目好几天了。
She had moaned about her headache all afternoon before taking medicine. = 在吃药之前，她整个下午都在抱怨头痛。

将来时:
He will moan about the extra work if you assign him too much. = 如果你给他安排太多工作，他会抱怨额外的工作。
She will moan about the cold weather all winter. = 她整个冬天都会抱怨寒冷的天气。


虚拟语气:
If he had known it would be so difficult, he wouldn't have moaned so much. = 如果他知道这么难，他不会抱怨那么多。
I wish she wouldn't moan about everything all the time. = 我希望她不要总是抱怨一切。

被动语态:
His complaints were often moaned about by his colleagues.  = 他的抱怨经常被同事们抱怨。
The problem was moaned about extensively before a solution was found. = 在找到解决方案之前，这个问题受到了广泛的抱怨。

疑问句:
Was he moaning because of the pain? = 他是因为疼痛而呻吟吗？
Is she always moaning about something? = 她是不是总是抱怨一些事情？

否定句:
He didn't moan about the long hours. = 他没有抱怨长时间的工作。
She didn't moan, but her face showed her displeasure. = 她没有呻吟，但她脸上露出了不悦的表情。
