canoe:/kəˈnuː/| n. |独木舟；轻舟 |A canoe is a narrow, lightweight boat propelled by a paddle.  It's typically made from a single hollowed-out tree trunk or other materials. |例句：We paddled the canoe across the lake. 我们划着独木舟穿过湖泊。|例句：He enjoyed a leisurely canoe trip down the river. 他享受了一次悠闲的泛舟之旅。|近义词：kayak, boat |反义词：ship, large boat

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He canoes down the river every weekend. 他每个周末都划独木舟下河。
They canoe on the lake during summer. 他们在夏天在湖上划独木舟。

一般过去时:
She canoed across the lake yesterday. 她昨天划着独木舟穿过湖泊。

词源:
The word "canoe" comes from the Caribbean word *kanū*.  It entered English via Spanish and French.  The original meaning refers to a dugout canoe, a boat made from a hollowed-out tree trunk.
