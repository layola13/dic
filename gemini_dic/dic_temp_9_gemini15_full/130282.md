non-successful: /ˌnɒn.səkˈsɛsfəl/ | adj. | 不成功的，未成功的 |  Not achieving the desired result; unsuccessful.  This word combines the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the adjective "successful".  It emphasizes the lack of success. |

例句：

His non-successful attempt to climb Mount Everest left him disappointed. = 他攀登珠穆朗玛峰的未成功尝试让他感到失望。

The non-successful project was eventually abandoned. = 这个不成功的项目最终被放弃了。


近义词：unsuccessful, fruitless, abortive, vain, ineffective

反义词：successful, fruitful, effective, triumphant


词性: 形容词
