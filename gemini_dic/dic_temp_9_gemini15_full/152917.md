pedestrian:/pəˈdɛstriən/| n. |行人，步行者 |A pedestrian is a person walking on foot, rather than traveling in a vehicle.  Pedestrians should use crosswalks to cross the street safely. 行人是步行的人，而不是乘坐交通工具的人。行人应该使用人行横道安全过马路。| walker, passerby | driver, motorist | 名词

词源:
The word "pedestrian" comes from the Late Latin word *pedester, meaning "going on foot," derived from Latin pedes (genitive pedis), meaning "foot."  The suffix "-ian" indicates a person or thing associated with a particular thing.
