sailer:/ˈseɪlər/| n. |水手，海员 | A person who works on a ship, especially as a member of the crew.  |例句：The sailor skillfully navigated the stormy seas.  这位水手熟练地驾驭着暴风雨的海浪。
|例句：Many sailors were lost at sea during the great storm.  在暴风雨中，许多水手葬身大海。|近义词：seaman, mariner |反义词：landlubber
