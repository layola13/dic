dyspepsy:/dɪsˈpɛpsi/| n. |消化不良，胃部不适|Indigestion or discomfort in the stomach.  Dyspepsia is a common symptom, not a disease itself, and can be caused by various factors including overeating, stress, or underlying medical conditions. |Example:  He suffered from dyspepsia after eating too much spicy food. 他吃太多辛辣食物后患上了消化不良。|近义词：indigestion, upset stomach, heartburn |反义词：good digestion
