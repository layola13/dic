restitching:/ˌriːˈstɪtʃɪŋ/| n. |重新缝合；修补|The act of sewing something again, often to repair a tear or damage.  It implies a previous stitching that has been undone or needs improvement. |例句：The tailor expertly performed the restitching, making the tear almost invisible.  裁缝熟练地进行了重新缝合，几乎看不见裂口了。

The restitching of the old sofa required patience and skill.  重新缝合旧沙发需要耐心和技巧。|近义词：repairing, mending, patching|反义词：ripping, tearing, unraveling|名词
