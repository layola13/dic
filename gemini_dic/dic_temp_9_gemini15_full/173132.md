quasi-affectionate: /ˈkwɑːzi əˈfɛkʃənət/ | adj. |似是而非的深情；表面上的深情；假装的深情 |


英文解析:  Appearing to be affectionate but not genuinely so; seemingly affectionate.  It suggests a feeling that is less than true affection, perhaps a mixture of genuine warmth and a calculated display of fondness.


例句:

He gave her a quasi-affectionate pat on the shoulder, more out of habit than genuine feeling.  他拍了拍她的肩膀，看似深情，其实更多是出于习惯而不是真情实感。


Her quasi-affectionate smile didn't quite reach her eyes, revealing a hint of underlying coldness.  她看似深情的笑容并没有到达她的眼睛，露出了潜藏的冷漠。


近义词:  pretend affectionate, seemingly affectionate, superficially affectionate, affectedly affectionate


反义词: genuinely affectionate, truly loving, wholeheartedly caring


词性: adjective (形容词)
