presubstituting: /ˌpriːˈsʌbstɪˌtjuːtɪŋ/ | v. |预先替代；预先替换 |  The act of substituting something beforehand.  It implies doing the substitution before a particular process or event.  This word is less common and often used in technical contexts. |

例句：

The engineers were presubstituting the faulty components before the launch of the satellite. =工程师们在卫星发射前预先替换了有故障的组件。

By presubstituting the variables, we simplified the complex equation. = 通过预先替换变量，我们简化了复杂的方程式。


近义词：preplacing, anticipating (in some contexts), substituting in advance

反义词：post-substituting, replacing afterward
