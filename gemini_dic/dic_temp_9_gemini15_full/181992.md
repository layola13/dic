rematriculated:/ˌriːmæˈtrɪkjuleɪtɪd/| v. |重新注册入学；复学|To re-enroll in a school or college after a period of absence.|He rematriculated at the university after a year's break. 他休学一年后重新注册了大学。|近义词：reenroll,re-register|反义词：withdraw,drop out


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rematriculates at the university every fall. 他每年秋季都在大学重新注册。
She rematriculates after taking a semester off.  她在休假一个学期后重新注册。


一般过去时:
He rematriculated at the university last year. 他去年重新注册了大学。
She rematriculated after a gap year. 她在间隔年后重新注册了。


现在完成时:
He has rematriculated twice since graduating high school. 自从高中毕业以来，他已经两次重新注册了。
She has rematriculated and is now studying engineering. 她已经重新注册，现在正在学习工程学。


过去完成时:
He had rematriculated before the new semester began. 新学期开始前他已经重新注册了。
She had rematriculated by the time the deadline passed. 截止日期过去时她已经重新注册了。


将来时:
He will rematriculate in the spring semester. 他将在春季学期重新注册。
She will rematriculate next year to pursue a master's degree. 她明年将重新注册攻读硕士学位。


虚拟语气:
If he had the money, he would rematriculate immediately. 如果他有足够的钱，他会立即重新注册。
I wish I had rematriculated sooner. 我真希望我早点重新注册。


被动语态:
He was rematriculated after appealing the decision. 在他申诉之后，他被重新注册了。(This is less common as rematriculation is usually an active choice)
The student was rematriculated following a successful application.  学生在申请成功后被重新注册了。(This is less common as rematriculation is usually an active choice)


疑问句:
Will he rematriculate next year? 他明年会重新注册吗？
Has she rematriculated yet? 她已经重新注册了吗？


否定句:
He did not rematriculate this year. 他今年没有重新注册。
She will not rematriculate until next fall. 她直到明年秋天才会重新注册。


PHRASE->

rematriculate with a different major 转专业后重新注册
rematriculate under a new name 以新的名字重新注册
