rechoreographing:/ˌriːkɔːriːˈɒɡrəfɪŋ/ | v. |重新编排舞蹈（动作）|The act of changing or revising the choreography of a dance or performance.  It involves reworking existing steps, movements, and sequences to create a new or improved version.  This can be done for various reasons, such as updating a classic piece, adapting it to a different setting, or reflecting a new artistic vision.|The company is rechoreographing the ballet for a modern audience.  这家公司正在为现代观众重新编排芭蕾舞剧。

The director decided to rechoreograph the musical number to improve its pacing.  导演决定重新编排音乐剧中的部分内容以改善节奏。


近义词：rearranging, revising, reworking, re-staging


反义词：leaving unchanged, preserving the original

词性：动词
