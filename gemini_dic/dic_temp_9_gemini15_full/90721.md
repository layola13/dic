humidness:/ˈhjuːmɪdnəs/ | n. | 湿度，潮湿 | The state or quality of being humid; dampness in the air.  It refers to the amount of water vapor present in the air. High humidity can make the air feel heavy and sticky. | Example: The high humidity made it feel much hotter than the actual temperature.  高温高湿使体感温度远高于实际温度。 The humidity in the rainforest is almost unbearable. 雨林中的湿度几乎让人难以忍受。|近义词：dampness, moisture, wetness | 反义词：dryness, aridity
