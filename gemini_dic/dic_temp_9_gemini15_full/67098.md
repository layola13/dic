explorator:/ˌɛksploˈreɪtər/| n. |探险家，探测者|A person who explores or investigates a subject, area, or situation.  An explorer, especially one who travels to little-known regions.|例句：The explorator braved the Amazon rainforest. 探险家勇敢地穿越亚马逊雨林。
例句：A seasoned explorator, he knew how to navigate by the stars. 他是一位经验丰富的探险家，他知道如何根据星辰导航。|近义词：explorer, investigator, pioneer |反义词：settler (in the sense of someone who stays and develops a place, rather than exploring it)
