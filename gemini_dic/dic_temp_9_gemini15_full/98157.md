inscape:/ˈɪnskeɪp/| n.|内景；内在本质；内在结构|The inherent qualities or inner nature of a person or thing; the inner world of a person, often expressed through art.  It refers to the unique essence or spirit that distinguishes one thing from another.  Often associated with the inner landscape of the mind or spirit.|例句：The artist sought to capture the inscape of the subject in her painting.  艺术家试图在她的画作中捕捉主题的内景。

The poem explores the inscape of the speaker's grief. 这首诗探索了说话者悲伤的内在本质。|近义词：essence, nature, spirit, inner self|反义词：exterior, outward appearance, superficiality|名词
