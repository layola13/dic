quantification:/ˌkwɒntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| /ˌkwɑːntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |量化；定量；数量确定|The act of quantifying; the process of measuring or expressing something as a quantity.  It involves assigning numerical values to variables or attributes. This is crucial in many fields for making objective comparisons and analysis.|例句：The quantification of risk is essential in investment decisions.  风险量化对于投资决策至关重要。

例句：Accurate quantification of the damage caused by the storm is still underway.  对风暴造成的损害的准确量化仍在进行中。


近义词：measurement, assessment, calculation, evaluation|反义词：qualification
