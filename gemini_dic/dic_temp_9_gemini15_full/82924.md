gumming:/ˈɡʌmɪŋ/| v.|粘着；(使)粘在一起；用树胶粘合|The process of something becoming sticky or adhesive, or the act of making something sticky.  It can also refer to the act of applying gum or a gum-like substance.|例句：The glue is gumming up the mechanism.  胶水把机械粘住了。The children were gumming the stickers onto the paper.孩子们正把贴纸粘到纸上。|近义词： sticking, gluing, adhering |反义词： detaching, separating
