lugger:/ˈlʌɡər/| n.|驳船，小货船，吃力地拖拉的人或物|A lugger is a type of sailing vessel, typically a small, sturdy boat used for carrying cargo or fishing.  It's often characterized by its shallow draft and ability to navigate shallow waters. The term can also refer to something or someone that lugs or drags heavily.|例句：The old lugger creaked and groaned as it battled the stormy seas. 这艘旧驳船在与暴风雨搏斗时发出嘎吱嘎吱的响声。 The hiker was a real lugger, struggling to carry his heavy backpack up the mountain.  这位徒步旅行者真是个累赘，费力地背着沉重的背包爬山。|近义词：barge, sloop, trawler |反义词：speedboat, yacht
