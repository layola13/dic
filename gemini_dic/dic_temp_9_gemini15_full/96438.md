indistinguishable: /ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbəl/ | adj. |无法区分的，难以辨别的 |  Not able to be distinguished; so alike as to be impossible to tell apart.  | The twins were indistinguishable.  | similar, identical | distinguishable, different | 形容词


例句：

The two paintings were indistinguishable; even the expert couldn't tell them apart.  = 两幅画无法区分；即使是专家也无法分辨它们。

The sounds of the birds were indistinguishable from the wind. = 鸟叫声与风声难以分辨。


词源：

in- (否定前缀) + distinguish (区分) + -able (可…的)


近义词：

identical, alike, same, undifferentiated, inseparable


反义词：

distinguishable, different, disparate, dissimilar


用法说明：

indistinguishable 主要用作形容词，修饰名词，表示无法区分或辨别。它通常强调两种或多种事物之间的相似性，以至于无法将它们区分开来。  该词通常与“from”连用，例如：indistinguishable from...


PHRASE->

indistinguishable from: 与...无法区分
almost indistinguishable: 几乎无法区分
visually indistinguishable:  视觉上无法区分
barely indistinguishable: 几乎无法区分
utterly indistinguishable: 完全无法区分
