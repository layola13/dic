chatty:/ˈtʃæti/ | adj. | 健谈的；喋喋不休的 |  Characterized by excessive talkativeness; loquacious.  | 例句：She's a very chatty person, always ready for a conversation. 她是一个非常健谈的人，总是准备好聊天。

例句：The children were so chatty during the movie that it was hard to concentrate. 孩子们在电影院里太吵闹了，很难集中注意力。

近义词：talkative, garrulous, loquacious, voluble | 反义词：taciturn, reticent, reserved, quiet

词性：形容词
