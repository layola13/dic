medevacking:/ˌmɛdəˈvækɪŋ/| v.|医疗后送；医疗撤离|The act of evacuating someone by medical means, typically by air or other rapid transport, usually for urgent medical reasons.  It often involves specialized medical personnel and equipment.|例句：The injured soldier was medevacked to a field hospital. 受伤的士兵被医疗后送至野战医院。

The injured climber was medevacked from the mountain. 受伤的登山者被从山上医疗后送。|近义词：airlifting, evacuating (medically), transporting (for medical care)|反义词：leaving behind (in a medical emergency)
