distributional:/ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuʃənəl/| adj. | 分布的；分布式的 | Relating to or involving distribution.  The term often refers to the frequency or pattern of occurrence of something across a range or space.  It's commonly used in linguistics, statistics, and computer science. |例句：The distributional analysis of the data revealed a significant correlation. 数据的分布式分析揭示了显著的相关性。

例句：Distributional semantics focuses on the meaning of words based on their context and co-occurrence patterns.  分布式语义学关注的是基于单词的上下文和共现模式的词义。
