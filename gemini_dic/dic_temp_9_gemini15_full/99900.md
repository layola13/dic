internationalised: /ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 国际化的 |  Made international in character or scope; having features of multiple nations.  This word reflects the increasing interconnectedness and interdependence of nations in various aspects, including culture, economy, and politics.  The process involves the adoption of global standards, practices, and perspectives. |


例句：The company has an internationalised workforce, with employees from over 20 countries.  这家公司拥有一支国际化的员工队伍，员工来自20多个国家。

The internationalised education system aims to cultivate global citizens. 国际化的教育体系旨在培养全球公民。


近义词：globalized, worldwide, cosmopolitan


反义词：localized, domestic, national


词性：形容词
