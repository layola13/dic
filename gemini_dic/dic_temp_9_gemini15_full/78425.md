get the better of:/ˌɡɛt ðə ˈbɛtər ʌv/| phrasal verb|战胜；胜过；压倒；占上风|To overcome or defeat someone or something.  It implies a struggle or competition.|例句：The team got the better of their rivals in a close match. 这支球队在一场激烈的比赛中战胜了他们的对手。

例句：His anger got the better of him, and he shouted at his colleague. 他的怒火战胜了他，他冲着同事大喊。


近义词：defeat, overcome, conquer, prevail over, outwit, triumph over


反义词：be defeated by, be overcome by, succumb to


词性:phrasal verb
