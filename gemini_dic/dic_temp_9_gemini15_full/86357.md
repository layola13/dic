hectoliter:/ˈhɛktoʊˌliːtər/ | n. |百升|A hectoliter is a unit of volume equal to 100 liters.  It is primarily used in some European countries for measuring the volume of liquids, especially wine and beer.| The winery produced 50 hectoliters of red wine this year.  这家酒厂今年生产了50百升红葡萄酒。|kiloliter, decaliter|milliliter, liter|
