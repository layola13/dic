/saɪnəˈrɛksiə/ | n. |犬食癖；狂犬症（罕用）|Cynorexia is a rare and unusual eating disorder characterized by an intense craving for dog food.  It's not a formally recognized diagnosis in standard medical texts, but rather a term used informally to describe this specific appetite.  The term blends "cyno" (dog) and "orexia" (appetite).|例句：While not a clinically recognized condition, some individuals report experiencing cynorexia, a strong desire to consume dog food.|近义词：pica (a broader term for eating non-nutritive substances)|反义词：anorexia (loss of appetite)|名词
