look-out-for: /ˈlʊk aʊt fɔːr/ | phrasal verb | 注意；当心；期待；寻找 | To watch carefully for something or someone; to be aware of and prepared for something; to anticipate something; to search for something.  |例句： We need to look out for potential dangers during the hike. 我们需要在徒步旅行中注意潜在的危险。

例句： I'm looking out for a new job with better benefits. 我正在寻找一份福利更好的新工作。


近义词：watch for, be on the lookout for, anticipate, expect, search for | 反义词：ignore, neglect, overlook


PHRASE->
look out for someone/something: 注意某人/某事
look out for yourself: 当心你自己
look out for trouble: 当心麻烦
look out for opportunities: 寻找机会
be on the lookout for: 在寻找


USAGE->


一般现在时:
We look out for wildlife on our nature walks. = 我们在自然散步时注意野生动物。
They look out for each other's safety. = 他们互相注意彼此的安全。


一般过去时:
I looked out for my friends in the crowded room. = 我在拥挤的房间里注意我的朋友们。
She looked out for any signs of trouble. = 她注意任何麻烦的迹象。


现在完成时:
We have looked out for that bird for weeks. = 我们已经寻找那只鸟好几个星期了。
They have looked out for each other since childhood. = 他们从小就互相照顾。


过去完成时:
I had looked out for a suitable apartment before moving. = 我在搬家之前已经寻找合适的公寓了。
She had looked out for that specific flower all day. = 她一整天都在寻找那种特定的花。


将来时:
We will look out for any suspicious activity. = 我们将注意任何可疑活动。
They will look out for their children's education. = 他们将关注他们孩子的教育。


虚拟语气:
If I knew where he was, I would look out for him. = 如果我知道他在哪里，我会注意他的。
I wish I had looked out for the warning signs earlier. = 我希望我早点注意到警告标志。


被动语态: (This phrasal verb is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
What are you looking out for? = 你在注意什么？
Are they looking out for anything specific? = 他们正在寻找什么特别的东西吗？


否定句:
He didn't look out for the falling rocks. = 他没有注意落石。
They didn't look out for the hidden dangers. = 他们没有注意隐藏的危险。
