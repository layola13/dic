satisfy:/ˈsætɪsfaɪ/ | vt. | 使满意；满足；符合 | To fulfill a need, desire, or requirement; to make someone pleased or content. |例句：The meal satisfied my hunger. 这顿饭满足了我的饥饿感。
The explanation satisfied my curiosity.  这个解释满足了我的好奇心。 |近义词：gratify, please, fulfill, meet |反义词：disappoint, frustrate, dissatisfy |

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new job satisfies her. = 这份新工作使她满意。
This explanation satisfies my questions. = 这个解释解答了我的疑问。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
