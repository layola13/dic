ejecting:/ɪˈdʒɛktɪŋ/| v.|喷射；弹出；驱逐；逐出 |The act of forcefully removing something or someone.  It often implies a sudden or forceful expulsion.|例句：The volcano was ejecting ash and lava. 火山正在喷出火山灰和熔岩。|例句：The computer automatically ejected the disk. 电脑自动弹出磁盘。|近义词：expelling, discharging, ousting, throwing out |反义词：inserting, admitting, receiving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine is ejecting the paper. = 机器正在弹出纸张。
He is constantly ejecting negative thoughts. = 他不断地排除负面想法。


一般过去时:
The pilot ejected from the plane. = 飞行员从飞机上弹射出来。
She ejected the SIM card from her phone. = 她从手机中取出SIM卡。


现在完成时:
The volcano has ejected tons of ash. = 火山已经喷出了数吨火山灰。
The system has ejected several users for violating the rules. = 系统已经因为违反规定而将几个用户逐出。


过去完成时:
The plane had ejected its payload before crashing. = 飞机在坠毁前已经投放了有效载荷。
He had ejected the disc before the computer froze. = 电脑死机之前他已经弹出了光盘。


将来时:

They will eject the disruptive student from class. = 他们将把这个捣乱的学生赶出课堂。


虚拟语气:
If the pressure increased, the valve would eject the fluid. = 如果压力增加，阀门就会排出液体。

被动语态:

疑问句:
