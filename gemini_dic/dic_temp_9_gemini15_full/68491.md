famished:/ˈfæmɪʃt/| adj.|极度饥饿的；饿坏了的|Extremely hungry; starving.|例句：After hiking for hours, we were famished.  几个小时的徒步旅行后，我们饿坏了。

例句：I'm absolutely famished; I haven't eaten all day. 我饿坏了，我一天都没吃东西。


近义词：starving, ravenous, hungry, voracious|反义词：full, satiated, replete|词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is famished after a long day's work. 他一天的工作后饿坏了。
They are always famished after football practice. 他们足球训练后总是饿坏了。


一般过去时:
She was famished after the marathon. 马拉松结束后她饿坏了。
We were famished when we finally reached the campsite. 当我们最终到达营地时，我们饿坏了。


现在完成时:
I have been famished since this morning.  从今天早上开始我就一直很饿。
They have been famished all day waiting for the food to arrive. 他们等了一整天食物送达，一直饿着肚子。


过去完成时:
The children had been famished before their mother finally got home.  孩子们在他们的母亲最终回家之前就已经饿坏了。
He had been famished for days before he found help. 他在找到帮助之前已经饿了几天了。


将来时:
I will be famished by the time we reach the restaurant. 等我们到达餐馆的时候，我就会饿坏了。
They will be famished if they don't eat something soon. 如果他们不尽快吃点东西，他们会饿坏的。


虚拟语气:
If I had known we would be famished, I would have packed snacks. 如果我知道我们会饿，我就带些零食了。
I wish I hadn't waited so long; I am famished now. 我真希望我没有等这么久；我现在饿坏了。


被动语态: (该词作形容词，没有被动语态)


疑问句:
Are you famished? 你饿了吗？
Is everyone famished after that long journey? 长途跋涉之后，大家都很饿吗？


否定句:
I am not famished yet. 我还没饿。
They were not famished because they had eaten earlier. 他们不饿，因为他们之前吃过了。
