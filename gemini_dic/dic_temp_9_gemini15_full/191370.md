scheduled:/ˈʃɛdʒuːld/| adj.|预定的，计划好的|Something that is scheduled has been planned and arranged to happen at a particular time.|The meeting is scheduled for 3 pm tomorrow. 明天的会议定在下午3点。|近义词：planned, arranged, programmed |反义词：unscheduled, unplanned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The train is scheduled to arrive at 10 a.m.  火车预定上午十点到达。
My appointment is scheduled for next week. 我的预约安排在下周。


一般过去时:
The concert was scheduled for last night, but it was cancelled. 音乐会原定于昨晚举行，但取消了。
The meeting was scheduled to begin at 2 pm, but it started late. 会议原定下午2点开始，但延后了。


现在完成时:
The project has been scheduled for completion by the end of the year.  该项目已安排在年底前完成。
Everything has been scheduled for the wedding. 婚礼的一切都已经安排好了。


过去完成时:
The presentation had been scheduled for Monday, but it was postponed.  演示原定于星期一进行，但被推迟了。
They had scheduled a meeting before the announcement. 他们在公告之前安排了一次会议。


将来时:
The launch is scheduled to happen next month.  发射计划在下个月进行。
The game is scheduled for next Saturday. 比赛定于下周六举行。


虚拟语气:
If the trip had been scheduled earlier, we could have gone. 如果行程早点安排好，我们就能去了。
I wish the meeting had been scheduled for a later time. 我希望会议安排在稍晚一些的时间。


被动语态:
The flight was scheduled to depart at noon. 航班原定于中午出发。
The repairs were scheduled for next week.  维修工作安排在下周进行。


疑问句:
Is the meeting scheduled for today? 会议安排在今天吗？
When is the performance scheduled? 演出安排在什么时候？


否定句:
The trip wasn't scheduled. 这趟旅行没有安排。
The class is not scheduled for tomorrow. 明天没有这节课。


PHRASE->
scheduled maintenance  计划维护

scheduled departure 预定出发时间
scheduled arrival 预定到达时间
strictly scheduled 严格按照计划
