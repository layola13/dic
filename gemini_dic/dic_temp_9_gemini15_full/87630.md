hewable: /ˈhjuːəbl/ | adj. | 可砍的，可劈的 | Capable of being hewn or cut with an axe or similar tool.  This word describes materials that are easily cut or shaped using a sharp tool like an axe.  | Example: The hewable timber was easily shaped into beams.  这块易于砍伐的木材很容易被制成梁。 | 近义词: cuttable,  workable | 反义词: unyielding, unworkable
