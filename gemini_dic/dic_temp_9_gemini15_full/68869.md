fast-trackers:/ˈfæstˌtrækərz/| n.| 快速晋升者，快速发展者 | Individuals or things that progress rapidly through a system or process, often bypassing typical stages.  They are characterized by accelerated growth, advancement, or development. | Example: The company is known for its fast-trackers, promoting employees quickly based on merit. 这家公司以其快速晋升者而闻名，根据能力迅速提升员工。  |近义词：high-flyers, high achievers,  rapid developers|反义词：slowpokes, underachievers
