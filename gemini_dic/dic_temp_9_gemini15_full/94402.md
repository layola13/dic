implicate:/ˈɪmplɪkeɪt/| vt.|牵连；暗示；包含；意味|To involve someone in a crime or wrongdoing, or to suggest that someone is involved.  To show that something is a consequence of something else; to imply.|例句：The evidence implicates him in the robbery.  证据表明他参与了抢劫案。

The study implicated a lack of exercise in the rise of heart disease. 这项研究表明缺乏运动与心脏病发病率上升有关。|近义词：involve, suggest, indicate, imply|反义词：exonerate, clear, absolve|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence implicates the suspect in the crime. = 证据牵连嫌疑犯参与了犯罪。
This research implicates poor diet in the development of certain cancers. = 这项研究表明不良饮食与某些癌症的发生有关。


一般过去时:
The witness implicated the defendant in the conspiracy. = 证人牵连被告参与了阴谋。
The investigation implicated several high-ranking officials in corruption. = 调查牵涉到几名高级官员的腐败行为。


现在完成时:
The investigation has implicated several politicians in the scandal. = 调查已经牵涉到几名政客卷入了丑闻。
Recent studies have implicated air pollution in respiratory illnesses. = 最近的研究表明空气污染与呼吸系统疾病有关。


过去完成时:
The police had implicated the driver in the hit-and-run accident before he confessed. = 在司机认罪之前，警方已经将他牵涉到这起肇事逃逸事故中。
It had been implicated that the company's CEO was aware of the illegal activities. =  人们已经暗示公司首席执行官知道这些非法活动。


将来时:
The upcoming report will likely implicate several companies in environmental damage. = 即将发布的报告可能会牵涉到几家公司对环境造成的损害。
Further investigations will implicate more individuals in the fraud. = 进一步的调查将牵涉到更多参与欺诈的个人。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence implicated him, he would be arrested. = 如果证据牵连到他，他就会被逮捕。
I wish the investigation hadn't implicated my friend. = 我真希望调查没有牵涉到我的朋友。


被动语态:
He was implicated in the scandal and lost his job. = 他卷入了丑闻并丢掉了工作。
The company was implicated in the environmental disaster. = 这家公司牵涉到这场环境灾难中。


疑问句:
Does the evidence implicate anyone else? = 证据是否牵连了其他人？
What factors are implicated in this disease? = 哪些因素与这种疾病有关？


否定句:
The investigation did not implicate the CEO in the wrongdoing. = 调查没有牵连首席执行官参与不法行为。
The report did not implicate any specific individuals. = 报告没有牵连任何特定个人。


PHRASE->
be implicated in = 牵涉到...
implicate oneself =  自陷困境
implicate a theory = 暗示一种理论
implicate in a crime = 牵涉到一起犯罪
implicated in a scandal = 牵涉到一个丑闻
