naysay:/ˈneɪseɪ/| v.|反对；否决；唱反调|To express opposition to or disagreement with something.  It often implies persistent or repeated opposition.|例句：He naysays every suggestion I make. 他反对我提出的每一个建议。
例句：Don't just naysay; offer some constructive criticism. 不要只是反对；提出一些建设性的批评。|近义词：object, oppose, contradict, dissent|反义词：approve, endorse, support, agree


USAGE->

He naysays every new policy. = 他反对每一项新政策。
They naysay the proposed changes. = 他们反对提议的更改。


一般过去时:
She naysayed the idea at the meeting. = 她在会议上否决了这个主意。
