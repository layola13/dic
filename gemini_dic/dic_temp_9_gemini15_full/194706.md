self-wisdom:/selfˈwɪzdəm/| n.| 自知之明，自我智慧 |  Self-wisdom refers to the understanding and acceptance of one's own strengths, weaknesses, values, and limitations. It involves self-reflection and a deep understanding of one's own inner world, leading to better decision-making and personal growth.  | Example:  Cultivating self-wisdom allows individuals to make choices aligned with their authentic selves.  培养自我智慧使个人能够做出与真实自我相符的选择。| 近义词：self-awareness, self-knowledge, introspection | 反义词：self-deception, ignorance, delusion | 名词
