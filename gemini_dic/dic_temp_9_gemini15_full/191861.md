sclerous:/ˈsklɪərəs/ | adj. | 硬化的；巩膜的；纤维化的 | Relating to or characterized by sclerosis; hard; firm.  The term often refers to the hardening of tissues due to an increase in fibrous connective tissue.  It can specifically refer to hardening of the sclera (the white of the eye). | 例句：The patient presented with sclerous changes in the arteries.  患者动脉出现了硬化变化。 | 例句：Sclerous plaques were observed during the ophthalmological examination. 在眼科检查中观察到硬化斑块。 | 近义词：hardened, indurated, fibrotic | 反义词：soft, pliable, flaccid
