cocculus: /ˈkɒkjʊləs/ | n. |木防己属植物；(Cocculus indicus) 山豆根 | A genus of climbing shrubs belonging to the family Menispermaceae, characterized by their bitter taste and often used medicinally or as insecticides.  The most well-known species is *Cocculus indicus*, also known as Indian cockle, fish berries, or Anamirta cocculus. | Example:  The extract from *Cocculus indicus* has traditionally been used as a pesticide. 山豆根的提取物传统上被用作杀虫剂。 | 近义词：Menispermum (同科植物) | 反义词：无
