decarbonize: /ˌdiːˈkɑːrbənaɪz/ | vt. | 脱碳；减少碳排放 | To reduce or remove carbon dioxide from (something), especially the atmosphere.  | 例句：Many countries are committed to decarbonizing their economies by 2050. 许多国家致力于到2050年实现经济脱碳。

例句：The company is investing heavily in renewable energy to decarbonize its operations. 这家公司正在大力投资可再生能源以使其运营脱碳。


近义词：decarbonise (BrE), decarbonate


反义词：carbonize, increase carbon emissions


词性:动词
