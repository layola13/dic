periodate:/ˈpɪəriədeɪt/| n.|高碘酸盐|A salt or ester of periodic acid.  Periodates are oxidizing agents used in organic chemistry and analytical chemistry.  They are typically used in the oxidation of alcohols, glycols and other organic compounds.|Example: Sodium periodate is a common reagent in organic synthesis.|近义词：periodic acid salt|反义词：无
