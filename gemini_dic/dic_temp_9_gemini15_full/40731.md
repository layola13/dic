convexness:/kənˈvɛksnəs/ | n. | 凸性，凸度 | The state or quality of being convex;  curvature outwards.  It describes a surface that curves outward, like the outside of a sphere.  | Example: The convexness of the lens focused the light. 凸透镜的凸面聚焦了光线。 The remarkable convexness of the earth's surface is evident in the curvature of the horizon. 地球表面的显著凸起体现在地平线的曲率上。 |近义词：curvature, roundness | 反义词：concavity, concaveness
