hotels:/hoʊˈtɛlz/ | n. | 旅馆；酒店 | Establishments providing lodging, typically with various services.  Hotels range from budget-friendly options to luxurious resorts, offering rooms, amenities, and sometimes dining options. | Example: We booked a hotel near the beach for our vacation. 我们预订了一家靠近海滩的酒店作为我们的度假场所。 |近义词：inns, motels, lodges | 反义词：private residence, home


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Hotels offer various amenities. = 旅馆提供各种便利设施。
Many hotels use online booking systems. = 许多酒店使用在线预订系统。


一般过去时:
