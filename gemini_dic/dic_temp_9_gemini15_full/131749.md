nonconfrontational: /ˌnɒnkɒnfrʌnˈteɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 不对抗的；不冲突的；和平的 | Not causing or involving conflict; peaceful.  |  He adopted a nonconfrontational approach to the negotiations. 他采取了一种不对抗的谈判方式。 | 近义词：peaceful, conciliatory, amicable, accommodating | 反义词：confrontational, aggressive, hostile, antagonistic
