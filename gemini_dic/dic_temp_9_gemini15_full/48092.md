declawed: /dɪˈklɔːd/ | adj. | 去爪的，已去爪的 |  Describes an animal, usually a cat, that has had its claws surgically removed.  |  The declawed cat was less able to defend itself.  去爪的猫更难以自卫。 The kitten was declawed before it was adopted.  这只小猫在被收养前就被去爪了。|  unclawed (not commonly used in this context) | clawed (having claws) |  adj.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat is declawed. = 这只猫被去爪了。
He is declawed. = 他被去爪了。(指动物)


一般过去时:
The vet declawed the cat. = 兽医给猫去爪了。
They declawed the lion. = 他们给狮子去爪了。


现在完成时:
The cat has been declawed. = 这只猫已经被去爪了。
Many cats have been declawed. = 许多猫都被去爪了。


过去完成时:
The cat had been declawed before the adoption. = 猫在被领养之前已经被去爪了。
The owner had declawed the cat years ago. = 主人几年前就给猫去爪了。


将来时:
The vet will declaw the cat tomorrow. = 兽医明天将给猫去爪。
They will have the cat declawed next week. = 他们下周将给猫去爪。


虚拟语气:
If the cat were declawed, it would be safer. = 如果猫被去爪，它会更安全。
I wish the cat hadn't been declawed. = 我希望猫没有被去爪。


被动语态:
The cat was declawed by the vet. = 猫被兽医去爪了。
The claws were declawed. = 爪子被去掉了。(较少用，通常直接用declawed)


疑问句:
Is the cat declawed? = 这只猫被去爪了吗？
Was the cat declawed? = 这只猫被去爪了吗？


否定句:
The cat is not declawed. = 这只猫没有被去爪。
The cat was not declawed. = 这只猫没有被去爪。


PHRASE->
declawed cat = 去爪的猫
