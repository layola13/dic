cross-referencing:/krɒsˈrɛfərɪŋ/| v. |交叉引用；相互参照|The act of referencing one part of a text or document to another, or to other related texts or documents, to provide further information or context. This is often done to clarify meaning, establish connections, or avoid repetition.  |Example:  The footnotes in the academic paper extensively cross-referenced related theories.  这篇学术论文中的脚注广泛地交叉引用了相关的理论。


The index cross-references all the major topics in the book.  该索引交叉引用了书中所有主要主题。|近义词：referencing, interlinking, correlating|反义词：isolating, separating|动词
