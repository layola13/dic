dentated:/ˈdɛnteɪtɪd/ | adj. | 有齿的；齿状的 | Having teeth or tooth-like projections.  The term often describes edges or surfaces with a jagged, tooth-like appearance.  | Example: The leaf margin was dentated, showing small, pointed teeth.  叶子的边缘是齿状的，显示出小的尖齿。 The saw blade had a finely dentated edge for precise cutting.  锯齿的刃口是细齿状的，用于精确切割。 | 近义词：toothed, serrated, jagged | 反义词：smooth, entire
