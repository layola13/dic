fin:/fɪn/ | n. |鳍；鱼翅；(船的)尾鳍；(飞机的)尾翼 | A fin is a limb of a fish, used for swimming.  It can also refer to similar structures on other aquatic animals or the stabilizing structures on vehicles. | Example: The shark used its powerful fins to propel itself through the water.  鲨鱼用它强壮的鳍在水中推进自己。 The airplane's fin helped stabilize it during flight. 飞机的尾翼帮助它在飞行中保持稳定。|近义词：flipper (for certain animals), winglet (for aircraft) |反义词：无
