domed:/doʊmd/| adj. |圆顶的；拱顶的；穹顶的|Having a dome-shaped roof or top.  The word evokes images of structures with a rounded, vaulted ceiling.|例句：The domed cathedral was a breathtaking sight.  那座圆顶大教堂景色令人叹为观止。
The planetarium featured a large domed ceiling.天文馆有一个巨大的圆顶天花板。|近义词：vaulted, cupolaed, arched |反义词：flat-roofed, open-topped
