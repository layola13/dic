cant:/kænt/| v. |不能，不会|To be unable to; lack the ability or means to.|I can't believe it! 我不敢相信！|近义词：cannot, be unable to|反义词：can, be able to


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I can't go to the party. = 我不能去参加派对。
He can't speak French. = 他不会说法语。


一般过去时:
I couldn't finish the work on time. = 我没能按时完成工作。
She couldn't come to the meeting. = 她没能来参加会议。


现在完成时:
I haven't been able to contact him. = 我一直联系不上他。
They haven't been able to solve the problem. = 他们一直没能解决这个问题。


过去完成时:
I couldn't have done it without your help. = 没有你的帮助我做不到。
She hadn't been able to sleep all night. = 她一夜都没睡着。


将来时:
I won't be able to attend the conference. = 我将无法参加会议。
He won't be able to make it to the dinner. = 他将无法参加晚宴。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I could finish the project. = 如果我有更多时间，我就能完成这个项目。
I wish I could go with you. = 我希望我能和你一起去。


被动语态:
The problem can't be solved easily. = 这个问题不容易解决。
The task cannot be completed in a day. = 这个任务一天内无法完成。


疑问句:
Can you help me with this? = 你能帮我这个忙吗？
Can't you see the sign? = 你难道没看见标志吗？


否定句:
I can't believe what I'm seeing. = 我不敢相信我所看到的。
He can't possibly know the answer. = 他不可能知道答案。


PHRASE->
can't help but = 不禁
can't wait to = 迫不及待地
can't stand = 受不了
can't seem to = 好像不能
can't make it = 不能来
