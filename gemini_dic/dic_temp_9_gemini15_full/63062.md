enroll:/ɪnˈroʊl/ | vt. |注册，登记；招收；使成为成员 |to register or become registered as a member of a course, school, etc.  |例句：I enrolled in a French class last week. 我上周注册了一门法语课。|例句：The university enrolled over 5000 new students this year.  今年大学招收了5000多名新生。|近义词：register, sign up, enlist |反义词：withdraw, drop out |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enrolls in a new online course every semester. 他每个学期都报名参加一个新的在线课程。
She enrolls her children in swimming lessons. 她为孩子们报名参加游泳课。


一般过去时:
I enrolled in that college five years ago. 五年前我报考了那所大学。
They enrolled their daughter in a prestigious school. 他们把女儿送进了一所名校。


现在完成时:
He has enrolled in three different universities. 他已经注册了三所不同的大学。
She has enrolled in a yoga class to improve her flexibility. 她报名参加了瑜伽课来提高柔韧性。


过去完成时:
I had enrolled in the program before the deadline. 我在截止日期前已经报名参加了该项目。
They had enrolled their son in a summer camp before he left. 他们在儿子离开之前已经让他参加了夏令营。


将来时:
I will enroll in a cooking class next month. 下个月我将报名参加烹饪课。
She will enroll her child in kindergarten next year. 明年她将把孩子送入幼儿园。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would enroll in a painting class. 如果我有更多的时间，我会报名参加绘画课。
I wish I had enrolled in that course earlier. 我真希望我早点报名参加那个课程。


被动语态:
Many students were enrolled in the advanced mathematics course. 许多学生被选入高等数学课程。
He was enrolled in the army last year. 他去年参军了。


疑问句:
Will you enroll in the university this year? 你今年会报考这所大学吗？
Have you enrolled for the marathon yet? 你报名参加马拉松了吗？


否定句:
I did not enroll in any extra classes this term. 这个学期我没有选修任何额外的课程。
She didn't enroll her son in that particular school. 她没有把儿子送进那所学校。
