homoplasmy:/ˈhoʊməˌplæzmi/| n. |同质性（指细胞或个体中只存在一种类型的细胞器基因组）|Homoplasmy refers to a genetic condition where all copies of an organelle genome within a cell or individual are identical. This is in contrast to heteroplasmy, where multiple types of organelle genomes coexist.|Example:  The researchers observed homoplasmy for the mitochondrial DNA in all the studied individuals.  研究人员在所有受试者中观察到线粒体DNA的同质性。|近义词：uniformity, homogeneity |反义词：heteroplasmy
