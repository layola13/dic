re-blossoms:/ˌriːˈblɒsəmz/| v.|重新开花；再次繁荣；复苏|To blossom again; to flourish anew; to experience a revival or resurgence.  The word combines the prefix "re-" (again) and the verb "blossoms" (flowers bloom).|例句：After a period of hardship, the business re-blossomed, exceeding all expectations.  这家企业在经历一段艰难时期后重新兴旺起来，超出了所有预期。

The rose bushes re-blossomed after the unexpected rain.  玫瑰花丛在意外降雨后再次开花。|近义词：reflourish, revive, regenerate|反义词：wither, decay, decline|动词
