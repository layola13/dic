pro-Protestant: /ˌproʊˈprɒtəstənt/ | adj. | 支持新教的；亲新教的 |  Favoring or supporting Protestantism.  This term describes individuals, groups, or policies that actively advocate for or show preference towards Protestantism.  It can refer to political stances, social movements, or individual beliefs. | The pro-Protestant sentiment in the region led to the establishment of several new churches.  |近义词：pro-Reformation, Protestant-supporting |反义词：anti-Protestant, pro-Catholic
