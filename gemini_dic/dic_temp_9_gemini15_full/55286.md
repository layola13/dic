dissociate:/dɪˈsoʊʃieɪt/|/dɪˈsəʊsieɪt/| vt. |使分离；使脱离；断绝关系 |To separate or disconnect something from something else; to break a connection or association.  |例句：He tried to dissociate himself from the scandal. 他试图与丑闻撇清关系。|例句：The doctor advised her to dissociate herself from stressful situations. 医生建议她远离有压力的环境。|近义词：separate, disconnect, detach, sever, disengage|反义词：associate, connect, unite, link, join|动词
