expenditor:/ɪkˈspɛndɪtər/ | n. |支出者，经费管理员|One who expends or disburses funds; a person responsible for managing and distributing funds.  The word suggests a more formal or official role than simply spending money. |The expenditor oversaw the distribution of funds for the construction project.  支出者负责监督建筑项目的资金分配。|disburser, administrator, manager |saver, hoarder |名词
