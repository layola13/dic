mollifications:/ˌmɒlɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 缓和；安慰；柔化 |  The act of mollifying; the state of being mollified; a softening or soothing.  Mollifications are actions or things that soothe or calm anger, distress, or harshness.  | Example: The mollifications offered by the company to its disgruntled employees included bonuses and improved working conditions. 公司为其不满的员工提供的安抚措施包括奖金和改善的工作条件。 |近义词： appeasements, soothings, softenings | 反义词：exacerbations, aggravations, intensifications | 名词
