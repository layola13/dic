canto:/ˈkæntoʊ/ | n. |诗节；篇章；乐章|A canto is a major division of a long poem, like a chapter in a book.  It's often used in epic poems or narrative poems.  |Example:  The epic poem is divided into twelve cantos. 这首史诗被分成十二个诗节。The final canto of the poem was particularly moving. 这首诗的最后一节特别感人。|近义词：section, chapter, division |反义词：无
