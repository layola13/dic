oneway:/ˈwʌnweɪ/ | adj. | 单向的；单程的 |  Going in only one direction; allowing movement or travel in only one direction.  | Example: This is a one-way street. 这是一条单行道。  | Example:  We took a one-way ticket to London. 我们买了去伦敦的单程票。 |近义词：unidirectional, single-direction | 反义词：two-way, round-trip |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The road is one-way. = 这条路是单行道。
This ticket is one-way. = 这张票是单程票。

一般过去时:
The street was one-way even then. = 那时候这条街也是单行道。
Their trip was a one-way journey. = 他们的旅行是一次单程旅程。

现在完成时:
The city has made that street one-way. = 市政府已将那条街改为单行道。
We have booked a one-way flight. = 我们已经预订了一张单程机票。

过去完成时:
They had already realized it was a one-way street. = 他们已经意识到这是一条单行道了。
I had forgotten it was a one-way ticket. = 我忘了这是一张单程票。

将来时:
The new highway will be one-way. = 新高速公路将是单行道。
We will buy one-way tickets. = 我们将买单程票。

虚拟语气:
If it were a one-way street, we wouldn't have gone that way. = 如果这是一条单行道，我们就不会走那条路了。
I wish I had known it was a one-way ticket. = 我真希望我知道这是一张单程票。

被动语态:
The street is being made one-way. = 这条街正在被改为单行道。

疑问句:
Is this a one-way street? = 这是一条单行道吗？
Are the tickets one-way or round-trip? = 这些票是单程票还是往返票？

否定句:
This isn't a one-way street. = 这不是一条单行道。
The journey is not one-way; it's a round trip. = 这趟旅程不是单程的；它是往返旅程。
