deconstructive:/ˌdiːkənˈstrʌktɪv/| adj.|解构的，去建构的|Analyzing and questioning traditional assumptions, structures, or interpretations, often to expose underlying power structures or biases.|The deconstructive approach to literature focuses on revealing hidden meanings and contradictions.|近义词：analytical, critical, subversive|反义词：constructive, affirmative
