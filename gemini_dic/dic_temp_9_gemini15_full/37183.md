comedians:/kəˈmiːdiən/| n. |喜剧演员|A comedian is a person who makes people laugh, typically by telling jokes or performing in a comedic style.  Comedians often work in various settings, including stand-up comedy clubs, television shows, and movies. They use humor to entertain and often to comment on social issues or current events.|Example:  Many famous comedians started their careers in small comedy clubs.  许多著名的喜剧演员都是从小型的喜剧俱乐部开始他们的职业生涯的。

Many young comedians aspire to perform on late-night television. 许多年轻的喜剧演员渴望在深夜电视节目中表演。|近义词：comic, humorist, funny person|反义词：tragedian, serious person
