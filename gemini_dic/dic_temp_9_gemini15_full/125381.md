multimodal:/ˌmʌltiˈmoʊdəl/| adj.|多模式的，多通道的|Relating to or involving multiple modes or channels of communication or data transmission.  For example, a multimodal system might combine text, images, audio, and video. |例句：This multimodal learning approach incorporates visual aids, audio recordings, and interactive exercises.  这个多模式学习方法结合了视觉辅助工具、录音和互动练习。|例句：The company's multimodal communication strategy reaches a wider audience through various platforms. 公司的多模式沟通策略通过各种平台接触更广泛的受众。|近义词：polymodal, multisensory|反义词：unimodal, single-modal

词源:
The word "multimodal" is derived from the prefix "multi-" (meaning "many" or "multiple") and the noun "modal" (referring to a mode or method).  "Modal" itself has roots in Latin "modus," meaning "manner" or "method."  Therefore, "multimodal" literally means "having many modes" or "using multiple methods."
