It can refer to legal detention, or simply a delay. | 例句：The police made several detainments during the protest.  警方在抗议期间进行了多次拘留。 The unexpected snowstorm caused significant detainments at the airport.  突如其来的暴风雪导致机场出现严重的延误。 | 近义词：arrests, apprehensions, delays, postponements | 反义词：releases, liberations, accelerations | 名词
