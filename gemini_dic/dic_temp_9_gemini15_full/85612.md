haughtiness:/ˈhɔːtɪnəs/| n. |傲慢；自大；高傲 |The quality of being haughty; arrogant superiority manifested in disdain of those considered inferior.  It suggests a condescending attitude and a feeling of superiority.  |例句：Her haughtiness alienated many of her colleagues. = 她的傲慢疏远了她许多同事。

例句：There was a certain haughtiness in his manner that put people off. = 他举止中带有一种傲慢，让人反感。


近义词：arrogance, pride, superciliousness, disdain, condescension |反义词：humility, modesty, humbleness


词源：源自于古法语haute，意思是“高”，最终源于拉丁语altus，意思是“高”。后缀-ness表示名词，表示“……的状态或性质”。因此，haughtiness字面意思就是“高高在上的状态”。
