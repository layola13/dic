inching:/ɪntʃɪŋ/| v.|慢慢移动；逐渐前进|Moving slowly or gradually.|The snail inched its way across the leaf.  蜗牛慢慢地爬过树叶。|The price of gasoline is inching upward.  汽油价格正在逐渐上涨。|creeping, crawling, progressing|speeding, rushing|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car inches forward in the heavy traffic. = 汽车在拥堵的交通中缓慢前进。
He inches closer to the edge of the cliff. = 他慢慢地靠近悬崖边。


一般过去时:
The climber inched his way up the steep mountain. = 登山者缓慢地向上攀登陡峭的山峰。
She inched the box across the floor. = 她慢慢地把箱子推过地板。


现在完成时:
The plant has inched its way towards the sunlight. = 植物已经慢慢地朝阳光生长。
The temperature has inched up to 30 degrees. = 气温已经缓慢上升到30度。


过去完成时:
The thief had inched open the window before entering the house. = 小偷在进入房子之前已经慢慢地打开了窗户。
They had inched their way through the dense forest. = 他们已经慢慢地穿过茂密的森林。


将来时:
The stock price will inch higher in the coming weeks. = 股价将在未来几周内缓慢上涨。
The project will inch closer to completion. = 项目将逐渐接近完成。


虚拟语气:
If the car inched forward, we could pass. = 如果汽车缓慢前进，我们就能通过。
I wish the line would inch forward faster. = 我希望队伍能更快地缓慢前进。


被动语态:
The door was inched open by a burglar. = 门被窃贼慢慢地打开。
The project is being inched towards completion. = 项目正在缓慢地接近完成。


疑问句:
Is the needle inching closer to the red zone? = 指针是不是正在慢慢地靠近红色区域？
Is the economy inching towards recovery? = 经济是不是正在缓慢地走向复苏？


否定句:
The car didn't inch forward, it stalled. = 汽车没有缓慢前进，而是停住了。
The negotiations didn't inch closer to a resolution. = 谈判并没有更接近解决方案。
