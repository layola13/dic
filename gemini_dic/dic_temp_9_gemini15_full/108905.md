ledger:/ˈlɛdʒər/| n.|分类账，账簿，记录簿|A ledger is a book or file for recording transactions.  It's a systematic record of financial accounts.  The term is often associated with accounting and bookkeeping.|例句：The accountant meticulously updated the ledger with each transaction. 会计仔细地用每次交易更新分类账。|例句：The company's ledger showed a significant profit for the quarter. 公司的分类账显示本季度盈利显著。|近义词：account book, record book, register|反义词：无
