ruffian-like:/ˈrʌfjənlaɪk/| adj. |流氓似的，恶棍般的|Resembling a ruffian; rough, violent, and lawless.  Characterized by the behavior or appearance of a ruffian.|例句：He had a ruffian-like swagger. 他有一种流氓似的傲慢姿态。

例句：The ruffian-like behavior of the gang led to their arrest.  这伙人的流氓行为导致了他们的逮捕。


近义词：thuggish, hooliganish, gangster-like, villainous, loutish


反义词：gentle, refined, courteous, polite, well-behaved
