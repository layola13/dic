confusion:/kənˈfjuːʒən/ | n. |困惑，混乱 | a state of being unclear or uncertain; a lack of understanding |The confusion over the new rules led to widespread errors. 新规的混乱导致了广泛的错误。  There was a great deal of confusion after the accident. 事故发生后一片混乱。| bewilderment, disorientation, chaos |clarity, order |

This situation creates confusion. = 这种情况造成了混乱。
