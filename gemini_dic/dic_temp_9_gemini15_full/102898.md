jawans:/dʒəˈwɑːnz/| n. |士兵，军人（印度语）|The word "jawans" is a Hindi word referring to soldiers or enlisted men in the Indian armed forces.  It is often used collectively to refer to a group of soldiers.  The term carries a sense of respect and camaraderie.|Example: The jawans fought bravely in the battle.  士兵们在战斗中英勇作战。  The jawans were commended for their dedication.  士兵们因其奉献精神而受到表彰。|近义词：soldiers, servicemen, troops, combatants |反义词： civilians
