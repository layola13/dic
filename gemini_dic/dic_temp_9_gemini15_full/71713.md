flavouring: /ˈfleɪvəriŋ/ | n. |调味品，调味料 |A substance used to add flavor to food or drink.  It can be natural or artificial. |例句：The cake was enhanced by the addition of almond flavouring.  这款蛋糕添加了杏仁调味料，味道更好了。

例句：Many processed foods contain artificial flavourings.  许多加工食品含有人工调味剂。


近义词：seasoning, spice, condiment |反义词：None (no direct antonym)
