palavering:/ˈpæləvərɪŋ/| v.|喋喋不休地谈论；空谈；闲聊 | To talk at length, especially in an idle or inconsequential way; to chatter. |例句：They spent hours palavering about nothing important. 他们花了好几个小时喋喋不休地谈论一些无关紧要的事情。 They were palavering about the upcoming election. 他们在闲聊即将到来的选举。|近义词：chattering, gossiping, prattling, jabbering |反义词：remaining silent, being concise
