queen-size:/kwɪnˈsaɪz/| adj.|女王尺寸的，加大双人床的|  Describes a bed larger than a double bed, typically 60 inches wide by 80 inches long.  It's often used to describe other furniture or items of similar proportions.|例句：We bought a queen-size bed for our new bedroom. = 我们为我们的新卧室买了一张加大双人床。

例句：The quilt is queen-size, so it fits perfectly on the bed. = 这床罩是加大双人床尺寸的，所以非常适合这张床。


近义词：double (in some contexts), full (in some contexts)
反义词：single, twin


词性:形容词
