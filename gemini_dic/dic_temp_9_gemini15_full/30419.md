centralest:/ˈsɛntrələst/| adj.| 最中心的；最重要的 | Most central; of the greatest importance.  This word is a superlative form of "central," and while grammatically correct, it's rarely used in formal writing.  It emphasizes the extreme degree of centrality. | The centralest issue is the economy.  = 最重要的问题是经济。 The centralest point of the city was the market square. = 城市最中心的地方是市场广场。| central, chief, main, primary | peripheral, outlying, secondary |形容词
