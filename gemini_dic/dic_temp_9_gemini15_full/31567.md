chasers:/ˈtʃeɪsər/| n.|追赶者；追逐者；（尤指）烈酒；|Someone or something that chases.  Often refers to a person pursuing someone or something, or a drink that is consumed quickly after another.|例句：The police were the chasers in the high-speed car chase.  警察是高速追车中的追赶者。|例句：He downed two chasers after his tequila shots. 他在喝龙舌兰酒后又喝了两杯烈酒。|近义词： pursuer, follower, follower,  (for drinks)  swigs, shots|反义词：chased (past participle of chase),  逃脱者(escaped)|名词
