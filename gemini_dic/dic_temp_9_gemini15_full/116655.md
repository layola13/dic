matters:/ˈmætərz/| n. v.|重要的事；问题；情况；有关系|Matters of importance should be dealt with promptly. 重要的事情应该迅速处理。The matter needs further investigation. 这件事需要进一步调查。|近义词：issues, affairs, concerns, problems|反义词：irrelevancies, trivialities|名词，动词

一般现在时:
The matter matters a great deal to me. = 这件事对我非常重要。
These matters require immediate attention. = 这些事情需要立即处理。


一般过去时:
The matter mattered greatly to him. = 这件事对他非常重要。
The court considered the matters carefully. = 法院仔细考虑了这些事情。


现在完成时:
The matter has mattered to us for years. = 这件事多年来对我们很重要。
These matters have already been discussed. = 这些事情已经被讨论过了。


过去完成时:
The matter had mattered significantly before the event. = 事件发生前，这件事非常重要。
They had already settled the matters before the meeting. = 会议前他们已经解决了这些事情。


将来时:
The matter will matter even more in the future. = 这件事将来会更加重要。

虚拟语气:
If this matter mattered less, I would be less stressed. = 如果这件事没那么重要，我就不会这么紧张了。
I wish the matter hadn't mattered so much. = 我希望这件事没那么重要。


被动语态:
The matter was handled efficiently. = 这件事处理得很有效率。
These matters are being addressed by the committee. = 这些事情正在由委员会处理。


疑问句:

否定句:
