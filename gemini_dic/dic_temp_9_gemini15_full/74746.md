freeware:/ˈfriːˌwɛr/ | n. |免费软件 |Software that is available for use at no cost.  Freeware is often distributed under a license that permits use, but may restrict modification, redistribution, or commercial use. |Example: Many useful utilities are available as freeware.  许多有用的实用程序可以作为免费软件获得。|近义词：shareware, open-source software (with limitations)|反义词：commercial software, paid software
