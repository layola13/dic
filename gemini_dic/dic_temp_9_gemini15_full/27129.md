calisaya:/ˌkælɪˈseɪə/| n. |卡利萨亚树（金鸡纳树的一种）|A species of Cinchona tree, a source of quinine.  The bark of the calisaya tree is rich in quinine, a crucial medicine historically used to treat malaria.|Example:  The bark of the calisaya tree was harvested for its quinine content. 卡利萨亚树的树皮因其奎宁含量而被采收。|近义词：Cinchona (general term)|反义词：无
