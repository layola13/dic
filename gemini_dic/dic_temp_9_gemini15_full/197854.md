shipbuilder:/ˈʃɪpˌbɪldər/| n. |造船者，造船厂|A shipbuilder is a person or company that builds ships.  They design, construct, and often repair vessels of various sizes and types.|Example:  The skilled shipbuilder crafted a magnificent sailing ship.|近义词：shipwright, ship constructor|反义词：N/A
