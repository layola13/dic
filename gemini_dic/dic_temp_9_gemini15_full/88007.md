high-speed: /ˈhaɪ spiːd/ | adj. |高速的，高速度的 |  Describes something that moves or operates at a very fast speed.  |例句：The high-speed train reached its destination quickly. 高速火车很快就到达了目的地。

例句：This high-speed internet connection allows for seamless streaming.  这个高速网络连接允许无缝流媒体播放。


近义词：fast, rapid, express, swift | 反义词：slow, sluggish, leisurely


词性：形容词
