sallowish:/ˈsæləʊɪʃ/ | adj. |略带黄色的，病态苍白的 | Slightly yellowish or sickly pale in complexion.  The adjective suggests a pale, unhealthy color, often associated with illness or lack of sunlight. |例句：Her sallowish complexion suggested she needed some time in the sun.  她略显黄的脸色暗示她需要晒晒太阳。

Her face was sallowish and drawn, a sign of her recent illness.她的脸苍白憔悴，是近期生病的迹象。|近义词：pale, sickly, jaundiced, wan |反义词：ruddy, rosy, florid


词源:  Derived from "sallow," meaning yellowish or sickly pale.  The suffix "-ish" adds a diminutive or slightly less intense quality.  Ultimately, "sallow" has origins in Old English *sealh, meaning willow tree, referring to the yellowish-green color of willow leaves.
