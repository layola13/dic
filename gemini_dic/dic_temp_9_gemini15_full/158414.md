pleasures:/ˈplɛʒərz/| n.|快乐；乐趣；令人高兴的事物|The pleasures of life include love, friendship, and good health.  生活中的乐趣包括爱情、友谊和健康。|The pleasures of a summer vacation are many. 夏日假期的乐趣很多。|joys, delights, gratifications|pains, sorrows, sufferings|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His pleasures are simple. = 他的快乐很简单。
My greatest pleasure is reading. = 我最大的乐趣是阅读。


一般过去时:
We shared many pleasures during our trip. = 我们在旅行中分享了许多快乐。
The concert gave us great pleasure. = 音乐会给我们带来了极大的快乐。


现在完成时:
I have experienced many pleasures in my life. = 我一生中经历过许多快乐。
She has found pleasure in her work. = 她从工作中找到了快乐。


过去完成时:
They had already experienced the pleasures of parenthood. = 他们已经体验过为人父母的乐趣。
We had never known such pure pleasure before. = 我们以前从未体验过如此纯粹的快乐。


将来时:
We will enjoy many pleasures in the future. = 我们将来会享受许多快乐。
She will find pleasure in her new job. = 她会在她的新工作中找到乐趣。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would indulge in more pleasures. = 如果我有更多时间，我会沉溺于更多乐趣。
I wish I could share these pleasures with you. = 我希望我能和你分享这些快乐。


被动语态:
Pleasures are often fleeting. = 快乐往往是短暂的。
The pleasures of the senses are easily overindulged. = 感官的快乐很容易沉溺其中。


疑问句:
What are your greatest pleasures in life? = 你生活中最大的快乐是什么？
What pleasures does this job offer? = 这份工作能提供什么乐趣？


否定句:
He found no pleasure in his work. = 他在工作中找不到乐趣。
There were no pleasures to be found in that harsh environment. = 在那个恶劣的环境中找不到任何乐趣。
