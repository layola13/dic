geck:/ɡɛk/ | n. |壁虎|A small lizard that is often found in warm climates and is known for its ability to climb walls and ceilings. Geckos are typically nocturnal and are known for their sticky toes.  |例句：I saw a gecko on the wall last night.  昨晚我在墙上看到一只壁虎。  |例句：Many species of geckos are kept as pets. 许多种类的壁虎被当作宠物饲养。|近义词：lizard|反义词：无
