contrives:/kənˈtraɪvz/| vt.|设计；图谋；设法做到|To plan cleverly or secretly, often in order to achieve something that is difficult or dishonest; to manage to do something despite difficulties.|例句：She contrived a plan to get revenge. 她精心策划了一个报复计划。|例句：He contrived to get a seat at the concert. 他设法在音乐会上弄到一个座位。|近义词：devise, invent, plot, scheme|反义词：fail, give up, abandon|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He contrives elaborate schemes. = 他精心策划着复杂的计划。
She contrives ways to help others. = 她想方设法帮助他人。

一般过去时:
They contrived a clever solution. = 他们想出了一个巧妙的解决办法。

将来时:

否定句:
