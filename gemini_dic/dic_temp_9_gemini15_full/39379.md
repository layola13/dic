conjunctiva:/kənˈdʒʌŋktɪvə/| n. |结膜|The conjunctiva is a thin, transparent membrane that lines the inside of the eyelids and covers the sclera (the white part of the eye). It protects the eye from foreign bodies and helps to keep it moist.  |Example:  The doctor examined the patient's conjunctiva for signs of infection.  医生检查了病人的结膜是否有感染迹象。

The inflammation of the conjunctiva is called conjunctivitis, also known as pinkeye.  结膜发炎被称为结膜炎，也称为红眼病。|近义词：None (due to its specific anatomical location)|反义词：None (no direct opposite)|词性：名词
