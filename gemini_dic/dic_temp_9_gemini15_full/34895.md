clipt:/klɪpt/| v. |剪短；修剪；|To cut something, usually with scissors or shears.|He clipped the hedges.|近义词：trim, shear, snip|反义词：lengthen


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He clips his fingernails every week. = 他每周修剪指甲。
She clips coupons from the newspaper. = 她从报纸上剪下优惠券。

一般过去时:
I clipped the article from the magazine. = 我从杂志上剪下了这篇文章。
The gardener clipped the rose bushes. = 园丁修剪了玫瑰花丛。

现在完成时:
She has clipped all the pictures from the album. = 她已经把相册里的所有照片都剪下来了。
He has clipped the grass this morning. = 他今天早上修剪了草坪。

过去完成时:
I had clipped the coupons before I went to the store. = 我去商店之前已经剪好了优惠券。
She had clipped the article before she read it. = 她阅读文章之前已经剪下来了。

将来时:
I will clip the pictures for my scrapbook. = 我会剪下照片做成剪贴簿。
He will clip the overgrown branches. = 他会剪掉那些长得过长的树枝。

虚拟语气:
If I had clipped the coupon, I would have saved some money. = 如果我剪了优惠券，我就能省些钱。
I wish I had clipped that interesting article. = 我真希望我剪下了那篇有趣的文章。

被动语态:
The hedges were clipped yesterday. = 树篱昨天被修剪了。
The coupons were clipped by my mother. = 优惠券是我母亲剪的。

疑问句:
Did you clip the article from the newspaper? = 你从报纸上剪下那篇文章了吗？
Have you ever clipped your own hair? = 你曾经自己剪过头发吗？

否定句:
I didn't clip the picture. = 我没有剪下那张照片。
She hasn't clipped her toenails yet. = 她还没剪脚指甲。
