scottie:/ˈskɒti/| n. |苏格兰梗犬；苏格兰人（昵称）|A small, rough-coated terrier dog of Scottish origin, characterized by its short legs and sturdy build.  It's also a nickname for a Scotsman.|例句：He owns a lovely Scottie with a distinctive black and tan coat. 他拥有一只可爱的黑褐相间苏格兰梗犬。|例句：My grandfather, a true Scottie, loved to play the bagpipes. 我的祖父，一个地道的苏格兰人，喜欢吹奏风笛。|近义词：Scottish terrier, Scot|反义词：无
