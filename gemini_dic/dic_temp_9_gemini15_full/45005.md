crystallomancy:/ˌkrɪstəlˈmænsi/| n. |水晶占卜|Divination using crystals; a method of foretelling the future by interpreting the patterns or images seen in a crystal ball or other crystal.  Crystallomancy is often associated with scrying, a broader term encompassing divination through reflective surfaces.|Example:  She practiced crystallomancy, gazing into the crystal ball for visions of the future. 她练习水晶占卜，凝视着水晶球以寻找未来的景象。|近义词：scrying, divination, crystal gazing|反义词：None specifically, but could be considered opposite of rational prediction or skepticism.|名词
