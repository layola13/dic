dispersal: /dɪˈspɜːrsəl/ | n. | 散布；分散；传播 | The spreading or scattering of something over a wide area.  |例句：The dispersal of seeds by wind is a common phenomenon. 风力传播种子是一种常见的现象。

例句：The dispersal of the crowd after the concert was orderly. 音乐会结束后人群井然有序地散去。


近义词：scattering, dissemination, distribution, spread | 反义词：concentration, gathering, accumulation | 词性：名词
