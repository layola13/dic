grows:/ɡroʊz/| /ɡrəʊz/| v.|生长；增长；变得；发展|To increase in size or amount gradually.|例句：The plant grows taller each day. 这棵植物每天都长得更高。|例句：Her business is growing rapidly. 她的生意发展迅速。|近义词：develop, expand, increase|反义词：decrease, shrink, diminish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tree grows tall. = 这棵树长得高。
The company grows steadily. = 公司稳步发展。


一般过去时:
The plant grew quickly last summer. = 去年夏天这棵植物长得很快。
He grew taller than his brother. = 他比他哥哥长得高。


现在完成时:
The child has grown significantly. = 这个孩子长得显著。
The business has grown since last year. = 自去年以来，生意已经增长了。


过去完成时:
The tree had grown very tall before the storm. = 在暴风雨来临之前，这棵树已经长得非常高了。
She had grown accustomed to the noise. = 她已经习惯了噪音。


将来时:
The city will grow larger in the future. = 未来这座城市将会更大。
The population will grow by 10%. = 人口将增长 10%。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would grow vegetables. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会种植蔬菜。
I wish I grew taller. = 我希望我长得更高。


被动语态:
The crops are grown in the fields. = 庄稼在田里种植。
The company's profits are grown steadily. = 公司的利润稳步增长。


疑问句:
Does the flower grow well? = 这朵花长得好吗？
How quickly does the plant grow? = 这棵植物长得有多快？


否定句:
The plant doesn't grow well in this soil. = 这棵植物在这种土壤里长得不好。
The company isn't growing as fast as expected. = 公司的增长速度不如预期快。


PHRASE->
grow up = 长大成人
grow out of = 脱离；长大到不再需要
grow into = 逐渐适应；渐渐长大到适合
grow on sb = 越来越喜欢
grow old = 变老
