nitrated:/ˈnaɪtreɪtɪd/ | adj. |硝酸化的；含硝酸的 | Treated with nitric acid or containing a nitrate group.  Nitrated compounds are often used in explosives and fertilizers. | 例句：The nitrated cellulose was used to make the guncotton.  硝化纤维素被用来制造枪棉。

The nitrated aromatic compounds are highly reactive. 硝化芳香族化合物具有很高的反应活性。


近义词：nitrified (in some contexts)


反义词：denitrated


词性：形容词
