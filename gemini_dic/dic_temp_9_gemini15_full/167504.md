previewers:/prɪˈvjuːərz/| n.|预览者，预告片观看者|People who watch previews or trailers before a movie or event.  Those who assess something before its official release.|例句：The previewers gave the film overwhelmingly positive reviews. 预览者对这部电影给予了压倒性的好评。

例句：The marketing team showed the previewers an early cut of the advertisement. 营销团队向预览者展示了广告的早期版本。
