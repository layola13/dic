monecian:/məˈniːʃən/| adj.| 单性别的；雌雄同体的 |  Relating to or characterized by having both male and female reproductive organs in a single organism.  The term is primarily used in botany and zoology to describe plants and animals that possess both male and female reproductive structures.  | Example:  Some flowering plants are monecian, meaning they have separate male and female flowers on the same plant.  一些开花植物是单性别的，这意味着它们在同一植株上具有单独的雄花和雌花。| 近义词：hermaphroditic, bisexual | 反义词：dioecious, unisexual |  形容词
