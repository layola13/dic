deciles:/ˈdesʌlz/| n. |十分位数，十分位 |Deciles are the values that divide a dataset into ten equal parts.  Each decile represents 10% of the data. For example, the first decile (D1) is the value below which 10% of the data falls, the second decile (D2) is the value below which 20% of the data falls, and so on. Deciles are often used in descriptive statistics to summarize and interpret data distributions. |The survey revealed that the 9th decile of income was $150,000.  这项调查显示，收入的第九十分位数为15万美元。| percentiles, quartiles |  | noun
