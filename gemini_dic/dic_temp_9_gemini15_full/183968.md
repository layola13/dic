reshapes:/ˌriːˈʃeɪp/| vt.|重塑；改变…的形状；彻底改变|To give a new form or shape to something; to fundamentally change the nature or appearance of something.|The sculptor reshaped the clay into a bird.  雕塑家将粘土重塑成一只鸟。|The experience reshaped his outlook on life.  这段经历彻底改变了他的人生观。|reform, transform, remodel|destroy, maintain, preserve|动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist reshapes the clay daily. = 艺术家每天都在重塑粘土。
She reshapes her business strategy every year. = 她每年都会重塑她的商业策略。

He reshaped the garden after the storm. = 暴风雨后，他重新设计了花园。
