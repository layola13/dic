reddest:/ˈrɛdəst/ | adj. | 最红的，最红色的 |  The superlative form of "red," indicating the highest degree of redness. It describes something that is more red than anything else being compared.  | Example:  The reddest apple was the sweetest.  最红的苹果最甜。 |近义词：deepest red, most scarlet | 反义词：palest, whitest
