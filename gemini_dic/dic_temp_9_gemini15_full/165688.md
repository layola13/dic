premorbidly:/ˌpriːˈmɔːrbɪdli/ | adv. |患病前地，病前地 | existing or occurring before the onset of a disease or disorder;  describing a state or characteristic before the manifestation of illness.  |例句：He exhibited premorbidly high levels of anxiety. 他在患病前就表现出高度焦虑。


例句：Her premorbid personality was quite outgoing and sociable. 她患病前的性格非常外向和合群。


近义词：antecedent, prior, previous


反义词：postmorbid


词性: 副词
