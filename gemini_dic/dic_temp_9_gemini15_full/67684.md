exuberant:/ɪɡˈzjuːbərənt/ | adj. | 兴高采烈的；充满活力的；繁茂的 |  Characterized by or showing great energy and enthusiasm; overflowing with life, vigor, or abundance.  |例句：The children were exuberant after receiving their presents. 孩子们收到礼物后兴高采烈。

例句：The exuberant growth of the plants was remarkable. 植物的繁茂生长令人瞩目。


近义词：enthusiastic, energetic, lively, vigorous, abundant, overflowing

反义词：depressed, lethargic, subdued, apathetic, sparse, meager


词源:

The word "exuberant" comes from the Latin word "exuberare," which means "to overflow" or "to abound."  The prefix "ex-" means "out of" or "from," and "uber" means "fruitful" or "plentiful." Thus, the word literally means "overflowing with abundance."
