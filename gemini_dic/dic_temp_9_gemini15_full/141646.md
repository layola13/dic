optimist:/ˈɒptɪmɪst/| /ˈɑːptɪmɪst/ | n. | 乐观主义者 | A person who tends to be hopeful and confident about the future or the success of something.  | He's a true optimist, always looking on the bright side. 他是个真正的乐观主义者，总是往好处想。| pessimist, idealist | pessimist, cynic | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is an optimist. = 他是个乐观主义者。
She remains an optimist despite the challenges. = 尽管面临挑战，她仍然保持乐观。


一般过去时:
He was an optimist in his youth. = 他年轻的时候是个乐观主义者。
She was an optimist, even in difficult times. = 她即使在困难时期也是个乐观主义者。


现在完成时:
He has always been an optimist. = 他一直都是个乐观主义者。
She has remained an optimist throughout her life. = 她一生都保持乐观。


过去完成时:
He had been an optimist before the setback. = 在遭遇挫折之前，他一直是个乐观主义者。
She had always been an optimist until that incident. = 直到那件事发生之前，她一直都是个乐观主义者。


将来时:
He will likely remain an optimist. = 他很可能仍然会保持乐观。
She will be an optimist, no matter what happens. = 无论发生什么，她都会保持乐观。


虚拟语气:
If I were an optimist, I would approach the problem differently. = 如果我是个乐观主义者，我会以不同的方式处理这个问题。
I wish I were more of an optimist. = 我希望我更乐观一些。


被动语态:
(This word is rarely used passively)  He was considered an optimist by his friends. = 他的朋友们认为他是个乐观主义者。
(This word is rarely used passively)  She wasn't seen as an optimist by her colleagues. = 她的同事们不把她看作乐观主义者。


疑问句:
Is he an optimist? = 他是个乐观主义者吗？
Are you an optimist or a pessimist? = 你是乐观主义者还是悲观主义者？


否定句:
He is not an optimist. = 他不是个乐观主义者。
She isn't always an optimist. = 她并不总是乐观。


PHRASE->
optimistic outlook = 乐观的前景
cautious optimist =谨慎的乐观主义者
remain optimistic = 保持乐观
optimistic about the future = 对未来充满乐观
an unwavering optimist = 一个坚定不移的乐观主义者


词源:
From Latin *optimus ("best") + -ist.  The word reflects the belief that the best outcome is most likely.
