skims:/skɪmz/| vt. n.|略读；浏览；擦过水面；撇去（液体表面的东西）|to read something quickly in order to get a general understanding of it; to move lightly or quickly over a surface; to remove something from the surface of a liquid|例句：I skimmed the article to see if it was relevant. 我浏览了这篇文章，看看它是否相关。
She skimmed a stone across the lake. 她将一块石头掠过湖面。

例句：He skimmed the cream from the top of the milk. 他撇去了牛奶表面的奶油。|近义词：scan, browse, glance, skim over|反义词：read thoroughly, study carefully|动词


USAGE->

She skims the surface of the water with her hand. = 她用手掠过水面。
