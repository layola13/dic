qualifies:/ˈkwɑːlɪfaɪz/| v.|使合格；具有…的资格；证明…合格|To meet the necessary requirements or standards; to be or become suitable or eligible.|例句：He qualifies for the job because he has the necessary experience. 他有必要的经验，所以符合这份工作的资格。|例句：Her excellent grades qualify her for a scholarship.  她优秀的成绩使她有资格获得奖学金。|近义词：entitles, authorizes, empowers|反义词：disqualifies, debars


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He qualifies for the scholarship. = 他有资格获得奖学金。
The candidate qualifies for the position. = 候选人符合该职位的资格。


一般过去时:
She qualified for the Olympics last year. = 她去年获得了奥运会的参赛资格。
The design qualified for the competition. = 该设计符合比赛的资格。


现在完成时:
He has qualified for the next round. = 他已经获得了下一轮的参赛资格。
She has qualified as a doctor. = 她已经取得了医生的资格。


过去完成时:
He had qualified for the race before his injury. = 他在受伤之前已经获得了比赛的资格。
They had qualified for the finals before the last match. = 他们最后一场比赛之前就已经获得了决赛资格。


将来时:
She will qualify for the promotion next year. = 她明年将获得晋升资格。
He will qualify as a lawyer soon. = 他很快就会成为一名律师。


虚拟语气:
If he studied harder, he would qualify for the team. = 如果他学习更努力，他就能进入球队。
I wish I qualified for the grant. = 我希望我有资格获得这笔拨款。


被动语态:
He was qualified for the post. = 他符合该职位的资格。
The application was qualified for review. = 该申请符合审查条件。


疑问句:
Does he qualify for the discount? = 他有资格享受折扣吗？
Do you qualify for the loan? = 你有资格申请贷款吗？


否定句:
He doesn't qualify for the program. = 他不符合该项目的资格。
She didn't qualify for the finals. = 她没有获得决赛资格。


PHRASE->
