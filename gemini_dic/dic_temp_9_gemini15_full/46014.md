cybernetics:/ˌsaɪbərˈnɛtɪks/| n. |控制论|The science of communication and control theory that is applied to the study of regulatory systems in living organisms and machines.|Cybernetics explores the common principles of communication and control in both biological and artificial systems.  |近义词：control theory, systems theory|反义词：无
