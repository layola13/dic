palisaded:/ˌpælɪˈseɪdɪd/| adj. |设有栅栏的；用栅栏围起来的|Having palisades; enclosed or protected by a palisade.  |The village was palisaded for protection against invaders. 这个村庄用栅栏围起来以防入侵者。|近义词：fenced, barricaded, fortified|反义词：open, unprotected


USAGE->

The garden is palisaded to keep animals out. = 花园设有栅栏以防动物进入。
The old town is palisaded on all sides. = 古镇四面都设有栅栏。
