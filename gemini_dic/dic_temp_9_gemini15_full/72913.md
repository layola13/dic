fomo:/ˈfoʊmoʊ/ | n. |  害怕错过 | Fear of missing out; anxiety that an exciting or interesting event may currently be happening elsewhere, often aroused by posts seen on social media.  |  例句：I'm experiencing serious FOMO because everyone's at the concert and I'm stuck at home.  我正在经历严重的害怕错过，因为每个人都在音乐会上，而我却被困在家里。  | 近义词：envy, jealousy, anxiety | 反义词：contentment, satisfaction | 名词
