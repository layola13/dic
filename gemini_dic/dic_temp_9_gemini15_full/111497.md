loafing:/ˈloʊfɪŋ/| v.|闲逛；懒散；游手好闲|The act of spending time idly or lazily;  doing nothing productive.|例句：He spent the afternoon loafing around the park. 他在公园里闲逛了一下午。|例句：Stop loafing and get to work! 别偷懒，赶紧工作！|近义词：idling, idling away time, lounging, lazing|反义词：working, toiling, bustling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He loafs around all day. = 他整天闲逛。
They loaf at the cafe every afternoon. = 他们每天下午都在咖啡馆里闲逛。

一般过去时:
I loafed around the house yesterday. = 我昨天在家里闲逛。
They loafed by the pool all day. = 他们整天在泳池边懒洋洋地躺着。

现在完成时:
He has been loafing around since he lost his job. = 自从失业以来，他一直在闲逛。
We have loafed away the whole summer. = 我们整个夏天都在懒散地度过。

过去完成时:
They had loafed around for hours before they finally decided to do something. = 他们在最终决定做点事情之前已经闲逛了几个小时。
She had loafed all morning before she started her assignment. = 在开始作业之前，她整个上午都在偷懒。

将来时:
I will loaf around tomorrow if the weather is nice. = 如果天气好，我明天会去闲逛。
They will loaf on the beach all day. = 他们会一整天都在海滩上懒洋洋地躺着。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would loaf around more often. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更经常地去闲逛。
I wish I could loaf around all day. = 我真希望我能整天闲逛。

被动语态: (loafing本身不常用于被动语态)
Time was loafed away. = 时间被浪费掉了。(较为不自然)
The afternoon was spent loafing. = 下午的时间用来闲逛了。(较为自然)


疑问句:
Are you just loafing around? = 你只是在闲逛吗？
What are you doing, just loafing? = 你在干什么，只是在闲逛吗？

否定句:
He doesn't loaf around much anymore. = 他不再经常闲逛了。
They didn't loaf around all day; they worked hard. = 他们并没有整天闲逛；他们努力工作了。


PHRASE->
loaf around = 闲逛
loaf about = 闲逛
loafing about = 闲逛
loafing around = 闲逛
to loaf it = 过着懒散的生活
