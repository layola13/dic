plastering: /ˈplæstərɪŋ/ | n. v. | 灰泥抹灰；粉刷 | The action or process of applying plaster to a surface.  |例句：The plastering of the walls is almost complete. 墙壁的粉刷工作几乎完成了。The uneven plastering needs to be redone. 不平整的灰泥需要重做。 | 近义词：rendering, pargetting | 反义词：removing plaster, stripping


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plasters the walls every day. 他每天粉刷墙壁。
She is plastering the ceiling. 她在粉刷天花板。


一般过去时:
They plastered the house last week. 他们上周粉刷了房子。
He plastered the crack in the wall. 他粉刷了墙上的裂缝。


现在完成时:
We have plastered the entire room. 我们已经粉刷了整个房间。
She has been plastering since morning. 她从早上就开始粉刷了。


过去完成时:
They had plastered the walls before painting them. 他们在粉刷墙壁之前已经粉刷好了。
The workers had plastered the ceiling before we arrived. 我们到达之前工人们已经粉刷好了天花板。


将来时:
I will plaster the kitchen tomorrow. 我明天将粉刷厨房。
They will be plastering the bathroom next week. 他们下周将粉刷浴室。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would plaster the walls myself. 如果我有时间，我会自己粉刷墙壁。

被动语态:
