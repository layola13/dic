levation:/lɛˈveɪʃən/| n. |抬高；海拔；高度；提升|The act of raising or lifting something to a higher level; the height of a place above sea level; elevation in social status or importance.|例句：The elevation of the bridge is quite high.  桥的海拔相当高。

例句：The elevation of the new CEO brought renewed optimism to the company. 新任CEO的提升给公司带来了新的乐观情绪。


近义词：height, altitude, uplift, promotion|反义词：depression, lowering, degradation|词性：名词
