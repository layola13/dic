mercaptan:/mərˈkæptæn/ | n. |硫醇；硫醇类化合物 |A mercaptan is an organic compound containing a sulfhydryl group (-SH).  They are also known as thiols.  They have a characteristic strong, unpleasant odor, often described as resembling rotten eggs.  Mercaptans are used in various applications, including the production of pesticides, pharmaceuticals, and as odorants in natural gas. | Example:  The strong smell of mercaptan indicated a gas leak.  硫醇的强烈气味表明有煤气泄漏。  The lab technician carefully handled the mercaptans due to their toxicity. 实验室技术人员小心地处理硫醇，因为它们有毒性。 |近义词:thiol |反义词: N/A
