geotechnology:/ˌdʒiːoʊtɛkˈnɑːlədʒi/ | n. | 地球技术；地质技术 | The application of technology to the study and exploitation of the Earth's resources.  This field encompasses a wide range of disciplines, including geology, geophysics, engineering, and computer science, to solve problems related to the Earth. | Example: Geotechnology plays a crucial role in the exploration and extraction of oil and gas.  地球技术在地油气勘探和开采中发挥着至关重要的作用。|近义词:geoscience, earth science |反义词: N/A | 名词
