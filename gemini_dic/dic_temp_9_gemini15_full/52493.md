diets:/ˈdaɪɛts/ | n. | 节食；饮食；规定食物 | A diet is a plan of what you eat and drink regularly.  It can refer to a specific eating plan for weight loss, health improvement, or managing a medical condition.  It can also refer to the food and drink consumed by a person or group. |  例句：She's on a strict diet to lose weight. 她正在严格节食以减肥。  |  例句：The Mediterranean diet is known for its health benefits. 地中海饮食以其健康益处而闻名。 | 近义词：regimen, food plan, eating habits | 反义词：gluttony, overeating | 名词
