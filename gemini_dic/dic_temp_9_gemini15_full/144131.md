outsearched:/ˌaʊtˈsɜːrtʃt/| v. |超过…在搜索中| To surpass someone or something in a search or investigation; to be more successful in finding something than someone else. This is a relatively rare word, formed by combining "out-" (meaning "to a greater extent than") with "searched."  It implies a competitive search process. |例句：The detective outsearched all his colleagues, uncovering crucial evidence that led to the suspect's arrest.  侦探超过了他所有的同事，发现了导致嫌疑人被捕的关键证据。


例句：The research team outsearched their rivals, discovering a new species of plant. 研究小组超过了他们的竞争对手，发现了一种新的植物物种。
