pneumonia:/njuːˈmoʊniə/|/pnuːˈməʊniə/| n. |肺炎|Inflammation of the lungs caused by infection, typically bacterial or viral.|例句：The doctor diagnosed her with pneumonia. 医生诊断她患有肺炎。|近义词：lung infection, pneumonitis|反义词：lung health


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Pneumonia affects millions of people worldwide every year. = 肺炎每年影响全世界数百万人。
He has pneumonia and needs immediate treatment. = 他患有肺炎，需要立即治疗。


一般过去时:
She suffered from pneumonia last winter. = 她去年冬天患过肺炎。
My grandfather died from pneumonia. = 我祖父死于肺炎。


现在完成时:
He has had pneumonia twice in his life. = 他一生中患过两次肺炎。
She has recovered from pneumonia after a month of treatment. = 她经过一个月的治疗后已康复。


过去完成时:
Before the diagnosis, he had already experienced several pneumonia symptoms. = 在诊断之前，他已经经历了几种肺炎症状。
They had never encountered such a severe case of pneumonia before. = 他们以前从未遇到过如此严重的肺炎病例。


将来时:
Without treatment, his pneumonia will worsen. = 不治疗，他的肺炎会恶化。
Doctors predict that the pneumonia outbreak will peak next month. = 医生预测肺炎疫情将在下个月达到高峰。


虚拟语气:
If I had known about the pneumonia risk, I would have taken more precautions. = 如果我知道肺炎的风险，我会采取更多预防措施。
I wish I had not gotten pneumonia. = 我真希望我没有得肺炎。
