caucused:/ˈkɔːkəst/| v.|秘密会议；参与秘密会议；|To meet privately as a caucus.|The senators caucused to discuss the upcoming bill. 参议员们秘密会商即将通过的法案。|confer, consult, deliberate|公开辩论,公开讨论|verb


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They caucus regularly to plan their strategy.  = 他们定期举行秘密会议来制定策略。
The party members caucus before making a decision. = 党内成员在做决定之前会进行秘密会商。


一般过去时:
The committee caucused last night to finalize the report. = 委员会昨晚秘密会商，最终确定了报告。
The delegates caucused before the vote. = 代表们在投票前进行了秘密会商。


现在完成时:
They have caucused several times this week. = 他们本周已经秘密会商过几次了。
The group has caucused to agree on a common goal. = 该团体已经秘密会商以达成共同目标。


过去完成时:
They had caucused before the public announcement. = 他们在公开宣布之前已经秘密会商过了。
The team had caucused extensively before the competition. = 团队在比赛前进行了大量的秘密会商。


将来时:
They will caucus again next week to discuss the budget. = 他们下周将再次秘密会商讨论预算。
The representatives will caucus before the next session. = 代表们将在下一次会议之前进行秘密会商。


虚拟语气:
If they had caucused earlier, they might have reached a better agreement. = 如果他们早点秘密会商，他们可能会达成更好的协议。
I wish they had caucused before making such a hasty decision. = 我希望他们在做出如此仓促的决定之前能秘密会商一下。


被动语态:
The matter was caucused upon extensively before a decision was made. = 在做出决定之前，此事经过了广泛的秘密会商。
The issue has been caucused over for weeks. = 这个问题已经秘密会商了好几个星期了。


疑问句:
Did they caucus before the vote? = 他们在投票前秘密会商了吗？
Will they caucus tomorrow? = 他们明天会秘密会商吗？


否定句:
They did not caucus before making the announcement. = 他们在宣布之前没有秘密会商。
The group will not caucus until next week. = 该团体直到下周才会秘密会商。


PHRASE->
caucus meeting 秘密会议

caucus on sth 就某事秘密会商
hold a caucus 举行秘密会议
party caucus 党内秘密会议
