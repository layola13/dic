romanize:/ˈroʊmənaɪz/ /ˈrəʊmənʌɪz/ | vt. | 罗马化；用罗马字母拼写 | To represent (a word, phrase, or text) using the Roman alphabet, especially as a transliteration of a word or text written in another script.  |  They romanized the Chinese characters for better understanding. 他们用罗马字母拼写汉字以便更好地理解。 The author romanized the Arabic title of the book. 作者用罗马字母拼写了这本书的阿拉伯语书名。 | transliterate, transcribe |  (反义词较模糊，可以理解为使用原文字母书写，但缺乏一个确切的单字)
