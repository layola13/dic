overmarch:/ˈoʊvərmɑːrtʃ/ | v. |越境行军，入侵 |To march over or across (a boundary or territory); to invade.  |例句：The army overmarched the border into enemy territory.  军队越过边界进入敌方领土。 |例句：The rebels overmarched the defensive lines.  叛军越过了防线。|近义词：invade, overrun, trespass |反义词：retreat, withdraw


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army overmarches the border. = 军队越过边界。
They overmarch the enemy lines. = 他们越过敌人的防线。


一般过去时:
The troops overmarched the river. = 部队越过了河流。
The invaders overmarched the kingdom. = 侵略者入侵了王国。


现在完成时:
The soldiers have overmarched the mountains. = 士兵们已经越过了山脉。
They have overmarched the designated area. = 他们已经越过了指定区域。


过去完成时:
The enemy had overmarched our defenses before we realized it. =  在我们意识到之前，敌人已经越过了我们的防线。
The rebels had overmarched the city by nightfall. = 到夜幕降临的时候，叛军已经占领了这座城市。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
