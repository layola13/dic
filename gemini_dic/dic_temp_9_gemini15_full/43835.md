crepance:/krɛˈpɑːns/| n.|冰裂隙，雪裂缝|A crevasse is a deep crack, or fracture, found in an ice sheet or glacier.  These fissures can be quite dangerous, as they can be hidden by snow bridges or be very difficult to see.|Example: The climbers carefully avoided the crevasse on the glacier.|近义词：fissure, crack, rift|反义词：solid surface, unbroken ice
