deaconed:/ˈdiːkənd/| v.|被任命为执事；被授予执事职位 |To ordain or appoint as a deacon.  The verb reflects the action of being appointed to the office of a deacon within a religious context, usually Christian. |Example: He was deaconed at the age of 25. 他25岁时被任命为执事。

The young man was deaconed last Sunday.  这个年轻人上周日被任命为执事。


近义词:ordained, appointed, consecrated

反义词:deposed, dismissed, removed
