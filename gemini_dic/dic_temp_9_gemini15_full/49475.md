delusion:/dɪˈluʒən/| n. |妄想；错觉；迷惑；欺骗 |A delusion is a false belief that is strongly held despite evidence to the contrary.  It is often a symptom of a mental illness. |例句：He suffered from delusions of grandeur, believing himself to be a powerful king. 他患有夸大妄想症，认为自己是一位强大的国王。


例句：Her delusion that she could fly led her to jump off a building. 她认为自己会飞的错觉让她从楼上跳了下去。


近义词：fantasy, illusion, hallucination, misconception |反义词：reality, truth, fact


词源：源自于中古英语 *delusio，来自古法语 *delusion，来自拉丁语 *de-（表示“离开”、“远离”）+ *ludere（“玩耍”、“欺骗”）。字面意思为“玩弄”、“欺骗”。
