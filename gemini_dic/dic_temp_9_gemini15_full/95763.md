inconstant:/ɪnˈkɒnstənt/ | adj. | 不稳定的；变化无常的；反复无常的 |  Unreliable; changeable; fickle.  The weather in this region is notoriously inconstant.  |  例句：His inconstant moods made it difficult to work with him.他的反复无常的情绪使和他一起工作变得困难。
|  例句：The inconstant nature of the stock market makes investing risky.股市的波动性使投资具有风险性。|近义词：fickle, changeable, erratic, volatile, unsteady |反义词：constant, steady, stable, reliable, dependable | 词性:形容词

词源:
The word "inconstant" comes from the Old French word "inconstant," which itself derives from the Latin "inconstans," combining the prefix "in-" (not) with "constans," meaning "standing firm" or "constant."
