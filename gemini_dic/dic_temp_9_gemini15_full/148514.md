oversufficiency:/ˌoʊvərsuˈfɪʃənsi/ | n. | 过剩；过量；过度充足 |  The state or quality of being oversufficient; an excessive or superfluous amount.  It implies having more than is needed or desired, often leading to waste or inefficiency. |  The oversufficiency of resources led to a decline in prices.  过剩的资源导致价格下跌。 The oversufficiency of food supplies resulted in significant waste.  过剩的食物供应导致了大量的浪费。 | 近义词：excess, surplus, superfluity, abundance | 反义词：scarcity, deficiency, shortage
