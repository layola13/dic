enquire:/ɪnˈkwaɪər/ | v. | 询问；打听；查问 | To ask for information | I will enquire about the price. 我会询问价格。 | ask, inquire, investigate |  ignore, neglect | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I enquire about the availability of the product. = 我询问产品的供应情况。
They regularly enquire about our services. = 他们经常询问我们的服务。


一般过去时:
She enquired about the job opening. = 她询问了职位空缺。
He enquired politely about the way to the station. = 他礼貌地询问去车站的路。


现在完成时:
I have enquired about the flight schedule. = 我已经询问了航班时刻表。
They have enquired about the possibility of a refund. = 他们已经询问了退款的可能性。


过去完成时:
She had enquired about the hotel before making a reservation. = 她在预订酒店之前已经询问过酒店的情况。
He had enquired about the history of the building. = 他已经询问过这座建筑的历史。


将来时:
I will enquire about the visa requirements. = 我将询问签证要求。
They will enquire about the delivery time. = 他们将询问交货时间。


虚拟语气:
If I were to enquire, I would want to know the truth. = 如果我要询问，我会想知道真相。
I wish I had enquired about the terms and conditions earlier. = 我希望我早点询问过条款和条件。


被动语态:
The matter was enquired into thoroughly. = 这件事被彻底调查了。
Information was enquired about regularly. = 信息被定期询问。


疑问句:
May I enquire about your plans? = 我可以询问一下你的计划吗？
Could you enquire about the delivery status for me? = 你能帮我询问一下交付状态吗？


否定句:
I did not enquire about the details. = 我没有询问细节。
They did not enquire about the price before purchasing. = 他们购买前没有询问价格。


PHRASE->
enquire after sb. = 询问某人的健康状况
enquire into sth. = 调查某事
enquire about sth. = 询问某事
make enquiries = 进行调查
enquire of sb. = 向某人询问
