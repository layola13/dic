ectozoon:/ˈɛktoʊzoʊn/| n. |外寄生虫|An ectozoon is an external parasite, an organism that lives on the outside of another organism (its host) and feeds on it.|Example: Ticks are ectozoa that feed on the blood of mammals.|近义词:external parasite, epizoan|反义词:endoparasite
