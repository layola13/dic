collocation's:/ˌkɒləˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |搭配，词语搭配|The study of collocations involves analyzing how words frequently appear together in natural language.  This helps understand the nuances of language and improve writing fluency.| Example: The collocations "make a decision" and "take a decision" are both grammatically correct, but have slightly different meanings and connotations.  |近义词：word combinations, phrases|反义词：None|名词
