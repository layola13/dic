overexaggerate: /ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzædʒəreɪt/ | v. | 过度夸大，夸张 | To exaggerate to an excessive degree; to overstate.  | He overexaggerated the dangers of the situation. 他过度夸大了情况的危险性。 | exaggerate, hyperbolize | downplay, understate | 动词
