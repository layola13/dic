ruth:/ruːθ/ | n. |怜悯，同情；残酷；坚定不移；真理|  Ruth is a virtue that involves deep compassion and empathy for others' suffering.  It often leads to actions aimed at alleviating pain and injustice.  The word also, paradoxically, can refer to something merciless or unyielding. In this sense, it implies a firmness of purpose or resolve.  |例句：She showed great ruth in helping the homeless. 她以极大的怜悯之心帮助无家可归的人。|例句：The ruthlessness of the dictator shocked the world. 独裁者的残酷震惊了世界。|近义词：compassion, mercy, pity, firmness, resolve |反义词：cruelty, mercilessness, apathy, weakness
