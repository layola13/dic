lascivious:/ləˈsɪvɪəs/| adj.|淫荡的；好色的；挑逗的|Suggestive of or tending to excite sexual desire; lustful.  The word implies a strong element of impropriety and often a lack of restraint.|例句：His lascivious gaze made her uncomfortable.  他的淫荡目光让她感到不舒服。

例句：The novel was criticized for its lascivious descriptions. 这部小说因其淫荡的描写而受到批评。


近义词：lecherous, lustful, libidinous, wanton, prurient|反义词：chaste, pure, innocent, virtuous|词性：形容词
