defibrillator:/dɪˈfɪbrɪleɪtər/ | n. | 除颤器 | A medical device used to treat life-threatening cardiac arrhythmias (irregular heartbeats) by delivering an electric shock to the heart.  It helps to restore the heart's normal rhythm. | The paramedics used a defibrillator to restart the patient's heart.  急救人员用除颤器使病人的心脏重新跳动。 | cardioverter, AED (automated external defibrillator) |  | noun
