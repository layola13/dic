recomplicate:/ˌriːˈkɑːmplɪkeɪt/| vt.|使再次复杂化；使更复杂|To make something complicated again, often after it has been simplified or made easier.|例句：The new regulations recomplicated the tax system, causing widespread confusion. 新规使税制再次复杂化，导致了广泛的混乱。

例句：Don't recomplicate the issue by introducing unnecessary details. 不要通过引入不必要的细节来使问题再次复杂化。


近义词：intricate, complicate, embroil, confuse |反义词：simplify, clarify, streamline
