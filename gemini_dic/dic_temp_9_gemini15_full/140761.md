omnivorism:/ˈɒmnɪvɔrɪzəm/| n. |杂食性；杂食动物的习性|The practice or characteristic of eating both plants and animals.|Human omnivorism has shaped our evolution and culture.|近义词：omnivorousness |反义词：herbivorism, carnivorism
