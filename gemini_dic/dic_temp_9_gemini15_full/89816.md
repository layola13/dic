hornbook:/ˈhɔːrnˌbʊk/ | n. |初级读本；儿童识字板 | A hornbook was a child's primer, consisting of a single printed sheet of hornbook lessons, mounted on a wooden paddle and protected by a transparent sheet of horn.  It was a common teaching tool in the 16th-18th centuries. | Example:  The teacher used a hornbook to teach the alphabet to her young students.  = 老师用初级读本教她的学生认字母。 |近义词: primer, alphabet book | 反义词: advanced textbook
