documentarians:/ˌdɒkjuːmənˈteəriənz/ | n. |纪录片制作人 |People who make documentaries.  They are involved in all aspects of documentary filmmaking, from conceiving the idea to filming, editing, and distributing the final product. |Example:  Many talented documentarians are working on important films about climate change.  许多有才华的纪录片制作人正在制作关于气候变化的重要影片。|近义词：filmmakers, documentary producers |反义词：fiction writers (in the context of film making)
