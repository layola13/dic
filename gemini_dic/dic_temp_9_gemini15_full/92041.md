hyperphalangia: /ˌhaɪpərˌfælænˈdʒiə/ | n. |多指（畸形）|A congenital anomaly characterized by an excessive number of phalanges (bones of the fingers or toes).  It can affect one or more digits and vary in severity. The extra phalanges can be rudimentary or fully formed. |Example: The child was born with hyperphalangia in his right thumb, resulting in an extra joint.|近义词:polydactyly (if the extra digits are fully formed), extra digit|反义词:oligodactyly (missing digits)
