pupivorous: /pjuːˈpɪvərəs/ | adj. | 食蛹的 |  Feeding on pupae (the stage of insect development between larva and adult).  The term describes animals or organisms that consume pupae as a primary or significant part of their diet.  | Example:  Many species of birds have pupivorous diets, especially during the breeding season.  许多鸟类，尤其是在繁殖季节，都有食蛹的饮食习惯。  | 近义词: insectivorous (食虫的), larva-eating (食幼虫的) | 反义词:  herbivorous (食草的), carnivorous (食肉的)
