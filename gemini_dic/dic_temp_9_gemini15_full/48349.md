decrypted:/dɪˈkrɪptɪd/ | adj. | 解密的；已解密的 |  Converted from a coded or encrypted form into an intelligible form.  | Example: The decrypted message revealed the spy's location.  解密的消息揭示了间谍的位置。 |近义词：decoded, deciphered | 反义词：encrypted, encoded
