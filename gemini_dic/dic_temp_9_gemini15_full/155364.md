pharmacosiderite:/ˌfɑːrməˈkoʊsaɪdəraɪt/| n. |药铁矿|A rare arsenate mineral, typically green or yellow-green in color, consisting of hydrous ferric arsenate. It is a secondary mineral found in oxidized ore deposits containing arsenic and iron.  It's a collector's mineral due to its beautiful crystal formations.|The pharmacosiderite sample exhibited vibrant green hues and distinct cubic crystals.|近义词：scorodite (similar chemical composition but different crystal structure)|反义词：无|名词
