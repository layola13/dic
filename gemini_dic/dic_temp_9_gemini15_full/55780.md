diversity:/daɪˈvɜːrsəti/ | n. |多样性，差异性 | The existence of a variety of different things or people, especially within a group or society.  |例句：The school boasts a great diversity of students from many different backgrounds. 这所学校拥有来自不同背景的大量学生，其学生群体非常多元化。


例句：Biological diversity is crucial for a healthy ecosystem. 生物多样性对于健康的生态系统至关重要。


近义词：variety, multiplicity, heterogeneity | 反义词：uniformity, homogeneity
