reaffronting:/ˌriːəˈfrʌntɪŋ/| v.|再次冒犯；再次对抗；再次挑战 |To offend or insult someone again; to confront or challenge someone again.|例句：His re-affronting of the judge led to a contempt of court charge.  他的再次冒犯法官导致了藐视法庭的指控。|例句：The company's re-affronting of its customers' concerns led to a major PR crisis. 公司再次无视顾客的担忧导致了重大的公关危机。|近义词：reproach, insult, defy, challenge|反义词：appease, conciliate, comply
