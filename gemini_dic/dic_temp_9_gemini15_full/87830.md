hid:/hɪd/| vt.|隐藏，遮盖|to put or keep something in a place where it cannot be seen or found|He hid the money under the floorboards. 他把钱藏在地板下。|She hid her face in her hands. 她用手捂着脸。|show, conceal, cover|reveal, uncover, expose|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He hides his feelings well. 他很擅长隐藏自己的感情。
They hide the evidence. 他们隐藏证据。


一般过去时:
She hid the present behind the sofa. 她把礼物藏在沙发后面。
The thief hid from the police. 小偷躲避警察。


现在完成时:
He has hid the letter from his wife. 他已将这封信藏起来没让妻子看到。
They have hidden the truth for years. 他们隐瞒真相多年了。


过去完成时:
She had hidden the key before she left. 她离开前已经把钥匙藏起来了。
They had hidden their feelings until now. 他们一直隐藏自己的感情直到现在。


将来时:
He will hide the truth from the authorities. 他将会向当局隐瞒真相。
She will hide the cookies from her brother. 她将会把饼干藏起来不让她的弟弟吃到。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have hid the evidence. 如果我知道的话，我就把证据藏起来了。
I wish I had hid my feelings better. 我希望我之前能更好地隐藏我的感情。


被动语态:
The evidence was hidden by someone. 证据被某人藏起来了。
The truth was hidden from the public. 真相被隐瞒了公众。


疑问句:
Where did he hide the money? 他把钱藏在哪里了？
Did she hide the letter? 她把信藏起来了吗？


否定句:
He didn't hide his disappointment. 他没有掩饰他的失望。
She didn't hide the truth from her husband. 她没有向她丈夫隐瞒真相。
