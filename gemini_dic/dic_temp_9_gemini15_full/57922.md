drillstock:/ˈdrɪlstɒk/| n.|钻杆；钻头杆|A drillstock is a long, strong rod or bar used to transmit power from a drilling machine to a drill bit, allowing for deeper drilling. It's essentially the shaft that connects the power source to the cutting tool.|例句：The drillstock was made of hardened steel to withstand the pressure of deep drilling. 钻杆由硬化钢制成，以承受深钻的压力。 The damaged drillstock needed to be replaced immediately.  损坏的钻杆需要立即更换。|近义词：drill rod, drill stem|反义词:|无
