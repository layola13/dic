combustible:/kəmˈbʌstəbl/ | adj. | 易燃的，可燃的 | Capable of catching fire and burning.  Something combustible is easily ignited and burns readily. | Example: Gasoline is a highly combustible substance.  汽油是一种高度易燃的物质。 The dry leaves are combustible and could easily start a fire. 干树叶易燃，很容易引发火灾。|近义词：flammable, inflammable, ignitable | 反义词：non-combustible, fireproof


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wood is combustible. = 这木材易燃。
This material is highly combustible. = 这种材料极易燃烧。

一般过去时:
The old building was highly combustible. = 那座旧建筑物极易燃烧。
The gas was combustible, and the leak caused a fire. = 煤气易燃，泄漏导致了火灾。

现在完成时:
The scientists have determined that the substance is combustible. = 科学家已经确定该物质易燃。
Many materials have proven to be combustible under certain conditions. = 许多材料在特定条件下已被证明易燃。

过去完成时:
They had known that the material was combustible before they started the experiment. = 他们在开始实验之前就知道该材料易燃。
The firefighters had determined that the building was already combustible before the fire started. = 消防员在火灾发生前已经确定该建筑物易燃。

将来时:
The new building materials will be non-combustible. = 新建材将是不燃的。(反义词作对比)
This fuel will be highly combustible. = 这种燃料将极易燃烧。

虚拟语气:
If the material were combustible, we would not use it near the flame. = 如果材料易燃，我们不会把它放在火焰附近。
If the substance were less combustible, the accident would not have happened. = 如果物质不易燃，事故就不会发生。

被动语态:
The fuel was labeled as combustible. = 燃料被贴上易燃的标签。
The building materials are being tested to ensure they are not combustible. = 建筑材料正在接受测试，以确保它们不易燃。

疑问句:
Is this material combustible? = 这种材料易燃吗？
Are those chemicals combustible? = 那些化学品易燃吗？

否定句:
The material is not combustible. = 这种材料不易燃。
That substance is not easily combustible. = 那物质不易燃。


PHRASE->
highly combustible = 高度易燃的
extremely combustible = 极易燃烧的
potentially combustible = 潜在易燃的
spontaneously combustible = 自燃的
combustible materials = 易燃材料

词源:
From Latin *combūrere "to burn completely," from com- "completely" + *ūrere "to burn."  The suffix "-ible" indicates capability or susceptibility.
