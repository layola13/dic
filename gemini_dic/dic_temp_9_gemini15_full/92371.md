hypochondriasm:/ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndrɪæzəm/ | n. | 疑病症；臆想病 |  The persistent belief that one is ill despite the absence of any medical evidence.  It's characterized by excessive worry about one's health and a tendency to interpret normal bodily sensations as signs of serious illness. |  He suffers from hypochondriasm and constantly worries about his health. 他患有疑病症，总是担心自己的健康。  She went to the doctor again today, another symptom of her hypochondriasm. 她今天又去看医生了，这是她疑病症的另一个症状。|近义词：health anxiety, illness anxiety disorder | 反义词：None (It's a condition, not a trait with a direct opposite) | 名词
