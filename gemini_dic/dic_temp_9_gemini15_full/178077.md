It can refer to the physical act of leaning back or to a structural slope or inclination.|例句：The recliner offered a comfortable reclination for reading.  躺椅提供了舒适的斜倚姿势方便阅读。

The gentle reclination of the hills was picturesque.  山丘的缓和倾斜景色如画。

近义词：leaning, inclination, slope, recumbency|反义词：erection, upright position|词性：名词
