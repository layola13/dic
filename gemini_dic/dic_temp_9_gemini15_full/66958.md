experientially:/ɪkˌspɪəriˈɛnʃəli/| adv. |经验上地，凭经验地|Based on or relating to experience.  It describes something understood or learned through direct experience rather than theory or abstract knowledge.|例句：Experientially, I found the program very helpful.  经验告诉我，这个项目非常有帮助。
例句：He learned experientially the importance of hard work. 他通过亲身体验了解了努力工作的重要性。|近义词：empirically, practically|反义词：theoretically, hypothetically|副词
