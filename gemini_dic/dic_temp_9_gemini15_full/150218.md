pandarize:/ˈpændəraɪz/| v. |拉皮条; 卖淫; 为…拉皮条|To act as a pander; to procure.  The term often implies facilitating sexual encounters, but can also refer to catering to someone's desires or weaknesses in a generally exploitative way.|例句：He was arrested for pandering to underage girls. 他因为为未成年女孩拉皮条而被捕。

例句：The politician panders to the public's fear of immigrants to gain votes.  这位政治家迎合公众对移民的恐惧以争取选票。


近义词：procure, pimp, cater to, indulge, exploit |反义词：refrain, abstain, resist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He panders to the wealthy elite. = 他迎合富裕精英阶层的喜好。
She panders to her boss's every whim. = 她百依百顺地满足老板的每一个怪念头。


一般过去时:
The website pandered to users' desire for celebrity gossip. = 该网站迎合用户对名流八卦的渴望。
He pandered to the crowd's prejudices. = 他迎合了人群的偏见。


现在完成时:
The media has pandered to the public's thirst for sensational news. = 媒体一直迎合公众对耸人听闻新闻的渴望。
They have pandered to the demands of their shareholders for too long. = 他们长期以来一直迎合股东的要求。


过去完成时:
The government had pandered to the demands of special interest groups for years. = 政府多年来一直迎合特殊利益集团的要求。
He had pandered to her every whim before she left him. = 在她离开他之前，他一直百依百顺地满足她的每一个怪念头。


将来时:
The company will pander to the market's demand for cheaper products. = 公司将迎合市场对廉价产品的需求。
He will pander to the voters' desire for lower taxes. = 他将迎合选民降低税收的愿望。


虚拟语气:
If he pandered less to the public, he might have a better reputation. = 如果他不那么迎合公众，他的名声可能会更好。
I wish the media wouldn't pander to such sensationalism. = 我希望媒体不要迎合这种耸人听闻的新闻。


被动语态:
The needs of the customers are pandered to by the company. = 公司迎合客户的需求。
His desires were pandered to by his wealthy friends. = 他富有的朋友们满足了他的愿望。


疑问句:
Does the media pander to public opinion? = 媒体是否迎合公众舆论？
Does the government pander to big business? = 政府是否迎合大企业？


否定句:
The politician refused to pander to the voters' demands. = 这位政治家拒绝迎合选民的要求。
She would not pander to his unreasonable requests. = 她不会迎合他无理的要求。


PHRASE->
pander to someone's desires = 迎合某人的欲望
pander to the lowest instincts = 迎合最卑劣的本能
pander to the masses = 迎合大众
pander to public opinion = 迎合公众舆论
pander to the media = 迎合媒体


词源:
源于希腊神话中潘达罗斯(Pandarus)的名字，他是特洛伊战争中的人物，为帕里斯和海伦的私奔牵线搭桥。  后来这个词演变为指代那些从中获利而为他人提供性服务的中间人。
