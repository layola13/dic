dolichocephalous:/ˌdɒlɪkoʊˈsɛfələs/| adj.|长头型的，长颅的 |Having a long head; characterized by a head whose length is significantly greater than its breadth.  This is often due to premature closure of the sagittal suture in the skull.|The ancient Egyptian pharaohs were often depicted as having dolichocephalic skulls.|近义词：long-headed|反义词：brachycephalic, round-headed

* **dolicho-:** From Greek *dolichos* (δολικός), meaning "long."
* **-cephalous:** From Greek *kephalē* (κεφαλή), meaning "head."
