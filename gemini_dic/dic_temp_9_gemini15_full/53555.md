disbarment:/dɪsˈbɑːrmənt/ | n. |律师资格取消；除名 | The act of officially removing a lawyer's right to practice law.  This is usually due to serious misconduct or unethical behavior. | Example:  His disbarment was a result of his fraudulent activities.  他的律师资格被取消是因为他的欺诈行为。  The lawyer faced disbarment after being found guilty of perjury.  这位律师因被判犯有伪证罪而面临律师资格被取消。 | 近义词：expulsion, removal, suspension | 反义词：admission to the bar, reinstatement
