nonmicrobic: /ˌnɑːnmaɪˈkroʊbɪk/ | adj. | 非微生物的 | Not relating to or caused by microbes.  This adjective describes something that is free from microbes or not associated with microbial activity.  | Example:  The nonmicrobic environment of the clean room is crucial for the experiment.  =  洁净室的无菌环境对实验至关重要。


The sterile lab maintained a strictly nonmicrobic atmosphere. =  无菌实验室保持着严格的无微生物环境。

近义词: sterile, germ-free, aseptic

反义词: microbic, microbial


词源:  The word is a combination of "non-" (meaning "not") and "microbic" (relating to microbes). "Microbic" itself is derived from "microbe," which comes from the Greek words "mikros" (small) and "bios" (life).
