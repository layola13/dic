maunders:/ˈmɔːndərz/| v.|漫谈；含糊其辞地说；闲逛；徘徊|To talk in a rambling, inconsequential way; to wander aimlessly.|例句：He maunders on about his childhood. 他絮絮叨叨地谈论着他的童年。 He maunders through the streets. 他在街上漫无目的地闲逛。|近义词：ramble, wander, stray, ramble on|反义词：focus, concentrate
