self-inflation:/ˌsɛlfɪnˈfleɪʃən/| n. |自我膨胀；自我夸大；通货膨胀|The act or process of something increasing in size or importance, especially in a way that is excessive or unjustified.  It often refers to an inflated sense of self-importance or a situation where prices rise due to internal factors. |例句：His self-inflation was evident in his boastful pronouncements.  他的自我膨胀在他夸夸其谈的声明中显而易见。

His claims of success bordered on self-inflation.  他声称的成功近乎自我膨胀。


近义词：ego inflation, self-aggrandizement, overestimation,  (in economic context) demand-pull inflation


反义词：humility, modesty, deflation (in economic context)


词性：名词
