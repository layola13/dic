inthrone:/ɪnˈθroʊn/| vt.|使登基；拥立；推崇|to place or establish someone on a throne; to raise to a position of power or importance|例句：The people inthroned him as their king. 人民拥立他为他们的国王。|例句：The critics inthroned her as the greatest actress of her generation. 批评家们把她奉为她那一代最伟大的女演员。|近义词：enthrone, install, elevate|反义词：dethrone, depose
