nationalisms:/ˈnæʃənəlɪzəm/| n.|民族主义|The belief that one's own nation is superior to others and that national interests should be placed above all other considerations.  It often involves a strong sense of national identity and pride, and can manifest in various ways, from cultural celebrations to political movements.|例句：The rise of nationalisms in the 19th century led to many wars.  19世纪民族主义的兴起导致了许多战争。|例句：His nationalisms blinded him to the injustices suffered by other nations. 他的民族主义使他看不到其他国家遭受的不公正待遇。|近义词：patriotism, nationalism, jingoism|反义词：internationalism, globalism|名词
