downgraded:/ˈdaʊnˌɡreɪdɪd/ | adj. v. | 降级的；降低的；调低的 |  Reduced to a lower grade, rank, or status;  to lower the grade, rank, or status of something or someone. |


例句：

The hotel downgraded my room because of overbooking.  =  由于预订过度，酒店将我的房间降级了。

His job was downgraded after the company restructuring. = 公司重组后，他的职位被降级了。


近义词：degraded, demoted, reduced, lowered


反义词：upgraded, promoted, elevated, enhanced


词性：动词(过去分词作形容词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They downgrade his position every year. = 他们每年都降低他的职位。
The company downgrades its software regularly. = 公司定期对其软件进行降级处理。

一般过去时:
They downgraded his position last year. = 他们去年降低了他的职位。
The company downgraded its software last month. = 公司上个月对其软件进行了降级处理。

现在完成时:
They have downgraded his position several times. = 他们已经几次降低了他的职位。
The company has downgraded its software to fix a bug. = 公司已将其软件降级以修复错误。

过去完成时:
They had downgraded his position before he resigned. = 他辞职之前，他们已经降低了他的职位。
The company had downgraded its software before the release. = 公司在发布之前已将其软件降级。

将来时:
They will downgrade his position if he doesn't improve. = 如果他不改进，他们将降低他的职位。
The company will downgrade its software next year. = 公司明年将对其软件进行降级处理。

虚拟语气:
If they downgraded my room, I would complain. = 如果他们降级我的房间，我会投诉。
I wish they hadn't downgraded the software. = 我真希望他们没有降级软件。

被动语态:
His position was downgraded without warning. = 他的职位在没有警告的情况下被降级了。
The software was downgraded to address security concerns. = 软件已降级以解决安全问题。

疑问句:
Was his position downgraded? = 他的职位被降级了吗？
Has the software been downgraded recently? = 软件最近是否已降级？

否定句:
They didn't downgrade his position. = 他们没有降级他的职位。
The company hasn't downgraded its software yet. = 公司还没有降级其软件。
