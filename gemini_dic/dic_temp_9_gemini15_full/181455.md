rejigging:/ˌriːˈdʒɪɡɪŋ/| v.|重新调整；改组；修补|The act of reorganizing or readjusting something.  It often implies making improvements or alterations to a system, plan, or structure.|例句：We need to rejig the marketing strategy to reach a wider audience. 我们需要重新调整营销策略以覆盖更广泛的受众。

例句：The company is rejigging its management structure to improve efficiency.  公司正在改组其管理结构以提高效率。

近义词：reorganize, restructure, revamp, overhaul, readjust, modify

反义词：dismantle, destroy, leave unchanged


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rejig their schedule every week. = 他们每周都调整他们的日程安排。
The team is constantly rejigging the project plan. = 团队不断地调整项目计划。
