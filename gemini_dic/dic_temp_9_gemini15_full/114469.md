makings:/ˈmeɪkɪŋz/| n.|构成要素，材料，素质，迹象|The essential elements or ingredients from which something is made or can be made.  It can also refer to the inherent qualities or characteristics that suggest a particular outcome or potential.|例句：The makings of a great chef are evident in his culinary skills.  他具备成为一名优秀厨师的素质。
The dark clouds are the makings of a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨即将来临。|近义词：ingredients, components, elements, materials, signs, indications|反义词：destruction, ruin, undoing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The cake has all the makings of a delicious dessert. = 这蛋糕具备成为美味甜点的所有要素。
These early successes are the makings of a promising career. = 这些早期的成功预示着光明的事业。


一般过去时:
The makings of a great novel were apparent in her first draft. = 她初稿中显现出一部伟大小说所具备的素质。
His aggressive behavior showed the makings of a bully. = 他的侵略性行为显示了他是个欺凌者的迹象。


现在完成时:
The team's hard work has shown the makings of a championship-winning squad. = 球队的努力工作已经展现出夺冠球队的素质。
Years of dedication have produced the makings of a world-renowned expert. = 多年的奉献造就了一位世界知名的专家。


过去完成时:
The evidence showed the makings of a conspiracy before the investigation even began. = 在调查开始之前，证据就显示出阴谋的迹象。
By the time the play ended, the makings of a star were clear. = 到戏剧结束时，一位明星的素质已经很明显了。


将来时:
The current economic trends show the makings of a recession. = 当前的经济趋势显示出经济衰退的迹象。
With her talent and dedication, she has the makings of a successful entrepreneur. =凭借她的天赋和奉献精神，她有成为成功企业家的潜质。


虚拟语气:
If the ingredients were of better quality, they would have the makings of an amazing meal. = 如果原料质量更好，它们就能成为一顿美餐。
If he had shown more discipline, he could have had the makings of an Olympic athlete. = 如果他更有纪律性，他本可以成为一名奥运运动员。


被动语态:
The success of the project was attributed to the careful planning and the makings of a strong team. = 项目的成功归功于周密的计划和强大的团队。
The potential of the new technology is being shaped by the makings of a new industry. = 新技术的潜力正在被一个新兴产业所塑造。


疑问句:
What are the makings of a successful business? = 成功企业的要素是什么？
Do you see the makings of a storm in those dark clouds? = 你从那些乌云中看到暴风雨的迹象了吗？


否定句:
The project lacked the makings of a successful enterprise. = 这个项目缺乏成为成功企业的素质。
The ingredients did not have the makings of a delicious pie. = 这些原料做不出美味的馅饼。


PHRASE->
the makings of something:  …的要素/构成
have the makings of:  具备…的潜质/有成为…的可能
show the makings of: 显示出…的迹象
all the makings of:  完全具备…的条件
the raw makings of: …的原始材料/基础
