impedite:/ɪmˈpɛdɪt/| vt.|妨碍；阻止；阻碍|To hinder or obstruct the progress of.|The heavy snow impeded travel. 大雪阻碍了出行。|近义词：hinder, obstruct, hamper, block|反义词：facilitate, aid, assist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heavy traffic impedes my journey to work. = 交通拥堵阻碍了我上班的路程。
Loud noises impede concentration. = 嘈杂的噪音妨碍集中注意力。

一般过去时:
The storm impeded the rescue efforts. = 暴风雨阻碍了救援工作。
A broken leg impeded his progress in the race. = 断腿阻碍了他比赛的进程。

现在完成时:
The ongoing construction has impeded access to the building. = 正在进行的施工已经阻碍了进入大楼的通道。
Years of conflict have impeded the country's economic development. = 多年的冲突阻碍了该国的经济发展。

过去完成时:
A flat tire had impeded their journey earlier that day. = 之前一天爆胎已经阻碍了他们的旅程。
Technical problems had impeded the launch of the satellite. = 技术问题已经阻碍了卫星的发射。

将来时:
Bad weather will impede the flight schedule. = 恶劣天气将会阻碍航班时刻表。
Further delays will impede the project's completion. = 进一步的延误将会阻碍项目的完成。

虚拟语气:
If the rain hadn't impeded us, we would have arrived on time. = 如果雨没有阻碍我们，我们就会准时到达。
I wish that nothing would impede my progress. = 我希望没有任何东西会阻碍我的进步。

被动语态:
The project was impeded by a lack of funding. = 该项目因缺乏资金而受阻。
Our plans were impeded by unforeseen circumstances. = 我们的计划被不可预见的情况所阻碍。

疑问句:
What impeded your progress on the project? = 什么阻碍了你项目的进展？
Did anything impede your ability to complete the task? = 有什么东西阻碍了你完成任务的能力吗？

否定句:
Nothing impeded her determination to succeed. = 没有什么能阻碍她成功的决心。
The smooth road did not impede the car's speed. = 平坦的道路并没有阻碍汽车的速度。


PHRASE->

词源:
impedite 源自拉丁语impedire，意思是“缠住脚，妨碍”。  "im-" 是一个前缀，表示“进入”或“在...之中”；"ped-" 源自拉丁语pes，意思是“脚”。  因此，字面意思是“把脚缠住”，引申为“妨碍”，“阻止”。
