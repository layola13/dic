例句：The courtiers engaged in excessive minioning to please the king. = 朝臣们过度地逢迎拍马以讨好国王。


近义词：servility, subservience, fawning, toadying, bootlicking | 反义词：independence, defiance, rebellion, insubordination | 词性: 动词
