dueled:/ˈduːəld/| v.|决斗；竞争|To fight a duel; to engage in a contest or competition.|例句：The two men dueled at dawn.  = 两个人在黎明时分决斗。|例句：The companies dueled for market share. = 两家公司为了市场份额而竞争。|近义词：fought, battled, competed|反义词：cooperated, collaborated
