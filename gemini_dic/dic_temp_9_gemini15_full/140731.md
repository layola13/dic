omnipotency:/ɒmˈnɪpətənsi/| n. |全能；无所不能|The state of being omnipotent; unlimited power.  The belief in omnipotency can lead to arrogance and a disregard for others.  |例句：The god in this mythology possesses omnipotency and can alter the fabric of reality at will. = 这个神话中的神拥有全能的力量，可以随意改变现实的结构。

例句：Many people attribute omnipotency to their chosen deity. = 许多人将全能归于他们所选择的 deity。


近义词：almightiness, supreme power, absolute power|反义词：impotence, powerlessness, weakness
