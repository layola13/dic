requesting:/rɪˈkwɛstɪŋ/ | v. |请求；要求 | The act of asking for something politely or formally.  It implies a desire for something and a belief that the other party can fulfill the request. |例句：He is requesting a transfer to another department. 他正在请求调到另一个部门。

例句：The customer is requesting a refund.  顾客正在要求退款。


近义词：asking, soliciting, demanding, imploring | 反义词：giving, offering, providing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is requesting a new car. = 他正在请求一辆新车。
They are requesting additional information. = 他们正在请求额外信息。


一般过去时:
She requested a meeting with the manager. = 她请求与经理会面。
He requested a raise in his salary. = 他要求加薪。


现在完成时:
They have requested a response by the end of the week. = 他们要求在本周末前给予回复。
She has requested that we send her the documents. = 她要求我们把文件寄给她。


过去完成时:
He had requested the information before the meeting. = 他在会议前已请求过这些信息。
They had requested a change in the schedule. = 他们之前已要求更改日程安排。


将来时:
She will request a leave of absence. = 她将请求休假。
They will request more funding for the project. = 他们将要求为该项目提供更多资金。


虚拟语气:
I wish he would stop requesting my help. = 我希望他能停止请求我的帮助。
It is requested that you arrive on time. = 要求你准时到达。


被动语态:
A response was requested by the deadline. = 在截止日期前收到了回复请求。
Additional funding is being requested for the project. = 该项目正在申请额外资金。


疑问句:
Is he requesting a promotion? = 他是在请求升职吗？
Are they requesting a new contract? = 他们在请求一份新合同吗？


否定句:
He is not requesting a raise. = 他没有要求加薪。
They are not requesting any changes to the plan. = 他们没有要求对计划做任何更改。


PHRASE->
