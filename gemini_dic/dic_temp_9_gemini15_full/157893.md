plaintive:/ˈpleɪntɪv/| adj.| 悲伤的；哀怨的；可怜的 | Expressing sorrow or melancholy; mournful.  It often describes a sound or tone of voice. |例句：The plaintive cry of the seagull echoed across the empty beach. 海鸥悲伤的叫声在空旷的海滩上回荡。

例句：Her plaintive voice filled the room with sadness. 她哀怨的声音充满了整个房间。


近义词：sad, mournful, sorrowful, doleful, wistful | 反义词：joyful, cheerful, happy, merry


词源:  From Old French *plaintif, from plainte "complaint," from plaindre "to complain, lament." Ultimately from the Latin *plangēre "to strike, beat, lament."
