resurfacer:/ˌriːˈsɜːrfəsər/ | n. | 再浮现者；路面修复机 | A machine used to smooth and repair roads and other surfaces.  It typically uses heated asphalt or concrete to level and resurface the area. | The city council hired a resurfacer to fix the potholes on Main Street.  市议会雇佣了一台路面修复机来修补主街上的坑洞。| road repair machine, pavement finisher |  (无明显反义词，可考虑unpaver 或 destroyer 但语义不完全对等)| 名词
