change:/tʃeɪndʒ/ | v. n. |改变；变化；兑换；更换 | To make or become different.  | The weather changed suddenly. 天气突然变了。  The company is undergoing a major change. 公司正在经历重大变革。 | alter, modify, transform | remain, stay, continue | verb, noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price changes every day. = 价格每天都在变。
He changes his mind frequently. = 他经常改变主意。

一般过去时:
She changed her clothes before going out. = 她出门前换了衣服。
The situation changed dramatically. = 局势发生了戏剧性的变化。

现在完成时:
They have changed their plans. = 他们已经改变了计划。
Nothing has changed since I last saw you. = 自从我上次见到你以来，什么也没有改变。

过去完成时:
He had changed his mind before I could convince him. = 我还没来得及说服他，他就已经改变主意了。
The company had changed its strategy several times. = 公司已经几次改变了战略。

将来时:
The weather will change tomorrow. = 明天天气将会改变。
They will change the policy next year. = 他们明年将改变政策。

虚拟语气:
I wish I could change the past. = 我真希望我能改变过去。
If I were you, I would change my approach. = 如果我是你，我会改变我的方法。

被动语态:
The rules were changed last week. = 规则上周被更改了。
The date of the meeting has been changed. = 会议日期已被更改。

疑问句:
Has anything changed? = 有什么变化吗？
Did you change your address? = 你换地址了吗？

否定句:
Nothing will change without your effort. = 没有你的努力，什么都不会改变。
He didn't change his answer. = 他没有改变他的答案。


PHRASE->

undergo a change = 经历改变
bring about a change = 促成改变
