gladliest:/ˈɡlædliəst/| adv.|最高兴地，最乐意地|Most gladly; with the greatest pleasure.  This word is the superlative form of "gladly," intensifying the willingness or pleasure.|例句：He accepted the invitation gladliest of all. 他最乐意接受邀请了。
例句：She responded to the offer gladliest, her eyes shining with excitement. 她欣喜若狂地接受了这个提议，眼睛里闪着兴奋的光芒。|近义词：most willingly, most eagerly, most happily|反义词：reluctantly, unwillingly, grudgingly|副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and as such doesn't have tenses like verbs.  Its usage is always to modify a verb, expressing the degree of willingness or pleasure.  It doesn't participate in passive voice or other verb-related grammatical structures.


PHRASE->
This word doesn't have common phrases associated with it because of its infrequent use.  Its meaning is entirely contained within itself.
