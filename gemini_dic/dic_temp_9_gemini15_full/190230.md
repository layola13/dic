sapogenin:/ˌsæpəˈdʒɛnɪn/| n. |皂苷配基|A steroid or triterpenoid aglycone obtained by hydrolysis of a saponin.  Sapogenins are the non-sugar component of saponins, which are glycosides found in many plants. They have a variety of biological activities and are used in the production of steroidal hormones. |例句：Many sapogenins are used in the pharmaceutical industry to synthesize steroid hormones.  许多皂苷配基被用于制药工业合成类固醇激素。

例句：The researchers isolated several novel sapogenins from the plant extract. 研究人员从植物提取物中分离出几种新的皂苷配基。


近义词：aglycone (though not all aglycones are sapogenins)


反义词：N/A (no direct opposite)


词性: 名词
