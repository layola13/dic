discovering: /dɪˈskʌvərɪŋ/ | v. | 发现；发觉；揭露 | The act of finding something new or previously unknown, or of bringing something to light.  It implies a process of exploration and investigation. | 例句：She is discovering new talents. = 她正在发现新的才能。

She is discovering the beauty of nature. = 她正在发现大自然的美丽。


近义词：finding, uncovering, unearthing, detecting | 反义词：hiding, concealing, obscuring | 词性：动词(现在分词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are discovering new species in the Amazon. = 他们在亚马逊发现新的物种。
Scientists are discovering new cures for diseases. = 科学家们正在发现治疗疾病的新疗法。


一般过去时:
Columbus discovered America in 1492. = 哥伦布在1492年发现了美洲。
They discovered a hidden treasure in the old house. = 他们在老房子里发现了一笔隐藏的宝藏。


现在完成时:
We have discovered a new way to solve the problem. = 我们已经发现了一种解决这个问题的新方法。
They have discovered that smoking is harmful to health. = 他们已经发现吸烟有害健康。


过去完成时:
By the time they arrived, the police had already discovered the stolen car. = 当他们到达时，警察已经找到了被盗的汽车。
She had discovered her talent for painting before she moved to Paris. = 在她搬到巴黎之前，她就已经发现了自己绘画的天赋。


将来时:
We will discover many more planets in the future. = 我们将来会发现更多行星。
Scientists believe they will discover a cure for cancer soon. = 科学家们相信他们很快就会发现治愈癌症的方法。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would discover more about this topic. = 如果我有更多时间，我会对这个话题有更多了解。
I wish I had discovered this earlier. = 我真希望我早点发现这个。


被动语态:
Many new stars were discovered by the telescope. = 许多新的恒星被望远镜发现了。
The truth was discovered eventually. = 真相最终被发现了。


疑问句:
Have you discovered anything interesting today? = 你今天发现了什么有趣的事情吗？
What did they discover during their expedition? = 他们在探险中发现了什么？


否定句:
They didn't discover any evidence at the crime scene. = 他们在犯罪现场没有发现任何证据。
I haven't discovered the solution to this puzzle yet. = 我还没有找到这个谜题的答案。
