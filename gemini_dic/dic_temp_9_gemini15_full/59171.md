dysfunctional:/dɪsˈfʌŋkʃənəl/ | adj. | 功能失调的；有毛病的；不能正常运转的 | Not functioning properly; impaired or failing to function.  Describes a system, process, or relationship that is not working correctly.  |例句：The dysfunctional family struggled with constant conflict and unresolved issues.  这个功能失调的家庭饱受持续不断的冲突和未解决的问题的困扰。

The company's dysfunctional management led to a decline in productivity.  公司管理不善导致生产力下降。|近义词：malfunctioning, impaired, broken, flawed, ineffective |反义词：functional, efficient, effective, well-functioning


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine is dysfunctional. = 发动机功能失调。
Their relationship is dysfunctional. = 他们的关系不正常。

一般过去时:
The machine was dysfunctional yesterday. = 机器昨天坏了。
The team was dysfunctional during the project. = 团队在项目期间运作不佳。

现在完成时:
The system has been dysfunctional for weeks. = 系统已经失灵几周了。
Their marriage has been dysfunctional for years. = 他们的婚姻多年来一直不和谐。

过去完成时:
The software had been dysfunctional before the update. = 软件在更新之前就已经失灵了。
The family had been dysfunctional long before the divorce. = 家庭在离婚很久以前就已经功能失调了。

将来时:
The network will be dysfunctional if we don't fix the server. = 如果我们不修理服务器，网络将无法正常工作。
Their relationship will likely remain dysfunctional without professional help. = 如果没有专业人士的帮助，他们的关系很可能依然会不和谐。

虚拟语气:
If the system were dysfunctional, we would have to replace it. = 如果系统失灵，我们就必须更换它。
I wish my family weren't so dysfunctional. = 我希望我的家庭不要如此不和谐。

被动语态:
The problem was caused by a dysfunctional component. = 问题是由一个功能失调的部件引起的。
The project was delayed because of a dysfunctional team. = 项目由于团队运作不佳而被耽搁。

疑问句:
Is the equipment dysfunctional? = 设备失灵了吗？
Is their family dysfunctional? = 他们的家庭功能失调吗？

否定句:
The system is not dysfunctional. = 系统没有失灵。
Their relationship is not dysfunctional; they are very happy. = 他们的关系很正常，他们非常幸福。
