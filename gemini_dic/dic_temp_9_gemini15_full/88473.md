histopathologist:/ˌhɪstoʊpəˈθɑːlədʒɪst/ | n. |组织病理学家 | A histopathologist is a medical doctor specializing in the diagnosis of diseases through the examination of tissues. They analyze tissue samples under a microscope to identify abnormalities and assist in the diagnosis and treatment of various diseases.  |The histopathologist examined the biopsy sample and confirmed the diagnosis of cancer.  组织病理学家检查了活检样本，并确诊为癌症。|pathologist,  physician |  (无明显反义词) |名词
