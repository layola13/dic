misrulers:/ˈmɪsˌruːlər/| n.| 暴君；虐待统治者 | A misruler is a person who governs badly or unjustly, often characterized by tyranny, oppression, or corruption.  They abuse their power and disregard the well-being of their people. | Example: The misrulers were overthrown in a popular uprising.  暴君在民众起义中被推翻了。 |近义词：tyrant, despot, oppressor |反义词：benevolent ruler, just leader
