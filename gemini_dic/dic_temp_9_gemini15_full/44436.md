cross-refer:/ˈkrɒsˌrɛfər/| vt. |交叉引用；相互参照|To refer to or connect with another source of information for further detail or verification.|The report cross-references several other studies.  这份报告交叉引用了几项其他研究。|近义词：refer to, correlate, connect|反义词：disconnect, separate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cross-reference the data with the existing records. = 他们将数据与现有记录交叉引用。
The librarian cross-references the books by subject and author. = 图书管理员按主题和作者对书籍进行交叉引用。

一般过去时:
The researcher cross-referenced the information from two different sources. = 研究人员交叉引用了来自两个不同来源的信息。
She cross-referenced the citations to ensure accuracy. = 她交叉引用了引文以确保准确性。

现在完成时:
We have cross-referenced all the relevant documents. = 我们已经交叉引用了所有相关的文件。
They have cross-referenced the data to find inconsistencies. = 他们已经交叉引用了数据以查找不一致之处。

过去完成时:
The editor had cross-referenced the facts before submitting the article. = 编辑在提交文章之前已经交叉引用了事实。
The team had cross-referenced the findings with previous research. = 该团队已将调查结果与之前的研究进行了交叉引用。

将来时:
They will cross-reference the information with the updated database. = 他们将把信息与更新的数据库交叉引用。
We will cross-reference the sources to verify the information. = 我们将交叉引用来源以验证信息。


虚拟语气:
It would be helpful if they cross-referenced the data more thoroughly. = 如果他们更彻底地交叉引用数据，那将会有所帮助。
If I had time, I would cross-reference all the entries. = 如果我有时间，我会交叉引用所有条目。


被动语态:
The information is cross-referenced with the company's internal database. = 信息与公司的内部数据库交叉引用。
The articles are cross-referenced to provide a comprehensive overview. = 文章被交叉引用以提供全面的概述。


疑问句:
Does the index cross-reference all the relevant terms? = 索引是否交叉引用了所有相关术语？
Have they cross-referenced the figures with the financial statements? = 他们是否已将数字与财务报表交叉引用？


否定句:
The system does not cross-reference the data automatically. = 该系统不会自动交叉引用数据。
They did not cross-reference the sources, resulting in errors. = 他们没有交叉引用来源，导致错误。
