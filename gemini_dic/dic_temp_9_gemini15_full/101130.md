investigating:/ɪnˈvɛstɪɡeɪtɪŋ/| v. |调查；研究；审查|The act of inquiring into or examining something systematically, often to discover facts or truth.|例句：The police are investigating the crime.  警察正在调查这起犯罪案件。|例句：Scientists are investigating the causes of climate change. 科学家们正在调查气候变化的原因。|近义词：examining, exploring, probing, researching|反义词：ignoring, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are investigating the matter thoroughly. = 他们正在彻底调查此事。
We are investigating new methods of teaching. = 我们正在研究新的教学方法。

一般过去时:
The committee investigated the allegations carefully. = 委员会仔细调查了这些指控。
She investigated the history of the building. = 她调查了这座建筑的历史。

现在完成时:
The police have investigated several suspects. = 警察已经调查了几名嫌疑人。
They have been investigating the case for months. = 他们已经调查这个案子几个月了。

过去完成时:
The detective had investigated similar cases before. = 这位侦探以前调查过类似的案件。
They had investigated all the leads before they found the culprit. = 他们在找到罪犯之前已经调查了所有线索。

将来时:
The team will investigate the accident scene tomorrow. = 小组将在明天调查事故现场。
We will be investigating the impact of the new policy. = 我们将调查这项新政策的影响。

虚拟语气:
If the police investigated more thoroughly, they might find the truth. = 如果警方更彻底地调查，他们可能会找到真相。
It's important that the matter be investigated fairly. = 重要的是要公平地调查此事。

被动语态:
The case is being investigated by the FBI. = 此案正在由联邦调查局调查。
The problem was investigated thoroughly last year. = 这个问题去年被彻底调查了。

疑问句:
Are they investigating the cause of the fire? = 他们正在调查火灾的原因吗？
What are they investigating now? = 他们现在正在调查什么？

否定句:
The police did not investigate the matter properly. = 警方没有妥善调查此事。
They are not investigating the complaint. = 他们没有调查投诉。
