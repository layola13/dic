ghostwritten:/ˈɡoʊstˌrɪtn/| adj.|代笔的；由他人代笔创作的|Written by someone else but published under someone else's name.  The author's identity is hidden or unknown.|例句：That bestselling novel is rumored to be ghostwritten. 那本畅销小说据说由他人代笔。

例句：The politician's autobiography was clearly ghostwritten; the style doesn't match his known speaking manner.  那位政客的自传显然是代笔的；文风与他已知的说话方式不符。


近义词：anonymously written, unattributed, penned by another


反义词：self-written, authored by oneself


词性:形容词
