insteep:/ɪnˈstiːp/| vt.|浸泡；使沉浸；使充满|To immerse or soak in a liquid; to absorb or become filled with something.|例句：She steeped the tea leaves in hot water. 她用热水冲泡茶叶。

例句：He was steeped in the history of the region. 他沉浸在该地区的歷史之中。|近义词：soak, immerse, saturate|反义词：dry, dehydrate|动词
