seity:/ˈseɪəti/| n. |实体性，存在性，实在性 |The philosophical concept of seity refers to the inherent nature or essence of a being, particularly God in some theological contexts. It emphasizes the self-existence and independence of a being.  |例句：The theologian explored the seity of God, arguing for its immutability and eternity.  神学家探讨了上帝的实体性，认为它是永恒不变的。|近义词：beingness, essence, existence |反义词：nonexistence, nothingness |名词
