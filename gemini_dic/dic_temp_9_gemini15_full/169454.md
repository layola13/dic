proofing:/ˈpruːfɪŋ/| n.|校对，校样，防渗处理|The process of correcting errors in a text or other material before it is published or printed.  It also refers to making something resistant to water or other substances.|例句：The editor is doing the final proofing of the manuscript. 编辑正在进行稿件的最终校对。  The building's waterproofing proofing is excellent. 这栋建筑的防水处理非常好。|近义词：editing, correction, revision, waterproofing, water-proofing|反义词：error, mistake


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The editor is proofing the manuscript. = 编辑正在校对稿件。
She proofs all her documents before submitting them. = 她提交所有文件之前都会校对。


一般过去时:
He proofed the document yesterday. = 他昨天校对了文件。
They proofed the entire book before publication. = 他们出版前校对了整本书。


现在完成时:
I have proofed the article three times. = 我已经校对这篇文章三次了。
She has proofed all the marketing materials. = 她已经校对了所有营销材料。


过去完成时:
The author had proofread the chapter before sending it to the editor. = 作者在将章节发送给编辑之前已校对过。
They had proofed the document twice before submitting it. = 他们在提交文件之前已经校对过两次了。


将来时:
We will proof the website before launching it. = 我们将在启动网站之前进行校对。
She will proof the report tomorrow. = 她明天将校对报告。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would proofread the entire book. = 如果我有更多时间，我会校对整本书。
I wish I had proofed the contract more carefully. = 我希望我更仔细地校对过合同。


被动语态:
The manuscript is being proofed by the editor. = 稿件正在由编辑校对。
The document was proofed before it was printed. = 文件在打印之前已校对过。


疑问句:

Is the proofing complete? = 校对完成了吗？


否定句:
