fan-like:/ˈfænlaɪk/| adj. |扇状的，像扇子的|Resembling a fan in shape or appearance.  The term often describes something that spreads out from a central point in a radiating manner, similar to the way a fan opens. |例句：The veins on the leaf were fan-like. 叶子上的叶脉呈扇形。

例句：The firework display created a beautiful, fan-like pattern in the night sky. 烟火表演在夜空中 tạo ra một mẫu hình giống như chiếc quạt tuyệt đẹp.


近义词：fan-shaped, radiating, spreading


反义词：concentrated, clustered, compact


词性:形容词
