remains:/rɪˈmeɪnz/| n. |剩余物；遗迹；残骸|The remains of the meal were quickly cleared away.  (这顿饭的残羹剩饭很快就被清理掉了。)  The remains of the ancient city are still visible today. (这座古城的遗迹至今仍可见。) | remnants, leftovers, vestiges |  nothingness, destruction | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The evidence remains inconclusive. = 证据仍然不确定。
Some doubts still remains. = 一些疑问仍然存在。


一般过去时:
The building remained empty for years. = 这座建筑空置多年。
The mystery remained unsolved. = 这个谜团仍然没有解开。


现在完成时:
The problem has remained unsolved. = 问题一直没有解决。
The book has remained a bestseller for months. = 这本书已经畅销了好几个月。


过去完成时:
The question had remained unanswered. = 这个问题一直没有得到回答。
The city had remained untouched by the war. = 这座城市在战争中幸免于难。


将来时:
The situation will remain unchanged. = 情况将保持不变。
He will remain in charge until next week. = 他将负责到下周。


虚拟语气:
If he remained silent, we would have won. = 如果他保持沉默，我们就会赢。
I wish she remained here longer. = 我希望她在这里待久一点。


被动语态:
The decision remained to be seen. = 决定有待观察。
The matter remained to be discussed. = 这件事有待讨论。


疑问句:
What remains to be done? = 还剩下什么要做？
Does anything remain? = 还剩下什么吗？


否定句:
Nothing remains of the old house. = 老房子什么都没剩下。
He remains unconvinced. = 他仍然不相信。


PHRASE->

as remains = 就其余而言
the remains of = …的遗迹/残骸
remains to be seen = 尚待观察
it remains to be seen = 还有待观察
what remains = 剩余部分


词源:

From Old French *remeindre, from Latin remanere "to remain," from re- "again" + manere "to stay, remain."  The word's history reflects its core meaning:  to stay in a particular place or condition.
