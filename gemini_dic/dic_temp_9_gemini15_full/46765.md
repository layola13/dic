danged:/dæŋd/| adj.|该死的，讨厌的，非常|A mild intensifier, often used as a euphemism for "damned" or "cursed," expressing annoyance, frustration, or emphasis. It can be used to modify nouns, verbs, or adjectives.|例句：That darned cat keeps scratching the furniture.  那只该死的猫总是抓坏家具。


例句：I've had a danged hard day at work. 我今天工作累死了。


近义词： darned, blasted, confounded, pesky


反义词: wonderful, great, fantastic


词性:形容词
