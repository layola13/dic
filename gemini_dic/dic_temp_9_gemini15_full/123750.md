moony:/ˈmuːni/| adj.| 忧郁的；痴情的；月光似的 |Dreamy, pensive, or romantic, often suggesting a distracted or slightly dazed state.  It can also describe something resembling the moon in appearance. |例句：He looked moony and lovesick after their breakup. 他分手后看起来忧郁而痴情。

例句：The moony landscape was bathed in silver light. 月光下的景色沐浴在银色的光芒中。

近义词：dreamy, pensive, romantic, lovesick, moonstruck

反义词：cheerful, bright, alert, practical
