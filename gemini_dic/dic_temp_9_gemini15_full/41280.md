cordially:/ˈkɔːrdiəli/ | adj. | 热情地；诚恳地；友好地 | In a warm and friendly way; sincerely. | 例句：He cordially invited us to his home. 他热情地邀请我们去他家。

例句：She cordially thanked her supporters. 她诚恳地感谢了她的支持者们。


近义词：warmly, sincerely, amiably, heartily | 反义词：coldly, distantly, unsympathetically


词源:
From Middle English *cordialment, from Old French cordialment, from cordial (adjective), from Latin cordiale, from cor "heart".  The sense evolution is from "heartfelt" to "warm and friendly".
