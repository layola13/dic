piperaceous:/ˌpaɪpəˈreɪʃəs/| adj.|胡椒科的；似胡椒的|Of or relating to the Piperaceae, a family of flowering plants that includes pepper.  The term often describes plants with similar characteristics to those in the Piperaceae family, such as jointed stems and aromatic leaves.|Example: Many piperaceous plants are used in traditional medicine.|近义词：peppery (referring to the flavor), aromatic|反义词：N/A
