divertimentos:/ˌdɪvɜːrˈtiˈmɛntoʊz/| n.| divertimento (复数)|消遣；娱乐； divertimento (通常指器乐组曲)|A divertimento is a piece of instrumental music, typically light and entertaining in style, often in several movements.  It's characterized by its bright and cheerful mood, and frequently features virtuosic displays of skill by the performers.|例句：The orchestra played a delightful divertimento by Mozart.  乐团演奏了莫扎特一首令人愉快的 divertimento。 The concert featured a charming divertimento for flute and harpsichord. 音乐会演奏了一首迷人的长笛和羽管键琴 divertimento。|近义词：entertainment, amusement, concert, composition|反义词：drudgery, toil, hard work
