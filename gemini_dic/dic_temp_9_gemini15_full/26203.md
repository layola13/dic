butterbird:/ˈbʌtəbɜːrd/| n. |黄鹂鸟(一种澳大利亚鸟类)|A butterbird is a type of Australian bird, known for its bright yellow plumage and distinctive call.  They are primarily insectivorous, feeding on insects and other small invertebrates.  Different species exist within the broader "butterbird" classification.|The Golden Bowerbird is a type of butterbird.|近义词：golden bowerbird (for specific species)|反义词：无
