earthly:/ˈɜːrθli/|adj.|尘世的；世俗的；人间的；可能的；极度的|relating to the earth or the world; worldly as opposed to spiritual or heavenly; possible or conceivable; used as an intensifier|例句：He had no earthly idea where he was. 他根本不知道自己在哪儿。

例句：Their earthly possessions were few. 他们的世俗财产很少。

近义词：mundane, worldly, terrestrial, temporal|反义词：heavenly, celestial, spiritual, divine

词性:形容词
