proclaimer:/prəˈkleɪmər/ | n. | 宣告者；公布者 | A person or thing that proclaims something.  | The proclaimer of the good news was met with cheers.  好消息的宣告者受到了欢呼。 | announcer, herald, declarer |  concealer,  suppressor |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The president proclaims a new law. = 总统颁布了一项新法律。
He proclaims his innocence. = 他宣称自己无罪。


一般过去时:
She proclaimed her love for him. = 她宣告了她对他的爱。
