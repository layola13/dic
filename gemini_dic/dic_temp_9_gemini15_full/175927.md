rationalises:/ˈræʃənəlaɪz/| vt.|使合理化；为…找借口|To provide a rational explanation for something, often one that is self-serving or untrue.  It involves attempting to justify actions or beliefs, often after the fact.|例句：He rationalised his decision to quit his job by saying he needed more time with his family. 他以需要更多时间陪伴家人为由，为辞职的决定找借口。|例句：The company rationalised its production process to reduce costs. 公司使生产流程合理化以降低成本。|近义词：justify, explain, excuse|反义词：irrationalise
