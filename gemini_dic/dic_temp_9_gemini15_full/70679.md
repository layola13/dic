finales:/fɪˈnɑːleɪz/ | n. | 结局；终曲；尾声 | The exciting finales of the concerts always leave the audience wanting more.  音乐会的激动人心的终场演出总是让观众意犹未尽。 | 近义词：endings, conclusions, codas | 反义词：beginnings, introductions


英文解析：A finale is the concluding part of something, especially a musical performance or a play.  It often refers to a spectacular or exciting end.  The plural form, "finales," indicates multiple concluding parts or events.


例句：

The movie had a dramatic finale that left the audience speechless. 这部电影有一个戏剧性的结局，让观众说不出话来。

The orchestra played a rousing finale to the symphony. 管弦乐队演奏了一首激动人心的交响乐终曲。


词源：源自意大利语“finale”，意为“结束”。  最终源于拉丁语“finis”，意为“结束”。
