olefinic:/ˌoʊləˈfɪnɪk/| adj. |烯烃的；含烯烃的| Relating to or denoting olefins (alkenes).  The term refers to unsaturated hydrocarbons containing at least one carbon-carbon double bond. These compounds are characterized by their reactivity due to the presence of the double bond.  | Example: The olefinic compounds reacted readily with bromine. 烯烃化合物很容易与溴反应。 |近义词：alkenic |反义词：saturated (referring to the lack of double bonds)
