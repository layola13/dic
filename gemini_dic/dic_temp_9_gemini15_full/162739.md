prebendship:/ˈpriːbɛndʃɪp/| n.|教区牧师的俸禄；教区牧师的职位|The position and income of a prebendary, a member of a cathedral chapter who receives a prebend (an endowment).  Prebendships were often lucrative and prestigious positions within the Church.|The prebendship was a source of considerable income for the cathedral chapter.|近义词：benefice, canonry|反义词：None specifically.
