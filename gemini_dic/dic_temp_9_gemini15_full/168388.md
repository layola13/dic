proactives:/proʊˈæktɪvz/ | adj. |主动的，积极主动的 |  Proactive describes someone or something that takes initiative and anticipates problems or opportunities rather than simply reacting to them. It emphasizes taking action to create positive outcomes. | 例句：The company took a proactive approach to customer service, anticipating potential issues and resolving them quickly. 公司采取了主动的客户服务方式，预先预计潜在的问题并迅速解决。

She adopted a proactive strategy to prevent future errors. 她采取了积极主动的策略来防止未来的错误。


近义词：active, initiative, anticipatory, preventative, forward-looking | 反义词：reactive, passive, unresponsive


词性:形容词
