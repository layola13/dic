overcautious:/ˌoʊvərˈkɔːʃəs/ | adj. | 过分谨慎的，过于小心的 | Excessively cautious; showing too much care and attention to avoid potential danger or mistakes.  |  He was overcautious in his investment strategy, missing out on several lucrative opportunities. 他在投资策略上过于谨慎，错过了几次有利可图的机会。  | 近义词：excessively careful, overly cautious, timid, apprehensive | 反义词：reckless, rash, impetuous, daring
