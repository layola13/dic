midday:/ˈmɪdˌdeɪ/| n. |正午，中午 |The time of day when the sun is highest in the sky, typically around 12 o'clock noon.  It refers to the middle of the day. |例句：We had lunch at midday. 我们中午吃的午饭。

例句：The midday sun beat down on us. 中午的阳光强烈地照射着我们。


近义词：noon, midday hour, high noon |反义词：midnight
