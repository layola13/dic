focus:/ˈfoʊkəs/ /ˈfəʊkəs/| n. v.|焦点；中心；专注；集中；清晰度|The central point or main subject of something.  To concentrate one's attention or efforts on something.|例句：The main focus of the meeting was the budget.  会议的重点是预算。

例句：She needs to focus on her studies. 她需要专注于学业。|近义词：concentration, attention, emphasis|反义词：distraction, vagueness, blur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The photographer focuses on the subject. = 摄影师专注于拍摄对象。
He focuses his energy on his work. = 他把精力集中在工作上。


一般过去时:
The camera focused sharply on the bird. = 相机清晰地对准了鸟。
She focused her attention on the problem. = 她把注意力集中在问题上。


现在完成时:
I have focused on this project for months. = 我已经专注于这个项目几个月了。
They have focused their efforts on finding a solution. = 他们一直在努力寻找解决方案。


过去完成时:
He had focused on his career before starting a family. = 在组建家庭之前，他一直专注于他的事业。
She had focused her energy on the task, completing it before the deadline. = 她把精力集中在任务上，在截止日期前完成了它。


将来时:
I will focus my efforts on improving my skills. = 我将把精力集中在提高我的技能上。
The company will focus on expanding its market share. = 公司将专注于扩大其市场份额。


虚拟语气:
If I could focus better, I would finish this faster. = 如果我能更好地集中注意力，我会更快地完成这个。
I wish I could focus on my work without interruptions. = 我希望我能专心工作，不受干扰。


被动语态:
The camera was focused by the photographer. = 相机由摄影师对焦。
Attention was focused on the speaker. = 注意力集中在演讲者身上。


疑问句:
What are you focusing on right now? = 你现在专注于什么？
Where is the focus of your research? = 你的研究重点在哪里？


否定句:
He couldn't focus on his work due to the noise. = 由于噪音，他无法集中精力工作。
She didn't focus enough on the details. = 她没有足够关注细节。



PHRASE->
in focus:  清晰的；焦点对准的
out of focus: 模糊的；焦点没有对准
bring into focus: 使清晰；使重点突出
focus group: 焦点小组
focus on: 集中于；专注于
