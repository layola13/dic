prerefine:/ˌpriːrɪˈfaɪn/| v.|预先精炼，预先提纯|To refine beforehand or in advance.  It implies a process of purification or improvement that happens before the main process.|The crude oil was prerefinned before being processed into gasoline. 原油在加工成汽油之前进行了预先精炼。|近义词：pre-treat, preprocess|反义词：post-refine


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They prerefine the sugar before packaging. = 他们在包装之前预先精炼糖。
The company prerefines the metal before it is used in manufacturing. = 公司在金属用于制造之前对其进行预先精炼。


一般过去时:
The engineers prerefinned the data before running the simulations. = 工程师们在运行模拟之前预先精炼了数据。
The chemist prerefinned the chemicals before the experiment. = 化学家在实验前预先提纯了化学药品。


现在完成时:
They have prerefinned the oil for several hours. = 他们已经预先精炼了油好几个小时了。
She has prerefinned all the samples for the analysis. = 她已经为分析预先精炼了所有样品。


过去完成时:
The team had prerefinned the materials before the project started. = 项目开始前，团队已经预先精炼了材料。
He had prerefinned the mixture before adding the catalyst. = 他在添加催化剂之前已经预先精炼了混合物。
