modernizing: /ˈmɒdənaɪzɪŋ/ | /ˈmɑːdərnˌaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |使现代化；现代化改造 |The act of making something more modern or up-to-date.  It involves updating methods, technologies, or systems to align with current standards and practices. |例句：The company is modernizing its manufacturing processes to improve efficiency. 公司正在对其制造流程进行现代化改造以提高效率。

例句：They are modernizing the old railway station. 他们正在对旧火车站进行现代化改造。


近义词：updating, renovating, revitalizing, reforming

反义词：antiquating, archaizing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are modernizing their software. = 他们正在使他们的软件现代化。
The government is modernizing its infrastructure. = 政府正在对其基础设施进行现代化改造。


一般过去时:
The factory modernized its equipment last year. = 工厂去年对其设备进行了现代化改造。
She modernized her home with new appliances. = 她用新的电器使她的家现代化了。


现在完成时:
They have modernized their marketing strategy. = 他们已经使他们的营销策略现代化了。
The city has modernized its public transportation system. = 这个城市已经将其公共交通系统现代化了。


过去完成时:

They had modernized their website before launching the new product. = 他们在推出新产品之前已经对其网站进行了现代化改造。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
