reclimbing:/ˌriːˈklaɪmɪŋ/| v.|重新攀登；再次攀爬|The act of climbing again, typically after a rest or a fall.  It implies a previous climbing attempt.|例句：After a short break, he resumed reclimbing the treacherous cliff.  重新休息后，他继续攀登险峻的悬崖。

例句：The mountaineers, undeterred by the storm, began reclimbing the mountain the next morning.  登山者们不畏惧暴风雨，第二天早上开始再次攀登山峰。


近义词：re-ascending, climbing again


反义词：descending, going down


词性：动词
