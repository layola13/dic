词源:
The word "leafcutter" is a compound word formed from "leaf" and "cutter."  "Leaf" comes from Old English *lēaf, and "cutter" comes from the Old English *cuttan, meaning "to cut."  Thus, "leafcutter" literally means "one that cuts leaves."
