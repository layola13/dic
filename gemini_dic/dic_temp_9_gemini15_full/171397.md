puckish:/ˈpʌkɪʃ/| adj.|淘气的，顽皮的，恶作剧的|Playful, mischievous, and slightly naughty; characterized by a playful mischievousness.|例句：He has a puckish grin. 他脸上带着淘气的笑容。

例句：The children were engaged in puckish activities. 孩子们在进行一些恶作剧活动。


近义词：mischievous, playful, impish, roguish|反义词：serious, well-behaved, docile


词源:  The word "puckish" derives from "Puck," a mischievous sprite or hobgoblin in Shakespeare's "A Midsummer Night's Dream."  The character's nature established the word's meaning.
