contorted:/kənˈtɔːrtɪd/|/ˈkɑːntɔːrtɪd/| adj.|扭曲的；歪曲的；别扭的 |Twisted out of shape; distorted.  The contorted metal of the wrecked car was a grim reminder of the accident.  |The contorted face of the patient reflected their intense pain.|  近义词：twisted, warped, deformed, distorted | 反义词：straight, untwisted, normal


例句:

The contorted branches of the old oak reached towards the sky. 老橡树扭曲的树枝伸向天空。

His contorted expression showed his struggle with the difficult problem. 他扭曲的表情显示了他与难题的斗争。


词源:

From Middle English *contorted, from Latin *contortus, past participle of contorquere "to twist violently," from com- "together" + torquere "to twist."


用法说明:

contorted 主要用作形容词，描述物体或表情的扭曲状态。它通常暗示着一种不自然的、强迫性的变形，而不是简单的弯曲。


PHRASE->

contorted features 扭曲的面容
contorted limbs 扭曲的四肢
