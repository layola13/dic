factored:/ˈfæktərd/ | adj. | 分解的；考虑周到的 |  Having been factored; considered as a factor.  A factored polynomial is one that has been expressed as a product of simpler polynomials.  | 例句：The equation is factored into two simpler equations.  这个方程被分解成两个更简单的方程。 The decision was made after carefully factoring all the risks.  在仔细权衡所有风险后做出了这个决定。 | 近义词：analyzed, considered, broken down | 反义词：unconsidered, ignored
