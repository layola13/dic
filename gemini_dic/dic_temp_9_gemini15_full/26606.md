cachepot:/ˈkæʃpɒt/| n. |花盆套；装饰花盆|A cachepot is a decorative container, usually made of ceramic, metal, or wicker, used to hold a plant pot. It is typically used for aesthetic purposes and does not have drainage holes.  |例句：She placed the terracotta pot inside a beautiful ceramic cachepot. 她把陶土花盆放在一个漂亮的花盆套里。

例句：The elegant cachepot enhanced the beauty of the orchid.  优雅的花盆套衬托了兰花的美丽。


近义词：decorative pot, planter (when referring to the function, not the decorative aspect) |反义词：None.


词性：名词
