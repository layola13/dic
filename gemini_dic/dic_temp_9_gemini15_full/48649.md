defenselessness:/dɪˈfɛnsləsnəs/ | n. | 无防御能力；无助；脆弱 | The state of being defenseless; vulnerability to attack or harm.  It implies a lack of protection or ability to defend oneself, either physically or emotionally. |例句：The defenselessness of the young birds made them easy prey for predators.  幼鸟的无助使它们很容易成为捕食者的猎物。

The defenselessness of the refugees was heartbreaking.难民的无助令人心碎。


近义词：vulnerability, helplessness, weakness, exposure, unprotectedness |反义词：protection, security, strength, defense, invulnerability |词性：名词
