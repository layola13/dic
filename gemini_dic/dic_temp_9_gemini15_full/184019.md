reshutting: /ˌriːˈʃʌtɪŋ/ | v. |重新关闭；再次关闭 | The act of shutting something again, often after it has been opened or interrupted.  |例句：The automatic door kept reshutting after I tried to enter. 自动门在我试图进入后不断地重新关闭。


例句：The constant reshutting of the gate was annoying.  不断地重新关闭大门很烦人。


近义词：reclosing, closing again, shutting again | 反义词：opening, unshuttering


词性：动词 (verb)
