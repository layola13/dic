frenchmen:/ˈfrɛntʃmən/| n. |法国男人；法国人（复数Frenchmen）|A Frenchman is a male citizen or inhabitant of France. The plural form is Frenchmen.|Example:  He met a charming Frenchman on his trip to Paris. 他在去巴黎的旅途中遇到一位迷人的法国人。


Example: Many Frenchmen are fluent in English. 许多法国人英语流利。


近义词：Frenchman (singular), French national, Frenchman from France


反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑与其他国籍的人作对比


词性:名词
