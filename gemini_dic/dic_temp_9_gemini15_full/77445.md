gawks:/ɡɔːk/| v.| 呆呆地看，傻瞪着 | To stare openly and stupidly. |例句：He gawked at the accident. 他呆呆地看着事故。

例句：The tourists gawked at the strange sights. 游客们傻瞪着那些奇怪的景象。


近义词：stare, gape, ogle | 反义词：ignore, overlook


词源:  The word "gawk" is of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old Norse or Old English words suggesting a connection to speaking foolishly or to being foolish.  The precise etymological path is unclear.
