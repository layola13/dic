overmodernizes:/ˌoʊvərˈmɑːdərnʌɪzɪz/| v.|过度现代化；过分现代化 |To make something excessively modern, often to the detriment of its original character or functionality.  It implies a loss of something valuable in the pursuit of modernity.|例句：The renovation overmodernized the historic building, stripping it of its original charm.  翻新工程使这座历史建筑过度现代化，使其失去了原有的魅力。

例句：They overmodernized the town square, replacing the charming old fountain with a stark, minimalist sculpture. 他们过度现代化了城镇广场，用一个简陋的极简主义雕塑取代了迷人的旧喷泉。

近义词：hypermodernize, ultramodernize

反义词：traditionalize, historicize
