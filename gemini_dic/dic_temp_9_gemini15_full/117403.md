medications:/ˌmɛdəˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |药物；治疗|Drugs or other substances used to treat or prevent disease.|The doctor prescribed several medications for his condition.  医生为他开了几种药。|  He's on multiple medications for his heart condition. 他因为心脏病服用多种药物。|treatments, remedies, drugs |disease, health|名词
