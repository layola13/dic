co-inheritor:/ˌkoʊɪnˈhɛrɪtər/| n. |共同继承人 |A co-inheritor is someone who inherits something jointly with others.  This implies a shared inheritance, not necessarily an equal share. |Example:  The brothers were co-inheritors of their father's estate.  兄弟俩是他们父亲遗产的共同继承人。|近义词：joint heir, fellow heir |反义词：sole heir


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The brothers are co-inheritors of the property. = 兄弟俩是这处房产的共同继承人。
She is a co-inheritor of her aunt's fortune. = 她是她姑妈财产的共同继承人之一。

一般过去时:
They were co-inheritors of a vast fortune. = 他们曾是巨额财富的共同继承人。
He was a co-inheritor of his grandfather's business. = 他是他祖父企业的共同继承人之一。

现在完成时:
We have been co-inheritors of this land for ten years. = 我们已经是这块土地的共同继承人十年了。
They have become co-inheritors of a significant legacy. = 他们已经成为一笔可观遗产的共同继承人。

过去完成时:
Before the lawsuit, they had been co-inheritors of the company. = 在诉讼之前，他们曾是这家公司的共同继承人。
She had been a co-inheritor of the family business until the recent dispute. = 直到最近的纠纷，她一直是家族企业的共同继承人。

将来时:
They will be co-inheritors of the family home. = 他们将是家族住宅的共同继承人。
We will become co-inheritors of the trust fund next year. = 我们明年将成为信托基金的共同继承人。

虚拟语气:
If they were co-inheritors, they would share the responsibility. = 如果他们是共同继承人，他们将共同承担责任。
I wish I were a co-inheritor of that valuable collection. = 我真希望我是那批珍贵藏品的共同继承人。

被动语态:
The siblings were named co-inheritors in the will. = 兄弟姐妹在遗嘱中被指定为共同继承人。
The property was left to several co-inheritors. = 这处房产留给了几个共同继承人。

疑问句:
Are they co-inheritors of the estate? = 他们是这笔遗产的共同继承人吗？
Who are the co-inheritors of this painting? = 这幅画的共同继承人是谁？

否定句:
They are not co-inheritors; she is the sole heir. = 他们不是共同继承人；她是唯一的继承人。
He was not a co-inheritor; he received nothing from the will. = 他不是共同继承人；他从遗嘱中什么也没得到。
