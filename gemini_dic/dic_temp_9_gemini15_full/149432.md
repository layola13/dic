packway:/ˈpækweɪ/| n.|小路；小径；通道|A narrow path or track, often informally made.  It usually implies a less formal or well-maintained path than a road or trail.|例句：We took a packway through the woods to avoid the main road. 我们穿过树林里的一条小路来避开主路。

例句：The packway was overgrown with weeds and difficult to follow.  这条小路杂草丛生，很难走。

近义词：path, trail, track, lane

反义词：highway, road, avenue


词性：名词
