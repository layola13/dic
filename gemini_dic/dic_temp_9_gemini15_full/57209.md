downcast:/ˈdaʊnkæst/| adj.|沮丧的；低垂的；（眼睛）低垂的|Having or showing a sad or depressed feeling; directed downwards.|She looked downcast after receiving the bad news. 她收到坏消息后看起来很沮丧。  His eyes were downcast. 他的眼睛低垂着。|近义词：depressed, dejected, gloomy, sad|反义词：cheerful, happy, upbeat


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is downcast about his failing grades. 他对考试不及格感到沮丧。
The branches are downcast under the weight of the snow. 树枝在雪的重量下低垂着。

一般过去时:
She was downcast after the breakup. 分手后她很沮丧。
His head was downcast as he walked away. 他走开时低着头。

现在完成时:
They have been downcast since their pet died. 他们的宠物死后，他们一直很沮丧。
The flowers have been downcast by the heavy rain.  大雨过后，花朵都低垂了。

过去完成时:
He had been downcast for weeks before he finally sought help. 在最终寻求帮助之前，他已经沮丧了好几个星期。
The sun had been downcast behind the clouds all day. 太阳一整天都被乌云遮挡着。

将来时:
She will be downcast if she doesn't get the job. 如果她没有得到这份工作，她会很沮丧。
The moon will be downcast below the horizon soon. 月亮很快就会落到地平线以下。

虚拟语气:
If I failed the exam, I would be downcast. 如果我考试不及格，我会很沮丧。
I wish I weren't so downcast all the time. 我希望我不总是这么沮丧。

被动语态:
His spirits were downcast by the news. 他的情绪被这个消息击垮了。
Her eyes were downcast in shame. 她的眼睛羞愧地低垂着。(注：被动语态在此处用法较少见)


疑问句:
Is he downcast about the project? 他对这个项目感到沮丧吗？
Are her eyes downcast because she's tired? 她的眼睛低垂是因为她累了么？

否定句:
He was not downcast by the criticism. 他没有因为批评而沮丧。
Her gaze was not downcast; she met my eyes directly. 她的目光没有低垂；她直接看着我的眼睛。
