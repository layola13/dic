immanently:/ˈɪmənəntli/ | adv. | 即将地；迫切地；内在地 |inherently; intrinsically; inevitably; immediately; in the near future | The crisis is immanently threatening our company.  这场危机即将威胁到我们的公司。| The project is immanently due.  这个项目即将到期。 | inevitably, shortly, presently | remotely, distantly

词源:

From Latin *immanenter (adverb), from immanens, present participle of immanēre ("to remain within, to abide").  The adverb's meaning evolved over time, shifting from a primarily philosophical sense ("inherently") to include the temporal sense of "immediately" or "shortly."
