rhynchophore:/ˈrɪŋkəfɔːr/| n. |喙甲虫；象鼻虫|A rhynchophore is a type of insect belonging to the superfamily Curculionoidea, characterized by a prominent elongated snout or rostrum.  Many are serious agricultural pests.|Example: The weevils are a type of rhynchophore known for damaging crops.|近义词: weevil, snout beetle|反义词: N/A
