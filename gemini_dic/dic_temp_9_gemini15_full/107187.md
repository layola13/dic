lagomorph:/ˈlæɡəmɔːrf/| n. |兔形目动物|A lagomorph is a member of the order Lagomorpha, which includes rabbits, hares, and pikas.  They are characterized by their long ears, strong hind legs, and characteristic rodent-like teeth, although they are not actually rodents.|Example: Rabbits and hares are familiar examples of lagomorphs.|近义词: none (as it's an order of animals)|反义词: none (in this taxonomic context)|名词
