slaughtering: /ˈslɔːtərɪŋ/ | v. |屠宰；残杀；大量杀害 | The act of slaughtering animals for food or other purposes; the killing of a large number of people or animals in a cruel or violent way. |例句：The farmer is slaughtering pigs for the upcoming festival.  这位农民正在为即将到来的节日屠宰猪。

The protesters accused the government of slaughtering innocent civilians. 抗议者指责政府屠杀无辜平民。

近义词：butchering, massacring, killing | 反义词：saving, preserving, protecting
