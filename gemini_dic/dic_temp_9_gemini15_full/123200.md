monochrome:/ˈmɒnəʊkrəʊm/ /ˈmɑːnəʊkroʊm/| adj.|单色的，黑白的|consisting of or using only one color.  A monochrome painting or photograph uses only shades of a single color, or black and white.|The artist preferred monochrome paintings, focusing on shades of grey.|近义词：monochromatic, single-colored|反义词：polychrome, multicolored
