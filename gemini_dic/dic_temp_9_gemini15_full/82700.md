guggenheim:/ˈɡʌɡənhaɪm/| n. |古根海姆（博物馆）|The Guggenheim refers to a family name associated with prominent philanthropists and the museums they founded, particularly the Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum in New York City, known for its distinctive architecture and modern art collection.  The name itself is of German origin.|The Guggenheim Museum in New York City is a must-see for art lovers.  纽约市的古根海姆博物馆是艺术爱好者必去的地方。|Metropolitan Museum of Art, Museum of Modern Art (MoMA)|  |名词
