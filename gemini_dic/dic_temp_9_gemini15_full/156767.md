pierrots:/piˈrəʊz/| n.|皮埃罗（滑稽演员）|A pierrot is a type of clown character, typically white-faced, that originated in French pantomime.  They are often associated with sadness or melancholy despite their comedic role.|The pierrots performed a delightful, if slightly melancholic, routine.|近义词：clown, jester|反义词：None specifically, but contrasts with serious dramatic characters.
