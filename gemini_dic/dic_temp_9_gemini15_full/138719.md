numb:/nʌm/| adj.|麻木的，失去感觉的|Describes a lack of feeling, either physically or emotionally.  It can refer to a loss of sensation in a body part, or a feeling of emotional detachment or apathy.|例句：My fingers are numb from the cold. 我的手指冻僵了。|例句：After the accident, she felt emotionally numb. 事故后，她感到情感麻木。|近义词：deadened, insensible, unfeeling, apathetic|反义词：sensitive, feeling, responsive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My leg is numb. = 我的腿麻了。
He feels numb from the waist down. = 他从腰部以下感觉麻木。


一般过去时:

She felt numb after hearing the bad news. = 听到坏消息后，她感到麻木。


现在完成时:
