keychains:/ˈkiːtʃeɪnz/ | n. |钥匙链，钥匙圈 | A keychain is a small chain or ring used to hold keys together.  Keychains are often decorative and can be made from various materials. | Example: I have a keychain with a picture of my dog on it. 我有一个印着我狗照片的钥匙链。 I collect keychains from different countries. 我收集来自不同国家的钥匙链。|近义词：key ring, key holder |反义词：无
