pinnatifid:/pɪˈnætɪfɪd/| adj.|羽状深裂的；羽状分裂的|  Having a pinnate (feather-like) division that extends more than halfway to the midrib or axis.  The lobes are often pointed and separated by deep clefts. |The leaf margin was pinnatifid, resembling a bird's feather.|近义词：pinnate, dissected|反义词：entire, undivided

词源:
From Latin *pinnatus ("feathered," from pinna ("feather," "wing")), + -fidus ("split," "cleft").  The word combines the concept of a feather-like structure ("pinnate") with the action of being split or divided ("-fid").
