hume:/hjuːm/| n.|休谟；休姆（人名）|David Hume (1711-1776), a Scottish philosopher and historian.  His works include A Treatise of Human Nature and An Enquiry Concerning Human Understanding. He is known for his empiricism, skepticism, and his contributions to moral philosophy.|例句：David Hume was a highly influential figure in the history of philosophy. 大卫·休谟是哲学史上极具影响力的人物。|近义词：philosopher, historian|反义词：无
