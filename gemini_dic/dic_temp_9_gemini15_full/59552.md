eavesdropped:/ˈiːvzdrɒpt/| v.|偷听，窃听|To listen secretly to a private conversation.|例句：I eavesdropped on their conversation and learned they were planning a surprise party. 我偷听了他们的谈话，得知他们正在策划一个惊喜派对。


例句：She was eavesdropping outside the window. 她正在窗外偷听。


近义词：listen in on, overhear, spy on|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eavesdrops on his neighbors' conversations. 他偷听邻居的谈话。
They often eavesdrop on their colleagues' gossip. 他们经常偷听同事们的八卦。


一般过去时:
I eavesdropped on their argument last night. 昨晚我偷听了他们的争吵。
She eavesdropped on the phone call between her parents. 她偷听了她父母之间的电话。


现在完成时:
He has eavesdropped on us several times. 他已经几次偷听我们的谈话了。
We have never eavesdropped on anyone's conversation. 我们从未偷听过任何人的谈话。


过去完成时:
I had eavesdropped on their conversation before they noticed me. 在他们注意到我之前，我已经偷听了他们的谈话。
They had eavesdropped on the meeting before revealing the information. 他们在透露信息之前就已经偷听了会议。


将来时:
I will eavesdrop on their conversation to find out the plan. 我将偷听他们的谈话以了解计划。
He will not eavesdrop on the meeting again. 他不会再偷听会议了。


虚拟语气:
If I had not eavesdropped, I wouldn't have known the secret. 如果我没有偷听，我就不会知道这个秘密。
I wish I hadn't eavesdropped on their private conversation. 我真希望我没有偷听他们的私人谈话。


被动语态:
Their conversation was eavesdropped on by a stranger. 他们的谈话被陌生人偷听了。
The meeting was being eavesdropped on by someone. 会议正被某人偷听。


疑问句:
Were you eavesdropping on us? 你在偷听我们吗？

否定句:
I did not eavesdrop on their conversation. 我没有偷听他们的谈话。
They said they didn't eavesdrop on the meeting. 他们说他们没有偷听会议。
