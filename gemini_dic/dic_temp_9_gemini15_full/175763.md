rapturously: /ˈræptʃərəsli/ | adj. | 狂喜地；欣喜若狂地 |  Characterized by or expressing great joy or delight.  It suggests an overwhelming feeling of intense pleasure and excitement. | 例句：The audience reacted rapturously to the singer's performance. 观众对歌手的表演报以热烈的欢呼。

The children rapturously greeted the arrival of Santa Claus. 孩子们欣喜若狂地迎接圣诞老人的到来。


近义词：ecstatically, enthusiastically, joyfully, exuberantly, deliriously


反义词：apathetically, indifferently, unenthusiastically, dispassionately


词源:  Derived from "rapture," which comes from the Latin word "rapere," meaning "to seize, snatch, carry off."  The sense of being "carried away" by emotion evolved into the meaning of ecstatic joy.
