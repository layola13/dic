nonteachers:/ˈnɑːnˌtiːtʃərz/ | n. | 非教师人员 | People who are not teachers; those who do not work as teachers.  This term often refers to staff members in an educational institution who are not involved in direct instruction of students. | Example: The nonteachers were invited to the staff meeting. 非教师人员被邀请参加员工会议。 |近义词: lay staff, support staff, administrative staff | 反义词: teachers, instructors, educators |名词
