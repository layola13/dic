nationalises: /ˈnæʃənəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 国有化 | To bring under state control.  The process of transferring ownership of a company or industry from the private sector to the state. | 例句：The government nationalised the railway system. = 政府将铁路系统国有化了。 | 例句：Many industries were nationalised after the war. = 战争后许多产业都被国有化了。 | 近义词：socialise, collectivise | 反义词：privatise


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government nationalises key industries. = 政府将关键产业国有化。
He nationalises his feelings. = 他压抑自己的感情。
