nephilim:/ˈnɛfɪlɪm/| n. |巨人，堕落天使的后代|Nephilim are beings mentioned in the Book of Genesis in the Hebrew Bible.  They are described as the offspring of the "sons of God" and the "daughters of men," often interpreted as a union between angelic beings and humans.  Their existence is a subject of much debate and interpretation among religious scholars and commentators. The description of Nephilim is often associated with violence, wickedness, and immense size.|The Book of Genesis describes the Nephilim as giants who terrorized the earth.  |近义词：giant, ogre, behemoth|反义词：dwarf, pygmy
