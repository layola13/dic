corruption:/kəˈrʌpʃən/| n. |腐败；贪污；堕落 |The act or state of being corrupt; dishonesty or fraudulent behavior. It can refer to individuals, organizations, or systems.  |Example:  The mayor was accused of corruption and misuse of public funds. 市长被指控贪污和挪用公款。


The widespread corruption in the government led to public protests. 政府的普遍腐败导致了公众抗议。|近义词： dishonesty, bribery, depravity, decay |反义词：integrity, honesty, probity |名词
