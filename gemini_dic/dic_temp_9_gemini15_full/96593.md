induction:/ɪnˈdʌkʃn/ | n. |归纳；感应；就职；引入 | The process of deriving general principles from particular facts or instances.  It also refers to the process by which an electric or magnetic field is produced. In a formal context, it can mean the act of formally admitting someone to a position or organization. | 例句：The scientist used induction to formulate a new theory.  科学家用归纳法制定了一个新理论。 | 例句：The induction ceremony was attended by many dignitaries. 就职典礼有许多显要人物出席。| 近义词：inference, deduction, initiation | 反义词：deduction
