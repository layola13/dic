heraldries:/ˈhɛrəl(d)riːz/| n. |纹章学；纹章；徽章学|The study of coats of arms, crests, and other heraldic devices.  It involves the history, symbolism, and design of these emblems.  Heraldry is a complex system with strict rules and traditions.|例句：The book delves into the fascinating world of heraldries, explaining the symbolism behind different crests.|近义词：armory, coat of arms|反义词：N/A
