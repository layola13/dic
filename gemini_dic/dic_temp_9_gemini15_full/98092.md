inquisitor:/ɪnˈkwɪzɪtər/| n. |宗教裁判官；严厉盘问者|A person who makes a thorough or formal inquiry, especially into allegations of wrongdoing;  a member of the historical Inquisition.|例句：The inquisitor relentlessly questioned the suspect. 宗教裁判官无情地盘问了嫌疑人。  The journalist acted as an inquisitor, pursuing every lead. 这名记者像个严厉盘问者一样，追查每一个线索。|近义词：investigator, examiner, interrogator |反义词：supporter, defender
