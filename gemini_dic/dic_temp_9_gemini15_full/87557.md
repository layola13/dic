heteronuclear:/ˌhɛtəroʊˈnjuːkliər/| adj. | 杂核的；异核的 | Consisting of, or relating to, atoms or nuclei of different elements.  This term is commonly used in chemistry and physics to describe molecules or compounds containing different atomic nuclei. For example, a heteronuclear diatomic molecule is composed of two different atoms, such as carbon monoxide (CO).| Example: Carbon monoxide (CO) is a heteronuclear diatomic molecule.|近义词:multi-nuclear, dissimilar-nuclear|反义词:homonuclear
