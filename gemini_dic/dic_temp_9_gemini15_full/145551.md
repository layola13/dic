overcommunication:/ˌoʊvərˌkəmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|过度沟通；信息过载|The act of communicating excessively or unnecessarily, often leading to confusion or frustration.  It can involve providing too much information, repeating information unnecessarily, or communicating in a way that is overwhelming or unclear.|例句：The project failed due to overcommunication; the team was swamped with conflicting instructions.  项目失败是由于沟通过度；团队被相互冲突的指示淹没了。|近义词：verbosity, redundancy, excessive communication|反义词：undercommunication, silence
