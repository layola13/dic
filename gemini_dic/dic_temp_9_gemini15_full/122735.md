moira:/ˈmɔɪrə/| n.|命运，天意，摩伊拉（希腊神话中的命运三女神之一）|The word "moira" refers to fate, destiny, or the inevitable course of events, often with an implication of inevitability or predestination.  It also refers to one of the three Moirai (or Moirae) in Greek mythology, the goddesses of fate.|例句：Many believe that their moira is predetermined. 许多人相信他们的命运是注定的。
