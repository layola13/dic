clip-cloped:/klɪpˈkloʊpt/ | v. |得得地走，发出得得的声音 |  Onomatopoeia describing the sound of horses' hooves or similar rhythmic sounds.  It imitates the sound of hooves striking the ground. |Example: The horse clip-cloped down the lane.  这匹马得得地沿着小路走。|近义词:  pat-a-pat,  trotted,  cantered |反义词:  slithered,  crept
