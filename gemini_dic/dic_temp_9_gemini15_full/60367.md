egrets:/ˈiːɡrɛts/| n.|白鹭|A egret is a type of wading bird in the heron family, characterized by its long legs, neck, and bill, and often having long plumes during the breeding season.|例句：The egret gracefully stalked its prey in the shallows.  白鹭优雅地在浅滩中搜寻猎物。|例句： We saw several snowy egrets fishing in the rice paddy. 我们看到几只雪鹭在稻田里捕鱼。|近义词：heron, crane|反义词：无
