oogonium:/oʊˈɡoʊniəm/| n. |卵囊；卵原细胞；造卵器|A oogonium is a cell in certain plants and algae that develops into an egg cell (ovum) through meiosis.  It is the female gametangium, the structure that produces the female gametes (eggs or ova). In oomycetes, oogonia are structures that contain several oospheres.|Example: The oogonium of *Achlya* is a large, spherical structure containing multiple nuclei.  卵囊（如水霉属植物中的卵囊）是一个大型球形结构，含有许多细胞核。|近义词:archegonium(苔藓植物的颈卵器),gametangium(配子囊)|反义词:antheridium(精子器)
