generosity:/ˌdʒɛnəˈrɒsəti/ /ˌdʒɛnəˈrɑːsəti/ | n. |慷慨；大方；宽宏大量 |The quality of being generous; willingness to give and share unselfishly.  It often involves giving more than is expected or required, showing kindness and compassion. |例句：Her generosity was legendary; she always gave to those in need.  她的慷慨是出了名的；她总是帮助那些需要帮助的人。

例句：His generosity in donating a large sum of money to the charity was deeply appreciated. 他慷慨地向慈善机构捐赠了一大笔钱，这受到了大家的深深感激。


近义词：liberality, magnanimity, benevolence, altruism |反义词：stinginess, selfishness, meanness, avarice

词源:
The word "generosity" is derived from the Old French word "generosité," which in turn comes from the Latin word "generositas."  "Generosus" in Latin meant "noble," "of noble birth," and later came to imply the qualities associated with nobility, such as kindness, magnanimity, and generosity.
