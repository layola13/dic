ludlamite:/ˈlʌdləmaɪt/| n. |绿帘石|A hydrated ferrous phosphate mineral, typically green in color and found in association with other phosphate minerals. It is named after its discoverer, H. Ludlam.|例句：Ludlamite is a relatively rare mineral, often found in small, well-formed crystals.|近义词：vivianite (similar chemical composition)|反义词:N/A
