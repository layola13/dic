push-buttons:/ˈpʊʃˌbʌtənz/ | n. |按钮，按纽 | Buttons that are pressed to activate a mechanism or function.  They are commonly used in various devices and appliances. | Example: The elevator has push-buttons for each floor. 电梯有各个楼层的按钮。  The remote control uses push-buttons to change channels. 遥控器使用按钮来切换频道。 |近义词：buttons, switches | 反义词：None.
