self-disciplines:/ˈsɛlfˌdɪsəplɪnz/| n.|自律；自我约束|The ability to control one's feelings and overcome weaknesses; the practice of training oneself to obey rules or a code of behavior.|例句：Developing strong self-disciplines is crucial for achieving long-term goals.  培养强大的自律能力对实现长期目标至关重要。

例句：Her self-disciplines allowed her to stick to her diet and exercise regime. 她的自律让她坚持饮食和锻炼计划。


近义词：self-control, self-regulation, willpower, discipline


反义词：self-indulgence, lack of discipline, impulsiveness


词性：名词
