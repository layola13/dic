oxychloride:/ˈɒksɪˌklɔːraɪd/| n. |氯氧化物|A chemical compound containing oxygen and chlorine combined with another element or radical. Oxychlorides are often formed by the reaction of a metal oxide with a chlorine-containing compound.|Example: Bismuth oxychloride is used in cosmetics.|近义词：chloride oxide|反义词：无
