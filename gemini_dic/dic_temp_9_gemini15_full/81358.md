graphs:/ɡræfs/ | n. |图表，曲线图 |A visual representation of data or a function.  Graphs can be used to display relationships between variables, show trends over time, or compare different data sets. |Example: The graph shows a clear upward trend in sales.  这个图表显示销售额有明显的上升趋势。|近义词：chart, diagram, plot |反义词：description (in the sense of a non-visual explanation)


USAGE->

This graph shows the relationship between price and demand. = 这个图表显示了价格与需求之间的关系。
