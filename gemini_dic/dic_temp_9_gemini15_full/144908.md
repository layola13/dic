over-stored:/ˌoʊvərˈstɔːrd/| adj.|储存过量的；过度储存的|  Describes a situation where something is stored in excess of what is needed or appropriate, often leading to inefficiency or waste.  It implies that the storage capacity is exceeded or that the stored items are more than necessary.| Example: The warehouse was over-stored with obsolete inventory, leading to significant losses.  仓库里储存了过多的过时库存，导致了巨大的损失。|近义词：overstocked, overloaded, congested|反义词：under-stored, insufficiently stocked

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The warehouse is over-stored with old furniture. = 仓库里堆满了旧家具。
The server is over-stored with unnecessary files. = 服务器存储了不必要的过多文件。


一般过去时:
The shop was over-stored before the big sale. = 大甩卖前，商店里储存了过多的商品。
The data center was over-stored last quarter. = 上个季度数据中心存储过载。


现在完成时:
The company has over-stored its products this year. = 今年公司产品积压过多。
The customer has over-stored their data in the cloud. = 客户在云端存储了过多的数据。


过去完成时:
They had over-stored the raw materials before the factory shut down. = 工厂关闭前，他们已经储存了过多的原材料。
The team had over-stored the information on the old server. = 团队在旧服务器上存储了过多的信息。


将来时:
We will be over-stored if we don't sell more products. = 如果我们不卖出更多产品，就会储存过量。
The system will be over-stored by next year. = 到明年，系统将存储过载。


虚拟语气:
If we hadn't over-stored the food, we wouldn't have wasted so much. = 如果我们没有储存过多的食物，就不会浪费那么多。
If they had known they would over-stored the data, they would have used a different system. = 如果他们知道会储存过多的数据，他们会使用不同的系统。


被动语态:
The warehouse was over-stored by the previous manager. = 仓库是由前任经理管理过度储存的。
The database was over-stored due to a software error. = 数据库由于软件错误而存储过载。


疑问句:
Is the server over-stored? = 服务器存储过载了吗？
Are we over-stored on raw materials? = 我们的原材料储存过量了吗？


否定句:
The shelves are not over-stored; there is plenty of space. = 架子上没有堆放过多的东西；空间很充足。
The company is not over-stored at this time. = 公司目前没有储存过量。
