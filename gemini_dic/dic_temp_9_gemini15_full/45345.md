cumulonimbi:/ˌkjuːmjʊləˈnɪmbaɪ/| n. |积雨云|Cumulonimbus clouds are large, towering clouds associated with thunderstorms and heavy precipitation. They are characterized by their anvil-shaped tops and often produce intense rainfall, hail, lightning, and strong winds.|例句：The cumulonimbus clouds were building rapidly, threatening a severe thunderstorm.  积雨云迅速堆积，预示着一场严重的雷暴即将到来。

例句：We saw a massive cumulonimbus cloud in the distance, a sign of an approaching storm. 我们在远处看到巨大的积雨云，那是暴风雨来临的预兆。|近义词：thundercloud, storm cloud|反义词：cirrus cloud (纤细卷云), stratus cloud (层云)|词性:名词
