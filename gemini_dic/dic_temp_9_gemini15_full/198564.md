shruggingly: /ˈʃrʌɡɪŋli/ | adv. |耸肩地，无所谓地|  In a manner that suggests indifference or lack of concern, typically by shrugging one's shoulders.  |例句：He shruggedly replied, "Whatever." 他耸耸肩，无所谓地说：“随便。”

例句：She answered my question shruggingly, clearly unconcerned. 她耸耸肩回答了我的问题，显然并不关心。


近义词：indifferently, apathetically, nonchalantly, carelessly


反义词：concernedly, earnestly, seriously, attentively


词性: 副词
