magnificat:/ˌmæɡnɪˈfɪkæt/| n.|《圣母颂》|A canticle, or hymn, of praise to God, based on Mary's song in Luke 1:46-55.  It is traditionally recited or sung during evening prayer (Vespers) in the Roman Catholic and some other Christian liturgies.  The word itself is the Latin word for "magnify," the first word of the hymn.|例句：The choir beautifully sang the Magnificat during Vespers.  合唱团在晚祷时美妙地演唱了《圣母颂》。|近义词：canticle, hymn, song of praise|反义词：无|名词
