cloak-and-dagger: /ˈkloʊk ænd ˈdæɡər/ | adj. | 披着斗篷和匕首的；秘密的；神秘的 |  Descriptive of espionage or intrigue, suggesting secrecy, mystery, and possibly danger. It evokes images of spies, secret agents, and clandestine operations. |

例句：
The cloak-and-dagger operation was shrouded in secrecy.  = 这次秘密行动笼罩在神秘之中。

The novel features a thrilling cloak-and-dagger plot involving international spies. = 这部小说讲述了一个惊险的间谍故事，涉及国际间谍。


近义词：secret, clandestine, covert, mysterious, underhanded,  espionage, intrigue


反义词：open, aboveboard, transparent, public, straightforward


词性：形容词
