glycogenesis:/ˌɡlaɪkoʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs/| n. |糖原生成，糖原合成 |The process of glycogen formation from glucose. Glycogenesis is primarily carried out in the liver and muscles and is stimulated by insulin.|例句：Glycogenesis is crucial for maintaining blood glucose levels during periods of fasting. 糖原生成对于在禁食期间维持血糖水平至关重要。|例句：The process of glycogenesis involves a series of enzymatic reactions. 糖原生成过程涉及一系列酶促反应。|近义词：glycogen synthesis|反义词：glycogenolysis
