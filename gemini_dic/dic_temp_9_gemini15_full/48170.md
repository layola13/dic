The company's new initiative focuses on decompartmentalizing information to improve team efficiency. 公司的新举措侧重于打破信息壁垒以提高团队效率。|近义词：integrating, unifying, connecting, interlinking |反义词：compartmentalizing, isolating, separating |动词

词源:  The word is formed by adding the prefix "de-" (meaning "to remove" or "to reverse") to "compartmentalizing," which itself comes from "compartment," meaning a separate section or division.  Therefore, "decompartmentalizing" literally means "removing the separation of compartments."
