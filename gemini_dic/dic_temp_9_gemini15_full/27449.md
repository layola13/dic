camouflager:/ˈkæməˌflɑːʒər/| n. |伪装者；伪装物；伪装者|A camouflager is a person or thing that camouflages something.  It can refer to the person or device doing the camouflage, or the camouflage itself.  The word emphasizes the active process of concealing.|例句：The army used expert camouflagers to hide their tanks.  军队使用了专业的伪装者来隐藏他们的坦克。
The artist’s camouflager blended seamlessly with the forest background. 艺术家的伪装作品与森林背景完美融合。|近义词：disguiser, concealer, masker |反义词：revealer, exposer
