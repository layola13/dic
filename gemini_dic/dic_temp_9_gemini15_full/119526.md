/ˌmaɪkrəˈskɑːpɪk/| adj. | 微观的，显微镜下的 | Extremely small; only visible with a microscope.  Microscopical refers to things that are so small they can only be seen using a microscope.  | Example: The microscopical structure of the cell was revealed through electron microscopy.  显微镜下的细胞结构通过电子显微镜显现出来。The scientist studied the microscopical organisms in the pond water. 科学家研究了池塘水中微观的生物。|近义词：microscopic, minute, tiny, infinitesimal | 反义词：macroscopic, visible, large
