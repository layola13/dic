resurgence:/rɪˈsɜːrdʒəns/| n. |复苏；再起；复活|The sudden resurgence of interest in vinyl records surprised many.  最近黑胶唱片突然重新受到欢迎，令许多人感到意外。|近义词：revival, comeback, reappearance |反义词：decline, downfall, extinction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A resurgence of interest in classic literature is occurring. = 对经典文学的兴趣正在复苏。
The resurgence of the economy is showing positive signs. = 经济复苏显示出积极的迹象。


一般过去时:
A resurgence of the disease occurred after a period of remission. = 在一段时间的缓解期后，这种疾病再次爆发。
The company experienced a resurgence in sales after launching a new marketing campaign. = 公司在推出新的营销活动后，销售额出现回升。


现在完成时:
There has been a resurgence of traditional music in recent years. = 近年来传统音乐出现了复苏。
The city has seen a resurgence in tourism since the renovation of its historic district. = 自从其历史街区翻新以来，这座城市旅游业出现了复苏。


过去完成时:
Before the resurgence of the industry, many workers had lost their jobs. = 在该行业复苏之前，许多工人已经失业了。
The resurgence of the team's morale followed a period where they had suffered several defeats. = 球队士气的复苏是在他们经历了几次失败之后发生的。


将来时:
Experts predict a resurgence in demand for skilled labor in the coming years. = 专家预测未来几年对熟练劳动力的需求将会回升。
We anticipate a resurgence of the bird population after the conservation efforts. = 我们预计在保护工作之后，鸟类数量将会回升。


虚拟语气:
If there were a resurgence of the old traditions, the community would be stronger. = 如果旧传统能够复苏，社区将会更加强大。
I wish there were a resurgence of civility in political discourse. = 我希望政治讨论中能够恢复文明礼貌。


被动语态:
The resurgence of the band was widely reported in the media. = 乐队的复苏得到了媒体的广泛报道。
The economic resurgence is being attributed to government policies. = 经济复苏被归因于政府政策。


疑问句:
Is there a resurgence of interest in this topic? =  人们对这个话题的兴趣是否正在复苏？
Will we witness a resurgence of this particular style of art? = 我们会见证这种特定艺术风格的复苏吗？


否定句:
There was no resurgence of the fighting after the ceasefire. = 停火后战斗没有再次爆发。
The company did not experience a resurgence in profits despite its new product launch. = 尽管公司推出了新产品，但利润并没有出现回升。
