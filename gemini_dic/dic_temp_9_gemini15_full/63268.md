enthusiastic: /ɛnˌθuːziˈæstɪk/ | adj. | 热情的，热心的 |  Characterized by or showing great enthusiasm.  It implies a strong feeling of excitement and interest. | 例句：She gave an enthusiastic speech about her project. 她热情洋溢地介绍了她的项目。

例句：He's an enthusiastic supporter of the team. 他是这个队的忠实支持者。


近义词：passionate, ardent, fervent, zealous | 反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic, lukewarm


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is enthusiastic about his new job. = 他对他的新工作充满热情。
They are enthusiastic learners. = 他们是充满热情的学习者。

一般过去时:
She was enthusiastic about the idea. = 她对这个主意很感兴趣。
We were enthusiastic participants in the event. = 我们是这次活动的热情参与者。

现在完成时:
He has been enthusiastic about music since childhood. = 他从小就对音乐充满热情。
They have remained enthusiastic supporters of the cause. = 他们一直是这项事业的热心支持者。

过去完成时:
She had been enthusiastic about the trip before the weather changed. = 天气变化之前，她对这次旅行充满热情。
We had already been enthusiastic about the project before the funding was secured. = 在资金到位之前，我们就已经对这个项目充满热情了。

将来时:
I will be enthusiastic about the challenge. = 我将对这个挑战充满热情。
They will be enthusiastic participants in the conference. = 他们将是这次会议的热情参与者。


虚拟语气:
If I were enthusiastic, I would volunteer. = 如果我很热情的话，我会自愿帮忙。
I wish I were more enthusiastic about my studies. = 我希望我对我的学习更热情一些。

被动语态:
His work was enthusiastically received. = 他的作品受到了热烈的欢迎。
The proposal was described as enthusiastic. = 该提案被描述为充满热情。

疑问句:
Are you enthusiastic about your work? = 你对你的工作热情吗？
Is she enthusiastic about the new policy? = 她对新政策热情吗？

否定句:
He is not enthusiastic about politics. = 他对政治不感兴趣。
They were not enthusiastic about the changes. = 他们对这些变化不热心。
