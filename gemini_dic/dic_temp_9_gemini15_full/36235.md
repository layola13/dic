cognitive: /ˈkɒɡnɪtɪv/ /ˈkɑːɡnɪtɪv/ | adj. | 认知的；感知的 | Relating to cognition; the mental processes involved in acquiring knowledge and understanding through thought, experience, and the senses.  | Example:  Cognitive development is a crucial aspect of childhood.  认知发展是儿童时期的一个关键方面。

The study explored the cognitive effects of sleep deprivation. 这项研究探讨了睡眠剥夺对认知的影响。|近义词：mental, intellectual, perceptual |反义词：unconscious, unaware

WORD ORIGIN->
From Latin *cognoscere "to know," from *co- "together" + *gnoscere "to know" (akin to Greek gignoskein "to know"). The suffix "-itive" forms adjectives from verbs.
