in-the-cards:/ˌɪn ðə kɑːrz/| adj.|意料之中的；很可能发生的|Likely to happen; expected.|例句：A pay raise is in the cards for those who have exceeded expectations.  那些超出预期的人加薪指日可待。|例句：With the current market trends, a recession is definitely in the cards. 根据目前的市场趋势，经济衰退势在必行。|近义词：probable, likely, expected, foreseeable|反义词：unlikely, improbable, unexpected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It is in the cards that he will win the election. = 他赢得选举是意料之中的。
The promotion is in the cards for her. = 她升职是意料之中的。


一般过去时:
It was in the cards that the project would be delayed. = 项目延期是意料之中的。
A family vacation was in the cards last summer. = 去年夏天一次家庭度假是意料之中的。


现在完成时:
It has been in the cards that they would eventually separate. = 他们最终会分开是意料之中的。
A major breakthrough has been in the cards for this research. = 这项研究取得重大突破是意料之中的。


过去完成时:
It had been in the cards that the company would go bankrupt. = 公司破产是意料之中的。
It had been in the cards that he would fail the exam. = 他考试不及格是意料之中的。


将来时:
It will be in the cards that the price will increase. = 价格上涨是意料之中的。
A new policy will be in the cards next year. = 明年将会有新的政策出台，这是意料之中的。


虚拟语气:
If it were in the cards, I would travel the world. = 如果有可能的话，我会周游世界。
If it had been in the cards, we would have invested in that company. = 如果有可能的话，我们会投资那家公司。


被动语态:
It was believed that a war was in the cards. = 人们认为战争是不可避免的。
It is predicted that a severe winter is in the cards. = 预计将会有一个严冬。


疑问句:
Is a pay rise in the cards for you this year? = 你今年有可能加薪吗？
Is another world war in the cards? = 另一次世界大战有可能发生吗？


否定句:
A peaceful solution is not in the cards. = 和平解决是不可能的。
Further negotiations are not in the cards at this point. = 在这一点上，进一步谈判是不可能的。


PHRASE->
be in the cards = 很可能发生
have something in the cards = 有计划要做某事
put something in the cards = 安排某事
it's in the cards that... = ...很可能
play one's cards right = 采取正确的策略
