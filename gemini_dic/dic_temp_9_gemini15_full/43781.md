cremates:/krɪˈmeɪts/ | vt. |火化 |To burn a dead body to ashes.  This is typically done as part of a funeral or burial rite.  The process involves high temperatures to reduce the body to bone fragments and ash. |例句：The family decided to cremate their loved one.  家人决定火化他们的亲人。


例句：After the funeral service, the body was cremated. 葬礼仪式结束后，遗体被火化了。


近义词：incinerate, burn (in this specific context)


反义词：bury, entomb


词性:动词
