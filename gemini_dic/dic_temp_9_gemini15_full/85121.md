hardhack:/ˈhɑːrdˌhæk/| n. |硬枝荚蒾；铁线莲；硬枝山茱萸|A shrub, typically with a hard, woody stem, often found in North America.  Several species of plants are commonly called hardhack, including species of Spirea (especially *Spirea tomentosa*) and *Hydrangea arborescens*. The name likely refers to the plant's tough, persistent stems.|The hardhack is a beautiful native shrub.  硬枝荚蒾是一种美丽的本地灌木。|近义词：steeplebush, meadowsweet (depending on the specific species)|反义词：无
