fibroma:/faɪˈbroʊmə/| n. |纤维瘤|A benign tumor composed of fibrous connective tissue.  Fibromas are generally slow-growing and non-cancerous, though some types can be aggressive or have a higher risk of malignancy.|  Example: The doctor discovered a small fibroma during the routine examination. 医生在例行检查中发现了一个小的纤维瘤。|近义词：fibrous tumor, myoma (if in muscle)|反义词：carcinoma, sarcoma|
