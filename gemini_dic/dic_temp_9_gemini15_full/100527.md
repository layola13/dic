intervisitation:/ˌɪntərˌvɪzɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |互访；互相探望；交叉访问|The act of visiting each other; reciprocal visits.  It often refers to visits between institutions or groups, such as prisons or schools. |例句：The agreement allows for intervisitation between the two prisons.  = 这项协议允许两所监狱之间进行互访。
|例句：The schools have a program of intervisitation to share best practices. =  这些学校有一个互访项目来分享最佳实践。|近义词：reciprocal visits, mutual visits, exchange visits|反义词：isolation, seclusion|词性:名词
