essayed:/eˈseɪd/ | vt. |尝试；试图；扮演；写作| To attempt or try; to undertake (a task or role); to write or compose (an essay). |例句：He essayed a smile, but it didn't quite reach his eyes. 他试着微笑，但笑容并没有到达他的眼睛。

He essayed a new role in the play. 他在剧中尝试了一个新的角色。|近义词：attempted, tried, endeavored, undertook |反义词：succeeded, accomplished
