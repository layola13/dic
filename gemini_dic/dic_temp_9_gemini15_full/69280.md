featherweights: /ˈfɛðərˌweɪts/ | n. |轻量级拳击手；轻量级的东西；轻量级人物 | A featherweight is a boxer who weighs less than a certain amount, typically between 118 and 126 pounds.  The term can also refer to something or someone that is very light or unimportant. |例句：He's a featherweight in the boxing world. 他是拳击界的一个轻量级人物。

He considered the problem a featherweight compared to the others. 他认为这个问题与其他问题相比微不足道。


近义词：lightweight, light, insignificant, unimportant

反义词：heavyweight, important, significant
