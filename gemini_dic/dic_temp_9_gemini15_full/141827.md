orbless: /ˈɔːrb lɪs/ | adj. | 无球的；没有光环的 | Lacking an orb or orbs; without a halo or similar radiant feature.  The term often implies a lack of power, glory, or influence associated with such imagery. |


例句：

The orbless moon hung in the inky sky. = 无光的月亮悬挂在墨黑的夜空中。

His orbless reign marked the end of an era of prosperity. = 他毫无光彩的统治标志着一个繁荣时代的结束。


近义词：gloomless, lustreless, dull, powerless

反义词： radiant, glorious, luminous, powerful

词性：形容词
