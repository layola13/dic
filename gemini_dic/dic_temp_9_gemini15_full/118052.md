mend:/mend/| vt. n.|修理，修补；改进；痊愈|To repair something that is broken or damaged.  It can also refer to improving a situation or healing a wound.|例句：He mended the broken chair. 他修理了坏掉的椅子。


例句：The tailor mended the rip in my trousers.  裁缝缝补了我裤子上的裂口。


近义词：repair, fix, patch, improve, heal|反义词：break, damage, worsen, impair|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mends his clothes regularly. = 他定期修理他的衣服。
She mends her ways after making a mistake. = 她犯错后改过自新。


一般过去时:
I mended the fence yesterday. = 我昨天修好了篱笆。
The doctor mended his broken arm. = 医生治好了他骨折的胳膊。


现在完成时:
She has mended all the broken toys. = 她已经修好了所有坏掉的玩具。
He has mended his relationship with his brother. = 他已经修复了他和兄弟的关系。


过去完成时:
They had mended the roof before the storm arrived. = 暴风雨来临之前他们已经修好了屋顶。
She had mended her ways before she met him. = 在遇见他之前，她已经改过自新了。


将来时:
I will mend the tear in my shirt tomorrow. = 我明天会缝补我衬衫上的破洞。
They will mend the broken vase carefully. = 他们会小心地修补那个破花瓶。


虚拟语气:
If I had a sewing machine, I would mend this dress myself. = 如果我有缝纫机，我会自己缝补这件衣服。
I wish I could mend the broken friendship. = 我希望我能修复破裂的友谊。


被动语态:
The broken window was mended quickly. = 破窗户很快就被修好了。
The relationship between them needs to be mended. = 他们之间的关系需要修复。


疑问句:
Can you mend this hole? = 你能缝补这个洞吗？
Will you mend the broken toy for me? = 你会帮我修好这个坏掉的玩具吗？


否定句:
I couldn't mend the broken vase. = 我无法修补那个破花瓶。
He didn't mend his ways despite warnings. = 尽管受到了警告，他还是没有改过自新。



PHRASE->
