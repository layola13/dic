gulling:/ˈɡʌlɪŋ/| n. v.|欺骗；愚弄；捕鸥|The act of deceiving or tricking someone, often for personal gain; also, the act of catching gulls.  This word has both noun and verb forms.  |例句：The gulling of the naive investor cost him his life savings.  欺骗那位天真的投资者让他损失了毕生的积蓄。  The fisherman spent the morning gulling seagulls. 渔夫花了整个上午捕海鸥。|近义词：cheating, swindling, defrauding, hoodwinking, trapping (for the verb); deception, trickery, fraud (for the noun)|反义词：honesty, truthfulness, fairness|v.
