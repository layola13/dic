octopus: /ˈɒk.tə.pʊs/ /ˈɑːk.tə.pʊs/ | n. | 章鱼 | A sea creature with eight arms, a soft body, and no internal skeleton.  It is known for its intelligence and ability to camouflage itself. | Example: The octopus cleverly hid amongst the rocks.  那只章鱼巧妙地躲藏在岩石之间。  The diver saw a giant octopus swimming in the deep ocean. 潜水员看到一只巨大的章鱼在深海中游动。|近义词：cephalopod | 反义词：无
