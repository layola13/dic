overmourn: /ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrn/ | vt. |过度悲伤；过于哀悼 | To mourn excessively or for too long.  It implies a grieving process that is unhealthy or unproductive due to its intensity or duration. |例句：She overmourned her father's death, neglecting her own health and well-being. 她过度哀悼父亲的去世，忽略了自己的健康和福祉。

例句：It's important not to overmourn the loss; remember the good times and move forward.  重要的是不要过度悲伤；记住美好的时光并继续前进。


近义词：excessively grieve, lament excessively,  overgrieve


反义词：accept,  move on,  heal


词性：动词
