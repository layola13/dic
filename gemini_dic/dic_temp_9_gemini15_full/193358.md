segregated:/ˈsɛɡrɪɡeɪtɪd/| adj. |隔离的；种族隔离的| Separated or kept apart from others, especially on the basis of race, religion, or ethnicity.  The term often refers to the systematic separation of people based on a particular characteristic, often resulting in inequality and discrimination.|例句：In the past, many schools were segregated by race. = 过去，许多学校按种族隔离。

例句：The neighborhood was highly segregated, with little interaction between different ethnic groups. = 这个街区隔离严重，不同种族群体之间几乎没有互动。


近义词：isolated, separated, partitioned, divided|反义词：integrated, unified, combined|词性：形容词
