coproprietor:/ˌkoʊˈproʊpraɪətər/| n. |共有业主；共同所有人|A copropietor is someone who jointly owns a property or business with one or more other people. They share the responsibilities and profits (or losses) associated with the ownership.|例句：The brothers are copropietors of a successful restaurant.  兄弟俩是那家成功餐厅的共有业主。|例句：She and her partner are copropietors of a small bookstore. 她和她的伙伴是那家小书店的共同所有人。|近义词：joint owner, partner, shareholder|反义词：sole proprietor, single owner
