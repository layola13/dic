recommendation's: /ˌrekəmenˈdeɪʃənz/ | n. | 建议（复数） | Recommendations are suggestions or proposals offered as a guide to action or conduct.  It is the plural form of "recommendation". |


例句：

The committee's recommendations were carefully considered.  委员会的建议受到了仔细的考虑。

Her recommendations for improving the system were well-received.  她关于改进系统的建议受到了好评。


近义词：suggestions, proposals, advice, counsel

反义词：prohibitions, objections, disapproval


词性：名词 (复数)
