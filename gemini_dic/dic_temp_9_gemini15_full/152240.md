patron-ship's:/ˈpeɪtrənʃɪps/| n. |赞助；资助；保护；惠顾；主顾关系|The act of being a patron; the state of being patronized; patronage.  It often refers to the relationship between a patron and a person or organization they support.  The possessive 's' indicates possession or belonging to a patron.|例句：The museum relies heavily on the patron-ship's generous donations.  博物馆严重依赖赞助人的慷慨捐赠。

The artist thrived under the patron-ship's guidance and financial support.这位艺术家在赞助人的指导和经济支持下蓬勃发展。|近义词：sponsorship, support, protection, patronage|反义词：neglect, abandonment, opposition

词源:
Patron (n.)  From Old French *patron, from Latin patronus "protector, defender, advocate," originally "father of a family," from pater "father."  The "-ship" suffix indicates a state, condition, or quality.  The possessive 's' denotes possession.
