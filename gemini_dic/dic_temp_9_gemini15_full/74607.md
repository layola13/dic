freck:/frɛk/| n. |雀斑；少量 |A small brown spot on the skin, especially on the face, caused by exposure to the sun.  |例句：She has freckles on her nose. 她鼻子上长着雀斑。|例句：The child's face was covered with freckles.  孩子的脸上布满了雀斑。|近义词：sunspot, blemish |反义词：clear skin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has many freckles. 他有很多雀斑。
She gets freckles in the summer. 夏天她会长雀斑。


一般过去时:
He had freckles as a child. 他小时候有雀斑。
She didn't have freckles before. 她以前没有雀斑。


现在完成时:
I've always had freckles. 我一直都有雀斑。
She's developed freckles since moving to Florida.自从搬到佛罗里达州后，她长出了雀斑。


过去完成时:
He had had freckles for years before they faded.  他的雀斑在褪去之前已经存在好多年了。
She hadn't noticed her freckles until recently. 直到最近她才注意到自己的雀斑。


将来时:
He will probably have freckles all his life. 他很可能一辈子都会长雀斑。
She expects to have fewer freckles next year. 她预计明年雀斑会少一些。


虚拟语气:
If I had fewer freckles, I would feel better about myself. 如果我的雀斑少一些，我会感觉更好。
I wish I didn't have so many freckles. 我希望我没有这么多雀斑。


被动语态:
(Freckles are not typically used in passive voice.)雀斑通常不用于被动语态。


疑问句:
Do you have freckles? 你有雀斑吗？
How many freckles does she have? 她有多少雀斑？


否定句:
He doesn't have any freckles. 他没有任何雀斑。
She didn't used to have freckles. 她以前没有雀斑。


PHRASE->
freckle-faced：长满雀斑的脸
a scattering of freckles：零星的雀斑
covered with freckles：布满雀斑
sun-kissed freckles: 晒出的雀斑
sprinkling of freckles: 少量的雀斑
