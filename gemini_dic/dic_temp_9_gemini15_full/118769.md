metallization:/ˌmɛtəlɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 金属化；金属镀层 | The process of coating a surface with a layer of metal.  This can be done through various techniques, such as sputtering, evaporation, or electroplating, to improve properties like conductivity, reflectivity, or corrosion resistance. | Example: The metallization of the semiconductor improved its conductivity significantly.  金属化处理显著提高了半导体的导电性。 | 近义词：metal plating, coating | 反义词：demetallization
