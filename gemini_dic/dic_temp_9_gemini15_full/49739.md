demographic:/ˌdɛməˈɡræfɪk/| n. |人口统计的；人口学的|relating to the population and different groups within it.|The demographics of the city have changed dramatically in recent years.  这个城市的社会人口结构近年来发生了巨大变化。|近义词：population statistics, population data|反义词：N/A|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The demographics of this country show a growing elderly population. = 这个国家的统计数据显示老年人口正在增长。
We are analyzing the demographics of our target market. = 我们正在分析目标市场的社会人口统计数据。


一般过去时:
The demographics of the town changed significantly after the factory closed. = 工厂关闭后，小镇的人口统计发生了显著变化。
Researchers studied the demographics of the region in the 1950s. = 研究人员研究了20世纪50年代该地区的人口统计数据。


现在完成时:
The company has collected demographic data on its customers. = 公司已经收集了客户的人口统计数据。
The government has released updated demographic projections. = 政府发布了更新的人口预测数据。


过去完成时:
By the time the census was taken, the demographics had shifted considerably.  = 在人口普查进行时，人口统计已经发生了相当大的变化。
Researchers found that the demographics had already changed dramatically before their study began. = 研究人员发现，在他们开始研究之前，人口统计数据已经发生了巨大的变化。


将来时:
The demographics will likely shift further in the next decade. = 未来十年，人口统计可能会进一步发生变化。
Experts predict that the demographics of the workforce will change significantly. = 专家预测，劳动力的社会人口统计数据将发生重大变化。


虚拟语气:
If the demographics were different, the political landscape would be altered. = 如果人口统计数据不同，政治格局就会改变。
It is important to consider what the demographics would be like in the future. = 重要的是要考虑未来的人口统计数据将会是什么样的。


被动语态:
Demographic data is being collected to assess the impact of the new policy. = 正在收集人口统计数据以评估新政策的影响。
The report was based on the latest demographic projections. = 该报告基于最新的社会人口统计预测。


疑问句:
What are the key demographics of your customer base? = 您的客户群体的关键人口统计数据是什么？
How do the demographics influence marketing strategies? = 人口统计数据如何影响营销策略？


否定句:
The study did not include detailed demographic information. = 这项研究没有包含详细的人口统计信息。
The marketing campaign failed to consider the relevant demographics. = 营销活动未能考虑相关的人口统计数据。

词源:
From Greek demos ("people") + graphein ("to write").  字面意思是“关于人民的书写”。
