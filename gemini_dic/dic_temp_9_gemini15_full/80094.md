gobang:/ˈɡoʊbæŋ/| n. |五子棋|A two-player abstract strategy board game played on a grid, where players take turns placing stones of their color to form a continuous line of five.  The first player to achieve this wins.|例句：We played a game of gobang during our lunch break.  我们在午休时间玩了一局五子棋。|近义词：Five in a row, Gomoku|反义词：无
