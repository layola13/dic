palingenesis:/ˌpæliˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ | n. | 再生；重演；返祖现象 | The reappearance of ancestral characteristics in an organism.  The theory that the development of an individual organism repeats the evolutionary history of its species. | 例句：The theory of palingenesis suggests that embryonic development recapitulates phylogeny. 再生理论认为胚胎发育重演了系统发育史。

例句：Some researchers believe that certain human behaviors are examples of palingenesis, echoing primal instincts. 一些研究人员认为某些人类行为是返祖现象的例子，反映了原始本能。


近义词：recapitulation, regeneration, rebirth | 反义词：destruction, extinction
