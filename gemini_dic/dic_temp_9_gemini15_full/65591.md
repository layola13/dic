evagination: /ˌɛvæɡɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 外翻；外凸；外翻成形 | The outward turning or protrusion of a membrane or layer of tissue.  It often refers to the process of forming an outward pouch or sac.  For example, the formation of the gut tube during embryonic development is a type of evagination. | The evagination of the intestinal tract during embryonic development is crucial. 外胚层肠管在胚胎发育过程中的外翻至关重要。| eversion, protrusion | invagination | 名词
