/aʊtˈbɪtʃ/| v.| 胜过（尤指女性）；超过；击败| To surpass or exceed, especially in a competitive or aggressive manner, often implying a display of dominance or skill.  The term is often used in a derogatory or informal context.|例句：She completely outbitched her rivals in the competition. =她在比赛中彻底击败了她的对手。|近义词：outdo, outperform, outmaneuver, surpass, exceed|反义词：be outdone, be surpassed, be defeated
