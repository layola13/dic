prickleback:/ˈprɪkəlˌbæk/| n. |刺鳍鱼，棘背鱼|A prickleback is a type of fish characterized by its elongated body, small scales, and often spiny dorsal fin.  Many species are found in intertidal zones and rocky shores.  They are generally small, bottom-dwelling fish with a variety of colors and patterns depending on the species.|例句：The intertidal zone was teeming with life, including several species of pricklebacks.  潮间带充满了生命，包括几种刺鳍鱼。|例句：We observed a large shoal of pricklebacks foraging amongst the rocks. 我们观察到一大群刺鳍鱼在岩石中觅食。|近义词：blenny (some species)|反义词：无
