hypersusceptibility: /ˌhaɪpərˌsʌsɛptəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 超敏性，过敏性 | Extreme susceptibility; an abnormally high sensitivity to a stimulus, substance, or disease.  | Example:  The patient exhibited hypersusceptibility to penicillin, experiencing a severe allergic reaction after a single dose.  病人对青霉素表现出超敏性，单剂量后就出现了严重的过敏反应。 |近义词：extreme sensitivity, oversensitivity, hyperreactivity |反义词：hyposensitivity, resistance, insensitivity | 名词
