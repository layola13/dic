misforming:/ˌmɪsˈfɔːrmɪŋ/| v. |误传；歪曲；使变形 |To give a false or misleading account of; to distort.  |例句：The media is misforming the public about the true nature of the event.媒体正在歪曲公众对事件真相的了解。
The heat misformed the plastic container.高温使塑料容器变形。|近义词：distorting, misrepresenting, warping |反义词：clarifying, correcting, representing accurately |动词
