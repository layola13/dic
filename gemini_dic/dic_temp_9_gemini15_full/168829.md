products:/ˈprɒdʌkts/ /ˈprɑːdʌkts/ | n. |产品，制品|Things that are produced or manufactured; goods.  The company's products are sold worldwide. 公司的产品销往世界各地。|近义词：goods, commodities, wares |反义词：services (in some contexts)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory produces many products. = 工厂生产许多产品。
Our company sells a wide range of products. = 我们公司销售各种各样的产品。

一般过去时:
They produced high-quality products last year. = 他们去年生产了高质量的产品。
The company launched several new products in 2022. = 公司在2022年推出了几款新产品。

现在完成时:
We have developed several new products this year. = 我们今年已经开发了几款新产品。
The company has improved its products significantly. = 公司已经显著改进了其产品。

过去完成时:
They had already finished producing the products before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经完成了产品的生产。
The company had launched similar products in the past. = 公司过去曾推出过类似的产品。

将来时:
We will launch our new products next month. = 我们下个月将推出我们的新产品。
They will increase the production of their products. = 他们将增加产品的产量。

虚拟语气:
If we had better products, we would sell more. = 如果我们有更好的产品，我们会卖出更多。
I wish we had a wider range of products. = 我希望我们有更广泛的产品系列。

被动语态:
Many products are manufactured overseas. = 许多产品在海外制造。
The products are being shipped to customers now. = 产品正在运送给客户。

疑问句:
What products does your company produce? = 贵公司生产什么产品？
What are the main products of this company? = 这家公司的主要产品是什么？

否定句:
We don't produce that type of product. = 我们不生产那种产品。
The company didn't launch any new products this quarter. = 本季度公司没有推出任何新产品。
