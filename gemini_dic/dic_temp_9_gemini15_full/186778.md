rills:/rɪlz/| n.|小溪，细流|Small streams of water; a small stream or brook.|例句：Tiny rills of water trickled down the hillside.  细细的溪流从山坡上涓涓流下。|例句：The rain created many rills in the soft earth. 雨水在松软的泥土中冲刷出许多细流。|近义词：streamlet, brook, creek|反义词：river, torrent


USAGE->

Many small rills flow through the meadow. = 许多小溪流过草地。
