decerpt:/dɪˈsɜːrpt/ | v. |摘录；节录；选录|To take a short extract from a longer piece of writing or music.|例句：I excerpted the most important parts of the report. 我摘录了报告中最重要部分。|近义词：extract, excerpt, abstract|反义词：synthesize, combine


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He excerpts relevant information from the book. 他从书中摘录相关信息。
She excerpts key passages from the novel. 她从小说中摘录关键段落。

一般过去时:
They excerpted the best parts of the speech. 他们摘录了演讲的最佳部分。
He excerpted several chapters for his thesis. 他为他的论文摘录了几章。

现在完成时:
I have excerpted the main points of the article. 我已经摘录了这篇文章的主要观点。
She has excerpted many quotes for her research paper. 她已经为她的研究论文摘录了许多引言。

过去完成时:
They had excerpted the data before the analysis. 他们在分析之前已经摘录了数据。
She had excerpted the relevant chapters before she started writing. 她开始写作之前已经摘录了相关的章节。

将来时:
I will excerpt the most interesting parts of the interview. 我将摘录采访中最有趣的部分。
He will excerpt the song for his presentation. 他将为他的演示摘录这首歌。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would excerpt more information. 如果我有更多时间，我会摘录更多信息。
I wish I had excerpted the entire chapter. 我希望我当时摘录了整章内容。

被动语态:
The key findings were excerpted from the study. 关键发现是从这项研究中摘录出来的。
The best passages were excerpted and published separately. 最好的段落被摘录并单独出版。

疑问句:
Have you excerpted the relevant sections? 你已经摘录了相关的部分了吗？
What parts did you excerpt from the document? 你从文件中摘录了哪些部分？

否定句:
He didn't excerpt any information from the website. 他没有从网站上摘录任何信息。
I didn't excerpt the entire article. 我没有摘录整篇文章。


PHRASE->
excerpt from = 从…摘录
excerpt of = …的节录
selected excerpt = 精选节录
brief excerpt = 简短节录
relevant excerpt = 相关节录
