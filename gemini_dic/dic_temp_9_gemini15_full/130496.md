nonacceptance:/ˌnɒnəkˈsɛptəns/|/ˌnɑːnækˈsɛptəns/| n. |不接受，拒收 |The refusal to accept something;  lack of acceptance.  This can refer to a refusal to accept a proposal, an offer, a payment,  or a person's ideas or behavior. |例句：The company's nonacceptance of our proposal led to the project's cancellation. 公司拒收我们的提案导致项目取消。

The board's nonacceptance of his resignation was a surprise to everyone. 董事会拒不接受他的辞职令所有人都感到意外。|近义词：rejection, refusal, disapproval|反义词：acceptance, approval
