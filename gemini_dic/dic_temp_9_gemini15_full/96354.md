indirect:/ˌɪnˈdaɪrɛkt/ | adj. | 间接的，迂回的 | Not direct; roundabout.  It implies a lack of straightforwardness or a separation from the main source or action.  | 例句：He gave an indirect answer to my question. 他对我提出的问题含糊其辞。| 例句：The indirect route took us through several small towns.  迂回的路线带我们穿过几个小镇。|近义词： circuitous, roundabout, oblique, implicit | 反义词：direct, explicit, straight
