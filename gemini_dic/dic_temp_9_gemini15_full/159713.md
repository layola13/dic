polychloride:/ˌpɒliˈklɔːraɪd/| n. |多氯化物|A compound containing more than one chlorine atom.  Polychlorides are often used in various industrial applications and can be found in different forms, from simple inorganic salts to complex organic molecules.  Their properties vary greatly depending on the specific compound.|Example:  Some pesticides contain polychlorides as active ingredients.|近义词：chlorinated compound |反义词：无
