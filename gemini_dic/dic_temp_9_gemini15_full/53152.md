diplopoda:/dɪˈplɒpədə/| n. |倍足纲动物，千足虫|Diplopoda is a class of myriapods, commonly known as millipedes.  They are characterized by their long, segmented bodies with two pairs of legs per segment (unlike centipedes, which have one pair per segment). They are herbivores or detritivores, feeding on decaying plant matter. | Example:  The child screamed when he saw a diplopoda crawling on the ground.  孩子看到一只千足虫在地上爬，尖叫起来。|近义词：millipede|反义词：centipede
