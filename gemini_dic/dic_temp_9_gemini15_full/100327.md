intersperses:/ˌɪntərˈspɜːrsz/| vt.|散布；点缀；插入|To place at intervals among other things.|The author interspersed anecdotes throughout the biography, making it more engaging. 作者在传记中穿插了一些轶事，使它更引人入胜。|近义词：scatter, distribute, intermix|反义词：concentrate, group


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He intersperses jokes throughout his lectures. = 他在讲座中穿插笑话。
She intersperses flowers among the vegetables in her garden. = 她在花园里将花朵散布在蔬菜之间。

一般过去时:
The teacher interspersed questions into the lesson. = 老师在课堂上穿插提问。
They interspersed songs with readings during the ceremony. = 他们在仪式上将歌曲与朗读穿插进行。

现在完成时:
She has interspersed personal reflections throughout her travelogue. = 她已在游记中穿插了个人感想。
He has interspersed his own opinions into the report. = 他已将自己的观点穿插到报告中。

过去完成时:
They had interspersed the slides with relevant images before the presentation. = 他们在演示文稿之前已经将幻灯片与相关的图像穿插在一起。
She had interspersed humorous comments throughout her serious speech. = 她在严肃的演讲中穿插了一些幽默的评论。

将来时:
He will intersperse examples in his upcoming presentation. = 他将在接下来的演示中穿插例子。
They will intersperse the music with poetry readings at the concert. = 他们在音乐会上将音乐与诗歌朗诵穿插进行。

虚拟语气:
If he interspersed more humor, the speech would be more engaging. = 如果他穿插更多幽默，演讲会更引人入胜。
I wish she had interspersed more visuals in her presentation. = 我希望她在演示文稿中穿插更多视觉效果。

被动语态:
Jokes were interspersed throughout his lecture. =  笑话穿插在他的讲座中。
Flowers are interspersed among the vegetables in the garden. = 花朵散布在花园里的蔬菜之间。

疑问句:
Are images interspersed throughout the report? = 报告中是否穿插了图片？
How often are anecdotes interspersed in the story? = 故事中每隔多久穿插一次轶事？

否定句:
He did not intersperse any jokes in his speech. = 他在演讲中没有穿插任何笑话。
The speaker did not intersperse any personal opinions into the lecture. = 演讲者没有在讲座中穿插任何个人意见。
