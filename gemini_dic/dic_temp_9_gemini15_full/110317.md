lighted:/ˈlaɪtɪd/| adj. |点亮的；被照亮的；充满光亮的| illuminated; shining with light; filled with light |例句：The room was softly lighted with candles.  房间里烛光柔和。

The city was lighted up for the festival.  城市为节日亮起了灯。


近义词：illuminated, lit, bright, glowing | 反义词：dark, unlit, dim


词源:  From the past participle of the verb "light," meaning "to ignite or illuminate."  The word's history traces back to Old English "līhtan," meaning "to shine, lighten."
