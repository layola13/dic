re-dialed:/ˌriːˈdaɪld/| v. |重新拨号|To dial a telephone number again.|例句：I re-dialed the number, but still got no answer. 我重新拨打了号码，但仍然没有得到回应。
I re-dialed the wrong number several times before realizing my mistake. 我在意识到自己的错误之前，重新拨打了错误的号码好几次。|近义词：redial, call back|反义词：disconnect, hang up|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-dials the number every few minutes. = 他每隔几分钟就重新拨打这个号码。
She re-dials frequently when she doesn't get an answer. = 当她没有得到回应时，她经常重新拨打。


一般过去时:
I re-dialed the number after the call dropped. = 电话中断后，我重新拨打了号码。
They re-dialed several times before reaching the correct person. = 他们在联系到正确的人之前重新拨打了多次。


现在完成时:
She has re-dialed the number countless times. = 她已经无数次重新拨打这个号码了。
He has re-dialed that number since this morning. = 从今天早上开始，他就一直在重新拨打那个号码。


过去完成时:
I had re-dialed the number three times before giving up. = 在放弃之前，我已经重新拨打了三次号码。
They had re-dialed many times before someone finally answered.  = 他们在有人最终接听电话之前已经重新拨打了许多次。


将来时:
I will re-dial the number if I don't hear back within an hour. = 如果一小时内没有回音，我会重新拨打号码。
She will re-dial the number tomorrow morning. = 她明天早上会重新拨打这个号码。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the correct number, I wouldn't have re-dialed so many times. = 如果我知道正确的号码，我就不会重新拨打那么多次了。
If she had re-dialed earlier, she might have reached him. = 如果她早点重新拨打，她也许就能联系到他了。


被动语态:
The number was re-dialed repeatedly. = 这个号码被反复重新拨打。
The wrong number was re-dialed by mistake. = 错误的号码被错误地重新拨打。


疑问句:
Have you re-dialed the number yet? = 你已经重新拨打号码了吗？
Will you re-dial the number later? = 你稍后会重新拨打号码吗？


否定句:
I didn't re-dial the number because I was too busy. = 我没有重新拨打号码，因为我太忙了。
She didn't re-dial the number after the first attempt. = 她第一次尝试后没有重新拨打号码。
