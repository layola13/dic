ria:/ˈriːə/| n.|河口湾，三角洲|A ria is a partially submerged river valley that is flooded by the sea.  It is a coastal landform characterized by its drowned river valleys, often with steep sides and a branching, dendritic pattern. Rias are typically found in areas where the land has subsided or the sea level has risen.|Example: The coastline is characterized by numerous picturesque rias.|近义词：estuary, inlet|反义词：无
