previewer:/prɪˈvjuːər/ | n. | 预览器 | A program or tool that allows users to view a preview of a file or document before opening it.  It often displays a smaller version or a summary of the content.  | Example: The image previewer showed a thumbnail of the high-resolution photograph.  预览器显示了高清照片的缩略图。 | 近义词：viewer, displayer | 反义词：N/A
