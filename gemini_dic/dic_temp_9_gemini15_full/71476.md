flamingoes:/fləˈmɪŋɡoʊz/| n. |火烈鸟（复数）|A flamingo is a type of wading bird with long legs, a long neck, and bright pink or reddish feathers. They are known for their distinctive curved beaks, which they use to filter feed on algae and small crustaceans.  Flamingoes often live in large flocks. |Example:  A flock of flamingoes stood gracefully in the shallow water.一群火烈鸟优雅地站在浅水中。

The flamingos took flight in a breathtaking spectacle. 火烈鸟起飞，场面壮观。|近义词：birds |反义词：无

词源:
