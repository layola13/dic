novitiateship: /nəˈvɪʃiətʃɪp/ | n. | 新手期；见习期；初学者阶段 | The state or period of being a novice; probationary period.  |  例句：Her novitiateship in the convent was a time of intense spiritual growth.  她的修道院见习期是精神上快速成长的时期。

例句：He completed his novitiateship and was then fully accepted into the order. 他完成了他的新手期，然后被正式接纳进入教派。


近义词：apprenticeship, probation, traineeship, initiation


反义词：mastership, expertise, veteran status


词性: 名词
