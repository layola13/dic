laypersons:/ˈleɪˌpɜːrsənz/| n.|非专业人士，外行|People who are not experts in a particular field.|例句：The doctor explained the procedure in terms understandable to laypersons.  医生用外行也能理解的方式解释了这个手术过程。

例句：This book simplifies complex scientific concepts for laypersons. 这本书将复杂的科学概念简化，方便非专业人士理解。


近义词：non-experts, amateurs, the public, civilians


反义词：experts, professionals, specialists
