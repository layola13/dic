discontinuer:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuːər/| v. |使中断；停止；终止|To discontinue something means to stop doing it or providing it.|The company discontinued the product due to low sales.  公司因销量低而停产该产品。|The doctor discontinued the medication after a week.  医生一周后停用了这种药。|近义词：cease, terminate, stop, end |反义词：continue, resume, start


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They discontinue the service every night. = 他们每晚都停止这项服务。
The factory discontinues production during weekends. = 工厂在周末停产。


一般过去时:
The project was discontinued due to funding issues. =  由于资金问题，该项目被终止了。
She discontinued her studies after getting married. = 她结婚后停止了学业。


现在完成时:
The government has discontinued the subsidy program. = 政府已经停止了补贴计划。
They have discontinued using that software. = 他们已经停止使用那个软件了。


过去完成时:
The company had discontinued the product before the new regulations were introduced. =  在新规出台之前，该公司已停产该产品。
He had discontinued his exercise routine before his injury. = 他在受伤之前就停止了锻炼。


将来时:
They will discontinue the train service next month. = 下个月他们将停止火车服务。
The school will discontinue the after-school program. = 学校将停止课后辅导项目。


虚拟语气:
If they discontinued the project, we would lose our jobs. = 如果他们终止了项目，我们就会失业。
I wish they hadn't discontinued my favorite show. = 我真希望他们没有停播我最喜欢的节目。


被动语态:
The product was discontinued because of safety concerns. = 该产品因安全问题而被停产。
The service will be discontinued without notice. = 服务将随时停止，恕不另行通知。


疑问句:
Will they discontinue the bus route? = 他们会取消这条公交线路吗？
Has the company discontinued its online store? = 公司已经关闭其在线商店了吗？


否定句:
The company did not discontinue its support for the software. = 公司并没有停止对该软件的支持。
They will not discontinue the service until next year. = 他们直到明年才会停止这项服务。


PHRASE->
discontinue production = 停止生产

discontinue payment = 停止支付
discontinue treatment = 停止治疗
