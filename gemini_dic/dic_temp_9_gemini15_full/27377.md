came-down:/keɪmˈdaʊn/| v.|下来，下降，沮丧，遗传|The word "came-down" is a phrasal verb, meaning its meaning is derived from the combination of the verb "came" and the preposition "down."  It has several meanings depending on context.  It can refer to a physical descent, a decline in something, or a feeling of depression.|例句：The plane came down safely in the field.  飞机安全地降落在田野里。
The price of oil came down after the announcement. 石油价格在公告后下降了。|近义词：descended, dropped, depressed, inherited|反义词：ascended, rose, elevated, elated|词性:动词
