hit-the-wall:/hɪt ðə wɔːl/| v. phr.|撞墙；达到极限；精疲力尽|英文解析：This idiom refers to the point when someone's physical or mental energy is completely depleted, resulting in a sudden decline in performance or ability. It often describes a situation where someone is facing an insurmountable obstacle or feeling overwhelmed by stress and exhaustion.|例句：After three days of non-stop work, she hit the wall and couldn't focus anymore. =  连续工作三天后，她精疲力尽，无法再集中精力。  I hit the wall during my marathon training and had to take a break. = 在马拉松训练中，我精疲力尽，不得不休息一下。|近义词：burn out, reach one's limit, exhaust oneself|反义词：be energized, be refreshed, be revitalized|词性:v. phr.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hits the wall when he tries to solve complex problems. = 当他试图解决复杂问题时，他会感到精疲力尽。
They hit the wall every time they try to implement a new strategy. = 他们每次尝试实施新战略时都会遇到瓶颈。


一般过去时:
She hit the wall during the final exam. = 她在期末考试期间精疲力尽。
We hit the wall after weeks of intense negotiations. = 经过几周的紧张谈判后，我们筋疲力尽。


现在完成时:
They have hit the wall several times during this project. = 在这个项目中，他们已经好几次精疲力尽了。
He has hit the wall with his writing and needs a break. = 他写作遇到了瓶颈，需要休息一下。


过去完成时:
By the time the deadline arrived, they had hit the wall and couldn't meet the requirements. = 到截止日期到来的时候，他们已经精疲力尽，无法满足要求。
She had hit the wall before the presentation, so her performance was not her best. = 演示之前她就精疲力尽了，所以她的表现并不理想。


将来时:
I think I will hit the wall if I continue working at this pace. = 如果我继续以这种速度工作，我想我会精疲力尽的。
They will likely hit the wall if they don't take regular breaks. = 如果他们不定期休息，他们很可能会精疲力尽。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't taken a break, I would have hit the wall during the race. = 如果我没有休息，我就会在比赛中精疲力尽。
If she had known she would hit the wall, she would have planned better. = 如果她知道自己会精疲力尽，她就会更好地计划。


被动语态:
The team was hit by the wall of resistance from management. = 团队受到了管理层阻力的冲击。 (Note:  Passive voice is less common with this idiom)


疑问句:
Did you hit the wall during your training? = 你在训练中精疲力尽了吗？
Will they hit the wall before completing the project? = 他们在完成项目之前会精疲力尽吗？


否定句:
He didn't hit the wall, even after working overtime. = 即使加班，他也没有精疲力尽。
She didn't hit the wall because she managed her time effectively. = 她没有精疲力尽，因为她有效地管理了时间。


PHRASE->
hit a brick wall = 遇到阻碍
hit a snag = 遇到挫折
hit the roof = 勃然大怒
hit the books = 用功读书
hit pay dirt = 找到有价值的东西
