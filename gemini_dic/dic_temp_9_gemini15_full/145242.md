overblame: /ˌoʊvərˈbleɪm/ | vt. |过度责备，责备过度 | To blame someone excessively or unfairly.  |例句：Don't overblame yourself for the accident; it was an unfortunate incident. 不要过度责备自己这场事故；这只是一个不幸的事件。

例句：He felt he was overblamed for the project's failure. 他觉得他对项目的失败被过度责备了。


近义词：criticize harshly, unfairly accuse, unduly censure

反义词：exonerate, pardon, forgive


词性：动词
