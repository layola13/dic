replans:/ˌriːˈplænz/| v.|重新计划，重新规划|To plan again; to make new plans.|例句：We had to replan our vacation due to the hurricane.  我们不得不因为飓风而重新规划我们的假期。|例句：The company replanned its marketing strategy after the product launch failed. 公司在产品发布失败后重新规划了其营销策略。|近义词：revise, redesign, reschedule|反义词：finalize, complete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They replan their project every week. = 他们每周都重新规划他们的项目。
We replan our marketing strategy regularly. = 我们定期重新规划我们的营销策略。


一般过去时:
She replanned her trip after the flight was cancelled. = 她的航班取消后，她重新规划了她的行程。
He replanned his entire business model. = 他重新规划了他的整个商业模式。
