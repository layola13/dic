shivery:/ˈʃɪvəri/ | adj. | 颤抖的；发冷的；令人毛骨悚然的 | Feeling cold and trembling slightly, often because of fear, illness, or cold weather.  It can also describe something that causes such a feeling. | 例句：The icy wind made me feel shivery.  冰冷的风使我感到发冷。  The old house had a shivery atmosphere.  这座老房子有一种令人毛骨悚然的氛围。 | 近义词：trembling, shaking, chilly, creepy, spooky | 反义词：warm, comfortable, calm
