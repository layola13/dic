refined:/rɪˈfaɪnd/| adj.|精致的；优雅的；优良的；提纯的；改进的 |Refined means free from impurities or imperfections; cultured, elegant, or sophisticated. It can refer to something that has been improved or made more precise.  |例句：He has refined tastes in art and music. 他在艺术和音乐方面有高雅的品味。|例句：The process refined the oil, removing impurities. 这个过程提纯了油，去除了杂质。|近义词：polished, cultured, sophisticated, purified, improved |反义词：crude, rough, unrefined, impure, raw |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chef refines his recipes constantly. = 厨师不断改进他的菜谱。
This process refines the metal. = 这个过程提炼金属。


一般过去时:
She refined her technique over many years. = 她多年来不断改进她的技术。
The gold was refined before being used in jewelry. = 黄金在用于珠宝首饰之前经过了提炼。


现在完成时:
They have refined their methods significantly. = 他们已经大大改进了他们的方法。
The company has refined its product line to meet customer demands. = 公司已经改进其产品线以满足客户需求。


过去完成时:
He had refined his argument before presenting it. = 他在陈述他的论点之前已经完善了它。
The scientists had refined their experiment design after the initial results. = 科学家们在最初的结果之后完善了他们的实验设计。


将来时:
We will refine the software before releasing it to the public. = 我们将在向公众发布软件之前改进它。
The engineers will refine the design based on the test results. = 工程师们将根据测试结果改进设计。


虚拟语气:
If they refined their approach, they might succeed. = 如果他们改进他们的方法，他们可能会成功。
I wish I had refined my essay before submitting it. = 我希望我在提交论文之前能润色一下。


被动语态:
The oil is refined in a large refinery. = 油在一个大型炼油厂中被提炼。
The data was refined to eliminate errors. = 数据经过了精炼以消除错误。


疑问句:
Has the design been refined yet? = 设计已经改进了吗？
Will the proposal be refined before presentation? = 提案在提交之前会进行修改吗？


否定句:

The product hasn't been refined enough for market launch. = 产品尚未经过充分改进以投放市场。
