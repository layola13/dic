portcullis:/pɔːrtˈkʌlɪs/| n. |吊桥；闸门；活动闸门 |A heavy, vertically sliding gate, formerly used to defend the entrance to a castle or fortified town.|The portcullis crashed down, sealing off the castle entrance.  吊桥重重落下，封锁了城堡入口。|The heavy portcullis was raised slowly.  沉重的吊桥缓缓升起。|近义词：gate, barrier, drawbridge |反义词：open gate, unguarded entrance |名词
