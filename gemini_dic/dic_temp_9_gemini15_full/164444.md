preenlargements:/ˌpriːɪnˈlɑːrdʒmənts/| n. |预先放大；预先扩大| Enlargements made in advance or before something else.  The term is not standard English and likely a neologism or a misspelling.  It could be interpreted as a combination of "pre-" (before) and "enlargements" (the act of making something larger).  There is no established dictionary definition or common usage.|Example: The preenlargements of the images were necessary for the final presentation. (预先放大的图片对于最终演示是必要的。) |近义词:preliminary enlargements, advance enlargements|反义词: reductions, diminutions|名词
