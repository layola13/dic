/mæskt/ | adj. |蒙面的；遮盖的；伪装的 | Masked refers to something or someone that is covered or hidden, often for concealment or protection.  It can refer to a physical covering, like a mask, or a metaphorical covering, like a hidden identity. |例句：The masked bandit robbed the bank.  蒙面匪徒抢劫了银行。

The masked singer captivated the audience. 蒙面歌手迷住了观众。|近义词：covered, concealed, disguised, hidden |反义词：unmasked, revealed, exposed |形容词
