entitles:/ɪnˈtaɪtəlz/ | vt. |赋予…权利；给…称号；使…有资格 |to give someone the right to do or have something; to give a title to something or someone. |例句：Your membership entitles you to a discount.  你的会员资格使你有权享受折扣。 |例句：The book is entitled "The History of Rome". 这本书名为《罗马史》。|近义词：authorize, empower, qualify |反义词：disqualify, prohibit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This contract entitles you to a free upgrade. = 这份合同赋予你免费升级的权利。
The painting is entitled "Sunrise". = 这幅画名为“日出”。


一般过去时:
The award entitled him to a year's paid leave. = 那个奖项使他有权享受一年的带薪休假。
The film was entitled "The Lost City". = 那部电影名为“失落之城”。


现在完成时:
This achievement has entitled her to international recognition. = 这一成就使她获得了国际认可。
The project has been entitled "Project Phoenix". = 该项目已被命名为“凤凰项目”。


过去完成时:
His prior experience had entitled him to the promotion. = 他之前的经验使他有资格获得晋升。
The manuscript had been entitled "A Midsummer Night's Dream". = 手稿的标题曾是“仲夏夜之梦”。


将来时:
The new law will entitle all citizens to free healthcare. = 新法律将使所有公民有权享受免费医疗。
The upcoming album will be entitled "Echoes of the Past". = 即将来临的专辑将被命名为“过去的回声”。


虚拟语气:
If I had won the lottery, it would have entitled me to a luxurious lifestyle. = 如果我中了彩票，我就能过上奢华的生活。
If the book were entitled differently, it might have sold better. = 如果这本书的标题不同，或许会卖得更好。


被动语态:
He was entitled to a pension after thirty years of service. = 他在服务三十年后有权获得养老金。
The article was entitled "The Impact of Climate Change". = 这篇文章名为“气候变化的影响”。


疑问句:
Does this ticket entitle me to a free drink? = 这张票能让我免费喝一杯吗？
What is the book entitled? = 这本书叫什么名字？


否定句:
This permit does not entitle you to park here. = 这张许可证不允许你在这里停车。
The painting wasn't entitled "Landscape". = 这幅画的名字不是“风景”。


PHRASE->
be entitled to = 有权…
entitled to receive = 有权获得
entitled to claim = 有权索赔
entitled to benefits = 有权享受福利
entitled to compensation = 有权获得赔偿
