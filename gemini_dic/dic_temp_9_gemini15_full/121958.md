missioned:/ˈmɪʃənd/| v. |委任；派遣；承担任务| To assign a task or duty to someone; to give someone a mission.|The general missioned the soldiers to capture the enemy stronghold.将军派遣士兵去攻占敌人的据点。|appoint, assign, delegate|refuse, neglect|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company missions its employees to various projects. = 公司指派员工参与各种项目。
He missions his team to achieve the sales target. = 他委派他的团队去实现销售目标。


一般过去时:
They missioned the young scientist to the Antarctic research station. = 他们派遣这位年轻的科学家前往南极研究站。
The government missioned a special task force to investigate the crime. = 政府委派了一个特别工作组来调查这起犯罪事件。


现在完成时:
The organization has missioned several teams to disaster relief efforts. = 该组织已派遣几支队伍参与救灾工作。
She has been missioned to lead the development of a new product. = 她已被委派领导新产品的开发工作。


过去完成时:
The captain had missioned his crew before the storm hit. = 船长在暴风雨来临之前已下达了任务给他的船员。
They had missioned the team to complete the report by the deadline, but they failed. = 他们曾委派该团队在截止日期前完成报告，但他们失败了。


将来时:
The company will mission a new team to the overseas office. = 公司将派遣一个新的团队前往海外办事处。
They will mission her to negotiate the contract. = 他们将委派她去谈判合同。


虚拟语气:
If they missioned her to the project, she would succeed. = 如果他们委派她负责这个项目，她将会成功。
I wish they had missioned me to that important task. = 我希望他们当时委派我负责那项重要的任务。


被动语态:
The soldiers were missioned to protect the border. = 士兵们被委派去保卫边境。
A special team was missioned to investigate the accident. = 一个特别小组被委派去调查事故。


疑问句:
Were they missioned to complete the task on time? = 他们被委派准时完成任务了吗？
Has she been missioned to lead the project? = 她已被委派领导这个项目了吗？


否定句:
They did not mission anyone to that dangerous area. = 他们没有委派任何人去那个危险区域。
The company hasn't missioned a new sales team yet. = 公司还没有委派一个新的销售团队。
