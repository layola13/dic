patroon:/pəˈtruːn/| n.|领主，地主（在荷兰殖民地纽约）|A patroon was a landowner in the Dutch colonies of New Netherland (present-day New York) who had the right to control a large tract of land and the people who lived on it.  They held significant power and often exerted control over their tenants, similar to feudal lords.|Example: The patroon system in New Netherland gave a few wealthy individuals immense power over large estates and their inhabitants.  领主制度在新荷兰给予少数富人对大庄园及其居民巨大的权力。|近义词：landlord, feudal lord|反义词：tenant, serf|名词
