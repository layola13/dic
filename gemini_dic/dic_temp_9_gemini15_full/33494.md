ciabattas:/tʃiəˈbætəz/| n. |恰巴塔面包|A type of Italian bread characterized by its oblong shape, irregular surface, and slightly chewy texture. It is made with a relatively high hydration dough, resulting in a characteristically open crumb.  |例句：I bought a loaf of ciabattas from the bakery this morning.  今天早上我从面包店买了一条恰巴塔面包。|例句：The ciabattas were perfect for dipping in the olive oil.  恰巴塔面包非常适合蘸橄榄油。|近义词：bread, roll|反义词：无
