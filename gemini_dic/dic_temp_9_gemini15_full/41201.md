coquette:/kəˈkɛt/| n. |轻浮女子，卖弄风情的女人 | A coquette is a woman who flirts and behaves in a way that is intended to attract attention from men. She often uses her charm and beauty to gain admiration or attention, but without serious intentions of forming a lasting relationship.  |She was a charming coquette, attracting the attention of many suitors. 她是一个迷人的轻浮女子，吸引了许多追求者。  |flirt, tease, vamp |prude, modest woman |名词
