iso:/ˈaɪsoʊ/|abbr.|国际标准化组织，国际标准|International Organization for Standardization; relating to or conforming to international standards.|The ISO 9000 series of standards is widely adopted in many industries.  ISO 9000系列标准已被许多行业广泛采用。|近义词：standard, norm|反义词：nonstandard, irregular


USAGE->

This product conforms to ISO standards. = 此产品符合ISO标准。
