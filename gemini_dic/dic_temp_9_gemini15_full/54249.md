disgorging:/dɪsˈɡɔːrdʒɪŋ/ | v. | 呕吐；涌出；(大量)排出 | The act of disgorging something, typically forcefully or in large quantities.  It implies a forceful expulsion or release, often of something unpleasant or unwanted.  |例句：The volcano was disgorging molten rock. 火山正在喷出熔岩。

The dam disgorged a torrent of water. 水坝排放出大量的水流。| 近义词：spewing, vomiting, ejecting, belching | 反义词：absorbing, retaining, swallowing
