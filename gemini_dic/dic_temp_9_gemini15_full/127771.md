negus:/ˈniːɡəs/| n.|尼古斯（埃塞俄比亚皇帝）|A Negus is the title of the emperor of Ethiopia.  The term is derived from the Ge'ez word ንጉሥ (nägüs), meaning "king".  Historically, the title was used for rulers of various kingdoms in the Horn of Africa before being adopted as the imperial title.  In modern usage, it's often used as a general term for a former Ethiopian emperor.|The last Negus of Ethiopia was Haile Selassie I. |近义词：emperor, monarch, sovereign|反义词：subject, commoner|名词
