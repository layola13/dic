employers:/ɪmˈplɔɪərz/ | n. |雇主，老板 |  People or organizations that employ others.  Employers are responsible for providing jobs, wages, and benefits to their employees. They also set workplace policies and manage their workforce. | Example: Many employers offer health insurance to their employees.  许多雇主为其员工提供医疗保险。

The company's employers are committed to creating a positive work environment. 公司的雇主致力于创造积极的工作环境。|近义词：employers, bosses, firms |反义词：employees, workers
