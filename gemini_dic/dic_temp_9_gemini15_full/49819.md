demonstrated:/ˈdɛmənstreɪtɪd/ | v. |证明；展示；示范|To clearly show the existence or truth of something by giving proof or evidence; to show how something works or is done.|例句：The experiment demonstrated the effectiveness of the new drug.  实验证明了这种新药的有效性。
例句：The teacher demonstrated how to solve the equation. 老师示范了如何解这个方程。|近义词：showed, proved, illustrated, exemplified|反义词：concealed, hid, refuted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He demonstrates his skills every day. = 他每天都展示他的技能。
The data demonstrates a clear trend. = 数据显示了一个清晰的趋势。


一般过去时:
She demonstrated her point with examples. = 她用例子证明了自己的观点。
The scientist demonstrated a new theory. = 科学家证明了一个新理论。


现在完成时:
They have demonstrated their commitment to the cause. = 他们已经证明了他们对这项事业的承诺。
Scientists have demonstrated the link between smoking and cancer. = 科学家已经证明了吸烟与癌症之间的联系。


过去完成时:
He had demonstrated his loyalty before. = 他以前就证明了他的忠诚。
The research had demonstrated the potential risks. = 研究已经证明了潜在的风险。


将来时:
She will demonstrate the new software next week. = 她下周将演示新的软件。
The professor will demonstrate the principles of physics. = 教授将演示物理学原理。


虚拟语气:
If he demonstrated more effort, he would succeed. = 如果他付出更多努力，他就会成功。
I wish he had demonstrated more understanding. = 我希望他当时表现出更多的理解。


被动语态:
The theory was demonstrated to be correct. = 该理论被证明是正确的。
The experiment's results were clearly demonstrated. = 实验的结果得到了清晰的证明。


疑问句:
How will you demonstrate your ability? = 你将如何证明你的能力？
Does this example demonstrate the concept? = 这个例子能证明这个概念吗？


否定句:
He did not demonstrate sufficient understanding. = 他没有表现出足够的理解。
The study did not demonstrate any significant effect. = 这项研究没有显示任何显著的效果。


PHRASE->

demonstrate practically =  实际操作演示
