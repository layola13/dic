nonscientifically:/ˌnɑːnˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪkli/ | adv. | 非科学地，不科学地 |  In a manner not based on or conforming to the methods or principles of science.  It often implies a lack of rigorous methodology or evidence. | Example: He nonscientifically concluded that the earth was flat based on his own observations. 他根据自己的观察非科学地得出地球是平的结论。 | 近义词：unscientifically, informally, anecdotally | 反义词：scientifically, rigorously, methodically
