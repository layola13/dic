flatcars:/ˈflætˌkɑːrz/| n. |平板车，敞篷货车 | A flatcar is a railroad car without sides or a top, used for carrying heavy or bulky goods.  |例句：The flatcars were loaded with steel beams. 平板车上装满了钢梁。  The heavy machinery was transported on flatcars.  重型机械是用平板车运输的。|近义词：open wagon, gondola car |反义词：boxcar, covered wagon
