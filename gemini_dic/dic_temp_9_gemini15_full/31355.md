characterizers:/ˈkærəktəraɪzərz/| n. |表征者；特征描述者；刻画者|People or things that describe or define the characteristics of something.  They highlight the defining traits or features. For example, in literature, characterizers could be specific details about a character that reveal their personality. In scientific studies, they might be measurable qualities used to categorize specimens.|The author used vivid imagery and strong dialogue as characterizers to bring the characters to life. 作者运用生动的意象和强烈的对话来刻画人物，使人物栩栩如生。|descriptors, identifiers, indicators, typifiers|non-characterizers

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist's brushstrokes characterize her unique style. = 艺术家的笔触体现了她独特的风格。
These features characterize this particular species of bird. = 这些特征是这种特定鸟类的特点。


一般过去时:
The historian characterized the era as one of great upheaval. = 历史学家将那个时代描述为一个动荡的时代。
His actions characterized him as a selfless individual. = 他的行为把他描绘成一个无私的人。


现在完成时:
Scientists have characterized the new virus as highly contagious. = 科学家已将这种新病毒归类为高传染性病毒。
Her work has characterized the impact of climate change on coastal communities. = 她的作品阐述了气候变化对沿海社区的影响。


过去完成时:
Historians had characterized the previous dynasty as corrupt and decadent. = 历史学家之前已将前朝描述为腐败和堕落的。
Researchers had characterized the substance before proceeding with the experiment. = 研究人员在进行实验前已经对该物质进行了表征。


将来时:
Researchers will characterize the newly discovered compound next year. = 研究人员将在明年对新发现的化合物进行表征。
The study will characterize the effects of the drug on the human body. = 这项研究将描述该药物对人体的影响。


虚拟语气:
If we characterized the problem differently, we might find a better solution. = 如果我们对问题有不同的描述，我们可能会找到更好的解决方案。
I wish we could characterize the situation more accurately. = 我希望我们能够更准确地描述这种情况。


被动语态:
The new material was characterized using advanced techniques. = 新材料是用先进技术进行表征的。
The patient's condition was characterized by severe fatigue. = 病人的病情特点是严重的疲劳。


疑问句:
How would you characterize the relationship between the two countries? = 你如何形容这两个国家之间的关系？
How can we best characterize the impact of social media on society? = 我们如何才能最好地描述社交媒体对社会的影响？


否定句:
The study did not characterize the long-term effects of the treatment. = 这项研究没有描述该治疗的长期影响。
The report failed to adequately characterize the complexity of the issue. = 该报告未能充分描述该问题的复杂性。
