chowders:/ˈtʃaʊdɚz/| n.|杂烩汤，浓汤|A chowder is a type of hearty soup or stew, typically containing seafood, potatoes, and milk or cream.  Different regions have their own variations. |例句：The clam chowder was rich and creamy. 这份蛤蜊杂烩汤浓郁又香醇。

例句：She made a delicious corn chowder for the potluck. 她为聚餐做了一份美味的玉米杂烩汤。


近义词：stew, soup, broth|反义词：None specifically.
