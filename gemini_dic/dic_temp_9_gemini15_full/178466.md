reconstructionary:/ˌriːkənˈstrʌkʃənəri/| adj.|重建的；复兴的；再造的 | Relating to or characteristic of reconstruction.  The adjective refers to the process of reconstructing something, whether it's a building, a society, or a system.  It implies a process of rebuilding or reforming after damage, destruction, or significant change. |例句：The reconstructionary efforts after the hurricane were immense.  飓风后的重建工作非常巨大。

The country embarked on a reconstructionary phase following the civil war.内战后，这个国家开始了重建阶段。


近义词：rebuilding, restorative, rehabilitative

反义词：destructive, ruinous, degenerative
