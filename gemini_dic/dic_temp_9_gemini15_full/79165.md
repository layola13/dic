glamorising: /ˈɡlæməraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 美化；使…具有魅力；渲染… | To represent or depict something in an attractive or idealized way, often exaggerating its positive aspects and ignoring its negative ones. | 例句：The film glamorises violence and drug use. 这部电影美化了暴力和吸毒。

例句：The advertisement glamorises a luxurious lifestyle. 这则广告美化了一种奢华的生活方式。


近义词：romanticizing, idealizing, romanticizing, embellishing, aggrandizing

反义词：denigrating, disparaging, exposing, criticizing, condemning

词性：动词
