priestlike: /ˈpriːstlaɪk/ | adj. |  像牧师的；神圣的；庄严的 | Resembling a priest in appearance, manner, or character; suggesting holiness or solemnity.  |例句：He had a priestlike air of calm and authority. 他有一种像牧师一样平静而权威的气质。

例句：The priestlike silence in the ancient temple was profound. 古庙中神圣的寂静是深沉的。


近义词：clerical, reverend, holy, solemn, devout


反义词：profane, worldly, irreverent, unholy


词性:形容词
