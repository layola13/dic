self-castigates: /ˌsɛlfˈkæstɪɡeɪts/ | vt. |自我惩罚；自我责备 | To punish oneself, often mentally or emotionally, for perceived faults or wrongdoing.  |例句：He self-castigates for every mistake, no matter how small. 他为每一个错误而自我惩罚，无论多么微不足道。

She self-castigates constantly for not achieving her goals. 她不断地因为没有实现目标而自责。


近义词：reproach oneself, berate oneself, chastise oneself, upbraid oneself, blame oneself | 反义词：self-forgive, self-congratulate
