proassessment:/proʊəˈsɛsmənt/ | n. |预评估；前期评估 |The process of evaluating something before a full assessment is conducted.  It often involves a preliminary review of information or a smaller-scale evaluation to determine the need for a more comprehensive assessment. | Example: The proassessment of the project indicated a high likelihood of success, prompting further investment.  预评估项目表明其成功可能性很高，促使进一步投资。|近义词: preliminary assessment, initial evaluation |反义词: final assessment, post-assessment
