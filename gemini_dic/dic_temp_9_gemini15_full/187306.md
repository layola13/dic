rockdusting: /ˈrɒkˌdʌstɪŋ/ | n. |岩石粉尘化；岩石粉碎；施用岩石粉 | The process of grinding rock into a fine powder, often for agricultural purposes.  |  例句：Rockdusting is a sustainable agricultural practice that replenishes soil nutrients.  岩石粉尘化是一种可持续的农业实践，可以补充土壤养分。


例句：The farmer used rockdusting to improve the fertility of his soil.  这位农民用岩石粉尘化来改善土壤肥力。


近义词：rock grinding, rock pulverization, soil amendment


反义词：无直接反义词，但可以考虑与之相反的土壤贫瘠化方法


词性: 名词
