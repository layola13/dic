defriend:/diːˈfrend/| vt.|删除好友，与...绝交|To remove someone from one's list of friends on a social networking site.  This verb is a blend of "de-" (meaning "to remove") and "friend."|例句：I had to defriend my ex on Facebook. 我不得不从Facebook上删除我的前男友。

例句：She defriended him after their argument. 他们争吵后，她把他从好友列表中删除了。


近义词：unfriend, delete, remove, block


反义词：friend, add, befriend


词性：动词
