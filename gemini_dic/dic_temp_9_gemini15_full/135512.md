nonplasticity:/ˌnɒnplæˈstɪsəti/| n. |非塑性；不变形性|The inability of a material to be molded or shaped permanently by pressure or heat.  It refers to the resistance to permanent deformation.  This concept is primarily used in materials science and engineering. | Example: The high nonplasticity of the material made it difficult to work with.  这种材料的高非塑性使其难以加工。|近义词：rigidity, inflexibility, inelasticity|反义词：plasticity, malleability, ductility|
