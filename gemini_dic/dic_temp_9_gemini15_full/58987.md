duty:/ˈduːti/| n.|责任；义务；职务；关税|

英文解析：A duty is something that you feel you must do because it is morally or legally right.  It can also refer to a task or function assigned to someone, or a tax imposed on imported goods.

例句：
It is our duty to protect the environment. = 保护环境是我们的责任。

It's her duty to care for her elderly parents. = 照顾年迈的父母是她的责任。


近义词：responsibility, obligation, task, function, tariff

反义词：neglect, irresponsibility, leisure


词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My duty is to serve my country. = 我的职责是为国效力。
His duty requires him to be on call 24/7. = 他的职责要求他24/7待命。


一般过去时:
His duty was to ensure the safety of the passengers. = 他的职责是确保乘客的安全。
She fulfilled her duty with diligence. = 她勤勤恳恳地履行了自己的职责。


现在完成时:
He has completed his duty. = 他已经完成了他的职责。
We have done our duty to help the victims. = 我们已经尽到了帮助受害者的责任。


过去完成时:
They had already completed their duty before the deadline. = 截止日期前他们已经完成了他们的职责。
She had fulfilled her duty as a citizen. = 她已经履行了作为公民的义务。


将来时:
I will perform my duty to the best of my ability. = 我将尽我所能履行我的职责。
He will have completed his duty by next week. = 他下周将完成他的职责。


虚拟语气:
If I were in his position, I would do my duty. = 如果我处于他的位置，我会履行我的职责。
I wish I had done my duty earlier. = 我希望我早点履行我的职责。


被动语态:
Duties were assigned to each member of the team. = 职责被分配给团队的每个成员。
The tax duty is levied on imported goods. = 进口商品征收关税。


疑问句:
What is your duty here? = 你在这里的职责是什么？
What are the duties of a police officer? = 警官的职责是什么？


否定句:

They failed to perform their duty. = 他们没有履行他们的职责。
