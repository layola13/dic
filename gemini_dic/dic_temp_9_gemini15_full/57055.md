doubting: /ˈdaʊtɪŋ/ | adj. | 怀疑的，不确定的 | Expressing or feeling doubt; uncertain.  | 例句：He cast a doubting glance at the suspicious package. 他怀疑地看了一眼那个可疑的包裹。


例句：Her doubting expression revealed her skepticism about the plan.  她怀疑的表情表露出了她对这个计划的怀疑。


近义词：skeptical, uncertain, hesitant, suspicious, dubious | 反义词：certain, sure, confident, convinced


词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is doubting the validity of the evidence. 他正在怀疑证据的有效性。
They are doubting their ability to finish the project on time. 他们怀疑自己能否按时完成项目。


一般过去时:
She doubted his sincerity. 她怀疑他的真诚。
I doubted whether he would come. 我怀疑他是否会来。


现在完成时:
I have doubted his intentions for a long time. 我很久以来就一直怀疑他的意图。
They have doubted the accuracy of the report. 他们一直怀疑报告的准确性。


过去完成时:
We had doubted his word before, so we were not surprised. 我们以前就怀疑过他的话，所以我们并不感到惊讶。
She had doubted her decision, but it proved to be correct. 她曾经怀疑过自己的决定，但事实证明它是正确的。


将来时:
I will doubt everything he says until I see proof. 在看到证据之前，我会怀疑他说的每一句话。
They will doubt the outcome if they don't get enough data. 如果他们得不到足够的数据，他们将会怀疑结果。


虚拟语气:
If I doubted his honesty, I wouldn't trust him. 如果我怀疑他的诚实，我就不会信任他。
I wish I hadn't doubted my instincts. 我真希望我没有怀疑我的直觉。


被动语态:
His motives were doubted by many people. 他的动机受到了许多人的怀疑。
The results of the experiment were doubted by the scientific community. 实验结果受到了科学界的质疑。


疑问句:
Are you doubting my competence? 你怀疑我的能力吗？
Does anyone doubt the truth of this statement? 有人怀疑这句话的真实性吗？


否定句:
I don't doubt his ability. 我不怀疑他的能力。
They didn't doubt the evidence presented. 他们没有怀疑所提供的证据。

beyond doubt: 毫无疑问
no room for doubt: 没有疑问的余地
in doubt: 怀疑，不确定
