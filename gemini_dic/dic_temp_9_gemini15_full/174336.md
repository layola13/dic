quickens:/ˈkwɪkənz/| v.|加快；活跃；振奋|To make or become quicker or faster; to become more lively or active.|例句：The pace of life quickens in the city. 城市里的生活节奏加快了。|例句：Her pulse quickened with excitement. 她兴奋得心跳加快了。|近义词：accelerates, hastens, speeds up|反义词：slows down, decelerates
