counterresponses:/ˈkaʊntər rɪˈspɑːnsɪz/ | n. | 反驳；反向回应 | Responses that oppose or contradict previous statements or actions.  Counterresponses are often used in debates, negotiations, or in response to criticism.  | Example:  The politician's counterresponses to the journalist's questions were carefully worded to avoid controversy.  政治家对记者问题的反驳措辞谨慎，以避免争议。

|近义词：rebuttals, refutations, rejoinders |反义词：agreements, concessions |词性:noun
