isolates:/ˈaɪsəleɪts/| v. n.|隔离；孤立；分离；提取|To separate something or someone from others; to set apart.|例句：The sick child was isolated to prevent the spread of the disease.  生病的孩子被隔离以防止疾病传播。

例句：Scientists isolated the virus from the infected cells. 科学家从受感染的细胞中分离出病毒。


近义词：separate, segregate, quarantine, seclude|反义词：integrate, unite, combine|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He isolates himself from the outside world. 他把自己与外界隔绝。
The lab isolates the DNA samples. 实验室分离DNA样本。


一般过去时:
They isolated the suspect from the other witnesses. 他们将嫌疑人与其他证人隔离开来。
The researchers isolated a new strain of bacteria. 研究人员分离出一种新的细菌菌株。


现在完成时:
She has isolated herself since the accident. 自事故发生以来，她一直把自己孤立起来。
The team has isolated the cause of the problem.  团队已经找到了问题的根源。


过去完成时:
They had isolated the virus before they started the experiment. 他们在开始实验之前已经分离出病毒了。
He had isolated himself for years before he finally sought help. 他在最终寻求帮助之前已经把自己孤立多年了。


将来时:
The government will isolate the affected area. 政府将隔离受灾地区。
Scientists will isolate the gene responsible for the disease. 科学家们将分离出导致该疾病的基因。


虚拟语气:
If they isolated the problem, they could solve it. 如果他们能找到问题所在，就能解决它。
I wish I could isolate myself from all the noise. 我真希望我能远离所有的噪音。


被动语态:
The patient was isolated in a special ward. 病人被隔离在一个特殊的病房里。
The virus was successfully isolated by the researchers.  病毒被研究人员成功分离出来。


疑问句:
How did they isolate the bacteria? 他们是如何分离细菌的？
Has the infected area been isolated yet? 受感染的地区已经被隔离了吗？


否定句:
They didn't isolate the variable correctly. 他们没有正确地分离变量。
The scientist couldn't isolate the specific gene. 科学家无法分离出特定的基因。
