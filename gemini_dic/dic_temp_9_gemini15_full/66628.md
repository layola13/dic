exist:/ɪɡˈzɪst/| vi.|存在，生存|To occupy a place in space or time; to be real or true.  To continue to live or be in a particular state or condition.|例句：Does life exist on other planets?  生命存在于其他星球上吗？
例句：The problem still exists, despite our efforts. 尽管我们努力了，问题仍然存在。|近义词：live, survive, endure, subsist|反义词：cease, perish, vanish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exists. = 他存在。
We exist in a complex world. = 我们生活在一个复杂的世界里。

一般过去时:
Dinosaurs existed millions of years ago. = 恐龙在数百万年前存在。
The company existed for over fifty years. = 这家公司存在了五十年以上。

现在完成时:
The problem has existed for a long time. = 这个问题已经存在很久了。
Humanity has existed for thousands of years. = 人类已经存在了数千年。

过去完成时:
Before the invention of the wheel, different forms of transportation existed. = 在发明轮子之前，存在着不同形式的交通工具。
The theory had existed for centuries before it was proven. = 这个理论存在了几个世纪才被证实。

将来时:
The project will exist for at least another year. = 这个项目至少还会存在一年。
Hopefully, this peace will exist for many years to come. = 希望和平能持续很多年。

虚拟语气:
If God existed, things would be different. = 如果上帝存在，情况就会不同。
I wish peace existed everywhere. = 我希望和平存在于世界各地。

被动语态:
(Exist is rarely used in the passive voice because it is intransitive.)  (存在很少用于被动语态，因为它是不及物动词)


疑问句:
Does God exist? = 上帝存在吗？
Does this theory still exist? = 这个理论还存在吗？

否定句:
Unicorns do not exist. = 独角兽不存在。
The myth no longer exists. = 这个神话不再存在了。


PHRASE->
exist in = 存在于...
exist on = 依赖于...生存
exist for = 为了...而存在
exist as = 作为...而存在
exist by = 靠...生存
