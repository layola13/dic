pupating:/ˈpjuːpeɪtɪŋ/ | v. |化蛹；处于蛹期 | The process of a larva transforming into a pupa.  This stage is crucial in the insect's life cycle.  | Example: The caterpillar is pupating in its silken cocoon.  毛毛虫正在丝茧中化蛹。  The butterfly is currently pupating, and we expect it to emerge in a few weeks. 蝴蝶目前正在化蛹，我们预计它几周后就会羽化。|近义词：metamorphosing (in the pupal stage) | 反义词：emerging (from pupa)
