/aɪ.æɪ.ɛs/|abbr.|互联网信息服务|Internet Information Services, a group of programs that are used to set up and manage websites on a computer that is connected to the internet.|例句：IIS is a powerful web server that's widely used for hosting websites. IIS是一个功能强大的Web服务器，广泛用于托管网站。|近义词：web server, application server|反义词：无
