reexpelled:/ˌriːɪkˈspɛld/| v.|重新驱逐，再次开除 | To expel again; to drive out a second time.  |例句：The student was reexpelled after violating school rules again.  学生再次违反校规，被再次开除。  |例句：The country reexpelled the diplomat for espionage.  该国因间谍活动而再次驱逐该外交官。|近义词：expel, oust, banish |反义词：admit, welcome
