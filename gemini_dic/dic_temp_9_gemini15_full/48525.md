deesis:/ˈdiːsɪs/| n. |恳求；祈求；祈禱；（尤指）圣餐礼拜仪式| A deesis is a type of religious image, typically an icon, depicting Christ enthroned, flanked by the Virgin Mary and John the Baptist, who are shown in an attitude of supplication or prayer.  The word also refers to the act of supplication or earnest prayer. |Example: The icon displayed a classic deesis, with Mary and John flanking Christ.  这幅图标展示了一个经典的恳求图像，玛利亚和约翰位于基督的两侧。|近义词：supplication, entreaty, prayer |反义词：demand, command
