overfilter:/ˈoʊvərˌfɪltər/| v.|过度过滤，滤除过多|To filter excessively, removing more than is necessary or desirable.  This can refer to removing too much detail, information, or nuance from something.|例句：The image was overfiltered, losing much of its original texture. 图片过度滤镜处理，失去了很多原始纹理。
例句：The editor overfiltered the article, removing important context. 编辑过度过滤了这篇文章，去掉了重要的语境。|近义词：over-process, over-refine, excessively filter|反义词：underfilter, retain
