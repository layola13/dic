reenergizes:/ˌriːˈɛnərˌdʒaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 重新充满活力；使恢复精力 | To give new energy to; revitalize.  |例句：A good night's sleep reenergizes me. =  一夜好觉让我恢复了精力。

例句：The invigorating hike reenergized the entire group. =  令人振奋的远足让整个团队都重新充满了活力。


近义词：revitalize, refresh, reinvigorate, stimulate


反义词：exhaust, drain, deplete


词性：动词

USAGE->

Regular exercise reenergizes the body and mind. = 定期锻炼能使身心充满活力。
