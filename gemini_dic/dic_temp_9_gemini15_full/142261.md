orphaning: /ˈɔːrfənɪŋ/ | v. | 使成为孤儿；遗弃；抛弃 | The act of leaving a child without parents or guardians; the state of being orphaned.  It can also refer to the act of abandoning or neglecting something, leaving it without support or maintenance. |

例句：

The death of both parents orphaned the child. = 父母双亡使这个孩子成了孤儿。

The company's sudden bankruptcy orphaned many projects. = 公司突然破产使许多项目无人问津。


近义词：abandoning, deserting, forsaking, neglecting


反义词：adopting, fostering, supporting, nurturing


词性：动词
