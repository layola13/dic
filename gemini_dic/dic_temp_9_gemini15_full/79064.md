giving-in: /ˈɡɪvɪŋ ɪn/ | v. | 屈服；让步；投降 |  To cease resisting; to surrender; to yield.  It implies a reluctant acceptance of defeat or someone else's will. |


例句：

After days of arguing, he finally gave in to her demands.  = 争论几天后，他最终屈服于她的要求。

The army gave in after a long siege. =  经过长期围攻后，军队投降了。


近义词： surrendering, yielding, submitting, capitulating

反义词： resisting, persevering, holding out, fighting back

词源:

The verb "give in" is a phrasal verb composed of the verb "give" and the adverbial particle "in."  "Give" has Germanic origins and its meaning of yielding or surrendering developed over time.  "In" functions to intensify the meaning of "give," indicating a complete cession or surrender.
