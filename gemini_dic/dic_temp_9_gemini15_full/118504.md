meso:/ˈmɛsoʊ/ | adj. | 中间的；中等的；介于两者之间的 |  Relating to or denoting a middle or intermediate state, position, form, or degree;  intermediate.  It's often used as a prefix in scientific terminology. | 例句：Mesolithic culture represents a transitional period between the Paleolithic and Neolithic eras.  中石器时代文化代表了旧石器时代和新石器时代之间的过渡时期。

例句：The mesothorax is the middle segment of the thorax in insects. 中胸是昆虫胸部的中间部分。


近义词：intermediate, median, middle, average | 反义词：extreme, outer, peripheral

词性：形容词
