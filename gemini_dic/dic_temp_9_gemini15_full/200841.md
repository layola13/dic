slime:/slaɪm/| n. |黏液；泥浆；卑鄙的人|A slimy, viscous substance.  It can refer to a sticky substance, often found in mud or produced by certain organisms, or figuratively, a despicable person.|例句：The snail left a trail of slime. = 蜗牛留下一条黏液痕迹。|例句：He's nothing but slime. = 他不过是个卑鄙小人。|近义词：mud, ooze, muck, filth, scum|反义词：cleanliness, purity
