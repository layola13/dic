hematinic:/ˌhiːməˈtɪnɪk/| n. |造血剂；补血药|A hematinic is a substance that promotes the formation of blood, especially red blood cells.  It is often used in the treatment of anemia.  |Example: Iron supplements are a common type of hematinic used to treat iron-deficiency anemia.  铁补充剂是治疗缺铁性贫血的常用造血剂。|近义词：hemopoietic agent, blood builder|反义词： (没有直接的反义词，但可以考虑与导致贫血的物质相对)

词源:
