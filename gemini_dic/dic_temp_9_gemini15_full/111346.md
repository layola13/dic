livelink:/ˈlaɪvˌlɪŋk/| n. |实时链接，直播链接|A live link is a direct connection to a live event or data stream, allowing real-time access and interaction.  It differs from a pre-recorded or delayed broadcast.|Example: The website provided a livelink to the concert, allowing viewers to watch it live.  该网站提供了一个音乐会的实时链接，让观众可以现场观看。  The research team used a livelink to monitor the experiment's data in real time. 研究小组使用实时链接来实时监控实验数据。|近义词：live stream, real-time connection|反义词：recording, delayed broadcast, offline link|名词
