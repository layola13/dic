overexploits:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsplɔɪts/| v.|过度开发，过度利用 | To exploit to an excessive degree.  |例句：The fishing industry overexploits the oceans, leading to dwindling fish populations.  过度捕捞导致鱼类数量减少。|近义词：overuse, deplete, exhaust |反义词：conserve, protect, preserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company overexploits its workers. = 公司过度剥削其员工。
They overexploit the natural resources. = 他们过度开发自然资源。


一般过去时:
The gold mine was overexploited in the 19th century. = 19世纪金矿被过度开采。
Farmers overexploited the land, leading to soil erosion. = 农民过度耕作土地，导致土壤侵蚀。


现在完成时:
We have overexploited the forest for timber. = 我们过度砍伐森林用于木材。
They have overexploited the aquifer, causing water shortages. = 他们过度开采地下含水层，导致缺水。


过去完成时:
The factory had overexploited its workforce before the strike. = 工厂在罢工前过度压榨员工。
They had overexploited the oil fields before the price collapsed. = 油价暴跌前，他们已经过度开采油田。


将来时:

We will overexploit the resources unless we change our practices. = 除非我们改变我们的做法，否则我们将过度利用资源。


虚拟语气:

否定句:
