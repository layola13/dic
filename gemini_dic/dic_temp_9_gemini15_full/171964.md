purana:/pʊˈrɑːnə/| n. |古经，史诗| A purana is a genre of ancient Indian Sanskrit texts.  They are vast collections of myths, legends, and teachings, often focusing on a particular deity or aspect of Hinduism.  They are considered important sources of Hindu theology, cosmology, and history, although their historical accuracy is debated.  |Example: The Bhagavata Purana is a popular and widely studied Purana.  婆伽梵歌是广为流传并被广泛研究的古经之一。|近义词：epic, legend, scripture |反义词：None  |名词
