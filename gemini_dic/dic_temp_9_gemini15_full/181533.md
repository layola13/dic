reknitting:/ˌriːˈnɪtɪŋ/| v.|重新编织；修补；恢复|The act of knitting something again, often to repair damage or to create a new item from old materials.  It can also refer to the metaphorical process of rebuilding or restoring something.|例句：She spent the weekend reknitting her favorite sweater after a cat attack. 她周末花了时间重新编织她最喜欢的毛衣，因为猫抓坏了它。|例句：The community is reknitting the social fabric after the devastating hurricane.  在破坏性飓风之后，社区正在重建社会结构。|近义词：rebuilding, repairing, mending, restoring|反义词：unraveling, destroying, dismantling|动词

She reknitts her scarves every winter. = 她每年冬天都重新编织她的围巾。
