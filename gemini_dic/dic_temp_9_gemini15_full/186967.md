risers:/ˈraɪzərz/| n.|踏板，升降机，上升部分|The vertical part of a stair between two treads.  Also refers to the vertical supports of a structure, such as in a stage or platform.  In technical contexts, it can refer to something that rises or increases.|例句：The risers on this staircase are quite steep. 这楼梯的踏板很陡。|例句：The stage risers were carefully positioned before the concert.  演出前舞台的升降台被小心地放置好。|近义词：steps, treads, supports|反义词：descenders (in the context of stairs), declines (in the context of something rising)
