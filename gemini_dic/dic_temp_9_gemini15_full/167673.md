prickly: /ˈprɪkli/ | adj. | 刺的；多刺的；易怒的；令人不快的 | Having prickles; covered with prickles or spines.  Characterized by irritability or ill temper. | 例句：The prickly pear cactus has many sharp spines.  仙人掌有很多尖刺。

The prickly comments made the atmosphere uncomfortable.  那些尖刻的评论使气氛变得不舒服。


近义词：thorny, spiny, irritable, touchy, grumpy | 反义词：smooth, pleasant, amiable, agreeable
