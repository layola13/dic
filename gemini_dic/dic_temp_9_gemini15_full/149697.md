The word "palpates" does not exist in standard English.  There is a word "palpate," however.  Therefore, I will analyze "palpate."


palpate: /ˈpælpeɪt/ | vt. | 触诊；触察 | To examine by touch, especially medically. |例句：The doctor palpated her abdomen to check for any abnormalities. 医生触诊了她的腹部以检查是否有任何异常。The nurse palpated the patient's pulse. 护士触诊了病人的脉搏。|近义词：feel, touch, examine |反义词：ignore, neglect |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor palpates the patient's throat. = 医生触诊病人的喉咙。
She palpates the lump on her arm. = 她触诊她胳膊上的肿块。


一般过去时:
The nurse palpated the patient's abdomen yesterday. = 护士昨天触诊了病人的腹部。
He palpated his injured knee. = 他触诊了他受伤的膝盖。


现在完成时:
The doctor has palpated several patients this morning. = 医生今天上午已经触诊了几位病人。
She has palpated the area for tenderness. = 她已经触诊了该区域是否有压痛。


过去完成时:
The surgeon had palpated the tumor before the operation. = 外科医生在手术前已经触诊了肿瘤。
They had palpated the patient's lymph nodes. = 他们已经触诊了病人的淋巴结。


将来时:
The physician will palpate the patient's chest. = 医生将触诊病人的胸部。
She will palpate the area for any swelling. = 她将触诊该区域是否有任何肿胀。


虚拟语气:
If I were a doctor, I would palpate the patient carefully. = 如果我是医生，我会仔细地触诊病人。
I wish I could palpate the mass to determine its consistency. = 我希望我能触诊肿块以确定其硬度。


被动语态:
The patient's abdomen was palpated by the doctor. = 病人的腹部被医生触诊了。
The lump was palpated for size and consistency. = 肿块被触诊以确定其大小和硬度。


疑问句:
Did the doctor palpate the patient's heart? = 医生触诊了病人的心脏吗？
Will you palpate the patient's neck? = 你会触诊病人的颈部吗？


否定句:
The doctor did not palpate the patient's lungs. = 医生没有触诊病人的肺部。
She did not palpate the area because it was too painful. = 她没有触诊该区域，因为太疼了。


PHRASE->
palpate the abdomen = 触诊腹部
palpate the pulse = 触诊脉搏
palpate the lymph nodes = 触诊淋巴结
palpate for tenderness = 触诊压痛
palpate carefully = 小心地触诊


词源:  From Latin *palpāre "to stroke, touch gently."  The word reflects the gentle, exploratory nature of the medical procedure.
