peacockisms:/ˈpiːkɒkɪzəm/| n.|炫耀的言行；卖弄学问；华而不实的话|Ostentatious or showy language or behavior, often intended to impress; pretentious displays of knowledge or erudition.|例句：His speech was full of peacockisms, designed to impress rather than inform.  他的演讲充满了炫耀的辞藻，目的是为了给人留下深刻印象而不是传达信息。


例句：Her Instagram feed is nothing but peacockisms – carefully curated photos and boastful captions. 她的Instagram信息流全是炫耀——精心策划的照片和夸夸其谈的标题。


近义词：ostentation, showing off, pretentiousness, bombast, grandiloquence|反义词：modesty, humility, simplicity, understatement
