overpackaging:/ˌoʊvərˈpækɪdʒɪŋ/ | n. |过度包装 |The excessive use of packaging materials for a product. This often leads to increased waste and environmental concerns.  |例句：The overpackaging of toys is a major contributor to landfill waste.  玩具的过度包装是垃圾填埋场垃圾的主要来源。|近义词：excess packaging, superfluous packaging |反义词：minimal packaging, eco-friendly packaging
