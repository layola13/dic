slipcovering:/ˈslɪpˌkʌvərɪŋ/| n. |套罩；套椅套|The act of covering furniture with slipcovers, or the slipcovers themselves.  Slipcovers are removable fabric coverings for furniture, typically sofas, chairs, and ottomans. They protect the furniture from wear and tear, and can also be used to change the look of a room. |例句：She bought new slipcovers to freshen up the living room. 她买了新的沙发套来使客厅焕然一新。

例句：The slipcovering of the antique chairs preserved their beauty.  给古董椅子套上套罩，保护了它们的美丽。


近义词：cover, upholstery (in some contexts) |反义词：uncovering, baring (in the context of furniture) |词性：名词
