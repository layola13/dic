flub:/flʌb/| vt. n.|弄糟；拙劣地处理；错误；失误|To make a mistake in; to bungle.  A mistake; a blunder.|例句：He flubbed his lines in the play. 他在剧中把台词说错了。|例句：That was a terrible flub in judgment. 那是一个可怕的判断失误。|近义词：bungle, botch, mess up, screw up|反义词：perfect, succeed
