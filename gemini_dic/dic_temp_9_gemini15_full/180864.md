rehinging:/ˌriːˈhɪndʒɪŋ/ | v. |重新安装铰链；重新调整；重新考虑 | The act of reattaching or replacing hinges;  the process of reconsidering or refocusing.  | Example: Rehinging the cabinet door was a simple fix.  重新安装橱柜门上的铰链是一个简单的修复。 The company is rehinging its marketing strategy. 公司正在重新调整其营销策略。 |近义词：re-fitting, readjusting, reconsidering |反义词：removing, dismantling
