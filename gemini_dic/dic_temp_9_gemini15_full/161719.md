powdry:/ˈpaʊdri/ | adj. | 粉状的；粉末状的；有粉末的 | Resembling powder; consisting of or covered with powder; soft and fine like powder.  | 例句：The cake had a lovely powdery sugar coating.  这个蛋糕有一层可爱的糖粉。


The makeup had a powdery finish. 这款化妆品有粉末状的妆效。


近义词：dusty, flaky, pulverized | 反义词：coarse, rough, grainy | 词性:形容词


USAGE->

The surface is powdery. = 表面是粉状的。
Her skin feels powdery. = 她的皮肤感觉很细腻，像涂了粉一样。


一般过去时:
The mixture became powdery. = 混合物变成了粉状的。

The cake had been powdery before it was iced. = 蛋糕在涂糖霜之前是粉状的。
