disables:/dɪsˈeɪbəlz/ | vt. |使残疾；使无效；禁止 | To deprive (someone) of the use of a limb or bodily function; to make (something) ineffective; to prohibit. | 例句：The accident disabled him for life.  事故使他终身残疾。 | 例句：The virus disabled the computer system. 病毒使电脑系统瘫痪。 | 近义词：cripples, incapacitates, invalidates | 反义词：enables, empowers


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The virus disables many computer systems. = 病毒使许多电脑系统瘫痪。
He disables the alarm system every night. = 他每晚都禁用报警系统。


一般过去时:
The storm disabled the power lines. = 暴风雨使电力线路瘫痪。
The doctor disabled his leg to stop the spread of infection. = 医生切除了他的腿以阻止感染扩散。


现在完成时:
The accident has disabled him. = 事故使他残疾了。
Years of overwork have disabled his ability to concentrate. = 多年的过度工作已经削弱了他集中精力的能力。


过去完成时:
The heavy rain had disabled the communication system before the rescue team arrived. = 大雨在救援队到达之前已经使通讯系统瘫痪。
He had disabled the security cameras before entering the building. = 他在进入大楼之前已经禁用了安全摄像头。


将来时:
The new software will disable the old security protocols. = 新软件将禁用旧的安全协议。
If the engine overheats, it will disable the entire system. = 如果发动机过热，它将使整个系统瘫痪。


虚拟语气:
If the software were disabled, we would have no way to access the data. = 如果软件被禁用，我们将无法访问数据。
I wish the government would disable those harmful websites. = 我希望政府能禁用那些有害的网站。


被动语态:
The car was disabled by a flat tire. = 汽车因为轮胎爆胎而无法行驶。
The network was disabled by a hacker. = 网络被黑客攻击而瘫痪。


疑问句:
Does this button disable the function? = 这个按钮是否禁用该功能？
What would disable the security system? = 什么会使安全系统瘫痪？


否定句:
The new system does not disable the old one. = 新系统不会禁用旧系统。
The law did not disable the right to protest. = 法律并没有禁止抗议的权利。


PHRASE->
disable access = 禁用访问
disable function = 禁用功能
disable device = 禁用设备
disable user = 禁用用户
disable feature = 禁用功能
