catering:/ˈkeɪtərɪŋ/| n. |餐饮服务；承办酒席|The provision of food and drink, typically at a social event or for a business.  Catering often involves planning, preparing, serving, and cleaning up after a meal or event.|例句：The company provides catering services for weddings and corporate events. 这家公司为婚礼和公司活动提供餐饮服务。|例句：We hired a catering company for our daughter's wedding. 我们为女儿的婚礼雇佣了一家餐饮公司。|近义词：provisioning, food service|反义词：self-service


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company caters for large events. = 这家公司承办大型活动。
We cater to our clients' needs. = 我们满足客户的需求。


一般过去时:
They catered the wedding reception last weekend. = 他们上周末承办了婚礼招待会。

现在完成时:
They have catered many successful events. = 他们已经承办了许多成功的活动。
We have catered to this market for years. = 我们多年来一直服务于这个市场。


过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
