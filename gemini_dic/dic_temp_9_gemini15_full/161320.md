postponers:/pəʊstˈpoʊnərz/ | n. | 拖延者；延期者 | People who habitually postpone or delay tasks or actions.  They tend to procrastinate and avoid responsibilities. | Example: The project was delayed because of the postponers on the team.  这个项目因为团队中的拖延者而被耽搁了。|近义词：procrastinators, delayers, deferrers | 反义词：prompt people, efficient people
