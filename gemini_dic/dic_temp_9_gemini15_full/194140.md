self-initiative:/ˌsɛlfɪˈnɪʃətɪv/| n. |主动性，自主性 |The quality or instance of taking action without being told to do so; proactive behavior.  It suggests a willingness to identify problems and find solutions independently, demonstrating self-reliance and responsibility. |Example:  Her self-initiative in solving the complex problem impressed her manager.  她主动解决复杂问题的能力给她的经理留下了深刻印象。|近义词：proactiveness, self-reliance, resourcefulness |反义词：passivity, dependence, inertia |名词
