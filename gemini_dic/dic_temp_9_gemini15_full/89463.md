honey: /ˈhʌni/ | n. |蜂蜜；亲爱的；甜心 | A sweet, viscous liquid made by honeybees from nectar.  It is used as a food and sweetener.  The term is also used as a term of endearment. |例句：The honey was delicious on the toast.  蜂蜜涂在吐司上很好吃。

例句：He called his wife "honey" affectionately. 他亲切地称呼他的妻子为“亲爱的”。|近义词：sweetener, darling, sweetheart |反义词：bitter, unpleasant
