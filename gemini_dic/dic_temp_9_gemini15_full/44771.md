crumple:/ˈkrʌmpl/| vt. & vi. |弄皱；弄褶；坍塌；萎缩|To become wrinkled or creased; to crush or compress into wrinkles or folds.  |例句：The newspaper crumpled in his hand. 报纸在他手中揉皱了。
The car crumpled on impact. 汽车撞击后严重变形。|近义词：crush, wrinkle, crease, fold |反义词：smooth, straighten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often crumbles the bread into the soup. 他经常把面包弄碎放到汤里。
The old man crumbles easily under pressure.  这位老人很容易在压力下垮掉。


一般过去时:
She crumpled the letter and threw it away. 她把信揉成一团扔掉了。
The building crumpled during the earthquake. 建筑物在地震中倒塌了。


现在完成时:
The leaves have crumpled in the frost. 叶子在霜冻中枯萎了。
The economy has crumpled under the weight of debt. 经济在债务的重压下崩溃了。


过去完成时:
The car had crumpled completely before the rescue team arrived.  救援队到达之前，汽车已经完全变形了。
He had crumpled under the intense questioning. 他在严厉的盘问下崩溃了。


将来时:
The leaves will crumple and fall in autumn. 秋天树叶会枯萎飘落。
The building will likely crumple if another earthquake hits. 如果再次发生地震，这座建筑很可能会倒塌。


虚拟语气:
If he were to lose this case, his reputation would crumple. 如果他输了这场官司，他的名誉将会受损。
I wish my dreams hadn't crumpled before my eyes. 我真希望我的梦想没有在我眼前破灭。


被动语态:
The paper was crumpled and thrown in the bin. 纸被揉成一团扔进了垃圾桶。
The car was completely crumpled in the accident. 汽车在事故中被撞得完全变形。


疑问句:
Did the box crumple during shipping? 箱子在运输过程中变形了吗？
Does the fabric crumple easily? 这布料容易起皱吗？


否定句:
The fabric didn't crumple; it held its shape well. 这布料没有起皱；它保持了很好的形状。
The building didn't crumple despite the strong winds.  尽管风很大，但建筑物没有倒塌。
