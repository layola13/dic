entrepot:/ˈɒntrəpəʊ/ /ˈɑːntrəpoʊ/| n. |仓库；转运站；贸易中心|A warehouse, especially one where goods are stored before being re-exported.  An entrepot is a location, typically a port city, that serves as a central trading hub for goods from different regions.|Example: Singapore was once a major entrepot for trade between East and West.  新加坡曾经是东西方贸易的主要转运站。|近义词：warehouse, depot, trading post, hub|反义词：consumer market, origin point|名词
