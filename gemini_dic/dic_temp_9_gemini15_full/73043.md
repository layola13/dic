footing:/ˈfʊtɪŋ/| n.|立足点，基础，地位，步态|The foundation or base upon which something rests; a person's position or standing; the way someone walks.|例句：The company is on a solid footing financially.  这家公司财务状况良好。

例句：He walked with a steady footing. 他步伐稳健。


近义词：base, foundation, position, standing, gait|反义词： instability, insecurity, uncertainty


词源：源自古英语fotung，意为“脚踏，足迹”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He maintains a strong footing in the industry. 他在行业中保持着牢固的地位。
The building's footing is secure. 这座建筑的地基很稳固。


一般过去时:
She lost her footing and fell. 她失去平衡摔倒了。
The company's footing was weakened by the scandal. 这家公司的声誉因丑闻而受损。


现在完成时:
They have gained a strong footing in the market. 他们在市场上站稳了脚跟。
The project has a solid footing now. 这个项目现在基础稳固了。


过去完成时:
Before the storm, they had already secured their footing. 暴风雨来临前，他们已经站稳了脚跟。
The team had lost its footing in the competition by then. 到那时，球队在比赛中已经失去了优势。


将来时:
We will have a better footing in the negotiations next week. 下周我们在谈判中将会有更有利的立场。
The company hopes to gain a firm footing in the Asian market in the coming years.  该公司希望在未来几年内在亚洲市场站稳脚跟。


虚拟语气:
If we had a better footing, we could negotiate a better deal. 如果我们的立场更好，我们就能谈成更好的交易。
I wish I had a stronger footing in my career. 我希望我的职业生涯能有更稳固的基础。


被动语态:
Their footing was undermined by the opposition. 他们的地位被反对派削弱了。
The building's footing is being inspected. 建筑的地基正在接受检查。


疑问句:
What is the footing of this argument? 这个论点的基础是什么？
Is the company's financial footing secure? 这家公司的财务状况稳固吗？


否定句:
The team doesn't have a strong footing in the league. 这支球队在联赛中没有稳固的地位。
The argument lacks a solid footing.  这个论据缺乏坚实的基础。


PHRASE->
