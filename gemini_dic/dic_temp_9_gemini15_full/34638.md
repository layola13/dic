clementines:/ˌklɛmənˈtiːnz/| n. |小柑橘，柑橘的一种|A clementine is a small, sweet citrus fruit, a hybrid of a mandarin orange and a sweet orange.  It's known for its easy-to-peel skin and juicy segments.|例句：I bought a bag of clementines for a snack. 我买了一袋小柑橘当零食。|例句：Clementines are a popular fruit during the winter holidays.  柑橘在冬季假期期间很受欢迎。|近义词：mandarin oranges, tangerines|反义词：无

词源:
The word "clementine" is named after Father Clément Rodier, a French missionary, who is credited with introducing the fruit to the public in the early 20th century.  The fruit itself is a hybrid, likely a natural cross between a mandarin orange and a sweet orange.
