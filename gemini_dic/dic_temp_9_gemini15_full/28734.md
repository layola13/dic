carnally:/ˈkɑːrnəli/| adj.|肉体的；世俗的；淫荡的| Relating to the flesh or the body; sensual; lustful.  The word emphasizes the physical or bodily aspects, often in a sexual or sensual context.  It can have both neutral and negative connotations depending on the context.|例句：He had a carnally satisfying meal. 他吃了一顿令人身心愉悦的饭。

例句：Their relationship was purely carnally based. 他们的关系纯粹是基于肉体的。

近义词：bodily, physical, sensual, lustful, fleshly

反义词：spiritual, intellectual, chaste, pure
