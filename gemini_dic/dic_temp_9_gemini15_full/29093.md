caryophyllaceous:/ˌkæriəˈfɪleɪʃəs/| adj.|石竹科的，像石竹的|Belonging to or characteristic of the Caryophyllaceae, a family of flowering plants that includes carnations, pinks, and chickweeds.  The term often describes plants with opposite leaves, swollen nodes, and typically five-petaled flowers.|Example: The caryophyllaceous flora of the alpine meadows was stunning.|近义词：pink-like (in some contexts)|反义词：无
