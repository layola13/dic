ftp:/ˈɛfˌtiːˈpiː/| n. |文件传输协议|File Transfer Protocol, a standard network protocol used to transfer computer files between a client and a server on a computer network.|例句：We use FTP to upload files to the server. 我们使用FTP将文件上传到服务器。|近义词：SFTP, SCP|反义词：无


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, standard verb tenses are not applicable.  The usage focuses on its function as a protocol.


PHRASE->
FTP server:  FTP服务器
FTP client: FTP客户端
FTP upload: FTP上传
FTP download: FTP下载
FTP address: FTP地址
