compendia:/kəmˈpɛndiə/| n. |摘要；概略；文摘集 |A compendium is a collection of concise but comprehensive information or works on a particular subject.  It's a summary or a collection of summaries.  |例句：This compendium of essays offers a good overview of the topic. 这本论文集对主题提供了很好的概述。|例句：The book is a compendium of facts and figures about the war. 这本书是关于战争的事实和数字的汇编。|近义词：summary, digest, anthology, compilation |反义词：expansion, detail
