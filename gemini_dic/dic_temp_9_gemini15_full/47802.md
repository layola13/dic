decarbonate:/ˌdiːkɑːrˈbeɪt/| vt.|脱碳；除去碳酸盐|To remove carbon dioxide or carbonates from something.  The process often involves heating or chemical treatment.|例句：The water was decarbonated to remove dissolved carbon dioxide.  水经过脱碳处理以去除溶解的二氧化碳。

例句：The limestone was decarbonated in a high-temperature kiln. 石灰石在高温窑中进行脱碳处理。


近义词：decarbonize, degas|反义词：carbonate
