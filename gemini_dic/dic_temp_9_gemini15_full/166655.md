presents:/ˈprɛzənts/ | n., v. |礼物；现在；呈现；介绍 |to give something to someone as a gift; the current time; to formally introduce something or someone; to show or offer something for consideration.  |例句：She presents her work with confidence. 她自信地展示她的作品。  He presents a compelling argument. 他提出了一个令人信服的论点。|近义词：gifts, offerings, introduces, displays |反义词：takes away, hides, conceals |名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He presents his findings every Monday. = 他每周一都会展示他的研究结果。
She presents a cheerful face to the world. = 她对世界展现出一张快乐的脸庞。


一般过去时:
They presented the award to the winner. = 他们把奖颁给了获奖者。
He presented his case to the judge. = 他向法官陈述了他的案情。


现在完成时:
She has presented her research at several conferences. = 她已经在几次会议上展示了她的研究成果。
He has presented many arguments against the proposal. = 他已经提出了许多反对该提案的论点。


过去完成时:
They had presented their arguments before the debate began. = 在辩论开始之前，他们已经陈述了他们的论点。
She had presented the evidence before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，她已经提交了证据。


将来时:
He will present his new project next week. = 他下周将展示他的新项目。
She will present the award at the ceremony. = 她将在典礼上颁奖。


虚拟语气:
If he presented more evidence, they would believe him. = 如果他提供更多证据，他们就会相信他。
I wish she had presented her ideas more clearly. = 我希望她能更清晰地表达她的想法。


被动语态:
The award was presented to the best student. = 奖项颁给了最好的学生。
The proposal will be presented to the board next month. = 该提案将于下月提交给董事会。


疑问句:
What will you present at the conference? = 你将在会议上展示什么？
Who presented the report yesterday? = 昨天是谁提交的报告？


否定句:
He did not present any evidence to support his claim. = 他没有提供任何证据来支持他的说法。
They did not present the case clearly enough. = 他们没有足够清晰地陈述案情。


PHRASE->
present tense = 现在时
present company excepted = 在座的各位除外
at present = 目前
for the present = 目前
present oneself = 出席；到场
