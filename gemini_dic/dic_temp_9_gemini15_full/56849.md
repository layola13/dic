dorsals:/ˈdɔːrsəlz/ | n. |背部，背侧的 | Relating to the back or posterior part of an organism or structure.  The term often refers to fins on the back of a fish or other aquatic animal, or to the spinal column in vertebrates. |Example: The fish's dorsals were a striking blue. 这条鱼的背鳍是醒目的蓝色。  The doctor examined the patient's dorsals for any signs of injury. 医生检查了病人的背部是否有任何受伤迹象。 |近义词：spinal, posterior |反义词：ventral
