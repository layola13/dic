nonrepentant:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈpɛntənt/| adj.| 拒不悔改的；执迷不悟的 |Unrepentant means showing no regret for one's actions or beliefs.  It describes someone who stubbornly refuses to acknowledge wrongdoing or change their behavior. |例句：He remained nonrepentant about his actions, even after being found guilty. 他即使被判有罪，也对自己的行为拒不悔改。  The nonrepentant criminal continued his life of crime after serving his sentence.  这位拒不悔改的罪犯刑满释放后继续犯罪。|近义词：unrepentant, impenitent, remorseless, unremorseful|反义词：repentant, remorseful, regretful

词源:
The word "nonrepentant" is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "repentant" (meaning "feeling or expressing remorse for one's past misdeeds").  "Repentant" itself comes from the Latin word "paenitens," the present participle of "paenitere," meaning "to repent."
