shape:/ʃeɪp/ | n. v. |形状，外形；塑造，影响 |The shape of the mountain is like a pyramid. 这座山形状像金字塔。|近义词：form, figure, mold |反义词：formlessness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cake is round. = 蛋糕是圆的。
The sculptor shapes the clay. = 雕塑家塑造粘土。


一般过去时:
The vase was broken into many shapes. = 花瓶被打碎成许多形状。
She shaped the dough into a loaf. = 她把面团塑造成一条面包。


现在完成时:
The wind has shaped these rocks over millions of years. = 风在数百万年的时间里塑造了这些岩石。
They have shaped their future through hard work.  = 他们通过努力工作塑造了自己的未来。


过去完成时:
The artist had shaped the clay before the kiln fired. = 艺术家在窑炉烧制之前已经塑造好了粘土。
He had shaped his arguments carefully before the debate. = 他在辩论之前仔细地构思了他的论点。


将来时:
The architect will shape the building's design. = 建筑师将塑造建筑的设计。
We will shape our future together. = 我们将共同塑造我们的未来。


虚拟语气:
If I were to shape my life differently, I would travel more. = 如果我要改变我的生活方式，我会多旅行。
I wish I had shaped my career path differently. = 我希望我当初能选择不同的职业道路。


被动语态:
The clay is being shaped by the artist. = 粘土正在被艺术家塑造。
The future is shaped by many factors. = 未来是由许多因素塑造的。


疑问句:
What shape is the earth? = 地球是什么形状的？
How do you shape your ideas? = 你如何塑造你的想法？


否定句:
The clouds were not shaped like animals. = 云朵不像动物。
He didn't shape his argument effectively. = 他没有有效地论证他的观点。
