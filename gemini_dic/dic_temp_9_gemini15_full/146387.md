overflowable: /ˌoʊvərˈfloʊəbəl/ | adj. | 可溢出的，可泛滥的 | Capable of overflowing; liable to overflow.  Describes something that can exceed its capacity and spill over its boundaries.  | 例句：The river is overflowable during the rainy season.  雨季时这条河容易泛滥。

The container is overflowable if you add too much liquid.  如果你添加过多的液体，容器就会溢出。


近义词：overfull, brimming, brimming over, liable to overflow | 反义词：impervious, sealed, leakproof


词性: 形容词
