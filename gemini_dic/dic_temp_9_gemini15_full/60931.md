elevated:/ˈɛləveɪtɪd/| adj.| 高高的；提高的；崇高的；兴奋的 | Raised to a higher level;  exalted in rank, character, or status;  being in a state of heightened emotion. |例句：The elevated train runs above the street. 高架列车在街道上方行驶。

例句：His elevated position in the company gave him considerable influence. 他在公司的高层职位赋予了他相当大的影响力。


近义词：raised, heightened, lofty, superior, exalted |反义词：depressed, lowered, humble, base


词源:  From Middle English *elevat, from Old French elever  "to raise, lift up", from Latin levare "to lift, raise".  The "-ed" suffix indicates the adjectival form.
