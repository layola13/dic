lobola:/ləˈboʊlə/| n.|彩礼，聘礼|Bridewealth;  the payment of goods or money by a prospective husband to his bride's family as part of a marriage ceremony.  It's a significant custom in many Southern African cultures.  The amount and type of lobola vary greatly depending on the specific culture and family.|Example:  The groom's family paid a substantial lobola to the bride's family.|近义词：bride price, dowry (though dowry is paid by the bride's family)|反义词：无
