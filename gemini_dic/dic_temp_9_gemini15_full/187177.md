roadworthiest:/ˈroʊdˌwɜːrðiɪst/|/ˈroʊdˌwɜːrðiɪst/| adj.| 最适于上路的；状况最好的 | Most suitable or fit for use on the road; in the best condition for road use.  This word is the superlative form of "roadworthy".|

The car, after extensive repairs, is now roadworthiest in the fleet.  这辆车经过大修后，现在是车队中最适合上路的。

The roadworthiest vehicles passed the inspection easily.  状况最好的车辆轻松通过了检查。


近义词：fittest, most suitable, in top condition

反义词：unroadworthy, unfit, dilapidated


词性：形容词
