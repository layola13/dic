clip-clops: /klɪpˈklɑːps/ | n. |嘚嘚的声音，马蹄声 | The sound of horses' hooves hitting the ground.  It's onomatopoeia, imitating the sound. | Example:  We heard the clip-clops of horses' hooves approaching. 我们听到马蹄声越来越近。  The clip-clops of the carriage faded into the distance.  马车嘚嘚的声音渐渐远去。 | 近义词：hoofbeats, clatter | 反义词：silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The horse's hooves make clip-clops on the pavement. = 马蹄在人行道上发出嘚嘚的声音。
We can hear clip-clops coming from the stable. = 我们能听到马厩里传来的嘚嘚声。

一般过去时:
The clip-clops of the carriage echoed through the streets. = 马车的嘚嘚声在街道上回响。
I heard the clip-clops as the rider passed by. = 骑手经过时，我听到了嘚嘚声。

现在完成时:
The clip-clops have stopped; the horses must have arrived. = 嘚嘚声停了，马一定到了。
We have heard those clip-clops many times before. = 我们以前多次听到过这种嘚嘚声。

过去完成时:
The clip-clops had ceased before we reached the house. = 我们到达房子之前，嘚嘚声就已经停止了。
By the time I looked out, the clip-clops had already faded away. = 当我向外看时，嘚嘚声已经消失了。

将来时:

The clip-clops will announce their arrival. = 嘚嘚声将预示着他们的到来。


虚拟语气:
