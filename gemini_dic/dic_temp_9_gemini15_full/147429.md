overnutrition: /ˌoʊvərnuˈtrɪʃən/ | n. |营养过剩，营养过多 | The state of having consumed more nutrients than the body requires, leading to potential health problems.  | Example:  Overnutrition, like malnutrition, can have serious health consequences. 营养过剩，就像营养不良一样，会带来严重的健康后果。|近义词：hypernutrition |反义词：malnutrition, undernutrition
