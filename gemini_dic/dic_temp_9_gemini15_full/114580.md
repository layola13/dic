malaysians:/məˈleɪʒən/| n. |马来西亚人|The word "Malaysians" refers to the people of Malaysia, a country in Southeast Asia.  It is a collective noun, referring to the diverse population of Malaysia, encompassing various ethnic groups including Malays, Chinese, Indians, and others.|There are millions of Malaysians who celebrate diverse cultures and traditions.|近义词：Malaysian people|反义词：无
