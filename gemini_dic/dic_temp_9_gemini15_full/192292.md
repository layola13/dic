screwballs:/ˈskruːbɔːlz/| n.|疯子；古怪的人；滑稽剧|Someone who is eccentric, unconventional, or crazy.  The term often implies a humorous or endearing quality to their eccentricity. It can also refer to a type of comedy film featuring such characters.|例句：He's a real screwball, always doing unexpected things. 他是个十足的疯子，总是做一些出乎意料的事情。

例句：That movie was a screwball comedy; it was full of wacky characters and situations. 那部电影是一部疯狂喜剧；充满了古怪的角色和场景。


近义词：eccentric, oddball, goofball, nut, crank|反义词：normal, conventional, sane

词性:名词
