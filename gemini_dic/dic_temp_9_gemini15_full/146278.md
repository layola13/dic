overfamous: /ˌoʊvərˈfeɪməs/ | adj. | 过度出名的，名气太大的 |  Describes someone or something that is famous to an excessive or undesirable degree; often implying a negative connotation, suggesting the fame is undeserved or has negative consequences. |  例句：He's overfamous for a reality TV star; his talent doesn't match his public profile. 他作为一个真人秀明星过于出名了；他的才能与他的公众形象不相符。  | 近义词：notoriously famous, infamously known, excessively popular | 反义词：obscure, unknown, underappreciated | 词性：形容词
