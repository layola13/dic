noncoplanar:/ˌnɒnkəʊˈpleɪnər/| adj. |非共面的|Not lying in the same plane.  This term is typically used in geometry and describes points, lines, or planes that do not share a common plane.  For example, three points that do not lie on the same straight line are noncoplanar. |The vertices of a tetrahedron are noncoplanar.|近义词:non-coplanar, not coplanar|反义词:coplanar
