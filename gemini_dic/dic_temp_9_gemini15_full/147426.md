overnursed:/ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrst/ | adj. |过度护理的，护理过度的 |  Describes a patient receiving more medical care than is necessary or beneficial, potentially leading to negative consequences. This can involve excessive testing, interventions, or hospitalizations. | The patient was overnursed, leading to increased stress and a longer recovery time.  |  overmedicated, overtreated | undernursed, neglected |
