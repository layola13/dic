resudation:/ˌriːsjuːˈdeɪʃən/| n. |再渗出，再分泌|The process of sweating again or the reappearance of sweat after it had subsided. It often refers to the re-emergence of fluids, such as sweat or sap, after a period of dryness or cessation.  |例句：The resudation of sweat after a period of rest was noticeable.  休息一段时间后，汗水的再次渗出很明显。

The resudation of sap from the wounded tree was a sign of its resilience. 受伤的树木再次渗出树液是其生命力的标志。|近义词：exudation, sweating, oozing |反义词：absorption, drying
