criss-cross:/ˈkrɪskrɒs/| adj., v.| 交叉的；纵横交错的 |  Describes something arranged or occurring in a pattern of intersecting lines; to move or arrange in a criss-cross pattern. |例句：The streets criss-crossed the town.  街道纵横交错地穿过小镇。

The farmer criss-crossed the field, inspecting his crops. 农民在田里来回走动，检查他的庄稼。|近义词：intertwined, intersecting, interwoven |反义词：parallel, straight, aligned |形容词，动词
