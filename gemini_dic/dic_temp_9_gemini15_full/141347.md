operand:/ˈɒpərænd/| n. |操作数，运算对象 |A quantity on which a mathematical operation is performed.  For example, in the equation 2 + 3 = 5, 2 and 3 are operands. | Example:  In the calculation 5 * 2, 5 and 2 are the operands.  在计算 5 * 2 中，5 和 2 是操作数。


The addition of two operands results in their sum.  两个操作数相加的结果是它们的和。


近义词:argument, parameter | 反义词:operator


词源:From Old French *operant (present participle of operer "to work"), from Latin operari "to work".  最终源于拉丁语operari，意为“工作”。
