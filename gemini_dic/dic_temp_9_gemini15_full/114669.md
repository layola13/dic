maliciously:/məˈlɪʃəsli/| adj. |恶意地；恶毒地 |In a way that is intended to cause harm|例句：He maliciously spread rumors about his colleague. 他恶意散布关于同事的谣言。  例句：She maliciously destroyed his reputation. 她恶意毁坏他的名誉。|近义词：spitefully, wickedly, malevolently|反义词：benignly, kindly, innocently|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He maliciously gossips about others. = 他恶意地搬弄是非。
They maliciously damage property. = 他们恶意损毁财产。

一般过去时:
She maliciously lied to the police. = 她恶意地向警方撒谎。
He maliciously broke the window. = 他恶意地打破了窗户。

现在完成时:
They have maliciously interfered in our business. = 他们恶意干涉了我们的业务。
She has maliciously spread false information. = 她恶意散布了虚假信息。

过去完成时:
He had maliciously planned the whole thing. = 他早已恶意策划了整件事。
They had maliciously sabotaged the project. = 他们早已恶意破坏了这个项目。

将来时:
They will maliciously accuse him. = 他们将会恶意指控他。
She will maliciously try to ruin his life. = 她将会恶意地试图毁掉他的生活。

虚拟语气:
If he were maliciously inclined, he would have done it. = 如果他心怀恶意，他就会这么做了。
I wish they hadn't acted so maliciously. = 我真希望他们没有那样恶意行事。

被动语态:
His reputation was maliciously damaged. = 他的名誉被恶意损毁了。
The evidence was maliciously withheld. = 证据被恶意隐瞒了。

疑问句:
Did he maliciously intend to harm her? = 他恶意地想要伤害她吗？
Was the damage maliciously inflicted? = 损害是恶意造成的吗？

否定句:
He did not maliciously intend to hurt anyone. = 他并没有恶意地想要伤害任何人。
She did not maliciously spread rumors. = 她没有恶意地散布谣言。


PHRASE->
maliciously damage =恶意损坏
maliciously spread =恶意散播
maliciously injure =恶意伤害
maliciously persecute =恶意迫害
maliciously defame =恶意诽谤

词源:
malicious + -ly
malicious 来自于拉丁语 malus (bad) + -cious (having the quality of).  -ly 是副词后缀。
