leisurewears: /ˈlɛʒərˌwɛrz/ | n. | 休闲服 | Clothing designed for relaxation and informal activities.  Leisurewear is comfortable and often casual in style, suitable for wearing at home, during leisure activities, or for casual outings.  It contrasts with formal wear or work attire. | Example:  She changed into her leisurewear after work. 她下班后换上了休闲服。


近义词: casual wear, loungewear, sportswear (depending on the specific type of leisurewear) | 反义词: formal wear, business attire


词性: noun
