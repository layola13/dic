immersive:/ɪˈmɜːrsɪv/ | adj. | 身临其境的，沉浸式的 |  Providing an experience that is so vivid and engaging that one feels completely surrounded by it.  It creates a sense of being fully present in a different environment or situation. | 例句：The virtual reality game offered an immersive experience, making players feel like they were actually in the game world.  虚拟现实游戏提供了身临其境的体验，让玩家感觉自己身处游戏世界中。

The new museum exhibit is designed to be immersive, using interactive displays and sensory elements.  新的博物馆展览旨在提供沉浸式体验，使用互动式显示屏和感官元素。


近义词：engrossing, captivating, absorbing, enveloping | 反义词：superficial, shallow, detached
