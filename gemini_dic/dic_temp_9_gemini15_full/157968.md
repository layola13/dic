planimetry:/plænˈɪmɪtri/| n. |平面测算；平面几何学 |The branch of geometry that deals with plane figures.  It involves measuring areas and perimeters of two-dimensional shapes.  |例句：Planimetry is a fundamental concept in surveying and engineering.  平面测算是测量和工程学中的一个基本概念。


例句：The architect used planimetry to determine the floor area of the building. 建筑师用平面测算来确定建筑物的楼层面积。


近义词：plane geometry, area measurement


反义词：stereometry (solid geometry)


词性: 名词
