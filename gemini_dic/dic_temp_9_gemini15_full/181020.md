reimprisons:/ˌriːɪmˈprɪzənz/| v.|再次监禁；重新关押 |To imprison someone again after they have been released from prison.  This word combines "re-" (again) and "imprisons" (the present tense of the verb "to imprison").|例句：He was reimprisoned for violating his parole. 他因为违反假释而再次被监禁。


例句：After being released, he was quickly reimprisoned for a similar crime.  他获释后，很快又因类似罪行再次入狱。


近义词：re-incarcerate, recommit, reincarcerate


反义词：release, free, liberate


词性：动词
