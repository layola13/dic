palindromes:/ˈpælɪnˌdroʊmz/ | n. | 回文；回文词 | A palindrome is a word, phrase, number, or other sequence of characters which reads the same backward as forward, such as madam or racecar.  Palindromes are often considered a type of wordplay or puzzle. | Example: "Madam, I'm Adam" is a famous palindrome.  “Madam, I'm Adam” 是一个著名的回文。| 近义词:reversible words, mirror words | 反义词:  None (the concept is inherently self-reversing)
