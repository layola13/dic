selvaged:/ˈsɛlvɪdʒd/| adj.|已缝边的；抢救的；| salvaged refers to something that has been rescued or saved from damage or destruction. It can also describe fabric that has its selvage edge intact. |例句：The firefighters salvaged some valuable paintings from the burning building. 消防员从燃烧的建筑物中抢救出一些珍贵的绘画作品。The salvaged wood was used to build a new fence. 抢救出来的木材被用来建造新的栅栏。|近义词：recovered, rescued, reclaimed |反义词：lost, ruined, destroyed
