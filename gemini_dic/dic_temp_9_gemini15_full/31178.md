changed:/tʃeɪndʒd/ | v. |改变，变化|The verb "changed" is the past tense and past participle of the verb "change," indicating that an alteration or transformation has occurred. It describes a shift from one state or condition to another.  |例句：The weather changed dramatically. 天气发生了巨大的变化。

The situation has changed significantly since last year.  自从去年以来情况发生了显著的变化。|近义词：altered, modified, transformed, shifted|反义词：remained, stayed, persisted|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weather changes frequently. = 天气经常变化。
He changes his mind often. = 他经常改变主意。

一般过去时:
The company changed its logo last year. = 公司去年更换了标志。
She changed her clothes before going out. = 她出门前换了衣服。

现在完成时:
They have changed their plans. = 他们已经改变了计划。
Everything has changed since I last saw you. = 自从我上次见到你以来，一切都变了。

过去完成时:
He had changed his mind before I could convince him. = 在我劝说他之前，他已经改变了主意。
The situation had changed drastically by the time we arrived. = 当我们到达时，情况已经发生了巨大的变化。

将来时:
The company will change its strategy next quarter. = 公司将在下个季度改变其战略。
I think he will change his behavior soon. = 我认为他很快就会改变行为。

虚拟语气:
If he changed his mind, he would be happier. = 如果他改变主意，他会更快乐。
I wish I had changed my major earlier. = 我真希望我早点换专业。

被动语态:
The rules were changed last month. = 规则在上个月被更改了。
The schedule is being changed. = 时间表正在被更改。

疑问句:
Has anything changed? = 有什么变化吗？
Did the plan change? = 计划改变了吗？

否定句:
Nothing has changed. = 什么都没变。
He didn't change his answer. = 他没有改变他的答案。
