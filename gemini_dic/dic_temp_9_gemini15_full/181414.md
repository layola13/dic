reiterated:/ˌriˈɪtəreɪtɪd/| v.| 重申；反复地说；再提；重做 | To say something again or repeatedly.  |例句：The president reiterated his commitment to economic reform. 总统重申了他对经济改革的承诺。
例句：She reiterated her request for a pay raise. 她再次请求加薪。|近义词：repeat, restate, reaffirm |反义词：retract, deny


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reiterates his point every meeting. = 他每次会议都重申他的观点。
They reiterate their support for the project. = 他们重申他们对该项目的支持。

一般过去时:
The teacher reiterated the instructions. = 老师重申了指示。
She reiterated her apology. = 她再次道歉。


现在完成时:
He has reiterated his position several times. = 他已经多次重申他的立场。
The government has reiterated its commitment to peace. = 政府已经重申了其对和平的承诺。

过去完成时:
She had reiterated her concerns before the meeting. = 在会议之前，她已经重申了她的担忧。
They had reiterated their opposition to the plan. = 他们之前已经重申了他们对该计划的反对。

将来时:
He will reiterate his arguments in the debate. = 他将在辩论中重申他的论点。
She will reiterate her request for information. = 她将再次请求提供信息。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would reiterate my request. = 如果我是你，我会再次提出我的请求。
I wish he would reiterate the key points. = 我希望他会重申要点。

被动语态:
His concerns were reiterated by several colleagues. = 他的担忧被几位同事重申了。
The statement was reiterated by the spokesperson. = 发言人重申了这一声明。

疑问句:
Will you reiterate the question, please? = 请你重述一下问题好吗？
Did he reiterate his offer? = 他重申了他的提议了吗？

否定句:
He did not reiterate his previous statement. = 他没有重申他之前的声明。
She refused to reiterate her accusations. = 她拒绝重申她的指控。
