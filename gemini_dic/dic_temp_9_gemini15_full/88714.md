hobbyist:/ˈhɒbiɪst/ /ˈhɑːbiɪst/| n. |业余爱好者，嗜好者 |A person who engages in a hobby. Someone who enjoys and participates in a pastime or activity for pleasure rather than professional reasons.  |He's a keen hobbyist, spending his weekends building model airplanes. 他是一位热衷的业余爱好者，周末都用来制作模型飞机。  She's a talented hobbyist photographer. 她是一位才华横溢的业余摄影爱好者。|enthusiast, amateur, devotee |professional, expert


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He collects stamps as a hobbyist. = 他作为业余爱好者收集邮票。
She paints landscapes as a hobbyist. = 她作为业余爱好者画风景画。

一般过去时:
He was a dedicated hobbyist in his younger days. = 他年轻时是一位专注的业余爱好者。
She enjoyed knitting as a hobbyist last year. = 去年她喜欢作为业余爱好者织毛衣。

现在完成时:
He has been a birdwatching hobbyist for many years. = 他多年来一直是观鸟爱好者。
She has completed several intricate crochet projects as a hobbyist. = 她作为业余爱好者已经完成了几个复杂的钩针编织项目。

过去完成时:
He had been a model train hobbyist before he took up photography. = 在他开始摄影之前，他曾是模型火车爱好者。
She had already established herself as a talented pottery hobbyist before she opened her shop. = 在开店之前，她就已经是一位有天赋的陶艺爱好者了。

将来时:
He will continue to be a passionate hobbyist in his retirement. = 他退休后将继续成为一名充满激情的业余爱好者。
She will become a more serious hobbyist in her free time. = 她将利用空闲时间成为一名更认真的业余爱好者。

虚拟语气:
If I were a hobbyist, I would try woodworking. = 如果我是一位业余爱好者，我会尝试木工。
I wish I had more time to be a hobbyist. = 我希望我有更多时间做业余爱好。

被动语态:
Many hobbyists are inspired by online tutorials. = 许多业余爱好者受到在线教程的启发。
