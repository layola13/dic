estrepement:/ˈɛstrɪpɪmənt/| n.|毁坏财产；破坏性行为|The act of committing waste on property, especially by a tenant.  It often involves actions that damage or diminish the value of the property.  This is a legal term, and the specific actions considered "estrepement" can vary by jurisdiction.|例句：The court issued an injunction to prevent the tenant from committing further estrepement on the property. = 法院发布禁令，以防止承租人进一步破坏该房产。|例句：The landlord accused the tenant of estrepement after discovering significant damage to the building.|房东在发现建筑物严重损坏后指控租客犯有毁坏财产罪。|近义词：waste, spoliation, damage, destruction|反义词：preservation, protection, repair
