intervening:/ˌɪntərˈviːnɪŋ/| adj.| 干预的；介入的；中间的 | Coming between; situated between; lying between.  |例句：The intervening years saw many changes.  中间的几年发生了很多变化。|例句：The police intervened to prevent a fight. 警察介入阻止了一场斗殴。|近义词： mediating, mediating, interceding |反义词： unrelated, unconnected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is intervening in the economy. = 政府正在干预经济。
This process intervenes between the two stages. = 这个过程介于这两个阶段之间。

一般过去时:
He intervened in the argument. = 他干预了这场争论。
A period of drought intervened between the two harvests. = 两次收割之间有一段干旱期。

现在完成时:
Several factors have intervened to complicate the situation. = 几个因素的介入使情况复杂化。
The intervening years have brought many advancements. = 这些年带来了许多进步。

过去完成时:
Before the police intervened, the situation had escalated. = 在警察介入之前，情况已经升级了。
It had intervened several times before this incident. = 在此事件之前，它已经介入过几次了。

将来时:
The army will intervene if necessary. = 必要时军队将进行干预。
A long period of rain is expected to intervene between the two heat waves. = 预计两波热浪之间将有一段长时间的降雨。

虚拟语气:
If only someone had intervened sooner, the tragedy could have been avoided. = 如果有人早点介入，悲剧本来是可以避免的。
I wish the court had intervened on my behalf. = 我希望法院能代表我进行干预。

被动语态:
The dispute was intervened by a neutral party. = 纠纷由中立方进行了调解。
The situation was intervened in by the authorities. = 当局介入处理了这种情况。

疑问句:
Did anyone intervene during the fight? = 在打斗过程中有人干预吗？
What factors intervened to cause the delay? = 哪些因素导致了延误？

否定句:
The teacher did not intervene in the students' argument. = 老师没有干预学生的争论。
No one intervened to stop the violence. = 没有人介入制止暴力。
