payphone:/ˈpeɪfoʊn/| n.|付费电话，投币式电话|A public telephone that requires payment, typically coins, to make a call.  |例句：I had to use a payphone because my phone was dead. 我不得不使用付费电话，因为我的手机没电了。|例句：There are fewer payphones on the streets these days. 如今街上付费电话越来越少了。|近义词：public telephone, call box|反义词：mobile phone, cellphone
