meads:/miːdz/| n.|草地；牧场；麦田；(尤指)蜜酒|Meads are areas of grassland, often used for grazing livestock or producing hay.  Meads can also refer to a type of alcoholic beverage made from fermented honey.|例句：The sheep grazed peacefully in the sun-drenched meads.  绵羊在阳光普照的草地上和平地吃草。


The children played happily in the flower-filled meads. 孩子们在鲜花盛开的草地上快乐地玩耍。|近义词：pasture, meadow, field|反义词：forest, desert, urban area|名词
