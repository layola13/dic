commentarial:/ˌkɒmənˈteəriəl/| adj.|评论性的；注释的；解说的|Relating to or characteristic of commentary; explanatory or interpretative.  It often refers to writings that explain or interpret a text.|例句：The book includes a comprehensive commentarial section explaining the difficult passages. 这本书包括一个全面的评论部分，解释了难懂的章节。 The professor's lecture provided a commentarial overview of the novel. 教授的讲座提供了对这部小说的评论性概述。|近义词：explanatory, interpretive, expository|反义词：uninterpreted, unexplicated
