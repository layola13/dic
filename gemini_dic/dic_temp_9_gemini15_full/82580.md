guardedness: /ˈɡɑːrdɪdnəs/ | n. |谨慎；戒心；防范；提防 |

英文解析：The state or quality of being guarded; cautiousness or wariness.  A feeling of suspicion or distrust that makes someone reluctant to share information or trust others.


例句：

Her guardedness prevented her from forming close relationships.  她的谨慎使她无法建立亲密关系。

His guardedness around strangers was understandable given his past experiences.  考虑到他过去的经历，他对陌生人的戒心是可以理解的。


近义词：caution, wariness, suspicion, reserve, reticence, mistrust


反义词：openness, frankness, trust, candor


词性：名词
