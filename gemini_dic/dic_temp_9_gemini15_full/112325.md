loreless:/ˈlɔːrləs/ | adj. |缺乏知识的；无知的；无传说故事的| Lacking lore or traditional knowledge; unlearned; lacking in legends or stories. |例句：The loreless generation struggles to understand the ancient customs.  = 缺乏传统知识的一代人难以理解古老的习俗。

例句：The museum's collection was sadly loreless, containing only artifacts without any accompanying stories. = 博物馆的收藏令人遗憾地缺乏传说故事，只有一些没有附带故事的文物。


近义词：ignorant, unlearned, uncultured, uninformed

反义词：learned, knowledgeable, erudite, well-informed
