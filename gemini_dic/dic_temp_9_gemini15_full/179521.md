reejecting: /ˌriːˈdʒɛktɪŋ/ | v. | 再次拒绝；重新拒绝 |  Rejecting again; refusing a second time.  It implies a previous rejection and a renewed refusal. |

例句：

The committee reejected his proposal after further review. = 委员会在进一步审查后再次否决了他的提案。

After being reejected by several publishers, she finally found a home for her manuscript. = 在被几家出版社再次拒绝后，她终于找到了她的手稿的出版商。


近义词：refusing again, denying again, rejecting anew


反义词：accepting, approving


词性：动词
