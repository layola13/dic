fanfiction:/ˈfænˌfɪkʃən/| n. |同人小说|Fictional stories written by fans of a particular work of fiction, often featuring characters and settings from that original work.  Fanfiction is a creative outlet for fans, allowing them to explore their favorite characters in new situations and storylines. It's often shared online through websites and platforms dedicated to fanfiction. |Example:  She writes Harry Potter fanfiction in her spare time. 她空闲时间写哈利·波特同人小说。|近义词：fan fiction, fan-made story|反义词：canonical story (official story)
