pronationalism: /ˌproʊˌnæʃənəlɪzəm/ | n. | 国家主义倾向；地方保护主义 |  A tendency to favor one's own nation, often to the detriment of others; prioritizing national interests above all else;  a form of nationalism focusing on regional or local interests.  | Example:  The politician's pronouncements revealed a strong strain of pronationalism, prioritizing domestic industries even at the cost of international trade agreements.  | 近义词: nationalism, protectionism, parochialism | 反义词: internationalism, globalism |  n.
