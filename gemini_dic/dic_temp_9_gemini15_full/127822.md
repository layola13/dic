nematocalyx:/ˌnɛmətoʊˈkeɪlɪks/| n. |线虫萼 | a botanical term referring to a calyx (the sepals of a flower) that is thread-like or worm-like in appearance.  It describes a calyx with slender, elongated sepals.  | Example: The *nematocalyx* of the rare orchid was a striking feature, its thin, wiry sepals extending far beyond the petals.  线虫萼这种稀有兰花的显著特征是其细长、坚韧的花萼远远超过花瓣。|近义词:  filiform calyx (thread-like calyx)|反义词:  broad calyx, robust calyx|名词
