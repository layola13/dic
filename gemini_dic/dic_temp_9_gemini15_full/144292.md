outspied:/ˌaʊtˈspaɪd/| v.| 侦察到；秘密监视到 |To secretly watch or observe (someone or something) for information.  This word implies a successful act of spying, unlike simply "spying".| 例句：The enemy's movements were outspied by our intelligence agents. = 我们的情报人员侦察到了敌人的行动。

The rival company’s new product design was outspied by our team. = 我们团队侦察到了竞争对手的新产品设计。| 近义词：discover secretly, detect, observe secretly, spy on | 反义词：overlook, miss, ignore |
