cragsman:/kræɡzmən/| n. |崖壁工人，攀岩者|A person who works on or around crags, often involved in activities like rock climbing or quarrying.  They are skilled in navigating and working in difficult and dangerous terrain.|例句：The cragsman expertly scaled the sheer cliff face. 崖壁工人熟练地攀登陡峭的悬崖峭壁。
The experienced cragsman secured the loose rocks before continuing his work.经验丰富的崖壁工人固定了松动的岩石后才继续工作。|近义词：climber, rock climber, quarryman|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who avoids heights or dangerous work.
