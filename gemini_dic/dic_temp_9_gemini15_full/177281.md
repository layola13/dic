reassigns:/ˌriːəˈsaɪnz/| vt.|重新分配，重新指派|To assign something again or to someone else.|例句：The manager reassigned the tasks to different team members.  经理将任务重新分配给了不同的团队成员。

例句：The court reassigned the case to a different judge. 法院将案件重新指派给了另一位法官。


近义词：reascribe, redistribute, reallocate|反义词：retain, keep


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reassigns projects based on employee skills. = 公司根据员工技能重新分配项目。
He reassigns tasks frequently to improve efficiency. = 他经常重新分配任务以提高效率。


一般过去时:
