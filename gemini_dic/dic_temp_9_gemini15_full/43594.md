creamery:/ˈkriːməri/| n.|乳制品厂；奶油制品店|A creamery is a place where butter, cheese, and other dairy products are made or sold.  It can be a factory-scale operation or a smaller, local shop.|例句：The local creamery makes award-winning cheddar cheese. 当地乳制品厂生产屡获殊荣的切达干酪。|例句：We bought some delicious ice cream from the creamery downtown. 我们在市中心的乳制品店买了一些美味的冰淇淋。|近义词：dairy, dairy farm, cheese factory|反义词：无|词性:名词
