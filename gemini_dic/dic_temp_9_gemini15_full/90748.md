hummingbirds:/ˈhʌmɪŋbɜːrdz/ | n. |蜂鸟 |Small, brightly colored birds with long, slender bills and wings that beat so rapidly they produce a humming sound. They are known for their hovering flight and ability to feed on nectar. |Example:  Hummingbirds are fascinating creatures known for their vibrant colors and incredible flight abilities.  蜂鸟是令人着迷的生物，以其鲜艳的色彩和令人难以置信的飞行能力而闻名。

The hummingbird zipped between the flowers, its wings a blur.  蜂鸟在花丛中穿梭，翅膀模糊不清。|近义词: none (unique creature)|反义词: none (unique creature)
