character:/ˈkærəktər/ | n. | 性格；人物；字符；特性 | A character is a person in a story or play.  A person's character refers to their qualities or personality traits.  It can also refer to a symbol or letter. | 例句：The main character in the novel is a detective.  这部小说的主人公是一位侦探。 | 例句：He has a strong character and never gives up easily. 他性格坚强，从不轻易放弃。 | 近义词：personality, person, symbol, attribute | 反义词：N/A
