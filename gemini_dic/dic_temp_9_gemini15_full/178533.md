reconverting:/ˌriːkənˈvɜːrtɪŋ/| v.|重新转换，再转换|The process of converting something back to its original state or a different form.  This often involves a reversal of a previous conversion process.|例句：The company is reconverting the old factory into a modern office space. 公司正在将旧工厂改造成现代化的办公空间。

例句：They are reconverting the digital files back into analog format. 他们正在将数字文件重新转换为模拟格式。


近义词：retransforming, reconverting, re-processing|反义词：converting (in a different direction), transforming (into a different form permanently)
