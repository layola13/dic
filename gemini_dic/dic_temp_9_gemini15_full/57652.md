drawable:/ˈdrɔːəbl/|/ˈdrɑːəbl/| adj.| 可绘制的，可绘画的，可提取的 |Something that is drawable can be drawn or depicted.  It can also refer to something that can be retrieved or accessed, especially from a resource such as a database or a resource file. |The landscape was beautifully drawable. 这幅风景画得非常漂亮。|The image is drawable from the app's resources. 这张图片可以从应用资源中提取。|paintable, depictable |undrawable |形容词


USAGE->

This software allows you to draw various shapes. = 这个软件允许你绘制各种形状。
