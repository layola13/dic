posit:/ˈpɒzɪt/ /ˈpɑːzɪt/| vt. n.|假定；断定；提出；认为；安置|To assume as a fact; to put forward as a proposition; to put or place.|The scientist posited a new theory about the origin of the universe.  科学家提出了一个关于宇宙起源的新理论。|He posited the existence of a hidden force. 他假定存在一种隐藏的力量。|assume, propose, suggest|deny, refute|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He posits a new theory. = 他提出一个新理论。
They posit that the earth is round. = 他们认为地球是圆的。

一般过去时:
She posited a different explanation. = 她提出了不同的解释。
The professor posited a challenging question. = 教授提出一个具有挑战性的问题。

现在完成时:
Scientists have posited many theories about black holes. = 科学家们已经提出了许多关于黑洞的理论。
Researchers have posited a link between diet and disease. = 研究人员已经假设饮食与疾病之间存在联系。

过去完成时:
They had posited that the results would be different. = 他们之前认为结果会不同。
Before the experiment, they had posited several hypotheses. = 实验之前，他们已经提出了几个假设。

将来时:
He will posit a new argument in his next paper. = 他将在他的下一篇文章中提出一个新的论点。
They will posit a solution to the problem. = 他们将提出解决这个问题的方法。

虚拟语气:
If he posited a different scenario, the outcome might be different. = 如果他假设不同的情况，结果可能会有所不同。
I wish he had posited a more realistic solution. = 我希望他提出一个更现实的解决方案。

被动语态:
A new theory was posited by the researchers. = 研究人员提出了一种新理论。
The existence of dark matter was posited by many scientists. = 许多科学家都假设存在暗物质。

疑问句:
What did the author posit in his book? = 作者在他的书中提出了什么？
What hypothesis did they posit for the experiment? = 他们为实验提出了什么假设？

否定句:
He did not posit a clear answer. = 他没有给出明确的答案。
The study did not posit a direct causal relationship. = 这项研究没有假设直接的因果关系。


PHRASE->
posit a hypothesis = 提出一个假设
posit a question = 提出一个问题
posit an argument = 提出一个论点
posit a solution = 提出一个解决方案
posit a theory = 提出一个理论
