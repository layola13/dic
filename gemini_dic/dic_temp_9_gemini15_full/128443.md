news:/nuːz/| n. |新闻，消息 |Information about recent events, especially as reported by the media.|例句：I heard some interesting news today. 我今天听到了一些有趣的消息。|近义词：information, report, tidings |反义词：silence, secrecy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The news is good. = 消息很好。
The news comes from many sources. = 新闻来自多个来源。

一般过去时:
The news was shocking. = 这个消息令人震惊。
The news spread quickly. = 消息迅速传播。

现在完成时:
The news has been confirmed. = 消息已经得到证实。
The news has changed everything. = 这个消息改变了一切。

过去完成时:
The news had already broken before I arrived. = 我到达之前消息就已经传开了。
We had heard the news before they announced it officially. = 在他们正式宣布之前，我们就已经听到这个消息了。

将来时:
The news will be broadcast later. = 新闻稍后播出。
We will know the news soon. = 我们很快就会知道消息。

虚拟语气:
If the news were true, I would be very surprised. = 如果消息是真的，我会非常惊讶。
I wish the news were better. = 我希望消息更好些。

被动语态:
The news was reported on television. = 新闻在电视上报道了。
The news is being discussed widely. = 这个新闻正在被广泛讨论。

疑问句:
What's the news? = 有什么新闻？
Is there any good news? = 有什么好消息吗？

否定句:
There is no good news. = 没有好消息。
The news wasn't true. = 消息不是真的。
