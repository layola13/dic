resistance:/rɪˈzɪstəns/| n.|抵抗，阻力，反抗|The act of opposing or withstanding something.  It can refer to physical force, opposition to a political regime, or the opposition of a material to the flow of electricity. |例句：The soldiers put up strong resistance against the enemy.  士兵们对敌人进行了顽强的抵抗。

例句：The resistance to change within the company is significant. 公司内部对变革的阻力很大。


近义词：opposition, defiance, obstruction, reluctance

反义词：compliance, submission, acquiescence, acceptance


词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bridge shows strong resistance to earthquakes. = 这座桥梁展现出强大的抗震能力。
He offers resistance to the new policy. = 他抵制这项新政策。


一般过去时:
The army faced fierce resistance during the invasion. = 军队在入侵期间遭遇了激烈的抵抗。
She showed resistance to his advances. = 她对他的追求表现出抗拒。


现在完成时:
The people have shown considerable resistance to the government's reforms. = 人民对政府的改革表现出相当大的抵制。
They have offered no resistance to the arrests. = 他们没有对逮捕有任何抵抗。


过去完成时:
The rebels had offered little resistance before surrendering. = 反叛者在投降前几乎没有抵抗。
The building had shown remarkable resistance to the fire. = 这座建筑物对火灾展现出非凡的抵抗力。


将来时:
The government anticipates strong resistance to the proposed tax increase. = 政府预计对提议的税收增加会有强烈的抵制。
We expect the material to offer high resistance to corrosion. = 我们预计这种材料将具有很高的耐腐蚀性。


虚拟语气:
If there were less resistance, progress would be faster. = 如果阻力较小，进步就会更快。
I wish there were less resistance to my ideas. = 我希望我的想法能少一些阻力。


被动语态:
The new design was met with considerable resistance. = 新设计遭到了相当大的抵制。
The proposed changes are being met with stiff resistance. = 提议的变革正在遭遇强烈的抵制。


疑问句:
What is the resistance of this wire? = 这根电线的电阻是多少？
Is there any resistance to the plan? = 对这个计划有任何抵制吗？


否定句:
He offered no resistance to the police. = 他没有反抗警察。
The material showed no resistance to the acid. = 这种材料对酸没有抵抗力。



PHRASE->
resistance to change: 对变革的抵抗
air resistance: 空气阻力
water resistance: 防水性
electrical resistance: 电阻
resistance movement: 抵抗运动

词源:
源自拉丁语resistentia，由resistere派生而来，意为“抵挡，反抗”。
