overappreciativeness:/ˌoʊvərəˈpriːʃiətɪvnəs/ | n. |过分感激；过度欣赏 |The state of being excessively appreciative or grateful.  It often implies an imbalance, where the appreciation expressed is disproportionate to the act or gesture received.  |例句：Her overappreciativeness made him uncomfortable; he felt she was exaggerating his small act of kindness.  她的过分感激让他不舒服；他觉得她在夸大他的小善举。
