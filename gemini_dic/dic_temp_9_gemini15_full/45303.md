cumacea:/suˈmeɪʃə/ | n. | 糠虾类 | Cumacea is an order of small, mostly benthic crustaceans. They are characterized by a laterally compressed body, a distinct head, and a well-developed carapace.  They inhabit a wide range of marine environments, from shallow coastal waters to deep-sea trenches.  They play a significant role in the marine food web as both predators and prey. | Example: The researchers collected numerous cumacea specimens from the deep-sea sediment samples.  研究人员从深海沉积物样本中收集了许多糠虾类标本。 | 近义词: amphipod (部分物种), mysid (部分物种) | 反义词:  无直接反义词
