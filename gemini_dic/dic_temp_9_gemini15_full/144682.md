outyielded:/ˌaʊtˈjiːldɪd/ | v. |超过产量，产量超过 |To produce or yield a greater amount than.  This is the past tense of the verb "outyield."|例句：Last year's harvest outyielded all previous records.  去年收成超过了以往所有纪录。
The new variety of wheat outyielded the old one by 20%.  新品种小麦比旧品种增产20%。|近义词：surpass, exceed, surpass in yield |反义词：underperform, underproduce |动词
