rejuvenation:/ˌriːdʒuːvəˈneɪʃən/ | n. |返老还童；恢复活力；复兴 |The process of making someone or something look or feel younger, fresher, or more lively.  It can refer to the renewal of youth, the revitalization of something old or worn, or the restoration of something to a former state of vigor. |例句：The spa treatment promised rejuvenation and a renewed sense of well-being.  水疗护理承诺返老还童和焕然一新的幸福感。

The mountain air and the fresh scent of pine contributed to her rejuvenation.  山间的空气和松树的清香有助于她的恢复活力。|近义词：renewal, revitalization, restoration, regeneration |反义词：decay, deterioration, decline, aging
