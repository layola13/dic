prickliest: /ˈprɪklɪəst/ | adj. | 最扎人的；最棘手的；最难对付的 | Most irritating, troublesome, or difficult.  | The prickliest of the bunch was undoubtedly the old mayor.  | thorny, difficult, challenging | smooth, easy, agreeable | 形容词


例句：

The prickliest rose bush had the most beautiful blooms. 最扎人的玫瑰丛开出了最美丽的花朵。

He was known for his prickly personality, making it difficult to work with him. 他以其棘手的性格而闻名，这使得与他共事变得困难。


近义词：

thorny, difficult, challenging, irritating, abrasive, contentious, touchy


反义词：

smooth, easy, agreeable, pleasant, amiable, docile


词源:

Prickliest is the superlative form of prickly.  "Prickly" comes from the Old English word *prician*, meaning "to prick," which ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic *prikōną.  The suffix "-iest" indicates the superlative degree, meaning "most."
