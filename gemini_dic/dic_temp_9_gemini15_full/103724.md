joshers:/ˈdʒɒʃəz/ | n. |爱开玩笑的人，戏弄者 | A joser is someone who likes to tell jokes and make light of things, often in a playful or slightly mischievous way. They are known for their humorous and witty remarks.  | Example: He's a real joser, always cracking jokes at inappropriate times.  他是个十足的滑稽人物，总是在不恰当的时候开玩笑。 |近义词: joker, wag, comedian, buffoon | 反义词: serious person, killjoy |  名词
