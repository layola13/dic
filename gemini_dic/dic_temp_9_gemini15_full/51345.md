detectivelike: /dɪˈtɛktɪvlaɪk/ | adj. | 像侦探一样的；侦探似的 | Resembling or characteristic of a detective; shrewd and observant.  |例句：He had a detectivelike gaze, noticing every detail in the room. 他有着侦探般敏锐的目光，注意到房间里的每一个细节。

例句：Her detectivelike questioning uncovered the truth. 她像侦探一样的提问揭露了真相。


近义词：investigative, inquiring, observant, sharp, shrewd

反义词：unobservant, careless, inattentive


词性:形容词
