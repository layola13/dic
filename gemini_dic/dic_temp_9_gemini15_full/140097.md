off-label:/ˈɒfˈleɪbl/| adj.|超说明书用药的；未经批准的|Used to describe the use of a medication for a purpose, or in a way, not specified in the official prescribing information.  This often happens when a drug is found to be effective for a condition other than the one it was originally approved for.|例句：The doctor prescribed the drug off-label to treat the patient's chronic pain.  医生超说明书用药来治疗病人的慢性疼痛。|近义词：unapproved, unauthorized, unlicensed|反义词：on-label, approved
