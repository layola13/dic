look-in:/ˈlʊkɪn/| n.| 察看；拜访；短暂访问 | A brief visit; a short call.  |例句：I'll pay you a look-in later this evening. 今晚晚些时候我会去看你。 |例句：She made a look-in at the library on her way home. 她回家途中顺便去了趟图书馆。|近义词：visit, call, drop-in |反义词：stay away, avoid


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He makes a look-in at the office every morning. = 他每天早上都会去办公室看看。
She often pays her grandmother a look-in on weekends. = 她周末经常去看望奶奶。

一般过去时:
They made a look-in at the museum yesterday. = 他们昨天去博物馆参观了一下。
He paid his friend a look-in after work. = 他下班后去看望了他的朋友。

现在完成时:
I haven't made a look-in at the gym this week. = 我这周还没去健身房。
She has paid her parents a look-in several times this month. = 这个月她已经几次去看望父母了。

过去完成时:
They had made a look-in at the shop before it closed. = 商店关门前他们去看了看。
She had paid him a look-in before he left for the airport. = 他去机场之前，她去看过他。

将来时:
I will pay you a look-in tomorrow. = 明天我会去看你。
We will make a look-in at the new restaurant next week. = 下周我们会去那家新餐厅看看。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would pay her a look-in. = 如果我有时间，我会去看她。
I wish I could make a look-in at the art gallery. = 我真希望我能去美术馆看看。

被动语态:

否定句:
