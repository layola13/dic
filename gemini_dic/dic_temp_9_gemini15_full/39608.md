consensually:/kənˈsɛnʃuəli/ | adv. |双方同意的；经双方同意的 |  done or agreed by all people involved; with the agreement of all people involved.  | Example: The decision was made consensually, after lengthy discussions with all stakeholders.  这个决定是在与所有利益相关者进行长时间讨论后，经双方同意做出的。 |近义词：mutually, jointly, unanimously | 反义词：unilaterally, forcibly, against one's will.
