exsiccate:/ˈɛksɪkeɪt/| vt.|使干燥；脱水|To dry thoroughly; desiccate.  The process removes moisture from a substance, often to preserve it or make it easier to handle.  |例句：The intense heat exsiccated the land, leaving it cracked and barren.  烈日炎炎使土地干涸龟裂，变得贫瘠不堪。

例句：The herb must be exsiccated before being ground into powder.  这种药草必须先干燥后才能磨成粉。


近义词：dehydrate, desiccate, dry up

反义词：hydrate, moisten

词性:动词
