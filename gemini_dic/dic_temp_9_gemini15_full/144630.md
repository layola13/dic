outwith: /ˌaʊtˈwɪθ/ | prep. |在…之外；除…以外 |  Outside of; beyond; except for. This word is primarily used in Scotland and other parts of the UK.  It indicates exclusion or something existing separately from a specified area or group. |


例句：

The decision was made outwith the remit of the committee.  = 这个决定是在委员会职权范围之外做出的。

All expenses outwith the project budget will be reimbursed separately. = 项目预算之外的所有费用将单独报销。


近义词：outside, beyond, except, excluding

反义词：within, inside, including

词性：介词
