reexhibited:/ˌriːˈɛɡzɪbɪtɪd/| v. |再次展出，重新展出 |To exhibit again; to show or display something publicly again after a previous exhibition.|The museum reexhibited the Picasso paintings after renovations.  博物馆在翻修后再次展出了毕加索的画作。|近义词：redisplayed, reshown |反义词：removed, hidden


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reexhibit the artwork annually. = 他们每年都重新展出这件艺术品。
The gallery reexhibits popular pieces regularly. = 画廊定期重新展出受欢迎的作品。

一般过去时:
The museum reexhibited the collection last year. = 博物馆去年重新展出了这个收藏品。
The artist reexhibited his sculptures at a new venue. = 艺术家在一个新的场地重新展出了他的雕塑。

现在完成时:
They have reexhibited the painting several times. = 他们已经多次重新展出了这幅画。
The museum has reexhibited the dinosaur fossils this spring. = 博物馆在这个春天重新展出了恐龙化石。

过去完成时:

将来时:
They will reexhibit the photographs next month. = 他们下个月将重新展出这些照片。
The city will reexhibit the historical documents in the new museum. = 该市将在新博物馆重新展出这些历史文献。

虚拟语气:
If they had more space, they would reexhibit the entire collection. = 如果他们有更多空间，他们就会重新展出整个收藏品。
I wish they would reexhibit the sculptures I saw last year. = 我希望他们能重新展出我去年看到的雕塑。

被动语态:
