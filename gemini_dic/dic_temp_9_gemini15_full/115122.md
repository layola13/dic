manging:/ˈmænɪdʒɪŋ/| adj. | 管理的；经营的；设法对付的 |  Describes something related to management or the act of managing. It can also describe someone skillfully handling a difficult situation. |例句：The managing director announced a new policy.  = 总经理宣布了一项新政策。

The team showed impressive managing skills during the crisis. =  团队在危机期间展现了令人印象深刻的管理技能。


近义词：administrative, executive, directorial, coping, handling

反义词：unmanaged, uncontrolled, chaotic

词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The managing director is overseeing the project. = 总经理正在监督这个项目。
She is managing her time effectively. = 她有效地管理着她的时间。


一般过去时:
He managed the company successfully for many years. = 他成功地管理公司多年。
They managed to finish the project on time. = 他们设法按时完成了项目。


现在完成时:
She has been managing this department for five years. = 她已经管理这个部门五年了。
They have managed to overcome many obstacles. = 他们已经克服了许多障碍。


过去完成时:
He had managed to secure funding before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前设法获得了资金。
They had managed the crisis effectively before the media found out. = 在媒体发现之前，他们有效地控制了危机。


将来时:
She will be managing the new branch next year. = 她明年将管理新分公司。
We will manage to find a solution. = 我们会找到解决方案的。


虚拟语气:
If he were managing the team, things would be different. = 如果他来管理团队，情况就会不一样。
I wish I were managing my time better. = 我希望我能更好地管理我的时间。


被动语态:
The company is being managed by a new CEO. = 公司由一位新的首席执行官管理。
The project is being managed efficiently. = 项目正在高效地管理。


疑问句:
Who is managing the project? = 谁在管理这个项目？
Are you managing your stress levels effectively? = 你有效地控制你的压力水平吗？


否定句:
He didn't manage to attend the meeting. = 他没能参加会议。
She isn't managing the crisis well. = 她没有很好地处理危机。
