gloried:/ˈɡlɔːrid/| v.| 过去式和过去分词 | 欢欣鼓舞；因…而自豪；充满光荣 | To feel or express great pride or joy, often in something that has been achieved.  |例句：She gloried in her daughter's success. 她为女儿的成功而感到骄傲。

例句：The team gloried in their victory.  球队为他们的胜利而欢欣鼓舞。


近义词：rejoiced, triumphed, exulted, reveled


反义词：despaired, mourned, lamented


词性：动词
