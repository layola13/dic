districting:/ˈdɪstrɪktɪŋ/| n. |行政区划；分区；选区划分|The process of dividing a geographic area into districts, often for electoral or administrative purposes.  This can involve complex considerations of population distribution, geography, and political factors. |Example:  The current districting map is highly controversial due to gerrymandering.  当前的选区划分地图由于人为操纵选区而备受争议。|近义词：zoning, partitioning, apportionment|反义词：unification, consolidation|名词
