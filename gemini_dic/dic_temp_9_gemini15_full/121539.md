mismarriages:/ˌmɪsˈmæɹɪdʒɪz/| n. |错误的婚姻；失败的婚姻；不匹配|A mismarriage is a marriage that is unhappy or unsuccessful, often because the partners are not well-suited to each other.  It can also refer to a marriage that is invalid due to legal reasons.|例句：Their marriage was a mismarriage from the start, filled with constant arguments and unhappiness. 他们的婚姻从一开始就是一场失败的婚姻，充满了不断的争吵和不快乐。

Their mismarriage led to a painful divorce after only a few years.他们的失败婚姻在短短几年后就导致了痛苦的离婚。|近义词：failed marriage, unhappy marriage, incompatible union |反义词：successful marriage, happy marriage, harmonious union
