lawsuit:/ˈlɔːsuːt/| n. |诉讼，诉讼案件 |A lawsuit is a claim or complaint brought before a court of law.  It is a legal action by a person or entity against another person or entity.  | Example: The company filed a lawsuit against its former employee for breach of contract.  这家公司就违反合同一事对前雇员提起诉讼。|近义词：litigation, action, case |反义词：settlement (和解)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He files a lawsuit every year. 他每年都提起诉讼。
The company is pursuing a lawsuit against its competitor. 这家公司正在对竞争对手提起诉讼。


一般过去时:
They lost the lawsuit last year. 他们去年输掉了官司。
She filed a lawsuit against her neighbor for noise pollution. 她因噪音污染而对邻居提起诉讼。


现在完成时:
He has filed numerous lawsuits in the past decade. 在过去的十年里，他已经提起过多次诉讼。
The plaintiff has already presented their case in the lawsuit. 原告已经在诉讼中陈述了他们的案情。


过去完成时:
Before the trial, they had settled the lawsuit out of court. 在审判之前，他们已经庭外和解了。
The judge had already ruled on several motions in the lawsuit. 法官已经就该诉讼中的几个动议做出了裁决。


将来时:
They will file a lawsuit if the problem is not resolved. 如果问题得不到解决，他们将提起诉讼。
The case will likely go to trial next year. 此案很可能明年开庭审理。


虚拟语气:
If I had the resources, I would file a lawsuit. 如果我有资源，我会提起诉讼。
I wish I hadn't filed that lawsuit; it was a waste of time and money. 我真希望我没有提起那场诉讼；那是在浪费时间和金钱。


被动语态:
The lawsuit was dismissed by the judge. 该诉讼被法官驳回。
The case is being actively litigated. 此案正在积极审理中。


疑问句:
What is the basis of this lawsuit? 这场诉讼的依据是什么？
When will the lawsuit be heard in court? 这场诉讼什么时候会在法庭上审理？


否定句:
They did not file a lawsuit against the corporation. 他们没有对公司提起诉讼。
The company chose not to pursue a lawsuit. 公司选择不提起诉讼。


PHRASE->
file a lawsuit 提起诉讼
win a lawsuit 赢得诉讼
lose a lawsuit 输掉诉讼
settle a lawsuit 和解诉讼
dismiss a lawsuit 驳回诉讼
