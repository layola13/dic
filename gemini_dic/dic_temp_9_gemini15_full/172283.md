put away:/pʊt əˈweɪ/ | vt. | 收拾；储存；抛弃；处理；|to move something to the place where it is usually kept; to store something; to get rid of something; to deal with something |例句：Please put away your toys. 请把你的玩具收起来。

例句：He put away a large amount of food. 他吃掉了大量的食物。


近义词：store, tidy, save, discard, dispose of | 反义词：take out, get out, bring out | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He puts away his clothes every night. 他每天晚上都收拾衣服。
She puts away extra food in the freezer. 她把多余的食物放进冰箱冷冻室。

一般过去时:
I put away all my books after studying. 我学习后把所有的书都收了起来。
They put away the chairs after the meeting. 会议结束后他们把椅子收了起来。

现在完成时:
She has put away all the Christmas decorations. 她已经收起了所有的圣诞装饰品。
We have put away a significant amount of money for retirement. 我们已经为退休存了一大笔钱。

过去完成时:
He had put away his work before he went to bed. 他睡觉前已经完成了工作。
They had put away the evidence before the police arrived. 警察到达之前，他们已经销毁了证据。

将来时:
I will put away the dishes after dinner. 晚饭后我会收拾碗筷。
She will put away her old clothes to make room for new ones. 她会把旧衣服收起来，为新衣服腾出空间。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would put away all my clutter. 如果我有更多的时间，我会把所有的杂物都收拾好。
I wish I had put away more money when I was younger. 我希望我年轻的时候存了更多的钱。

被动语态:
The toys were put away by the children. 玩具被孩子们收了起来。
The documents were put away in the safe. 文件被锁进了保险箱。

疑问句:
Have you put away your laundry yet? 你洗好的衣服收起来了吗？
Where did he put away the tools? 他把工具收在哪里了？

否定句:
He didn't put away his tools after using them. 他用完工具后没有收起来。
She hasn't put away the groceries yet. 她还没有把杂货收起来。
