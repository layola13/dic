impudent:/ˈɪmpjʊdənt/| adj.|放肆的；无礼的；鲁莽的|Showing a lack of respect; impertinent.  It often implies a boldness or defiance in one's disrespect. |例句：His impudent remarks angered the teacher. 他无礼的言辞激怒了老师。
例句：It was impudent of him to interrupt the speaker. 他打断演讲者，真是太放肆了。|近义词：insolent, impertinent, cheeky, saucy|反义词：respectful, polite, courteous|词性:形容词
