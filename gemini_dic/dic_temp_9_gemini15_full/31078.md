champagne:/ʃæmˈpeɪn/| n.|香槟酒|A sparkling wine made in the Champagne region of France, using a specific method of production.|例句：We celebrated with a bottle of champagne. 我们用一瓶香槟酒庆祝。

例句：The champagne was chilled to perfection. 这香槟酒冰镇得恰到好处。


近义词：sparkling wine|反义词：still wine
