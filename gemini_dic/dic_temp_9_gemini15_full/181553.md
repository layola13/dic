relacquering: /ˌriːˈlækərɪŋ/ | v. | 再涂漆；重新上漆 | The act of applying a new layer of lacquer to something.  This is often done to restore or protect a surface. | 例句：The antique table needed relacquering to restore its shine. 这张古董桌子需要重新上漆才能恢复光泽。

The artist decided to relacquer the chair to update its appearance. 艺术家决定重新给椅子上漆以更新其外观。| 近义词：revarnishing, refinishing | 反义词：stripping (removing lacquer)
