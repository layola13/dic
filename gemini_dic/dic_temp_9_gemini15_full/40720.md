converts:/kənˈvɜːrts/| vt.|转换；使转变；兑换；皈依|To change something from one form, state, system, etc., to another; to change someone's religious beliefs.|例句：The software converts the documents into PDF format.  这个软件将文档转换成PDF格式。|例句：He converted to Christianity after a period of soul-searching.  经过一段时间的苦苦寻觅后，他皈依了基督教。|近义词：transforms, changes, translates, switches|反义词：maintains, preserves, keeps


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He converts dollars to euros. = 他把美元兑换成欧元。
The machine converts raw materials into finished products. = 机器将原材料转换成成品。

一般过去时:
She converted her old barn into a studio. = 她把旧谷仓改造成工作室。
They converted to a new energy source. = 他们改用了一种新的能源。

现在完成时:
He has converted to Buddhism. = 他已经皈依佛教。
The company has converted all its data to the new system. = 公司已经将其所有数据转换为新系统。

过去完成时:
They had converted the building before the new tenants moved in. = 新租户搬进来之前，他们已经改造了那栋建筑。
She had converted all her savings into stocks. = 她已经把所有的积蓄都兑换成了股票。

将来时:
We will convert the attic into a bedroom. = 我们将把阁楼改造成卧室。
They will convert the old factory into a museum. = 他们将把旧工厂改造成博物馆。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would convert my garage into a workshop. = 如果我有更多钱，我会把车库改造成工作室。
I wish I could convert my old car into an electric one. = 我希望我能把我的旧车改造成电动车。

被动语态:
The old house was converted into apartments. = 老房子被改造成公寓。
The data is being converted to a new format. = 数据正在转换为新的格式。

疑问句:
Does the software convert videos to MP4? = 这个软件能把视频转换成MP4吗？
Can this machine convert pounds to kilograms? = 这台机器能把磅转换成公斤吗？

否定句:
The system doesn't convert that type of file. = 该系统不转换那种类型的文件。
He didn't convert his savings into gold. = 他没有把积蓄兑换成黄金。


PHRASE->
convert to = 转换为
convert into = 改造成
convert from = 从...转换
data conversion = 数据转换
currency conversion = 货币兑换
