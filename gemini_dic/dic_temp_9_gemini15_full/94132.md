impedes:/ɪmˈpiːdz/ | vt. |妨碍；阻碍 |To obstruct or hinder the progress of.  |例句：The heavy snow impeded travel. 大雪阻碍了出行。
例句：Construction work is impeding traffic flow. 建筑工程阻碍了交通流量。|近义词：hinder, obstruct, hamper, block |反义词：facilitate, aid, assist |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bad weather impedes our journey. = 恶劣的天气阻碍了我们的旅程。
Traffic congestion impedes the flow of vehicles. = 交通拥堵阻碍了车辆的通行。

一般过去时:
The storm impeded communication. = 暴风雨阻碍了通讯。
A broken leg impeded his progress in the race. = 断腿阻碍了他比赛的进程。


现在完成时:
Unexpected delays have impeded the project's completion. = 意想不到的延误阻碍了项目的完成。
Numerous obstacles have impeded our efforts. = 许多障碍阻碍了我们的努力。


过去完成时:
The previous accident had impeded the traffic flow for hours. = 此前的交通事故已阻碍了交通流量数小时。
Technical problems had impeded the launch of the satellite. = 技术问题阻碍了卫星的发射。


将来时:
Further strikes will impede the delivery of goods. = 进一步的罢工将阻碍货物的交付。
The new regulations will impede economic growth. = 新规定将阻碍经济增长。


虚拟语气:
If the rain hadn't impeded our plans, we would have gone hiking. = 如果雨没有阻碍我们的计划，我们就去远足了。
I wish that nothing would impede our success. = 我希望没有什么会阻碍我们的成功。


被动语态:
The project was impeded by funding shortages. = 该项目因资金短缺而受阻。
Progress was impeded by unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，进展受到了阻碍。


疑问句:
What impeded your progress? = 什么阻碍了你的进步？
Does anything impede your ability to complete the task? = 有什么东西阻碍你完成任务吗？


否定句:
Nothing impeded their plans. = 没有什么阻碍他们的计划。
The lack of resources did not impede their determination. = 资源缺乏并没有阻碍他们的决心。

词源:
From Latin *impedire, from in- ("in," "into") + ped- ("foot").  The original meaning was "to entangle the feet," hence "to hinder" or "obstruct."
