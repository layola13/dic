prophetically:/prɒˈfetɪkli/| adv.|预言地；预示地|In a way that suggests that something will happen in the future; prophetically.  This word describes something that indicates or suggests something that will happen in the future, often in a way that seems mysterious or insightful.|例句：The old woman prophetically warned of the coming storm.  老妇人预言般地预警了即将到来的暴风雨。

例句：He prophetically predicted the market crash. 他预言般地预测了市场崩盘。


近义词：predictively, precognitively, omenously


反义词：unpredictably, unexpectedly


词性:副词
