eyeglance:/ˈaɪɡlæns/| n.| 一瞥，匆匆一瞥 | A quick look.  It implies a brief and often cursory observation, not a detailed examination. | Example: He gave the painting a quick eyeglance before moving on. 他匆匆一瞥那幅画便离开了。|近义词：glance, glimpse, peek |反义词：stare, scrutiny
