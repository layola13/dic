resonantly:/ˈrezənəntli/| adv.|共鸣地，回响地，洪亮地 |  In a resonant manner; with resonance.  It describes something that is produced or delivered with a strong, clear, and deep tone that seems to vibrate or echo. | 例句：The singer's voice resonated resonantly throughout the hall. = 歌手的歌声在整个大厅里洪亮地回响。

The speaker delivered his message resonantly, captivating the audience. = 演讲者以洪亮的声音发表了他的讲话，吸引了听众。| 近义词：deeply, powerfully, vibrantly | 反义词：faintly, weakly, quietly
