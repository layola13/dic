saengerbund:/ˈzɛŋərˌbʊnd/| n. |歌手协会；合唱团；歌唱联盟|A Sängerbund is a German term referring to a singing society or choral union, often associated with male choruses and a strong tradition of German folk songs and lieder.  These organizations are known for their disciplined performances and commitment to preserving musical heritage. |The local Sängerbund put on a spectacular performance of Brahms's "Liebeslieder Walzer."  当地歌手协会精彩演绎了勃拉姆斯的《爱情歌曲圆舞曲》。|choral society, singing club, vocal ensemble| (no direct antonym, but a contrasting term could be) instrumental ensemble. |名词
