monopolized:/məˈnɒpəlaɪzd/| vt.|垄断，独占|To control or possess something entirely, to the exclusion of others.  |例句：The company monopolized the market for years. 这家公司多年来垄断了市场。|例句：He monopolized the conversation, preventing others from speaking. 他独占了谈话，阻止了其他人发言。|近义词：dominate, control, corner, hoard |反义词：share, distribute, relinquish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The corporation monopolizes the software market. = 这家公司垄断了软件市场。
He monopolizes the conversation at every party. = 他在每个聚会上都独占谈话。

一般过去时:
Standard Oil monopolized the oil industry in the late 19th century. = 标准石油公司在19世纪后期垄断了石油工业。
She monopolized the attention of the entire room. = 她吸引了整个房间所有人的注意力。

现在完成时:
They have monopolized the local newspaper market for decades. = 几十年来，他们一直垄断了当地报纸市场。
He has monopolized the discussion since the meeting began. = 从会议开始到现在，他一直主导着讨论。

过去完成时:
Before the antitrust laws were passed, the company had monopolized the steel industry. = 在反托拉斯法通过之前，该公司已经垄断了钢铁行业。
The speaker had monopolized the stage for over an hour before anyone dared to interrupt. = 演讲者在别人敢于打断他之前，已经独占了舞台一个多小时。

将来时:
Without regulation, one company will likely monopolize the new technology market. = 如果没有监管，一家公司可能会垄断新的技术市场。
She will monopolize the conversation if you don't intervene. = 如果你不干预，她将会独占谈话。

虚拟语气:
If he didn't monopolize the resources, the project would have been more successful. = 如果他不独占资源，这个项目将会更成功。
I wish he wouldn't monopolize the conversation all the time. = 我希望他不要总是独占谈话。

被动语态:
The market was monopolized by a single corporation. = 市场被一家公司垄断。
The conversation was monopolized by the most vocal participant. = 谈话被最健谈的参与者所主导。

疑问句:
Does this company monopolize the market? = 这家公司是否垄断了市场？
Did he monopolize the discussion intentionally? = 他是故意独占讨论的吗？

否定句:
The government actively prevents companies from monopolizing industries. = 政府积极阻止公司垄断行业。
She did not monopolize the conversation; she actively encouraged others to participate. = 她并没有独占谈话，她积极鼓励其他人参与。
