revitalises:/ˌriːˈvaɪtəlaɪzɪz/ | v. |使复兴；使恢复活力；使重新充满活力 | To give new life or energy to something; to restore to a vigorous state.  | The new manager revitalised the company's image. 新经理使公司的形象焕然一新。 |The government's new policies are intended to revitalise the economy. 政府的新政策旨在振兴经济。| invigorate, rejuvenate, refresh |  deteriorate, weaken, exhaust |动词
