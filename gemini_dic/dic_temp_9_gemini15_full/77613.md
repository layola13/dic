geezer:/ˈɡiːzə(r)/| n. |老家伙，老头子 | An old man, especially one who is considered eccentric or strange.  The term can be used affectionately or derisively, depending on context and tone. | He's a bit of a geezer, always telling the same stories. 他有点古怪，总是讲同样的故事。 | old man, old timer, codger | young person | noun
