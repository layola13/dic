frustrated:/frʌˈstreɪtɪd/| adj. |挫败的；灰心的；感到沮丧的 |Feeling or expressing distress and annoyance caused by inability to change or achieve something.|  He felt frustrated by his inability to solve the problem. 他因为无法解决问题而感到沮丧。|近义词：discouraged, disheartened, disappointed |反义词：satisfied, pleased, content


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am frustrated with my slow progress. = 我对自己的缓慢进展感到沮丧。
He is frustrated because he can't find a job. = 他因为找不到工作而感到沮丧。

一般过去时:
I was frustrated by the traffic jam. = 我因为交通堵塞而感到沮丧。
She was frustrated after failing the exam. = 她考试不及格后感到沮丧。

现在完成时:
I have been frustrated with this project for weeks. = 我几周来一直对这个项目感到沮丧。
She has been frustrated by her lack of opportunities. = 她一直因为缺乏机会而感到沮丧。

过去完成时:
I had been frustrated with my job for years before I quit. = 在我辞职之前，我已经对我的工作感到沮丧多年了。
They had been frustrated by the lack of support. = 他们因为缺乏支持而感到沮丧。

将来时:
I will be frustrated if I don't finish the report on time. = 如果我没有按时完成报告，我会感到沮丧。
He will be frustrated if his plan fails. = 如果他的计划失败了，他会感到沮丧。

虚拟语气:
I would be frustrated if I couldn't go to the concert. = 如果我去不了音乐会，我会很沮丧。
If I were you, I wouldn't be so frustrated. = 如果我是你，我不会这么沮丧。

被动语态:
He was frustrated by the unexpected delay.  = 他因意外延误而感到沮丧。
The students were frustrated by the difficult exam. = 学生们因为考试太难而感到沮丧。

疑问句:
Are you frustrated with your work? = 你对你的工作感到沮丧吗？
Is he frustrated by the situation? = 他对这种情况感到沮丧吗？

否定句:
I am not frustrated with the results. = 我对结果并不沮丧。
She was not frustrated by the criticism. = 她并没有因为批评而感到沮丧。


PHRASE->
feel frustrated = 感到沮丧
become frustrated = 变得沮丧
extremely frustrated = 极其沮丧
deeply frustrated = 深感沮丧
increasingly frustrated = 越来越沮丧
