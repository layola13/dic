dissidents:/ˈdɪsɪdənts/| n.|持不同政见者；异议者|People who oppose official policy, especially in a political context. They often express their dissent through protests, activism, or other forms of resistance.|例句：The government cracked down on dissidents and suppressed any form of opposition.  政府镇压异议人士并压制任何形式的反对意见。|例句：Many dissidents fled the country to escape persecution. 许多持不同政见者逃离该国以逃避迫害。|近义词：protesters, rebels, opponents, nonconformists|反义词：conformists, supporters, loyalists|名词
