preverifying: /ˌpriːˈvɛrɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 预先验证；预先核实 |  The act of verifying something beforehand. It implies checking or confirming the accuracy, validity, or authenticity of something before it is put into use or accepted.  | Example: Preverifying the data ensures accuracy in the final report. = 预先验证数据确保最终报告的准确性。  The team spent hours preverifying the software before its release. = 团队在软件发布前花了数小时进行预先验证。| 近义词：prechecking, verifying beforehand, confirming in advance | 反义词：post-verifying, neglecting to verify
