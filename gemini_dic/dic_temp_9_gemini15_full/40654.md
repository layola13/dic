conventions:/kənˈvɛnʃənz/| n.|习俗；惯例；会议；公约|Established customs, practices, or beliefs of a particular group or society.  A formal meeting or gathering, especially of representatives or delegates. |例句：The conventions of polite society dictate that you should not interrupt.  礼貌社会习俗规定你不应该打断别人。

例句：The international convention on climate change is taking place next month. 下个月将举行关于气候变化的国际公约。|近义词：customs, traditions, practices, rules, meetings, conferences|反义词：unconventionality, innovation, originality|名词

将来时:
