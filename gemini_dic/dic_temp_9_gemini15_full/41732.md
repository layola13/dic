corroding:/kəˈroʊdɪŋ/| v.|腐蚀；侵蚀；破坏|The process of gradually destroying something by chemical action.  This typically involves a slow and steady deterioration of a material due to chemical reactions. |例句：Acid rain is corroding the statues. 酸雨正在腐蚀雕像。

例句：The salt water is corroding the metal hull of the ship.  盐水正在腐蚀船的金属船体。


近义词：eroding, decaying, deteriorating, rusting


反义词：preserving, protecting, strengthening


词性:动词
