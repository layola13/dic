parallelopiped:/ˌpærəˈlɛləpaɪpɛd/| n. |平行六面体|A parallelepiped is a three-dimensional figure whose six faces are parallelograms.  It's a generalization of a rectangular prism; a rectangular prism is a special case of a parallelepiped where all angles are right angles.|The crystal structure was modeled as a parallelepiped.  这个晶体结构被建模成一个平行六面体。
The box is a parallelepiped. 这个盒子是一个平行六面体。|rectangular prism, rhomboid|sphere, cube(in specific cases)|名词
