nausea:/ˈnɔːziə/|/ˈnɔːʒə/| n. |恶心，呕吐感 |A feeling of sickness with an inclination to vomit.  It can be caused by various factors, including illness, pregnancy, or medication.| The chemotherapy caused severe nausea. 化疗导致了严重的恶心。| I woke up with a feeling of nausea. 我醒来时感到恶心。| vomiting, sickness, queasiness |  wellness, good health | 名词


USAGE->
This word is a noun, and therefore doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs do.  The usage will change based on the sentence structure.


例句一：The smell of the rotten food caused intense nausea.  腐烂食物的气味引起了强烈的恶心。

例句二：Morning sickness, characterized by nausea and vomiting, is a common symptom of pregnancy. 晨吐，其特征是恶心和呕吐，是怀孕的常见症状。

例句三：She experienced bouts of nausea throughout her illness.  她在整个病程中都经历了阵阵恶心。

例句四：The medication has a side effect of nausea.  这种药有恶心副作用。
