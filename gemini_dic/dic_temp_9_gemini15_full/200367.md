skydome:/ˈskaɪdoʊm/| n. |天穹，穹顶体育馆|A skydome is a large, domed structure, often used for sporting events or concerts, with a roof that is either retractable or transparent to allow natural light.  It is also used figuratively to refer to the sky or the celestial dome.|例句：The concert was held in the impressive skydome.  这场音乐会在令人印象深刻的穹顶体育馆举行。
