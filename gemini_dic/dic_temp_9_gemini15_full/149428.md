packrat: /ˈpækræt/ | n. |囤积狂；收藏癖；收集癖| A person who keeps a lot of things they don't need, often in a disorganized way.  They are often unable to throw things away, even if they are broken or useless. |例句：My uncle is a real packrat; his garage is filled with junk he refuses to get rid of.  我的叔叔是个十足的囤积狂；他的车库里堆满了他不肯扔掉的垃圾。|近义词：hoarder, collector (in a negative context), clutterbug |反义词：minimalist, purger

词源:

The word "packrat" is a compound word.  "Pack" refers to the act of gathering and storing things, and "rat" is used figuratively to describe someone who hoards things like a rat hoards food.  The imagery suggests someone who gathers and hides away possessions in a secretive and somewhat undesirable way.
