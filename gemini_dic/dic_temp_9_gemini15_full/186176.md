rhesus:/ˈriːsəs/| n. |恒河猴；(指血液类型)恒河猴的|The word "rhesus" refers to the rhesus macaque (Macaca mulatta), a species of Old World monkey.  It's also used to describe the rhesus factor in blood, an antigen first discovered in the rhesus macaque.|Example: The researchers studied the social behavior of rhesus monkeys in their natural habitat.  研究人员研究了恒河猴在其自然栖息地中的社会行为。 The patient's blood type is O negative, meaning they lack the rhesus factor. 病人的血型是O型阴性，这意味着他们缺乏恒河猴因子。|近义词: macaque,  Old World monkey |反义词: None specifically applicable.
