re-numbering:/ˌriːˈnʌmbərɪŋ/| n. |重新编号；改编号码 |The process of assigning new numbers to items that were previously numbered.  This often involves changing the existing numbering system.  For example, a company might re-number its products after a merger to create a unified system. |例句：The re-numbering of the streets caused some initial confusion.  重新编号的街道导致了一些最初的混乱。 The database required re-numbering after the data migration. 数据库在数据迁移后需要重新编号。|近义词：renumbering, re-sequencing |反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be leaving the numbering unchanged. |名词
