screener:/ˈskriːnər/| n. |筛选器；审查员；初选；预选赛 |A screener is a person or thing that screens.  This can refer to a person who reviews applications or submissions, a device that filters something, or a preliminary selection process. |例句：The hiring manager acted as a screener, reviewing applications before interviews. 这位招聘经理担任筛选员，在面试前审查申请。

例句：The security screener checked my bag at the airport. 安检人员在机场检查了我的行李。

近义词：filter, sifter, reviewer, selector |反义词：approver (in some contexts)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The screener checks each application carefully. = 筛选员仔细检查每一份申请。
He screens potential candidates daily. = 他每天筛选潜在的候选人。


一般过去时:
The committee screened the proposals last week. = 委员会上周筛选了这些提案。
She screened the applicants based on their qualifications. = 她根据申请人的资格对申请人进行了筛选。


现在完成时:
They have screened hundreds of applications. = 他们已经筛选了数百份申请。
We have screened the film and found some issues. = 我们已经看过这部电影，发现了一些问题。


过去完成时:
The company had already screened the candidates before the interviews. = 公司在面试前已经筛选过候选人了。
The judge had screened the evidence before making a decision. = 法官在做出决定之前已经审查过证据了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
