re-endowment:/ˌriːɪnˈdaʊmənt/ | n. | 重新捐赠；再次捐赠 | The act of giving something, typically money or property, again after it has already been given once.  It often implies a renewal or replenishment of a previous endowment. | Example: The university received a re-endowment from a grateful alumnus.  大学收到了一位心怀感激的校友的再次捐赠。|近义词：renewed endowment, fresh funding, replenishment |反义词：disendowment, depletion


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The foundation re-endows the scholarship program annually. = 该基金会每年都会重新捐赠奖学金项目。
He re-endows his charitable trust every five years. = 他每五年重新捐赠一次他的慈善信托基金。

一般过去时:
They re-endowed the church after the fire. = 火灾后，他们重新捐赠了教堂。
The hospital re-endowed its research department last year. = 去年医院重新捐赠了它的研究部门。

现在完成时:
She has re-endowed her family's foundation several times. = 她已经多次重新捐赠了她家族的基金会。
The university has re-endowed its library with new books. = 大学已经用新书重新捐赠了它的图书馆。

过去完成时:
Before the expansion, they had re-endowed the school with new equipment. = 在扩建之前，他们已经用新设备重新捐赠了学校。
They had re-endowed the museum's collection with rare artifacts. = 他们已经用稀有文物重新捐赠了博物馆的藏品。

将来时:
They will re-endow the hospital wing next year. = 他们明年将重新捐赠医院的病房。
The company will re-endow its employee scholarship fund next quarter. = 公司将在下个季度重新捐赠其员工奖学金基金。

虚拟语气:
If they re-endowed the program, it would continue for another decade. = 如果他们重新捐赠该项目，它将持续另一个十年。
I wish they had re-endowed the research institute years ago. = 我希望几年前他们已经重新捐赠了该研究所。

被动语态:
The scholarship fund was re-endowed by a generous donor. = 奖学金基金由一位慷慨的捐助者重新捐赠。
The museum's collection is being re-endowed with new exhibits. = 博物馆的藏品正在用新的展品重新捐赠。

疑问句:
Will the trust be re-endowed soon? = 信托基金很快就会重新捐赠吗？
Has the foundation re-endowed the project yet? = 基金会已经重新捐赠该项目了吗？

否定句:
They did not re-endow the charity this year. = 他们今年没有重新捐赠该慈善机构。
The school has not been re-endowed with new computers. = 学校还没有重新捐赠新的电脑。
