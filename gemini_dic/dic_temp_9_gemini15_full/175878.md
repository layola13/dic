ratchets:/ˈræ tʃɪts/| n. v.|棘轮，棘爪；(使)棘轮旋转|A ratchet is a mechanism that allows movement in one direction only.  It typically consists of a wheel with teeth and a pawl that engages with the teeth to prevent backward movement.|例句：The ratchet on the jack prevented it from slipping.  棘轮千斤顶防止其滑落。 The machine ratcheted up the tension. 机器逐渐增高了张力。|近义词：pawl, catch, click|反义词：freewheel
