simulator:/ˈsɪmjuleɪtər/ | n. | 模拟器，模拟装置 | A device or program that imitates the conditions of something, especially for training purposes.  | 例句：The flight simulator helped the pilot practice emergency landings.  飞行模拟器帮助飞行员练习紧急着陆。 | 例句：The software is a simulator for testing new code. 该软件是用于测试新代码的模拟器。 | 近义词：model, emulator, mock-up | 反义词：reality, actuality | 名词
