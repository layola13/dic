orthoclase:/ˈɔːrθəʊkleɪs/| n. |正长石|A common feldspar mineral, a potassium aluminum silicate. It's a major constituent of many igneous and metamorphic rocks. Orthoclase is often found in granite, syenite, and gneiss.  It is typically pink, white, or colorless, and has a glassy luster.|Example: The geologist identified orthoclase crystals in the granite sample.  地质学家在花岗岩样品中发现了正长石晶体。|近义词：feldspar, potassium feldspar|反义词：N/A
