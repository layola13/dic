creaky:/ˈkriːki/| adj.| 发嘎吱嘎吱响的；破旧的；嘶哑的 | Making a harsh, squeaking sound.  Often used to describe old or worn-out objects or voices. | 例句：The old door creaked open. 这扇旧门吱呀一声开了。

The speaker's voice was creaky and weak. 说话人的声音又嘶哑又微弱。


近义词：rusty, squeaky, raspy, shaky | 反义词：smooth, silent, strong, clear


词源:  The word "creaky" originates from the Middle English word "creken," meaning "to creak." This in turn likely comes from an earlier Germanic root related to words suggesting friction or harsh sounds.


用法说明:

一般现在时:
The door creaks every time someone opens it. = 每当有人开门时，门都会发出吱吱的声音。
The hinges creak loudly. = 合页发出很大的吱吱声。


一般过去时:
The floorboards creaked under his weight. = 地板在他体重下发出吱吱作响。
The old chair creaked as he sat down. = 他坐下时，旧椅子发出吱吱声。


现在完成时:
The stairs have creaked for years. = 楼梯吱吱作响已经很多年了。
The floorboards have creaked since the house was built. = 自从房子建成以来，地板就一直吱吱作响。


过去完成时:
The door had creaked before he even touched the handle. = 他还没碰到门把手，门就已经吱吱作响了。
The old house had creaked and groaned all night. = 这栋旧房子整夜都在吱吱嘎嘎地响。


将来时:
The hinges will creak if you don't oil them. = 如果你不加油，合页就会吱吱作响。
The floorboards will probably creak when we walk across them. = 当我们走过时，地板可能会发出吱吱声。


虚拟语气:
If the door creaked less, it would be less annoying. = 如果门吱吱作响的声音小一些，就不会那么烦人了。
I wish the chair didn't creak so much. = 我希望椅子不要吱吱作响得这么厉害。


被动语态:
The old door was creaked open by the wind. = 旧门被风吱呀一声推开了。(这句被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The floorboards are creaked underfoot. = 地板在脚下发出吱吱声。(这句被动语态也略显不自然，主动语态更常用)


疑问句:
Does the floor creak when you walk on it? = 你走过时地板会吱吱响吗？
Why does the chair creak so loudly? = 为什么椅子吱吱响的声音这么大？


否定句:
The door didn't creak when I opened it. = 我开门时，门没有吱吱响。
The new floorboards don't creak at all. = 新地板一点也不吱吱响。
