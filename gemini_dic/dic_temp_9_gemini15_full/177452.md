rebathed:/ˌriːˈbeɪðd/| v. |再次沐浴；重新洗浴|To bathe again; to bathe once more.|例句：After a long hike, I rebathed myself and felt refreshed.  长途跋涉后，我重新洗了个澡，感觉神清气爽。

例句：The artist rebathed the canvas in a new coat of paint.艺术家重新给画布涂上了一层新颜料。


近义词：re-washed, cleansed again, bathed anew |反义词：dried, left unwashed
