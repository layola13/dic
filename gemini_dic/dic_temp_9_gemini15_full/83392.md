ha'ing:/ˈhævɪŋ/ | v. |拥有；进行；经历 |  The present participle of "have".  It functions as a noun, adjective, or verb depending on context. As a noun, it often represents possession or experience. As a verb, it's part of a continuous tense. As an adjective, it describes something characterized by possession or experience. | 例句：Having a car is convenient. (Noun: possession)  I am having a great time. (Verb: continuous tense)  Having seen the movie, I can tell you it's good. (Adjective: experience) | 近义词: possessing, experiencing, undergoing | 反义词: lacking, missing
