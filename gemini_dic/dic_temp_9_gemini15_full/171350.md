publicists:/ˈpʌblɪsɪsts/ | n. |新闻发言人；宣传员 |People who work to create and manage the public image of a person, company, or organization. They are responsible for promoting their clients through various media channels. |The publicists worked tirelessly to generate positive media coverage for the new movie.  新闻发言人们不知疲倦地为新电影制造积极的媒体报道。 |press agents, spin doctors, public relations officers |(无明确反义词，可考虑：critics, detractors) |名词
