palinode:/ˈpælɪnoʊd/| n. | 悔过诗；撤回声明；收回前言 | A poem or literary work in which the author retracts or refutes something previously written or said.  It's a formal retraction or recantation, often expressing regret or a change of heart. | Example:  The poet wrote a palinode, apologizing for the harsh criticisms in his earlier work.  = 这位诗人写了一首悔过诗，为之前作品中的尖锐批评道歉。


近义词：retraction, recantation, apology, retraction of statement

反义词：affirmation, confirmation, reiteration

词性：名词
