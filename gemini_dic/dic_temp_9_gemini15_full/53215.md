director:/dəˈrɛktər/ | n. |导演，主管，负责人 |A director is a person who manages or oversees a particular activity or department.  A film director is responsible for the creative aspects of filmmaking. |例句：Steven Spielberg is a famous film director. 史蒂文·斯皮尔伯格是一位著名的电影导演。|例句：The marketing director is responsible for developing and implementing marketing strategies. 市场总监负责制定和实施营销策略。|近义词：manager, supervisor, administrator |反义词：subordinate, follower
