classing-up: /ˈklæsɪŋ ʌp/ | v. | 使变得更高雅；使更具品味 | To improve the quality or social standing of something or someone; to make something or someone more refined or elegant. | 例句：He's been classing up his act lately, trying to attract a more sophisticated clientele. 他最近一直在提升自己的行为，试图吸引更精致的客户。


They're classing up the neighborhood with their new Victorian-style house. 他们用他们新的维多利亚风格的房子使这个街区变得更高雅。


近义词：elevating, refining, upgrading, improving, enhancing | 反义词：degrading, cheapening, vulgarizing, coarsening
