gleans:/ɡliːnz/| vt.| 收集（信息等）；拾取（谷物等）|to gather something, usually in small amounts, or to collect information bit by bit|The archaeologist gleaned valuable information from the ancient ruins. 考古学家从古代遗址中收集到了宝贵的信息。|gather, collect, assemble|waste, squander|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gleans information from various sources. = 他从各种来源收集信息。
She gleans wheat from the field. = 她从田里拾麦穗。

一般过去时:
They gleaned valuable insights from the discussion. = 他们从讨论中获得了宝贵的见解。
The farmers gleaned the remaining grain after the harvest. = 收割后，农民们拾起了剩下的谷物。

现在完成时:
She has gleaned enough evidence to support her theory. = 她已经收集到足够的证据来支持她的理论。
The researchers have gleaned important data from their experiments. = 研究人员从他们的实验中收集到了重要的数据。

过去完成时:
He had gleaned all the necessary facts before writing the report. = 他在写报告之前已经收集了所有必要的事实。
The detective had gleaned crucial clues from the crime scene. = 侦探已经从犯罪现场收集到了关键线索。

将来时:
They will glean insights from the upcoming conference. = 他们将从即将举行的会议中获得见解。
The students will glean knowledge from their studies. = 学生们将从学习中获得知识。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would glean more information about this topic. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会收集更多关于这个话题的信息。
I wish I could glean the secrets of the universe. = 我希望我能解开宇宙的秘密。


被动语态:

否定句:
