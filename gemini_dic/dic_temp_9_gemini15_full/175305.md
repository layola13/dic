rainwater:/ˈreɪnwɔːtər/| n. |雨水|Water that has fallen as rain.|The rainwater collected in the barrels will be used to water the garden.  雨水收集在桶里将被用来浇灌花园。|The plants thrived on the rainwater. 植物依靠雨水茁壮成长。|spring water,  fresh water|sea water,  sewage.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Rainwater collects in the gutters. = 雨水积聚在排水沟里。
Rainwater often contains pollutants. = 雨水通常含有污染物。


一般过去时:
Rainwater flooded the basement last night. = 昨晚雨水淹没了地下室。
The farmer collected rainwater to irrigate his crops. = 农民收集雨水来灌溉他的庄稼。


现在完成时:
We have collected rainwater for drinking. = 我们已经收集了雨水饮用。
The reservoir has been filled with rainwater this year. = 今年水库已经充满了雨水。


过去完成时:
The ground was saturated because it had rained heavily; the rainwater had already seeped into the soil. = 地面已经饱和，因为雨下得很大；雨水已经渗透到土壤中。
Before the drought, they had stored rainwater in large tanks. = 在干旱之前，他们已经在大型水箱中储存了雨水。


将来时:
Rainwater will be harvested for future use. = 雨水将被收集起来以备将来使用。
They will use rainwater to supplement their water supply. = 他们将用雨水来补充他们的供水。


虚拟语气:
If it rained more often, we wouldn't have a water shortage. = 如果雨水更多，我们就不会缺水。
I wish the rainwater hadn't damaged the crops. = 我希望雨水没有损坏庄稼。


被动语态:
The garden was watered with rainwater. = 花园是用雨水浇灌的。
Rainwater is being used to generate electricity. = 雨水正被用来发电。


疑问句:
Is this rainwater safe to drink? = 这雨水能安全饮用吗？
Can we use rainwater for irrigation? = 我们能用雨水灌溉吗？


否定句:
The rainwater didn't damage the house. = 雨水没有损坏房子。
They didn't use rainwater for their experiment. = 他们没有用雨水做实验。
