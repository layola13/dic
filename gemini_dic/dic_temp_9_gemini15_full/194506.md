self-safeties: /ˌsɛlfˈseɪftiz/ | n. | 自保措施；自我保护措施 | Self-safeties refer to actions or mechanisms designed to protect oneself from harm or danger.  These can be physical, procedural, or behavioral.  They are proactive measures taken to minimize risk. | 例句：The hiker carried a first-aid kit and a satellite phone as self-safeties in case of emergencies. 登山者携带急救箱和卫星电话作为紧急情况下的自保措施。 The company implemented strict self-safeties to prevent data breaches.  公司实施严格的自保措施以防止数据泄露。 | 近义词：safety precautions, protective measures, safeguards | 反义词：risk-taking, recklessness
