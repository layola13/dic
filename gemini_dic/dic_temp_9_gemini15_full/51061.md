desolder:/ˌdiːˈsɒldə(r)/| vt.| 除去焊料；脱焊 |To remove solder from a joint.  This typically involves heating the solder to melt it and then removing it with a tool such as a desoldering iron or braid.| The technician carefully desoldered the component from the circuit board. 技术人员小心地将元件从电路板上脱焊。|近义词：unsolder, remove solder |反义词：solder
