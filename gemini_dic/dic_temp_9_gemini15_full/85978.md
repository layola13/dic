headsets:/ˈhɛd.sɛts/ | n. |耳机，头戴式耳机 |A headset is a device that you wear over your ears to listen to sound, often used with a computer or phone.  It typically includes a microphone for communication.| Example: I need a new headset for my computer. 我需要一副新的电脑耳机。  I use a wireless headset for conference calls. 我用无线耳机进行电话会议。|近义词：earphones, headphones|反义词：None
