family:/ˈfæməli/ | n. | 家庭，家族 | A group of people related by blood or marriage, typically consisting of parents and their children.  |  My family consists of my parents, two brothers, and me. 我的家庭由我的父母、两个兄弟和我组成。|  household, kin, clan | individual, single person | 名词
