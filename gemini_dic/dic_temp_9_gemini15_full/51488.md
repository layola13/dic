detracter:/dɪˈtræktər/| n. |诋毁者，贬低者 |A detractor is a person who belittles or criticizes someone or something.  They often express negative opinions and try to diminish the value or importance of the subject. |His detractors claimed his success was due to luck, not skill.  他的诋毁者声称他的成功是由于运气而不是技巧。|critic, disparager, belittler|supporter, admirer, praiser|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He detracts from the team's reputation. = 他损害了团队的名誉。
Her constant criticism detracts from her work. = 她不断的批评损害了她的工作。


一般过去时:
The media detracted from the athlete's achievements. = 媒体贬低了这位运动员的成就。
His negative comments detracted from the overall mood. = 他消极的评论破坏了整体气氛。


现在完成时:
His actions have detracted from his credibility. = 他的行为损害了他的信誉。
Their negativity has detracted from the project's success. = 他们的消极态度影响了项目的成功。


过去完成时:
The scandal had detracted from the company's image before the meeting. = 丑闻在会议之前就损害了公司的形象。
The previous failures had detracted from their confidence. = 之前的失败已经损害了他们的信心。


将来时:
His upcoming trial will detract from his political career. = 他即将到来的审判将损害他的政治生涯。
The bad weather will detract from the enjoyment of the picnic. = 恶劣的天气将影响野餐的乐趣。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't been so arrogant, it wouldn't have detracted from his performance. = 如果他不那么傲慢，就不会影响他的表现了。
If only his negativity hadn't detracted from the team's spirit. = 但愿他的消极态度没有影响团队的精神。


被动语态:
His reputation was detracted from by false accusations. = 他的名誉因虚假指控而受损。
The project's success was detracted from by unforeseen circumstances. = 项目的成功因不可预见的情况而受损。


疑问句:
Does his behavior detract from the overall image? = 他的行为是否损害了整体形象？
Will the budget cuts detract from the quality of the product? = 预算削减会影响产品的质量吗？


否定句:
His achievements did not detract from his humble personality. = 他的成就并没有削弱他谦逊的个性。
The minor setbacks did not detract from their overall success. = 小挫折并没有影响他们的整体成功。


PHRASE->

detract from something = 从…中减损
detract from the beauty = 损害美观
detract from the value = 降低价值
detract from the enjoyment = 影响乐趣
detract from the credibility = 损害信誉
