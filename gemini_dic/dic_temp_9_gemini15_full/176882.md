reaggravated:/ˌriːˈæɡrəveɪtɪd/| adj.|加重的；恶化的；再次加重的|Made worse or more severe.|The injury was reaggravated by his return to work too soon.  他的过早复工加重了他的伤势。|近义词：worsened, exacerbated, intensified|反义词：improved, alleviated, soothed|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The situation reaggravates the existing problems. = 这种情况加剧了现有问题。
This policy reaggravates social inequality. = 这项政策加剧了社会不平等。


一般过去时:
The storm reaggravated the flooding. = 暴风雨加剧了洪水。
His careless actions reaggravated the conflict. = 他粗心大意的行为加剧了冲突。


现在完成时:
The drought has reaggravated the food shortage. = 干旱加剧了粮食短缺。
His smoking has reaggravated his lung condition. = 他的吸烟恶化了他的肺部状况。


过去完成时:
The previous injury had been reaggravated by the fall. = 之前的伤势因跌倒而再次恶化。
The conflict had been reaggravated by years of mistrust. = 多年的不信任加剧了冲突。


将来时:
Further delays will reaggravate the already tense situation. = 进一步的延误将加剧本已紧张的局势。
Ignoring the problem will reaggravate the damage. = 忽视问题会加剧损害。


虚拟语气:
If he continued to work, he knew his injury would be reaggravated. = 如果他继续工作，他知道他的伤势会恶化。
If we hadn't intervened, the situation would have been reaggravated. = 如果我们没有干预，情况会更加恶化。


被动语态:
The problem was reaggravated by the recent economic downturn. = 最近的经济衰退加剧了这个问题。
The tension was reaggravated by the media's coverage. = 媒体的报道加剧了紧张局势。


疑问句:
Has the recent rain reaggravated the flood situation? = 最近的雨水是否加剧了洪水情况？
Will this decision reaggravate the existing tensions? = 这个决定会加剧现有的紧张局势吗？


否定句:
The treatment didn't reaggravate the inflammation. = 治疗并没有加剧炎症。
The new policy did not reaggravate the existing issues. = 新政策并没有加剧现有问题。
