parish:/ˈpærɪʃ/ | n. |教区；区；教区居民 | A parish is a district having its own church and priest or minister, or in some countries, a local administrative division.  It is typically a small area within a larger region, often associated with a specific religious community. |例句：The new church serves the entire parish. 新教堂为整个教区服务。

例句：Our parish is holding a fundraising event next week. 我们教区下周将举办一个筹款活动。


近义词：district, area, community | 反义词：diocese (a larger area encompassing multiple parishes) | 词性:名词
