mid-range:/mɪdˈreɪndʒ/| adj.|中档的，中等的|  Describes something that is neither high-end nor low-end in terms of price, quality, or performance. It occupies a position between extremes.  |例句：This hotel offers mid-range accommodation at reasonable prices. 这家酒店提供价格合理的经济型住宿。

例句：The restaurant serves mid-range dishes, appealing to a broad customer base. 这家餐馆供应中档菜肴，吸引了广泛的顾客群。


近义词：moderate, average, intermediate

反义词：high-end, top-of-the-line, low-end, budget


词性:形容词
