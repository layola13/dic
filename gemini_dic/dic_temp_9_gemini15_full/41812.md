corticose:/kɔːrˈtɪkoʊs/| adj.|皮质的，皮层的；树皮状的|Having a bark-like or corky texture.  The term refers to a texture resembling tree bark, often thick, rough, and somewhat spongy.|例句：The old tree trunk had a thick, corticose surface. 这棵老树的树干表面粗糙，像树皮一样。The corticose texture of the fungus was noticeable.|近义词：corticated, bark-like, corky|反义词：smooth, glabrous
