mistiest:/ˈmɪstiːst/| adj.| 最多雾的，最朦胧的 | Most covered with mist; foggiest.  The adjective "mistiest" is the superlative form of "misty," indicating the highest degree of mistiness. It describes a location or condition with the greatest amount of mist or fog. | Example: The mistiest morning of the year brought a mystical quality to the landscape.  今年最雾的早晨给这片风景带来了一种神秘的氛围。|近义词：foggiest, cloudiest, haziest |反义词： clearest, sunniest
