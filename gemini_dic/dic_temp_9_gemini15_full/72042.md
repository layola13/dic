flirt:/flɜːrt/| /flɝːt/ | n., v. |调情；轻浮的人；轻浮的动作 |To behave as though sexually attracted to someone, but without serious intentions.  To playfully tease or touch someone. |例句：He was flirting with the waitress. 他在和女服务员调情。

She gave him a flirtatious glance. 她给了他一个调情的眼神。


近义词：tease, coquet, woo,  dally | 反义词：ignore, shun, spurn
