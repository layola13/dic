pedesis:/pɪˈdiːsɪs/| n. |布朗运动；微粒运动|The erratic, random movement of microscopic particles suspended in a fluid, caused by collisions with the fluid molecules.  This phenomenon is also known as Brownian motion.|Example:  The pedesis of pollen grains in water is easily observable under a microscope.  水中的花粉颗粒的布朗运动在显微镜下很容易观察到。|近义词：Brownian motion|反义词：N/A
