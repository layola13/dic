redefeating:/ˌriːdiˈfiːtɪŋ/ | v. | 重复击败；再次战胜 |  The act of defeating someone or something again after a previous defeat.  It implies a repeated victory over the same opponent or challenge. | Example:  The champion was confident in redefeating his rival.  冠军有信心再次击败他的对手。 |近义词: repeatedly defeating, conquering again, overcoming anew | 反义词: being defeated, losing again
