chaffs:/tʃæfs/| n. v.|谷壳；开玩笑；戏弄；|The verb "chaff" means to tease playfully. The noun "chaff" refers to the husks of grain separated from the seed.  |例句：He chaffed his friend about his new haircut. 他开玩笑地取笑了他朋友的新发型。The farmer separated the wheat from the chaff. 农民把麦粒和麦糠分开了。|近义词： (v.) tease, banter, jest; (n.) rubbish, trash|反义词： (v.) praise, compliment; (n.) grain, seed|名词，动词


USAGE->

He chaffs his friends mercilessly. = 他无情地戏弄他的朋友们。
The machine separates the grain from the chaff. = 机器将谷物与谷壳分离。
