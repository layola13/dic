mosquito-nets: /ˌmɒˈskiːtoʊ.nɛts/ | n. |蚊帐| A net, typically made of fine mesh, hung over a bed or sleeping area to keep out mosquitoes and other insects.  | Example:  We slept soundly under the mosquito net. 我们在蚊帐下睡得很香。 |近义词：insect net, bed net | 反义词：None
