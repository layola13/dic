octopod:/ˈɑːktəpɒd/|/ˈɑːktəpɑːd/| n. | 章鱼 | An octopod is a cephalopod mollusk of the order Octopoda, characterized by eight arms.  They are known for their intelligence, camouflage abilities, and venomous bite (in some species). | Example: The diver spotted a giant Pacific octopod hiding amongst the rocks.  潜水员在岩石中发现了一只巨大的太平洋章鱼。  The researcher studied the complex behaviors of the common octopod. 研究人员研究了普通章鱼的复杂行为。|近义词：octopus |反义词：None.
