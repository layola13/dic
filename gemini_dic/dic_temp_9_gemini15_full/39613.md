consent-to:/kənˈsɛnt tuː/| v.|同意，赞成 |to give agreement or permission|例句：I consent to the terms and conditions. 我同意条款和条件。|近义词：agree to, approve of, accept|反义词：object to, refuse, disagree with


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He consents to the proposal. = 他同意这个提议。
They consent to our request. = 他们同意我们的请求。

一般过去时:
She consented to the marriage. = 她同意了这桩婚事。
The board consented to the merger. = 董事会同意了合并。

现在完成时:
Have they consented to the changes? = 他们同意这些改变了吗？
We have consented to their terms. = 我们已经同意了他们的条件。

过去完成时:
He had already consented to the plan before the meeting. = 他在会议之前就已经同意了这个计划。
They had consented to the interview earlier that week. = 他们在那周早些时候已经同意了采访。


将来时:
Will she consent to the operation? = 她会同意手术吗？
They will consent to our terms, I'm sure. = 我相信他们会同意我们的条件。

虚拟语气:
If he consented to the deal, we would be in a much better position. = 如果他同意这笔交易，我们的处境会好得多。
I wish they consented to our plan. = 我希望他们同意我们的计划。

被动语态:
The proposal was consented to by the committee. = 该提议得到了委员会的同意。
His request was consented to by his manager. = 他的请求得到了经理的同意。

疑问句:
Does he consent to the terms? = 他同意条款吗？
Will they consent to our request? = 他们会同意我们的请求吗？

否定句:
He did not consent to the search. = 他不同意搜查。
She did not consent to the interview. = 她不同意接受采访。
