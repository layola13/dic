over-mixing:/ˈoʊvərˌmɪksɪŋ/ | n. |过度混合，搅拌过度|The act of mixing something, such as a batter or dough, for too long, resulting in a less desirable texture or outcome.  Over-mixing can lead to tough, chewy baked goods or a lack of airiness in certain dishes.|Example: Over-mixing the cake batter resulted in a dense and heavy cake.  过度搅拌蛋糕糊导致蛋糕变得浓稠而沉重。|近义词：overbeating, excessive mixing |反义词：undermixing
