isometrics:/ˌaɪsoʊˈmɛtrɪks/ | n. |等长运动，等长练习 |The branch of exercise physiology that deals with the application of isometric exercises.  Isometric exercises involve contracting muscles without changing the length of the muscle or the angle at the joint.  These exercises are useful for strengthening muscles and improving muscle tone, particularly for rehabilitation after injury or surgery. |Example:  Isometric exercises, such as planks and wall sits, are a great way to build strength. 等长运动，例如平板支撑和靠墙静蹲，是增强力量的好方法。|近义词：static exercise, static contraction |反义词：isotonic exercise, dynamic exercise |名词
