call-sick:/kɔːl sɪk/| phrasal verb| 告病假；请病假 | To call one's employer to inform them that one will not be coming to work because of illness.  |例句：I had to call sick this morning because I woke up with a terrible headache.  今天早上我不得不请病假，因为我醒来时头痛得厉害。

例句：She called sick yesterday, claiming she had the flu. 昨天她请病假，声称自己得了流感。


近义词：take a sick day, phone in sick | 反义词：come to work, work through illness

词性:phrasal verb
