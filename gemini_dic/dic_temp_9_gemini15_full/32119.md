/ˈtʃɛtnɪk/| n. |切特尼克（南斯拉夫游击队成员）|A Chetnik was a member of a Serbian royalist guerrilla movement during World War II in Yugoslavia.  The term is now often used as a derogatory slur. |The Chetniks fought against both the Axis and the Partisans during the war.|近义词：Yugoslav royalist guerrilla (during WWII), partisan (in a negative context)|反义词：Partisan (in the context of WWII Yugoslavia)|名词
