demobilising: /ˌdiːˈməʊbəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 解散；复员；使…停止运转 |  The act of demobilizing; the process of disbanding or returning from military service.  It can also refer to the act of stopping the operation or function of something. |

例句：

The government is demobilizing the army after the war. = 战争结束后，政府正在遣散军队。

The company is demobilising its workforce due to economic downturn. = 由于经济低迷，公司正在裁员。


近义词：disbanding, discharging, decommissioning, dismantling

反义词：mobilizing, activating
