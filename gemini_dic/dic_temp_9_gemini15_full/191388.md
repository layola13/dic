schematics:/ʃɪˈmætɪks/| n. |电路图；原理图；图表|A schematic is a diagram showing the arrangement of components in a system, such as an electronic circuit or a mechanical device. It simplifies complex systems by representing them with symbols and lines.  |The engineer reviewed the schematics before starting the assembly. 工程师在开始组装前检查了电路图。  The schematics for the new building were impressive. 新建筑的图纸令人印象深刻。|diagram, blueprint, plan|complex drawing, photograph|名词
