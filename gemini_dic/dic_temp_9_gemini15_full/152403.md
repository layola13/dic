paving:/ˈpeɪvɪŋ/| n.|铺路；铺面；路面|The process of laying down a hard surface, such as concrete or asphalt, on a road, path, or other area.  It also refers to the surface itself.|例句：The new paving on the driveway looks great. 新铺的车道路面看起来很棒。|例句：The uneven paving made walking difficult. 不平整的路面使行走困难。|近义词：surfacing, laying, construction|反义词：destruction, demolition|名词
