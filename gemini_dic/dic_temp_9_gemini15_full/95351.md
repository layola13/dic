incentivizing: /ɪnˈsɛntaˌvaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 激励；刺激；鼓励 |  The act of providing an incentive; motivating someone to do something.  | 例句：The company is incentivizing employees to work harder by offering bonuses. 公司通过提供奖金来激励员工更加努力工作。

例句：The government is incentivizing renewable energy development through tax breaks. 政府通过减税来鼓励可再生能源的发展。


近义词：motivating, stimulating, encouraging, inspiring | 反义词：discouraging, demotivating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company incentivizes its employees with regular bonuses. = 公司定期发放奖金来激励员工。
They incentivize healthy eating habits by providing healthy snacks. = 他们通过提供健康零食来鼓励健康的饮食习惯。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
