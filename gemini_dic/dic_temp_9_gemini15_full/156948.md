pilfery:/ˈpɪlfəri/| n. |偷窃；少量盗窃；偷盗行为 |The act of stealing, especially in small quantities.  It often implies petty theft or dishonesty. |例句：The pilfery of office supplies is a surprisingly common problem.  办公室用品的少量盗窃是一个非常普遍的问题。

例句：He was accused of pilfery, but the evidence was weak. 他被指控偷窃，但证据不足。


近义词：theft, larceny, stealing, petty theft, shoplifting

反义词：honesty, generosity


词性：名词
