prophylactics:/ˌprɒfɪˈlæktɪks/| n. |预防措施；避孕套|Preventive measures designed to prevent disease or pregnancy.  Prophylactics can refer to various methods, including medications, vaccines, and condoms, aimed at preventing the onset of a disease or unwanted pregnancy.|例句：The doctor prescribed prophylactics to prevent the spread of the infection.医生开了预防药以防止感染蔓延。|例句：Using prophylactics during sexual intercourse is crucial for preventing sexually transmitted infections. 性交时使用避孕套对于预防性传播感染至关重要。|近义词：preventives, safeguards, precautions|反义词：None directly, but "risk factors" could be considered an antonym in a specific context.|名词
