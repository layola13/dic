rebook:/ˌriːˈbʊk/| vt.|重新预订，再次预订|To book or reserve something again, typically after a previous booking has been canceled or changed.|例句：I had to rebook my flight after the storm delayed it.  我不得不因为暴风雨延误而重新预订我的航班。|例句： We rebooked our hotel room for a larger one. 我们重新预订了一个更大的酒店房间。|近义词：re-reserve, re-schedule|反义词：cancel


USAGE->

They rebook their flights every year. = 他们每年都重新预订航班。
She rebooks her appointments regularly. = 她定期重新预约她的约会。
