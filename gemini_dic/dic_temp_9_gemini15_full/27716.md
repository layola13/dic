caned:/keɪnd/| v.| 用藤条抽打；严厉批评 | to beat (someone) with a cane; to criticize severely. |例句：The teacher caned the disobedient student.老师用藤条抽打了不听话的学生。The critic caned the author's new novel. 批评家严厉批评了作者的新小说。|近义词：flogged, lashed, thrashed, criticized, reprimanded |反义词：praised, commended

一般现在时:
He canes his dog when it misbehaves. 他家的狗犯错时，他会用藤条抽打它。
They cane the students for minor infractions. 他们因为轻微的违规行为而体罚学生。
