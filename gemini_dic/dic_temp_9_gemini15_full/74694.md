freegan:/ˈfriːɡæn/| n.|免费食物者，反消费主义者|A freegan is a person who obtains much of their food from dumpsters and other discarded sources.  They are often motivated by environmental and anti-consumerist ideals. |例句：Many freegans believe that discarding edible food is wasteful and morally wrong. 许多免费食物者认为丢弃可食用食物是一种浪费且道德上错误的行为。

例句：The freegan community often shares tips and resources on finding discarded food. 免费食物者社群经常分享寻找丢弃食物的技巧和资源。

近义词：dumpster diver, scavenger|反义词：consumer, shopaholic
