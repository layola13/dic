goggle-box:/ˈɡɒɡl ˌbɒks/| n. |电视机|Television set;  a colloquial term for a television. The term originated in Britain and is still commonly used there.  It is less common in other English-speaking countries.| Example: "I spent the evening glued to the goggle-box." = 我整个晚上都盯着电视看。|近义词：TV, television, telly (British English)|反义词：无
