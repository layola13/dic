microscopist: /ˈmaɪkrəskəpɪst/ | n. | 显微镜操作员；显微镜学家 | A microscopist is a scientist or technician who uses a microscope to examine specimens and interpret the results.  They may specialize in a particular area, such as cytology or histology. | The experienced microscopist quickly identified the rare protozoa.  经验丰富的显微镜操作员很快识别出了这种罕见的原生动物。 The microscopist meticulously documented her findings with detailed drawings and photographs. 显微镜学家一丝不苟地用详细的图画和照片记录了她的发现。 | biologist, cytologist, histologist |  (无明显的反义词) | 名词
