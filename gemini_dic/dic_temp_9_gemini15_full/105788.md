kis:/kɪs/ | vt. n. |亲吻，轻吻 | To touch with the lips as a sign of affection, greeting, or respect.  |例句：He kissed her gently on the cheek. 他轻轻地吻了她的脸颊。 |例句：The mother gave her child a tender kiss. 母亲给了孩子一个温柔的吻。|近义词：smooch, peck, buss |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kisses her every morning. = 他每天早上都吻她。
She kisses the baby goodnight. = 她亲吻婴儿道晚安。

一般过去时:
They kissed passionately under the moonlight. = 他们在月光下热情地接吻。
He kissed her hand respectfully. = 他恭敬地吻了她的手。

现在完成时:
I have kissed many frogs in my life. = 我一生亲吻过许多青蛙。（比喻）
She has never kissed anyone before. = 她以前从未吻过任何人。

过去完成时:
He had kissed her before he proposed. = 他在她求婚之前就吻了她。
They had kissed several times before the argument. = 他们争吵之前已经亲吻了好几次。

将来时:

He will kiss his bride at the wedding. = 他将在婚礼上亲吻他的新娘。

虚拟语气:

I wish I could kiss her right now. = 我希望我现在能吻她。

The baby was kissed gently by its mother. = 婴儿被母亲轻轻地吻了。

疑问句:

否定句:
