hyperspace:/ˈhaɪpərspeɪs/| n. |超空间，高维空间|A region beyond the normal three dimensions of space, often postulated in science fiction and theoretical physics. It is often depicted as a realm where faster-than-light travel is possible.|例句：In the movie, the spaceship jumps to hyperspace to escape the enemy.  在电影中，宇宙飞船跃迁到超空间以躲避敌人。

例句：The theoretical physicist proposed a new model of hyperspace that could explain dark matter.  理论物理学家提出了一种新的超空间模型，可以解释暗物质。


近义词：higher dimension, extra dimension|反义词：normal space, three-dimensional space|名词
