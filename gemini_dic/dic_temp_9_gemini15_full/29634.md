catered:/ˈkeɪtərd/| v.| 迎合；提供饮食；满足…的需求 | To supply food and drinks, typically at an event.  |例句：The party was catered by a local restaurant.  这个派对由当地一家餐馆承办餐饮。|例句：The hotel catered to the needs of business travelers. 这家酒店满足商务旅客的需求。|近义词：supplied, provided, served |反义词：ignored, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The restaurant caters to a wide range of tastes. = 这家餐馆迎合各种各样的口味。
They cater events of all sizes. = 他们承办各种规模的活动。

一般过去时:
We catered our daughter's wedding last year. = 去年我们承办了女儿的婚礼。
The company catered the conference. = 公司为会议提供了餐饮服务。

现在完成时:
She has catered many high-profile events. = 她已经承办过许多大型活动。
We have catered for over 100 guests this year. = 今年我们已经为超过100位宾客提供餐饮服务。

过去完成时:
They had catered the event before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经为活动准备好了餐饮。
The chef had catered many parties before opening his own restaurant. = 大厨在开自己的餐馆之前已经承办过许多派对。

将来时:
We will cater your next party. = 我们将承办你的下一个派对。
They will cater the wedding reception. = 他们将承办婚礼招待会。

虚拟语气:
If we had more staff, we could cater more events. = 如果我们有更多员工，我们就能承办更多活动。
I wish I could cater the event myself, but I'm too busy. = 我希望我能自己承办这次活动，但是我太忙了。

被动语态:
The event was catered by a professional catering company. = 这场活动由一家专业的餐饮公司承办。
The food was catered to the specific dietary needs of the guests. = 食物是根据客人的具体饮食需求提供的。

疑问句:
Does this restaurant cater to vegetarians? = 这家餐馆迎合素食者吗？
Will you cater the party or should we hire a caterer? = 你会承办这个派对还是我们应该雇佣一个承办人？

否定句:
The restaurant doesn't cater to large groups. = 这家餐馆不接待大型团体。
They didn't cater the event themselves; they hired a caterer. = 他们没有自己承办活动；他们雇了一个承办人。
