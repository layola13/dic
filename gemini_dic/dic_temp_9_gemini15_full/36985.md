colum:/ˈkɑːləm/| n. |专栏；柱；列；圆柱|A vertical section of a printed page or screen, typically containing a series of articles or advertisements.  A vertical support or pillar. A line of figures or items arranged vertically.|例句：He writes a regular column for the newspaper. 他为这家报纸定期撰写专栏文章。

例句：The architect designed elegant columns to support the building. 建筑师设计了优雅的柱子来支撑建筑物。|近义词：columnar, pillar, post |反义词：row (horizontal arrangement)
