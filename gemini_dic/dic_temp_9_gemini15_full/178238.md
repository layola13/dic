recompense:/ˈrɛkəmpɛns/ | n./vt.|补偿；报答；报酬 | To make amends for a wrong; to repay (someone) for a service, loss, or injury.  |例句：The company offered a recompense for the damaged goods. 公司为损坏的货物提供了赔偿。

例句：He received a generous recompense for his years of service. 他因为他多年的服务而获得了丰厚的报酬。


近义词：compensation, amends, reward, repayment | 反义词：penalty, punishment


词性：名词和动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company recompenses employees for overtime. = 公司补偿员工的加班费。
He recompenses his workers fairly. = 他公平地报答他的工人。


一般过去时:
They recompensed the victims of the accident. = 他们赔偿了事故的受害者。
She recompensed him for his help. = 她报答了他的帮助。


现在完成时:
The government has recompensed the farmers for their losses. = 政府已经赔偿了农民的损失。
He has recompensed me for my efforts. = 他已经报答了我的努力。


过去完成时:
They had recompensed the injured party before the trial. = 在审判之前，他们已经赔偿了受伤的一方。
She had recompensed her assistant for the extra work. = 她已经补偿了她的助理的额外工作。


将来时:
The insurance company will recompense you for the damage to your car. = 保险公司将赔偿你汽车的损坏。
They will recompense the workers for their lost wages. = 他们将补偿工人们失去的工资。


虚拟语气:
If the company had acted responsibly, they would have recompensed the customers. = 如果公司负责任地行事，他们就会赔偿顾客。
I wish they would recompense me for my time. = 我希望他们能补偿我的时间。


被动语态:
The damaged goods were recompensed by the seller. = 损坏的货物由卖家赔偿。
The workers will be recompensed for their hard work. = 工人们将因为他们的辛勤工作而得到报答。


疑问句:
Will the company recompense us for the delay? = 公司会赔偿我们因为延误而造成的损失吗？
How will you recompense your friend for their kindness? = 你将如何报答朋友的善意？


否定句:
The company did not recompense the employee for their lost wages. = 公司没有赔偿员工失去的工资。
They refused to recompense the victim. = 他们拒绝赔偿受害者。
