infantries:/ˈɪnfəntriːz/| n. |步兵部队；步兵兵种|The plural form of "infantry," referring to multiple units of foot soldiers.  It denotes the collective body of infantry soldiers or the various infantry regiments or divisions within an army.|例句：The allied infantries advanced on the enemy lines. 盟军步兵部队向敌军防线推进。
例句：Several infantries participated in the large-scale military exercise.好几支步兵部队参加了这次大型军事演习。|近义词：foot soldiers, ground troops|反义词：airborne troops, cavalry|词性:n.
