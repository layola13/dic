chipmunk:/ˈtʃɪpmʌŋk/| n. |花栗鼠|A small, striped rodent with large cheek pouches, often found in North America and Eurasia.|例句：I saw a chipmunk scampering across the path. 我看到一只花栗鼠在小路上飞奔。

例句：The chipmunk stuffed its cheeks full of nuts.  花栗鼠把脸颊塞满了坚果。


近义词：ground squirrel (部分种类)|反义词：无

词源:
The word "chipmunk" is believed to be of Algonquian origin, possibly from a word meaning "red squirrel" or a similar description.  The specific Algonquian language and precise etymology are uncertain, but the word reflects early contact between European settlers and Indigenous peoples of North America.
