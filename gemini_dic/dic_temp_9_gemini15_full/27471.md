campaned:/kæmˈpeɪnd/| v.|举行运动；积极从事；奔走；| to actively promote or support a cause or idea; to campaign |The politician campaigned tirelessly for election.|近义词：campaign for, advocate for, promote|反义词：oppose, resist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They campaign for better schools. = 他们在为更好的学校而奔走。
She campaigns against animal cruelty. = 她积极从事反对虐待动物的运动。


一般过去时:
He campaigned for the presidency. = 他竞选总统。
They campaigned vigorously for environmental protection. = 他们为环境保护积极奔走。


现在完成时:
She has campaigned for years to improve healthcare. = 她为改善医疗保健奔走了多年。
They have campaigned extensively to raise awareness. = 他们为提高认识进行了广泛的宣传。


过去完成时:
He had campaigned for months before the election. = 大选前他已经奔走了几个月。
They had campaigned extensively before the bill was passed. = 法案通过前他们进行了大量的宣传活动。


将来时:
She will campaign for a new library. = 她将为一个新的图书馆而奔走。
They will campaign aggressively to win the election. = 他们将积极竞选以赢得选举。


虚拟语气:
If he campaigned harder, he might win. = 如果他更努力地竞选，他可能会获胜。
If she had campaigned more effectively, she would have won. = 如果她更有效地开展竞选活动，她就会赢了。


被动语态:
The issue was campaigned for by many groups. = 许多团体都为这个问题奔走。
The election was campaigned hard for by both sides. = 双方都为这次选举进行了激烈的竞选。


疑问句:
Are they campaigning for a specific candidate? = 他们在为某个特定的候选人竞选吗？
Will you be campaigning for the environmental cause? = 你会为环保事业奔走吗？


否定句:
He didn't campaign for the position. = 他没有竞选这个职位。
They did not campaign effectively. = 他们的竞选活动开展得不力。


PHRASE->
campaign against = 反对…而展开运动
campaign for = 为…而奔走
election campaign = 选举运动
marketing campaign = 市场营销活动
public awareness campaign = 公众意识宣传活动
