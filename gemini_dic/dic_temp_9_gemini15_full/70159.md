fibbed:/fɪbd/| v.|撒谎，说谎 | To tell a lie; to lie. |例句：He fibbed about his age to get into the club. 他谎报年龄以进入俱乐部。


He fibbed to his mother about breaking the vase. 他向他母亲撒谎说没有打破花瓶。|近义词：lied,  prevaricated,  equivocated |反义词：told the truth


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fibs about his accomplishments. 他夸大自己的成就。
She often fibs to avoid trouble. 她经常撒谎以避免麻烦。

一般过去时:
I fibbed to my teacher about my homework. 我向老师撒谎了我的作业。
He fibbed to his parents about where he was last night. 他向父母撒谎了他昨晚在哪里。

现在完成时:
She has fibbed to me many times. 她多次对我撒谎。
He has fibbed about his qualifications for the job. 他关于自己工作的资格撒了谎。

过去完成时:
I had fibbed to them before, so they didn't believe me. 我以前就对他们撒过谎，所以他们不相信我。
She had fibbed countless times, making it hard to trust her. 她无数次撒谎，这使得很难相信她。

将来时:
I will fib if I have to. 如果必须的话我会撒谎。
He will likely fib again if given the opportunity.  如果给他机会，他很可能会再次撒谎。

虚拟语气:
If I fibbed, I would feel guilty. 如果我撒谎，我会感到内疚。
I wish I hadn't fibbed to her. 我真希望我没有对她撒谎。

被动语态:
The truth was fibbed about by the witness.  证人对真相撒了谎。
His age was fibbed about on his application. 他的年龄在申请表上被谎报了。

疑问句:
Did you fib about your experience? 你对你的经验撒谎了吗？
Has she ever fibbed to you before? 她以前对你撒过谎吗？

否定句:
I didn't fib about my qualifications. 我没有对我的资格撒谎。
He wouldn't fib to his best friend. 他不会对他最好的朋友撒谎。


PHRASE->
fib a little:  稍微撒个小谎
fib about something:  就某事撒谎
a harmless fib: 一个无伤大雅的谎言
a white lie: 一个善意的谎言
a fibber: 说谎者
