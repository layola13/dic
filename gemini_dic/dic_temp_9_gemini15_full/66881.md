expectorator: /ɪkˈspektəreɪtər/ | n. | 祛痰剂；咳痰者 | A person who expectorates; a device for expectorating.  | Example:  The doctor prescribed an expectorant to help the patient clear their lungs. 医生开了祛痰药帮助病人清理肺部。  | 近义词: expectorant (n.), cough medicine (n.) | 反义词:  None specifically, but words suggesting suppression of cough or mucus could be considered antonyms in context. | 名词

词源:
