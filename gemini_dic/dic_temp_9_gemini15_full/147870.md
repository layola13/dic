overreached:/ˌoʊvərˈiːtʃt/| v.| 权力过大；越权；做得过分；|To exceed one's authority or limits; to go too far.  |例句：The company overreached in its expansion plans and lost a significant amount of money. 公司扩张计划做得太过火，损失了一大笔钱。
例句：He overreached himself by criticizing the senior manager directly. 他直接批评高级经理，有些过分了。|近义词：exceed, trespass, go too far |反义词：underestimate, restrain, hold back


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He overreaches his authority frequently. = 他经常滥用职权。
They overreach in their marketing campaigns. = 他们的营销活动做得太过火了。


一般过去时:
The government overreached in its attempt to control the media. = 政府试图控制媒体的行为太过分了。
She overreached herself by taking on too many projects at once. = 她同时承担了太多的项目，力不从心。


现在完成时:
The company has overreached in its attempts to dominate the market. = 这家公司试图垄断市场，做得太过火了。
He has overreached in his ambition and lost everything. = 他野心太大，结果什么都失去了。


过去完成时:

She had overreached by promising more than she could deliver. = 她承诺得太多，结果无法兑现。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
