gracious:/ˈɡreɪʃəs/| adj.|优雅的；亲切的；慈祥的；有礼貌的 |Showing kindness and courtesy; courteous.  The adjective "gracious" describes someone who is pleasant, kind, and courteous in their behavior and interactions with others. It can also describe something that is elegant or refined. |例句：She has a gracious manner. 她举止优雅。

例句：He gave a gracious speech. 他发表了一篇优雅的演讲。


近义词：courteous, polite, kind, charming, elegant, refined

反义词：rude, impolite, discourteous, ungracious, crude


词源：源自古法语gracieux，最终源自拉丁语gratia（恩典，感激）。
