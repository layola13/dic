particles:/ˈpɑːrtɪkəlz/| n.|粒子，微粒|Tiny pieces of matter.  Particles can be atoms, molecules, or other small units.|例句：Dust particles filled the air.  灰尘颗粒充满了空气。
例句：The scientist studied the behavior of subatomic particles. 科学家研究了亚原子粒子的行为。|近义词：specks, grains, bits|反义词：whole, mass
