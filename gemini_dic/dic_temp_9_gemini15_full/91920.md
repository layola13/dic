hypermarket:/ˈhaɪpəmɑːrkɪt/ | n. |大型超市，超级市场 | A very large supermarket selling a wide variety of goods at discounted prices.  |  例句：We did our weekly shop at the hypermarket. 我们在大型超市进行了每周的购物。

例句：The new hypermarket offers a vast selection of products.  这家新开的超级市场提供种类繁多的商品。


近义词：supermarket, megastore | 反义词：small shop, boutique


词性：名词
