exams:/ɪɡˈzæmz/ | n. |考试，测验 | Examinations are a way to assess students' understanding of the subject matter. 考试是评估学生对学科理解的一种方式。 |近义词：tests, assessments, examinations |反义词：relaxation, break


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The exams are next week. = 考试在下周。
He takes many exams every semester. = 他每个学期都要参加很多考试。

一般过去时:
I passed all my exams last year. = 我去年所有的考试都过了。
She failed her driving exam. = 她驾驶考试不及格。

现在完成时:
I have finished all my exams. = 我已经完成了所有的考试。
She has never failed an exam before. = 她以前从未考试不及格过。

过去完成时:
I had already completed my exams before the holiday started. = 假期开始前我已经完成了考试。
They had studied hard for the exams before taking them. = 他们在参加考试之前为考试做了充分的准备。

将来时:
I will take the entrance exams next month. = 我下个月将参加入学考试。
She will have finished all her exams by the end of the term. = 学期结束前她将完成所有考试。

虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I would have passed my exams. = 如果我学习更努力，我就通过考试了。
I wish I had done better on my exams. = 我希望我的考试成绩更好。

被动语态:
The exams are graded by the professor. = 考试由教授评分。
The results of the exams will be announced next week. = 考试结果将于下周公布。

疑问句:
When are the exams? = 考试是什么时候？
What subjects are included in the exams? = 考试包括哪些科目？

否定句:
I didn't study for the exams. = 我没有为考试学习。
She wasn't prepared for the exams. = 她没有为考试做好准备。
