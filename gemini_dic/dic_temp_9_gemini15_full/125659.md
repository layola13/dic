mump:/mʌmp/| n. | 腮腺炎；愠怒；嘟囔；沉默寡言 | A viral infection that causes swelling of the salivary glands (especially the parotid glands).  Also, a sullen silence or a mumbled complaint. |例句：He had the mumps as a child. 他小时候得过腮腺炎。

例句：She sat in a mump, refusing to speak to anyone. 她闷闷不乐地坐着，拒绝和任何人说话。|近义词：mumps (disease), sulk, grumble, pout |反义词：cheerful, talkative, happy
