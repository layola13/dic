pendence:/ˈpɛndəns/| n.| 依赖；悬而未决；未决状态 | Dependence is a state of relying on someone or something for support.  It can also refer to something that is unresolved or pending. |例句：Her dependence on her parents worried her.  她的依赖父母让她担忧。
例句：The pendency of the case caused delays. 此案的未决状态导致了延误。|近义词：reliance, dependency, uncertainty |反义词：independence, self-reliance, resolution
