randomness:/ˈrændəmnis/| n. |随机性，随意性 |The inherent unpredictability of a process or event; lack of pattern or regularity.  It refers to the quality of being without any clear pattern or plan. |例句：The randomness of the lottery ensures fairness. 彩票的随机性确保了公平性。

例句：The apparent randomness of the data actually hides a complex underlying pattern. 数据的表面随机性实际上隐藏着复杂的潜在模式。


近义词：chance, haphazardness, arbitrariness, fortuitousness| 反义词：predictability, order, regularity, determinism

词源：源自于中古英语 *random,  最终来自古法语 randon "swift, impetuous, violent,"  可能来自日耳曼语源。
