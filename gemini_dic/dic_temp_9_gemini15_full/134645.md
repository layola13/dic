nonmalignancy: /ˌnɒn məˈlɪɡnənsi/ | n. | 非恶性；良性 | The absence of malignancy; a benign condition.  It refers to a condition or growth that is not cancerous and does not have the potential to spread to other parts of the body.  | Example: The biopsy results showed nonmalignancy, relieving the patient's anxiety.  生物活检结果显示为良性，减轻了病人的焦虑。 |近义词: benignity, innocuousness | 反义词: malignancy, cancer |  名词
