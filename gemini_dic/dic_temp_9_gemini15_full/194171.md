self-interviews:/ˌsɛlfˈɪntərvjuːz/ | n. | 自我面试；自我访谈 | The process of conducting an interview with oneself, often used for self-reflection, job application preparation, or practicing interview skills.  | Example:  She conducted several self-interviews to prepare for her upcoming job interview. 她进行了几次自我面试，为即将到来的工作面试做准备。|近义词：self-reflection, introspection, self-assessment | 反义词：interview with others
