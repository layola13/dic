macroscopically:/ˌmækrəˈskɑːpɪkli/| adj. |宏观地，肉眼可见地 |Visible to the naked eye or with the aid of a simple magnifying glass; relating to large-scale structures or processes.  It describes something that can be observed without the use of a microscope or other magnification tools.  |Example: The cracks in the concrete were macroscopically visible. 混凝土中的裂缝肉眼可见。 The experiment showed macroscopically significant changes. 实验显示出宏观上显著的变化。|近义词：visibly, overtly, outwardly|反义词：microscopically, invisibly
