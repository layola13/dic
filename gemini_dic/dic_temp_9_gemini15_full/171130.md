psychoanalyse: /ˌsaɪkoʊəˈnælɪs/ | vt. |精神分析|To interpret thoughts, feelings, and behavior using the principles of psychoanalysis.  This involves exploring the unconscious mind to understand the root causes of psychological issues.| 例句：The therapist attempted to psychoanalyse the patient's recurring nightmares. 治疗师试图对病人的反复出现的噩梦进行精神分析。

The doctor decided to psychoanalyse the patient's childhood trauma to understand their current anxieties. 医生决定对病人的童年创伤进行精神分析以了解他们目前的焦虑。


近义词：analyse, interpret, explore (the unconscious) | 反义词：N/A | 词性：动词

词源:  The word "psychoanalyse" is derived from "psycho-" (relating to the mind) and "-analyse" (to examine methodically).  It's a blend of Greek and English roots, reflecting its origins in the field of psychology, largely founded by Sigmund Freud.
