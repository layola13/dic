prolegomenary:/ˌproʊˈlɛɡəməneri/| adj. |导论的；绪论的；前言的 |Preliminary remarks or discussions; introductory.  It often refers to a section of a book or work that precedes the main body, providing background information or context.  |例句：The author included a prolegomenary chapter outlining the historical context of the study. 作者在书中加入了一个绪论章节，概述了这项研究的历史背景。

The prolegomenary remarks set the stage for the main argument.  前言的陈述为主要论点奠定了基础。|近义词：preliminary, introductory, prefatory, preliminary |反义词：concluding, final, conclusive |形容词
