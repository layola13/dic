inebriation:/ɪnˌibrɪˈeɪʃən/| n. |醉酒；陶醉；沉醉|The state of being intoxicated by alcohol; drunkenness.  It can also refer to a state of exhilaration or overwhelming emotion.|例句：His inebriation led to a car accident. 他的醉酒导致了一场车祸。

例句：The inebriation of victory was palpable. 胜利的陶醉之情是显而易见的。|近义词：intoxication, drunkenness, tipsiness, exhilaration|反义词：sobriety, temperance, calmness|词性：名词
