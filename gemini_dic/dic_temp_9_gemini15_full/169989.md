protea:/ˈproʊtiːə/| n. |山龙眼属植物；普罗蒂亚花|A protea is a flowering plant belonging to the genus Protea, which is characterized by its large, showy flower heads and diverse forms.  They are native to regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. Many species are cultivated as ornamentals for their striking appearance and long-lasting flowers.|The protea is South Africa's national flower. |近义词：sugarbush (某些种类)|反义词：无
