resorts:/rɪˈzɔːrts/| n. |度假胜地；手段；求助|A place where people go for rest, recreation, or a particular activity.  It can also refer to a course of action adopted to solve a problem.|例句：We are planning a trip to a mountain resort. 我们计划去山区度假。|例句：He resorts to violence when he's angry. 他生气时会诉诸暴力。|近义词：destination, haven, refuge, strategy, tactic |反义词：neglect, avoidance |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The family resorts to the beach every summer. = 这个家庭每年夏天都去海滩度假。
This company resorts to unethical practices to increase profits. = 这家公司为了增加利润而采取不道德的手段。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

They will likely resort to intimidation tactics if negotiations fail. = 如果谈判失败，他们很可能会采取恐吓策略。


虚拟语气:
