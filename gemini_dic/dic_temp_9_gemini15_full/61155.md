else:/ɛls/ | adv. conj. | 否则；其他；另外 |  An adverb or conjunction indicating an alternative or exception.  It introduces a clause or phrase stating what happens or will happen if the condition in the main clause is not met.  It can also refer to something or someone other than what has already been mentioned. |

例句：
If it rains, we'll stay inside; else, we'll go for a walk. = 如果下雨，我们就待在室内；否则，我们就去散步。

He has no other friends else me. = 除了我，他再没有别的朋友了。


近义词：otherwise, or, alternatively
反义词：None (it doesn't have a direct antonym)
词性: 副词，连词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I'll go to the party, else I'll stay home and read. = 我要去参加聚会，否则我就待在家里看书。
If you don't study hard, you'll fail the exam; else you'll pass. = 如果你不努力学习，你就会考试不及格；否则你就会及格。


一般过去时:
He tried to call her, else he would have sent a text. = 他试图打电话给她，否则他会发短信。
They went to the beach, else they would have stayed at home. = 他们去了海滩，否则他们会待在家里。


现在完成时:
She has finished her work, else she would have stayed late. = 她已经完成了她的工作，否则她会加班。
We have already eaten, else we would be hungry now. = 我们已经吃过了，否则我们现在会饿。


过去完成时:
He had already left, else we would have seen him. = 他已经离开了，否则我们会看到他。
They had finished the project, else they would have been in trouble. = 他们已经完成了项目，否则他们就会有麻烦了。


将来时:
I will go to the doctor, else I will feel worse. = 我去看医生，否则我会感觉更糟。
We will call you tomorrow, else we will send an email. = 我们明天会给你打电话，否则我们会发邮件。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would buy a car; else I would take the bus. = 如果我有更多的钱，我会买一辆车；否则我会坐公共汽车。
If she had studied harder, she would have passed the exam; else she would have failed. = 如果她学习更努力，她就能通过考试；否则她就会不及格。


被动语态:
The work was completed on time, else delays would have occurred. = 工作按时完成，否则就会延误。
The problem was solved quickly, else serious consequences would have resulted. = 问题很快得到解决，否则就会造成严重后果。


疑问句:
Is there anything else I can help with? = 我还有什么可以帮忙的吗？
Would you like something else to drink? = 你想喝点别的吗？


否定句:
There is nothing else to do. = 没有别的事情可做了。
I don't want anything else. = 我什么也不想要了。

somebody else = 其他人
