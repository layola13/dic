hindering:/ˈhɪndəriŋ/| v.|阻碍，妨碍|To make it difficult for something to happen or for someone to do something.|The bad weather is hindering the rescue efforts. 恶劣的天气阻碍了救援工作。  His lack of confidence is hindering his progress. 他缺乏自信阻碍了他的进步。|hampering, obstructing, impeding|facilitating, assisting, aiding|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heavy rain is hindering our travel plans. = 大雨阻碍了我们的旅行计划。
Noise is hindering his concentration. = 噪音妨碍了他的注意力。

一般过去时:
The storm hindered the ship's departure. = 风暴阻碍了船的出发。
Technical problems hindered the project's completion. = 技术问题阻碍了项目的完成。

现在完成时:
Unexpected delays have hindered the project's progress. = 意想不到的延误阻碍了项目的进展。
Numerous obstacles have hindered our efforts. = 许多障碍阻碍了我们的努力。

过去完成时:
A previous injury had hindered his athletic performance. = 之前的伤势阻碍了他的运动表现。
Financial difficulties had hindered their plans to buy a house. = 经济困难阻碍了他们买房的计划。

将来时:
The strike will hinder production next week. = 下周的罢工将阻碍生产。
Further complications will hinder our ability to finish on time. = 进一步的复杂情况将阻碍我们按时完成工作。

虚拟语气:
If the traffic wasn't hindering us, we would be there by now. = 如果交通没有阻碍我们，我们现在就到了。
I wish nothing would hinder my success. = 我希望没有什么能阻碍我的成功。

被动语态:
The construction work was hindered by the bad weather. = 建设工程因恶劣天气而受阻。
Progress is being hindered by a lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，进展受到了阻碍。

疑问句:
What is hindering your progress? = 什么在阻碍你的进步？
Is anything hindering the investigation? = 有什么东西在阻碍调查吗？

否定句:
Nothing hindered his determination. = 没有什么能阻碍他的决心。
The lack of funds did not hinder the scientists’ research. = 资金缺乏并没有阻碍科学家的研究。
