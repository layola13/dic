portrayal:/pɔːrˈtreɪəl/| n.| 描绘；描写；扮演；刻画 |The act of portraying something or someone; a representation or depiction.  It can refer to a visual representation (like a painting) or a descriptive account.  It also refers to an actor's performance of a role.|例句：Her portrayal of the character was both convincing and moving.她的角色刻画既令人信服又感人。

The movie's portrayal of the war was criticized for its historical inaccuracies. 这部电影对战争的描写因其历史上的不准确性而受到批评。|近义词：depiction, representation, interpretation, presentation|反义词：misrepresentation, distortion
