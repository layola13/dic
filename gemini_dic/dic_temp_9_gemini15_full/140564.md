oligarchical:/ˌɒlɪˈɡɑːkɪkəl/| adj.|寡头政治的，寡头统治的| Relating to or characteristic of an oligarchy; ruled by a small group of people.  |Example: The country's oligarchical system stifled dissent and prevented genuine political participation.  这个国家的寡头政治体系压制了异见，阻止了真正的政治参与。|近义词：autocratic, authoritarian, plutocratic|反义词：democratic, egalitarian
