pseudonymic: /ˌsuːdəˈnɪmɪk/ | adj. | 假名的；匿名的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a pseudonym; using a pseudonym.  | 例句：He published several novels under a pseudonymic name. 他用笔名出版了几部小说。 | 近义词：anonymous, incognito,  | 反义词：real, genuine


例句：

The author chose to remain pseudonymic, protecting their identity from the public eye. 作者选择保持匿名，保护自己不被公众知晓。

Her pseudonymic writings gained popularity before her true identity was revealed.  她的匿名作品在她的真实身份被揭露之前就获得了很高的知名度。
