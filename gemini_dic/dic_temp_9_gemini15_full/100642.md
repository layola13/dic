intolerantly:/ɪnˈtɑːlərəntli/ | adv. | 不宽容地；顽固地 |  Showing or characterized by intolerance.  It describes the manner in which someone acts or behaves when they are intolerant. | He spoke intolerantly about the protesters. 他不耐烦地谈论抗议者。 She reacted intolerantly to different opinions. 她对不同的意见反应很激烈。 | tolerantly, patiently | tolerantly, patiently | 副词


USAGE->

They treat others intolerantly. = 他们待人刻薄。
He speaks intolerantly about immigrants. = 他对移民的言论很不宽容。
