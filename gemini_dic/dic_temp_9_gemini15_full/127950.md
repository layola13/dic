neoplasia:/niːˈoʊpleɪʒə/| n. |新生物，肿瘤形成|The abnormal growth of cells, resulting in the formation of a mass or tumor.  This can be benign or malignant. |Example:  The biopsy revealed the presence of neoplasia in the lung.  活检显示肺部存在肿瘤。  The doctor suspects a neoplasia based on the patient's symptoms. 医生根据病人的症状怀疑是肿瘤。 |近义词：tumor formation, tumorigenesis, abnormal growth |反义词：aplasia (absence of tissue development), normal cell growth
