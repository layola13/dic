guano:/ˈɡwɑːnoʊ/| n.|鸟粪, 蝙蝠粪|The word "guano" refers to the excrement of seabirds and bats, accumulated over time in large quantities.  It is a valuable natural fertilizer due to its high nitrogen and phosphate content.|例句：Peruvian guano is known for its high nutrient content. 秘鲁鸟粪以其高营养含量而闻名。

The islands off the coast of Peru are rich in guano deposits.  秘鲁沿海岛屿富含鸟粪沉积物。|近义词：dung, manure, droppings|反义词:|None|词性:名词
