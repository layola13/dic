holing:/ˈhoʊlɪŋ/ | v. | 打洞；藏匿；躲藏 |  To make a hole in something; to hide or conceal something or someone. |例句：The children were holing up in the attic. 孩子们躲在阁楼里。|近义词：burrowing, sheltering, concealing |反义词：exposing, revealing


USAGE->

They are holing up in a cabin in the woods. = 他们在森林里的小屋里躲藏。

一般过去时:
