incantations:/ˌɪnˌkænˈteɪʃənz/| n.|咒语；符咒；念咒 | Charms, spells, or magical formulas consisting of words or phrases chanted or sung to produce a desired effect.  They are often used in rituals or ceremonies. |例句：The witch uttered incantations to summon the spirits.  女巫念咒召唤精灵。

例句：Ancient texts describe powerful incantations used for healing. 古代文本描述了用于治疗的强大咒语。


近义词：spells, charms, conjurations, invocations |反义词：None directly opposite, but "rational explanation" could be considered a contrasting concept. |词性:n.
