orogen:/ˈɒrədʒən/ | n. |造山运动；山脉 |The process of mountain building, involving the deformation and uplift of the Earth's crust.  Orogeny is a complex geological process involving plate tectonics, faulting, folding, and metamorphism. |例句：The Himalayas are a result of a major orogeny. 喜马拉雅山脉是重大造山运动的结果。

The Appalachian Mountains were formed by a long series of orogenies. 阿巴拉契亚山脉是由一系列漫长的造山运动形成的。|近义词：mountain building, tectonics, uplift |反义词：erosion, denudation |名词
