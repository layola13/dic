scrappy:/ˈskræpi/| adj.|零碎的；顽强的；精力充沛的；斗志旺盛的 |Characterized by a determined, spirited, and often aggressive attitude;  showing determination and resilience despite difficulties.  Also refers to something consisting of small, irregular pieces. |例句：He's a scrappy underdog, always fighting back. 他是个顽强的弱者，总是奋力反击。  The artist used scrappy pieces of fabric in her collage.艺术家在拼贴画中使用了零碎的布料。|近义词：determined, tenacious, spirited, feisty, gritty, ragged |反义词：passive, docile, weak, meek, compliant
