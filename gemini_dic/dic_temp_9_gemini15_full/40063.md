contacting:/kənˈtæktɪŋ/ | v. | 联系，联络 | The act of communicating with someone or something.  This word is the present participle of the verb "contact." | Example: I am contacting the company about a faulty product. 我正在联系该公司关于一个有缺陷的产品。  |近义词：reaching, getting in touch with | 反义词：ignoring, avoiding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am contacting the supplier regularly. = 我定期联系供应商。
We are contacting all customers with the update. = 我们正在联系所有客户以更新信息。


一般过去时:
I contacted the office this morning. = 我今天早上联系了办公室。
They contacted him by phone yesterday. = 他们昨天通过电话联系了他。


现在完成时:
I have contacted them three times already. = 我已经联系他们三次了。
She has contacted the relevant authorities. = 她已经联系了相关部门。


过去完成时:
I had contacted him before he left. = 他离开之前我已经联系过他了。
They had contacted the police earlier that day. = 他们那天早些时候已经联系了警察。


将来时:
I will contact you as soon as I have the information. = 我一有信息就会联系你。
We will be contacting you next week to schedule a meeting. = 我们下周会联系你安排会议。


虚拟语气:
If I contacted them now, they might help. = 如果我现在联系他们，他们可能会帮忙。
I wish I had contacted the bank sooner. = 我真希望我早点联系银行。


被动语态:
He was contacted by the police. = 他被警方联系了。
The customers will be contacted soon. = 客户很快就会被联系。


疑问句:
Have you contacted the hospital? = 你联系医院了吗？
Are you contacting them today? = 你今天联系他们吗？


否定句:
I didn't contact him yesterday. = 我昨天没有联系他。
They haven't contacted us yet. = 他们还没有联系我们。


PHRASE->
contact information = 联系方式
make contact = 取得联系
stay in contact = 保持联系
lose contact = 失去联系
out of contact = 失去联系
