manichordon:/ˈmænɪkɔːrdən/| n. |曼尼科德琴（一种早期键盘乐器）|A manichordon is a stringed keyboard instrument, a precursor to the harpsichord and piano.  It produced sound by plucking strings with small plectra attached to the keys.  The sound is quieter and more delicate than that of a harpsichord. |The concert featured a rare performance on a restored manichordon.|近义词：harpsichord, clavichord |反义词：None
