coak:/koʊk/| vt.|焦化；烘焙；使…结块|To heat (coal, etc.) to a high temperature in the absence of air, driving off volatile substances.  |例句：The coal was coaked to produce coke.  煤被焦化以生产焦炭。

例句：The baker coaked the bread until it was perfectly golden brown.  面包师烘焙面包直到它变成完美的金棕色。


近义词：bake, char, carbonize|反义词：None specifically.


词性:动词
