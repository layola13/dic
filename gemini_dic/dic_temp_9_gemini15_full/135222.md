nonoxygenous: /ˌnɒnɒkˈsɪdʒənəs/ | adj. | 无氧的 | Not containing or requiring oxygen.  This term is used primarily in biological and chemical contexts to describe environments, processes, or organisms that do not utilize or require free oxygen. | Example:  "Nonoxygenous bacteria thrive in anaerobic environments." = “厌氧细菌在厌氧环境中茁壮成长。” | 近义词: anaerobic | 反义词: oxygenous, aerobic
