millimeter:/ˈmɪlɪmiːtər/ | n. |毫米|A millimeter is a unit of length equal to one thousandth of a meter (0.001 meter).  It is a common unit used in various fields, particularly those involving precise measurements. |例句：The thickness of the paper is approximately 0.1 millimeters. 这张纸的厚度大约是0.1毫米。
The screw is 5 millimeters in diameter.  这颗螺丝的直径是5毫米。|近义词：millimetre (British spelling)|反义词：kilometer, meter, centimeter
