deteriority:/dɪˌtɪəriˈɒrəti/| n. |恶化；变坏；衰退 |The deterioration of the building was evident in its crumbling facade. 建筑物的恶化在其崩塌的立面上显而易见。|近义词：decline, degradation, worsening |反义词：improvement, betterment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The building's condition shows deterioration. = 建筑物的状况显示出恶化。
His health is showing signs of deterioration. = 他的健康状况正在恶化。


一般过去时:
The quality of the product showed a rapid deterioration. = 产品的质量迅速下降。
The relationship deteriorated after the argument. = 争吵后，关系恶化了。


现在完成时:
The situation has deteriorated significantly since the accident. = 事故发生以来，情况已经严重恶化。
The economy has deteriorated over the past year. = 过去一年经济一直在恶化。


过去完成时:
The bridge had deteriorated significantly before the collapse. = 大桥在倒塌之前已经严重恶化。
His health had deteriorated considerably before he sought medical attention. = 他在就医之前健康状况已经相当恶化。


将来时:
The weather is forecast to deteriorate throughout the week. = 预报本周天气将持续恶化。
If we don't act, the situation will deteriorate further. = 如果我们不采取行动，情况将会进一步恶化。


虚拟语气:
If the situation had not deteriorated, we would have succeeded. = 如果情况没有恶化，我们就会成功。
I wish the situation hadn't deteriorated so quickly. = 我真希望情况没有恶化得这么快。


被动语态:
The building was allowed to deteriorate. = 这栋建筑被允许年久失修。
The situation is being allowed to deteriorate. = 情况被允许恶化。


疑问句:
Has the patient's condition deteriorated? = 病人的病情恶化了吗？
How much has the environment deteriorated? = 环境恶化了多少？


否定句:
The situation hasn't deteriorated as much as we feared. = 情况并没有像我们担心的那样恶化。
The quality of life has not deteriorated significantly. = 生活质量并没有显著下降。


PHRASE->
