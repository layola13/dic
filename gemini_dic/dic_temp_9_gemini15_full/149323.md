paced:/peɪst/| v. adj. n.|  踱步；调整速度；节奏；步调；| to walk at a steady speed, especially without hurrying;  to control the speed or rate of something. |例句：He paced nervously back and forth. 他紧张地来回踱步。 The project is being paced carefully.  这个项目正在谨慎地推进。|近义词：walk, stride,  meter, tempo, rhythm|反义词：rush, hasten, accelerate|动词，形容词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He paces the floor while he waits. = 他等待时在地板上踱步。
She paces her work to meet the deadline. = 她调整工作进度以赶上截止日期。


一般过去时:
He paced the room anxiously last night. = 他昨晚焦虑地在房间里踱步。
The director paced the film's release to maximize profits. = 导演调整电影的上映时间以最大化利润。


现在完成时:
He has paced himself well throughout the marathon. = 他在马拉松比赛中一直很好地控制着自己的节奏。
They have paced the development of the new product carefully. = 他们谨慎地控制着新产品的开发进度。


过去完成时:
He had paced himself too quickly in the first half of the race. = 他在比赛的前半段节奏太快了。
The company had paced its expansion too aggressively. = 公司的扩张步伐过于激进。


将来时:
She will pace herself during the exam. = 她将在考试期间控制好自己的节奏。
They will pace the rollout of the new software gradually. = 他们将逐步推出新软件。


虚拟语气:
If he had paced himself better, he would have won the race. = 如果他节奏更好一些，他就能赢得比赛。
If they had paced the project more effectively, it would have been completed on time. = 如果他们更有效地控制项目进度，它就能按时完成。


被动语态:
The race was paced by the lead runner. = 比赛的节奏由领跑者控制。
The project's development is being paced by market demand. = 项目的开发进度由市场需求决定。


疑问句:
How does she pace her work? = 她如何安排工作进度？
How will they pace the introduction of the new product? = 他们将如何安排新产品的推出？


否定句:
He didn't pace himself during the race. = 他在比赛中没有控制好自己的节奏。
They didn't pace the project effectively. = 他们没有有效地控制项目进度。
