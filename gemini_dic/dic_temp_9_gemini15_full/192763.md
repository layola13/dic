seahorse: /ˈsiːhɔːrs/ | n. |海马|A small marine fish with a horse-like head and a curled tail.  They are known for their unique appearance and the male's role in carrying the eggs.|例句：The seahorse gracefully moved through the coral reef. 海马优雅地游过珊瑚礁。
The aquarium has a large exhibit of different species of seahorses. 水族馆有一个大型展览，展出不同种类的海马。|近义词：sea dragon (though not a direct synonym, shares similar marine habitat and unusual appearance)|反义词：None|名词

词源:
The word "seahorse" is a compound word.  "Sea" refers to the marine environment where they live, and "horse" refers to the resemblance of their head to that of a horse.  The name is descriptive of its appearance.
