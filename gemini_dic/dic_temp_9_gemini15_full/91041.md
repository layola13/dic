husks:/hʌsks/| n.|谷壳；外壳；残余物|The dry outer covering of a seed or fruit, especially of grain.  It often refers to the inedible parts remaining after the useful part has been removed.|例句：The farmer carefully separated the wheat kernels from their husks.  农民仔细地将麦粒与麦壳分开。

例句：After the party, there were just husks of food left on the table. 派对结束后，桌子上只剩下残羹剩饭了。


近义词：shells, peels,chaff, remnants

反义词：kernels, cores, essence
