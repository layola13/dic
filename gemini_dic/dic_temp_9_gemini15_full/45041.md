cuban:/ˈkjuːbən/| adj. n. |古巴的；古巴人 |Of or relating to Cuba, its people, or its culture.  A native or inhabitant of Cuba.| 例句：He is a Cuban cigar aficionado. 他是一位古巴雪茄爱好者。

例句：She enjoys listening to traditional Cuban music. 她喜欢听传统的古巴音乐。


近义词：Cuban-American (if referring to a person of Cuban descent living in America)


反义词：无


词性：形容词，名词
