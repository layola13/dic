lighters:/ˈlaɪtərz/| n.|打火机|A small device used to light things, typically cigarettes or a fire, using a flame produced by a chemical reaction.|例句：He pulled out a lighter and lit his cigarette. 他拿出打火机点燃了他的香烟。|例句：The lighter ran out of fuel. 打火机没油了。|近义词：fire starter, igniter|反义词:extinguisher
