cough: /kɒf/ /kɑːf/ | v. n. | 咳嗽 |  To expel air from the lungs with a sudden, sharp sound, typically as a result of irritation or illness; the act of coughing. |例句：He coughed violently. 他剧烈地咳嗽。

例句：She has a bad cough. 她得了严重的咳嗽。


近义词：hack, splutter,  wheeze| 反义词：None | 词性：动词，名词


USAGE->

He coughs every morning. = 他每天早上都咳嗽。
