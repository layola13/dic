gossip:/ˈɡɒsɪp/ /ˈɡɑːsɪp/| n. v.|闲话，流言蜚语；说闲话，搬弄是非|

英文解析:
Noun: Informal talk or stories about other people, especially things that are not true.  The act of spreading rumors or discussing private matters of others.

Verb: To spread rumors or talk about other people's private lives.


例句:
(Noun)
Office gossip is rampant. = 办公室里流言蜚语很多。

I heard some juicy gossip about Sarah and Tom. = 我听到一些关于萨拉和汤姆的劲爆八卦。


(Verb)
Don't gossip about your colleagues. = 不要在背后议论你的同事。

She loves to gossip with her friends. = 她喜欢和朋友们说闲话。


近义词:
rumor, scuttlebutt, hearsay, chatter, idle talk, tale-bearing, scandal


反义词:
silence, discretion, secrecy


词性:
Noun, Verb

过去完成时:
