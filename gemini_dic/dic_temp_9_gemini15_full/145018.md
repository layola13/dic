overadvertise:/ˌoʊvərˈædvərtaɪz/| vt.|过度宣传，广告做过头|To advertise something too much or too aggressively.|例句：The company overadvertised its new product, leading to disappointment when sales didn't meet expectations. 公司对新产品进行了过度宣传，导致销售额未达预期时令人失望。|例句：They overadvertised their services, promising unrealistic results. 他们过度宣传他们的服务，承诺不切实际的结果。|近义词：hype, overpromote, oversell|反义词：underadvertise, downplay|动词
