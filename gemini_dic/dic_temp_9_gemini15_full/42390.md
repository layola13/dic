It is typically used to resist forces or stresses that might otherwise cause instability or failure.  | Example: The bridge's design incorporated a series of counterbraces to withstand strong winds.  桥梁的设计包含一系列的反撑以抵御强风。 |近义词: brace, strut, support |反义词:  (less direct antonyms, context dependent) unsupported, unstable |名词
