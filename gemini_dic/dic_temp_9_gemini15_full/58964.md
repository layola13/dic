duststorms:/ˈdʌstˌstɔːrmz/| n. |沙尘暴 |A dust storm is a meteorological phenomenon characterized by strong winds and a massive amount of dust or sand being lifted into the atmosphere, reducing visibility significantly.  Dust storms often occur in arid and semi-arid regions. |The dust storm reduced visibility to near zero.  沙尘暴使能见度降至几乎为零。The farmers suffered greatly from the severe dust storm. 农民遭受了严重的沙尘暴的巨大损失。|sandstorm, sandstorm, haboob|calm, clear weather|名词
