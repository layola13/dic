overfloods:/ˌoʊvərˈflʌdz/ | v. |泛滥，淹没 | To flood or overflow; to be overwhelmed by an excessive amount of something. |例句：The river overflooded its banks after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，河水漫过了河岸。


The heavy workload overflooded him, leaving him feeling exhausted.  繁重的工作让他不堪重负，感到精疲力尽。|近义词：inundate, deluge, swamp |反义词：drain, dry up |动词
