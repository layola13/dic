polygonization:/ˌpɒliɡənəˈzeɪʃən/|/ˌpɑːliɡənəˈzeɪʃən/| n. |多边形化；多边形形成|The process of converting a curved surface or a set of points into a set of polygons. This is commonly used in computer graphics and geographic information systems (GIS) to approximate complex shapes with simpler polygons for easier processing and rendering.  |Example: The polygonization algorithm efficiently converted the irregular coastline into a series of connected polygons.  多边形化算法有效地将不规则的海岸线转换为一系列连接的多边形。|近义词：tessellation, triangulation|反义词：smoothing, simplification (in the context of removing polygons)|
