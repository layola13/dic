deprecate:/ˈdɛprɪkeɪt/| vt.|贬低；反对；逐渐淘汰；轻视|To express disapproval of; to belittle; to regard something as being of little value.  The company deprecated the use of its older software, encouraging users to upgrade to the newer version. 公司不赞成使用旧软件，鼓励用户升级到新版本。|近义词：disapprove of, condemn, belittle, disparage|反义词：approve, praise, endorse|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They deprecate the use of slang in formal writing. = 他们反对在正式写作中使用俚语。
The company deprecates outdated technology. = 公司淘汰过时的技术。


一般过去时:
The critics deprecated his performance. = 批评家们贬低了他的表演。
The government deprecated the old tax system. = 政府废除了旧的税收制度。


现在完成时:
The industry has deprecated that method of production. = 该行业已经淘汰了这种生产方法。
They have deprecated the use of harmful chemicals. = 他们已经不再使用有害化学物质了。


过去完成时:
Before the new system was implemented, they had deprecated the old one. = 新系统实施之前，他们已经淘汰了旧系统。
The committee had deprecated the proposal long before the vote. = 在投票很久之前，委员会就已经否决了该提案。


将来时:
They will deprecate the current model next year. = 他们明年将淘汰目前的型号。
The company will deprecate the old software soon. = 公司很快就会淘汰旧软件。


虚拟语气:
If they deprecated the old system earlier, we wouldn't have these problems. = 如果他们早点淘汰旧系统，我们就不会有这些问题了。
I wish they had deprecated the harmful practice years ago. = 我希望他们几年前就淘汰了这种有害的做法。


被动语态:
The old system was deprecated in favor of a newer one. = 旧系统被淘汰，取而代之的是一个新的系统。
The software is being deprecated and will soon be unavailable. = 该软件正在被淘汰，很快将无法使用。


疑问句:
Does the company deprecate the use of external libraries? = 公司是否反对使用外部库？
Will this feature be deprecated in the next update? = 此功能将在下一次更新中被淘汰吗？


否定句:
They do not deprecate the use of new technologies. = 他们并不反对使用新技术。
The company did not deprecate the software until after several security breaches. = 直到发生了几次安全漏洞后，公司才淘汰了该软件。


PHRASE->
deprecate strongly = 强烈反对
deprecate the use of = 反对使用…
deprecate a proposal = 否决一项提案
deprecate an action = 反对一项行动
deprecate the idea of = 反对…的想法


词源:
From Latin *dēprecāre "to pray against, avert," from dē- "away" + precāri "to pray."  The sense evolved from "to pray against" to "to express disapproval of."
