receding:/rɪˈsiːdɪŋ/| adj. |消退的；后退的；衰退的 | Going back or becoming less; withdrawing.  |例句：The floodwaters are finally receding.  洪水终于退去了。
例句：His hairline is receding. 他的发际线后移了。


近义词：retreating, withdrawing, fading, declining |反义词：advancing, progressing, growing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tide is receding. = 潮水正在退去。
The glacier is slowly receding. = 冰川正在缓慢消退。

一般过去时:
The pain receded after taking the medicine. = 服药后疼痛减轻了。
The army receded from the border. = 军队从边境撤军了。

现在完成时:
The floodwaters have receded. = 洪水已经退去了。
His influence has receded in recent years. = 近年来他的影响力下降了。

过去完成时:
The symptoms had receded before the doctor arrived. = 在医生到达之前症状已经减轻了。
The threat of war had receded after the negotiations. = 谈判之后战争的威胁消退了。

将来时:
The floodwaters will recede soon. = 洪水很快就会退去。
Hopefully, the economic downturn will recede next year. = 希望经济低迷明年会好转。

虚拟语气:
If the storm receded, we could go home. = 如果风暴消退，我们就能回家了。
I wish my anxieties would recede. = 我希望我的焦虑能够消退。

被动语态:
The problem was eventually receded. = 这个问题最终得到了解决。(此处被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The threat was receded by the peace agreement. = 和平协议消除了威胁。(此处被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用)


疑问句:
Is the hairline receding? = 发际线后移了吗？
Is the tide receding quickly? = 潮水退得快吗？

否定句:
The symptoms are not receding. = 症状没有减轻。
The economic crisis is not receding. = 经济危机没有好转。
