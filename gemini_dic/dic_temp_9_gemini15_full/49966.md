deniers:/dɪˈnaɪərz/| n. |否认者；怀疑者|A person who denies something, especially the existence of climate change.  Someone who denies a particular fact or truth.  |例句：Climate change deniers often cite anecdotal evidence to support their claims. 气候变化否认者经常引用轶事证据来支持他们的说法。
|例句：The deniers of the Holocaust should be condemned.  否认大屠杀的人应该受到谴责。|近义词：doubters, skeptics, disbelievers |反义词：believers, affirmers |名词
