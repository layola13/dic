nonretailers:/ˌnɑːnˈriːteɪlərz/ | n. |非零售商|Those businesses or individuals who are not involved in the sale of goods or services directly to consumers.  They may be involved in wholesale, manufacturing, or other aspects of the supply chain.|Example:  Many nonretailers, such as wholesalers and manufacturers, play a crucial role in getting products to consumers.  许多非零售商，例如批发商和制造商，在将产品送达消费者方面发挥着至关重要的作用。|近义词：wholesalers, distributors, manufacturers |反义词：retailers
