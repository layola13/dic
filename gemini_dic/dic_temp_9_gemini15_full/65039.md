etcetera:/ɛtˈsɛtərə/ | pron.|等等；诸如此类 |  An abbreviation of the Latin phrase "et cetera," meaning "and the rest" or "and so forth." It's used to indicate that a list of items is not exhaustive. | Example:  The store sold apples, bananas, oranges, etcetera.  这家商店卖苹果、香蕉、橘子等等。 |近义词: and so on, and so forth, and the like |反义词: none
