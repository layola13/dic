non-science:/ˈnɑːnˈsaɪəns/| adj.|非科学的，非科学性的|Not related to or based on science; lacking scientific basis or method.|The argument presented lacked scientific rigor and fell into the realm of non-science.  这个论点缺乏科学严谨性，属于非科学范畴。|近义词：unscientific, nonscientific, anti-scientific|反义词：scientific


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb or noun forms in the same way "science" does.  Therefore, tense and voice conjugations are not applicable in the same manner.  We'll show examples of its use in different sentence structures.

Declarative Sentences:
His explanation was purely non-science. = 他的解释纯粹是非科学的。
The claims made in the article are considered non-science by many experts. =  许多专家认为文章中的说法是非科学的。


Interrogative Sentences:
Is that theory considered non-science? = 那个理论被认为是非科学的吗？
Are these practices based on non-science? = 这些做法是基于非科学的吗？


Negative Sentences:
That's not non-science; it's pseudoscience. = 那不是非科学的；而是伪科学。
The research wasn't non-science; it was poorly conducted science. = 这项研究并非非科学的；而是科学研究做得不好。


Comparative Sentences:
The approach is more non-science than real science. = 这种方法比真正的科学更接近非科学。
Their conclusions are less based on non-science than we initially thought. = 他们的结论比我们最初认为的更少基于非科学。


PHRASE->
There aren't common set phrases built directly around "non-science".  However, it can be used in descriptive phrases like these:

area of non-science = 非科学领域
realm of non-science = 非科学的范畴
claims of non-science = 非科学的主张
evidence of non-science = 非科学的证据
pursuit of non-science = 对非科学的追求
