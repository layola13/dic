den:/den/| n.|兽穴；窝；隐蔽处；（尤指）简陋的住所 | A den is a wild animal's home, often a hole or cave.  It can also refer to a secluded or private room. |例句：The fox made its den under the roots of an old oak tree.  狐狸在古老橡树的树根下筑巢。

The children have a den in the attic where they play. 孩子们在阁楼上有一个游戏室。

|近义词：lair, burrow, hideout |反义词：open, exposed
