outskirmishes: /ˌaʊtˈskɜːrmɪʃɪz/ | n. | 边缘冲突，小规模冲突 | Small battles or skirmishes that take place on the outskirts or fringes of a larger conflict.  These are often preliminary engagements or minor battles, not the main fighting. |例句：The main army avoided direct confrontation, preferring instead to engage in a series of outskirmishes.  主要军队避免直接冲突，而是选择进行一系列的小规模冲突。

例句：The rebels launched several outskirmishes before the major offensive.  叛军在主要攻势发动前发起了几次小规模冲突。


近义词： skirmishes, minor battles, border clashes, preliminary engagements

反义词：major battles, decisive engagements, main fighting
