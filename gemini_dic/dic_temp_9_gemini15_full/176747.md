readapted:/ˌriːˈæptɪd/| v.|重新改编，重新适应|To adapt something again; to adapt something for a new purpose or context.|例句：The play was readapted for a younger audience. 这部戏被重新改编以适合更年轻的观众。  例句：The company readapted its strategy after the market downturn. 公司在市场低迷后重新调整了战略。|近义词：revise, modify, adjust|反义词：原创, original
