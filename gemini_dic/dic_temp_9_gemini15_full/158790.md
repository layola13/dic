plug-up: /plʌɡ ʌp/ | vt. |堵塞；塞满；阻碍 | To fill or block something, usually an opening or passage, completely.  |例句：The drain is plugged up with hair.  下水道被头发堵住了。

例句：The snow plugged up the roads, making travel impossible.  大雪堵塞了道路，使交通中断。


近义词：block, obstruct, clog, jam, fill


反义词：unplug, unclog, clear, open


词性:动词


USAGE->
