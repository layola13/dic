jumbles:/ˈdʒʌmbəlz/ | n. | 混乱；杂乱无章的东西；混杂 | A confused or disordered mass; a mixture.  |The papers were in a jumble on the desk. 桌上的文件乱成一团。|近义词：mess, muddle, confusion |反义词：order, organization


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The toys are all in a jumble. = 玩具都乱成一团了。
This information is a complete jumble. = 这些信息完全是一团糟。

一般过去时:
He jumbled all the papers together. = 他把所有的文件都混在一起了。
The earthquake jumbled the city. = 地震把城市弄得乱七八糟。


现在完成时:
She has jumbled up all the ingredients. = 她把所有的配料都弄混了。
They have jumbled the files again. = 他们又把文件弄乱了。


过去完成时:
He had jumbled the instructions before starting the task. = 他在开始任务之前就把说明弄乱了。
She had jumbled the words in her sentence. = 她把句子里的词语都弄乱了。


将来时:
I will jumble the letters before you try to unscramble them. = 我会在你尝试解开字母谜之前把字母弄乱。
He will jumble the cards before the game begins. = 游戏开始前他会把牌洗乱。


虚拟语气:

I wish I hadn't jumbled the files. = 我真希望我没有把文件弄乱。


被动语态:
The documents were jumbled during the move. = 文件在搬家过程中被弄乱了。
The words were jumbled in the telegram. = 电报中的文字被弄乱了。


疑问句:

否定句:
Don't jumble the wires. = 不要把电线弄乱。
