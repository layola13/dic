neurosciences:/ˌnjʊərəʊˈsaɪənsɪz/| n. |神经科学|The scientific study of the nervous system, including its structure, function, development, genetics, biochemistry, pharmacology, and pathology.  Neuroscience encompasses various subfields such as neuroanatomy, neurophysiology, neurochemistry, neuropharmacology, and behavioral neuroscience.  It seeks to understand how the nervous system works at all levels, from the molecular and cellular to the systems and behavioral. |例句：Advances in neurosciences have led to better treatments for neurological disorders. 神经科学的进步导致了对神经系统疾病的更好治疗。

例句：Neurosciences research is crucial for understanding brain functions and developing effective therapies. 神经科学研究对于理解大脑功能和开发有效的疗法至关重要。
