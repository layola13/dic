re-correct:/ˌriːkəˈrekt/| vt.|重新校正，再次更正|To correct something again, often after a previous correction has been found to be insufficient or incorrect.  It implies a second or further attempt at achieving accuracy.|例句：The professor re-corrected the student's paper after finding several more grammatical errors. 教授在发现更多语法错误后再次更正了学生的论文。

例句：The software automatically re-corrected the misspelled words in my document.  软件自动重新校正了我文档中拼写错误的单词。


近义词：revise, amend, rectify, adjust


反义词：err, misinterpret, make mistakes


词性:动词
