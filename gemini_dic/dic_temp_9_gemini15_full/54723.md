despair:/dɪˈspɛər/| n. |绝望，沮丧|The feeling of having lost all hope; the state of being hopeless.  It often involves a sense of helplessness and misery. |例句：After failing the exam, he fell into despair.  考试不及格后，他陷入了绝望。|例句：Her despair was evident in her tear-filled eyes. 她绝望的神情在她泪汪汪的眼睛里显而易见。|近义词：despondency, hopelessness, dejection|反义词：hope, optimism, cheerfulness|名词
