dermatologies:/ˌdɜːrməˈtɒlədʒiz/| n. |皮肤病学，皮肤科；皮肤病学研究|The study of the skin, its structure, functions, and diseases.  It encompasses diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of skin disorders.|例句：She's specializing in dermatologies at a leading hospital. = 她在一所顶尖医院专攻皮肤病学。|例句：Advances in dermatologies have led to more effective treatments for acne. = 皮肤病学的发展导致了更有效的痤疮治疗方法。|近义词：dermatology (singular)|反义词：无|名词
