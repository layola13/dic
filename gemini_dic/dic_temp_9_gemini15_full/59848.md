ectoprocta:/ˈɛktoʊprɒktə/| n. |外肛动物|Ectoprocta is a phylum of aquatic invertebrate animals, commonly known as bryozoans. They are colonial animals, meaning they live in colonies of many interconnected individuals called zooids.  These colonies can form a variety of shapes and sizes, often encrusting on hard surfaces.  They are filter feeders, using lophophores (a crown of ciliated tentacles) to capture food particles from the water. |Example:  The study focused on the diversity of ectoprocta in the coral reef ecosystem.  这项研究集中在珊瑚礁生态系统中外肛动物的多样性。|近义词:bryozoans |反义词:  (no direct antonym)|名词
