overcapacity:/ˌoʊvər kəˈpæsɪti/| n. |生产过剩；产能过剩；超负荷|Excess capacity beyond what is needed or used.  It often refers to a situation where a company, industry, or economy produces more goods or services than are demanded by the market. This can lead to lower prices, reduced profits, and even business closures.|The factory is suffering from overcapacity, leading to layoffs.  这家工厂产能过剩，导致裁员。|The airline industry experienced a period of overcapacity after the pandemic. 航空业在大流行后经历了一段产能过剩时期。|undercapacity, sufficient capacity|limited capacity
