co-occurring:/ˌkoʊəˈkɜːrɪŋ/| adj. |同时发生的，共生的| Occurring at the same time or in the same place.  This term often implies a relationship or association between the events or phenomena. |例句：Depression and anxiety are often co-occurring disorders. 抑郁症和焦虑症往往是同时发生的疾病。|例句：The study examined co-occurring risk factors for heart disease.  这项研究考察了心脏病的共同危险因素。|近义词：concurrent, simultaneous, concomitant |反义词：separate, independent, distinct
