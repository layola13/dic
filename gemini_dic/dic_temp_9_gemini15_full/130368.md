non-Unitarians:/ˌnɑːnjuːnɪˈtɛəriənz/| n. |非一神论者；非唯一神论者 |People who do not subscribe to Unitarianism, a theological movement emphasizing the oneness of God.  They may hold diverse religious beliefs, including trinitarian or polytheistic views. |Example:  The survey included both Unitarians and non-Unitarians to get a broad range of perspectives on religious tolerance.  调查包括一神论者和非一神论者，以获得关于宗教宽容的广泛视角。
