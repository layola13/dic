sjambokking:/ˈʃæmbɒkɪŋ/| v.|鞭打；抽打|The act of whipping or flogging with a sjambok.  It often implies a harsh or brutal punishment.|例句：The farmer was accused of sjambokking his workers.  农场主被指控鞭打他的工人。|例句：The sound of sjambokking echoed through the night.  鞭打的声响回荡在夜空中。|近义词：flogging, whipping, lashing|反义词：None specifically, but implies the opposite of gentle treatment.
