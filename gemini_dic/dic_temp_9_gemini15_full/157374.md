pipettes:/pɪˈpɛt/| n. |移液管，吸量管 |A pipette is a laboratory tool used to transfer a precise volume of liquid.  It typically consists of a cylindrical tube with a narrow tip and a bulb or piston for drawing up and dispensing the liquid. |例句：The scientist carefully used a pipette to measure the exact amount of reagent needed for the experiment.  科学家小心地使用移液管测量实验所需的精确试剂量。

The technician meticulously filled the pipette with the sample before transferring it to the test tube.  技术员在将样品转移到试管之前，一丝不苟地用移液管填充样品。|近义词：dropper, burette |反义词：None
