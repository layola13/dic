murmur:/ˈmɜːrmər/| n., v.|低语；喃喃自语；低沉的声音 | a low, indistinct sound; to speak or utter in a low, indistinct tone; a low continuous sound |例句：The crowd murmured their approval.  人群低声表示赞同。  She murmured something about being tired. 她低声说了些什么关于累了的话。|近义词：mutter, whisper, rumble|反义词：shout, roar, bellow|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He murmurs a prayer every night. = 他每天晚上都低声祈祷。
They murmur their dissatisfaction to each other. = 他们低声抱怨彼此的不满。

一般过去时:
She murmured a sweet nothing in his ear. = 她在他耳边低声说了些甜言蜜语。
The audience murmured their approval of the performance. = 观众低声表达了对演出的赞赏。

现在完成时:
He has murmured his complaints to the manager several times. = 他已经多次向经理抱怨了。
The wind has murmured through the trees all night. =  风整夜都在树林间低语。

过去完成时:
She had murmured her agreement before he finished speaking. = 他还没说完话，她就已低声表示同意。
The crowd had murmured their discontent for hours before the riot began. = 在暴动开始前几个小时，人群就一直在低声表达不满。

将来时:
She will murmur sweet nothings to her baby. = 她将对她宝宝低声细语。
The wind will murmur through the leaves. = 风将穿过树叶低语。

虚拟语气:
If I could murmur my feelings, I would. = 如果我能低声表达我的感受，我会的。
I wish I could murmur a secret to the wind. = 我希望我能向风低语一个秘密。

被动语态:
His complaints were murmured throughout the office. = 他的抱怨在整个办公室里都被低声传播。
The song was murmured softly by the singer. = 歌曲被歌手轻声低吟。

疑问句:
What are you murmuring about? = 你在低声嘀咕什么？
Did you hear what she was murmuring? = 你听到她在低声说什么了吗？

否定句:
He didn't murmur a word. = 他一个字也没说。
She didn't murmur her objections. = 她没有低声表达她的反对意见。


PHRASE->
a murmur of voices = 低低的说话声
a murmur of approval = 一阵低声赞同
a murmur of discontent = 一阵低声不满
to murmur agreement = 低声表示同意
to murmur thanks = 低声道谢
