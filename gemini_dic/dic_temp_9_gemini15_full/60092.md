eelpot:/ˈiːlpɒt/| n. |鳗鱼笼，捕鳗鱼的陷阱 |A eelpot is a trap used for catching eels. It's typically made of wicker or other materials that allow water to flow through but prevent eels from escaping.  |Example: The fisherman checked his eelpots at dawn.  渔夫黎明时检查了他的鳗鱼笼。|近义词：eel trap, fish trap |反义词：N/A
