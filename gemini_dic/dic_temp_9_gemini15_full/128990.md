nipping:/ˈnɪpɪŋ/| v. |轻咬；捏；剪短；袭击；冻结|To bite gently; to pinch; to cut or trim; to attack suddenly; to affect with a sharp cold.|例句：The puppy was nipping at my heels. 小狗轻咬着我的脚跟。
The gardener was nipping off the dead flowers. 园丁正在剪掉枯萎的花朵。

近义词：biting, pinching, snipping, nipping at|反义词：None specifically opposite, but could consider words implying gentleness versus harshness.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nips the flower heads off regularly. 他定期剪掉花头。
The dog is constantly nipping at the mailman's ankles. 狗一直在咬邮递员的脚踝。


一般过去时:
She nipped the loose thread. 她剪断了松散的线。
The frost nipped the young plants. 霜冻损坏了幼苗。


现在完成时:
The cold wind has nipped at my nose. 寒风冻得我的鼻子有点疼。
He has nipped the problem in the bud. 他已扼杀了问题于萌芽状态。


过去完成时:
The gardener had nipped the weeds before the rain started. 园丁在雨下之前就除掉了杂草。
The frost had nipped the blossoms before they could fully open. 霜冻在花朵完全开放之前就损害了它们。


将来时:
She will nip the stems of the roses. 她将修剪玫瑰的茎。
The gardener will nip the problem in the bud. 园丁会扼杀问题于萌芽状态。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would nip it in the bud. 如果我是你，我会扼杀它于萌芽状态。
I wish I had nipped that problem in the bud. 我真希望我当时扼杀了那个问题于萌芽状态。


被动语态:
The flowers were nipped by the frost. 花朵被霜冻损坏了。
The loose ends were neatly nipped off. 松散的线头被整齐地剪掉了。


疑问句:
Are you nipping the herbs? 你在修剪香草吗？
Is the cold weather nipping at your ears?  寒冷的天气是不是让你耳朵有点疼？


否定句:
The gardener didn't nip the weeds. 园丁没有除草。
She didn't nip the problem in the bud. 她没有扼杀问题于萌芽状态。


PHRASE->
nip in the bud: 扼杀于萌芽状态
nip and tuck:  细微的调整
nip it:  解决它
a nip of something: 一小口（酒等）
nip something in the bud: 及时制止


词源:
源自古英语 *cnīpan，意为“捏，夹”。  Related to the word "knife".
