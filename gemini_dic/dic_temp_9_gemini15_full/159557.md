politicize:/ˈpɒlətaɪz/ /ˈpɑːlətaɪz/| vt.|使政治化；赋予政治意义|To give a political character to something; to make something a matter of political concern or action.  |例句：The media tried to politicize the environmental issue.  媒体试图将环境问题政治化。
例句：Don't politicize this simple problem; it's just a matter of logistics. 不要把这个问题政治化；这只是一个后勤问题。|近义词：politicize, make political, ideologize|反义词：depoliticize, depersonalize|动词
