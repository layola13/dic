例句：The email contained a noncongratulatory message about the project's delay. 这封邮件包含了一条关于项目延误的非祝贺性消息。


近义词：disapproving, critical, disapproving, condemnatory


反义词：congratulatory, celebratory, commendatory

词性：形容词
