gluttonous:/ˈɡlʌtənəs/| adj.|贪吃的；贪婪的| excessively greedy and eager to eat and drink a lot; characterized by excessive indulgence.|例句：The gluttonous child ate all the cookies before dinner.  这个贪吃的孩子在晚饭前吃掉了所有的饼干。
例句：His gluttonous appetite led to health problems.  他贪吃的胃口导致了健康问题。|近义词：voracious, ravenous, insatiable|反义词：abstemious, temperate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is gluttonous. = 他很贪吃。
She is always gluttonous at buffets. = 她在自助餐上总是吃很多。

一般过去时:
He was gluttonous at the party. = 他在派对上吃得很撑。
They were gluttonous with the desserts. = 他们狼吞虎咽地吃着甜点。

现在完成时:
He has been gluttonous all his life. = 他一辈子都很贪吃。
She has been gluttonous lately, gaining weight. = 她最近很贪吃，体重增加了。

过去完成时:
He had been gluttonous all day, so he wasn't hungry for dinner. = 他一整天都吃得很撑，所以晚饭不饿了。
They had been gluttonous at the wedding feast, leaving little for others. = 他们在婚礼宴会上吃得很撑，留给别人的很少。

将来时:
He will be gluttonous at the Thanksgiving feast. = 他将在感恩节盛宴上吃很多。
She will likely be gluttonous if given unlimited access to sweets. = 如果让她无限量地吃甜食，她很可能会吃很多。

虚拟语气:
If he weren't so gluttonous, he would be healthier. = 如果他不那么贪吃，他会更健康。
I wish I weren't so gluttonous. = 我希望我不那么贪吃。

被动语态:
His gluttonous behavior was noted by everyone. = 他的贪吃行为被每个人都注意到了。
(Note:  "Gluttonous" itself is rarely used passively.)


疑问句:
Is he gluttonous? = 他贪吃吗？
Are they always so gluttonous? = 他们总是这么贪吃吗？

否定句:
He is not gluttonous; he eats moderately. = 他不贪吃；他吃得很适量。
She is not gluttonous, she just has a large appetite. = 她不贪吃，只是胃口比较大。
