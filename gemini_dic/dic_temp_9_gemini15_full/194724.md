self-written:/ˌsɛlfˈrɪtn/| adj.| 自写的，亲笔写的 |Something that is self-written is written by the person who is talking about it or using it.  It emphasizes that the work is original and not copied or plagiarized. |例句：This is a self-written essay, not a plagiarized one. 这是一篇自写的文章，不是剽窃的。|例句：She submitted a self-written poem for the competition. 她提交了一首自写的诗歌参加比赛。|近义词：original, hand-written, personally written |反义词：plagiarized, copied, borrowed
