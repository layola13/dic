chickabiddy:/ˈtʃɪkəˌbɪdi/| n. |小鸡；小姑娘；小家伙|A term of endearment for a small child, especially a girl, or a small bird, typically a chick.  It's a playful and affectionate diminutive. |例句：Look at the little chickabiddy!  看看这只小鸡！  She called her granddaughter her "little chickabiddy". 她叫她孙女“小鸡仔”。|近义词：chick, little one, sweetie, darling|反义词：adult, grown-up
