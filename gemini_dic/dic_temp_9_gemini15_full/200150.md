skills:/skɪlz/| n. |技能，技巧，能力 |The ability to do something well; expertise.  Skills are developed through practice and learning. |例句：He possesses excellent communication skills. 他拥有极好的沟通技巧。

例句：She needs to improve her problem-solving skills. 她需要提高她的解决问题的能力。


近义词：abilities, aptitudes, talents, expertise, proficiency |反义词：inabilities, deficiencies, incompetence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has excellent problem-solving skills. = 他有极好的解决问题的能力。
My skills are improving every day. = 我的技能每天都在提高。

一般过去时:
Her presentation skills were impressive. = 她的演讲技巧令人印象深刻。
I lacked the necessary skills to complete the task. = 我缺乏完成这项任务所需的技能。


现在完成时:
I have developed many new skills this year. = 今年我培养了许多新技能。
She has honed her writing skills over many years. = 她多年来磨练了自己的写作技巧。


过去完成时:
He had already acquired the skills needed for the job before the interview. = 面试前他已经掌握了这份工作所需的技能。
By the time she finished the course, she had mastered several new skills. = 到她完成课程的时候，她已经掌握了几项新技能。


将来时:
I will learn new skills in the upcoming training. = 我将在接下来的培训中学习新技能。
She will need advanced technical skills for this position. = 她将需要这项职位的高级技术技能。


虚拟语气:
If I had better communication skills, I would be more successful. = 如果我有更好的沟通技巧，我会更成功。
I wish I had the skills to build a house. = 我希望我有建造房子的技能。


被动语态:
These skills are highly valued by employers. = 这些技能受到雇主的青睐。
Many skills are learned through experience. = 许多技能都是通过经验学习的。


疑问句:
What skills do you possess? = 你拥有哪些技能？
What skills are required for this job? = 这份工作需要什么技能？


否定句:
He doesn't have the necessary skills for this project. = 他没有完成这个项目所需的技能。
She lacked the technical skills to operate the machine. = 她缺乏操作机器的技术技能。
