outliner:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪnər/| n. |大纲制定者；轮廓描绘者；概要编写者 |A person who creates outlines.  Someone who makes summaries or plans. |He's a skilled outliner, able to create clear and concise summaries of complex topics.|近义词：summarizer, planner, sketcher |反义词：detailer


USAGE->
