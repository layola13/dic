free-up:/friː ˈʌp/| vt.|腾出（时间、空间、资源等）|To make (something) available for use; to release or liberate (something or someone) from a constraint or obligation.|例句：We need to free up some time to discuss this project. 我们需要腾出一些时间来讨论这个项目。
例句：The new software freed up valuable resources.  新的软件释放了宝贵的资源。|近义词：release, liberate, vacate, clear|反义词：restrict, tie up, occupy, constrain|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They free up resources every week. = 他们每周都腾出资源。
The new system frees up valuable time for employees. = 新系统为员工腾出了宝贵的时间。


一般过去时:
The sale freed up some much-needed cash. = 这次销售腾出了急需的现金。
We freed up a whole afternoon for the meeting. = 我们腾出了整整一个下午开会。


现在完成时:
They have freed up significant budget for this year's project. = 他们已经为今年的项目腾出了可观的预算。
The company has freed up several employees to work on the new project. = 公司已经腾出了几名员工来从事新项目。


过去完成时:
They had freed up the conference room before the meeting began. = 会议开始前他们已经腾出了会议室。
She had freed up her schedule to attend the conference. = 她已经腾出了时间参加会议。


将来时:
We will free up more space in the warehouse next month. = 我们下个月将腾出仓库更多的空间。
The new policy will free up more time for teachers to focus on students. = 新政策将为教师腾出更多时间专注于学生。


虚拟语气:
If we could free up more resources, we could finish the project earlier. = 如果我们可以腾出更多资源，我们可以更早完成项目。
I wish I could free up some time to help you. = 我真希望我能腾出一些时间来帮助你。


被动语态:
More resources will be freed up for research next year. = 明年将有更多资源用于研究。
The schedule was freed up after the unexpected cancellation. = 意外取消后，日程安排空了出来。


疑问句:
Can we free up some time for a quick chat? = 我们能腾出一些时间快速聊聊吗？
How can we free up more storage space on our computers? = 我们如何才能释放电脑上更多的存储空间？


否定句:
We couldn't free up enough staff to meet the deadline. = 我们无法腾出足够的员工来赶上最后期限。
They haven't freed up the necessary funds for the project yet. = 他们还没有为该项目腾出必要的资金。
