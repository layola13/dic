morphemic:/mɔːrˈfiːmɪk/ | adj. | 词素的，形态学的 | Relating to morphemes; of or relating to morphemes.  Morphemic analysis is the study of the smallest units of meaning in a language. | Example:  The morphemic structure of the word "unbreakable" reveals three morphemes: "un-", "break", and "-able".  词语“unbreakable”的词素结构揭示了三个词素：“un-”、“break”和“-able”。 | 近义词：morphological | 反义词：None
