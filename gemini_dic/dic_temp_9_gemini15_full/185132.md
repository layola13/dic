retinol:/ˈrɛtɪnɒl/ | n. |视黄醇|A vitamin A derivative used in skincare and cosmetics to reduce wrinkles and acne. It's a potent antioxidant that boosts collagen production and cell turnover.|例句：Many skincare products contain retinol to combat the signs of aging. 许多护肤品都含有视黄醇来对抗衰老迹象。|近义词：retinoid|反义词：None specifically, but products without retinol could be considered an indirect antonym.
