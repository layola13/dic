miswired: /ˌmɪsˈwaɪərd/ | adj. | 接线错误的；电路接错的 |  Incorrectly wired; having wires connected wrongly.  | Example: The device malfunctioned because it was miswired.  这个设备因为接线错误而出现故障。 |近义词: incorrectly wired, wrongly connected | 反义词: correctly wired, properly connected


USAGE->

The lights are miswired, causing a short circuit. = 灯光接线错误，导致短路。
