chiseled:/ˈtʃɪzld/| adj.|雕刻的；轮廓清晰的；棱角分明的|Having sharp, clear outlines; appearing to have been carved with a chisel.  The word suggests precision and often implies a certain degree of hardness or strength.|例句：His jawline was sharply chiseled. 他的下巴轮廓非常清晰。

His face was chiseled and strong. 他的脸庞棱角分明，坚毅有力。|近义词：sculpted, carved, sharp, defined|反义词：blurred, undefined, soft, rounded|词性:形容词
