nonconcessive: /ˌnɒnkənˈsesɪv/ | adj. | 非让步的；不表示让步的 |  Not making a concession; not yielding or conceding a point.  It describes a clause or statement that doesn't acknowledge or accept an opposing argument or fact.  |


例句：
His success, despite his lack of experience, was a nonconcessive triumph. = 尽管缺乏经验，他的成功仍然是一个非让步的胜利。

The judge's decision, nonconcessive of the defendant's pleas, was final. = 法官的决定，不理会被告的请求，是最终的。


近义词：unyielding, uncompromising, absolute, irrespective


反义词：concessive, yielding, compromising


词性：形容词
