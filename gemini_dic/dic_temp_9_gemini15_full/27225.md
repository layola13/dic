calluses: /ˈkæləsɪz/ | n. |老茧，胼胝 | thickened and hardened part of the skin, especially on the hands or feet, caused by persistent pressure or friction.  |例句：Years of playing the guitar have given him calluses on his fingertips.  多年弹吉他使他的指尖长满了老茧。

例句：The farmer's hands were rough and covered with calluses.  这位农民的手粗糙，布满了老茧。


近义词：hard skin, corn


反义词：soft skin


词性：名词

USAGE->

The constant friction creates calluses on his hands. =  持续的摩擦在他手上造成了老茧。
He has calluses on his feet from walking long distances. = 他因为长途跋涉而在脚上长了老茧。


一般过去时:
