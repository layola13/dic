pursued:/pərˈsuːd/| v.|追求，追捕，继续从事|To follow or chase someone or something.  To continue doing something even though it is difficult.|例句：The police pursued the robbers. 警察追捕强盗。
He pursued his studies despite financial difficulties. 尽管经济困难，他仍然继续学习。|近义词：chase, follow, seek, continue|反义词：abandon, forsake, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pursues his dreams relentlessly. = 他坚持不懈地追求梦想。
She pursues a career in medicine. = 她追求医学事业。

一般过去时:
They pursued the fleeing suspect. = 他们追捕逃跑的嫌疑犯。
The company pursued a new marketing strategy. = 公司采用了新的营销策略。

现在完成时:
We have pursued this matter for months. = 我们已经关注这件事好几个月了。
She has pursued her passion for art throughout her life. = 她一生都在追求她对艺术的热情。

过去完成时:
They had pursued the thief for hours before catching him. = 他们追捕小偷几个小时才抓住他。
The company had pursued expansion in the Asian market for years. = 公司多年来一直在寻求在亚洲市场扩张。

将来时:
I will pursue my education further. = 我将继续深造。
The government will pursue economic reforms. = 政府将继续推行经济改革。


虚拟语气:
If I pursued my passion, I would be happier. = 如果我追求我的热情，我会更快乐。
I wish I had pursued a different career path. = 我希望我选择了不同的职业道路。


被动语态:
The thief was pursued by the police. = 小偷被警察追捕。
The matter is being pursued by the authorities. = 这件事正在由当局调查。


疑问句:
Why are you pursuing this goal? = 你为什么要追求这个目标？
What are they pursuing at the moment? = 他们现在在追求什么？


否定句:
He did not pursue his legal options. = 他没有寻求法律途径。
The company did not pursue that project. = 公司没有进行那个项目。
