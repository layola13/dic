play the game:/pleɪ ðə ɡeɪm/| v. phr.|参与游戏；遵守规则；按照既定方式行事|To participate in a game, competition, or system according to its rules or conventions. It can also imply conforming to social or professional norms.|例句：He refused to play the game and told the truth. 他拒绝逢迎，说了实话。|例句：To get promoted, you have to play the game. 要升职，你必须学会逢迎。|近义词：participate, conform, comply|反义词：rebel, defy, resist|动词词组
