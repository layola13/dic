hydroxylated:/haɪˈdrɒksɪleɪtɪd/| adj.|羟基化的；被羟基化的| Treated chemically to add hydroxyl groups.  The process introduces a hydroxyl group (-OH) to a molecule, often altering its properties. | Example: Hydroxylated fatty acids are important components of cell membranes.  羟基化脂肪酸是细胞膜的重要组成部分。|近义词：hydroxylated, hydroxylized|反义词：dehydroxylated
