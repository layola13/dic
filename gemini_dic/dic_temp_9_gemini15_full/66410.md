executively:/ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪvli/| adv. |以行政方式地；高效率地；管理人员地|In a manner characteristic of or appropriate to an executive; efficiently and effectively.|The project was managed executively, meeting all deadlines and exceeding expectations.  这个项目以高效的方式管理，满足所有截止日期并超出预期。|efficiently, effectively, decisively|inefficiently, ineffectively, haphazardly|副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense, voice, and mood are not applicable.  It modifies verbs, describing *how* an action is performed.


Examples illustrating different aspects of usage:

* **Emphasis on efficiency:** He completed the task executively, saving the company valuable time. 他高效地完成了任务，为公司节省了宝贵的时间。
* **Emphasis on managerial style:** The CEO handled the crisis executively, restoring confidence in the market.  首席执行官以行政人员的方式处理了危机，恢复了市场信心。
* **Emphasis on decisiveness:** She acted executively, making the tough decision needed to resolve the conflict. 她果断地采取行动，做出了解决冲突所需的艰难决定。

PHRASE->
There are no common set phrases specifically using "executively."  Its usage is more about modifying a verb's action than creating idiomatic expressions.  However,  phrases that highlight similar concepts are:

* **In an executive manner:**  Similar in meaning to "executively."
* **With executive efficiency:** Highlights the efficiency aspect.
* **With decisive action:** Focuses on the decisiveness often implied by the word.
* **In a highly effective way:** A more general synonym.
* **With streamlined procedures:** Implies efficiency and organization.
