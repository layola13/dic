outrush: /ˈaʊtrʌʃ/ | v. | 涌出；奔涌而出 | To rush or flow out forcefully.  |例句：The crowd outrushed the stadium after the game.  比赛结束后，人群涌出体育场。

The water outrushed from the broken pipe. 水从破裂的管道中喷涌而出。|近义词：gush, surge, rush, pour |反义词：inflow, influx


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water outrushes from the pipe. = 水从管道中涌出。
The crowd outrushes the stadium after every game. = 每场比赛后，人群都涌出体育场。


一般过去时:
The crowd outrushed the stadium. = 人群涌出了体育场。
Water outrushed from the burst dam. = 水从决堤的大坝中涌出。


现在完成时:
The river has outrushed its banks several times this year. = 今年这条河已经几次决堤了。
The refugees have outrushed the war-torn country. = 难民们已经逃离了饱受战争蹂躏的国家。


过去完成时:
The water had outrushed its container before we could react. = 我们还没来得及反应，水就已经从容器里溢出来了。
The people had outrushed the building before the fire reached them. = 火势蔓延到他们之前，人们就已经逃出了大楼。


将来时:
The crowd will outrush the stadium when the concert ends. = 音乐会结束后，人群将会涌出体育场。
If the dam breaks, the water will outrush into the valley. = 如果大坝决堤，水将会涌入山谷。


虚拟语气:
If the dam had not been reinforced, the water would have outrushed and caused devastation. = 如果大坝没有加固，水就会涌出来造成破坏。
If the gate were opened suddenly, the water would outrush. = 如果突然打开闸门，水就会涌出来。


被动语态:
The stadium was outrushed by the excited fans. =  兴奋的球迷们涌出了体育场。（被动语态略显不自然，很少使用）
The river bank was outrushed by the flood water. = 河岸被洪水冲垮了。（更贴切的表达方式）


疑问句:
Will the people outrush the burning building? = 人们会从燃烧的大楼里逃出来吗？
Did the water outrush from the damaged pipe? = 水是从损坏的管道里涌出来的吗？


否定句:
The water did not outrush from the container. = 水没有从容器里涌出来。
The crowd didn't outrush the stadium peacefully. = 人群没有平静地涌出体育场。


PHRASE->
an outrush of emotion = 一股情感的涌动
an outrush of refugees = 难民的涌出
a sudden outrush of air = 空气的突然喷出
an outrush of blood = 血液的涌出
the outrush of lava = 岩浆的喷涌


词源:
The word "outrush" is a combination of the prefix "out-" (meaning "outward" or "away from") and the verb "rush" (meaning "to move rapidly").  It's a relatively straightforward derivation indicating a forceful outward movement.
