/əˈprɛʃər/ | n. |压迫；压制；沉重负担 | The oppression of the minority group was evident in their limited access to education and employment. 少数群体的压迫在其受教育和就业机会有限方面显而易见。|近义词：suppression, tyranny, persecution |反义词：liberation, freedom


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government oppresses its people. = 政府压迫人民。
He feels the oppression of poverty. = 他感到贫穷的压迫。


一般过去时:
The dictator oppressed the people for many years. = 独裁者多年来压迫人民。
They oppressed the rebels brutally. = 他们残酷地压制了叛乱分子。


现在完成时:
The ruling class has oppressed the poor for centuries. = 统治阶级几个世纪以来一直压迫穷人。
The regime has oppressed its citizens through censorship. = 该政权通过审查制度压制其公民。


过去完成时:
The people had been oppressed for so long that they revolted. = 人民长期受压迫，最终反抗了。
The empire had oppressed its colonies for generations. = 这个帝国几代人都压迫着它的殖民地。


将来时:
The new leader will not oppress the people. = 新领导人不会压迫人民。
They will continue to oppress those who disagree. = 他们将继续压制那些持不同意见的人。


虚拟语气:
If the king didn't oppress his subjects, the kingdom would prosper. = 如果国王不压迫他的臣民，王国就会繁荣。
I wish the government would stop oppressing the minority groups. = 我希望政府能够停止压迫少数民族。


被动语态:
The people were oppressed by the regime. = 人民受到政权的压迫。
The rights of the citizens were oppressed by the law. = 公民的权利受到法律的压迫。


疑问句:
Were the people oppressed under his rule? = 在他的统治下，人民是否受到了压迫？
Is this a form of oppression? = 这是压迫的一种形式吗？


否定句:
The government does not oppress its citizens. = 政府没有压迫其公民。
They did not intend to oppress anyone. = 他们无意压迫任何人。
