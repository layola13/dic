rainey:/ˈreɪni/| adj.|多雨的，阴雨的|Rainy describes weather that is characterized by rain.  It implies a significant amount of rainfall, often over a period of time.|例句：It's a rainy day today. 今天是雨天。

例句：We had a rainy weekend. 我们度过了个雨周末。


近义词：stormy, showery, wet|反义词：sunny, dry|词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It is rainy outside. = 外面在下雨。
The weather is rainy today. = 今天天气多雨。


一般过去时:
It was rainy yesterday. = 昨天是雨天。
The weather was rainy all week. = 整个星期天气都很阴雨。


现在完成时:
It has been rainy for three days. =  已经连续三天雨。
We have had a lot of rainy days recently. = 最近雨天很多。


过去完成时:
It had been rainy all morning. =  整个上午一直都在下雨。
They had already experienced several rainy days before the trip. = 他们在旅行之前已经经历了好几天雨天了。


将来时:
It will be rainy tomorrow. = 明天会下雨。
The forecast predicts it will be rainy next week. = 预报说下周会下雨。


虚拟语气:
If it were rainy tomorrow, we would stay home. = 如果明天下雨，我们就待在家里。
I wish it weren't rainy today. = 我希望今天不下雨。


被动语态:
(The term "rainy" is rarely used in passive voice.)


疑问句:
Is it rainy outside? = 外面在下雨吗？
Will it be rainy this weekend? = 这个周末会下雨吗？


否定句:
It is not rainy today. = 今天不下雨。
It wasn't rainy yesterday. = 昨天不下雨。
