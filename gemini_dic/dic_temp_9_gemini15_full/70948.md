firefights:/ˈfaɪərfaɪts/| n.|交火，枪战|A firefight is a short, intense exchange of gunfire between two or more armed groups.  It often occurs in a relatively confined space and involves rapid, close-quarters combat.|例句：The soldiers engaged in a fierce firefight with the insurgents. 士兵们与叛乱分子发生了激烈的交火。

The police responded to reports of a firefight near the city center.  警方回应了关于市中心附近发生枪战的报告。|近义词：gun battle, shootout, exchange of fire|反义词：peaceful negotiation, truce
