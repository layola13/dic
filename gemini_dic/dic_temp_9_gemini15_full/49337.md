delimits:/dɪˈlɪmɪts/| vt.|限定；划定界限；区分|To set the limits or boundaries of something.|The red line delimits the area where construction is allowed.  红线划定了允许施工的区域。|The mountains delimit the valley.  群山环绕着山谷。|近义词：define, bound, confine, circumscribe|反义词：extend, broaden


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fence delimits the garden. = 栅栏限定了花园的范围。
The rules delimit what is acceptable behavior. = 规则限定了什么是可接受的行为。


一般过去时:
The treaty delimited the borders between the two countries. = 条约划定了两国之间的边界。

过去完成时:
