koftas:/ˈkɒftɑːz/| n.| 烤肉丸，肉丸子 |Meatballs, typically made from ground meat (lamb, beef, chicken, or a mixture), herbs, and spices, often served in a sauce or on skewers.|  Example:  The restaurant is famous for its delicious lamb koftas. 这家餐馆以其美味的羊肉丸而闻名。|近义词：meatballs, kebabs|反义词：无
