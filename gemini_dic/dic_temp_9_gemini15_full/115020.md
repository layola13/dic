mandible:/ˈmændɪbəl/| n. |下颌骨，下颚|The mandible is the lower jawbone in vertebrates. It's the largest and strongest bone in the face, playing a crucial role in chewing and speech.  |例句：The impact fractured his mandible.  撞击导致他的下颌骨骨折。

The surgeon carefully repaired the fractured mandible. 外科医生仔细地修复了骨折的下颌骨。|近义词：lower jaw|反义词：maxilla (上颌骨)|名词
