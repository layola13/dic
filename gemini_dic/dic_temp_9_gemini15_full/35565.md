coalitionist:/ˌkoʊəˈlɪʃənɪst/ | n. | 联盟成员；结盟者 | A person who supports or is a member of a coalition, especially a political coalition.  Someone who advocates for or participates in a coalition. | Example:  The coalitionist spoke passionately about the need for unity.  这位联盟成员热情洋溢地谈论了团结的必要性。|近义词：ally, member, supporter | 反义词：opponent, adversary
