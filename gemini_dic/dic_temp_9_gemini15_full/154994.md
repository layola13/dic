petals:/ˈpetəlz/| n. |花瓣|The colorful, modified leaves that surround the reproductive parts of a flower.  Petals often attract pollinators.|例句：The rose petals were a vibrant shade of red. 玫瑰花瓣是鲜艳的红色。

例句：The delicate petals of the cherry blossom floated on the breeze.  樱花的娇嫩花瓣随风飘舞。


近义词：blossom, bloom


反义词：None (no direct antonym)
