ramrodded:/ˈræmˌrɑːdɪd/| v.|强行推行，强迫通过，猛烈推进 | forcefully pushed or driven through;  to push or force (something) through with great determination or aggression. |例句：He ramrodded the bill through Congress despite strong opposition. 他不顾强烈反对，强行将法案通过国会。  The manager ramrodded the new policy on the reluctant staff. 经理强迫犹豫不决的员工接受新政策。|近义词：forced, pushed, shoved, imposed |反义词：permitted, allowed, facilitated


USAGE->

He ramrods his ideas through the team. = 他强行向团队灌输他的想法。
They ramrod new policies onto their employees. = 他们强迫员工接受新政策。
