dropper:/ˈdrɒpər/ /ˈdrɑːpər/| n. |滴管；点滴器；（价格）下跌者|A dropper is a small tube with a bulb at one end, used for dropping liquids.  It can also refer to someone or something that causes a price to drop. |例句：The nurse used a dropper to administer the medicine. 护士用滴管给病人服用药。

The price of oil is falling; the oil companies are the droppers. 油价下跌；石油公司是价格下跌的因素。|近义词：pipette, dispenser |反义词：raiser (in the context of prices)
