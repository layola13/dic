grooms:/ɡruːmz/| n.|新郎；马夫；(使)整洁；照料|A groom is a man who is getting married.  The groom is responsible for taking care of the horses.  Grooming is the act of making oneself neat and tidy. |例句：The groom looked handsome in his tuxedo. 新郎穿着燕尾服显得英俊潇洒。 The groom carefully prepared the horses for the race. 马夫仔细地为赛马做准备。近义词：bridegroom, husband (in context of marriage),  stableman (in context of horses) |反义词：bride (female counterpart)
