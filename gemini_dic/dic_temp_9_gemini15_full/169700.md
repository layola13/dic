propounds:/prəˈpaʊndz/| vt.|提出，阐述，宣称|To propound a theory or idea is to suggest it for consideration or discussion.  It implies a formal or systematic presentation, often involving a degree of authority or conviction.|例句：The professor propounded a new theory of gravity. 教授提出了一种新的引力理论。|例句：He propounded his views on education with great passion. 他充满激情地阐述了他对教育的观点。|近义词：put forward, advance, propose, suggest|反义词：suppress, conceal, withhold|动词
