exogenous:/ˈɛksɒdʒənəs/ | adj. | 外生的；外源性的；外来的 | Originating outside the organism or system.  Something exogenous comes from an external source.  | Example:  The exogenous shock to the economy caused by the pandemic led to widespread job losses. 外源性经济冲击（由疫情引起）导致了广泛的失业。  The researcher injected an exogenous variable into the experiment to test its effect. 研究人员向实验中引入了外生变量以测试其影响。 | 近义词: external, extrinsic, foreign | 反义词: endogenous, intrinsic, internal |  词性：形容词
