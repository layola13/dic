flaunty: /ˈflɔːnti/| adj.|炫耀的；招摇的；华而不实的 | ostentatious; showy; characterized by excessive display; gaudy. |例句：Her flaunty display of wealth was quite offensive.  她炫耀财富的行为相当令人反感。

例句：He wore a flaunty, expensive suit to the party. 他穿着件炫耀且昂贵的西装去参加派对。


近义词：showy, ostentatious, gaudy, flamboyant, pretentious

反义词：modest, humble, unassuming, understated


词源：源自动词flaunt，其含义是“炫耀”，最终来源于古法语 *flaunter，意思是“挥舞，炫耀”。
