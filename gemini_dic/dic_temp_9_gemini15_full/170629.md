pseudo-Irish:/ˈsuːdoʊˌaɪrɪʃ/|/ˈsjuːdəʊˌaɪrɪʃ/| adj.| 假爱尔兰的；冒充爱尔兰人的 |  Appearing or claiming to be Irish, but not genuinely so; often used to describe something that imitates or superficially resembles Irish culture but lacks authenticity.  |例句：The band's music was criticized for being pseudo-Irish, lacking the genuine feeling of traditional Irish folk music.乐队的音乐因其伪爱尔兰风格而受到批评，缺乏真正的爱尔兰民间音乐的情感。


例句：The restaurant's attempt at Irish stew was rather pseudo-Irish, using unusual ingredients and lacking the traditional flavour. 这家餐厅尝试制作的爱尔兰炖肉相当伪爱尔兰，使用了不寻常的食材，缺乏传统的风味。

近义词：faux-Irish, sham-Irish, imitation Irish

反义词：authentically Irish, genuinely Irish


词性:形容词
