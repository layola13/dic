colonial:/kəˈloʊniəl/| adj.|殖民的；殖民地的；殖民主义的 | Relating to or characteristic of a colony or colonies.  The term often refers to the historical period of European colonization of other parts of the world. |例句：The colonial government imposed harsh taxes on the native population. 殖民政府对当地居民征收苛捐杂税。

例句：The building exhibits distinct colonial architecture. 这座建筑展现出明显的殖民时期建筑风格。


近义词：imperial, dominion,  settler


反义词：indigenous, native, independent
