cutback:/ˈkʌtbæk/| n. v.|削减；缩减；减少|A reduction in the amount of money spent or produced.|The company announced a cutback in production due to falling sales. 公司由于销量下降而宣布减产。|近义词：reduction, decrease, curtailment|反义词：increase, expansion


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government makes cutbacks in spending every year. = 政府每年都在削减开支。
Our company implements cutbacks to improve efficiency. = 我们公司实施削减以提高效率。


一般过去时:
They made significant cutbacks in their marketing budget last quarter. = 上个季度他们大幅削减了营销预算。
The factory underwent cutbacks resulting in layoffs. = 工厂进行了裁员导致的减产。


现在完成时:
The company has implemented several cutbacks this year. = 公司今年已经实施了几项削减措施。
We have made cutbacks to our staff due to economic downturn. = 由于经济低迷，我们已经裁减了员工。


过去完成时:
They had already made cutbacks before the crisis hit. = 在危机爆发之前，他们就已经进行了削减。
The company had implemented cutbacks in anticipation of lower profits. = 公司预料到利润下降，已经实施了削减措施。


将来时:
The government will make further cutbacks in welfare spending. = 政府将进一步削减福利支出。
They will announce cutbacks in their workforce next week. = 他们下周将宣布裁员。


虚拟语气:
If they made cutbacks earlier, the situation would be better now. = 如果他们早些时候进行削减，现在的情况会更好。
I wish they hadn't made such drastic cutbacks. = 我真希望他们没有进行如此大幅度的削减。


被动语态:
Cutbacks were made to the research and development department. = 对研发部门进行了削减。
Significant cutbacks are being made to the budget. = 预算正在大幅削减。


疑问句:
Are there going to be further cutbacks? = 还会有进一步的削减吗？
What kind of cutbacks are they planning? = 他们计划进行什么样的削减？


否定句:
They didn't make any cutbacks this year. = 他们今年没有进行任何削减。
The company will not make any further cutbacks. = 公司不会进行进一步的削减。


PHRASE->
budget cutbacks 预算削减

spending cutbacks 开支削减

service cutbacks 服务削减
