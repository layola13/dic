federate:/ˈfɛdəreɪt/| vt. vi. n.|联合；结盟；联邦制|To unite or combine into a federation or league; to form a federation.|例句：The states federated to form a new nation.  各州联合起来组成了一个新国家。  The two companies federated to increase their market share. 这两家公司联合起来以增加市场份额。|近义词：unite, combine, consolidate, ally|反义词：separate, divide, disunite|名词，动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The states federate to create a stronger union. = 各州联合起来创建一个更强大的联盟。
They federate their resources for mutual benefit. = 他们联合他们的资源以获得共同利益。

一般过去时:
The colonies federated to escape British rule. = 各殖民地联合起来以摆脱英国的统治。
The two companies federated last year. = 这两家公司去年联合了。

现在完成时:
Many countries have federated to form international organizations. = 许多国家已经联合起来组成了国际组织。
The organizations have federated to achieve a common goal. = 这些组织已经联合起来以实现共同的目标。

过去完成时:
Before the war, the tribes had federated against a common enemy. = 在战争爆发前，各部落已经联合起来对抗共同的敌人。
They had federated for years before the agreement was reached. = 在达成协议之前，他们已经联合多年了。

将来时:
The two groups will federate to improve their influence. = 这两个团体将联合起来以提高他们的影响力。
We believe that the countries will federate in the near future. = 我们相信这些国家将在不久的将来联合起来。

虚拟语气:
If the nations federated, they would have more power. = 如果这些国家联合起来，它们将拥有更大的权力。
It would be better if they federated their efforts. = 如果他们联合他们的努力会更好。

被动语态:
The new nation was federated by the agreement of its constituent states. = 新国家是由其组成州的协议联合而成的。
Their resources were federated to maximize efficiency. = 他们的资源被联合起来以最大限度地提高效率。

疑问句:

否定句:
The two groups did not federate despite numerous attempts. = 尽管多次尝试，这两个团体并没有联合起来。
The plan to federate the states failed. = 联合各州的计划失败了。
