psyching:/ˈsaɪkɪŋ/| v.|心理准备；激励；鼓舞；吓唬|To mentally prepare oneself or another for something; to try to influence or manipulate someone psychologically.|例句：I spent the morning psyching myself up for the big presentation.  我花了整个上午做心理准备，为大型演示做准备。|例句：He was psyching his team up before the game. 他在比赛前给他的团队打气。|近义词：preparing, motivating, encouraging, intimidating|反义词：discouraging, depressing, calming


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is psyching himself up for the marathon. = 他正在为马拉松做心理准备。
She psyches her kids up before their school performances. = 她会在孩子们的学校演出之前鼓励他们。


一般过去时:
I psyched myself up for the job interview. = 我为求职面试做了心理准备。
The coach psyched the team up before the final match. = 教练在决赛前给球队鼓劲。


现在完成时:
He has psyched himself up for the challenge. = 他已经为挑战做好了心理准备。
She has psyched her students up for the exams. = 她已经鼓励她的学生准备考试了。


过去完成时:
I had psyched myself up for a difficult conversation. = 我已经为一场艰难的谈话做好了心理准备。
They had psyched themselves up for a long journey. = 他们已经为长途旅行做好了心理准备。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If I had psyched myself up better, I would have done better. = 如果我当时的心理准备更好，我就会做得更好。
I wish I had psyched myself up more before the speech. = 我真希望我在演讲前能做好更多的心理准备。


被动语态:
The athletes were psyched up by their coach. = 运动员们受到了教练的鼓舞。
The team was psyched up for the competition. = 球队为比赛做好了心理准备。


疑问句:
Are you psyching yourself up for the test? = 你在为考试做心理准备吗？
Is he psyching his opponent up? = 他在吓唬他的对手吗？


否定句:
I didn't psych myself up enough for the exam. = 我没有为考试做好充分的心理准备。
She didn't psych herself up for the meeting. = 她没有为会议做好心理准备。


PHRASE->

psych up = 激励；鼓舞

psyched out =  精神崩溃;被吓倒
