polypary:/ˈpɒlɪpəri/| n. |珊瑚群体；珊瑚礁；水螅体群 | A polypary is the hard, skeletal structure formed by a colony of polyps, particularly in corals and hydrozoans.  It provides support and protection for the individual polyps.  The structure can take many forms, from branching structures to massive formations. | Example: The divers marveled at the vibrant colors of the polypary on the reef.  潜水员惊叹于珊瑚礁上珊瑚群体的鲜艳色彩。 The ancient polypary revealed much about the past ecosystem. 古代珊瑚群体揭示了以往生态系统的许多信息。|近义词：coral reef, coral colony |反义词：无
