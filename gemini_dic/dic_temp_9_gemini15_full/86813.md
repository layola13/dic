hematites:/ˈhɛmətaɪts/| n. |赤铁矿|A common iron oxide mineral, consisting of iron(III) oxide (Fe₂O₃), that is the most important ore of iron. Hematite is typically reddish-brown to black in color and has a metallic luster.
