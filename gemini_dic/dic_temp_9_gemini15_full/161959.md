prank:/præŋk/| n. v.|恶作剧，玩笑| A prank is a playful trick or joke, often mischievous but not intended to cause serious harm.  |例句：He played a prank on his friend by putting itching powder in his shoes. 他恶作剧地在他朋友的鞋子里放了痒痒粉。 |例句：They pranked the teacher by replacing his chair with a bucket. 他们恶作剧地把老师的椅子换成了一个水桶。|近义词：trick, joke, jape |反义词：serious act, sincere gesture


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often pranks his classmates. = 他经常捉弄他的同学。
She pranks people online. = 她在线上恶作剧别人。

一般过去时:
They pranked the teacher yesterday. = 他们昨天捉弄了老师。
I pranked my brother with a whoopie cushion. = 我用屁垫捉弄了我的哥哥。

现在完成时:
He has pranked me several times this week. = 这周他已经捉弄我几次了。
She has never pranked anyone so badly. = 她从未如此恶劣地捉弄过任何人。

过去完成时:
They had pranked him before he even arrived. = 在他到达之前他们就已经捉弄他了。
We had pranked the boss many times before he finally caught on. = 在老板最终识破之前，我们已经捉弄了他很多次了。

将来时:
I will prank him on his birthday. = 我将在他的生日那天捉弄他。
They will prank her with a fake spider. = 他们将用假蜘蛛捉弄她。

虚拟语气:
If I were him, I wouldn't prank anyone. = 如果我是他，我不会捉弄任何人。
I wish I hadn't pranked her; she's really upset. = 我真希望我没有捉弄她；她真的很生气。

被动语态:

The teacher was pranked by the students. = 老师被学生们捉弄了。

疑问句:

否定句:
