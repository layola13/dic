resmoothing:/ˌriːˈsmuːðɪŋ/| n. | 再平滑；再次磨光 | The process of smoothing something again, often after an initial smoothing process has been insufficient or the surface has become rough again.  This term is often used in technical contexts, particularly in manufacturing or image processing. | Example: The resmoothing of the concrete surface was necessary after the initial smoothing proved inadequate.  再次对混凝土表面进行平滑处理是必要的，因为最初的平滑处理效果不足。 |近义词: re-polishing, refinishing | 反义词: roughening, texturing | 名词
