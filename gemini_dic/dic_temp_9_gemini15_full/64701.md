escapology:/ɪˈskeɪpɒlədʒi/ | n. |逃避现实；逃避主义；逃脱术 |The study or practice of escaping from confinement or unpleasant situations;  a way of avoiding problems or responsibilities by distracting oneself.  It often involves engaging in activities that provide temporary relief but don't address the underlying issues. |例句：He used video games as a form of escapology to avoid dealing with his relationship problems. 他用电子游戏作为逃避现实的方式来避免处理他的感情问题。 |近义词：evasion, avoidance, escapism |反义词：confrontation, engagement
