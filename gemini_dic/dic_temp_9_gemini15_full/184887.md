retain:/rɪˈteɪn/ | vt. | 保持；保留；雇用 | To keep possession of something; to continue to have something.  |例句：The city retains much of its medieval character.  这座城市保留着许多中世纪的特色。
例句：She retained her composure despite the bad news.  尽管听到坏消息，她仍然保持镇静。 | keep, preserve, hold | lose, relinquish, release | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retains all his original documents. 他保留着他所有的原始文件。
We retain the right to refuse service. 我们保留拒绝服务的权利。

一般过去时:
The company retained a consultant. 公司聘请了一位顾问。
She retained her sense of humor even in difficult times.  即使在困难时期，她也保持着幽默感。

现在完成时:
They have retained their leading position in the market. 他们一直保持着在市场上的领先地位。
I have retained a lawyer to handle the case. 我已经聘请了一位律师来处理这个案子。

过去完成时:
He had retained the information for many years. 他已经保留了这些信息很多年了。
She had retained her youthful appearance despite her age. 尽管上了年纪，她仍然保持着青春的容貌。

将来时:
The museum will retain the painting in its collection. 博物馆将保留这幅画在其收藏中。
We will retain our current pricing strategy. 我们将保留我们目前的定价策略。

虚拟语气:
If only I could retain my youth!  如果我能保持青春就好了！
It is important that we retain our focus.  重要的是我们要保持专注。


被动语态:
The information was retained by the police.  这些信息被警方保留了。
The lawyer was retained by the defendant.  被告聘请了这位律师。

疑问句:
Does he retain any memory of the accident? 他还记得那次事故的任何事情吗？
Will the company retain all its employees? 公司会保留所有员工吗？

否定句:
He did not retain the information. 他没有记住这些信息。
The company failed to retain its market share. 公司未能保持其市场份额。
