gunpowdery:/ˈɡʌnˌpaʊdəri/| adj. |火药味的，像火药的，易爆的 |Resembling gunpowder in color, texture, or explosive potential.  The word suggests a dark, grainy appearance and potentially explosive nature, either literally or figuratively.|例句：The air in the old castle had a thick, gunpowdery smell.  古老城堡里的空气弥漫着浓重的火药味。

The old building was in a gunpowdery state, ready to collapse at any moment. 这栋老建筑物处于随时可能倒塌的危险状态。|近义词：explosive, combustible, inflammable |反义词：stable, inert, safe
