nonsacrificing: /ˌnɒnˈsækrɪfaɪsɪŋ/ | adj. | 不牺牲的，不自我牺牲的 | Not involving or requiring sacrifice; unwilling to make sacrifices.  |  例句：He adopted a nonsacrificing approach to the project, prioritizing efficiency over personal time. 他采取了一种不牺牲的方式来进行这个项目，优先考虑效率而不是个人时间。


例句：The nonsacrificing policy led to delays and increased costs.  不牺牲的政策导致了延误和成本增加。


近义词：unselfish, self-less, giving


反义词：sacrificial, self-sacrificing,  stingy


词性：形容词
