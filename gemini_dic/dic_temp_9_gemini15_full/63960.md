episiotomies:/ˌɛpɪziˈɒtəmiːz/| n.|会阴切开术（复数）|The surgical incision of the perineum during childbirth to enlarge the vaginal opening.  This procedure is performed to prevent tearing of the perineum during delivery.|例句：Episiotomies are sometimes necessary during childbirth to prevent severe tearing. 会阴切开术有时在分娩过程中是必要的，以防止严重的撕裂。

例句：The doctor explained the risks and benefits of episiotomies before the delivery.  医生在分娩前解释了会阴切开术的风险和益处。


近义词：perineal incision (会阴切开)


反义词：None (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑“自然分娩，无会阴切开”作为相对的概念)


词性：名词 (复数)
