incitement:/ɪnˈsaɪtmənt/ | n. |煽动；激励；鼓动 |The act of provoking unlawful behavior or violence.  It often involves persuading someone to commit an illegal act. |例句：The police are investigating the incitement to riot.  警方正在调查煽动暴乱的行为。|例句：His speech was full of incitement to hatred. 他的演讲充满了煽动仇恨的言论。|近义词：instigation, provocation, goading, stimulation |反义词：deterrence, dissuasion, calming

词源:
The word "incitement" comes from the Old French word "inciter," meaning "to incite, provoke, stir up."  The prefix "in-" intensifies the meaning, and the suffix "-ment" forms a noun.  The ultimate origin is the Latin verb "incitare,"  meaning "to urge on, spur on, incite."
