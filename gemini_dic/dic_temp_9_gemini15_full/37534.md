commonholder: /ˈkɑːmənˌhoʊldər/ | n. |共有持有人，共同持有人|A person or entity that holds a common interest in something, typically shares or other securities.  This term is often used in the context of corporate finance and investment.  It refers to someone who owns shares of a company's common stock.| The commonholders voted to approve the merger. 共有持有人投票批准了合并。| shareholder, stockholder |  sole owner | n.
