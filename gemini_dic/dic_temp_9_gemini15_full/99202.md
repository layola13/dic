interclavicular:/ˌɪntərkləˈvɪkjələr/| adj.| 锁骨间的；锁骨上的 | Situated between the clavicles (collarbones).  The term refers to the anatomical region or structures located between the two clavicles. | Example: The interclavicular ligament connects the two clavicles.  锁骨间韧带连接着两块锁骨。|近义词：interclaviculary|反义词：None specifically.  The antonym would describe a location *not* between the clavicles.
