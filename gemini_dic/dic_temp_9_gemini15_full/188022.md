roughener:/ˈrʌfənər/| n. |粗糙剂，使粗糙的东西|A substance or process that makes a surface rougher.  It can refer to a tool, material, or technique used to create a rough texture.|The sandpaper acted as a roughener on the wooden surface.  砂纸充当了木制表面的粗糙剂。|The factory uses a chemical roughener to improve grip on its products. 工厂使用化学粗糙剂来提高其产品的抓地力。|smoother|polisher|n.
