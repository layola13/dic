retailer:/ˈriːteɪlər/| n. |零售商，零售业者|A retailer is a business or person that sells goods to the public in relatively small quantities for use or consumption rather than for resale.|例句：Amazon is a major online retailer. 亚马逊是一个主要的在线零售商。  例句：The local retailer offered a discount for cash payments. 当地零售商提供现金支付折扣。|近义词：vendor, merchant, dealer|反义词：wholesaler, manufacturer
