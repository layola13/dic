conjectured:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərd/ | v. |推测；猜测 | To form an opinion or idea without sufficient evidence; to guess. |例句：The scientists conjectured that the earthquake was caused by tectonic plate movement.  科学家推测地震是由板块运动引起的。

例句：She conjectured that he was unhappy about the decision. 她推测他对这个决定不高兴。


近义词：speculated, guessed, surmised, hypothesized | 反义词：knew, confirmed, proved | 词性：动词
