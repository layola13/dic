here-or-there: /ˌhɪr ɔːr ðɛr/ | adv. | 到处；这里或那里 |  This adverb indicates a lack of specific location, suggesting something is scattered or happens in various places without a fixed point. It implies randomness or a lack of order. |例句：Books were scattered here or there. 书散落各处。

例句：I found my keys here or there in the room. 我在房间里到处找到了我的钥匙。


近义词：everywhere, anywhere, somewhere | 反义词：nowhere, specifically


词性: 副词
