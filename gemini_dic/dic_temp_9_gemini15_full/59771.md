economizes:/ɪˈkɑːnəmaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 节省；节约；精打细算 | To use something sparingly; to be economical.  |  She economizes on electricity by turning off lights when she leaves a room. 她通过离开房间时关灯来节省电。 They economize by buying in bulk. 他们通过批量购买来节省开支。 |  conserves, saves, thriftily uses | spends lavishly, squanders | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He economizes on fuel. = 他节省燃料。
She economizes on her grocery shopping. = 她精打细算地购物。

一般过去时:
They economized during the recession. = 他们在经济衰退期间节省开支。
We economized on our vacation spending. = 我们在度假支出上节省了开支。

现在完成时:
She has economized significantly this year. = 她今年已经节省了很多。
He has economized on his energy consumption. = 他已经节省了他的能源消耗。

过去完成时:
They had economized for months before buying the house. = 他们在买房子之前已经省了好几个月了。
We had economized on everything before the trip. = 我们在旅行之前已经节省了一切开支。

将来时:
We will economize on our travel expenses. = 我们将节省我们的旅行费用。

虚拟语气:
