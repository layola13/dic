masted:/ˈmæstɪd/| adj.|有桅杆的；装有桅杆的|Having a mast or masts.  A masted ship is one equipped with a mast or masts, essential for sailing.|例句：The masted schooner sailed gracefully across the bay.  这艘有桅杆的双桅帆船优雅地驶过海湾。|近义词： rigged, equipped (with masts)|反义词： mastless
