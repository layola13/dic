senten:/ˈsɛntən/ | n. |句子|A sentence is a group of words that expresses a complete thought. It typically contains a subject and a predicate and follows grammatical rules.  Sentences can be declarative (making a statement), interrogative (asking a question), imperative (giving a command), or exclamatory (expressing strong emotion).|The dog barked loudly. = 狗大声吠叫。  She is a talented musician. = 她是一位有天赋的音乐家。|phrase, clause|fragment
