downshifter:/ˈdaʊnʃɪftər/| n. |过简生活者，减速生活者 |A person who chooses to simplify their lifestyle, often by reducing their work hours, income, or material possessions.  They prioritize experiences and personal well-being over career advancement and material wealth.|  He's a downshifter, having left his high-pressure job to live a simpler life in the countryside. 他是一位过简生活者，辞去了高压工作，在乡下过着更简单的生活。|近义词：minimalist, simpler,  |反义词：workaholic,  materialist


USAGE->

He downshifts his lifestyle. = 他简化了他的生活方式。
She downshifts to spend more time with family. = 她放慢生活节奏，以便有更多时间陪伴家人。


一般过去时:
They downshifted after the birth of their child. = 他们在孩子出生后过起了简朴的生活。
