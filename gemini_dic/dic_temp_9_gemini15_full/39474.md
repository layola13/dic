conniver:/kəˈnaɪvər/ | n. | 纵容者；暗中勾结者 | A conniver is a person who secretly plots or conspires to do something immoral or illegal, often to gain an advantage.  They are characterized by their clandestine and deceitful behavior. | Example: The conniver secretly manipulated events to ensure his promotion.  纵容者暗中操纵事件以确保自己升职。 |近义词：conspirator, plotter, schemer | 反义词：honest person, upright individual
