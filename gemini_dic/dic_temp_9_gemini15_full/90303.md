houseplant:/ˈhaʊsplænt/| n. |室内植物，盆栽植物 |A houseplant is a plant that is grown indoors, typically in a pot or container.  Houseplants are often chosen for their aesthetic appeal, air-purifying qualities, or ease of care. | Example: I have many houseplants in my living room, including a spider plant and a peace lily. 我客厅里有很多室内植物，包括吊兰和白鹤芋。  She enjoys taking care of her various houseplants. 她喜欢照顾她各种各样的室内植物。|近义词：indoor plant, potted plant |反义词：outdoor plant, wild plant
