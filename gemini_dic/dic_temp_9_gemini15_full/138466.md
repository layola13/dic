novelism:/ˈnɒvəlɪzəm/ | n. | 小说主义；小说创作；小说风格 | The body of novels produced in a particular period or by a particular writer or school.  The characteristic features or style of a novelist's work.  |例句：The novelism of the Victorian era was characterized by social realism and moral questioning. 维多利亚时代的小说创作以社会现实主义和道德质疑为特征。

例句：Her novelism is marked by a distinctive use of imagery and symbolism. 她的小说风格以独特的意象和象征手法为特点。


近义词：novelization, fiction-writing, storytelling


反义词：non-fiction writing, factual writing


词性：名词
