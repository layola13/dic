preimbibing: /ˌpriːɪmˈbɪbɪŋ/ | v. | 预先浸泡；预先吸收 | The act of imbibing beforehand; soaking or saturating something before a process.  This word is not commonly used and is essentially a combination of "pre-" and "imbibing".  It implies an action taken before the main imbibing process. | Example: The brewer preimbibed the barley to help with the malting process.  酿酒师预先浸泡大麦以利于麦芽制作过程。  | 近义词: pre-soaking, pre-wetting | 反义词:  post-soaking, drying
