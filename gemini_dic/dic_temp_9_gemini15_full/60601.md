elbows:/ˈɛlboʊz/| n. |肘，肘部 |The elbows are the joints connecting the forearm and upper arm.  Elbows are often used as a point of leverage. |例句：He bumped his elbows on the table. 他把肘撞到了桌子上。

例句：She rested her elbows on the desk. 她把肘支在桌子上。


近义词：joint (in this context)


反义词：无


词性: 名词


词源:  From Old English *elnboga, literally "elbow," from *eln "elbow" + *boga "bend," "bow."  The word reflects the bent shape of the elbow joint.
