progressing:/prəˈɡresɪŋ/| v.|正在进步，进展中 |Making progress; advancing.  |例句：The project is progressing well. 项目进展顺利。|例句：The negotiations are progressing slowly. 谈判进展缓慢。|近义词：advancing, developing, proceeding|反义词：regressing, deteriorating, failing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is progressing smoothly. = 项目进展顺利。
He is progressing well in his studies. = 他的学习进展良好。

一般过去时:
The plant progressed rapidly after the rain. = 雨后植物生长迅速。
The company progressed steadily throughout the year. = 公司全年稳步发展。

现在完成时:
We have been progressing steadily towards our goal. = 我们一直在稳步朝着目标前进。
She has progressed significantly in her career. = 她的事业取得了显著进步。

过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, the construction had been progressing for months. = 我到达时，建设已经进行了几个月了。
They had progressed significantly in their research before the deadline. = 截止日期前，他们的研究取得了重大进展。

将来时:
The team will be progressing to the next stage of the competition. = 球队将进入比赛的下一阶段。
We will be progressing with the repairs as soon as possible. = 我们将尽快进行维修。

虚拟语气:
If the negotiations were progressing better, we could reach an agreement. = 如果谈判进展顺利，我们可以达成协议。
I wish the project was progressing faster. = 我希望项目进展更快。

被动语态:
The work is being progressed diligently. = 工作正在认真地进行。
The matter is being progressed by the relevant department. = 相关部门正在处理此事。

疑问句:
Is the construction progressing as planned? = 建设是否按计划进行？
How is the research progressing? = 研究进展如何？

否定句:
The project isn't progressing as quickly as we hoped. = 项目进展不如我们希望的那么快。
The talks are not progressing well. = 会谈进展不顺利。


PHRASE->
make progress: 取得进展
