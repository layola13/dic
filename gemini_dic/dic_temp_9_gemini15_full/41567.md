corporate:/ˈkɔːrpərət/| adj. n.| 公司的；法人的；团体的； | Relating to a corporation or corporations.  A large corporate entity often has many subsidiaries.  |例句：He works for a large corporate law firm. 他在一个大型公司律师事务所工作。|例句：The corporate culture is very competitive. 公司文化竞争非常激烈。|近义词：company, business, organizational |反义词：individual, private, personal
