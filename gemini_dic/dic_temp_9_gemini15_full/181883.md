relumine:/rɪˈluːmɪn/| vt.|使…再发光；使…重现光明；使…重新焕发活力|To shine or cause to shine again; to restore brightness or brilliance to something; to revitalize or re-energize.|例句：The artist relumined the old painting, restoring its vibrant colors.  艺术家使这幅旧画重新焕发光彩，恢复了它鲜艳的色彩。

例句：The coach's speech relumined the team's spirits after their losing streak. 教练的讲话在他们连败之后重新点燃了球队的斗志。

近义词：reilluminate, brighten, revitalize, re-energize|反义词：extinguish, darken, dull, deplete|词性:动词
