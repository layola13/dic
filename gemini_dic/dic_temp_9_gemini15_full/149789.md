pale:/peɪl/| adj.|苍白的，灰白的，暗淡的，无力的|Lacking color or intensity; appearing faint or washed out.  It can describe a person's complexion, colors, or even light. |例句：Her face was pale with fear.  她吓得脸色苍白。

例句：The pale sunlight filtered through the clouds.  暗淡的阳光透过云层过滤。


近义词：wan, ashen, pallid, faint, weak|反义词：ruddy, florid, vibrant, bright


词源:  The word "pale" comes from Old English *pāl, meaning "pale, wan."  This in turn is related to other Germanic words with similar meanings.
