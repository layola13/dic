RFID:/ˈɑːrˌɛfˈaɪdiː/|/ˈɑːrˌɛfˈaɪˌdiː/| n. |无线射频识别|Radio-Frequency Identification.  It's a technology that uses radio waves to automatically identify and track tags attached to objects. The tags contain electronically stored information.  This information can be read from a distance without the need for visual contact or physical contact with the tag.|例句：The company uses RFID tags to track its inventory. 这家公司使用RFID标签来追踪其库存。

例句：We implemented an RFID system to improve efficiency in our warehouse. 我们实施了RFID系统以提高仓库效率。


近义词：wireless tagging, electronic tagging

反义词：manual tracking, visual inspection


词性: 名词
