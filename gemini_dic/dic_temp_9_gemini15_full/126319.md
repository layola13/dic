myopathic:/ˌmaɪəˈpæθɪk/| adj.|肌病的；肌病性的|Relating to or characteristic of myopathy;  affected by or pertaining to disease of the muscles.  It describes conditions affecting the structure and function of muscles.|例句：The patient presented with myopathic symptoms, including muscle weakness and wasting.  这位病人出现了肌病症状，包括肌肉无力和萎缩。

The doctor suspected a myopathic condition after reviewing the patient's muscle biopsy results.  医生在审查病人的肌肉活检结果后怀疑是一种肌病。


近义词：muscular, myalgic (in some contexts)|反义词：normal (in the context of muscle function)
