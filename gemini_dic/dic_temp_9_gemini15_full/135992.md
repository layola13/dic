nonrandomly:/ˌnɑːnˈrændəmli/ | adv. |非随机地，非偶然地 |Not randomly; in a way that is not determined by chance.  It implies a deliberate or systematic selection or occurrence, as opposed to a haphazard or unpredictable one. |例句：The samples were selected nonrandomly to ensure representation from all demographics. 样本是非随机选择的，以确保代表所有人口统计数据。

The participants were nonrandomly assigned to different treatment groups.  参与者被非随机分配到不同的治疗组。


近义词：deliberately, intentionally, purposefully, systematically | 反义词：randomly, haphazardly, accidentally, arbitrarily


词性:副词
