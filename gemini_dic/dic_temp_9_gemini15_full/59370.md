earnings:/ˈɜːrnɪŋz/| n. |收入，盈利|Money received, especially on a regular basis, for work or through investments.  Earnings can refer to wages, salaries, profits from a business, or investment returns.|例句：Her annual earnings are over $100,000.  她的年收入超过10万美元。|例句：The company's earnings increased by 15% this quarter.  本季度公司盈利增长了15%。|近义词：income, revenue, profits, proceeds|反义词：losses, deficits
