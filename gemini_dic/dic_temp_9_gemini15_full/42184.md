coulis:/ˈkuːliː/| n.|库利斯，浓缩酱汁|A coulis is a purée of fruits, vegetables, or nuts, used as a sauce or condiment.  It's often strained to remove seeds and skins for a smooth texture. |The raspberry coulis was the perfect complement to the vanilla ice cream.  覆盆子库利斯是香草冰淇淋的完美搭配。|puree, sauce, purée| (no direct antonym, but opposite in function to a solid topping)|名词
