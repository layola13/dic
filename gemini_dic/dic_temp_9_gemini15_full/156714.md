piecener:/ˈpiːsnər/| n.|拼缝工，修补匠|A piecener is a person who mends or patches things, especially fabrics or garments.  They are skilled in repairing tears, holes, and other damages.|Example: The piecener skillfully repaired the tear in my favorite dress.  拼缝工巧妙地修补了我最喜欢的裙子上的破洞。|近义词：mender, patcher, repairer|反义词：destroyer, ruiner
