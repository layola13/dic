haiduck:/ˈhaɪdʌk/| n.|海达克(一种历史上的克罗地亚轻步兵)|A Haiduck was a member of a light infantry unit in the historical Croatian military.  They were known for their irregular warfare tactics and skills in mountain fighting. The term also sometimes refers to a brigand or outlaw.|The Haiducks were renowned for their bravery and skill in guerilla warfare.|近义词：irregular soldier,  partisan, freebooter|反义词：regular soldier,  law-abiding citizen|名词
