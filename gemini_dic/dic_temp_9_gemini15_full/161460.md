potamography:/ˌpɒtəˈmɒɡrəfi/|/n.|河流学；河川学|The scientific study of rivers, including their morphology, hydrology, and ecology.  It encompasses various aspects of river systems, from their origin to their mouth.|Potamography is a crucial field for understanding river ecosystems and managing water resources effectively.|近义词：river science, fluvial geomorphology|反义词：无
