piffled:/ˈpɪfəld/| v.|胡说八道，喋喋不休地谈论琐事|To talk or write at length about unimportant things; to trifle.|He piffled on about the weather for an hour. 他喋喋不休地谈论了一个小时的天气。|近义词：babbled, chattered, prattled|反义词：spoke concisely, was silent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He piffles on about his problems all day. = 他整天喋喋不休地抱怨他的问题。
She piffles incessantly about trivial matters. = 她没完没了地唠叨一些琐碎的事情。


一般过去时:
He piffled away the afternoon talking nonsense. = 他整个下午都在胡说八道。
She piffled about her day at work, detailing every insignificant event. = 她喋喋不休地讲述了她一天的工作，每一个微不足道的事情都详细描述。


现在完成时:
He has piffled away most of his savings on useless gadgets. = 他把大部分积蓄都花在了无用的电子产品上，真是浪费。
She has piffled for hours, achieving nothing of substance. = 她唠叨了几个小时，却一无所获。


过去完成时:
He had piffled for years before anyone realized the seriousness of his situation. = 在别人意识到他处境严重之前，他已经胡说八道了好几年了。
She had piffled away her chance at promotion by gossiping too much. = 她因为嚼舌根太多，错过了晋升的机会。


将来时:
He will piffle away his inheritance on frivolous pursuits. = 他将把他的遗产浪费在毫无意义的事情上。
She will piffle endlessly about her latest holiday. = 她会没完没了地谈论她最近的假期。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't piffled so much, he might have finished the project on time. = 如果他不那么唠叨，他可能就能按时完成项目了。
I wish she wouldn't piffle on about things that don't matter. = 我希望她不要再唠叨那些无关紧要的事情了。


被动语态:
His time was piffled away on meaningless tasks. = 他的时间浪费在了毫无意义的任务上。
The meeting was piffled away with pointless discussions. = 会议被毫无意义的讨论浪费掉了。


疑问句:
Does he often piffle about unimportant things? = 他经常谈论一些无关紧要的事情吗？
Was she piffling when you arrived? = 你到的时候她在胡说八道吗？


否定句:
He didn't piffle; he spoke directly and to the point. = 他没有胡说八道；他说话直接了当。
She didn't piffle about the details; she gave a concise summary. = 她没有对细节喋喋不休；她做了简要的总结。
