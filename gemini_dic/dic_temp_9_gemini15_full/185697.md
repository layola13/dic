reversible:/rɪˈvɜːrsəbl/ | adj. | 可逆的；可翻转的；可撤销的 | capable of being reversed or changed back to a previous condition.  It implies a process or state that can be undone or reversed.  | Example: The process is reversible, so we can easily undo the changes. 这个过程是可逆的，所以我们可以很容易地撤销更改。 The jacket is reversible, so it can be worn inside out. 这件夹克是可逆的，所以可以里外反穿。 |近义词：invertible, returnable, reciprocal | 反义词：irreversible, permanent, irreversible
