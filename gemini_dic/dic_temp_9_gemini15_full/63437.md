entreating: /ɪnˈtriːtɪŋ/ | adj. |恳求的；哀求的 | Pleading earnestly; begging.  |例句：Her entreating eyes begged him to stay.  她的恳求的眼神乞求他留下。|近义词：beseeching, imploring, supplicating |反义词：demanding, commanding |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The child is entreating his mother for candy. = 孩子正在恳求妈妈给他糖果。
She is entreating her boss for a raise. = 她正在恳求老板给她加薪。


一般过去时:
He entreated her to reconsider. = 他恳求她重新考虑。
They entreated the judge for mercy. = 他们恳求法官宽恕。


现在完成时:
She has been entreating him for weeks to help. = 她已经恳求他几周帮忙了。
They have entreated the government for assistance. = 他们一直在恳求政府提供援助。


过去完成时:
He had entreated her many times before she finally agreed. = 在她最终同意之前，他已经多次恳求她了。
They had entreated the gods for a good harvest. = 他们曾经祈求神灵保佑丰收。


将来时:
She will entreat her father for permission. = 她将恳求父亲允许。
They will entreat the committee to reconsider their decision. = 他们将恳求委员会重新考虑他们的决定。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would entreat him for forgiveness. = 如果我是你，我会恳求他原谅。
I wish I had entreated her to stay. = 我真希望我恳求她留下。


被动语态:
He was entreated by the crowd to speak. = 他被人群恳求发言。
The matter was entreated by the council. = 这件事被议会恳求。


疑问句:
Was he entreating for help? = 他是在恳求帮助吗？
Are they entreating for their lives? = 他们是在恳求他们的性命吗？


否定句:
She did not entreat him to change his mind. = 她没有恳求他改变主意。
They didn't entreat the audience for applause. = 他们没有恳求观众鼓掌。


PHRASE->
an entreating voice = 恳求的声音
an entreating look = 恳求的眼神
an entreating gesture = 恳求的手势
with an entreating tone = 用恳求的语气
an entreating letter = 一封恳求信


词源:
The word "entreating" comes from the Old French word "entraiter," meaning "to treat," "to handle," or "to negotiate."  This in turn derives from the Latin "inter" (between) and "tractare" (to handle, to treat).  The sense evolved from "handling a matter" to "pleading earnestly" over time.
