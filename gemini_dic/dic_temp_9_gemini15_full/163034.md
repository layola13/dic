preclassify:/ˌpriːˈklæsɪfaɪ/ | vt. |预先分类；预先归类 |To classify something before a more thorough classification.  This often involves a quick, initial sorting based on readily observable characteristics,  serving as a preliminary step to a more detailed analysis.  |例句：The samples were preclassified by size before undergoing detailed chemical analysis. 样本在进行详细的化学分析之前按大小进行了预先分类。
The images were preclassified into categories of animals, plants and objects. 图片被预先分类为动物、植物和物体类别。|近义词：pre-sort, categorize, preliminarily classify |反义词：post-classify,  final classify |动词
