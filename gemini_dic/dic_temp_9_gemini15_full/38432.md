concertina:/ˌkɒnsərˈtiːnə/| n. |手风琴；折叠式手风琴 | A musical instrument similar to an accordion, but smaller and usually having a hexagonal shape when closed.  It is played by squeezing the bellows and pressing the buttons. | Example: The musician played a lively tune on his concertina.  那位音乐家用手风琴演奏了一首欢快的曲子。|近义词：accordion, melodeon |反义词：无
