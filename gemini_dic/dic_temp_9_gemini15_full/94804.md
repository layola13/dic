impulsing: /ɪmˈpʌlsɪŋ/ | v. | 冲动地行动；推动；激励 |  The act of acting on impulse; giving impetus to something; motivating or stimulating. | 例句：He impulsed his way through the crowd. 他冲进人群。  |  The new technology impulsed the development of the industry. 新技术推动了产业发展。 | 近义词：urging, prompting, driving, goading | 反义词：restraining, inhibiting, hindering
