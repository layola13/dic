skivvied:/ˈskɪvid/| v.|偷懒；逃避工作；偷窃（尤指小件物品）|To avoid work or duties; to shirk.  Often implies stealing small items.  The term originates from British slang.|例句：He skivvied off work early. 他早早逃了班。|例句：They were accused of skivving and stealing stationery. 他们被指控偷懒并偷窃文具。|近义词：shirk, dodge, slack, pilfer|反义词：work diligently, toil, bust a gut
