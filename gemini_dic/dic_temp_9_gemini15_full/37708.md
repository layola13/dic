compar:/kəmˈpɛər/| v.|比较，对照|To examine or appraise (two or more objects, people, or things) in order to determine their similarities or differences.|例句：Let's compare the two plans before making a decision.让我们在做决定之前比较一下这两个计划。
例句：The teacher compared the students' essays.老师比较了学生的论文。|近义词：contrast, liken, match|反义词：differentiate, distinguish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He compares apples and oranges. = 他比较苹果和橘子。
They compare prices before buying. = 他们在购买前比较价格。

一般过去时:
She compared her work to her colleague's. = 她把自己的工作与同事的进行了比较。
We compared notes after the exam. = 考试后我们互相交流了答案。

现在完成时:
I have compared several options. = 我已经比较了几个选项。
They have compared their results with the previous study. = 他们已经将他们的结果与之前的研究进行了比较。

过去完成时:
She had compared all the data before drawing conclusions. = 在得出结论之前，她已经比较了所有数据。
They had compared the two proposals before making a decision. = 他们在做出决定之前已经比较了这两个提案。

将来时:
I will compare the two products tomorrow. = 我明天将比较这两个产品。
He will compare the different approaches to the problem. = 他将比较解决这个问题的不同方法。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would compare the prices first. = 如果我是你，我会先比较一下价格。
I wish I had compared the two cars before buying one. = 我希望我在买车之前能比较一下这两辆车。

被动语态:
The two paintings were compared by the art critic. = 这两幅画被艺术评论家进行了比较。
The different methods are being compared in this study. = 在这项研究中，正在比较不同的方法。

疑问句:
How do you compare these two candidates? = 你如何比较这两个候选人？
Can you compare the features of these two phones? = 你能比较一下这两款手机的功能吗？

否定句:
He did not compare the two options carefully. = 他没有仔细比较这两个选项。
They didn't compare their answers with each other. = 他们没有互相比较答案。


PHRASE->
compare notes = 互相交流信息
compare and contrast = 比较和对比
compare to = 比作
in comparison with = 与...相比
beyond compare = 无与伦比
