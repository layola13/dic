fulfil:/fʊlˈfɪl/ | /fʊlˈfɪl/ | vt. |履行；实现；满足；完成 | To carry out or bring to completion (a promise, a task, etc.).  To satisfy (a requirement, a need, etc.). |例句：He fulfilled his promise to help me. 他履行了帮助我的承诺。

He fulfilled all the requirements for the job. 他满足了这份工作的所有要求。


近义词：achieve, accomplish, complete, satisfy | 反义词：fail, neglect, disregard | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fulfils his duties diligently. 他勤勤恳恳地履行职责。
She fulfils her potential. 她发挥了自己的潜能。


一般过去时:
They fulfilled their contract. 他们履行了合同。
She fulfilled her lifelong dream. 她实现了毕生的梦想。


现在完成时:
I have fulfilled my obligations. 我已经履行了我的义务。
We have fulfilled our quota for this month. 我们已经完成了这个月的配额。


过去完成时:
He had fulfilled his part of the bargain. 他已经履行了自己那一部分的交易。
She had fulfilled her promise before he arrived.  在她到达之前，她已经履行了诺言。


将来时:
We will fulfil our commitment to the project. 我们将履行对项目的承诺。
They will fulfil their dreams. 他们将会实现他们的梦想。


虚拟语气:
If only I could fulfil my ambitions! 要是我能实现我的抱负就好了！
I wish I could fulfil my parents' expectations. 我希望我能满足父母的期望。


被动语态:
His request was fulfilled promptly. 他的请求得到了迅速的满足。
All the conditions of the contract were fulfilled. 合同的所有条件都得到了履行。


疑问句:
Will the company fulfil its promises? 公司会履行承诺吗？
Have you fulfilled your responsibilities? 你已经履行你的职责了吗？


否定句:
He failed to fulfil his duty. 他没有履行他的职责。
The company did not fulfil its contractual obligations. 公司没有履行合同义务。


PHRASE->
fulfil a dream: 实现梦想
fulfil a prophecy: 实现预言
fulfil a need: 满足需求
fulfil a role: 扮演角色
fulfil an obligation: 履行义务


词源:
The word "fulfil" originates from the Old English "fyllan," meaning "to fill," combined with the prefix "ful-" (meaning "full").  The word evolved through Middle English "fulfillen" to its modern form.
