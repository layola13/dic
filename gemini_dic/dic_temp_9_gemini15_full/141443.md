opiate:/ˈoʊpiət/ | n. |鸦片剂；麻醉剂 |a drug containing opium or derived from it, used to relieve pain.  Opiates act on the opioid receptors in the brain and other parts of the body.  They have a sedative effect and can cause physical dependence.|例句：Morphine is a powerful opiate used to treat severe pain. 吗啡是一种用于治疗剧烈疼痛的强效鸦片类药物。

The doctor prescribed an opiate to ease her post-surgical pain. 医生开了鸦片类药物来缓解她术后的疼痛。

|近义词：narcotic, analgesic |反义词：stimulant |名词
