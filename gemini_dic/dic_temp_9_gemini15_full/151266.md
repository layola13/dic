parents-in-law: /ˈpɛrəntsɪnˌlɔː/ | n. |岳父岳母；公公婆婆|

英文解析:The parents of one's spouse.  This term refers to the parents of your husband or wife.  It can be used to refer to both parents collectively or individually (e.g., "my father-in-law," "my mother-in-law").

例句:

I get along well with my parents-in-law. =我和我的岳父岳母相处融洽。

My parents-in-law are visiting us next week. =我的岳父岳母下周要来看我们。


近义词: spouse's parents


反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a descriptive term, not one with a direct opposite.)


词性: Noun (plural)
