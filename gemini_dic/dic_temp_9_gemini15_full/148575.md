WORD ORIGIN->

The word "overtalkativeness" is formed from:

* **over-**:  A prefix meaning "too much," "excessive," or "beyond."
* **talkative**: An adjective describing someone who talks a lot.
* **-ness**: A suffix that turns an adjective into a noun, indicating a state, quality, or condition.

Therefore, "overtalkativeness" literally means "the state of being excessively talkative."
