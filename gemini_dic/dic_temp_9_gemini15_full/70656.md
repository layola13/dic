filtrating:/ˈfɪltreɪtɪŋ/| v. |过滤；渗透；逐渐影响|The process of passing a liquid or gas through a filter to remove impurities or unwanted substances.  It can also refer to the gradual spread or influence of something.|例句：The kidneys are filtering waste products from the blood.  肾脏正在过滤血液中的废物。

例句：Her anxieties were slowly filtrating into her work performance. 她的焦虑慢慢地影响了她的工作表现。

近义词：straining, sieving, permeating, seeping, influencing

反义词：blocking, obstructing, preventing
