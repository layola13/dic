cambium:/ˈkæmbiəm/| n. |形成层|A layer of actively dividing cells between the xylem and phloem in plants, responsible for secondary growth.  It produces new xylem and phloem cells, causing the stem or root to increase in diameter. |Example: The cambium layer is vital for the growth and development of woody plants.  形成层对于木本植物的生长发育至关重要。|近义词：meristem (more general term)|反义词：无|名词
