metagenome:/ˈmɛtəˌdʒinoʊm/| n. |宏基因组|The complete genetic material recovered directly from environmental samples.  It represents the collective genomes of multiple organisms inhabiting a particular environment.  Metagenomics is the study of metagenomes.|例句：Researchers analyzed the metagenome of the soil sample to identify the microbial communities present. 研究人员分析了土壤样本的宏基因组，以识别存在的微生物群落。|例句：The study of the ocean's metagenome revealed a vast diversity of previously unknown microorganisms. 对海洋宏基因组的研究揭示了大量以前未知的微生物的多样性。|近义词：environmental genome, microbiome (though microbiome typically refers to the genomes of a specific community, often within a host)|反义词：genome (refers to the genetic material of a single organism)|名词
