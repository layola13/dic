intervallum:/ˌɪntərˈvæləm/| n.|间隔，间歇，时期|An interval is a period of time between two events or points in time.  It can also refer to a space or gap between things.|例句：There was a long interval between the first and second acts of the play.  第一幕和第二幕之间有很长的间隔。

例句：The doctor checked her blood pressure at regular intervals.医生定期检查她的血压。


近义词：gap, space, pause, break, period|反义词：continuity, uninterruptedness|词性:名词
