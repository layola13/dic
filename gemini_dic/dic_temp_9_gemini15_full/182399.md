reneges:/rɪˈniːɡz/| v.|食言；违约；背弃|To go back on a promise, undertaking, or contract.|He reneged on his promise to help us. 他违背了他帮助我们的承诺。|近义词：break one's word, go back on, default on|反义词：keep one's word, fulfill, honor
