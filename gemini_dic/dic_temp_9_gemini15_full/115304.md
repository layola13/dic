mannequin:/ˈmænɪkɪn/| n. |人体模型，模特儿|A mannequin is a life-size model of the human body, especially one used to display clothes.  It can also refer to a person who models clothes.|例句：The shop window displayed several mannequins dressed in the latest fashion.  这家商店橱窗里展示着几个穿着最新款服装的人体模型。|近义词：model, dummy, display figure|反义词：living person
