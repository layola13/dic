hark:/hɑːrk/| int. v.| 听！仔细听！|To listen attentively; to give heed.  It is an archaic word, now mainly used in literary contexts or to create a sense of old-fashioned style.| Hark!  What was that noise? 听！那是什么声音？|Listen, attend| Ignore, disregard| int. v.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
Hark! I hear a sound. = 听！我听到一个声音。
They hark to the call of the wild. = 他们倾听着大自然的召唤。


一般过去时:
He harked back to his childhood memories. = 他回想起童年的回忆。
She harked to the old stories her grandmother told. = 她倾听着祖母讲述的古老故事。


现在完成时:
We have harked to the advice of the experts. = 我们已经听取了专家的建议。
They have harked to the wisdom of their ancestors. = 他们一直遵循着祖先的智慧。


过去完成时:
He had harked to the warning signs, but it was too late. = 他曾注意过警告信号，但为时已晚。
She had harked to the whispers of the wind, and felt a chill. = 她曾倾听过风的低语，感到一丝寒意。


将来时:
We will hark to your words with great interest. = 我们将怀着极大的兴趣倾听你的话语。
They will hark to the advice of the elders. = 他们将听从长者的建议。


虚拟语气:
If I had harked to his advice, I would not be in this mess. = 如果我听了他的建议，我就不会陷入困境。
I wish I had harked to my intuition. = 我真希望我听了我的直觉。


被动语态:
His words were harked to by the entire audience. = 他的话语受到了全体观众的倾听。
The warnings were harked to, but unfortunately, too late. = 警告得到了重视，但不幸的是，为时已晚。


疑问句:
(Not directly applicable as an interrogative verb.  The imperative nature of "hark" makes direct questioning unusual.)


否定句:
He did not hark to the warnings of danger. = 他没有理会危险的警告。
They did not hark to the pleas for help. = 他们没有理会求助的呼喊。


PHRASE->
hark back to: 回想起，追溯到
hark to: 倾听，注意
hark! : 听！
(Limited phrasal verbs due to archaic nature)
