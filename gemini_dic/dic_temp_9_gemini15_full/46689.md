dams:/dæmz/| n.|水坝；拦河坝|A dam is a barrier constructed to contain or control the flow of water.  They are often used to create reservoirs for water storage, hydroelectric power generation, or flood control.|例句：The Hoover Dam is a massive structure that generates hydroelectric power. 胡佛水坝是一个巨大的水电站。|例句：The villagers built a small dam to irrigate their fields. 村民们建造了一个小水坝来灌溉农田。|近义词：barrier, weir, dike|反义词：opening, breach
