euryalida:/ˌjʊəriˈælɪdə/| n. |海星纲的一目|A taxonomic order within the class Asteroidea (starfish), characterized by having relatively large, often robust arms and a granular or spiny texture.  Members of this order are typically found in deeper waters and are known for their varied feeding strategies.|例句：The deep-sea expedition discovered several new species of euryalid starfish.|近义词：brittle star (in a broader sense, as both are echinoderms)|反义词：无
