respectfulness:/rɪˈspektfʊlnəs/| n. |恭敬；尊重；礼貌|The quality of showing respect; polite and considerate behavior.  It involves treating others with courtesy and deference, acknowledging their worth and dignity.|例句：Her respectfulness towards her elders was admirable.  她对长辈的尊敬令人钦佩。

例句：The students showed great respectfulness during the assembly. 学生们在集会上表现出极大的尊重。


近义词：courtesy, politeness, deference, consideration, civility

反义词：disrespect, rudeness, impoliteness, discourtesy, insolence

词性：名词
