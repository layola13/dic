idolatrous: /aɪˈdɑːlətrəs/ | adj. | 偶像崇拜的；盲目崇拜的 |  Characterized by or given to idolatry; worshipping idols.  Showing excessive or blind devotion or admiration. | 例句：His idolatrous devotion to the leader bordered on fanaticism.  他的对领导的盲目崇拜近乎狂热。

The idolatrous practices of the ancient civilization are well documented.  古代文明的偶像崇拜行为有充分的记载。


近义词：worshipping, adoring, venerating, revering


反义词：iconoclastic, atheistic, skeptical


词性:形容词
