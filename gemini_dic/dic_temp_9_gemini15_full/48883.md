defragments:/dɪˈfræɡmənts/| vt.|碎片整理；消除碎片|To remove fragmentation from (a computer disk or other storage medium).  This process reorganizes files to improve access speed and efficiency.|例句：The technician defragmented my hard drive to improve its performance.  技术人员对我的硬盘进行了碎片整理以提高其性能。


例句：Regularly defragmenting your computer's hard drive can prevent performance issues. 定期对计算机硬盘进行碎片整理可以防止性能问题。


近义词：optimize, consolidate, reorganize


反义词：fragment, scatter


词性:动词
