porch: /pɔːrtʃ/ | n. | 门廊，走廊 | A covered structure in front of the entrance to a building.  | Example: We sat on the porch and watched the sunset. 我们坐在门廊上看日落。 | 近义词：veranda, stoa | 反义词：interior


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The porch needs painting. = 门廊需要粉刷。
He sits on the porch every evening. = 他每天晚上都坐在门廊上。

一般过去时:
They decorated the porch for the party. = 他们为聚会装饰了门廊。
We left our shoes on the porch. = 我们把鞋子放在门廊上。

现在完成时:
We have added a swing to the porch. = 我们在门廊上加了一个秋千。
They have repainted the porch twice this year. = 他们今年已经粉刷了两次门廊。

过去完成时:
The porch had already collapsed before the storm hit. =暴风雨来临之前，门廊就已经倒塌了。
They had cleaned the porch before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经打扫了门廊。

将来时:
We will build a new porch next spring. = 我们明年春天将建造一个新的门廊。
They will decorate the porch with flowers for the wedding. = 他们将在婚礼上用鲜花装饰门廊。

虚拟语气:
If we had a larger porch, we could have a bigger party. = 如果我们有一个更大的门廊，我们就可以举办一个更大的派对。
I wish we had a porch to relax on. = 我希望我们有一个门廊可以放松一下。

被动语态:
The porch was damaged by the storm. = 门廊被暴风雨损坏。
The porch is being repaired this week. = 门廊本周正在维修。

疑问句:
Is there a porch on the house? = 房子上有一个门廊吗？
Does your porch get much sun? = 你的门廊阳光充足吗？

否定句:
The house doesn't have a porch. = 这房子没有门廊。
We didn't use the porch because it was raining. = 因为下雨，我们没有使用门廊。


PHRASE->
front porch = 前廊

covered porch = 遮盖式门廊

porch swing = 门廊秋千
