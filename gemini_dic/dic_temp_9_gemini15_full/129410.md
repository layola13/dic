nominally:/ˈnɒmɪnəli/| /ˈnɑːmənəli/ | adj. |名义上地；表面上地 |In name only; appearing to be but not actually being something.  It suggests a discrepancy between the official title or description and the reality. |例句：He is nominally the head of the department, but he has little real power. 他名义上是部门主管，但实际上权力很小。

例句：The company is nominally independent, but it's heavily influenced by its parent company. 这家公司名义上是独立的，但实际上受到母公司的很大影响。


近义词：apparently, ostensibly, superficially, seemingly

反义词：actually, really, truly, in reality
