deescalation: /ˌdiːˈɛskəleɪʃən/ | n. | 缓和；降级；冲突降级 | The process of reducing the intensity or severity of a conflict or potentially violent situation.  It involves taking steps to calm things down and avoid escalation. | Example:  The police successfully implemented de-escalation techniques to prevent the situation from becoming violent.  警方成功地实施了缓和策略，防止局势恶化。


| Example: The de-escalation of tensions between the two countries was a welcome development.  两国之间紧张局势的缓和是一个可喜的进展。 | 近义词：de-intensification, calming, pacification | 反义词：escalation, intensification, provocation | 词性：名词
