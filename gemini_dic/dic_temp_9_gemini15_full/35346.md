co-effect:/ˌkoʊɪˈfekt/| n. |协同效应，互效作用 |The combined effect of two or more things; synergistic effect.  It refers to the situation where the combined effect of multiple factors is greater than the sum of their individual effects. |例句：The co-effect of the medicine and the therapy improved his condition significantly.  药物和疗法的协同作用显著改善了他的病情。|The co-effect of climate change and deforestation is leading to increased desertification. 气候变化和森林砍伐的协同效应导致荒漠化加剧。|近义词：synergy, synergy effect, combined effect, interaction|反义词：antagonism, counteraction|名词
