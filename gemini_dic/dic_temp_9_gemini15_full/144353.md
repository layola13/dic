outstations:/ˈaʊtˌsteɪʃənz/| n.|分站；派出所；外地办事处|Branch offices or other facilities located away from a main office or headquarters.  These are typically smaller than the main office and serve a specific geographical area or perform specialized functions.|例句：The company has several outstations across the country. 这家公司在全国各地设有几个分站。

例句：Our outstation in the remote village provides essential services to the community. 我们在偏远村庄的分站为社区提供基本服务。


近义词：branch offices, field offices, regional offices, sub-offices

反义词：headquarters, main office, central office

词性:名词
