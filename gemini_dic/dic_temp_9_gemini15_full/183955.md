resettling: /ˌriːˈsɛtlɪŋ/ | v. |重新安置；重新定居 |The act of settling again, especially after displacement or relocation.  It implies a more permanent establishment than a temporary stay. |例句：The government is working on a plan for resettling refugees. 政府正在制定安置难民的计划。
例句：After the flood, many families were resettling in temporary housing. 洪水过后，许多家庭都搬到了临时住所重新安置。|近义词：relocating, reinstating, rehousing |反义词：displacing, evicting
