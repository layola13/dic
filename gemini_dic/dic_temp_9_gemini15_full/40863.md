cook-out:/ˈkʊkˌaʊt/| n. |户外野餐；烧烤聚会 |An outdoor gathering where food is cooked and eaten, typically involving grilling.  It often implies a casual and social atmosphere. |例句：We're having a cook-out at the park this weekend.  = 我们这个周末在公园举办烧烤聚会。|例句：The family enjoyed a cook-out in their backyard. = 这家人在自家后院享受了一次烧烤聚会。|近义词：barbecue, picnic, grill-out |反义词：indoor party, formal dinner.
