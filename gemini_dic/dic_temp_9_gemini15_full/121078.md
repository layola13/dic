miscoordinate:/ˌmɪskəʊˈɔːrdɪneɪt/| vt.|使协调不良；使配合失调|To fail to coordinate properly; to be out of coordination.|The team's miscoordinated efforts led to a disastrous outcome.  团队协调不力导致了灾难性的结果。|coordinate, harmonize|disorganize, disarrange|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They miscoordinate their efforts frequently. = 他们经常协调不好他们的努力。
The two departments miscoordinate their schedules. = 两个部门的日程安排相互冲突。


一般过去时:
The construction project miscoordinated its materials delivery. = 建设项目物料交付协调不力。
The musicians miscoordinated their performance last night. = 音乐家们昨晚的演出配合失调。


现在完成时:
The company has miscoordinated its marketing campaign. = 公司的营销活动协调不力。
The team has miscoordinated its tasks repeatedly. = 团队反复协调不好任务。


过去完成时:
The project had miscoordinated its resources before the deadline. = 项目在截止日期前资源协调不力。
They had miscoordinated their travel plans, leading to missed connections. = 他们旅行计划协调不力，导致错过了交通工具。
