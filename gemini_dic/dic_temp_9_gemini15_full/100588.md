/ɪnθ/ | pron. | 那 | That which; the thing that.  |  "I don't know what he wants, but I'll give him what he needs." 我不知道他想要什么，但我给他他需要的东西。 |  that, which |  this |  pronoun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？


一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she accomplished in her career? = 她在事业上取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the movie started? = 电影开始前他们吃了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天做什么？
What will we have for lunch? = 我们午餐吃什么？


虚拟语气:
If I knew what to do, I would do it. = 如果我知道该做什么，我就会去做。
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built in that area? = 那个地区正在建造什么？


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道该说什么。
He didn't tell me what he wanted. = 他没告诉我他想要什么。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What about...? = ......怎么样？
What if...? = 如果......将会怎样？
What for? = 为什么？为了什么？
No matter what = 无论如何
