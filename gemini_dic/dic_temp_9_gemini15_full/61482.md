embread:/ɛmˈbred/| vt.|裹面包屑；裹面包糠|To coat (food) with breadcrumbs.  This is typically done before frying or baking to create a crispy crust.|例句：I'm going to embread the chicken before frying it. 我要在油炸鸡肉之前裹上面包屑。

例句：She carefully embreaded the fish fillets before baking them.  她在烘烤鱼片之前仔细地裹上了面包屑。


近义词：bread, coat (with breadcrumbs)
反义词：无


词性:动词
