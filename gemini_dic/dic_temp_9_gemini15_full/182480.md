renounces:/rɪˈnaʊnsəs/| vt.|放弃；抛弃；否认；宣布放弃|To formally give up or reject something, especially a claim, right, or title.  It implies a deliberate and often public act of relinquishment.|例句：He renounced his claim to the throne. 他放弃了他对王位的继承权。|例句：She renounced her citizenship. 她放弃了她的国籍。|近义词：abandon, relinquish, forsake, repudiate|反义词：claim, accept, embrace, retain|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He renounces his beliefs every day. = 他每天都放弃他的信仰。
She renounces her inheritance. = 她放弃了她的遗产。

一般过去时:
He renounced his citizenship last year. = 他去年放弃了他的国籍。
She renounced her faith in God. = 她放弃了对上帝的信仰。

现在完成时:
He has renounced his violent past. = 他已经放弃了他暴力过去。
She has renounced all claims to the property. = 她已经放弃了对该房产的所有索赔。

过去完成时:
He had renounced his title before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他已经放弃了他的头衔。
She had renounced her family before moving to a new country. = 在搬到一个新的国家之前，她已经与她的家人断绝了关系。

将来时:
He will renounce his position next month. = 他下个月将辞职。
She will renounce her membership in the club. = 她将退出俱乐部的会员资格。

虚拟语气:
If he renounced his beliefs, he would be happier. = 如果他放弃他的信仰，他会更快乐。
I wish she had renounced her bad habits earlier. = 我希望她早点改掉坏习惯。

被动语态:
His claim was renounced by the court. = 他的说法被法院驳回了。
Her membership has been renounced by the organization. = 她的会员资格已被该组织取消。

疑问句:
Will he renounce his title? = 他会放弃他的头衔吗？
Has she renounced her American citizenship? = 她是否放弃了她的美国国籍？

否定句:
He did not renounce his beliefs. = 他没有放弃他的信仰。
She will not renounce her family. = 她不会放弃她的家人。
