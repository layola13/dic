caviled:/ˈkævɪld/| vt.|吹毛求疵；挑剔；故意找茬|To raise trivial objections; find fault unnecessarily.  To make petty or frivolous objections.|例句：He caviled at every detail of the plan. 他对计划的每一个细节都吹毛求疵。

例句：She constantly caviled at her colleagues' work, creating a negative atmosphere. 她不断地挑剔同事的工作，制造了负面的氛围。


近义词：criticize, quibble, carp, nitpick|反义词：praise, commend, approve


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cavils at every little thing. = 他对每一件小事都吹毛求疵。
They cavil about the price of the goods. = 他们对商品的价格斤斤计较。


一般过去时:
She caviled at the quality of the materials. = 她对材料的质量吹毛求疵。
He caviled at my suggestion, saying it was impractical. = 他对我的建议吹毛求疵，说它不切实际。


现在完成时:
They have caviled at the design for months. = 他们几个月来一直在对设计吹毛求疵。
She has caviled at every decision made by the committee.  = 她对委员会做出的每一个决定都吹毛求疵。


过去完成时:
He had caviled at the proposal before it was even presented. = 在提案提出之前，他就已经对它吹毛求疵了。
They had caviled at minor details, delaying the project. = 他们对细枝末节吹毛求疵，延误了项目。


将来时:
He will cavil at any attempt to change the rules. = 他会对任何试图改变规则的尝试都吹毛求疵。
They will probably cavil at the cost of the renovation. = 他们可能会对装修的费用吹毛求疵。


虚拟语气:
If he caviled at every detail, the project would never be finished. = 如果他对每一个细节都吹毛求疵，项目将永远无法完成。
I wish he hadn't caviled so much during the presentation. = 我希望他在演示期间不要那么吹毛求疵。


被动语态:
His ideas were caviled at by the board. = 他的想法受到了董事会的吹毛求疵。
The proposal was caviled at because of its vague wording. = 提案因为措辞含糊而受到了吹毛求疵。


疑问句:
Does he always cavil at everything? = 他总是对所有事情都吹毛求疵吗？
Why did they cavil at such a minor point? = 为什么他们对这样一个微不足道的问题吹毛求疵？


否定句:
He didn't cavil at the suggestions, instead, he offered helpful advice. = 他没有对建议吹毛求疵，而是提出了有益的建议。
She didn't cavil at the extra work, she simply completed it. = 她没有对额外的工作吹毛求疵，她只是完成了它。


PHRASE->
caviled at: 吹毛求疵于……
caviled about: 对……吹毛求疵
caviled over: 对……斤斤计较
caviled with: 与……争论细节
constantly caviled:  不断吹毛求疵


词源:
From Middle English *cavelen, from Old French caviller, of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old French cave, "cave," implying a hidden, subtle criticism.
