nasturtium: /nəˈstɜːrʃəm/ | n. |旱金莲，金莲花 | A flowering plant with round, shield-shaped leaves and brightly colored flowers.  They are often grown as ornamentals and some species are edible. | The nasturtiums are blooming beautifully in the garden. 花园里的旱金莲开得真漂亮。  |  None specifically provided in standard dictionaries. | weed (in some contexts) | noun
