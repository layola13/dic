filmmaker:/ˈfɪlmˌmeɪkər/ | n. |电影制作人 |A filmmaker is a person who makes films, encompassing various roles like director, producer, writer, and editor.  They are responsible for the creative vision and execution of a film project. | Example:  Quentin Tarantino is a renowned filmmaker known for his unique style.  昆汀·塔伦蒂诺是一位以其独特风格而闻名的著名电影制作人。|近义词：film director, moviemaker, director |反义词：film viewer, audience
