rummager:/ˈrʌmɪdʒər/| v.|翻找，搜寻|To search unsystematically and thoroughly through something.|例句：She rummaged in her handbag for her keys. 她翻遍了她的手提包找钥匙。

例句：He rummaged through the drawers looking for his passport. 他翻箱倒柜地找他的护照。


近义词：search, hunt, ferret, delve


反义词：organize, tidy


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rummages through his closet every morning. 他每天早上都在翻找他的衣柜。
She rummages in her bag for her phone. 她翻包找她的手机。


一般过去时:
I rummaged through the attic yesterday. 我昨天翻找了阁楼。
They rummaged in the old trunk for family heirlooms. 他们翻找旧箱子寻找传家宝。


现在完成时:
I have rummaged through all my files, but I can't find it. 我已经翻遍了我的所有文件，但我还是找不到。
She has rummaged through the cupboard several times today. 她今天已经好几次翻找了橱柜。


过去完成时:
He had rummaged through the box before I arrived. 我到达之前他已经翻找过那个箱子了。
We had rummaged through the entire house before we found it. 我们翻遍了整个房子才找到它。


将来时:
I will rummage through the basement tomorrow. 我明天将翻找地下室。
They will rummage through the old photographs this weekend. 他们这个周末将翻找旧照片。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rummage through that old chest. 如果我有更多时间，我会翻找那个旧箱子。
I wish I could rummage through the library archives. 我真希望我能翻找图书馆的档案。


被动语态:
The attic was rummaged through by the movers. 阁楼被搬家工人翻找过了。
The drawers were rummaged through, leaving everything in disarray. 抽屉被翻乱了，所有东西都乱七八糟的。


疑问句:
Did you rummage through my things? 你翻找过我的东西吗？
Will you rummage through the suitcase for me? 你会帮我翻找一下手提箱吗？


否定句:
I didn't rummage through your bag. 我没有翻找你的包。
He wouldn't rummage through the boxes without permission. 他不会未经许可翻找箱子。
