crypt:/krɪpt/ | n. |地窖；墓穴；密码；隐秘处 | A crypt is an underground chamber or vault, typically one beneath a church, used as a burial place.  It can also refer to a secret or hidden place, or a system of secret writing. |例句：The ancient crypt contained many sarcophagi.  古老的地窖里有很多石棺。

The team used a complex crypt to protect their sensitive data.  团队使用复杂的密码来保护他们的敏感数据。|近义词：vault, tomb, sepulcher, code, cipher |反义词：open, exposed, revealed
