refreshful: /rɪˈfreʃfəl/ | adj. |令人精神焕发的，使人清爽的 |  Feeling refreshed and invigorated; restoring energy and vitality.  The word implies a feeling of renewed energy and vitality, often after rest, relaxation, or a refreshing experience. | 例句：The cool breeze was refreshful after the long hike. 长途跋涉之后，凉爽的微风令人精神焕发。


The mountain air was refreshful and invigorating. 山间空气清新宜人，令人精神振奋。|近义词：invigorating, refreshing, revitalizing, restorative |反义词：exhausting, tiring, debilitating
