encloud:/ɪnˈklaʊd/ | vt. |使布满云；使阴暗；使模糊|To cover or obscure with or as if with clouds; to darken or overshadow.|例句：The sky enclouded quickly after sunset.日落后天空很快就阴暗了。|例句：His mind was enclouded by grief.他的思绪被悲伤所笼罩。|近义词：obscure, cloud over, overshadow |反义词：clear, brighten, illuminate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The clouds encloud the mountain peaks. = 云彩遮蔽了山峰。
The worries encloud her judgement. = 忧虑使她的判断蒙上阴影。

一般过去时:
The storm enclouded the entire valley. = 暴风雨遮蔽了整个山谷。
Grief enclouded his usually cheerful spirit. = 悲伤笼罩了他通常快乐的精神。

现在完成时:
The heavy rain has enclouded the visibility. = 大雨已经降低了能见度。
Doubt has enclouded his confidence in the project. = 怀疑已经使他对这个项目失去了信心。

过去完成时:
The fog had enclouded the coastline before the ship arrived. = 在船到达之前，雾已经笼罩了海岸线。
His anger had enclouded his reason before he made the decision. = 他在做决定之前，愤怒已经蒙蔽了他的理智。

将来时:
The approaching storm will encloud the entire region. = 即将到来的暴风雨将笼罩整个地区。
Fear will encloud his ability to perform well. = 恐惧将会影响他的良好表现能力。

虚拟语气:
If the sun were to disappear, the world would be enclouded in darkness. = 如果太阳消失了，世界将会被黑暗笼罩。
If he had not enclouded his mind with doubts, he might have succeeded. = 如果他没有让怀疑蒙蔽了他的思想，他也许会成功。

被动语态:
The mountain was enclouded by a thick fog. = 山被浓雾笼罩着。
His future was enclouded by uncertainty. = 他的未来充满了不确定性。

疑问句:
Was the view enclouded by the mist? = 景色被雾遮挡了吗？
Is his judgement enclouded by his emotions? = 他的判断受到情绪的影响吗？

否定句:
The weather did not encloud the picnic. = 天气并没有影响野餐。
He refused to let sadness encloud his outlook on life. = 他拒绝让悲伤影响他的人生观。
