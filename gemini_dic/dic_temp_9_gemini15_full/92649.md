ibises:/ˈaɪbɪsiːz/| n.|朱鹭属鸟类|Ibises are a group of long-legged wading birds belonging to the family Threskiornithidae.  They are characterized by their long, down-curved bills, which they use to probe mud and water for food.  Many species exhibit striking plumage, often with iridescent colours.|例句：We saw several scarlet ibises wading in the shallows. 我们看到几只猩红朱鹭在浅滩涉水。|例句：The sacred ibis was revered by the ancient Egyptians.  古埃及人崇拜圣鹮。|近义词：bird, wader|反义词:|词性:n.
