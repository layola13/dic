embodies:/ɪmˈbɑːdiz/| vt.|体现；包含；具体表现|To represent or express something such as a quality or idea.  To include or contain something.|例句：His paintings embody the spirit of the times. 他的画作体现了那个时代的精神。

例句：The new building embodies modern architectural design. 这座新建筑体现了现代建筑设计。


近义词：incarnate, represent, personify, exemplify|反义词：exclude, omit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He embodies the spirit of innovation. 他体现了创新的精神。
This policy embodies our commitment to equality.这项政策体现了我们对平等的承诺。


一般过去时:
The sculpture embodied the artist's vision. 这尊雕塑体现了艺术家的远见。
Her performance embodied grace and power. 她的表演体现了优雅和力量。


现在完成时:
His work has embodied many different artistic styles. 他的作品已经体现了许多不同的艺术风格。
The novel has embodied the struggles of a generation. 这部小说体现了一代人的奋斗。


过去完成时:
The previous laws had embodied outdated social values.以前的法律体现了过时的社会价值观。
The play had embodied the themes of love and loss. 这部戏剧体现了爱与失去的主题。


将来时:
The upcoming film will embody the director's unique style.即将上映的电影将体现导演独特的风格。
The new design will embody sustainable practices. 新的设计将体现可持续的实践。


虚拟语气:
If the design embodied more creativity, it would be more successful.如果设计更具创意，它将会更成功。
I wish the project embodied a more collaborative spirit.我希望这个项目能体现更强的合作精神。


被动语态:
The ideals of freedom and justice were embodied in the constitution. 自由和正义的理想体现在宪法中。
The company's values are embodied in its mission statement.公司的价值观体现在其使命宣言中。


疑问句:
Does this painting embody the artist's true feelings? 这幅画是否体现了艺术家真实的感受？
Does the policy truly embody the government's intentions? 这项政策是否真正体现了政府的意图？


否定句:
The design did not embody the client's vision. 该设计没有体现客户的愿景。
The film failed to embody the complexities of the characters. 这部电影未能体现人物的复杂性。


PHRASE->
embody in: 体现在...中
embody the spirit of: 体现...的精神
embody a concept: 体现一个概念
embody principles: 体现原则
embody values: 体现价值观
