cowlike:/ˈkaʊlaɪk/| adj.|像牛一样的；迟钝的；笨拙的 | Resembling a cow; especially in being slow-moving, docile, or placid.  It can also imply clumsiness or lack of intelligence. |例句：The cowlike creature grazed peacefully in the meadow.  这只像牛一样的生物在草地上平静地吃草。

The farmer described his new ox as being rather cowlike in its movements.  这位农民形容他新买的牛在行动上相当迟钝。


近义词： bovine, oxlike, sluggish, dull, clumsy

反义词： agile, nimble, quick-witted, sharp


词源:  The word is a compound of "cow" and the suffix "-like," meaning "resembling" or "having the characteristics of."  "Cow" comes from the Old English "cū," related to Proto-Germanic "*kōuz" and Proto-Indo-European "*gʷṓws," all meaning "cow." The suffix "-like" is from Old English "-līċ,"  a suffix of resemblance.
