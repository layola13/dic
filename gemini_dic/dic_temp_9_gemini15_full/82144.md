groom:/ɡruːm/ | n./v.| 新郎；马夫；照料；打扮；训练 | groom refers to a man who is about to be married or is newly married, a person who takes care of horses, or the act of cleaning, preparing, or training something.  |例句：He is the groom at the wedding. 他是婚礼上的新郎。|近义词：bridegroom, bridegroom, stableman |反义词：bride


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grooms his horse every morning. = 他每天早上都给他的马梳理毛发。
She grooms her dog regularly. = 她定期给她的狗梳理毛发。


一般过去时:
The groom nervously adjusted his tie. = 新郎紧张地调整了他的领带。
He groomed the horse before the race. = 他在比赛前为马梳理毛发。


现在完成时:
They have groomed the dog for the competition. = 他们已经为比赛给狗打扮好了。
She has groomed herself for the important meeting. = 她已经为重要的会议打扮好了自己。


过去完成时:
He had groomed the horse for hours before the show. = 在演出前，他已经为马梳理毛发数小时了。
She had groomed her hair carefully before the interview. = 面试前，她仔细地梳理了头发。


将来时:
He will groom the dog for the show tomorrow. = 他明天将为展览给狗梳理毛发。
She will groom herself meticulously for the gala. = 她将为盛会精心打扮自己。


虚拟语气:
If he groomed his horse more often, it would be healthier. = 如果他更经常地给他的马梳理毛发，它会更健康。
I wish I had groomed myself better for the interview. = 我希望我面试时打扮得更好一些。


被动语态:
The horses are groomed twice a day. = 这些马一天梳理两次毛发。
The dog was groomed professionally for the show. = 这只狗为展览进行了专业的梳理。


疑问句:
Does he groom his horse himself? = 他自己给他的马梳理毛发吗？
Will she groom her hair for the party? = 她会为派对梳理头发吗？


否定句:
He doesn't groom his horse properly. = 他没有正确地给他的马梳理毛发。
She didn't groom her hair before the meeting. = 她没有在会议前梳理头发。
