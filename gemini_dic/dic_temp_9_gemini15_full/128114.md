nestlings:/ˈnestlɪŋz/| n. |雏鸟，幼鸟| Young birds that have recently hatched from their eggs and are still in the nest.  They are dependent on their parents for food and protection. | Example: The mother bird carefully fed the nestlings in her nest.  母鸟小心翼翼地喂养窝里的雏鸟。  The nestlings chirped loudly, begging for more food.  雏鸟大声鸣叫，乞求更多的食物。|近义词：chicks, young birds|反义词：adult birds
