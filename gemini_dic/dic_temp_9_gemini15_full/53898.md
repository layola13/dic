discotheque:/ˌdɪskoʊˈtek/| n. |迪斯科舞厅|A discotheque is a nightclub where people dance to disco music. It is characterized by its lively atmosphere, often featuring a dance floor, a DJ playing popular music, and a lighting system designed to enhance the experience. The term is a blend of "disco" (short for discotheque) and "theque," referring to the French word for "place" or "establishment."  Discotheques were particularly popular in the 1970s and are still found in many parts of the world today, although the term is less common now than in the past.|例句：They went to a discotheque and danced all night. 他们去迪斯科舞厅跳了一整夜。|近义词：nightclub, dance club, disco|反义词：library, quiet place
