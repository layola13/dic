post-Einsteinian: /ˌpoʊst aɪnˈstaɪniən/ | adj. | 爱因斯坦之后(的)；后爱因斯坦时代的 | Relating to or denoting the period in physics and related fields following the development of Einstein's theories of relativity.  It signifies a period characterized by advancements and reinterpretations built upon Einstein's groundbreaking work. | Example: Post-Einsteinian physics grapples with the complexities of quantum gravity.  爱因斯坦之后的物理学努力解决量子引力的复杂性。 | 近义词: post-relativistic | 反义词: pre-Einsteinian
