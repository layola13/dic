depredate:/ˈdɛprɪdeɪt/| vt.|贬低；使贬值；掠夺；破坏|To depreciate something means to reduce its value or worth, either financially or in terms of reputation. It can also mean to plunder or raid a place.  |例句：The constant criticism depreciated her confidence.  持续的批评贬低了她的自信。

The invading army depredated the countryside.  侵略军掠夺了乡村。|近义词：devalue, belittle, diminish, plunder, ravage|反义词：appreciate, enhance, improve, protect


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government depreciates the currency to boost exports. = 政府贬值货币以促进出口。
He constantly depreciates his own accomplishments. = 他不断贬低自己的成就。


一般过去时:
The antique's value depreciated significantly over time. = 古董的价值随着时间的推移大幅贬值。
The rebels depredated the villages, stealing food and livestock. = 叛乱分子掠夺了村庄，偷走了食物和牲畜。


现在完成时:
The value of the property has depreciated considerably since we bought it. = 自从我们购买以来，房产的价值已经大幅贬值。
The invaders have depredated the region, leaving it in ruins. = 侵略者已经掠夺了该地区，使其成为废墟。


过去完成时:
The company's stock had depreciated sharply before the merger announcement. = 在合并公告发布之前，该公司的股票已经大幅贬值。
The bandits had depredated the caravan before the authorities arrived. = 在当局到达之前，强盗已经掠夺了商队。


将来时:
The car's value will depreciate rapidly in the first few years. = 汽车的价值在最初几年会迅速贬值。
They predict that the drought will depredate the agricultural output. = 他们预测干旱将破坏农业产量。


虚拟语气:
If the government hadn't depreciated the currency, exports would have suffered. = 如果政府没有贬值货币，出口就会受到影响。
If the army had not depredated the city, it would have remained prosperous. = 如果军队没有掠夺这座城市，它就会保持繁荣。


被动语态:
The currency was depreciated to make exports more competitive. = 货币被贬值以使出口更有竞争力。
The villages were depredated by the marauding soldiers. = 村庄被四处抢劫的士兵掠夺了。


疑问句:
Has the value of your investment depreciated? = 你的投资价值贬值了吗？
Were the towns depredated during the war? = 在战争期间城镇被掠夺了吗？


否定句:
The company's profits did not depreciate last quarter. = 该公司上季度的利润没有下降。
The invaders did not depredate the peaceful villages. = 侵略者没有掠夺和平的村庄。
