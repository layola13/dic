meteoroid:/ˈmiːtiərɔɪd/| n. |流星体|A small rocky or metallic body in outer space. Meteoroids are smaller than asteroids and, unlike asteroids, completely burn up in the atmosphere.|Example:  A meteoroid entered the Earth's atmosphere and became a spectacular meteor.|近义词: meteor (when entering atmosphere), space rock|反义词: planet, star

The meteoroid travels through space at high speed. = 流星体以高速在太空中穿行。
Meteoroids frequently enter Earth's atmosphere. = 流星体经常进入地球大气层。


一般过去时:
A large meteoroid exploded over Tunguska in 1908. = 1908年，一颗巨大的流星体在通古斯上空爆炸。
