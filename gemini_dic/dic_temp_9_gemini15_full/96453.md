indivisible:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪzəbəl/| adj.|不可分割的，不可分的| incapable of being divided or separated.  It implies a fundamental unity or wholeness that cannot be broken down into smaller parts. |The atom was once considered indivisible, but modern physics has shown it to be composed of smaller particles.  原子曾被认为是不可分割的，但现代物理学已经证明它是由更小的粒子组成的。| inseparable, unified | divisible, separable |形容词
