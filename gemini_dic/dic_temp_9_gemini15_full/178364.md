reconfirming:/ˌriːkənˈfɜːrmɪŋ/| v.|再次确认；重新确认 |  The act of confirming something again, usually to ensure accuracy or to eliminate doubt.  It implies a previous confirmation already exists. |例句：We are reconfirming your flight details to ensure everything is correct. 我们正在再次确认您的航班信息，以确保一切正确。

例句：The hotel is reconfirming all reservations due to the recent storm.  酒店正在因最近的风暴而再次确认所有预订。


近义词：confirming again, verifying, checking, double-checking

反义词：canceling, denying, refuting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reconfirm the booking every week. = 他们每周都再次确认预订。
We reconfirm our commitment to quality. = 我们再次确认我们对质量的承诺。


一般过去时:
He reconfirmed the appointment yesterday. = 他昨天再次确认了约会。
She reconfirmed her attendance at the meeting. = 她再次确认她将参加会议。


现在完成时:
I have reconfirmed the delivery time with the supplier. = 我已经与供应商再次确认了交货时间。
They have reconfirmed their participation in the project. = 他们已经再次确认他们参与该项目。


过去完成时:
Before the deadline, they had reconfirmed their decision. = 在截止日期之前，他们已经再次确认了他们的决定。
She had reconfirmed the address before sending the package. = 在寄包裹之前，她已经再次确认了地址。


将来时:
We will reconfirm the details with the client tomorrow. = 我们明天将与客户再次确认细节。
He will reconfirm his travel plans before leaving. = 他会在出发前再次确认他的旅行计划。


虚拟语气:

I wish he had reconfirmed the meeting time. = 我希望他曾经再次确认会议时间。

否定句:
