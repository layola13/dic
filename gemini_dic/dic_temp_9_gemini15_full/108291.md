laureate:/ˈlɔːriət/|/ˈlɔːriˌeɪt/| n. |获奖者，桂冠诗人 |A person who is honored with an award, especially a Nobel Prize or similar prestigious award.  A laureate is often associated with high achievement and recognition in their field. |The Nobel laureate delivered a compelling speech.  诺贝尔奖获得者发表了一篇引人入胜的演讲。|winner, recipient, champion |loser, failure|名词
