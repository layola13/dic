ribbed:/rɪbd/| adj.|有棱的；有肋骨的；罗纹的；|Having ridges or raised lines.  It can refer to the bones in a ribcage, textured fabric, or other objects with a similar pattern.|例句：The sweater is ribbed for extra warmth. 这件毛衣是罗纹的，为了保暖。

例句：The sculptor created a ribbed texture on the clay. 雕塑家在粘土上创造了一种有棱纹的纹理。


近义词：ridged, furrowed, corrugated, textured|反义词：smooth, plain


词源:  From Middle English *ribbed, from Old English *ribb, "rib". The word's meaning has broadened over time to include any surface with a ribbed pattern.
