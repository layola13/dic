capriccioso: /ˌkæprɪˈtʃoʊsoʊ/ | adj. | 突发奇想的；反复无常的；异想天开的；随性的 |

英文解析:  Characterized by unpredictable changes of mood or behavior; impulsive and erratic.  Often used to describe music that is whimsical, playful, and full of unexpected turns.

例句:

The composer's capriccioso style kept the audience on the edge of their seats.  作曲家反复无常的风格让观众始终屏息凝神。

Her capriccioso nature made it difficult to plan anything with her. 她反复无常的性格使得很难和她一起计划任何事情。


近义词: whimsical, fanciful, erratic, impulsive, fickle, volatile

反义词: predictable, consistent, steady, reliable, stable

词性: adjective
