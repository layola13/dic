prox:/prɒks/| adj.|邻近的，接近的；代理的|Near or close in space or time; acting on behalf of another.|The proximal cause of the accident was driver error. 事故的直接原因是驾驶员的错误。|近义词：nearby, adjacent, approximate|反义词：distant, remote, far
