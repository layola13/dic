pellagrose: /ˌpɛləˈɡroʊs/ | n. | 糙皮病 | A severe vitamin deficiency disease, characterized by dermatitis, diarrhea, and dementia. It is caused by a lack of niacin (vitamin B3).  | Example:  The prolonged malnutrition led to the development of pellagra in the impoverished community.  长期营养不良导致贫困社区中出现糙皮病。 | 近义词: niacin deficiency, vitamin B3 deficiency | 反义词:  (lacks a direct antonym, but could be considered the opposite of good nutrition)  良好的营养状况
