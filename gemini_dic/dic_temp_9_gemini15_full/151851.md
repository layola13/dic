passion: /ˈpæʃən/ | n. |热情，激情，酷爱，强烈爱好 | intense emotion, a strong and barely controllable emotion.  It can also refer to a strong liking for something. |例句：Her passion for music is evident in her performances.  她的音乐热情在她精彩的表演中显而易见。

例句：He pursued his passion for photography with unwavering dedication. 他以坚定不移的奉献精神追求他对摄影的热爱。


近义词：enthusiasm, zeal, fervor, ardor | 反义词：apathy, indifference


词源:  The word "passion" comes from the Old French word "passion," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "passio," meaning "suffering," "feeling," or "experience."  The original meaning related to the suffering of Christ, but the word evolved to encompass strong emotions in general.
