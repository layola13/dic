rashest:/ˈræʃɪst/| adj.|最轻率的；最鲁莽的|Most reckless or impetuous.  It describes the superlative degree of rashness.  |例句：His rashest decision was to invest all his savings in a risky venture. 他最轻率的决定是将他所有的积蓄都投资到一项风险很大的事业中。


例句：That was the rashest thing he'd ever done. 那是他做过最鲁莽的事情了。


近义词：most reckless, most impetuous, most foolhardy


反义词：most cautious, most prudent, most deliberate


词性：形容词
