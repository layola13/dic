checkpoint:/ˈtʃɛkˌpɔɪnt/| n.|检查站，关卡，关键点|A checkpoint is a point at which progress is checked or a point of control.|例句：Soldiers set up a checkpoint on the road.  士兵在路上设立了一个检查站。|例句： Reaching the summit was a major checkpoint in our climb. 达到山顶是我们攀登过程中的一个重要检查点。|近义词：control point, inspection point, milestone|反义词：completion, finish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police set up a checkpoint on the highway. = 警察在高速公路上设立了一个检查站。
This project has several checkpoints to ensure its success. = 这个项目设有多个检查点以确保成功。

一般过去时:
They reached a checkpoint after hours of hiking. = 他们在徒步几小时后到达了一个检查站。
The team passed all the checkpoints in the project. = 团队通过了项目的所有检查点。

现在完成时:
We have passed all the checkpoints so far. = 到目前为止，我们已经通过了所有检查点。
The company has implemented several checkpoints in the production process. = 公司在生产过程中实施了几个检查点。

过去完成时:
They had already passed the checkpoint before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经通过了检查站。
The project had reached a critical checkpoint before the funding was secured. = 在资金到位之前，项目已经到达了一个关键检查点。
