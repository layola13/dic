quasi-exiled:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈɛɡzaɪld/ | adj. |  准流亡的，半流亡的 |  Describing someone who is forced to live outside their home country, but not in a formal or legally defined sense of exile.  This might be due to political pressure, threats, or other circumstances that make it unsafe or impractical to return.  |  He lives a quasi-exiled existence in a neighboring country, constantly looking over his shoulder. 他在一个邻国过着准流亡的生活，总是提心吊胆。  The writer, facing censorship, chose a form of self-imposed quasi-exile.  这位作家面对审查制度，选择了一种自我强加的准流亡。 | exiled, expatriate |  fully integrated, resident |  adj.
