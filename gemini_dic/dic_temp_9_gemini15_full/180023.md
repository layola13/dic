refired:/ˌriːˈfaɪərd/| v.|重新雇用；解雇后重新雇用|To hire someone again after they have been fired.  This often implies a reinstatement after a period of unemployment or disciplinary action.|例句：The company refired Sarah after realizing their mistake. 公司意识到错误后重新雇用了萨拉。|例句：He was refired after a year of unemployment. 他失业一年后被重新雇用。|近义词：rehire, reinstate|反义词：fire, dismiss, let go


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They refire employees who demonstrate improved performance. = 他们重新雇用表现有所改善的员工。
The company refires its best workers after layoff. = 公司在裁员后重新雇用其最好的员工。

一般过去时:
The factory refired many workers after the strike. = 罢工后工厂重新雇用了许多工人。
She was refired last month after a period of suspension. = 她上个月停职一段时间后被重新雇用。

过去完成时:

The board had refired the CEO before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，董事会已经重新雇用了首席执行官。
