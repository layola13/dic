exestuate:/ɪkˈsɛstʃueɪt/| v.|使加剧；使恶化 |To make (something bad) worse or more serious.  |例句：The heavy rain exestuated the already difficult driving conditions. 大雨使本来就艰难的驾驶条件更加恶化。|例句：His angry words exestuated the conflict between them. 他愤怒的话语加剧了他们之间的冲突。|近义词：aggravate, worsen, exacerbate |反义词：alleviate, mitigate, improve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The drought exestuated the food shortage. = 干旱加剧了粮食短缺。
Her anxiety exestuated her health problems. = 她的焦虑加剧了她的健康问题。

一般过去时:
The storm exestuated the flooding in the town. = 暴风雨加剧了城镇的洪水。
His careless actions exestuated the situation. = 他粗心的行为使情况恶化。

现在完成时:
The conflict has been exestuated by recent events. = 最近发生的事件加剧了冲突。
Years of neglect have exestuated the problem. = 多年的忽视加剧了这个问题。

过去完成时:
The previous drought had exestuated the water crisis. = 先前的干旱已经加剧了水危机。
His previous mistakes had exestuated the current difficulty. = 他之前的错误已经加剧了目前的困境。

将来时:
Further cuts in funding will exestuate the problem. = 进一步削减资金将加剧这个问题。
The upcoming heatwave will exestuate the fire risk. = 即将来临的热浪将加剧火灾风险。


虚拟语气:
If he had not interfered, the situation would not have been exestuated. = 如果他不干预，情况就不会恶化。
If we ignore the problem, it will only be exestuated further. = 如果我们忽视这个问题，它只会进一步恶化。

被动语态:
The situation was exestuated by the government's inaction. = 政府的不作为加剧了局势。
The crisis was exestuated by a series of unfortunate events. = 一系列不幸的事件加剧了这场危机。

疑问句:
What factors exestuated the economic downturn? = 哪些因素加剧了经济衰退？
How did his actions exestuate the already tense situation? = 他的行为是如何加剧本来就紧张的局势的？

否定句:
The new policy did not exestuate the problem; it actually helped to alleviate it. = 新政策并没有加剧问题，实际上它有助于缓解问题。
His calm demeanor did not exestuate the situation. = 他冷静的态度并没有加剧局势。
