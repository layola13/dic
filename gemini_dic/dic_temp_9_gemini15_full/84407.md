hallooing: /həˈluːɪŋ/ | v. |大声喊叫；呼喊 | The act of shouting or calling out loudly.  |例句：The hunters were hallooing to each other across the field.  猎人们在田野里互相大声喊叫。

例句：He was hallooing for help after falling into the river. 他掉进河里后大声呼救。


近义词：shouting, yelling, calling out, roaring | 反义词：whispering, murmuring


词源:  Derived from "halloo," an interjection used to call out or attract attention,  ultimately from Old French "halou,"  a hunting cry.

一般过去时:
