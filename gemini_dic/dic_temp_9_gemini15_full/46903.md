/ˈdɑːrkiŋ/| adj.|黑暗的；昏暗的；阴沉的|Darking refers to something that is characterized by a lack of light; gloomy or somber in mood or appearance.  It can describe physical darkness or a metaphorical darkness of mood or spirit.|例句：The room was darking, making it difficult to see.|近义词：dark, shadowy, dusky, dim|反义词：bright, light, luminous
