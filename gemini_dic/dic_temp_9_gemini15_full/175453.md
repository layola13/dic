rammed:/ræmd/| v. |塞满；猛撞；夯实|to push or force (something) into a place violently or forcefully; to hit (something) forcefully.|例句：He rammed the door shut. 他用力关上了门。|例句：The car rammed into a tree. 汽车猛撞到一棵树上。|近义词：cram, stuff, pack, crash|反义词：extract, remove, unload


USAGE->
