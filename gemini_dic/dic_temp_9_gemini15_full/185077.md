reticketed:/ˌriːˈtɪkɪtɪd/| v. |重新签发机票|To issue a new ticket, often replacing an existing one. This usually happens due to changes in flight schedules, cancellations, or passenger requests.  | Example:  The airline reticketed me on a later flight after my original one was cancelled. = 我的原航班取消后，航空公司给我重新签发了另一班较晚的航班的机票。

Example: The passenger service agent reticketed all the affected passengers. = 值机代理为所有受影响的乘客重新签发了机票。|近义词： reissue, rebook | 反义词：cancel
