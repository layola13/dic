radio:/ˈreɪdioʊ/| n.|无线电；收音机|A device that receives and amplifies radio waves and converts them into sound.|例句：I listen to the radio every morning. 我每天早上都听收音机。|例句：The radio is playing my favorite song. 收音机正在播放我最喜欢的歌曲。|近义词：receiver, wireless, broadcast|反义词：television (in some contexts)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I listen to the radio every day. = 我每天都听收音机。
The radio broadcasts news throughout the day. = 收音机全天播放新闻。

一般过去时:
I listened to the radio yesterday. = 我昨天听了收音机。
The radio broadcast an emergency alert last night. = 收音机昨晚播发了紧急警报。

现在完成时:
I have listened to the radio many times. = 我听过很多次收音机了。
The radio station has played that song repeatedly. = 这个电台反复播放那首歌。

过去完成时:
I had listened to the radio before I went to bed. = 我睡觉前听过收音机。
The radio station had already closed when we arrived. = 我们到达时，广播电台已经关闭了。

将来时:
I will listen to the radio tomorrow. = 我明天将听收音机。
The radio will broadcast the game live. = 收音机将现场直播比赛。

虚拟语气:
If I had a radio, I would listen to the news. = 如果我有一台收音机，我会听新闻。
I wish I had known the radio was broken. = 我真希望知道收音机坏了。

被动语态:
The news was broadcast on the radio. = 新闻在收音机上播放了。
The radio is being repaired. = 收音机正在修理中。

疑问句:
Do you listen to the radio often? = 你经常听收音机吗？
What is playing on the radio? = 收音机正在播放什么？

否定句:
I don't listen to the radio very often. = 我不经常听收音机。
The radio wasn't working. = 收音机坏了。
