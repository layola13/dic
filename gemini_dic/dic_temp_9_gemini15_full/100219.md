interrogation:/ˌɪntɛroˈɡeɪʃən/| n.|审问；讯问；疑问|The act of questioning someone formally, especially a suspect in a crime.  It can also refer to the process of asking questions in order to gain information or understanding.|例句：The police conducted a thorough interrogation of the suspect.  警方对嫌疑人进行了彻底的审问。

例句：The interrogation lasted for several hours.  审问持续了几个小时。


近义词：questioning, inquiry, examination, quizzing|反义词：answer, response, explanation


词源:  From the Latin *interrogatio*, from *interrogare* "to ask," from *inter* "between" + *rogare* "to ask".
