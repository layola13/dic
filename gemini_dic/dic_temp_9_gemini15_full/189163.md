sackful: /ˈsækˌful/ | n. | 一袋…；满满一袋 | A sackful is a quantity of something that fills a sack.  It implies a large, but somewhat imprecise, amount. |例句：He carried a sackful of potatoes. 他扛着一袋土豆。  She gave me a sackful of old clothes. 她给了我满满一袋旧衣服。|近义词：bagful, load,  amount |反义词: handful (implies a small amount)
