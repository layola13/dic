secreting:/sɪˈkriːtɪŋ/| v.|分泌；隐藏；泄露|The process of producing and releasing a substance from a cell or gland.  It can also refer to the act of keeping something hidden or the act of revealing a secret. |例句：The glands are secreting hormones.  腺体正在分泌荷尔蒙。

例句：She was secreting information to the enemy. 她正在向敌人泄露情报。


近义词：exuding, emitting, concealing, revealing


反义词：absorbing, retaining


词性:动词
