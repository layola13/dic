octavo:/ˈɒkteɪvoʊ/| n.|八开本；八开书籍|A book size produced by folding a sheet of paper in half three times, resulting in eight leaves (sixteen pages).  It's a common book size, generally smaller than quarto but larger than a duodecimo.|例句：The novel was published in octavo format. 这部小说以八开本的形式出版。|例句：Many classic literature books are printed in octavo. 许多经典文学书籍都是用八开本印刷的。|近义词：book, volume|反义词:folio
