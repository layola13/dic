dividedness: /dɪˈvaɪdədnəs/ | n. | 分裂；不一致；意见分歧 | The state of being divided; a lack of unity or agreement.  It can refer to internal conflict, disagreement within a group, or a separation into parts. |

例句：

The country is suffering from deep political dividedness.  = 这个国家正遭受着严重的政治分裂。

The dividedness among the team members hampered their progress on the project. = 团队成员之间的分歧阻碍了他们的项目进展。


近义词：disunity, fragmentation, discord, schism, dissension

反义词：unity, harmony, concord, agreement, consensus


词源:

Derived from the verb "divide," meaning to separate into parts. The suffix "-edness" is a noun suffix indicating a state or condition.  The word's evolution reflects the increasing complexity of understanding social and political structures where internal conflicts are common.
