reassemblies:/ˌriːəˈsɛmbliz/ | n. |重新组装；重新集合 | The reassemblies of the machine parts took several hours.  机器零件的重新组装花了几个小时。|近义词：reconstructions, reunions |反义词：disassemblies, dispersals

USAGE->

She reassembles her thoughts before speaking. = 她说话前会重新整理思绪。
