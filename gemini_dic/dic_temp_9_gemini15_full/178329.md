recondition:/ˌriːkənˈdɪʃən/| vt.|翻新；修复；使恢复原状|To restore something to good condition after wear or damage; to overhaul and repair.|The mechanic reconditioned the old engine, making it run like new.  技工翻新了旧引擎，使其运转如新。|The company reconditions used cars before selling them. 公司在出售二手车之前对其进行翻新。|restore, renovate, overhaul|destroy, damage, ruin|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They recondition old furniture. = 他们翻新旧家具。
We recondition engines for classic cars. = 我们为老爷车翻新发动机。

一般过去时:
He reconditioned his bicycle last weekend. = 他上周末翻新了他的自行车。
The factory reconditioned all the damaged parts. = 工厂翻新了所有损坏的零件。

现在完成时:
She has reconditioned several antique clocks. = 她已经翻新了几只古董钟表。
They have reconditioned the entire building. = 他们已经翻新了整栋建筑。

过去完成时:
The mechanic had reconditioned the engine before the race. = 技工在比赛前已经翻新了发动机。
They had reconditioned the house before they moved in. = 他们在搬进去之前已经翻新了房子。

将来时:
We will recondition the equipment next month. = 我们下个月将翻新设备。
The company will recondition the old machinery. = 公司将翻新旧机器。

虚拟语气:
