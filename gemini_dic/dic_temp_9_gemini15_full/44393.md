cross-fertilizes: /ˌkrɒsˈfɜːrtɪlaɪzɪz/| vt. |交叉授粉；互相影响；相互借鉴|To fertilize (a plant) with pollen from a different variety or species; to exchange ideas, methods, or influences between different groups or fields.|例句：The two companies cross-fertilized their ideas, resulting in a groundbreaking new product.  两家公司互相借鉴彼此的想法，最终创造出一款具有突破性的新产品。


例句：The different departments cross-fertilize each other's expertise. 不同的部门互相借鉴彼此的专业知识。


近义词：interbreed, hybridize, pollinate, influence, inspire


反义词：isolate, segregate


词性:动词
