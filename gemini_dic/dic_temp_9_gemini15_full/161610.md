poulaine:/puːˈleɪn/| n.|尖头鞋；（中世纪的）长尖头鞋|A poulaine is a type of shoe, popular in medieval Europe, characterized by its extremely long, pointed toe.  The length of the point varied over time and could be quite dramatic, sometimes even requiring the wearer to attach the point to their leg with a chain.|例句：The knight's poulaines were so long they nearly tripped him.  这位骑士的尖头鞋太长了，差点把他绊倒。|近义词：pointed shoe, long-toed shoe|反义词：round-toed shoe
