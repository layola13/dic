ribbon:/ˈrɪbən/| n.|丝带，带子，奖章|A narrow strip of material, typically used for tying or decoration.  It can also refer to a strip of something else, such as a ribbon of land.|例句：She tied a ribbon in her hair. 她把丝带系在头发上。|例句：He won a blue ribbon for his baking. 他因烘焙获得了蓝丝带奖。|近义词：band, strip, sash|反义词：无
