hard-line:/ˈhɑːdlaɪn/| adj.|强硬路线的；顽固的；保守的 |Uncompromising and inflexible in one's opinions or policies.  A hard-line approach often involves taking a firm and unyielding stance, even if it means alienating others.  |例句：The government adopted a hard-line stance on immigration.  政府对移民问题采取了强硬立场。

例句：He's known for his hard-line views on social issues. 他以其在社会问题上的强硬观点而闻名。


近义词： uncompromising, inflexible, rigid, staunch, resolute

反义词：flexible, compromising, lenient, moderate, tolerant

词性：形容词
