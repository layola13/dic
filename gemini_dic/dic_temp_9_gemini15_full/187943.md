roti:/ˈroʊti/| n. |印度薄饼|A flatbread from the Indian subcontinent, typically made from whole wheat flour.  It's cooked on a griddle or tawa and is a staple food in many parts of India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, and other South Asian countries.  Different variations exist, depending on the region and ingredients.|例句：I had roti and curry for dinner.  我晚餐吃了印度薄饼和咖喱。|例句：The roti was perfectly cooked and slightly crispy. 这个印度薄饼烤得恰到好处，略带脆感。|近义词：chapati, paratha, flatbread |反义词：无
