gravies:/ˈɡreɪviːz/| n. |肉汁；浓汁 |Thick sauce made from the juices of cooked meat, often thickened with flour or cornstarch.  Gravies are typically served over meat, potatoes, or other dishes. |例句：The roast chicken was delicious, especially with the rich brown gravy.  烤鸡很好吃，尤其是配上浓郁的棕色肉汁。

例句：She made a thick gravy from the leftover turkey drippings. 她用剩下的火鸡油做了一大碗浓稠的肉汁。


近义词：sauce, jus|反义词：dryness
