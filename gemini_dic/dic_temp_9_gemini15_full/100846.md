intros:/ˈɪntroʊz/| n. |介绍，引言，序言 | Introductions, often a brief overview or preliminary remarks before a main presentation, performance, or piece of writing.  |Example: The intros to the songs on this album are all quite catchy. 这张专辑歌曲的开头都非常动听。|近义词：introductions, prefaces, preludes, overtures |反义词：conclusions, endings

The band plays a short intro before each song. 乐队在每首歌之前演奏一段简短的引言。
This book's intro explains the author's motivations. 这本书的引言解释了作者的动机。

一般过去时:
The speaker gave a compelling intro to his lecture. 演讲者对他的演讲作了一个引人入胜的介绍。

过去完成时:

将来时:

疑问句:
