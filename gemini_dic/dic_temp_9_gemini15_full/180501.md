regenerating: /ˌriːˈdʒɛnəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. adj. | 再生的；使再生；复兴 |  The process of renewing or reviving something.  It can refer to biological processes (like cell regeneration) or to the renewal of a system, community, or idea. |


例句：

The forest is regenerating after the fire. = 森林在大火之后正在再生。

The city is regenerating its downtown area. = 这个城市正在重建其市中心地区。


近义词：reviving, renewing, revitalizing, repairing, rebuilding

反义词：degenerating, decaying, destroying


词性：动词（现在分词）和形容词


USAGE->

The body is constantly regenerating cells. = 身体不断地再生细胞。

This plant is regenerating quickly after being cut. = 这种植物被砍伐后再生很快。

过去完成时:
