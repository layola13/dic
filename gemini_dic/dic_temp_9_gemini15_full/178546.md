reconvoke:/ˌriːˈkɒnvoʊk/ | vt. |重新召集，再次召开 | To call together again; to convene again.  |例句：The committee was reconvoked to discuss the new evidence. 委员会重新召集讨论新的证据。|例句：The court was reconvoked to hear the appeal. 法院重新开庭审理上诉。|近义词：reconvene, resummon |反义词：adjourn, dismiss |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee reconvenes next week. = 委员会下周重新召开。
They reconvoke the meeting every month. = 他们每月重新召开会议。

一般过去时:
The meeting was reconvoked after a short break. = 会议短暂休息后重新召开。
The judge reconvoked the court to deliver the verdict. = 法官重新开庭宣判。

现在完成时:
They have reconvoked the assembly several times this year. = 今年他们已经几次重新召开了集会。
The board has reconvoked to discuss the budget. = 董事会已重新召开会议讨论预算。

过去完成时:
They had reconvoked the meeting before the new information was revealed. = 在新信息披露之前，他们已经重新召开了会议。
The council had reconvoked before the final decision was made. = 在最终决定做出之前，理事会已经重新召开了会议。

将来时:
The president will reconvoke the parliament next month. = 总统下个月将重新召集议会。
We will reconvoke the conference if necessary. =如有必要，我们将重新召开会议。

虚拟语气:
If the evidence were stronger, they would reconvoke the trial. = 如果证据更充分，他们就会重新开庭审判。
I wish they would reconvoke the committee to address these issues. = 我希望他们能重新召集委员会来解决这些问题。

被动语态:
The meeting was reconvoked by the chairman. = 会议由主席重新召集。
The conference will be reconvoked next week. = 会议将于下周重新召开。

疑问句:
Will the committee be reconvoked? = 委员会还会重新召开吗？
When will the meeting be reconvoked? = 会议什么时候重新召开？

否定句:
The committee did not reconvoke after the initial adjournment. = 委员会初次休会后没有重新召开。
They will not reconvoke the meeting without further notice. = 没有进一步通知，他们不会重新召开会议。
