collaborated:/kəˈlæbəreɪtɪd/| v. |合作，协作|To work jointly on an activity or project.|The two companies collaborated on the new product development.  两家公司合作开发新产品。|The scientists collaborated on a research paper.  科学家们合作撰写了一篇研究论文。| cooperated, teamed up | competed, worked independently | 动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
They collaborate on many projects. = 他们合作许多项目。
We collaborate closely with our partners. = 我们与合作伙伴紧密合作。

一般过去时:
The artists collaborated on a mural. = 这些艺术家合作创作了一幅壁画。
They collaborated on a new business plan. = 他们合作制定了一个新的商业计划。

现在完成时:
We have collaborated on several successful campaigns. = 我们已经合作开展了几次成功的活动。
They have collaborated extensively in the past. = 他们过去有过广泛的合作。

过去完成时:
They had collaborated before on similar projects. = 他们之前曾在类似的项目上合作过。
She had collaborated with him on several occasions. = 她曾多次与他合作。

将来时:
We will collaborate on a new book next year. = 我们明年将合作出版一本新书。
They will collaborate on a joint venture. = 他们将合作进行合资企业。

虚拟语气:
If they collaborated, the project would be finished sooner. = 如果他们合作，项目就能更快完成。
I wish they had collaborated more effectively. = 我希望他们合作更有效率。

被动语态:
The project was collaborated on by three different teams. = 该项目由三个不同的团队合作完成。
The research was collaborated on by leading scientists. = 这项研究是由顶尖科学家合作完成的。

疑问句:
Did they collaborate on the report? = 他们合作写报告了吗？
Have you ever collaborated with someone from a different culture? = 你有没有和不同文化背景的人合作过？

否定句:
They did not collaborate on the project. = 他们没有参与该项目的合作。
We have not collaborated with that company before. = 我们以前没有和那家公司合作过。
