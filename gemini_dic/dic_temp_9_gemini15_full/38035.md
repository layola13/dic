company: /ˈkʌmpəni/ | n. | 公司，同伴，陪伴 | A business organization; a group of people; the state of being with someone. | Examples:  He works for a large technology company. 他为一家大型科技公司工作。 I enjoyed the company of my friends. 我喜欢和朋友们在一起。|近义词：firm, business, association, companionship |反义词：solitude, isolation
