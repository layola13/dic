eked-out:/ɛktˈaʊt/| v. |勉强维持，节省度日，艰难地过活 |to make (something, especially money or supplies) last longer by using or spending it carefully or sparingly.  It implies a struggle to make ends meet or a situation of scarcity. |They eked out a living on a small farm. 他们在小农场上勉强维持生计。|They eked out a meager existence in the slums.他们在贫民窟里艰难地生存。|近义词：scrape by, make do, get by, struggle along|反义词：survive comfortably, thrive, prosper|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They eke out a living from their small garden. = 他们靠自家的小菜园勉强维持生计。
We eke out our resources by reducing unnecessary expenses. = 我们通过减少不必要的开支来节省资源。


一般过去时:
The family eked out a living on the farm during the drought. = 干旱期间，这家人在农场上勉强维持生计。
She eked out the remaining food for the week. = 她把剩下的食物省着用了一周。


现在完成时:
They have eked out a comfortable life despite many challenges. = 尽管面临诸多挑战，他们还是勉强过上了舒适的生活。(注意：此处"eked out"略带反讽意味，表示虽然舒适，但仍然是经过努力得来的)
We have eked out our savings for our children's education. = 我们省吃俭用攒钱供孩子们上学。


过去完成时:
They had eked out a living for years before they finally got a better job. = 在他们最终找到一份更好的工作之前，他们已经勉强维持生计多年了。
Before the inheritance, they had eked out a meagre existence. = 在继承遗产之前，他们过着贫困的生活。


将来时:
We will have to eke out our supplies until the next shipment arrives. = 我们必须省着用现有的物资，直到下一批货物到达。
They will eke out a living in the city, facing countless hardships. = 他们在城市里艰难度日，面临着无数的困难。


虚拟语气:
If they hadn't eked out their resources, they would have gone bankrupt. = 如果他们不节省资源，他们早就破产了。
I wish we hadn't had to eke out our income last year. = 我真希望我们去年不用那么省吃俭用了。


被动语态:
Their meager savings were eked out carefully. = 他们微薄的积蓄被仔细地省着用。
A living was eked out by the family through hard work. = 这家人靠辛勤劳动勉强维持生计。(被动语态更强调动作本身)


疑问句:
How did they eke out a living in such difficult times? = 他们在那样艰难的时期是如何勉强维持生计的？
How can we eke out these limited resources? = 我们该如何省着用这些有限的资源？


否定句:
They didn't need to eke out their resources; they were quite wealthy. = 他们不需要节省资源；他们非常富有。
We didn't eke out our budget this month; we even had some left over. = 这个月我们没有节省开支，甚至还有一些剩余。
