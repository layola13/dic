reverting:/rɪˈvɜːrtɪŋ/ | v. |恢复原状，回复，退回|To return to a previous state, condition, or practice.  It often implies a return to an earlier or less developed state.|例句：The system reverted to its default settings after the power outage. 断电后系统恢复到默认设置。

例句：After the initial excitement, he reverted to his usual quiet self.  最初的兴奋过后，他又恢复了他往日的平静。


近义词：return, revert back, relapse, regress | 反义词：progress, advance, develop


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system reverts to its default settings automatically. = 系统会自动恢复到默认设置。
He reverts to his old habits whenever he's stressed. = 每当他压力大的时候，他就会故态复萌。


一般过去时:
The company reverted to its original business plan. = 公司恢复了最初的商业计划。
She reverted to her childhood memories. = 她回忆起了童年的往事。


现在完成时:
The patient has reverted to his old bad habits. = 病人又恢复了他以前的不良习惯。
They have reverted to using older technology. = 他们又开始使用旧技术了。


过去完成时:
The country had reverted to a dictatorship after the coup. = 政变之后，这个国家又变成了独裁统治。
Before the changes, the company had reverted to its previous strategy. = 在改革之前，公司已经恢复了之前的策略。


将来时:
The software will revert to the previous version if the update fails. = 如果更新失败，软件将恢复到以前的版本。
He believes the situation will revert to normal soon. = 他相信情况很快就会恢复正常。


虚拟语气:
If he were to revert to his old ways, I would be disappointed. = 如果他故态复萌，我会很失望。
I wish the situation would revert to its former glory. = 我希望情况能恢复到以前的辉煌。


被动语态:
The decision was reverted by the higher management. = 该决定被高层管理人员撤销了。 (Note:  "revert" is less commonly used in the passive voice;  a more natural phrasing might be "The decision was reversed.")
The settings were reverted to their defaults. = 设置已恢复为默认值。


疑问句:
Will the system revert to its previous state? = 系统会恢复到之前的状态吗？
Did the patient revert to his old symptoms? = 病人是否恢复了旧症状？


否定句:
The government refused to revert its decision. = 政府拒绝改变其决定。
He didn't revert to his old bad habits after his treatment. = 他治疗后没有恢复旧的不良习惯。
