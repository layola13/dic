incondensible: /ˌɪnkənˈdɛnsəbəl/ | adj. | 不能冷凝的；不易冷凝的 |  Unable to be condensed; not easily liquefied by cooling.  The term often refers to gases that resist condensation under normal conditions. | Example: Helium is an incondensible gas at room temperature. 氦气在室温下是不能冷凝的气体。 | 近义词：non-condensable, uncondensed | 反义词：condensable
