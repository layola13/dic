lushed:/lʌʃt/| adj.|茂盛的；繁茂的；浓密的；醉醺醺的| Lush describes something that is full, rich, and abundant, often in a visually appealing way. It can refer to vegetation, colors, or even a feeling of intense pleasure or intoxication.  |例句：The garden was lush with vibrant flowers.  花园里鲜花盛开，生机勃勃。 The lush green valley was a sight to behold.  翠绿的山谷令人叹为观止。|近义词：luxuriant, abundant, opulent, intoxicated|反义词：barren, sparse, meager, sober|
