papism:/ˈpeɪpɪzəm/| n.|天主教；教皇制度|The belief in and adherence to the doctrines and practices of the Pope and the Roman Catholic Church.  It often carries a negative connotation, implying excessive or blind adherence to papal authority and possibly corruption or abuse of power within the church.|He accused the church of papism and tyranny. 他指责教会实行天主教制度和暴政。|Roman Catholicism, Catholicism|Protestantism, secularism|名词
