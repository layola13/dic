prefreeze: /ˈpriːfriːz/ | v. | 预先冷冻；提前冷冻 | To freeze something before it is used or processed.  This typically involves freezing food items to preserve them for later use or to facilitate processing. | 例句：We prefreeze the berries before making jam. = 我们在做果酱之前先冷冻浆果。  The company prefreezes the vegetables to maintain freshness. = 公司预先冷冻蔬菜以保持新鲜度。 | 近义词：freeze beforehand, pre-chill | 反义词：thaw, defrost | 动词
