privation:/praɪˈveɪʃən/| n.|匮乏；缺乏；贫困|The state of lacking or missing something essential, especially the necessities of life.  It often implies a significant hardship or deprivation.|例句：The refugees suffered terrible privation during their long journey. 难民在漫长的旅程中遭受了可怕的匮乏。

例句：Years of privation had left her weak and vulnerable. 多年的匮乏使她虚弱不堪。


近义词：deprivation, want, hardship, scarcity, poverty|反义词：abundance, plenty, affluence
