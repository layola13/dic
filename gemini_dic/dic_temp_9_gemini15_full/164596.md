preexcusing: /ˌpriːɪkˈskjuːzɪŋ/ | v. | 预先道歉；事先请求原谅 |  Preexcusing is the act of apologizing or seeking forgiveness in advance for something one anticipates doing or has already done. It often implies a slight anticipation of offense or inconvenience. | 例句：He preexcused himself for any inconvenience his late arrival might cause. 他事先为他的迟到可能造成的任何不便表示歉意。


He preexcused his absence from the meeting, citing a prior engagement. 他预先为自己缺席会议做了解释，理由是之前有约。


近义词：preapologizing, anticipating forgiveness | 反义词：无直接反义词，但可以理解为与“承担责任”，“勇于面对后果”等相对。| 动词
