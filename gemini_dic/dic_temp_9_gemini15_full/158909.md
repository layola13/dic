plunk:/plʌŋk/| vt. n.|咚地一声放下；沉重地放下；|to drop or put down something heavily or without care|He plunked the suitcase on the floor. 他把行李箱重重地扔在地上。|近义词：drop, thud, dump|反义词：place carefully, gently put


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plunks his guitar down on the table. = 他把吉他重重地放在桌子上。
She plunks the coins into the jar. = 她把硬币咚地一声扔进罐子里。

一般过去时:
He plunked the money down on the counter. = 他把钱重重地拍在柜台上。
She plunked herself down on the sofa. = 她重重地坐到沙发上。

现在完成时:
He has plunked all his savings into the stock market. = 他已将他所有的积蓄都投入股市。
She has plunked down a hefty deposit on the house. = 她已经为房子付了一大笔定金。

过去完成时:
He had plunked the letter onto the desk before leaving. = 他离开前已把信重重地扔在桌子上。
She had plunked herself down before realizing it was wet. = 她坐下去后才意识到椅子是湿的。

将来时:
He will plunk the bag on the floor when he arrives. = 他到达时会把包扔在地上。
She will plunk the keys on the table and go to bed. = 她会把钥匙扔在桌子上然后去睡觉。

虚拟语气:
If I had a million dollars, I would plunk it on a yacht. = 如果我有百万美元，我会把它花在一艘游艇上。
I wish I could plunk my worries away. = 我希望我能摆脱我的烦恼。

被动语态:
The book was plunked onto the shelf carelessly. = 书被粗心地扔到书架上。
The bag was plunked down by the annoyed traveler. = 生气的旅客把包重重地扔在地上。

疑问句:
Did he plunk the glass on the table? = 他是不是把玻璃杯重重地放在桌子上？
Where did she plunk the remote control? = 她把遥控器扔到哪里了？

否定句:
He didn't plunk the vase down; he placed it gently. = 他没有把花瓶重重地放下；他是轻轻地放的。
She didn't plunk the baby in the crib; she carefully laid him down. = 她没有把婴儿扔进婴儿床；她小心翼翼地把他放下。
