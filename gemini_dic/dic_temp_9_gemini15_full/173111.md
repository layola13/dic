quasi-absolute:/ˈkwɑːziˌæbsəluːt/| adj.| 准绝对的；近乎绝对的 |  Approaching or resembling an absolute; almost but not entirely absolute.  It implies a degree of incompleteness or qualification to the absolute nature. |例句：The company enjoys a quasi-absolute monopoly in the market. 这家公司在这个市场上享有准绝对垄断地位。


例句：His power in the village was quasi-absolute. 他在村里的权力几乎是绝对的。


近义词：near-absolute, virtually absolute, almost absolute


反义词：relative, conditional, limited


词性：形容词
