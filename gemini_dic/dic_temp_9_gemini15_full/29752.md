catosaurus:/ˌkætəˈsɔːrəs/| n. |卡塔龙（一种恐龙）|Catosaurus is an extinct genus of sauropodomorph dinosaur from the Early Jurassic Period (Hettangian to Sinemurian stages) of North America.  It is characterized by its relatively small size compared to other sauropods and its unique features of the skull and vertebrae.|The fossils of Catosaurus were found in the Kayenta Formation of Arizona.  |近义词：暂无  |反义词：暂无
