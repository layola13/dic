roundheaded:/ˈraʊndˌhedɪd/| adj.|圆头的；傻气的|Having a round head;  sometimes used informally to describe someone as foolish or simple.  |例句：The roundheaded pins were difficult to insert into the small holes.  圆头钉很难插入小孔中。

例句：He's a bit roundheaded, always falling for the same tricks. 他有点傻，总是上同样的当。


近义词：round-topped (for objects),  dull-witted (for people)


反义词：sharp-witted (for people), pointed (for objects)

词性:形容词
