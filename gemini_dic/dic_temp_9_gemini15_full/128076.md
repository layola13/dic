nervine:/ˈnɜːrvaɪn/| n. adj.|神经镇静剂；神经的|A nervine is a substance that soothes or calms the nerves.  It can be a medication or a herbal remedy. As an adjective, it describes something relating to or affecting the nerves.|例句：The doctor prescribed a nervine to help her sleep. 医生开了镇静剂帮助她睡眠。  Lavender is often considered a nervine. 薰衣草通常被认为具有镇静神经的作用。|近义词：sedative, tranquilizer, calmative|反义词：stimulant|名词，形容词
