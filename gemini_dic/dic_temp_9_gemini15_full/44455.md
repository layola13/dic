cross-tongue:/ˌkrɒsˈtʌŋ/| adj.| 跨语言的，语言交叉的 |  Relating to or involving the interaction or mixing of two or more languages.  This term often refers to code-switching, borrowing, or interference between languages in speech or writing. | Example: The cross-tongue influence of English on Mandarin is evident in the increasing use of loanwords. 跨语言的英语对普通话的影响在越来越多的外来词的使用中显而易见。|近义词：multilingual, interlingual |反义词：monolingual
