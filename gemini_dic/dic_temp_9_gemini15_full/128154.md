nettle:/ˈnetl/| n. v.|荨麻；激怒|A nettle is a plant with stinging hairs.  The verb means to irritate or annoy.|例句：The nettle stung my arm. 荨麻蛰了我的胳膊。|例句：His constant criticism nettled her. 他不断的批评激怒了她。|近义词：irritate, annoy, vex, provoke|反义词：soothe, calm, please|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The nettles sting my skin. = 荨麻刺痛我的皮肤。
He nettles me with his constant complaints. = 他不断的抱怨让我很烦。


一般过去时:
The nettle stung my hand. = 荨麻蛰了我的手。
His rude remarks nettled me yesterday. = 他昨天的粗鲁话语激怒了我。


现在完成时:
I have been nettled by his behaviour. = 我被他行为激怒了。
The stinging nettles have irritated my leg. = 这些蜇人的荨麻已经刺激到我的腿了。


过去完成时:
I had been nettled by his earlier comments. = 我被他之前的评论激怒了。
She had already been nettled by his repeated insults. = 她已经被他多次的侮辱激怒了。


将来时:
That remark will nettle him. = 那句话会激怒他。
The children will probably nettle their mother with their constant noise. = 孩子们可能会因为他们不断的吵闹而激怒他们的母亲。


虚拟语气:
If I touched that plant, it would nettle me. = 如果我碰到那棵植物，它会蜇我。
I wish he wouldn't nettle me with his sarcasm. = 我希望他不要用他的讽刺来激怒我。


被动语态:
He was nettled by her indifference. = 他被她的冷漠激怒了。
My skin was nettled by the plant. = 我的皮肤被植物蛰了。


疑问句:
Does that plant nettle you? = 那棵植物会蜇你吗？
What nettles you so much? = 什么事让你这么生气？


否定句:
That didn't nettle him at all. = 那根本没有激怒他。
The comment didn't nettle her as much as she expected. = 这评论并没有像她预期的那样激怒她。
