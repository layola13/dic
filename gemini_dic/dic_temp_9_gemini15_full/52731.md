dike:/daɪk/| n.|堤坝，防水坝|A dike is an artificial wall or embankment built to prevent the flooding of low-lying areas, especially by the sea.|The Dutch built many dikes to protect their land from the sea.  荷兰人建造了许多堤坝来保护他们的土地免受海水侵袭。|dam, embankment, levee|valley, ravine
