enwind:/ɪnˈwaɪnd/| vt.|缠绕；盘绕；使卷曲|To wind or coil something around something else.  The act of winding or coiling something around something else.|例句：She enwound the ribbon around the gift. 她把丝带缠绕在礼物上。
例句：The ivy enwound the ancient oak.  常春藤缠绕着古老的橡树。|近义词：wrap, coil, twine|反义词：unwrap, unwind
