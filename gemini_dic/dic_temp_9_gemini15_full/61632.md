emigre:/ˈeɪmɪɡreɪ/| n.| 移民，流亡者 |A person who has left their own country in order to settle in another, especially for political reasons.  |例句：Many émigrés fled their home countries during the war. 许多移民在战争期间逃离了他们的祖国。|近义词：exile, refugee, expatriate |反义词：immigrant (in the context of returning home), native


例句：The émigrés formed a close-knit community in their new home. 流亡者在他们的新家形成了一个紧密的社区。

USAGE->
一般现在时:
Many émigrés seek political asylum.  许多移民寻求政治庇护。
He is an émigré from Cuba. 他是来自古巴的移民。

一般过去时:
Thousands of émigrés left the country after the coup. 政变之后，成千上万的移民离开了这个国家。
She was an émigré who had lived in France for many years. 她是一位在法国生活多年的移民。

现在完成时:
They have been émigrés for over a decade. 他们已经流亡十多年了。
She has successfully integrated into the émigré community. 她成功地融入了移民社区。


过去完成时:
Before the revolution, he had already been an émigré. 在革命之前，他已经成为了一名移民。
The family had been émigrés for generations. 这个家庭几代都是移民。


将来时:
More émigrés will likely seek refuge in neighboring countries. 更多的移民可能会在邻国寻求庇护。
He will become an émigré next year. 他明年将成为一名移民。


虚拟语气:
If I were an émigré, I would miss my homeland. 如果我是个移民，我会想念我的祖国。
I wish I had not been an émigré during my youth. 我真希望我年轻的时候不是个移民。


被动语态:
Many émigrés are often overlooked by society. 许多移民经常被社会忽视。
The émigré community was greatly affected by the new policy. 移民社区受到了新政策的极大影响。


疑问句:
Are you an émigré? 你是移民吗？
Were they émigrés from Russia? 他们是来自俄罗斯的移民吗？


否定句:
He is not an émigré; he's a tourist. 他不是移民；他是个游客。
