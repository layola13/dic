re-interview:/ˌriːˈɪntəvjuː/ | v. | 再面试，重新面试 | To interview someone again, often after an initial interview.  This might be due to needing further information, reconsidering a previous decision, or to assess changes in the candidate's situation. | Example:  They re-interviewed all the shortlisted candidates. 他们再次面试了所有入围的候选人。  The company decided to re-interview me after receiving positive feedback from my references. 公司在收到我的推荐人的正面评价后，决定再次面试我。| 近义词：re-examine, reassess | 反义词：initial interview, first interview | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-interview candidates regularly. = 他们定期对候选人进行复试。
The manager re-interviews applicants who are borderline. = 经理会再次面试那些处于及格线上的申请者。
