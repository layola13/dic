itemize:/ˈaɪtəmˌaɪz/ | vt. |逐条列举，列出要点|To mention or deal with (things) individually, one by one.  |The report itemized the company's assets.这份报告逐条列举了公司的资产。| enumerate, list, detail |  combine, aggregate | verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He itemizes his expenses each month. = 他每月都逐条列出他的开支。
She itemizes the ingredients in her recipe. = 她列出了食谱中的配料。

一般过去时:
The teacher itemized the key points on the board. = 老师在黑板上逐条列出了要点。
They itemized all the damages they suffered. = 他们列出了他们遭受的所有损失。

现在完成时:
She has itemized all the gifts she received. = 她已经列出了她收到的所有礼物。
We have itemized the problems and potential solutions. = 我们已经列出了问题和可能的解决方案。

过去完成时:
He had itemized his expenses before submitting the report. = 他在提交报告之前已经列出了他的开支。
They had itemized the evidence before presenting their case. = 他们在陈述案情之前已经列出了证据。

将来时:
I will itemize the tasks for the project tomorrow. = 我明天将列出该项目的任务。
She will itemize her purchases before paying. = 她将在付款前列出她的购买清单。

虚拟语气:
If I were to itemize my concerns, it would be a long list. = 如果我要列出我的担忧，那将是一长串清单。
I wish I had itemized my expenses more carefully. = 我希望我当时更仔细地列出我的开支。

被动语态:
The items were itemized in the invoice. = 发票上逐条列出了这些项目。
The details of the contract will be itemized in the appendix. = 合同细节将在附录中逐条列出。

疑问句:
Did you itemize the costs involved in the project? = 你是否列出了项目中涉及的成本？
Have you itemized all the necessary equipment? = 你是否列出了所有必要的设备？

否定句:
He didn't itemize his expenses, so it was difficult to track them. = 他没有列出他的开支，所以很难追踪它们。
She didn't itemize the ingredients, making the recipe hard to follow. = 她没有列出配料，使得食谱难以遵循。
