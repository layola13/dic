/hwɑːt/|pron.|什么，什么东西|What refers to something unknown or unspecified.  It is used to ask for information, to express uncertainty, or to introduce a statement of something. |What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？|which, which thing|nothing, something


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？


一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you been doing? = 你一直在做什么？
What has she accomplished this year? = 她今年取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you eaten before you got sick? = 你生病之前吃了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们讨论了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like? = 天气将会怎样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done to improve the situation? = 正在采取什么措施来改善现状？


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道该说什么。
I don't understand what you mean. = 我不明白你的意思。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What about...? = ……怎么样？
What if...? = 如果……将会怎样？
What for? = 为什么？为了什么？
No matter what = 无论如何
