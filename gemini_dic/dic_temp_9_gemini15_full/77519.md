gazumping:/ɡəˈzʌmpɪŋ/ | n. | 抬价抢购；出价更高买走（已达成协议的房产） |  The act of making a higher offer for a property that is already under offer to another buyer.  This practice is often considered unethical and frustrating for the original buyer. |  The seller gazumped the first buyer by accepting a higher offer from someone else.  卖家接受了另一位买家更高的报价，从而抢走了第一位买家的房子。

| 近义词：undercutting, overbidding | 反义词：underselling |  词性：名词
