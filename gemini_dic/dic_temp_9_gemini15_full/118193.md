mentors: /ˈmɛntɔrz/ | n. |导师，指导者 | A mentor is an experienced and trusted advisor who guides and supports a less experienced person.  Mentors provide guidance, advice, and encouragement to help individuals develop their skills and achieve their goals. They often share their knowledge and experience, offering insights and perspectives that can help mentees navigate challenges and make informed decisions. | Example:  She found a mentor who helped her navigate the complexities of starting her own business. 她找到一位导师，帮助她克服创业的复杂性。  He served as a mentor to many young professionals throughout his career. 他在职业生涯中担任过许多年轻专业人士的导师。 | 近义词：advisor, coach, guide, tutor, sponsor | 反义词：opponent, adversary
