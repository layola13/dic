outdelivered:/ˌaʊtdiˈlɪvərd/| vt. |超过；胜过；交付超出预期|  To surpass or exceed in performance or delivery; to deliver more than expected.|

例句：
The company outdelivered on its promises, exceeding sales targets by 20%.  = 公司兑现了其承诺，销售目标超出20%。

The team outdelivered their previous best performance, setting a new record. = 团队的表现超过了他们之前的最佳成绩，创造了新的纪录。


近义词：surpassed, exceeded, outperformed, surpassed expectations

反义词：underperformed, failed to deliver, fell short


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company outdelivers its competitors in customer service. = 公司在客户服务方面胜过竞争对手。
He consistently outdelivers his colleagues in terms of productivity. = 就生产力而言，他始终胜过他的同事。

一般过去时:
Our team outdelivered expectations on the project. = 我们的团队在项目上的表现超出了预期。
She outdelivered her opponent in the debate. = 她在辩论中胜过了她的对手。

现在完成时:
The marketing campaign has outdelivered its projected ROI. = 营销活动已超出其预期的投资回报率。
He has consistently outdelivered on his promises. = 他一直信守承诺，甚至超出预期。

过去完成时:
The company had outdelivered its previous year's performance before the market downturn. = 在市场低迷之前，该公司已经超过了上一年的业绩。
They had outdelivered their rivals in the race for market share. = 他们在争夺市场份额的竞争中已经胜过了竞争对手。

将来时:
We believe this new strategy will outdeliver the previous one. = 我们相信这项新战略将胜过之前的战略。
The new product is expected to outdeliver the competition in terms of features. = 预计新产品在功能方面将胜过竞争对手。

虚拟语气:
If we had invested more, we might have outdelivered the competition. = 如果我们投入更多，我们可能会胜过竞争对手。
I wish we had outdelivered our targets last quarter. = 我希望我们上个季度能够超出目标。

被动语态:
The target was outdelivered by a significant margin. = 目标被大幅度地超过了。
Their promises were outdelivered by a considerable amount. = 他们的承诺被大大地超过了。

疑问句:
Did the team outdeliver their goals? = 团队是否完成了他们的目标？
Will the new technology outdeliver the current system? = 新技术能否胜过现有系统？

否定句:
The project did not outdeliver its initial budget. = 项目没有超出最初的预算。
The company failed to outdeliver its competitors in innovation. = 公司未能胜过竞争对手的创新能力。
