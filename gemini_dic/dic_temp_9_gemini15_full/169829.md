prosecute:/ˈprɒsɪkjuːt/ /ˈprɑːsɪkjuːt/ | vt. | 起诉；检举；从事；继续 | To institute legal proceedings against (a person or organization).  To carry on or continue (a course of action).|例句：The police decided to prosecute him for drunk driving. =警方决定以酒后驾车罪起诉他。

The company will prosecute its plans to expand into new markets. = 公司将继续其进军新市场的计划。|近义词：sue, indict, pursue, continue | 反义词：acquit, dismiss


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government prosecutes criminals. = 政府起诉罪犯。
She prosecutes her research diligently. = 她勤奋地进行她的研究。

一般过去时:
They prosecuted the case vigorously. = 他们积极地进行了诉讼。
He prosecuted his studies with great enthusiasm. = 他以极大的热情继续他的学业。

现在完成时:
The authorities have prosecuted several individuals for fraud. = 当局已经起诉了几名涉嫌欺诈的个人。
She has prosecuted her campaign tirelessly. = 她不知疲倦地开展了她的竞选活动。

过去完成时:
The police had prosecuted many cases before this one. = 在这起案件之前，警方已经办理过许多案件。
They had prosecuted their plans to build a new factory. = 他们已经完成了建造新工厂的计划。

将来时:
The prosecutor will prosecute the defendant next week. = 检察官下周将起诉被告。
The company will prosecute its expansion into Asia. = 公司将继续其在亚洲的扩张。

虚拟语气:
If they had more evidence, they would prosecute. = 如果他们有更多证据，他们就会起诉。
If I were you, I would prosecute the matter further. = 如果我是你，我会进一步追究此事。

被动语态:
The case was prosecuted effectively. = 此案得到了有效的起诉。
The expansion plans are being prosecuted with determination. = 扩张计划正在坚定地执行。

疑问句:
Will the police prosecute him? = 警方会起诉他吗？
Are they prosecuting the case properly? = 他们正在正确地进行诉讼吗？

否定句:
The police did not prosecute the case due to lack of evidence. = 由于缺乏证据，警方没有起诉。
They will not prosecute their claim further. = 他们不会进一步追究他们的索赔。



PHRASE->

prosecute a war = 继续战争
prosecute a business = 经营生意
