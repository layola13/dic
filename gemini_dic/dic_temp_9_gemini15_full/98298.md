insidiate: /ɪnˈsɪdieɪt/ | vt. | 暗中破坏；暗算；使陷害 | To undermine or weaken secretly or gradually; to plot against; to betray. |

例句：

The enemy attempted to insidiate the peace talks. = 敌人试图暗中破坏和平谈判。

They insidiated their rival by spreading false rumors. = 他们通过散布谣言来暗算他们的竞争对手。


近义词：sabotage, undermine, subvert, betray, plot against

反义词：support, aid, assist, bolster


词源：

源自拉丁语 *insidiare，意思是“to lie in wait; to ambush”。  其前缀 *in- 表示“in, into, on, upon”, 而 *sidiare 源自 *sedere,  意思是“to sit”。  因此，其本义是“to sit in wait”，引申为暗中破坏或陷害。
