nonconscious:/ˌnɒnˈkɒnʃəs/| adj.|无意识的，下意识的|Not conscious; occurring without conscious awareness or control.|Many bodily functions, such as breathing and digestion, are nonconscious. 许多身体机能，例如呼吸和消化，都是无意识的。|conscious, subconscious|conscious, aware|


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like "what" does.  Instead, we'll show how it's used in different sentence structures.

肯定句:
He had a nonconscious reaction to the loud noise. 他对巨大的噪音做出了下意识的反应。
Her nonconscious biases influenced her decision.  她的潜意识偏见影响了她的决定。


否定句:
The action was not nonconscious; it was deliberate.  这个行为并非无意识的；它是故意的。
It wasn't a nonconscious slip of the tongue; she meant what she said. 这不是无意识的口误；她就是那个意思。


疑问句:
Was his response nonconscious or intentional? 他的反应是无意识的还是故意的？
Could the error have been nonconscious?  这个错误可能是无意识的吗？
