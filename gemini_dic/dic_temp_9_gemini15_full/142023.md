organizers:/ˈɔːrɡənaɪzərz/ | n. |组织者，主办者 |People or groups responsible for planning and managing an event, activity, or organization.  |The organizers of the marathon did a fantastic job.  马拉松赛的组织者做得非常出色。| planners, coordinators, directors |disorganizers, participants (in a less leading role) |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The organizers plan the event carefully. = 组织者仔细地策划活动。
The organizers manage the logistics effectively. = 组织者有效地管理后勤工作。

一般过去时:
The organizers planned a successful conference last year. = 组织者去年策划了一个成功的会议。

现在完成时:
The organizers have secured all the necessary permits. = 组织者已经获得了所有必要的许可证。
The organizers have finalized the schedule. = 组织者已经敲定了日程安排。

将来时:
The organizers will announce the winners tomorrow. = 组织者明天将宣布获胜者。
The organizers will provide all participants with necessary information. = 组织者将向所有参与者提供必要的信息。
