/ˌhaɪpəʊˈkɒndrɪəs/ /ˌhaɪpəʊˈkɑːndrɪəs/ | n. | 疑病症；臆想病 | Hypochondria is a psychological disorder characterized by excessive preoccupation with health and persistent fear of having a serious illness, despite medical reassurance to the contrary.  People with hypochondria often misinterpret normal bodily sensations as signs of a serious disease. | He suffers from hypochondria and constantly worries about his health. 他患有疑病症，不断担心自己的健康。| anxiety disorder, health anxiety |  (无明确反义词，可考虑：health, well-being) | 名词
