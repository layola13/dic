overreprimand:/ˌoʊvərˈrɛprɪmænd/| vt.|过度训斥，严厉责备|To reprimand excessively or unjustly.  It implies a rebuke that is too harsh or prolonged for the offense.|例句：The teacher overreprimanded the student for a minor infraction.  老师因为一件小错而过度训斥了学生。


例句：He felt he had been overreprimanded for a simple mistake. 他觉得因为一个简单的错误而受到了过度训斥。|近义词：rebuke severely, chastise excessively, scold harshly|反义词：praise, commend, overlook|词性：动词
