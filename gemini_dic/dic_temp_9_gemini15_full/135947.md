nonpurposive:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːpəsɪv/| adj. |非目的性的，无目的的 |Lacking a specific aim or purpose; unintentional.  It describes actions or events that happen without a deliberate plan or intention. |例句：His wandering through the city was a nonpurposive activity, simply enjoying the sights and sounds. 他在城市里漫无目的地游荡，只是享受着眼前的景色和声音。

例句：The accidental discovery was a nonpurposive finding, a serendipitous event.  这一偶然的发现是一个非目的性的发现，一个偶然事件。


近义词：aimless, unintentional, random, haphazard, chance, fortuitous


反义词：deliberate, intentional, purposeful, designed, planned


词性：形容词
