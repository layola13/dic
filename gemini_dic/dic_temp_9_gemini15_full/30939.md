chairmanship:/ˈtʃeərmənʃɪp/| n. |主席职位；主席身份；领导权|The position or duties of a chairman; the act or period of holding this position.  It also refers to the manner in which a chairman performs their duties.|例句：The chairmanship of the committee was given to Sarah.  委员会主席职位给了萨拉。

例句：His chairmanship of the meeting was efficient and fair. 他主持会议的方式高效且公平。


近义词：presidency, leadership, headship|反义词：subordination, followership|词性：名词


词源：来自chairman（主席）+ -ship（表示职位、身份、状态的后缀）。Chairman源自chair（椅子，此处指会议主席的位置）+ man（人）。
