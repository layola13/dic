They often perform during religious festivals and celebrations, incorporating indigenous and Catholic traditions.|例句：The concheros danced with fervent energy, their movements reflecting centuries of tradition.  康切罗斯人充满热情地舞蹈，他们的动作反映了几个世纪的传统。|近义词：dancers, performers, troupes|反义词:None specifically applicable.|名词
