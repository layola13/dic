fovilla:/fəˈvɪlə/| n.|火山灰；细小的火山碎屑物|Fine ash or dust ejected from a volcano.  The term often refers to the very fine particles, smaller than lapilli.|Example: The fovilla from the eruption blanketed the surrounding villages.  火山爆发产生的火山灰覆盖了周围的村庄。|近义词：volcanic ash, volcanic dust|反义词：lava (large volcanic rock)|名词
