guidepost:/ˈɡaɪdˌpoʊst/ | n. |路标，指导原则 | A post or sign that indicates direction or gives guidance; something that serves as a guide or direction. |例句：The guidepost pointed the way to the village.  路标指示通往村庄的路。|例句：His advice served as a guidepost for my career decisions. 他的建议成为了我职业生涯决策的指导原则。|近义词：landmark, signpost, beacon, indicator |反义词：misdirection, hindrance
