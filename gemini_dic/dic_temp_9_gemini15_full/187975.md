rottweilers:/ˈrɒtˌwaɪlərz/| n. |罗威纳犬|A breed of large, powerful dog originating in Rottweil, Germany, known for its loyalty, strength, and protective instincts.  Rottweilers are often used as guard dogs and police dogs, but require experienced owners due to their size and strength.|例句：My neighbor owns a Rottweiler, a very loyal and protective dog. 我的邻居养了一只罗威纳犬，它非常忠诚和具有保护性。|例句：The police department utilizes Rottweilers in their K-9 unit. 警察部门在他们的警犬队中使用罗威纳犬。|近义词：guard dog, police dog, mastiff|反义词：None specifically, but smaller, less powerful breeds could be considered antonyms in terms of physical characteristics.
