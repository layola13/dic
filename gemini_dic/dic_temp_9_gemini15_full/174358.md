quicksilver: /ˈkwɪksɪlvər/ | n. |水银，汞；活泼的人；变化无常的事物 | Mercury; a lively, quick-witted person; something inconstant or changeable. |例句：The thermometer contains quicksilver.  温度计里装着水银。
