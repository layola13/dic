self-designer:/ˈsɛlf dɪˈzaɪnər/| n.|自我设计师，自主设计者|A person who designs or creates something for themselves, often without professional training or assistance.  This can apply to various fields like fashion, interior design, or even personal development.|例句：She's a self-designer; she created her own wedding dress.|近义词：independent designer, self-taught designer|反义词：professional designer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a self-designer of his own life. = 他是自己人生的自我设计师。
She designs her own clothes. = 她自己设计衣服。


一般过去时:
He designed his own website last year. = 他去年设计了自己的网站。
She self-designed her garden. = 她自己设计了自己的花园。


现在完成时:
He has self-designed several pieces of furniture. = 他已经自己设计了几件家具。
She has self-designed her entire wardrobe. = 她已经自己设计了全部的衣橱。


过去完成时:
He had self-designed his logo before the company launched. = 公司成立之前，他已经自己设计好了标志。
She had self-designed her home before moving in. = 她搬进去之前已经自己设计好了房子。


将来时:
He will self-design a new app for his business. = 他将为自己的生意设计一个新的应用程序。
She will self-design a new dress for the party. = 她将为派对自己设计一件新衣服。


虚拟语气:
If I were a self-designer, I would create my dream home. = 如果我是个自我设计师，我会创造我的梦想之家。
I wish I had the skills to self-design my own car. = 我真希望我有能力自己设计我的汽车。


被动语态:
His website was self-designed. = 他的网站是他自己设计的。
Her garden was self-designed with great care. = 她的花园是她精心自己设计的。


疑问句:
Did he self-design that incredible piece of art? = 他是自己设计那件令人难以置信的艺术品吗？
Is she a self-designer of her own fashion choices? = 她是自己时尚选择的自我设计师吗？


否定句:
He didn't self-design the building; he hired an architect. = 他没有自己设计那栋建筑；他雇佣了一位建筑师。
She doesn't self-design her clothes anymore; she buys them ready-made. = 她不再自己设计衣服了；她买现成的。
