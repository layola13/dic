returnable:/rɪˈtɜːrnəbl/| adj.|可退回的，可归还的|Able to be returned; capable of being sent back.  This typically refers to goods that can be returned to a store for a refund or exchange, or containers that can be returned for reuse.|例句：This item is returnable within 30 days of purchase. 这件商品可在购买后30天内退货。

例句：The bottles are returnable; please bring them back to the store.  这些瓶子可以退回；请把它们带回商店。


近义词：refundable, repayable,  replaceable


反义词：non-returnable, irreplaceable


词性：形容词
