nondevelopable: /ˌnɒn.dɪˈvɛləpəbl/ | adj. | 不可展的；不能发展的 |  Not capable of being developed or unfolded into a plane surface without stretching or tearing.  This term is primarily used in geometry and material science, referring to surfaces that cannot be flattened without distortion.  | Example:  A sphere is a nondevelopable surface. 球面是一个不可展的曲面。 The material chosen for the wing must be a developable surface, so we can’t use this non-developable material.机翼选择的材料必须是可展曲面，因此我们不能使用这种不可展材料。 | 近义词：unfoldable, inextensible | 反义词：developable
