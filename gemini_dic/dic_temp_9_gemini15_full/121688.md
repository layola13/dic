misposition:/ˌmɪspəˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 位置错误；错位 | The incorrect placement of something.  It refers to something being in the wrong place or position. This could be a physical object, a piece of information, or even a conceptual element. | Example: The misposition of the valve caused the engine to overheat.  位置错误的阀门导致发动机过热。 The misposition of facts in his report led to a flawed conclusion. 他报告中事实的错误陈述导致了错误的结论。 | 近义词：malposition, displacement, dislocation | 反义词：correct positioning, proper placement |  词性：名词
