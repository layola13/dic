lapilli:/læˈpɪli/ | n. |火山小碎屑，火山砾|Small pieces of volcanic rock, typically 2–64 mm in diameter, ejected during an eruption.  They are intermediate in size between volcanic ash and volcanic bombs. |The eruption produced a rain of lapilli that covered the surrounding area. 火山爆发产生了覆盖周围地区的火山砾雨。|volcanic cinders, volcanic ash (smaller), volcanic bombs (larger)|—|名词
