caroming:/kəˈrʊmɪŋ/| v.|碰撞；反弹；|The act of striking a surface and rebounding.|The ball caromed off the wall and into the pocket. 球撞到墙上弹进了球袋。The light caromed off the polished surface. 光线从抛光的表面反射出来。|近义词：rebounding, ricocheting|反义词：stopping, halting
