It refers to the characteristic of something not causing death. |The nonfatalness of the injury allowed for a quicker recovery.  这次受伤的非致命性使其恢复得更快。 The study focused on the nonfatalness of the new drug. 这项研究关注的是这种新药的非致命性。|近义词：harmlessness, innocuousness |反义词：fatality, lethality
