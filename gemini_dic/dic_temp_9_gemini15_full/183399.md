reproof:/rɪˈpruːf/ | n. |责备；谴责；批评 |  The act of reproving; rebuke; criticism.  Reproof often implies gentle but firm correction or admonition, aiming to improve behavior or attitude.  |例句：He received a sharp reproof from his boss for his mistakes. 他因为犯错受到了老板严厉的责备。

His mother's gentle reproof made him reflect on his actions. 他母亲的温和责备使他反省了自己的行为。|近义词：rebuke, reprimand, admonition, censure, chastisement |反义词：praise, commendation, approval
