grapple-with:/ˈɡræpəl wɪð/| v. |努力解决；与…搏斗； | to struggle with a problem or difficult task| 例句：The team is grappling with a complex technical problem. 团队正在努力解决一个复杂的技术问题。

例句：She's been grappling with the decision for weeks.  她已经为此决定苦苦挣扎了好几个星期了。


近义词：struggle with, contend with, wrestle with, tackle, confront| 反义词：ignore, neglect, avoid | 动词
