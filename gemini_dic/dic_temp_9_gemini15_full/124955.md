muffled:/ˈmʌfld/ | adj. | 闷热的；压抑的；(声音)低沉的；模糊不清的 |  Describes a sound that is quiet and unclear, or something that is subdued or suppressed. It can also refer to something wrapped or covered to prevent sound or heat from escaping. |例句：The music was muffled by the thick walls. 厚厚的墙壁使音乐听起来很闷。

The distant thunder sounded muffled. 远处的雷声听起来很低沉。


近义词：dampened, muted, subdued, softened | 反义词：clear, loud, distinct | 词性:形容词

词源:
The word "muffled" comes from the Middle English word "moflen," meaning "to wrap or cover," which ultimately has Germanic origins.  The sense of "to deaden a sound" developed later.
