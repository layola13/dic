self-storage:/ˈsɛlf ˈstɔːrɪdʒ/| n. |自助仓储|Self-storage refers to the industry of renting individual storage units to customers for the purpose of storing their belongings.  These units are typically located in secure facilities and are often rented on a month-to-month basis.  |例句：We rented a self-storage unit to store our furniture while we were overseas. 我们租了一个自助仓储单元来存放我们的家具，因为我们出国了。

例句：Many people use self-storage to declutter their homes and free up space. 许多人使用自助仓储来清理家里的杂物并腾出空间。


近义词：storage rental, mini-storage, storage facility


反义词：无


词性：名词
