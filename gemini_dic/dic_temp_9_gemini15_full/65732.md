ever:/ˈevər/ | adv. |曾经，永远，究竟 | Always or at any time.  It is used to emphasize a statement or question, or to refer to an indefinite time in the past or future. |例句：Have you ever been to Paris? 你去过巴黎吗？
I will never forget you. 我永远不会忘记你。|近义词：always, ever, at all times |反义词:never, not at all


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I always eat breakfast. = 我总是吃早餐。
She never goes to the movies. = 她从不去看电影。

一般过去时:
I always went to school by bus. = 我过去总是坐公交车去学校。
Did you ever meet my brother? = 你曾经见过我的兄弟吗？

现在完成时:
I have never been to Japan. = 我从未去过日本。
Have you ever seen a whale? = 你见过鲸鱼吗？

过去完成时:
I had never seen such a beautiful sunset before. = 我以前从未见过如此美丽的日落。
Had you ever considered moving to the countryside? = 你曾经考虑过搬到乡下吗？

将来时:
I will ever cherish this moment. = 我将永远珍惜这一刻。
Will you ever forgive me? = 你会原谅我吗？

虚拟语气:
If I ever won the lottery, I would travel the world. = 如果我中了彩票，我就会环游世界。
I wish I had ever met my grandparents. = 我希望我曾经见过我的祖父母。

被动语态:
This question has never been answered. = 这个问题从未得到解答。
Will this problem ever be solved? = 这个问题会得到解决吗？

疑问句:
Have you ever been to London? = 你去过伦敦吗？
Did you ever think you would meet someone like her? = 你曾经想过你会遇到像她这样的人吗？

否定句:
I have never seen such a thing. = 我从未见过这样的事。
I will never do that again. = 我再也不会那样做了。


PHRASE->
ever since = 从那时起
ever so = 非常
for ever = 永远
if ever = 如果曾经
no matter ever = 无论如何
