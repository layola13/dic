constructing:/kənˈstrʌktɪŋ/ | v. | 建造；构建；构成 |  The act of building or creating something.  It involves putting parts together to form a whole. | 例句：They are constructing a new bridge across the river. = 他们在河上建造一座新桥。

例句：The architect is constructing a detailed plan for the new building. = 建筑师正在为新建筑物制定详细的计划。


近义词：building, erecting, creating, forming, establishing

反义词：demolishing, destroying, deconstructing

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are constructing a new highway. = 他们正在建造一条新的高速公路。
We are constructing a detailed report. = 我们正在撰写一份详细的报告。

一般过去时:
They constructed a magnificent castle. = 他们建造了一座宏伟的城堡。
The team constructed a successful marketing strategy. = 该团队制定了一个成功的营销策略。

现在完成时:
They have constructed several buildings this year. = 他们今年已经建造了几座建筑物。
The company has constructed a solid reputation. = 这家公司已经建立了良好的声誉。

过去完成时:
They had constructed the foundation before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经建好了地基。
The artist had constructed a complex narrative in her painting. = 艺术家在她的绘画中构建了一个复杂的叙事。

将来时:
They will construct a new stadium next year. = 他们明年将建造一个新的体育场。
The government will construct new policies to address the issue. = 政府将制定新的政策来解决这个问题。

虚拟语气:
If they constructed a better system, it would be more efficient. = 如果他们建造一个更好的系统，它将更有效率。
I wish they had constructed the bridge more safely. = 我希望他们当初建造这座桥梁时更安全一些。

被动语态:
The building is being constructed by a local firm. = 这座建筑物正在由一家当地公司建造。
A new plan is being constructed to improve the system. = 正在制定一项新计划以改进系统。

疑问句:
Are they constructing a new school in the neighborhood? = 他们是不是在附近建造一所新学校？
What are they constructing in that field? = 他们在那个田地里建造什么？

否定句:
They are not constructing a new factory. = 他们没有建造新的工厂。
The government did not construct the necessary infrastructure. = 政府没有建设必要的基础设施。


PHRASE->
