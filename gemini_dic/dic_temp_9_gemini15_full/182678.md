reoxidized:/ˌriːˈɒksɪdaɪzd/| adj.|再氧化的；重新氧化的|Having been oxidized again after a previous reduction.  The process involves the addition of oxygen or the removal of electrons from a substance that had previously been reduced.|例句：The reoxidized metal showed signs of corrosion. 再氧化的金属显示出腐蚀迹象。
The reoxidized copper lost its desirable color. 再氧化的铜失去了其理想的颜色。|近义词：re-oxygenated, oxidized again|反义词：reduced
