improves:/ɪmˈpruːvz/ | v. |改进；改善；提高|To make or become better.|The new training program improves employee skills. 新的培训项目提高了员工的技能。| enhances, betters, ameliorates | worsens, deteriorates, declines | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Regular exercise improves cardiovascular health. = 定期锻炼可以改善心血管健康。
This medicine improves my sleep quality. = 这药改善了我的睡眠质量。

一般过去时:
The situation improved after the storm passed. = 风暴过后情况有所好转。
He improved his English through constant practice. = 他通过不断练习提高了他的英语水平。

现在完成时:
The company has improved its efficiency significantly. = 公司的效率显著提高了。
She has improved her performance greatly this year. = 她今年的业绩有了很大的提高。

过去完成时:
They had improved their technology before launching the new product. = 他们在推出新产品之前已经改进了他们的技术。
The team had improved its strategy after analyzing the data. = 该团队在分析数据后改进了自己的策略。

将来时:
This new software will improve our workflow. = 这款新软件将改善我们的工作流程。
The doctor believes the treatment will improve his condition. = 医生相信这种治疗会改善他的病情。

虚拟语气:
If you studied harder, you would improve your grades. = 如果你学习更努力，你的成绩就会提高。
I wish I could improve my public speaking skills. = 我希望我能提高我的公开演讲技巧。

被动语态:
The design was improved after customer feedback. = 在收到客户反馈后，设计得到了改进。
The system is being improved to enhance security. = 正在改进系统以增强安全性。

疑问句:
Will this new method improve productivity? = 这种新方法会提高生产力吗？
Does this change improve the overall design? = 这种改变是否改善了整体设计？

否定句:
The new policy did not improve the situation. = 新政策并没有改善局面。
The medication didn't improve her symptoms. = 药物并没有改善她的症状。


PHRASE->
improve upon = 改进...
improve oneself = 自我提升
markedly improve = 明显改善
steadily improve = 稳步提高
gradually improve = 逐渐改善
