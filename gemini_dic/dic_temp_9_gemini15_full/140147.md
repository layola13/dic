offcut:/ˈɒfkʌt/| n. |边角料，剩余物 |A piece of material that is left over after the main part has been used.  |例句：The offcuts from the woodworking project were used to make smaller items.|近义词：scrap, remnant, leftover |反义词：main piece, whole piece
