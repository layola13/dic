nonsecretarial: /ˌnɒnˌsɛkrɪˈtɛəriəl/ | adj. |非秘书的，非文秘的 | Not relating to or involving the work of a secretary.  This adjective describes something or someone that is not associated with secretarial duties or a secretarial role.  |Example:  He held a nonsecretarial position in the company. 他在公司担任非秘书职位。

The department offered both secretarial and nonsecretarial jobs. 该部门提供秘书和非秘书职位。

近义词: administrative, clerical (in some contexts), managerial


反义词: secretarial


词性: adjective
