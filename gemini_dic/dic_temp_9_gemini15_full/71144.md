fishtails:/ˈfɪʃˌteɪlz/| n.|鱼尾;（车）甩尾；（船）船尾；|The rear end of a car or other vehicle that sways from side to side, especially when losing control.  Also refers to the tail fin of a fish.|例句：The car fishtailed on the icy road.  这辆车在结冰的路面上甩尾了。|例句：The fish's fishtails moved gracefully in the water. 鱼的尾巴优雅地在水中摆动。|近义词：skid, swerve, tailspin|反义词：straight, controlled.
