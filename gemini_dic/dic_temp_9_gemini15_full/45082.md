cubists:/ˈkjuːbɪsts/| n. |立体主义者|People who practice Cubism, an early 20th-century avant-garde art movement that revolutionized European painting and sculpture.|The cubists challenged traditional perspectives by fragmenting and reassembling objects.|近义词：modern artists, avant-garde artists|反义词：classical artists, realist artists
