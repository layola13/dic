misruling:/ˌmɪsˈruːlɪŋ/| n. adj.|错误统治；不公正的统治；管理不善|The act of governing badly or unjustly;  a period of bad or unjust government.  It can also describe something that is wrongly ruled or decided.|例句：The misruling of the country led to widespread poverty and unrest.  这个国家的错误统治导致了普遍的贫困和动荡。

The dictator's misruling caused immense suffering.  独裁者的错误统治造成了巨大的痛苦。

近义词：maladministration, tyranny, oppression, bad governance|反义词：just rule, good governance, wise leadership|名词
