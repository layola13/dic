foss:/fɒs/| n. |化石；残余|A foss is the remains of an ancient organism preserved in rock or other substances. Fossils provide valuable evidence of past life and ecosystems.  |Example: The museum showcased a remarkable collection of dinosaur fossils.  博物馆展示了一批非凡的恐龙化石。|近义词：remains, relic, fossil remains|反义词：living organism, contemporary
