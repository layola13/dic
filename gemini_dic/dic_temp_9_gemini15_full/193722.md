self-conservation:/ˌsɛlfˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃən/| n.|自我保护，自保|The act of preserving or protecting oneself from harm or destruction.  It involves actions taken by an organism or system to maintain its own existence and integrity.|例句：Self-conservation is a fundamental instinct in all living beings. 自我保护是所有生物的基本本能。

例句：The bear's self-conservation instincts kicked in when it sensed danger.  当熊感觉到危险时，它的自我保护本能就启动了。


近义词：self-preservation, survival, protection|反义词：self-destruction, self-sacrifice
