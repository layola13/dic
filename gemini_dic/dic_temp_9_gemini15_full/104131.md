juncate:/ˈdʒʌŋkeɪt/| n. v.|郊游；游乐；(尤指官方)出访；|A juncate is a pleasure trip, especially an official one.  It can also refer to the act of taking such a trip. |例句：The committee embarked on a juncate to inspect the new facilities.  委员会进行了一次考察新设施的郊游。The politicians went on a lavish juncate to the south of France.  政客们去法国南部进行了一次奢华的出访。|近义词：excursion, outing, trip, jaunt|反义词：sedentary lifestyle, confinement|名词，动词
