fantasizing: /ˈfæntəsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 幻想；空想 |  To imagine or daydream about something, especially something pleasant or exciting.  It often implies a detachment from reality. |

例句：

She spent hours fantasizing about her dream vacation in Italy. = 她花了几个小时幻想她在意大利的梦想假期。

He was fantasizing about winning the lottery. = 他在幻想中彩。


近义词：daydreaming, dreaming, imagining, envisioning

反义词：facing reality, being realistic


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He fantasizes about becoming a famous musician. = 他幻想成为一名著名的音乐家。
They fantasize about living in a castle. = 他们幻想住在城堡里。

一般过去时:
I fantasized about flying when I was a child. = 我小时候幻想过飞行。
She fantasized about meeting her favorite author. = 她幻想过见到她最喜欢的作家。

现在完成时:
He has fantasized about this moment for years. = 他多年来一直幻想这一刻。
She has fantasized about traveling the world. = 她一直幻想环游世界。

过去完成时:
I had fantasized about winning the race before I even started. = 我甚至在比赛开始前就幻想过赢得比赛。
They had fantasized about a life together before they met. = 他们相遇之前就幻想过一起生活。

将来时:
I will fantasize about my future success. = 我将幻想我未来的成功。
She will fantasize about her wedding day. = 她将幻想她的婚礼。

虚拟语气:
If I had the power, I would fantasize about changing the world. = 如果我有能力，我会幻想改变世界。
I wish I could fantasize about something positive for once. = 我希望我这次能幻想一些积极的事情。

被动语态:  (Fantasizing is rarely used in the passive voice because it's usually an active process.)
The story is often fantasized about by children. = 这个故事经常被孩子们幻想。
His achievements are often fantasized about by his younger siblings. = 他的成就经常被他的弟弟妹妹们幻想。


疑问句:
Do you often fantasize about your future? = 你经常幻想你的未来吗？
What do you fantasize about most? = 你最常幻想什么？

否定句:
I don't fantasize about unrealistic things. = 我不会幻想不切实际的事情。
She doesn't fantasize about things that are harmful. = 她不会幻想有害的事情。


PHRASE->

fantasize about: 幻想关于...
fantasize of: 幻想...
lose oneself in fantasizing: 迷失在幻想中
engage in fantasizing: 沉溺于幻想
indulge in fantasizing: 放纵幻想
