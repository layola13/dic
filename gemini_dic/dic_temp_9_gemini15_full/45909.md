cutlets:/ˈkʌtlɪts/| n.|肉饼，炸肉排|Small, flat pieces of meat, often breaded and fried or pan-fried.  They can be made from various meats, such as chicken, pork, lamb, or beef.|例句：I had chicken cutlets for dinner last night.  昨晚我吃了鸡肉饼。

例句：The restaurant is famous for its delicious veal cutlets. 这家餐馆以其美味的 veal cutlets（小牛肉饼）而闻名。


近义词：patties, escalopes, medallions|反义词：无
