buy:/baɪ/| vt.|买，购买|to obtain (something) by giving money or something else in exchange for it.  |例句：I bought a new car last week. 我上周买了一辆新车。|例句：She bought a gift for her mother. 她为母亲买了一件礼物。|近义词：purchase, acquire, obtain|反义词：sell


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I buy groceries every Saturday. = 我每个星期六都买杂货。
He buys a newspaper every morning. = 他每天早上都买报纸。

一般过去时:
They bought a house in the countryside. = 他们在乡下买了一栋房子。
She bought a beautiful dress for the party. = 她为派对买了一件漂亮的裙子。

现在完成时:
I have bought all the ingredients for the cake. = 我已经买齐了做蛋糕的所有材料。
He has bought a new computer recently. = 他最近买了一台新电脑。

过去完成时:
She had bought the tickets before the show started. = 演出开始前她已经买好了票。
They had bought a new car before their trip. = 他们的旅行之前已经买了一辆新车。


将来时:
I will buy a new phone next month. = 我下个月会买一部新手机。
We will buy tickets for the concert tomorrow. = 我们明天将买演唱会的票。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would buy a bigger house. = 如果我有更多钱，我会买一栋更大的房子。
I wish I could buy that painting. = 我真希望我能买下那幅画。

被动语态:
The tickets were bought online. = 票是在网上买的。
The house is being bought by a young couple. = 这栋房子正被一对年轻夫妇买下。

疑问句:
Did you buy anything interesting? = 你买了什么有趣的东西吗？
Where did he buy that shirt? = 他在哪儿买的这件衬衫？

否定句:
I didn't buy anything at the store. = 我在商店什么也没买。
They didn't buy the car because it was too expensive. = 他们没有买那辆车，因为它太贵了。


PHRASE->
buy into:  相信，接受 (e.g., I don't buy into that conspiracy theory.)
buy off: 收买 (e.g., They tried to buy off the witness.)
buy out: 收购 (e.g., The company was bought out by a larger firm.)
buy up: 抢购，买光 (e.g., People bought up all the tickets.)
buy time: 拖延时间 (e.g., We need to buy time to develop a solution.)
