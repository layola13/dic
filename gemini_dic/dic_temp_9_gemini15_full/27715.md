canebrake:/ˈkeɪnˌbreɪk/| n. |甘蔗林，芦苇丛|A dense thicket of cane.  Canebrakes are often found in swampy or marshy areas.|例句：The runaway slaves hid in the dense canebrake. 逃亡的奴隶藏身于茂密的甘蔗林中。  The alligator lurked in the shadows of the canebrake.  鳄鱼潜伏在甘蔗林的阴影中。|近义词：cane field, thicket, swamp|反义词：open field, clearing
