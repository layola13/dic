fizziness:/ˈfɪznɪs/| n. |嘶嘶声；泡沫；兴奋；活力 |The quality or state of being fizzy; the escape of gas from a liquid, producing bubbles.  It can also refer to a lively or excited feeling.|例句：The fizziness of the soda was refreshing. 这苏打的嘶嘶声很清爽。

The fizziness in her voice betrayed her excitement. 她声音里的兴奋劲儿泄露了她的激动。|近义词：effervescence, sparkle, liveliness|反义词：flatness, stillness, dullness
