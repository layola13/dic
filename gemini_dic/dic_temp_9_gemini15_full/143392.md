outfish:/ˌaʊtˈfɪʃ/| vt.|胜过…的捕鱼量，技压…|To catch more fish than (someone); to surpass in fishing skill.|例句：The experienced fisherman easily outfished the novice.  经验丰富的渔夫轻松地超过了新手捕鱼量。|例句：He outfished all the other contestants in the fishing competition. 他在钓鱼比赛中胜过了所有其他参赛者。|近义词：outshine, outdo, surpass|反义词：be outfished by
