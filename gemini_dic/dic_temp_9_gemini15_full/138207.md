nosethril:/ˈnoʊzθrɪl/ | n. | 鼻孔 |  A nostril is one of the two openings in the nose through which air passes.  | Example:  He breathed deeply, flaring his nostrils. 他深深地吸了一口气，鼻孔张开。  |近义词：nare | 反义词：无
