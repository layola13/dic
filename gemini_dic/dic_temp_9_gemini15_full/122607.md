modernizers:/ˈmɒdənaɪzəz/| n.|现代化者，革新者|People or things that promote or bring about modernization.  Those who advocate for or implement modernizing changes in a system, society, or technology.|例句：The modernizers pushed for reforms in education and infrastructure. 现代化者推动了教育和基础设施改革。

例句：The country's modernizers successfully implemented new agricultural techniques.  该国的现代化者成功地实施了新的农业技术。


近义词：reformers, innovators, progressives, updaters

反义词：traditionalists, conservatives, reactionaries

词性：名词
