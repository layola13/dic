gasteropodous:/ˈɡæstrəpɒdəs/| adj.|腹足类的；具有腹足的|Having or relating to a gastropod (a mollusk of the class Gastropoda, such as a snail or slug).  The term describes animals with a single shell or no shell, and a muscular foot used for locomotion.|例句：Many gasteropodous species are herbivores, grazing on algae and plants. 许多腹足类物种是草食动物，以藻类和植物为食。

例句：The gasteropodous snail left a silvery trail as it moved across the damp leaves. 这只腹足类蜗牛在潮湿的树叶上爬行时留下一条银色的痕迹。


近义词：gastropodal (less common)


反义词：There isn't a direct antonym, but terms describing animals with different locomotion methods could be considered contrasting, such as cephalopodous (for cephalopods like squid and octopus).


词性:形容词
