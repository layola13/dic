buttons:/ˈbʌtənz/| n.|纽扣，按钮|Small, usually round objects used to fasten clothing or as controls on devices.|The shirt has four buttons.|近义词：fasteners, knobs|反义词：zippers, laces


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He buttons his shirt every morning. = 他每天早上都扣衬衫扣子。
She buttons her coat before going outside. = 她出门前扣上外套。


一般过去时:
I buttoned my jacket quickly. = 我迅速地扣上了我的夹克。
He buttoned up his coat against the cold. = 他为了抵御寒冷而扣紧了外套。


现在完成时:
She has buttoned all the buttons on her dress. = 她已经扣好了她裙子上的所有扣子。
He has buttoned his shirt and is ready to go. = 他已经扣好了衬衫，准备出发了。


过去完成时:
I had buttoned my coat before the rain started. = 下雨开始之前我已经扣好了我的外套。
She had buttoned up her jacket, so she wasn't cold. = 她已经扣上了夹克，所以不冷。


将来时:
I will button my shirt after I shower. = 我洗完澡后会扣衬衫。
She will button up her coat when it gets colder. = 天冷的时候她会扣上外套。


虚拟语气:
If I had more buttons, I would repair my jacket. = 如果我有更多的纽扣，我就会修理我的夹克。
I wish I had buttoned my coat before leaving. = 我真希望离开前扣上了外套。


被动语态:
The buttons were sewn onto the shirt by hand. = 这些纽扣是手工缝在衬衫上的。
The buttons are being replaced on the coat. = 外套上的纽扣正在更换。


疑问句:
Are all the buttons done? = 扣子都扣好了吗？
Have you buttoned your shirt yet? = 你扣好衬衫了吗？


否定句:
He didn't button his shirt properly. = 他没有把衬衫扣好。
She hasn't buttoned up her coat yet. = 她还没扣上外套。
