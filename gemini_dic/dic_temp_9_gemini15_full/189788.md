saluter:/səˈluːtər/ | n. |敬礼者，致敬者 | A person who salutes.  |例句：The soldier stood at attention, a proud saluter.  这名士兵立正站好，是一个骄傲的敬礼者。|近义词：greeter, honorer |反义词：insulter, offender |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He salutes the flag every morning. = 他每天早上都向国旗敬礼。
She salutes her superiors with respect. = 她恭敬地向上司敬礼。

一般过去时:
The soldier saluted his commanding officer. = 这名士兵向他的指挥官敬礼。
They saluted each other before the battle began. = 在战斗开始前，他们互相敬礼。

现在完成时:
He has saluted the flag many times. = 他已经多次向国旗敬礼了。
She has saluted the new president. = 她已经向新总统敬礼了。

过去完成时:
The soldiers had saluted before the general arrived. = 将军到达之前，士兵们已经敬礼了。
They had saluted the fallen soldiers. = 他们已经向阵亡的士兵敬礼了。

将来时:
He will salute the flag tomorrow. = 他明天将向国旗敬礼。
They will salute the winners of the competition. = 他们将向比赛的获胜者敬礼。

虚拟语气:
If I were a soldier, I would salute the flag. = 如果我是士兵，我会向国旗敬礼。
I wish I had saluted him earlier. = 我真希望我早点向他敬礼。

被动语态:
The flag was saluted by the troops. = 国旗受到了部队的敬礼。
The general was saluted with respect. = 将军受到了恭敬的敬礼。

疑问句:
Did he salute the officer? = 他向军官敬礼了吗？
Will you salute the flag? = 你会向国旗敬礼吗？

否定句:
He did not salute the flag. = 他没有向国旗敬礼。
They did not salute their enemies. = 他们没有向他们的敌人敬礼。
