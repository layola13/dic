photocells:/ˈfoʊtoʊsɛlz/ | n. |光电池；光电管 | A photocell is an electronic device that converts light energy into electrical energy.  It is typically used in applications requiring light detection or light-to-electricity conversion. | Example:  Solar panels are made up of many photocells.  太阳能电池板由许多光电池组成。 The photocell in the streetlight activated it at dusk. 街灯里的光电管在黄昏时启动了它。|近义词：photosensor, light sensor |反义词：无
