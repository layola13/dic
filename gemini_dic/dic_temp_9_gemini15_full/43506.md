craved:/kreɪvd/| v.|渴望，热切地想要|To have a strong desire or longing for something.|例句：I craved a chocolate milkshake after my workout.  我锻炼后渴望喝一杯巧克力奶昔。

例句：She craved the simple pleasures of life after years of hardship.  在多年的苦难之后，她渴望简单的生活乐趣。


近义词：longed for, yearned for, desired, wanted|反义词：disliked, abhorred|词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I crave chocolate every day. = 我每天都渴望巧克力。
They crave excitement and adventure. = 他们渴望刺激和冒险。

一般过去时:
He craved a sense of belonging. = 他渴望归属感。
She craved her mother's love and attention. = 她渴望得到母亲的爱和关注。

现在完成时:
I have craved this dish for weeks. = 我已经渴望这道菜好几个星期了。
She has craved freedom since she was a child. = 她从小就渴望自由。

过去完成时:
He had craved success for many years before achieving it. = 在获得成功之前，他多年来一直渴望成功。
They had craved peace and quiet after the hectic party. = 喧闹的聚会之后，他们渴望平静和安宁。

将来时:
I will crave a warm bath after a long hike. = 长途跋涉后，我会渴望洗个热水澡。
We will crave simpler times in the future. = 未来我们会渴望更简单的时光。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would crave less instant gratification. = 如果我有更多时间，我就不会那么渴望即时满足。
I wish I didn't crave so much sugar. = 我真希望我不那么渴望糖分。

被动语态:
This dish is craved by many. = 这道菜被许多人渴望。
Freedom is craved by people all over the world. = 全世界的人们都渴望自由。

疑问句:
What do you crave most in life? = 你生活中最渴望什么？
Does he crave attention? = 他渴望关注吗？

否定句:
I don't crave spicy food. = 我不渴望吃辣的食物。
She doesn't crave fame or fortune. = 她不渴望名利。


PHRASE->
crave for:渴望得到
crave something intensely:极度渴望某物
crave to do something:渴望做某事
crave the comfort of:渴望…的安慰
to crave attention:渴望关注
