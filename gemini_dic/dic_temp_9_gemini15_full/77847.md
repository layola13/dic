generously:/ˈdʒɛnərəsli/ | adj. |慷慨地；大方地；丰盛地 | in a generous manner; abundantly.  The adjective "generous" means showing a readiness to give more of something, such as money, time, or help, than is strictly necessary or expected. "Generously" is the adverbial form, modifying verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs. |例句：He generously donated a large sum of money to the charity. 他慷慨地向慈善机构捐赠了一大笔钱。

She generously offered her help to her friend. 她慷慨地向朋友提供了帮助。


近义词：liberally, bountifully, abundantly, freely |反义词：stingily, sparingly, meagerly

词源:
