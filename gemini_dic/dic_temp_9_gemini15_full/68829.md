fasciola:/fæsˈiːələ/| n. |吸虫属，肝片吸虫|A genus of parasitic flatworms, including the liver fluke (Fasciola hepatica).  The word refers to both the genus and specific species within that genus.  |例句：Fasciola hepatica is a significant parasite affecting livestock. 肝片吸虫是一种严重影响牲畜的寄生虫。|例句：Research into the life cycle of Fasciola is ongoing. 对肝片吸虫生命周期的研究正在进行中。|近义词：liver fluke (specific species)|反义词：N/A|名词
