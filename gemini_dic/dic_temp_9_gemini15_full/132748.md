nondogmatic:/ˌnɒnˌdɒɡˈmætɪk/ | adj. |非教条的；不墨守成规的 | Not bound by or following dogma; tolerant of different views.  It emphasizes open-mindedness and a willingness to consider alternative perspectives. |例句：His approach to teaching was refreshingly nondogmatic, encouraging students to question and explore. 他教学方法令人耳目一新，鼓励学生质疑和探索。

例句：The nondogmatic nature of the discussion allowed for a free exchange of ideas.  讨论的非教条性质允许自由交流思想。


近义词：open-minded, tolerant, flexible, liberal, eclectic

反义词：dogmatic, rigid, inflexible, authoritarian, doctrinaire
