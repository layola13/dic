helmsman:/ˈhɛlmsmən/ | n. |舵手，掌舵者 | A helmsman is the person who steers a ship or boat.  | The experienced helmsman skillfully navigated the stormy seas.  经验丰富的舵手熟练地驾驭着暴风雨的海面。|  steersman, pilot |  passenger | noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The helmsman steers the ship. = 舵手掌舵。
He is the helmsman on this vessel. = 他是这艘船的舵手。


一般过去时:
The helmsman steered the ship through the storm. = 舵手驾船驶过暴风雨。
He was the helmsman during the voyage. = 航行期间他是舵手。


现在完成时:
The helmsman has steered many ships. = 舵手驾驶过许多船只。
He has been the helmsman for ten years. = 他做了十年舵手了。


过去完成时:
The helmsman had steered the ship safely before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，舵手已安全地驾驶着船只。
Before the accident, he had been the helmsman for five years. = 事故发生前，他做了五年舵手。


将来时:
The helmsman will steer the ship to the harbor. = 舵手将驾驶船只驶向港口。
He will be the helmsman on the next voyage. = 他将在下次航行中担任舵手。


虚拟语气:
If he were the helmsman, the ship would be safer. = 如果他是舵手，船会更安全。
I wish I were a helmsman and could sail the ocean. = 我希望我是一个舵手，可以航海。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
