crocker:/ˈkrɒkər/| n.|陶器匠；（尤指）制粗陶器的人；|A crocker is a potter, especially one who makes coarse earthenware.  The term often carries a slightly derogatory connotation, suggesting inferior quality or craftsmanship.|例句：He was a crocker, known for his sturdy but unglamorous pots. 他是个陶器匠，以其结实但不起眼的陶器而闻名。|近义词：potter, ceramicist|反义词： (无明确反义词，可考虑与高级陶艺家相对) |名词
