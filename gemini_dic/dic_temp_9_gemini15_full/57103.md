doulas:/ˈduːləz/| n.|助产士，陪产者|A doula is a trained professional who provides continuous physical, emotional, and informational support to a person before, during, and just after childbirth.  They do not perform medical tasks.|例句：She hired a doula to help her through labor. 她雇了一位助产士来帮助她分娩。|例句：The doula provided emotional support and encouragement throughout the birthing process. 助产士在整个分娩过程中提供了情感支持和鼓励。|近义词：midwife (but with a different scope of practice), birth coach|反义词：无
