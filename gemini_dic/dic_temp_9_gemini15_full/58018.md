drizzling:/ˈdrɪzəlɪŋ/| v. |毛毛雨，细雨；下毛毛雨|  The verb "drizzling" describes a light rain falling in very small drops. It suggests a gentle, almost misty rain, rather than a heavy downpour. |例句：It's drizzling outside, so take an umbrella.  外面下着毛毛雨，带把伞吧。|例句：The rain was drizzling gently onto the leaves. 雨轻轻地飘落在树叶上。|近义词：sprinkling, misting, showering (light rain)|反义词：pouring, downpouring (heavy rain)|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It is drizzling lightly. = 天正在下着毛毛雨。
The rain is drizzling steadily. = 雨稳稳地下着毛毛雨。

一般过去时:
It drizzled all day yesterday. = 昨天一整天都在下毛毛雨。
The rain drizzled softly throughout the night. = 雨夜里轻轻地下着毛毛雨。

现在完成时:
It has been drizzling since morning. = 从早上开始就一直在下毛毛雨。
It has drizzled on and off all afternoon. = 下午断断续续地下着毛毛雨。

过去完成时:
It had been drizzling for hours before the sun came out. = 太阳出来之前已经下了几个小时的毛毛雨了。
They had drizzled the cake with chocolate sauce. = 他们已经用巧克力酱淋了蛋糕。


将来时:
It will likely be drizzling tomorrow. = 明天可能会下毛毛雨。
They will drizzle the salad with vinaigrette. = 他们会用香醋调味汁淋沙拉。


虚拟语气:
If it were drizzling, we would stay inside. = 如果下着毛毛雨，我们就待在家里。
I wish it were drizzling instead of pouring. = 我希望是下毛毛雨而不是倾盆大雨。


被动语态:
The cake was drizzled with icing. = 蛋糕上淋上了糖霜。
The vegetables were drizzled with olive oil. = 蔬菜上淋上了橄榄油。


疑问句:
Is it drizzling outside? = 外面在下毛毛雨吗？
Was it drizzling when you left? = 你走的时候在下毛毛雨吗？


否定句:
It isn't drizzling right now. = 现在没有下毛毛雨。
It didn't drizzle yesterday. = 昨天没有下毛毛雨。


PHRASE->

constant drizzling = 持续不断的毛毛雨
heavy drizzling = 比较大的毛毛雨 (相对而言)
