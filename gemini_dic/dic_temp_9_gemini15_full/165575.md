premedicating: /ˌpriːˈmɛdɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先用药；预防性给药 |  Giving medication beforehand to prevent or reduce the effects of something, such as pain or anxiety.  | 例句：The patient was premedicated with an analgesic before the surgery. = 病人在手术前预先服用了止痛药。  The dentist premedicated the child to reduce anxiety before the procedure. = 牙医在孩子进行治疗前给他预先服药以减轻焦虑。 | 近义词：prophylactically medicating, pre-treating | 反义词：post-treating
