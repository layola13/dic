diapery:/ˈdaɪpəri/| n.|尿布；婴儿用品|Diapery refers to cloth used as diapers, or more broadly, to baby linens and clothing.  It can also refer to a decorative pattern resembling diaper folds.|Example: The baby's diapery was neatly folded in the drawer. 婴儿的尿布整齐地叠放在抽屉里。|近义词：baby linens, infant clothing, nappies (British English)|反义词：adult clothing
