heathenship:/ˈhiːðənʃɪp/| n. |异教徒身份；异教；异端邪说 |The state or condition of being a heathen; heathen practices or beliefs;  paganism.  It often carries a negative connotation, implying a lack of civilized or religious refinement. |例句：The villagers' heathenship was a source of fear and suspicion to the neighboring towns. 村民的异教信仰让邻近城镇的人感到恐惧和怀疑。

例句：Accusations of heathenship led to the persecution of many innocent people.  对异教的指控导致许多无辜者遭到迫害。


近义词：paganism, idolatry, barbarism, savagery

反义词：piety, orthodoxy, civilization, refinement

词性:名词
