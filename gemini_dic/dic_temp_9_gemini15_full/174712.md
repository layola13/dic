quotient:/ˈkwoʊʃənt/ | n. | 商 | The result of division.  It is the number of times one quantity is contained in another. For example, in the division problem 12 ÷ 3 = 4, 4 is the quotient. | The quotient of 12 and 3 is 4.  12和3的商是4。


The average quotient of their scores was 85. 他们的分数平均数是85。
