pratt:/præt/| n. v.|唠叨；空话；傻瓜；笨蛋；|to talk foolishly or at length; a foolish or silly person|He's such a pratt, always rambling on about nothing. 他真是个傻瓜，总是喋喋不休地说些废话。|近义词：chatterbox, babbler, fool, idiot|反义词：wise person, genius


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prats incessantly about his travels. = 他没完没了地唠叨他的旅行。
She prats on and on about her problems. = 她没完没了地唠叨她的问题。

一般过去时:
He prattled on about his childhood. = 他喋喋不休地谈论他的童年。
She prattled away about her new job. = 她喋喋不休地谈论她的新工作。

现在完成时:
He has prattled away all afternoon. = 他整个下午都在唠叨。
She has prattled on about the same thing for hours. = 她已经几个小时都在喋喋不休地说同一件事。

过去完成时:
He had prattled on for so long that everyone was bored. = 他唠叨了这么久，以至于每个人都厌烦了。
She had prattled away about her plans before we could interrupt. =在我们打断她之前，她已经喋喋不休地谈论她的计划了。


将来时:
He will prattle on about politics all evening. = 他整个晚上都会喋喋不休地谈论政治。
She will prattle away to anyone who will listen. = 她会对任何愿意倾听的人喋喋不休。


虚拟语气:
If he prattled less, people would listen more. = 如果他少说废话，人们就会多听一些。
I wish she wouldn't prattle on so much. = 我希望她不要说得太多。

被动语态:
His stories are often prattled about by his friends. = 他的故事经常被他的朋友们传诵（消极含义，指没完没了地重复）。
The news was prattled over the airwaves. = 这个消息在广播中被喋喋不休地传播（消极含义）。

疑问句:
Does he always prattle on like that? = 他总是那样唠叨吗？
Will she prattle on endlessly at the meeting? = 会议上她会没完没了地唠叨吗？

否定句:
He didn't prattle at all during the meeting. = 他在会议上一点也没唠叨。
She didn't prattle about her personal life. = 她没有谈论她的私生活。
