ings:/ɪŋz/ | n. |动名词；复数形式 |  The "-ings" is a suffix added to verbs to form nouns (gerunds) or to indicate plural form of certain nouns ending in "-ing".  It represents the act of doing something or a resulting state. The plural form is mostly used for nouns already ending in "-ing".| Examples:  Swimming is my favorite sport. (gerund) The paintings are beautiful. (plural noun)|近义词: (depends on context, e.g., for gerunds: action, process, activity; for plural nouns: none directly equivalent)|反义词: (depends on context, no direct antonyms)|名词
