decidedness: /dɪˈsaɪdədnəs/ | n. | 果断；坚决；坚定 |  The quality of being decided; firmness of purpose; resoluteness.  It implies a clear and unwavering commitment to a course of action. |

例句：

Her decidedness in the face of adversity impressed everyone.  她的临危不乱给每个人留下了深刻的印象。

The decidedness of his tone left no room for argument. 他语气坚决，不容置疑。


近义词：resolution, determination, firmness, resoluteness, decisiveness

反义词：indecision, hesitation, wavering, vacillation


词源：源自动词decide (决定)，加上后缀-edness，表示“具备…的性质”。  Decide  itself comes from the Old French *decider, from Latin decidere "to decide, determine," from de- "off, away" + caedere "to cut, fall."  The sense development is from "to cut off" to "to bring to an end" to "to make a decision."
