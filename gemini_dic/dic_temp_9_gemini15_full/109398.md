lentisk:/ˈlɛntɪsk/| n. |黄连木；乳香树|A shrub or small tree of the genus Pistacia, especially Pistacia lentiscus, having evergreen leaves and producing a resin.  The resin is sometimes used in perfumes and incense. |The lentisk shrubs grew abundantly along the Mediterranean coastline.|近义词：mastic tree|反义词：无
