ramble:/ˈræmbəl/ | v. |闲聊；漫谈；漫步；蔓延 | To talk or write at length in a rambling or inconsequential way; to walk or wander aimlessly. |例句：He rambled on about his childhood. 他絮絮叨叨地谈论他的童年。 |例句：We rambled through the countryside. 我们在乡间漫步。 |近义词：wander, meander, stray, digress |反义词：focus, concentrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rambles on endlessly. = 他没完没了地唠叨。
She rambles through the woods every morning. = 她每天早上在树林里漫步。


一般过去时:
He rambled on about his vacation. = 他絮絮叨叨地谈论他的假期。
They rambled through the fields for hours. = 他们在田野里漫步了几个小时。


现在完成时:
He has rambled through most of Europe. = 他走遍了欧洲大部分地区。
She has rambled on about this topic for too long. = 她关于这个话题已经唠叨太久了。


过去完成时:
He had rambled for miles before he found shelter. = 他走了几英里路才找到庇护所。
They had rambled through the forest all day. = 他们整天都在森林里漫步。


将来时:
We will ramble along the coast tomorrow. = 我们明天将在海岸边漫步。
He will ramble on about politics again tonight. = 他今晚又会没完没了地谈论政治。


虚拟语气:
If he rambled less, people would listen more. = 如果他不那么唠叨，人们就会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't ramble on so much. = 我希望他不要说得太多。


被动语态:
The story was rambled through in a confusing way. = 故事以一种令人困惑的方式被支离破碎地叙述出来。
The path is rarely rambled upon. = 这条路很少有人走。


疑问句:
Does he often ramble when he talks? = 他说话时经常唠叨吗？
Will you ramble in the park this afternoon? = 你今天下午会在公园里散步吗？


否定句:
He didn't ramble; he gave a concise speech. = 他没有漫无边际地说话；他做了一个简洁的演讲。
She doesn't ramble when she's focused. = 她专注的时候不会漫无目的地说话。


PHRASE->

ramble about: 漫谈
ramble across: 偶然发现
ramble in: 随意进入
