英文解析:
A plexus is a network of interwoven parts, especially nerves, blood vessels, or lymphatic vessels.  It can also refer to a complex interwoven system or structure.  The term often implies a complex and intricate arrangement.

例句:
The brachial plexus supplies nerves to the arm and hand.  臂丛神经为手臂和手提供神经支配。

The solar plexus is a network of nerves located in the abdomen.  太阳神经丛是位于腹部的神经网络。


近义词:
network, mesh, web, interlacement


反义词:
simple, uncomplicated
