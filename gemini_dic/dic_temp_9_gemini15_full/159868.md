polymerising: /pəˈlɪməraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 聚合；聚合反应 | The process of combining many small molecules (monomers) to form a large molecule (polymer). This process is crucial in the creation of various synthetic materials and natural substances. | Example:  The scientists are studying the polymerising process of different monomers to create new types of plastics. 科学家们正在研究不同单体的聚合过程，以创造新型塑料。 | 近义词: polymerisation, polymerization | 反义词: depolymerisation, depolymerization
