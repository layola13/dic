on-end: /ˌɒnˈɛnd/| /ˌɑːnˈɛnd/ | adj. |竖着；笔直地；连续不断地 |  On end means upright or vertically, or continuously without interruption. It can also refer to something being placed or standing on its end. |

例句：
The chairs were all standing on end.  = 椅子都竖着放着。

The children were up all night on end. = 孩子们整夜都没睡。


近义词：upright, vertically, continuously, consecutively

反义词：lying down, horizontally, intermittently


词性:形容词
