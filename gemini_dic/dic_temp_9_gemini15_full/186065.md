rewriting:/ˌriːˈraɪtɪŋ/| n. |改写；重写|The act of writing something again, typically to improve it or make it clearer.  It often involves revising, editing, and restructuring existing text.|例句：The editor requested a rewriting of the entire chapter.  编辑要求重写整章内容。|近义词：revision, reworking, editing |反义词：first draft


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The author is rewriting his novel. = 作者正在改写他的小说。
She rewrites her essays multiple times. = 她多次重写她的论文。

一般过去时:
He rewrote the letter several times. = 他重写了那封信好几次。
They rewrote the script before the play. = 他们在演出前重写了剧本。

现在完成时:
I have rewritten the report three times. = 我已经重写了报告三次。
She has rewritten the entire document. = 她已经重写了整篇文档。

过去完成时:
He had rewritten the poem before submitting it. = 他在提交诗歌之前已经重写过了。
They had rewritten the contract before signing. = 他们在签署合同之前已经重写过了。

将来时:
I will rewrite the email tomorrow. = 我明天将重写这封电子邮件。
She will rewrite the article after receiving feedback. = 她将在收到反馈后重写这篇文章。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rewrite the whole thing. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重写全部内容。
I wish I had rewritten that section earlier. = 我希望我早点重写那一部分。

被动语态:
The article is being rewritten by a professional editor. = 这篇文章正在由一位专业编辑重写。
The document was rewritten last week. = 这份文件是上周重写的。

疑问句:
Are you rewriting your thesis? = 你正在重写你的论文吗？
Will they rewrite the agreement? = 他们会重写协议吗？

否定句:
I am not rewriting the essay. = 我没有在重写论文。
He did not rewrite the letter. = 他没有重写信。
