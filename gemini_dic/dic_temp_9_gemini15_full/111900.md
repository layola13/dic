logwood:/ˈlɒɡwʊd/| n. |苏木；苏木材|A very hard, dense, reddish-brown wood from a tropical American tree (Haematoxylum campechianum), formerly much used as a dyestuff.  The wood yields a red dye hematoxylin, used in microscopy.|The logwood was used to dye the fabric a deep crimson.|近义词：haematoxylum|反义词：N/A
