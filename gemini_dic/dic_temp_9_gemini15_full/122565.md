moderated:/ˈmɒdəreɪtɪd/ /ˈmɑːdəreɪtɪd/| adj. v.|缓和的；适度的；主持的；调节的|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "moderated" means characterized by moderation; not extreme or excessive.  As a verb, "moderated" means to preside over (a discussion or meeting); to act as a moderator; to make or become less extreme, intense, rigorous, or violent.


例句:
The meeting was well-moderated, ensuring everyone had a chance to speak.  会议进行得很顺利，确保每个人都有机会发言。

The discussion remained moderated throughout, despite some heated points.  尽管有一些激烈的观点，但讨论始终保持克制。


近义词:
tempered, controlled, regulated, chaired, moderated (adj.), mediated


反义词:
extreme, excessive, uncontrolled, unrestrained, radical (adj.), inflamed (v.)


词性:
adjective, verb (past participle)
