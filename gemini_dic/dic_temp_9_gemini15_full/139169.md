obituarian:/əˈbɪtʃuˌɛəriən/| n. |讣告撰写人；悼词撰写者 |A person who writes obituaries.  An obituarian researches the life of the deceased and writes a biographical sketch that is often published in a newspaper. |Example:  The newspaper's obituarian meticulously researched the life of the renowned scientist before writing his obituary.  报纸的讣告撰写人仔细研究了这位著名科学家的生平，然后撰写了他的讣告。|近义词： obituary writer |反义词：N/A
