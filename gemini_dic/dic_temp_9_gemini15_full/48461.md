deemphasized:/ˌdiˈɛmfəsaɪzd/| adj.|不强调的，轻描淡写的|Reduced in emphasis or importance; played down.|The report deemphasized the negative aspects of the project.  这份报告淡化了项目的负面影响。|近义词：downplayed, understated, minimized|反义词：emphasized, highlighted, stressed|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company deemphasizes profits in favor of customer satisfaction. = 公司为了客户满意度而淡化利润。
They deemphasize the importance of testing. = 他们不重视测试的重要性。


一般过去时:
The speaker deemphasized the controversial aspects of the topic. = 演讲者淡化了该主题的有争议方面。
He deemphasized his role in the success. = 他淡化了他在成功中的作用。


现在完成时:
The media has deemphasized the story recently. = 近期媒体已淡化了这个故事。
The government has deemphasized the threat. = 政府已经淡化了这一威胁。


过去完成时:
They had deemphasized the risks involved before the project began. = 项目开始前，他们已经淡化了相关的风险。
She had deemphasized her qualifications in the interview. = 她在面试中淡化了自己的资历。


将来时:
The company will deemphasize traditional marketing methods. = 公司将淡化传统的营销方法。
They will deemphasize the problem in their next report. = 他们将在下一份报告中淡化这个问题。


虚拟语气:
If they deemphasized the negative findings, the results would be more positive. = 如果他们淡化负面发现，结果会更积极。
I wish they had deemphasized the cost factor. = 我希望他们当时能淡化成本因素。


被动语态:
The importance of the issue was deemphasized by the media. = 媒体淡化了这个问题的重要性。
The negative aspects were deemphasized in the final report. = 最终报告中淡化了负面方面。


疑问句:
Was the role of the individual deemphasized in the study? = 在这项研究中是否淡化了个人的作用？
Has the importance of exercise been deemphasized in modern society? = 在现代社会中是否淡化了锻炼的重要性？


否定句:
The report did not deemphasize the risks involved. = 报告并没有淡化所涉及的风险。
They did not deemphasize the seriousness of the situation. = 他们没有淡化局势的严重性。


PHRASE->
deemphasize the role of… = 淡化……的作用
deemphasize the importance of… = 淡化……的重要性

deemphasize the threat of… = 淡化……的威胁
deemphasize the impact of… = 淡化……的影响


词源:
de- (prefix indicating removal or reversal) + emphasize (to give special importance or prominence to)
