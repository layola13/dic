proclivity:/prəˈklɪvɪti/| n. |倾向，癖好，偏好 | A natural or habitual inclination or tendency.  It suggests a strong predisposition towards a particular behavior, often one that is considered undesirable or negative. |He has a proclivity for exaggeration. 他有夸大的倾向。|She showed a proclivity for mischief in her youth. 她年轻时就表现出喜欢恶作剧的倾向。|近义词：tendency, inclination, predisposition, propensity |反义词：aversion, dislike, repugnance |名词
