establish: /ɪˈstæblɪʃ/ | vt. | 建立，创立，确立 | To set up or create something (like a business, system, or organization) on a firm or permanent basis.  To prove or show something to be true. To settle or make someone feel comfortable or secure in a place or position. |例句：The company was established in 1980. 这家公司成立于1980年。 |例句：The scientists established a link between smoking and lung cancer. 科学家们证实了吸烟与肺癌之间的联系。|近义词：found, institute, create, confirm, prove |反义词：destroy, abolish, dismantle, refute, disprove |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They establish new rules every year. = 他们每年都制定新的规章制度。
The government establishes policies to improve the economy. = 政府制定政策以改善经济。

一般过去时:
She established a successful career in law. = 她创立了成功的法律事业。
He established his reputation as a brilliant scientist. = 他确立了自己作为杰出科学家的声誉。

现在完成时:
They have established a strong presence in the market. = 他们在市场上已经确立了强大的地位。
We have established a good working relationship with our clients. = 我们已经与客户建立了良好的工作关系。

过去完成时:
Before the war, they had established a thriving trade network. = 在战争爆发前，他们已经建立了一个繁荣的贸易网络。
She had established her independence before she turned thirty. = 她三十岁之前就已自立。

将来时:
They will establish a new branch in London next year. = 他们明年将在伦敦设立一个新的分公司。
The company will establish its headquarters in New York. = 公司将在纽约设立总部。

虚拟语气:
If they established a better communication system, they would be more efficient. = 如果他们建立更好的沟通系统，他们会更高效。
I wish they had established clear guidelines earlier. = 我希望他们早点制定明确的指导方针。

被动语态:
The rules were established by the committee. = 这些规章制度是由委员会制定的。
The company's foundation was established in 1850. = 该公司的基础成立于1850年。

疑问句:
Has the company established a new marketing strategy? = 公司是否制定了新的营销策略？
Will they establish a new research facility? = 他们会建立一个新的研究设施吗？

否定句:
They did not establish any clear goals for the project. = 他们没有为该项目设定任何明确的目标。
The research failed to establish a causal link between the two variables. = 这项研究未能确定这两个变量之间的因果关系。


PHRASE->
establish a company  = 成立公司
establish a fact = 确定事实
establish contact = 建立联系
establish oneself =  确立自己的地位
establish a precedent = 创建先例

词源:
Old French *establir, from Latin stabilis 'stable'.  The meaning evolved from "to make firm" to "to set up firmly".
