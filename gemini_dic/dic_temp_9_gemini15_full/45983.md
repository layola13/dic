The term is less common in modern medical terminology, often replaced by more specific descriptions of cyanide poisoning effects.|Example: The victim's symptoms suggested cyanopathy, necessitating immediate administration of antidote.|近义词：cyanide poisoning, hydrocyanic acid poisoning|反义词：None (it describes a pathological state)|名词
