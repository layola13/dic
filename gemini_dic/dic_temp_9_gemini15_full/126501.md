myxomatosis:/ˌmɪksəˈməʊtəsɪs/| n. |黏液瘤病|A highly contagious viral disease of rabbits, characterized by swelling of the mucous membranes, especially around the eyes, nose, and genitals.  It is often fatal.| 例句：The widespread outbreak of myxomatosis decimated the rabbit population.  黏液瘤病的广泛爆发使兔子的数量锐减。

The rapid spread of myxomatosis among the wild rabbits caused a significant decline in their numbers.  野兔之间黏液瘤病的迅速传播导致其数量大幅下降。|近义词：rabbit disease, viral infection|反义词：无
