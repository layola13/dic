mixedness: /ˈmɪkstnəs/ | n. | 混合性，多样性 | The state or quality of being mixed; a mixture.  It refers to the condition where different elements or things are combined, resulting in a heterogeneous composition. This can apply to various contexts, including cultural, racial, social, or even physical mixtures. | Example: The mixedness of the city's population is reflected in its diverse cuisine.  这座城市的混合人口体现在其多元化的美食中。The mixedness of the soil contributed to the variety of plants growing there. 土壤的混合性促进了那里各种植物的生长。| 近义词：heterogeneity, variety, diversity | 反义词：homogeneity, uniformity, sameness | 名词
