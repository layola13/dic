counteradvertising: /ˌkaʊntərˈædvərtaɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 反向广告，抵制广告 | Advertising that aims to counteract or neutralize the effects of another advertisement.  It often involves highlighting the negative aspects of a competitor's product or campaign.  | Example: The company launched a counteradvertising campaign to combat the misleading claims made by its rival.  这家公司发起了一场反向广告宣传活动，以对抗其竞争对手的误导性说法。 | 近义词: competitive advertising,  negative advertising | 反义词:  supportive advertising, positive advertising
