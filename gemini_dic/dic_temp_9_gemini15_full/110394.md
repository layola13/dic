ligulated:/ˈlɪɡjuleɪtɪd/| adj.|舌状的；带舌状突起的|Having a ligule or ligules; strap-shaped.  The term often refers to a flower's corolla or other structures having a strap-like shape.  |例句：The florets of this daisy are ligulated.  这个雏菊的花朵是舌状的。

例句：The ligulated petals of the sunflower create a radiant appearance.向日葵的舌状花瓣创造出灿烂的外观。


近义词：strap-shaped, ribbon-like, tongue-shaped|反义词：tubular (referring to flower shapes)|词性：形容词


词源：源自拉丁语 *ligula，意为“小舌头”，指花瓣或叶片的舌状结构。  The suffix "-ated" indicates the possession of or being characterized by the described feature.
