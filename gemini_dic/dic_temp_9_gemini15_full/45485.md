curcuma:/ˈkɜːrkjuːmə/| n. |姜黄；姜黄粉|Curcuma is a genus of plants whose rhizomes are used as a spice and a coloring agent.  It is commonly known as turmeric.  The rhizomes are ground into a bright orange-yellow powder used in cooking and traditional medicine. |Example:  The vibrant yellow color of the curry came from the curcuma.|近义词：turmeric, Indian saffron|反义词：无
