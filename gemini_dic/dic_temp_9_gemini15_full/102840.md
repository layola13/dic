jasmine:/ˈdʒæzmɪn/| n.|茉莉花|A fragrant flower, typically white or yellow, with a strong, sweet smell, often used in perfumes and teas.|The jasmine tea smells wonderful.  茉莉花茶闻起来很香。|The garden is filled with the scent of jasmine. 花园里充满了茉莉花的香味。|rose, honeysuckle|—|noun
