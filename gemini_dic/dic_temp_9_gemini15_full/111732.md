loculicidal: /ˌloʊkjʊˈlɪsaɪdl/ | adj. |室裂的；裂室的|

英文解析:Relating to or denoting dehiscence of a fruit or seed vessel in which the splitting occurs along the lines of separation between the carpels.  Describing a type of fruit or seed pod that opens along the partitions separating the locules (chambers containing seeds).


例句:
The loculicidal capsule of the poppy opens to release its seeds.  罂粟的室裂蒴果裂开释放种子。

The loculicidal dehiscence of the fruit allows for efficient seed dispersal.  果实的室裂开裂有利于种子有效传播。


近义词:septal, valvular (in specific contexts)


反义词:loculicidal的直接反义词不常见，但可以考虑用描述不开裂的词语，例如：indehiscent (不开裂的)


词性:形容词
