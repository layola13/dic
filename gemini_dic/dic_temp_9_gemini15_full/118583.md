mesothelium:/ˌmɛsoʊˈθiːliəm/| n. |间皮|A thin membrane that lines the surfaces of serous cavities, such as the pleural, pericardial, and peritoneal cavities.  It consists of a single layer of flattened mesothelial cells resting on a basement membrane. It secretes a lubricating fluid that reduces friction between organs.|The mesothelium of the pleura was inflamed.  间膜的间皮发炎了。|Epithelium|Endothelium|
