incite:/ɪnˈsaɪt/ | vt. |煽动；激起；激励 | To encourage or stir up (violent or unlawful behavior).  |例句：The speaker's words incited the crowd to riot.  演讲者的言辞煽动了人群暴动。|例句：His inflammatory remarks incited hatred and violence. 他煽动性的言论激起了仇恨和暴力。|近义词：provoke, instigate, rouse, stir up |反义词：calm, soothe, pacify |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He incites violence in his speeches. = 他在演讲中煽动暴力。
The protesters incite the public to demonstrate. = 抗议者煽动公众示威。


一般过去时:
The rebels incited a revolution. = 反叛者煽动了一场革命。
His actions incited a great deal of anger. = 他的行为激起了很大的愤怒。


现在完成时:
They have incited several riots over the past year. = 他们在过去一年中煽动了几次暴乱。
Her speech has incited much debate. = 她的演讲引发了大量的争论。


过去完成时:
The agitators had incited the workers before the strike. = 煽动者在罢工前煽动了工人。
They had incited a riot by spreading false rumours. = 他们通过散布谣言煽动了一场暴乱。


将来时:
He will incite more unrest with his policies. = 他的政策将煽动更多的动荡。
I fear they will incite a war. = 我担心他们会挑起战争。


虚拟语气:
If he incited a rebellion, he would be imprisoned. = 如果他煽动叛乱，他将被监禁。
I wish he hadn't incited such hatred. = 我真希望他没有煽动如此大的仇恨。


被动语态:
The crowd was incited to violence by the agitator. = 群众被煽动者煽动起来进行暴力活动。
The riot was incited by false information. = 暴乱是由虚假信息煽动的。


疑问句:
Did his words incite the violence? = 他的话是否煽动了暴力？
What incited the rebellion? = 什么煽动了叛乱？


否定句:
He did not incite any violence. = 他没有煽动任何暴力。
The leader attempted to incite the people, but failed. = 领导试图煽动人民，但失败了。


PHRASE->
incite to rebellion: 煽动叛乱
incite to violence: 煽动暴力
incite hatred: 煽动仇恨
incite a riot: 煽动暴乱
incite fear: 煽动恐惧

词源:
incite 源自古法语 inciter，意思是“激励，激发”，最终源自拉丁语 incitare，意思是“推动，激励”。  incitare 由 in- (表示“进入”或“到...中”) 和 citare (意思是“召唤，激发”) 组合而成。
