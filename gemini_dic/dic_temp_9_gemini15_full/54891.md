dispone:/dɪˈspoʊz/ | vt. |处理；处置；安排；使倾向于 | to get rid of something; to arrange or settle something; to make someone inclined to a particular action or feeling | 例句：The company disposed of its waste responsibly. 公司负责任地处理了其废物。  The committee disposed of the matter quickly. 委员会迅速解决了这个问题。 | 近义词：dispose of, get rid of, arrange, settle | 反义词：retain, keep, acquire | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disposes of his old clothes every year. = 他每年都处理掉旧衣服。
She disposes of her time wisely. = 她明智地安排她的时间。


一般过去时:
They disposed of the evidence carefully. = 他们小心地处理了证据。
He disposed of the problem efficiently. = 他有效地解决了这个问题。


现在完成时:
We have disposed of all the rubbish. = 我们已经处理掉了所有的垃圾。
She has disposed of her old car. = 她已经处理掉了她的旧车。


过去完成时:
They had disposed of the documents before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们已经处理掉了文件。
He had disposed of the unwanted furniture before moving. = 他搬家之前已经处理掉了不需要的家具。


将来时:
They will dispose of the toxic waste properly. = 他们将妥善处理有毒废物。
She will dispose of the old computer soon. = 她很快就会处理掉旧电脑。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would dispose of these papers properly. = 如果我有更多时间，我会妥善处理这些文件。
I wish I had disposed of the old books earlier. = 我真希望我早点处理掉那些旧书。


被动语态:

疑问句:
