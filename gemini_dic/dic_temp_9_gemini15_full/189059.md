sabotages:/ˌsæbəˈtɑːʒ/| v.|破坏活动；暗中破坏|To deliberately destroy or damage something, especially in order to hinder a cause or movement.|例句：The rebels sabotaged the railway lines.  叛军破坏了铁路线路。

例句：The workers sabotaged the production line to protest their low wages. 工人们破坏了生产线以抗议他们的低工资。


近义词：undermine, cripple, wreck, disrupt|反义词：repair, mend, fix|动词
