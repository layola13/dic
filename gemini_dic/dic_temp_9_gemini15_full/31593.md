chasten:/ˈtʃeɪsən/| vt.|惩戒；责备；使痛改前非|To correct by punishment or suffering; to subdue or restrain.  To cause (someone) to feel shame or remorse; to humble.|例句：His teacher chastened him for his bad behavior.  他的老师因他的不良行为而责备他。

The experience chastened him, making him more cautious in the future.  这次经历使他痛改前非，以后更加谨慎。|近义词：rebuke, reprimand, correct, discipline|反义词：praise, commend, reward


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chastens his students regularly. = 他定期惩戒他的学生。
She chastens her dog for chewing her shoes. = 她责备她的狗啃她的鞋子。

一般过去时:
My father chastened me for breaking the vase. = 我父亲因为打破花瓶而责备我。
The judge chastened the defendant for his lies. = 法官因被告说谎而责备他。

现在完成时:
The scandal has chastened the politician. = 这场丑闻使这位政治家痛改前非。
Life's hardships have chastened her spirit. = 人生的苦难磨练了她的意志。

过去完成时:
He had chastened his son many times before this incident. = 在这件事发生之前，他已经多次惩戒他的儿子了。
She had chastened herself for her impulsive actions. = 她因自己的冲动行为而自责。

将来时:
The teacher will chasten the disruptive student. = 老师将惩戒那个扰乱课堂秩序的学生。
I will chasten myself for my mistakes. = 我将为我的错误进行自我反省。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would chasten myself for my arrogance. = 如果我是你，我会为我的傲慢而自责。
I wish I had chastened him sooner. = 我真希望我早点责备他。

被动语态:
The child was chastened for his misbehavior. = 这个孩子因行为不端而受到责备。
He was chastened by the harsh criticism. = 他被严厉的批评所教训。

疑问句:
Will you chasten him for his actions? = 你会因为他的行为而责备他吗？
Should I chasten my child for this minor offense? = 我应该因为这件小错而责备我的孩子吗？

否定句:
He did not chasten his son for his minor mistakes. = 他没有因为儿子的轻微错误而责备他。
She will not chasten herself for what she did. = 她不会为她所做的事情而自责。


PHRASE->
chasten one's spirit = 砥砺精神
chasten one's ambitions = 降低野心
chasten one's pride = 抑制骄傲
chasten the soul = 净化心灵
chasten the flesh = 克制肉欲


词源:
From Old French *chaster, from Latin castus ("pure, chaste").  The original meaning related to purification and making pure, later evolving to include the sense of correction and discipline.
