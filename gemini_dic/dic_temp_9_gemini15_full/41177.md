copygraph:/ˈkɒpiɡræf/ | n. |复制图，影印图 |A copygraph is a reproduction of an image or text, typically made using a photocopying or similar process. It is often used in the context of informal or quick reproductions, as opposed to more formal printing methods.  |Example:  The teacher made a copygraph of the worksheet for each student. 老师为每个学生制作了一份工作表的影印件。|近义词：photocopy, duplicate, reproduction |反义词：original
