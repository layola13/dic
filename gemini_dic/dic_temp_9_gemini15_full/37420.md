commercials:/kəˈmɜːrʃəlz/ | n. |商业广告，广告节目 |Short, paid announcements on television or radio that promote products or services.  Commercials are a key part of advertising, aiming to persuade viewers or listeners to buy a product or use a service.|例句：I hate watching commercials during my favorite TV show. = 我讨厌在我最喜欢的电视节目中看广告。

例句：Many commercials use humor to attract attention. = 许多广告使用幽默来吸引注意力。

近义词：advertisements, ads, spots

反义词：无
