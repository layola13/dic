happened:/ˈhæpənd/ | v. |发生|To occur; to take place; to come to pass.  It describes an event or action that has already taken place. |What happened at the party last night? 昨晚派对发生了什么？|occur, transpired, took place|didn't happen, ceased|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What happens every morning? =每天早上发生什么？
What usually happens in this situation? =在这种情况下通常会发生什么？


一般过去时:
What happened at the meeting? =会议上发生了什么？
What happened to your car? =你的车怎么了？


现在完成时:
What has happened since I left? =我离开后发生了什么？
What has happened to the project? =这个项目发生了什么？


过去完成时:
What had happened before the police arrived? =警察到达之前发生了什么？
What had happened to cause the delay? =是什么导致了延误？


将来时:
What will happen next? =接下来会发生什么？
What will happen if it rains? =如果下雨会发生什么？


虚拟语气:
If I had known what would happen, I would have stayed home. =如果我知道会发生什么，我就待在家里了。
I wish I had known what happened. =我希望我知道发生了什么。


被动语态:
What was happened to the vase? =花瓶怎么了？（口语化，不建议使用，更佳表达是What happened to the vase?）
It was reported that a serious accident happened. =据报道发生了一起严重的交通事故。


疑问句:
What happened? =发生了什么？
What happened to him? =他怎么了？


否定句:
Nothing happened. =什么也没发生。
That didn't happen. =那没有发生。


PHRASE->
What happened to...? = ...怎么了？
So what happened then? = 然后发生了什么？
What happened next? =接下来发生了什么？
Whatever happened to...? = ...后来怎么样了？（表示关心或好奇）
What's happened to...? = ...怎么了？（强调现在的情况）
