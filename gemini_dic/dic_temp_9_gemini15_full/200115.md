skiff:/skɪf/| n.|小艇，轻舟|A small, light boat, often used for fishing or pleasure.  |例句：He rowed his skiff out to the middle of the lake. 他划着他的小艇到湖中央。|例句：The fishermen repaired their skiff after the storm.  渔民在暴风雨后修理了他们的轻舟。|近义词：dinghy, shallop, punt|反义词：ship, barge
