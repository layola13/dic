labourer:/ˈleɪbərər/ | n. |劳动者，体力劳动者 | A labourer is a person who does unskilled manual work, typically for a wage.  | Example: He worked as a labourer on a construction site. 他在建筑工地当一名劳动者。 |近义词：worker, manual worker, operative |反义词：manager, executive
