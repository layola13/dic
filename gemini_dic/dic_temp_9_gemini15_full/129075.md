nitrol:/ˈnaɪtroʊl/| n. |硝化物，硝基化合物|A nitrol is a chemical compound containing a nitro group (-NO2).  Nitrols are widely used in various industries, including explosives, pharmaceuticals, and dyes.  They can be highly reactive and potentially dangerous.|Example:  Many explosives contain nitrol groups as a key component in their chemical structure.  许多炸药都含有硝基作为其化学结构中的关键成分。|近义词：nitro compound|反义词：无
