overdocument:/ˌoʊvərˈdɑːkjʊmənt/| v.|过度记录；文件过多|To create an excessive amount of documentation for something.  This can be inefficient and counterproductive, obscuring important information within a mass of unnecessary detail.|例句：The project was overdocumented; the sheer volume of reports made it difficult to find the key information.  项目文件过多；大量的报告使得难以找到关键信息。|近义词：overburden with documentation, excessively document|反义词：underdocument


USAGE->

They overdocument every minor change. = 他们对每一个微小的改动都做了过多的记录。
We overdocument our processes to an unnecessary degree. = 我们对流程进行了不必要的过度记录。


一般过去时:
