detox:/ˈdiːtɒks/ | n. v. | 排毒；戒毒 |

英文解析：The process of removing toxins from the body or of freeing oneself from an addiction.  It can refer to a physical process (like removing toxins from the body through diet or medication) or a metaphorical process (like freeing oneself from a bad habit).

例句：
She's on a detox diet to cleanse her system. = 她正在进行排毒饮食来清洁她的身体系统。

He went to rehab for a detox program. = 他去康复中心参加戒毒项目。


近义词：purification, cleansing, detoxification, rehabilitation

反义词：intoxication, poisoning

词性：名词和动词
