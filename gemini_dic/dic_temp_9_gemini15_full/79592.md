glore:/ɡlɔːr/| n.|光荣；荣耀；壮丽；辉煌|glory, splendor, magnificence,  a feeling of pride and satisfaction because of an achievement|The athlete gloried in his victory.  这位运动员为他的胜利而感到光荣。|近义词：glory, splendor, magnificence|反义词：shame, disgrace, ignominy


USAGE->

The sun glorifies the mountain peaks. = 太阳使山峰更加壮丽。
He glorifies his achievements too much. = 他过分夸耀自己的成就。
