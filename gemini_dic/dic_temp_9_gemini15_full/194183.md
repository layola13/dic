self-isolations:/ˈsɛlfˌaɪsəˈleɪʃənz/ | n. |自我隔离；自我隔离行为 | The act of isolating oneself, typically to prevent the spread of a contagious disease.  This often involves staying at home and avoiding contact with others. | 例句：Government guidelines recommended self-isolations for anyone showing symptoms of the virus. = 政府指南建议任何出现病毒症状的人都进行自我隔离。

例句：Many people chose self-isolations to protect their vulnerable family members. = 许多人选择自我隔离以保护他们脆弱的家人。


近义词：quarantine, isolation, confinement | 反义词：socialization, interaction


词性：名词 (plural)
