podetium: /poʊˈdiːtiəm/ | n. | 伪茎；菌柄 |

英文解析：A podetium is a stalk-like structure found in some lichens, especially in the genus Cladonia.  It's not a true stem, as it lacks vascular tissue, but it elevates the reproductive structures (apothecia) above the thallus (the main body of the lichen).  The podetium can be various shapes and colors, and its characteristics are important for lichen identification.

例句：
The lichen displayed a branched podetium, characteristic of the Cladonia species. =  这种地衣展示了一个分支状的伪茎，这是枝状地衣属的特征。

The mature podetium bore several apothecia at its apex. = 成熟的伪茎在其顶端长有几个子囊盘。


近义词：stalk (in a general sense, but not a true botanical stem)

反义词：无  (没有直接的反义词，只能用描述性词语如"thallus"来对比)

词性：名词
