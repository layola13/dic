/maʊ/| n.|猫|A small domesticated carnivorous mammal with soft fur, a short snout, and retractible claws.  It is widely kept as a pet. |例句：The cat sat on the mat.  猫坐在垫子上。|例句：My cat loves to chase mice. 我的猫喜欢追老鼠。|近义词：feline, kitty, puss|反义词：dog


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The cat sleeps all day. = 猫整天睡觉。
The cats chase the birds. = 猫追逐鸟类。


一般过去时:
The cat slept on the sofa yesterday. = 猫昨天睡在沙发上。
The cats chased mice last night. = 猫昨晚追老鼠。


现在完成时:
The cat has slept for hours. = 猫已经睡了好几个小时了。
The cats have caught many mice. = 猫已经抓到了很多老鼠。


过去完成时:
The cat had already eaten before I got home. = 我回家之前猫已经吃过了。
The cats had chased the birds away before I opened the door. = 我开门之前猫已经把鸟赶走了。


将来时:
The cat will sleep in the sun tomorrow. = 猫明天将在阳光下睡觉。
The cats will chase the butterflies in the garden.  = 猫将在花园里追蝴蝶。


虚拟语气:
If I had a cat, I would name it Mittens. = 如果我有一只猫，我会给它取名米腾斯。
I wish my cat were more playful. = 我希望我的猫更爱玩。


被动语态:
The cat was petted by the child. = 猫被孩子抚摸。
The mice were chased by the cats. = 老鼠被猫追赶。


疑问句:
Is the cat sleeping? = 猫在睡觉吗？
Are the cats hungry? = 猫饿了吗？


否定句:
The cat is not sleeping. = 猫没有睡觉。
The cats did not catch the bird. = 猫没有抓住那只鸟。
