palefaces: /ˈpeɪlfeɪsɪz/ | n. | 白脸人；（尤指）白人 | A derogatory term for white people, especially used by Native Americans.  It often carries a historical connotation of colonialism and oppression. | Example:  The old stories spoke of the coming of the palefaces and the loss of their land.  老故事讲述了白人的到来以及他们土地的丧失。 | 近义词: whites (less offensive), Caucasians (more formal) | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with terms referring to indigenous populations) | 名词
