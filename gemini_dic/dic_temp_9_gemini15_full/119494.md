micropegmatite:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈpɛɡmətaɪt/ | n. | 微细伟晶岩 | A fine-grained igneous rock consisting of an intergrowth of quartz and feldspar, typically forming a graphic texture.  It is a variety of pegmatite characterized by its extremely small crystal size. |The micropegmatite exhibited a distinctive graphic texture under the microscope.  显微镜下，这种微细伟晶岩呈现出独特的文字结构。 The presence of micropegmatite in the granite indicates a specific cooling history. 花岗岩中微细伟晶岩的存在表明其具有特定的冷却历史。|pegmatite,granophyre |aphanite |
