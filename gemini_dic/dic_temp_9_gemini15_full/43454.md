crash-landed:/ˈkræʃˌlændɪd/ | v. |紧急迫降，坠毁着陆 | To land an aircraft or spacecraft unexpectedly and violently, usually due to an emergency.  |例句：The pilot crash-landed the plane in a field after experiencing engine failure.  飞行员在发动机故障后将飞机紧急迫降在田野里。|例句：The small plane crash-landed in the forest, injuring all passengers. 小型飞机坠毁在森林里，所有乘客都受伤了。|近义词：forced landing, emergency landing |反义词：smooth landing, successful landing |动词
