end:/ɛnd/| n. v.|结束，结尾；终止|The end of the story was unexpected. 故事的结尾出乎意料。|近义词：finish, conclusion, termination|反义词：beginning, start


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The meeting ends at 5 pm. = 会议下午五点结束。
The show ends soon. = 节目很快就要结束了。


一般过去时:
The movie ended late last night. = 电影昨晚很晚才结束。
The game ended in a draw. = 比赛以平局告终。


现在完成时:
The project has ended successfully. = 项目已经成功结束。
The rain has finally ended. = 雨终于停了。


过去完成时:
The concert had ended before we arrived. = 我们到达之前音乐会已经结束了。
She had ended her speech by the time he came in. = 他进来的时候，她已经结束了演讲。


将来时:
The class will end in ten minutes. = 课十分钟后结束。
The party will end at midnight. = 派对将在午夜结束。


虚拟语气:
If the war ended tomorrow, many people would be relieved. = 如果战争明天结束，很多人都会松一口气。
I wish this nightmare would end. = 我希望这个噩梦快点结束。


被动语态:
The meeting was ended abruptly. = 会议突然结束了。
The project will be ended next month. = 项目将在下个月结束。


疑问句:
When does the movie end? = 电影几点结束？
Where does this road end? = 这条路在哪里结束？


否定句:
The meeting didn't end until late. = 会议直到很晚才结束。
The party won't end until dawn. = 派对直到黎明才会结束。
