phytotomist:/ˌfaɪtəˈtɒmɪst/| n. |植物解剖学家|A phytotomist is a botanist specializing in the anatomy of plants.  They study the internal structure of plants, including their tissues, organs, and cells.| Example: The phytotomist meticulously examined the plant's vascular system under the microscope. 植物解剖学家仔细地用显微镜检查了植物的维管系统。|近义词：plant anatomist, botanical anatomist |反义词：无
