cowls:/kaʊlz/| n.|兜帽；头巾；烟囱罩；引擎罩|A cowl is a garment covering the head and neck, often part of a monk's habit or a hooded cloak. It can also refer to a covering for a chimney or engine.  |例句：The monk wore a brown cowl.  这位僧侣穿着一件棕色的兜帽。

The car's cowl was damaged in the accident.  汽车的引擎罩在事故中受损了。|近义词：hood, hooding, muffler|反义词：bare head, open top
