cyclotrons:/ˈsaɪklətrɒnz/| n.|回旋加速器|A cyclotron is a type of particle accelerator that uses a magnetic field to bend charged particles into a spiral path, accelerating them to high speeds.  It's used in various scientific research and medical applications.|例句：Scientists used a cyclotron to accelerate protons to high energies for research. 科学家们用回旋加速器将质子加速到高能用于研究。|例句：The hospital utilizes a cyclotron to produce radioisotopes for medical imaging. 这家医院使用回旋加速器生产用于医学成像的放射性同位素。|近义词：particle accelerator, synchrotron|反义词：无
