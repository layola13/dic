reformeress:/rɪˈfɔːrməres/| n. |改革者（女性）|A woman who advocates or works for reform, especially in social or political matters.  A female reformer.|She was a tireless reformeress, fighting for women's suffrage.|近义词：reformist, activist, campaigner |反义词：reactionary, conservative


例句：

The reformeress dedicated her life to improving the lives of the poor.  这位改革者将一生奉献给了改善穷人的生活。

Many reformeresses played crucial roles in the women's rights movement.  许多女改革家在妇女权利运动中发挥了至关重要的作用。
