puddly:/ˈpʌdli/| adj.|泥泞的；积水的|Covered with or full of puddles; resembling a puddle.|The path was puddly after the rain. 雨后小路泥泞不堪。  The field was puddly and muddy. 田地里积满了水，又泥泞。|近义词：muddy, soggy, slushy|反义词：dry, arid
