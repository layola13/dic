reschool:/ˌriːˈskuːl/| vt.| 再教育；重新训练 | To educate or train someone again, often in a different way or for a different purpose.  It often implies a need for improvement or correction in previous training or education.|例句：The company decided to reschool its employees on new safety procedures. 公司决定对员工进行新的安全程序再培训。|例句：The program aims to reschool adults who lack basic literacy skills. 该项目旨在对缺乏基本读写能力的成年人进行再教育。|近义词：retrain, re-educate, rehabilitate |反义词：de-school (informal), leave uneducated
