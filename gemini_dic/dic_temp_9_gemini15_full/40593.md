convalescently:/ˌkɒnvəˈlesntli/ | adv. | 恢复健康地；逐渐康复地 |  In a way that shows or suggests recovery from illness or injury.  |例句：He was convalescing convalescently after his surgery. 他手术后正在逐渐康复。

例句：She smiled convalescently, a flicker of her old self returning. 她虚弱地笑了笑，昔日的影子又回来了。


近义词：recoveringly, recuperatively


反义词：weakly, sickly


词性:副词
