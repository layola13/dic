misemphasizes:/ˌmɪsˈɛmfəsaɪz/| vt.| 歪曲重点；过分强调；误解重点 | To give undue or inappropriate emphasis to; to misrepresent by emphasizing the wrong aspects.  It implies a distortion of the true importance of something. |例句：The article misemphasizes the role of the individual, neglecting the impact of societal forces. 这篇文章歪曲了个人作用的重点，忽略了社会力量的影响。

The report misemphasizes the positive aspects of the project, ignoring the significant drawbacks. 这份报告过分强调了项目的积极方面，忽略了严重的缺点。|近义词：overemphasize, misrepresent, distort |反义词：emphasize correctly, clarify, highlight accurately |词性:动词
