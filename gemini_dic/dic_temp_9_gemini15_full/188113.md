roundsman:/ˈraʊndzmæn/| n. |巡查员，巡逻员，送货员|A roundsman is a person who regularly makes a circuit of a particular area, typically to deliver goods or services, or to check on security.  They might be employed by a newspaper, a milk company, or a security firm. |Example: The roundsman delivered the newspapers before sunrise each day.  巡查员每天日出前送报纸。  The night roundsman checked all the doors and windows to ensure security. 夜间巡查员检查所有门窗以确保安全。|近义词：patrolman, deliveryman, inspector|反义词：stay-at-home worker
