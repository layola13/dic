municipalizer:/mjuːˈnɪsɪpəlaɪzər/| n. |市政管理者；使…市政化的人或事物 |One who, or that which, municipalizes.  The process of bringing something under municipal control or administration. |The new municipalizer oversaw the transition of the water system to city control.  新的市政管理者负责将供水系统移交给城市管理。|近义词：municipal administrator, city manager |反义词：privatizer
