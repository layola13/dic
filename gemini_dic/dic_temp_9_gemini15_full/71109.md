fisheries:/ˈfɪʃəriːz/ | n. |渔业；渔场 |The industry or practice of catching fish, or the places where fish are caught.  It encompasses the catching, processing, and selling of fish and other aquatic life. |例句：The collapse of the local fisheries has devastated the coastal communities. 当地渔业的崩溃摧毁了沿海社区。

例句：Sustainable fisheries management practices are crucial for preserving marine ecosystems. 可持续的渔业管理措施对于保护海洋生态系统至关重要。
