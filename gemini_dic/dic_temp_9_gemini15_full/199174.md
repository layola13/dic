silicofluoride:/ˌsɪlɪkoʊˈflʊəraɪd/| n. |硅氟化物|A salt of silicofluoric acid (H2SiF6), containing the anion [SiF6]2−.  These compounds are typically colorless crystalline solids. They are used in various applications, including water fluoridation and as a hardener in concrete. |Example: Sodium silicofluoride is commonly used in water fluoridation to prevent tooth decay. 钠硅氟化物通常用于饮用水加氟以预防龋齿。|近义词：fluorosilicate|反义词：无
