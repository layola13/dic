croissants:/krwɑːˈsɑːnt/| n. |羊角面包|A crescent-shaped roll of rich, buttery dough, typically eaten for breakfast.  It is a type of viennoiserie pastry. |例句：I bought some croissants for breakfast this morning.  今天早上我买了些羊角面包当早餐。

例句：The bakery sells a variety of delicious croissants, including almond croissants and chocolate croissants. 这家面包店出售各种美味的羊角面包，包括杏仁羊角面包和巧克力羊角面包。
