oceanographically:/ˌoʊʃənəˈɡræfɪkli/ | adj. | 海洋学地；从海洋学角度 | Relating to or characteristic of oceanography.  The study of the physical characteristics of the ocean, including its currents, waves, and tides, as well as its chemical composition and biological life. | 例句：The area was oceanographically surveyed before the construction of the offshore platform.  该区域在近海平台建设前进行了海洋学调查。

例句：The data was analyzed oceanographically to understand the impact of climate change on marine ecosystems. 数据经过海洋学分析，以了解气候变化对海洋生态系统的影响。


近义词：hydrographically (水文地)


反义词：terrestrially (陆地地)


词性：形容词
