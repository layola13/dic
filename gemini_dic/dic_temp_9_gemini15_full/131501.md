noncollinear:/ˌnɒnkəˈlɪniə(r)/| adj. |非共线的|Not lying on the same straight line.  This adjective describes points, lines, or other geometric objects that do not share a common straight line.  For example, three points are noncollinear if they do not all lie on the same line. | Example: The three points A, B, and C are noncollinear. 三点A、B和C是非共线的。


The points forming a triangle are noncollinear.构成三角形的点是非共线的。|近义词：unaligned, non-linear|反义词：collinear
