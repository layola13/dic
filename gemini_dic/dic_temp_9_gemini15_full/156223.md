phthalin:/ˈθælin/| n. |邻苯二酚酞|A colorless crystalline organic compound, C₂₀H₁₄O₄, related to phthalic acid and used in the preparation of phenolphthalein and other dyes.|Phthalin is a colorless compound that is the reduced form of phenolphthalein. It is used as a reagent in various chemical reactions and in the production of certain dyes.|The synthesis of phthalin involves the reduction of phenolphthalein.|合成邻苯二酚酞涉及到酚酞的还原反应。|fluorescein, phenolphthalein|  |名词
