/ˈhɪpəpɒtəməs/ | n. |河马| Hippopotamus is a large, semi-aquatic mammal native to sub-Saharan Africa.  They are known for their barrel-shaped body, short legs, and large mouth.  They are herbivores and spend a significant amount of time in water to regulate their body temperature. | Example:  The hippopotamus opened its massive jaws. 这只河马张开了它巨大的嘴巴。|近义词: river horse |反义词:  None specifically.
