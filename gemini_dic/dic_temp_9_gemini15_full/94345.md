impinges: /ɪmˈpɪndʒɪz/ | v. | 侵犯；影响；撞击 | To have an effect or impact on something or someone.  |例句：The noise impinged on my concentration.  噪音影响了我的注意力。

The new regulations impinge on our freedom of speech. 新的法规限制了我们的言论自由。|近义词：affect, influence, encroach, infringe |反义词：support, aid, assist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music impinges on my ears. = 音乐冲击着我的耳朵。
His actions impinge on my personal life. = 他的行为影响着我的个人生活。

一般过去时:
The storm impinged on the coastal towns. = 暴风雨袭击了沿海城镇。
His words impinged on her self-esteem. = 他的话语伤害了她的自尊心。

现在完成时:
The ongoing construction has impinged on the neighborhood's peace. =  持续的施工已经影响了社区的安宁。
His bad habits have impinged on his health. = 他的坏习惯已经影响了他的健康。

过去完成时:
The previous government's policies had impinged on economic growth. = 前政府的政策已经影响了经济增长。
The heavy rain had impinged on the visibility. = 大雨已经影响了能见度。

将来时:
The new development will impinge on the local ecosystem. = 新的发展项目将会影响当地的生态系统。
The rising sea levels will impinge on coastal communities. = 海平面上升将会影响沿海社区。

虚拟语气:
If the project proceeds, it will impinge on the environment. = 如果项目继续进行，它将会影响环境。
I wish the noise wouldn't impinge on my sleep. = 我希望噪音不会影响我的睡眠。

被动语态:
Our privacy was impinged upon by the media. = 我们的隐私受到了媒体的侵犯。
The project's timeline was impinged upon by unexpected delays. = 项目的时间表受到了意外延误的影响。

疑问句:
Does the new law impinge on our rights? = 新法律是否侵犯了我们的权利？
How does pollution impinge on the environment? = 污染如何影响环境？

否定句:
The decision did not impinge on their daily routines. = 这个决定没有影响他们的日常生活。
The changes didn't impinge on the overall outcome. = 这些改变没有影响最终结果。


PHRASE->

impinge significantly = 显著影响
