novocaine:/ˈnoʊvoʊkeɪn/ | n. |奴佛卡因，普鲁卡因 |A brand name for procaine, a local anesthetic used to numb an area before a medical procedure.  It works by blocking nerve signals that transmit pain sensations.|例句：The dentist used novocaine to numb my tooth before the filling.  牙医在补牙前用奴佛卡因使我的牙齿麻木。 The injection of novocaine temporarily relieved the pain.  注射奴佛卡因暂时缓解了疼痛。|近义词：procaine, lidocaine, xylocaine |反义词：pain, sensation
