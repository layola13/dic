punchballs: /ˈpʌntʃbɔːlz/ | n. |沙袋；拳击沙袋 | A punchball is a heavy bag filled with sand or other material, used for boxing or martial arts training.  It's hung from the ceiling or stand and allows practitioners to practice punches and strikes without injuring a partner. | Example: He spent an hour hitting the punchball to improve his punching power. 他花了一个小时击打沙袋来提高他的击拳力量。  |近义词：heavy bag, punching bag | 反义词：无
