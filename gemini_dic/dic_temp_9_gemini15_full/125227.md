multiculturalism:/ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəlɪzəm/ | n. |多元文化主义|The presence of, or support for the presence of, several distinct cultural or ethnic groups within a society.  It emphasizes the coexistence and interaction of different cultures, often promoting tolerance and understanding between them. |Example: Canada is often cited as a successful example of multiculturalism, celebrating its diverse population.  加拿大经常被认为是多元文化主义的成功典范，庆祝其多元化的居民。|近义词：cultural diversity, pluralism |反义词：cultural homogeneity, monoculturalism
