hydrospeed:/ˈhaɪdroʊspiːd/| n. |水流冲浪|A hydrospeed is a small, buoyant board or mat used for navigating fast-flowing rivers.  It's a thrilling water sport that involves lying prone on the board and using your body to steer and control your descent down the river.  |例句：He went hydrospeeding down the Colorado River. 他在科罗拉多河上玩水流冲浪。

例句：She rented a hydrospeed and spent the afternoon navigating the rapids. 她租了一个水流冲浪板，下午都在激流中航行。


近义词：riverboarding (though riverboarding sometimes involves a slightly different type of equipment)


反义词：无


词性：名词
