dissonant:/ˈdɪsənənt/| adj.|不和谐的；刺耳的；不一致的 |  Characterized by harshness or discordance of sound; inharmonious.  Also describes ideas or elements that clash or are incongruous. |例句：The dissonant chords jarred on the ears.  = 刺耳的和弦使人感到不舒服。The two ideas are dissonant and cannot be reconciled. = 这两个想法是互相冲突的，无法调和。|近义词：discordant, jarring, incongruous, conflicting |反义词：harmonious, consonant, consistent
