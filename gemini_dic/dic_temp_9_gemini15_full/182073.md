remove:/rɪˈmuːv/ | vt. |去除，移除，消除 |to take away or get rid of something |例句：Please remove your shoes before entering.  请进门前脱鞋。

例句：The surgeon removed the tumor successfully. 外科医生成功地切除了肿瘤。


近义词：eliminate, delete, take away, extract | 反义词：add, install, insert, attach | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They remove the trash every day. = 他们每天都清理垃圾。
He removes his hat when he enters a building. = 他进楼时会脱帽。


一般过去时:
I removed the stain from the carpet. = 我去除了地毯上的污渍。
She removed the old wallpaper yesterday. = 她昨天揭掉了旧壁纸。


现在完成时:
We have removed all the obstacles. = 我们已经移除了所有障碍。
They have removed the faulty parts. = 他们已经拆除了有故障的部件。


过去完成时:
He had removed the files before the audit. = 他在审计之前已经删除了文件。
She had removed her jewelry before going to bed. = 她睡觉前已经摘掉了首饰。


将来时:
I will remove the furniture tomorrow. = 我明天会搬走家具。
They will remove the snow from the driveway. = 他们会把车道上的雪清除掉。


虚拟语气:
If I removed the obstacle, I would succeed. = 如果我移除了障碍，我就能成功。
I wish I had removed the file before it was corrupted. = 我真希望在文件损坏之前我已经删除了它。


被动语态:
The old building will be removed next week. = 旧建筑物下周将被拆除。
The problem was removed quickly. = 问题很快就被解决了。


疑问句:
Will they remove the car? = 他们会把车移走吗？
Did you remove the files? = 你删除文件了吗？


否定句:
I cannot remove the stain. = 我无法去除污渍。
He did not remove the obstacle. = 他没有清除障碍。


PHRASE->
remove from = 从…去除
remove oneself = 离开
remove doubt = 消除疑虑
remove suspicion = 消除嫌疑
remove a blockage = 清除阻塞


词源:
The word "remove" comes from the Old French word "remuer," meaning "to stir, move, or agitate."  The prefix "re-" intensifies the action, implying a thorough or complete moving or taking away.  The modern English word evolved through Middle English "remueven."
