prerefer:/ˌpriːrɪˈfɜːr/| v.|预先提及，预先参考|To mention or refer to something before it is formally introduced or discussed.|例句：The committee prereferred the matter to a subcommittee for initial review. 委员会将此事预先提交给小组委员会进行初步审查。The author prereferred to several key studies in the introduction. 作者在引言中预先参考了几项关键研究。|近义词：preface, prelude, anticipate|反义词：postpone, defer
