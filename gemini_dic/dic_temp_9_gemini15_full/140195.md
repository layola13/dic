officer:/ˈɒfɪsər/ /ˈɑːfɪsər/| n. |官员，警官，军官 | A person holding a position of authority, especially in the armed forces, police, or government.  |The police officer arrested the suspect.  警察逮捕了嫌疑犯。|The army officer gave orders to his troops.  军官向他的部队下达了命令。|agent, official, functionary | civilian, private citizen |名词

He is a police officer. = 他是一名警官。
She works as a government officer. = 她是一名政府官员。

一般过去时:
He was a high-ranking officer. = 他是一位高级官员。
