non-unified: /ˌnɒnˈjuːnɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 未统一的，不统一的 | Not unified; lacking unity or coherence.  It describes something that is not brought together into a single, whole entity or system.  | Example: The country's laws are non-unified, leading to inconsistencies in their application.  这个国家的法律不统一，导致执行不一致。 |近义词: disparate, fragmented, unintegrated, disunited | 反义词: unified, integrated, coherent, consolidated |


USAGE->

The company's marketing strategies are non-unified. = 这家公司的营销策略不统一。
These regulations are non-unified across different states. = 这些法规在不同州之间不统一。


一般过去时:
The team's efforts were non-unified, resulting in failure. = 团队的努力不统一，导致失败。
His opinions on the matter were non-unified and contradictory. = 他对此事的意见不统一且相互矛盾。
