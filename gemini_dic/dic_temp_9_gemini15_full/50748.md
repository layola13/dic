词源:

The word "derringer" is derived from the name of Henry Deringer (1786-1822), a Philadelphia gunsmith who manufactured a popular type of small pistol.  While he didn't invent the design, his name became associated with this style of firearm.  The spelling "derringer" is now more common than the original spelling "Deringer."
