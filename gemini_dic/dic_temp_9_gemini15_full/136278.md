nonreplicate: /ˌnɒnˈreplɪkeɪt/ | adj. | 非复制的，不复制的 | Not replicating or reproducing; not involving replication.  This adjective describes something that does not create copies or duplicates of itself.  It's often used in scientific and technical contexts. |

例句：

The experiment used a nonreplicate design to minimize error. = 实验使用了非复制设计以最大限度地减少误差。

The software guarantees a nonreplicate output for each unique input. = 该软件保证每个唯一输入的输出都是非复制的。


近义词：unique, unrepeated, original

反义词：replicated, duplicated, copied

词性：形容词
