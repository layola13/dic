profitability:/ˌprɒfɪtəˈbɪləti/|/ˌpraːfɪtəˈbɪləti/| n. |盈利能力；获利能力 |The ability of a business to make a profit.  It is often measured by comparing profits to revenues, assets, or equity.  High profitability indicates a successful and efficient business operation. |例句：The profitability of the company has increased significantly this year.今年公司的盈利能力显著提高。

例句：We need to improve the profitability of our products.我们需要提高我们产品的盈利能力。


近义词：profitableness,  earnings, yield, return


反义词：unprofitability, loss, deficit
