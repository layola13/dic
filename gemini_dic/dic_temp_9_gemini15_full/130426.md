nonability:/ˌnɒnəˈbɪləti/ | n. |无能，无力 |Lack of ability; inability.  It refers to the state of being unable to do something.  This word is less common than "inability" and often implies a more profound or persistent lack of ability. |例句：His nonability to cope with stress led to his breakdown.  他的压力应对无能导致了他的崩溃。
  His nonability to swim prevented him from joining the lifeguard team. 他不会游泳，这使他无法加入救生员队伍。|近义词：inability, incapacity, incompetence |反义词：ability, competence, capability |
