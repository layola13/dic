pitched-battles: /ˈpɪtʃt ˈbætəlz/ | n. | 激烈的战斗，遭遇战 |  A pitched battle is a major battle fought at a predetermined location, often involving planned strategies and large numbers of troops.  It is characterized by sustained and intense fighting.  | 例句：The two armies met in a pitched battle that lasted for days.  两军在持续数日的激烈的战斗中相遇。

The battle of Gettysburg was a bloody pitched battle.  葛底斯堡战役是一场血腥的遭遇战。


近义词：major battle, set-piece battle, engagement, conflict | 反义词：skirmish, minor engagement | 词性:noun
