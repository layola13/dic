longan:/ˈlɒŋɡæn/| n. |龙眼|A small, round fruit with a sweet, translucent flesh and a hard brown shell.  Longans are native to Southeast Asia and are often eaten fresh or used in desserts.|例句：I bought a bag of fresh longans at the market. 我在市场上买了一袋新鲜的龙眼。|例句：The dessert was made with longan and lychee. 这道甜点是用龙眼和荔枝做的。|近义词：lychee (although slightly different in taste and texture)|反义词: |
