reencouraged:/ˌriːɪnˈkɜːrɪdʒd/| v. |重新鼓励；再次鼓舞|To encourage again; to inspire renewed hope or confidence.|例句：After the setback, the coach reencouraged the team.  = 遭遇挫折后，教练重新鼓励了队员们。

例句：The positive feedback reencouraged her to pursue her artistic dreams. =  积极的反馈再次鼓舞她追求艺术梦想。


近义词：reinvigorate, reanimate, inspire anew, hearten again |反义词：discourage, dispirit, demoralize |词性：动词
