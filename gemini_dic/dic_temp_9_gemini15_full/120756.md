misapplication:/ˌmɪsˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |误用，错用，误施 |The incorrect or inappropriate use of something.  This often refers to a tool, method, or principle being used in a way that is contrary to its intended purpose or that leads to undesirable results. |例句：The misapplication of pesticides led to environmental damage.  农药的误用导致了环境破坏。

The misapplication of funds resulted in the project's failure.  资金的误用导致了项目的失败。


近义词：abuse, misuse, malapplication, wrong use |反义词：correct application, proper use, appropriate use |词性：名词
