icings:/ˈaɪsɪŋz/| n. |糖霜；结冰；霜状物 |A sweet, often creamy, topping for cakes and other desserts.  It can be made from sugar, butter, eggs, and other ingredients.  Icings are used to decorate and add flavor to baked goods. |例句：The cake was decorated with beautiful, colorful icings.  这款蛋糕装饰着漂亮多彩的糖霜。

The thick icing on the gingerbread cookies made them extra delicious. 姜饼上的厚厚糖霜使它们格外美味。|近义词：frosting, glaze |反义词：bare, undecorated |名词
