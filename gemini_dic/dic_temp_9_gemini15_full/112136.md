The word "look" is not "loock".  There is no word "loock" in English.  Assuming you meant "look", here's the analysis:

look:/lʊk/ /lʌk/ | v. n. | 看，瞧；外观；样子 | To direct one's gaze towards something; to appear a certain way.  |例句：Look at that beautiful bird! 看那只美丽的鸟！  I like the look of that dress. 我喜欢那件衣服的样子。|近义词：see, gaze, glance, appear, seem |反义词：ignore, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I look at the sunset every evening. = 我每天晚上都看日落。
They look happy together. = 他们在一起看起来很幸福。


一般过去时:
He looked at the map carefully. = 他仔细地看了看地图。
She looked tired after the long journey. = 长途旅行后，她看起来很疲惫。


现在完成时:
I have looked for my keys everywhere. = 我到处都找过我的钥匙了。
They have looked very busy lately. = 最近他们看起来很忙。


过去完成时:
I had looked at the report before the meeting. = 我在会议之前已经看过报告了。
She had looked forward to the vacation for months. = 她几个几个月都期待着这次假期。


将来时:
I will look for a new job next year. = 明年我将寻找一份新工作。
We will look back on this day fondly. = 我们将 fondly 回忆这一天。


虚拟语气:
If I looked harder, I might find it. = 如果我再仔细找找，也许就能找到了。
I wish I looked better in that photo. = 我希望在那张照片里我看起来更好。


被动语态:
The painting was looked at by many people. = 这幅画被很多人观看过。
The problem needs to be looked into carefully. = 这个问题需要仔细调查。


疑问句:
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
How does it look to you? = 你觉得它怎么样？


否定句:
I don't look my age. = 我看起来不像我的年龄。
He didn't look at me when I spoke. = 我说话时，他没看我。
