hypergenesis:/ˌhaɪpərˈdʒɛnəsɪs/| n. |超生成；过度生成；过度发育|The excessive or abnormal development of an organ or tissue.  It refers to a process where something grows or develops beyond its normal limits. This can apply to various fields, including biology and geology.|Example:  The rapid hypergenesis of the tumor necessitated immediate surgical intervention.  肿瘤的快速过度生长需要立即进行手术干预。|近义词：overgrowth, overproduction, excessive development|反义词：underdevelopment, atrophy, hypoplasia|
