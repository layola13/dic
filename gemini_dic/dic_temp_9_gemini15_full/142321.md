orthodoxness:/ˈɔːθədɒksnəs/ | n. | 正统性；正教性；保守性 | The state or quality of being orthodox; adherence to established doctrines, beliefs, or practices.  It often implies a resistance to change or innovation. |  The orthodoxness of his views surprised many of his more liberal colleagues.  他的观点的正统性让许多更自由的同事感到惊讶。|  conformity, conventionality, traditionalism |  heterodoxy, radicalism, innovation
