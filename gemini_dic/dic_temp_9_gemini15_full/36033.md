codiscovered:/ˌkoʊdɪˈskʌvərd/| v. |共同发现，合作发现 |To discover something together or at the same time as someone else.  |Example:  The two scientists co-discovered a new species of bacteria.  两位科学家共同发现了一种新的细菌。|近义词: jointly discovered, discovered together |反义词:  concealed, hidden


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They co-discover new planets every year. = 他们每年都共同发现新的行星。
We co-discover interesting facts during our research. = 我们在研究中共同发现了有趣的事实。


一般过去时:
The researchers co-discovered a new cure for cancer. = 研究人员共同发现了一种新的癌症疗法。
We co-discovered the truth behind the mystery. = 我们共同发现了这个谜团背后的真相。


现在完成时:
They have co-discovered several important archaeological artifacts. = 他们已经共同发现了几个重要的考古文物。

过去完成时:
