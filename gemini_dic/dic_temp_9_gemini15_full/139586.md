obviators:/ˈɒbvieɪtərz/ | n. |消除者；防止者；避免者 |One who or that which obviates.  Someone or something that removes a need or difficulty. |例句：The new software is an obviator of many previous problems. 新软件消除了许多以前的问题。  The invention of the airplane was an obviator of long sea voyages. 飞机的发明避免了漫长的海上航行。|近义词：remover, eliminator, preventer, avoider |反义词：aggravator, exacerbator


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new system obviates the need for manual data entry. = 新系统消除了手动数据录入的需要。
This design obviates many potential problems. = 这个设计避免了许多潜在的问题。


一般过去时:
The invention of the printing press obviated the need for hand-copying books. = 印刷术的发明消除了手工抄写书籍的需要。
His careful planning obviated any potential delays. = 他细致的计划避免了任何潜在的延误。


现在完成时:
Technological advancements have obviated many of the challenges faced by previous generations. = 技术进步消除了前几代人面临的许多挑战。
The new policy has obviated the need for further negotiations. = 新政策消除了进一步谈判的需要。


过去完成时:
They had obviated the risk by taking precautions. = 他们通过采取预防措施避免了风险。
The early warning system had obviated a potential disaster. = 预警系统避免了一场潜在的灾难。


将来时:
The new bridge will obviate the need for a long detour. = 新桥将消除绕行很长时间的需要。
This technology will obviate the need for manual labor. =这项技术将消除对体力劳动的需求。


虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, we could have obviated the crisis. = 如果我们早点采取行动，就能避免这场危机。
If only they had obviated the problem earlier. = 他们要是早点解决这个问题就好了。


被动语态:
The problem was obviated by a quick solution. = 问题被一个快速的解决方案解决了。
The difficulties were obviated through careful planning. = 通过周密的计划，困难被克服了。


疑问句:
Can this new technology obviate the need for human intervention? = 这项新技术能否消除对人工干预的需求？
Does this solution obviate all the risks? = 这种解决方案能消除所有风险吗？


否定句:
The current system does not obviate the need for regular maintenance. = 当前的系统并不能消除定期维护的需要。
Unfortunately, this approach did not obviate the underlying issues. = 不幸的是，这种方法并没有解决根本问题。
