cites:/saɪts/ | v. | 引用；表彰；提及 | To mention something as proof, support, or example.  | The professor cites several studies to support his theory. 教授引用了几项研究来支持他的理论。 | quotes, mentions, refers to |  omits, ignores | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cites many sources in his paper. = 他在他的论文中引用了许多资料。
She cites the latest research findings. = 她引用了最新的研究结果。

一般过去时:
The lawyer cited a precedent in his argument. = 律师在他的论证中引用了一个先例。
The article cited several experts on the topic. = 这篇文章引用了几位该领域的专家。

现在完成时:
The author has cited this work many times. = 作者多次引用过这项作品。
The scientist has cited numerous studies in her report. = 科学家在她的报告中引用了许多研究。

过去完成时:
He had cited that article in his previous publication. = 他在他之前的出版物中引用过那篇文章。
She had cited several historical events to illustrate her point. = 她引用了好几个历史事件来说明她的观点。


将来时:
He will cite new evidence in his upcoming presentation. = 他将在即将到来的演讲中引用新的证据。
She will cite relevant case law in her legal brief. = 她将在她的法律简报中引用相关的案例法。


虚拟语气:
If he cited that study, his argument would be stronger. = 如果他引用那项研究，他的论点会更有力。
I wish she had cited more recent sources. = 我希望她能引用更多近期的资料。


被动语态:
This study has been cited extensively. = 这项研究已被广泛引用。
His work was cited by many other researchers. = 他的作品被许多其他研究人员引用。


疑问句:
Which sources did she cite in her dissertation? = 她在她的论文中引用了哪些资料？
What studies did the article cite to support its claims? = 这篇文章引用了哪些研究来支持它的说法？


否定句:
He did not cite any sources in his essay. = 他在论文中没有引用任何资料。
The report failed to cite the relevant legislation. = 该报告未能引用相关的立法。



PHRASE->
cite as = 引用为...
cite evidence = 引用证据
cite a source = 引用来源
cite an example = 举例说明
frequently cited = 经常被引用
