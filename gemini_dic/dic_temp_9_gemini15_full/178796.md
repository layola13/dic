recur:/rɪˈkɜːr/| /rɪˈkɝː/| v.|再发生，反复出现|To happen or occur again, especially after a period of time.|The problem of poverty keeps recurring in many societies. 贫困问题在许多社会中不断反复出现。  The theme of betrayal recurs throughout the novel. 背叛的主题贯穿整部小说。|reappear, repeat, reiterate|cease, end, stop|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem recurs frequently. = 这个问题经常反复出现。
This pattern recurs throughout history. = 这种模式贯穿于整个历史。

一般过去时:
The pain recurred after a few days. = 几天后疼痛又复发了。
The same mistake recurred in his work. = 他工作中同样的错误又出现了。

现在完成时:
The symptoms have recurred several times. = 症状已经反复出现好几次了。
The disease has recurred after a period of remission. = 疾病在缓解期后又复发了。

过去完成时:
The problem had recurred before they found a solution. = 在他们找到解决方案之前，问题已经反复出现过。
He had recurred to that old trick again. = 他又故伎重演了。

将来时:
I fear the problem will recur next year. = 我担心这个问题明年还会再次出现。
The symptoms may recur in the future. = 症状将来可能会再次出现。

虚拟语气:
If the problem recurred, we would have to find a new solution. = 如果问题再次出现，我们将不得不找到一个新的解决方案。
I wish the pain wouldn't recur. = 我希望疼痛不会再次发生。

被动语态:
The problem was recurred to in the meeting. = 会议中再次提到了这个问题。(被动语态用法较少见，通常用主动语态)
The topic was recurred to frequently in the discussion. = 在讨论中经常反复提到这个话题。(被动语态用法较少见，通常用主动语态)

疑问句:
Will the problem recur? = 这个问题还会再次出现吗？
Does the pain recur often? = 疼痛经常复发吗？

否定句:
The problem did not recur. = 问题没有再次出现。
The symptoms did not recur after treatment. = 治疗后症状没有复发。


PHRASE->
recur again = 再次发生
recur frequently = 频繁发生
recur to = 回到；求助于
recur with = 伴随...再次出现
recurrence of = ...的复发
