hydrocele:/ˈhaɪdroʊˌsiːl/| n. |精液囊肿，鞘膜积液 |A hydrocele is a swelling in the scrotum that occurs when fluid collects in the thin sheath surrounding a testicle.  It's usually painless and often goes away on its own, especially in infants.  |Example: The doctor diagnosed the baby with a hydrocele.  医生诊断婴儿患有鞘膜积液。 The athlete experienced a painful hydrocele after a strenuous workout. 运动员在剧烈运动后出现了疼痛的鞘膜积液。 |近义词： scrotal swelling, testicular swelling |反义词：  None specifically, but the absence of a hydrocele could be described as a normal scrotum.
