reenkindle: /ˌriːˈkɪndl/ | vt. |重新点燃；复燃；重新激起 | To reawaken or revive something, especially an emotion, feeling, or activity.  |例句：The unexpected reunion reenkindled their old romance.  意外的重逢重新燃起了他们过去的浪漫情缘。
例句：The success of the first project reenkindled their enthusiasm for the next. 第一个项目的成功重新激发了他们对下一个项目的热情。|近义词：reignite, revive, reawaken, rekindle |反义词：extinguish, quench, suppress
