nonactivists: /ˌnɒnˈæktɪvɪsts/ | n. |非活动分子，不积极分子 |People who do not participate in activism or political action.  They may be apathetic, unconcerned, or simply choose not to engage in such activities. | Example:  The survey revealed a significant portion of the population were nonactivists, indifferent to the political climate.  调查显示，相当一部分人口是非积极分子，对政治气候漠不关心。|近义词：bystanders, onlookers, passive observers |反义词：activists, campaigners, protesters
