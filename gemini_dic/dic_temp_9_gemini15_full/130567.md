nonactivated: /ˌnɒnˈæktɪveɪtɪd/ | adj. | 未激活的，未启动的 | Not activated; not made active or operational.  The term describes something that is in a state of inactivity or dormancy, awaiting activation to become functional. | Example: The software remains nonactivated until you enter the license key.  未激活的软件直到您输入许可证密钥后才能使用。 The emergency system was nonactivated during the drill.  演习期间紧急系统未启动。 |近义词：inactive, dormant, quiescent, latent |反义词：activated, active, operational
