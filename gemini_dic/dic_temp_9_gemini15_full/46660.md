damoiselle:/ˌdæməˈzɛl/| n. |贵妇，少女，（尤指）年轻女子 |A young unmarried woman, especially one of noble birth.  The term often carries a connotation of refinement and elegance. |例句：The damoiselle curtsied gracefully before the king.  这位贵妇人优雅地向国王行屈膝礼。

例句：He rescued the distressed damoiselle from the burning building. 他从燃烧的建筑物中救出了那位遇险的少女。


近义词：maiden, young woman, lady, girl


反义词：None directly applicable, but  "crone" or "hag" could be considered antonyms in terms of age and implied youthfulness and grace.


词性: noun
