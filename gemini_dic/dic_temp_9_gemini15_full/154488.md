peroxiding:/pəˈrɒksɪdɪŋ/| v.| 过氧化作用；用过氧化物处理 | The process of treating something with peroxide, typically to bleach or disinfect.  | Example:  She was peroxiding her hair to lighten it. = 她正在用过氧化物漂白头发。

She's peroxiding the wound to disinfect it. = 她正在用过氧化物消毒伤口。


近义词: bleaching, disinfecting, oxidizing


反义词: reducing (in a chemical context)


词性:动词
