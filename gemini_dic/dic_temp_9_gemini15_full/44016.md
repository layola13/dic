criminologist:/ˌkrɪmɪˈnɒlədʒɪst/|/ˈkrɪmɪnɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |犯罪学家|A criminologist is a social scientist who studies crime and criminals. They analyze the causes of crime, the characteristics of offenders, and the effectiveness of crime prevention and control strategies.  Criminologists often work in universities, government agencies, or law enforcement. |The criminologist presented her findings on the recent increase in cybercrime.  犯罪学家展示了她关于近期网络犯罪增长的研究结果。|sociologist, forensic scientist|—|
