calcutta:/kælˈkʌtə/| n. |加尔各答|Calcutta is the former name of Kolkata, a city in India.  It was the capital of British India for a long time and a major center of trade and culture. The name's origin is debated, but it's likely derived from the name of a village or settlement near the Hooghly River.  The city is known for its rich history, colonial architecture, and vibrant cultural scene. |The British changed the name of Calcutta to Kolkata in 2001. |Kolkata,  Kolkota|  (No direct antonym, as it's a proper noun)|名词
