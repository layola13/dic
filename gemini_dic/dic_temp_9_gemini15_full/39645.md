conservancy: /kɒnˈsɜːvənsi/ | /kənˈsɜːrvənsi/ | n. |保护区；保护协会；保护管理 |

英文解析：An area of land, water, or both, set aside for the protection of natural resources and wildlife.  It can also refer to an organization dedicated to conservation efforts.

例句：

The wildlife conservancy protects endangered species. |  野生动物保护区保护濒危物种。

The river conservancy is working to improve water quality. | 河流保护协会正在努力改善水质。


近义词：reserve, sanctuary, preservation, protection

反义词：destruction, devastation, depletion

词源:

The word "conservancy" comes from the Latin word "conservare," meaning "to preserve" or "to keep safe."  The suffix "-ancy" indicates a state or quality. Therefore, "conservancy" literally means "the state of being preserved" or "an act of preserving."
