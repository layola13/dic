incorporealism:/ˌɪnkɔːrˈpɔːriəlɪzəm/| n. |非实体论；非物质主义|The philosophical belief that reality is fundamentally non-physical or immaterial.  It emphasizes the primacy of mind or spirit over matter. This can manifest in various forms, from idealism to spiritualism.  |Example:  His artistic expression was a manifestation of his deep-seated incorporealism, focusing on the ethereal and intangible aspects of existence.  他的艺术表达是他根深蒂固的非实体论的表现，关注存在中的空灵和非物质方面。|近义词：idealism, spiritualism, immaterialism|反义词：materialism, physicalism|名词
