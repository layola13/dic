reinitiating:/ˌriːɪˈnɪʃieɪtɪŋ/ | v. |重新开始，重新启动 |  The act of starting something again after it has been stopped or interrupted.  It implies a resumption of a previously existing process or activity. |例句：The company is reinitiating its marketing campaign after a brief hiatus. 公司在短暂休整后重新启动了营销活动。

例句：After the system crash, the technicians are reinitiating the server. 系统崩溃后，技术人员正在重新启动服务器。


近义词：restart, recommence, resume, begin again | 反义词：terminate, cease, end, discontinue | 词性:动词
