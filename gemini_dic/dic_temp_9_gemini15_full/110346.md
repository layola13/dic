lightless:/ˈlaɪtləs/| adj.|无光的；黑暗的；没有光明的|Without light; dark; lacking brightness or illumination.  The adjective describes a state of absence of light, either literally or metaphorically.|例句：The cellar was lightless and damp. 地窖又黑又潮湿。

例句：The future seemed lightless after her death. 她死后，未来似乎一片黯淡。|近义词：dark, shadowy, unlit, obscure|反义词：bright, illuminated, luminous, radiant
