reinterview:/ˌriːˈɪntəvjuː/ | vt. | 再面试；重新面试 | To interview someone again.  This often happens when there's a need for further clarification, additional information, or a second opinion on a candidate. | Example: The committee decided to reinterview the top three candidates.  委员会决定再次面试前三名候选人。

The company decided to reinterview all applicants after the initial screening process was deemed insufficient.  公司决定在初步筛选过程被认为不足后再次面试所有申请人。

|近义词：interview again,  reinvestigate (in a broader context)|反义词： N/A |动词|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reinterview candidates every year. = 他们每年都会重新面试候选人。
The HR department reinterviews promising applicants. = 人力资源部门会重新面试有潜力的申请人。

一般过去时:
The manager reinterviewed the candidate after receiving new information. = 经理在收到新信息后重新面试了候选人。
We reinterviewed several individuals for the position. = 我们为这个职位重新面试了几个人。

现在完成时:
They have reinterviewed all the shortlisted candidates. = 他们已经重新面试了所有入围的候选人。
The hiring manager has reinterviewed John twice. = 聘用经理已经两次重新面试了约翰。


过去完成时:
The panel had reinterviewed the applicant before making a final decision. = 面试小组在做出最终决定之前已经重新面试了申请人。
Before the final decision, they had reinterviewed the top two candidates. = 在最终决定之前，他们已经重新面试了前两名候选人。


将来时:
They will reinterview the candidates next week. = 他们下周将重新面试候选人。
The company will reinterview the applicant following the background check. = 公司将在背景调查后重新面试申请人。


虚拟语气:
If they had more time, they would reinterview all the applicants. = 如果他们有更多时间，他们就会重新面试所有申请人。
I wish they would reinterview the candidate; I have new information. = 我希望他们能重新面试这位候选人；我有一些新的信息。


被动语态:
The candidate was reinterviewed by the hiring manager. = 候选人被招聘经理重新面试了。
All applicants will be reinterviewed next week. = 所有申请人下周都将被重新面试。


疑问句:
Will they reinterview the candidates? = 他们会重新面试候选人吗？
Should we reinterview him? = 我们应该重新面试他吗？


否定句:
They did not reinterview the candidate. = 他们没有重新面试候选人。
The company will not reinterview any applicants without a valid reason. = 公司没有正当理由不会重新面试任何申请人。


PHRASE->
reinterview for a position = 为某个职位重新面试
reinterview a candidate = 重新面试一位候选人
reinterview the applicant = 重新面试申请人
be reinterviewed for a job = 被重新面试以获得工作
undergo a reinterview = 经历重新面试


词源:
re- (prefix indicating repetition or again) + interview (verb meaning to conduct a formal meeting with someone to assess their suitability)
