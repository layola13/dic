counter:/ˈkaʊntər/ | n., v. |柜台；计数器；反驳；对抗；| 英文解析：As a noun, a counter refers to a flat surface over which transactions are conducted, a device for counting, or something that opposes or contrasts.  As a verb, it means to express opposition to a claim or argument, to act in opposition to something, or to count. |例句：The bank teller worked behind the counter. (柜员在柜台后面工作。)  He used a counter to track his steps. (他用计数器追踪他的步数。) She countered his argument with evidence. (她用证据反驳了他的论点。) He countered the opponent's attack. (他反击了对手的进攻。) |近义词：(n.)  desk, table, register,  (v.) oppose, refute, contradict |反义词：(v.) support, agree, concur
