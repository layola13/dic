disrobed:/dɪsˈroʊbd/| v. |脱衣服的；赤裸的|To remove one's clothing; to undress.  The word implies a removal of clothing, often in a more formal or deliberate manner than simply "undressing".|例句：He disrobed before entering the bath. 他在进入浴室前脱掉了衣服。

例句：The statue depicted a disrobed woman.  雕像描绘了一个赤裸的女人。


近义词：undressed, unclothed, nude, naked


反义词：clothed, dressed


词性: Verb
