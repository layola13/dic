nonwithdrawable: /ˌnɒn wɪðˈdrɔːəbl/ | adj. | 不可提取的，不可支取的 |  Describes funds or assets that cannot be withdrawn or accessed easily or immediately.  This often applies to financial accounts or investments with restrictions on access. |  Example:  The retirement fund is nonwithdrawable until age 65.  退休基金在65岁之前不可提取。

The money in this account is nonwithdrawable until the end of the year.  这个账户里的钱在年底之前不可提取。| 近义词: inaccessible, unavailable, unwithdrawable | 反义词: withdrawable, accessible
