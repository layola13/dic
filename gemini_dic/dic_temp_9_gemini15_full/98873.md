intendedness: /ɪnˈtɛndɪdnəs/ | n. |意图性，故意性 |The quality or state of being intended; the fact of having been planned or purpose.  It refers to the deliberate nature of an action or event, implying a conscious choice or plan behind it.  It contrasts with accident or chance. |例句：The intendedness of his actions was clear from the precision of his movements.  他的行动的意图性从他动作的精准度就可以看出来。

The intendedness of the legislation was to protect consumers. 这部法律的意图是保护消费者。


近义词：purposefulness, design, intentionality, deliberation |反义词：accident, chance, randomness, fortuity |词性: 名词
