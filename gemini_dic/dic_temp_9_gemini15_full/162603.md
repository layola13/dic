preannexes:/ˌpriːˈæneksɪz/| v. |预先吞并；预先附加|To annex beforehand; to add something before something else.  The term suggests an action taken in anticipation of a larger event or process.|例句：The empire preannexed several smaller territories before initiating the main invasion.  帝国在发动主要侵略之前预先吞并了几个较小的领土。

例句：The company preannexed the relevant clauses to the contract before sending it to the client. 公司在将合同发送给客户之前预先附加了相关条款。


近义词：precede, preface, attach beforehand


反义词：postpone, detach, append afterward

词性：动词
