evacuee:/ˌɛvəˈkuˌiː/| n. |疏散人员，撤离者 |A person who has been evacuated from a place of danger.  |例句：The evacuees were given temporary shelter in a nearby school. 疏散人员被安置在附近的一所学校里作为临时住所。  |例句：Many evacuees lost everything in the fire. 许多疏散人员在火灾中失去了一切。|近义词：refugee, displaced person |反义词：resident, settler


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government evacuates citizens from the disaster zone. = 政府将公民从灾区疏散。
He evacuates his home before the hurricane arrives. = 他在飓风到来之前撤离了他的家。


一般过去时:
The city evacuated thousands of people during the flood. = 在洪水期间，城市疏散了数千人。
They evacuated the building after the bomb threat. = 炸弹威胁后，他们疏散了大楼。


现在完成时:
The authorities have evacuated all residents from the affected area. = 当局已将受灾地区的所有居民疏散。
We have evacuated our pets to a safer location. = 我们已将我们的宠物转移到更安全的地方。


过去完成时:
The villagers had evacuated their homes before the earthquake struck. = 在地震发生之前，村民们已经撤离了他们的家园。
They had evacuated the hospital patients to another facility. = 他们已将医院的病人转移到另一个设施。


将来时:
The army will evacuate the injured soldiers from the battlefield. = 军队将把受伤的士兵从战场上撤离。
We will evacuate the building if there is a fire. = 如果发生火灾，我们将疏散大楼。


虚拟语气:
If there were a fire, we would evacuate immediately. = 如果发生火灾，我们将立即疏散。
It would have been better if they had evacuated earlier. = 如果他们早点撤离就好了。


被动语态:
The town was evacuated overnight. = 该镇在一夜之间被疏散了。
The residents were evacuated by bus. = 居民们被公共汽车疏散。


疑问句:
Were all the residents evacuated? = 所有居民都撤离了吗？
Have the animals been evacuated yet? = 动物们已经被疏散了吗？


否定句:
They did not evacuate the building in time. = 他们没有及时疏散大楼。
The people were not evacuated quickly enough. = 人们没有被及时疏散。
