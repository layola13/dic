escapism:/ɪˈskeɪpɪzəm/| n. |逃避现实；逃避主义|The act of escaping from unpleasant realities, often by indulging in entertainment or fantasy.  It's a way to temporarily forget worries and anxieties. |例句：Reading fantasy novels is a form of escapism for her.  阅读奇幻小说是她逃避现实的一种方式。


例句：He uses video games as escapism from the pressures of his job. 他用电子游戏来逃避工作压力。


近义词：withdrawal, avoidance, daydreaming, fantasy


反义词：realism, facing reality, engagement


词性: 名词
