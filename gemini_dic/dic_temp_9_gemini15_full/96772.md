inequilateral:/ˌɪniˈkwɪlætərəl/| adj.|不等边的，不等角的|Having sides or angles of unequal length or measure.  An inequilateral triangle has sides of different lengths and angles of different measures.|例句：The triangle drawn on the blackboard is inequilateral.  黑板上画的三角形是不等边的。

例句：This inequilateral polygon has five sides of varying lengths. 这个不等边多边形有五个长度不同的边。


近义词：unequal-sided, dissimilar-sided, irregular


反义词：equilateral


词性：形容词
