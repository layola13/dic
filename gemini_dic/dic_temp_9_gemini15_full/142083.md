oriel:/ˈɔːriəl/ | n. |凸窗，耳窗 | A bay window projecting from the wall of a building, especially one supported by a bracket or corbel.  An oriel window is a type of bay window that projects from the wall of a building and is supported by a bracket or corbel, rather than by a supporting wall below.  It often has a decorative appearance and provides extra light and space to a room. | Example: The oriel window in the library offered a stunning view of the garden.  图书馆的凸窗提供了花园的壮丽景色。The old house had a charming oriel window overlooking the street. 那栋老房子有一个迷人的凸窗俯瞰着街道。 | 近义词：bay window, projecting window | 反义词：recessed window
