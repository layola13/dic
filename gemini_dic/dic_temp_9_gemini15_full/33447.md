churned:/tʃɜːrnd/| v.|搅拌；翻腾；焦虑不安|To stir or mix (something) vigorously; to agitate or disturb violently; to feel or cause to feel great anxiety or distress.|例句：She churned the butter by hand. 她用手搅黄油。|例句：The sea churned violently in the storm.暴风雨中大海汹涌澎湃。|近义词：stir, agitate, mix, toss|反义词：calm, still, peaceful
