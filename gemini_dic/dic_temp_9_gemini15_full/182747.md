reparative:/rɪˈpærətɪv/| adj.|修复的；矫正的；弥补的 |  relating to or aiming at the repair or correction of something.  Often used in the context of therapy aimed at repairing emotional damage. | 例句：The reparative therapy aimed to heal the emotional wounds of the past.  修复疗法旨在治愈过去的感情创伤。

The program offered reparative services to victims of the disaster. 该项目为灾难受害者提供修复服务。|近义词：corrective, remedial, restorative |反义词：destructive, damaging
