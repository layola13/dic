indopacific:/ˌɪndoʊpəˈsɪfɪk/ | adj. | 印度洋-太平洋地区的；印太地区的 | Relating to the geographical area encompassing the Indian Ocean and the Pacific Ocean.  This term is increasingly used in geopolitical contexts to refer to the interconnected region spanning from the western coast of India to the western coast of the Americas. | The Indo-Pacific region faces significant challenges in terms of security and economic stability. 印度洋-太平洋地区在安全和经济稳定方面面临重大挑战。 | 近义词: Asia-Pacific (less encompassing), Indo-Pacific region | 反义词: N/A
