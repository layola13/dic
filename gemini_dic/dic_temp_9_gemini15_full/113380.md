lycopodite:/ˈlaɪkəpədaɪt/| n. |石松类植物；石松；卷柏|A lycopodite is a plant belonging to the Lycopodiopsida, a class of vascular plants that includes clubmosses, spike mosses, and quillworts.  They are characterized by their small, scale-like leaves and often branching stems. Many are epiphytes, growing on other plants. |Example: The ancient forest floor was carpeted with a thick layer of lycopodite.  古老的森林地面覆盖着一层厚厚的石松。  The researcher identified several species of lycopodite in the remote mountain region. 研究人员在偏远山区发现了几个石松物种。|近义词：club moss, spike moss, quillwort |反义词：  (没有直接的反义词，可根据语境用flowering plant等作对比)|名词
