fuses:/ˈfjuːzɪz/| n. |保险丝；熔丝；导火线| a safety device that melts and breaks an electric circuit if the current becomes too strong.  It protects electrical equipment from damage.  |Example: The fuse blew because of the overload.  保险丝烧断了是因为超负荷。
The old house was a tinderbox, waiting for a single spark to ignite the fuses and set it ablaze. 这栋老房子像个火药桶，只等火星一点燃导火线就将熊熊燃烧。|近义词：safety device, circuit breaker |反义词：N/A
