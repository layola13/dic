engineless:/ˈɛnʒɪnlɛs/| adj.|无引擎的，不带发动机的|Lacking an engine.  This adjective describes something that does not have an engine.|例句：The engineless glider soared effortlessly through the sky.  这架无动力滑翔机轻松地翱翔于天空。

例句：The engineless cart was pushed uphill by several strong men.  几个壮汉把这辆无引擎的手推车推上了山坡。


近义词：motorless, powerless (in the context of engines)
反义词：engine-powered, motorized


词性:形容词
