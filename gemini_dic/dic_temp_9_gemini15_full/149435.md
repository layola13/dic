/pæks/| n. |图象存档与通讯系统；PACS|Picture Archiving and Communication System.  A medical imaging technology which provides digital storage and retrieval of medical images, such as X-rays and CT scans.  It allows healthcare professionals to access and share images from different locations.|例句：The hospital implemented a new PACS to improve image management and collaboration among doctors.  这家医院实施了新的PACS系统以改善图像管理和医生之间的协作。|近义词：medical imaging system, image management system|反义词：None|名词
