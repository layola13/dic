rearms:/ˌriːˈɑːrmz/ | v. | 重整军备；重新武装 | To provide (someone or something) with weapons again; to re-equip with arms.  | The country is rearming after the conflict. 这个国家冲突后正在重新武装。  | re-equip, rearm oneself, refit | disarm | v.
