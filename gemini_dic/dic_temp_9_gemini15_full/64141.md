equaling:/ˈiːkwəlɪŋ/| v.|等于，相当于|The verb "equaling" means to be equal to or the same as something else in quantity, value, or degree.  It's the present participle of the verb "equal."|例句：The sum of two and two equaling four.  二加二等于四。
The team's performance equaled their expectations. 球队的表现达到了他们的预期。|近义词：equal to, equivalent to, matching|反义词：exceeding, surpassing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The total cost is equaling $100.  总成本等于100美元。
His effort is equaling his ambition.他的努力与他的雄心相符。


一般过去时:
The number of attendees equaled last year's. 出席人数与去年相同。
Her score equaled the highest in the class.她的分数等于班上最高的。


现在完成时:
The contributions have equaled the target amount. 捐款已达到目标金额。
The damage has equaled the previous year's. 损失与前一年相当。


过去完成时:
The damage had equaled the insurance coverage. 损失已达到保险范围。
By the end of the year, the sales had equaled our projections.到年底，销售额已达到我们的预期。


将来时:
The population will equal one million by 2050. 到2050年，人口将达到100万。
Their efforts will equal the challenge.他们的努力将与挑战相当。


虚拟语气:
If the results equaled our predictions, we would be very happy. 如果结果与我们的预测相符，我们会非常高兴。
I wish my income equaled my expenses.我希望我的收入等于我的支出。


被动语态:
The task was equaled only by his dedication.这项任务只有他的奉献精神才能与之匹敌。(注意被动语态用得较少)
The difficulty of the problem was equaled by its importance.问题的难度与其重要性相当。(注意被动语态用得较少)


疑问句:
Does the area equaling this one? 这个面积与那个面积相等吗？
Does the length of this rope equaling that of the other? 这根绳子的长度与那根绳子的长度相等吗？


否定句:
The two values do not equal each other. 这两个值不相等。
His performance did not equal expectations. 他的表现没有达到预期。
