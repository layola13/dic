homebody:/ˈhoʊmˌbɑːdi/| n. |宅人，居家的人 |A person who prefers to stay at home rather than go out.  |She's a homebody and rarely goes out. 她是个宅女，很少出门。|近义词：stay-at-home, home lover |反义词：social butterfly, extrovert

She stays home most weekends. = 她大多数周末都待在家里。

He was a homebody in his younger years. = 他年轻时是个宅男。
