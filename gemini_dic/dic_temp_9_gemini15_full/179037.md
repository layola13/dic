redemocratized:/ˌriːdɪˈmɒkrətaɪzd/ | v. |使重新民主化；恢复民主 | To restore democratic principles and processes to a country or region after a period of authoritarian rule or instability.  The process often involves establishing free and fair elections, protecting human rights, and promoting the rule of law. | 例句：After the fall of the dictator, the country was slowly redemocratized.  = 独裁者垮台后，这个国家逐渐恢复了民主。

The country has been successfully redemocratized after decades of military rule. = 在几十年的军事统治后，这个国家成功地实现了重新民主化。| 近义词：democratize, liberalize, revitalize | 反义词：authoritarianize, totalitarianism,  dictatorialize
