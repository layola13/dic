cougar:/ˈkuːɡər/| n.|美洲狮，山狮|A cougar is a large, wild cat found in the Americas.  It is also known as a mountain lion, puma, panther, or catamount.|例句：The cougar stalked its prey through the dense forest.  美洲狮在茂密的森林中潜伏着猎物。
例句：Sightings of cougars are rare in this area.  在这个地区很少看到美洲狮。|近义词：puma, mountain lion, panther, catamount|反义词：None directly applicable.
