chestnut:/ˈtʃɛsˌnʌt/|/ˈtʃɛsˌnʌt/| n. adj.| 栗子；栗色；栗棕色的|A chestnut is the nut of the chestnut tree, typically brown and encased in a spiky shell. It can also refer to a reddish-brown color. |例句：The roasted chestnuts smelled delicious. 烤栗子的香味真诱人。

The horse had a beautiful chestnut coat. 这匹马有着美丽的栗色毛发。|近义词：maroon, brown, hazel|反义词：pale
