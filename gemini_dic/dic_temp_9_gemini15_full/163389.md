preconsideration: /ˌpriːkənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 预先考虑，预想 | The act of considering something beforehand; prior consideration.  It involves thinking about something before it happens, anticipating potential issues, and planning accordingly. | Example:  The preconsideration of safety measures prevented a serious accident.  预先考虑安全措施避免了一场严重的事故。  |近义词: forethought, anticipation, premeditation | 反义词: impulsiveness, spontaneity | 名词
