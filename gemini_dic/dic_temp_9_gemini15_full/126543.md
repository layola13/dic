nacre:/ˈneɪkər/| n. |珍珠母，珠光；云母|The iridescent inner layer of a mollusk shell,  characterized by its smooth, pearly surface.  It is often used in jewelry and other decorative items. |例句：The inside of the oyster shell shimmered with beautiful nacre.  牡蛎壳内侧闪耀着美丽的珍珠母光泽。|例句：The buttons on his coat were made of genuine nacre. 他外套上的扣子是用真正的珍珠母制成的。|近义词：pearl, mother-of-pearl |反义词：N/A
