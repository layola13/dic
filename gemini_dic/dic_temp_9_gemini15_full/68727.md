farm:/fɑːrm/| /fɑːrm/| n. v.|农场；耕作；饲养|A farm is an area of land used for growing crops or raising livestock.  It can range from a small family-run operation to a large industrial complex.  Farming involves various activities such as planting, harvesting, and caring for animals.|例句：He works on a dairy farm. 他在一个奶牛场工作。|例句：They farm rice in this region.  他们在这一地区种植水稻。|近义词：ranch, plantation, estate|反义词：city, urban area|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They farm wheat every year. = 他们每年种植小麦。
We farm our own vegetables. = 我们自己种植蔬菜。

一般过去时:
He farmed that land for twenty years. = 他耕种那块地二十年了。
My grandfather farmed this land. = 我爷爷耕种过这块地。

现在完成时:
They have farmed this land for generations. = 他们世世代代耕种这块土地。
We have farmed organically for five years. = 我们已经进行有机耕作五年了。

过去完成时:
They had farmed the land before the drought hit. = 干旱来临之前，他们就已经耕种这块地了。
He had farmed successfully for many years before retiring. = 他退休前成功耕作了很多年。

将来时:
They will farm a larger area next year. = 他们明年将耕种更大的面积。
We will farm more sustainably in the future. = 未来我们将更可持续地耕作。

虚拟语气:
If I had a farm, I would grow apples. = 如果我有一个农场，我会种植苹果。
I wish I could farm my own land. = 我希望我能耕种自己的土地。

被动语态:
The land was farmed for centuries. = 这块土地被耕种了几个世纪。
The crops are farmed using modern techniques. = 这些作物是用现代技术种植的。

疑问句:
Do they farm coffee beans? = 他们种植咖啡豆吗？
What do you farm on your land? = 你在你的土地上种植什么？

否定句:
He doesn't farm anymore. = 他不再务农了。
They didn't farm that field last year. = 他们去年没有耕种那块地。
