demain:/dəˈmæ̃/| n.|明天|Tomorrow.  It refers to the next day after the current day.|例句：I will go to the park demain. 我明天要去公园。|近义词：tomorrow, the following day|反义词：yesterday, today


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I usually wake up early demain. = 我通常明天早起。
Demain is a weekday. = 明天是工作日。


一般过去时:
We planned to leave demain. = 我们计划明天离开。（这里的demain指过去计划中的明天）
He said he would call demain. = 他说他会明天打电话。


现在完成时:
I have decided to travel demain. = 我已经决定明天旅行。
She has arranged a meeting for demain. = 她已经安排了明天的会议。


过去完成时:
I had already finished my work before demain. = 我在明天之前就已经完成了我的工作。（指在过去某一时间点之前，相对于那个时间点而言的“明天”）
They had planned a trip for the demain of that day. = 他们已经为那一天的第二天计划了一次旅行。


将来时:
I will go to the cinema demain. = 我明天要去电影院。
We will have a party demain evening. = 我们明天晚上要举行一个派对。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would go to the beach demain. = 如果我有更多时间，我明天就去海滩。
I wish I could finish the project demain. = 我希望我明天能完成这个项目。


被动语态:
The meeting will be held demain. = 会议将在明天举行。
The work should be completed by demain. = 工作应该在明天之前完成。


疑问句:
Will you be free demain? = 你明天有空吗？
What are you doing demain? = 你明天做什么？


否定句:
I will not go to school demain. = 我明天不去上学。
She is not available demain afternoon. = 她明天下午没空。


PHRASE->
au demain = until tomorrow (法语) 直到明天
demain matin = tomorrow morning 明天早上
demain soir = tomorrow evening 明天晚上
demain après-midi = tomorrow afternoon 明天下午
le lendemain = the next day 次日 (虽然不是demain的直接词组，但语义相近)
