proliferating:/prəˈlɪfəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 迅速繁殖；激增；扩散 |  The verb "proliferate" means to increase rapidly in number; to multiply or spread rapidly.  It often refers to cells, organisms, or ideas. | 例句：Cancer cells proliferate uncontrollably.  癌症细胞不受控制地增殖。| 例句：Rumors of the company's impending closure were proliferating throughout the city. 公司即将倒闭的谣言在全城迅速蔓延。 | 近义词：multiply, spread, increase, mushroom, burgeon | 反义词：decrease, diminish, dwindle, decline


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weeds are proliferating rapidly in the garden. = 杂草在花园里迅速蔓延。
New businesses are proliferating in the city center. = 市中心涌现出许多新企业。

一般过去时:
The rabbit population proliferated after the introduction of new vegetation. = 引进新的植被后，兔子数量激增。
The rumors proliferated quickly among the employees. = 谣言迅速在员工中传播。

现在完成时:
The invasive species has proliferated throughout the ecosystem. = 这种入侵物种已经在整个生态系统中蔓延。
Social media has proliferated since the beginning of the 21st century. = 自21世纪初以来，社交媒体迅速发展。

过去完成时:
Before the intervention, the bacteria had proliferated to dangerous levels. = 在干预之前，细菌已繁殖到危险的水平。
The weeds had proliferated extensively before the gardener noticed. = 在园丁注意到之前，杂草已经蔓延得很厉害。

将来时:
Experts predict that the use of artificial intelligence will proliferate in the coming years. = 专家预测，人工智能的应用将在未来几年迅速普及。
Without control, the algae will proliferate and choke the lake. = 如果不加以控制，藻类将迅速繁殖并使湖泊窒息。

虚拟语气:
If we don't take action, the problem will proliferate. = 如果我们不采取行动，问题将会恶化。
If the disease had not proliferated so quickly, many lives could have been saved. = 如果这种疾病没有如此迅速地蔓延，许多生命本来可以得救。

被动语态:
The virus is being proliferated by unsanitary conditions. = 病毒因不卫生条件而迅速传播。
The new technology was rapidly proliferated across the country. = 新技术在全国迅速推广。

疑问句:
Is the use of this technology proliferating in developing countries? = 这种技术在发展中国家的使用是否正在普及？
Are the rumors proliferating despite our efforts to control them? = 尽管我们努力控制，谣言还在传播吗？

否定句:
The company's efforts to control the spread of misinformation did not prevent it from proliferating. = 公司控制虚假信息传播的努力并没有阻止其蔓延。
The number of endangered species is not proliferating; it is declining. = 濒危物种的数量并没有增加，而是在减少。
