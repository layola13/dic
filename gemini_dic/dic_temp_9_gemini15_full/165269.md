preinsures:/ˌpriːɪnˈʃʊərz/| v. |预先投保；预先保险|To insure something before it is needed or before a risk becomes apparent.  This typically involves obtaining insurance coverage in advance of a potential loss or event. |Example: The company preinsures all its shipments to protect against potential damage during transit. 公司预先为其所有货物投保，以防运输途中可能造成的损坏。|近义词：insure in advance, secure insurance beforehand |反义词：post-insure (this word is rarely used)|动词
