hereto:/ˌhɪərˈtuː/| adv.|到此，至此，直到现在|This adverb indicates up to this point in time or up to this place. It often implies a sense of completeness or finality regarding a preceding action or event.|例句：The negotiations have been arduous, but we have come this far, and we are hopeful that an agreement can be reached hereto.  谈判很艰难，但我们已经走到这一步了，我们希望能够就此达成协议。|近义词：hitherto, until now, so far, thus far|反义词：hereafter, henceforth
