caricatures:/kæˈrɪkətʃərz/ | n. | 漫画，讽刺画；夸张的描绘 | A caricature is a picture, description, or imitation of a person in which certain striking characteristics are exaggerated in order to create a comic or grotesque effect.  It often involves distortion or simplification of features to highlight a particular trait or aspect of the subject's personality or appearance. | Example: The newspaper published a caricature of the politician with an oversized nose and ears.  这家报纸刊登了一幅该政治家的漫画，画中他的鼻子和耳朵被夸张地放大。  |近义词：cartoon, lampoon, parody | 反义词：accurate portrait, realistic depiction | 名词
