deceptions:/dɪˈsɛpʃənz/| n. |欺骗；诡计；骗局 |The plural form of "deception," referring to instances or acts of misleading or deceiving someone.  It implies a deliberate attempt to make someone believe something that is untrue. |例句：His elaborate deceptions finally came to light.  他的精心策划的欺骗最终败露了。

例句：The magician's deceptions were so convincing that the audience was completely fooled. 魔术师的障眼法如此令人信服，以至于观众都被完全骗过了。


近义词：tricks, frauds, hoaxes, schemes, illusions, falsehoods

反义词：truth, honesty, frankness, sincerity, veracity


词性:名词
