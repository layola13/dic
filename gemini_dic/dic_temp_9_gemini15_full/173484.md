quasi-fashionably:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈfæʃənəbli/| adj.|似是而非时尚地，有点时尚地|The term describes something that attempts to be fashionable but falls short of genuine style or trendiness.  It suggests a superficial or slightly awkward attempt at fashion.  For example, someone might wear an outfit that is "quasi-fashionably" retro, meaning it tries to evoke a retro style but doesn't quite succeed.|The band's quasi-fashionably ripped jeans were a bit too much for the conservative audience.  乐队的有点时尚的破洞牛仔裤对保守的观众来说有点过头了。  He attempted a quasi-fashionably bohemian look, but ended up looking dishevelled. 他试图营造一种有点时尚的波西米亚风格，但最终看起来却蓬头垢面。|fashionably, stylishly|unfashionably, unstylishly
