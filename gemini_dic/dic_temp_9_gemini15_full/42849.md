court-martialling: /ˌkɔːrtˈmɑːrʃəlɪŋ/ | v. |军事法庭审判| The act of trying a member of the armed forces in a court-martial.  It involves a formal military trial for offenses against military law. |例句：The soldier was court-martialed for desertion.  士兵因逃兵罪被军事法庭审判。

例句：He faced court-martialling after refusing a direct order. 他因拒绝直接命令而面临军事法庭审判。


近义词：military trial, court-martial (noun form) | 反义词：acquittal, exoneration
