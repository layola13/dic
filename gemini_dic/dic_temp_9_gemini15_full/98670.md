insulated:/ˈɪnsəleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 隔热的；与世隔绝的；孤立的 | protected from outside influences | The house is well insulated against the cold.  这房子隔热性能良好，防止寒冷。

The island community felt insulated from the mainland's problems.  这个岛屿社区感觉与大陆的问题隔绝。 | 近义词：isolated, protected, shielded | 反义词：exposed, vulnerable, connected
