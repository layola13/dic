compensation:/ˌkɒmpenˈseɪʃən/ /ˌkɑːmpənˈseɪʃən/| n. |补偿；赔偿；报酬|The act of compensating for something; something given to make up for a loss, damage, or injury.  It can refer to financial payment, but also to something that offsets a negative aspect. |例句：The company offered compensation for the damage caused by the fire. 公司为火灾造成的损失提供了赔偿。|例句：Her kindness was compensation for her lack of beauty.  她的善良弥补了她缺乏美貌的不足。|近义词：remuneration, recompense, indemnity, amends|反义词：loss, penalty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He receives generous compensation for his work. = 他因工作获得丰厚的报酬。
The company provides compensation to injured employees. = 公司向受伤的员工提供赔偿。

一般过去时:
She received compensation for her injuries. = 她因受伤获得了赔偿。
The court awarded him compensation for his losses. = 法院判决他获得损失赔偿。


现在完成时:
They have received compensation for the delayed flight. = 他们已经收到了航班延误的赔偿。
The government has promised compensation to the victims. = 政府已承诺向受害者提供赔偿。


过去完成时:
He had already received compensation before the lawsuit was settled. = 在诉讼解决之前，他已经收到了赔偿。
They had not yet received compensation for the damaged goods. = 他们还没有收到损坏货物的赔偿。


将来时:
She will receive compensation for her medical expenses. = 她将获得医疗费用的赔偿。
The insurance company will provide compensation for the stolen property. = 保险公司将赔偿被盗财产。


虚拟语气:
If the company offered fair compensation, I would have accepted the job. = 如果公司提供公平的报酬，我就会接受这份工作。
I wish I had received better compensation for my efforts. = 我希望我的努力能得到更好的报酬。


被动语态:
Compensation was paid to the victims of the accident. = 事故受害者获得了赔偿。
The damage will be compensated for by the insurance company. = 保险公司将赔偿损失。


疑问句:
What compensation will you receive for overtime work? = 你加班会得到什么报酬？
What is the compensation for this position? = 这个职位的报酬是多少？


否定句:
He did not receive any compensation for his lost time. = 他没有收到任何因损失时间而获得的赔偿。
The company offered no compensation for the inconvenience. = 公司没有为不便提供任何赔偿。
