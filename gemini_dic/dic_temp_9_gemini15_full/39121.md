confucius:/kənˈfjuːʃəs/| n. |孔子|Confucius (551–479 BCE) was a Chinese philosopher and politician of the Spring and Autumn period.  His teachings, which emphasized morality, social order, and good governance, profoundly influenced Chinese culture and later East Asian societies. He is revered as a teacher and political figure.  His philosophy is known as Confucianism. |Example:  Confucius's teachings continue to be relevant today. 孔子的教诲至今仍具有现实意义。 The study of Confucius is a cornerstone of Chinese education.  学习孔子是中华教育的基石。|近义词：sage, philosopher, teacher |反义词：N/A
