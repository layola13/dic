rake:/reɪk/| n. vt.|耙；恶棍；浪荡子；（尤指尤指对女性）寻欢作乐者；严厉批评；搜刮；获得；|a tool with teeth for gathering leaves or hay; a dissolute man; to gather with a rake; to criticize severely; to obtain something, especially money or information,  in a skillful way.|例句：He used a rake to gather the fallen leaves. 他用耙子收集落叶。|例句：The newspaper article raked the politician over the coals for his dishonesty. 这篇报纸文章严厉批评了这位政客的不诚实行为。|近义词：comb, harrow, rakehell, scoundrel |反义词：tidy, orderly


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He rakes the leaves every autumn. = 他每年秋天都用耙子耙落叶。
She rakes the garden regularly. = 她定期耙花园。


一般过去时:
I raked the gravel path yesterday. = 我昨天耙了砾石路。
He raked the fire to make it burn brighter. = 他拨弄火堆使它烧得更旺。


现在完成时:
I have raked the lawn three times this week. = 我本周已经三次耙草坪了。
She has raked in millions from her latest business venture. = 她从她最新的商业冒险中赚取了数百万美元。


过去完成时:
He had raked the leaves before the rain started. = 下雨前他已经把树叶耙好了。
They had raked through all the evidence before reaching a conclusion. = 他们在得出结论之前已经仔细检查了所有证据。


将来时:
I will rake the leaves tomorrow. = 我明天将耙树叶。
She will rake through the old documents for the information. = 她将仔细查找旧文件以获取信息。


虚拟语气:
If I had a rake, I would rake the leaves. = 如果我有一把耙子，我会耙树叶。
I wish I had raked the garden earlier. = 我希望我早点耙花园。


被动语态:
The leaves were raked by the gardener. = 树叶被园丁耙了。
The information was raked up by the journalist. = 信息被记者挖出来了。


疑问句:
Do you rake your lawn regularly? = 你定期耙草坪吗？
Will you rake the leaves today? = 你今天要耙树叶吗？


否定句:
He didn't rake the leaves this morning. = 他今天早上没有耙树叶。
She doesn't rake the garden often. = 她不经常耙花园。
