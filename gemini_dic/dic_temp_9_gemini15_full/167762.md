primarily: /praɪˈmɛrəli/ | adv. | 主要地，首要地 | 主要地；首先；起初 | The project was primarily funded by private donations.  这个项目主要由私人捐款资助。| The children were primarily concerned with playing. 孩子们主要关心的是玩耍。| mainly, chiefly, principally | secondarily, subordinately


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He primarily focuses on his studies. = 他主要专注于他的学习。
She primarily works from home. = 她主要在家工作。


一般过去时:
They primarily relied on word-of-mouth advertising. = 他们主要依靠口碑宣传。
He primarily used a hammer for the job. = 他这项工作主要用锤子。


现在完成时:
We have primarily focused on customer satisfaction. = 我们主要关注客户满意度。
She has primarily worked in the marketing department. = 她主要在市场部工作。


过去完成时:
They had primarily completed the research before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前主要完成了研究工作。
He had primarily used traditional methods. = 他主要使用了传统方法。


将来时:
We will primarily use renewable energy sources. = 我们将主要使用可再生能源。
They will primarily focus on expanding their business. = 他们将主要专注于扩大业务。


虚拟语气:
If I were primarily concerned with money, I would take the higher-paying job. = 如果我主要关心的是钱，我会选择薪水更高的工作。
I wish I had primarily focused on my studies in college. = 我希望我大学主要专注于学习。


被动语态:
The decision was primarily influenced by financial considerations. = 这个决定主要受到财务因素的影响。
The project is primarily funded by government grants. = 这个项目主要由政府拨款资助。


疑问句:
Is the problem primarily caused by human error? = 这个问题主要是由人为错误造成的吗？
Are the company's profits primarily driven by sales? = 公司的利润主要由销售额驱动吗？


否定句:
The success wasn't primarily due to luck. = 成功并非主要归功于运气。
The problem wasn't primarily caused by technical issues. = 这个问题并非主要由技术问题引起。
