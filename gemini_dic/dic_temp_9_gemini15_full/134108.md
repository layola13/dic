nonimmigrants:/ˌnɑːnˈɪmɪɡrənts/ | n. | 非移民 |  A person who is residing in a country temporarily, usually for work or study, and does not intend to become a permanent resident.  They typically hold a temporary visa. | Example:  Many nonimmigrants come to the US for educational purposes.  许多非移民来美国是为了学习。| 近义词: temporary residents, alien residents (legal) | 反义词: immigrants, citizens
