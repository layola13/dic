misbuilding: /ˈmɪsbɪldɪŋ/ | n. | 错误建筑，劣质建筑 | A poorly constructed building, often with structural defects or flaws.  It refers to a building that is not built to code or is otherwise substandard. | Example: The investigation revealed the apartment complex was a misbuilding, riddled with safety hazards.  调查显示公寓大楼是一座劣质建筑，充斥着安全隐患。|近义词：faulty construction, substandard building, defective structure | 反义词：well-built building, sound structure | 名词
