preshipping:/ˈpriːʃɪpɪŋ/| n. |预运，发货前准备|The process of preparing goods for shipment, including tasks like packaging, labeling, and documentation.  It often involves activities that occur before the actual transportation of goods begins.|例句：Careful preshipping procedures are crucial to avoid delays and damage during transit.  仔细的预运流程对于避免运输过程中的延误和损坏至关重要。

例句：The preshipping inspection revealed a minor defect in one of the units. 预运检查发现其中一个单元存在轻微缺陷。


近义词：pre-shipment, pre-dispatch, preparation for shipment|反义词：post-shipping, delivery
