gatecrashed: /ˈɡeɪtkræʃt/ | v. | 擅自闯入；不请自来 | To enter a party or event without an invitation. | 例句：They gatecrashed the party and caused a lot of trouble. 他们未经邀请就闯入了派对，并制造了很多麻烦。

例句：

Several uninvited guests gatecrashed the wedding reception.  好几个不速之客擅自闯入了婚礼招待会。


The teenagers gatecrashed the concert, hoping to see their favorite band.  这些青少年擅自闯入了音乐会，希望能看到他们最喜欢的乐队。


近义词：crash, barge in, intrude, trespass


反义词：be invited, be welcomed


词性：动词

词源：
The word "gatecrash" is a compound word.  "Gate" refers to the entrance to a place, often implying exclusivity or a need for invitation. "Crash" in this context means to enter forcefully or uninvited.  The combination suggests forcefully entering a place without permission, usually a party or event.  The term originated in the early 20th century.
