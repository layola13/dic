gangbusters:/ˈɡæŋˌbʌstərz/| adj.|非常成功；非常有效；势不可挡|Extremely successful or effective; overwhelmingly successful.  The term originally referred to police officers who successfully cracked down on criminal gangs.|The new marketing campaign is gangbusters; sales are exceeding all expectations. 新的营销活动非常成功；销售额超过了所有预期。|近义词：highly successful, tremendously effective, booming, wildly popular|反义词：unsuccessful, ineffective, failing, flopping


USAGE->

The business is gangbusters this year. = 今年生意兴隆。
This new product is gangbusters in the market. = 这款新产品在市场上非常畅销。


一般过去时:
Last year's sales were gangbusters. = 去年销售额非常高。
