pectoriloquism:/ˌpɛktərɪˈlokwɪzəm/| n. |胸部语音；胸语症|The phenomenon of sounds, particularly the voice, seeming to originate from the chest of the speaker, often associated with certain lung conditions.|The doctor noticed the patient's pectoriloquism, a clear indication of a possible lung issue.  医生注意到病人的胸部语音，这清楚地表明可能存在肺部问题。|近义词:whispering pectoriloquy|反义词:None specifically defined.
