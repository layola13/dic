ladyless: /ˈleɪdiːləs/ | adj. |  无女士的；缺少女性的 | Lacking ladies or women; without women.  The term often implies a social setting or environment where women are absent or underrepresented, and can carry a negative connotation suggesting a lack of balance or diversity. |  例句：The club felt ladyless without its female members.  俱乐部没有女会员，感觉少了些什么。

例句：The all-male board meeting felt rather ladyless. 只有男性的董事会会议感觉相当缺乏女性。


近义词：womanless, female-free (less common and can sound more clinical)

反义词：lady-filled, well-represented by women


词性:形容词
