floors:/flɔːrz/| n.|楼层，地板|The building has ten floors. 这栋建筑有十层楼。|近义词：stories, levels|反义词：ceiling, roof|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The building has five floors. = 这栋建筑有五层楼。
How many floors does your apartment building have? = 你的公寓楼有多少层？


一般过去时:
The old house had only two floors. = 那栋旧房子只有两层。
We lived on the third floor last year. = 去年我们住在三楼。


现在完成时:
They have added three more floors to the hospital. = 他们已经给医院加盖了三层楼。
This building has undergone many renovations since we bought it; it used to only have three floors. =自从我们买下这栋楼以来，它经历了很多次翻修；以前它只有三层楼。


过去完成时:
Before the extension, the school had only four floors. = 在扩建之前，学校只有四层楼。
By the time the fire broke out, people had already evacuated the upper floors. = 火灾发生时，人们已经撤离了高层楼。


将来时:
The new hotel will have twenty floors. = 新酒店将会有二十层楼。
They will add another floor to the existing building next year. =他们明年将在现有建筑上再加一层楼。


虚拟语气:
If the building had more floors, it could accommodate more people. = 如果这栋楼有更多楼层，它就能容纳更多的人。
I wish this apartment had hardwood floors instead of carpet. = 我希望这套公寓铺的是硬木地板而不是地毯。


被动语态:
The floors were cleaned yesterday. = 地板昨天打扫了。
The new floors are being installed next week. = 新地板下周安装。


疑问句:
How many floors are there in this building? = 这栋楼有多少层？
Which floor is your office on? = 你的办公室在几楼？


否定句:
The building doesn't have a basement floor. = 这栋楼没有地下室。
The elevator doesn't service all the floors. = 电梯不服务所有楼层。


PHRASE->
upper floors = 高层
lower floors = 低层
ground floor = 底层/一楼
top floor = 最高层
floor plan = 平面图
