dispense:/dɪˈspɛns/| vt.|分发，分配；免除；调剂（药方）|to distribute or give out something, especially in set amounts; to officially excuse someone from a duty or punishment; to prepare and give out medicine|The pharmacist dispensed the medication. 药剂师配发了药物。

The teacher dispensed the test papers to the students.老师把试卷分发给学生们。

The judge dispensed justice fairly.法官公正地执行了正义。

The company dispensed with the services of several employees.公司解雇了几名员工。


近义词：distribute, administer, allot, pardon

反义词：retain, withhold, keep, collect
