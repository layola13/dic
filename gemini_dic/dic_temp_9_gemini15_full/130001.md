non-normal:/ˌnɒnˈnɔːrməl/| adj. |非正态的，非正常的|Not conforming to the standard or norm; deviating from what is typical or expected.  It often refers to statistical distributions that are not normal distributions (following a bell curve).|例句：The data showed a non-normal distribution, requiring a different statistical analysis. 数据显示非正态分布，需要不同的统计分析。


The results of the experiment were non-normal, indicating a potential problem with the methodology. 实验结果是非正态的，表明方法可能存在问题。


近义词：abnormal, atypical, unusual, irregular |反义词：normal, typical, standard, regular
