demoralised: /dɪˈmɒrəlaɪzd/ /dɪˈmɔːrəlaɪzd/ | adj. |士气低落的；泄气的；沮丧的 |

英文解析:  Having lost confidence or enthusiasm; disheartened.  Characterized by a loss of spirit, courage, or hope.

例句:

The team was demoralised after their crushing defeat.  球队在惨败后士气低落。

The constant criticism left her feeling demoralised and discouraged.  不断的批评让她感到沮丧和灰心。


近义词: disheartened, discouraged, despondent, depressed, dejected, downcast, demoralized

反义词: encouraged, inspired, motivated, uplifted, exhilarated


词性: 形容词
