cannonading:/ˌkænənˈeɪdɪŋ/| n. | 炮击；连续炮轰 | The continuous, heavy firing of cannons.  It often implies a sustained and intense bombardment. | 例句：The city endured days of relentless cannonading.  这座城市承受了数日无情的炮击。

The enemy's cannonading caused significant damage to the fortifications. 敌人的炮击对防御工事造成了重大破坏。| 近义词：bombardment, shelling, shelling, cannonade | 反义词：ceasefire, truce | 名词
