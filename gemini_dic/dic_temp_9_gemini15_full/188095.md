rounder:/ˈraʊndər/| n. adj. |圆形物；圆润的人；多面手；使圆润；使完善|A person or thing that is round or has a rounded shape.  Someone who is versatile and skilled in many areas.|The potter skillfully shaped the clay into a rounder. 陶工熟练地将粘土塑造成更圆的东西。He's a real rounder, good at everything from cooking to coding. 他真是个多面手，从烹饪到编程样样精通。|近义词：all-rounder, versatile, well-rounded |反义词：specialist, one-sided


USAGE->
