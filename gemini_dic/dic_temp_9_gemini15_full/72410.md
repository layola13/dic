flues:/fluːz/| n.|烟道；烟囱；风管|A flue is a pipe, tube, or duct for conveying smoke, gases, or air.|The chimney flues were blocked with soot.|近义词：chimney, vent, duct|反义词：None

The flues carry smoke away from the fireplace. = 烟道将烟从壁炉中带走。
