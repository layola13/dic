chlorotic:/kləˈrɑːtɪk/ | adj. |  叶绿素缺乏的；黄化的；失绿的 |  Lacking in chlorophyll, resulting in yellowing or discoloration.  This term usually refers to plants exhibiting a yellowing of leaves due to insufficient chlorophyll.  | The plant showed chlorotic leaves due to iron deficiency.  这株植物由于缺铁而出现了黄叶。 The gardener noticed the chlorotic appearance of the lettuce. 园丁注意到莴苣的叶子发黄了。 |  pale, yellowed, anemic |  healthy, green, vibrant
