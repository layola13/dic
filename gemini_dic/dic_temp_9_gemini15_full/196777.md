sexologist:/ˌsɛksˈɒlədʒɪst/| n. |性学家|A sexologist is a scientist or professional who studies human sexuality, including sexual behavior, sexual health, and sexual dysfunction.  They may conduct research, provide therapy, or offer education on various aspects of sex and sexuality.|例句：The sexologist presented compelling data on the changing attitudes towards sex in modern society.  性学家展示了关于现代社会对性态度变化的令人信服的数据。|近义词：sex therapist, sexuality researcher |反义词：无
