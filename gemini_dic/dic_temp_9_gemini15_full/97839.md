inlace:/ɪnˈleɪs/| vt.| 交织；镶嵌；编织|To interweave or interlace; to decorate (a surface) by inlaying pieces of other material.|例句：The artist skillfully inlaced the silver threads into the fabric.  艺术家巧妙地将银线编织到织物中。  The craftsman inlaced the wood with mother-of-pearl. 工匠用珍珠母镶嵌木材。|近义词：interweave, interlace, inlay|反义词：separate, unbraid
