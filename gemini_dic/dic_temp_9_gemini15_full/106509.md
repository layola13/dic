kreatinin:/krɪˈætɪnɪn/| n. |肌酸酐|Creatinine is a chemical waste product made by your muscles.  It's filtered out of your blood by your kidneys and excreted in your urine.  High levels of creatinine in the blood can indicate kidney problems.|Your doctor may order a creatinine blood test to assess your kidney function.  The test measures the amount of creatinine in your blood.|近义词：creatine (肌酸,  but not a direct synonym, as creatinine is a metabolic byproduct of creatine)|反义词：无|名词
