emanator:/ˈɛməneɪtər/ | n. |散发者，发射器 |A person or thing that emanates something, especially a smell, light, or feeling.  Something that emits or radiates. |例句：The old house was an emanator of damp and decay. 这栋老房子散发着潮湿和腐烂的气息。|例句：The sun is a powerful emanator of light and heat. 太阳是强大光和热的散发源。|近义词：emitter, source, radiator, dispenser |反义词：absorber


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flower emanates a sweet fragrance. = 这朵花散发着甜美的香味。
That machine emanates a low hum. = 那台机器发出低沉的嗡嗡声。


一般过去时:
The speaker emanated confidence. = 演讲者散发着自信。
The factory emanated a noxious smell. = 工厂散发着难闻的气味。


现在完成时:
The perfume has emanated a subtle scent all day. = 香水整天都散发着淡淡的香味。
The situation has emanated tension among the employees. = 情况已在员工中造成紧张气氛。


过去完成时:
The room had emanated a strange odor before we entered. = 我们进去之前，房间里散发着奇怪的气味。
The event had emanated a sense of unease in the community. = 此事件在社区中已造成不安情绪。


将来时:
The new product will emanate a sense of luxury. = 新产品将散发奢华感。
The concert will emanate vibrant energy. = 音乐会将散发充满活力的能量。


虚拟语气:
If the lamp emanated more light, we could see better. = 如果灯能发出更多光线，我们就能看得更清楚。
I wish the room emanated a more pleasant smell. = 我希望房间能散发更令人愉悦的气味。


被动语态:
A strong scent was emanated by the freshly cut grass. = 新鲜的割草散发出一股浓烈的香味。（被动语态较少用）
No clear signal was emanated from the device. = 设备没有发出清晰的信号。（被动语态较少用）


疑问句:
What kind of energy does the sun emanate? = 太阳散发什么类型的能量？
Does the speaker emanate authority? = 演讲者散发权威感吗？


否定句:
The device did not emanate any signal. = 该设备没有发出任何信号。
The room did not emanate a welcoming atmosphere. = 房间没有散发温馨的氛围。


PHRASE->
emanate from = 来自于...
emanate a sense of = 散发出...的感觉
emanate an aura of = 散发出...的光环
emanate light and heat = 发光发热
emanate power and strength = 散发力量和实力
