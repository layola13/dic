lugworm:/ˈlʌɡwɜːrm/| n. |沙蚕，海蚯蚓|A marine worm that lives in burrows in the sand or mud, known for its characteristic U-shaped burrows and its role in marine ecosystems.  Lugworms are segmented worms with a prominent proboscis used for feeding.|例句：The lugworm is a common inhabitant of intertidal zones. 沙蚕是潮间带的常见居民。|例句：We dug up several lugworms while searching for bait. 我们在寻找诱饵时挖出了几条沙蚕。|近义词：sandworm, clam worm|反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑与陆地蠕虫对比)|名词
