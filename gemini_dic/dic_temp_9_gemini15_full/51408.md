deterministically:/ˌdiːtɜːrmɪˈnɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 确定性地；必然地 |  In a deterministic manner; inevitably.  It implies that an outcome is completely determined by preceding events and conditions, leaving no room for chance or randomness. | 例句：The program runs deterministically, producing the same output every time with the same input.  该程序确定性地运行，每次使用相同的输入都会产生相同的输出。

The computer model simulates the system's behavior deterministically.  计算机模型确定性地模拟了系统的行为。


近义词： inevitably, invariably, predictably | 反义词：randomly, stochastically, probabilistically | 词性: 副词
