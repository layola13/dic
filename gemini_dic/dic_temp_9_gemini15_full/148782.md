overutilize:/ˌoʊvərˈjuːtəlaɪz/ | vt. |过度使用，滥用 | To use something excessively or to a point of waste or inefficiency.  |例句：The company overutilized its resources, leading to financial difficulties. 公司过度使用资源，导致财务困难。
The doctor warned against overutilizing antibiotics. 医生警告不要过度使用抗生素。|近义词：misuse, abuse, overuse, exploit |反义词：underuse, conserve, preserve |动词
