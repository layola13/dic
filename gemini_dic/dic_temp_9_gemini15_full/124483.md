mouldered:/ˈmoʊldərd/ | v. |腐朽；腐烂；衰败 | To decay or rot, especially gradually.  It implies a slow and often unnoticed process of decomposition. | The old wooden fence had mouldered away.  古老的木栅栏已经腐烂了。

The ancient ruins had mouldered into dust.  古老的废墟已经腐朽成尘土。|decay, rot, decompose, crumble|flourish, thrive|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wood moulders slowly in the damp cellar. = 木头在潮湿的地窖里慢慢腐烂。
The old books are mouldering on the shelf. = 旧书在书架上慢慢腐朽。


一般过去时:
The building mouldered for years before it finally collapsed. = 这栋建筑物腐朽多年后最终倒塌了。
The fruit mouldered quickly in the heat. = 水果在高温下迅速腐烂。


现在完成时:
The timbers have mouldered significantly since the storm. = 自从暴风雨以来，木材已经严重腐烂了。
The old paintings have mouldered over time. = 老画作随着时间的推移而腐朽。


过去完成时:
The documents had mouldered before anyone discovered them. = 文件在任何人发现它们之前就已经腐烂了。
The remains had mouldered in the ground for centuries. = 遗骸在地下腐烂了几个世纪。


将来时:
The neglected house will moulder away if it is not repaired. = 如果不修理，这栋被忽视的房子将会腐烂。
The food will moulder if it is left out in the sun. = 食物如果放在阳光下会腐烂。


虚拟语气:
If the wood hadn't been treated, it would have mouldered long ago. = 如果木材没有经过处理，它早就腐烂了。
I wish the old photo hadn't mouldered away. = 我真希望这张旧照片没有腐烂。


被动语态:
The ancient city was slowly mouldered by the elements. = 古城被自然元素慢慢腐蚀。
The statue had been mouldered beyond recognition. = 雕像已经腐烂得无法辨认。


疑问句:
Has the old chest mouldered away completely? = 旧箱子完全腐烂了吗？
Is the fruit mouldering in the basket? = 水果在篮子里腐烂了吗？


否定句:
The metal didn't moulder because it was treated with a special coating. = 金属没有腐烂，因为它涂上了特殊的涂层。
The stone walls haven't mouldered in centuries. = 石墙几个世纪以来都没有腐烂。


PHRASE->
mouldered away: 腐烂消失
mouldered to dust: 腐朽成尘土
mouldered ruins: 腐朽的废墟
slowly mouldered: 慢慢腐烂
mouldered remains: 腐朽的遗骸

词源:
From Middle English *mouldren, from Old English *muldrien, from *muld "dust, earth".  The word ultimately connects to the Proto-Indo-European root *mel- "to grind".  The sense development is from "to become dust" to "to decay".
