infographics:/ˌɪnfəˈɡræfɪks/| n. |信息图表；信息图形 |Visual representations of information, data, or knowledge intended to present complex information quickly and clearly.  They often combine images, charts, and minimal text. |Example: The infographics clearly illustrated the company's growth over the past decade.  信息图表清晰地展示了公司过去十年的增长。|近义词：data visualization, charts, diagrams |反义词：None
