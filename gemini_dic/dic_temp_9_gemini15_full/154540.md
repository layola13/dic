perplexed:/pərˈplekst/| adj.|困惑的，茫然的|Confused and bewildered; unsure how to react or respond.|例句：He was perplexed by the unexpected turn of events. 他对事情的意外转变感到困惑。|例句：The complex instructions left her feeling perplexed.  复杂的指示让她感到困惑。|近义词：confused, baffled, bewildered, puzzled|反义词：clear, certain, understood


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is perplexed by the riddle. = 他对这个谜语感到困惑。
The situation perplexes me. = 这种情况让我困惑。


一般过去时:
I was perplexed by her sudden anger. = 我对她突然发怒感到困惑。
The strange noise perplexed the neighbors. = 奇怪的噪音让邻居们感到困惑。


现在完成时:
The mystery has perplexed investigators for years. = 这个谜团多年来一直困扰着调查人员。
This problem has perplexed me for days. =这个问题困扰了我好几天。


过去完成时:
She had been perplexed by his behavior for weeks before she confronted him. = 在她质问他之前，她已经对他几周的行为感到困惑了。
They had been perplexed by the conflicting reports. = 他们一直对相互矛盾的报告感到困惑。


将来时:
The new regulations will perplex many businesses. = 新规章制度将使许多企业感到困惑。
This difficult question will perplex the students. = 这个难题将会让学生们感到困惑。


虚拟语气:
I would be perplexed if I received such a letter. = 如果我收到这样的信，我会感到困惑。
If I were you, I would be perplexed by this situation. = 如果我是你，我会对这种情况感到困惑。


被动语态:
Many people are perplexed by the complexity of the tax system. = 很多人对税收制度的复杂性感到困惑。
The audience was perplexed by the ambiguous ending of the play. = 观众对戏剧模棱两可的结局感到困惑。


疑问句:
Are you perplexed by the instructions? = 你对说明感到困惑吗？
Is he perplexed by the sudden change of plans? = 他对计划的突然改变感到困惑吗？


否定句:
I am not perplexed by his actions. = 我并不对他所作所为感到困惑。
She was not perplexed by the difficult problem. = 她并没有被这个难题难倒。


PHRASE->
be perplexed by = 对...感到困惑

utterly perplexed = 非常困惑
become perplexed = 开始困惑
seem perplexed = 看起来困惑
