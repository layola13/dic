chorus-girl: /ˈkɔːrəsɡɜːrl/ | n. | 女合唱队员，歌舞团女演员 | A woman who sings and dances in a chorus line, especially in a musical theatre production.  | Example: She dreamed of becoming a chorus girl on Broadway. 她梦想成为百老汇的歌舞女郎。 | 近义词: chorus dancer, showgirl, dancer | 反义词:  soloist (反义词比较牵强，因为侧重于表演形式而非性别)
