nonexperienced:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspɪəriənst/ | adj. |缺乏经验的，没有经验的 |Lacking experience; inexperienced.  The word combines "non-" (meaning "not") and "experienced" (meaning "having experience"). It describes someone or something that hasn't had much or any experience in a particular field or activity. |例句：He is a nonexperienced programmer, so he often makes mistakes. 他是一个没有经验的程序员，所以他经常犯错误。

The nonexperienced hikers got lost in the woods. 那些没有经验的徒步旅行者在树林里迷路了。|近义词：inexperienced, unskilled, green, naive |反义词：experienced, skilled, seasoned, veteran
