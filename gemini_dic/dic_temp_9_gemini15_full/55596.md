disunites:/dɪsˈjuːnaɪts/| vt.|使分离；使分裂；使不团结|To separate or divide; to cause to lack unity or agreement.|The political scandal disunited the party.  政治丑闻使该党分裂。|The conflicting reports disunited the team.  相互矛盾的报告使团队产生了分歧。|unifies, unites|harmonizes, integrates

Their differing opinions disunite the group. = 他们不同的意见使小组不团结。
