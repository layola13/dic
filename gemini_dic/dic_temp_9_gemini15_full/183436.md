reptant:/ˈrɛptənt/| adj.|匍匐的；爬行的；蔓生的|Moving or spreading along the ground or a surface; creeping.  The word often describes plants that spread along the ground, or animals that move by crawling. |例句：The reptant ivy covered the old stone wall.  (匍匐的常春藤覆盖着古老的石墙。)

The reptant snake slithered through the grass. (爬行的蛇在草丛中蜿蜒而行。)

|近义词：creeping, crawling, prostrate, trailing|反义词：erect, upright|形容词
