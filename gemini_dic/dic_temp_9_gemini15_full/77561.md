gear-up:/ɡɪrˈʌp/ | phrasal verb |  准备；做好准备 | To prepare oneself or something for an activity or event.  |  例句：We need to gear up for the marathon. 我们需要为马拉松做好准备。

例句：Let's gear up for a long day of hiking. 我们来为一天的徒步旅行做好准备吧。


近义词：prepare, get ready, equip, brace


反义词：relax, unwind, slack off


词性：phrasal verb
