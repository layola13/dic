mesopodiale:/ˌmɛsəˈpoʊdiəl/| n. |腕掌骨|The mesopodiale refers to the intermediate segment of the foot or hand, located between the carpus (wrist) and the phalanges (fingers or toes) in the case of the hand, and between the tarsus (ankle) and the phalanges in the case of the foot.  It consists of the metacarpal bones in the hand and the metatarsal bones in the foot.  These bones are long bones that articulate with the carpus/tarsus proximally and the phalanges distally. |The metacarpals of the hand form the mesopodiale. |近义词: metacarpus (hand), metatarsus (foot)|反义词:  carpus, tarsus, phalanges|名词
