dwarfling:/ˈdwɔːrfliŋ/ | n. | 矮人；小矮人 | A dwarfling is a small dwarf, often used in fantasy literature to describe a young or diminutive member of a dwarf race.  It can also imply a lack of the typical strength or characteristics associated with adult dwarves. | Example:  The dwarfling, barely taller than a hobbit, bravely faced the dragon.  这个小矮人，身高 barely 比霍比特人高，勇敢地面对着巨龙。|近义词：little dwarf, small dwarf | 反义词：giant, giantess
