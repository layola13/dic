individ:/ˈɪndɪvɪdʒuəl/| adj. n.|个体的；个人|individual means single and separate from others.  It can describe a single person or thing, or the qualities of being unique and independent.|例句：Each individual has their own unique talents.  每个个体都有自己独特的才能。|例句：He is a very individual person. 他是一个非常有个性的人。|近义词：single, separate, unique, distinct|反义词：collective, group, combined
