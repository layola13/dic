penanced:/ˈpɛnənst/| v.|赎罪，苦修|to perform penance; to do penance for a sin or wrongdoing.|例句：He penanced himself by fasting for forty days. 他通过禁食四十天来赎罪。|例句：She penanced for her sins by volunteering at a homeless shelter. 她通过在无家可归者收容所做义工来赎罪。|近义词：atoned, repented, atoned for|反义词：celebrated, enjoyed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He penances every Friday. = 他每个星期五都进行苦修。
She penances for her past mistakes. = 她为过去的错误进行赎罪。

一般过去时:
He penanced for his sins. = 他为他的罪过赎罪。
She penanced herself with rigorous fasting. = 她通过严格的禁食来惩罚自己。

现在完成时:
He has penanced for years. = 他已经赎罪多年了。
She has penanced for her actions. = 她已经为她的行为赎罪了。

过去完成时:
He had penanced before he felt forgiven. = 在他感到被宽恕之前，他已经进行了赎罪。
She had penanced extensively before seeking redemption. = 在寻求救赎之前，她已经进行了大量的赎罪。

将来时:
He will penance for his transgression. = 他将为他的罪行赎罪。
She will penance by donating to charity. = 她将通过捐款给慈善机构来进行赎罪。

虚拟语气:
If he had penanced earlier, he might have felt better. = 如果他早点赎罪，他可能会感觉更好。
I wish I had penanced for my mistakes. = 我希望我为我的错误赎罪。

被动语态:
Penance was performed by the monks. = 苦修是由僧侣们进行的。
His sins were penanced through prayer and fasting. = 他的罪过通过祈祷和禁食得到了赎罪。

疑问句:
Did he penance for his actions? = 他为他的行为赎罪了吗？
How long will she penance? = 她将进行多久的赎罪？

否定句:
He did not penance for his wrongdoing. = 他没有为他的错误行为赎罪。
She did not penance sufficiently to ease her guilt. = 她进行的赎罪不足以减轻她的内疚。


PHRASE->

undergo penance = 经历赎罪
