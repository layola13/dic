infiltration's:/ˌɪnfɪlˈtreɪʃənz/| n.|渗透；渗入；潜入|The act or process of infiltrating or being infiltrated.  It refers to the slow, gradual penetration of something into something else.|例句：The infiltration of enemy spies caused serious security concerns. 敌方间谍的渗透造成了严重的安保问题。

例句：The slow infiltration of water into the basement caused significant damage. 水慢慢渗入地下室造成了很大的损失。


近义词：penetration, seepage, permeation, ingress

反义词：exclusion, expulsion, ejection

词性：名词 (复数形式)
