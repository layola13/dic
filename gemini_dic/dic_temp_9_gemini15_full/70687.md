finality:/faɪˈnæləti/ | n. |最终性；不可改变性；确定性 |The state or quality of being final; conclusiveness.  It refers to the state of being complete, ended, or settled, leaving no room for further action or change.  |例句：The finality of death is a difficult concept for many to grasp.  死亡的最终性是许多人难以理解的概念。

The judge's decision brought a sense of finality to the long legal battle.  法官的判决为这场旷日持久的法律斗争带来了最终的确定性。

|近义词：conclusiveness, definitiveness, settlement, resolution |反义词：indecisiveness, uncertainty, ambiguity |名词
