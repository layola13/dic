shuffles:/ˈʃʌfəlz/ | v. |洗牌；拖着脚走；(使)凌乱；偷偷摸摸地移动 | To move one's feet by sliding them along the ground; to move something around in a disorganized way; to mix cards. |例句：He shuffled the cards before dealing. 他在发牌前洗牌了。  He shuffled his feet nervously. 他紧张地拖着脚走。|近义词：slide, drag, jumble,  rearrange |反义词：arrange, organize, straighten


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He shuffles the cards every time he plays poker. = 他每次玩扑克都洗牌。
She shuffles her feet when she's embarrassed. = 她害羞时会拖着脚走。

一般过去时:
The magician shuffled the deck quickly. = 魔术师迅速地洗牌。
He shuffled into the room, trying to be quiet. = 他偷偷摸摸地走进房间，试图不发出声音。

现在完成时:
He has shuffled the deck three times already. = 他已经洗牌三次了。
She has shuffled her papers all morning. = 她整个上午都在整理她的文件。

过去完成时:
He had shuffled the cards before the game began. = 游戏开始前他已经洗牌了。
She had shuffled through the documents, looking for a specific one. = 她已经翻阅了文件，寻找一份特定的文件。

将来时:
He will shuffle the cards before the next round. = 他将在下一轮之前洗牌。
She will shuffle her feet nervously as she waits for her turn. = 当她等待轮到她时，她会紧张地拖着脚走。

虚拟语气:
If he shuffled the cards properly, the game would be fair. = 如果他正确地洗牌，游戏就会公平。
I wish I hadn't shuffled the papers; now I can't find anything. = 我真希望我没有把文件弄乱；现在我什么也找不到。

被动语态:
The cards were shuffled by the dealer. = 牌是由发牌人洗的。
The papers were shuffled and placed in a pile. = 文件被整理好并堆放在一起。

疑问句:
Did he shuffle the cards? = 他洗牌了吗？
Has she shuffled the documents yet? = 她整理文件了吗？

否定句:
He didn't shuffle the cards properly. = 他没有正确地洗牌。
She hasn't shuffled through the reports yet. = 她还没有浏览报告。
