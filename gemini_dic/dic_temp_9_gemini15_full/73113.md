for free:/fɔːr friː/| adj. adv.|免费的，无偿的|Something that is given or offered without charge.  It indicates that no payment is required.|例句：I got the tickets for free.  我免费得到了票。|例句：The museum is free for children under 12.  12岁以下儿童可免费参观博物馆。|近义词：gratis, complimentary, free of charge|反义词：paid, costly, expensive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The concert is for free tonight. = 今晚音乐会免费。
They offer the service for free. = 他们免费提供这项服务。


一般过去时:
The restaurant gave us a dessert for free. = 餐厅免费给了我们一份甜点。
He got the software for free. = 他免费得到了这个软件。


现在完成时:
I have received several free samples for free. = 我已经免费收到了几份样品。
She has downloaded many apps for free. = 她已经免费下载了很多应用程序。


过去完成时:
They had already offered the tickets for free before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，他们已经免费提供了门票。
We had obtained the information for free previously. = 我们之前已经免费获得了信息。


将来时:
The company will offer the product for free next month. = 公司下个月将免费提供该产品。
We will be able to access the database for free soon. = 我们很快就能免费访问数据库。


虚拟语气:
If the tickets were for free, I would go. = 如果门票免费，我会去的。
I wish the software were for free. = 我希望这个软件是免费的。


被动语态:
The tickets were given away for free. = 门票被免费赠送了。
The information was made available for free. = 信息被免费提供。


疑问句:
Is the entry for free? = 入场免费吗？
Are the drinks for free? = 饮料免费吗？


否定句:
The concert wasn't for free. = 音乐会不是免费的。
The meal wasn't offered for free. = 这顿饭没有免费提供。
