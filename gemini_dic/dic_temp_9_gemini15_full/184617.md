restrainingly:/rɪˈstreɪnɪŋli/ | adv. |克制地；抑制地；拘谨地 |  In a way that shows restraint or control of one's feelings, actions, or expression.  It implies a conscious effort to suppress or moderate something. |例句：She smiled restrainingly, concealing her true feelings. 她克制地笑了笑，掩盖了她的真实感受。

例句：He spoke restrainingly, avoiding any harsh words. 他说话克制，避免任何尖刻的言语。


近义词：reservedly, cautiously, discreetly, temperately |反义词：unrestrainedly, wildly, freely, openly


词性：副词
