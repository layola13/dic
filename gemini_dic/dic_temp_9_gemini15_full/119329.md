microbion:/ˈmaɪkroʊbaɪən/| n. |微生物；共生微生物|A microbion is a microscopic organism, especially one that lives in a symbiotic relationship with another organism.  This term is less common than "microorganism" or specific terms like "bacteria" or "fungi".  It often implies a beneficial or at least non-harmful relationship.|Example: The human gut is teeming with microbions that aid in digestion.|近义词：microorganism, symbiont|反义词：macroscopic organism
