hydrofluosilicate:/ˌhaɪdroʊˌfluːoʊˈsɪlɪkeɪt/| n. |氟硅酸盐|A salt of hydrofluosilicic acid (H2SiF6).  Hydrofluosilicates are typically formed by the reaction of hydrofluosilicic acid with a base. They are often used in various industrial applications, including water fluoridation. |Example: Sodium hydrofluosilicate is commonly used as a fluoridating agent in water treatment plants.  氟硅酸钠通常用作水处理厂中的氟化剂。|近义词:fluorosilicate|反义词: N/A
