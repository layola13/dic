re-crowning: /ˌriːˈkraʊnɪŋ/ | v. | 重新加冕；再次加冕 | The act of crowning someone again, either literally (as in a monarch) or figuratively (as in restoring something to its former glory).  It implies a previous crowning and a renewal or restoration of that status. | Example: The festival concluded with the re-crowning of the May Queen.  节日以五月女王的再次加冕而告终。 The re-crowning of the champion reaffirmed his dominance.  冠军的再次加冕重申了他的统治地位。 | 近义词: recrown, reinstate, restore | 反义词: dethrone, depose
