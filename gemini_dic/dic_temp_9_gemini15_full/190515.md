satellite:/ˈsætəlaɪt/| n. |卫星，人造卫星，附属物 |An object that orbits a planet or star.  A satellite can be natural, like the Moon, or artificial, like those used for communication or observation. |Example: The Hubble Space Telescope is a satellite orbiting Earth.  哈勃太空望远镜是绕地球运行的一颗卫星。 The moon is a natural satellite of the Earth. 月球是地球的天然卫星。|近义词：moon (for natural satellite), orbiter |反义词：planet (for what a satellite orbits)
