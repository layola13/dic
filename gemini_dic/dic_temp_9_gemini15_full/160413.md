poppies:/ˈpɒpiz/ /ˈpɑːpiz/| n. |罂粟花；罂粟|The bright red poppies swayed gently in the breeze.  鲜红的罂粟花在微风中轻轻摇曳。|The field was ablaze with poppies.  田野里盛开着一片罂粟花。|近义词：corn poppy, field poppy|反义词:无


USAGE->

Poppies bloom in the spring. = 罂粟花在春天盛开。
She grows poppies in her garden. = 她在花园里种植罂粟花。


一般过去时:

词源:
The word "poppy" comes from the Old English word "popig," which is believed to be of Celtic origin.  The Celtic word likely referred to the milky juice of the poppy plant.
