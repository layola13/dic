parados:/ˈpærədɒs/ | n. |悖论，似非而是的论点 |A statement or proposition that seems self-contradictory or absurd but in reality expresses a possible truth.  |Example: The paradox of life is that we are born to die.  生命的悖论是我们生来就是要死的。|近义词：contradiction, anomaly, enigma |反义词：truth, certainty
