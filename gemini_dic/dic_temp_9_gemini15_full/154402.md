permed:/pɜːmd/ | adj. | 卷发的；烫发的 | Having hair that has been permed.  This usually refers to hair that has been chemically treated to create curls or waves. | 例句：Her permed hair bounced as she walked.  她的烫发随着她走路而飘动。

例句：She has lovely permed hair. 她有一头漂亮的烫发。


近义词：curled, wavy, styled  | 反义词：straight, unstyled | 词性：形容词
