founded:/ˈfaʊndɪd/| v.|建立的，创立的|To establish or create something, typically an institution, organization, or settlement.  |例句：The university was founded in 1850. 这所大学建于1850年。|例句：She founded a charity to help homeless people. 她创办了一个慈善机构来帮助无家可归的人。|近义词：established, instituted, created, set up|反义词：destroyed, abolished, dismantled|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company founds new branches every year. = 这家公司每年都成立新的分公司。
He rarely founds new businesses. = 他很少创办新企业。


一般过去时:
They founded the company in 2000. = 他们于2000年创立了这家公司。
The city was founded centuries ago. = 这个城市是几个世纪前建立的。


现在完成时:
They have founded several successful startups. = 他们已经创办了几家成功的创业公司。
She has founded a non-profit organization dedicated to animal welfare. = 她已经创立了一个致力于动物福利的非营利组织。


过去完成时:
Before he became famous, he had founded several unsuccessful businesses. = 在他成名之前，他已经创办了几家不成功的企业。
They had founded the school long before I was born. = 他们在我出生很久以前就创办了这所学校。


将来时:
We will found a new research institute next year. = 我们明年将成立一个新的研究机构。
The government plans to found a new national park. = 政府计划建立一个新的国家公园。


虚拟语气:
If they had founded the company earlier, they would have been more successful. = 如果他们早些创立公司，他们会更成功。
I wish I had founded my own business years ago. = 我真希望几年前就创办了自己的公司。


被动语态:
The organization was founded on the principles of equality and justice. = 这个组织是基于平等与正义的原则建立的。
The museum was founded by a wealthy philanthropist. = 这个博物馆是由一位富有的慈善家创办的。


疑问句:
When was the organization founded? = 这个组织是什么时候成立的？
Who founded the company? = 谁创办了这家公司？


否定句:
They did not found the company until 1990. = 直到1990年他们才创办了这家公司。
He has not yet founded a successful business. = 他还没有创办成功的企业。


PHRASE->
founded on = 基于...
founded in = 在...成立
well-founded = 有充分理由的
ill-founded = 没有根据的
newly founded = 新成立的

词源:
The word "founded" comes from the Old French word "fonder," meaning "to cast, to build, to establish."  This ultimately derives from the Latin word "fundare," which has the same basic meaning.
