emulsioning:/ɪˈmʌlʒənɪŋ/ | v. | 乳化；制成乳状液 | The process of forming an emulsion, a mixture of two or more liquids that do not normally mix, such as oil and water.  This is achieved by using an emulsifier, which reduces the surface tension between the liquids and allows them to combine. | Example: The chef is emulsioning the egg yolks and oil to create a creamy sauce.  厨师正在将蛋黄和油乳化以制成奶油酱。 The machine is designed for efficient emulsioning of various liquids. 这台机器设计用于高效乳化各种液体。 |近义词：emulsifying, blending, mixing | 反义词：separating, stratifying
