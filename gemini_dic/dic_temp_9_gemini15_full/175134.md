rafting:/ˈræftɪŋ/| n. |漂流，乘木筏旅行 |The activity of traveling down a river or other waterway on a raft.  |例句：We went rafting on the Colorado River.  我们去科罗拉多河漂流了。|例句：White-water rafting is an exciting but dangerous sport. 激流漂流是一项刺激但危险的运动。|近义词：river-running, floating|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with activities like hiking or cycling.|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They go rafting every summer. = 他们每年夏天都去漂流。
He rafts down the river every weekend. = 他每个周末都沿河漂流。

一般过去时:
We rafted down the Amazon River last year. = 我们去年在亚马逊河上漂流。
She went rafting with her friends. = 她和朋友们一起去了漂流。

现在完成时:
I have never been rafting before. = 我以前从未漂流过。
They have rafted many rivers around the world. = 他们漂流过世界各地的许多河流。

过去完成时:
He had already rafted that river twice before. = 他之前已经两次漂流过那条河了。
We had never seen anything like that rafting trip before. = 我们以前从未见过像那次漂流旅行那样的景象。

将来时:
We will go rafting next month. = 我们下个月要去漂流。
They will be rafting in the Grand Canyon. = 他们将在科罗拉多大峡谷漂流。

疑问句:
