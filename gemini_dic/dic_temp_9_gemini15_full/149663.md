pairs:/pɛərz/ | n. | 对；双；夫妇 | A pair of shoes. 一双鞋。 |近义词：couple, duo, set |反义词：single, individual


USAGE->
一般现在时:
These are a pair of socks. = 这些是一双袜子。
We need two pairs of scissors. = 我们需要两把剪刀。


一般过去时:
They bought a pair of gloves yesterday. = 他们昨天买了一副手套。
I saw a pair of birds on the tree. = 我看见树上有一对鸟。


现在完成时:
She has found a pair of earrings she likes. = 她找到了一对她喜欢的耳环。
We have collected several pairs of antique vases. = 我们收藏了几对古董花瓶。
