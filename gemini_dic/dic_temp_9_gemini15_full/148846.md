overwarmed:/ˌoʊvərˈwɔːrmd/ | adj. |过热；过暖的 |  Describes something that has been heated or warmed to an excessive degree, often to the point of discomfort or damage.  | Example: The room was overwarmed, making it difficult to sleep.  房间太热了，难以入睡。  |近义词：too warm, excessively heated, superheated | 反义词：underwarmed, cool, chilled |  形容词
