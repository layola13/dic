overglamorizing: /ˌoʊvərˈɡlæməˌraɪzɪŋ/ | v. |过度美化；过分渲染 | To exaggerate the glamour or attractiveness of something.  It often implies a distortion of reality to present a more appealing or idealized image. | 例句：The advertisement overglamorized the product's capabilities, leading to disappointed customers.  广告过度美化了产品的性能，导致顾客失望。


The travel brochure overglamorized the island, making it seem far more luxurious than it actually was. 旅游手册过度渲染了这个岛屿，使其看起来比实际情况奢华得多。| 近义词：romanticize, idealize, embellish | 反义词：understate, downplay, minimize |  动词
