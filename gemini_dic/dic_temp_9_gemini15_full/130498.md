nonaccessible: /ˌnɒnækˈsɛsəbl/ | adj. | 不可访问的，难以接近的 |  Not able to be accessed; unavailable or unreachable. This term is often used in the context of computer systems, websites, or physical locations. It implies a barrier to access, whether physical, technological, or otherwise. |


例句：

The server is currently nonaccessible due to maintenance. = 服务器目前由于维护而无法访问。

The remote mountain village is nonaccessible by car during winter. = 冬季期间，汽车无法到达那个偏远的乡村。


近义词：inaccessible, unreachable, unavailable, unapproachable

反义词：accessible, reachable, available, approachable

词性：形容词
