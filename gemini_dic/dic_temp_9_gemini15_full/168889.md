profert:/ˈprɒfɜːt/ | n. |提出；出示；引证 | The formal act of producing a document in court.  It is the act of bringing forth or presenting something, especially a document or evidence, in a formal setting, particularly a court of law.  | Example: The lawyer made a profert of the contract during the trial. = 律师在审判期间出示了合同。  The plaintiff's profert of the deed proved ownership. = 原告出示的地契证明了所有权。 | 近义词：production, presentation, tender | 反义词：suppression, concealment | 名词
