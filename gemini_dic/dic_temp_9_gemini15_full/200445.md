slagging:/ˈslæɡɪŋ/| n. v.|渣滓；严厉批评；诋毁；污蔑|The act of criticizing someone severely or harshly;  to criticize severely.  It can also refer to the process of forming slag, a byproduct of smelting.|例句：The slagging of the opposition party was relentless during the election campaign.  选举期间对反对党的严厉批评是无情的。The steel mill produces large quantities of slag as a byproduct. 钢厂生产大量的炉渣作为副产品。|近义词：criticism, condemnation, denunciation, vilification, abuse|反义词：praise, commendation, compliment
