fishers:/ˈfɪʃərz/ | n. |渔民；渔夫；(某些动物的)捕食者 | People who catch fish, or animals that hunt and eat fish.  |例句：The fishers returned with a bountiful catch.  渔民们满载而归。 |例句：Otters are skilled fishers. 水獭是熟练的捕食者。|近义词：fishermen, anglers, trappers |反义词：N/A
