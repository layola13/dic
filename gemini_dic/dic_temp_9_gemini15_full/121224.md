misemployments:/ˌmɪsɪmˈplɔɪmənts/ | n. | 误用；滥用；错用 | The misuse or wrong use of something, especially skills, abilities, or resources.  It often implies a failure to utilize something to its full potential or for its intended purpose. | Example:  The misemployments of talented individuals in low-skilled jobs are a waste of human capital.  才华横溢的人才被误用于低技能的工作是人力资本的浪费。|近义词：misuses, misapplications, abuses | 反义词：optimal use, effective use, proper use
