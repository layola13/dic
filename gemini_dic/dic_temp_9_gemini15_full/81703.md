greenhorns:/ˈɡriːnhɔːrnz/ | n. | 菜鸟；新手；乳臭未干的小子 | Inexperienced or naive persons, especially newcomers to a place or situation.  The term often carries a slightly derogatory or humorous connotation. | Example: The greenhorns in the office made several costly mistakes during their first week.  办公室里的那些新手在他们工作的第一周犯了好几个代价高昂的错误。|近义词：newbie, novice, tyro, beginner | 反义词：expert, veteran, pro
