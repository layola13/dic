nonsocialistic: /ˌnɒnˌsoʊʃəlˈɪstɪk/ | adj. |非社会主义的|Not relating to or characteristic of socialism; opposed to socialism.  It describes something that is not aligned with the principles or practices of socialism, which advocates for social ownership and control of the means of production and distribution of goods. |The nonsocialistic policies of the government led to increased inequality.  政府的非社会主义政策导致了不平等的加剧。|近义词：antisocialist, noncommunist, capitalist  |反义词：socialistic, communist
