persistingly: /pərˈsɪstɪŋli/ | adv. | 坚持地；执着地 | In a persistent manner; stubbornly or continuously.  |例句：He persistingly pursued his dream, despite numerous setbacks. 他不顾多次挫折，坚持不懈地追求梦想。

例句：She persistingly questioned the politician until he finally answered. 她坚持不懈地质问那位政治家，直到他最终回答为止。


近义词：tenaciously, stubbornly, resolutely, doggedly, persistently


反义词：reluctantly, unwillingly, readily, easily


词源：源自persist (v.) + -ingly (adv. suffix).  persist  源自拉丁语persistere，由per-（完全地）和 sistere（站立）组成，字面意思是“坚持站立”。 -ingly 是副词后缀，表示“以…的方式”。
