erector:/ɪˈrektər/| n. |直立肌；勃起肌；起重机；竖立器 | A muscle that erects or raises a part of the body;  a device that erects or raises something.  It can also refer to a person or thing that erects something. |例句：The erector spinae muscles help to maintain posture.  直立肌有助于维持姿势。

例句：The construction crew used a powerful erector to lift the steel beams into place. 建筑队使用强大的起重机将钢梁吊装到位。


近义词：elevator, raiser, constructor (in the context of building) | 反义词：depressor (in the context of muscles) |  词性：名词
