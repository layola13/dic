hast:/hæst/| v.| 你有(古英语第二人称单数现在时)|The second-person singular present tense form of the verb "to have" in Old English and some early Modern English.| 例句：Hast thou seen the king? = 你看见国王了吗？|近义词： possess, own, have | 反义词: lack, want


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I have a car. = 我有一辆车。
You have a beautiful smile. = 你有一个美丽的笑容。


一般过去时:
I had a dream last night. = 我昨晚做了一个梦。
She had a lot of fun at the party. = 她在派对上玩得很开心。


现在完成时:
I have had this job for five years. = 我已经做了这份工作五年了。
They have had many successes in their career. = 他们在事业上取得了许多成功。


过去完成时:
I had had breakfast before I went to work. = 我去上班之前已经吃过早餐了。
She had had several interviews before she got the job. = 在得到这份工作之前，她已经参加过几次面试了。


将来时:
I will have finished the work by tomorrow. = 我明天将完成这项工作。
They will have moved to a new house by next year. = 他们明年将搬到新房子。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would buy a house. = 如果我有钱，我会买一栋房子。
I wish I had known sooner. = 我希望我早点知道。


被动语态:
The car has been repaired. = 汽车已经被修理好了。
The problem has been solved. = 问题已经被解决了。


疑问句:
Have you seen my keys? = 你看到我的钥匙了吗？
Has she finished her homework? = 她完成作业了吗？


否定句:
I do not have a car. = 我没有车。
She did not have time to go to the shop. = 她没有时间去商店。
