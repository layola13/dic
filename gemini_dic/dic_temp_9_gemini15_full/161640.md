pound:/paʊnd/ | n. v. | 磅；英镑；重击；捣碎 |  A unit of weight (avoirdupois), equal to 16 ounces; the basic monetary unit of the United Kingdom.  To strike or beat heavily; to crush or pulverize. |  例句：The package weighs five pounds. (这个包裹重五磅。) He pounded on the door. (他用力敲门。) | 近义词：(n.) kilogram, dollar, strike, batter; (v.) beat, strike, crush | 反义词：(n.) ounce, gram; (v.) caress, soothe | 词性: 名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pounds the nails into the wood. = 他把钉子钉进木头里。
The machine pounds the grain into flour. = 机器把谷物碾成面粉。


一般过去时:
She pounded the dough. = 她揉面团。
The horse pounded the ground with its hooves. = 马用蹄子猛烈地践踏地面。


现在完成时:
He has pounded the pavement looking for a job. = 他为了找工作四处奔波。
They have pounded the enemy's defenses. = 他们猛攻敌人的防线。


过去完成时:
She had pounded the table in anger. = 她气愤地拍桌子。
The rain had pounded the roof all night. = 雨整夜猛烈地敲打着屋顶。


将来时:
He will pound the rice into powder. = 他将把米碾成粉。
They will pound the enemy into submission. = 他们将把敌人打服。


虚拟语气:
If he pounded the door harder, someone might answer. = 如果他更用力敲门，也许会有人回应。
I wish I hadn't pounded the table so hard. = 我真希望我刚才没有那么用力拍桌子。


被动语态:
The table was pounded repeatedly. = 桌子被反复敲打。
The grain is pounded into flour by the mill. = 谷物由磨坊碾成面粉。


疑问句:
How many pounds does the bag weigh? = 这个包重多少磅？
Does he pound the keyboard while he is working? = 他工作时会猛敲键盘吗？


否定句:
He didn't pound the meat enough. = 他没有把肉充分地捶打。
The rain didn't pound the roof last night. = 昨晚雨没有猛烈地敲打屋顶。


PHRASE->
