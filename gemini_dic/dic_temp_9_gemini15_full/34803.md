climber: /ˈklaɪmər/ | n. | 登山者，攀登者 | A person who climbs mountains or rocks.  | Example:  Experienced climbers often use specialized equipment. 经验丰富的登山者经常使用专业设备。  |近义词: mountaineer, alpinist | 反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑用 non-climber)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He climbs mountains every weekend. 他每个周末都爬山。
She climbs the stairs two at a time. 她一次爬两级楼梯。

一般过去时:
They climbed Mount Everest last year. 他们去年攀登了珠穆朗玛峰。
He climbed a tree to retrieve his kite. 他爬上一棵树去取他的风筝。

现在完成时:
She has climbed all the major peaks in the Alps. 她已经攀登了阿尔卑斯山脉的所有主要山峰。
He has climbed that mountain three times. 他已经爬过那座山三次了。

过去完成时:
They had climbed for hours before they reached the summit. 他们在到达山顶之前已经攀登了几个小时。
She had climbed several mountains before attempting K2. 在尝试攀登K2之前，她已经攀登过几座山了。

将来时:
We will climb Kilimanjaro next summer. 我们明年夏天将攀登乞力马扎罗山。
They will climb the rock face using ropes and carabiners. 他们将使用绳索和登山扣攀登岩壁。

虚拟语气:
If I were a better climber, I would attempt Annapurna. 如果我是一个更好的登山者，我会尝试攀登安娜普尔纳峰。
I wish I could climb that sheer cliff. 我真希望我能攀登那座陡峭的悬崖。

被动语态:
The mountain was climbed by many people last year. 那座山去年被很多人攀登过。
The rock face is being climbed by a team of experts. 岩壁正在由一个专家团队攀登。

疑问句:
Does he climb often? 他经常爬山吗？
Have you ever climbed a mountain? 你曾经爬过山吗？

否定句:
She does not climb mountains. 她不爬山。
They didn't climb the highest peak. 他们没有攀登最高峰。


PHRASE->
rock climber: 岩石攀登者
mountain climber: 登山者
ice climber: 冰雪攀登者
experienced climber: 经验丰富的登山者
skilled climber: 技术娴熟的登山者
