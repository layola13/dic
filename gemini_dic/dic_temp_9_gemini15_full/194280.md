self-openers: /ˈsɛlf ˈoʊpənərz/ | n. | 自动开启装置；自动开启式产品 | Devices or products that open automatically.  They often utilize mechanisms such as springs, hinges, or electronic sensors to facilitate self-opening. |Example: The self-openers on the doors made entering the building very convenient. 自动门上的自动开启装置使进入大楼非常方便。  The new refrigerator features self-opening doors for easy access. 新冰箱具有自动开启的门，方便取用。|近义词：automatic openers, self-starting devices |反义词：manual openers, hand-operated devices |名词
