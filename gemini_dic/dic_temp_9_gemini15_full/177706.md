recant:/rɪˈkænt/| vt.|公开否认；撤回；放弃|To withdraw or retract a statement or belief, often formally or publicly.|例句：He recanted his earlier testimony. 他撤回了之前的证词。|例句：The scientist recanted his theory after new evidence emerged.  科学家在出现新的证据后撤回了他的理论。|近义词：retract, revoke, repudiate, withdraw|反义词：affirm, confirm, endorse, uphold|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recants his statement every day. = 他每天都收回自己的说法。
They recant their beliefs publicly. = 他们公开放弃他们的信仰。

一般过去时:
She recanted her confession. = 她撤回了她的供词。
He recanted his support for the project. = 他撤回了对该项目的支持。

现在完成时:
The witness has recanted his testimony. = 证人已经撤回了他的证词。
The church has recanted its previous position on the issue. = 教会已经撤回了其先前在这个问题上的立场。

过去完成时:
He had recanted his accusations before the trial. = 他在审判前已经撤回了他的指控。
They had recanted their earlier statements. = 他们已经撤回了之前的声明。

将来时:
She will recant her statement tomorrow. = 她明天将收回她的说法。
He will likely recant his support for the candidate. = 他很可能会撤回对候选人的支持。

虚拟语气:
If he were to recant his claims, the case would be dropped. = 如果他撤回他的说法，案件就会被撤销。
I wish he would recant his accusations. = 我希望他能撤回他的指控。

被动语态:
His statement was recanted under pressure. = 他的声明在压力下被撤回。
The theory was quickly recanted by the scientific community. = 该理论很快被科学界所摒弃。

疑问句:
Will he recant his testimony? = 他会撤回他的证词吗？
Did she recant her support for the bill? = 她撤回了对该法案的支持吗？

否定句:
He refused to recant his beliefs. = 他拒绝放弃他的信仰。
She would not recant her statement despite the pressure. = 尽管有压力，她还是不愿收回她的说法。


PHRASE->
