harlequins:/ˈhɑːrliˌkwɪnz/| n.|小丑；杂技演员；(尤指戏曲中的)丑角|A harlequin is a type of clown, often associated with pantomime or commedia dell'arte, characterized by their brightly colored, patched costume and mischievous nature.  They are often acrobatic and comedic performers.|例句：The children were delighted by the harlequins' acrobatic feats. 孩子们被小丑的杂技表演逗乐了。

The harlequin's colorful costume was a delight to watch.  小丑五彩缤纷的服装令人赏心悦目。|近义词：clown, jester, buffoon|反义词：serious performer, tragic actor
