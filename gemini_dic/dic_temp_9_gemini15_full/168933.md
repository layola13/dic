profferer:/ˈprɒfərər/| n. |提供者，提出者 |A profferer is a person who offers or presents something.  The word emphasizes the act of offering, often suggesting a willingness to give or share. |例句：The profferer of the bribe was caught red-handed.  贿赂提供者被当场抓住。|例句：He was a generous profferer of advice and assistance. 他是一位乐于提供建议和帮助的人。|近义词：offerer, giver, presenter |反义词：receiver, recipient
