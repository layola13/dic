owner-occupier:/ˈoʊnər ˈɑːkjupaɪər/ | n. |自住业主 |A person who owns and lives in a property.  This term is commonly used in real estate and housing market analysis. | Example:  The bank requires proof that the applicant is an owner-occupier before approving the mortgage.  银行在批准抵押贷款之前，要求申请人提供自己是自住业主的证明。|近义词: homeowner, resident owner |反义词: landlord, tenant


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The owner-occupier pays the property taxes. = 自住业主支付房产税。
He is an owner-occupier of a beautiful Victorian house. = 他是一位美丽的维多利亚式房屋的自住业主。


一般过去时:
She was an owner-occupier until she sold her house. = 她一直是自住业主，直到她卖掉房子。
They were owner-occupiers for over twenty years. = 他们做了二十多年的自住业主。


现在完成时:
He has been an owner-occupier since 2010. = 自2010年以来，他一直是自住业主。
We have only recently become owner-occupiers. = 我们最近才成为自住业主。


过去完成时:
Before the market crash, they had been owner-occupiers for five years. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经做了五年的自住业主。
She had never been an owner-occupier before buying that condo. = 在买下那套公寓之前，她从未做过自住业主。


将来时:
They will be owner-occupiers next year. = 他们明年将成为自住业主。
We will become owner-occupiers once the mortgage is approved. = 一旦抵押贷款获得批准，我们将成为自住业主。


虚拟语气:
If I were an owner-occupier, I would renovate the kitchen. = 如果我是自住业主，我会装修厨房。
I wish I were an owner-occupier so I could make changes to the property. = 我希望我是自住业主，这样我就可以对房产进行改动了。


被动语态:
The property was previously owned by an owner-occupier. = 这处房产以前由一位自住业主拥有。
Many properties are being bought by owner-occupiers in this area. = 在这个地区，许多房产正被自住业主买下。


疑问句:
Is she an owner-occupier? = 她是一位自住业主吗？
Are they owner-occupiers of that large house? = 他们是不是那栋大房子的自住业主？


否定句:
He is not an owner-occupier; he rents his apartment. = 他不是自住业主；他租了他的公寓。
They are not owner-occupiers; they are landlords. = 他们不是自住业主；他们是房东。
