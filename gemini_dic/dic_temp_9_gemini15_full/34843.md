clinicians:/klɪˈnɪʃənz/| n.|临床医生，临床实践者|Practitioners of medicine who provide direct patient care, often specializing in a particular area of medicine.|例句：The clinicians at the hospital are highly skilled and experienced. 医院的临床医生技术精湛，经验丰富。例句：Clinicians are essential for delivering effective healthcare. 临床医生对于提供有效的医疗保健至关重要。|近义词：doctors, physicians, medical practitioners|反义词：researchers (in some contexts)
