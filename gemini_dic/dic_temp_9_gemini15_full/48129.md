decoders:/dɪˈkoʊdərz/ | n. |解码器，译码器 |A decoder is a device or program that converts encoded data into a more understandable format.  It's essentially the opposite of an encoder. Decoders are used in various applications, from converting digital signals to audio in a stereo system to interpreting computer code. | Example: The satellite receiver uses a decoder to translate the signal into a watchable television program.  卫星接收器使用解码器将信号转换成可观看的电视节目。 The computer uses a decoder to translate binary code into instructions.  计算机使用解码器将二进制代码翻译成指令。 |近义词：translator, interpreter |反义词：encoder
