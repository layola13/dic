geminating:/ˈdʒɛməneɪtɪŋ/ | v. |  双生；发芽；(使)成对出现 |  The act of producing twins or of growing in pairs.  It can also refer to the sprouting of a seed or the process of a plant producing two seeds from a single ovule. | 例句：The farmer observed the geminating seeds pushing their way through the soil.  农民观察到发芽的种子正在破土而出。

The twins are a result of a geminating egg. 这对双胞胎是双生卵的结果。


近义词：sprouting, budding, twinning | 反义词：dying, withering
