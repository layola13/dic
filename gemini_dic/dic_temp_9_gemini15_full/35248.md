cluniac:/ˈkluːnjæk/| adj.|克吕尼的；克吕尼派的；|Of or relating to Cluny, a town in France famous for its Benedictine abbey; also relating to the Cluniac order of Benedictine monks.  |例句：The Cluniac architecture of the abbey is breathtaking. 克吕尼修道院的建筑令人叹为观止。
The Cluniac reforms brought about significant changes in monastic life. 克吕尼改革给修道院生活带来了重大变化。|近义词：Benedictine (in the context of monastic orders)|反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting secular or non-monastic life could be considered antonyms in context.|形容词
