USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs do.  Therefore, tense forms are not applicable.  The usage focuses on how the noun is used in different sentence structures.

The doctor assessed the curativeness of the treatment.  医生评估了治疗的疗效。
The study highlighted the high curativeness of the herbal remedy.  该研究强调了草药疗法的疗效很高。

The report detailed the curativeness demonstrated in clinical trials.  报告详细说明了临床试验中证明的疗效。

We have observed the remarkable curativeness of this natural treatment. 我们已经观察到了这种自然疗法的显著疗效。

Doctors had hoped for a higher curativeness from the experimental drug.  医生们曾希望实验药物有更高的疗效。
