reimplement:/ˌriːˈɪmplɪment/| vt.|重新实现，重新执行|To implement (a system, plan, etc.) again, often with improvements or changes.  It implies a previous implementation that is being replaced or updated.|例句：We need to reimplement the security system after the recent breach. 我们需要在最近的漏洞事件后重新实施安全系统。
例句：The company decided to reimplement the project using a new programming language. 公司决定使用新的编程语言重新实施该项目。|近义词：revise, revamp, rebuild|反义词：abandon, scrap
