mandiocas: /mænˈdiːkəs/ | n. |木薯(复数) | The plural form of "mandioca," referring to multiple cassava plants or their roots.  Mandiocas are a staple food in many parts of the world. | 例句：The farmer harvested a large quantity of mandiocas this year.  今年这位农民收获了大量的木薯。

The mandiocas were processed into flour and tapioca.  这些木薯被加工成木薯粉和西米。|近义词：cassavas (more common English term)|反义词：无
