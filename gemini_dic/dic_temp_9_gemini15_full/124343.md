motifs:/moʊˈtiːfs/| n.|主题；动机；图案；乐句|A motif is a recurring subject, theme, idea, image, character type, or situation that appears in a literary work or other creative work.  It can be a symbol, a metaphor, or a recurring event that contributes to the overall meaning and effect of the work.  In music, a motif is a short melodic or rhythmic phrase that is repeated and developed throughout a composition.|例句：The novel's recurring motif of betrayal significantly impacts the characters' relationships. 这部小说的背叛主题反复出现，极大地影响了人物之间的关系。

The recurring motif of the red rose symbolizes love and passion in the poem.  这首诗中反复出现的红玫瑰图案象征着爱情和激情。|近义词：theme, subject, pattern, design, idea, image|反义词：randomness, chance, disorder
