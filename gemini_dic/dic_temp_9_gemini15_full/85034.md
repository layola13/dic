harbor:/ˈhɑːrbər/ /ˈhɑːrbɚ/| n. v.|港口；避难所；怀有（感情）|A place on the coast where ships can find shelter, usually a sheltered body of water; to keep (a thought, feeling, etc.) in one's mind; to provide shelter or refuge for.|例句：The ship sailed into the harbor. 这艘船驶进了港口。  The harbor was filled with boats of all sizes. 港口里挤满了各种大小的船只。|近义词：port, haven, shelter|反义词：open sea, expose
