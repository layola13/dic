gallicism:/ˈɡælɪsɪzəm/| n. |法国习语；法语说法；受法语影响的表达方式|A word, phrase, or idiom borrowed from French and used in another language, often with a slightly different meaning or nuance.  It can also refer to a characteristic feature of French language or culture that is adopted by another language or culture.|例句：The use of "rendezvous" is a common gallicism in English. 使用“rendezvous”是英语中常见的法国习语。

例句：His writing style is peppered with gallicisms, making it unique and charming. 他的写作风格中充满了法语习语，使其独特而迷人。


近义词：Frenchism, foreignism | 反义词：无
