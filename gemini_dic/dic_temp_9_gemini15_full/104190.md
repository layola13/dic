junking:/ˈdʒʌŋkɪŋ/| v.|丢弃；报废；乱扔；|The act of discarding or getting rid of something, often something considered worthless or unwanted. It can also imply a careless or haphazard disposal.|例句：They're junking all the old computers. 他们正在丢弃所有旧电脑。

例句：The city is junking its old buses and replacing them with electric ones.  这个城市正在报废旧公交车，并用电动公交车替换它们。


近义词：discarding, scrapping, dumping, throwing away, disposing of


反义词：keeping, preserving, saving, repairing, restoring


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He junks old appliances regularly. = 他定期丢弃旧家电。
They junk unwanted clothes at the charity shop. = 他们把不需要的衣服扔到慈善商店。

一般过去时:
She junked her old car last week. = 她上周报废了她的旧车。
We junked the broken furniture. = 我们丢弃了坏掉的家具。

现在完成时:
They have junked several tons of waste. = 他们已经丢弃了几吨废物。
He has junked his old habits. = 他已经改掉了他的旧习惯。

过去完成时:
They had junked the old equipment before the new arrived. = 新设备到达之前，他们已经报废了旧设备。
She had junked the letter before reading it. = 她还没读就扔掉了那封信。

将来时:
We will junk this broken chair tomorrow. = 我们明天将丢弃这张坏掉的椅子。
They will junk the outdated technology. = 他们将淘汰过时的技术。

虚拟语气:
If I had more space, I wouldn't junk so many things. = 如果我有更多空间，我就不会丢弃这么多东西。
I wish I hadn't junked that old photo album. = 我真希望我没有丢弃那个旧相册。

被动语态:
The old cars are being junked. = 旧车正在被报废。
The broken toys were junked by the children. = 破玩具被孩子们扔掉了。

疑问句:
Are they junking the old building? = 他们要拆除那栋旧楼吗？
Will you junk the broken vase? = 你要丢弃那个破花瓶吗？

否定句:
He doesn't junk things easily. = 他不会轻易丢弃东西。
They didn't junk the valuable paintings. = 他们没有丢弃那些贵重的画作。
