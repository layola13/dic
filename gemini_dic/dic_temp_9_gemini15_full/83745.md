hailshot: /ˈheɪlʃɒt/ | n. |霰弹枪射击；散弹；（尤指）随意或不精确的射击 | A hailshot is a shot fired from a shotgun, typically consisting of numerous small pellets or shot.  It's often used to describe a wide, less precise shot compared to a single bullet.  The term can also be used metaphorically. | 例句：The hunter fired a hailshot at the flock of birds.  猎人向那群鸟射了一枪散弹。

The police fired a hailshot to disperse the crowd. 警方发射霰弹以驱散人群。


近义词：shotgun blast, scatter shot, spray of bullets | 反义词：single shot, precise shot | 词性:名词
