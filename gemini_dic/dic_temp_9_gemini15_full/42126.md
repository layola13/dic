cottoned:/ˈkɒtənd/|v.|理解，领悟；习惯于|to begin to understand something; to become accustomed to something|例句：I finally cottoned on to what he was saying.  我终于理解他在说什么了。|例句：She's cottoned on to the fact that he's lying. 她已经意识到他在说谎了。|近义词：understand, grasp, comprehend, get used to|反义词：misunderstand, fail to grasp


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He usually cottoned on to the jokes quickly. 他通常很快就能理解笑话。
They cotton on to new trends easily. 他们很容易就能理解新的趋势。


一般过去时:
I cottoned on to her secret after a while. 过了一段时间，我明白了她的秘密。
He quickly cottoned on to the trick. 他很快领会了这个技巧。


现在完成时:
She has cottoned on to the fact that he's been cheating on her. 她已经意识到他一直在欺骗她。
I have finally cottoned on to the meaning of the poem. 我终于领会了这首诗的意思。


过去完成时:
By then, I had cottoned on to their plan. 到那时，我已经明白了他们的计划。
She had cottoned on to his deception before he confessed. 在他坦白之前，她就看穿了他的欺骗。


将来时:
I think you will cotton on to the solution eventually. 我想你最终会找到解决办法的。
They will soon cotton on to what we're doing. 他们很快就会明白我们在做什么。


虚拟语气:
If only I had cottoned on to the problem earlier! 要是我早点明白这个问题就好了！
If she had cottoned on sooner, she could have avoided the trouble. 如果她早点明白过来，就能避免麻烦了。


被动语态:
The solution was quickly cottoned on to by the team.  团队很快领会了这个解决方案。
His sarcasm wasn't easily cottoned on to by everyone. 并非每个人都能轻易理解他的讽刺意味。


疑问句:
Did you cotton on to the hidden meaning? 你领会到了隐藏的含义吗？
Have you cottoned on to the significance of this event? 你领会到这次事件的重要性了吗？


否定句:
I didn't cotton on to the joke at first. 我一开始没明白这个笑话。
He didn't cotton on to the change in plans. 他没有领会计划的改变。
