disbursal:/dɪsˈbɜːrsəl/| n. |支付；发放；支出 |The act of paying out money; distribution.  It often refers to the distribution of funds from a central source to various recipients. |例句：The disbursal of funds to the various departments was delayed.  资金向各部门的发放被推迟了。|例句：The company announced a speedy disbursal of bonuses to its employees. 公司宣布将迅速向员工发放奖金。|近义词：payment, disbursement, distribution, allocation |反义词：receipt, collection, accumulation |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company disburses salaries at the end of each month. = 公司每月底支付工资。
The government disburses funds for various social programs. = 政府拨款用于各种社会项目。

一般过去时:
They disbursed the funds quickly and efficiently. = 他们迅速有效地支付了资金。
The organization disbursed aid to the victims of the disaster. = 该组织向灾难受害者发放援助。

现在完成时:
The bank has disbursed a significant amount of money this year. = 银行今年已支付了大量的资金。
The government has disbursed the emergency relief funds. = 政府已经拨付了紧急救济款项。

过去完成时:
They had disbursed all the money before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已支付了所有款项。
The committee had disbursed the grant money to the researchers. = 委员会已将拨款发放给了研究人员。

将来时:
The company will disburse the dividends next week. = 公司下周将支付股息。

虚拟语气:
If the bank disbursed the loan immediately, the project would start on time. = 如果银行立即支付贷款，项目就能按时启动。
