contributor:/kənˈtrɪbjʊtər/ | n. | 贡献者，投稿人 | A person who contributes something, especially money or time, to a common fund or cause; someone who submits material (such as an article or photograph) for publication. |  He is a major contributor to the charity. 他是该慈善机构的主要捐款人。 |  She is a regular contributor to the magazine. 她是该杂志的常客稿件撰写人。 | donor, giver, supporter | receiver, taker


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The contributor regularly donates to the charity. =这位捐赠者定期向慈善机构捐款。
He contributes articles to the online newspaper. = 他向在线报纸投稿。


一般过去时:
She contributed significantly to the project's success. = 她为项目的成功做出了重大贡献。
Many people contributed to the relief effort. = 许多人参与了救济工作。


现在完成时:
They have contributed generously to the cause. = 他们慷慨地为这项事业捐款。
She has contributed several articles to this journal. = 她已经向这份期刊投稿了几篇文章。


过去完成时:
He had already contributed his share before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经贡献了自己的份额。
They had contributed significantly to the research before the funding ran out. = 在资金耗尽之前，他们已经对研究做出了重大贡献。


将来时:
She will contribute to the discussion tomorrow. = 她明天将参与讨论。
He will contribute a chapter to the upcoming book. = 他将为即将出版的书撰写一章。


虚拟语气:
If he contributed more, we could achieve our goal sooner. = 如果他多贡献一些，我们就能更快地实现目标。
I wish she had contributed her ideas earlier. = 我希望她早点提出她的想法。


被动语态:
Significant funds were contributed by anonymous donors. = 大笔资金来自匿名捐赠者。
Articles are regularly contributed to the magazine by various writers. = 各个作者定期向杂志投稿。


疑问句:
Who contributed the most to the project? = 谁对这个项目贡献最大？
How much did each contributor donate? = 每位捐款者捐了多少钱？


否定句:
He did not contribute anything to the discussion. = 他没有对讨论做出任何贡献。
She hasn't contributed to the project yet. = 她还没有为该项目做出贡献。


PHRASE->

major contributor = 主要贡献者
anonymous contributor = 匿名贡献者
guest contributor = 客座投稿人
regular contributor = 常客投稿人
financial contributor = 资金贡献者
