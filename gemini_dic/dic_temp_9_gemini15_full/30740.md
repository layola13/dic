/sɪˈzəriən ˈsɛkʃənz/ | n. |剖腹产 | Cesarean sections, also known as C-sections, is a surgical procedure in which a baby is delivered through an incision in the mother's abdomen and uterus.  It is typically performed when vaginal delivery is not possible or is considered too risky for the mother or baby.  | The doctor recommended a Cesarean section because the baby was breech.  医生建议剖腹产，因为婴儿是臀位。 |  Surgical birth, abdominal delivery |  Vaginal delivery (natural childbirth)  |  名词
