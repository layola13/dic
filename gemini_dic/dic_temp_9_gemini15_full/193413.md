seismology:/saɪzˈmɒlədʒi/| n. |地震学|The scientific study of earthquakes and the propagation of elastic waves through the Earth.  Seismology involves the detection, recording, and analysis of seismic waves to understand the Earth's internal structure, tectonic processes, and earthquake hazards. | Example:  Seismology helps us understand the Earth's internal structure and predict earthquakes. 地震学帮助我们了解地球内部结构并预测地震。


| Example:  Researchers in seismology are constantly developing new techniques for monitoring seismic activity. 地震学研究人员不断开发监测地震活动的新技术。|近义词：earthquake science, seismic studies|反义词：无|
