overtaught:/ˌoʊvərˈtɔːt/| adj.|教得过多的；过度传授的| excessively taught;  having received too much instruction or information.  The students felt overtaught and overwhelmed by the sheer volume of material.|近义词：overburdened, overloaded, saturated|反义词：undertaught, neglected


USAGE->

The teacher overteaches the students every class. = 老师每节课都教得过多。
This course overteaches the basic concepts. = 这门课程对基本概念讲解过多。
