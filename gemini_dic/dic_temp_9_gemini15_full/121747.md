misprovoke:/ˌmɪs prəˈvoʊk/ | vt. | 误激怒；无意中激怒 | To provoke unintentionally or mistakenly.  | 例句：His insensitive comments misprovoked his friend, leading to a heated argument.  他的不敏感言论无意中激怒了他的朋友，导致了一场激烈的争吵。

例句：The comedian's joke, though meant to be lighthearted, misprovoked several audience members.  喜剧演员的玩笑，虽然本意是轻松的，却无意中激怒了几位观众。


近义词：irritate unintentionally, inadvertently anger, unintentionally offend


反义词：soothe, appease, placate


词性：动词
