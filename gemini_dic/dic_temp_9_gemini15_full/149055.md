ownership: /ˈoʊnərʃɪp/ | n. | 所有权，拥有；所有制 | The state or fact of owning something.  It refers to the legal right to possess, use, and dispose of property.  |例句：The ownership of the land is disputed. 这块土地的所有权存在争议。

例句：He transferred the ownership of the car to his son. 他把汽车的所有权转给了儿子。


近义词：possession, control, title, proprietorship | 反义词：lack of ownership, dispossession


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He owns the house. = 他拥有这栋房子。
The company holds the patent ownership. = 公司拥有这项专利的产权。


一般过去时:
She owned a large estate. = 她曾经拥有一个大型庄园。
They lost ownership of the business. = 他们失去了这家企业的拥有权。


现在完成时:
We have acquired ownership of the building. = 我们已经获得了这座建筑的所有权。
They have held ownership of the land for generations. = 他们世世代代拥有这块土地。


过去完成时:
Before the merger, they had already transferred ownership of the subsidiary. = 在合并之前，他们已经转让了子公司所有权。
She had lost ownership of her property before the lawsuit. = 在诉讼之前，她已经失去了自己财产的所有权。


将来时:
He will gain ownership of the property next month. = 他下个月将获得该房产的所有权。
The company will soon transfer ownership to its new owners. = 公司很快就会将其所有权转让给新业主。


虚拟语气:
If he had the money, he would easily acquire ownership of the company. = 如果他有足够的钱，他就能轻易获得公司的所有权。
I wish I had ownership of that island. = 我真希望拥有那个岛屿的所有权。


被动语态:
The property was owned by his grandfather. = 这处房产是他祖父拥有的。
Ownership of the painting is being disputed in court. = 这幅画的所有权正在法庭上争执。


疑问句:
Who owns this car? = 这辆车是谁的？
Does she have ownership of the intellectual property? = 她拥有这项知识产权的所有权吗？


否定句:
He doesn't have ownership of the land. = 他不拥有这块土地。
They did not retain ownership of the copyright. = 他们没有保留版权的所有权。



PHRASE->
joint ownership:  共有权
sole ownership: 单独所有权
fractional ownership:  部分所有权
intellectual property ownership: 知识产权所有权
ownership structure: 所有权结构

词源:
Ownership comes from the Old English word "āgen," meaning "own," which is related to the Old High German word "eigen," also meaning "own."  The suffix "-ship" denotes a state or condition, adding to the meaning of possession and control.
