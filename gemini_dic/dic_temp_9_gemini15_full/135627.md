nonpractically: /ˌnɒnˈpræktɪkli/ | adj. | 不切实际地，实际上不可行地 |  Not practical; not feasible in practice.  It implies a lack of practicality or usefulness in a real-world context.  The word combines "non-" (meaning "not") and "practically" (meaning "in a practical manner").


例句：

His plan was nonpractically ambitious. = 他的计划不切实际地雄心勃勃。

The solution, while theoretically sound, was nonpractically complex. = 虽然理论上合理，但这个解决方案实际上过于复杂。


近义词：impractically, impractically, unfeasibly, unrealistically, ideally


反义词：practically, realistically, feasibly


词性: 形容词
