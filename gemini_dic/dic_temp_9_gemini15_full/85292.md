harmonizes:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使和谐；协调；和睦相处 | To bring into harmony or agreement; to combine pleasingly. |The colors in the painting harmonize beautifully.  这幅画中的颜色搭配得非常漂亮。|The different musical instruments harmonize perfectly.不同的乐器完美地和谐统一。|近义词：accord, blend, match | 反义词：clash, conflict, discord


USAGE->

The colors harmonize beautifully. = 颜色搭配得很漂亮。
The singers harmonize perfectly. = 歌手们和声完美。
