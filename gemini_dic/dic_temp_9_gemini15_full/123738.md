moonshots:/ˈmuːnʃɒts/| n. |登月计划；雄心勃勃的目标；创新项目 |An ambitious and innovative project, especially one with a high risk of failure but with the potential for significant impact.  It originates from the Apollo 11 moon landing program. |Example: The company's new moonshot project aims to develop a cure for cancer. 这家公司新的登月计划项目旨在开发一种癌症的治疗方法。|近义词：ambitious project, innovative endeavor, breakthrough |反义词：small project, incremental improvement
