disinter:/dɪsˈɪntər/| vt.|掘出；挖出(尤指埋葬的东西)|To remove (something, especially a body) from a grave.  The verb "disinter" implies a careful and deliberate unearthing, often with a specific purpose, such as exhumation for forensic investigation or reburial in a different location.|例句：The archaeologists disinterred ancient artifacts from the burial site. 考古学家从墓地挖掘出古代文物。

例句：They disinterred the body to perform a second autopsy. 他们掘出尸体进行第二次尸检。


近义词：exhume, unearth, dig up|反义词：bury, inter, entomb|动词
