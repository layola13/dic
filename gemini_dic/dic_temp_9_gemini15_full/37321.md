commemorational:/ˌkəˌmɛməˈreɪʃənəl/| adj.|纪念的，追悼的| relating to the remembrance of a person or event, especially one of historical significance.  The adjective describes something designed to remember or honor a person or event.|例句：The commemorative stamp marked the 50th anniversary of the war. 这枚纪念邮票标志着战争50周年。

例句：The city held a commemorative service for the victims of the disaster.  城市为灾难的受害者举行了一场纪念仪式。


近义词：memorial, celebratory, remembering


反义词：forgetful, unmindful


词源:  From "commemorate" + "-ional".  "Commemorate" itself comes from Latin *commemorāre, from *com- (together) + memorāre (to remind, remember), from memoria (memory).
