gassing:/ˈɡæsɪŋ/| n. v.| 煤气中毒；施放毒气；吹嘘；闲聊 | The act of filling something with gas; the act of killing someone by using poisonous gas; boastful talk; idle talk. |例句：The gassing of the civilians was a terrible war crime.  平民被毒气袭击是一场可怕的战争罪行。


例句：He's always gassing on about his achievements. 他总是吹嘘自己的成就。

近义词：suffocation, poisoning, boasting, chatting


反义词：silencing,  refuting (for boasting aspect)


词性：noun, verb

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is gassing up his car. = 他正在给他的车加油。(filling with gas)
They are gassing about the football match. = 他们在闲聊足球比赛。(idle talk)

一般过去时:
The soldiers gassed the enemy trenches. = 士兵们向敌军战壕施放毒气。(using poisonous gas)
She gassed on about her trip to Europe. = 她滔滔不绝地讲述了她去欧洲的旅行。(boastful talk)

现在完成时:

He has been gassing about his new job all morning. = 他整个上午都在吹嘘他的新工作。(boastful talk)
