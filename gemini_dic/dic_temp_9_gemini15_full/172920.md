quakes:/kweɪks/ | n. |地震；震动 |Sudden violent shaking of the ground, typically causing great destruction, as a result of movements within the earth's crust or volcanic action.  |例句：The quakes caused widespread damage and loss of life. 地震造成大范围破坏和人员伤亡。|例句：The small quakes were barely felt. 轻微的地震几乎感觉不到。|近义词：earthquakes, tremors, shocks |反义词：calm, stillness
