self-trials:/ˈsɛlf ˈtraɪəlz/| n.|自我试验，自我测试|Self-trials refer to experiments or tests that an individual conducts on themselves, often to understand their own capabilities, limitations, or reactions to certain stimuli.  These trials can be informal and unscientific, or they can be more rigorous and systematic, depending on the individual's goals and resources.|例句：He conducted self-trials with different meditation techniques to see which one worked best for him. 他进行了不同的冥想技巧的自我试验，看看哪一种对他最有效。|例句：Her self-trials with various diets helped her understand her body's unique nutritional needs. 她对各种饮食的自我试验帮助她了解了她身体独特的营养需求。|近义词：self-experimentation, personal testing, self-assessment|反义词：controlled experiment, clinical trial|名词
