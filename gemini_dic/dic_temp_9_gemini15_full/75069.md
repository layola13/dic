frights:/fraɪts/ | n. |令人害怕的事物；惊吓；恐惧 |Things that frighten or scare someone; a sudden frightening experience; a feeling of fear. |例句：The old house was full of frights. 这栋老房子充满了令人害怕的东西。
The sudden noise gave me a fright. 突然的噪音吓了我一跳。|近义词：scares, terrors, horrors |反义词：comfort, reassurance

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud thunder gives me frights. = 响亮的雷声让我害怕。
Those scary movies give children frights. = 那些恐怖电影让孩子们害怕。


一般过去时:
The dark alley gave me a fright. = 黑巷让我吓了一跳。
The sudden appearance of a snake gave her several frights. = 蛇的突然出现让她吓了好几跳。


现在完成时:
I've had several frights in this haunted house. = 我在这个鬼屋里吓了好几次。
She's had many frights in her life. = 她一生中经历过许多惊吓。


过去完成时:
I had already had a few frights before I saw the ghost. = 在我看到鬼之前，我已经吓了几次了。
They had experienced many frights before reaching the summit. = 他们在到达山顶之前经历过许多惊吓。


将来时:
The dark shadows will give me frights tonight. = 今晚黑暗的影子会让我害怕。
I think that haunted house will give many people frights. = 我认为那栋鬼屋会吓到很多人。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would avoid that place, it gives many frights. = 如果我是你，我会避开那个地方，它会吓到很多人。
I wish I hadn't watched that movie; it gave me such frights. = 我真希望我没有看那部电影；它把我吓坏了。


被动语态:
Children are easily given frights by loud noises. = 孩子们很容易被巨大的噪音吓到。
Many people were given frights by the unexpected thunderstorm. = 许多人被突如其来的雷雨吓了一跳。


疑问句:

否定句:

The quiet countryside gave me no frights. = 安静的乡村没有让我害怕。
