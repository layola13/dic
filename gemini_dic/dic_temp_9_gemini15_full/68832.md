fascism: /ˈfæʃɪzəm/ | n. |法西斯主义 | A political ideology characterized by dictatorial power, forcible suppression of opposition, and strong regimentation of society and the economy.  It is often associated with extreme nationalism, militarism, and the belief in a single-party state.  | Example: The rise of fascism in Italy during the 1920s led to a period of authoritarian rule.  法西斯主义在20世纪20年代的意大利兴起导致了一段专制统治时期。


Example:  The fascist regime suppressed all dissent. 法西斯政权压制了所有异见。


近义词: authoritarianism, totalitarianism, despotism | 反义词: democracy, liberalism, anarchism
