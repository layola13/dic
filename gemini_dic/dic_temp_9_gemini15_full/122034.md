missupposes:/ˌmɪs səˈpoʊz/| vt.|误以为，误解|To have a wrong idea or belief about something or someone.  |例句：He missupposes that I am rich. 他误以为我很富有。|例句：I missupposed the meaning of the poem. 我误解了这首诗的意思。|近义词：misunderstand, misconceive, mistake|反义词：understand, comprehend
