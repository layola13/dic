ccd:/ˌsiːsiːˈdiː/ |abbr.|  缩写词|  电荷耦合器件；光盘；压缩光盘| Charge-Coupled Device; Compact Disc;  Compact Cassette Disc |The image sensor in many digital cameras is a CCD.  许多数码相机中的图像传感器是CCD。|近义词:CMOS(在图像传感器领域)|反义词:无


USAGE->
(CCD作为缩写词，本身不作动词、名词、形容词等词性变化，其用法取决于它所指代的具体含义。)

* **Charge-Coupled Device (电荷耦合器件):**  This is a type of semiconductor device used for capturing images.
例句1：The astronomer used a CCD camera to capture images of distant galaxies.  天文学家使用CCD相机拍摄遥远星系的图像。
例句2：Modern CCD sensors offer significantly improved image quality compared to older technologies. 与旧技术相比，现代CCD传感器提供了显著改进的图像质量。


* **Compact Disc (光盘):** This is a digital optical disc data storage format.
例句1：I listened to my favorite music on a compact disc. 我用光盘听我最喜欢的音乐。
例句2：The information was stored on a CD-ROM.  信息存储在一个CD-ROM上。


* **Compact Cassette Disc (压缩盒式磁带):** This is a less common term, sometimes used to refer to a small, compact cassette tape.
例句1：I found an old compact cassette disc with my childhood songs. 我找到了一盘装有我童年歌曲的旧的压缩盒式磁带。
例句2：The audio quality of the compact cassette disc was surprisingly good. 压缩盒式磁带的音质出奇地好。(此例句略显不自然，因为CCD很少用于指代盒式磁带。)

PHRASE->
(CCD本身不构成短语，其用法取决于其指代的含义)  例如，与"Compact Disc"相关的短语：
CD-ROM: CD-ROM drive. CD-ROM驱动器
CD-R: CD-R disc. CD-R光盘
CD-RW: CD-RW burner. CD-RW刻录机
audio CD: audio CD player. 音频CD播放器
data CD: data CD creation. 数据光盘创建
