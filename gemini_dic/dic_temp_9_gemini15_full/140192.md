officeful: /ˈɒfɪsfʊl/ | n. |办公室的；充满办公室氛围的 |A word rarely used, describing something full of the characteristics of an office. It suggests a feeling of officiousness, bureaucracy, or the general atmosphere of an office environment.  The word is formed by combining "office" and the suffix "-ful," which indicates "full of."  It carries a slightly negative connotation, implying excessive formality, routine, or tediousness.  |The officeful of paperwork was overwhelming.  办公室里堆积如山的文书工作令人难以招架。|  近义词：bureaucratic, officious, formal |反义词：informal, relaxed, casual
