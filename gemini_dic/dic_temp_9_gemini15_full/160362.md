popcorn: /ˈpɒpkɔːrn/ /ˈpɑːpkɔːrn/ | n. | 爆米花 |  A snack food made from kernels of corn that puff up when heated. It is often seasoned with butter, salt, or other flavorings. | 例句：We bought a large bucket of popcorn at the cinema. = 我们在电影院买了一大桶爆米花。

例句：The popcorn was so delicious, I ate the whole bag. = 爆米花太好吃了，我吃掉了整袋。


近义词：corn, puffed corn | 反义词：无


词源:The word "popcorn" is a compound word, combining "pop" and "corn". "Pop" refers to the sound the kernels make when they burst open during heating, and "corn" refers to the type of grain used.  The term first appeared in print in the late 19th century, reflecting the increasing popularity of this snack.  The origin of the corn itself traces back to ancient Mesoamerica.
