gendered:/ˈdʒɛndərd/ | adj. | 性别的；具有性别特征的 | Relating to or characterized by gender.  The term often highlights how social constructs and expectations shape our understanding of gender roles and identities.  It can refer to the assignment of gender roles to individuals or things, or to the ways in which gender influences language, behavior, or social structures. | Example:  Many children's toys are gendered, with dolls marketed towards girls and trucks towards boys.  许多儿童玩具都带有性别色彩，洋娃娃针对女孩销售，卡车针对男孩销售。|近义词：sexed, sex-specific |反义词：gender-neutral, ungendered | 词性：形容词
