debating:/dɪˈbeɪtɪŋ/| v. |辩论；讨论；争论|The act of engaging in a formal discussion or argument, often with the aim of persuading others to adopt a particular viewpoint. It involves presenting reasoned arguments, refuting opposing viewpoints, and potentially reaching a conclusion.  |例句：They are debating the merits of different economic policies. 他们正在辩论不同经济政策的优缺点。

例句：The students are debating whether capital punishment is justified. 学生们正在辩论死刑是否正当。


近义词：arguing, discussing, disputing, considering


反义词：agreeing, concurring, consenting


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They debate the issue every week. = 他们每周都辩论这个问题。
We are debating the pros and cons of this plan. = 我们正在辩论这个计划的利弊。


一般过去时:
They debated the topic for hours. = 他们就这个话题辩论了几个小时。
The committee debated the proposal before voting. = 委员会在投票前对提案进行了辩论。


现在完成时:
They have debated this many times. = 他们已经多次辩论过这个问题了。
We have debated the merits of both sides. = 我们已经辩论了双方的优缺点。


过去完成时:
They had debated the issue before the meeting. = 他们曾在会议前辩论过这个问题。
The team had debated the strategy for days. = 该团队已经就策略辩论了几天。


将来时:
They will debate the motion tomorrow. = 他们明天将辩论这项动议。
We will be debating the new policy next week. = 我们下周将讨论新政策。


虚拟语气:
If they debated more openly, they might reach a better solution. = 如果他们更公开地辩论，他们可能会找到更好的解决方案。
I wish they debated the matter more thoroughly. = 我希望他们更彻底地讨论这个问题。


被动语态:
