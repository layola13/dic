cylindroid:/ˈsɪlɪndrɔɪd/| n. |圆柱体状物，类圆柱体|A geometrical solid resembling a cylinder but having a slightly curved surface.  It is often used in optics and other scientific fields to describe shapes that are approximately cylindrical but not perfectly so. |例句：The lens had a cylindroidal shape, slightly bulging in the middle.  该透镜呈类圆柱形，中间略微凸起。
