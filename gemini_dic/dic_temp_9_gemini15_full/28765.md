The carnivoracity of the piranhas was shocking. 食人鱼的贪食令人震惊。


近义词：voracity, gluttony, rapacity | 反义词：vegetarianism, abstinence


词源：源自carnivore（食肉动物）+ -acity（表示性质或状态的后缀）。  Carnivore itself comes from Latin "carnis" (flesh) + "vorare" (to devour).
