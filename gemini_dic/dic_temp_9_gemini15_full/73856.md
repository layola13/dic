formulas:/ˈfɔːrmjʊləz/ | n. | 公式；配方；方案 |  Formulas are mathematical expressions that represent relationships between variables.  They are used to solve problems and make predictions. | Example:  The formula for the area of a circle is πr².  圆的面积公式是πr²。  The secret recipe includes a complex formula of herbs and spices. 秘密配方包括一种复杂的草药和香料配方。 |近义词：equations, expressions, recipes |反义词：无
