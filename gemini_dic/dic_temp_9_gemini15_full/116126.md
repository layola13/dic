marxism:/ˈmɑːksɪzəm/ | n. | 马克思主义 | A body of thought deriving its inspiration from Karl Marx's writings, encompassing historical materialism, critique of capitalism, class struggle, and revolutionary ideas.  It advocates for a classless society and the abolition of private property. |  Many countries adopted Marxist principles after the Russian Revolution. 许多国家在俄国革命后采用了马克思主义原则。

The study of Marxism is crucial to understanding 20th-century history. 研究马克思主义对于理解20世纪的历史至关重要。|  Communism, Socialism | Capitalism, Liberalism | 名词
