fulfiller:/fʊlˈfɪlər/| n. |履行者；实现者；满足者| A person or thing that fulfills a role, need, or expectation.  Someone who completes tasks or promises. |例句：He was a dedicated fulfiller of his duties. 他是一位尽职尽责的责任履行者。

例句：The company is seeking a fulfiller to manage its logistics. 这家公司正在寻找一位履行者来管理其物流。


近义词：completer, achiever, performer, implementer |反义词：neglecter, defaulter, shirker |词性：名词
