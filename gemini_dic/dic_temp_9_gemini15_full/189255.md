saddleback:/ˈsæd(ə)lˌbæk/| n. adj.|鞍背；鞍状的；(指山峰)马鞍形的|A saddleback is a hill or mountain with a shape resembling a saddle.  The term can also be used to describe something with a similar shape, such as a type of pig. |例句：The hikers admired the saddleback mountain from afar.  远处的登山者欣赏着马鞍形山峰。 The saddleback pig is known for its unusual appearance.  鞍背猪以其奇特的外观而闻名。|近义词：ridge, hump, peak|反义词：valley, plain
