prosaist:/ˈproʊzeɪst/| n. |散文作家；散文学家| A writer of prose, especially fiction.  Prosaists are distinguished from poets by their use of everyday language and structure rather than poetic meter and rhyme.|Example:  Many consider Jane Austen a master prosaist, celebrated for her witty and insightful prose.  许多人认为简·奥斯汀是一位散文大师，以其诙谐而富有洞察力的散文而闻名。|近义词：novelist, writer, author|反义词：poet
