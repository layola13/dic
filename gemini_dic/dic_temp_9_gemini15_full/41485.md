corns:/kɔːrnz/| n.|玉米粒，鸡眼| Kernels of maize or other cereal grain; also, a hardened area of skin.  |例句：The farmer harvested a bumper crop of corns.  这位农民收获了一大批玉米。

例句：He had a painful corn on his little toe.  他的小脚趾上长了一个痛苦的鸡眼。|近义词：kernels, calluses|反义词：smooth skin
