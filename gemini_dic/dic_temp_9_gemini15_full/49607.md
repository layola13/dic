demi-gorge:/ˈdɛmiɡɔːrdʒ/| n.|半峡谷，半山口|A narrow, steep-sided valley or gorge, typically smaller than a full gorge.  It often refers to a pass or defile in mountainous terrain.|例句：The hikers traversed the treacherous demi-gorge, clinging to the rocky walls.  徒步旅行者穿越了险峻的半峡谷，紧紧抓住岩石峭壁。|例句：The demi-gorge offered a spectacular view of the surrounding peaks. 半峡谷提供了周围山峰的壮丽景色。|近义词：ravine, defile, chasm|反义词：plain, plateau
