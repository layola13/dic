deterrents: /dɪˈtɜːrənts/ | n. |威慑物；制止因素 | Something that discourages or is intended to discourage someone from doing something.  A deterrent is something that makes someone less likely to do something.  | 例句：The high price of cigarettes acts as a deterrent to young smokers. 高昂的香烟价格对年轻吸烟者起到了威慑作用。


The heavy prison sentences are intended as a deterrent to potential criminals.  严厉的监禁判决旨在震慑潜在的罪犯。|近义词：disincentive, preventative, restraint |反义词：incentive, encouragement


USAGE->
一般现在时:
High prices act as a deterrent to many shoppers. = 高价对许多购物者起到了威慑作用。
This policy serves as a deterrent to illegal activities. = 这项政策对非法活动起到了威慑作用。

一般过去时:
The threat of punishment served as a deterrent to further wrongdoing. = 惩罚的威胁阻止了进一步的错误行为。
The harsh penalties acted as a deterrent to potential offenders. = 严厉的惩罚对潜在的罪犯起到了威慑作用。

现在完成时:
The new security measures have acted as a deterrent to crime in the area. = 新的安全措施对该地区的犯罪起到了威慑作用。
Stricter laws have proven to be a deterrent to drunk driving. = 更严格的法律已被证明对酒后驾车起到了威慑作用。

过去完成时:
The existing laws had not been effective deterrents to drug trafficking. = 现有的法律对贩毒并没有起到有效的威慑作用。
Previous attempts at deterrents had failed to stop the problem. = 此前的威慑尝试未能阻止问题。

将来时:
Increased police patrols will act as a deterrent to vandalism. = 加强巡逻将对破坏行为起到威慑作用。
The new regulations will hopefully serve as a deterrent to future violations. = 新规有望对未来的违规行为起到威慑作用。

虚拟语气:
If there were stricter penalties, it would be a greater deterrent. = 如果有更严厉的处罚，它将起到更大的威慑作用。
If the government implemented stronger deterrents, crime rates might decrease. = 如果政府实施更强有力的威慑措施，犯罪率可能会下降。

被动语态:
Harsh punishments are used as deterrents to crime. = 严厉的惩罚被用作犯罪的威慑手段。
Effective deterrents are being developed to combat terrorism. = 正在开发有效的威慑措施以打击恐怖主义。

疑问句:
What deterrents are in place to prevent this from happening again? = 有什么措施可以防止这种情况再次发生？
What effective deterrents can we implement to reduce speeding? = 我们可以实施哪些有效的威慑措施来减少超速？

否定句:
The proposed measures were not effective deterrents. = 提出的措施并没有起到有效的威慑作用。
The lack of consequences proved to be no deterrent. = 缺乏后果证明并没有起到威慑作用。
