repenter's:/rɪˈpɛntərz/| n.|悔过者的；忏悔者的|The word "repenter's" is a possessive form of the noun "repenter," referring to something belonging to or associated with a person who repents.  It often appears in phrases describing the actions, feelings, or possessions of someone who is remorseful for past actions.|例句：The repenter's tears flowed freely. 悔过者的泪水 freely 流淌。 The repenter's journey toward forgiveness was long and arduous. 悔过者通往宽恕的道路漫长而艰辛。|近义词：penitent's, contrite's|反义词：unrepentant's
