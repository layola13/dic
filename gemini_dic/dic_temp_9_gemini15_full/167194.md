pretence:/prɪˈtɛns/| n.|假装，伪装，虚饰|The act of pretending; a false show or appearance.|例句：His cheerful pretence couldn't hide his anxiety. 他快乐的假装掩盖不了他的焦虑。|近义词：simulation, affectation, sham|反义词：genuineness, authenticity, reality|名词


to:/tuː, tə/| prep.|到，向，对于|Expressing motion in the direction of, or arrival at, a particular location or state.|例句：She walked to the store. 她走到商店。|近义词：toward, onto, towards|反义词：from, away from|介词


pretence-to:/prɪˈtɛns tuː/| n. phr.|对…的假装，声称拥有…|A claim or show of having something that one does not actually possess or be.|例句：His pretence to wealth was easily seen through. 他声称拥有财富很容易就被识破了。  |近义词：false claim, affected possession, feigned ownership|反义词：genuine possession, authentic ownership|名词短语

USAGE->

Their pretence to innocence is unconvincing. = 他们假装清白的说法令人难以信服。


一般过去时:

His pretence to royalty was quickly exposed. = 他假扮皇室成员的把戏很快就被揭穿了。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
