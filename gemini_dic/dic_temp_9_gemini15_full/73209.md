forcefully:/ˈfɔːrsfəli/ | adv. | 强有力地；坚决地；强行地 |  In a forceful manner; with strong determination or energy.  It implies action or expression characterized by strength and intensity. |

例句：

He forcefully pushed open the door. 他用力推开了门。

She forcefully argued her case before the judge. 她强有力地向法官陈述了自己的案情。


近义词：strongly, powerfully, vigorously, resolutely, emphatically

反义词：weakly, gently, mildly, hesitantly

词性：副词
