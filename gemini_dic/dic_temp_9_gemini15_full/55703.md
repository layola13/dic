diurnally:/ˈdaɪɜːrnəli/| adv. |昼夜地，每天地| happening or occurring during the day; daily|例句：The plant opens its flowers diurnally. 这植物每天都开花。

例句：He diurnally checks his email. 他每天都查看邮件。

近义词：daily, daily, everyday

反义词：nocturnally


词源:

The word "diurnally" is derived from the Latin word "diurnus," meaning "daily" or "belonging to the day."  The suffix "-ally" is an adverbial suffix, indicating manner or circumstance.  Therefore, "diurnally" literally means "in a daily manner" or "daily."
