permuter:/pərˈmjuːtər/ | n. |置换器，排列器 | A device or algorithm that generates permutations (arrangements) of a set of items. 词源:
The word "permuter" is derived from the verb "permute," meaning to arrange or order in a particular way, especially to arrange all possible orderings of a set.  "Permute" itself comes from the Late Latin word *permutare*, a combination of *per-* (through, thoroughly) and *mutare* (to change).  Therefore, a permuter is a device or algorithm that performs the action of permuting.
