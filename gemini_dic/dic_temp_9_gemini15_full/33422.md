churchwardenize:/ˌtʃɜːrtʃˈwɔːrdənˌaɪz/ | vt. |使具有教堂监事的气质或行为；使变得古板守旧|To cause (someone or something) to take on the characteristics of a churchwarden, especially in terms of being conservative, traditional, and somewhat officious.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting an excessive adherence to rules and traditions.|例句：The new regulations were criticized for churchwardenizing the school's atmosphere, stifling creativity and free expression.  新的规章制度因使学校氛围变得古板守旧而受到批评，扼杀了创造力和自由表达。|近义词：formalize, conventionalize, fossilize|反义词：modernize, liberalize
