overlarding: /ˌoʊvərˈlɑːrdɪŋ/ | v. |过度施肥；过度使用肥料 | The act of applying excessive amounts of fertilizer to soil, often resulting in negative consequences for plant health and the environment.  Overlarding can lead to nutrient imbalances, soil degradation, and water pollution. | The farmer's overlarding of the field resulted in a bumper crop initially, but the soil was depleted in the following years. = 农民对田地的过度施肥 initially导致了丰收，但在接下来的几年里，土壤却枯竭了。 |  fertilizing excessively, overmanuring |  underfertilizing
