/ˌmʌltiˈkɒnɪk/ | adj. | 多锥面的；多圆锥的 | Relating to or denoting a map projection in which the surface of the Earth is projected onto a series of intersecting cones.  This type of projection is useful for representing large areas with minimal distortion, particularly in mid-latitudes. | Example: The multiconic projection is often used for topographic maps of large regions.  多圆锥投影常用于绘制大区域的地形图。 | 近义词: polyconic (less common) | 反义词: planimetric (flat projection)
