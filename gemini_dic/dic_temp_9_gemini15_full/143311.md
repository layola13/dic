outduels:/ˌaʊtˈduːəlz/| vt.|胜过(尤指在决斗中)|to defeat someone, especially in a duel or contest|例句：The seasoned swordsman outdueled his younger opponent.  经验丰富的剑客击败了他的年轻对手。
例句：In a fierce debate, she outdueled her rival with her sharp wit and compelling arguments. 在激烈的辩论中，她凭借敏捷的才智和令人信服的论据击败了她的对手。|近义词：defeat, outsmart, surpass|反义词：lose to, be defeated by|动词
