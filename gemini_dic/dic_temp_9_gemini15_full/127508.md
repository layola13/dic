/nekˈmoʊld/ | n. |颈模；颈圈模具| A neckmould is a mold used to shape the neck area of a garment, typically in the manufacturing of clothing or other items requiring a shaped neck.  It is used to create consistent and accurate necklines. | Example: The tailor used a neckmould to ensure a perfect fit around the customer's neck.  裁缝用颈模来确保衣服完美贴合顾客的脖子。|近义词：neck block, collar form|反义词：N/A
