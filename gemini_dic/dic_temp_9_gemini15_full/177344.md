reattempted:/ˌriːəˈtɛmptɪd/| v. |再次尝试，重新尝试|To attempt something again after a previous failure.|The athlete reattempted the high jump after a failed first attempt. 运动员在第一次跳高失败后再次尝试。|近义词：tried again, attempted again|反义词：gave up, abandoned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reattempts the exam every year. 他每年都重考。
They reattempt the project with a new strategy. 他们用新的策略重新尝试这个项目。

一般过去时:
She reattempted the recipe after burning the first batch. 她第一次烤糊了之后又重新尝试了这个食谱。
He reattempted the climb after his initial failure. 他第一次攀登失败后再次尝试。

现在完成时:
I have reattempted the puzzle several times but still haven't solved it. 我已经多次尝试拼图了，但仍然没有解开。
They have reattempted the experiment with improved methodology. 他们用改进的方法重新进行了实验。

过去完成时:
She had reattempted the test before realizing her mistake. 她意识到自己的错误之前已经重考过了。
They had reattempted the negotiations before reaching an agreement.  他们在达成协议之前已经重新进行了谈判。

将来时:
He will reattempt the marathon next year. 他明年将再次参加马拉松比赛。
She will reattempt the surgery after further consultation.  经过进一步的会诊后，她将再次进行手术。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would reattempt the project. 如果我有更多时间，我会重新尝试这个项目。
It's a shame he didn't reattempt the interview; he might have succeeded. 很可惜他没有再次尝试面试；他可能已经成功了。

被动语态:
The experiment was reattempted under different conditions. 实验在不同的条件下进行了重新尝试。
The painting was reattempted using a different technique. 这幅画用不同的技法重新绘制。

疑问句:
Will you reattempt the question? 你会重新尝试回答这个问题吗？
Have they reattempted the mission? 他们重新尝试执行任务了吗？

否定句:
He did not reattempt the exam. 他没有重考。
She will not reattempt the climb. 她不会再次尝试攀登。
