fuelers:/ˈfjuːlər/| n.|加油者；燃料供应者|A person or thing that fuels something.  This can refer to a person who fuels vehicles, a machine that fuels aircraft, or even a metaphorical source of motivation or inspiration.|例句：The airport employs many fuelers to keep the planes running. 机场雇佣了许多加油员来保证飞机的运行。

例句：Her passion for justice fueled her tireless work for human rights. 她对正义的热情激励着她为争取人权而不知疲倦地工作。|近义词：refueler, supplier, provider|反义词:depleter, consumer
