meager:/ˈmiːɡər/| adj.| 贫乏的；不足的；瘦弱的 |  Lacking in quantity or quality; scanty; poor; thin.  |例句：The meager harvest barely provided enough food for the winter.  = 歉收几乎无法提供足够的粮食过冬。

例句：He survived on a meager diet of bread and water. = 他靠着少得可怜的面包和水生存。


近义词：scanty, scanty, insufficient, paltry,  thin, slight,  inadequate

反义词：abundant, plentiful, ample, copious, substantial,  rich,  luxurious


词源：源自古法语 *megre，意为“瘦弱的”，最终源自古高地德语 *mag(a)r，意为“瘦弱的，贫乏的”。
