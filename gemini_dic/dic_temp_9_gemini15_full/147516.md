overparking: /ˌoʊvərˈpɑːrkɪŋ/ | n. | 超时停车；占用过多停车位 | The act of parking a vehicle for longer than the permitted time or in a way that obstructs other vehicles or pedestrians.  It often leads to fines or towing. |例句：Overparking in this area results in a hefty fine.  在这个区域超时停车会导致巨额罚款。

例句：The overparking of delivery trucks blocked the alleyway. 送货卡车占用过多停车位阻塞了小巷。


近义词：illegal parking, exceeding parking limits, parking violation | 反义词：proper parking, timely removal of vehicle | 词性: noun
