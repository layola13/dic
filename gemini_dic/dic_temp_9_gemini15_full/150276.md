panegyrical:/ˌpænɪˈdʒɪrɪkəl/| adj.|赞美的，颂扬的|  Characterized by or expressing praise, especially excessive or fulsome praise.  It implies a formal and often elaborate expression of praise.|例句：The panegyrical speech lauded the achievements of the retiring CEO.  赞美的演讲赞扬了即将退休的首席执行官的成就。

例句：His panegyrical essay on the poet was filled with glowing tributes. 他关于这位诗人的颂扬性文章充满了热烈的赞美。


近义词：laudatory, eulogistic, encomiastic, celebratory, commendatory

反义词：critical, censorious, condemnatory, derogatory


词性：形容词
