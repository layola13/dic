galloped:/ˈɡæləpt/| v.|飞奔，疾驰|To run at a gallop; move rapidly.|The horse galloped across the field.  这匹马飞奔过田野。  The children galloped down the hill. 孩子们飞奔下山。|ran, hurried, dashed|walked, crawled|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gallops his horse every morning. = 他每天早上骑马飞奔。
The horse gallops across the field. = 马飞奔过田野。


一般过去时:
The horse galloped across the field. = 马飞奔过田野。

The horse has galloped many miles today. = 这匹马今天已经跑了许多英里。

将来时:

I wish I could gallop like the wind. = 我真希望我能像风一样飞奔。


被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice because it requires a subject performing the action.)
