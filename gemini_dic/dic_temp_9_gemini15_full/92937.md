identifying: /aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v.ing | 识别；鉴定；确认 | Identifying the problem is the first step to solving it.  识别问题是解决问题的第一步。 | 近义词：recognizing, pinpointing, determining | 反义词：misidentifying, overlooking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are identifying the suspects in the lineup. = 他们正在辨认嫌疑犯。
We are identifying the needs of our customers. = 我们正在确定客户的需求。

一般过去时:
The police identified the thief from the security footage. = 警方根据监控录像认出了小偷。
She identified her attacker in court. = 她在法庭上指认了袭击者。

现在完成时:
Scientists have identified a new species of plant. = 科学家们已经发现了一种新的植物物种。
They have identified the cause of the accident. = 他们已经查明了事故的原因。

过去完成时:
The investigators had identified the killer before the arrest. = 调查人员在逮捕之前已经确定了凶手。
She had already identified the problem before I pointed it out. = 我指出问题之前，她已经发现了。

将来时:
We will identify the best candidate for the job. = 我们将确定最合适的候选人。
They will identify the risks associated with the project. = 他们将确定与该项目相关的风险。

虚拟语气:
If we identified the problem earlier, we could have prevented it. = 如果我们早点发现问题，就能预防了。
I wish I had identified the problem sooner. = 我希望我早点发现这个问题。

被动语态:
The suspect was identified by a witness. = 嫌疑人被一名目击者认出。
The problem was identified as a software glitch. = 该问题被确定为软件故障。

疑问句:
Can you identify the missing piece? = 你能找到丢失的那块吗？
How did you identify the solution? = 你是如何找到解决方案的？

否定句:
I couldn't identify the voice on the phone. = 我无法辨认电话里的声音。
The experts failed to identify the cause of the illness. = 专家们未能确定疾病的病因。
