pitiably:/ˈpɪtɪəbli/| adj.|可怜地，悲惨地| causing or deserving pity; in a pitiable manner.  It describes something or someone that evokes pity or compassion due to their unfortunate or helpless condition. |例句：He looked pitiably thin and weak after his illness. 他生病后看起来可怜巴巴的，又瘦又弱。
The abandoned puppy whimpered pitiably.  被遗弃的小狗可怜地呜咽着。|近义词：pathetically, miserably, sadly, wretchedly |反义词：gloriously, triumphantly, wonderfully

词源:
