reintroducing:/ˌriːˈɪntrəˈduːsɪŋ/| v. |重新介绍；再次引进；重新提出|The act of introducing something again.  This can refer to a person, a concept, a product, or anything that was previously introduced but is now being presented again, perhaps to a new audience or in a new context. |例句：The company is reintroducing its classic product line after years of focusing on newer models. 公司在专注于更新型号多年后，正在重新推出其经典产品线。

例句：The professor is reintroducing the concept of quantum physics to the students, using a more accessible approach. 教授正在使用更容易理解的方法向学生重新介绍量子物理的概念。


近义词：reintroducing, re-presenting, reviving, relaunching |反义词：discontinuing, abandoning, discarding |词性：动词
