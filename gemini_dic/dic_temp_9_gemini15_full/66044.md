exarchate:/ˈɛksɑːrkeɪt/ | n. |主教辖区；总主教区；（东正教）牧首辖区 | A territory governed by an exarch, especially a territory within the Eastern Orthodox Church under the authority of a patriarch.  An exarchate is a specific administrative area within a larger religious jurisdiction, often characterized by a degree of autonomy. | The exarchate of Ravenna played a significant role in the early medieval period.  拉文纳的牧首辖区在早期中世纪时期发挥了重要作用。 The newly established exarchate covers several dioceses. 新成立的牧首辖区涵盖了几个教区。 | diocese, province |  (no direct antonym, but could be considered the opposite of a smaller, independent parish) | 名词
