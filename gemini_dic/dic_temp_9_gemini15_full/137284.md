nonsynchronousness:/ˌnɒnˈsɪŋkrənəsnəs/ | n. |不同步性，异步性 |The lack of synchronization or coordination; the state of not occurring at the same time or rate.  This often refers to events, processes, or systems that are not aligned in time. |例句：The nonsynchronousness of the two systems led to data loss.  两个系统的不同步导致了数据丢失。


The nonsynchronousness between the music and the dancers' movements was jarring. 音乐和舞者动作的不同步令人不适。|近义词：asynchrony, desynchronization, lack of coordination |反义词：synchronicity, synchronism, coordination
