postmaster:/ˈpoʊstˌmæstər/| n. |邮政局长，邮务员 |The postmaster is responsible for the efficient operation of the post office. 邮政局长负责邮局的有效运作。|近义词：post office manager, mail carrier (部分职责重叠)|反义词：无

词源:
The word "postmaster" is a compound word.  "Post" comes from the Latin word "positus," meaning "placed" or "set," referring to the placement of mail or messages.  "Master" refers to the person in charge or responsible for something. Therefore, a "postmaster" is the person in charge of a post office.
