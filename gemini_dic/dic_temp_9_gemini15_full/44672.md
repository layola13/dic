crucifier:/ˈkruːsɪfaɪər/| n. | 十字架佩戴者，（尤指）十字军战士 | A crucifier is someone who carries or wears a crucifix, especially a crusader.  The term often implies a strong religious devotion and association with the Crusades. | Example:  He wore the crucifix of a crucifier, a symbol of his faith and his participation in the holy wars. 他佩戴着十字军战士的十字架，象征着他的信仰和他参与圣战。|近义词:crusader, devotee, believer | 反义词:heretic, infidel
