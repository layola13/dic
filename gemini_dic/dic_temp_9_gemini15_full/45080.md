cubism:/ˈkjuːbɪzəm/ | n. |立体主义|A 20th-century style of painting and sculpture characterized by the use of geometric shapes and fragmented forms to represent objects.|The painting is a prime example of Cubism, with its fractured planes and overlapping shapes.|近义词：geometric abstraction|反义词：representational art
