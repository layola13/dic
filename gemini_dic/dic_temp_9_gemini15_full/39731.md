consistency:/kənˈsɪstənsi/ | n. |一致性，连贯性，浓度 |The state or quality of being consistent; agreement or harmony; steadfastness.  It refers to the state of being uniform, regular, or unchanging.  This can apply to various aspects, such as behavior, principles, quality, or texture. |例句：The consistency of his work is impressive.他的作品质量始终如一，令人印象深刻。
例句：There is a lack of consistency in the application of these rules.  这些规章的执行缺乏一致性。|近义词：uniformity, regularity, coherence, steadiness |反义词：inconsistency, variability, fluctuation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team maintains a high level of consistency in their performance. = 这个团队保持着高水平的一致性表现。
Her work shows great consistency in style and technique. = 她的作品在风格和技巧上展现出高度的一致性。


一般过去时:
The factory showed great consistency in its production last year. = 该工厂去年生产保持了高度的一致性。
His arguments lacked consistency throughout the debate. = 他在辩论中论点前后不一致。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
