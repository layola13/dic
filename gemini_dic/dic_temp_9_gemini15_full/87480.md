hetchel: /ˈhetʃəl/ | n. v. | 梳麻机；梳理（纤维）| A hetchel is a tool used to comb or straighten fibers, especially flax or hemp, to prepare them for spinning.  The verb form means to comb or straighten fibers using a hetchel. | The flax was carefully hetcheled before spinning.  = 亚麻在纺纱前被仔细地梳理了。  He used a hand hetchel to prepare the fibers. = 他用手工梳麻机来准备纤维。| comb, hackle | tangle, knot | n. v.
