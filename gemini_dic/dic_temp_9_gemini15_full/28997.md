carthamin:/ˈkɑːθəmin/| n. |胭脂红，红花素|A red crystalline pigment obtained from safflower (Carthamus tinctorius). It is used as a dye and food coloring.  |例句：Carthamin is a valuable dye extracted from the safflower plant. 胭脂红是从红花植物中提取的一种珍贵的染料。|例句：The vibrant color of the fabric is due to the carthamin dye.  织物的鲜艳颜色是由于胭脂红染料。|近义词：safflower dye, carthamic acid|反义词：无|名词
