discusses:/dɪˈskʌsɪz/| v.|讨论，议论|To talk about something with another person or group of people, often in order to reach a decision or to exchange ideas.  |例句：The committee discusses the budget every month.  委员会每月讨论预算。

例句：They discussed the problem for hours but couldn't find a solution. 他们讨论了这个问题几个小时，但找不到解决方案。|近义词：talks about, debates, considers, examines |反义词：ignores, neglects


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He discusses the issue with his colleagues regularly. 他定期与同事讨论这个问题。
They discuss current events during their lunch break.  他们在午休时间讨论时事。


一般过去时:
We discussed the project last night. 我们昨晚讨论了这个项目。
She discussed her concerns with her manager. 她与经理讨论了她的担忧。


现在完成时:
They have discussed the matter several times. 他们已经讨论过这个问题好几次了。
He has discussed his future plans with his family. 他已经和家人讨论了他的未来计划。


过去完成时:
They had discussed the proposal before the meeting. 会议之前他们已经讨论过这个提案了。
She had discussed the options with her advisor. 她已经和她的顾问讨论过各种选择。


将来时:
We will discuss the contract tomorrow. 我们明天将讨论合同。
They will discuss the strategy at the next meeting. 他们将在下次会议上讨论战略。


虚拟语气:
If we discussed it more openly, we might find a solution. 如果我们更公开地讨论它，我们可能会找到一个解决方案。
I wish we had discussed the issue earlier. 我希望我们早点讨论这个问题。


被动语态:
The issue was discussed at the meeting. 会议上讨论了这个问题。
The proposal will be discussed next week.  提案将在下周讨论。


疑问句:
Will they discuss the budget today? 他们今天会讨论预算吗？
Have they discussed the options yet? 他们讨论过这些选择了吗？


否定句:
They didn't discuss the problem at all. 他们根本没有讨论这个问题。
He doesn't want to discuss the matter further. 他不想进一步讨论这个问题。


PHRASE->
discuss at length: 详细讨论
discuss in detail: 详细讨论
discuss the matter: 讨论此事
discuss the issue: 讨论这个问题
open to discussion:  开放讨论
