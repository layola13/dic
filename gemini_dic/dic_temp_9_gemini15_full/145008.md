overadjustments:/ˌoʊvərəˈdʒʌstmənts/| n.|过度调整；矫枉过正|The act of adjusting something too much, often resulting in a worse outcome than the initial problem.  This term often implies an attempt to compensate for a perceived deficiency or error, but the correction itself becomes excessive or inappropriate.|例句：His overadjustments to the camera settings resulted in blurry photos. 他的相机设置过度调整导致照片模糊。
例句：The overadjustments made to the economy led to increased instability. 经济的过度调整导致了更大的不稳定。|近义词：overcompensation, overcorrection|反义词：underadjustment, fine-tuning
