lod:/lɒd/| /lɑːd/ | vt. |放置，安置，寄存；负担；(正式)使…承担(重担)|to place or put something somewhere; to burden someone with something; to impose (a burden) on someone|例句：He lodged a complaint with the police. 他向警方投诉。|例句：They lodged in a small hotel. 他们在一家小旅馆投宿。|近义词：place, deposit, put|反义词：remove, retrieve, lift


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lodges his complaints regularly. = 他定期提出投诉。
She lodges her luggage at the hotel. = 她把行李寄存在旅馆。


一般过去时:
They lodged a protest against the decision. = 他们对该决定提出抗议。
He lodged a formal complaint with the authorities. = 他向当局正式投诉。


现在完成时:
She has lodged a formal complaint with the authorities. = 她已经向当局正式投诉了。
They have lodged their protest with the relevant bodies.  = 他们已向相关机构提出了抗议。


过去完成时:
He had lodged a complaint before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他已经投诉了。
She had lodged her suitcase in the storage room. = 她已将她的手提箱放在储藏室里。


将来时:
He will lodge a formal complaint tomorrow. = 他明天将正式投诉。
She will lodge the documents with the court. = 她将把文件交给法庭。


虚拟语气:
If I lodged a complaint, it might help. = 如果我投诉，或许会有帮助。
I wish I had lodged a complaint sooner. = 我真希望我早点投诉。


被动语态:
The complaint was lodged with the manager. = 投诉已向经理提出。
The suitcases were lodged safely in the storeroom. = 行李箱安全地存放在储藏室里。


疑问句:
Where did he lodge his complaint? = 他在哪里投诉的？
Where did you lodge your luggage? = 你把行李寄存在哪里？


否定句:
He didn't lodge a complaint. = 他没有投诉。
She didn't lodge her passport with the hotel. = 她没有把护照寄存在旅馆。


PHRASE->
lodge a complaint = 提出投诉
lodge a protest = 提出抗议
lodge a claim = 提出索赔
lodge an appeal = 提出上诉
lodge a bid = 投标
