resprout: /ˌriːˈspraʊt/ | v. | 再发芽；重新生长 | To begin to grow again after being cut, damaged, or dormant.  | The plants resprouted after the fire. 植物在大火之后重新生长。 The cut grass quickly resprouted.  被割的草很快又长出来了。 |  regrow, regenerate | wither, die | 动词
