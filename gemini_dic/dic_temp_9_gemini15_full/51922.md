/diː/| det. pron.|那，那个；什么|The word "di" is a shortened form of "that" or "the" in some dialects or informal speech, primarily in African American Vernacular English (AAVE). It can also function as a question word similar to "what". Its usage depends heavily on context and dialect.  There is no standard dictionary definition for "di" as a standalone word in general English.|例句：He said di car was broken. = 他说那辆车坏了。 Di gonna happen? = 那会发生什么？|近义词:that, the, what|反义词:this, these


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Di car is red. = 那辆车是红色的。
Di house is big. = 那房子很大。

一般过去时:
Di dog barked. = 那条狗叫了。
Di rain stopped. = 雨停了。


现在完成时:
Di work is done. = 工作完成了。
Di movie has ended. = 电影结束了。

过去完成时:
Di game had started before I arrived. = 我到达之前比赛已经开始了。
Di food had been cooked. = 食物已经做好了。


将来时:
Di sun will rise tomorrow. = 太阳明天会升起。
Di meeting will be long. = 会议会很长。

虚拟语气:
If di weather was nice, we would go to the beach. = 如果天气好，我们就去海滩。
I wish di problem was easier. = 我希望这个问题更容易。

被动语态:
Di window was broken. = 窗户被打破了。
Di cake is being eaten. = 蛋糕正在被吃。

疑问句:
Di time is it? = 几点了？(AAVE usage)
Di you want? = 你想要什么？(AAVE usage)

否定句:
Di food wasn't good. = 食物不好吃。
Di car didn't start. = 车子没发动。


PHRASE->
Di way = 那样的方式 (AAVE usage)
Di more = 而且 (AAVE usage)
Di less = 更少 (AAVE usage)
Di same = 同样的 (AAVE usage)
Di best = 最好的 (AAVE usage)


需要注意的是，"di"的用法主要限于非正式场合和特定方言，在标准英语写作中不常见。  上述例句中的"di"部分，在标准英语中通常会用"that", "the", 或"what"替换。
