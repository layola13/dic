goodwife:/ˈɡʊdwaɪf/| n. |良家妇女，妻子|A goodwife is a woman who is a good wife, typically characterized by her domestic skills, piety, and obedience to her husband.  The term is now largely archaic and carries a slightly patronizing or outdated connotation.|Example: The goodwife baked bread and tended the garden while her husband worked in the fields.  良家妇女在丈夫下地干活的时候在家烤面包和侍弄花园。|近义词：wife, spouse, helpmeet|反义词：None specifically, but implies the opposite of a bad or unfaithful wife.
