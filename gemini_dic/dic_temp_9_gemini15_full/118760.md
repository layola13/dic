metallicities:/ˌmɛtəlˈɪsətiz/| n. |金属丰度；金属含量|The abundances of metals in stars or other celestial objects.  This term refers to the relative proportion of elements heavier than helium compared to hydrogen.  Astronomers use metallicity to understand the age and evolution of stars and galaxies.| 例句：The star's high metallicity suggests it formed in a relatively enriched environment. 这颗恒星的高金属丰度表明它是在一个相对富集的环境中形成的。

The study revealed a correlation between galaxy morphology and metallicity. 这项研究揭示了星系形态和金属丰度之间的相关性。|近义词：metal abundance, elemental abundance|反义词：metal deficiency, low abundance|名词
