mosasaurus:/ˌmoʊsəˈsɔːrəs/| n. |沧龙|A large extinct marine reptile of the Late Cretaceous period.  Mosasaurus was a powerful predator with a long, slender body, a large head, and strong jaws filled with sharp teeth.
