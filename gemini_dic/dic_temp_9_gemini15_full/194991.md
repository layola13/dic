semi-transparent: /ˌsɛmi trænzˈpærənt/ | adj. | 半透明的 |  Partially transparent; allowing some light to pass through but diffusing it so that objects behind cannot be clearly seen.  | The stained-glass window was semi-transparent, casting a soft, colored light into the room.  | translucent, diaphanous | opaque, solid | 形容词
