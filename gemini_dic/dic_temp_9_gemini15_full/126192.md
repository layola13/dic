muzzlers:/ˈmʌzələrz/| n.| 嘴套者；戴嘴套的动物；(尤指)限制言论者 |People or animals that wear muzzles; those who suppress free speech, especially.  |例句：The police were accused of being muzzlers of the press.  警察被指控压制新闻自由。|例句：The aggressive dog was fitted with a muzzle by its owner, making it a muzzler.  这条攻击性强的狗被主人戴上了嘴套，变成了一个“戴嘴套者”。|近义词：gaggers, suppressors, censors|反义词：free speakers, advocates of free speech
