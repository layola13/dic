postlaryngeal: /ˌpoʊstləˈrɪndʒəl/ | adj. | 声带后的，喉后的 | Relating to the area behind the larynx (voice box).  The term is primarily used in medical and anatomical contexts to describe structures, processes, or locations situated posterior to the larynx. |  The postlaryngeal space is important for airway management.  声门后空间对于气道管理很重要。|近义词: retropharyngeal,  pharyngeal (in some contexts)|反义词: prelaryngeal


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb or noun forms.  Therefore, tense conjugation is not applicable.  Its usage is limited to descriptive phrases within sentences.

例句1:  The tumor was located in the postlaryngeal region. 肿瘤位于喉后区域。
例句2: Postlaryngeal swelling can cause breathing difficulties. 喉后肿胀会导致呼吸困难。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "postlaryngeal".  Its use is strictly within medical terminology.
