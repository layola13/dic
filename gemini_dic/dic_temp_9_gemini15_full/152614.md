peacher:/ˈpiːtʃər/| n. |桃子种植者；桃子销售商；（尤指）贩卖非法物品的人|A person who grows or sells peaches;  often used informally to refer to someone who deals in illegal goods, especially drugs.|例句：He was known as a peacher in the neighborhood, always having the best peaches. 他在附近被称为桃子种植者，总是拥有最好的桃子。|例句：The police suspected him of being a peacher, dealing in illicit substances. 警方怀疑他是贩卖非法物质的人。|近义词：fruit grower, dealer, trafficker|反义词：consumer, buyer

She peaches fresh produce at the market. = 她在市场上销售新鲜农产品。
