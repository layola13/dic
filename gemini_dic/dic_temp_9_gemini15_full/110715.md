lineman:/ˈlaɪnmən/ | n. | 电力线工人，线路员 | A lineman is a skilled worker who installs, maintains, and repairs power lines and other electrical equipment.  They often work outdoors in all kinds of weather. | Example: The lineman climbed the pole to fix the damaged power line.  电力线工人爬上电线杆修理损坏的电线。 The lineman worked tirelessly to restore power after the storm.  暴风雨过后，电力线工人夜以继日地抢修电力。|近义词：power lineman, linesman, cable splicer | 反义词：office worker, desk job
