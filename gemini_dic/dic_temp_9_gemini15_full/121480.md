mislabelling:/ˌmɪsˈleɪbəlɪŋ/| n.|错误标签；误标；错贴标签|The act of incorrectly labeling something.  This can refer to products, information, or anything that needs a label.|例句：The mislabelling of the medicine led to several patients receiving the wrong dosage.  药物的错误标签导致几名病人服用了错误的剂量。

例句：The company was fined for mislabelling its organic products. 这家公司因其有机产品的错误标签而被罚款。


近义词：incorrect labeling, wrong labeling, false labeling|反义词：correct labeling, accurate labeling|名词
