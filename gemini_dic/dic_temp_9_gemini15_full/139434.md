obsidian:/ɒbˈsɪdiən/ | n. |黑曜岩，黑曜石 | A naturally occurring volcanic glass formed as an extrusive igneous rock.  It is usually black in color, but can also be found in other colors depending on the mineral inclusions.  Obsidian is hard, brittle, and has a conchoidal fracture, meaning it breaks along smooth, curved surfaces. It has been used by humans for thousands of years for tools and jewelry. |  The arrowhead was made of obsidian. 这支箭头是用黑曜岩制成的。 The necklace was crafted from polished obsidian. 这条项链是用抛光的黑曜石制成的。 |  volcanic glass, black glass |  (no direct antonym, but contrasting terms could be granite, sedimentary rock) | noun
