freezing:/ˈfriːzɪŋ/| adj.|冰冻的；极冷的；令人震惊的|extremely cold; causing something to freeze; extremely shocking or unpleasant.|例句：The lake was freezing over.  湖面结冰了。
The news was absolutely freezing. 这个消息令人震惊。|近义词：icy, frigid, chilling, shocking|反义词：boiling, scorching, balmy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water is freezing.  水正在结冰。
This weather is freezing cold.  这天气冷得要命。

一般过去时:
The pipes froze last night.  昨晚管道冻住了。
It froze solid overnight.  它一夜之间冻得结结实实。

现在完成时:
The pond has frozen over. 池塘已经结冰了。
It has frozen solid. 它已经冻实了。


过去完成时:
The river had frozen before the storm hit.  暴风雨来临之前，河水已经结冰了。
The food had frozen solid in the freezer. 食物在冰箱里已经冻得硬邦邦的。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If it froze tonight, the plants would die. 如果今晚结冰，植物就会死掉。
If the temperature froze, we'd have to stay inside. 如果温度降到冰点以下，我们就得待在室内。


被动语态:
The meat was frozen for preservation. 肉被冷冻起来保存。
The vegetables were frozen quickly to retain nutrients. 蔬菜被快速冷冻以保留营养。


疑问句:

否定句:

It isn't freezing cold today. 今天并不很冷。
