constructs:/ˈkɒnstrʌkts/ /ˈkɑːnstrʌkts/| n. v.|结构，建筑物；构建，建造|

英文解析:As a noun, "constructs" refers to things that are built or made, often complex structures or systems.  As a verb, it means to build or create something, often something complex or abstract.

例句 (Noun):
The ancient pyramids are remarkable constructs. = 古代金字塔是了不起的建筑物。

The theoretical constructs of the paper are difficult to grasp. = 论文中的理论结构难以理解。


例句 (Verb):
They are constructing a new bridge across the river. = 他们正在建造一座横跨河流的新桥。

She constructs her arguments with careful precision. = 她以细致的精确度构建她的论点。


近义词 (Noun): structures, buildings, edifices, frameworks, systems; (Verb): builds, creates, forms, establishes, develops

反义词 (Noun): destructs, ruins, demolition; (Verb): destroys, demolishes, dismantles


词性: Noun, Verb
