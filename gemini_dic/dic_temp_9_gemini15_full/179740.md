reerecting:/ˌriːɪˈrektɪŋ/| v.|重建，重新竖立|The act of erecting something again;  rebuilding or putting something upright again.  It implies a previous erection that was taken down or destroyed.|例句：They are reerecting the statue after it was damaged in the storm.  他们在暴风雨过后正在重建雕像。

例句：The construction crew is reerecting the fallen scaffolding. 建筑队正在重新搭建倒塌的脚手架。


近义词：rebuilding, reconstructing, restoring


反义词：demolishing, destroying, tearing down


词性：动词
