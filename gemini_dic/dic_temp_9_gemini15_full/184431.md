respray:/riːˈspreɪ/| vt.|重新喷涂；再喷漆|To spray again; to apply a new coat of paint or other substance by spraying.|例句：We need to respray the car after the accident.  我们需要在事故后重新喷漆汽车。
例句：The artist decided to respray the mural with brighter colours.  艺术家决定用更鲜艳的颜色重新喷涂壁画。|近义词：repaint, re-coat|反义词：strip (remove paint)|动词
