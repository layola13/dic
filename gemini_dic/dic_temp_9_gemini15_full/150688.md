para-church:/ˈpærəˌtʃɜːrtʃ/| n. | 副教会机构；非官方教会组织 | An organization that operates outside the formal structure of a church but shares similar goals and often works in cooperation with churches.  These organizations often focus on specific ministries or outreach programs. | Example:  Many para-church organizations provide vital support to missionaries around the world.  许多副教会机构为世界各地的传教士提供重要的支持。|近义词: independent ministry, affiliated ministry, faith-based organization |反义词:  official church, established church
