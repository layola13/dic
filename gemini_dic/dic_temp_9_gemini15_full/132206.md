nondebater:/ˌnɒnˈdeɪtər/| n.|非辩论者，不善辩论者|A person who does not engage in debates or discussions, or who is not skilled at debating.  Someone who avoids arguments or intellectual sparring.|He's a nondebater by nature, preferring quiet contemplation to heated discussions. 他天性就是一个不爱辩论的人，比起激烈的讨论，他更喜欢安静的沉思。|近义词：non-participant, arguer averse, disputer averse|反义词：debater, arguer
