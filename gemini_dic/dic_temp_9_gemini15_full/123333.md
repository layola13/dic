monogynian:/ˌmɒnəˈdʒaɪniən/| adj. |一妻制的；一雌蕊的| Having only one wife or one pistil.  The term refers to a system where a male has only one wife, or, in botany, a flower with only one pistil.|例句：Some cultures are traditionally monogynian, with men having only one wife.  一些文化传统上是一妻制的，男人只有一个妻子。|例句：The botanist identified the flower as monogynian due to its single pistil. 植物学家由于其单一的雌蕊而将这种花鉴定为一雌蕊的。|近义词： monogamous (in the context of marriage)|反义词：polygynous|词性:形容词
