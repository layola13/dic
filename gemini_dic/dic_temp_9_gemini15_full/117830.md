mellifluous:/ˈmɛlɪfluəs/| adj.|甜美流利的；悦耳的 |Sweet-sounding; flowing smoothly and pleasantly.|Her mellifluous voice captivated the audience.  她的甜美嗓音迷住了听众。|The singer's mellifluous tones filled the hall. 歌手的甜美歌声充满了大厅。|euphonious, dulcet, honeyed|harsh, grating, strident|形容词

词源:
From Middle English *meliflou, from Old French *meliflou, from Latin mel (honey) + fluō (I flow).  字面意思是“像蜂蜜一样流淌”。
