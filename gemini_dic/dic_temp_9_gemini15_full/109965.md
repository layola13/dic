liberator: /ˈlɪbəreɪtər/ | n. | 解放者，解放军 | A person who liberates someone or something.  Someone or something that sets people free from oppression, slavery, or confinement. | 例句：Nelson Mandela was a liberator who fought against apartheid.  纳尔逊·曼德拉是一位反对种族隔离的解放者。


The Allied forces were the liberators of many occupied countries during World War II.  在二战期间，盟军是许多被占领国家的解放者。| 近义词：redeemer, deliverer, rescuer | 反义词：oppressor, enslaver, captor | 名词
