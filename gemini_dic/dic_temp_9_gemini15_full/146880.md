overinforms:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfɔːrmz/| v.|信息过载；提供过多信息| to give someone more information than they need or want, often resulting in confusion or overwhelm.  |例句：The marketing materials overinformed the customer, leading to indecision.  市场营销材料信息过多，导致客户犹豫不决。|例句：He overinformed his presentation, losing the audience's attention.  他的演示文稿信息过多，失去了听众的注意力。|近义词：overload, inundate, overwhelm|反义词：underinform, simplify
