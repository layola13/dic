hamper:/ˈhæmpər/| n. v.| 妨碍；篮子；| A hamper is a large basket, typically used for carrying food or clothes.  As a verb, it means to hinder or impede. |例句：The heavy rain hampered our progress. 大雨阻碍了我们的进展。The picnic hamper was overflowing with goodies. 野餐篮里装满了好吃的东西。|近义词：hinder, obstruct, impede, restrain|反义词：aid, assist, facilitate

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bad weather hampers our outdoor activities. = 恶劣的天气妨碍了我们的户外活动。
This policy hampers economic growth. = 这一政策阻碍了经济增长。


一般过去时:
The snowstorm hampered travel. = 暴风雪阻碍了出行。
The lack of funding hampered the project. = 资金不足阻碍了项目进展。


现在完成时:
The delays have hampered the project's completion. = 延误已经阻碍了项目的完成。
Technical difficulties have hampered our efforts. = 技术难题阻碍了我们的努力。


过去完成时:
The previous rain had hampered the team's ability to play. = 先前的雨水已经影响了球队的比赛能力。
A broken wheel had hampered their journey. = 轮胎坏了阻碍了他们的旅程。


将来时:
The strike will hamper production. = 罢工将阻碍生产。
Further delays will hamper the schedule. = 进一步的延误将影响进度。


虚拟语气:
If the traffic hadn't hampered us, we would have arrived on time. = 如果交通没有阻碍我们，我们就会准时到达。
If the regulations weren't so strict, it wouldn't hamper the business. = 如果规定没那么严格，就不会阻碍业务了。


被动语态:
Our progress was hampered by the unexpected storm. = 我们的进展被突如其来的暴风雨阻碍了。
The project is being hampered by a lack of resources. = 项目由于缺乏资源而受到阻碍。


疑问句:
Will the bad weather hamper our plans? = 恶劣的天气会影响我们的计划吗？

否定句:
