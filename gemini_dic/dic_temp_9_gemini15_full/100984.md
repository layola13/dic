invalidated:/ɪnˈvælɪdeɪtɪd/ | v. | 使无效；使作废；推翻 | To make something invalid or no longer effective.  | The new evidence invalidated the original theory. 新证据使原来的理论无效。  The court invalidated the contract. 法院宣布合同无效。 | nullified, voided, annulled, quashed | validated, confirmed, substantiated | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new evidence invalidates the original theory. = 新证据使原来的理论无效。
This policy invalidates their previous agreement. =这项政策使他们之前的协议无效。

一般过去时:
The court invalidated the contract. = 法院宣布合同无效。
The discovery invalidated his previous research. = 这一发现使他之前的研究成果无效。

现在完成时:
The recent findings have invalidated many earlier assumptions. = 最近的发现使许多早先的假设无效。
Several studies have invalidated the effectiveness of that treatment. = 几项研究已经证明该疗法的无效性。


过去完成时:
They realized that their calculations had invalidated the whole experiment. = 他们意识到他们的计算使整个实验无效。
The judge had invalidated the will before the inheritance could be settled. = 法官在遗产能够解决之前已经宣布遗嘱无效。

将来时:
The upcoming legislation will invalidate many existing regulations. =即将颁布的立法将使许多现行法规无效。
New scientific breakthroughs will likely invalidate current theories. = 新的科学突破可能会使目前的理论失效。

虚拟语气:
If the judge had invalidated the contract, we would have been in trouble. =如果法官宣布合同无效，我们就会陷入困境。
If the test results invalidated the study, we would have to start over. = 如果测试结果使研究无效，我们就必须重新开始。

被动语态:
The contract was invalidated by the court. = 合同被法院宣布无效。
The experiment was invalidated by the flawed data. = 实验因数据有误而失效。

疑问句:
Will the new law invalidate our current licenses? = 新法律会使我们目前的许可证无效吗？
Does this evidence invalidate the defendant's alibi? = 这证据是否推翻了被告的不在场证明？

否定句:
The evidence did not invalidate his claim. = 证据并没有使他的说法无效。
The new regulations did not invalidate the previous agreement. = 新规章并没有使之前的协议无效。
