lakh:/læk/| n. |十万；百万|A lakh is a unit of 100,000 in the Indian numbering system. It's commonly used in South Asia.  |例句：He earned a lakh of rupees last year. 他去年赚了十万卢比。|近义词：hundred thousand, one hundred thousand|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The house costs one lakh rupees. = 这房子值十万卢比。
He earns two lakhs annually. = 他每年赚二十万。


一般过去时:
They received one lakh rupees in compensation. = 他们收到了十万卢比的赔偿。
She spent five lakhs on her wedding. = 她花在婚礼上的钱是五十万。


现在完成时:
We have saved over two lakhs this year. = 我们今年已经攒了二十多万。
He has invested lakhs in the stock market. = 他已经投资了几十万在股市。


过去完成时:
They had already spent a lakh before the project even started. = 在项目开始之前他们就已经花掉了十万。
She had saved several lakhs before buying the car. = 在买车之前她已经存了好几万。


将来时:
I will earn a lakh next month. = 我下个月将会赚十万。
We will invest lakhs in our business. = 我们将在我们的生意中投资几十万。


虚拟语气:
If I had a lakh of rupees, I would buy a new car. = 如果我有十万卢比，我会买一辆新车。
I wish I had a lakh to invest. = 我希望我有一万可以投资。


被动语态:
A lakh of rupees was donated to the charity. = 十万卢比捐给了慈善机构。
Lakhs were spent on the infrastructure project. = 几十万花在了基础设施项目上。


疑问句:
How many lakhs did you earn this year? = 今年你赚了多少钱（十万为单位）？
Did he spend a lakh on that painting? = 他在那幅画上花了十万吗？


否定句:
I don't have a lakh of rupees. = 我没有十万卢比。
They didn't spend a lakh on the renovation. = 他们没有花十万在装修上。
