hadrosaurus:/ˌhædrəˈsɔːrəs/| n. |鸭嘴龙|Hadrosaurus is a genus of hadrosaurid ornithopod dinosaur that lived in North America during the Late Cretaceous period.  They were large, herbivorous dinosaurs known for their flat, duck-like bills. |The discovery of Hadrosaurus foulkii was a landmark event in paleontology.|近义词：duck-billed dinosaur|反义词：无
