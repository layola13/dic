off-whites:/ˌɒfˈwaɪts/| adj. |米白色，灰白色|Slightly off-white, suggesting a color that is not pure white but has a slight tint of another color, often beige, cream, or gray.  It often refers to a shade of white that is slightly tinged with another color, creating a softer, less stark appearance than pure white. |The walls were painted a subtle off-white.|近义词：cream, beige, ivory, eggshell|反义词：black, dark
