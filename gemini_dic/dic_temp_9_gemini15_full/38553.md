USAGE->
一般现在时:
The study draws several important conclusions. = 这项研究得出了一些重要的结论。
His conclusions are based on solid evidence. = 他的结论是基于确凿证据的。


一般过去时:
The scientist presented his conclusions at the conference. = 科学家在会议上陈述了他的结论。
The committee reached its conclusions after a long discussion. = 委员会经过长时间的讨论后得出了结论。


现在完成时:
Researchers have reached some surprising conclusions. = 研究人员得出了一些令人惊讶的结论。
Scientists have drawn several conclusions from the data. = 科学家们从数据中得出了一些结论。


过去完成时:
They had already drawn their conclusions before the new evidence emerged. = 在新的证据出现之前，他们就已经得出了结论。
After reviewing the evidence, the judge had reached his conclusions. = 在审查证据后，法官得出了他的结论。


将来时:
We will reach our conclusions after further investigation. = 我们将在进一步调查后得出结论。
The report will present its conclusions next week. = 该报告将于下周公布其结论。


虚拟语气:
If we had more data, we might reach different conclusions. = 如果我们有更多数据，我们可能会得出不同的结论。
I wish I had come to more definitive conclusions. = 我希望我能得出更明确的结论。


被动语态:
The conclusions were presented in the final report. = 结论在最终报告中提出。
Different conclusions were drawn by different researchers. = 不同的研究人员得出了不同的结论。


疑问句:
What conclusions did the study reach? = 这项研究得出了什么结论？
What are your conclusions about this matter? = 你对这件事的结论是什么？


否定句:
The study did not reach any definitive conclusions. = 这项研究没有得出任何明确的结论。
I do not agree with the conclusions presented in the article. = 我不同意文章中提出的结论。
