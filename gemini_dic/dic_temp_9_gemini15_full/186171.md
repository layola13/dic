rheophore:/ˈriːəfɔːr/| n.|载流子|A rheophore is a material or structure that conducts or transports charge carriers (such as electrons or ions).  It is often used in the context of electrochemical devices or systems where efficient charge transport is crucial.  The term emphasizes the carrying or bearing of the charge.  |例句：The electrolyte acts as a rheophore, facilitating ion transport between the electrodes. 电解质充当载流子，促进电极之间的离子传输。


The design of the rheophore is critical to the performance of the battery.  载流子的设计对电池的性能至关重要。|近义词：conductor, carrier, transmitter|反义词：insulator
