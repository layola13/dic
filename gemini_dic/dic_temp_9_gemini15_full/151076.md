parapsychological:/ˌpærəsaɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 超心理学的；与超心理学相关的 | Relating to or denoting parapsychology.  Parapsychology is the study of purported psychic phenomena, such as extrasensory perception (ESP), psychokinesis (PK), and other related abilities.  | Example:  The parapsychological experiments yielded inconclusive results.  超心理学实验的结果尚无定论。 |近义词：psychic, paranormal | 反义词：normal, scientific (in a conventional sense)
