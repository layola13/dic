plaint:/pleɪnt/| n.|抱怨；诉讼；控诉|A complaint or expression of grievance.  It can also refer to a legal action.|例句：He lodged a plaint against his neighbor for noise pollution. 他就噪音污染向他的邻居提出了控诉。

例句：The farmer's plaint was about the lack of rain.  农民的抱怨是关于缺雨的。


近义词：complaint, grievance, protest, accusation

反义词：praise, commendation, approval

词性：名词
