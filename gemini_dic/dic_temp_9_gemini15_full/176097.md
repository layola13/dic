/rɔːz/ | n. | 原料，生料；未加工的材料 | Raw materials are the basic materials from which a product is made.  原材料是制造产品的基本材料。|近义词：materials, components, ingredients | 反义词：finished, processed, refined


/rɔːz/ | adj. | 生的；未加工的；未煮过的；天然的；粗糙的；不熟练的；赤裸的；严酷的；残酷的 |  The chef used raw fish in the dish.  厨师在菜里用了生鱼。 |近义词：uncooked, unprocessed, crude, rough | 反义词：cooked, processed, refined, smooth, skilled
