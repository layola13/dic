on-boards:/ˌɒnˈbɔːdz/ /ˌɑːnˈbɔːrdz/ | vt. |让…上船；雇用；使…适应|To bring (someone) onto a boat, plane, or other vehicle; to hire or employ (someone); to integrate (someone or something) into a system or group.|例句：The company on-boarded ten new employees last month.  公司上个月聘用了十名新员工。  |例句：The new software was on-boarded successfully. 新软件成功上线了。|近义词：employ, hire, integrate, recruit |反义词：fire, dismiss, discharge |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company on-boards new employees every month. = 公司每月都会雇佣新员工。
He on-boards new clients with a welcome package. = 他会用迎新礼包来帮助新客户适应。


一般过去时:
They on-boarded the new software last week. = 他们上周上线了新软件。
She on-boarded the passengers quickly and efficiently. = 她快速有效地让乘客登机了。


现在完成时:
We have on-boarded over fifty new employees this year. = 今年我们已经雇佣了五十多名新员工。
The project team has successfully on-boarded the new system. = 项目团队已经成功上线了新系统。


过去完成时:
They had on-boarded all the necessary personnel before the project started. = 项目开始前，他们已经雇佣了所有必要的人员。
The company had already on-boarded the new software before the launch date. = 在发布日期之前，公司已经上线了新软件。


将来时:
We will on-board the new recruits next week. = 我们下周将接收新员工。
They will on-board the passengers as soon as the plane is ready. = 飞机准备好后，他们就会让乘客登机。


虚拟语气:
If we on-boarded more experienced staff, the project would run more smoothly. = 如果我们雇佣更有经验的员工，项目就会进行得更顺利。
I wish we had on-boarded the new system sooner. = 我希望我们能早点上线新系统。


被动语态:
The new employees were on-boarded efficiently. = 新员工得到了有效的入职培训。
The software was on-boarded without any problems. = 软件上线没有任何问题。


疑问句:
How many employees did they on-board last quarter? = 上个季度他们雇佣了多少员工？

否定句:
The company did not on-board any new employees last month. = 上个月公司没有雇佣任何新员工。
They haven't on-boarded the new system yet. = 他们还没有上线新系统。
