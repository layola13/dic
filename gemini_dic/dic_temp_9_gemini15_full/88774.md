hodge:/hɒdʒ/| n.|杂烩；混合物；大杂烩|A hodgepodge is a mixture of different things, often in a disorganized or haphazard way.  It can refer to a collection of items, ideas, or people that are not well-organized or related.|例句：The garden was a hodgepodge of different flowers and plants. 花园里各种花草杂乱地混在一起。|例句：His speech was a hodgepodge of unrelated ideas. 他的演讲是一堆毫不相关的想法的杂烩。|近义词：mixture, medley, mishmash, jumble|反义词：order, uniformity, organization|名词
