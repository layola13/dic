cerebrology:/ˌsɛrəˈbrɒlədʒi/| n. |脑科学，脑学|The scientific study of the brain and its functions.  It encompasses various aspects, including brain structure, function, development, and disorders.|Example:  Advances in cerebrology have led to better treatments for neurological diseases.  脑科学的进步导致了对神经疾病的更好治疗。|近义词：neurology, neuroscience|反义词：无
