martinmas:/ˈmɑːrtɪnmæs/ | n. |圣马丁节|The feast day of Saint Martin of Tours, celebrated on November 11th.  It marks the traditional beginning of winter and was historically a time for celebrations and feasting.  The name derives from the saint's name and "mass," referring to the religious service held on that day.|The village held a large bonfire celebration on Martinmas.|近义词：Saint Martin's Day|反义词：无
