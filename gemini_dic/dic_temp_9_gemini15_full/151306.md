parietary:/pəˈraɪɪtəri/ | adj. |壁的；壁细胞的；与壁相关的| Relating to a wall or the walls of a cavity or organ.  The term often refers to anatomical structures associated with walls, such as the parietal bones of the skull or parietal cells in the stomach. |The parietal bones form the sides and roof of the skull.  壁骨构成颅骨的侧面和顶部。

The parietal cells in the stomach secrete hydrochloric acid. 胃壁细胞分泌盐酸。


近义词：mural (referring to walls), wall-like

反义词：visceral (referring to internal organs)


词源:  From Latin *paries, meaning "wall."  The term's usage in anatomy and biology reflects its connection to the walls of body cavities and organs.
