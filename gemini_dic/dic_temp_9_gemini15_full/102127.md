isotropism:/aɪˈsɒtrəpɪzəm/| n. |各向同性；等向性 |The property of being isotropic;  having the same properties in all directions.  Isotropism is often observed in materials at the macroscopic level, though it's rarely perfectly true at the microscopic level.|例句：The isotropism of the material ensured uniform expansion under heat.  这种材料的各向同性确保了受热时均匀膨胀。


The isotropism of the crystalline structure contributes to its unique optical properties. 晶体结构的各向同性使其具有独特的的光学特性。|近义词：homogeneity, uniformity|反义词：anisotropy
