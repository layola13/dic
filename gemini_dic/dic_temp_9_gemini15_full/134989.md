nonnavigable: /ˌnɑːnˈnævɪɡeɪbəl/ | adj. | 不可航行的 | Not navigable; incapable of being navigated.  This adjective describes waterways or routes that are too shallow, obstructed, or otherwise unsuitable for ships or boats to travel through.  | 例句：The river became nonnavigable after the heavy rains.  暴雨过后，这条河变得无法航行了。  The narrow, shallow creek was nonnavigable even for small boats.  即使是小船，那条狭窄而浅的溪流也无法航行。 | 近义词：impassable, unnavigable | 反义词：navigable
