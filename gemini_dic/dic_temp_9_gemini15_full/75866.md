functionalize: /ˈfʌŋkʃənəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使具有功能；使发挥作用 | To give a (usually chemical) substance or material a specific function or purpose, often by modifying its chemical structure.  | Example:  They functionalized the polymer surface to improve its biocompatibility.  他们使聚合物表面具有功能以提高其生物相容性。 |近义词：modify, adapt, activate | 反义词：deactivate, inactivate

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They functionalize the nanoparticles for drug delivery. = 他们使纳米粒子具有药物递送功能。
The company functionalizes its products to meet customer needs. = 公司使产品具有功能以满足客户需求。

一般过去时:
Scientists functionalized the carbon nanotubes to enhance conductivity. = 科学家使碳纳米管具有功能以增强导电性。
The researchers functionalized the surface to improve adhesion. = 研究人员使表面具有功能以提高粘合性。

现在完成时:
We have functionalized the biomaterial to promote cell growth. = 我们已经使生物材料具有功能以促进细胞生长。
They have functionalized the electrodes for improved sensitivity. = 他们已经使电极具有功能以提高灵敏度。

过去完成时:
The engineers had functionalized the components before assembly. = 工程师在组装前已经使组件具有功能。
They had functionalized the sensor to detect specific molecules. = 他们已经使传感器具有功能以检测特定分子。

将来时:
We will functionalize the polymer with specific groups for targeted delivery. = 我们将使聚合物具有特定基团的功能，以实现靶向递送。
They will functionalize the surface to repel water. = 他们将使表面具有功能以防水。


虚拟语气:
If we functionalized the material, it would improve its performance. = 如果我们使材料具有功能，它将提高其性能。
It would be beneficial if they functionalized the catalyst. = 如果他们使催化剂具有功能将是有益的。


被动语态:
The surface was functionalized with a silane coupling agent. = 表面用硅烷偶联剂进行了功能化处理。
The polymers are functionalized to enhance their properties. = 聚合物被功能化以增强其性能。


疑问句:

否定句:
