crutch:/krʌtʃ/ | n. |拐杖；支撑；依靠 | A crutch is a support used by someone who has difficulty walking, typically because of a leg injury or disability.  It can also refer to something or someone that provides support or reliance, often in a figurative sense. | Example: He used a crutch to walk after his accident. 他在事故后用拐杖走路。  She leaned heavily on her family as a crutch during difficult times.  她在困难时期非常依赖她的家人作为支撑。 | 近义词：support, prop, cane | 反义词：strength, independence | 名词
