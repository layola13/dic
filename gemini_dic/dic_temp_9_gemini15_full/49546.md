dematerialization: /ˌdiːməˌtɪəriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |非物质化；去物质化；脱实；虚拟化 | The process of converting something from a physical form to a digital or virtual form, or the reduction of the physical presence of something.  It often involves reducing the reliance on physical materials or processes. | Example: The dematerialization of the music industry through digital downloads has significantly impacted record sales.  音乐产业通过数字下载的非物质化极大地影响了唱片销量。  The dematerialization of libraries through digital archives allows for greater accessibility. 图书馆通过数字档案馆的非物质化实现了更大的可访问性。 | 近义词：virtualization, digitization, immaterialization | 反义词：materialization, physicalization | 名词
