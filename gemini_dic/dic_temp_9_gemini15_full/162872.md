precedents:/ˈprɛsɪdənts/| n.|先例；前例；判例|Something that has happened before that can be used as a reason for or example of what may happen again.  A precedent is a previous case or decision that is used as a guide for similar cases in the future.  It can also refer to a custom or practice established by previous examples.|

The judge's decision set a precedent for future cases involving similar circumstances.  法官的判决为将来涉及类似情况的案件树立了先例。

The company followed the precedent set by its competitors.这家公司效仿了其竞争对手的先例。


近义词：example, model, instance, case, paradigm

反义词：innovation, novelty, first
