haley:/ˈheɪli/| n. |海莉（女子名）|Haley is a female given name of Irish origin.  It is a variant of the name Hailey, which is derived from the Old English word "haeg," meaning "hay," and "lēah," meaning "meadow" or "clearing." The name thus evokes images of a sunny meadow filled with hay.  It has become increasingly popular as a given name in recent decades.|My daughter's name is Haley. = 我女儿的名字是海莉。|近义词: Hailey, Hayley |反义词: (无)| 名词
