regrowth:/ˈriːɡroʊθ/| n. |再生；重新生长；复苏|The new growth of plants, hair, or other organic matter after a period of dormancy or damage.  It can also refer to economic or social recovery. |例句：The regrowth of the forest after the fire was remarkable.  森林大火后的再生长令人瞩目。

The economic regrowth following the recession was slow but steady.  经济衰退后的复苏缓慢而稳定。|近义词：renewal, regeneration, recovery |反义词：decay, decline, depletion
