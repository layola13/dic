persuaders:/pərˈsweɪdərz/ | n. |说服者；劝说者 |Those who attempt to influence the opinions or actions of others.  They use various techniques to convince their audience. | Example: The marketing team hired skilled persuaders to promote the new product.  市场营销团队聘请了熟练的说服者来推广新产品。 |近义词：influencers, advocates, promoters |反义词：obstructors, opponents, dissuaders
