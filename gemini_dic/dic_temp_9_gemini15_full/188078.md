round-the-clock:/ˌraʊnd ðə ˈklɒk/| adj.|昼夜不停的，全天候的|  Around the clock means happening or operating continuously for 24 hours a day.  It emphasizes the continuous nature of something, without any interruption. |例句：The hospital runs a round-the-clock emergency service. 医院提供全天候的急诊服务。|例句：They worked round-the-clock to meet the deadline. 他们夜以继日地工作以赶上最后期限。|近义词：continuous, uninterrupted, 24/7, all day and night|反义词：intermittent, sporadic, occasional|词性:形容词
