chamberer:/ˈtʃeɪmbərər/| n.|密室居住者；（尤指）隐居者；|A chamberer is someone who lives in a chamber, often implying seclusion or secrecy.  The term often carries a connotation of someone who is reclusive or mysterious.  It can also refer to someone who works in or is associated with a chamber, such as a legislative chamber.|例句：He lived the life of a chamberer, rarely seen by the outside world. 他过着隐居的生活，很少被外界看到。|近义词：recluse, hermit, recluse, loner|反义词：socialite, extrovert
