hearkens:/ˈhɑːrkənz/ | v. |倾听，留心 | To listen attentively; to give heed.  |例句：He hearkened to the old woman's tale. 他仔细倾听了老妇人的故事。 |例句：The crowd hearkened to the speaker's words.  人群仔细聆听着演讲者的发言。|近义词：listen, attend, heed |反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hearkens to the advice of his elders. = 他倾听长者的建议。
She hearkens to the whispers of the wind. = 她倾听着风的低语。


一般过去时:
They hearkened to the siren's call. = 他们倾听了塞壬的呼唤。
He hearkened to the pleas of his people. = 他倾听了人民的恳求。


现在完成时:
I have hearkened to many stories in my life. = 我一生中倾听了许多故事。
She has hearkened to the warnings of the prophets. = 她倾听了先知的警告。


过去完成时:
He had hearkened to the rumors before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前已经听说了这些谣言。
They had hearkened to the advice of their lawyer. = 他们在听取了律师的建议之后。


将来时:
We will hearken to the wisdom of the ages. = 我们将聆听时代的智慧。
She will hearken to the voice of her conscience. = 她将倾听自己良心的声音。


虚拟语气:
If I hearkened to your advice, I wouldn't be in this mess. = 如果我听了你的建议，我就不会陷入这种困境。
I wish I had hearkened to my father's warnings. = 我真希望我听了父亲的警告。


被动语态:
His words were hearkened to by the entire assembly. = 他的话语受到了全体与会者的倾听。
The advice was hearkened to by the young man. = 年轻人听了这个建议。


疑问句:
Will you hearken to my explanation? = 你会听我的解释吗？
Did they hearken to the evidence presented? = 他们听了提出的证据吗？


否定句:
He did not hearken to the warnings of the storm. = 他没有倾听暴风雨的警告。
She did not hearken to the pleas for mercy. = 她没有倾听求饶的声音。


PHRASE->
hearken unto = 倾听…的话
hearken to reason = 听从理智
hearken back to = 回溯到
hearken to the call of = 响应…的号召
hearken to one's conscience = 倾听自己的良心
