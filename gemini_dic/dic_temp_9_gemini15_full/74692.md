freeganism:/ˈfriːɡənɪzəm/| n. |自由觅食主义；反消费主义|A lifestyle of dumpster diving and foraging for discarded food and other goods as a form of protest against consumerism and waste.|Example:  Many freegans believe that discarding edible food is morally wrong and actively seek out discarded goods.  许多自由觅食主义者认为丢弃可食用的食物在道德上是错误的，并积极寻找被丢弃的物品。|近义词：anti-consumerism,  dumpster diving|反义词：consumerism, materialism
