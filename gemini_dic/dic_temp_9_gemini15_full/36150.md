coexists:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪsts/| v.|共存，同时存在|To exist at the same time or in the same place.|例句：Many different cultures coexist peacefully in this city.  这个城市许多不同的文化和平共处。|例句：The two species can coexist in the same habitat. 这两种物种可以在相同的栖息地共存。|近义词：cohabit, coexist, live together|反义词：conflict, clash


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Different cultures coexist in many parts of the world. = 世界许多地方的不同文化共存。
These two ideas coexist peacefully in his philosophy. = 这两种思想在他的哲学体系中和平共存。


一般过去时:
The two nations coexisted peacefully for many years. = 这两个国家和平共处多年。
Dinosaurs and early mammals coexisted for millions of years. = 恐龙和早期哺乳动物共存了数百万年。


现在完成时:
Many different belief systems have coexisted throughout history. = 许多不同的信仰体系贯穿整个历史一直共存。
These two political parties have coexisted for decades. = 这两个政党共存了几十年。


过去完成时:
Before the war, these two tribes had coexisted without major conflict. = 战争爆发前，这两个部落一直和平共处，没有大的冲突。
It was discovered that these species had coexisted millions of years ago. = 人们发现这些物种在数百万年前就已经共存。


将来时:

Experts predict that these technologies will coexist for some time. = 专家预测这些技术将共存一段时间。


虚拟语气:
If these two factions coexisted, there would be less violence. = 如果这两个派系能和平共处，暴力事件就会减少。
It would be ideal if humans and nature could coexist harmoniously. = 如果人类和自然能够和谐共存，那将是理想的。


被动语态:

疑问句:
Can different cultures coexist successfully? = 不同的文化能够成功地共存吗？

否定句:
