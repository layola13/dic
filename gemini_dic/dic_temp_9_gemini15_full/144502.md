outtongued: /ˌaʊtˈtʌŋd/ | adj. |  被说服的；被驳倒的；言语上被击败的 |  Outtongued describes someone who has been defeated in an argument or debate because their opponent was more eloquent or persuasive. It implies a verbal defeat, not necessarily a physical one. | 例句：He was completely outtongued by her eloquent and persuasive arguments. 他被她雄辩而有说服力的论点完全驳倒了。


He felt outtongued and frustrated after the debate.  辩论后，他感到言语受挫且沮丧。| 近义词：defeated, silenced, overwhelmed (verbally), bested | 反义词：victorious, triumphant, eloquent (in a winning way) |  形容词
