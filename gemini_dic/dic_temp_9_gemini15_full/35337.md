co-author:/koʊˈɔːθər/|/ˈkoʊɔːθər/| n. |合著者，共同作者 |A co-author is a person who writes a book, article, or other piece of writing with another person or people.  |例句：She is the co-author of a bestselling novel. 她是一本畅销小说的合著者。

例句：He and his colleague are co-authors on a new research paper.他和他的同事是这篇新研究论文的合著者。


近义词：collaborator, joint author
反义词：sole author, single author
词性：名词
