enriching:/ɪnˈrɪtʃɪŋ/ | adj. |使富裕的；使充实的；增进的 | Making something better or more interesting.  |例句：Traveling is an enriching experience. 旅行是一次充实的经历。

The enriching soil helped the plants grow. 肥沃的土壤帮助植物生长。|近义词：improving, enhancing, fulfilling |反义词：depleting, impoverishing, stultifying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new program is enriching the lives of many students. = 这项新计划丰富了许多学生的生活。
This experience is enriching my understanding of the world. = 这段经历丰富了我对世界的理解。


一般过去时:
The workshop enriched my knowledge of art history. = 那次工作坊丰富了我对艺术史的知识。
The trip enriched our cultural understanding. = 这趟旅行丰富了我们的文化理解。


现在完成时:
Volunteering has enriched my life in countless ways. = 志愿工作以无数的方式丰富了我的生活。
This book has enriched my understanding of the subject. = 这本书加深了我对这个主题的理解。


过去完成时:
Having lived abroad had enriched her perspective. = 在国外生活使她开阔了视野。
They had enriched the soil with compost before planting. = 他们在种植之前用堆肥改良了土壤。


将来时:
This course will enrich your understanding of literature. = 这门课程将丰富你对文学的理解。
The new technology will enrich our lives in many ways. = 新技术将以多种方式丰富我们的生活。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pursue more enriching activities. = 如果我有更多时间，我会追求更多充实的活动。
I wish I had participated in more enriching experiences during my youth. = 我希望在我年轻的时候参与更多充实的经历。


被动语态:
Our lives are enriched by the beauty of nature. = 我们的生活因大自然的美丽而丰富。
His knowledge was enriched by years of study. = 他多年的学习丰富了他的知识。


疑问句:
What activities are most enriching for you? = 什么活动对你来说最充实？
What experiences have you found most enriching? = 你发现哪些经历最充实？


否定句:
The monotonous work did not enrich his life. = 单调的工作并没有丰富他的生活。
The lack of cultural events didn't enrich the community. = 缺乏文化活动并没有丰富这个社区。
