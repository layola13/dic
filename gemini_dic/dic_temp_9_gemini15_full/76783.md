gamification:/ˌɡeɪmɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |游戏化；游戏机制化 |The application of game-design elements and game principles in non-game contexts.  Gamification aims to improve user engagement, motivation, and productivity by making tasks more fun and rewarding.|例句：The company uses gamification to encourage employee participation in training programs. 这家公司使用游戏化来鼓励员工参与培训项目。|近义词：game mechanics, ludification|反义词：de-gamification
