jacketless:/ˈdʒækɪtləs/| adj.|  无夹克的，没穿夹克的 |Without a jacket.  The adjective "jacketless" describes a person or thing that is not wearing or having a jacket. |例句：He felt cold without his jacket; he was jacketless. 他没穿夹克感觉很冷；他没穿夹克。

例句：The jacketless man shivered in the chilly wind.  没穿夹克的男人在寒冷的风中瑟瑟发抖。


近义词：unjacketed, coatless


反义词：jacketed
