rivalling: /ˈraɪvəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 竞争的；对抗的；可与…媲美的 |  Equalling or surpassing another in quality, ability, or achievement.  |例句：His new novel is rivalling his earlier successes. 他的新小说与他之前的成功作品不相上下。

例句：Her performance was rivalling that of the established stars. 她的表演可以与那些著名的明星相媲美。


近义词：competing, matching, equalling, surpassing, rivaling


反义词：inferior, second-rate, subpar


词源:  Derived from the word "rival," ultimately from the Old French "rival" meaning "one who lives near another," possibly implying competition for resources or attention.  The "-ing" suffix transforms the noun "rival" into a present participle, functioning as an adjective.
