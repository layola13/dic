/luː/ | n. | 白细胞, 白血球 | A type of white blood cell.  Leucocytes are part of the body's immune system and help fight infection. |Example: The doctor ordered a blood test to count the number of leukocytes. 医生开了个验血，来计算白细胞的数量。|近义词：leukocyte, white blood cell |反义词：  N/A
