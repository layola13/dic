mythologized:/mɪˈθɑːlədʒaɪzd/| v. |神话化；赋予神话色彩|To treat or represent (someone or something) as if they were legendary or mythical.  To create myths or legends about.|例句：The founding of Rome has been heavily mythologized over the centuries. 罗马的建立几个世纪以来被严重神话化了。The story of King Arthur has been mythologized over time.亚瑟王的故事随着时间的推移被神话化了。|近义词：romanticize, fictionalize, legendary, legendary.|反义词：demythologize,  debunking, factualize.
