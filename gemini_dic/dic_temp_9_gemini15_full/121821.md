misregulating: /ˌmɪsˈreɡjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 调节不当；错误调节 |  The act of improperly or incorrectly regulating something. It implies a failure to control or adjust something to the proper level or function.  | 例句：Misregulating the body's immune system can lead to autoimmune diseases.  错误调节身体的免疫系统会导致自身免疫性疾病。

The constant misregulating of the hormone levels caused significant health problems. 激素水平的持续错误调节导致了严重的健康问题。


近义词：maladjusting, malfunctioning, mismanaging, deranging


反义词：regulating, adjusting, controlling, optimizing


词性：动词(现在分词)
