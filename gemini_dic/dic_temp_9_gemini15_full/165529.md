premanifested:/ˌpriːˈmænɪfɛstɪd/| adj.| 预先显示的；预先声明的 |  Describes something that has been shown or declared beforehand, typically in the context of cargo manifests or similar documentation.  It implies a prior indication or knowledge of something before its official manifestation. | Example: The premanifested cargo was quickly processed through customs.  预先申报的货物很快通过了海关。 The premanifested problems with the software were addressed before the launch. 软件预先显示的问题在发布前得到了解决。| 近义词：predeclared, foreshadowed, preannounced | 反义词：unforeseen, unexpected, unannounced |
