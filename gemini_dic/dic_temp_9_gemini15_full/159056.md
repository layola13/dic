pneumonias:/njuːˈmoʊniəz/ | n. |肺炎（复数） | Pneumonias are lung infections caused by various microorganisms, such as bacteria, viruses, or fungi.  They are characterized by inflammation of the air sacs in one or both lungs. | Example: Several cases of pneumonias were reported in the hospital last week.  上周医院报告了几例肺炎病例。|近义词: lung infections, pulmonary infections |反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑healthy lungs) |名词
