countdown:/ˈkaʊntdaʊn/| n. |倒计时，倒数|A countdown is a sequence of numbers that decrease from a given number to zero, often used to mark the time remaining before an event.  For example, a rocket launch might have a countdown from 10 to 0. |例句：The countdown to the New Year began.  新年倒计时开始了。

The countdown for the launch was interrupted. 发射倒计时被打断了。|近义词：countback,  timer|反义词：count-up
