hacksaws: /ˈhæksɔːz/ | n. | 弓锯；钢锯 | A hacksaw is a hand saw with a fine-toothed blade, typically used for cutting metal.  It's characterized by its thin, rigid blade held in a frame that allows for controlled sawing. |  例句：He used a hacksaw to cut through the metal pipe. 他用弓锯切割金属管道。


例句：I need a hacksaw to cut this bolt. 我需要一把弓锯来锯断这个螺栓。


近义词：hand saw (for some applications), metal saw


反义词：无


词性：名词
