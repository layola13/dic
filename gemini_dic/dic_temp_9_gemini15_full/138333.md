nothing-doing:/ˈnʌθɪŋ ˈduːɪŋ/| n. phr.|无所事事；没有进展|It means there is no activity or progress.  It is used informally to describe a state of inactivity or a lack of progress.|例句： "Nothing doing" said the manager, "We're not taking on any new staff this year."  经理说：“不行，”“今年我们不招任何新员工。”

例句：After weeks of negotiations, it's still nothing doing.  经过几周的谈判，仍然没有进展。


近义词： inactivity, stagnation, no progress, dead end |反义词： activity, progress, advancement

词性: noun phrase
