decanted:/dɪˈkæntɪd/| adj.|已倾析的；澄清的|Having been decanted.  The process of carefully pouring a liquid from one container to another, especially to separate sediment.|例句：The wine had been decanted for several hours before serving.  上菜前，这瓶酒已经醒酒数小时了。|近义词：poured, strained, clarified|反义词：unsettled, unclarified


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sommelier decants the wine. =侍酒师正在醒酒。
He decants the old port carefully. = 他小心翼翼地将陈年波特酒倒出来。


一般过去时:
She decanted the red wine before dinner. = 晚餐前她把红酒醒酒了。
They decanted the sediment from the bottom of the bottle. = 他们把瓶底的沉淀物倒掉了。


现在完成时:
We have decanted the champagne. = 我们已经把香槟酒倒出来了。
The wine has been decanted for an hour. = 这葡萄酒已经醒酒一小时了。


过去完成时:
He had decanted the wine before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经把酒醒好了。
They had decanted the old brandy, removing the sediment. = 他们已经将陈年白兰地倒出，去除了沉淀物。


将来时:
I will decant the wine before the party. = 聚会之前我会把酒醒好。
She will decant the vintage port tomorrow. = 她明天会把年份波特酒倒出来。


虚拟语气:
If I had a decanter, I would decant the wine. = 如果我有个醒酒器，我会把酒醒好。
I wish I had decanted the wine earlier. = 我真希望早点把酒醒好。


被动语态:
The wine was decanted carefully. = 酒被小心地倒出来了。
The sediment was decanted from the bottle. = 瓶中的沉淀物被倒出来了。


疑问句:
Has the wine been decanted yet? = 酒已经醒了吗？
Should we decant this bottle of wine? = 我们应该把这瓶酒醒开吗？


否定句:
They didn't decant the wine. = 他们没有醒酒。
She hadn't decanted the wine before serving it. = 她上菜前没有醒酒。


PHRASE->
Decant the wine: 醒酒
Decant a bottle of wine: 倒出一瓶酒
Decant the sediment:  去除沉淀物
Carefully decant: 小心地倾倒
Decant into a carafe: 倒入酒瓶
