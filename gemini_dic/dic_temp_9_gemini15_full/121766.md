misquoter:/mɪsˈkwəʊtər/ | n. | 误引用者，曲解者 | One who misquotes or distorts the words or meaning of another.  A misquoter deliberately or unintentionally alters the original meaning of a quotation. | Example: The journalist was accused of being a misquoter, twisting the politician's words to fit his narrative.  这位记者被指控为误引用者，歪曲了政治家的言论以符合他的叙述。|近义词：distorter, perverter, misinterpreter | 反义词：accurate reporter, faithful reproducer | 名词

The student did not misquote the source intentionally. = 学生并非故意误引用资料来源。
