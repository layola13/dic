formaldehyde:/fɔːrˈmældihaɪd/ | n. | 甲醛 | A colorless, pungent gas, CH₂O, used chiefly in the manufacture of resins, plastics, and disinfectants. It is a known carcinogen. | Example:  Formaldehyde is a common preservative used in some biological specimens. 甲醛是某些生物标本中常用的防腐剂。

The prolonged exposure to formaldehyde can cause serious health problems. 长期接触甲醛会引起严重的健康问题。|  近义词：methanal | 反义词：无
