misread:/ˌmɪsˈriːd/| vt.|误读，误解|To interpret or understand something incorrectly.  |例句：I misread the map and got lost. 我看错地图迷路了。|例句：He misread the situation and made a terrible mistake. 他误解了形势，犯了一个严重的错误。|近义词：misinterpret, misunderstand |反义词：understand, interpret correctly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I misread the instructions. = 我误读了说明。
She misreads the signs of the times. = 她误读了时代的迹象。

一般过去时:
I misread the email. = 我误读了邮件。
He misread the poem's meaning. = 他误读了这首诗的意思。

现在完成时:
I have misread the contract. = 我误读了合同。
She has misread the data several times. = 她已经几次误读了数据。

过去完成时:
I had misread the address before I arrived. = 我到达之前就误读了地址。
They had misread the clues leading to the solution. = 他们误读了解决问题的线索。

将来时:
I will misread the text if I don't wear my glasses. = 如果我不戴眼镜，我会误读文本。
They will misread the graph and make a wrong decision. = 他们会误读图表并做出错误的决定。

虚拟语气:
If I had not misread the sign, I would have turned left. = 如果我没有误读标志，我就会左转。
I wish I had not misread the instructions. = 我真希望我没有误读说明。

被动语态:

The message was misread due to poor communication. = 由于沟通不畅，信息被误读了。

疑问句:

否定句:
I didn't misread the book. = 我没有误读这本书。
They didn't misread the signals. = 他们没有误读信号。
