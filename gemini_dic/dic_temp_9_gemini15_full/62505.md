endowed:/ɪnˈdaʊd/| adj. v.|赋予的；捐赠的；天生具有…的|Provided or supplied with something, especially a talent or quality.  To give or bequeath an endowment to (someone or something).|例句：She was endowed with a sharp mind. 她天生聪明。 The university was endowed by a wealthy benefactor. 这所大学由一位富有的捐助者捐赠。|近义词：gifted, talented, blessed, equipped |反义词：deprived, lacking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He endows the charity annually. = 他每年都捐赠给那个慈善机构。
The school is endowed with modern facilities. = 这所学校配备了现代化的设施。


一般过去时:
She endowed her daughter with a large sum of money. = 她给了她女儿一大笔钱。
The philanthropist endowed the hospital with a new wing. = 慈善家为医院捐赠了一个新的病房。


现在完成时:
The foundation has endowed numerous research projects. = 该基金会已经资助了许多研究项目。
He has been endowed with exceptional musical talent. = 他天生拥有非凡的音乐天赋。


过去完成时:
The artist had endowed his paintings with deep emotional meaning. = 艺术家在他的画作中注入了深刻的情感。
Before the ceremony, the family had endowed the church with a new organ. = 典礼前，这家人为教堂捐赠了一架新的管风琴。


将来时:
She will endow her scholarship fund with a further donation. = 她将向她的奖学金基金再捐赠一笔款项。
The government will endow the research institute with more funding. = 政府将为该研究所提供更多资金。


虚拟语气:
If he were endowed with more patience, he would succeed. = 如果他更有耐心，他就会成功。
I wish I were endowed with the gift of eloquence. = 我希望我拥有雄辩的天赋。


被动语态:
The hospital was endowed with state-of-the-art equipment. = 医院配备了最先进的设备。
The scholarship was endowed by a generous donor. = 这笔奖学金是由一位慷慨的捐赠者提供的。


疑问句:

Was the project endowed by a private company? = 这个项目是由一家私营公司资助的吗？


否定句:
The program was not endowed with sufficient resources. = 该项目没有足够的资源。
He was not endowed with the physical strength for the task. = 他没有完成这项任务所需的体力。
