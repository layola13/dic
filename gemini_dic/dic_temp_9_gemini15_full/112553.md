lovelessness:/ˈlʌvləsnəs/| n. |无爱，缺乏爱，冷淡|The state or quality of being without love; a lack of love or affection; coldness.|例句：His lovelessness made her feel isolated and unhappy. 他的冷漠让她感到孤立和不快乐。

例句：The lovelessness in their marriage led to its eventual dissolution.  他们婚姻中的无爱最终导致了婚姻的破裂。


近义词：coldness, indifference, apathy, unfeelingness |反义词：love, affection, warmth, tenderness
