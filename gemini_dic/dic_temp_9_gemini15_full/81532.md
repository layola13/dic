gravitational:/ˌɡrævɪˈteɪʃənəl/| adj.|万有引力的，重力的|relating to or caused by gravitation.  The force of attraction between all matter.|例句：The gravitational pull of the moon affects the tides. 月球的万有引力影响着潮汐。

例句：The astronauts experienced weightlessness due to the reduced gravitational force in space. 宇航员在太空中由于重力减小而经历了失重。


近义词：gravitative


反义词：None (no direct antonym, but "repulsive" could be considered opposite in a physics context)


词性:形容词
