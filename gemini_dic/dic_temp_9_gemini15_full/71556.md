flashboard:/ˈflæʃbɔːrd/| n.|闪光板，信息板|A flashboard is a board or panel, often made of wood or plastic, used to display information quickly and temporarily. It can be used for announcements, warnings, or other brief messages.  It often involves some mechanism for quickly changing the displayed information.|例句：The teacher used a flashboard to show the students the spelling of the word.  老师用闪光板给学生们展示单词的拼写。The construction crew used a flashboard to warn drivers of road closures. 建设队用闪光板警告司机道路封闭。|近义词：notice board, bulletin board, sign|反义词:permanent display
