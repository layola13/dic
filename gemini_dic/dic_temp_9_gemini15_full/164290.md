predynastic: /ˌpriːdaɪˈnæstɪk/ | adj. | 前王朝的，王朝之前的 |  Relating to the period in ancient Egypt before the first dynasty.  This era is characterized by the development of a complex society, including agriculture, settlements, and the beginnings of a centralized government, but before the establishment of a formal dynasty and pharaonic rule. | Example: Predynastic Egypt saw the development of sophisticated pottery and artistic styles.  前王朝时期的埃及出现了复杂的陶器和艺术风格。  The predynastic period is crucial for understanding the origins of Egyptian civilization. 前王朝时期对于理解埃及文明的起源至关重要。|近义词：prehistoric, protodynastic | 反义词：dynastic
