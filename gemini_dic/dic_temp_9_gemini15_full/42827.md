couriered:/ˈkʊriərd/| v.|用快递送达；快递|To send something by courier.|The package was couriered overnight.  包裹隔夜快递送达了。|The documents were couriered to the client. 文件已快递给客户。|sent, dispatched, expedited|delayed, held back|动词


USAGE->

They courier documents every day. = 他们每天都快递文件。
We courier packages to clients worldwide. = 我们向全球客户快递包裹。


一般过去时:
She couriered the letter yesterday. = 她昨天快递了这封信。
He couriered the package to his sister. = 他把包裹快递给了他姐姐。


现在完成时:
I have couriered all the important documents. = 我已经快递了所有重要的文件。
They have couriered the package to the correct address. = 他们已将包裹快递到正确的地址。


过去完成时:
She had couriered the package before the deadline. = 她在截止日期前已经快递了包裹。
We had couriered the documents before the meeting started. = 我们在会议开始前已经快递了文件。


将来时:
