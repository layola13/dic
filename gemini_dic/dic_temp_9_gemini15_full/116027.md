marsupialian:/ˌmɑːrsʊˈpeɪliən/| adj. n.|有袋动物的；有袋动物|An adjective describing animals belonging to the infraclass Marsupialia, characterized by the presence of a pouch in which the young are raised.  Also used as a noun referring to any member of this group.|Example: Kangaroos are marsupialian mammals. 袋鼠是有袋哺乳动物。  The marsupialian fauna of Australia is incredibly diverse. 澳大利亚的有袋动物区系极其多样化。|近义词：pouched (adj.)|反义词：placental (adj.)
