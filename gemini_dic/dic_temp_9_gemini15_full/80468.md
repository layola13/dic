gonna: /ˈɡɒnə/ | /ˈɡɑːnə/ | v. | 将要，打算 | Contraction of "going to," used to express the future tense.  It's informal. |例句：I'm gonna go to the store. = 我打算去商店。

I'm gonna eat dinner. = 我打算吃晚饭。


近义词：going to, will, about to | 反义词：N/A | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am going to the park. = 我要去公园。
She is going to the market. = 她要去市场。


一般过去时:
He was going to leave. = 他正打算离开。
They were going to study. = 他们打算学习。


现在完成时:
I have been going to do it. = 我一直打算做这件事。
She has been going to call him. = 她一直打算给他打电话。


过去完成时:
I had been going to visit her. = 我本来打算去看望她。
They had been going to travel. = 他们本来打算旅行。


将来时:
I am going to finish this tomorrow. = 我打算明天完成这个。
We are going to have a party next week. = 我们下周打算举办一个派对。


虚拟语气:
If I were going to do that, I would tell you. = 如果我打算那样做，我会告诉你。
I wish I were going to be there. = 我希望我能在那里。


被动语态:
The project was going to be finished on time. = 项目本来打算按时完成。
The meeting was going to be held tomorrow. = 会议本来打算明天举行。


疑问句:
Are you going to buy a new car? = 你打算买一辆新车吗？
Is she going to attend the conference? = 她打算参加会议吗？


否定句:
I am not going to do that. = 我不打算那样做。
He was not going to give up. = 他不打算放弃。


PHRASE->
gonna get = 将要得到
gonna be = 将会是
gonna do = 将要做
gonna have = 将会有
gonna see = 将会看见
