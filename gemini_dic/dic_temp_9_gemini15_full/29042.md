cartouches:/kɑːˈtuːʃɪz/| n.|卷轴；图饰；弹药筒；|Cartouches are ornamental designs, often in the shape of a scroll, used to decorate a building or object. They can also refer to the cases holding explosive charges in firearms or other weapons.|The ancient Egyptians used cartouches to enclose the names of pharaohs.  = 古埃及人用卷轴来包围法老的名字。 The artist skillfully incorporated cartouches into the design of the tapestry. = 艺术家巧妙地将卷轴图案融入挂毯的设计中。|decorations, ornaments, embellishments| (近义词缺乏精确的反义词，因为cartouche本身并非描述状态)|名词
