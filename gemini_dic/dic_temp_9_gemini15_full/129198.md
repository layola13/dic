nobleman:/ˈnoʊblmən/| n. |贵族，绅士 |A nobleman is a man who belongs to the nobility, holding a hereditary title and rank.  They often possess significant wealth, land, and social influence. |例句：The nobleman graciously hosted a grand ball at his castle.这位贵族在他的城堡里盛情款待了一个盛大的舞会。|例句：Many noblemen supported the king during the war.许多贵族在战争期间支持国王。|近义词：gentleman, aristocrat, peer |反义词：commoner, peasant
