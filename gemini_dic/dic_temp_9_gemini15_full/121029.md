miscomputes:/ˌmɪskəmˈpjuːts/| vt.| 计算错误，误算 | To calculate incorrectly.  This verb implies an error in calculation, not a simple mistake in judgment. |例句：He miscomputed the total cost of the project by hundreds of dollars. 他把项目的总成本计算错误了几百美元。


例句：The software miscomputed the trajectory, leading to a navigation error.  该软件误算了轨迹，导致导航错误。


近义词：miscalculate, err, make a mistake in calculation


反义词：compute accurately, calculate correctly


词性:动词
