paratroop:/ˈpærətruːp/| n. |伞兵；空降兵| A paratrooper is a soldier who is trained to parachute from an aircraft.  Paratroops are elite soldiers known for their specialized training and ability to deploy rapidly behind enemy lines. |Example: The paratroops landed behind enemy lines under the cover of darkness.  伞兵在夜色的掩护下降落在敌后。  The paratroop regiment was deployed to the conflict zone. 空降兵团被部署到冲突地区。|近义词：airborne troops, sky soldier|反义词：ground troops
