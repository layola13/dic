lanker:/ˈlæŋkər/| n. |瘦长的人；懒汉| A lanker is a tall, thin person, often implying a lack of physical strength or energy;  it can also refer to a lazy or idle person.|例句：He was a lanker of a man, all arms and legs. 他是个瘦高个子，全是胳膊腿。|近义词：gawky, lanky, skinny, spindly, stringbean |反义词: stout, burly, stocky
