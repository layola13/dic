selecting: /səˈlɛktɪŋ/ | v. | 选择；挑选 | The act of choosing or picking something from a group.  This word is the present participle of the verb "select". | 例句：He is selecting a new car. 他正在选一辆新车。
They are selecting candidates for the job. 他们正在挑选该职位的候选人。 | 近义词：choosing, picking, opting,  | 反义词：rejecting, discarding, ignoring | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am selecting the best apples. = 我正在挑选最好的苹果。
She is selecting a dress for the party. = 她正在为派对挑选一件衣服。

一般过去时:
I selected a red pen. = 我选择了一支红色的笔。
They selected him as the team leader. = 他们选他当队长。

现在完成时:
I have selected the winner. = 我已经选出了获胜者。
She has selected a university to attend. = 她已经选择了一所大学就读。

过去完成时:
I had selected my courses before the deadline. = 我在截止日期前已经选好了课程。
They had selected the location for the meeting. = 他们已经在会议前选好了地点。

将来时:
I will select a gift for her birthday. = 我将为她的生日挑选一份礼物。
We will select a new president next month. = 我们下个月将选出一位新总统。

虚拟语气:
If I were selecting a team, I would choose the most skilled players. = 如果我来选队，我会选择最熟练的球员。
I wish I had selected a different path in my career. = 我真希望我当初选择了一条不同的职业道路。

被动语态:
The winner was selected by a panel of judges. = 获胜者是由评审团选出的。
The best candidates will be selected for interviews. = 最合适的候选人将被选中参加面试。

疑问句:
Are you selecting a new phone? = 你在选新手机吗？
Have they selected a date for the wedding? = 他们选好婚礼日期了吗？

否定句:
I did not select that option. = 我没有选择那个选项。
She has not selected a major yet. = 她还没有选择专业。
