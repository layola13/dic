sleepish:/ˈsliːpɪʃ/ | adj. |困倦的，想睡的 | Slightly sleepy; drowsy.  The word suggests a mild state of sleepiness, not the intense tiredness of being exhausted. |例句：I felt sleepish after a long day at work. 我工作了一整天后感到困倦。

例句：The warm sun made me feel quite sleepish.  温暖的阳光让我感到相当困倦。


近义词：drowsy, sleepy, lethargic, somnolent | 反义词：wide-awake, alert, vigilant | 词性：形容词
