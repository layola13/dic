protozoon:/ˌproʊtəˈzoʊən/| n. |原生动物|A single-celled microscopic animal of a group of phyla, the Protozoa. Protozoa are eukaryotic organisms, meaning their cells contain a membrane-bound nucleus.  They are found in various habitats, including soil, water, and as parasites in other organisms. | Example:  Amoebas and paramecia are examples of protozoa.  原生动物的例子有变形虫和草履虫。|近义词：protist (a broader term)|反义词：metazoan (multicellular animal)
