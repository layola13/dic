foreshorten:/fɔːˈʃɔːtn/| vt.|缩短；透视缩短|To represent (an object or figure) in a picture or drawing as shorter than it actually is, especially to give an impression of depth.  This effect is created by the fact that objects further away appear smaller than those closer to the viewer.|例句：The artist foreshortened the figure to create a sense of depth.  艺术家将人物缩短以营造深度感。

The perspective foreshortened the length of the road. 透视使道路看起来变短了。|近义词：shorten, contract, abbreviate|反义词：elongate, extend, lengthen|动词
