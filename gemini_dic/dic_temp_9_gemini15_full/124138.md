mortiferous:/mɔːrˈtɪfərəs/| adj.|致死的，致命的；令人沮丧的 | causing or capable of causing death; depressing or humiliating | The mortiferous effects of the poison were immediate.  这种毒药的致命效果是即时的。|近义词：deadly, lethal, fatal, deadly, depressing |反义词：life-giving, invigorating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The snake's venom is mortiferous. = 蛇的毒液是致命的。
This news is mortiferous to his ambitions. = 这个消息对他雄心勃勃的计划是致命的打击。


一般过去时:
The disease proved mortiferous to many villagers. = 这种疾病证明对许多村民是致命的。
The criticism was mortiferous to his self-esteem. = 这次批评打击了他的自尊心。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:

词源:
From Latin *mortifer, from mors, mort- "death" + ferre "to bear, carry".  The word combines "death" and "bearing," literally meaning "death-bearing."
