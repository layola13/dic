munchers:/ˈmʌntʃərz/| n. |大吃特吃的人；猛嚼者 |People who eat something noisily or greedily.  It often implies a large quantity of food is being consumed.  The term is informal and slightly derogatory. |例句：The munchers at the buffet table were clearing out the desserts.  自助餐桌上的那些大吃特吃的人把甜点都吃光了。

The kids were such munchers, they finished the whole pizza in minutes.  孩子们真是吃货，几分钟就把整个披萨吃完了。 |近义词：glutton, gorger, eater, consumer|反义词：nibbler, taster, picke
