cell:/sɛl/| n.|细胞；电池；单间牢房；蜂窝；小房间|A cell is the basic structural and functional unit of all living organisms.  It is a small, membrane-bound unit containing the fundamental molecules of life.|例句：Plant cells contain chloroplasts for photosynthesis. 植物细胞含有用于光合作用的叶绿体。|例句：My phone's battery cell needs replacing. 我的手机电池需要更换。|近义词：unit, compartment, chamber|反义词：organism (in the context of being the opposite of a single cell)|词性:名词
