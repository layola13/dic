preintention:/ˌpriːɪnˈtɛnʃən/| n. |预先的意图；预谋；预先计划|The act or process of forming an intention before acting; premeditation.  It refers to the mental state preceding conscious intention, a preparatory phase of action planning.  It's a concept often discussed in philosophy of action and cognitive psychology.|例句：The preintention to deceive was evident in his carefully constructed alibi.  他的精心编造的不在场证明表明了他事先有欺骗的意图。|近义词：forethought, premeditation, planning, design|反义词：impulsiveness, spontaneity|名词
