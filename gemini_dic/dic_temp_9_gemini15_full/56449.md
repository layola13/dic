dolomite:/ˈdɒləmaɪt/ /ˈdɑːləmaɪt/| n. |白云石|A carbonate mineral composed of calcium and magnesium carbonate, CaMg(CO₃)₂.  It is commonly found in sedimentary rocks and is used in various industrial applications.|例句：The geologist identified the sample as dolomite. 地质学家确定该样本为白云石。|例句：Dolomite is used in the production of cement and refractories. 白云石用于水泥和耐火材料的生产。|近义词：magnesian calcite|反义词：无
