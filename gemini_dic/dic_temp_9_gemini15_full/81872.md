griefs:/ɡriːfs/| n.|悲伤，忧愁，苦恼|grief refers to intense sorrow, especially caused by loss, misfortune, or death.  It is a deep and often prolonged feeling of sadness.|例句：Her grief over the loss of her husband was overwhelming. 她因失去丈夫而悲伤欲绝。


例句：The family is still grieving the death of their son.  这个家庭仍在为他们儿子的去世而悲痛。


近义词：sorrow, sadness, anguish, distress, woe


反义词：joy, happiness, gladness, mirth


词性：名词
