nontimbered:/ˌnɒnˈtɪmbəd/ | adj. |非木材的；非木质的 |Not made of or relating to timber.  This term is used to describe forest products and resources other than wood. It emphasizes the diverse economic and ecological values beyond lumber production.  | Example: Nontimbered forest products include mushrooms, berries, and medicinal plants.  非木材林产品包括蘑菇、浆果和药用植物。|近义词：non-wood, secondary forest products |反义词：timbered, wooded
