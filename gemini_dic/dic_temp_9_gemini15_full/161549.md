potpie:/ˈpɑːtpaɪ/| n. |馅饼|A baked or fried dish consisting of meat and vegetables in a thick gravy, enclosed in pastry.  It's a hearty and comforting dish, often served as a main course. |例句：My grandmother's chicken pot pie is the best I've ever tasted.  我奶奶做的鸡肉馅饼是我吃过的最好吃的。|例句：For a quick dinner, I heated up a frozen pot pie.为了快速解决晚餐，我加热了一个冷冻馅饼。|近义词：pie, stew, casserole |反义词：salad (in terms of heaviness and richness)

词源:
The word "potpie" is a compound word.  "Pot" refers to the pot in which the filling was originally cooked, and "pie" refers to the pastry crust.  The combination reflects the historical method of preparing the dish.
