overbake:/ˌoʊvərˈbeɪk/| vt.|烤过头；过度烘烤|To bake (something) for too long, resulting in dryness or burning.  |例句：I overbaked the cookies, and now they're too hard. 我把饼干烤过头了，现在太硬了。|例句：The bread was overbaked and had a slightly burnt taste. 面包烤过头了，有一点焦味。|近义词：burn, scorch, dry out|反义词：underbake
