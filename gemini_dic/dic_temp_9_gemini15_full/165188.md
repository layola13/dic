preinhabitant: /ˌpriːɪnˈhæbɪtənt/ | n. |先前居住者，原住民 | A person who lived in a place before it was settled by another group of people.  This term emphasizes the prior occupancy of a place before a new population arrived.  It's often used in historical or archaeological contexts. | Example: The preinhabitants of this valley left behind many artifacts.  这个山谷的先前居住者留下了许多文物。|近义词：original inhabitant, prior resident, former occupant |反义词：newcomer, immigrant, settler |名词
