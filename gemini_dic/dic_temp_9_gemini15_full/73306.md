forecastles:/ˈfɔːrkæslz/| n.|船首楼，艏楼|The forecastle is the forward part of a ship's upper deck, often enclosed and used for crew quarters.  It's a structure built on the main deck, at the front of the ship.|Example: The sailors slept in the cramped forecastle. 船员们睡在狭小的船首楼里。|近义词：forecastle deck, bow|反义词：stern
