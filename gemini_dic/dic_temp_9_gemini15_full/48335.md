decriminalizations:/ˌdiːkrɪmɪnəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |非罪化；除罪化|The process of removing criminal penalties from an act or behavior.  It's often used in the context of social and political reform, aiming to reduce the burden on the justice system and address issues of social justice.  |例句：The decriminalization of marijuana is a hotly debated topic in many countries.  大麻非罪化是许多国家热烈讨论的话题。

The decriminalizations of certain victimless crimes have led to a decrease in prison populations.  某些无受害者犯罪的非罪化导致监狱人口减少。|近义词：legalization, liberalization|反义词：criminalization
