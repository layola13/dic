cubile:/ˈkjuːbaɪl/ | n. |兽窝；卧处；巢穴 | A cubile is a resting place, especially for a wild animal.  It can refer to a den, lair, or nest.  The term evokes a sense of shelter and seclusion. | Example: The lioness returned to her cubile to nurse her cubs. 母狮回到她的兽窝喂养幼狮。 |近义词：den, lair, nest, burrow |反义词：open, exposed
