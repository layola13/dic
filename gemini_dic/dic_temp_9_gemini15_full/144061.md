outroaring: /ˌaʊtˈrɔːrɪŋ/ | v. | 大声喧哗着；盖过…的声音 |  The verb "outroaring" means to roar louder than someone or something else; to exceed in loudness. It implies a contest or comparison of roaring sounds.  | 例句：The lion outroared all the other animals in the jungle.  =  狮子咆哮声盖过了丛林里其他所有动物。


The waves outroared the storm's fury. = 海浪声盖过了暴风雨的怒吼。


近义词：outbawling, outshouting, outyelling,  drowning out


反义词：whispering, murmuring


词性：动词
