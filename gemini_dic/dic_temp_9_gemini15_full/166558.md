prescience:/ˈpreʃəns/| n.|预知；先见之明|The ability to know or predict what will happen in the future.  It implies a superior understanding or insight beyond ordinary perception.  |例句：Her prescience in the stock market made her a fortune.  她的股票市场预见力让她赚了一大笔钱。


例句：The author's prescience about the social impact of technology is astonishing.  作者对科技社会影响的预见令人震惊。


近义词：foresight, foreknowledge, prophecy, intuition


反义词：hindsight, shortsightedness


词性：名词
