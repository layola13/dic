podgiest:/ˈpɑːdʒist/| adj.|最笨拙的，最笨重的|Most clumsy or awkward.  The word implies a lack of grace or dexterity, often in a humorous or endearing way. It's an informal and somewhat rare word.|例句：He moved with the podgiest of gaits, tripping over his own feet. 他走起路来笨手笨脚的，总是绊倒自己。


例句：The podgiest attempt at a cake I've ever seen was still delicious. 我见过的最笨拙的蛋糕尝试仍然很好吃。


近义词：clumsiest, gawkiest, most awkward, most ungainly|反义词：graceful, nimble, dexterous, elegant
