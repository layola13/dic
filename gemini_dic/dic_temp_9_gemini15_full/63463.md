entrepreneurship:/ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːrʃɪp/ | n. |创业精神；企业家才能 | The process of designing, launching and running a new business, which is often initially a small business.  It's characterized by innovation, risk-taking, and the creation of value.  Entrepreneurship involves identifying opportunities, gathering resources, and managing risks to create a profitable venture. |  His entrepreneurship led him to start a successful tech company.  他的创业精神使他创办了一家成功的科技公司。 | enterprise, initiative, business acumen | employment, dependence | 名词
