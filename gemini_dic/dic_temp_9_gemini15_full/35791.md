cockcrow:/ˈkɒkˌkroʊ/ /ˈkɑːkˌkroʊ/| n.| 鸡叫，报晓 |The sound made by a rooster at dawn.|例句：The cockcrow woke me up this morning.  鸡叫声今天早上把我吵醒了。
The cockcrow announced the start of a new day. 鸡叫声宣告新一天的开始。|近义词：dawn chorus, crowing|反义词：None|名词
