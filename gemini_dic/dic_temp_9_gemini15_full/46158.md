cynic:/ˈsɪnɪk/| n. |愤世嫉俗者，犬儒主义者 | A person who believes that people are motivated purely by self-interest; a person who distrusts human sincerity or goodness.  |例句：He's such a cynic; he never believes anyone is truly altruistic. 他是个愤世嫉俗的人；他从不相信任何人真正利他。

例句：Her cynical remarks about the politician's promises left everyone feeling disillusioned. 她对这位政治家承诺的愤世嫉俗的评论让每个人都感到幻灭。


近义词：pessimist, skeptic, doubter |反义词：optimist, idealist
