shape-shifts:/ʃeɪpʃɪfts/| v. |变形；变化形态 |To change one's shape or form, often in a magical or supernatural way.  It can also refer to a more metaphorical change in character or appearance.|例句：The creature shape-shifted into a dragon.  那生物变形成一条龙。

The company shape-shifted its marketing strategy to attract a younger demographic. 公司改变了其营销策略以吸引更年轻的受众群体。|近义词：metamorphose, transform, transfigure |反义词：remain the same, stay constant |动词
