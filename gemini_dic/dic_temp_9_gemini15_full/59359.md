earner:/ˈɜːrnər/| n. |赚钱的人，收入者 |A person who earns money, especially through employment.  |She's a high earner in the finance industry.  她是金融行业的高收入者。|high earner, breadwinner|low earner, unemployed|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a high earner. = 他是一位高收入者。
She earns a good living. = 她生活过得不错。


一般过去时:
He was a successful earner in his prime. = 他在鼎盛时期是一位成功的赚钱者。
She earned a lot of money last year. = 她去年赚了很多钱。


现在完成时:
He has been a consistent earner throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中一直是一位稳定的赚钱者。
She has earned enough money to buy a house. = 她已经赚够了买房子的钱。


过去完成时:
He had been a top earner before the economic downturn. = 在经济衰退之前，他一直是顶级赚钱者。
She had earned a considerable amount before starting her own business. = 在开始自己的事业之前，她已经赚了一笔可观的钱。


将来时:
He will be a high earner in the future. = 他将来会成为高收入者。
She will earn more money with her promotion. = 她升职后会赚更多钱。


虚拟语气:
If he were a higher earner, he would buy a bigger house. = 如果他的收入更高，他会买一栋更大的房子。
I wish I were a better earner. = 我希望我是一个更好的赚钱者。


被动语态:
The position is only for high earners. = 这个职位只适合高收入者。
This amount of money was earned through hard work. = 这笔钱是通过努力工作赚来的。


疑问句:
Is she a good earner? = 她是一个赚钱高手吗？
How much does he earn? = 他赚多少钱？


否定句:
He is not a high earner. = 他不是高收入者。
She didn't earn much money last month. = 她上个月没赚多少钱。
