franked:/fræŋkt/| adj.|已加盖邮戳的；免邮资的 | Having had postage prepaid, typically by means of a franking machine.  |The letter was franked and ready to be sent. 这封信已加盖邮戳，可以寄出了。|近义词：prepaid, stamped|反义词：unstamped, unpaid


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company franks all its outgoing mail. = 公司为所有寄出的邮件都支付邮资。
He franks his letters using a special machine. = 他使用一台专用机器为他的信件盖邮戳。


一般过去时:
She franked the envelope before dropping it in the mailbox. = 她把信封盖上邮戳后才投入邮箱。
They franked the parcels and sent them off. = 他们给包裹盖上邮戳后寄了出去。


现在完成时:
We have franked all the invitations for the wedding. = 我们已经为婚礼的所有请柬都盖上了邮戳。
The office has franked thousands of letters this month. = 这个月办公室已经加盖了数千封信的邮戳。


过去完成时:

They had franked all the documents before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经为所有文件盖上了邮戳。
