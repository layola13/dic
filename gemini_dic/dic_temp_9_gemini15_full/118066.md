menders:/ˈmɛndərz/ | n. | 修补者，修理工 | People who mend things, especially clothes or shoes.  They are skilled in repairing damaged items. | Example: The menders skillfully repaired the torn fabric.  修补匠熟练地修补了撕裂的织物。 The shoe menders on the street corner were busy fixing shoes for their customers. 街角的修鞋匠正忙着为顾客修鞋。 |近义词：repairers, fixers, cobblers (for shoes) |反义词：destroyers, breakers
