ironed:/ˈaɪənd/| adj. v.|熨烫过的；已熨平的|Having been pressed with a hot iron to remove creases and wrinkles.|The shirt was neatly ironed.|近义词：pressed, smoothed|反义词：creased, wrinkled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She irons her clothes every Sunday. = 她每周日都熨烫衣服。
He irons his shirts before work. = 他上班前熨烫衬衫。


一般过去时:
I ironed my dress for the party. = 我为派对熨烫了我的裙子。
She ironed all the laundry yesterday. = 她昨天熨烫了所有的衣服。


现在完成时:
I have ironed all the shirts. = 我已经把所有的衬衫都熨烫好了。
She has ironed the tablecloth for dinner. = 她已经为晚饭熨烫了桌布。


过去完成时:
I had ironed the clothes before he arrived. = 他来之前我已经熨好衣服了。
She had ironed the shirts before going to work. = 她上班前已经熨好衬衫了。


将来时:
I will iron the clothes tomorrow. = 我明天会熨衣服。
She will iron the dress for the interview. = 她会为面试熨烫裙子。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would iron the shirts. = 如果我有时间，我会熨烫衬衫。
I wish I had ironed my dress before going out. = 我真希望出门前熨烫了我的裙子。


被动语态:
The shirts were ironed by my mother. = 衬衫是我妈妈熨烫的。
The tablecloth is being ironed now. = 桌布正在被熨烫。


疑问句:
Have you ironed the shirts yet? = 你熨好衬衫了吗？
Did she iron the dress? = 她熨烫了那件裙子吗？


否定句:
I didn't iron my clothes today. = 我今天没有熨衣服。
She hasn't ironed the tablecloth yet. = 她还没熨烫桌布。


PHRASE->
iron out = 解决（问题）
iron-clad = 坚不可摧的；非常牢固的
iron will = 坚强的意志
iron fist = 铁腕；严厉的手段
iron age = 铁器时代
