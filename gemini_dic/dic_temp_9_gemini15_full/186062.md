rewriter's:/ˈriːˌraɪtərz/| n. |改写者，重写者 |A rewriter is a person who rewrites something, such as a text or a piece of music.  They may improve clarity, style, or accuracy. |Example: The editor hired a rewriter to make the article more engaging for a wider audience.  编辑雇佣了一位改写者来使文章对更广泛的读者更具吸引力。|近义词：editor, reviser, reviser,  amendator|反义词：original author, first writer|名词
