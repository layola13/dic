eking:/ˈiːkɪŋ/| v.|勉强维持；增加少量； |to increase or add to something, often by a small amount; to barely manage or survive.|The family were eking out a living on a small farm.  这家靠一个小农场勉强维持生计。|He eked out a meager existence. 他勉强维持着贫困的生活。|augment, supplement|starve, perish|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They are eking out a living. = 他们在勉强维持生计。
He ekes out his income by tutoring. = 他通过辅导来增加收入。


一般过去时:
She eked out a living by sewing. = 她靠缝纫勉强维持生计。
They eked out their supplies until help arrived. = 他们勉强维持着物资直到救援到来。


现在完成时:
We have eked out a comfortable life. = 我们已经过着舒适的生活（虽然可能经历了艰辛）。
They have eked out a victory. = 他们勉强取得了胜利。


过去完成时:
They had eked out a living in the city for years. = 他们在城市里多年来勉强维持生计。
She had eked out her savings until the end of the month. = 她勉强维持着积蓄直到月底。


将来时:
We will be eking out a living in the countryside. = 我们将在乡下勉强维持生计。
They will eke out a win if they play well. = 如果他们打得好，他们将勉强获胜。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I wouldn't be eking out a living. = 如果我有更多钱，我就不会勉强维持生计了。
If they had worked harder, they might have eked out a better result. = 如果他们更努力工作，他们可能会取得更好的结果。


被动语态:
A living was eked out by many families in the village. = 村里许多家庭都勉强维持着生计。
A victory was eked out in the final seconds of the game. = 比赛最后几秒勉强赢得了胜利。


疑问句:
How are they eking out a living? = 他们如何勉强维持生计？
Are they eking out a profit this quarter? = 他们这个季度能否勉强盈利？


否定句:
They are not eking out a living; they are thriving. = 他们不是勉强维持生计，他们正在蓬勃发展。
He didn't eke out a living through honest work. = 他不是通过诚实工作来勉强维持生计的。
