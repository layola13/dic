dentation:/denˈteɪʃən/| n. |牙齿；齿状；齿列|The arrangement or condition of the teeth.  It can refer to the number, kind, and arrangement of teeth in a jaw, or the process of teeth formation.|例句：The dentist examined the child's dentition carefully.  牙医仔细检查了孩子的牙齿状况。

例句：The irregular dentition caused difficulty in chewing. 不规则的牙齿排列导致咀嚼困难。


近义词：teeth, set of teeth, dental arrangement | 反义词：无


词源:  From Middle French *dentation, from Latin dens, dentis ("tooth").  The suffix "-ation" indicates a process or state.
