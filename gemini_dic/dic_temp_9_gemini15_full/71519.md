flans:/flænz/| n.|法式布丁；蛋奶酥|A flan is a custard dessert with a rich, creamy texture, often baked in a pastry crust.  It's similar to a crème caramel but may have variations in ingredients and preparation.|例句：The restaurant is famous for its delicious caramel flan. 这家餐厅以其美味的焦糖布丁而闻名。|近义词：custard, crème caramel, crème brûlée|反义词：无
