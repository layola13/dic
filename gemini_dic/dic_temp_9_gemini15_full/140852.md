on-the-fly:/ˌɒnðəˈflaɪ/| adj. |即兴的；临时的；当场做的|Immediately; without previous preparation or planning.  It refers to something done spontaneously or improvised, often in response to an immediate need or situation. |例句：He made an on-the-fly decision to change his route. 他临时决定改变路线。

例句：The team had to come up with an on-the-fly solution to the problem. 团队不得不当场想出一个解决方案。


近义词：improvised, impromptu, spontaneous, extemporaneous

反义词：preplanned, premeditated, deliberate


词性：形容词
