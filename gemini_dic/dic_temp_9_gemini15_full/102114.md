isotonic:/ˌaɪsəˈtɒnɪk/ | adj. | 等渗的，等张的 | Having the same osmotic pressure as a particular fluid, typically a body fluid.  Isotonic solutions are used in medicine to avoid damaging cells by osmosis. | Example:  An isotonic saline solution is often used in intravenous drips.  等渗盐水溶液常用于静脉滴注。

The isotonic sports drink helped replenish my electrolytes after a long run.  等渗运动饮料帮助我在长跑后补充电解质。


近义词:isosmotic, isohydric | 反义词:hypertonic, hypotonic


词源: From Greek *iso- (equal) + tonos (tension).  The term refers to solutions having the same osmotic pressure, meaning they have the same concentration of solutes that cannot cross the membrane.
