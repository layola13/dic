prebuy: /ˈpriːbaɪ/ | v. | 预购，提前购买 | To buy something before it is officially released or available.  | 例句：Many fans pre-bought tickets for the concert months in advance.  许多歌迷提前几个月就预购了演唱会的门票。


例句：I pre-bought the new video game so I could play it the moment it was released. 我预购了新的电子游戏，这样我就能在它发行的那一刻玩到它了。


近义词：reserve, order in advance, pre-purchase


反义词：post-purchase (购买后)


词性：动词
