footfight:/ˈfʊtfaɪt/| n.|脚踢打斗，脚斗|A fight using only the feet.  This is typically informal and often implies a chaotic or disorganized fight.|例句：The two rivals engaged in a furious footfight in the mud.  两个对手在泥地里进行了一场激烈的脚踢打斗。|近义词：scuffle, brawl, tussle|反义词：peaceful encounter, negotiation
