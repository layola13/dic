guider:/ˈɡaɪdər/| n. |指导者；向导；导游|A person who guides or leads others.  Someone who shows the way or gives directions.|例句：He hired a local guider to show him around the mountain. 他雇了一位当地导游带他游览山区。|例句：The guider led the tourists through the ancient ruins. 向导带领游客穿过古老的废墟。|近义词：leader, conductor, escort, instructor |反义词：follower, misled
