cooperation:/ˌkoʊˌɑːpəˈreɪʃən/| n. |合作，协作|The act of working together toward a common goal; joint action.  It involves mutual assistance and a shared purpose.|例句：The project's success depended on the cooperation of all team members.  这个项目的成功取决于所有团队成员的合作。

例句：International cooperation is crucial to addressing climate change. 国际合作对于解决气候变化至关重要。


近义词：collaboration, teamwork, partnership, synergy|反义词：conflict, opposition, antagonism|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team cooperates effectively. = 团队有效地合作。
We cooperate on many projects. = 我们在许多项目上合作。


一般过去时:
They cooperated on the research. = 他们在研究方面进行了合作。
The countries cooperated to resolve the crisis. = 各国合作以解决危机。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
