rectifications:/ˌrektɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |改正；纠正；修正|The process of correcting errors or faults.  It involves identifying and fixing problems to achieve accuracy, consistency, or compliance with standards. |例句：The report needs several rectifications before publication. 这份报告需要在发表前进行一些修正。

例句：After careful review, we made several rectifications to the software.  仔细审查后，我们对软件进行了一些修改。


近义词：corrections, amendments, adjustments, repairs

反义词：errors, mistakes, faults, defects
