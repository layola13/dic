hare:/hɛər/| n. |野兔，兔子|A hare is a mammal, similar to a rabbit but typically larger and faster, with longer ears and legs. Hares are known for their speed and agility. They are prey animals for many predators.|例句：The hare darted across the field, escaping the fox.  野兔飞快地穿过田野，逃脱了狐狸的追捕。

The hunter spotted a hare hiding in the tall grass. 猎人发现一只藏在高草丛中的野兔。|近义词：rabbit, jackrabbit|反义词：tortoise (in the context of a race)|名词
