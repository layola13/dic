cold-blooded:/ˈkoʊldˈblʌdɪd/| adj.|冷血的；残酷的；（动物）冷血的|Having or showing a lack of feeling or sympathy.  Often used to describe someone cruel or unfeeling, or animals that are not warm-blooded.|例句：The murderer showed a cold-blooded disregard for human life.  凶手表现出对人命的冷血漠视。

例句：Snakes are cold-blooded reptiles. 蛇是冷血爬行动物。|近义词：callous, heartless, cruel, ruthless|反义词：warm-hearted, compassionate, empathetic|词性：adjective
