mass-producing:/ˈmæs prəˈduːsɪŋ/| v. | 大规模生产，批量生产 | The act of producing something in large quantities, typically using automated or standardized methods.  | Example:  The factory is mass-producing new smartphones.  这家工厂正在大规模生产新款智能手机。  |近义词：manufacturing on a large scale, bulk producing, large-scale production | 反义词：handcrafting, bespoke production, custom production | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mass-produce clothing in that factory. = 那个工厂大规模生产服装。
We mass-produce toys for children. = 我们大规模生产儿童玩具。


一般过去时:
The company mass-produced cars last year. = 公司去年大规模生产汽车。
Ford mass-produced the Model T. = 福特公司大规模生产了T型车。


现在完成时:
They have mass-produced millions of units. = 他们已经大规模生产了数百万件产品。
We have mass-produced this product for over a decade. = 我们已经大规模生产这种产品超过十年了。


过去完成时:
They had mass-produced the product before the market demand increased. = 在市场需求增加之前，他们就已经大规模生产了该产品。
Before the war, they had mass-produced weapons for the army. = 战争爆发前，他们已经为军队大规模生产武器了。


将来时:
They will mass-produce electric vehicles next year. = 他们明年将大规模生产电动汽车。
The company will mass-produce a new line of laptops. = 公司将大规模生产新款笔记本电脑系列。


虚拟语气:
If they mass-produced it, the price would be lower. = 如果他们大规模生产它，价格就会更低。
I wish they mass-produced this particular model. = 我希望他们能大规模生产这种特定型号。


被动语态:
Millions of units are mass-produced annually. = 每年都有数百万件产品被大规模生产。
The product is being mass-produced in several factories. = 该产品正在几家工厂被大规模生产。


疑问句:
Are they mass-producing anything new? = 他们正在大规模生产新的东西吗？
Is this product mass-produced or handmade? = 这种产品是大规模生产的还是手工制作的？


否定句:
They don't mass-produce that model anymore. = 他们不再大规模生产那种型号了。
We didn't mass-produce the product until last year. = 直到去年我们才开始大规模生产该产品。
