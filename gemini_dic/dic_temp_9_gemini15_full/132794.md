nondynastical: /ˌnɑːnˌdaɪˈnæstɪkəl/ | adj. | 非王朝的；非世袭的 | Not relating to or characteristic of a dynasty or hereditary succession.  The term implies a lack of hereditary power transmission. | Example: The nondynastical succession to the throne surprised many.  非王朝的继承王位让许多人感到惊讶。  The country embraced a nondynastical system of government. 这个国家采用了非世袭的政府体制。 | 近义词: nonhereditary, elective | 反义词: dynastic, hereditary |
