moss:/mɒs/ /mɔːs/| n.|苔藓，藓类植物|A small, green, flowerless plant that grows in damp places, typically on walls, rocks, or trees.  Moss is often found in shady, humid environments and plays a crucial role in the ecosystem.|例句：The old stone wall was covered in a thick layer of moss.  古老的石墙上覆盖着一层厚厚的苔藓。

例句：We found soft moss growing under the trees.我们在树下发现了柔软的苔藓。


近义词：lichen, algae|反义词：None (苔藓没有明确的反义词)|词性:名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The moss grows on the north side of the tree. = 苔藓生长在树的北侧。
Moss covers the damp rocks. = 苔藓覆盖着潮湿的岩石。


一般过去时:
The moss grew quickly after the rain. = 雨后苔藓长得很快。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

The stones had been covered in moss for many years. = 这些石头多年来一直被苔藓覆盖着。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

词源:

The word "moss" comes from the Old English word "mose," which itself has uncertain origins, possibly related to Proto-Germanic words associated with wet or swampy areas.  Its evolution reflects its association with damp environments and its prevalence in various languages.
