knows:/noʊz/| v.|知道，了解|To have knowledge or information about something.  It implies certainty.|例句：She knows the answer. 她知道答案。

例句：He knows how to drive a car. 他知道怎么开车。


近义词：understands, is aware of, is familiar with|反义词：ignores, is unaware of, is unfamiliar with|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I know the answer. = 我知道答案。
They know how to cook. = 他们知道怎么烹饪。

一般过去时:
I knew the answer yesterday. = 我昨天知道答案。
She knew him well. = 她很了解他。

现在完成时:
I have known him for ten years. = 我认识他十年了。
She has known the truth for a while. = 她知道真相有一段时间了。

过去完成时:
I had known him before we met. = 我在遇见他之前就认识他了。
They had known the secret for years. = 他们知道这个秘密很多年了。

将来时:
I will know the results tomorrow. = 我明天就知道结果了。
We will know the truth soon. = 我们很快就会知道真相。

虚拟语气:
If I knew the answer, I would tell you. = 如果我知道答案，我会告诉你。
I wish I knew what to do. = 我真希望我知道该做什么。

被动语态:
(The passive voice is not typically used with "know" because it's not usually followed by a direct object that can be the subject of a passive sentence.  Instead, we might use a related verb like "be informed".)  The information was made known to the public. 信息已公布于众。

疑问句:
Do you know the answer? = 你知道答案吗？
Does he know what to do? = 他知道该做什么吗？

否定句:
I don't know the answer. = 我不知道答案。
He doesn't know her name. = 他不知道她的名字。


PHRASE->
know-how: 专业知识；技能
know-it-all: 万事通；自以为是的人
in the know: 内行；知情者
let me know: 告诉我
get to know: 逐渐了解
