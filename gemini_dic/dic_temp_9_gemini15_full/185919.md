It often refers to the continuous flow of money or assets within a system, such as debt repayment or inventory turnover. |例句：The company's success depends on its high revolvency of inventory. 这家公司的成功取决于其高库存周转率。  The credit card's high revolvency rate indicates a large amount of outstanding debt.  这张信用卡的高周转率表明有大量的未偿债务。|近义词：rotation, circulation, turnover |反义词：stagnation, standstill |名词
