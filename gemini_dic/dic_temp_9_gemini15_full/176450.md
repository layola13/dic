re-loaded:/ˌriˈloʊdəd/ | v. |重新装填，重新加载 | To load something again.  This typically refers to loading ammunition into a weapon, or loading data, software, or a webpage into a system. |例句：He quickly re-loaded his rifle and fired again. 他迅速重新装填了他的步枪并再次射击。 |例句：The website re-loaded after a brief interruption. 网站在短暂中断后重新加载。|近义词：reload, recharge, refresh |反义词：unload, empty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reloads his gun every few minutes. = 他每隔几分钟就重新装填一次枪。
She reloads the webpage when it crashes. = 当网页崩溃时，她会重新加载它。

一般过去时:
They re-loaded the truck with supplies. = 他们用物资重新装载了卡车。
He re-loaded the software after the update failed. = 更新失败后，他重新加载了软件。

过去完成时:
