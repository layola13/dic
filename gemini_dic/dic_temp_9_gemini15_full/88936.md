holding-point:/ˈhoʊldɪŋ pɔɪnt/| n. | 暂缓点；待定点；关键点；坚持点 | A holding point is a temporary stopping place or position, often used in a process or plan where further action is delayed until a certain condition is met or further information is obtained. It can also refer to a strategic point in a plan or operation that needs to be maintained. |例句：The project is at a holding point while we await the final budget approval.  项目处于暂停状态，等待最终预算审批。

The troops established a holding point on the ridge to observe enemy movements. 部队在山脊上建立了一个据点来观察敌人的动向。|近义词：checkpoint, staging point, stopping point, interim point |反义词：goal, endpoint, culmination
