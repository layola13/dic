revalidation:/ˌriːvælɪˈdeɪʃən/| n. |重新验证；重新确认；有效期重新确认|The act of validating something again;  restoring something to validity.  It often refers to renewing or confirming the validity of a license, certificate, or other credential. |例句：The doctor's license requires revalidation every five years.  医生的执照需要每五年重新验证一次。
例句：The company underwent a rigorous revalidation process to maintain its ISO certification. 这家公司经历了严格的重新验证流程以保持其 ISO 认证。|近义词：renewal, reaffirmation, recertification |反义词：invalidation, revocation
