chinwag: /ˈtʃɪnwæɡ/ | v. | 闲聊，聊天 | To chat informally; to have a friendly conversation. | 例句：Let's chinwag about the weekend. = 我们来聊聊周末吧。  They were chinwagging away happily in the corner. = 他们在角落里愉快地闲聊着。 | 近义词：chat, gossip, converse | 反义词：argue, quarrel


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They chinwag every day. = 他们每天都聊天。
We chinwag about the latest news. = 我们聊聊最新的新闻。

一般过去时:
We chinwagged for hours. = 我们聊了好几个小时。
They chinwagged about their childhood memories. = 他们聊起了童年的回忆。

现在完成时:
I have chinwagged with him several times. = 我已经和他聊过几次了。
They have chinwagged about the project all morning. = 他们整个上午都在聊这个项目。

过去完成时:
They had chinwagged before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他们已经聊过了。
We had chinwagged about the problem for a long time. = 我们已经就这个问题聊了很久。

将来时:
We will chinwag after work. = 我们下班后会聊聊。
They will chinwag about their plans for the summer. = 他们会聊聊他们夏天的计划。

虚拟语气:

I wish I could chinwag with my friends more often. = 我希望我能更经常地和朋友们聊天。
