cephalanthium:/ˌsɛfəˈlænθiəm/| n. |头状花序|A dense, globose inflorescence in which numerous small flowers are clustered together on a common receptacle, resembling a single flower.  This type of inflorescence is characteristic of certain plant families, such as the Compositae (Asteraceae) and Dipsacaceae.|The cephalanthium of the buttonbush (Cephalanthus occidentalis) is a striking example of this type of flower cluster.|近义词：flower head, capitulum|反义词：None directly applicable, but consider terms describing solitary flowers.|名词
