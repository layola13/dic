retire:/rɪˈtaɪər/ | /rɪˈtaɪə(r)/| v. |退休；退役；撤退；收回；|To stop working permanently, especially because of age or ill health.  |He retired at 65 after a long career. 他65岁退休，结束了他漫长的职业生涯。|She retired from competitive swimming last year. 她去年退出了竞技游泳。|近义词：resign, withdraw, cease,  give up|反义词：begin, start, commence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retires next month. = 他下个月退休。
She retires early every day. = 她每天都早退。


一般过去时:
He retired from the army last year. = 他去年从军队退役了。
She retired to her room after dinner. = 晚饭后她回到房间休息了。


现在完成时:
He has retired from his job. = 他已经退休了。
She has retired to the countryside. = 她已经退休到乡下了。


过去完成时:
He had retired before his health deteriorated. = 在他的健康恶化之前，他已经退休了。
She had retired from her position before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，她已经辞去了职务。


将来时:
He will retire next year. = 他明年将退休。
She will retire to a small village by the sea. = 她将退休到海边的一个小村庄。


虚拟语气:
If I were to retire tomorrow, I would travel the world. = 如果我明天退休，我会环游世界。
I wish I could retire early. = 我希望我能早点退休。


被动语态:
(Not typically used in passive voice, as "retire" is usually an intransitive verb.  A passive construction would be awkward.)


疑问句:
When will you retire? = 你什么时候退休？
Has he retired yet? = 他退休了吗？


否定句:
He didn't retire until he was 70. = 直到70岁他才退休。
She hasn't retired yet, although she's thinking about it. = 她还没退休，虽然她正在考虑此事。


PHRASE->
retire from = 从...退休
retire to = 退到...；退休到...
early retirement = 早期退休
forced retirement = 强迫退休
retirement plan = 退休计划
