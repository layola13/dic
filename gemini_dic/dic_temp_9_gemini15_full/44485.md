crosscheck:/ˈkrɒstʃɛk/| vt. n.|交叉检查，复核|To verify something by checking it against another source or method.  It implies a more thorough and rigorous check than a simple verification.|例句：The accounts department cross-checks all invoices before payment. 会计部门在付款前会交叉检查所有发票。


例句：We need to crosscheck the data from both surveys to ensure accuracy.我们需要交叉检查两个调查的数据以确保准确性。


近义词：verify, check, confirm, double-check


反义词：neglect, overlook


词性:动词(vt.)，名词(n.)
