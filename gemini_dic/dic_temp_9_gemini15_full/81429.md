gratefulness:/ˈɡreɪtfʊlnəs/ | n. | 感激；感谢；感恩 | The feeling of being grateful and thankful.  It involves recognizing and appreciating the good things in one's life, often stemming from kindness or benefits received from others or from fortunate circumstances. |例句：She expressed her deep gratitude for their help. 她表达了她对他们帮助的深深感激。

例句：I felt a surge of gratefulness when I received the unexpected gift. 收到这份意外的礼物时，我感到一阵感激。


近义词：thankfulness, appreciation, gratitude, acknowledgment | 反义词：ingratitude, ungratefulness
