signages:/ˈsaɪnɪdʒɪz/| n. |标牌；标识系统 |Signs, especially commercial signs, collectively.  The airport has excellent signages to guide passengers. 机场有极好的标识系统来引导乘客。|近义词：signs, displays, indicators |反义词：None.

These signages direct customers to the right stores. = 这些标牌引导顾客到正确的商店。
