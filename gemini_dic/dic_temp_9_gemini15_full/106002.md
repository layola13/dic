knapsacks:/ˈnæpsæk/| n.|背包，旅行包|A knapsack is a bag carried on the back, typically by hikers or backpackers, for carrying supplies or equipment.|例句：He packed his knapsack with food and water for the hike. 他把背包装满了食物和水去远足。

例句：She bought a new knapsack for her upcoming camping trip. 她为即将到来的露营旅行买了一个新的背包。|近义词：backpack, rucksack|反义词:handbag, purse
