retriable: /rɪˈtraɪəbl/ | adj. | 可重试的，可再尝试的 | Capable of being tried again; able to be attempted a second time.  This usually refers to a process or task that can be restarted after a failure. |

例句：

The system automatically retries failed transactions.  系统自动重试失败的事务。

The error message indicated that the operation was retriable. 错误信息表明该操作可以重试。


近义词：repeatable, retryable, re-attemptable


反义词：irreparable, irretrievable, unrepeatable


词性:形容词


词源:  Derived from the verb "retry" (to try again), which itself combines "re-" (again) and "try" (to attempt).  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or possibility.
