jolted:/dʒɒltɪd/| v. |猛推；震动；使震惊|To jolt means to give a sudden, violent shake or push to something or someone. It can also mean to shock or surprise someone greatly. |例句：The sudden noise jolted me awake.  突然的噪音把我震醒了。

The earthquake jolted the city. 地震震动了这座城市。

近义词：shook, jarred, startled, surprised |反义词：soothed, calmed
