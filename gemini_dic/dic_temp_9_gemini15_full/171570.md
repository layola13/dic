puller:/ˈpʊlər/| n.|拉动者；拉力器；拔河者|A person or thing that pulls.  A device used for pulling.|例句：He was a strong puller in the tug-of-war. 他是拔河比赛中一个强壮的拉绳者。

例句：The mechanic used a puller to remove the wheel bearing. 技师用拉力器拆卸了轮轴承。|近义词：tractor, extractor, remover|反义词：pusher


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The machine pulls the heavy object. = 机器拉动重物。
