nonoxidizing: /ˌnɒnˈɒksɪdaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 非氧化的；不致氧化的 | Not causing or involving oxidation.  A nonoxidizing environment or condition lacks the necessary oxidizing agents (typically oxygen) to promote oxidation reactions.  |  例句：The reaction was carried out under nonoxidizing conditions to prevent the formation of unwanted byproducts.  该反应在非氧化条件下进行，以防止形成不需要的副产物。


例句：A nonoxidizing atmosphere is crucial for the synthesis of this compound. 非氧化气氛对于这种化合物的合成至关重要。
