overrigidity: /ˌoʊvərɪˈdʒɪdɪti/ | n. | 过度僵硬；过分死板 | Excessive stiffness or inflexibility;  an extreme adherence to rules or routines.  It often implies a negative consequence resulting from this inflexibility. |The overrigidity of the company's policies stifled innovation. 公司政策的过度僵硬扼杀了创新。  The overrigidity of his teaching methods alienated many students. 他教学方法的过分死板疏远了许多学生。 |近义词：inflexibility, rigidity, stiffness, inflexibility, dogmatism |反义词：flexibility, adaptability, suppleness
