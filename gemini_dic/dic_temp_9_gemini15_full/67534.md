extradotal: /ˌɛkstrəˈdoʊtəl/ | adj. | 额外的；附加的；非法的 |  Relating to or situated outside the usual or proper limits or boundaries; especially, outside the scope of established legal procedure.  It often implies something done outside official channels or beyond established authority. | 例句：The extradotal agreement reached by the two countries was not legally binding.  两个国家达成的额外协议不具有法律约束力。

The extradotal actions of the police officer led to an internal investigation. 警官的越权行为导致了内部调查。| 近义词：extralegal, unofficial, unauthorized | 反义词：legal, official, authorized |
