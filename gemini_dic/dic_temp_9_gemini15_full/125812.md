murmured: /ˈmɜːrmərd/ | v. | 低语；小声说；喃喃自语 | To speak softly and indistinctly; to utter in a low tone. |例句：She murmured her agreement. 她低声表示同意。

She murmured something about being tired. 她低声说了些什么关于很累的话。


近义词：whispered, muttered, mumbled | 反义词：shouted, bellowed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He murmurs his prayers every night. 他每天晚上都低声祈祷。
She murmurs sweet nothings to her baby. 她对她的宝宝轻声细语。


一般过去时:
He murmured a reply that I couldn't understand. 他低声回答了一句我听不懂的话。
She murmured her thanks and left. 她低声说了声谢谢就离开了。


现在完成时:
She has murmured her complaints to several people. 她已经向几个人抱怨过了（轻声地）。
He has murmured apologies for his actions. 他已经为自己的行为低声道歉了。


过去完成时:
He had murmured his concerns to his wife before the meeting. 在会议之前，他已向妻子低声诉说了他的担忧。
She had murmured a warning, but nobody heard her. 她低声警告过，但没有人听到。


将来时:
She will murmur her secrets to her diary. 她会把她的秘密低声告诉她的日记。
He will murmur his love for her in her ear. 他会在她耳边低声诉说对她的爱。


虚拟语气:
If he murmured his apologies, she might forgive him. 如果他低声道歉，她可能会原谅他。
I wish she had murmured her love for me. 我希望她曾低声对我表达过爱意。


被动语态:
His name was murmured in awe by the crowd. 他的名字被人群敬畏地低声提及。
Her concerns were murmured among the workers. 她的担忧在工人们中被低声议论。


疑问句:
What did he murmur to her? 他对她低声说了什么？
What was murmured during the meeting? 会议期间低声说了些什么？


否定句:
He did not murmur a word. 他一句话也没说。
She didn't murmur a single complaint. 她没有低声抱怨一句。


PHRASE->

词源:
The word "murmur" comes from the Old French word "murmurer," which in turn derives from the Latin word "murmur," meaning "a low humming sound."  The Latin word is likely onomatopoeic, meaning it imitates the sound it describes.
