classifications: /ˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 分类，分类法 | The act or process of classifying something; a system of categories for classifying things.  It refers to the arrangement of things into groups or categories based on shared characteristics. | Example: The classifications of animals are based on their physical characteristics and genetic makeup. 动物的分类是基于它们的物理特征和基因构成。  The library uses a complex system of classifications to organize its books. 图书馆使用复杂的分类系统来组织书籍。 | 近义词：categorizations, categorizations, groupings, systems | 反义词：randomness, disorder
