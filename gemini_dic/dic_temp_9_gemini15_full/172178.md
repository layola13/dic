pursuit:/pərˈsuːt/ | n. |追求；追捕；职业| The act of pursuing something or someone; a course of action followed or a task undertaken.  It can refer to the act of chasing or hunting something, or the dedicated following of a goal or hobby. |例句：His pursuit of knowledge led him to travel the world. 他的求知欲使他周游世界。

例句：The police are in pursuit of the escaped prisoner. 警察正在追捕逃犯。

近义词：chase, pursuit, quest, search | 反义词：abandonment, neglect
