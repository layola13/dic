neuromancer:/ˈnjuːrəʊmænsər/| n. |神经漫游者，赛博朋克小说|A person who can directly interface with a computer system through their nervous system, often portrayed in science fiction as having enhanced abilities.  The term also refers to the 1984 cyberpunk novel by William Gibson, which popularized the term and the genre.|Example:  The novel *Neuromancer* is considered a seminal work of cyberpunk literature.  神经漫游者小说被认为是赛博朋克文学的开山之作。|近义词:cyberpunk,  data-thief (in the context of the novel)|反义词: None directly applicable.
