patamar:/pætəˈmɑːr/| n.|平台；阶地；水平；阶段|A patamar is a level or stage, often used to describe a plateau or a period of stability.  It can also refer to a raised platform or terrace.|例句：The company reached a patamar of success after years of hard work.  这家公司经过多年的努力，达到了成功的巅峰。|例句：The hikers rested on a natural patamar overlooking the valley.  远足者在一个俯瞰山谷的天然平台上休息。|近义词：plateau, level, stage, phase|反义词：decline, descent, drop
