frats:/fræts/| n.|兄弟会，联谊会| Fraternity is a term often used interchangeably with frat. Frats are social organizations, typically for male college students. They often involve social events, philanthropic activities, and a sense of brotherhood.  |例句：He pledged a frat his freshman year. 他大一加入了一个兄弟会。|例句：Many frats participate in charity work. 许多兄弟会参与慈善工作。|近义词：fraternities, brotherhoods|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with solitary activities or non-affiliated student groups.
