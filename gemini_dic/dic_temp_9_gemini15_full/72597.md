fly-driver: /ˈflaɪˌdraɪvər/ | n. |飞行员,驾驶员 |A person who operates a flying machine, especially an airplane.  |例句：The fly-driver expertly navigated the aircraft through the storm.  飞行员熟练地驾驶飞机穿过暴风雨。|近义词：pilot, aviator, airman |反义词：passenger


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fly-driver flies the plane every day. = 飞行员每天都驾驶飞机。
He expertly controls the aircraft during take off. = 他在起飞过程中熟练地控制飞机。


一般过去时:
The fly-driver flew the plane to London yesterday. = 飞行员昨天驾驶飞机去了伦敦。
She skillfully landed the plane in adverse weather conditions. = 她熟练地在恶劣天气条件下降落了飞机。


现在完成时:
The fly-driver has flown over 10,000 hours. = 这位飞行员已经飞行超过一万小时了。
He has successfully completed many challenging missions. = 他已经成功完成了许多具有挑战性的任务。


过去完成时:

They had completed their pre-flight checks before takeoff. = 他们在起飞前已经完成了飞行前的检查。

虚拟语气:
