Pollux: /ˈpɒlʌks/ | n. |北河二（双子座星）；波吕克斯（希腊神话人物）|

英文解析:Pollux is the name of a bright star in the constellation Gemini, and also a figure in Greek mythology, one of the Dioscuri (twin brothers).  In astronomy, it's a binary star system.  In mythology, he was known for his bravery and loyalty.

例句:

The star Pollux is easily visible in the winter sky. = 北河二在冬季的夜空中很容易看到。

In Greek mythology, Pollux and his brother Castor were inseparable. = 在希腊神话中，波吕克斯和他的兄弟卡斯托尔形影不离。


近义词: (In astronomical context) Castor (another star in Gemini)
