luxation:/lʌkˈseɪʃən/| n.|脱臼；关节脱位|The displacement of a bone from its joint.  A luxation is a complete dislocation of a joint, resulting in the articular surfaces of the bones no longer being in contact.|例句：The athlete suffered a shoulder luxation during the game.  这位运动员在比赛中肩膀脱臼了。

例句：The doctor successfully reduced the hip luxation. 医生成功地复位了髋关节脱位。


近义词：dislocation, displacement|反义词：reduction (of a luxation)

词性：名词
