词源:  The word "internationalities" is derived from "international," which combines "inter-" (between) and "national" (of or relating to a nation).  The suffix "-ities" denotes a plural or a collection of qualities or characteristics.  Therefore, "internationalities" refers to the various aspects and relationships between nations, encompassing their interactions, policies, and overall dynamics.
