noblemen:/ˈnoʊblmən/ | n. |贵族男子，贵绅 |The word "noblemen" refers to men of noble birth or high social rank, possessing titles of nobility.  They often hold significant power and influence within society. |Example: The noblemen gathered in the castle to discuss matters of state.  贵族们聚集在城堡里讨论国家大事。 |近义词：gentlemen, aristocrats, lords |反义词：commoners, peasants
