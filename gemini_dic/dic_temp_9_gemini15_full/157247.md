pinned-up: /pɪnt ʌp/ | adj. |别住的；被别住的；扎起来的；高高竖起的 |  Describes something that is fastened or held in place with pins, or something that is gathered and secured at the top.  It can refer to hair, fabric, or other items. |

例句：

Her hair was pinned-up in an elegant bun.  她的头发被优雅地盘成一个发髻。

The poster was pinned-up on the notice board. 海报被别在布告栏上。


近义词：fastened, secured, tied, gathered

反义词：loose, unfastened, undone

词性：形容词
