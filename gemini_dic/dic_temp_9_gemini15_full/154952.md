/ˈpɛstɪsʌɪd/| n.|杀虫剂|A substance used for destroying insects or other organisms harmful to cultivated plants or to animals.|例句：The farmer used a strong pesticide to kill the aphids on his crops.  农民用强效杀虫剂杀死了作物上的蚜虫。|近义词：insecticide, biocide|反义词:None
