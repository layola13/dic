namby-pambies:/ˈnæmbi ˈpæmbiz/| n.|娘娘腔，软弱的人，优柔寡断的人|A namby-pamby is a person who is considered weak, effeminate, or overly sentimental.  The term often implies a lack of decisiveness or strength of character.|例句：He's such a namby-pamby; he can't even stand up for himself.|近义词：wimp, milksop, softie, weakling|反义词：tough guy, strongman, macho
