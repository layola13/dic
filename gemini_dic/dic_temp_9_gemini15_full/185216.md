retractable: /rɪˈtræktəbl/ | adj. | 可伸缩的，可收回的 | Capable of being drawn back or retracted.  Something retractable can be pulled back or folded away into a smaller space. | 例句：The car's headlights are retractable.  这辆车的车灯是可伸缩的。

The airplane's landing gear is retractable.  飞机的起落架是可收回的。


近义词：retractable, withdrawable, collapsible, foldable


反义词：fixed, permanent, immovable, inflexible


词性：形容词
