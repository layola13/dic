retarget:/ˌriːˈtɑːrɡɪt/|/ˈriːtɑːrɡɛt/| vt.|重新定位目标；再次瞄准；重新定向广告|To aim or direct (something, such as advertising) at a different or new target audience or market.|The company decided to retarget its marketing campaign to a younger demographic. 公司决定将其营销活动重新定位到更年轻的人群。|近义词：redirect, refocus, reorient|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We retarget our ads based on user behavior. = 我们根据用户行为重新定位广告。
They retarget their marketing efforts towards a specific niche. = 他们将营销工作重新定位到一个特定的细分市场。


一般过去时:
The company retargeted its marketing campaign after poor initial results. = 公司在最初结果不佳后重新定位了其营销活动。
We retargeted our social media ads to reach a wider audience. = 我们重新定位了我们的社交媒体广告以覆盖更广泛的受众。


现在完成时:
They have retargeted their efforts several times this year. = 他们今年已经多次重新调整了他们的努力方向。
The marketing team has retargeted its ads to improve conversion rates. = 营销团队已经重新定位了其广告以提高转化率。


过去完成时:
After the product launch failed, they had retargeted their message to focus on a different benefit. = 产品发布失败后，他们已经重新调整了信息重点，关注不同的优势。
The company had retargeted its campaign before realizing the mistake in the initial strategy. = 在意识到最初策略的错误之前，公司已经重新定位了其活动。


将来时:
We will retarget our ads next month to increase brand awareness. = 我们下个月将重新定位我们的广告以提高品牌知名度。
The marketing department will retarget its efforts towards a more affluent customer base. = 市场部将把精力重新投向更富裕的客户群体。


虚拟语气:
If we had more data, we would retarget our campaign more effectively. = 如果我们有更多的数据，我们将更有效地重新定位我们的活动。
It would be beneficial if we retarget our ads to a younger demographic. = 如果我们将广告重新定位到更年轻的人群，将会有益。


被动语态:
The ads were retargeted to a more specific audience. = 广告被重新定位到更具体的受众。
The marketing strategy is being retargeted to improve sales. = 营销策略正在被重新定位以提高销量。


疑问句:
Will they retarget their product to a different market? = 他们会将他们的产品重新定位到不同的市场吗？
How often do you retarget your online advertisements? = 你多久重新定位一次你的在线广告？


否定句:
We did not retarget our campaign despite the poor results. = 尽管结果不佳，我们也没有重新定位我们的活动。
They will not retarget their efforts towards that particular segment. = 他们不会将他们的精力重新投向那个特定的细分市场。


PHRASE->

retargeting audience = 重新定位受众
retargeting pixels = 重新定位像素
dynamic retargeting = 动态重新定位
