consignation:/ˌkɒnsɪɡˈneɪʃən/| /ˌkɑːnˌsaɪɡˈneɪʃən/ | n. |托运；寄售；委托；交付 |The act of consigning something; the state of being consigned.  It often refers to the act of delivering goods to someone else for sale or storage. |例句：The consignment of goods to the warehouse was delayed due to bad weather. 货物因天气恶劣而延迟交付仓库。

例句：The artist made a consignment of his paintings to the gallery.  艺术家将他的画作委托给画廊出售。

近义词：shipment, delivery, entrustment, consignment


反义词：retention, withholding


词性: 名词
