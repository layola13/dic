earthlight:/ˈɜːrθlaɪt/ | n. | 地球光；地照 | The faint light reflected from the earth onto the moon's surface.  It is most visible during a new moon. | 例句：The earthlight faintly illuminated the dark side of the moon. 地球光微弱地照亮了月球的暗面。

The earthlight was barely visible through the clouds.  透过云层，几乎看不到地球光。| 近义词：moonlight (in the context of reflected light) | 反义词：sunlight
