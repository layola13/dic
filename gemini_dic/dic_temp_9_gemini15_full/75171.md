frivolous:/ˈfrɪvələs/| adj.|轻浮的；不重要的；琐碎的 | lacking seriousness; silly; trivial.  Frivolous activities are not important and are often done for fun or amusement. They lack substance or seriousness. |例句：His frivolous remarks annoyed everyone.  他轻浮的言论惹恼了所有人。

例句：She dismissed his concerns as frivolous. 她认为他的担忧是无关紧要的。


近义词：silly, trivial, inconsequential, flippant, trifling, superficial


反义词：serious, important, significant, substantial, weighty


词性：形容词
