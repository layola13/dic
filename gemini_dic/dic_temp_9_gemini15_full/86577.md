helio:/ˈhiːliˌoʊ/| /ˈhiːlioʊ/| pref.|日，太阳|来自希腊语hēlios（太阳）,  表示与太阳相关的含义。用作词头，构成复合词。|例句：Helio-centric theory is the theory that places the sun at the center of the solar system.  日心说是将太阳置于太阳系中心的理论。|近义词：solar|反义词：lunar


USAGE->
This is a prefix, not a standalone word,  therefore it doesn't have verb conjugations or other grammatical usages like a typical noun, verb, or adjective.  It only combines with other words.
