rotated:/roʊˈteɪtɪd/| adj. |旋转的，转动的|Having been turned around an axis.  The image was rotated 90 degrees.|The rotated image was clearer. 旋转后的图像更清晰了。|近义词：spun, turned, revolved |反义词：stationary, fixed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The earth rotates around the sun. = 地球绕着太阳旋转。
The wheel rotates smoothly. = 轮子旋转得很平稳。


一般过去时:
He rotated the tires on his car. = 他换了他的汽车轮胎。
The teacher rotated the students' seating arrangements. = 老师调整了学生的座位安排。


现在完成时:
The company has rotated its management team. = 公司已经轮换了管理团队。
The crops have been rotated successfully this year. = 今年作物轮作成功了。


过去完成时:
The engine had rotated thousands of times before it broke down. = 引擎在损坏之前已经旋转了数千次。
After the storm had rotated out to sea, the weather calmed down. = 暴风雨旋转到海里之后，天气平静下来了。


将来时:
The satellite will rotate on its axis. = 卫星将绕其轴自转。
They will rotate the crops next year. = 他们明年将轮作农作物。


虚拟语气:
If the earth did not rotate, we would not have day and night. = 如果地球不旋转，我们就没有白天和黑夜。
It would be easier if they rotated the tasks among the team members. = 如果他们能够在团队成员之间轮换任务，将会更容易。


被动语态:
The picture was rotated by 90 degrees. = 图片旋转了90度。
The crops are rotated annually to improve soil health. = 为了改善土壤健康，农作物每年轮作。


疑问句:
Has the wheel been rotated yet? = 轮子旋转了吗？
How many times has the Earth rotated since you were born? = 自你出生以来，地球旋转了多少次？


否定句:
The door didn't rotate properly. = 门没有正常旋转。
The engine did not rotate fast enough. = 引擎转速不够快。
