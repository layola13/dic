colature:/ˈkɒlətʃər/| n. |花腔；装饰音；(音乐) 音群装饰|A colature is a rapid succession of notes, usually embellishing a single tone, often found in vocal music.  It's a type of ornamentation used to add expressive detail and virtuosity.  Colatures are typically short melodic flourishes that are not harmonically independent. |Example: The soprano's performance was dazzling, showcasing impressive control and agility in the many colatures.  女高音的表演令人眼花缭乱，展示了她精湛的技巧和在众多花腔中的灵活性。|近义词：ornamentation, embellishment, flourish |反义词：plainness, simplicity |名词
