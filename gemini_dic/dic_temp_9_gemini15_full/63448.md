entrenched:/ɪnˈtrentʃt/ | adj. | 根深蒂固的；牢固确立的 | firmly established and difficult or unlikely to change | His views are deeply entrenched. 他的观点根深蒂固。 |  firmly established, ingrained |  superficial, changeable | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
His beliefs are entrenched. = 他的信仰根深蒂固。
The system is entrenched in society. = 这套制度在社会中根深蒂固。

一般过去时:
Their prejudices were deeply entrenched. = 他们的偏见根深蒂固。
The tradition was entrenched for centuries. = 这个传统已经沿袭了几个世纪。


现在完成时:
The habits have become entrenched over time. = 这些习惯随着时间的推移已经根深蒂固。
The inequality has been entrenched for too long. = 不平等现象已经持续太久了。


过去完成时:
The problem had been entrenched long before the investigation started. = 在调查开始之前，这个问题就已经根深蒂固了。
The conflict had been entrenched for generations. = 这场冲突已经持续了几代人了。


将来时:
These policies will become entrenched if we don't act now. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，这些政策将会根深蒂固。
The current economic system will likely remain entrenched for years to come. = 当前的经济体系很可能在未来几年内依然根深蒂固。


虚拟语气:
If the system were not so entrenched, reform would be easier. = 如果这个制度不是如此根深蒂固，改革将会更容易。
I wish these outdated laws weren't so entrenched. = 我希望这些过时的法律不会如此根深蒂固。


被动语态:
The idea was entrenched in the minds of the public. = 这种观念已经深入人心。
The practice is entrenched in our culture. = 这种做法已经根深蒂固于我们的文化之中。


疑问句:
Are these beliefs deeply entrenched? = 这些信仰是否根深蒂固？
Is the power structure entrenched? = 权力结构是否根深蒂固？


否定句:
The old ways are not entrenched here. = 在这里，旧习俗并没有根深蒂固。
His ideas are not easily entrenched. = 他的想法并不容易根深蒂固。
