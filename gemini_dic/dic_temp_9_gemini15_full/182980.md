replayed:/ˌriːˈpleɪd/| v. |重播；重演；再比赛|To play again; to act out again; to play a game or match again.  |例句：The coach replayed the game footage to analyze the mistakes. 教练回放了比赛录像来分析错误。

例句：The actors replayed the scene several times before they were satisfied. 演员们在满意之前反复排练了这个场景好几次。

近义词：reacted, repeated, reproduced |反义词：stopped, ended

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He replays the song every morning. 他每天早上都会重播这首歌。
They replay the game highlights on TV. 他们在电视上重播比赛精彩片段。


一般过去时:
She replayed the last level of the game. 她重玩了游戏的最后一关。
We replayed the scene to understand better. 我们重演了那个场景以便更好地理解。


现在完成时:
I have replayed that movie three times. 我已经重播那部电影三次了。

过去完成时:
