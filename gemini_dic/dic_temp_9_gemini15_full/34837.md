This is in contrast to a freestone fruit, where the flesh separates easily from the pit.|例句：Peaches can be clingstone or freestone varieties.  桃子可以是粘核品种或离核品种。|  例句：This clingstone peach is so juicy! 这个粘核桃又甜又多汁！|近义词：stone fruit (for the general category)|反义词：freestone|名词
