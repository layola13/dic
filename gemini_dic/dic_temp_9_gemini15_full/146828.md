overimpressionability: /ˌoʊvərɪmprɛʃənəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 过度敏感；易受影响性 |  The state or quality of being excessively susceptible to outside influences or impressions; extreme sensitivity.  This word is a relatively rare and less common term. It combines "over-" (excessive) with "impressionability" (the quality of being easily influenced).  It suggests a vulnerability to external stimuli that goes beyond normal receptiveness, leading to potentially overwhelming emotional or psychological responses.


例句：

His overimpressionability made him easily swayed by others' opinions.  他的过度敏感使他很容易被别人的意见左右。


Her overimpressionability led to frequent emotional outbursts. 她的过度敏感导致频繁的情绪爆发。



近义词：hypersensitivity, extreme sensitivity, suggestibility


反义词：insensitivity, impassivity, resilience


词性：名词
