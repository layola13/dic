melees:/ˈmeliːz/| n. |混战，扭打，冲突 |A melee is a confused fight or struggle at close quarters.  It often involves many people and is characterized by its chaotic and disorganized nature. |例句：The police struggled to control the melee that erupted after the football match.  警察努力控制足球比赛后爆发的混战。

例句：A terrifying melee broke out in the crowded marketplace.  拥挤的市场上爆发了一场可怕的混战。


近义词：brawl, scuffle, fracas, scrimmage, affray |反义词：peace, order, calm |词性:名词
