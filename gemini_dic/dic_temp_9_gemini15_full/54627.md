dismissal:/dɪsˈmɪsəl/ | n. |解雇；解散；驳回；不予理会 | The act of dismissing someone from a job or position; the act of rejecting or ignoring something.  | Example:  His dismissal from the company was unexpected. 他被公司解雇是意料之外的。

The manager's dismissal of the employee's concerns was unprofessional.  经理对员工担忧的漠视是不专业的。|近义词：discharge, termination, rejection, dismissal from office|反义词：appointment, hiring, acceptance, consideration|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company dismisses underperforming employees. 公司解雇表现不佳的员工。
He dismisses her concerns as trivial. 他认为她的担忧微不足道而将其忽略。


一般过去时:
They dismissed the case due to lack of evidence. 由于缺乏证据，他们驳回了此案。
The teacher dismissed the class early. 老师提前放学了。


现在完成时:
The court has dismissed the charges against him. 法院已经驳回对他提出的指控。
She has been dismissed from her position three times. 她已经被解雇三次了。


过去完成时:
They had dismissed the idea before the meeting even started. 会议还没开始他们就已经否决了那个想法。
The judge had dismissed the previous lawsuits.  法官已经驳回了之前的诉讼。


将来时:
The board will dismiss the CEO if the profits continue to fall. 如果利润继续下降，董事会将解雇首席执行官。
He will likely be dismissed from his team if he continues this behavior. 如果他继续这种行为，他很可能会被逐出队伍。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence were stronger, the judge would not have dismissed the case. 如果证据更充分，法官就不会驳回此案。
I wish he hadn't dismissed my concerns so readily. 我希望他没有如此轻易地驳回我的担忧。


被动语态:
He was dismissed for misconduct. 他因行为不端而被解雇。
The case was dismissed due to insufficient evidence.  由于证据不足，此案被驳回。


疑问句:
Will you be dismissed if you fail the exam? 如果你考试不及格会被解雇吗？
Was the appeal dismissed by the court? 上诉被法院驳回了吗？


否定句:
The court did not dismiss the case. 法院没有驳回此案。
They would not dismiss the possibility of a merger. 他们不会排除合并的可能性。


PHRASE->
summary dismissal: 即时解雇
dismiss an appeal:驳回上诉
dismiss a claim:驳回索赔
dismiss from mind: 从脑海中摒除
dismiss with contempt: 轻蔑地拒绝
