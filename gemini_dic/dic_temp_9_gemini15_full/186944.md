ripper:/ˈrɪpər/| n., adj.| 撕裂者；裂缝；（尤指）残忍的凶手；极好的 | A person or thing that rips;  (especially) a brutal murderer; something excellent |例句：The old building had a huge ripper in the wall. 那栋旧建筑的墙上有个大裂缝。|近义词：tearer, murderer, killer, fiend (for murderer sense); awesome, fantastic (for excellent sense)|反义词：mender, healer, repairer (for opposite of ripping); terrible, awful (for opposite of excellent sense)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rips the paper. = 他撕碎了纸。
The machine rips the fabric. = 机器撕裂了织物。


一般过去时:
She ripped her dress. = 她撕破了她的裙子。
The strong wind ripped the roof off. = 强风掀掉了屋顶。


现在完成时:
The dog has ripped up my shoes. = 狗撕烂了我的鞋子。
He has ripped several pages from the book. = 他从书中撕掉了好几页。


过去完成时:
They had ripped the poster down before I arrived. = 我到之前他们已经撕下了海报。
The thief had ripped open the safe. = 小偷已经撬开了保险箱。


将来时:
I will rip this old shirt. = 我将会撕掉这件旧衬衫。
The storm will rip the trees from the ground. = 风暴会把树连根拔起。


虚拟语气:
If I ripped that painting, I would be in trouble. = 如果我撕坏那幅画，我会惹上麻烦。
I wish I hadn't ripped my favorite jeans. = 我真希望我没有撕坏我最喜欢的牛仔裤。


被动语态:
The fabric was ripped during the washing. = 织物在清洗过程中被撕破了。
The poster was ripped down by the wind. = 海报被风刮掉了。


疑问句:
Did the dog rip your clothes? = 狗撕烂了你的衣服吗？
Has the wind ripped the tent? = 风把帐篷撕破了吗？


否定句:
He didn't rip the envelope open. = 他没有撕开信封。
She hasn't ripped the pages from the notebook. = 她没有从笔记本上撕下那些页。
