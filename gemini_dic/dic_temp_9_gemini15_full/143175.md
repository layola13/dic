outclimbed:/ˌaʊtˈklaɪmd/| v.|超越，攀登超过|To surpass in climbing; to climb higher than.|例句：The experienced climber outclimbed all the others and reached the summit first.  经验丰富的登山者超越了其他人，第一个到达了山顶。

例句：The ambitious young entrepreneur outclimbed his rivals in the business world.  雄心勃勃的年轻企业家在商界超越了他的竞争对手。


近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip|反义词：be outclimbed, be surpassed
