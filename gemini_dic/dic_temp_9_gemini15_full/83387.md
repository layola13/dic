h-bomb:/ˈeɪtʃbɒm/ | n. | 氢弹 | A hydrogen bomb, also known as a thermonuclear bomb, is a very powerful bomb that uses nuclear fusion to release a huge amount of energy.  It is significantly more powerful than an atomic bomb (A-bomb). | The detonation of the first h-bomb was a watershed moment in the Cold War.  氢弹的爆炸是冷战中的一个转折点。 The threat of h-bomb war loomed large over the world for decades.  几十年来，氢弹战争的威胁笼罩着世界。 | atomic bomb, thermonuclear weapon |  (none readily available as a direct antonym;  "conventional weapon" is a partial antonym, indicating a non-nuclear weapon) | noun
