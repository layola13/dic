outsmelled:/ˌaʊtˈsmɛld/ | vt. | 胜过…的嗅觉 | to surpass in the sense of smell; to have a better sense of smell than.  This is a relatively rare word, formed by adding the prefix "out-" to the verb "smell".  It implies a superior ability to detect or discern odors. | 例句：The bloodhound outsmelled the other dogs, tracking the criminal with ease.  猎犬的嗅觉胜过其他狗，轻松地追踪到了罪犯。

The trained scent dog outsmelled the amateur, finding the hidden contraband. 受过训练的警犬嗅觉胜过业余人士，找到了隐藏的违禁品。| 近义词：outdo (in smelling), surpass (in olfactory ability) | 反义词：be outsmelled by
