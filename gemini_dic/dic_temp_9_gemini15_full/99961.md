interoperative:/ˌɪntərˈɑːpərətɪv/| adj.|手术间的；手术中的；手术期间的|Relating to or occurring during a surgical operation.  The term describes something that happens within the context of a surgical procedure, from the initial incision to the final closure.|例句：Interoperative complications can arise unexpectedly during surgery. 手术中可能会出现意外并发症。

例句：The surgeon meticulously documented all interoperative findings. 外科医生一丝不苟地记录了所有手术中的发现。


近义词：surgical, intraoperative


反义词：postoperative, preoperative


词性：形容词
