paneer:/pəˈniːr/| n. |印度奶酪|A fresh cheese made from cow's or buffalo's milk, commonly used in Indian cuisine. It is not aged and has a mild, slightly sweet flavor.  |  Example:  The paneer tikka was delicious.  印度奶酪串很美味。|  近义词：Indian cheese, chenna (similar but not identical)| 反义词：None (no direct antonym)| 名词
