plunks:/plʌŋks/ | v. | 咚地一声放下；掷下；投入 | To drop or put down something with a dull thud.  |例句：He plunked the heavy bag onto the floor. 他咚地一声把沉重的袋子扔到地板上。|例句：She plunked a coin into the vending machine. 她把一枚硬币投入自动售货机。|近义词：drop, thump, plop |反义词：lift, raise

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plunks the guitar strings. = 他拨动吉他弦。
She plunks down her purse. = 她把钱包重重地放下。

一般过去时:
He plunked the book on the table. = 他把书砰地一声放在桌子上。
She plunked the money on the counter. = 她把钱重重地拍在柜台上。

现在完成时:
He has plunked all his savings into the stock market. = 他已将他所有的积蓄都投入股市。
She has plunked herself down on the sofa. = 她已重重地坐在沙发上。

过去完成时:
He had plunked the letter into the mailbox before he left. = 他离开前已把信投入邮箱。
She had plunked her head down on the desk in exhaustion. = 她精疲力竭地把头重重地趴在桌子上。

将来时:
He will plunk the suitcase down on the floor. = 他会把行李箱重重地放在地板上。
She will plunk the keys on the table. = 她会把钥匙放在桌子上。

虚拟语气:
If he plunked the money down now, he could buy the car. = 如果他现在把钱付清，他就能买到那辆车。
I wish I had plunked for the bigger house. = 我真希望当初我买了那栋更大的房子。

被动语态:
The money was plunked onto the table. = 钱被重重地放在桌子上。
The heavy box was plunked down by the worker. = 那个沉重的箱子被工人重重地放了下来。

疑问句:
Did he plunk the glass on the floor? = 他是不是把玻璃杯扔到地板上了？
Where did she plunk the package? = 她把包裹扔到哪里了？

否定句:
He didn't plunk the coins into the jar.  = 他没有把硬币投入罐子里。
She didn't plunk the bag down heavily. = 她没有重重地放下包。


PHRASE->
plunk down (money): 付钱
plunk oneself down: 猛地坐下
plunk into: 投入(时间、精力等)
plunk something down: 咚地放下某物
plunk away at:  持续努力做某事
