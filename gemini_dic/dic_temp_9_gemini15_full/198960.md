sight-reader:/ˈsaɪtˌriːdər/ | n. |视奏者；乐谱阅读者 | A person who can read and play music at sight, without prior practice.  |Example:  He's a skilled sight-reader, able to play complex pieces after only glancing at the score. 他是一位熟练的视奏者，能够只看一眼乐谱就能演奏复杂的乐曲。|近义词：musician, performer |反义词： N/A

She sight-reads beautifully. = 她视奏得非常优美。
