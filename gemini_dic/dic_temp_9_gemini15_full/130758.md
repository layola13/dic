nonamphibian:/ˌnɒnˈæmfɪbiən/ | adj. |非两栖类的；非两栖动物的 | Not belonging to the class Amphibia; not amphibious.  |Example:  Snakes are nonamphibian reptiles.  蛇是非两栖爬行动物。


The word "nonamphibian" is a compound word formed from the prefix "non-" and the noun "amphibian".

* **non-:**  A prefix meaning "not," "opposite of," or "lacking."

* **amphibian:** From  Latin *amphibia*, from *amphi-*, combining form of *amphi* "both, on both sides" + *bios* "life".  Referring to animals that can live both on land and in water.


USAGE->
This word is primarily used as an adjective, therefore verb conjugations are not applicable.  The usage focuses on describing animals or characteristics.

Example sentences demonstrating usage:

Adjective:
The zoo houses a variety of nonamphibian reptiles. 动物园饲养着各种各样的非两栖爬行动物。
That creature is nonamphibian; it's a mammal. 那生物不是两栖动物；它是哺乳动物。


近义词: terrestrial (if referring to land-dwelling), nonaquatic


反义词: amphibian


词性: adjective
