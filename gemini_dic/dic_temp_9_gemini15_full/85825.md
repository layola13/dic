hazily:/ˈheɪzɪli/ | adv. | 模糊地；朦胧地；含糊不清地 | In a hazy manner; unclearly or vaguely.  It describes something that is not clearly seen, understood, or remembered. |例句：I remember the event hazily.  = 我模模糊糊地记得这件事。  He spoke hazily about his plans. = 他含糊不清地谈论他的计划。 |近义词：vaguely, indistinctly, dimly, unclearly |反义词：clearly, distinctly, sharply, precisely
