siphoniata:/ˌsaɪfəˈneɪtə/| n. |虹吸管类动物；虹吸类动物|A group of extinct cephalopod mollusks characterized by siphuncles that are located in the ventral part of the shell.  They are known from the fossil record and are related to ammonoids and nautiloids.|The siphoniata flourished during the Paleozoic Era. 虹吸管类动物在古生代繁盛。|近义词：cephalopod (broad term)|反义词：N/A
