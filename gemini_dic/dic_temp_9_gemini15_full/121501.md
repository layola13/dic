mislearned:/ˌmɪsˈlɜːrnd/| adj.|错误学习的，误学的|learned incorrectly or imperfectly; wrongly understood or remembered.|例句：He mislearned the rules of grammar and made many mistakes in his writing. 他语法规则学错了，写作中犯了很多错误。|近义词：misunderstood, misinterpreted, wrongly learned|反义词：correctly learned, well-understood


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mislearns the vocabulary every time. 他每次都把词汇记错。
They mislearn the concept of the lesson. 他们误解了这节课的概念。

一般过去时:
I mislearned the pronunciation of that word. 我把那个词的发音记错了。
She mislearned the historical facts. 她把历史事实记错了。

现在完成时:
He has mislearned a lot of things in his life. 他一生中有很多东西都学错了。
They have mislearned the rules of the game. 他们已经把游戏规则搞错了。

过去完成时:
I had mislearned the song lyrics before the performance. 我在演出前就把歌词记错了。
We had mislearned the directions, so we got lost. 我们方向搞错了，所以迷路了。

将来时:
She will mislearn the new grammar rules if she doesn't pay attention. 如果她不注意，她将会把新的语法规则学错。
They will mislearn the instructions if they rush through them. 如果他们匆匆忙忙地完成，他们会误解说明。

虚拟语气:
If I had not mislearned the formula, I would have solved the problem. 如果我没有把公式记错，我就能解出这道题了。
I wish I hadn't mislearned the song. 我真希望我没有把歌记错。

被动语态:
The rules were mislearned by many students. 许多学生都把规则学错了。
The historical events were mislearned by the children. 孩子们把历史事件学错了。

疑问句:
Did he mislearn the instructions? 他是不是把说明搞错了？
Have they mislearned the concept? 他们是不是误解了这个概念？

否定句:
He did not mislearn the information. 他没有把信息记错。
She did not mislearn the steps of the process. 她没有把这个过程的步骤搞错。
