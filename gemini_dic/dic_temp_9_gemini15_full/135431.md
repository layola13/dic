nonpersistencies:/ˌnɒn.pəˈsɪs.tən.siz/ | n. |非持久性，短暂性 |The lack of permanence or persistence; instances of something not lasting.  This term often refers to data or processes that do not survive system restarts or power failures. |例句：The application's nonpersistencies led to data loss after each reboot.  应用程式的非持久性导致每次重新启动后数据丢失。The project suffered from several nonpersistencies in its initial design.  该项目的初始设计存在若干非持久性问题。 |近义词：transience, ephemerality, impermanence |反义词：permanence, persistence, durability | 名词
