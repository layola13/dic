nonunderstandable:/ˌnɒnˌʌndərˈstændəbl/ | adj. | 无法理解的，令人费解的 |  not understandable; incomprehensible.  This refers to something that cannot be grasped or comprehended by the mind. It emphasizes the inability to understand due to complexity, obscurity, or lack of clarity. | Example: The professor's lecture was nonunderstandable to most of the students.  教授的讲座对大多数学生来说都难以理解。 | 近义词：incomprehensible, unintelligible, obscure, perplexing | 反义词：understandable, clear, comprehensible | 词性：形容词
