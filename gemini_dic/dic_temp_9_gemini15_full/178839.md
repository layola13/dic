recycle:/ˌriːˈsaɪkl/| vt. n.|回收利用；再循环|To convert waste materials into reusable objects.|例句：We should recycle paper, plastic, and glass. 我们应该回收纸张、塑料和玻璃。 We need to recycle more to protect the environment.我们需要更多地回收利用以保护环境。|近义词：reuse, reclaim, regenerate|反义词：discard, waste, throw away|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They recycle newspapers every week. = 他们每周都回收报纸。
We recycle plastic bottles at the recycling center. = 我们在回收中心回收塑料瓶。

一般过去时:
She recycled all her old clothes. = 她回收了她所有的旧衣服。
He recycled the cardboard boxes yesterday. = 他昨天回收了纸板箱。

现在完成时:
Have you recycled your aluminum cans yet? = 你已经回收你的铝罐了吗？
They have recycled a significant amount of waste this year. = 他们今年已经回收了大量的废物。

过去完成时:
She had recycled all the plastic before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，她已经回收了所有塑料。
They had recycled thousands of bottles by the end of the month. = 到月底，他们已经回收了数千个瓶子。

将来时:
We will recycle more in the future. = 我们将来会回收更多。
They will recycle the glass bottles tomorrow. = 他们明天将回收玻璃瓶。

虚拟语气:
If we recycled more, we would reduce landfill waste. = 如果我们回收更多，我们将减少垃圾填埋场的垃圾。
I wish we recycled more efficiently. = 我希望我们回收效率更高。

被动语态:
The plastic bottles were recycled at the factory. = 塑料瓶在工厂被回收。
The newspapers are recycled weekly. = 报纸每周都被回收。

疑问句:
Do you recycle your trash? = 你回收你的垃圾吗？
Does your company have a recycling program? = 你的公司有回收计划吗？

否定句:
He doesn't recycle his plastic bags. = 他不回收他的塑料袋。
They didn't recycle the glass jars. = 他们没有回收玻璃罐子。
