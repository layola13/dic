denude:/dɪˈnuːd/| vt.|剥去…的覆盖物；使赤裸；使荒芜|to remove the covering of something; to make bare or empty; to strip|The strong winds denuded the trees of their leaves.  强风吹落了树叶。|The prolonged drought denuded the land of its vegetation.  长时间的干旱使土地失去了植被。|strip, bare, divest|clothe, cover, clothe, adorn|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wind denudes the trees of their leaves every autumn. = 每到秋天，风都会吹落树叶。
He denudes the land of its natural resources for profit. = 他为了利益剥夺土地的自然资源。


一般过去时:
The storm denuded the coastline of its sand dunes. = 暴风雨剥夺了海岸线的沙丘。
The company denuded the forest of its ancient trees. = 公司砍伐了森林里古老的树木。


现在完成时:
Years of overgrazing have denuded the pasture. = 多年过度放牧使牧场荒芜了。
The relentless sun has denuded the mountain of its snow. = 无情的阳光使山上的积雪融化了。


过去完成时:
Before the flood, the river had denuded the banks of their vegetation. = 洪水泛滥之前，河流已经冲刷掉了河岸上的植被。
The miners had denuded the mountainside of its ore. = 矿工们已经剥夺了山坡上的矿石。


将来时:
The next storm will denude the beach of its sand. = 下一场暴风雨将把沙滩上的沙子冲走。
Continued deforestation will denude the region of its biodiversity. = 持续的森林砍伐将使该地区丧失生物多样性。


虚拟语气:
If they continued this practice, they would denude the forest completely. = 如果他们继续这种做法，他们将彻底毁掉这片森林。
I wish they hadn't denuded the hillside of its trees. = 我真希望他们没有砍掉山坡上的树木。


被动语态:
The forest was denuded by illegal logging. = 森林因非法采伐而被破坏。
The land was denuded by years of drought. = 这片土地因多年的干旱而荒芜。


疑问句:
Has the drought denuded the fields of their crops? = 干旱是否使田地里的庄稼枯萎了？
Will the construction project denude the area of its natural beauty? = 建设项目是否会破坏该地区的自然美景？


否定句:
The government is trying to prevent the forest from being denuded. = 政府正在努力防止森林被破坏。
They didn't denude the land of its topsoil. = 他们没有剥夺土地表层的土壤。
