humbug:/ˈhʌmbʌɡ/| n., v.|胡说八道；骗局；欺骗；反对|Nonsense; deception; to oppose or ridicule something; to deceive.|例句：His story was pure humbug. 他的故事纯属胡说八道。 He humbugged the old woman out of her money. 他骗取了老妇人的钱财。|近义词：nonsense, hoax, swindle, fraud|反义词：truth, reality, honesty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He humbugs his friends often. = 他经常欺骗他的朋友。
She humbugs about her work. = 她对她的工作夸夸其谈。

一般过去时:
They humbugged the investors. = 他们欺骗了投资者。
He humbugged me into buying that car. = 他骗我买了那辆车。

现在完成时:
She has humbugged me several times. = 她已经骗过我几次了。
They have humbugged the public for years. = 他们多年来一直在欺骗公众。

过去完成时:
He had humbugged them before, so they were wary. = 他以前骗过他们，所以他们很警惕。
They had humbugged the authorities for a long time. = 他们欺骗当局很长时间了。

将来时:

She will humbug us about her achievements. = 她会对她的成就夸夸其谈。

虚拟语气:

I wish he hadn't humbugged us. = 我希望他没有欺骗我们。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

She wouldn't humbug her own family. = 她不会欺骗她自己的家人。
