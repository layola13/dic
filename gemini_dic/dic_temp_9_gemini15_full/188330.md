rubbishes:/ˈrʌbɪʃɪz/| v. |乱扔垃圾；贬低；否认； |To treat something as worthless or insignificant; to dismiss something as nonsense.  |He rubbishes all my ideas. 他贬低我所有的想法。|近义词：disparages, dismisses, belittles |反义词：values, respects, praises |动词


USAGE->

He rubbishes my efforts every time. = 他每次都贬低我的努力。
They rubbish the opposition's arguments. = 他们贬低反对派的论点。


一般过去时:
She rubbished his claims about the incident. = 她驳斥了他关于这件事的说法。
He rubbished the evidence presented in court. = 他驳斥了法庭上提出的证据。


现在完成时:
They have rubbished all attempts at compromise. = 他们已经否定了所有妥协的尝试。
She has rubbished his theory repeatedly. = 她一再地驳斥了他的理论。


过去完成时:

词源:
The word "rubbish" ultimately derives from the Old French word "robusshe," meaning "refuse" or "trash."  The verb form "rubbishes" is a relatively modern development, reflecting the increasing use of the noun as a verb (a process called "verbification").
