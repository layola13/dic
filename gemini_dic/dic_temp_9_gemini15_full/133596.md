nonflirtatiously:/ˌnɒnˌflɜːrteɪʃəsli/ | adj. |非调情地，不轻浮地 | Without flirting or attempting to flirt.  It describes an action or behavior that lacks any romantic or suggestive intent. | Example: He complimented her work nonflirtatiously, simply stating that it was well done. 他不带任何调情意味地称赞了她的作品，只是说它做得很好。 |近义词：platonically, sincerely, genuinely |反义词：flirtatiously, coquettishly
