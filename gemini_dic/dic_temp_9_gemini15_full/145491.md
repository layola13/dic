overcloak: /ˌoʊvərˈkloʊk/ | vt. |  覆盖；遮盖；掩盖 | To cover or conceal something completely.  |例句：The heavy fog overcloaked the entire valley.  浓雾笼罩着整个山谷。

例句：They tried to overcloak their true intentions. 他们试图掩盖他们的真实意图。


近义词：cover, conceal, shroud, mask, obscure | 反义词：reveal, uncover, expose | 词性:动词
