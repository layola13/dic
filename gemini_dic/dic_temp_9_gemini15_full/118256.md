It involves all aspects of getting a product from the manufacturer to the consumer, including marketing, pricing, and display. |例句：Effective merchandising is key to boosting sales.  有效的商品销售是提高销售额的关键。

例句：The company invested heavily in merchandising its new product line. 这家公司大力投资其新产品线的销售。
