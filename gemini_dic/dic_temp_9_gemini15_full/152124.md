pathology:/pəˈθɒlədʒi/ | n. | 病理学；病理状态 | The scientific study of disease; the nature of a disease, especially as manifested by signs and symptoms.  | Example: The pathology report confirmed the presence of cancerous cells.  病理报告证实了癌细胞的存在。  The pathology of the situation was clear: years of neglect.  情况的病理很明显：多年的忽视。 |  近义词：morbidity, disease, ailment | 反义词：health, wellness
