refocusses:/ˌriːˈfoʊkəsɪz/ | vt. |重新聚焦；重新集中注意力 | To direct one's attention or efforts again to something.  |例句：He refocussed his efforts on completing the project. 他重新集中精力完成项目。 |例句：After the distraction, she refocussed on her studies. 分心之后，她重新集中精力学习。|近义词：reorient, concentrate, center|反义词：distract, diffuse|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refocusses his energy on his work every morning. 他每天早上都会重新集中精力工作。
She refocusses her attention after a break. 休息后，她重新集中注意力。


一般过去时:
The team refocussed its strategy after the setback.  团队在遭遇挫折后重新调整了策略。
He refocussed his camera on the bird. 他把相机重新对准了那只鸟。


现在完成时:
She has refocussed her life priorities since having children. 自从有了孩子，她已经重新调整了生活优先级。
They have refocussed their marketing efforts on a younger demographic. 他们已经将营销重点重新放在了年轻一代身上。


过去完成时:
He realised he had refocussed his efforts too late. 他意识到他已经太晚才重新集中精力了。
They wished they had refocussed their resources earlier. 他们希望他们早点重新分配资源。


将来时:
The company will refocus its business model next year. 公司明年将重新调整其商业模式。
She will refocus her attention on her health after the holiday. 假期结束后，她将把注意力重新集中在健康上。


虚拟语气:
If he refocussed his efforts, he would succeed. 如果他重新集中精力，他就会成功。
I wish I had refocussed sooner. 我希望我早点重新集中精力。


被动语态:
The project's goals were refocussed after the initial failure.  项目目标在最初失败后重新确定了。
The marketing campaign was refocussed to target a different audience. 营销活动重新调整目标受众。


疑问句:
Has he refocussed his attention on the problem? 他是否重新将注意力放在了这个问题上？
Will they refocus their resources on research and development? 他们会将资源重新集中在研发上吗？


否定句:
He didn't refocus his energy, and the project failed. 他没有重新集中精力，项目失败了。
The company hasn't refocussed its strategy, and the results are disappointing. 公司没有重新调整战略，结果令人失望。
