handwrite:/ˈhændˌraɪt/ | vt. |手写，亲笔写|To write something by hand, rather than typing or printing it.|She handwrote a thank-you note to her grandmother. 她亲笔给祖母写了一封感谢信。|write, pen|type, print|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I handwrite my shopping lists every week. = 我每周都手写我的购物清单。
He handwrites all his letters. = 他所有的信都亲笔书写。


一般过去时:
She handwrote the poem for her friend's birthday. = 她为朋友的生日亲笔写了一首诗。
They handwrote the invitations to the wedding. = 他们亲笔写了婚礼请柬。


现在完成时:
I have handwrote all my assignments this semester. = 这学期我所有的作业都是手写的。
She has handwrote several letters to her pen pal. = 她已经给她笔友手写了好几封信。


过去完成时:
He had handwrote the essay before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经手写了论文。
They had handwrote the script before the rehearsal. = 他们在排练前已经手写了剧本。


将来时:
I will handwrite the address on the envelope. = 我将用手在信封上写地址。
She will handwrite a personalized message in each card. = 她将在每张卡片上亲笔写一条个性化信息。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would handwrite all my letters. = 如果我有更多时间，我会亲笔写所有信件。
I wish I could handwrite more beautifully. = 我希望我能写得更漂亮。


被动语态:
The invitations were handwrote by the bride herself. = 请柬是由新娘本人亲笔书写的。
The address was handwrote neatly on the package. = 地址工整地写在包裹上。


疑问句:
Did you handwrite that letter? = 你是手写的这封信吗？
Will she handwrite the thank-you notes? = 她会亲笔写感谢信吗？


否定句:
I did not handwrite the report; I typed it. = 我没有手写报告；我是打字的。
He will not handwrite the whole book; it's too long. = 他不会手写整本书；太长了。


PHRASE->
