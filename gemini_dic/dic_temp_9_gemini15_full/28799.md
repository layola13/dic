carols:/ˈkærəlz/| n.|圣诞颂歌；赞美诗|Religious songs or hymns, especially those sung at Christmas.|例句：We sang carols around the Christmas tree. 我们在圣诞树旁唱颂歌。|例句：The choir performed beautiful carols during the service. 合唱团在仪式上演唱了优美的赞美诗。|近义词：hymns, songs, chants|反义词：None
