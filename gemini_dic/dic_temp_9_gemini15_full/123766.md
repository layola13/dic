moorland:/ˈmʊrlænd/| n.|荒野，荒地|Open, uncultivated land, typically high-lying, covered with heath, and often boggy.  It is characterized by its rough, uneven terrain and often sparse vegetation. |The sheep grazed peacefully on the vast moorland.  绵羊在广阔的荒野上平静地吃草。 The hikers got lost in the dense moorland.  徒步旅行者迷失在茂密的荒野中。|heathland, wasteland, upland|cultivated land, farmland|名词
