preredeemed:/ˌpriːrɪˈdiːmd/| adj.| 预先赎回的；预先偿还的 | Already redeemed beforehand; previously purchased or paid for.  This term often applies to financial instruments or rewards programs where something is redeemed before its official redemption date or time.| 例句：The preredeemed gift card was unusable because it had expired. = 预先兑换的礼品卡因过期而无法使用。

The preredeemed bonds yielded a higher return than expected. = 预先赎回的债券收益高于预期。


近义词：prepaid, pre-purchased, advanced


反义词：unredemed, outstanding


词性: adjective
