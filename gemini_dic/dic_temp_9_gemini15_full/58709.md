dumpy:/ˈdʌmpi/| adj.|矮胖的；简陋的；破旧的 |  Describing someone or something as dumpy suggests they are short and stout, or that a place or thing is unattractive and poorly maintained.  It carries a slightly negative connotation. |例句：The dumpy little house needed a fresh coat of paint.  那座矮胖的小房子需要重新粉刷一下。

例句：She was a dumpy woman with kind eyes. 她是一个矮胖的女人，眼神很慈祥。

近义词：short and stout, squat,  dowdy, shabby, dilapidated

反义词：tall and slender, elegant, stylish, well-maintained
