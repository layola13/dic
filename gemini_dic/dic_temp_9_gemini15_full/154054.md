pergolas:/pərˈɡoʊləz/| n.|凉棚，葡萄架|A pergola is an outdoor structure, typically a framework of posts and beams, often covered with climbing plants, providing shade and shelter.|例句：The pergola was covered in fragrant jasmine.  这凉棚上爬满了芬芳的茉莉花。|例句：We sat under the pergola, enjoying the cool shade. 我们坐在凉棚下，享受着凉爽的阴凉。|近义词：arbor, trellis, bower|反义词：无
