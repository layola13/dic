proselytizer:/ˈprɒsɪlaɪzər/|/ˈprɑːsəlaɪzər/ | n. |传教者；积极劝诱者 | A proselytizer is a person who attempts to convert others to their own religious or political beliefs.  They actively seek to persuade others to join their cause. |例句：The zealous proselytizer traveled the country, spreading his message of faith.  那位热心的传教者走遍全国，传播他的信仰信息。

The persistent proselytizer wouldn't take no for an answer.  那个执着的劝诱者不接受拒绝的回答。|近义词：missionary, convert, evangelist, recruiter |反义词：apostate, heretic (in a religious context)
