litigator:/ˈlɪtɪɡeɪtər/| n. |诉讼律师，从事诉讼的人|A litigator is a lawyer who represents clients in court.  They specialize in arguing cases and presenting evidence.  They often handle complex legal matters and are skilled in negotiation and advocacy.|Example:  She's a highly successful litigator known for her aggressive courtroom style. 她是一位非常成功的诉讼律师，以其积极的庭审风格而闻名。|近义词：lawyer, attorney, advocate|反义词：mediator (调解员)
