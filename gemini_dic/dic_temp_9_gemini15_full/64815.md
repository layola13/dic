/ɛsp/|abbr.|尤其，特别，特指|especially;  specifically; in particular|例句：I like all kinds of music, esp. classical music. 我喜欢各种各样的音乐，尤其喜欢古典音乐。|近义词：especially, particularly, specifically|反义词：generally, commonly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He espouses this view. 他特别支持这个观点。
She espouses a vegetarian lifestyle. 她特别推崇素食生活方式。

一般过去时:
They espoused the cause of freedom. 他们特别拥护自由事业。
He espoused her wholeheartedly. 他全心全意地爱着她。


现在完成时:
She has espoused this belief for many years. 她多年来一直特别信奉这种信念。
They have espoused a new strategy. 他们已经特别采用了一种新策略。

过去完成时:
They had espoused that political party before the election. 他们在选举前就特别支持那个政党。
She had espoused a healthier diet after her doctor's advice. 在听取医生的建议后，她已经特别开始注重健康饮食。


将来时:
He will espouse this idea in his next speech. 他将在下一次演讲中特别阐述这个想法。
They will espouse the use of renewable energy. 他们将特别倡导使用可再生能源。

虚拟语气:
If he espoused that cause, he would gain many supporters. 如果他特别支持那件事业，他会赢得许多支持者。
I wish she had espoused a more balanced approach. 我希望她当时采取了更均衡的方法。

被动语态:
This view is espoused by many experts. 许多专家都特别支持这个观点。
That idea was espoused by the government. 那个想法得到了政府的特别支持。

疑问句:
Does he espouse this controversial theory? 他是否特别支持这个有争议的理论？
Which political party do they espouse? 他们特别支持哪个政党？


否定句:
He does not espouse violence. 他不支持暴力。
She did not espouse that particular philosophy. 她并没有特别信奉那种哲学。
