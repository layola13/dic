implanters:/ɪmˈplæntərz/ | n. | 植入器；植入者 | A person or thing that implants something, especially a medical device.  It can refer to the medical device itself or the person or machine that inserts it. |例句：The surgeons used advanced implanters to place the artificial hip joint.  外科医生使用先进的植入器植入人工髋关节。

The company developed new implanters for delivering medication directly to the brain. 这家公司开发了用于将药物直接输送到大脑的新型植入器。


近义词：inserters, injectors | 反义词：removers, extractors | 词性：名词
