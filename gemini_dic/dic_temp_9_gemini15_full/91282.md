hydrography:/haɪˈdrɒɡrəfi/| n. |水文地理学；水文测量|The scientific discipline that deals with the measurement and description of the physical features of the oceans, seas, lakes, rivers, and other bodies of water.  It includes the study of tides, currents, water depth, and the seabed. |例句：The hydrography of the region is complex, with many shallow reefs and strong currents.  该地区的水文地理很复杂，有很多浅礁和强流。

例句：Accurate hydrography is crucial for safe navigation.  精确的水文测量对于安全航行至关重要。


近义词：oceanography, bathymetry|反义词：None|词性:名词
