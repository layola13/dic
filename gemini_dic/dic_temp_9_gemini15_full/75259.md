fronde:/frɒnd/| n.|叶状体；树叶；（尤指）蕨类植物的叶子|A frond is a large, divided leaf, especially of a fern or palm.  It often refers to the leaf-like structures of certain plants,  distinguished by their large size and often deeply divided or dissected structure.  The term can also be used more broadly for any leaf-like structure, even if not strictly a leaf in botanical terms.|The fern unfurled its fronds in the sunlight.  蕨类植物在阳光下舒展着它的叶状体。|leaf, foliage|stem, branch|名词
