perceive:/pərˈsiːv/| vt.|感知，察觉，理解|to become aware of something through the senses or to understand something.|例句：I perceived a change in his attitude. 我觉察到他态度的变化。
例句：He perceived that she was unhappy. 他意识到她不开心。|近义词：notice, detect, understand, comprehend|反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I perceive a subtle difference. = 我察觉到细微的差别。
They perceive the world differently. = 他们对世界的感知不同。

一般过去时:
She perceived a threat in his words. = 她从他的话语中察觉到威胁。
We perceived the danger too late. = 我们太晚才意识到危险。

现在完成时:
I have perceived a pattern in his behavior. = 我已经察觉到他行为中的规律。
Scientists have perceived a significant shift in climate patterns. = 科学家们已经觉察到气候模式的显著变化。

过去完成时:
He had perceived the deception before anyone else. = 他比任何人都早察觉到欺骗。
They had perceived the problem but failed to act. = 他们已经意识到问题，但没有采取行动。

将来时:
I will perceive the truth eventually. = 我最终会明白真相。
We will perceive the results of the experiment next week. = 我们下周将了解实验的结果。

虚拟语气:
If I perceived the danger earlier, I would have acted differently. = 如果我早点意识到危险，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had perceived the seriousness of the situation. = 我希望我当时意识到了情况的严重性。

被动语态:
The change was perceived by many people. = 许多人都觉察到了变化。
The signal was not perceived by the receiver. = 接收器没有接收到信号。

疑问句:
Do you perceive any problems with the plan? = 你认为这个计划有什么问题吗？
How do you perceive your role in the team? = 你如何看待你在团队中的角色？

否定句:
I didn't perceive any hostility in his tone. = 我没有在他语气中察觉到任何敌意。
They failed to perceive the importance of the issue. = 他们没有意识到这个问题的重要性。


PHRASE->
perceive as = 把…视为
perceive to be = 认为是
perceive oneself as = 自认为是
perceive the difference = 察觉到差异
perceive the meaning = 理解意思
