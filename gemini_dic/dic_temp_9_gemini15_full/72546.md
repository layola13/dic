fluter:/ˈfluːtər/| v.|轻微地颤动；飘动；（鸟）拍打翅膀；|to move lightly and quickly, or to make something do this|The butterfly fluttered its wings. 蝴蝶拍打着翅膀。 The flag fluttered in the breeze. 旗帜在微风中飘动。| 近义词：flutter, flap, waver | 反义词：stay still, remain stationary


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird flutters its wings. = 鸟儿拍打着翅膀。
The leaves flutter in the wind. = 叶子在风中飘动。

一般过去时:
The butterfly fluttered around the flower. = 蝴蝶在花朵周围飞舞。
The flag fluttered in the strong wind yesterday. = 旗帜昨天在强风中飘动。

现在完成时:
The hummingbird has fluttered around the feeder all morning. = 蜂鸟整个上午都在喂食器周围飞舞。
The leaves have fluttered down from the tree. = 叶子从树上飘落下来了。

过去完成时:
The bird had fluttered away before I could see it clearly. = 我还没看清，鸟儿就飞走了。
The flag had fluttered proudly all day before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，旗帜自豪地飘扬了一整天。

将来时:
The butterflies will flutter around the garden in the spring. = 春天蝴蝶会在花园里飞舞。
The leaves will flutter down when the wind picks up. = 风一起，叶子就会飘落下来。

虚拟语气:
If the wind blew harder, the flag would flutter more vigorously. = 如果风更大一些，旗帜就会飘动得更剧烈。
I wish the butterflies would flutter closer so I could take a picture. = 我希望蝴蝶能飞近一点，这样我就能拍张照片了。

被动语态:
The flag was fluttered by the wind. = 旗帜被风吹得飘动。
The pages were fluttered nervously by the speaker. = 演讲者紧张地翻动着书页。（这里flutter指翻动）


疑问句:
Does the flag flutter in the wind? = 旗帜在风中飘动吗？
Will the butterflies flutter today? = 蝴蝶今天会飞舞吗？

否定句:
The flag didn't flutter in the still air. = 在静止的空气中，旗帜没有飘动。
The bird didn't flutter its wings. = 鸟儿没有拍打翅膀。


PHRASE->
flutter by = 飞过
flutter around = 在...周围飞舞
flutter down = 飘落
flutter nervously = 紧张地拍打（翅膀/翻动）
flutter with excitement = 兴奋地颤抖
