homebuyer: /ˈhoʊmbaɪər/ | n. |购房者，买房人 | A person who buys a home.  | Example: The homebuyer secured a mortgage to finance the purchase.  购房者获得抵押贷款来资助购买。 |近义词：house buyer, purchaser |反义词：home seller

This homebuyer considers the commute time. = 这个购房者正在考虑通勤时间。


一般过去时:
The homebuyer found a perfect house last week. = 购房者上周找到了一套完美的房子。
