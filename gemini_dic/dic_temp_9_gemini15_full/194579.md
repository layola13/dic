self-study:/ˈsɛlf ˈstʌdi/| n. |自学，独立学习|The act of learning something independently, without the guidance of a teacher or instructor.  It often involves using resources like books, online courses, or other materials.|例句：She improved her French through self-study. = 她通过自学提高了法语水平。|例句：Self-study is crucial for success in this field. =  在这个领域，自学至关重要。|近义词：independent study, private study, individual learning |反义词：classroom learning, formal education|名词
