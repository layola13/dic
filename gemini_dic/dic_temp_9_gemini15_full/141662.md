opting:/ˈɒptɪŋ/|/ˈɑːptɪŋ/| v.|选择；决定|choosing or making a decision; selecting something from a range of possibilities.|She is opting for a career in medicine. 她选择从事医学事业。 He opted to stay home instead of going to the party. 他选择待在家里而不是去参加聚会。|choosing, selecting, deciding|rejecting, refusing, declining|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are opting for a healthier lifestyle. = 他们正在选择一种更健康的生活方式。
I am opting out of the competition. = 我选择退出比赛。


一般过去时:
She opted to take the early train. = 她选择乘坐早班火车。
He opted against further education. = 他选择不继续深造。


现在完成时:
We have opted for a more sustainable solution. = 我们已经选择了一个更可持续的解决方案。
They have opted to remain silent. = 他们选择保持沉默。


过去完成时:
I had opted out before the deadline. = 我在截止日期之前就退出了。
She had opted for the simpler design. = 她之前选择了更简单的设计。


将来时:
He will opt for a different approach next time. = 下次他会选择不同的方法。
They will be opting to work remotely. = 他们将选择远程工作。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would opt for the red dress. = 如果我是你，我会选择那件红裙子。
I wish I had opted for a different career path. = 我真希望我选择了一条不同的职业道路。


被动语态:
The option was opted for by the majority. = 大多数人选择了这个选项。(注意：被动语态用得较少)
A new strategy is being opted for by the company. = 公司正在选择一项新的战略。(注意：被动语态用得较少)


疑问句:
Will you be opting for the buffet or the à la carte menu? = 你会选择自助餐还是点菜菜单？
Are they opting to participate in the event? = 他们选择参加这个活动吗？


否定句:
He did not opt to join the club. = 他没有选择加入俱乐部。
She opted not to answer the question. = 她选择不回答这个问题。


PHRASE->
opt in = 选择加入
opt out = 选择退出
opt for = 选择
opt to = 选择
to opt against = 选择反对
