kooks:/kuːks/| n.|怪人，疯子，傻瓜|A kook is a person who is considered eccentric, unconventional, or crazy.  The term is often used informally and can be slightly derogatory.|例句：He's a bit of a kook, always wearing mismatched clothes. 他有点怪，总是穿着不配套的衣服。

例句：Some people consider her a kook for her unusual beliefs. 有些人认为她因为她的奇特信仰而古怪。


近义词：eccentric, oddball, weirdo, crackpot|反义词：normal, conventional, sane
