non-sentient:/ˌnɒnˈsɛnʃənt/| adj. |无感知的，无知觉的 |Lacking the capacity for feeling or sensation; inanimate.  It refers to things that do not possess consciousness, feelings, or the ability to experience sensations. |Example:  Rocks are non-sentient objects.  岩石是无感知的物体。

The computer program, while complex, is ultimately non-sentient.  这个电脑程序虽然复杂，但最终是没有知觉的。

|近义词：insentient, inanimate, unconscious, unfeeling |反义词：sentient, conscious, feeling, animate |词性:形容词
