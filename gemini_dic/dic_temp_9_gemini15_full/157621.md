pities:/ˈpɪtiz/| v.|同情，怜悯|To feel pity or sympathy for someone or something.|例句：I pity him for his misfortune. 我同情他的不幸遭遇。She pitied the lost dog. 她可怜那只迷路的狗。|近义词：sympathize with, commiserate with|反义词：rejoice in, gloat over


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I pity the homeless. = 我同情无家可归的人。
He pities his struggling brother. = 他同情他苦苦挣扎的兄弟。

一般过去时:
She pitied the crying child. = 她同情那个哭泣的孩子。
They pitied the victims of the earthquake. = 他们同情地震的受害者。

现在完成时:
I have pitied many people in my life. = 我一生中同情过许多人。
She has pitied him since he lost his job. = 自从他失业以来，她就一直同情他。

过去完成时:
I had pitied him before I knew the whole story. = 在我了解整个故事之前，我就已经同情他了。
They had pitied her for years before she finally got help. = 在她最终得到帮助之前，他们已经同情她多年了。

将来时:
I will pity whoever loses the competition. = 我会同情任何输掉比赛的人。
They will pity the defeated team. = 他们会同情失败的球队。

虚拟语气:
I would pity him if I were in his shoes. = 如果我处在他的位置，我会同情他。
If I knew his situation, I would pity him more. = 如果我知道他的处境，我会更同情他。

被动语态:
The homeless are pitied by many. = 许多人同情无家可归的人。
The victims were pitied by the whole country. = 全国人民都同情受害者。

疑问句:
Do you pity the poor? = 你同情穷人吗？
Does he pity the animals? = 他同情动物吗？

否定句:
I don't pity him; he brought it on himself. = 我不同情他；这是他自己造成的。
She doesn't pity those who cheat. = 她不同情那些作弊的人。
