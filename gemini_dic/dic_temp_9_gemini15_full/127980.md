neozoic:/ˌniːoʊˈzoʊɪk/ | adj. | 新生代的 | Relating to or denoting the Cenozoic Era, the period of geological time from 66 million years ago to the present day, characterized by the dominance of mammals.  |The Neozoic Era saw the rise of mammals and the diversification of flowering plants. 新生代时期见证了哺乳动物的兴起和开花植物的多样化。|  Paleozoic, Mesozoic |Precambrian|  adj.
