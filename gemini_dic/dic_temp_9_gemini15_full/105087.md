kermes:/ˈkɜːrmɪz/ | n. |胭脂红；胭脂虫；角蜡蚧 | A kermes is a type of scale insect, specifically the females of certain species in the genus *Kermes*, that produce a crimson dye.  The dye, also called kermes, was historically important and highly valued.  The term can also refer to the resulting crimson color itself. | The ancient Greeks and Romans used kermes dye for fabrics. | 近义词：cochineal (though from a different insect), crimson dye | 反义词：N/A
