osman:/ˈɒzmən/| n. |奥斯曼；奥斯曼人；奥斯曼帝国 |A member of a Turkic people who established a vast empire that controlled much of southeastern Europe, Western Asia, and North Africa.  The Ottoman Empire lasted from the 13th to the early 20th century.  The term can also refer to things related to the empire or its culture. |例句：The Ottoman Empire was a significant power for centuries. 奥斯曼帝国几个世纪以来都是一股重要的力量。 |例句：He studied Ottoman history in university. 他在大学学习奥斯曼历史。|近义词：Turk (in some contexts) |反义词：None specifically.
