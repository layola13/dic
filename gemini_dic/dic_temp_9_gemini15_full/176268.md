re-cover:/ˌriːˈkʌvər/ | vt.  |重新覆盖；恢复；重新获得 |To cover again; to regain or restore to a former state.|The roof needs re-covering. 屋顶需要重新覆盖。|The team recovered from their early setback.  球队从早期的挫折中恢复过来。|recover, regain, restore | damage, lose


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They recover their losses quickly. = 他们迅速弥补了损失。
She recovers from illness easily. = 她很容易从疾病中恢复过来。


一般过去时:
He recovered his stolen bicycle. = 他找回了被盗的自行车。
The company recovered from the financial crisis. = 公司从金融危机中恢复过来。


现在完成时:
We have recovered all the lost data. = 我们已经找回了所有丢失的数据。
She has recovered from her operation. = 她已经从手术中康复了。


过去完成时:
They had recovered their strength before the final race. = 他们在最后一场比赛之前已经恢复了体力。
The economy had recovered somewhat before the new regulations were implemented. = 在新的规章实施之前，经济已经有所复苏。


将来时:
The doctors expect him to recover fully. = 医生预计他会完全康复。
They will recover the stolen jewels. = 他们会找回被盗的珠宝。


虚拟语气:
If he recovered his health, he would return to work. = 如果他恢复了健康，他就会回去工作。
I wish I could recover the lost photos. = 我希望我能找回丢失的照片。


被动语态:
The stolen painting was recovered by the police. = 失窃的油画被警方找回。
The damaged files were successfully recovered. = 受损的文件已成功恢复。


疑问句:
Will they ever recover from this tragedy? = 他们会从这场悲剧中恢复过来吗？
Has she fully recovered from her injury? = 她完全从伤病中恢复了吗？


否定句:
