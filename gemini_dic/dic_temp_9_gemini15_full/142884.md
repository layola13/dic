out-of-the-blue:/ˌaʊt əv ðə bluː/| adj. |出乎意料的，突然的| Unexpected; sudden; without warning.  The phrase implies something happening completely unexpectedly and without any prior indication. |例句：The news of her promotion came completely out of the blue.  她的晋升消息完全是出乎意料的。

例句：He received an out-of-the-blue invitation to a party. 他收到一份突如其来的派对邀请。


近义词：unexpected, surprising, unforeseen, sudden, abrupt

反义词：expected, anticipated, predictable


词性:形容词
