/ˈreɪkəl/| n.|耙；挠；刮擦|A rakel is a tool used for gathering and moving loose materials, such as hay or leaves, or for smoothing a surface.  It typically has a long handle and a broad, toothed head.|例句：He used a rakel to gather the leaves into piles. 他用耙子把树叶堆成一堆。|例句：The painter used a rakel to smooth the wet paint. 画家用刮刀来抹平湿润的颜料。|近义词：rake, scraper, comb|反义词:None.
