pertinence:/ˈpɜːtɪnəns/| n. |相关性，切题，贴切|Relevance or appropriateness; the quality of being pertinent.|The pertinence of his remarks was immediately apparent.  他的话语的贴切性立刻显而易见。|The study lacked pertinence to the real world. 这项研究缺乏与现实世界的相关性。|relevance, appropriateness, applicability|irrelevance, inappropriateness, inapplicability|名词

否定句:
