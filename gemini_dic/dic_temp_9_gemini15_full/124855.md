mucosa:/mjuˈkoʊsə/ | n. |粘膜 |A mucous membrane;  the membrane lining body cavities and passages that communicate with the exterior, such as the respiratory, digestive, and genitourinary tracts.  It secretes mucus, which lubricates and protects the surface. | Example: The nasal mucosa is highly vascularized and plays a crucial role in filtering inhaled air. 鼻粘膜高度血管化，在过滤吸入的空气中起着至关重要的作用。 |近义词：mucous membrane |反义词： (无直接反义词，可考虑与皮肤等非粘膜组织相对比)
