quasi-comprehensive:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ kɒmprɪˈhɛnsɪv/| adj. | 准全面的；近乎全面的 |  Approaching but not fully achieving comprehensiveness; nearly complete but lacking some elements.  It suggests a degree of completeness that falls short of true comprehensiveness. | The quasi-comprehensive curriculum covered most, but not all, aspects of the subject.  这门准全面的课程涵盖了该科目的大部分内容，但并非全部。|近义词：nearly comprehensive, almost complete, largely comprehensive | 反义词：incomplete, partial, superficial
