profoundly:/prəˈfaʊndli/ | adv. | 深刻地；极度地 | deeply and intensely; to a great extent or degree.  |例句：His words profoundly affected me.  他的话深深地影响了我。|例句：I was profoundly saddened by the news.  这个消息让我极其悲伤。|近义词：deeply, intensely, greatly, significantly |反义词：superficially, slightly, marginally
