regurgitants:/rɪˈɡɜːrdʒɪtənts/| n.|反刍物；呕吐物|Things that are regurgitated, especially undigested food.  The term often implies a forceful or involuntary expulsion.|Example: The bird ejected a mass of regurgitants onto the ground.  这只鸟把一大堆反刍物吐到了地上。|近义词：vomitus, emesis|反义词：ingested food


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird regurgitates food to feed its young. = 鸟反刍食物喂养幼鸟。
He regurgitates information without understanding it. = 他不理解就复述信息。


一般过去时:
The owl regurgitated a pellet of undigested bones. = 猫头鹰吐出了一个未消化的骨头球。
She regurgitated the lecture notes verbatim. = 她逐字背诵了讲义。


现在完成时:
The baby has regurgitated milk several times. = 婴儿已经吐了几次奶。
He has regurgitated the same arguments repeatedly. = 他反复重复同样的论点。


过去完成时:
The patient had regurgitated blood before the doctor arrived. = 病人在医生到达之前已经吐血了。
She had regurgitated the entire chapter before the exam. = 她在考试前已经背诵了整章内容。


将来时:
The sick child will likely regurgitate his dinner. = 那生病的孩子很可能会吐出他的晚餐。
He will regurgitate the information for the presentation. = 他将为演示文稿复述信息。


虚拟语气:
If he ate less, he wouldn't regurgitate so much. = 如果他少吃点，就不会吐这么多。
I wish I hadn't regurgitated that embarrassing story. = 我真希望我没有复述那个令人尴尬的故事。


被动语态:
The food was regurgitated by the bird. = 食物被鸟反刍了。
The information was regurgitated without critical thinking. = 信息是在没有批判性思维的情况下复述的。


疑问句:
Has the baby regurgitated again? = 婴儿又吐奶了吗？
Did he regurgitate the answer from the textbook? = 他是从课本上背诵答案的吗？


否定句:
The cat did not regurgitate its hairball. = 猫没有吐出毛球。
She did not regurgitate the information she'd learned. = 她没有复述她学到的信息。
