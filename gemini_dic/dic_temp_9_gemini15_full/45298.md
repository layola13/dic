culvert:/ˈkʌlvərt/| n.|暗渠；涵洞|A culvert is a covered channel or pipe that allows water to flow under a road, railway, or other obstruction.|例句：The culvert under the road was blocked by debris.  这条路下的涵洞被碎石堵塞了。

例句：They built a new culvert to improve drainage. 他们建造了一个新的暗渠以改善排水。|近义词：conduit, drain, channel|反义词：bridge, overpass
