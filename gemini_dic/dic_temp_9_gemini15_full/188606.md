rumbler:/ˈrʌmblər/| n.| 隆隆声；抱怨者；低沉的声音 | A low, continuous, rumbling sound.  It can also refer to a person who grumbles or complains frequently. |例句：The distant thunder was a low rumbler.  远处雷声隆隆作响。|近义词：grumbler, growler, murmurer |反义词：whisperer,  (for sound)  a clear and distinct sound

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine rumbles quietly. = 发动机静静地隆隆作响。
He rumbles his complaints to anyone who will listen. = 他向任何愿意倾听的人抱怨。


一般过去时:
The train rumbled through the tunnel. = 火车隆隆地穿过隧道。
She rumbled a protest about the unfairness of it all. = 她对这一切的不公平表达了不满。


现在完成时:
The volcano has rumbled ominously all day. = 火山整天都发出不祥的隆隆声。
He has rumbled the truth behind the conspiracy. = 他已经发现了阴谋背后的真相。


过去完成时:
The ground had rumbled before the earthquake struck. = 地震发生前地面已经隆隆作响。
She had rumbled his plan before he even tried to execute it. = 他甚至还没尝试执行他的计划，她就识破了。


将来时:
The storm will rumble through the night. = 暴风雨将持续整夜隆隆作响。
They will rumble their dissatisfaction to the management. = 他们将向管理层表达他们的不满。


虚拟语气:
If the engine rumbled louder, I would be concerned. = 如果引擎隆隆声更大，我会担心。
I wish the thunder wouldn't rumble so loudly. = 我希望雷声不要响得这么大。


被动语态:
The news was rumbled by the press. = 这个消息被媒体披露了。
His secret plan was rumbled by his own colleagues. = 他的秘密计划被他自己的同事发现了。


疑问句:
Does the stomach rumble when you are hungry? = 你饿的时候胃会咕咕叫吗？
What is that rumbling sound? = 那隆隆的声音是什么？


否定句:
The car didn't rumble as much as it used to. = 这辆车不像以前那样隆隆作响了。
He didn't rumble their scheme. = 他没有识破他们的诡计。
