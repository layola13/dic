catechism:/ˈkætɪkɪzəm/| n.|教理问答；教义问答；|A catechism is a summary of the principles of Christian religion in the form of questions and answers, used for instruction, especially of children.  It can also refer to a similar instructional method in other fields.|例句：The children learned the catechism by heart.孩子们熟记了教理问答。|例句：The teacher used a catechism to explain the basic principles of grammar.老师用教理问答的方式解释语法基本原理。|近义词：instruction, teaching, indoctrination,|反义词：heresy, freethinking
