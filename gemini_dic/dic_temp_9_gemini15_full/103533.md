joeys:/ˈdʒoʊiz/ | n. | 幼小的袋鼠；（尤指）小袋鼠 | Young kangaroos, especially those still in their mother's pouch.  | Example:  The mother kangaroo protected her joeys from the predators.  这只母袋鼠保护着她的幼崽免受捕食者的伤害。


Example: We saw several joeys hopping around their mother in the field. 我们看到几只小袋鼠在田野里蹦蹦跳跳地围着它们的母亲。


近义词：baby kangaroo, young kangaroo
反义词：无
