cauterises:/ˈkɔːtəraɪz/| vt.| 烧灼；止血；使…失去活力 |To burn the surface of (a wound) with a hot instrument to stop bleeding or prevent infection.  |例句：The surgeon cauterised the wound to stop the bleeding. 外科医生用烧灼法止住了伤口出血。

The doctor cauterised the bleeding artery to prevent further blood loss. 医生烧灼了出血的动脉以防止进一步失血。|近义词：burn, sear, sear, stigmatize|反义词：heal, soothe
