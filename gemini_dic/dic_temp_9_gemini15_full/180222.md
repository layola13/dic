refracting:/rɪˈfræktɪŋ/ | adj. | 折射的 | Relating to the bending of light as it passes from one medium to another.  The process describes how light changes direction when it enters a different substance, such as from air to water. | Example:  The refracting telescope uses lenses to bend light and magnify distant objects. 折射望远镜利用透镜弯曲光线并放大远处物体。

The refracting power of the lens is crucial for the camera's performance. 透镜的折射能力对于相机的性能至关重要。


近义词：bending, deflecting | 反义词：reflecting (in the context of light) | 词性：形容词
