reemerged:/ˌriːɪˈmɜːrdʒd/ | v. |重新出现；再次涌现 | To appear or become known again after a period of time.  |例句：After a long illness, he reemerged into the public eye.  一场大病之后，他又重新出现在公众视野中。

例句：The old traditions have reemerged in recent years. 近年来，古老的传统又重新出现了。


近义词： reappear, resurface, return, revive | 反义词： disappear, vanish, fade


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reemerges every spring. 他每年春天都会重新出现。
The issue reemerges in every election cycle.  这个问题在每一次选举周期都会再次出现。


一般过去时:
The sun reemerged from behind the clouds. 太阳从云层后面重新露了出来。
The lost documents reemerged after decades.  那些丢失的文件在几十年后又重新出现了。


现在完成时:
The problem has reemerged after a period of inactivity.  这个问题在一段时间的沉寂之后又重新出现了。
She has reemerged as a leading figure in the industry. 她已重新成为业界的领军人物。


过去完成时:
By the time the storm passed, the sun had reemerged.  暴风雨过去的时候，太阳已经重新出现了。
After the scandal, his reputation had reemerged, albeit slightly tarnished.  丑闻过后，他的名誉虽然略微受损，但还是重新出现了。


将来时:
The comet will reemerge in the night sky next year.  这颗彗星明年将会重新出现在夜空中。
We believe the trend will reemerge in the coming months. 我们相信这种趋势将在未来几个月重新出现。


虚拟语气:
If the economy improved, consumer confidence would reemerge. 如果经济好转，消费者信心就会重新出现。
I wish the lost painting would reemerge someday. 我希望丢失的画作有一天能重新出现。


被动语态:
The issue was reemerged during the debate.  这个问题在辩论中被重新提及。
The evidence was reemerged by the defense team.  证据被辩护团队重新提出。


疑问句:
Will the band reemerge with a new album? 乐队会带着新专辑重新出现吗？
Has the disease reemerged in the region?  这种疾病在这个地区重新出现了吗？


否定句:
The missing child did not reemerge. 失踪的孩子没有重新出现。
The company's profits did not reemerge after the recession.  公司在经济衰退后利润没有重新增长。

词源:
