mullein: /ˈmʌlən/ | n. |毛蕊花，毛地黄 | A tall plant with woolly leaves and yellow flowers, often found in dry, open areas.  Mullein is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for various ailments.  |例句：The field was dotted with tall stalks of mullein.  这片田野上点缀着高高的毛蕊花茎。

例句：He used mullein leaves to make a soothing tea. 他用毛蕊花叶子泡了一杯舒缓的茶。
