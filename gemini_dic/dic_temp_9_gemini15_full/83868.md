half:/hɑːf/| /hæf/ | pron. n. adj. adv.| 一半；半数；半个 |  A part, portion, or share that is one of two equal parts of a whole.  |例句：I ate half the cake. 我吃了一半蛋糕。|例句：She spent half her life in Paris. 她一生中一半时间都在巴黎度过。|近义词：halfway, moiety |反义词：whole, entire


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Half of the students are absent today. =今天有一半学生缺席。
Half the work is finished. = 一半的工作完成了。


一般过去时:
Half the class went on a field trip. = 一半的班级去了一次实地考察。
Half of the apples were rotten. = 一半的苹果都烂了。


现在完成时:
We have finished half the project. = 我们已经完成了项目的一半。
She has eaten half the pizza. = 她已经吃掉了披萨的一半。


过去完成时:
They had already finished half the race. = 他们已经完成了比赛的一半。
Half the book had been read before he went to bed. = 他睡觉前已经读了一半的书。


将来时:
I will eat half the sandwich for lunch. = 我午餐将吃掉半个三明治。
Half of the class will be going to the museum. = 一半的班级将去博物馆。


虚拟语气:
If I had half the money, I would buy a car. = 如果我有这笔钱的一半，我会买一辆车。
I wish I had half the energy I used to have. = 我希望我还有以前一半的精力。


被动语态:
Half of the cake was eaten. = 蛋糕的一半被吃掉了。
Half the building was destroyed by the fire. = 这栋建筑的一半被大火烧毁了。


疑问句:
Is this half the amount? = 这是总数的一半吗？
Did you only eat half? = 你只吃了一半吗？


否定句:
It's not half as good as it used to be. = 它没有以前一半好。
I didn't eat half the pizza. =我没有吃掉披萨的一半。
