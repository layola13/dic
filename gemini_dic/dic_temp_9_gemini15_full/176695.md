reacting:/riˈæktɪŋ/ | v. | 反应；回应；起反应 |  The act of responding to something. This can be a physical, chemical, or emotional response.  |例句：The audience was reacting enthusiastically to the performance. 观众对演出反应热烈。

例句：He was reacting badly to the medication. 他对药物反应很差。


近义词：responding, replying, answering | 反义词：ignoring, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is reacting to the news. 他正在对这个消息作出反应。
She reacts calmly under pressure.  她在压力下反应冷静。


一般过去时:
I reacted angrily to his insults. 我对他侮辱的回应很生气。
They reacted quickly to the emergency. 他们对紧急情况反应迅速。


现在完成时:
She has reacted poorly to the change in plans. 她对计划的改变反应很差。
He has reacted well to the treatment. 他对治疗反应良好。


过去完成时:
I had reacted defensively before he finished speaking. 他还没说完话，我就已经开始采取防御姿态了。
They had reacted with shock at the unexpected announcement.  面对突如其来的宣布，他们震惊地做出了反应。


将来时:
How will the market react to the new product? 市场会如何对新产品作出反应？
I think she will react positively to your proposal. 我认为她会对你的提议作出积极的回应。


虚拟语气:
If he reacted more quickly, the accident could have been avoided. 如果他反应更快，事故本来是可以避免的。
I wish he had reacted differently. 我希望他的反应有所不同。


被动语态:
The chemical was reacted with acid. 这种化学物质与酸发生了反应。
His proposal was reacted to negatively by the board.  他的提案遭到了董事会的负面回应。


疑问句:
How are you reacting to the stress? 你如何应对压力？
How did she react to the bad news? 她对坏消息的反应如何？


否定句:
He isn't reacting to my calls. 他没有回应我的电话。
They didn't react in time to prevent the collision.  他们没有及时反应来防止碰撞。


PHRASE->
react to: 对…作出反应
react against: 反抗；反对
react with: 与…发生反应 (化学)
chemical reacting: 化学反应
overreacting: 过度反应
