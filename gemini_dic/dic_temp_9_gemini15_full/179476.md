redyed:/ˌriːˈdaɪd/| v.|重新染色|To dye (something) again.  This usually implies a change of color or a restoration of color.|例句：She redyed her hair a vibrant red. 她把头发重新染成了鲜艳的红色。

例句：The faded carpet was redyed to match the furniture.  褪色的地毯被重新染色以与家具相匹配。


近义词：redye, recolor, refresh (color)


反义词：bleach, fade

词性:动词
