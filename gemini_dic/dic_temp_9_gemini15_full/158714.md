ploughing:/ˈplaʊɪŋ/| v.|耕作，犁地|The act of turning over soil with a plough to prepare land for planting.  It is a fundamental agricultural process.|例句：The farmer is ploughing the field.  农民正在耕地。

例句：They're ploughing the land to plant corn this spring. 他们正在耕地，准备春天种玉米。


近义词：tilling, cultivating, plowing


反义词：harvesting

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ploughs the field every spring. 他每年春天都耕地。
The farmer ploughs the land before planting. 农民在种植前耕地。


一般过去时:
They ploughed the field yesterday. 他们昨天耕地了。
The farmer ploughed the land before the rain. 农民在下雨前耕地。


现在完成时:
He has ploughed the entire field. 他已经耕完了整块地。
They have ploughed the land for the new crop. 他们已经为新作物耕地了。


过去完成时:
They had ploughed the field before the storm hit. 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经耕过地了。
The farmer had ploughed the land before planting the seeds. 农民在播种前已经耕过地了。


将来时:
They will plough the field next week. 他们下周将耕地。
The farmer will plough the land after the rain stops. 雨停后，农民将耕地。


虚拟语气:
If it weren't raining, we would be ploughing the field. 如果不下雨，我们就会耕地。
I wish I had ploughed the field earlier. 我真希望我早点耕地。


被动语态:
The field was ploughed last week. 这块地是上周耕的。
The land is being ploughed by a tractor. 这块地正在用拖拉机耕。


疑问句:
Have they ploughed the field yet? 他们耕地了吗？
Is the farmer ploughing the land now? 农民现在正在耕地吗？


否定句:
They didn't plough the field yesterday. 他们昨天没有耕地。
He hasn't ploughed the field yet. 他还没耕地。


PHRASE->
deep ploughing 深耕
plough back 投入再生产
plough through 费力地完成
plough the sands 徒劳无功
plough ahead 继续努力


词源:
From Old English *plōh, from Proto-Germanic *plūgōn (compare Dutch ploeg, German Pflug), of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to a root meaning "to bend" or "to curve".
