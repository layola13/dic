interacts:/ˈɪntərækts/| v. |相互作用，互动|To act together or in a way that affects each other.  It implies a reciprocal relationship or influence between two or more things.|The chemicals interact to produce a new substance. 化学物质相互作用产生了一种新的物质。

The two companies interact frequently in the marketplace. 两家公司在市场上经常互动。|interact with,  engage with, influence |separate, isolate|动词
