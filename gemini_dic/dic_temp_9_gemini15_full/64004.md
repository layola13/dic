epistolographic:/ˌɛpɪstələˈɡræfɪk/| adj. |书信体的；书信写作的| relating to or characteristic of epistolography (the art or practice of letter writing).  It describes writing that takes the form of letters or resembles the style of letters.| The novel's epistolographic structure allowed the reader to intimately experience the characters' emotional journeys.|近义词：epistolary|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be terms describing non-letter-based narratives.
