pagoda:/pəˈɡoʊdə/| n. |宝塔，佛塔 |A pagoda is a tiered tower, usually a Buddhist temple, with multiple eaves.  Pagodas are common in East and Southeast Asia. |例句：The ancient pagoda stood majestically on the hill. 这座古老的宝塔雄伟地矗立在山上。|例句：We visited a beautiful pagoda during our trip to Japan. 我们在去日本的旅行中参观了一座美丽的宝塔。|近义词：temple, stupa |反义词：None.
