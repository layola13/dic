larus:/ˈlɛərəs/| n. |鸥属|A genus of large gulls belonging to the family Laridae.  Larus is a genus that includes many species of gulls, known for their coastal and inland habitats, varied diets, and distinctive calls.|Example: The *Larus argentatus*, or herring gull, is a common sight along many coastlines.  银鸥（Larus argentatus）在许多海岸线上很常见。|近义词：gull (a more general term)|反义词：N/A
