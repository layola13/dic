predescent:/ˌpriːˈdɛsənt/| n.|先祖，祖先；先前的下降|The word "predescent" refers to ancestry or lineage, particularly emphasizing the descent from earlier generations.  It is less common than synonyms like "ancestor" or "forebear."  It can also refer to a prior descent, as in a previous fall or decline.|例句：The family's predescent were all skilled artisans.  这个家族的祖先都是熟练的工匠。The predescent of the mountain climbers was treacherous. 登山者的先前下降是危险的。|近义词：ancestor, forebear, progenitor, antecedent|反义词：descendant, posterity|名词
