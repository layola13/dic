engallant:/ɛnˈɡælənt/| adj.|英勇的，勇敢的，骑士风度的|Gallant means brave, noble-minded, or chivalrous.  It often describes a heroic or courageous act, especially one performed by a knight. It can also describe someone who is courteous and attentive, especially towards women.|例句：He performed a gallant rescue of the child from the burning building. 他英勇地从燃烧的建筑物中救出了孩子。


例句：He was a gallant knight, always ready to defend the weak. 他是一位英勇的骑士，总是乐于保护弱者。


近义词：brave, courageous, heroic, chivalrous, valiant|反义词：cowardly, craven, pusillanimous


词源：源自古法语 *galant,  最终源自于拉丁语 *gallus “Gaul (高卢人)”, 指的是古高卢人的勇敢。  随着时间的推移，含义演变为指具有高卢人特性的勇敢和骑士精神。
