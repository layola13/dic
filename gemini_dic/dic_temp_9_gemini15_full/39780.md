consonant-with:/ˈkɒnsənənt wɪð/| adj.|与…一致的；与…协调的|Conforming to or agreeing with something.  It implies a harmonious relationship or agreement. |例句：Her actions were consonant with her beliefs. 她的行为与她的信仰一致。

例句：His statement is consonant with the facts. 他的陈述与事实相符。


近义词：consistent with, in agreement with, in harmony with, compatible with|反义词：inconsistent with, at odds with, contrary to|词性:形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
His lifestyle is consonant with his high income. = 他的生活方式与他的高收入相符。
Her opinions are consonant with the party line. = 她的观点与党的路线一致。


一般过去时:
His behaviour was consonant with his upbringing. = 他的行为与他的教养相符。
The results were consonant with our predictions. = 结果与我们的预测一致。


现在完成时:
Her actions have been consonant with her promises. = 她的行动一直与她的承诺一致。
The evidence has been consonant with the defendant's claims. = 证据一直与被告的主张一致。


过去完成时:
The evidence had been consonant with the theory before the new discovery. = 在新的发现之前，证据一直与理论相符。
His previous behaviour had been consonant with his current actions. = 他之前的行为与他现在的行为一致。


将来时:
His future plans will be consonant with his career goals. = 他未来的计划将与他的职业目标一致。
Their actions will be consonant with international law. = 他们的行动将符合国际法。


虚拟语气:
If his actions were consonant with his words, people would trust him more. = 如果他的行动与他的话语一致，人们会更信任他。
I wish his behaviour were more consonant with his position. = 我希望他的行为更符合他的职位。


被动语态:
His explanation was found to be consonant with the available evidence. = 他的解释被发现与现有证据相符。
The policy is deemed consonant with the public interest. = 该政策被认为符合公众利益。


疑问句:
Is his behaviour consonant with company policy? = 他的行为符合公司政策吗？
Are the findings consonant with existing research? = 研究结果与现有研究一致吗？


否定句:
His actions were not consonant with his professed ideals. = 他的行为与他所宣称的理想不符。
Her response was not consonant with the seriousness of the situation. = 她的回应与局势的严重性不相符。
