penitentiaries:/ˌpɛnɪˈtɛnʃəriːz/| n. |监狱，感化院 |A penitentiary is a prison, especially one for convicted felons.  Penitentiaries are designed to house and rehabilitate individuals convicted of serious crimes. They are often characterized by high security measures and structured routines. | Example:  He was sentenced to ten years in a state penitentiary. 他被判处十年州立监狱监禁。


The notorious criminal spent his remaining years in a federal penitentiary.臭名昭著的罪犯在他余生的大部分时间里都在联邦监狱度过。
