disasters:/dɪˈzɑːstərz/| n.|灾难，灾害|An event that causes great and often sudden damage or suffering; a catastrophe.|例句：The earthquake was a major disaster, causing widespread destruction and loss of life.  这场地震是一场巨大的灾难，造成了广泛的破坏和人员伤亡。


例句：Natural disasters such as floods and hurricanes can have devastating consequences.  像洪水和飓风这样的自然灾害可能造成毁灭性的后果。


近义词：catastrophes, calamities, catastrophes, tragedies|反义词：blessings, good fortune, success|词性:名词
