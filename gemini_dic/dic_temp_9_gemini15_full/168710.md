proctodaeum: /ˌprɒktoʊˈdiːəm/ | n. | 直肠 | The hindgut of an insect, formed by invagination of the ectoderm, and typically including the rectum and anus.  It is the posterior part of the insect's digestive tract, responsible for the final stages of digestion and the elimination of waste. | Example: The proctodaeum of the grasshopper is well-developed and plays a crucial role in water balance.  直翅目昆虫的直肠非常发达，在水分平衡中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：hindgut (less specific)|反义词：foregut, midgut|
