divorceless:/daɪˈvɔːrsləs/ | adj. | 未离婚的，从未离婚的 |  Not having experienced divorce.  The word implies a state of being, suggesting a continuous lack of divorce rather than simply not being divorced at a particular point in time. | Example:  She comes from a divorceless family. 她来自一个从未有过离婚的家庭。  He maintained a divorceless marriage for over fifty years. 他保持了五十多年的未离婚婚姻。 | 近义词：married (if referring to a currently married person), undivorced | 反义词：divorced
