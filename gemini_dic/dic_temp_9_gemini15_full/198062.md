shoeshine: /ˈʃuːʃaɪn/ | n. v. |擦鞋；擦鞋匠 |

英文解析:The noun "shoeshine" refers to the act of polishing or cleaning shoes to improve their appearance.  As a verb, it means to polish or clean shoes.


例句:

n.  The shoeshine boy worked tirelessly on the busy street corner. = 擦鞋童在繁忙的街角辛勤工作。

v.  He shoeshined his shoes before the important meeting. = 他在重要会议前擦亮了他的鞋子。


近义词: shoe polishing, cleaning shoes


反义词: soiling shoes, dirtying shoes


词性: noun, verb
