remoteliest: /rɪˈmoʊtɪst/ | adj. | 最偏远的，最遥远的 | Most distant or secluded; furthest removed.  This word is the superlative form of "remote." It suggests the greatest degree of remoteness. | Example: The remoteliest village in the country was finally reached by the construction crew.  这个国家最偏远的村庄终于被建筑队到达了。|近义词: most distant, most secluded, farthest, outmost | 反义词: nearest, closest, central
