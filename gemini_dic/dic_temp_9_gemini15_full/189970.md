sanctions:/ˈsæŋkʃənz/ | n. |制裁；处罚；批准 |  Sanctions are penalties imposed on a country, organization, or individual for violating laws or international agreements. They can take various forms, including economic restrictions, trade embargoes, travel bans, and asset freezes.  |例句：The United Nations imposed sanctions on the country after its invasion of a neighboring state. 联合国在其入侵邻国后对该国实施制裁。

例句：The government gave its sanction to the construction project. 政府批准了该建设项目。| 近义词：penalties, punishments, embargoes, restrictions | 反义词：approvals, permissions, authorizations | 词性：名词
