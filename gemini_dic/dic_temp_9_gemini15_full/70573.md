filleted:/fɪˈleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 去骨的，剔除骨头的 |  Describes fish or meat that has had its bones removed.  | Example:  The chef prepared a delicious filleted salmon.  厨师准备了一条美味的去骨三文鱼。 | 近义词：boneless, deboned | 反义词：boned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fish is filleted before cooking. = 鱼在烹饪前被去骨。
He fillets the chicken expertly. = 他熟练地将鸡肉去骨。


一般过去时:
She filleted the fish quickly and efficiently. = 她快速有效地将鱼去骨。
They filleted all the trout for the dinner party. = 他们为晚宴将所有的鳟鱼都去骨了。


现在完成时:
I have filleted all the salmon for tonight's dinner. = 我已经为今晚的晚餐将所有三文鱼都去骨了。
She has filleted hundreds of fish in her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她已经去过数百条鱼的骨头了。


过去完成时:
He had filleted the fish before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经将鱼去骨了。
They had filleted the cod before they started cooking. = 在他们开始烹饪之前，他们已经将鳕鱼去骨了。


将来时:
I will fillet the trout tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上将去骨鳟鱼。
She will fillet the chicken for the stew. = 她将为炖菜去骨鸡肉。


虚拟语气:
If I had a filleting knife, I would fillet the fish myself. = 如果我有一把去骨刀，我会自己去骨鱼。
I wish I had filleted the fish earlier; now it's too late. = 我希望我早点把鱼去骨；现在太晚了。


被动语态:
The fish was filleted by the chef. = 鱼被厨师去骨了。
The chicken will be filleted before it is cooked. = 鸡肉将在烹饪前被去骨。


疑问句:
Has the fish been filleted yet? = 鱼去骨了吗？
Will the salmon be filleted before grilling? = 三文鱼在烤之前会被去骨吗？


否定句:
He didn't fillet the fish properly. = 他没有正确地将鱼去骨。
The fish wasn't filleted before it was cooked. = 鱼在烹饪前没有去骨。
