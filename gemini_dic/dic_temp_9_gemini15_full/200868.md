slinger:/ˈslɪŋər/| n. v.|投石者；投掷者；吊索工人；（俚语）酒鬼；（俚语）骗子|

英文解析:A slinger can refer to someone who slings or throws things, particularly stones or projectiles.  It can also be a slang term for a drunkard or a swindler.  In a more technical sense, it can refer to a worker who uses a sling (a rope or chain used for lifting heavy objects).

例句:
He was a skilled slinger, able to hit his target from a great distance. 他是一位熟练的投石者，能够从很远的距离击中目标。

The construction worker was a slinger, operating the crane's hoisting mechanism.  这位建筑工人是一个吊索工，操作着起重机的提升机构。


近义词:thrower, hurler, pitcher, tosser (informal), swindler (in slang context), drunkard (in slang context)

反义词:receiver, target (depending on context)


词性:noun, verb
