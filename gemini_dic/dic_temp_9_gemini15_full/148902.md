overwhipt:/ˌoʊvərˈhwɪpt/ | adj. |  过度搅拌的；过度兴奋的 | Excessively whipped or beaten; excessively agitated or excited.  It describes something that has been subjected to excessive whipping or agitation, resulting in a less desirable state. This can apply literally to food items or figuratively to emotions or states of being. |例句：The cream was overwhipped and curdled. 奶油搅拌过度，凝结了。  The crowd was overwhipt with excitement. 人群兴奋过度。|近义词：overbeaten, overstimulated, overexcited |反义词：underwhipped, calm, composed
