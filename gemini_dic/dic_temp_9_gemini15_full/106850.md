laboured:/ˈleɪbərd/| adj.|费力的；吃力的；冗长的；笨拙的| characterized by great effort; strained; difficult;  lacking fluency or grace; overly elaborate or detailed. |例句：He gave a laboured explanation. 他做了一个费力的解释。
例句：The novel's plot felt laboured and unconvincing. 这部小说的情节显得牵强且缺乏说服力。|近义词：strained, strenuous, arduous, ponderous, clumsy, awkward|反义词：easy, effortless, fluent, graceful, simple
