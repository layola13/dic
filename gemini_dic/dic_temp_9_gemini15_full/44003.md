criminalizes: /ˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 宣布…为犯罪；定为犯罪 | To declare something illegal; to make something a crime. | The law criminalizes the possession of certain drugs.  这项法律将持有某些毒品定为犯罪。 |  outlaws, prohibits, bans | legalizes, permits, allows | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government criminalizes drug trafficking. = 政府将贩毒定为犯罪。
This country criminalizes hate speech. = 这个国家将仇恨言论定为犯罪。


一般过去时:
They criminalized gambling in the 19th century. = 他们在19世纪将赌博定为犯罪。
The state criminalized same-sex marriage until recently. = 该州直到最近才将同性婚姻定为犯罪。


现在完成时:
Many countries have criminalized human trafficking. = 许多国家已经将人口贩卖定为犯罪。
Society has criminalized certain forms of violence. = 社会已经将某些形式的暴力定为犯罪。


过去完成时:
They had criminalized prostitution long before the new law was passed. = 在新法律通过之前很久，他们就已经将卖淫定为犯罪了。
The authorities had criminalized dissent for years. = 当局多年来一直将异议定为犯罪。


将来时:
They will criminalize the use of AI in warfare. = 他们将把在战争中使用人工智能定为犯罪。
The new legislation will criminalize certain types of cybercrime. = 新立法将把某些类型的网络犯罪定为犯罪。


虚拟语气:
If they criminalized downloading copyrighted material, many people would be affected. = 如果他们将下载受版权保护的材料定为犯罪，许多人将受到影响。
I wish they would criminalize the use of child labor. = 我希望他们能将使用童工定为犯罪。


被动语态:
Drug use was criminalized decades ago. = 几十年前吸毒就被定为犯罪了。
The act of protesting was criminalized under the new regime. = 在新政权下，抗议行为被定为犯罪。


疑问句:
Does this law criminalize the sharing of personal information? = 这部法律是否将分享个人信息定为犯罪？
Will they criminalize the use of this technology? = 他们会将这项技术的应用定为犯罪吗？


否定句:
The law does not criminalize the possession of small amounts of marijuana. = 这部法律并没有将持有少量大麻定为犯罪。
This country does not criminalize peaceful protest. = 这个国家没有将和平抗议定为犯罪。


PHRASE->
