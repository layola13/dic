ike:/aɪk/| v.|喜欢，喜爱 |to have a feeling of liking something or someone | I like chocolate ice cream. 我喜欢巧克力冰淇淋。|近义词：love, enjoy|反义词：dislike, hate


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I like apples. = 我喜欢苹果。
She likes to dance. = 她喜欢跳舞。

一般过去时:
I liked that movie. = 我喜欢那部电影。
They liked playing games. = 他们喜欢玩游戏。

现在完成时:
I have liked her since we were kids. = 从我们是小孩子的时候我就喜欢她了。
He has liked this band for years. = 他喜欢这个乐队很多年了。

过去完成时:
I had liked her for a long time before I finally told her. = 在我最终告诉她之前，我已经喜欢她很久了。
She had liked painting before she started her career. = 在她开始她的职业生涯之前，她喜欢绘画。

将来时:
I will like this job. = 我将会喜欢这份工作。
They will like the gift. = 他们会喜欢这个礼物。

虚拟语气:
I wish I liked broccoli. = 我希望我喜欢西兰花。
If I liked spicy food, I would eat more of it. = 如果我喜欢辣的食物，我会多吃一些。


被动语态:
(The verb "like" is rarely used in the passive voice.  A more natural way to express passive sentiment would be to rephrase the sentence.)


疑问句:
Do you like pizza? = 你喜欢披萨吗？
Does he like his new job? = 他喜欢他的新工作吗？

否定句:
I don't like mushrooms. = 我不喜欢蘑菇。
She doesn't like loud music. = 她不喜欢吵闹的音乐。


PHRASE->
like crazy = 疯狂地
like this = 像这样
like that = 像那样
look like = 看起来像
would like to = 想要
