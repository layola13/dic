highspeed: /ˈhaɪspiːd/ | adj. |高速的，高速度的 |  Describes something that operates or moves at a very high speed.  It is often used to describe trains, internet connections, and data transfer. | 例句：The highspeed train arrived on time. 这列高速列车准时到达。

The highspeed internet connection allowed for seamless video conferencing. 高速互联网连接允许无缝视频会议。

| 近义词：fast, rapid, swift, express | 反义词：slow, sluggish | 词性:形容词
