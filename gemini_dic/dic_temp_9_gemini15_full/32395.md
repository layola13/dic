chilli: /ˈtʃɪli/ | n. adj. |辣椒；辣椒粉；辛辣的 |  A type of pepper, typically small and round, that is usually red or green and has a pungent flavor.  It can refer to the fruit itself, the powder made from the dried fruit, or the characteristic spicy flavor. |例句：I added some chilli to the soup to make it spicier. 我在汤里加了一些辣椒，使它更辣。


例句：This chilli powder is very hot.  这种辣椒粉很辣。

近义词：pepper, red pepper, cayenne pepper, hot pepper
反义词：mild, bland

词性: 名词，形容词
