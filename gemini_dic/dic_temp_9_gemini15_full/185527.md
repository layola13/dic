revalidations:/ˌriːvælɪˈdeɪʃənz/ | n. |重新验证；复查；再确认 | The act of validating something again; a second or subsequent validation.  It often implies a previous validation has been questioned or needs updating. |例句：The revalidations of the test results took several days.  重新验证测试结果花费了好几天。

例句：The company's security protocols require regular revalidations of user access.  公司的安全协议要求定期重新验证用户访问权限。


近义词：re-checks, verifications, confirmations, reassessments


反义词：invalidations, cancellations
