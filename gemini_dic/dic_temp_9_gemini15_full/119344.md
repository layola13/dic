词源:

The word "microchemical" is a combination of "micro-" (meaning "small") and "chemical" (relating to chemistry).  The prefix "micro-" comes from the Greek word "mikros," meaning "small."  Therefore, microchemical refers to chemical processes or analyses performed on a very small scale.
