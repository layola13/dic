landscapes:/ˈlændskeɪps/| n.|风景，景色；地貌；景观|The word "landscape" refers to a view or prospect of a region, especially one of a picturesque or scenic quality.  It can also refer to the overall visual appearance of a place, including its natural features and human-made structures.  Furthermore, it can be used in a more abstract sense to describe a particular situation or field of activity.|例句：The rolling hills and lush green fields created a breathtaking landscape.  连绵起伏的丘陵和郁郁葱葱的田野构成了一幅令人叹为观止的风景。

The artist captured the rugged landscape of the mountains in his painting.艺术家在他的画作中捕捉到了崎岖的山区景色。|近义词：scenery, vista, view, panorama|反义词：None directly, but could use words implying lack of visual interest like "monotony", "uniformity".|名词
