dessert:/dɪˈzɜːrt/ | n. | 甜点 | A sweet course served at the end of a meal.  Dessert is typically something sweet, such as cake, pie, ice cream, or pudding.  It's often served after the main course of a meal. | Example:  I'll have the chocolate cake for dessert. = 我要巧克力蛋糕当甜点。


Example: We finished the meal with a delicious fruit dessert. = 我们用美味的水果甜点结束了这顿饭。


近义词: sweet, afters, pudding


反义词: None (no direct opposite, but could be considered opposite to the main course)


词性: noun
