predrafts:/ˈpriːdræfts/| n. |预稿；初稿|A predraft is a preliminary version of a document, typically created before the final draft is written. It serves as a foundation for further development and refinement.|例句：The author spent weeks working on the predraft of his novel before submitting it for feedback. 作者在提交小说以获取反馈之前，花了几个星期的时间来撰写初稿。|近义词：draft, preliminary version, first draft|反义词：final draft, finished product
