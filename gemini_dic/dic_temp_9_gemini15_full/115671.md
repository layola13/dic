margarines:/ˈmɑːrɡərɪnz/ | n. |人造黄油，玛琪琳 |A food product made from vegetable oils or animal fats, used as a substitute for butter.  It is typically made by emulsifying vegetable oils with water, milk solids, and salt. |例句：Many people use margarines as a healthier alternative to butter. 很多人使用人造黄油作为黄油的更健康替代品。|例句：The recipe calls for two tablespoons of margarines. 这个食谱需要两汤匙人造黄油。|近义词：butter substitutes, spreads |反义词：butter
