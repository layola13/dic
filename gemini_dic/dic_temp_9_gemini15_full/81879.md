grieved:/ɡriːvd/| v. |悲伤的；感到悲痛的 |To feel intense sorrow, especially over a loss.  |例句：She grieved deeply over the loss of her pet dog. 她为失去心爱的宠物狗而深感悲痛。
例句：The family grieved the death of their patriarch.  家人为家长的去世而悲痛。|近义词：mourned, sorrowed, lamented |反义词：rejoiced, celebrated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grieves for his lost love. = 他为失去的爱人而悲伤。
They grieve over the loss of their home. = 他们为失去家园而悲伤。


一般过去时:
She grieved for many months after her husband's death. = 她丈夫去世后悲伤了好几个月。
We grieved the loss of our childhood friend. = 我们为失去童年好友而悲痛。


现在完成时:
They have grieved over this tragedy for years. = 他们为这场悲剧悲伤了好几年了。
She has grieved the loss of her father since he passed away. = 自从父亲去世后，她就一直为失去父亲而悲伤。


过去完成时:
They had grieved for a long time before they finally started to heal. = 他们在最终开始痊愈之前已经悲伤了很久。
She had grieved the loss of her job before she found a new one. = 在找到新工作之前，她一直为失去工作而悲伤。


将来时:
He will grieve deeply when he learns of the news. = 当他得知这个消息时，他会非常悲伤。
They will grieve the loss of their community center. = 他们将为社区中心消失而悲伤。


虚拟语气:
If I lost my family, I would grieve terribly. = 如果我失去我的家人，我会非常悲痛。
I wish I hadn't grieved so much over such a small matter. = 我真希望我没有为这么小的一件事悲伤那么多。


被动语态:
The loss was deeply grieved by the entire community. = 整个社区都为这场损失深感悲痛。（较少用被动语态）
The death of the artist was widely grieved. = 艺术家的去世受到了广泛的哀悼。（较少用被动语态）


疑问句:
Does he still grieve for his lost friend? = 他还在为失去的朋友而悲伤吗？
Are they grieving the loss of their business? = 他们正在为失去生意而悲伤吗？


否定句:
He did not grieve openly, but his sadness was evident. = 他没有公开悲伤，但他的悲伤是显而易见的。
They did not grieve as much as expected. = 他们没有像预期的那样悲伤。
