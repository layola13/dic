nonindulgently:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdʌldʒəntli/ | adv. | 不纵容地；不迁就地；不放纵地 | Without indulging or giving in to desires or whims; strictly; severely.  |例句：She disciplined her child nonindulgently, prioritizing structure and responsibility. 她对孩子采取不纵容的教育方式，优先考虑纪律和责任。

例句：He reviewed the proposal nonindulgently, pointing out its flaws without hesitation. 他不迁就地审查了提案，毫不犹豫地指出了其缺陷。


近义词：strictly, severely, austerely, rigorously, uncompromisingly

反义词：indulgently, leniently, permissively, tolerantly


词源：

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not"
* **indulge**: verb meaning "to allow oneself to enjoy the pleasure of; to give way to; to gratify"  (源自古法语 *endulger*,  最终源自拉丁语 *indulgere*  "to yield to; to grant")
* **-ently**: adverbial suffix
