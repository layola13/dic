forsooth:/fɔːrˈsuːθ/| adv. |的确，实在，真正地 |Truly; indeed; in truth.  The word implies a slightly archaic or formal tone, often suggesting a degree of emphasis or even ironic understatement. |例句：Forsooth, my lord, I have seen it with my own eyes.  的确，老爷，我亲眼所见。

例句：Forsooth, the rumors are true; the king is dead.  的确，谣言是真的；国王死了。


近义词：indeed, truly, verily, in truth, actually


反义词：falsely, untruthfully, falsely


词性:副词
