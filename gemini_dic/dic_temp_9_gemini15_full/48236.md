decontaminating: /ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 去污，净化，消毒 | The process of removing contaminants from something.  This is the present participle form of the verb "decontaminate". | 例句：The team is decontaminating the area after the chemical spill.  团队正在化学品泄漏后对该区域进行净化。

The process of decontaminating the equipment took several hours.  净化设备的过程花费了几个小时。


近义词：purifying, sterilizing, cleaning, sanitizing | 反义词：contaminating, polluting | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are decontaminating the site. = 他们正在净化现场。
We decontaminate all tools before use. = 我们在使用所有工具之前都会对其进行消毒。

一般过去时:
They decontaminated the area quickly. = 他们迅速净化了该区域。
The workers decontaminated the spill site. = 工人们净化了泄漏现场。

现在完成时:
They have decontaminated the hospital ward. = 他们已经净化了医院病房。
We have decontaminated the equipment thoroughly. = 我们已经彻底净化了设备。

过去完成时:
They had decontaminated the lab before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经净化了实验室。
The team had decontaminated the site by the time the emergency services arrived. = 急救人员到达时，团队已经净化了现场。

将来时:
They will decontaminate the affected area tomorrow. = 他们明天将净化受影响的区域。
We will decontaminate the equipment after each use. = 我们将在每次使用后对设备进行消毒。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would decontaminate everything properly. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们会妥善地净化一切。
It is important that the equipment be decontaminated before reuse. = 设备在再次使用前必须进行消毒非常重要。

被动语态:
The area is being decontaminted. = 该区域正在被净化。
The equipment was decontaminated yesterday. = 设备昨天被净化了。

疑问句:
Are they decontaminating the building? = 他们正在净化建筑物吗？
Was the site properly decontaminated? = 该地点是否被妥善净化？

否定句:
They did not decontaminate the area sufficiently. = 他们没有充分净化该区域。
The equipment was not decontaminated properly. = 设备没有被正确地消毒。


PHRASE->
decontaminating agent = 去污剂
decontaminating procedure = 去污程序
decontaminating equipment = 去污设备
decontaminating solution = 去污溶液
decontaminating process = 去污过程


词源:
de- (away from, completely) + contaminate (to make impure) + -ing (present participle)  de- 表示“除去”，contaminate 来自拉丁语 contaminare，意为“弄脏”，-ing 为现在分词后缀。
