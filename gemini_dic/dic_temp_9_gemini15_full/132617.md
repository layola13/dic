nondisadvantaged:/ˌnɒnˌdɪsədˈvɑːntɪdʒd/ | adj. |未受歧视的，不受不利影响的 |Not experiencing or subject to disadvantage.  This term is used to describe individuals or groups who haven't faced systemic or significant obstacles due to factors like race, gender, socioeconomic status, or disability.  It emphasizes the absence of disadvantage, rather than the presence of advantage. | Example: The study focused on the academic performance of nondisadvantaged students.  这项研究关注的是未受歧视的学生的学业成绩。 The program aims to support nondisadvantaged children in accessing quality education. 该项目旨在支持未受歧视的儿童获得优质教育。 |近义词:privileged, advantaged, favored |反义词:disadvantaged, deprived, underprivileged |形容词
