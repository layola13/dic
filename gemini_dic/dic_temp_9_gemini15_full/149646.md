painting:/ˈpeɪntɪŋ/| n.|绘画，油画；涂漆|A painting is a work of art created using paint, typically on a canvas or other surface.  It can be a representation of something real or imaginary, or an abstract expression of color and form. |例句：The museum has a remarkable collection of 19th-century paintings.  这个博物馆收藏了许多19世纪的油画。|例句：He spent the afternoon painting the fence. 他下午花了时间粉刷篱笆。|近义词：picture, artwork, canvas|反义词：sculpture (反义词并非完全对等，而是指不同艺术形式)|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He paints landscapes. = 他画风景画。
She is painting a portrait. = 她正在画一幅肖像画。

一般过去时:
They painted the house blue. = 他们把房子漆成蓝色。
I painted a picture of my cat. = 我画了一幅我猫的画。

现在完成时:
He has painted many masterpieces. = 他已经创作了许多杰作。
She has painted the entire room. = 她已经把整个房间都粉刷好了。

过去完成时:
They had painted the mural before the festival. = 他们在大节日前就画好了壁画。
She had painted several canvases before starting her latest work. = 在开始创作她的最新作品之前，她已经画了几幅画布。

将来时:
I will paint a picture for you. = 我会为你画一幅画。
They will be painting the bridge next week. = 他们下周将粉刷这座桥。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would paint every day. = 如果我有更多时间，我每天都会画画。
I wish I had painted more during my youth. = 我希望我年轻时画得更多。

被动语态:
The painting was damaged in the fire. = 这幅画在火灾中受损。
The house is being painted at the moment. = 房子正在被粉刷。

疑问句:
Who painted this masterpiece? = 谁画的这幅杰作？
What are you painting? = 你在画什么？

否定句:
He doesn't paint very often. = 他不经常画画。
She didn't paint the whole picture. = 她没有画完整幅画。
