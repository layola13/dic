reamended:/rɪˈɛndɪd/| adj.|修订的；经修改的；推荐的|Amended means modified or improved.  It often refers to a text, law, or proposal that has been changed or corrected.  The word also implies a suggestion or recommendation for improvement.|例句：The amended bill was finally passed by the Senate.  经修改的法案最终由参议院通过了。|例句：The doctor recommended an amended diet plan. 医生推荐了一个修改后的饮食计划。|近义词：revised, corrected, modified, improved, recommended|反义词：original, unchanged


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee amends the rules regularly. = 委员会定期修改规则。
He amends his report based on new data. = 他根据新的数据修改了他的报告。

一般过去时:
They amended the contract last week. = 他们上周修改了合同。
She amended her statement after realizing a mistake. = 她意识到错误后修改了她的声明。

现在完成时:
The company has amended its policy on employee benefits. = 公司已经修改了其员工福利政策。
We have amended the schedule to accommodate everyone. = 我们已经修改了时间表以适应所有人。

过去完成时:
They had amended the proposal before submitting it. = 他们在提交提案之前已经修改了它。
She had amended her application before resubmitting it. = 她在再次提交申请之前已经修改了它。

将来时:
The government will amend the tax laws next year. = 政府明年将修改税法。
They will amend the document before publication. = 他们在出版前将修改该文件。


虚拟语气:
If they amended the rules, it would be fairer. = 如果他们修改了规则，就会更公平。
I wish they had amended the contract earlier. = 我希望他们早点修改合同。

被动语态:
The document was amended by the legal team. = 文件由法律团队修改。
The rules are amended periodically. = 规则定期修改。

疑问句:
Has the proposal been amended? = 提案是否已被修改？
Will the law be amended soon? = 法律很快就会被修改吗？

否定句:
The contract was not amended. = 合同没有被修改。
They did not amend their report. = 他们没有修改他们的报告。
