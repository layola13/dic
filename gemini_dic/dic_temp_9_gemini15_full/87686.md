hexamerism:/ˈhɛksəmərɪzəm/| n. |六聚体现象；六聚体形成|The formation of a hexamer, a molecule composed of six subunits.  Hexamerism is observed in various biological systems, such as the arrangement of proteins in certain viruses or the structure of some enzymes. |Example: The study explored the hexamerism of the viral capsid protein.  这项研究探讨了病毒衣壳蛋白的六聚体现象。|近义词：oligomerization, polymerization|反义词：monomerism (less common, implies formation of single units)
