cobnut: /ˈkoʊbnʌt/ | n. |榛子；小型坚果| A small, round nut, typically brown in color, with a hard shell and a sweet, edible kernel. Cobnuts are similar to hazelnuts but generally larger and more rounded. They are often used in confectionery and baking.|例句：The children gathered cobnuts from the hedgerow.孩子们从树篱里收集榛子。|例句：She added chopped cobnuts to the cake batter.她往蛋糕糊里加了切碎的榛子。|近义词：hazelnut, filbert |反义词：None specifically;  perhaps "large nut" in a comparative sense.
