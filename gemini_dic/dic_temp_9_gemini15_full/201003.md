slogans:/ˈsloʊɡənz/ | n. |口号，标语 | A slogan is a short and memorable phrase used in advertising or political campaigns to attract attention or promote a product, idea, or cause.  It is designed to be easily remembered and repeated. | Example: "Just do it" is a famous slogan for Nike.  “Just do it”是耐克著名的口号。|近义词：motto, catchphrase, tagline |反义词：无
