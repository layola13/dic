climaxed:/ˈklaɪmækst/| v.|达到高潮；结束|To reach the highest point of intensity or excitement; to culminate.|The movie climaxed with a thrilling car chase.  这部电影以惊险的汽车追逐达到高潮。|The tension climaxed in a dramatic confrontation. 紧张局势在戏剧性的对抗中达到高潮。|culminated, peaked|anticlimactic


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The story climaxes with a surprising twist. = 故事以令人惊讶的转折达到高潮。
The concert usually climaxes with a firework display. = 音乐会通常以烟火表演达到高潮。


一般过去时:
