interlocks:/ˈɪntərˌlɒks/| v. n.|互锁，连锁；紧密结合|To connect or lock together, often intricately.  It can refer to physical objects fitting together securely, or to systems or processes that are interdependent.|例句：The gears interlocked perfectly. 齿轮完美地互锁在一起。 The two companies' strategies interlocked, creating a powerful synergy. 两家公司的战略相互关联，产生了强大的协同效应。|近义词：intertwine, connect, link, mesh|反义词：disconnect, separate, detach|动词，名词

词源:
The word "interlock" is a combination of the prefix "inter-" (meaning "between" or "among") and the verb "lock," ultimately deriving from Old English "loc."  The meaning evolved to describe the reciprocal action of locking together.
