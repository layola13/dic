natters:/ˈnætərz/| v.|唠叨；喋喋不休；闲聊 | to talk continuously and inconsequentially, especially about unimportant things. |例句：He natters on endlessly about his garden. 他没完没了地唠叨他的花园。|例句：She was nattering away to her friend on the phone. 她一直在电话里和她朋友喋喋不休地闲聊。|近义词：chatter, babble, prattle, blabber |反义词：be silent, be quiet

She natters to her cat for hours. = 她对着她的猫唠叨几个小时。
