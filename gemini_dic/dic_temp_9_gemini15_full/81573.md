grazings:/ˈɡreɪzɪŋz/| n.|放牧；牧草；啃食|The act of grazing animals, or the land used for grazing.  It can also refer to the act of lightly touching or brushing against something.|例句：The sheep's grazings were lush and green.  绵羊的牧场青翠欲滴。
The artist's brushstrokes were light, almost like grazings. 艺术家的笔触很轻，几乎像是轻轻擦过。|近义词：pasture, foraging, browsing|反义词：cultivation


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The cattle graze in the pasture every day. 牛每天都在牧场吃草。
The sheep are grazing peacefully in the field.  羊儿们在田野里平静地吃草。


一般过去时:
The goats grazed on the hillside yesterday. 山羊昨天在山坡上吃草。
The cows grazed contentedly in the meadow. 奶牛心满意足地在草地上吃草。


现在完成时:
The horses have grazed most of the grass in the paddock. 马已经吃掉了围场里大部分的草。
The sheep have been grazing here for weeks.  羊群在这里吃草已经有几周了。


过去完成时:
The deer had grazed all the leaves before the storm. 鹿在暴风雨来临之前已经吃掉了所有的树叶。
The cattle had already grazed the field before we arrived. 在我们到达之前，牛群已经吃完了田里的草。


将来时:
The farmers will rotate the cattle's grazing areas next month. 农民们下个月将轮换牛的放牧区域。
The sheep will graze in this field for the rest of the summer. 羊群将在余下的夏季在这个田野里吃草。


虚拟语气:
If the sheep had grazed less, there would be more grass left. 如果羊吃得少一些，就会剩下更多的草。
I wish the goats hadn't grazed so close to the house. 我真希望山羊不要在离房子这么近的地方吃草。


被动语态:
The pasture was grazed heavily last year.  去年牧场被过度放牧了。
The field is being grazed by the herd.  田地正在被牛群啃食。


疑问句:
Are the cows grazing in the north field? 牛在北边的田里吃草吗？
Have the sheep been grazing near the river? 羊群一直在河边吃草吗？


否定句:
The goats didn't graze in the garden this morning. 山羊今天早上没有在花园里吃草。
The cattle haven't grazed this area yet. 牛群还没有吃过这个区域的草。
