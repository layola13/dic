riposted:/rɪˈpɒstɪd/ | v. | 反驳；回击；迅速反击 | To reply quickly and sharply, especially in a witty or effective way. |He riposted with a clever remark that silenced his opponent. 他以一句巧妙的评论回击了他的对手，使对手哑口无言。| retorted, rejoined, answered back |agreed, conceded | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He ripostes with witty remarks. = 他用俏皮话反驳。
She ripostes to every criticism. = 她对每一次批评都进行反驳。

一般过去时:
He riposted sharply to the accusation. = 他尖锐地反驳了这一指控。
She riposted with a well-aimed argument. = 她以一个有力的论点回击。

现在完成时:
He has riposted effectively to all challenges. = 他有效地应对所有挑战。
She has riposted to the rumors with a strong statement. = 她以强烈的声明回应了谣言。

过去完成时:
He had riposted before she could finish her sentence. = 在她说完话之前，他已经反驳了。
She had riposted several times during the debate. = 在辩论中，她已经几次反驳了。

将来时:
He will riposte with evidence to support his claim. = 他将拿出证据来支持他的说法。
She will riposte forcefully to any unfair treatment. = 她将有力地反击任何不公平待遇。

虚拟语气:
