heterocarpous:/ˌhɛtərəˈkɑːrpəs/| adj.|异果的，具有不同果实的|Producing or having fruits of more than one kind;  characterized by the production of different kinds of fruits on the same plant. This often arises from variations in the pollination process or environmental factors.|例句：The plant exhibited a heterocarpous fruiting pattern, producing both large and small berries. 这株植物呈现出异果的结实模式，产生了大小不同的浆果。

The species is known for its heterocarpous nature, with fruits varying in size and shape depending on the location on the plant.  该物种以其异果的特性而闻名，果实的大小和形状取决于植物上的位置。|近义词：polycarpous (多果的), diverse-fruited|反义词：homocarpous (同果的), uniform-fruited|词性:形容词
