rede:/riːd/| n. |赎回，补偿；兑换；赎金|The act of redeeming something, such as a pledge or a promise.  It can also refer to the act of exchanging something, typically a coupon or voucher, for goods or services.  Or it can mean the money paid to release a person or thing from captivity or a lien.|例句：The company offered a rede on their faulty product. = 公司为其有缺陷的产品提供了补偿。

例句：He paid the rede to free his brother from prison. = 他付了赎金把弟弟从监狱里赎了出来。

近义词：ransom, compensation, repayment, redemption

反义词：forfeiture, loss


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bank redeems the bonds. = 银行赎回债券。
She redeems her coupons for a free coffee. = 她用优惠券兑换一杯免费咖啡。

一般过去时:
He redeemed his promise. = 他履行了诺言。
They redeemed the hostages. = 他们赎回了人质。

现在完成时:
He has redeemed himself through hard work. = 他通过努力工作赎回了自己。
She has redeemed all her reward points. = 她已经兑换了她所有的奖励积分。

过去完成时:
He had redeemed his debt before he died. = 他死前已经还清了债务。
They had redeemed the painting from the auction house. = 他们已从拍卖行赎回了这幅画。


将来时:
She will redeem the voucher tomorrow. = 她明天将兑换代金券。
He will redeem his pledge to donate to charity. = 他将兑现向慈善机构捐款的承诺。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would redeem my watch from the pawn shop. = 如果我有钱，我会从当铺赎回我的手表。
I wish I could redeem the lost time. = 我真希望能够弥补失去的时间。


被动语态:
The bonds were redeemed by the bank. = 债券被银行赎回。
The hostages were redeemed after long negotiations. = 人质经过长时间的谈判后被赎回。


疑问句:
Can I redeem this coupon here? = 我可以在这里兑换这张优惠券吗？
How can I redeem my loyalty points? = 我怎样才能兑换我的积分？


否定句:
