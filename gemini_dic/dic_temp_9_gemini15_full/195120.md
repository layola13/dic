semicontinuously: /ˌsɛmɪkənˈtɪnjuəsli/ | adv. | 半连续地，间歇地 |  Describes something that happens or is done intermittently, not continuously but with some regularity. It suggests a pattern of stops and starts, rather than a constant, unbroken process. |

例句：

The machine operates semicontinuously, pausing briefly between cycles. = 机器半连续运行，循环之间短暂暂停。

The experiment was conducted semicontinuously over a period of several weeks.  = 实验在几周内间歇性地进行。


近义词：intermittently, sporadically, periodically, discontinuously (with nuances in frequency and regularity)


反义词：continuously, constantly, incessantly


词性：副词
