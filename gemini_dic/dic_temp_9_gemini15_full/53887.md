discordant:/ˈdɪskɔːrdənt/| adj.|不和谐的；不一致的；刺耳的 |Disagreeing or incongruous.  Characterized by a harsh, jarring, or unpleasant sound.|The discordant notes of the out-of-tune piano jarred on the ears.  不和谐的钢琴音刺耳难听。|The two accounts of the incident were completely discordant. 这两起事件的描述完全不一致。|harmonious, consonant, consistent | harmonious, consonant, consistent |形容词
