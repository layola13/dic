clifftop:/ˈklɪfˌtɒp/ /ˈklɪfˌtɑːp/| n.|悬崖顶，峭壁顶端|A clifftop is the top or edge of a cliff.  It often offers stunning views.|例句：We walked along the clifftop path, enjoying the breathtaking ocean views. 我们沿着悬崖顶的小路走，欣赏着令人叹为观止的海景。

例句：The clifftop restaurant offered panoramic views of the coastline. 悬崖顶上的餐厅提供了海岸线的全景视野。


近义词：cliff edge, precipice top, headland


反义词：cliff bottom, cliff base


词性:名词
