oestrogenicity: /ˌɛstrəʊdʒɪˈnɪsəti/ | n. | 雌激素活性；雌激素作用 | The ability of a substance to mimic the effects of estrogen.  This refers to the capacity of a compound to bind to estrogen receptors and exert estrogenic effects, potentially impacting the endocrine system.  | Example:  The study investigated the oestrogenicity of various environmental pollutants.  这项研究调查了各种环境污染物的雌激素活性。 |近义词: estrogenic activity, estrogenic effect |反义词: anti-estrogenicity
