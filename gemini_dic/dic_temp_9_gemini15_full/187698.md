roomies:/ˈruːmiːz/| n.|室友，同住者|People who share a room or a house.  Roomies are individuals who live together, often sharing expenses and responsibilities.|例句：My roomies and I are throwing a party this weekend.我的室友们和我这个周末要开个派对。|例句：Finding good roomies can be challenging. 找到好的室友可能很有挑战性。|近义词：housemates, flatmates|反义词：None (反义词不适用)|名词
