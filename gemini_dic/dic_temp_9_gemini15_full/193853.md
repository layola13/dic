self-disapproval: /ˌsɛlf dɪsˈpruːvəl/ | n. |自我厌恶；自我谴责 |  The feeling of dissatisfaction or condemnation of oneself; a negative self-evaluation. It often stems from perceived failures, shortcomings, or actions that violate one's personal values or standards.  |  例句：His self-disapproval was palpable after he failed the exam. 他考试不及格后，自我厌恶之情溢于言表。

例句：She felt intense self-disapproval for her outburst at the meeting.  她在会议上爆发后，感到强烈的自我谴责。


近义词：self-condemnation, self-reproach, self-criticism, guilt, shame | 反义词：self-acceptance, self-love, self-esteem


词性：名词
