placate:/ˈpleɪkeɪt/ | vt. | 安抚；抚慰；劝慰 | to make someone less angry or hostile |  He tried to placate his angry wife with flowers. 他试图用鲜花来安抚他生气的妻子。 | appease, soothe, mollify | antagonize, provoke, irritate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He placates his boss with flattery. = 他用奉承来讨好他的老板。
She placates her crying baby with a bottle. = 她用奶瓶安抚她哭闹的婴儿。

一般过去时:
The manager placated the angry customer with a refund. = 经理通过退款安抚了愤怒的顾客。
He placated his friend by apologizing sincerely. = 他真诚地道歉来安抚他的朋友。

现在完成时:
She has placated her parents by getting good grades. = 她通过取得好成绩来安抚了她的父母。
They have placated the situation by negotiating a compromise. = 他们通过协商妥协来平息了局势。

过去完成时:
He had placated the crowd before the speech began. = 演讲开始前他已经安抚了人群。
She had placated her anxieties by taking a deep breath. = 她通过深呼吸来平息了她的焦虑。


将来时:
They will placate the protesters with promises of reform. = 他们将通过承诺改革来安抚抗议者。
I will placate my hunger with a snack. = 我将用零食来缓解我的饥饿。

虚拟语气:
If I had apologized, I might have placated him. = 如果我道歉了，或许就能安抚他了。
I wish I could have placated her grief. = 我希望我能安抚她的悲伤。


被动语态:
The angry mob was placated by the police. = 愤怒的暴徒被警察安抚了。
The situation was placated by a timely intervention. = 局势因及时的干预而平息了。


疑问句:
How can we placate the shareholders? = 我们如何才能安抚股东？
Will a gift placate her? = 礼物能安抚她吗？


否定句:
His apology did not placate her. = 他的道歉并没有安抚她。
Nothing could placate the angry mob. = 没有任何东西能够安抚愤怒的暴徒。


PHRASE->

placate public opinion = 安抚公众舆论
placate the opposition = 安抚反对者
placate a restless crowd = 安抚不安的人群
