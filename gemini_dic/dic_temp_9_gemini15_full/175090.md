radish:/ˈrædɪʃ/| n. |小萝卜，萝卜|A small, round root vegetable with a pungent flavor, typically red or white.|例句：I like to add thinly sliced radishes to my salads. 我喜欢在我的沙拉里加入薄薄的萝卜片。
例句：The farmer grew a variety of radishes, including red and white ones.  这位农民种植了各种萝卜，包括红色和白色的。|近义词：turnip (though larger and less pungent)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with sweeter root vegetables like carrots.
