disappointed:/ˌdɪs.əˈpɔɪn.tɪd/| adj. |失望的，沮丧的 |Feeling unhappy because something you hoped for did not happen or was not as good as you expected.  |例句：I was disappointed with the film. = 我对这部电影感到失望。

例句：He was deeply disappointed by the results of the election. =他对选举的结果深感失望。


近义词：let down, disheartened, frustrated, dejected |反义词：pleased, satisfied, delighted, content |词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am disappointed with the results. =我对结果感到失望。
She is disappointed in her performance. =她对自己的表现感到失望。


一般过去时:
I was disappointed by his behavior. =我对他行为感到失望。
They were disappointed that they missed the train. =他们因为错过了火车而感到失望。


现在完成时:
I have been disappointed many times in my life. =我一生中多次感到失望。
She has been disappointed with her new job. =她对她的新工作感到失望。


过去完成时:
I had been disappointed before, so I was prepared for the worst. =我以前曾失望过，所以我做好了最坏的打算。
They had been disappointed with the service several times. =他们对服务感到失望过好几次。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
I would be disappointed if you didn't come to my party. =如果你不来参加我的派对，我会很失望。
I wish I hadn't been so disappointed. =我希望我当时没有那么失望。


被动语态:
No one wants to be disappointed. =没有人想失望。
His hopes were disappointed. =他的希望落空了。


疑问句:

否定句:
