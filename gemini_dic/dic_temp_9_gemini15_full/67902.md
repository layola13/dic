face:/feɪs/| n. v.|脸，表面，外观；面对，正视|The human face is a complex and expressive organ. 人脸是一个复杂而富于表情的器官。  She has a kind face. 她有一张慈祥的脸。|近义词：countenance, visage, expression|反义词：back, rear


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He faces many challenges. = 他面临许多挑战。
I face the rising sun every morning. = 我每天早晨面对冉冉升起的太阳。


一般过去时:
They faced the music after their mistake. = 他们犯错后勇敢地承担后果。
She faced her fears and jumped off the cliff. = 她战胜恐惧，跳下了悬崖。


现在完成时:
We have faced numerous obstacles in this project. = 在这个项目中，我们已经克服了许多障碍。
She has faced similar situations before. = 她以前也遇到过类似的情况。


过去完成时:
They had faced numerous setbacks before achieving success. = 他们在取得成功之前经历过无数的挫折。
He had faced a difficult decision before he finally decided. = 在最终做出决定之前，他面临着一个艰难的抉择。


将来时:
We will face many difficulties in the future. = 我们将来会面临许多困难。
She will face a tough opponent in the next round. = 在下一轮比赛中，她将面对一个强劲的对手。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't face him directly. = 如果我是你，我不会直接面对他。
I wish I hadn't faced that difficult situation. = 我真希望我没有遇到那种困境。


被动语态:
The problem must be faced immediately. = 这个问题必须立即解决。
The issue was faced by the committee last week. = 上周委员会讨论了这个问题。


疑问句:
Which way does the building face? = 这栋建筑朝哪个方向？
What does she face now? = 她现在面临什么？


否定句:
He didn't face the consequences of his actions. = 他没有承担自己行为的后果。
They didn't face the challenges bravely. = 他们没有勇敢地面对挑战。
