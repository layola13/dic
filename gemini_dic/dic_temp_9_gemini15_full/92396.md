hypodermic:/ˌhaɪpəˈdɜːrmɪk/| adj., n.|皮下的；皮下注射的；皮下注射器| Relating to or denoting the area beneath the skin; administered beneath the skin.  A hypodermic needle is used for injecting medicine or other substances beneath the skin.|The doctor administered a hypodermic injection of morphine to relieve the patient's pain. 医生给病人注射了皮下吗啡来缓解疼痛。| subcutaneous, parenteral |  (no direct antonym, but could consider "topical" as opposite in terms of administration)|形容词，名词
