sappy:/ˈsæpi/| adj.|多愁善感的；感伤的；充满感情的；愚蠢的；无味的|excessively sentimental; mawkish; foolish or silly; lacking in taste or flavor |例句：The movie had a sappy ending that made me cry. 这部电影有一个多愁善感的结局，让我哭了。|例句：His sappy poems were full of clichés.  他的伤感诗歌充满了陈词滥调。|近义词：sentimental, mawkish, mushy, sugary |反义词：unsentimental, cynical, hard-boiled, realistic
