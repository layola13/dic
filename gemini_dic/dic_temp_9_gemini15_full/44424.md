cross-pollinates:/krɒsˈpɒləneɪts/ | v. | 异花授粉；交叉授粉；互相影响 | To transfer pollen from the flower of one plant to the flower of another plant of the same species, resulting in cross-fertilization.  This term also applies metaphorically to the exchange of ideas, influences, or practices between different groups or systems. |例句：Bees cross-pollinate flowers as they gather nectar. 蜜蜂在采花蜜时进行异花授粉。The two cultures cross-pollinated, leading to a rich artistic exchange. 这两种文化相互影响，导致了丰富的艺术交流。|近义词：fertilize, hybridize, influence |反义词：self-pollinate
