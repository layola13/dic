hypothetical:/ˌhaɪpəˈθɛtɪkəl/| adj. |假设的，假定的|  Based on a hypothesis; supposed or assumed rather than known to be true.  It refers to something that is theoretical or conceptual, not necessarily real or factual. |例句：The hypothetical scenario was used to test the emergency response system.  假设的情况被用来测试紧急响应系统。

例句：We discussed the hypothetical possibility of a meteor striking Earth. 我们讨论了陨石撞击地球的假设可能性。


近义词：theoretical, assumed, supposed, conjectural, presumptive

反义词：real, actual, factual, concrete


词性：形容词
