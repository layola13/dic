rumour:/ˈruːmə(r)/|/ˈrʊmər/| n. |谣言，传闻 | A rumour is an unconfirmed story or piece of information that is passed from person to person.  Rumours are often based on speculation or hearsay and may or may not be true. |例句：I heard a rumour that he's leaving the company. 我听说有谣言说他要离开公司了。

例句：The rumour spread quickly through the town. 这个谣言迅速传遍了整个城镇。


近义词：gossip, hearsay, report, speculation |反义词：fact, truth |名词
