infecter:/ɪnˈfɛktər/| n. |感染者，感染源 | A person or thing that infects others; someone or something that causes infection.  | Example: The doctor identified the infecter as a contaminated water source.  医生将感染源确定为受污染的水源。|近义词：carrier, source, vector |反义词：cure, remedy |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The virus infects many people every year. = 病毒每年感染许多人。
He infects others with his negativity. = 他以他的消极情绪影响他人。


一般过去时:
The bacteria infected the wound. = 细菌感染了伤口。
She infected her friends with her enthusiasm. = 她用自己的热情感染了她的朋友们。


现在完成时:
The disease has infected thousands of people. = 这种疾病已经感染了成千上万的人。
He has infected the team with his laziness. = 他用自己的懒惰影响了整个团队。


过去完成时:
The doctor realized that the patient had been infected weeks ago. = 医生意识到病人几周前就已经被感染了。
They had infected the entire computer system with a virus. = 他们已经用病毒感染了整个计算机系统。


将来时:
Scientists predict that the new strain will infect many more people. = 科学家预测这种新毒株将感染更多的人。
I fear that his bad attitude will infect the whole department. = 我担心他的坏态度会影响整个部门。


虚拟语气:
If only I hadn't touched that, I wouldn't have been infected. = 要是我没碰那个东西就好了，我就不会被感染了。
If the water were cleaner, fewer people would be infected. = 如果水更干净，被感染的人就会更少。


被动语态:
Many people were infected by the virus last year. = 去年许多人被病毒感染。
The computer system was infected by a malicious program. = 计算机系统被恶意程序感染。


疑问句:
Was the food infected? = 食物被感染了吗？
How many people have been infected? = 有多少人被感染了？


否定句:
The doctor confirmed that the patient was not infected. = 医生证实病人没有被感染。
This plant is not infected by any disease. = 这种植物没有被任何疾病感染。


PHRASE->
infected wound: 感染的伤口

infected person: 感染者
highly infected: 高度感染的
newly infected: 新感染的
