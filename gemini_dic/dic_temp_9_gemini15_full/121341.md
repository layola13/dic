misgivings:/ˌmɪsˈɡɪvɪŋz/| n.|疑虑，担忧，不安|Feelings of doubt or apprehension about something.|例句：She had misgivings about the plan. 她对这个计划有疑虑。|例句：His misgivings were confirmed when the project failed. 当项目失败时，他的担忧得到了证实。|近义词：doubts, anxieties, concerns, apprehensions|反义词：confidence, assurance, certainty|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I have misgivings about the new policy. = 我对新政策心存疑虑。
He always expresses his misgivings openly. = 他总是公开表达他的担忧。


一般过去时:
She had serious misgivings before the surgery. = 她手术前非常担忧。
They voiced their misgivings at the meeting. = 他们在会议上表达了他们的担忧。


现在完成时:
I've had misgivings about this decision for weeks. = 我对这个决定已经担忧了好几个星期了。
Have you had any misgivings about your investment? = 你对你的投资有任何疑虑吗？


过去完成时:
He had had misgivings about the trip, which proved to be true. = 他对这次旅行有过担忧，事实证明他是对的。
They had had misgivings about the candidate's character. = 他们对候选人的品格有过担忧。


将来时:
I will have misgivings about leaving him alone. = 我将会担心把他一个人留下。
She anticipates she will have misgivings about the upcoming exam. = 她预料到她会对即将到来的考试感到担忧。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't have misgivings, I would have accepted the offer. = 如果我没有疑虑的话，我就已经接受了这个提议。
I wish I didn't have such serious misgivings about the future. = 我希望我对未来没有那么严重的担忧。


被动语态:
No misgivings were expressed by the board members. = 董事会成员没有表达任何疑虑。
The plan was abandoned because of the misgivings expressed by the public. = 由于公众表达的担忧，该计划被放弃了。


疑问句:
Do you have any misgivings about this project? = 你对这个项目有任何疑虑吗？
What misgivings do you have concerning the new strategy? = 你对新的策略有什么担忧？


否定句:
I have no misgivings about her abilities. = 我对她能力没有疑虑。
He showed no misgivings in his decision to resign. = 他在决定辞职时没有表现出任何担忧。


PHRASE->
