hopped:/hɑːpt/| /hɒpt/ | v. |跳跃；迅速移动；（使）快速前进|to move by jumping on one leg or with both feet together; to move or travel quickly or suddenly; to cause something to move quickly or suddenly|例句：The rabbit hopped across the field. 兔子跳过田野。

The frog hopped into the pond. 青蛙跳进了池塘。


近义词：jump, leap, skip, spring| 反义词：crawl, creep, stumble

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hops on one foot. 他单脚跳。
The kangaroo hops across the plains. 袋鼠在平原上跳跃。


一般过去时:
I hopped on the bus. 我跳上了公共汽车。
She hopped over the fence. 她跳过了栅栏。


现在完成时:
He has hopped over the hurdle. 他已经跳过了障碍。
The rabbit has hopped into its burrow. 兔子已经跳进了它的洞穴。


过去完成时:
The bird had hopped onto the branch before I could see it.  在我看到它之前，鸟已经跳到了树枝上。
They had hopped off the train before it came to a complete stop. 火车完全停下来之前，他们已经跳下了车。


将来时:
I will hop on the train at 8 a.m. 我将在早上8点跳上火车。
She will hop over the stream easily. 她会轻松地跳过小溪。


虚拟语气:
If I were a bird, I would hop from branch to branch. 如果我是一只鸟，我会从一根树枝跳到另一根树枝。
I wish I could hop as high as that kangaroo. 我真希望我能像那只袋鼠一样跳得那么高。


被动语态:  (hopped is rarely used passively)
The ball was hopped by the child.  球被孩子踢来踢去。（不太常用）
The beanbag was hopped across the room.  沙包被踢过房间。（不太常用）


疑问句:
Did he hop across the street? 他跳过了街道吗？
Will she hop on the train with us? 她会和我们一起上火车吗？


否定句:
He didn't hop on the bus. 他没有跳上公共汽车。
The frog didn't hop into the pond. 青蛙没有跳进池塘。


PHRASE->
hop in:  跳进来
hop on: 跳上
hop off: 跳下
hop over: 跳过
hop to it:  赶快行动


词源:
源自古英语 *hoppan，与古高地德语 *hoppan 和古弗里西亚语 *hoppa 相关，其起源可能来自拟声词。
