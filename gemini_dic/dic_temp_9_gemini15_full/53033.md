diodont: /ˈdaɪoʊdɒnt/ | adj. | 双齿的；具两颗牙齿的 | Having two teeth.  The term typically refers to a specific type of tooth structure in certain animals, often implying a characteristic pair of prominent teeth.  | Example:  The fossil shows evidence of a diodont jaw structure.  化石显示了一种双齿颌结构的证据。 | 近义词: bidental (less common) | 反义词: polydont (many teeth), edentate (toothless)
