flatiron:/ˈflætˌaɪərn/| n. |熨斗；熨衣板；(纽约)熨斗大厦|A flatiron is a triangular-shaped iron used for pressing clothes. It can also refer to a building with a similar shape, famously the Flatiron Building in New York City.  |例句：She used a flatiron to press the wrinkles out of her shirt. 她用熨斗熨平衬衫上的褶皱。

例句：The Flatiron Building is a landmark in New York City.  熨斗大厦是纽约市的标志性建筑。

近义词：iron (for the tool), triangular building

反义词：无
