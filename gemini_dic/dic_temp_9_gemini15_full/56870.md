doryphoros:/ˈdɔːriˌfɔːrɒs/| n. |持矛者；(古希腊)持矛少年像|The word "doryphoros" refers to a classical Greek sculpture, specifically a bronze statue of a young man holding a spear, often considered a canonical example of Classical Greek sculpture.  It is also used to describe similar statues or the type of figure depicted.  The original bronze is lost, but numerous Roman marble copies survive.  The most famous copy is called the "Spear-Bearer".|The Doryphoros is a masterpiece of classical Greek sculpture. = 持矛者是希腊古典雕塑的杰作。|近义词: spearman, hoplite |反义词:None
