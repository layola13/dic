fluoranthene:/ˌfluːəˈræθin/| n. |荧蒽|A polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbon (PAH) with the chemical formula C₁₆H₁₀.  It's a colorless to pale yellow crystalline solid found in coal tar, petroleum, and tobacco smoke. It's also a component of airborne particulate matter and is considered a potential carcinogen. |例句：Studies have shown a correlation between high levels of fluoranthene in soil and increased cancer risk.  研究表明土壤中高浓度的荧蒽与癌症风险增加之间存在关联。

例句：The researchers analyzed the presence of fluoranthene and other PAHs in the river sediment. 研究人员分析了河流沉积物中荧蒽和其他多环芳烃的存在。


近义词：benz[j]aceanthrylene (a related PAH)
