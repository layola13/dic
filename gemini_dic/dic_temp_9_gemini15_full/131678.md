例句：The union's nonconciliatory stance led to a prolonged strike. 工会的强硬立场导致了旷日持久的罢工。


近义词：uncompromising, unyielding, intractable, inflexible, adamant


反义词：conciliatory, appeasing, accommodating, compromising, yielding
