polyphosphoric: /ˌpɒliˈfɒsfɒrɪk/ | adj. | 多磷酸的 |  Relating to or denoting acids containing more than one phosphate group.  Polyphosphoric acids are a group of condensed phosphoric acids with the general formula H<sub>n+2</sub>P<sub>n</sub>O<sub>3n+1</sub>. They are strong acids used in various chemical processes, including dehydration reactions and polymer synthesis.
