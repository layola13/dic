/ˌnɒnˈkærəktərz/ | n. |非字符；非人物角色|Noncharacters refer to elements in a text or system that are not considered characters or figures in the traditional sense.  They might be symbols, punctuation marks, whitespace, or other non-alphanumeric elements.  In a story, noncharacters might be inanimate objects or background details that don't actively participate in the plot. |Example:  In programming, spaces and tabs are considered noncharacters when parsing code.  在编程中，空格和制表符在解析代码时被认为是非字符。  |近义词:non-alphanumeric characters, extraneous elements|反义词: characters, figures, protagonists
