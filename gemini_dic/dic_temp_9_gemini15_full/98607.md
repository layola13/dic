instructive:/ɪnˈstrʌktɪv/| adj.|有益的；教育性的；启发的|Providing knowledge or information; teaching something useful.  The museum's exhibit on ancient civilizations was very instructive.|近义词：educational, informative, enlightening, helpful, useful|反义词：uninformative, unhelpful, useless


例句：

The documentary was highly instructive, offering valuable insights into the subject.  这部纪录片非常有益，提供了对该主题宝贵的见解。

His lecture was instructive and engaging, keeping the audience captivated. 他的演讲既有教育意义又引人入胜，让听众着迷。


词源:

From instruct + -ive.  The verb "instruct" comes from Latin *in- (in, into) + struere (to build, construct, arrange).  The suffix "-ive" forms adjectives.  Therefore, "instructive" literally means "serving to build or construct knowledge."
