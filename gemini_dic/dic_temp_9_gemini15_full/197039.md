shallowing:/ˈʃæləʊɪŋ/ | n. |变浅；变浅的过程|The shallowing of the river made navigation difficult. 河水变浅使得航行困难。|近义词：reduction in depth, lessening of depth|反义词：deepening


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river is shallowing gradually. = 河水正在逐渐变浅。
The lake is shallowing due to drought. = 由于干旱，湖水正在变浅。

一般过去时:
The seabed shallowed significantly after the earthquake. = 地震后海底明显变浅了。
The well shallowed over the years. = 这口井多年来逐渐变浅了。

现在完成时:
The coastal waters have shallowed considerably. = 沿海水域已经相当变浅了。
The reservoir has shallowed due to overuse. = 水库由于过度使用而变浅了。

过去完成时:
The river had shallowed considerably before the drought. = 在干旱之前，河水已经相当变浅了。
The lake had shallowed significantly by the time we arrived. = 当我们到达时，湖水已经明显变浅了。

将来时:
The bay will shallow further due to sedimentation. = 由于沉积作用，海湾将进一步变浅。
The canal will shallow if we don't dredge it regularly. = 如果我们不定期疏浚，运河将会变浅。


虚拟语气:
If the river shallows further, we'll have to find a new source of water. = 如果河水进一步变浅，我们将不得不寻找新的水源。
If the lake had not shallowed, the fish would not have died. = 如果湖水没有变浅，鱼就不会死了。


被动语态:
The channel was shallowed by the recent storms. = 由于最近的风暴，航道变浅了。
The riverbed is being shallowed by the ongoing construction. = 河床正在由于持续的施工而变浅。


疑问句:
Is the sea shallowing near the coast? = 海岸附近的海洋是否正在变浅？
Has the pool shallowed since last summer? = 自从去年夏天以来，游泳池是否变浅了？


否定句:
The lake is not shallowing this year. = 湖水今年没有变浅。
The river didn't shallow significantly during the last rainy season. = 在上次雨季，河水没有明显变浅。


PHRASE->
gradual shallowing = 逐渐变浅
significant shallowing = 明显变浅
coastal shallowing = 海岸变浅
rapid shallowing = 快速变浅
artificial shallowing = 人为变浅


词源:
shallow (adj.) + -ing (suffix indicating a process or action)  浅的 +  表示过程或动作的后缀
