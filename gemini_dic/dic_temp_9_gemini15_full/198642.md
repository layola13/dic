shutdown:/ʃʌtˈdaʊn/ | vt. n. | 关闭；停工；停业 | To stop the operation of (a machine, system, process, etc.).  |例句：The computer system will shutdown automatically at midnight.  电脑系统将在午夜自动关闭。|例句：The factory had to shutdown due to the strike. 工厂由于罢工不得不停工。|近义词：close down, turn off, cease operation |反义词：start up, power on, begin operation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shuts down his computer every night. = 他每天晚上都关掉他的电脑。
The system shuts down automatically after an hour of inactivity. = 系统在一小时不活动后会自动关闭。

一般过去时:
I shut down the server last night. = 我昨晚关掉了服务器。
They shut down the factory for maintenance. = 他们关闭工厂进行维护。

现在完成时:
We have shut down the project. = 我们已经关闭了这个项目。
She has shut down her business. = 她已经关闭了她的生意。

过去完成时:
The power had shut down before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，电力就已经中断。
They had shut down the plant before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，他们就已经关闭了工厂。

将来时:
The government will shutdown the illegal gambling sites. = 政府将关闭非法赌博网站。
We will shutdown the system for upgrades tomorrow. = 我们明天将关闭系统进行升级。

虚拟语气:
If the server were to shutdown, we would lose all our data. = 如果服务器关闭，我们将丢失所有数据。
I wish I had shut down the computer before the power outage. = 我希望停电前我已经关掉了电脑。

被动语态:
The system was shut down remotely. = 系统被远程关闭。
The factory is being shut down due to financial difficulties. = 工厂由于财务困难正在关闭。

疑问句:
Will the system shutdown automatically? = 系统会自动关闭吗？
Did you shutdown the computer before leaving? = 你离开前关电脑了吗？

否定句:
I didn't shutdown the computer properly. = 我没有正确地关闭电脑。
They haven't shut down the project yet. = 他们还没有关闭这个项目。
