issuance:/ˈɪʃuəns/| n. |发行；颁布；发布|The act of issuing something, such as documents, securities, or statements.  It refers to the official process of making something available or putting it into circulation. |例句：The issuance of the new regulations caused considerable confusion. 新规的发布造成了相当大的混乱。

例句：The issuance of the bonds was delayed due to market volatility. 债券的发行由于市场波动而被推迟。


近义词：release, publication, distribution, promulgation

反义词：recall, revocation, cancellation


词性:名词
