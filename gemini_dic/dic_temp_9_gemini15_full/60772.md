electrolyze:/ɪˈlɛktrəlaɪz/ | vt. | 电解 | To decompose (a compound) by passing an electric current through it, typically a solution containing the compound’s ions.  This process involves oxidation at the anode and reduction at the cathode. |例句：We will electrolyze water to produce hydrogen and oxygen. 我们将电解水以产生氢气和氧气。

例句：The chemist electrolyzed the salt solution to obtain pure sodium. 化学家电解盐溶液以获得纯净的钠。


近义词：decompose (in an electrolytic context), dissociate (in an electrolytic context)


反义词：synthesize (in an electrolytic context, referring to the reverse process)


词性:动词
