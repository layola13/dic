gige:/dʒɪɡ/| n.|零工，短期工作；演出；|a short-term job, especially one that is freelance or casual; a performance or show.|例句：She found a gig playing music at a local bar. 她找到了一份在当地酒吧演奏音乐的零工。|例句：The band played a great gig last night. 乐队昨晚演出很棒。|近义词：job, assignment, performance, show|反义词：permanent job, full-time position
