overbravely:/ˌoʊvərˈbreɪvli/ | adv. |过于勇敢地，胆大妄为地 |  Excessively or recklessly brave.  It suggests a bravery that is excessive or ill-advised, potentially leading to negative consequences. |例句：He overbravely charged into the burning building, endangering his own life. 他过于勇敢地冲进燃烧的建筑物，危及了自己的生命。

He acted overbravely, ignoring the clear dangers. 他行事过于鲁莽，无视明显的危险。|近义词：recklessly, rashly, foolhardily,temerariously |反义词：cowardly, timidly, cautiously
