reconvening:/ˌriːkənˈviːnɪŋ/ | v. |重新召开，再次集合 | The act of convening again; assembling or gathering together once more.  |例句：The committee is reconvening next week to discuss the budget. 委员会下周将重新召开会议讨论预算。

例句：After a brief recess, the court reconvened to hear the closing arguments.  短暂休庭后，法庭重新开庭听取结案陈词。


近义词：reassembling, reuniting, regrouping | 反义词： adjourning, dispersing


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee reconvenes every Tuesday. = 委员会每周二重新召开会议。
They reconvene to discuss the proposal. = 他们重新召开会议讨论提案。


一般过去时:
The council reconvened after a long break. = 理事会在长时间休会后重新召开会议。
The meeting reconvened promptly at 2 PM. = 会议准时在下午两点重新召开。


现在完成时:
The court has reconvened to hear further evidence. = 法院已经重新开庭听取进一步的证据。
The group has reconvened several times since the incident. = 自那件事发生以来，该小组已经重新集会过几次。


过去完成时:
They had reconvened before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经重新集会了。
The team had reconvened to review the progress. = 团队已经重新集会以审查进展。


将来时:
The board will reconvene next month to make a decision. = 董事会将于下个月重新召开会议以做出决定。
The conference will reconvene after the lunch break. = 会议将在午休后重新召开。


虚拟语气:
If they reconvened, we could present our findings. = 如果他们重新召开会议，我们就可以提交我们的调查结果。
I wish the committee had reconvened sooner. = 我希望委员会早点重新召开会议。


被动语态:
The meeting was reconvened by the chairman. = 会议由主席重新召开。
The group will be reconvened at a later date. = 该小组将在稍后日期重新召开。


疑问句:
Will the committee reconvene soon? = 委员会很快会重新召开会议吗？
When will the court reconvene? = 法院什么时候重新开庭？


否定句:
The meeting did not reconvene until the next day. = 会议直到第二天才重新召开。
They did not reconvene after the initial postponement. = 他们并没有在最初推迟后重新召开会议。

词源:
re- (prefix indicating repetition) + convene (from Old French convenir, from Latin convenire "to come together," from com- "together" + venire "to come")
