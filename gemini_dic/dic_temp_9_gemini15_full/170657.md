pseudo-Persians:/ˈsuːdoʊ pɜːrʒənz/| adj.|伪波斯；貌似波斯的|Appearing to be Persian but not genuinely so; often used to describe carpets, rugs, or other items that imitate the style or design of Persian items but are of inferior quality or made in a different location.|例句：The rug was advertised as a Persian rug, but it was later revealed to be a pseudo-Persian imitation.|近义词：faux Persian, imitation Persian|反义词：authentic Persian
