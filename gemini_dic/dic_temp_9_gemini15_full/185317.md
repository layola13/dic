retribution:/ˌrɛtrɪˈbjuːʃən/ | n. | 报应；惩罚；报复 | The act of punishing someone for wrongdoing, especially in a severe or disproportionate way.  It often implies a deserved punishment, and can be inflicted by a higher power, a person, or even fate itself. | 例句：The criminal finally faced retribution for his crimes. 罪犯最终受到了应有的惩罚。

例句：The dictator's regime faced retribution for its atrocities. 独裁政权因其暴行而受到了惩罚。


近义词：vengeance, punishment, payback, reprisal, requital | 反义词：forgiveness, pardon, mercy | 词性：名词
