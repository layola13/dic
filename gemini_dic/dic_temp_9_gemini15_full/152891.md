pedata:/pɛˈdeɪtə/| n.|有脚的|A pedate leaf is a compound leaf whose leaflets are arranged in a palmate fashion, resembling a bird's foot.  It is characterized by its divided leaflets that radiate from a common point.|例句：The plant's pedate leaves are a distinctive feature. 这植物的鸟足状叶是其显著特征。|近义词：palmate, digitate|反义词：simple (leaf)|词性:名词


USAGE->
无适用语法讲解，因为pedata本身是一个名词，不作动词使用。


PHRASE->
pedate leaf: 鸟足状叶
pedate tendril: 鸟足状卷须
pedate venation: 鸟足状脉序
palmately pedate:  掌状鸟足状的 (更详细描述叶片结构)
bipinnate pedate: 二回鸟足状的 (更详细描述叶片结构)
