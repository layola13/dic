fast-forward:/ˌfæstˈfɔːrwərd/| vt.|快进；快速前进；提前；|to move quickly to a later point in time or a process|例句：Let's fast-forward to the next chapter.  让我们快进到下一章。
例句：The company decided to fast-forward its expansion plans. 公司决定提前其扩张计划。|近义词：advance, accelerate, expedite|反义词：rewind, regress, delay|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fast-forwards through boring parts of the movie. = 他快进电影中无聊的部分。
They fast-forward their plans to meet the deadline. = 他们加快计划以赶上最后期限。


一般过去时:
I fast-forwarded through the commercials. = 我快进了广告。
She fast-forwarded the meeting to discuss the urgent issue. = 她将会议提前以讨论紧急问题。


现在完成时:
We have fast-forwarded the project by several weeks. = 我们已经将项目提前了几周。
They have fast-forwarded their careers through hard work and dedication. = 他们通过努力工作和奉献精神快速提升了职业生涯。


过去完成时:
He had fast-forwarded the video to find the important clip. = 他已经快进了视频以找到重要的片段。
They had fast-forwarded the negotiations to reach a quick agreement. = 他们已经加快了谈判以达成快速协议。


将来时:
I will fast-forward to the end of the book. = 我将快进到书的结尾。
They will fast-forward the development of the new product. = 他们将加快新产品的开发。


虚拟语气:
If I had a fast-forward button for life, I would use it wisely. = 如果我生活中有一个快进按钮，我会明智地使用它。
I wish I could fast-forward through this difficult period. = 我希望我能度过这段艰难时期。


被动语态:
The meeting was fast-forwarded due to time constraints. = 由于时间限制，会议提前了。
The project is being fast-forwarded to meet the client's demands. = 项目正在被加快以满足客户的需求。


疑问句:
Can you fast-forward the presentation to the key findings? = 你能快进演示文稿到主要发现部分吗？
Should we fast-forward the process to complete it sooner? = 我们应该加快流程以便更快地完成它吗？


否定句:
I didn't fast-forward the movie because I wanted to see everything. = 我没有快进电影，因为我想看所有内容。
They didn't fast-forward their plans, they took a more cautious approach. = 他们没有加快计划，而是采取了更谨慎的方法。
