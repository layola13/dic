livid:/ˈlɪvɪd/| adj.| 青紫的；盛怒的；极度愤怒的 | Extremely angry; furiously enraged;  pale and discolored, as from a bruise.  |例句：He was livid with rage after he found out about the betrayal. 他发现背叛后，气得脸色发青。

例句：The impact left a livid mark on his arm.  撞击在他的手臂上留下了一块青紫的印记。


近义词：furious, enraged, irate, incensed, wrathful, angry

反义词：calm, placid, serene, tranquil, composed


词源:  From Middle English *livid, from Old French *livid, from Latin lividus "lead-colored, bluish, black and blue," from līvor "blueness, paleness." The sense of "extremely angry" developed later, likely from the association of a livid complexion with rage.
