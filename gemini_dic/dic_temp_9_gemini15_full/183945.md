resettable: /ˌriˈsɛtəbəl/ | adj. | 可重置的，可复位的 | Able to be reset; capable of being returned to a previous state or setting.  This adjective describes something that can be easily returned to its original or default state after being changed or altered. |


例句：
The circuit breaker is resettable. = 断路器是可复位的。

This software has a resettable password feature. = 这个软件具有可重置密码的功能。


近义词：reversible, restorable


反义词：irreversible, irretrievable


词性：形容词
