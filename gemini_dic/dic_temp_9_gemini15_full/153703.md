pep-talk:/ˈpɛpˌtɔːk/| n. |激励性讲话，鼓舞士气的话|A pep talk is a short, enthusiastic speech intended to encourage or inspire someone.  It's often given before a competition, important event, or challenging task to boost confidence and motivation.|例句：The coach gave the team a pep talk before the big game. 教练在比赛前给球队做了一次鼓舞士气的讲话。

例句：She needed a pep talk to get herself ready for the presentation. 她需要一次激励性讲话来让自己准备好做演讲。


近义词：encouragement, inspiration, motivation, exhortation, rousing speech|反义词：discouragement, criticism, demotivation
