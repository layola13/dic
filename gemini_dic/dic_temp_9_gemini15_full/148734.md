/ˌoʊvərˈtriːts/| vt.|过度治疗；治疗过度|To treat (a disease or condition) excessively or with unnecessary measures.  This often leads to negative consequences, such as side effects or increased risk of complications. |例句：The doctor overtreats his patients, leading to unnecessary medication.|近义词：overmedicate, overprescribe|反义词：under treat
