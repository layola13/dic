foothills:/ˈfʊthɪlz/| n.|山麓，山脚|The foothills are the lower slopes of a mountain range, lying between the mountains and the plains.  They are often characterized by gentler slopes and less rugged terrain than the mountains themselves. |例句：We hiked through the foothills before reaching the higher peaks. 我们在到达较高的山峰之前徒步穿越了山麓。

例句：The wildflowers bloomed profusely in the foothills in spring. 春天，野花在山麓盛开。


近义词：outskirts, lower slopes, base, foot

反义词：mountain peaks, summit, top


词性：名词
