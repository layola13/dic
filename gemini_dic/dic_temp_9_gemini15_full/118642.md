messing around: /ˈmɛsɪŋ əˈraʊnd/ | v. |胡闹；瞎搞；浪费时间 | To spend time doing unimportant things or activities without a specific purpose; to trifle; to fool around. |例句：Stop messing around and get to work!  别胡闹了，赶紧工作！

例句：They were just messing around in the park. 他们在公园里只是瞎玩。


近义词：fooling around, trifling, idling, playing around, goofing off

反义词：working hard, being serious, being productive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are messing around in the garden. = 他们在花园里瞎玩。
We mess around a lot when we're together. = 我们在一起的时候经常胡闹。


一般过去时:
He messed around all day and didn't finish his homework. = 他整天瞎混，没完成作业。
They messed around with the equipment and broke it. = 他们乱动设备把它弄坏了。


现在完成时:
I've been messing around too much lately and need to focus. = 我最近玩得太久了，需要集中精力。
They have messed around with the settings, and now the system is malfunctioning. = 他们乱改设置，现在系统出故障了。


过去完成时:
He had messed around for years before he finally got serious about his career. = 他混了几年后才认真对待他的事业。
She realized she had messed around too long and missed the deadline. = 她意识到自己玩得太久了，错过了截止日期。


将来时:
I will mess around tomorrow if I finish my work early. = 如果我早点完成工作，我明天会去玩玩。
They will be messing around at the beach all day. = 他们会在海滩上玩一整天。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't messed around so much in college, I would have a better job now. = 如果我大学期间没有那么胡闹，我现在会有一份更好的工作。
I wish I hadn't messed around with that dangerous chemical. = 我真希望我没有乱动那种危险的化学物质。


被动语态:  (Messing around is rarely used passively)  The equipment was messed around with, resulting in damage. 设备被乱动，导致损坏。


疑问句:

否定句:
I don't mess around when it comes to my work. = 我工作时从不马虎。
He didn't mess around; he got straight to the point. = 他没有浪费时间，直接进入主题。
