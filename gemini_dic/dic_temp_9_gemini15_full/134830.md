nonmischievous:/ˌnɒnˈmɪstʃɪvəs/| adj. |不淘气的；不恶作剧的；规矩的|Not mischievous; well-behaved.  This word describes someone or something that is not prone to causing trouble or behaving in a playful but naughty way. It emphasizes the absence of mischievousness.|The nonmischievous child quietly played with his blocks.|近义词：well-behaved, obedient, docile, good, innocent|反义词：mischievous, naughty, troublesome, unruly, wicked
