discomfits:/dɪsˈkʌmfɪts/| v.|使不安；使窘迫；使不舒服 |to cause (someone) to feel uneasy or embarrassed; to make (someone) feel uncomfortable. |例句：The unexpected question discomfited her.  意想不到的问题让她感到不安。  The humid weather discomfited the tourists. 潮湿的天气让游客感到不舒服。|近义词：embarrass, unsettle, disconcert |反义词：comfort, reassure, soothe |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bad news discomfits him. = 这个坏消息让他不安。
Loud noises discomfit the baby. = 吵闹的声音让婴儿感到不安。

一般过去时:
The sudden silence discomfited everyone. = 突然的寂静让每个人都感到不安。
Her critical remarks discomfited the speaker. = 她尖锐的评论让演讲者感到尴尬。

现在完成时:
The constant criticism has discomfited her. = 持续的批评让她感到不安。
His unexpected success has discomfited his rivals. = 他意外的成功让他的对手感到不安。

过去完成时:
He had been discomfited by her sharp tongue before. = 他以前就被她尖刻的言语弄得不安。
They had discomfited the enemy with a surprise attack. = 他们通过突然袭击让敌人措手不及。

将来时:
The upcoming exam will discomfit many students. = 即将到来的考试会让许多学生感到不安。
His speech will discomfit the opposition party. = 他的演讲将会让反对党感到不安。

虚拟语气:
If I told him the truth, it would discomfit him greatly. = 如果我告诉他真相，他会非常不安。
I wish that my mistake hadn't discomfited my friends. = 我真希望我的错误没有让我朋友们感到不安。

被动语态:
He was discomfited by the unexpected turn of events. = 他被出乎意料的事件弄得不安。
The team was discomfited by the opponent's strong performance. = 球队被对手强大的表现打败了（感到不安）。

疑问句:
Does the thought of public speaking discomfit you? = 公开演讲的想法让你不安吗？
Will the new policy discomfit the employees? = 新政策会让员工感到不安吗？

否定句:
The news didn't discomfit her at all. = 这个消息一点也没有让她不安。
The friendly atmosphere didn't discomfit the newcomers. = 友好的氛围并没有让新来的人感到不安。
