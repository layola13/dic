polish-up: /ˈpɑːlɪʃ ʌp/ | vt. |擦亮；改进；润色| to make something cleaner, shinier, or more attractive by polishing it; to improve something by making small changes| 例句：He spent the afternoon polishing up his boots. 他下午花时间擦亮了他的靴子。

例句：She polished up her presentation before the big meeting. 她在大型会议之前润色了她的演示文稿。


近义词：improve, refine, enhance | 反义词：deteriorate, worsen


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He polishes up his shoes every morning. = 他每天早上都擦亮他的鞋子。
She polishes up her writing skills regularly. = 她定期提高她的写作技巧。


一般过去时:
I polished up my old car yesterday. = 我昨天擦亮了我的旧车。
They polished up their business plan before submitting it. = 他们在提交商业计划书之前对其进行了润色。


现在完成时:
He has polished up his presentation for tomorrow's conference. = 他已经为明天的会议润色了他的演示文稿。
She has polished up her resume to apply for the job. = 她已经润色了她的简历以申请这份工作。


过去完成时:
I had polished up my shoes before the interview. = 我在面试前已经擦亮了我的鞋子。
They had polished up their proposal before the client meeting. = 他们在与客户会面之前已经润色了他们的提案。


将来时:
I will polish up my car this weekend. = 我这个周末将擦亮我的车。
She will polish up her speech before the competition. = 她将在比赛前润色她的演讲。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would polish up my essay. = 如果我有更多时间，我会润色我的论文。
I wish I had polished up my presentation better. = 我希望我更好地润色了我的演示文稿。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
