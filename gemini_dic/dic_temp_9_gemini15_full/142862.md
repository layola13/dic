out-jumps:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌmps/|v.|跳出；突然跳出来|The verb "out-jumps" describes the action of jumping out or surpassing someone or something in a jump. It implies a sudden and unexpected movement.|例句：A rabbit out-jumps from the bush.  兔子从灌木丛中跳了出来。

The frog out-jumps the others in the competition. 青蛙在比赛中跳得比其他的远。|近义词：leaps out, springs out, darts out|反义词：jumps in, dives in


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He out-jumps his opponent in every game. 他在每场比赛中都跳得比对手远。
The kangaroo out-jumps the fence. 袋鼠跳过了栅栏。


一般过去时:
The cat out-jumped the dog. 猫跳过了狗。
She out-jumped her personal best. 她跳过了自己的最好成绩。


现在完成时:
He has out-jumped all his competitors this season.  这个赛季他跳得比所有竞争对手都远。
The athlete has out-jumped the previous record. 运动员打破了之前的纪录。


过去完成时:
The horse had out-jumped the hurdle before the rider realised it. 马在骑手意识到之前就已经越过了障碍。
They had out-jumped their expectations in the competition. 他们在比赛中的表现超过了预期。


将来时:
The athlete will out-jump his rival tomorrow. 运动员明天将跳得比他的对手远。
I think the team will out-jump the others in the finals. 我认为这个队将在决赛中跳得比其他队远。


虚拟语气:
If he trained harder, he would out-jump everyone. 如果他训练更刻苦，他就能跳得比任何人都远。
I wish I could out-jump that wall. 我希望我能跳过那堵墙。


被动语态:
He was out-jumped by a younger competitor. 他被一个更年轻的竞争对手超过了。
The record was out-jumped last year.  这个纪录去年被打破了。


疑问句:
Did he out-jump the others? 他跳得比其他人远吗？
Can she out-jump the high jump bar? 她能跳过高跳杆吗？


否定句:
He didn't out-jump his opponent. 他没有跳过他的对手。
She couldn't out-jump the hurdle. 她没能跳过障碍。
