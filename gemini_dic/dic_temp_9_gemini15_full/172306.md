put-to-use: /pʊt tuː juːs/ | v. | 使用；应用；派上用场 | To utilize or employ something for a practical purpose.  |例句：We finally put the old tools to use in the garden. 我们最终在花园里使用了旧工具。

例句：The new software was quickly put to use by the team. 新软件很快就被团队使用了。


近义词：employ, utilize, apply, implement | 反义词：neglect, ignore, abandon | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They put their skills to use in the project. = 他们在项目中运用他们的技能。
We put our knowledge to use every day. = 我们每天都在运用我们的知识。


一般过去时:
She put her creativity to use in designing the dress. = 她运用她的创造力设计了这件衣服。
The company put its resources to use efficiently. = 公司有效地利用了它的资源。


现在完成时:
He has put his experience to use in mentoring young people. = 他运用他的经验指导年轻人。
They have put their new equipment to use successfully. = 他们成功地使用了新设备。


过去完成时:
Before the deadline, they had put all their efforts to use. = 在截止日期之前，他们已经用尽了全力。
She had put her savings to use to buy a house. = 她用积蓄买了一套房子。


将来时:
We will put this new technology to use next year. = 我们明年将使用这项新技术。
They will put their training to use in the upcoming competition. = 他们将在即将到来的比赛中运用他们的训练成果。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would put our ideas to use. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会运用我们的想法。
I wish I could put my knowledge to use in a more meaningful way. = 我希望我能以更有意义的方式运用我的知识。


被动语态:
The resources were put to use effectively. = 资源得到了有效的利用。
The new strategy is being put to use gradually. = 新战略正在逐步实施。


疑问句:
How can we put these resources to use? = 我们如何利用这些资源？
Will this new machine be put to use soon? = 这台新机器很快就会投入使用吗？


否定句:
They didn't put their talents to use. = 他们没有发挥他们的才能。
The plan wasn't put to use because of budget constraints. = 由于预算限制，该计划没有被实施。


PHRASE->
put to good use = 充分利用
put to practical use = 实际应用
put to immediate use = 立即使用
put to the test = 检验；考验
put to shame = 使蒙羞
