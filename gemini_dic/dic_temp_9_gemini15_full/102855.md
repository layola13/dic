jaspoid:/ˈdʒæspɔɪd/| n.|碧玉，玉髓|A jaspoid is a type of chalcedony, a cryptocrystalline variety of quartz, that resembles jasper in appearance.  It's typically opaque and has a rich, varied color due to the inclusion of impurities. Unlike jasper, which is usually massive, jaspoid can occur in various forms, including nodules and veins.|Example: The geologist identified the sample as a jaspoid due to its banded structure and opaque appearance.  这名地质学家根据其带状结构和不透明外观，确定该样本为玉髓。|近义词：jasper, chalcedony|反义词：transparent quartz
