monomineralic: /ˌmɒnəʊmɪnˈærəlɪk/ | adj. | 单矿物的；由单一矿物组成的 | Consisting of only one mineral.  This term is typically used in geology and petrology to describe rocks or other geological materials. | 例句：The rock sample was found to be monomineralic, composed entirely of quartz.  该岩石样本被发现是单矿物的，完全由石英组成。


The igneous rock is monomineralic, exhibiting a uniform texture and composition. 这块火成岩是单矿物的，呈现均匀的质地和成分。| 近义词：unimineralic | 反义词：polymineralic, multimineralic
