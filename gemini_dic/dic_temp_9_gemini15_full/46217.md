cysticercus:/ˌsɪstɪˈsɜːrkəs/| n. |囊尾蚴|A larval stage of a tapeworm, typically found in the tissues of an intermediate host (such as a pig or human). It's a small, bladder-like cyst containing the scolex (head) of the developing tapeworm.  | Example:  The patient's neurological symptoms were caused by cysticercosis, an infection with cysticerci in the brain.  患者的神经系统症状是由囊虫病引起的，这是一种脑内囊尾蚴感染。|近义词: larval tapeworm, bladder worm |反义词: adult tapeworm
