prepar:/prɪˈpɛər/ | vt. |准备，预备 | to make something or someone ready for something that will happen in the future.  |例句：I'm preparing dinner. 我正在准备晚餐。|例句：She is preparing for the exam. 她正在准备考试。|近义词：ready, get ready, arrange |反义词：destroy, ruin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prepares his lessons carefully. = 他认真准备他的课程。
They prepare the food for the party. = 他们为派对准备食物。

一般过去时:
She prepared a delicious meal. = 她准备了一顿美味的饭菜。
We prepared the room for the guests. = 我们为客人准备了房间。


现在完成时:
Have you prepared your presentation? = 你准备好了你的演示文稿了吗？
They have prepared everything for the trip. = 他们已经为旅行准备了一切。


过去完成时:
He had prepared the documents before the meeting. = 他在会议前已准备好了文件。
She had prepared herself mentally for the challenge. = 她已经做好心理准备迎接挑战。


将来时:
I will prepare the report tomorrow. = 我明天将准备报告。
They will prepare a welcome speech for the new employee. = 他们将为新员工准备欢迎词。


虚拟语气:
If I had prepared better, I would have passed the test. = 如果我准备得更好，我就通过考试了。
I wish I had prepared more for the interview. = 我希望我为面试做了更多准备。


被动语态:
The food was prepared by a famous chef. = 这道菜是由一位名厨准备的。
The documents were prepared in advance. = 文件是提前准备好的。


疑问句:
Are you preparing for the marathon? = 你在为马拉松做准备吗？
Have they prepared the materials yet? = 他们准备好材料了吗？


否定句:
I didn't prepare for the quiz. = 我没有为小测验做准备。
They haven't prepared the budget yet. = 他们还没有准备预算。


PHRASE->
prepare for = 为...做准备
prepare to = 准备...
be prepared for = 为...做好准备
prepare a speech = 准备演讲
prepare a meal = 准备一顿饭
