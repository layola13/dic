intensifying: /ɪnˈtɛnsɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 加剧；增强 |  The act of making something more intense or stronger.  It describes the process of increasing the strength, force, or degree of something. |


例句：

The intensifying heat made it difficult to work outdoors. =  越来越热的天气使得户外工作变得困难。

The doctor prescribed medication to stop the intensifying pain. = 医生开了药来止住越来越剧烈的疼痛。


近义词：aggravating, escalating, heightening, amplifying, exacerbating

反义词：decreasing, lessening, diminishing, weakening, mitigating

词性：动词 (present participle of intensify)
