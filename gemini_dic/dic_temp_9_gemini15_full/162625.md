preapplied:/ˌpriːəˈplaɪd/| adj.|预先涂抹的；预先敷设的；预先应用的 | Already applied beforehand; previously applied.  The term usually refers to a substance (like a coating or adhesive) that has been applied before another process or step. |例句：The preapplied adhesive made assembly much faster. 预先涂抹的粘合剂使组装速度快得多。

The preapplied sealant prevented leaks. 预先涂抹的密封剂防止了泄漏。


近义词：precoated, pre-treated, beforehand


反义词：unapplied, post-applied


词性：形容词
