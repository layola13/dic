rougheners: /ˈrʌfənərz/ | n. |粗糙剂，起粗化作用的物质 | Substances or processes that make a surface rough.  Rougheners are used in various applications to increase friction, improve grip, or enhance other surface properties. For example, they might be added to paints to create a textured finish or applied to tools to improve their ability to grip materials. |例句：The manufacturer added rougheners to the paint to improve its adhesion to the metal surface. 制造商在油漆中添加了粗糙剂，以提高其对金属表面的附着力。

The road surface was treated with rougheners to improve traction in icy conditions.  路面经过粗糙剂处理，以提高冰雪条件下的牵引力。|近义词：abrasives, texturizers |反义词：smoothers, polishers
