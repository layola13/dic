ideograph:/ˈaɪdiəɡræf/ | n. |表意文字，语素文字 | A written character that represents an idea or object directly, rather than a sound;  a symbol.  Ideographs are often used in languages such as Chinese and Japanese. | Example:  The Chinese character for "tree" is an ideograph; it doesn't sound like "tree" in any language, but visually represents the concept of a tree.  |近义词：logogram, pictogram | 反义词：alphabet, phonetic character | 词性: 名词
