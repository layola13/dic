micromanipulate:/ˌmaɪkroʊməˈnɪpjuleɪt/| vt.|微操纵；精细操作；过度控制|To control or manipulate something with great precision, often at a very small scale; to manage or control something in an excessive or overly detailed way.|例句：The surgeon used specialized instruments to micromanipulate the delicate tissue.  外科医生使用特制器械来精细操作娇嫩的组织。

例句：The manager's micromanagement style stifled creativity and demoralized the team. 经理过度控制的管理风格扼杀了创造力并使团队士气低落。


近义词：manipulate, control, manage, oversee, direct


反义词：delegate, empower, trust
