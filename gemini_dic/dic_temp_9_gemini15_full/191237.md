scatches:/ˈskætʃɪz/| n.|抓痕，刮痕|Scratches are marks left on a surface by something sharp.|例句：The cat left scratches on the furniture. 猫在家具上留下了抓痕。|近义词：marks, scrapes, abrasions|反义词：smoothness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat scratches the furniture. = 猫抓家具。
He scratches his head in confusion. = 他困惑地挠着头。


一般过去时:
The dog scratched at the door. = 狗抓挠着门。
She scratched her arm on a thorn. = 她的胳膊被荆棘划破了。


现在完成时:
I have scratched my car. = 我把我的车刮花了。
He has scratched the surface of the problem. = 他只触及了问题的表面。


过去完成时:
She had scratched the lottery ticket before realizing it was a winner. = 在意识到中彩之前，她已经把彩票刮开了。
They had scratched the paint off the wall before I arrived. = 我到达之前，他们已经把墙上的油漆刮掉了。


将来时:
I will scratch the itch on my back. = 我会挠挠我的后背痒。
He will scratch the surface of the paint. = 他将会刮掉表面的油漆。


虚拟语气:
If I scratched the lottery ticket, I would be rich. = 如果我刮开彩票，我就会富有。
I wish I hadn't scratched the car. = 我真希望我没有刮花那辆车。


被动语态:
The table was scratched by the dog. = 桌子被狗抓伤了。
The paint was scratched off the wall. = 墙上的油漆被刮掉了。


疑问句:
Did the cat scratch the sofa? = 猫抓沙发了吗？
Have you ever scratched your eye by accident? = 你有没有不小心抓伤过眼睛？


否定句:
The dog didn't scratch the floor. = 狗没有抓地板。
He didn't scratch the surface of the issue. = 他没有触及问题的表面。


PHRASE->
scratch the surface: 触及表面

scratch your head: 挠头
from scratch: 从零开始
scratch off: 刮掉
