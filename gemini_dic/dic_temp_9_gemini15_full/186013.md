rewetting:/ˌriːˈwɛtɪŋ/| n.| 再润湿；重新湿润 |The act of making something wet again.  It often refers to the process of reintroducing water to a previously dry substance or area. |例句：The rewetting of the peat bog helped to restore its ecosystem.  重新湿润泥炭沼泽有助于恢复其生态系统。


例句：The rewetting of the dried-out soil improved crop yields.  重新湿润干涸的土壤提高了作物产量。


近义词：rehydration, re-moistening, dampening


反义词：drying, desiccation


词性:名词
