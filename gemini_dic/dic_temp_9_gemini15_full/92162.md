hypersomnolence:/ˌhaɪpəˈsɒmnələns/| n. |嗜睡症，过度嗜睡|Excessive sleepiness, characterized by prolonged or excessive sleep, and often accompanied by difficulty staying awake during the day.  It's more than just feeling tired; it's a persistent and overwhelming urge to sleep. |Example:  His hypersomnolence was so severe that he fell asleep at his desk several times a day.  他的嗜睡症非常严重，以至于他一天在办公桌前睡了好几次。 |近义词：excessive sleepiness, somnolence, sleepiness, lethargy |反义词：insomnia, wakefulness
