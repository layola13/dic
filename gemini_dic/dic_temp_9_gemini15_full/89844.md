horn: /hɔːrn/ | n. | 角；喇叭；号角 | A hard, pointed, usually bony, projection growing from the head of an animal, or a similar projection on an object.  Also, a device for producing a loud sound, typically using compressed air. |例句：The bull lowered its horns. 公牛低下它的角。


The car horn blared loudly.  汽车喇叭大声鸣叫。


近义词：antler, tusk, trumpet, bugle | 反义词：None


词源:  From Old English *horn, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch hoorn and German Horn. The sense "musical instrument" developed in Middle English.
