depopulator: /ˌdiːˈpɒpjʊleɪtə(r)/ | n. | 人口减少者；使人口减少的事物 | A depopulator is something or someone that causes a significant decrease in the population of a place or area.  This could be a natural disaster, a war, disease, or even a government policy. |The drought acted as a depopulator in the rural area, forcing many families to migrate to the city. 干旱使农村地区人口减少，迫使许多家庭迁移到城市。|近义词：population reducer, depopulator,  population decliner | 反义词：population increaser, repopulator
