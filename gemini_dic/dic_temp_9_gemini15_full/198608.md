shuffly:/ˈʃʌfli/| adj.|拖沓的；笨拙的；步履蹒跚的|Moving or acting in a clumsy or slow way, often with shuffling feet.|例句：He walked shuffly down the street, his head bowed. 他拖着步子走在街上，低着头。|例句：The old man moved shuffly and with difficulty.  老人行动迟缓且吃力。|近义词：awkward, clumsy, unsteady, shambling|反义词：graceful, nimble, agile, spry
