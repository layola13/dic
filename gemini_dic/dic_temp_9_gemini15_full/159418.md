polarizer: /ˈpoʊləraɪzər/ | n. |偏振器，偏光器 | A device that transmits light of a particular polarization and blocks light of other polarizations.  It selectively absorbs or reflects light waves vibrating in certain directions. | The camera lens contains a polarizer to reduce glare and enhance color saturation.  相机镜头包含一个偏振器以减少眩光并增强色彩饱和度。  The sunglasses use a polarizing filter to reduce the intensity of sunlight. 这副太阳镜使用偏振滤光片来降低阳光的强度。 | polarizing filter, polarizing lens | depolarizer | 名词
