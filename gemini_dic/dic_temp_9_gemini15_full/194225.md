self-maintenance:/ˌsɛlfˈmeɪntənəns/| n. |自我维持，自我保养|The ability of a system or organism to maintain itself without external assistance.  This includes the processes necessary for survival and continued operation.  It often implies a degree of autonomy and resilience. |例句：The car requires minimal self-maintenance.|近义词：self-preservation, self-sufficiency|反义词：dependence, reliance
