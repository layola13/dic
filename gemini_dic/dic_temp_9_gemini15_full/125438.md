multiplatform: /ˌmʌltiˈplætfɔrm/ | adj. | 多平台的，跨平台的 |  Able to operate on multiple computer platforms or operating systems.  This refers to software, applications, or games that can run on different systems like Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, and Android without requiring significant modifications. | Example:  The game is multiplatform, meaning it's available on both PC and consoles. = 这个游戏是跨平台的，这意味着它可以在PC和游戏机上运行。


The game's multiplatform release broadened its potential audience. = 游戏的跨平台发布扩大了其潜在受众。

近义词: cross-platform, platform-independent | 反义词: platform-specific, single-platform | 词性: adjective


词源:  The word is a combination of "multi-" (meaning many or multiple) and "platform" (referring to a computer system or operating system).  The term emerged with the increasing diversity of computing platforms and the need for software compatibility across them.
