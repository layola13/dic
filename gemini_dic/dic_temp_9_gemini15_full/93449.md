illumines:/ɪˈluːmɪnz/| vt.|照亮；阐明；使辉煌|To shine light on; to brighten; to make clear or understandable; to inspire or exalt.|例句：The moon illumines the night sky. 月亮照亮夜空。 The author's insights illumine the complex issue. 作者的见解阐明了这个复杂的问题。|近义词：light up, brighten, enlighten, illuminate|反义词：darken, obscure, dim


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun illumines the earth. = 太阳照亮地球。
The artist's work illumines the human condition. = 艺术家的作品阐明了人类的处境。

一般过去时:
The candles illumined the room. = 蜡烛照亮了房间。
Her speech illumined the path forward. = 她的演讲阐明了前进的道路。

现在完成时:
His discoveries have illumined the field of medicine. = 他的发现已经照亮了医学领域。
The new research has illumined the mystery of the disease. = 新的研究已经阐明了这种疾病的谜团。

过去完成时:
The ancient texts had illumined the history of the civilization. = 古代文本已经阐明了这个文明的历史。
The detective's investigation had illumined the crime. = 侦探的调查已经阐明了这起犯罪。

将来时:
The new streetlights will illumine the streets. = 新的路灯将照亮街道。
The upcoming conference will illumine the latest developments in technology. = 即将来临的会议将阐明技术领域的最新发展。

虚拟语气:
If the sun illumined the cave, we could see better. = 如果太阳照亮洞穴，我们就能看得更清楚。
If only his words had illumined the situation earlier. = 但愿他的话早点阐明情况。

被动语态:
The room was illumined by the setting sun. = 房间被落日照亮。
The problem was illumined by a simple solution. = 问题被一个简单的解决方案阐明。

疑问句:
What illumines the dark corners of the room? = 什么照亮了房间的黑暗角落？
Does this evidence illumine the case any further?  = 这证据能进一步阐明此案吗？

否定句:
The darkness was not illumined by any light. = 黑暗没有被任何光线照亮。
The explanation did not illumine the situation at all. = 这个解释根本没有阐明情况。
