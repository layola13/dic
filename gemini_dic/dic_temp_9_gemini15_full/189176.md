sacque:/sæk/| n.|茄克衫，宽松外衣|A loose, often sleeveless, outer garment, typically worn by women.  It can be made from various materials and styles, often reaching below the waist.|例句：She wore a velvet sacque over her dress. 她穿着一件天鹅绒茄克衫在裙子外面。|例句：The antique sacque was a beautiful example of 18th-century fashion. 这件古董茄克衫是18世纪服装的精美典范。|近义词：jacket, coat, shawl|反义词：None specifically|名词
