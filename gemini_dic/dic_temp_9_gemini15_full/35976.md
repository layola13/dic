codename:/ˈkoʊdˌneɪm/| n. |代号，暗号|A codename is a secret name used to identify a person, thing, or operation.  It is often used in military, intelligence, or other clandestine operations to maintain secrecy and security.  For example, a secret mission might be given a codename to prevent its true nature from being discovered. |例句：The top-secret project was given the codename "Nightingale".  顶级秘密项目被赋予了代号“夜莺”。

The spy used a codename to protect his identity. 间谍使用代号来保护他的身份。|近义词：alias, designation, code word |反义词：real name, true identity
