prostheses:/ˈprɒsθiːziːz/| n.|假体；义肢；人工器官|Artificial replacements for missing or damaged body parts.  They can be used to replace limbs, organs, or other body parts.|例句：She received a new set of prostheses after the accident.  她事故后得到了一套新的假肢。|例句：Advanced prostheses are now controlled by the wearer's brain signals. 先进的假肢现在可以通过佩戴者的脑电波控制。|近义词：artificial limbs, implants, replacements|反义词：natural limbs, original organs|名词
