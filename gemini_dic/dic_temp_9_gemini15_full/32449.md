chimney-stack:/ˈtʃɪmni stæk/| n. |烟囱（群）, 烟囱排气筒 | A chimney stack is a structure consisting of one or more chimneys built together, often found in industrial settings or large buildings.  It's essentially a cluster of chimneys sharing a common base. |Example: The factory's chimney stack belched out thick black smoke.  那家工厂的烟囱排放出浓厚的黑烟。 The old power plant had a massive chimney stack, a landmark of the city. 那座旧发电厂有一个巨大的烟囱，是城市的标志性建筑。|近义词：chimney group, flue stack|反义词：None
