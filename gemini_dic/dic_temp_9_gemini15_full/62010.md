enamor:/ɪˈnæmər/| vt.|使迷恋；使倾心 |To inspire love or strong affection in; to charm.  |例句：He was enamored of her beauty. 他被她的美貌迷住了。|例句：She became enamored with the city's vibrant culture. 她迷上了这座城市充满活力的文化。|近义词：captivate, charm, enchant, fascinate|反义词：repulse, disgust, repel|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enamors her with his charm. = 他用他的魅力迷住了她。
The beautiful scenery enamors the tourists. = 美丽的景色迷住了游客们。

一般过去时:
I was enamored by her kindness. = 我被她的善良迷住了。
The old castle enamored the children. = 古老的城堡迷住了孩子们。

现在完成时:
He has enamored himself to the cause. = 他已经全身心地投入到这项事业中。
She has been enamored by the beauty of nature. = 她一直被大自然的美丽所倾倒。

过去完成时:
They had enamored themselves with the idea of traveling the world. = 他们曾经迷恋上环游世界的想法。
She had been enamored with him for years before they finally met. = 在他们最终相遇之前，她已经爱慕他多年了。

将来时:
The new technology will enamor many consumers. = 新技术将吸引许多消费者。
He will surely enamor her with his musical talent. = 他一定会用他的音乐天赋迷住她。

虚拟语气:
If he were to enamor her, he would have to work hard. = 如果他想迷住她，他必须努力。
I wish I could enamor her with my words. = 我希望我能用我的话语打动她。

被动语态:
She was enamored by his romantic gestures. = 她被他浪漫的举动迷住了。
Many people are enamored by the simplicity of the design. = 许多人被设计的简洁所吸引。

疑问句:
Is he enamored with her? = 他被她迷住了吗？
Are you enamored by the music? = 你被音乐迷住了吗？

否定句:
He was not enamored by her attitude. = 他并不喜欢她的态度。
She is not enamored with the idea. = 她并不喜欢这个主意。
