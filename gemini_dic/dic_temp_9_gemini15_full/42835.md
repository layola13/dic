courser:/ˈkɔːrsər/| n.|猎犬；快马；赛马|A courser is a type of horse, typically a swift and powerful one, often used for hunting or racing.  It can also refer to a hunting dog, particularly one used for hunting coursers (hares).|例句：The knight mounted his trusty courser and rode into battle.  骑士骑上他忠实的快马冲进了战场。

例句：The hunter's courser was renowned for its speed and stamina. 猎人的猎犬以其速度和耐力而闻名。|近义词：steed, charger, racer, hound|反义词：nag, donkey
