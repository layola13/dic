engorge:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒ/| vt.|使充满；使膨胀；暴饮暴食|To fill or swell excessively; to eat or drink greedily.|例句：The river engorged after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，河水暴涨。
The glutton engorged himself on the feast.  那个贪吃的人在盛宴上暴饮暴食。|近义词：saturate, glut, gorge|反义词：empty, deplete|动词
