lancing:/ˈlænsɪŋ/| v.|刺，戳；切开；排脓|To pierce or cut with a lancet or similar sharp instrument; to make a small incision into (something).  The act of making a small incision, especially to release pus or fluid.|The doctor lanced the boil to drain the pus.  医生切开了脓肿以排出脓液。  The surgeon lanced the abscess. 外科医生切开了脓肿。|puncturing, incising, cutting|healing, closing|动词
