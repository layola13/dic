reenforced:/ˌriːɪnˈfɔːrst/| v. | 加固；增强；强化 | To strengthen or support, especially with additional material.  |例句：The bridge was reenforced with steel beams.  桥梁用钢梁加固了。

例句：The teacher reenforced the lesson with extra examples. 老师用额外的例子强化了课程。


近义词：strengthen, bolster, fortify | 反义词：weaken, undermine


词源:

* **re-:**  prefix meaning "again" or "back"
* **enforce:**  from Old French *enforcier* (to strengthen), from *en-* (in) + *forcier* (to force, strengthen).  The root ultimately traces back to Latin *fortis* (strong).


用法说明:


一般现在时:
The company reenforces its security protocols regularly. = 公司定期加强其安全协议。
They reenforce their arguments with statistical data. = 他们用统计数据来加强他们的论点。


一般过去时:
The army reenforced its position on the hill. = 军队加强了他们在山上的阵地。
She reenforced her claim with new evidence. = 她用新的证据强化了她的说法。


现在完成时:
The government has reenforced its border controls. = 政府已经加强了边境管控。
They have reenforced the walls of the old castle. = 他们已经加固了古堡的城墙。


过去完成时:
He had reenforced the structure before the earthquake. = 地震发生前他已经加固了建筑物。
The engineers had reenforced the bridge supports to prevent collapse. = 工程师们已经加固了桥梁支架以防止坍塌。


将来时:
We will reenforce the team with additional personnel. = 我们将用额外的人员来加强团队。
They will reenforce the dam to withstand the flood. = 他们将加固大坝以抵御洪水。


虚拟语气:
If they had reenforced the building, it wouldn't have collapsed. = 如果他们加固了建筑物，它就不会倒塌了。
I wish they had reenforced the security measures earlier. = 我希望他们早点加强安全措施。


被动语态:
The walls were reenforced with concrete. = 墙壁用混凝土加固了。
The decision was reenforced by the board of directors. = 该决定得到了董事会的支持（强化）。


疑问句:

Will the company reenforce its marketing strategy? = 公司会加强其营销策略吗？


否定句:
They did not reenforce the damaged section of the road. = 他们没有加固道路受损的路段。
The company has not reenforced its cybersecurity measures sufficiently. = 公司没有充分加强其网络安全措施。
