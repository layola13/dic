didym:/ˈdɪdɪm/| n. |睾丸；孪生；成对的物体|A didym is a testicle, or more broadly, any paired organ or structure.  The term is derived from the Greek word for twin.  It's less common in modern usage, often replaced by more specific anatomical terms.|Example: In some older anatomical texts, the testes are referred to as the didyms.|近义词：testicle, testis, twin|反义词：single, solitary|名词
