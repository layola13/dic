incliner:/ɪnˈklaɪnər/| n. |斜坡；倾斜器；倾斜装置|A device or structure that inclines or causes something to incline.  It can refer to a physical object that creates a slope, or a mechanism that tilts something. |The mountain road had many steep incliners. 这条山路有很多陡峭的斜坡。 The scientist used an incliner to study the effects of gravity on plant growth. 科学家使用了一个倾斜器来研究重力对植物生长的影响。|slope, ramp, gradient |leveler, flattener |名词
