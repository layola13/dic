horsepowers:/ˈhɔːrsˌpaʊərz/| n.|马力，功率|The term "horsepower" refers to a unit of measurement of power, typically used to describe the output of engines or motors.  It represents the rate at which work is done.  One horsepower is approximately equal to 746 watts. |例句：The car's horsepower is impressive. 这辆车的马力令人印象深刻。

The new engine boasts 300 horsepower. 新引擎拥有300马力。


近义词：power, wattage, output|反义词：None (no direct opposite)|词性:名词
