ligulate:/ˈlɪɡjuleɪt/| adj.|舌状的；带状的|Having a ligule or ligules; strap-shaped.  A ligulate floret is a ray floret in a composite flower head, having a strap-shaped corolla.|例句：The dandelion has ligulate flowers.  蒲公英有舌状花。

The daisy's petals are ligulate.  雏菊的花瓣是舌状的。


近义词：strap-shaped, ribbon-shaped|反义词：tubular (referring to flower shapes)|词性：形容词
