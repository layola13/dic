restamped:/ˌriːˈstæmpt/| v. |重新盖章；重新标记|To stamp again; to apply a new stamp or mark.|The document was restamped with a new date. 这份文件被重新盖上了一个新的日期章。|近义词：re-stamp, re-mark|反义词：remove stamp, erase mark|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They restamp the packages every day.  他们每天都重新盖包装的章。
He restamps the old letters with new postage. 他用新的邮票重新盖在旧信上。


一般过去时:
The official restamped the passport.  官员重新盖了护照的章。
She restamped the returned mail. 她重新盖了退回邮件的章。


现在完成时:
They have restamped all the documents.  他们已经重新盖了所有文件的章。
We have restamped the defective products. 我们已经重新盖了有缺陷产品的章。


过去完成时:
The manager had restamped the forms before the meeting.  经理在会议前已经重新盖了表格的章。
They had restamped the envelopes before sending them. 他们在寄出信封之前已经重新盖了章。


将来时:
We will restamp the parcels tomorrow. 我们明天将重新盖包裹的章。
He will restamp the visa in the morning. 他将在早上重新盖签证章。


虚拟语气:
If they needed it, they would restamp the document. 如果他们需要的话，他们会重新盖这个文件的章。
