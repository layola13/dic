intergossip:/ˌɪntərˈɡɒsɪp/| n. |相互闲聊，互相八卦|Informal talk or gossip between people.  It often implies a shared understanding or inside knowledge among the participants.  |Example: The intergossip in the office concerned the upcoming merger.  办公室里的相互闲聊都围绕着即将到来的合并。|近义词：chitchat,  gossiping,  back-and-forth,  camaraderie (if positive)|反义词：silence,  secrecy,  serious discussion
