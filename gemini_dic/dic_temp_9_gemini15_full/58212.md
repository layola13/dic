drug-on-the-market: /ˈdrʌɡ ɑːn ðə ˈmɑːrkɪt/ | n. |上市药物| A drug that has been approved by regulatory authorities and is available for sale to the public.  It implies the drug has completed clinical trials and is deemed safe and effective for its intended use.  |Example:  The new drug-on-the-market offers a promising treatment for Alzheimer's disease.  新型上市药物为阿尔茨海默病的治疗提供了有希望的疗法。|近义词：approved drug, marketed drug, commercially available drug |反义词：experimental drug, investigational drug, unapproved drug | noun
