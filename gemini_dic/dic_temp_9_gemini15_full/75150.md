fritillaries:/ˈfrɪtɪləriːz/ | n. |贝母属植物；棋盘花 |Fritillaries are a genus of flowering plants in the lily family, known for their checkered or mottled patterns.  They are often cultivated as ornamentals. |例句：The garden was ablaze with the colors of various fritillaries. 花园里各种贝母花盛开，色彩斑斓。|例句：Several species of fritillaries are endangered due to habitat loss. 由于栖息地丧失，几种贝母属植物濒临灭绝。|近义词：checker lily, snake's head |反义词：无
