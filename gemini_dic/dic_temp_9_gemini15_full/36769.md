collusive:/kəˈluːsɪv/| adj.|共谋的；串通的|Involving or characterized by collusion; secret or illegal cooperation.  The collusive agreement between the companies to fix prices was discovered by the authorities.  |近义词：conspiratorial, conniving, colluding|反义词：honest, fair, aboveboard


例句：

The two companies were accused of engaging in collusive bidding practices.  = 这两家公司被指控从事串通投标行为。

The investigation revealed a collusive relationship between the judge and the defendant. = 调查揭露了法官和被告之间存在串通关系。


词源：

From "collusion," ultimately from Latin *col- "together" + *ludere "to play," implying secret or deceitful cooperation.
