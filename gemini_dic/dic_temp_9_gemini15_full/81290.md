grants:/ɡrænts/| n. v.| 拨款；授予；准予 |  The noun "grant" refers to a sum of money given by an organization, typically a government, to an individual or group for a specific purpose. As a verb, it means to bestow or agree to give something requested. |例句：The government granted a research grant to the university.  政府给大学拨了一笔研究经费。


The university granted him a scholarship. 大学给他提供了一份奖学金。|近义词：award, bestow, allowance |反义词：deny, refuse, withhold |名词，动词
