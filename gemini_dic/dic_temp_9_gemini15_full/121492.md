misleaders:/mɪsˈliːdərz/ | n. |误导者，欺骗者 |People or things that mislead.  Those who intentionally or unintentionally give false information or guidance, leading others astray. | Example: The misleaders spread false rumors about the company's financial status, causing investors to panic and sell their shares. 误导者散布关于公司财务状况的谣言，导致投资者恐慌并抛售股票。 |近义词：deceivers, deceivers, fraudsters, tricksters |反义词：truth-tellers, guides, mentors
