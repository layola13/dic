paler:/ˈpeɪlər/ | adj. | 苍白的，较浅的 | Less bright or intense in color;  lighter in shade.  |例句：Her face was paler than usual after the accident. 事故后，她的脸色比平时苍白。

例句：The painted wall was paler than the original design.  粉刷的墙比最初的设计颜色浅。


近义词：lighter, fainter, wan, pallid | 反义词：darker, deeper, richer


词源:  From Old English *pāl, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch vaal and German fahl ‘pale, faded’.  The comparative form 'paler' is formed by adding the comparative suffix '-er'.
