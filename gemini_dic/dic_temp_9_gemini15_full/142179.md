词源:
The word "ornamentality" derives from the noun "ornament" (装饰品) and the suffix "-ality" (表示性质或状态).  "Ornament" itself comes from the Old French word "ornement," ultimately deriving from the Latin word "ornare" (to adorn or decorate).
