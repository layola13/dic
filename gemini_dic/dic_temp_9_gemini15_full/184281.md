resound:/rɪˈzaʊnd/| v.|回响，共鸣，大声地说|to continue to sound; to be filled with the sound of something|The applause resounded through the hall.  掌声在礼堂回响。

The singer's voice resounded with power and emotion. 歌手的歌声充满力量和情感。

近义词：echo, reverberate, boom, resonate|反义词：fade, die away,


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The church bells resound every hour. 教堂的钟声每小时都会回响。
His words resound with truth and wisdom. 他的话语充满真理和智慧。


一般过去时:
The thunder resounded across the valley. 雷声在山谷回响。
Her laughter resounded through the empty house. 她的笑声在空荡的房子里回荡。


现在完成时:
The news of their victory has resounded throughout the country. 他们胜利的消息已传遍全国。
His achievements have resounded through history. 他的成就已载入史册。


过去完成时:
The cheers had resounded long after the game ended. 比赛结束后很久，欢呼声还在回荡。
The impact of his speech had resounded throughout the community. 他的演讲的影响在整个社区引起了反响。


将来时:
The concert will resound with beautiful music. 音乐会将充满优美的音乐。
His name will resound through the ages. 他的名字将流芳百世。


虚拟语气:
If only his voice could resound across the generations! 但愿他的声音能传遍几代人！
I wish my message could resound more clearly. 我希望我的信息能更清晰地传达。


被动语态:
His name was resounded throughout the land. 他的名字传遍了全国。
The hall was resounded with music. 礼堂里回荡着音乐声。


疑问句:
Does the music resound throughout the building? 音乐是否在整个建筑物中回响？
Will his ideas resound with future generations? 他的想法会引起后代的共鸣吗？


否定句:
The message did not resound with the audience. 信息没有引起观众的共鸣。
His voice did not resound as clearly as expected. 他的声音不如预期那样洪亮。


PHRASE->
resound with:充满...；回荡着...
resoundingly:响亮地；彻底地
resound in:在...回响
resound throughout:传遍...
resound across:横跨...回响


词源:
From Old French *resoner, from Latin resonare "to resound," from re- "again" + sonare "to sound."
