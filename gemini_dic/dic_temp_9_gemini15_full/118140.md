mensio:/ˈmɛnʃiˌoʊ/| n. |提及，说起，论及|The mention of his name brought a smile to her face. 提起他的名字，她脸上露出了笑容。|近义词：reference, mention, remark |反义词：silence, omission


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The author mentions several important points in the book. 作者在书中提到了几个重要的点。
He mentions his childhood often in his speeches. 他在演讲中经常提到他的童年。


一般过去时:
She mentioned that she was feeling unwell. 她提到她感觉不舒服。
The professor mentioned a relevant article during the lecture. 教授在讲座中提到了一篇相关的文章。


现在完成时:
He has mentioned the project several times. 他已经多次提到这个项目了。
She has mentioned her concerns to the manager. 她已经向经理表达了她的担忧。


过去完成时:
They had mentioned the problem before the meeting. 他们在会议之前就已经提到了这个问题。
He had mentioned his intention to leave before the announcement. 他在宣布之前就已经提到了他离开的意图。


将来时:
I will mention your suggestion at the next meeting. 我将在下次会议上提及你的建议。
She will mention the incident in her report. 她将在她的报告中提及此事。


虚拟语气:
If I had mentioned it earlier, things might have been different. 如果我早点提到它，情况可能就不同了。
I wish I had mentioned the deadline to him. 我真希望我向他提到了截止日期。


被动语态:
The issue was mentioned briefly in the newspaper. 这个问题在报纸上被简短地提到了。
His contribution was mentioned in the acknowledgments. 他的贡献在致谢中被提及。


疑问句:
Did he mention anything about the trip? 他有没有提到关于这次旅行的任何事情？
What did she mention in her email? 她在邮件中提到了什么？


否定句:
He didn't mention anything about his plans. 他没有提到任何关于他的计划。
She didn't mention seeing him at the party. 她没提到在派对上见过他。


PHRASE->
make mention of: 提及
at the mention of: 一提到...
with mention of: 附带提及
no mention of: 没有提到
a passing mention of: 一笔带过地提及
