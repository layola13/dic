/ˌmʌltiˈpɑːrtiɪzəm/ | n. | 多党制 | A political system in which multiple political parties have the capacity to gain control of government offices, separately or in coalition.  Unlike a one-party system, multi-party systems allow for a wider range of political viewpoints to be represented and for power to be shared among different groups. |  Many European countries operate under a multi-party system.  许多欧洲国家实行多党制。

The existence of a multi-party system often leads to coalition governments. 多党制的出现通常会导致联合政府。|  one-party system, single-party system |  one-party system, dictatorship |
