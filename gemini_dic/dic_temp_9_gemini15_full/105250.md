khan:/kɑːn/| n. |可汗，汗王；首领；统治者|A khan is a title used historically for various rulers and leaders, particularly in Central Asia.|例句：Genghis Khan united the Mongol tribes. 成吉思汗统一了蒙古各部落。|近义词：chieftain, leader, ruler |反义词：subject, follower
