/poʊz/ | n. |姿势，姿态 |  A position of the body; the way someone holds their body.  | He struck a heroic pose for the photograph. 他摆了个英雄般的姿势拍照。| posture, stance | slouch |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He maintains a relaxed pose. = 他保持着放松的姿势。
She strikes a graceful pose. = 她摆出优雅的姿势。


一般过去时:
He posed for the artist. = 他为艺术家摆姿势。
She posed for a photograph. = 她摆姿势拍照。
