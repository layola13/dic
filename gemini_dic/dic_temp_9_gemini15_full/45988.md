cyanotic:/saɪəˈnɒtɪk/| adj.|紫绀的，发青的|Having a bluish discoloration of the skin and mucous membranes due to insufficient oxygen in the blood.|The baby was cyanotic, indicating a serious respiratory problem.  婴儿脸色发青，表明存在严重的呼吸问题。|近义词：blue, bluish, purplish|反义词：pink, rosy


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The patient is cyanotic around the mouth. = 病人的嘴周围发青。
Her lips are cyanotic due to the cold weather. = 她的嘴唇因为寒冷天气而发青。


一般过去时:
The climber became cyanotic at high altitude. = 登山者在高海拔地区脸色发青。
He was cyanotic after the strenuous exercise. = 剧烈运动后，他脸色发青。


现在完成时:
The baby has been cyanotic since birth. = 这个婴儿从出生以来就一直脸色发青。
She has become cyanotic after a long period of breathlessness. = 她在长时间呼吸困难后脸色发青。


过去完成时:
The patient had been cyanotic for several hours before arriving at the hospital. = 病人在到达医院前几个小时就已经脸色发青了。
He had become cyanotic after a long period of underwater swimming. = 他在长时间水下游泳后脸色发青。


将来时:
The doctor predicts the patient will become cyanotic if the oxygen level drops further. = 医生预测如果氧气水平进一步下降，病人将会脸色发青。
If he continues to exert himself, he will likely become cyanotic. = 如果他继续剧烈运动，他很可能会脸色发青。


虚拟语气:
If he had received prompt medical attention, he wouldn't have become so cyanotic. = 如果他及时得到医疗救助，他就不会脸色发青得这么厉害。
It would be alarming if the baby became cyanotic during feeding. = 如果婴儿在喂奶时脸色发青，那将令人担忧。


被动语态:
His lips were found to be cyanotic by the paramedics. = 急救人员发现他的嘴唇发青。
The patient's extremities were noted as cyanotic upon examination. = 检查时发现病人的四肢发青。


疑问句:
Is the baby cyanotic? = 这个婴儿脸色发青吗？
Are his fingertips cyanotic? = 他的指尖发青吗？


否定句:
The child is not cyanotic. = 这个孩子没有脸色发青。
Her skin was not cyanotic despite the low oxygen levels. = 尽管氧气水平低，她的皮肤并没有发青。


PHRASE->
severely cyanotic = 严重发青
peripherally cyanotic = 周围性发青
centrally cyanotic = 中枢性发青
mildly cyanotic = 轻微发青
acutely cyanotic = 急性发青


词源:
From Greek *kynos (dog) + -otic (relating to).  The original meaning referred to a blue-gray coloration of the skin, likened to a dog's color. The connection may relate to the bluish tinge of some dog breeds.
