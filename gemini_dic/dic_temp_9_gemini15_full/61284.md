embalmers:/ɪmˈbɑːlmərz/ | n. | 殓葬师，遗体防腐师 |  A person whose job is to embalm dead bodies.  Embalmers prepare bodies for burial or cremation by using chemicals to prevent decomposition. | The embalmers worked diligently to preserve the body for the funeral. 殓葬师们勤奋地工作，为葬礼保存遗体。 | morticians, undertakers |  — |  noun
