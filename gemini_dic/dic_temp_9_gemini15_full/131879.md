noncontaminable: /ˌnɒnkənˈtæmɪnəbəl/ | adj. |不易污染的，不会被污染的 | Incapable of being contaminated or polluted.  Something that is noncontaminable resists the intrusion of contaminants. This term often applies to materials, environments, or processes designed to prevent contamination. | Example: The noncontaminable packaging ensured the food's freshness.  不易污染的包装确保了食物的新鲜。 The laboratory uses noncontaminable equipment to maintain sterile conditions. 实验室使用不易污染的设备来维持无菌条件。 | 近义词: uncontaminated, sterile, pure, clean | 反义词: contaminable, polluted, impure, unclean |
