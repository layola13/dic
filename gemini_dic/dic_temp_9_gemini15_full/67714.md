exuberant: /ɪɡˈzjuːbərənt/ | adj. | 兴高采烈的；充满活力的；茂盛的 |  Characterized by or showing great energy and enthusiasm; overflowing with enthusiasm and excitement; growing luxuriantly. |

例句：

The children were exuberant after receiving their presents. 孩子们收到礼物后非常兴奋。

Her exuberant personality made her a popular figure. 她热情奔放的个性使她成为一个受欢迎的人物。


近义词：effervescent, ebullient, enthusiastic, vivacious, buoyant

反义词：depressed, gloomy, despondent, lethargic, subdued


词源:

源自于拉丁语 *exuberare，由 *ex- (向外) + *uber (丰盛的) 构成。  最初指的是植物生长茂盛，后来引申到人的情绪和活力。
