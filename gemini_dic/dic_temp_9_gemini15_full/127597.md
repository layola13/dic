neede:/niːd/| v.|需要，必须|to require or want something; to be necessary or essential.  |例句：I need a new car. 我需要一辆新车。|例句：We need to leave now. 我们现在需要离开。|近义词：require, want, necessitate|反义词：surplus, abundance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I need a new phone. = 我需要一部新手机。
They need more time to finish the project. = 他们需要更多时间来完成项目。

一般过去时:
I needed help yesterday. = 我昨天需要帮助。
She needed a break from work. = 她需要休息一下工作。

现在完成时:
I have needed a haircut for weeks. = 我已经好几周需要理发了。
They have needed assistance since the accident. = 自从事故发生以来，他们一直需要帮助。

过去完成时:
I had needed the document before the meeting. = 我在会议之前就需要那份文件了。
They had needed more information to make a decision. = 他们需要更多信息才能做出决定。

将来时:
I will need your support next week. = 下周我需要你的支持。
We will need to book the tickets in advance. = 我们需要提前预订机票。

虚拟语气:
I wish I didn't need to work so hard. = 我希望我不需要这么努力工作。
If I needed help, I would ask you. = 如果我需要帮助，我会向你求助。

被动语态:
More resources are needed for this project. = 这个项目需要更多资源。
Your help is needed urgently. = 你的帮助迫切需要。

疑问句:
Do you need anything else? = 你还需要什么吗？
What do you need from me? = 你需要我做什么？

否定句:
I don't need your help. = 我不需要你的帮助。
They didn't need any extra time. = 他们不需要额外的时间。
