cochampions:/ˌkoʊˈtʃæmpiənz/| n. |联合冠军；并列冠军|Two teams were declared co-champions after a tie in the final game.  一场比赛结束后，两队因打成平局而被宣布为联合冠军。|近义词：joint champions, tied champions|反义词：sole champion
