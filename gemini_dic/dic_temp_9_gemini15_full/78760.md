gilded:/ˈɡɪldɪd/| adj.|镀金的；虚饰的；华美的|Covered with a thin layer of gold; superficially attractive but of little substance.|例句：The gilded cage of the rich and famous often hides unhappiness. 富人和名人的金丝笼往往隐藏着不幸。|例句：His gilded promises turned out to be empty words. 他的华丽承诺原来是空话。|近义词：gold-plated, ornamented, embellished|反义词：plain, unadorned, austere

USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sun gilds the mountain peaks. = 太阳给山峰镀上金光。
This experience gilds my memories. = 这段经历使我的回忆更加美好。
