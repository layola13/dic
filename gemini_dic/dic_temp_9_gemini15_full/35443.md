co-signatory:/ˌkoʊˈsɪɡnətɔːri/| n. |共同签署人，连署签字人 |A co-signatory is a person who signs a document along with another person or people, thereby sharing responsibility or endorsement.  |例句：The loan agreement required two co-signatories. 这份贷款协议需要两位共同签署人。

例句：She acted as a co-signatory on her son's mortgage application. 她作为共同签署人参与了她儿子的抵押贷款申请。


近义词：joint signatory, co-signer, endorser


反义词：sole signatory


词性: 名词
