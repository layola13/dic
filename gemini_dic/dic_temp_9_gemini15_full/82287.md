groundwater:/ˈɡraʊndˌwɔːtər/| n. |地下水|Water that exists underground in the soil or in rocks.|The well provided ample groundwater for the village. 这口井为村庄提供了充足的地下水。|近义词：subterranean water, subsurface water|反义词：surface water


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Groundwater supplies many communities with drinking water. =地下水为许多社区提供饮用水。
The level of groundwater fluctuates throughout the year. =地下水位全年波动。

一般过去时:
Farmers relied heavily on groundwater for irrigation last summer. =去年夏天，农民们严重依赖地下水灌溉。
The groundwater level dropped significantly during the drought. =干旱期间地下水位大幅下降。

现在完成时:
We have depleted the groundwater resources in this area. =我们已经耗尽了该地区的地下水资源。
Scientists have studied the impact of groundwater pollution for decades. =科学家们几十年来一直在研究地下水污染的影响。

过去完成时:
Before the well was drilled, the community had relied on rainwater harvesting. =在打井之前，社区一直依靠雨水收集。
The aquifer had already been severely depleted before the new regulations were implemented. =在新规实施之前，含水层就已经严重枯竭了。

将来时:
Increased demand will likely deplete groundwater supplies in the future. =未来增加的需求可能会耗尽地下水供应。
We will monitor the groundwater levels closely next year. =明年我们将密切监测地下水位。

虚拟语气:
If we conserved water more effectively, we wouldn't be facing a groundwater crisis. =如果我们更有效地节约用水，我们就不会面临地下水危机。
I wish we had acted sooner to protect our groundwater resources. =我希望我们早点采取行动保护我们的地下水资源。

被动语态:
Groundwater is often contaminated by agricultural runoff. =地下水经常被农业径流污染。
The aquifer is being replenished slowly after the drought. =干旱过后，含水层正在缓慢补充。

疑问句:
Is the groundwater safe to drink in this region? =这个地区的地下水安全饮用吗？
How deep is the groundwater table in this area? =这个地区地下水位有多深？

否定句:
This area does not have sufficient groundwater for irrigation. =这个地区没有足够的地下水用于灌溉。
The well didn't produce enough groundwater to meet the demand. =这口井产生的地下水不足以满足需求。
