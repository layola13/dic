deluge:/ˈdɛljuːdʒ/|/ˈdɛljuːʒ/| n., v.| 洪水；泛滥；大量涌入 | A deluge is a large amount of something, especially water, that falls or comes somewhere very quickly.  It can also refer to a large quantity of something that arrives suddenly. |例句：The heavy rain caused a deluge in the city.  暴雨导致了该城市的洪水泛滥。

The recent news reports have brought a deluge of phone calls to the office. 最近的新闻报道给办公室带来了大量的电话。|近义词：flood, inundation, torrent, spate |反义词：drought, shortage, scarcity |名词，动词

The rain deluges the streets every afternoon. = 下午雨水经常淹没街道。
