flavour: /ˈfleɪvər/ (US) /ˈfleɪvə/ (UK) | n. |味道，风味；特色，情趣 | The distinctive taste of a food or drink; a particular kind of taste or smell.  It can also refer to a distinctive quality, characteristic, or aspect of something. | 例句：The chocolate cake had a rich, intense flavour. 这块巧克力蛋糕有着浓郁醇厚的味道。

例句：This wine has a fruity flavour. 这款酒有果香。


近义词：taste, savor, aroma, nuance, character | 反义词：tastelessness, blandness


词源：源自古法语 *flavor,  最终源自拉丁语 *flavour,  意为“香味，气味”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cake has a delicious flavour. = 这蛋糕味道很好。
This tea possesses a subtle flavour. = 这茶有一种微妙的香味。


一般过去时:
The soup lacked flavour yesterday. = 昨天的汤缺乏味道。
The pie had a strange flavour. = 这馅饼有一种奇怪的味道。


现在完成时:
They have added more flavour to the dish. = 他们给这道菜加了更多的调味料。
I have never tasted such a unique flavour. = 我从未尝过如此独特的味道。


过去完成时:
She had already added the flavouring before I arrived. = 我到达之前，她已经加了调味料。
The chef had forgotten to add the key flavour. = 大厨忘了加入关键的调味料。


将来时:
The new recipe will have a spicy flavour. = 新菜谱会有辛辣的味道。
This dish will develop a deeper flavour after a few days. = 这道菜几天后会更有味道。


虚拟语气:
If it had more flavour, I would eat it. = 如果它味道更好，我会吃它。
I wish this coffee had more flavour. = 我希望这咖啡更有味道。


被动语态:
The food is flavoured with herbs and spices. = 这食物用香草和香料调味。
The flavour is enhanced by the addition of vanilla. = 加入香草后，味道得到了提升。


疑问句:
What is the flavour of this sauce? = 这酱汁是什么味道？
Does this dish have a strong flavour? = 这道菜味道浓吗？


否定句:
The food has no flavour. = 这食物没有味道。
The drink lacked flavour. = 这饮料没有味道。


PHRASE->
full of flavour = 味道浓郁
lack of flavour = 缺乏味道
a hint of flavour = 一丝味道
flavour enhancer = 增味剂
flavour profile = 风味特征
