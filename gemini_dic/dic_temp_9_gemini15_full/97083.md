infers:/ɪnˈfɜːrz/| vt.|推断，推论|To deduce or conclude (information) from evidence and reasoning.  It implies a process of reasoning from known facts to unknown conclusions.| 例句：From her expression, I inferred that she was angry.  从她的表情，我推断她生气了。| 例句：The detective inferred the killer's motive from the evidence at the scene.  侦探从现场的证据推断出凶手的动机。|近义词：deduce, conclude, gather, surmise|反义词：assert, state, declare|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He infers the meaning from the context. = 他从上下文推断意思。
She infers that the data is inaccurate. = 她推断数据不准确。

一般过去时:
The police inferred the suspect's location from the phone records. = 警方根据电话记录推断嫌疑人的位置。
I inferred from his silence that he didn't agree. = 我从他的沉默中推断出他不赞同。

现在完成时:
Scientists have inferred the existence of dark matter from its gravitational effects. = 科学家们从暗物质的引力效应推断出它的存在。
She has inferred that the project will be delayed. = 她推断该项目将会延期。

过去完成时:
Having observed the clues, the detective had inferred the culprit's identity. = 观察了线索后，侦探推断出了罪犯的身份。
They had inferred, before the meeting, that a compromise was needed. = 会议前他们已经推断出需要妥协。


将来时:
Researchers will infer the climate patterns from the ice core samples. = 研究人员将从冰芯样本中推断气候模式。
I will infer the best course of action based on the available information. = 我将根据现有信息推断出最佳行动方案。


虚拟语气:
If I were to infer from the limited data, I would conclude that the experiment failed. = 如果我要根据有限的数据进行推断，我会得出实验失败的结论。
I wish I could infer the author's intentions more clearly. = 我希望我能更清楚地推断出作者的意图。

被动语态:
The criminal's motive was inferred from his behavior. = 罪犯的动机是从他的行为中推断出来的。
The age of the artifact was inferred from carbon dating. = 文物的年代是根据碳定年法推断出来的。

疑问句:
What can you infer from this evidence? = 你能从这些证据中推断出什么？
What did she infer from his letter? = 她从他的信中推断出什么？

否定句:
I cannot infer any meaning from his cryptic message. = 我无法从他神秘的信息中推断出任何意思。
The scientist did not infer a causal relationship between the two phenomena. = 科学家并没有推断出这两种现象之间的因果关系。
