lobeline:/ˈloʊbəliːn/| n. |萝贝林, 烟碱类生物碱 |Lobeline is an alkaloid found in various plants of the genus Lobelia, particularly Lobelia inflata (Indian tobacco).  It has been used in some smoking cessation aids, though its efficacy is debated.  It acts as a nicotinic acetylcholine receptor agonist, though less potent than nicotine itself. |例句：Some over-the-counter smoking cessation products contain lobeline.  一些非处方戒烟产品含有萝贝林。|例句：The pharmacological effects of lobeline are similar to, but weaker than, those of nicotine.  萝贝林的药理作用与尼古丁相似，但较弱。|近义词：nicotine (partially, in terms of effect)|反义词：无直接反义词|名词
