homecomer:/ˈhoʊmˌkʌmər/ | n. | 回家的人，归国的人 | A person who has returned home, especially after a long absence or journey.  | Example: The homecoming parade celebrated the victorious homecomers.  归乡游行庆祝凯旋而归的人们。  |近义词：returnee,  repatriate | 反义词：emigrant, expatriate
