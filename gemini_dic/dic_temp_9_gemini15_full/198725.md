sibyllist:/ˈsɪbɪlɪst/| n.|女预言家；通灵者|A sibyllist is a person who claims to possess prophetic powers, often associated with ancient Greek and Roman traditions.  They are believed to be able to communicate with the divine or supernatural and reveal future events or hidden truths. The term often carries a connotation of mystery and perhaps even a touch of madness.|例句：The sibyllist's pronouncements were cryptic and open to various interpretations.  这位女预言家的预言晦涩难懂，有多种解释。|近义词：prophet, seer, diviner, oracle|反义词：skeptic, realist
