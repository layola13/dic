picturesqueness:/ˌpɪktʃəˈresknəs/ | n. |优美如画，风景如画，秀丽 | The quality of being picturesque;  the quality of being charming or quaint. 例句：The picturesqueness of the Cotswolds attracts many tourists.  科茨沃尔德地区的秀丽景色吸引了许多游客。


近义词：charm, beauty, quaintness, loveliness, scenic beauty

反义词：ugliness, bleakness, drabness
