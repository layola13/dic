misauthorize:/ˌmɪsˈɔːθəraɪz/| vt.|滥用职权，未经授权使用|To use or give authority to someone or something wrongly or without proper authorization.  It implies an abuse of power or a violation of established procedures.|例句：The manager misauthorized the use of company funds for personal expenses.  经理滥用职权，将公司资金用于个人开支。

例句：He misauthorized the release of sensitive information to the press. 他未经授权向媒体发布了敏感信息。


近义词：abuse, misuse, usurp, misappropriate

反义词：authorize, sanction, approve


词性：动词
