reassume:/ˌriːəˈsuːm/| vt.|重新承担，再次担任|To take on or begin again something one has already done or held.  It implies a return to a previous state or position.|例句：After a brief hiatus, she reassumed her duties as CEO.  她短暂休整后重新担任了首席执行官的职务。|例句：He reassumed his position at the head of the table. 他重新坐回了桌子的首位。|近义词：resume, retake, reinstate|反义词：abandon, relinquish, resign
