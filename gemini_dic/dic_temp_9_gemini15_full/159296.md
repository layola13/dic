pointed:/ˈpɔɪntɪd/| adj.|尖的；尖锐的；直率的；明确的|Having a sharp end or tip.  Also used to describe something direct and frank in expression, or clearly indicating something.|例句：He used a pointed stick to poke the fire. 他用一根尖棍子拨火。

例句：Her remarks were pointed and critical. 她的言辞尖锐而批评性。


近义词：sharp, acute, direct, explicit|反义词：blunt, dull, indirect, vague|词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The needle is pointed. = 这根针很尖。
His criticism is pointed. = 他的批评很尖锐。


一般过去时:
The pencil was pointed. = 铅笔被削尖了。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
If the knife were pointed, it would be dangerous. = 如果刀很尖，那就很危险。
If she had been more pointed, he would have understood. = 如果她更直接一些，他就会明白了。


被动语态:
The pencil is being pointed. = 铅笔正在被削尖。
The issue was pointedly ignored. = 这个问题被有意忽略了。


疑问句:
Is the pencil pointed? = 铅笔尖吗？
Was her criticism pointed? = 她的批评尖锐吗？


否定句:
The stick isn't pointed. = 这根棍子不尖。
His remarks weren't pointed enough. = 他的话不够尖锐。

词源:
