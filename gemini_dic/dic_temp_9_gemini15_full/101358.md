ionisable: /aɪˈaɪznəbəl/ | adj. | 可电离的，可离子化的 | Able to be ionized; capable of forming ions.  | Example:  Many salts are ionisable in water. 许多盐类在水中可电离。

The substance is highly ionisable. 这物质高度可电离。


近义词: ionizable


反义词: non-ionisable, unionizable


词性: adjective


词源:  The word "ionisable" is derived from the word "ion," which comes from the Greek word ἰόν (ion), meaning "going," referring to the movement of charged particles.  The suffix "-isable" indicates the capability of being ionized.
