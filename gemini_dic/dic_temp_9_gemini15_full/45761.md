cushioning:/ˈkʊʃənɪŋ/| n. |缓冲；减震；缓和|The act of cushioning something or being cushioned;  a soft material used to cushion something. It refers to the protection or support provided against impact or shock.  |例句：The car's suspension provides excellent cushioning. 这辆车的悬挂系统提供了极好的缓冲。

The thick carpet provided cushioning for my feet. 厚厚的地毯让我的脚感觉很舒适。|近义词：padding, buffering, damping, mitigation |反义词：impact, shock, jarring
