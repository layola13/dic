pour:/pɔːr/| /pʊər/ | vt.  n. |倾倒，倒；倾注；下（雨雪）|To cause (a liquid, etc.) to flow from a container, etc.|例句：He poured a glass of milk. 他倒了一杯牛奶。|例句：The rain poured down all day.  雨一整天都下个不停。|近义词：spill, cascade,  |反义词:collect, gather.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She pours milk into her cereal every morning. = 她每天早上都会把牛奶倒进麦片里。
He pours his heart out to his friend. = 他向朋友倾诉衷肠。


一般过去时:
I poured the water into the vase. = 我把水倒进花瓶里。
The heavy rain poured down last night. = 昨晚下了一场大雨。


现在完成时:
She has poured all the tea. = 她已经把所有的茶都倒好了。
It has poured with rain all day. = 整天都在下大雨。


过去完成时:
He had poured the coffee before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经把咖啡倒好了。
Before the storm hit, it had poured steadily for hours. =暴风雨来临之前，已经持续下了好几个小时的大雨。


将来时:
I will pour you a glass of wine. = 我会给你倒一杯酒。
They will pour the concrete tomorrow. = 他们明天会浇灌混凝土。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pour myself a cup of tea. = 如果我有更多时间，我会给自己倒一杯茶。
If it hadn't poured so heavily, we would have gone for a walk. = 如果雨下得没那么大，我们就去散步了。


被动语态:
The concrete was poured yesterday. = 混凝土昨天浇注了。
The wine is being poured now. = 酒现在正在倒。


疑问句:
Will you pour me a drink? = 你会给我倒一杯饮料吗？
Is it still pouring outside? = 外面还在下大雨吗？


否定句:
He didn't pour the milk. = 他没有倒牛奶。
It wasn't pouring when we left. = 我们离开时没有下雨。


PHRASE->
pour out:倾诉；大量涌出
pour over:仔细研读；反复考虑
pour in:大量涌入
pour forth:奔涌而出；大量涌出
pour down:倾盆而下
