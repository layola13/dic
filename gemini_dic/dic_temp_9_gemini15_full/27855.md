canonizer:/ˈkænənaɪzər/| n. | 承认…为圣徒者；使…成为经典；规范化者 | A person or thing that canonizes;  one who declares someone a saint; something that establishes something as authoritative or classic.  |例句：The Pope acted as the canonizer, officially declaring the nun a saint. 教皇充当了承认圣徒者，正式宣布这位修女为圣徒。

The process of canonization itself is a complex canonizer, establishing a set of rules and regulations.  规范化过程本身就是一个复杂的规范化者，它建立了一套规则和规章制度。|近义词：sanctifier,  authorizer, standardizer |反义词：heretic, denier,  desecrator |名词
