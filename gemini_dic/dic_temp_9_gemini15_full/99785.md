interment:/ɪnˈtɜːrmənt/| n. |埋葬，下葬 |The act of burying a body.  It can also refer to the state of being buried. |例句：The interment took place at dawn.  = 葬礼在黎明时分举行。

The interment of the soldier was a solemn occasion. = 这位士兵的葬礼是一个庄严的场合。


近义词：burial, entombment |反义词：exhumation
