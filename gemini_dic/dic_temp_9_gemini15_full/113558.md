macaroni:/ˌmækəˈroʊni/ | n. |通心粉，意大利面 |A type of pasta made from durum wheat flour. It is typically short, tube-shaped, and cooked in boiling water. |例句：I like to eat macaroni and cheese for dinner. 我喜欢吃通心粉和奶酪当晚餐。|例句：She added some grated cheese to the macaroni. 她往通心粉里加了一些磨碎的奶酪。|近义词：pasta, noodles |反义词：无
