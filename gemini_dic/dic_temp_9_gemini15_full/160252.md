pontil:/ˈpɒntɪl/| n.|点;  (玻璃吹制中的)点杖|A pontil is a metal rod used by glassblowers to support a molten glass object during shaping.  It leaves a small pontil mark on the finished piece.|The glassblower used a pontil to manipulate the hot glass.|近义词：rod, stick|反义词:
