noncapillaries:/ˌnɒnkæˈpɪləriz/| n. |非毛细血管|Noncapillaries refer to blood vessels that are not capillaries.  They are larger vessels that transport blood throughout the body, excluding the smallest ones that facilitate exchange between blood and tissues. |Example:  The study focused on the effects of the drug on noncapillary blood vessels. 这项研究的重点是该药物对非毛细血管的影响。|近义词: larger blood vessels, arterioles, venules |反义词: capillaries
