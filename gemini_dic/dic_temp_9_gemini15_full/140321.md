It suggests a system or regime marked by tyranny and the suppression of others.  |Example: The people suffered under years of ogreism, marked by arbitrary arrests and public executions.  人民在多年的暴政下受苦，其特征是任意逮捕和公开处决。|近义词：tyranny, despotism, oppression, brutality |反义词：benevolence, kindness, justice, fairness |名词
