compartments:/kəmˈpɑːrtmənts/ | n. |隔间；舱；隔层 | Separate sections or divisions within a larger structure or container.  |例句：The suitcase has several compartments for different items.  这个手提箱有几个隔层用来存放不同的物品。

例句：The ship's cargo was divided into different compartments. 船上的货物被分成不同的舱室。


近义词：divisions, sections, chambers, cells |反义词：open space, entirety
