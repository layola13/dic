ooelogist:  /oʊˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 卵学家 |  A person who studies eggs, especially bird eggs, their structure, development, and classification.  |  The ooelogist meticulously documented the size, color, and markings of each bird egg. 卵学家一丝不苟地记录了每枚鸟蛋的大小、颜色和斑点。| ornithologist,  egg collector |  (No direct antonym exists; perhaps "ornithophobe" if considering a phobia of birds, which are related to egg study.) | 名词
