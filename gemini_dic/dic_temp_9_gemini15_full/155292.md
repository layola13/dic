phantasize-about: /ˈfæntəsaɪz əˈbaʊt/ | v. | 幻想；空想；梦想 | To imagine or daydream about something, especially something pleasant or unreal.  | 例句：I often phantasize about winning the lottery. 我经常幻想赢得彩票。

I sometimes phantasize about living on a tropical island. 我有时会幻想住在热带岛屿上。


近义词：daydream about, dream about, fantasize about, imagine, envision

反义词：focus on reality, be realistic


USAGE->

She phantasizes about traveling the world. = 她幻想环游世界。
He phantasizes about becoming a famous musician. = 他幻想成为一名著名的音乐家。


一般过去时:
I phantasized about escaping to a quiet cottage. = 我幻想逃到一个安静的小屋。
They phantasized about their future together. = 他们幻想他们共同的未来。


现在完成时:
