self-union:/ˈsɛlf ˈjuːnjən/| n.|自我联合，自结合|The process by which an individual or entity unites with itself, often implying a merging of disparate parts or aspects of the self into a unified whole. This can be a psychological, spiritual, or philosophical concept.  It is rarely used as a standalone term and often appears within a broader context.|例句：The artist's self-union was evident in the harmonious blend of styles in her latest work.  艺术家在她最新的作品中风格和谐的融合，体现了她自我统一的境界。|近义词：self-integration, unification, synthesis, wholeness |反义词：fragmentation, dissociation, division
