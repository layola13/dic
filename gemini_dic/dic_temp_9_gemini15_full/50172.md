deodorize:/ˌdiˈoʊdəraɪz/| vt.| 除臭；去味 | To remove or neutralize unpleasant smells.  |例句：We need to deodorize the room after the fire.  我们需要在火灾后给房间除臭。

例句：The air freshener helped deodorize the musty basement. 空气清新剂帮助除去了潮湿地下室的霉味。


近义词：disinfect, purify, freshen


反义词：odorize, stink up

词源:
The word "deodorize" is formed from the prefix "de-" (meaning "to remove" or "to deprive of"), and the word "odor," meaning "smell."  The suffix "-ize" turns a noun into a verb, indicating the action of removing odor.  Therefore, "deodorize" literally means "to remove odor."
