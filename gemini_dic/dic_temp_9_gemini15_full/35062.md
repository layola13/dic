closereefed: /ˈkloʊzˌriːfd/ | adj. | 收紧帆的；帆收得很紧的 | Close-reefed describes a sail that has been reduced to its smallest size by tightly furling it. This is done in extremely strong winds or stormy weather to reduce the sail area and prevent the boat from capsizing.  | The boat was closereefed and struggling against the ferocious storm.  船只收紧了帆，艰难地对抗着猛烈的风暴。 The captain ordered the crew to closereef all sails before the squall hit. 船长命令船员在暴风雨来临之前收紧所有帆。| reefed, furled | unfurled, full sail | 形容词
