moldings:/ˈmoʊldɪŋz/ | n. |线脚；装饰条；造型；铸件 |Decorative strips or shapes used to adorn or finish something, often made of wood, plaster, or plastic.  They can be used on walls, ceilings, floors, and furniture. |例句：The ornate moldings on the ceiling added to the room's elegance. 天花板上的华丽线脚增添了房间的优雅。

The house features beautiful crown moldings. 这房子有漂亮的天花板线脚。


近义词：trim, ornamentation, cornice, frame


反义词：plainness, simplicity


词源：源自于动词mold，意思是“塑造，成型”，加上名词后缀-ing，表示“成型的东西”，最终演变为“线脚，装饰条”的意思。  The word ultimately derives from the Old English word *mōldan, meaning "to shape" or "to form".
