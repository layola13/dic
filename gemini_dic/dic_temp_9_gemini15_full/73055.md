footmark:/ˈfʊtˌmɑːrk/ | n. |足迹；脚印 | A mark made by a foot; a footprint.  |例句：The detective carefully examined the footmarks in the mud.  侦探仔细检查了泥地里的足迹。

例句：I found a faint footmark on the dusty floor. 我在尘土飞扬的地板上发现了一个淡淡的脚印。

近义词： footprint, track, impression | 反义词：None  | 词性：名词
