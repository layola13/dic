miscategorizing:/ˌmɪsˈkætəɡəraɪzɪŋ/| v. |错误分类；误分类|The act of placing something into the wrong category or class.  This often implies a mistake in judgment or understanding.|例句：Miscategorizing these documents led to significant delays in the project.  错误地分类这些文件导致项目严重延误。

例句：He was miscategorizing his expenses, leading to an inaccurate budget. 他错误地分类了他的支出，导致预算不准确。


近义词：misclassifying, mislabeling, misfiling, wrongly classifying

反义词：categorizing correctly, properly classifying
