launders:/ˈlɔːndərz/| vt.|洗涤（衣物）|To wash (clothes) in a washing machine or by hand.|例句：I need to launder my shirts before the interview. 我需要在面试前洗我的衬衫。|例句：She launders all the family's clothes every week. 她每周都洗全家的衣服。|近义词：wash, clean|反义词：soil, dirty|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She launders her clothes every Saturday. = 她每个星期六都洗衣服。
He launders his shirts before important meetings. = 他在重要会议前洗衬衫。

一般过去时:
