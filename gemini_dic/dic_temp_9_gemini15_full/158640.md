plighting:/ˈplaɪtɪŋ/| v.|保证；许诺；誓约|The act of pledging or promising solemnly;  giving one's word or troth, especially in marriage.  It often implies a strong commitment and seriousness. |例句：He plighted his troth to her at the altar. 他在祭坛上向她许下誓言。|例句：The couple plighted their love for each other before their families. 这对夫妇在家人面前表达了彼此的爱意。|近义词：pledging, promising, vowing, betrothing|反义词：breaking a promise, reneging
