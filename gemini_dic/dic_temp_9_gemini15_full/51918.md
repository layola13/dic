dhurrie:/ˈdʒʊri/| n. |杜丽地毯，粗呢地毯|A dhurrie is a type of flat-woven carpet or rug, typically made in India, characterized by its simple design and durable construction.  They are often made from cotton, wool, or a blend of materials. They are known for their relatively inexpensive price and their versatility in decorating various styles of homes.|The dhurrie rug added a touch of rustic charm to the living room. |近义词：rug, carpet, throw rug|反义词： (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with intricately designed carpets)
