serfdom:/ˈsɜːrfdəm/ | n. |农奴制度；农奴身份 | The state of being a serf; the system of serfdom.  Serfdom was a system of feudalism in which peasants were bound to the land and owed services to their lord. |  例句：The abolition of serfdom in Russia was a significant historical event.  俄罗斯废除农奴制是一件重要的历史事件。

例句：Life under serfdom was characterized by hardship and oppression.  农奴制度下的生活以艰辛和压迫为特征。


近义词：peonage, bondage, servitude | 反义词：freedom, liberty

词源:
