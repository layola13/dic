newcomers:/ˈnuːˌkʌmərz/| n.| 新来者，新手 | People who have recently arrived in a place or joined a group.  |Example: The newcomers to the city were overwhelmed by the traffic.|近义词：arrivals, newcomers, immigrants |反义词：veterans, old-timers
