relinquishes: /rɪˈlɪŋkwɪʃɪz/ | v. | 放弃；让与；交出 | To give up something; to surrender or release something. |例句：He relinquished his claim to the throne. 他放弃了他对王位的继承权。|例句：The company relinquished control of its subsidiary. 这家公司放弃了对其子公司的控制权。 |近义词：abandon, surrender, cede, forfeit, relinquish  |反义词：retain, keep, hold, acquire, seize | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He relinquishes his rights every year. 他每年都放弃他的权利。
She relinquishes her position to a younger colleague. 她把职位让给了更年轻的同事。


一般过去时:
He relinquished his claim to the property. 他放弃了他对该房产的所有权。
The general relinquished command of the troops. 将军放弃了对部队的指挥权。


现在完成时:
She has relinquished her ambition to become a doctor. 她已经放弃了她成为医生的雄心。
He has relinquished his pursuit of wealth. 他已经放弃了他追求财富的努力。


过去完成时:
They had relinquished their hope of rescue. 他们已经放弃了获救的希望。
The soldiers had relinquished their weapons before surrendering. 士兵们在投降前已经交出了武器。


将来时:
She will relinquish her job next month. 她下个月将辞职。
He will relinquish his seat on the board. 他将辞去董事会职务。


虚拟语气:
If he relinquished his control, the company might succeed. 如果他放弃控制权，公司可能会成功。
I wish he had relinquished his stubbornness earlier. 我希望他早点放弃他的顽固。


被动语态:
His rights were relinquished by him. 他的权利被他放弃了。
Control of the company was relinquished by the CEO. 公司的控制权由首席执行官放弃。


疑问句:
Will he relinquish his position? 他会放弃他的职位吗？
Did she relinquish her claim? 她放弃了她的索赔吗？


否定句:
He did not relinquish his power easily. 他并没有轻易放弃他的权力。
She refused to relinquish her belief. 她拒绝放弃她的信念。

词源:
From Old French *relinquir, from Latin relinquere "to leave behind, abandon," from re- "again" + linquere "to leave."
