corduroyed:/ˈkɔːrdərɔɪd/| adj.|灯芯绒的；用灯芯绒制成的|Covered or made of corduroy.  Corduroy is a type of fabric with a distinctive ribbed texture.|例句：She wore a corduroyed jacket. = 她穿着一件灯芯绒外套。

例句：The sofa was upholstered in rich, corduroyed fabric. = 沙发是用质地优良的灯芯绒面料包裹的。


近义词：velveteen, ribbed (describing the texture)
反义词：smooth, plain (describing the texture)

词性:形容词
