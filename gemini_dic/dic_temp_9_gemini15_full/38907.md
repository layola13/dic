confesser:/kənˈfɛsər/| n. v.|忏悔者；告解神父；承认者|One who confesses; a priest who hears confessions.|例句：The confessor listened patiently to the penitent's confession. 告解神父耐心地听着忏悔者的忏悔。 The confessor admitted his guilt. 忏悔者承认了他的罪行。|近义词：penitent,  declarer,  avower|反义词：denier,  concealer|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He confesses his sins every week. 他每周都忏悔他的罪过。
She confesses her love for him. 她承认爱他。


一般过去时:
He confessed his crime to the police. 他向警方承认了他的罪行。
She confessed her mistake to her boss. 她向老板承认了她的错误。


现在完成时:
He has confessed to the murder. 他已经承认了谋杀罪。
She has confessed all her secrets. 她已经承认了她所有的秘密。


过去完成时:
He had confessed his guilt before the trial. 他在审判前已经承认了他的罪行。
She had confessed her feelings before he left. 他离开之前，她已经表白了她的感情。


将来时:
He will confess his wrongdoing soon. 他很快就会承认自己的错误。
She will confess everything to her parents. 她会把一切都告诉她的父母。


虚拟语气:
If he confessed his sins, he would feel better. 如果他忏悔他的罪过，他会感觉更好。
If she confessed her feelings, he might forgive her. 如果她表白了她的感情，他可能会原谅她。


被动语态:
His guilt was confessed by him. 他的罪行被他承认了。(较为不自然，少用)
The truth was confessed by the witness.  证人承认了真相。(较为不自然，少用)


疑问句:
Will he confess his involvement? 他会承认他的参与吗？
Did she confess to the crime? 她承认了罪行吗？


否定句:
He did not confess to the authorities. 他没有向当局承认。
She refused to confess her part in the scheme. 她拒绝承认她在该计划中扮演的角色。



PHRASE->

make a confession 坦白交代


词源:
The word "confessor" comes from the Latin word "confessor," meaning "one who confesses."  The Latin verb "confiteri" means "to acknowledge," "to avow," or "to admit."  The word's evolution reflects its religious origins, primarily associated with the sacrament of confession in Christianity.
