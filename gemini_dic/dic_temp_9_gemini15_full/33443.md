churn out:/tʃɜːrn aʊt/| vt.|大量生产；快速制作；粗制滥造|to produce something quickly and in large quantities, often without much care or quality.|The factory churns out thousands of shoes every day.这家工厂每天生产数千双鞋。|近义词：mass-produce, produce rapidly, crank out|反义词：handcraft, painstakingly create


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They churn out new products every month. = 他们每个月都大量生产新产品。
The factory churns out widgets at an astonishing rate. = 这家工厂以惊人的速度大量生产小玩意儿。


一般过去时:
The company churned out low-quality toys last year. = 公司去年大量生产低质量玩具。
She churned out several novels in a short period. = 她短时间内快速创作了几部小说。


现在完成时:
They have churned out hundreds of articles this year. = 他们今年已经粗制滥造了数百篇文章。
The writer has churned out countless blog posts. = 这个作家已经写出了无数的博客文章。


过去完成时:
Before the deadline, they had churned out all the required reports. = 在截止日期之前，他们已经完成了所有必需的报告。
By the end of the year, she had churned out three books. = 到年底，她已经完成了三本书。


将来时:
The publishing house will churn out another bestseller next year. = 出版社明年将再推出另一部畅销书。
They will churn out many more products in the future. = 未来他们将生产更多产品。


虚拟语气:
If they had more time, they wouldn't churn out such low-quality work. = 如果他们有更多时间，他们就不会粗制滥造出这样低质量的作品。
I wish they didn't churn out so many low-quality products. = 我希望他们不要生产这么多低质量的产品。


被动语态:
Thousands of cars are churned out by the factory each year. = 工厂每年生产数千辆汽车。
Many novels are churned out by this prolific author. = 这位多产的作家创作了许多小说。


疑问句:
Does this company churn out only cheap products? = 这家公司只生产廉价产品吗？
How many books does he churn out every year? = 他每年创作多少本书？


否定句:
They don't churn out high-quality goods. = 他们不生产高质量的商品。
The artist doesn't churn out his work; he carefully crafts each piece. =这位艺术家并不粗制滥造他的作品；他仔细地制作每一件作品。

词源:
The verb "churn" originally meant to agitate or shake violently, like churning butter.  The "out" adds the sense of producing something in a continuous and rapid manner.  Therefore, "churn out" implies a process of rapid and often repetitive production.
