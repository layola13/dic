radiating:/ˈreɪdieɪtɪŋ/| adj.| 发光的；辐射的；散发（热量等）的；| radiating means spreading outwards from a central point. It can describe light, heat, or other forms of energy, or more figuratively, emotions or influence.  |例句：The sun was radiating heat. 太阳正在散发热量。

The fire radiated warmth throughout the room. 火温暖了整个房间。|近义词：emitting, emanating, spreading, diffusing |反义词：absorbing, concentrating, focusing
