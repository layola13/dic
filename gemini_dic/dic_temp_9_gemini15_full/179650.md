reengagement:/ˌriːɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt/ | n. |重新参与；重新投入；再婚 | The act of engaging again; a renewed commitment or involvement.  It can refer to a renewed commitment to a task, relationship, or cause.  |例句：The company's reengagement with its customers led to increased sales. 公司与客户的重新联系导致销售额增加。

The reengagement of the former employees boosted the project's morale. 前员工的重新参与提升了项目的士气。


近义词：reinvolvement, recommitment, resumption | 反义词：disengagement, withdrawal, abandonment | 词性：名词
