misbuild:/ˌmɪsˈbɪld/| v.| 错误建造，建造不良 | To construct something badly or incorrectly.  |例句：The house was misbuilt, leading to structural problems. 这房子建造得很糟糕，导致了结构问题。  The bridge was misbuilt and collapsed after only a few years. 这座桥建造不良，几年后就倒塌了。|近义词：build poorly, construct badly, botch |反义词：build well, construct properly
