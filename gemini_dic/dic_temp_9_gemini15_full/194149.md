self-instructions:/ˌsɛlfɪnˈstrʌkʃənz/| n. |自我指导，自我指示，自我规训|Instructions given to oneself; guidance or direction provided by oneself.  It often refers to a process of learning or behavioral modification where an individual sets their own goals and provides their own feedback.|Example:  Through self-instructions, she managed to overcome her fear of public speaking. 通过自我指导，她克服了公开演讲的恐惧。|近义词：self-direction, self-management, self-training|反义词：external guidance, coercion
