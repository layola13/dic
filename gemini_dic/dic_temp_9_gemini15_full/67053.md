explicate:/ˈɛkspləkeɪt/ | vt. |解释，阐明| to make something clear; to explain something in detail. |例句：The professor explicated the complex theory in a clear and concise manner. 教授以清晰简洁的方式解释了复杂的理论。|例句：She explicated her reasons for leaving the job. 她解释了她辞职的原因。|近义词：explain, elucidate, clarify |反义词：obscure, obfuscate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He explicates the poem line by line. = 他逐行解释这首诗。
The teacher explicates the grammar rules clearly. = 老师清晰地解释了语法规则。

一般过去时:
The critic explicated the author's intentions. = 批评家阐明了作者的意图。
She explicated the historical context of the painting. = 她解释了这幅画的历史背景。

现在完成时:
He has explicated the difficult concepts in his book. = 他在他的书中阐明了那些难以理解的概念。
The scholar has explicated the ancient text for modern readers. = 学者为现代读者解释了古代文本。

过去完成时:
They had explicated the problem before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经解释清楚了这个问题。
The students had explicated the assignment thoroughly before submitting it. = 学生们在提交作业之前已经彻底地解释清楚了作业。

将来时:

She will explicate her ideas more fully in the next chapter. = 她将在下一章更全面地阐述她的想法。

虚拟语气:
If he explicated his ideas more clearly, people would understand better. = 如果他更清晰地阐述他的想法，人们就会更好地理解。
I wish the author had explicated the ending more effectively. = 我希望作者能更有效地解释结尾。

被动语态:
The theory was explicated in detail in the textbook. = 这理论在教科书中得到了详细的解释。
The poem's meaning was explicated by the professor. = 教授解释了这首诗的意思。

疑问句:
How will you explicate this complex equation? = 你将如何解释这个复杂的方程式？
Will the speaker explicate the controversial aspects of the topic? = 演讲者会解释这个话题的有争议的方面吗？

否定句:
He did not explicate his position clearly enough. = 他没有足够清晰地阐明他的立场。
The article failed to explicate the underlying causes of the problem. = 这篇文章未能解释问题的根本原因。
