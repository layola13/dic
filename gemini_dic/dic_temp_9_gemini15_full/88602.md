hittite:/ˈhɪtaɪt/| n. |赫梯人；赫梯语；赫梯文物|The Hittites were an ancient Anatolian people who established a large empire in the Bronze Age.  Their civilization is known for its advanced metalworking, legal codes, and military prowess.|例句：The Hittite empire flourished for centuries before its eventual decline. 赫梯帝国在最终衰落之前繁荣了几个世纪。|例句：Scholars continue to decipher Hittite cuneiform tablets to learn more about their culture. 学者们继续破译赫梯楔形文字泥板以了解更多关于他们的文化。|近义词：Anatolian (referring to the region)|反义词：None.
