nonsmokers:/ˈnɒnˌsmoʊkərz/ | n. | 不吸烟者 | People who do not smoke tobacco.  | Example: The restaurant has a section for nonsmokers. 这家餐厅有为不吸烟者准备的区域。  |近义词：non-smokers, smokers' abstainers |反义词：smokers
