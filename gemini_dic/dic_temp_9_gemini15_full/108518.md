lays:/leɪz/| v.|放置；产卵；下蛋；铺设；呈现在；陈述；提出；设计；计划|to put something down gently or carefully; to produce eggs; to arrange something in a flat layer; to present something in a particular way; to state something; to plan or design something|例句：She lays the baby gently in the crib. 她轻轻地把婴儿放在婴儿床里。|例句：The hen lays eggs every day. 母鸡每天都下蛋。|近义词：put, place, deposit, set, produce|反义词：remove, lift, take away
