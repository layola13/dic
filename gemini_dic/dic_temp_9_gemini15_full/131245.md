noncarbonate:/ˌnɒnˈkɑːrbənət/| adj. |非碳酸盐的|Not containing carbonate.  This term is used to describe rocks, minerals, and sediments that lack carbonate minerals (like calcite or dolomite).  They are typically composed of silicate minerals or other non-carbonate materials.|例句：The noncarbonate sediments were primarily composed of quartz and feldspar.  非碳酸盐沉积物主要由石英和长石组成。

The analysis revealed a high proportion of noncarbonate minerals in the sample. 分析显示样品中含有大量的非碳酸盐矿物。|近义词：siliceous, silicious|反义词：carbonate
