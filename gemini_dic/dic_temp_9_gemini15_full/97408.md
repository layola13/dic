infringed:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒd/| vt.|侵犯，违反|To violate or break a law, rule, agreement, etc.; to encroach upon.  |例句：The company infringed the copyright of the song. 这家公司侵犯了这首歌的版权。 |例句：Their actions infringed upon my rights. 他们的行为侵犯了我的权利。|近义词：violate, breach, transgress, encroach upon|反义词：respect, uphold, obey


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He infringes the law. = 他违反了法律。
They infringe the rules. = 他们违反了规则。

一般过去时:
She infringed the copyright. = 她侵犯了版权。
They infringed on our privacy. = 他们侵犯了我们的隐私。

现在完成时:
He has infringed the agreement. = 他已经违反了协议。
We have infringed upon their territory. = 我们已经侵犯了他们的领土。

过去完成时:
They had infringed the treaty before the war broke out. = 战争爆发前，他们就已经违反了条约。
She had infringed upon his rights many times. = 她已经多次侵犯了他的权利。

将来时:
He will infringe the patent. = 他将会侵犯专利。
They will infringe the contract. = 他们将会违反合同。

虚拟语气:
If he infringed the law, he would be punished. = 如果他违反了法律，他将会受到惩罚。
I wish they hadn't infringed upon my time. = 我真希望他们没有占用我的时间。

被动语态:
The copyright was infringed. = 版权被侵犯了。
The rules were infringed. = 规则被违反了。

疑问句:
Did they infringe the agreement? = 他们违反了协议吗？
Has he infringed upon your rights? = 他侵犯了你的权利吗？

否定句:
He did not infringe the law. = 他没有违反法律。
They did not infringe upon our privacy. = 他们没有侵犯我们的隐私。
