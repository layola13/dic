destabilizes: /ˌdiːˈsteɪbəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使不稳定；动摇；破坏稳定 | To make (something) unstable or insecure.  It often refers to systems, governments, or situations. | 近义词：undermine, weaken, upset, disrupt | 反义词：stabilize, strengthen, secure


例句：

The economic crisis destabilizes the government.  = 经济危机动摇了政府。

The recent political upheaval destabilizes the region. = 最近的政治动荡使该地区不稳定。


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The constant changes destabilize the market. =  持续的变化使市场不稳定。
This policy destabilizes the social order. = 这项政策破坏了社会秩序。


一般过去时:
The earthquake destabilized the building's foundations. = 地震破坏了建筑物的基础。
The sudden resignation destabilized the company. = 突然的辞职使公司动荡不安。


现在完成时:
The drought has destabilized the agricultural sector. = 干旱已经破坏了农业部门。
Years of conflict have destabilized the entire country. = 多年的冲突已经使整个国家不稳定。


过去完成时:
The previous government's policies had destabilized the economy before the new one took over. = 前政府的政策在换届之前就已经破坏了经济。
The company's poor management had destabilized the workforce before the strike. = 公司糟糕的管理在罢工前就已经动摇了员工队伍。


将来时:
The proposed reforms will destabilize the existing power structure. = 提出的改革将破坏现有的权力结构。
Further military intervention will destabilize the peace process. = 进一步的军事干预将破坏和平进程。


虚拟语气:
If we continue down this path, it will destabilize the whole system. = 如果我们继续走这条路，它将使整个系统不稳定。
If they had not intervened, the situation would have destabilized further. = 如果他们没有干预，情况将进一步恶化。


被动语态:
The region was destabilized by the influx of refugees. = 该地区因难民涌入而动荡不安。
The government was destabilized by the opposition's actions. = 政府因反对派的行动而动荡不安。


疑问句:
Will this new law destabilize the financial markets? = 这项新法律会扰乱金融市场吗？
Does the current situation destabilize the peace negotiations? = 目前的情况是否会破坏和平谈判？


否定句:
The government's actions did not destabilize the country. = 政府的行动并没有使国家动荡不安。
The reforms did not destabilize the social order. = 改革没有破坏社会秩序。
