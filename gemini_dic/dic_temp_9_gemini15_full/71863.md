fleshing:/ˈfleʃɪŋ/| v.|去肉；剥皮；使丰满；具体化|To remove flesh or skin from something; to make something fuller or more complete; to give something substance or detail.|例句：The tanner is fleshing the hides. 制革工人正在剥动物的皮。  The sculptor was fleshing out the details of the statue. 雕塑家正在完善雕塑的细节。|近义词：skinning, flaying, detailing, elaborating|反义词：thinning, simplifying
