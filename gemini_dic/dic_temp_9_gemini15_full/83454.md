habitually:/həˈbɪtʃuəli/ | adv. |习惯性地，惯常地 | habitually means in a habitual manner; as a habit. It describes actions or behaviors that are performed regularly or frequently, often without conscious thought.  | He habitually checks his phone before bed. 他习惯睡前查看手机。|She habitually arrives late to meetings. 她习惯性地开会迟到。 | usually, customarily, regularly | unusually, occasionally, rarely | 副词
