minibus:/ˈmɪnɪbʌs/| n. |小型巴士，小巴 |A minibus is a small bus, typically carrying between 8 and 16 passengers.  It's often used for transporting small groups of people, for example, on airport transfers or for school trips.|例句：We took a minibus to the airport. 我们乘坐小巴去机场。

例句：The school uses minibuses to transport students on field trips. 学校用小巴送学生去实地考察。

近义词：small bus, van (in some contexts)
反义词：large bus, coach


词性：名词
