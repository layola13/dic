prancer:/ˈprænsər/| n. v.|小跑的人；轻快地跑；腾跃|A prancer is someone or something that prances, which means to move in a lively way with high steps.  It can also refer to a reindeer. |例句：The children pranced excitedly around the Christmas tree. 孩子们兴奋地在圣诞树周围蹦蹦跳跳。 The prancer led the sleigh.  那只轻快奔跑的驯鹿拉着雪橇。|近义词：dancer,stepper,jumper|反义词：crawler, shuffler


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prances around the room. = 他在房间里蹦蹦跳跳。
The horse prances gracefully. = 这匹马优雅地腾跃。

一般过去时:
The ballerina pranced across the stage. = 芭蕾舞演员在舞台上轻盈地跳跃。
The children pranced with joy. = 孩子们高兴地跳跃。

现在完成时:
The reindeer have pranced all through the night. = 驯鹿整夜都在欢快地奔跑。
She has pranced her way to victory. = 她一路轻快地跳跃走向胜利。

过去完成时:
The horse had pranced before the race began. = 赛马开始前，马已经轻快地跳跃了。
The children had pranced until they were tired. = 孩子们跳到累了才停下来。

将来时:
The dancers will prance on stage tonight. = 舞蹈家们今晚将在舞台上表演轻快跳跃。
He will prance around when he hears the good news. = 他听到好消息时会蹦蹦跳跳。

虚拟语气:
If he were a dancer, he would prance with grace. = 如果他是个舞蹈演员，他会优雅地跳跃。
I wish I could prance like a ballerina. = 我希望我能像芭蕾舞演员一样轻盈地跳跃。

被动语态:
The toy horse is pranced by the child. =  玩具马被孩子玩得蹦蹦跳跳。
(This is a less common usage for "prance" in passive voice,  more naturally described as "The child pranced the toy horse.")


疑问句:
Does the horse prance easily? = 这匹马容易腾跃吗？
Will they prance during the parade? = 他们会在游行期间轻快地跳跃吗？

否定句:
The tired horse did not prance. = 那匹疲惫的马没有跳跃。
He doesn't prance around like that anymore. = 他不再那样蹦蹦跳跳了。
