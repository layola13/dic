demilitarizations: /ˌdiːˌmɪlɪtəraɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非军事化；解除武装 | The process of removing military forces or weapons from a place or region.  It often involves the dismantling of military bases, the withdrawal of troops, and the destruction of weapons.  This process can be voluntary or imposed, and is often a key element in peace agreements or conflict resolution. | Example: The demilitarizations of the border zone were a crucial step towards lasting peace.  边界地区的非军事化是实现持久和平的关键一步。 The demilitarizations of the islands reduced the risk of conflict. 岛屿的非军事化降低了冲突的风险。| 近义词：disarmament, neutralization | 反义词：militarization
