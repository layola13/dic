forgivingness:/fərˈɡɪvɪŋnəs/| n.|宽恕；饶恕；宽容|The quality of being ready or willing to forgive someone who has done something wrong.  It emphasizes the act of pardoning and letting go of resentment or anger.|例句：Her forgivingness surprised everyone.  她的宽容让每个人都感到惊讶。

例句：His forgivingness towards his enemies was legendary. 他对敌人的宽容是出了名的。


近义词：clemency, mercy, pardon, tolerance, indulgence|反义词：vengefulness, unforgivingness, resentment
