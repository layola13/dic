hypsometry:/haɪˈpɒsmɪtri/| n. |测高法，高度测定|The study of the altitudes of landforms, typically using contour lines on a map to represent elevation. It involves measuring and mapping the heights of various points on the Earth's surface, providing information about the topography and relief of an area.  |Example: Hypsometry is crucial for understanding the distribution of landforms and for various applications in geography, geology, and environmental science.  高度测定对于理解地貌分布以及地理、地质和环境科学的各种应用至关重要。|近义词：altimetry, elevation measurement, topographic surveying|反义词：planimetry (measuring horizontal distances)|名词
