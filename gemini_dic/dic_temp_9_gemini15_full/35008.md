clone:/kloʊn/|ˈkləʊn| n./v.|克隆；复制品|

英文解析：A clone is an exact copy of a biological organism, or a cell, or a gene, produced asexually from a single ancestor.  As a verb, it means to produce such a copy.

例句：
Scientists successfully cloned a sheep. = 科学家成功地克隆了一只绵羊。

The cloned plant looked exactly like its parent. = 克隆的植物看起来与其母本完全一样。


近义词：copy, duplicate, replica

反义词：original, unique


词性:Noun, Verb
