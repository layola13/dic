patient: /ˈpeɪʃənt/ | n. adj. | 病人；患者；病人；耐心的；忍耐的 |

英文解析:
As a noun, "patient" refers to a person receiving medical treatment or care. As an adjective, it describes someone who is able to wait calmly without getting annoyed or upset.

例句：
The doctor examined the patient carefully.  医生仔细检查了病人。

The nurse was very patient with the crying child. 护士对哭闹的孩子非常有耐心。


近义词:
(noun) client, sufferer, customer
(adjective) tolerant, enduring, forbearing, persevering


反义词:
(adjective) impatient, irritable, intolerant


词性:
Noun, Adjective
