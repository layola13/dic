reinforce:/ˌriːɪnˈfɔːrs/| vt.|加强，加固；增强；补充兵员|To strengthen or support, especially with additional material or personnel.  It implies making something stronger or more effective.|例句：We need to reinforce the walls before the storm. 我们需要在暴风雨来临之前加固墙壁。

例句：The general reinforced his troops with fresh soldiers.将军用新兵加强了他的部队。


近义词：strengthen, support, bolster, buttress|反义词：weaken, undermine, sabotage|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reinforce the bridge regularly. = 他们定期加固桥梁。
The teacher reinforces positive behaviors. = 老师强化积极的行为。

一般过去时:
The army reinforced its position. = 军队巩固了它的阵地。
She reinforced her argument with evidence. = 她用证据加强了她的论点。

现在完成时:
We have reinforced the security measures. = 我们已经加强了安全措施。
The company has reinforced its commitment to sustainability. = 公司已经加强了其对可持续发展的承诺。

过去完成时:
They had reinforced the dam before the flood. = 洪水来临之前，他们已经加固了水坝。
She had reinforced her belief in his innocence. = 她已经更加坚信了他的清白。

将来时:
We will reinforce the team with new members. = 我们将用新成员加强团队。
The government will reinforce its border controls. = 政府将加强其边境管控。

虚拟语气:
If we reinforced the structure, it would be more stable. = 如果我们加固结构，它会更稳定。
I wish they had reinforced the defenses earlier. = 我希望他们早些加强防御。

被动语态:
The bridge was reinforced last year. = 这座桥去年进行了加固。
The argument was reinforced by new data. = 论点得到了新数据的支持。

疑问句:
How can we reinforce the team's morale? = 我们如何增强团队士气？
Will the company reinforce its marketing campaign? = 公司会加强其营销活动吗？

否定句:
They did not reinforce the damaged section of the road. = 他们没有加固道路受损的路段。
The evidence did not reinforce his alibi. = 证据并没有加强他的不在场证明。


PHRASE->
reinforce concrete = 钢筋混凝土
reinforce learning = 强化学习
reinforce one's position = 巩固立场
reinforce the message = 加强信息
reinforce the idea = 加强观念


词源:
From Old French *renforcer, from re- (intensive prefix) + enforcier "to strengthen," from en- (in) + forcier "to force," from Latin fortis "strong."
