pollinated: /ˈpɑːləneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 受粉的；授粉的 |  Having been pollinated; fertilized.  | 例句：The flowers were beautifully pollinated by the bees.  蜜蜂给花朵进行了美丽的授粉。

The apple tree has been successfully pollinated. 这棵苹果树成功授粉了。


近义词：fertilized, inseminated | 反义词：unpollinated


词源:  From *pollinate* + *-ed*.  *Pollinate* itself comes from the Latin *pollen*, meaning "fine flour" (referring to pollen), and the suffix *-ate*, indicating a process or action.
