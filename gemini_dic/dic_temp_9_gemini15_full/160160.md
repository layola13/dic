pompous:/ˈpɒmpəs/ | /ˈpɑːmpəs/| adj.| 傲慢的；自大的；浮夸的；华而不实的 | Characterized by excessive self-importance; arrogant.  Often used to describe someone who speaks or behaves in a way that is intended to impress others, but comes across as arrogant and pretentious. |例句：He gave a pompous speech filled with meaningless jargon. 他发表了一篇充满无意义术语的傲慢演讲。|例句：Her pompous manner alienated many people. 她傲慢的态度疏远了许多人。|近义词：pretentious, arrogant, haughty, self-important |反义词：humble, modest, unassuming
