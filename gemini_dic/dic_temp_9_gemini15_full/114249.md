mailman:/ˈmeɪlmæn/| n.|邮递员|A person whose job is to deliver mail.|The mailman delivered the package this morning.  今早邮递员送来了包裹。|postman, letter carrier|n/a|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mailman delivers mail every day. = 邮递员每天送邮件。
He usually arrives around noon. = 他通常在中午左右到达。


一般过去时:
The mailman delivered a package yesterday. = 邮递员昨天送了一个包裹。
Did the mailman come this morning? = 邮递员今天早上来过吗？


现在完成时:
The mailman has already delivered the mail. = 邮递员已经送了邮件。
Has the mailman been here yet? = 邮递员来过了吗？

The mailman had already left before I got home. = 我到家之前邮递员已经离开了。

将来时:
The mailman will deliver the mail tomorrow. = 邮递员明天送邮件。
Will the mailman be late today? = 邮递员今天会迟到吗？


虚拟语气:

I wish the mailman would come sooner. = 我希望邮递员能早点来。
