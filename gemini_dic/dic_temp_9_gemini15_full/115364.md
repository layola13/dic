manoeuvre: /məˈnuːvər/ | n. v. |  策略行动；调动；操纵；设法对付 |  A planned movement of troops or warships.  The skillful or clever management of a situation. |

例句：
The general planned a daring manoeuvre to outflank the enemy.  将军策划了一次大胆的军事行动来包围敌人。

The politician skillfully manoeuvred himself into a position of power.  这位政治家巧妙地把自己提升到了权力地位。


近义词： tactic, strategy, maneuver, ploy, scheme, trick


反义词： inaction, passivity


词性：名词和动词
