overdrink:/ˌoʊvərˈdrɪŋk/| vt. |酗酒，喝醉|To drink an excessive amount of alcohol.|例句：He overdrank at the party and missed his train home. 他在派对上喝得烂醉，错过了回家的火车。|近义词：binge drink, overindulge in alcohol|反义词：abstain from alcohol, drink moderately
