overcautioning: /ˌoʊvərˈkɔːʃənɪŋ/ | n. | 过分谨慎；过度小心 | The act of being excessively cautious or careful;  taking precautions to an unnecessary or excessive degree.  It often implies that the level of caution is detrimental to progress, efficiency, or effectiveness. | 例句：His overcautioning prevented him from taking the necessary risks to advance his career.  他的过度谨慎阻止了他冒险前进事业。


His overcautioning about the project's timeline led to missed deadlines. 他对项目时间表的过度谨慎导致了错过最后期限。


近义词：excessive caution, overcarefulness, hypervigilance | 反义词：recklessness, negligence, carelessness | 词性:名词
