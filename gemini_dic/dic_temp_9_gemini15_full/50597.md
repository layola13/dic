derbies:/ˈdɜːrbiz/ | n. |德比帽；德比赛；德比鞋 | A derby is a type of hat, a horse race, or a type of shoe.  The word's origins are varied depending on the context.  As a hat, it's a stiff felt hat with a round crown and a narrow brim. As a race, it typically refers to a major sporting event, often between rival teams or individuals. As a shoe, it's a type of men's lace-up shoe. |
