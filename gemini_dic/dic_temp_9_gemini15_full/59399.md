earth-shatteringly:/ˈɜːθˌʃætərɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人震惊地；极其；非常 | Extremely; to a shocking degree.  The adverb emphasizes the intensity or surprising nature of something. |例句：The news of the company's collapse was earth-shatteringly bad. 公司倒闭的消息令人震惊。

例句：The concert was earth-shatteringly loud. 这场音乐会的声音震耳欲聋。
