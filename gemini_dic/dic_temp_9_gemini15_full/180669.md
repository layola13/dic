regrator:/ˈreɡreɪtər/| n.|囤积居奇者；哄抬物价者|One who buys goods in large quantities to resell them at a higher price, often creating artificial shortages and driving up prices.  A regrator is essentially a type of profiteer who manipulates market supply to their advantage, often to the detriment of consumers.|The regrator bought up all the wheat, causing a famine in the town.|近义词：profiteer, speculator, monopolist|反义词：altruist, philanthropist|名词
