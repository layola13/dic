ladled:/ˈleɪdld/| v.|舀，盛，用勺子舀|To scoop or serve with a ladle.  The verb implies using a ladle to transfer a liquid or semi-liquid substance.|例句：She ladled the soup into bowls. 她用勺子把汤盛进碗里。

例句：He ladled out generous portions of stew. 他慷慨地舀出大份的炖菜。


近义词：scooped, dipped, poured


反义词：None directly opposite, but words suggesting the opposite action might include poured (if referring to a liquid that isn't easily ladled), or emptied.


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
She ladles soup every day. = 她每天都舀汤。
He ladles the gravy onto the potatoes. = 他把肉汁舀到土豆上。

一般过去时:
She ladled the soup into bowls yesterday. = 昨天她用勺子把汤盛进碗里。
He ladled out generous portions of stew at the party. = 他在派对上慷慨地舀出大份的炖菜。

现在完成时:
She has ladled out all the soup. = 她已经把所有的汤都舀完了。
He has ladled the sauce onto the pasta. = 他已经把酱汁舀到意大利面上。

过去完成时:
She had ladled the soup before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经把汤舀好了。
He had ladled out all the ice cream before the children got there. = 孩子们到之前，他已经把所有的冰淇淋都舀完了。

将来时:
She will ladle the stew into serving dishes. = 她将把炖菜舀进盛菜的盘子里。
He will ladle the batter onto the hot griddle. = 他将把面糊舀到热的烤盘上。

虚拟语气:
If she had a bigger ladle, she would ladle the soup faster. = 如果她有一个更大的勺子，她就能更快地舀汤。
I wish he had ladled more gravy onto my potatoes. = 我希望他往我的土豆上多舀些肉汁。

被动语态:
The soup was ladled into bowls by the waitress. = 汤由服务员舀进碗里。
The sauce was ladled onto the plates. = 酱汁被舀到盘子里。

疑问句:
Did she ladle the soup herself? = 她自己舀汤了吗？
Will he ladle the gravy evenly? = 他会均匀地舀肉汁吗？

否定句:
She didn't ladle enough soup for everyone. = 她舀的汤不够大家吃。
He didn't ladle the sauce carefully enough, and it spilled. = 他没有小心地舀酱汁，结果洒了。


PHRASE->
ladled out: 慷慨地给予
ladled in:  (Less common, implying adding something gradually)
ladled up: (Similar to ladled out)
ladled onto: 舀到...上面
