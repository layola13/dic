inveigle:/ɪnˈveɪɡəl/ | vt. | 诱骗；巧妙地劝诱 | To persuade (someone) to do something, typically by using deception or flattery.  | 例句：He inveigled his way into the party. 他巧妙地混进了派对。

The con artist inveigled the elderly woman into giving him her life savings.  那个骗子诱骗这位老太太交出了她的毕生积蓄。


近义词：coax, entice, persuade, wheedle, cajole | 反义词：deter, dissuade


词源:
Old French *enveogler "to entangle," from en- "in" + veogle "snare, trap," of uncertain origin.
