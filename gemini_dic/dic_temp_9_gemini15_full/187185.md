roaming:/ˈroʊmɪŋ/ |/ˈrəʊmɪŋ/| v. n. adj.| 漫游；漫步；漫游的；漫步的 | To move about or travel freely over a wide area.|例句：The cattle were roaming freely in the field. 牛群在田野里自由自在地漫游。  The dog loves roaming the woods. 这只狗喜欢在树林里漫游。|近义词：wandering, rambling, roving|反义词：stationary, fixed
