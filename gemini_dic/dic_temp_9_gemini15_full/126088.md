mutability:/ˌmjuːtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 易变性；可变性 | The quality or state of being mutable; capacity of being changed.  It refers to the ability or tendency of something to change or be changed.  | Example: The mutability of human emotions is a common theme in literature. 人类情感的易变性是文学中的一个常见主题。The mutability of the weather made planning the outdoor event difficult. 天气的多变使得户外活动的计划变得困难。 | 近义词：changeability, variability, fluctuation | 反义词：constancy, immutability, permanence |  名词
