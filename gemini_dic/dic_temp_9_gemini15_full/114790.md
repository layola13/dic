maltreating: /ˌmælˈtriːtɪŋ/ | v. |虐待；粗暴对待 |  The act of treating someone or something badly or cruelly.  It involves causing physical or emotional harm, neglect, or exploitation. |

例句：

The children were maltreating the puppy by pulling its tail and throwing it around. = 孩子们虐待小狗，扯着它的尾巴把它扔来扔去。

She was accused of maltreating her elderly mother by neglecting her basic needs. = 她被指控虐待她年迈的母亲，忽视了她的基本需求。


近义词：abuse, ill-treat, mistreat, brutalize, torment

反义词：cherish, care for, nurture, protect
