egocentrics:/ˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪks/| n. |以自我为中心的人，利己主义者 |A person who is excessively self-centered and self-absorbed; someone who considers only their own needs and desires.  Egocentrics often lack empathy and struggle to understand the perspectives of others.  |例句：He's such an egocentric; he never thinks about anyone but himself. 他是个十足的利己主义者；他从不考虑别人，只考虑自己。

例句：Her egocentric behavior alienated many of her friends. 她的以自我为中心的行为疏远了她许多朋友。


近义词：narcissist, self-centered person, egotist, solipsist |反义词：altruist, selfless person, empathetic person
