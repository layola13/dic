servery:/ˈsɜːrvəri/| n. |自助餐台；供应处；服务台 |A place where food or drinks are served, often in a self-service or buffet style.  It can also refer to a place where other services are provided.|例句：The servery was crowded during lunchtime.  午餐时间自助餐台挤满了人。|例句：The hospital's servery provides meals for patients and staff. 医院的供应处为病人和员工提供膳食。|近义词：buffet, counter, cafeteria, dispensing area |反义词：None specifically, but opposites could include places where service is not self-serve, like a restaurant with table service.|名词
