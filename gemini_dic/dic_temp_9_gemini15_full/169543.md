proper:/ˈprɒpər/ /ˈprɑːpər/| adj.|合适的，正确的，得体的，规矩的，正派的|Proper means conforming to standards of correctness, appropriateness, or good taste. It can refer to behavior, language, form, or other things. |例句：It's proper etiquette to say "please" and "thank you."  使用“请”和“谢谢”是得体的礼仪。

例句：He wore a proper suit to the interview. 他穿了一套合适的西装去面试。


近义词：appropriate, suitable, correct, fitting, decent

反义词：improper, unsuitable, incorrect, wrong, indecent

词性：形容词
