outdancing:/ˌaʊtˈdænsɪŋ/| v.|胜过…的舞技，跳得比…好|To surpass in dancing skill; to dance better than.|例句：She outdanced all her competitors in the competition. 她在那次比赛中胜过了所有竞争对手的舞技。

例句：The young girl outdanced the professional dancer, surprising everyone in the audience.  年轻女孩的舞技胜过专业舞者，让台下观众大吃一惊。


近义词：outshine, outdo, excel


反义词：be outdanced by


词性：动词
