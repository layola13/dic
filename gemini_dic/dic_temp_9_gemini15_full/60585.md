elaterium:/ˌɛləˈtɪəriəm/| n. |苦瓜子，泻药|A purgative substance obtained from the fruit of the squirting cucumber (Ecballium elaterium). It's a powerful cathartic known for its drastic effect on the bowels.  The active principle is elaterin.|The doctor prescribed elaterium to relieve the patient's severe constipation.|近义词：cathartic, purgative|反义词：constipant
