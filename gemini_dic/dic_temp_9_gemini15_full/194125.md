self-inconsistent:/ˌsɛlfɪnˈkənˈsɪstənt/| adj. |自相矛盾的，前后不一的|  Describes a system, theory, or statement that contradicts itself; containing internal inconsistencies.|The theory is self-inconsistent, as its assumptions lead to contradictory conclusions.  这个理论自相矛盾，因为它的假设会导致相互矛盾的结论。|近义词：contradictory, paradoxical, illogical|反义词：consistent, coherent, logical|
