reascension:/ˌriːəˈsɛnʃən/| n. |复位，重新登基，再次升天 |The act of ascending again; a return to a former position or state of power or importance.  This word is relatively rare and often found in specific contexts like religious or historical narratives.  |Example: The king's reascension to the throne was met with jubilation. 国王再次登基受到了热烈的庆祝。The reascension of the rocket after a brief pause was a testament to its advanced engineering. 火箭在短暂暂停后再次升空，证明了其先进的工程技术。|近义词：resurrection, reinstatement, restoration |反义词：deposition, dethronement, descent
