harbored:/ˈhɑːrbərd/| v.| 窝藏；怀有（感情、想法等）；庇护 | to give shelter or refuge to (someone or something) | The old house harbored a family of raccoons. 这栋旧房子里住着一家人浣熊。 |近义词：sheltered, concealed, contained | 反义词：revealed, exposed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harbors resentment towards his former boss. 他对他的前任老板怀恨在心。
The port harbors many fishing boats.  港口停泊着许多渔船。


一般过去时:
She harbored a secret for years. 她多年来保守着一个秘密。
The fugitive was harbored by a sympathetic local. 逃犯得到一位热心当地人的庇护。


现在完成时:
They have harbored doubts about the project's success. 他们一直对项目的成功心存疑虑。
The country has harbored refugees for decades. 这个国家几十年来一直在收容难民。


过去完成时:
He had harbored hopes of winning the lottery, but he didn't. 他曾抱有赢得彩票的希望，但没有实现。
The city had harbored many secrets throughout its history. 这个城市在其历史上隐藏着许多秘密。


将来时:
They will harbor no ill will towards their neighbors. 他们不会对邻居怀有恶意。
The island will harbor many species of birds. 这个岛屿将栖息着许多种鸟类。


虚拟语气:
If they had harbored any doubts, they would have spoken up. 如果他们有任何疑问，他们就会说出来。
I wish I hadn't harbored such negative thoughts. 我真希望我没有怀有如此负面的想法。


被动语态:
The criminals were harbored by a corrupt official. 罪犯受到腐败官员的庇护。
The refugees were harbored in a nearby village. 难民被安置在附近的村庄里。


疑问句:
Did they harbor any suspicions about the crime? 他们对这起犯罪有任何怀疑吗？
Does the building harbor any dangerous materials? 这座建筑物是否藏有危险材料？


否定句:
He did not harbor any ill feelings towards her. 他对她没有任何恶意。
The organization does not harbor terrorists. 该组织不窝藏恐怖分子。
