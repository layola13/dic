photo:/ˈfoʊtoʊ/| n.|照片，相片 |A picture produced by photography.|例句：I took a photo of the sunset. 我拍了一张日落的照片。|近义词：picture, image, snapshot|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I take photos every day. = 我每天都拍照。
She takes photos of her cat. = 她给她猫拍照。

一般过去时:
I took a photo of the Eiffel Tower. = 我拍了一张埃菲尔铁塔的照片。
They took many photos during their trip. = 他们在旅行期间拍了很多照片。

现在完成时:
I have taken many photos this year. = 今年我已经拍了很多照片了。
She has taken a lot of photos of her family. = 她已经拍了很多她家人的照片。

过去完成时:
I had taken several photos before the battery died. = 电池没电之前我已经拍了几张照片了。
They had taken hundreds of photos by the end of the trip. = 到旅行结束时，他们已经拍了数百张照片。

将来时:
I will take a photo of you tomorrow. = 我明天会给你拍照。
We will take photos at the wedding. = 我们将在婚礼上拍照。

虚拟语气:
If I had a camera, I would take a photo. = 如果我有相机，我会拍张照片。
I wish I had taken more photos. = 我真希望我拍更多照片。

被动语态:
The photo was taken by my friend. = 这张照片是我朋友拍的。
Many photos are taken with smartphones nowadays. = 现在很多照片都是用智能手机拍摄的。

疑问句:
Did you take a photo? = 你拍照了吗？
Have you ever taken a photo with a professional camera? = 你曾经用专业相机拍照吗？

否定句:
I didn't take any photos. = 我没有拍任何照片。
She doesn't like having her photo taken. = 她不喜欢别人给她拍照。
