grubble:/ˈɡrʌbəl/| v.| 搜寻，摸索；寻找食物 | to search blindly or eagerly, especially for food; to search in a clumsy or unsystematic way |例句：The dog was grubbling around in the dirt for a lost bone.  这只狗在地里四处扒拉寻找丢失的骨头。

The children were grubbling in the garden for worms. 孩子们在花园里翻土找蚯蚓。

近义词：rummage, hunt, forage, search |反义词：organize, arrange, tidy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grumbles around in his pockets. = 他在他口袋里摸索。
She grumbles for food in the cupboard. = 她在橱柜里翻找食物。

一般过去时:
The dog grubbled in the trash can. = 那条狗在垃圾桶里翻找。
They grubbled through the old boxes. = 他们翻找着那些旧箱子。

现在完成时:
He has grubbled for hours but found nothing. = 他已经找了好几个小时，但什么也没找到。
She has grubbled through all her drawers. = 她已经翻遍了她所有的抽屉。

过去完成时:
The cat had grubbled under the sofa before I arrived. = 我到之前，猫已经在沙发底下翻找过了。
They had grubbled in the attic for hours. = 他们在阁楼里翻找了好几个小时。

将来时:
I will grubble around for my keys. = 我会四处寻找我的钥匙。
He will grubble through the files tomorrow. = 他明天会翻阅文件。

虚拟语气:
If I were a dog, I would grubble for bones. = 如果我是一条狗，我会寻找骨头。
I wish I hadn't grubbled through that old box. = 我真希望我没有翻找那个旧箱子。

被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)
The ground had been grubbled over by the pigs. = 地面已经被猪翻找过了。
The drawer was grubbled through by the detective. = 抽屉被侦探翻找过了。

疑问句:
Is she grubbling for something? = 她是不是在找什么东西？
Was he grubbling in the garden? = 他是在花园里翻找吗？

否定句:
He didn't grubble for his wallet. = 他没有翻找他的钱包。
She doesn't grubble in the dirt. = 她不在地里翻找。


PHRASE->
grubble around: 瞎找
grubble for: 寻找
grubble in: 在…里翻找
grubble through: 翻找
grubble up: 挖出
