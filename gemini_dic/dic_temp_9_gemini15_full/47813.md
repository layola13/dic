decarbonizes:/ˌdiːˈkɑːrbənʌɪz/| v.|脱碳；减少碳排放|To reduce or remove carbon dioxide from (something), especially the atmosphere.  The process often involves transitioning to cleaner energy sources and improving energy efficiency.|例句：The company is decarbonizing its operations by investing in renewable energy. 这家公司正在通过投资可再生能源来减少碳排放。

例句：Many countries are committed to decarbonizing their economies by mid-century. 许多国家致力于在本世纪中叶之前实现经济脱碳。


近义词：decarbonise, decarbonate


反义词：carbonize, increase carbon emissions


词性:动词
