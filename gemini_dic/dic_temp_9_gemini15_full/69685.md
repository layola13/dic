fenes:/ˈfiːniːz/| n.|窗子，窗户|The word "fenes" is a rarely used archaic term for windows. It is derived from Latin "fenestra" meaning "window".  The word is primarily found in literary contexts and older texts.|例句：The fenes of the ancient cathedral were stained glass.  古代大教堂的窗子是彩色玻璃的。|近义词：window, casement|反义词：wall
