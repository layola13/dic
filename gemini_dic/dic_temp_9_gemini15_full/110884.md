lionlike: /ˈlaɪənlaɪk/ | adj. | 像狮子的；勇猛的；威严的 | Resembling a lion in appearance, strength, or courage.  The word combines "lion" with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance. | 例句：He had a lionlike roar that filled the room. 他发出像狮子一样的吼声，充满了整个房间。

例句：Her lionlike courage impressed everyone. 她像狮子一样的勇气给每个人留下了深刻的印象。


近义词：leonine, majestic, fierce, powerful, courageous

反义词：timid, cowardly, meek, weak, gentle


词性:形容词
