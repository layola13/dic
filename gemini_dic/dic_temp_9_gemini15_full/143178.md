outcoach:/ˌaʊtˈkoʊtʃ/| vt.|技战术上胜过，在教练方面胜过 |To surpass or defeat (another coach or team) through superior coaching strategies and tactics.  |例句：The experienced coach outcoached his younger opponent, leading his team to victory.  经验丰富的教练以其高超的战术策略击败了年轻的对手，带领他的球队取得了胜利。

例句：The opposing team's superior strategy meant they completely outcoached us. 对手的策略更胜一筹，这意味着他们在教练方面完全胜过我们。


近义词：outsmart, outmaneuver, outwit | 反义词：be outcoached by
