eyalet:/ˈaɪəlet/| n.|省份，行省；（奥斯曼帝国的）省|A large administrative division of a country, especially one that is geographically distinct or has a degree of autonomy.  Historically, it often referred to a province in the Ottoman Empire.|The Ottoman Empire was divided into numerous eyalets, each governed by a beylerbey.|近义词：province, region, territory|反义词：country, nation
