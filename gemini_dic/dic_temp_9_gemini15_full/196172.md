septums: /ˈsɛptəmz/ | n. |隔膜；隔板| A septum is a wall or partition that divides a space into smaller compartments.  It is often found in anatomical structures, such as the nose or heart. |例句：The nasal septum divides the nostrils. 鼻中隔分隔鼻孔。

The heart has septums between its chambers. 心脏的各个腔室之间有隔膜。|近义词：partition, division, wall |反义词：opening, aperture
