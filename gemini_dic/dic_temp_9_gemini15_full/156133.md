photosynthesising:/ˌfoʊtoʊˈsɪnθəsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 光合作用 | The process by which green plants and some other organisms use sunlight to synthesize foods with the help of chlorophyll.  | Example: Plants are photosynthesising in the sunlight. 植物在阳光下进行光合作用。 The algae are photosynthesising at an incredible rate. 藻类以惊人的速度进行光合作用。|近义词:photosynthesizing (US spelling)|反义词: N/A
