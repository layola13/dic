fraken:/ˈfræŋkən/| adj.|弗兰肯斯坦式的；拼凑的，粗制滥造的|  Describes something that is created by combining disparate parts, often in a haphazard or unnatural way, evoking the imagery of Frankenstein's monster.  It implies a lack of elegance, coherence, or naturalness.|例句：The movie's plot felt fraken, with scenes stitched together without a clear narrative thread. 这部电影的情节感觉很粗制滥造，场景拼凑在一起，没有清晰的叙事线索。|近义词：patchwork, cobbled-together, hybrid, Frankensteinian|反义词：seamless, coherent, natural, elegant|形容词
