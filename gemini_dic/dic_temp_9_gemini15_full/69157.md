fawn:/fɔːn/| n. v.|小鹿；奉承；浅黄褐色；|A young deer, especially one in its first year.  To try to gain favor from someone by flattering them.|例句：The fawn playfully chased its mother.  这只小鹿顽皮地追逐着它的母亲。

The politician fawned over the wealthy donor.  这位政客奉承这位富有的捐赠者。|近义词：deer, kid, flatter,  coax |反义词：ignore, snub,  disdain|名词，动词
