hardiness:/ˈhɑːrdinɪs/| n. |坚韧；耐寒性；抗病性 |The ability to endure difficult conditions;  strength and resilience.  It can refer to the ability of a plant to withstand harsh weather, a person's ability to overcome adversity, or the resistance of an organism to disease. |例句：The hardiness of the wheat crop ensured a good harvest despite the drought.  小麦作物的耐旱性确保了即使在干旱的情况下也能获得好收成。

The remarkable hardiness of the survivors impressed everyone.  幸存者令人惊叹的坚韧精神给每个人留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：toughness, resilience, endurance, fortitude, resistance |反义词：fragility, weakness, vulnerability, susceptibility
