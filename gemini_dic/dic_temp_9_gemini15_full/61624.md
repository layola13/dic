emigrates:/ˈɛməˌɡreɪts/| v.|移居国外，移民|To leave one's own country to settle permanently in another.|例句：Many people emigrate from developing countries to seek better opportunities. 许多人从发展中国家移居国外以寻求更好的机会。

例句：My grandparents emigrated to the United States in the 1950s. 我的祖父母在20世纪50年代移居美国。


近义词：migrate, relocate, expatriate|反义词：immigrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He emigrates to Canada every year. 他每年都移民加拿大。(This example is slightly unnatural, as emigration is usually a one-time event.  A better example would be about habitual actions related to emigration preparation.)
She emigrates to Australia next month. 她下个月要移民澳大利亚。


一般过去时:
They emigrated to Brazil last year. 他们去年移民到巴西。
My family emigrated from Ireland in the 19th century. 我的家族在19世纪从爱尔兰移民出来。


现在完成时:
He has emigrated to New Zealand. 他已经移民新西兰了。
She has emigrated from her homeland and started a new life. 她已经从她的祖国移民出来，开始了新的生活。


过去完成时:
They had emigrated to France before the war broke out. 战争爆发前他们已经移民法国了。
She had emigrated to America before he even met her.  在他遇到她之前，她就已经移民美国了。


将来时:
We will emigrate to Spain next spring. 我们明年春天将移民西班牙。
They will emigrate as soon as they sell their house. 他们一卖掉房子就会移民。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would emigrate to Italy. 如果我有钱，我会移民意大利。
I wish I could emigrate to a warmer climate. 我希望我能移民到气候更温暖的地方。


被动语态:
Few people are emigrated forcefully in this era. 在这个时代很少有人被迫移民。(This sentence is grammatically correct but unnatural. The passive voice is rarely used with "emigrate".)
(A better, more natural passive example is difficult to construct with "emigrate" because it typically implies a voluntary action.)



疑问句:
Will you emigrate next year? 你明年会移民吗？
When did your parents emigrate? 你父母是什么时候移民的？


否定句:
He did not emigrate to the US. 他没有移民美国。
They haven't emigrated yet. 他们还没有移民。


PHRASE->
emigrate from... 从...移民
emigrate to... 移民到...
emigration plans 移民计划
emigration process 移民流程
forced emigration 被迫移民
