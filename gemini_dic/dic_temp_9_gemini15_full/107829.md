larcenies:/ˈlɑːrsəniz/| n. |盗窃罪(复数)|The plural form of larceny, meaning the unlawful taking and carrying away of someone else's personal property with the intent to permanently deprive the owner of it.  It refers to multiple instances of theft.|例句：The police investigated a series of larcenies in the neighborhood.  警方调查了该街区一系列的盗窃案。|例句：Multiple larcenies were reported, leading to increased police patrols. 多起盗窃案被举报，导致警方巡逻加强。|近义词：thefts, robberies (though robbery often involves violence or threat)|反义词：legitimate acquisition, rightful ownership|名词
