hoistaway:/hɔɪstəˈweɪ/ | vt. | 吊起并运走；吊运；移除 | To lift and carry away something using a hoist or crane.  |例句：They hoisted away the damaged car. 他们用吊车吊走了损坏的汽车。
例句：The crane hoisted away the heavy equipment. 起重机吊走了重型设备。|近义词：lift, remove, transport | 反义词：lower, place, install
