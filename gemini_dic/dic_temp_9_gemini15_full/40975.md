coopted:/ˈkoʊ.ɒptɪd/|/ˈkəʊ.ɒptɪd/| vt.| 吸收；利用；劫持；篡夺；|To coopt means to take something for your own use, especially without permission. It can also refer to the act of adopting or incorporating something into a larger structure or system.  The word often carries a negative connotation, implying a sense of unfairness or manipulation. |例句：The larger company cooped the smaller firm's technology.  更大的公司窃取了这家小公司技术。

The opposition party accused the government of coopting their policies. 反对党指责政府窃取了他们的政策。|近义词：appropriate, adopt, usurp, commandeer |反义词：reject, relinquish, abandon|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They coopt ideas from various sources. = 他们从各种来源吸收想法。
The committee coopts new members annually. = 委员会每年吸收新成员。


一般过去时:
The government coopted the opposition leader's ideas. = 政府窃取了反对党领导人的想法。
The company coopted the smaller firm's technology. = 这家公司窃取了这家小公司技术。


现在完成时:
They have coopted many of the protestors' demands. = 他们已经采纳了许多抗议者的要求。
The movement has coopted elements from other ideologies. = 这个运动已经吸收了其他意识形态的元素。


过去完成时:
The company had coopted several smaller businesses before it went public. = 在上市之前，该公司已经收购了几家小型企业。
They had coopted the best aspects of their competitors' designs. = 他们已经吸收了竞争对手设计中的最佳方面。


将来时:
They will coopt the latest technology for their new product. = 他们将为他们的新产品采用最新的技术。
The organization will likely coopt more volunteers next year. = 该组织明年可能会吸收更多志愿者。


虚拟语气:
If they coopted our ideas, we would be credited. = 如果他们采纳了我们的想法，我们会得到认可。
I wish they hadn't coopted our research without permission. = 我真希望他们没有未经许可就窃取我们的研究成果。


被动语态:
His ideas were coopted by the mainstream media. = 他的想法被主流媒体所采用。
The smaller company's technology was coopted by the larger corporation. = 小公司的技术被大公司窃取了。


疑问句:
Were their ideas coopted by the government? = 他们的想法是被政府窃取了吗？
Has the company coopted any innovative technologies recently? = 公司最近吸收了哪些创新技术？


否定句:
They did not coopt any outside funding. = 他们没有吸收任何外部资金。
The company refused to coopt the competitor's strategies. = 公司拒绝采用竞争对手的策略。
