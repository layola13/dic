populin:/ˈpɒpjʊlɪn/| n. |白杨素；杨树素|A phenolic glycoside found in the leaves and bark of poplar trees (genus Populus).  It is known for its potential medicinal properties and is being studied for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.| Example:  Researchers are investigating the potential therapeutic applications of populin. 研究人员正在研究白杨素的潜在治疗应用。|近义词: salicin (related compound)|反义词:  (no direct antonym)|名词
