matured:/məˈtʃʊrd/ | adj. v. | 成熟的；使成熟 |  Developed to a point of full function or development; having attained full development or growth. |


英文解析:  The adjective "matured" describes something or someone that has reached a state of full development or completeness. This can refer to physical growth, emotional development, or the aging process of things like wine or cheese.  The verb form indicates the process of reaching this state.

例句:

He is a matured young man who handles responsibilities well. = 他是一个成熟的年轻人，能够很好地处理责任。

The wine has matured beautifully over the years. = 这葡萄酒多年来酿造得非常出色。


近义词: ripe, developed, adult, seasoned, full-grown

反义词: immature, undeveloped, young, unripe, green


词性: Adjective (adj.), Verb (v.)


词源:  From Middle English maturen, from Old French maturer, from Latin maturus "ripe, mature."
