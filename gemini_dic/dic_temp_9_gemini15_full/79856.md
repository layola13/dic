gmat:/dʒiːˈmæt/ | n. |管理专业研究生入学考试 | Graduate Management Admission Test, a standardized test for admission to graduate management programs, such as MBA programs.  The GMAT assesses analytical, writing, quantitative, and verbal reasoning skills. | Example:  He is studying hard for the GMAT. 他正在努力准备GMAT考试。 | 近义词:GRE (Graduate Record Examinations), MBA entrance exam | 反义词: None
