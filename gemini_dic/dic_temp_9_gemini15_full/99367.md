interdepends:/ˌɪntər.dɪˈpɛndz/ | v. |相互依赖，互相依存 | To be mutually dependent; to rely on each other.  | The success of the project interdepends on the cooperation of all team members. 这个项目的成功取决于所有团队成员的合作。|近义词：rely on each other, depend on one another | 反义词：independent, autonomous | 动词
