premaxillary:/ˌpriːˈmæksɪləri/| adj.|前上颌的|Relating to the premaxilla, a bone in the upper jaw of vertebrates.  The premaxillary bone forms the anterior part of the upper jaw and usually bears the incisor teeth.|例句：The premaxillary bones are fused in most mammals.  大多数哺乳动物的前上颌骨是融合的。|The study focused on the development of the premaxillary suture.  这项研究的重点是前上颌缝的发育。|近义词：anterior maxillary|反义词：posterior maxillary|
