The name likely comes from its association with bakeries where flour and other ingredients attract the flies.  There's also a possible link to the idea that these flies are small enough to fit into a shoe.|例句：A shoefly buzzed around the picnic basket. 一只鞋蝇在野餐篮周围嗡嗡作响。
