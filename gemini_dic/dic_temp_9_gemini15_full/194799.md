semantic:/sɪˈmæn.tɪk/ | adj. n. |语义的；语义学 | Relating to meaning in language or logic.  The study of meaning in language. |例句：The semantic ambiguity of the sentence made it difficult to understand. 这句话的语义模糊使它难以理解。

例句：The semantic field of "love" includes words like affection, passion, and devotion. “爱”的语义场包括诸如喜爱、激情和奉献之类的词语。


近义词：meaningful, significant | 反义词：nonsensical, meaningless | 词性: adjective, noun
