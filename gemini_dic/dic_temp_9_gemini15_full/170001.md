protectable: /prəˈtɛktəbl/ | adj. | 可保护的，受保护的 |  capable of being protected; able to be defended or shielded from harm or damage. |


例句：

This software is protectable under copyright law.  = 这款软件受版权法保护。

The government has implemented measures to ensure that the endangered species are protectable. = 政府已实施措施以确保濒危物种得到保护。


近义词：defensible, safeguardable, secure, shielded


反义词：undefendable, vulnerable, exposed, unprotected


词性：形容词
