over-hurriedly: /ˌoʊvərˈhʌridli/ | adv. | 过于匆忙地，仓促地 |  The word is formed by adding the prefix "over-" to the adverb "hurriedly," intensifying the meaning. It describes an action performed with excessive haste and lack of care. | 例句：He over-hurriedly packed his bag and missed his flight. 他过于匆忙地收拾行李，错过了航班。  She over-hurriedly wrote the essay and it was full of mistakes. 她仓促地写完了论文，里面充满了错误。 | 近义词：rashly, hastily, precipitately | 反义词：leisurely, calmly, deliberately
