remoulded:/ˌriːˈmɔːldɪd/ | v. |重塑；改造；重新塑造 |To shape or form (something) again; to reshape.  The process involves changing the existing form or structure of something to create a new or improved version. |例句：The clay was remoulded into a new shape.  黏土被重新塑造成一个新的形状。

The company remoulded its image after the scandal. 这家公司在丑闻之后重塑了形象。|近义词：reshape, reform, reconstruct, remodel |反义词：destroy, demolish
