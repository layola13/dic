nonmuscularly:/ˌnɒnˈmʌskjʊləli/ | adv. |非肌肉地，非肌性地 |Without the use of muscles.  This adverb describes actions or processes that occur without muscular involvement.  |例句：The puppet moved nonmuscularly, controlled by strings.  木偶非肌肉地移动，由绳子控制。

The heart beats nonmuscularly through electrical impulses. 心脏通过电脉冲而非肌肉收缩跳动。


近义词：mechanically, automatically, passively


反义词：muscularly
