lagged:/læɡd/| adj. v. |滞后的；落后的；延迟的；|  Meaning: Past participle of "lag," indicating a delay or falling behind. It can describe something that is behind schedule, slower than expected, or not up to date. |例句：The project lagged behind schedule.  项目进度滞后。

The economy lagged for several months after the crisis. 危机过后，经济滞后了几个月。


近义词：delayed, retarded, behind, overdue |反义词：ahead, advanced, leading


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system lags frequently. = 系统经常滞后。
The economy lags behind other countries. = 经济落后于其他国家。


一般过去时:
The train lagged behind schedule. = 火车延误了。
The runners lagged in the race. = 赛跑者在比赛中落后了。


现在完成时:
The project has lagged considerably. = 项目已经大大滞后了。
The region has lagged in economic development. = 该地区在经济发展方面落后了。


过去完成时:
The team had lagged behind all season. = 这个队整个赛季都落后了。
The negotiations had lagged for weeks. = 谈判已经拖延了数周。


将来时:
The construction is expected to lag. = 预计建设将会滞后。
The country's growth rate will lag behind expectations. = 该国的增长率将低于预期。


虚拟语气:
If the project had not lagged, we would have finished on time. = 如果项目没有滞后，我们就能按时完成了。
If the economy lagged further, we might face a recession. = 如果经济进一步滞后，我们可能会面临衰退。


被动语态:
The report was lagged in its submission. = 报告提交延迟了。
The development has been lagged by funding shortages. = 发展由于资金短缺而滞后了。


疑问句:
Is the software lagging? = 软件卡了吗？
Is the performance lagging behind? = 表现落后了吗？


否定句:
The growth did not lag. = 增长没有滞后。
The team did not lag behind the others. = 该队没有落后于其他队伍。


PHRASE->
time lag = 时间延迟
lag behind = 落后于
lag indicator =滞后指标
cognitive lag = 认知迟滞
lag effect =滞后效应

词源:
From Old Norse *lag, meaning "slow, sluggish."  The word's meaning evolved over time to encompass the sense of falling behind or delaying.
