harmonization:/ˌhɑːrmənaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |协调；和谐；统一|The process of bringing different elements into agreement or conformity; the state of being in agreement or conformity.  It often refers to the process of making things consistent or compatible, especially in areas like regulations, standards, or policies.  |例句：The harmonization of accounting standards across different countries is a complex process. 各国会计准则的协调是一个复杂的过程。

例句：The harmonization of different musical instruments creates a beautiful melody.  不同乐器的协调创造出优美的旋律。


近义词：coordination, unification, integration, alignment, standardization|反义词：discord, conflict, disharmony, inconsistency
