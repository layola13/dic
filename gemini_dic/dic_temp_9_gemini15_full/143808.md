outpaint:/ˌaʊtˈpeɪnt/| vt.|外延绘制，扩展绘画|To extend or expand an existing image beyond its original boundaries by generating new content that seamlessly blends with the original.  This is often done using AI image generation techniques.|例句：I used an AI tool to outpaint the landscape, adding more mountains in the distance. 我用AI工具扩展了风景画，在远处添加了更多山脉。

例句：The artist outpainted the edges of the canvas, creating a wider scene.  艺术家扩展了画布的边缘，创造了一个更广阔的场景。


近义词：extend, expand, enlarge, augment|反义词：crop, reduce, shrink|动词
