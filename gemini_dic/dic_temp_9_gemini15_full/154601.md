persevering: /ˌpɜːrsɪˈvɪərɪŋ/ | adj. | 坚持不懈的，锲而不舍的 |  Characterized by persistent determination; steadfast.  It implies a continued effort despite difficulties or discouragement. |

例句：

Her persevering spirit enabled her to overcome many obstacles.  她的坚持不懈的精神使她克服了许多障碍。

He was persevering in his studies, even when faced with setbacks. 他学习非常刻苦，即使面对挫折也毫不气馁。


近义词：persistent, tenacious, steadfast, resolute, determined


反义词：yielding, giving up, quitting, irresolute, wavering


词源:  From the verb "persevere," which comes from the Old French word "perseverer," ultimately derived from the Latin "perseverare," meaning "to persist," "to continue steadfastly."  The prefix "per-" means "through," and "severare" relates to "severe" in the sense of strict or firm.
