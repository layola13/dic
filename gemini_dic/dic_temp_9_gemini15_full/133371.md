nonextractive:/nɒnɪkˈstræktɪv/ | adj. |非抽取式；非提取的 | Not involving the extraction or selection of specific information from a source; referring to methods or approaches that operate on the entire input without selectively choosing parts.  This term is often used in the context of text summarization and question answering, where a nonextractive method generates a summary or answer by paraphrasing or generating new text instead of directly copying phrases from the source material. | Example: The system used a nonextractive summarization method, generating a concise overview that did not directly quote the source text.  | 近义词: abstractive, generative | 反义词: extractive
