documentaries:/ˌdɒkjuˈmentəriːz/| n. |纪录片|Documentary films are non-fiction films that present factual information. They often explore a specific topic in depth, using interviews, archival footage, and other techniques to tell a story.  |Example:  Many documentaries explore environmental issues. 许多纪录片探讨环境问题。


Example:  The documentary won an award at the film festival. 这部纪录片在电影节上获奖了。|近义词：documentary films, factual films |反义词：fiction films, feature films|名词
