deckings:/ˈdɛkɪŋz/ | n. |  地板; 甲板;  铺板 |  Deckings refers to a surface made of planks or boards, often laid horizontally, used for flooring, especially outdoors, or as a covering for a structure's upper surface, like a deck.  It can also refer to the material used for such a surface. | Example: The deckings on the patio were made of treated redwood.  露台上的铺板是用防腐红木做的。The ship's deckings were slippery with rain. 船的甲板被雨淋湿了，很滑。 | 近义词：flooring, platform, planking | 反义词：foundation, substructure
