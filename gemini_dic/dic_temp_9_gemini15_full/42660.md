/ˈkaʊntərˌstɛpɪŋ/ | n. | 反步, 对抗性策略 | The act of responding to an opponent's move with a countermove, often unexpected or disruptive. It implies a strategic shift or reversal to gain advantage.  | Example:  The company's counterstepping strategy involved launching a new product to undercut its competitor.  公司通过推出新产品来削弱竞争对手，采取了反制策略。 |近义词: countermeasure, riposte, counterattack | 反义词: cooperation, compliance |  无
