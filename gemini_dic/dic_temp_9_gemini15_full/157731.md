/plæs/ | n. | 地方，场所；职位；位置；空间 | A place is a particular position or area. It can refer to a location, a position in society or a job, or a point in time or space. |例句：This is a nice place to live.  这是一个宜居的好地方。|例句：He has a good place in the company. 他在公司担任要职。|近义词：spot, location, position, site |反义词：nowhere


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This is a good place to study. = 这是一个适合学习的好地方。
Where is the best place to eat? = 最好的就餐地点在哪里？

一般过去时:
We found a nice place to have a picnic. = 我们找到一个野餐的好地方。
Where did you live before? = 你以前住在哪里？

现在完成时:
I have finally found a place to live. = 我终于找到了住处。
Have you ever been to that place? = 你去过那个地方吗？

过去完成时:
We had already left the place before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，我们已经离开了那个地方。
Had they found a suitable place to camp before nightfall? = 天黑之前他们找到合适的露营地了吗？


将来时:
We will find a better place to live next year. = 我们明年会找到一个更好的居住地。
Where will we meet? = 我们将在哪里见面？

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would buy a place by the sea. = 如果我有更多钱，我会在海边买个房子。
I wish I had a place of my own. = 我希望我有自己的住所。

被动语态:
The place was cleaned thoroughly. = 这个地方被彻底打扫干净了。
The meeting place has been changed. = 会议地点已经改变了。

疑问句:
Where is this place? = 这是什么地方？
What kind of place is this? = 这是什么样的场所？

否定句:
There is no place like home. = 没有什么地方比家更好了。
I couldn't find a parking place. = 我找不到停车位。


PHRASE->
in place = 在适当的位置
out of place = 不在适当的位置;格格不入
take place = 发生
in place of = 代替
place an order = 下订单
