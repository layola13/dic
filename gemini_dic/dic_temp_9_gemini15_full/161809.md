practi: /ˈpræktɪs/ | v. |练习，实践| to perform or carry out an action repeatedly in order to improve one's skill or ability; to engage in a particular activity regularly.|例句：I practice the piano every day. = 我每天练习钢琴。|例句：She practices yoga to stay healthy. = 她练习瑜伽以保持健康。|近义词：exercise, rehearse, drill|反义词：neglect, ignore

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I practice the guitar every day. = 我每天练习吉他。
They practice speaking English in class. = 他们在课堂上练习说英语。

一般过去时:
I practiced the piano for two hours yesterday. = 昨天我练习钢琴两个小时。
She practiced her presentation before the meeting. = 会议前她练习了自己的演讲。

现在完成时:
I have practiced yoga for five years. = 我练习瑜伽五年了。
He has practiced medicine for over twenty years. = 他行医二十多年了。

过去完成时:
I had practiced my speech many times before I gave it. = 我在发表演讲之前已经练习了很多次了。
They had practiced the dance routine for weeks before the performance. = 他们在演出前已经排练了舞蹈几周了。

将来时:
I will practice my driving skills tomorrow. = 我明天将练习我的驾驶技巧。
She will practice her French before her trip to Paris. = 她将在去巴黎旅行之前练习她的法语。

虚拟语气:
If I practiced more, I would be a better musician. = 如果我多练习，我会成为一个更好的音乐家。
I wish I had practiced harder for the exam. = 我希望我为考试更努力地练习了。

被动语态:
The piano piece was practiced diligently by the student. = 这首钢琴曲被学生勤奋地练习了。
The new dance routine is being practiced by the team. = 新的舞蹈套路正在由团队练习。

疑问句:
Do you practice any sports? = 你练习任何运动吗？
Does he practice the violin regularly? = 他经常练习小提琴吗？

否定句:
I don't practice enough. = 我练习得不够。
She didn't practice for the test, so she failed. = 她没有为考试练习，所以她不及格了。
