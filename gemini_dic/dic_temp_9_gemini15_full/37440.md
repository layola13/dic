commiserated:/kəˈmɪzəreɪtɪd/| v.| 同情，怜悯 | To express sympathy or feel pity for someone.  |例句：I commiserated with her on the loss of her job. 我对她失去工作表示同情。

例句：We commiserated with the family after their tragic loss.  我们对这个家庭在悲剧性损失后表示慰问。


近义词：sympathize, empathize, console, pity

反义词：rejoice, celebrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I commiserate with those who are struggling. = 我同情那些正在挣扎的人。
She commiserates with her friends when they have problems. = 当她的朋友们遇到问题时，她会同情他们。

一般过去时:
He commiserated with the losing team. = 他同情输掉的队伍。
They commiserated with the victims of the flood. = 他们同情洪水中的受害者。

现在完成时:
I have commiserated with her many times about her illness. = 我多次对她生病表示同情。
We have commiserated with him since his accident. = 自从他出事以来，我们一直在同情他。

过去完成时:
He had commiserated with her before he left. = 他离开前曾对她表示同情。
They had commiserated with each other before the announcement. = 他们在宣布之前互相表示了同情。


将来时:
I will commiserate with him when I see him. = 我见到他时会同情他。
We will commiserate with the family after the funeral. = 葬礼后我们会向家属表示慰问。

虚拟语气:
If I were in her shoes, I would commiserate with her. = 如果我处在她的位置，我会同情她。
I wish I could commiserate with her more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地同情她。

被动语态:
His suffering was commiserated by many. = 许多人同情他的痛苦。
Her loss was commiserated by all her colleagues. = 她所有的同事都同情她的损失。

疑问句:
Did you commiserate with him about his problems? = 你对他遇到的问题表示同情了吗？
Will you commiserate with her when she calls? = 她打电话来的时候你会同情她吗？

否定句:
I did not commiserate with him; I felt he deserved it. = 我没有同情他；我觉得他活该。
She refused to commiserate with her rival's failure. = 她拒绝同情她对手的失败。


PHRASE->
commiserate with someone = 同情某人
commiserate on/over something = 对某事表示同情
commiserate about something = 对某事表示同情
to commiserate deeply = 深切同情
commiserate with someone's plight = 同情某人的困境

词源:
Comes from the Late Latin word *commiserari*, meaning "to share pity,"  from *com-* (together) + *miserari* (to pity).
