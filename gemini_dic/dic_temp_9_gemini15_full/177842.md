receptacle:/rɪˈsɛptəkl/| n. |容器，插座，接受器 |A receptacle is a container, especially one that is designed to hold something.  It can also refer to a socket for an electrical plug. |例句：The flower has a receptacle at its base.  这朵花底部有一个花托。|例句：  He plugged the appliance into the wall receptacle. 他把电器插到墙上的插座里。|近义词：container, holder, socket, vessel |反义词：source, origin


USAGE->

The vase serves as a receptacle for flowers. = 花瓶是盛放鲜花的容器。
This box is a receptacle for old letters. = 这个盒子是放旧信件的容器。
