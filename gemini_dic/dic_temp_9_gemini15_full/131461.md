noncohabitation:/ˌnɒnkəʊhæbɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |非同居；不同居生活 |The state of not living together, especially in a romantic relationship.  This term often refers to couples who are in a committed relationship but choose not to live together.  It can also describe situations where family members or roommates do not share a residence. | Example:  Their noncohabitation was a conscious decision, allowing them to maintain individual independence.  他们的非同居是一种深思熟虑的决定，使他们能够保持个人的独立性。 |近义词：separate living, living apart |反义词：cohabitation, living together
