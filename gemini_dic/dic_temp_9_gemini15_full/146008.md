overdosing:/ˌoʊvərˈdoʊzɪŋ/ | v. |过量服用；药物过量 | The act of taking an excessive amount of a drug, typically leading to adverse health effects or death.  |例句：He was rushed to the hospital after overdosing on painkillers. 他因服用过量止痛药而被紧急送往医院。

例句：The singer was found dead from an apparent drug overdose.  这位歌手被发现死于明显的药物过量。


近义词：drug overdose, taking too much, excessive consumption


反义词：underdosing, taking too little


词性：动词
