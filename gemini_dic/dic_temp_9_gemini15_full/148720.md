overtrain:/ˌoʊvərˈtreɪn/| vt.|过度训练|To train (someone or something) excessively, often to the point of injury or exhaustion.  This can refer to physical training, mental training, or even the training of animals or machines.|例句：He overtrained for the marathon and ended up with a knee injury. 他马拉松训练过度，结果膝盖受伤了。|例句：The software engineers overtrained the AI model, leading to unexpected results. 软件工程师过度训练了AI模型，导致了意想不到的结果。|近义词：overwork, exhaust, overexert|反义词：undertrain, rest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overtrain their athletes every day. = 他们每天都过度训练他们的运动员。
She overtrains her dog with too many commands. = 她用过多的指令过度训练她的狗。
