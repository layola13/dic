imbitterer: /ɪmˈbɪtərər/ | n. | 使人痛苦的人或事 | Someone or something that causes bitterness or resentment.  | The constant criticism from her boss was a major imbitterer in her life.  她的老板不断批评，是她生活中主要的痛苦根源。 | 近义词：source of bitterness, affliction, tormentor | 反义词：source of joy, comfort, solace

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The constant criticism imbitters him. = 持续的批评使他痛苦。
That situation imbitters many people. = 那种情况让很多人痛苦。


一般过去时:
The loss of his job imbittered him for years. = 失去工作让他痛苦了好几年。
The unfair treatment imbittered her against the company. = 不公平的待遇让她对公司心生怨恨。


现在完成时:
Years of hardship have imbittered him. = 多年的苦难使他变得痛苦。
The betrayal has imbittered her relationship with her friend. = 背叛使她和朋友的关系变得痛苦。


过去完成时:
He had been imbittered by previous failures before he started the new project. = 在开始新项目之前，他已经被之前的失败弄得痛苦不堪。
She had been imbittered by the injustice she had suffered. = 她因遭受的不公正待遇而感到痛苦。


将来时:
This decision will imbitter many people. = 这个决定会让很多人痛苦。
Further setbacks will only imbitter him further. = 进一步的挫折只会让他更加痛苦。


虚拟语气:
If I had not experienced that, I would not have been so imbittered. = 如果我没有经历那件事，我就不会这么痛苦。
I wish I hadn't been so imbittered by his actions. = 我真希望我没有因为他行为而如此痛苦。


被动语态:
He was imbittered by the injustice. = 他因不公正而痛苦。
Many were imbittered by the outcome of the election. = 许多人对选举的结果感到痛苦。


疑问句:
What imbitters you so much? = 什么事让你这么痛苦？
What experiences imbitter people? = 哪些经历会使人痛苦？


否定句:
The experience did not imbitter him; instead, it made him stronger. = 那次经历并没有让他痛苦，反而使他更加坚强。
She refused to let the setback imbitter her spirit. = 她拒绝让挫折打击她的精神。
