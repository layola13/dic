homestall:/ˈhoʊmˌstɔːl/| n. |家园；宅基地；农场住宅|A homestall is a homestead, typically a small farm including the house and surrounding buildings.  It often evokes a sense of rural simplicity and self-sufficiency.|例句：They lived a quiet life on their homestall, raising chickens and growing vegetables.  他们在家园过着平静的生活，养鸡种菜。|例句：The old homestall was falling apart, a testament to years of neglect.  古老的农舍正在倒塌，证明了多年来的忽视。|近义词：homestead, farmstead, farm |反义词：city, metropolis
