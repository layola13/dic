preexclusions:/ˌpriːɪkˈskluʒənz/| n. |预先排除；预先排除的事项|Things excluded beforehand;  items or conditions that are ruled out in advance.  Preexclusions are often used in contracts or agreements to define what is not included or covered.|Example: The contract contained several preexclusions regarding liability for natural disasters.  这份合同包含几项关于自然灾害责任的预先排除条款。|近义词：exclusions, exceptions, provisos|反义词：inclusions
