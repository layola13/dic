phreatic:/friˈætɪk/| adj.|地下水的；潜水面的；与地下水有关的 | Relating to or denoting groundwater, especially that which is in the zone of saturation.  Phreatic eruptions are explosive volcanic eruptions driven by steam generated when magma interacts with groundwater. | The phreatic zone is saturated with groundwater.  地下水区域是饱和的。

The phreatic eruption caused significant damage to the surrounding area.  潜水面喷发对周围地区造成了重大破坏。|近义词：subterranean, subsurface |反义词：surface, aboveground
