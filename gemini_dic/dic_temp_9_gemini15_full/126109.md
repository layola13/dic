mutative:/ˈmjuːtətɪv/| adj.| 可变的；易变的；突变的 | Capable of undergoing or causing mutation; characterized by or causing change or alteration.  It often refers to genetic changes, but can also apply to other types of change or variation. | Example: The mutative effects of radiation on DNA are well-documented. 辐射对DNA的诱变效应是有据可查的。The mutative nature of the political landscape made predictions difficult.  政治局势的变幻莫测使得预测变得困难。|近义词：changeable, variable, mutable, transformative |反义词：static, unchanging, immutable
