semipalmated: /ˌsɛmiˈpælmeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 半蹼的 | Partially webbed.  This term describes birds' feet with webbing between only part of the toes, typically the three front toes. This adaptation aids in swimming and wading in shallow water.  | Example:  The semipalmated sandpiper has partially webbed feet, ideal for its habitat. 半蹼鹬的脚是半蹼的，非常适合它的栖息地。 | 近义词: partially web-footed | 反义词: fully webbed, unwebbed
