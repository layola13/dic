droopingness:/ˈdruːpɪŋnəs/| n. |下垂；萎靡；衰弱 | The state or quality of being drooping; a lack of energy or vitality; sadness or depression.  It often describes a physical state (like wilting plants) or an emotional state (like low spirits).|例句：The droopingness of the flowers indicated they needed water. 花朵下垂的样子表明它们需要浇水。

The extreme droopingness of his shoulders betrayed his exhaustion. 他肩膀极度下垂的样子暴露了他的疲惫。|近义词：sagging, wilting, dejection, depression, lethargy|反义词：uprightness, vigor, cheerfulness, liveliness
