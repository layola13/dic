reinfusions: /ˌriːɪnˈfjuːʒənz/ | n. | 再输液；再次输注 | The act of infusing something again, typically a fluid such as blood or medication, into a vein or other part of the body.  | Example:  The patient required several reinfusions of blood after the surgery.  患者手术后需要多次输血。


Example: The doctor ordered reinfusions of the saline solution to maintain hydration. 医生下令再次输注盐水溶液以维持水分。|近义词：reintroduction, reinjection |反义词：removal, extraction |名词
