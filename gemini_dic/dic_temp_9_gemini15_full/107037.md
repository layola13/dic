lactobacillus:/ˌlæktəʊbæˈsɪləs/| n. |乳酸菌|A genus of Gram-positive bacteria that ferment sugars to produce lactic acid.  They are commonly found in fermented foods and the human gut, and are important for human health.  Many species are used as probiotics.|例句：Lactobacillus is a crucial component of many probiotic products. 乳酸菌是许多益生菌产品的重要组成部分。|例句：The presence of Lactobacillus in yogurt contributes to its health benefits. 酸奶中乳酸菌的存在有助于其健康益处。|近义词：probiotic bacteria, lactic acid bacteria|反义词：pathogenic bacteria (某些致病菌)|名词
