reanimation:/ˌriːænɪˈmeɪʃən/| n.|复活；复苏；再生；动画制作|The act or process of reviving or restoring to life or consciousness; the process of bringing something back to life or activity.  It can also refer to the process of creating animation.|例句：The doctors performed reanimation techniques on the patient. 医生对病人进行了复苏术。

例句：The old film was given a new lease of life through reanimation. 这部老电影通过动画制作获得了新生。


近义词：resuscitation, revival, restoration, animation

反义词：death, demise, extinction
