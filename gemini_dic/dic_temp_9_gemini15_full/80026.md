go to bed:/ɡoʊ tuː bɛd/ | phrasal verb | 睡觉 | To go to bed means to go to your bed to sleep. It refers to the act of preparing for and entering sleep.  | 例句：I usually go to bed at 11 pm. 我通常晚上11点睡觉。

It’s time to go to bed, children.孩子们，该睡觉了。


近义词： retire, go to sleep, turn in | 反义词： wake up, get up


词性: phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I go to bed early on weekdays. = 我工作日早睡。
She goes to bed after she finishes her homework. = 她完成作业后就睡觉。


一般过去时:
I went to bed late last night. = 我昨晚睡得很晚。
They went to bed immediately after dinner. = 他们吃完晚饭后立刻就睡觉了。


现在完成时:
I have gone to bed already. = 我已经睡了。
She has gone to bed because she feels unwell. = 她因为感觉不舒服而睡了。


过去完成时:
I had gone to bed before the phone rang. = 电话响之前我已经睡了。
They had gone to bed when the storm started. = 暴风雨开始的时候他们已经睡了。


将来时:
I will go to bed soon. = 我很快就要睡觉了。
We will go to bed after watching the movie. = 我们看完电影后就去睡觉。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would go to bed earlier. = 如果我是你，我会早点睡觉。
I wish I had gone to bed earlier last night. = 我希望昨晚早点睡觉。


被动语态: (This phrasal verb is not typically used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
What time do you usually go to bed? = 你通常几点睡觉？
Does he go to bed early every night? = 他每晚都早睡吗？


否定句:
I don't go to bed until midnight. = 我直到午夜才睡觉。
He didn't go to bed until he finished reading the book. = 他直到看完书才睡觉。


PHRASE->
go to bed early: 早睡
go to bed late: 晚睡
go to bed hungry: 饿着肚子睡觉
go straight to bed: 直接上床睡觉
get ready for bed: 准备睡觉


词源:
"Go" is from Old English *gān, meaning "to go."  "Bed" is from Old English *bedd, meaning "bed." The phrase developed naturally as a description of the action of going to one's bed for the purpose of sleeping.
