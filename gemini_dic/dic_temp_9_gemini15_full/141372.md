operativity:/ˌɒpərəˈtɪvɪti/| /ˌɑːpəˈreɪtɪvɪti/| n. |可操作性；有效性；运作能力|The capacity to operate or be operated; the state of being operative; effectiveness.  It refers to the degree to which something can be used or functions effectively.  This could be applied to systems, machines, plans, or even people.|例句：The operativity of the new software is being tested. 新软件的可操作性正在测试中。

The high operativity of the manufacturing process resulted in increased productivity. 制造过程的高效运作导致生产力提高。


近义词：effectiveness, efficiency, functionality, workability |反义词：ineffectiveness, malfunction, failure


词源:  Derived from the word "operate," ultimately from the Latin "operari" (to work, to labor). The suffix "-ivity" denotes a state or quality.
