inclusively:/ɪnˈkluːsɪvli/| adv. | 包括地，包含地 |  Inclusively means in a way that includes or encompasses everything or everyone mentioned.  It emphasizes the completeness of the inclusion. | The price is $100, inclusively of tax. (价格包含税款，为100美元)
The range is 1 to 10, inclusively. (范围是从1到10，包括1和10) | comprehensively, completely | exclusively | 副词


近义词：comprehensively, completely
反义词：exclusively
