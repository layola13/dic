piffler:/ˈpɪflər/| n. |喋喋不休的人，琐碎小事；吹毛求疵者 |A person who talks or writes a lot about unimportant things; a trifler.  Someone who is overly concerned with trivial matters or who engages in petty criticism. |例句：He's a real piffler, always complaining about insignificant details. 他是个真正的喋喋不休的人，总是抱怨一些无关紧要的细节。

He spent hours piffling over the placement of a single comma. 他花了好几个小时纠结于一个逗号的放置。

近义词：trifler, nitpicker, quibbler, fusser, fault-finder |反义词： pragmatist, realist
