out-of-line:/ˌaʊt əv laɪn/| adj.|不合规矩的，不合适的，越轨的|Deviating from what is considered normal, acceptable, or expected.  It implies a transgression of rules, norms, or expectations.|例句：His behavior was completely out of line; he needs to apologize. 他的行为完全不合适；他需要道歉。

例句：That comment was out of line and deeply offensive. 那句话太出格了，非常冒犯人。


近义词：improper, inappropriate, unacceptable, unconventional, unruly, aberrant


反义词：acceptable, appropriate, proper, conventional, standard


词性：形容词
