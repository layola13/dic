monotower:/ˈmɒnəʊˌtaʊər/| n. |单塔；单塔式建筑|A single, tall tower, often used for specific purposes like observation or communication.  It's typically a solitary structure, unlike a group of towers.|例句：The monotower stood tall against the skyline, a beacon in the distance. 单塔耸立在城市天际线上，像远处的一座灯塔。 The old communication monotower was abandoned years ago.  那个旧的通讯单塔几年前就被废弃了。|近义词：tower, spire, turret |反义词：multi-towered complex
