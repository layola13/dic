elysium:/ɪˈlɪziəm/| n. |极乐世界；天堂；理想境界 |A paradise or ideal place of great happiness.  Elysium is often used to describe a place of perfect happiness and beauty, often associated with the afterlife in Greek mythology. |例句：The tropical island was an elysium of sun-drenched beaches and crystal-clear waters.  这个热带岛屿是阳光普照的海滩和清澈海水组成的极乐之地。|例句：After years of hard work, she finally achieved her elysium of owning a cozy cottage by the sea.  经过多年的努力，她终于实现了拥有海边舒适小屋的梦想（理想境界）。|近义词：paradise, heaven, utopia |反义词：hell, inferno, dystopia
