rollings:/ˈroʊlɪŋz/ | adj. |滚动的；起伏的；连续不断的| Rolling hills are a characteristic feature of the English countryside.  起伏的丘陵是英格兰乡村的特色。The rolling thunder echoed across the valley. 隆隆的雷声在山谷回响。 |近义词：undulating, waving, continuous |反义词：still, stationary, flat


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The river is rolling steadily towards the sea. = 河水稳定地流向大海。
The ball is rolling across the floor. = 球在房间里滚来滚去。


一般过去时:
The stones rolled down the hill. = 石头滚下山坡。
The credits rolled at the end of the movie. = 电影结束时出现了字幕。


现在完成时:
The snow has been rolling down the mountain all day. = 雪整天都在从山上滚下来。
The farmer has been rolling the fields to prepare for planting. = 农民一直在翻耕田地以准备播种。


过去完成时:
The boulder had rolled down the mountain before the avalanche. = 巨石在雪崩之前已经滚下了山。
The credits had already rolled before I got to my seat. = 我到座位前字幕已经放完了。


将来时:
The ball will roll down the hill. = 球会滚下山。
The clouds will roll in later this afternoon. = 下午晚些时候乌云将会滚滚而来。


虚拟语气:
If the ball rolled faster, it would reach the bottom sooner. = 如果球滚得更快，它会更快到达底部。
I wish the rain would stop rolling in. = 我希望雨能停止。


被动语态:

The hills were rolled by the wind. = 山丘被风吹拂着起伏。


疑问句:

否定句:
