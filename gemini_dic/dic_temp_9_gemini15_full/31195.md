changers:/ˈtʃeɪndʒərz/| n.|改变者，改革者，兑换者|People or things that cause change; those who exchange currency.|The social changers are fighting for equality. 社会变革者正在争取平等。 The bank changers exchanged dollars for euros. 银行兑换员将美元兑换成欧元。|近义词：reformers, innovators, converters|反义词：conservatives, traditionalists

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The changers influence society. = 改变者影响着社会。
These machines change money. = 这些机器兑换钱币。


一般过去时:
The reformers changed the laws. = 改革者改变了法律。
He changed the money at the airport. = 他在机场兑换了钱。


现在完成时:
They have changed the system. = 他们已经改变了系统。
She has changed the currency many times. = 她已经多次兑换货币了。


过去完成时:
They had changed their minds before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经改变了主意。
The bank had already closed before he could change the money. = 在他兑换钱之前银行就已经关门了。


将来时:
We will change the plan tomorrow. = 我们明天将改变计划。
He will change the money at the bank. = 他将在银行兑换钱。


虚拟语气:
If I were a changer, I would make a difference. = 如果我是个改变者，我会有所作为。
I wish I had changed my major earlier. = 我希望我早点换专业。


被动语态:
The rules were changed last year. = 规则去年被改变了。
The money was changed at a very unfavorable rate. = 钱以很不利的汇率兑换了。


疑问句:
Who are the key changers in this movement? = 这个运动中的关键改变者是谁？
Where can I change this money? = 我在哪里可以兑换这笔钱？


否定句:
They did not change their position. = 他们没有改变立场。
He couldn't change the large bill. = 他无法兑换这张大面额钞票。
