gentrify:/ˈdʒɛntrɪfaɪ/ | v. |使（地区）高档化；使（地区）中产阶级化 | To improve (a place) by driving out lower-income residents or businesses and replacing them with higher-income ones, often resulting in increased property values and changes in the character of the neighborhood.  | The neighborhood has been gentrified in recent years, with new cafes and boutiques replacing the old shops.  近几年，这个街区已经被高档化了，新的咖啡馆和精品店取代了旧商店。|近义词：upgrade, improve, upscale|反义词：deteriorate, decline, decay|动词
