sinapic:/sɪˈnæpɪk/ | adj. | 芥子酸的；与芥子酸相关的 | Relating to or derived from sinapic acid, a phenolic acid found in plants.  Sinapic acid is involved in plant defense mechanisms and has antioxidant properties. |  The sinapic acid content of the plant extract was analyzed.  植物提取物中的芥子酸含量进行了分析。 |  ferulic, caffeic |  (no direct antonym, but could consider terms referring to absence of sinapic acid) |  形容词
