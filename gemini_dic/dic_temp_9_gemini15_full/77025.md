gargoulette:/ˌɡɑːrɡuˈlɛt/| n.| 滴水嘴；排水口 | A gargoulette is a decorative spout, often shaped like a grotesque figure, used to drain water from a roof or building.  It is a type of gargoyle, but typically smaller and less dramatically sculpted.  |例句：The old chateau had charming gargoulettes shaped like grinning monkeys, directing rainwater away from the walls.  古老的城堡有可爱的小滴水嘴，形状像咧嘴笑的猴子，将雨水引流到远离墙壁的地方。|近义词：gargoyle, spout, drainpipe |反义词：N/A
