mopsey:/ˈmɑːpsi/| n. |傻瓜，笨蛋；(尤指)小孩|A mopsey is a foolish or silly person, especially a child.  The word carries a somewhat affectionate connotation, suggesting a playful silliness rather than genuine incompetence. |例句：The little mopsey tripped over his own feet.  那个小傻瓜绊倒了自己的脚。

例句：Don't be such a mopsey;  clean your room! 不要这么傻，去打扫你的房间！


近义词：silly, goofy, dunce, simpleton

反义词：clever, intelligent, smart, shrewd

词性：名词
