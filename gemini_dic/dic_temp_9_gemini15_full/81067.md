grainfield: /ˈɡreɪnfiːld/ | n. | 谷田，麦田 | A field where grain is grown.  |例句：The sun shone brightly on the vast grainfield.  阳光普照着广阔的谷田。

例句：We drove past endless grainfields on our way to the coast.  我们去海边的路上经过了无边无际的麦田。


近义词：cornfield, wheatfield | 反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a pasture or forest. | 词性：名词
