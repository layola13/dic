nonproportional:/ˌnɒnprəˈpɔːʃənəl/| adj. |不成比例的，不相称的 | Not proportional; not in proportion.  It refers to a relationship where the change in one variable does not correspond to a directly proportional change in another. For example, if you increase input A by 10%, but output B only increases by 5%, the relationship between A and B is nonproportional. |The punishment was nonproportional to the crime.  惩罚与罪行不成比例。|  proportional, proportionate | inversely proportional, directly proportional | 形容词
