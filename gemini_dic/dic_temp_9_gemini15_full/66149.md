exceptor:/ɪkˈsɛptər/| n.|异议者；反对者；抗议者|One who objects or takes exception.  An exceptor is someone who raises an objection or expresses disapproval. They may challenge a decision, a statement, or a course of action.|例句：The exceptor voiced his concerns about the proposed changes to the project.  异议者对拟议的项目变更表达了他的担忧。

例句：Several exceptors filed formal complaints against the company's policies.  几位异议者对公司的政策提出了正式投诉。


近义词：objector, protester, dissenter, opponent

反义词：supporter, proponent, approver


词性：名词
