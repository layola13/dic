cooperativeness:/koʊˌɔːpəreɪtɪvnəs/| n. |合作性，合作精神|The willingness to cooperate and work together towards a common goal.  It often involves compromise, mutual respect, and a shared understanding of objectives.  |例句：The team's success was largely due to their exceptional cooperativeness.|近义词：collaboration, teamwork, synergy|反义词：uncooperativeness, antagonism, opposition|名词
