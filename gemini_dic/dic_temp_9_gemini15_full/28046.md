cape:/keɪp/ | n. | 海角；披风；岬 | A cape is a point of land that extends into the sea, or a sleeveless garment worn over the shoulders.  | Example:  The ship rounded the rocky cape.  那艘船绕过了岩石嶙峋的海角。
The superhero wore a flowing red cape.  超级英雄披着飘逸的红色披风。 |  promontory, headland | inland | noun
