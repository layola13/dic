derived:/dɪˈraɪvd/ | adj. v. | 派生的；衍生的；获得的 | Obtained from a source; deduced or inferred | The word "derived" is derived from the Latin word "derivāre," meaning "to draw off" or "to turn aside."  | Example:  His wealth is derived from his family's business.  他的财富来自他家族的企业。 |近义词：obtained, deduced, inferred | 反义词：original, innate, inherent |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The data is derived from multiple sources. = 数据来自多个来源。
This word is derived from Latin. = 这个词源于拉丁语。


一般过去时:
The conclusion was derived from the evidence. = 结论是从证据中推导出来的。
Scientists derived a new theory from the experiment. = 科学家们从实验中推导出了一个新的理论。


现在完成时:
Many English words have been derived from French. = 许多英语单词是从法语中派生出来的。
The information has been derived from reliable sources. = 信息来自可靠的来源。


过去完成时:
The hypothesis had been derived from previous research. = 假设是从之前的研究中推导出来的。
He had derived great satisfaction from his work. = 他从他的工作中获得了极大的满足感。


将来时:
The results will be derived from the upcoming survey. = 结果将来自即将进行的调查。
We will derive the formula from the given equation. = 我们将从给定的方程式中推导出公式。


虚拟语气:
If we had more data, we could derive a more accurate model. = 如果我们有更多的数据，我们就能推导出一个更精确的模型。
I wish I could derive more meaning from this book. = 我希望我能从这本书中获得更多意义。


被动语态:
The information was derived by the research team. = 信息是由研究小组获得的。
The new drug is being derived from natural sources. = 新药正在从天然来源中提取。


疑问句:
Where was this information derived? = 这个信息是从哪里获得的？
How is the conclusion derived? = 结论是如何推导出来的？


否定句:

His happiness was not derived from material possessions. = 他的快乐并非来自物质财富。
