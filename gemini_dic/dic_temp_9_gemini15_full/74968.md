fricassee:/ˌfrɪkəˈseɪ/| n. & vt.|炖菜；（尤指鸡肉）炖菜；炖（尤指鸡肉）|A fricassee is a dish of meat (usually chicken) cut into pieces and stewed or fried in a white sauce.  It often includes vegetables and herbs.|例句：The chef prepared a delicious chicken fricassee with mushrooms and cream.  厨师做了一道美味的奶油蘑菇鸡肉炖菜。|例句：She decided to fricassee the rabbit for dinner. 她决定把兔子炖了当晚餐。|近义词：stew, ragout|反义词：roast, grill
