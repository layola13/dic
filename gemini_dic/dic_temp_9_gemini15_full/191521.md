schmoozing:/ʃmuːzɪŋ/| v.|闲聊；奉承；拉关系|To engage in casual conversation, often with the aim of making connections or gaining favor.  It often implies insincere flattery.|例句：He spent the evening schmoozing with potential investors. 他整个晚上都在和潜在投资者闲聊。

例句：She was schmoozing the boss in an attempt to get a promotion. 她为了升职而逢迎老板。

近义词：networking, socializing, flattering, ingratiating

反义词：ignoring, snubbing, alienating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He schmoozes with clients every week. = 他每周都和客户闲聊。
They schmooze to build relationships. = 他们通过闲聊建立关系。


一般过去时:
She schmoozed the VIPs at the party. = 她在派对上跟重要人物套近乎。
They schmoozed all night at the conference. = 他们在会议上聊了一整夜。


现在完成时:
He has schmoozed his way to the top. = 他靠着逢迎拍马爬到了顶峰。
She has schmoozed numerous contacts in the industry. = 她在这个行业里已经和许多人建立了联系。


过去完成时:

They had schmoozed the potential buyers for weeks. = 他们已经和潜在买家套了几个星期的近乎。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

疑问句:
