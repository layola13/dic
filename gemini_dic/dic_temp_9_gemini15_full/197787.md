shingle:/ˈʃɪŋɡəl/| n.|瓦片；带状疱疹；招牌；|a thin piece of wood, slate, or other material used to cover roofs and walls.  Also refers to a skin condition.|例句：The roof was covered with grey shingles. 屋顶覆盖着灰色的瓦片。

The doctor diagnosed him with shingles. 医生诊断他患上了带状疱疹。|近义词：tile, slate, clapboard|反义词：none.
