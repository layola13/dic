flightiest: /ˈflaɪtiɪst/ | adj. | 极度轻浮的，反复无常的，不稳定的 | Extremely frivolous, capricious, or erratic.  It suggests a lack of seriousness and a tendency towards impulsive behavior. | 例句：Her flightiest behavior made it difficult to trust her. 她的行为极其轻浮，让人难以信任。


例句：His flightiest ideas often led to unexpected consequences. 他那些反复无常的想法常常导致意想不到的后果。


近义词：frivolous, capricious, erratic, fickle, irresponsible, unsteady, volatile


反义词：serious, responsible, steady, reliable, stable, constant


词性：形容词


词源：源自动词flit (快速移动; 闪现)，加上形容词后缀-iest (表示最高级)。  The sense of lightness and instability is derived from the image of something flitting about erratically.
