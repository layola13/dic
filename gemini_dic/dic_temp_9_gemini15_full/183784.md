rescueless: /ˈreskjuːləs/ | adj. | 无救的；无法营救的；绝望的 |  Lacking rescue; hopeless; beyond help.  The word implies a state of utter helplessness and despair, suggesting that there is no possibility of rescue or salvation. |


例句：

The trapped miners were in a rescueless situation, with little hope of survival.  被困的矿工们处于无法营救的境地，几乎没有生存希望。

After days adrift at sea, their rescueless plight prompted a massive international search effort.  在海上漂流多日后，他们绝望的困境促使了大规模的国际搜救行动。


近义词：hopeless, desperate, irreversible, irremediable

反义词：salvageable, rescuable, hopeful


词性：形容词
