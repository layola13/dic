mussing: /ˈmʌsɪŋ/ | v. |弄乱；弄皱；使蓬乱 | To make untidy or disheveled; to rumple or wrinkle.  |例句：She was mussling her hair in frustration. 她沮丧地揉乱了自己的头发。 |近义词：rumpling, disheveling, tousling, messing up |反义词：smoothing, straightening, tidying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is mussling his hair. = 他正在揉乱他的头发。
She musses her papers every day. = 她每天都弄乱她的文件。


一般过去时:
He mussed his hair before the meeting. = 他在会议前揉乱了他的头发。
She mussed up the report. = 她把报告弄乱了。


现在完成时:
He has mussed his tie. = 他已经弄皱了他的领带。
She has mussed up the bed. = 她已经弄乱了床铺。


过去完成时:
He had mussed his shirt before the interview. = 他在面试前已经弄皱了他的衬衫。
She had mussed up the documents before leaving. = 她离开前已经弄乱了文件。


将来时:
He will muss his hair if he continues running. = 如果他继续跑，他会弄乱他的头发。
She will muss the dress if she isn't careful. = 如果她不小心，她会弄皱这件衣服。


虚拟语气:

I wish I hadn't mussed my papers. = 我真希望我没有弄乱我的文件。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
