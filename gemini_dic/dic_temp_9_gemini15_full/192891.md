seatbelt:/ˈsiːtbelt/ | n. |安全带 | A strap or belt designed to secure a person in a seat, typically in a vehicle.  | Example: Always fasten your seatbelt before driving.  系好安全带再开车。|近义词：safety belt, restraint |反义词：无
