daboia:/ˈdæboɪə/| n. |沙地蝰蛇属|A genus of venomous vipers found in Africa and Asia.  They are characterized by their large size, stout bodies, and heat-sensing pits.  The bite of a daboia can be very dangerous.|例句：The daboia russelii is responsible for a significant number of snakebite deaths in India.  印度眼镜蛇是印度大量蛇咬致死事件的罪魁祸首。|近义词：viper, snake|反义词:|无
