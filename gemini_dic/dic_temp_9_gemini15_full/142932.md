out-trade:/ˌaʊtˈtreɪd/| vt.|以贸易战胜，在贸易方面胜过|To surpass or defeat in trade or commerce.|例句：The smaller company out-traded its larger competitor by focusing on niche markets.  这家规模较小的公司通过专注于利基市场，在贸易上战胜了其规模更大的竞争对手。|例句：Their innovative marketing strategies allowed them to out-trade their rivals in the global market. 他们创新的营销策略使他们在全球市场上战胜了竞争对手。|近义词：outsmart, outdo, outmaneuver|反义词：be out-traded by, undersell


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The small business out-trades its competitors in customer service. = 这家小企业在客户服务方面胜过其竞争对手。
Our company out-trades all others in this region. = 我们公司在这个地区胜过所有其他公司。

一般过去时:
Last year, they out-traded their rivals in the Asian market. = 去年，他们在亚洲市场上战胜了竞争对手。
The startup out-traded established brands with a disruptive product. = 这家初创公司凭借一款颠覆性产品战胜了老牌品牌。

现在完成时:
They have out-traded their rivals consistently over the past five years. = 在过去的五年里，他们始终胜过竞争对手。
Our new strategy has out-traded the old one in terms of profit. = 就利润而言，我们的新战略已经胜过了旧战略。

过去完成时:
By the end of the year, they had out-traded every other firm in the industry. = 到年底，他们已经战胜了该行业中的其他所有公司。
The company had out-traded its competitors before the market crash. = 在市场崩溃之前，该公司已经战胜了其竞争对手。

将来时:
We expect to out-trade our competitors with this new product launch. = 我们预计凭借这款新产品的推出将胜过竞争对手。
With this investment, they will out-trade their rivals in the long term. = 通过这项投资，他们将在长期内胜过竞争对手。

虚拟语气:
If we had better marketing, we could out-trade them easily. = 如果我们的营销更好，我们可以轻松地战胜他们。
I wish we had out-traded our competitors last quarter. = 我希望我们上个季度能战胜竞争对手。

被动语态:
They were out-traded by a smaller, more agile company. = 他们被一家规模更小、更灵活的公司战胜了。
Our market share was out-traded by aggressive pricing from our competitors. = 我们的市场份额被竞争对手的激进定价所蚕食。

疑问句:
Can we out-trade our competitors with our current strategy? = 我们能否用目前的战略战胜竞争对手？
Will they be able to out-trade us in the next financial year? = 他们能在下一个财政年度战胜我们吗？

否定句:
We didn't out-trade them last year, but we will this year. = 我们去年没有战胜他们，但今年会。
The company failed to out-trade its rivals due to poor management. = 由于管理不善，该公司未能战胜竞争对手。
