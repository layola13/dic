slitter:/ˈslɪtər/| n. v.| 切割机；切缝机；切割者 | A machine that slits or cuts material into strips or pieces.  A person or thing that slits. |例句：The paper slitter neatly divided the large sheet into smaller sections.  纸张切割机整齐地将大张纸分成较小的部分。 The factory uses a high-speed slitter to process rolls of fabric. 工厂使用高速切缝机来加工纺织品卷。|近义词：cutter, slicer, divider |反义词：joiner, assembler
