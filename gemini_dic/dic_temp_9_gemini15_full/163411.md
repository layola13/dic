preconstituents:/ˌpriːkənˈstɪtʃuənts/ | n. |预先成分，预先构成物 |  The word refers to the elements or components that exist or are combined before a process or structure is formed.  It emphasizes the existence of these components *before* the final product or entity comes into being.  This term is often used in fields like chemistry, linguistics, and psychology. | Example: The preconstituents of the cake batter included flour, sugar, eggs, and butter.  蛋糕面糊的预先成分包括面粉、糖、鸡蛋和黄油。|近义词: components, ingredients, elements |反义词:end products, results
