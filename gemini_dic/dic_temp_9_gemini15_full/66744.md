exorcize:/ˈɛksɔːrsɑɪz/ | vt. |驱魔，去除(坏事)| to drive out an evil spirit; to get rid of something unpleasant or unwanted |例句：The priest attempted to exorcize the demon from the possessed woman. = 牧师试图将恶魔从被附身的女子身上驱逐出去。|例句：He tried to exorcize the bad memories from his mind. = 他试图从脑海中驱除不好的回忆。|近义词：expel, banish, eradicate |反义词：summon, invite, encourage
