sibyl:/ˈsɪbəl/| n. |女预言家；预言者 |A sibyl is a woman in ancient Greek and Roman religion and literature who is believed to have prophetic powers.  They were often associated with oracles and their pronouncements were considered to be divinely inspired.  The Sibylline Books, a collection of prophecies, are a famous example of this association.  |例句：The sibyl's prophecy foretold the downfall of the king. 女预言家的预言预示着国王的垮台。|例句：Many sought the advice of the sibyl, hoping to glimpse their future. 许多人寻求女预言家的建议，希望能窥见他们的未来。|近义词：prophet, seer, oracle, clairvoyant |反义词：N/A |名词
