reinforced:/ˌriːɪnˈfɔːrst/| adj. | 加强的；加固的；得到强化的 | strengthened; made stronger or more effective.  Reinforced concrete is much stronger than ordinary concrete.  |近义词：strengthened, fortified, buttressed |反义词：weakened, undermined


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The steel bars reinforce the concrete. = 钢筋增强了混凝土的强度。
This evidence reinforces my belief. = 这证据加强了我的信念。


一般过去时:
They reinforced the bridge before the storm. = 他们在暴风雨前加固了桥梁。
The teacher reinforced the lesson with examples. = 老师用例子巩固了这节课的内容。


现在完成时:
The company has reinforced its security measures. = 公司已经加强了安全措施。
Recent events have reinforced the need for change. = 最近发生的事件更加突显了变革的必要性。


过去完成时:
They had reinforced the dam before the flood. = 他们在洪水来临之前已经加固了水坝。
She had reinforced her argument with new data. = 她已经用新的数据强化了她的论点。


将来时:
We will reinforce the walls before the earthquake. = 我们将在地震前加固墙壁。
The government will reinforce its efforts to combat crime. = 政府将加强打击犯罪的力度。


虚拟语气:
If they reinforced the structure, it would be safer. = 如果他们加固了结构，它会更安全。
I wish they had reinforced the defenses. = 我希望他们当时加固了防御工事。


被动语态:
The building was reinforced with steel beams. = 这座建筑物用钢梁加固了。
The message was reinforced through repetition. = 通过重复，信息得到了加强。


疑问句:
Were the walls reinforced properly? = 墙壁加固得妥当吗？
Has the bridge been reinforced since the accident? = 事故后桥梁是否进行了加固？


否定句:
The structure wasn't reinforced adequately. = 这结构没有得到充分的加固。
The argument wasn't reinforced by sufficient evidence. = 论据缺乏足够的证据来支持。
