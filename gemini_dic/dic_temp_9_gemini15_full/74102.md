fota:/ˈfoʊtə/ | n. |空中下载技术(Firmware Over-The-Air)|Firmware Over-The-Air (FOTA) is a wireless technology that allows for the remote updating of firmware on embedded systems.  This eliminates the need for physical access to the device for updates. It's commonly used in devices like smartphones, vehicles, and industrial equipment.|Example: My car received a FOTA update that improved its fuel efficiency. 我的汽车收到了一个FOTA更新，提高了燃油效率。|近义词：remote firmware update, wireless update|反义词：manual firmware update
