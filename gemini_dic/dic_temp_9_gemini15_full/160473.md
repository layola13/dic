popup:/ˈpɒpʌp/ /ˈpɑːpʌp/| n., v.|弹出窗口，突然出现|A popup is a window that appears suddenly on a computer screen, often containing an advertisement or other information.  As a verb, it means to appear suddenly.|例句：An advertisement popup appeared on my screen.  一个广告弹出窗口出现在我的屏幕上。
A new window popped up when I clicked the link. 当我点击链接时，一个新窗口弹了出来。|近义词：appear, emerge, spring up|反义词：disappear, vanish
