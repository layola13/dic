interviewing: /ˈɪntərˌvjuːɪŋ/ | v. | 面试；采访 | The act of conducting interviews, either to assess a candidate for a job or to gather information for a news story or research.  It involves asking questions and listening to responses. | 例句：The company is interviewing candidates for the open position.  公司正在面试空缺职位的候选人。

例句：The journalist is interviewing the celebrity about their new movie.  记者正在采访这位名人在他们新电影的相关事宜。


近义词：questioning, interrogating, surveying


反义词：N/A (no direct antonym, but opposites might include "hiring" if referring to the job context, or "ignoring" if referring to the information-gathering context)


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is interviewing candidates every day. = 他每天都在面试候选人。
She interviews sources for her articles. = 她为她的文章采访消息来源。


一般过去时:
They interviewed five applicants yesterday. = 他们昨天面试了五个申请人。
The police interviewed the witness about the accident. = 警察就事故采访了目击者。


现在完成时:
I have interviewed over 100 people for this project. = 我已经为这个项目面试了100多人。
She has interviewed many experts in her field. = 她已经采访了她所在领域的许多专家。


过去完成时:
They had interviewed all the candidates before lunch. = 他们午餐前已经面试了所有候选人。
The reporter had interviewed the mayor before the press conference. = 记者在新闻发布会之前已经采访了市长。


将来时:
We will be interviewing potential employees next week. = 我们下周将要面试潜在的员工。
He will interview the author tomorrow. = 他明天将采访这位作者。


虚拟语气:
If I were interviewing for that job, I would be nervous. = 如果我去面试那份工作，我会很紧张。
I wish I had interviewed more candidates before making a decision. = 我希望在做决定之前我已经面试了更多候选人。


被动语态:
Many applicants were interviewed for the position. = 许多申请人被面试了这个职位。
The witness was being interviewed by the detectives. = 目击者正在接受警探的采访。


疑问句:
Are you interviewing for the marketing position? = 你在面试市场营销职位吗？
Have they interviewed all the potential hires yet? = 他们已经面试所有潜在的雇员了吗？


否定句:
They did not interview anyone for the job. = 他们没有为这份工作面试任何人。
She wasn't interviewing for the manager's role. = 她没有面试经理的职位。



PHRASE->
interview for a job = 面试工作
conduct an interview = 进行面试
prepare for an interview = 准备面试
job interview = 工作面试
panel interview = 小组面试
