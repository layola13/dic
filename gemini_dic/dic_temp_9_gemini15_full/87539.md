heteroglossic:/ˌhɛtərəˈɡlɒsɪk/| adj.| 多语的；异质的；多元话语的 |  Characterized by the presence of multiple voices or perspectives.  It describes a text or discourse that incorporates different languages, dialects, registers, or points of view, reflecting the complex and often conflicting nature of social reality.  | Example:  The novel's heteroglossic nature is evident in its use of colloquialisms, formal language, and multiple narrative voices. 这部小说的多语性体现在它使用了口语、正式语言和多种叙事声音。|近义词：polyphonic, multivocal, diverse | 反义词：monoglossic, univocal
