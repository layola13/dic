honouring: /ˈɒnərɪŋ/ | /ˈɑːnərɪŋ/ | v. | 尊敬；纪念；表彰 | To show great respect for someone or something.  |例句：The ceremony was held to honouring the victims of the war.  仪式是为了纪念战争的受害者而举行的。

The company is honouring its commitment to environmental protection.  这家公司正在履行其对环境保护的承诺。


近义词：celebrating, commemorating, respecting, recognizing | 反义词：dishonouring, disrespecting, ignoring

词源:
The word "honouring" comes from the Old French word "honorer," meaning "to honour," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "honor," meaning "honor, respect, reverence."  The "-ing" suffix indicates the present participle form of the verb.
