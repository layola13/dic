half-smiling: /ˈhæf ˈsmaɪlɪŋ/ | adj. | 半笑，带着一丝微笑的 |  Describes a smile that is not full or complete, often suggesting slight amusement, reserve, or uncertainty. It implies a partial or hesitant smile.  | Example: She gave him a half-smiling, enigmatic look. 她给了他一个半笑不笑、神秘莫测的眼神。  He offered a half-smiling apology. 他报以一个半笑歉意的表情。|近义词：partially smiling, faintly smiling, slightly smiling | 反义词：frowning, scowling
