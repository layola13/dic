It can refer to the redistribution of funds from a larger grant to smaller organizations or individuals, or to a renewal of funding for a previously supported project. | Example: The foundation announced re-grants to support local community projects.  这个基金会宣布再次拨款以支持地方社区项目。|近义词：regranting,  redistribution,  refunding |反义词：withdrawal, revocation |名词
