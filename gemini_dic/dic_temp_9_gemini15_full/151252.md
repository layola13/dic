parenthesis:/pəˈrɛnθəsɪs/| n. |圆括号；括号；插入语|A parenthesis is a word, phrase, or sentence inserted as an aside within another sentence.  It is usually enclosed in parentheses ( ).  It can also refer to a parenthetical remark or expression.|例句：The extra information is enclosed in parenthesis.  额外信息用括号括起来。

例句：He added a parenthetical remark about the weather. 他补充了一句关于天气的插话。


近义词：brackets, interpolation, aside|反义词：N/A|词性:noun
