preexamination:/ˌpriːɪɡˌzæməˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 预考，预试 | A preliminary examination; a test or trial examination given before a final examination.  | Example: The preexamination helped students identify their weak areas before the final exam. 预考帮助学生在期末考试前找出自己的薄弱环节。 | 近义词：trial examination, preliminary test | 反义词：final examination
