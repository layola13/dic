gash's:/ɡæʃz/| n.| 裂口；伤口（gash的复数形式）| A gash is a long deep cut, especially one in the flesh.  The word often implies a violent or sudden injury.|例句：The accident left a deep gash on his leg.  这次事故在他的腿上留下了一道很深的伤口。|例句：The surgeon carefully stitched up the gash on the patient's arm.  外科医生小心地缝合了病人手臂上的伤口。|近义词：cut, wound, laceration |反义词：healing, closure
