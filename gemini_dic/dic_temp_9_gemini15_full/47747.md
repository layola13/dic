decaling:/ˈdiːkeɪlɪŋ/| n.|脱胶；脱层；去除贴面|The process of removing a decal or adhesive label from a surface.  Decaling involves separating the decal from its backing and applying it to a new surface or removing it completely.|例句：The decaling of the old stickers was a painstaking process.  旧贴纸的脱胶过程非常费力。
The car's paint job required careful decaling to avoid damage. 汽车的喷漆需要小心地去除贴花以避免损坏。|近义词：removing, stripping, peeling|反义词：applying, affixing, sticking
