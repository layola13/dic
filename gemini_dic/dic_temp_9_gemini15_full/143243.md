outdebating: /ˌaʊtˈdeɪtɪŋ/ | v. |  辩论胜过；在辩论中击败 | To defeat someone in a debate.  |  例句：He outdebated his opponent with his superior knowledge of the subject. 他凭借其在该主题上的渊博知识，在辩论中击败了他的对手。


例句：The young lawyer skillfully outdebated the seasoned prosecutor.  年轻的律师巧妙地击败了经验丰富的检察官。


近义词：outargue, surpass in debate, defeat in argument


反义词：be outdebated, lose a debate


词性：动词
