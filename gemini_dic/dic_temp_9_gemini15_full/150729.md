parachrose:/ˈpærəˌkroʊz/ | n. |副色；伴色 |A secondary color, especially one that is produced by the mixing of two primary colors. Parachrose is less commonly used and more often found in specialized contexts like art or color theory. |Example: The painting featured a vibrant blue as the main color, but the subtle parachrose of green added depth and complexity.|近义词: secondary color, complementary color, tint, shade|反义词: primary color
