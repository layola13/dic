planish:/ˈplænɪʃ/ | vt. | 打光，压平，锤光 | To make (a metal surface) smooth and even by hammering or rolling.  |例句：The blacksmith planished the metal until it shone.  铁匠把金属打磨光滑直到它闪闪发光。

例句：The artisan carefully planished the silver to create a mirror-like finish. 工匠小心地锤打银器，使其具有镜面般的光泽。


近义词：smooth, flatten, level, polish | 反义词：roughen, wrinkle, crease | 词性：动词

词源:
