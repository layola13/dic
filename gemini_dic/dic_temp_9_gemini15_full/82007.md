griper:/ˈɡraɪpər/| n. |抱怨者，牢骚者 |A griper is a person who frequently complains or grumbles.  They often focus on negative aspects and rarely express appreciation. | Example: He's a real griper; he's always complaining about something. 他是个十足的抱怨者；他总是抱怨一些事情。|近义词：complainer, grumbler, whiner |反义词：optimist, complaisant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gripes about the weather every day. = 他每天都抱怨天气。
She gripes constantly about her job. = 她不断抱怨她的工作。


一般过去时:
He griped about the long lines at the store yesterday. = 昨天他抱怨商店的长队。
She griped to her boss about the unfair workload. = 她向老板抱怨工作量不公平。


现在完成时:
He has griped about this problem for months. = 他已经抱怨这个问题好几个月了。
She has griped repeatedly about the lack of support. = 她一再抱怨缺乏支持。


过去完成时:
He had griped about the project before the meeting even started. = 会议还没开始他就已经抱怨这个项目了。
She had griped extensively about the delays before finally resigning. = 在最终辞职之前，她已经广泛地抱怨过延误。


将来时:
He will gripe about the new rules as soon as they're implemented. = 这些新规一旦实施，他就会抱怨。
She will gripe incessantly if she doesn't get her way. = 如果她不能如愿以偿，她会没完没了地抱怨。


虚拟语气:
If he didn't gripe so much, people would like him better. = 如果他不那么爱抱怨，人们会更喜欢他。
I wish she wouldn't gripe about everything all the time. = 我希望她不要总是抱怨所有的事情。


被动语态:
His complaints are often griped about by his colleagues. = 他的抱怨经常被他的同事们议论。
The new policy was griped about extensively by the staff. = 新政策受到了员工的广泛抱怨。


疑问句:
Does he often gripe about his coworkers? = 他经常抱怨他的同事吗？
Why is she always griping about her life? = 她为什么总是抱怨她的生活？


否定句:
He doesn't gripe as much as he used to. = 他不像以前那样抱怨多了。
She didn't gripe about the changes this time. = 这次她没有抱怨这些改变。


PHRASE->
a griper of the first order = 头号抱怨者
a chronic griper = 慢性抱怨者
professional griper = 职业抱怨者
habitual griper = 习惯性抱怨者
to gripe and moan = 抱怨和呻吟
