pneumatics:/njuːˈmætɪks/ | n. |气体力学；气动装置 |The study of the mechanical properties of gases and their application in various systems.  Pneumatics is used in many industrial applications, such as controlling machinery with compressed air. |例句：The factory uses pneumatics to power its robotic arms.  这家工厂用气动装置驱动其机械臂。

例句：We are studying the principles of pneumatics in our engineering class. 我们在工程课上学习气体力学的原理。


近义词：compressed air systems, gas mechanics, air pressure systems |反义词：hydraulics (液压学)
