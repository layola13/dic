co-official:/ˌkoʊ əˈfɪʃəl/ | adj. | 共同的官员；协同官员的 |  An adjective describing a person who holds a position of authority jointly with another.  It implies shared responsibility and authority. | Example: The co-officials signed the treaty together. 共同的官员一起签署了条约。 | 近义词: joint official, fellow official | 反义词: sole official, independent official |  adj.
