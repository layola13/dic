re-pressuring: /ˌriːˈprɛʃərɪŋ/ | v. | 再施压，再次施加压力 | The act of applying pressure again, often to someone who has already been pressured.  This implies a renewed or continued attempt to influence or persuade someone. |  例句：The company is re-pressuring its suppliers to lower their prices. 公司正在再次向供应商施压，要求他们降低价格。


例句：The government is re-pressuring the bank to lend more money to small businesses. 政府正在再次向银行施压，要求其向小型企业发放更多贷款。


近义词：re-applying pressure, renewing pressure, reiterating pressure,  insisting, urging


反义词：relieving pressure, easing pressure,  yielding, giving in


词性：动词 (verb)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-pressure their employees to meet unrealistic deadlines. = 他们再次向员工施压，要求他们完成不切实际的最后期限。
The sales team re-pressures clients to make a purchase. = 销售团队再次向客户施压，促使其购买。


一般过去时:
The manager re-pressured the team after the missed deadline. = 在错过最后期限后，经理再次向团队施压。
She re-pressured him to reconsider his decision. = 她再次向他施压，让他重新考虑他的决定。


现在完成时:
They have re-pressured us several times this month. = 这个月他们已经好几次再次向我们施压了。
The board has re-pressured the CEO to increase profits. = 董事会再次向首席执行官施压，要求其提高利润。


过去完成时:
They had re-pressured the negotiator before the final meeting. = 在最后一次会议之前，他们已经再次向谈判代表施压了。
The landlord had re-pressured the tenant to pay the overdue rent. = 房东已经再次向租户施压，要求其支付逾期租金。


将来时:
The government will re-pressure the company to comply with regulations. = 政府将会再次向公司施压，要求其遵守规章制度。
He will re-pressure her to accept the job offer. = 他将再次向她施压，让她接受这份工作机会。


虚拟语气:
If they re-pressured him, he might reconsider. = 如果他们再次向他施压，他可能会重新考虑。
I wish they hadn't re-pressured her into signing the contract. = 我真希望他们没有再次向她施压让她签合同。


被动语态:
The employees were re-pressured to work overtime. = 员工们再次被迫加班。

疑问句:

否定句:
They did not re-pressure the witness to change their testimony. = 他们没有再次向证人施压以改变证词。
She refused to be re-pressured into accepting the assignment. = 她拒绝再次被迫接受这项任务。
