nondeliveries:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈlɪvəriːz/ | n. |未送达的货物；未完成的任务 | Instances where goods or services were not delivered as expected.  This term often refers to a quantity or number of such instances.  |例句：The company reported a high number of nondeliveries due to the recent storm. 最近的风暴导致该公司报告了大量的货物未送达。

The high number of nondeliveries led to customer complaints.  大量的货物未送达导致客户投诉。

|近义词：undelivered items, failed deliveries, missed deliveries |反义词：deliveries, successful deliveries |名词
