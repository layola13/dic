exhaustion:/ɪɡˈzɔːʃən/ | n. | 精疲力竭；耗尽；衰竭 | The state of being extremely tired and having no energy left.  It can be physical, mental, or both. | 例句：After running the marathon, I felt complete exhaustion.  马拉松跑完后，我感到精疲力竭。


例句：The constant pressure at work led to her mental exhaustion.  工作上的持续压力导致她精神疲惫。


近义词：fatigue, tiredness, weariness, depletion | 反义词：energy, vigor, vitality


词源：源自于动词 exhaust，其拉丁词根是 *ex- (out) + *haurire (to draw).  最初的意思是“抽干，耗尽”，后来引申出“使精疲力竭”的意思。
