flockless:/ˈflɒkləs/| adj.|不群居的；没有成群结队的；单独的|Not tending to form flocks or groups; solitary.  The flockless sheep wandered alone in the vast pasture.  这只不群居的羊独自在广阔的牧场上漫游。|近义词：solitary, lone, independent|反义词：gregarious, sociable
