heartstrings: /ˈhɑːrtstrɪŋz/ | n. | 心弦；感情；最深处的感情 | The deepest emotions; feelings that evoke strong emotional responses.  |  The music tugged at my heartstrings.  那音乐拨动了我的心弦。 |近义词：emotions, feelings, sentiments | 反义词：apathy, indifference
