grayest:/ˈɡreɪɪst/ | adj. | 最灰的，最黯淡的 | Most gray; lacking brightness or color.  The adjective is the superlative form of "gray". It describes the highest degree of grayness. | Example: The grayest day of the year was yesterday.  昨天是一年中最灰暗的一天。 The sky was the grayest I'd ever seen. 天空是我见过的最灰暗的天空。 | 近义词：darkest, dullest, most somber | 反义词：brightest, whitest, most vibrant |  形容词
