committing: /kəˈmɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 犯（罪）；做（错事）；承诺；投入；委托 |

英文解析:The present participle of "commit."  It describes the act of performing an action, often one that is serious or has consequences, or the act of making a pledge or dedication.

例句:

He is committing a serious crime. = 他正在犯一项严重的罪行。

She is committing herself to finishing the project on time. = 她正致力于按时完成项目。


近义词: perpetrating, engaging in, pledging, dedicating, entrusting


反义词:  absolving, releasing, neglecting, ignoring


词性: Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are committing fraud. = 他们在进行诈骗。
He is committing to a healthier lifestyle. = 他正在致力于更健康的生活方式。


一般过去时:
She committed a mistake. = 她犯了一个错误。

现在完成时:
He has committed many errors. = 他犯了很多错误。

过去完成时:

She had committed to the project before she found out the difficulties. = 在她发现困难之前，她已经承诺了这个项目。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
