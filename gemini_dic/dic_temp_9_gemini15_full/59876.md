ecumenists:/ɪˈkjuːmənɪsts/| n. |大公教会主义者；普世主义者|Advocates of ecumenism,  the movement promoting unity among Christian churches.  They strive for cooperation and understanding between different denominations. |例句：Many ecumenists believe that Christian churches should work together to address social issues. 许多大公教会主义者认为基督教各教会应该携手解决社会问题。

例句：The ecumenists organized a joint service to demonstrate interfaith harmony. 大公教会主义者们组织了一场联合礼拜来展示不同信仰之间的和谐。


近义词： ecumenical advocates, interfaith activists |反义词：sectarian, denominationalist
