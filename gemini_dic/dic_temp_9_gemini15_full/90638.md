humanity:/hjuːˈmænɪti/|/ˈhjuːmænɪti/| n. | 人类；人性；仁慈 | The quality or state of being human.  It can refer to the entire human race, the characteristics that make humans unique, or the kindness and compassion shown towards others. |例句：The future of humanity is at stake. 人类的未来岌岌可危。

例句：She showed great humanity in helping the homeless. 她帮助无家可归的人，表现出极大的仁慈。|近义词：human race, humankind, mankind, benevolence, compassion |反义词：inhumanity, cruelty, barbarity |名词
