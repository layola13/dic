dramaturgies:/ˈdræməˌtɜːrdʒiz/| n.|戏剧理论与实践；戏剧学；编剧方法|The study and practice of dramatic composition and theatrical production, encompassing aspects of playwriting, directing, and performance.  It explores the theories and techniques used to create and interpret dramatic works.|例句：The professor's lecture focused on the various dramaturgies employed in Shakespeare's plays. 教授的讲座重点介绍了莎士比亚戏剧中采用的各种戏剧理论与实践。|例句：Her innovative dramaturgies challenged traditional notions of theatrical storytelling. 她创新的戏剧理论挑战了传统的戏剧叙事观念。|近义词：dramatic theory, playwriting theory, theatrical theory|反义词：N/A
