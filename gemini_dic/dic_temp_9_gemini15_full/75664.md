fulcra:/ˈfʌlkrə/ | n. |支点；支柱；中心点 | A fulcrum is a support on which a lever rests or is pivoted.  It's the point around which a lever rotates.  In a broader sense, it can refer to a central or essential point of support or reliance. | Example: The seesaw's fulcrum was worn down, making it unstable.  跷跷板的支点磨损了，使其不稳定。  The fulcrum of his argument was the statistical data. 他论证的中心点是统计数据。 | 近义词：pivot, support, base, cornerstone | 反义词： periphery, edge, outer limit |
