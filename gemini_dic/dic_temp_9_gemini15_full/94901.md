in front of: /ɪn frʌnt ɒv/ | prep. | 在...前面 |  Indicates location or position before something or someone.  It shows that something is situated ahead of another thing or person. |The car parked in front of the house.  这辆车停在房子前面。| behind, before | behind | preposition


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog sits in front of the fireplace. = 狗坐在壁炉前。
The books are in front of me. = 书在我的前面。


一般过去时:
He stood in front of the class. = 他站在教室前面。
The painting hung in front of the window. = 画挂在窗户前面。


现在完成时:
She has always sat in front of the television. = 她总是坐在电视机前。
They have placed the flowers in front of the statue. = 他们把花放在雕像前面。


过去完成时:
The car had stopped in front of the building. = 车停在了那栋楼的前面。
He had placed his notes in front of him. = 他把笔记放在了他面前。


将来时:
I will stand in front of you. = 我将站在你面前。
They will put the table in front of the sofa. = 他们会把桌子放在沙发前面。


虚拟语气:
If I were in front of him, I would tell him the truth. = 如果我站在他面前，我会告诉他真相。
I wish I were in front of the ocean right now. = 我真希望我现在就在海边。


被动语态:
The stage was set up in front of the audience. = 舞台被设置在观众面前。
The presentation was given in front of a large crowd. = 演示文稿在很多人面前进行了。


疑问句:
Is the chair in front of the desk? = 椅子在桌子前面吗？
What is in front of the building? = 建筑物前面是什么？


否定句:
The car isn't in front of the garage. = 车不在车库前面。
He didn't stand in front of her. = 他没有站在她面前。
