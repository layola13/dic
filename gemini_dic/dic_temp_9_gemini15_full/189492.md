sakti:/ˈʃʌkti/| n.| Shakti (力量，能力)|Shakti is a Sanskrit word that refers to divine feminine energy, power, or force.  It represents the dynamic energy of the universe and is often associated with the goddess Adi Shakti. In some contexts, it can also refer to personal power or capability.  |例句：The goddess Shakti is revered for her immense power.  女神萨克蒂因其巨大的力量而受到尊敬。|近义词：power, energy, strength, might|反义词：weakness, impotence, feebleness|名词
