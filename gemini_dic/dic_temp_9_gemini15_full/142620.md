osteoporoses:/ˌɒstiːoʊpəˈroʊsɪs/| n. |骨质疏松症|A disease characterized by low bone mass and microarchitectural deterioration of bone tissue, leading to enhanced bone fragility and a consequent increase in fracture risk.|例句：Osteoporosis is a serious condition that affects millions of people worldwide. 骨质疏松症是一种严重影响全世界数百万人的疾病。|近义词：osteopenia (骨质减少)|反义词：bone density (骨密度)
