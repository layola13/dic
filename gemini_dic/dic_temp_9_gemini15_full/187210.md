rob:/rɒb/| /rɑːb/| vt.|抢劫；盗窃；剥夺|To take property unlawfully from a person or place by force or threat of force.|例句：The robbers robbed the bank and escaped with a large sum of money. 抢劫犯抢劫了银行，带着一大笔钱逃跑了。|例句：They robbed him of his dignity.  他们剥夺了他的尊严。|近义词：steal, plunder, burglarize|反义词：give, donate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He robs banks. = 他抢劫银行。
She robs people of their hope. = 她剥夺了人们的希望。

一般过去时:
They robbed the store last night. = 他们昨晚抢劫了商店。
The thief robbed me of my wallet. = 小偷偷走了我的钱包。

现在完成时:
The thieves have robbed several houses in the neighborhood. = 窃贼已经抢劫了附近几户人家。
He has been robbed of his inheritance. = 他被剥夺了继承权。

过去完成时:
They had robbed the museum before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们已经抢劫了博物馆。
She had been robbed of her chance to win. = 她已经被剥夺了获胜的机会。

将来时:
They will rob the train tomorrow. = 他们明天将抢劫火车。
He will be robbed of his freedom. = 他将被剥夺自由。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't rob a bank. = 如果我是你，我不会去抢银行。
I wish they hadn't robbed me. = 我希望他们没有抢劫我。

被动语态:
The bank was robbed last night. = 银行昨晚被抢劫了。
He was robbed of his watch. = 他的手表被抢走了。

疑问句:
Was the house robbed? = 房子被抢劫了吗？
Were they robbed at gunpoint? = 他们是在枪口下被抢劫的吗？

否定句:
He didn't rob the bank. = 他没有抢劫银行。
They were not robbed. = 他们没有被抢劫。


PHRASE->
rob a bank = 抢劫银行
rob someone blind = 彻底洗劫某人
rob someone of something = 剥夺某人某物
be robbed of something = 被剥夺某物
rob Peter to pay Paul = 拆东墙补西墙
