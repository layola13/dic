prerecognizes:/ˌpriːˈrɛkəɡnaɪzɪz/| vt.|预先识别，预先认知|To recognize or identify something beforehand or in advance.  It implies a prior awareness or anticipation.|例句：The system prerecognizes potential threats and automatically blocks them.  系统预先识别潜在威胁并自动阻止它们。

例句：The experienced doctor prerecognized the symptoms of the rare disease. 经验丰富的医生预先识别出了这种罕见病的症状。


近义词：anticipate, foresee, prefigure, predict


反义词：misinterpret, overlook, misunderstand


词性:动词
