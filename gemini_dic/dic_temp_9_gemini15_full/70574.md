filleter:/fɪˈletər/ | vt. | 去骨；切成片 | To remove the bones from (meat, fish, or poultry).  |例句：The butcher filleted the salmon expertly.  屠夫熟练地将鲑鱼去骨。|例句：She filleted the chicken breast before cooking it.  她在烹饪鸡胸肉之前先将其去骨。|近义词：bone, debone | 反义词：bone (in the sense of adding bones)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fillets the fish every morning. = 他每天早上都去鱼骨。
She fillets the chicken breasts for dinner. = 她为晚餐去鸡胸肉的骨头。

一般过去时:
The chef filleted the tuna yesterday. = 厨师昨天去掉了金枪鱼的骨头。
I filleted the salmon for the party. = 我为派对去掉了鲑鱼的骨头。

现在完成时:
He has filleted hundreds of fish this week. = 他本周已经去掉了数百条鱼的骨头。
She has already filleted the cod for tonight's meal. = 她已经为今晚的饭菜去掉了鳕鱼的骨头。

过去完成时:
He had filleted all the fish before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经去掉了所有鱼的骨头。
She had filleted the chicken before she seasoned it. = 她给鸡肉调味之前已经去掉了鸡骨。

将来时:
I will fillet the trout for tomorrow's lunch. = 我将为明天的午餐去鳟鱼的骨头。
They will fillet the salmon for the banquet. = 他们将为宴会去鲑鱼的骨头。

虚拟语气:
If I had a sharper knife, I would fillet the fish more easily. = 如果我有一把更锋利的刀，我会更容易地去鱼骨。
I wish I could fillet the fish as expertly as he does. = 我希望我能像他一样熟练地去鱼骨。

被动语态:
The fish was filleted by the experienced chef. = 这条鱼是由经验丰富的厨师去骨的。
The chicken breasts are being filleted for the sandwiches. = 鸡胸肉正在被去骨，用于制作三明治。

疑问句:
Do you know how to fillet a fish? = 你知道怎么去鱼骨吗？
Have they filleted all the salmon yet? = 他们已经把所有的鲑鱼都去骨了吗？

否定句:
I cannot fillet this fish; the bones are too small. = 我不能去这条鱼的骨头，骨头太小了。
He didn't fillet the meat properly; there are still bones in it. = 他没有正确地去肉骨，里面还有骨头。
