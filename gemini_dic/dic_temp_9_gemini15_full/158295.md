players:/ˈpleɪərz/ | n. | 玩家；演员；参与者 | People who play a game or sport, or actors in a play.  | 例句：The players celebrated their victory.  球员们庆祝他们的胜利。

The star players are injured.  明星球员受伤了。


近义词：participants, performers, actors | 反义词：spectators, audience
