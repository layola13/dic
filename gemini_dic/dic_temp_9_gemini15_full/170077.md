protesters:/proʊˈtɛstərz/| n. |抗议者，示威者 |People who are expressing their opposition to something by protesting.  They often participate in demonstrations, marches, or rallies to make their voices heard and advocate for change. |例句：The protesters marched through the streets, chanting slogans against the new law. 抗议者们走上街头，高喊反对新法的口号。
The protesters occupied the government building for three days. 抗议者们占领了政府大楼三天。|近义词：demonstrators, activists, dissenters |反义词：supporters, conformists
