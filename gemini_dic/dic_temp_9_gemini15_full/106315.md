kobotics:/koʊˈbɑːtɪks/ | n. | 协作机器人技术；人机协作机器人学 | The study and application of robots that collaborate with humans in a shared workspace.  Kobotics focuses on designing robots that are safe, adaptable, and intuitive to work alongside people, enhancing human capabilities rather than replacing them.  | Example:  Advances in kobotics are leading to more collaborative robots in manufacturing and healthcare settings.  协作机器人技术方面的进步正在导致制造业和医疗保健领域出现更多协作机器人。 | 近义词：collaborative robotics, human-robot collaboration, human-robot interaction | 反义词：automation (in the sense of fully replacing human workers) | 名词
