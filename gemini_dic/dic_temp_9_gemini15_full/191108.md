scapegoated:/ˈskeɪpˌɡoʊtɪd/ | v. | 成为替罪羊；被指责 | To blame someone or something for the wrongdoings, mistakes, or faults of others.  |  The minority group was scapegoated for the country's economic problems. 少数民族被指责为该国经济问题的罪魁祸首。  |  blame, accuse, incriminate | exonerate, absolve, vindicate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They scapegoat the new employee for every mistake. = 他们把每一个错误都归咎于新员工。
The manager scapegoats his subordinates regularly. = 经理经常把责任推卸给他的下属。


一般过去时:
The company scapegoated the marketing team for the failed product launch. = 公司将失败的产品发布归咎于营销团队。
He was scapegoated for the accident, even though he wasn't at fault. = 他被指责为事故的责任人，即使他并无过错。


现在完成时:
They have scapegoated the immigrants for rising crime rates. = 他们一直将不断上升的犯罪率归咎于移民。
The government has scapegoated the media for its unpopular policies. = 政府一直将自己不受欢迎的政策归咎于媒体。


过去完成时:
The CEO had scapegoated the lower-level staff before taking responsibility. = 首席执行官在承担责任之前，已经将责任推卸给了下级员工。
She had been scapegoated many times before she finally spoke up. = 在她最终勇敢地说出来之前，她已经被多次当作替罪羊了。


将来时:
They will scapegoat the interns for the project delay. = 他们将把项目延误归咎于实习生。
The politician will likely scapegoat his opponents for the upcoming election. = 这位政治家很可能会将他即将到来的竞选失败归咎于他的对手。


虚拟语气:
If they had a scapegoat, they wouldn't have to take responsibility. = 如果他们有替罪羊，他们就不必承担责任了。
I wish they hadn't scapegoated me for their mistakes. = 我真希望他们没有因为他们的错误而把我当作替罪羊。


被动语态:
The junior staff were scapegoated for the project failure. = 项目失败的责任被推卸给了初级员工。
He was constantly scapegoated by his superiors. = 他经常被上司当作替罪羊。


疑问句:
Were they scapegoated for the incident? = 他们因为那件事而被当作替罪羊了吗？
Who is being scapegoated for this crisis? = 谁在为这场危机承担责任？


否定句:
They didn't scapegoat anyone for the loss. = 他们没有因为损失而责怪任何人。
The investigation showed that nobody was scapegoated unfairly. = 调查显示没有人被不公平地当作替罪羊。
