词源:
The word "effigiate" comes from the Late Latin word *effigiare*, meaning "to fashion," "to shape," or "to form an image."  This in turn is from the Latin *effigies*, meaning "likeness," "image," or "portrait."  The prefix *ef-* is an intensive prefix.
