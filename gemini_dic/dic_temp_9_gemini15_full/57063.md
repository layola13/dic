doubtous:/ˈdaʊtəs/| adj.| 可疑的；不确定的；值得怀疑的 |  Doubtful or questionable; causing or inspiring doubt.  The evidence presented was doubtous and inconclusive.  | 例句：The origins of the painting are doubtous. 这幅画的起源令人怀疑。 The future of the project is doubtous. 这个项目的未来令人担忧。| 近义词：dubious, questionable, suspicious, uncertain | 反义词：certain, definite, sure, reliable |
