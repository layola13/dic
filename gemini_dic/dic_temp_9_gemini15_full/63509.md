It involves systematically counting or listing items. |Example: The enumeration of the casualties was a grim task.  列举伤亡人数是一项令人沮丧的任务。  The teacher provided an enumeration of the key concepts. 老师列举了关键概念。|近义词：list, catalog, recital, inventory |反义词：omission, exclusion
