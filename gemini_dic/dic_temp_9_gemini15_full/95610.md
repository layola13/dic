incommunicative:/ˌɪn.kəˈmjuː.nɪkətɪv/| adj.|不善于沟通的；沉默寡言的|Not willing or able to communicate freely or easily.  A person who is incommunicative doesn't talk much and may seem unfriendly or distant.|例句：He became incommunicative after the accident. 事故后他变得沉默寡言。|例句：She was usually quite communicative, but today she was unusually incommunicative. 她通常很健谈，但今天却异常沉默。|近义词： taciturn, reticent, reserved, uncommunicative |反义词： communicative, talkative, garrulous
