dismantles:/dɪsˈmæntlz/| vt.|拆除；解散；使瓦解|To take apart something; to disassemble; to break down a system or organization.|例句：The old factory was dismantled and its parts sold for scrap.  旧工厂被拆除，零件被当作废料卖掉了。|例句：The government is dismantling the social welfare system. 政府正在瓦解社会福利制度。|近义词：demolish, disassemble, break down, destroy|反义词：assemble, construct, build, erect|动词
