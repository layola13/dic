cockleshells:/ˈkɒkəlʃɛlz/ | n. | 蛤蜊壳；小贝壳 | The shells of cockles, small edible bivalve mollusks.  Cockleshells are often found on beaches and are sometimes used in crafts or decoration. |例句：The children collected cockleshells on the beach.孩子们在海滩上收集贝壳。

The waves tossed the cockleshells onto the shore. 海浪把贝壳冲上了岸。


近义词：seashells, shells | 反义词：无


词性：名词


词源：来自古英语 *cocle,  意为“cockle”（一种蛤蜊）。  "-shell"是后缀，表示贝壳。
