posted:/ˈpoʊstɪd/| adj. v. |已张贴的；公布的；邮寄的|Past participle of "post," meaning to put up or publish something publicly, or to send something by mail.|例句：The announcement was posted on the company's website.  这个通知已发布在公司的网站上。|例句：The letter was posted yesterday. 这封信昨天寄出。|近义词：published, displayed, mailed, sent|反义词：removed, withdrawn, unpublished


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He posts updates regularly on his blog. = 他定期在他的博客上发布更新。
They post letters every week. = 他们每周都寄信。

一般过去时:
She posted the package last Friday. = 她上周五寄出了包裹。
He posted a picture of his vacation on Instagram. = 他在Instagram上发布了一张度假照片。


现在完成时:
I have posted the report to the forum. = 我已将报告发布到论坛。
They have posted all the flyers. = 他们已经张贴了所有传单。


过去完成时:
She had posted the letter before the deadline. = 她在截止日期前寄出了信。
He had posted several updates before the server crashed. = 服务器崩溃之前，他已经发布了好几个更新。


将来时:
I will post the results tomorrow. = 我明天将公布结果。
They will post the package next week. = 他们下周将寄出包裹。


虚拟语气:
If I had posted the article earlier, I would have received more comments. = 如果我早点发表文章，我会收到更多评论。
I wish I had posted the updates sooner. = 我希望我早点发布更新。


被动语态:
The announcement was posted on the notice board. = 公告张贴在布告栏上。
The package was posted yesterday. = 包裹昨天寄出。


疑问句:
Have you posted the letter yet? = 你寄信了吗？
Where was the notice posted? = 通知贴在哪里？


否定句:
I didn't post the comment on the forum. = 我没有在论坛上发表评论。
They haven't posted the package yet. = 他们还没有寄出包裹。
