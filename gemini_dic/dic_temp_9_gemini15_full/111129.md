literates:/ˈlɪtəreɪts/| n. |识字的人，有文化的人|People who can read and write.  Literates are those who possess the ability to read and write.|The program aims to increase the number of literates in rural areas.  该项目旨在增加农村地区的识字人数。| educated, learned, scholarly | illiterates, unlettered, uneducated | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Many people are literates. = 很多人都是识字的。
The number of literates is increasing. = 识字的人数正在增加。

一般过去时:
More people were literates than before. = 识字的人比以前多了。
He was a literate man. = 他是一个有文化的人。

现在完成时:
Many people have become literates. = 许多人已经成为识字的人。
The government has helped many people become literates. = 政府帮助许多人成为识字的人。

过去完成时:
Before the program, fewer people had been literates. = 在项目之前，识字的人更少。
They had been literates for many years. = 他们识字很多年了。


将来时:
More people will be literates in the future. = 未来将会有更多的人识字。
We will help more people become literates. = 我们将帮助更多的人成为识字的人。

否定句:

He wasn't a literate person before. = 他以前不是一个识字的人。
