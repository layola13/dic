geezerhood:/ˈɡiːzərhʊd/| n. |老年；老糊涂；老家伙的阶段|The state or period of being a geezer; old age, especially with its associated characteristics of eccentricity or grumpiness.  It often implies a certain amount of endearing quirkiness or wisdom associated with old age.|例句：He embraced his geezerhood with gusto, spending his days gardening and arguing politics with his neighbours. 他兴致勃勃地享受着他的老年生活，每天都在花园里侍弄花草，并和邻居们就政治问题争论不休。|例句：Her geezerhood didn't diminish her sharp wit and insightful observations.  她的老年并没有削弱她敏捷的才智和富有洞察力的见解。|近义词：old age, senescence, senior years|反义词：youth, adolescence, young adulthood
