seas:/siːz/| n.|海洋；海；众多；大量 |The plural of "sea". Refers to the interconnected body of salt water covering most of the earth's surface.  It can also refer to a large expanse of water, or figuratively, a vast quantity of something. |例句：The ship sailed across the seven seas. 这艘船横渡了七大洋。  The seas of grain stretched as far as the eye could see.  一望无际的麦田。|近义词：ocean, ocean, waters|反义词：land, earth


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sea is calm today. = 今天大海很平静。
The seas are rough during storms. = 暴风雨期间大海很汹涌。


一般过去时:
The sea was calm yesterday. = 昨天大海很平静。
The seas were rough last night. = 昨晚大海很汹涌。


现在完成时:
The sailors have crossed many seas. = 水手们已经横渡过许多海洋。
The storm has calmed the seas. = 暴风雨已经平息了大海。


过去完成时:
They had already crossed the seas before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前他们已经横渡了海洋。
The ship had been tossed by the seas for days. = 这艘船已经被大海颠簸了好几天。


将来时:
We will sail the seas next summer. = 我们明年夏天将航海。
The forecast predicts rough seas next week. = 预报说下周大海会很汹涌。


虚拟语气:
If the seas were calmer, we would go sailing. = 如果大海更平静，我们就去航海。
I wish the seas were less turbulent. = 我希望大海不那么汹涌。


被动语态:
The ship was damaged by the seas. = 这艘船被大海损坏。
The seas are often affected by the weather. = 大海经常受天气影响。


疑问句:
Are the seas calm enough for swimming? = 大海够平静可以游泳吗？
What color are the seas at sunset? = 日落时大海是什么颜色？


否定句:
The seas were not calm enough to sail. = 大海不够平静无法航行。
We did not see the seas during our trip. = 我们在旅途中没有看到大海。
