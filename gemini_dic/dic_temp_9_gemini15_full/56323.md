dogmatical:/dɒɡˈmætɪkəl/| adj.| 武断的；教条的；独断的 |  Characterized by or given to the expression of opinions very strongly or positively as if they were facts.  It implies an unwillingness to consider other viewpoints. |例句：His dogmatical pronouncements on the subject annoyed many of his colleagues.  他的关于这个主题的武断言论激怒了许多同事。

例句：She has a dogmatical approach to teaching, refusing to consider alternative methods. 她采用了一种武断的教学方法，拒绝考虑其他方法。


近义词：opinionated, assertive, doctrinaire, authoritarian |反义词：open-minded, tolerant, flexible, compromising


词性:形容词
