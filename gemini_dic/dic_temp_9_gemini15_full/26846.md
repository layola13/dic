cairn:/kern/| n.| 石堆；石冢；路标 | A pile of stones, typically one built on a mountaintop or other prominent location as a landmark, memorial, or burial marker.  Cairns can be naturally formed or constructed by humans. They serve various purposes, from marking trails to commemorating events or individuals. | Example: Hikers often add a stone to a cairn to mark their passage.  徒步旅行者经常在石堆上加一块石头来标记他们的足迹。|近义词：pile, heap, mound |反义词：None specifically, but could consider "flat ground" as a contrasting concept.| 名词
