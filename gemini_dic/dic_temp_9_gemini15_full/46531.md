USAGE->
This word is a proper noun and doesn't have typical verb conjugations like "what".  It's usage is primarily as a title.  Therefore, grammatical tenses don't apply in the same way.  Examples demonstrate its use in sentences.


Example 1: The Dalai Lama visited the United States.  达赖喇嘛访问了美国。

Example 2:  Many people admire the teachings of the Dalai Lama. 许多人钦佩达赖喇嘛的教诲。

Example 3:  I read a book about the life of the Dalai Lama. 我读了一本关于达赖喇嘛生平的书。
