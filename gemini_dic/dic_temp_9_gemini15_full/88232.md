hindlimbs:/ˈhaɪndˌlɪmz/| n.|后肢|The hindlimbs are the rear legs of a four-legged animal. They are used for locomotion, support, and other functions depending on the species.|例句：The kangaroo's powerful hindlimbs allow it to hop great distances. 袋鼠强壮的后肢使它能够跳跃很远的距离。
The dog injured its hindlimbs in the accident.  这只狗在事故中伤到了后肢。|近义词：rear legs, back legs|反义词：forelimbs
