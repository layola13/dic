camaraderie:/ˌkæməˈrɑːdəri/| n. |同志情谊；友情；团结精神 |A feeling of good friendship and trust among the members of a group.  It implies mutual respect, support, and a shared sense of purpose or identity. |例句：The team developed a strong camaraderie through their shared experiences and challenges.  团队通过共同的经历和挑战培养了强烈的同志情谊。

The soldiers shared a deep camaraderie forged in the heat of battle.  士兵们在激烈的战斗中建立了深厚的战友情谊。

近义词：comradeship, fellowship, solidarity, unity

反义词：antagonism, hostility, alienation, discord
