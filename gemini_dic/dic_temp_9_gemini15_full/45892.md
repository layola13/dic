cuticles:/ˈkjuːtɪkəlz/ | n. | 角质层，表皮 | The cuticles are the thin layer of skin at the base of the fingernails and toenails.  They protect the nail matrix, the area where the nail grows from.  |例句：She carefully pushed back her cuticles before applying nail polish. 她细心地推回指甲角质层后再涂指甲油。
She suffered from dry, cracked cuticles.  她的角质层干燥开裂。|近义词：eponychium |反义词：nail plate
