jeepers:/ˈdʒiːpərz/| int.|哎呀；我的天哪；|表示惊讶、恐惧或兴奋的感叹词，类似于"Goodness!" 或 "Wow!"。|Jeepers creepers, where'd you get that? 哎呀，你从哪儿弄到那个的？|近义词：Goodness!, Wow!, Gee!, Holy cow!|反义词：无


USAGE->
(Jeepers is an interjection, so it doesn't have verb tenses or grammatical structures like nouns and verbs.)


PHRASE->
Jeepers creepers:  (This is the most common phrase using "jeepers.")  This phrase intensifies the expression of surprise or astonishment.  哎呀;我的天哪
Jeepers! : A shorter, more casual version.  哎呀！
Oh jeepers!: Adds emphasis and a bit more emotion.  哦，我的天哪！
Jeepers, what a mess!:  Used to express exasperation or disbelief at a situation. 我的天啊，这多乱啊！
Well, jeepers!: Expresses surprise or disbelief in a more measured way.  哎，我的天哪！
