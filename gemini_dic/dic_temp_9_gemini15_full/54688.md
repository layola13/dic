disorderly: /dɪsˈɔːrdərli/ | adj. | 混乱的；无秩序的；不守规矩的 |  Lacking order or organization; unruly; uncontrolled.  | 例句：The children were running around in a disorderly manner. 孩子们无秩序地跑来跑去。

The room was in a disorderly state after the party. 派对后房间一片混乱。


近义词：chaotic, untidy, messy, unruly, turbulent | 反义词：orderly, organized, neat, tidy, calm


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The students are disorderly in class. 学生们在课堂上很吵闹。
His belongings are always disorderly. 他的东西总是乱七八糟的。
