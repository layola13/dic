ozonize:/ˈoʊzənaɪz/| vt.|臭氧化；用臭氧处理|To treat (something) with ozone.  The process often involves passing ozone gas through or over a substance to disinfect, deodorize, or oxidize it.|例句：The water was ozonized to remove impurities.  水经过臭氧处理以去除杂质。 They ozonized the air in the room to eliminate odors. 他们用臭氧处理房间的空气以消除异味。|近义词：sterilize, disinfect, purify|反义词：contaminate
