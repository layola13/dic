illiterate:/ɪˈlɪtərət/| adj.|文盲的；不识字的|Unable to read or write.  This term refers to someone lacking the ability to read and write, often due to a lack of education or opportunity.  It can also sometimes be used figuratively to describe someone lacking knowledge or understanding in a particular area.|例句：He is illiterate and cannot sign his name. 他不识字，不能签名。

例句：The program aims to help illiterate adults learn to read and write. 该项目旨在帮助文盲成年人学习阅读和写作。


近义词：unschooled, uneducated (in this context), unlettered

反义词：literate, educated


词性：形容词
