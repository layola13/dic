osteogenesis:/ˌɒstiəʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/| n. |成骨作用，骨生成|The formation of bone tissue.  It involves the differentiation of mesenchymal stem cells into osteoblasts, which then synthesize and deposit the organic components of bone matrix. This process is crucial for skeletal development, fracture healing, and bone remodeling.|例句：The process of osteogenesis is essential for bone growth and repair. 成骨作用对于骨骼生长和修复至关重要。|例句：Disruptions in osteogenesis can lead to skeletal abnormalities. 成骨作用的紊乱会导致骨骼异常。|近义词：bone formation, ossification|反义词：bone resorption, osteolysis|名词
