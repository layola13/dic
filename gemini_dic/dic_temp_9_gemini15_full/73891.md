forray:/fɔːreɪ/ | n. | 远征；袭击；尝试；探索 | A foray is a sudden attack or raid, often into enemy territory. It can also refer to a brief attempt or initial venture into a new field or activity. |例句：The knights launched a foray into enemy territory.  骑士们向敌方领土发起了突袭。

The company's foray into the renewable energy market was successful.  该公司进军可再生能源市场的尝试获得了成功。


近义词：raid, incursion, expedition, venture, attempt | 反义词：retreat, withdrawal


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The army makes a foray into the enemy's land. = 军队对敌人的土地进行了一次袭击。
She makes a foray into the world of painting. = 她尝试涉足绘画的世界。


一般过去时:
The Vikings made a foray into England. = 维京人袭击了英格兰。
He made a foray into writing poetry. = 他尝试写诗。


现在完成时:
They have made several forays into the unexplored jungle. = 他们已经对未开发的丛林进行了多次探险。
She has made a successful foray into the business world. = 她成功地进军了商界。


过去完成时:
Before the battle, they had made a daring foray into the enemy's camp. = 在战斗之前，他们曾大胆地突袭了敌人的营地。
Before starting his own business, he had made a foray into freelance writing. = 在创业之前，他曾尝试过自由撰稿。


将来时:
The explorers will make a foray into the Amazon rainforest. = 探险家们将对亚马逊雨林进行探险。
Next year, she will make a foray into the field of artificial intelligence. = 明年，她将进军人工智能领域。


虚拟语气:
If they had made a more successful foray, they would have won the war. = 如果他们进行了一次更成功的袭击，他们就会赢得战争。
If I were to make a foray into public speaking, I would be nervous. = 如果我要尝试公开演讲，我会很紧张。


被动语态:
A foray into the mountains was made by the explorers. = 探险家们对山区进行了探险。
A foray into the new market was planned by the company. = 公司计划进军新市场。


疑问句:
Will the army make a foray tonight? = 军队今晚会发起袭击吗？
Did the explorers make a successful foray? = 探险家们的探险成功了吗？


否定句:
The army did not make a foray that night. = 军队那天晚上没有发起袭击。
He did not make a foray into the competitive market. = 他没有进军竞争激烈的市场。
