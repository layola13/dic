cultivator: /ˈkʌltɪveɪtər/ | n. |耕耘者；耕作机；培养者 | A person or thing that cultivates; a machine for cultivating soil; someone who cultivates something (e.g., a skill, relationship).  |例句：He is a skilled cultivator of rare orchids. 他是一位熟练的珍稀兰花培育者。 |例句：The farmer used a cultivator to prepare the soil for planting. 农民用耕作机准备播种的土壤。|近义词：farmer, planter, grower, developer |反义词：neglecter, destroyer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer cultivates his land every spring. = 农民每年春天都耕种他的土地。
She cultivates a positive attitude towards life. = 她培养积极的人生态度。

一般过去时:
They cultivated a large field of wheat last year. = 他们去年种植了一大片小麦田。
He cultivated a friendship with his colleague. = 他与同事培养了友谊。

现在完成时:
We have cultivated a strong relationship with our customers. = 我们已经与客户建立了牢固的关系。
She has cultivated her skills in painting for many years. = 她多年来一直培养自己的绘画技巧。

过去完成时:
They had cultivated the land before the rain came. = 雨来之前他们已经耕种了土地。
She had cultivated her talent since childhood. = 她从小就培养自己的天赋。

将来时:
We will cultivate a new variety of rice next year. = 我们明年将种植新品种水稻。
He will cultivate his garden this weekend. = 他这个周末将耕种他的花园。

虚拟语气:
If I had cultivated my talents, I might have been more successful. = 如果我培养了自己的才能，我可能会更成功。
I wish I had cultivated better communication skills. = 我希望我培养了更好的沟通技巧。

被动语态:
The land was cultivated by the farmer. = 这块土地是由农民耕种的。

疑问句:

否定句:
