exacted:/ɪɡˈzæktɪd/| vt.|强行索取；榨取；迫使；精确地表达|to demand and obtain something from someone, especially by force or authority; to get something, especially by great effort. |例句：The government exacted heavy taxes from the people.  政府向人民征收重税。

例句：The soldiers exacted obedience from the prisoners. 士兵们强迫犯人服从。


近义词：demand, extract, extort, wring, enforce


反义词：give, relinquish, offer


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The landlord exacts high rent from his tenants. = 房东向他的租户收取高额租金。
They exact obedience from their employees. = 他们强迫员工服从。


一般过去时:
The king exacted a heavy tribute from the conquered provinces. = 国王向被征服的省份勒索了巨额贡赋。
The police exacted a confession from the suspect. = 警察从嫌疑人那里逼供。


现在完成时:
The company has exacted a heavy toll on the environment. = 这家公司给环境造成了巨大的破坏。
The drought has exacted a high price from farmers. = 干旱给农民带来了巨大的损失。


过去完成时:
The dictator had exacted absolute loyalty from his followers. = 独裁者曾强迫他的追随者绝对效忠。
The invaders had exacted revenge on the defeated army. = 侵略者向被打败的军队报复了。


将来时:
The new law will exact a higher price from polluters. = 新法律将对污染者处以更高的代价。
The government will exact compliance with the new regulations. = 政府将强制执行新的规章制度。


虚拟语气:
If the government exacted less taxes, the people might be happier. = 如果政府少征税，人民可能会更快乐。
If they had exacted a higher price, they would have made more profit. = 如果他们要价更高，他们就会赚更多钱。


被动语态:
Heavy taxes were exacted from the citizens. =  公民们被征收了重税。
A confession was exacted from the prisoner. = 囚犯被逼供了。


疑问句:

否定句:
The government did not exact any further taxes this year. = 政府今年没有再征收任何税款。
The police could not exact a confession from the suspect. = 警察无法从嫌疑人那里逼供。
