heavier:/ˈhɛviər/| adj.| 更重的，更沉重的 | More weighty; greater in weight.  It describes something that has more weight than something else. |例句：This box is heavier than that one. 这个箱子比那个箱子重。

例句：The heavier suitcase was difficult to lift.  更重的行李箱很难提起。


近义词：weightier, heavier in weight, more substantial


反义词：lighter, less weighty


词性：形容词
