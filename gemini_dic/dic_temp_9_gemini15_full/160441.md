popularization: /ˌpɑːpjʊləraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |普及；推广；大众化 |The process of making something popular or widely known, especially an idea, product, or technology.  The act of making something accessible and understandable to a wider audience. |例句：The popularization of the internet has revolutionized communication.  互联网的普及彻底改变了沟通方式。

The popularization of science is crucial for public understanding. 科学的普及对于公众理解至关重要。

|近义词：dissemination, spread, promotion, simplification |反义词：obscuration, suppression, confinement, specialization |词性：名词
