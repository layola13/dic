place's:/pleɪsɪz/ | n. | 地方的；场所的 |  The possessive form of "place," indicating belonging or association with a place. It's often used to describe something related to or characteristic of a specific location. | 例句：The place's atmosphere was enchanting.  = 这个地方的气氛很迷人。

The museum's exhibits showcased the place's history. = 博物馆的展品展示了这个地方的历史。| 近义词：location's, area's, spot's | 反义词：None (possessive forms don't have direct opposites) | 名词
