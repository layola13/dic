leonidas:/liˈɒnɪdəs/| n. |列奥尼达斯|Leonidas I was a king of Sparta who famously led 300 Spartan soldiers against the massive Persian army at the Battle of Thermopylae.  He is remembered for his courage and unwavering defense against overwhelming odds, even in the face of certain death. His story has become a symbol of bravery and self-sacrifice.|例：The movie "300" depicts the heroic last stand of Leonidas and his men at Thermopylae.  电影《300勇士》描绘了列奥尼达斯及其部下在温泉关进行的英勇抵抗。|近义词：hero, warrior, champion |反义词：coward, traitor
