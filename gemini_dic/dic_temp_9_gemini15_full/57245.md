downlighter:/ˈdaʊnˌlaɪtər/| n. |筒灯，射灯 |A downlighter is a recessed lighting fixture that directs light downwards.  It is typically installed in ceilings and is used to provide general or task lighting. |Example:  The kitchen was beautifully lit by a series of downlighters. 厨房里一系列筒灯照得非常漂亮。|近义词：recessed light, spotlight (in some contexts)|反义词：uplighter
