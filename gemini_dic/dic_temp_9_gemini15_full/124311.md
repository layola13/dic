mothering-sundays: /ˈmʌðərɪŋ ˈsʌndeɪz/ | n. | 母亲节 | Mothering Sunday is a traditional Christian holiday celebrated in the UK and some other countries, falling on the fourth Sunday of Lent.  It's a day for families to come together, often involving visits to one's mother.  Historically, it was a day for servants to visit their mothers and for people to visit their "mother church".  The name refers to the act of "mothering", or showing care and affection. | Example:  We always have a special family dinner on Mothering Sunday.  我们总是在母亲节举行特别的家庭晚餐。  She visited her mother in the countryside for Mothering Sunday. 她母亲节去乡下看望母亲。| 近义词: Mother's Day (in the US) | 反义词: N/A
