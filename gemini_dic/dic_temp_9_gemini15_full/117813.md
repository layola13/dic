melissic:/məˈlɪsɪk/| adj. |蜜蜂的；蜜蜡的| Relating to or derived from bees or beeswax.  The term often refers to the chemical components found in beeswax or bee products. |The melissic acid content of the beeswax determined its quality.  蜜蜡中蜜蜂酸的含量决定了它的质量。|  近义词：apicultural (relating to beekeeping), cerous (relating to wax)|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be related to non-bee products or synthetic materials.
