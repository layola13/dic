co-operator:/koʊˈɒpəreɪtər/ | n. |合作者，协作者 |A person who works together with others.  Someone who willingly participates in a common effort. |例句：He was a valuable co-operator in the project. 他是这个项目中宝贵的合作者。|例句：The success of the program depends on the co-operation of all co-operators.  这个项目的成功取决于所有合作者的合作。|近义词：collaborator, partner, associate |反义词：obstructor, opponent, saboteur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The co-operator helps with the project. = 合作者帮助这个项目。
She co-operates with her team effectively. = 她有效地与她的团队合作。


一般过去时:
