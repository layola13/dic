legislating:/ˈlɛdʒɪsleɪtɪŋ/ | v. |立法；制定法律 | The act of making or enacting laws.  It involves the process of creating, debating, amending, and passing legislation. | Example:  The government is legislating to control pollution.  政府正在立法控制污染。|近义词：lawmaking, enacting, framing | 反义词：repealing, abolishing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are legislating new environmental regulations. = 他们正在制定新的环境法规。
Congress legislates on various issues annually. = 国会在每年就各种问题进行立法。


一般过去时:
The parliament legislated a new tax law last year. = 议会去年制定了一项新的税法。
They legislated against discrimination. = 他们制定了反对歧视的法律。


现在完成时:
The government has legislated several important reforms. = 政府已经制定了几项重要的改革法案。
They have legislated to improve public safety. = 他们已经立法以改善公共安全。


过去完成时:
They had legislated against child labor before the 20th century. = 他们在20世纪之前就制定了反对童工的法律。
The country had legislated similar measures in the past. = 该国过去曾制定过类似措施。


将来时:
The legislature will legislate on healthcare next year. = 立法机关明年将就医疗保健问题进行立法。
They will legislate to address the economic crisis. = 他们将立法解决经济危机。


虚拟语气:
If they legislated more effectively, we would see better outcomes. = 如果他们更有效地立法，我们将看到更好的结果。
It is vital that they legislate to protect endangered species. = 他们立法保护濒危物种至关重要。


被动语态:
New laws are being legislated to improve infrastructure. = 正在制定新的法律来改善基础设施。
A new tax bill was being legislated at the time. = 当时正在制定一项新的税收法案。


疑问句:
Is the government legislating on climate change? = 政府是否正在就气候变化问题进行立法？
What issues is the parliament legislating on currently? = 议会目前正在就哪些问题进行立法？


否定句:
The government is not legislating on that issue at present. = 政府目前没有就该问题进行立法。
They did not legislate against monopolies effectively. = 他们没有有效地制定反对垄断的法律。


PHRASE->
legislating against: 制定反对…的法律
legislating for:  为…立法
legislating on: 就…立法
legislating to: 为…而立法
legislating with: 与…一起立法


词源:
From Middle English *legislatinge, from Old French *legislat, from Latin *legislāt- (stem of legislāre), from lex, legis ("law") + ferre ("to carry, bring").  The word traces back to the Roman practice of bringing laws before the people.
