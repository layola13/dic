dastard:/ˈdæstərd/| n. adj.|懦夫；胆小鬼；卑鄙的人|A dastard is a person who is cowardly and despicable, especially one who commits a cowardly and despicable act.  The word often implies a sense of moral turpitude beyond mere cowardice.|例句：He was branded a dastard for betraying his friends. 他被斥为背叛朋友的懦夫。

例句：The dastardly act of arson shocked the community.  纵火这一卑劣行径震惊了整个社区。|近义词：coward, poltroon, craven, villain|反义词：hero, brave, courageous|词性:noun, adjective
