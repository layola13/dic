retouched:/rɪˈtʌtʃt/| v. |润色；修饰；修改；重新着色| To improve a photograph, painting, etc. by making small changes or additions.  It often implies enhancing the image, usually digitally, to improve its appearance.|例句：The photographer retouched the image to remove blemishes. 摄影师润色了图像以去除瑕疵。

The artist retouched the painting to correct a mistake. 艺术家润色了画作以纠正一个错误。


近义词：edited, improved, enhanced, amended, refined|反义词：damaged, ruined, worsened, deteriorated|动词
