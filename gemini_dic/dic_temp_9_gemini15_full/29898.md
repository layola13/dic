cauterized:/ˈkɔːtəraɪzd/ | vt. | 烧灼；止血 | To burn the surface of (a wound) with a hot instrument to stop bleeding or prevent infection.  | 例句：The surgeon cauterized the wound to stop the bleeding. 外科医生用烧灼法止住了伤口出血。 | 例句：The doctor cauterized the cut to prevent infection. 医生烧灼伤口以防止感染。 | 近义词： sear, burn, sear, cauterise | 反义词：heal
