epigeous:/ɪˈpɪdʒiəs/| adj.|地上生的；地上发的；出土的 |Above or on the ground; growing above the ground.  Epigeous germination refers to the type of seed germination where the cotyledons emerge above the ground.  |例句：The seeds of this plant exhibit epigeous germination, with the cotyledons becoming photosynthetic.  这种植物的种子表现出地上发芽，子叶会进行光合作用。|例句：Many beans have epigeous germination.  许多豆类都是地上发芽的。|近义词：aboveground, emergent|反义词：hypogeous
