count's: /kaʊnts/ | n. |伯爵的；计数的 |The possessive form of "count," indicating belonging to a count (a nobleman).  It can also refer to the plural of "count" (an act of counting).|例句：The count's castle was magnificent. = 伯爵的城堡非常壮丽。 The count's are accurate. = 计数是准确的。|近义词：earl's (if referring to a nobleman), tally's|反义词： N/A
