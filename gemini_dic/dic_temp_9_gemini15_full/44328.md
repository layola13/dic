/krɒpt/| /krɑːpt/| v. n.| 收获；剪短；突然出现；|To harvest or gather a crop; to shorten something by cutting; to appear suddenly.|例句：The farmers cropped the wheat in the autumn. 秋天农民收割了小麦。  The editor cropped the photo to remove unnecessary details. 编辑裁剪照片以去除不必要的细节。|近义词：harvest, gather, cut, trim|反义词：grow, lengthen, expand|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They crop the fields every year. = 他们每年收割农田。
He crops his hair short. = 他把头发剪短。


一般过去时:
The photographer cropped the image. = 摄影师裁剪了图像。
She cropped the flowers from her garden. = 她从花园里收割了鲜花。


现在完成时:
Farmers have cropped the rice paddies. = 农民们已经收割了稻田。
He has cropped the edges of the picture. = 他已经裁剪了图片的边缘。


过去完成时:
They had cropped the wheat before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经收割了小麦。
She had cropped the photograph before printing it. = 她打印照片之前已经裁剪过了。


将来时:
We will crop the tomatoes next week. = 我们下周将收割西红柿。
They will crop the image to fit the frame. = 他们将裁剪图像以适合框架。


虚拟语气:
If I had cropped the photo differently, it would look better. = 如果我用不同的方式裁剪照片，它看起来会更好。
I wish I had cropped the video before uploading it. = 我真希望我在上传之前剪辑了视频。


被动语态:
The image was cropped to fit the magazine layout. = 图像被裁剪以适合杂志版面。
The excess fabric was cropped away. = 多余的布料被剪掉了。


疑问句:
Have you cropped the pictures yet? = 你已经裁剪图片了吗？
Will they crop the fields this week? = 他们本周会收割农田吗？


否定句:
I didn't crop the image. = 我没有裁剪图像。
They haven't cropped the sunflowers yet. = 他们还没有收割向日葵。
