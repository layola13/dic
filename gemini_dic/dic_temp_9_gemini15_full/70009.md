fetid:/ˈfɛtɪd/| adj.|恶臭的，腐臭的|Having a strong, unpleasant smell; foul-smelling.  The fetid air hung heavy in the room, making it difficult to breathe.|近义词：foul, rank, putrid, malodorous, noisome|反义词：fragrant, sweet-smelling, aromatic


Etymology:

From Middle English *fetid, from Old French fetide, from Latin foetidus "ill-smelling," from foedus "foul, putrid, offensive."
