quadrupler:/kwɑːˈdruːplər/| v. |使四倍；四倍于|To make something four times as large or as much.  It can also refer to something that is four times as large or as much.|The company quadrupled its profits this year.  今年公司利润翻了四番。|The population of the city has quadrupled in the last fifty years.  这个城市的人口在过去五十年里增长了四倍。|multiply, increase fourfold|halve, decrease, diminish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He quadruples his efforts to meet the deadline. = 他加倍努力以赶上截止日期。(This example uses "quadruples" figuratively, implying a significant increase)
The machine quadruples the input signal. = 这台机器将输入信号放大四倍。


一般过去时:
The investment quadrupled in value. = 这项投资增值了四倍。
The company quadrupled its production last quarter. = 公司上个季度的产量翻了四番。


现在完成时:
The population has quadrupled since the war. = 自战争以来，人口增长了四倍。
My savings have quadrupled in the last ten years. = 我的积蓄在过去十年里翻了四倍。


过去完成时:
The value of the property had quadrupled before they sold it. = 他们在出售房产之前，房产价值已经翻了四番。
The number of students had quadrupled by the time the new building was opened. = 等新楼启用时，学生人数已经翻了四倍。


将来时:
They predict that the price will quadruple next year. = 他们预测价格明年将上涨四倍。
The company expects its profits to quadruple within the next decade. = 公司预计其利润将在未来十年内翻四番。


虚拟语气:
If the investment had quadrupled, we would have retired early. = 如果投资翻了四番，我们就会提前退休。
I wish my income had quadrupled last year. = 我希望我的收入去年翻了四番。


被动语态:
The data was quadrupled by the processing algorithm. = 数据被处理算法扩大了四倍。
The image was quadrupled in size by the software. = 图片大小被软件扩大了四倍。


疑问句:
Has the number of participants quadrupled? = 参加人数翻了四番了吗？
Will the production costs quadruple? = 生产成本会翻四番吗？


否定句:
The price didn't quadruple as expected. = 价格并没有像预期的那样翻四番。
The company's profits did not quadruple this year. = 公司今年的利润并没有翻四番。
