lover:/ˈlʌvər/| n.|爱人，情人；爱好者 | A person who has a romantic relationship with someone.  |例句：He is my lover. 他是我的爱人。|例句：She's a lover of classical music. 她是一位古典音乐爱好者。|近义词：sweetheart, darling, partner, aficionado |反义词：enemy, hater


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He loves her deeply. = 他深深地爱着她。
They are lovers. = 他们是恋人。


一般过去时:
They were lovers in college. = 他们在大学时是恋人。
He loved her passionately. = 他热情地爱着她。


现在完成时:
I have loved you since I first met you. = 从我第一次见到你我就爱上你了。
They have been lovers for five years. = 他们已经恋爱五年了。


过去完成时:
He had loved her for many years before they got married. = 在他们结婚之前，他已经爱了她很多年了。
She had been his lover before he met his wife. = 在他遇到他妻子之前，她是他的情人。


将来时:
I will always love you. = 我会永远爱你。
They will be lovers for a long time, I think. = 我想他们会做很长时间的情侣。


虚拟语气:
If I were your lover, I would protect you. = 如果我是你的爱人，我会保护你。
I wish I had a lover. = 我希望我有一个爱人。


被动语态:
(This word is rarely used in the passive voice in this context.)
She was loved by many men. = 她被许多男人爱着。(This emphasizes being loved, not the romantic relationship.)


疑问句:
Is he your lover? = 他是你的爱人吗？
Are they still lovers? = 他们还是恋人吗？


否定句:
He is not my lover. = 他不是我的爱人。
They were never lovers. = 他们从来都不是恋人。
