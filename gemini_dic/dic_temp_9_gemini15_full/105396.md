kiddle:/ˈkɪdl/ | v. | 欺骗，哄骗；轻柔地抚摸 | To deceive or trick someone, often in a gentle or playful way; to gently stroke or caress. | 例句：He kiddled the children into believing his story. 他哄骗孩子们相信他的故事。

He kiddled the kitten gently. 他轻轻地抚摸着小猫。


近义词：deceive, trick, coax, caress, fondle | 反义词：be honest, be truthful


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kiddles the children with his stories. = 他用他的故事哄骗孩子们。
She kiddles the dog's fur. = 她抚摸着狗的毛。


一般过去时:
He kiddled me into buying that car. = 他哄骗我买了那辆车。
She kiddled the baby to sleep. = 她轻轻地哄着婴儿睡觉。


现在完成时:
He has kiddled his way into her heart. = 他哄骗获得了她的芳心。

过去完成时:

She had kiddled the puppy until it fell asleep. = 她一直抚摸小狗直到它睡着。
