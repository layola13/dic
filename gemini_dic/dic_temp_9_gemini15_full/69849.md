ferrite:/ˈfɛraɪt/| n. |铁氧体|A ceramic material that is magnetic and is used in various electronic components. Ferrite is a compound of iron oxide and one or more other metallic elements. It's known for its high permeability and low eddy current losses.|Example:  Many transformers use ferrite cores to improve efficiency.  许多变压器使用铁氧体磁芯以提高效率。|近义词：magnetic ceramic, ferromagnetic material|反义词：diamagnetic material
