furnisher:/ˈfɜːrnɪʃər/| n. |家具供应商，装潢者|A person or company that supplies or provides furniture.  They may also provide other furnishings for homes or businesses, such as carpets, curtains, and lighting.| Example:  The furnisher delivered the new sofa on time.  家具供应商准时送来了新的沙发。|近义词:supplier, provider, decorator|反义词:customer, buyer


USAGE->

The furnisher supplies high-quality furniture. = 家具供应商供应高质量的家具。
He furnishes his apartment with antique pieces. = 他用古董家具装饰他的公寓。
