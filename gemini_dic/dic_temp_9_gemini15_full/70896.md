fipple:/ˈfɪpəl/| n. |吹口,吹嘴|A fipple is a type of mouthpiece used in some wind instruments, such as recorders and flutes, that directs the airflow to create sound. It is characterized by its usually flat, narrow, and often slightly curved shape, which channels the air across a sharp edge to produce the sound.  |Example: The recorder's fipple needs to be cleaned regularly to ensure optimal performance.  吹笛的吹口需要定期清洁以确保最佳性能。|近义词：mouthpiece, windway|反义词:None
