pronotary:/ˈproʊnəˌtɛri/| n. |书记员；记录员；(某些鸟类的)莺类|A pronotary is a person who keeps records or minutes of proceedings, especially in a court of law or other official body.  The term also refers to a type of bird, specifically a type of warbler.|例句：The court pronotary meticulously recorded every word spoken during the trial. 法庭书记员一丝不苟地记录了审讯中说的每一句话。

The pronotary warbler is known for its bright yellow plumage.  莺类以其鲜艳的黄色羽毛而闻名。|近义词：scribe, clerk, registrar, notary |反义词：N/A
