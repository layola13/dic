mushrooming:/ˈmʌʃruːmɪŋ/| v.|迅速发展；激增；|  The rapid growth or spread of something, like a mushroom.  |例句：The city's population is mushrooming.  这个城市的居民人口正在迅速增长。
例句：  Interest in online learning is mushrooming. 对在线学习的兴趣正在激增。|近义词：exploding, booming, burgeoning|反义词：declining, dwindling, shrinking|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The number of startups is mushrooming. = 初创企业的数量正在激增。
Online shopping is mushrooming rapidly. = 在线购物正在迅速发展。

一般过去时:
The company's profits mushroomed last year. = 该公司的利润去年激增。
The popularity of that song mushroomed after the music video was released. = 那首歌的流行度在音乐视频发布后激增。

现在完成时:
The use of smartphones has mushroomed in recent years. = 近年来智能手机的使用激增。
The city has mushroomed into a sprawling metropolis. = 这个城市已经发展成为一个庞大的都市。

过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, the crowd had mushroomed. = 当我到达时，人群已经变得非常庞大。
Before the announcement, the rumors had mushroomed. = 在公告之前，谣言已经四起。

将来时:
The demand for renewable energy is expected to mushroom in the coming decades. = 未来几十年，对可再生能源的需求预计将激增。
We anticipate that the project will mushroom in scope and complexity. = 我们预计该项目的范围和复杂性将会扩大。

虚拟语气:
If the economy improves, the business might mushroom. = 如果经济好转，企业可能会迅速发展。
If they had invested earlier, their business would have mushroomed. = 如果他们早些投资，他们的企业早就发展壮大了。

被动语态:
The problem was mushroomed by the lack of communication. = 由于缺乏沟通，问题变得越来越严重。
The company's reputation was mushroomed by the scandal. = 该公司的声誉因丑闻而受损。

疑问句:
Is the technology mushrooming too rapidly for us to adapt? =  这项技术发展得太快以至于我们无法适应吗？
Are the costs mushrooming beyond our control? = 成本是否正在失控地增长？

否定句:
The project did not mushroom as quickly as we had hoped. = 该项目并没有像我们希望的那样快速发展。
The rumors did not mushroom into a full-blown crisis. = 谣言并没有演变成一场全面的危机。
