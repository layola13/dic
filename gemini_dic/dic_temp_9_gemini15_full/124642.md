mouthier:/ˈmaʊθiər/| adj.|爱说话的；夸夸其谈的；喋喋不休的|More talkative or given to outspokenness than usual; tending to speak freely or excessively.|The children were getting mouthier as the day went on.  孩子们随着一天的过去变得越来越爱说话了。|He became mouthier after a few drinks. 他喝了几杯酒后变得更爱夸夸其谈了。|loquacious, voluble, garrulous| taciturn, reticent, reserved
