for good: /fɔːr ɡʊd/ | adv. |永久地，永远地 |permanently; forever|例句：He left his hometown for good. 他永远离开了他的家乡。|例句：She decided to quit smoking for good. 她决定永远戒烟。|近义词：permanently, forever, eternally |反义词：temporarily, briefly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He works for good. = 他为善事工作。
They strive for good grades. = 他们努力争取好成绩。


一般过去时:
She worked for good. = 她为善事工作过。
He gave up smoking for good. = 他彻底戒烟了。


现在完成时:
I have been a vegetarian for good. = 我已经永久成为素食者了。
She has quit her job for good. = 她已经永远辞去了工作。


过去完成时:
He had left the company for good. = 他已经永远离开了那家公司。
They had decided to move for good. = 他们已经决定永远搬家了。


将来时:
I will live here for good. = 我将永远住在这里。
She will leave the country for good. = 她将永远离开这个国家。


虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would leave for good. = 如果我中了彩票，我会永远离开。
I wish I could stay here for good. = 我希望我能永远待在这里。


被动语态:
The decision was made for good. = 决定已经永远做出了。
The agreement was broken for good. = 协议已经永远破裂了。


疑问句:
Is this the end for good? = 这是最终结束了吗？
Will they stay for good? = 他们会永远留下吗？


否定句:
He didn't leave for good.  = 他并没有永远离开。
She didn't give up hope for good. = 她并没有永远放弃希望。


PHRASE->
for good and all = 永远地
once and for all = 一劳永逸地
for the good of = 为了…的利益
good for nothing = 一无是处
for the greater good = 为了更大的利益
