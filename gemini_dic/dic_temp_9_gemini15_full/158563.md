pleurae:/ˈplʊəriːz/| n.|胸膜(复数)|The pleurae are serous membranes that line the chest cavity and cover the lungs. They reduce friction during breathing.|例句：The pleurae secrete a lubricating fluid that allows the lungs to expand and contract smoothly. 胸膜分泌润滑液，使肺部能够平滑地扩张和收缩。|例句：Inflammation of the pleurae, known as pleurisy, can cause sharp chest pain. 胸膜炎，即胸膜炎症，会导致剧烈胸痛。|近义词：pleural membranes|反义词：无


USAGE->  (由于pleurae是名词，故不适用各种时态变化，以下列举一些例句展现其在不同句式中的用法)


单数形式(pleura):
肯定句:The visceral pleura covers the lungs.  脏层胸膜覆盖肺部。
否定句:The parietal pleura is not directly attached to the lungs.壁层胸膜并没有直接附着在肺部。
疑问句:Is the pleural cavity filled with fluid? 胸膜腔是否充满了液体？

复数形式(pleurae):
肯定句:The pleurae are delicate membranes. 胸膜是纤薄的膜。
否定句:The pleurae are not easily visible during a routine physical exam. 在常规体检中，胸膜不容易看到。
疑问句:Are both pleurae inflamed in this case? 在这种情况下，两侧胸膜都发炎了吗？
