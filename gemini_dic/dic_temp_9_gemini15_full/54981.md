disproportions:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːʃənz/| n.|不成比例，失调|The state or fact of being out of proportion; lack of balance or symmetry.  It refers to a significant difference in size, amount, or degree between things that should be similar or related.|例句：The disproportions in wealth between the rich and poor are shocking.  贫富之间的财富差距令人震惊。

例句：The disproportions in representation of different ethnic groups in the media are a matter of concern. 媒体对不同种族群体的代表性失衡令人担忧。


近义词：imbalance, disparity, disproportionality, inequity

反义词：proportionality, balance, symmetry

词性: noun
