nitrometer:/naɪˈtrɒmɪtər/ | n. |硝酸盐测定仪|An instrument for measuring the amount of nitrates or nitrogen oxides in a substance, often used in environmental monitoring and agricultural analysis.  It typically involves chemical reactions to determine the nitrogen content. |Example: The researchers used a nitrometer to analyze the nitrate levels in the soil samples.  研究人员使用硝酸盐测定仪分析土壤样品中的硝酸盐含量。|近义词：nitrogen oxide meter, nitrate analyzer |反义词：无
