chuckler:/ˈtʃʌklər/| n.|轻声笑的人，窃笑的人|A chuckler is a person who chuckles frequently or easily.  They often find humor in situations others might not. |例句：He was a quiet chuckler, often found smiling to himself. 他是个轻声笑的人，经常独自微笑。  The comedian had the audience chuckling; even the serious-faced chuckler in the front row cracked a smile.|近义词：giggler, sniggler|反义词：crybaby, grump


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chuckles at every joke. = 他对每个笑话都轻声笑。
She chuckles softly to herself. = 她轻声自语地笑着。

一般过去时:
