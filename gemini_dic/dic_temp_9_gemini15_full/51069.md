despaired:/dɪsˈpeəd/| v. |绝望，丧失希望|To lose all hope; to feel utterly discouraged or disheartened.|例句：He despaired of ever finding a job. 他绝望了，认为自己永远找不到工作。

例句：After many failed attempts, she despaired of ever achieving her goal.  多次尝试失败后，她对实现目标感到绝望。

近义词：despond, lost hope, give up hope | 反义词：hopeful, optimistic | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He despairs of his future. 他对未来感到绝望。
She despairs over her failing health. 她因健康状况恶化而感到绝望。


一般过去时:
He despaired at the news. 他听到这个消息后绝望了。
She despaired after losing her job. 丢掉工作后，她感到绝望。


现在完成时:
I have despaired many times in my life. 我一生中多次感到绝望。
They have despaired of finding a solution. 他们对找到解决办法已经绝望了。


过去完成时:
He had despaired of success before he finally achieved it. 在最终取得成功之前，他曾一度对成功感到绝望。
She had despaired of ever seeing him again. 她曾一度绝望地认为再也见不到他了。


将来时:
I fear I will despair if things don't improve. 我担心如果情况没有好转，我会绝望。
They will despair if the project fails. 如果项目失败，他们会绝望的。


虚拟语气:
If I were in his shoes, I would despair. 如果我处在他的位置，我也会绝望。
I wish I didn't despair so easily. 我希望自己不要这么容易就绝望。


被动语态:  (Despair is not typically used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Does he despair of his situation? 他对自己的处境绝望吗？
Will she despair if she doesn't get the job? 如果她没有得到这份工作，她会绝望吗？


否定句:
He did not despair, even in the face of adversity. 即使面对逆境，他也没有绝望。
She did not despair; she continued to fight for her rights. 她没有绝望；她继续为自己的权利而斗争。


PHRASE->
despair of: 对...绝望
despair over: 为...而绝望
in despair: 绝望地
despairingly: 绝望地
fall into despair: 陷入绝望
