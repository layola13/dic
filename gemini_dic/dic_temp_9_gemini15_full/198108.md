shooter:/ˈʃuːtər/ | n. |射手；枪手；射击者；（电影）枪战片 | A person who shoots a gun or other weapon; a film genre featuring gunfights.  |Example: He's a skilled shooter and won the competition. 他是一位技艺高超的射手，赢得了比赛。

Example: That new shooter movie looks exciting. 那部新的枪战片看起来很刺激。 |近义词：marksman, gunman, sharpshooter |反义词：target, victim
