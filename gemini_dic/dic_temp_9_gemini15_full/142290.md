orthid:/ˈɔːrθɪd/| n.|矫形器，支具|An orthid is a device used to support or correct a deformity or injury, particularly in the musculoskeletal system.  It can be custom-made or commercially available and is often used in the treatment of fractures, sprains, and other orthopedic conditions.|例句：The doctor prescribed an orthid to help stabilize his injured knee. 医生开了个矫形器来帮助稳定他受伤的膝盖。The patient wore an orthid for several weeks after surgery. 术后几周，病人一直戴着矫形器。|近义词：brace, splint, support|反义词:None directly applicable.
