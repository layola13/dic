non-itemized:/ˌnɑːnˈaɪtəmaɪzd/ | adj. | 未列明的，未逐项列出的 | Not listed individually; presented as a single total.  This contrasts with itemized expenses, where each expense is listed separately. | Example:  The donation was non-itemized on the tax return.  = 这笔捐款在纳税申报单上没有逐项列出。 | 近义词：unitemized, lump-sum, aggregated | 反义词：itemized, detailed
