introspectively:/ˌɪntrəˈspɛktɪvli/| adv.|内省地，沉思地 | Examining one's own thoughts and feelings.  |例句：She introspectively considered her recent mistakes. 她内省地思考了她最近犯的错误。|近义词：reflectively, contemplatively, thoughtfully |反义词：unreflectively, superficially |副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He introspectively considers his life choices. 他内省地思考着他的人生选择。
She introspectively examines her emotions. 她内省地审视自己的情感。


一般过去时:
He introspectively reviewed his past actions. 他内省地回顾了他过去的行为。
She introspectively pondered her future. 她内省地思考着她的未来。


现在完成时:
He has introspectively analyzed his behavior for years.  多年来，他一直内省地分析着自己的行为。
She has introspectively learned from her past experiences. 她一直通过内省从过去的经历中学习。


过去完成时:
He had introspectively assessed his capabilities before taking on the project. 他在承担项目之前已经内省地评估了自己的能力。
She had introspectively questioned her motives before acting.  她在行动之前已经内省地质疑过自己的动机。


将来时:
He will introspectively evaluate the results of his experiment. 他将内省地评估实验的结果。
She will introspectively reflect on her journey. 她将内省地反思自己的旅程。


虚拟语气:
If he were to introspectively examine his feelings, he would understand better. 如果他能内省地审视自己的感受，他会更好地理解。
If she had introspectively considered the consequences, she wouldn't have made that decision. 如果她事先内省地考虑过后果，她就不会做出那个决定了。


被动语态:  (This adverb is rarely used in the passive voice.  The action of introspection is inherently active.)


疑问句:  (This adverb doesn't directly form questions, but it can modify verbs in questions.)
Does she introspectively analyze her dreams? 她是否内省地分析她的梦？
How introspectively did he approach the problem? 他在处理这个问题时有多么内省？


否定句:
He did not introspectively examine his role in the conflict. 他没有内省地检视自己在冲突中的角色。
She did not introspectively consider the alternative solutions. 她没有内省地考虑其他的解决方案。
