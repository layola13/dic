refamiliarizing: /ˌriːfəˈmɪljərizaɪŋ/ | v. | 使重新熟悉；使再熟悉 | Making something familiar again; causing someone to become familiar with something once more.  | The process of refamiliarizing myself with the city after a long absence was surprisingly enjoyable.  我长时间离开后重新熟悉这座城市的过程令人惊讶地愉快。 | familiarizing, reacquainting | unfamiliarizing, estranging | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is refamiliarizing himself with the software. = 他正在重新熟悉这个软件。
We are refamiliarizing the students with the safety procedures. = 我们正在让学生重新熟悉安全程序。


一般过去时:
