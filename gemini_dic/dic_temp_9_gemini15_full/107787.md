lapped:/læpt/ | v. | 轻轻拍打；重叠；(水)拍打；领先一圈 | To strike or slap gently; to overlap; (of water) to strike against something repeatedly; to be a lap ahead in a race. | 例句：The waves lapped gently against the shore. 海浪轻轻地拍打着海岸。  The shingles lapped over each other.  瓦片相互重叠。 | 近义词：splash, overlap, wash | 反义词：dry, separate
