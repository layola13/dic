prefects:/ˈpriːfɛkts/| n.|级长，班长，监察员|A prefect is a student in a school who has been given extra responsibilities, such as keeping order or helping younger students.|例句：The prefects are responsible for maintaining discipline in the school.  级长负责维持学校纪律。|例句：She was a prefect in her final year at school.  她在学校最后一年担任级长。|近义词：monitor, student leader|反义词:N/A
