detainee:/ˌdiːteɪˈniː/| n. |被拘留者，被扣押者 |A person held in custody, especially by the authorities.  A detainee is someone who has been arrested and is being held by law enforcement, but has not yet been formally charged with a crime. They may be held for questioning, investigation, or pending trial.  |例句：The police questioned the detainee for hours.  警方讯问了被拘留者数小时。

The detainees were held in a temporary facility. 被拘留者被关押在一个临时设施中。|近义词：prisoner, captive, suspect |反义词：free person, released person |名词
