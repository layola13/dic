self-exalted:/ˌsɛlfɪɡˈzɔːltɪd/| adj.|自命不凡的；自高自大的|Having or showing an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities.  It implies an inflated ego and a tendency to look down on others.|例句：His self-exalted manner alienated many of his colleagues. 他自命不凡的态度使许多同事疏远了他。


例句：The self-exalted CEO refused to listen to any criticism.  这位自命不凡的首席执行官拒绝听取任何批评。


近义词：arrogant, haughty, conceited, supercilious, pompous


反义词：humble, modest, unassuming, self-effacing


词性：形容词
