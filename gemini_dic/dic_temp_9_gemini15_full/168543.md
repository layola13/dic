procedural:/prəˈsiːdʒərəl/ | adj. |程序的；过程的；步骤的 | Relating to or involving a series of actions or steps followed in a regular, definite order.  It often describes systems, methods, or programming. |例句：The company has a strict procedural process for handling customer complaints.  这家公司有一套严格的程序来处理客户投诉。


例句：The software's procedural generation creates unique levels each time you play.  该软件的程序化生成每次游戏都会创建独特的关卡。


近义词：methodical, systematic, step-by-step, routine | 反义词：random, haphazard, unsystematic


词源：源自procedure (程序，步骤)，加后缀-al构成形容词。 procedure 来自于拉丁语procedere，意思是“前进，继续”。
