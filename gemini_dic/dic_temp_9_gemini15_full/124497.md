moule:/muːl/| n.| 模具，模型 | A mould is a hollow container into which liquid or semi-liquid material is poured so that it can set and take the shape of the container.  It can also refer to a type of shell-fish. | Example: The baker used a special mould to create the intricate cake design.  烘焙师用特殊的模具制作了复杂的蛋糕图案。The mussels were cooked in their shells, which acted as natural moulds. 贻贝是用它们的贝壳烹制的，贝壳起到了天然模具的作用。|近义词：form, shape, cast, shell |反义词：original, unshaped
