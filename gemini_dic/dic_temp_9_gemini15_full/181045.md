reinclining: /ˌriːɪnˈklaɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 重新倾斜；再次倾斜 |  To lean or incline again; to cause to lean or incline again.  The word suggests a renewed or repeated action of leaning. | 例句：The tower, after initial repairs, began reinclining dangerously.  塔在初步维修后，又开始危险地倾斜。


The ship, despite stabilization efforts, reinclined to port.  尽管进行了稳定工作，船只还是再次向左倾斜。| 近义词：re-lean, tilt again, slant again | 反义词：straighten, upright
