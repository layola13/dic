overborrowing:/ˌoʊvərˈbɔːroʊɪŋ/ | n. |过度借贷，举债过多 |The act of borrowing more money than is prudent or sustainable, often leading to financial difficulties.  It implies taking on more debt than can be comfortably repaid. |例句：Overborrowing by developing countries led to a debt crisis in the 1980s.  发展中国家的过度借贷导致了20世纪80年代的债务危机。|例句：The company's overborrowing ultimately resulted in bankruptcy.  该公司的过度借贷最终导致了破产。|近义词：excessive borrowing, heavy indebtedness, profligate borrowing |反义词：fiscal prudence, responsible borrowing, saving, frugality |名词
