provide:/prəˈvaɪd/ | vt. | 提供，供应 | To make something available for someone to use; to supply or furnish. | The company provides excellent training for its employees.  这家公司为其员工提供优秀的培训。 | He provided food and shelter for the homeless. 他为无家可归的人提供食物和住所。| supply, furnish, offer | withhold, deny


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school provides textbooks to all students. 学校为所有学生提供教科书。
They provide excellent customer service. 他们提供优质的客户服务。

一般过去时:
She provided a delicious meal for her guests. 她为她的客人准备了一顿美味的饭菜。
The government provided financial aid to the victims. 政府向受害者提供了经济援助。

现在完成时:
They have provided many opportunities for young people. 他们已经为年轻人提供了许多机会。
We have provided all the necessary information. 我们已经提供了所有必要的信息。

过去完成时:
He had provided all the documents before the meeting. 他在会议前已经提供了所有文件。
She had provided a detailed explanation of the process. 她已经对这个过程做了详细的解释。

将来时:
The company will provide additional support. 公司将提供额外的支持。
We will provide updates as soon as possible. 我们会尽快提供更新。

虚拟语气:
If they provided more funding, we could finish the project earlier. 如果他们提供更多资金，我们就能早点完成项目。
I wish they provided better customer service. 我希望他们能提供更好的客户服务。

被动语态:
Food and water were provided to the refugees.难民得到了食物和水的供应。
The necessary equipment will be provided by the company.必要的设备将由公司提供。

疑问句:
Does the hotel provide Wi-Fi? 这家酒店提供无线网络吗？
What services does this company provide? 这家公司提供什么服务？

否定句:
The company does not provide health insurance. 这家公司不提供医疗保险。
They did not provide enough information. 他们没有提供足够的信息。
