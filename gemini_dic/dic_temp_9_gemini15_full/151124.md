parastatals:/ˈpærəˌsteɪtəlz/| n.|半官方机构，准国营企业|State-owned or state-controlled commercial enterprises.  These are organizations that operate independently from the government but receive significant government funding or oversight.|Many developing countries rely on parastatals to provide essential services such as electricity and transportation.|近义词：quasi-governmental organizations, semi-public entities|反义词：privately owned companies
