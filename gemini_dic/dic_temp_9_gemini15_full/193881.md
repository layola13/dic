self-displays:/ˈsɛlf dɪˈspleɪz/ | n. |自我展示，自我表现 |The act of showcasing one's abilities, personality, or attributes to others, often intentionally.  This can encompass a wide range of behaviors, from boasting about achievements to subtly highlighting positive traits through actions and demeanor. |例句：Her extravagant jewelry and designer clothes were a clear self-display of wealth.  她的奢华珠宝和名牌服装是对财富的明显自我展示。

例句：The artist's self-displays at the gallery opening were met with mixed reviews. 艺术家在画廊开幕式的自我展示受到了褒贬不一的评价。


近义词：self-promotion, exhibitionism, showboating, ostentation

反义词：self-effacement, modesty, humility
