preheating:/ˌpriːˈhiːtɪŋ/| n.|预热|The process of heating something, such as an oven or engine, before it is used to improve its efficiency or performance.  It's often done to ensure even cooking or a smoother start.|例句：The recipe instructed me to preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.  = 食谱指示我将烤箱预热到华氏350度。

例句：We need to preheat the engine before driving in cold weather. = 我们需要在寒冷天气驾驶前预热发动机。|近义词：warming up, heating up|反义词：cooling down
