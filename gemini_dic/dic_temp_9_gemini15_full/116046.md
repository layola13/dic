martial:/ˈmɑːrʃəl/| adj.|军事的；战争的；尚武的；婚姻的|Relating to war, soldiers, or military life; warlike.  The word also has a less common meaning relating to marriage.|例句：He has a martial spirit. 他具有尚武精神。

例句：The country is in a state of martial law. 这个国家处于军事管制状态。


近义词：military, warlike, bellicose, belligent|反义词：peaceful, civilian


词源: From Old French *marcial, from Latin martialis, from Mars, the Roman god of war.  The connection to marriage comes from the archaic use of "martial" to describe things associated with Mars, who was also associated with fertility and procreation.
