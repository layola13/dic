idealism:/aɪˈdiːəlɪzəm/| n. |理想主义；唯心主义|The philosophical theory that ideas are the primary reality, and matter is derivative from mind.|例句：His idealism led him to believe that he could change the world. 他的理想主义使他相信自己能够改变世界。

例句：Many young people embrace idealism when they first enter the workforce.许多年轻人初入职场的时侯都会接受理想主义。


近义词：utopianism, perfectionism|反义词：materialism, pragmatism|词性:名词
