crapple:/ˈkræpəl/| n. |糟糕的苹果；劣质产品|A crapple is a slang term for a bad or inferior apple, often extended metaphorically to describe any product or thing of poor quality.  It combines "crap" (meaning rubbish or excrement) and "apple".|例句：This new phone is a total crapple; it keeps crashing.|近义词：dud, lemon, clunker|反义词：masterpiece, gem
