inductively:/ɪnˈdʌktɪvli/ | adv. | 演绎地，归纳地 | Proceeding from particular facts or instances to a general conclusion.  It is the opposite of deductive reasoning. | Example:  The scientist inductively reasoned that all swans are white based on his observations. 科学家根据他的观察，归纳推理出所有天鹅都是白色的。 |近义词：inferentially, empirically | 反义词：deductively
