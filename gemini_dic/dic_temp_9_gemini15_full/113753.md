macroon:/ˈmækrʊn/| n.| 马卡龙 |A small, sweet meringue-based confection made with egg whites, icing sugar, granulated sugar, almond powder or ground almonds, and food coloring.  They are known for their smooth, delicate texture and vibrant colors. | Example:  The pastry chef carefully arranged the colorful macarons on the display plate.  = 糕点厨师小心地将五彩缤纷的马卡龙摆放在展示盘上。|近义词：meringue, confection|反义词：无
