pretending: /prɪˈtɛndɪŋ/ | v. |假装，伪装 |To behave as if something is true when it is not.  |例句：He was pretending to be asleep. 他假装睡着了。|近义词：feigning, simulating, affecting |反义词：being genuine, being truthful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pretending to be sick. = 他假装生病。
They are pretending they don't know me. = 他们假装不认识我。


一般过去时:
She pretended she hadn't seen him. = 她假装没看见他。
We pretended to be interested in his story. = 我们假装对他的故事感兴趣。


现在完成时:
They have been pretending to work all morning. = 他们整个上午都在假装工作。
She has pretended to be happy for years. = 她多年来一直假装快乐。


过去完成时:
He had pretended to be a doctor before the truth came out. = 在真相大白之前，他一直假装自己是医生。
They had pretended not to notice the mess. = 他们假装没注意到那团糟。


将来时:
I will pretend I didn't hear that. = 我会假装没听到。
She will pretend to be surprised when she receives the gift. = 收到礼物时，她会假装很惊讶。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would pretend not to see him. = 如果我是你，我会假装没看见他。
I wish I could pretend I didn't care. = 我真希望我能假装不在乎。


被动语态:
(Pretending is rarely used in the passive voice.)  The play is about a child pretending to be a superhero. = 这部戏讲述了一个孩子假扮超级英雄的故事。(This example uses pretending as a gerund, not in a passive construction)


疑问句:
Is he pretending to be deaf? = 他是在装聋吗？
Are they pretending to understand? = 他们是在假装理解吗？


否定句:
He wasn't pretending; he really was tired. = 他不是假装的；他真的累了。
She didn't pretend to like the food. = 她没有假装喜欢那食物。


PHRASE->
pretend to be = 假装是...
pretend to do sth = 假装做某事
pretend innocence = 假装无辜
pretend sleep = 假装睡觉
make believe/pretend = 假装


词源:
The word "pretend" comes from the Old French word "pretendre," meaning "to claim," "to strive for," or "to allege."  The meaning shifted over time to its current sense of feigning or simulating.
