nitrate:/ˈnaɪtreɪt/ | n. |硝酸盐；硝酸酯|A salt or ester of nitric acid. Nitrates are commonly found in fertilizers and explosives.  They can also be naturally occurring in some foods.|例句：Excessive nitrate in drinking water can be harmful to health.  饮用水中过量的硝酸盐对健康有害。
The dynamite contained a high concentration of nitrates.  这种炸药含有高浓度的硝酸盐。|近义词：salt, ester |反义词：无

词源:

The word "nitrate" comes from the Latin word "nitrum," meaning "natron" (a natural mineral containing sodium carbonate).  The suffix "-ate" indicates a salt of an acid, in this case, nitric acid.
