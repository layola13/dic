manager:/ˈmænɪdʒər/ | n. |经理，管理者 | A person responsible for controlling or administering an organization or group of staff.  | He is the manager of a large department store. 他是一家大型百货公司的经理。 | director, supervisor, administrator, executive | subordinate, employee | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The manager manages the team effectively. = 经理有效地管理团队。
She manages a large portfolio of clients. = 她管理着大量的客户。


一般过去时:
The manager managed the project successfully. = 经理成功地管理了这个项目。
He managed to finish the report on time. = 他设法按时完成了报告。


现在完成时:
The manager has managed this department for five years. = 经理已经管理这个部门五年了。
She has managed to overcome many challenges. = 她已经克服了许多挑战。


过去完成时:
The manager had managed several teams before this one. = 这位经理在此之前已经管理过几个团队。
They had managed to secure funding before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前设法获得了资金。


将来时:
The manager will manage the new branch next year. = 经理明年将管理新分公司。
He will manage to find a solution. = 他将设法找到解决方案。


虚拟语气:
If he were the manager, he would handle it differently. = 如果他是经理，他会以不同的方式处理。
I wish I had a manager who understood my needs. = 我希望我有一个了解我需求的经理。


被动语态:

The project was managed effectively. = 项目得到了有效的管理。


疑问句:

否定句:
