croziers:/ˈkroʊʒər/| n.|牧羊杖；主教权杖|A crozier is a shepherd's crook, often elaborately decorated, and also a ceremonial staff carried by a bishop or abbot as a symbol of his office.|例句：The archbishop carried his crozier proudly during the procession. 大主教在游行中骄傲地拿着他的牧羊杖。
The abbot's crozier was made of intricately carved wood.  方丈的权杖是用精细雕刻的木头制成的。|近义词：pastoral staff, bishop's staff|反义词：None
