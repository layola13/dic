half-life:/ˈhɑːfˌlaɪf/| n. |半衰期；半生期|The time it takes for half of a given amount of a substance to decay or be transformed.|The radioactive isotope carbon-14 has a half-life of 5,730 years.|近义词：decay time|反义词：full life
