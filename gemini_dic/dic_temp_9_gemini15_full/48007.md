deciphered:/dɪˈsaɪfərd/ | v. | 解密；破译；解释 | To decipher something means to succeed in understanding it, especially something that is difficult to read or understand.  | The archaeologist deciphered the ancient inscription. 考古学家破译了古代铭文。 | solved, interpreted, explained | encrypted, encoded | 动词
