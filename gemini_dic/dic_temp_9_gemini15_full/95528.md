inclusions:/ɪnˈkluːʒənz/| n.|包含物；内含物；内含矿物|Things that are included within something else;  the act of including something.|The rock sample contained several inclusions of quartz.  岩石样本包含几处石英内含物。 The report's inclusions of statistical data were very helpful. 报告中包含的统计数据非常有帮助。|components, contents, elements|exclusions, omissions|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The document includes several important points. = 这份文件包含几个要点。
This package includes a free gift. = 这个包裹包含一份免费礼物。


一般过去时:
The museum included a new exhibit last year. = 博物馆去年增加了一个新的展览。
The final cost included all taxes. = 最终成本包含所有税款。


现在完成时:
They have included all the necessary information. = 他们已经包含了所有必要的信息。
She has included a photograph in her essay. = 她在论文中添加了一张照片。


过去完成时:
He had included a note in the package before sending it. = 他在寄送包裹之前已经附上了一张便条。
We had included the extra expenses in our budget. = 我们已经在预算中包含了额外的费用。


将来时:
The next edition will include more images. = 下一版将包含更多图像。
We will include your suggestions in the final report. = 我们将在最终报告中包含您的建议。


虚拟语气:
If the proposal included a better budget, it would be approved. = 如果提案包含更好的预算，它就会被批准。
I wish the contract included a clause about termination. = 我希望合同中包含关于终止的条款。


被动语态:
Several new features were included in the software update. = 软件更新中包含了一些新功能。
Your feedback will be included in the next iteration. = 您的反馈将包含在下一个迭代中。


疑问句:
Does the price include shipping? = 价格包含运费吗？
Does the course include practical exercises? = 这门课程包含实践练习吗？


否定句:
The report did not include any financial data. = 报告中没有包含任何财务数据。
The initial proposal did not include enough detail. = 初始提案没有包含足够的细节。
