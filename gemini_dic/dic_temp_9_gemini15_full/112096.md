longitudinal:/ˌlɒŋɡɪˈtjuːdɪnəl/ /ˌlɑːŋɡɪˈtuːdɪnəl/ | adj. |纵向的；经度的；纵切的 | Relating to length or distance; extending in a lengthwise direction.  It can also refer to studies that follow the same subjects over a long period. |例句：The study used a longitudinal design, following the participants for ten years. 这项研究使用了纵向设计，对参与者进行了十年的跟踪研究。

例句：The building has a strong longitudinal axis. 这座建筑物有一条强烈的纵向轴线。


近义词：lengthwise, axial, lengthwise


反义词：transverse, lateral, crosswise


词性：形容词
