roams:/roʊmz/ | v. | 漫游；闲逛；徘徊 | To move about or travel freely over a wide area. | 例句：The lion roams the African savanna.  狮子在非洲大草原上漫游。


The wolf roams the forest in search of prey.  狼在森林里四处游荡寻找猎物。


近义词：wander, roam, stray, ramble | 反义词：stay, remain, settle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He roams the streets every night. = 他每晚都在街上闲逛。
The cattle roam freely in the pasture. = 牛群在牧场上自由自在地漫游。

一般过去时:
She roamed the countryside last summer. = 去年夏天她漫游了乡下。
The explorers roamed the unexplored jungle. = 探险家们漫游了未开发的丛林。

现在完成时:
They have roamed the world for many years. = 他们已经周游世界多年了。
The nomads have roamed these lands for centuries. = 游牧民族几个世纪以来一直在这片土地上游牧。

过去完成时:
The dog had roamed far from home before it was found. = 这条狗在被找到之前已经离家很远了。
Before the settlement, the tribes had roamed the plains for generations. = 在定居之前，部落世世代代在平原上游牧。

将来时:
We will roam the beaches tomorrow. = 我们明天将在海滩上漫步。
The sheep will roam the hills after the snow melts. = 雪融化后，羊群将在山上漫游。

虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I would roam the country. = 如果我有一辆车，我就会周游全国。
I wish I could roam the world freely. = 我希望我能自由自在地周游世界。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The area is rarely roamed by tourists. = 这个地区很少有游客光顾。
The forest was roamed by wild animals. = 森林里有很多野生动物出没。

疑问句:
Does the bear roam this area? = 熊会在这一带出没吗？
Where do the elephants roam? = 象群在什么地方漫游？

否定句:
The sheep don't roam freely in this fenced area. = 羊群在这个围栏区域内不能自由漫游。
He doesn't roam the streets anymore. = 他不再在街上闲逛了。


PHRASE->
roam freely  自由漫游
roam the streets 在街上闲逛
roam the countryside 漫游乡村
roam the world 周游世界
roam at will 任意漫游

词源:
Old English rōm "room, space," from Proto-Germanic *rūmaz, from Proto-Indo-European *rew- ("to row, sweep").  The sense development is from "space to move in" to "to move freely in space."
