hunt:/hʌnt/| /hʌnt/| v., n.|狩猎；搜寻；追捕|To search for and pursue game or other wild animals; to seek or search for something.|例句：The hunters went into the forest to hunt deer. 猎人进森林打鹿。|例句：I'm hunting for a new job. 我正在寻找一份新工作。|近义词：chase, seek, search, pursue|反义词：be found, be discovered|动词，名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hunts deer every weekend. = 他每个周末都打鹿。
They hunt for clues in the case. = 他们在调查案件中寻找线索。

一般过去时:
She hunted for hours but found nothing. = 她搜寻了几个小时，但什么也没找到。
We hunted rabbits in the field. = 我们在田野里打兔子。

现在完成时:
They have hunted in this area for years. = 他们在这个地区打猎多年了。
I have been hunting for a lost wallet. = 我一直在寻找丢失的钱包。

过去完成时:
He had hunted all day before he finally caught a fox. = 他搜寻了一整天后终于抓到了一只狐狸。
They had hunted for the treasure for months without success. = 他们寻找宝藏几个月都没有成功。

将来时:
We will hunt for mushrooms in the woods tomorrow. = 我们明天将在树林里寻找蘑菇。
They will hunt down the criminals. = 他们将追捕罪犯。

虚拟语气:
If I had a gun, I would hunt. = 如果我有一支枪，我会去打猎。
I wish I could hunt with my friends next weekend. = 我希望下周末能和朋友们一起去打猎。

被动语态:
The deer were hunted by the hunters. = 鹿被猎人猎杀了。
The criminals are being hunted by the police. = 罪犯正在被警方追捕。

疑问句:
Do you hunt? = 你打猎吗？
What are you hunting for? = 你在寻找什么？

否定句:
He doesn't hunt anymore. = 他不再打猎了。
They didn't hunt successfully today. = 他们今天狩猎没有成功。
