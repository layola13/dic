energy:/ˈɛnərɡi/| n.|能量，活力，精力 |The capacity to do work or cause change.  It can refer to physical, mental, or emotional strength.|例句：The energy from the sun powers our planet.  太阳的能量驱动着我们的星球。

例句：She has boundless energy and enthusiasm for her work. 她对工作有着无限的活力和热情。


近义词：power, force, strength, vigor, vitality |反义词：inertia, lethargy, weakness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun provides energy to the earth. = 太阳为地球提供能量。
She expends a lot of energy at work. = 她工作时消耗很多精力。


一般过去时:
The experiment released a lot of energy. = 实验释放了大量的能量。
He lacked the energy to finish the race. = 他缺乏完成比赛的精力。


现在完成时:
We have conserved energy all year. = 我们全年都在节约能源。
She has shown remarkable energy throughout the crisis. = 在整个危机中，她表现出了非凡的活力。


过去完成时:
They had expended all their energy before reaching the summit. = 他们在到达山顶之前已经耗尽了所有精力。
The car had lost most of its energy by the time it stopped. = 车子停下来的时候已经耗掉了大部分能量。


将来时:
We will need more energy in the future. = 未来我们将需要更多的能源。
The project will require considerable energy and resources. = 这个项目需要大量的精力和资源。


虚拟语气:
If I had more energy, I would go for a run. = 如果我有更多精力，我会去跑步。
I wish I had the energy to clean the house. = 我希望我有精力打扫房子。


被动语态:
Energy is produced by various methods. = 能量是由各种方法产生的。
The building is designed to conserve energy efficiently. = 这座建筑的设计目的是高效节约能源。


疑问句:
What is the source of this energy? = 这种能量的来源是什么？
Where do we get our energy from? = 我们的能量从哪里来？


否定句:
The machine doesn't have enough energy to operate. = 这台机器没有足够的能量来运转。
He felt too tired; he had no energy left. = 他感到太累了，没有力气了。
