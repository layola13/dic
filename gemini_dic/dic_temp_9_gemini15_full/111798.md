log:/lɒɡ; lɑːɡ/| n., v.| 原木；记录；航海日志；对数；逻辑；(v.) 记录；伐木 |

英文解析:
As a noun, "log" can refer to a large piece of wood, a record of events (like a ship's log or a flight log), or a mathematical logarithm. As a verb, "log" means to record something or to cut down trees.

例句:
The fire was fueled by a large log.  = 火是用一根大木头烧起来的。

He logged his flight hours meticulously. = 他一丝不苟地记录了他的飞行时间。


近义词:
(n.) record, entry, register, timber, trunk
(v.) record, register, note, fell


反义词:
(n.)  None directly opposite, depends on the context.  For "timber", perhaps "small branches".
(v.) None directly opposite, depends on the context (e.g., the opposite of "logging" could be "planting").


词性:
Noun, Verb
