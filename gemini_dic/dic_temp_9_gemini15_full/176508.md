re-projected:/ˌriːprəˈdʒɛktɪd/ | adj. | 再投射的；重新预测的 |  Having been projected again; having undergone a re-projection.  This usually refers to data, images, or plans that have been projected once and then modified and projected again.  | 例句：The re-projected budget shows a significant increase in spending. = 再投射的预算显示支出大幅增加。

The re-projected sales figures are more optimistic than the initial ones. = 重新预测的销售数字比最初的更乐观。


近义词：recast, revised, remapped | 反义词：original, initial | 词性：形容词
