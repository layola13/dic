freeway:/ˈfriːweɪ/| n.|高速公路|A freeway is a divided highway with controlled access, meaning that vehicles can enter and exit only at designated interchanges.  Freeways typically have multiple lanes in each direction and are designed for high-speed traffic.|例句：We took the freeway to get to the airport quickly. 我们走高速公路去机场以便快速到达。|例句：The freeway was congested during rush hour. 高峰期高速公路很拥堵。|近义词：expressway, highway, motorway|反义词：local road, back road
