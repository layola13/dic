pantechnicons:/ˌpæntekˈnɪkɑːnz/ | n. |万事通；包罗万象的机器；博览会| A pantechnicon is a large building or establishment that contains a comprehensive collection of goods or services, or a machine designed to perform many functions.  The term is now largely archaic. |例句：The Victorian pantechnicon offered everything from furniture to exotic spices. 维多利亚时代的万事通商店出售从家具到异域香料的一切商品。|近义词：emporium, department store, universal machine |反义词：specialized store, single-function device |名词
