/ˈɡruːplɛt/| n. |小群；小组 |A small group; a diminutive of group.  The term often implies a less formal or less officially recognized group than the term "group" suggests. |Example: A grouplet of children gathered around the storyteller.  一群孩子围着讲故事的人。  A small grouplet of protesters demonstrated outside the building.  一小群抗议者在楼外示威。|近义词：small group, cluster, clique |反义词：large group, multitude
