interbranchial: /ˌɪntərˈbræŋkiəl/ | adj. | 鳃间的；位于鳃之间的 | Situated between the gills.  The term refers to the anatomical location between gills in aquatic animals, particularly fish.  | Example: The interbranchial septum separates the gills in this species of fish.  鳃间隔将这种鱼的鳃分开。 | 近义词: interlamellar (if referring to gill lamellae) | 反义词: intrabranchial (within the gills)
