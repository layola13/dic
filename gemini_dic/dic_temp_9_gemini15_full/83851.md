halbert:/ˈhæl.bərt/| n. |戟；长柄武器|A halberd is a type of polearm, a weapon with a long handle and a blade or head at the end.  It was commonly used in the Middle Ages and Renaissance as a weapon of war.  The halberd typically has an axe blade on one side and a spear point on the other, sometimes with a hook or hammer. |The soldier wielded his halberd with deadly accuracy.|pike, partisan, glaive |sword, dagger|名词
