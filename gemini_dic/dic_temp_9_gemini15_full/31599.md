chastening: /ˈtʃeɪsənɪŋ/ | adj. | 惩戒的；令人沮丧的；使人谦卑的 |  Serving as a rebuke or correction; humbling; discouraging.  |例句：The chastening experience taught him humility.  令人沮丧的经历教会了他谦逊。

The chastening effect of failure made him rethink his approach.  失败的教训让他重新思考自己的方法。


近义词：corrective, humbling, discouraging, rebuking, mortifying | 反义词：encouraging, uplifting, rewarding, exalting | 词性：形容词
