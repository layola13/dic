preremunerated: /ˌpriːriˈmjuːnəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 预先酬谢的；预付报酬的 |  Already paid; compensated in advance.  The word combines "pre-" (before) and "remunerate" (to pay).  It describes something that has been paid for or compensated before the work or service is performed. |


例句：

The consultant received a preremunerated fee for his services. = 顾问预先收取了他的服务费。

The speaker was preremunerated for his presentation. = 演讲者预先收取了演讲费。


近义词：prepaid, advanced, compensated in advance

反义词：unpaid, outstanding, due


词性：形容词
