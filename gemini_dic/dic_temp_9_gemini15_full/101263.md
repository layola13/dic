invoking: /ɪnˈvoʊkɪŋ/ | v. | 祈求；调用；引起；实施 | To invoke something is to call upon it, or to bring it into effect.  This can be a formal or informal act, and the "something" can be a law, a spirit, a feeling, or a procedure. |例句：The shaman invoked the spirits of his ancestors.  萨满祭司祈求了他祖先的灵魂。

The judge invoked the law to settle the dispute. 法官援引法律来解决纠纷。


近义词：summon, call upon, evoke, implement, activate | 反义词：ignore, neglect, suppress


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He invokes the name of God in his prayers. 他在祈祷中呼唤上帝的名字。
They invoke the rules of the game to justify their actions. 他们援引游戏规则来为自己的行为辩护。


一般过去时:
She invoked her right to remain silent. 她行使了她保持沉默的权利。
The programmer invoked a function to process the data. 程序员调用了一个函数来处理数据。


现在完成时:
They have invoked emergency powers to deal with the crisis. 他们已经动用了紧急权力来应对危机。
She has invoked the help of her friends many times. 她多次求助于她的朋友。


过去完成时:
He had invoked the protection of the court before the trial. 他在审判前已请求法院的保护。
The system had invoked a security protocol after detecting an intrusion. 系统在检测到入侵后已调用了一个安全协议。


将来时:
They will invoke the contract if necessary.  如有必要，他们将援引合同。
The software will invoke the operating system's functions. 该软件将调用操作系统的功能。


虚拟语气:
If we invoked the right procedures, we would have avoided the error. 如果我们使用了正确的程序，我们就不会犯这个错误。
I wish he had invoked the relevant legal precedent. 我希望他当时援用了相关的法律先例。


被动语态:
The law was invoked to justify the police action.  法律被用来为警方的行动辩护。
The subroutine was invoked automatically by the main program. 子程序由主程序自动调用。


疑问句:
Did they invoke the emergency clause in the contract? 他们在合同中使用了紧急条款吗？
What function will be invoked next? 下一步将调用哪个函数？


否定句:
He did not invoke the relevant statute. 他没有援引相关的法令。
The program failed because it did not invoke the necessary library. 程序失败是因为它没有调用必要的库。



PHRASE->
invoke a law 援引法律
invoke a function 调用函数
invoke a spirit 召唤灵魂
invoke a debate 引起一场辩论
invoke a memory 唤起回忆

词源:
From Old French *invoquer, from Latin invocare, from in- "in, upon, into" + vocare "to call".
