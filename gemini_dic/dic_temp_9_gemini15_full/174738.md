rabbani:/ræˈbɑːni/| adj. n.| 拉巴尼的；宗教领袖；|Of or relating to a rabbi or rabbinical authority; relating to or characteristic of a religious leader.  A religious leader, especially a Muslim one.|The scholar delivered a rabbani lecture on Islamic jurisprudence. 学者发表了一篇关于伊斯兰法学的拉巴尼讲座。|近义词：religious leader, spiritual leader, cleric|反义词：layperson, secularist|形容词，名词
