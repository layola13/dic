cheddars:/ˈtʃɛdərz/| n. |切达干酪(复数)|A type of firm, slightly sharp-tasting cheese, originally from the village of Cheddar in Somerset, England.  Cheddars are known for their smooth texture and range of flavors depending on age and production methods.|例句：We bought a block of cheddars for our cheese platter. 我们买了一块切达干酪放在我们的奶酪拼盘上。

例句：Many different varieties of cheddars are available in supermarkets.  超市里有很多不同种类的切达干酪。


近义词：cheeses (general term), cheddar cheese (singular)


反义词：无


词性: 名词
