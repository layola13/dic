motivo:/moʊˈtiːvoʊ/| n. |动机；主题；主旨；旋律；图案|A motivo is a reason or explanation for doing something.  It can also refer to a recurring theme, subject, or melody in a piece of music or art.  In design, it refers to a repeated decorative pattern.|例句：His motivo for leaving was unclear. 他离开的原因不明确。 The central motivo of the novel is the struggle for freedom. 这部小说的中心主题是争取自由。|近义词：reason, cause, theme, subject, design, pattern|反义词：result, effect
