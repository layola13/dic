sebum:/ˈsiːbəm/| n.|皮脂|An oily or waxy substance secreted by sebaceous glands of the skin.  It lubricates the skin and hair.|例句：Excessive sebum production can lead to acne. 多余的皮脂分泌会导致痤疮。|例句：The dermatologist examined the patient's skin for signs of sebum imbalance. 皮肤科医生检查了病人的皮肤，寻找皮脂不平衡的迹象。|近义词：oil, grease, fat|反义词：dryness
