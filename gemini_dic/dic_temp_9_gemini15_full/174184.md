queerer:/ˈkwɪərər/| adj.|更古怪的；更奇异的；更反常的 |More queer; more strange or unusual.  It is the comparative form of the adjective "queer".|例句：His behavior grew queerer by the day.  他日渐行为古怪。 The queerer the story, the more intriguing it became. 故事越古怪，就越引人入胜。|近义词：stranger, odder, weirder |反义词：normal, ordinary, typical
