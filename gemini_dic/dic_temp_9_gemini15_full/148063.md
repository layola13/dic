oversanguine:/ˌoʊvərˈsæŋɡwɪn/| adj.| 过分乐观的；过于自信的 | Excessively optimistic; too confident.  |The investors were oversanguine about the success of their new venture.  投资者对他们新企业的成功过于乐观。|近义词：overoptimistic, excessively hopeful, too confident |反义词：pessimistic, doubtful, apprehensive
