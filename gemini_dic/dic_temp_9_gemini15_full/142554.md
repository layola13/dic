ostentate:/ˈɒstənˌteɪt/ /ɑːˈstɛnteɪt/| vt.|炫耀，夸示|to show (something) off in a very noticeable way; to display (something) ostentatiously.|例句：He ostentated his wealth by driving a luxurious car. 他开着一辆豪华车来炫耀他的财富。
例句：She ostentated her knowledge of art history during the conversation. 她在谈话中炫耀她对艺术史的了解。|近义词：show off, flaunt, display, parade|反义词：hide, conceal, downplay

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ostentates his wealth. = 他炫耀他的财富。
They ostentate their achievements. = 他们炫耀他们的成就。

一般过去时:
He ostentated his new car to his friends. = 他向朋友炫耀他的新车。
She ostentated her culinary skills at the party. = 她在派对上炫耀她的烹饪技巧。

现在完成时:
He has ostentated his success throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中都炫耀他的成功。
She has ostentated her connections in high society. = 她一直炫耀她在上流社会的交际关系。

过去完成时:
He had ostentated his power before the meeting. = 他在会议之前就炫耀了他的权力。
She had ostentated her beauty in every photograph. = 她在每一张照片里都炫耀她的美貌。

将来时:
He will ostentate his new house to everyone. = 他会向所有人炫耀他的新房子。
She will ostentate her singing talent at the concert. = 她将在音乐会上炫耀她的歌唱才能。

虚拟语气:
If he were richer, he would ostentate his wealth even more. = 如果他更有钱，他会更炫耀他的财富。
If she had won the lottery, she would have ostentated her fortune. = 如果她中了彩票，她就会炫耀她的财富。

被动语态:
His wealth was ostentated by his extravagant lifestyle. = 他奢侈的生活方式炫耀了他的财富。
Her achievements were ostentated by the media. = 她的成就被媒体大肆宣扬。

疑问句:
Does he ostentate his success? = 他炫耀他的成功吗？
Why does she ostentate her knowledge? = 她为什么炫耀她的知识？

否定句:
He doesn't ostentate his feelings. = 他不炫耀他的感情。
She didn't ostentate her new dress. = 她没有炫耀她的新裙子。


PHRASE->

词源:
源自拉丁语 *ostentare，意思是“展示，展示出来”，由 *osten-（“显示”）和 -are（动词后缀）构成。
