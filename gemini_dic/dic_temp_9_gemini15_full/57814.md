drench:/drentʃ/ | vt.  | 湿透；浸透；使充满 | To make someone or something completely wet. |例句：The rain drenched us to the bone.  雨把我们淋透了。

例句：He drenched the thirsty plants with water. 他用大量的水浇灌干渴的植物。


近义词：soak, saturate, inundate | 反义词：dry


词源:源自古英语 *drencan，意思是“使湿透”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain drenches the streets every afternoon. = 下午雨水总是把街道淋湿。
He drenches his toast in butter. = 他把吐司面包浸在黄油里。


一般过去时:
The sudden downpour drenched the hikers. = 突然而至的大雨淋湿了徒步旅行者。
She drenched her hair with conditioner.  = 她用护发素把头发浸透了。


现在完成时:
The continuous rain has drenched the fields. = 持续的雨水已经把田地浸透了。
They have drenched the garden with water. = 他们已经把花园浇透了。


过去完成时:
The ground was already drenched because it had drenched the night before. = 地面已经湿透了，因为前一天晚上就下过大雨。
We were drenched because it had drenched earlier. = 我们都湿透了，因为之前下过雨。


将来时:
The forecast says it will drench the region tomorrow. = 预报说明天该地区将有大雨。
I think the rain will drench everything. = 我想雨会把一切都淋湿。


虚拟语气:
If it drenched tomorrow, we would cancel the picnic. = 如果明天下雨，我们会取消野餐。
I wish it hadn't drenched yesterday. = 我真希望昨天没有下雨。


被动语态:
The clothes were drenched by the unexpected rain. = 衣服被突如其来的雨淋湿了。
The whole area was drenched by the flood. = 整个地区都被洪水淹没了。


疑问句:
Was the fabric drenched by the washing machine? = 洗衣机把布料弄湿了吗？
Is the soil drenched enough for planting? = 土壤是否湿润到足以种植？


否定句:
The rain didn't drench the crops this year. = 今年雨水没有把庄稼淋湿。
Don't drench the cake with too much syrup. = 不要用太多糖浆把蛋糕浸透。
