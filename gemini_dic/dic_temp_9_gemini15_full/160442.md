popularizations:/ˌpɑːpjʊləraɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |普及；推广；通俗化 |The act of making something popular or widely known, often by simplifying complex ideas or making them more accessible to a wider audience.  It involves explaining complex subjects in a simpler, more understandable way. |Example: The popularizations of science through television documentaries have increased public interest in scientific topics. 科学通过电视纪录片的普及提高了公众对科学主题的兴趣。|近义词：dissemination, simplification, popularization (singular)|反义词：obscuration, complication, specialization|
