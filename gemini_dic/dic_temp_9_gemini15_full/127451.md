nebula:/ˈnɛbjʊlə/| n. |星云|A nebula is a cloud of gas and dust in outer space, visible as a luminous patch or spot.  Nebulae often form from the remnants of dying stars or serve as the birthplaces of new stars.|Example: The Orion Nebula is a beautiful example of a stellar nursery.|近义词：cloud (in space), cosmic cloud|反义词：N/A
