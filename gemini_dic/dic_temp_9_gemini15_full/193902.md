self-doomed:/ˌsɛlfˈduːmd/| adj.| 注定自我毁灭的，自取灭亡的 | doomed to destruction or failure by one's own actions or decisions.  The company's self-doomed strategy led to its bankruptcy.  这家公司的自取灭亡的战略导致了它的破产。|近义词：self-destructive, ill-fated, fated, doomed |反义词：successful, fortunate, auspicious


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plan is self-doomed from the start. = 这个计划从一开始就注定要失败。
His actions are self-doomed. = 他的行为注定要失败。


一般过去时:
Their project was self-doomed due to poor planning. = 他们的项目由于计划不周而注定失败。
The empire was self-doomed by internal conflicts. = 这个帝国由于内部冲突而自取灭亡。
