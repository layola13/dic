quasi-admire:/ˈkwɑːzi ædˈmaɪər/| adj.|似是而非的钦佩，表面上的赞赏| seemingly admiring but not truly so; a superficial or insincere form of admiration.|例句：His quasi-admiration for her work was evident in his backhanded compliments. 他对她作品的似是而非的钦佩，从他拐弯抹角的赞美中可见一斑。|近义词：pseudo-admiration, feigned admiration, superficial respect|反义词：genuine admiration, heartfelt appreciation|形容词
