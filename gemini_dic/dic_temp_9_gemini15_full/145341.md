overbuilding:/ˌoʊvərˈbɪldɪŋ/ | n. | 过度建设，建筑过剩 | The act of constructing more buildings than are needed or can be supported by the market or infrastructure.  This often leads to vacant properties and economic hardship.  | Example: The overbuilding in the city center led to a glut of empty office spaces.  城市中心地区的过度建设导致了大量空置的办公空间。 | 近义词: excessive construction, surplus construction | 反义词: underbuilding, controlled development |
