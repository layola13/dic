heterochronism:/ˌhɛtərəˈkroʊnɪzəm/| n. |异时性，发育异时性|A deviation from the normal time of occurrence of developmental events.  This can involve changes in the timing of developmental processes, such as the onset, offset, or duration of a particular stage. Heterochronies are important in evolutionary biology because they can lead to significant morphological changes. | Example:  The accelerated development of sexual maturity in some species is an example of heterochronism.  某些物种性成熟的加速发育是异时性的一个例子。|近义词：developmental timing alteration, evolutionary change in timing|反义词：isochronism (同时间性)|名词
