ricochetting: /ˈrɪkəʃeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 反弹，弹跳 |  The act of a projectile bouncing off a surface at an angle.  It describes the movement of something that rebounds repeatedly after hitting a surface. |例句：The bullet ricochetting off the wall narrowly missed him. 子弹击中墙壁后反弹，险些击中他。

The ball kept ricochetting off the trees. 球不断地从树上弹开。


近义词：bouncing, rebounding, reflecting | 反义词：straight, direct


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ball ricochets off the wall.  球从墙上反弹。
Light ricochets off the surface of the water. 光线从水面反射。


一般过去时:
The bullet ricocheted off the tree trunk. 子弹从树干上反弹。
The sound ricocheted through the canyon. 声音在峡谷中回荡。


现在完成时:
The rumors have ricocheted across the internet. 谣言已经在互联网上传播开来。
The stone has ricocheted several times before coming to rest. 石头反弹了几次才停下来。


过去完成时:
The ball had ricocheted several times before I caught it.  在我接住球之前，它已经反弹了好几次。
The news had ricocheted through the town before we even heard about it.  在我们听到这个消息之前，它已经在镇上传开了。
