It encompasses the Holocene Epoch and beyond, essentially referring to the time period from the end of the last ice age to the present day. The term is often used in geological and paleontological contexts to describe events, formations, or organisms that existed after the Pleistocene. | Example:  The Post-Pliocene sedimentary deposits in this area are rich in fossils. = 这个地区的更新世以后沉积物富含化石。  The study focuses on Post-Pliocene climate change and its impact on biodiversity. = 这项研究的重点是更新世以后的气候变化及其对生物多样性的影响。| 近义词: Holocene, Recent, postglacial | 反义词: Pleistocene, pre-Pleistocene | adj.
