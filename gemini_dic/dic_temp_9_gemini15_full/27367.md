camcorder:/ˈkæmkɔːrdər/| n. |便携式摄像机|A portable device that records moving images and sound; a combination of a camera and a video recorder.|例句：He used a camcorder to film his daughter's graduation ceremony. 他用便携式摄像机拍摄了他女儿的毕业典礼。

例句：She bought a new camcorder with high-definition capabilities. 她买了一台具有高清功能的新型摄像机。


近义词：video camera, video recorder|反义词：None.
