protoporphyria:/ˌproʊtoʊˈpɔːrfiəriə/| n. |原卟啉症|A rare genetic disorder characterized by an accumulation of protoporphyrin in the blood, causing sensitivity to sunlight.|Example:  The patient presented with symptoms consistent with erythropoietic protoporphyria, including severe photosensitivity.|近义词:erythropoietic protoporphyria (EPP) |反义词:  None specifically, but absence of protoporphyrin accumulation would be the opposite.|名词
