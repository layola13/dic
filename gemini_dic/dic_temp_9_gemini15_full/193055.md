secretions:/sɪˈkriːʃənz/| n. |分泌物；分泌 |Substances produced and discharged by a gland or cell.  Secretions can be hormones, enzymes, mucus, or other substances that have a specific function in the body. | 例句：The doctor examined the patient's secretions to diagnose the infection.  医生检查了病人的分泌物以诊断感染。

The body's secretions help protect it from infection. 人体的分泌物有助于保护它免受感染。|近义词：excretions, discharges |反义词：absorption
