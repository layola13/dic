hero-worshiper:/ˈhɪroʊ ˈwɜːrʃɪpər/|/ˈhɪərəʊ ˈwɜːʃɪpə(r)/| n. |英雄崇拜者|A person who excessively admires and imitates a particular person, often a celebrity or historical figure.  They often idealize this person and may overlook their flaws.|He is a hero-worshiper of Elon Musk, copying his every business decision.|近义词：admirer, devotee, idolater |反义词：critic, detractor


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He hero-worships that famous actor. = 他崇拜那位著名的演员。
She hero-worships every athlete she meets. = 她崇拜她遇到的每一个运动员。


一般过去时:
He hero-worshipped his older brother. = 他崇拜他的哥哥。
They hero-worshipped the rock star. = 他们崇拜那位摇滚明星。


现在完成时:
She has hero-worshipped this singer for years. = 她多年来一直崇拜这位歌手。
He has hero-worshipped this historical figure since childhood. = 他从小就崇拜这位历史人物。


过去完成时:
He had hero-worshipped that artist before he discovered his flaws. = 在他发现那名艺术家的缺点之前，他一直崇拜他。
They had hero-worshipped the politician until the scandal broke. = 直到丑闻爆发之前，他们一直崇拜这位政治家。


将来时:
She will continue to hero-worship her favorite author. = 她将继续崇拜她最喜欢的作家。
He will likely hero-worship this athlete for the rest of his career. = 他可能会在余生中继续崇拜这位运动员。


虚拟语气:
If I were a hero-worshiper, I would try to emulate their success. = 如果我是个英雄崇拜者，我会尝试效仿他们的成功。
I wish I hadn't been such a hero-worshiper in my youth. = 我真希望我年轻时不要那么崇拜英雄。


被动语态:
That famous athlete is hero-worshipped by many young people. = 许多年轻人崇拜那位著名的运动员。
The historical figure was hero-worshipped for centuries. = 这位历史人物被崇拜了几个世纪。


疑问句:
Is he a hero-worshiper of that particular celebrity? = 他是那个特定名人的崇拜者吗？
Are you a hero-worshipper of any particular person? = 你崇拜任何特定的人吗？


否定句:
He is not a hero-worshiper; he has a more critical view. = 他不是英雄崇拜者；他对事情有更批判性的看法。
She was not a hero-worshiper; she admired them but maintained her independence. = 她不是英雄崇拜者；她崇拜他们，但保持独立。
