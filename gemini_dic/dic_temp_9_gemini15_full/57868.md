/drʌɡ/ | n. | 毒品，药；|A drug is any substance that alters the function of the body or mind.  Drugs can be legal, like prescription medications, or illegal, like heroin or cocaine. Many drugs can be habit-forming and lead to addiction. |例句：He was arrested for possession of illegal drugs. 他因持有非法毒品而被捕。|例句：The doctor prescribed her some strong pain-relieving drugs. 医生给她开了些强效止痛药。|近义词：narcotic, medicine, medication|反义词：n/a|名词
