headache:/ˈhɛdeɪk/| n.|头痛|A pain in the head.|I have a terrible headache. 我头痛得厉害。|近义词：headache, cephalalgia|反义词：pain relief


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I often get headaches when I'm stressed. = 我压力大的时候经常头痛。
She usually takes aspirin for her headaches. = 她通常服用阿司匹林来缓解头痛。


一般过去时:
He had a severe headache yesterday. = 他昨天头痛得很厉害。
I took some medicine because I had a headache. = 我因为头痛吃了药。


现在完成时:
I have had a headache all day. = 我一整天都头痛。
She has had frequent headaches recently. = 她最近经常头痛。


过去完成时:
I had already taken painkillers before the headache became unbearable. = 头痛变得难以忍受之前，我已经服用了止痛药。
He had suffered from headaches for years before he sought medical help. = 在寻求医疗帮助之前，他已经多年饱受头痛之苦。


将来时:
I think I will have a headache after this long drive. = 我想长途驾驶后我会头痛。
She expects to have a headache if she doesn't get enough sleep. = 如果她睡不够，她预计会头痛。

I wish I didn't have such a bad headache. = 我真希望我不头痛得这么厉害。
