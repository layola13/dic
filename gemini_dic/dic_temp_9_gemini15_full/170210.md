protovertebral:/ˌproʊtoʊˈvɜːrtɪbrəl/| adj.|原椎的；原脊椎的|Relating to or denoting the primitive segments of the embryonic vertebral column.  These segments are precursors to the fully formed vertebrae.  The term often appears in embryological contexts.|例句：The protovertebral segments are crucial for the development of the spine. 原椎体节对于脊柱的发育至关重要。

The study of protovertebral development revealed a complex interplay of genetic and environmental factors. 对原椎发育的研究揭示了遗传和环境因素之间复杂的相互作用。


近义词：primitive vertebral, embryonic vertebral (in specific contexts)|反义词：mature vertebral, fully formed vertebral|词性:形容词
