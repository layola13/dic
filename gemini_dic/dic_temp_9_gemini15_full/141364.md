operationally:/ˌɒpəˈreɪʃənəli/ | /ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃənəli/ | adj. | 操作上地；运作上地 |  In a way that relates to the practical working of a system or organization.  It describes something from the perspective of its practical function and execution. |

例句：

Operationally, the new system is a significant improvement.  = 从操作上来说，新系统是一个显著的改进。

Operationally, the team is ready to launch the project. = 从运作角度来看，团队已准备好启动项目。


近义词：functionally, practically, in practice

反义词：theoretically, conceptually


词性:形容词
