hangover:/ˈhæŋˌoʊvər/ /ˈhæŋˌəʊvər/| n. |宿醉；后遗症|The unpleasant physical effects experienced after drinking too much alcohol.  A hangover typically includes headache, nausea, vomiting, dizziness, and fatigue. It is caused by dehydration, inflammation, and the body's reaction to alcohol breakdown products. |I had a terrible hangover after the party last night.  昨晚派对后我宿醉难受极了。

I woke up with a splitting headache – a classic hangover symptom. 我醒来头痛欲裂——这是宿醉的典型症状。


近义词：aftereffect,repercussion|反义词：None
