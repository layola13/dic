reglement:/ˈrɛɡjʊlmɑːŋ/|/ˈreɡjʊleɪmənt/| n.|规章，规程，条例|A regulation or rule, especially one that is official.  It often refers to a set of rules or regulations governing a specific activity or organization.|Example: The company has a strict reglement on the use of social media during work hours.  这家公司对工作时间使用社交媒体有严格的规章。|近义词：rule, regulation, ordinance, bylaw|反义词：chaos, disorder
