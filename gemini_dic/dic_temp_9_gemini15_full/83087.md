gushed:/ɡʌʃt/| v.|涌出；滔滔不绝地说；| to flow or pour out rapidly and abundantly; to speak or write enthusiastically or effusively.|例句：Water gushed from the broken pipe. 水从破裂的管道中涌出。|例句：She gushed about her new boyfriend. 她滔滔不绝地谈论她的新男友。|近义词：poured, streamed, flowed, raved, enthused|反义词：trickled, dribbled, mumbled


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The water gushes from the spring. = 水从泉水中涌出。
He gushes about his new car every time we meet. = 每次我们见面，他都会滔滔不绝地谈论他的新车。


一般过去时:
Blood gushed from the wound. = 血从伤口涌出。
She gushed over the baby's cuteness. = 她对婴儿的可爱赞不绝口。


现在完成时:
The river has gushed over its banks several times this year. = 今年这条河已经几次决堤了。
He has gushed about his achievements all evening. = 他整个晚上都在夸耀他的成就。


过去完成时:
Before the dam broke, water had gushed into the valley. = 在水坝决堤之前，水已经涌进了山谷。
She had gushed about her trip to Europe for hours before I finally got a word in. = 在我终于插上话之前，她已经滔滔不绝地谈论了她去欧洲的旅行好几个小时了。


将来时:
If the pipe bursts, water will gush everywhere. = 如果管道爆裂，水会到处涌出。
I'm sure she will gush about her engagement to everyone she knows. = 我确信她会向她认识的所有人炫耀她的订婚。


虚拟语气:
If the dam had been stronger, the water wouldn't have gushed over it. = 如果水坝更坚固，水就不会溢出来了。
If I had met her, I'm sure I would have gushed about how much I loved her books. = 如果我遇到她，我肯定我会滔滔不绝地谈论我有多爱她的书。


被动语态:
The water was gushed out of the container. = 水从容器中涌出。
Her success was gushed over by the media. = 她的成功受到了媒体的热烈赞扬。


疑问句:
Does the blood gush from the wound heavily? = 血从伤口大量涌出吗？
Will she gush about her vacation again? = 她还会再次滔滔不绝地谈论她的假期吗？


否定句:
The water didn't gush; it merely trickled. = 水并没有涌出；它只是滴落。
He didn't gush; he simply stated the facts. = 他没有滔滔不绝地说；他只是陈述了事实。
