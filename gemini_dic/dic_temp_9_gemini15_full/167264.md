pretests:/ˈpriːˌtɛsts/ | n. |预测试验；入学考试 | Preliminary tests conducted before a main test or examination to assess readiness or identify areas needing improvement.  They are often used to gauge a student's understanding of the material before a major exam or to determine placement in a course. | Example: The students took pretests to evaluate their preparedness for the final exam.  学生们参加了预测试验以评估他们期末考试的准备情况。|近义词： preliminary tests, diagnostic tests |反义词：post-tests, final exams
