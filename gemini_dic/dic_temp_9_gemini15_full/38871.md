confederator:/kənˈfɛdəreɪtər/| n. | 同盟者；邦联成员；共谋者 | A person who is a member of a confederacy or who is allied with another.  Someone who works with others in a secret or illegal activity. | Example: The Confederate soldiers fought bravely during the Civil War.  南方邦联士兵在内战中英勇作战。  He was accused of being a confederator in the robbery. 他被指控是这起抢劫案的同谋。 |近义词：ally, accomplice, conspirator |反义词：opponent, enemy, adversary| 名词
