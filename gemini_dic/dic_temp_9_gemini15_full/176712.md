reactivated:/ˌriˈæktɪveɪtɪd/ | v. (过去分词) | 重新启动；使重新活跃；重新启用 |  To make something active again; to restart something that was inactive or stopped. |例句：The dormant volcano reactivated after centuries of inactivity.几百年沉寂后，休眠火山重新喷发。  |例句：The company reactivated its marketing campaign after a period of low sales.公司在销售低迷一段时间后重新启动了营销活动。|近义词：restart, reactivate, reawaken, revive | 反义词：deactivate, inactivate, disable | 动词


USAGE->

They reactivate the account every month. = 他们每月重新激活账户。
We reactivate the project after a long delay. = 我们在长时间延迟后重新启动项目。
