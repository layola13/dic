renders:/ˈrɛndərz/ | vt. |呈现；使成为；翻译；表演；给予；回报；融化；分解；破裂| to cause something to be or become; to present something in a particular way; to translate something; to perform a role or part; to give or provide something; to melt or dissolve; to break down or decompose; to crack or split |

例句：

The artist renders the scene with remarkable detail.  艺术家以惊人的细节描绘了这幅场景。

The software renders 3D models into realistic images.  该软件将3D模型渲染成逼真的图像。


近义词：presents, portrays, depicts, translates, produces, yields


反义词：obscures, conceals, hides


词性：动词
