oversaturated: /ˌoʊvərˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 饱和的；过度饱和的；过度充斥的 |

英文解析:  Containing or having absorbed more of something (especially a liquid or gas) than can be held or retained; excessively filled or supplied with something.  This can refer to literal saturation (like a saturated solution) or figurative saturation (like an oversaturated market).

例句:
The market is oversaturated with cheap smartphones. = 市场充斥着廉价的智能手机。

The sponge was oversaturated with water and dripping. = 海绵吸饱了水，滴答着。


近义词: saturated, flooded, inundated, overloaded, brimming

反义词: unsaturated, deficient, lacking
