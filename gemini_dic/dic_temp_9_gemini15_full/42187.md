couloir:/kwɑːˈlɔːr/|/ˈkuːlwɑːr/| n. |雪道；冰沟；峡谷 | A couloir is a narrow, steep, and often snow-filled gully or channel on a mountainside.  It is typically formed by erosion and often has steep, rocky walls. |Example:  The climbers carefully navigated the narrow couloir, wary of potential avalanches. 登山者小心翼翼地穿过狭窄的雪道，警惕着潜在的雪崩。

The climbers were challenged by the icy couloir on their ascent.  登山者在攀登过程中受到了冰雪沟的挑战。|近义词：chasm, gully, ravine, chute|反义词：plain, plateau
