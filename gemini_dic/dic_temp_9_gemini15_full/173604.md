quasi-infinitely:/ˈkwɑːzi ɪnˈfɪnɪtli/ | adj. | 近乎无限的，几乎无限的 |  Describes something that is extremely large or extensive, approaching infinity but not actually infinite.  It implies a practical limit, even if that limit is very high. |  The universe appears quasi-infinitely large to our current understanding. 宇宙在我们目前的理解看来近乎无限大。 The number of possible combinations in this game is quasi-infinitely large.  这个游戏中可能的组合数量近乎无限大。 | near-infinite, virtually infinite | finite, limited
