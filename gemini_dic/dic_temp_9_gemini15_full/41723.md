corrodent:/kəˈroʊdənt/ | adj. | 腐蚀性的，侵蚀性的 |  Something that is corrosive; having the power to wear away or destroy a substance gradually by chemical action.  | Example:  The corrodent acid ate through the metal pipe.  腐蚀性酸腐蚀了金属管道。
The highly corrodent substance damaged the engine. 高腐蚀性的物质损坏了引擎。|近义词：erosive, caustic, corrosive | 反义词：protective, harmless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The acid corrodes the metal. = 酸腐蚀金属。
This chemical corrodes quickly. = 这种化学物质腐蚀得很快。

一般过去时:
The rain corroded the statue. = 雨水腐蚀了雕像。
The salt water corroded the ship's hull. = 盐水腐蚀了船体。

现在完成时:
The acid has corroded the pipe. = 酸已经腐蚀了管道。
The sea has corroded the cliff face. = 海水已经腐蚀了悬崖峭壁。

过去完成时:
The pipe had corroded before we noticed it. = 我们注意到之前，管道就已经腐蚀了。
The engine had corroded due to prolonged exposure to saltwater. =由于长期暴露在盐水中，发动机已经腐蚀了。

将来时:

The constant dampness will corrode the building's foundations. = 持续的潮湿会腐蚀建筑的地基。
