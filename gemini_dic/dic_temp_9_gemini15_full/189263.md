saddler:/ˈsædlər/| n. |鞍匠；马具制造商|A saddler is a craftsman who makes and repairs saddles and other leather goods for horses.  They often work with leather, stitching, and other specialized tools. |The saddler crafted a beautiful new saddle for the champion racehorse.  鞍匠为冠军赛马制作了一个漂亮的新马鞍。| cobbler, leatherworker |  (无明确反义词)|名词
