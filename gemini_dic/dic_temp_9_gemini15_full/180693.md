regressors:/rɪˈɡrɛsərz/| n.|回归量，回归因子|In statistics, regressors are the independent variables in a regression model.  They are the variables used to predict the dependent variable. For example, in a model predicting house prices, regressors might include square footage, number of bedrooms, and location.|例句：The study used age, income, and education as regressors to predict voting behavior. 这项研究使用年龄、收入和教育作为回归量来预测投票行为。  The multiple linear regression model included several regressors, each representing a different factor influencing the outcome.多元线性回归模型包含多个回归量，每个回归量代表影响结果的不同因素。|近义词：independent variables, predictors, explanatory variables|反义词：dependent variables, response variables
