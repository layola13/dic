non-ascetic: /ˌnɒn əˈsɛtɪk/ | adj. | 非禁欲的；享乐的 | Not practicing or characterized by asceticism; indulging in pleasure or luxury.  |  例句：He led a non-ascetic life, enjoying fine food and travel. 他过着非禁欲的生活，享受美食和旅行。


例句：Their lifestyle is decidedly non-ascetic, filled with lavish parties and expensive vacations. 他们的生活方式绝对是非禁欲的，充满了奢华的派对和昂贵的假期。


近义词：hedonistic, pleasure-seeking, self-indulgent, luxurious


反义词：ascetic, austere, abstemious, spartan


词性：形容词
