cichoraceous: /ˌsɪkəˈreɪʃəs/ | adj. | 菊科植物的；苦苣菜属的 | Relating to or characteristic of the genus *Cichorium*, especially the common chicory (*Cichorium intybus*).  The term often implies a slightly bitter taste or a somewhat coarse texture, reminiscent of chicory. | The cichoraceous plants in the field were a vibrant blue. 田野里的菊科植物呈现出鲜艳的蓝色。  The salad contained several cichoraceous leaves, giving it a slightly bitter edge.沙拉里包含几种菊科植物的叶子，让它带有一丝苦味。 |  近义词：chicory-like, bitter (in the context of taste) | 反义词：sweet (in the context of taste), delicate (in the context of texture)
