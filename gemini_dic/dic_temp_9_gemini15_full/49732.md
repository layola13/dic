democratizing: /dɪˈmɒkrətaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使民主化；普及；大众化 |  The act of making something accessible to or controlled by the common people.  It involves spreading power and influence more widely within a society or organization.  |例句：The internet is democratizing information access.  互联网正在使信息获取民主化。

例句：The company is democratizing its decision-making process by involving employees at all levels.  该公司通过让所有级别的员工参与决策过程来实现决策过程的民主化。


近义词：popularizing, spreading, equalizing, liberalizing

反义词：centralizing, concentrating, restricting, elitizing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is democratizing the healthcare system. = 政府正在使医疗体系民主化。
They are democratizing education through online learning platforms. = 他们正在通过在线学习平台使教育大众化。


一般过去时:
The reforms democratized the political system. =  这些改革使政治制度民主化。
The invention of the printing press democratized the spread of knowledge. = 印刷术的发明使知识的传播民主化。


现在完成时:
Technology has democratized access to information. = 科技已经使人们更容易获取信息。
The movement has democratized access to healthcare in many areas. =  这场运动已经在许多地区使人们更容易获得医疗保健。


过去完成时:
The country had democratized its electoral process before the recent election. =  这个国家在最近的选举之前已经使其选举过程民主化。
They had democratized the education system by the time the new generation entered school. =  当新一代学生入学时，他们已经使教育体系民主化。


将来时:
The new policies will democratize the financial market. =  新的政策将使金融市场民主化。
AI tools will democratize the creation of art and design. = AI工具将使艺术和设计的创作大众化。


虚拟语气:
If we democratized the legal system, more people would have access to justice. = 如果我们使法律体系民主化，更多人将能够获得正义。
I wish they would democratize the access to higher education. = 我希望他们能使高等教育更容易获得。


被动语态:
The educational system is being democratized through various reforms. = 教育体系正在通过各种改革而民主化。
The decision-making process was democratized by involving all stakeholders. = 通过让所有利益相关者参与，决策过程实现了民主化。


疑问句:
Is the government seriously trying to democratize the media? = 政府是否认真尝试使媒体民主化？
How can we democratize the process of wealth distribution? = 我们如何使财富分配过程民主化？


否定句:
The current system does not democratize opportunities fairly. =  目前的制度并没有公平地使机会民主化。
The company did not democratize its decision-making processes effectively. = 公司没有有效地使它的决策过程民主化。


PHRASE->
democratizing education = 使教育民主化
democratizing access = 使获取…更容易
democratizing the workplace = 使工作场所民主化
democratizing technology = 使技术普及
democratizing information = 使信息民主化

词源:  Comes from "democracy" (from Greek demos "people" + kratos "power, rule") + "-izing" (a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become").  It's a relatively modern word reflecting the increasing focus on spreading power and access throughout society.
