ossifiers:/ˈɒsɪfaɪərz/ | n. | 骨化物；使骨化的人或物 |  Substances or processes that cause ossification.  The term often refers to something that becomes rigid and inflexible, losing its flexibility and adaptability.  | 例句：The calcified deposits acted as ossifiers, hardening the arteries.  这些钙化沉积物起到了骨化剂的作用，使动脉硬化。

The slow bureaucratic processes were ossifiers, preventing the company from adapting to the changing market.  缓慢的官僚程序是僵化的因素，阻止了公司适应不断变化的市场。| 近义词：calcifiers, hardeners, rigidifiers | 反义词：softeners, flexiblizers
