endow:/ɪnˈdaʊ/| vt.|赋予；捐赠；资助|To provide or equip (someone or something) with a quality, ability, or asset.|The school was endowed with a large sum of money by a generous benefactor. 这所学校得到一位慷慨的捐助者的巨额捐款。|provide, grant, bestow|deprive, withhold|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The university endows scholarships to promising students. = 大学为有前途的学生提供奖学金。
She endows her paintings with a sense of mystery. = 她赋予她的画作一种神秘感。


一般过去时:
The king endowed the church with vast lands. = 国王将大片土地捐赠给教堂。
He endowed his daughter with a significant inheritance. = 他给女儿留下了可观的遗产。


现在完成时:
The foundation has endowed numerous research projects. = 该基金会已经资助了许多研究项目。
This experience has endowed me with greater resilience. = 这段经历赋予我更大的韧性。


过去完成时:
They had endowed the hospital with state-of-the-art equipment before the opening. = 他们在医院开业前已经为其配备了最先进的设备。
She had endowed her son with a strong sense of responsibility before he left home. = 在儿子离家前，她已经培养了他强烈的责任感。


将来时:
The philanthropist will endow a new wing of the museum. = 慈善家将为博物馆捐赠一个新的侧翼。
The government will endow the project with sufficient funds. = 政府将为该项目提供足够的资金。


虚拟语气:
If the government endowed the arts more generously, we would have more talented artists. = 如果政府对艺术的资助更慷慨，我们将会有更多有才华的艺术家。
I wish the company had endowed me with more training opportunities. = 我希望公司能给我更多培训机会。


被动语态:
The hospital was endowed with advanced medical technology. = 医院配备了先进的医疗技术。
The scholarship was endowed by an anonymous donor. = 这笔奖学金由一位匿名捐赠者捐赠。


疑问句:
Does the company endow its employees with stock options? = 公司是否为其员工提供股票期权？
Will the government endow this research project? = 政府会资助这个研究项目吗？


否定句:
The government did not endow the project with sufficient funding. = 政府没有为该项目提供足够的资金。
The artist did not endow his work with much emotion. = 这位艺术家并没有在他的作品中注入太多情感。
