omnivore:/ˈɒmnɪvɔːr/ /ˈɑːmnɪvɔːr/| n. |杂食动物|An animal that eats both plants and animals.  Omnivores have digestive systems that can process both types of food.  They play an important role in various ecosystems. |Example: Bears are omnivores; they eat berries, fish, and other animals. 熊是杂食动物；它们吃浆果、鱼和其他动物。  Humans are also omnivores. 人类也是杂食动物。|近义词：generalist, feeder|反义词：herbivore, carnivore
