hamamelis:/ˌhæməˈmɪlɪs/| n.|金缕梅|Hamamelis is a genus of flowering plants in the family Hamamelidaceae, commonly known as witch hazel.  The genus includes several species, some of which are cultivated for their medicinal and ornamental properties.  They are known for their distinctive flowers which bloom in late autumn or winter.|The witch hazel extract is often used in skin care products. |近义词:witch hazel|反义词: N/A
