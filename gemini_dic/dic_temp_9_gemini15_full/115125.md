mangler:/ˈmæŋɡlər/ | vt. n. |损坏；弄糟；歪曲；毁坏者 | To severely damage or mutilate something; to spoil or ruin something; a machine used to smooth and iron laundry. |例句：The storm mangled the roof of the house.  暴风雨严重破坏了房子的屋顶。
The newspaper mangled the facts in its report. 报纸歪曲了报道中的事实。


近义词：mutilate, damage, ruin, spoil, butcher | 反义词：repair, restore, improve
