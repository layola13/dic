escaper:/ɪˈskeɪpər/| n. |逃脱者，逃犯|A person who escapes from a place, especially a prison or a dangerous situation.|例句：The escaper was eventually caught by the police.  逃脱者最终被警方抓获。|例句：He was a daring escaper, known for his clever plans. 他是一个大胆的逃脱者，以其巧妙的计划而闻名。|近义词：fugitive, runaway, escapee|反义词：captive, prisoner


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The prisoner escapes every night.  = 囚犯每晚都越狱。
She escapes her boring job by reading books. = 她通过看书来逃避她无聊的工作。

一般过去时:
The bird escaped from its cage. = 那只鸟从笼子里逃了出来。
He escaped serious injury in the accident. = 他在事故中逃过一劫。

现在完成时:
He has escaped from prison twice. = 他两次越狱。
She has escaped the clutches of poverty. = 她摆脱了贫困的魔爪。

过去完成时:
He had escaped before the police arrived. = 警员到达之前他已经逃跑了。
They had escaped the burning building. = 他们已经逃出了燃烧的建筑物。

将来时:
He will escape punishment. = 他将会逃脱惩罚。
They will escape to the mountains. = 他们将逃到山上。

虚拟语气:
If I were him, I would escape. = 如果我是他，我会逃跑。
I wish I could escape this feeling. = 我真希望我能摆脱这种感觉。

被动语态:
The criminal was escaped from jail. = 罪犯越狱了。(注意：此句被动语态略显不自然，更常用的表达是"The criminal escaped from jail.")
The secret was escaped from the press. = 这个秘密没有被媒体泄露。(同样，更常用的表达是"The press didn't reveal the secret.")

疑问句:
Did he escape? = 他逃脱了吗？
How did she escape? = 她是怎么逃脱的？

否定句:
He did not escape the consequences. = 他没有逃避后果。
She couldn't escape her responsibilities. = 她无法逃避她的责任。


PHRASE->
escape from = 从...逃脱
escape to = 逃到...
escape notice = 未被注意到
escape detection = 未被发现
narrow escape = 九死一生
