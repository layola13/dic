irradiators:/ɪˈreɪdieɪtərz/| n.| 辐射装置；照射器 | Devices that emit radiation, often used in various industrial and medical applications.  They can be used to sterilize medical equipment, food, or other materials, or for other radiation-based treatments.|例句：The food processing plant uses powerful irradiators to sterilize its products. 食品加工厂使用强大的辐射装置来灭菌其产品。|例句：Medical irradiators are carefully regulated to ensure safety. 医疗用辐射装置受到严格监管以确保安全。|近义词：radiators, emitters |反义词：shields, absorbers
