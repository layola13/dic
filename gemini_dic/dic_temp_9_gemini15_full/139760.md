octad:/ˈɒkteɪd/ | n. |八重奏；八个一组 | A group or set of eight; a musical composition for eight voices or instruments.  |Example: The octet performed a beautiful rendition of the Mozart piece. 八重奏演奏了莫扎特的一首优美的乐曲。|近义词：octet, group of eight |反义词：None specifically, but could be considered opposite to a smaller group like a quartet or trio.
