nonflagrant:/ˈnɒnˈfleɪɡrənt/| adj.|非恶意的；不明显的；不显著的|Not blatant or obvious; not scandalous or outrageous.  It implies a lack of clear evidence or intention to cause harm or offense.  The opposite of flagrant.|The judge found the evidence against the defendant nonflagrant, leading to his acquittal.  法官发现针对被告的证据不明显，导致他被无罪释放。|近义词：subtle, inconspicuous, unobtrusive, understated|反义词：flagrant, blatant, obvious, glaring
