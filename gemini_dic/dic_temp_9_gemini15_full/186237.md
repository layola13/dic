rhinoceroses:/raɪˈnɑːsərəʊsɪz/| n. |犀牛复数|The plural of rhinoceros.  It refers to more than one rhinoceros.  The word is often used in scientific contexts or when specifically referring to multiple individuals.|例句：Several rhinoceroses were spotted near the watering hole.  几只犀牛在水坑附近被发现。|近义词：rhinos (informal)|反义词：无
