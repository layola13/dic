eyepatch:/ˈaɪpætʃ/| n. |眼罩|A patch worn over one eye, often for medical reasons or as a costume accessory.  It's typically a piece of cloth or other material. |例句：The pirate wore a black eyepatch over his left eye. 海盗在他的左眼上戴着黑色眼罩。|例句：After his eye surgery, he had to wear an eyepatch for a few weeks.  他的眼科手术后，他不得不戴几周的眼罩。|近义词：eye shield, eye cover |反义词：None specifically, but "contact lens" could be considered a functional opposite in some contexts.
