obstetrics:/ɒbˈstɛtrɪks/ | n. |妇产科学 |The branch of medicine and surgery concerned with childbirth and the care of women during pregnancy and childbirth.  |例句：She's studying obstetrics at university. 她正在大学学习妇产科学。|例句：The hospital has a renowned obstetrics department. 这家医院拥有著名的妇产科部门。|近义词：midwifery (助产学), tocology (产科学，现已较少使用)|反义词：无
