shuffling:/ˈʃʌflɪŋ/| v. n. adj.| 拖着脚走；洗牌；混乱；|拖着脚走；洗牌；混乱；|例句：He was shuffling along the street. 他沿着街道拖着脚走。The magician shuffled the cards skillfully. 魔术师熟练地洗牌。|近义词：dragging, scrambling, mixing|反义词：marching, orderly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shuffles his feet nervously. = 他紧张地拖着脚走。
They shuffle the deck before each game. = 他们每局游戏前都洗牌。


一般过去时:
She shuffled the papers on her desk. = 她把桌上的文件弄乱了。
He shuffled away, avoiding eye contact. = 他拖着脚走开了，避免眼神接触。


现在完成时:
He has shuffled the cards several times. = 他已经洗了几次牌了。
They have shuffled the evidence to hide the truth. = 他们已经篡改证据来掩盖真相。


过去完成时:

将来时:
