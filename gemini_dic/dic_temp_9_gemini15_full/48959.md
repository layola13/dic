degelling:/dɪˈdʒɛlɪŋ/ | n. | 脱胶；去胶；除胶 | The process of removing gel or a gel-like substance.  It often refers to removing a gel from a surface or material. |Example: The degelling of the petroleum jelly was necessary before further processing.  脱胶过程在进一步加工之前是必要的。|近义词：degelation, ungelling |反义词：gelling, gelation
