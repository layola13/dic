nicknackery:/ˈnɪkˌnækəri/| n. |小玩意儿；小装饰品；琐碎物品 |The word "nicknackery" refers to a collection of small, often insignificant or trivial objects, typically decorative or ornamental.  It suggests a somewhat cluttered or disorganized accumulation of such items.  The term carries a slightly whimsical or slightly derogatory connotation, implying a lack of order or importance. |The attic was filled with years of accumulated nicknackery.|近义词：trinkets, knick-knacks, gewgaws, gimcracks |反义词：essentials, necessities

例句：

Her house was full of nicknackery. = 她的房子里摆满了小玩意儿。

The shop sold various kinds of nicknackery. = 这家商店出售各种各样的小饰品。


注意：nicknackery本身不包含动词、形容词等其他词性变化。  其用法主要局限于名词。  也没有相应的语法时态变化需要解释。  以下部分根据要求补充，但并不适用于nicknackery本身。
