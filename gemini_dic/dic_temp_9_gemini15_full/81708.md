greening:/ˈɡriːnɪŋ/| n. v.|绿化；变绿；环保；|The process of making something greener, environmentally friendly, or more sustainable.  It can refer to the act of planting trees and vegetation, adopting environmentally conscious practices, or transitioning to renewable energy sources.|例句：The city council is committed to the greening of public spaces.  市议会致力于公共场所的绿化。

The company's greening initiatives have reduced its carbon footprint. 该公司的环保举措减少了其碳足迹。|近义词：environmentalization, ecologization|反义词：deforestation, desertification|名词,动词

词源:
The word "greening" comes directly from the adjective "green," meaning having the color of growing plants, and implying freshness, youth, and environmental friendliness.  The suffix "-ing" transforms the adjective into a noun or verb, indicating the process or act of becoming green or making something green.  The evolution of its meaning reflects the increasing global concern for environmental sustainability.
