coops:/kuːps/| n.|鸡舍；合作社|A coop is a small, simple structure, typically made of wood, used to house poultry, especially chickens.  It can also refer to a cooperative organization. |例句：The chickens are safe in their coop.  鸡们在鸡舍里很安全。

例句：The farmers formed a coop to sell their produce. 农民们组建了一个合作社来销售他们的农产品。|近义词：henhouse, poultry house,  cooperative, association |反义词：N/A
