in-bed: /ɪnˈbɛd/ | adj. | 床上的；在床上的 |  Describes something or someone that is in or on a bed.  It often implies a state of rest or sleep, but not always. | 例句：The baby is in-bed. = 婴儿在床上。  The book is in-bed with me. = 这本书在我床上。 | 近义词：on the bed, asleep, bedded | 反义词：out of bed, awake


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat is in-bed. = 猫在床上。
The book remains in-bed. = 书还在床上。

一般过去时:
The children were in-bed early last night. = 昨晚孩子们很早就睡了。
The letter was in-bed with the other documents. = 这封信和其他文件放在一起。

现在完成时:
The dog has been in-bed all day. = 这条狗整天都在床上。
The project has been in-bed for review.  = 这个项目已经提交审查了。


过去完成时:
The patient had been in-bed for a week before the surgery. = 患者在手术前已经卧床一周了。
The documents had been in-bed since the meeting. = 文件自从会议之后就放在床上了。


将来时:
She will be in-bed by ten o'clock. = 她十点钟之前会睡在床上。
The report will be in-bed with the others by Friday. = 报告会在周五之前和其他的报告放在一起。


虚拟语气:
If she were in-bed, she would be resting. = 如果她在床上，她就会休息。
I wish the problem were in-bed and solved. = 我希望问题已经解决。


被动语态:
The clothes were in-bed carefully. = 衣服被小心地放在床上。
The documents were in-bed for safekeeping. = 文件被妥善保管。


疑问句:
Is the baby in-bed? = 婴儿在床上吗？
Is the proposal in-bed for consideration? = 这个提案正在考虑中吗？


否定句:
The cat is not in-bed. = 猫不在床上。
The plan is not in-bed yet. = 计划还没有被正式提出。


PHRASE->
in-bed with (someone/something): 与…狼狈为奸；与…勾结
in-bed with illness: 卧病在床
in-bed and breakfast: 住宿加早餐
stay in-bed: 卧床休息
get in-bed: 上床睡觉
