guardroom:/ˈɡɑːdruːm/ | n. |警卫室，值班室 |A room where guards are stationed, often in a military or security context.  It's a place for security personnel to rest, keep watch, and store equipment. |例句：The guardroom was dimly lit, and the guards were playing cards. 警卫室灯光昏暗，警卫们在玩牌。 The sergeant checked in at the guardroom every hour.  中士每小时都在警卫室报到。|近义词：guardhouse, watchroom, sentry box |反义词：open area, public space
