gingle:/ˈdʒɪŋɡəl/| n.|小铃声，短歌，广告歌|A short, catchy tune, especially one used in advertising or as part of a children's rhyme.|例句：The jingle for the new soda is incredibly catchy.  新的苏打水的广告歌非常朗朗上口。|例句：The children sang a simple jingle about the farm animals.孩子们唱了一首关于农场动物的简单小歌。|近义词：tune, ditty, jingle bell|反义词：long song, aria
