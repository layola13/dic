constance:/ˈkɒnstəns/ /ˈkɑːnstəns/| n.|恒定；坚定；忠贞；[女子名]康斯坦斯|Constancy refers to the state or quality of being unchanging or unwavering. It implies steadiness, firmness, and reliability.  It can be used to describe a person's character, a physical phenomenon, or a situation. |例句：Her constancy in the face of adversity was admirable.  她面对逆境时的坚定令人钦佩。

例句：The constancy of the North Star has guided sailors for centuries. 北极星的恒定指引了水手几个世纪。


近义词：steadfastness, firmness, stability, perseverance, faithfulness, loyalty|反义词：inconsistency, inconstancy, fickleness, instability, volatility|词性:名词
