offertories:/ˈɒfərtəriːz/| n. |奉献物；献礼；圣餐礼献钱；献诗| Offerings made to a church or religious institution, especially during a religious service.  It can also refer to the musical piece played during the offering of alms. |例句：The offertories collected during the service will go to the local charity.  奉献仪式上收集的捐款将捐给当地慈善机构。

The offertories included beautiful flowers and generous donations.  奉献物包括美丽的鲜花和慷慨的捐款。|近义词：contributions, donations, offerings |反义词：takings(收入)
