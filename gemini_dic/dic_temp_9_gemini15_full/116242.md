mass-marketing:/ˈmæs ˈmɑːrkɪtɪŋ/| n. |大众市场营销|The practice of selling goods or services to a large number of people, typically through advertising on television, radio, and the internet.  It aims to reach a broad audience with a standardized product or message, rather than targeting specific customer segments.|例句：The company employed mass-marketing techniques to launch its new product. 这家公司采用大众市场营销手段来推出其新产品。  Many consumer goods companies rely on mass-marketing strategies. 许多消费品公司依靠大众市场营销策略。|近义词：broadcasting,  general marketing, undifferentiated marketing|反义词：niche marketing, targeted marketing, personalized marketing|名词
