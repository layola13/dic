harmonized:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪzd/| vt. adj.| 使协调；和谐的；统一的 | To bring into harmony or agreement; to make consistent or uniform. |例句：The two countries harmonized their trade policies. = 这两个国家协调了它们的贸易政策。 The colors in the painting are beautifully harmonized. = 画中的颜色和谐美丽。|近义词：coordinated, unified, synchronized |反义词：discordant, conflicting, disparate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee harmonizes the different viewpoints. = 委员会协调不同的观点。
She harmonizes her work and family life. = 她协调工作和家庭生活。


一般过去时:
They harmonized the conflicting interests of the parties involved. = 他们协调了相关各方的冲突利益。
The orchestra harmonized the different instruments beautifully. = 管弦乐队和谐地演奏了不同的乐器。


现在完成时:

虚拟语气:
