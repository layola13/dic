prognathi:/prɒɡˈnæθi/| adj.|前突的；前颌突出的|Having a projecting jaw or chin.  The term often refers to a condition where the jaw protrudes excessively.|例句：The patient presented with prognathic features, a prominent characteristic of certain genetic disorders.  这位病人表现出前突的特征，这是某些遗传疾病的突出特征。


例句：His prognathic jaw made chewing difficult.  他的前突下巴使咀嚼变得困难。
