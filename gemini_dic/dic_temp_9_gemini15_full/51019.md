desks:/dɛsks/| n.|书桌，办公桌|A desk is a piece of furniture with a flat surface and usually drawers, used for writing or working at.|例句：There are three desks in the office. 办公室有三张书桌。

例句：He sat at his desk and started to work. 他坐在书桌前开始工作。|近义词：table, writing table|反义词：无|名词
