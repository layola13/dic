in turn:/ɪn tɜːrn/| adv.|轮流地；反过来；因而；结果|In turn means one after another in a particular order, or as a consequence of something. It can also suggest a reciprocal relationship.|例句：We each took turns speaking. 我们轮流发言。

例句：The rain caused flooding, which in turn damaged many homes. 雨水导致洪水，进而损坏了许多房屋。

近义词：in sequence, consequently, therefore, reciprocally|反义词：simultaneously, independently, at once|词性：副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They take turns cleaning the house. = 他们轮流打扫房子。
The students answer questions in turn. = 学生们轮流回答问题。


一般过去时:
We took turns driving. = 我们轮流开车。
The children answered the questions in turn. = 孩子们轮流回答问题。


现在完成时:
They have taken turns caring for their aging parents. = 他们轮流照顾他们年迈的父母。
The team members have presented their findings in turn. = 团队成员们轮流展示了他们的发现。


过去完成时:
They had taken turns watching the baby before the parents arrived. = 父母到达之前，他们轮流照看婴儿。
The witnesses had given their testimonies in turn before the judge. = 证人们在法官面前依次作证。


将来时:
We will take turns cooking dinner this week. = 我们这周将轮流做饭。
The speakers will present their ideas in turn at the conference. = 演讲者们将在会议上轮流陈述他们的想法。


虚拟语气:
If we took turns, the work would be easier. = 如果我们轮流做，工作就会更容易。
If they had taken turns, the project would have been completed sooner. = 如果他们轮流做，项目本可以早点完成。


被动语态:
The tasks were completed in turn by the team members. = 任务由团队成员轮流完成。
The questions were answered in turn by each student. = 问题由每个学生轮流回答。


疑问句:
Do they take turns mowing the lawn? = 他们轮流割草吗？
Will the candidates speak in turn during the debate? = 在辩论中候选人会轮流发言吗？


否定句:
They did not take turns; one person did all the work. = 他们没有轮流做；一个人做了所有工作。
The students did not answer the questions in turn; they all spoke at once. = 学生们没有轮流回答问题；他们同时都说了。
