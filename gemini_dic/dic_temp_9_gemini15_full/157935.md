planers:/ˈpleɪnərz/ | n. |刨床；刨工；平滑器 |A planer is a machine used to plane (smooth or flatten) surfaces, typically wood or metal.  It uses cutting tools to remove material, creating a flat and even finish.|例句：The carpenter used a planer to smooth the rough edges of the wood. 木匠用刨床来刨平木材的粗糙边缘。The factory uses several planers for mass production of flat metal sheets. 工厂使用多台刨床进行金属板材的批量生产。|近义词：shaper, jointer |反义词：roughener (反义词较为宽泛，因为“刨平”的反义词是“弄粗糙”)
