shrimati:/ʃrɪˈmɑːtiː/| n. | 施里玛蒂（印度教妇女尊称）| A respectful title used before the name of a married Hindu woman.  It is equivalent to "Mrs." in English, but carries a more formal and respectful connotation. |例句：Shrimati Indira Gandhi was a prominent figure in Indian politics.  施里玛蒂英迪拉·甘地是印度政坛上的重要人物。|近义词：Mrs., Madam (in some contexts)|反义词:None.


USAGE->
This word is a title and doesn't have verb conjugations.  It's used as a prefix before a woman's name.


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases directly using "Shrimati" as it's a title.  It's always used with a name.  For example: Shrimati Sharma, Shrimati Patel, etc.
