clear-up: /klɪər ʌp/ | vt. n.|清理，澄清，解决 | To make something clean and tidy; to remove something that is unwanted or unpleasant; to make something easier to understand. |例句：I need to clear up my desk. 我需要清理我的桌子。


例句：The police are trying to clear up the mystery surrounding the disappearance.  警方正在努力查清失踪案背后的谜团。


近义词：tidy up, clean up, resolve, clarify, explain


反义词：mess up, confuse, complicate


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He clears up his room every day. = 他每天都打扫他的房间。
We clear up the kitchen after dinner. = 我们晚饭后清理厨房。


一般过去时:
She cleared up the spilled milk. = 她清理了洒出的牛奶。
They cleared up the misunderstanding. = 他们澄清了误解。


现在完成时:
I have cleared up all the mess. = 我已经清理了所有的杂乱。
He has cleared up the confusion. = 他已经消除了混淆。


过去完成时:
She had cleared up the kitchen before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经打扫干净了厨房。
They had cleared up the problem before the meeting. = 会议前他们已经解决了这个问题。


将来时:
I will clear up the garden tomorrow. = 我明天将清理花园。
We will clear up this matter soon. = 我们很快就会解决这件事。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would clear up the garage. = 如果我有更多时间，我会清理车库。
I wish I could clear up this misunderstanding. = 我希望我能消除这个误解。


被动语态:
The mess was cleared up quickly. = 杂乱很快就被清理干净了。
The misunderstanding will be cleared up soon. = 误解很快就会被澄清。


疑问句:
Have you cleared up your room yet? = 你已经打扫好你的房间了吗？
Will you clear up the table after lunch? = 午餐后你会清理桌子吗？


否定句:
He didn't clear up his toys. = 他没有收拾他的玩具。
She hasn't cleared up the misunderstanding yet. = 她还没有澄清误解。


PHRASE->
clear up a misunderstanding = 消除误解
clear up a problem = 解决问题
clear up the mess = 清理混乱
clear up the room = 打扫房间
clear up after someone = 为某人善后


词源:
The word "clear" comes from Old English *clēor, meaning "bright, pure, clear". The suffix "-up" indicates a completion or thoroughness of the action.  Therefore, "clear-up" literally means to make something bright, pure, and completely clean.
