haggis:/ˈhækɪs/| n.|羊杂碎|A savoury pudding containing sheep's pluck (heart, liver, and lungs), minced with onion, oatmeal, suet, spices, and traditionally enclosed in a sheep's stomach before boiling or baking.| Haggis is a traditional Scottish dish.  羊杂碎是苏格兰传统菜肴。|近义词：pudding (in a broader sense)|反义词：无
