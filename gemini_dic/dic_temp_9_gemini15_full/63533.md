enuresis: /ˌɛnjʊˈriːsɪs/ | n. |遗尿症，夜尿症 | Involuntary urination, especially by children at night, after the age at which bladder control is normally acquired.  It is a medical condition that can be caused by various factors, including physical problems, psychological issues, and sleep disorders. |The doctor diagnosed her son with enuresis.  医生诊断她儿子患有遗尿症。|  bedwetting, nocturnal enuresis |continence, bladder control |名词
