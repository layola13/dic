oxygenizable: /ˌɒksɪdʒɪˈneɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可氧化；可被氧化的 | Capable of being oxidized or combining with oxygen.  This term describes substances that can readily react with oxygen, often undergoing a chemical change. |

The iron in this compound is readily oxygenizable.  这个化合物中的铁很容易被氧化。

The high reactivity of this material makes it highly oxygenizable.  这种材料的高反应活性使其极易被氧化。

近义词：oxidizable

反义词：unoxidizable
