expropriate:/ɪkˈsproʊprɪeɪt/ | vt. | 征用；剥夺；挪用 | To take possession of (something, especially property) without the owner's consent, typically for public use or for the benefit of the state.  |  The government expropriated land for the new highway. 政府征用了这块地用于修建新公路。 | seize, confiscate, commandeer | restore, return |动词

一般现在时:
The government expropriates land for public projects. = 政府征用土地用于公共项目。
They often expropriate resources without compensation. = 他们经常无偿征用资源。


一般过去时:
The king expropriated the farmer's land. = 国王征用了农民的土地。
The company expropriated the mineral rights. = 公司征用了矿产权利。


现在完成时:
The state has expropriated vast tracts of land. = 国家已经征用了大片土地。
They have expropriated the assets of the failing company. = 他们已经征用了这家即将倒闭公司的资产。


过去完成时:
The government had expropriated the property before the owner could react. = 在业主做出反应之前，政府已经征用了该房产。
The rebels had expropriated all the food supplies. = 叛乱分子已经征用了所有食品供应。


将来时:
The city will expropriate the building for a new park. = 该市将征用该建筑物用于新建公园。
The government will likely expropriate more land for infrastructure projects. = 政府可能会为基础设施项目征用更多土地。


虚拟语气:
If the government expropriated that land, there would be a public outcry. = 如果政府征用那块土地，将会引起公众强烈抗议。
I wish they hadn't expropriated my family's ancestral home. = 我真希望他们没有征用我们家的祖传房屋。


被动语态:
The land was expropriated for the construction of a new railway. = 这块土地被征用用于修建新的铁路。
The factory's assets were expropriated by the government. = 工厂的资产被政府征用了。


疑问句:
Was the property expropriated legally? = 这处房产是否合法被征用？
Will the government expropriate more land in the future? = 政府将来还会征用更多土地吗？


否定句:
The government did not expropriate the land without compensation. = 政府并非无偿征用这块土地。
The company will not expropriate the property without due process. = 公司不会在未经正当程序的情况下征用该房产。
