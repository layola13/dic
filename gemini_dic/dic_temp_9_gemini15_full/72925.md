fondish:/ˈfɒndɪʃ/| adj.|略微喜欢的，有点喜欢的|Slightly fond; having a slight liking for something or someone.  It suggests a less intense feeling than "fond."|例句：I'm fondish of chocolate, but I wouldn't say I'm addicted. 我有点喜欢巧克力，但我不会说我上瘾了。


例句：She's fondish of her new neighbor, but hasn't gotten to know them well yet. 她有点喜欢她的新邻居，但还没真正了解他们。


近义词：somewhat fond of, rather fond of, slightly partial to|反义词：disliking, averse to, indifferent to|词性:adjective
