gain:/ɡeɪn/ | n./v. | 获得，增加；收益；利润 | To obtain or acquire something desired or valuable; to increase in amount, size, or degree. |例句：She gained a lot of experience working abroad. 她通过在国外工作获得了许多经验。
The company gained a significant market share. 公司获得了可观的市场份额。 |近义词：acquire, obtain, achieve, earn |反义词：lose, forfeit, miss


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gains weight easily. = 他很容易增重。
She gains confidence through practice. = 她通过练习获得了自信。

一般过去时:
The team gained a victory in the final match. = 球队在决赛中获得了胜利。
They gained valuable insights from the research. = 他们从研究中获得了宝贵的见解。

现在完成时:
I have gained a better understanding of the problem. = 我对这个问题有了更好的理解。
She has gained considerable weight since she stopped exercising. = 她自从停止锻炼以来体重增加了许多。

过去完成时:
He had gained a reputation as a talented artist before he moved to Paris. = 他在搬到巴黎之前就已经获得了天才艺术家的名声。
They had gained significant experience in the field before starting their own business. = 他们在创办自己的公司之前，已经在该领域积累了丰富的经验。

将来时:
We will gain a competitive advantage with this new technology. = 通过这项新技术，我们将获得竞争优势。
She will gain valuable skills during her internship. = 在实习期间，她将获得宝贵的技能。

虚拟语气:
If I worked harder, I would gain more knowledge. = 如果我更努力工作，我就能获得更多知识。
I wish I had gained more experience before taking on this project. = 我希望在承担这个项目之前，我已经积累了更多经验。

被动语态:
The knowledge was gained through years of study. = 这些知识是通过多年的学习获得的。
A lot of weight was gained during the holiday. = 假期期间体重增加了许多。

疑问句:
What did you gain from the experience? = 你从这次经历中得到了什么？
What will we gain by investing in this project? = 我们投资这个项目将获得什么？

否定句:
He didn't gain any weight. = 他没有增重。
The company did not gain any profit that year. = 那一年公司没有盈利。


PHRASE->
gain access to: 获得进入…的权限
gain control of: 控制
gain momentum: 获得动力
gain ground: 进步；取得进展
gain insight into: 了解；洞悉
