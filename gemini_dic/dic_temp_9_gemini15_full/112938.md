lugging:/ˈlʌɡɪŋ/| v.| 费力地拖拉；吃力地搬运 |The act of carrying or dragging something heavy with difficulty.  It implies strenuous effort and often suggests the weight is cumbersome or the distance is considerable.| 例句：Lugging those heavy suitcases up the stairs was exhausting.  费力地把那些沉重的行李箱搬上楼梯真累人。 He was lugging a large sack of potatoes. 他吃力地扛着一大袋土豆。|近义词：dragging, hauling, transporting, carrying|反义词：lifting (lightly), carrying (easily)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lugs groceries home every week. = 他每周都费力地把杂货扛回家。
They are lugging heavy equipment to the construction site. = 他们正在费力地把重型设备搬运到建筑工地。

一般过去时:
She lugged the old piano down the stairs. = 她费力地把那架旧钢琴搬下楼。
We lugged our backpacks through the muddy trail. = 我们费力地背着背包穿过泥泞的小路。

现在完成时:
He has lugged all the furniture into the new house. = 他已经把所有的家具都搬进了新房子。
They have lugged countless boxes across the yard. = 他们已经把无数的箱子搬过了院子。

过去完成时:
They had lugged the supplies for miles before they found a suitable campsite. = 在找到合适的营地之前，他们已经费力地把物资搬运了好几英里。
She had lugged the heavy bag all day, resulting in a sore shoulder. = 她一整天都费力地背着沉重的包，导致肩膀酸痛。

将来时:
We will lug the tent and sleeping bags to the mountain tomorrow. = 我们明天将把帐篷和睡袋搬到山上。
They will be lugging the harvest home after the long day in the fields. = 长时间在田地里劳作后，他们将把收成搬回家。

虚拟语气:
If I had known it was so heavy, I wouldn't have lugged it myself. = 如果我知道它这么重，我就不会自己搬了。
I wish I hadn't lugged that heavy box up the stairs. = 我真希望我没有把那个沉重的箱子搬上楼梯。

被动语态:
The heavy boxes were lugged into the warehouse by the workers. = 那些沉重的箱子被工人们搬进了仓库。
The equipment is being lugged across the field by a tractor. = 设备正在由拖拉机拖过田地。

疑问句:
Are you lugging that heavy box all by yourself? = 你一个人费力地搬那个沉重的箱子吗？
What are they lugging through the forest? = 他们在森林里费力地搬运什么？

否定句:
He didn't lug the boxes himself; he hired a helper. = 他没有自己搬箱子；他雇了一个帮手。
They wouldn't lug such heavy things without help. = 他们不会在没有帮助的情况下搬运这么重的东西。

lug something along: 拖着某物走
lug something off: 费力地把某物搬走
