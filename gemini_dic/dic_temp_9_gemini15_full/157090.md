pinacate:/ˌpɪnəˈkɑːteɪ/| n.|  仙人掌鼠；平顶火山；(尤指墨西哥索诺拉沙漠的)  一种小型火山| A pinacate is a small, flat-topped volcano, especially one found in the Sonoran Desert of Mexico.  The term can also refer to the desert tortoise, *Gopherus morafkai*, sometimes called the Sonoran gopher tortoise. | The Sonoran Desert is known for its pinacates.  索诺拉沙漠以其平顶火山而闻名。|  volcano, desert tortoise |  (无明显反义词)|名词
