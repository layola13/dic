incubator: /ˈɪŋkjuˌbeɪtər/ | n. |孵化器；培养箱；创业孵化器 |

英文解析:A device or environment that provides the necessary conditions for something to develop or grow, such as eggs, bacteria, or new businesses.  It can refer to a physical apparatus or a metaphorical environment that fosters growth.

例句:

The farmer used an incubator to hatch the chicken eggs. = 农民用孵化器孵小鸡的蛋。

The university's business incubator helped several startups succeed. = 大学的创业孵化器帮助几家初创企业成功。


近义词:hatchery, nursery, breeding ground


反义词:inhibitor, suppressor


词性:名词
