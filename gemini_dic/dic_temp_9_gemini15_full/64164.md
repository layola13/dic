equanimities:/ˌiːkwəˈnɪmətiz/| n. |平静；沉着；泰然自若|The ability to remain calm and even-tempered, especially in a difficult situation.  It often refers to a state of mental calmness, composure, and evenness of temper, especially under stress.|She faced the crisis with remarkable equanimity.|近义词：calmness, composure, serenity, tranquility|反义词：agitation, anxiety, panic, distress|
