prenomination: /ˌpriːˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 预提名，预先提名 | The act of nominating someone or something before the official nomination process.  It often involves informal discussions or preliminary selections.  | Example: The prenomination of candidates for the mayoral race began several months before the official announcement.  = 市长竞选候选人的预提名在正式宣布前几个月就开始了。|近义词：preselection, preliminary nomination |反义词：postnomination
