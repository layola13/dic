perspirability: /ˌpɜːrspɪrəˈbɪləti/ | n. |透气性，排汗性 | The ability of a fabric or material to allow sweat or moisture to pass through it.  This property is crucial for comfort, especially in clothing and footwear.  | Example: The new fabric boasts superior perspirability, keeping you cool and dry even during intense workouts.  新的面料具有优越的透气性，即使在剧烈运动中也能保持凉爽和干燥。 |近义词: breathability, ventilation |反义词: impermeability, airtightness
