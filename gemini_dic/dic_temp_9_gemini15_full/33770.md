circulators:/ˈsɜːrkjuleɪtərz/ | n. |循环器，循环装置 | Devices that facilitate the circulation of fluids (like blood, air, or coolant) or other materials.  They are used in various applications, from medical devices to industrial processes. |Example:  The heart is a vital circulator, pumping blood throughout the body. 心脏是重要的循环器，将血液泵送到全身。

The cooling system relies on a series of circulators to maintain optimal temperature. 冷却系统依靠一系列循环器来维持最佳温度。|近义词：pumps, conveyors |反义词：obstructors, blockages
