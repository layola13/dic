The team rallies around their leader. = 团队团结在领导周围。
He rallies his troops before the battle. = 他在战斗前激励他的军队。


一般过去时:
The people rallied against the government's decision. = 人们抗议政府的决定。
The price of the stock rallied after a period of decline. = 股价在经历一段时间的下跌后反弹。

疑问句:

否定句:
