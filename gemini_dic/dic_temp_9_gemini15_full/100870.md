intruder:/ɪnˈtruːdər/| n. |入侵者，闯入者|A person who enters a place without permission.  An intruder broke into the house last night.  昨晚有个入侵者闯进了房子。|近义词：trespasser, invader, burglar |反义词：owner, resident
