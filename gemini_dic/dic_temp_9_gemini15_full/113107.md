lunchrooms:/ˈlʌntʃruːmz/| n.|员工餐厅，午餐室|A lunchroom is a room where people can eat lunch, typically provided by an employer for their employees.  It's often equipped with tables, chairs, and sometimes a refrigerator and microwave.|Example: The company lunchroom is always busy around noon. 公司餐厅中午总是很忙。  The new lunchroom has improved employee morale. 新的员工餐厅提高了员工的士气。|近义词：cafeteria, dining hall|反义词：None
