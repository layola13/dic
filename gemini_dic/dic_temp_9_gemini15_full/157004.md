pillorize:/ˈpɪləraɪz/| vt.|公开谴责；严厉批评|To attack or criticize (someone or something) severely and publicly.|The politician was pilloried in the press for his controversial remarks.  这位政治家因其有争议的言论而受到媒体的严厉批评。|近义词：criticize, condemn, denounce, lambaste|反义词：praise, commend, applaud


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They pillory him for his unpopular opinions. = 他们因他不受欢迎的观点而公开谴责他。
The media pillories the company for its unethical practices. = 媒体因其不道德的行为而严厉批评该公司。


一般过去时:
The newspaper pilloried the mayor for his mismanagement of funds. = 报纸因市长资金管理不善而公开谴责他。
Critics pilloried the movie for its poor plot and acting. = 批评家因其糟糕的情节和表演而严厉批评这部电影。


现在完成时:
The public has pilloried the CEO for his involvement in the scandal. = 公众因其卷入丑闻而公开谴责了首席执行官。
The artist has been pilloried for years for his controversial artwork. = 多年来，这位艺术家一直因其有争议的艺术作品而受到严厉批评。


过去完成时:
They had pilloried him before for similar mistakes. = 他们以前也因类似的错误而公开谴责过他。
The press had already pilloried the company for its previous scandals. = 媒体已经因该公司之前的丑闻而严厉批评该公司。


将来时:
The media will surely pillory him for his recent actions. = 媒体肯定会因他最近的行为而公开谴责他。
They will pillory the new policy for its unfairness. = 他们会因为这项新政策的不公平而严厉批评它。


虚拟语气:
If he made such a mistake, he would be pilloried by the public. = 如果他犯了这样的错误，他会被公众公开谴责。
I wish they hadn't pilloried him so harshly. = 我真希望他们不要对他如此严厉地批评。


被动语态:
He was pilloried for his lack of judgment. = 他因缺乏判断力而受到公开谴责。
The new law was pilloried by human rights activists. = 人权活动家严厉批评了这项新法律。


疑问句:
Will he be pilloried for his actions? = 他会因为他的行为而受到公开谴责吗？
Why was she pilloried by the media? = 为什么她受到媒体的严厉批评？


否定句:
They did not pillory him for his honest mistakes. = 他们并没有因为他的诚实错误而公开谴责他。
The public did not pillory the company for its attempt to improve. = 公众并没有因为该公司试图改进而严厉批评它。
