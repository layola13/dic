materials:/məˈtɪəriəlz/ | n. | 材料，物资 |  Materials are the substances used to make something.  They can be raw materials like wood or metal, or processed materials like plastic or fabric. | Example: The architect specified the building materials.  建筑师指定了建筑材料。

Example: We need to gather the necessary materials for the project. 我们需要收集项目所需的材料。


近义词：substances, components, supplies, resources | 反义词：N/A | 词性：名词
