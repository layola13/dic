motorcading:/ˈmoʊtərˌkeɪdɪŋ/| n.|摩托车队行驶；摩托车旅行|The act of traveling in a group of motorcyclists.  It often implies a planned and organized ride, sometimes for a specific purpose or event.|例句：The motorcycle club enjoyed their annual motorcading through the scenic countryside.  摩托车俱乐部享受着他们一年一度穿越风景如画的乡村的摩托车旅行。

例句： We went motorcading along the coast last weekend.  上周末我们沿着海岸线进行了摩托车旅行。


近义词：motorcycle touring, group motorcycle ride|反义词：None
