internationalism:/ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlɪzəm/| n. |国际主义|The principle of cooperation among nations, for the benefit of all.  It emphasizes the importance of international cooperation and understanding, often advocating for global governance and the reduction of national conflicts.|例句：The rise of internationalism in the 20th century led to the formation of international organizations like the United Nations.  20世纪国际主义的兴起导致了联合国等国际组织的成立。|近义词：globalism, cosmopolitanism |反义词：nationalism, isolationism, protectionism|名词
