heathens:/ˈhiːðənz/| n. |异教徒，未开化的人 | People who do not belong to a widely held religion, especially one of the main world religions.  The term often carries negative connotations, implying a lack of civilization or morality. |例句：Some ancient societies were considered heathens by the conquering empires.  一些古代社会被征服的帝国视为异教徒。

例句：They called the indigenous people heathens because of their different religious practices.  他们因为当地居民不同的宗教习俗而称他们为异教徒。


近义词：pagans, infidels, unbelievers |反义词：believers, the faithful, churchgoers
