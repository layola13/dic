mucedin:/mjuːˈsɪdɪn/ | n. |麦胶蛋白|A protein found in wheat flour, responsible for the dough's elasticity and strength. It is a type of gluten. |The baker added more mucedin to the dough to improve its elasticity. 面包师在面团中加入更多麦胶蛋白以提高其弹性。|gluten, gliadin|  (无明确反义词，可考虑“缺乏弹性的蛋白质”等描述性反义)|名词
