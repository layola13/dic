cryptosporidia:/ˌkrɪptəʊˌspɔːrɪdiə/| n. |隐孢子虫|A genus of single-celled parasitic protozoa that causes cryptosporidiosis, a diarrheal illness.  They are widespread in the environment and can contaminate water supplies. |Example: Cryptosporidia is a common cause of waterborne illness.  隐孢子虫是常见的水传播疾病的病原体。  Cryptosporidiosis outbreaks are often linked to contaminated recreational water. 隐孢子虫暴发通常与受污染的娱乐用水有关。|近义词:  parasite, protozoan|反义词: N/A
