loosened:/ˈluːsənd/ | vt. adj. |放松的；松开的；变松的 | To make or become less tight or firm.  |例句：He loosened his tie. 他松了松领带。

例句：The screws had loosened and the shelf fell down. 螺丝松了，架子掉了下来。


近义词：relaxed, slackened, eased | 反义词：tightened, fastened, secured | 词性：动词(过去分词作形容词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He loosens his belt after a big meal. 他大餐后会解开皮带。
The soil loosens easily in spring. 春天土壤很容易松动。


一般过去时:
She loosened her grip on the bag. 她放松了对包的抓握。
The wind loosened the tiles on the roof. 风把屋顶的瓦片吹松了。


现在完成时:
The constant rain has loosened the ground.  持续的雨水已经使地面松软了。
The long journey has loosened my muscles. 漫长的旅程使我的肌肉放松了。


过去完成时:
The rope had loosened, causing the bucket to fall. 绳子已经松了，导致桶掉了下来。
He had loosened his tie before he began to speak. 他在开始说话之前已经松了领带。


将来时:
The heat will loosen the paint.  高温会使油漆脱落。
I will loosen my grip on the situation. 我将放松对局势的控制。


虚拟语气:
If the screws hadn't loosened, the shelf wouldn't have fallen. 如果螺丝没有松动，架子就不会掉下来了。
If I loosened my schedule, I could take a vacation. 如果我放宽行程安排，我就可以休假了。


被动语态:
The soil was loosened by the heavy rain.  土壤被大雨冲刷得松软了。
The knot was loosened with difficulty.  那个结很难解开。


疑问句:
Has the nut loosened? 螺母松了吗？
Did the wind loosen the shutters? 风把百叶窗吹松了吗？


否定句:
The knot didn't loosen easily.  这个结不容易解开。
The screws haven't loosened yet. 螺丝还没松动。


PHRASE->
loosen up: 放松；放松身心
loosen one's grip: 放松抓握
loosen one's tongue:  开始说话；变得健谈
loosen the purse strings:  花钱大方；慷慨解囊
loosen one's bowels:  通便


词源:
Loose + -ened (past participle suffix)  源于古英语 *lōs,  意思是“松散的，自由的”。  "-ened" 是过去分词后缀，表示动作的结果。
