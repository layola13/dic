contrasted:/kənˈtræstɪd/| v. |对比的，对照的|To show a difference between things; to compare and show differences. |例句：The two paintings were contrasted to highlight their different styles. 这两幅画被对比以突出它们不同的风格。|近义词：compared, differentiated, juxtaposed |反义词：similar, alike, identical


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist contrasts colors to create depth. =艺术家对比色彩以创造深度。
He contrasts his views with those of his colleagues. = 他将自己的观点与同事的观点进行对比。


一般过去时:
She contrasted the two theories in her presentation. =她在演示中对比了这两个理论。
The teacher contrasted the characters in the story. =老师对比了故事中的人物。


现在完成时:
We have contrasted several approaches to the problem. =我们已经对比了几种解决问题的方法。
She has contrasted her experiences in two different countries. =她对比了她在两个不同国家的经历。


过去完成时:
He had contrasted the two paintings before making his decision. =他在做出决定之前已经对比了两幅画。
They had contrasted the data from different sources before drawing conclusions. =他们在得出结论之前已经对比了来自不同来源的数据。


将来时:
We will contrast the results with previous findings. =我们将把结果与之前的发现进行对比。
She will contrast the advantages and disadvantages of each option. =她将对比每个选项的优缺点。


虚拟语气:
If you contrasted the two images, you would see the difference. =如果你对比这两张图片，你就会看到区别。
I wish I had contrasted the evidence more carefully. =我希望我当时更仔细地对比了证据。


被动语态:
The images were contrasted to emphasize their differences. =对比图像以强调它们的差异。
The two approaches are contrasted in the following section. =这两种方法在下一节中进行了对比。


疑问句:
How did the author contrast the two characters? =作者是如何对比这两个角色的？
How are these two concepts contrasted in the text? =文本中这两个概念是如何对比的？


否定句:
The report did not contrast the different viewpoints sufficiently. =该报告没有充分对比不同的观点。
He did not contrast the data effectively in his analysis. =他在分析中没有有效地对比数据。


PHRASE->
in contrast to = 与…形成对比
by contrast = 相比之下
in sharp contrast to = 与…形成鲜明对比
by way of contrast = 相反地
as contrasted with = 与…相比
