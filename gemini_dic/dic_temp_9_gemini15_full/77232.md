gastroduodenitis:/ˌɡæstroʊˌduːədəˈnaɪtɪs/| n. |胃十二指肠炎|Inflammation of the stomach and duodenum.  Gastroduodenitis is a condition characterized by inflammation of the lining of both the stomach (gastr-) and the duodenum (-duodenitis), the first part of the small intestine. This inflammation can be caused by various factors, including infections (like Helicobacter pylori), autoimmune disorders, long-term use of nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), and excessive alcohol consumption.  Symptoms can range from mild discomfort to severe pain, nausea, vomiting, and digestive issues.  Diagnosis typically involves physical examination, blood tests, endoscopy, and biopsy. Treatment often includes addressing the underlying cause, managing symptoms with medication, and lifestyle modifications. |The patient was diagnosed with gastroduodenitis after experiencing persistent abdominal pain and nausea. 这位病人因持续腹痛和恶心被诊断为胃十二指肠炎。|近义词：gastritis, duodenitis|反义词：None (It describes a condition, not a state that has an opposite)|名词
