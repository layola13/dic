menorahs:/məˈnɔːrəz/| n.|  烛台（复数）|A menorah is a branched candlestick, traditionally with seven branches, used in Jewish religious ceremonies.  The menorah is a symbol of Judaism and is often used in celebrations such as Hanukkah.|例句：Many families display menorahs in their windows during Hanukkah. 许多家庭在光明节期间在窗户上展示烛台。

The synagogue was decorated with beautiful menorahs. 会堂装饰着美丽的烛台。
