expansionism:/ɪkˈspænʃənɪzəm/| n. |扩张主义，扩张政策 |The policy of extending a nation's power and influence through diplomacy or military force.  It often involves territorial acquisition and the subjugation of other peoples. |Example:  The country's aggressive expansionism led to a series of costly wars. 这个国家的侵略性扩张主义导致了一系列代价高昂的战争。|近义词：imperialism, imperialism, expansion, aggrandizement |反义词：pacifism, isolationism |名词
