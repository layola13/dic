sistine:/ˈsɪstaɪn/| adj. |西斯廷的；(尤指)西斯廷教堂的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Sistine Chapel in the Vatican Palace, especially its frescoes by Michelangelo.  |The Sistine Chapel is famous for its breathtaking frescoes. 西斯廷教堂以其令人叹为观止的壁画而闻名。|  近义词：Vatican (in context)| 反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The Sistine Chapel attracts millions of visitors every year. = 西斯廷教堂每年吸引数百万游客。
This painting reminds me of the Sistine Chapel ceiling. = 这幅画让我想起了西斯廷教堂的屋顶。


一般过去时:
Michelangelo painted the Sistine Chapel ceiling. = 米开朗基罗绘制了西斯廷教堂的屋顶。
We visited the Sistine Chapel last summer. = 我们去年夏天参观了西斯廷教堂。


现在完成时:
The church has restored many of the Sistine Chapel frescoes. = 教堂已经修复了许多西斯廷教堂的壁画。
Scholars have studied Michelangelo's techniques in the Sistine Chapel for centuries. = 数百年来，学者们一直在研究米开朗基罗在西斯廷教堂的技法。


过去完成时:
Before the restoration, parts of the Sistine Chapel frescoes were severely damaged. = 在修复之前，西斯廷教堂的部分壁画严重受损。
They had already seen the Sistine Chapel before their trip to Rome. = 在他们去罗马旅行之前，他们已经看过西斯廷教堂了。


将来时:
We will visit the Sistine Chapel on our next trip to Italy. = 我们下次去意大利旅行时将参观西斯廷教堂。
