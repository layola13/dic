complying:/kəmˈplaɪɪŋ/| adj. v.|遵从的；顺从的；应允的 |  Acting in accordance with a wish, command, rule, or request.  |例句：He was complying with the regulations. 他正在遵守规章制度。

例句：The company is complying with all safety standards. 公司正在遵守所有安全标准。


近义词：obedient, conforming, compliant

反义词：disobedient, defiant, rebellious

词性:形容词(adj.)  动词(v.)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He complies with the rules. 他遵守规则。
They comply with all the regulations. 他们遵守所有规章制度。

一般过去时:
She complied with his request. 她答应了他的请求。
We complied with the law. 我们遵守了法律。

现在完成时:

They have complied with all the requirements. 他们已经满足了所有要求。

过去完成时:
She had complied with the instructions before the deadline. 她在截止日期前已经遵守了指示。
They had complied with the agreement. 他们已经履行了协议。

将来时:
He will comply with the new regulations. 他将遵守新的规章制度。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
