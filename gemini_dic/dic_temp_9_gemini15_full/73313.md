forecloses: /ˌfɔːrˈkloʊz/ | vt. | 停止抵押贷款；取消抵押；强行拍卖；(尤指银行)取消(贷款) | To legally take possession of a property because the owner has not made the mortgage payments.  | 例句：The bank foreclosed on his house because he failed to pay his mortgage. = 因为他不付抵押贷款，银行取消了他房子的抵押贷款。


例句：The lender foreclosed on the property after repeated missed payments. = 由于多次未能付款，贷款人对该房产进行了强制拍卖。


近义词：repossess, seize, auction off | 反义词：extend (a loan), refinance
