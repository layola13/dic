discipling: /ˈdɪsɪplɪŋ/ | v. | 训练；管教；指导；塑造品格 | The act of teaching and training someone, especially in a particular skill or discipline, often with the aim of moral or character development.  It implies a more structured and intentional approach than simple teaching. | 例句：The church emphasizes discipling new believers in the faith. = 教堂强调对新信徒进行信仰教导。

例句：He is committed to discipling the next generation of leaders. = 他致力于培养下一代领导者。


近义词：training, mentoring, coaching, instructing, educating, nurturing


反义词：neglecting, spoiling, abandoning


词性：动词
