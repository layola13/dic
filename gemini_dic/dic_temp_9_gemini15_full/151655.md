particulates:/pɑːˈtɪkjuleɪts/| n.|微粒，颗粒物|Tiny particles of matter, especially those suspended in the air or water.  These can be solid or liquid and are often pollutants.|例句：Air pollution is largely caused by particulates emitted from vehicles and industrial processes.  空气污染很大程度上是由车辆和工业过程排放的颗粒物引起的。

例句：The researchers analyzed the size and composition of the particulates found in the river water. 研究人员分析了河水中发现的颗粒物的大小和成分。
