pensionably: /ˈpɛnʃənəbli/ | adj. | 可领取养老金的；达到领取养老金的年龄的 | Capable of receiving a pension; of or relating to the age at which one is eligible for a pension. |


例句：

He is now pensionably old and enjoys his retirement. 他现在到了领取养老金的年龄，享受着退休生活。

She reached pensionable age last year.  她去年达到了领取养老金的年龄。


近义词：retirement-eligible, pensionable


反义词：too young to retire, ineligible for pension


词性：形容词
