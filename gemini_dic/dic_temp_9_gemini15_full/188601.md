rumbaing:/rʌmˈbeɪɪŋ/| v. |跳伦巴舞；以伦巴舞的节奏移动|Dancing the rumba, or moving rhythmically in a manner suggestive of the rumba.|The couple was rumbaing on the dance floor.  这对夫妇正在舞池里跳伦巴舞。
They were rumbaing down the street, their hips swaying to an unseen rhythm. 他们沿着街道以伦巴舞的节奏摇摆着臀部前进。|近义词：dancing, swaying, grooving|反义词：standing still, remaining motionless|动词
