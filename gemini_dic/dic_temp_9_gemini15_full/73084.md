footstone: /ˈfʊtˌstoʊn/ | n. |基石，奠基石，墓碑底座 | A footstone is a stone forming the base of a monument or tombstone; it can also refer to a foundation stone, a stone laid as the first part of a building's foundation.  |例句：The footstone of the monument was engraved with the deceased's name and dates.  墓碑底座刻着死者的姓名和日期。

The church was built on a solid footstone. 这座教堂建在坚固的基石上。| 近义词：foundation stone, base stone, cornerstone | 反义词：topstone, capstone
