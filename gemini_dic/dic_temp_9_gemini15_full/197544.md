shellbark:/ˈʃɛlbɑːrk/| n. |壳斗；壳斗树；（尤指）山核桃树|The hard, woody outer shell of a nut, especially a hickory nut.  The term also refers to hickory trees themselves, especially those with thick, shaggy bark.|例句：The shellbark hickory is known for its incredibly strong wood.  = 山核桃树以其极其坚硬的木材而闻名。

The squirrel buried its acorns under the shellbark. = 松鼠把橡子埋在山核桃树下。|近义词：hickory, shagbark|反义词：无
