hydroplaner:/ˈhaɪdrəˌpleɪnər/| n. |水上飞机驾驶员；水滑者| A person who pilots a hydroplane or who performs the sport of hydroplaning.  The term can refer to someone who operates a hydroplane aircraft or someone who participates in the water sport of hydroplaning, where a board or skis glide on the surface of water. |Example: The skilled hydroplaner expertly navigated the choppy waters.  熟练的水上飞机驾驶员熟练地驾驭着波涛汹涌的水域。|近义词：hydroplane pilot (for aircraft), water skier (for sport)|反义词：N/A
