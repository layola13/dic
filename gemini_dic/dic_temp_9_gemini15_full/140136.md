off-the-track:/ˌɒf ðə træk/ | adj. | 脱轨的；偏离正轨的；离题的 |  Deviating from a planned course, schedule, or topic; not proceeding as expected.  |例句：The project is off-the-track and needs to be re-evaluated.  这个项目偏离了正轨，需要重新评估。
The conversation went off-the-track when someone mentioned politics.  当有人提到政治时，谈话就偏离了主题。

近义词：derailed, sidetracked, astray, amiss, divergent

反义词：on track, on course, focused, relevant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The train is off the track. = 火车脱轨了。
His argument is off the track. = 他的论点离题了。

一般过去时:
The discussion went off the track. = 讨论偏离了主题。
The car went off the track during the race. = 赛车比赛中，那辆车跑偏了。

现在完成时:
The project has gone off the track. = 项目已经偏离了正轨。
We have been off the track for hours.  我们已经偏离正轨好几个小时了。


过去完成时:
The negotiations had gone off the track before he arrived. = 他到达之前，谈判就已经破裂了。
They realised they had been off the track for days.  他们意识到他们已经偏离正轨好几天了。

将来时:
If we continue like this, the project will go off the track. = 如果我们继续这样下去，项目就会偏离正轨。
I think the meeting will go off the track if we don't stick to the agenda. 我认为如果我们不遵守议程，会议就会偏离主题。

虚拟语气:
If we had stayed on track, we would have finished on time. = 如果我们一直按计划进行，我们就能按时完成。
I wish the conversation hadn't gone off the track. 我真希望谈话没有偏离主题。

被动语态:
The train was found off the track. = 火车被发现脱轨了。
The discussion was easily sidetracked and went off the track.  讨论很容易被转移话题，偏离了主题。

疑问句:
Is the project still on track, or has it gone off the track? = 这个项目还在正轨上吗，还是偏离了正轨？
Has the conversation gone off the track?  谈话偏离主题了吗？

否定句:
The plan is not off the track; it's progressing well. = 计划没有偏离正轨；进展顺利。
The train remained firmly on the track; it did not go off the track. 火车稳稳地行驶在轨道上；没有脱轨。
