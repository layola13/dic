itinerary:/aɪˈtɪnəreri/ | n. | 旅行计划；行程；路线 | A detailed plan or route of a journey.  | Example: We carefully planned our itinerary before our trip to Europe. 我们在去欧洲旅行之前仔细规划了行程。  |近义词：schedule, plan, route | 反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The itinerary includes a visit to the Eiffel Tower. = 行程包括参观埃菲尔铁塔。
Our itinerary specifies the meeting time. = 我们的行程规定了会议时间。


一般过去时:
The itinerary we followed last year was very exciting. = 我们去年遵循的行程非常令人兴奋。
Their itinerary for the conference was packed. = 他们会议的行程安排得很紧凑。


现在完成时:
We have finalized the itinerary for the upcoming conference. = 我们已经敲定了即将举行的会议行程。
She has altered the itinerary several times. = 她已经多次更改了行程。


过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, they had already deviated from the itinerary. = 当我们到达时，他们已经偏离了行程。
We had adjusted the itinerary before the unexpected snowstorm. = 在意外的暴风雪来临之前，我们已经调整了行程。


将来时:
The itinerary will be emailed to all participants next week. = 行程安排将于下周发送给所有参与者。
We will stick to the itinerary as much as possible. = 我们将尽可能坚持行程安排。


虚拟语气:
If we had a more detailed itinerary, we wouldn't have gotten lost. = 如果我们有一个更详细的行程，我们就不会迷路了。
I wish I had known the itinerary beforehand. = 我希望我事先知道行程安排。


被动语态:
The itinerary was distributed to the guests upon arrival. = 行程安排在客人到达时分发。
The itinerary is being revised to accommodate the changes. = 行程安排正在修改以适应变化。


疑问句:
What is the itinerary for the day? = 今天的行程是什么？
Does the itinerary include transportation? = 行程中是否包括交通？


否定句:
The itinerary does not include meals. = 行程中不包括膳食。
We did not strictly follow the itinerary. = 我们没有严格按照行程安排进行。
