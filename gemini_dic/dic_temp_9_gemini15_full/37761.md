compassionating: /kəmˈpæʃənˌeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 表示同情；怜悯；同情地对待 |  The act of showing compassion; feeling or expressing sympathy and concern for others' suffering.  It often involves actively trying to alleviate the suffering. | 例句：She was compassionating the homeless man by giving him food and a blanket. = 她怜悯那个无家可归的人，给了他食物和毯子。


She spent her life compassionating the sick and the poor. = 她一生都在同情病人和穷人。


近义词：sympathizing, empathizing, pitying | 反义词：being indifferent, being cruel, being uncaring | 词性：动词
