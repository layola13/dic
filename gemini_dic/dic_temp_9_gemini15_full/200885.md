slinkingly:/ˈslɪŋkɪŋli/| adv.|偷偷摸摸地，鬼鬼祟祟地，潜行地 |Moving in a quiet and sneaky way, often to avoid being seen or noticed.  |例句：He slinked away into the shadows. 他偷偷摸摸地溜进了阴影里。

例句：She slinkingly approached the door, hoping not to be discovered. 她鬼鬼祟祟地靠近门，希望能不被发现。


近义词：stealthily, furtively, secretly, sneakily

反义词：openly, boldly, conspicuously, brazenly

词性:副词
