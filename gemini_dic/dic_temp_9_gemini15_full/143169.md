outclassed:/ˌaʊtˈklɑːst/ | vt. |胜过，超越，技高一筹|To surpass or exceed in quality or ability.|The young chess player completely outclassed his opponent.|近义词：surpass, excel, overshadow |反义词：be outdone, be inferior to


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outclasses his rivals in every aspect. = 他在各个方面都胜过他的对手。
Our team consistently outclasses the competition. = 我们的团队总是胜过竞争对手。


一般过去时:
She outclassed her competitors in the final round. = 她在决赛中胜过了她的竞争对手。
The champion easily outclassed his challenger. = 冠军轻松地击败了他的挑战者。


现在完成时:
He has outclassed all other contenders this season. = 这个赛季他一直胜过所有其他竞争者。
Our company has consistently outclassed its rivals in innovation. = 我们公司在创新方面一直胜过竞争对手。


过去完成时:
By the end of the game, she had clearly outclassed her opponent. = 到比赛结束时，她已经明显地胜过了她的对手。
They had outclassed their rivals in previous matches. = 他们之前在比赛中已经胜过了对手。


将来时:
He will outclass his opponents with his superior skills. = 他将凭借其高超的技能胜过对手。
Our new product will likely outclass the existing options on the market. = 我们的新产品很可能会胜过市场上现有的选择。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he might have outclassed his opponent. = 如果他训练更刻苦，他也许就能胜过对手了。
If only I had practiced more, I could have outclassed him. = 如果我练习得更多，我就能胜过他了。


被动语态:
He was outclassed by a younger, faster runner. = 他被一个更年轻、速度更快的跑步者超越了。
Their strategy was completely outclassed by our innovative approach. = 他们的策略完全被我们的创新方法超越了。


疑问句:
Was he outclassed in the debate? = 他在辩论中被击败了吗？
Does their technology outclass ours? = 他们的技术是否胜过我们的？


否定句:
He was not outclassed in the competition. = 他在比赛中没有被击败。
Their product did not outclass ours in terms of quality. = 他们的产品在质量上并没有胜过我们的。



PHRASE->

outclassed in every way = 各方面都胜过
