globuliferous:/ɡloʊˈbjuːlɪfərəs/ | adj. | 球状体生的；具有球状体的 | Producing or bearing small globes or globules;  characterized by the presence of globules.  The term often refers to plants producing berry-like fruits or structures. | 例句：The globuliferous plant produced small, round berries.  这株球状体生的植物结出了小的圆形浆果。

The globuliferous algae are a fascinating subject of study.  球状体生的藻类是一个引人入胜的研究课题。


近义词：globular, spherical, round | 反义词：flat, angular, acicular
