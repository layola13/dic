materiality:/məˌtɪəriˈæləti/ | n. |重要性；物质性；实质性 |The quality of being material or of relating to physical matter; the state of being real or actual, as opposed to being abstract or imaginary.  It also refers to the significance or relevance of something, especially in accounting or law. |例句：The materiality of the evidence was questioned by the defense.  辩方对证据的重要性提出了质疑。

例句：The company assessed the materiality of the environmental impact of its operations. 公司评估了其运营对环境影响的重要性。


近义词：importance, significance, relevance, substance, essence

反义词：insignificance, irrelevance, triviality, immateriality
