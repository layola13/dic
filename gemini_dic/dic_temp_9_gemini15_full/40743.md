conveyer:/kənˈveɪər/| n.|传送带，输送机|A conveyor is a mechanical device used to transport materials from one place to another.  It typically consists of a continuously moving surface, such as a belt or chain, that carries items along a predetermined path.|例句：The factory uses a conveyor belt to move the boxes to the packaging area. 工厂使用传送带将箱子运送到包装区。

The airport uses a conveyor system to transport luggage to the baggage claim. 机场使用传送系统将行李运送到行李提取处。|近义词：transporter, carrier, belt|反义词：None
