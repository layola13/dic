lagoons:/ləˈɡuːnz/| n.|泻湖；环礁湖|A lagoon is a shallow body of water, often separated from a larger body of water by a barrier reef or a strip of land.  They are often found in coastal areas and are known for their calm waters and unique ecosystems.|例句：The turquoise lagoon was teeming with colorful fish.  这片绿松石色的泻湖里充满了五彩缤纷的鱼。

The tranquil lagoon offered a perfect spot for swimming and snorkeling.宁静的泻湖是游泳和浮潜的理想场所。|近义词：inlet, bay, sound|反义词：ocean, sea
