/ɪzəm/ | n. | 主义；学说；(尤指不好的)行为，作风 | A system of beliefs or principles, especially one that forms the basis of a political or economic theory.  |  例句：Nationalism is a strong force in many countries.  民族主义在许多国家都是一股强大的力量。 |例句：He was criticized for his sexism. 他因其性别歧视而受到批评。| 近义词：doctrine, theory, creed, ism | 反义词：无
