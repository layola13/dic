litterateur: /ˌlɪtəˈrɑːtər/ | n. | 文学人士，作家 | A person who is skilled in or knowledgeable about literature; a writer.  |  Example: He was a celebrated litterateur, known for his insightful essays and captivating novels. 他是一位著名的文学人士，以其深刻的散文和引人入胜的小说而闻名。 | 近义词：author, writer, man of letters, literary figure | 反义词：illiterate
