nougats:/ˈnuːɡɑːts/| n.|牛轧糖|A nougat is a confection made of nuts, sugar, and sometimes other ingredients, such as dried fruit or honey.  It has a chewy texture.|例句：The nougat was incredibly rich and creamy. 这块牛轧糖非常浓郁香甜。|例句：She bought a box of assorted nougats as a gift. 她买了一盒什锦牛轧糖作为礼物。|近义词：candy, sweet, confection|反义词：None specifically.
