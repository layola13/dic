词源:
The word "mountainboarder" is a compound word formed by combining "mountainboard" and "-er."  "Mountainboard" itself is a combination of "mountain" (referring to the terrain) and "board" (referring to the type of equipment used). The suffix "-er" denotes a person who performs an action or is associated with a thing.  Therefore, a mountainboarder is literally a person who rides a mountainboard.
