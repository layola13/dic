outlook:/ˈaʊtˌlʊk/| n.|前景，展望；观点，看法；外表，容貌|The outlook for the economy is uncertain.  经济前景不明朗。|His outlook on life is very positive. 他的人生观非常积极。|prospect, view, perspective, aspect|pessimism, negativity


USAGE->

一般现在时:
My outlook on life is optimistic. = 我的人生观很乐观。
The company's outlook remains strong. = 公司的前景依然强劲。


一般过去时:
The outlook for the project was initially positive. = 项目的前景最初是积极的。
Their outlook on the situation changed dramatically. = 他们对局势的看法发生了巨大变化。


现在完成时:
The outlook has improved significantly since last year. = 自去年以来，前景已显著改善。
My outlook on relationships has changed after my recent experience. = 我最近的经历改变了我对人际关系的看法。


过去完成时:
Before the announcement, the outlook had been quite bleak. = 在公告发布之前，前景相当黯淡。
By the end of the meeting, their outlook had become much clearer. = 到会议结束时，他们的观点变得清晰得多。


将来时:
The outlook for next quarter will be challenging. = 下季度的展望将充满挑战。
We will assess the outlook and make a decision accordingly. = 我们将评估前景并据此做出决定。


虚拟语气:
If the outlook were better, we would invest more. = 如果前景更好，我们会投资更多。
I wish the outlook for the future were brighter. = 我希望未来的前景更加光明。


被动语态:
The outlook is widely considered to be positive. = 这前景普遍被认为是积极的。
The outlook was negatively impacted by the recent events. = 近期事件对前景产生了负面影响。


疑问句:
What is the outlook for the coming year? = 来年的前景如何？
What is your outlook on this matter? = 你对这件事的看法是什么？


否定句:
The outlook is not good. = 前景不容乐观。
The outlook is not as positive as we had hoped. = 前景不如我们希望的那么乐观。


PHRASE->

long-term outlook: 长期展望
short-term outlook: 短期展望
global outlook: 全球展望
economic outlook: 经济展望
market outlook: 市场展望
