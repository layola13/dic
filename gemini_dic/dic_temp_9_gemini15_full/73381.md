forehander:/ˈfɔːrˌhændər/| n. |正手击球者，正手球员|A player who uses their dominant hand on the forehand side (typically the right hand for right-handed players).  A forehander is adept at hitting forehand shots.|He's a powerful forehander, known for his aggressive baseline game. 他是一位强有力的正手击球者，以其积极的底线球技而闻名。|近义词:right-hander (in tennis)|反义词:backhander
