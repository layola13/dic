enabled:/ɪˈneɪbld/ | adj. | 已启用的；有效的；有能力的 |  Enabled describes something that has been activated, made possible, or given the ability to function.  It implies a previous state of being inactive or incapable. | 例句：The new software enabled us to automate the process. = 新软件使我们能够自动化这个流程。

The feature is enabled by default. = 此功能默认启用。


近义词：activated, empowered, authorized, permitted | 反义词：disabled, deactivated, prohibited


词源:  Comes from the verb "enable," which is formed from the prefix "en-" (meaning "to cause to be") and the noun "able" (meaning "having the power or skill to do something").  Therefore, "enabled" literally means "caused to be able."


USAGE->

一般现在时:

一般过去时:

过去完成时:
