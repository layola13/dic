ghat:/ɡɑːt/| n.|河岸，台阶，码头|A ghat is a flight of steps leading down to a body of water, especially in India, often used for religious ceremonies or bathing.|例句：The pilgrims descended the ghat to bathe in the sacred river. 朝圣者下到河岸在圣河中沐浴。|近义词：landing, wharf, steps, embankment|反义词:cliff, precipice
