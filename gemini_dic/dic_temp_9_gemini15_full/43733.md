creeklet:/ˈkriːklɪt/| n. |小溪，细流 | A small stream; a brook.  |例句：A tiny creeklet flowed through the meadow.  一条细小的溪流流过草地。  |例句：The children played in the shallow creeklet. 孩子们在浅浅的小溪里玩耍。|近义词：brook, streamlet, rivulet |反义词：river, ocean
