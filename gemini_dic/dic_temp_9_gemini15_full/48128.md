decoder:/dɪˈkoʊdər/ | n. |解码器，译码器 | A device or program that converts coded data into a more usable form.  It translates data from one format to another, often from a compressed or encrypted format to a readable format.  For example, a decoder might convert a digital audio signal into an analog signal that can be played through speakers. | Example: The satellite dish uses a decoder to receive and translate the broadcast signal.  卫星天线使用解码器接收和翻译广播信号。The video decoder converts the compressed video stream into images displayed on the screen. 视频解码器将压缩的视频流转换为显示在屏幕上的图像。|近义词：translator, decipherer |反义词：encoder, encryptor | 名词
