displacements:/dɪsˈpleɪsmənts/ | n. | 位移；迁移；取代；移置 | The act of moving something from its place or position; the amount or extent of such movement; the removal or replacement of something by something else.  |例句：The earthquake caused significant displacements of the ground. 地震导致地面发生了明显的位移。

例句：The recent factory closure led to many displacements of workers. 最近的工厂倒闭导致许多工人失业(被迫迁移)。


近义词：movement, shift, relocation, removal, substitution | 反义词：fixation, stability, permanence | 词性:名词
