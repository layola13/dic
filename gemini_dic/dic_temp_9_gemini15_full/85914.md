headlamp:/ˈhedlæmp/| n.|前照灯，头灯|A headlamp is a light fixed to the front of a vehicle, such as a car or bicycle, to illuminate the road ahead.  It can also refer to a light worn on the head, such as a miner's lamp.|例句：The car's headlamps were on full beam. 这辆车的车灯是远光灯。|例句：The hiker used a headlamp to navigate the dark trail.  徒步旅行者用头灯在黑暗的小路上导航。|近义词：headlight, headlight, front light|反义词：tail light
