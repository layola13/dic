de-icer:/ˈdiːaɪsər/ | n. |除冰剂，融冰剂 | A substance used to melt ice, typically on roads, pavements, or aircraft.  |例句：The airport uses a special de-icer on the runways during winter. 机场在冬天使用特殊的除冰剂在跑道上。|例句：We spread de-icer on the driveway to prevent ice from forming. 我们在车道上撒上除冰剂以防止结冰。|近义词：ice melt, ice remover |反义词：icer (反义词较为模糊，可理解为“结冰物”或“使结冰的物质”)
