remilitarizes: /ˌriːˈmɪlɪtəraɪzɪz/ | vt. |  重新军事化 | To restore military control or presence to (a place or area).  | 例句：The government remilitarized the border region after the recent conflict. 政府在最近的冲突后重新军事化了边境地区。

The country is remilitarizing its islands in response to growing regional tensions.  该国正响应日益增长的地区紧张局势而对其岛屿进行重新军事化。


近义词：rearm, fortify, reinforce


反义词：demilitarize


词性：动词
