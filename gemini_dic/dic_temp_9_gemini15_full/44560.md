crotchets:/ˈkrɒtʃɪts/| n.|符点；括号；怪癖；不满；|A crotchet is a musical note equal to a quarter of a whole note.  It can also refer to a peculiar habit or quirk, or a mark of punctuation. |例句：The composer used many crotchets in the piece.  作曲家在这首乐曲中使用了许多四分音符。|近义词：quirk, oddity, peculiarity|反义词：normality, regularity

词源:
From Middle English *crochet, from Old French croche, from croche, a hook, from crocher, to hook. The musical sense developed from the shape of the note. The meaning "whimsical notion" developed later.
