haulse:/hɔːls/ | vt. |拖，拉，拽|To pull or drag with effort.|The farmer hauled the heavy bale of hay.|近义词：pull, drag, tug |反义词：push


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hauls the heavy boxes every day. = 他每天都搬运沉重的箱子。
She hauls the wagon up the hill. = 她把马车拉上山。

一般过去时:
They hauled the boat ashore. = 他们把船拖到岸上。
He hauled the rope to pull the car. = 他拉绳子来拉车。

现在完成时:
They have hauled all the lumber to the construction site. = 他们已经把所有木材运到建筑工地。
We have hauled the furniture to the new house. = 我们已经把家具搬到新房子里。

过去完成时:
The workers had hauled the materials before the rain started. = 工人们在下雨前已经把材料运来了。
He had hauled the heavy equipment before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经搬运了重型设备。

将来时:
They will haul the cargo to the port tomorrow. = 他们明天将把货物运送到港口。
We will haul the old car to the junkyard. = 我们将把旧车拖到垃圾场。

虚拟语气:
If they hauled the nets earlier, they would have caught more fish. = 如果他们早点收网，他们就能捕到更多鱼。
I wish I had hauled the furniture myself, it would have been cheaper. = 我真希望我自己搬家具，那样会更便宜。

被动语态:
The heavy equipment was hauled to the construction site by truck. = 重型设备由卡车运送到建筑工地。
The boat was hauled out of the water for repairs. = 船被拖出水面修理。

疑问句:
Will they haul the goods by truck or train? = 他们会用卡车还是火车运送货物？
How do they haul such heavy loads? = 他们是如何搬运如此重的货物？

否定句:
He didn't haul the garbage out this morning. = 他今天早上没有把垃圾倒掉。
They cannot haul that heavy machine without a crane. = 他们没有起重机无法搬运那台重型机器。


PHRASE->
haul in: 拉进
haul away: 拖走
haul up: 往上拉
haul down: 向下拉
haul off: 猛拉
