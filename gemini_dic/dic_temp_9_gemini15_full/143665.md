outlander:/ˈaʊtlændər/| n.|外地人；局外人；异乡人；（苏格兰电视剧）《异乡人》|A person who lives in a place far from their home country or region; someone unfamiliar or foreign to a particular place or group.  The term also refers to the popular Starz television series. |例句：He felt like an outlander in the bustling city. 他在繁华的城市里感到像个局外人。

例句：The novel "Outlander" is adapted into a popular TV series. 小说《异乡人》被改编成一部热门电视剧。

近义词：foreigner, stranger, alien, expatriate

反义词：native, local, insider
