denationalizes:/ˌdiːˌnæʃənəlaɪzɪz/ | vt. |使脱离国有；取消国有化；|To remove something from national ownership or control; to end the nationalization of something.  |The government denationalized the railway system, transferring ownership to private companies. 政府将铁路系统私有化，将其所有权转让给私营公司。|privatize, deregulate|nationalize, socialize|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government denationalizes industries to boost competition. = 政府将产业私有化以促进竞争。
The company denationalizes its assets to increase profitability. = 公司将其资产私有化以提高盈利能力。


一般过去时:
The country denationalized its airlines in the 1990s. = 该国在20世纪90年代将其航空公司私有化。
They denationalized the energy sector, leading to significant changes. = 他们将能源部门私有化，导致了重大变化。


现在完成时:
Many countries have denationalized their telecommunications industries. = 许多国家已经将其电信产业私有化。
The government has denationalized several state-owned enterprises. = 政府已经将几家国有企业私有化。


过去完成时:
Before the privatization, the company had been denationalized by the previous administration. = 在私有化之前，该公司已被前任政府私有化。
They had denationalized the postal service before introducing market reforms. = 在引入市场改革之前，他们已经将邮政服务私有化。


将来时:
The government will denationalize the water utility next year. = 政府将在明年将水务公司私有化。
They plan to denationalize the steel industry in the coming decade. = 他们计划在未来十年内将钢铁工业私有化。


虚拟语气:
If the government denationalized the banks, the economy might recover faster. = 如果政府将银行私有化，经济可能会更快复苏。
