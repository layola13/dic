correlation:/ˌkɒrəˈleɪʃən/| n. |相关性，关联；相互关系 |The statistical relationship between two or more variables.  It describes the strength and direction of the linear relationship between them. A correlation does not necessarily imply causation. |例句：There is a strong positive correlation between smoking and lung cancer. 吸烟和肺癌之间存在很强的正相关关系。

例句：Researchers found a negative correlation between exercise and stress levels. 研究人员发现运动和压力水平之间存在负相关关系。

近义词：connection, association, relationship, interdependence |反义词：independence, lack of connection
