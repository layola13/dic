metazoon:/ˈmɛtəzuːn/| n. |多细胞动物|A multicellular animal; an animal composed of many cells, in contrast to a protozoon.  Metazoans exhibit a higher level of organization than protozoans, with specialized tissues and organs. |例句：Sponges are the simplest metazoans. 海绵是最简单的多细胞动物。|例句：The evolution of metazoans is a complex and fascinating subject. 多细胞动物的进化是一个复杂而迷人的课题。|近义词：multicellular animal, animal|反义词：protozoon
