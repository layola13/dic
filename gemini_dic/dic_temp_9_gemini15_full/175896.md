rather than:/ˈrɑːðər ðæn/ | conj. |而不是；与其…宁可… |This conjunction expresses a preference or alternative. It indicates that one thing is chosen or preferred over another.  |I'd rather go to the beach rather than stay home. 我宁愿去海滩，而不愿待在家里。|instead of, in preference to |rather than...not.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I prefer tea rather than coffee. = 我更喜欢茶而不是咖啡。
He chooses walking rather than driving. = 他选择步行而不是开车。


一般过去时:
She opted for a quiet evening rather than a noisy party. = 她选择了一个安静的夜晚，而不是一个吵闹的派对。
They chose to study rather than go out. = 他们选择学习而不是出去玩。


现在完成时:
We have chosen to save money rather than spend it. = 我们选择了存钱而不是花掉它。
They have opted for a simpler life rather than a luxurious one. = 他们选择了简朴的生活而不是奢侈的生活。


过去完成时:
I had preferred to stay home rather than go to the concert. = 我宁愿待在家里，而不是去听音乐会。
They had decided to walk rather than take a taxi. = 他们决定步行而不是坐出租车。


将来时:
I will choose to work from home rather than commute. = 我将选择在家工作而不是通勤。
She will likely choose a smaller car rather than a large SUV. = 她可能会选择一辆小型汽车而不是大型SUV。


虚拟语气:
I would rather stay in tonight rather than go out. = 我宁愿今晚待在家里，而不是出去。
It would be better to walk rather than drive in this traffic. = 在这种交通状况下，步行比开车更好。


被动语态:
A simpler solution was preferred rather than a complex one. = 一个简单的解决方案比一个复杂的解决方案更受青睐。
Cycling is often chosen rather than driving in city centres. = 在市中心，骑自行车往往比开车更受欢迎。


疑问句:
Would you rather have tea or coffee? = 你是想喝茶还是咖啡？
Would you prefer to stay or go? = 你是愿意留下还是离开？


否定句:
I do not want to argue, rather than engage in constructive dialogue. = 我不想争论，而是参与建设性对话。
He didn't choose to complain, rather than find a solution. = 他没有选择抱怨，而是寻找解决方案。


PHRASE->
rather than that = 与其那样
would rather...than...= 宁愿...而不愿...
sooner than = 宁愿...而不愿...
better than = 比...更好
instead of = 代替
