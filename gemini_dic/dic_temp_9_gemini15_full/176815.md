readopting:/ˌriːˈɑːptɪŋ/ | v. | 重新采用，再次采用 | The act of adopting something again after a period of not using it.  It implies a previous adoption and a subsequent abandonment before the renewed adoption. | Example: The company is readopting its original marketing strategy after a failed experiment with a new approach.  公司在新的营销策略试验失败后，重新采用了最初的营销策略。|近义词：reinstating, resuming, reviving |反义词：abandoning, discarding, rejecting | 动词
