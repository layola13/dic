misdeed:/ˈmɪsdiːd/| n. |恶行；坏事；不法行为|An immoral or illegal act.  A misdeed can range from a minor offense to a serious crime. It emphasizes the wrongfulness of the action.|例句：His misdeeds finally caught up with him.  他的恶行最终受到了惩罚。

例句：The company was punished for its misdeeds. 这家公司因其恶行而受到了惩罚。

近义词：wrongdoing, bad act, offense, crime, transgression

反义词：good deed, virtuous act

词性:名词
