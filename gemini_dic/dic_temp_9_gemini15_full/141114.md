ontogenic:/ˌɒn.təˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 个体发育的；发生于个体发育的 | Relating to or characteristic of ontogeny, the development of an individual organism or anatomical or behavioral feature from its origin to maturity.  | Example:  The ontogenic development of the brain is a complex process.  大脑的个体发育是一个复杂的过程。  The study focused on the ontogenic changes in bird song. 这项研究集中于鸟鸣的个体发育变化。 | 近义词：developmental | 反义词：phylogenetic
