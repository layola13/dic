imperfect:/ɪmˈpɜːrfɪkt/ | adj. | 不完美的；有缺陷的；未完成的 | Not perfect; having flaws or shortcomings; incomplete.  | The plan was imperfect, but it was the best we could devise.  这个计划并不完美，但这是我们所能想到的最好的计划。| perfect, complete, flawless | perfect, complete, flawless | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system is imperfect. = 这个系统并不完美。
His work is often imperfect. = 他的工作常常不完美。


一般过去时:
The painting was imperfect in several places. = 这幅画在好几个地方都不完美。
Her understanding of the subject was imperfect. = 她对这个主题的理解不完整。


现在完成时:
My attempts have been imperfect, but I will keep trying. = 我的尝试一直不完美，但我将继续努力。
The results have shown some imperfect data. = 结果显示了一些不完美的数据。


过去完成时:
The design had been imperfect from the start. = 这个设计从一开始就不完美。
We realized that our preparations had been imperfect. = 我们意识到我们的准备工作做得不充分。


将来时:
The product will likely be imperfect in its first iteration. = 该产品在第一个迭代中可能会不完美。
Their understanding of the situation will remain imperfect. = 他们对局势的理解将仍然不完整。


虚拟语气:
If the system were perfect, it wouldn't be imperfect. = 如果系统是完美的，它就不会不完美。
I wish my work were perfect, but it is imperfect. = 我希望我的作品是完美的，但它并不完美。


被动语态:
The design was considered imperfect by the critics. = 设计被评论家认为是不完美的。
The results were shown to be imperfect. = 结果被证明是不完美的。


疑问句:
Is the translation imperfect? = 翻译不完美吗？
Are there any imperfect aspects of the plan? = 计划中有哪些不完美的地方？


否定句:
The product is not imperfect; it is simply incomplete. = 产品并不完美；它只是不完整。
The solution is not imperfect, but rather impractical. = 解决方案并不完美，而是不切实际。
