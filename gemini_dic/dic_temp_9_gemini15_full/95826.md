incorporation:/ˌɪnkɔːrpəˈreɪʃən/| n. |合并；公司；结合|The act of incorporating something; the state of being incorporated.  It can refer to the formation of a corporation, the inclusion of something within something else, or the combining of different things into a whole. |例句：The incorporation of new technology improved efficiency.  新技术的融入提高了效率。|例句：The company's incorporation was finalized last week. 公司的注册成立在上周完成。|近义词：integration, amalgamation, merger, consolidation|反义词：separation, division, segregation|名词
