emanate:/ˈɛməneɪt/| v.|散发；发出；起源于|to come out from a source; to originate|The aroma emanated from the bakery.  香味从面包店散发出来。|A sense of peace emanated from her.  一种平静的感觉从她身上散发出来。|近义词：radiate, proceed, issue, spring|反义词：absorb, vanish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Confidence emanates from him. = 自信从他身上散发出来。
A strange smell emanates from the drain. = 一种奇怪的气味从下水道散发出来。

一般过去时:
Warmth emanated from the fireplace. = 温暖从壁炉散发出来。
A loud noise emanated from the street. = 街上传来一阵巨响。

现在完成时:
A strong sense of accomplishment has emanated from the team. = 团队已经散发出了强烈的成就感。
Rumors have emanated from various sources. = 各种来源都传出了谣言。

过去完成时:
Before the meeting, tension had emanated from the room. = 会议之前，房间里弥漫着紧张气氛。
It was clear that anger had emanated from him earlier. = 很明显，他之前曾怒气冲冲。

将来时:
A new energy will emanate from the company after the restructuring. = 重组之后，公司将焕发出新的活力。
We expect that great success will emanate from this project. = 我们预计这个项目将取得巨大成功。

虚拟语气:
If only happiness could emanate from every corner of the world. = 但愿幸福能从世界每个角落散发出来。
I wish a stronger sense of unity would emanate from our group. = 我希望我们小组能更有凝聚力。

被动语态:
The light was emanated from a distant star. = 光是从一颗遥远的恒星发出的。(较少用被动)
The fragrance is emanated throughout the garden. = 香气弥漫在整个花园里。(较少用被动)

疑问句:
Where does this strange music emanate from? = 这奇怪的音乐是从哪里传来的？
Does the light emanate from the sun? = 光是从太阳发出的吗？


否定句:
No light emanated from the room. = 房间里没有光线发出。
No sound emanated from the device. = 设备没有发出任何声音。


PHRASE->
emanate from = 源于；来自
emanate confidence = 散发自信
emanate power = 散发力量
emanate warmth = 散发温暖
emanate peace = 散发平静


词源:
emanate 源自拉丁语“emanare”，意为“流出”，由“e-”（出）和“manare”（流）构成。
