hemicrania:/ˌhɛmɪˈkreɪniə/| n.| 半侧头痛 | A severe, persistent headache affecting only one side of the head.  It is often characterized by intense, throbbing pain and can be associated with other neurological symptoms.  The term is used to describe a type of headache, often considered a subtype of migraine. | Example:  She suffered from hemicrania, a debilitating headache that only affected the left side of her head.  她患有半侧头痛，这种使人衰弱的头痛只影响她的左侧头部。|近义词：migraine, one-sided headache |反义词：cephalalgia (general headache)
