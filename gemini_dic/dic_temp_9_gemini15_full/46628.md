damnability:/ˌdæmnəˈbɪləti/| n. |该当谴责性；可诅咒性；罪恶性 |The quality or state of being deserving of damnation or condemnation; extreme wickedness.  It refers to the inherent worthiness of punishment, especially eternal punishment.  The concept often arises in theological discussions about sin and divine justice.| Example: The damnability of his actions was evident to everyone.  他的行为的罪恶性对每个人都很明显。  The sheer damnability of the crime shocked the community. 这起犯罪的罪恶性震惊了整个社区。|近义词：condemnability, culpability, blameworthiness |反义词：innocence, blamelessness, righteousness |名词
