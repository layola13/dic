domine:/ˈdɒmɪniː/|/ˈdɑːmɪniː/| n.|统治者；主人；支配者|The word "domine" is a Latin word meaning "lord," "master," or "ruler." It signifies a person who holds power and authority over others.  The word is less common in modern English but persists in certain contexts and derivatives.|例句：The domineering general controlled his troops with an iron fist.  这位专横的将军铁腕统治他的军队。|例句：He acted as the domine in his household, making all the decisions. 他在家中扮演着当家作主人的角色，所有决定都由他做。|近义词：master, lord, ruler, sovereign|反义词：subject, servant, subordinate|名词
