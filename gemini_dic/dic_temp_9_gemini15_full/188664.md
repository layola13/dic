rumour-monger:/ˈruːməmɒŋɡə(r)/| n. |造谣者，传谣者 |A rumour-monger is a person who spreads rumours or unconfirmed stories, often with malicious intent. They enjoy creating and sharing gossip, regardless of the truth or consequences. |例句：He's a notorious rumour-monger;  everyone avoids him. 他是个臭名昭著的造谣者；每个人都躲着他。|例句： Don't listen to that rumour-monger; it's probably not true. 不要听那个造谣者的；那可能不是真的。|近义词：gossip, talebearer, scandalmonger |反义词：truth-teller, fact-checker |名词
