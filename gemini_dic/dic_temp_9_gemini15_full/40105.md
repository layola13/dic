contrast:/ˈkɒntrɑːst/ /ˈkɑːntræst/ | n., v. | 对比，反差；使对比 | To compare in order to show differences; a difference or unlikeness between similar things. |例句：The contrast between the two paintings is striking. 这两幅画的对比非常鲜明。

The stark contrast between rich and poor is a major social problem. 富人和穷人之间的巨大反差是一个主要的社会问题。|近义词：difference, opposition, comparison |反义词：similarity, resemblance, likeness |

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The colors contrast beautifully. = 这些颜色对比得很漂亮。
The two styles contrast sharply. = 这两种风格形成鲜明对比。

一般过去时:
The speaker contrasted the two political systems. = 演讲者对比了这两种政治制度。
Her performance contrasted favorably with his. = 她的表演与他的相比要好得多。

现在完成时:
The artist has contrasted light and shadow effectively. = 艺术家有效地对比了光影。
We have contrasted our findings with previous research. = 我们已经将我们的研究结果与之前的研究进行了对比。

过去完成时:
He had contrasted the two approaches before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前已经对比了这两种方法。
They had contrasted their experiences in different cultures. = 他们已经对比了他们在不同文化中的经历。

将来时:
She will contrast the historical data with the present situation. = 她将把历史数据与现状进行对比。
We will contrast the advantages and disadvantages of each option. = 我们将对比每个选项的优缺点。

虚拟语气:
If the colors contrasted more, the painting would be more vibrant. = 如果颜色对比更强烈，这幅画就会更加生动。
I wish the evidence contrasted more clearly with the defendant's claims. = 我希望证据能更清晰地与被告的声明形成对比。

被动语态:
The two theories were contrasted in the article. = 这两种理论在文章中进行了对比。
The results were contrasted with those of the control group. = 结果与对照组的结果进行了对比。

疑问句:
How do the two characters contrast? = 这两个角色是如何形成对比的？
Does the author contrast different perspectives? = 作者是否对比了不同的观点？

否定句:
The painting doesn't contrast the colors effectively. = 这幅画没有有效地对比颜色。
The study doesn't contrast the two methods sufficiently. = 这项研究没有充分对比这两种方法。


PHRASE->
in contrast to = 与...形成对比
by contrast = 相比之下
in sharp contrast = 形成鲜明对比
a striking contrast = 显著的对比
a marked contrast = 明显的对比
