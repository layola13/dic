overplanning:/ˌoʊvərˈplænɪŋ/| n.|过度计划；计划过度|The act of planning something excessively or to an unnecessary degree, often leading to inefficiency or anxiety.  It involves creating overly detailed plans, leaving little room for flexibility or spontaneity.|例句：His overplanning for the trip resulted in a stressful and inflexible itinerary. 他的旅行计划过度详尽，导致行程紧张而缺乏灵活性。

例句：The team's overplanning for the project led to delays and burnout.  团队对项目的过度计划导致了延误和倦怠。|近义词：excessive planning, meticulous planning, overpreparation|反义词：improvisation, spontaneity, winging it|词性:名词
