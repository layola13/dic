patellectomy:/ˌpætəˈlɛktəmi/| n. |髌骨切除术|The surgical removal of the patella (kneecap).  It is typically performed to treat severe patellar fractures, osteoarthritis, or other conditions affecting the kneecap. |例句：The surgeon recommended a patellectomy to alleviate the patient's chronic knee pain.  医生建议进行髌骨切除术以减轻患者的慢性膝盖疼痛。

例句：After a severe car accident, the patient required a patellectomy to repair extensive damage to the patella.  严重车祸后，病人需要进行髌骨切除术来修复髌骨的广泛损伤。


近义词：patellar resection (髌骨切除)


反义词：patellar reconstruction (髌骨重建), patellar repair (髌骨修复)


词性: 名词
