movements:/ˈmuːvmənts/ | n. | 运动；活动；动向；乐章 |  Movements are actions or changes of position or place.  This word can refer to physical actions, like dancing or walking, or to larger-scale changes, like political movements or the movements of celestial bodies. |例句：The dancer's movements were graceful and fluid.  舞者的动作优雅流畅。


The bird's movements were erratic and unpredictable. 鸟的动向飘忽不定。|近义词：motions, actions, activities, shifts |反义词：stillness, inactivity, stagnation |名词
