inauspiciousness:/ˌɪnɔːˈspɪʃəsnəs/| n. | 不吉利；不祥之兆；不顺利 | The feeling of ill omen or bad luck; lack of promise of success. It refers to a situation or event that suggests misfortune or failure is likely. |例句：The stormy weather added to the inauspiciousness of the occasion.  暴风雨天气加剧了这次活动的坏兆头。

The inauspiciousness of the project's start date, falling on Friday the 13th, worried the team.  项目开始日期的不吉利，恰逢星期五13日，让团队感到担忧。|近义词：unfavorable omen, ill omen, bad luck, misfortune, adversity |反义词：auspiciousness, good fortune, success, prosperity
