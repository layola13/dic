iciest:/ˈaɪsɪst/| adj. |最冷的，冰冷的 |Most cold; extremely cold.  The adjective "iciest" is the superlative form of "icy," meaning extremely cold or frosty. It can also be used figuratively to describe something unfriendly or unwelcoming. |例句：The iciest wind howled through the mountain pass.  = 最寒冷的风呼啸着穿过山口。The meeting had an iciest atmosphere after the disagreement. = 争吵之后，会议的气氛极其冷淡。|近义词：coldest, freezing, frigid, glacial |反义词：warmest, hottest, mildest
