schlubs:/ʃlʌbz/| n.|邋遢的人，笨拙的人，失败者|A schlub is a clumsy, unattractive, or socially awkward person.  The term often implies a lack of style or sophistication.  It carries a somewhat negative connotation but can sometimes be used playfully or self-deprecatingly.|例句：He's such a schlub; he always wears mismatched clothes. 他真是个邋遢鬼，总是穿着不搭配的衣服。

He considered himself a schlub compared to the other successful businessmen. 与其他成功的商人相比，他认为自己是个失败者。|近义词：loser, slob, klutz, oaf|反义词：heartthrob, sophisticate, charmer
