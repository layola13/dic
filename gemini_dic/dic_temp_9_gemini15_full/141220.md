oorial:/ˈɔːriəl/| n. |大袋鼠；欧里尔羊|A large kangaroo, particularly those of the genus *Macropus*.  The term is also sometimes applied to certain species of sheep. |Example: The oorial is a powerful and agile animal.  大袋鼠是一种强壮而敏捷的动物。|近义词：kangaroo (for the kangaroo species),  sheep (for the sheep species)|反义词：None specifically, but small marsupials or small livestock could be considered antonyms in terms of size.
