cockboat:/ˈkɒkˌboʊt/ | n. |小艇，小划艇 |A small boat, especially one used for carrying supplies or people to and from a larger ship.  It is often rowed or sailed. |例句：The sailors used a cockboat to reach the shore.  水手们用小艇到达岸边。

The captain sent a cockboat to retrieve the lost supplies. 船长派出一艘小艇去取回丢失的补给。

|近义词：dinghy, skiff, yawl |反义词：暂无 |名词
