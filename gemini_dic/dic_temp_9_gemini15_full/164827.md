prefixation:/ˌpriːfɪkˈseɪʃən/ | n. | 前缀法；加前缀 | The process of forming a new word by adding a prefix to an existing word.  This is a common method of word formation in many languages, including English. For example, adding the prefix "un-" to "happy" creates the word "unhappy". | Example: The prefixation of "un-" to "do" creates the word "undo". 前缀“un-”加在“do”前面构成了单词“undo”。 |近义词： affixation, derivation | 反义词：None
