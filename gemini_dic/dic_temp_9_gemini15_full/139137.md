obese:/oʊˈbiːs/| adj.|肥胖的；臃肿的|extremely fat; grossly overweight.|The doctor said he was obese and needed to lose weight.医生说他肥胖，需要减肥。|overweight, corpulent, stout|slim, thin, slender|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is obese. = 他很肥胖。
She is obese and needs to change her lifestyle. = 她很肥胖，需要改变生活方式。


一般过去时:
He was obese last year. = 他去年很肥胖。
She was obese before she started her diet. = 她开始节食前很肥胖。


现在完成时:
He has been obese for many years. = 他肥胖很多年了。
She has been obese since childhood. = 她从小就肥胖。


过去完成时:
He had been obese for a decade before he decided to have surgery. = 在他决定做手术之前，他已经肥胖十年了。
She had been obese her whole life until she found a solution. = 她一生都肥胖，直到她找到了解决办法。


将来时:
He will be obese if he continues to eat junk food. = 如果他继续吃垃圾食品，他会继续肥胖。
She will likely still be obese next year unless she makes significant changes. = 除非她做出重大改变，否则她明年很可能仍然肥胖。


虚拟语气:
If he were not obese, he would be much healthier. = 如果他不肥胖，他会健康得多。
I wish I were not obese. = 我希望我不那么肥胖。


被动语态:
(Obese is rarely used in the passive voice.  The focus is usually on the person being obese, not the state of being obese itself.)


疑问句:
Is he obese? = 他肥胖吗？
Is she considered obese according to her BMI? = 根据她的BMI，她算肥胖吗？


否定句:
He is not obese. = 他不肥胖。
She is not obese, but she is overweight. = 她不肥胖，但她超重。


PHRASE->
morbidly obese: 病态肥胖的
severely obese: 严重肥胖的
become obese: 变得肥胖
degree of obesity: 肥胖程度
childhood obesity: 儿童肥胖


词源:
From Late Latin obesus, from ob- "to" + esus, past participle of edere "to eat".  The word literally means "eaten up".
