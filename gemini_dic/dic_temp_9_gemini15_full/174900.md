racoonda:/ræˈkuːndə/| n. |浣熊|A raccoon is a medium-sized mammal with grayish-brown fur, a bushy tail with dark rings, and a masked face.| Example: The raccoon rummaged through the garbage can. 那只浣熊翻找垃圾桶。|近义词：coati,  procyon|反义词: None
