confusables: /kənˈfjuːzəbəlz/ | n. | 易混淆的词语 | Words that are easily confused with each other because of their similar spelling, pronunciation, or meaning.  They often pose challenges for learners of a language.  | Example:  "The words 'accept' and 'except' are common confusables." = “'accept'和'except'是常见的易混淆词。” | 近义词: similar-sounding words, easily mistaken words | 反义词:  clearly distinguishable words
