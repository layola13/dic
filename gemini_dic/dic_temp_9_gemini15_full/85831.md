hazy:/ˈheɪzi/| adj.|朦胧的；模糊的；烟雾弥漫的|Lacking clarity or distinctness; obscured by mist, fog, or smoke.|The hazy morning sun struggled to penetrate the thick fog.  朦胧的晨曦努力穿透浓雾。

The hazy memory of my childhood is slowly fading.  我对童年模糊的记忆正在慢慢消逝。|cloudy, misty, foggy, blurry, indistinct|clear, sharp, distinct, precise|形容词
