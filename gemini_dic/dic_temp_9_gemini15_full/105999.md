knapsack:/ˈnæpsæk/| n. |背包，旅行包 |A knapsack is a bag carried on one's back, typically used for hiking or camping.  It often has straps to go over the shoulders and sometimes a waist strap for added support. |例句：He packed his knapsack with food and water for the hike. 他为远足打包了食物和水在背包里。|例句：She bought a new knapsack for her backpacking trip. 她为背包旅行买了一个新的背包。|近义词：backpack, rucksack, haversack |反义词：handbag, suitcase
