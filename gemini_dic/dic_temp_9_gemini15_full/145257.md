overboastful: /ˌoʊvərˈboʊstfəl/ | adj. | 过于自夸的，吹嘘过度的 | Excessively boastful; tending to exaggerate one's own accomplishments or importance.  |例句：His overboastful account of his achievements made everyone uncomfortable. 他对自身成就的夸夸其谈让每个人都感到不舒服。 |近义词：vain, boastful, arrogant, swaggering, self-aggrandizing |反义词：modest, humble, self-effacing


词源:

* **over-**:  prefix indicating excess or to a great degree. (表示过度或很大程度的前缀)
* **boastful**: adjective meaning full of boasts; excessively proud and self-satisfied. (形容词，意思是充满自夸；极其骄傲和自满)

The word "overboastful" is a relatively straightforward combination of these two elements, signifying a level of boasting that surpasses what is considered acceptable or reasonable. (这个词是这两个词元素的直接组合，表示夸耀的程度超过了可接受或合理的范围。)


用法说明:

This word is an adjective and is used to describe a person or their behavior.  It implies an excessive and often irritating display of self-importance or bragging. (这个词是一个形容词，用来形容一个人或他们的行为。它暗示了过度的，常常是令人恼怒的自我重要性或吹嘘。)


例句:


Her overboastful manner alienated many potential friends.  她的夸夸其谈疏远了许多潜在的朋友。


His overboastful claims were quickly debunked by the facts.  他夸夸其谈的说法很快就被事实驳斥了。
