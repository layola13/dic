reminds:/rɪˈmaɪndz/ | vt. |提醒，使想起 | to cause someone to remember something; to make someone aware of something. |例句：This song reminds me of my childhood. 这首歌让我想起了我的童年。
He reminded me to call my mother. 他提醒我给妈妈打电话。|近义词：recall, recollect, prompt |反义词：forget, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The picture reminds me of my grandmother. = 这张照片让我想起了我的祖母。
This smell reminds him of his childhood home. = 这种气味让他想起了他的童年之家。

一般过去时:
She reminded me that I had a meeting. = 她提醒我有一个会议。
He reminded his wife to pick up the children. = 他提醒他的妻子去接孩子。

现在完成时:
Everything reminds me of how much I've changed. = 一切都让我想起了我改变了多少。
This experience has reminded me of the importance of hard work. = 这段经历让我记起了努力工作的重要性。

过去完成时:
I had reminded him several times, but he still forgot. = 我已经提醒他好几次了，但他还是忘了。
She had reminded her son to study for the test before he went out. = 在他出去之前，她已经提醒儿子复习考试了。

将来时:
I will remind you again tomorrow. = 我明天会再次提醒你。
She will remind him to lock the door before he leaves. = 她会在她离开之前提醒他锁门。

虚拟语气:
If only I had reminded him earlier. = 要是我早点提醒他就好了。
I wish I had reminded her to bring her umbrella. = 我真希望我提醒她带伞。

被动语态:
I was reminded of my promise. = 我被提醒了我的承诺。
He was reminded to submit his report by the deadline. = 他被提醒要在截止日期前提交报告。

疑问句:
Does this song remind you of anything? = 这首歌让你想起什么了吗？
What reminds you of your childhood? = 什么让你想起你的童年？

否定句:
The movie didn't remind me of anything. = 这部电影没有让我想起任何东西。
Nothing reminds me of my former life. = 没有什么让我想起我以前的生活。


PHRASE->
remind oneself of something = 提醒自己某事
remind someone to do something = 提醒某人做某事
remind someone about something = 提醒某人某事
remind someone of something = 使某人想起某事
be reminded of something = 被提醒某事
