canned:/kænd/ | adj. | 罐装的；录制好的；陈腐的 |  Processed and preserved in a can or tin.  Also describes something pre-recorded or lacking originality. | 例句：I prefer fresh tomatoes to canned tomatoes. 我更喜欢新鲜的西红柿而不是罐装的西红柿。

例句：The band played a canned performance of their greatest hits. 乐队演奏了他们最受欢迎歌曲的录制版本。


近义词：tinned, preserved, pre-recorded, stale, clichéd | 反义词：fresh, original, live


词性: 形容词
