half-defiantly: /ˌhæf dɪˈfaɪəntli/ | adv. | 半不服气地，勉强地，不情愿地 |  Expressing defiance or resistance in a hesitant or incomplete manner. It suggests a mixture of reluctance and opposition. |例句：He half-defiantly mumbled an apology. 他半不服气地嘟囔着道歉了。


He half-defiantly agreed to help. 他勉强同意帮忙。


近义词：reluctantly, grudgingly, unwillingly | 反义词：willingly, eagerly, enthusiastically


词性: 副词
