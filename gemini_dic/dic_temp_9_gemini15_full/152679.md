pealing:/ˈpiːlɪŋ/| v.|剥皮；响亮地敲打；发出响亮的声音|The act of removing the skin or outer layer from something, or the sound of bells ringing loudly.  |例句：She was peeling potatoes for dinner. 她正在为晚餐削土豆。
The church bells were pealing merrily. 教堂的钟声响亮而欢快地敲响。|近义词：shelling, ringing, chiming|反义词：sticking, adhering


USAGE->

The bells are pealing in celebration. = 钟声在庆祝中响亮地敲响。
