redevelopment's:/ˌriːdɪˈvɛləpməntz/ | n. | 再开发（的） | The process of improving an area by replacing old buildings with new ones, or by improving existing ones.  It often involves a combination of demolition, construction, and infrastructure upgrades.  | 例句：The city's redevelopment's plan includes new parks and affordable housing.  城市的再开发计划包括新的公园和经济适用房。

The redevelopment's impact on the local community was significant.  再开发对当地社区的影响是巨大的。


近义词：renovation, renewal, revitalization, reconstruction | 反义词：demolition (in the sense of complete destruction without rebuilding), neglect | 词性：名词所有格
