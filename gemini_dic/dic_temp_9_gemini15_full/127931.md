neonate:/ˈniːoʊneɪt/| n. |新生儿|A neonate is a newborn child, especially one less than four weeks old.  It refers to the period immediately following birth.  The term often implies a focus on the medical care and development of the infant during this vulnerable time.|The neonate was carefully monitored in the intensive care unit. 新生儿在重症监护室接受了仔细的监测。|infant, newborn, baby|adult, elderly
