logometric:/ˌlɒɡəˈmetrɪk/| adj.|对数的，测量的|Relating to or using logarithms in measurement or calculation.  The term often appears in the context of statistical analysis, particularly in fields like genomics and bioinformatics.|例句：The study employed logometric analysis to compare gene expression levels between the two groups. 这项研究采用对数分析来比较两组之间的基因表达水平。|近义词：logarithmic|反义词：linear
