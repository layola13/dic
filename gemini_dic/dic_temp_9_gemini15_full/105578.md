kindest:/ˈkaɪndɪst/| adj.|最仁慈的，最和蔼的 | Most benevolent; showing the greatest degree of gentleness, kindness, and consideration.  |例句：She showed the kindest heart to the homeless people. 她对无家可归的人表现出最仁慈的心肠。
例句：He offered the kindest words of comfort to the grieving family. 他向悲伤的家庭提供了最安慰的话语。 |近义词：most compassionate, most benevolent, most gentle |反义词:cruelest, most unkind, most harsh
