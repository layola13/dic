megazostrodon:/ˌmɛɡəˈzɒstrədɒn/| n. |巨带齿兽|A genus of extinct cynodonts that lived during the Triassic period.  Megazostrodon is considered by some to be a transitional form between cynodonts and mammals.|Megazostrodon was a small, shrew-like creature. The fossil evidence suggests it was a nocturnal animal. |近义词：暂无|反义词：暂无|名词
