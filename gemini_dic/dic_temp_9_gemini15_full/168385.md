proactive:/ˌproʊˈæktɪv/| adj.|积极主动的，先发制人的|Taking initiative to prevent problems or achieve goals before they arise.  It emphasizes taking action rather than simply reacting to events.|例句：She took a proactive approach to her studies, always completing assignments ahead of time. 她对学习采取了积极主动的方式，总是提前完成作业。

例句：The company adopted a proactive strategy to address potential safety issues. 公司采取了积极主动的策略来解决潜在的安全问题。


近义词：aggressive, anticipatory, preventative, preemptive|反义词：reactive, passive, indolent
