militarises:/ˈmɪlɪtəraɪzɪz/| v.|军事化；使军事化 |To give military character to; to equip or organize for war.  The process of a nation or region becoming increasingly militarized. |例句：The government is militarising the border region. 政府正在对边境地区进行军事化。

例句：The country has rapidly militarized its economy, focusing on defense production.  这个国家迅速将其经济军事化，重点放在国防生产上。


近义词：arm, fortify, weaponize |反义词：demilitarize, pacifize
