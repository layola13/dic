/ˌɛfdiˈeɪ/ |abbr.| 美国食品药品监督管理局 |The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is a federal agency of the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services, responsible for protecting and promoting public health through the control and supervision of food safety, tobacco products, dietary supplements, prescription and over-the-counter pharmaceutical drugs (medications), vaccines, biopharmaceuticals, blood transfusions, medical devices, electromagnetic radiation emitting devices (ERED), cosmetics, and veterinary products. |The FDA approved the new drug for market. 美国食品药品监督管理局批准了该新药上市。|近义词：Food and Drug Administration |反义词：无
