legalise:/ˈliːɡəlaɪz/| vt.|使合法化；批准|To make something legal.  The act of making something legal, previously illegal.|例句：The government is planning to legalise cannabis. 政府计划使大麻合法化。|例句：Many countries have legalised same-sex marriage. 许多国家已经将同性婚姻合法化。|近义词：authorize, sanction, validate|反义词：prohibit, outlaw, ban|动词
