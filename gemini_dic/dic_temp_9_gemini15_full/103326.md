jigglers:/ˈdʒɪɡlərz/| n.|晃动的东西；摇摆的东西；（尤指）乳房|Something that jiggles; something that moves or shakes rhythmically, especially breasts.|例句：The jelly jigglers on the dessert table were very tempting.  甜品桌上摇晃的果冻非常诱人。
例句：The loose stones on the path were real jigglers. 路上松动的石头一直在晃动。|近义词：wobblers, shakers, dancers|反义词：stillness, stability

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The jelly jiggles. = 果冻在晃动。
The pendant jiggles slightly. = 吊坠轻轻晃动。

一般过去时:
The car jiggled violently. = 车子剧烈晃动。
The earthquake jiggled the buildings. = 地震使建筑物晃动。

过去完成时:
