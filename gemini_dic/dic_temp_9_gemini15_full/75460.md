frumentation:/ˌfruːmɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. |谷物供应；粮食分配 |The act of supplying grain or other food; the distribution of grain as rations.  Frumentation was a crucial aspect of Roman imperial policy, ensuring the loyalty of the populace by providing them with essential food supplies. |例句：The emperor ordered a large-scale frumentation to alleviate the famine. 皇帝下令进行大规模的粮食供应以缓解饥荒。| The regular distribution of grain to the Roman citizens was a vital part of the frumentation system.  罗马公民定期领取粮食是粮食供应系统的重要组成部分。|近义词：provisioning, distribution, rationing |反义词：famine, starvation |名词
