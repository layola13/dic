fluxes:/ˈflʌksɪz/| n.|磁通量；变化；流量；熔剂 |The continuous flow or change of something.|The magnetic flux through the coil increased. 线圈中的磁通量增加了。|The fluxes of energy and matter in the ecosystem are complex. 生态系统中能量和物质的流量是复杂的。|近义词：flows, changes, currents|反义词：stasis, constancy


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The magnetic flux constantly changes. = 磁通量不断变化。
The river's flow fluxes depending on rainfall. = 河流的流量根据降雨量而变化。


一般过去时:
The company's profits fluxed last quarter. = 公司的利润在上个季度波动。
The population fluxed dramatically after the war. = 战争后人口剧烈波动。


现在完成时:
The situation has fluxed significantly since the announcement. = 自从公告发布以来，情况已经发生了重大变化。
The market has fluxed throughout the year. = 市场全年都在波动。


过去完成时:
The team's morale had fluxed before the crucial game. = 在关键比赛之前，球队的士气已经低落。
The price of oil had fluxed wildly before the crisis. = 在危机之前，油价剧烈波动。


将来时:
The economy is expected to flux in the coming months. = 预计经济将在未来几个月内波动。
The weather is likely to flux throughout the day. = 天气可能全天都在变化。


虚拟语气:
If the magnetic flux were to increase, the current would also increase. = 如果磁通量增加，电流也会增加。
If the situation had not fluxed, the outcome would have been different. = 如果情况没有变化，结果就会不同。


被动语态:
The data was fluxed by the inaccurate sensor readings. = 数据因传感器读数不准确而出现波动。
The market is being fluxed by global economic uncertainty. = 市场正受到全球经济不确定性的影响而波动。


疑问句:

否定句:

The temperature remained stable; it did not flux. = 温度保持稳定；它没有变化。
