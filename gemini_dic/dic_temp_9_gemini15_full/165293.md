preinterested:/ˌpriːˈɪntrɪstɪd/| adj. |预先感兴趣的，事先感兴趣的 |  Describes someone who is interested in something before it happens or is fully presented. It suggests a prior interest or anticipation. | Example:  He was preinterested in the upcoming conference, having heard positive reviews from colleagues. 他对即将到来的会议很感兴趣，因为他从同事那里听到了好评。  | 近义词：anticipated, expectant, beforehand interested | 反义词：uninterested, indifferent
