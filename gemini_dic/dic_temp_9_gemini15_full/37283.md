commandeers: /ˌkɒmənˈdɪrz/ /ˌkɑːmənˈdɪrz/ | vt. | 征用；强占；霸占 | To take control of (something, especially a vehicle or building) without authority | The army commandeered his car.  军队征用了他的汽车。  The rebels commandeered the radio station.  叛军占领了广播电台。 | seize, requisition, expropriate | relinquish, surrender, release | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government commandeers private property for public use. = 政府征用私人财产用于公共用途。
They commandeer resources for their own purposes. = 他们为自身目的征用资源。


一般过去时:
The military commandeered all available vehicles. = 军方征用了所有可用的车辆。
The protesters commandeered a building and held a sit-in. = 抗议者占领了一座建筑物并举行了静坐抗议。


现在完成时:
The authorities have commandeered several buildings in the disaster zone. = 当局已征用了灾区的多座建筑物。
The rebels have commandeered a significant amount of supplies. = 叛军已经征用了大量的物资。


过去完成时:
The police had commandeered the suspect's car before he could escape. = 警方在他逃跑之前已经征用了嫌疑人的汽车。
They had commandeered the airport before the scheduled flight. = 他们在预定航班起飞前已经占领了机场。


将来时:
The army will commandeer all available transportation. = 军队将征用所有可用的交通工具。
They will commandeer the building for emergency shelter. = 他们将征用该建筑物作为紧急避难所。


虚拟语气:
If the government commandeered all the wheat, there would be a famine. = 如果政府征用了所有的麦子，就会发生饥荒。
I wish they hadn't commandeered my boat. = 我真希望他们没有征用我的船。


被动语态:
His car was commandeered by the police. = 他的车被警方征用了。
The building is being commandeered for use as a hospital. = 该建筑物正在被征用作医院使用。


疑问句:
Was the bridge commandeered by the enemy? = 那座桥是被敌人征用的吗？
Have they commandeered any additional resources? = 他们是否征用了任何额外的资源？


否定句:
The army did not commandeer any civilian vehicles. = 军队没有征用任何民用车辆。
They wouldn't commandeer property without due process. = 他们不会在没有正当程序的情况下征用财产。
