nonadaptive:/ˌnɒnəˈdæptɪv/| adj. |非适应性的，不适应的|Not adapted or adaptable to a particular environment or situation.  It implies a lack of flexibility or the inability to adjust to changing conditions. |Example:  The nonadaptive immune system is a general defense mechanism against pathogens, unlike the adaptive immune system.  非适应性免疫系统是一种针对病原体的普遍防御机制，与适应性免疫系统不同。|近义词：unadaptable, inflexible, rigid |反义词：adaptive, adaptable, flexible
