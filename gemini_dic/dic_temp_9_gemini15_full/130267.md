non-stop:/ˌnɒnˈstɒp/| adj.|不间断的；连续不断的|without stopping; continuous|例句：The flight was non-stop from London to New York.  伦敦到纽约的航班是直飞的。|例句：He talked non-stop for two hours. 他连续说了两个小时。|近义词：continuous, uninterrupted, ceaseless, incessant|反义词：interrupted, intermittent, sporadic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The train runs non-stop to the city center. = 火车直达市中心。
He works non-stop every day. = 他每天都连续工作。


一般过去时:
The music played non-stop throughout the party. = 音乐在整个派对期间持续播放。
She drove non-stop for ten hours. = 她连续开了十个小时的车。


现在完成时:
They have worked non-stop to finish the project. = 他们为了完成项目一直不停地工作。
We have been running non-stop for an hour. = 我们已经连续跑了一个小时了。


过去完成时:
The machine had run non-stop for days before it broke down. = 机器连续运转了好几天才坏掉。
They had driven non-stop until they reached their destination. = 他们一直不停地开车直到到达目的地。


将来时:
The concert will be non-stop for three hours. = 音乐会将持续三个小时不停歇。
We will travel non-stop to our holiday destination. = 我们将直达我们的度假目的地。


虚拟语气:
If the rain kept falling non-stop, the river would flood. = 如果雨一直不停地下，河水就会泛滥。
I wish the noise would stop; this non-stop construction is driving me crazy. = 我希望噪音能停止；这没完没了的施工快把我逼疯了。


被动语态:
The work was completed non-stop through the night. = 工作通宵达旦地完成了。
The music is played non-stop in the shopping mall. = 购物中心里一直在播放音乐。


疑问句:
Will the movie be non-stop or will there be an intermission? = 电影会是连续播放的吗？还是会有中场休息？
Is the flight non-stop or does it have a layover? = 这趟航班是直飞的吗？还是中途要转机？


否定句:
The party wasn't non-stop; there were several breaks. = 派对不是连续不断的；中间有好几次休息。
The show wasn't non-stop; there was a 15-minute intermission. = 演出不是连续不断的；中间有15分钟的中场休息。
