circulates:/ˈsɜːrkjuleɪts/| v. |循环；流通；传播 |To move continuously around a place or system.  It can refer to physical objects, information, or ideas. |例句：Blood circulates throughout the body. = 血液在全身循环。
The rumour circulated quickly through the school. = 谣言迅速在学校传播开来。|近义词：circulate, flow, spread, disseminate |反义词：stagnate, halt, stop


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The blood circulates constantly. = 血液不断循环。
News circulates rapidly in the digital age. = 在数字时代，新闻传播迅速。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

The new policy will circulate widely throughout the company. = 新政策将在公司内部广泛传播。


虚拟语气:
