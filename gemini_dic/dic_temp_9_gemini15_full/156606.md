picnics:/ˈpɪknɪks/| n.|野餐|A picnic is a meal eaten outdoors, typically in a relaxed and informal setting.  It often involves bringing prepared food and drinks to a scenic location. |例句：We had a lovely picnic by the lake. 我们在湖边享受了一次愉快的野餐。

例句：The family packed a picnic basket for their day trip to the park.  这个家庭为他们去公园的一天旅行打包了一个野餐篮。


近义词：outdoor meal, outing


反义词：indoor meal, formal dinner
