epiphyseal:/ˌɛpɪˈfiːziəl/| adj. |骨骺的；骺端的| Relating to the epiphysis, the end part of a long bone.  The epiphyseal plate is the area of growing cartilage in a long bone. |例句：The epiphyseal line is visible on the X-ray. X光片上可见骨骺线。|例句：Fractures involving the epiphyseal plate can affect bone growth. 涉及骨骺板的骨折会影响骨骼生长。|近义词：epiphysial|反义词：diaphyseal
