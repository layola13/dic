escudos:/ɪˈskuːdoʊz/| n. |盾牌（复数）;  埃斯库多（葡萄牙语系国家货币，复数）|Escudos refers to the plural form of the word "escudo," which was the name of the currency unit in several Portuguese-speaking countries and some former Portuguese colonies.  It can also refer to shields, literally or figuratively. |Example:  The museum displayed ancient Roman escudos. = 博物馆展示了古罗马的盾牌。The old escudos were replaced by the euro. = 旧的埃斯库多被欧元取代了。|近义词：shields (as a physical object), coins (as currency)|反义词：无|名词
