It often refers to the presence of undesirable microorganisms that can cause contamination or infection. | Example: The nonsterility of the surgical instruments led to a post-operative infection.  手术器械的非无菌性导致了术后感染。|近义词：contamination, infection, impurity |反义词：sterility, cleanliness, purity
