fight-shy-of:/faɪt ʃaɪ əv/ | adj. | 害怕战斗的；回避战斗的 |  Reluctant or unwilling to engage in conflict or confrontation.  This term describes someone who avoids fights or arguments, often to the point of being considered cowardly. | He was fight-shy-of the bully, preferring to walk away rather than confront him. 他害怕那个恶霸，宁愿走开也不愿与他对抗。 | 近义词:  timid, cowardly, pusillanimous, non-confrontational | 反义词: aggressive, combative, belligerent |  形容词
