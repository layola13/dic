outeats:/ˌaʊtˈiːts/| vt.|吃得比…多；胜过|To eat more than someone else; to surpass in eating.|例句：He outeats everyone in the family. 他比家里的每个人都吃得多。|例句：In the eating contest, she completely outeats her opponents. 在吃东西比赛中，她完全胜过了对手。|近义词：outdo, surpass, exceed|反义词：underperform, be outdone
