scholars:/ˈskɒlərz/ /ˈskɑːlərz/| n. |学者，奖学金获得者 |  A scholar is a person who studies a subject in great detail, especially at an advanced level, often with the aim of becoming an expert.  Scholars are typically found in universities or other academic institutions, and their work often involves research and the publication of their findings.  | Example:  Many scholars believe that climate change is a serious threat.  许多学者认为气候变化是一个严重的威胁。

The renowned scholar dedicated his life to studying ancient civilizations.这位著名的学者毕生致力于研究古代文明。 |近义词:student, academic, expert, intellectual |反义词:ignoramus, dunce, layman
