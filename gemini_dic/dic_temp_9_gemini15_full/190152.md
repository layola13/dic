sanitizer:/ˈsænɪtaɪzər/| n.|消毒剂，杀菌剂|A substance that kills or inhibits the growth of microorganisms, especially bacteria and viruses.  It's used to clean and disinfect surfaces and objects.|Example:  The nurse used a hand sanitizer before examining the patient. 护士在检查病人之前使用了洗手液。|近义词：disinfectant, antiseptic|反义词：contaminant
