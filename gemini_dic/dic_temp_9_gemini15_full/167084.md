presuppose:/ˌpriːˈsʌpəʊz/|/prɪˈsʌpəʊz/| vt.|预先假定；以…为前提|To assume beforehand; to suppose as a precondition.  The argument presupposes a prior understanding of the subject matter.  This plan presupposes that we have enough funding. |近义词：assume, postulate, presume|反义词：deny, refute|动词


例句：

The theory presupposes a universe governed by natural laws. = 这一理论预设了一个受自然规律支配的宇宙。

His success presupposes hard work and dedication. = 他的成功是以辛勤工作和奉献为前提的。


词源:

* **pre-**:  prefix meaning "before" or "in advance"
* **suppose**: from Old French *supposer*, from Latin *supponere* "to place under, substitute," from *sub-* "under" + *ponere* "to put, place."


用法说明：


一般现在时:

He presupposes that I am aware of the problem. = 他预设我知道这个问题。

They presuppose a positive response from the customer. = 他们预设顾客会有积极的回应。


一般过去时:

She presupposed that he would be there on time. = 她预设他会准时到那里。

The scientist presupposed the existence of dark matter. = 科学家预设了暗物质的存在。


现在完成时:

We have presupposed a certain level of competence in our employees. = 我们已经预设了员工具备一定的胜任能力。

They have presupposed the success of the project.  = 他们已经预设了项目的成功。


过去完成时:

They had presupposed that the meeting would be short. = 他们预设会议会很短。

The detective had presupposed the suspect's guilt. = 侦探已经预设了嫌疑人的罪行。


将来时:

I will presuppose your cooperation in this matter. = 我将预设你在这件事上会合作。

They will presuppose a fair trial for the accused. = 他们将预设被告会得到公正的审判。


虚拟语气:

If we presupposed a different scenario, the outcome might be different. = 如果我们预设不同的情景，结果可能会有所不同。

I wish I had not presupposed his honesty. = 我真希望我没有预设他的诚实。


被动语态:

It is presupposed that all participants are familiar with the rules. = 预设所有参与者都熟悉规则。

The argument was presupposed on flawed logic. = 论证是以有缺陷的逻辑为前提的。


疑问句:

Does this theory presuppose a flat earth? = 这一理论是否预设地球是平的？

What does the author presuppose about the reader's knowledge? = 作者对读者的知识预设了什么？


否定句:

This theory does not presuppose any supernatural intervention. = 这一理论并没有预设任何超自然干预。

The study does not presuppose prior knowledge of statistics. = 这项研究不需要预先了解统计学。


PHRASE->
