ovipositor:/ˈoʊvɪpɒzɪtər/ | n. |产卵器；输卵管| A tube-like organ possessed by some female insects and other animals through which eggs are laid.  It is often specialized for inserting eggs into a specific location, such as plant tissue or the body of another organism. |Example: The wasp used its ovipositor to lay its eggs inside the caterpillar.  黄蜂用它的产卵器把卵产在毛虫体内。| 近义词：egg-laying tube, egg-depositing organ | 反义词：无
