fellon:/ˈfɛlən/| n.|重罪犯；恶棍|A felon is a person who has been convicted of a serious crime, typically one punishable by imprisonment for more than one year.  Felons often lose certain rights, such as the right to vote or own firearms. |The felon was sentenced to ten years in prison.  这个重罪犯被判处十年监禁。|criminal, convict, offender|law-abiding citizen, innocent person|名词
