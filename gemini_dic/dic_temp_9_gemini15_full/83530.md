hadith:/hæˈdiːθ/| n. |圣训，先知穆罕默德的言行录 | A hadith is a record of the sayings and actions of the Prophet Muhammad, and is a major source of guidance in Islam.  Hadiths are collected in books called hadith collections. |Example:  The hadith emphasizes the importance of charity. 这段圣训强调了施舍的重要性。|近义词：saying, tradition, anecdote |反义词：N/A
