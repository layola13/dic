sens:/sɛns/ | n. |感觉，意义，意识 |The sense of touch is very important.触觉非常重要。|近义词：feeling, meaning, perception |反义词：nonsense, absurdity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He senses danger. 他感觉到危险。
The dog senses my fear. 狗感觉到我的害怕。


一般过去时:
I sensed a change in his mood. 我感觉到他情绪的变化。
She sensed that something was wrong. 她感觉到有什么不对劲。


现在完成时:
Have you sensed any improvement? 你感觉到了任何改善吗？
I have sensed a shift in public opinion. 我感觉到民意的转变。


过去完成时:
He had sensed the impending doom. 他已经预感到厄运将至。
She had sensed his deception from the start. 她从一开始就感觉到他的欺骗。


将来时:
I will sense the difference. 我将感觉到区别。
They will sense the opportunity. 他们将感觉到机会。


虚拟语气:
If I sensed danger, I would leave. 如果我感觉到危险，我会离开。
I wish I had sensed his true intentions. 我真希望我当时察觉到他的真实意图。


被动语态:
The danger was sensed by everyone.  危险被每个人都感觉到了。
The change was sensed immediately.  变化被立即觉察到了。


疑问句:
Do you sense anything unusual? 你感觉有什么异常吗？
What do you sense is wrong? 你感觉哪里不对？


否定句:
I didn't sense any hostility. 我没有感觉到任何敌意。
He didn't sense the danger. 他没有感觉到危险。


PHRASE->
make sense  有意义
common sense 常识
in a sense  从某种意义上说
out of sense  失去理智
sense of humor 幽默感
