lankiness:/ˈlæŋkinɪs/| n. |瘦长；细长；高挑|The quality or state of being lanky;  being tall and thin.  It often implies a somewhat ungainly or awkward appearance due to disproportionate height and limb length.|例句：His lanky frame made him look awkward in the formal suit.  他的瘦长身材让他穿着正式西装显得笨拙。

例句：Her lanky figure was accentuated by the flowing gown.  她高挑的身材被飘逸的长袍衬托得更加突出。


近义词：slimness, tallness, slenderness, gauntness


反义词：stockiness, stoutness, corpulence
