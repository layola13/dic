pays:/peɪz/|v.|支付，报酬|To give someone money for work done, goods received, or a debt incurred.|例句：He pays his workers a fair wage. 他付给他的工人公平的工资。|例句：I have to pay my bills this week. 我这周必须付账单。|近义词：compensate, remunerate, settle|反义词：owe, receive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pays his bills on time. = 他按时支付账单。
She pays attention in class. = 她上课认真听讲。

一般过去时:
I paid for the groceries yesterday. = 我昨天付了杂货的钱。
They paid a lot of money for the house. = 他们花了许多钱买房子。

现在完成时:
He has paid off his student loans. = 他已经还清了学生贷款。
She has paid her dues to the society. = 她已经为社会贡献了自己的力量。

过去完成时:
I had paid the rent before the deadline. = 我在截止日期前已经付了房租。
They had paid all their debts before they left the country. = 他们在离开国家之前已经还清了所有债务。

将来时:
I will pay you back next week. = 我下周会还你钱。
She will pay for the damages. = 她将赔偿损失。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would pay for the trip. = 如果我有更多钱，我会支付旅行费用。
I wish I could pay off my mortgage sooner. = 我希望我能更快地还清抵押贷款。

被动语态:
The workers are paid weekly. = 工人们每周领工资。
The bill was paid in full. = 账单已全额支付。

疑问句:
How much did you pay for that car? = 你那辆车花了多少钱？
Will you pay by credit card or cash? = 你是用信用卡还是现金支付？

否定句:
He didn't pay attention to the warning. = 他没有注意警告。
They haven't paid the taxes yet. = 他们还没交税。


PHRASE->
pay off = 还清；取得成功
pay back = 还钱；报复
pay attention = 注意；留心
pay a visit = 拜访
pay the price = 付出代价
