gods:/ɡɑːdz/| n. |神，众神 |Plural of god.  Refers to a supernatural being or beings, typically worshipped as the creator and ruler of the universe.  In many religions, gods have specific roles and powers. The concept of gods varies greatly across different cultures and belief systems. |Example: Many ancient civilizations worshipped multiple gods. 许多古代文明崇拜多个神。 The gods of Olympus were powerful and influential. 奥林匹斯山诸神强大而有影响力。|近义词：deities, divinities |反义词：mortals, humans
