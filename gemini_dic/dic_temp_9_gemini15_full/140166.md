offenses:/ˈɒfɛnsɪz/ /ˈɔːfɛnsɪz/| n.|罪行；冒犯；进攻|the actions or practices of an individual, group, or nation that are considered unlawful or illegal, or that cause offense or injury to others.|例句：He was charged with several serious offenses. 他被指控犯有几项严重罪行。|例句：His remarks caused great offense to many people. 他的话冒犯了许多人。|近义词：crimes, misdeeds, transgressions, insults|反义词：compliance, obedience, respect
