litigations: /ˌlɪtɪˈɡeɪʃənz/ | n. | 诉讼，讼诉 | The plural form of "litigation," referring to multiple lawsuits or legal proceedings.  It encompasses all the stages of a legal battle, from initial filing to final judgment. | Example: The company is facing numerous litigations related to its faulty products. 这家公司正面临多起与其有缺陷产品相关的诉讼。 | 近义词：lawsuits, legal proceedings, actions | 反义词：settlements (和解)
