erects:/ɪˈrekt/| vt.|竖立；建造；使…直立|To raise or set upright; to construct or build (something, especially a building or structure).|例句：The workers erected a new building in the city center. 工人们在市中心建造了一座新楼。|例句：He erected a fence around his garden. 他在花园周围竖起了一道栅栏。|近义词：construct, build, raise, establish|反义词：demolish, destroy, raze, collapse

一般现在时:
He erects a tent every time he goes camping. 他每次去野营都搭帐篷。
The company erects skyscrapers all over the world.  这家公司在世界各地建造摩天大楼。

一般过去时:
They erected a monument to honor the fallen soldiers. 他们竖立了一座纪念碑来纪念阵亡的士兵。
The ancient Romans erected many impressive structures. 古罗马人建造了许多令人印象深刻的建筑。
