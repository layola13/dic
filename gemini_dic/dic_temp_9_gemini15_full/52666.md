digitizers:/ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪzərz/| n. |数字化仪，数位转换器|A digitizer is a device that converts analog data, such as images or sounds, into digital data that can be processed by a computer.|Example:  The artist used a digitizer to create a digital painting from her traditional sketch.  艺术家用数字化仪将她的传统素描转换成数字绘画。|近义词:converter, scanner, encoder|反义词:analog device
