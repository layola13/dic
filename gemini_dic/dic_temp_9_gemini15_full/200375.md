skyjacked:/ˈskaɪdʒækt/ | v. |劫持飞机|To seize control of an aircraft in flight, typically by force or threat of force.  This term specifically refers to the hijacking of an airplane. |例句：The terrorists skyjacked the plane and demanded a ransom.  恐怖分子劫持了飞机并索要赎金。

The plane was skyjacked and diverted to a different country. 这架飞机被劫持并改道至另一个国家。|近义词：hijack, commandeer |反义词：release, relinquish
