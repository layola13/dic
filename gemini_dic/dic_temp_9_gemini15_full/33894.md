circumstellar: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈstɛlə(r)/ | adj. | 周恒星的；围绕恒星的 | Relating to the area around a star.  | Example:  Circumstellar dust clouds can obscure the view of a star. 周恒星尘埃云可能会遮挡恒星的视线。  |近义词： periastral (if referring to a specific point closest to the star) | 反义词： interstellar (between stars) |
