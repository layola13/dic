pods:/pɑːdz/|n.|豆荚；吊舱；（飞机等的）吊挂舱|A pod is a long, narrow, seed-containing case, especially of a pea or bean.|The pea pods are ready to harvest.  豌豆荚已经可以收割了。|The spaceship deployed its landing pods.  宇宙飞船释放了它的着陆舱。|capsule, case, container|—


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plant produces many pods. = 这植物产出许多豆荚。
He always carries his fishing pods. = 他总是带着他的钓鱼用具。


一般过去时:
She picked the pods from the plants. = 她从植物上摘下豆荚。
The pilot ejected from the pod. = 飞行员从吊舱中弹射出来。


现在完成时:
They have harvested all the pods. = 他们已经收割了所有的豆荚。
The engineers have successfully tested the escape pod. = 工程师们已经成功地测试了逃生舱。


过去完成时:
The farmers had already collected the pods before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，农民们已经收割了豆荚。
The astronauts had activated the emergency pod before the ship exploded. = 宇宙飞船爆炸之前，宇航员们启动了紧急逃生舱。


将来时:
We will plant more seeds to get more pods next year. = 明年我们将种植更多种子以获得更多豆荚。
The company will launch a new satellite with advanced pods.  公司将发射一颗带有先进吊舱的新卫星。


虚拟语气:
If the pods were ripe, we would harvest them. = 如果豆荚成熟了，我们会收割它们。
If the pod had deployed properly, they would have survived. = 如果吊舱正常展开，他们就能活下来。


被动语态:
The pods are harvested by hand. = 豆荚是手工收割的。
The escape pods were designed for emergency situations. = 逃生舱是为紧急情况设计的。


疑问句:

How many pods are on the spacecraft? = 宇宙飞船上有多少个吊舱？


否定句:
