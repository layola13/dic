fickler:/ˈfɪklər/| adj.|反复无常的人；善变的；不稳定的|A fickle person is someone who changes their mind or feelings often and suddenly.  They are unreliable and unpredictable.|例句：He's so fickle; he can't decide what he wants for dinner.|近义词：capricious, erratic, mercurial, volatile, temperamental|反义词：constant, steadfast, reliable, dependable
