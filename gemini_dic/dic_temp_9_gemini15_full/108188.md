lattices:/ˈlætɪsɪz/ | n. | 格子，点阵，晶格 | A lattice is a structure consisting of a regular arrangement of points in space.  It can refer to a network of intersecting lines or points, often used in describing the structure of crystals or other materials.  The term is also used in mathematics and computer science. | Example: The crystal structure of diamond is a diamond lattice.  金刚石的晶体结构是金刚石晶格。

The atoms in a salt crystal are arranged in a cubic lattice.  盐晶体中的原子排列成立方晶格。


近义词：grid, network, framework | 反义词：amorphous structure (无定形结构)
