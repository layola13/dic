prefacer: /ˈprɛfəsər/ | n. |  序言撰写者；前言作者 | A person who writes a preface.  | Example: The prefacer of the book explained the author's intentions.  这本书的序言作者解释了作者的意图。 |近义词:  introducer, foreword writer | 反义词:  N/A | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The prefacer writes a concise introduction. = 序言撰写者写了一个简洁的引言。
She often prefaces books for her friends. = 她经常为朋友的书写序言。


一般过去时:
The prefacer wrote a compelling preface. = 序言撰写者写了一个引人入胜的序言。
He prefaced his speech with a joke. = 他在演讲开始时讲了一个笑话。


现在完成时:
The prefacer has written many prefaces for various books. = 序言撰写者已经为各种书籍撰写了许多序言。
She has prefaced her argument with a relevant anecdote. = 她用一个相关的轶事来开头论证。


过去完成时:
The prefacer had already written the preface before the deadline. = 序言撰写者在截止日期前已经写好了序言。
He had prefaced his remarks with a disclaimer. = 他在发言前加上了免责声明。


将来时:
The prefacer will write a preface for the new edition. = 序言撰写者将为新版撰写序言。
She will preface her presentation with a brief overview. = 她将在演示文稿开始时做一个简短的概述。


虚拟语气:
If I were a prefacer, I would write a personal introduction. = 如果我是一个序言撰写者，我会写一个个人介绍。
I wish the prefacer had explained the controversial points. = 我希望序言作者解释了有争议的观点。


被动语态:
The preface was written by a renowned scholar. = 序言是由一位著名的学者撰写的。

疑问句:

否定句:

He did not preface his opinion with any evidence. = 他没有用任何证据来开头表达他的观点。
