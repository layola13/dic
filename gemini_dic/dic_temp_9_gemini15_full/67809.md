eyestalk:/ˈaɪˌstɔːk/| n. |眼柄；(甲壳类动物的)眼柄|A mobile stalk bearing the eye of certain crustaceans, such as shrimps and crabs.  It allows for independent movement of each eye. |例句：The shrimp's eyestalks moved independently, scanning its surroundings.  这只虾的眼柄独立地移动着，扫描着周围的环境。

例句：The researcher carefully examined the crab's eyestalks under a microscope. 研究人员在显微镜下仔细检查了螃蟹的眼柄。


近义词：eye-stalk (less common variant) | 反义词：None
