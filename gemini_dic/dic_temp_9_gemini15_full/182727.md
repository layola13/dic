repairing:/rɪˈpɛərɪŋ/| v. |修理；修复；弥补|The act of restoring something to a good condition; mending.  It involves fixing damage or defects. |例句：He's repairing the roof. 他正在修理屋顶。

例句：The mechanic is repairing my car. 技工正在修理我的车。


近义词：mending, fixing, restoring, patching, reconditioning |反义词：breaking, damaging, destroying |词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are repairing the bridge. = 他们正在修理桥梁。
We are repairing the fence regularly. = 我们定期修理栅栏。


一般过去时:
He repaired the broken chair. = 他修理了坏掉的椅子。
She repaired the tear in her dress. = 她缝补了她衣服上的破洞。


现在完成时:
They have repaired the road. = 他们已经修好了路。
We have repaired the leak in the roof. = 我们已经修好了屋顶的漏水处。


过去完成时:
He had repaired the car before the race. = 他在比赛前已经修好了车。
She had repaired the computer before it crashed. = 电脑崩溃前她已经修好了它。


将来时:
They will repair the machine tomorrow. = 他们明天将修理机器。
We will repair the damage as soon as possible. = 我们将尽快修复损坏的部分。


虚拟语气:
If I had the tools, I would repair the bicycle. = 如果我有工具，我会修理自行车。
I wish I could repair the broken vase. = 我真希望我能修好这个破花瓶。


被动语态:
The car is being repaired at the garage. = 汽车正在修理厂修理。
The bridge was repaired last year. = 这座桥去年修好了。


疑问句:
Are they repairing the house? = 他们正在修理房子吗？
Is the computer being repaired? = 电脑正在修理吗？


否定句:
He didn't repair the appliance. = 他没有修理电器。
They haven't repaired the roof yet. = 他们还没修好屋顶。


PHRASE->

in need of repair: 需要修理
beyond repair:  无法修理
