eradicates:/ɪˈrædɪkeɪts/ | vt. | 根除；消灭；杜绝 | To completely destroy or get rid of something harmful or unwanted.  | 例句：The government is committed to eradicating poverty. 政府致力于消除贫困。

例句：The new medicine successfully eradicates the virus. 新药成功地消除了病毒。


近义词：eliminate, exterminate, abolish, destroy, remove | 反义词：cultivate, foster, promote, encourage | 词性：动词


USAGE->

The government eradicates poverty through various programs. = 政府通过各种项目来消除贫困。
This pesticide eradicates pests effectively. = 这款杀虫剂能有效杀灭害虫。


一般过去时:
The disease was eradicated in the 1970s. = 这疾病在20世纪70年代被根除了。
They eradicated the weeds from the garden. = 他们从花园里除掉了杂草。


现在完成时:

The campaign has eradicated illiteracy in many areas. = 这场运动已经在许多地区根除了文盲。


过去完成时:

The team had eradicated the infestation by the end of the week. = 团队在本周末前已经根除了虫害。

虚拟语气:
If we acted decisively, we could eradicate this problem. = 如果我们果断行动，就能根除这个问题。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

词源:
The word "eradicate" comes from the Latin word "eradicāre," which is a combination of "ex-" (out) and "radix" (root).  Therefore, literally, it means "to root out" or "to remove completely."
