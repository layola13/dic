precomment:/ˈpriːkɒment/ | n. |预评论，事前评论 |A precomment is a comment made before a formal discussion or publication. It often serves to express an opinion or provide preliminary feedback.| 例句：The precomment on the proposal highlighted several areas needing further clarification. 预评论中指出了提案中几个需要进一步澄清的方面。|近义词： preliminary remark, advance comment, preliminary observation |反义词：postcomment, afterthought
