serenity:/səˈrenɪti/| n. |平静；宁静；安详|A state of being calm, peaceful, and untroubled; tranquility.  It implies a freedom from disturbance and a feeling of composure.|例句：The serenity of the countryside was a welcome change from the city's noise.  乡村的宁静与城市喧嚣形成了令人愉快的对比。

例句：After a long day, she found serenity in meditation.  漫长的一天后，她在冥想中找到了平静。|近义词：calmness, peace, tranquility, quietude |反义词：turmoil, chaos, anxiety, stress |词性：名词
