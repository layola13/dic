radicalizations: /ˌrædɪkəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 激进化；激进化 | The process of becoming radical or causing someone to become radical.  It often refers to the adoption of extremist political or religious ideologies. |例句：The rapid radicalizations of young people online is a serious concern.  网络上年轻人的迅速激进化令人担忧。


例句：The government is working to counter the radicalizations of terrorist groups. 政府正在努力打击恐怖组织的激进化。


近义词：extremization,  politicization (in a radical context)


反义词：moderation,  liberalization,  de-radicalization


词性：名词
