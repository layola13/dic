mountainside:/ˈmaʊntənsaɪd/| n. |山腰，山坡 |The side of a mountain.  A sloping area on the side of a mountain.|例句：We hiked up the mountainside to reach the summit. 我们沿着山坡徒步到达山顶。
  
例句：Wildflowers bloomed profusely on the sunny mountainside. 向阳的山坡上鲜花盛开。


近义词：slope, hillside, mountainslope |反义词：mountaintop, summit, peak |词性：名词
