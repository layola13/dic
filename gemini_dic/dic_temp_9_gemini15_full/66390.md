execution:/ˌɛksɪˈkjuːʃən/ | n. | 执行；处决；完成；死刑 | The execution of the plan was flawless.  计划的执行完美无瑕。 The prisoner was sentenced to execution. 囚犯被判处死刑。 | 近义词：performance, implementation, carrying out, capital punishment | 反义词：suspension, postponement, cancellation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government executes its policies effectively. = 政府有效地执行其政策。
They execute their duties diligently. = 他们勤勉地履行职责。

一般过去时:
The project was executed on time and within budget. = 项目按时并在预算内完成。
The criminal was executed yesterday. = 罪犯昨天被处决了。

现在完成时:
We have executed all the tasks assigned to us. = 我们已经完成了所有分配给我们的任务。
The death penalty has been executed in many countries. = 死刑在许多国家被执行。

过去完成时:
They had executed the plan before the meeting. = 他们曾在会议前执行了计划。
The court had already executed the sentence. = 法院已经执行了判决。

将来时:
They will execute the order immediately. = 他们将立即执行命令。
The company will execute its expansion plan next year. = 公司将于明年执行其扩张计划。

虚拟语气:
If they executed the plan properly, they would have succeeded. = 如果他们正确地执行计划，他们就会成功。
I wish they had executed the plan more carefully. = 我希望他们更仔细地执行计划。

被动语态:
The plan was executed flawlessly. = 计划被完美地执行了。
The sentence will be executed tomorrow. = 判决将于明天执行。

疑问句:
How will the plan be executed? = 计划将如何执行？
When was the execution carried out? = 行刑是什么时候执行的？

否定句:
They did not execute the plan as planned. = 他们没有按照计划执行计划。
The death penalty was not executed in that case. = 在那种情况下，死刑没有被执行。
