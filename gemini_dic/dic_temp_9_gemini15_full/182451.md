renomination:/ˌriːˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃən/|/ˌriːˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |再次提名；重新任命 |The act of nominating someone again for the same position or office.  It implies a previous nomination. |例句：His renomination was met with widespread support.他的再次提名得到了广泛的支持。

例句：The president's renomination is expected to be a formality.  总统的再次提名预计将是例行公事。


近义词：reappointment, reappointment


反义词：resignation, dismissal


词性：名词
