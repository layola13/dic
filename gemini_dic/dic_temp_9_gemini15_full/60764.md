electrolyses:/ɪˈlɛktrəlaɪsɪz/ | n. | 电解 | The process of decomposing a substance using electricity.  Electrolysis involves passing an electric current through a substance to cause a chemical change.  | Example: The electrolysis of water produces hydrogen and oxygen. 电解水会产生氢气和氧气。 | 近义词:electrolytic decomposition | 反义词:synthesis
