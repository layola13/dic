peeked:/piːkt/| v.|偷看，窥视|To look quickly and secretly at something.  |例句：I peeked through the keyhole. 我透过钥匙孔偷看。|例句：She peeked at the present before her birthday. 她在生日前偷看了礼物。|近义词：glance, glimpse, spy|反义词：stare, gaze


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He peeks through the blinds. = 他透过百叶窗偷看。
She peeks at her phone during the meeting. = 她在会议期间偷偷看手机。

一般过去时:
I peeked into the room. = 我偷偷地看了一眼房间。
They peeked at the answers before the test. = 他们在考试前偷看了答案。

现在完成时:
She has peeked at my diary. = 她已经偷看过我的日记了。
He has peeked at the letter several times. = 他已经好几次偷看过那封信了。

过去完成时:
I had peeked at the results before he announced them. = 在他宣布结果之前，我已经偷偷看过结果了。
They had peeked into the box before opening it. = 他们打开盒子之前已经偷偷看过盒子里面了。

将来时:
I will peek at the menu before ordering. = 我点菜前会先看看菜单。
She will peek into the room to see if he is there. = 她会偷偷看一下房间里有没有他。

虚拟语气:
If I had peeked, I would have seen the surprise. = 如果我偷看的话，我就看到惊喜了。
I wish I hadn't peeked at her email. = 我真希望我没有偷看她的邮件。

被动语态:
The letter was peeked at by someone. = 那封信被人偷看了。
The box was peeked into by the curious child. = 好奇的孩子偷看了盒子。

疑问句:
Did you peek at my notes? = 你偷看我的笔记了吗？
Have they peeked at the answer key? = 他们偷看过答案了吗？

否定句:
I didn't peek at your paper. = 我没有偷看你的试卷。
He didn't peek at the gift. = 他没有偷看礼物。
