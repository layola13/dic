shimmers:/ˈʃɪmərz/| v.|闪光，闪烁，微光|To shine with a soft, unsteady light.|The heat made the air shimmer. 热气使空气闪烁。 The water shimmered in the sunlight. 水在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：glitter, gleam, twinkle|反义词：dull, dim


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The candle shimmers in the dark.  = 蜡烛在黑暗中闪烁。
The silk fabric shimmers beautifully. = 丝绸织物闪闪发光。

一般过去时:
The stars shimmered in the night sky. = 星星在夜空中闪烁。
The lake shimmered under the moonlight. = 月光下，湖面波光粼粼。

现在完成时:
The heat has shimmered the distant landscape. = 热气使远处的景色显得模糊不清，微微闪动。
The dew has shimmered the spiderweb. = 露珠使蜘蛛网闪闪发光。

过去完成时:
The mirage had shimmered in the desert heat. = 海市蜃楼在沙漠的热浪中闪烁过。
The painting had shimmered with vibrant colors before fading. = 这幅画在褪色之前曾闪耀着鲜艳的色彩。

将来时:
The city lights will shimmer in the distance. = 城市的灯光将在远处闪烁。
The newly polished car will shimmer in the sun. = 新抛光的汽车将在阳光下闪闪发光。

虚拟语气:
If the diamond shimmered more, it would be more valuable. = 如果钻石闪耀得更亮，它就会更有价值。
I wish the snow would shimmer under the moonlight like in the painting. = 我希望雪能在月光下像画中一样闪闪发光。

被动语态:

The fabric is shimmered with gold thread. = 面料上绣有金线，闪闪发光。

疑问句:
Does the water shimmer in the sunlight? = 水在阳光下闪闪发光吗？

否定句:
The dull metal did not shimmer. = 这种暗淡的金属没有闪光。
The surface of the water did not shimmer today. = 今天水面没有闪光。
