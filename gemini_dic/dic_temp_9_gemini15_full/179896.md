refait:/rəˈfɛ/| v.|重做，翻新|To make or do something again, often in a better way.|The tailor had to refait the suit because of a mistake in the stitching.  裁缝不得不重做这套西装，因为缝纫处有错误。| redo, remake, refurbish | ruin, destroy | 动词
