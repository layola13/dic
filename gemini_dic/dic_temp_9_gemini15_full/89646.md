hooking:/ˈhʊkɪŋ/ | v. |钩住；吸引；使着迷 | To catch or secure something with a hook; to capture someone's attention or interest. |例句：He was hooking the fish. 他正在钩鱼。  |例句：The movie's plot kept me hooking from beginning to end. 这部电影的情节让我从头到尾都着迷。|近义词：catching, captivating, engaging, intriguing |反义词：boring, uninteresting, repulsive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hooks the fish with a skillful hand. = 他熟练地用钩子钩鱼。
She hooks the audience with her captivating performance. = 她以迷人的表演吸引了观众。

一般过去时:
The fisherman hooked a large tuna. = 渔夫钓到了一条大金枪鱼。
The advertisement hooked many consumers. = 这则广告吸引了许多消费者。

现在完成时:
He has hooked several big fish this morning. = 他今天早上已经钓了几条大鱼。
The company has hooked a large client. = 这家公司已经赢得了一位大客户。

过去完成时:
She had hooked the bait onto the hook before casting the line. = 她把鱼饵钩在鱼钩上之后才抛线。
They had hooked up a deal before the meeting concluded. = 他们在会议结束前达成了协议。

将来时:
I will hook the trailer to the car. = 我将把拖车钩到车上。
He thinks he will hook a big one this weekend. = 他认为这个周末他会钓到一条大鱼。

虚拟语气:
If I had a better hook, I would catch more fish. = 如果我有一个更好的鱼钩，我会钓到更多鱼。
If the marketing campaign had been more hooking, we would have achieved better results. = 如果营销活动更吸引人，我们会取得更好的成果。

被动语态:
The fish was hooked and reeled in. = 鱼被钩住并卷了上来。
The audience was hooked by the speaker's story. = 听众被演讲者的故事吸引住了。

疑问句:
Did you hook anything today? = 你今天钓到什么了吗？
Was the presentation hooking enough to attract investors? = 演示是否足够吸引人以吸引投资者？

否定句:
He didn't hook any fish all day. = 他整天一条鱼也没钓到。
The campaign failed to hook the target audience. = 这场活动未能吸引目标受众。
