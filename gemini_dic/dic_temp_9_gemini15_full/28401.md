carbonaro:/ˌkɑːrbəˈnɑːroʊ/| n.|碳纳罗；（意大利菜）碳烤意面|A carbonara is a pasta dish originating from Italy.  It typically includes pasta, guanciale (cured pork jowl), eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, and black pepper. The exact origin and history are debated, with various claims and regional variations.|例句：He ordered a classic carbonara for dinner. 他晚餐点了一份经典的碳烤意面。|例句：The restaurant is famous for its creamy carbonara. 这家餐馆以其奶油味十足的碳烤意面而闻名。|近义词：pasta dish, noodle dish|反义词：无
