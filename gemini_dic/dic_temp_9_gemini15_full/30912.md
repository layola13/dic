chain-reacts:/tʃeɪn riˈækts/| v.|连锁反应；(尤指核反应)链式反应|To undergo or cause a chain reaction.  A chain reaction is a sequence of events where each event causes the next, often leading to a rapid and escalating outcome.  This is particularly relevant in nuclear physics and chemistry, but can also be used metaphorically.|例句：The explosion chain-reacted throughout the building. 爆炸在整个建筑物中发生连锁反应。

The news of the scandal chain-reacted across social media.  丑闻的消息在社交媒体上引发了连锁反应。|近义词：cascade, snowball, escalate|反义词：stagnate, cease
