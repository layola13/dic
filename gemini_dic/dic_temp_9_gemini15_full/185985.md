rewatch:/ˌriːˈwɑːtʃ/| vt.|重看，再看|To watch something again.|例句：I'm going to rewatch my favorite movie tonight. 今晚我要重看我最喜欢的电影。|例句：She rewatched the documentary to understand the details better. 她重看纪录片以便更好地理解细节。|近义词：watch again, review, revisit|反义词:  None specifically.  The opposite would be to not watch it again.
