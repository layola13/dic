rephotograph:/ˌriːˈfoʊtəɡræf/| vt.|重新拍照；翻拍|To photograph something again, often with improved techniques or to correct flaws in a previous photograph.|例句：The photographer decided to rephotograph the scene using a different lens. 摄影师决定用不同的镜头重新拍摄这个场景。

例句：The damaged photograph was rephotographed to enhance the details. 受损的照片被重新拍摄以增强细节。
