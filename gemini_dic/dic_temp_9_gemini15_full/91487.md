hymenopter:/haɪˈmɛnɒptər/| n.|膜翅目昆虫|A hymenopteran is an insect belonging to the order Hymenoptera, characterized by membranous wings and a typically narrow waist.  This order includes ants, bees, wasps, and sawflies. They are known for their complex social structures (in many species) and diverse ecological roles. |Example: The honeybee is a well-known example of a hymenopter.  蜜蜂是膜翅目昆虫的一个著名例子。  The study of hymenopterans is a significant field in entomology.  膜翅目昆虫的研究是昆虫学的一个重要领域。|近义词：insect (general term)|反义词：无
