manang:/məˈnæŋ/| n. |玛南（尼泊尔语中对已婚妇女的称呼）|A term of respect used in Nepal to address or refer to a married woman, often an elder or someone of higher social standing.  It carries connotations of respect and seniority. |  My aunt Manang gave me wise advice.  我的玛南阿姨给了我明智的建议。 |  阿姨，大姐 (depending on context)|  年轻人 (younger woman) | 名词
