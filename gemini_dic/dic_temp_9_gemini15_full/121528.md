mismanaged:/ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒd/| v. adj.|管理不善的；处理不当的|The verb "mismanage" means to manage badly or inefficiently, resulting in poor outcomes.  It implies a lack of skill, foresight, or care in handling something.|例句：The company was mismanaged, leading to its bankruptcy. 这家公司管理不善，导致破产。

The project was seriously mismanaged from the start.  这个项目从一开始就严重管理不善。

近义词：mishandle, misdirect, botch, blunder,  administer poorly

反义词：manage well, administer effectively, direct skillfully


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mismanage the resources. = 他们管理资源不善。
He mismanages his time consistently. = 他总是时间管理不好。


一般过去时:
The team mismanaged the campaign. = 团队把活动搞砸了。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
