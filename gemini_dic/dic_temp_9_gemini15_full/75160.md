fritting:/ˈfrɪtɪŋ/| v.|  fritter away |无谓地浪费；消磨；|To waste time or money on unimportant things. |He frittered away his inheritance on gambling and fast cars. 他把继承的遗产都挥霍在赌博和豪车上。|squander, waste, dissipate|save, conserve, accumulate|动词


USAGE->

She fritters her money on useless trinkets. = 她把钱浪费在无用的装饰品上。
