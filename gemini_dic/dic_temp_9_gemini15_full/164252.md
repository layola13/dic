predoubtfully: /ˌpriːˈdaʊtflɪ/ | adv. | 犹豫地；迟疑地；不确定地 |  The word "predoubtfully" is a relatively rare and somewhat archaic adverb. It suggests a hesitation or uncertainty that is felt before expressing doubt. It implies a degree of apprehension or fear before expressing skepticism or uncertainty.  It combines the prefix "pre-" (before) with "doubtfully," intensifying the sense of hesitant doubt. | 例句：He predoubtfully admitted his involvement in the scheme. 他犹豫地承认自己参与了该计划。


|近义词：hesitantly, uncertainly, dubiously, tentatively, cautiously | 反义词：confidently, assuredly, certainly |  词性: 副词
