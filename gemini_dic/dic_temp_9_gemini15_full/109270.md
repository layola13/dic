lemnian:/ˈlɛmniən/| adj.|莱姆诺斯的；莱姆诺斯岛的|Of or relating to Lemnos, a Greek island in the Aegean Sea.  The adjective is used to describe things originating from or associated with Lemnos.|The Lemnian clay was famous in ancient times for its use in pottery.|莱姆诺斯的粘土因其在古代陶器中的用途而闻名。|近义词：Aegean (in a broader sense)|反义词：无
