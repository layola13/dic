micrometeorite:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmiːtiəraɪt/| n. |微陨石 |A micrometeorite is a very small meteorite, typically less than one millimeter in size. They are constantly bombarding the Earth's atmosphere and surface.  | Example: Micrometeorites are constantly impacting spacecraft, causing small but cumulative damage. 微陨石不断撞击航天器，造成微小但累积性的损坏。 |近义词: cosmic dust, space dust |反义词:macrometeorite
