hypermobility:/ˌhaɪpərmoʊˈbɪləti/| n. |过度活动性，关节过度灵活|The excessive range of motion in one or more joints.  This condition can be caused by various factors, including genetic predisposition, connective tissue disorders, and certain medical conditions.|例句：Her hypermobility made her prone to dislocations.她的关节过度灵活让她容易脱臼。

例句：The gymnast's exceptional hypermobility contributed to her success. 这名体操运动员非凡的关节过度灵活促成了她的成功。


近义词：joint laxity, articular hyperlaxity|反义词：joint stiffness, ankylosis|词性：名词
