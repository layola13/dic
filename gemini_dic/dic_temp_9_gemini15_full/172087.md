purpling:/ˈpɜːrpliŋ/ | adj. | 变紫的；紫色的 | Turning purple or becoming purplish in color.  The adjective describes something in the process of turning purple, or having a purplish hue. |例句：The sunset painted the sky with purpling hues. 夕阳给天空涂上了紫色的色彩。  The bruises on his arm were slowly purpling. 他胳膊上的淤青慢慢地变成了紫色。 |近义词：purple, purplish, violet-tinged |反义词：bleaching, paling
