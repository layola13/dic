recoiler:/rɪˈkɔɪlər/| n. | 后坐力；畏缩者；退却者 | A recoiler is someone or something that recoils.  It can refer to a person who shrinks back from something unpleasant or dangerous, or to a mechanism that experiences recoil, such as a gun. | Example: The powerful rifle had a significant recoil.  强大的步枪有明显的後座力。 The recoiler in the debate refused to answer the difficult question.  在辩论中，那个畏缩的人拒绝回答那个棘手的问题。|近义词：shrinker, flincher, recoiler(mechanism)|反义词：advancer, aggressor,  bold person


USAGE->
