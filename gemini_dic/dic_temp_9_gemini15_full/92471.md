hyposulphate:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈsʌlfeɪt/| n. |连二亚硫酸盐|Hyposulphate refers to a salt of hyposulfurous acid, a compound that is not very stable and rarely encountered.  It is more commonly referred to as dithionate.  The term is often used interchangeably with dithionate, though technically they are slightly different.
