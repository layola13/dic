outputting:/ˈaʊtˌpʊtɪŋ/| v. |输出；产生；输出（数据）|The act of producing or sending out something, especially data or information.|例句：The computer is outputting the results of the calculation.  计算机正在输出计算结果。

例句：The factory is outputting a large number of cars each day. 工厂每天生产大量的汽车。


近义词：producing, generating, emitting |反义词：inputting, receiving
