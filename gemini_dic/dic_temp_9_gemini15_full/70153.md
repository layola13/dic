fiascos:/fiˈæskəʊz/| n. |彻底的失败；惨败 | A fiasco is a complete failure, often a public or embarrassing one. It implies a degree of absurdity or ludicrousness to the failure.  | The party was a complete fiasco; the cake fell on the floor, the music system broke down, and nobody showed up.  这场派对彻底失败了；蛋糕掉在地上，音响系统坏了，而且没有人来。|failure, debacle, catastrophe, disaster |success, triumph |名词
