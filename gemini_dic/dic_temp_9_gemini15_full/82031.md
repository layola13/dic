grisaille:/ɡriˈzɑːj/| n.|灰色画;灰度画|A monochromatic painting executed in shades of gray to simulate the effect of sculpture or low-relief;  a painting done in shades of gray.  It often serves as an underpainting for a more colorful work. |例句：The artist created a stunning grisaille of a classical statue.  这位艺术家创作了一幅令人惊叹的古典雕像灰色画。

The museum showcased a collection of 17th-century grisailles.  博物馆展出了17世纪的一批灰色画。|近义词：monochrome painting, grayscale painting|反义词：polychrome painting
