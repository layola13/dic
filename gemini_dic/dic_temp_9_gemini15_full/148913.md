overwillingness: /ˌoʊvərˈwɪlɪŋnəs/ | n. |过分乐意；过度热心；急于求成| Excessive willingness or eagerness, often to a degree that is considered inappropriate or detrimental.  It implies a readiness to do something that might be unwise or harmful.| The overwillingness of the junior staff to take on extra responsibilities led to burnout.  过分乐意的初级员工承担额外责任导致了倦怠。  |近义词：eagerness, enthusiasm, avidity, keenness|反义词：reluctance, unwillingness, hesitation, apathy|名词
