outfitting:/ˈaʊtfɪtɪŋ/| n. |装备；配备；服装供应 |The process of providing someone or something with equipment or supplies, especially clothing.|例句：The store offers a complete outfitting service for climbers.这家商店为登山者提供全套装备服务。|近义词：equipment, provision, supply |反义词：disarming, stripping


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shop outfits climbers with the best gear. = 这家商店为登山者配备最好的装备。
They outfit their expedition with state-of-the-art technology. = 他们为他们的探险队配备了最先进的技术。


一般过去时:
The team was outfitted for the Arctic expedition. = 该团队已为北极探险做好了装备准备。
We outfitted the boat with new sails before the race. = 我们在比赛前为船配备了新的帆。


现在完成时:
They have outfitted several expeditions to the Himalayas. = 他们已经为几支喜马拉雅探险队配备了装备。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
