geographers:/dʒiˈɑːɡrəfərz/ | n. |地理学家 |People who study geography. Geographers analyze spatial relationships, patterns, and processes on Earth's surface. They use various methods, including fieldwork, remote sensing, and Geographic Information Systems (GIS), to understand the distribution of human and physical features, environmental changes, and the interactions between them. |Many geographers work in universities, research institutions, government agencies, and private companies. |近义词：cartographers (制图师), spatial scientists (空间科学家) |反义词：N/A
