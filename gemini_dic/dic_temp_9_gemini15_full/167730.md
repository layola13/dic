prig:/prɪɡ/| n.|假道学，自命不凡的人|A person who behaves in a way that is annoying because they think they are morally superior to other people.  They often display excessive self-righteousness and pretense of superior virtue.|He's such a prig; he criticizes everyone for their minor flaws.|近义词：hypocrite, sanctimonious, self-righteous|反义词：humble, unassuming
