cloister:/ˈklɔɪstər/| n. |修道院回廊；隐居；隔离 |A cloister is a covered walkway, especially one that is part of a monastery or convent and has an open arcade. It often provides a tranquil place for reflection and meditation.  |例句：The monks walked in the cloister, contemplating their prayers. 修道士在回廊里行走，沉思祈祷。

例句：She sought refuge in the cloister, far from the distractions of the world. 她寻求在隐居中避难，远离世俗的干扰。|近义词：arcade, gallery, quadrangle|反义词：open space, public area|
