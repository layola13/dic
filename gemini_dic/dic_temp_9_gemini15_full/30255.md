celsius:/ˈsɛlsiəs/| n.|摄氏度|Celsius is a scale and unit of measurement for temperature.  It is named after Anders Celsius, a Swedish astronomer who developed a similar scale.  Water freezes at 0 degrees Celsius and boils at 100 degrees Celsius at standard atmospheric pressure.|例句：The temperature today is 25 degrees Celsius. 今天的温度是25摄氏度。|例句：Water boils at 100 degrees Celsius. 水在100摄氏度沸腾。|近义词：centigrade|反义词：Fahrenheit|名词
