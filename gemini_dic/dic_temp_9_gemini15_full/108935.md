leek:/liːk/| n.|韭葱，大蒜|A leek is a vegetable of the onion family, with long, flat, dark green leaves and a white bulb.  It has a mild, oniony flavor.|Example:  I added some chopped leeks to the soup. 我在汤里加了一些切碎的韭葱。|近义词：onion, scallion, shallot|反义词：None specifically, but perhaps "strong-flavored vegetables" could be considered a contrast.
