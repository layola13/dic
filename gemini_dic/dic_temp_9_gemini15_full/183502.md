repump:/riːˈpʌmp/| vt.|重新抽送；再次泵送|To pump again or anew; to pump something again.  This word is formed by the prefix "re-" (again) and the verb "pump".|例句：The firefighters had to repump the water out of the flooded basement. 消防员不得不再次将水从被淹的地下室抽出去。

例句：The mechanic repumped the car's brakes after replacing the brake fluid. 技工更换了刹车油后，又给汽车的刹车重新加压。|近义词：re-circulate, pump again |反义词：drain, empty
