miss-a-cue:/mɪs ə kjuː/| v.|错过提示，误解信号|to fail to notice or understand a signal or hint; to misunderstand a cue in a conversation or performance.|例句：He missed a cue during the play and the audience laughed. 他在戏剧中错过了一个提示，观众都笑了。

例句：I think I missed a cue in the conversation and now she's angry. 我觉得我在谈话中错过了一个暗示，现在她生气了。


近义词：misunderstand, misinterpret, overlook|反义词：understand, comprehend
