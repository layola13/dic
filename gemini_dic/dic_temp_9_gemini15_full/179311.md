redocumented:/ˌriːˈdɒkjuˌmentɪd/| v. |重新记录，重新编写文档|To document something again, often with revisions or updates.  This usually implies a more thorough or accurate documentation than the previous one.  It suggests a process of reviewing, correcting, and expanding upon existing documentation.|例句：The software's functionality has been significantly enhanced, so the manual needs to be redocumented.|近义词：revise, update, amend, re-document|反义词：delete, omit
