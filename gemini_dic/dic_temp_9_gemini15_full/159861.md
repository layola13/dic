polymathy:/ˈpɒliˌmæθi/| n. |博学；多才多艺|The quality of possessing knowledge in many different fields of study.  It represents a breadth of expertise across diverse areas, rather than deep specialization in a single field. |  His polymathy was evident in his writings, which spanned philosophy, science, and literature.  他的博学在他的著作中显而易见，这些著作涵盖了哲学、科学和文学。| erudition, versatility, scholarship | specialization, ignorance | 名词
