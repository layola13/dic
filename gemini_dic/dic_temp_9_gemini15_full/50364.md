depopulate:/diːˈpɒpjuleɪt/ |/diːˈpɑːpjuleɪt/| vt. |使人口减少；使无人居住| To reduce the population of (a place) significantly; to remove the inhabitants from (a place).| 例句：The war depopulated the entire region. = 战争使整个地区人口锐减。

The plague depopulated the city in the 14th century. = 14世纪瘟疫使这座城市人口锐减。


近义词：empty, evacuate, thin out | 反义词：populate, colonize
