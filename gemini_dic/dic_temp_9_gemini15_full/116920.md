mayors:/ˈmeɪərz/| n.|市长们|The plural form of "mayor," referring to multiple individuals holding the office of mayor.  Mayors are elected officials responsible for the administration of a city or town.|例句：The mayors of several cities met to discuss regional issues.  几个城市的市长们会面讨论区域问题。|近义词：city executives, municipal leaders|反义词：无
