papillose:/pæˈpɪləʊs/| adj.|乳突状的，具乳突的|Covered with small nipple-like protuberances.  The term is often used to describe the surface texture of plants, insects, or other organisms.|例句：The leaf surface was papillose, giving it a slightly rough texture.  叶面呈乳突状，使其触感略粗糙。|例句：The insect's wings were papillose, with tiny bumps visible under magnification.  昆虫的翅膀是乳突状的，在放大镜下可以看到微小的凸起。|近义词：rough, bumpy, warty|反义词：smooth, glabrous
