infusive:/ɪnˈfjuːsɪv/ | adj. | 注入的；灌输的；浸润的 |  Having the quality of infusing; tending to infuse or instill.  It describes something that gradually permeates or instills something else, often subtly influencing a feeling, quality, or characteristic. | 例句：The infusive atmosphere of the old library calmed her nerves.  =  古老图书馆里充满感染力的氛围使她平静下来。

The infusive power of his words inspired the audience. = 他话语中充满感染力的力量激励了观众。


近义词：permeative, pervasive, penetrating, inspiring | 反义词：superficial, shallow, uninspiring


词源：源自动词 "infuse" (注入，灌输)，加后缀 "-ive" (形容词后缀)，表示具有…性质的。  "infuse" 来自于拉丁语 "infundere" (to pour in),  由 "in-" (into) 和 "fundere" (to pour) 构成。
