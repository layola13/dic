/pelt/ | n. | 盘子；碟子 | A flat, usually round, dish used for serving food.  |  She put the cake on a plate. 她把蛋糕放在盘子上。 |  dish, platter |  bowl (反义词,部分情况)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plate is clean. = 盘子是干净的。
He uses a large plate for his dinner. = 他用一个大盘子吃晚饭。


一般过去时:
The waiter brought a plate of food. = 服务员端来一盘食物。
She dropped the plate and it broke. = 她打碎了盘子。


现在完成时:
I have washed all the plates. = 我已经洗完所有的盘子了。
She has finished eating from her plate. = 她已经吃完了盘子里的食物。


过去完成时:
I had already put the plates away before he arrived. = 他来之前我已经把盘子收起来了。
They had broken several plates during the party. = 他们在派对上打碎了好几个盘子。


将来时:
I will wash the plates after dinner. = 晚饭后我会洗盘子。
They will serve the dessert on small plates. = 他们会用小盘子盛放甜点。


虚拟语气:
If I had a bigger plate, I could serve more food. = 如果我有一个更大的盘子，我就能盛放更多食物。
I wish I hadn't dropped the plate. = 我真希望我没有打碎盘子。


被动语态:
The plates are washed by the dishwasher. = 盘子是用洗碗机洗的。
The food was served on elegant plates. = 食物是用精致的盘子盛放的。


疑问句:
Is there a plate for me? = 有我的盘子吗？
What kind of plates do you have? = 你有什么样的盘子？


否定句:
There aren't enough plates for everyone. = 盘子不够每个人用。
He didn't use a plate for his soup. = 他喝汤没用盘子。


PHRASE->
a plate of food 一盘食物
a clean plate 干净的盘子
a cracked plate 破裂的盘子
a dinner plate 餐盘
a side plate 副盘
