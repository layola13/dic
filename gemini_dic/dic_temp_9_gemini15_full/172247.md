pusillanimously:/ˌpjuːsɪˈlænɪməsli/| adv.|胆怯地；懦弱地；优柔寡断地| acting in a cowardly or faint-hearted way.|The general, pusillanimously, ordered a retreat.将军胆怯地命令撤退。|近义词：cowardly, timidly, fearfully|反义词：bravely, courageously, boldly


USAGE->

He usually acts pusillanimously in the face of danger. 他通常在危险面前表现得很胆小。
She pusillanimously avoids confrontation. 她胆怯地避免冲突。
