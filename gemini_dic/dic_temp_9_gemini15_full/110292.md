lige:/ˈliːʒ/| n.|  效忠；忠诚；臣服 |  liege refers to the feudal relationship of loyalty and service owed by a vassal to his lord. It implies a strong bond of obligation and allegiance.  |例句：The knight swore allegiance to his liege lord.  骑士向他的领主宣誓效忠。 |近义词：allegiance, fealty, loyalty |反义词：treason, betrayal, disloyalty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pledges his allegiance to his liege. = 他向他的领主宣誓效忠。
The vassal owes service to his liege. = 臣子必须服侍他的领主。


一般过去时:
The knight served his liege faithfully. = 骑士忠诚地服侍他的领主。
The people submitted to their liege. = 人民臣服于他们的领主。


现在完成时:
The kingdom has long been loyal to its liege. = 王国长期以来一直忠于它的领主。
The vassal has served his liege for many years. = 臣子已经服侍他的领主多年了。


过去完成时:
The people had already pledged their loyalty to their liege before the war began. = 在战争开始之前，人民已经向他们的领主宣誓效忠。
The knight had served his liege since he was a boy. = 骑士从孩提时代起就服侍他的领主。


将来时:
The vassal will continue to serve his liege. = 臣子将继续服侍他的领主。
The people will remain loyal to their liege. = 人民将继续忠于他们的领主。


虚拟语气:
If he were a true liegeman, he would defend his lord. = 如果他是真正的臣子，他会保卫他的领主。
I wish my liege were more just. = 我希望我的领主更公正一些。


被动语态:
The king was served by his loyal liegemen. = 国王受到他忠诚的臣民的服侍。
His commands were obeyed by his liege. = 他的命令被他的领主服从。


疑问句:
To whom does the vassal owe allegiance? = 臣子向谁效忠？
Who is the liege lord of this land? = 这片土地的领主是谁？


否定句:
The vassal did not betray his liege. = 臣子没有背叛他的领主。
He was not a true liegeman to the king. = 他不是国王真正的臣子。
