词源:

* **hyper-**:  From Greek *hupér*, meaning "over," "above," "beyond."  This prefix indicates excess or intensification.

* **aggressive**: From Latin *aggressus*, past participle of *aggredi*, meaning "to attack," "to go to," "to approach."
