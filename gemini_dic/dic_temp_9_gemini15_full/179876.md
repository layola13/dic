reexpress:/ˌriːɪkˈspres/| vt.|重新表达；再表达|To express something again or in a different way.|例句：He reexpressed his concerns to the committee. 他向委员会再次表达了他的担忧。|例句：She reexpressed her ideas in simpler terms. 她用更简单的术语重新表达了她的想法。|近义词：rephrase, restate, reformulate|反义词：None specifically, but words like "obfuscate" or "misrepresent" are antonyms in the sense of doing the opposite.|动词
