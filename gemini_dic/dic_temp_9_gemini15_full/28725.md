carnage:/ˈkɑːrnɪdʒ/ | n. | 大屠杀；残杀；浩劫 | The noun "carnage" refers to the killing of a large number of people, typically in a violent and chaotic way. It suggests widespread destruction and bloodshed.  | The battle resulted in terrible carnage.  这场战斗导致了可怕的大屠杀。|  slaughter, massacre, butchery, bloodshed | peace, harmony, tranquility | 名词
