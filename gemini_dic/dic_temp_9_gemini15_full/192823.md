sear:/sɪər/| vt. n.|搜索，搜寻；烧焦，枯萎|to look for something carefully and thoroughly; to burn the surface of something|例句：I searched my bag for my keys. 我翻遍了我的包找钥匙。  The fire seared the wood.  大火烧焦了木头。|近义词：hunt, seek, scan, scorch|反义词：find (as a noun), ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He searches for information online every day. = 他每天都在网上搜索信息。
She searches the house for her lost cat. = 她正在家里搜寻她丢失的猫。

一般过去时:
They searched the forest for the missing child. = 他们搜寻了森林寻找失踪的孩子。
The intense heat seared the paint on the car. = 剧烈的热度把汽车上的油漆烧焦了。

现在完成时:
I have searched everywhere for my glasses. = 我到处都找过我的眼镜了。
The sun has seared the grass. = 太阳把草晒枯萎了。


过去完成时:
She had searched for hours before she found it. = 她找了好几个小时才找到它。
The prolonged drought had seared the farmland. = 长期的干旱使农田枯萎了。


将来时:
We will search the area tomorrow. = 我们明天将搜查那个地区。
The intense heat will sear the metal. = 剧烈的热度会烧焦金属。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would search the archives. = 如果我有时间，我会搜索档案。
If the sun were stronger, it would sear the leaves. = 如果太阳更强，它会把叶子晒焦。


被动语态:
The house was searched by the police. = 这所房子被警察搜查了。
The steak was seared to perfection. = 牛排煎得恰到好处。


疑问句:
Did you search the internet for that information? = 你有在网上搜索那个信息吗？
Was the wood seared by the fire? = 木头是被火烧焦的吗？


否定句:
I didn't search the entire building. = 我没有搜查整栋楼。
The fire didn't sear the furniture. = 火没有烧焦家具。
