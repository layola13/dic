roadworks:/ˈroʊdˌwɜːrks/| n. |道路工程，道路施工|Roadworks refer to work being carried out on a road, often causing disruption to traffic.  This includes repairs, construction, or maintenance activities.|例句：The roadworks are causing major delays this morning.  今早的道路施工导致了严重的延误。|例句： We need to find an alternative route due to the ongoing roadworks. 由于正在进行的道路施工，我们需要寻找一条替代路线。|近义词：road construction, road repair, street works|反义词：open road, clear road
