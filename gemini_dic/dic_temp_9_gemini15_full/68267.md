fakeers:/ˈfeɪkərz/| n.|骗子，江湖骗子|A person who deceives others, especially by pretending to have skills or knowledge they do not possess.  They often exploit people's trust for personal gain. |例句：The fakeers promised to cure his illness with a magic potion, but it was all a scam.  那些骗子承诺用魔药治愈他的疾病，但这完全是个骗局。  |近义词：imposters, charlatans, swindlers, con artists |反义词：truth-tellers, honest people
