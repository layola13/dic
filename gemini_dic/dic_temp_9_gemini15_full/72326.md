flow:/floʊ/| n. v.|流动；流程；流畅；流量|The continuous movement of something, such as a liquid, gas, or electricity; a smooth, continuous movement; a sequence of events or actions.|例句：The river flows smoothly to the sea. 河水平缓地流向大海。The workflow is efficient. 工作流程很有效率。|近义词：stream, current, course, sequence |反义词：stagnation, standstill


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water flows constantly. = 水不断地流动。
The river flows to the ocean. = 河流流向大海。

一般过去时:
The lava flowed down the mountain. = 岩浆流下山。
The traffic flowed smoothly this morning. = 今早交通很流畅。

现在完成时:
