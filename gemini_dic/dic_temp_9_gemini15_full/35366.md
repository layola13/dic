co-instantaneous: /ˌkoʊɪnˌstænˈteɪniəs/ | adj. |同时发生的，同时存在的 | Existing or occurring at the same time.  The term emphasizes the simultaneity and often implies a close connection or interaction between the events or phenomena. |  The eruption of the volcano and the earthquake were co-instantaneous events. 火山爆发和地震是同时发生的事件。 The two signals arrived co-instantaneously at the receiver. 两个信号同时到达接收器。 |近义词：simultaneous, concurrent, coincident |反义词：sequential, successive
