semi-porcelain:/ˈsɛmi ˈpɔːrsələn/| n.|半瓷|A type of ceramic ware that is less refined and less expensive than true porcelain, but stronger and more durable.  It is typically made with a higher proportion of clay and a lower firing temperature than porcelain.|例句：The restaurant's tableware was made of semi-porcelain, a practical choice for everyday use.  这家餐馆的餐具是用半瓷制成的，这是日常使用的实用选择。|近义词：earthenware, stoneware|反义词：porcelain
