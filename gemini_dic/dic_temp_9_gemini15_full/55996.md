do:/duː/| /duː/| v.|做，进行，完成|To perform or carry out an action or task; to bring about a result. It can also be used as an auxiliary verb to form questions and negative sentences.|例句：I do my homework every day. 我每天做作业。|例句：Do you like apples? 你喜欢苹果吗？|近义词：perform, accomplish, execute|反义词：undo, destroy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I do my homework every day. = 我每天做我的家庭作业。
She does yoga every morning. = 她每天早上做瑜伽。

一般过去时:
He did his best. = 他尽力了。
They did the dishes after dinner. = 他们晚饭后洗了碗。

现在完成时:
I have done my work. = 我已经完成了我的工作。
She has done a great job. = 她做得很好。

过去完成时:
I had done my chores before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经完成了我的家务。
They had done all the preparations before the party. = 派对开始前他们已经完成了所有的准备工作。

将来时:
I will do my best to help you. = 我会尽力帮助你。
She will do the laundry tomorrow. = 她明天会洗衣服。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would do it differently. = 如果我是你，我会做得不一样。
I wish I had done it differently. = 我希望我当时做得不一样。

被动语态:
The work was done quickly. = 工作很快完成了。
The dishes are being done now. = 碗现在正在洗。

疑问句:
Do you understand? = 你明白吗？
Does she like cats? = 她喜欢猫吗？

否定句:
I don't like coffee. = 我不喜欢咖啡。
He doesn't play the piano. = 他不弹钢琴。


PHRASE->
do one's best = 尽力而为
do over = 重做
do without = 没有...也行
do up = 装饰；系好
do away with = 废除
