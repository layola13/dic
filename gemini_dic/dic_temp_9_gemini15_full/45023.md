ctenoid:/ˈsiːtənɔɪd/| adj.|栉鳞的；有栉鳞的|Having ctenoid scales; relating to or characterized by ctenoid scales. Ctenoid scales are a type of fish scale characterized by tiny, comb-like projections or spines along the posterior edge.  |Example: Many species of fish, such as perch and flounder, possess ctenoid scales.|近义词：scaly (in a general sense)|反义词：cycloid (referring to another type of fish scale)
