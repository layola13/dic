outmanned:/ˌaʊtˈmæn/| v. |人数少于，兵力不足|To have fewer people than (another group, force, etc.).  It implies a disadvantage due to numerical inferiority.|The rebels were vastly outmanned by the government troops. 叛军人数远远少于政府军。| The small police force was badly outmanned by the rioters.  小警察部队在人数上远逊于暴徒。| outnumbered, undersupplied | well-manned, adequately staffed | 动词
