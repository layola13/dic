mistreatment:/mɪsˈtriːtmənt/| n. |虐待；虐待行为 |The act of treating someone badly or unfairly, often involving violence, neglect, or exploitation.  It can refer to physical, emotional, or psychological harm. |例句：The mistreatment of animals is a serious offense. 动物虐待是一项严重的罪行。

例句：The report detailed the horrific mistreatment suffered by the refugees. 报告详细描述了难民遭受的可怕虐待。


近义词：abuse, ill-treatment, maltreatment, cruelty, oppression, exploitation

反义词：good treatment, kindness, care, compassion


词性:名词
