secludes: /sɪˈkluːdz/ | vt. |使隔离；使隐居；使隐蔽 | To keep (someone or something) apart from other people or things. To place or keep in seclusion. |例句：The mountains secluded the small village from the outside world.  山峦将这个小山村与外界隔绝开来。

The dense forest secluded the hikers from any sign of civilization.  茂密的森林使徒步旅行者与任何文明的迹象隔绝。


近义词：isolate, separate, insulate, seclude, shut off, quarantine |反义词：expose, reveal, integrate, connect


词性：动词
