fonda:/ˈfɒndə/| n. |基金；捐赠；储备金|A fonda is a sum of money or other assets that are set aside for a specific purpose, such as supporting a charitable cause, funding a project, or providing financial security.|例句：The university established a fonda to support research in renewable energy.  大学设立了一个基金来支持可再生能源的研究。|近义词：fund, endowment, grant |反义词：debt, deficit
