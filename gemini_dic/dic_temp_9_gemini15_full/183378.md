reprogrammed:/ˌriːˈproʊɡræmd/| v. |重新编程，改造|To change the programming of (a computer or other electronic device); to alter the genetic makeup of (a cell or organism).  |例句：The software was reprogrammed to fix the bug.  软件被重新编程以修复错误。

例句：Scientists successfully reprogrammed adult skin cells into stem cells. 科学家成功地将成年皮肤细胞重新编程为干细胞。

近义词：recoded, refashioned, remodelled


反义词：originally programmed, unchanged


词性: verb
