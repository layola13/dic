purring:/ˈpɜːrɪŋ/| n. |轻微的呼噜声；低沉的隆隆声|The sound a cat makes when it is content or sleepy.  It can also refer to a similar low, continuous humming or rumbling sound.|例句：The cat sat purring on my lap. 猫在我腿上发出呼噜声。

The engine was purring smoothly. 发动机平稳地发出低沉的隆隆声。|近义词：humming, rumbling, murmuring|反义词：roaring, screaming|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The kitten is purring contentedly. = 小猫舒服地发出呼噜声。
The engine purrs quietly. = 发动机安静地发出低沉的隆隆声。

一般过去时:
The cat purred loudly after eating. = 猫吃饱后大声地发出呼噜声。
The old car purred along the highway. = 那辆旧车沿着高速公路平稳地行驶。

现在完成时:
The cat has been purring for an hour. = 猫已经呼噜了一个小时了。
The machine has purred continuously since it was started. = 机器启动后一直持续地发出低沉的隆隆声。

过去完成时:
The cat had purred itself to sleep. = 猫已经呼噜着睡着了。
The engine had purred quietly before the sudden noise. = 在突然的噪音之前，发动机一直安静地发出低沉的隆隆声。

将来时:
The cat will purr when I pet it. = 当我抚摸它时，猫会发出呼噜声。
The new car will purr like a dream. = 新车将会像梦一样平稳地行驶。

虚拟语气:
If the cat were purring, it would be happy. = 如果猫在发出呼噜声，它就会很高兴。
I wish the engine would purr more quietly. = 我希望发动机能更安静地发出低沉的隆隆声。

被动语态:
(Purring is rarely used in the passive voice.)  The sound of purring was heard from the next room. = 从隔壁房间传来了呼噜声。


疑问句:
Is the cat purring? = 猫在发出呼噜声吗？
Was the engine purring smoothly before it broke down? = 发动机在坏掉之前运转平稳吗？

否定句:
The cat wasn't purring; it was hissing. = 猫没有发出呼噜声；它在嘶嘶地叫。
The engine didn't purr; it made a loud grinding noise. = 发动机没有发出低沉的隆隆声；它发出了很大的摩擦声。


PHRASE->
a contented purring = 满足的呼噜声
a soft purring = 轻柔的呼噜声
a deep purring = 深沉的呼噜声
a loud purring = 大声的呼噜声
a rumbling purr = 隆隆的呼噜声
