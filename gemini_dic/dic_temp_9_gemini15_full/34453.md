clavicle:/ˈklævɪkəl/| n.| 锁骨 | The clavicle, also known as the collarbone, is a long bone that connects the scapula (shoulder blade) to the sternum (breastbone). It plays a crucial role in shoulder movement and provides support to the upper body.  | Example: He fractured his clavicle during a skiing accident. 他在滑雪事故中锁骨骨折了。|近义词：collarbone|反义词：None
