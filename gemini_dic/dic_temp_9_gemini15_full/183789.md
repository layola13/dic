resculpt:/ˈriːskʌlpt/| vt.|重塑；重新塑造|To shape or mold something again; to give a new form or shape to something.|例句：The sculptor decided to resculpt the clay figure. 雕塑家决定重新塑造这个粘土雕像。

例句：The company is trying to resculpt its image after the scandal. 公司正试图在丑闻后重塑其形象。

近义词：reshape, remold, reform, remodel|反义词：destroy, demolish, ruin|动词
