prisoning: /ˈprɪzənɪŋ/ | v. |监禁；囚禁|The act of imprisoning someone or something.  It implies the confinement of a person or thing, often against their will.  The word emphasizes the act of the confinement itself rather than the state of being confined. |例句：The prisoning of political dissidents is a violation of human rights.  政治异见的囚禁是对人权的侵犯。
The swift prisoning of the suspect prevented further crime.  对嫌疑人的迅速监禁阻止了进一步的犯罪。|近义词：imprisoning, incarcerating, confining, jailing |反义词：releasing, freeing, liberating |动词
