face-off:/ˈfeɪs ɔːf/| n. v.|面对，对抗，对峙|A direct confrontation or contest, especially a brief, intense one.  It can refer to a physical confrontation, a verbal argument, or a competitive event.|例句：The two hockey players had a face-off at the center ice.  两个冰球运动员在中圈进行了面对面较量。

The debate turned into a face-off between the two candidates. 这场辩论演变成了两位候选人之间的直接对抗。|近义词：confrontation, showdown, clash, encounter|反义词：agreement, cooperation, harmony|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The two teams face off at the start of each game. = 每场比赛开始时，两队都会进行面对面的较量。
He often faces off against his rivals in business negotiations. = 他经常在商务谈判中与竞争对手对抗。
