preinquisitions:/ˌpriːˌɪŋkwɪˈzɪʃənz/| n. |预审；初步调查|Preliminary inquiries or investigations, often conducted before a full-scale inquiry or trial.  These investigations aim to gather initial information and evidence to determine whether a more thorough investigation is warranted.|The police conducted preinquisitions before arresting the suspect.  警方在逮捕嫌疑人之前进行了预审。| preliminary investigations, initial inquiries | full-scale investigations, trials | 名词
