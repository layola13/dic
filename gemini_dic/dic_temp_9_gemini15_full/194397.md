self-proving: /ˈsɛlf ˈpruːvɪŋ/ | adj. | 自证的，无需证明的 |  Something that is self-proving is inherently evident or requires no further proof or verification.  It demonstrates its truth or validity within itself. |  The document's digital signature makes it self-proving.  该文件的数字签名使其无需证明。

The witness's testimony was self-proving.  证人的证词是自证的。


近义词: self-evident, obvious, demonstrable, undeniable

反义词: requiring proof, needing verification, disputable, questionable


词性: adjective
