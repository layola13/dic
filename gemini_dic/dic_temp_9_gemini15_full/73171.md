The forbiddenness of the act was clear.  = 这行为的禁止性是显而易见的。

Complex Sentence:
Because of the forbiddenness surrounding the subject,  many people remained silent. = 由于围绕这个主题的禁忌，许多人保持沉默。


Compound Sentence:
The forbiddenness of the ritual was emphasized, and  consequently, few dared to participate. = 仪式是被禁止的这一点被强调了，因此，很少有人敢参与。
