disparaging: /dɪˈspærɪdʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 轻蔑的；贬低的；诋毁的 | Expressing a negative opinion of someone or something; belittling.  |例句：His disparaging remarks about her work deeply hurt her. 他对她工作的贬损言论深深地伤害了她。


例句：The critic's disparaging review caused the film's box office to plummet. 批评家贬低的评论导致电影票房暴跌。


近义词：belittling, derogatory, contemptuous, scornful, insulting, denigrating


反义词：complimentary, praising, flattering, laudatory


词性:形容词
