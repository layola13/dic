careening: /kəˈriːnɪŋ/ | v. | 摇晃着行进；蹒跚；急速倾斜 |  To move swiftly and wildly, often in an uncontrolled manner; to lean or sway to one side.  | The drunk driver careened down the street.  醉酒司机摇摇晃晃地驶过街道。  The ship careened violently in the storm. 船在暴风雨中剧烈倾斜。 |  lurch, swerve, tilt, veer |  steady, stabilize | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car careens down the hill. = 汽车摇摇晃晃地冲下山坡。
He careens through life without a plan. = 他漫无目的地生活。


一般过去时:
The bus careened sharply around the corner. = 公共汽车急转弯。
The rollercoaster careened through the twists and turns. = 过山车在蜿蜒曲折的轨道上飞驰。


现在完成时:
The ship has careened several times in the rough seas. = 这艘船在汹涌的海面上已经倾斜了好几次。
The project has careened towards failure. = 项目已经朝着失败的方向发展。


过去完成时:
The car had careened off the road before it burst into flames. = 汽车偏离道路后才起火。
The economy had careened into recession before the government intervened. = 政府干预之前，经济已经陷入衰退。


将来时:
The boat will careen dangerously if the storm intensifies. = 如果风暴加剧，船会危险地倾斜。
The company's stock price will likely careen downward without intervention. = 如果不干预，公司股价可能会急剧下跌。


虚拟语气:
If the driver hadn't been drunk, the car wouldn't have careened into the tree. = 如果司机没醉酒，汽车就不会撞到树上了。
If the ship had not careened, the passengers would not have been injured. = 如果船没有倾斜，乘客就不会受伤。


被动语态:
The car was careened into a ditch. = 车子被撞进沟里了。
The boat was careened by the strong wind. = 船被强风吹得倾斜。


疑问句:
Did the car careen off the road? = 车子是不是冲出了道路？
Will the plane careen during the turbulence? = 飞机在颠簸中会倾斜吗？


否定句:
The ship did not careen during the voyage. = 船在航行中没有倾斜。
The driver carefully avoided careening the car. = 司机小心地避免了汽车的摇晃。


PHRASE->
careen out of control = 失控
careen wildly = 疯狂地摇晃
careen into something = 撞上...
careen towards disaster = 朝着灾难发展
careen down a hill = 冲下山坡


词源:
Ultimately derives from the Old French word "carène," meaning "keel" (the lowest structural part of a hull). The nautical sense of "to heel over" evolved into the more general sense of "to swerve wildly."
