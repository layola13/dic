forkful: /ˈfɔːkfʊl/ | n. | 一叉的食物 | A forkful is the amount of food that can be lifted on a fork at one time.  It emphasizes the quantity held, not the type of food. | Example: He ate a forkful of mashed potatoes. 他吃了一叉土豆泥。 |近义词: mouthful, spoonful | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑用a tiny amount/bit)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eats a forkful of salad every day. = 他每天吃一叉沙拉。
She takes a forkful of pasta. = 她吃了一口意大利面。


一般过去时:
The child ate a large forkful of cake. = 孩子吃了一大叉蛋糕。
I took a forkful of the stew and tasted it. = 我舀了一叉炖菜尝了尝。


现在完成时:
He has already eaten three forkfuls of the pie. = 他已经吃了三叉派了。
She has only had one forkful of her dinner. = 她只吃了一叉饭。


过去完成时:
I had already eaten several forkfuls before they arrived. = 他们到达之前我已经吃了好几叉了。
She had barely touched a forkful of her food. = 她几乎没动过她盘子里的食物。


将来时:
I will take a forkful of this delicious-looking dessert. = 我要尝一口这个看起来美味的甜点。
He will probably eat several forkfuls of the roast beef. = 他可能会吃好几叉烤牛肉。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the food was so spicy, I wouldn't have taken such a large forkful. = 如果我知道食物这么辣，我就不会吃这么一大叉了。
I wish I had taken a smaller forkful; this is too much. = 我真希望我少吃点，太多了。


被动语态:
A forkful of the pudding was carefully placed on his plate. = 一叉布丁小心地放在了他的盘子里。
(This is less common; the passive voice is less natural with this word)


疑问句:
How many forkfuls have you eaten? = 你吃了多少叉？
Would you like another forkful? = 你想再吃一叉吗？


否定句:
He didn't eat a single forkful of his vegetables. = 他一口蔬菜都没吃。
She didn't want another forkful of the rich dessert. = 她不想再吃那份丰盛的甜点了。


PHRASE->
a generous forkful:  一大叉
a small forkful: 一小叉
a single forkful:  一叉
another forkful: 再来一叉
several forkfuls: 好几叉
