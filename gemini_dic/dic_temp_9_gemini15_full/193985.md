self-exhibition:/ˌsɛlfˌɛksɪˈbɪʃən/| n. |自我展示，自我表现 |The act of displaying oneself or one's work to others, often for the purpose of seeking attention, admiration, or validation.  This can range from subtle displays of talent or personality to more overt and attention-seeking behaviors.  It can be a positive way to share one's skills and creativity, or a negative behavior if it becomes excessive or exploitative.|例句：Her extravagant social media posts could be seen as a form of self-exhibition. = 她铺张浪费的社交媒体帖子可以被看作是一种自我展示。

His self-exhibition at the art gallery attracted many visitors. = 他在艺术画廊的自我展示吸引了许多参观者。|近义词：self-promotion, showing off, display, ostentation|反义词：self-effacement, humility, modesty|
