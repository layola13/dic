mesodermic:/ˌmɛzoˈdɜrmɪk/| adj. | 中胚层的；中胚层发育的 | Relating to the mesoderm, the middle layer of the three primary germ layers of an embryo.  The mesoderm develops into connective tissues, muscle, bone, and blood. | 例句：The heart is a mesodermic structure.  心脏是中胚层结构。


The mesodermic cells differentiate into various tissues. 中胚层细胞分化成各种组织。


近义词：mesoblastic (less common)


反义词：ectodermic, endodermic


词性：形容词

词源:
From "meso-" (middle) + "-derm" (skin, layer), referring to its position as the middle layer of the three primary germ layers in an embryo.  The "-derm" suffix is derived from the Greek word "derma" (skin).
