instrument:/ˈɪnstrəmənt/ | n. |工具；乐器；仪器；手段 |A tool or device used for a particular purpose.  It can also refer to a musical instrument or a means of achieving something. |例句：The surgeon used a variety of instruments during the operation. 外科医生在手术中使用了各种器械。

例句：The pianist played a beautiful melody on the instrument. 钢琴家用乐器演奏了一首优美的旋律。


近义词：tool, appliance, device, implement, means |反义词：None


词源：源自古法语 *instrument，最终源自拉丁语 *instrumentum，意为“工具，器械”，由 *instruere 派生而来，意为“装备，建造”。
