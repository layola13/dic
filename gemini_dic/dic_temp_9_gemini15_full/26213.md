butterer: /ˈbʌtərər/ | n. | 黄油涂抹者；谄媚者 | A person who spreads butter.  Also, figuratively, someone who flatters excessively. | 例句：The baker is a skilled butterer of bread.  = 那位面包师是个熟练的面包涂黄油的人。

The politician was a master butterer, charming everyone with his smooth words. = 这位政客是个老练的谄媚者，他用甜言蜜语迷住了所有人。

近义词：flatterer, fawner, toady | 反义词：critic, cynic
