dowerless:/ˈdaʊərləs/| adj.|无嫁妆的，没有陪嫁的|Lacking a dowry; without a dowry.  The term refers to a woman who does not bring property or wealth into a marriage.|Example:  She was a dowerless bride, relying entirely on her husband's family for support. 她是一位没有嫁妆的新娘，完全依靠丈夫的家庭来维持生计。|近义词：unendowed, portionless|反义词：dowered, wealthy
