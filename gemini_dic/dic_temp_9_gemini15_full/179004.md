redefinitions:/ˌriːˌdɛfɪˈnɪʃənz/ | n. |重新定义；新的定义 | The act of defining something again, often with a different or updated meaning.  This involves altering the established understanding or interpretation of a concept, term, or object. Redefinitions can be driven by societal shifts, technological advancements, or new discoveries.  | Example: The redefinitions of gender roles in the 21st century have led to increased social fluidity.  重新定义21世纪的性别角色导致了社会流动性的增加。|近义词：reinterpretations, reinterpretations, new definitions |反义词：original definitions, established meanings |名词
