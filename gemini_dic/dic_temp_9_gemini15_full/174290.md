quests:/kwɛsts/| n.|任务；探险；追求|A quest is a long and difficult search for something.  It can also refer to a search for knowledge or adventure.|例句：The knight embarked on a quest for the Holy Grail.  这位骑士踏上了寻找圣杯的征程。|例句：Her lifelong quest for knowledge led her to become a professor. 她毕生追求知识，最终成为了一名教授。|近义词：search, pursuit, mission, expedition|反义词：completion, accomplishment


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He quests for glory. 他追求荣耀。
She quests for knowledge. 她追求知识。


一般过去时:
They quested for the lost city. 他们寻找失落的城市。
He quested after truth. 他追寻真理。


现在完成时:
She has quested for years to find her family history. 她多年来一直在寻找她的家族历史。
They have quested tirelessly for a cure.  他们不知疲倦地寻找治愈方法。


过去完成时:
They had quested for days before they found the treasure.  他们在找到宝藏之前已经寻找了好几天。
He had quested long and hard before he finally achieved his goal. 他经过长时间的努力才最终实现了他的目标。


将来时:
She will quest for the lost artifact. 她将寻找失落的文物。
We will quest together for answers. 我们将一起寻找答案。


虚拟语气:
If I were to quest for something, it would be peace. 如果我要追求什么，那就是和平。
I wish I could quest for a simpler life. 我希望我能追求更简单的生活。


被动语态:
The treasure was quested for by many adventurers.  许多冒险家都在寻找这笔宝藏。
The truth is often quested for, but rarely found. 真理往往被人追寻，却很少被找到。


疑问句:
What are you questing for? 你在追求什么？
What did they quest for in the jungle? 他们在丛林里寻找什么？


否定句:
He did not quest for riches. 他不追求财富。
She does not quest for fame. 她不追求名利。


PHRASE->
on a quest: 正在进行一项任务
a quest for: 对…的追求
a noble quest: 一项崇高的任务
a perilous quest: 一项危险的任务
a lifelong quest: 一生的追求
