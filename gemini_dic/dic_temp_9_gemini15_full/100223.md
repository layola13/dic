interrogatively:/ɪnˈtɛrəɡətɪvli/| adv.|疑问地，质问地|In a manner suggesting a question; questioningly.|He looked at me interrogatively.|近义词：questioningly, inquiringly|反义词：assertively, declaratively


例句：

He raised his eyebrows interrogatively. 他带着疑问地扬起了眉毛。

She stared at him interrogatively, waiting for an answer. 她疑问地盯着他，等待着回答。
