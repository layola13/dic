daisies:/ˈdeɪziːz/| n.|雏菊（复数）|Daisy is a common name for plants in the genus Bellis and sometimes other genera in the family Asteraceae.  They are typically small, with a yellow center and white, yellow, or pink petals. Daisies are often associated with springtime and innocence. | 例句：The field was covered in a blanket of daisies.  田野里铺满了雏菊。

There were so many daisies in the meadow. 草地上有许多雏菊。
