gambeson:/ˈɡæmbɪzən/| n. |棉袄；茄克衫；护甲 |A gambeson is a quilted garment, typically worn as a padded undergarment or as a standalone piece of protective clothing.  Historically, it was worn by medieval soldiers and combatants under their armor to provide additional cushioning against blows.  In modern times, it might be worn for historical reenactment, SCA combat, or as a fashion item. | Example: The knight wore a gambeson under his chainmail for extra protection.  骑士在锁子甲下穿着一件棉袄以获得额外的保护。  The archer wore a simple gambeson as his only armor.  弓箭手只穿着一件简单的棉袄作为盔甲。|近义词：quilted jacket, padded coat, acolchado (Spanish)|反义词：None directly applicable, but perhaps "armor" in the context of protection.
