ostentator:/ˌɒstɛnˈteɪtə(r)/| n. |炫耀者；夸夸其谈者|A person who makes a show of their achievements or possessions; a boastful person.  |例句：He was an ostentator, always flaunting his wealth. 他是个炫耀者，总是炫耀他的财富。  |近义词：boaster, show-off, braggart |反义词：humble, modest |名词

词源:
The word "ostentator" comes from the Latin word "ostentare," meaning "to show," "to display," or "to exhibit."  The suffix "-tor" indicates an agent or doer of the action.  Therefore, an "ostentator" is literally someone who performs the act of ostentation.
