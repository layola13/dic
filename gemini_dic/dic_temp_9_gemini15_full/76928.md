gap:/ɡæp/ | n. | 差距；缝隙；空白；缺口 | A gap is a space or opening between two objects or parts of an object. It can also refer to a difference or lack of something. | 例句：There's a gap in the fence.  篱笆上有个缺口。  There's a significant gap between rich and poor. 富人和穷人之间存在着巨大的差距。 | 近义词：chasm, break, interval, void | 反义词：continuity, closure, fullness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There is a gap in the market. = 市场存在一个缺口。
A large gap separates the two buildings. = 两座建筑之间隔着很大的空隙。

一般过去时:
A gap opened up in the ground. = 地面上出现了一个裂缝。
A significant gap existed between their opinions. = 他们的意见存在很大的分歧。

现在完成时:
A gap has opened up in our team since John left. = 自从约翰离开后，我们的团队出现了一个空缺。
The gap between the two groups has narrowed. = 两组之间的差距已经缩小了。

过去完成时:
A huge gap had already appeared before the repair started. = 在修理开始之前就已经出现了一个巨大的裂缝。
A significant gap had existed between the two countries for decades. = 这两个国家几十年来一直存在着巨大的差距。

将来时:
A gap will appear in the schedule if we don't finish on time. = 如果我们不能按时完成，日程安排中就会出现空缺。
A wider gap will exist between the rich and the poor in the future. = 未来贫富差距将会更大。

虚拟语气:
If there were no gap between the rich and poor, society would be more equitable. = 如果没有贫富差距，社会将更加公平。
I wish there weren't such a wide gap in my knowledge of the subject. = 我希望我对这个主题的知识差距不要这么大。

被动语态:
The gap was filled by a new employee. = 这个空缺由一名新员工填补。
The gap between the two sides was bridged through negotiation. = 双方之间的差距通过谈判弥合了。

疑问句:
Is there a gap in your understanding? = 你有什么不明白的地方吗？
What is the gap between the two measurements? = 这两个测量值之间的差距是多少？

否定句:
There is no gap in the wall. = 墙上没有缝隙。
There was no significant gap in their knowledge. = 他们的知识没有明显的差距。
