corniculate: /kɔːˈnɪkjuleɪt/ | adj. | 角状的；具角的 | Having small horn-like projections.  The term describes a structure or surface that is adorned with small, horn-like projections or processes. These projections can be pointed, curved, or otherwise irregular in shape. | Example: The corniculate cartilage of the larynx is a small, horn-shaped structure.  喉部的角状软骨是一个小的角状结构。 | 近义词：horned,  protruding,  pointed | 反义词：smooth,  flat
