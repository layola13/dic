elaborates:/ɪˈlæbəreɪts/| vt.|详述；详细阐述；精心制作|To explain or describe something in detail; to develop or produce something in detail.|例句：The professor elaborates on the theory in the next chapter. 教授在下一章详细阐述了这个理论。|例句：She elaborates her designs with intricate details. 她用复杂精细的细节来完善她的设计。|近义词：explains, details, expands, develops|反义词：summarizes, abbreviates, condenses|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He elaborates his ideas clearly. = 他清晰地阐述了他的想法。
She elaborates on the historical context. = 她详细阐述了历史背景。

一般过去时:
The architect elaborated the building's design. = 建筑师详细阐述了建筑物的设。
He elaborated on his travel experiences. = 他详述了他的旅行经历。

现在完成时:
She has elaborated a complex plan. = 她已经制定了一个复杂的计划。
They have elaborated the details of the agreement. = 他们已经详细说明了协议的细节。

过去完成时:
He had elaborated his argument before the debate. = 他在辩论前已经详细阐述了他的论点。
She had elaborated a detailed report before the meeting. = 她在会议前已经撰写了一份详细的报告。

将来时:
He will elaborate on the topic in his next presentation. = 他将在下一次演示中详细阐述这个主题。
They will elaborate the strategy for the upcoming campaign. = 他们将为即将到来的活动制定详细的策略。

虚拟语气:
If he elaborated further, we would understand better. = 如果他进一步详细说明，我们会更好地理解。
I wish she had elaborated on the challenges she faced. = 我希望她能详细说明她面临的挑战。

被动语态:
The plan was elaborated by a team of experts. = 该计划是由专家团队制定的。
The theory has been elaborated in numerous publications. = 该理论已在许多出版物中得到详细阐述。

疑问句:
Will he elaborate on his proposal? = 他会详细说明他的提案吗？
Did she elaborate on the reasons for her decision? = 她是否详细说明了她做出决定的原因？

否定句:
He did not elaborate on the sensitive issue. = 他没有详细说明这个敏感的问题。
She didn't elaborate further on the subject. = 她没有进一步详细说明这个主题。
