multitaskers:/ˈmʌltiˌtæskərz/ | n. | 多任务处理者；能够同时处理多项任务的人 | People who can effectively handle multiple tasks simultaneously.  They possess the ability to switch between different activities without significant loss of efficiency or accuracy. | Example:  Many modern professionals pride themselves on being effective multitaskers.  许多现代专业人士都以自己是高效的多任务处理者而自豪。  |近义词：multi-handlers, jugglers |反义词：single-taskers
