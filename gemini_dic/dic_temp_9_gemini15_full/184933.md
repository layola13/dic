retarding:/rɪˈtɑːrdɪŋ/ | adj. | 延迟的；妨碍的；减速的 | Slowing down the progress or development of something.  |例句：The heavy rain was retarding the progress of the construction.  大雨延误了建筑工程的进度。

例句：Economic sanctions are retarding the country's growth.  经济制裁正在阻碍该国的经济增长。


近义词：delaying, hindering, impeding, slowing, inhibiting | 反义词：accelerating, hastening, speeding up | 词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heavy traffic is retarding my journey. = 拥堵的交通正在延误我的行程。
This medication is retarding the spread of the disease. = 这种药物正在延缓疾病的传播。


一般过去时:
The storm retarded the ship's arrival. = 暴风雨延误了船的到达。
The cold weather retarded the plant's growth. = 寒冷的天气阻碍了植物的生长。


现在完成时:
The drought has retarded the harvest. = 干旱已经延误了收成。
Regulations have retarded the development of new technologies. = 法规已经阻碍了新技术的开发。


过去完成时:
The previous delays had retarded the project's completion. = 先前的延误已经延误了项目的完成。
The lack of funding had retarded the research significantly. = 资金不足已严重阻碍了研究。


将来时:
Increased bureaucracy will retard the company's expansion. = 越来越多的官僚作风将阻碍公司的扩张。
The expected snowstorm will retard travel considerably. = 预计的暴风雪将大大延误出行。


虚拟语气:
If we don't address the issue, it will retard our progress. = 如果我们不解决这个问题，它将阻碍我们的进步。
I wish that these obstacles hadn't retarded our efforts. = 我希望这些障碍没有阻碍我们的努力。


被动语态:
The project was retarded by unforeseen circumstances. = 该项目因不可预见的情况而延误。
The development of the new drug was retarded by funding issues. = 新药的开发因资金问题而受阻。


疑问句:
Is anything retarding the progress of the negotiations? = 有什么东西在阻碍谈判的进程吗？
What factors are retarding economic growth in this region? = 哪些因素正在阻碍该地区的经济增长？


否定句:
The new technology did not retard the production process. = 新技术并没有延缓生产过程。
Nothing could retard his determination to succeed. = 没有什么能够阻碍他成功的决心。
