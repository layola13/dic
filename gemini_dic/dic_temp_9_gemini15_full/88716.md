hobbyists:/ˈhɑːbiɪsts/ | n. |业余爱好者，嗜好者 | People who engage in hobbies as a pastime.  They often dedicate significant time and effort to their pursuits. | Example: Many hobbyists collect stamps, coins, or other items.  许多业余爱好者收集邮票、硬币或其他物品。  |近义词：amateurs, enthusiasts |反义词：professionals

Hobbyists often share their creations online. = 业余爱好者经常在网上分享他们的作品。
Many hobbyists spend hours on their passions. = 许多业余爱好者花数小时在他们的爱好上。
