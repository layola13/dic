ego-surfing:/ˈiːɡoʊˌsɜːrfɪŋ/| n. |自我搜索，自我关注式网络搜索|The act of repeatedly searching for oneself online, often to check one's online presence, reputation, or mentions.  It's driven by a desire for validation or self-promotion.|例句：He spends hours each day ego-surfing, checking his social media profiles for new likes and comments. 他每天花几个小时自我搜索，查看他的社交媒体资料是否有新的点赞和评论。|例句：Her ego-surfing habit became a problem when it started interfering with her work. 她的自我搜索习惯成为一个问题，因为它开始影响她的工作。|近义词：online vanity searching, self-promotion, validation seeking|反义词：self-deprecation, humility
