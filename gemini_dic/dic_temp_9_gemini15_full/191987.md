scorer:/ˈskɔːrər/ | n. | 得分者；记分员 | A person or thing that scores or keeps score.  | Example: He was the top scorer in the league. 他是联赛中得分最高的球员。 |近义词：points-getter, marker | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scorer records each point accurately. = 记分员准确地记录每一分。
She is a high scorer in this game. = 她是这场比赛中的高分选手。


一般过去时:
The scorer tallied the final score. = 记分员计算了最终得分。
He was the top scorer last season. = 他是上赛季的最佳射手。
