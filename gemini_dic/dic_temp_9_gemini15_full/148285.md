oversoar:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔːr/| vt.|超越，凌驾于…之上|To fly or soar above or beyond (something).  It implies surpassing a limit or expectation.  The word suggests a significant degree of exceeding.|例句：The eagle oversoared the mountain peaks.  鹰飞越了山峰。 The company's success oversoared all expectations. 公司的成功超出了所有预期。|近义词：surpass, transcend, exceed, outstrip|反义词：underperform, fall short


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The eagle oversoars the mountain peaks. = 鹰飞越山峰。
He oversoars his rivals in skill. = 他在技巧上超越了他的对手。


一般过去时:
The plane oversoared the clouds. = 飞机飞越了云层。
She oversoared her previous best score. = 她超过了她之前的最高分。


现在完成时:
The athlete has oversoared all previous records. = 运动员已经打破了所有之前的记录。
The project has oversoared its initial budget. = 项目已经超出了最初的预算。


过去完成时:
They had oversoared their expectations before the final results were announced. = 在最终结果公布之前，他们就已经超出了预期。
The team had oversoared their previous performance before the playoffs. = 在季后赛之前，球队已经超越了他们之前的表现。


将来时:
The rocket will oversoar the atmosphere. = 火箭将飞越大气层。
She will oversoar her competitors in the race. = 她将在比赛中超越她的竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If he trained harder, he would oversoar his limitations. = 如果他训练更努力，他就能克服自己的局限性。
I wish I could oversoar my fears. = 我希望我能克服我的恐惧。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
