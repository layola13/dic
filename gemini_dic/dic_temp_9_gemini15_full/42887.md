courts-martial:/ˈkɔːrts ˈmɑːrʃəl/| n.|军事法庭；军事法庭审判|A court-martial is a military court that tries members of the armed forces for offenses against military law.  It's a legal proceeding conducted within the military justice system.|The soldier faced a court-martial for desertion.|military tribunal, court of inquiry|civilian court.
