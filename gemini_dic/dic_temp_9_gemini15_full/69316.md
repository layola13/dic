feces:/ˈfiːsiːz/| n. |粪便，排泄物 |Excrement discharged from the bowels.  It's a euphemism for excrement, often used in a more formal or scientific context. |例句：The farmer spread the feces as fertilizer on his fields. = 这位农民把粪便作为肥料撒在田里。

例句：The analysis of the feces revealed the presence of harmful bacteria. = 粪便分析显示存在有害细菌。


近义词：excrement, dung, stool, manure |反义词：无


词源：
From Latin *fex, *ficēs (plural), "dregs, sediment," of uncertain origin.  The word's evolution reflects a long-standing societal discomfort with directly naming bodily waste.
