nontutorial:/ˌnɒntjuːˈtɔːriəl/| adj. |非教程的，非指导性的|Not relating to or involving tutorials or teaching.  It emphasizes the absence of a structured teaching or instructional approach.|例句：The software offers a nontutorial approach to learning, encouraging exploration and self-discovery.  该软件提供了一种非教程式的学习方法，鼓励探索和自我发现。

例句：This nontutorial method relies heavily on user interaction and problem-solving.  这种非教程方法严重依赖用户的互动和问题解决。


近义词：unguiding, self-directed, independent, exploratory


反义词：tutorial, instructional, guided, didactic


词性:形容词
