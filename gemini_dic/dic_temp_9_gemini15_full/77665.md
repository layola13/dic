gelidly:/ˈdʒɛlɪdli/| adv.|冰冷地；冷淡地|In a gelid manner; extremely cold.  The word suggests a coldness that is intense and perhaps even unfriendly or aloof.|例句：He stared gelidly at the intruder. 他冰冷地盯着入侵者。

例句：She responded gelidly to his advances. 她冷淡地回应了他的追求。


近义词：frosty, icily, coldly, frigidly|反义词：warmly, heartily, enthusiastically|副词
