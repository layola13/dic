pesticide:/ˈpɛstɪsaɪd/| n. |杀虫剂，农药|A chemical substance used to kill harmful insects, rodents, fungi, weeds, and other pests.|例句：Farmers use pesticides to protect their crops from pests.  农民使用杀虫剂来保护他们的庄稼免受害虫侵害。|例句：Excessive use of pesticides can harm the environment.  过量使用杀虫剂会损害环境。|近义词：insecticide, herbicide, fungicide|反义词：natural pest control


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer uses pesticides on his crops. = 农民在他的庄稼上使用杀虫剂。
They spray pesticides regularly. = 他们定期喷洒农药。
