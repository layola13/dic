legislatrix:/ləˈdʒɪslətrɪks/| n.|立法者（尤指女性）|A legislatrix is a woman who makes laws, typically a female legislator.  The term emphasizes the female gender.| Example:  She is a respected legislatrix known for her progressive policies. 她是一位以其进步政策而闻名的受人尊敬的女立法者。|近义词：lawmaker, legislator, lawgiver|反义词：None specifically contrasting, but could be contrasted with words implying law-breaking or opposition to lawmaking.
