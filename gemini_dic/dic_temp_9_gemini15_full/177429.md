rebaptise:/ˌriːˈbæptaɪz/| vt.|重新施洗；再洗礼|To baptize again; to baptize a second time, especially after a previous baptism has been deemed invalid.|例句：He decided to rebaptise himself in the river. 他决定在河里重新给自己施洗。例句：The sect rebaptised converts from other denominations. 该教派对来自其他教派的皈依者重新施洗。|近义词：rechristen, reconsecrate|反义词：baptize (in the sense of a first baptism)
