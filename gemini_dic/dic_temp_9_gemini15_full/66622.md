exiling:/ɪɡˈzaɪlɪŋ/ | v. |流放；放逐；驱逐 |The act of forcing someone to leave their country or home, often as a punishment.  It implies a permanent or indefinite removal from one's homeland. |例句：The dictator was known for exiling his political opponents. 独裁者以流放其政治对手而闻名。

例句：His exiling from his homeland left him feeling lost and alone. 他被流放出祖国，让他感到迷茫和孤独。


近义词：banishing, deporting, ousting, removing


反义词：welcoming, accepting, repatriating


词性:动词


USAGE->

They are exiling political dissidents. = 他们正在流放持不同政见的政治人物。
He exiles his enemies to a faraway island. = 他把敌人流放到遥远的岛屿上。


一般过去时:
The king exiled the traitor. = 国王流放了叛徒。

现在完成时:
The government has exiled thousands of people. = 政府已经流放了成千上万的人。

过去完成时:

The family had been exiled for generations. = 这个家族几代人都被流放了。


将来时:
