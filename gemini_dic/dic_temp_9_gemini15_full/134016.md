nonhouseholders: /ˌnɒnˈhaʊsˌhoʊldərz/ | n. | 非户主，非住户 | People who do not own or rent a house and are not the head of a household.  They may be living with family, friends, or in other arrangements. | Example: The survey excluded nonhouseholders, focusing solely on homeowners.  这项调查排除了非户主，只关注房屋所有者。 The study found a higher rate of poverty among nonhouseholders.  这项研究发现非户主的贫困率更高。 | 近义词: non-homeowners, non-residents (in some contexts) | 反义词: householders, homeowners
