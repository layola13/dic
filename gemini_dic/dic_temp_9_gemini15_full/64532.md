errability:/ˌɛrəˈbɪləti/| n. |易错性；出错的倾向|The inherent capacity to make mistakes or errors.  It refers to the fallibility or imperfection of judgment, perception, or action. |例句：The errability of human judgment is a constant source of problems. 人类判断的易错性是问题的根源。


例句：The high errability rate of the new software led to its recall. 新软件的高出错率导致其被召回。


近义词：fallibility, imperfection, liability to error, proneness to mistakes


反义词：infallibility, impeccability, certainty


词性:名词
