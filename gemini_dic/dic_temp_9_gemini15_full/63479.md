entrusting: /ɪnˈtrʌstɪŋ/ | v. | 托付；委托；相信 | The act of giving someone responsibility for something or someone.  It implies a degree of trust and confidence in the recipient's ability and integrity. |例句：She entrusted her children to the care of her sister. 她把孩子们托付给姐姐照顾。

She entrusted him with a secret. 她向他吐露了一个秘密。


近义词：committing, confiding, delegating | 反义词：withholding, keeping, concealing | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I entrust my car to the mechanic every year for servicing. 我每年都把我的车托付给技师保养。
He entrusts his financial affairs to a professional advisor. 他把他的财务事务托付给一位专业的顾问。

一般过去时:
They entrusted the project to a young, ambitious team. 他们把项目委托给一个年轻有抱负的团队。
I entrusted my most valuable possessions to a safety deposit box. 我把最贵重的物品存放在保险箱里。

现在完成时:
She has entrusted her business to her capable son. 她已将她的生意托付给了她能干的儿子。
We have entrusted the task to a reputable company. 我们已将这项任务委托给了一家信誉良好的公司。

过去完成时:
He had entrusted the documents to his lawyer before leaving the country. 他在离开国家之前已将文件托付给了他的律师。
The company had entrusted the design to a renowned architect. 这家公司已将设计委托给了一位著名的建筑师。

将来时:
I will entrust the painting to a reputable art gallery for auction. 我将把这幅画委托给一家信誉良好的艺术馆拍卖。
They will entrust the management of the property to a property management company. 他们将把房产的管理委托给一家物业管理公司。

虚拟语气:
If I had a trustworthy friend, I would entrust him with my secrets. 如果我有值得信赖的朋友，我会把我的秘密告诉他。
I wish I could entrust my future to someone I completely trust. 我希望我能把我的未来托付给一个我完全信任的人。

被动语态:
The task was entrusted to the most experienced member of the team. 任务被委托给了团队中最有经验的成员。
The children were entrusted to the care of their grandparents. 孩子们被托付给了他们的祖父母照顾。

疑问句:
To whom did you entrust the responsibility? 你把责任托付给了谁？
Whom would you entrust with such a sensitive matter? 你会把这么敏感的事情托付给谁？

否定句:
I wouldn't entrust my money to that bank. 我不会把我的钱托付给那家银行。
They did not entrust the mission to an inexperienced soldier. 他们没有把任务委托给一个没有经验的士兵。


PHRASE->
entrust A to B: 把A托付给B

entrust a task to someone: 把任务委托给某人
entrust one's fate to someone/something: 把自己的命运托付给某人/某物
entrust with a secret: 委以重任；透露秘密
