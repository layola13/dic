overintellectualizes:/ˌoʊvərɪntɛlɛktʃuəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 过度思考；滥用智力 | To interpret or analyze something in an excessively intellectual or complicated way, often to the point of obscuring the simple truth or practicality.  | He overintellectualizes simple problems, making them seem far more complex than they are. 他把简单的问题想得太复杂了，使它们看起来比实际复杂得多。 | overthink, overanalyze | simplify,  underestimate | 动词
