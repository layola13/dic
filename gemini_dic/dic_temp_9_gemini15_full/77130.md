gasbags:/ˈɡæsˌbæɡz/| n. |夸夸其谈的人；吹牛的人；空话连篇的人|A gasbag is a person who talks a lot, especially about unimportant things, or who boasts a lot.  They are often seen as full of hot air and lacking substance.|例句：He's such a gasbag; he never stops talking about himself. 他真是个夸夸其谈的人，没完没了地谈论自己。|例句： Don't listen to that gasbag, he's just trying to impress you. 别听那个吹牛鬼说话，他只是想给你留下好印象。|近义词：windbag, blowhard, boaster, chatterbox|反义词：quiet person, listener, modest person
