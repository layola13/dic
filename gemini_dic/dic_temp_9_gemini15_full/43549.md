crayonist:/ˈkreɪənɪst/| n. |蜡笔画家，使用蜡笔作画的人 |A crayonist is an artist who uses crayons as their primary medium for creating artwork.  They might specialize in particular styles or subjects, but the common thread is the use of crayons.  | Example: The young crayonist's vibrant artwork filled the gallery with bold colors and playful imagery.  这位年轻的蜡笔画家充满活力的作品以大胆的色彩和俏皮的意象装点了画廊。|近义词：artist, illustrator (if the crayonist creates illustrations) |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with artists using other media like painters or sculptors.
