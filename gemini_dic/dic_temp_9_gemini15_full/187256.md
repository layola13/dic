roborean:/roʊboʊriən/| n. |拟人机器人，类人机器人|A roborean is a robot designed to resemble and/or mimic human behavior and appearance.  They often incorporate advanced AI and sophisticated mechanics.|例句：The researchers unveiled their latest creation – a highly advanced roborean capable of complex emotional expressions.  研究人员展示了他们的最新作品——一个能够表达复杂情感的先进类人机器人。|近义词：android, humanoid robot |反义词：machine (non-humanoid), automaton (lacking human-like qualities)
