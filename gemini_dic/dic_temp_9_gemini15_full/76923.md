gaoled:/ɡeɪld/| v.|监禁，入狱|To imprison or confine in jail.|The thief was gaoled for five years.  小偷被监禁五年。|He was gaoled for his crimes. 他因犯罪而入狱。|imprisoned, incarcerated, jailed|freed, released
