contiguously:/kənˈtɪɡjuəsli/ | adv. |连续地；毗邻地；紧密地 |In a contiguous manner; without interruption.|例句：The houses were built contiguously, sharing walls and creating a continuous streetscape.  这些房子连在一起建造，共用墙壁，形成了一条连续的街道景观。


例句：The chapters in the book flowed contiguously, each building upon the previous one.  这本书的章节流畅地衔接在一起，每一章都建立在前一章的基础上。


近义词：continuously, consecutively, uninterruptedly | 反义词：discontinuously, separately, intermittently | 词性:副词
