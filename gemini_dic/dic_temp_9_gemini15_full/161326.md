postpositional:/ˌpəʊstpəˈzɪʃənəl/| adj.|后置的，后位的|Placed after the word it modifies or governs.  A postpositional adjective, adverb, or phrase follows the word it modifies. This is common in some languages, especially agglutinative languages, where multiple suffixes are added to the end of a word.|例句：In some languages, the adjective is postpositional to the noun.  在某些语言中，形容词位于名词之后。|近义词：suffixal (in some contexts)|反义词：prepositional
