patchwork:/ˈpætʃwɜːrk/| n. |拼缝物；大杂烩；拼凑之作 |A patchwork is something made from different pieces of material sewn together, or a collection of different things.  It often implies a lack of uniformity or a haphazard combination. |例句：Her quilt was a beautiful patchwork of different fabrics.  她的被子是由不同面料缝制而成的美丽的拼缝物。

例句：The government's economic policy seems like a patchwork of unrelated ideas.  政府的经济政策似乎是一堆不相关的想法的大杂烩。|近义词：hodgepodge, medley, collage, assortment |反义词：uniformity, consistency, coherence |名词
