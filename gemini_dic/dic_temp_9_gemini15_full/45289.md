cultures:/ˈkʌltʃərz/| n. |文化（复数）|The shared beliefs, values, customs, behaviors, and artifacts that characterize a group or society.  Cultures can be national, regional, ethnic, or even organizational. They encompass everything from language and religion to food and art.|The cultures of Southeast Asia are incredibly diverse.  东南亚的文化极其多样化。|The cultures of different countries often clash.  不同国家的文化常常发生冲突。|civilizations, traditions, societies|barbarism, savagery (in some contexts)
