disney:/ˈdɪzni/| n. |迪斯尼；迪士尼公司；迪斯尼乐园|The word "Disney" refers to The Walt Disney Company, a multinational mass media and entertainment conglomerate,  as well as its various associated brands, theme parks, and characters.  It is often used as a shorthand for the company's magical and family-friendly image.|例句：I'm going to Disneyland next week. 我下周要去迪斯尼乐园。

例句： Disney movies are known for their animation and storytelling. 迪斯尼电影以其动画和故事讲述而闻名。
