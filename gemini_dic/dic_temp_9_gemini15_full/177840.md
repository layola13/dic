recentrifuging: /ˌriːsɛntˈrɪfjuːdʒɪŋ/ | v. | 再离心；再次离心 | The process of centrifuging something again.  This term is used primarily in scientific contexts, particularly in laboratory settings where centrifugation is a common technique for separating substances. | Example: After the initial centrifugation, the supernatant was recentrifuged at a higher speed to ensure complete separation of the components. = 初次离心后，上清液以更高的速度再次离心，以确保组分的完全分离。 The sample needed recentrifuging to remove any remaining impurities. = 样本需要再次离心以去除任何残留的杂质。 | 近义词: re-centrifugation (n.) | 反义词: N/A
