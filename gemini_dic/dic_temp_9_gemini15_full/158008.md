planometer:/plæˈnɒmɪtər/|/ˈplænəˌmiːtər/| n. |平面仪，求积仪 |A planimeter is a measuring instrument used to determine the area of a plane surface, such as an irregular shape on a map or drawing.  It works by tracing the perimeter of the shape, and the instrument mechanically calculates the area. |The architect used a planimeter to measure the area of the irregularly shaped lot. 建筑师用平面仪测量形状不规则的地块面积。
The surveyor employed a planimeter to calculate the acreage of the field. 测量员用平面仪计算田地的面积。|area meter, integrator|—|
