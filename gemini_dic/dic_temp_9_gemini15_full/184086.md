resignation:/ˌrɛzɪɡˈneɪʃən/| n. |辞职；辞职书；顺从；放弃 |The act of resigning or relinquishing a position, office, or claim.  It can also refer to the acceptance of something unpleasant without protest. |例句：He handed in his resignation after a disagreement with his boss. 他在和老板发生争执后递交了辞职信。

例句：She showed great resignation in the face of her difficulties. 面对困难，她表现出极大的顺从。

近义词：quitting, relinquishment, surrender, acceptance

反义词：retention, persistence, defiance
