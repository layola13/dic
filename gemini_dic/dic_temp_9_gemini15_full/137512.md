/ˈnɑːnˌtreɪdər/| n. |非交易者，非贸易商|A nontrader is someone who does not participate in trading activities, such as buying and selling securities or commodities.  They may be investors who hold assets for long-term growth rather than actively trading them. |Example:  Many retirees are nontraders, preferring the stability of long-term investments.  许多退休人员是非交易者，他们更喜欢长期投资的稳定性。|近义词：investor (if holding assets), non-participant|反义词：trader, speculator
