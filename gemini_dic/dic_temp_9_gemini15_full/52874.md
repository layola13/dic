diminishment: /dɪˈmɪnɪʃmənt/ | n. | 减少；缩小；贬低 | The gradual diminution of his power was evident.  他的权力逐渐减少，这是显而易见的。| reduction, lessening, decrease, decline | increase, enhancement, growth | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The value of the property shows a slow diminution. = 房地产的价值显示出缓慢的减少。
The constant noise causes a diminution in my concentration. = 持续的噪音导致我的注意力下降。


一般过去时:
The diminution of the forest worried the local residents. = 森林的减少让当地居民担忧。
The company experienced a sharp diminution in profits last quarter. = 公司上个季度的利润大幅下降。


现在完成时:
The recent drought has caused a significant diminution in water levels. = 最近的干旱导致水位大幅下降。
The team’s performance has shown a gradual diminution in recent matches. = 球队在最近的比赛中表现逐渐下降。


过去完成时:
By the end of the war, the army had suffered a considerable diminution in numbers. = 到战争结束时，军队的人数已大大减少。
Before the new policy, the company's market share had experienced a steady diminution. = 在新政策出台之前，公司的市场份额一直在稳步下降。


将来时:
Continued pollution will lead to a further diminution of the ozone layer. = 持续的污染将导致臭氧层进一步减少。
Experts predict a rapid diminution in the polar ice caps in the coming decades. = 专家预测未来几十年极地冰盖将迅速减少。


虚拟语气:
If there were no diminution in the workforce, we would be able to complete the project on time. = 如果员工没有减少，我们就能按时完成项目。
I wish there were no diminution in the quality of the product. = 我希望产品质量不会下降。


被动语态:
The building's value was diminished by the recent fire. = 建筑物的价值因最近的火灾而贬值。
His reputation was diminished by the scandal. = 他的名誉因丑闻而受损。


疑问句:
Is there any diminution in the number of students enrolled? = 学生入学人数减少了吗？
Has the recent economic downturn caused a diminution in consumer spending? = 最近的经济衰退导致消费者支出减少了吗？


否定句:
There was no diminution in his enthusiasm for the project. = 他对项目的热情没有减弱。
The quality of the work showed no diminution over time. = 工作的质量随着时间的推移没有下降。
