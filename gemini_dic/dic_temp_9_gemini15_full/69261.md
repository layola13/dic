featherbrained: /ˈfɛðərbreɪnd/ | adj. | 轻浮的；愚蠢的；缺乏头脑的 | Foolish or irresponsible; lacking in intelligence or good judgment.  |  He's so featherbrained, he forgot his own birthday. 他如此轻浮，竟然忘了自己的生日。 She made a featherbrained decision to quit her job without another lined up. 她做了一个轻率的决定，没有找到下一份工作就辞职了。 |近义词：giddy, flighty, scatterbrained, airheaded, silly |反义词：sensible, intelligent, wise, shrewd
