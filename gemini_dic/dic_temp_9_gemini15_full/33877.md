circumspectively:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈspektɪvli/ | adv. | 谨慎地；小心地；考虑周到地 | acting in a cautious manner, considering all circumstances before acting or speaking.  | The lawyer circumspectively examined the evidence before making any statements. 律师在发表任何声明之前都谨慎地审查了证据。 | cautiously, prudently, carefully | recklessly, carelessly, rashly | 副词
