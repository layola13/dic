durst:/dɜːrst/| v.|胆敢；敢于|Archaic form of "dare," expressing a lack of courage or a hesitant willingness to do something. It is rarely used in modern English.|例句：I durst not speak my mind.  我不敢说出我的想法。|例句：He durst not challenge the king. 他不敢挑战国王。|近义词：dare, have the nerve to,  |反义词：be certain, be sure
