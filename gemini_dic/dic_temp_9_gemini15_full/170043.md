proteins:/ˈproʊtiːnz/| n. |蛋白质|Proteins are large, complex molecules that are essential for the structure, function, and regulation of the body's tissues and organs. They are made up of amino acids, which are linked together in a specific sequence.  Proteins perform a vast array of functions, including acting as enzymes, hormones, and structural components.|例句：Proteins are essential nutrients for building and repairing tissues. 蛋白质是构建和修复组织的必需营养素。

例句：A balanced diet should contain sufficient proteins. 平衡的饮食应该包含足够的蛋白质。


近义词： polypeptides (but less commonly used in general contexts)


反义词：  None (no direct antonym, but concepts like "carbohydrates" or "lipids" represent contrasting classes of macromolecules)


词性: 名词
