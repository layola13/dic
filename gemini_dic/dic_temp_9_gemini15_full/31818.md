checkup:/ˈtʃɛkʌp/ | n. |体检；检查 | A checkup is a medical examination to check a person's general health.  It often involves a physical examination, blood tests, and other diagnostic procedures. |例句：I have a yearly checkup with my doctor. 我每年都会去看医生做体检。

例句：The mechanic gave the car a thorough checkup before I bought it.  在买车之前，机械师对汽车进行了彻底检查。


近义词：examination, inspection, review | 反义词：neglect


词源:  The word "checkup" is a compound word formed from "check" and "up".  "Check" meaning to examine or verify, and "up" indicating a thorough or complete action.  Its origins trace back to the early 20th century, reflecting the increasing importance of preventative healthcare.
