disciplinarian:/ˌdɪsɪplɪˈneəriən/| n.|纪律严明的人，严格执行纪律者|A disciplinarian is a person who believes in or practices strict discipline.  They enforce rules and regulations firmly and consistently. |例句：The headmaster was a strict disciplinarian, and all the students feared him. 校长是一位纪律严明的人，所有学生都怕他。

例句：She's a disciplinarian at home, making sure her children follow a strict schedule.  她在家里是个纪律严明的人，确保她的孩子们遵守严格的时间表。

近义词：taskmaster, enforcer, authoritarian

反义词：permissive, lenient
