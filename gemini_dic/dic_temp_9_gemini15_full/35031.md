close-cropped:/ˈkloʊs krɒpt/ | adj. | 修剪得很短的；剪得很短的 |  Describes hair that has been cut very short. It can also refer to other things that are cut very close, like vegetation or a photograph. | 例句：He had close-cropped hair. 他留着很短的头发。  | 例句：The lawn was close-cropped. 草坪修剪得很短。 | 近义词：short, clipped, trimmed | 反义词：long, unkempt
