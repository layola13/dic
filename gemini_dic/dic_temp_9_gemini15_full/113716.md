macrobiotics:/ˌmækrəʊbaɪˈɒtɪks/ | n. |宏观生物学，宏观营养学 |The study of the relationship between food and health, emphasizing whole, unprocessed foods and a balanced approach to nutrition.  It is based on the philosophy that food should promote harmony and balance within the body and with nature. | Macrobiotics emphasizes the importance of eating whole, unprocessed foods like grains, vegetables, and beans. |近义词：holistic nutrition, natural diet |反义词：junk food diet, processed food diet
