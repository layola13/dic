domination:/ˌdɒmɪˈneɪʃən/|/n.|统治；支配；控制；支配地位|The act or fact of dominating or controlling; the state of being dominant. It can refer to political, social, or economic control, often implying power and authority over others.|例句：The company's domination of the market is undeniable. 这家公司对市场的支配地位是不可否认的。|例句：His domination over his employees was oppressive. 他对员工的控制是压迫性的。|近义词：rule, control, authority, supremacy, mastery|反义词：subjugation, submission, subservience, inferiority
