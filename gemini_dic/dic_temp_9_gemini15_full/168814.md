production:/prəˈdʌkʃən/ | n. | 生产；产量；产品；作品；戏剧演出 | The act of producing something.  It can refer to the process of making goods, creating art, or putting on a theatrical performance. |例句：The factory's production increased by 10% last year.  去年这家工厂的产量增加了10%。

The movie's production was plagued by delays. 这部电影的制作因延误而受阻。|近义词：manufacture, output, creation, performance |反义词：consumption, destruction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company produces high-quality goods. = 这家公司生产高质量的商品。
Our farm produces a lot of apples every year. = 我们农场每年生产大量的苹果。

一般过去时:
They produced a wonderful play last night. = 他们昨晚演出了一部精彩的戏剧。
The factory produced thousands of cars last month. = 这个工厂上个月生产了数千辆汽车。

现在完成时:
We have produced three reports this week. = 我们本周已经制作了三份报告。
She has produced several successful films. = 她已经制作了几部成功的电影。

过去完成时:
They had produced a prototype before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经制作了一个原型。
The company had produced similar products in the past. = 这家公司过去生产过类似的产品。

将来时:
We will produce more electric cars in the future. = 我们将来会生产更多电动汽车。
They will produce a new album next year. = 他们明年将发行一张新专辑。

虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we could produce more. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们可以生产更多。
I wish I had produced a better presentation. = 我希望我做了一个更好的演示。

被动语态:
The report was produced by a team of experts. = 这份报告是由一个专家团队制作的。
Thousands of cars are produced daily at the factory. = 工厂每天生产数千辆汽车。

疑问句:
How many cars does the factory produce each day? = 工厂每天生产多少辆汽车？
What kind of products does your company produce? = 贵公司生产什么类型的产品？

否定句:
The factory didn't produce enough goods to meet demand. = 工厂生产的商品不足以满足需求。
They haven't produced any new designs this year. = 他们今年还没有设计出任何新设计。
