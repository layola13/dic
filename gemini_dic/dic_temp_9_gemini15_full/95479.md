incivility's:/ɪnˈsɪvɪlətiz/| n. |不礼貌的行为；粗鲁的行为|The possessive form of "incivility," referring to the actions or characteristics of incivility. It signifies the state or quality of being uncivil, rude, or discourteous.|例句：The incivility's impact on workplace morale was significant.  不礼貌的行为对工作场所士气的影响是巨大的。|近义词：rudeness's, discourtesy's, impoliteness's |反义词：courtesy's, politeness's, civility's


USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and doesn't have verb conjugations.  It describes the actions or effects of incivility.  Therefore, standard verb tenses are not applicable.

PHRASE->
The phrase "incivility's impact," "incivility's effects," "incivility's consequences", "incivility's prevalence", "incivility's root causes" are commonly used.
