overemphasising: /ˌoʊvərˈɛmfəsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过分强调 |  The act of placing undue or excessive stress on something; giving something more importance than it deserves.  It implies an exaggeration or disproportionate focus. | 例句：He was overemphasising the minor flaws in the project, overshadowing its overall success. 他过分强调了项目中的一些小瑕疵，掩盖了项目的整体成功。

例句：The marketing campaign overemphasized the product's features, neglecting the actual customer needs.  市场营销活动过分强调了产品的特性，而忽略了实际的客户需求。


近义词：overstating, exaggerating, stressing excessively, highlighting unduly


反义词：understating, downplaying, minimizing


词源:

* **over-**: prefix indicating "excessive" or "too much"
* **emphasize**: from  Late Middle English: from Old French *emphasiser, from late Latin *emphasizāre, from Greek *emphasizein ‘to emphasize’, from *emphasis (see emphasis).
