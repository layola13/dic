monographed:/ˈmɒnəɡræft/| adj. | 专著的；论文集的 | Described or treated in a monograph.  A monograph is a scholarly work of writing on a single subject or an aspect of a subject. | Example:  The newly discovered species was monographed in a recent journal article.  最近一篇期刊文章对新发现的物种进行了专著论述。|近义词：detailed, exhaustive, comprehensive|反义词：summary, brief, cursory|形容词
