scantlet:/ˈskæntlɪt/| n. |少量；一点点；少许|A scantlet is a small amount or a little bit of something.  It implies a very small quantity, often insufficient.  The word is archaic and rarely used in modern English.|例句：He only had a scantlet of hope left. 他只剩下少许希望了。

例句：There was a scantlet of food remaining after the party. 派对后剩下少许食物。


近义词：bit, smidgen, trifle, morsel,  pinch


反义词：abundance, plenty, profusion


词性:名词
