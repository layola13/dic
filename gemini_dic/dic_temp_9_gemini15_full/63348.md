entoil:/ɪnˈtɔɪl/| vt.| 使费力；使困苦；纠缠；|to involve someone in something complicated or unpleasant that is difficult to get out of.|The project will enoil us in endless paperwork.|使陷入；牵涉；|近义词：involve, engage, entangle|反义词：disentangle, liberate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heavy workload entails long hours. = 繁重的工作量意味着要长时间工作。
This job entails a lot of travel. = 这份工作需要大量的出差。

一般过去时:
The accident entailed significant costs. = 这起事故带来了巨大的损失。
The decision entailed many unforeseen consequences. = 这一决定带来了许多意料之外的后果。


现在完成时:
The investigation has entailed countless hours of work. = 调查工作已经耗费了无数个小时。
The project has entailed more expense than anticipated. = 这个项目比预期的花费更多。


过去完成时:
The previous administration had entailed massive debts. = 上届政府留下了巨额债务。
They had entailed themselves in a risky venture. = 他们把自己卷入了一项风险很大的冒险事业。


将来时:
The new regulations will entail further changes. = 新规将带来进一步的改变。
The expansion will entail hiring more staff. = 扩张将需要雇佣更多员工。


虚拟语气:
If the plan entailed too much risk, we would abandon it. = 如果这个计划风险太大，我们将放弃。
I wish the project hadn't entailed so much stress. = 我希望这个项目没有带来这么大的压力。


被动语态:
They were entailed in a long legal battle. = 他们卷入了一场漫长的法律诉讼。
The company was entailed in a scandal. = 公司卷入了一起丑闻。


疑问句:
Does this task entail any special skills? = 这个任务需要什么特别的技能？
What does this position entail? = 这个职位包括哪些职责？


否定句:
The job doesn't entail any overnight travel. = 这份工作不需要夜间出差。
The contract doesn't entail any hidden fees. = 合同中没有任何隐藏费用。


PHRASE->
be entailed in = 卷入…
entailed by = 由…引起的
entailed upon = 强加于…
entailed with = 与…纠缠
entailed in a dispute = 卷入纠纷
