highness:/ˈhaɪnəs/| n. |殿下，尊贵 |The word "highness" is a title of respect used to address or refer to a royal person, typically a prince or princess. It emphasizes their elevated social standing and authority.  |Her Royal Highness, Princess Anne, visited the school.  她的殿下，安妮公主访问了学校。|royalty, majesty, excellence|lowliness, humbleness|名词
