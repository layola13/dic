presidiums:/prɪˈzɪdiəm/| n.|主席团；领导班子；执政机构|A presidium is a small group of people who have the highest authority within an organization, especially a political party or government.  It often acts as an executive committee or governing body.|例句：The party's presidium met to discuss the upcoming election.  该党的中央政治局开会讨论即将到来的选举。|例句：The presidium of the student council decided on the new rules. 学生会主席团决定了新的规章制度。|近义词：executive committee, governing body, leadership, board|反义词：membership (as a whole), rank and file
