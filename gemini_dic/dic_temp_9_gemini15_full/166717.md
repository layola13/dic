preshowed:/prɪˈʃoʊd/| v. |预先展示，预演|To display or perform something before a main event or public showing.  It implies a rehearsal or preview intended for a select audience or for testing purposes, rather than a full public performance.|例句：The band preshowed their new songs at a small club before the official album release. 乐队在正式专辑发行前在一个小俱乐部预演了他们的新歌。

例句：The director preshowed the film to a test audience to gather feedback.  导演向测试观众预映了这部电影以收集反馈。


近义词：preview, rehearse, pre-screen


反义词：postshow


词性:动词
