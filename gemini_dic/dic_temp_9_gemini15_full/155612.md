philosophization:/ˌfɪləˈsɒfaɪzeɪʃən/| n. |哲学化；哲学思考；哲学论证 |The act of engaging in philosophical thought or inquiry; the process of making something philosophical.  It involves applying philosophical methods and concepts to a particular subject matter or problem. |例句：The text demonstrates a clear philosophization of political theory. 这篇文章清晰地展现了对政治理论的哲学思考。  The philosophization of everyday life can be a rewarding pursuit. 将日常生活的哲学化可以是一项有益的追求。|近义词：reflection, contemplation, theorization |反义词：practicality, empiricism
