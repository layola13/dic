nares:/ˈneərz/| n. |鼻孔，鼻腔|The external openings of the nostrils.  They are also sometimes referred to as the anterior nares.  The internal nares are the openings into the nasal cavity from the pharynx.|例句：The horse's nares flared as it sensed danger. 这匹马的鼻孔张开，因为它感到了危险。

The doctor examined the patient's nares for any signs of infection.医生检查了病人的鼻孔是否有感染迹象。|近义词：nostrils|反义词：无|词性 n.
