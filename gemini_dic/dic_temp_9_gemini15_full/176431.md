re-inspect:/ˌriːɪnˈspɛkt/| vt.|复查，再次检查|To inspect again or anew; to examine a second time.  |例句：The building will be re-inspected next week.  下周将对该建筑物进行复查。|例句：After the accident, the engineers re-inspected the bridge's structure.事故发生后，工程师们对桥梁结构进行了复查。|近义词：reexamine, review, check again|反义词：ignore, overlook|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-inspect the equipment regularly. = 他们定期检查设备。
We re-inspect the products before shipment. = 我们在发货前复查产品。

一般过去时:
The inspector re-inspected the damaged goods. = 检查员复查了损坏的货物。
They re-inspected the building after the earthquake. = 地震后他们对建筑物进行了复查。

现在完成时:
They have re-inspected the bridge three times this year. = 他们今年已经三次复查了这座桥。
The authorities have re-inspected the factory following complaints. = 在收到投诉后，当局已经对工厂进行了复查。

过去完成时:
The manager had re-inspected the documents before making a decision. = 经理在做决定之前已经复查了文件。
They had re-inspected the site several times before construction began. = 在施工开始之前，他们已经多次复查了现场。

将来时:
The team will re-inspect the pipeline next month. = 团队下个月将对管道进行复查。
We will re-inspect the house after the renovations are complete. = 装修完成后我们将对房子进行复查。

虚拟语气:
If they re-inspected the data, they might find the error. = 如果他们复查数据，可能会发现错误。
It is essential that the work be re-inspected before approval. = 在批准之前，必须对工作进行复查。

被动语态:
The documents were re-inspected by the supervisor. = 文件由主管进行了复查。
The system will be re-inspected following the software update. = 软件更新后，系统将被复查。

疑问句:
Will the product be re-inspected before delivery? = 产品在交付前会复查吗？
Have they re-inspected the faulty equipment? = 他们是否已经复查了有故障的设备？

否定句:
They did not re-inspect the warehouse after the fire. = 火灾后他们没有复查仓库。
The company will not re-inspect the goods unless requested. = 除非有要求，否则公司不会复查货物。
