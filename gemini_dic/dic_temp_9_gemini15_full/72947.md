fontaine:/fɒ̃ˈtɛn/| n. |喷泉；泉水；源泉 |A fontaine is a structure that spouts water, often decorative and found in parks or public spaces. It can also refer to a spring or source of something.  |例句：The Trevi Fontaine in Rome is a famous landmark. 罗马的许愿池是一个著名的地标。|例句：The discovery of penicillin was a fontaine of new medical treatments. 青霉素的发现是新的医疗方法的源泉。|近义词：fountain, spring, well, source |反义词：dryness, drought
