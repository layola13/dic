cooper:/ˈkuːpər/ | n. |桶匠，制桶工，合作社成员 |A cooper is a person who makes or repairs wooden barrels, casks, or other containers.  The word also refers to a member of a cooperative. |例句：My grandfather was a cooper, skilled in crafting sturdy oak barrels.  我的祖父是一位桶匠，擅长制作结实的橡木桶。


例句：The local farmers formed a cooper to sell their produce collectively. 当地农民组建了一个合作社来集体销售他们的农产品。

近义词：barrel maker, cask maker,  (for cooperative member) member, shareholder

反义词：None directly applicable.  (For cooperative member, antonyms would relate to the opposite of cooperation)
