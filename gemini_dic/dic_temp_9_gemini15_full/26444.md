byroads:/ˈbaɪroʊdz/ | n. |小路；偏僻道路 |  Secondary or less important roads, often unpaved or less frequently traveled than main roads. They often lead to smaller settlements or rural areas. | 例句：We took the byroads to avoid the traffic on the highway. = 我们走小路以避开高速公路上的交通拥堵。 | 例句：The byroads were overgrown and barely passable. = 小路杂草丛生，几乎无法通行。| 近义词：back roads, country lanes, side roads | 反义词：highways, main roads, freeways | 名词
