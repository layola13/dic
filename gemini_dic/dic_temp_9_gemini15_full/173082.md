quartets:/kwɔːˈtɛts/| n. |四重奏；四重唱；四人一组|A quartet is a group of four people or things, often musical performers.  It can refer to a musical composition written for four instruments or voices.|Example: The string quartet played a beautiful piece by Beethoven.  这支弦乐四重奏演奏了一首贝多芬的优美乐曲。 The family formed a quartet to play cards. 这个家庭组成了一个四人小组打牌。|近义词：quadruplet, group of four|反义词：solo, duet, trio
