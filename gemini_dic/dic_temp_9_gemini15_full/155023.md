petiolar:/pɪˈtiːələr/| adj.|叶柄的；似叶柄的|Relating to or resembling a petiole (the stalk that joins a leaf to a stem).  The term often describes structures that are similar in function or appearance to a petiole, even if not directly derived from one.|例句：The petiolar venation of the leaf was clearly visible. 叶子的叶柄脉络清晰可见。 The petiolar appendage supported the flower. 叶柄状附属物支撑着花朵。|近义词：stalked, pedunculate|反义词：sessile
