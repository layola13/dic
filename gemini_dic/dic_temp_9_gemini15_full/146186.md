overexercises:/ˌoʊvərˈɛksərsaɪzɪz/ | n. |过度锻炼；过量运动|Excessive physical exertion; exceeding the recommended limits of physical activity.|例句：Overexercises can lead to injuries and burnout. 过度运动会导致受伤和精疲力竭。|近义词：overtraining, overexertion |反义词：undertraining, rest
