imploding:/ɪmˈploʊdɪŋ/ | v. |内爆；崩溃；瓦解 |  The process of collapsing inward, often violently.  This can refer to a structure collapsing in on itself, a system failing catastrophically, or a situation rapidly deteriorating.  | 例句：The old building imploded in a cloud of dust.  那座旧建筑物在一片尘土中坍塌了。


The company is imploding due to internal conflicts. 这家公司由于内部冲突而走向崩溃。| 近义词：collapse, cave in, crumble, fall apart | 反义词：explode, expand, flourish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The building is imploding. = 建筑物正在内爆。
The economy is imploding. = 经济正在崩溃。


一般过去时:
The star imploded. = 星星内爆了。
The project imploded last month. = 项目上个月失败了。


现在完成时:
The company has imploded. = 公司已经崩溃了。
The situation has imploded since the scandal broke. = 自丑闻爆发以来，情况已经恶化了。


过去完成时:
The bridge had imploded before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，桥梁已经坍塌了。
The relationship had imploded before they even got married. = 他们的关系甚至在结婚前就已经破裂了。


将来时:
The reactor will implode if the pressure isn't released. = 如果不释放压力，反应堆将内爆。
The government might implode under the pressure of the crisis. = 政府可能在危机的压力下崩溃。


虚拟语气:
If the pressure increased, the vessel would implode. = 如果压力增加，容器就会内爆。
If he continued to act like that, their marriage would implode. = 如果他继续那样做，他们的婚姻就会破裂。


被动语态:
The building was imploded safely. = 建筑物被安全地爆破拆除。(被动语态中"implode" 更多指人为的爆破拆除)
The system was imploded by a software error. = 系统因软件错误而崩溃。(被动语态中"implode" 更强调系统自身的崩溃)


疑问句:
Is the company imploding? = 公司正在崩溃吗？
Will the project implode? = 项目会失败吗？


否定句:
The building did not implode. = 建筑物没有内爆。
The negotiations did not implode, despite the tension. = 尽管紧张，谈判并没有破裂。
