precapitalist: /ˌpriːˈkæpɪtəlɪst/ | adj. | 前资本主义的 |  Describing a system or period of time before the emergence of capitalism as the dominant economic system.  It often refers to societies characterized by feudalism, mercantilism, or other pre-industrial economic structures.  | Example: The precapitalist agrarian economy relied heavily on peasant labor. 前资本主义的农业经济严重依赖农民劳动。  The study focused on precapitalist social structures in medieval Europe. 这项研究的重点是中世纪欧洲的前资本主义社会结构。| 近义词: pre-industrial, feudal, antebellum (in the context of the US South) | 反义词: capitalist, post-capitalist
