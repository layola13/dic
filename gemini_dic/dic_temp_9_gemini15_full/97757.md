initiation:/ɪˌnɪʃiˈeɪʃən/| n. |开始，启动；入门仪式；接纳；|The act of beginning something.  It can refer to the starting of a process, project, or organization, or to a formal ceremony marking someone's admission into a group or organization. |例句：The initiation of the project was delayed due to unforeseen circumstances.  项目的启动因不可预见的情况而被推迟。

The initiation ceremony for the new members was held last night. 新成员的入门仪式昨晚举行。|近义词： commencement, beginning, introduction, induction |反义词：conclusion, termination, end


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company initiates new projects every year. = 公司每年启动新的项目。
He initiates conversations easily. = 他很容易开始谈话。


一般过去时:
They initiated the plan last month. = 他们上个月启动了这个计划。
She initiated a lawsuit against the company. = 她对公司提起诉讼。


现在完成时:
They have initiated several new programs this year. = 他们今年已经启动了几项新计划。

过去完成时:

将来时:
