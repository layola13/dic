industrializes:/ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzɪz/| v. |使工业化；使产业化|To develop industry on a large scale in (a region or country).  The process of transforming an economy from primarily agricultural to one based on manufacturing and industry. |例句：The country is rapidly industrializing, creating many new jobs. 这个国家正在迅速工业化，创造了许多新的就业机会。|例句：The government implemented policies to industrialize the rural areas. 政府实施了政策来使农村地区工业化。|近义词：develop, modernize|反义词：deindustrialize, agrarianize
