heirs:/ɛərz/| n.|继承人，后裔|People who inherit something, especially property or a title, from someone who has died.|The heirs to the throne are next in line for the crown. 王位继承人将是下一个继承王位的人。  The family heirs fought over the inheritance. 家族继承人为了遗产而争吵。|successors, inheritors|none|名词
