graduateship: /ˈɡrædʒuətʃɪp/ | n. |研究生身份；毕业生身份；毕业奖学金 | A position or status of a graduate;  a scholarship or grant awarded to a graduate student.  It can also refer to the period of time one holds such a status. |


例句：

He received a graduateship to study astrophysics at Cambridge. 他获得了一笔奖学金，可以在剑桥大学攻读天体物理学。

Her graduateship allowed her to focus solely on her research.  她的研究生身份让她能够专注于研究。


近义词：postgraduate studentship, fellowship, scholarship


反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑与本科生身份(undergraduateship)对比


词性：名词
