pleuritical:/pluːrɪtɪkəl/| adj.|胸膜的；胸膜炎的|Relating to the pleura, the membrane surrounding the lungs.  The term often refers to conditions affecting the pleura, such as pleurisy (inflammation of the pleura).|例句：He experienced pleuritical pain in his chest. 他胸部感到胸膜痛。

例句：The doctor suspected a pleuritical infection based on the patient's symptoms.  医生根据病人的症状怀疑是胸膜感染。


近义词：pleural (more formal)|反义词：None specifically, but words describing the opposite of inflammation or disease of the pleura could be considered antonyms in context.


词源：From "pleura" + "-ical", meaning "relating to".  "Pleura" itself originates from the Greek word πλευρά (pleura), meaning "side" or "rib," referring to its location around the ribs.
