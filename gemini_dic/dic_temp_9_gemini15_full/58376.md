dualities:/duˈælɪtiz/ | n. |二元性；对立面；两重性 | The inherent contradictions or opposing forces within a system or concept.  Duality refers to the state of having two parts or aspects. It often implies a tension or conflict between these two parts.  For example, the duality of human nature encompasses both good and evil. |例句：The play explores the dualities of love and hate. 这部戏剧探讨了爱与恨的二元性。


The poem highlights the dualities of life and death. 这首诗突出了生与死的二元性。


近义词：oppositions, contradictions, antinomies, paradoxes | 反义词：unity, singularity, oneness | 词性：名词
