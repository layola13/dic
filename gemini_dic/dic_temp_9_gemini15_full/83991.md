half-decade: /ˌhæfˈdɛkeɪd/ | n. | 五年 | A period of five years.  It's a compound word combining "half" and "decade". | Example:  A half-decade ago, she started her own business. 五年前，她创办了自己的公司。 | 近义词：five years | 反义词：无
