nonconclusions:/ˌnɒnkənˈkluːʒənz/ | n. |非结论；未下结论 |Things that are not conclusions; statements or ideas that do not definitively resolve a matter.  They may be preliminary findings, speculations, or simply points of discussion that haven't reached a final determination. |Example: The committee's report presented several nonconclusions, leaving many questions unanswered.  委员会的报告提出了几个未下结论的观点，留下许多悬而未决的问题。|近义词：speculations, hypotheses, suppositions |反义词：conclusions, resolutions, decisions |名词
