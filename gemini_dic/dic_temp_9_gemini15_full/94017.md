impaneled:/ɪmˈpænəld/| v. |组成陪审团；任命（陪审团成员）|To select and swear in a jury.  The process involves choosing potential jurors from a pool of eligible citizens and then questioning them to ensure their impartiality.|The judge impaneled a jury of twelve people.  法官选出了一组由十二个人组成的陪审团。|The court impaneled the grand jury to investigate the case. 法院组成大陪审团调查此案。|summon, convene, select |dismiss, discharge|
