eccentrical:/ɪkˈsɛntrɪkəl/ | adj. |古怪的；异乎寻常的；反常的 |  Odd or unconventional in behavior; departing from the norm.  Eccentric people often have unusual habits or interests that set them apart from others. They may be unconventional in their dress, speech, or behavior.  |例句：He is known for his eccentric behavior. = 他以其古怪的行为而闻名。

His eccentric aunt collected rubber ducks. = 他古怪的阿姨收集橡皮鸭。


近义词：odd, peculiar, quirky, unconventional, unusual | 反义词：conventional, normal, ordinary, typical
