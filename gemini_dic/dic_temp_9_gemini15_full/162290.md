preaccommodation:/ˌpriːəkɒməˈdeɪʃən/ | n. | 预先安排；预先适应；先期适应 | The process of making arrangements or adjustments in advance.  It often implies anticipating needs or challenges and proactively addressing them before they arise. This can apply to various contexts, from physical spaces to social situations or mental preparations. | Example: The hotel offered preaccommodation services, ensuring a smooth check-in process.  酒店提供预先入住服务，确保入住流程顺利。  The athlete's preaccommodation training helped him adapt quickly to the high altitude. 运动员的预先适应性训练帮助他快速适应高海拔环境。| 近义词：preparation, anticipation, prearrangement | 反义词：improvisation, spontaneity |  名词
