reflexology:/ˌriːflekˈsɒlədʒi/ | n. | 反射疗法 | The application of pressure to specific points on the feet, hands, or ears to stimulate corresponding areas of the body and promote healing and well-being.  It is based on the principle that these points correspond to different organs and systems in the body. | Example:  She found relief from her headaches through regular reflexology sessions. = 她通过定期进行反射疗法缓解了头痛。

Example:  Many people believe that reflexology can help improve circulation and reduce stress. = 许多人认为反射疗法有助于改善血液循环和减轻压力。


近义词：acupressure (指压疗法), massage (按摩)
反义词：None (没有直接的反义词，因为反射疗法本身不是一个有相反含义的概念)


词性: 名词
