delighter:/dɪˈlaɪtər/| n. |令人高兴的人或事|A person or thing that gives great pleasure.  |例句：The new puppy is a real delighter for the children.  新来的小狗让孩子们非常开心。|例句：His performance was a delighter for the audience. 他的表演让观众非常高兴。|近义词：pleasure, joy, delight, source of happiness|反义词：disappointment, displeasure, annoyance
