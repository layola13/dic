pare-down:/perˈdaʊn/| vt.|精简，简化，削减|To reduce something in size, amount, or extent; simplify.|例句：We need to pare down our expenses.  我们需要削减开支。

例句：The editor pared down the manuscript to make it more concise. 编辑精简了手稿使其更简洁。

近义词：reduce, simplify, curtail, trim

反义词：expand, enlarge, augment, increase

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pares down his workload every week. = 他每周都精简他的工作量。
They pare down the budget carefully. = 他们仔细地削减预算。


一般过去时:
She pared down her belongings before moving. = 她搬家前精简了她的物品。
The company pared down its staff last year. = 公司去年裁减了员工。


现在完成时:
We have pared down our debt significantly. = 我们已经大幅减少了债务。
They have pared down the report to the essentials. = 他们已将报告精简到要点。


过去完成时:
He had pared down his collection of stamps before the auction. = 拍卖前，他已经精简了他的邮票收藏。
They had pared down the project scope to meet the deadline. = 为了赶上截止日期，他们已经缩小了项目的范围。


将来时:
We will pare down our marketing budget next quarter. = 下个季度我们将削减营销预算。
She will pare down her social media presence. = 她将精简她在社交媒体上的存在。


虚拟语气:
If we pared down our spending, we would have more savings. = 如果我们削减开支，我们将有更多积蓄。
I wish I had pared down my workload earlier. = 我真希望我早点精简我的工作量。


被动语态:
The document was pared down for clarity. = 文件被精简以提高清晰度。
The proposal was pared down to fit the time constraints. = 建议书被精简以适应时间限制。


疑问句:
Have you pared down your possessions lately? = 你最近精简你的财产了吗？
Will they pare down the number of employees? = 他们会裁减员工人数吗？


否定句:
He didn't pare down his speech enough. = 他的演讲没有精简到足够程度。
They haven't pared down their expenses yet. = 他们还没有削减开支。


PHRASE->

pare down the size = 缩小规模
