disbelievingly:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːvɪŋli/| adv.|不相信地；怀疑地 |Expressing disbelief or doubt.  It describes the manner in which something is done or said, indicating a lack of faith or trust. |例句：He stared disbelievingly at the empty plate. 他难以置信地盯着空盘子。

例句：She shook her head disbelievingly at his outrageous claim. 她难以置信地摇了摇头，表示对他的荒唐说法不相信。


近义词： incredulously, skeptically, doubtfully, unbelievingly

反义词：believingly, trustingly, confidently


词源: dis- (否定前缀) + believ (believe 的词根) + -ing (现在分词后缀) + -ly (副词后缀)  The word is formed by adding negative prefix "dis-" to the present participle of "believe" and then adding the adverbial suffix "-ly".  It literally means "in a manner of not believing".
