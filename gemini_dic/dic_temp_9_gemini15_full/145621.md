overconsiderations:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃənz/| n. |过度考虑；反复斟酌；过分考虑|Excessive or unnecessary thought or consideration given to something.  It often implies a negative consequence, such as anxiety, indecision, or missed opportunities. |例句：His overconsiderations prevented him from making a timely decision.  他的过度考虑妨碍了他及时做出决定。

例句：Her overconsiderations of every detail led to paralysis by analysis. 她对每一个细节的过度考虑导致了分析瘫痪。


近义词：overthinking, rumination, excessive deliberation, meticulousness (in a negative context)

反义词：impulsiveness, decisiveness, spontaneity


词性：名词
