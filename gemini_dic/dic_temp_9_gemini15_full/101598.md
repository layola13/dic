irrationality: /ˌɪræʃəˈnæləti/ | n. | 非理性；不合理；荒谬 | The quality or state of being irrational; lack of reason or logic.  It often refers to behavior, decisions, or beliefs that are not based on reason or logic. |  例句：The irrationality of his actions surprised everyone.  他的行为的不理性让每个人都感到惊讶。

例句：Her decision was based on pure irrationality, not on facts. 她的决定完全基于非理性，而不是事实。


近义词：unreasonableness, illogicality, absurdity, folly

反义词：rationality, reasonableness, logic, sense
