inanimated: /ɪˈnænɪmeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 无生命的，无生气的 | Lacking life; lifeless.  The word describes something that is not alive and does not possess the qualities of living organisms, such as growth, reproduction, or response to stimuli. | 例句：The desert landscape seemed inanimated and desolate.  沙漠景观看起来荒凉而死寂。

The rocks and stones are inanimated objects.  岩石和石头是无生命的物体。


近义词：lifeless, inanimate, dead, inert, unfeeling


反义词：living, animate, alive, vibrant, lively

词性：形容词
