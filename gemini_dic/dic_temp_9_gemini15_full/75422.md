frugivores:/ˈfruːdʒɪvɔːrz/| n. |食果动物|Animals that primarily eat fruit.  Frugivores play a crucial role in seed dispersal in many ecosystems.  Many bird and primate species are frugivores.|Many tropical birds are frugivores, relying on fruit as their main food source.|herbivores, granivores|carnivores|名词
