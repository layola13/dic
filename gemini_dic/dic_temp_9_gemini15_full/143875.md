outpoisoning: /ˌaʊtˈpɔɪzənɪŋ/ | v. |  毒性超过；毒死 | To surpass in poisonous effect; to kill by poisoning. |  This word is rare and primarily found in specialized contexts. It implies a superior level of toxicity compared to something else, or the act of killing through poisoning. | 例句：The new pesticide is said to outpoison its predecessors, offering greater effectiveness against pests. 新型杀虫剂据说比其前代毒性更强，对害虫更有效。


The venom of the black mamba outpoisons that of most other snakes. 黑曼巴蛇的毒液毒性超过大多数其他蛇。| 近义词：overpower, surpass, exceed (in toxicity) | 反义词：underpoison (not a standard word, but conveys the opposite meaning) |
