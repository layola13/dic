palmists:/ˈpɑːlmɪsts/ | n. | 掌相家，算命先生 | People who practice palmistry, the art of predicting the future or interpreting character from the lines on the palm of the hand.  |  Many palmists claim to be able to predict a person's future based on their palm lines.  许多算命先生声称能够根据掌纹预测一个人的未来。 | fortune tellers,  seers,  clairvoyants |  (无明显反义词) | 名词
