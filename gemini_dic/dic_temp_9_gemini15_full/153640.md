penthouses:/ˈpɛntaʊzɪz/| n. |顶层公寓，阁楼公寓|  A penthouse is a luxury apartment on the top floor of a building, often with extra features like a rooftop terrace or balcony.  Penthouses are typically spacious and offer stunning views.|  例句：They bought a stunning penthouse overlooking the city. 他们买了一套俯瞰城市的漂亮顶层公寓。

例句：The penthouse apartment boasts a private rooftop pool. 这套顶层公寓拥有一个私人的屋顶游泳池。


近义词：luxury apartment, top-floor apartment | 反义词：basement apartment, ground-floor apartment | 词性：名词


词源:  The word "penthouse" comes from the Old French word "pentis," meaning "lean-to" or "shed."  Originally, a penthouse was a small, often sloped structure built on top of a building, similar to a shed or attic. Over time, the meaning evolved to refer to the entire top-floor apartment in a building, especially a luxurious one.
