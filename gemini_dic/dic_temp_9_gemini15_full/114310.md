maintainable: /meɪnˈteɪnəbl/ | adj. | 可维护的，可持续的 | Capable of being maintained; easy to maintain.  Something that is maintainable is easy to keep in good condition or working order, or easy to repair.  |例句：The software is highly maintainable, making updates and bug fixes straightforward.  这个软件具有很高的可维护性，使得更新和修复错误变得简单直接。

The car's design is maintainable, requiring only routine maintenance. 这辆车的的设计易于维护，只需要进行例行的维护。

近义词：serviceable, sustainable, manageable, repairable | 反义词：unmaintainable, unsustainable, unmanageable, irreparable

词源:  Derived from the verb "maintain," which comes from the Old French "maintenir," meaning "to keep up, sustain, uphold."  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or possibility.
