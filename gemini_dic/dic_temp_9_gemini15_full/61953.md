emulsifies:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪz/| v.|乳化；使乳化|To mix two or more liquids that usually don’t mix, such as oil and water, into a stable mixture.|The soap emulsifies the grease, making it easier to wash away. 肥皂使油脂乳化，更容易洗掉。|近义词：blend, mix|反义词：separate, segregate
