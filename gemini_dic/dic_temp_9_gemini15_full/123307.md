monogoneutic:/ˌmɒnəɡoʊˈnjuːtɪk/|/ˌmɑːnəɡoʊˈnjuːtɪk/| adj. | 单次繁殖的；一次性繁殖的 |  Referring to organisms that reproduce only once in their lifetime.  This is in contrast to iteroparous organisms, which reproduce multiple times. | Example:  Salmon are a monogoneutic species, spawning once before they die.  鲑鱼是一种一次性繁殖的物种，它们产卵一次后就死了。|近义词： semelparous | 反义词： iteroparous |  形容词
