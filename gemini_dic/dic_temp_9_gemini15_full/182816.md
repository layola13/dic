repealing:/rɪˈpiːlɪŋ/| v.|废除，撤销|The act of revoking or annulling a law, order, or agreement.|例句：The government is repealing the unpopular tax law. 政府正在废除不受欢迎的税法。|例句：They are considering repealing the ban on smoking in public places.他们正在考虑取消在公共场所吸烟的禁令。|近义词：rescinding, annulling, abrogating, voiding|反义词：enacting, establishing, instituting|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government repeals outdated laws. = 政府废除过时的法律。
They repeal the regulation every year. = 他们每年都会废除这项规章。


一般过去时:
Congress repealed the prohibition law. = 国会废除了禁酒令。
The court repealed the previous ruling. = 法院撤销了之前的裁决。


现在完成时:
They have repealed several discriminatory laws. = 他们已经废除了几项歧视性法律。
The company has repealed its previous policy. = 公司已经废除了其之前的政策。


过去完成时:
The judge had repealed the order before the appeal. = 法官在申诉之前已经撤销了命令。
They had repealed the tax before the election. = 他们在选举前已经取消了税收。


将来时:
The senate will repeal the act next month. = 参议院下个月将废除该法案。
They will repeal the law if public opinion changes. = 如果民意发生变化，他们将废除这项法律。


虚拟语气:
If they repealed the law, many people would benefit. = 如果他们废除了这项法律，许多人将从中受益。
I wish they would repeal the unfair regulation. = 我希望他们能废除不公平的规章。


被动语态:
The law was repealed last year. = 该法律去年被废除了。
The decision will be repealed soon. = 该决定很快就会被撤销。


疑问句:
Will the government repeal the tax? = 政府会废除这项税收吗？
Has the law been repealed yet? = 这项法律已经被废除了吗？


否定句:
The government did not repeal the law. = 政府没有废除这项法律。
They will not repeal the regulation this year. = 他们今年不会废除这项规章。


PHRASE->
repeal a law = 废除法律
repeal a regulation = 废除规章
repeal a policy = 废除政策
repeal an order = 撤销命令
repeal a ban = 取消禁令
