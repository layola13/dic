marks:/mɑːks/| n.|分数，标记，痕迹，显著特征|Marks are used to assess student performance.|近义词：scores, grades, signs, traces|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gets good marks in math. = 他数学成绩很好。
The teacher marks the papers carefully. = 老师仔细批改试卷。

一般过去时:
She marked all the important points in the book. = 她把书中所有重要的点都做了标记。
I received high marks on my exam. = 我考试得了高分。

现在完成时:
They have marked all the answers. = 他们已经批改完所有答案了。
He has earned excellent marks throughout his academic career. = 他在整个学习生涯中都取得了优异的成绩。

过去完成时:
She had already marked the papers before the meeting. = 会议之前她已经批改完试卷了。
The professor had marked the exams before the students left. = 学生离开之前教授已经批改完试卷了。

将来时:
He will mark the essays tomorrow. = 他明天将批改论文。
They will receive their marks next week. = 他们下周将收到成绩。

虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I would have gotten better marks. = 如果我学习更努力的话，我会取得更好的成绩。
I wish I had received higher marks on the test. = 我希望我在测试中得到更高的分数。

被动语态:
The papers are marked by the teacher. = 试卷由老师批改。
The exams were marked fairly and accurately. = 考试评分公平准确。

疑问句:
What marks did you get on the test? = 你考试得了多少分？
How many marks are there on this page? = 这页上有多少个标记？

否定句:
He didn't get good marks in history. = 他历史成绩不好。
She didn't mark the important parts of the document. = 她没有标记文件的重点部分。
