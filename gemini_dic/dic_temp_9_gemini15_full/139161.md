obi:/ˈoʊbiː/| n. |腰带，带子；（日本）武士的腰带|A long sash or belt, especially a wide, often elaborately decorated one worn as part of a traditional Japanese kimono.  It can also refer to similar belts in other cultures.|例句：She wore a beautiful obi with her kimono. 她穿着和服，系着一条漂亮的腰带。

例句：The martial artist tied his obi tightly before the demonstration. 武术家在表演前紧紧地系好了他的腰带。

近义词：sash, belt, girdle

反义词：None (the antonym depends on the context,  e.g., if contrasting with "loose" it would be "tight")

词性：Noun
