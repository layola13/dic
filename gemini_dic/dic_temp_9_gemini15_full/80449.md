gong:/ɡɒŋ/|/ɡɔŋ/| n. |锣；（尤指大型的）铜锣 |A gong is a large, flat, circular metal plate that makes a loud, resonant sound when struck with a mallet.  It is often used in musical performances, religious ceremonies, or as a signal. |例句：The monk struck the gong to signal the start of the meditation.  和尚敲响了锣，表示冥想开始。|例句：The orchestra concluded the piece with a resounding gong.管弦乐队以响亮的锣声结束了乐曲。|近义词：cymbal, bell |反义词： N/A
