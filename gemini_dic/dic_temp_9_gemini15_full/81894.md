griffinesque:/ˌɡrɪfɪˈnɛsk/| adj.|格里芬式的，像狮鹫的；奇异的，怪诞的|Resembling a griffin; fantastic or grotesque in appearance or nature.  The term often suggests a majestic yet somewhat frightening or awe-inspiring quality, combining elements of strength, power, and perhaps a touch of the bizarre.|例句：The architecture of the castle was griffinesque, with gargoyle-like figures and imposing towers.  城堡的建筑风格是格里芬式的，有着像石像鬼一样的雕像和雄伟的塔楼。

The painting depicted a griffinesque creature, part lion, part eagle, with a serpentine tail. 这幅画描绘了一个格里芬式的生物，一部分是狮子，一部分是鹰，还有一条蛇形的尾巴。|近义词：grotesque, fantastical, monstrous, bizarre|反义词：ordinary, commonplace, normal, mundane
