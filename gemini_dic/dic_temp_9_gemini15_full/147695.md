overpraising:/ˈoʊvərˈpreɪzɪŋ/| v.|过分赞扬，吹捧|The act of praising excessively; giving excessive or undeserved praise.  It can imply insincerity or a lack of objectivity.|例句：Overpraising children can lead to inflated egos. 过分赞扬孩子会导致他们自负。

例句：His achievements were overpraised by the media. 他的成就被媒体过分吹捧了。


近义词：extolling, flattering, lauding, lionizing, aggrandizing


反义词：criticizing, belittling, downplaying, underestimating


词性：动词
