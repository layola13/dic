overpatient:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪʃənt/ | adj. | 过度耐心的；过于忍耐的 | Excessively patient; showing too much patience.  It often implies a level of patience that is detrimental or unproductive. | 例句：He was overpatient with the disruptive student, allowing the bad behavior to continue.  他过度包容那个捣乱的学生，导致不良行为持续下去。


例句：Her overpatient approach to managing the project led to missed deadlines. 她对项目管理过于耐心，导致错过最后期限。


近义词：excessively patient, overly tolerant, long-suffering (in a negative context)


反义词：impatient, intolerant, hasty


词性: adjective
