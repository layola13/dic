nontractability: /ˌnɒntrækˈtæbɪləti/ | n. | 难处理性；棘手性；难以解决 | The inability to be solved or managed easily.  It refers to a problem or situation that is extremely difficult, if not impossible, to deal with or resolve. This could be due to complexity, lack of information, or inherent resistance to solutions. | Example: The nontractability of the political conflict led to years of instability.  政治冲突的棘手性导致了多年的动荡不安。The nontractability of the software bug frustrated the developers.  软件错误的难以解决让开发人员感到沮丧。 | 近义词：intractability, intractableness,  insolubility,  unmanageability | 反义词：tractability, manageability, solvability
