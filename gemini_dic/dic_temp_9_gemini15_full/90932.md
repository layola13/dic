hurdy-gurdy:/ˈhɜːdi ˈɡɜːrdi/| n. |手摇风琴|A hurdy-gurdy is a stringed musical instrument played by turning a crank that rotates a wheel which rubs against the strings to produce sound.  It is also known as a vielle à roue. |The street musician played a lively tune on his hurdy-gurdy.|近义词：vielle à roue, wheel fiddle|反义词:None.
