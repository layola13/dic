nervure:/ˈnɜːrvjuːr/| n.|叶脉；翅脉；纹理|A nervure is a vein or rib in a leaf or insect wing.  It refers to the system of veins that support and transport fluids within a leaf or wing.  The term is often used in botany and entomology. |Example: The delicate nervures of the butterfly's wings were beautifully intricate. 蝴蝶翅膀上精致的叶脉错综复杂。 The leaf's nervures were clearly visible. 叶子上的叶脉清晰可见。|近义词：vein, rib,  streak|反义词：无
