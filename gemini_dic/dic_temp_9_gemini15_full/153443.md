penner:/ˈpɛnər/| n. |（尤指粗俗的）写作者，笔者，涂鸦者 | A penner is a person who writes, especially one who writes in a crude or vulgar way.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting a lack of skill or sophistication. |例句：He's a prolific penner of inflammatory articles. 他是一个多产的煽动性文章的作者。 He's just a penner, not a true artist. 他只是一个写作者，而不是一个真正的艺术家。|近义词：scribe, writer, author, scribbler |反义词：artist (in the context of sophisticated writing)
