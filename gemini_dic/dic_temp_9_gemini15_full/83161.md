guttersnipe:/ˈɡʌtərˌsnaɪp/| n. |街童，流浪儿|A guttersnipe is a neglected and often ragged child who lives in the streets and survives by begging or petty theft.  They are often depicted as being involved in criminal activities or living in impoverished conditions. |例句：The guttersnipe, despite his tough exterior, showed a surprising kindness to the lost puppy.  这个街童，尽管外表粗犷，却对丢失的小狗表现出令人惊讶的善良。

例句：The film portrayed a stark reality of life for guttersnipes in the Victorian era. 这部电影刻画了维多利亚时代街童生活的残酷现实。|近义词：street urchin, waif, ragamuffin |反义词：privileged child, well-to-do child |名词
