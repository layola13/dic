seasoner:/ˈsiːzənər/| n. |调味品；调味料|A substance used to add flavor to food.  Seasoners can include herbs, spices, salt, pepper, and other flavor enhancers.|例句：Salt and pepper are common seasoners. 盐和胡椒是常见的调味品。

例句：She added a blend of exotic seasoners to the stew. 她往炖菜里加了一混合多种异域香料的调味料。


近义词：spice, condiment, seasoning


反义词：None (no direct antonym)


词性：noun
