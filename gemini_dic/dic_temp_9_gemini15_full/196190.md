sequela:/ˈsiːkwəlɑː/| n. |后遗症，后果 |A sequela is a condition that is the consequence of a previous disease or injury.  It refers to a pathological condition resulting from a prior disease, injury, or other trauma.  Sequelae can be immediate or develop later. |Example:  Paralysis can be a sequela of a stroke.  中风后遗症可能是瘫痪。
