skirmishes:/ˈskɜːrmɪʃɪz/ | n. |小规模冲突，小冲突，摩擦|A brief and minor fight, especially one between small groups of soldiers.|例句：The army engaged in several skirmishes with the rebels.  军队与叛军发生了几次小规模冲突。|例句：There were a few skirmishes between rival football fans after the game.  比赛结束后，几个敌对的足球球迷之间发生了一些小冲突。|近义词：encounter, clash, fray, brawl |反义词：major battle, war
