sabrebill: /ˈseɪbərˌbɪl/ | n. |剑嘴鸟|A bird of the genus *Ramphastos*, characterized by a large, often brightly colored bill resembling a sabre.  These birds are found in the Neotropics.  They are known for their distinctive calls and their role in seed dispersal.|The sabrebill perched on a branch, its massive bill catching the sunlight.|近义词：toucan (a broader term)|反义词：无
