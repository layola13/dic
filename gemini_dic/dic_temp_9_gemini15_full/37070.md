Combativity is often displayed through aggressive behavior and a readiness to engage in conflict. |例句：His combativity surprised everyone; he'd always seemed so mild-mannered.  他的好斗性让每个人都感到惊讶；他一直看起来那么温和。

例句：The team showed great combativity in the final match, despite being down by two goals.  尽管落后两球，球队在决赛中展现了强大的斗志。
