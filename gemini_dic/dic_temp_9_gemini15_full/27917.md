canters:/ˈkæntərz/| v.|慢跑，缓步跑|To ride or run at a moderate pace, faster than a walk but slower than a gallop.|例句：The horse cantered gracefully across the field.  这匹马优雅地慢跑过田野。|例句：She cantered along the beach, enjoying the sea breeze. 她沿着海滩慢跑，享受着海风。|近义词：trot, lope|反义词：gallop, sprint|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He canters his horse every morning. = 他每天早上骑马慢跑。
The children canter happily along the path. = 孩子们沿着小路快乐地慢跑。

一般过去时:
The jockey cantered his horse towards the finish line. = 骑师骑着马慢跑向终点线跑去。
We cantered through the woods yesterday. = 我们昨天在树林里慢跑。

现在完成时:
She has cantered several miles this morning. = 她今天早上已经慢跑了数英里。
They have cantered across the plains many times. = 他们多次在平原上慢跑。

过去完成时:
He had cantered for hours before he stopped. = 他慢跑了几个小时才停下来。
They had cantered to the top of the hill before the rain started. = 下雨前他们已经慢跑到山顶了。

将来时:
We will canter along the river tomorrow. = 我们明天将在河边慢跑。
The riders will canter across the open fields. = 骑手们将在开阔的田野上慢跑。

虚拟语气:
If I had a horse, I would canter along the beach. = 如果我有一匹马，我会在海滩上慢跑。
I wish I could canter as gracefully as she does. = 我希望我能像她一样优雅地慢跑。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The horse was cantered gently by the rider. = 这匹马被骑手轻轻地慢跑着。(不太自然)
The race was won by the horse that cantered the fastest. = 比赛是由跑得最快的马赢得的。(此处canter 并非被动语态的核心)


疑问句:
Does he often canter his horse? = 他经常骑马慢跑吗？
Will they canter tomorrow morning? = 他们明天早上会慢跑吗？

否定句:
He doesn't canter his horse every day. = 他不是每天都骑马慢跑。
They didn't canter through the park yesterday. = 他们昨天没有在公园里慢跑。

canter past: 慢跑经过
