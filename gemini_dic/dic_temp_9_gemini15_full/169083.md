progressions:/prəˈɡreʃənz/| n. |进展；级数；一系列；发展 | A series of changes or developments.  It can refer to a sequence of numbers, a series of events, or a gradual improvement or worsening. |例句：The doctor monitored the progression of the disease.  医生监测了疾病的进展。

例句：The musical piece showcased a beautiful progression of chords. 这首乐曲展现了优美的和弦进行。

近义词：advancements, developments, sequences, series, evolutions |反义词：regressions, declines, deteriorations |词性:名词


词源:  From Old French *progression, from Latin progressio, from progredi "to go forward," from pro- "forward" + gradi "to step, go."
