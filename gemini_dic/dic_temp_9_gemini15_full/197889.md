shipwrecking:/ˈʃɪpˌrɛkɪŋ/| v.|船只失事；使遇难；破坏；|The act of wrecking or destroying a ship; the destruction of a ship by storm, rocks, etc.; causing the ruin or failure of something.|例句：The storm caused the shipwrecking of many vessels. 暴风雨导致许多船只失事。|例句：His ambition led to the shipwrecking of his career. 他的野心导致了他的事业失败。|近义词：wrecking, ruining, destroying|反义词：saving, rescuing, preserving|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm is shipwrecking many boats. = 暴风雨正在使许多船只失事。
He is shipwrecking his reputation with his actions. = 他正因自己的行为而毁坏自己的名声。

一般过去时:
The pirates shipwrecked the merchant vessel. = 海盗使那艘商船失事。
The company's poor management shipwrecked its chances of success. = 公司糟糕的管理葬送了它成功的希望。

现在完成时:
The harsh weather has shipwrecked countless ships over the centuries. = 恶劣的天气几个世纪以来已经使无数船只失事。
His reckless behavior has shipwrecked his family life. = 他鲁莽的行为毁了他的家庭生活。

过去完成时:
They had shipwrecked their hopes before the final decision was made. = 在最终决定做出之前，他们就已经破灭了希望。
The company had shipwrecked its financial stability through poor investments. = 公司通过不良投资已经损害了其财务稳定性。

将来时:
The approaching hurricane will shipwreck many coastal buildings. = 即将来临的飓风将使许多沿海建筑物遇难。
If he continues this way, his actions will shipwreck his marriage. = 如果他继续这样下去，他的行为将毁掉他的婚姻。

虚拟语气:
If they had been more careful, they wouldn't have shipwrecked their business. = 如果他们更小心一点，就不会毁掉他们的生意了。
I wish I hadn't shipwrecked our friendship with that careless remark. = 我真希望我没有因为那句不经意的评论而毁掉我们的友谊。

被动语态:
Many ships were shipwrecked during the great storm. = 在那场大风暴中，许多船只失事。
His career was shipwrecked by a series of unfortunate events. = 他的事业因一系列不幸的事件而毁于一旦。

疑问句:
Were any ships shipwrecked in the recent typhoon? = 最近的台风中是否有船只失事？
Was his reputation shipwrecked by the scandal? = 他的名誉被丑闻毁了吗？

否定句:
The captain skillfully avoided the rocks and didn't shipwreck his ship. = 船长巧妙地避开了礁石，没有使他的船失事。
They managed to avoid the pitfalls and didn't shipwreck their business. = 他们设法避免了陷阱，没有毁掉他们的生意。
