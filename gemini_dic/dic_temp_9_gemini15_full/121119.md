misdecision:/ˌmɪsdɪˈsɪʒən/| n. |错误决定，失误决定|A misdecision is a decision that is wrong or unwise, often leading to negative consequences. It implies a lack of proper judgment or consideration.  |Example:  The company's misdecision to invest in that failing project cost them millions. 公司错误地投资那个失败的项目导致他们损失数百万。

The misdecision to ignore the warning signs led to the accident. 忽视警告标志的错误决定导致了事故。|近义词：bad decision, wrong choice, poor judgment, blunder, mistake |反义词：wise decision, sound judgment, good choice
