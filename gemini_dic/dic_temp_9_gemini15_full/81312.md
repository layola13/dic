grapefruits:/ˈɡreɪpˌfruːts/ | n. |柚子 |A grapefruit is a large, round citrus fruit with a thick rind and a slightly bitter, acidic taste.  It is a hybrid fruit, a cross between a pomelo and an orange.  Grapefruits are rich in vitamin C and are often eaten for breakfast or as part of a healthy diet. |例句：I had a grapefruit for breakfast this morning.  今天早上我吃了个柚子。

例句：She added some grapefruit segments to her salad. 她往沙拉里加了一些柚子瓣。


近义词：citrus fruit, pomelo (partially) | 反义词：无
