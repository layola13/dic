self-teachers: /ˈsɛlf ˈtiːtʃərz/ | n. | 自学者 | People who learn a subject or skill independently, without formal instruction.  They utilize various resources like books, online courses, tutorials, and personal projects to acquire knowledge and expertise. | Example: Many self-teachers have achieved remarkable success in their chosen fields.  许多自学者在他们选择的领域取得了显著的成就。 | 近义词: independent learners, autodidacts | 反义词: students (in a formal setting) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Self-teachers often utilize online resources. = 自学者经常利用在线资源。
Many self-teachers find success through dedicated practice. = 许多自学者通过刻苦练习获得成功。


一般过去时:
She was a self-teacher who mastered several programming languages. = 她是一位自学者，掌握了几种编程语言。
He became a self-teacher after leaving formal education. = 他离开正规教育后成为了一名自学者。


现在完成时:
He has become a skilled musician through self-teaching. = 他通过自学成为了一名熟练的音乐家。
She has taught herself three foreign languages. = 她自学了三种外语。


过去完成时:
Before the project, he had been a self-teacher for five years. = 在项目开始之前，他已经自学了五年。
By the time she applied, she had already become a proficient self-teacher. = 当她申请时，她已经成为一名熟练的自学者了。


将来时:
He will continue to be a self-teacher throughout his life. = 他将一生都继续作为一名自学者。
She will learn to code through self-teaching next year. = 她明年将通过自学学习编程。


虚拟语气:
If I were a self-teacher, I would focus on practical application. = 如果我是自学者，我会专注于实践应用。
I wish I had been a more disciplined self-teacher in my youth. = 我希望我年轻时能成为一个更有纪律的自学者。


被动语态:
Many skills can be learned through self-teaching. = 许多技能可以通过自学来学习。(被动语态中"self-teachers"很少用作主语)
The project was completed by a group of dedicated self-teachers. = 这个项目是由一群专注的自学者完成的。


疑问句:

否定句:

She wasn't a self-teacher initially, but she became one later. = 她最初不是自学者，但她后来成为了自学者。
