minoans:/mɪˈnoʊənz/| n. |米诺斯人|The Minoans were a Bronze Age Aegean civilization that flourished on the island of Crete from approximately 2700 to 1450 BC.  They are known for their advanced culture, including their impressive palaces, sophisticated art, and writing system (Linear A).|例句：The Minoan civilization is famous for its elaborate palaces and frescoes.|近义词：Cretians (in a limited context)|反义词：无
