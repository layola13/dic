hundreds:/ˈhʌndrədz/| n.|几百；许多|The word "hundreds" refers to a quantity of something that is in the range of 100 to 999, or a large, unspecified number. It is often used to express approximation rather than an exact count. |例句：There are hundreds of stars in the night sky. 夜空中有数百颗星星。  Hundreds of people attended the concert.  数百人参加了音乐会。|近义词：many, numerous, scores|反义词：few, a handful
