pace-making:/ˈpeɪsˌmeɪkɪŋ/| n. adj.|起搏；起搏的；|The act or process of setting the pace or speed, often in a competitive context.  It can refer to the setting of a pace in a race, or the role of a pacemaker in medicine. |例句：The team's pace-making in the early stages of the race set the tone for the entire competition.  这个团队在比赛早期的领跑节奏为整场比赛定下了基调。

例句：His pace-making abilities were crucial to the team's success.  他的领跑能力对团队的成功至关重要。


近义词：leading, guiding, setting the pace |反义词：hindering, obstructing, slowing down
