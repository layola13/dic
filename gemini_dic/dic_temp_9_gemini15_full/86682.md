hellish:/ˈhɛlɪʃ/| adj.|地狱般的；可怕的；极坏的|extremely bad or unpleasant; like hell. The journey was hellish.|例句：The traffic was hellish this morning.  今早的交通状况糟糕透顶。

例句：The weather was hellish; it was scorching hot and humid. 天气糟糕透顶；又热又潮湿。


近义词：terrible, dreadful, awful, horrific, infernal|反义词：heavenly, delightful, pleasant, wonderful
