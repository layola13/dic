micro-scale: /ˈmaɪkroʊskeɪl/ | adj. | 微观的，微尺度的 | Relating to or denoting processes or structures occurring on a very small scale, typically less than a millimeter.  | 例句：The experiment was conducted on a micro-scale to save resources.  实验在微观尺度上进行以节省资源。  


例句：Micro-scale manufacturing is becoming increasingly important in various industries. 微型制造在各个行业中变得越来越重要。


近义词：minute, tiny, microscopic, nanoscale (for even smaller scales)


反义词：macroscale, large-scale


词性:形容词
