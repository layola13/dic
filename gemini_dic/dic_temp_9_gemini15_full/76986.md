garden-variety: /ˈɡɑːrdn vərɑɪəti/ | adj. |普通的；常见的；平凡的 |  This adjective describes something that is common, ordinary, or unexceptional.  It implies a lack of distinction or special features. The term originates from the idea that garden plants are typically common and not particularly rare or unusual. | 例句：He drives a garden-variety sedan. 他开着一辆普通的轿车。

例句：The restaurant served garden-variety American food. 这家餐馆供应普通的美国菜。


近义词：common, ordinary, commonplace, typical, standard, usual, unexceptional

反义词：unusual, extraordinary, exceptional, rare, unique, remarkable


词性：形容词
