escaping:/ɪˈskeɪpɪŋ/ | v. | 逃跑；逃脱；避免；泄露 | The act of escaping from a place, situation, or person; avoiding something unpleasant or dangerous.  |例句：He was lucky to escape from the burning building. 他很幸运从燃烧的建筑物中逃了出来。

例句：They escaped serious injury in the accident. 他们在事故中幸免于重伤。


近义词： fleeing, evading, avoiding | 反义词： confronting, facing, encountering | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is escaping from prison. = 他正在越狱。
She escapes her responsibilities. = 她逃避她的责任。


一般过去时:
The prisoner escaped last night. = 囚犯昨晚逃跑了。
We escaped the storm. = 我们躲过了暴风雨。


现在完成时:
He has escaped from custody several times. = 他已经几次逃脱了拘留。
They have escaped the worst of the crisis. = 他们躲过了这场危机的最糟糕的部分。


过去完成时:
The thief had escaped before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，小偷已经逃跑了。
She had escaped the clutches of poverty. = 她已经摆脱了贫困的困境。


将来时:
He will escape punishment. = 他将会逃脱惩罚。
They will escape to the mountains. = 他们将逃往山区。


虚拟语气:
If only I could escape this feeling. = 但愿我能摆脱这种感觉。
I wish I could escape the noise. = 我真希望我能逃离噪音。


被动语态:
The prisoner was escaped. = 囚犯被越狱了。(此处是被动语态，但表达方式略显不自然，通常用“The prisoner escaped”更常见)
The problem was escaped. = 问题被回避了。(此处是被动语态，但表达方式略显不自然，通常用“They escaped the problem”更常见)


疑问句:
How did he escape? = 他是怎么逃脱的？
Can we escape this situation? = 我们能摆脱这种情况吗？


否定句:
He couldn't escape his fate. = 他无法逃脱他的命运。
They didn't escape the consequences. = 他们没有逃避后果。


PHRASE->
escape from = 从...逃脱
escape to = 逃往...
escape notice = 未被注意到
escape the attention of = 未被...注意
narrow escape = 死里逃生
