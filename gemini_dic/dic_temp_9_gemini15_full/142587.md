osteogen:/ˈɒstiədʒən/| n. |成骨作用；成骨细胞|The process of bone formation;  the formation of bone tissue.  It involves the differentiation of mesenchymal stem cells into osteoblasts, which then synthesize and deposit bone matrix. |例句：The fracture healed quickly thanks to robust osteogen.  骨折由于强劲的成骨作用而快速愈合。|例句：Researchers are studying ways to stimulate osteogen for bone regeneration. 研究人员正在研究刺激成骨作用以促进骨骼再生的方法。|近义词：ossification, bone formation|反义词：bone resorption, osteolysis|名词
