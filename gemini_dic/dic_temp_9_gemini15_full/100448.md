intertroop:/ˈɪntə(r)truːp/| n. |部落间的，各部落间的|Relating to or occurring between different troops or groups, especially in a military or scouting context.  It suggests interaction, communication, or conflict between separate units.|例句：Intertroop rivalry was fierce during the scouting competition.  部落间的竞争在童子军比赛中非常激烈。


例句：The intertroop communication system proved vital during the emergency. 部落间的通讯系统在紧急情况下至关重要。


近义词：intergroup, interunit


反义词：intratroop (within a single troop)


词性: 名词
