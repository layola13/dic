presymptoms:/ˈpriːsɪmptəmz/| n. |前兆症状，先兆|Early warning signs or symptoms that precede the onset of a disease or condition.  These symptoms are often subtle and may not be immediately recognized as indicators of a more serious problem.|例句：Experiencing fatigue and headaches could be presymptoms of the flu.|近义词：prodroma, early warning signs|反义词：aftereffects, sequelae
