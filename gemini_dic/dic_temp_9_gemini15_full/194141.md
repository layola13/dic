self-initiatives:/ˌsɛlfɪˈnɪʃətɪvz/| n. |自主性行动；主动性；自我激励的行动 |Self-initiatives refer to actions or projects undertaken independently and proactively, without external prompting or direction.  They demonstrate a person's drive, resourcefulness, and commitment to taking charge of their own development or situation. |Example:  Her self-initiatives in streamlining the workflow significantly improved team efficiency.  她的主动改进工作流程显著提高了团队效率。|近义词：own initiatives, personal projects, proactive steps|反义词：passivity, inaction, dependence
