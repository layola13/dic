palatal:/ˈpælətl/| adj.|腭的；腭音的|Relating to the palate, the roof of the mouth.  The word can refer to sounds produced with the tongue near the palate (palatal sounds) or to anatomical structures related to the palate.|The palatal sounds in English include /j/ as in "yes" and /ʃ/ as in "ship".|近义词：palatine|反义词：None explicitly, but could be contrasted with terms referring to other parts of the mouth, like "dental" or "velar".
