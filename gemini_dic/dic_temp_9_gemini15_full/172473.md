pyracanth:/ˌpaɪrəˈkænθ/| n. |火棘, 火棘属植物|Pyracantha is a genus of evergreen shrubs in the rose family, Rosaceae.  They are known for their dense clusters of small white flowers followed by abundant bright berries that persist through the winter. These berries are popular with birds. |The pyracantha bushes were laden with bright red berries. 火棘灌木上挂满了鲜红的浆果。|近义词：firethorn (common name)|反义词：无
