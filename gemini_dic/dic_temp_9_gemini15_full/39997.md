consultantship:/kənˈsʌltənʃɪp/| n.|咨询职位；顾问身份|The position or role of a consultant; the state of being a consultant.  It refers to the job or engagement of providing expert advice or services.|例句：She secured a lucrative consultantship with a major corporation. 她获得了一家大公司一份利润丰厚的咨询职位。|例句：His consultantship lasted for six months. 他的顾问职位持续了六个月。|近义词：advisory role, consultancy, advisory position|反义词：employment (as an employee, not a consultant)
