renewability: /rɪˌnuːəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可再生性，可更新性 | The ability of something to be renewed or replaced.  It refers to resources that can be replenished naturally over a relatively short period.  This is often used in the context of energy sources, but also applies to other resources like forests and water. | Example:  The government is investing heavily in renewable energy sources to reduce carbon emissions.  政府正在大力投资可再生能源以减少碳排放。  The renewability of the forest depends on sustainable logging practices. 森林的可再生性取决于可持续的采伐实践。 | 近义词：sustainability, replaceability, replenishability | 反义词：non-renewability, exhaustibility
