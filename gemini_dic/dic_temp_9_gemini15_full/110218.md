lifeboat:/ˈlaɪfˌboʊt/| n. |救生艇|A small boat carried by a larger vessel (such as a ship or aircraft) for use in emergencies, especially to save people if the larger vessel sinks.  It is usually equipped with life-saving equipment.|例句：The passengers were ordered to abandon ship and take to the lifeboats. 乘客们被命令弃船并乘坐救生艇。

例句：The lifeboat crew rescued the survivors from the sinking ship. 救生艇艇员从沉船中救出了幸存者。


近义词：rescue boat, life raft
