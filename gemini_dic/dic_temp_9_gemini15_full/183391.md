repromises:/ˈriːˈprɑːmɪsɪz/| n.|再次承诺；重申承诺|The act of promising something again, often after a previous promise has been broken or doubted.  It implies a renewed commitment or assurance.|例句：His repromises didn't convince anyone after his previous failures. 他之前的失败让他再次承诺也无法取信于人。|近义词：reassurance, reaffirmation, reiteration|反义词：breach of promise, reneging
