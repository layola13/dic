movement:/ˈmuːvmənt/ |/ˈmuːvmənt/| n. |运动，移动；活动；乐章；运动；趋势；运转；迁移 |The act of moving or the process of being moved; a change of place or position. It can also refer to a group of people with a shared political aim; a significant development or change; a section of a musical work; the working parts of a mechanism. |例句：The movement of the planets is fascinating. 行星的运行令人着迷。

例句：The civil rights movement achieved significant progress. 民权运动取得了重大进展。


近义词：motion, activity, progress, trend,  campaign,  march


反义词：stillness, inactivity, stagnation
