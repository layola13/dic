castaways:/ˈkæstəweɪz/| n. |漂流者；遇难者|People who have been shipwrecked or otherwise stranded and left to survive on their own.  They are often depicted as struggling against the elements and facing various challenges to their survival.|例句：The castaways built a raft to escape the deserted island.  漂流者们建造了一艘木筏来逃离荒岛。

例句：After months at sea, the castaways were finally rescued.  在海上漂流了几个月后，漂流者们最终获救了。


近义词：shipwrecked people, survivors, the stranded


反义词：rescuers, saviors


词性：名词
