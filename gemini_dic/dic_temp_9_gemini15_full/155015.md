petering:/ˈpiːtərɪŋ/ | v. |逐渐减弱；逐渐消失；衰落| Gradually becoming less strong or active; declining; fading away.  |The excitement of the party was petering out.  派对的兴奋劲儿逐渐消失了。|The project is petering out due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，项目正在逐渐萎缩。| diminishing, fading, waning | intensifying, escalating | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The engine is petering out. = 引擎正在逐渐熄火。
The sound of the music is petering out. = 音乐声渐渐消失了。

一般过去时:
The conversation petered out. = 对话逐渐结束。
His enthusiasm petered out after a few weeks. = 他的热情几周后就消退了。

现在完成时:
Interest in the project has petered out. = 对这个项目的兴趣已经消退了。
The storm has petered out. = 暴风雨已经逐渐平息了。

过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, the fire had petered out. = 我们到达时，火势已经逐渐熄灭了。
The argument had petered out before he could apologize. = 在他道歉之前，争论已经平息了。

将来时:
The effects of the medicine will peter out eventually. = 药物的效果最终会逐渐消失。
The protests will likely peter out without strong leadership. = 没有强有力的领导，抗议活动可能会逐渐平息。

虚拟语气:
If the funding had not petered out, the project would have been completed. = 如果资金没有减少，项目早就完成了。
If only the rain would peter out. = 但愿雨能停下来。

被动语态:
The debate was petered out by the chairman. = 辩论被主席终止了。 (This is less common, as "peter out" is usually intransitive.)


疑问句:
Is the storm petering out? = 暴风雨正在平息吗？
Is his interest in the subject petering out? = 他对这个主题的兴趣正在减退吗？


否定句:
The argument didn't peter out; it escalated. = 争论并没有平息；反而升级了。
The protest didn't peter out; it continued for weeks. = 抗议活动并没有平息；它持续了几周。


PHRASE->

peter out gradually = 逐渐减弱
peter out slowly = 慢慢地消失
peter out completely = 完全消失
peter out quietly = 静悄悄地消失
peter out and die =  逐渐消失并死亡 (mainly used metaphorically)
