organisms:/ˈɔːrɡənɪzəmz/| n. |有机体，生物|Living things, including plants, animals, and microorganisms.|例句：All living organisms need water to survive. 所有生物都需要水才能生存。|近义词：creatures, beings, life forms|反义词：inanimate objects, non-living things

一般现在时:
Organisms reproduce to ensure the continuation of their species. = 有机体繁殖以确保其物种的延续。
Many organisms thrive in diverse environments. = 许多生物在不同的环境中茁壮成长。


一般过去时:
Scientists studied the organisms found in the deep sea. = 科学家研究了在深海中发现的有机体。
