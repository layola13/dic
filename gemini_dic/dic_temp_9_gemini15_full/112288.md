loquaciously:/ləˈkweɪʃəsli/| adv.| 健谈地；饶舌地；滔滔不绝地 |Speaking in a fluent and rapid manner; talkative.  |例句：He loquaciously described his adventures in the Amazon. 他滔滔不绝地描述了他亚马逊河的冒险经历。

例句：She loquaciously recounted the details of her day. 她饶舌地叙述了她一天的细节。


近义词：talkatively, garrulously, volubly, fluently


反义词： taciturnly, silently, quietly


词性：副词
