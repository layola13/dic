pull-strings:/pʊlˈstrɪŋz/ | v. |暗中操纵，幕后操控 |To secretly use influence or connections to achieve a desired outcome.  |例句：He pulled strings to get his son the job. 他暗中活动，帮儿子弄到那份工作。

例句：They pulled strings to get the legislation passed. 他们暗中操纵，使这项法案得以通过。


近义词：manipulate, influence, control, orchestrate | 反义词：be powerless, be helpless |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pulls strings to get what he wants. = 他暗中操纵以得到他想要的东西。
They pull strings in the government. = 他们在政府里暗中操控。


一般过去时:
She pulled strings to get her daughter into the university. = 她暗中活动，把女儿送进了大学。
The director pulled strings to secure funding for the project. = 导演暗中活动以确保项目的资金。


现在完成时:
He has pulled strings for years to maintain his position. = 他多年来一直暗中活动以维持自己的地位。
They have pulled strings to get the contract. = 他们暗中活动以获得合同。


过去完成时:
She had pulled strings before the meeting to ensure a positive outcome. = 会议前她就暗中活动以确保圆满的结果。
The company had pulled strings to avoid paying taxes. = 这家公司暗中活动以避免缴税。


将来时:
He will pull strings to get the promotion. = 他将会暗中活动以获得晋升。
They will pull strings to influence the election. = 他们将暗中活动以影响选举。


虚拟语气:
If he pulled strings, he would get the job. = 如果他暗中活动，他就能得到这份工作。
If they had pulled strings earlier, the result would have been different. = 如果他们早点暗中活动，结果就会不同。


被动语态:
Strings were pulled to get him released from prison. = 他被暗中活动营救出狱。
The decision was influenced by those who pulled strings behind the scenes. = 这个决定受到了幕后操纵者的影响。


疑问句:
Did he pull strings to get that award? = 他是不是暗中活动才获得那个奖项？
Who pulled strings to get this project approved? = 谁暗中活动使这个项目获得批准？


否定句:
He didn't pull strings; he earned the promotion fairly. = 他没有暗中活动；他是凭真本事获得晋升的。
They didn't pull strings; the success was due to hard work. = 他们没有暗中活动；成功是由于辛勤工作。


PHRASE->
pull strings for someone  = 为某人暗中活动
pull strings behind the scenes = 幕后操纵
pull strings to get something = 暗中活动以获得某物
pull strings in one's favor = 暗中活动以利于自己
pull strings with someone = 与某人暗中勾结


词源:
The phrase "pull strings" originates from the manipulation of marionettes or puppets.  The strings are pulled by a puppeteer to control the puppets' actions. This imagery extended metaphorically to describe the secret manipulation of people or events by those with influence.
