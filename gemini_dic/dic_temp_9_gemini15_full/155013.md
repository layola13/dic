peterel:/ˈpiːtərəl/| n. |海燕|A small, slender seabird of the family Hydrobatidae, typically having dark plumage and long, narrow wings.  They are known for their ability to skim low over the water.| Example:  The storm-petrel skimmed the waves, its tiny body barely visible against the grey sea.  暴风雨海燕掠过海面，它小小的身体在灰色的海面上几乎看不见。|近义词：storm-petrel, seabird|反义词：None specifically;  perhaps larger seabirds.|名词
