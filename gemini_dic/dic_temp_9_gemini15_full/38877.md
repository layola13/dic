conferences:/ˈkɒnfərənsɪz/| n.|会议；研讨会|A formal meeting for discussion, especially one involving experts or specialists in a particular field.|例句：The company is holding a series of conferences to discuss the new product. 公司正在举行一系列会议来讨论新产品。

例句：We attended an international conference on climate change. 我们参加了一个关于气候变化的国际会议。


近义词：meeting, convention, seminar, symposium|反义词：None|词性:n.
