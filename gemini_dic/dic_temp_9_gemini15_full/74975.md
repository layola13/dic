fricatrice:/frɪkəˈtriːs/| n. |油炸食物，煎炸调味汁|A fricatrice is a dish, typically consisting of fried or sautéed ingredients, often vegetables or meats, seasoned with a flavorful sauce or dressing.  It can refer to the sauce itself or the entire dish. The term is less common in modern English and is more often encountered in historical culinary texts. |Example: The chef prepared a delicious fricatrice of mushrooms and onions, seasoned with herbs and a touch of wine.  厨师准备了一道美味的蘑菇洋葱油炸菜，用香草和一点葡萄酒调味。|近义词：fritters, sauté, ragout|反义词：boiled, raw
