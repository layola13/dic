kittenish:/ˈkɪtənɪʃ/| adj.|像小猫的；调皮的；爱撒娇的；幼稚的| Playful, capricious, and charming, like a kitten.  The word suggests a lighthearted, sometimes mischievous, and often endearing quality. |例句：Her kittenish behavior charmed everyone at the party. 她像小猫一样淘气可爱的行为迷住了派对上的每个人。

例句：The kittenish antics of the puppies kept us entertained for hours. 小狗们像小猫一样顽皮的举动让我们玩乐了好几个小时。


近义词：playful, mischievous, coquettish, capricious, childish, girlish


反义词：serious, mature, solemn, adult, grave


词性：形容词
