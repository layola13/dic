potentate:/ˈpoʊtənˌteɪt/| n. |当权者；统治者；有权势的人 |A potentate is a person who holds significant power, especially a ruler of a country or territory.  The term often implies absolute or considerable authority. |例句：The potentate ruled his kingdom with an iron fist.  这位当权者铁腕统治他的王国。|例句：For centuries, the potentate's family had held sway over the land.几个世纪以来，这位当权者的家族一直统治着这片土地。|近义词：ruler, sovereign, monarch, emperor, king, dictator |反义词：subject, subordinate, commoner |名词
