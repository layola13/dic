dromond:/ˈdrɒmɒnd/| n. |轻型快速帆船，达罗蒙德帆船|A dromond was a type of fast, lightly built sailing ship, particularly prevalent in the Mediterranean during the Middle Ages.  They were often used by the Arabs, and later by other Mediterranean powers, for both merchant and military purposes.  Their speed and maneuverability made them effective in raiding and commerce. |The dromond was a significant vessel in medieval naval warfare.|近义词：galley, carrack |反义词：无
