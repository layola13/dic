psoriasis:/sɔːˈraɪəsɪs/| n.|银屑病，牛皮癣|A chronic autoimmune disease that causes raised, red, scaly patches to appear on the skin.|例句：He suffers from psoriasis, which causes significant discomfort. 他患有银屑病，这给他带来了很大的不适。|例句：The doctor prescribed a topical cream to treat her psoriasis. 医生开了外用药膏来治疗她的银屑病。|近义词：skin disease, eczema (有时)|反义词：healthy skin
