claustrophobia:/ˌklɔːstrəˈfoʊbiə/| n. |幽闭恐惧症|An extreme or irrational fear of confined places.|例句：Her claustrophobia made it impossible for her to ride in elevators.  她的幽闭恐惧症使她无法乘坐电梯。


例句：He suffers from claustrophobia and avoids small, enclosed spaces. 他患有幽闭恐惧症，避免狭小封闭的空间。


近义词：confined-space anxiety,  enclosed-space phobia


反义词：None (It's a phobia, not a state of being that has a direct opposite)


词性:名词
