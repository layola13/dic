counter-suggestion:/ˈkaʊntər səˈdʒɛstʃən/| n. |反建议，对策|A counter-suggestion is a suggestion that is different from or opposite to a previous suggestion.  It offers an alternative plan or idea.|  He offered a counter-suggestion to the initial proposal, suggesting a more cost-effective approach. 他对最初的提议提出了一个反建议，建议采取一种更经济有效的方法。|近义词：alternative suggestion, opposing suggestion, counterproposal|反义词：endorsement, approval
