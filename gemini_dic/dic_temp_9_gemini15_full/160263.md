pontoons:/pɒnˈtuːnz/| n.|浮桥；浮筒；(船的)浮箱|A pontoon is a flat-bottomed boat, often made of metal or wood, used to support a floating structure, such as a bridge, dock, or platform.  They can also be used as part of a ship's structure to increase buoyancy.|例句：The soldiers crossed the river on a pontoon bridge.士兵们通过浮桥过河。|例句：The damaged ship was kept afloat by several pontoons.受损的船只由几个浮筒支撑着，才没有沉没。|近义词：float, raft, barge|反义词：N/A
