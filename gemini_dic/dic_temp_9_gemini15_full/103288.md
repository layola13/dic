jibbahs:/ˈdʒɪbæz/| n.| 吉巴（一种长袍）|A jibbah is a long, loose-fitting robe, typically worn by men in some Middle Eastern and South Asian cultures.  It's often made of lightweight cotton or linen and is considered modest and comfortable attire.|The sheikh wore a flowing white jibbah.  这位酋长穿着一件飘逸的白袍。|近义词：thobe, kandura, thawb|反义词：无
