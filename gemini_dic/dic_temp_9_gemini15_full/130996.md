nonattainability: /ˌnɒnəˌteɪnəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不可实现性，无法达到 | The state or quality of being unattainable; impossibility of achievement.  It refers to something that cannot be reached or achieved, often due to inherent limitations or insurmountable obstacles. | The nonattainability of perfect happiness is a fundamental truth.  不可实现完美的幸福是一个基本真理。|  unattainableness, inaccessibility | attainability, achievability
