offsides:/ˈɒfsʌɪdz/ | /ˈɑːfsʌɪdz/ | adj.|越位(足球)|In football, being in an offside position means a player is closer to the opponent's goal line than both the ball and the second-to-last defender when the ball is played to them.  This is a violation of the rules, resulting in an indirect free kick to the opposing team.|例句：The referee called the striker offside.  裁判判罚前锋越位。|例句：He was caught offside and the attack was stopped. 他被判越位，进攻停止了。|近义词：illegal, foul|反义词：onsides
