persistive:/pərˈsɪstɪv/| adj.|坚持不懈的；持续的|Persisting; continuing to exist.|The persistive nature of the problem required a long-term solution.  这个问题的持续性需要一个长期的解决方案。|persistent, enduring, continuous|intermittent, fleeting, temporary|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem persists. = 问题持续存在。
He persists in his efforts. = 他坚持他的努力。


一般过去时:
The symptoms persisted for several days. = 症状持续了好几天。
She persisted in her belief. = 她坚持她的信念。


现在完成时:
The rain has persisted all day. = 雨下了一整天。
They have persisted in their demands. = 他们坚持他们的要求。


过去完成时:
The drought had persisted for months before the rains came. = 在雨水到来之前，干旱已经持续了几个月。
He had persisted in his studies despite the difficulties. = 尽管困难重重，他还是坚持学习。


将来时:
The cold weather will persist into next week. = 冷天气将会持续到下周。
She will persist in her efforts to succeed. = 她将坚持努力以取得成功。


虚拟语气:
If he persisted, he might succeed. = 如果他坚持下去，他可能会成功。
I wish he persisted in his training. = 我希望他坚持他的训练。


被动语态:
The problem cannot be easily overcome; it persists. = 这个问题不容易克服；它持续存在。
The idea was persistently challenged, but it persisted.  这个想法一直受到质疑，但它仍然存在。


疑问句:
Does the pain persist? = 疼痛持续吗？
Will this trend persist? = 这种趋势会持续下去吗？


否定句:
The improvement didn't persist. = 进步没有持续。
The effect didn't persist long. = 效果持续时间不长。


PHRASE->
persist in = 坚持
persist with = 坚持做…
persistently challenge = 持续挑战
persist despite = 尽管…仍然坚持
persist throughout = 贯穿始终坚持
