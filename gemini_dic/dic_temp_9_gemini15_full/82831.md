gulbenkian:/ɡʌlˈbɛŋkiən/| n. |古尔班基安；古尔班基安基金会 |The word "Gulbenkian" refers to Calouste Gulbenkian (1869-1955), an Armenian oil magnate and philanthropist.  It is also used to refer to institutions and foundations established by him, most notably the Calouste Gulbenkian Foundation. |The Gulbenkian Museum houses an impressive collection of art and artifacts. 古尔班基安博物馆收藏了大量的艺术品和文物。|近义词: (depending on context) foundation, institute, trust |反义词: None
