mediated:/ˈmiːdieɪtɪd/| adj. v.| 调解的；由…促成的；通过…传递的 |  To bring about (a result) through the agency of something or someone; to be transmitted or experienced through a medium.  |例句：The conflict was mediated by a neutral third party.  冲突由一个中立的第三方调解。

The agreement was mediated through intense negotiations.  协议是通过紧张的谈判达成的。|近义词：facilitated, arbitrated, channeled |反义词：direct, unmediated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mediates disputes between neighbors. = 他调解邻居之间的纠纷。
The news is mediated through various channels. = 新闻通过各种渠道传播。


一般过去时:
She mediated the argument between her friends. = 她调解了朋友之间的争吵。
The message was mediated by a messenger. = 信息是由信使传达的。


现在完成时:
They have mediated several conflicts this year. = 他们今年已经调解了几次冲突。
The information has been mediated through social media. = 信息已通过社交媒体传播。


过去完成时:
He had mediated countless disputes before his retirement. = 他退休前调解过无数的纠纷。
The news had been mediated by a biased source. = 新闻是由有偏见的来源传达的。


将来时:
A neutral party will mediate the upcoming negotiations. = 一个中立方将调解即将进行的谈判。
The information will be mediated through the internet. = 信息将通过互联网传播。


虚拟语气:
If only someone would mediate the conflict, peace might be achieved. = 如果有人调解冲突，也许就能实现和平。
If he had mediated earlier, the situation wouldn't have escalated. = 如果他早点调解，情况就不会恶化了。


被动语态:
The dispute was mediated successfully. = 纠纷已成功调解。
The signal is mediated by the nervous system. = 信号由神经系统传递。


疑问句:
Was the agreement mediated fairly? = 协议是否公平地达成？
How is the information mediated to the public? = 信息如何向公众传播？


否定句:
He did not mediate the conflict; he simply observed. = 他没有调解冲突，他只是旁观。
The message was not mediated directly; it was relayed through several people. = 信息不是直接传达的；它是通过几个人转述的。
