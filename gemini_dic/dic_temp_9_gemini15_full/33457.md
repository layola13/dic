chute:/ʃuːt/| n.|滑槽；滑道；降落伞；倾泻；急流|A chute is a sloping channel or trough, typically made of wood or metal, down which things can slide.  It can also refer to a parachute or a rapid flow of water.|例句：The children enjoyed sliding down the water chute.孩子们喜欢从滑水道上滑下来。|The emergency chute deployed successfully.紧急滑槽成功展开。|近义词：slide, trough, channel, flume|反义词:lift, elevator
