fisherwoman:/ˈfɪʃərˌwʊmən/| n.|女渔民|A woman who fishes for a living or as a hobby.|例句：The fisherwoman skillfully cast her net into the sea. 那位女渔民熟练地将渔网撒入大海。|例句：She's a fisherwoman who has been fishing for over 20 years. 她是一位拥有超过20年捕鱼经验的女渔民。|近义词：female fisher, fishwife (有时带有贬义)|反义词：fisherman


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fisherwoman fishes every day. = 女渔民每天都捕鱼。
She catches a lot of fish. = 她捕了很多鱼。


一般过去时:
The fisherwoman fished all day yesterday. = 女渔民昨天捕鱼捕了一整天。
She caught a big fish yesterday. = 她昨天钓到了一条大鱼。


现在完成时:
The fisherwoman has fished in this lake many times. = 这名女渔民在这个湖里捕鱼很多次了。
She has caught many different kinds of fish. = 她已经钓到过许多不同种类的鱼。


过去完成时:
