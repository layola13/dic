noncultivability:/ˌnɒnkʌltɪˈveɪbɪlɪti/|/ˌnɑːnkʌltɪˈveɪbɪlɪti/| n. |不可培养性，难培养性 |The inability of a microorganism to grow in standard laboratory media.  This can be due to various factors, including stringent nutritional requirements, the need for specific growth factors, or the presence of inhibitors in the media.  |Example: The noncultivability of many environmental bacteria hinders our understanding of their ecological roles.  许多环境细菌的不可培养性阻碍了我们对其生态作用的理解。|近义词：uncultivability, unculturability |反义词：cultivability, culturability
