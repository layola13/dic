overzealousness:/ˌoʊvərˈzɛləsnis/| n. |过分热心；过度热忱|Excessive enthusiasm or diligence;  an excessive eagerness to do something.  It often implies that the enthusiasm is misplaced or leads to negative consequences.|The overzealousness of the new employee led to several costly mistakes.  新员工的过度热心导致了几次代价高昂的错误。|近义词：excessive enthusiasm, overeagerness, fanaticism|反义词：apathy, indifference, nonchalance
