outoffices:/ˈaʊt əf ˈɒfɪsɪz/| n.|不在办公室；外出办公|This term refers to a situation where someone is not in their usual workplace, either temporarily or permanently. It can also refer to the temporary closure of an office.  |例句：He's out of offices until Friday. 他到周五才回办公室。  |例句：The office is out of offices for renovations.|近义词：away from the office, unavailable, closed|反义词：in the office, available, open|名词
