currycomb:/ˈkʌriˌkoʊm/| n. |马梳；刷子|A currycomb is a grooming tool used to clean and brush the hair of horses and other animals. It typically has a surface of many small, closely set teeth or loops of metal or rubber.|例句：I use a currycomb to remove dirt and loose hair from my horse's coat. = 我用马梳去除我马身上的泥土和松散的毛发。|例句：The groom used a currycomb and brush to prepare the horse for the race. =  groom 用马梳和刷子为赛马做准备。|近义词：grooming brush, mane comb |反义词:None.
