entheasm:/ˈɛnθuːziæzəm/| n. |热情，热忱| Enthusiasm is a strong feeling of excitement and interest in something.  It's a positive and passionate feeling that motivates someone to pursue a goal or engage in an activity. |Example:  Her enthusiasm for the project was infectious. 她对这个项目的热情极具感染力。


Example: The team's enthusiasm carried them through the difficult final stages of the competition.  团队的热情帮助他们度过了比赛艰难的最后阶段。|近义词：passion, zeal, fervor, ardor |反义词：apathy, indifference, lassitude


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shows great enthusiasm for his work. 他对他的工作表现出极大的热情。
Her enthusiasm inspires everyone around her.  她的热情激励着周围的每一个人。

一般过去时:
They displayed considerable enthusiasm at the meeting. 他们在会议上表现出相当的热情。
His enthusiasm waned after several setbacks.  在几次挫折后，他的热情减退了。

现在完成时:
She has shown unwavering enthusiasm throughout the project.  她在整个项目中都表现出坚定不移的热情。
We have maintained our enthusiasm despite the challenges.  尽管面临挑战，我们仍然保持着热情。

过去完成时:
They had lost their initial enthusiasm by the time the project ended.  到项目结束时，他们已经失去了最初的热情。
I had never seen such enthusiasm in a group before. 我以前从未见过一个群体如此热情。

将来时:
He will bring his usual enthusiasm to the task. 他将一如既往地热情地投入到这项工作中。
We expect to see increased enthusiasm next year. 我们预计明年会看到更多的热情。

虚拟语气:
If he had more enthusiasm, he would finish the project sooner. 如果他更有热情，他就会更快地完成项目。
I wish I had his enthusiasm for learning. 我真希望我有他学习的热情。

被动语态:
His enthusiasm was praised by his colleagues. 他的热情受到了同事们的赞扬。
The project benefited from the enthusiasm shown by the team.  该项目受益于团队所表现出的热情。

疑问句:
Does she have the enthusiasm to take on this challenge? 她有热情来应对这个挑战吗？
What is the source of their enthusiasm? 他们热情的来源是什么？


否定句:
He showed no enthusiasm for the idea. 他对这个主意毫无热情。
Their lack of enthusiasm was disheartening. 他们缺乏热情令人沮丧。
