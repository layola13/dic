separates:/ˈsɛpəreɪts/| vt. |分离，分开；隔开；区分|To divide or cause to divide into parts.  It can also refer to the act of making a distinction or difference between things.|例句：The teacher separates the students into groups. 老师把学生分成小组。

The fence separates the garden from the street.  栅栏把花园和街道隔开。|近义词：divides, distinguishes, isolates|反义词：unites, combines, joins|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He separates the eggs. = 他把鸡蛋分开。
The river separates the two towns. = 这条河隔开了这两个城镇。


一般过去时:
She separated the laundry into whites and colors. = 她把衣服分成白色和彩色两类。
They separated after a long argument. = 他们经过长时间的争吵后分开了。


现在完成时:
He has separated the wheat from the chaff. = 他已经把麦子和糠分开了。
They have separated for good. = 他们已经彻底分开了。


过去完成时:
They had separated before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他们就已经分开了。
She had separated the ingredients before she started baking. = 在开始烘焙之前，她已经把配料分开了。


将来时:
I will separate the recyclable materials from the trash. = 我将把可回收材料从垃圾中分离出来。
They will separate the twins into different classrooms. = 他们将把双胞胎分到不同的教室。


虚拟语气:
If I separated the tasks, I could finish them faster. = 如果我把任务分开，我就能更快地完成它们。
I wish I had separated my finances from my business partner's. = 我真希望我当时把我的财务和我的商业伙伴的财务分开。


被动语态:
The students are separated into different ability groups. = 学生们被分成不同的能力组。
The garbage will be separated at the recycling center. = 垃圾将在回收中心进行分类。


疑问句:
Does this machine separate the solids from the liquids? = 这台机器能把固体和液体分开吗？
How do you separate fact from fiction in this case? = 在这种情况下，你如何区分事实和虚构？


否定句:
I didn't separate the eggs before cooking. = 我做饭前没有把鸡蛋分开。
They didn't separate their finances. = 他们没有分开他们的财务。
