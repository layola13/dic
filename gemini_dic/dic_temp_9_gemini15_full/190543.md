satire:/ˈsætaɪər/| n.|讽刺；讽刺作品|The use of humor, irony, exaggeration, or ridicule to expose and criticize people's stupidity or vices, particularly in the context of contemporary politics and other topical issues.|例句：The novel is a satire of modern society. 这部小说是对现代社会的讽刺。|近义词：irony, mockery, lampoon|反义词：praise, flattery
