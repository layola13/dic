reacknowledging: /ˌriːækˈnɒlɪdʒɪŋ/ | v. | 再次承认；重新确认 |  To acknowledge again; to reaffirm.  It implies a previous acknowledgement that is being reiterated or emphasized. |


例句：

The company is reacknowledging its commitment to environmental sustainability after recent criticism. = 在最近受到批评后，该公司正在再次确认其对环境可持续性的承诺。

Reacknowledging the debt, the government announced new austerity measures. = 政府再次承认债务问题，并宣布了新的紧缩措施。


近义词：reasserting, reaffirming, reiterating, confirming


反义词：denying, rejecting, disavowing


词性：动词

USAGE->
