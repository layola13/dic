obstetricians:/ˌɒbstɛˈtrɪʃənz/ | n. | 妇产科医生 | A medical doctor specializing in the care of women during pregnancy, childbirth, and the postpartum period.  They provide prenatal care, manage labor and delivery, and provide postnatal care for both mother and baby. | Example: The obstetricians at the hospital are highly skilled and experienced.  这家医院的妇产科医生技术精湛，经验丰富。  | 近义词：gynecologist (if also specializing in women's health), perinatologist (if specializing in high-risk pregnancies) | 反义词：N/A

词源:
