sandy:/ˈsændi/ | adj. | 沙质的，沙色的，沙滩的 | Containing or resembling sand; of the color of sand.  |例句：The beach was sandy and the water was clear.  沙滩是沙质的，水很清澈。|例句：She wore a sandy-colored dress. 她穿着一件沙色的连衣裙。|近义词：gritty, sandy-colored, gravelly | 反义词：rocky, smooth


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The beach is sandy. = 海滩是沙质的。
The desert is sandy and hot. = 沙漠又沙又热。

一般过去时:
The beach was sandy yesterday. = 昨天海滩是沙质的。
The sand was very sandy after the storm. = 暴风雨过后沙子非常沙质。

The beach has been sandy for centuries. = 这片海滩几百年来都是沙质的。

将来时:
The beach will remain sandy. = 海滩将保持沙质。

虚拟语气:
If the beach were sandy, we would go there. = 如果海滩是沙质的，我们会去那里。

疑问句:

否定句:
