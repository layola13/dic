rewiring:/ˌriˈwaɪərɪŋ/| n. |重新布线；彻底改变；改组 |The process of replacing or changing the wiring in a building or system.  It can also refer to a significant change or reorganization of something. |例句：The house needs rewiring. 这房子需要重新布线。

例句：The company underwent a complete rewiring of its management structure. 这家公司对其管理结构进行了彻底的改组。


近义词：reorganization, restructuring, overhaul, revamp |反义词：maintenance, preservation


词性：名词
