hyperproductions:/ˌhaɪpərpɹəˈdʌkʃənz/| n. |超额生产；过度生产；大量生产|The excessive creation or output of something, often beyond what is needed or demanded.  It often implies inefficiency or waste.|例句：The factory's hyperproductions led to a surplus of inventory and lower profits.  这家工厂的超额生产导致库存过剩和利润下降。|例句：The hyperproductions of cheap plastic toys are contributing to environmental pollution.廉价塑料玩具的过度生产正在加剧环境污染。|近义词：overproduction, mass production, excessive production|反义词：underproduction, scarcity, shortage|名词
