officered:/ˈɒfɪsərd/ | adj. |  军官的；有军官的 |  Provided with officers; having officers.  This word describes something related to or equipped with officers, typically military officers.  | Example:  The officered ship sailed smoothly. 这艘配备军官的船航行顺利。  |近义词： officered, manned (in the context of a ship or vessel) | 反义词：unofficered, unmanned
