counterraids:/ˈkaʊntərˌreɪdz/| n.|反击，报复性袭击|A counterraid is a military attack made in response to an earlier attack. It's a retaliatory action aimed at harming the original attacker.|例句：The enemy launched a counterraid after our initial assault. 敌人在我军最初的攻击后发起了反击。|例句：The police conducted a counterraid to apprehend the escaped criminals.警方进行了反击行动以逮捕逃犯。|近义词：retaliation, reprisal, counterattack|反义词：surrender, retreat
