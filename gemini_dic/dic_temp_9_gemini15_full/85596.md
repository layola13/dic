hatter:/ˈhætər/| n.|帽商，疯帽匠|A hatter is a person who makes or sells hats.  The term is also associated with the Mad Hatter from Lewis Carroll's "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland".|例句：The old hatter had been crafting hats for over fifty years.  这位老帽匠已经制作帽子超过五十年了。

例句：The Mad Hatter's tea party was a chaotic affair.  疯帽匠的茶会是一场混乱的事件。


近义词：milliner (for women's hats), hat maker


反义词：无


词性：名词
