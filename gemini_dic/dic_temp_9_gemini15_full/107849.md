lardery:/ˈlɑːdəri/| n. |食品储藏室；食品室|A room or place for storing food, typically in a large house or estate.|例句：The larder was well-stocked with fresh produce. 食品储藏室里储备了充足的新鲜农产品。
例句：She replenished the larder with groceries. 她用杂货补充了食品储藏室。|近义词：pantry, storeroom |反义词:none
