faxable: /ˈfæksəbl/ | adj. | 可传真的 | Capable of being sent by fax.  Something that is faxable can be transmitted via a facsimile machine.  This typically refers to documents or images that are suitable for transmission in this manner. |


例句：

This document is faxable.  = 这份文件可以传真。

The image resolution is high enough to make it faxable. = 图片分辨率足够高，可以传真。


近义词：transmittable (by fax), sendable (by fax)


反义词：unfaxable


词性：形容词
