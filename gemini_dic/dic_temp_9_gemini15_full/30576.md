ceratosaurus:/ˌsɛrəˈtɔːsɔːrəs/| n. |角鼻龙|A genus of large theropod dinosaur that lived in the Late Jurassic period, characterized by a prominent horn on its nose and two smaller horns above its eyes.|例句：Ceratosaurus was a fearsome predator of its time. 角鼻龙是当时可怕的掠食者。|例句：The fossil remains of Ceratosaurus have been found in North America and Africa. 角鼻龙的化石遗骸在北美和非洲被发现。|近义词：Allosaurus, Megalosaurus|反义词：无
