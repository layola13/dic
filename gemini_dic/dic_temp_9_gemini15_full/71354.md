flacker:/ˈflækər/| v.|闪烁，摇曳，飘动|To shine unsteadily; flicker.  It often describes a light source that is weak or intermittent.  The word can also imply a wavering or unsteady movement.|例句：The candle flackered in the breeze.  蜡烛在微风中闪烁。

The dying embers flackered before going out completely.  将熄的余烬闪烁了一下然后完全熄灭。|近义词：flicker, shimmer, glimmer, waver|反义词：steadily shine, burn brightly|动词
