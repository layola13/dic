lathes:/leɪðz/| n.|车床|A lathe is a machine tool that rotates a workpiece about an axis of rotation to perform various machining operations such as cutting, sanding, knurling, drilling, or deformation, facing, turning, thread cutting etc.  Lathes are used to create cylindrical or conical shapes, as well as more complex parts.|The machinist carefully adjusted the lathe to create the precise dimensions required.|近义词：machine tool, turning machine|反义词：None.
