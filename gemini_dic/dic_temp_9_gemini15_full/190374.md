sarge:/sɑːrdʒ/| n.|中士，警官|A sergeant is a non-commissioned officer of relatively high rank in the army, marines, or air force.  The term is also sometimes used informally to address a male sergeant.|例句：The sarge yelled at us for being late.  中士因为我们迟到而对我们大吼。

例句：He saluted the sarge and then quickly fell in line. 他向中士敬礼，然后迅速排好队。


近义词：corporal, lieutenant (in some contexts)
反义词：private (in terms of rank)

词性: 名词
