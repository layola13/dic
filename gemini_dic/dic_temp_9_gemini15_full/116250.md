mass-producers:/ˈmæs prəˈduːsərz/| n.|大规模生产商，批量生产者|Those who manufacture goods on a large scale, often using automated systems.  They prioritize efficiency and cost-effectiveness over individual craftsmanship.|例句：Many mass-producers rely on economies of scale to remain competitive.  许多大规模生产商依靠规模经济来保持竞争力。|例句：The rise of mass-producers led to a significant decrease in the price of many consumer goods. 大规模生产商的兴起导致许多消费品的價格大幅下降。|近义词：manufacturers, large-scale producers, bulk producers|反义词：artisans, craftspeople, small-scale producers|名词
