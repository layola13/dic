moseys:/ˈmoʊziːz/| v.|闲逛，漫步|To walk at a slow, leisurely pace.|He moseyed down the street, enjoying the warm sunshine. 他沿着街道慢悠悠地走着，享受着温暖的阳光。|近义词：stroll, amble, saunter|反义词：hurry, rush


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He moseys down the street every afternoon. = 他每天下午都在街上闲逛。
She moseys through the park, enjoying the flowers. = 她漫步在公园里，欣赏着花朵。


一般过去时:
They moseyed into the bar and ordered drinks. = 他们慢悠悠地走进酒吧，点了些酒。
He moseyed home after a long day at work. = 他在工作了一整天后，慢悠悠地回家了。


现在完成时:
We've moseyed all over town today. = 我们今天在城里到处闲逛。
She has moseyed along the beach for hours. = 她沿着海滩漫步了好几个小时。


过去完成时:
They had moseyed through the market before lunch. = 他们在午餐前在市场上闲逛。
He had moseyed into the garden before he saw her. = 他在看到她之前已经漫步到花园里了。


将来时:
I will mosey into town later this afternoon. = 我今天下午晚些时候会去镇上闲逛。
We will mosey down to the beach tomorrow. = 我们明天会到海滩上闲逛。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would mosey through the museum. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会在博物馆里慢慢逛逛。
I wish I could mosey around the countryside this weekend. = 我希望这个周末我能去乡下走走。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The path was often moseyed along by tourists. = 游客经常沿着这条路闲逛。(不太自然)
The park is often moseyed through by locals. = 当地人经常在公园里闲逛。(不太自然)


疑问句:
Will you mosey along with me? = 你愿意和我一起闲逛吗？
Does he often mosey through the woods? = 他经常在树林里闲逛吗？


否定句:
I didn't mosey around; I ran all the way. = 我没有闲逛；我一路跑过来的。
They didn't mosey home; they took a taxi. = 他们没有闲逛回家；他们打的回去了。

mosey around = 闲逛
mosey down = 沿着...走
