inclusive: /ɪnˈkluːsɪv/ | adj. | 包容的；包含的；包括的 |  Including all the things or people that are mentioned or involved.  |例句：The price is inclusive of tax.  价格包含税款。|例句：Our school is inclusive and welcomes students of all backgrounds. 我们的学校具有包容性，欢迎各种背景的学生。|近义词：comprehensive, encompassing, broad |反义词：exclusive, restrictive, limited


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price is inclusive of VAT. = 价格包含增值税。
This tour is inclusive of all meals. = 此行程包含所有餐点。

一般过去时:
The package was inclusive of flights and accommodation. = 该套餐包含机票和住宿。
The meeting was inclusive of all stakeholders. = 会议包括所有利益相关者。


现在完成时:
The company has been inclusive of diverse employees for years. = 多年来，该公司一直包容多元化的员工。
The policy has been inclusive of all citizens. = 该政策包含所有公民。


过去完成时:
The agreement had been inclusive of all necessary clauses. = 该协议已包含所有必要的条款。
The previous version had been inclusive of all the features. = 上一个版本包含所有功能。


将来时:
The new program will be inclusive of all age groups. = 新计划将包括所有年龄段的人。
The next meeting will be inclusive of all team members. = 下一次会议将包括所有团队成员。


虚拟语气:
If the plan were inclusive of everyone, it would be more successful. = 如果计划包含所有人，它会更成功。
I wish the policy were more inclusive of marginalized groups. = 我希望这项政策能更包容边缘群体。


被动语态:
All expenses are included in the inclusive price. = 所有费用都包含在全包价中。
The students were included in the inclusive program. = 学生们被纳入包容性计划。


疑问句:
Is the price inclusive of delivery? = 价格包含送货吗？
Is the event inclusive of children? = 活动包含儿童吗？


否定句:
The original plan wasn't inclusive enough. = 原计划不够包容。
The policy is not inclusive of undocumented immigrants. = 该政策不包括无证移民。
