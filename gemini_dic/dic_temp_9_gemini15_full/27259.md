calorimetry:/ˌkæləˈrɪmɪtri/| n. |量热法，热量测定|The science or process of measuring the amount of heat involved in a chemical or physical change.  It involves measuring the heat transfer between a system and its surroundings.|Example:  Calorimetry was used to determine the heat of combustion of the fuel. 量热法被用来测定燃料的燃烧热。|近义词：thermometry, heat measurement|反义词：N/A
