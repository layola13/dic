/ˌriːˈtiːtʃɪŋ/ | v. | 再教；重新教授 | The act of teaching something again, often to improve understanding or address misconceptions.  It involves revisiting previously taught material. | Example: Reteaching the concept of fractions is necessary for some students.  重新教授分数的概念对一些学生来说是必要的。The teacher decided reteaching the lesson would be beneficial.老师决定重新教授这节课是有益的。 | 近义词：re-instruction, review, repetition | 反义词：initial teaching, first teaching | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reteach the difficult concepts. = 他们重新教授那些困难的概念。
The school regularly re-teaches core subjects. = 学校定期重新教授核心科目。


一般过去时:
The teacher retaught the lesson after the poor test scores. = 考试成绩差后，老师重新教授了这节课。
She retaught the material using different methods. = 她用不同的方法重新教授了这些材料。


现在完成时:
They have retaught that section of the textbook three times. = 他们已经三次重新教授了教科书的那一部分。
The instructor has retaught the lesson to several students.  = 讲师已经向几位学生重新教授了这节课。


过去完成时:
After the students still didn't understand, the teacher had retaught the chapter twice. = 在学生们仍然不明白之后，老师已经重新教授了该章节两次。
He had retaught the complex equations before the final exam. = 在期末考试前，他已经重新教授了那些复杂的方程式。


将来时:
The professor will reteach the material next week. = 教授下周将重新教授这些材料。
We will reteach the problematic parts of the lesson. = 我们将重新教授课程中存在问题的部分。


虚拟语气:
If the students failed the test again, the teacher would reteach the material. = 如果学生们再次考试不及格，老师就会重新教授这些材料。
It would be helpful if the teacher retaught the confusing aspects of the lesson. = 如果老师能重新教授这节课中令人困惑的部分，那将很有帮助。


被动语态:
The lesson was retaught using a different approach. = 这节课是用不同的方法重新教授的。
The difficult concepts are being retaught in small groups. = 那些困难的概念正在小组中重新教授。


疑问句:
Will the material be retaught? = 这些材料会重新教授吗？
Should we reteach the lesson to the students who failed the quiz? = 我们是否应该重新教授给那些小测不及格的学生？


否定句:
They did not reteach the lesson. = 他们没有重新教授这节课。
The teacher didn't feel it was necessary to reteach the topic. = 老师觉得没有必要重新教授这个话题。
