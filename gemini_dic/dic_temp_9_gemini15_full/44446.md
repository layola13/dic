cross-sections:/ˈkrɒs ˈsɛkʃənz/| n. |横截面；剖面图|A cross-section is a view of an object or structure as it would appear if it were cut through at a particular plane.  It's often used to show the internal structure or composition of something.|例句：The geologist examined the cross-sections of the rock formations. 地质学家检查了岩层的剖面图。|例句：The diagram shows a cross-section of the engine.  该图显示了发动机的横截面。|近义词：profile, section, diagram|反义词：N/A
