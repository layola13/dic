militarys:/ˈmɪlɪtɛriz/| n.|军事；军队；军事行动|The term "militarys" is the plural form of "military," referring to armed forces or the affairs of the armed forces. It can also refer to a group of military personnel or a system of military organization.  It is less commonly used than "military" in singular form.|例句：The militarys of the world are constantly evolving. 全球军队都在不断发展。|例句：Several militarys participated in the peacekeeping mission.  几个国家的军队参与了维和行动。|近义词：armed forces, armies, navies, air forces|反义词：civilian life, peace
