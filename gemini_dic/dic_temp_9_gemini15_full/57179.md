dowitcher:/ˈdaʊɪtʃər/| n.|丘鹬|A dowitcher is a shorebird of the genus Limnodromus, characterized by a long, straight bill and probing behavior in mudflats to find food.  They are known for their distinctive calls and migratory habits.|例句：The dowitcher probed the mud with its long bill, searching for worms.  这只丘鹬用它长长的喙探入泥中寻找蠕虫。

The dowitcher was spotted near the coast during its migration. 在迁徙期间，人们在海岸附近发现了这只丘鹬。|近义词：sandpiper, snipe|反义词:|
