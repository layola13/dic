captaining:/ˈkæptɪnɪŋ/| v.|担任队长；指挥；掌舵|The act of leading or commanding a team, ship, or aircraft.  It implies responsibility, leadership, and skill. |例句：He is captaining the football team this year. 他今年担任足球队队长。

例句：She skillfully captained the ship through the storm. 她熟练地驾驶着船只度过了风暴。


近义词：leading, commanding, directing, skippering


反义词：following, obeying


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is captaining the team. = 他正在担任队长。
She captains the ship. = 她掌舵。


一般过去时:
He captained the team last year. = 他去年担任队长。
She captained the ship during the voyage. = 在航行期间她掌舵。


现在完成时:
He has captained the team for five years. = 他担任队长五年了。
She has captained many ships. = 她掌舵过许多船只。


过去完成时:
He had captained the team before his injury. = 他受伤前担任过队长。
She had captained several vessels before this one. = 在这艘船之前她掌舵过几艘船。


将来时:
He will captain the team next season. = 下个赛季他将担任队长。
She will captain the new flagship. = 她将掌舵新的旗舰。


虚拟语气:
If he captained the team, they might win. = 如果他担任队长，他们可能会赢。
If she had captained the ship differently, the accident might have been avoided. = 如果她以不同的方式掌舵，事故可能就会避免。


被动语态:
The team was captained by a skilled leader. = 这支队伍由一位经验丰富的领导者担任队长。
The ship was skillfully captained through the difficult passage. = 这艘船被熟练地驾驶通过了艰难的水道。


疑问句:
Who is captaining the team? = 谁是队长？
Who captained the ship? = 谁掌舵的？


否定句:
He is not captaining the team this year. = 他今年不担任队长。
She did not captain the ship on that voyage. = 那次航行她没有掌舵。


PHRASE->
captaining a team = 担任一个队的队长
captaining a vessel = 掌舵一艘船
captaining a project = 领导一个项目
captaining the effort = 领导这项工作
captaining a flight = 驾驶航班


词源:
源自captain（队长，船长）+ -ing（动名词后缀）。 captain 一词源自拉丁语 caput ("head")，表示领导或指挥。  -ing 后缀表示动作或状态。
