bustard:/ˈbʌstɑːrd/| n. |鸨，(尤指)大鸨|A bustard is a large, long-legged bird belonging to the family Otididae.  They are known for their impressive size,  elaborate mating displays, and terrestrial habits.  Many species are threatened or endangered due to habitat loss and hunting. |The great bustard is the heaviest flying bird in Europe.  欧洲最大的飞鸟是大鸨。|近义词：None directly equivalent.  Other large birds could be considered depending on context (e.g., crane, ostrich).|反义词：None directly opposite.
