quiche:/kiːʃ/| n.|基什(一种法国蛋奶馅饼)|A quiche is a savory custard tart with a pastry crust. It typically contains eggs, cream, cheese, and various other ingredients such as meat, vegetables, or seafood.  |例句：I made a delicious quiche Lorraine for dinner. 我做了美味的洛林基什当晚餐。|例句：The restaurant is famous for its vegetarian quiche. 这家餐厅以其素食基什而闻名。|近义词：tart, flan, pie|反义词：无
