ecotok:/ˈɛkoʊtɒk/| n.|生态毒素|Ecotoxin refers to any toxic substance produced by or resulting from the activities of living organisms or ecological processes.  It encompasses a wide range of pollutants, from naturally occurring toxins to synthetic chemicals released into the environment. These substances can harm or kill living organisms, disrupt ecosystems, and pose risks to human health.|例句：Pesticides can be a significant source of ecotoxins in agricultural areas.  农药可能是农业地区生态毒素的重要来源。|近义词：environmental toxin, pollutant, contaminant|反义词：N/A|名词
