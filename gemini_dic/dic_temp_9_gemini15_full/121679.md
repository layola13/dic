misplaying:/ˌmɪsˈpleɪɪŋ/ | v. | 玩错；演奏失误；误解；曲解 | To play incorrectly or badly; to misunderstand or misinterpret something. |例句：He was misplaying the chords on the guitar. 他吉他琴弦按错了。

例句：The team's misplaying of the strategy led to their defeat. 球队对策略的误读导致了他们的失败。

近义词：misinterpreting, mishandling, botching, bungling | 反义词：mastering, excelling, interpreting correctly
