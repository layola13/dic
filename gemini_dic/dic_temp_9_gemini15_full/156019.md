photology:/foʊˈtɑːlədʒi/| n. |摄影学，光学研究|The study of light and its effects, especially in relation to photography.  It encompasses the physical properties of light, the chemical processes involved in photography, and the artistic aspects of image creation.| Example:  He had a deep understanding of photology, which allowed him to master the art of black and white photography. 他对摄影学有深入的了解，这使他掌握了黑白摄影的艺术。|近义词：photography, optics|反义词：None.
