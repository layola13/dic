entrepreneurial:/ˌɒntrəprəˈnɜːriəl/ /ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːriəl/ | adj. |创业的；具有企业家精神的 | Relating to entrepreneurs or entrepreneurship; characterized by enterprise and initiative.  |例句：He showed great entrepreneurial spirit in starting his own business. 他在创业方面展现了极大的企业家精神。

例句：The government is encouraging entrepreneurial activity among young people.  政府正在鼓励年轻人进行创业活动。


近义词：enterprising, innovative, businesslike, go-getting

反义词：unenterprising, passive, lethargic


USAGE->

He shows entrepreneurial skills. = 他展现出创业技能。
She possesses entrepreneurial spirit. = 她拥有创业精神。

过去完成时:
