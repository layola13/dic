pathless:/ˈpæθləs/| adj.|没有路的；人迹罕至的；|Without a path or track; trackless.  The adjective describes a place or area that lacks paths or trails, making it difficult or impossible to traverse easily. It often implies a sense of remoteness, wilderness, or untamed nature. |例句：The explorers ventured into the pathless wilderness. 探险家们冒险进入人迹罕至的荒野。

例句：The hikers got lost in the pathless mountains.  徒步旅行者迷失在没有路的群山中。


近义词：trackless, untracked, uncharted, inaccessible


反义词：accessible, well-trodden, paved


词性:形容词
