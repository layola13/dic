ments:/ments/| n.|评论；意见；想法；备注| comments refer to expressions of opinions or observations.  They can be written or spoken. |例句：The comments section on the article was filled with angry readers. 文章的评论区充满了愤怒的读者。

例句：He made some insightful comments about the current economic situation. 他对当前的经济形势发表了一些有见地的评论。


近义词：remarks, observations, opinions, notes|反义词：silence, inaction


词源:源自于动词"comment"的复数形式，"comment"源自于拉丁语"commentari"，意思是"写注释"。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What comments do you have on this proposal? = 你对这个提案有什么意见？
Their comments are always insightful. = 他们的评论总是很有见地。


一般过去时:
What comments did he make at the meeting? = 他在会议上说了些什么？
Her comments were quite critical. = 她的评论相当尖锐。


现在完成时:
What comments have you received so far? = 到目前为止你收到了什么评论？
They have made several helpful comments on the design. = 他们对设计提出了一些有益的建议。


过去完成时:
What comments had she made before the presentation? = 演示之前她做了什么评论？
He had already made his comments before the discussion began. = 讨论开始之前他已经发表了自己的意见了。


将来时:
What comments will you give tomorrow? = 你明天会发表什么评论？
We will carefully consider all comments received. = 我们将仔细考虑所有收到的评论。


虚拟语气:
If I had made better comments, the results might have been different. = 如果我做了更好的评论，结果可能会有所不同。
I wish I had made more thoughtful comments. = 我希望我当时能发表更周到的评论。


被动语态:
His comments were widely praised. = 他的评论受到了广泛赞扬。
All comments will be reviewed by the team. = 所有评论都将由团队审核。


疑问句:
What are your comments on the matter? = 你对这件事有什么看法？
Are there any further comments? = 还有其他评论吗？


否定句:
I have no comments on that. = 我对此没有评论。
She made no comments about the incident. = 她对这起事件没有发表任何评论。


PHRASE->
make comments = 发表评论
add comments = 添加评论
offer comments = 提供评论
receive comments = 收到评论
negative comments = 负面评论
