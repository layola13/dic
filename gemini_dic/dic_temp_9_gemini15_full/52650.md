digitalizes:/ˈdɪdʒɪtəlaɪzɪz/ | vt. |数字化；使…电子化 | To convert information into a digital format.  This often involves scanning documents, images, or audio and video into a computer system. | 例句：The company is digitalizing its entire archive. 公司正在将其整个档案数字化。 | 例句： They are digitalizing old film reels to preserve them. 他们正在将旧胶片数字化以保存它们。 | 近义词：computerize, digitize | 反义词：dematerialize
