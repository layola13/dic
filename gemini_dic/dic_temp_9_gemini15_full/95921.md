incrimination:/ˌɪnkrɪmɪˈneɪʃən/| n. | 罪名指控；牵连；归罪 | The act of accusing someone of a crime or wrongdoing; the state of being accused.  It can also refer to evidence that implicates someone in a crime. | Example: The witness's testimony led to the incrimination of the suspect.  证人的证词导致了对嫌疑人的指控。

The incrimination of the CEO was a major blow to the company's reputation.  对首席执行官的指控严重损害了公司的声誉。 | accusation, indictment, imputation | exoneration, acquittal | noun
