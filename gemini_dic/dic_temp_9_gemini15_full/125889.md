muscovy:/ˈmʌskovi/| n.|莫斯科；麝鸭；|Muscovy refers to things relating to Moscow, Russia, or to the Muscovy duck.  The word's origin lies in the historical Muscovy, the former name for the Grand Duchy of Moscow, which eventually became Russia.| 例句：The Muscovy duck is known for its distinctive appearance. 麝鸭以其独特的长相而闻名。  The ambassador travelled to Muscovy for diplomatic talks. 大使前往莫斯科进行外交谈判。|近义词：Russia (in reference to Moscow),  Barbary duck (for the duck)|反义词：无
