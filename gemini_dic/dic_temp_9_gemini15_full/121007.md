miscoding:/ˈmɪsˌkoʊdɪŋ/ | n. | 错码，编码错误 | The process or result of incorrectly encoding information, such as DNA or computer data.  This can lead to errors in translation or execution. | Example:  Miscoding of the DNA sequence resulted in a non-functional protein. DNA序列的错码导致蛋白质功能异常。 |近义词：error, mistake, fault, malfunction |反义词：accurate coding, correct encoding | 名词
