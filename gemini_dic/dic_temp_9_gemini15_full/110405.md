likability:/ˌlaɪkəˈbɪləti/| n. |可爱，讨人喜欢，受欢迎程度 |The quality of being likable; the extent to which someone is liked.  It encompasses various traits that make someone appealing and enjoyable to be around.  |例句：Her likability is undeniable; everyone enjoys her company.她的讨人喜欢是毋庸置疑的；每个人都喜欢和她在一起。

例句：The candidate's high likability boosted his chances of winning the election.  候选人的高人气提高了他赢得选举的机会。


近义词：appeal, charm, attractiveness, popularity

反义词：unlikeability, repulsiveness, unpopularity
