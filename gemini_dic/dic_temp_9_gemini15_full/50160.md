denying:/dɪˈnaɪɪŋ/ | v. |否认；拒绝；不承认 | To state that something is not true; to refuse to accept or admit something. |例句：He denied all knowledge of the crime. 他否认自己知道这起犯罪。

He denied himself the pleasure of a holiday. 他拒绝给自己放假享乐。|近义词：refuting, contradicting, rejecting |反义词：affirming, admitting, confessing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He denies the accusations. 他否认这些指控。
They deny any involvement in the incident. 他们否认参与了这起事件。

一般过去时:
She denied seeing the suspect. 她否认见过嫌疑犯。
The company denied responsibility for the accident. 公司否认对事故负责。

现在完成时:
He has denied the allegations repeatedly. 他一再否认这些指控。
They have denied access to the documents. 他们一直拒绝访问这些文件。

过去完成时:
He had denied the truth until the evidence surfaced. 直到证据出现，他才否认事实。
They had denied any wrongdoing before the investigation began. 在调查开始之前，他们否认有任何不当行为。

将来时:
She will deny the charges in court. 她将在法庭上否认这些指控。
He will deny having said anything of the sort. 他将否认说过那样的话。

虚拟语气:
If he denied the truth, we would be in trouble. 如果他否认真相，我们将陷入困境。
I wish he denied the rumor before it spread so far. 我希望他在谣言传播这么远之前就否认它。

被动语态:
The accusations were denied by the suspect. 嫌疑人否认了这些指控。
Access to the information was denied to the public. 公众被拒绝访问这些信息。

疑问句:
Does he deny the allegations? 他是否否认这些指控？
Did she deny her involvement? 她是否否认参与其中？

否定句:
He doesn't deny that it was difficult. 他不否认这很难。
She didn't deny her feelings for him. 她没有否认她对他的感情。
