horsebacks:/ˈhɔːrsˌbæks/| n. |骑马；马背；马背上 |The act of riding a horse; the back of a horse.  |例句：They went for a ride on horseback. = 他们骑马去了。They rode horseback across the plains. = 他们骑马横越平原。|近义词：horsemanship, equestrianism |反义词：walking, driving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They ride horseback every weekend. = 他们每个周末都骑马。
He often goes horseback riding in the park. = 他经常在公园骑马。

一般过去时:
She rode horseback through the forest yesterday. = 她昨天骑马穿过森林。
We went horseback riding last summer. = 我们去年夏天去骑马了。

现在完成时:
I have never ridden horseback before. = 我以前从未骑过马。
They have been horseback riding for many years. = 他们骑马已经很多年了。

过去完成时:
He had ridden horseback many times before the competition. = 在比赛之前他已经骑过很多次马了。
She had never felt so free as she did when she rode horseback across the open fields. = 当她骑马穿过开阔的田野时，她从未感到如此自由。

将来时:
We will go horseback riding next week. = 我们下周要去骑马。
She will ride horseback in the upcoming competition. = 她将在即将到来的比赛中骑马。

虚拟语气:
If I had a horse, I would ride horseback every day. = 如果我有一匹马，我每天都会骑马。
I wish I could ride horseback like a professional. = 我希望我能像专业人士一样骑马。

被动语态:
The horses were ridden horseback by experienced riders. = 这些马是由经验丰富的骑手骑的。（此句略显不自然，被动语态在此场景应用较少）
Horseback riding lessons are offered at the stables. = 马厩提供骑马课程。

疑问句:
Do you enjoy riding horseback? = 你喜欢骑马吗？
Have you ever tried horseback riding? = 你曾经尝试过骑马吗？

否定句:
They don't ride horseback often. = 他们不经常骑马。
He didn't go horseback riding today. = 他今天没有去骑马。
