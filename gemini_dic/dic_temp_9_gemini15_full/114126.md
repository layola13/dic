mahabharatam: /ˌmɑːhɑːˈbɑːrətəm/ | n. | 印度史诗《摩诃婆罗多》 | The Mahabharatam is a long and complex epic poem from ancient India, narrating the story of a dynastic war between two branches of a royal family. It explores themes of dharma (righteousness), karma (action and consequence), and the human condition.  | Example: The Mahabharatam is considered one of the two major Sanskrit epics of ancient India.  《摩诃婆罗多》被认为是古代印度的两大梵文史诗之一。| 近义词: Ramayana (another major Indian epic) | 反义词: None (as it's a proper noun) | 名词
