It emphasizes the deliberate and self-inflicted nature of the starvation. | Example: The self-starved patient required immediate medical attention.  自我饥饿的病人需要立即就医。  The athlete's self-starved condition led to a decline in performance. 运动员自我饥饿的状态导致成绩下降。| 近义词: malnourished, emaciated, undernourished | 反义词: well-fed, nourished, plump | 形容词
