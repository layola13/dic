cost-effectively: /ˌkɒst ɪˈfektɪvli/ | /ˌkɑːst ɪˈfektɪvli/ | adv. |经济有效地，成本效益高地 | Achieving a desired outcome while minimizing costs.  This emphasizes both effectiveness and efficiency in resource allocation. |例句：The company implemented a new system to produce goods cost-effectively. 公司实施了一个新系统以经济有效地生产商品。

The new software allows us to manage projects more cost-effectively. 新软件使我们能够更经济有效地管理项目。


近义词：efficiently, economically, profitably, inexpensively |反义词：wastefully, expensively, inefficiently


词源:

* **cost:**  From Old English *cost, meaning "price, expense, tax."  Ultimately of Germanic origin.

* **effective:** From Middle English *effectif, from Old French *effectif, from Latin *effectivus, meaning "effective, efficient."

* **-ly:**  An adverbial suffix indicating manner.


USAGE->
(Since "cost-effectively" is an adverb, it doesn't have verb conjugations.  The following examples illustrate its usage in different sentence structures.)

They manage their resources cost-effectively. = 他们经济有效地管理资源。
We always strive to work cost-effectively. = 我们总是努力经济有效地工作。
