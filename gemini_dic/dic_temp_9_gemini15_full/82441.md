grumbled:/ˈɡrʌmbəld/ | v. |咕哝；抱怨；低语 | To mutter or complain in a low voice.  |例句：He grumbled about the long wait. 他抱怨等得太久。|例句：She grumbled something under her breath. 她低声咕哝着什么。|近义词：muttered, complained, grumbled, muttered, whined |反义词：praised, complimented


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grumbles about his job every day. = 他每天都抱怨他的工作。
She grumbles whenever she has to do chores. = 每当她不得不做家务时，她就会抱怨。

一般过去时:
He grumbled about the cold weather yesterday. = 他昨天抱怨天气冷。
She grumbled that the food was too salty. = 她抱怨食物太咸了。

现在完成时:
He has grumbled about his boss for months. = 他已经抱怨他的老板几个月了。
She has grumbled repeatedly about the noise. = 她反复抱怨噪音。

过去完成时:
He had grumbled about the project before the meeting. = 他在会议之前就抱怨过这个项目。
She had grumbled all day before finally speaking up. = 她抱怨了一整天，才最终说出来。

将来时:
He will grumble if the service is slow. = 如果服务慢，他会抱怨的。
She will grumble about the heat all summer. = 整个夏天她都会抱怨天气热。

虚拟语气:
I wish he wouldn't grumble so much. = 我希望他不要抱怨那么多。
If he were happier, he wouldn't grumble constantly. = 如果他更快乐，他就不会不停地抱怨。


被动语态:
His complaints were grumbled about by everyone. = 他的抱怨被每个人都抱怨了。(This usage is less common and sounds unnatural)
The unfair treatment was grumbled about by the workers. = 工人们抱怨不公平的待遇。 (This is a more natural example of the passive)


疑问句:
Why are you grumbling all the time? = 你为什么总是抱怨？
What are they grumbling about now? = 他们现在在抱怨什么？


否定句:
He didn't grumble about the work this time. = 这次他没有抱怨工作。
She didn't grumble, but her face showed her displeasure. = 她没有抱怨，但她的表情显示出她的不满。


PHRASE->
grumble about: 抱怨...
grumble to oneself: 自言自语地抱怨
grumble and groan: 抱怨和呻吟
grumble under one's breath: 小声抱怨
a grumbling stomach: 咕咕叫的肚子
