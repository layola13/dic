oolitic:/ˌoʊəˈlɪtɪk/| adj.|鲕状的，球粒状的|Relating to or consisting of oolites; composed of small, rounded grains (oolites).  The term describes a sedimentary rock texture characterized by small, spherical grains, typically formed by precipitation of calcium carbonate around a nucleus in shallow, agitated waters.|例句：The limestone sample showed a distinct oolitic texture.|近义词：spherulitic, pisolitic|反义词：massive, crystalline
