misreforming:/ˌmɪsriˈfɔːrmɪŋ/| v.|误改，歪曲，曲解 |To reform incorrectly or wrongly; to distort or misrepresent something that has been reformed.  It implies a flawed or inaccurate attempt at improvement or restructuring. |例句：The government's misreforming of the education system led to widespread dissatisfaction. 政府对教育体制的误改导致了广泛的不满。

The company's misreforming of its internal processes resulted in decreased efficiency.  公司的内部流程误改导致效率下降。|近义词：distort, misrepresent, corrupt, pervert |反义词：reform correctly, improve, rectify
