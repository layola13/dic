sedimentary:/ˌsɛdəˈmɛntəri/| adj. |沉积的；沉淀的| Relating to or denoting rocks formed from sediment deposited by water or air.  Sedimentary rocks are formed from layers of sediment that have been compressed and cemented together over time. Examples include sandstone, shale, and limestone. |例句：The Grand Canyon is famous for its spectacular display of sedimentary rock formations.  大峡谷以其壮观的沉积岩层而闻名。

例句：Many fossils are found in sedimentary rocks because they are formed in environments where organic matter can be preserved. 许多化石都发现于沉积岩中，因为它们是在可以保存有机质的环境中形成的。


近义词：depositional, stratified  | 反义词：igneous, metamorphic | 词性：adj.
