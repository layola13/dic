contractions:/kənˈtrækʃənz/| n. |缩写词；收缩；收缩(过程)|Shortened forms of words, phrases, or sentences, created by omitting one or more letters and often replacing them with an apostrophe.  Contractions are commonly used in informal writing and speech to make language more concise and natural.|Examples:  It's (it is), can't (cannot), they're (they are), shouldn't (should not),  I've (I have).|近义词：abbreviations, shortenings|反义词：full forms, expansions


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He's working late tonight. = 他今晚加班。
They're going to the cinema. = 他们要去电影院。


一般过去时:
She wasn't happy with the results. = 她对结果不满意。
We didn't go to the party. = 我们没去参加聚会。


现在完成时:
I've finished my work. = 我已经完成了我的工作。
They've been to Paris. = 他们去过巴黎。


过去完成时:
She'd already left when I arrived. = 我到达时她已经离开了。
He'd finished his dinner before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前他已经吃完了晚餐。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I weren't so tired. = 我希望我不那么累。


被动语态:
