lucule:/ˈljuːkjuːl/| n. |小室；小房间；果皮内的小隔间|A small chamber or compartment, especially one within a fruit.  The term is often used in botany to refer to the compartments within a fruit that contain seeds.|Example: The botanist carefully examined the lucule of the peppercorn to count the seeds within.|近义词：cell, compartment, locule|反义词：None
