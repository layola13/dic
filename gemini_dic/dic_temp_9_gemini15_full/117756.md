melanuric:/ˌmɛləˈnjʊrɪk/| adj.|黑尿的；含黑色素的|Relating to or characterized by the presence of melanin in the urine, giving it a dark color.  This condition can indicate various medical issues.|The patient's melanuric urine suggested a potential liver problem.  患者黑尿提示潜在的肝脏问题。|近义词：pigmented (in urine) |反义词：acholuric (absence of bile pigment in urine)
