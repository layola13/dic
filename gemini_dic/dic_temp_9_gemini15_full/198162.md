shore:/ʃɔːr/ | n. |海岸，岸边 |The land bordering the sea or a lake.  A shore is the land along the edge of a sea, ocean, lake, or other large body of water. |例句：We walked along the shore and collected seashells. 我们沿着海岸散步并收集贝壳。

例句：The waves crashed against the shore. 海浪拍打着海岸。


近义词：beach, coast, bank, seaside | 反义词：sea, ocean, deep


词源:  Old English *scōr, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch schor and German Schorre ‘sandy shore, mudflat’.
