pectineal:/pekˈtɪniəl/| adj.|耻骨的；耻骨肌的|Relating to or situated near the pectineal line of the pubis.  The pectineal line is a ridge on the superior ramus of the pubis bone.|例句：The pectineal muscle originates from the pectineal line of the pubis.  耻骨肌起源于耻骨的耻骨线。

The pectineal ligament is a strong structure. 耻骨韧带是一个坚固的结构。|近义词：pubic|反义词：None.


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective, so tense doesn't apply in the same way as verbs.  Its usage is descriptive.

Declarative: The pectineal muscle is an important hip adductor. 耻骨肌是重要的髋内收肌。

PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "pectineal".  It's a primarily anatomical term.
