satiation:/ˌsæʃiˈeɪʃən/| n. |满足；饱足；厌倦|The state of being completely satisfied or having had enough of something.|After a large meal, I experienced a feeling of satiation.  饱餐一顿后，我感到很满足。|The endless repetition led to satiation.  无休止的重复导致了厌倦。|satisfaction, repletion, fullness|hunger, dissatisfaction, starvation|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Satiation is a natural response to hunger. = 饱腹感是对饥饿的自然反应。
He feels satiation after eating a big dinner. = 他吃了一顿大餐后感到很满足。

一般过去时:
Satiation set in after he finished the entire cake. = 他吃完整个蛋糕后感到饱了。
The feeling of satiation lasted for hours. = 饱腹感持续了好几个小时。

现在完成时:
She has reached a point of satiation with her work. = 她对她的工作已经达到了厌倦的程度。
I have achieved satiation with this project. = 我对这个项目已经感到满足了。

过去完成时:
He had reached satiation before the second course arrived. = 第二道菜上来之前他已经吃饱了。
By the time dessert arrived, she had already reached satiation. = 甜点上来的时候，她已经吃饱了。
