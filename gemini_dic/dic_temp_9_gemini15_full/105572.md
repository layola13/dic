kinda:/ˈkaɪndə/| adv.|有点，稍微，差不多|It is an informal contraction of "kind of," meaning "somewhat" or "to some extent."  It's used to soften or downplay a statement.| I'm kinda hungry. 我有点饿。|  sort of, somewhat, a little|  very, extremely|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I kinda like this song. = 我有点喜欢这首歌。
He kinda understands the problem. = 他有点理解这个问题。


一般过去时:
She kinda felt uncomfortable. = 她感觉有点不舒服。
They kinda forgot about the meeting. = 他们有点忘了会议。


现在完成时:
I've kinda gotten used to it. = 我有点习惯了。
We've kinda lost track of time. = 我们有点迷失了时间。


过去完成时:
He had kinda suspected something was wrong. = 他有点怀疑出了问题。
They had kinda prepared for the worst. = 他们有点做好了最坏的打算。


将来时:
I think I'll kinda miss her. = 我想我会有点想念她。
It will kinda be difficult. = 这会有点困难。


虚拟语气:
If I kinda knew, I would tell you. = 如果我有点知道的话，我会告诉你的。
I wish I kinda understood. = 我希望我有点理解。


被动语态:
It was kinda overlooked. = 它有点被忽视了。
The problem is kinda being ignored. = 这个问题有点被忽视了。


疑问句:
Are you kinda tired? = 你有点累吗？
Is he kinda upset? = 他有点沮丧吗？


否定句:

It's not kinda important. = 这不太重要。
