precipitations:/ˌprɪˌsɪpɪˈteɪʃənz/| n. |降水，沉淀|The process of precipitating a substance from a solution; the action or process of precipitation.  Precipitation refers to any form of water that falls from the atmosphere to the Earth's surface. This includes rain, snow, sleet, and hail.  |例句：Heavy precipitations caused widespread flooding. 大雨导致了大范围的洪水。
例句：The experiment resulted in the precipitation of a white solid. 实验导致了一种白色固体的沉淀。|近义词：precipitation, rainfall, snowfall, showers|反义词：evaporation, sublimation|名词
