sedging:/ˈsɛdʒɪŋ/| n. |割草；割取莎草；莎草丛|The act of cutting or gathering sedge, a type of grass-like plant.  It can also refer to the process of removing unwanted vegetation, or the act of cutting grass.|例句：The gardener was busy with the sedging of the overgrown areas. 园丁忙着割除杂草丛生的区域。|例句：The sedging along the riverbank provided a natural habitat for wildlife. 河岸边的莎草丛为野生动物提供了天然的栖息地。|近义词：reaping, mowing, weeding|反义词：planting, cultivating
