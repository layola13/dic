combo:/ˈkɒmboʊ/ /ˈkɑːmboʊ/| n.|组合，套餐，连击|A combination of things, often offered together at a reduced price.  It can also refer to a series of actions or events that occur together or a sequence of attacks in a game or fight.|例句：The restaurant offers a combo meal including a burger, fries, and a drink. 这家餐厅提供包含汉堡、薯条和饮料的套餐。

例句：He landed a devastating combo in the boxing match. 他在拳击比赛中打出了一套毁灭性的连击。|近义词：combination, set, deal|反义词：single, separate
