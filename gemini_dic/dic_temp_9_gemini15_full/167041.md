presuggestions:/ˌpriːsəˈdʒɛʃənz/ | n. |预先建议，初步建议 | Preliminary suggestions or recommendations made before a final decision is reached.  These suggestions are offered before more detailed plans or proposals are developed. |The presuggestions for the project included a revised timeline and budget.  项目预先建议包括修订后的时间表和预算。


|近义词： preliminary suggestions, initial proposals, advance recommendations |反义词：final decisions, conclusive proposals |词性：名词
