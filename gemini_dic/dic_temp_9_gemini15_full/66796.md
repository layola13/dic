expandability: /ɪkˌspændəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可扩展性，扩展能力 | The ability to be expanded or increased in size, scope, or capacity.  It refers to the potential for growth and adaptation.  This can apply to physical objects, systems, or organizations. |

例句：

The expandability of the software allows for easy addition of new features.  软件的可扩展性允许轻松添加新功能。

The company's success is due in part to its expandability and adaptability to market changes.  公司的成功部分归功于其可扩展性和适应市场变化的能力。


近义词：scalability, extensibility, flexibility, adaptability

反义词：inflexibility, rigidity, limitation
