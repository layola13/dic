cors:/kɔːrs/| n.|跨源资源共享|Cross-Origin Resource Sharing.  A mechanism that allows web pages from one origin (domain, protocol, and port) to access resources from a different origin. It's a crucial security feature that prevents malicious websites from accessing data from other websites without permission.|Example: The browser implemented CORS to prevent unauthorized access to the API.|近义词:cross-domain access|反义词:same-origin policy
