noncorrespondence:/ˌnɒnkɒrəˈspɒndəns/ | n. | 不对应；不符；不一致 | The lack of agreement or similarity between things; a failure to match or correspond.  It often refers to a discrepancy between expectation and reality, or between two sets of data or information. | Example: There was a noncorrespondence between the witness's testimony and the physical evidence.  证词与物证不符。  The noncorrespondence of the two maps caused considerable confusion. 两张地图的不符造成了相当大的混乱。 | 近义词：discrepancy, disparity, inconsistency, mismatch | 反义词：correspondence, agreement, conformity, consistency
