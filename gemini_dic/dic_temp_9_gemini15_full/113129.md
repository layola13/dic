lungs:/lʌŋz/| n.|肺 |The lungs are the organs of respiration in the body. They are responsible for taking in oxygen and expelling carbon dioxide.  They are located in the chest cavity, one on each side of the heart. |例句：The doctor listened to the patient's lungs with a stethoscope.医生用听诊器听了病人的肺部。|例句：Smoking damages your lungs.吸烟会损害你的肺。|近义词：pulmonary system|反义词:N/A|名词
