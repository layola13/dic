redubbing:/ˌriːˈdʌbɪŋ/ | v. | 重新配音 | The process of replacing the original audio track of a film, television program, or other audio-visual media with a new one, typically in a different language or with improved sound quality.  | Example: The movie was redubbed in Spanish for release in Latin America.  这部电影被重新配音成西班牙语，以便在拉丁美洲发行。 |近义词: re-recording, overdubbing | 反义词:  None directly opposite, but could be considered the opposite of leaving the original audio unchanged. | 动词
