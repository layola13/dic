goalie:/ˈɡoʊliː/ | n. |守门员，门将 | A goalie is a player whose main job is to prevent the opposing team from scoring in sports like hockey, soccer, or lacrosse.  They are responsible for guarding the goal. | Example: The goalie made a spectacular save to keep the game tied.  守门员做出精彩扑救，使比赛保持平局。  The hockey goalie was a wall, stopping almost every shot. 冰球守门员像一堵墙，几乎挡住了每一次射门。 |近义词： goalkeeper, netminder | 反义词：forward, attacker | 名词
