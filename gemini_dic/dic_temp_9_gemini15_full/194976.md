semi-Russians: /ˌsɛmiˈrʌʃənz/ | n. | 半俄罗斯人，具有部分俄罗斯血统的人 | People of mixed ancestry, part of which is Russian.  They may be individuals with one Russian parent and one parent of another ethnicity, or those with a more complex heritage including Russian ancestry. The term can be used descriptively or, depending on context, pejoratively. | Example:  Many semi-Russians live in countries bordering Russia.  许多具有部分俄罗斯血统的人居住在与俄罗斯接壤的国家。 | 近义词:  part-Russian, Russian-mixed | 反义词:  full-blooded Russian, non-Russian
