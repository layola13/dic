refutabilities:/ˌrɛfjʊˈteɪbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可反驳性；可驳斥性 | The capacity or potential to be refuted or disproven.  Refutabilities are crucial in scientific inquiry, where theories must be testable and potentially falsifiable.  | Example: The study's refutabilities were limited due to the small sample size.  这项研究的可反驳性由于样本量小而受到限制。 | 近义词：falsifiability, disprovability | 反义词：incontestability, irrefutability | 名词
