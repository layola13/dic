fastback:/ˈfæstbæk/| n. |掀背车，斜背车 |A car with a sloping rear end that incorporates the rear window and tailgate.  This design maximizes cargo space. |例句：He bought a new fastback sports car. 他买了一辆新的掀背式跑车。

例句：The fastback design is popular for its sleek appearance and practicality. 掀背车设计因其时尚的外观和实用性而广受欢迎。


近义词：hatchback (较为通用的说法) |反义词：sedan (传统三厢车)


词性：名词


词源:  The word "fastback" is a compound word.  "Fast" refers to the sloping, streamlined nature of the rear end, while "back" refers to the rear of the vehicle.  The term likely emerged in the automotive industry in the mid-20th century as this style of car body became more prevalent.
