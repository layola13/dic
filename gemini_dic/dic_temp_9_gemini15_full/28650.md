caricature:/ˌkærɪˈkætʃər/| n. v.|漫画，讽刺画；夸张的描绘|A caricature is a picture, description, or imitation of a person or thing in which certain striking characteristics are exaggerated in order to create a comic or grotesque effect.  It can be a drawing, a written description, or even an imitation of someone's behavior.|例句：The cartoonist's caricature of the politician was hilarious.  这位漫画家对这位政治家的讽刺漫画非常滑稽。|例句：His portrayal of the character was more of a caricature than a realistic representation. 他对这个人物的刻画与其说是真实的描绘，不如说是夸张的漫画。|近义词：cartoon, parody, lampoon, exaggeration|反义词：accurate representation, realistic portrayal|名词
