proselytization:/ˌprɒsɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |布道；传教；劝诱改宗|The act of converting or attempting to convert someone to a different religion or belief.  It often implies an aggressive or forceful approach.|例句：The aggressive proselytization of the new religious group caused tension within the community.  积极的劝诱新宗教团体改宗的行为导致了社区内部的紧张关系。  The government banned proselytization in public spaces. 政府禁止在公共场所传教。|近义词：conversion, evangelization, missionary work|反义词：secularism, atheism
