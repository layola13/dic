despotisms:/ˈdɛspəˌtɪzəm/| n.|专制统治；暴政；专制主义|The rule of a despot; a form of government in which absolute power is vested in a single ruler.|例句：The people suffered under years of despotisms.  人民在多年的暴政下受苦。|例句：Many historians have studied the rise and fall of despotisms throughout history. 许多历史学家研究了历史上专制统治的兴衰。|近义词：tyranny, autocracy, dictatorship|反义词：democracy, freedom, liberty|名词

词源:
From the French word "despotisme," ultimately derived from the Ancient Greek word "despotes" (δεσπότης), meaning "master," "lord," or "tyrant."  The suffix "-ism" denotes a system, practice, or doctrine.
