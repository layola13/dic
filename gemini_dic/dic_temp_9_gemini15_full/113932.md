maffler:/ˈmæflər/| n.|围巾，マフラー|A muffler is a scarf, typically worn around the neck to provide warmth or as a fashion accessory.  It can be made from various materials, such as wool, silk, or cotton.|例句：She wrapped a warm muffler around her neck to protect herself from the cold wind. 她围上了一条暖和的围巾以抵御寒风。

例句：He wore a stylish muffler that matched his coat. 他戴着一条与其外套相配的时尚围巾。


近义词：scarf, shawl, neck warmer|反义词：无|词性：名词
