insincere:/ɪnˈsɪnsɪr/| adj.|不真诚的；虚伪的|Not genuine or sincere; hypocritical.  It implies a lack of genuine feeling or honesty, often with the intention to deceive or manipulate. |例句：His apology seemed insincere; he didn't sound truly sorry.  他的道歉听起来不真诚；他听起来并不真的抱歉。

例句：Her insincere praise made me uncomfortable. 她的虚伪赞美让我感到不舒服。


近义词：dishonest, hypocritical, false, feigned, artificial, affected

反义词：sincere, genuine, honest, truthful, heartfelt

词性：形容词
