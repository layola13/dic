gill-less:/ˈdʒɪlləs/| adj.|无鳃的|Lacking gills.  This term is primarily used in zoology and biology to describe aquatic animals that do not possess gills for respiration.  The absence of gills implies that the creature breathes through other means, such as lungs or through the skin.|例句：The gill-less fish species relies on atmospheric oxygen for survival.  这种无鳃鱼类依靠大气中的氧气生存。|近义词：branchialess|反义词：gilled
