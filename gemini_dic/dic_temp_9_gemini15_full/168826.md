productized:/ˈprɒdʌktaɪzd/ /ˈprɑːdʌktaɪzd/| v.|产品化，商品化|To transform something (a service, skill, idea, etc.) into a product that can be sold or offered as a commercial good.|The company productized its customer service expertise by creating a software-as-a-service offering.  这家公司通过创建软件即服务产品将他们的客户服务专业知识产品化了。|近义词：commercialized, marketed, monetized |反义词：de-productized


USAGE->

They productize their innovative ideas. = 他们将他们的创新理念产品化。
We productize our services for a wider market. = 我们将我们的服务产品化，以面向更广阔的市场。

The startup productized its algorithm last year. = 这家初创公司去年将其算法产品化了。
