outvaluing: /ˌaʊtˈvæljuɪŋ/ | v. | 超过…的价值；比…更有价值 |  To surpass in value or importance.  |


例句：

The antique vase outvalues all the other items in the collection. = 这只古董花瓶比收藏中的其他所有物品更有价值。

His contributions to the project outvalued his initial investment tenfold. = 他对项目的贡献比他最初的投资高出十倍。


近义词：surpassing, exceeding, surpassing in value, outweighing

反义词：undervaluing, underestimating


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artwork outvalues the furniture. = 这件艺术品比家具更有价值。
His experience outvalues his qualifications. = 他的经验比他的资历更有价值。


一般过去时:
The old car outvalued the new one in sentimental value. = 旧车在情感价值上超过了新车。
The antique chair outvalued my initial offer. = 古董椅子的价值超过了我的最初报价。


现在完成时:
The investment has outvalued the initial deposit significantly. = 这项投资的价值已大大超过了最初的存款。
The property has outvalued expectations. = 房地产的价值已经超过了预期。


过去完成时:
The painting had outvalued its original purchase price by the time it was sold. = 这幅画在出售时，其价值已经超过了最初的购买价格。
By the time of the auction, the collection had outvalued the initial appraisal. = 到拍卖的时候，这批藏品的价值已经超过了最初的估价。


将来时:
This technology will outvalue all other methods in the future. = 这项技术将来会比所有其他方法更有价值。
Experts believe that the land will outvalue neighboring properties. = 专家认为这块土地的价值将超过邻近的房产。


虚拟语气:
If the stock market continued to rise, our investment would outvalue our initial goal. = 如果股市继续上涨，我们的投资将超过我们的初始目标。
If I had known, I would have bought the property, as it would have outvalued the apartment. = 如果我知道的话，我会买下那处房产，因为它会比公寓更有价值。


被动语态:
The antique was outvalued by the experts. = 这件古董被专家们评估出更高的价值。
The benefits of this project are outvalued by its cost. = 这个项目的效益被其成本所抵消。


疑问句:
Does this diamond outvalue that ruby? = 这颗钻石的价值比那颗红宝石高吗？
Does his contribution outvalue his salary? = 他的贡献价值超过他的薪水吗？


否定句:
The benefits of the project did not outvalue the costs. = 该项目的效益没有超过成本。
The painting did not outvalue the expectation. = 这幅画的价值没有超过预期。


PHRASE->
outvalue significantly = 大大超过价值
outvalue considerably = 相当超过价值
outvalue by a margin = 以一定幅度超过价值
outvalue in terms of = 就…而言价值更高
outvalue the competition = 价值超过竞争对手
