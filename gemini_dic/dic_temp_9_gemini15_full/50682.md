dermatitides:/ˌdɜːrməˈtaɪtɪdiːz/| n. |皮肤炎（复数）|Inflammations of the skin.  The plural form of dermatitis.  It refers to various skin conditions characterized by inflammation, redness, itching, and sometimes blistering.|例句：Various factors can trigger dermatitides, including allergies, irritants, and infections.  各种因素都可能引发皮肤炎，包括过敏、刺激物和感染。

例句：The doctor diagnosed her with several types of dermatitides after a thorough examination. 医生在仔细检查后诊断她患有几种类型的皮肤炎。


近义词：skin inflammations, skin rashes (depending on the specific type) |反义词：healthy skin


词源：From "dermatitis" + "-ides" (plural suffix).  "Dermatitis" itself comes from Greek "derma" (skin) + "-itis" (inflammation).
