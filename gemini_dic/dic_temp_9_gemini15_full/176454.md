re-marked:/ˌriːˈmɑːrkt/ | vt. | 重新标记；再次评论 | To mark or comment on something again.  It implies a previous marking or comment that is being revisited or altered. |例句：The teacher re-marked the exam papers after a student pointed out a grading error.  老师在学生指出评分错误后重新批改了试卷。

The professor re-marked on the student's insightful analysis of the text. 教授再次评论了学生对文本的深刻分析。|近义词：remark again, re-evaluate, reconsider |反义词：ignore, overlook
