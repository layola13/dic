skittles:/ˈskɪtəlz/| n.|彩球；糖果；九柱戏|Small, hard candies, usually fruit-flavored, that are often colorful and oblong in shape.  The name also refers to a game played with nine pins. |例句：The children enjoyed eating a bag of skittles.孩子们喜欢吃一包彩球糖。

例句：He spent the afternoon playing skittles at the pub. 他下午在酒吧玩九柱戏。|近义词：sweets, candies, sweets, bowling (in the context of the game)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with less sugary treats or other games.
