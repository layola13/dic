plump-up:/plʌmp ʌp/| vt.|使…丰满；使…鼓起；使…膨胀 | To make something fuller or rounder, often by adding something to it.  It implies an improvement in appearance or condition.| 例句：She plumped up the cushions on the sofa. 她把沙发上的靠垫弄得鼓鼓的。

She plumped up the soufflé with a little more egg white. 她用多一点的蛋清使苏芙蕾蓬松起来。


近义词：inflate, swell, puff up, fill out |反义词：deflate, shrink, flatten |词性:动词
