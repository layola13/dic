chubbiness:/ˈtʃʌbɪnəs/| n.|丰满；圆胖；胖乎乎|The state or quality of being chubby; plumpness, especially of a child.  It often implies a pleasantly round and soft appearance, rather than being overweight.|例句：The baby's chubbiness was adorable.  婴儿的胖乎乎的样子很可爱。 The chubbiness of her cheeks made her look younger. 她胖乎乎的脸颊让她看起来更年轻。|近义词：plumpness, roundness, fullness|反义词：thinness, gauntness, emaciation
