flauntingly:/ˈflɔːntɪŋli/ | adv. | 炫耀地；招摇地 |  In a way that shows off something you are proud of in a very noticeable way.  It often implies arrogance or a lack of sensitivity to others' feelings. |例句：He flauntingly displayed his wealth, driving a new sports car every week. 他炫耀地展示他的财富，每周都开一辆新的跑车。

She flauntingly ignored the rules, walking right past the security guard. 她公然无视规则，径直走过保安。

| 近义词：ostentatiously, showily, conspicuously | 反义词：humbly, modestly, unassumingly |  无
