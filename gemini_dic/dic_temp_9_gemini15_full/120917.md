miscatalog:/ˌmɪsˈkætəlɒɡ/| vt.| 错误分类；归档错误 |To catalog incorrectly; to place something in the wrong category in a catalog or system.  |例句：The librarian miscataloged the book, placing it under fiction instead of non-fiction. 图书管理员将这本书归类错误，把它放在了小说而不是非小说类。 The museum curator miscataloged the ancient artifact, leading to confusion about its origin. 博物馆馆长将古代文物归类错误，导致对其起源的混淆。|近义词：misclassify, misfile, misindex |反义词：catalog correctly, properly classify|动词
