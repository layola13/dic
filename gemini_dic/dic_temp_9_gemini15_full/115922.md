marque:/mɑːrk/|n.|商标；品牌；型号|A marque is a brand or make of a product, especially a car.  It is often associated with a particular image or reputation.|Example:  Ferrari is a prestigious marque of sports car. 法拉利是著名的跑车品牌。The new marque of phone is very popular. 新出的手机品牌很受欢迎。|近义词：brand, make, label|反义词：generic (in some contexts)
