outmastered:/ˌaʊtˈmɑːstəd/ | v. | 被超越，技不如人 |  To be surpassed or defeated in skill or ability.  It implies a significant difference in skill level, suggesting a complete defeat. |The young chess player was outmastered by the grandmaster.  年轻的棋手被大师击败了。|  surpassed, defeated, bested | mastered, excelled | v.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outmasters his opponents in every game. 他在每一场比赛中都胜过对手。
She consistently outmasters her colleagues in problem-solving.  她在解决问题方面始终胜过她的同事。


一般过去时:
The champion outmastered his challenger in the final round. 冠军在决赛中击败了他的挑战者。
Last year's winner was outmastered by a newcomer.  去年的冠军被一位新人击败了。


现在完成时:
He has outmastered all his previous rivals. 他已经超越了他以前所有的对手。
She has outmastered the challenges presented to her. 她已经克服了她面临的挑战。


过去完成时:
By the end of the match, he had outmastered his opponent's strategies. 比赛结束时，他已经胜过了对手的策略。
They had outmastered their previous best scores. 他们已经超过了他们之前的最好成绩。


将来时:
He will outmaster his opponent with his superior technique. 他将凭借其高超的技术击败对手。
She is confident that she will outmaster the current record. 她相信她会打破目前的纪录。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he might have outmastered his opponent. 如果他训练更努力，他可能会击败他的对手。
I wish I could outmaster my fear of public speaking. 我希望我能克服我对公开演讲的恐惧。(Note:  "outmaster" here is used figuratively)


被动语态:
His opponent was outmastered by his superior skill. 他的对手被他的高超技艺击败了。
The previous record was outmastered by a new competitor.  之前的记录被一位新的竞争者打破了。


疑问句:
Was he outmastered by a younger player? 他是被一个更年轻的选手击败的吗？
Will she be outmastered in the upcoming competition?  她会在即将到来的比赛中被击败吗？


否定句:
He was not outmastered; he simply lost due to bad luck. 他并没有被击败；他只是因为运气不好而输了。
She was not outmastered; she withdrew from the competition due to injury. 她没有被击败；她由于受伤而退出了比赛。
