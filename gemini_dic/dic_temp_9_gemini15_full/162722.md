prebargains:/ˌpriːˈbɑːrɡɪnz/| n. |预先谈判；预先协商| Preliminary negotiations or discussions before formal bargaining begins.  These discussions often lay the groundwork for a more formal negotiation process by establishing a common understanding of the issues and potential solutions.  |例句：The prebargains between the union and management were surprisingly productive. = 工会和管理层之间的预先谈判出奇地富有成效。

例句：Prebargains on the contract proved essential in avoiding a strike. = 合同的预先谈判被证明对于避免罢工至关重要。


近义词：preliminary talks, advance discussions, initial negotiations


反义词：final negotiations, concluding talks


词性：名词
