horsewood:/ˈhɔːrswʊd/| n. |马木，铁木|A type of very hard and durable wood, often used for tools and furniture, typically from trees of the genus *Hymenaea*.  It is known for its strength and resistance to decay.|The craftsman used horsewood to create a sturdy chair.|近义词：ironwood, lignum vitae|反义词：balsa wood
