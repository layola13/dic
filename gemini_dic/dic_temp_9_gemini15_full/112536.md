They decorated their love-nest with photographs and mementos of their travels. 他们用照片和旅行纪念品装饰他们的爱巢。


近义词：lover's retreat, haven, hideaway, boudoir (more formal) | 反义词：None directly, but words implying loneliness or separation could be considered antonyms in context.  | 词性: 名词
