concusses:/kənˈkʌsɪz/| vt.|使脑震荡；猛烈撞击；震动|To violently shake or jar; to inflict a concussion on.|The impact concussed him, leaving him disoriented and dazed.  这次撞击使他脑震荡，让他感到迷茫和昏昏沉沉。The earthquake concussed the buildings, causing significant structural damage. 地震剧烈震动了建筑物，造成了严重的结构性破坏。|impact, shake, jolt|soothe, calm|动词
