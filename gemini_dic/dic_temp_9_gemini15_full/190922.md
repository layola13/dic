scabs:/skæbz/| n.|痂，硬皮；（尤指罢工期间的）破坏分子|A scab is a crust that forms over a wound as it heals.  It can also refer to a worker who continues to work during a strike, considered a traitor by striking workers.|例句：The scab on his knee was almost gone. 他膝盖上的痂几乎消失了。

The striking workers called their colleagues who crossed the picket line "scabs". 罢工工人称越过纠察线的同事为“破坏分子”。|近义词：crust, scabrous|反义词：none  (反义词需要根据语境来定，例如，如果指伤口愈合，则反义词可以是“open wound”; 如果指罢工期间的人，则没有直接的反义词)|名词
