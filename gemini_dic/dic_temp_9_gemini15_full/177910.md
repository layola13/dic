rechart:/riːˈtʃɑːrt/ | vt. |重新绘制图表；重新制图|To make a new chart or graph, often based on updated or revised data.|The marketing team had to rechart the sales figures after discovering a data entry error.  市场团队在发现数据输入错误后不得不重新绘制销售图表。|近义词：redraw, remake, revise|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rechart the data every month. = 他们每月重新绘制数据图表。
We rechart the course of the project regularly. = 我们定期重新规划项目的进程。


一般过去时:
He recharted the map after finding a new route. = 他在找到一条新路线后重新绘制了地图。
She recharted the company's financial performance. = 她重新绘制了公司的财务业绩图表。


现在完成时:
They have recharted the sales figures for the last quarter. = 他们已经重新绘制了上一季度的销售数据图表。
The team has recharted the project timeline. = 团队已经重新规划了项目时间线。


过去完成时:
After the storm, they had recharted the damaged coastline. = 暴风雨过后，他们重新绘制了受损的海岸线地图。
Before the presentation, she had recharted all the key data points. = 在演示文稿之前，她已经重新绘制了所有关键数据点。


将来时:
They will rechart the budget next week. = 他们下周将重新规划预算。
We will rechart the company's strategy after the market analysis. = 市场分析后，我们将重新规划公司战略。


虚拟语气:
If the data were inconsistent, we would rechart it. = 如果数据不一致，我们将重新绘制它。
I wish we had recharted the course earlier. = 我希望我们早点重新规划路线。


被动语态:
The graph was recharted to reflect the new information. = 图表被重新绘制以反映新信息。
The project timeline is being recharted due to unforeseen delays. = 由于不可预见的延误，项目时间表正在被重新规划。


疑问句:
Will they rechart the data before the meeting? = 他们在会议前会重新绘制数据图表吗？
Should we rechart the project plan? = 我们应该重新规划项目计划吗？


否定句:
They did not rechart the map. = 他们没有重新绘制地图。
We will not rechart the data without further investigation. = 没有进一步调查，我们不会重新绘制数据。
