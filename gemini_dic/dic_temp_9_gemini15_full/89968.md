horsetail:/ˈhɔːrseˌteɪl/| n.|木贼;  хвощ|A plant of the genus Equisetum, characterized by jointed, hollow stems and whorls of scale-like leaves.  Horsetails are ancient plants, with some species dating back to the Jurassic period. They are often found in damp, shady areas.|The horsetail grew abundantly near the stream.  木贼在小溪边大量生长。|Equisetum, scouring rush|flowering plant|
