/ˌoʊvərɑːbˈdʒɛktɪfaɪz/ | v. |过度物化 |To treat someone or something as an object, often in a way that is disrespectful or dehumanizing, to a greater extent than is considered appropriate or acceptable.  This goes beyond simply objectifying and implies an excessive or harmful level of objectification. | The media often overobjectifies women, reducing them to their physical attributes and ignoring their other qualities.  媒体经常过度物化女性，将其简化为其身体特征，而忽略了她们的其他品质。| dehumanize, depersonalize |  empower, humanize |动词
