discontinuee:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuˌiː/| n. |停止者；中断者；被终止的人或事物|A discontinuee is someone or something that has been discontinued or stopped.  This word is less common and often implies a formal or legal cessation.|The factory's discontinuance left many workers as discontinuees, searching for new employment.  工厂的停产使许多工人成为被终止雇佣的人，正在寻找新的工作。|近义词：former subscriber, ex-customer, terminated party|反义词：continuee (此词很少使用)|名词
