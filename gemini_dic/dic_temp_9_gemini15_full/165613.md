premier:/ˈprɛmɪər/| adj., n.|首要的；首位的；首映；首相|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "premier" means first in importance, order, or rank; chief; leading.  As a noun, it refers to the prime minister or premier of a country, or the first public performance of a play, movie, etc.

例句:
The premier of the film was a huge success. = 电影的首映非常成功。

The country's premier announced new economic policies. = 该国的首相宣布了新的经济政策。


近义词:
chief, principal, leading, foremost, top, first, inaugural (for noun sense: prime minister)

反义词:
last, final, secondary, minor (for adjective sense)


词性:
adjective, noun
