scrumpies:/ˈskrʌmpiːz/| n. |粗制滥造的衣服；粗糙的苹果派|Informal British English:  A scrumpie is a type of rough, homemade clothing, often associated with a rustic or untidy appearance. It can also refer to a type of rustic apple pie. The term carries a slightly derogatory connotation, suggesting something is poorly made or of low quality. |例句：Her clothes were described as scrumpies and mismatched.|近义词：rags, tatters, makeshift clothing |反义词：finery, elegant clothes
