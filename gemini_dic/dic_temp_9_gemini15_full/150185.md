pancakes:/ˈpænkeɪks/| n.|煎饼，薄饼|Flat, thin cakes made from batter and fried or cooked on a hot surface.  They are often served with syrup, fruit, or other toppings.|例句：I made a stack of pancakes for breakfast. 我做了好多煎饼当早餐。

例句：We had pancakes with maple syrup and blueberries. 我们吃了淋上枫糖浆和蓝莓的煎饼。


近义词：crepes (薄饼), fritters (油炸小饼)


反义词：无


词性:名词
