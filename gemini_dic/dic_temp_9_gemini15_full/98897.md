intensification:/ɪnˌtɛnsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |增强，强化；加剧|The process of making something more intense or stronger.  It involves increasing the strength, force, or degree of something. |例句：The intensification of the conflict led to a full-scale war.  冲突的加剧导致了全面战争。|例句：The intensification of farming practices has led to soil degradation. 集约化农业导致了土壤退化。|近义词：augmentation, escalation, amplification, exacerbation |反义词：reduction, lessening, mitigation, alleviation |名词
