half-shouted:/ˌhɑːfˈʃaʊtɪd/| adj.| 半喊着；低声喊叫 | Partially shouted; spoken in a voice between a whisper and a shout.  It implies a lack of full force or volume in the shouting. |例句：He half-shouted the news across the room. 他在房间里半喊着把消息喊了出去。
She half-shouted his name, hoping he'd hear her. 她低声喊着他的名字，希望他能听到。|近义词：muttered, whispered loudly|反义词：yelled, screamed

He half-shouts instructions to his team. 他向他的团队半喊着发出指示。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't half-shout; he spoke in a normal voice. 他没有半喊；他用正常的音量说话。
She didn't half-shout; she spoke clearly and calmly. 她没有半喊；她清晰而平静地说话。
