lucidly:/ˈluːsɪdli/| adv. |清晰地；明了地；透彻地 |In a clear and easy-to-understand manner; intelligibly.  It emphasizes clarity and intelligibility of thought, expression, or style. |例句：She explained the complex theory lucidly, making it accessible to everyone. 她清晰地解释了这个复杂的理论，使每个人都能理解。

例句：He lucidly described the events leading up to the accident. 他清晰地描述了导致事故发生的事件。


近义词：clearly, understandably, intelligibly, plainly, transparently

反义词：obscurely, vaguely, unclearly, confusedly


词性:副词
