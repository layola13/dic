loggerheads:/ˈlɒɡəhedz/| n.|势不两立，激烈争论|At loggerheads: in a state of conflict or disagreement.  The phrase originates from the game of loggerheads, a children's game involving butting heads.|例句：The two brothers are always at loggerheads.  两个兄弟总是争吵不休。

例句：The government and the unions are at loggerheads over the new pay deal.  政府和工会在新的薪酬协议上发生了冲突。


近义词：conflict, disagreement, quarrel, feud, contention


反义词：agreement, harmony, concord, accord


词性：名词
