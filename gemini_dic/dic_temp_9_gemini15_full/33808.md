circumflex:/ˈsɜːrkəmfleks/| n. |抑扬音符，^；圆拱形；弯曲|A circumflex is a diacritical mark (^) placed above a letter to indicate a particular pronunciation or modification of the letter's sound.  It can also refer to an arch or curved shape.|Example: The word "hôtel" uses a circumflex above the "e" to indicate a specific pronunciation.  酒店这个词在e上面用抑扬音符来表示特定的发音。|近义词：diacritic, accent mark|反义词：None

Word Origin:
