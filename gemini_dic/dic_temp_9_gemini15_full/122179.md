misty:/ˈmɪsti/ | adj. | 雾蒙蒙的，模糊的 | Covered with or characterized by mist; hazy.  |例句：The misty morning obscured the view.  雾蒙蒙的早晨遮挡了视线。
The future looked misty and uncertain to him. 未来对他来说显得模糊而不可预测。|近义词：foggy, hazy, cloudy, nebulous |反义词：clear, bright, sunny
