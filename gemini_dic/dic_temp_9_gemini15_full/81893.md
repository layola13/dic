griffin:/ˈɡrɪfɪn/| n.|狮鹫，格里芬|A mythical creature with the body of a lion and the head and wings of an eagle.  Griffins are often depicted as guardians of treasure or as symbols of power and strength.|例句：The griffin statues guarded the entrance to the ancient tomb.  狮鹫雕像守护着古墓的入口。

The artist depicted a majestic griffin soaring through the clouds. 艺术家描绘了一只雄伟的狮鹫在云层中翱翔。|近义词：mythical beast, chimera|反义词:None.
