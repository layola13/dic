muset:/mjuːˈzeɪt/ | v. |沉思；冥想；默想 |To muse is to think deeply or dreamily about something.  It often implies a state of quiet contemplation or reflection. |例句：I mused on the strangeness of the situation. 我沉思着这情况的古怪之处。

例句：She mused for a long time about her future. 她长时间地冥想她的未来。


近义词：ponder, contemplate, reflect, meditate | 反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He muses on the meaning of life. = 他沉思着人生的意义。
She muses about her childhood memories. = 她正在回忆童年往事。


一般过去时:
He mused on the problem for hours. = 他花了几个小时思考这个问题。
She mused about her lost love. = 她默默地想着她失去的爱人。


现在完成时:
I have mused over this question many times. = 我已经多次思考过这个问题。
She has mused on the nature of happiness. = 她一直在思考幸福的本质。


过去完成时:
He had mused for days before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前思考了几天。
She had mused over the letter before replying. = 她仔细考虑了这封信后才回复。


将来时:
I will muse on this topic later. = 我稍后会思考这个话题。
She will muse on the possibilities. = 她将思考各种可能性。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would muse more often. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更经常地冥想。
I wish I could muse peacefully without interruption. = 我希望我可以平静地冥想而不会被打扰。


被动语态:  (The verb "muse" is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
What are you musing about? = 你在思考什么？
Does he often muse on philosophical questions? = 他经常思考哲学问题吗？


否定句:
He didn't muse over the details. = 他没有仔细考虑细节。
She did not muse on the implications. = 她没有思考其含义。


PHRASE->
muse on/upon: 沉思……
muse about: 沉思……
muse over: 仔细考虑……
lose oneself in musings: 陷入沉思
a muse of inspiration: 灵感的缪斯
