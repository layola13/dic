outcompeted:/ˌaʊt kəmˈpiːtɪd/| v.|胜过，竞争获胜|  To surpass in competition; to defeat in a competitive situation. It implies a complete victory, often leaving the competitor significantly behind.| 例句：The smaller company was outcompeted by the larger corporation.  较小的公司被更大的公司竞争打败了。

The invasive species outcompeted the native plants for resources. 侵入物种在资源竞争中胜过了本地植物。


近义词：surpassed, defeated, outstripped, overshadowed, trumped| 反义词：defeated (by), lost to, succumbed to| 词性：动词 (过去分词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The stronger team outcompetes the weaker team regularly. = 更强的队伍经常胜过较弱的队伍。
This product outcompetes all others in the market. = 此产品在市场上胜过所有其他产品。


一般过去时:
The startup outcompeted its rivals and secured a large market share. = 这家初创公司击败了竞争对手，并获得了很大的市场份额。
Our team outcompeted the opposing team in the final match. = 我们的团队在决赛中击败了对方团队。


现在完成时:
They have outcompeted their competitors in several key markets. = 他们在几个主要市场上已经击败了竞争对手。
The company has outcompeted its rivals through innovation. = 这家公司通过创新击败了竞争对手。


过去完成时:
By the time the merger was announced, the smaller firm had already been outcompeted. = 在合并宣布之前，这家较小的公司已经被击败了。
The incumbent had been outcompeted by a new challenger before the election. = 在选举之前，现任者已经被一个新的挑战者击败了。


将来时:
We believe we will outcompete our rivals with our new technology. = 我们相信我们将凭借我们的新技术击败竞争对手。
Analysts predict that Company A will outcompete Company B in the coming year. = 分析师预测A公司将在来年击败B公司。


虚拟语气:
If they had invested more, they might have outcompeted their rivals. = 如果他们投资更多，他们可能已经击败了竞争对手。
I wish we had outcompeted them in that important deal. = 我真希望我们在那笔重要的交易中击败他们。


被动语态:
The smaller company was outcompeted in the marketplace. = 这家较小的公司在市场上被击败了。
The local businesses were outcompeted by the large chain stores. = 当地的企业被大型连锁店击败了。


疑问句:
Was the incumbent outcompeted by a younger candidate? = 现任者是被一位更年轻的候选人击败了吗？
Will the new product outcompete existing options? = 新产品会击败现有选择吗？


否定句:
The new strategy did not outcompete the old one. = 新策略没有击败旧策略。
They failed to outcompete the established players in the market. = 他们在市场上未能击败老牌企业。


PHRASE->
be outcompeted by = 被...竞争击败
outcompeted in the market = 在市场上被击败
outcompeted by innovation = 被创新击败
outcompeted by a stronger competitor = 被更强大的竞争对手击败
outcompeted due to lack of resources = 由于缺乏资源而被击败
