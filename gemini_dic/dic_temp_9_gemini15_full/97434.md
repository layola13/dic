infuriates:/ɪnˈfjʊəriˌeɪts/ | vt. |使愤怒；激怒 | To make someone extremely angry. |例句：His constant interruptions infuriate me.  他不断打断我，这让我很生气。|例句：The traffic jam infuriated the commuters. 交通堵塞激怒了通勤者。|近义词：enrages, maddens, provokes, angers |反义词：calms, soothes, pleases


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His behavior infuriates me. = 他的行为让我很生气。
The noise infuriates the neighbors. = 噪音让邻居们很生气。

一般过去时:
The news infuriated him. = 这个消息让他很生气。
Her rudeness infuriated everyone present. = 她的粗鲁行为激怒了在场的所有人。

现在完成时:
His actions have infuriated me beyond belief. = 他的行为让我难以置信地生气。
The delay has infuriated the passengers. = 延误让乘客们非常生气。

过去完成时:
I had been infuriated by his constant lies. = 我一直被他不断的谎言激怒着。
They had already been infuriated by the injustice. = 他们已经被这种不公正对待激怒了。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish that wouldn't infuriate you. = 我希望那不会让你生气。

疑问句:

否定句:
