diffusers:/dɪˈfjuːzərz/ | n. |扩散器，漫射器 |A diffuser is a device or substance that spreads something, such as light, sound, or a liquid, over a wider area.  It can also refer to a person or thing that spreads or promotes something. |例句：The photographer used a diffuser to soften the harsh sunlight. 摄影师用扩散器来柔化刺眼的阳光。

例句：The air diffuser filled the room with a pleasant scent. 空气扩散器使房间充满宜人的香味。


近义词：disperser, spreader, distributor |反义词：concentrator, focuser |词性: 名词


词源:  From the verb *diffuse*, meaning "to spread out," which comes from the Latin *diffundere*, meaning "to pour out in different directions."  The suffix *-er* indicates an agent or instrument.
