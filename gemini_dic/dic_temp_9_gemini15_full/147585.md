overphilosophizes:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪləsəfaɪzɪz/| v.|过度哲学化；想得太多；|To think or analyze excessively about philosophical issues, often to the point of being impractical or unproductive.  It implies an over-application of philosophical concepts to everyday situations. |例句：He overphilosophizes every simple decision, delaying action indefinitely. 他把每一个简单的决定都过度哲学化，无限期地拖延行动。

He overphilosophizes simple problems, making them unnecessarily complex. 他把简单的问题想得太复杂了，使它们变得不必要地复杂。


近义词：overintellectualizes, overthinks, overanalyzes|反义词：simplifies, pragmatizes


词源:
over- (prefix indicating excess) + philosophize (from philosophy + -ize, meaning "to engage in or practice")  The word combines the prefix "over-" indicating an excessive degree, with the verb "philosophize," which means to engage in philosophical speculation or reasoning.
