copies:/ˈkɑːpiz/| n.|副本；复印件；拷贝|A copy is a reproduction of something, typically a document or artwork.  It can also refer to an instance of something.|例句：I made several copies of the document. 我做了几份文件的副本。

例句：She bought a limited edition copy of the book. 她买了一本该书的限量版。


近义词：duplicate, reproduction, imitation|反义词：original

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He copies my work. = 他抄袭我的作品。
She copies notes during the lecture. = 她在课堂上抄笔记。

一般过去时:
They copied the design from a competitor. = 他们从竞争对手那里抄袭了设计。
He copied the information from the website. = 他从网站上复制了信息。

现在完成时:
I have copied all the files to the external hard drive. = 我已将所有文件复制到外置硬盘。
She has copied the entire chapter. = 她已经复制了整章内容。

过去完成时:
He had copied the answer before the exam started. = 考试开始前他已经抄好了答案。
They had copied the data before the server crashed. = 服务器崩溃前他们已经复制了数据。

将来时:
I will copy the document for you. = 我会为你复印这份文件。
She will copy the recipe from the cookbook. = 她将从食谱中抄录食谱。

虚拟语气:
If I had a printer, I would copy the document. = 如果我有打印机，我会复印这份文件。
I wish I had copied the notes earlier. = 我真希望我早点抄了笔记。

被动语态:
The document was copied many times. = 这份文件被复制了很多次。
The information is being copied to the cloud. = 信息正在被复制到云端。

疑问句:
Did you copy the assignment? = 你抄作业了吗？
Can you copy this for me? = 你能帮我复印这个吗？

否定句:
I didn't copy his work. = 我没有抄袭他的作品。
She didn't copy the address correctly. = 她没有正确地抄写地址。
