goatherds:/ˈɡoʊθərdz/ | n. |牧羊人（尤指放山羊的） |People who herd goats.  Goatherds are often found in mountainous or hilly regions where goats can graze. They are responsible for the care and protection of their goats, guiding them to pastures and ensuring their safety. |例句：The goatherds led their flocks to higher pastures. 牧羊人带领他们的羊群到更高的牧场上。  The ancient goatherds knew the mountain paths better than anyone. 古代的牧羊人比任何人都更了解山路。|近义词：shepherd, herder |反义词：N/A
