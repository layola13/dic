overwin:/ˌoʊvərˈwɪn/| vt.|战胜，克服|To defeat or overcome (someone or something), especially in a contest or struggle.  |例句：She overwon her fear of public speaking. 她克服了她的怯场。|例句：The team overwon their opponents in a thrilling final match.  球队在令人兴奋的决赛中战胜了对手。|近义词：conquer, defeat, overcome, vanquish|反义词：lose, succumb, yield|动词
