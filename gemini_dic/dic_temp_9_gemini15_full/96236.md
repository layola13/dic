indican:/ˈɪndɪkæn/| n. |靛蓝原苷； indican|一种存在于某些植物（如靛蓝植物）中的糖苷，水解后会产生靛蓝。Indican is a colorless compound found in plants, particularly those of the Indigofera genus.  Upon hydrolysis, it yields indoxyl, which then oxidizes to form indigo, a blue dye. |例句：The indican content of the indigo plant leaves varies depending on the growing conditions.  靛蓝植物叶片中的靛蓝原苷含量取决于生长条件。

The presence of indican in urine can indicate a problem with intestinal bacteria. 尿液中靛蓝原苷的存在可能表明肠道细菌存在问题。|近义词：indoxyl glucoside (化学术语)|反义词：无
