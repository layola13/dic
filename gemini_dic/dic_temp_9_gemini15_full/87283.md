USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like "What" in the example.  It modifies verbs, describing *how* an action is performed.


Example Sentence 1:
The professor approached the ancient manuscript hermeneutically, carefully considering the cultural and historical context. 教授用解释学的方法研究古代手稿，仔细考虑了文化和历史背景。

We must engage with this complex issue hermeneutically, acknowledging the multiple perspectives involved. 我们必须用解释学的方法处理这个复杂的问题，承认所涉及的多种观点。


PHRASE->
There aren't common phrases directly using "hermeneutically."  Its usage is primarily within academic and theological discussions.  Phrases involving related concepts might include:
