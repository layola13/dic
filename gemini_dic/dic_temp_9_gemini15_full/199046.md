significa:/sɪɡˈnɪfɪkə/ | n. |意义，含义，重要性 |The significance of the discovery is still unknown.  这个发现的意义仍然未知。|近义词：meaning, importance, implication |反义词：insignificance, triviality


英文解析：Significance refers to the importance or meaning of something.  It can be the implication of an event or the weight attached to a particular action or statement.


例句：

The significance of this event is far-reaching.  这件事的影响深远。

He failed to grasp the significance of her words. 他没有理解她话语的含义。


词源:  From Latin *significare "to make a sign, signify," from signum "sign" + -ficare, suffix forming verbs from nouns.


用法说明:

一般现在时:
The event holds great significance. = 这件事具有重大意义。
This finding has little significance. = 这个发现意义不大。


一般过去时:
The discovery held great significance for scientists. = 这一发现对科学家们具有重大意义。
His words carried little significance at the time. = 当时他的话没有什么意义。


现在完成时:
The research has revealed the significance of the problem. = 研究已经揭示了这个问题的重要性。
Scientists have yet to fully understand the significance of their findings. = 科学家们尚未完全理解他们发现的意义。


过去完成时:
They had not understood the significance of the warning. = 他们没有理解警告的意义。
By then, the significance of the event had become clear. = 到那时，事件的意义已经变得清晰了。


将来时:
This discovery will have great significance in the future. = 这一发现将在未来具有重大意义。
Future research will reveal the significance of these findings. = 未来的研究将揭示这些发现的意义。


虚拟语气:
If the significance were clearer, we could act accordingly. = 如果意义更清晰，我们可以采取相应的行动。
I wish I had understood the significance earlier. = 我希望我早点明白其中的意义。


被动语态:
The significance of the data was underestimated. = 数据的重要性被低估了。
The true significance is yet to be determined. = 真正的意义还有待确定。


疑问句:
What is the significance of this finding? = 这个发现的意义是什么？
What significance do these results hold? = 这些结果有什么意义？


否定句:
The results have no significance. = 结果没有意义。
The study failed to demonstrate any significance. = 这项研究未能显示任何意义。


PHRASE->
