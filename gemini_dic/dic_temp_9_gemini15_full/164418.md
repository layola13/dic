preendorsed: /ˌpriːɪnˈdɔːrst/ | adj. | 预先认可的，预先批准的 |  Already approved or endorsed beforehand.  This term implies an approval that occurred before a formal process or event.  It suggests a preemptive approval, often granted based on anticipation or prior knowledge. | The preendorsed checks were ready for distribution. 预先认可的支票已准备好分发。 The project received a preendorsed budget allocation. 该项目获得了预先批准的预算拨款。 |  approved, sanctioned, authorized | disapproved, rejected, denied | 形容词
