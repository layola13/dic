reappoint:/ˌriːəˈpɔɪnt/ | vt. |重新任命，再次委任 | To appoint someone to a position again.  |例句：The board decided to reappoint him as CEO. 董事会决定再次任命他为首席执行官。
例句：The president reappointed the same judge to the Supreme Court. 总统再次任命了同一位法官到最高法院。|近义词：reinstall, reappoint|反义词：dismiss, remove
