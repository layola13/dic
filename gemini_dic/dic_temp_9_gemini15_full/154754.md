persuasives:/pərˈsweɪsɪvz/| n.|有说服力的论据；令人信服的事物|Things that persuade; arguments or reasons that are effective in influencing someone's opinions or actions.|His arguments were persuasive, and I changed my mind.|近义词：arguments, reasons, inducements|反义词：disincentives, deterrents
