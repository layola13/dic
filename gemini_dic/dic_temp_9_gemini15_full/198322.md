shovelled:/ˈʃʌvld/ | v. | 铲；挖掘；大量地吃 | To move (earth, snow, etc.) with a shovel.  Also, to eat a large amount of something quickly. | 例句：He shovelled the snow from the driveway. 他用铲子铲走了车道上的雪。 He shovelled down his food. 他狼吞虎咽地吃掉了他的食物。 | 近义词：dug, scooped,  pitched (for snow) | 反义词： piled, heaped (opposite action)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shovels snow every winter. = 他每年冬天都铲雪。
She shovels the gravel into the wheelbarrow. = 她用铲子把碎石铲进独轮车里。

一般过去时:
They shovelled the path yesterday. = 他们昨天铲了路。
The children shovelled the sand into the bucket. = 孩子们把沙子铲进桶里。


现在完成时:
I have shovelled all the snow from the sidewalk. = 我已经把人行道上的雪都铲干净了。
He has shovelled a mountain of food. = 他已经吃了一大堆食物。


过去完成时:
She had shovelled the snow before the storm hit. = 在暴风雪来临之前，她已经把雪铲掉了。
They had shovelled the dirt into the hole. = 他们已经把土铲进洞里了。


将来时:
We will shovel the driveway tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上会铲车道。
He will shovel the grain into the silo. = 他会把谷物铲进筒仓里。


虚拟语气:
If I had a shovel, I would shovel the snow. = 如果我有一把铲子，我就会铲雪。
I wish I had shovelled the snow earlier. = 我真希望我早点把雪铲掉。


被动语态:
The snow was shovelled by the city workers. = 雪是由城市工人铲掉的。
The sand was shovelled into the truck. = 沙子被铲进了卡车。


疑问句:

否定句:

She hasn't shovelled the path yet. = 她还没铲路。
