non-mutual:/ˌnɑːnˈmjuːtʃuəl/| adj. |非相互的，单向的|Not involving mutual feelings, actions, or obligations; one-sided.  It describes a relationship or situation where the feelings, actions, or obligations are not reciprocated.|The relationship between the boss and the employee was non-mutual; the boss expected loyalty but offered little in return.  老板和雇员之间的关系是非相互的；老板期望忠诚，但回报甚少。|近义词：one-sided, unilateral, reciprocal (反义词)|反义词：mutual, reciprocal
