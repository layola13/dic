overgo:/ˌoʊvərˈɡoʊ/ | v. |超过，胜过；经历；仔细检查| to surpass or exceed; to undergo or experience; to examine thoroughly | 例句：His achievements overgo those of his predecessors. 他的成就超过了他的前辈。 |近义词：surpass, exceed, undergo, review |反义词：undergo (in the sense of not experiencing something), neglect

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project overgoes its initial budget. = 这个项目超过了最初的预算。
His skills overgo mine. = 他的技能超过我的。

一般过去时:
The patient overwent a difficult surgery.  = 病人经历了一次艰难的手术。
The committee overwent the proposal carefully. = 委员会仔细审查了该提案。

现在完成时:
She has overgone many hardships in her life. = 她一生经历了许多苦难。
We have overgone the documents and found no errors. = 我们仔细检查了文件，没有发现错误。

过去完成时:
He had overgone several setbacks before achieving success. = 他在取得成功之前经历了几次挫折。
They had overgone the data multiple times before presenting their findings. = 他们在提交研究结果之前多次仔细检查了数据。


将来时:
The team anticipates that the project will overgo its deadline. = 团队预计该项目将超过截止日期。
I believe the matter will overgo a thorough investigation. = 我相信此事将会经过彻底调查。

虚拟语气:
If the plan overwent expectations, we would celebrate. = 如果计划超出了预期，我们将庆祝。
I wish the movie had overgone my expectations. = 我希望这部电影能超出我的预期。

被动语态:
The proposal was overgone by the committee. = 该提案被委员会审查了。
The project's budget was overgone. = 项目预算超支了。


疑问句:
Does the performance overgo the expectations? = 表演是否超过了预期？
Will the cost overgo the budget? = 成本会超过预算吗？

否定句:
The results did not overgo our predictions. = 结果没有超出我们的预测。
His efforts did not overgo the challenges. = 他的努力没有克服挑战。


PHRASE->
overgo oneself:  = to surpass one's own previous achievements (超越自己)
overgo the limit: = to exceed a limit (超过限制)
overgo the bounds: = to go beyond the limits (超过界限)
overgo expectations: = to surpass expectations (超出预期)
overgo the difficulty: = to overcome the difficulty (克服困难)
