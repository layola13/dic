ok:/oʊˈkeɪ/ | int. adj. adv. | 好，可以，行；不错的，令人满意的；好地，令人满意地 |  An expression of assent, agreement, or acceptance.  It can also mean "satisfactory" or "all right". |例句：

Okay, let's start. = 好吧，我们开始吧。

Okay, I understand. = 好吧，我明白了。


近义词： alright, all right, fine, good, yes


反义词： no, unacceptable, bad


USAGE->

一般现在时:
It's okay to leave now. = 现在离开是可以的。
Everything is okay. = 一切都很好。


一般过去时:
It was okay yesterday. = 昨天还好。
Everything was okay. = 一切都好。


现在完成时:
It has been okay so far. = 到目前为止还好。
Everything has been okay. = 一切都很好。


过去完成时:
It had been okay until then. = 直到那时为止还好。
Everything had been okay until the accident. = 直到事故发生前一切都很顺利。


将来时:
It will be okay. = 一切都会好起来的。
Everything will be okay.  = 一切都会好起来的。


虚拟语气:
It would be okay if you helped me. = 如果你帮我一下就好了。
I wish it were okay. = 我希望一切安好。


被动语态:
It's okay to be left alone. = 被独自留下也没关系。
The problem was okayed by the manager. = 这个问题得到了经理的批准。(okay 作动词使用)


疑问句:
Is everything okay? = 一切都好吗？
Is it okay if I leave early? = 我早点离开可以吗？


否定句:
It's not okay to cheat. = 作弊是不对的。
It wasn't okay what he did. = 他所做的事情是不对的。


PHRASE->

Okay then = 那么好吧
Okay, bye = 好吧，再见
That's okay = 没关系
Not okay = 不行
It's okay with me = 我同意
