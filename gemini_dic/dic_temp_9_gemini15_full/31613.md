chastises:/ˈtʃæstɪzɪz/| vt.|惩戒；责备；批评|To reprimand or punish severely; to criticize harshly.|例句：The teacher chastised the student for his disruptive behavior. 老师因学生扰乱课堂秩序而惩罚了他。|例句：The critic chastised the author for the lack of originality in his novel. 批评家严厉批评了作者小说缺乏原创性。|近义词：rebuke, reprimand, scold, punish|反义词：praise, commend, reward|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher chastises students who misbehave. = 老师惩罚那些行为不端的学生。
He often chastises his children for their bad habits. = 他经常因孩子们的坏习惯而责备他们。

一般过去时:
My father chastised me for breaking the vase. = 我父亲因为打破花瓶而惩罚了我。
She chastised him for his careless mistake. = 她因他粗心的错误而责备了他。


现在完成时:
The principal has chastised several students this week. = 本周校长已经惩罚了几名学生。
She has chastised him repeatedly for his lateness. = 她已经多次因为他的迟到而责备他了。

过去完成时:
The coach had chastised the team for their poor performance before the game. = 教练在比赛前因球队的糟糕表现而责备了球队。
He had chastised her many times for the same offense. = 他已经多次因为同样的罪过而责备她了。

将来时:
The judge will chastise the defendant for perjury. = 法官将因伪证罪而惩罚被告。
I know he will chastise me if I don't finish my homework. = 我知道如果我不完成作业，他会责备我。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't chastise him so harshly. = 如果我是你，我不会这么严厉地责备他。
I wish I hadn't chastised her so severely. = 我真希望我没有那么严厉地责备她。

被动语态:
The student was chastised for cheating on the exam. = 学生因考试作弊而受到惩罚。
He was chastised publicly for his misconduct. = 他因不当行为而受到公开谴责。


疑问句:
Will the teacher chastise the students for their noise? = 老师会因为学生的吵闹而惩罚他们吗？
Did your parents chastise you often when you were a child? = 你小时候父母经常责备你吗？


否定句:
The boss did not chastise the employee for the minor error. = 老板没有因为这个小错误而责备员工。
I will not chastise you for this mistake, but don't repeat it. = 我不会因为这个错误而责备你，但不要再犯了。
