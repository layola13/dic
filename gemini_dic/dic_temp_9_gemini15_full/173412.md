quasi-endlessly:/ˈkwɑːziˈɛndlɪsli/| adv. |几乎无限地，近乎无限地 |  Describes something that continues for a very long time, almost without end, though not truly infinitely. It implies a sense of near-infinity or protracted duration.  |The game seemed to continue quasi-endlessly.|近义词：almost endlessly, nearly infinitely, virtually endlessly |反义词：briefly, shortly, finitely
