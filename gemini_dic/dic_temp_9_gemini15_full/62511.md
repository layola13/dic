endows:/ɪnˈdaʊz/| vt.|赋予；捐赠；使具有|To provide with a quality, ability, or asset.|The school endows its students with a strong work ethic. 学校培养学生强烈的职业道德。|近义词：grant, bestow, equip|反义词：deprive, divest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The university endows scholarships to deserving students. = 大学为有资格的学生提供奖学金。
This position endows him with considerable authority. = 这个职位赋予他相当大的权力。

一般过去时:
The king endowed the church with vast lands. = 国王将大片土地捐赠给教堂。
She endowed her daughter with a generous inheritance. = 她给女儿留下了丰厚的遗产。

现在完成时:
The foundation has endowed several hospitals with new equipment. = 该基金会已为几家医院捐赠了新设备。
His education has endowed him with exceptional skills. = 他的教育赋予了他非凡的技能。

过去完成时:
The philanthropist had endowed the museum with a priceless collection of art before his death. = 这位慈善家在他去世前已将一件无价的艺术收藏捐赠给了博物馆。
They had endowed the project with sufficient funding to ensure its success. = 他们已经为该项目提供了足够的资金以确保其成功。

将来时:
The government will endow the research project with substantial funding. = 政府将为该研究项目提供大量资金。
He will endow his son with his entire estate upon his death. = 他将在去世后将全部遗产留给他的儿子。


虚拟语气:
If the government endowed the arts more generously, we would see more artistic innovation. = 如果政府更慷慨地资助艺术，我们将看到更多艺术创新。
I wish they had endowed the library with more modern technology. = 我希望他们能为图书馆配备更现代化的技术。

被动语态:
The hospital was endowed with new medical equipment by a generous donor. = 医院得到了一位慷慨捐赠者的捐赠，获得了新的医疗设备。
The students were endowed with a strong sense of responsibility by their teachers. = 学生们受到了老师的培养，具有强烈的责任感。

疑问句:
Does this position endow the employee with decision-making power? = 这个职位是否赋予员工决策权？
What qualities does this experience endow you with? = 这段经历赋予你什么品质？

否定句:
The company did not endow its employees with sufficient training. = 公司没有为员工提供足够的培训。
The government failed to endow the project with adequate resources. = 政府未能为该项目提供足够的资源。
