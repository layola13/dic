indispensables:/ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbəlz/| n.|不可缺少之物，必需品|Things that are absolutely necessary;  essential items or people.|The tools are indispensable for the job. 这些工具对于这项工作是必不可少的。|近义词：essentials, necessities, must-haves|反义词：unnecessary, superfluous, nonessential


USAGE->
一般现在时:
These tools are indispensable for the project. = 这些工具对项目来说是必不可少的。
Food and water are indispensable for survival. = 食物和水对生存是必不可少的。

一般过去时:
His expertise was indispensable to the success of the mission. = 他的专业知识对任务的成功至关重要。
The map proved indispensable during our hike. = 在我们的徒步旅行中地图证明是必不可少的。

现在完成时:
His contributions have been indispensable to the company's growth. = 他的贡献对公司的发展至关重要。
Her support has been indispensable throughout the difficult times. = 在艰难时期，她的支持至关重要。

过去完成时:
The old equipment had been indispensable before the new machines arrived. = 在新机器到来之前，旧设备是必不可少的。
His advice had been indispensable in making the decision. = 他的建议在做决定时至关重要。

将来时:
Reliable transportation will be indispensable for our trip. = 可靠的交通工具对我们的旅行将是必不可少的。
A good translator will be indispensable for the international conference. = 对于国际会议来说，一位优秀的翻译将是必不可少的。

虚拟语气:
If we had the indispensable equipment, we could finish the project earlier. = 如果我们有必不可少的设备，我们就能早点完成项目。
I wish I had the indispensable skills to solve this problem. = 我希望我有解决这个问题的必不可少的技能。

被动语态:
The software is considered indispensable by many users. = 许多用户认为该软件是必不可少的。
The plan was deemed indispensable by the committee. = 该计划被委员会认为是必不可少的。

疑问句:
Are these tools indispensable for the experiment? = 这些工具对于实验来说是必不可少的吗？
Is his presence indispensable for the meeting? = 他出席会议是必不可少的吗？

否定句:
That feature is not indispensable; it can be removed. = 该功能并非必不可少；可以将其移除。
The extra decorations are not indispensable for the party. = 额外的装饰对于派对来说并非必不可少。
