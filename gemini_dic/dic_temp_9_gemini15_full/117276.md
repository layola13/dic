mechanizations:/ˌmɛkənaɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n.|机械化；机械操作；自动化|The process of introducing machines or automated systems into an operation, industry, or process.  It often refers to the replacement of human or animal labor with machinery.|例句：The mechanizations of agriculture led to increased food production.  农业机械化导致粮食产量增加。

The mechanizations in the factory improved efficiency. 工厂的机械化提高了效率。|近义词：automation, automation, industrialization|反义词：manual labor, handwork
