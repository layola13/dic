siliqua:/ˈsɪlɪkwə/| n. |豆荚；银币；少量|A siliqua is a pod, especially one containing seeds.  Historically, it also referred to a small Roman silver coin.  The word is used figuratively to denote a small amount or particle.|例句：The gardener carefully collected the siliquae from the bean plants.  园丁小心地收集豆荚里的种子。|近义词：pod, husk, capsule, carpel|反义词：N/A
