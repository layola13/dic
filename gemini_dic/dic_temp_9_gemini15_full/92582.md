I've:/aɪv/| pron.|我已经，我曾|I've is a contraction of "I have," used as an auxiliary verb in the present perfect tense.  It indicates an action completed in the past with a connection to the present.|I've finished my work. = 我已经完成了我的工作。
I've lived here for five years. = 我在这里住了五年了。|  |  |pronoun
