provisions:/prəˈvɪʒ(ə)nz/ | n. |供应品；条款；准备 | Supplies of food, drink, etc., especially for a journey or a particular period.  The terms of a contract or agreement.  The act of providing or supplying something. |例句：We need to make provisions for the journey. 我们需要为旅途准备物资。 The contract includes provisions for termination. 合同包括终止条款。|近义词：supplies, stipulations, arrangements |反义词：lack, absence, deprivation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company makes provisions for employee retirement. = 公司为员工退休提供资金。
These provisions cover all eventualities. = 这些条款涵盖所有情况。


一般过去时:
They made ample provisions for the winter. = 他们为冬天准备了充足的物资。
The treaty made provisions for peaceful resolution of disputes. = 条约规定了和平解决争端的条款。


现在完成时:
The government has made provisions for disaster relief. = 政府已为救灾做好了准备。
We have included provisions for future expansion in the plan. = 我们在计划中纳入了未来扩张的条款。


过去完成时:
They had made provisions for their children's education before they died. = 他们去世前已为子女的教育做好了准备。
The agreement had already made provisions for arbitration. = 该协议已经规定了仲裁条款。


将来时:
We will make provisions for unforeseen circumstances. = 我们将为不可预见的情况做好准备。
The new law will make provisions for stronger environmental protection. = 新法律将规定更严格的环境保护措施。


虚拟语气:
If we had made better provisions, we would not be in this situation. = 如果我们准备得更好，就不会陷入这种境地。
I wish we had made provisions for unexpected guests. = 我希望我们为不速之客做好了准备。


被动语态:
Provisions were made for the refugees. = 为难民提供了供应品。
Adequate provisions were not made for the event. = 此事件的准备不足。


疑问句:
What provisions have you made for the trip? = 你为这次旅行准备了什么？
What provisions are included in the contract? = 合同中包含哪些条款？


否定句:
They made no provisions for bad weather. = 他们没有为恶劣天气做任何准备。
The contract did not make provisions for liability. = 合同没有规定责任条款。


PHRASE->
make provisions for = 为...做准备
provision of services = 服务提供
provision of information = 信息提供
emergency provisions = 应急物资
legal provisions = 法律条款

词源:
provisions 源自古法语 provision，意为“供应，提供”，最终源自拉丁语 providere，意为“预见，提供”。
