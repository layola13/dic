misorganizing: /ˌmɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |  组织混乱；安排不当 |  The act of organizing something badly or incorrectly, leading to inefficiency or disorder. It implies a lack of planning, system, or effectiveness in the organization process. | 例句：Misorganizing the files led to a significant delay in the project.  = 文件的混乱安排导致项目严重延误。  The misorganizing of the conference schedule caused considerable confusion. = 会议日程安排混乱造成了相当大的困惑。| 近义词：disorganizing, cluttering, muddling, jumbling | 反义词：organizing, systematizing, streamlining
