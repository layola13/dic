misinterpreting:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɜːrpətɪŋ/| v.|误解，曲解|To understand or explain incorrectly.|He was misinterpreting the instructions. 他误解了说明。|近义词：misunderstand, misconstrue|反义词：understand, correctly interpret|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are misinterpreting the data. = 他们在误解数据。
I am misinterpreting your intentions. = 我误解了你的意图。


一般过去时:
He misinterpreted the map and got lost. = 他误读了地图，迷路了。
The journalist misinterpreted the politician's statement. = 记者误解了这位政治家的声明。


现在完成时:
I have misinterpreted your silence as disapproval. = 我一直误解你的沉默为反对。
She has misinterpreted the signs and symptoms of the disease. = 她误解了该疾病的体征和症状。


过去完成时:
We had misinterpreted the clues, leading to the wrong conclusion. = 我们误解了线索，导致了错误的结论。
The detective realized he had misinterpreted the evidence. = 侦探意识到他误解了证据。


将来时:
They will misinterpret my actions. = 他们会误解我的行为。
He will probably misinterpret the email. = 他可能会误解这封邮件。


虚拟语气:
If I had not misinterpreted his words, I would have reacted differently. = 如果我没有误解他的话，我会采取不同的反应。
I wish I hadn't misinterpreted her expression. = 我真希望我没有误解她的表情。


被动语态:
The message was misinterpreted by the recipient. = 这条信息被收信人误解了。
His words were misinterpreted by the audience. = 他的话被听众误解了。


疑问句:
Are they misinterpreting the instructions? = 他们是否误解了说明？
Could I have misinterpreted your meaning? = 我可能误解了你的意思吗？


否定句:
We did not misinterpret the contract. = 我们没有误解合同。
I hope I am not misinterpreting your words. = 我希望我没有误解你的话。


PHRASE->
misinterpreting signals = 误读信号
misinterpreting the situation = 误解情况
misinterpreting body language = 误解肢体语言
misinterpreting someone's actions = 误解某人的行为
misinterpreting the facts = 误解事实
