multielectrode:/ˌmʌltiˈɛləktrəʊd/| n. |多电极|A multielectrode is a device or system that uses multiple electrodes to record or stimulate electrical activity.  It's often used in neuroscience, biomedical engineering, and other fields requiring the simultaneous measurement or manipulation of electrical signals from multiple points. | Example: Researchers used a multielectrode array to record neural activity in the brain.  研究人员使用多电极阵列记录大脑中的神经活动。|近义词：multichannel electrode array, electrode array |反义词：single electrode
