footnote:/ˈfʊtˌnoʊt/ | n. |脚注，尾注 |A note of explanation or reference placed at the bottom of a page.  |例句：The footnote clarified the meaning of the obscure word.  脚注解释了那个模糊词语的意思。|近义词：endnote, annotation |反义词：main text, body
