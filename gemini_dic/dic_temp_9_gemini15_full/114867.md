/ˈmæməndɒm/ | n. |巨兽统治；猛犸象时代；庞然大物的世界|The reign or dominion of mammoths; a world or era dominated by mammoths or something similarly huge and powerful.  The term evokes a sense of immense size, power, and perhaps even a bygone era.|例句：The age of dinosaurs could be considered a kind of "saurodom," while the Pleistocene epoch was a period of mammondom. = 恐龙时代可以被认为是一种“蜥蜴统治”，而更新世时期是猛犸象统治的时期。|近义词：megadom, behemothdom, reign, dominion|反义词： insignificance, triviality
