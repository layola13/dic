misreferencing:/ˌmɪsˈrɛfərənsɪŋ/ | n. | 误引用，错误引用 | The act of incorrectly citing or referencing a source.  This can involve inaccuracies in the bibliographic information, misrepresenting the author's intent, or failing to properly attribute the source material. | Example:  The student's essay was marked down due to misreferencing of several key sources.  学生的论文因为错误引用了几个主要来源而被扣分了。|近义词：misquotation, misattribution, incorrect citation | 反义词：accurate referencing, proper citation | 名词
