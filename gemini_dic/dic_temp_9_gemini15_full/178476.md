reconsult:/ˌriːkənˈsʌlt/| vt.|再次咨询；重新商议|To consult again; to seek advice or information again.  |例句：The doctor asked me to reconsult him after a week.  医生让我一周后再次复诊。

例句：The committee decided to reconsult with the experts before making a final decision. 委员会决定在做出最终决定之前再次咨询专家。


近义词：re-examine, review, reconsider|反义词：ignore, disregard|词性:verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reconsults his doctor regularly. = 他定期复诊。
They reconsult the map before continuing their journey. = 他们在继续旅程之前再次查看地图。


一般过去时:
She reconsulted her lawyer about the contract. = 她就合同再次咨询了她的律师。
The team reconsulted their data to verify their findings. = 该团队再次查阅了他们的数据以验证他们的发现。


现在完成时:
He has reconsulted the specialist three times this year. = 他今年已经三次复诊了专家。
They have reconsulted the blueprints and found a crucial error. = 他们已经重新查看了蓝图，发现了一个关键错误。


过去完成时:
The client had reconsulted several firms before choosing us. = 客户在我们之前已经咨询过几家公司。
She had reconsulted her notes before giving her presentation. = 她做演讲之前已经再次查阅了她的笔记。


将来时:
I will reconsult the manual if I encounter any problems. = 如果遇到任何问题，我将再次查阅手册。
They will reconsult the stakeholders before implementing the new policy. = 他们将在实施新政策之前再次咨询利益相关者。


虚拟语气:
If I had reconsulted the map, I wouldn't have gotten lost. = 如果我再次查看地图，我就不会迷路了。
It would have been wise to reconsult the expert before proceeding. = 在继续之前再次咨询专家本来是明智的。


被动语态:
The plan was reconsulted by the board before approval. = 该计划在批准之前被董事会重新审议。
The documents were reconsulted numerous times during the investigation. = 在调查期间，文件被多次重新查阅。


疑问句:
Should we reconsult the architect about the design? = 我们应该就设计再次咨询建筑师吗？
Will they reconsult the witnesses before filing charges? = 他们会在提起诉讼之前再次咨询证人吗？


否定句:
They did not reconsult the historical records. = 他们没有重新查阅历史记录。
We should not reconsult the old data; it's unreliable. = 我们不应该重新查阅旧数据；它不可靠。


PHRASE->
