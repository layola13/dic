epicurish: /ˌɛpɪˈkʊrɪʃ/ | adj. | 耽于享乐的；追求享乐的 |  Characterized by or given to the pursuit of pleasure; hedonistic.  |  He led an epicurish lifestyle, indulging in fine wines, gourmet foods, and luxurious travel. 他过着放荡享乐的生活，沉溺于美酒佳肴和奢华的旅行。 | 近义词：hedonistic, pleasure-seeking, voluptuous | 反义词：ascetic, austere, self-denying |  形容词

词源:
