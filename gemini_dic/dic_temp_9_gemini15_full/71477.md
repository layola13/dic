flamingos:/fləˈmɪŋɡoʊz/|/ˈflæmɪŋɡoʊz/| n.|火烈鸟|A flamingo is a type of wading bird with long legs and a long neck, known for its bright pink or reddish plumage.  Flamingos are often found in large flocks in shallow water. |Example: We saw a flock of flamingos standing in the shallow lake. 我们看到一群火烈鸟站在浅水湖里。  The flamingos' bright pink feathers stood out against the blue water. 火烈鸟鲜艳的粉色羽毛在蓝色的水面上格外醒目。|近义词：bird, wading bird|反义词：无
