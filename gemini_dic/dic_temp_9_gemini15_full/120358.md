minimized:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪzd/| v.|最小化；使减到最小；轻描淡写|To reduce something to the smallest possible amount or degree; to make something seem less important than it is.|例句：The company minimized its losses by cutting costs. 公司通过削减成本将损失降到了最低。|例句：He minimized the impact of his mistake. 他轻描淡写地描述了他的错误。|近义词：reduce, lessen, downplay|反义词：maximize, exaggerate, amplify|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They minimize their expenses. = 他们尽量减少开支。
We minimize the risks involved. = 我们尽量减少所涉及的风险。

一般过去时:
She minimized the damage. = 她将损失降到了最低。
He minimized the importance of the issue. = 他轻描淡写了这个问题的重要性。

现在完成时:
They have minimized their environmental impact. = 他们已经将对环境的影响降到了最低。
We have minimized the number of errors. = 我们已经将错误的数量降到了最低。

过去完成时:
She had minimized her involvement in the project. = 她已经尽量减少了她对项目的参与。
They had minimized the risk before the launch. = 他们在发布之前已经尽量减少了风险。

将来时:
We will minimize disruption during the renovation. = 我们将在装修期间尽量减少中断。
They will minimize the use of plastic. = 他们将尽量减少塑料的使用。

虚拟语气:
If we minimized costs, we could achieve our goal. = 如果我们降低成本，我们就能实现目标。
I wish they had minimized the noise. = 我希望他们能尽量减少噪音。

被动语态:
The risks were minimized by careful planning. = 通过仔细的计划，风险被降到了最低。
The impact of the storm was minimized by the emergency services. = 紧急服务部门将风暴的影响降到了最低。

疑问句:
How can we minimize the impact of climate change? = 我们如何才能尽量减少气候变化的影响？
Did they minimize the potential losses? = 他们是否尽量减少了潜在损失？

否定句:
They did not minimize the seriousness of the situation. = 他们没有轻描淡写局势的严重性。
We cannot minimize the importance of education. = 我们不能低估教育的重要性。
