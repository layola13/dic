harappa: /həˈrɑːpə/ | n. | 哈拉帕 | Harappa is the name of an ancient city in present-day Pakistan, and also refers to the archaeological culture associated with it.  The Harappan civilization, also known as the Indus Valley Civilization, flourished during the Bronze Age.  The city is renowned for its sophisticated urban planning, advanced sanitation systems, and standardized weights and measures.  Excavations at Harappa have yielded numerous artifacts, providing valuable insights into the life and culture of this ancient civilization. |  The excavations at Harappa revealed a highly developed urban center. = 哈拉帕的挖掘揭示了一个高度发达的城市中心。

The discovery of Harappa revolutionized our understanding of ancient South Asia. = 哈拉帕的发现彻底改变了我们对古代南亚的认识。 | 近义词：Indus Valley Civilization (印度河谷文明) | 反义词：无
