fulcrate:/ˈfʌlkreɪt/| n. |支点，支柱|A fulcrum is the support on which a lever rests or is pivoted.  It's the point around which a lever rotates.  The fulcrum is essential for a lever to work effectively, enabling the application of force to move a load.|例句：The fulcrum of the seesaw was placed exactly in the center.  跷跷板的支点正好放在中央。

The rock served as a fulcrum for the lever. 这块石头充当了杠杆的支点。|近义词：pivot, support, base|反义词：N/A
