judgements:/ˈdʒʌdʒmənts/ | n. | 判断；判决；意见 | The act of forming an opinion or making a decision about something.  It can refer to a legal ruling, a critical assessment, or a general opinion. |例句：The judge's judgements were fair and impartial.  法官的判决公正无私。

例句：Her judgements about art were often insightful. 她对艺术的见解往往很有见地。


近义词：decisions, verdicts, opinions, assessments, evaluations | 反义词：N/A (no direct antonyms, but could be contrasted with things like "ignorance" or "acceptance" depending on context) | 词性: noun
