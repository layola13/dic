imbody:/ɪmˈbɒdi/| vt.|体现，具体化，包含|To represent or be a perfect example of a quality, idea, or feeling.  |例句：Her paintings embody the spirit of the age. 她的画作体现了那个时代的精神。|例句：The novel embodies many of the author's own experiences. 这部小说包含了作者自身的许多经历。|近义词：embody, represent, personify, exemplify|反义词：abstract, disembody


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He embodies courage. = 他体现了勇气。
This building embodies modern architecture. = 这座建筑体现了现代建筑风格。

一般过去时:
The sculpture embodied the ideal of beauty. = 这座雕塑体现了美的理想。
Her performance embodied the character's inner turmoil. = 她的表演体现了人物内心的骚动。

现在完成时:
His actions have embodied selflessness. = 他的行为体现了无私。
The film has embodied the struggles of the working class. = 这部电影体现了工人的挣扎。

过去完成时:
The artist had embodied his vision in the painting. = 艺术家已将他的理念融入绘画中。
They had embodied their principles in their actions. = 他们已经将他们的原则体现在行动中。

将来时:
The new design will embody sustainability. = 新的设计将体现可持续性。
The play will embody the themes of love and loss. = 这出戏将体现爱与失落的主題。

虚拟语气:
If he embodied honesty, people would trust him more. = 如果他体现诚实，人们会更信任他。
I wish the project embodied more innovation. = 我希望这个项目能体现更多创新。

被动语态:
Courage was embodied in his actions. = 勇气体现在他的行动中。
Modern design principles are embodied in this building. = 现代设计原则体现在这座建筑中。

疑问句:
Does this design embody the company's values? = 这个设计体现了公司的价值观吗？
What does this symbol embody? = 这个符号象征着什么？

否定句:
His actions did not embody the spirit of teamwork. = 他的行为没有体现团队精神。
The painting did not embody the artist's intended message. = 这幅画没有体现艺术家想要表达的信息。


PHRASE->

embody the essence of = 体现...的精髓
embody a concept = 体现一个概念
embody a principle = 体现一个原则
