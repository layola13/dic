priests:/priːsts/| n.|牧师；僧侣|A priest is a person, typically a man, who has been ordained to perform religious duties and ceremonies, especially in the Christian, Jewish, or Muslim religions.  They often lead religious services, provide spiritual guidance, and perform rituals.|Example: The priests led the congregation in prayer.  牧师带领会众祈祷。|Example:  Many priests dedicate their lives to serving their communities. 许多牧师致力于为他们的社区服务。|近义词：clergymen, ministers, rabbis, imams|反义词：layperson, parishioner
