dhansak:/ˈdænˌsæk/| n. | धनसक (印地语)| A lentil-based curry from South Asia, typically containing lentils, vegetables, and meat (often chicken or lamb). It is known for its sweet and sour flavor profile, often achieved through the use of tamarind and sugar.  |例句：I'm making dhansak for dinner tonight. 今晚我做 धनसक 当晚餐。
