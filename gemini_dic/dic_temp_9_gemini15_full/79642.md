glossing:/ˈɡlɒsɪŋ/| /ˈɡlɑːsɪŋ/ | v. |注释；润色；掩饰；镀光|The act of providing brief explanations or translations of words or phrases, often within a text. It can also refer to making something appear superficially attractive or improving its appearance.  |例句：The professor is glossing the difficult passages in the text for the students. 教授正在为学生们注释文本中的难句。

例句：He glossed over the important details in his report. 他在他的报告中轻描淡写地带过了重要的细节。

近义词：annotating, explaining, elucidating, embellishing, varnishing

反义词：obscuring, concealing, downplaying

词性：动词
