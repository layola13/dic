inspect:/ɪnˈspekt/ | vt. | 检查；视察；检阅 | To examine something carefully in order to check its quality, condition, or to discover any problems. |例句：The engineer inspected the bridge for damage.  工程师检查桥梁是否有损坏。|例句：The teacher inspected the students' homework. 老师检查了学生的作业。|近义词：examine, survey, review |反义词：ignore, neglect |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The manager inspects the factory every week. = 经理每周检查工厂一次。
She inspects the quality of the products before they are shipped. =  她在产品发货前检查产品的质量。

一般过去时:
They inspected the building after the fire. = 火灾后，他们检查了建筑物。
The police inspected the car for fingerprints. = 警察检查了汽车上的指纹。

现在完成时:
The authorities have inspected the mine for safety hazards. = 当局已经检查了矿井的安全隐患。
We have inspected all the equipment and found no problems. = 我们已经检查了所有设备，没有发现问题。

过去完成时:
They had inspected the aircraft before the flight. = 他们起飞前已经检查过飞机了。
The inspector had already inspected the warehouse before the shipment arrived. =  货物到达之前，检查员已经检查过仓库了。

将来时:
We will inspect the site tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上将视察现场。
The team will inspect the bridge for structural integrity. =  团队将检查桥梁的结构完整性。

虚拟语气:
If I were the manager, I would inspect the process more closely. = 如果我是经理，我会更仔细地检查流程。
It is essential that the goods be inspected before delivery. = 货物在交付前必须经过检查。

被动语态:
The building was inspected last week. = 这座建筑物上周进行了检查。
The products are inspected regularly for defects. = 产品定期检查是否有缺陷。

疑问句:
Will you inspect the documents before signing them? = 你会在签署文件之前检查它们吗？
Have they inspected the equipment yet? = 他们检查过设备了吗？

否定句:
He didn't inspect the goods properly. = 他没有好好检查货物。
They did not inspect the premises thoroughly. = 他们没有彻底检查场所。
