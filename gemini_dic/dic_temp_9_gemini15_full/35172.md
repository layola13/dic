clownship:/ˈklaʊnʃɪp/| n. |小丑行为；滑稽表演；傻气；愚蠢的行为|The act or behavior of a clown; buffoonery; foolishness.  It often involves exaggerated gestures, silly jokes, and generally ridiculous behavior intended to amuse.  |例句：His clownship during the serious meeting was highly inappropriate.  他的傻气行为在严肃的会议上非常不合适。

例句：The comedian's clownship was well-received by the audience.  这个喜剧演员的小丑表演受到了观众的欢迎。


近义词：buffoonery, tomfoolery, horseplay, antics, silliness

反义词：seriousness, gravity, solemnity, dignity
