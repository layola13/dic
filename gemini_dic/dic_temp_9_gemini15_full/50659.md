derivations:/ˌdɛrɪˈveɪʃənz/ | n. |派生词；衍生；起源 |The process of forming new words from existing ones;  the origin or development of something from a source.  It can refer to the process itself, or the resulting words or concepts. |例句：The derivations of many English words can be traced back to Latin.  许多英语单词的派生可以追溯到拉丁语。

例句：Understanding the derivations of words helps to understand their meanings better. 理解单词的派生有助于更好地理解它们的含义。


近义词：origins, etymologies, developments, offshoots


反义词：sources (in the sense of original, undifferentiated material)


词性:名词
