defiling:/dɪˈfaɪlɪŋ/| v.|玷污；亵渎；弄脏|To make something unclean or impure, especially something sacred or respected; to violate or desecrate.  |例句：The vandals defiled the ancient monument.  破坏者亵渎了古迹。

例句：The muddy boots defiled the freshly cleaned floor. 泥泞的靴子弄脏了刚擦干净的地板。

近义词：pollute, contaminate, profane, desecrate, violate

反义词：purify, cleanse, sanctify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They defile the river with industrial waste. = 他们用工业废料污染河流。
He defiles his body with drugs. = 他用毒品糟蹋自己的身体。

一般过去时:
The soldiers defiled the village. = 士兵们玷污了村庄。
She felt defiled by his actions. = 她感到被他行为玷污了。


现在完成时:
They have defiled the sacred ground. = 他们已经亵渎了圣地。
The scandal has defiled the reputation of the company. = 这场丑闻玷污了公司的声誉。


过去完成时:
The vandals had defiled the statue before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，破坏者已经亵渎了雕像。
They had defiled the temple with graffiti. = 他们已经在庙宇上涂鸦，玷污了它。


将来时:
They will defile the land with their toxic waste. = 他们将用有毒废物污染土地。
He will defile the memory of his ancestors.  = 他将会玷污他祖先的记忆。


虚拟语气:
If they defiled the temple, they would be punished severely. = 如果他们亵渎了寺庙，他们将受到严厉的惩罚。
I wish they hadn't defiled the river. = 我真希望他们没有污染这条河。


被动语态:
The river was defiled by the factory's discharge. = 河流被工厂的排放物污染了。
The reputation of the politician was defiled by the accusations. = 这位政治家的名誉被指控玷污了。


疑问句:
Did they defile the sacred site? = 他们是否亵渎了圣地？
Has the environment been defiled by human activity? = 人类活动是否已经污染了环境？


否定句:
They did not defile the tomb. = 他们没有亵渎坟墓。
We will not defile our heritage. = 我们不会玷污我们的遗产。


PHRASE->
defile the environment = 污染环境

defile a sacred place = 亵渎圣地

词源:
From Old French *defouler, from Latin *defoulare, from de- + foulare "to trample, to press."  The meaning shifted over time from "to trample" to "to make foul or unclean."
