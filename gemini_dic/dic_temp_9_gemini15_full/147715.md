overpressure:/ˈoʊvərˌprɛʃər/|/n.|超压，超压状态|Excess pressure above a standard or normal pressure.  It often refers to pressure exceeding a safe or designed limit.  The context is crucial to understanding the specific meaning.|例句：The pipeline experienced a sudden overpressure, causing a rupture. 管道经历了突然的超压，导致破裂。|例句：The scuba diver suffered from overpressure during a rapid ascent. 潜水员在快速上升过程中遭受了超压。|近义词：excess pressure, high pressure, superpressure|反义词：underpressure, low pressure, vacuum
