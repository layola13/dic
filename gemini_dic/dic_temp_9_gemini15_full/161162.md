postglacial:/ˌpoʊstˈɡleɪʃəl/| adj.| 冰河期后的；冰川期后的 | Relating to the period of time after the last ice age.  The postglacial period is characterized by warming temperatures, melting glaciers, and rising sea levels. | Example: Postglacial rebound is a process where the land rises after the weight of a glacier is removed.  冰后回弹是一个在冰川重量移除后土地上升的过程。|近义词：post-ice age |反义词：glacial
