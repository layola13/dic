enwrap:/ɪnˈræp/| vt.| 包裹；缠绕；吸引；使陷入 | to cover or surround something completely | The vines enwrapped the old house.  藤蔓缠绕着老房子。  The fog enwrapped the mountain.  雾气笼罩着山峰。 | envelop, enclose, enfold | unravel, expose | 动词


USAGE->

The ivy enwraps the ancient walls. = 常春藤缠绕着古老的城墙。
