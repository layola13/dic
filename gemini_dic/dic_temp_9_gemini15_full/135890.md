nonprovocatively:/ˌnɒnprəˈvɒkətɪvli/ | adv. | 不激怒地；不挑衅地 | In a way that is not likely to cause anger or offense.  It describes actions or behavior that are intended to avoid provoking a negative reaction. | Example: He spoke nonprovocatively, hoping to de-escalate the situation. 他说话不激怒人，希望能缓和局势。|近义词：calmly, peacefully, gently, inoffensively |反义词：aggressively, provocatively, antagonistically
