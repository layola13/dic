clearing:/ˈklɪərɪŋ/ | n. v. | 清理；空地；澄清 |  The act of removing obstacles or obstructions; an open space in a forest; the act of making something clear or understandable. |例句：The clearing of the forest revealed a hidden stream.  森林的清理露出了隐藏的小溪。

The police are working on clearing up the crime scene.  警察正在努力清理犯罪现场。|近义词：cleanup, cleaning, elucidation |反义词：obstruction, confusion, obfuscation


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He clears his throat before speaking. = 他在说话前清了清嗓子。
She clears the table after dinner. = 晚餐后她收拾桌子。


一般过去时:
They cleared the path through the woods. = 他们清理了穿过树林的小路。
The storm cleared the air. = 暴风雨净化了空气。


现在完成时:
We have cleared the deck for the party. = 我们已经为聚会清理了甲板。
She has cleared her name of the accusations. = 她已经洗清了对她的指控。


过去完成时:
They had cleared the land before planting the crops. = 他们在种植庄稼之前已经清理了土地。
He had cleared the misunderstanding before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经消除了误解。


将来时:
We will clear the snow from the driveway tomorrow. = 我们明天将清理车道上的积雪。
They will clear the debt within a year. = 他们将在一年内还清债务。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would clear my desk. = 如果我有更多时间，我会整理我的办公桌。
I wish I could clear up this confusion. = 我希望我能消除这种混乱。


被动语态:
The forest is being cleared for development. = 森林正在被清理以用于开发。
The misunderstanding was cleared up quickly. = 误解很快就被澄清了。


疑问句:
Has the payment been cleared yet? = 付款已经结清了吗？
Will the path be cleared before the hike? = 在远足之前小路会被清理吗？


否定句:
He didn't clear the dishes after dinner. = 晚餐后他没有收拾盘子。
The explanation didn't clear up my doubts. = 解释并没有消除我的疑虑。


PHRASE->
