persist:/pərˈsɪst/| v. |坚持；持续；固执 |To continue to exist; to continue doing something despite difficulties |例句：He persisted in his efforts despite the setbacks. 他尽管遇到挫折，但仍然坚持努力。|近义词：continue, endure, persevere |反义词:cease, stop, quit |动词
