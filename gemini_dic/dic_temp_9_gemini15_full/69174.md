faxing:/ˈfæksɪŋ/| n. v.|传真；发送传真|The act of sending or receiving documents via fax machine.  It refers to the process of transmitting documents electronically over telephone lines.|例句：Faxing the document took only a few minutes. 传真文件只花了幾分鐘。|例句：She's faxing the contract to the client right now. 她現在正在把合同傳真給客戶。|近义词：transmitting, sending|反义词：receiving (in the context of faxing)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We fax documents regularly. = 我们定期传真文件。
He faxes the reports every Friday. = 他每个星期五都传真报告。

一般过去时:
I faxed the application yesterday. = 我昨天传真了申请表。
They faxed the order to the supplier. = 他们把订单传真给了供应商。

现在完成时:
She has faxed all the necessary forms. = 她已经传真了所有必要的表格。
We have faxed the contract to them twice. = 我们已经给他们传真了两次合同。

过去完成时:
He had faxed the report before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他已经传真了报告。
They had faxed the documents before leaving the office. = 他们离开办公室前已经传真了文件。

将来时:
I will fax you the details later. = 我稍后会把细节传真给你。
She will fax the information as soon as possible. = 她会尽快传真信息。

虚拟语气:
If I had a fax machine, I would fax the document. = 如果我有传真机，我会传真文件。
I wish I had faxed the report earlier. = 我希望我早点传真了报告。

被动语态:
The document was faxed to the wrong address. = 文件传真到了错误的地址。
The reports are being faxed at this moment. = 报告正在被传真。

疑问句:
Did you fax the documents? = 你传真文件了吗？
Have they faxed the information yet? = 他们已经传真信息了吗？

否定句:
I didn't fax the report. = 我没有传真报告。
They haven't faxed the contract yet. = 他们还没有传真合同。
