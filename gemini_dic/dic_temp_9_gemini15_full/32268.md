chide:/tʃaɪd/| vt.|责备；训斥；斥责|To scold or rebuke someone mildly.|The teacher chided the student for being late.老师因学生迟到而责备他。|rebuke, reprimand, scold|praise, commend|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chides his children frequently. = 他经常责备他的孩子们。
She chides him for his carelessness. = 她责备他的粗心大意。


一般过去时:
My mother chided me for breaking the vase. = 我妈妈因为我打破花瓶而责备我。
The boss chided the employee for his mistake. = 老板因员工犯错而责备他。


现在完成时:
He has chided her several times for the same mistake. = 他因为同样的错误已经责备她好几次了。
She has chided me for my lateness. = 她因为我的迟到已经责备过我了。


过去完成时:
She had chided him before he even finished explaining. = 在他甚至完成解释之前，她就责备了他。
The teacher had chided the class for their disruptive behavior. = 老师因为他们扰乱秩序的行为已经责备了全班。


将来时:
I will chide him if he does it again. = 如果他再这样做，我会责备他。
She will chide her son for his poor grades. = 她会因为儿子的糟糕成绩而责备他。


虚拟语气:
I would chide him if I were you. = 如果我是你，我会责备他。
If I had known, I would have chided him earlier. = 如果我知道的话，我会早点责备他。


被动语态:
He was chided for his behavior. = 他因为他的行为而受到责备。
The children were chided for making a mess. = 孩子们因为弄脏东西而受到责备。


疑问句:
Will you chide him for his actions? = 你会因为他的行为而责备他吗？
Did she chide you for being late? = 她因为你迟到而责备你了吗？


否定句:
I didn't chide him, I just gave him some advice. = 我没有责备他，我只是给他一些建议。
She didn't chide the children, she just explained the rules. = 她没有责备孩子们，只是解释了规则。


PHRASE->
chide someone gently = 轻声责备某人
chide someone severely = 严厉地责备某人
chide someone for their mistakes = 因为某人的错误而责备某人
chide someone into obedience = 训斥某人使其服从
chide someone about something = 因为某事而责备某人
