overprotectiveness:/ˌoʊvərprəˈtɛktɪvnəs/| n. |过度保护；溺爱|The excessive tendency to protect someone, often to the point of hindering their independence and development.  It can stem from various sources like anxiety, fear, or a desire to control.  Overprotectiveness can have negative consequences for the individual being protected, leading to a lack of self-reliance and difficulty navigating challenges. |例句：Her overprotectiveness prevented her son from developing essential life skills.  她的过度保护妨碍了儿子发展必要的生存技能。

例句：The parents' overprotectiveness stifled the child's creativity and independence.  父母的过度保护扼杀了孩子的创造力和独立性。


近义词：excessive protectiveness, over-solicitude, sheltering, coddling |反义词：neglect, unconcern, laissez-faire parenting


词性：名词
