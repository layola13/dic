canalize: /ˈkænəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使…入渠道；引导；集中；规范 | To direct or confine (something, typically a natural resource or a process) within channels or boundaries.  |例句：The government canalized the river to prevent flooding.  政府修建河道以防止洪水。

例句：They canalized their efforts to achieve a common goal. 他们集中精力以实现共同目标。


近义词：channel, direct, guide, regulate, constrain | 反义词：disperse, scatter, diffuse | 词性：动词


USAGE->

The government canalizes the river flow. = 政府引导河流的流向。
He canalizes his anger into productive work. = 他将愤怒转化为富有成效的工作。

一般过去时:
They canalized the surplus water into irrigation ditches. = 他们将多余的水引入灌溉沟渠。
The company canalized its resources into research and development. = 公司将其资源投入研发。
