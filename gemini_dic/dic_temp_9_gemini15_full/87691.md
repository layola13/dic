hexamethylenediamine: /ˌheksəˌmɛθɪlˈɛndiˌæmin/ | n. | 六亚甲基二胺 | Hexamethylenediamine is an organic compound with the formula H₂N(CH₂)₆NH₂. It is a diamine, meaning it contains two amine functional groups (-NH₂).  It is a colorless solid that is used primarily in the production of nylon 6,6. |  Hexamethylenediamine is a key ingredient in the production of nylon 6,6. The reaction of hexamethylenediamine with adipic acid forms the nylon 6,6 polymer. | 近义词: 1,6-diaminohexane | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, as it's a specific chemical compound) |  名词
