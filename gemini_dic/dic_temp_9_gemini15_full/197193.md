shapelessly:/ˈʃeɪpləslɪ/| adv.|不成形地；无定形地；混乱地|In a shapeless manner; lacking a definite form or structure.|The clay was worked shapelessly before being molded into a pot.  粘土在塑造成陶罐之前被不成形地揉捏。

The old sweater lay shapelessly on the chair.  那件旧毛衣不成形地躺在椅子上。


近义词：formlessly, formlessly, without shape, haphazardly


反义词：neatly, tidily, shapely,  formally


词性: 副词
