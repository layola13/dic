pies:/paɪz/| n.|馅饼|A pie is a baked dish which typically has a filling of fruit, meat, or vegetables enclosed in a pastry crust.  Pies are often served as desserts or main courses, depending on the filling.|例句：She baked a delicious apple pie for dessert. 她烤了一个美味的苹果派作为甜点。|例句：The shepherd's pie was hearty and filling.  这道牧羊人派很丰盛，令人饱腹。|近义词：tart,pastry|反义词：无
