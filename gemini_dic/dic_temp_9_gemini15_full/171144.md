psychobabbler:/ˈsaɪkoʊˌbæblər/| n.|心理胡言乱语者，滥用心理学术语的人|Someone who uses psychological jargon superficially and imprecisely, often to impress or obfuscate.  They typically lack a genuine understanding of the concepts they employ.|Example: He's a real psychobabbler; he throws around terms like "narcissistic personality disorder" without any real clinical basis. 他是个十足的心理胡言乱语者；他随意抛出像“自恋型人格障碍”这样的术语，没有任何真正的临床依据。|近义词：charlatan, quack, pseudointellectual|反义词：expert, professional, specialist
