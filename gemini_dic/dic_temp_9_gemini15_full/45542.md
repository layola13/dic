curioso:/kʊriˈoʊsoʊ/| adj.|好奇的，求知欲强的|Curious means having a strong desire to know or learn something. It implies an inquisitive nature and a keen interest in discovering new information or understanding things better.|例句：The children were curious about the strange noise.孩子们对奇怪的声音感到好奇。|例句：She's always been curious about other cultures. 她一直对其他文化很感兴趣。|近义词：inquisitive, inquisitive, inquiring, interested|反义词：indifferent, apathetic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is curious about the new technology. = 他对这项新技术很好奇。
They are curious to know the results. = 他们很想知道结果。


一般过去时:
I was curious about her past. = 我对她的过去很好奇。
We were curious to see what would happen next. = 我们很好奇接下来会发生什么。


现在完成时:
She has been curious about astronomy since childhood. = 她从小就对天文学很好奇。
They have been curious to learn more about the project. = 他们一直很好奇想了解更多关于这个项目的信息。


过去完成时:
I had been curious about his sudden departure for days. = 我已经对他的突然离开好奇了好几天了。
They had been curious to know the answer before the announcement. = 在宣布答案之前，他们就很好奇想知道答案。


将来时:
He will be curious to see the finished product. = 他将很好奇地看到成品。
She will be curious about the outcome of the election. = 她将对选举的结果感到好奇。


虚拟语气:
If I were curious, I would ask more questions. = 如果我很感兴趣，我会问更多问题。
I wish I were more curious about history. = 我真希望我对历史更感兴趣。


被动语态:
His behavior was found curious by everyone. = 他的行为让每个人都觉得奇怪。
The matter is considered curious by the investigators. = 这个事件被调查人员认为很奇怪。


疑问句:
Are you curious about anything? = 你对什么好奇吗？
Is he curious about her secret? = 他对她秘密好奇吗？


否定句:
She is not curious about politics. = 她对政治不感兴趣。
They were not curious about the details. = 他们对细节不感兴趣。


PHRASE->
A curious case = 一个奇怪的案例
Curiously enough = 说来也巧
A curious coincidence = 一个巧合
Curiosity killed the cat = 好奇害死猫
Curious about = 对...好奇
