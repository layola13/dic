coenogamy:/ˌsiːnˈɒɡəmi/| n. |共同婚配制，群婚制 |A form of marriage where several men and women are married to each other simultaneously; a system of communal marriage.  |Example:  The anthropologist studied a remote tribe practicing coenogamy. 人类学家研究了一个实行群婚制的偏远部落。|近义词：communal marriage, group marriage |反义词：monogamy, polygamy
