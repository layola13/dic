castrated:/ˈkæstreɪtɪd/ | adj. |阉割的；去势的 | deprived of the ability to reproduce sexually;  emasculated.  The term often refers to the removal of testicles in males, but can also refer to the removal or destruction of ovaries in females, or metaphorical castration. | 例句：The farmer castrated the male piglets to prevent unwanted breeding.  这个农民阉割了雄性小猪以防止不需要的繁殖。

The singer's performance felt castrated by the censorship.  这位歌手的表演由于审查制度而显得无力。


近义词：emasculated, gelded, neutered | 反义词：intact, fertile
