pedestrially:/pəˈdɛstrɪəli/| adv.| 步行地；像行人一样地 | In a pedestrian manner; on foot.  The word implies a lack of excitement or imagination, a mundane or commonplace way of doing something. |例句：He crossed the street pedestrially, showing no urgency. 他步行过街，毫不着急。

例句：She approached the problem pedestrially, lacking any innovative solutions. 她以一种平淡无奇的方式处理这个问题，缺乏任何创新的解决方案。


近义词：on foot, by foot, prosaically, unimaginatively


反义词：rapidly, swiftly, imaginatively, creatively


词性: 副词
