mandioca:/mæn.diˈoʊ.kə/| n. |木薯 |Cassava, also known as manioc or yuca, is a starchy tuberous root native to South America.  It's a crucial staple food in many tropical regions, providing carbohydrates and calories to millions.  The root is processed into various foods, including flour, starch, and various dishes. |例句：Many people in the tropics rely on mandioca as a primary source of food. 许多热带地区的人们依靠木薯作为主要的粮食来源。|例句：We made tapioca pudding using flour derived from mandioca. 我们用木薯粉做了木薯布丁。|近义词：cassava, yuca, tapioca root |反义词：无
