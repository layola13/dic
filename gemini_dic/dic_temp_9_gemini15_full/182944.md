repiner:/rɪˈpaɪnər/ | n. |抱怨者，牢骚者 |A person who habitually complains or expresses discontent.  A repiner dwells on misfortunes and is prone to self-pity. | Example: He was a repiner, always finding fault with everything and everyone. 他是个抱怨者，总是对所有人和事吹毛求疵。 |近义词：grumbler, complainer, whiner |反义词：optimist,  content person | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The repiner constantly complains about his job. = 抱怨者不断抱怨他的工作。
She is a repiner, always finding fault with the world. = 她是个抱怨者，总是挑剔这个世界。


一般过去时:
He repined about his lost opportunities. = 他为失去的机会而抱怨。
The old man repined over his failing health. = 老人为日渐衰弱的健康而抱怨。
