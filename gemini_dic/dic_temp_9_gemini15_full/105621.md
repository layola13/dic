kinesthetic:/ˌkɪnɪˈsθɛtɪk/| adj.|动觉的；感觉运动的；本体感觉的 |Relating to a sense of body position and movement.  It involves the awareness of your body's position and movement in space.  |例句：He has excellent kinesthetic awareness, making him a skilled dancer. 他拥有极好的动觉意识，这使他成为一名技艺精湛的舞蹈家。

例句：The ballet dancer demonstrated remarkable kinesthetic skill.  这位芭蕾舞者展现了非凡的动觉技巧。


近义词：sensory, proprioceptive |反义词：None directly opposite, but could be considered the opposite of lacking body awareness. |词性:形容词
