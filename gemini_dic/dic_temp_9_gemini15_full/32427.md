chimango:/tʃɪˈmæŋɡoʊ/| n. |栗翅鹰|A small, reddish-brown hawk found in South America, known for its distinctive chestnut-colored wings.  It is a common sight in open areas and feeds on small animals such as rodents and reptiles.|Example: The chimango circled lazily above the pampas, searching for prey. 栗翅鹰在潘帕斯草原上懒洋洋地盘旋，寻找猎物。|近义词:red-shouldered hawk (a more general term)|反义词: None specifically, but terms like "large bird of prey" could be considered antonyms in a comparative sense.|名词
