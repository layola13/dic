scours:/ˈskaʊrz/| vt.|擦洗；冲洗；彻底搜查|To rub or scrub hard; to clean something thoroughly by rubbing it hard.  Also, to search thoroughly and systematically.|例句：She scoured the pan until it shone. 她用力擦洗平底锅直到它闪闪发光。|例句：The police scoured the area for clues. 警方彻底搜查了该区域寻找线索。|近义词：scrub, scour, clean, search |反义词：dirty, neglect
