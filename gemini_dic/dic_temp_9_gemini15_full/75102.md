fringes:/frɪndʒɪz/| n.|边缘；刘海；穗状物；次要部分 |The outer edges or boundaries of something; a decorative border of hanging threads or strips; a group or part of something that is less important than the main part.|例句：The fringes of the forest were overgrown with weeds. 森林的边缘长满了杂草。|例句：She wore her hair in fringes. 她留着刘海。|近义词：borders, edges, margins, outskirts|反义词：center, core, middle
