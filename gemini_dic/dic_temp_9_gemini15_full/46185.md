cypress:/ˈsaɪprəs/ | n. |柏树，柏木 |An evergreen coniferous tree with scale-like leaves and usually a pyramidal shape.  Cypress trees are often associated with mourning and cemeteries. |例句：The cemetery was lined with tall cypress trees. 墓地旁排列着高大的柏树。

例句：We planted a cypress tree in the garden to commemorate our grandmother. 我们在花园里种了一棵柏树来纪念我们的祖母。


近义词：cedar, juniper |反义词：无
