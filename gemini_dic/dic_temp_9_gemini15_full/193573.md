self-analysis:/ˌsɛlf əˈnæləsɪs/| n. |自我分析，自我反省|The process of carefully examining one's own thoughts, feelings, and behaviors to gain a better understanding of oneself.  Self-analysis can be a valuable tool for personal growth and development, helping individuals identify strengths and weaknesses, understand motivations, and make positive changes in their lives.|例句：Through self-analysis, she realized her procrastination stemmed from fear of failure. 通过自我分析，她意识到她的拖延症源于对失败的恐惧。|例句：Regular self-analysis is crucial for effective leadership. 定期进行自我分析对于有效的领导至关重要。|近义词： introspection, self-examination, self-reflection |反义词：self-deception, denial |名词
