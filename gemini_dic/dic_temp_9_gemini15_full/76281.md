g'day:/ɡəˈdeɪ/| int.|你好，早上好(澳大利亚英语)|A common Australian English greeting, similar to "hello" or "good day". It's an informal and friendly way to greet someone, often used throughout the day, not just in the morning. |G'day, mate! How's it going?  你好，老兄！最近怎么样？|近义词：hello, hi, howdy, good morning, good day|反义词:None
