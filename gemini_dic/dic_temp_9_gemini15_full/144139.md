outselling:/ˌaʊtˈsɛlɪŋ/| v. | 销售量超过，比…卖得好 | Selling more than something else.  | Example:  Their new product is outselling all its competitors. 他们的新产品销量超过所有竞争对手。 |近义词:surpassing, exceeding, outscoring|反义词:underselling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This book is outselling all the others. = 这本书的销量超过所有其他书籍。
That restaurant is outselling its neighbor. = 那家餐馆的销量超过了它隔壁的餐馆。


一般过去时:
Last year, their album outselling all expectations. = 去年，他们的专辑销量超出了所有预期。
The concert tickets outselling within hours of going on sale. = 演唱会门票在开售几小时内就售罄了。


现在完成时:
The company has been outselling its rivals for years. = 这家公司多年来一直比竞争对手卖得好。
This model has outselling all previous versions. = 此型号的销量已超过所有之前的版本。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, the game had outselling its predecessor. = 到年底，这款游戏的销量已超过其前作。
Before the promotion, the product hadn't outselling its competitors. = 促销之前，该产品的销量没有超过竞争对手。


将来时:
Analysts predict that the new phone will outselling the old model. = 分析师预测这款新手机的销量将超过旧款。
We expect that our new product will outselling the competition. = 我们预计我们的新产品将胜过竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If they had marketed it better, it might have outselling the competition. = 如果他们营销做得更好，它可能会超过竞争对手。
If only we had known, we could have outselling them. = 如果我们早知道，我们本可以超过他们的销量。


被动语态:
The old model was outselling by the new one. = 旧款被新款超过了销量。
Their product is being outselling by a new competitor. = 他们的产品正在被一个新的竞争对手超越销量。


疑问句:
Is the new model outselling the old one? = 新款的销量是否超过了旧款？
Are our products outselling those of our competitors? = 我们的产品销量是否超过了竞争对手？


否定句:
Our product isn't outselling the competition this month. = 我们本月产品销量没有超过竞争对手。
The new phone hasn't outselling the older models yet. = 新手机的销量还没有超过旧款。
