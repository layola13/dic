prepone:/prɪˈpoʊn/| vt.|提前，将…提前|To move something to an earlier time or date.|例句：The meeting has been preponed to Monday. 会议已提前到星期一举行。例句：They preponed the deadline by a week. 他们将截止日期提前了一周。|近义词：advance, accelerate, bring forward|反义词：postpone, delay


USAGE->

They prepone the meeting every week. = 他们每周都提前开会。
We prepone our departure time regularly. = 我们经常提前出发。

The teacher preponed the exam by two days. = 老师把考试提前了两天。

过去完成时:

将来时:
