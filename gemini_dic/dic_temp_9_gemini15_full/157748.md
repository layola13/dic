placation:/pleɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. | 安抚；抚慰；平息 | The act of appeasing or pacifying someone or something; the state of being appeased or pacified.  | Example: The government's placation of the protesters did little to quell the unrest.  政府安抚抗议者的行为几乎没有平息动乱。|近义词：appeasement, pacification, conciliation |反义词：provocation, aggravation, exacerbation
