colonnaded:/ˌkɒləˈneɪdəd/| adj.|柱廊式的，有柱廊的|Having a colonnade or colonnades.  A colonnade is a series of columns supporting a roof or entablature. |例句：The ancient Roman forum was a colonnaded space. 古罗马广场是一个柱廊式空间。The hotel boasts a beautiful colonnaded walkway. 这家酒店拥有美丽的柱廊式走道。|近义词：columned, porticoed|反义词：uncolumned
