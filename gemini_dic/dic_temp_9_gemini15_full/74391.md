framers:/ˈfreɪmər/| n.|创作者；制定者；结构者；（尤指）宪法起草人|A framer is someone who creates or constructs something, especially a document or structure.  The term often refers to the authors of the United States Constitution. |例句：The framers of the Constitution intended to create a system of checks and balances.  宪法起草人旨在建立一个制衡体系。


例句：The skilled framers built a sturdy house. 熟练的建筑工人建造了一座坚固的房子。


近义词：constructor, creator, author, architect, designer


反义词：destroyer, deconstructor
