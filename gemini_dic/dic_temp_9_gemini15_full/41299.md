cordoning:/kɔːrˈdɔːnɪŋ/ | v. |  警戒，隔离 |  The act of surrounding or isolating an area, typically to prevent access or movement. This is often done for security, safety, or to control the spread of something. | 例句：Police cordoned off the street after the bomb threat.  警察在爆炸威胁后封锁了街道。

例句：The area was cordoned off while the bomb squad investigated.  爆炸物处理小组调查期间，该区域被封锁。


近义词：isolating, blocking off, sealing off, quarantining

反义词：opening up, freeing, unblocking
