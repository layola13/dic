It can refer to literal darkness or to a figurative sense of mystery or uncertainty. | The shadowiness of the alley made me uneasy.  这条小巷的阴暗让我不安。 The shadowiness of his past remained a mystery. 他过去的神秘依然是个谜。 | darkness, obscurity, ambiguity, mystery | brightness, clarity, transparency, illumination |  形容词
