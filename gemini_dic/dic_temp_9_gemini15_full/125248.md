multidimensionally:/ˌmʌltiˈdaɪmɛnʃənəli/ | adv. | 多维度地，多方面地 | In a manner involving or relating to multiple dimensions.  This term suggests considering multiple factors or perspectives simultaneously, rather than a single, isolated viewpoint.  | 例句：The problem needs to be addressed multidimensionally, considering social, economic, and environmental impacts.  这个问题需要多方面考虑，包括社会、经济和环境影响。

The study approached the issue multidimensionally, analyzing data from various sources. 这项研究多角度地探讨了这个问题，分析了来自不同来源的数据。| 近义词：holistically, comprehensively, multifacetedly | 反义词：unidimensionally, simplistically

词源:
The word is formed from the prefix "multi-" (meaning many or multiple), "dimension" (referring to a measurable extent of something), and the adverbial suffix "-ally" (indicating manner or quality).  "Dimension" itself has roots in Latin "dimensio," from "dimeteri," meaning "to measure out."  Thus, the word literally means "in a manner of many measurements or aspects."


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adverb and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Its usage is descriptive, modifying the verb of the sentence to indicate a multidimensional approach.
