counterresponse: /ˈkaʊntər rɪˈspɑːns/ | n. | 反响；反作用；对策 | A response that opposes or contradicts an initial action or statement.  It often involves a reaction to a previous stimulus or action. | Example: The government's new policy faced a strong counterresponse from environmental groups. 政府的新政策遭到了环保组织的强烈反弹。| 近义词：reaction, retort, counteraction, rebuttal | 反义词：support, endorsement, agreement
