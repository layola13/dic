live:/lɪv/| /lɪv/| v. adj. n.| 活着；居住；经历；直播；现场的| To be alive; to exist; to reside in a place; to experience something; to broadcast in real time; existing or happening at the present time.|例句：He lives in London. 他住在伦敦。|例句：They lived through a terrible storm. 他们经历了一场可怕的风暴。|近义词：reside, dwell, inhabit, experience, broadcast|反义词：die, deceased, dead|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I live in New York. = 我住在纽约。
They live a simple life. = 他们过着简朴的生活。

一般过去时:
We lived in Paris for five years. = 我们在巴黎住了五年。
She lived a very exciting life. = 她过着非常刺激的生活。

现在完成时:
He has lived here since 2010. = 他从2010年就住在这里了。
I have lived through many challenges. = 我经历了许多挑战。

过去完成时:
They had lived in that house for ten years before they moved. = 他们在搬家之前在那栋房子里住了十年。
She had lived a carefree life before the accident. = 事故发生之前，她过着无忧无虑的生活。

将来时:
We will live in the countryside one day. = 我们有一天会住在乡下。
They will live to regret their decision. = 他们最终会后悔他们的决定。

虚拟语气:
If I lived in a big city, I would have more opportunities. = 如果我住在繁华的城市，我会拥有更多机会。
I wish I lived closer to my family. = 我希望我住得离家人更近一些。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The house was lived in by many families. = 这房子被许多家庭居住过。


疑问句:
Where do you live? = 你住在哪里？
Do they live here? = 他们住在这里吗？

否定句:
I don't live in a big house. = 我不住在大房子里。
They didn't live there very long. = 他们没有在那里住很久。


PHRASE->
live music = 现场音乐
live broadcast = 直播
live together = 住在一起
live long and prosper = 长命百岁（星际迷航常用语）
live and learn = 活到老，学到老
