pend:/pend/ | v. | 悬挂；取决于；暂停；悬而未决 | To hang or be suspended; to be undecided or unsettled; to delay or postpone.  | The lamp pended from the ceiling.  吊灯悬挂在天花板上。| The success of the project pends on funding.  项目的成功取决于资金。 | hang, depend, suspend | decide, conclude, resolve | 动词


USAGE->

He pends the picture on the wall. = 他把画挂在墙上。
The outcome pends on several factors. = 结果取决于几个因素。

She pended the decision until next week. = 她把决定推迟到下周。

过去完成时:
