overamplify:/ˌoʊvərˈæmplɪfaɪ/| vt.|过度放大；夸大；过分强调|To exaggerate or overemphasize something.  This often involves distorting the truth or making something seem more significant than it actually is.|例句：The media overamplified the minor incident, creating unnecessary panic. 媒体过度渲染了这件小事，造成了不必要的恐慌。The politician overamplified the benefits of his policies.  这位政治家夸大了其政策的好处。|近义词：exaggerate, inflate, magnify, overstate|反义词：downplay, understate, minimize
