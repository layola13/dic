recip:/ˈrɛsɪp/ | n. |食谱，配方；方法，诀窍|A recipe is a set of instructions for preparing a particular dish, including a list of ingredients and the steps involved in cooking or baking it.  It can also refer to a formula or method for achieving a particular result.|Example:  I followed the recipe carefully, and the cake turned out perfectly. 我仔细按照食谱做了，蛋糕做得完美无缺。


|Example: The recipe for success involves hard work and dedication.成功的秘诀在于努力工作和奉献。|近义词：formula, method, instruction |反义词：无|名词
