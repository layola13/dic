ophthalmite:/ˌɒfθælˈmaɪt/| n. |眼炎|Inflammation of the eye,  often involving the conjunctiva (the membrane lining the eyelid and covering the sclera) and other structures.  It can be caused by various factors including infections, allergies, or injuries.|例句：The ophthalmite was caused by a bacterial infection. 这眼炎是由细菌感染引起的。 The patient presented with severe ophthalmite and photophobia. 病人出现严重眼炎和畏光。|近义词：conjunctivitis, keratitis|反义词:healthy eye, normal eye.
