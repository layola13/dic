inexperience:/ˌɪnɪkˈspɪəriəns/| adj. |缺乏经验的，经验不足的 |Lack of experience; the state of being inexperienced.  It often implies a lack of skill or knowledge due to a limited exposure to a particular activity or situation. |例句：His inexperience showed in his clumsy handling of the situation. 他的缺乏经验在他笨拙的处理方式中显露无疑。
His inexperience as a manager led to several costly mistakes. 他作为经理的缺乏经验导致了几次代价高昂的错误。|近义词：naiveté, greenness, unfamiliarity |反义词：experience, expertise, proficiency |词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His inexperience prevents him from handling complex tasks. 他的缺乏经验阻止了他处理复杂的任务。
Her inexperience is evident in her work. 她的缺乏经验在她的工作中显而易见。


一般过去时:
His inexperience hampered his progress in the company.  他缺乏经验阻碍了他公司里的进步。
Their inexperience caused the project to fail. 他们的缺乏经验导致了项目的失败。


现在完成时:
Her inexperience has led to several misunderstandings. 她的缺乏经验导致了一些误解。
His inexperience has not been a significant impediment so far. 到目前为止，他的缺乏经验还没有成为一个很大的障碍。


过去完成时:
They hadn't realized the extent of their inexperience until the project went wrong. 直到项目出错，他们才意识到自己缺乏经验的程度。
I hadn't anticipated the challenges due to my inexperience. 我没有预料到由于我的缺乏经验而带来的挑战。


将来时:
His inexperience will likely hinder his chances of promotion. 他的缺乏经验可能会阻碍他晋升的机会。
Their inexperience will probably cause delays in the project. 他们的缺乏经验可能会导致项目延误。


虚拟语气:
If he had more experience, he wouldn't make so many mistakes. 如果他更有经验，他不会犯那么多错误。
If only she had more experience, the situation would be different.  如果她更有经验就好了，情况就会不一样。


被动语态:
His mistakes were attributed to his inexperience. 他的错误归咎于他的缺乏经验。
The project's failure was partly caused by the team's inexperience. 项目的失败部分是由团队缺乏经验造成的。


疑问句:
Is his inexperience a major concern? 他的缺乏经验是一个主要问题吗？
Does their inexperience pose a risk to the project? 他们的缺乏经验会对项目构成风险吗？


否定句:
His performance wasn't hampered by inexperience. 他的表现没有受到缺乏经验的影响。
The problem wasn't due to inexperience, but rather negligence.  问题不是由于缺乏经验，而是由于疏忽。
