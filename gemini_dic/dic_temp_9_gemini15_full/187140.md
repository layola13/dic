road-test:/ˈroʊdˌtɛst/| vt. n. |公路测试；试车|To test a vehicle (especially a new model) on the road.  A test drive of a vehicle.|例句：The engineers road-tested the new car for a week before its release. 工程师在发布新车之前对其进行了为期一周的公路测试。

例句：The company is conducting road tests on its new electric vehicle.  公司正在对其新款电动汽车进行公路测试。


近义词：test drive, trial run


反义词：None (antonyms are not directly applicable)


词性: Verb and Noun
