repercussions:/ˌriːpərˈkʌʃənz/ | n. | 后果，反响，影响 | The consequences or effects of an action or event, especially on a larger scale.  | The repercussions of the scandal were felt throughout the company.  这次丑闻的影响波及整个公司。| consequences, effects, ramifications | benefits, advantages |名词
