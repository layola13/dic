laced:/leɪst/| v. adj. | 系带；掺杂；|to fasten or tie with a lace or laces; to interweave or mix something into something else.|例句：She laced her boots tightly before the hike.  她在远足前紧紧地系紧了靴子。 The cake was laced with rum. 蛋糕里掺杂了朗姆酒。|近义词：tie, fasten, mix, blend |反义词：unlace, untie, separate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He laces his shoes every morning. = 他每天早上系鞋带。
She laces the fabric together carefully. = 她小心翼翼地把织物缝合在一起。

一般过去时:
I laced up my running shoes before the race. = 我在比赛前系好了跑鞋。
They laced the stew with herbs. = 他们用香草调味炖菜。

现在完成时:
She has laced the curtains beautifully. = 她已经漂亮地系好了窗帘。
He has laced his speech with humor. = 他在演讲中穿插了一些幽默。

过去完成时:
She had laced her fingers together nervously. = 她紧张地把手指交叉在一起。
He had laced the poison into the victim's drink. = 他已将毒药掺入受害者的饮料中。

将来时:
I will lace my skates before I go ice-skating. = 我去滑冰前会系好冰鞋。
They will lace the bread with cinnamon. = 他们会在面包里掺入肉桂。


虚拟语气:
If I had laced my boots tighter, I wouldn't have tripped. = 如果我把靴子系得紧一些，我就不会绊倒了。
I wish I had laced the sauce with more ginger. = 我希望我把酱汁里加更多姜。

被动语态:
The shoes were laced neatly. = 鞋子系得很整齐。
The fabric is laced with silver threads. = 织物上缀有银线。

疑问句:
Are your boots laced properly? = 你的靴子系好了吗？
Is the drink laced with anything? = 这饮料里加了什么东西吗？

否定句:
He didn't lace his shoes properly. = 他没有把鞋系好。
She didn't lace the cake with enough sugar. = 她没有在蛋糕里加足够的糖。
