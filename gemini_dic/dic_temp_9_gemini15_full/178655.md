recreation's:/ˌriːkriˈeɪʃənz/ | n. |娱乐活动（recreation的属格）|the act of recreating or being recreated; a form of amusement or enjoyment. It refers to activities done for enjoyment, relaxation, or pleasure, often in contrast to work or daily routines.  The possessive form 'recreation's' indicates that something belongs to or is associated with recreation. | Example: The recreation's facilities were well-maintained. (娱乐设施维护得很好。) |近义词：entertainment, leisure, amusement, pastime |反义词：work, toil, labor |名词
