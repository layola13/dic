destruction:/dɪˈstrʌkʃən/ | n. |破坏，毁灭 |The destruction of the rainforest is a serious environmental problem.  雨林的破坏是一个严重的环境问题。 The earthquake caused widespread destruction. 地震造成了大范围的破坏。|近义词：ruin, devastation, demolition |反义词：construction, creation, preservation |名词


USAGE->
