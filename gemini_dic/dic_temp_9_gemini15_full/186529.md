ricotta:/rɪˈkɒtə/| n. |意大利乳清干酪|A fresh, white Italian cheese made from sheep's or goat's whey (the liquid remaining after milk has been curdled and strained). It has a creamy, slightly sweet, and tangy flavor.  It is often used in desserts, pasta fillings, and savory dishes.|例句：The ricotta cheese in the cannoli was incredibly smooth and creamy.  这款卡诺利的乳清干酪非常顺滑香浓。|近义词：cottage cheese, whey cheese|反义词：hard cheese
