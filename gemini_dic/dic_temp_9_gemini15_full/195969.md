sent:/sent/| v. |派遣；送；使进入某种状态|to cause something to go or be carried to a particular place|The package was sent by airmail.包裹是空邮寄来的。|近义词：transmit, dispatch, forward|反义词：receive, collect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sends emails every day. = 他每天都发邮件。
She sends her children to school. = 她送孩子们去上学。


一般过去时:
They sent a letter to their parents. = 他们寄了一封信给他们的父母。
He sent me a message yesterday. = 他昨天给我发了一条消息。


现在完成时:
I have sent the application. = 我已经提交了申请。
She has sent him several emails. = 她已经给他发了多封邮件。


过去完成时:
They had sent the package before the deadline. = 他们在大限之前已经寄出了包裹。
She had sent him a warning. = 她已经给他发出了警告。


将来时:
I will send you the documents tomorrow. = 我明天会把文件寄给你。
They will send a team to investigate. = 他们将派一个团队去调查。


虚拟语气:
If I had sent the email earlier, I would have received a reply. = 如果我早点发邮件，我就收到回复了。
I wish I had sent the flowers. = 我真希望我送了鲜花。


被动语态:
The package was sent to the wrong address. = 包裹寄错了地址。
The invitations were sent out last week. = 请柬上周寄出了。


疑问句:
Did you send the report? = 你发送报告了吗？
When will the goods be sent? = 货物什么时候寄出？


否定句:
I didn't send the email. = 我没有发送邮件。
They haven't sent the payment yet. = 他们还没有付款。


PHRASE->
send out = 发送；发出
send for = 派人去请；召唤
send off =  送行；解雇
send in = 提交；送交
send back = 退回；遣送
