overfrequently:/ˌoʊvərˈfriːkwəntli/ | adv. |过于频繁地，过分频繁地 |  This adverb describes the occurrence of something happening too often. It emphasizes the excessive nature of the frequency. |例句：He visited his mother overfrequently, which made her feel pressured. 他过于频繁地探望母亲，这让她感到压力很大。  |近义词：too often, excessively often, repeatedly |反义词：rarely, infrequently, seldom
