epen:/ˈɛpən/ | n. | 钢笔 |  A pen is a writing instrument used to apply ink to a surface for writing or drawing.  It typically consists of a reservoir for ink and a point for dispensing the ink. | Example: I need a pen to sign this contract. 我需要一支钢笔来签署这份合同。 |近义词：stylus, writing instrument | 反义词：None.
