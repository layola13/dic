refortified:/ˌriːˈfɔːrtɪfaɪd/| adj.| 再强化了的；重新设防的 | Strengthened or fortified again.  This word describes something that has been fortified once and then fortified a second time, implying a higher level of strength or protection than a single fortification. |例句：The refortified walls withstood the siege.  再强化的城墙经受住了围攻。

The refortified wine has a more intense flavor.  重新强化了的葡萄酒味道更浓郁。


近义词：reinforced, strengthened, buttressed | 反义词：weakened, undermined, depleted | 词性:形容词
