demystified: /ˌdiːˈmɪstɪfaɪd/ | adj. |  解释清楚的；去神秘化的；易于理解的 | Made clear and easy to understand;  no longer mysterious or confusing. |  例句：The complex scientific concepts were demystified in the lecture.  这次讲座把复杂的科学概念解释清楚了。  The author demystified the process of starting a business. 作者解释清楚了创业的过程。 | 近义词：clarified, explained, simplified, elucidated | 反义词：mystified, obscured, complicated, perplexing
