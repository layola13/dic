precessional:/prəˈsɛʃənəl/| adj. |岁差的；进动的|Relating to or causing precession, the slow change in the orientation of the rotational axis of a rotating body.  |The precessional movement of the Earth's axis causes the slow shift in the position of the celestial poles. 地球自转轴的进动导致天极位置缓慢变化。| 近义词：gyratory, rotational | 反义词：stationary
