inheritable:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtəbl/ | adj. | 可遗传的，可继承的 | Able to be inherited; capable of being passed down from parent to offspring or predecessor to successor.  | Example:  Many genetic diseases are inheritable. 许多遗传疾病是可遗传的。
The family business is inheritable, passing down through generations. 家族企业是可继承的，代代相传。|近义词：hereditary, transmissible | 反义词：nonhereditary, acquired
