engulf:/ɪnˈɡʌlf/ | vt. |吞没；淹没；使陷入 |To overwhelm (someone or something) completely; to surround and cover completely.  |例句：The flames quickly engulfed the entire building.  火焰迅速吞噬了整栋建筑物。
The tsunami engulfed coastal villages. 海啸吞没了沿海村庄。|近义词：submerge, overwhelm, swallow, inundate |反义词：emerge, rescue, save |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rising tide engulfs the beach every day. = 涨潮每天都会淹没海滩。
Fear engulfs him when he thinks about the future. = 当他想到未来时，恐惧感吞噬了他。


一般过去时:
The forest fire engulfed hundreds of acres of land. = 森林大火吞噬了数百英亩的土地。
Sadness engulfed her after she heard the news. = 她听到这个消息后，悲伤淹没了她。


现在完成时:
The country has been engulfed in civil war for years. = 这个国家多年来一直陷入内战。
Anxiety has engulfed me since I received the email. = 自从收到邮件以来，焦虑就一直困扰着我。


过去完成时:
The city had been engulfed by darkness before the power outage. = 在停电之前，城市已经被黑暗吞噬了。
She had been engulfed in her work all day. = 她一整天都埋头工作。


将来时:
The volcano will engulf the nearby villages if it erupts. = 如果火山爆发，它将吞噬附近的村庄。
If we don't act now, despair will engulf us. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，绝望将吞噬我们。


虚拟语气:
If the river were to rise, it would engulf the town. = 如果河水上涨，它将会淹没小镇。
I wish I hadn't been engulfed by doubt at that critical moment. = 我希望在那关键时刻我没有被怀疑所吞噬。


被动语态:
The small boat was engulfed by the waves. = 小船被海浪吞噬了。
The town was engulfed in flames after the explosion. = 爆炸后，小镇被火焰吞噬了。


疑问句:
Will the rising waters engulf our homes? = 涨潮会淹没我们的家园吗？
Was the village engulfed by the flood? = 村庄被洪水淹没了吗？


否定句:
The fire did not engulf the entire forest. = 火势并没有吞噬整个森林。
He tried to escape, but the darkness didn't engulf him completely. = 他试图逃跑，但黑暗并没有完全吞噬他。


PHRASE->
be engulfed in = 沉浸在...中；被...吞噬
engulfed by = 被...吞噬
engulfed in flames = 陷入火海
engulfed with = 被...充满
engulfed by grief = 沉浸在悲伤中
