forgivers:/fərˈɡɪvərz/| n. |宽恕者，饶恕者|Those who forgive others; people characterized by their willingness to pardon offenses.|The forgivers in the community showed remarkable compassion towards the offenders.|近义词：pardoners, remitters|反义词：punishers, avengers|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The forgivers readily pardon those who repent. = 宽恕者很容易原谅那些悔过的人。
These forgivers offer forgiveness freely. = 这些宽恕者慷慨地给予宽恕。


一般过去时:
The forgivers pardoned the criminal after his sincere apology. = 在罪犯真诚道歉后，宽恕者原谅了他。
The forgivers forgave the debt. = 宽恕者原谅了债务。


现在完成时:
The forgivers have shown great mercy. = 宽恕者们表现出了极大的仁慈。
The court has forgave the young man for his mistake. = 法庭已经原谅了这个年轻人犯下的错误。


过去完成时:
The forgivers had already forgiven him before he apologized. = 在他道歉之前，宽恕者们已经原谅了他。
The judge had already forgiven her for the crime before she begged for mercy. = 在她请求宽恕之前，法官已经原谅了她的罪行。


将来时:
The forgivers will forgive him if he sincerely repents. = 如果他真诚悔过，宽恕者们将会原谅他。
The jury will forgive her. = 陪审团将原谅她。


虚拟语气:
If I were a forgiver, I would offer a second chance. = 如果我是个宽恕者，我会给他第二次机会。
If they were forgivers, they would have understood his plight. = 如果他们是宽恕者，他们就会理解他的困境。


被动语态:
The offenders were forgiven by the forgivers. = 罪犯们被宽恕者们原谅了。
The debt was forgiven by the forgivers. = 债务被宽恕者们免除了。


疑问句:
Are they truly forgivers? = 他们真是宽恕者吗？
Who are the forgivers in this situation? = 在这种情况下，谁是宽恕者？


否定句:
They were not forgivers; they sought revenge. = 他们不是宽恕者；他们在寻求报复。
Some people are not forgivers and never forget the wrongs done to them. = 有些人不是宽恕者，他们永远不会忘记别人对他们造成的伤害。
