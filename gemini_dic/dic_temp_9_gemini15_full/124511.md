mounding:/ˈmaʊndɪŋ/ | n. |堆积；成堆；隆起 |The process of forming mounds or heaps; a mass or heap of something.  It can refer to the act of piling up earth or other materials, or the resulting formation. |例句：The mounding of the soil created raised beds for planting.  土壤堆积形成了用于种植的高畦。

The ants created a mounding of earth near their nest. 蚂蚁在巢穴附近堆起了土堆。|近义词：heaping, piling, accumulation |反义词：leveling, flattening, smoothing |
