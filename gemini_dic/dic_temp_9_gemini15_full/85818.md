hazels:/ˈheɪzəlz/ | n. |榛子；榛树；榛色 | Hazels are the nuts of the hazel tree, and the tree itself is also called a hazel.  The word can also refer to a light brown color. |例句：We gathered hazelnuts from the woods. 我们从树林里采集榛子。

The leaves of the hazel tree are turning a beautiful hazel color. 榛树的叶子正变成美丽的榛色。|近义词：hazelnuts, filberts |反义词：无

词源:

The word "hazel" comes from Old English *hæsel, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hasel, meaning "hazel tree."  The connection to the nut is straightforward, as the word refers to both the tree and its fruit.
