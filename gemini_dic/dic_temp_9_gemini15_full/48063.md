declar:/dɪˈklɛər/ | vt. | 宣布；声明；断言；表明；申报 | To state clearly and definitely; to announce officially.  |例句：The government declared war. 政府宣战了。 |例句：She declared her love for him. 她向他表白了爱意。 |近义词：announce, proclaim, pronounce, assert |反义词：conceal, suppress, withhold |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He declares his innocence. = 他宣称自己无罪。
The company declares its profits annually. = 公司每年申报利润。

一般过去时:
She declared her intentions clearly. = 她清楚地表明了她的意图。
The judge declared a mistrial. = 法官宣布审判无效。

现在完成时:
They have declared their support for the candidate. = 他们已经宣布支持这位候选人。
The government has declared a state of emergency. = 政府已经宣布进入紧急状态。

过去完成时:
He had declared bankruptcy before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他已经宣布破产了。
She had declared her love for him before he left. = 他离开之前，她已经向他表白了爱意。

将来时:
They will declare the winner tomorrow. = 他们明天将宣布获胜者。
The president will declare a national holiday. = 总统将宣布全国假日。

虚拟语气:
If he declared his love, she might accept. = 如果他表白，她可能会接受。
I wish he had declared his intentions earlier. = 我希望他早点表明他的意图。

被动语态:
War was declared in 1914. = 战争于1914年爆发。
The winner will be declared at the end of the competition. = 比赛结束后将宣布获胜者。

疑问句:
Will they declare a winner soon? = 他们很快会宣布获胜者吗？
Did he declare his assets honestly? = 他是否诚实地申报了他的资产？

否定句:
He didn't declare his income properly. = 他没有正确申报他的收入。
She wouldn't declare her feelings. = 她不愿意表达她的感情。
