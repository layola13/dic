patesi:/pəˈteɪsi/| n.|帕特西（古代美索不达米亚国王称号）|The term "patesi" refers to a high-ranking official, often a governor or ruler, in ancient Sumerian city-states.  It was a title of power and authority,  precursor to the later title of "lugal" (king).|The patesi of Lagash ruled wisely. = 拉格什的帕特西统治得非常英明。|近义词:governor, ruler, prince, sovereign|反义词:subject, citizen.
