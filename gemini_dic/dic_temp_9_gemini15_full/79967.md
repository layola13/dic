go back on:/ɡoʊ bæk ɒn/| phrasal verb|食言；违背；放弃|To break a promise or agreement; to fail to keep one's word; to renege on a commitment.  |例句：He went back on his promise to help me. 他违背了他帮助我的承诺。

例句：The company went back on its agreement to increase salaries. 公司违背了提高工资的协议。


近义词：break, renege on, violate, disregard, retract|反义词：keep, uphold, honor, fulfill|词性:phrasal verb
