daredevilries:/ˈdɛəˌdɛvəlriz/| n. | 胆大妄为的行为；冒险行为；鲁莽行为 | Reckless and daring feats or actions; acts of foolhardiness.  The plural form of "daredevilry".|例句：His daredevilries on his motorcycle amazed and terrified onlookers.  他骑摩托车的胆大妄为的行为让旁观者既惊讶又害怕。

例句：The film depicted numerous daredevilries performed by stuntmen.  这部电影描绘了特技演员表演的许多冒险行为。


近义词：recklessness, foolhardiness, audacity, bravado, stunts


反义词：caution, prudence, timidity, carefulness


词源:  Derived from "daredevil" (a person who performs daring feats) + "-ries" (a suffix forming plural nouns).  "Daredevil" itself comes from "dare" (to have the courage to do something) + "devil" (a personification of evil or recklessness).


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, tense variations (present, past, future etc) are not applicable.  It describes actions, not actions in progress.
