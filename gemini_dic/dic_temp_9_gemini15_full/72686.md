fobbing:/ˈfɒbɪŋ/| v.|欺骗，搪塞，敷衍|To deceive or evade someone, especially by means of a trick or excuse.|例句：He was accused of fobbing off investors with false promises. 他被指控以虚假承诺欺骗投资者。|例句：Don't try to fob me off with that excuse; I know you're lying. 不要试图用那个借口敷衍我；我知道你在撒谎。|近义词：duping, cheating, swindling, palming off|反义词：honesty, truthfulness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often fobs his work off on others. = 他经常把工作推卸给别人。
She fobs her responsibilities onto her colleagues. = 她把责任推卸给同事。


一般过去时:
They fobbed the customers off with cheap imitations. = 他们用廉价的仿制品蒙骗顾客。
He fobbed me off with a ridiculous story. = 他用一个荒谬的故事敷衍我。


现在完成时:
He has fobbed off his creditors for months. = 他已经敷衍债权人好几个月了。
She has been fobbed off with empty promises. = 她一直被空洞的承诺所蒙骗。


过去完成时:
They had fobbed the inspector off with false documents. = 他们用伪造的文件蒙骗了检查员。
She had been fobbed off with a substandard product. = 她被一个劣质产品蒙骗了。


将来时:
He will probably fob the blame off on someone else. = 他可能会把责任推卸给别人。
They will try to fob the problem off onto the next team. = 他们会试图把问题推卸给下一个团队。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't fobbed me off, I would have known the truth. = 如果他没有敷衍我，我就知道真相了。
I wish he hadn't fobbed off his responsibilities. = 我真希望他没有推卸责任。


被动语态:
The customers were fobbed off with substandard goods. = 顾客被劣质商品欺骗了。
The problem was fobbed off onto the junior staff. = 问题被推卸给了初级员工。


疑问句:
Was he fobbed off with a fake certificate? = 他是否被假证书欺骗了？
Are they trying to fob us off with lies? = 他们试图用谎言欺骗我们吗？


否定句:
He didn't fob me off with excuses. = 他没有用借口敷衍我。
They wouldn't fob their responsibility off on others. = 他们不会把责任推卸给别人。


PHRASE->
fob off (on someone): 把...推卸给（某人）
fob someone off with something: 用某事物敷衍某人
fob something off as something: 把某物伪装成某物
fob a watch (old usage):  (旧用法) 卖掉一块表
to be fobbed off: 被敷衍，被欺骗
