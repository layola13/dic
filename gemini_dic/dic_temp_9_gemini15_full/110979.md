liquidator:/ˈlɪkwɪdeɪtər/ | n. | 清算人；清算公司 | A person or company appointed to wind up the affairs of a bankrupt business.  | The liquidator sold off the company's assets to pay its debts. 清算人变卖了公司的资产以偿还债务。 | administrator, receiver |  instigator, builder
