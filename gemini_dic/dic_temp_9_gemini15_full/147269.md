overmine:/ˌoʊvərˈmaɪn/ | vt. |过度开采；破坏；削弱 |To mine (a mineral deposit) excessively or to the point of exhaustion or damage.  It can also refer to the weakening or undermining of something, often figuratively. |例句：They overmined the coal seam, leading to subsidence.  他们过度开采煤层，导致地陷。

例句：The constant criticism overmined his confidence.  持续的批评削弱了他的信心。

近义词：exhaust, deplete, undermine, weaken

反义词：conserve, preserve, strengthen, reinforce

词性：动词
