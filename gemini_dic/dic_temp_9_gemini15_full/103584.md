join:/dʒɔɪn/| v.|加入，连接，参加|to become a member of a group or organization; to connect or fasten things together|例句：I joined the army last year. 我去年参军了。|例句：The two pieces of wood were joined together with glue. 两块木头用胶水粘在一起了。|近义词：unite, combine, participate|反义词：leave, separate, quit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He joins the club every year. 他每年都加入这个俱乐部。
She joins us for lunch. 她和我们一起吃午餐。

一般过去时:
They joined the party late. 他们很晚才参加聚会。
I joined the choir last month. 我上个月加入了合唱团。

现在完成时:
She has joined the gym and started working out. 她已经加入了健身房并开始锻炼了。
He has joined the company for five years. 他加入这家公司五年了。

过去完成时:
Before he joined the team, they had already won the championship.  在他加入球队之前，他们已经赢得了冠军。
After she had joined the project, everything went smoothly.  在她加入项目之后，一切都很顺利。

将来时:
I will join you later. 我稍后会加入你们。
They will join the meeting tomorrow. 他们明天将参加会议。

虚拟语气:
If I joined the club, I would meet new people. 如果我加入这个俱乐部，我会结识新朋友。
I wish I had joined the trip. 我真希望我参加了那次旅行。

被动语态:
He was joined by his colleagues. 他受到了同事们的加入。
The two companies were joined together through a merger. 两家公司通过合并结合在一起。

疑问句:
Will you join us for dinner? 你会和我们一起吃晚饭吗？
Did he join the competition? 他参加了比赛吗？

否定句:
I didn't join the meeting. 我没有参加会议。
She won't join the club. 她不会加入俱乐部。


PHRASE->
join in: 参加
join up: 参军；联合
join hands: 携手
join forces: 合力
join the dots: 找出联系
