neanderthal:/niˈændərˌθɔːl/| n. |尼安德特人 |An extinct species of human beings who lived in Europe and western Asia until about 25,000 years ago.  They were characterized by a stocky build, large brow ridges, and a receding chin.|例句：Recent studies have shed new light on the Neanderthal lifestyle. 近期研究揭示了尼安德特人生活方式的新信息。
例句：The discovery of Neanderthal tools suggests a higher level of intelligence than previously thought. 尼安德特人工具的发现表明他们的智力水平比之前认为的更高。|近义词：early human, hominid |反义词：homo sapiens
