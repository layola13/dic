cymatium:/saɪˈmeɪʃəm/| n. |波浪形饰带，卷曲饰面|A cyma is a molding with a double curve, concave at one end and convex at the other.  It is often used as an architectural ornament on cornices and other decorative elements.  The term "cymatium" specifically refers to the uppermost molding, often a cyma recta (with a concave curve followed by a convex curve), in a classical cornice. |The temple's cornice featured a beautifully carved cymatium.| 近义词：molding, cornice, trim | 反义词：无
