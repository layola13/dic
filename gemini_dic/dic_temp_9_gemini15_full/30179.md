celerity:/səˈlɛrɪti/ | n. |迅速，敏捷；迅速发生|Speed, swiftness, rapidity of motion or action.  The celerity with which the news spread was astonishing.|例句：The celerity of his response surprised everyone. 他反应之迅速令所有人惊讶。

例句：The project was completed with remarkable celerity.  这个项目以惊人的速度完成了。


近义词：speed, rapidity, swiftness, velocity, quickness

反义词：slowness, sluggishness, tardiness


词源:  From Latin *celeritas, from celer "swift".
