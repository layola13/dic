captors:/ˈkæptərz/| n. |俘虏者；绑架者|People who have captured someone and are holding them prisoner.  They are often responsible for the captive's safety and well-being, although this is not always the case. |例句：The captors demanded a ransom for the release of the hostages.  俘虏者要求支付赎金以释放人质。

The captors treated the prisoners cruelly. 绑匪残酷地对待囚犯。|近义词：kidnappers, abductors, jailers|反义词：rescuers, liberators
