catfish:/ˈkætˌfɪʃ/| n.|鲶鱼|A catfish is a type of fish with long barbels (whiskers) around its mouth.  They are found in freshwater rivers and lakes around the world. Some species are popular food sources while others can be quite dangerous.|例句：We caught several catfish during our fishing trip. 我们在钓鱼旅行中钓到几条鲶鱼。

例句：The catfish in that river are known to be very large. 那条河里的鲶鱼以体型巨大而闻名。


近义词：bullhead (某些种类),  whiskers (指其触须)
反义词：None.  (没有明确的反义词)

词性:名词
