cross-fertilises:/ˌkrɒsˈfɜːtəlaɪzɪz/| v. |交叉授粉；互相影响；互相借鉴 |To fertilize (a plant) with pollen from a different plant of the same species, or to exchange ideas, methods, or influences between different groups or fields.  | Example: The two research teams cross-fertilized their ideas, leading to a breakthrough.  两个研究团队互相借鉴了彼此的想法，从而取得了突破。


|近义词:interbreed, hybridize, influence, interact|反义词:isolate, separate|动词


USAGE->

The two cultures cross-fertilize each other's artistic traditions. = 这两种文化互相借鉴彼此的艺术传统。
