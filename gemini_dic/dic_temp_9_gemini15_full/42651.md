counterspells: /ˈkaʊntərspɛlz/ | n. | 反咒语，对抗法术 | A spell or action designed to counteract or negate the effect of another spell.  It often involves a magical response to an offensive spell. | Example: The wizard cast a counterspell to nullify the dragon's fiery breath.  =  巫师施放了一个反咒语来抵消巨龙的烈焰吐息。 |近义词: countermagic, anti-spell |反义词: spell, incantation |名词
