redrew:/ˌriːˈdruː/| vt.|重画，重新绘制|To draw again or differently.|The architect redrew the plans after the client's feedback. 建筑师根据客户的反馈重新绘制了图纸。|近义词：redraw, sketch again, revise, re-draft|反义词：erase, obliterate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He redrews the map every year. 他每年都会重新绘制地图。
She redrews the picture with new colors. 她用新的颜色重新绘制了这幅画。


一般过去时:
The artist redrew the portrait several times.艺术家重画了这幅肖像好几次。
