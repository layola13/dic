midday:/mɪdˈdeɪ/| n. |正午，中午|The middle of the day, typically from 12 noon to 1 p.m.|例句：We had lunch at midday. 我们中午吃的午饭。|例句：The sun was high in the sky at midday. 中午太阳高悬空中。|近义词：noon, midday|反义词：midnight
