piggy:/ˈpɪɡi/| n. |小猪；储蓄罐；（非正式）贪婪的人|A young pig; a small container, often made of clay or metal, used to save money; (informal) a greedy person. |例句：The farmer's wife fed the piggy.  农妇喂了小猪。 The piggy bank was full of coins. 存钱罐里装满了硬币。 He's such a piggy, always wanting more. 他真是个贪婪鬼，总是想要更多。|近义词：piglet, money box, hog (informal:greedy person)|反义词：generous person (for greedy meaning)
