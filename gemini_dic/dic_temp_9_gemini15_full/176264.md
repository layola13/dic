re-corking:/ˌriːˈkɔːrkɪŋ/ | n. |重新塞木塞；重新用软木塞塞住 | The act of replacing a cork stopper in a bottle, container, or other item.  This often involves removing the old cork and inserting a new one. | Example: The winemaker oversaw the re-corking of the aged bottles.  酿酒师监督着陈年葡萄酒的重新塞瓶工作。


The re-corking of the old decanter improved its seal.  重新塞上旧酒壶的软木塞改善了它的密封性。| 近义词：re-sealing, stoppering | 反义词：uncorking, unstopping | 名词
