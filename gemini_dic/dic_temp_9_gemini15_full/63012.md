enquired:/ɪnˈkwaɪəd/ | v. | 询问；打听 | To ask for information; to make an inquiry. |  He enquired about the price of the tickets. 他询问了票价。  She enquired after her friend's health. 她问候朋友的健康状况。 | asked, investigated, questioned |  ignored, disregarded | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enquires about the weather forecast every morning. 他每天早上都询问天气预报。
They enquire about the availability of rooms before booking. 他们预订前会询问房间是否可用。

一般过去时:
I enquired about the job opening at the company. 我询问了公司的工作机会。
She enquired if there were any vegetarian options on the menu. 她询问菜单上是否有素食选择。

现在完成时:
He has enquired about the possibility of a transfer. 他已经询问了调动的可能性。
She has enquired several times about the delivery status. 她已经多次询问了送货状态。

过去完成时:
They had enquired about the flight schedule before arriving at the airport. 他们到达机场前已经询问了航班时刻表。
I had enquired about the hotel's cancellation policy prior to booking. 我预订前已经询问了酒店的取消政策。

将来时:
I will enquire about the train times tomorrow. 我明天将询问火车时刻。
She will enquire about the cost of the repairs. 她将询问修理费用。

虚拟语气:
If I enquired politely, I think they would help me. 如果我礼貌地询问，我想他们会帮助我。
I wish I had enquired about the deadline earlier. 我希望我早点询问截止日期。

被动语态:
The price was enquired about by several customers. 价格被几个顾客询问了。
The matter is being enquired into by the police. 这件事正在被警方调查。

疑问句:
Did you enquire about the lost luggage? 你询问了丢失的行李吗？
Have they enquired about the new policy? 他们询问了新政策吗？

否定句:
He did not enquire about the terms and conditions. 他没有询问条款和条件。
She didn't enquire about the possible side effects. 她没有询问可能的副作用。


PHRASE->
enquire about = 询问关于…
enquire after = 问候（某人的健康状况）
enquire into = 调查；探究
make enquiries = 进行询问
enquire of = 向…询问
