doubter:/ˈdaʊtər/| n. |怀疑者，不信者|A doubter is someone who is not easily convinced or who questions the truth or validity of something.  They tend to be skeptical and hesitant to accept things at face value. |He remained a doubter of the new technology, despite its proven success. 他仍然对这项新技术持怀疑态度，尽管它已被证明是成功的。|skeptic, questioner, cynic|believer, enthusiast

一般过去时:
