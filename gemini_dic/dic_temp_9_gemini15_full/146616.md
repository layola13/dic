overhaul: /ˈoʊvərhɔːl/ | vt. n. | 大修；彻底检查；改进 | To thoroughly examine and repair something, especially a machine or system.  |例句：The car needs a complete overhaul. 这辆车需要彻底大修。

The company is undergoing a major overhaul of its operations.  这家公司正在对其运营进行重大改革。|近义词：repair, renovate, revamp, revise | 反义词：neglect, abandon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overhaul the engine every year. = 他们每年都会大修发动机。
The mechanic overhauls cars for a living. = 技工以修理汽车为生。

一般过去时:
We overhauled the old system last month. = 我们上个月彻底检修了旧系统。
The engineers overhauled the entire factory. = 工程师们对整个工厂进行了大修。

现在完成时:
They have overhauled the software several times. = 他们已经多次修改了软件。
The company has overhauled its marketing strategy. = 公司已经彻底修改了其营销策略。

过去完成时:
They had overhauled the engine before the race. = 比赛前他们已经检修过发动机了。
The team had overhauled the plan before submitting it. = 团队在提交计划之前已经对其进行了彻底的修改。

将来时:
We will overhaul the entire building next year. = 我们明年将对整栋建筑物进行大修。
The government will overhaul the healthcare system. = 政府将改革医疗体系。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would overhaul the equipment. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会彻底检修设备。
It's time we overhauled our outdated policies. = 我们该彻底改革我们过时的政策了。

被动语态:
The engine was overhauled by a skilled mechanic. = 发动机是由一位熟练的技工修理的。
The system is being overhauled to improve efficiency. = 正在对系统进行大修以提高效率。

疑问句:
Will they overhaul the bridge soon? = 他们会很快修理这座桥吗？
Has the computer system been overhauled recently? = 电脑系统最近是否进行了大修？

否定句:
They didn't overhaul the brakes. = 他们没有检修刹车。
The company hasn't overhauled its security protocols yet. = 公司尚未检修其安全协议。


PHRASE->

system overhaul: 系统大修
