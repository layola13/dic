pettish:/ˈpetɪʃ/| adj.|易怒的；脾气坏的；任性的|Characterized by or given to petulance; childishly sulky or bad-tempered.|例句：He was in a pettish mood after his team lost the game.  他球队输球后脾气很坏。|例句：Her pettish refusal to cooperate made the project more difficult.|她任性地拒绝合作，使项目更加困难。|近义词：irritable, grumpy, sulky, cranky, peevish|反义词：good-natured, amiable, pleasant, cheerful
