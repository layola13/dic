credentials: /krɪˈdɛnʃəlz/ | n. |资历，证明文件；凭据 |  Evidence of one's qualifications, especially those related to employment or a particular skill.  Credentials can be formal documents (like diplomas or certificates) or informal evidence of skills and experience. |

例句：

He submitted his credentials to the hiring manager.  他向招聘经理提交了他的资历证明。

Her impressive credentials secured her the job. 她令人印象深刻的资历为她赢得了这份工作。


近义词：qualifications, qualifications, testimonials, certifications, evidence, proof


反义词：lack of qualifications, incompetence


词性：名词
