posthepatic: /ˌpoʊsthɪˈpætɪk/ | adj. |肝后（的）| Relating to the area beyond the liver; occurring after the liver.  The term typically refers to the circulatory system and the flow of blood after it has passed through the liver. | Example: Posthepatic jaundice is caused by blockage of the bile ducts after the liver. 肝后性黄疸是由肝脏后胆管阻塞引起的。| 近义词: posthepatic biliary  | 反义词: prehepatic
