halidom:/ˈhælɪdəm/| n.|圣物，圣地，神圣之地|A halidom is a sacred object, place, or thing, often associated with religious relics or consecrated ground.  The term evokes a sense of sanctity and reverence.|例句：The ancient chalice was treated with the utmost reverence, as it was considered a halidom.  这古老的圣杯受到了最高的敬意，因为它被认为是圣物。

例句：Pilgrims journeyed to the halidom to seek blessings and spiritual renewal.  朝圣者前往圣地寻求祝福和精神上的更新。


近义词：relic, shrine, sanctuary|反义词：profane, unholy, sacrilege|词性:名词
