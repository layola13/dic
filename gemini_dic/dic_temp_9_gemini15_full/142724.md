otoconite:/ˈoʊtəˌkoʊnaɪt/ | n. |耳石 |A minute, crystalline particle of calcium carbonate found in the inner ear; otoconia are involved in balance and hearing.  Otoconites are tiny crystals that help the inner ear sense gravity and movement. | The malfunction of otoconites can lead to vertigo. 耳石功能障碍会导致眩晕。|  The otoconites in the inner ear are essential for maintaining balance. 内耳中的耳石对于保持平衡至关重要。|  近义词：otolith (more general term) | 反义词：无
