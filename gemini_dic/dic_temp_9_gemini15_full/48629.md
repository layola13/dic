defend:/dɪˈfɛnd/| vt. |保卫，辩护，为…辩护 |to protect someone or something from attack or criticism; to argue in favour of someone or something |例句：The soldiers defended the city bravely.  士兵们英勇地保卫了这座城市。

例句：He defended his actions in court. 他在法庭上为自己辩护。

近义词：protect, guard, uphold, champion |反义词：attack, assail, surrender, betray |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He defends his country. 他保卫他的国家。
She defends her ideas passionately. 她激情地捍卫她的想法。

一般过去时:
They defended their home against the invaders. 他们保卫家园抵御入侵者。
He defended himself against the accusations. 他为自己反驳了这些指控。

现在完成时:
The army has defended the border for decades.  军队几十年来一直在保卫边境。
She has defended her innocence throughout the trial.  她在整个审判过程中一直维护自己的清白。

过去完成时:
The soldiers had defended the castle for months before surrendering. 士兵们在投降前已经保卫了这座城堡数月之久。
They had defended their position strongly, but ultimately lost the battle. 他们顽强地捍卫了自己的阵地，但最终还是输掉了战斗。

将来时:
We will defend our rights. 我们将捍卫我们的权利。
The government will defend its policy. 政府将捍卫其政策。

虚拟语气:
If they had defended the city better, they would have won the war. 如果他们更好地保卫城市，他们就会赢得战争。
I wish I had defended myself more effectively. 我真希望我当时能更有效地为自己辩护。

被动语态:
The city was defended bravely.  这座城市得到了英勇的保卫。
His actions were defended by his lawyer. 他的行为得到了律师的辩护。

疑问句:
How will you defend yourself against these charges? 你将如何为自己反驳这些指控？
Will they defend their position? 他们会捍卫他们的立场吗？

否定句:
He did not defend his friend. 他没有为他的朋友辩护。
They failed to defend the castle. 他们未能保卫城堡。
