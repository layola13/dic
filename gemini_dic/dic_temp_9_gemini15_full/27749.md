cankers:/ˈkæŋkərz/| n.|溃疡；腐烂；坏疽|A canker is a sore or ulcer, especially in the mouth or on the skin.  It can also refer to a diseased or decayed state.|例句：The gardener noticed several cankers on the apple tree. 园丁注意到苹果树上有一些溃疡。

例句：The child developed a painful canker sore inside their cheek. 孩子脸颊内侧长了一个疼痛的溃疡。


近义词：ulcer, sore, lesion, blight


反义词：health, healing, wholeness
