principally:/ˈprɪnsɪpəli/ | adv. | 主要地；基本上 |  chiefly, mainly; for the most part.  It emphasizes that something is the most important or significant factor. | 例句：The project was principally funded by government grants. = 这个项目主要由政府拨款资助。
The success of the business is principally due to its innovative marketing strategies. = 这家企业的成功主要归功于其创新的营销策略。| 近义词：mainly, chiefly, primarily, essentially | 反义词：secondarily, subordinately, incidentally

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He principally focuses on his studies. = 他主要专注于他的学习。
They principally use renewable energy sources. = 他们主要使用可再生能源。


一般过去时:
She principally worked on the design aspects of the project. = 她主要负责项目的规划设计方面。
He principally relied on his intuition. = 他主要依靠他的直觉。


现在完成时:
We have principally concentrated on improving customer service. = 我们主要致力于改善客户服务。
They have principally focused their efforts on research and development. = 他们主要把精力集中在研发上。


过去完成时:
They had principally completed the first phase of the project. = 他们主要完成了项目的第一个阶段。
She had principally relied on her family for support. = 她主要依靠她的家人来支持。


将来时:
He will principally work from home. = 他主要将在家里工作。
They will principally use digital marketing techniques. = 他们将主要使用数字营销技巧。


虚拟语气:
If he were to focus principally on his studies, he would likely succeed. = 如果他主要专注于他的学习，他很可能会成功。
I wish I had principally focused on my career earlier. = 我希望我早些主要专注于我的事业。


被动语态:
The funds were principally allocated to infrastructure projects. = 这些资金主要被分配到基础设施项目。
The decision was principally influenced by political considerations. = 这个决定主要受到政治因素的影响。


疑问句:
What principally motivated you to take this job? = 什么主要促使你接受这份工作？
What principally concerns you about this proposal? = 你对这个提案主要担心什么？


否定句:
He didn't principally rely on luck; he worked hard. = 他并非主要依靠运气；他努力工作了。
The company's failure wasn't principally due to lack of funding. = 公司的失败并非主要由于缺乏资金。


PHRASE->
principally concerned = 主要关注
principally involved = 主要参与
principally responsible = 主要负责
principally based on = 主要基于
principally aimed at = 主要针对


词源:
principally源自principal (主要的，首要的) + -ly (副词后缀)。principal的词源追溯到拉丁语principalis，意思是“主要的，第一位的”，源于princeps，“第一公民，首领”。
