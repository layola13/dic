leakage:/ˈliːkɪdʒ/ | n. |泄漏；渗漏 | The escape of a fluid through an opening or crack.  For example, water leakage from a pipe, gas leakage from a tank, or information leakage from a secure system. |例句：There was a significant water leakage in the basement after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，地下室发生了严重的漏水。

The roof has a slight leakage. 屋顶有轻微漏水。


近义词：leak, seepage, effusion, escape |反义词：tightness, sealing, impermeability |词性:名词


词源:

The word "leakage" originates from the Old English word "lēcan," meaning "to leak, to drip."  This evolved through Middle English and eventually to the modern English "leak," with "-age" being a suffix indicating an action or process.
