elogy:/ˈɛlədʒi/| n. |挽歌，哀歌|A eulogy is a speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly, typically someone who has recently died.  It often expresses sorrow and grief, but also celebrates the life and achievements of the deceased.|例句：The eulogy at his funeral was incredibly moving.  他的葬礼上的悼词感人至深。

例句：She delivered a heartfelt eulogy at her grandmother's memorial service.  她在祖母的追悼会上发表了一篇发自肺腑的悼词。


近义词：tribute, panegyric, encomium | 反义词：insult, condemnation, denunciation | 词性:名词
