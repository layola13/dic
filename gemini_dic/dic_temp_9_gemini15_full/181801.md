reliquaries:/ˈrɛlɪkwɛəriːz/| n.|圣骨匣；圣物盒|A reliquary is a container for sacred relics, typically the bones of saints or other holy objects.  These containers are often ornately decorated and made of precious materials, reflecting the importance of the relics they hold.|The museum's collection included several exquisite reliquaries from the medieval period.  博物馆的藏品包括几件中世纪精美的圣骨匣。|shrine, casket, container|profane object|名词
