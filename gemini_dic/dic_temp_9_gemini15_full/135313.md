nonpatriotic:/ˌnɒnˌpætriˈɒtɪk/| adj.|不爱国的；非爱国的|Lacking patriotism; showing a lack of love or loyalty to one's country.  This adjective describes someone or something that does not demonstrate patriotism or national pride.|The nonpatriotic sentiments expressed in the online forum were met with criticism.|近义词：unpatriotic, disloyal, treasonous|反义词：patriotic, loyal
