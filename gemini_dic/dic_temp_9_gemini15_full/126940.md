narrated:/ˈnæreɪtɪd/| v. |叙述的；讲述的 |To tell a story or give an account of something.  |Example: He narrated the story of his adventures in the Amazon. 他讲述了他亚马逊探险的故事。  The film was narrated by Morgan Freeman. 这部电影由摩根·弗里曼旁白。|近义词： recounted, related, described |反义词： silenced, unheard

一般现在时:
He narrates his life story every week. 他每周都讲述他的生活故事。
She narrates the news with a calm voice. 她用平静的声音播报新闻。

一般过去时:
The author narrated a captivating tale of love and loss. 作者讲述了一个引人入胜的爱恨情仇的故事。
He narrated the events leading up to the accident. 他叙述了导致事故发生之前的事件。

现在完成时:
She has narrated several bestselling novels. 她已经为几部畅销小说做过旁白。
He has narrated his experiences in a powerful memoir. 他在他的有力回忆录中讲述了他的经历。

过去完成时:
Before the trial began, the witness had already narrated his version of events. 在审判开始之前，证人已经讲述了他对事件的看法。
She had narrated the story many times before, so she knew it by heart. 她以前讲过很多次这个故事了，所以她烂熟于心。

将来时:
He will narrate his experiences to the students tomorrow. 他明天将向学生们讲述他的经历。
She will narrate the documentary about the war. 她将为关于战争的纪录片配音。

虚拟语气:
If he narrated the story differently, it would have been more impactful. 如果他以不同的方式讲述这个故事，它将会更有影响力。
I wish he had narrated the story more clearly. 我希望他把故事讲得更清楚一些。

被动语态:
The story was narrated in a compelling way. 故事以引人入胜的方式讲述。
The documentary will be narrated by a famous actor. 这部纪录片将由一位著名的演员配音。

疑问句:
Who narrated the audiobook? 谁为有声书配音？
Will she narrate the upcoming film? 她会为即将上映的电影配音吗？

否定句:
He did not narrate the events accurately. 他没有准确地叙述这些事件。
She didn't narrate the story from her own perspective. 她没有从自己的角度讲述这个故事。
