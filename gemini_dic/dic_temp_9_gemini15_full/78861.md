gingery:/ˈdʒɪndʒəri/ | adj. |略带姜味的；红褐色的；暴躁的，易怒的 | Having the flavor or aroma of ginger; of a reddish-brown color; irritable or fiery in temperament. |

英文解析:The word "gingery" primarily describes something with the taste or smell of ginger.  It can also refer to a reddish-brown color, similar to the color of ginger.  Furthermore, it can describe a person's temperament, suggesting they are easily angered or irritable.

例句:

The cake had a gingery flavor.  = 蛋糕有姜味。

Her hair was a beautiful gingery red. = 她的头发是美丽的红褐色。

He was feeling rather gingery after the argument. = 争吵后他感觉有点暴躁。


近义词:  (for flavor) spicy, zesty; (for color) reddish-brown, auburn; (for temperament) irritable, choleric, grumpy, snappy

反义词: (for flavor) bland, tasteless; (for color) pale, light; (for temperament) calm, placid, even-tempered


词源:  Derived from "ginger," ultimately from Old French *gingivre and ultimately from Latin zingiber, from Greek zingiberis, and ultimately from Sanskrit singabera.
