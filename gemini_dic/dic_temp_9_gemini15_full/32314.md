chigre:/ˈtʃɪɡrə/| n. |恙螨，恙虫|A chigger is a larval mite of the family Trombiculidae that is parasitic on vertebrates.  Chiggers are tiny arachnids that burrow into the skin and feed on lymph fluid, causing intense itching and irritation.  They are often found in tall grasses and weeds.|Example:  I got several chigger bites after hiking through the tall grass.  我穿过高高的草地后被好几只恙螨叮咬了。|近义词：mite, redbug, harvest mite |反义词：无
