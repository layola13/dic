plonking:/ˈplɒŋkɪŋ/ | v. |砰地放下；笨拙地放下；无目的地放置 |To place something down heavily and without care.  It often implies a lack of grace or a sense of being clumsy or unceremonious. |例句：He plonked the suitcase on the floor. 他砰地把行李箱扔到地板上。|例句：She plonked herself down on the sofa. 她笨拙地坐到沙发上。|近义词：dropping, dumping, thudding, plopping |反义词：placing carefully, setting gently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plonks his keys on the table every day. = 他每天都把钥匙砰地放在桌子上。
She plonks the books down haphazardly. = 她随意地把书砰地扔下。
