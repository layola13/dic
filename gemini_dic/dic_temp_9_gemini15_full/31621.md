chat: /tʃæt/ | v. n. | 聊天，闲谈 | To talk informally; a casual conversation. | 例句：They chatted about their day. 他们聊了聊他们的一天。  | 例句：We had a long chat about our plans. 我们就我们的计划进行了长时间的聊天。| 近义词：converse, talk, gossip | 反义词：silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They chat every day. = 他们每天聊天。
We chat online regularly. = 我们定期在线聊天。

一般过去时:
I chatted with my friend yesterday. = 我昨天和我的朋友聊了天。
They chatted for hours. = 他们聊了几个小时。

现在完成时:
I have chatted with him several times. = 我已经和他聊过几次了。
She has chatted with her family on video call. = 她通过视频通话与家人聊过天了。

过去完成时:
We had chatted for an hour before the meeting started. = 会议开始前我们已经聊了一个小时。
They had chatted about the project before deciding on a plan. = 在决定计划之前，他们已经讨论过这个项目了。

将来时:
I will chat with her later. = 我稍后会和她聊聊。
We will chat about it tomorrow. = 我们明天再聊这个。

虚拟语气:
I wish I could chat with him now. = 我希望我现在能和他聊聊。
If I had more time, I would chat with you longer. = 如果我有更多时间，我会和你聊更久。

被动语态:
The matter was chatted about extensively. = 这件事被广泛讨论过。
The problem is being chatted about online. = 这个问题正在网上被讨论。

疑问句:
Do you often chat with your friends? = 你经常和朋友聊天吗？
What are you chatting about? = 你在聊什么？

否定句:
I didn't chat with anyone today. = 我今天没有和任何人聊天。
They don't chat much anymore. = 他们现在聊得少了。


PHRASE->
chat room = 聊天室
