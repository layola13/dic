fatique:/fəˈtiːɡ/| n.|疲劳，劳累|Extreme tiredness resulting from mental or physical exertion or illness.|例句：The long journey caused extreme fatigue. 漫长的旅程导致了极度疲劳。|近义词：tiredness, exhaustion, weariness|反义词：energy, vigor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I feel fatigue after a long day of work. = 长时间工作后我感到疲劳。
She experiences fatigue regularly. = 她经常感到疲劳。


一般过去时:
He suffered from fatigue after the marathon. = 马拉松后他感到疲劳。
I felt intense fatigue yesterday. = 我昨天感到非常疲劳。


现在完成时:
I have experienced persistent fatigue for weeks. = 我已经持续几周感到疲劳。
She has battled fatigue since her illness. = 自从生病以来，她一直在与疲劳作斗争。


过去完成时:
He had already felt fatigue before the race even began. = 比赛开始之前他已经感到疲劳了。
She had experienced such fatigue before, so she knew what to expect. = 她以前经历过这种疲劳，所以知道会发生什么。


将来时:
I will likely feel fatigue after the hike tomorrow. = 明天徒步旅行后我可能会感到疲劳。
She anticipates feeling fatigue after the long flight. = 她预料到长途飞行后会感到疲劳。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't worked so hard, I wouldn't feel this fatigue. = 如果我不那么努力工作，我就不会感到这么疲劳。
I wish I didn't feel this overwhelming fatigue. = 我希望我不感到如此强烈的疲劳。


被动语态:
His fatigue was caused by lack of sleep. = 他的疲劳是由于睡眠不足引起的。
The fatigue is often attributed to stress. = 疲劳通常归因于压力。


疑问句:
Is your fatigue affecting your daily life? = 你的疲劳是否影响了你的日常生活？
Are you experiencing any unusual fatigue? = 你是否感到任何异常疲劳？


否定句:
I don't feel any fatigue today. = 我今天没有感到任何疲劳。
She doesn't suffer from chronic fatigue. = 她没有患慢性疲劳症。


PHRASE->
mental fatigue: 精神疲劳
physical fatigue: 身体疲劳
chronic fatigue: 慢性疲劳
fatigue fracture: 疲劳骨折
combat fatigue: 战时疲劳
