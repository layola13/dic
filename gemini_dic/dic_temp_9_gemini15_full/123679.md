moodishly:/ˈmuːdɪʃli/ | adv. | 忧郁地；脾气坏地；喜怒无常地 | In a moody or sulky manner; characterized by or displaying a gloomy or sullen mood.  |例句：She spoke moodishly about her lost love. 她忧郁地谈论着失去的爱人。

She walked moodishly through the park, avoiding eye contact with anyone. 她郁闷地走在公园里，避免与任何人进行眼神接触。


近义词：sulkily, sullenly, glumly, morosely | 反义词：cheerfully, happily, brightly, joyfully | 词性：副词
