revocations:/ˌrevəˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |撤销；废除；取消 |The act of revoking something, such as a license, permit, or order.  It refers to the formal annulment or cancellation of something previously granted or enacted. |例句：The company announced the revocations of several faulty products. 公司宣布撤销几款有缺陷的产品。

例句：The revocations of their driving licenses left them stranded.  吊销他们的驾照使他们陷入困境。


近义词：rescindment, annulment, cancellation, repeal

反义词：granting, confirmation, ratification

词性：名词
