punchin:/ˈpʌntʃɪn/ | n. |冲压；冲压件 |A punchin is a process of shaping metal by pressing it into a die. It is also the resulting shaped metal piece.  |例句：The factory uses punchin to create metal parts for cars.  这家工厂使用冲压来制造汽车零件。  |例句：This metal bracket is a precision punchin. 这个金属支架是一个精密冲压件。 |近义词：stamping, pressing |反义词：casting (铸造), forging (锻造)
