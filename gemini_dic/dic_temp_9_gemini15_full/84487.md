haltingness:/ˈhɔːltɪŋnəs/| n. |犹豫；迟疑；不流畅；结巴 |The haltingness of her speech betrayed her nervousness.  她的说话结巴暴露了她的紧张。|近义词：hesitation, faltering, stammering |反义词：fluency, smoothness


例句：

The haltingness of his explanation made it difficult to understand. 他解释的犹豫使得难以理解。

Her performance was marred by a certain haltingness in her movements. 她的表演因为动作的某种迟疑而受损。
