metre:/ˈmiːtər/ | n. |米；公尺；韵律；节拍|A unit of length equal to 100 centimeters (approximately 39.37 inches).  It is the base unit of length in the International System of Units (SI).|The table measures two metres in length. 这张桌子长两米。|The poem has a regular metre. 这首诗有规律的韵律。|kilometer, yard|inch, foot|名词
