craftiest:/ˈkræftiɪst/| adj.|最狡猾的，最精明的|Most skillful and clever, often in a deceitful way.  It is the superlative form of "crafty."|例句：The craftiest fox in the forest outsmarted all the hunters.  森林里最狡猾的狐狸智胜了所有的猎人。

例句：He used his craftiest tactics to win the negotiation. 他用了最精明的策略赢得了谈判。


近义词：most cunning, most shrewd, most sly, most ingenious, most wily

反义词：naive, ingenuous, simple, gullible, forthright


词性：形容词
