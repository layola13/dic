semilapidified:/ˌsɛmiˈlæpɪdəfaɪd/| adj. |半石化的；部分石化的 |Partially turned to stone; in a transitional state between organic material and complete petrification.  The process is incomplete, leaving some organic material still present. |The ancient tree trunk was semi-lapidified, showing both petrified wood and remnants of its original bark.  这棵古老的树干部分石化了，既有石化的木头，也有其原始树皮的残留物。| petrifed, lithified | unpetrified, organic |
