durians:/ˈdʊəriənz/ | n. |榴莲|Durians are large, spiky fruits known for their pungent smell and creamy texture.  They are native to Southeast Asia and are considered a delicacy by some, while others find their odor repulsive. The taste is often described as a mix of sweet, creamy, and savory flavors, with hints of garlic, onion, and even turpentine.  |The durian fruit is famous for its strong smell.|近义词:暂无|反义词:暂无
