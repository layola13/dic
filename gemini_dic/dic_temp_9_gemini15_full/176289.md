re-dialling: /ˌriːˈdaɪəlɪŋ/ | v. | 重新拨号 | The act of dialing a phone number again, typically after a failed attempt.  |  例句：After the call dropped, I tried re-dialling, but still couldn't get through.  重新拨号后，我仍然无法接通。  | 近义词：redialing, calling back, trying again | 反义词：disconnecting, hanging up | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I re-dial the number every few minutes. = 我每隔几分钟就重新拨打号码。
He re-dials numbers until he gets through. = 他一直重拨号码直到接通为止。


一般过去时:
She re-dialled the number after the line went dead. = 电话中断后，她重新拨打了号码。
They re-dialled several times before giving up. = 他们尝试重拨了几次才放弃。


现在完成时:
I have re-dialled that number countless times. = 我已经无数次重拨了那个号码。
She has re-dialled all morning without success. = 她整个上午都在重拨，但没有成功。


过去完成时:
He had re-dialled the number several times before his battery died. = 他的电池没电之前，他已经重拨了好几次号码。
They had re-dialled the number numerous times before they finally got through. = 在他们最终接通之前，他们已经重拨了无数次。


将来时:
I will re-dial the number if I don't get an answer. = 如果我没有得到回应，我将重新拨打号码。
They will re-dial tomorrow morning. = 他们明天早上会重新拨打。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better phone, I wouldn't have to keep re-dialling. = 如果我有一个更好的电话，我就不用一直重拨了。
I wish I hadn't re-dialled; it only made things worse. = 我真希望我没有重拨，那样只会让事情变得更糟。


被动语态:
The number is being re-dialled repeatedly. = 号码正在反复重拨。
The number was re-dialled several times without success. = 号码重拨了好几次，但没有成功。


疑问句:
Are you re-dialling the number? = 你正在重拨号码吗？
Has he re-dialled yet? = 他已经重拨了吗？


否定句:
I am not re-dialling; I'm giving up. = 我不重拨了，我放弃了。
She did not re-dial the number after the first attempt. = 她第一次尝试后没有重拨号码。


PHRASE->
re-dialling a number = 重拨号码
repeatedly re-dialling = 反复拨打
automatically re-dialling = 自动重拨
trying to re-dial = 试图重拨
failing to re-dial = 无法重拨
