cielo:/ˈtʃɛloʊ/| n.|天空，天|Italian word for "sky," often used poetically or romantically. It evokes a sense of vastness, beauty, and serenity.  |例句：The painter captured the vibrant colors of the cielo in his masterpiece.  画家在他的杰作中捕捉到了天空鲜艳的色彩。|近义词：sky, heavens, firmament|反义词：earth, ground
