disrespected:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspɛktɪd/| adj.|被不尊重的，不受尊重的| Treated without respect; dishonored.  The feeling of being disrespected can be deeply hurtful.|例句：I felt disrespected when he ignored my suggestions. 我觉得被他忽略我的建议而受到了不尊重。

例句：She felt deeply disrespected by his casual attitude. 她觉得他漫不经心的态度让她非常受辱。


近义词：insulted, dishonored, slighted, demeaned, belittled

反义词：respected, honored, valued, esteemed


词源:
The word "disrespected" is formed by adding the prefix "dis-" (meaning "not" or "opposite of") to the past participle of the verb "respect."  "Respect" itself has roots in the Latin word "respectare," meaning "to look at again, to regard, to consider."

过去完成时:
