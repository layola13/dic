playing-around: /ˈpleɪɪŋ əˈraʊnd/ | v. |胡闹，玩耍，摆弄，瞎搞 |To engage in casual or informal activity; to experiment or try things out without a serious purpose; to trifle or flirt.  |例句：The children were playing around in the garden. 孩子们在花园里玩耍。


例句：He was just playing around with the computer, not trying to break it. 他只是在摆弄电脑，并不是想把它弄坏。


近义词：fooling around, messing around, trifling, dabbling, experimenting


反义词：working hard, being serious, focusing


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are playing around in the park. = 他们在公园里玩耍。
We are playing around with new ideas for the project. = 我们正在尝试项目的新想法。

一般过去时:
The kids played around in the sand. = 孩子们在沙滩上玩耍。
She played around with the camera settings. = 她摆弄着相机的设置。

现在完成时:
They have been playing around all afternoon. = 他们整个下午都在玩耍。
We have played around with several different designs. = 我们已经尝试了几种不同的设计。

过去完成时:
They had played around before dinner. = 他们晚饭前玩耍过。
She had played around with the software before the presentation. = 她在演示文稿之前已经试用过该软件。

将来时:
They will play around in the snow tomorrow. = 他们明天将在雪地里玩耍。
We will play around with the budget numbers later. = 我们稍后会调整预算数字。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would play around with photography more often. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常地玩摄影。
I wish I could play around with that new software. = 我真希望我能玩玩那个新软件。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)  The toys were played around with by the children. = 玩具被孩子们玩耍过。


疑问句:
Are they playing around again? = 他们又在玩耍了吗？
Were you playing around with the controls? = 你是不是在摆弄控制器？

否定句:
They weren't playing around; they were working hard. = 他们不是在玩耍；他们正在努力工作。
He didn't play around with the dangerous chemicals.  = 他没有玩弄危险的化学品。


PHRASE->
play around with (something):  摆弄...，尝试...
play around with (someone):  与...调情
play around (informally):  随意玩耍
play around at (a place):  在...地方玩耍
play around with (an idea):  尝试（一个想法）
