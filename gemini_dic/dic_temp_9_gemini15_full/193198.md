seditioner:/sɪˈdɪʃənər/| n. |煽动者，叛乱者 |A person who incites rebellion or violence against a lawful authority.  |例句：The seditioner was arrested for his attempts to overthrow the government. 煽动者因试图推翻政府而被捕。|近义词：rebel, insurgent, agitator, mutineer |反义词：loyalist, patriot


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The seditioner spreads false rumors. = 煽动者散布谣言。
He incites the people to violence. = 他煽动人民暴力。


一般过去时:
The seditioner planned a coup. = 煽动者策划了一场政变。
They arrested the seditioner last night. = 他们昨晚逮捕了煽动者。


现在完成时:
The seditioner has caused much unrest. = 煽动者已经引起了很多动荡。
They have identified the seditioner. = 他们已经找到了煽动者。


过去完成时:
The police had already tracked the seditioner before the riot. = 在暴乱发生前，警方已经追踪到了煽动者。
The rebellion had been planned by a group of seditioners. = 叛乱是由一群煽动者策划的。


将来时:
The seditioner will face trial soon. = 煽动者很快将面临审判。
The authorities believe the seditioner will attempt another attack. = 当局认为煽动者将会再次发动袭击。


虚拟语气:
If he were not a seditioner, he would be free. = 如果他不是煽动者，他就会自由。
I wish the seditioner had not escaped. = 我真希望煽动者没有逃脱。


被动语态:
The seditioner was captured by the police. = 煽动者被警方抓获。
The seditioner's actions were condemned by the public. = 煽动者的行为受到公众谴责。


疑问句:
Is he really a seditioner? = 他真的是煽动者吗？
Who is the seditioner behind the protests? = 领导抗议的煽动者是谁？


否定句:
He is not a seditioner; he's a political activist. = 他不是煽动者；他是一位政治活动家。
The evidence did not prove he was a seditioner. = 证据没有证明他是煽动者。

词源:
From Middle English *sediciounour, from Old French sedicioner, from sedition (see sedition).
