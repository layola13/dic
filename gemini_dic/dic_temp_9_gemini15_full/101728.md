irresistable: /ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəbl/ | adj. | 不可抗拒的，难以抵挡的 |  Something irresistible is so attractive, tempting, or enjoyable that one cannot resist it.  It describes something that compels attention or action. |

例句：
The chocolate cake was irresistible. = 巧克力蛋糕太诱人了，无法抗拒。

The offer was simply irresistible. = 这个提议实在太诱人了，无法拒绝。


近义词：alluring, tempting, captivating, enchanting, compelling, fascinating

反义词：resistible, repellent, repulsive, unattractive

词源:
The word "irresistible" comes from the prefix "ir-" (meaning "not") + "resist" (meaning "to withstand, oppose") + "-ible" (a suffix forming adjectives).  Therefore, it literally means "not able to be resisted."
