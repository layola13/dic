rejects:/rɪˈdʒɛkts/| vt. n.|拒绝；驳回；淘汰；不合格品|To refuse to accept, consider, or grant something.|例句：The committee rejected his proposal. 委员会否决了他的提案。|例句：The factory produces many rejects.  这家工厂生产许多次品。|近义词：refuse, deny, dismiss|反义词：accept, approve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rejects all offers of help. 他拒绝一切帮助。
They reject the new policy. 他们拒绝新的政策。

一般过去时:
She rejected his marriage proposal. 她拒绝了他的求婚。
The court rejected the appeal. 法院驳回了上诉。

现在完成时:
He has rejected several job offers. 他已经拒绝了好几份工作邀请。
The company has rejected our bid. 公司拒绝了我们的投标。

过去完成时:
She had rejected his advances before he even proposed.  在她求婚之前她就拒绝了他的追求。
They had rejected the plan before the meeting even started.  会议还没开始他们就已经否决了计划。


将来时:
I think he will reject my idea. 我认为他会拒绝我的想法。
They will reject the application if it's incomplete.如果申请不完整，他们会拒绝。

虚拟语气:
If he rejected the offer, he would regret it. 如果他拒绝了这个提议，他会后悔的。
I wish he hadn’t rejected my help. 我真希望他没有拒绝我的帮助。

被动语态:
His application was rejected. 他的申请被拒绝了。
The proposal was rejected by the board.  该提案被董事会否决了。

疑问句:
Did they reject your application? 他们拒绝你的申请了吗？
Will the committee reject the proposal? 委员会会否决这项提案吗？

否定句:
He did not reject my suggestion. 他没有拒绝我的建议。
The company doesn't reject qualified applicants.  公司不拒绝合格的申请者。
