gombo:/ˈɡʌmboʊ/| n.|冈博汤，秋葵汤|A thick soup or stew, typically containing okra, a dark roux, and meat or shellfish.  It's a staple of Louisiana Creole and Cajun cuisine.|Example:  We had a delicious gumbo for dinner last night. 昨晚我们吃了一顿美味的冈博汤。|近义词：stew, soup|反义词：N/A
