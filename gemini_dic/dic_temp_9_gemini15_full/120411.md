ministries:/ˈmɪnɪstriːz/ | n. | 部门；各部；职责；工作 | The word "ministries" refers to government departments or branches responsible for specific areas of public administration.  It can also refer to the work or duties of a minister or priest. | Example 1: The government's various ministries are working together on this project. 政府的各个部门正在合作开展这个项目。 | Example 2:  The ministries of the church are vital to its function. 教会的职责对其运作至关重要。|近义词：departments, offices, functions |反义词：N/A |名词
