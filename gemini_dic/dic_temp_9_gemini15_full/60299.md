eggs:/ɛɡz/| n.|鸡蛋；卵|The plural form of "egg," referring to the oval-shaped reproductive bodies produced by birds, reptiles, amphibians, and some other animals.  Eggs are often eaten as food. |例句：I bought a dozen eggs at the supermarket. 我在超市买了十二个鸡蛋。

例句：The hen laid two brown eggs this morning. 母鸡今天早上下了两个棕色的蛋。


近义词：ovums (formal), spawn (fish eggs)
反义词：None directly, but  "none" could be considered an antonym in the context of having no eggs.
词性:名词
