sharan:/ˈʃærən/| n. |沙伦（女子名）；（希伯来语）平原| A female given name of Hebrew origin, meaning "plain".  It is also a place name, referring to a plain in Israel. |例句：Her name is Sharan. 她的名字是沙伦。

例句：The Sharan Plain is known for its fertile land. 沙伦平原以其肥沃的土地而闻名。


近义词：Sharon (variant spelling)


反义词：无


词性：名词
