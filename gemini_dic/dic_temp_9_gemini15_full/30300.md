cenobitical:/ˌsiːnəˈbɪtɪkəl/| adj. | 共栖的；禁欲生活的；苦行僧的 | Relating to or characteristic of cenobitism, a system of living in a community, especially a monastic community, sharing everything in common.  The word implies a communal and often austere lifestyle. | Example: The cenobitical life of the monks involved shared meals, prayer, and work. 共栖生活的僧侣生活包括共享膳食、祈祷和工作。 | 近义词：communal, monastic, ascetic | 反义词：individualistic, solitary, hedonistic
