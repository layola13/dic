overstimulates:/ˌoʊvərˈstɪmjuleɪts/| v. |过度刺激；兴奋过度 |To stimulate excessively; to excite to an excessive degree.  This often leads to negative consequences like anxiety, stress, or burnout. |例句：The bright lights and loud music overstimulated the child, making him fussy and irritable.  = 耀眼的光线和嘈杂的音乐过度刺激了孩子，使他变得烦躁易怒。
The constant stream of information overstimulates many people, leading to information overload. = 不断涌入的信息使许多人过度兴奋，导致信息过载。|近义词：overexcite, overtax, overwhelm |反义词：understimulate, soothe, calm
