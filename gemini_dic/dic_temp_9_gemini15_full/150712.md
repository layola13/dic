parabolist: /ˈpærəbəlɪst/ | n. | 寓言家，讲寓言的人 | A person who tells or writes parables.  Someone skilled in using parables to teach or illustrate a moral or spiritual lesson. | Example: The parabolist used the story of the sower to illustrate the importance of perseverance.  寓言家用播种者的故事来说明坚持不懈的重要性。| 近义词：storyteller, moralist, allegorist | 反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to those who oppose moral instruction.
