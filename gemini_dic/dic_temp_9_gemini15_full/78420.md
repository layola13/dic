get over:/ˌɡɛtˈoʊvər/ (US) /ˌɡɛtˈəʊvə/ (UK) | v. |克服；恢复；原谅；结束；越过 |To overcome a problem, illness, or feeling; to recover from something; to forgive someone; to finish something; to cross or jump over something.  |例句：He's trying to get over his fear of public speaking. 他正在努力克服他的公开演讲恐惧症。

例句：She's still getting over the flu. 她仍在恢复感冒。


近义词： overcome, recover from, forgive, finish, cross, jump |反义词：succumb to, dwell on, harbor resentment against, remain unfinished, stay put


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I get over my anxiety by exercising. = 我通过锻炼来克服我的焦虑。
They get over their disagreements quickly. = 他们很快就能克服分歧。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
I have gotten over my initial shyness. = 我已经克服了最初的羞怯。
They have gotten over the hurdle of funding. = 他们已经克服了资金的障碍。


过去完成时:

将来时:
I will get over this heartbreak eventually. = 我最终会从这段恋情中走出来的。
He will get over the wall easily. = 他很容易就能翻过墙。


虚拟语气:
