emotions: /ɪˈmoʊʃənz/ | n. |情感，情绪 | Feelings or states of consciousness, such as joy, sorrow, fear, and anger.  Emotions are subjective experiences that are typically accompanied by physiological changes, such as changes in heart rate, blood pressure, and respiration. They play a crucial role in human behavior and social interactions. |例句：He struggled to control his emotions during the difficult conversation. 他努力控制在艰难的谈话中的情绪。

例句：Her emotions were a rollercoaster after receiving the news.  她收到消息后情绪像坐过山车一样起伏不定。


近义词：feelings, sentiments, passions | 反义词：apathy, indifference
