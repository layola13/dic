decarbonizations:/ˌdiːkɑːrbənaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 脱碳化（行为或过程） | The process of reducing carbon dioxide emissions.  It involves transitioning to cleaner energy sources, improving energy efficiency, and implementing carbon capture technologies. | Example:  The government is investing heavily in decarbonizations initiatives to combat climate change.  政府正在大力投资脱碳化倡议以应对气候变化。  |近义词: carbon reduction, decarbonisation |反义词: carbon intensification
