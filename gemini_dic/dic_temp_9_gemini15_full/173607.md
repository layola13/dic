quasi-informal: /ˈkwɑːzaɪɪnˈfɔːrməl/ | adj. | 准非正式的，介于正式和非正式之间的 |  Describes language or communication style that is somewhat informal but not entirely casual; it sits between formal and informal styles, often characterized by a relaxed yet polite tone. It might include some colloquialisms or contractions but avoids slang and overly familiar language.  | Example: "The email was written in a quasi-informal style, using contractions but maintaining a respectful tone."  | Near synonyms: semi-formal, informal but polite, relaxed but professional | Antonyms: formal, stiff, overly casual |  词性:形容词
