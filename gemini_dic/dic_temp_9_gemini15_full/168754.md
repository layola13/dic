prodelta:/ˈproʊˌdeltə/| n. |三角洲前缘沉积 |A prodelta is the gently sloping, fine-grained sediment deposit that extends beyond the main delta front into deeper water. It's characterized by slow-moving currents and finer sediments, like clays and silts, compared to the coarser sediments of the delta front itself.  The prodelta is typically the site of deposition of hemipelagic sediments, which are a mixture of terrestrial and marine-derived materials. |The prodelta environment is crucial for understanding the evolution of deltas and coastal environments.  The study of prodelta sediments can provide valuable insights into past sea-level changes, climate variations, and sediment transport processes.  |近义词：foredelta, delta plain (partially)|反义词：delta front
