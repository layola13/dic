implicate:/ˈɪmplɪkeɪt/| vt.|牵连；暗示；含蓄地表达|to suggest that someone is guilty of a crime or responsible for something bad, but without saying it directly; to show that something is connected to something else|The evidence implicates him in the robbery.  证据表明他参与了抢劫案。  The study implicates a link between smoking and lung cancer. 这项研究暗示了吸烟与肺癌之间的联系。|incriminate, involve, suggest|exculpate, exonerate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence implicates the suspect in the crime. = 证据牵连嫌疑犯参与了犯罪。
This study implicates poor diet in heart disease. = 这项研究暗示不良饮食与心脏病有关。


一般过去时:
The witness implicated the defendant in the fraud. = 证人牵连被告参与了欺诈案。
The report implicated the company in environmental damage. = 报告暗示该公司造成了环境破坏。


现在完成时:
The investigation has implicated several high-ranking officials. = 调查已经牵连了几位高级官员。
Recent research has implicated stress as a contributing factor to illness. = 最近的研究表明压力是导致疾病的一个因素。


过去完成时:
By the end of the trial, the prosecution had implicated all the defendants. = 在审判结束时，检方已经牵连了所有被告。
It turned out that the faulty wiring had implicated the entire electrical system. = 事实证明，错误的线路已经牵连了整个电力系统。


将来时:
The upcoming report will likely implicate several government agencies. = 即将来临的报告可能会牵连几个政府机构。
Further investigation will implicate more people in the scandal. = 进一步的调查将牵连更多的人卷入丑闻。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence implicated him, he would be arrested. = 如果证据牵连了他，他就会被逮捕。
If the study had implicated a different cause, the conclusions would be different. = 如果研究暗示了不同的原因，结论就会不同。


被动语态:
He was implicated in a major financial scandal. = 他卷入了一场重大的金融丑闻。
The company was implicated in the environmental disaster. = 公司卷入了这场环境灾难。


疑问句:
Does the new evidence implicate anyone else? = 新证据是否牵连了其他人？
Does this study implicate any specific genes in the disease? = 这项研究是否暗示了该疾病中的任何特定基因？


否定句:
The investigation did not implicate the CEO in the wrongdoing. = 调查没有牵连首席执行官参与不法行为。
The report did not implicate any specific individuals. = 报告没有牵连任何特定个人。



PHRASE->
be implicated in = 卷入...
implicated in a crime = 牵涉到犯罪
implicate oneself = 自我牵连
implicated evidence = 牵连的证据
indirectly implicate = 间接牵连
