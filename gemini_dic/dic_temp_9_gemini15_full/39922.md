constrained:/kənˈstreɪnt/ | adj. | 受约束的；受限制的；被迫的 | limited or restricted in scope, extent, or freedom; feeling or expressing constraint |  The constrained budget forced us to cut some features. 受限的预算迫使我们削减了一些功能。  The constrained atmosphere made it difficult to relax. 拘谨的气氛让人难以放松。 | restricted, limited, confined, hampered | free, unrestricted, open |  形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is constrained by a tight deadline. = 这个项目受到严格期限的限制。
Her movements are constrained by her injury. = 她的行动受到伤势的限制。


一般过去时:
We were constrained by the rules. = 我们受规则限制。
The team felt constrained by the lack of resources. = 团队因缺乏资源而感到受限。


现在完成时:
The company has been constrained by economic downturn. = 公司一直受到经济衰退的限制。
His creativity has been constrained by the rigid structure. = 他的创造力受到僵化结构的限制。


过去完成时:
They had been constrained by previous commitments. = 他们之前受之前的承诺限制。
The artist had felt constrained by the traditional style. = 艺术家曾感到受传统风格的限制。


将来时:
The project will be constrained by the available funding. = 项目将受到可用资金的限制。

虚拟语气:
If the budget were not constrained, we could do more. = 如果预算不受限制，我们可以做得更多。
I wish I weren't constrained by time. = 我希望我不受时间的限制。


被动语态:

疑问句:
