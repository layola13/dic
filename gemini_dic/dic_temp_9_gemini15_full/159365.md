例句：She remained poker-faced when she heard the bad news, giving no indication of her feelings. 她听到坏消息时仍然面无表情，没有表现出任何情绪。


近义词：expressionless, impassive, emotionless, stoic, unemotional

反义词：expressive, animated, emotional, demonstrative

词源:
The term originates from the game of poker, where players try to conceal their hand and emotions through an expressionless face.  The image of a player with an unchanging expression during a poker game gave rise to the figurative use of "poker-faced" to describe someone who is concealing their feelings.
