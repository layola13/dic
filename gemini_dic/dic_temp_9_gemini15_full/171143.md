psychobabbled:/ˈsaɪkoʊˌbæbəld/ | v. |  胡说八道（心理学术语）；使用心理学术语来含糊其辞 | To talk vaguely and using psychological jargon, often in a way that is meaningless or misleading. | 例句：He psychobabbled about repressed childhood trauma, but offered no real solutions. 他含糊其辞地谈论着被压抑的童年创伤，但却没提供任何真正的解决方案。


例句：The therapist's session was filled with psychobabble, leaving the patient more confused than before.  治疗师的谈话充满了心理学术语的胡言乱语，让病人比以前更困惑了。


近义词：jargonize, waffle, ramble, obfuscate


反义词：speak plainly, articulate clearly, communicate directly


词性：动词
