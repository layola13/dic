chopstick:/ˈtʃɒpˌstɪk/|/ˈtʃɑːpˌstɪk/| n. |筷子|A pair of thin sticks used for eating, especially in East Asian countries. Chopsticks are typically made of wood, bamboo, or plastic.|例句：I need a pair of chopsticks to eat this noodle soup.  我需要一双筷子来吃这碗面汤。|例句：She skillfully used chopsticks to pick up the small dumplings. 她熟练地用筷子夹起小小的饺子。|近义词：eating sticks|反义词：fork, spoon
