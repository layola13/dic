finpike:/ˈfɪnpaɪk/| n. |尖吻鱼，细长鱼| A finpike is a type of fish characterized by its long, slender body and pointed snout.  They are often found in freshwater environments. |例句：The finpike darted through the reeds. 尖吻鱼穿过芦苇迅速游动。|近义词：gar, needlefish |反义词:  None specifically, but words describing bulky fish could be considered antonyms.
