quib:/kwɪb/| v. n.| 诡辩；吹毛求疵；小争论 |To argue or raise objections about a trivial matter.  To make petty objections or criticisms. |例句：He quibbled over the price of the car. 他对汽车的价格吹毛求疵。


例句：They quibbled about the details of the contract. 他们对合同的细节争论不休。

近义词： cavil, carp, quibble,  bicker

反义词：agree, concede, accept


词性：动词，名词
