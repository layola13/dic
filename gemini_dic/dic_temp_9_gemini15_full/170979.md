pseudoprophet:/ˈsuːdoʊˈprɑːfɪt/| n. |假先知，冒牌先知 |A pseudoprophet is someone who falsely claims to be a prophet or to have divine inspiration, often for personal gain or to deceive others.  They may make predictions that are vague or easily misinterpreted, or promote beliefs that are not based on genuine religious or spiritual authority. | Example: The cult leader was exposed as a pseudoprophet after his predictions failed to materialize and his financial dealings were investigated.  那位邪教领袖在预言落空且其财务交易受到调查后，被揭露为假先知。|近义词：false prophet, imposter, charlatan, fraud |反义词：true prophet, genuine spiritual leader
