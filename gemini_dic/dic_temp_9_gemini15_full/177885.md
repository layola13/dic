rechallenge:/ˌriːˈtʃæləndʒ/| vt.|再次挑战，重新质疑|To challenge again; to question or dispute something again.|例句：The scientist decided to rechallenge the existing theory with new evidence.  科学家决定用新的证据再次挑战现有的理论。

例句：The athlete decided to rechallenge the world record after his previous attempt failed. 运动员在之前的尝试失败后决定再次挑战世界纪录。


近义词：challenge again, question again, dispute again|反义词：accept, concede|动词
