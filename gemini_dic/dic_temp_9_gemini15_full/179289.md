redividing:/ˌriːdɪˈvaɪdɪŋ/| v.|重新划分，再分配 |The act of dividing something again, often after a previous division.  It implies a further subdivision or rearrangement of already divided elements. |例句：The company is redividing its marketing budget to focus on digital advertising. 公司正在重新分配其市场营销预算，重点放在数字广告上。

The government is redividing the electoral districts to reflect population changes. 政府正在重新划分选区以反映人口变化。|近义词：reapportioning, redistributing, subdividing |反义词：unifying, consolidating

They are redividing the land into smaller plots. = 他们正在将土地重新划分成更小的地块。
The team is redividing the tasks to improve efficiency. = 团队正在重新分配任务以提高效率。

一般过去时:

过去完成时:

After the initial division, they had redivided the inheritance among the heirs. = 在最初的分配之后，他们已经将遗产重新分配给了继承人。
