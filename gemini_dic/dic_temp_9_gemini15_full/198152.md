shopping:/ˈʃɒpɪŋ/ /ˈʃɑːpɪŋ/| n.|购物；采购|The act of buying goods, typically in stores.|例句：I enjoy shopping for clothes. 我喜欢买衣服。|例句：We did our weekly shopping at the supermarket. 我们在超市进行了每周的购物。|近义词：purchasing, buying|反义词：selling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I go shopping every Saturday. = 我每个星期六都去购物。
She shops for groceries online. = 她在线购买杂货。

一般过去时:
We went shopping yesterday. = 我们昨天去购物了。
He shopped for a new car last week. = 他上周买了一辆新车。

现在完成时:
I have been shopping all morning. = 我整个上午都在购物。
She has shopped at that store many times. = 她在那家店买过很多次东西了。

过去完成时:
I had already finished shopping before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经买完了东西。
They had shopped at the mall before the sale started. = 销售开始之前他们已经在商场购物了。

将来时:
I will go shopping tomorrow. = 我明天要去购物。
We will be shopping for furniture next weekend. = 我们下周末将要购买家具。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would go shopping more often. = 如果我有更多钱，我会更频繁地去购物。
I wish I could go shopping right now. = 我真希望我现在能去购物。

被动语态:
The groceries were shopped for online. = 杂货是在网上购买的。 (This is less common; a more natural phrasing would be "The groceries were bought online.")
The sale items were quickly shopped up. = 促销商品很快就被抢购一空。(This uses "shop up" informally)

疑问句:
Are you going shopping today? = 你今天要去购物吗？
What are you shopping for? = 你买什么？

否定句:
I am not going shopping today. = 我今天不去购物。
We didn't go shopping last weekend. = 我们上周末没去购物。


PHRASE->
go shopping = 去购物
window shopping = 逛街(不买东西)
online shopping = 网购
Christmas shopping = 圣诞节购物
shopping spree = 疯狂购物
