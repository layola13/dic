harmfully: /ˈhɑːrmfəli/ | adv. |有害地，伤人地 |In a way that causes harm; detrimentally.|例句：The chemical was handled harmfully, leading to a spill.  这种化学物质处理不当，导致泄漏。|近义词：injuriously, detrimentally, damagingly |反义词：beneficially, helpfully, advantageously


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory operates harmfully, polluting the river. = 工厂运作有害，污染了河流。
They speak harmfully about their neighbors. = 他们恶意地谈论他们的邻居。


一般过去时:
He acted harmfully, causing significant damage. = 他行事有害，造成了重大损失。
She spoke harmfully to her friend, hurting her feelings. = 她对朋友恶语相向，伤了她的心。


现在完成时:
Their actions have harmfully impacted the environment. = 他们的行为已经对环境造成了有害的影响。
The company has harmfully misled its customers. = 这家公司已经误导了它的客户。


过去完成时:
They had harmfully neglected their responsibilities. = 他们此前一直有害地忽视了他们的责任。
The government had harmfully ignored the warnings. = 政府此前一直有害地忽略了警告。


将来时:
This policy will harmfully affect small businesses. = 这项政策将对小型企业产生有害影响。
His actions will harmfully damage his reputation. = 他的行为将会严重损害他的名誉。


虚拟语气:

I wish they hadn't harmfully criticized my work. = 我真希望他们没有恶意批评我的工作。


被动语态:

The community was harmfully affected by the decisions. = 社区受到了这些决定的有害影响。


疑问句:

否定句:
The drug was not harmfully tested. = 该药物没有经过有害的测试。
The project was not harmfully influenced by outside forces. = 该项目没有受到外部势力的有害影响。

词源:

harmfully 是由 harmful (有害的) + -ly (副词后缀) 构成。 Harmful 源自古英语 *hearme "harm, injury,"  与古高地德语 *hermo, 古诺尔斯语 *hermi 相关。  "-ly"  是常见的英语副词后缀，表示方式或状态。
