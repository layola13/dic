fizzed:/fɪzd/| v.|发出嘶嘶声；（饮料）变汽；失败；|to make or let out a hissing sound; (of a drink) to lose its carbonation; to fail.|The soda fizzed when I opened it.  = 我打开苏打水时，它发出嘶嘶声。

The party fizzled out after midnight. = 派对在午夜后就失败了。|fizz, hiss, sputter|boom, succeed|动词
USAGE->

The soda fizzes when it's opened. = 打开苏打水时，它会发出嘶嘶声。
The champagne fizzes in the glass. = 香槟在杯中冒泡。

The battery fizzed and died. = 电池发出嘶嘶声然后没电了。
The project fizzled out due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，项目失败了。

The drink has fizzed out. = 饮料已经没气了。
The excitement has fizzled out. = 兴奋劲儿已经过去了。
