polybasic:/ˌpɑːliˈbeɪsɪk/| adj. | 多元的；多碱性的 |  Having or containing more than one basic group.  A polybasic acid is an acid that can donate more than one proton (H⁺) per molecule. This term is commonly used in chemistry to describe acids with multiple acidic hydrogens.  | Example: Phosphoric acid (H₃PO₄) is a polybasic acid because it can donate three protons.  磷酸(H₃PO₄)是一种多碱性酸，因为它可以提供三个质子。| 近义词：polyprotic, multibasic | 反义词：monobasic
