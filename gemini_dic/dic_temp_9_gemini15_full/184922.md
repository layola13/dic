retape:/riːˈteɪp/| vt.|重新录音；重新粘贴|To record something again, typically onto a different tape or medium;  To attach something again using tape.|例句：We had to retape the interview because of background noise.  我们不得不因为背景噪音重新录音。|例句：I had to retape the poster after it fell off the wall. 我不得不把从墙上掉下来的海报重新粘贴上。|近义词：rerecord, re-record, reattach|反义词：remove, detach


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retapes old family movies onto DVDs. = 他把旧家庭电影重新录制到DVD上。
She retapes the loose edges of the carpet. = 她重新粘贴地毯松动的边缘。

一般过去时:
They retaped the entire performance last night. = 他们昨晚重新录制了整个演出。
The technician retaped the wires to the computer. = 技术人员重新将电线粘贴到电脑上。

现在完成时:
We have retaped the audio several times, and it is still not perfect. = 我们已经多次重新录音了，但仍然不完美。
She has retaped the torn pages of the book. = 她已经重新粘贴了书中撕破的页面。

过去完成时:
They had retaped the broken vase before the guests arrived. = 客人到来之前，他们已经重新粘贴好破花瓶了。
The artist had retaped the canvas before adding more paint. = 艺术家在添加更多颜料之前已经重新粘贴了画布。

将来时:
I will retape the interview tomorrow. = 我明天将重新录制采访。
He will retape the wallpaper after painting the wall. = 他会在粉刷墙壁后重新粘贴壁纸。

虚拟语气:
If I had a better microphone, I would retape the song. = 如果我有一个更好的麦克风，我会重新录制这首歌。
If she had more tape, she would retape the ripped poster. = 如果她有更多胶带，她会重新粘贴撕破的海报。

被动语态:
The interview was retaped due to technical difficulties. = 由于技术问题，采访被重新录制了。
The broken picture frame was retaped and put back on the wall. = 破损的相框被重新粘贴好，然后放回墙上。

疑问句:
Will you retape the audio before you send it? = 你在发送音频之前会重新录音吗？
Do you need to retape that loose seam? = 你需要重新粘贴那个松开的缝线吗？

否定句:
I did not retape the audio because I ran out of time. = 我没有重新录音，因为时间不够了。
She did not retape the poster because she couldn't find any tape. = 她没有重新粘贴海报，因为找不到胶带。
