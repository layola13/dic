reaccustoms:/ˌriːəˈkʌstəm/| vt.|使重新习惯，使习惯于|To accustom again; to make familiar again.|例句：It took me a while to reaccustom myself to the cold climate after living in the tropics.  在热带地区生活之后，我花了一段时间才重新适应寒冷的气候。

例句：She quickly reaccustomed herself to the demands of her job after her maternity leave. 产假结束后，她很快又适应了工作上的要求。


近义词：rehabituate, readjust, refamiliarize|反义词：disaccustom, estrange|词性：动词
