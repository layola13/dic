caged:/keɪdʒd/| adj.|关在笼中的；被囚禁的；受限制的 | confined or trapped within a cage or other enclosure; restricted or limited in freedom or movement.  |例句：The bird was caged in a small wire enclosure.  这只鸟被关在一个小的铁丝笼子里。

The singer felt caged by the constraints of her record contract.  这位歌手感觉她的唱片合约限制了她。

|近义词：imprisoned, confined, trapped, restricted |反义词：free, liberated, released
