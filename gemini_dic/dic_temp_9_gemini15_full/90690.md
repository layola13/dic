humbugs:/ˈhʌmbʌɡz/| n.|骗子；欺骗；胡说八道|A humbug is a person who is dishonest or deceitful, or something that is worthless or untrue.  It can also refer to nonsense or something that is meant to deceive.|例句：He's a complete humbug; don't trust a word he says. 他是个十足的骗子；别相信他说的任何话。|例句：That whole story is a humbug; it's simply not true.  整个故事都是骗人的；根本不是真的。|近义词：fraud, swindler, sham, hoax, deception|反义词：truth, honesty, genuineness
