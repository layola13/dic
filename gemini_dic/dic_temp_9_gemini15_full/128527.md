next:/nɛkst/| adj., adv., n.|下一个的，其次的；然后；下一次|Following immediately afterwards in time or order.  It can function as an adjective, adverb, or noun. |例句：The next train leaves at 8:00. 下一趟火车8点出发。

例句：What's next on the agenda?  议程上接下来是什么？


近义词：following, subsequent, afterward, then|反义词：previous, prior, before

词性：adj., adv., n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The next bus arrives in ten minutes. = 下一趟巴士十分钟后到达。
What is the next step in the process? = 这个流程的下一步是什么？


一般过去时:
He went to the next room. = 他去了隔壁房间。
The next day, she received a letter. = 第二天，她收到了一封信。


现在完成时:
What have you done next? = 你接下来做了什么？
I haven't decided what to do next. = 我还没决定接下来做什么。


过去完成时:
After he finished his work, he knew what to do next. = 完成工作后，他知道接下来该做什么。
Having completed the first task, she considered what to do next. = 完成第一个任务后，她考虑接下来做什么。


将来时:
What will you do next? = 你接下来会做什么？
We will discuss this matter next week. = 我们下周将讨论这件事。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would do the next thing on my list. = 如果我有更多时间，我会做我清单上的下一件事。
I wish I knew what to do next. = 我希望知道接下来该做什么。


被动语态:
The next item on the agenda will be discussed next. = 议程上的下一项将在接下来讨论。
The next step in the process will be completed by the team. = 流程的下一步将由团队完成。


疑问句:
What comes next? = 接下来的会是什么？
What's next in line? = 接下来是谁？


否定句:
There is nothing next on my schedule. = 我的日程表上没有接下来的安排。
I don't know what comes next. = 我不知道接下来会发生什么。


PHRASE->
next to:  紧挨着
next time: 下次
next door: 隔壁
the next day: 第二天
next week: 下周
