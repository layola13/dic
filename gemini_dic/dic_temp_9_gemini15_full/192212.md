scratchily:/ˈskrætʃɪli/| adj. |沙哑地；刺耳地；潦草地|Sounding harsh and unpleasant, like something being scratched; also, written or done in a careless or untidy way.|例句：He spoke scratchily into the phone. 他沙哑地对着电话说话。|例句：The report was written scratchily and lacked detail. 报告写得潦草，缺乏细节。|近义词：raspy, hoarse, rough, untidy, careless|反义词：smoothly, clearly, neatly, carefully

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat scratches scratchily at the door. = 猫抓门的声音很刺耳。
He speaks scratchily when he is tired. = 他累了的时候说话声音很沙哑。


一般过去时:
She wrote the letter scratchily and hurriedly. = 她潦草而匆忙地写了信。
He coughed scratchily throughout the presentation. = 他在整个演示过程中一直沙哑地咳嗽。


现在完成时:
The dog has scratched scratchily at the carpet all morning. = 这狗整个上午一直在用刺耳的声音抓地毯。
He has been speaking scratchily for days because of a bad cold. = 因为重感冒，他好几天说话都很沙哑。


过去完成时:

She had written scratchily in her notes, making them difficult to read. = 她笔记写得很潦草，很难阅读。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If he spoke more clearly, instead of scratchily, he would be easier to understand. = 如果他说话清晰点，而不是沙哑地，就更容易理解了。
I wish he hadn't written so scratchily; I can barely read it. = 我真希望他不要写得这么潦草，我几乎看不清。


被动语态:
The message was written scratchily and was difficult to decipher. = 这条消息写得潦草，难以辨认。

疑问句:
Is he speaking scratchily because he has a cold? = 他是因为感冒才说话沙哑的吗？
Was the signature scratchily written or deliberately unclear? = 签名是潦草写的还是故意写得模糊不清的？


否定句:
His voice was not scratchily, but pleasantly smooth and clear. = 他的声音不是沙哑的，而是令人愉悦的流畅而清晰。
She didn't write scratchily; her handwriting was neat and legible. = 她写字不潦草；她的字迹工整易读。
