rynd:/raɪnd/| n.|瘦削的；（苏格兰）山脊；|A rynd is a narrow, steep-sided ridge or a projecting piece of land, especially in Scotland.  It can also refer to something lean or thin.  The word is primarily used in Scotland.|例句：The hikers traversed the steep rynd. 登山者穿越了陡峭的山脊。The horse was a rynd creature, barely able to stand. 这匹马瘦骨嶙峋，几乎站不起来。|近义词：ridge, spine, peak, crag, lean, thin|反义词：fat, plump, obese, broad, wide
