capitulation:/ˌkæpɪtʃuˈleɪʃən/| n.| 投降；屈服；让步 | The act of surrendering or ceasing resistance; the act of yielding or submitting.  It can refer to a formal surrender in war, or a less formal yielding to pressure or demands. | Example: The enemy's capitulation brought an end to the long and bloody war.  敌人的投降结束了这场漫长而血腥的战争。

The city's capitulation to the invaders was swift and unexpected. 城市迅速而意外地向入侵者投降。|近义词：surrender, submission, concession, yielding |反义词：resistance, defiance, opposition, revolt |名词
