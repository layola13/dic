perspicil:/pərˈspɪsɪl/| n.|透镜；显微镜镜头|A perspicil is a magnifying glass or lens, especially one used in a microscope.  It is a less common term now, often replaced by more modern equivalents.|例句：The scientist carefully adjusted the perspicil to examine the tiny specimen. 科学家仔细调整透镜以检查微小的样本。|近义词：lens, magnifier, microscope objective|反义词：None specifically, but terms implying obscuring or blurring could be considered antonyms.|名词
