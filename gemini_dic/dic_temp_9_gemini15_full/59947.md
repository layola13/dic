ediacaran:/ˌiːdiˈækərən/| adj. n.|埃迪卡拉纪的；埃迪卡拉纪地层|Of or relating to the Ediacaran Period, a geologic period of the Proterozoic Eon, from 635 to 541 million years ago, characterized by the appearance of the first macroscopic multicellular organisms.|The Ediacaran Period is marked by the appearance of diverse, mostly soft-bodied, multicellular organisms, representing a major step in the evolution of life on Earth.  Ediacaran fossils are found worldwide.|近义词：Proterozoic (in a broader context)|反义词：Phanerozoic (referring to the following eon)
