nontoxicities:/ˌnɒnˌtɒksɪˈsɪtiz/ | n. |无毒性；无毒物质 |The absence of toxic effects; lack of poisonous qualities.  This term often refers to the lack of harmful effects of a substance, material, or process on living organisms or the environment.  | Example: The study demonstrated the nontoxicities of the new compound in animal models.  这项研究证明了这种新型化合物在动物模型中的无毒性。 |近义词：harmlessness, innocuousness, safety |反义词：toxicity, poisonousness, harmfulness


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense forms are not applicable.  Its usage is primarily descriptive.

Example 2:  Researchers are investigating the nontoxicities of various nanomaterials. 研究人员正在调查各种纳米材料的无毒性。
