deliriousness:/dɪˈlɪriəsnəs/| n. |神志不清；胡言乱语；极度兴奋|A state of extreme mental confusion and excitement, typically associated with a high fever or other illness.|The patient's deliriousness made it difficult for the doctors to diagnose the problem.  病人的神志不清使得医生难以诊断病情。


Her deliriousness prevented her from understanding simple instructions.  她的神志不清让她无法理解简单的指示。
