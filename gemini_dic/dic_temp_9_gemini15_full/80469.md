gonoblastid: /ˌɡoʊnoʊˈblæstɪd/ | n. |生殖芽, 胚芽 | A gonoblastid is a structure in certain invertebrates, particularly hydrozoans, that produces gonozooids (individuals specialized for reproduction). It's essentially a reproductive bud or outgrowth.  | Example: The gonoblastid developed into a mature gonozooid, ready to release gametes.  生殖芽发育成了成熟的生殖个体，准备释放配子。 |近义词: reproductive bud, gonozooid precursor |反义词:  None readily available.  |名词
