non-germinating:/ˌnɒnˈdʒɜːmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |不发芽的，未发芽的 | Not sprouting or beginning to grow.  This term is typically used to describe seeds or spores that have not yet begun the process of germination. | Example: The non-germinating seeds were discarded.  这些未发芽的种子被丢弃了。 The farmer examined the non-germinating seeds to determine the cause of the poor yield.  农民检查了未发芽的种子以确定产量低的原因。|近义词：dormant, inactive, undeveloped |反义词：germinating, sprouting, growing
