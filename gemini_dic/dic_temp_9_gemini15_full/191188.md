scarification:/ˌskærɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |刻痕；结疤；[医] 瘢痕形成；刻伤|The process of making marks or scars on skin or flesh, typically as part of a ritual or custom.  It can also refer to the resulting scar tissue.| 例句：The ancient tribe practiced scarification as a rite of passage.  这个古老的部落将刻痕作为一种成年礼。| 例句：The surgery resulted in significant scarification on his arm. 这场手术导致他的手臂上留下了明显的疤痕。| 近义词：marking, branding, cicatrization| 反义词：healing, smoothing
