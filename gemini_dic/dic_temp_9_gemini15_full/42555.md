counternatural:/ˌkaʊntərˈnætʃərəl/ | adj. |违反自然的；反自然的；不自然的 | Contrary to the ordinary course of nature; unnatural.  It suggests something that goes against the expected or usual way things happen in the natural world. This can refer to physical processes, behaviors, or even moral principles. |例句：His counternatural calmness in the face of danger surprised everyone. 他面对危险时反常的冷静让每个人都感到惊讶。


例句：The counternatural chill in the summer air was unsettling. 夏天空气中反常的寒冷令人不安。


近义词：unnatural, abnormal, unnatural, antinatural


反义词：natural, normal
