preacceptances:/ˌpriːəkˈsɛptənsɪz/| n. |预先接受；预先认可|Plural form of preacceptance.  Refers to instances where something is accepted or approved before the official process or deadline.  This often relates to applications, orders, or proposals that have been tentatively accepted pending further review or confirmation. |Example: The company received numerous preacceptances for their new product before the official launch. 公司在正式发布前收到了许多新产品的预先接受订单。|近义词：provisional acceptances, tentative approvals |反义词：rejections, denials
