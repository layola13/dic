regarnishing:/ˌriːˈɡɑːrnɪʃɪŋ/| v.|重新装饰；再装饰|The act of decorating or adorning something again;  re-embellishing.  It often implies a restoration or improvement upon a previous decoration.|例句：After the party, we spent the next day regarnishing the living room.  派对结束后，我们花了一天时间重新装饰客厅。

例句：She regarnished the cake with fresh berries before serving it.  她在上菜之前用新鲜浆果重新装饰了蛋糕。


近义词：redecorate, re-embellish, refurbish, adorn anew


反义词：strip, dismantle, despoil


词性：动词
