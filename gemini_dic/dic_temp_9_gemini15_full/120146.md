minced:/mɪnst/ | vt. |切碎；剁碎；(说话)吞吞吐吐地表达|To cut into very small pieces; to express something in a hesitant or indirect way.|例句：She minced the garlic before adding it to the sauce. 她把蒜切碎后再加到酱汁里。|例句：He minced his words, avoiding a direct answer. 他吞吞吐吐地说，避免直接回答。|近义词：chop, grind, hash, mumble|反义词：whole, uncut, direct


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He minces the onions every morning. = 他每天早上都切碎洋葱。
She minces her words when talking to her boss. = 她跟老板说话时吞吞吐吐的。


一般过去时:
I minced the meat for the meatballs. = 我把肉切碎做肉丸子。
He minced his words, afraid to offend her. = 他说话吞吞吐吐的，害怕得罪她。


现在完成时:
She has minced all the vegetables for the stew. = 她已经把所有蔬菜都切碎做炖菜了。

虚拟语气:
