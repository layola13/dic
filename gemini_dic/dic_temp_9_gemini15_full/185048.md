rethinker:/ˌriːˈθɪŋkər/| n. |重新思考者，反思者 |A person who habitually rethinks or reconsiders their beliefs, ideas, or actions.  Someone who challenges existing assumptions and seeks new perspectives. |Example:  She's a rethinker, constantly questioning established norms and seeking innovative solutions. 她是一个重新思考者，不断质疑既定规范并寻求创新解决方案。|近义词：reformer, innovator, revisionist |反义词：dogmatist, traditionalist


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The rethinker questions the status quo. = 重新思考者质疑现状。
She rethinks her approach regularly. = 她定期重新思考自己的方法。


一般过去时:
The rethinker reconsidered his initial plan. = 重新思考者重新考虑了他的初步计划。
He rethought his entire strategy after the failure. = 失败后，他重新思考了他的整个策略。


现在完成时:
The company has hired several rethinkers to innovate their products. = 公司已经聘请了几位重新思考者来创新他们的产品。
She has rethought her life goals many times. = 她已经多次重新思考过自己的人生目标。


过去完成时:
He had rethought his position before entering the debate. = 他在参加辩论之前已经重新考虑了他的立场。
They had reexamined their assumptions after the new data emerged. = 新数据出现后，他们重新审查了他们的假设。


将来时:
The team will rethink their marketing strategy next quarter. = 团队将在下个季度重新思考他们的营销策略。
She will rethink her decision if new evidence is presented. = 如果提出新的证据，她将重新考虑她的决定。


虚拟语气:
If I were a rethinker, I would challenge the current paradigm. = 如果我是一个重新思考者，我会挑战当前的范式。
I wish I had rethought my actions before. = 我希望我之前能够重新思考我的行为。


被动语态:
The existing system is being rethought by many. = 许多人正在重新思考现有的制度。
His ideas were rethought by the committee before implementation. = 他的想法在实施之前被委员会重新考虑了。


疑问句:
Is he a rethinker? = 他是一个重新思考者吗？
Are they rethinking their approach? = 他们正在重新思考他们的方法吗？


否定句:
He is not a rethinker; he sticks to his beliefs. = 他不是一个重新思考者；他坚持自己的信念。
They didn't rethink their decision, despite the setbacks. = 尽管遇到挫折，他们也没有重新考虑他们的决定。
