radicalising: /ˈrædɪkəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使激进化；使极端化 | To make someone adopt radical, often extreme, political or religious beliefs.  |  例句：The government is accused of radicalising young people through its propaganda. 政府被指控通过其宣传使年轻人激进化。 | 近义词：extremizing, inflaming, inciting | 反义词：moderating, calming, pacifying | 动词

He is constantly trying to radicalise his followers. = 他不断试图使他的追随者激进化。
