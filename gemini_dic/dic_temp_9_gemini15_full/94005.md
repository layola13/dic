impales:/ɪmˈpeɪlz/ | vt. | 刺穿；使…受折磨 | To pierce or stab with a sharp pointed object; to fix or fasten something onto a sharp point.  The word often implies a violent or forceful action. | 例句：The hunter impaled the fish on his spear.  猎人用长矛刺穿了鱼。

The warrior impaled his enemy's heart.  战士刺穿了他的敌人的心脏。


近义词：pierce, transfix, spear, skewer | 反义词：extract, remove

词源:

From Old French *empaler, from Late Latin *impālare, from Latin pālus "stake, pale".  The prefix "im-" indicates "in" or "into".  Therefore, the word literally means "to drive into a stake".
