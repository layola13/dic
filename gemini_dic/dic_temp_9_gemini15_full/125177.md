multicampus:/ˈmʌltiˌkæmpəs/| adj.|多校区的；多校的|Having multiple campuses or locations.  A multicampus university has several separate physical locations.|例句：The university system is multicampus, with campuses in three different cities.  这个大学系统是多校区的，在三个不同的城市都有校区。

例句：Many students prefer a multicampus university because of the diverse learning environments. 许多学生更喜欢多校区的大学，因为学习环境多样化。


近义词：multicentred, decentralized


反义词：single-campus, centralized

词性：形容词
