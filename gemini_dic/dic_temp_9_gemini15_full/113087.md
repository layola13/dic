lunatical:/ˈluːnətɪkl/| adj. |疯狂的；精神错乱的；古怪的 | exhibiting or characterized by extreme madness; wildly foolish or impractical.  |例句：His lunatical behavior alarmed his family.  他的疯狂行为吓坏了他的家人。
例句：The plan was lunatical from the start.  这个计划从一开始就非常荒谬。|近义词：insane, mad, crazy, deranged, demented, frantic |反义词：sane, rational, sensible, reasonable |词性:形容词
