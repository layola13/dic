disciplinarians:/ˌdɪsɪplɪˈneəriənz/| n. |纪律严明的人；强调纪律的人|A disciplinarian is a person who believes in or enforces strict discipline.  They often maintain order through firm rules and punishments.  They can be found in various roles, from parents and teachers to military officers and coaches.|例句：The headmaster was a strict disciplinarian, known for his unwavering adherence to rules. 校长是一位严厉的纪律严明者，以其对规章制度的坚定遵守而闻名。

The sergeant was a tough disciplinarian, ensuring that every soldier followed orders precisely.  中士是一位严厉的纪律严明者，确保每位士兵都精确地服从命令。|近义词：taskmaster, enforcer, authoritarian|反义词：permissive, lenient, indulgent|词性:noun
