importers:/ɪmˈpɔːrtərz/| n.|进口商，进口公司|Those who import goods or services into a country.|例句：Many importers rely on efficient logistics networks. 许多进口商依靠高效的物流网络。

例句：The importers are responsible for paying customs duties. 进口商负责支付关税。


近义词：importers of goods, buyers, dealers|反义词：exporters|词性:名词


USAGE->
