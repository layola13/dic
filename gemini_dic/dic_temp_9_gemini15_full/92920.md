identical:/aɪˈdɛntɪkəl/| adj.|完全相同的；同一的|Exactly alike; the same.|The twins are identical. 这对双胞胎长得一模一样。|近义词：same, alike, indistinguishable|反义词：different, dissimilar, unlike|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The two paintings are identical. =这两幅画一模一样。
These two products are identical in quality. = 这两件产品的质量相同。


一般过去时:
Their answers were identical. = 他们的答案完全相同。
The twins wore identical dresses. = 双胞胎姐妹穿了相同的衣服。


现在完成时:
We have found two identical solutions. = 我们找到了两个相同的解决方案。
They have produced several identical copies. = 他们制作了几份完全相同的副本。


过去完成时:
The reports had been identical in all aspects. = 这些报告在各方面都完全相同。
It turned out that their plans had been identical. = 事实证明他们的计划完全一样。


将来时:
The results will be identical, I am sure. = 我确信结果将完全相同。
The new model will be almost identical to the old one. = 新型号将与旧型号几乎完全相同。


虚拟语气:
If the conditions were identical, the results would be the same. = 如果条件相同，结果也会相同。
I wish my two cars were identical. = 我希望我的两辆车完全相同。


被动语态:
The twins were dressed in identical clothes. = 双胞胎穿着相同的衣服。
The two documents were found to be identical. = 发现这两份文件是相同的。


疑问句:
Are the two samples identical? = 这两个样本完全相同吗？
Is this design identical to the original one? = 这个设计与原始设计相同吗？


否定句:
The results were not identical. = 结果并不相同。
The two cars are not identical, though they look similar. = 这两辆车虽然看起来相似，但并不完全相同。


PHRASE->
identical twins: 同卵双胞胎
identical copies: 完全相同的副本
identical in every respect: 各方面都相同
virtually identical: 几乎完全相同
genetically identical: 基因相同的


词源:
identical 源自于拉丁语词语 "identidem,"  意思是 "repeatedly," "again and again."  后来演变为 "idem,"  意思是 "the same."  通过法语 "identique"  最终进入英语。  因此，它的核心含义一直与“相同”相关。
