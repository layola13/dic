entire:/ɛnˈtaɪər/| adj.|全部的；完整的；整个的|The entire project was a success.  整个项目都成功了。|近义词：complete, whole, full, total |反义词：partial, incomplete, fractional


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The entire team works hard. = 整个团队都很努力。
The entire process takes a week. = 整个过程需要一周时间。

一般过去时:
The entire city was destroyed. = 整个城市都被摧毁了。
He ate the entire cake. = 他吃掉了整个蛋糕。

现在完成时:
She has read the entire book. = 她已经读完了整本书。
We have completed the entire assignment. = 我们已经完成了所有的作业。

过去完成时:
They had finished the entire report before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前完成了整份报告。
I had consumed the entire bottle of water. = 我已经喝完了整瓶水。

将来时:
He will paint the entire house. = 他将会把整栋房子都粉刷一遍。
We will explore the entire island. = 我们将探索整个岛屿。

虚拟语气:
If I had the entire day, I would finish it. = 如果我有整天的时间，我就会完成它。
I wish I had the entire amount of money. = 我真希望我有全部的钱。

被动语态:
The entire building was renovated. = 整栋建筑都被翻新了。
The entire plan was carefully reviewed. = 整个计划都经过仔细审查。

疑问句:
Is the entire team present? = 全队都到齐了吗？
Did you read the entire article? = 你读完了整篇文章吗？

否定句:
The entire story isn't true. = 故事并非全部属实。
He didn't finish the entire task. = 他没有完成全部任务。


PHRASE->

词源:
The word "entire" comes from the Old French word "entier," meaning "whole, complete."  This, in turn, derives from the Latin word "integer," also meaning "whole, untouched, complete."  The suffix "-er" in Old French and related words is a comparative suffix.
