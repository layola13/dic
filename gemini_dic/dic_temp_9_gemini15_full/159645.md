polonaises:/ˌpɒləˈneɪz/| n. |波兰舞曲；波兰舞曲风格的乐曲|A polonaise is a Polish dance in 3/4 time, typically stately and ceremonious.  It's also a musical composition in this style.|The orchestra played a lively polonaise.|近义词：mazurka (another Polish dance)|反义词：None specifically, but contrasting styles would be quicker, less formal dances.
