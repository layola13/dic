concent:/kənˈsɛnt/| n.|同意，一致，和谐；浓度|agreement in opinion; harmony; the degree of concentration of a substance|The team reached a concent on the project plan.  团队就项目计划达成了一致。|近义词：accord, agreement, harmony|反义词：discord, disagreement, disharmony


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team consents to the new plan. = 团队同意新的计划。
Everyone consents to the decision. = 每个人都同意这个决定。

一般过去时:
They consented to our request. = 他们同意了我们的请求。
She consented to marry him. = 她同意嫁给他。

现在完成时:
We have consented to their terms. = 我们已经同意了他们的条件。
He has consented to the interview. = 他已经同意接受采访。

过去完成时:
They had consented to the deal before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就已经同意了这项交易。
She had consented to the operation earlier that week. = 她在那周早些时候就已经同意了手术。

将来时:
They will consent to the changes next week. = 他们下周将同意这些改变。
I believe she will consent to help us. = 我相信她会同意帮助我们。

虚拟语气:
If they consented, we could proceed. = 如果他们同意，我们就可以继续进行。
I wish they consented to our proposal. = 我希望他们同意我们的提议。

被动语态:
The proposal was consented to by the board. = 该提案得到了董事会的同意。
His request was consented to by the manager. = 他的请求得到了经理的同意。

疑问句:
Will they consent to the merger? = 他们会同意合并吗？
Does she consent to the terms of the contract? = 她同意合同条款吗？

否定句:
They did not consent to the plan. = 他们不同意这个计划。
She did not consent to participate in the study. = 她不同意参与这项研究。
