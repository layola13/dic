musicologists:/ˌmjuːzɪˈkɒlədʒɪsts/| n. |音乐学家|Scholars who study music, its history, theory, and cultural context.  They analyze musical works, composers, and the social impact of music.|  Many musicologists believe that Bach's music transcends cultural boundaries. 许多音乐学家认为巴赫的音乐超越了文化界限。|music historians, music scholars|  (无明确反义词)|名词
