reshipper:/rɪˈʃɪpər/| n. |转运商，再发货人 | A reshipper is a company or individual that receives goods from a supplier and then forwards them to a customer, often with additional services like repackaging or consolidation.  They act as an intermediary in the shipping process. | Example:  The online retailer used a reshipper to consolidate orders from multiple suppliers before sending them to the customer.  网上零售商使用转运商将来自多个供应商的订单合并后再发给客户。 | 近义词：forwarder, consolidator | 反义词：N/A
