reallocate:/ˌriːˈæləkeɪt/| vt.|重新分配，重新配置|To allocate (resources, etc.) again or differently.|The company had to reallocate its budget to cover unexpected expenses.  公司不得不重新分配预算以支付意外开支。|The government reallocated funds to improve infrastructure. 政府重新分配资金以改善基础设施。|allocate, redistribute, reassign|misallocate, underallocate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reallocate resources annually. = 他们每年重新分配资源。
The company reallocates its marketing budget every quarter. = 公司每个季度都会重新分配其营销预算。

一般过去时:
We reallocated the funds to the most needed project. = 我们将资金重新分配给了最需要的项目。
The manager reallocated the tasks among the team members. = 经理在团队成员之间重新分配了任务。

现在完成时:
They have reallocated the staff to different departments. = 他们已将员工重新分配到不同的部门。
The company has reallocated its investment portfolio this year. = 公司今年已重新调整其投资组合。


过去完成时:
The project had already been reallocated before the meeting. = 在会议之前，项目已经被重新分配了。
They had reallocated their resources to focus on the new product. = 他们已经重新分配了资源，以专注于新产品。

将来时:
We will reallocate the budget next month. = 我们下个月将重新分配预算。
The school will reallocate classrooms to accommodate the increased enrollment. = 学校将重新分配教室以适应入学人数的增加。

虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would reallocate funds to research. = 如果我们有更多资金，我们将把资金重新分配给研究。
It would be wise to reallocate resources if the project fails. = 如果项目失败，重新分配资源将是明智之举。

被动语态:
The budget was reallocated to emergency services. = 预算被重新分配给了紧急服务部门。
Resources are being reallocated to address the crisis. = 资源正在被重新分配以应对危机。

疑问句:
Will the company reallocate its profits to shareholders? = 公司会将其利润重新分配给股东吗？
How will the funds be reallocated? = 资金将如何重新分配？

否定句:
They did not reallocate the resources effectively. = 他们没有有效地重新分配资源。
The company will not reallocate the budget until next year. = 公司直到明年才会重新分配预算。
