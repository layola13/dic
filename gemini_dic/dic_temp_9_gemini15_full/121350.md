misgovernments:/ˌmɪsˈɡʌvənmənts/| n.| 不良治理；施政失误 | Acts of misgovernment are often characterized by corruption, inefficiency, and a disregard for the needs of the people. 不良治理的行为往往以腐败、低效和漠视人民需求为特征。|近义词：maladministration, mismanagement, bad governance |反义词：good governance, just rule
