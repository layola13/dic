siks:/sɪks/ | num. | 六 | The number six is halfway between five and seven.  六这个数字介于五和七之间。 | 近义词：sixer (informal) | 反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Six plus four equals ten. = 六加四等于十。
There are six apples in the basket. = 篮子里有六个苹果。


一般过去时:
There were six cars in the parking lot. = 停车场里有六辆车。
She had six kittens. = 她有六只小猫。


现在完成时:
I have seen that movie six times. = 我看过那部电影六次了。
We have lived here for six years. = 我们住在这里六年了。


过去完成时:
They had already finished six tasks before lunch. = 他们午餐前已经完成了六项任务。
She had baked six cakes by the time the guests arrived. = 客人到达时，她已经烤了六个蛋糕。


将来时:
I will buy six roses for my wife. = 我将为我的妻子买六朵玫瑰花。
We will be in the city for six days. = 我们将在城里待六天。


虚拟语气:
If I had six million dollars, I would travel the world. = 如果我有六百万美元，我会环游世界。
I wish I had six more hours to finish this project. = 我希望我有额外六个小时来完成这个项目。


被动语态:
Six people were invited to the party. = 六个人被邀请参加聚会。
Six houses were damaged by the storm. = 六所房子被暴风雨损坏。


疑问句:
How many sides does a hexagon have? (Answer: Six) = 六边形有多少边？（答案：六）
Are there six students in the class? = 班上有六个学生吗？


否定句:
There are not six people in the room; there are only five. = 房间里不是六个人，只有五个人。
I didn't have six cookies; I only had five. = 我没有六块饼干，只有五块。


PHRASE->

six pack = 六块腹肌
