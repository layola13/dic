molar-tooth:/ˈmoʊlər tuːθ/| n. |磨牙|A molar tooth is one of the large teeth at the back of the mouth, used for grinding food.  They typically have a broad, flat surface.|例句：He chipped a molar tooth while eating a hard candy. 他吃硬糖时崩掉了一颗磨牙。|例句：The dentist examined his molars for cavities. 牙医检查了他的磨牙是否有龋齿。|近义词：cheek tooth, grinder|反义词：incisor, canine
