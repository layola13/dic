gels:/dʒɛlz/| n.|凝胶；胶状物|A gel is a colloid in a solid or semisolid state consisting of a network of interconnected particles that traps a liquid.  Gels are often translucent and have a jelly-like consistency.|例句：The hair gel made his hair stiff and shiny.  发胶使他的头发又硬又亮。|例句：The agar-agar formed a firm gel after cooling.  琼脂冷却后形成了坚固的凝胶。|近义词：jelly, jell, colloid|反义词：liquid, solution|名词
