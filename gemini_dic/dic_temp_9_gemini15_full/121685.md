mispolicy:/ˌmɪsˈpɑːləsi/| n.|错误的政策，失策|A mispolicy is a mistaken or unwise policy or course of action. It often leads to negative consequences.|The government's mispolicy on economic reform led to widespread unemployment.|近义词：bad policy, wrong decision, blunder, mistake|反义词：sound policy, good policy, wise decision
