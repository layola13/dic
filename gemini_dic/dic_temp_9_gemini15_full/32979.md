choreography:/kɔːriˈɒɡrəfi/| /kɔːriˈɑːɡrəfi/| n.|舞蹈编排；舞蹈设计；编舞|The art of composing and arranging dance movements and patterns; the design or plan of a dance.  It encompasses the steps, patterns, and movements of dancers, as well as the overall structure and style of a dance performance.|例句：The choreography for the ballet was breathtaking.  这出芭蕾舞的编舞令人叹为观止。|例句：The choreographer created a stunning visual spectacle.  编舞师创造了一个令人惊艳的视觉盛宴。|近义词：staging, arrangement, composition|反义词：improvisation, randomness|名词
