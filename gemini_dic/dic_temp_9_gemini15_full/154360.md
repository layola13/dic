perlite:/ˈpɜːrlaɪt/|/ˈpɜːrlaɪt/| n. |珍珠岩|A volcanic glass that has been expanded by heating.  It is lightweight, porous, and has good insulating properties, making it useful in various applications. |例句：The gardener used perlite to improve the drainage of the potting mix. 园丁用珍珠岩来改善盆栽土的排水性。|例句：The lightweight perlite insulation helped reduce energy costs in the building. 轻质珍珠岩保温材料有助于降低建筑物的能源成本。|近义词：pumice, vermiculite|反义词：dense material
