resuscitate:/rɪˈsʌsɪteɪt/| vt.| 使复活；使复苏；使恢复生机 |To revive someone or something from apparent death or unconsciousness; to restore to life, consciousness, or vigor. |例句：The paramedics worked frantically to resuscitate the accident victim.  医护人员拼命抢救车祸受害者。

例句：The doctor managed to resuscitate the patient after he stopped breathing. 医生设法在病人停止呼吸后使其复苏。


近义词：revive, revitalize, reanimate |反义词：kill, extinguish, terminate |词性：动词

词源:
