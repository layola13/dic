repleading: /ˌriːˈpliːdɪŋ/ | v. | 再陈述，再辩论 |  The act of filing a new or amended pleading in a lawsuit after a previous pleading has been deemed insufficient. It involves re-submitting a claim or defense with alterations or additions.  |  The judge rejected the initial complaint, forcing the plaintiff to file a repleading. = 法官驳回了最初的起诉，迫使原告重新提交诉状。 The defendant's repleading clarified their position on the key issue. = 被告的再次陈述阐明了他们在关键问题上的立场。 | 近义词：restatement, amendment, revision | 反义词：withdrawal, dismissal
