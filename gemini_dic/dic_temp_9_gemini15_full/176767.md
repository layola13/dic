readeption:/riˈdɛmpʃən/| n. |救赎；赎回；偿还；赎罪|The act of redeeming or being redeemed; deliverance from sin, error, or evil.  It often refers to spiritual redemption, but can also apply to financial or other forms of release.|例句：He found redemption in helping others. 他在帮助他人中找到了救赎。|例句：The company offered redemption for the faulty product. 公司为有缺陷的产品提供了退款。|近义词：salvation, deliverance, release, atonement |反义词：damnation, condemnation, ruin
