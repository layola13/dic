selenate:/ˈsɛləneɪt/| n.|硒酸盐|A salt or ester of selenic acid. Selenates are compounds containing the selenate anion (SeO₄²⁻). They are analogous to sulfates, with selenium replacing sulfur.|Example: Sodium selenate is used as a nutritional supplement for selenium deficiency.|近义词:selenic acid salt|反义词: N/A
