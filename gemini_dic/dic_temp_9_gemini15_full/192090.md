scourge:/skɜːrdʒ/| n., v.|鞭打；灾祸；祸害；严厉惩罚|A scourge is a person or thing that causes great suffering or misfortune.  As a verb, it means to whip or punish severely.|例句：The new tax was seen as a scourge on the poor.  新税被视为对穷人的灾难。 The dictator scourged his enemies. 独裁者鞭打他的敌人。|近义词：plague, affliction, torment, whip|反义词：blessing, boon, benefit|名词，动词
