pyroxylin:/paɪˈrɒksɪlɪn/| n.|火棉,硝化纤维|A highly flammable substance made by treating cellulose with nitric acid; used in making explosives and lacquers.|例句：Pyroxylin is a highly combustible material and must be handled with care. 火棉是一种高度易燃的材料，必须小心处理。|例句：Guncotton, a type of pyroxylin, was once widely used as a propellant. 枪棉，一种火棉，曾经被广泛用作推进剂。|近义词：guncotton, nitrocellulose|反义词：无

词源:
The word "pyroxylin" comes from the Greek words "pyr" (fire) and "xylon" (wood), referring to its flammable nature and origin from cellulose (a component of wood).  It reflects the material's origin from wood and its highly flammable properties.
