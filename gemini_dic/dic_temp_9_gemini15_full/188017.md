roughdraw:/ˈrʌfdrɔː/| v.|粗略地画，草绘|To make a quick, imprecise drawing or sketch, often as a preliminary step before a more refined version.  It implies a lack of detail and precision.|例句：I roughdrew a map to show them the way. 我草绘了一张地图给他们指路。

例句：The architect roughdrew the building's plans before starting the detailed blueprints. 建筑师在开始绘制详细蓝图之前先粗略地勾画了建筑物的平面图。|近义词：sketch, outline, doodle, draft|反义词：finely draw, meticulously draw, delineate
