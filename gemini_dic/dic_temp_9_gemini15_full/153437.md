pennate:/ˈpɛneɪt/ | adj. | 羽状的；羽状排列的 | Having parts arranged like a feather;  relating to or resembling a feather.  The term is often used to describe the arrangement of structures in biology, particularly in reference to muscles or veins. | 例句：The pennate muscles in the wings of birds allow for powerful flight. 鸟类翅膀上的羽状肌使其能够强劲飞行。

The leaves of this plant exhibit a beautiful pennate venation.  这种植物的叶子呈现美丽的羽状脉序。| 近义词：pinnate, plumose | 反义词：parallel, unipinnate (in the context of leaf venation)
