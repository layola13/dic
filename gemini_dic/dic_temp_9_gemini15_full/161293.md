postorbital:/ˌpoʊstoʊrˈbaɪtl/| adj.|眶后的，眼眶后的| Situated behind the eye socket or orbit.  The term refers to a bone, process, or structure located posterior to the eye socket in the skull.|例句：The postorbital bar is a significant feature in the skull of many reptiles.  许多爬行动物的颅骨都有重要的眶后条。

The postorbital process of the frontal bone provides attachment for muscles.额骨的眶后突为肌肉提供附着点。|近义词：posterior orbital, behind the orbit|反义词：preorbital
