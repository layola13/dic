injectors:/ɪnˈdʒektərz/| n. |喷射器，注射器|A device that injects a fluid, such as fuel or medication, under pressure.|例句：The car's fuel injectors were clogged, causing poor performance.  汽车的燃油喷射器堵塞了，导致性能下降。

例句：The nurse used a syringe to inject the medicine. 护士用注射器注射药物。|近义词：injectors, nozzles, syringes |反义词:extractors, absorbers.
