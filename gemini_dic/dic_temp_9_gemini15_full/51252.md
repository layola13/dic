destructuring:/diːˈstrʌktʃərɪŋ/ | n. |解构；结构分解|The process of breaking down a complex structure into its constituent parts.  This term is often used in programming to refer to extracting values from objects or arrays into distinct variables. | Example: Destructuring the JSON response simplified data access.  解构JSON响应简化了数据访问。 |近义词：decomposition, disassembly, analysis |反义词：construction, synthesis, assembly |名词
