polyembryonic: /ˌpɒliɛmˈbraɪɒnɪk/ /ˌpɑːliɛmˈbraɪɑːnɪk/ | adj. | 多胚的；多胚发生的 |  Producing or relating to more than one embryo from a single fertilization.  This phenomenon is common in some plants and certain insects. |

例句：

Some citrus fruits exhibit polyembryonic development, resulting in multiple seedlings from a single seed.  一些柑橘类水果表现出多胚发育，导致单个种子产生多个幼苗。

The polyembryonic nature of certain parasitic wasps allows for increased offspring survival.  某些寄生黄蜂的多胚特性能够提高后代的存活率。


近义词：multiple-embryonic


反义词：monoembryonic


词性：形容词
