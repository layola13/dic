singing:/ˈsɪŋɪŋ/ | v. n. | 唱歌，演唱 | The act or process of producing musical sounds with the voice;  a song or piece of music.  | 例句：She loves singing in the shower. 她喜欢在淋浴时唱歌。


The birds were singing sweetly in the trees. 鸟儿们在树上甜美地歌唱。 | 近义词：chanting, humming, warbling | 反义词：whispering, muttering


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sings every morning. 他每天早上唱歌。
They sing in a choir. 他们在合唱团唱歌。

一般过去时:
She sang a beautiful song last night. 她昨晚唱了一首美丽的歌。
We sang karaoke at the party. 我们在派对上唱卡拉OK。

现在完成时:
He has sung in many concerts. 他已在许多音乐会上演唱。
She has sung this song countless times. 她已经唱过这首歌无数次了。

过去完成时:
They had sung before the audience arrived. 观众到达之前他们已经唱过了。
I had sung that song many times before. 我之前已经唱过很多次那首歌了。

将来时:
She will sing at the wedding. 她将在婚礼上唱歌。
They will sing a duet tomorrow. 他们明天将演唱二重唱。

虚拟语气:
If I could sing, I would join a choir. 如果我会唱歌，我会加入合唱团。
I wish I could sing like a bird. 我希望我能像鸟儿一样唱歌。

被动语态:
The song was sung by a famous singer. 这首歌是由一位著名的歌手演唱的。
The national anthem is sung at every game. 国歌在每场比赛中都会演唱。

疑问句:
Does he sing well? 他唱歌好吗？
Can you sing this song? 你能唱这首歌吗？

否定句:
He doesn't sing very often. 他不经常唱歌。
She didn't sing at the concert. 她没有在音乐会上唱歌。
