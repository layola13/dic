genes:/dʒiːnz/| n. |基因|Units of heredity that are transferred from a parent to offspring and are held to determine some characteristic of the offspring.|  Our genes determine many of our physical characteristics. 我们的基因决定了我们的许多生理特征。|Hereditary factors, traits|No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with environment or nurture.|名词
