preannex:/ˌpriːˈæneks/| v.|预先吞并；提前兼并|To annex beforehand; to incorporate before the official annexation.|The government planned to preannex the territory before the official treaty was signed. 政府计划在正式条约签署之前预先吞并该领土。|近义词：annex, incorporate, seize|反义词：cede, relinquish

The company preannexes smaller firms to gain market share. = 公司预先兼并小型企业以获得市场份额。
