overfed:/ˌoʊvərˈfed/ | adj. |喂食过量的；吃得太饱的 |  Given more food than is needed or wanted; excessively fed.  | Example: The overfed dog lay lazily in the sun.  这只吃得太饱的狗懒洋洋地躺在阳光下。  |近义词:surfeited, gorged, stuffed | 反义词:underfed, starved

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog is overfed; he needs less food.  这只狗喂食过量了；它需要少吃点。
She is always overfed at family gatherings.  她在家庭聚会上总是吃得太多。


一般过去时:
The piglets were overfed last week.  小猪上周喂食过量了。
He felt overfed after the huge Thanksgiving dinner.  感恩节大餐后他感觉吃得太饱了。


现在完成时:
The farmer has overfed his cattle this season.  这个农民这个季度给他的牛喂食过量了。
I have overfed myself; I feel quite unwell. 我吃太多了，感觉不太舒服。


过去完成时:
The guests had overfed themselves before the dessert was served.  在甜点上桌之前，客人们已经吃饱了。
She had overfed the birds, and they were all too plump. 她喂鸟喂多了，鸟都太胖了。


将来时:
We will overfeed the plants if we water them too much. 如果我们浇水过多，我们会把植物喂过头。
If we don't watch the portions, we will overfeed the children. 如果我们不注意分量，我们会把孩子们喂过头。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't overfed the hamster, it wouldn't have gotten sick. 如果我没有给仓鼠喂太多，它就不会生病了。
I wish I hadn't overfed myself last night. 我真希望我昨晚没有吃太多。


被动语态:
The animals were overfed by the careless zookeeper. 动物们被粗心的动物管理员喂食过量了。
The plants have been overfed with fertilizer. 植物被施肥施多了。


疑问句:
Are the puppies overfed? 小狗们喂食过量了吗？
Was the horse overfed before the race?  赛马在比赛前喂食过量了吗？


否定句:
The birds were not overfed; they needed more food. 鸟儿没有喂食过量；它们需要更多食物。
The kittens were not overfed; they were still hungry. 小猫没有喂食过量；它们仍然饿着。
