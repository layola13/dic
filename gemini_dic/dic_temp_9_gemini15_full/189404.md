sahibs:/ˈsɑːb/| n.|先生们，老爷们（殖民地时期对印度人的称呼）|A term used, primarily during the British Raj in India, to refer to British men, especially those of high social standing.  It is now often considered a derogatory term.|例句：The sahibs enjoyed their afternoon tea on the veranda.  先生们在阳台享用他们的下午茶。|近义词：gentlemen, masters, officials|反义词：natives, servants
