device:/dɪˈvaɪs/| n.|装置，设备|A thing made or adapted for a particular purpose, especially a piece of mechanical or electronic equipment.|例句：This device helps you to open the bottle easily. 这个装置可以帮助你轻松打开瓶子。|例句：He invented a new device for measuring blood pressure. 他发明了一种新的血压测量装置。|近义词：appliance, gadget, instrument, tool|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This device measures temperature accurately. = 这个装置精确地测量温度。
The device transmits data wirelessly. = 该设备无线传输数据。
