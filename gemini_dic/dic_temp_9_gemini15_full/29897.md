cauterize:/ˈkɔːtəraɪz/ | vt. | 烧灼；止血 | To burn the surface of (a wound) with a hot instrument or a chemical agent to stop bleeding or prevent infection.  | 例句：The doctor cauterized the wound to stop the bleeding. 医生烧灼伤口以止血。  | 例句：The surgeon used a laser to cauterize the bleeding vessel. 外科医生用激光烧灼出血的血管。 | 近义词：burn, sear, sear | 反义词：heal, cure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The surgeon cauterizes the wound. = 外科医生烧灼伤口。
He cauterizes his cuts regularly. = 他定期烧灼他的伤口。


一般过去时:
The doctor cauterized the deep gash. = 医生烧灼了深深的伤口。

The medic has cauterized all the wounds. = 医疗人员已经烧灼了所有伤口。

词源:
From Late Latin *cauterizāre, from Latin cautērium ("hot iron"), from cautūs ("burned," past participle of cautere ("to burn")).
