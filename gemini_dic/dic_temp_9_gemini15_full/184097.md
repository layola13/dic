resilvering: /ˌriːˈsɪlvərɪŋ/ | v. | 再镀银；重新镀银 | To coat something again with silver.  The process involves applying a new layer of silver to a previously silvered surface, often to restore its reflectivity or conductivity. | Example: The antique mirror needed resilvering to restore its clarity.  = 古董镜子需要重新镀银以恢复其清晰度。The old teapot was resilvered to look brand new. = 旧茶壶重新镀银后看起来焕然一新。| 近义词：replating, recoating | 反义词：desilvering
