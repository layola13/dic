extrudes:/ɪkˈstruːdz/ | vt. |挤出，突出；发射 | To force, press, or push out (something) through a small opening.  |例句：The volcano extruded lava.  火山喷出了熔岩。

例句：The machine extrudes plastic into various shapes. 这台机器将塑料挤压成各种形状。


近义词：protrude, project, eject, force out | 反义词：retract, withdraw


词源:  From Latin *extrudere, from ex- "out" + trudere "to push, thrust".
