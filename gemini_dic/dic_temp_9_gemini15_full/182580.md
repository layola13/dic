reoccupies:/ˌriːˈɒkjʊpaɪz/| v. |重新占用，重新占据 |To occupy again; to take possession of something again.  |The army reoccupied the city after a brief withdrawal.  军队在短暂撤退后重新占领了这座城市。 The family reoccupied their home after the renovations were complete.  装修完成后，这家人重新住进了他们的家。 |occupy, reclaim, retake |abandon, vacate, leave
