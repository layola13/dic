debit:/ˈdɛbɪt/| n. v.|借方；借项；记入借方；在账户上记入借方|

英文解析:A debit is an accounting entry that increases the balance of an asset or expense account, and decreases the balance of a liability or equity account.  It represents a charge or expense. As a verb, it means to record a debit in an account.

例句:
The company debited my account for the purchase. = 公司因我的购买而记入了我的账户借方。

My bank statement shows a debit of $100. = 我的银行对账单显示100美元的借方。


近义词:charge, expense, withdrawal

反义词:credit


词性:Noun, Verb
