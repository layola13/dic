reaccredit:/ˌriːˈækrɛdɪt/| vt.|重新认证，重新认可|To accredit again; to grant accreditation to (someone or something) once more.|例句：The university was reaccredited after meeting the new standards. 大学在满足新的标准后重新获得了认证。  例句：The organization was reaccredited for its commitment to ethical practices. 该组织因其对道德规范的承诺而再次获得认证。|近义词：recertify, revalidate, reaffirm|反义词：deaccredited, disapprove
