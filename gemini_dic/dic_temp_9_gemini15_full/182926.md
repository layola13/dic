repetitor:/rɛˈpɪtɪtər/| n. |复读机；辅导教师；(尤指)预科教师；重复者|A repetitor is a person who repeats something, especially a teacher who helps students repeat lessons or a device that repeats a signal.|例句：He worked as a repetitor at a prestigious university. 他在一所名牌大学担任辅导教师。  例句：The repetitor helped the student understand the complex concepts. 复读机帮助学生理解复杂的概念。|近义词：tutor, coach, repeater|反义词：instructor(in the sense of original teacher), originator
