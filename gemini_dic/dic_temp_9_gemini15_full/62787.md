engross:/ɪnˈɡroʊs/| vt.| 全神贯注于；使全神贯注；正式书写；|To occupy or engage (someone's attention) completely.|He was so engrossed in his book that he didn't hear the phone ring. 他全神贯注于书中，没有听到电话铃声。|absorb, preoccupy, captivate|distract, bore|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He engrosses himself in his work. = 他专心致志地工作。
She engrosses her time in painting. = 她把时间都花在绘画上。


一般过去时:
They engrossed themselves in the movie. = 他们全神贯注地看电影。
He engrossed the document carefully. = 他仔细地书写了文件。


现在完成时:
I have engrossed myself in learning a new language. = 我一直专心致志地学习一门外语。
She has engrossed many important legal documents. = 她已经书写了许多重要的法律文件。


过去完成时:

The lawyer had engrossed the contract before the meeting. = 律师在会议之前已经书写好了合同。
