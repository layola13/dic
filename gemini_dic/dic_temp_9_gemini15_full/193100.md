sectorial:/sekˈtɔːriəl/| adj.|部门的；行业的；扇形的；|Relating to or affecting a sector or sectors of the economy or society.  It often implies a division or compartmentalization within a larger whole. |The government implemented sectorial policies to boost the economy.|近义词：sectional, divisional, departmental|反义词：comprehensive, holistic, overall
