digitalizing: /ˈdɪdʒɪtəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |数字化；使…数字化 | The process of converting information into a digital format, typically from an analog format.  This involves representing information using discrete numerical values.  It is widely applied to various fields, including images, audio, video, and documents. |例句：The library is digitalizing its entire collection. 图书馆正在将其全部藏书数字化。

The company is digitalizing its workflow to improve efficiency. 公司正在将其工作流程数字化以提高效率。


近义词：converting to digital, digitizing


反义词：analogizing


词性：动词

词源:
The word "digitalizing" is derived from "digital," which comes from the Latin word "digitus," meaning "finger."  The use of fingers for counting is the origin of the connection to discrete numerical values.  The suffix "-izing" indicates the process of making something digital.
