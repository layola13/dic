fenestrate: /ˈfɛnɪstreɪt/ | vt. | 开窗于；在…上开孔 | To pierce with holes or openings, like windows; to furnish with windows.  | The architect decided to fenestrate the entire south wall to maximize natural light. 建筑师决定在整个南墙上开窗以最大限度地利用自然光。 | perforate, pierce, punch |  solid, unbroken | 动词

He fenestrates the wall. 他在墙上开窗。
The artist fenestrates the canvas. 艺术家在画布上打孔。
