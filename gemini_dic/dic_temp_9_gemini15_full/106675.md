kvetch:/kvetʃ/| v.|抱怨，牢骚，诉苦 |To complain or grumble, often about minor things.  |例句：He spent the whole evening kvetching about his job. 他整个晚上都在抱怨他的工作。

例句：She constantly kvetches about the weather. 她不断抱怨天气。


近义词：complain, grumble, whine, grouse, moan |反义词：praise, compliment, celebrate


词源：来自意第绪语（Yiddish）单词“kvetchn”，意思是“抱怨”。  意第绪语本身源于中古高地德语。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kvetchs about the traffic every day. = 他每天都抱怨交通。
They kvetch about their boss's decisions. = 他们抱怨老板的决定。


一般过去时:
She kvetched about the long lines at the store. = 她抱怨商店的长队。
He kvetched to his friend about his problems. = 他向朋友抱怨他的问题。


现在完成时:
I have kvetched enough for one day. = 我今天抱怨够了。
They have kvetched repeatedly about the poor service. = 他们反复抱怨糟糕的服务。


过去完成时:
She had kvetched all morning before finally calling the repairman. = 在最终打电话给修理工之前，她抱怨了整个上午。
He had kvetched so much that nobody wanted to listen anymore. = 他抱怨得太多了，以至于没有人想再听了。


将来时:
I will kvetch if the flight is delayed again. = 如果航班再次延误，我会抱怨。
They will kvetch to the manager about the poor quality of the food. = 他们会向经理抱怨食物质量差。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't kvetch so much. = 如果我是你，我不会抱怨那么多。
I wish I hadn't kvetched so loudly yesterday. = 我真希望我昨天不要抱怨那么大声。


被动语态:
His complaints were often kvetched about by his colleagues.  = 他的抱怨经常被他的同事们抱怨。 (Note: Passive voice is less common with this verb)
The problem was constantly being kvetched about. = 这个问题不断地被人抱怨。


疑问句:
Does he often kvetch about his work? = 他经常抱怨他的工作吗？
Why are they kvetching so much today? = 他们今天为什么抱怨这么多？


否定句:
He didn't kvetch about the meal at all. = 他根本没有抱怨这顿饭。
She doesn't usually kvetch, but today she's really upset. = 她通常不会抱怨，但今天她真的很生气。


PHRASE->
kvetch about something = 抱怨某事
kvetch to someone = 向某人抱怨
a constant kvetching = 不断的抱怨
kvetching incessantly = 不停地抱怨
to kvetch and moan = 抱怨和呻吟
