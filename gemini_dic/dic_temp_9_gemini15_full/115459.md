manuductor:/ˌmænjuːˈdʌktər/| n. |引导者，向导，指挥者 |A manuductor is a person or thing that guides or leads.  It often refers to someone who leads others, particularly in a formal or official capacity.  The word suggests a more authoritative or skilled guidance than a simple guide. |Example: The experienced hiker acted as a manuductor for the group through the treacherous mountain pass.  经验丰富的徒步旅行者充当了小组穿越险峻山路的向导。|近义词：guide, leader, conductor, escort |反义词：follower, subordinate |名词
