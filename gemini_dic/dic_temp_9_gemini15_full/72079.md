floatable: /ˈfloʊtəbəl/ | adj. | 可漂浮的，能浮起的 | Able to float; capable of being floated.  | 例句：The wooden raft was floatable on the calm lake. 这木筏在平静的湖面上可以漂浮。

The lightweight material is floatable, making it ideal for life jackets.  这种轻型材料可以漂浮，非常适合制作救生衣。 | 近义词：buoyant, afloat | 反义词：submersible, sinking
