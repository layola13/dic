extraction: /ɪkˈstrækʃən/ | n. | 提取；摘录；开采；拔牙 | The process of removing something from something else.  This can refer to physical objects, information, or data. |例句：The extraction of oil from the ground is a complex process.  从地下开采石油是一个复杂的过程。

例句：The dentist performed a difficult extraction. 牙医进行了一次艰难的拔牙手术。


近义词：removal, extraction, derivation,  extirpation

反义词：insertion, implantation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company extracts data from its customer database. = 这家公司从客户数据库中提取数据。
They extract minerals from the earth. = 他们从地球上开采矿物。


一般过去时:
Scientists extracted DNA from the ancient bones. = 科学家从古老的骨骼中提取了DNA。
The surgeon extracted the bullet from his leg. = 外科医生从他的腿上取出了子弹。

过去完成时:
