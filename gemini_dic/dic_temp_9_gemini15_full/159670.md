polyacrylate: /ˌpɒliˈækrileɪt/ | /ˌpɑːliˈækrɪleɪt/ | n. | 聚丙烯酸酯 | A polymer derived from acrylic acid or its derivatives.  Polyacrylates are used in a wide variety of applications due to their versatility and properties, including adhesives, coatings, and superabsorbents. | Example:  Many disposable diapers contain superabsorbent polyacrylate polymers. 许多一次性尿布都含有超吸水性聚丙烯酸酯聚合物。
