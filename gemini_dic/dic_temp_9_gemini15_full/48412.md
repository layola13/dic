dedication: /ˌdɛdɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 奉献；献身；题词 | The act of dedicating something to a person, cause, or purpose;  the quality of being dedicated or committed to something.  It implies a significant commitment of time, effort, and resources.  | 例句：Her dedication to her work is admirable.  她的敬业精神令人钦佩。

例句：His dedication to his family is unwavering. 他对家人的奉献是坚定不移的。


近义词：commitment, devotion, consecration, dedication, allegiance


反义词：negligence, apathy, indifference, carelessness


词性：名词
