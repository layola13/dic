derecho: /dəˈreɪtʃoʊ/ | n. | 直线风暴；强风带 | A derecho is a widespread, long-lived wind storm associated with a band of rapidly moving showers or thunderstorms.  These storms are characterized by damaging winds that extend for hundreds of miles. | Example: The derecho caused widespread power outages and significant property damage.  这场直线风暴导致大范围停电和重大财产损失。| 近义词：severe thunderstorm, squall line | 反义词：calm, breeze
