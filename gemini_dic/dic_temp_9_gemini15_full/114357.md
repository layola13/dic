majorat: /ˌmeɪdʒəˈræt/ | n. | 长子继承权；世袭财产 | The right of the eldest son to inherit the family estate.  This legal term refers to the system where the eldest son inherits the entire estate, excluding other siblings.  | Example: The majorat ensured that the family's vast landholdings remained intact for generations.  长子继承权确保了家族的大片土地世代完整地保留下来。 | 近义词：primogeniture | 反义词：equal inheritance, division of inheritance | 名词
