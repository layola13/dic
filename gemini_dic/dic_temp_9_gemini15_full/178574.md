records:/ˈrɛkɔːrdz/| n. v.|记录；唱片；履历|

英文解析:The noun "records" refers to written accounts of events, facts, or figures. It can also refer to phonograph records (vinyl records) or a person's history or achievements.  As a verb, "records" means to set down in writing or some other permanent form; to register officially; to achieve a noteworthy performance.

例句:

The police kept detailed records of the investigation.  警察详细记录了调查过程。

She holds the world record for the 100-meter dash. 她保持着100米短跑的世界纪录。


近义词:accounts, documents, annals, chronicles, registrations, achievements, performances

反义词:oblivion, forgetfulness (for the noun sense of keeping records)


词性:Noun, Verb
