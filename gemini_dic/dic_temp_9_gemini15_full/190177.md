sanskritization:/ˌsænskərɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. | संस्कृतीकरण (sanskṛtīkaraṇa)|  The process by which a lower caste or class adopts the customs, values, and practices of a higher caste or class, typically the Brahmin caste in Hindu society.
