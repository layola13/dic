schleped:/ʃlɛpt/| v.|拖，拉，费力地搬运|To carry or drag something heavy or cumbersome with difficulty.|例句：I schlepped all the groceries home in one trip. 我一趟把所有杂货都扛回家了。|例句：He schlepped his heavy suitcase up the stairs. 他费力地把沉重的行李箱搬上楼梯。|近义词：lug, haul, drag, heave|反义词：carry lightly,  transport easily


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He schleps groceries every Saturday. 他每个星期六都搬运杂货。
She schleps her backpack to school every day. 她每天都把背包费力地背去学校。

一般过去时:
I schlepped the furniture all the way to the new apartment. 我把家具搬到新公寓里。
They schlepped the heavy equipment across the field. 他们费力地把重型设备搬过田野。

现在完成时:
We have schlepped all the boxes upstairs. 我们已经把所有的箱子搬到楼上了。
She has schlepped her computer to every meeting. 她把电脑带到每一个会议上。


过去完成时:
He had schlepped the piano up three flights of stairs before he realized it wouldn't fit. 他把钢琴搬到三楼后才意识到它放不下。
They had schlepped all the supplies to the campsite before it started to rain. 在下雨之前，他们已经把所有的物资搬到了营地。


将来时:
I will schlep the luggage to the hotel. 我会把行李搬到酒店。
They will schlep the equipment to the construction site tomorrow. 他们明天会把设备搬到建筑工地。

虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I wouldn't have to schlep all these boxes. 如果我有车，我就不用费力地搬这些箱子了。
I wish I didn't have to schlep this heavy bag. 我真希望不用搬这个沉重的包。


被动语态:
The boxes were schlepped upstairs by the movers. 箱子由搬运工搬到楼上。
The equipment is being schlepped to the new location. 设备正在被搬运到新的地点。


疑问句:
Did he schlep the piano by himself? 他一个人搬钢琴吗？
Will you schlep the groceries for me? 你会帮我搬杂货吗？


否定句:
I didn't schlep the equipment myself; I got help. 我没有自己搬运设备；我得到了帮助。
She wouldn't schlep the heavy boxes without help. 她不会在没有帮助的情况下搬运沉重的箱子。
