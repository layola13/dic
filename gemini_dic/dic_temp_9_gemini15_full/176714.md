reactivating:/ˌriˈæktɪveɪtɪŋ/ | v. |重新激活；使重新活跃 |  The process of making something active again after a period of inactivity.  This word is the present participle of the verb "reactivate." It describes an ongoing action. |例句：The company is reactivating its marketing campaign after a long hiatus. 公司在长期休整后正在重新启动其营销活动。


The system is reactivating itself after a power outage. 系统在断电后正在自行重新启动。|近义词：restarting, renewing, reviving, reactivating  |反义词：deactivating, disabling, shutting down |动词
