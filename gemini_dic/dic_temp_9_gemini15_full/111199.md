lithology:/lɪˈθɑːlədʒi/| n. |岩性学，岩石学|The study of the physical characteristics of rocks.  It involves describing and classifying rocks based on their mineral composition, texture, and structure.|例句：The lithology of the area consists primarily of sandstone and shale.  该地区的岩性主要由砂岩和页岩组成。|近义词：petrology (more comprehensive)|反义词：N/A
