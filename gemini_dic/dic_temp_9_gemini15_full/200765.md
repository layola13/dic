sleuthing:/ˈsluːθɪŋ/| n.|侦探工作；侦查|The act of investigating or detecting, especially in a methodical or thorough way.  It often implies a degree of secrecy or cunning.|例句：The sleuthing required patience and attention to detail.  侦探工作需要耐心和对细节的关注。

例句：Her sleuthing uncovered a web of deceit. 她的侦探工作揭露了一个欺骗的网络。|近义词：investigating, detecting, inquiring, probing|反义词：ignoring, overlooking, neglecting|名词
