gateway:/ˈɡeɪtˌweɪ/| n.| 网关，门户，通道 | A gateway is an entrance, a point of access, or a means of access to something.  It can refer to physical entrances like a gate or doorway, or metaphorical entrances to systems or networks. |例句：The old stone gateway led to a hidden courtyard. 这座古老的石门通向一个隐藏的庭院。

The company's new software acts as a gateway to a vast database. 这家公司的新软件充当了通往庞大数据库的通道。

近义词：entrance, access point, portal, passage |反义词：exit, barrier, blockade

词源:
The word "gateway" comes from the Old English words "gate" (meaning gate) and "weg" (meaning way).  Therefore, it literally means "gate way," or the path through a gate.  Its modern usage extends beyond the literal meaning to include any point of access or entry.
