recovered:/rɪˈkʌvərd/ | adj. v. |恢复的；痊愈的；重新获得的 |  To regain or retrieve something that was lost, stolen, or missing; to return to a normal state of health, mind, or strength after illness, injury, or loss. | 例句：He recovered his stolen bicycle. 他找回了被偷的自行车。  | 例句：She has fully recovered from her illness. 她已完全康复。 |近义词：regained, retrieved, recouped, healed, restored | 反义词：lost, ruined, damaged, deteriorated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recovers quickly from injuries. = 他伤后恢复得很快。
She recovers her composure after a moment. = 她片刻之后恢复了镇静。


一般过去时:
The police recovered the stolen painting. = 警方找回了被盗的画作。
He recovered from a serious illness last year. = 他去年从一场重病中康复。


现在完成时:
They have recovered all the lost data. = 他们已经找回了所有丢失的数据。
She has fully recovered from the surgery. = 她已经完全从手术中康复了。


过去完成时:
He had recovered his balance before he fell. = 他在摔倒之前已经恢复了平衡。（这个例子略显牵强，过去完成时在recovered中使用频率较低）
By the time I arrived, she had recovered her spirits. = 当我到达时，她已经恢复了精神。


将来时:
Doctors expect him to recover fully. = 医生预计他会完全康复。
They hope to recover the stolen money soon. = 他们希望很快就能追回被盗的钱款。


虚拟语气:
If he had taken his medicine, he would have recovered sooner. = 如果他吃了药，他就会恢复得更快。
I wish I could recover the lost file. = 我真希望我能找回丢失的文件。


被动语态:
The stolen jewels were recovered by the police. = 被盗的珠宝被警方找回。
The lost manuscript was eventually recovered. = 丢失的手稿最终被找回。


疑问句:
Has he recovered from his operation yet? = 他手术后恢复了吗？
Have you recovered your lost keys? = 你找到丢失的钥匙了吗？


否定句:
He hasn't recovered from his illness yet. = 他还没从病中恢复过来。
The company hasn't recovered its lost market share. = 公司还没有恢复失去的市场份额。

PHRASE->
recover from = 从...中恢复
recover oneself = 恢复镇静
recover costs = 挽回损失
speedy recovery = 迅速康复
make a full recovery = 完全康复
