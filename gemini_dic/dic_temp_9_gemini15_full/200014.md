skegger:/ˈskɛɡər/| n. |流浪汉；下层社会的人；（尤指）酗酒的流浪汉|A skegger is a derogatory term for a homeless person, especially one who is an alcoholic or appears disheveled and unkempt.  The term carries strong negative connotations and should be avoided.|例句：He was described as a grizzled skegger begging on the street. 他被描述成一个在街上乞讨的满脸皱纹的流浪汉。  The police often encounter skeggers in the city's underpasses.  警察经常在城市地下通道遇到流浪汉。|近义词：bum, hobo, tramp, derelict|反义词：gentleman, lady
