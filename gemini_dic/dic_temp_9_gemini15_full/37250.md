comic:/ˈkɒmɪk/ /ˈkɑːmɪk/| n. adj. |漫画，滑稽的，喜剧的 |A comic is a funny story told in pictures.  A comic situation is funny and unexpected. |例句：He drew a comic strip about his cat. 他画了一部关于他猫的连环漫画。

词源:
The word "comic" comes from the Latin word "comicus," meaning "belonging to comedy."  The term evolved through Old French and Middle English, ultimately retaining its connection to comedy and humor.
