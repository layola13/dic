peptogenic:/ˌpɛptoˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj. |促使消化不良的；引起消化不良的 |Causing or tending to cause indigestion or dyspepsia.  The word refers to substances or conditions that promote the formation of peptones, which can sometimes lead to digestive upset.  Peptones are partially digested proteins. | Example: The spicy food had a peptogenic effect on his stomach.  这辛辣的食物对他的胃有不良影响。| 近义词：dyspeptic, indigestible | 反义词：easily digestible
