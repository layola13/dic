meep:/mip/| int.| 拟声词；（尤指小鸟或其他小动物的）细弱叫声 |A high-pitched, soft sound, often used to represent the sound of a small bird or other small animal.  It can also express a feeling of surprise or delight.|例句：The little bird meeped softly in its nest.  小鸟在巢里轻轻地叫了一声。“Meep!” exclaimed the child, delighted with the toy. 孩子高兴地叫了一声：“咪！”|近义词： peep, cheep, chirp|反义词： roar, bellow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird meeps softly. = 鸟儿轻轻地叫着。
The chick meeps for its mother. = 小鸡在叫唤它的妈妈。

一般过去时:
The bird meeped all night. = 鸟儿整夜都在叫。
The kitten meeped when it was hungry. = 小猫饿了的时候会发出细微的叫声。

现在完成时:
The bird has meeped several times. = 鸟儿已经叫了几声了。
The baby has meeped all morning. = 宝宝一上午都在发出细微的叫声。

过去完成时:
The bird had meeped before I woke up. = 我醒来之前，鸟儿就已经叫过了。
The kitten had meeped before its owner arrived. = 主人到达之前，小猫就已经叫过了。

将来时:
The bird will meep when it sees food. = 鸟儿看到食物就会叫。
The chick will probably meep when it hatches. = 小鸡孵出来后可能会发出细微的叫声。


虚拟语气:
If the bird meeped louder, I would hear it. = 如果鸟叫得更大声一点，我就能听见了。
I wish the bird would meep again. = 我希望鸟儿再叫一声。

被动语态:
(The word "meep" is rarely used in the passive voice due to its onomatopoeic nature.)


疑问句:
Did the bird meep? = 鸟叫了吗？
Does the chick meep often? = 小鸡经常叫吗？

否定句:
The bird didn't meep today. = 鸟今天没叫。
The kitten hasn't meeped since yesterday. = 从昨天起小猫就没叫过了。


PHRASE->
(The word "meep" is rarely used in phrases. Its primary function is as an onomatopoeia.)
