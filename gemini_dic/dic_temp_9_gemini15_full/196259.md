serenade:/ˌsɛrəˈneɪd/| n. v. |小夜曲； serenading |  A serenade is a musical performance, typically a song, given outdoors at night, especially by a lover to their beloved.  It can also refer to the act of performing such a song. |例句：He serenaded his girlfriend with a beautiful song under her window. 他在女友的窗下为她唱了一首优美的歌曲。

例句：The band gave a lovely serenade in the town square. 乐队在广场上演奏了一首优美的夜曲。


近义词：ballad, love song, nocturne |反义词：None  |词性：Noun, Verb
