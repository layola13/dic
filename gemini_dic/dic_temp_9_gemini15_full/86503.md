heired:/ˈhɛrd/| v.|继承|To receive something, typically property or a title, as an heir.|例句：She heired her grandmother's estate. 她继承了她祖母的遗产。|近义词：inherited, received|反义词：bequeathed, left


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He heires the family business. = 他继承家族企业。
She heires a large sum of money. = 她继承了一大笔钱。

一般过去时:
My uncle heired a fortune. = 我的叔叔继承了一笔财富。
They heired the throne after the king's death. = 国王死后他们继承了王位。

现在完成时:
He has heired his father's house. = 他已经继承了他父亲的房子。
She has heired a significant amount of wealth. = 她已经继承了大量的财富。

过去完成时:
Before the will was read, she had heired nothing. = 在遗嘱宣读之前，她什么也没继承。
He had heired the title before his father's death. = 他在他父亲死前就已经继承了头衔。

将来时:
She will heir the family farm. = 她将继承家族农场。
They will heir the company after their parents retire. = 他们的父母退休后，他们将继承公司。

虚拟语气:
If he heired the company, he would modernize it. = 如果他继承了公司，他会使它现代化。
I wish I had heired my grandfather's collection of stamps. = 我真希望我继承了我祖父的邮票收藏。

被动语态:
The estate was heired by his nephew. = 这笔遗产由他的侄子继承。
The throne was heired by the eldest son. = 王位由长子继承。

疑问句:
Who heired the property? = 谁继承了这处房产？
Will she heir her mother's business? = 她会继承她母亲的生意吗？

否定句:
He did not heir anything from his parents. = 他没有从父母那里继承任何东西。
She did not heir the title. = 她没有继承头衔。
