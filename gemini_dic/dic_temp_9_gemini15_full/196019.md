sentineling: /ˈsɛntɪnəlɪŋ/ | v. | 担任哨兵；警戒；守卫 | The act of acting as a sentinel; keeping watch; guarding.  |  The soldiers were sentineling the border. 士兵们正在警戒边境。  | 近义词：guarding, patrolling, watching | 反义词：abandoning, neglecting

The guards are sentineling the perimeter. = 警卫们正在警戒周边区域。
