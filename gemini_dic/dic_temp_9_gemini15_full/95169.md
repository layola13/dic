inattention:/ˌɪnəˈtɛnʃən/| n. |注意力不集中，疏忽 |Lack of attention; carelessness.  It can refer to a single instance of not paying attention or a general tendency to be easily distracted.|His inattention to detail led to several mistakes in the report.  他的粗心大意导致报告中出现几个错误。The teacher noticed the student's inattention during the lesson. 老师注意到学生上课时注意力不集中。|近义词：negligence, carelessness, distraction, oversight|反义词：attention, focus, concentration|名词
