fitt:/fɪt/| v. n. adj.|适合，安装，合身；状态；合适的|to be of the right size or shape; to be suitable or appropriate; a sudden, brief attack or spasm; a sudden outburst of emotion.|例句：Those shoes fit perfectly. 这双鞋很合脚。  The new software fits our needs. 新软件符合我们的需求。|近义词：suit, match, adapt|反义词：misfit, unsuitable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dress fits me well. = 这件衣服很合身。
This key fits the lock. = 这把钥匙配得上这把锁。

一般过去时:
The puzzle pieces fit together perfectly. = 拼图碎片完美地拼合在一起。
The old glove didn't fit my hand anymore. = 那只旧手套不再适合我的手了。

现在完成时:
I have finally found a job that fits my skills. = 我终于找到了一份符合我技能的工作。
He has fit the new window perfectly. = 他已经完美地安装了新窗户。


过去完成时:
The old coat had fitted him well when he was younger. = 当他年轻的时候，那件旧外套很合身。
She had fit the pieces together before I arrived. = 我到达之前，她已经把碎片拼合在一起了。


将来时:
This shirt will fit you better after it's washed. = 这件衬衫洗过之后会更合身。
I think this piece will fit in the puzzle. = 我认为这块碎片可以放入拼图中。


虚拟语气:
If the shoes fit, I would buy them. = 如果鞋子合脚，我会买下它们。
If the program fitted our needs, we would use it. = 如果程序符合我们的需求，我们会使用它。


被动语态:
The new carpet was fitted yesterday. = 新地毯昨天铺好了。
The window was fitted by a professional. = 窗户是由专业人士安装的。


疑问句:
Does this jacket fit you? = 这件夹克适合你吗？
Does this part fit into the machine? = 这个部件适合这台机器吗？


否定句:
The shoes don't fit me. = 这双鞋不合脚。
The plan didn't fit the budget. = 这个计划超支了。


PHRASE->
fit in:  融入；适应
fit for: 适合于
keep fit: 保持健康
fit out: 配备
in fit: 处于良好状态
