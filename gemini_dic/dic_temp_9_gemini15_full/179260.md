redistilling: /ˌriːdɪˈstɪlɪŋ/ | v. | 再蒸馏；重新提炼；重新考虑 |  To distill again; to subject to a second or further distillation.  It can also mean to re-examine or reconsider something carefully. |


例句：

The chemist is redistilling the alcohol to increase its purity. = 化学家正在对酒精进行再蒸馏以提高其纯度。

He's redistilling his ideas after the project's failure. = 项目失败后，他正在重新考虑他的想法。


近义词：reprocessing, refining, re-examining, reconsidering


反义词：diluting, weakening


词性：动词
