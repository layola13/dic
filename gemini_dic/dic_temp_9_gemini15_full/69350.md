federally:/ˈfɛdərəli/| adv.|联邦地；在联邦政府的管辖下| relating to or imposed by the federal government of a country.|The program is federally funded.  这个项目是由联邦政府资助的。|The issue is handled federally, not at the state level.|这个问题是在联邦层面处理的，而不是在州一级。|nationally, centrally|locally, regionally

一般现在时:
The government federally regulates the banking industry. = 政府对银行业进行联邦监管。
Federally mandated safety standards are implemented across the country. = 联邦规定的安全标准在全国范围内实施。


一般过去时:
The project was federally funded last year. = 该项目去年获得了联邦政府的资助。
The law was federally enacted in 1965. = 这部法律于1965年由联邦政府颁布。


现在完成时:
The government has federally protected several endangered species. = 政府已经对几种濒危物种进行了联邦保护。
The agency has federally investigated numerous cases of fraud. = 该机构已经对许多欺诈案件进行了联邦调查。


过去完成时:
The issue had already been decided federally before the state could intervene. = 在州政府能够介入之前，这个问题已经由联邦政府决定了。
They had federally regulated the industry for decades before the changes. = 在改革之前，他们已经对该行业进行了数十年的联邦监管。


将来时:
The new law will be federally implemented next year. = 新法将于明年由联邦政府实施。
They will federally prosecute the criminals. = 他们将对罪犯进行联邦起诉。


虚拟语气:
If this were a federally funded project, we would have more resources. = 如果这是一个联邦资助的项目，我们将拥有更多资源。
I wish the decision had been made federally rather than at the state level. = 我希望这个决定是由联邦政府而不是州政府做出的。


被动语态:
The regulations are federally enforced. = 这些规章由联邦政府强制执行。
The investigation is being federally overseen. = 调查正在接受联邦政府的监督。


疑问句:
Is this product federally approved? = 这个产品获得了联邦批准吗？
Are these regulations federally mandated? = 这些规章是联邦政府强制规定的吗？


否定句:
The company is not federally regulated. = 这家公司不受联邦政府监管。
The decision was not federally approved. = 该决定未获得联邦批准。
