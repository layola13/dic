grass:/ɡræs/| n.|草，牧草|Grass is a common plant found in many parts of the world.  草是世界许多地方常见的一种植物。|近义词：herb, turf, vegetation|反义词：pavement, concrete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The grass grows quickly in the spring. = 春天草长得很快。
We mow the lawn every week. = 我们每周都修剪草坪。


一般过去时:
The grass was green and lush last week. = 上周草又绿又茂盛。
They cut the grass yesterday. = 他们昨天割了草。


现在完成时:
The grass has grown significantly since the rain. = 雨后草长得很快。
We have already mowed the lawn twice this month. = 这个月我们已经修剪了两次草坪。


过去完成时:
The grass had already been cut before the picnic. = 野餐前草已经被割了。
They had not watered the grass for weeks. = 他们已经好几周没浇草了。


将来时:
The grass will be taller next week. = 下周草会更高。
We will mow the grass tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上会割草。


虚拟语气:
If the grass were taller, we wouldn't be able to see the flowers. = 如果草更高，我们就看不到花了。
I wish the grass were greener. = 我希望草更绿。


被动语态:
The grass is often cut by the gardener. = 草坪通常由园丁修剪。
The lawn was damaged by the recent storm. = 草坪在最近的风暴中受损。


疑问句:
Is the grass wet? = 草地湿了吗？
How often do you cut the grass? = 你多久割一次草？


否定句:
The grass isn't growing well this year. = 今年草长得不好。
They didn't cut the grass last weekend. = 他们上周末没有割草。
