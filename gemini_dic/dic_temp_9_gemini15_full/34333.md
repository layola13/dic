clashing: /ˈklæʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 冲突的；不协调的；格格不入的 |  Describes things that are in conflict or are jarringly different and do not go well together.  This can refer to colors, styles, ideas, or personalities. |

例句：

The clashing colors of the painting were jarring to the eye. = 画中冲突的色彩刺眼。

Their clashing personalities led to frequent arguments. = 他们格格不入的性格导致了频繁的争吵。


近义词：conflicting, discordant, jarring, incongruous, incompatible

反义词：harmonious, compatible, consistent, matching


词性：形容词
