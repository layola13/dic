reconceal: /ˌriːkənˈsiːl/ | vt. |重新隐藏；再次隐藏 | To hide something again after it has been found or revealed.  |例句：He reconcealed the documents in a secret compartment. 他把文件重新藏在一个秘密隔间里。

例句：After the police found the evidence, the suspect tried to reconceal it. 警方找到证据后，嫌疑人试图再次将其隐藏起来。


近义词：hide again, re-hide, conceal anew | 反义词：reveal, expose, uncover | 词性：动词
