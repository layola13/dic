dragnets:/ˈdræɡnɛts/ | n. |拖网；大规模搜捕 | A dragnet is a large net used for fishing, or, figuratively, a large-scale operation to apprehend criminals or gather information.  It implies a sweeping, comprehensive approach, often involving many people and resources. |例句：The police set up a dragnet to catch the escaped prisoners. = 警方设置了 dragnet 来抓捕逃犯。

The police launched a massive dragnet after the bank robbery. = 银行抢劫案发生后，警方展开了大规模搜捕。

近义词：sweeping operation, roundup, manhunt | 反义词：selective search, targeted investigation | 词性:noun
