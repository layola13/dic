reinfection's:/ˌriːɪnˈfɛkʃənz/| n.|复数名词，再感染|The act or instance of being infected again, especially with the same disease.|例句：Concerns about reinfections are rising with the emergence of new variants.  对新变种出现后再次感染的担忧正在上升。


例句：The study investigated the risk factors associated with COVID-19 reinfections. 这项研究调查了与COVID-19再感染相关的危险因素。


近义词：repeated infections, secondary infections


反义词：initial infection, primary infection


词性：名词
