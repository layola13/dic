loosed:/luːst/| v. |释放，解开，松开|To release from confinement; to make loose or less tight.|The dog loosed itself from its leash.  狗挣脱了它的皮带。|The sailor loosed the sails. 水手松开了帆。|tighten, bind, fasten|secure, restrain|v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He looses the dog every morning. 他每天早上都放狗。
She looses her hair before bed. 她在睡觉前会把头发解开。

The prisoner loosed himself from his chains. 囚犯挣脱了锁链。
