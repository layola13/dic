draws:/drɔːz/| /drɑːz/| vt. n.|拉，拽；绘画；吸引；提取；(尤指比赛)平局|To pull something along a surface. To make a picture with a pencil, pen, or crayon. To attract or interest someone. To take something from a source.|例句：She draws a picture of a cat. 她画了一幅猫的画。 He draws strength from his family. 他从家人那里汲取力量。|近义词：pull, attract, sketch, depict|反义词：push, repel
