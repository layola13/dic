slid:/slaɪd/| v.|滑动，滑行|to move smoothly along a surface|The children slid down the grassy hill.孩子们从草坡上滑了下来。The drawers slid open easily. 抽屉很容易就滑开了。|近义词：glide, slip, scoot|反义词：stick, jam


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slides the book across the table. 他把书滑过桌子。
The ice skater slides gracefully on the ice. 花样滑冰运动员优雅地在冰上滑行。

一般过去时:
She slid into the seat beside me. 她滑到我旁边的座位上。
The car slid on the icy road. 汽车在结冰的路面上打滑。

现在完成时:
The children have slid down the slide many times. 孩子们已经滑滑梯很多次了。
He has slid into a deep depression. 他已经陷入深深的抑郁之中。

过去完成时:
Before I knew it, he had slid the paper into his pocket.  在我还没反应过来之前，他已经把纸塞进了口袋。
The thief had slid away before the police arrived.  警察到达之前，小偷已经溜走了。

将来时:
I think the snow will slide off the roof soon. 我想雪很快就会从屋顶上滑下来。
They will slide into the next round of competition.  他们将晋级下一轮比赛。

虚拟语气:
If he had slid the door open more gently, it wouldn't have made that noise. 如果他更轻柔地推开房门，就不会发出那种声音了。
I wish I had slid into that job opportunity. 我真希望我当初能得到那个工作机会。

被动语态:
The box was slid under the table. 箱子被滑到桌子底下。
The tray was slid smoothly across the counter. 托盘被平稳地滑过柜台。

疑问句:
Did the window slide open by itself? 窗户是自己滑开的吗？
How did the rock slide down the mountain? 岩石是如何从山上滑下来的？

否定句:
The door didn't slide open easily. 门没有很容易就滑开。
The plan didn't slide into place as smoothly as we'd hoped. 计划并没有像我们希望的那样顺利进行。


PHRASE->
slide into: 滑入，陷入
slide past: 从…旁边滑过
slide down: 滑下
slide off: 滑落
slide away:溜走
