oneliner: /ˈwʌnlaɪnər/ | n. | 单行本，单行语句，一句笑话 | A one-liner is a short, humorous statement, often a joke or witty remark, that can be delivered in a single line.  It's concise and impactful. | Example:  "I used to hate facial hair, but then it grew on me."  我以前讨厌面部毛发，但后来我习惯了。(这是一个单行笑话) | 近义词：short joke, quip, epigram | 反义词：long speech, essay, novel |  n.
