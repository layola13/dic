embodying:/ɪmˈbɑːdiɪŋ/| v.| 体现；包含；具体化 | To represent or be a perfect example of a quality, idea, or feeling.  |例句：Her paintings embody the spirit of the 1960s.  她的绘画作品体现了20世纪60年代的精神。 |例句：The new building embodies the latest architectural design. 这座新建筑体现了最新的建筑设计理念。|近义词：incarnate, exemplify, represent, personify |反义词：negate, contradict, disavow |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is embodying the role of Hamlet perfectly. = 他完美地诠释了哈姆雷特这个角色。
The sculpture embodies the artist's vision. = 这尊雕塑体现了艺术家的构思。

一般过去时:
She embodied grace and elegance on stage. = 她舞台上展现了优雅和高贵。
The novel embodied the struggles of the working class. = 这部小说体现了工人的挣扎。

现在完成时:
His work has embodied the spirit of innovation. = 他的作品体现了创新的精神。
The museum has embodied the history of the city. = 这个博物馆体现了这座城市的历史。

过去完成时:
The play had embodied the themes of love and loss. = 这部戏剧已经体现了爱情和失去的主题。
The painting had embodied the artist's emotions. = 这幅画已经体现了艺术家的情感。

将来时:
The new building will embody sustainable design principles. = 新建筑将体现可持续的设计理念。
The film will embody the story of a brave hero. = 这部电影将体现一个勇敢英雄的故事。

虚拟语气:
If the sculpture embodied more emotion, it would be more powerful. = 如果雕塑能体现更多情感，它就会更有力量。
I wish the painting embodied a clearer message. = 我希望这幅画能传达一个更清晰的信息。

被动语态:
The ideals of democracy are embodied in the constitution. = 民主理想体现在宪法中。
The artist's vision was embodied in the final artwork. = 艺术家的构想体现在最终的艺术作品中。

疑问句:
Does this design embody the client's needs? = 这个设计是否体现了客户的需求？
Does the novel embody the author's personal experiences? = 这部小说是否体现了作者的个人经历？

否定句:
The film did not embody the spirit of the original novel. = 这部电影没有体现原著的精神。
His actions did not embody the values he claimed to uphold. = 他的行为没有体现他声称坚持的价值观。


PHRASE->

embody the principle of = 体现…的原则
embody a concept = 体现一个概念
