preenclose:/ˌpriːɪnˈkloʊz/ | vt. |预先封闭；预先封存|To enclose or seal something beforehand, typically to protect or preserve it.  This word is relatively rare and often found in specialized contexts.|例句：The documents were pre-enclosed in a tamper-evident bag for security. 文件预先被封存在防篡改袋中以确保安全。

例句：The lab technician pre-enclosed the samples to prevent contamination. 实验室技术员预先封存样本以防止污染。|近义词：seal, enclose, secure, protect |反义词：open, expose, unseal
