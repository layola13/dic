lets-on:/lets ɔn/| v.|假装，冒充；表现出；泄露|To pretend; to feign; to give the appearance of; to reveal unintentionally.|例句：He lets on that he's rich, but I know he's broke. 他假装自己很有钱，但我知他身无分文。

He lets on that he doesn't care, but I can see it bothers him. 他假装不在乎，但我看得出这让他很困扰。


近义词：pretend, feign, affect, simulate|反义词：reveal honestly, admit, confess|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lets on that he knows everything. = 他假装什么都知道。
She lets on that she's not interested. = 她假装不感兴趣。


一般过去时:
He let on that he was a doctor. = 他假装自己是医生。
She let on that she hadn't seen him. = 她假装没看见他。


现在完成时:
He has let on that he's leaving the company. = 他已经暗示他要离开公司了。
She has let on that she's unhappy with her job. = 她已经暗示她对她的工作不满意。


过去完成时:
He had let on that he was going to quit his job before he actually did. = 他在真正辞职之前就暗示过他要辞职。
She had let on that she was having problems with her family. = 她早就暗示她与家人之间有问题。


将来时:
He will let on that he's busy. = 他会假装很忙。
She will let on that she doesn't understand. = 她会假装不懂。


虚拟语气:
If he let on that he was guilty, he would be in trouble. = 如果他假装有罪，他会惹上麻烦。
If she let on that she knew the secret, she would be in danger. = 如果她泄露了她知道秘密，她会处于危险之中。


被动语态:
It was let on that the company was in financial trouble. = 公司有财务问题这件事被暗示出来了。
It was let on that the project was behind schedule. = 项目进度落后这件事被暗示出来了。


疑问句:
Does he let on that he's worried? = 他会表现出他很担心吗？
Does she let on that she's upset? = 她会表现出她很不高兴吗？


否定句:
He didn't let on that he knew the answer. = 他没有表现出他知道答案。
She didn't let on that she was feeling unwell. = 她没有表现出她感觉不舒服。



PHRASE->
let on to somebody about something: 向某人透露某事
let something on: 泄露某事
let it be known: 让大家知道
let it slip: 无意中泄露
let the cat out of the bag: 泄露秘密
