jarful:/ˈdʒɑːrfʊl/| n. | 一罐…；满满一罐… | A jarful is a container, typically a jar, that is full of something. It emphasizes the fullness of the jar.  | Example:  She brought a jarful of homemade jam to the picnic. 她带了一罐自制果酱去野餐。  |近义词：a full jar, a jar of... | 反义词：an empty jar
