perpetuity:/pɜːrˈpjuːɪti/| n. |永久；永恒；永久性；无限期|A perpetuity is something that continues forever or for an indefinitely long time.  It often refers to an investment that pays out indefinitely. |Example: The endowment ensures the museum's perpetuity.  这笔捐赠确保了博物馆的永久存在。

Example: He left his estate in perpetuity to his descendants. 他将他的遗产永久地留给了他的后代。|近义词：eternity, permanence, immortality |反义词：termination, end, cessation

词源:

From Middle French *perpétuité, from Latin perpetuitas, from perpetuus "continuous, lasting," from per- "through" + petere "to seek, strive for."  The sense development is from "continuing without interruption" to "lasting forever."
