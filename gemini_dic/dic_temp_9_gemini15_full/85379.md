harsher:/ˈhɑːrʃər/| adj.| 更严厉的，更苛刻的 | More severe, cruel, or unpleasant.  It is the comparative form of "harsh".|例句：The teacher's harsher tone silenced the class. 老师更严厉的语气使课堂安静下来。
The harsher penalties deterred potential criminals. 更严厉的惩罚阻止了潜在的罪犯。|近义词：severer, stricter, rougher, more rigorous |反义词：milder, gentler, kinder, more lenient |词性:形容词
