prophetic: /prəˈfɛtɪk/ | adj. | 预言的；预示的；先知的 | Relating to or characteristic of a prophet or prophecy; having or showing the ability to predict the future.  | 例句：The oracle delivered a prophetic warning about the impending war.  先知给出了关于即将到来的战争的预言警告。


例句：His prophetic vision accurately foretold the market crash. 他的预言准确地预言了市场崩盘。


近义词：predictive, precognitive, visionary, prescient


反义词：unforeseeable, unpredictable
