indor: /ˈɪndɔːr/ | adj. | 室内的；户内的 | Indoor refers to something that takes place or is located within a building or enclosed space.  It contrasts with "outdoor." | Example: The indoor swimming pool is open year-round.  室内游泳池全年开放。| 近义词：interior, inside | 反义词：outdoor, exterior


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plants are thriving indoors. = 这些植物在室内茁壮成长。
We play board games indoors when it rains. = 下雨的时候我们在室内玩棋盘游戏。


一般过去时:
They held the party indoors because of the bad weather. = 因为天气不好，他们把派对办在了室内。
We kept the children indoors during the storm. = 暴风雨期间，我们把孩子们留在室内。


现在完成时:
We have always kept our pets indoors. = 我们一直把宠物养在室内。
They have moved their workout equipment indoors for the winter. = 他们已经把健身器材搬到室内过冬了。


过去完成时:
They had already moved the furniture indoors before the rain started. = 雨开始下之前，他们已经把家具搬到室内了。
We had spent the entire day indoors, avoiding the heat. = 我们一整天都待在室内，避开酷暑。


将来时:
We will have the concert indoors next month. = 我们下个月将在室内举行音乐会。
They will likely keep the animals indoors during the night. = 他们很可能在晚上把动物留在室内。


虚拟语气:
If it were colder, we would prefer to stay indoors. = 如果更冷的话，我们会更喜欢待在室内。
I wish we had stayed indoors instead of going to the beach. = 我真希望我们待在室内而不是去海滩。


被动语态:
The meeting was held indoors. = 会议在室内举行。
The plants are regularly watered indoors. = 室内植物定期浇水。


疑问句:
Is the event being held indoors or outdoors? = 活动是在室内还是室外举行？
Are you comfortable working indoors all day? = 你整天在室内工作感觉舒服吗？


否定句:
The game wasn't played indoors; it was played outdoors. = 游戏不是在室内进行的；而是在室外进行的。
The plants won't survive if they are not kept indoors during winter. = 如果冬天不把植物放在室内，它们就活不了。
