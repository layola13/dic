puceron:/py.sʁɔ̃/| n. |蚜虫|An aphid is a small insect that feeds on the sap of plants. They are often found in large colonies and can cause significant damage to crops.  |例句：The roses are covered in pucerons. 玫瑰上布满了蚜虫。 The farmer sprayed pesticide to kill the pucerons on his lettuce. 农民喷洒杀虫剂来杀死他莴苣上的蚜虫。|近义词：aphid, plant louse |反义词: N/A
