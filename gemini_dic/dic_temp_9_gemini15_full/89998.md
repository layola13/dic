horticultor:/ˈhɔːrtɪkʌltʃərɪst/|/n.|园艺家，栽培学家|A horticulturist is a person who cultivates or studies the art, science, technology, and business of growing plants, especially fruits, vegetables, flowers, and ornamental plants.|例句：The horticulturist carefully pruned the rose bushes.  这位园艺家仔细地修剪了玫瑰花丛。|例句：A skilled horticulturist can coax even the most stubborn plants to flourish. 一位熟练的园艺家甚至可以诱使最顽固的植物繁茂生长。|近义词：gardener, cultivator, plantsman|反义词：N/A
