mace:/meɪs/| n.|权杖；警棍；香料；|A mace is a ceremonial staff, often made of metal and bearing a crown or other decorative head.  It can also refer to a heavy club used as a weapon, or a spice derived from the mace tree. |例句：The king carried a golden mace as a symbol of his power. 国王拿着金权杖作为权力的象征。

The police officer used his mace to subdue the suspect. 警官用警棍制服了嫌疑犯。


近义词：scepter, club, truncheon|反义词：None specifically.
