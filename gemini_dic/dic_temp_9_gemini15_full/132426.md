nondependences:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈpɛndənsiz/| n.|非依赖性，独立性|The absence of dependence; independence.  The state of not being dependent on something or someone. This term often appears in technical contexts, particularly in fields like engineering and computer science, referring to systems or components that can operate without reliance on other systems or components.|例句：The system boasts high nondependences, meaning it can function even with partial component failure.  该系统具有高度的非依赖性，这意味着即使部分组件发生故障，它也能正常工作。|近义词：independence, autonomy, self-sufficiency|反义词：dependence, reliance, interdependency|名词
