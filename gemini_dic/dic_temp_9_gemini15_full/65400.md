euphoniad:/juːˈfoʊniæd/| n. |优美的声音；悦耳的音调|A euphoniad is a pleasing or harmonious sound or combination of sounds, especially in speech or music.  It often refers to a piece of music or a passage of writing characterized by its beautiful sound.  The term is less common than synonyms like "euphony" but carries a similar meaning with a stronger emphasis on the specific instance of beautiful sound. |Example: The poet crafted each line as a euphoniad, delighting the reader with its melodious flow.  诗人精心打造每一行诗句，使其悦耳动听，使读者感到愉悦。|近义词：euphony, melodious sound, sweet sound |反义词：cacophony, discordant sound |名词
