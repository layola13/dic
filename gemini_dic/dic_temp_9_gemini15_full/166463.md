prerevisions:/ˌpriːrɪˈvɪʒənz/| n. |预先修改；预审；预先修订|The act of revising something before it is finalized or published.  It involves making changes, corrections, or improvements to a draft, plan, or document in advance of its official release or implementation.  Prerevisions aim to enhance quality, accuracy, and effectiveness before the final version is presented.|Example: The prerevisions of the contract took several weeks.  预先修改合同花费了好几周时间。  The editor requested prerevisions before the manuscript went to print. 编辑要求在稿件付印前进行预先修改。|近义词：preliminary revisions, pre-edits, pre-corrections|反义词：post-revisions, finalization|名词
