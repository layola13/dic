crosshatch:/ˈkrɒsˌhætʃ/| /ˈkrɑːsˌhætʃ/| v. n.|交叉阴影；交叉线纹|To mark with a pattern of intersecting lines.  This can refer to both the action of creating the pattern and the pattern itself.  The pattern is often used for shading or texture in drawing or design.|例句：The artist crosshatched the drawing to create depth.  艺术家用交叉阴影法为绘画增加了深度。The fabric had a distinctive crosshatch pattern. 这块布料有独特的交叉线纹图案。|近义词：hatch, grid, network|反义词：solid, plain
