noncolorfast: /ˌnɒnˈkʌlərfɑːst/ | adj. | 不耐色的，褪色的 |  Describes a fabric or dye that loses its color easily when washed, exposed to sunlight, or otherwise treated.  This adjective indicates a lack of colorfastness. | Example:  The noncolorfast fabric faded significantly after just one wash.  这块不耐色的布料洗一次就严重褪色了。 | 近义词：fade-prone, fugitive | 反义词：colorfast, color-resistant
