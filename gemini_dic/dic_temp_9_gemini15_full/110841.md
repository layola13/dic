lintel:/ˈlɪntəl/| n.| 门楣；过梁 | A horizontal structural member over an opening (such as a door or window) that carries the weight of the material above it.  |The lintel above the doorway was carved with intricate designs. 门上的过梁刻有复杂的图案。 The builders carefully installed the reinforced concrete lintel. 建筑工人仔细地安装了钢筋混凝土过梁。| threshold, header |  (无明确反义词，可考虑用 requiring support) | 名词
