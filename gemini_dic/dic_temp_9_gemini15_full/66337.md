exculpate:/ˈɛksʌlpeɪt/ | vt. | 开脱；使免罪；辩护 | To clear from blame or guilt. To free from guilt or blame.  | The evidence exculpated the suspect.  证据证明嫌犯无罪。|The lawyer worked hard to exculpate his client. 律师努力为他的当事人开脱罪责。| absolve, exonerate, vindicate | incriminate, condemn, convict |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lawyer exculpates his client. = 律师为他的当事人辩护。
She attempts to exculpate herself from the blame. = 她试图为自己开脱罪责。


一般过去时:
The jury exculpated the defendant. = 陪审团宣告被告无罪。
The new evidence exculpated him from any wrongdoing. = 新证据证明他与任何不法行为无关。


现在完成时:
The investigation has exculpated the mayor. = 调查已经证明市长无罪。
The court has exculpated him from all charges. = 法院已经为他撤销所有指控。


过去完成时:
The witness testimony had exculpated her before the trial concluded. = 证人证词在审判结束前就已经为她开脱了罪责。
The judge had already exculpated him by the time the new evidence emerged. = 新证据出现时，法官已经为他开脱了罪责。


将来时:
The results of the test will exculpate him. = 测试结果将会证明他无罪。
We believe the upcoming investigation will exculpate her. = 我们相信即将进行的调查将会为她洗清冤屈。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence were stronger, it would exculpate him completely. = 如果证据更充分，它就能完全为他开脱。
If the witness had testified earlier, it might have exculpated him. = 如果证人早些作证，可能会为他开脱。


被动语态:
He was exculpated by the court. = 他被法院宣告无罪。
The suspect was exculpated after a thorough investigation. = 经过彻底调查后，嫌疑人被洗清了嫌疑。


疑问句:
Will the new evidence exculpate the accused? = 新证据会为被告开脱吗？
Can this testimony truly exculpate him from the crime? = 这份证词真的能为他洗脱罪名吗？


否定句:
The evidence did not exculpate him. = 证据并没有为他开脱。
They failed to exculpate themselves from responsibility. = 他们未能为自己免除责任。


PHRASE->
exculpate oneself = 为自己开脱
be exculpated from = 免于…的责备
fully exculpate = 完全开脱
partially exculpate = 部分开脱
exculpate completely = 完全洗清


词源:
From Latin *exculpāre, from ex- "out" + culpa "fault, blame".
