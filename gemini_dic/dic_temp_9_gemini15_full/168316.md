pro-Peruvian: /ˌproʊ pəˈruːviən/ | adj. | 亲秘鲁的，支持秘鲁的 |  Favoring or supporting Peru or its people and interests.  It suggests a positive attitude towards Peru and its culture, politics, or economy. |

例句：

He holds pro-Peruvian views on trade agreements. = 他对贸易协定持有亲秘鲁的观点。

Her pro-Peruvian stance is evident in her political activism. = 她的政治行动主义中明显体现了她亲秘鲁的立场。


近义词：Peruvian-supporting, Peru-friendly

反义词：anti-Peruvian


词性:形容词
