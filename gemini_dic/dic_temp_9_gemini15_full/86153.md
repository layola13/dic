heartlet:/ˈhɑːrtlɪt/| n. |小小心脏；小爱；微小的爱心|A diminutive of heart, suggesting something small and charmingly like a heart.  It often implies a small, tender, or affectionate feeling. |例句：The artist painted a heartlet on the corner of the canvas.  艺术家在画布的角落画了一个小爱心。

例句：She felt a heartlet of sympathy for the lost puppy. 她对那只走失的小狗感到一丝同情。


近义词：small heart, tiny heart, affection


反义词：None directly opposite, but antonyms of the implied meanings could include indifference, hatred.


词性:名词
