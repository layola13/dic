lightened:/ˈlaɪtnəd/| v.| 使变轻；使轻松；使愉快；照亮 | To make or become lighter in weight, color, or intensity; to make less serious or burdensome; to illuminate. |例句：The news lightened his mood.  = 这个消息使他心情轻松起来。
The room was lightened by a single lamp. =  房间里只有一盏灯照亮。 |近义词：brightened, eased, relieved |反义词：darkened, burdened, aggravated


USAGE->

He lightens the load by sharing the work. = 他分担工作以减轻负担。
The sun lightens the room. = 太阳照亮了房间。
