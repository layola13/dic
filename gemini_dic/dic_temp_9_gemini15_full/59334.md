earli: /ˈɜːrli/ | adj. | 早的，早期的 | Early means happening or done before the usual or expected time.  It can also refer to something that existed or occurred in a time before the present. | 例句：He woke up early this morning. 他今天早上起得很早。  The early bird catches the worm.  早起的鸟儿有虫吃。 | 近义词：ahead of time, premature, beforehand | 反义词：late, tardy
