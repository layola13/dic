cogwheel:/ˈkɒɡwiːl/ /ˈkɑːɡwiːl/| n. |齿轮，轮齿|A cogwheel is a wheel with a series of teeth or cogs that mesh with the teeth of another cogwheel to transmit rotary motion.  It's a fundamental part of many machines and mechanisms.|The clock's cogwheels were intricately designed.|近义词：gear, toothed wheel|反义词：None specifically
