overflowed:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊd/| v.| 溢出；泛滥；充满；洋溢 | to flow over the brim or limits of something; to be filled to excess | The river overflowed its banks after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，河水漫过了河岸。 The audience overflowed with enthusiasm. 观众热情洋溢。| flooded, inundated, brimmed | contained, emptied | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The bathtub overflows when I fill it too much. = 当我放太多水时，浴缸会溢出来。
The river often overflows in the spring. = 河水春天经常泛滥。


一般过去时:
The milk overflowed the glass. = 牛奶溢出了杯子。
My emotions overflowed after I heard the good news. = 听到好消息后，我的情绪激动不已。


现在完成时:
The river has overflowed three times this year. = 今年这条河已经泛滥了三次。
My inbox has overflowed with spam emails. = 我的收件箱塞满了垃圾邮件。


过去完成时:
The river had overflowed before the emergency services arrived. = 紧急服务人员到达之前，河水就已经泛滥了。
The cup had overflowed, leaving a mess on the table. = 杯子已经溢出来了，桌子上弄了一团糟。


将来时:
The river will overflow if it rains heavily again. = 如果再次下大雨，河水将会泛滥。
I think my heart will overflow with joy if I win the lottery. = 我想如果我中了彩票，我的心将会充满喜悦。


虚拟语气:
If the dam had broken, the river would have overflowed catastrophically. = 如果水坝决堤，河水就会灾难性地泛滥。
If I had more time, my creativity would overflow. = 如果我有更多的时间，我的创造力就会奔涌而出。


被动语态:
The streets were overflowed by the rising tide. = 街道被上涨的潮水淹没了。
The stadium was overflowed with fans. = 体育场挤满了球迷。


疑问句:

否定句:
The cup didn't overflow. = 杯子没有溢出来。
The river hasn't overflowed this year. = 今年河水没有泛滥。
