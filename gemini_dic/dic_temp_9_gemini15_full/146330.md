overfertility:/ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrtaɪləti/| n. |生育过剩，过度生育|Excessive fertility, often resulting in an undesired or unsustainable population increase.  It can refer to individual cases of high fertility or to broader societal trends.|例句：The overfertility in the region led to food shortages and environmental strain.  该地区的生育过剩导致粮食短缺和环境压力。

例句：The government implemented policies to address the issue of overfertility and promote family planning.  政府实施政策以解决生育过剩问题并推广计划生育。


近义词：hyperfertility, excessive reproduction

反义词：infertility, subfertility


词性：名词
