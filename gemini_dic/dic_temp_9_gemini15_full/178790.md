recuperations: /ˌriːkjuːpəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. | 康复；恢复；痊愈；重获 | The process of recovering from illness, injury, or loss.  It refers to the act of regaining health, strength, or possessions after a period of illness, hardship, or loss. |例句：His recuperation after the surgery was slow but steady.  他的手术后康复缓慢但稳定。

例句：The economic recuperation of the country took several years.  该国的经济复苏花了数年时间。


近义词：recovery, rehabilitation, convalescence, restoration |反义词：deterioration, decline, relapse |词性:名词
