lothario:/ləˈθeəriˌoʊ/| n. | ловелас, 花花公子 | A man who readily seduces women; a seducer.  A Lothario is characterized by his charm, attractiveness, and his pursuit of numerous romantic relationships, often without commitment or emotional depth.  | Example: He was known as a Lothario, charming his way through countless affairs. 他以花花公子的身份闻名，用魅力周旋于无数风流韵事之中。| 近义词:womanizer, seducer, philanderer, rake | 反义词:faithful, monogamous, loyal
