platformed:/ˈplætˌfɔːrmd/| adj. |被平台化的，在平台上发布的|  Having been given a platform or a means of expression or distribution, often online.  This usually implies a degree of control or curation by the platform. |例句：The artist's work was platformed on several major social media sites.  这位艺术家的作品在几个主要的社交媒体网站上发布。

The band's new single was platformed by a major streaming service.  乐队的最新单曲由一家主要的流媒体服务提供商发布。


近义词：published, distributed, featured, showcased |反义词：suppressed, censored, ignored |词性：形容词
