radicchio:/rəˈdɪkiəʊ/| n. | 意大利菊苣 | Radicchio is a type of red leaf lettuce with a slightly bitter taste, often used in salads and as a garnish. It's known for its vibrant red color and slightly crisp texture.  | Example: The salad included radicchio, goat cheese, and walnuts.  这道沙拉包括意大利菊苣、山羊奶酪和核桃。 | 近义词：red leaf lettuce, chicory | 反义词：无
