northernism:/ˈnɔːðənɪzəm/ | n. |北方习语；北方特色；北方主义|A distinctive feature of the speech, culture, or other characteristics of the northern part of a country or region.  It can refer to dialect, customs, or political ideologies associated with the North.| Example: The strong emphasis on community is a key northernism in this region.  这个地区的社区意识强烈是其重要的北方特色。|近义词：regionalism, localism |反义词：southernism (in a contrasting context)
