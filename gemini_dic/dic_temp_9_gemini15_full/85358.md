harrow's:/ˈhærəʊz/| n. |哈罗的（所有格）|The possessive form of "harrow," referring to something belonging to or associated with a harrow.  It can also refer to the Harrow School, a prestigious school in England. |Example:  The farmer sharpened the harrow's tines.  (农民磨快了耙子的齿。) The Harrow's impressive grounds hosted the annual sports day. (哈罗公学令人印象深刻的场地举办了年度运动会。)|近义词: (depending on context)  school's, institution's |反义词: N/A
