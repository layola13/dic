extinguisher:/ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃər/ | n. |灭火器 | A device used to put out fires, typically containing a pressurized agent that is released to extinguish flames.  |Example: He grabbed the fire extinguisher and sprayed the flames. 他抓起灭火器，喷向火焰。|近义词:fire suppressant|反义词:incendiary
