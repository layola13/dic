grillade:/ɡrɪˈlɑːd/ | n. | 烤肉，烧烤肉 | Grilled meat, often served as a main course.  It can refer to the act of grilling as well. | Example:  The restaurant is famous for its delicious grillade. 这家餐厅以其美味的烤肉而闻名。


近义词：barbecue, broil, grill | 反义词：boiled, steamed

词源:
From French *grillade*, from *griller* "to broil, grill".  Ultimately related to Latin *cribrare* "to sift".  The connection lies in the perforated nature of grills.
