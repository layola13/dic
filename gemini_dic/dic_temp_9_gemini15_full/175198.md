ragtags:/ˈræɡtæɡz/| n.|乌合之众；衣衫褴褛的人；破烂东西|A ragtag group is a disorganized and miscellaneous collection of people or things.  It often implies a lack of uniformity or quality.|例句：The army was a ragtag collection of poorly trained soldiers. 这支军队是由一群训练不足的士兵组成的乌合之众。

例句：The flea market was full of ragtags and secondhand goods. 跳蚤市场里满是破烂货和二手货。|近义词：rabble, riffraff, assortment, miscellany|反义词：elite, organized group, well-equipped
