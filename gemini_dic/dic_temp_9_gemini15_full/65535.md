euthanasias:/ˌjuːθəˈneɪʒəz/| n.|安乐死（复数）|The act of intentionally ending a life to relieve pain and suffering;  the practice of euthanasia.|例句：The debate surrounding euthanasias remains highly controversial.  围绕安乐死的辩论仍然极具争议性。
例句：  Different countries have different laws regarding euthanasias. 不同的国家对安乐死有不同的法律规定。|近义词：mercy killing, assisted suicide|反义词：life support, life-prolonging treatment|名词
