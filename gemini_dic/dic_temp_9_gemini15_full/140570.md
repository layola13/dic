oligarchy:/ˈɒlɪɡɑːki/ | /ˈɑːlɪɡɑːrki/ | n. |寡头政治；寡头统治 |A government ruled by a small group of people.  This group typically holds power because of their wealth, family connections, or military strength, and often does not represent the interests of the wider population. |Example: The country was run by an oligarchy of wealthy landowners. 这个国家由一群富有的地主所组成的寡头统治。|近义词：plutocracy, aristocracy |反义词：democracy, republic
