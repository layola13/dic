geocaching:/ˈdʒioʊˌkæʃɪŋ/| n.| 地理寻宝 | The outdoor recreational activity of searching for and finding hidden containers ("geocaches") using GPS coordinates.  It involves using a GPS device or smartphone app to locate hidden containers, called geocaches, placed by other participants.  | Example:  Many people enjoy geocaching as a way to explore new places and enjoy the outdoors. 许多人喜欢地理寻宝，以此探索新地方并享受户外活动。

|近义词：treasure hunting (寻宝)|反义词：None|词性:名词
