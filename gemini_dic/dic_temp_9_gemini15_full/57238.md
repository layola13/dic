downhills:/ˈdaʊnˌhɪlz/| n.|下坡路；下坡；下行路段|Downhills refer to a downward slope or decline, often used in the context of roads, paths, or even metaphorical situations representing a decline or deterioration.|例句：The cyclists sped down the steep downhills.  自行车手快速冲下了陡峭的下坡路。|例句：The company is facing some downhills in the market. 这家公司在市场上面临一些困境。|近义词：descents, declines, slopes|反义词：uphills, ascents, inclines
