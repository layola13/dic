deinoceras:/ˌdaɪnəʊˈsɛrəs/| n. |恐角兽|Deinoceras is an extinct genus of large, rhinoceros-like uintatheres that lived during the Eocene epoch in North America.  They were characterized by their massive size, bony protuberances on their skulls, and prominent tusks.  |例句：The fossil remains of Deinoceras provide valuable insights into the Eocene ecosystem. 恐角兽的化石遗骸为了解始新世生态系统提供了宝贵的见解。
