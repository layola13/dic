no-fault:/ˈnoʊ fɔːlt/ | adj. | 无过错的，无责任的 |  Describes a system or situation where liability or responsibility is not assigned based on fault or blame.  This is often used in insurance and legal contexts. | Example:  In a no-fault insurance system, your own insurance company pays for your injuries regardless of who caused the accident.  在无过错保险制度下，无论是谁造成的事故，你自己的保险公司都会支付你的医药费。 | 近义词：blameless, faultless, irresponsible  | 反义词：at-fault, liable, responsible |
