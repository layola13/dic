out-win:/aʊtˈwɪn/| v.|胜出，超过|To surpass or defeat someone or something.|例句：Our team out-won their team by a wide margin. 我们的团队以很大的优势战胜了他们的团队。|近义词：outdo, surpass, exceed, defeat|反义词：lose, be defeated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The stronger team usually out-wins the weaker one. = 更强的队伍通常会战胜较弱的队伍。
He out-wins his competitors in sales every month. = 他每个月在销售方面都胜过他的竞争对手。


一般过去时:
She out-won all her opponents in the chess tournament. = 她赢得了国际象棋锦标赛所有对手。
The underdog out-won the champion in an unexpected upset. = 黑马爆冷击败了冠军。


现在完成时:
Our company has out-won its rivals in the market share. = 我们公司在市场份额上已经超过了竞争对手。
He has out-won his personal best time in the marathon. = 他在马拉松比赛中超越了自己的最佳成绩。


过去完成时:
They had out-won their opponents in every previous match. = 在之前的每一场比赛中，他们都战胜了对手。
Before the final race, she had out-won all her competitors. = 在决赛之前，她已经战胜了所有竞争对手。


将来时:
We expect to out-win our competitors in the upcoming election. = 我们预计在即将到来的选举中会战胜我们的竞争对手。
With this new strategy, we believe we will out-win the market. = 通过这个新战略，我们相信我们会赢得市场。


虚拟语气:
If we had trained harder, we might have out-won them. = 如果我们训练更努力，我们可能会战胜他们。
If only I had prepared better, I would have out-won my opponent. = 如果我准备得更好，我就会战胜我的对手。


被动语态:
The smaller company was out-won by the larger corporation. = 这家小公司被更大的公司击败了。
Their proposal was out-won by a more innovative one. = 他们的提案被一个更具创新性的提案击败了。


疑问句:
Will our team out-win the championship this year? = 今年我们的团队会赢得冠军吗？
Did they out-win their rivals in the last game? = 他们在上一场比赛中战胜了他们的对手吗？


否定句:
We didn't out-win them in the competition. = 我们在比赛中没有战胜他们。
They failed to out-win their competitors in the sales race. = 他们在销售竞争中未能战胜他们的竞争对手。


PHRASE->
out-win the competition = 战胜竞争
out-win the market = 赢得市场
out-win the odds = 克服困难取得胜利
out-win expectations = 超出预期
out-win the game = 赢得比赛
