sickest:/ˈsɪkɪst/| adj.|最恶心的；最严重的；最酷的|The most extreme or intense example of something, often used informally and can have both positive and negative connotations.  In a positive sense, it means "the best," "the most awesome,"  while in a negative sense, it means "the worst," "the most disgusting," or "the most ill".|例句：That's the sickest skateboard trick I've ever seen! = 那是我看过最酷的滑板技巧！
例句：The smell in that room was sickest. = 那个房间里的气味太恶心了。|近义词:(positive) awesome, amazing, incredible; (negative) disgusting, awful, terrible|反义词:(positive) terrible, awful; (negative) amazing, awesome
