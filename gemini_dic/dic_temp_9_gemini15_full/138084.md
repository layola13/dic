normals:/ˈnɔːrməlz/ | n. | 规范；常态；正态分布；平均水平 |  Normals refer to what is typical, usual, or expected.  It can refer to a standard or average, or to a statistical distribution.  | 例句：The temperature is back to normal after the storm.暴风雨过后，气温恢复正常了。| 例句：The doctor said my blood pressure is within the normal range.医生说我的血压在正常范围内。| 近义词：averages, standards, means, norms | 反义词：abnormalities, exceptions, anomalies | 名词
