feudatary:/ˈfjuːdəˌtɛri/ | n. | 封臣；受封者 | A person who holds land in fealty to a lord; a vassal.  | Example:  The king's feudatary swore allegiance to him. 国王的封臣向他宣誓效忠。 | 近义词：vassal, retainer | 反义词：lord, sovereign | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a feudatary of the Duke. = 他是公爵的封臣。
The feudatary pays homage to his lord. = 封臣向他的领主致敬。


一般过去时:
He was a powerful feudatary in the kingdom. = 他是王国里一位强大的封臣。
The feudatary served his lord faithfully. = 封臣忠实地侍奉他的领主。


现在完成时:
The feudatary has sworn allegiance to the new king. = 该封臣已向新国王宣誓效忠。
Many feudatories have benefited from the king's generosity. = 许多封臣都受益于国王的慷慨。


过去完成时:
The feudatary had already pledged his support before the battle. = 在战斗开始之前，这位封臣就已经承诺支持。
Before the rebellion, the feudatary had been loyal to the crown. = 在叛乱之前，这位封臣一直忠于王室。


将来时:
He will be a powerful feudatary in the years to come. = 在未来几年里，他将成为一位强大的封臣。
The new feudatary will owe allegiance to the count. = 新的封臣将效忠于伯爵。


虚拟语气:
If he were a feudatary, he would have more land. = 如果他是封臣，他就会拥有更多的土地。
I wish I were a feudatary and could live in a castle. = 我希望我是一个封臣，可以住在城堡里。


被动语态:
The land was held by a feudatary. = 这块土地由一个封臣持有。
The feudatary's loyalty was questioned by the king. = 国王怀疑这位封臣的忠诚。


疑问句:
Was he a feudatary of the crown? = 他是王室的封臣吗？
Who are the feudatories of this region? = 这个地区的封臣是谁？


否定句:
He was not a feudatary, but a freeholder. = 他不是封臣，而是一个自由民。
She was not a feudatary of the local lord. = 她不是当地领主的封臣。

词源:
From Old French *feudataire, from feud, fief ("fief, feudal estate") + -aire ("-ary").  The ultimate origin is from the Germanic word *fehu, meaning "cattle, wealth, property," reflecting the early connection between land ownership and wealth in feudal societies.
