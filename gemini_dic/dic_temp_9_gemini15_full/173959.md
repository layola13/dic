quasi-soberly:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈsoʊbərli/| adv.|似是而非地清醒地；假装清醒地| appearing sober but not truly so; in a way that suggests sobriety but is actually deceptive or only partially genuine. |例句：He nodded quasi-soberly, his eyes betraying a hint of mischief. 他似是而非地清醒地点点头，眼神中却流露出几分调皮。

例句：She listened to the somber news quasi-soberly, a flicker of anxiety betraying her composure. 她似是而非地冷静地听着这个令人悲伤的消息，一丝焦虑出卖了她的镇定。

近义词：seemingly soberly, soberly but deceptively, with a pretense of sobriety

反义词：obviously drunk, openly intoxicated, blatantly inebriated

词源:
