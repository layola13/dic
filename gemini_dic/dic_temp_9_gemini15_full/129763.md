non-expansion:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspænʃən/| adj.|不扩张；非膨胀；无膨胀|Not expanding or tending to expand.  The term often describes a situation where something does not increase in size, scope, or influence. |例句：The non-expansion of the universe is a theory that contradicts the prevailing cosmological model.  宇宙不膨胀理论与现行的宇宙模型相矛盾。|近义词：constriction, contraction, shrinkage|反义词：expansion, growth, enlargement|形容词
