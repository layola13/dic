decorated:/ˈdekəreɪtɪd/| adj.|装饰过的，装修过的|Adorned with decorations; embellished.  It implies the addition of something aesthetically pleasing or visually interesting.|The Christmas tree was beautifully decorated with ornaments and lights. 圣诞树装饰得很漂亮，挂满了饰品和彩灯。|The newly decorated apartment felt bright and airy. 新装修的公寓感觉明亮通风。|ornate, embellished, adorned|plain, undecorated, bare|形容词

USAGE->
