hydroponicists:/ˌhaɪdrəˈpɒnɪsɪsts/ | n. | 水耕栽培专家 | Experts in the field of hydroponics, specializing in soilless plant cultivation techniques. They design, manage, and optimize hydroponic systems for efficient and high-yield plant growth. |  Dr. Lee is one of the leading hydroponicists in the country, renowned for her innovative techniques. 李博士是国内领先的水耕栽培专家之一，以其创新技术而闻名。  The team of hydroponicists successfully increased lettuce yields by 40% using a new nutrient solution. 水耕栽培专家团队使用新的营养液成功地将莴苣产量提高了40%。 |  hydroponic experts, soilless cultivation specialists |  (no direct antonym, but the opposite would be someone specializing in traditional soil-based agriculture)
