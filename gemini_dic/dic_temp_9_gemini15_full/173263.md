quasi-conservatively:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ kənˈsɜːrvətɪvli/ | adv. | 准保守地，近乎保守地 |  This adverb describes actions or policies that are largely conservative but with some deviations or qualifications. It suggests a cautious approach that leans towards traditional methods but isn't strictly adherent to them. |  The government approached the economic reforms quasi-conservatively, implementing some market-oriented changes while maintaining significant state control. 政府以准保守的方式处理经济改革，在保持国家对经济的重大控制的同时，实施了一些市场导向的变革。| 近义词：conservatively (but with modifications), cautiously, moderately | 反义词：radically, aggressively, liberally
