silicosis:/ˌsɪlɪˈkoʊsɪs/| n. |矽肺病 |A serious lung disease caused by inhaling silica dust.  Silicosis is a progressive disease, meaning it gets worse over time.  There is no cure, but treatment can help manage symptoms. |例句：Long-term exposure to silica dust in the mining industry can lead to silicosis. 长期接触矿业中的硅尘会导致矽肺病。

例句：He developed silicosis after years of working in a sandstone quarry. 他在采石场工作多年后患上了矽肺病。

近义词：pneumoconiosis (a general term for lung diseases caused by dust inhalation)

反义词：None (It's a disease, not a state that has a direct opposite)
