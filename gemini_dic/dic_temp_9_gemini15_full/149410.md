packed-up:/pækt ˈʌp/| v. |打包完毕；收拾好；|To have finished packing something into bags or containers.  It can also imply a completed process of preparation for departure or storage.|例句：I've packed up all my clothes for the trip. 我已经把所有衣服都打包好了准备旅行。

例句：She packed up her desk before leaving the office. 她在离开办公室之前收拾好了她的办公桌。


近义词：packed, bundled, prepared, ready

反义词： unpacked, scattered, disordered


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He packs up his lunch every day. = 他每天都打包午餐。
They pack up their belongings before moving. = 他们在搬家之前打包行李。

一般过去时:
We packed up the tent after the camping trip. = 野营结束后，我们收拾了帐篷。
She packed up her old toys and donated them. = 她打包了她旧的玩具并捐赠了它们。

现在完成时:
I have packed up all the Christmas decorations. = 我已经把所有的圣诞装饰都收起来了。
They have packed up and are ready to leave. = 他们已经打包完毕，准备出发了。

过去完成时:
They had packed up their bags before the announcement. = 他们在公告发布之前就已经打包好了行李。
We had packed up the car before the storm hit. =暴风雨来临之前，我们已经把车收拾好了。

将来时:
We will pack up and leave early tomorrow morning. = 我们明天一大早就会打包离开。
She will pack up her things after the meeting. = 会议结束后，她会收拾她的东西。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pack up all my books. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把我的所有书都打包好。
I wish I had packed up my summer clothes earlier. = 我真希望我早点把夏天的衣服收起来。

被动语态:
The boxes were packed up by the movers. = 箱子是由搬家公司打包的。
The luggage was packed up and sent to the airport. = 行李打包完毕并送往机场。

疑问句:
Have you packed up your things yet? = 你把东西收拾好了吗？
Did they pack up the equipment properly? = 他们是否正确地打包了设备？

否定句:
He hasn't packed up his suitcase yet. = 他还没打包好他的行李箱。
She didn't pack up her belongings before the fire. = 火灾发生前她没有收拾好她的东西。
