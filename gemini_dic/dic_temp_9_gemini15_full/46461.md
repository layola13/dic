dahlias:/ˈdeɪliəz/| n.|大丽花|Dahlias are a genus of bushy, tuberous, herbaceous perennial plants native mainly to Mexico. They are grown for their showy flowers, which come in a wide variety of colors, shapes, and sizes.|例句：The garden was ablaze with color, thanks to the many dahlias in bloom. 花园里鲜花盛开，大丽花功不可没。|近义词：flower, bloom|反义词:无
