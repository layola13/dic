deejays:/ˈdiːdʒeɪz/| n. |迪斯科舞曲唱片骑师；DJ|Disc jockeys, often shortened to DJs, are people who play music, typically recorded music, for a live audience. They may also announce songs, introduce artists, or interact with the audience.  |例句：The deejays at the club kept the energy high all night.  俱乐部的DJ们整晚都保持着高昂的活力。

例句：Many aspiring deejays start by practicing at home before performing in clubs. 许多有抱负的DJ会在俱乐部演出之前在家练习。
