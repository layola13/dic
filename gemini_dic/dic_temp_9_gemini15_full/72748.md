fogeyish: /ˈfoʊdʒiʃ/ | adj. | 老朽的；守旧的；过时的 |  Slightly old-fashioned or out of date in one's ideas or behavior; exhibiting the characteristics of a fogey.  | 例句：His fogeyish views on women's rights were quite shocking. 他对妇女权利的守旧观点令人震惊。

His fogeyish attire made him stand out from the crowd.  他过时的穿着使他显得与众不同。


近义词：old-fashioned, outdated, antiquated, reactionary, conservative

反义词：modern, progressive, forward-thinking, avant-garde

词性: 形容词
