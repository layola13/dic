paperweight: /ˈpeɪpərweɪt/ | n. |镇纸|A heavy object used to hold down papers on a desk or table to prevent them from being blown away or scattered.  It is often decorative. |例句：I have a beautiful crystal paperweight on my desk. 我书桌上放着一个漂亮的水晶镇纸。

例句：He used a heavy book as a makeshift paperweight. 他用一本厚书当临时镇纸。


近义词：deskweight, paper holder


反义词：None (no direct antonym)

词性:名词
