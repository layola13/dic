reemploying:/ˌriːɪmˈplɔɪɪŋ/| v. |重新雇用，再次雇用 |The act of employing someone again after a period of unemployment or termination.  It implies a previous employment relationship. |例句：The company is considering reemploying some of the laid-off workers. 公司正在考虑重新雇用一些被解雇的工人。
The factory is reemploying skilled machinists after upgrading its equipment. 工厂在升级设备后正在重新雇用熟练的机械师。|近义词：rehiring, reengaging|反义词：firing, dismissing, laying off|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reemploys many former employees. = 公司重新雇用许多以前的员工。
They reemploy seasonal workers every spring. = 他们每年春天都重新雇用季节性工人。

一般过去时:
The factory reemployed all its workers after the strike. = 罢工后工厂重新雇用了所有工人。
She was reemployed by her former boss after a year of unemployment. = 失业一年后，她被以前的老板重新雇用了。

现在完成时:
The company has reemployed over 100 workers this year. = 今年公司已经重新雇用了100多名工人。
They have reemployed many skilled technicians since the expansion. = 自从扩张以来，他们已经重新雇用了许多熟练的技术人员。

过去完成时:
Before the project restarted, they had reemployed several key engineers. = 项目重启之前，他们已经重新雇用了几位关键工程师。
The government had reemployed many veterans by the end of the war. = 到战争结束时，政府已经重新雇用了许多退伍军人。

将来时:
The company will reemploy its staff once the merger is complete. = 合并完成后，公司将重新雇用其员工。
We will reemploy you if your performance improves. = 如果你的表现有所改善，我们将重新雇用你。

虚拟语气:
If the economy improved, the company would reemploy more workers. = 如果经济好转，公司将会重新雇用更多工人。
I wish they would reemploy me; I miss the work. = 我希望他们能重新雇用我；我怀念那份工作。

被动语态:
Many former employees were reemployed by the company. = 许多前任员工被公司重新雇用。
The workers are being reemployed gradually. = 工人们正在逐步被重新雇用。

疑问句:
Will the company be reemploying anyone soon? = 公司很快会重新雇用任何人吗？
Were those employees reemployed after the restructuring? = 重组后那些员工被重新雇用了吗？

否定句:
The company did not reemploy the manager after the scandal. = 丑闻后，公司没有重新雇用那位经理。
They will not reemploy anyone who violated company policy. = 他们不会重新雇用任何违反公司政策的人。
