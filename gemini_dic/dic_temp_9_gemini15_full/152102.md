pathmaker:/ˈpæθˌmeɪkər/| n. |开拓者，先驱，创建者 |A pathmaker is a person who creates or establishes a new path, direction, or way of doing things, often in a field or industry. They are innovators and pioneers who forge new trails for others to follow.  |Example:  Marie Curie was a pathmaker in the field of radioactivity.居里夫人是放射性领域的开拓者。

The company's CEO is a true pathmaker, leading the industry into uncharted territory.  这家公司的首席执行官是一位真正的开拓者，带领着行业进入未知领域。|近义词：pioneer, innovator, trailblazer, leader, visionary |反义词：follower, imitator
