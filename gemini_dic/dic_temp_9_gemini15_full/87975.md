high-hatted:/ˈhaɪˌhætɪd/| adj.| 戴高帽的；自命不凡的；傲慢的 | Wearing a high hat; affectedly superior or arrogant.  The word describes someone who acts superior or snobbish, often implying a pretentiousness associated with the wearing of a top hat. |例句：He walked into the room with a high-hatted air, ignoring everyone. 他趾高气扬地走进房间，无视所有人。

例句：The high-hatted critic dismissed the young artist's work as amateurish.  这位自以为是的评论家轻蔑地将这位年轻艺术家的作品斥为业余作品。


近义词：supercilious, arrogant, snobbish, condescending, haughty, pretentious


反义词：humble, modest, unassuming, down-to-earth


词性：形容词
