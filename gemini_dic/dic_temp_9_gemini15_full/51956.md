diachronicness: /ˌdaɪəˈkrɒnɪknəs/ | n. |历时性，历时性研究| The quality or state of being diachronic;  the study of something, especially language, through time, showing its development and evolution.  It contrasts with synchronic study, which focuses on a single point in time. |例句：The diachronicness of the language is evident in the changes in pronunciation over the centuries.  语言的历时性体现在几个世纪以来发音的变化中。

The study of the diachronicness of social structures reveals fascinating shifts in power dynamics. 对社会结构历时性的研究揭示了权力动态中引人入胜的变化。|近义词：historical development, evolution, temporal perspective |反义词：synchronicity, simultaneity
