slinked:/slɪŋkt/| v.|偷偷地走，溜走|To move smoothly and quietly, especially in a sneaky or furtive manner.  It often implies a sense of shame, guilt, or stealth.|例句：The cat slinked through the tall grass. 猫偷偷地穿过高高的草丛。

例句：He slinked away after he was caught stealing. 他被抓到偷东西后偷偷溜走了。


近义词：crept, glided, slipped, slithered|反义词：strutted, swaggered, marched
