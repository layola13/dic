maltreat:/ˌmælˈtriːt/| vt.|虐待；粗暴对待|To treat (a person or animal) roughly or cruelly.  It implies causing physical or emotional harm or suffering.|例句：He was accused of maltreating his children. 他被指控虐待他的孩子。|例句：The animals were clearly being maltreated in the factory farm.  这些动物显然在工厂化农场里受到了虐待。|近义词：abuse, ill-treat, mistreat, brutalize|反义词：cherish, care for, nurture
