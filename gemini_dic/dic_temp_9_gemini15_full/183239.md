repressionist: /rɪˈprɛʃənɪst/ | n. |压制者；镇压者 | A person who practices or supports repression, especially political repression.  Someone who advocates for or engages in the suppression of dissent, freedom of expression, or other basic human rights.  They often use force, censorship, or intimidation to maintain control. |例句：The regime was criticized for its brutal repressionist tactics.  这个政权因其残酷的压制手段而受到批评。

The government's actions were condemned by many as repressionist. 许多人谴责政府的行为是压制性的。| 近义词：suppressor, authoritarian, dictator, tyrant | 反义词：liberal, democrat, freedom fighter, activist |
