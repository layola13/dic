perceptive: /pərˈsɛptɪv/ | adj. | 有洞察力的；感知敏锐的 | having or showing sensitive insight.  |例句：He was a perceptive young man, quick to understand the complexities of human relationships. 他是一个有洞察力的年轻人，很快就理解了人际关系的复杂性。

例句：Her perceptive comments revealed a deep understanding of the situation. 她敏锐的评论揭示了她对局势的深刻理解。


近义词： insightful, discerning, observant, sensitive, intuitive | 反义词： obtuse, insensitive, unobservant, unaware


词源:  From Latin *percipere "to perceive," from per- "completely" + capere "to take, seize."  The word's evolution reflects its core meaning:  a complete taking in or understanding of something.
