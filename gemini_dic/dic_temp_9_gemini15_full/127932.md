neonates:/ˈniːoʊneɪts/| n.|新生儿|A neonate is a newborn infant, typically up to four weeks old.  The term often refers to the period immediately following birth and encompasses the critical adjustment to extrauterine life.|Example: The neonates in the intensive care unit required specialized attention.|近义词：newborn, infant, baby|反义词：adult
