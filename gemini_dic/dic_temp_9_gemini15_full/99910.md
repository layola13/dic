internationalizations:/ˌɪntərˌnæʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 国际化（复数） | The processes of making something international or available in multiple languages and cultures.  It often involves adapting products, services, or content to meet the needs and preferences of different markets. | Example:  The internationalizations of the software involved translating the user interface into numerous languages and adapting the features for various cultural contexts.  国际化软件的过程包括将用户界面翻译成多种语言以及根据不同的文化背景调整功能。 | 近义词：globalizations, worldwide adaptations | 反义词：localization (in the sense of focusing on a single market)
