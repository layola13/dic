It implies a firm stance and refusal to give in. | Example:  His nonconcessionary attitude led to a breakdown in negotiations.  他的不让步态度导致谈判破裂。 | 近义词：unyielding, inflexible, uncompromising, adamant | 反义词：concessionary, compromising, yielding, flexible
