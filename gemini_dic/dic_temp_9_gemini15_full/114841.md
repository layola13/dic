mammas:/ˈmɑːmɑːz/| n.|妈妈们，妈咪们|The plural form of "mamma," a term of endearment for mother.  It can also be used more generally to refer to mothers collectively.|Example: The mammas were chatting about their children at the park. 妈妈们在公园里聊着她们的孩子。|近义词：mothers, moms, mummies|反义词：None


USAGE->
This word is a noun and does not conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, grammatical tenses are not applicable.  The usage depends on the context and the intended meaning, typically conveying a sense of plurality or collective reference to mothers.

Example 1: The mammas brought their children to the playground. 妈妈们带孩子们去了游乐场。
Example 2:  All the mammas agreed that naptime was essential. 所有的妈妈们都同意午睡很重要。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases specifically using "mammas".  The word is primarily used as a simple plural noun.  Phrases involving "mama" or "mommy" might be considered analogous, but they are different words.
