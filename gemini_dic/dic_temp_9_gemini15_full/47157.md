dawdles:/ˈdɔːdəlz/| v.|磨蹭；虚度光阴|To waste time; to be slow in doing something.|例句：He dawdled over his breakfast, completely ignoring the time.|他磨磨蹭蹭地吃早餐，完全不理会时间。
例句：Don't dawdle, we're going to be late!|别磨蹭了，我们要迟到了！|近义词：procrastinate, delay, linger, tarry|反义词：hurry, rush, hasten|动词
