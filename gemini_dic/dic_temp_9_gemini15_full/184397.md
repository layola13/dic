responds:/rɪˈspɑːndz/| v. |回应；反应；响应 |To answer or react to something that has been said or done. |例句：He responds quickly to emails. 他很快回复邮件。|例句：The government responded to the crisis by increasing taxes. 政府通过增税来应对危机。|近义词：answer, reply, react, react to |反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He responds to my questions patiently. = 他耐心地回答我的问题。
She responds well to treatment. = 她对治疗反应良好。

一般过去时:
He responded promptly to the invitation. = 他迅速回应了邀请。
She responded angrily to his accusations. = 她愤怒地回应了他的指控。

现在完成时:
He has responded to all the emails. = 他已经回复了所有邮件。
She has responded positively to the new medication. = 她对新药的反应积极。

过去完成时:
He had already responded before I could ask. = 我还没来得及问，他已经回复了。
She had responded with a smile before he finished speaking. = 他还没说完话，她就笑着回应了。

将来时:
He will respond to your query soon. = 他会很快回复你的询问。
She will respond to the emergency call immediately. = 她会立即回应紧急呼叫。

虚拟语气:
If he responded quickly, we could finish on time. = 如果他快速回应，我们就能按时完成。
I wish she had responded to my letter. = 我希望她能回复我的信。

被动语态:
His email was responded to promptly. = 他的邮件得到了及时的回复。
The proposal was responded to with enthusiasm. = 该提案受到了热情的回应。

疑问句:
How did she respond to your proposal? = 她对你的提议有何回应？
Will he respond to the criticism? = 他会回应批评吗？

否定句:
He didn't respond to my calls. = 他没有回应我的电话。
She hasn't responded to my email yet. = 她还没回复我的邮件。


PHRASE->
respond to = 回应
respond in kind = 以牙还牙
respond positively = 积极回应
respond negatively = 消极回应
respond appropriately = 恰当回应
