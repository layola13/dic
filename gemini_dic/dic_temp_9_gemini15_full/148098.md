overse:/ˌoʊvərˈsiː/ |/ˌəʊvəˈsiː/| adv.|在国外；在海外；|across or to the other side of;beyond;on the further side of.|例句：He went overseas to study. 他去海外留学了。例句：She works for a company with offices overseas. 她在一家在海外设有办事处的公司工作。|近义词：abroad, internationally, foreign|反义词：domestically, locally, inland


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They oversee the project. = 他们负责监督这个项目。
She oversees the marketing department. = 她负责监督市场部。

一般过去时:
He oversaw the construction of the bridge. = 他监督了这座桥的建设。
They oversaw the implementation of the new policy. = 他们监督了新政策的实施。


现在完成时:
We have overseen many successful projects. = 我们已经监督了许多成功的项目。
She has overseen the department for ten years. = 她已经监督这个部门十年了。


过去完成时:
They had overseen the project before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经监督了这个项目。
He had overseen several similar projects in the past. = 他过去曾监督过几个类似的项目。


将来时:
She will oversee the merger of the two companies. = 她将监督两家公司的合并。
We will oversee the production process carefully. = 我们将仔细监督生产过程。


虚拟语气:
If I were in charge, I would oversee the operation more effectively. = 如果我负责，我会更有效地监督这项业务。
It would be better if someone more experienced oversaw the project. = 如果由更有经验的人来监督这个项目会更好。


被动语态:
The project was overseen by a team of experts. = 这个项目由一个专家团队负责监督。
The construction was overseen by a qualified engineer. = 建设工作由一位合格的工程师负责监督。


疑问句:
Who oversaw the investigation? = 谁负责监督调查？
Who will oversee the project next year? = 明年谁将负责监督这个项目？


否定句:
He did not oversee the project personally. = 他没有亲自监督这个项目。
The company did not oversee the safety regulations properly. = 公司没有正确监督安全规章。
