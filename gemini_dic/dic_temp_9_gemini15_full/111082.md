listless:/ˈlɪstlɪs/| adj.|倦怠的；懒散的；无精打采的|Lacking energy and enthusiasm.  Showing little or no interest in anything.|例句：She felt listless and unable to concentrate. 她感到倦怠无力集中注意力。

例句：He lay listlessly on the sofa, staring at the ceiling. 他无精打采地躺在沙发上，盯着天花板。


近义词：apathetic, lethargic, languid, indifferent, unenthusiastic|反义词：energetic, enthusiastic, vigorous, lively, spirited|词性:形容词
