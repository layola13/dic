declarant:/dɪˈklɛərənt/| n. |陈述者；声明人；申报人 |A declarant is a person who makes a formal statement, especially one made under oath in a legal context.  It can refer to someone who declares something, such as their income or goods for customs. |例句：The declarant testified in court about what they saw. 陈述人在法庭上作证他们所看到的事情。  The declarant's statement was crucial to the investigation. 陈述人的陈述对调查至关重要。|近义词：witness, informant, affiant |反义词：defendant (in some contexts) |名词
