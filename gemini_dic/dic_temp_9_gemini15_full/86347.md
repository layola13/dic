hectares:/ˈhɛkteərz/| n. |公顷|A hectare is a unit of area equal to 10,000 square meters (approximately 2.47 acres).  It's commonly used in land measurement, particularly in agriculture and real estate.|The farm covers 100 hectares.  这个农场占地100公顷。|The forest reserve spans many hectares.  森林保护区占地很多公顷。|acres, square meters|square kilometers|名词
