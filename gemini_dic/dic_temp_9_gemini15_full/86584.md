heliocentricity:/ˌhiːliəʊˈsɛntrɪsɪti/| n. |日心说|The astronomical model in which the Earth and planets revolve around the Sun at the center of the Solar System.  It is the belief that the Sun is the center of the universe.|The acceptance of heliocentricity marked a paradigm shift in astronomy.|近义词：solar centrism|反义词：geocentrism
