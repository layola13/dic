eulogistically:/ˌjuːləˈdʒɪstɪkli/| adj.|赞美地；颂扬地；溢美之词地 |  Speaking or writing in a way that praises highly.  It often implies excessive praise or flattery. | 例句：The deceased was eulogistically described as a visionary leader.  已故者被赞美地描述为一位有远见的领导者。

The obituary eulogistically recounted his many achievements.  讣告溢美之词地叙述了他的许多成就。


近义词：praisingly, commendatorily, flatteringly, laudatorily

反义词：critically, disparagingly, negatively, condemningly

词性: adjective
