exculpates:/ˈɛksʌlpeɪts/| vt.| 开脱；使免罪；证明…无罪 | To clear from blame or guilt; to vindicate.  |例句：The new evidence exculpates the suspect. 新证据证明了嫌疑人的清白。
例句：The lawyer worked tirelessly to exculpate his client. 律师不遗余力地为他的当事人脱罪。| 近义词：absolve, exonerate, vindicate | 反义词：incriminate, condemn, convict | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence exculpates him. = 证据证明了他的清白。
She exculpates her friend from any wrongdoing. = 她为她的朋友开脱任何不当行为。


一般过去时:
The judge exculpated the defendant. = 法官宣告被告无罪。
The witness testimony exculpated the suspect. = 目击证人的证词为嫌疑人洗脱了嫌疑。


现在完成时:
The investigation has exculpated the police officer. = 调查已经证明了警官的清白。
The court has exculpated her from all charges. = 法院已将她免除所有指控。


过去完成时:
The lawyer had exculpated his client before the trial. = 律师在审判前已经为他的当事人脱罪。
The evidence presented had exculpated the accused. = 提交的证据已经为被告洗脱了罪名。


将来时:
The results of the test will exculpate him. = 测试结果将证明他的清白。
We believe that the upcoming investigation will exculpate her. = 我们相信即将进行的调查将为她洗脱罪名。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence were presented properly, it would exculpate him. = 如果证据能被恰当地呈现，它就能证明他的清白。
If I had more time, I could exculpate myself. = 如果我有更多的时间，我就能为自己开脱。


被动语态:
He was exculpated by the jury. = 他被陪审团宣告无罪。
She was exculpated after a long and arduous trial. = 经过漫长而艰苦的审判后，她被宣告无罪。


疑问句:
Does this evidence exculpate the accused? = 这证据能为被告开脱吗？
Will the new findings exculpate the suspect? = 新的发现会为嫌疑人洗脱罪名吗？


否定句:
The evidence does not exculpate him. = 证据不能证明他的清白。
Nothing could exculpate him from the crime. = 没有什么能让他免于犯罪的指控。


PHRASE->
exculpate oneself = 为自己开脱
exculpate someone from blame = 为某人开脱罪责
fully exculpate = 完全开脱
be exculpated from = 免于...的指责
exculpate a crime = 为罪行开脱
