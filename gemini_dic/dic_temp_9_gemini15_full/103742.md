jotted:/ˈdʒɒtɪd/ | v. |匆匆记下，草草写下 |To write something quickly and briefly. |例句：I jotted down the address on a scrap of paper. 我在一张纸片上匆匆记下了地址。|近义词：scribbled, scrawled, noted |反义词：elaborated, detailed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I jot down important information. = 我记下重要的信息。
He jots down ideas as they come to him. = 他随时记下想到的点子。


一般过去时:
She jotted down the main points of the meeting. = 她记下了会议的主要内容。
They jotted a quick note to each other. = 他们互相匆匆写了个便条。


现在完成时:
I have jotted down everything I needed to remember. = 我已经记下了一切我需要记住的东西。
She has jotted down several ideas for her next project. = 她为她的下一个项目记下了几个想法。


过去完成时:
He had jotted down the phone number before he lost his phone. = 他在丢手机之前记下了电话号码。
They had jotted down the directions before they set off. = 他们在出发前记下了路线。


将来时:
I will jot down the recipe before I forget it. = 我会在忘记之前记下食谱。
She will jot down the address on a napkin. = 她会在餐巾纸上记下地址。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would jot down the details. = 如果我有时间，我会记下细节。
I wish I had jotted down the speaker's name. = 我真希望我记下了演讲者的名字。


被动语态:
The important information was jotted down. = 重要的信息被记下了。
The address was jotted down on a small piece of paper. = 地址被记在一小张纸上。


疑问句:
Did you jot down the instructions? = 你记下说明了吗？
Have you jotted down the phone number yet? = 你已经记下电话号码了吗？


否定句:
I didn't jot down the details. = 我没有记下细节。
He didn't jot down her email address. = 他没有记下她的电子邮件地址。
