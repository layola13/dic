dethrone: /ˌdiːˈθroʊn/ | vt. |废黜；使...失去王位；打败；使…失去权威 | To remove a person from a position of power, especially a king or queen from their throne.  |例句：The rebels dethroned the king and established a republic. 反叛者废黜了国王，建立了共和国。

The coup dethroned the dictator. 政变推翻了独裁者。


近义词：oust, displace, unseat, overthrow, supplant | 反义词：enthrone, install, appoint
