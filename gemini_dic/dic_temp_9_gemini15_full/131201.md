nonburnable:/ˌnɒnˈbɜːrnəbl/|/ˌnɑːnˈbɜːrnəbl/| adj. |不易燃烧的；耐火的|Not easily burned or capable of being burned.  This adjective describes materials that resist combustion or burning.  |例句：The nonburnable trash should be disposed of separately.  不易燃烧的垃圾应单独处理。

例句：Many modern buildings use nonburnable materials in their construction for fire safety. 许多现代建筑在施工中使用不易燃烧的材料以确保消防安全。


近义词：fireproof, fire-resistant, incombustible, refractory|反义词：flammable, combustible, inflammable|词性：形容词
