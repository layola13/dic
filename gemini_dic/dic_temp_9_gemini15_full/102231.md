itemise: /ˈaɪtəmaɪz/ | vt. |列举；逐条列明 | To list or specify individually.  |  The report itemised all the expenses incurred during the project.  报告逐条列明了项目期间产生的所有费用。 | enumerate, detail, specify | combine, summarize | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He itemises his expenses each month. = 他每月都逐条列出他的开支。
She itemises the ingredients in her recipe. = 她逐条列出了食谱中的配料。

一般过去时:
The accountant itemised the company's assets. = 会计列举了公司的资产。
They itemised the damage to their car after the accident. = 事故后，他们逐条列出了汽车的损坏情况。

现在完成时:
He has itemised all the potential risks. = 他已经列出了所有潜在的风险。
She has itemised the points for discussion in the meeting. = 她已经列出了会议上要讨论的要点。

过去完成时:
The lawyer had itemised the evidence before presenting it in court. = 律师在法庭上出示证据之前已将其逐条列明。
They had itemised the costs before submitting the proposal. = 他们在提交提案之前已经列出了成本。

将来时:
She will itemise the contributions made by each team member. = 她将列出每个团队成员的贡献。
He will itemise the tasks to be completed this week. = 他将列出本周要完成的任务。

虚拟语气:
If I were to itemise my reasons, it would take hours. = 如果我要逐条列出我的理由，那将需要数小时。
I wish I had itemised my receipts more carefully. = 我真希望我当时更仔细地列出我的收据。

被动语态:
The expenses were itemised in the financial report. = 财务报告中逐条列明了各项开支。
The tasks to be completed were itemised in the project plan. = 项目计划中列出了要完成的任务。

疑问句:
Did he itemise the charges on the bill? = 他在账单上逐条列明了费用吗？
Have you itemised all the necessary equipment? = 你是否列出了所有必要的设备？

否定句:
The report did not itemise the individual contributions. = 报告没有列出个人的贡献。
She didn't itemise the details of her travel expenses. = 她没有详细列出她的差旅费。


PHRASE->
itemise the details = 详细列举
itemise the costs = 列出成本
itemise the benefits = 列出好处
itemise the points = 列出要点
itemise the reasons = 列举原因
