e-ticket:/ˈiːtɪkɪt/| n. |电子客票，电子票 |An e-ticket is an electronic ticket, usually sent via email, that serves as proof of purchase for travel, events, or other services.  It replaces the traditional paper ticket. | Example: I received my e-ticket for the concert in my inbox. 我在我的收件箱里收到了音乐会的电子票。

I printed out my e-ticket before heading to the airport. 我在去机场之前打印了我的电子客票。|近义词：electronic ticket, digital ticket |反义词：paper ticket, physical ticket |名词
