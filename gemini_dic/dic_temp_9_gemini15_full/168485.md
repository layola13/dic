probes:/proʊbz/| n. v.|探针；探测；探究|To examine or investigate thoroughly; a slender instrument used to explore a cavity or wound.|例句：Scientists used probes to study the planet's atmosphere. 科学家们用探测器研究了这颗行星的大气层。
例句：The doctor used a probe to examine the patient's wound. 医生用探针检查了病人的伤口。|近义词：investigate, explore, examine, test|反义词：ignore, neglect|名词，动词
