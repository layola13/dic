microbe:/ˈmaɪkroʊb/ | n. |微生物 |A microscopic organism, especially a bacterium; a germ.  |例句：Many microbes are beneficial to human health, aiding in digestion and other bodily functions.  许多微生物有益于人体健康，有助于消化和其他身体机能。|例句：The scientist studied the microbes found in the soil sample. 科学家研究了土壤样本中发现的微生物。|近义词：germ, bacterium, virus, organism |反义词：macroscopic organism
