computable:/kəmˈpjutəbl/ | adj. | 可计算的，可计算的 |  capable of being computed; able to be calculated or determined by a computational process.  It implies that there exists an algorithm or procedure that can produce the result in a finite number of steps. |  The area of the circle is computable using the formula πr².  圆形的面积可以使用公式πr²计算。  The solution to this equation is computable. 这个方程的解是可计算的。| calculable, determinable | uncomputable, undecidable |
