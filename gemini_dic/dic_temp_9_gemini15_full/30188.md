celestially:/səˈlɛstriəli/| adv.| 天上地;天体地;神圣地 | In a celestial manner; relating to the heavens or the sky.  It suggests a divine or heavenly quality, often implying a majestic or awe-inspiring nature. | 例句：The stars shone celestially above the silent landscape.群星在天空中闪耀，照亮了寂静的景色。

例句：The choir sang celestially, their voices blending in perfect harmony. 唱诗班的天籁之音，他们的声音完美融合。
