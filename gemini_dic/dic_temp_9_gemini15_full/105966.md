klutziness:/ˈklʌtsɪnəs/| n. |笨拙；笨手笨脚；愚笨|The state or quality of being clumsy or awkward; lack of coordination or grace.  It often implies a lack of dexterity and grace in movement or action, sometimes extending to a lack of social grace.|例句：Her klutziness was evident as she tripped over the rug.  她的笨拙在她绊倒地毯上显而易见。

例句：The klutziness of his attempt to fix the computer only made things worse. 他笨手笨脚地试图修理电脑，结果只会让事情变得更糟。

近义词：awkwardness, clumsiness, gaucheness, maladroitness

反义词：gracefulness, dexterity, elegance, skill


词源:源于德语单词“klutz”，意思是“笨蛋”或“笨拙的人”。  This word then entered Yiddish and eventually made its way into English.
