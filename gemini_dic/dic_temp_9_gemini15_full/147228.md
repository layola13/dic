overmeddled:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛdəld/| v.| 过度干涉；多管闲事 | To interfere excessively or in an unwelcome way. |例句：He overmeddled in his neighbor's affairs, causing unnecessary conflict. 他过度干涉邻居的事务，导致不必要的冲突。|近义词：interfered, butted in, meddled excessively |反义词：ignored, left alone, minded one's own business
