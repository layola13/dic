pitahaya:/ˌpɪtəˈhaɪə/| n. |火龙果|A pitahaya is a fruit of several cactus species.  It is also known as dragon fruit.  The fruit has a vibrant pink or yellow skin and a white or red flesh with small, black seeds.  It is sweet and slightly tart, and is often eaten fresh or used in desserts and drinks.|The pitahaya we bought at the market was incredibly sweet.|dragon fruit, night-blooming cereus fruit|  (no direct antonym)|名词
