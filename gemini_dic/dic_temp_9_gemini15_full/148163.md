overserve:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrv/| vt.|过度服务；供应过多|To serve or supply more than is needed or wanted.  This often implies excessive or unwanted attention or service. |例句：The waiter overserved the customer, leading to an intoxicated state.  服务员过度为顾客提供酒水，导致顾客醉酒。


例句：The restaurant overserved us with appetizers; we couldn't finish them all. 餐厅给我们上了太多的开胃菜；我们吃不完。


近义词：overcater, oversupply, ply


反义词：underserve, neglect
