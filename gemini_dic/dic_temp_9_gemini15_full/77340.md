gaucho:/ˈɡaʊtʃoʊ/| n. |牛仔，牧人|A gaucho is a horseman, typically a cattle herder, of the South American pampas.  They are known for their distinctive skills in riding and their unique culture. |The gaucho expertly guided his horse across the rugged terrain.  这位牛仔熟练地引导他的马穿越崎岖的地形。|cowboy, vaquero |(无明显反义词)|名词
