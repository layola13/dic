imaged:/ˈɪmɪdʒd/| v. |成像，想像，描绘|To form a mental image of; to create a visual representation of something.|例句：I imaged the scene in my mind. 我在脑海中想象了那个场景。|例句：The data was imaged onto the screen. 数据被显示在屏幕上。|近义词：pictured, visualized, depicted|反义词：unimagined, abstract


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He images the future with hope. = 他怀着希望想象未来。
She images the data regularly. = 她定期对数据成像。

一般过去时:
I imaged a beautiful sunset last night. = 昨晚我设想了美丽的日落。
The scientist imaged the cells under the microscope. = 科学家在显微镜下对细胞成像。


现在完成时:
They have imaged the tumor successfully. = 他们已成功对肿瘤成像。
We have imaged the problem clearly in our minds. = 我们已经清晰地在我们脑海中描绘了这个问题。


过去完成时:
She had imaged the consequences before taking action. = 她采取行动前已经设想过后果。
The researchers had imaged the sample multiple times. = 研究人员已经多次对样品成像。


将来时:
They will image the brain using fMRI. = 他们将使用功能性磁共振成像来对大脑成像。
We will image the product for the marketing brochure. = 我们将为营销手册对产品成像。


虚拟语气:
If I imaged the situation differently, I might have acted otherwise. = 如果我对情况的设想不同，我可能会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had imaged the potential risks more carefully. = 我希望我当初能更仔细地设想潜在的风险。


被动语态:
The object was imaged using X-rays. = 该物体使用X射线成像。
The data will be imaged and analyzed. = 数据将被成像和分析。


疑问句:
How did they image the faint signal? = 他们是如何对微弱信号成像的？
Was the object clearly imaged? = 物体是否清晰成像？


否定句:
He couldn't image the extent of the damage. = 他无法想象损害的程度。
The system failed to image the target properly. = 系统未能正确地对目标成像。
