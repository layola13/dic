lead:/liːd/ /led/| n./v.|引导，领导，铅 |  To guide, conduct, or direct; to be in charge of; a  heavy, soft, bluish-gray metallic element.  |例句：He led the team to victory. 他带领团队取得了胜利。|例句：The lead singer's voice was amazing. 主唱的声音很棒。|近义词：guide, conduct, manage, direct,  control |反义词：follow, obey


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He leads the company. = 他领导这家公司。
She leads a busy life. = 她过着忙碌的生活。


一般过去时:
They led the parade. = 他们带领着游行队伍。
He led me to the hidden room. = 他带我去了那个隐蔽的房间。


现在完成时:
She has led a fulfilling life. = 她过着充实的生活。
He has led the project for two years. = 他领导这个项目两年了。


过去完成时:
They had led the charge before the enemy arrived. = 在敌人到达之前，他们已经冲锋陷阵了。
She had led a simple life before she became famous. = 在她成名之前，她过着简朴的生活。


将来时:
He will lead the discussion tomorrow. = 他明天将主持讨论。
She will lead the team to success. = 她将带领团队走向成功。


虚拟语气:
If he led the team, we would win. = 如果他领导团队，我们会赢。
I wish I had led a more adventurous life. = 我希望我过着更冒险的生活。


被动语态:
The team was led to victory by him. = 团队被他带领着取得了胜利。
The meeting was led by the chairman. = 会议由主席主持。


疑问句:
Who led the expedition? = 谁领导了这次探险？
Does she lead the choir? = 她领导唱诗班吗？


否定句:
He didn't lead the way. = 他没有带路。
She doesn't lead a very exciting life. = 她过着并不刺激的生活。


PHRASE->
lead the way = 带路
take the lead = 带头
lead to = 导致
in the lead = 领先
lead a life = 过着…样的生活
