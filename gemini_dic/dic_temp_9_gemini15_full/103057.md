jemmy:/ˈdʒɛmi/| n., v.|撬棍；撬开；|A jemmy is a crowbar or similar tool used for prying something open.  As a verb, it means to force open with a jemmy.|例句：He used a jemmy to force the window open. 他用撬棍撬开了窗户。  The thief jemmied the lock. 小偷撬开了锁。|近义词：crowbar, pry bar, lever|反义词：seal, secure
