preconcentrate:/ˌpriːˈkɒnsənˌtreɪt/| vt.|预浓缩；预先浓缩|To increase the concentration of something before further processing or analysis.  This often involves removing a solvent or other less desirable component.|例句：The sample was preconcentrated before gas chromatography analysis. 样品在气相色谱分析前进行了预浓缩。

The researchers preconcentrated the pollutants before measuring their concentrations. 研究人员在测量污染物浓度之前对其进行了预浓缩。|近义词：concentrate, enrich|反义词：dilute
