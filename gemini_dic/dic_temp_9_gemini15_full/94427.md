implodent:/ɪmˈploʊdənt/ | adj. | 向内爆发的；内爆的 | Implodent refers to something that collapses inward, often with a sudden and forceful action.  It describes the process or tendency of something to implode. | Example: The abandoned building suffered an implodent collapse.  废弃的建筑物发生了内爆坍塌。The implodent force of the star's gravity caused a supernova.恒星引力的内爆力量导致了超新星爆发。| 近义词：inward-collapsing, collapsing inward | 反义词：explosive, expanding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The star is imploding. = 这颗恒星正在内爆。
The building is imploding. = 这座建筑物正在内爆。

一般过去时:
The pressure vessel imploded. = 压力容器内爆了。
The old factory imploded during demolition. = 旧工厂在拆除过程中内爆了。

现在完成时:
The economy has imploded. = 经济已经崩溃了。
The stock market has imploded. = 股市已经崩盘了。

过去完成时:
The bubble had imploded before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，泡沫就已经破裂了。
The system had imploded before we could fix it. = 在我们能够修复之前，系统已经崩溃了。

将来时:
The reactor will implode if the containment fails. = 如果安全壳失效，反应堆将内爆。
The company will implode without further investment. = 如果没有进一步的投资，公司将走向崩溃。

虚拟语气:
If the pressure were increased, the container would implode. = 如果增加压力，容器就会内爆。
If the funding were cut, the project would implode. = 如果资金被削减，项目就会失败。

被动语态:
The building was imploded using controlled explosives. = 这座建筑物是用受控炸药爆破的。（虽然imploded本身是主动词，但在特定语境下可以被动）
The old bridge was imploded to make way for a new one. = 旧桥被爆破以腾出空间建造新桥。（同上）

疑问句:
Will the building implode safely? = 这座建筑物会安全内爆吗？
Did the star implode violently? = 这颗恒星内爆剧烈吗？

否定句:
The structure did not implode. = 结构没有内爆。
The balloon did not implode. = 气球没有内爆。
