presumptive:/prɪˈzʌmptɪv/| adj.|推定的；假定的； presumptive evidence 推定证据|Based on assumption or probability rather than certainty.  It suggests something is likely to be true but hasn't been definitely proven.|例句：The presumptive heir to the throne is the prince.  推定王位继承人是王子。

例句：The police have a presumptive suspect, but they need more evidence.  警方有了一个推定的嫌疑人，但他们需要更多证据。


近义词：supposed, assumed, probable, likely

反义词：certain, definite, proven


词源：from Latin *praesumere "to assume beforehand," from prae- "before" + sumere "to take."
