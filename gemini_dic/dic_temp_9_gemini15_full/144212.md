outsizes:/aʊtˈsaɪz/| vt.|超过，胜过，规模比…大|to surpass in size, amount, or importance|The new stadium outsizes the old one by a significant margin. 新体育馆比旧体育馆规模大得多。|exceed, surpass, overshadow|undersize, dwarf


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The new building outsizes the old one.  = 新建筑比旧建筑大。
His influence outsizes his actual power. = 他的影响力超过了他的实际权力。


一般过去时:
The storm outsized all previous weather events this year. = 今年的这场风暴超过了之前所有的天气事件。
Her generosity outsized her financial means. = 她的慷慨超过了她的经济能力。


现在完成时:
The success of the project has outsized initial expectations. = 项目的成功超出了最初的预期。
Their contribution has outsized their initial investment. = 他们的贡献超过了最初的投资。


过去完成时:
The damage from the earthquake had outsized any previous predictions. = 地震造成的损失超过了之前的任何预测。
The impact of the decision had outsized its intended effect. = 该决定的影响超出了其预期的效果。


将来时:
The company's growth will outsize that of its competitors. = 公司的增长将超过其竞争对手。
The demand for the product will likely outsize the supply. = 对该产品的需求可能会超过供应。


虚拟语气:
If the project were successful, its impact would outsize our initial projections. = 如果项目成功，其影响将超过我们最初的预测。
I wish my skills outsized my current responsibilities. = 我希望我的技能能够胜任我目前的职责。


被动语态:
The problem was outsized by the proposed solution. = 问题被提出的解决方案所克服。
The initial costs were outsized by the long-term benefits. = 初始成本被长期的利益所超过。


疑问句:
Does the impact of this decision outsize its cost? = 这个决定的影响是否超过了它的成本？
Does the new product outsize the existing model in terms of features? = 新产品在功能方面是否超过了现有型号？


否定句:
The benefits did not outsize the risks involved. = 收益没有超过所涉及的风险。
The small company's resources did not outsize the project's needs. = 这家小公司的资源不足以满足项目的需求。


PHRASE->
outsize the competition = 胜过竞争对手
outsize the market = 占据市场主导地位
outsize in scale = 规模巨大
outsize in ambition = 野心勃勃
outsize the expectations = 超出预期
