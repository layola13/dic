magnetizers: /ˈmæɡnətaɪzərz/ | n. | 磁化器；磁化者 | A magnetizer is a person or device that magnetizes something.  It can refer to a person who practices or believes in the power of magnetism for healing or other purposes, or a machine that creates a magnetic field. |例句：The technician used a powerful magnetizer to align the magnetic domains in the hard drive. 技术人员用强大的磁化器来调整硬盘中的磁畴。
