refines:/rɪˈfaɪnz/| vt.|精炼；使优美；使纯净|To purify or improve something by removing unwanted elements; to make something more elegant or sophisticated.|例句：The process refines the crude oil into gasoline.  这个过程将原油提炼成汽油。|例句：He refined his writing style over many years. 他多年来一直在改进自己的写作风格。|近义词：purify, perfect, polish, enhance|反义词：roughen, coarsen, degrade


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory refines crude oil into gasoline. = 工厂将原油提炼成汽油。
She refines her ideas before presenting them. = 她会在提出想法之前仔细推敲。


一般过去时:
They refined the sugar for better taste. = 他们提炼了糖以获得更好的口感。
He refined his argument after the debate. = 辩论后，他完善了自己的论点。


现在完成时:
The company has refined its production process. = 公司已经改进了其生产流程。
Scientists have refined the technique significantly. = 科学家们已经大大改进了这项技术。


过去完成时:
They had refined the design before the launch. = 他们在产品发布之前已经完善了设计。
She had refined her presentation skills through practice. = 她通过练习提高了自己的演讲技巧。


将来时:
We will refine the software before releasing it. = 我们将在发布软件之前对其进行优化。
They will refine their strategy based on market feedback. = 他们将根据市场反馈来改进策略。


虚拟语气:
If they refined the process, it would be more efficient. = 如果他们改进这个流程，效率就会更高。
I wish they had refined the product before releasing it. = 我真希望他们在发布产品之前能对其进行改进。


被动语态:
The oil is refined in a large refinery. = 原油在一个大型炼油厂中进行提炼。
The design has been refined several times. = 该设计已经多次改进。


疑问句:
How is the sugar refined? = 糖是如何精炼的？
Has the plan been refined sufficiently? = 计划是否已经充分完善？


否定句:
They did not refine the data before analysis. = 他们在分析之前没有精炼数据。
The company hasn't refined its marketing strategy yet. = 公司尚未改进其营销策略。
