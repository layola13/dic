ill-informed:/ˌɪlɪnˈfɔːrmd/| adj.|消息不灵通的，缺乏信息的|Lacking adequate knowledge or information; poorly informed.|His ill-informed comments showed a lack of understanding of the situation. 他不了解情况，发表了不当的评论。|well-informed, knowledgeable|well-informed, educated|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is ill-informed about the new policy. = 他对新政策不了解。
They are ill-informed regarding the current economic climate. = 他们对当前的经济形势不了解。


一般过去时:
She was ill-informed about the meeting's location. = 她对会议的地点不了解。
The journalist was ill-informed about the details of the scandal. = 这位记者对丑闻的细节不了解。


现在完成时:
He has been ill-informed throughout this entire process. = 在整个过程中，他一直缺乏信息。
They have been ill-informed about the changes in company policy. = 他们对公司政策的变更一直不了解。


过去完成时:
She had been ill-informed about the project's deadline. = 她对项目的截止日期一直不了解。
They had been ill-informed regarding the risks involved. = 他们一直对所涉及的风险不了解。


将来时:
He will be ill-informed unless he reads the briefing materials. = 除非他阅读简报材料，否则他将缺乏信息。
They will be ill-informed if they don't attend the training session. = 如果他们不参加培训课程，他们将缺乏信息。


虚拟语气:
If he were better informed, he wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果他消息灵通些，就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I were better informed about the situation. = 我希望我对情况更了解。


被动语态:
The public was ill-informed by the misleading headlines. = 误导性的标题使公众缺乏信息。
The decision was made based on ill-informed assumptions. = 这个决定是基于缺乏信息的前提做出的。


疑问句:
Is he ill-informed about the consequences? = 他对后果不了解吗？
Are they ill-informed about the latest developments? = 他们对最新进展不了解吗？


否定句:
He is not ill-informed; he simply chose to ignore the facts. = 他并非缺乏信息；他只是选择忽略事实。
They are not ill-informed; they are deliberately misleading us. = 他们并非缺乏信息；他们是在故意误导我们。


PHRASE->
ill-informed decision = 错误的决定

ill-informed guess = 盲目的猜测
ill-informed public = 消息闭塞的公众
