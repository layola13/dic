corks:/kɔːrks/| n.|软木塞；软木；软木制品|The word "cork" refers to the bark of the cork oak tree, which is harvested and used to make stoppers for bottles, insulation materials, and other products.  It also refers to the stopper itself.|例句：He popped the cork on the champagne bottle. 他打开了香槟酒瓶的软木塞。

例句：The cork keeps the wine fresh.  软木塞使葡萄酒保持新鲜。


近义词：stopper (塞子), bung (塞子)

反义词：None (没有直接的反义词)

词性:名词
