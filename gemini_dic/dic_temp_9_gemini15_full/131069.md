nonbaronial: /ˌnɒnˈbeɪrəuniəl/ | adj. |非男爵的，非贵族统治的 | Not relating to or characteristic of barons or baronial rule.  The term describes something that is not associated with barons, their power, or their system of governance. It implies a lack of aristocratic or feudal influence. | Example: The nonbaronial peasantry experienced a different set of social and economic conditions.  非贵族农民经历了一套不同的社会和经济条件。 |近义词: unbaronial, non-noble, plebeian | 反义词: baronial, aristocratic
