riverbank:/ˈrɪvərˌbæŋk/| n. |河岸，河边 |The land along the edge of a river.  It is often characterized by vegetation and a damp environment.  Riverbanks can be steep or gently sloping. |例句：We sat on the riverbank and watched the sunset. 我们坐在河岸上看日落。
The children played on the riverbank, building sandcastles.孩子们在河岸边玩耍，堆沙堡。|近义词：riverside, bank, shore |反义词：midstream, riverbed
