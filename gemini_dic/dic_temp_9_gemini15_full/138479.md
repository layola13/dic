novels:/ˈnɒvəlz/ /ˈnɑːvəlz/| n.|小说|A novel is a long fictional narrative, typically involving a complex plot, characters, and setting. Novels are generally longer and more complex than short stories.|例句：She enjoys reading historical novels. 她喜欢读历史小说。

例句：Many famous authors have written award-winning novels. 许多著名作家都写过获奖小说。


近义词：stories, tales, romances|反义词：nonfiction, factual accounts|词性:名词
