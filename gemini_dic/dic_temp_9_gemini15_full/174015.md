quasi-traditional:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ træˈdɪʃənəl/ | adj. |准传统的；似传统的|  Approaching or resembling a tradition but not completely conforming to it; partly traditional.  It implies a blend of traditional elements with modern or innovative aspects. |例句：The band's music is quasi-traditional, blending folk melodies with electronic instrumentation.乐队的音乐是准传统的，将民间旋律与电子乐器融合在一起。

The restaurant offers a quasi-traditional menu, incorporating modern twists on classic dishes. 这家餐馆提供一份准传统的菜单，在经典菜肴中融入了现代元素。


近义词：semiformal,  conventional (in some contexts),  modern-traditional


反义词：radical, avant-garde, entirely unconventional


词性：形容词
