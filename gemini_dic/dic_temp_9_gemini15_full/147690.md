overpractices:/ˈoʊvərˈpræktɪsɪz/ | v. |过度练习，练习过度 | To practice something excessively; to rehearse something to an excessive degree, often resulting in negative consequences such as fatigue, burnout, or decreased performance. |例句：He overpracticed the piano piece and now his fingers are sore. 他钢琴曲练习过度，现在手指都疼了。


例句：She overpracticed for the competition, leading to anxiety and a poor performance. 她为比赛练习过度，导致焦虑和糟糕的表现。


近义词：overtrain, overwork, overdo


反义词：underpractice, neglect


词性:动词
