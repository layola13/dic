词源:
The word "rheumatism" ultimately derives from the Greek word "rheuma," meaning "a flow, discharge, current."  This reflects the ancient belief that the condition was caused by a flow of humors (bodily fluids) in the body.  The suffix "-ism" indicates a condition or disease.
