drunkards:/ˈdrʌŋkɑːrdz/| n.|酒鬼，醉汉|A drunkard is a person who habitually drinks too much alcohol.  They often exhibit signs of alcohol abuse and dependence.  The term carries a strong negative connotation.|The drunkard stumbled down the street, barely able to stand.|alcoholic, sot, tippler|teetotaler, abstainer|名词
