creamers:/ˈkriːmərz/| n.|奶油；咖啡伴侣|A creamer is a liquid or powdered substance added to coffee or tea to make it creamier and sweeter. It can be made from dairy cream, milk, or non-dairy alternatives.|例句：I like my coffee with a splash of creamer. 我喜欢在我的咖啡里加一点奶油。|例句：They offer a variety of flavored creamers. 他们提供各种口味的咖啡伴侣。|近义词：coffee creamer, milk, half-and-half|反义词：N/A
