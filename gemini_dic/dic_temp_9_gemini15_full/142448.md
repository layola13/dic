oscillatory:/ˈɒsɪlətəri/ /ˈɑːsɪlətɔːri/ | adj. | 振荡的；摆动的；周期性的 | Relating to or characterized by oscillation; fluctuating or varying regularly between two points or states.  | The oscillatory motion of the pendulum was clearly visible. 摆的摆动清晰可见。The market showed oscillatory behavior throughout the day.  市场全天都表现出波动行为。| fluctuating, vibrating, pulsating | stable, constant, steady | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pendulum oscillates back and forth.  摆来回摆动。
The needle oscillates between the two points. 指针在两点之间摆动。


一般过去时:
The machine oscillated violently before breaking down. 机器在故障前剧烈振动。
The temperature oscillated between 20 and 25 degrees Celsius. 温度在20到25摄氏度之间波动。


现在完成时:
The stock market has oscillated wildly this year. 股市今年波动剧烈。
The signal has oscillated between high and low frequencies. 信号在高频和低频之间波动。


过去完成时:
The engine had oscillated erratically before it finally stopped. 发动机在最终停止之前已经不规则地振动。
The patient's heart rate had oscillated dangerously before the treatment. 病人的心率在治疗前危险地波动。


将来时:
The price of oil will likely oscillate throughout the next quarter. 油价未来一季度可能会波动。
The machine will oscillate at a higher frequency than before. 机器将以比以前更高的频率振动。


虚拟语气:
If the voltage oscillated less, the system would be more stable. 如果电压波动较小，系统将更稳定。
I wish the needle wouldn't oscillate so much. 我希望指针不要摆动得这么厉害。


被动语态:
The data was oscillated by the interference. 数据被干扰所扰动。
The frequency is oscillated by an external source. 频率被外部源所振荡。


疑问句:
Does the machine oscillate during operation? 机器在运行过程中是否振动？
How frequently does the signal oscillate? 信号多久振动一次？


否定句:
The needle does not oscillate. 指针不摆动。
The system does not oscillate under normal conditions. 系统在正常情况下不振动。
