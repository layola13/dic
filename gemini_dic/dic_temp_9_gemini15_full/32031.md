cherishable: /ˈtʃerɪʃəbl/ | adj. |值得珍惜的，可爱的|

英文解析:Worth cherishing; delightful and precious.  Something that is cherishable is worthy of being treasured and kept safe. It evokes feelings of fondness, love, or deep appreciation.


例句:

Those childhood memories are truly cherishable. = 那些童年的回忆真是值得珍惜。

The antique vase is a cherishable heirloom passed down through generations. =  这个古董花瓶是世代相传的珍贵传家宝。


近义词: precious, valuable, treasured, prized, delightful, beloved


反义词: worthless, valueless, despised, undesirable


词性: adjective
