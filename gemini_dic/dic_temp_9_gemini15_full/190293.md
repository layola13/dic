Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SOX):  This is the most common phrase associated with the name Sarbanes.  It refers to the important legislation designed to protect investors by improving the accuracy and reliability of corporate disclosures.

Sarbanes-Oxley compliance: This phrase refers to the process of adhering to the regulations set forth by the Sarbanes-Oxley Act.

Sarbanes-Oxley audit: This term describes the audit conducted to ensure compliance with the Sarbanes-Oxley Act.

Section 302 of Sarbanes-Oxley:  This refers to a specific section of the act dealing with corporate responsibility for financial reports.

Section 404 of Sarbanes-Oxley: This refers to another crucial section of the act relating to management assessment of internal controls.
