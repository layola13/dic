reminiscence: /ˌrɛmɪˈnɪs(ə)ns/ | n. |回忆，怀旧；往事 | The act or process of remembering past experiences; a memory or collection of memories, especially pleasant ones.  It often implies a feeling of nostalgia or sentimentality. |例句：His reminiscences of childhood were filled with warmth and laughter.  他的童年回忆充满了温暖和欢笑。

例句：She shared her reminiscences of her travels in Europe. 她分享了她欧洲旅行的回忆。


近义词：memory, recollection, remembrance, retrospection | 反义词：forgetfulness, oblivion


词源:  From Middle French *reminiscence, from Latin reminiscentia, from reminisci "to remember," from re- "again" + reminisci "to call to mind," from memini "I remember."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often reminisces about his college days. 他经常回忆他的大学时光。
She reminisces about her childhood frequently. 她经常回忆她的童年。


一般过去时:
He reminisced about his travels in Asia last night. 他昨晚回忆起在亚洲的旅行。
She reminisced about her first job at the party. 她在聚会上回忆起她的第一份工作。


现在完成时:
I have reminisced about many things this week.  我这周回忆了很多事情。
She has reminisced about her grandparents several times. 她已经几次回忆起她的祖父母。


过去完成时:
He had reminisced about the past before he fell asleep. 他睡着前回忆起了过去。
She had reminisced about her childhood before she wrote the book.  在她写书之前，她已经回忆起她的童年。


将来时:
I will reminisce about this trip for years to come. 我将在未来几年回忆这次旅行。
She will reminisce about her wedding day for the rest of her life. 她余生都会回忆起她的婚礼。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would reminisce about old friends. 如果我有时间，我会回忆起老朋友。
I wish I could reminisce about my youth more often. 我希望我能更经常地回忆我的青春。


被动语态:
His life story is reminisced about fondly by many. 许多人 fondly 回忆起他的生活故事。
The event was reminisced about by the attendees years later.  几年后，与会者回忆起这次活动。


疑问句:
Do you often reminisce about the past? 你经常回忆过去吗？
Will you reminisce about your time in school? 你会回忆起你在学校的时光吗？


否定句:
He doesn't reminisce about his early years. 他不回忆他的早年时光。
She didn't reminisce much about the trip. 她对这次旅行的回忆不多。
