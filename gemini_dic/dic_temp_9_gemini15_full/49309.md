delighted:/dɪˈlaɪtɪd/| adj.|欣喜的；高兴的；快乐的|Feeling or expressing great pleasure or happiness.|I was delighted to hear the good news. 我听到这个好消息很高兴。|pleased, glad, thrilled, overjoyed|disappointed, unhappy, sad, miserable|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am delighted to meet you. = 我很高兴认识你。
She is delighted with her new job. = 她对她的新工作很满意。

一般过去时:
He was delighted by the gift. = 他对这份礼物感到很高兴。
They were delighted to see their friends. = 他们很高兴见到他们的朋友。

现在完成时:
I have been delighted with your progress. = 我一直对你的进步感到高兴。
We have been delighted to have you as a guest. = 我们很高兴能请你做客。

过去完成时:
She had been delighted to receive such a warm welcome. = 她对如此热烈的欢迎感到非常高兴。
They had been delighted to learn of the good news earlier. = 他们早些时候得知这个好消息后感到非常高兴。

将来时:
I will be delighted to help you. = 我很乐意帮助你。
They will be delighted to see you again. = 他们将很高兴再次见到你。

虚拟语气:
I would be delighted if you could come. = 如果你能来，我会很高兴。
She would have been delighted to accept the invitation, but she was busy. = 她本来很乐意接受邀请，但她很忙。

被动语态:
He was delighted by the unexpected success. = 他对意外的成功感到高兴。（被动语态在此处较少见，通常用主动语态）
The audience was delighted by the performance. = 观众对演出感到很高兴。（被动语态在此处较少见，通常用主动语态）

疑问句:
Are you delighted with your results? = 你对你的结果满意吗？
Isn't he delighted to be here? = 他不高兴来这里吗？

否定句:
I am not delighted with the outcome. = 我对结果不满意。
She was not delighted by his behavior. = 她对他的行为不满意。


PHRASE->

delighted by/at sth = 因...而高兴
