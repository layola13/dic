diffusibility:/ˌdɪfjuːzəˈbɪləti/| n. |扩散性，弥散性 |The ability of a substance to spread or be spread throughout a medium.  This property is influenced by factors such as temperature, pressure, and the nature of the substance and the medium. |例句：The diffusibility of oxygen in water is crucial for aquatic life. 氧气在水中的扩散性对水生生物至关重要。

The high diffusibility of the gas allows it to quickly permeate the material.  这种气体的扩散性很高，使其能够快速渗透材料。|近义词：permeability, spreadability, dispersibility |反义词：impermeability, insolubility
