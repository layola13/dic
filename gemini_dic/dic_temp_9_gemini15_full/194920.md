semi-instinctive:/ˌsɛmiɪnˈstɪŋktɪv/| adj.| 半本能的，部分本能的 | Partially instinctive; partly learned or developed through experience but also influenced by innate tendencies.  It describes behaviors or actions that are not entirely automatic or reflex but show elements of both learned and innate responses. | Example:  Her response was semi-instinctive; she reacted quickly, but her actions were also guided by past experiences. 她反应是半本能的；她反应很快，但她的行为也受到过去经验的指导。|近义词：intuitive, partly instinctive | 反义词：fully conscious, deliberate, learned
