petrous:/ˈpiːtrəs/| adj.|岩状的；石头似的；坚硬的|Of or resembling stone or rock; hard.  The term often refers to the petrous part of the temporal bone in the skull.|例句：The petrous portion of the temporal bone is very dense and hard. 颞骨的岩部非常致密坚硬。

例句：The surgeon carefully navigated the petrous apex during the operation.  手术过程中，外科医生小心地绕过了岩尖。

近义词：rocky, stony, hard, solid|反义词：soft, yielding, pliable|词性：形容词
