nexus: /ˈnɛksəs/ | n. | 核心；联系；关系；焦点 | A connection or series of connections linking two or more things.  A central or crucial point or element. | 例句：The nexus between poverty and crime is well documented. 贫困与犯罪之间的联系已被充分证明。

例句：The city serves as a nexus for trade and commerce. 这个城市是贸易和商业的中心。


近义词：center, core, hub, link, connection, relationship

反义词：separation, isolation, disconnect


词性: 名词
