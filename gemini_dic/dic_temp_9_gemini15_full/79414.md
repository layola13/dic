glinting:/ˈɡlɪntɪŋ/| adj. |闪闪发光的，闪烁的| shining with reflected light; sparkling.  |例句：The glinting sunlight on the water was beautiful.  阳光在水面上闪闪发光，非常美丽。


The glinting jewels caught her eye. 闪闪发光的珠宝吸引了她的目光。


近义词：sparkling, shimmering, glittering, flashing| 反义词：dull, matte


词源:  Comes from the verb "glint," which ultimately derives from the Old English word "glentan," meaning "to gleam" or "to shine."  The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb into a present participle, functioning as an adjective.
