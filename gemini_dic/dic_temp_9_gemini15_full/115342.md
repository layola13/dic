manoeuvre: /məˈnuːvər/ | /mənˈuːvər/ | n., v. | 调动；策略；演习；机动；巧妙地操纵 |

英文解析: A manoeuvre is a planned movement of troops or military equipment. It can also refer to a skillful or clever action or movement, often to achieve a particular aim.  As a verb, it means to skillfully move or control something.

例句:

n. (名词):

The general planned a complex manoeuvre to outflank the enemy.  = 将军计划了一次复杂的军事调动以包围敌人。

The political manoeuvre was designed to gain public support. = 这一政治策略旨在赢得公众支持。


v. (动词):

She expertly manoeuvred the car through the narrow streets. = 她熟练地驾驶汽车穿过狭窄的街道。

He manoeuvred himself into a position of power. = 他巧妙地让自己获得了权力。


近义词: (Noun) tactic, strategy, maneuver, operation, scheme; (Verb) manipulate, handle, control, steer, navigate


反义词: (Noun)  inaction, passivity; (Verb)  mismanage, fumble


词性: Noun, Verb
