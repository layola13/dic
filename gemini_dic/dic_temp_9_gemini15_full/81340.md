graphics:/ˈɡræfɪks/ | n. |图形；图像；图表|The branch of art dealing with the creation and manipulation of images.  Graphics are used extensively in design, advertising, and computer applications.  |例句：The book's graphics were stunning. 这本书的插图非常精彩。

例句：The presentation included a variety of charts and graphics.  演示文稿包含各种图表和图像。


近义词：illustrations, visuals, images, diagrams, pictures|反义词：text, words|词性：名词
