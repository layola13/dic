prioritized:/praɪˈɔːrɪtaɪzd/| v.|优先处理，优先考虑|To give something a higher priority than other things.|例句：The manager prioritized the urgent tasks first.  经理首先优先处理紧急任务。
例句：We need to prioritize our spending to stay within budget. 我们需要优先考虑我们的支出以保持预算内。|近义词：prioritized, ranked, emphasized, stressed|反义词：neglected, ignored,de-emphasized


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She prioritizes her family above all else. = 她把家人放在首位。
He prioritizes efficiency in his work. = 他在工作中优先考虑效率。

一般过去时:
They prioritized the project deadline. = 他们优先考虑了项目截止日期。
The company prioritized employee safety. = 公司优先考虑员工安全。

现在完成时:
She has prioritized her health since her illness. = 自从生病以来，她一直把健康放在首位。
He has prioritized learning a new language. = 他一直优先学习一门外语。

过去完成时:
They had prioritized the emergency response. = 他们已经优先处理了紧急救援。
She had prioritized her studies before the exam. = 考试前她已经把学习放在首位。

将来时:
We will prioritize customer satisfaction. = 我们将优先考虑客户满意度。
They will prioritize research and development. = 他们将优先考虑研发。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would prioritize my well-being. = 如果我是你，我会优先考虑我的健康。
I wish I had prioritized my time better last semester. = 我希望上学期能更好地安排时间。

被动语态:
The tasks were prioritized by the project manager. = 任务由项目经理确定优先级。
The needs of the customers are prioritized by the company. = 公司优先考虑客户的需求。

疑问句:
What did you prioritize this morning? = 你今天早上优先处理了什么？
How do you prioritize your tasks? = 你如何优先安排你的任务？

否定句:
He didn't prioritize his sleep. = 他没有优先考虑睡眠。
They didn't prioritize customer feedback. = 他们没有优先考虑客户反馈。
