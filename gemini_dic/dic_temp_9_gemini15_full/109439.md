leotard:/ˈliːəˌtɑːrd/ | n. |紧身衣，体操服 | A close-fitting, one-piece garment, typically worn by dancers, gymnasts, and acrobats.  It usually covers the torso and legs, sometimes with sleeves. |Example: The ballerina wore a beautiful pink leotard.  芭蕾舞演员穿着一件漂亮的粉色紧身衣。|近义词：unitard, bodysuit |反义词：loose-fitting clothing
