garrett:/ˈɡærɪt/| n.|阁楼，顶楼房间|A garrett is a room or rooms at the top of a house, especially one that is small and poorly lit.  Garrets are often in the attic and used for storage or as living quarters for poorer people.|例句：He lived in a small, cramped garrett. 他住在一个狭小拥挤的阁楼房间里。|例句：The artist worked in a garrett, inspired by the city views below.  这位艺术家在一个阁楼里工作，灵感来自下面的城市景色。|近义词：attic, loft|反义词：basement, ground floor|名词
