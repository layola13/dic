horned:/hɔːrnd/| adj.|有角的，角状的|Having horns.  The word describes something possessing horns, often animals but can also refer to objects with horn-like protrusions.|例句：The horned beast charged across the field.  这只长角的野兽冲过田野。
例句：The horned helmet was a symbol of his power. 这顶有角的头盔是他权力的象征。|近义词：cornute, antlered|反义词：hornless|形容词
