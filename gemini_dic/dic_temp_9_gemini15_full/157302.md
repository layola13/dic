pintucking:/ˈpɪntʌkɪŋ/| n. |细褶缝纫；细褶装饰|The process of creating small, evenly spaced tucks in fabric, typically used for decorative purposes on clothing or other textiles.  This technique adds texture and visual interest.|例句：The pintucking on the blouse added a touch of elegance.  这件衬衫上的细褶增添了一丝优雅。|例句：She spent hours perfecting the pintucking on her quilt. 她花了几个小时完善她被子上的细褶。|近义词：pleating, tucking|反义词：无
