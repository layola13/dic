momentums:/məˈmɛntəm/| n.|动量，势头，冲力|The noun "momentum" refers to the quantity of motion of a moving body, measured as a product of its mass and velocity.  It also figuratively refers to the impetus gained by a process or course of events. |例句：The car gained momentum as it went downhill. 这辆车下坡时速度越来越快。 The project has lost momentum due to funding cuts.  由于资金削减，该项目失去了动力。|近义词：impetus, impetus, drive, force|反义词：inertia, stagnation
