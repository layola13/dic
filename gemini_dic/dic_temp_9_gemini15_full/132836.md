noneditorially:/ˌnɒnˌedɪˈtɔːriəli/| adv.|非编辑地，非编辑性地|Not done or produced by an editor or editorial staff.  It implies something wasn't subject to the usual editorial review or processes.|Example: The raw data was presented noneditorially, without any interpretation or analysis.  非编辑地展示了原始数据，没有任何解释或分析。|近义词：unedited, unreviewed, raw|反义词：editorially, reviewed, processed|副词
