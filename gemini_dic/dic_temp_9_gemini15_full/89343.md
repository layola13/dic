homologate:/ˈhɒmələɡeɪt/| /ˈhɑːmələɡeɪt/| vt.|批准；认可；使相同；|To officially approve or sanction something.|The government homologated the new drug for use. 政府批准了这种新药的使用。|approve, ratify, sanction, validate|reject, disapprove, invalidate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee homologates new proposals every month. = 委员会每月批准新的提案。
She homologates every document before submission. = 她会在提交之前审核每一份文件。


一般过去时:
The council homologated the building plans last week. = 理事会批准了上周的建筑计划。
He homologated the results of the experiment. = 他认可了实验的结果。


现在完成时:
They have homologated three new products this year. = 他们今年已经批准了三种新产品。
The agency has homologated the safety standards. = 该机构已经批准了安全标准。


过去完成时:
The court had homologated the agreement before the appeal. = 在上诉之前，法院已经批准了该协议。
She had homologated the design before starting production. = 在开始生产之前，她已经批准了设计。


将来时:
They will homologate the changes to the policy next month. = 他们下个月将批准对该政策的更改。
The board will homologate the budget proposal. = 董事会将批准预算提案。


虚拟语气:
If they homologated the application, we could start the project. = 如果他们批准了申请，我们就可以开始项目了。
I wish the authorities would homologate our request. = 我希望当局能批准我们的请求。


被动语态:
The new regulation was homologated by the parliament. = 新规章得到了议会的批准。
The design was homologated after rigorous testing. = 该设计经过严格测试后获得批准。


疑问句:
Will the committee homologate the research findings? = 委员会会批准研究结果吗？
Has the government homologated the new technology? = 政府已经批准这项新技术了吗？


否定句:
The organization did not homologate the controversial proposal. = 该组织没有批准有争议的提案。
They did not homologate the application due to insufficient evidence. = 由于证据不足，他们没有批准申请。
