diverter:/daɪˈvɜːrtər/ | n. | 分流器；转移器；导流器 | A device or structure that diverts something, such as water, traffic, or attention, from one course or direction to another.  | Example: The dam has a diverter to control the flow of water. 这座水坝有一个分流器来控制水流。  The police set up a traffic diverter to avoid congestion. 警察设置了一个交通分流器以避免交通拥堵。|近义词：deflector, shunt, switch | 反义词：concentrator


USAGE->

The diverter redirects the water flow. = 分流器使水流改道。
This device diverts traffic efficiently. = 这个装置有效地分流交通。
