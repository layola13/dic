ession:/ˈɛʃən/| n.|会议；一段时间；表达；时期 |A session is a period of time during which a meeting, class, or activity takes place.  It can also refer to a period of activity or work.  |例句：The Senate is now in session. 参议院现在正在开会。
The therapy session lasted for an hour.  治疗疗程持续了一个小时。|近义词：meeting, period, sitting |反义词：recess, adjournment


USAGE->

The committee holds a session every Tuesday. = 委员会每周二举行一次会议。
This session focuses on problem-solving. = 本课程重点讲解解决问题的方法。
