counterblasts:/ˈkaʊntəblæsts/| n. v.|反击；反驳|A counterblast is a strong reply or reaction against something. It can be a forceful response to an attack or criticism, or a measure taken to counteract something harmful.|例句：The government launched a counterblast against the opposition's criticism. 政府对反对派的批评发起了反击。The company's new product is a counterblast to its competitor's recent success. 该公司的新产品是对其竞争对手近期成功的反击。|近义词：retaliation, counterattack, rejoinder|反义词：submission, surrender|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company counterblasts its competitor's marketing campaign. = 公司反击其竞争对手的营销活动。
He counterblasts every criticism with facts. = 他用事实反驳每一个批评。


一般过去时:
The army counterblasted the enemy's attack. = 军队反击了敌人的进攻。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

She had counterblasted his accusations with irrefutable evidence. = 她用无可辩驳的证据反驳了他的指控。
