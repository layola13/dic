chiseling: /ˈtʃɪzəlɪŋ/ | v. | 凿刻；欺骗；慢慢地侵蚀 | The act of shaping or cutting with a chisel;  the act of defrauding or cheating someone gradually;  the slow erosion or wearing away of something. |例句：He was chiseling away at the stone. 他正在凿石头。 He was chiseling his employer out of money. 他正在慢慢地骗取雇主的钱。 The wind was chiseling away at the cliff face. 风正在慢慢地侵蚀悬崖峭壁。| 近义词：carving, sculpting, defrauding, swindling, eroding | 反义词：building, constructing, restoring, protecting
