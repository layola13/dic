retyping: /ˌriːˈtaɪpɪŋ/ | n. |重新打字；复印|The act of typing something again.  This often involves correcting errors or creating a new copy of an existing document.  It can also refer to the process of re-entering data that has been lost or corrupted.|例句：The retyping of the entire manuscript took several hours.  重新打字整个手稿花了好几个小时。|例句：The retyping of the damaged files was a tedious task. 重新录入损坏的文件是一项乏味的工作。|近义词：re-entry, recopying|反义词：original typing, first draft
