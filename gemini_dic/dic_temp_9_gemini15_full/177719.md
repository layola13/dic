recapitulates:/riˈkæpɪtʃʊleɪts/ | vt. | 总结；概括；重述 | To summarize and state again the main points of something.  | The lecturer recapitulated the main points of his lecture.  讲师总结了他讲座的主要内容。 | summarize, restate, review |  | verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recapitulates the key arguments in his conclusion. 他在结论中概括了关键论点。
The teacher recapitulates the lesson at the end of class. 老师在课堂结束时复习了课程。


一般过去时:
She recapitulated the events leading up to the accident. 她复述了导致事故发生的一系列事件。
The lawyer recapitulated the witness's testimony. 律师总结了证人的证词。


现在完成时:
The author has recapitulated the historical context in the introduction. 作者在引言中总结了历史背景。
He has recapitulated his findings in a concise report. 他在一个简明的报告中总结了他的发现。


过去完成时:
After the meeting, she recapitulated what had been discussed. 会议结束后，她总结了讨论的内容。
They had recapitulated the plan several times before implementation. 他们在实施计划之前已经多次复述了该计划。


将来时:
The professor will recapitulate the chapter's main ideas next week. 教授下周将总结本章的主要思想。
She will recapitulate the story for the children before bedtime. 她将在睡前为孩子们复述故事。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would recapitulate the entire book. 如果我有更多时间，我会总结整本书。
I wish he had recapitulated the instructions more clearly. 我希望他更清楚地复述了说明。


被动语态:
The key findings were recapitulated in the executive summary. 关键发现总结在执行摘要中。

疑问句:
Does the conclusion recapitulate the main points effectively? 结论是否有效地总结了要点？
Will the speaker recapitulate the main points at the end of the presentation? 演讲者会在演讲结束时总结要点吗？


否定句:
The report did not recapitulate all the relevant data. 该报告并未总结所有相关数据。
He didn't recapitulate the instructions clearly enough. 他没有足够清楚地复述说明。
