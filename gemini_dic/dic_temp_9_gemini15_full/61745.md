empanelment: /ɪmˈpænəlment/ | n. | 陪审团成员的选定；编组；列入名单 | The act of selecting and assembling a panel, especially a jury.  It refers to the process of formally listing or selecting individuals for a specific purpose, often a jury or a committee. | Example: The empanelment of the jury took several hours.  陪审团成员的选定花了几个小时。  The empanelment of experts for the inquiry was highly controversial.  为调查而挑选专家小组的做法极具争议。 | 近义词：selection, appointment, constitution | 反义词：disbandment, dismissal
