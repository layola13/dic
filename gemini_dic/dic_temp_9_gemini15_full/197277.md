sharping:/ˈʃɑːrpɪŋ/ | v. | 削尖；诈骗；敲诈 | To make something sharp; to defraud someone. | Example: He was sharping his pencil. 他在削铅笔。  The con artist was sharping the elderly woman out of her savings. 那个骗子正在诈骗这位老妇人的积蓄。 |近义词：honing, whetting, defrauding, swindling | 反义词：blunting, dulling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sharpens his knives every week. = 他每周都磨刀。
She sharpens her pencils before class. = 她上课前削铅笔。
