scenter:/ˈsɛntər/| n.|中心，中央|The center of the city is very busy. 城市中心非常繁忙。|近义词：middle, core, heart|反义词：periphery, edge, outside|名词


英文解析：The word "center" refers to the middle point or the most important part of something.  It can be a physical location, a point of focus, or a central idea.


例句：

The center of the target was hit.  靶心被击中了。

He is the center of attention. 他是众人瞩目的焦点。


词源：

The word "center" comes from the Old French word "centre," which in turn is derived from the Latin word "centrum," meaning "center point" or "midpoint" of a circle.


用法说明：

center作为名词，可以指：

1. 物理中心：The geographical center of the country is in the plains.（国家的地理中心位于平原。）
2. 焦点：The debate centered on the issue of immigration.（辩论的焦点是移民问题。）
3. 中心思想：The central theme of the novel is love.（这本小说的中心思想是爱情。）

center也可以用作动词，意思是“以…为中心”，例如：The discussion centered around the new policy.（讨论围绕着新政策展开。）
