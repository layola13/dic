im'd:/ɪmd/| v. |发短信；发送即时消息|  It's a shortened form of "I messaged."  It's primarily used in informal contexts to indicate that the speaker sent a message, often via instant messaging services.  |例句：I im'd him about the meeting. = 我给他发了短信，告诉他会议的事。 I im'd her to let her know I'd be late.= 我给她发了短信告诉她我会迟到。|近义词：texted, messaged, emailed |反义词：None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I im' him every day. = 我每天给他发短信。
She ims her friends regularly. = 她经常给朋友们发短信。

一般过去时:
I im'd her last night. = 我昨晚给她发了短信。
He im'd me about the project. = 他给我发了短信，谈论那个项目。

现在完成时:
I've im'd him several times. = 我给他发了好几次短信了。
She's im'd her boss about the issue. = 她已经就这个问题给老板发了短信。

过去完成时:
I had im'd him before he called. = 我在他打电话之前已经给他发过短信了。
She had im'd her colleague before the meeting started. = 会议开始前，她已经给她同事发过短信了。

将来时:

I wish I'd im'd him earlier. = 我真希望我早点给他发短信。

被动语态:
(Not applicable.  "Im'd" is typically not used in the passive voice.)
