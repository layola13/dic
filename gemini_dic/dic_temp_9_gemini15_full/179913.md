refect:/rɪˈflɛkt/|v.|反射；反映；沉思|to throw back light, heat, sound, or an image; to show or be a sign of something|The mirror reflected my image.  镜子反射了我的影像。|The statistics reflect a worrying trend.  这些统计数据反映了一种令人担忧的趋势。|近义词：mirror, show, indicate|反义词：refract


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lake reflects the mountains. = 湖面反射着山峦。
The data reflects the company's success. = 数据反映了公司的成功。

一般过去时:
The mirror reflected my face. = 镜子反射了我的脸。
Her actions reflected her true character. = 她的行为反映了她的真实性格。

现在完成时:
The events have reflected poorly on the government. = 这些事件给政府带来了负面影响。
His choices have reflected his values. = 他的选择反映了他的价值观。

过去完成时:
The incident had reflected badly on his reputation. = 那件事严重损害了他的名誉。
The results had reflected the hours of hard work. = 结果反映了他们付出的辛勤劳动。


将来时:
The new policy will reflect the government's priorities. = 新政策将反映政府的优先事项。
The changes will reflect the needs of the community. = 这些变化将反映社区的需求。

虚拟语气:
If the glass reflected more light, the room would be brighter. = 如果玻璃反射更多的光线，房间就会更明亮。
I wish my work reflected my potential better. = 我希望我的工作能更好地体现我的潜能。

被动语态:
The image was reflected in the still water. = 影像倒映在平静的水面上。
The company's values are reflected in its mission statement. = 公司的价值观体现在其使命宣言中。

疑问句:
Does the mirror reflect accurately? = 镜子反射得准确吗？
Does this data reflect the reality? = 这个数据反映了现实吗？

否定句:
The surface didn't reflect the light effectively. = 表面没有有效地反射光线。
His words didn't reflect his true feelings. = 他的话语并没有反映他的真实感受。


PHRASE->
reflect on/upon:  仔细考虑
reflect back: 反射回
reflect light: 反射光线
reflect poorly on:  对...有负面影响
reflect credit on:  为...增光
