fruitier:/ˈfruːtiər/| adj.|水果的；果味的；充满水果味的|Having the characteristics of fruit; tasting or smelling of fruit.|The fruitier the cake, the better it tastes.  这个蛋糕越有水果味，就越好吃。The shop sells a variety of fruitier jams. 这家商店出售各种水果味果酱。|fruitful, juicy, fruity|tasteless, bland


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The cake is fruitier than I expected. = 这个蛋糕比我预期的更水果味。
This juice tastes fruitier than that one. = 这果汁比那果汁更水果味。


一般过去时:
The pie was fruitier last year. = 去年这馅饼更水果味。
The jam tasted fruitier before it was stored. = 果酱在储存前味道更浓郁。


现在完成时:
The market has become fruitier with the arrival of new fruits. = 随着新水果的到来，市场变得更丰富了（水果味更浓）。
She has made the cake fruitier by adding more berries. = 她通过添加更多浆果使蛋糕更水果味。


过去完成时:
The dessert had been fruitier before some of the fruits dried out. = 在一些水果变干之前，甜点更水果味。
The fruit salad had become less fruitier after sitting out for too long. = 水果沙拉放置太久后水果味变淡了。


将来时:
The orchard will be fruitier next year. = 明年果园的水果味会更浓郁。
The new recipe will make the dessert fruitier. = 新食谱会使甜点更水果味。


虚拟语气:
If the recipe were fruitier, it would be more appealing. = 如果食谱更水果味，它会更吸引人。
I wish the wine were fruitier. = 我希望这酒更水果味。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

The yogurt isn't as fruitier as I had hoped. = 酸奶没有我想象的那么水果味。
