sextettes:/sekˈstɛts/| n. |六重奏；六行诗|A sextet is a musical composition for six voices or instruments, or a poem with six lines.  It often refers to a group of six singers or instrumentalists performing together.  In poetry, it can be a stanza of six lines or a poem consisting of six lines.|例句：The choir sang a beautiful sextet. 合唱团演唱了一首优美的六重奏。

The poet crafted a poignant sextet about lost love.  诗人创作了一首关于失恋的令人心酸的六行诗。|近义词：ensemble (for music), stanza (for poetry) |反义词：solo, duet, trio, quartet, quintet (depending on context)
