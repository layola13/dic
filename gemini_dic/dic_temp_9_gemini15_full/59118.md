dynamic:/daɪˈnæmɪk/ | adj., n. | 动态的；动力的；有活力的；动态；动力 |  Characterized by constant change, activity, or progress; forceful and energetic.  A force that produces motion. |

例句：

The dynamic city is full of energy and opportunity.  = 这个充满活力的城市充满了活力和机遇。

His dynamic leadership inspired the team to achieve great things. = 他充满活力的领导力激励团队取得了巨大的成就。


近义词：active, energetic, forceful, powerful, vibrant

反义词：static, passive, inert, sluggish, dormant

词性：adj., n.


词源：

From Greek dynamis, meaning "power," "might," or "force".  The word evolved through Latin and Old French before entering English.
