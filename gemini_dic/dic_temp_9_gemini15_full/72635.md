flyout:/ˈflaɪaʊt/| n.|弹出菜单，展开式菜单|A flyout is a small, temporary window or menu that appears on a screen, typically when a user interacts with a button or other control. It provides quick access to related options or information.|例句：The software's flyout menu offers quick access to frequently used tools.  这个软件的弹出菜单提供了对常用工具的快速访问。|近义词：popup menu, dropdown menu, context menu|反义词：embedded menu, integrated menu
