/ˌoʊvərˈhwɪps/ | v. |过度鞭打；过度挥动；(马)过分使用鞭子 | To whip excessively or beyond what is necessary or appropriate.  This can refer to physically whipping something or someone, or to using a whip metaphorically, such as overusing a particular tactic or argument. |例句：The trainer overwhipped the horse, leaving it injured and traumatized.  训练员过度鞭打马匹，导致马匹受伤并受到创伤。

The coach overwhipped his team with criticism, leading to low morale. 教练对他的队员进行了过度批评，导致士气低落。| 近义词：overbeat, overlash, flog excessively | 反义词：underwhip (not a common word, implies insufficient whipping) |动词
