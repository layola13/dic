overculled:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌld/| adj.|过度捕捞的，过度采集的|Excessively culled; exceeding the sustainable harvesting level.  This often refers to the removal of too many animals or plants from a population, leading to negative ecological consequences.|例句：The deer population in the forest is overculled, threatening the ecosystem's balance. 森林里的鹿群被过度捕杀，威胁着生态系统的平衡。|近义词：overharvested, depleted, overfished|反义词：underpopulated, abundant
