dampened:/ˈdæmpənd/| v. |使潮湿；抑制；减弱 |to make something slightly wet; to make something less strong or intense|The rain dampened the ground. 雨淋湿了地面。 The bad news dampened our spirits. 坏消息使我们情绪低落。|近义词： dampen, deaden, muffle, subdue, restrain, check, curb, moderate|反义词：intensify, heighten, enhance, boost


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain dampens the spirits of the hikers. = 雨水打压了徒步旅行者的兴致。
He dampens the enthusiasm of the team. = 他压制了团队的热情。


一般过去时:
The cold wind dampened my enthusiasm. = 冷风使我热情减退。
The news dampened her hopes for a promotion. = 这个消息使她升职的希望落空。


现在完成时:
The continuous rain has dampened the soil. = 持续的降雨使土壤潮湿。
The criticism has dampened his confidence. = 批评打击了他的信心。


过去完成时:
The previous setbacks had dampened their spirits before the final game. = 之前的挫折在决赛前已经打击了他们的士气。
The earlier failure had dampened her desire to try again. = 早先的失败已经削弱了她再次尝试的欲望。


将来时:
The forecast predicts that the storm will dampen the spirits of the festival attendees. = 预报预测风暴将打击节日参与者的兴致。
I fear that the bad weather will dampen our plans for a picnic. = 我担心坏天气会打乱我们的野餐计划。


虚拟语气:
If the rain had not dampened the field, the game would have been played. = 如果雨没有淋湿场地，比赛就会进行了。
I wish the news hadn't dampened his spirits so much. = 我真希望这个消息没有让他那么沮丧。


被动语态:
Their spirits were dampened by the unexpected loss. = 意外的失败打击了他们的士气。
His enthusiasm was dampened by the lack of support. = 缺乏支持使他的热情减退。


疑问句:
Did the rain dampen the picnic? = 雨淋湿了野餐吗？
Will the cold weather dampen the turnout for the concert? = 寒冷的天气会影响音乐会的出席人数吗？


否定句:
The good news did not dampen her spirits. = 好消息并没有让她沮丧。
The lack of support did not dampen his determination. = 缺乏支持并没有削弱他的决心。
