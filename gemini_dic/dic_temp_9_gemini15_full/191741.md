scientist:/ˈsaɪəntɪst/ | n. |科学家 |A person who is studying or has expert knowledge of science.  |例句：Marie Curie was a brilliant scientist who made significant contributions to the field of radioactivity. 居里夫人是一位杰出的科学家，对放射性研究领域做出了重大贡献。|例句：Many scientists are working on finding a cure for cancer. 许多科学家正在努力寻找治愈癌症的方法。|近义词：researcher, scholar, expert |反义词：layperson, amateur
