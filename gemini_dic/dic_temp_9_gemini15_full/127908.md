neologian:/niːˈɒlədʒən/| n. |新词创造者；造词家 | A person who creates or invents new words.  Neologians are often found in fields like linguistics, marketing, and technology, where new concepts require new terminology. | Example:  The marketing neologian coined the catchy term to describe the new product.  市场营销方面的造词家创造了这个朗朗上口的词来形容新产品。 |近义词：word-coiner, innovator (in terminology) |反义词：N/A
