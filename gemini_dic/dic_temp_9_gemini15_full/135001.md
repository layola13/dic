nonnecessities:/ˌnɑːnˌnesˈɛsətiz/| n.|非必需品，非生活必需品|Things that are not essential for survival or basic needs.  They are often considered luxuries or wants rather than needs.|例句：Luxury cars and designer clothes are considered nonnecessities by many. 许多人认为豪华轿车和名牌服装是非必需品。

例句：We cut back on nonnecessities to save money during the recession.  我们在经济衰退期间减少了非必需品的开支以节省资金。


近义词：luxuries, extras, dispensables, superfluities


反义词：necessities, essentials, necessities of life


词性：名词
