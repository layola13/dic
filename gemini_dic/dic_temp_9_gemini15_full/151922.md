pasteurize:/ˈpæstʃəraɪz/| vt.|巴氏消毒|To heat (a liquid, such as milk) to a temperature that destroys harmful bacteria but does not alter the flavor or quality of the liquid.  This process is named after Louis Pasteur, who developed it.|例句：The milk is pasteurized to kill harmful bacteria. 这牛奶经过巴氏消毒以杀死有害细菌。|例句：We pasteurize all our juices to ensure safety. 我们对所有果汁进行巴氏消毒以确保安全。|近义词：sterilize (but sterilize is more thorough)|反义词：contaminate|动词
