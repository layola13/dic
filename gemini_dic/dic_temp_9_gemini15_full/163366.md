preconjectures:/ˌpriːkənˈdʒɛktʃərz/| n. |预想，预先推测|Preliminary speculations or assumptions made before sufficient evidence is available to form a definite conclusion.  They are often based on incomplete or limited information and are subject to revision as more data becomes available. |The scientist's preconjectures about the experiment's outcome proved to be largely inaccurate after the data was analyzed.  科学家对实验结果的预想在数据分析后被证明大部分是不准确的。|hypotheses, suppositions, anticipations|conclusions, facts, certainties|名词
