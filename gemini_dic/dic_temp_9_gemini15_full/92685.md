icebergs:/ˈaɪsbɜːrz/| n.|冰山|A large mass of ice floating in the sea, detached from a glacier or ice sheet.|例句：The Titanic sank after hitting an iceberg. 泰坦尼克号撞上冰山后沉没了。  例句：We saw several icebergs during our cruise in Alaska. 我们在阿拉斯加的航行中看到了几座冰山。|近义词：ice floe, glacier (partially)|反义词：land, earth
