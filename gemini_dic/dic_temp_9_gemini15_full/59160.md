dyno:/ˈdaɪnoʊ/| n.|测功机；动力计|A dyno is a device used to measure the power output of an engine or motor.  It's commonly used in automotive and industrial settings to test engine performance and efficiency.|例句：The mechanic used a dyno to test the horsepower of the car engine. = 机械师用测功机测试汽车发动机的马力。  The engineers used a dyno to measure the torque output of the new motor. =工程师们用测功机测量新型发动机的扭矩输出。|近义词：dynamometer, power meter|反义词:|
