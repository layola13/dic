pseudo-Italian:/ˌsuːdoʊɪˈtæliən/ | adj. | 似意大利的；伪意大利式的 |  Appearing or claiming to be Italian, but not genuinely so; often used to describe something that imitates or superficially resembles Italian style or characteristics, but lacks authenticity.  | Example: The restaurant's décor was pseudo-Italian, with cheap imitations of classical statues and overly ornate furniture.  这家餐馆的装饰是伪意大利风格的，摆放着廉价的古典雕像复制品和过于华丽的家具。| 近义词：faux-Italian, mock-Italian, imitation-Italian | 反义词：authentically Italian, genuine Italian |  形容词
