enticement:/ɪnˈtaɪsmənt/| n. |诱惑；引诱；吸引 |The act of enticing; something that entices.  It refers to something that tempts or attracts someone, often to do something that might not be in their best interest. |Example: The enticement of a higher salary persuaded her to take the new job.  诱惑更高的薪水说服她接受了这份新工作。|近义词：allurement, lure, temptation, attraction |反义词：repulsion, deterrent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The advertisement offers an enticement to potential customers. = 这则广告对潜在客户具有诱惑力。
This new policy provides an enticement for employees to work harder. = 这项新政策激励员工更努力工作。


一般过去时:
The promise of a reward served as an enticement to complete the task. = 奖励的承诺成为完成任务的诱因。
The sweet smell of freshly baked bread was a powerful enticement. = 新鲜烘焙面包的香味是一种强大的诱惑。


现在完成时:
The marketing campaign has used various enticements to increase sales. = 营销活动使用了各种诱因来增加销量。
The company has offered many enticements to attract top talent. = 公司提供了许多诱因来吸引顶尖人才。


过去完成时:
The lure of adventure had already been a strong enticement before she even started her journey. = 在她开始旅程之前，冒险的诱惑就已经很强烈了。
The possibility of promotion had been a great enticement for him to work overtime. = 升职的可能性一直是他加班的巨大诱惑。


将来时:
The new features will be a great enticement for users to upgrade. = 新功能将成为吸引用户升级的巨大诱惑。
The prospect of travel will be a strong enticement for many to save money. = 旅行的前景将成为许多人存钱的强大诱因。


虚拟语气:
If the enticement were greater, more people would participate. = 如果诱惑更大，更多的人会参与。
I wish the enticement had been stronger earlier. = 我希望早些时候诱惑更强烈一些。


被动语态:
Customers are easily influenced by clever enticements. = 顾客很容易受到巧妙诱惑的影响。
The project was completed thanks to the enticement of a large bonus. = 项目由于巨额奖金的诱惑而得以完成。


疑问句:
What is the main enticement of this offer? = 此优惠的主要诱因是什么？
What kind of enticements are used in this marketing strategy? = 此营销策略中使用了哪些类型的诱因？


否定句:
There was no enticement to stay longer. = 没有理由待更久。
The lack of enticement led to low participation rates. = 缺乏诱因导致参与率低。
