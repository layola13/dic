shipwreck:/ˈʃɪprɛk/| n.|船舶失事；沉船|The destruction of a ship at sea, typically by being wrecked on rocks or a sandbank.  It can also refer to the remains of a wrecked ship.|例句：The shipwreck was discovered by a team of marine archaeologists. 这艘沉船被一支海洋考古队发现。

例句：The coast is littered with the wreckage of many shipwrecks. 海岸上散落着许多沉船的残骸。


近义词：wreck, ruin, disaster|反义词：salvage, rescue|词性：名词
