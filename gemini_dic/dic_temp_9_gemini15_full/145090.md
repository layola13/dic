overapplauding:/ˌoʊvərəˈplɔːdɪŋ/| v.|过度鼓掌|Giving excessive applause; applauding for too long or too enthusiastically.|例句：The audience overapplauded the singer, making her blush.  观众对歌手过度鼓掌，让她脸红了。 The speaker was clearly embarrassed by the overapplauding. 演讲者显然对过度鼓掌感到尴尬。|近义词：overpraising, excessively applauding|反义词：underapplauding, ignoring
