predictive:/prɪˈdɪktɪv/ | adj. | 预测的；预见的 | Relating to or having the ability to predict something.  Predictive models use historical data to make forecasts about the future.  | 例句：The predictive model accurately forecast the next quarter's sales.  预测模型准确地预测了下一季度的销售额。

The predictive text feature on my phone is very helpful. 我的手机上的预测文本功能非常有用。

近义词：forecasting, prognostic, prescient | 反义词：unpredictable, unforeseen
