reteaches:/ˌriːˈtiːtʃɪz/| v.|再教；重新教授|To teach something again.  This usually implies a previous attempt at teaching that was not fully successful or needs reinforcement.|例句：The teacher reteaches the difficult concept to the students who didn't understand it the first time.老师重新讲解那些第一次没理解的学生难以理解的概念。

例句：She reteaches the material to ensure everyone understands. 她重新教授这些材料以确保每个人都理解。|近义词：re-explain, review, reiterate|反义词：forget, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reteaches the lesson every week. 他每周都复习这节课。
The school reteaches the fundamentals to struggling students. 学校为学习有困难的学生重新教授基础知识。


一般过去时:
The tutor reteached the grammar rules. 家教重新讲解了语法规则。
She reteached the chapter after the students failed the quiz. 学生们测验不及格后，她重新讲授了这一章节。


现在完成时:
The teacher has reteached that section three times. 老师已经三次重新讲解了那部分内容。
He has reteached the material to several different classes. 他已经向好几个不同的班级重新教授了这些材料。


过去完成时:
They had reteached the lesson before the final exam. 他们在期末考试前已经重新讲授了这节课。
She had reteached the difficult concepts several times, but some students still struggled. 她已经多次重新讲解了那些难点，但有些学生仍然难以理解。


将来时:
The professor will reteach the subject next semester. 教授下学期将重新教授这门课程。
I will reteach the material if necessary.如有必要，我会重新讲解这些材料。


虚拟语气:
If I were the teacher, I would reteach the entire chapter. 如果我是老师，我会重新讲解整章内容。
If the students had asked, she would have reteached the section. 如果学生们问了，她就会重新讲解那部分内容。


被动语态:
The lesson was reteached due to student confusion. 由于学生困惑，这节课被重新讲授了。
The material is being reteached to ensure understanding. 正在重新教授这些材料以确保理解。


疑问句:
Will the teacher reteach the topic?老师会重新讲解这个话题吗？
Did they reteach the lesson after the test? 考试后他们重新讲授这节课了吗？


否定句:
He didn't reteach the difficult section. 他没有重新讲解那部分难点。
The teacher did not reteach the lesson, opting for a different approach. 老师没有重新讲解这节课，而是选择了不同的方法。
