re-coded:/ˌriːˈkoʊdɪd/ | adj. |重新编码的，重新编码过的 |  Having been encoded again;  having undergone a process of re-encoding.  This usually implies a change or improvement to the original code.  | Example: The software was re-coded to improve its efficiency.  软件被重新编码以提高效率。 | 近义词:reprogrammed, rewritten, revised | 反义词: originally coded, first encoded |  形容词
