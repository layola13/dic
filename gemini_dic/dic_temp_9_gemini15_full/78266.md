germanify: /ˈdʒɜːrmənɪfaɪ/ | vt. |使德意志化；使具有德国特色|To make something German in character or style.  This can refer to language, culture, or other aspects. |例句：He tried to germanify the names of the streets. 他试图将街道名称德意志化。

例句：The restaurant attempted to germanify its menu by adding traditional German dishes. 这家餐馆试图通过添加传统的德国菜来使菜单具有德国特色。


近义词：germanize


反义词：de-germanize (This is not a standard word, but conveys the opposite meaning)


词性:动词
