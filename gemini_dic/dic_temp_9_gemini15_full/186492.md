It's commonly used in various industrial applications and sometimes in cosmetics. | Example:  The study investigated the properties of methyl ricinoleate as a potential biolubricant.  这项研究调查了甲基蓖麻酸酯作为潜在生物润滑剂的特性。 | 近义词:  (depending on context) fatty acid ester, castor oil derivative | 反义词: N/A
