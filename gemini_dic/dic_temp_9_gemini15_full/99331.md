intercut:/ˌɪntərˈkʌt/ | vt. | 交替剪辑，插剪 | To edit (film or video) by alternating between two or more scenes or sequences.  | Example: The director intercut scenes of the robbery with flashbacks to the planning stages.  导演将抢劫的场景与计划阶段的闪回交替剪辑。 |近义词：alternate, juxtapose | 反义词：N/A
