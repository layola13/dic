looming:/ˈluːmɪŋ/| adj.|逼近的；隐约可见的；阴森的；| appearing large and threatening; impending.|The looming storm clouds threatened rain.  逼近的暴风雨云预示着下雨。|The looming deadline caused him stress.  迫近的最后期限给他带来了压力。|近义词：impending, approaching, threatening, imminent|反义词：distant, receding, far-off


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The storm is looming on the horizon. = 风暴正在地平线上逼近。
A crisis is looming in the country. = 该国正面临一场危机。

一般过去时:
A sense of danger loomed over the city. = 一种危险感笼罩着这座城市。
The shadow of war loomed large in their minds. = 战争的阴影在他们的脑海中挥之不去。


现在完成时:
The threat of war has been looming for months. = 战争的威胁已经持续了好几个月了。
Problems have been looming since the beginning of the project. = 从项目开始以来，问题就一直潜伏着。


过去完成时:
The possibility of failure had loomed large in his mind before the presentation. = 在演讲之前，失败的可能性在他心中挥之不去。
It had loomed over her for a long time before she finally confronted the issue. = 在她最终面对这个问题之前，它已经困扰了她很久了。


将来时:
A major recession will be looming if the government doesn't act quickly. = 如果政府不迅速采取行动，一场严重的经济衰退即将来临。
More difficulties are looming in the future. = 未来将会面临更多困难。


虚拟语气:
If a crisis were looming, we would need to take immediate action. = 如果危机迫在眉睫，我们需要立即采取行动。
I wish the deadline wasn't looming so close. = 我希望最后期限不要那么快就到了。


被动语态:
The threat was loomed over by the authorities. = 威胁被当局夸大了。(被动语态在此处略显生硬，需谨慎使用。)
The dark clouds loomed over the town. =  乌云笼罩着小镇。(被动语态在此处不适用)


疑问句:
Is a financial crisis looming? = 金融危机是否迫在眉睫？
Are any serious problems looming? = 是否有什么严重的问题即将出现？


否定句:
No immediate threat is looming. = 目前没有迫在眉睫的威胁。
There were no looming dangers. = 没有迫在眉睫的危险。


PHRASE->

loom large:  显得重要；显得突出
loom up:  显现；出现
loom over:  笼罩；压在…之上
loom in the distance: 在远处隐约可见
loom on the horizon:  在地平线上隐约可见
