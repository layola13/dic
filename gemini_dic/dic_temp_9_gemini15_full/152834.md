peculium:/pɪˈkjuːliəm/| n. |私产，私有财产，零花钱 |  A peculium was a slave's private property, which they could accumulate and dispose of as they pleased.  This was particularly relevant in Roman law.  The term also refers to a minor's allowance or savings. |Example:  The slave carefully saved his peculium to buy his freedom.  奴隶小心翼翼地攒着他的私产来买自由。

The Roman slave's peculium was often the source of his later independence. 罗马奴隶的私产通常是他后来获得独立的来源。|近义词：private property, personal possession, allowance |反义词：communal property, shared ownership
