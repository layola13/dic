impudence:/ˈɪmpjuːdəns/| n. |鲁莽；放肆；无礼|The quality of being impudent; boldness or disrespect.  It often implies a lack of respect for authority or social norms.  Impudence can range from mild cheekiness to blatant insolence.|例句：Her impudence in talking back to the teacher was shocking.她的顶撞老师的行为令人震惊。|例句：The child's impudence was met with a stern warning.孩子的无礼受到了严厉的警告。|近义词：insolence, impertinence, audacity, cheek, effrontery|反义词：respectfulness, deference, politeness, humility|名词
