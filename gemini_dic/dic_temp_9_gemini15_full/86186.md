heatable:/ˈhiːtəbəl/| adj.| 可加热的；能加热的 | Capable of being heated.  Something that is heatable can be subjected to heat without being damaged or destroyed.  This often implies a specific design or material that allows for efficient and safe heating. | 例句：This mug is heatable in a microwave. = 这个杯子可以在微波炉里加热。

This material is heatable to high temperatures without losing its structural integrity. =这种材料可以在高温下加热而不会失去其结构完整性。

近义词：heat-resistant,  warmable

反义词：unheatable,  heat-sensitive
