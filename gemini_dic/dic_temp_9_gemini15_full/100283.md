intersegment:/ˌɪntərˈsɛɡmənt/| adj. |节间的，节段间的| Relating to or situated between segments or sections.  The term often appears in contexts involving anatomy, particularly in zoology and biology, referring to structures or processes that connect or occur between body segments.|Example: Intersegmental muscles connect adjacent body segments in insects.  节间肌肉连接昆虫相邻的体节。|近义词：intersectional, between-segment|反义词：intrasegmental
