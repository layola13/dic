duressor: /dəˈrɛsər/ | n. | 胁迫者，施压者 | The person who compels or coerces another.  A duressor uses force, threats, or other forms of pressure to influence someone's actions. | Example: The duressor threatened to harm the witness's family if they testified.  胁迫者威胁说，如果证人作证，就会伤害其家人的安全。


|近义词: coercer, intimidator, bully, blackmailer |反义词: benefactor, helper, supporter |词性:名词
