crash-lands:/ˈkræʃˌlændz/| v.|坠毁着陆，紧急迫降|To land an aircraft, spacecraft, or vehicle suddenly and violently, typically due to an emergency.|The pilot crash-landed the plane in a field after engine failure.  飞行员在引擎故障后将飞机紧急迫降在田野里。|The small plane crash-landed in a cornfield. 这架小飞机坠毁在一片玉米地里。| emergency landing, forced landing | safe landing |  v.
