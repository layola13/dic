senza:/ˈsɛntsa/| adv.|  没有，无；不带|  Italian adverb meaning "without." It is often used in musical scores and other contexts to indicate the absence of something. | 例句：The piece should be played senza vibrato.  这首曲子应该不带颤音演奏。
例句：She sang the song senza accompaniment. 她清唱这首歌，没有伴奏。|近义词：without, lacking|反义词：with
