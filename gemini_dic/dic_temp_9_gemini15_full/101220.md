invision:/ɪnˈvɪʒən/| vt.|想象；设想；预想|To form a mental image of something; to imagine something that is not yet real.  It often implies a creative or hopeful aspect, envisioning a future possibility or ideal.|例句：I envision a future where technology solves many of our current problems. 我设想未来科技将解决我们当前的许多问题。
例句：The architect envisioned a building that would be both functional and beautiful. 建筑师设想了一座既实用又美丽的建筑。|近义词：imagine, visualize, foresee, contemplate|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I envision a bright future for my company. = 我设想我的公司会有一个光明的未来。
She envisions a world free from poverty. = 她设想一个没有贫穷的世界。

一般过去时:
He envisioned a new marketing strategy. = 他设想了一个新的营销策略。
They envisioned a successful product launch. = 他们设想了一个成功的产品发布。

现在完成时:
We have envisioned many possibilities for this project. = 我们已经设想了这个项目的许多可能性。
She has envisioned her dream house for years. = 她多年来一直设想着她的梦想之家。

过去完成时:
They had envisioned a different outcome. = 他们曾经设想过不同的结果。
I had envisioned a more relaxing vacation. = 我曾经设想过一个更轻松的假期。

将来时:
We will envision a new approach to the problem. = 我们将设想解决这个问题的新方法。
She will envision a better way of doing things. = 她将设想一种更好的做事方法。

虚拟语气:
If I could envision the future, I would be a millionaire. = 如果我能预见未来，我将成为百万富翁。
I wish I could envision a solution to this crisis. = 我希望我能设想出解决这场危机的办法。

被动语态:
A brighter future is envisioned by many people. = 许多人设想了一个更光明的未来。
A new strategy was envisioned by the team. = 该团队设想了一个新的策略。

疑问句:
What do you envision for the future of education? = 你设想教育的未来是什么样的？
What did they envision for the new product? = 他们为新产品设想了什么？

否定句:
I cannot envision a scenario where this plan fails. = 我无法设想这个计划失败的情况。
They did not envision the challenges they would face. = 他们没有预见到他们将面临的挑战。
