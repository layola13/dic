discradle:/dɪsˈkreɪdl/ | v. | 从摇篮中取出；使脱离依赖；使…失去稳定 | To remove from a cradle; to take away support or stability from; to unsettle. | 例句：The earthquake discradled the building, causing it to collapse. 地震使建筑物失去稳定，导致其倒塌。  | 例句：He had to discradle himself from his parents' financial support. 他必须摆脱对父母经济上的依赖。 | 近义词：unsettle, destabilize, dislodge | 反义词：stabilize, support, secure | 动词
