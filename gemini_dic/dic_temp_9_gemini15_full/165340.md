preinvolve:/ˌpriːɪnˈvɒlv/| v.|预先涉及；预先卷入|To involve beforehand; to cause to be involved in advance.  The term is relatively rare and often found in specialized contexts.|例句：The committee preinvolved itself in the negotiations to ensure a smooth process. 委员会预先参与了谈判以确保流程顺利进行。 The company's preinvolvement in the project ensured a head start.  公司的预先参与确保了项目领先优势。|近义词：preengage, anticipate, foreshadow|反义词：exclude, uninvolve


USAGE->

They preinvolve themselves in every aspect of the project. = 他们预先参与项目的各个方面。
The company preinvolves its employees in the decision-making process. = 公司让员工预先参与到决策过程中。
