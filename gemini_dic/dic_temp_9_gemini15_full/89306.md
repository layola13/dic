homoeozoic: /ˌhoʊmiːoʊˈzoʊɪk/ | adj. | 同源的；同生的 |  Relating to or denoting organisms of similar origin or development.  The term often refers to organisms that share a common ancestor or have similar embryonic development.  It's a less common word and often used in scientific contexts, particularly in biology and paleontology.


例句：

The homoeozoic species exhibit similar anatomical structures, suggesting a close evolutionary relationship.  =  同源物种表现出相似的解剖结构，这表明它们之间存在密切的进化关系。

Further research is needed to determine the exact homoeozoic relationships between these newly discovered fossils. = 需要进一步的研究来确定这些新发现的化石之间确切的同源关系。


近义词：homologous, conspecific, cognate


反义词：heterologous, dissimilar, unrelated
