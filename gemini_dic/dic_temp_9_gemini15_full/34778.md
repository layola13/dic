climates:/ˈklaɪmɪts/| n.|气候（复数）|The prevailing weather conditions of a region, averaged over a long period.|例句：The climates of the world vary greatly. 世界各地气候差异很大。
例句：Tropical climates are characterized by high temperatures and humidity. 热带气候的特点是高温高湿。|近义词：weathers, environments|反义词:None.
