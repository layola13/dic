sago:/ˈseɪɡoʊ/| n. |西米；西米淀粉|A starchy substance obtained from the pith of various tropical palm trees, especially Metroxylon sagu, used as a food.  It is often used to make puddings and other desserts. |例句：The dessert was made with sago and coconut milk. 这道甜点是用西米和椰奶做的。|例句：Sago pearls are a popular ingredient in Asian cuisine. 西米珍珠是亚洲菜系中一种很受欢迎的食材。|近义词：starch, tapioca|反义词：无
