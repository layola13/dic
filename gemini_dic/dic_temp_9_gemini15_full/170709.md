pseudoanthropological: /ˌsuːdoʊˌænθrəpəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 似人类学的；伪人类学的 |  Appearing to be anthropological but lacking scientific rigor or accuracy; falsely claiming to be based on anthropological principles.  It often refers to studies or theories that mimic anthropological methods but lack the proper methodology, critical analysis, or ethical considerations. |

例句：His pseudoanthropological theories, based on anecdotal evidence and unsubstantiated claims, were widely criticized by the academic community.  他的伪人类学理论，基于轶事证据和未经证实的声明，受到了学术界的广泛批评。


The pseudoanthropological study failed to account for crucial cultural variables. 这项伪人类学研究未能考虑重要的文化变量。


近义词：quasi-anthropological, specious, spurious, sham


反义词：genuine, authentic, rigorous, valid


词性：形容词
