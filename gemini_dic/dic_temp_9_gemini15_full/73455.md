forensic: /fəˈrɛnzɪk/ | adj. | 法医的；与法庭有关的 | Relating to or denoting the application of scientific methods and techniques to the investigation of crime.  Forensic science involves the use of various scientific disciplines, such as biology, chemistry, and physics, to analyze evidence and solve crimes. | The forensic evidence was crucial in convicting the suspect. 法医证据对定罪至关重要。 | scientific, legal | unscientific, illogical
