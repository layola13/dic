class:/klɑːs/|ˈklæs/| n.|班级；种类；阶级；课程|A group of students who are taught together; a category of things or people having some shared characteristics; a social rank or status; a period of instruction.|例句：She teaches a class of twenty students. 她教一个二十名学生的班级。  My favorite class is history. 我最喜欢的课是历史课。|近义词：group, category, grade, lesson, session|反义词：individual, unique


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The class begins at 8 a.m. = 课程早上8点开始。
What class are you taking this semester? = 这个学期你上什么课？


一般过去时:
The class ended early yesterday. = 昨天的课早早结束了。
Did you attend the history class? = 你参加历史课了吗？


现在完成时:
I have finished my homework for class. = 我已经完成了我的家庭作业。
She has attended all the classes this semester. = 这个学期她所有的课都参加了。


过去完成时:
The class had already started when I arrived. = 我到达的时候课已经开始了。
They had completed the project before the class ended. = 课程结束前他们已经完成了项目。


将来时:
The class will start next Monday. = 课程下周一开学。
Will you be able to attend the class tomorrow? = 你明天能来上课吗？


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would take another class. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会再选一门课。
I wish I could take a cooking class. = 我希望我能上一门烹饪课。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
