degradation's:/ˌdeɡrəˈdeɪʃənz/| n.| 退化；降级；堕落；贬低 | The process of being degraded or the state of being degraded; a lowering in rank, status, or quality.  It often implies a decline from a previous higher state or condition. |例句：The degradation of the environment is a serious concern. 环境恶化是一个严重的问题。

The degradation of his character was evident in his actions.  他的品格堕落在他行为中显而易见。


近义词： deterioration, decline, decay, debasement, depreciation

反义词： improvement, enhancement, elevation, upgrading


词性：名词 (复数形式)
