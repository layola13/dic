redigestion:/ˌriːdaɪˈdʒɛstʃən/| n. |再消化；重新消化|The process of digesting something again, typically food that has already been partially digested.  It can also refer to the mental process of reconsidering or re-evaluating something.|例句：The ruminant's re-digestion of food allows for greater nutrient absorption. 反刍动物对食物的再消化使得营养吸收更充分。


The incomplete digestion of food can lead to re-digestion in the intestines.食物消化不完全会导致肠道内再次消化。|近义词：reprocessing, re-examination|反义词：initial digestion, first digestion|名词
