flooring:/ˈflɔːrɪŋ/ | n. |地板，地面铺设 | Floor covering, material used to cover a floor.  It can refer to the material itself or the process of installing it. |例句：The new flooring is made of hardwood. = 新的地板是用硬木做的。

例句：They are installing new flooring in the kitchen. = 他们正在厨房安装新的地板。

近义词：floor covering, pavement, surfacing | 反义词：ceiling
