dragbolt:/ˈdræɡbəʊlt/ | n. |拉杆螺栓，拖动螺栓 | A drag bolt is a type of bolt used to secure objects together, often characterized by a large head and a threaded shank.  It's frequently used in applications where significant pulling or dragging force is involved, hence the name. | Example: The heavy machinery was secured to the trailer using several large drag bolts.  重型机械用几根大型拉杆螺栓固定在拖车上。The bridge was reinforced with drag bolts to withstand the stress. 桥梁用拉杆螺栓加固以承受压力。|近义词：anchor bolt, eye bolt |反义词： (no direct antonym,  consider bolts designed for different purposes as contextual opposites)|名词
