embarrass:/ɪmˈbærəs/ | vt. |使尴尬；使窘迫 | To cause (someone) to feel awkward, ashamed, or uneasy.  |例句：His clumsy behavior embarrassed his parents. 他笨拙的行为使他的父母尴尬。|例句：The unexpected question embarrassed her.  这个意外的问题让她很尴尬。|近义词：humiliate, shame, mortify |反义词：embolden, encourage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He embarrasses me with his loud singing. = 他大声唱歌让我很尴尬。
The comedian embarrasses the audience with his jokes. = 喜剧演员用他的笑话让观众尴尬。


一般过去时:
The incident embarrassed him deeply. = 那件事让他非常尴尬。
Her public confession embarrassed everyone present. = 她的公开忏悔让在场的所有人都感到尴尬。


现在完成时:
His actions have embarrassed the entire family. = 他的行为已经让整个家庭蒙羞。
This situation has embarrassed me more than I care to admit. = 这种情况比我愿意承认的更让我尴尬。


过去完成时:
I had already embarrassed myself before I even started the presentation. = 我在开始演讲之前就已经让自己出丑了。
She had embarrassed him several times before he finally confronted her. = 在他最终与她对质之前，她已经几次让他难堪了。


将来时:
His speech will embarrass him if he doesn't prepare properly. = 如果他不好好准备，他的演讲会让他难堪。
I am worried that the truth will embarrass her. = 我担心真相会让她尴尬。


虚拟语气:
If I said that, it would embarrass everyone. = 如果我说那样的话，大家都会觉得尴尬。
I wish I hadn't embarrassed myself in front of her. = 我真希望我没有在她面前出丑。


被动语态:
He was embarrassed by her sharp criticism. = 他被她尖锐的批评弄得很尴尬。
She was embarrassed to find her mistake. = 她发现自己的错误后感到很尴尬。


疑问句:
Does his behavior embarrass you? = 他的行为让你尴尬吗？
Will this decision embarrass the company? = 这个决定会让公司难堪吗？


否定句:
His words did not embarrass her at all. = 他的话一点也没让她尴尬。
I tried not to embarrass myself during the interview. = 我努力在面试中避免让自己难堪。


PHRASE->
embarrass oneself = 自讨没趣
embarrass somebody = 使某人尴尬
be embarrassed about something = 对某事感到尴尬
feel embarrassed = 感到尴尬
to my embarrassment = 令我尴尬的是
