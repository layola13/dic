ringtail: /ˈrɪŋteɪl/ | n. |环尾（动物）|A ringtail is a small nocturnal mammal with a long, bushy tail marked with rings.  They are known for their intelligence and adaptability.  | Example: The ringtail cautiously crept along the branch, its ringed tail swaying.  环尾猫小心翼翼地沿着树枝爬行，它环状的尾巴摇摆着。|近义词：ring-tailed cat (more specific)|反义词：无
