slicer:/ˈslaɪsər/| n. |切片机，切片器 |A slicer is a kitchen tool used to cut food into thin slices.  It can be manual or electric. |例句：She used a meat slicer to prepare the sandwiches. 她用切肉机准备三明治。|例句：The pizza slicer made quick work of the pie. 比萨切片器很快就把比萨切好了。|近义词：cutter, chopper, dicer |反义词：None

词源:
The word "slicer" is derived from the verb "slice," which comes from the Old Norse word "slīsa," meaning "to slit" or "to cut."  The suffix "-er" indicates an agent or instrument.  Therefore, a slicer is something that slices.
