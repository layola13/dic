eelbuck:/ˈiːlbʌk/| n. |鳗鱼皮钱包；(俚语) 骗子，狡猾的人 |An eelbuck is a slang term for a swindler or a cunning person, sometimes referring to a wallet made of eel skin.  The term's origin likely stems from the eel's slippery nature, associating it with deception and evasion. | Example: "He was a real eelbuck, always finding ways to get out of paying his debts."  = 他是个真正的骗子，总是能找到办法逃避债务。|近义词: swindler, con man, sharper, rogue |反义词: honest person, trustworthy person |名词
