proficients:/proʊˈfɪʃənts/| n. |熟练者，精通者 |Those who are highly skilled or expert in a particular field.  Proficients often demonstrate mastery and a high level of competence. |例句：The company is looking for proficients in software development. 这家公司正在寻找软件开发方面的熟练人才。

例句：She's a proficient in several foreign languages.  她精通几种外语。


近义词：experts, masters, specialists, virtuosos |反义词：novices, beginners, amateurs,inept


词源: proficient (adj.) + -s (plural suffix)  Proficient itself comes from the Old French *proficient, from Latin *proficiens, present participle of *proficere "to make progress, advance," from pro- "forward" + facere "to make, do."
