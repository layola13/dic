hillside:/ˈhɪlˌsaɪd/| n. |山坡，山腰 |The sloping side of a hill.  A hillside is the side of a hill, often characterized by its incline and vegetation.| 例句：We built a cabin on the hillside. 我们在山坡上建了一间小屋。

The wildflowers bloomed profusely on the sunny hillside. 向日葵在阳光充足的山坡上盛开。


近义词：slope, incline, declivity | 反义词：summit, peak, top
