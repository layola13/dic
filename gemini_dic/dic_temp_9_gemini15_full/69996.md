fetching: /ˈfetʃɪŋ/ | adj. |迷人的；吸引人的；讨人喜欢的 |Attractive and charming; pleasing.  |例句：She wore a fetching red dress. 她穿着一件迷人的红裙子。

例句：The sunset was a fetching sight.  日落景象很迷人。


近义词：attractive, appealing, alluring, captivating, charming

反义词：unattractive, unappealing, repulsive, repellent


词源:  The word "fetching" originates from the verb "fetch," meaning to go and bring something back.  Over time, its meaning shifted to describe something that attracts or brings someone or something to it, thus acquiring its current adjectival meaning of attractive or charming. This semantic shift is an example of metaphorical extension.
