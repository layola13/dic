mesosphere:/ˈmezoʊsfɪr/| n. |中间层（大气层）|The mesosphere is the layer of the Earth's atmosphere above the stratosphere and below the thermosphere. It is characterized by decreasing temperature with increasing altitude.|例句：Meteors burn up in the mesosphere.  流星在中间层燃烧殆尽。|例句：The mesosphere is a region of the atmosphere where temperatures decrease with altitude. 中间层是大气中温度随高度降低的区域。|近义词：stratosphere, thermosphere, ionosphere|反义词：troposphere (in terms of proximity to Earth's surface)|词性:名词
