seamers:/ˈsiːmər/| n.|缝纫工；接缝；（板球）擅长投侧旋球的投球手|A seamer is a person who sews seams, a line of stitching, or in cricket, a bowler who bowls seam bowling.|例句：The experienced seamer bowled a brilliant spell.  这位经验丰富的投球手投出了一段精彩的球。|例句：She is a skilled seamer, known for her precise stitching. 她是一位熟练的缝纫工，以缝纫精确而闻名。|近义词：stitcher, bowler (in cricket context), seamstress|反义词：无
