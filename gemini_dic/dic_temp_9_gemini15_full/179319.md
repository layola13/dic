redolent:/ˈredələnt/| adj.|芬芳的；充满…气息的；使人想起…的|Having a strong smell; fragrant.  Also suggesting or evoking (a feeling, memory, or other sense impression).|例句：The air was redolent with the scent of pine needles. 空气中弥漫着松针的香味。

例句：The old house was redolent of history. 这座老房子充满了历史的气息。


近义词：fragrant, aromatic, sweet-smelling, evocative, suggestive|反义词：odorless, scentless, bland


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The room is redolent of lavender. = 房间里充满了薰衣草的香味。
This perfume is redolent of summer. = 这款香水充满了夏日的气息。


一般过去时:
The garden was redolent with the scent of roses. = 花园里充满了玫瑰的香味。
The air was redolent of rain after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，空气中充满了雨水的味道。


现在完成时:
The memory has remained redolent of childhood. = 这段记忆仍然充满了童年的气息。
The old book has become redolent with the scent of age. = 这本旧书已经充满了岁月的味道。


过去完成时:
The house had been redolent of woodsmoke before the fire went out. = 在火熄灭之前，房子里充满了柴火的烟味。
The air had been redolent with the smell of baking bread. = 空气中曾经弥漫着烘焙面包的香味。


将来时:
The garden will be redolent with the scent of lilies. = 花园里将弥漫着百合花的香味。
The kitchen will be redolent of roasting chicken. = 厨房里将充满烤鸡的香味。


虚拟语气:
If the room were redolent of roses, it would be more romantic. = 如果房间里弥漫着玫瑰的香味，就会更浪漫。
I wish my home were redolent with the scent of fresh-baked bread. = 我希望我的家充满新鲜烘焙面包的香味。


被动语态:
The air was redolent with the scent of spices. = 空气中弥漫着香料的香味。(被动语态中redolent本身不发生变化)
The atmosphere was redolent with anticipation. =  气氛中充满了期待。(被动语态中redolent本身不发生变化)


疑问句:
Is the kitchen redolent of something delicious? = 厨房里是不是弥漫着美味的食物香气？
Is the air redolent of rain? = 空气中是不是充满了雨水的味道？


否定句:
The room was not redolent of anything pleasant. = 房间里没有任何令人愉快的味道。
The air was not redolent with the scent of flowers. = 空气中没有弥漫着花香。


PHRASE->

redolent of decay = 散发出腐败的气息


词源:
From Medieval Latin *redolēns, present participle of redolēre "to smell sweetly, emit a fragrance", from re- + olēre "to smell".
