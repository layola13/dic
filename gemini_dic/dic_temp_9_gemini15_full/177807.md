receiving:/rɪˈsiːvɪŋ/| v.|接收；收到；领受|The act of taking something that is offered or given.|例句：He is receiving treatment for a serious illness. 他正在接受重病治疗。
例句：The company is receiving many applications for the job.  公司收到了很多求职申请。|近义词：getting, accepting, obtaining|反义词：sending, transmitting, dispatching


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He receives many emails every day. = 他每天收到很多邮件。
They receive a salary at the end of the month. = 他们每月月底领取工资。

一般过去时:
She received a letter from her friend. = 她收到她朋友的一封信。
I received a gift on my birthday. = 我生日收到了礼物。

现在完成时:
Have you received my email yet? = 你收到我的邮件了吗？
They have received numerous awards for their work. = 他们因他们的工作获得了无数奖项。

过去完成时:
He had received the news before I called him. = 我打电话给他之前他已经收到消息了。
She had received many complaints about the service. = 她已经收到了很多关于服务的投诉。

将来时:
I will receive my paycheck next week. = 我下周将收到我的薪水。
We will receive an answer within 24 hours. = 我们将在24小时内收到答复。

虚拟语气:
If I received a million dollars, I would travel the world. = 如果我收到一百万美元，我会环游世界。
I wish I had received your message earlier. = 我希望我早点收到你的消息。

被动语态:
The package was received yesterday. = 包裹昨天收到了。
The information is being received by the authorities. = 信息正被当局接收。

疑问句:
Did you receive my message? = 你收到我的消息了吗？
What did he receive as a gift? = 他收到了什么礼物？

否定句:
I didn't receive your invitation. = 我没有收到你的邀请。
She hasn't received any replies to her emails. = 她还没有收到任何邮件回复。

PHRASE->
receiving end = 承受者；受害者
receiving line = 接待队伍
receiving station = 受信站；接收站
receiving area = 接收区
receiving department = 收发室
