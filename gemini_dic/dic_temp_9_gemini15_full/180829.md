reharvests:/ˌriːˈhɑːrvɛsts/ | v. |再收获，二次收获 |To harvest again; to harvest a crop a second time or more.  |例句：The farmer reharvests the late-season apples for cider.  农民再次收获晚季苹果用于酿苹果酒。

例句：The company reharvests data from its customer database to improve marketing strategies. 公司再次收集客户数据库中的数据以改进营销策略。


近义词：reap, gather, collect (in the context of harvesting) | 反义词：plant, sow (opposite actions) | 词性: Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer reharvests the crop every year. = 农民每年都二次收获庄稼。
They reharvest the data for analysis. = 他们重新收集数据进行分析。

一般过去时:
The team reharvested the data last week. = 团队上周重新收集了数据。
She reharvested the herbs after the rain. = 雨后她再次收割了药草。

现在完成时:
We have reharvested the fields twice this season. = 这个季节我们已经二次收割了田地。
They have reharvested the information for a new report. = 他们已经为一份新的报告重新收集了信息。

过去完成时:
The farmer had reharvested the wheat before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，农民已经二次收割了小麦。
They had reharvested the data before the presentation. = 他们在演示文稿之前已经重新收集了数据。

将来时:
We will reharvest the grapes next month. = 我们下个月将再次采摘葡萄。
They will reharvest the information for the updated study. = 他们将为更新的研究重新收集信息。

虚拟语气:
If we reharvested the crop, we would have more yield. = 如果我们再次收割庄稼，我们的产量会更高。
I wish we had reharvested the herbs before the frost. = 我希望我们在霜冻前再次收割了药草。

被动语态:
The data was reharvested by the research team. = 数据由研究团队重新收集。
The crops are being reharvested for the second time. = 庄稼正在进行第二次收割。

疑问句:
Will they reharvest the soybeans this year? = 他们今年还会再次收割大豆吗？
Have you reharvested the information yet? = 你已经重新收集信息了吗？

否定句:
They did not reharvest the corn this season. = 他们这个季节没有再次收割玉米。
We will not reharvest the data until next week. = 我们直到下周才会重新收集数据。
