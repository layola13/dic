megacities:/ˈmɛɡəˌsɪtiz/| n. |特大城市，巨型城市|A megacity is a very large city, typically one with a population of over 10 million people.  Megacities are characterized by their immense size, complex infrastructure, and significant influence on global affairs. |Example: Tokyo is one of the world's most populous megacities.  东京是世界上人口最多的特大城市之一。


Example:  Many megacities face challenges related to overpopulation, pollution, and resource management. 许多特大城市面临着人口过剩、污染和资源管理方面的挑战。|近义词：metropolis, large city, global city|反义词：small town, village
