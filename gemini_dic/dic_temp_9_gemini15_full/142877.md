out-of-plumb: /ˌaʊt əv plʌm/ | adj. | 不垂直的；不笔直的；歪斜的 | Not perfectly vertical or straight; leaning or tilting.  | The leaning tower of Pisa is famously out-of-plumb. 比萨斜塔以其倾斜而闻名。 The picture on the wall is out-of-plumb. 墙上的画歪了。 |近义词：askew, crooked, tilted, slanted |反义词：plumb, straight, vertical, level


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The fence is out-of-plumb. = 栅栏不垂直。
That tower is always out-of-plumb. = 那座塔总是倾斜的。


一般过去时:
The shelf was out-of-plumb after the earthquake. = 地震后架子歪了。
The painting was out-of-plumb, so I straightened it. = 画歪了，所以我把它扶正了。


现在完成时:
The house has been out-of-plumb for years. = 这房子多年来一直歪斜。
The old building has become increasingly out-of-plumb. = 这座旧建筑越来越倾斜了。


过去完成时:
The wall had been out-of-plumb before the renovation. = 在装修之前，墙就歪了。
The tower had been out-of-plumb for centuries. = 这座塔已经倾斜了几个世纪。


将来时:
The new building will not be out-of-plumb. = 新建筑不会歪斜。
If we don't fix it, the fence will become out-of-plumb. = 如果我们不修理，栅栏会变得歪斜。


虚拟语气:
If the building were out-of-plumb, it would be dangerous. = 如果建筑物倾斜，那就危险了。
I wish the bookcase weren't out-of-plumb. = 我希望书柜不要歪斜。


被动语态:
The wall was found to be out-of-plumb during the inspection. = 在检查过程中发现墙是歪的。
The leaning tower is often photographed because it is out-of-plumb. = 因为比萨斜塔倾斜，所以经常被拍照。


疑问句:
Is the door out-of-plumb? = 门歪了吗？
Are those shelves out-of-plumb? = 那些架子歪了吗？


否定句:
The new building is not out-of-plumb. = 新建筑没有歪斜。
The picture is not out-of-plumb; it's perfectly straight. = 图片没有歪，它是完全笔直的。


PHRASE->
out-of-plumb building = 倾斜的建筑
out-of-plumb wall = 歪斜的墙
out-of-plumb structure = 不垂直的结构
out-of-plumb chimney = 倾斜的烟囱
out-of-plumb alignment = 不垂直的对准

词源:

The word comes from the use of a plumb bob (a weight on a string used to determine verticality).  "Out of plumb" literally means "not aligned with a plumb bob," indicating something is not vertical.
