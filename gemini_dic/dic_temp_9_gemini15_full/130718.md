/nɒnˌælɪˈɡeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非指控；未指控 | The absence of allegations; the state of not having any accusations made.  This word is a relatively rare and newly coined term, formed by combining "non-" (meaning "not") with "allegations."  It implies a lack of accusations or claims of wrongdoing. | Example:  The investigation concluded with nonallegations against the suspect.  调查以对嫌疑人没有指控而告终。 | 近义词: lack of accusations, absence of charges, no claims | 反义词: allegations, accusations, charges
