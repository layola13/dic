jeweller:/ˈdʒuːələr/| n. |珠宝商，珠宝匠|A jeweller is a person who makes, repairs, or sells jewellery.  They work with precious metals and gemstones to create beautiful and valuable pieces.|例句：The jeweller carefully appraised the diamond necklace.  这位珠宝商仔细评估了那条钻石项链。|例句：She took her broken bracelet to the jeweller for repair. 她把断了的手镯拿到珠宝店修理。|近义词：jeweler, goldsmith|反义词：无
