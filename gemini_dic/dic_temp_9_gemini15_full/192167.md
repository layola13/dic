scrapable: /ˈskreɪpəbl/ | adj. | 可刮除的，可清除的 |  Able to be scraped or removed by scraping.  Something that is scrapable can have its surface layer removed easily by scraping. | Example: The paint was scrapable, so we were able to remove it without damaging the underlying wood.  这层油漆可以刮掉，所以我们能够在不损坏下面的木头的情况下把它清除掉。| 近义词：removable,  extractable | 反义词：indestructible, unremovable
