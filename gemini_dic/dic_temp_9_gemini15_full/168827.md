productor:/prəˈdjuːsər/ | n. | 制片人；生产者；生产商 | A productor is a person who is in charge of the finances and organization of a film, play, or television program.  It can also refer to an organism that produces something, such as a plant producing oxygen. | Example: Steven Spielberg is a famous film productor. 史蒂文·斯皮尔伯格是一位著名的电影制片人。  The farmer is a productor of organic vegetables. 这个农民是生产有机蔬菜的生产商。|近义词：producer, manufacturer, originator |反义词：consumer, user | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The producer oversees the entire project. = 制片人负责整个项目。
She produces high-quality goods. = 她生产高质量的商品。


一般过去时:
He produced a successful film last year. = 他去年制作了一部成功的电影。
The factory produced thousands of cars. = 工厂生产了数千辆汽车。


现在完成时:
They have produced several award-winning documentaries. = 他们已经制作了几部获奖纪录片。
The company has produced innovative products. = 这家公司已经生产出创新产品。


过去完成时:

将来时:
We will produce a new album next year. = 我们明年将制作一张新专辑。
The company will produce more electric vehicles. = 公司将生产更多电动汽车。


虚拟语气:

I wish I could produce my own music. = 我真希望我能自己制作音乐。

疑问句:
