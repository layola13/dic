crackers:/ˈkrækərz/| n.|脆饼干，爆竹，疯子|Thin, crisp biscuits, usually salty; devices that explode with a loud bang; informal term for a crazy person. |例句：We had crackers and cheese for a snack. 我们零食吃了脆饼干和奶酪。  The children lit the crackers to celebrate. 孩子们燃放爆竹庆祝。 He's a bit of a cracker; you have to watch him carefully. 他有点疯，你得小心看着他。|近义词：biscuit, firecracker, lunatic|反义词：cake(in the context of texture), quiet person
