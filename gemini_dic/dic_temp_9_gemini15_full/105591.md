kindling: /ˈkɪndəlɪŋ/ | n. |引火物，柴火；点燃的欲望|Easily combustible material, such as small twigs or dry leaves, used to start a fire.  The act of igniting something; the state of being excited or stimulated.|The campfire was quickly started with some dry kindling. |近义词：tinder, fuel, tinderwood |反义词：extinguisher


例句：

He gathered kindling to start a bonfire. 他收集引火物来生篝火。

The dry leaves served as excellent kindling. 干树叶是很好的引火物。


词源:

源自古英语 *kyndelen，意思是“点燃”。
