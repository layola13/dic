flow's:/floʊz/| n. |流动；流程；流畅；|The flow of the river is very strong. 河流的流速很快。|近义词：stream, current, course |反义词：stagnation, standstill


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river flows smoothly. = 河水流畅地流淌。
The traffic flows well this morning. = 今早交通状况良好。

一般过去时:
The water flowed rapidly after the rain. = 雨后水流湍急。
The project flowed smoothly until the last minute. = 项目进展顺利，直到最后一刻。

现在完成时:
The river has flowed this way for centuries. = 这条河已经这样流淌了几个世纪。
The information has flowed freely since the internet was invented. = 自从互联网发明以来，信息传播自由。

过去完成时:
The river had flowed over its banks before the dam was built. = 在建造水坝之前，河水已经漫过河岸。
The project had flowed smoothly until unexpected problems emerged. = 项目进展顺利，直到出现意外问题。

将来时:
The river will flow faster in the spring. = 春天河水会流得更快。
The data will flow seamlessly between the two systems. = 数据将在两个系统之间无缝流动。

虚拟语气:
If the river flowed faster, it would erode the banks more quickly. = 如果河水流得更快，它就会更快地侵蚀河岸。
If the project had flowed more smoothly, we would have finished earlier. = 如果项目进展更顺利，我们就能早点完成。

被动语态:
The water is flowed through the pipes. = 水通过管道流动。
The information is flowed to the public. = 信息被传播给公众。

疑问句:
How does the river flow? = 河水怎么流？
Does the traffic flow freely? = 交通是否畅通？

否定句:
The river doesn't flow in that direction. = 河水不朝那个方向流。
The ideas didn't flow easily during the meeting. = 会议期间思路并不流畅。
