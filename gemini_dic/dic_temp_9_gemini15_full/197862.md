shiplet:/ˈʃɪplət/| n. |芯粒，小芯片|A small, independent chip, often part of a larger system-on-a-chip (SoC).  It's a relatively new term in the semiconductor industry, referring to a functional unit within a larger chip. Shiplets allow for greater design flexibility and potentially faster manufacturing processes.| Example:  The new processor uses several shiplets to improve performance and reduce manufacturing complexity.  新的处理器使用多个芯粒来提高性能并降低制造复杂性。|近义词：die, microchip (but with a strong emphasis on being a part of a larger system)|反义词：None directly applicable, as it describes a component rather than a concept with an opposite.|名词
