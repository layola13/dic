secularised: /ˈsɛkjʊləraɪzd/ | adj. | 世俗化的；非宗教化的 |  Made worldly or temporal; not subject to or bound by religious rule; free from religious influence.  | 例句：Many European countries have become increasingly secularised in recent decades. 近年来，许多欧洲国家日益世俗化。 | 近义词：nonreligious, worldly, profane | 反义词：religious, sacred, spiritual
