predefence: /ˌpriːdɪˈfɛns/ | n. | 预答辩 | The pre-defence is a trial run of the PhD thesis defence, allowing the candidate to receive feedback before the official defence.  It is an opportunity to identify weaknesses in the research and presentation before the final examination.  | The pre-defence was helpful in identifying gaps in my analysis.  |  trial run, rehearsal | final defence
