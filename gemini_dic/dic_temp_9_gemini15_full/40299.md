continuum:/kənˈtɪnjuəm/ | n. | 连续统；连续体 | A continuous sequence or range.  It often refers to something that changes gradually without noticeable breaks or interruptions.  | Example:  The evolution of language is a continuum, not a series of distinct stages.  语言的演变是一个连续统一体，而不是一系列截然不同的阶段。 |近义词：sequence, range, spectrum | 反义词：discontinuity, interruption
