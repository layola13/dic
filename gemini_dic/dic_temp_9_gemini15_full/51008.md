decision: /dɪˈsɪʒən/ | n. | 决定，决议 | The act of deciding or determining something; a conclusion reached after consideration.  | Example:  The committee reached a decision after a lengthy debate.  委员会经过长时间的辩论后做出了决定。  |近义词：determination, resolution, verdict | 反义词：indecision, uncertainty | 名词
