milter:/ˈmɪltər/| n.|邮件过滤器，垃圾邮件过滤器|A milter is a program that filters email messages, typically to prevent spam or viruses from being delivered.  It acts as an intermediary between a mail server and its clients, inspecting messages before they are accepted or sent.|Example: The email server uses a milter to block unsolicited commercial email.|近义词：spam filter, email filter|反义词：无|名词
