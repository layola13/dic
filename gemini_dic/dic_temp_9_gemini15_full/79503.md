globe:/ɡloʊb/|n.|地球，世界，球体，天球|A globe is a spherical model of Earth, showing its surface features such as countries, oceans, and mountains. It can also refer to a sphere representing a celestial body or a system of knowledge. |例句：The geography teacher used a globe to show the students the location of different countries.  地理老师用地球仪向学生展示不同国家的地理位置。

例句：The company operates on a global scale. 这家公司在全球范围内运营。


近义词：sphere, world, earth, planet|反义词：N/A
