damascus:/dəˈmæskəs/|/ˈdæməsəs/| n. |大马士革|The capital and largest city of Syria, known for its rich history and cultural heritage. Damascus is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world, with a history spanning millennia.  It is famous for its steel, textiles, and its role as a significant trading center along the Silk Road.|例句：Damascus is a city steeped in history. = 大马士革是一个历史悠久的城市。|例句：We visited the ancient souks of Damascus. = 我们参观了大马士革古老的集市。|近义词：Aleppo (another major Syrian city)|反义词：None|名词
