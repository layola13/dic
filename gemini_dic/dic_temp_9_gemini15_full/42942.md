coventry:/ˈkʌvən(t)ri/| n. |考文垂（英国城市）；(to send someone to Coventry) 联合抵制；冷落 |The city of Coventry in central England.  To be sent to Coventry is to be deliberately ignored or ostracized by a group. |例句：He was sent to Coventry after he betrayed his friends. 他背叛朋友后被大家冷落了。|近义词：ostracize, boycott, shun |反义词：include, welcome
