crank-in:/kræŋkˈɪn/| n. v.|电影开拍；（尤指演员）进组|The start of filming, especially when an actor begins work on a film.  |例句：The crank-in date for the movie is next Monday. 这部电影的开拍日期是下周一。 The lead actress finally did her crank-in after a long delay.  女主角在长时间的延误后终于进组了。|近义词： commencement of filming, start of shooting, filming start|反义词：crank-out, wrap
