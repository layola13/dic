multinationally:/ˌmʌltiˈneɪʃənəli/| adv.| 多国地，跨国地 | Operating across multiple national borders; involving many nations.  | Example: The company operates multinationally, with offices in several countries. 这家公司在多个国家设有办事处，业务遍布全球。 |近义词：internationally, transnationally | 反义词：locally, domestically
