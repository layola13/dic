horseback:/ˈhɔːrsbæk/| n.|骑马；马背|The act or practice of riding a horse; the back of a horse.  It refers to both the act of riding and the location on a horse.|例句：She rode horseback across the fields. 她骑马穿过田野。

例句：He fell off his horseback. 他从马背上摔了下来。|近义词：equestrianism, horsemanship|反义词：walking, cycling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rides horseback every morning. 他每天早上骑马。
They often go horseback riding in the park. 他们经常去公园骑马。

一般过去时:
She rode horseback to the village yesterday. 她昨天骑马去了村庄。
We went horseback riding on the beach last summer. 我们去年夏天在海滩骑马。

现在完成时:
I have ridden horseback many times. 我骑过很多次马了。
They have never been horseback riding before. 他们以前从未骑过马。

过去完成时:
He had ridden horseback before he joined the army. 他参军之前骑过马。
She had never been horseback riding until she visited the ranch. 直到她参观了牧场，她才骑过马。

将来时:
We will ride horseback in the mountains next week. 我们下周将在山上骑马。
They will go horseback riding after the wedding. 婚礼结束后他们将去骑马。

虚拟语气:
If I had a horse, I would ride horseback every day. 如果我有一匹马，我每天都会骑马。
I wish I could ride horseback like a professional. 我希望我能像专业人士一样骑马。

被动语态:
The horse was ridden horseback by the experienced rider. 这匹马被经验丰富的骑手骑着。 (This sentence is somewhat awkward,  passive voice is less common with "horseback")
The trail is often used for horseback riding. 这条小路经常被用来骑马。(The focus is on the trail, not the action of riding)

疑问句:
Do you like riding horseback? 你喜欢骑马吗？
Have you ever ridden horseback before? 你以前骑过马吗？

否定句:
He doesn't ride horseback very often. 他不经常骑马。
She hasn't been horseback riding in years. 她多年没骑过马了。
