nonwork:/ˈnɒnwɜːrk/| n. |非工作时间；业余时间；非工作活动|Time or activities not related to one's job or profession.  It often refers to leisure time, personal pursuits, or unpaid activities.|例句：I spend my nonwork hours volunteering at the animal shelter. 我把非工作时间都用来在动物收容所做志愿者。|例句：Her nonwork activities include painting and gardening. 她的业余活动包括绘画和园艺。|近义词：leisure time, free time, downtime, spare time|反义词：work, job, employment|名词
