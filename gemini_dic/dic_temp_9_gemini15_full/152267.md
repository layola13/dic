patronly:/ˈpætrənli/ | adj. | 施舍似的；以恩主的姿态；高傲的，傲慢的 | Displaying or characterized by a condescendingly kind or benevolent manner; superior, as if granting a favor.  |  He spoke to the employees in a patronly tone, making them feel belittled. 他用一种施舍似的语气对员工说话，使他们感到被轻视。  | condescending, superior, lofty | humble, respectful |


例句2:  She offered help in a patronly way, which offended him. 她以一种高傲的姿态提供帮助，这让他生气了。


词源:  Derived from "patron," meaning a person who gives support or protection to someone or something, often in a condescending or superior way.  The suffix "-ly" transforms the noun into an adjective, describing a manner or quality.


用法说明：

patronly通常用来形容带有优越感、居高临下、甚至轻蔑的施舍或帮助。它强调的是行为者在给予帮助时所表现出的傲慢态度，而非帮助本身的善意。


近义词和反义词的选择应根据具体的语境而定。 例如，“condescending”更强调的是轻蔑和居高临下的态度，“superior”更强调的是优越感，“lofty”更强调的是高傲和自大。 反义词则需要表达谦逊、尊重等相反的含义。
