ecumenical:/ˌɛkjuˈmɛnɪkəl/| adj.|大公的；普世的；具有世界性的|Relating to or promoting unity among different churches or Christian denominations.  It emphasizes the common beliefs and practices shared by various Christian groups, aiming to overcome divisions and work together for common goals. |The ecumenical council addressed issues of faith and unity among different Christian churches.  大公会议讨论了不同基督教教会之间的信仰和团结问题。|universal, catholic, interdenominational|divisive, sectarian|形容词
