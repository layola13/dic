silence:/ˈsaɪləns/ | n. |沉默，寂静 |The absence of sound; stillness.  It can also refer to a period of quiet or the act of keeping quiet. |例句：The silence in the room was deafening.  房间里的寂静令人震耳欲聋。

例句：She requested silence during the lecture.  她在讲座期间要求保持安静。


近义词：quiet, hush, stillness, calm, peace |反义词：noise, sound, uproar, clamor, tumult


词源：源自古法语 *silence  和拉丁语 *silentium，意为“沉默，安静”。
