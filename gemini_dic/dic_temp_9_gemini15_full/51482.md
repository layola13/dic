detoxify:/dɪˈtɒksɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 解毒；排毒 | To remove poisons or toxins from something, especially the body.  | 例句：The doctor recommended a detoxifying diet to cleanse her system. 医生建议她采用排毒饮食来清洁身体系统。 | 例句：The treatment aims to detoxify the patient's liver.  治疗旨在为病人解肝毒。 | 近义词：purify, cleanse, clean | 反义词：poison, contaminate


USAGE->
