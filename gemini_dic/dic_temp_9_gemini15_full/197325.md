shaving:/ˈʃeɪvɪŋ/ | n. v. | 剃须；刮毛；削；切成薄片 | The act of removing hair from the body, usually with a razor; the process of cutting something into thin slices.  | 例句：He enjoys his morning shaving routine. 他喜欢他早晨的剃须程序。  
He needs a shaving brush. 他需要一把剃须刷。

近义词：hair removal, trimming, scraping | 反义词：growing (hair)
