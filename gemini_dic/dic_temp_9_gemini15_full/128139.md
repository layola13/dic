netiquettes:/ˈnɛtɪkɛt/| n. |网络礼仪|Rules of etiquette that apply to the Internet.  These rules govern online communication and behavior, promoting respectful and considerate interactions among users.  They cover various aspects of online interactions, including email, social media, forums, and online gaming.|Example:  Good netiquette dictates that you should proofread your emails before sending them.  良好的网络礼仪要求你在发送电子邮件之前校对。|近义词：online etiquette, internet etiquette, cyber etiquette|反义词：online rudeness, cyberbullying|名词
