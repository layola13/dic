skeletonizer: /ˈskɛlətənaɪzər/ | n. |骨架化者；使成骨架状之物 |A skeletonizer is something or someone that reduces something to a skeletal form or structure, often by removing non-essential parts. This term is often used in biology and entomology to refer to insects that consume the soft tissues of leaves, leaving behind only the skeletal framework of veins.  |例句：The leaf miner is a type of skeletonizer insect.  这种潜叶虫是一种骨架化昆虫。  The artist created a skeletonizer sculpture by removing the inner layers of wood.艺术家通过去除木材的内层创造了一个骨架状雕塑。|近义词：defoliator, skeletonizer insect |反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be terms related to growth or preservation.
