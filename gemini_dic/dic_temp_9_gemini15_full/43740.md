creeling:/ˈkriːlɪŋ/| n. |用钓丝钓鱼，用罾捕鱼|The act of fishing with a creel, a type of basket used to carry fish.  Creeling typically involves using a line and hook, or sometimes a small net. |例句：He spent the afternoon creeling for trout in the stream. 他在小溪里钓鳟鱼度过了下午。|近义词：fishing, angling|反义词：None specifically.
