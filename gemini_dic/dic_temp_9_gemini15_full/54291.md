disgustedness:/dɪsˈɡʌstɪdnəs/| n. |厌恶感，反感|A feeling of strong dislike or disapproval caused by something unpleasant or offensive.  It often involves a sense of revulsion or disgust. |例句：Her disgustedness was palpable as she watched him eat with his fingers. 她看着那个人用手指吃饭，厌恶之情溢于言表。

例句：The sight of the rotting food filled her with disgustedness.  腐烂的食物让她感到极度厌恶。|近义词：repulsion, aversion, loathing, abhorrence |反义词：delight, pleasure, enjoyment |词性:名词
