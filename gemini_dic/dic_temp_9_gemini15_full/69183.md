faze:/feɪz/ | vt. | 使不安；使困窘；使惊慌 | To disturb or disconcert someone; to deter or dismay. |The news didn't faze him.  = 这消息没有使他不安。|Nothing could faze her determination. = 什么都不能动摇她的决心。|近义词：disconcert, unsettle, perturb | 反义词：calm, reassure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud noise doesn't faze me. = 响亮的声音不会让我不安。
Does the criticism faze him? = 批评会让他不安吗？


一般过去时:
The unexpected question fazed her. =  意外的问题让她慌了神。
The storm didn't faze the experienced sailors. = 暴风雨没有让经验丰富的船员们惊慌。


现在完成时:
Nothing has fazed her throughout this difficult project. = 在这个困难的项目中，没有什么能让她不安。
The challenges have fazed some of the team members. = 这些挑战让一些团队成员感到不安。


过去完成时:
The earlier setbacks had fazed him slightly before the final match. = 之前的挫折在决赛前让他有点不安。
She had been fazed by his aggressive behavior, so she didn't answer. = 她被他咄咄逼人的行为吓到了，所以没有回答。


将来时:
I don't think anything will faze her in the future. = 我认为将来没有什么能让她不安。
The upcoming presentation might faze some students. = 即将到来的演示可能会让一些学生感到不安。


虚拟语气:
If that news didn't faze him, nothing would. = 如果那消息都没让他不安，那么什么都不会。
I wish that the pressure didn't faze her so much. = 我希望压力不会让她这么不安。


被动语态:
He wasn't fazed by their threats. = 他没有被他们的威胁吓倒。
She was fazed by the unexpected turn of events. = 她被意想不到的事件转折弄得不知所措。


疑问句:
Does the thought of public speaking faze you? = 公开演讲的想法会让你不安吗？
Will the difficult exam faze the students? = 困难的考试会让学生们不安吗？


否定句:
The difficult task didn't faze her at all. = 艰巨的任务根本没有让她不安。
He wasn't fazed by the criticism. = 他没有被批评吓倒。


PHRASE->
be fazed by = 被...吓倒
not fazed by = 不受...影响
completely fazed = 完全不知所措
slightly fazed = 有点不安
utterly fazed = 完全惊慌失措
