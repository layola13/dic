oxidizement:/ˌɒksɪˈdaɪzmənt/| n. |氧化作用，氧化；氧化过程|The process of oxidizing or being oxidized.  It involves the loss of electrons by a molecule, atom, or ion. Oxidizement is often accompanied by a change in oxidation state.|例句：The oxidizement of iron produces rust.  铁的氧化作用会产生锈蚀。

The oxidizement of the metal was accelerated by the presence of moisture. 金属的氧化作用由于潮湿而加速。|近义词：oxidation, rusting |反义词：reduction
