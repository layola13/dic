cable-car: /ˈkeɪblˌkɑːr/ | n. |缆车|A cable car is a type of vehicle that is suspended from and pulled along by a cable.  It is often used to transport people up and down steep hills or mountains. |例句：We took a cable car up to the top of the mountain. 我们乘坐缆车到达山顶。|例句：The cable car ride offered breathtaking views of the valley. 缆车之旅提供了令人叹为观止的山谷景色。|近义词：aerial tramway, funicular |反义词：None
