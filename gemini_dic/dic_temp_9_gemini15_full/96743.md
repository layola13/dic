ineligibly:/ɪˈnɛlɪdʒəbli/ | adv. | 不合格地，无资格地 |  In a way that makes someone ineligible.  It describes an action or state that renders someone unqualified or unsuitable. | The application was ineligibly filled out, leading to its rejection.  申请填写不合格，导致被拒绝。|  unsuitably, improperly | eligibly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Instead, it modifies verbs and describes how an action is performed.

Example 1: He answered the question ineligibly, making his response unclear. 他含糊不清地回答了问题，使他的回答不清楚。
Example 2: She was ineligibly chosen for the team due to her lack of experience. 由于缺乏经验，她被不合格地选入球队。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases directly using "ineligibly".  The word is used independently to modify verbs describing actions leading to ineligibility.
