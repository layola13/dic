identifiable:/ˌaɪˈdɛntɪfaɪəbl/| adj.| 可识别的，可辨认的 | Able to be recognized or identified.  |例句：The thief was identifiable from the security footage.  小偷在安全录像中是可识别的。 |例句：The bird's song was easily identifiable.  这只鸟的歌声很容易辨认。|近义词：recognizable, distinguishable, perceivable |反义词：unidentifiable, unrecognizable, indistinguishable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The suspect is identifiable by his distinctive scar. = 嫌疑人可以通过他独特的伤疤被认出来。
This plant is identifiable by its unique leaves. = 这种植物可以通过它独特的叶子来识别。

一般过去时:
The painting was identifiable as a forgery. = 这幅画被认定为赝品。
The voice on the recording was identifiable as belonging to the witness. = 录音中的声音被认定属于证人。

现在完成时:
The body has been identifiable as that of the missing professor. = 该尸体已被确认是失踪教授的尸体。
The cause of the accident has been identifiable as driver error. = 事故原因已被确定为驾驶员错误。

过去完成时:
The stolen car had been identifiable through its license plate number. = 被盗车辆已通过车牌号码被识别。
The culprit had been identifiable from a previous case file. = 罪犯已从之前的案卷中被识别出来。

将来时:
The new product will be identifiable by its bright packaging. = 新产品将通过其鲜艳的包装来识别。
The location will be identifiable on the map. = 位置在地图上是可以识别的。

虚拟语气:
If the signature were identifiable, we could solve the mystery. = 如果签名可以识别，我们就能解开这个谜团。
If the cause had been identifiable earlier, the tragedy could have been prevented. = 如果原因能早些确定，悲剧本来是可以避免的。

被动语态:
The missing child was identifiable by his distinctive birthmark. = 失踪儿童可以通过他独特的胎记被认出来。
The source of the contamination was identifiable through laboratory analysis. = 污染源通过实验室分析被确定。

疑问句:
Is the suspect identifiable from the witness testimony? = 嫌疑人能从证人证词中被认出来吗？
Is the painting identifiable as a masterpiece? = 这幅画能被认定为杰作吗？

否定句:
The handwriting was not identifiable. = 笔迹无法辨认。
The attacker was not identifiable from the blurry security footage. = 从模糊的安全录像中无法辨认出袭击者。
