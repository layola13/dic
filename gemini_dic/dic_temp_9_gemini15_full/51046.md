desolated:/ˈdɛsəleɪtɪd/| adj.|荒凉的；凄凉的；孤寂的|  Desolated describes a place that is deserted and in a state of ruin or decay, often implying a feeling of sadness and loneliness. It can also refer to someone who is feeling extremely unhappy and alone. |例句：The earthquake left the town desolated. 地震使小镇变得一片荒凉。

The desolated landscape offered little hope of survival. 荒凉的景象几乎不给人留下生存的希望。

近义词：barren, desolate, deserted, lonely, forlorn, devastated|反义词：populated, thriving, lively, cheerful, happy|词性：形容词
