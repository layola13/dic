since:/sɪns/| conj. prep. adv.|自从；因为；既然|

英文解析:
As a conjunction, "since" introduces a clause of time or reason.  It indicates that an event or state started in the past and continues to the present (temporal since) or that a reason or cause exists (causal since). As a preposition, "since" indicates a point in time from which something has been happening. As an adverb, it means "from then until now" or "ago".


例句:
Since I was a child, I've loved animals. = 从我还是个孩子的时候起，我就喜欢动物了。

I haven't seen him since last year. = 从去年以来我就没见过他了。

Since you're here, why don't you stay for dinner? = 既然你来了，为什么不留下来吃晚饭呢？

I've been working here since 2010. = 自从2010年以来，我一直在那儿工作。

It's been a long time since we last met. = 自从我们上次见面已经很久了。


近义词:
because, as, from, ever since, because of


反义词:
until, before, although


词性:
conjunction, preposition, adverb
