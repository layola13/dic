radiochemistry:/ˌreɪdiəʊˈkɛmɪstri/| n. |放射化学|The science that deals with the chemical properties and reactions of radioactive substances.  It involves the study of radioactive isotopes, their production, separation, and applications in various fields.  |例句：Radiochemistry plays a crucial role in nuclear medicine. 放射化学在核医学中起着至关重要的作用。|例句：Researchers are using radiochemistry techniques to study the environmental fate of pollutants. 研究人员正在使用放射化学技术来研究污染物的环境归宿。|近义词：nuclear chemistry|反义词：无
