counterjumper:/ˈkaʊntərdʒʌmpər/| n.|柜台后面的人，店员，售货员|A counterjumper is a shop assistant or salesperson who works behind a counter.  The term is now largely considered dated and somewhat derogatory.|例句：In the old days, the counterjumper was often seen as a lowly member of society.  = 在过去，柜台后面的人常被看作社会地位低下的人。

He was a counterjumper at a department store before he started his own business. = 在他创业之前，他是一家百货公司的店员。|近义词：shop assistant, salesperson, clerk|反义词：customer, shopper
