nestling: /ˈneslɪŋ/ | n. |雏鸟；幼兽| A young bird that has not yet left its nest.  |例句：The nestling opened its beak wide, begging for food.  雏鸟张开嘴，乞求食物。|例句： We found a nestling fallen from its nest. 我们发现一只从巢里掉下来的雏鸟。|近义词：chick, young bird, fledgling |反义词：adult bird
