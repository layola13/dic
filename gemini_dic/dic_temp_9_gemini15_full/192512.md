scrutation:/ˌskruːˈteɪʃən/| n. |仔细检查；详细审查；监视 |The act of examining or inspecting something closely and thoroughly.  It often implies a critical or searching examination, looking for flaws or hidden meanings. |例句：The proposal was subjected to intense scrutiny before it was approved.  这项提案在获得批准之前受到了严格的审查。

The police investigation involved a thorough scrutiny of all the evidence. 警方调查对所有证据进行了彻底的审查。
