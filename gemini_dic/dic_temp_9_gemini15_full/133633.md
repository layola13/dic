nonforests:/ˈnɒnˌfɒrɪsts/ | n. | 非森林地区，非森林地带 | Areas of land that are not forested.  These areas can include grasslands, deserts, tundra, and other non-wooded landscapes. | Example: The study focused on the biodiversity of nonforests in the region. 这项研究关注该地区非森林地区的生物多样性。|近义词：open land, non-wooded areas, grasslands, deserts (depending on context)|反义词：forests, woodlands, woods|名词


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense, voice, and mood are not applicable.  However, we can show usage in different sentence structures:


Declarative Sentence:
The nonforests were surprisingly diverse. = 非森林地区出奇地多样化。
Many species thrive in nonforests. = 许多物种在非森林地区繁盛。

Interrogative Sentence:
Are these areas classified as nonforests? = 这些地区是否被归类为非森林地区？
What are the dominant features of the local nonforests? = 当地非森林地区的主要特征是什么？

Exclamatory Sentence:
How vast the nonforests are! = 非森林地区多么广阔！
The beauty of the nonforests is breathtaking! = 非森林地区的美景令人叹为观止！

Negative Sentence:
These plains are not nonforests; they are grasslands. = 这些平原并非非森林地区；它们是草原。
The study did not include all types of nonforests. = 这项研究并未涵盖所有类型的非森林地区。
