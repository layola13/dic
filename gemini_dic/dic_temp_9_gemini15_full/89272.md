homocercal:/ˌhoʊməˈsɜːrkl/| adj.| 正尾形的；等尾形的 | Having a tail fin that is symmetrical, with the upper and lower lobes approximately equal in size and shape.  This is the typical tail fin shape of most bony fishes. | Example:  Most teleost fish possess a homocercal tail.  大多数硬骨鱼拥有正尾形的尾鳍。| Near synonyms: symmetrical, isocercal (although isocercal is more specifically referring to the internal structure)| Antonyms: heterocercal, diphycercal |
