pistillidium:/pɪsˈtɪlɪdiəm/| n. |雌器苞，颈卵器|The pistillidium is the female reproductive organ in bryophytes (mosses, liverworts, and hornworts).  It is a flask-shaped structure that produces archegonia, which contain the egg cells.  |例句：The mature pistillidium of this moss species is easily identifiable by its elongated neck.  这个苔藓物种成熟的雌器苞很容易通过其细长的颈部识别。

The archegonia within the pistillidium are fertilized by sperm released from antheridia. 雌器苞内的颈卵器被来自精子器释放的精子受精。|近义词：archegoniophore (in some contexts)|反义词：antheridium|
