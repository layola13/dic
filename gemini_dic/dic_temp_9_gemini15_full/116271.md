massasauga:/ˌmæsəˈsɔːɡə/| n.|东部响尾蛇|A venomous pit viper of the genus Sistrurus, found in eastern North America.  It is characterized by its relatively small size and distinctive rattle.  Different subspecies exist across its range.|例句：The massasauga rattlesnake is a protected species in many areas. 东部响尾蛇在许多地区是受保护物种。|例句：Several massasaugas were sighted near the wetland. 在湿地附近发现了数条东部响尾蛇。|近义词：rattlesnake (a more general term)|反义词：无
