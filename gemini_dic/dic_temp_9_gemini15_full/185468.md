reunifications:/ˌriːjuːnɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |重新统一，统一行为|The process of bringing together things that were previously separated, especially countries or groups of people.  This term often refers to the act of reuniting previously divided entities, particularly nations or political groups.  |例句：The reunifications of Germany after the Cold War were celebrated worldwide.  二战后德国的重新统一在全世界受到庆祝。

The ongoing reunifications of families separated by conflict are a testament to human resilience.  持续不断的冲突导致的家庭团聚是人类韧性的证明。


近义词：unifications, mergers, consolidations |反义词：divisions, separations, fragmentations |名词
