demo's:/ˈdɛmoʊz/| n. |演示；样品；样机|demonstration的缩写形式，指演示、样品或样机。  它通常指软件、产品或服务的演示版本。|例句：The company gave us a demo of their new software. 公司给我们演示了他们的新软件。
例句：We saw a demo of the car at the auto show. 我们在车展上看到了这款车的样车。|近义词：demonstration, sample, prototype|反义词：finished product, final version


USAGE->
This word is a shortened form of "demonstrations," and thus does not have verb conjugations like "is," "was," etc.  It functions as a noun.  Therefore, typical verb tenses are not applicable.  Instead, we will show example sentences using different grammatical structures with the noun "demos."


一般现在时(with a linking verb):
The demos are impressive. = 演示很令人印象深刻。
The demos are available online. = 演示版可在网上获得。


一般过去时(with a linking verb):
The demos were well-received. = 演示很受欢迎。
The demos were short and to the point. = 演示简短扼要。

疑问句:
