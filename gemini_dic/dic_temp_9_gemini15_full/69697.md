fenians:/ˈfiːniənz/| n.|芬尼安人，爱尔兰共和兄弟会成员|Members of the Irish Republican Brotherhood, a secret society dedicated to achieving Irish independence from British rule, particularly active in the 19th century.  The name derives from the ancient Irish warrior band, the Fianna. |The Fenians were responsible for a number of bombings and other acts of violence in the late 19th century.  |近义词：Irish Republican, Irish nationalist|反义词：British loyalist
