finagler:/fɪˈnæɡlər/| n. v.|欺骗者；耍手段者；(尤指通过巧妙手段)获得…的人|A finagler is someone who uses clever or dishonest methods to achieve something.  They often manipulate situations or people to get what they want. |例句：He's a bit of a finagler, always trying to get a better deal. 他有点耍手段，总是试图得到更好的交易。  She finagled her way into the VIP section. 她设法混进了贵宾区。|近义词：manipulator, schemer, trickster, swindler |反义词：honest person, straightforward person
