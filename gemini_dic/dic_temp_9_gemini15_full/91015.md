husbands:/ˈhʌzbəndz/| n.|丈夫们；已婚男人们|The word "husbands" is the plural form of "husband," referring to multiple married men.  It denotes men who are married and legally bound to their wives.|My husbands' families are coming to visit this weekend. 我的丈夫们的家人这周末要来拜访。  The husbands all went to the pub after work. 丈夫们下班后都去了酒吧。|wives, spouses|wives, single men|名词
