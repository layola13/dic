juryman:/ˈdʒʊəriˌmæn/| n. |陪审员|A male member of a jury.  A juryman is a man who is a member of a jury, a group of people sworn to give a verdict in a legal case based on the evidence presented.  | Example: The juryman listened intently to the witness's testimony.  这位陪审员专心致志地听着证人的证词。|近义词：juror, jury member|反义词：无
