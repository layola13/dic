primordiality:/ˌpraɪmɔːrdiˈæləti/| n. |原始性；初始状态；最初的时期|The state of being primordial; existence from the beginning; the earliest stage of existence or development.  Primordiality refers to the earliest stage of something's existence or development, often implying a fundamental or essential nature.  It suggests a state of being before significant change or differentiation has occurred. |The primordiality of the universe is a topic of much scientific debate. 宇宙的原始性是科学界争论不休的话题。|Originality, primitiveness|Modernity, sophistication|名词
