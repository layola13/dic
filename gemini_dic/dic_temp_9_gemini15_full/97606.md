inhale:/ɪnˈheɪl/| vt. |吸入，吸气|To breathe in.  |例句：He inhaled deeply before speaking. 他在说话之前深吸了一口气。
例句：She inhaled the sweet scent of roses. 她吸入玫瑰花的甜香。|近义词：breathe in, inspire|反义词：exhale, breathe out|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He inhales deeply before every dive. 他每次潜水前都深吸一口气。
She inhales the fresh mountain air. 她吸入清新的山间空气。


一般过去时:
He inhaled the smoke and coughed. 他吸入烟雾然后咳嗽了。
She inhaled deeply and then held her breath. 她深吸一口气然后屏住了呼吸。


现在完成时:
He has inhaled too much dust, making him cough. 他吸入了过多的灰尘，导致他咳嗽。
She has inhaled the perfume's fragrance all day. 她一整天都在闻着香水的香味。


过去完成时:
He had inhaled a significant amount of smoke before he collapsed. 他在倒下之前已经吸入了大量的烟雾。
She had inhaled the toxic fumes before she realized the danger.  在她意识到危险之前，她已经吸入了有毒的气体。


将来时:
He will inhale deeply before attempting the high jump. 他将在尝试跳高之前深吸一口气。
She will inhale the sweet aroma of the freshly baked bread. 她将吸入新鲜烘焙面包的甜香。


虚拟语气:
If he inhaled deeply, he might have performed better. 如果他深吸一口气，他可能会表现得更好。
I wish I had not inhaled that cigarette smoke. 我真希望我没有吸入那香烟的烟雾。


被动语态:
The fumes were inhaled by the workers. 这些烟雾被工人们吸入了。
The sweet fragrance was inhaled by everyone in the room. 屋子里每个人都闻到了甜美的香味。


疑问句:
Did he inhale the poison by accident? 他是不是意外吸入了毒药？
Does she inhale the smoke from the factory? 她吸入工厂的烟雾吗？


否定句:
He did not inhale the entire dose of medicine. 他没有吸入全部剂量的药。
She doesn't inhale second-hand smoke easily. 她不容易吸入二手烟。


PHRASE->
