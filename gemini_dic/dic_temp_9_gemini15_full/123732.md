moonshine:/ˈmuːnʃaɪn/ | n. |月光；私酿酒；胡说八道 | Illicitly distilled liquor; something deceptive or illusory.  Moonshine refers to strong alcoholic liquor made illegally, often in remote areas. It can also be used figuratively to describe something deceptive or unreal. |Example: The old man made moonshine in his barn.  老汉在他的谷仓里酿私酒。
The politician's promises were pure moonshine.  这位政客的承诺全是空话。 |近义词：bootleg liquor, illicit liquor, moonshining |反义词：legitimate liquor, legal alcohol
