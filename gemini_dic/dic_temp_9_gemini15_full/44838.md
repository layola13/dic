crustal:/ˈkrʌstəl/| adj.|地壳的；地层的|Relating to the Earth's crust.  The word derives from "crust," meaning the outer layer of something, ultimately from Latin *crusta* meaning "crust, shell."|例句：The earthquake caused significant crustal deformation.  这次地震造成了显著的地壳变形。
例句：The study focused on crustal movements in the region.  这项研究集中在该地区的地壳运动。|近义词：tectonic, lithospheric|反义词：mantle, core|词性:形容词
