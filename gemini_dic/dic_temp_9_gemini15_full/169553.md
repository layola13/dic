propers:/ˈprɒpərz/ | adj. | 正确的；合适的；得体的 |Proper means conforming to standards of correctness, appropriateness, or good taste. It implies a standard of behavior, conduct, or form that is considered correct or suitable in a particular context.  |例句：It's proper to say "thank you" when someone helps you.  = 当有人帮助你时，说“谢谢”是合适的。

例句：She wore a proper dress for the wedding. = 她穿了一件适合婚礼的得体礼服。


近义词：correct, appropriate, suitable, fitting, decent, seemly |反义词：improper, inappropriate, unsuitable, wrong, indecent
