redressed:/rɪˈdrɛst/| vt.|改正，纠正；重新穿衣；补偿|To correct or rectify a wrong or injustice; to put on clothes again; to compensate for a wrong or injury.|例句：The company redressed the grievances of its employees by raising their salaries. 公司通过提高员工工资来纠正其员工的不满。|例句：After changing, she redressed herself in her finest gown. 换好衣服后，她穿上了她最漂亮的礼服。|近义词：remedy, rectify, amend, compensate|反义词：worsen, aggravate
