ceromancy:/ˈsɛrəmænsi/| n.|蜡烛占卜|The practice of divination using melted wax.  Ceromancy involves interpreting the shapes formed by molten wax as it cools and solidifies, believing these shapes to hold symbolic meaning and offer insights into the future or hidden matters. |Example:  She practiced ceromancy, hoping the wax would reveal the identity of her secret admirer. = 她进行蜡烛占卜，希望蜡烛能揭示她秘密仰慕者的身份。
The results of her ceromancy session were surprisingly accurate. = 她占卜的结果出奇地准确。|近义词：divination, fortune-telling|反义词：N/A
