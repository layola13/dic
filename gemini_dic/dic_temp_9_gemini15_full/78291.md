germinated:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪtɪd/| v. |发芽；萌芽；开始发展|To begin to grow, especially from a seed or spore.  It implies the beginning of growth from a dormant state.|The seeds germinated after a week in warm soil.  种子在温暖的土壤中一周后发芽了。


The beans have finally germinated.  豆子终于发芽了。|sprout, sprout, shoot, bud,  |decay, rot|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The seeds germinate in spring. = 种子在春天发芽。
Plants germinate under the right conditions. = 植物在合适的条件下发芽。


一般过去时:
The seeds germinated quickly. = 种子很快发芽了。
The idea germinated in his mind. = 这个想法在他脑海中萌芽了。


现在完成时:
The seeds have germinated. = 种子已经发芽了。
My plan has germinated. = 我的计划已经开始发展了。


过去完成时:

将来时:
The seeds will germinate soon. = 种子很快就会发芽。

否定句:
