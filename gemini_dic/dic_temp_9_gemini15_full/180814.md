reharden:/ˌriːˈhɑːrdən/ | vt. | 再硬化；重新硬化 | To make (something, especially metal) hard again after it has been softened or weakened.  This usually involves a heat treatment process. | The metal was rehardended after being bent. 金属弯曲后被重新硬化。  The blacksmith rehardended the plowshare. 铁匠重新硬化了犁铧。| harden, toughen | soften, weaken | 动词
