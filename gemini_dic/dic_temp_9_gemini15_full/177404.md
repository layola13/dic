reawoke:/ˌriːəˈwoʊk/| vt.|重新唤醒；重新激起；使…复活|To awaken again; to rouse from sleep or inactivity; to rekindle or revive.|例句：The old memories reawoke in him.  那些旧的记忆在他心中苏醒了。
