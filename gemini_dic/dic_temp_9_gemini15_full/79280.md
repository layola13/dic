glaucomas:/ɡlɔːˈkoʊməz/| n. |青光眼（复数）|A group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, which is vital for good vision.  The damage is often caused by increased pressure in the eye.  Different types of glaucoma exist, and they can affect people of all ages, although it's more common in older adults.|例句：Many glaucomas are treatable, but early detection is crucial to prevent vision loss.  许多青光眼是可以治疗的，但是早期发现对于预防视力丧失至关重要。

例句：Untreated glaucomas can lead to blindness. 未经治疗的青光眼会导致失明。


近义词：eye disease, optic neuropathy|反义词：N/A
