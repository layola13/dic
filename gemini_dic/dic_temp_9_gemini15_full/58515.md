dues:/duːz/ | n. |会费；应付款；惩罚；报应 | Money that is owed, especially regular payments to a group or organization.  It can also refer to the consequences or punishment for one's actions. |例句：Membership dues are $50 a year.  会员费每年50美元。

例句：He had to pay his dues before he could be accepted into the club. 他必须先缴纳会费才能被俱乐部接纳。


近义词：fees, charges, payments, penalties, retribution

反义词：remuneration (in the context of payment), reward (in the context of consequences)

词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dues are payable monthly. = 会费每月支付。
What are the club dues? = 俱乐部会费是多少？


一般过去时:
I paid my dues last week. = 我上周付了会费。
The dues were increased last year. = 会费去年提高了。


现在完成时:
Have you paid your dues yet? = 你付会费了吗？

过去完成时:
He had already paid his dues before the deadline. = 他在截止日期之前已经付清了会费。
They had not paid their dues for several months. = 他们好几个月没交会费了。


将来时:
I will pay my dues next month. = 我下个月会交会费。
The dues will be automatically deducted from your account. = 会费将自动从您的账户中扣除。


虚拟语气:

I wish I had paid my dues earlier. = 我真希望我早点交会费。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
