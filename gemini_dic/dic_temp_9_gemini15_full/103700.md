jonquil:/ˈdʒɒŋkwɪl/ /ˈdʒɑːŋkwɪl/| n. |黄水仙，小黄水仙|A jonquil is a type of narcissus with fragrant yellow flowers.  Jonquils are known for their sweet scent and bright yellow color, often used in gardens and bouquets.|The garden was filled with the sweet scent of jonquils.|近义词：narcissus, daffodil|反义词：无
