multiplexor:/ˈmʌltiplɛksər/ | n. |多路复用器，多路选择器 | A device that selects one of several input signals and forwards the selected input into a single output line.  It acts like an electronic switch that allows multiple signals to share a single transmission path. | Example: The multiplexor in the router selects which data packet to send next.  多路复用器在路由器中选择下一个要发送的数据包。 |近义词：selector, switch |反义词：demultiplexor
