panentheistic:/ˌpænɪnθiˈɪstɪk/| adj.|泛神论的|Panentheism is a philosophical concept that asserts that God is both immanent in and transcendent to the world.  It differs from pantheism, which identifies God with the world, and theism, which sees God as separate from the world.  In panentheism, the world is part of God, but God is also greater than the world.  |Example:  His philosophical views leaned towards a panentheistic understanding of the divine.|近义词：pantheistic (but with crucial distinctions)|反义词：atheistic, deistic
