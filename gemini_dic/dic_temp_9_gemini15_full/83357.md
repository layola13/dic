gyreful: /ˈdʒaɪərfəl/ | adj. |旋转的；回旋的；循环往复的 |  Descriptive of something characterized by circular or spiral motion; swirling.  It suggests a sense of continuous movement and often implies a mesmerizing or hypnotic quality. |例句：The gyreful dance of the dervishes captivated the audience.  旋转的苦行僧舞蹈迷住了观众。


The gyreful patterns of ocean currents are crucial to understanding climate. 海洋环流的旋转模式对于理解气候至关重要。


近义词：circular, swirling, rotating, revolving, spiraling, vortical


反义词：linear, straight, direct, static


词性：形容词
