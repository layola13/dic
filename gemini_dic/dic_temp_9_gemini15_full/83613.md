haematothorax:/ˌhiːmætəˈθɔːræks/| n. |血气胸|A collection of blood and air in the pleural space (the space between the lung and the chest wall). This condition is usually caused by trauma, such as a penetrating chest wound or rib fracture, that damages both the lung and blood vessels.|Example: The patient presented with a haematothorax following a car accident.  病人车祸后出现了血气胸。|近义词:hemothorax|反义词:pneumothorax (气胸, only air in pleural space)
