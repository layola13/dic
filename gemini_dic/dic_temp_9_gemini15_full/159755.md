polyelectronic: /ˌpɒliɪˌlekˈtrɒnɪk/ /ˌpɑːliɪˌlɛkˈtrɑːnɪk/ | adj. | 多电子的 | Relating to or involving more than one electron.  This adjective is used primarily in chemistry and physics to describe systems or processes that involve multiple electrons.  For example, a polyelectronic atom has more than one electron orbiting its nucleus.


例句：

The calculation of the energy levels of a polyelectronic atom is a complex problem.  多电子原子的能级计算是一个复杂的问题。

Polyelectronic effects significantly influence the chemical reactivity of molecules. 多电子效应显著影响分子的化学反应活性。


近义词：multielectronic (less common)


反义词：monoelectronic (describing a system with only one electron)


词性：形容词
