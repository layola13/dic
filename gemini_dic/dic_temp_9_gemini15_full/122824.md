molehills:/ˈmoʊlˌhɪlz/| n. |土堆；小障碍；微不足道的事|Small mounds of earth thrown up by moles.  The term is often used figuratively to represent small problems or insignificant obstacles.|He made molehills out of mountains. 他小题大做。|近义词：bumps, hummocks, minor problems|反义词：mountains, major problems
