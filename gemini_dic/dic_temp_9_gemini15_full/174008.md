quasi-theatrically:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ θiˈætrɪkli/| adv.|近乎戏剧性地，做作地|in a manner that resembles theatrical performance, often implying exaggeration or artificiality.|例句：He announced his resignation quasi-theatrically, with a flourish and a dramatic sigh. 他近乎戏剧性地宣布辞职，做了个手势并夸张地叹了口气。


例句：She reacted to the news quasi-theatrically, gasping and clutching her chest. 她对这个消息的反应近乎戏剧化，她倒吸了一口气，捂着胸口。


近义词：dramatically, melodramatically, artificially, ostentatiously


反义词：naturally, sincerely, genuinely, simply


词性：副词

词源：quasi- (拉丁语，意为“好像，近似”) + theatrically (源于希腊语 theatron, 意为“剧场”)  该词是 quasi- 与 theatrically 的结合，表示“近似戏剧性地”。
