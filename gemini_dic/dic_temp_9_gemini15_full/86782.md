hem:/hɛm/ | n. v. | 哼；衣襟；摺边 |  A hem is the edge of a piece of cloth that has been folded over and sewn down.  As a verb, it means to fold over and sew the edge of (a piece of cloth). | 例句：She carefully hemmed the dress. 她仔细地缝好了裙子的边。 The hem of her skirt was frayed. 她的裙子下摆磨损了。 | 近义词：border, edge, fringe | 反义词：unhemmed


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He hems his own shirts. = 他自己缝衬衫的边。
She hems the curtains regularly. = 她定期缝窗帘的边。

一般过去时:
I hemmed my trousers yesterday. = 我昨天缝了我的裤子。
They hemmed the tablecloth before the party. = 他们在派对前缝好了桌布。

现在完成时:
She has hemmed all the napkins. = 她已经缝好了所有的餐巾。
He has hemmed and hawed all morning. = 他整个上午都在犹豫不决。

过去完成时:
I had hemmed the dress before the wedding. = 我在婚礼前已经缝好了裙子。
She had hemmed and hawed before finally agreeing. = 她犹豫再三之后才最终同意。

将来时:
I will hem the skirt tomorrow. = 我明天会缝裙子。
They will hem the new curtains next week. = 他们下周会缝新的窗帘。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would hem the trousers myself. = 如果我有时间，我会自己缝裤子。
I wish I had hemmed the dress sooner. = 我希望我早点缝好裙子。

被动语态:
The curtains were hemmed by my grandmother. = 窗帘是我祖母缝的。
The dress needs to be hemmed. = 这件裙子需要缝边。

疑问句:
Have you hemmed the tablecloth yet? = 你缝好桌布了吗？
Will you hem the dress for me? = 你会帮我缝这件裙子吗？

否定句:
I didn't hem the skirt. = 我没有缝裙子。
She hasn't hemmed the curtains yet. = 她还没缝窗帘。


PHRASE->
hem and haw = 犹豫不决
turn up the hem = 把衣边往上翻
let down the hem = 放下衣边
take up the hem =  缩短衣边
a rolled hem = 卷边


词源:
Old English *hemma, from Proto-Germanic *haimmaz (cf. Old High German heimo "home," Old Norse heimr "home").  The connection to "home" is through the sense of the hem as the boundary or edge of a garment.
