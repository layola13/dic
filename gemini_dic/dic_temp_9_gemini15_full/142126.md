origin:/ˈɒrɪdʒɪn/ /ˈɔːrɪdʒɪn/| n. |起源，由来，出身 |The origin of the universe is a mystery.  宇宙的起源是一个谜。|The word "origin" refers to the point or place where something begins, its source, or its earliest known form.  It can refer to the beginning of something concrete, like a river, or something abstract, like an idea. |近义词：source, beginning, root, commencement |反义词：end, conclusion, termination |名词
