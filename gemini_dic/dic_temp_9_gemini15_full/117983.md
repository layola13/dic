memorate:/ˈmeməreɪt/| vt.|纪念，庆祝|To commemorate is to remember and celebrate an important person or event, often with a ceremony or special activity.  It implies a formal or significant act of remembrance.|例句：We will commemorate the 50th anniversary of the school's founding with a special ceremony. 我们将举行一个特别的仪式来纪念学校建校50周年。
例句：A statue was erected to commemorate the fallen soldiers.  一座雕像被竖立起来以纪念阵亡的士兵。|近义词：celebrate, remember, honor, observe|反义词：forget, ignore, neglect|动词
