interfraternal:/ˌɪntərfrəˈtɜːrnəl/| adj. |兄弟会间的；兄弟会成员之间的| Relating to or occurring between different fraternities.  The term usually refers to interactions, competitions, or events involving multiple fraternities.|例句：The interfraternal council organized a charity fundraiser.  兄弟会理事会组织了一场慈善募捐活动。|近义词：intercollegiate (if referring to college fraternities), intergroup|反义词：intrafraternal (within a single fraternity)
