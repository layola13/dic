reachabilities:/ˌriːtʃəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可达性（复数）|The qualities or states of being reachable;  the extent to which something can be reached or accessed.  It often refers to multiple aspects of accessibility, including physical, digital, or social access.|例句：The reachabilities of the remote villages were severely hampered by the heavy snowfall.  偏远村庄的可达性因大雪而严重受阻。


The study analyzed the reachabilities of different online platforms for diverse user groups.  这项研究分析了不同在线平台对不同用户群体的可达性。
