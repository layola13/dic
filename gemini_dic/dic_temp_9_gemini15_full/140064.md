The study showed that the oestrogenized environment negatively impacted the development of aquatic life. 研究表明，雌激素化的环境对水生生物的发育产生了负面影响。


近义词：oestrogenic (adj.)  | 反义词：  No direct antonym, but could use terms suggesting a lack of oestrogen or its opposite effects, like androgenized (for example). | 词性：形容词
