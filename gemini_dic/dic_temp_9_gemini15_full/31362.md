charade:/ʃəˈreɪd/| n. |哑剧；字谜游戏；障眼法|A game in which players act out a word or phrase without speaking, for others to guess;  a deceptive action or pretense.|例句：We played charades last night.  昨晚我们玩了哑剧游戏。

例句：His resignation was a mere charade to cover up his wrongdoing.他的辞职不过是为了掩盖他的罪行而做的障眼法。

近义词：pantomime, pretense, deception|反义词：reality, truth|词性:名词
