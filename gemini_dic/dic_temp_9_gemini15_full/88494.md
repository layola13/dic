histories:/ˈhɪstəriːz/| n. |历史（复数）| Accounts of past events, especially those relating to a particular person, group, period, or subject.  Histories often involve interpretation and analysis, rather than just a simple chronological listing of facts. | Example: The histories of ancient Rome are filled with tales of emperors and gladiators.  古代罗马的历史充满了关于皇帝和角斗士的故事。 The histories of different cultures often diverge significantly.不同文化的历史往往大相径庭。|近义词：annals, chronicles, records |反义词：future, present
