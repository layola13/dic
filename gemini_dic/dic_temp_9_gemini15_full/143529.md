outgrowing:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊɪŋ/| v.|超过；长大到…不再适合；比…长得快|To grow beyond or too large for; to surpass in growth or development.|例句：He's outgrowing his clothes. 他长得太快，衣服穿不下了。|例句：The company is outgrowing its current facilities. 公司发展迅速，现有的设施已不敷使用。|近义词：surpassing, exceeding, surpassing in growth|反义词：shrinking, diminishing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plant is outgrowing its pot. = 这棵植物长得超过了花盆。
She is outgrowing her childhood fears. = 她正在克服童年的恐惧。


一般过去时:
He outgrew his shyness. = 他克服了他的害羞。
The small business outgrew its original location. = 这家小企业发展壮大了，原来的地方不够用了。


现在完成时:
The town has outgrown its infrastructure. = 这个城镇的建设已经跟不上发展速度了。
I have outgrown my old habits. = 我已经改掉了我的旧习惯。


过去完成时:
By the time he graduated, he had outgrown his interest in music. = 到他毕业的时候，他对音乐的兴趣已经减弱了。
The company had outgrown its initial funding. = 公司发展壮大，最初的资金已经不够用了。


将来时:
This city will outgrow its current transportation system. = 这个城市很快就会超过现有交通系统。
She will outgrow this phase of her life. = 她将会度过人生的这一阶段。


虚拟语气:
If he continued to work hard, he would outgrow his limitations. = 如果他继续努力工作，他会克服他的局限性。
I wish I had outgrown my procrastination habits years ago. = 我真希望几年前就能改掉拖延的坏习惯。


被动语态:
His old shoes were outgrown quickly. = 他的旧鞋子很快就被穿小了。
The small town is being outgrown by the expanding city. = 小镇正在被不断扩张的城市所超越。


疑问句:
Is he outgrowing his clothes? = 他是不是长得太快，衣服穿不下了？
Are they outgrowing their current market share? = 他们的市场份额是否正在被超越？


否定句:
He hasn't outgrown his fear of heights yet. = 他还没有克服恐高症。
The company didn't outgrow its competitors this year. = 公司今年没有超过竞争对手。


PHRASE->
outgrow one's clothes = 长大到衣服穿不下
outgrow a problem = 解决问题
outgrow a habit = 改掉习惯
outgrow one's responsibilities = 承担责任的能力增强
outgrow one's limitations = 克服局限性
