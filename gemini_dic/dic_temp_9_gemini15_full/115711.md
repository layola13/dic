margins:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnz/ | n. | 页边空白；利润；余地 | The margins of the page were too narrow. 页边空白太窄了。  The company's profit margins are shrinking. 公司的利润率正在下降。 We need to allow for a margin of error. 我们需要考虑到误差的余地。 | 近义词：edges, limits, profits | 反义词：center, core


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The margins are too narrow. = 页边距太窄了。
What are the profit margins this quarter? = 本季度的利润率是多少？


一般过去时:
The margins were wider last year. = 去年页边距更宽。
The profit margins were higher before the recession. = 经济衰退前利润率更高。


现在完成时:
The margins have been reduced. = 页边距已被缩小。
Profit margins have improved significantly. = 利润率已显著提高。


过去完成时:
The margins had already been set before the printing began. = 印刷开始前页边距已设定好。
The company had realised that their profit margins had been declining for years. = 公司已经意识到他们的利润率多年来一直在下降。


将来时:
The margins will be adjusted. = 页边距将被调整。
We expect the profit margins to increase next year. = 我们预计明年的利润率将会提高。


虚拟语气:
If the margins were wider, the text would be easier to read. = 如果页边距更宽，文本将更容易阅读。
I wish the profit margins were higher. = 我希望利润率更高。


被动语态:
The margins were carefully measured. = 页边距被仔细测量。
The profit margins are being closely monitored. = 利润率正在受到密切监控。


疑问句:
What are the margins of the document? = 文件的页边距是多少？
What are the acceptable profit margins? = 可接受的利润率是多少？


否定句:
The margins are not wide enough. = 页边距不够宽。
The profit margins are not as high as we had hoped. = 利润率不如我们希望的那么高。


PHRASE->
margin of error: 误差范围
profit margin: 利润率
operating margin: 营业利润率
net margin: 净利润率
call margin: 保证金


词源:
From Old French *marge, from Latin margo, margin- "edge, border, brink".  The sense of "profit margin" developed later.
