dissociation:/ˌdɪˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 分离；解离；分裂；脱协会 | The separation or disconnection of something from something else.  It can refer to a mental process, a chemical process, or a social process.  |

例句：

The trauma caused a dissociation of her memories. = 创伤导致了她记忆的分离。

The dissociation of the molecule into its constituent atoms requires a significant amount of energy. = 将分子分解成其组成原子需要大量的能量。


近义词：separation, detachment, disconnection, alienation, split

反义词：association, connection, integration, unification


词性：名词
