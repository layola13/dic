migrational:/maɪˈɡreɪʃənəl/| adj. |迁徙的；移徙的；迁移的 | Relating to or characterized by migration.  The term often refers to the movement of animals, but can also apply to the movement of people or other things. |例句：The bird's migrational patterns are fascinating.  这只鸟类的迁徙模式令人着迷。

例句：The study focused on the migrational habits of the monarch butterfly. 这项研究重点关注帝王蝶的迁徙习性。


近义词：migratory, nomadic, wandering

反义词：stationary, sedentary, immobile


词源：From "migration" + "-al,"  "migration" itself derives from Latin *migrāt- (stem of migrāre "to wander, remove oneself")
