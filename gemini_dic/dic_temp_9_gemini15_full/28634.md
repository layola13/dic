cargoes:/ˈkɑːrɡoʊz/ | n. |货物（复数） |The plural form of "cargo," referring to multiple shipments of goods.  It signifies the goods carried by a ship, airplane, truck, or other means of transportation. |The ships were laden with cargoes of grain and timber.  这些船装载着谷物和木材货物。|The port is busy handling cargoes from all over the world.  这个港口正忙于处理来自世界各地的货物。|freight, shipments, goods, merchandise|  |名词
