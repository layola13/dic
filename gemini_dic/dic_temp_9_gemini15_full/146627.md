overhandling: /ˌoʊvərˈhændlɪŋ/ | n. |过度处理，过分操纵 |The act of handling something too much, often resulting in damage or inefficiency.  It implies excessive intervention or manipulation, leading to negative consequences. |例句：The overhandling of the delicate antique vase caused it to crack.  过度摆弄那件精美的古董花瓶导致它裂开了。

The overhandling of the project led to delays and increased costs. 项目的过度干预导致了延误和成本增加。|近义词： mishandling, mismanagement, overprocessing |反义词： underhandling (less common), proper handling
