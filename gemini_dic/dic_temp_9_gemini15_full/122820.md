molecule:/ˈmɒlɪkjuːl/ /ˈmɑːlɪkjuːl/| n. |分子|A molecule is the smallest particle of a substance that has all the chemical properties of that substance. It consists of two or more atoms held together by chemical bonds.|Water is a molecule made up of two hydrogen atoms and one oxygen atom.  水是由两个氢原子和一个氧原子组成的分子。|  The air we breathe is a mixture of different molecules. 我们呼吸的空气是由不同的分子混合而成的。|atom, particle|  (反义词较难直接对应，可考虑宏观物质如：substance, matter)|名词
