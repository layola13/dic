patchworks:/ˈpætʃˌwɜːrks/ | n. | 拼缝物；混杂物 | A patchwork is something made from different pieces of material sewn together, or a collection of different things.  It often implies a lack of uniformity or a haphazard arrangement.  | Example: Her quilt was a beautiful patchwork of different fabrics.  她的被子是由不同布料拼凑而成的美丽的拼缝物。

The garden was a patchwork of colours. 花园是一片色彩斑斓的景象。|近义词：collage, medley, mosaic, assortment |反义词：uniformity, homogeneity
