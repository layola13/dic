sealskin: /ˈsiːlskɪn/ | n. |海豹皮，海豹皮制品 | The skin of a seal, often used to make coats, hats, and other garments.  It is known for its warmth, water resistance, and luxurious feel. |例句：The coat is made of sealskin. 这件外套是用海豹皮做的。

例句：She wore a sealskin hat to protect herself from the cold. 她戴着一顶海豹皮帽子来御寒。|近义词：seal fur, fur (general term)|反义词：cotton, silk (alternative materials)
