renderers:/ˈrɛndərərz/ | n. |渲染器，呈现器 | A program or component that converts data into a visual or auditory format.  For example, in computer graphics, a renderer takes 3D models and converts them into 2D images that can be displayed on a screen. In audio, a renderer might convert digital audio data into analog signals for playback through speakers. | Example:  The game uses a highly advanced renderer to create realistic graphics.  这款游戏使用高度先进的渲染器来创建逼真的图形。

The new software features a powerful renderer for high-resolution images. 这款新软件具有强大的高分辨率图像渲染器。|近义词：visualizer, interpreter, translator |反义词：None (no direct antonym)
