reclusely:/rɪˈkluːsli/| adv.|隐居地；隐遁地；僻静地 |In a secluded or solitary manner; characteristic of a recluse.  It describes a way of life characterized by seclusion and avoidance of society. |例句：He lived reclusely in a remote cabin in the mountains. 他隐居在山里的一间偏僻的小屋里。

例句：She lived reclusely, rarely seeing anyone outside her immediate family. 她过着隐居的生活，很少见除了直系亲属以外的人。


近义词：solitarily, secludedly, hermit-like

反义词：socially, openly, publicly


词性: 副词
