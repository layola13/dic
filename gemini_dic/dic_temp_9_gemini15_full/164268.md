predreading: /ˌpriːˈriːdɪŋ/ | n. |预读；阅读准备 |The act of reading something before a more thorough reading, often to gain a general understanding or prepare for a discussion.  |例句：Predreading the chapter helped me understand the complex concepts more easily. 预读章节帮助我更容易理解复杂的概念。

例句：Doing some predreading before the lecture will make it much easier to follow. 在讲座之前进行一些预读将使理解变得更容易。


近义词：preview, skimming, scanning |反义词：postreading


词性：名词
