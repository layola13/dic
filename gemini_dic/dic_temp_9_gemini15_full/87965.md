high-flyer:/ˈhaɪˌflaɪər/| n.| 高飞者；成功人士；佼佼者 | A high-flyer is someone who is very successful, especially in their career.  They are ambitious and achieve a lot. |例句：He's a high-flyer in the finance industry. 他是金融业的成功人士。

She's always been a high-flyer, achieving top marks in all her exams. 她一直都是佼佼者，每次考试都取得高分。|近义词：high-achiever, achiever, star, go-getter |反义词：underachiever, failure, slacker |名词
