concord's:/ˈkɒŋkɔːrdz/| n.|和谐；一致；协定；康科德（地名）|Concord refers to a state of agreement or harmony between people or things.  It can also refer to a formal agreement or treaty, or to the town of Concord, Massachusetts. |例句：The two countries signed a concord to end the war.  两个国家签署了一项协定以结束战争。

例句：There was a general concord of opinion on the matter. 在这件事上，大家的意见非常一致。


近义词：harmony, agreement, accord, unity |反义词：discord, disagreement, conflict, strife|词性：名词
