replumbed:/ˌriːˈplʌmbd/| v. |重新安装管道；彻底检修| To replace or repair the plumbing system in a building or other structure.  The process involves replacing or repairing pipes, fixtures, and other components of the plumbing system. |例句：The old house needed to be replumbed before we could move in. 我们搬进去之前需要重新安装老房子的管道。  The entire building was replumbed after the flood. 洪水过后，整栋楼都重新安装了管道。|近义词：repipe, renovate (plumbing), overhaul (plumbing)|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be leaving the plumbing in disrepair.|动词
