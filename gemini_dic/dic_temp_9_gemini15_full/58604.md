dullest:/ˈdʌləst/| adj.|最乏味的，最沉闷的|Most lacking in interest or excitement; tedious.  The dullest part of the lecture was the lengthy description of the methodology.  The lecture's lengthy description of the methodology was the dullest part.|近义词：boring, tedious, monotonous, uninteresting |反义词：exciting, interesting, stimulating, vibrant
