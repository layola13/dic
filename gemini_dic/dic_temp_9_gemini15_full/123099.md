mongols:/ˈmɒŋɡəlz/ /ˈmɑːŋɡəlz/| n.|蒙古人；蒙古族；蒙古语|The word "Mongols" refers to a group of people inhabiting Mongolia and surrounding regions in Central Asia.  They are known for their rich history, nomadic culture, and significant role in shaping the course of Asian history. The term can also refer to the Mongolian language or to things associated with the Mongol Empire. |Example: The Mongols built a vast empire spanning much of Asia.  蒙古人建立了一个横跨亚洲大部分地区的庞大帝国。|近义词:Mongolians|反义词:None
