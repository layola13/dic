inrushing:/ˈɪnrʌʃɪŋ/| adj.|涌入的；蜂拥而入的|The word "inrushing" describes something rushing or flowing inwards. It often implies a large quantity or a forceful movement.|例句：The inrushing tide swept away the sandcastles.  汹涌的潮水冲走了沙堡。|例句：There was a feeling of inrushing energy as she stepped onto the stage.  当她踏上舞台时，她感到一股涌入的能量。|近义词：incoming, flooding, surging|反义词：outgoing, receding, ebbing|形容词
