pini:/ˈpiːniː/| n.|松树；松树木|A pini is a type of evergreen coniferous tree belonging to the genus Pinus.  They are characterized by needle-like leaves and cones. Pinis are widely distributed across the Northern Hemisphere and are known for their timber, resin, and nuts.|例句：The pini trees stood tall against the stormy sky.  那些松树挺立在暴风雨的天空下。|近义词：pine tree, conifer|反义词：无
