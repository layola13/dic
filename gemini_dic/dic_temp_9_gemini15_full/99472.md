interfered:/ˌɪntərˈfɪrd/ | v. | 干涉；干扰；妨碍 | To get involved in and affect something, often in a way that is unwanted or harmful.  |例句：He interfered in his daughter's marriage. 他干涉了他女儿的婚姻。

例句：The noise interfered with my concentration. 噪音干扰了我的注意力。


近义词：meddle, intervene, impede, obstruct | 反义词：aid, assist, help


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He interferes in other people's business. 他干涉别人的事情。
She interferes constantly. 她总是干涉。


一般过去时:
They interfered with the police investigation. 他们妨碍了警方的调查。
He interfered in the argument. 他插手了这场争论。


现在完成时:
She has interfered too much in her son's life. 她对她儿子的生活干涉太多了。
They have interfered with our plans. 他们打乱了我们的计划。


过去完成时:
He had interfered before, so no one was surprised. 他以前就干涉过，所以没有人感到惊讶。
She realized she had interfered in a situation she didn't understand. 她意识到她干涉了一件她并不理解的事情。


将来时:
I know he will interfere if we don't tell him. 我知道如果我们不告诉他，他会干涉。
They will interfere with the project again. 他们还会再次干扰这个项目。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't interfered, everything would have been fine. 如果他没有干涉，一切都会很好。
I wish they hadn't interfered in our affairs. 我希望他们没有干涉我们的事情。


被动语态:
The investigation was interfered with by unknown parties. 调查受到了不明身份人士的干扰。
Our plans were interfered with by the unexpected storm. 我们的计划被突如其来的暴风雨打乱了。


疑问句:
Did anyone interfere with the experiment? 有没有人干涉实验？
Were you interfered with during the meeting? 会议期间你受到了干扰吗？


否定句:
He didn't interfere in our conversation. 他没有插手我们的谈话。
She did not interfere with his work. 她没有干涉他的工作。


PHRASE->
interfere with: 干扰；妨碍
interfere in: 干涉；插手
interfere in someone's affairs: 干涉某人的事情
interfere with the progress: 妨碍进度
interfere with the signal: 干扰信号

词源:
interfere源于古法语 *enterferir，由拉丁语*interferire演变而来，其中inter-表示“在…之间”，ferire表示“打击；撞击”。  因此，其本义是指“在两者之间碰撞或发生冲突”。  随着时间的推移，其含义演变为“干涉；干扰”。
