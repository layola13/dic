nondegenerates:/ˌnɒnˈdɪdʒənəreɪts/| n. |非退化元素，非简并的|Elements or structures that are not degenerate;  lacking degeneracy.  In mathematics, it often refers to matrices or operators that have a full set of linearly independent eigenvectors. In physics, it can refer to energy levels that are not identical.|例句：The nondegenerates of the matrix are easily calculated using standard methods.  该矩阵的非退化元素可以使用标准方法轻松计算。The system exhibits nondegenerate energy levels under these conditions. 在这些条件下，系统表现出非简并能级。|近义词：distinct, separate, independent|反义词：degenerate, singular
