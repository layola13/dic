omits:/əˈmɪts/ | vt. |省略，遗漏|To leave out or exclude (something), either intentionally or accidentally.|The report omits any mention of the casualties.这份报告省略了任何关于伤亡的提及。|近义词：leave out, exclude, neglect |反义词：include, add


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He omits important details in his reports. 他在他的报告中省略了重要的细节。
The author omits a crucial chapter from the book. 作者从书中省略了一个重要的章节。

一般过去时:
She omitted to mention the meeting. 她忘记提及会议了。
They omitted several key facts from their presentation. 他们在演示中省略了一些关键事实。

现在完成时:
He has omitted several steps in the instructions. 他在说明中省略了几步。
She has omitted to send the email. 她忘记发送邮件了。

过去完成时:
They had omitted to inform us of the changes. 他们忘记告知我们这些变化了。
We had omitted checking the equipment before starting. 我们在开始之前忘记检查设备了。

将来时:
I will omit the technical details for now. 我现在将省略技术细节。
He will omit the less important points in his speech. 他将在他的演讲中省略不太重要的点。

虚拟语气:
If he omitted that detail, the report would be misleading. 如果他省略了那个细节，报告就会具有误导性。
I wish I hadn't omitted to call her. 我真希望我没有忘记给她打电话。

被动语态:
Some details were omitted from the final draft. 一些细节从最终草稿中被省略了。
The important information was omitted by mistake. 重要的信息被错误地省略了。

疑问句:
Did he omit anything important? 他省略了什么重要的东西吗？
What did they omit from the agenda? 他们在议程中省略了什么？

否定句:
He did not omit any information. 他没有遗漏任何信息。
She did not omit to sign the contract. 她没有忘记签合同。


PHRASE->
omit to do sth.  = 忘记做某事
omit from = 从...中省略
omit details = 省略细节
omit mentioning = 省略提及
omitted words = 省略的词语
