frostier: /ˈfrɔːstiər/ | adj. | 更冷的；更冷淡的；更严厉的 | More cold; colder; less friendly; more severe.  |

例句：

The air grew frostier as the sun set. = 日落后，空气变得更冷了。

Her tone became frostier after his rude remark. = 他无礼的言辞后，她的语气变得更冷淡了。


近义词：icier, colder, chillier, less friendly, more unfriendly, severe, unfriendly


反义词：warmer, friendlier, milder, more genial


词性: adjective
