minette:/mɪˈnɛt/| n. |小猫；暗淡的黑色；一种细小的煤|A small, usually black cat; a dark, grayish-black color; a type of fine coal.|例句：The minette played with a ball of yarn.  这只小猫玩着一团毛线。


例句：The dress was a deep minette. 这件衣服是深灰黑色。


近义词：kitten, black cat,  dark gray|反义词：large cat, light color


词源:  The word "minette" is a diminutive of the French word "minuit," meaning "midnight,"  referring to the dark gray-black color.  Its use to describe a small cat likely developed from the association of the color with a small, dark cat.


用法说明:

minette作为名词主要有三个含义：

1. **小猫:**  通常指小而黑色的猫。  This usage is more common in informal settings.

2. **暗淡的黑色:** 指一种深灰黑色，介于黑色和灰色之间。 This is often used to describe colors in fabrics or other materials.

3. **一种细小的煤:**  在矿业领域，minette 指一种细小的煤。 This usage is more specialized and less common.


例句：

1.  The artist used a minette charcoal to create the subtle shading. (暗淡的黑色) 艺术家用深灰黑色的炭笔来创造微妙的阴影。

2.  A tiny minette slept curled up on the windowsill. (小猫) 一只小小的黑猫蜷缩在窗台上睡觉。

3.  The mine produced a high-quality minette. (一种细小的煤)  这个矿井生产高质量的细煤。

There are no common idiomatic phrases specifically using "minette."  The word is generally used as a standalone noun.
