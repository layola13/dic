mini-bus: /ˈmɪniˌbʌs/ | n. | 小型巴士 | A small bus, typically carrying between 8 and 20 passengers.  It is smaller than a coach but larger than a car. | 例句：We took a mini-bus to the airport. 我们乘坐一辆小型巴士去机场。


例句：The school uses mini-buses to transport students. 学校用小型巴士运送学生。


近义词：minibus, small bus


反义词：coach, large bus


词性: 名词
