photorelief:/ˈfoʊtoʊrɪliːf/| n.|照片浮雕|A photographic image that is produced on a three-dimensional surface, often using a process that creates raised or recessed areas to give a sculptural effect.|The artist created a stunning photorelief depicting the rugged mountain landscape.|近义词：photo sculpture, bas-relief (if recessed),  high-relief (if raised)|反义词：photo flat print
