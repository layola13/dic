restlessly:/ˈrɛstlɪsli/| adv.|不安地；焦躁地；坐立不安地|in a way that shows you are anxious and cannot relax|He paced restlessly around the room. 他在房间里焦躁地踱步。|She waited restlessly for the phone to ring. 她焦急地等待电话铃响。|calmly, peacefully|restfully, quietly|副词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He restlessly waits for the exam results. 他焦急地等待考试结果。
She restlessly taps her fingers on the table. 她不安地敲打着桌子。

一般过去时:
He restlessly paced the floor all night. 他整夜都在地板上焦躁地踱步。
She restlessly tossed and turned in her bed. 她不安地辗转反侧。

现在完成时:
He has been restlessly waiting for news for hours. 他已经焦急地等待消息几个小时了。
She has restlessly searched for her lost keys all morning. 她整个上午都在焦急地寻找丢失的钥匙。

过去完成时:
He had been restlessly anticipating the meeting for days. 他几天来一直焦急地期待着会议。
She had restlessly waited for her flight for hours before it was delayed.  她在航班延误之前已经焦急地等待了几个小时。

将来时:
He will restlessly await the verdict of the jury. 他将焦急地等待陪审团的裁决。
She will restlessly watch the clock as the deadline approaches.  随着截止日期的临近，她将焦急地注视着时钟。

虚拟语气:
If he were not so restless, he would be a better student. 如果他不那么焦躁，他会成为一个更好的学生。
If she weren't so restless, she would sleep better. 如果她不那么焦躁，她会睡得更好。

被动语态: (此词为副词，不能用于被动语态)
The situation was handled restlessly. (不自然，应改为：The situation was handled in a restless manner.)  情况处理得非常焦躁不安。(不自然，应改为：情况处理得非常焦躁不安。)
The news was received restlessly. (不自然，应改为：The news was received with restlessness.) 消息被焦急地接收。(不自然，应改为：消息被焦急地接收。)


疑问句: (此词为副词，一般不用于疑问句作谓语)
Does he act restlessly when he is nervous? 他紧张的时候会表现得焦躁不安吗？
Is she restlessly waiting for her friend? 她正在焦急地等待她的朋友吗？


否定句:
He didn't act restlessly during the presentation. 他在演示过程中没有表现出不安。
She didn't wait restlessly; she was quite calm. 她并没有焦急地等待；她很平静。
