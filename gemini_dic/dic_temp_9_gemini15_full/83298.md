gynantherous:/ˌɡaɪnˈænθərəs/ | adj. |雌雄蕊的，雌雄蕊同生的|Having both stamens and pistils in the same flower.  This term describes flowers that possess both male (stamen) and female (pistil) reproductive organs.  It contrasts with unisexual flowers which have only one type of reproductive organ.|例句：The gynantherous flower displayed both pollen and ovules, ensuring self-pollination.|近义词：hermaphrodite, bisexual, monoclinous |反义词：unisexual, diclinous
