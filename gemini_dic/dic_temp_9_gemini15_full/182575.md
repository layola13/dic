reobtain:/ˌriːəbˈteɪn/| vt.|重新获得，恢复|To obtain something again after having lost it or given it up.|The company is trying to reobtain its market share.  这家公司正试图重新获得其市场份额。|He reobtained his driving license after completing a retraining course. 他完成再培训课程后重新获得了驾照。|obtain, regain, recover|lose, forfeit, relinquish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reobtains his lost keys every week. 他每周都找回丢失的钥匙。
They reobtain their lost profits through diligent work. 他们通过勤奋工作重新获得了失去的利润。


一般过去时:
She reobtained her passport after a long wait. 她经过长时间等待后重新获得了护照。
The museum reobtained the stolen artifact.  博物馆重新获得了被盗的文物。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

They had reobtained the necessary permits before starting construction. 他们在开始施工前已经重新获得了必要的许可证。


将来时:
