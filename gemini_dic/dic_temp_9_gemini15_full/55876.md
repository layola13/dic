divisibly:/dɪˈvɪzəbli/ | adv. | 可整除地，可分地 |  Divisibly describes the state of being able to be divided without a remainder.  It often refers to mathematical division. |  The number 12 is divisibly by 2, 3, 4, and 6.  12可以被2,3,4,6整除。 |  evenly, exactly | indivisibly


USAGE->

This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't conjugate like verbs or change form like nouns or adjectives.  It doesn't have tenses or voice.  Its usage is primarily in mathematical or theoretical contexts.

Example 1:
The cake was cut divisibly into eight equal slices. 蛋糕被切成八块相等的切片。

Example 2:
The problem is solvable only if the number is divisibly by three. 只有当数字能被3整除时，这个问题才能解决。


PHRASE->

There are no common phrases directly using "divisibly".  The concept is usually expressed differently.  For example, instead of "divisibly by 3", one might say "divisible by 3" (adjective form) or  "can be divided by 3".
