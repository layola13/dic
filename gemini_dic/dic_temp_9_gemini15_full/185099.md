retightened:/ˌriːˈtaɪtnd/| v. |重新拧紧，重新拉紧 | To tighten something again.  This implies a previous tightening that has loosened or become insufficient. |例句：The mechanic retightened the bolts on the engine. 机械师重新拧紧了发动机上的螺栓。

例句：After the earthquake, they retightened all the screws in the house. 地震过后，他们重新拧紧了房子里的所有螺丝。


近义词：tighten again, refasten


反义词：loosen

词性：动词
