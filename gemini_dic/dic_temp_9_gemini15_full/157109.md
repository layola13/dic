pinboard: /ˈpɪnbɔːrd/ | n. | 布告栏，图钉板 | A board, typically of cork or similar material, used for displaying items by pinning them with pins.  |  She pinned the photograph to the pinboard. 她把照片钉在布告栏上。 | notice board, bulletin board |  (无明确反义词) | 名词
