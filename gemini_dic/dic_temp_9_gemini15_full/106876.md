labyrinth:/ˈlæbərɪnθ/| n. |迷宫；复杂局面| A labyrinth is a complex network of winding passages designed to confuse and disorient those who enter.  It can also refer to a confusing or complicated situation. |例句：The ancient city was a labyrinth of narrow, twisting streets.  古老的城市是一座狭窄弯曲街道组成的迷宫。|  例句：He found himself lost in a labyrinth of legal paperwork. 他发现自己迷失在复杂的法律文书中。|近义词：maze, tangle, web |反义词：straight path, simple solution
