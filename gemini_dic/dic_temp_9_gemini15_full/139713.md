ocellus:/oʊˈsɛləs/| n. |小眼；单眼；眼点|A simple eye, especially one of the light-sensitive organs found on the bodies of some insects and other invertebrates.  It is usually cup-shaped and contains a single lens. Unlike compound eyes, ocelli provide only rudimentary vision, primarily detecting light intensity and direction. |Example: The insect's ocelli detected a sudden change in light intensity.  这只昆虫的单眼察觉到光线强度的突然变化。The butterfly has three ocelli on its head. 蝴蝶头上长着三个单眼。|近义词：simple eye, eyespot|反义词：compound eye
