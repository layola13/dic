diamido: /daɪˈæmɪdoʊ/ | adj. | 二酰胺的；含有两个酰胺基的 |  Describes a chemical compound containing two amide groups (-CONH2).  These groups are derived from carboxylic acids and amines. Diamides are found in various organic molecules and play roles in different chemical processes.  |  The diamide linkage is crucial in the structure of proteins. | 近义词: diacyl amide (less common) | 反义词: N/A
