mosquito:/məˈskiːtoʊ/| n. |蚊子|A small flying insect with a long proboscis for sucking blood, typically found near water and biting humans and animals.|例句：The mosquito bite caused a large, itchy welt. 蚊子叮咬导致一个又大又痒的肿块。

例句：Several species of mosquito transmit diseases such as malaria and Zika virus.  几种蚊子传播疟疾和寨卡病毒等疾病。


近义词：gnat (smaller), insect


反义词：无


词性:名词
