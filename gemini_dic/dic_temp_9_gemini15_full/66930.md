expenditure:/ɪkˈspɛndɪtʃər/ | n. |支出；花费；消费 |The act of spending or using something, especially money; an amount of money spent.  |例句：The expenditure on education has increased significantly. 教育支出显著增加。|例句：Our monthly expenditure is quite high. 我们的月支出相当高。|近义词：expense, outlay, cost |反义词：income, revenue, saving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Our expenditure on food is quite high. = 我们在食物上的支出很高。
The company's expenditure on research and development is increasing. = 公司在研发上的支出正在增加。

一般过去时:
Last year's expenditure was lower than this year's. = 去年的支出低于今年。
Their expenditure on travel exceeded their budget. = 他们的旅行支出超过了预算。

现在完成时:
We have reduced our expenditure this year. = 今年我们减少了支出。
The government has increased its expenditure on healthcare. = 政府增加了医疗保健支出。

过去完成时:
By the end of the year, they had exceeded their planned expenditure. = 到年底，他们的支出已经超过了计划。
Before the project started, they had underestimated the expenditure. = 项目开始前，他们低估了支出。

将来时:
Next year's expenditure is expected to be even higher. = 明年的支出预计会更高。
We will carefully monitor our expenditure in the coming months. = 未来几个月我们将仔细监控我们的支出。

虚拟语气:
If our expenditure were lower, we could save more money. = 如果我们的支出更低，我们就能省更多钱。
I wish our expenditure on marketing had been less. = 我希望我们市场营销的支出少一些。

被动语态:
A significant portion of the budget was allocated to expenditure on infrastructure. = 一大部分预算被分配到基础设施支出上。
Expenditure on new equipment is being carefully reviewed. = 新设备的支出正在被仔细审查。

疑问句:
What is the total expenditure for this project? = 这个项目的总支出是多少？
How much expenditure is allocated to each department? = 各部门分配了多少支出？

否定句:
There was no unnecessary expenditure on the project. = 这个项目没有不必要的支出。
We did not exceed our budgeted expenditure. = 我们没有超过预算支出。
