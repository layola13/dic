lurker:/ˈlɜːrkər/ | n. |潜伏者，潜藏者，窥探者 | A person who lurks; someone who spends time online, especially in forums or chat rooms, reading posts and messages but rarely or never contributing.  | Example: He was a lurker on the forum for months before finally posting a comment. 他在论坛上潜伏了几个月才最终发表评论。 |近义词：spy, watcher, observer |反义词：participant, contributor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat lurks in the bushes. = 猫潜伏在灌木丛中。
He lurks in the shadows. = 他潜伏在阴影中。

一般过去时:
The thief lurked in the alleyway. = 小偷潜伏在小巷里。
She lurked behind the door. = 她躲在门后。

现在完成时:
They have lurked online for years. = 他们已经在线潜伏多年了。
The criminal has lurked in the city for months. = 罪犯在城市里潜伏了几个月。

过去完成时:
The predator had lurked in the dark before attacking. = 捕食者在袭击之前潜伏在黑暗中。
He had lurked in the building for hours. = 他在楼里潜伏了几个小时。

将来时:
The lion will lurk in the tall grass. = 狮子将潜伏在高高的草丛中。
She will lurk in the library, observing others. = 她将潜伏在图书馆里，观察别人。

虚拟语气:
If I were a cat, I would lurk in the sun. = 如果我是猫，我会在阳光下潜伏。
I wish I could lurk unseen. = 我希望我能不被察觉地潜伏。

被动语态:
The enemy was lurked upon by the soldiers. = 士兵们潜伏着监视着敌人。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The meeting was being lurked upon by a spy. = 会议正被间谍窥探着。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，主动语态更常用)


疑问句:
Is he lurking in the shadows? = 他是否潜伏在阴影中？
Where is the snake lurking? = 蛇潜伏在哪里？

否定句:
He doesn't lurk in dark corners. = 他不会潜伏在黑暗的角落里。
She didn't lurk in the bushes. = 她没有潜伏在灌木丛中。
