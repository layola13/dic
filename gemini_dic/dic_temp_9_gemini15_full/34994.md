cloistering:/ˈklɔɪstərɪŋ/| v.|隐居；使与世隔绝；禁闭|The act of confining or secluding someone or something, often in a secluded or monastic setting.  It implies a sense of isolation and removal from the outside world. |例句：The monks dedicated their lives to cloistering themselves in prayer and contemplation.  僧侣们致力于隐居祈祷和沉思。

The nuns spent years cloistering themselves away from worldly affairs. 修女们多年来与世隔绝。


近义词：secluding, isolating, secluding, immuring, confining


反义词：integrating, exposing, opening up


词源：源自中古英语 *cloistren，最终源自拉丁语 claustrum，“封闭空间，监狱”。
