disrespectfully:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspɛktfʊli/ | adv. | 不尊重地，无礼地 | In a way that shows a lack of respect.  |例句：He spoke disrespectfully to the judge. 他对法官说话很不尊重。|例句：She acted disrespectfully towards her elders. 她对长辈的行为很不尊重。|近义词：rudely, impolitely, offensively |反义词：respectfully, politely, courteously


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He speaks disrespectfully to his teachers. = 他对老师说话很不尊重。
They treat their parents disrespectfully. = 他们对父母不尊重。


一般过去时:
He spoke disrespectfully to the officer. = 他对警官说话很不尊重。
She acted disrespectfully during the ceremony. = 她在仪式上表现得很无礼。


现在完成时:
He has consistently spoken disrespectfully to his colleagues. = 他一直对同事说话很不尊重。
She has behaved disrespectfully towards her superiors. = 她对上司的行为一直很不尊重。


过去完成时:
He had already spoken disrespectfully to her before she even said anything. = 她还没说什么，他就已经对她很不尊重了。
They had acted disrespectfully throughout the entire meeting. = 他们在整个会议期间一直表现得很无礼。


将来时:
He will likely speak disrespectfully again if he's not reprimanded. = 如果不加以训斥，他很可能会再次出言不逊。
She will probably continue to act disrespectfully if no consequences follow. = 如果没有后果，她可能会继续无礼地行事。


虚拟语气:
If he spoke disrespectfully, he would be punished. = 如果他说话不尊重，他将会受到惩罚。
I wish he hadn't spoken disrespectfully to his mother. = 我真希望他没对他母亲那样说话。


被动语态:
His behaviour was considered disrespectful. = 他的行为被认为是不尊重的。
The remark was deemed disrespectful by many. = 许多人认为这番话很不尊重。


疑问句:
Did he speak disrespectfully? = 他说话不尊重吗？
Was her behaviour disrespectful? = 她的行为不尊重吗？


否定句:
He did not speak disrespectfully to the guests. = 他没有对客人无礼。
She did not act disrespectfully at the event. = 她在那次活动中没有表现出不尊重。


PHRASE->
act disrespectfully = 行为不敬
speak disrespectfully = 说话不敬
treat disrespectfully = 对待不敬
behave disrespectfully = 表现不敬
look disrespectfully = 看待不敬


词源:
dis- (否定前缀) + respect (尊敬) + -fully (副词后缀)
