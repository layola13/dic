cookware:/ˈkʊkˌweər/| n. |厨具，炊具 |Cookware refers to the pots, pans, and other utensils used for cooking.  It encompasses a wide variety of items, from simple saucepans to complex multi-cookers.|例句：She bought a new set of cookware for her kitchen. 她为厨房买了一套新的厨具。

例句：The cookware was made of high-quality stainless steel. 这套厨具是由高质量的不锈钢制成的。
