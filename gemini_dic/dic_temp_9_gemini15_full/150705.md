parabolas:/pəˈræbələz/| n. |抛物线(复数)|A parabola is a symmetrical U-shaped curve.  It is a conic section formed by the intersection of a right circular cone with a plane parallel to a generator of the cone.|The trajectory of a projectile under the influence of gravity is a parabola.|近义词：quadratic curve|反义词：无
