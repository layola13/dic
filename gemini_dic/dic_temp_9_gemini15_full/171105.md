psyches:/ˈsaɪkiːz/| n.|精神，心灵；心态；心理状态 |The plural of "psyche".  Refers to the mind, soul, or spirit; also the mental or emotional state of a person or group.|例句：The psyches of the athletes were tested during the intense competition. 运动员们在激烈的比赛中受到了心理考验。
例句：The team's psyches were boosted by their recent victory. 球队的士气因最近的胜利而大振。|近义词：minds, spirits, souls, mentalities |反义词：bodies (in contrast), physical states |名词
