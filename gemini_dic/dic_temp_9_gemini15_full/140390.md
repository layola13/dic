oilseed:/ˈɔɪlsiːd/ | n. |油料种子 |Oilseeds are seeds that contain a significant amount of oil, typically extracted for various uses.  They are important agricultural commodities used in food, fuel, and industrial applications. |Example: Soybean is a major oilseed crop grown worldwide.  大豆是全球种植的主要油料作物。  Sunflower seeds are another popular type of oilseed. 向日葵籽是另一种常见的油料种子。|近义词：oil-bearing seed |反义词：无
