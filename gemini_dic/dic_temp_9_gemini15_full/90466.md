hubbing:/ˈhʌbɪŋ/| n. v.|轮毂加工；中心枢纽；|The process of creating hubs, often in the context of wheel manufacturing or networking.  It can also refer to the act of establishing a central point of activity or connection.|例句：The company specializes in hubbing high-precision metal parts for the aerospace industry. 这家公司专门从事航空航天工业高精度金属零件的轮毂加工。|例句：The new transportation hub is designed to improve connectivity across the region. 新建的交通枢纽旨在改善该地区的连通性。|近义词：centering, concentrating, networking|反义词：decentralizing, dispersing|名词，动词
