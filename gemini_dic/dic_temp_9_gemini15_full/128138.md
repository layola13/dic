netiquette:/ˈnɛtɪkɛt/| n. |网络礼仪|The set of rules for behaving properly online.  It encompasses appropriate communication styles, etiquette for online forums, social media, email, and other digital platforms.  It aims to create a positive and respectful online environment.|Example:  Good netiquette dictates that you should proofread your emails before sending them.  良好的网络礼仪要求你在发送电子邮件之前校对。|近义词：online etiquette, Internet etiquette, cyber etiquette |反义词：online rudeness, cyberbullying
