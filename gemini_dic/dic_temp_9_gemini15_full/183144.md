reposting: /ˌriːˈpoʊstɪŋ/ | n. | 再次发布；重新发布 | The act of posting something again, typically on social media.  This often involves sharing content that was originally posted by someone else. | Example: The reposting of the video went viral.  再次发布的视频迅速走红。 | 近义词: republishing, resharing, re-uploading | 反义词: deleting, removing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is reposting the article on his blog. 他正在在他的博客上重新发布这篇文章。
She reposts funny memes every day. 她每天都会重新发布一些搞笑的迷因。


一般过去时:
I reposted your tweet yesterday. 我昨天转发了你的推文。
They reposted the announcement on their website. 他们重新在其网站上发布了公告。


现在完成时:
She has reposted several times today. 她今天已经重新发布了好几次了。
He has reposted all the important updates. 他已经重新发布了所有重要的更新。


过去完成时:
They had reposted the news before the meeting. 他们在会议之前就已经重新发布了新闻。
She had reposted the photo before realizing it was inappropriate. 她意识到照片不合适之前就已经重新发布了。


将来时:
I will repost the video later. 我稍后会重新发布这个视频。
They will repost the announcement tomorrow. 他们明天将重新发布公告。


虚拟语气:
If I had seen the post earlier, I would have reposted it. 如果我早点看到那篇文章，我就转发了。
I wish I had reposted that inspiring quote. 我真希望我转发了那句鼓舞人心的名言。


被动语态:
The article was reposted thousands of times. 这篇文章被转发了数千次。
The image is being reposted across various social media platforms. 这张图片正在各个社交媒体平台上被重新发布。


疑问句:
Have you reposted my story yet? 你转发我的故事了吗？
Will he repost the information on LinkedIn? 他会在领英上重新发布信息吗？


否定句:
I didn't repost the image because it was low quality. 我没有转发那张图片，因为它质量太差。
She hasn't reposted anything on Instagram recently. 她最近没有在Instagram上重新发布任何东西。


PHRASE->
reposting comments: 重新发布评论
reposting photos: 重新发布照片
reposting videos: 重新发布视频
reposting content: 重新发布内容
reposting on social media: 在社交媒体上重新发布
