nonconstructively:/ˌnɒn kənˈstrʌktɪvli/ | adv. | 非建设性地，破坏性地 |  In a way that does not help to solve a problem or improve a situation; destructively.  | 例句：He criticized the plan nonconstructively, offering no alternative suggestions. 他对计划进行了非建设性的批评，没有提出任何替代方案。

例句：The meeting became unproductive due to nonconstructive arguments.  会议由于非建设性的争论而变得毫无成效。


近义词：destructively, harmfully, unhelpfully, negatively

反义词：constructively, helpfully, positively, supportively
