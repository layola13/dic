hammerers:/ˈhæmərərz/| n. |猛击者，锤击工|People who repeatedly strike with a hammer, often in a forceful or aggressive manner.  The term often implies a repetitive or intense action. |例句：The hammerers worked tirelessly, shaping the metal into intricate designs.  猛击者们不知疲倦地工作，将金属塑造成复杂的设计。

The relentless hammerers on the construction site made it difficult to concentrate. 建筑工地上无休止的锤击声让人难以集中注意力。|近义词：smiths, strikers, pounders|反义词：carvers (in the context of delicate work)
