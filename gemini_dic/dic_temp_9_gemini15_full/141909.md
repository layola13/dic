orderly:/ˈɔːrdərli/ | adj. | 整洁的；有秩序的；规规矩矩的 |  Characterized by order and regularity; neat and tidy.  It implies a state of being well-arranged and organized, free from confusion or disorder. |例句：He kept his desk orderly. 他保持他的书桌整洁。
The orderly rows of books impressed me.  整齐的书架给我留下了深刻印象。|近义词：neat, tidy, systematic, methodical |反义词：disorderly, chaotic, messy, untidy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The room is orderly. = 房间很整洁。
Her work is always orderly. = 她的工作总是井井有条。


一般过去时:
The classroom was orderly after the students left. = 学生离开后教室很整洁。
His files were orderly before the incident. = 事件发生前他的档案井然有序。


现在完成时:
They have kept the garden orderly all year. = 他们一年到头都保持花园整洁。
She has always been orderly in her approach to tasks. = 她处理事情一向井井有条。


过去完成时:
The house had been orderly before the party started. = 派对开始前房子很整洁。
Everything had been orderly until the unexpected visitor arrived. = 直到意外访客到来之前，一切都很井然有序。


将来时:
The office will be orderly after the cleaning. = 打扫之后办公室将会很整洁。
We will keep the lab orderly for the inspection. = 我们将保持实验室整洁以便检查。


虚拟语气:
If the room were orderly, I would feel more relaxed. = 如果房间整洁的话，我会感觉更放松。
I wish my desk were more orderly. = 我希望我的桌子更整洁一些。


被动语态:
The shelves are kept orderly by the librarian. = 书架由图书管理员保持整洁。
The files were kept orderly by the secretary. = 文件由秘书保持整洁。


疑问句:
Is your room orderly? = 你的房间整洁吗？
Are the documents orderly filed? = 文件是否按顺序归档？


否定句:
The room is not orderly. = 房间不整洁。
His thoughts were not orderly. = 他的思绪混乱不堪。
