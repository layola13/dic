misbehaviors:/ˌmɪsbiˈheɪvjərz/| n.|不良行为；不端行为|Improper or unacceptable behavior.  It often refers to actions that violate rules, norms, or expectations.|例句：The teacher punished the students for their misbehaviors in class.  老师惩罚了学生们在课堂上的不良行为。
The company took disciplinary action against its employees for repeated misbehaviors. 公司对员工屡教不改的不端行为采取了纪律处分。|近义词：bad behavior, misconduct, wrongdoing, infractions|反义词：good behavior, obedience, propriety|名词
