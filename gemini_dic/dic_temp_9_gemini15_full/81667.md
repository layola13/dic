green-manuring:/ˈɡriːnˈmænəriŋ/ | n. |绿肥施用；绿肥法 |The practice of plowing green crops into the soil to improve its fertility.  This adds organic matter, improves soil structure, and suppresses weeds. |Example: Green-manuring is a sustainable agricultural practice that enhances soil health.  绿肥施用是一种可持续的农业实践，能增强土壤健康。

The farmer used green-manuring to enrich the soil before planting corn.  这位农民在种植玉米前使用了绿肥法来改良土壤。|近义词：cover cropping, soil improvement |反义词：soil depletion,  chemical fertilization
