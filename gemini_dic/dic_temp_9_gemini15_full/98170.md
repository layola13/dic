inscribes:/ɪnˈskraɪb/| vt.|刻写；铭刻；题写；登记|To write or carve (words or symbols) on something, especially as a permanent record.|例句：He inscribed his name on the trophy. 他在奖杯上刻上了他的名字。|例句：The artist inscribed a dedication on the painting.艺术家在画上题词。|近义词：carve, engrave, etch|反义词：erase, delete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He inscribes his name on every book he owns. = 他在他的每一本书上都刻上自己的名字。
She inscribes inspirational quotes on her journal. = 她把励志名言写在日记里。


一般过去时:
They inscribed a message on the time capsule. = 他们把信息刻在了时间胶囊上。
The author inscribed a copy of his book for me. = 作者为我签署了一本他的书。


现在完成时:
He has inscribed his initials on the wall. = 他已将他的姓名首字母刻在了墙上。
She has inscribed many names in her address book. = 她已经在她的通讯录里登记了许多名字。


过去完成时:
They had inscribed the date on the cornerstone before the ceremony. = 仪式开始前，他们已将日期刻在了奠基石上。
She had inscribed her will before her illness worsened. = 她的病恶化之前，她已经立下了遗嘱。


将来时:

They will inscribe the winning team's name on the cup. = 他们将在奖杯上刻下获胜队的名字。


虚拟语气:
If I had a time machine, I would inscribe a message to my future self. = 如果我有一台时光机，我会给我未来的自己留言。
I wish I could inscribe my feelings into this poem. = 我希望我能把我的感情写进这首诗里。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
