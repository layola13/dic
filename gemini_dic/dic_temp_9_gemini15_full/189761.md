saltwater:/ˈsɔːltˌwɔːtər/| n. |盐水；海水 |Salty water, typically from the ocean or sea.  It is characterized by its high salt content. |Example:  The saltwater fish died when placed in freshwater. 海水鱼放在淡水中死了。  The boat floated on the saltwater.  船漂浮在海面上。 |近义词：sea water, ocean water |反义词：freshwater
