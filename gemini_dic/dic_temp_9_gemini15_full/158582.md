pleurocarpic:/ˌplʊəroʊˈkɑːrpɪk/| adj. |侧生孢蒴的；侧枝结果的| Relating to or characterized by having sporangia or fruit borne on short lateral branches, not at the apex of the main stem.  This term is primarily used in botany, referring to the reproductive structure of certain mosses and liverworts.| Example: Many pleurocarpic mosses form extensive mats on the forest floor.  许多侧生孢蒴的苔藓在森林地面上形成大片的垫状物。|近义词:laterally fruiting |反义词:acrocarpous
