masticater:/ˈmæstɪkeɪtər/| n. |咀嚼者，嚼碎者|A masticator is someone or something that chews or grinds food.  It can refer to a person, animal, or a machine designed for chewing or grinding. |例句：The powerful jaws of the alligator make it a formidable masticator.  短吻鳄强大的颌骨使它成为可怕的咀嚼者。

The machine is a masticator used in the food processing industry. 这台机器是食品加工业中使用的咀嚼机。|近义词：chewer, grinder|反义词：None specifically, but words implying swallowing without chewing could be considered antonyms.

词源:
From Late Latin *masticāre, from Latin masticare "to chew," from Greek mastikhē "a chewing," from massein "to chew."  The word's path reflects the long history of humans and animals relying on chewing to break down food for digestion.
