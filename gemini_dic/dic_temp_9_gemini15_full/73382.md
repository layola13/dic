forehands:/ˈfɔːrˌhændz/ | n. | 正手击球 | The act of hitting a ball with the front of your hand, especially in tennis or similar sports.  It refers to the strokes played with the palm facing forward.  | Example:  His forehands were powerful and accurate.  他的正手击球有力且精准。


Example: She improved her forehands significantly after taking lessons. 她上完课后，正手击球有了显著提高。


近义词：forehand stroke (正手击球)


反义词：backhand (反手)


词性:名词
