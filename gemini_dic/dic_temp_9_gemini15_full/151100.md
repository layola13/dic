parasending:/ˌpærəˈsɛndɪŋ/| n. |副发送；旁路发送|The process of sending a message or data through a secondary or alternative route, often used as a backup or to improve reliability.|例句：The system uses parasending to ensure message delivery even if the primary route fails.  系统使用副发送来确保即使主要路由失败也能发送消息。|近义词：redundancy, backup transmission|反义词：primary sending, direct sending|名词
