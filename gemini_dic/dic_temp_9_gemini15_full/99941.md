interns:/ˈɪntɜːrnz/ | n. | 实习生 | People who work in an organization, often for a limited time, to gain experience.  Interns typically receive little or no pay and are usually students or recent graduates. |  Many interns find that their internship leads to a full-time job.  许多实习生发现他们的实习会带来一份全职工作。

Many companies offer paid internships to attract top talent. 许多公司提供带薪实习以吸引顶尖人才。| trainee, apprentice | employee |  n.
