paleographist:/ˌpeɪliˈɒɡrəfɪst/|/ˈpeɪlioʊɡræfɪst/| n. |古文字学家|A paleographist is a scholar who studies ancient writing systems and scripts.  They analyze the physical characteristics of historical documents to understand their origins, dating, and authorship.  This involves expertise in handwriting analysis, material science, and historical context.|The paleographist meticulously examined the medieval manuscript, noting the variations in script and ink.|近义词：paleographer, epigrapher|反义词：None
