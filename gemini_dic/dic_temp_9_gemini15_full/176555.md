re-shifted:/ˌriːˈʃɪftɪd/| v.|重新调整，重新分配|The company re-shifted its marketing strategy after a poor sales quarter. 公司在销售季度不佳后重新调整了其营销策略。|近义词：realigned, rearranged, repositioned|反义词：left unchanged, maintained


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-shift their priorities every year. = 他们每年都会重新调整他们的优先级。
The company re-shifts its resources to meet changing demands. = 公司会重新分配资源以满足不断变化的需求。


一般过去时:
The team re-shifted its focus to product development. = 团队将重点重新转向产品开发。
He re-shifted the boxes to make more room. = 他重新摆放了箱子以便腾出更多空间。


现在完成时:
They have re-shifted their investments into renewable energy. = 他们已经将其投资重新转向可再生能源。
The government has re-shifted its focus to education reform. = 政府已经将重点重新转向教育改革。


过去完成时:
She had re-shifted her plans after receiving new information. = 在收到新信息后，她已经重新调整了她的计划。
They had re-shifted the blame onto someone else. = 他们已经把责任推卸给了别人。


将来时:
We will re-shift our marketing efforts towards social media. = 我们将把营销工作重新转向社交媒体。
The company will re-shift its production to a new facility. = 公司将把生产转移到一个新的工厂。


虚拟语气:
If the situation changed, we would re-shift our strategy immediately. = 如果情况改变，我们将立即调整策略。
I wish we had re-shifted our resources sooner. = 我希望我们早点重新分配资源。


被动语态:
The blame was re-shifted to the junior staff. = 责任被推卸给了初级员工。
The schedule was re-shifted to accommodate the new project. = 时间表被重新调整以适应新项目。


疑问句:
Will they re-shift their focus to the domestic market? = 他们会将重点重新转向国内市场吗？
Have they re-shifted the furniture in the office? = 他们是否已经重新摆放了办公室的家具？


否定句:
They did not re-shift their positions on the issue. = 他们在这个问题上并没有改变立场。
The company has not re-shifted its focus from its core business. = 公司没有将重点从核心业务转移。


PHRASE->
re-shift priorities = 重新调整优先级
re-shift focus = 重新集中注意力
re-shift resources = 重新分配资源
re-shift the blame = 推卸责任
re-shift the burden = 转嫁负担
