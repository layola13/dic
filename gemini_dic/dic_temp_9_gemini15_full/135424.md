nonpersecutory: /ˌnɒnˌpɜːrsɪˈkjuːtəri/ | adj. | 非迫害性的 | Not involving or intended to cause persecution.  This adjective describes actions, policies, or systems that do not aim to oppress, harass, or discriminate against a particular group or individual.  It implies a lack of intent to inflict harm based on factors like race, religion, or political belief.

例句：

The court ruled that the police actions were nonpersecutory, finding no evidence of discriminatory intent. = 法院裁定警方的行为是非迫害性的，未发现歧视意图的证据。

The new immigration policy aims to be nonpersecutory and fair to all applicants. = 新的移民政策旨在对所有申请者采取非迫害性和公平的态度。


近义词：tolerant, equitable, impartial, unbiased, non-discriminatory


反义词：persecutory, oppressive, discriminatory, biased, intolerant


词性:形容词
