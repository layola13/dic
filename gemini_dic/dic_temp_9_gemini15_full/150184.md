pancaked:/ˈpænkeɪkt/| v. |压扁；使成薄饼状 |To flatten something, often forcefully, to a thin, pancake-like shape.  |例句：The car pancaked after the impact.  车祸后汽车被压扁了。|例句：The hail pancaked the crops.  冰雹把庄稼压扁了。|近义词：flatten,squash,compress |反义词：inflate, expand


USAGE->
一般现在时:

He pancakes the dough before frying it. = 他在煎之前把面团压扁。

一般过去时:
