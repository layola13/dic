cabbalistic:/ˌkæbəˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|卡巴拉的；神秘的；秘传的 | Relating to the Kabbalah, a system of Jewish mysticism.  The term often implies something esoteric, mysterious, or secret. | Example:  The novel contained many cabbalistic symbols and allusions, leaving readers puzzled.  这部小说包含许多卡巴拉的象征和典故，使读者感到困惑。  |近义词：esoteric, mystical, occult |反义词：clear, straightforward, explicit
