flu:/fluː/| n.|流感，流行性感冒|Influenza, commonly known as the flu, is a contagious respiratory illness caused by influenza viruses.  Symptoms can include fever, cough, sore throat, muscle aches, and fatigue.  |例句：The flu is going around; many people are sick. 流感正在流行；许多人都病了。|例句：She missed a week of work due to the flu. 因为流感她缺勤了一周。|近义词：influenza, grippe|反义词：health, wellness|名词
