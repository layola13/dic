eail:/eɪl/ | n. | 邮件 | email is a short form of electronic mail, referring to messages sent electronically over a network.|  I sent an email to my boss. 我给老板发了一封邮件。| message, letter |  snail mail | noun


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I send emails every day. = 我每天都发邮件。
He receives many emails at work. = 他在工作中收到很多邮件。


一般过去时:
She sent an email yesterday. = 她昨天发了一封邮件。
They received my email last week. = 他们上周收到了我的邮件。


现在完成时:
I have sent three emails this morning. = 我今天早上已经发了三封邮件。
She has received no reply to her email. = 她还没有收到她邮件的回复。


过去完成时:
He had sent the email before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经发了邮件。
They had received several emails before leaving. = 他们离开前已经收到了几封邮件。


将来时:
I will send an email tomorrow. = 我明天会发一封邮件。
She will receive the email by Friday. = 她将在周五收到邮件。


虚拟语气:
If I had sent the email earlier, he would have received it. = 如果我早点发邮件，他就会收到了。
I wish I had sent that email sooner. = 我真希望我早点发那封邮件。


被动语态:
The email was sent yesterday. = 邮件是昨天发的。
The emails are being processed now. = 邮件正在处理中。


疑问句:
Did you send the email? = 你发了邮件吗？
Has she received my email yet? = 她收到我的邮件了吗？


否定句:
I didn't send the email. = 我没有发邮件。
She hasn't received my email yet. = 她还没有收到我的邮件。
