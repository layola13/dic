lamplighter:/ˈlæmpˌlaɪtər/| n. |燃气路灯工人|A person whose job was to light and extinguish gas lamps in streets and public areas, typically at night.|例句：In the old days, lamplighters were an essential part of city life.  从前，燃气路灯工人是城市生活中不可或缺的一部分。|例句：The lamplighter made his rounds, carefully igniting each gas lamp.  燃气路灯工人巡视，小心地点燃每一盏煤气灯。|近义词：gaslighter (in a historical context)|反义词：None specifically, but the job is now obsolete.
