girdled:/ˈɡɜːrld/ | vt. |用带子束缚；环绕；包围；| to encircle or surround something, often tightly; to fasten a belt or girdle around something or someone. |例句：The city was girdled by a high wall.  这座城市被高墙环绕。

例句：She girdled her waist with a silken sash. 她用丝带束起腰肢。


近义词：encircle, surround, encompass, encase | 反义词：unenclosed, open, free


词性：动词
