elucidates:/ɪˈluːsɪdeɪts/ | vt. |阐明；解释；说明 | To make something clear; to explain something in a way that is easy to understand.  | The professor elucidates complex theories in a simple and accessible manner. 教授以简单易懂的方式阐明了复杂的理论。| clarifies, explains, illuminates | obfuscates, confuses |动词

He elucidates the concept clearly. 他清晰地阐明了这个概念。
