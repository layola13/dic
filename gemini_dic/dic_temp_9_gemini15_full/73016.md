footbrakes:/ˈfʊtbreɪks/| n.|脚刹，脚踏式制动器|Footbrakes are brakes operated by a pedal that is pressed by the foot. They are commonly found in cars, bicycles, and other vehicles.|例句：The car's footbrakes failed, causing a serious accident. 这辆车的脚刹失灵，导致一起严重的事故。|例句：He slammed on the footbrakes to avoid hitting the pedestrian. 他猛地踩下脚刹以避免撞到行人。|近义词：brakes, foot pedals|反义词：accelerator, gas pedal
