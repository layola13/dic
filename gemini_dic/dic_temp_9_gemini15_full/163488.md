preconversation: /ˌpriːkɒnvərˈseɪʃən/ | n. |会谈前；会谈前的准备 |A preconversation is a period of time or activity that takes place before a conversation, often involving preparation or anticipation. It might include reviewing notes, thinking about talking points, or mentally preparing for the interaction.  | Example:  The preconversation involved reviewing the client's file and outlining key discussion points. 会谈前的准备工作包括回顾客户档案和列出关键讨论要点。|近义词：preparation, preliminary discussion, groundwork |反义词：post-conversation, aftermath
