codling:/ˈkɒdlɪŋ/| n.|小苹果；苹果蠹蛾幼虫|A small apple, especially one of a variety grown for cooking.  Also, the larva of the codling moth, a pest that infests apples.|例句：The orchard yielded a bumper crop of codlings this year.今年果园产出了大量的小苹果。|例句：The codlings had burrowed deep into the apples. 苹果蠹蛾幼虫已深深地钻入苹果中。|近义词：small apple, appleworm (for the larva)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a large, mature apple.
