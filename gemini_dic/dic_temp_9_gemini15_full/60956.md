elevate:/ˈɛləveɪt/| vt.| 提升；举起；提高；使兴奋 | to raise to a higher level; to lift up; to improve or enhance; to uplift emotionally | 例句：The elevator elevated us to the tenth floor. 电梯把我们送到了十楼。

例句：
The news elevated her spirits.  这个消息使她精神振奋。


近义词：raise, lift, hoist, uplift, enhance, improve

反义词：depress, lower, degrade, diminish

词性:动词


USAGE->

The company elevates its employees to higher positions based on merit. 公司根据能力提升员工到更高的职位。
