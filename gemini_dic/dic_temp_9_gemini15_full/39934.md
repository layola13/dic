construct:/kənˈstrʌkt/ | v., n. | 建造；构造；构建；结构 | To build or create something, typically something complex or requiring skill;  a thing constructed; a structure. |例句：They are constructing a new bridge. 他们正在建造一座新桥。

The architect presented a detailed construct of the building. 建筑师展示了建筑物的详细结构。|近义词：build, create, fabricate, form, edifice |反义词：destroy, demolish, deconstruct
