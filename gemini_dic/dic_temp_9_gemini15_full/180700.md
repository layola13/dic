regrets:/rɪˈɡrɛts/ | n. | 后悔，遗憾 |  A feeling of sadness, repentance, or disappointment over something that has happened or been done, or something that has not been done.  | I have no regrets about my decision. 我对我的决定没有任何后悔。  |  Repentance, remorse, sorrow | Satisfaction, contentment | noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He regrets his actions. = 他后悔自己的行为。
She regrets missing the concert. = 她后悔错过了音乐会。

一般过去时:
I regretted telling him the truth. = 我后悔告诉了他真相。
They regretted their hasty decision. = 他们后悔自己草率的决定。

现在完成时:
I have regretted this decision for years. = 我多年来一直后悔这个决定。
She has regretted her words since she spoke them. = 从她说了那些话起，她就一直后悔。

过去完成时:
He had regretted not studying harder before the exam. = 他考试前后悔没有更努力学习。
We had regretted our absence from the party. = 我们后悔没有参加派对。

将来时:
I will regret this if I don't try. = 如果我不尝试，我会后悔的。
They will regret ignoring the warning. = 他们会后悔忽视警告的。

虚拟语气:
I wish I hadn't said that; I regret saying it. = 我真希望我没说过那些话；我后悔说了那些话。
He regrets that he missed the opportunity.  = 他后悔错过了那个机会。

被动语态:
His actions are regretted by many. = 他的行为受到许多人的谴责。
The missed opportunity is deeply regretted. = 错失的机会令人深感遗憾。

疑问句:
Do you have any regrets? = 你有什么后悔的事吗？
Does she regret her choice of career? = 她后悔自己选择的职业吗？

否定句:
I have no regrets. = 我没有任何后悔。
He doesn't regret his actions. = 他不后悔自己的行为。


PHRASE->
have regrets = 感到后悔
express regrets = 表示遗憾
deep regrets = 深深的遗憾
offer regrets = 表示歉意
with regret = 遗憾地
