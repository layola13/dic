restaining:/rɪˈsteɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 再染色，重新染色 |  The act of staining something again, typically after it has faded or been damaged.  It involves applying a stain to restore or change the color of a surface. | 例句：The antique furniture required restaining to restore its original luster.  = 这件古董家具需要重新染色以恢复其原有的光泽。

The artist decided to restain the wooden sculpture with a darker shade. = 艺术家决定用较深的颜色重新染色木雕。


近义词：recoloring, refinishing | 反义词：bleaching, stripping (removing existing stain) | 动词
