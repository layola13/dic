neighing: /ˈneɪɪŋ/ | n. |马嘶声|The sound a horse makes.  |例句：The neighing of the horses filled the stable. 马的嘶鸣声充满了马厩。|近义词： whinny |反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The horse is neighing loudly. = 马大声嘶鸣。
They neigh every morning. = 它们每天早上都嘶鸣。


一般过去时:
The horse neighed before the race. = 赛马之前，马嘶叫了一声。
We heard the horses neighing last night. = 昨晚我们听到马嘶鸣。


现在完成时:
The horse has neighed several times today. = 这匹马今天已经嘶鸣了好几次。
The foal has never neighed before. = 这只小马驹以前从未嘶鸣过。


过去完成时:
The horse had neighed before the rider mounted. = 骑手骑上马之前，马已经嘶鸣了。
The horses had neighed all night long. = 马儿整夜都在嘶鸣。


将来时:
The horse will neigh when it sees its owner. = 马看到它的主人就会嘶鸣。
The foals will likely neigh soon. = 小马驹们很可能会很快嘶鸣。


虚拟语气:
If the horse neighed, I would know it was nearby. = 如果马嘶鸣，我就知道它在附近。
I wish the horse would neigh so I could find it. = 我希望马能嘶鸣，这样我就能找到它。


被动语态:
(Neighing is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Did you hear the horse neighing? = 你听到马嘶鸣了吗？
Is that horse neighing again? = 那匹马又在嘶鸣了吗？


否定句:
The horse didn't neigh this morning. = 马今天早上没有嘶鸣。
The foal hasn't neighed yet. = 小马驹还没嘶鸣。


PHRASE->
a loud neighing = 一阵响亮的嘶鸣
a soft neighing = 一阵轻柔的嘶鸣
the horse's neighing = 马的嘶鸣
a long neighing = 长长的嘶鸣
a sudden neighing = 突然的嘶鸣
