progenote:/ˈproʊdʒɪnoʊt/ | n. | 祖先基因组；原始基因组 | The progenote is a hypothetical ancestor of all extant life. It represents the earliest form of life from which all other organisms have evolved.  The concept of a progenote is crucial in understanding the origins of life and the evolution of biological systems.  Researchers are still debating the exact nature and characteristics of the progenote. | 近义词：ancestor, precursor | 反义词：descendant
