preshipment:/ˈpriːʃɪpmənt/| n. |装运前；发货前|The process or period of time before goods are shipped.  It often involves activities like inspection, documentation, and preparation for transportation. |例句：Preshipment inspection is crucial to ensure the quality of the goods.  装运前检验对于确保货物质量至关重要。

例句：We need to complete all preshipment procedures before the deadline. 我们需要在截止日期前完成所有装运前手续。


近义词：pre-shipment, before shipment, prior to shipment


反义词：post-shipment, after shipment


词性:名词
