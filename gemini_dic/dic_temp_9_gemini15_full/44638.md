crownlet: /ˈkraʊnlət/ | n. | 小皇冠；小王冠 | A small crown.  It often refers to a decorative element resembling a crown, rather than an actual regal crown.  | Example: The ballerina wore a crownlet of sparkling jewels in her hair.  舞者在头上戴着一顶闪闪发光的珠宝小皇冠。 |近义词：coronet, diadem (depending on context) | 反义词：None directly; antonyms would relate to the opposite of a crown's symbolism (e.g., subjugation, defeat).
