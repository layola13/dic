governorship:/ˈɡʌvənərʃɪp/| n. |州长职位；总督职位；管理；统治|The office or term of office of a governor.  It refers to the position of authority and responsibility held by a governor, typically of a state, province, or colony.|例句：He aspired to the governorship of the state. 他渴望担任该州州长。|例句：The governorship is a powerful position. 州长职位是一个有权力的职位。|近义词：governance, administration, rule|反义词：subjection, subordination
