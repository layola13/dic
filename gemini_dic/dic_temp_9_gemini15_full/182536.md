rents:/rents/| n.|租金；租费|The money paid for the use of something, especially property.|例句：The rents in this city are incredibly high.  这个城市的租金非常高。|例句：They raised the rents by 10%.他们把租金提高了10%。|近义词：rental, lease, hire charge|反义词：N/A|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rent is due on the first of the month. = 房租在每月第一天到期。
He pays his rent on time every month. = 他每个月都按时付房租。


一般过去时:
They rented a house last year. = 他们去年租了一套房子。
We paid the rent last week. = 我们上周付了房租。


现在完成时:
She has paid her rent for this month. = 她已经付了这个月的房租。
We have lived here since we rented this apartment. = 自从我们租了这套公寓以来，我们就住在这里。


过去完成时:
They had already paid the rent before the landlord called. = 房东打电话之前，他们已经付了房租。
I had never rented an apartment before moving to the city. = 在搬到城市之前，我从未租过公寓。


将来时:
We will rent a car for our vacation. = 我们将在假期租一辆车。
They will pay the rent next week. = 他们下周将支付房租。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would rent a bigger apartment. = 如果我有更多钱，我会租一套更大的公寓。
I wish I could rent a house by the sea. = 我希望我能租到海边的房子。


被动语态:
The apartment was rented quickly. = 这套公寓很快就被租出去了。
The house is being rented out for the summer. = 这房子正在出租度假。


疑问句:
How much is the rent? = 租金是多少？
What is included in the rent? = 租金包括什么？


否定句:
They didn't pay the rent on time. = 他们没有按时付房租。
We haven't rented a car yet. = 我们还没租车。
