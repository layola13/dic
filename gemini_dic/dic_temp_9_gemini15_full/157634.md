piton:/ˈpaɪtɑːn/| n. |岩钉；冰锥|A piton is a metal spike, usually of iron or steel, driven into a rock face for support, especially in climbing.|例句：Experienced climbers often carry a variety of pitons for different rock types. 经验丰富的登山者经常携带各种类型的岩钉以适应不同的岩石类型。

The climbers hammered pitons into the rock face to secure their ropes. 登山者将岩钉敲入岩壁以固定绳索。|近义词：spike, peg|反义词：无
