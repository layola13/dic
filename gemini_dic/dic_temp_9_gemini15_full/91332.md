hydrophora:/haɪˈdrɒfərə/| n. |水肿；积水|A condition characterized by the abnormal accumulation of fluid in body tissues, cavities, or organs.  This can manifest in various forms depending on the location and cause of the fluid buildup.  It's often a symptom of an underlying medical condition rather than a disease itself.| Example:  The patient presented with hydrophora in the lower extremities, indicating potential heart failure. 患者下肢出现水肿，这表明可能有心力衰竭。|近义词：edema, swelling, effusion|反义词：dehydration|名词
