flexed:/flekst/| adj. v.|弯曲的；屈服的；紧张的|Bent or curved;  made or become tense or rigid.|The athlete flexed his muscles before the race. 运动员在比赛前弯曲了他的肌肉。  He flexed his arm to show off his biceps. 他弯曲手臂炫耀他的二头肌。|近义词：bent, curved, tense, strained|反义词：straight, relaxed, loose
