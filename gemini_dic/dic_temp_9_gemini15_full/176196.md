re-ask:/ˌriːˈæsk/| vt.|重新询问，再次询问|To ask again; to ask a second time.  This often implies a previous question was unclear, incomplete, or unsatisfactory.|例句：I had to re-ask the question because he didn't understand me the first time. = 我不得不重新问这个问题，因为他第一次没听懂我。

例句：She re-asked him about his plans for the weekend. = 她再次问他关于他周末计划的事情。


近义词：ask again, inquire again, query again|反义词：answer, respond|词性:动词
