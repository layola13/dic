non-stock:/ˈnɒnˈstɒk/ | adj. |非股票的，非库存的 |Not relating to or involving stocks or stock certificates; not held in stock.  This term typically refers to something that is not part of a company's publicly traded shares or inventory. |例句：The company offered non-stock compensation to its employees. 这家公司向其员工提供非股票补偿。  The shop had a non-stock item that they had to order. 这家商店有一种非库存商品，他们不得不订购。|近义词：unlisted, non-equity |反义词：stock, in-stock
