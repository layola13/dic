rosiny:/ˈrɒzɪni/| adj.|松香的，像松香的|Resembling or having the properties of rosin, a solid resin obtained from pine trees.  It often describes something sticky, brittle, or having a yellowish color.|The rosiny bow stuck to the strings of the violin.|近义词：resinous, gummy|反义词：smooth, slippery
