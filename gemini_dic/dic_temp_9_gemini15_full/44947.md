cryptomat:/krɪpˈtoʊmæt/| n. |密码机，加密机 |A cryptomat is a machine used for encrypting and decrypting messages or data.  It typically involves complex algorithms and cryptographic keys to ensure secure communication. |Example: The bank uses a cryptomat to secure its online transactions. 银行使用密码机来保护其在线交易的安全。|近义词：encryption device, cipher machine |反义词：None  |名词

词源:
The word "cryptomat" is a blend of "crypto" (from "cryptography," meaning secret writing) and "mat" (short for "machine" or "automaton").  It's a relatively modern coinage reflecting the technological nature of the device.
