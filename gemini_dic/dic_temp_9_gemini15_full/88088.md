highresolution: /ˌhaɪˌrezəˈluːʃən/ | adj. | 高分辨率的 | Having a high level of detail and clarity, typically referring to images or displays.  It indicates a large number of pixels or other data points used to create the image, resulting in a sharp and detailed picture. | Example: The high-resolution photograph revealed intricate details of the landscape.  高分辨率的照片展现了景观的复杂细节。 The monitor boasts a stunning high-resolution display. 这台显示器拥有令人惊叹的高分辨率显示效果。 | 近义词: sharp, detailed, clear, crisp | 反义词: low-resolution, blurry, indistinct |  形容词
