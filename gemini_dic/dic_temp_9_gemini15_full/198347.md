showboat:/ˈʃoʊboʊt/| n. v.|炫耀者；夸夸其谈者；戏船；|A showboat is a person who behaves in a showy or boastful manner, often to attract attention.  It can also refer to a boat used for theatrical performances.|例句：He's such a showboat; he always has to be the center of attention. 他真是个爱炫耀的人；他总是要成为关注的中心。

He tried to showboat his skills on the basketball court but missed every shot. 他试图在篮球场上炫耀他的技巧，但却一个球都没投进。|近义词：boaster, braggart, exhibitionist|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming|名词，动词

否定句:

PHRASE->

词源:
The word "showboat" originally referred to a riverboat used for theatrical performances. The showy nature of these performances led to the word's figurative use to describe boastful or attention-seeking behavior.
