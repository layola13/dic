conducibleness:/ˌkɒndjuːˈsɪbəlnəs/| n. |适宜性，有助性|The conducibleness of the environment to learning is crucial. 环境对学习的适宜性至关重要。|近义词：suitability, aptness,  propitiousness|反义词：inhibition, hindrance, impediment|

英文解析：The quality or state of being conducive; the extent to which something contributes to a particular outcome or state.  It refers to the capacity of something to promote or encourage a particular outcome.


例句：

The conducibleness of a quiet atmosphere to concentration is well-known.  安静的环境有助于集中注意力，这是众所周知的。

The study examined the conducibleness of different teaching methods to student engagement.  该研究考察了不同教学方法对学生参与度的适宜性。


词源:  Derived from the adjective "conducive," which comes from the Latin word "conduce," meaning "to lead" or "to contribute."  The suffix "-ness" transforms the adjective into a noun, signifying the quality or state of being conducive.
