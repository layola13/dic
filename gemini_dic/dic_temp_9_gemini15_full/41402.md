It can be a simple hand-held device or a more complex machine used in agricultural settings.  | Example:  She used a corncutter to quickly shuck the corn for the barbecue. 她用剥玉米粒器快速地剥玉米准备烧烤。 |近义词：corn stripper, corn shucker |反义词：None
