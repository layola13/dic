gas-up:/ɡæs ʌp/| vt.|加油；加燃料|To fill a vehicle's fuel tank with gasoline or other fuel.|例句：I need to gas up my car before the road trip. 我需要在公路旅行前给我的车加油。|例句：Let's gas up the lawnmower before we start mowing.  让我们在开始割草之前给割草机加油。|近义词：refuel, fill up|反义词：drain, empty
