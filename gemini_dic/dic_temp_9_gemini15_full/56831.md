dorms:/dɔːrmz/| n.|宿舍；寝室|Plural of dorm.  Dorms are buildings or rooms where students live, especially in a college or university.|Example:  Many students live in the dorms on campus.  许多学生住在校园宿舍。|近义词：residence halls, student housing|反义词：private accommodation, off-campus housing
