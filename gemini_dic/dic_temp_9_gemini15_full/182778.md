repatriates:/ˌriːˈpeɪtrieɪts/ | v. |遣返；归还；使回国 |To return (someone) to their own country.  |The government repatriated the refugees to their homeland. 政府将难民遣送回国。|近义词：repatriate, send back, return |反义词：expatriate, exile


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company repatriates its employees annually. = 公司每年遣返其员工。
She repatriates her profits to her home country. = 她将利润汇回祖国。


一般过去时:
The soldiers were repatriated after the war ended. = 战争结束后，士兵们被遣返回国。
He repatriated his family to the United States. = 他将家人遣返回美国。


现在完成时:
They have repatriated all the hostages. = 他们已经遣返了所有的人质。
The government has repatriated thousands of refugees. = 政府已经遣返了数千名难民。


过去完成时:
The company had repatriated its assets before the sanctions were imposed. = 在制裁实施之前，该公司已将其资产遣返回国。
Before the crisis, they had repatriated most of their investment. = 在危机爆发前，他们已经将大部分投资遣返回国。


将来时:
The government will repatriate the remaining citizens next month. = 政府将于下个月遣返其余公民。
We will repatriate the stolen artifacts to their country of origin. = 我们将把被盗文物遣返回其原产国。


虚拟语气:
If the war ended, they would repatriate the prisoners of war. = 如果战争结束，他们将会遣返战俘。
It would be good if they repatriated all the refugees safely. = 如果他们能安全地遣返所有难民就好了。


被动语态:
The refugees were repatriated to their villages. = 难民们被遣返回他们的村庄。
The stolen paintings are being repatriated to Italy. = 被盗的画作正在被遣返回意大利。


疑问句:
Will the government repatriate the political dissidents? = 政府会遣返政治异议人士吗？
When will the artifacts be repatriated? = 文物什么时候会被遣返？


否定句:
The government did not repatriate the illegal immigrants. = 政府没有遣返非法移民。
They refused to repatriate the stolen treasures. = 他们拒绝遣返被盗的宝藏。
