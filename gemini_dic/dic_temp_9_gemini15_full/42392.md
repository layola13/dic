countercampaign: /ˈkaʊntərkæmpeɪn/ | n. | 反宣传活动，反攻宣传 | A campaign designed to counteract or oppose another campaign.  It often involves refuting the claims or arguments of the opposing campaign, and presenting a different perspective or set of facts. | Example: The politician launched a countercampaign to address the negative advertising from his opponent.  这位政治家发起了一场反宣传活动来应对对手的负面广告。|近义词：counteroffensive, countermeasure, rebuttal | 反义词：campaign (in the context of being the original campaign being countered)
