coniferization:/ˌkɒnɪfəraɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |松柏化；针叶林化|The process by which a landscape or ecosystem becomes dominated by coniferous trees.  This can be a natural process or result from human intervention, such as afforestation with conifers. |例句：The coniferization of the area led to a decrease in biodiversity.  该地区的松柏化导致生物多样性下降。The rapid coniferization of the mountain slopes was a concern for environmentalists. 山坡的快速松柏化令环保主义者担忧。|近义词：conification, afforestation (with conifers)|反义词：deforestation, deciduousness
