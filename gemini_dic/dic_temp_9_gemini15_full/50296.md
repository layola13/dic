deplane:/dɪˈpleɪn/ | vt. |下飞机；离开飞机 | To leave an aircraft.  |例句：Passengers deplaned quickly and efficiently after the flight.  乘客们在航班结束后迅速有效地下飞机了。

例句：We deplaned at Gate 12. 我们在12号登机口下飞机。


近义词：disembark, alight


反义词：board, embark


词性：动词


USAGE->

词源:
The word "deplane" is a back-formation from "plane," meaning "airplane".  It's a relatively modern word, appearing in the 20th century as air travel became commonplace.  The prefix "de-" implies the act of leaving or removing oneself from something.
