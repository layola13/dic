reunitedly:/ˌriːˈjuːnɪtɪdli/| adv.| 团圆地；重聚地 | In a way that shows or suggests reunion or being reunited.  It describes the manner in which something is done, emphasizing the aspect of coming together again. |例句：The families reunitedly celebrated the return of their loved ones.  家庭团圆地庆祝亲人的回归。

The long-lost siblings reunitedly embraced after decades apart. 分隔几十年后，失散已久的兄弟姐妹团聚相拥。


近义词：together, collectively, jointly, in union


反义词：separately, individually, apart


词性: 副词
