mariposa:/ˌmæriˈpoʊsə/| n.|蝴蝶|The word "mariposa" is Spanish for "butterfly." It is used in English, particularly in the names of places and organizations associated with butterflies or California, where the word is common.  |Example: The Mariposa Grove of giant sequoia trees is a breathtaking sight.  (玛丽波萨巨杉树林是一个令人叹为观止的景象。)  |近义词：butterfly, moth (depending on context)|反义词：无
