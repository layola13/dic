glibbest:/ˈɡlɪbɪst/| adj.|最油嘴滑舌的；最圆滑的；最滔滔不绝的 |Most fluent and voluble, often to the point of being insincere or shallow.  It is the superlative form of "glib".|例句：His glibbest pronouncements failed to convince anyone of his sincerity. 他最油嘴滑舌的声明未能让任何人相信他的真诚。

His glibbest sales pitch, though impressive, lacked substance. 他最圆滑的推销词，虽然令人印象深刻，但却缺乏实质内容。


近义词：fluent, articulate, voluble, smooth-talking, silver-tongued, facile

反义词：halting, hesitant, stammering, inarticulate, tongue-tied


词源：源自中古英语 *glibbe，意为“光滑的；闪亮的”。这个词可能与古英语 *glīdan（“滑动”）有关，暗示着说话流畅但缺乏深度的特点。
