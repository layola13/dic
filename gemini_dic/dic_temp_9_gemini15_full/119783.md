mignonette:/ˌmɪnjəˈnɛt/| n.| mignonette (植物)细香葱；(香水)含细香葱香的香水 |A mignonette is a small, fragrant plant with small, greenish-yellow flowers. It is often used in perfumes and potpourri.  The word also refers to a type of perfume with a similar scent.|例句：The garden was filled with the delicate fragrance of mignonette. 花园里充满了细香葱的芬芳。|The perfume was a delicate blend of rose and mignonette. 这款香水是玫瑰和细香葱的精致混合。|近义词：reseda (指植物)|反义词：无
