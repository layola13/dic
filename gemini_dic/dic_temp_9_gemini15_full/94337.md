imping:/ɪmˈpɪŋ/| vt.|撞击；侵犯；影响|To strike or impinge upon; to encroach or infringe upon; to have an effect or impact on.|例句：The falling tree impinged upon the house.  倒下的树撞上了房子。


例句：The loud music impinged on my concentration. 嘈杂的音乐影响了我的注意力。


近义词：strike, affect, encroach, infringe|反义词：support, aid, assist|动词
