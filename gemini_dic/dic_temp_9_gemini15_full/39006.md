confirms:/kənˈfɜːrmz/ | vt. | 确认；证实；批准 | To state or show that something is definitely true or correct.  |例句：The evidence confirms my suspicions.证据证实了我的怀疑。

例句：The laboratory tests confirmed that she had the virus. 实验室测试证实她感染了病毒。


近义词：verify, substantiate, validate, corroborate | 反义词：deny, refute, disprove | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He confirms the appointment. 他确认了预约。
She confirms the booking. 她确认了预订。


一般过去时:
They confirmed the news. 他们证实了这个消息。
He confirmed his attendance. 他确认了出席。


现在完成时:
She has confirmed her participation. 她已经确认了她的参与。
We have confirmed the details. 我们已经确认了细节。


过去完成时:
They had confirmed the results before the announcement. 他们在宣布之前已经确认了结果。
He had confirmed his travel plans. 他已经确认了他的旅行计划。


将来时:
She will confirm the date tomorrow. 她明天将确认日期。
They will confirm the order soon. 他们很快就会确认订单。


虚拟语气:
If he confirmed the story, I would believe him. 如果他证实了这个故事，我会相信他。
I wish he would confirm the information. 我希望他能确认这个信息。


被动语态:
The booking was confirmed. 预订已确认。
The details were confirmed yesterday. 细节昨天已确认。


疑问句:
Has she confirmed her arrival yet? 她是否已经确认到达？
Did he confirm the meeting time? 他是否确认了会议时间？


否定句:
He did not confirm the rumour. 他没有证实这个谣言。
She hasn't confirmed the address yet. 她还没有确认地址。


PHRASE->
confirm the booking: 确认预订
confirm the order: 确认订单
confirm the details: 确认细节
confirm the date: 确认日期
confirm the time: 确认时间


词源:
From Old French *confirmer, from Latin confirmare "to strengthen, make firm," from com- "together" + firmare "to make firm," from firmus "firm, strong."
