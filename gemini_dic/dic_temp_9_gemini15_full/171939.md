/ˈpʌpɪtɪərɪŋ/ | n. |木偶戏；操纵木偶术 | The art or skill of manipulating puppets.  It involves skillful control of puppets to create a performance, often telling a story or conveying a message. | The puppeteering in the show was incredibly lifelike.  这场演出中的木偶表演栩栩如生。 The children were mesmerized by the puppeteering. 孩子们被木偶表演迷住了。| puppetry, marionette manipulation |  (无明显反义词，可考虑 "lack of skill in puppetry" 或 "static display") | 名词
