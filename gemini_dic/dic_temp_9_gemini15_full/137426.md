nontheist:/ˈnɒnθiːɪst/|/ˈnɑːnθiːɪst/| n. |非神论者|A person who does not believe in the existence of God or gods.  A nontheist may or may not have a belief system; the defining feature is a lack of belief in a deity.|例句：Many nontheists are also atheists, but some nontheists may hold other beliefs that are not strictly theistic. 许多非神论者也是无神论者，但一些非神论者可能持有其他非严格神论的信仰。|近义词：atheist, agnostic, secularist |反义词：theist, believer
