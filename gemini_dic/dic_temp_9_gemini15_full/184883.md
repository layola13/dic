retailing:/ˈriːteɪlɪŋ/| n.|零售业；零售；零售业务|The act of selling goods to consumers, typically in small quantities.  Retailing involves various activities, from sourcing products to displaying them attractively and processing transactions.|例句：The company's retailing strategy focuses on online sales.|近义词：retail trade, retail sales|反义词：wholesaling
