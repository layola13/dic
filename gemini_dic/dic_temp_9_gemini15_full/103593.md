joinder:/ˈdʒɔɪndər/ | n. |结合；合并；共同起诉；加入 | The act of joining or being joined; the state of being joined.  It can refer to the legal process of adding parties or claims to an existing lawsuit, or to the act of joining things together generally. |例句：The joinder of the two companies created a powerful new entity.  两个公司的合并创造了一个强大的新实体。

例句：The plaintiff sought joinder of additional defendants in the lawsuit. 原告寻求在诉讼中加入其他被告。


近义词：union, combination, conjunction, merger | 反义词：separation, division, severance | 词性：名词
