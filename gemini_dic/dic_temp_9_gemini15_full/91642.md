hyperbolism:/haɪˈpɜːrbəlɪzəm/| n. |夸张法，夸大其词|The use of exaggeration as a rhetorical device or figure of speech.  It is often used to create emphasis or humor, and is not meant to be taken literally. |例句：Her description of the party was pure hyperbolism; it wasn't nearly as wild as she claimed.  她的派对描述完全是夸张；并没有她说的那么疯狂。

例句：The comedian's act relied heavily on hyperbolism, exaggerating everyday situations for comedic effect.  这位喜剧演员的表演很大程度上依赖于夸张，夸大日常生活中的情境以达到喜剧效果。|近义词：exaggeration, overstatement, magnification |反义词：understatement, litotes
