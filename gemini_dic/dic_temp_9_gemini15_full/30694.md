certificated:/ˈsɜːrtɪfɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 经认证的，合格的 | Having received an official certificate showing that one has met certain standards or qualifications.  | Example: He is a certificated teacher. 他是一位持证教师。  |近义词：qualified, licensed, accredited, authorized | 反义词：unqualified, unlicensed, unauthorized |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school only hires certificated teachers. = 学校只雇佣持证教师。
She is a certificated accountant. = 她是一位持证会计师。


一般过去时:
He was certificated as a pilot last year. = 他去年获得了飞行员执照。
The building was certificated as fire-safe. = 这座建筑物被认证为防火安全。


现在完成时:
They have been certificated to operate heavy machinery. = 他们已经获得操作重型机械的资格认证。
She has certificated as a yoga instructor. = 她已经获得了瑜伽教练资格证书。


过去完成时:
He had already been certificated before he applied for the job. = 他申请这份工作之前就已经获得了相关证书。
The product had been certificated by the relevant authority. = 该产品已获得相关机构的认证。


将来时:
The company will be certificated by next month. = 公司将在下个月获得认证。
He will be certificated as a doctor soon. = 他很快就会成为一名合格的医生。


虚拟语气:
If he were certificated, he could get the job. = 如果他有证书，他就能得到这份工作。
I wish I were certificated to teach English. = 我希望我有资格教英语。


被动语态:
The documents were certificated by the notary public. = 文件由公证人进行了认证。
The software is being certificated for compatibility. = 该软件正在进行兼容性认证。


疑问句:
Is he certificated to drive a truck? = 他有驾驶卡车的资格吗？
Are all the products certificated? = 所有产品都经过认证了吗？


否定句:
He is not certificated to practice medicine. = 他没有行医资格。
The building is not yet certificated for occupancy. = 这座建筑物尚未获得入住许可。
