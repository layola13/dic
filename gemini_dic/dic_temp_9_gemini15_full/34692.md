clerkship:/ˈklɜːrkʃɪp/ | n. |实习期；书记员职位；职员身份 | A period of time spent working as a clerk, often as part of professional training.  It can refer to a period of practical experience in a professional field, such as law or medicine, undertaken by students or graduates to gain practical skills and experience. | Example:  He completed a clerkship at a prestigious law firm. 他在一个著名的律师事务所完成了实习。  She is currently undertaking a surgical clerkship. 她目前正在进行外科实习。 |近义词:internship, residency, apprenticeship |反义词:employment(in the sense of permanent job)
