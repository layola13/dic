reimbursing: /ˌriːɪmˈbɜːrsɪŋ/ | v. | 报销，偿还 | Paying back money that has been spent.  | The company will reimburse you for travel expenses. 公司将报销您的差旅费。 | refund, repay, compensate |  deduct, charge


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reimburses employees for business travel. = 公司报销员工的商务旅行费用。
She reimburses her assistant for parking fees. = 她报销她助理的停车费。


一般过去时:
They reimbursed me for the damaged goods. = 他们赔偿了我损坏的商品。
The insurance company reimbursed him for the stolen jewelry. = 保险公司赔偿了他被盗的珠宝。


现在完成时:
I have been reimbursed for the conference expenses. = 我的会议费用已经报销了。
She has not yet been reimbursed for her medical bills. = 她的医疗费用尚未报销。


过去完成时:
He had already been reimbursed before he submitted the claim. = 他在提交索赔之前就已经报销了。
They had not been reimbursed for the damages until after a long wait. = 他们在漫长的等待之后才得到损坏赔偿。


将来时:
They will reimburse you as soon as the paperwork is processed. = 文件处理完毕后，他们将向您报销。
The government will reimburse farmers for crop losses. = 政府将向农民赔偿农作物损失。


虚拟语气:
If they reimbursed me promptly, I would be much happier. = 如果他们及时向我报销，我会高兴得多。
I wish they would reimburse me for the extra expenses incurred. = 我希望他们能报销我额外发生的费用。


被动语态:
Expenses will be reimbursed at the end of the month. = 费用将在月末报销。
The claim was reimbursed within two weeks. = 索赔在两周内得到报销。


疑问句:
Will the company reimburse me for this expense? = 公司会报销这笔费用吗？
Are you reimbursed for mileage? = 你报销里程费吗？


否定句:

The company will not reimburse expenses without proper documentation. = 没有适当的文件，公司将不会报销费用。
