metamorphic:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfɪk/| adj.|变质的；蜕变的|Of or relating to metamorphism; changed in form or nature.  The term describes rocks that have been transformed by heat, pressure, or chemical reactions.|例句：The metamorphic rocks showed evidence of intense heat and pressure.  变质岩显示出剧烈高温高压的证据。|近义词：transformed, altered, changed|反义词：unchanged, original, pristine
