nonrefundability: /ˌnɒnˌriːfʌndəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可退款性，不予退款 | The inability to receive a refund.  This refers to a situation where a payment, typically for a good or service, cannot be returned.  This characteristic is often associated with non-refundable tickets or deposits. | Example: The nonrefundability of the airline ticket meant I lost my money when the flight was canceled.  = 由于机票不可退款，航班取消时我损失了我的钱。 The hotel's policy of nonrefundability discourages last-minute cancellations. = 酒店不予退款的政策劝退了最后一刻的取消。| 近义词：irreversibility, irretrievability | 反义词：refundability
