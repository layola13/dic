outmerchant:/ˌaʊtˈmɜːrtʃənt/ | n. | 外商，境外商人 | A person or company that conducts business outside their home country.  An outmerchant might be involved in importing goods, exporting goods, or establishing foreign subsidiaries. | Example: The outmerchant successfully negotiated a deal to import rare spices.  这位外商成功地谈妥了一笔进口稀有香料的交易。|近义词：foreign trader, international businessman, exporter, importer | 反义词：domestic merchant, local trader
