lawyers:/ˈlɔɪərz/ | n. | 律师们 | Lawyers are trained legal professionals who advise clients on legal matters and represent them in court.  律师是受过专业法律训练的专业人士，他们就法律事务为客户提供咨询并在法庭上代表他们。|  The lawyers presented compelling evidence in court. 律师们在法庭上提供了令人信服的证据。  Many lawyers specialize in a particular area of law. 许多律师专门从事某个特定法律领域。|  attorneys, barristers, solicitors |  (无明确反义词，可考虑：layperson, non-lawyer) | 名词
