marauder: /məˈrɔːdər/ | n. | 掠夺者；抢劫者 | A marauder is someone who roams around in search of things to steal or people to attack, often violently. They are typically involved in pillaging and raiding.  | Example: The marauders raided the village, stealing food and livestock.  掠夺者袭击了村庄，偷走了食物和牲畜。| 近义词: raider, pillager, looter, plunderer | 反义词: protector, defender, benefactor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The marauder steals food from the villagers. = 掠夺者从村民那里偷食物。
Marauders often attack undefended settlements. = 掠夺者经常袭击没有防御的定居点。


一般过去时:
The marauders attacked the city last night. = 掠夺者昨晚袭击了这座城市。
A band of marauders plundered the caravan. = 一伙掠夺者洗劫了商队。


现在完成时:
The marauders have destroyed many villages. = 掠夺者已经摧毁了许多村庄。
They have stolen everything of value. = 他们已经偷走了所有有价值的东西。


过去完成时:
The villagers had already fled before the marauders arrived. = 在掠夺者到达之前，村民们已经逃跑了。
The army had anticipated the marauder's attack. = 军队已经预料到了掠夺者的袭击。


将来时:
The marauders will attack again soon. = 掠夺者很快会再次袭击。
We will defend our village against the marauders. = 我们将保卫我们的村庄免受掠夺者的袭击。


虚拟语气:
If the marauders were stopped, many lives would be saved. = 如果阻止了掠夺者，许多生命将会得救。
I wish the marauders would leave us alone. = 我希望掠夺者能放过我们。


被动语态:

疑问句:
