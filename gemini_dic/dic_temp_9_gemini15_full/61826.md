empiricisms:/ɪmˈpɪrɪsɪzəm/| n. |经验主义；经验论|The philosophical position that emphasizes experience and observation as the primary sources of knowledge.  Empiricism contrasts with rationalism, which prioritizes reason and deduction.  It's a belief that knowledge comes primarily from sensory experience. |Example:  His research is based on empiricism, relying heavily on observational data. 他的研究基于经验主义，严重依赖观察数据。

Example: The success of the treatment demonstrates the empiricism behind its development.  治疗的成功证明了其发展背后的经验主义。|近义词：experientialism, positivism|反义词：rationalism, idealism|名词
