prequotes:/ˌpriːˈkwəʊts/| n.|预先报价，预先估价|A prequote is a preliminary estimate of the cost of goods or services before a formal quote is issued.|例句：We received a prequote for the project, which helped us budget accordingly. 我们收到了项目的预先报价，这帮助我们相应地编制预算。|近义词：estimate, preliminary quote, price estimate|反义词：final quote, invoice
