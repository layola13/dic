shut-eye:/ʃʌtˈaɪ/| n. |睡眠，打盹儿|A period of sleep.  It is informal. |例句：I need to get some shut-eye before the long drive. 我需要在长途驾驶前睡一会儿。

例句：After a long day of work, all I wanted was some shut-eye.  劳累了一天后，我唯一想做的就是睡一会儿。


近义词：sleep, nap, rest |反义词：wakefulness, insomnia

词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I need some shut-eye every night. = 我每晚都需要睡一会儿。
He gets enough shut-eye. = 他睡眠充足。


一般过去时:
I got eight hours of shut-eye last night. = 我昨晚睡了八个小时。
She didn't get much shut-eye because of the noise. = 因为噪音，她没怎么睡好。


现在完成时:
I haven't had enough shut-eye this week. = 这周我睡眠不足。
She has finally gotten some shut-eye after days of sleepless nights. = 经过几天的不眠之夜后，她终于睡着了。


过去完成时:
I had gotten a good night's shut-eye before the exam, so I felt refreshed. = 考试前我睡了个好觉，所以感觉很精神。
They had hardly had any shut-eye for days before the project deadline. = 在项目截止日期前的几天里，他们几乎没有睡过觉。


将来时:
I will need a lot of shut-eye after the marathon. = 马拉松之后我需要好好睡一觉。
We will get plenty of shut-eye on our vacation. = 我们会在假期好好休息。


虚拟语气:
If I had gotten more shut-eye, I wouldn't be so tired. = 如果我睡得更多，我就不会这么累了。
I wish I could get some shut-eye right now. = 我真希望我现在能睡一会儿。


被动语态:
(This term is rarely used in passive voice.)


疑问句:
Did you get enough shut-eye last night? = 你昨晚睡够了吗？
How much shut-eye did he get? = 他睡了多久？


否定句:
I didn't get any shut-eye last night. = 我昨晚没睡着。
He couldn't get any shut-eye because of the barking dog. = 因为狗叫，他睡不着觉。


PHRASE->
catch some shut-eye = 稍微睡一会儿

plenty of shut-eye = 充足的睡眠
