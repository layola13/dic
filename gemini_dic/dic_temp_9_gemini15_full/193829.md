self-destructs: /ˌsɛlf dəˈstrʌkts/ | v. | 自毁，自我毁灭 |  To destroy oneself or itself automatically.  This usually refers to a mechanism or process built into something that causes it to destroy itself under certain conditions, such as a timer or a trigger event.  |

例句：

The missile self-destructs after ten seconds if it doesn't find its target. =  如果导弹在十秒钟内找不到目标，它就会自毁。

The faulty software self-destructed, wiping all the data. =  有故障的软件自行销毁，删除了所有数据。


近义词：annihilate, obliterate, destroy, perish


反义词：survive, endure, persist


词性：动词
