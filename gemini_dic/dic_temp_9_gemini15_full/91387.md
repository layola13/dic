hydrotherapists:/ˌhaɪdroʊˈθɛrəpɪsts/| n. | 水疗治疗师 | A hydrotherapist is a healthcare professional who uses water for therapeutic purposes. They may work in hospitals, rehabilitation centers, or private practices.  They develop and implement treatment plans using various aquatic techniques to improve patients' physical function, reduce pain, and enhance overall well-being. | Example: The hydrotherapist recommended a series of underwater exercises to strengthen my leg muscles.  水疗治疗师建议我进行一系列水下运动来增强腿部肌肉。  |近义词: aquatic therapist, water therapist | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, the opposite would be a therapist using non-aquatic methods)|  名词
