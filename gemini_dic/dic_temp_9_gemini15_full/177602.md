rebuffed:/rɪˈbʌft/| adj. |被拒绝的，遭到回绝的 |Rejected; refused.  The proposal was rebuffed by the committee.  |近义词：rejected, refused, spurned |反义词：accepted, approved


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often rebuffed her advances. = 他经常回绝她的示好。
They routinely rebuff suggestions from junior staff. = 他们通常会回绝来自初级员工的建议。


一般过去时:
My request for a raise was rebuffed by my boss. = 我的加薪请求被老板拒绝了。
She was rebuffed by the man she had a crush on. = 她被她暗恋的男人拒绝了。


现在完成时:
He has been rebuffed repeatedly in his attempts to secure funding. = 他在寻求资金的尝试中屡屡受挫。
She has rebuffed all attempts to contact her. = 她回绝了所有试图联系她的尝试。


过去完成时:
He had already been rebuffed several times before he finally gave up. = 在他最终放弃之前，他已经多次被拒绝了。
They had been rebuffed by the bank, so they had to seek alternative financing. = 他们已经被银行拒绝了，所以他们不得不寻求替代融资。


将来时:
I expect my proposal will be rebuffed. = 我预计我的提案会被拒绝。
They fear that their application will be rebuffed. = 他们担心他们的申请会被拒绝。


虚拟语气:
If I had known my proposal would be rebuffed, I wouldn't have submitted it. = 如果我知道我的提案会被拒绝，我就不会提交了。
I wish my attempts to reach him hadn't been rebuffed. = 我希望我试图联系他没有被拒绝。


被动语态:
His advances were rebuffed. = 他的示好被拒绝了。
The plan was rebuffed by the board of directors. = 该计划被董事会否决了。


疑问句:
Was your application rebuffed? = 你的申请被拒绝了吗？
Were their efforts to secure funding rebuffed? = 他们争取资金的努力被拒绝了吗？


否定句:
My request was not rebuffed; it was accepted. = 我的请求没有被拒绝；它被接受了。
The proposal wasn't rebuffed immediately; it was reviewed first. = 该提案没有立即被拒绝；它首先被审查了。


PHRASE->
a rebuffed lover = 被拒绝的情人
a rebuffed proposal = 被拒绝的提案
a rebuffed attempt = 被挫败的尝试
to rebuff someone's advances = 回绝某人的示好
to be rebuffed sharply = 被严厉地拒绝


词源:
From Old French *rebufler, from re- + bufler "to strike, butt," from Vulgar Latin *buffulare, of uncertain origin.  The sense development is from "to strike back" to "to repel" to "to refuse."
