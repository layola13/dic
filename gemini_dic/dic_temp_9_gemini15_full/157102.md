pinata:/pɪˈnjɑːtə/| n. |彩罐，皮纳塔（装满糖果、玩具等，悬挂起来，用棍子打碎的容器）|A piñata is a container, typically made of papier-mâché, filled with candies, toys, and other treats.  It is hung up and broken open by blindfolded participants using a stick.  This is a popular tradition in many Latin American countries and is often used at parties and celebrations.  |The children took turns hitting the piñata until it broke open, showering everyone with candy.|近义词：party favor, game|反义词：无
