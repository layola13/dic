mutational:/mjuːˈteɪʃənəl/| adj.| 突变的，变异的 | Relating to or caused by mutation.  The word describes something that is related to or a result of a mutation, a change in the DNA sequence of an organism.  | 例句：The study investigated the mutational effects of UV radiation on skin cells. 这项研究调查了紫外线辐射对皮肤细胞的突变效应。

The mutational landscape of the cancer revealed several key driver genes.  癌症的突变图谱揭示了几个关键驱动基因。|近义词：mutative, variant |反义词：unchanged, stable
