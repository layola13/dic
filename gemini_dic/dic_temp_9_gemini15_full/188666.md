rumourer: /ˈruːmərər/ | n. | 传谣者，造谣者 | A person who spreads rumors.  | The rumourer was quickly identified and discredited.  传谣者很快就被认出并被揭穿了。 | gossiper, whisperer, talebearer | truth-teller, fact-checker | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a rumourer. = 他是一个传谣者。
She rumours things constantly. = 她不断地散播谣言。


一般过去时:
He was a rumourer in his youth. = 他年轻时是个传谣者。
She rumoured that he was fired. = 她谣传他被解雇了。


现在完成时:
He has been a rumourer for years. = 他多年来一直是个传谣者。
She has rumoured many false stories. = 她散布过许多虚假的故事。


过去完成时:

She had rumoured the news before it was officially announced. = 在新闻正式宣布之前，她就已散布了这个消息。
