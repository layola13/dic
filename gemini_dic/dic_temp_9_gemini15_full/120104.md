mimetic:/mɪˈmɛtɪk/| adj.| 模仿的；模拟的 | Relating to or characterized by mimicry.  It often refers to the imitation of the form, appearance, or behavior of one organism by another. | Example: The mimetic coloration of the insect helped it blend in with its surroundings.  这种昆虫的拟态颜色帮助它融入周围环境。|近义词： imitative, simulative, copycat | 反义词：original, innovative

词源:
From Greek *mimos* ("mime," "imitator") + *-ētikos* ("-ic").  The word ultimately traces back to the ancient Greek concept of mimesis, which referred to the imitation of nature or reality in art and literature.
