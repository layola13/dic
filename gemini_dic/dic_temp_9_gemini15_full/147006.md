overlander:/ˈoʊvərˌlændər/ | n. | 越野旅行者；长途旅行者 | A person who travels overland, especially by car or motorcycle, often for extended periods and to remote locations.  They often engage in self-sufficient travel, relying on their own resources and preparation. | Example:  He's an experienced overlander, having traversed the Sahara Desert multiple times. 他是一位经验丰富的越野旅行者，曾多次穿越撒哈拉沙漠。  She documented her overlanding journey across South America in a blog. 她在一个博客上记录了她横跨南美洲的越野旅行。 | 近义词：explorer, adventurer, traveler | 反义词：homebody, stay-at-home
