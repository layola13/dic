monopetalous:/ˌmɒnəˈpɛtələs/|/ˌmɑːnəˈpɛtəlʌs/| adj. |单瓣的；单花瓣的 |Having a single petal or having petals united to form a tube.  A monopetalous flower has its petals fused together to form a single unit, as opposed to a polypetalous flower where the petals are separate. |Example: The corolla of the flower is monopetalous, forming a long tube. 这朵花的花冠是单瓣的，形成一个长管。|近义词: gamopetalous |反义词: polypetalous
