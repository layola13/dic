indigents:/ˈɪndɪdʒənts/ | n. |贫困者，穷人 | People who are poor and needy.  The indigent population often lacks access to basic necessities.  |例句：Many indigent families rely on charitable organizations for food and shelter. 许多贫困家庭依靠慈善组织提供食物和住所。

例句：The city provides various services to its indigent residents. 这个城市为其贫困居民提供各种服务。


近义词：poor, needy, destitute, impoverished | 反义词：wealthy, affluent, rich


词源:  From Latin *indigere "to lack, need," from in- "not" + digeri "to distribute, arrange" (akin to Latin digitus "finger").  The sense development is from "lacking" to "needy."
