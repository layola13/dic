paralipsis:/ˌpærəˈlɪpsɪs/ | n. | 省略，含蓄提及 |  The rhetorical device of mentioning something by saying you're not going to mention it.  It's a way of subtly emphasizing a point by pretending to pass over it.  |  I won't mention his numerous infidelities, or his questionable business dealings, or his general lack of morality.  |  omission, suppression,  passing over |  emphasis, direct mention | noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I don't mention his bad habits. = 我不提他的坏习惯。
We omit details for brevity. = 为了简洁起见，我们省略了一些细节。


一般过去时:
She avoided mentioning the sensitive topic. = 她避免提及那个敏感的话题。
They passed over the awkward moment in silence. = 他们默默地忽略了那个尴尬的时刻。


现在完成时:
I have avoided mentioning his name all this time. = 我一直避免提及他的名字。
They have omitted many important facts from their report. = 他们在报告中省略了许多重要的细节。


过去完成时:
He had already glossed over the mistake before I noticed it. = 我注意到之前，他已经轻描淡写地带过了那个错误。
They had suppressed evidence crucial to the case. = 他们已经隐瞒了对案件至关重要的证据。


将来时:
I will not dwell on the negative aspects. = 我不会详述消极方面。
We will avoid mentioning his past failures. = 我们将避免提及他过去的失败。


虚拟语气:
If I were to mention his flaws, I would be deemed impolite. = 如果我提及他的缺点，我会被认为是不礼貌的。
I wish I could simply ignore the issue, but I can't. = 我真希望我可以简单地忽略这个问题，但我不能。


被动语态:
His misdeeds were conveniently overlooked. = 他的恶行被方便地忽略了。
The uncomfortable truth was subtly passed over. = 那令人不安的真相被巧妙地略过了。


疑问句:
Should we omit this detail? = 我们应该省略这个细节吗？
Did they intentionally suppress the information? = 他们是有意隐瞒这些信息吗？


否定句:
I did not mention his poor performance. = 我没有提及他糟糕的表现。
They didn’t address the elephant in the room. = 他们没有处理那个显而易见的问题。


PHRASE->
pass over: 略过
gloss over: 轻描淡写
omit to: 忘记做
suppressed information: 被压制的信息
leave out: 省略
