overlands:/ˈoʊvərˌlændz/| adv.|陆路地；经陆路|Across or through the land; by land.  This adverb describes travel or transportation that occurs on land, as opposed to by sea or air.|例句：They travelled overlands to reach the remote village.他们经陆路到达那个偏远的村庄。|例句：The goods were transported overlands instead of by ship.货物是经陆路运输而不是海运。|近义词：by land, overland, terrestrially|反义词：by sea, by air, by water
