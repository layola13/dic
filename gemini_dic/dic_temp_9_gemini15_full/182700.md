repacked:/ˌriːˈpækt/| v. |重新包装，重新打包|To pack something again, often after it has been unpacked or after its original packaging has been damaged.  It implies a re-arrangement or a change to the original packaging.|例句：I had to repack the suitcase after discovering I'd overpacked. 我不得不重新打包行李箱，因为我发现装得太满了。

例句：The damaged goods were repackaged for shipment.  损坏的货物重新包装后才发货。


近义词：repackage, repackage, re-wrap|反义词：unpack, unwrap|词性：verb
