rescreen:/ˌriːˈskriːn/ | vt. | 重新放映；再次审查 | To show (a film, etc.) again; to review or examine again. | 例句：The film was so popular they decided to rescreen it.  这部电影很受欢迎，他们决定重新上映。
The committee will rescreen all applications. 委员会将再次审查所有申请。 | 近义词：re-show, review, re-examine | 反义词：cancel, delete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rescreen the classic films every year. = 他们每年都重映经典电影。
The company rescreens all job applicants. = 公司会重新审查所有求职者。


一般过去时:
The cinema rescreened the award-winning movie last week. = 上周电影院重映了那部获奖电影。
The judge rescreened the evidence before making a decision. = 法官在做出决定之前重新审查了证据。
