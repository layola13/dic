hemispheres:/ˈhɛmɪsfɪrz/ | n. | 半球；半脑 |  Two halves of a sphere, especially of the earth or brain.  | Example: The Earth is divided into two hemispheres: the Northern and Southern Hemispheres. 地球分为两个半球：北半球和南半球。


Example: The left hemisphere of the brain controls the right side of the body. 大脑左半球控制身体右侧。|近义词：halves |反义词：whole sphere
