dilater:/daɪˈleɪtər/ | n. |扩张器，扩张剂 | An instrument or substance that dilates or expands an opening or passage, such as a blood vessel or pupil.  | Example: The doctor used a dilator to widen the patient's cervix during the examination. 医生在检查期间用扩张器扩张病人的子宫颈。 |近义词：expander, enlarger |反义词：constrictor
