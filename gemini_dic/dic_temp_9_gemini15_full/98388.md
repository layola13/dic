insolent: /ˈɪnsələnt/ | adj. | 粗鲁的；无礼的；傲慢的 | Showing a rude and arrogant lack of respect.  |  例句：His insolent remarks angered the teacher.  他的无礼言辞激怒了老师。

例句：The child's insolent behavior was unacceptable.  孩子的无礼行为是不可接受的。


近义词：disrespectful, rude, impertinent, cheeky, impudent | 反义词：respectful, polite, courteous


词源:  From Old French *insolent, from Latin insolēns, present participle of insolēre "to be unusual, unaccustomed," from in- "not" + solēre "to be accustomed."  The original sense was "unusual," but it evolved to mean "unusually bold" and then "rude."
