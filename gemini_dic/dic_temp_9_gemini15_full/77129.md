gasbag:/ˈɡæsˌbæɡ/| n. |夸夸其谈的人；吹牛大王|A gasbag is a person who talks a lot, especially about unimportant things, in a boastful or inflated way. They tend to be long-winded and self-important.|He's such a gasbag; he never stops talking about himself.|近义词：windbag, blowhard, boaster|反义词：quiet person, listener
