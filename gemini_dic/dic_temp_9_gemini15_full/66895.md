expedite:/ˈɛkspədaɪt/| vt.| 加速；促进；迅速办理 | To make (a process or action) happen sooner or more quickly. | 例句：The company expedited the shipping process to meet the customer's deadline. 公司加快了运输流程以满足客户的截止日期。 | 例句： We need to expedite the approval process for this project. 我们需要加快这个项目的审批流程。| 近义词：accelerate, hasten, facilitate | 反义词：delay, hinder, obstruct | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They expedite the delivery of goods. = 他们加快货物交付速度。
The government expedites the visa application process. = 政府加快签证申请流程。

一般过去时:
The manager expedited the report's completion. = 经理加快了报告的完成速度。
They expedited the shipment to ensure timely arrival. = 他们加快了货运速度以确保及时到达。

现在完成时:
The company has expedited the development of the new product. = 公司已经加快了新产品的开发速度。
We have expedited the repair process to minimize downtime. = 我们已经加快了维修流程以最大限度地减少停机时间。

过去完成时:
They had expedited the order before the customer's urgent request. = 在客户紧急请求之前，他们已经加快了订单处理速度。
The team had expedited the project, finishing it ahead of schedule. = 团队提前完成了项目，他们加快了项目进度。

将来时:
The company will expedite the delivery of the package. = 公司将加快包裹的投递速度。
We will expedite the process to meet the deadline. = 我们将加快流程以满足截止日期。

虚拟语气:
If we expedited the process, we would finish earlier. = 如果我们加快流程，我们会更早完成。
I wish they had expedited the response to my inquiry. = 我希望他们能加快对我的询问的回复速度。

被动语态:
The shipment was expedited to reach the customer quickly. = 货物被加快运输以快速到达客户手中。
The application was expedited due to its urgency. = 由于申请的紧急性，它被加快处理。

疑问句:
Can we expedite this process? = 我们能加快这个流程吗？
Will they expedite the repair? = 他们会加快维修速度吗？

否定句:
They did not expedite the process. = 他们没有加快流程。
The company did not expedite the delivery. = 公司没有加快递送速度。


PHRASE->
expedite the process = 加速流程

词源:
From Middle French *expédier, from Latin expedire "to disentangle, extricate, set free," from ex- "out" + ped-, stem of pes "foot."  The sense evolution is from "untying" to "freeing up" to "making easier and faster."
