paragraph:/ˈpærəɡræf/ | n. |段落，节 | A paragraph is a self-contained unit of a discourse, usually consisting of several sentences that develop a single topic or idea.  | Example: The first paragraph of the essay introduced the main argument.  第一段落介绍了论文的主要论点。|近义词：section, passage |反义词：无


USAGE->

The paragraph describes a beautiful sunset. = 这段文字描述了一个美丽的日落。
