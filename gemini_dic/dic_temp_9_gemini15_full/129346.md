noisomenesses:/ˈnɔɪsəmnəsɪz/| n.|令人作呕的事物；令人厌恶的情况|The plural form of noisome, referring to multiple instances of something offensive or disgusting to the senses, especially smell or appearance.  It suggests a collection or accumulation of unpleasant things.|例句：The noisomenesses of the overflowing landfill were unbearable.  垃圾填埋场溢出的令人作呕的情况令人难以忍受。 The noisomenesses emanating from the poorly maintained factory were a constant source of complaint.  维护不良的工厂散发出的令人厌恶的情况一直是投诉的来源。|近义词：disgusts, offensivenesses, abominations|反义词：pleasures, delights, fragrances
