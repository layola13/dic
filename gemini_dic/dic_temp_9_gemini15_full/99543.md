intergraft:/ˈɪntəɡræft/| n. |移植体；嵌合体|A graft of tissue or organ from one part of the body to another, or from one individual to another.  It refers to the transplanted material itself. |Example: The surgeon carefully placed the intergraft into the recipient site.|近义词：graft, implant|反义词：removal, excision|名词
