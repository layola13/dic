invalidated: /ɪnˈvælɪdeɪtɪd/ | v. | 使无效；使作废；推翻 | To make something invalid or null; to prove something wrong or untrue. |  The new evidence invalidated the original theory. 新证据使原来的理论无效。  The court invalidated the contract. 法院宣布合同无效。 |  nullify, invalidate, annul, quash | validate, confirm, substantiate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new law invalidates many previous agreements. = 新法律使许多以前的协议无效。
This evidence invalidates his claim. = 这证据使他的说法无效。


一般过去时:
The judge invalidated the will. = 法官宣布遗嘱无效。
The higher court invalidated the lower court's decision. = 上级法院推翻了下级法院的判决。


现在完成时:
Recent studies have invalidated long-held beliefs about the origins of the universe. = 最近的研究推翻了长期以来关于宇宙起源的信念。
The discovery has invalidated previous theories. = 这一发现使以前的理论无效。


过去完成时:
The new evidence had invalidated their previous conclusions. = 新证据已经推翻了他们之前的结论。
They realized that their assumptions had invalidated their calculations. = 他们意识到他们的假设使他们的计算无效。


将来时:
The upcoming legislation will invalidate many existing regulations. = 即将颁布的立法将使许多现行法规失效。
This change in policy will invalidate our current permits. = 这一政策变化将使我们现有的许可证失效。


虚拟语气:
If the results were different, it would invalidate our hypothesis. = 如果结果不同，它将使我们的假设无效。
If the evidence had been stronger, it would have invalidated the defense's arguments. = 如果证据更充分，它就会使辩方的论点无效。


被动语态:
His argument was invalidated by new evidence. = 他的论点被新的证据推翻了。
The contract was invalidated due to a technicality. = 合同因技术原因而被宣布无效。


疑问句:
Does this new information invalidate our findings? = 这些新信息是否使我们的发现无效？
Will this change in policy invalidate our existing contracts? = 这一政策变化会使我们现有的合同无效吗？


否定句:
The court did not invalidate the contract. = 法院没有宣布合同无效。
Nothing could invalidate the truth of his statement. = 没有任何东西能够推翻他陈述的真实性。
