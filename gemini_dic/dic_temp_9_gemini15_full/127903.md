neolithic:/ˌniːəˈlɪθɪk/| adj.|新石器时代的|of or relating to the Neolithic Period.  The Neolithic Period is characterized by the use of polished stone tools, the development of agriculture, and the establishment of settled communities.|例句：Neolithic tools were found at the dig site.  考古发掘现场发现了新石器时代的工具。The transition from the Paleolithic to the Neolithic age was a major turning point in human history. 从旧石器时代向新石器时代的过渡是人类历史上的一个重要转折点。|近义词：New Stone Age|反义词：Paleolithic
