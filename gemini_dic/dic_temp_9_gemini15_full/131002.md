nonattender:/ˌnɒnəˈtɛndər/ | n. |未出席者，缺席者 | A person who does not attend.  Someone who fails to show up for an event, meeting, or class. | The professor noted a high percentage of nonattenders in his morning lecture.  教授注意到他的上午讲座缺席率很高。| absentee, nonparticipant | attender, participant | 名词
