reget:/rɪˈɡrɛt/| v.| 后悔，惋惜 | To feel sorry about something you have done or something that has happened.  |例句：I regret telling him the truth. 我后悔告诉他真相了。 |例句：She regrets missing the concert. 她后悔错过了这场音乐会。|近义词：rue, repent, be sorry for |反义词：rejoice, celebrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I regret my decision. = 我后悔我的决定。
We regret to inform you that... = 我们很遗憾地通知您……

一般过去时:
He regretted his actions. = 他后悔他的行为。
They regretted not going to the party. = 他们后悔没有去参加聚会。

现在完成时:
I have regretted this ever since. = 从那以后我就一直后悔这件事。
She has regretted her hasty words. = 她一直后悔自己说过的话。

过去完成时:
I had regretted my impulsive choice before it was too late. = 在为时已晚之前我就后悔了我的冲动选择。
They had regretted their decision long before the consequences became apparent. = 早在后果显现之前，他们就后悔他们的决定了。

将来时:
I will regret this someday. = 我总有一天会后悔这件事的。
You will regret not taking this opportunity. = 你会后悔没有抓住这个机会的。

虚拟语气:
I would regret it if I didn't try. = 如果我不尝试，我会后悔的。
I wish I hadn't done it; I regret it deeply. = 我真希望我没有那样做；我非常后悔。

被动语态:
His absence was deeply regretted by all. = 大家都很遗憾他的缺席。
The decision was later regretted by the company. = 公司后来后悔了这个决定。

疑问句:
Do you regret anything you've done? = 你后悔做过任何事吗？
Will you regret this in the future? = 你将来会后悔吗？

否定句:
I don't regret a single thing. = 我一点也不后悔。
He doesn't regret his decision. = 他不后悔他的决定。


PHRASE->
regret to say = 遗憾地说

deeply regret = 深深后悔
have no regrets = 毫无遗憾
express one's regret = 表达遗憾
