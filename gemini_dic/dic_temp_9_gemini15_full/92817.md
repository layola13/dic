iconographer:/ˌaɪkənˈɒɡrəfər/| n. |圣像画家；图标学家|A person who creates or studies icons, especially religious icons.  An iconographer is skilled in the art of creating religious images, often following traditional techniques and styles.  They may also study the history, symbolism, and meaning behind different icons.|The iconographer meticulously painted the Madonna and Child, following centuries-old techniques.|近义词：icon painter, image maker |反义词：N/A
