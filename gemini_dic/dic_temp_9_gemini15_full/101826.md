irritators:/ˈɪrɪteɪtərz/| n. |令人恼怒的事物；刺激物|Things or people that annoy or irritate.|The constant noise from the construction site was a major irritant.  持续不断的建筑工地噪音是一个主要的令人恼火的事物。|The itchy rash was caused by an irritant.  这种痒痒的皮疹是由刺激物引起的。|annoyances, provocations, nuisances|soothing agents, calming influences|名词
