photo-recce:/ˈfoʊtoʊ ˈrɛsi/| n.|照片侦察|Photo reconnaissance, a shortened form of "photographic reconnaissance," refers to the process of taking photographs of an area or object from the air, typically for military or intelligence purposes. The photographs are then analyzed to gather information about the target.  |例句：The pilot conducted a photo-recce mission over the enemy territory.  飞行员在敌方领土上执行了照片侦察任务。

The photo-recce revealed the location of the enemy's artillery. 照片侦察揭示了敌军炮兵的位置。|近义词：aerial photography, reconnaissance photography, spy photography|反义词：None directly applicable; the antonym would relate to the *absence* of reconnaissance, such as secrecy or concealment.|名词
