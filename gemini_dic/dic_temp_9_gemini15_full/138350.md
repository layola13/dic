noticed:/ˈnoʊtɪst/ | v. | 注意到，察觉到 | To become aware of something or someone by seeing, hearing, feeling, etc.  |例句：I noticed a mistake in the report. 我注意到报告中有一个错误。  |例句：She noticed that he was upset. 她注意到他很难过。|近义词：observed, perceived, detected |反义词：ignored, overlooked


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I notice the bird singing every morning. = 我每天早上都注意到鸟在唱歌。
He notices every detail. = 他注意到每一个细节。

一般过去时:
I noticed a strange sound last night. = 我昨晚注意到一个奇怪的声音。
They noticed the change in his behavior. = 他们注意到他行为的变化。

现在完成时:
I have noticed a pattern in his actions. = 我已经注意到他行为中的模式。
She has noticed an improvement in her health. = 她已经注意到她的健康状况有所改善。

过去完成时:
I had noticed the problem before he mentioned it. = 在他提到这个问题之前，我已经注意到了。
They had noticed the discrepancy in the accounts. = 他们已经注意到账目上的差异。

将来时:
I will notice any changes immediately. = 我会立即注意到任何变化。
She will notice the difference in quality. = 她会注意到质量上的差异。

虚拟语气:
If I had noticed the error sooner, I would have corrected it. = 如果我早点注意到错误，我就会纠正它。
I wish I had noticed the sign. = 我真希望我注意到了那个标志。

被动语态:
The mistake was noticed by the editor. = 编辑注意到了这个错误。
The change in policy was noticed by many people. = 许多人都注意到了政策的变化。

疑问句:
Did you notice anything unusual? = 你注意到什么异常了吗？
Have you noticed any improvement? = 你注意到任何改进了吗？

否定句:
I didn't notice the time. = 我没有注意到时间。
He didn't notice her sadness. = 他没有注意到她的悲伤。


PHRASE->
notice something amiss = 注意到异样
notice to quit = 驱逐通知
take notice = 注意
noticeable improvement = 明显的改进
at short notice = 仓促之间
