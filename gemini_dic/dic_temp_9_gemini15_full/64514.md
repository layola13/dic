eroteme:/ɪˈroʊtiːm/| n. |疑问句，问题|A question, especially one put formally or officially.  The term is often used in linguistics and rhetoric to refer to a grammatically complete interrogative sentence. |例句：The eroteme "What is your name?" is a simple question requiring a direct answer.  = 疑问句“你叫什么名字？”是一个需要直接回答的简单问题。

The eroteme at the heart of the debate was whether the policy was effective. = 辩论的核心问题是这项政策是否有效。
