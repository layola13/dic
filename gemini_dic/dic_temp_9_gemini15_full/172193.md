purveyor's: /pərˈveɪərz/ | n. | 供货商的；供应者的 |  A purveyor is a person or company that supplies goods or services.  The possessive 's' indicates ownership or association.  For example, "the purveyor's catalog" refers to the catalog belonging to or produced by the purveyor. | The purveyor's selection of cheeses was impressive.  这家供应商的奶酪选择令人印象深刻。  The restaurant is known for its purveyor's commitment to sustainable practices. 这家餐厅以其供应商对可持续实践的承诺而闻名。 | supplier, provider, vendor | consumer, receiver  | 名词所有格
