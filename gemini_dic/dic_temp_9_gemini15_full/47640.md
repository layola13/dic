debited:/ˈdɛbɪtɪd/ | v. | 借记；记入借方；在账户中扣除 | To debit an account means to record a sum of money as owing to that account.  It's the opposite of crediting an account. | Example: My bank account was debited $100 for the purchase. 我的银行账户因购买而被记入了100美元的借方。  The company debited my credit card for the full amount. 公司从我的信用卡中扣除了全款。| 近义词：charge, deduct, withdraw | 反义词：credit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bank debits my account every month. = 银行每月都从我的账户中扣款。
They debit the expense account regularly. = 他们定期记入费用账户的借方。


一般过去时:
They debited my account by mistake. = 他们错误地从我的账户中扣款了。
The accountant debited the wrong account. = 会计记入了错误的账户借方。


现在完成时:
My account has been debited twice this week. = 我的账户本周已被扣款两次了。
The system has automatically debited your card. = 系统已自动从您的卡中扣款。


过去完成时:
The money had already been debited from my account. = 钱已经从我的账户中扣除了。
Before the transaction, the account had never been debited. = 在交易之前，该账户从未被记入借方。


将来时:
Your account will be debited next month. = 下个月将从您的账户中扣款。
The payment will be debited from your account automatically. = 付款将自动从您的账户中扣除。


虚拟语气:
If they debited my account again, I would complain. = 如果他们再次从我的账户中扣款，我会投诉。
I wish they hadn't debited my account so much. = 我希望他们不要从我的账户中扣除那么多钱。


被动语态:
My account was debited without my authorization. = 我的账户未经我的授权就被扣款了。
The funds were debited from the wrong account. = 资金从错误的账户中扣除了。


疑问句:
Has your account been debited yet? = 你的账户已经被扣款了吗？
When was the money debited from your account? = 什么时候从你的账户中扣款的？


否定句:
My account wasn't debited. = 我的账户没有被扣款。
The payment hasn't been debited yet. = 付款还没有被扣除。


PHRASE->
debit card = 借记卡
debit entry = 借方分录
debit balance = 借方余额
debit memo = 借方通知单
debit note = 借方通知单

词源:
The word "debit" comes from the Old French word "debiter," meaning "to owe" or "to debit," which in turn has its roots in the Latin word "debitum," meaning "something owed."  The suffix "-ed" indicates the past tense or past participle.
