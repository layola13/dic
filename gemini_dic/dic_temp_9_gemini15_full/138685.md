nuisancer: /ˈnuːsənsər/ | n. |讨厌鬼，令人讨厌的人或事 | A person or thing that is annoying or causes trouble.  | The noisy neighbor was a real nuisance.  | 近义词：pest, bother, annoyance | 反义词：delight, blessing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a nuisance to everyone in the office. = 他是办公室里每个人的讨厌鬼。
The constant dripping of the tap is a nuisance. = 水龙头不断滴水真让人讨厌。


一般过去时:
The barking dog was a nuisance last night. = 昨晚那条狗的叫声很烦人。
That loud music was a nuisance during my study time. = 我学习的时候那吵闹的音乐真烦人。


现在完成时:
His constant complaining has been a nuisance for years. = 他不断的抱怨多年来一直很烦人。
The traffic has been a nuisance all morning. = 早上交通一直很堵。


过去完成时:
The broken elevator had been a nuisance for weeks before they fixed it. = 电梯坏了几个星期才修好，真是让人讨厌。
The leaky roof had been a nuisance all winter. = 漏雨的屋顶整个冬天都让人很烦。


将来时:
The construction work will be a nuisance for the next few months. = 未来几个月施工会很烦人。
That new factory will likely be a nuisance to the residents nearby. = 那家新工厂可能会给附近的居民带来麻烦。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't such a nuisance, we'd enjoy his company more. = 如果他不那么讨厌，我们会更享受他的陪伴。
I wish that noise wouldn't be such a nuisance. = 我希望那种噪音不会那么烦人。


被动语态:
The constant noise was considered a nuisance by the residents. = 居民认为持续的噪音很烦人。
The overgrown weeds were declared a public nuisance. = 杂草丛生被宣布为公共危害。


疑问句:
Is the dog barking a nuisance to your neighbors? = 狗叫声让你的邻居感到困扰吗？
Is the loud music a nuisance to you? = 吵闹的音乐让你感到困扰吗？


否定句:
The new building isn't a nuisance at all. = 新建筑一点也不烦人。
The quiet library isn't a nuisance to anyone. = 安静的图书馆不会给任何人带来困扰。


PHRASE->
public nuisance: 公害
a real nuisance: 真讨厌
a bit of a nuisance: 有点讨厌
a terrible nuisance: 非常讨厌
what a nuisance!: 真讨厌！
