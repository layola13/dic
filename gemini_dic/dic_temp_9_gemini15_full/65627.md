evangelism:/ɪˈvændʒəlɪzəm/| n. |福音传教；布道；传教活动 |The active propagation of the Christian gospel by preaching, teaching, and personal witness.  It often involves missionary work and attempts to convert people to Christianity. |例句：The church is committed to evangelism both at home and abroad. 这间教堂致力于在国内外进行福音传教。

例句：His evangelism focused on reaching out to the marginalized communities.  他的传教活动侧重于接触边缘群体。


近义词：missionary work, proselytism, preaching, spreading the gospel |反义词：secularism, atheism, agnosticism |词性:名词
