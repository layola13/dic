septemvirate:/ˌsɛptɛmˈvɑɪrət/| n. |七人委员会；七人统治集团 |A septemvirate is a governing body composed of seven people.  It is often used to describe a group of seven individuals holding significant power or authority, particularly in ancient Rome.  |Example: The Roman septemvirate oversaw the administration of justice.  罗马的七人委员会负责司法管理。|近义词：septemviri, council, committee, board |反义词：monarchy (单人统治), autocracy (独裁统治)
