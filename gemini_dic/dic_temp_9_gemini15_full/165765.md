apprenticeship:/əˈprɛntɪʃɪp/ | n. | 学徒期，实习期 | A period of time spent learning a trade or skill from a more experienced person.  It typically involves practical training and on-the-job experience. | He completed a three-year apprenticeship as a carpenter. 他完成了为期三年的木匠学徒期。| internship, traineeship |  mastering, expertise |  名词

She starts her apprenticeship next week. = 她下周开始她的学徒期。
