callings:/ˈkɔːlɪŋz/ | n. | 天赋；使命；职业；召唤 |  A calling is a strong feeling of suitability for a particular career or purpose in life. It often implies a sense of duty or destiny.  |  She felt a strong calling to become a doctor. 她感到强烈的使命感要成为一名医生。  |  vocation, mission, purpose |  indifference, apathy | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
His calling is teaching. = 他的使命是教书。
What are your callings in life? = 你人生的使命是什么？


一般过去时:
Her calling to the ministry was evident from a young age. = 她对教会的使命感从小就显而易见。
Many people felt a strong calling to help during the crisis. = 许多人在危机期间感到强烈的使命感去帮助他人。


现在完成时:
He has answered his calling to serve others. = 他已经回应了他为他人服务的使命。
She has followed her calling to become a writer. = 她追随她成为一名作家的使命。


过去完成时:
Before he retired, he had dedicated his life to his calling as a teacher. = 在他退休之前，他将自己的一生都献给了他作为教师的使命。
They had long since abandoned their original callings. = 他们早就放弃了他们最初的使命。


将来时:
She will pursue her calling as a musician. = 她将追求她作为音乐家的使命。
He believes he will find his true calling one day. = 他相信有一天他会找到他真正的使命。


虚拟语气:
If I had known my true calling earlier, I would have pursued it sooner. = 如果我早知道我的真正使命，我就会早点追求它。
I wish I had recognized my calling years ago. = 我希望几年前我就认识到我的使命。


被动语态:
His calling was ignored for many years. = 他的使命被忽视了很多年。
Their callings were often misunderstood by others. = 他们的使命常常被别人误解。


疑问句:
What is your calling in life? = 你的人生使命是什么？
Do you feel a strong calling to any particular profession? = 你对任何特定职业有强烈的使命感吗？


否定句:
He didn't feel a strong calling to a career in business. = 他对商业生涯没有强烈的使命感。
She denied that she had a spiritual calling. = 她否认她有精神上的使命感。
