orchidology:/ˌɔːrkɪˈdɒlədʒi/| n. |兰科植物学|The scientific study of orchids.  It encompasses their taxonomy, morphology, physiology, ecology, genetics, and conservation.  |例句：Her passion for orchidology led her to explore remote rainforests in search of new species.  她的兰科植物学热情促使她探索偏远的热带雨林以寻找新的物种。|例句：The professor's lecture on orchidology covered the intricate pollination mechanisms of various orchid species. 教授关于兰科植物学的讲座涵盖了各种兰花物种复杂的授粉机制。|近义词：orchid science, orchid botany|反义词：无
