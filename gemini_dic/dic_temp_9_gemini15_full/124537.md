mountainsides:/ˈmaʊntənsaɪdz/ | n. |山坡；山腰 | The sides of mountains; slopes of mountains.  Mountainsides are often covered with trees or other vegetation. | 例句：Wildflowers bloomed profusely on the mountainsides.  山坡上鲜花盛开。

The hikers rested on the sunny mountainsides. 登山者在阳光照射的山坡上休息。|近义词：slopes, hillsides |反义词：mountaintops, summits
