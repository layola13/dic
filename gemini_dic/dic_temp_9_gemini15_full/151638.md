particularising: /pəˈtɪkjʊləraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 特指；详细说明；具体化 |  The act of making something specific or detailed; giving particular attention to individual details or aspects.  It often implies moving from a general statement to a more precise and focused description. |

例句：

The professor particularised the historical context of the novel, clarifying its social and political significance.  教授详细阐述了这部小说的历史背景，阐明了它的社会和政治意义。

He particularised his concerns about the project, listing specific areas needing improvement. 他详细说明了他对该项目的担忧，列出了需要改进的具体领域。


近义词： specifying, detailing, elucidating, explicating, illustrating

反义词： generalising, summarising, abstracting


词性: 动词
