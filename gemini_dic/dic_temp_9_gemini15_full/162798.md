prebudgetary: /ˌpriːˈbʌdʒɪtəri/ | adj. | 预算前的，预算编制前的 | Relating to the period or activities before a budget is prepared or approved.  It refers to the planning, estimation, and preliminary stages involved in creating a budget. | The prebudgetary discussions were intense, as various departments vied for funding. 预算前的讨论非常激烈，各部门都在争夺资金。 |  pre-budget, pre-financial | post-budgetary |
