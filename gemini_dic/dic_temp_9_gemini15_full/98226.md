inseminate:/ˈɪnsɪmɪneɪt/| vt.|授精；使受精；播种|To introduce semen or pollen into (an animal or plant) for the purpose of fertilization.  The process involves introducing male reproductive material into the female reproductive tract to enable fertilization.|例句：The farmer inseminated his cows artificially.  农民对他的奶牛进行了人工授精。

例句：The bee inseminates the flower. 蜜蜂给花授粉。


近义词：fertilize, impregnate|反义词：sterilize
