cityscapes:/ˈsɪtiˌskeɪps/| n. |城市景观；城市风光 |Cityscapes are photographs, paintings, or other visual representations of city scenes.  They often focus on the architecture, streets, and overall atmosphere of a city. |例句：The artist's cityscapes vividly capture the energy and chaos of modern urban life.  这位艺术家的城市景观生动地捕捉了现代都市生活的活力和混乱。

The photographer's stunning cityscapes showcased the beauty of the city at night. 摄影师令人惊叹的城市景观展现了城市夜晚的美丽。


近义词：urban landscapes, city views, urban scenes |反义词：rural landscapes, countryside views
