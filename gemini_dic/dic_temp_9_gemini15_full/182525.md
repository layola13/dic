rente:/ˈrɛnt/| n.|租金，租费|Rent is the money paid for the use of something, especially property.|The rent is due on the first of the month.  租金在每月一号到期。|  My rent is too high.我的房租太高了。|近义词：lease, hire charge|反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rent is high in this city. = 这个城市的房租很高。
They rent a house near the beach. = 他们租了一栋靠近海滩的房子。


一般过去时:
We rented a car for our vacation. = 我们租了一辆车去度假。
She rented a small apartment downtown. = 她租了一间市中心的小公寓。


现在完成时:
They have rented this apartment for five years. = 他们已经租这套公寓五年了。
We have rented several cars this year. = 今年我们已经租了几辆车了。


过去完成时:
They had rented the apartment before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，他们已经租了那套公寓。
We had rented a car, so we didn't need to take a taxi. = 我们已经租了一辆车，所以不需要打车。


将来时:
We will rent a larger house next year. = 我们明年将租一栋更大的房子。
They will rent out their apartment while they are away. = 他们不在的时候会把公寓出租。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would rent a bigger apartment. = 如果我有更多钱，我会租一套更大的公寓。
I wish I could rent a place with a garden. = 我希望我能租到一个带花园的地方。


被动语态:
The apartment was rented quickly. = 这套公寓很快就租出去了。
The car is being rented by a tourist. = 这辆车正在被一个游客租用。


疑问句:
How much is the rent? = 房租多少钱？
Do they rent out rooms? = 他们出租房间吗？


否定句:
We didn't rent a car. = 我们没有租车。
They don't rent out their property. = 他们不出租他们的房产。


PHRASE->
rent out = 出租
