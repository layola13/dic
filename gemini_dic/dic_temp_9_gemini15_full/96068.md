It often refers to behavior or language that is considered inappropriate or offensive in a particular social context. |例句：Her indecorous outburst shocked the other guests at the dinner party.  她的粗鲁言行震惊了晚宴上的其他宾客。


例句：The comedian's indecorous jokes offended many in the audience.  喜剧演员不得体的笑话冒犯了许多观众。
