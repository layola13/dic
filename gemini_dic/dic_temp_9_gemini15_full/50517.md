depriving: /dɪˈpraɪvɪŋ/ | v. |剥夺；使丧失；使缺乏 | To prevent (a person or place) from having or enjoying something.  |例句：The drought is depriving farmers of their livelihood.  干旱正在剥夺农民的生计。

例句：His illness deprived him of the ability to walk.  他的疾病剥夺了他走路的能力。


近义词：depleting, bereaving, divesting | 反义词：providing, supplying, enriching


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are depriving the children of education. = 他们正在剥夺孩子们受教育的权利。
The government is depriving citizens of their basic rights. = 政府正在剥夺公民的基本权利。

一般过去时:
The storm deprived the city of power. = 暴风雨使这座城市断电了。
They deprived him of his inheritance. = 他们剥夺了他的继承权。

现在完成时:
Years of war have deprived the country of its resources. = 多年的战争使这个国家失去了资源。
The company has deprived its employees of their bonuses. = 公司已经剥夺了员工的奖金。

过去完成时:
They had deprived him of his freedom before he could speak. = 在他能说话之前，他们就剥夺了他的自由。
The harsh winter had deprived the animals of food. = 严冬使动物们缺乏食物。

将来时:
The new policy will deprive many people of their jobs. = 新政策将使许多人失业。
This project will deprive the community of green space. = 这个项目将剥夺社区的绿地。

虚拟语气:
If they deprived us of water, we would suffer greatly. = 如果他们切断我们的水源，我们将遭受巨大的痛苦。
I wish they hadn't deprived me of my chance. = 我真希望他们没有剥夺我的机会。

被动语态:
The villagers were deprived of their land. = 村民们被剥夺了土地。
He felt deprived of his rights. = 他感到自己的权利被剥夺了。

疑问句:
Are they depriving the animals of their natural habitat? = 他们是否正在剥夺动物的自然栖息地？
What are they depriving the community of? = 他们正在剥夺社区什么？

否定句:
They did not deprive him of his property. = 他们没有剥夺他的财产。
The government does not intend to deprive citizens of their freedom of speech. = 政府无意剥夺公民的言论自由。

PHRASE->
be deprived of = 被剥夺…
deprive someone of something = 剥夺某人某物
deprivation of rights = 权利的剥夺
severe deprivation = 严重的剥夺
sensory deprivation = 感官剥夺
