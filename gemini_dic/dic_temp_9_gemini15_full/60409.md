eighteens:/ˌeɪˈtiːnz/| n. |十八个；十八岁的人|The word "eighteens" is the plural form of "eighteen," referring to a group of eighteen things or people, or to a period of time associated with being eighteen years old.  It's less common than using "eighteen" with a plural noun. For example, instead of "There are eighteens waiting," you'd say "There are eighteen people waiting." |例句：There were eighteens competing in the race. = 十八个人参加了比赛。  She spent her eighteens traveling the world. = 她把十八岁那几年花在了环游世界上面。|近义词： eighteen (singular), a group of eighteen|反义词：无
