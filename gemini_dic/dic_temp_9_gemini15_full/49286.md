delicacies:/ˈdɛlɪkəsiːz/| n. |美味佳肴，精致食品 |Delicious foods considered special because of their taste or quality.  They are often rare or expensive.|例句：The restaurant is famous for its seafood delicacies. 这家餐厅以其海鲜美味佳肴而闻名。

例句：The chef prepared a selection of exquisite delicacies for the banquet.  厨师为宴会准备了一系列精致的美味佳肴。


近义词：dainties, treats, goodies, rarities


反义词：rubbish, trash, garbage


词性：名词
