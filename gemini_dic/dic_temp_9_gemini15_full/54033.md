discuss:/dɪˈskʌs/| vt.|讨论，议论|To talk about something with someone else, exchanging ideas and opinions.|例句：We need to discuss the project before starting. 我们需要在开始之前讨论一下这个项目。|近义词：talk about, debate, consider|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We discuss this issue every week. = 我们每周都讨论这个问题。
They discuss their plans for the future. = 他们讨论着他们未来的计划。

一般过去时:
We discussed the problem yesterday. = 我们昨天讨论了这个问题。
They discussed the matter at length. = 他们详细地讨论了这件事。

现在完成时:
We have discussed this topic many times. = 我们已经讨论过这个话题很多次了。
They have discussed the proposal thoroughly. = 他们已经彻底讨论了这个提案。

过去完成时:
They had already discussed the contract before the meeting. = 会议之前他们已经讨论过合同了。
We had discussed the budget several times before making a decision. = 我们在做决定之前已经多次讨论过预算。

将来时:
We will discuss the matter further tomorrow. = 我们明天将进一步讨论此事。
They will discuss the new strategy next week. = 他们下周将讨论新的策略。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would discuss the details further. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会更详细地讨论细节。
I wish we could discuss this in person. = 我希望我们能够当面讨论这件事。

被动语态:
The problem was discussed at the meeting. = 会议上讨论了这个问题。
The proposal is being discussed by the committee. = 委员会正在讨论这个提案。

疑问句:
What are we going to discuss today? = 我们今天要讨论什么？
Will we discuss this issue during the meeting? = 我们会在会议上讨论这个问题吗？

否定句:
We didn't discuss the budget at all. = 我们根本没有讨论预算。
They did not discuss the matter with him. = 他们没有和他讨论这件事。


PHRASE->
discuss a topic/issue: 讨论一个话题/问题
discuss the matter further: 进一步讨论此事
discuss in detail: 详细讨论
open to discuss:  愿意讨论
discuss at length:  详细讨论
