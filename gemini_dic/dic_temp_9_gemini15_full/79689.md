glowering:/ˈɡlaʊərɪŋ/| adj.|怒视的；阴沉的；阴郁的|Looking angry and sullen; characterized by anger or sullenness.  It often describes a facial expression or atmosphere.|例句：He gave her a glowering look. 他怒视着/狠狠瞪了她一眼。

例句：The sky was glowering, threatening rain. 天空阴沉沉的，像是要下雨。


近义词：scowling, sullen, angry, menacing, threatening, dark, gloomy

反义词：smiling, cheerful, bright, sunny, pleasant

词性:形容词
