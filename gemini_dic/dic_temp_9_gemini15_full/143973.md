outrageous:/aʊtˈreɪdʒəs/| adj.|令人震惊的；无法容忍的；极端的；过分的|extremely shocking and unacceptable; exceeding all reasonable bounds;  going beyond all reasonable limits.|例句：His behavior was outrageous. 他的行为令人震惊。

例句：The price of that car is outrageous! 那辆车的价格太离谱了！


近义词：shocking, scandalous, appalling, offensive, monstrous, egregious

反义词：acceptable, reasonable, tolerable, moderate, mild

词源:
The word "outrageous" comes from the Old French word "outrage," meaning "violence, injury, wrong."  The prefix "out-" intensifies the meaning, signifying "beyond" or "exceeding."  Therefore, "outrageous" literally means "beyond all reasonable bounds" or "excessive to an unacceptable degree."
