messed:/mɛst/| v. adj.|搞砸了；弄乱了|To make a mess of something; to spoil or ruin something;  to be in a state of disorder or confusion.|例句：I messed up the cake. 我把蛋糕弄砸了。|例句：My room is all messed up. 我的房间乱七八糟的。|近义词：ruined, spoiled, botched, fouled up, screwed up|反义词：fixed, repaired, cleaned, organized|动词，形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He messes up his work often. = 他经常把工作搞砸。
She messes with my things. = 她乱动我的东西。


一般过去时:
I messed up the interview. = 我把面试搞砸了。
He messed around with the settings. = 他乱改设置。
