evincement:/ɪˈvɪnsmənt/| n. | 表明；显示；证据 | The evincement of guilt was clear in his nervous demeanor.  他的紧张举止清楚地表明了内疚。|  近义词：demonstration, manifestation, evidence, proof | 反义词：concealment, suppression, hiding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence evinces his innocence. = 证据表明他是无辜的。
Her behavior evinces a lack of respect. = 她的行为表明缺乏尊重。

一般过去时:
His trembling hands evinced his fear. = 他颤抖的双手表明了他的恐惧。
The documents evinced the company's illegal activities. = 文件显示了该公司的非法活动。

现在完成时:
The results have evinced the effectiveness of the new treatment. = 结果已经证明了新疗法的有效性。
Recent studies have evinced a link between diet and heart disease. = 最近的研究已经表明了饮食与心脏病之间的联系。

过去完成时:
The historical records had evinced a long-standing feud between the two families. = 历史记录表明这两个家族之间长期存在世仇。
Previous experiments had evinced the instability of the compound. = 先前的实验已经证明了该化合物的稳定性差。

将来时:
Further investigation will evince the truth. = 进一步调查将证明真相。
The upcoming trial will evince the defendant's guilt or innocence. = 即将来临的审判将证明被告的罪责或无罪。

虚拟语气:
If the evidence evinced his guilt, he would be convicted. = 如果证据表明他有罪，他就会被定罪。
If the study had evinced different results, we would have changed our approach. = 如果研究显示不同的结果，我们会改变我们的方法。

被动语态:
His guilt was evinced by his confession. = 他的罪行由他的供词所证明。
The company's wrongdoing was evinced by leaked documents. = 该公司的违法行为由泄露的文件所证明。

疑问句:
What does this behavior evince? = 这种行为表明了什么？
Does the data evince any significant trends? = 数据是否表明任何显著的趋势？

否定句:
The evidence did not evince any wrongdoing. = 证据没有表明任何不当行为。
His calm demeanor did not evince any nervousness. = 他平静的态度没有表现出任何紧张。
