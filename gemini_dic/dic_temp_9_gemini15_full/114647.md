malformed:/mælˈfɔːrmd/| adj.|畸形的，格式错误的|Improperly formed or structured; not conforming to the expected or required form.  It often refers to data, code, or physical structures that are not correctly formed.|例句：The XML document was malformed, causing the parser to fail.  XML文档格式错误，导致解析器失败。

The program crashed because of a malformed input file. 程序因格式错误的输入文件而崩溃。


近义词：deformed, misshapen, faulty, flawed, incorrect, irregular

反义词：well-formed, correctly formed, properly structured, regular


词性：形容词
