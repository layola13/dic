forcipate:/ˈfɔːrsɪpeɪt/ | adj. |钳状的；用钳子夹住的 | Having the form or nature of forceps; resembling forceps.  | The forcipate leaves of the plant were easily identified.  植物钳状的叶子很容易辨认。| 近义词：pincer-like, forceps-shaped | 反义词：unpincered


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations in the same way as "to be" or "to go".  Therefore, tense-based conjugations are not applicable.  The word describes a state of being.

词源:
From Latin *forcipat- (stem of forcipatus), past participle of forcipare "to furnish with forceps," from forfex, forfic- "scissors, forceps."  The suffix "-ate" indicates having the characteristics of.
