mynah:/ˈmaɪnɑː/ | n. |八哥鸟|A mynah is a type of bird known for its ability to mimic human speech.  They are typically found in Asia and Australia.  Many species exist, differing in size and plumage.|例句：The mynah bird in the park repeated everything it heard. 公园里的八哥鸟重复着它听到的一切。

The mynah bird sang a surprisingly accurate rendition of a popular song. 那只八哥鸟唱了一首非常准确的流行歌曲。|近义词：starling (in some contexts)|反义词：无|
词性:名词
