monographing:/ˈmɒnəɡrɑːfɪŋ/|/ˈmɑːnəɡræfɪŋ/| v.| 专著写作；撰写专论 | The act of writing a monograph, a scholarly work on a specific subject.  | Example:  He spent years monographing the life and works of the obscure poet. 他花了数年时间撰写这位默默无闻的诗人的生平和作品专著。  |近义词：writing a treatise, composing a scholarly paper |反义词：generalizing, summarizing(in the context of writing)

She monographs on various aspects of 19th-century literature. 她撰写关于19世纪文学各个方面的专著。
