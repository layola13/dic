gelignite:/ˈdʒɛlɪnaɪt/| n. |炸胶，雷管|A type of explosive, a dynamit, consisting of nitroglycerin absorbed in a porous siliceous substance.  It is used in mining and quarrying.|例句：The miners used gelignite to blast through the rock.  矿工们用炸胶炸开岩石。

例句：The demolition crew carefully placed the gelignite charges before detonating them. 拆除队在引爆前小心地放置炸药。
