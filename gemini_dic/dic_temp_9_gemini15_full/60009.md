editorship:/ˈedɪtərʃɪp/| n. |编辑职位；编辑工作；主编身份|The position or function of an editor, especially of a newspaper or magazine.  It involves overseeing the content and style of publications.|例句：She gained valuable experience during her editorship of the student newspaper. 她在她担任学生报纸编辑期间获得了宝贵的经验。|例句：The editorship of this prestigious journal is highly sought after. 这份著名期刊的主编职位非常抢手。|近义词：editorial role, chief editorship |反义词：authorship (in the sense of writing, not editing)
