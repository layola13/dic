deadpanner:/ˈdedpænər/| n. |面无表情的人；一本正经开玩笑的人|A deadpanner is someone who delivers jokes or lines with a completely straight face and unemotional expression, often for comedic effect.  They may appear serious or impassive, even while saying something humorous or absurd. |例句：He's a natural deadpanner; his jokes always land because of his perfectly serious delivery. 他是个天生的面无表情的喜剧演员；他讲的笑话总是很成功，因为他表情非常严肃。

例句：The comedian was a master deadpanner, his monotone voice contrasting sharply with the absurdity of his jokes.  这位喜剧演员是一位面无表情表演的大师，他单调的嗓音与他笑话的荒谬形成鲜明对比。


近义词：stoic, impassive, poker-faced, comedian (if used for comedic effect) |反义词：expressive, animated, demonstrative |词性：名词


词源:  The word "deadpan" itself comes from the combination of "dead" (meaning without expression or feeling) and "pan" (referring to the face).  The suffix "-er" is added to create the noun "deadpanner," indicating a person who exhibits the quality of being deadpan.
