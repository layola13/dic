inobservant: /ˌɪnəbˈzɜːrvənt/ | adj. | 不注意的；视而不见的 | Not attentive or observant; failing to notice things.  | 例句：He was inobservant of the small details. 他对细节不够注意。

He's so inobservant that he didn't even notice the change in her hair. 他太不细心了，甚至都没注意到她头发的变化。


近义词：unobservant, unperceptive, heedless, careless, inattentive | 反义词：observant, attentive, perceptive, mindful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is inobservant of his surroundings. 他对周围环境不够注意。
She is inobservant of the subtle cues in the conversation. 她没有注意到谈话中细微的暗示。


一般过去时:
He was inobservant during the meeting. 他在会议上心不在焉。
She was inobservant of the danger signs. 她没有注意到危险信号。


现在完成时:
He has been inobservant all his life. 他一辈子都不怎么留心。
She has been inobservant of the changes in the market. 她一直没有注意到市场上的变化。


过去完成时:
He had been inobservant of the clues, which led to the mistake. 他之前没有注意到线索，导致了错误。
She had been inobservant of his feelings, which hurt him deeply. 她一直没有注意到他的感受，这深深地伤害了他。


将来时:
He will be inobservant of the details, I'm afraid. 我担心他会忽略细节。
She will be inobservant of the deadline and miss it again. 她会忽略截止日期，再次错过。


虚拟语气:
If he were more observant, he wouldn't have made that mistake. 如果他更细心些，就不会犯那个错误了。
If she had been more observant, she would have noticed the problem earlier. 如果她更细心些，就会早点发现问题了。


被动语态:  (This adjective is rarely used in passive voice)
His inobservance was evident to everyone.  他的不细心大家都看得出来。(This focuses on the noun form "inobservance")


疑问句:
Is he inobservant by nature? 他天生就那么不注意吗？
Was she inobservant of the signs of illness? 她没注意到生病的迹象吗？


否定句:
He is not inobservant; he simply chooses to ignore things. 他并非不注意，只是选择忽略事情。
She was not inobservant; she deliberately overlooked the evidence. 她并非没有注意到；她是故意忽略了证据。

chronically inobservant: 习惯性地不注意的
