self-portraits:/ˈsɛlfˌpɔːrtrɪts/| n.|自画像|A self-portrait is a portrait of an artist created by that artist, typically a painting, drawing, photograph, or sculpture.  Self-portraits offer insights into the artist's self-perception, their artistic style, and the historical context in which they lived.|Example:  Frida Kahlo painted many striking self-portraits that reflected her physical and emotional pain.  弗里达·卡洛画了许多引人注目的自画像，反映了她的身心痛苦。|近义词：self-representation, likeness|反义词：portrait of another person
