disowner:/dɪsˈoʊnər/| vt.| 否认与…的关系；声明与…无关；放弃；抛弃 | To refuse to acknowledge or accept as one's own; to renounce; to repudiate.  |例句：He disowned his son after the scandal.  他在这场丑闻后与儿子断绝了关系。

例句：The company disowned the faulty product. 公司否认有缺陷的产品是他们生产的。


近义词：renounce, repudiate, disclaim, reject | 反义词：acknowledge, claim, own, accept | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disowns his past mistakes. 他否认他过去的错误。
She disowns any responsibility for the project's failure. 她否认对项目失败负任何责任。


一般过去时:
They disowned their illegitimate child. 他们抛弃了他们的私生子。
He publicly disowned his former business partner. 他公开与他以前的商业伙伴断绝关系。


现在完成时:
She has disowned her family's wealth. 她已经放弃了她家族的财富。
He has disowned all connection with the organization. 他已经否认与该组织有任何联系。


过去完成时:
The king had disowned his heir before his death. 国王在他死前已经放弃了他的继承人。
They had disowned their beliefs after experiencing hardship. 他们在经历了苦难后已经放弃了他们的信仰。


将来时:
She will disown her inheritance if she doesn't like the conditions. 如果她不喜欢条件，她将放弃她的遗产。
He will disown his son if he continues this behavior. 如果他继续这种行为，他将与儿子断绝关系。


虚拟语气:
If he disowned his past, he might have a better future. 如果他否认他的过去，他可能会有更好的未来。
I wish I had disowned that bad investment years ago. 我真希望几年前我就放弃了那笔糟糕的投资。


被动语态:
The inheritance was disowned by the beneficiary. 继承权被受益人放弃了。
His claims were disowned by the company.  他的说法被公司否认了。


疑问句:
Would he disown his family for money? 他会为了钱而抛弃他的家人吗？
Has she disowned her principles? 她是否放弃了她的原则？


否定句:
He did not disown his child, despite the pressure. 尽管有压力，他也没有抛弃他的孩子。
She would not disown her beliefs, even in the face of persecution. 她不会放弃她的信仰，即使面临迫害。
