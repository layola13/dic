pedantry:/ˈpedəntri/| n.|卖弄学问；炫耀学识；学究气；迂腐；书呆子气 |The excessive display of learning or scholarship; an ostentatious and inappropriate display of knowledge.  |例句：His pedantry was tiresome; he constantly interrupted the conversation to correct minor grammatical errors. 他的卖弄学问令人厌烦；他不断打断谈话来纠正细微的语法错误。
|例句：The professor's pedantry alienated many of his students.  这位教授的迂腐使得许多学生疏远了他。|近义词：ostentation, showiness, affectation, scholasticism|反义词：humility, modesty, simplicity
