intercloud:/ˈɪntərˌklaʊd/ | n. | 互联云，云互联 | A network of interconnected cloud computing environments, allowing for seamless data and application sharing between different cloud platforms.  It's designed to overcome the limitations of single-cloud deployments and provide greater flexibility and scalability. | Example:  The company uses an intercloud solution to integrate its various cloud services, enhancing data accessibility and resource management.  = 公司使用互联云解决方案来整合其各种云服务，从而增强数据访问和资源管理。|近义词：multi-cloud, hybrid cloud |反义词：single cloud, isolated cloud |名词
