pince-nez:/pɛ̃sˈneɪ/| n. | 鼻夹眼镜 | A pair of eyeglasses that are held in place by pinching the bridge of the nose, without earpieces.  They were popular in the 18th and 19th centuries. | Example:  She adjusted her pince-nez before reading the letter. 她调整了一下鼻夹眼镜才开始读信。 | 近义词: spectacles, eyeglasses, glasses | 反义词: N/A
