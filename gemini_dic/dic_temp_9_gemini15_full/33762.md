circulated:/ˈsɜːrkjʊleɪtɪd/ | v. | 传播；循环；发行 | To move or cause to move continuously along a more or less circular route;  to spread widely; to issue or distribute (something, especially printed matter) to a number of people.  | 例句：The rumor circulated quickly throughout the office.  谣言迅速在办公室里传播开来。

The bank circulated a new five-dollar bill. 银行发行了一种新的五美元钞票。


近义词：spread, distribute, disseminate, diffuse | 反义词：stagnate, withhold, suppress | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The blood circulates throughout the body. = 血液在全身循环。
News circulates quickly in this small town. = 在这个小镇上，消息传播得很快。


一般过去时:
The leaflet was circulated among the students. = 传单在学生中散发。
They circulated the petition widely. = 他们广泛散发请愿书。


现在完成时:
The information has circulated widely online. = 该信息已在网上广泛传播。
The rumor has circulated for weeks.  = 这个谣言已经流传了好几周了。


过去完成时:

The company had circulated the memo before the meeting. = 公司在会议前已经散发了备忘录。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
