plane:/pleɪn/| n. adj.|飞机；平面；平坦的|A plane is a machine that flies.  飞机是一种飞行器。|近义词：aircraft, airplane, flat|反义词：curved, bumpy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plane flies high above the clouds. = 飞机飞得很高，在云层之上。
The pilot carefully navigates the plane. = 飞行员小心地驾驶飞机。


一般过去时:
The plane landed safely. = 飞机安全着陆了。
We took a plane to Paris last year. = 我们去年乘飞机去了巴黎。


现在完成时:
I have flown on many planes. = 我乘坐过许多飞机。
The plane has already departed. = 飞机已经起飞了。


过去完成时:
The plane had already left before we arrived at the airport. = 我们到达机场之前，飞机已经离开了。
They had boarded the plane before the announcement. = 他们在广播之前已经登机了。


将来时:
We will take a plane to London next month. = 我们下个月将乘飞机去伦敦。
The plane will arrive at 6 pm. = 飞机将于下午6点到达。


虚拟语气:
If I had a plane, I would fly around the world. = 如果我有一架飞机，我就会环游世界。
I wish I were on a plane right now. = 我真希望我现在正在飞机上。


被动语态:
The plane was repaired quickly. = 飞机很快就被修理好了。
The flight was delayed due to bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，航班被延误了。


疑问句:
Is this the right plane? = 这是正确的飞机吗？
What time does the plane depart? = 飞机几点起飞？


否定句:
The plane did not arrive on time. = 飞机没有准时到达。
I don't want to fly on a small plane. = 我不想乘坐小型飞机。
