cosurety:/ˈkoʊsjuːrəti/| n. |共同保证人；连带保证人 |A cosurety is a person who shares the responsibility for guaranteeing a debt or obligation with another person.  They are jointly and severally liable, meaning each cosurety is responsible for the full amount of the debt, not just their share. |Example:  The bank required a cosurety to approve the loan application. 这家银行要求一位共同保证人才能批准贷款申请。|近义词：guarantor, surety, bondsman |反义词：debtor, obligor
