condores:/ˈkɒndɔːrz/| n. |秃鹫（复数）|Large, New World vultures known for their size and scavenging habits.  They are typically found in mountainous regions of South America and California.|例句：The condors soared effortlessly on the thermal updrafts.  秃鹫轻松地利用热气流翱翔。|例句：We saw a flock of condors circling high above the canyon. 我们看到一群秃鹫在峡谷上方盘旋。|近义词：vulture, carrion bird|反义词：None specifically, but words implying active hunting could be considered antonyms.
