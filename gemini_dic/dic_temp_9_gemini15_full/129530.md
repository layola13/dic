non-believer:/ˌnɒn bɪˈliːvər/| n. |不信者，怀疑论者|A person who does not believe in a particular religion or in the existence of God.  Someone who doubts or refuses to accept something. |He is a non-believer in organized religion. 他是一个不信奉有组织宗教的人。|She remained a non-believer despite the overwhelming evidence. 尽管证据确凿，她仍然是一个怀疑论者。|近义词：skeptic, doubter, infidel |反义词：believer, faithful
