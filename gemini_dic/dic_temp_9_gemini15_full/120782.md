misappropriate:/ˌmɪsˌəˈproʊprɪeɪt/| vt.|挪用；盗用；滥用|To use something (especially money or property) that is not yours for your own benefit, often illegally.  This implies a wrongful taking or use of something entrusted to one's care.|The accountant was accused of misappropriating company funds.|近义词：embezzle, steal, divert, pilfer|反义词：donate, contribute, return


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misappropriates company funds. = 他挪用公司资金。
They misappropriate resources regularly. = 他们经常挪用资源。


一般过去时:
The manager misappropriated the charitable donations. = 经理挪用了慈善捐款。
She misappropriated her father's inheritance. = 她挪用了她父亲的遗产。


现在完成时:
He has misappropriated thousands of dollars. = 他已经挪用了数千美元。
The company has misappropriated public funds for years. = 这家公司多年来一直挪用公共资金。
