communalists:/kəˈmjuːnəlɪsts/| n.| 共产主义者；集体主义者 |Advocates of communal living or ownership.  People who believe in or support communalism. |例句：The communalists established a cooperative farm where resources were shared. 共产主义者们建立了一个合作农场，资源共享。

例句：Some communalists believe in the abolition of private property. 一些集体主义者相信废除私有财产。


近义词：collectivists, socialists (in some contexts)

反义词：individualists, capitalists
