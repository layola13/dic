osteomalacia:/ˌɒstiːoʊməˈleɪʃə/ /ˌɑːstiːoʊməˈleɪʃə/| n. |骨软化症|A metabolic bone disease characterized by soft, weakened bones.  It's caused by a deficiency of vitamin D, calcium, or phosphate, leading to inadequate mineralization of the bone matrix.  This results in bone pain, muscle weakness, and an increased risk of fractures.|  The elderly woman suffered from osteomalacia due to insufficient sunlight exposure.| 近义词：rickets (in children)| 反义词：osteosclerosis| 名词
