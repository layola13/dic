jerkers:/ˈdʒɜːrkərz/| n. |傻瓜，笨蛋，粗鲁的人|A jerker is someone who is considered rude, inconsiderate, or annoying.  They might be clumsy, insensitive, or act in a way that disrupts others. The term often carries a colloquial and informal tone. |例句：He's such a jerker; he cut me off in traffic and didn't even apologize. 他真是个傻瓜；他在交通中插队，甚至没有道歉。

例句：Stop being such a jerker and help me with this. 别那么粗鲁了，帮我一下。|近义词：idiot, fool, jerk, oaf, lout|反义词：gentleman, lady, considerate person|名词
