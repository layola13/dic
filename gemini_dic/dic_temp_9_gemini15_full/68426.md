falsify:/ˈfɔːlsɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 篡改；伪造；歪曲 | To make false or incorrect; to alter fraudulently.  |例句：He falsified the documents to support his claim. 他伪造文件以支持他的说法。 |例句：The witness attempted to falsify his testimony.  证人试图歪曲他的证词。|近义词：counterfeit, forge, fabricate, misrepresent |反义词：verify, authenticate, confirm
