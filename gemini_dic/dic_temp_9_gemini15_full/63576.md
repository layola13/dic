environmentalist:/ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəlɪst/| n. |环保主义者|A person who is concerned about and actively works to protect the environment.  Environmentalists advocate for policies and actions that promote sustainability and reduce environmental damage. | Many environmentalists are protesting the new pipeline. 许多环保主义者正在抗议新的管道工程。|conservationist, ecologist, green activist |anti-environmentalist, polluter
