pharmaceutics:/ˌfɑːrməˈsuːtɪks/| n. |药物学；制药学|The science of preparing and dispensing drugs.  It encompasses the design, development, and production of medications, including dosage forms and delivery systems.|例句：Pharmaceutics is a crucial field for the development of new and effective medicines. 药物学是开发新型有效药物的关键领域。|例句：The course covers various aspects of pharmaceutics, including drug formulation and stability. 这门课程涵盖药物学的各个方面，包括药物制剂和稳定性。|近义词：pharmacy, pharmacology|反义词：无
