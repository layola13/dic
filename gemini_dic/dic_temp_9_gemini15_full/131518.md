noncombustion:/ˌnɒnkəmˈbʌʃ(ə)n/| n. |非燃烧；不燃烧|The process or state of not burning.  It refers to any process or situation where combustion, or burning, does not occur.| Example: The noncombustion of the fuel led to a shutdown of the engine.  燃料不燃烧导致发动机关闭。|近义词：incombustion, non-ignition|反义词：combustion, ignition|名词
