radon:/ˈreɪdɑːn/|/ˈreɪdɒn/| n. |氡|A radioactive chemical element; a colorless, odorless, tasteless gas.  It is a decay product of radium and is a significant source of indoor air pollution.| 例句：Radon is a naturally occurring radioactive gas.  氡是一种天然存在的放射性气体。| 例句：High levels of radon can increase the risk of lung cancer. 高浓度的氡会增加患肺癌的风险。|近义词：radioactive gas|反义词：无
