preequipping:/ˌpriːɪˈkwɪpɪŋ/ | v. | 预先装备；提前配备 |  To equip beforehand; to provide with equipment in advance.  | Example: The army preequipped the soldiers with advanced weaponry before deployment.  军队在部署之前为士兵配备了先进的武器。 | 近义词: pre-equip, prepare, provision | 反义词:  disarm, deplete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They preequip the aircraft with emergency supplies. = 他们预先为飞机配备应急物资。
The company preequips its employees with the necessary tools. = 公司为员工预先配备必要的工具。

一般过去时:
The team preequipped the laboratory with new equipment. = 团队为实验室预先配备了新设备。
She preequipped her camping gear before the trip. = 她出行前预先准备好了露营装备。

现在完成时:
We have preequipped the vehicles with GPS trackers. = 我们已经为车辆预先配备了GPS追踪器。
They have preequipped the building with fire safety systems. = 他们已经为大楼预先配备了消防安全系统。

过去完成时:
The explorers had preequipped themselves with survival kits. = 探险家们已经预先为自己准备了生存工具包。
The engineers had preequipped the bridge with reinforced supports. = 工程师们已经为桥梁预先配备了加固支架。

虚拟语气:
