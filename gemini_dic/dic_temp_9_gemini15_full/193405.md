seismographically:/ˌsaɪzˈmɒɡrəfɪkli/| adv.|地震仪地;用地震仪测量的|Adverb describing something done using or relating to a seismograph.  It indicates a method of measurement or recording related to seismic activity.|The earthquake was seismographically recorded at a magnitude of 7.0.|近义词：seismically|反义词：None


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't conjugate like verbs or decline like nouns.  It doesn't have tenses or other grammatical variations in the same way as verbs or nouns.  Its usage is consistent.


例句1：The researchers analyzed the data seismographically to determine the epicenter of the earthquake.  研究人员用地震仪分析数据以确定地震的震中。

例句2：The fault line was seismographically mapped to understand its potential for future seismic activity. 断层线通过地震仪绘制地图，以了解其未来地震活动的潜力。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "seismographically".  Its use is primarily in technical contexts.
