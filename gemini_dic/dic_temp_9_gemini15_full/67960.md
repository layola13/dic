facilitated:/fəˈsɪlɪteɪtɪd/ | v. | 促进；使容易；帮助；方便 | To make (an action or process) easy or easier.  |例句：The new software facilitated communication between departments. 新软件促进了部门之间的沟通。

例句：The teacher facilitated the students' learning by providing clear explanations. 老师通过清晰的解释来促进学生的学习。


近义词：aided, assisted, helped, eased, simplified | 反义词：hindered, obstructed, impeded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher facilitates learning through interactive activities. = 老师通过互动活动来促进学习。
This program facilitates efficient workflow. = 这个程序促进了高效的工作流程。

一般过去时:
The mediator facilitated a peaceful resolution to the conflict. = 调解人促成了冲突的和平解决。
The grant facilitated the research project. = 拨款促进了研究项目。

现在完成时:
Technological advancements have facilitated global communication. = 科技进步促进了全球沟通。
The new bridge has facilitated travel between the two cities. = 新桥方便了两座城市之间的交通。

过去完成时:
They had facilitated the process by preparing all the necessary documents beforehand. = 他们事先准备好了所有必要的文书，从而促进了流程的进行。
The previous administration had facilitated the economic growth. = 上届政府促进了经济增长。

将来时:
The new policy will facilitate international trade. = 新政策将促进国际贸易。
The training program will facilitate employee skill development. = 培训项目将促进员工技能发展。

虚拟语气:
If the government facilitated the process, the project would have been completed earlier. = 如果政府促进了这个流程，项目本可以更早完成。
I wish they had facilitated the discussion more effectively. = 我希望他们能更有效地促进讨论。

被动语态:
The agreement was facilitated by the UN. = 协议是由联合国促成的。
The process is facilitated by using the latest technology. = 通过使用最新的技术来促进这个流程。

疑问句:
How can we facilitate the integration of new employees? = 我们如何才能促进新员工的融入？
What measures can be taken to facilitate the transition? = 可以采取哪些措施来促进过渡？

否定句:
The lack of funding did not facilitate the research. = 资金不足没有促进研究。
His stubbornness did not facilitate the negotiations. = 他的固执没有促进谈判。

词源:
From Latin *facilitāre, from facilis ("easy").  The word entered English in the 16th century.
