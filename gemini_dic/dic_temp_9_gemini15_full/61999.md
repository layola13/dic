enameled:/ɪˈnæməld/| adj.| 搪瓷的；上釉的 | Covered with enamel; having a smooth, hard, glossy surface.  |例句：The kitchen had enameled countertops.  厨房有搪瓷台面。|例句：Her teeth were beautifully enameled. 她的牙齿经过了美丽的搪瓷处理。|近义词：glazed, varnished, lacquered |反义词：unpolished, matte

词源:
The word "enameled" comes from the Old French word "esmal," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "smaltum," meaning "a kind of blue pigment."  Over time, the meaning expanded to include other colored glazes and the process of applying them.
