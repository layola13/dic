objectifiable:/əbˈdʒɛktɪfaɪəbl/ | adj. | 可客观化的，可具体化的 | Capable of being made objective or concrete; capable of being treated as an object.  This implies that something abstract or subjective can be given a tangible or measurable form.  | The study aimed to objectify the subjective experience of pain through quantifiable physiological measures. 这项研究旨在通过可量化的生理指标来客观化疼痛的主观体验。 | tangible, concrete, measurable | subjective, intangible, abstract | 形容词
