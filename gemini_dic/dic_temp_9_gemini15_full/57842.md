dressing:/ˈdrɛsɪŋ/ | n. |调味汁，敷料，打扮，装饰 |A dressing is a liquid or semi-liquid sauce used to add flavor and moisture to food. It can also refer to the act of putting on clothes or the decoration of something.  |例句：She made a delicious vinaigrette dressing for the salad. 她为沙拉做了一种美味的香醋酱。

例句：The doctor applied a dressing to the wound. 医生在伤口上敷了药。

近义词：sauce, condiment, adornment, attire

反义词：undressing (去除衣物), plainness (朴素)

词性:名词
