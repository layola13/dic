classlist: /ˈklɑːslɪst/ | n. |班级名单，学生名单| A list of students in a class.  This list usually contains names and sometimes other identifying information like student IDs. | Example: The teacher checked the classlist before starting the lesson.  老师在上课前检查了班级名单。|近义词：roster, register |反义词：无
