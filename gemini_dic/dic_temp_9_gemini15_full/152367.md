pausers:/ˈpɔːzərz/ | n. |暂停者；犹豫者 |A pauser is someone who hesitates or pauses frequently, often before speaking or acting.  They might be characterized by their tendency to take breaks or delays in their actions or speech. This word is less common and usually appears in specific contexts. |例句：The pausers in the debate seemed to be stalling for time.  辩论中的那些犹豫者似乎是在拖延时间。  |近义词：hesitators, procrastinators, dawdlers |反义词：decisive people, impulsive people |名词
