reprisal:/rɪˈpraɪzəl/| n.|报复；报偿|An act of retaliation; revenge.  It often involves similar actions to those that caused the initial offense. Reprisals are typically carried out by a state or group against an enemy.|例句：The bombing was a reprisal for the earlier attack.  这次轰炸是对之前袭击的报复。

例句：The government threatened reprisals against any further acts of terrorism. 政府威胁要对任何进一步的恐怖主义行为进行报复。

近义词：revenge, retaliation, retribution, counterattack |反义词：forgiveness, pardon, reconciliation |词性:名词
