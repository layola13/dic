self-significance:/ˈsɛlf sɪɡˈnɪfɪkəns/| n. |自我重要性，自以为是|The feeling of being important or significant, often to an excessive degree.  This can manifest as arrogance, conceit, or a sense of entitlement. It often stems from a need for validation or a low self-esteem attempting to compensate. |例句：His self-significance prevented him from listening to others' opinions. 他自以为是，听不进别人的意见。

例句：Her constant boasts revealed a deep-seated need for self-significance. 她不断的吹嘘暴露了她内心深处对自我重要性的渴望。


近义词：self-importance, egotism, conceit, narcissism, arrogance

反义词：humility, modesty, self-effacement
