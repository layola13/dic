self-formation:/ˌsɛlf fɔːrˈmeɪʃən/| n. |自我形成；自我塑造；自主发展|The process by which something, especially a person's character or personality, develops or is shaped by its own inherent forces or internal factors rather than external influences.  This often involves conscious effort and self-reflection.|例句：His self-formation was greatly influenced by his travels and experiences.他的自我形成深受他的旅行和经历的影响。|例句：The self-formation of a star involves the gravitational collapse of a gas cloud.恒星的自我形成涉及气体云的引力坍缩。|近义词：self-development, self-cultivation, self-realization|反义词：external imposition, indoctrination|名词
