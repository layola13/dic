/ˈdʒɪmiz/ | n. | 吉米（人名）；撬棍 |  A jimmy is a slang term for a crowbar or a similar tool used for levering or prying.  It can also refer to a person named Jimmy.  |  He used a jimmy to force the lock. 他用撬棍撬开了锁。  The jimmy was a useful tool for the repairman. 对修理工来说，撬棍是个有用的工具。| crowbar, lever |  (no direct antonym)
