/tʃælɪndʒ/| n. vt.|挑战，难题；质疑|A challenge is something difficult that tests your ability or strength.  It can also be an invitation to compete.|例句：The new job presented a real challenge. 这份新工作带来了真正的挑战。|例句：He challenged her to a race. 他向她挑战赛跑。|近义词：test, trial, contest, question|反义词：acceptance, surrender


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He challenges his friends to a game of chess every week. = 他每周都向朋友挑战下棋。
The mountain climbers challenge the harsh weather conditions. = 登山者挑战恶劣的天气条件。

一般过去时:
She challenged the authority of the government. = 她质疑政府的权威。
They challenged the results of the election. = 他们对选举结果提出质疑。

现在完成时:
He has challenged many opponents in his career. = 他在他的职业生涯中挑战过许多对手。
Scientists have challenged the traditional theories. = 科学家们对传统理论提出了挑战。

过去完成时:
The team had challenged the rules before the competition. = 该队在比赛前就对规则提出了质疑。
She had already challenged his conclusions before he presented them. = 他提出结论之前，她就已经对他的结论提出质疑了。

将来时:
They will challenge the decision in court. = 他们将在法庭上对该决定提出质疑。
She will challenge herself to learn a new language. = 她将挑战自己学习一门外语。

虚拟语气:
If I were stronger, I would challenge him to a fight. = 如果我更强壮，我会向他挑战决斗。
I wish I could challenge the unfair system. = 我希望我能挑战这个不公平的制度。

被动语态:
His authority was challenged by many people. = 他的权威受到了许多人的质疑。
The plan was challenged because of its flaws. = 该计划因其缺陷而受到质疑。

疑问句:
Does the task challenge your abilities? = 这项任务能挑战你的能力吗？
What challenges do you face in your work? = 你在工作中面临哪些挑战？

否定句:
He didn't challenge the decision. = 他没有质疑这个决定。
They didn't challenge the status quo. = 他们没有挑战现状。


PHRASE->

rise to the challenge = 迎接挑战
a formidable challenge = 一项艰巨的挑战
