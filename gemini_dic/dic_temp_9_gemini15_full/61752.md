词源:
The word "empassion" comes from the Old French "empassioner," which combines the prefix "em-" (meaning "in" or "into") with the word "passion."  The ultimate root lies in the Latin word "pati," meaning "to suffer" or "to feel," which evolved to encompass strong emotions.
