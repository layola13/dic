containerless:/kənˈteɪnərləs/| adj. | 无容器的，不需容器的 | Without a container; not requiring a container.  This term often refers to processes or manufacturing techniques that do not involve the use of crucibles, molds, or other containment vessels. | Example: Containerless processing is used to produce high-quality materials with minimal contamination.  无容器加工用于生产具有最小污染的高质量材料。|近义词：uncontained, free-floating |反义词：contained, enclosed |形容词
