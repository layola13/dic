precommenting:/ˌpriːˈkɒmɛntɪŋ/| v.|预先注释；提前注释|The act of adding comments to code before writing the code itself.  It is a form of planning and documentation that helps clarify the purpose and functionality of the code before implementation. |Example:  Precommenting the algorithm helped me stay organized during development. = 预先注释算法帮助我在开发过程中保持条理。


The team decided to precomment all functions before coding. = 团队决定在编码之前预先注释所有函数。|近义词：planning, outlining, designing, documenting|反义词：postcommenting (if such a word exists, it would mean adding comments after coding)|动词
