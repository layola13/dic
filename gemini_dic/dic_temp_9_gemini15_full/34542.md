cleanwood:/ˈkliːnwʊd/| n.|干净的木材，净材|Cleanwood refers to timber that is free from defects such as knots, rot, and insect damage.  It is often used in high-quality construction and furniture making. |Example: The sculptor carefully selected cleanwood for his intricate carving.  雕塑家仔细挑选干净的木材进行他复杂的雕刻。The furniture is made of high-grade cleanwood. 这家具是用高级净材做的。|近义词: clearwood, sound timber|反义词: knotty wood, rotten wood
