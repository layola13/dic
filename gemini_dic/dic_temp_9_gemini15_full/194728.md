selfie:/ˈsɛlfi/| n. |自拍；自拍照 |A selfie is a photograph that one has taken of oneself, typically with a smartphone or webcam and shared via social media.  |例句：She took a selfie with her favourite celebrity. 她和自己最喜欢的明星一起自拍了一张照片。|例句：I posted a selfie on Instagram to show my new haircut. 我在Instagram上发布了一张自拍，展示我的新发型。|近义词：self-portrait (更正式)|反义词：无
