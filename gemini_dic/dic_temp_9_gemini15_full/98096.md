inquisitors:/ɪnˈkwɪzɪtərz/| n.|宗教法庭审问者；严厉盘问者|Those who conduct inquisitions, often with harsh methods.  They are characterized by their intense questioning and investigation, often in a religious or political context.|例句：The inquisitors subjected the accused to brutal torture.  宗教法庭审问者对被告施以残酷的酷刑。|例句：The journalist acted like an inquisitor, relentlessly pursuing the truth.  这位记者像个严厉盘问者一样，不懈地追寻真相。|近义词：investigators, interrogators, examiners|反义词：advocates, defenders
