over-designs:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈzaɪnz/| v. |过度设计；设计过度|To design something with more features, complexity, or cost than is necessary or desirable.  This often leads to inefficiency, increased complexity, and higher costs.|例句：The software was over-designed, making it slow and difficult to use.  软件设计过度，导致它运行缓慢且难以使用。

The marketing campaign was over-designed, resulting in confusion among the target audience. 市场活动设计过度，导致目标受众感到困惑。|近义词：overengineer, complicate, embellish |反义词：simplify, streamline, minimize
