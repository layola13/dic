got:/ɡɑːt/ /ɡɒt/ | v. | 得到；抓住；患病；理解 | The past tense and past participle of "get". It has a wide range of meanings depending on context.  |  I got a new car. 我买了一辆新车。 | I got sick yesterday. 我昨天病了。 | obtain, receive, understand | lose, miss | Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I get up early every day. = 我每天早起。
She gets good grades in school. = 她在学校成绩优异。

一般过去时:
I got a letter from my friend. = 我收到了朋友的一封信。
He got a new job last week. = 他上周找到了一份新工作。

现在完成时:
I have got a lot of work to do. = 我有很多工作要做。(英国英语常用have got)
She has gotten used to the new environment. = 她已经习惯了新的环境。(美国英语常用has gotten)

过去完成时:
I had gotten there before the rain started. = 在下雨之前我已经到了那里。(美国英语常用had gotten)
They had got everything ready before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经准备好了一切。(英国英语常用had got)

将来时:
I will get a promotion soon. = 我很快就会升职。
We will get there by bus. = 我们将乘公共汽车到达那里。

虚拟语气:
If I got the chance, I would travel the world. = 如果我有机会，我会环游世界。
I wish I had gotten a better grade on the test. = 我希望我在考试中取得更好的成绩。

被动语态:
The problem got solved quickly. = 问题很快得到了解决。
The window got broken during the storm. = 窗户在暴风雨中被打碎了。

疑问句:
Did you get my email? = 你收到我的邮件了吗？
What did you get for your birthday? = 你生日收到了什么礼物？

否定句:
I didn't get the job. = 我没有得到那份工作。
She didn't get any sleep last night. = 她昨晚一点觉也没睡。
