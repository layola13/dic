prattling:/ˈprætliŋ/| v. |喋喋不休地说话；胡说八道 |To talk continuously and rapidly, especially about unimportant things; to chatter.  |例句：The children were prattling away happily. 孩子们兴高采烈地喋喋不休。

例句：She prattled on endlessly about her trip. 她没完没了地絮叨她的旅行。


近义词：babbling, chattering, garrulous, jabbering | 反义词：silent, quiet, taciturn


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The baby prattles incessantly. = 婴儿不停地咿呀学语。
He prattles on about politics all day. = 他整天喋喋不休地谈论政治。


一般过去时:
She prattled about her day at school. = 她喋喋不休地谈论她在学校的一天。
They prattled away until the sun set. = 他们一直唠叨到日落。


现在完成时:
The toddler has been prattling since morning. = 这个幼儿从早上就开始咿呀学语了。
She has prattled away all the important details. = 她已经把所有重要的细节都絮叨掉了。


过去完成时:
He had prattled for hours before anyone noticed. = 他已经唠叨了几个小时才有人注意到。
They had prattled themselves hoarse. = 他们已经唠叨得嗓子都哑了。


将来时:
She will prattle about her new shoes all afternoon. = 她整个下午都会喋喋不休地谈论她的新鞋子。
He will prattle on until someone stops him. = 他会一直唠叨下去，直到有人阻止他。


虚拟语气:
If she prattled less, people would listen more. = 如果她少说一些，人们就会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't prattle so much. = 我希望他不那么唠叨。


被动语态:
His ideas were prattled away by the group. = 他的想法被小组的人喋喋不休地掩盖了。 (This is less common usage)
The topic was prattled to death. =  这个话题被说得烂熟了。(This is less common usage)


疑问句:
Is he still prattling about his vacation? = 他还在喋喋不休地谈论他的假期吗？
Were the children prattling incessantly? = 孩子们是不是一直在喋喋不休？


否定句:
He didn't prattle about his problems. = 他没有喋喋不休地谈论他的问题。
She didn't prattle on about her trip. = 她没有没完没了地絮叨她的旅行。
