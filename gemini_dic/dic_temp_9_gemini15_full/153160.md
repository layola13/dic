pelican:/ˈpɛləkən/| n. |鹈鹕|A large water bird with a long beak and a pouch under its beak for catching fish.  Pelicans are known for their distinctive appearance and cooperative fishing habits.|例句：We saw a brown pelican diving for fish near the shore. 我们在岸边看到一只棕鹈鹕在潜水捕鱼。

例句：A flock of pelicans soared gracefully over the ocean.  一群鹈鹕优雅地飞翔在海洋上空。

近义词：暂无完全近义词，可根据语境选择其他大型水鸟名称。

反义词：暂无

词性：名词
