slam:/slæm/ | vt., n. |猛击，砰地关上；猛烈批评；灌篮 | To strike or shut with force and noise; to criticize harshly; (in basketball) to throw the ball forcefully through the hoop.  | 例句：He slammed the door in anger. 他生气地砰地关上门。 He slammed the government's policies. 他猛烈批评政府的政策。  | 近义词：bang, crash, smash, criticize, condemn | 反义词：gently close, praise


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slams the door every time he leaves. = 他每次离开都砰地关上门。
She slams her fist on the table. = 她重重地把拳头砸在桌子上。

一般过去时:
He slammed the book shut. = 他砰地把书合上了。
The car slammed into the wall. = 汽车猛撞到墙上。

现在完成时:
He has slammed the door several times today. = 他今天已经砰地关了好几次门了。
She has slammed the opposition's arguments. = 她已经猛烈抨击了反对派的论点。

过去完成时:
He had slammed the brakes just before the accident. = 事故发生前他紧急刹车了。
They had slammed the door on any further negotiations. = 他们已经断然拒绝了进一步的谈判。

将来时:
He will slam the ball through the hoop. = 他将把球用力灌篮。
She will slam the report on the table. = 她将会把报告重重地拍在桌子上。

虚拟语气:
If he slammed the door, I would have heard it. = 如果他砰地关上门，我就会听到。
I wish he hadn't slammed the door so hard. = 我真希望他不要把门关得那么响。

被动语态:
The door was slammed shut. = 门被砰地关上了。
His arguments were slammed by the critics. = 他的论点受到了评论家的猛烈抨击。

疑问句:
Did he slam the door? = 他是不是砰地关上门了？
Did she slam the brakes? = 她紧急刹车了吗？

否定句:
He didn't slam the door. = 他没有砰地关上门。
She didn't slam her fist on the table. = 她没有把拳头砸在桌子上。
