capitulator:/kəˈpɪtʃuleɪtər/| n. |投降者；屈服者 |One who capitulates; one who surrenders.  A capitulator is someone who yields or surrenders, especially in war or under pressure. |Example: The capitulator was forced to sign a treaty surrendering his territory.  投降者被迫签署了一项条约，放弃了他的领土。|近义词：surrenderer, concessionaire |反义词：victor, conqueror |名词
