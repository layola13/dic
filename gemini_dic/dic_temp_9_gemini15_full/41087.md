coppicing:/ˈkɒpɪsɪŋ/ | n. | 砍伐（树木）; 矮林培育法 | The practice of cutting a tree trunk close to the ground to encourage the growth of new shoots.  This method is used to manage woodlands and produce fuelwood or other products.  | Example: Coppicing is a traditional woodland management technique that promotes biodiversity. = 矮林培育法是一种传统的林地管理技术，可以促进生物多样性。

The ancient practice of coppicing has been revived in some areas for its environmental benefits. = 在一些地区，古老的矮林培育法因其环境效益而得以恢复。


近义词：pollarding (修剪树枝), stooling (类似coppicing，但更强调从树桩上发芽)
反义词：clear-cutting (皆伐), deforestation (森林砍伐)

词源:  From Middle English *coppicen, from Old French *copice, from copir "to cut".  The word ultimately derives from the Latin *capere, meaning "to take" or "to seize".
