launder:/ˈlɔːndər/ /ˈlɔːn(d)ər/| vt.|洗（衣服）；洗钱|To wash clothes; to conceal the origins of illegally obtained money.|例句：She laundered her clothes after a long day of work. 她长久工作后洗了衣服。|例句：The government is trying to crack down on money laundering. 政府正在努力打击洗钱活动。|近义词：wash, clean, bleach|反义词：soil, dirty
