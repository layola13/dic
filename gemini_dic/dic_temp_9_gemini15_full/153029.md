peeks:/piːks/| n. v.|偷看；瞥见；高峰；|To look quickly and secretly at something.  A quick look.|例句：She peeked through the keyhole. 她透过锁眼偷看。


例句：The mountain peaks were covered in snow.  山峰覆盖着白雪。|近义词：glance, glimpse, peak|反义词：stare, gaze


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He peeks through the blinds. 他透过百叶窗偷看。
She often peeks at her phone during meetings. 她经常在会议期间偷看手机。

一般过去时:
I peeked into the room to see if anyone was there. 我偷偷地往房间里看了一眼，看看有没有人在。
The cat peeked out from behind the curtain. 猫从窗帘后面探出头来。

现在完成时:
She has peeked at the exam answers. 她已经偷看过考试答案了。
He has peeked into my private files! 他已经偷看过我的私人文件了！

过去完成时:
I had peeked at the present before my birthday. 我在我生日之前已经偷偷看过礼物了。
They had peeked into the room, seeing nobody. 他们已经偷偷看过房间，什么人也没看到。

将来时:
I will peek at the recipe before I start cooking. 我会在开始做饭之前偷看一下食谱。
She will peek at the letter before opening it. 她会在打开信之前先偷看一下。

虚拟语气:
If I peeked, I would see something interesting. 如果我偷看，我会看到一些有趣的东西。
I wish I hadn't peeked at my sister's diary. 我真希望我没有偷看过我妹妹的日记。

被动语态:
The letter was peeked at by someone. 这封信被某人偷看过。
His privacy was peeked at by the reporters. 他的隐私被记者们偷窥了。

疑问句:
Did she peek at the answers? 她有偷看答案吗？
Has he peeked into my bag? 他偷看过我的包了吗？

否定句:
I didn't peek at your paper. 我没有偷看你试卷。
She doesn't peek at others' work. 她不偷看别人的作业。
