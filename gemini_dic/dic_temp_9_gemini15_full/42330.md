counter-tenor: /ˈkaʊntərˌtɛnər/ | n. |男高音(一种男声演唱技巧)| A male singer with a vocal range that extends into the higher register typically associated with female voices.  Counter-tenors use a falsetto or head voice technique to produce these high notes.  |  The counter-tenor's soaring high notes brought the audience to their feet. 男高音那高亢的音符让观众起立鼓掌。 | tenor, alto | bass |
