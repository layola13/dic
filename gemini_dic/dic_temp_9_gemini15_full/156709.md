pieced:/piːst/| v.|拼凑，修补|To join or fit together (pieces of something) to form a whole.|例句：She pieced together the fragments of the broken vase. 她把破碎的花瓶碎片拼凑起来。|例句：He pieced the story together from various sources. 他从各种来源拼凑出这个故事。|近义词：assembled, mended, repaired, reconstructed|反义词：destroyed, demolished, dismantled
