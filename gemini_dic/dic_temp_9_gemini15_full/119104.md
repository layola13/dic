metrologies:/məˈtrɒlədʒiz/ | n. |计量学(复数) |The science of measurement and its applications.  It encompasses the theory and practice of measurement, including the definition of units, the development of measurement standards, and the calibration of measuring instruments. |例句：Advances in metrologies have led to more precise measurements in various fields.  计量学的进步导致了各个领域更精确的测量。

The study of metrologies is crucial for ensuring the accuracy and reliability of scientific experiments.  计量学的研究对于确保科学实验的准确性和可靠性至关重要。|近义词：measurement science, measurement technology |反义词：None (the antonym would be the absence of accurate measurement) |名词
