noctambulism:/ˌnɒktˈæmbjʊlɪzəm/ | n. |梦游;夜游症 | The act of walking or performing other actions while asleep; sleepwalking.  It's a parasomnia, a sleep disorder.  |例句：His noctambulism was so severe he often wandered outside in his sleep.他的梦游很严重，经常在睡梦中外出。

例句：The doctor diagnosed her condition as noctambulism after observing her sleep patterns.医生观察了她的睡眠模式后，诊断她的病情为梦游症。


近义词：sleepwalking | 反义词：N/A


词源:
From Latin *noctambulatio, from nox, noctis "night" + ambulare "to walk".
