rebutted:/rɪˈbʌtɪd/| v.|反驳；驳斥；反证|To offer a counterargument against a claim or evidence.  To refute or disprove.|例句：He rebutted the accusations with evidence of his innocence. 他用证据反驳了对他清白的指控。


例句：The lawyer skillfully rebutted the witness's testimony.  律师巧妙地反驳了证人的证词。


近义词：refute, contradict, counter, deny, oppose


反义词：confirm, support, endorse, corroborate


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rebuts all criticism. = 他反驳所有的批评。
She rebuts his arguments effectively. = 她有效地反驳了他的论点。


一般过去时:
He rebutted the charges against him. = 他反驳了针对他的指控。
The scientist rebutted the opposing theory. = 科学家反驳了相反的理论。


现在完成时:
They have rebutted the claims made in the article. = 他们已经反驳了文章中提出的说法。
She has repeatedly rebutted his accusations. = 她一再反驳他的指控。


过去完成时:
He had already rebutted the argument before the meeting. = 会议前他已经反驳了这个论点。
The defense lawyer had thoroughly rebutted the prosecution's case. = 辩护律师已经彻底反驳了检方的案件。


将来时:
He will rebut the accusations in court. = 他将在法庭上反驳这些指控。
She will rebut any attempt to discredit her work. = 她将反驳任何试图诋毁她工作的企图。


虚拟语气:
If he could, he would rebut the false statements. = 如果可以，他会反驳那些不实言论。
I wish I had been able to rebut his claims more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地反驳他的说法。


被动语态:
His argument was rebutted by several experts. = 他的论点被几位专家反驳了。
The theory was effectively rebutted by new research. = 这一理论被新的研究有效地反驳了。


疑问句:
Can you rebut this evidence? = 你能反驳这个证据吗？
How will he rebut the opponent’s claims? = 他将如何反驳对手的说法？


否定句:
He couldn't rebut the evidence presented against him. = 他无法反驳针对他的证据。
She failed to rebut the accusations convincingly. = 她没能令人信服地反驳这些指控。



PHRASE->
rebut a claim = 反驳一项说法

rebut evidence = 反驳证据
