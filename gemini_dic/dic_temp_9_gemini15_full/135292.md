nonpassengers: /ˌnɑːnˈpæsɪndʒərz/ | n. |非乘客 |People who are not passengers; those who are not traveling on a vehicle or conveyance.  This term often refers to crew members, staff, or other individuals present on a vehicle but not paying fares or considered passengers for transportation purposes. | Example:  The number of nonpassengers on the train, including the crew and maintenance staff, was surprisingly high.  = 火车上非乘客的人数，包括工作人员和维护人员，出奇地高。

The security check included all passengers and nonpassengers. = 安全检查包括所有乘客和非乘客。


近义词: crew, staff, personnel | 反义词: passengers


词性: 名词
