indogen:/ˈɪndədʒən/| n.|内源性物质；内生基因|A substance produced within an organism, or a gene that is expressed within the organism.  It often refers to substances produced by the body itself, rather than being introduced from an external source.| Example:  The study focused on the role of indogens in regulating blood pressure.  这项研究侧重于内源性物质在调节血压中的作用。|近义词：endogenous substance, intrinsic factor|反义词：exogenous substance
