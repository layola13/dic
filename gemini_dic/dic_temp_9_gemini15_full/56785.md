doppler:/ˈdɒplər/| /ˈdɑːplər/ | n. adj. |多普勒,多普勒效应|The Doppler effect is the change in frequency or wavelength of a wave (such as light or sound) for an observer who is moving relative to the source of the wave.|例句：The Doppler effect is used in radar guns to measure the speed of vehicles. 多普勒效应用于雷达测速枪来测量车辆的速度。|例句：A Doppler ultrasound can detect blood flow in vessels. 多普勒超声波可以检测血管中的血流。|近义词：frequency shift|反义词：无|名词，形容词
