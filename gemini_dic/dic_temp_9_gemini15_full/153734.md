pepperers:/ˈpɛpərərz/| n. |胡椒粉撒布者；（尤指）大量使用胡椒粉的人|A pepperer is someone who liberally uses pepper in their cooking or on their food.  The word suggests a generous, perhaps even excessive, application of pepper.|例句：He's a real pepperer; every dish he makes is incredibly spicy. 他真是个胡椒粉撒布狂；他做的每道菜都辣得不得了。|近义词：seasoner, spice enthusiast|反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可考虑与"underspicer"  相对，但此词非正式词汇)|名词
