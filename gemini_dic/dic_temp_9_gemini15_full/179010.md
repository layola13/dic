redelegating: /ˌriːˈdɛləɡeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 再委派；重新委派 |  The act of delegating a task or responsibility again, often after an initial delegation has proved unsuccessful or inefficient.  It implies a transfer of authority or responsibility to a different person or group. |

例句：
The manager, unhappy with the initial team's progress, decided to redelegate the project to a more experienced group.  经理对最初团队的进展不满意，决定将项目重新委派给一个更有经验的小组。

The CEO redelegating the task to another department after the first team missed the deadline.  首席执行官在第一个团队错过截止日期后将任务重新委派给另一个部门。


近义词：reassigning, redistributing, re-allocating

反义词：centralizing, concentrating, retaining
