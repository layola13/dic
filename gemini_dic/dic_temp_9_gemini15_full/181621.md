relaunching: /ˌriːˈlɔːntʃɪŋ/ | v. |重新启动，再次推出 | The act of launching something again, often after a period of inactivity or following a failure.  It implies improvements or changes have been made. | 例句：The company is relaunching its flagship product with improved features.  公司正在重新推出其旗舰产品，并改进了一些功能。

The marketing campaign is being relaunched next month with a new strategy.  下个月将采用新策略重新启动营销活动。


近义词：reintroduction, revival, restart, relaunch


反义词：discontinuation, termination, cessation


词性：动词(gerund)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are relaunching the website next week. = 他们下周将重新启动网站。
The company is constantly relaunching its products to stay competitive. = 公司不断重新推出产品以保持竞争力。

一般过去时:
They relaunched the product after addressing customer feedback. = 在处理客户反馈后，他们重新推出了该产品。
The museum relaunched its exhibition with new artifacts. = 博物馆用新的文物重新推出了它的展览。

现在完成时:
They have relaunched their marketing campaign three times this year. = 今年他们已经三次重新启动了他们的营销活动。
The government has relaunched its environmental protection program. = 政府已经重新启动了它的环境保护项目。

过去完成时:
Before the relaunch, they had conducted extensive market research. = 在重新推出之前，他们已经进行了广泛的市场调查。
They had already invested heavily before the product was relaunched. = 在产品重新推出之前，他们已经投入了巨资。

将来时:
They will relaunch the app with a new user interface. = 他们将用新的用户界面重新推出该应用程序。
The band will relaunch their tour next spring. = 乐队将在明年春天重新开始他们的巡演。

虚拟语气:
If they had listened to customer feedback, the relaunch would have been more successful. = 如果他们听了客户的反馈，重新推出就会更成功。
It's time they relaunched their brand image; if only they would. = 是时候他们重新塑造品牌形象了；如果他们愿意就好了。

被动语态:
The product is being relaunched next quarter. = 该产品将在下个季度重新推出。
The website was relaunched after a major security breach. = 网站在发生重大安全漏洞后重新启动。

疑问句:
When is the company relaunching its product? = 公司什么时候重新推出它的产品？
Is the project being relaunched after the delay? = 项目在延误后是否会重新启动？

否定句:
They did not relaunch the product due to budget constraints. = 由于预算限制，他们没有重新推出该产品。
The company will not be relaunching its failing product line. = 公司将不会重新推出其失败的产品线。
