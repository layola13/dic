deaness:/ˈdiːnəs/| n. |女院长，女系主任|A female dean, typically of a college or university.  The term is less common than "dean" and often used to specifically highlight the female gender in contexts where the default assumption might be male.|Example:  She was the first deaness of the law school. 她是的法学院第一位女院长。|近义词：female dean, headmistress (in some contexts)|反义词：None (it's a specific title)
