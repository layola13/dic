graphic:/ˈɡræfɪk/| adj.| 图形的；图像的；生动的；图解的 | Relating to visual art, especially in the form of drawings or prints.  It can also describe something vividly described or depicted. | 例句：The book contained many graphic illustrations. 这本书包含许多生动的插图。

例句：The accident report included graphic details of the injuries. 事故报告包含了对伤情的生动描述。


近义词：visual, pictorial, vivid, descriptive | 反义词：vague, indistinct, unclear

词源:
The word "graphic" comes from the Greek word "graphein" (γράφειν), meaning "to write" or "to draw."  Over time, its meaning evolved to encompass visual representations and depictions, particularly those created through drawing or printing.
