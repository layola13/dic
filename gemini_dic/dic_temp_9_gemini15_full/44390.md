cross-fertilizable: /ˌkrɒsˈfɜːrtɪlaɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可异花授粉的；可互相借鉴的 | Capable of being cross-fertilized; able to benefit from the exchange of ideas or practices between different groups or systems.  | Example:  The two research teams found their projects to be cross-fertilizable, leading to significant advancements. 这两个研究团队发现他们的项目可以互相借鉴，从而取得了重大进展。 | 近义词：interchangeable, mutually beneficial, synergistic | 反义词：incompatible, isolated, independent
