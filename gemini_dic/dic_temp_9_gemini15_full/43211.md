crache:/kræʃ/| n.|临时托儿所；临时保育处|A crèche is a nursery, especially one provided at a workplace or other institution for the children of employees or students.  |Example: The company provides a crèche for its employees' children. 这家公司为员工的孩子们提供了一个托儿所。|近义词：nursery, day-care center|反义词：N/A|名词
