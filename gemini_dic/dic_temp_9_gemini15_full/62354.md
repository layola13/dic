endgame:/ˈɛndˌɡeɪm/| n. |结局；最终阶段；战略的最后阶段 |The final stage of a long process, especially a conflict or competition.  It often refers to a decisive or crucial phase where the ultimate outcome is determined.  |例句：The endgame of the war involved intense negotiations between the warring factions. = 战争的最终阶段涉及交战各方之间的激烈谈判。

The endgame of his career was marked by a series of highly successful projects. = 他职业生涯的最后阶段以一系列非常成功的项目为标志。


近义词：climax, conclusion, finale, denouement |反义词：beginning, start, commencement |词性：名词
