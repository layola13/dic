columnists:/ˈkɑːləmnɪsts/| n.|专栏作家|A columnist is a writer who regularly contributes articles or columns to a newspaper, magazine, or website.  They typically offer opinions, analyses, or commentary on a particular topic or range of topics.|Example:  Many famous columnists have strong opinions on political issues. 许多著名的专栏作家对政治问题有强烈的意见。

Many successful columnists have built a loyal following through their engaging writing style.许多成功的专栏作家通过引人入胜的写作风格建立了忠实的追随者。|近义词：writers, commentators, journalists|反义词：reader, audience
