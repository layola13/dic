empanels:/ɪmˈpænəl/| vt.|编入名单；选入陪审团|To select or enroll (someone) as a member of a panel, especially a jury.|The judge empaneled the jury for the trial.  法官为审判选入了陪审团。|The company empaneled a group of experts to review the proposal. 公司召集了一组专家来审查该提案。|appoint, select, choose|disqualify, reject|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The judge empanels the jury carefully. = 法官仔细地选任陪审团。
They empanel a new committee each year. = 他们每年都组建一个新的委员会。


一般过去时:
The court empaneled a jury of twelve people. = 法庭选任了一个由十二人组成的陪审团。
She was empaneled to serve on the grand jury. = 她被选入大陪审团服役。
