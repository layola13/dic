offhand:/ˌɔːfˈhænd/ | adj. | 未经考虑的；即兴的；随便的 |  Without premeditation or preparation; impromptu.  |  He gave an offhand remark about the weather. 他随意地评论了一下天气。  |  casual, impromptu, spontaneous | premeditated, deliberate, planned | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often gives offhand answers. = 他经常给出未经考虑的答案。
Her offhand comments are sometimes hurtful. = 她不经大脑的评论有时会伤人。


一般过去时:
He gave an offhand response to the question. = 他对这个问题做出了即兴的回答。
She made an offhand remark about my new haircut. = 她随意地评论了我的新发型。


现在完成时:
He has always given offhand opinions on politics. = 他一直以来对政治都发表一些即兴的意见。
She has often made offhand gestures that I found offensive. = 她经常做出一些我认为冒犯人的随意举动。


过去完成时:
He had already given an offhand answer before I could ask the question properly. = 在我能够正确地问出问题之前，他已经给出了一个未经考虑的答案。
She had made several offhand comments about the project before realizing her mistake. = 在意识到自己的错误之前，她已经对这个项目做了一些不经意的评论。


将来时:
He will probably give an offhand reply. = 他可能会给出仓促的答复。
She will likely make an offhand suggestion at the meeting. = 她可能会在会议上提出一个随意的建议。


虚拟语气:
If he had thought more carefully, he wouldn't have given such an offhand answer. = 如果他考虑得更仔细一些，他就不会给出如此草率的答案。
I wish she hadn't made such an offhand remark about my family. = 我真希望她不要对我家人做出如此轻率的评论。


被动语态:
His offhand remarks were often misunderstood. = 他不经大脑的评论经常被误解。
The suggestion was dismissed as an offhand comment. = 这个建议被认为是一个随意的评论而被驳回。


疑问句:
Was that an offhand remark, or did you mean it? = 那是一句随口说说的话，还是你认真的？
Is it appropriate to give offhand answers in formal settings? = 在正式场合给出未经考虑的答案是否合适？


否定句:
His answer wasn't offhand; he'd clearly thought about it. = 他的回答并非即兴的；他显然已经考虑过了。
Her behavior was not offhand; it was deliberate. = 她的行为并非随意；是故意的。
