secured:/sɪˈkjʊrd/ | adj. v. |安全的；牢固的；已获得的；已担保的 |  protected from loss, damage, or attack; obtained or arranged for something |The building is secured against intruders. 这栋建筑物防范入侵者。 |近义词：safe, protected, guaranteed |反义词：unsecured, vulnerable, unprotected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He secures the door every night. 他每晚都锁门。
They secure funding for their project. 他们为他们的项目筹集资金。


一般过去时:
She secured a loan from the bank. 她从银行获得了一笔贷款。
We secured the package tightly with rope. 我们用绳子紧紧地捆绑包裹。


现在完成时:
They have secured a place at university. 他们已经获得了一个大学名额。
He has secured his future by investing wisely. 他通过精明的投资确保了自己的未来。


过去完成时:
She had secured a job before graduation.  她在毕业前已经找到了一份工作。
They had secured the building before the storm hit. 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经保护好了建筑物。


将来时:
We will secure the perimeter before the event begins. 活动开始前，我们将确保周边安全。
She will secure a contract with the client next week. 她下周将与客户签订合同。


虚拟语气:
If we secured more funding, we could expand the project. 如果我们能获得更多资金，我们就能扩大项目规模。
I wish I had secured a better position. 我真希望我当时能得到一个更好的职位。


被动语态:
The building was secured by guards. 这栋建筑由警卫守护。
The deal was secured after weeks of negotiation.经过几周的谈判后，这笔交易达成了。


疑问句:
Is the area secured? 这个区域安全吗？
Have you secured the necessary permits? 你已经获得必要的许可证了吗？


否定句:
He hasn't secured a job yet. 他还没找到工作。
The documents were not securely secured. 文件没有被安全地保护起来。
