overfloat:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊt/ | v. | 溢出，泛滥 | To overflow; to flood.  The verb implies exceeding a container's capacity and spilling over. | The river overfloated its banks after the heavy rain.  河水暴雨过后漫过了河岸。|  overflow, inundate |  underflow, drain |  动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bathtub overfloats when we fill it too high. = 当我们把浴缸装得太满时，它就会溢出。
The river often overfloats its banks in spring. = 春天这条河经常泛滥。


一般过去时:
The milk overfloated the glass. = 牛奶溢出了杯子。
The reservoir overfloated during the monsoon season. = 雨季期间水库水位上涨溢出。
