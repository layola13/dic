precooked:/ˌpriːˈkʊkt/| adj.|预先煮熟的；预先烹调的|Already cooked before being sold or served.  Precooked foods are convenient but may lack the flavor of freshly cooked meals.|例句：The precooked chicken saved me a lot of time. 预先煮熟的鸡肉节省了我很多时间。
例句：I bought some precooked meals for the week. 我买了一些预先烹调好的食物以备一周食用。|近义词：ready-made, prepared, cooked beforehand|反义词：raw, uncooked
