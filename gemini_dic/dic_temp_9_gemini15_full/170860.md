pseudoheroic:/ˌsuːdoʊhɪˈroʊɪk/| adj. |伪英雄的；冒牌英雄的；虚张声势的 | seemingly heroic but actually lacking genuine heroism;  pretending to be heroic.  The character's actions, while outwardly impressive, revealed a pseudoheroic nature upon closer examination. | 近义词：pretentious, affected, bombastic, showy | 反义词：genuine, authentic, heroic, humble


例句：

His pseudoheroic attempts to rescue the cat from the tree only resulted in him getting stuck himself.  他的伪英雄行为试图从树上救下猫，结果却把自己也困住了。

The novel explores the pseudoheroic posturing of a man who craves admiration but lacks true courage. 这部小说探讨了一个渴望赞赏却缺乏真正勇气的人的虚张声势的姿态。
