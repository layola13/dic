enargite:/ɪˈnɑːrdʒaɪt/| n. |辉铜矿|A sulfosalt mineral composed of copper, arsenic, and sulfur, with the chemical formula Cu3AsS4.  It is an important ore of copper.|Enargite crystals are often found in hydrothermal veins and are typically dark gray to black in color.  The mineral is named for its distinct, easily visible crystals.|The enargite deposit yielded a significant amount of copper ore.|近义词：tennantite (similar copper arsenosulfide)|反义词：无
