consignatory: /ˌkɒnˈsɪɡnətəri/ /kənˈsɪɡnəˌtɔːri/ | n. | 签署人，签字人 | A person who signs a document, especially a contract or agreement.  | Example: All the consignatories to the treaty agreed to the terms.  所有条约的签署人都同意这些条款。


The consignatories of the agreement met to discuss its implementation.  协议的签署人会面讨论其执行情况。|近义词：signer, signatory, subscriber | 反义词：N/A | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a consignatory to the contract. = 他是这份合同的签署人之一。
She is a consignatory on the document. = 她是这份文件的签署人之一。


一般过去时:
He was a consignatory to the previous agreement. = 他是先前协议的签署人之一。
She was a consignatory on the old contract. = 她是旧合同的签署人之一。


现在完成时:
They have been consignatories to many important documents. = 他们已经签署过许多重要的文件。
She has been a consignatory on several agreements this year. = 今年她已经签署了几份协议。


过去完成时:
He had been a consignatory to the document before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他曾是该文件的签署人。
She had been a consignatory on the contract for over a decade. = 她担任这份合同的签署人已经十多年了。


将来时:
He will be a consignatory to the upcoming treaty. = 他将是即将签订的条约的签署人之一。
She will be a consignatory on the new agreement. = 她将是新协议的签署人之一。


虚拟语气:
If he were a consignatory, he would have more influence. = 如果他是签署人，他将更有影响力。
If she had been a consignatory, the outcome might have been different. = 如果她是签署人，结果可能会有所不同。


被动语态:
The document was signed by many consignatories. = 这份文件由许多签署人签署。
The agreement was finalized after all consignatories had signed. = 所有签署人签字后，协议才最终确定。


疑问句:
Are you a consignatory to this contract? = 你是这份合同的签署人吗？
Who are the consignatories on this document? = 这份文件的签署人是谁？


否定句:
He is not a consignatory to that agreement. = 他不是那份协议的签署人。
She was not a consignatory on the original document. = 她不是原文件的签署人。


PHRASE->
consignatory to a treaty = 条约签署人
consignatory of a contract = 合同签署人
multiple consignatories = 多个签署人
principal consignatory = 主要签署人
list of consignatories = 签署人名单

词源:
From "consign" (to entrust) + "-atory" (denoting a person or thing that performs an action).  The word reflects the act of entrusting oneself to the terms of a document through signature.
