puls:/pʌls/| n.|脉搏，脉冲，推动，活力|a regular beating or throbbing, especially of the heart or arteries; a short burst of energy or activity.|例句：I could feel my pulse quicken with excitement. 我能感觉到我的脉搏因兴奋而加快。|例句：The electric pulse started the machine. 电脉冲启动了机器。|近义词：beat, throb, impulse|反义词：stillness, calm
