rekeying:/ˌriːˈkiːɪŋ/| v.|重新输入密钥；重新编码|The process of entering a key or code again, often for security purposes, such as changing a password or updating encryption keys.|例句：The system requires rekeying after a security breach.  系统在安全漏洞后需要重新输入密钥。|例句：Regular rekeying of the encryption key is crucial for maintaining data security. 定期重新输入加密密钥对于维护数据安全至关重要。|近义词：re-encrypting, re-encoding|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be to leave the key unchanged.|动词

They rekey the system every month. = 他们每月对系统重新编码。
We rekey the database regularly. = 我们定期对数据库重新输入密钥。
