reargument:/ˌriˈɑːrɡjumənt/ | n. | 再辩论，复议 | A renewed argument or discussion, often after a previous decision or judgment.  It typically involves presenting new evidence or arguments to reconsider a previous conclusion. | The court granted a reargument to allow the defendant to present newly discovered evidence. = 法院准予再辩论，允许被告提交新发现的证据。  The lawyer requested a reargument based on a misinterpretation of the law in the original ruling. = 律师要求复议，理由是原判对法律的误解。 | reconsideration, rehearing, review | acceptance, agreement | noun
