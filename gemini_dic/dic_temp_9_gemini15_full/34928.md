cloaked:/kloʊkt/ | adj. | 披着斗篷的；掩盖的；隐藏的 | Covered or hidden; disguised.  The word often implies secrecy or mystery. | 例句：The spy moved through the shadows, cloaked in darkness.  间谍在黑暗中潜行。

The figure was cloaked in a long, dark robe.  那个身影披着长长的黑色长袍。


近义词：hidden, concealed, masked, shrouded, veiled | 反义词：exposed, revealed, unveiled


词源:  From the past participle of the verb *cloak*, ultimately deriving from the Middle English word *cloke*, meaning "a loose outer garment," which in turn comes from the Old Norse word *klákr*.
