sculptress:/ˈskʌlptres/| n.|女雕刻家|A woman who sculpts.  A sculptress is a female artist who creates three-dimensional works of art using various materials such as clay, stone, metal, or wood.  |例句：The museum showcased the works of a renowned sculptress from Italy.  这个博物馆展出了意大利一位著名女雕刻家的作品。

The famous sculptress received numerous awards for her innovative techniques.这位著名的女雕刻家因其创新的技法获得了无数奖项。|近义词：female sculptor, woman sculptor|反义词：sculptor (male)|词性：名词
