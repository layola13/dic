lamina:/ˈlæmɪnə/| n.|薄片；薄板；叶片|A thin layer, membrane, or plate of tissue, especially in plants or minerals.  For example, in botany, it refers to a thin, flat leaf-like structure. In geology, it refers to a thin layer of rock or mineral. |例句：The geologist carefully examined the lamina of the rock sample.  地质学家仔细检查了岩石样品的薄片。

The leaves of the fern are composed of many delicate laminae. 蕨类的叶子由许多精致的叶片构成。|近义词：layer, sheet, film, flake|反义词：block, mass, chunk|名词
