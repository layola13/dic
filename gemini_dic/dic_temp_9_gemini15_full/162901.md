perception: /pərˈsɛpʃən/ | n. |感知，感觉，理解 |The ability to see, hear, or become aware of something through the senses.  It also refers to the way in which something is understood or interpreted. |例句：His perception of the situation was quite different from mine. 他的看法与我的大相径庭。|例句：The child's perception of colours is still developing.孩子的颜色感知能力仍在发展中。|近义词：understanding, awareness, comprehension, insight |反义词：misunderstanding, misinterpretation, ignorance |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His perception of the world is quite unique. = 他对世界的感知非常独特。
My perception of the problem is different from yours. = 我对这个问题的看法与你不同。


一般过去时:
Her perception of the event was clouded by emotion. = 她对事件的感知被情绪蒙蔽了。
His perception of the music changed after he learned more about its history. = 在他了解更多关于音乐的历史后，他对音乐的感知发生了改变。


现在完成时:
My perception of her has changed since we last met. = 自从上次见面以来，我对她的看法改变了。
Scientists have improved their perception of the universe through advanced technology. = 科学家们通过先进的技术提高了他们对宇宙的认知。


过去完成时:
By the end of the book, my perception of the main character had completely shifted. = 读到书的结尾，我对主人公的看法已经完全改变了。
Before the experiment, their perception of the phenomenon was limited. = 实验之前，他们对这种现象的认知是有限的。


将来时:
My perception of the situation will likely change once I have all the facts. = 一旦我掌握了所有事实，我对局势的看法可能会改变。
With further research, our perception of climate change will become clearer. = 通过进一步的研究，我们对气候变化的认知会变得更加清晰。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better perception of the situation, I would have acted differently. = 如果我对情况有更好的理解，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had a clearer perception of what is really happening. = 我希望我能更清楚地理解正在发生的事情。


被动语态:
The painting's perception is influenced by the viewer's background. = 画作的感知会受到观看者背景的影响。
The company's public perception is being carefully managed. = 公司的公众形象正在被仔细地管理。


疑问句:
What is your perception of the current economic climate? = 你对当前经济形势的看法是什么？
How did his perception of the world change over time? = 他的世界观是如何随着时间推移而改变的？


否定句:
His perception of the situation was not accurate. = 他对情况的感知并不准确。
Their perception of the problem did not match reality. = 他们对问题的认知与现实不符。
