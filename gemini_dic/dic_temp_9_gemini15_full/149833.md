paleornithology:/ˌpeɪliːɔːrnɪˈθɒlədʒi/ | n. |古鸟类学|The scientific study of prehistoric birds.  It involves the study of fossil birds, their evolution, and their relationships to modern birds and other extinct animals. |例句：Paleornithology has revealed much about the evolutionary history of birds. 古鸟类学揭示了鸟类进化史的许多信息。|近义词：paleozoology |反义词：暂无
