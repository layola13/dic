gyve:/dʒaɪv/| n. vt.|脚镣；束缚；限制 | A gyve is a shackle or fetter for the legs or ankles.  It can also refer to something that restricts or confines. |例句：The prisoner's gyves were heavy and uncomfortable.  囚犯的脚镣又沉又不舒服。
The rules felt like gyves on her creativity.  这些规则让她感觉创作受到了束缚。|近义词：fetter, shackle, restraint, constraint |反义词：freedom, liberty, release
