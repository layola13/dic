impuissance:/ɪmˈpjuɪsns/| n.|无能；无力；无效|Lack of power or effectiveness.  A feeling of helplessness.|例句：His feelings of impuissance stemmed from his inability to influence events. 他的无力感源于他无法影响事件的发生。

例句：The impuissance of the international community in the face of the crisis was shocking. 国际社会面对危机时的无力令人震惊。


近义词：powerlessness, weakness, ineffectiveness, futility, helplessness

反义词：power, strength, effectiveness, potency


词源:  From Old French *impuissance, from Latin *impotentia, from in- "not" + potentia "power".
