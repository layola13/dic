rogue:/roʊɡ/|ˈrəʊɡ|/| n., adj., v.|流氓；无赖；恶棍；不守规矩的人；淘气的；难以驾驭的；(尤指)不寻常的；离群的；偷偷摸摸地做；流浪；擅自行动；|英文解析：A rogue is a dishonest or unprincipled person.  As an adjective, it describes something that is mischievous, unpredictable, or unusual. As a verb, it means to act in a mischievous or independent way.|例句：He was a charming rogue who always managed to get his way. 他是个魅力十足的无赖，总是能心想事成。|近义词：villain, scoundrel, rascal, miscreant, mischievous, wayward, unruly|反义词：saint, angel, honest, principled, virtuous, obedient, well-behaved|词性：n., adj., v.
