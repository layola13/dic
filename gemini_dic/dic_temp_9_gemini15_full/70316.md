fief:/fiːf/| n.|采邑，封地|A fief was a piece of land granted by a lord to a vassal in return for military service or other obligations.  The system of fiefs was a key element of feudalism.|Example: The knight received a fief from the king in exchange for his loyalty and military service.  骑士从国王那里得到一块封地以换取他的忠诚和服役。|近义词：estate, manor, holding|反义词：none.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The king grants many fiefs to his loyal subjects. = 国王授予许多封地给他的忠诚臣民。
He holds a large fief in the south. = 他在南方拥有一大片封地。


一般过去时:
The lord gave his son a fief. = 领主给了他儿子一块封地。
My ancestor once owned this fief. = 我的祖先曾经拥有这块封地。


现在完成时:
The family has held this fief for centuries. = 这个家族世世代代拥有这块封地。
He has inherited a large fief from his father. = 他继承了他父亲的一大片封地。


过去完成时:
The vassal had lost his fief before the war. = 战争爆发前，这位封臣已经失去了他的封地。
They had held the fief for generations before it was seized. = 在被没收之前，他们世世代代拥有这块封地。


将来时:
The prince will inherit his father's fief. = 王子将继承他父亲的封地。
The family will likely lose the fief. = 这个家族很可能会失去这块封地。


虚拟语气:
If I had a fief, I would build a castle. = 如果我有一块封地，我会建造一座城堡。
I wish I owned a fief in the countryside. = 我希望我在乡下拥有一块封地。


被动语态:
The fief was granted to him by the duke. = 这块封地是由公爵授予他的。
The fief is being contested by two rival families. = 这块封地正被两个敌对家族争夺。


疑问句:
Whose fief is this? = 这是谁的封地？
What are the obligations associated with this fief? = 与这块封地相关的义务是什么？


否定句:
He did not inherit a fief from his father. = 他没有从他父亲那里继承封地。
She did not own a single fief. = 她没有拥有任何一块封地。
