formulary:/ˈfɔːrmjʊləri/ | n. |处方集；药典；配方书 | A formulary is an official list of approved drugs that may be prescribed by doctors.  It often specifies the approved dosage and administration of each medication.  The hospital's formulary restricts the use of certain expensive medications. |近义词：pharmacopoeia, compendium |反义词：N/A
