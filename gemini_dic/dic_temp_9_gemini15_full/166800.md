prespecify:/priːˈspesɪfaɪ/| vt. |预先指定；预先规定|To specify (something) in advance; to determine or stipulate beforehand.|The project's requirements were prespecified in the initial proposal.  项目的需求在最初的提案中已预先规定。|近义词：predetermine, predetermine, specify in advance|反义词：leave unspecified, improvise


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They prespecify the parameters before running the simulation. = 他们在运行模拟之前预先指定参数。
The company prespecifies the quality standards for all its products. = 公司预先指定其所有产品的质量标准。


一般过去时:
The researchers prespecified their hypotheses before beginning the experiment. = 研究人员在开始实验之前预先指定了他们的假设。
The engineer prespecified the dimensions of the bridge components. = 工程师预先规定了桥梁部件的尺寸。


现在完成时:
We have prespecified the data input for the analysis. = 我们已经为分析预先指定了数据输入。
They have prespecified the criteria for evaluating the candidates. = 他们已经预先指定了评估候选人的标准。


过去完成时:
The team had prespecified the meeting agenda a week in advance. = 团队在一周前就预先指定了会议议程。
She had prespecified the colors for the design project. = 她已经为设计项目预先指定了颜色。


将来时:
We will prespecify the test conditions before commencing the trial. = 我们将在试验开始前预先指定测试条件。
They will prespecify the budget allocation for each department. = 他们将预先指定每个部门的预算分配。


虚拟语气:
If we prespecified the variables, the results would be more reliable. = 如果我们预先指定变量，结果会更可靠。
It would be helpful if they prespecified the timeline for the project. = 如果他们预先指定项目的进度表，将会有所帮助。


被动语态:
The deadlines were prespecified by the management team. = 截止日期由管理团队预先指定。
The materials needed were prespecified in the construction plan. = 所需材料在施工计划中已预先指定。


疑问句:
Were the experimental conditions prespecified? = 实验条件是否已预先指定？
Have the requirements for the software been prespecified? = 软件的要求是否已预先指定？


否定句:
They did not prespecify the sampling method. = 他们没有预先指定抽样方法。
The company did not prespecify the delivery date. = 公司没有预先指定交货日期。
