savin:/ˈseɪvɪn/| n. | 节省；储蓄；救助 | Saving money is important for the future.  储蓄金钱对未来很重要。|近义词：economy, thrift, preservation |反义词：waste, extravagance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He saves money every month. = 他每个月都存钱。
We are saving for a new car. = 我们正在攒钱买新车。


一般过去时:
She saved a lot of money last year. = 她去年存了很多钱。
They saved the drowning child. = 他们救了那个溺水的小孩。


现在完成时:
I have saved enough money for a vacation. = 我已经攒够了度假所需的钱。
They have saved many lives through their charity work. = 通过慈善工作，他们已经挽救了许多生命。


过去完成时:
He had saved a considerable amount before he retired. = 他退休前已经存了一大笔钱。
She had saved the document before the computer crashed. = 电脑崩溃之前她已经保存了文档。


将来时:
We will save more money next year. = 我们明年将存更多的钱。
They will save the company from bankruptcy. = 他们将拯救公司免于破产。


虚拟语气:
If I had saved more money, I could have bought a house. = 如果我存更多钱，我就能买房子了。
If they had saved the evidence, they would have won the case. = 如果他们保存了证据，他们就会赢得官司。


被动语态:
Much money was saved by reducing expenses. = 通过减少开支节省了很多钱。
The company was saved from collapse by the government bailout. = 这家公司因政府的经济援助而免于倒闭。


疑问句:
How much money did you save this month? = 这个月你存了多少钱？
Were the documents saved before the power outage? = 停电前文档保存了吗？


否定句:
He didn't save any money this month. = 这个月他没存任何钱。
They couldn't save the old building from demolition. = 他们没能阻止旧建筑被拆除。



PHRASE->
savings account = 储蓄账户
cost savings = 成本节约
energy saving = 节能
data saving = 数据保存
money saving = 省钱
