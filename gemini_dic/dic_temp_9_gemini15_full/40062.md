contacted:/ˈkɑːntæktɪd/ | v. | 联系，接触 | To communicate with someone, typically by phone or email.  | 例句：I contacted the company about the problem. 我就这个问题联系了公司。

例句：She contacted her doctor to schedule an appointment. 她联系了她的医生预约了就诊。


近义词：reached, contacted, got in touch with | 反义词：ignored, avoided


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I contact my friends regularly. = 我定期联系我的朋友们。
He contacts his clients every week. = 他每周都联系他的客户。

一般过去时:
I contacted the hotel yesterday. = 我昨天联系了酒店。
They contacted the police after the incident. = 事件发生后，他们联系了警方。

现在完成时:
I have contacted them several times. = 我已经联系他们好几次了。
She has contacted the bank about the issue. = 她已经就这个问题联系了银行。

过去完成时:
I had contacted the supplier before the meeting. = 我在会议之前已经联系了供应商。
They had contacted the authorities prior to the event. = 他们在活动之前已经联系了有关部门。

将来时:
I will contact you tomorrow. = 我明天会联系你。
He will contact the office when he arrives. = 他到达后会联系办公室。

虚拟语气:
If I had contacted him earlier, I would have received a response. = 如果我早点联系他，我就收到回复了。
I wish I had contacted the lawyer sooner. = 我真希望我早点联系律师。

被动语态:
The client was contacted by phone. = 客户通过电话被联系到了。
The problem was contacted to the department in charge. = 问题被联系到了主管部门。

疑问句:
Have you contacted the landlord yet? = 你联系房东了吗？
Did he contact you back? = 他回你电话了吗？

否定句:
I haven't contacted him yet. = 我还没联系他。
They didn't contact the police. = 他们没有联系警方。


PHRASE->
contacted by phone = 通过电话联系
contacted via email = 通过邮件联系
contacted in person = 当面联系
contacted the authorities = 联系了当局
contacted for a job interview = 被联系进行工作面试
