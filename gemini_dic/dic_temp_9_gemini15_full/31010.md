challan:/tʃælən/| n.|罚款单；通知单；清单|A challan is an official document, typically issued by authorities, serving as a notice, receipt, or citation, often related to a fine or payment.  It can also refer to a list or inventory.|例句：The police officer issued me a challan for speeding. = 警官因超速给我开了一张罚款单。

例句：The shopkeeper gave me a challan for the goods I purchased. = 店主给了我一张我购买商品的清单。


近义词：citation, ticket, invoice, receipt, bill|反义词：N/A|词性:名词

词源:
The word "challan" is derived from the Hindi word "चालान" (chālan), meaning "receipt," "bill," or "voucher."  It entered English primarily through the administrative practices of British India, where it was used in similar contexts as it is today.
