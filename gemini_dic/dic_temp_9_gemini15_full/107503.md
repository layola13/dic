lancinate:/ˈlænsɪneɪt/| vt.|锐痛；剧痛；折磨；使痛苦|To cause a sharp, intense pain; to wound deeply, both physically and emotionally.  The word often implies a piercing or stabbing sensation.|例句：The sharp pain lancinated her shoulder.  剧烈的疼痛刺痛了她的肩膀。

例句：His betrayal lancinated her heart. 他的背叛深深刺痛了她的心。

近义词：agonize, torment, pierce, wound

反义词：soothe, comfort, relieve

词性:动词
