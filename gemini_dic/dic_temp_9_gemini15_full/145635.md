overconsumes:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsuːmz/| vt.|过度消费；消耗过多|To consume something in excess; to use or spend more of something than is necessary or reasonable.|例句：The factory overconsumes energy and needs to improve its efficiency. 工厂过度消耗能源，需要提高效率。例句：He overconsumes alcohol and needs to seek help. 他酗酒，需要寻求帮助。|近义词：exhaust, deplete, squander|反义词：conserve, save, economize|动词
