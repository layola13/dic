predominancy:/prɪˈdɒmɪnənsi/| /prɪˈdɑːmɪnənsi/| n. |优势；支配；卓越；盛行|The state or fact of being predominant; the quality or condition of being more powerful, numerous, or important than others.|The predominance of one political party made it difficult to pass any legislation.|近义词：prevalence, preponderance, ascendancy|反义词：subordination, inferiority, minority


例句:

The predominance of blue in the painting is striking.
绘画中蓝色的主导地位非常醒目。

The company's predominance in the market is undeniable.
该公司在市场上的主导地位是不可否认的。
