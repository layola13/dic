nonliterally:/ˌnɑːnˈlɪtərəli/| adv.|非字面地，并非按字面意思|Not literally; in a figurative or metaphorical sense.  It emphasizes that something is not to be taken at face value, but rather interpreted symbolically or indirectly.|例句：He said he was "dying" of boredom, but he didn't mean it nonliterally. 他说他“快无聊死了”，但他并非字面意思。|近义词：figuratively, metaphorically, symbolically|反义词：literally
