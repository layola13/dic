dry-cleaned:/ˌdraɪˈkliːnd/ | adj. | 干洗的；经过干洗的 |  Describes clothing or fabrics that have been cleaned using a dry cleaning process, rather than being washed with water.  | Example: My dry-cleaned suit looks brand new. 我的干洗西装看起来崭新如初。  | 近义词：professionally cleaned, chemically cleaned | 反义词： laundered, hand-washed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dress is dry-cleaned every month. = 这件衣服每个月都干洗。
My shirts are dry-cleaned weekly. = 我的衬衫每周都干洗。


一般过去时:
I dry-cleaned my coat last week. = 我上周干洗了我的外套。
She had her wedding dress dry-cleaned before the ceremony. = 她婚前把婚纱干洗了。


现在完成时:
I have dry-cleaned all my winter clothes. = 我已经把所有冬衣都干洗了。
She has never had her expensive silk scarf dry-cleaned. = 她从没把昂贵的丝巾拿去干洗过。


过去完成时:
I had already dry-cleaned my suit before the important meeting. = 在重要会议之前我已经把西装干洗了。
They had dry-cleaned the curtains before the guests arrived. = 客人到来之前，他们已经把窗帘干洗了。


将来时:
I will dry-clean my dress for the party. = 我会把我的裙子干洗一下去参加派对。
We will have our carpets dry-cleaned next week. = 我们下周会把地毯拿去干洗。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would have my suit dry-cleaned. = 如果我有更多钱，我会把西装拿去干洗。
If I were going to a wedding, I would get my dress dry-cleaned. = 如果我要去参加婚礼，我会把裙子拿去干洗。


被动语态:
The clothes are being dry-cleaned now. = 衣服正在被干洗。
The suit was dry-cleaned yesterday. = 西装昨天被干洗了。


疑问句:
Is your coat dry-cleaned regularly? = 你的外套定期干洗吗？
Have the clothes been dry-cleaned yet? = 衣服干洗好了吗？


否定句:
I didn't dry-clean my shirt. = 我没有干洗我的衬衫。
