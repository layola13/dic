over-speculate:/ˌoʊvərˈspɛkjuleɪt/| v. |过度推测，过分猜测 |To speculate excessively or beyond what is reasonable or justified.  It often implies making assumptions based on insufficient evidence, leading to inaccurate conclusions. |例句：Investors should avoid over-speculating on volatile stocks.  投资者应该避免对波动性股票进行过度推测。

例句：He over-speculated on the market's future and lost a significant amount of money. 他对市场的未来进行了过度推测，损失了一大笔钱。


近义词：overestimate, overthink, conjecture wildly, guess wildly

反义词：underestimate, be certain, be sure


词性：动词
