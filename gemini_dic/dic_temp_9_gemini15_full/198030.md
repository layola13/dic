shoddily:/ˈʃɒdɪli/ | adj. |劣质地；粗制滥造地；马虎地 | Made or done in a careless or slipshod way.  The work was done shoddily and had to be redone. |例句：The house was shoddily constructed, with poor materials and workmanship. 这房子建造粗制滥造，材料和工艺都很差。

The furniture was shoddily made and fell apart after only a few months. 这家具做工粗糙，几个月后就散架了。


近义词：poorly, badly, carelessly, haphazardly, slipshodly


反义词：carefully, meticulously, expertly, skillfully, thoroughly
