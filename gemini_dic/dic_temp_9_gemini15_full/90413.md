hoy:/hɔɪ/| int.|嘿，喂|A casual greeting, similar to "hi" or "hey".  It is used informally to get someone's attention or to begin a conversation.| Example: Hoy!  Over here!  嘿！这边！|近义词：hi, hey, |反义词:无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Hoy, are you busy? = 喂，你忙吗？
Hoy, what's the time? = 喂，几点了？


一般过去时:
Hoy, I saw you yesterday! = 喂，我昨天看到你了！
Hoy, did you hear that? = 喂，你听到那个了吗？


现在完成时:
Hoy, I've finished my work! = 喂，我完成了我的工作！
Hoy, have you seen John? = 喂，你看到约翰了吗？


过去完成时:
Hoy, I had already eaten when you called. = 喂，你打电话的时候我已经吃过了。
Hoy, they had left before we arrived. = 喂，在我们到达之前他们已经离开了。


将来时:
Hoy, I'll call you later. = 喂，我待会儿给你打电话。
Hoy, we'll meet tomorrow. = 喂，我们明天见。


虚拟语气:
Hoy, if I had known, I wouldn't have gone. = 喂，如果我知道的话，我就不会去了。
Hoy, I wish I had more time. = 喂，我希望我有更多的时间。


被动语态:
Hoy, the decision was made yesterday. = 喂，决定是昨天做出的。
Hoy, the car is being repaired. = 喂，车正在修理中。


疑问句:
Hoy, what's your name? = 喂，你叫什么名字？
Hoy, where are you going? = 喂，你去哪儿？


否定句:
Hoy, I don't know the answer. = 喂，我不知道答案。
Hoy, she didn't come. = 喂，她没来。


PHRASE->
Hoy there! = 喂！
Hoy, mate! = 喂，老兄！ (British English)
Hoy, you! = 喂，你！
Hoy-hoy! = (Informal, playful greeting, often expressing excitement)
