repaying:/ˌriːˈpeɪɪŋ/| v.|偿还，报答|The act of paying back money or returning a favour.  It implies a previous debt or obligation.|例句：He is repaying his student loan. 他正在偿还他的学生贷款。
例句：She's repaying her friend's kindness by helping her move. 她正在通过帮助朋友搬家来报答朋友的善意。|近义词：refunding, compensating, reciprocating|反义词：owing, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am repaying my debt. = 我正在偿还我的债务。
They are repaying their loan monthly. = 他们每月偿还贷款。


一般过去时:
I repaid my debt last month. = 我上个月还清了债务。
She repaid the favour by baking a cake. = 她通过烤蛋糕来回报这份恩情。


现在完成时:
I have repaid all my debts. = 我已经还清了所有债务。
They have been repaying their mortgage for five years. = 他们已经还了五年房贷了。


过去完成时:
I had repaid the money before he asked. = 他问我之前我已经还钱了。
She had repaid the kindness by helping him with his project. = 她通过帮助他完成项目来回报他的好意。


将来时:
I will repay you next week. = 我下周会还你钱。
They will repay the loan in full by the end of the year. = 他们将在年底前全额偿还贷款。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would repay you immediately. = 如果我有更多钱，我会立即还你。
I wish I could repay your generosity. = 我希望我能报答你的慷慨。


被动语态:
The debt will be repaid soon. = 这笔债务很快就会还清。
The favour was repaid with gratitude. = 这份恩情得到了感激的回报。


疑问句:
Are you repaying your loan on time? = 你按时偿还贷款吗？
How are you going to repay this debt? = 你打算如何偿还这笔债务？


否定句:
I haven't repaid the money yet. = 我还没还钱。
He didn't repay the loan as agreed. = 他没有按照约定偿还贷款。


PHRASE->
repaying a debt = 还债
repaying a loan = 还贷款
repaying a favour = 报答恩情
repaying kindness = 报答善意
repaying trust = 报答信任
