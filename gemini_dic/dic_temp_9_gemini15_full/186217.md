rhime:/raɪm/| n.|押韵，谐音|A rhyme is a repetition of similar sounds in two or more words, most often at the end of lines in poems or songs.|The poem ended with a perfect rhyme.|近义词：rhythm, verse|反义词: prose


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The words "cat" and "hat" rhyme. = “cat”和“hat”押韵。
This song has a simple, catchy rhyme scheme. = 这首歌有一个简单上口的押韵方案。


一般过去时:
The poet used internal rhyme effectively. = 诗人有效地使用了内部押韵。
Shakespeare's sonnets often employed perfect rhyme. = 莎士比亚十四行诗经常使用完美的押韵。


现在完成时:
She has written many poems with perfect rhymes. = 她已经写了很多押韵完美的诗歌。
The children have learned to recognize rhyming words. = 孩子们已经学会了识别押韵的单词。


过去完成时:
He had already composed a rhyme before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前就已经创作好一首押韵的诗歌了。
They had never noticed the subtle rhyme in the lyrics. = 他们从未注意到歌词中微妙的押韵。


将来时:
She will write a poem that rhymes with the previous one. = 她将写一首与上一首押韵的诗歌。
The students will learn about different types of rhyme. = 学生们将学习不同类型的押韵。


虚拟语气:
If it rhymed better, the poem would be more successful. = 如果押韵更好，这首诗就会更成功。
I wish the ending lines rhymed perfectly. = 我希望结尾的诗句完美押韵。


被动语态:
The poem was written with a specific rhyme scheme in mind. = 这首诗是按照特定的押韵方案写的。
The rhyme is often considered essential in poetry. = 押韵通常被认为是诗歌中必不可少的。


疑问句:
Does this word rhyme with "moon"? = 这个词与“月亮”押韵吗？
How many rhymes are there in this stanza? = 这个节中有多少个押韵？


否定句:
The words "dog" and "fog" don't rhyme perfectly. = “狗”和“雾”的押韵并不完美。
That line doesn't rhyme with anything else in the poem. = 那行诗与诗歌中的任何其他部分都不押韵。
