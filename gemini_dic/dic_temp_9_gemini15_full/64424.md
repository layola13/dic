ergot:/ˈɜːrɡɒt/| n.|麦角菌；麦角病；麦角碱|A fungus that grows on rye and other grains, causing a disease called ergotism.  Ergot also refers to the sclerotium (hardened mass of fungal mycelium) of the fungus and the alkaloids it produces, which can have potent pharmacological effects.|The ergot on the rye caused a significant reduction in the harvest. 麦角感染黑麦导致收成大幅减少。|The ergot alkaloids have been used in medicine for centuries. 麦角碱已被用于医药数百年。|fungus, blight|healthy grain|名词
