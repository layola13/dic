relacquer:/ˌriːˈlækər/| vt.|重新涂漆，再上漆|To apply a new coat of lacquer to (something).  This usually implies a restoration or repair process, rather than a simple new coat of paint.|例句：The antique table was expertly relacquered, restoring its original shine.  这古董桌子经过熟练的重新涂漆，恢复了原来的光泽。

例句：We need to relacquer the front door to protect it from the elements. 我们需要重新给前门上漆以保护它免受风雨侵蚀。


近义词：revarnish, refinish, repaint|反义词：remove lacquer, strip paint|词性：动词
