incalculability: /ˌɪnˌkælkjʊləˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可预测性；难以计算性 | The inability to be calculated or predicted;  the quality of being unpredictable.  It often refers to something so complex or chaotic that it defies precise measurement or forecasting. | The incalculability of the stock market makes long-term predictions unreliable.  股票市场的不可预测性使得长期预测不可靠。  The incalculability of human behavior often leads to unexpected outcomes. 人类行为的不可预测性常常导致意想不到的结果。 | unpredictability, uncertainty, randomness | calculability, predictability | 名词
