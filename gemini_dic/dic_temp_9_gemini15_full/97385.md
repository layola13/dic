infrared: /ˌɪnfrəˈred/ | adj. | 红外线的；红外线的 |  Relating to or denoting radiation whose wavelength is longer than that of visible light but shorter than that of microwaves. Infrared radiation is invisible to the human eye but can be detected as heat. | 例句：Infrared cameras are used to detect heat signatures. 红外线摄像机用于检测热信号。

例句：The remote control uses infrared light to communicate with the television. 遥控器使用红外线与电视机通信。


近义词：thermal (in some contexts)


反义词：ultraviolet


词性：形容词
