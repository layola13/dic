potus:/ˈpoʊtəs/| n.|美国总统|President of the United States;  an abbreviation of "President of the United States". It's used informally in speech and writing to refer to the current officeholder.|Example:  The POTUS addressed the nation last night. 美国总统昨晚向全国发表讲话。|近义词：President, Commander-in-Chief|反义词：无
