half-linen:/ˈhæfˌlɪnən/| adj.|半亚麻的；亚麻混纺的|A fabric blend of linen and another fiber, typically cotton.  It combines the desirable properties of linen, such as breathability and durability, with the softness and affordability of cotton.|例句：The shirt is made of a comfortable half-linen fabric. 这件衬衫是用舒适的半亚麻布料制成的。The half-linen tablecloth felt cool and crisp. 这张半亚麻桌布摸起来凉爽而挺括。|近义词：linen blend, cotton-linen mix|反义词：pure linen, pure cotton|
