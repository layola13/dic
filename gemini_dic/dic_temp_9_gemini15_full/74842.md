frequence:/ˈfriːkwəns/| n.|频率，频繁|The frequency of the sound waves determines the pitch.  声音波的频率决定音高。|近义词：recurrence, rate|反义词：rarity, infrequency|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The frequency of bus service is high. = 公交车的发车频率很高。
The frequency of his visits is decreasing. = 他来访的频率正在下降。


一般过去时:
The frequency of earthquakes was higher last year. = 去年地震的频率更高。
The frequency of his calls decreased last month. = 上个月他来电的频率降低了。

过去完成时:
