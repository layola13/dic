disobedient: /ˌdɪsəˈbiːdiənt/ | adj. | 不服从的；顽皮的 | Not obeying or showing a lack of obedience.  |例句：The disobedient child refused to eat his vegetables.  顽皮的孩子拒绝吃蔬菜。|例句：Her disobedient dog ran into the street. 她那不听话的狗跑到了街上。|近义词：rebellious, defiant, unruly, insubordinate |反义词：obedient, compliant, docile


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is disobedient to his parents. 他不听父母的话。
The dog is disobedient and will not sit. 这只狗不听话，不肯坐下。


一般过去时:
The child was disobedient yesterday.  昨天这个孩子很顽皮。
She was disobedient and ignored the teacher's instructions. 她不听话，无视老师的指示。


现在完成时:
He has been disobedient all his life. 他一辈子都很不听话。
The students have been disobedient repeatedly, causing disruption in class. 学生们屡次违反纪律，扰乱课堂秩序。


过去完成时:
The dog had been disobedient for weeks before they decided to give it away.  在那只狗被送走之前，它已经不听话好几个星期了。
They realized they had been disobedient to their parents' wishes. 他们意识到他们一直没有遵守父母的意愿。


将来时:
I think he will be disobedient again. 我认为他还会再次不听话。
If you continue to be disobedient, you will face consequences. 如果你继续不听话，你将面临后果。


虚拟语气:
If he were more obedient, life would be easier. 如果他更听话一些，生活就会轻松些。
I wish my children were not so disobedient. 我希望我的孩子们不要这么不听话。


被动语态:
His disobedient behavior was not tolerated. 他的不听话行为不被容忍。
The disobedient act was severely punished.  不听话的行为受到了严厉的惩罚。


疑问句:
Is he disobedient? 他不听话吗？

否定句:
He is not disobedient. 他不顽皮。
The children were not disobedient today. 孩子们今天很听话。
