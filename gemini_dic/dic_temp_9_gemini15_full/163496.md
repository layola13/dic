preconveyed:/ˌpriːkənˈveɪd/ | adj. | 预先传达的；预先告知的 |  Already communicated or imparted beforehand.  It implies that information or a message was given in advance. |  The preconveyed instructions ensured a smooth operation. 预先传达的指示确保了操作顺利进行。 The team was aware of the preconveyed risks.  团队已经了解了预先告知的风险。| pre-informed, pre-announced | unannounced, unanticipated |形容词


USAGE->

The manager preconveys the meeting agenda. = 经理预先传达会议议程。
They preconvey important information to the team. = 他们预先向团队传达重要信息。


一般过去时:
