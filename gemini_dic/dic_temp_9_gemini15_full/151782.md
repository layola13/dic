pass for:/pæs fɔːr/| v.|冒充；被认为是；通过|To be accepted or regarded as something or someone.  It often implies a deception or a mistaken identity.|例句：He tried to pass for a doctor, but his lack of knowledge gave him away. 他试图冒充医生，但他缺乏知识暴露了他。

例句：She passes for a native speaker, even though she's only been studying English for two years.  尽管她只学习英语两年，但她听起来像母语人士。


近义词： impersonate, feign, masquerade as | 反义词：be exposed as, be revealed as


词性:动词
