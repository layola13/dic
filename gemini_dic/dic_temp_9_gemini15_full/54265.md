disgradation:/ˌdɪsɡreɪˈdeɪʃən/ | n. | 贬低；降级；堕落；衰败 | The process of being degraded or losing status, quality, or value.  It often implies a decline from a previous higher state. |例句：The disgradation of the environment is a serious concern. 环境的恶化令人担忧。

例句：His disgradation from captain to private was a harsh punishment. 他从上尉降为列兵的处罚很严厉。


近义词：degradation, debasement, deterioration, decline, downfall | 反义词：elevation, improvement, enhancement, advancement
