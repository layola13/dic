reentrenching: /ˌriːɪnˈtrentʃɪŋ/ | v. | 重新筑防御工事；巩固；退守；节俭；紧缩 |  Strengthening a position of power or security; consolidating one's position; reducing expenditure; withdrawing to a more defensible position. |

例句：

The company is reentrenching its position in the market by launching a new advertising campaign. = 公司正在通过推出新的广告宣传活动来巩固其在市场上的地位。

The army is reentrenching its defenses in anticipation of an attack. = 军队正在加固防御工事，以防遭到攻击。


近义词：consolidate, fortify, strengthen, retrench, tighten belts

反义词：expand, weaken, compromise, spend freely
