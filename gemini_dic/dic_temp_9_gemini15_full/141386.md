operculum:/ɒˈpɜːrkjʊləm/| n. |鳃盖；盖；帽状物|A bony, cartilaginous, or chitinous plate that covers or protects a structure, such as the gills of a fish or the opening of a snail shell.  It often acts as a lid or cover.|例句：The fish used its operculum to cover its gills. 这条鱼用鳃盖盖住它的鳃。|例句：The snail retreated into its shell, closing the operculum behind it. 蜗牛缩进壳里，在后面关上了盖子。|近义词：lid, cover, cap|反义词：opening, aperture
