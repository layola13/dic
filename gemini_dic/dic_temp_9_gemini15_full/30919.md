chaining:/ˈtʃeɪnɪŋ/ | n. v. | 链式反应；连接；束缚；|The act of linking things together in a chain or series.  It can refer to a physical process (like chaining bicycles together) or a more abstract process (like chaining ideas together in an argument).|例句：The prisoners were chained together. 囚犯们被锁在一起。

The events chained together in an unexpected way. 事件以出人意料的方式环环相扣。|近义词：linking, connecting, binding, stringing |反义词：separating, disconnecting, unlinking |名词，动词
