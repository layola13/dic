self-liking:/selfˈlaɪkɪŋ/| n.|自恋，自我欣赏|Self-liking refers to a feeling of excessive pride or satisfaction in oneself and one's abilities.  It can be a positive trait in moderation, fostering self-confidence, but in excess, it can become narcissistic and detrimental to relationships.|Example: Her self-liking was evident in her constant boasting about her achievements.  她的自恋在她不断吹嘘自己的成就中显而易见。|近义词：self-love (when positive), narcissism, conceit, vanity |反义词：self-deprecation, humility, modesty|名词
