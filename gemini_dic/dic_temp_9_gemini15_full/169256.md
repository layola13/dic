promenades:/ˈprɒməneɪdz/|/ˈprɑːməneɪdz/| n. |散步；漫步；（尤指）海滨大道 |A leisurely walk, especially one taken for pleasure in a public place.  |例句：We took a leisurely promenade along the beach. 我们沿着海滩悠闲地散步。

例句：The city's promenade offers stunning views of the ocean. 这个城市的滨海大道可以欣赏到令人惊叹的海景。


近义词：stroll, walk, stroll, ramble |反义词：rush, sprint, dash |词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They promenade along the beach every evening. = 他们每天晚上都在海滩上散步。
We promenade in the park after dinner. = 我们晚餐后在公园里散步。


一般过去时:
She promenaded through the gardens yesterday. = 她昨天在花园里散步。
They promenaded hand-in-hand along the river. = 他们手牵着手沿着河边散步。


现在完成时:
We have promenaded along this path many times. = 我们沿着这条路散步过很多次了。
They have promenaded through the city's historical district. = 他们已经漫步在这个城市的古城区了。


过去完成时:
They had promenaded for hours before they found a place to rest. = 他们在找到休息的地方之前已经散步了好几个小时了。
She had promenaded through the town square before realizing she was lost. = 她漫步穿过市中心广场后才意识到自己迷路了。


将来时:
We will promenade along the seaside tomorrow. = 我们明天将在海边散步。
They will promenade through the park during their visit. = 他们访问期间会在公园里散步。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would promenade along the river. = 如果我有更多时间，我会沿着河边散步。
I wish I could promenade through the fields of wildflowers. = 我希望我能漫步在野花田里。


被动语态:
The promenade was enjoyed by many tourists. = 许多游客都享受了这次散步。
The new promenade is being built near the harbor. = 新的海滨大道正在港口附近建造。


疑问句:
Do you like to promenade in the evenings? = 你喜欢晚上散步吗？
Where do they usually promenade? = 他们通常在哪里散步？


否定句:
They did not promenade today because of the rain. = 因为下雨，他们今天没有散步。
We don't often promenade in the city center. = 我们不经常在市中心散步。


PHRASE->
evening promenade = 傍晚散步
coastal promenade = 海滨漫步
festive promenade = 节庆漫步
royal promenade = 皇家漫步(通常指一种隆重的仪式性散步)
leisurely promenade = 悠闲散步


词源:
From French *promenade*, from *promener* "to walk," from Old French *promener*, from Vulgar Latin **promittere*, from Latin *promittere* "to lead forth," from *pro-* "forth" + *mittere* "to send."  The noun sense developed from the verb.
