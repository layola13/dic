entrained:/ɪnˈtreɪnd/| v.| 卷入；夹带；诱导；使同步 | To cause (something) to be carried along by a current of air or water; to cause (someone or something) to become involved in or participate in something;  to bring into a particular state or condition. |例句：The river entrained sediment downstream.  河流挟带着泥沙向下游流动。  The children were entrained in the excitement of the game. 孩子们被比赛的兴奋所感染。|近义词：carry, involve, induce, synchronize |反义词：exclude, detach, separate
