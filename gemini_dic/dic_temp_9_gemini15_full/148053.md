overs:/ˈoʊvərz/| n.|超过，剩余部分；加时赛；(尤指)过量；过度；(板球)额外局|The word "overs" has its origins in the Middle English word "over," meaning "above" or "beyond."  In its noun form, it signifies an excess or surplus of something. In sports, particularly cricket, it refers to a set of six consecutive balls bowled by a single bowler.  The plural form "overs" is used to denote multiple such sets.|例句：The team played several overs in extra time.  球队在加时赛中打了几个局。

例句：There were several overs of rain.  下了好几场阵雨。|近义词：excess, surplus, extra, additional|反义词：deficit, shortage, lack
