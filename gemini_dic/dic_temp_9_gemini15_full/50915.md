deservingly:/dɪˈzɜːrvɪŋli/ | adv. | 应受地；理所当然地 | In a way that deserves something.  It implies that something is earned or deserved. | 例句：She was deservingly praised for her hard work. 她因辛勤工作而理所当然地受到赞扬。

例句：He deservingly won the award for his outstanding performance. 他因杰出的表现而理所当然地赢得了奖项。


近义词：rightfully, justly, properly, legitimately

反义词：unjustly, undeservedly, improperly


词性：副词
