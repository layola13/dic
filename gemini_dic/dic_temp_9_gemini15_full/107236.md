lairds:/ˈleɪərdz/| n.|苏格兰地主；庄园主|A laird is a Scottish landowner, especially one who holds land under feudal tenure.  The term often carries connotations of a traditional, often aristocratic, rural lifestyle.|The laird of the estate hosted a grand feast for the villagers.  这位庄园主为村民们举办了一场盛大的宴会。|landowner, proprietor, gentleman farmer|tenant, renter|名词
