liberating: /ˈlɪbəreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 解放的；使人自由的；令人耳目一新的 |  Giving freedom or liberation; freeing from confinement, oppression, or restraint.  It can also describe something that is refreshing or exhilarating. |

例句：

The new policy was liberating for small businesses.  = 新政策对小型企业来说是解放性的。

The feeling of flying is a liberating experience. = 飞翔的感觉是一种令人解放的体验。


近义词：emancipating, freeing, liberating, enfranchising

反义词：oppressive, restrictive, confining, enslaving


词性：形容词
