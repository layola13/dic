overaggravated: /ˌoʊvərˈæɡrəveɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度加重的；加剧的；恶化的 | Excessively aggravated; worsened beyond what is normal or expected.  This word is not a standard dictionary entry; it's a combination of "over" and "aggravated".  It describes a condition or situation that has been made significantly worse than it already was. |  The already tense situation was further overaggravated by the unexpected news.
