cellar:/ˈsɛlər/| n. |地下室，地窖|A cellar is an underground floor of a building, often used for storage.  It's typically cool and dark.|例句：We keep our wine in the cellar. 我们把酒放在地下室。|例句：The cellar was damp and musty. 地窖潮湿而霉味重。|近义词：basement, vault|反义词：attic, roof


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cellar stores many old bottles of wine. = 地窖储存着许多年份久的酒。
He checks the cellar for leaks every spring. = 他每年春天都检查地窖是否有渗漏。


一般过去时:
They found some old boxes in the cellar. = 他们在地下室里发现了一些旧箱子。
The family used the cellar to store vegetables during the winter. = 这个家庭冬天用地窖储存蔬菜。


现在完成时:
We have stored our winter supplies in the cellar. = 我们把冬天的物资储存在地窖里了。
They haven't used the cellar for years. = 他们多年没用过地窖了。


过去完成时:
The water had already flooded the cellar before the pump started working. = 抽水机开始工作之前，水已经淹没了地窖。
They had stored their furniture in the cellar during the renovation. = 他们在装修期间把家具储存在地窖里。


将来时:
We will clean out the cellar next weekend. = 我们下周末将清理地窖。
They will use the cellar to host a wine tasting. = 他们将利用地窖举办品酒会。


虚拟语气:
If we had a bigger cellar, we could store more wine. = 如果我们有一个更大的地窖，我们就可以储存更多的酒。
I wish the cellar wasn't so damp. = 我真希望地窖不要那么潮湿。


被动语态:
The cellar is cleaned twice a year. = 地窖一年打扫两次。
The old wine bottles were discovered in the cellar. = 这些旧酒瓶是在地窖里发现的。


疑问句:
Is there anything valuable in the cellar? = 地窖里有什么值钱的东西吗？
What's stored in your cellar? = 你的地窖里储存着什么？


否定句:
There isn't much space in the cellar. = 地窖空间不大。
He didn't find what he was looking for in the cellar. = 他在地窖里没有找到他想要的东西。
