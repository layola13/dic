marginalising: /ˈmɑːrɡɪnəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |边缘化；使...处于社会边缘 | To treat a person, group, or concept as insignificant or peripheral.  |  The government's policies are marginalising the poor and elderly. 政府的政策正在边缘化穷人和老年人。  The media's focus on celebrity culture marginalizes important social issues. 媒体对名流文化的关注使重要的社会问题边缘化。 | exclude, sideline, ostracize | integrate, include, empower | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They are marginalizing the minority group. = 他们正在边缘化少数民族群体。
The company marginalizes its employees' concerns. = 公司忽视了员工的担忧。


一般过去时:
The media marginalized the protests. = 媒体淡化了抗议活动。
Society marginalized those with disabilities. = 社会边缘化了残疾人。


现在完成时:
They have marginalized the voices of the working class. = 他们已经边缘化了工人阶级的呼声。
The government has marginalized the concerns of rural communities. = 政府已经忽视了农村社区的担忧。


过去完成时:
The policy had already marginalized the poor before the new law was implemented. = 在新法律实施之前，这项政策已经边缘化了穷人。
The company had marginalized its research and development department for years. = 多年来，公司一直忽视其研发部门。


将来时:
The new technology will marginalize traditional methods. = 新技术将使传统方法边缘化。
Increased automation will marginalize many low-skilled workers. = 自动化程度的提高将使许多低技能工人边缘化。


虚拟语气:
If we don't act now, we will marginalize an entire generation. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，我们将边缘化整整一代人。
I wish they hadn't marginalized his contributions to the project. = 我真希望他们没有忽视他对项目的贡献。


被动语态:
The concerns of the marginalized communities are often overlooked. = 边缘化社区的担忧往往被忽视。
The proposal was marginalized due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，该提案被搁置。


疑问句:
Are we marginalizing certain groups unintentionally? = 我们是否无意中边缘化了某些群体？
How can we prevent marginalizing the vulnerable populations? = 我们如何才能防止边缘化弱势群体？


否定句:
We should not marginalize anyone based on their background. = 我们不应该基于任何人的背景而边缘化他们。
The company does not marginalize its employees' opinions. = 公司不会忽视员工的意见。
