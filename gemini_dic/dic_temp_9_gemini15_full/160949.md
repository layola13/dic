post-restorations:/ˌpoʊstˌrɛstəˈreɪʃənz/| n. |修复后，修复工作完成之后|The period after the completion of restoration work;  the state of a thing after it has been restored.  It often refers to the condition, monitoring, or further actions taken after a restoration project.|例句：Post-restorations, the building's structural integrity was carefully monitored.  修复完成后，对建筑物的结构完整性进行了仔细监测。

The museum documented the artwork's condition in detail in their post-restoration report. 博物馆在其修复后报告中详细记录了艺术品的状况。|近义词：after-restoration, post-repair, following restoration|反义词：before restoration, pre-restoration
