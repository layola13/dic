midway:/ˈmɪdˌweɪ/| adv. n.|中途，半路|Halfway; in the middle of a journey or process.|The hikers reached a midway point on their journey.  徒步旅行者到达了旅程的中途点。|The midway was filled with exciting games and rides.  中途充满了令人兴奋的游戏和游乐设施。|halfway, halfway point|end, destination|副词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plane is midway through its flight. = 飞机正在飞行途中。
We are midway through the project. = 我们项目进行到一半了。


一般过去时:
They stopped midway up the mountain. = 他们在半山腰停了下来。
The meeting ended midway through the presentation. = 会议在演示中途结束了。


现在完成时:
We have reached the midway point in our fundraising campaign. = 我们已经达到了筹款活动的中途点。
They have already made it midway across the lake. = 他们已经划到湖中央了。


过去完成时:
They had already passed the midway point before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经过了中途点。
We had almost reached the midway point when we ran out of water. = 我们快到中途点时，水就用完了。


将来时:
We will stop for a rest midway through our hike. = 我们将在徒步旅行中途休息一下。
The race will be stopped midway if the weather gets worse. = 如果天气变坏，比赛将在中途停止。


虚拟语气:
If we had started earlier, we would have reached the midway point by now. = 如果我们早点出发，现在应该已经到达中途点了。
I wish we were midway through the project instead of just starting. = 我希望我们项目已经进行到一半而不是刚刚开始。


被动语态:
The race was stopped midway due to heavy rain. = 由于大雨，比赛中途停止了。
The project was halted midway due to funding issues. = 由于资金问题，项目中途停止了。


疑问句:
Are we midway there yet? = 我们到中途了吗？
Is the meeting midway through? = 会议进行到一半了吗？


否定句:
We didn't stop midway; we continued to the end. = 我们没有中途停下来；我们一直走到最后。
The journey was not midway complete; there was still a long way to go. = 旅程并没有完成一半；还有很长的路要走。
