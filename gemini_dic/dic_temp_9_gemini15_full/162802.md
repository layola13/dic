prebuilds:/ˈpriːbɪldz/| n. |预构建的软件包，预编译的构建|Prebuilds are software packages that are compiled and ready to use without further compilation. They often save time and resources by eliminating the need for users to build the software from source code.|Example:  The project provides prebuilds for Windows, macOS, and Linux.  该项目提供了适用于Windows、macOS和Linux的预构建包。|近义词：ready-mades, compiled packages |反义词：source code, uncompiled code|名词
