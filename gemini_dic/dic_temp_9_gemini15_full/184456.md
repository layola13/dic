rest:/rɛst/| n. v.|休息；剩余部分；静止；安息|The state of being inactive or still; freedom from activity or disturbance.|例句：I need a rest after working all day.  我工作了一整天，需要休息一下。|例句：The rest of the students arrived late. 其余的学生迟到了。|近义词：repose, sleep, remainder, residue|反义词：activity, work, motion


USAGE->
