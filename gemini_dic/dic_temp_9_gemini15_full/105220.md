keyseat:/ˈkiːsiːt/| n. |键槽|A keyseat is a slot or groove cut into a shaft or other machine part to receive a key.  A key is a mechanical device used to prevent relative rotation between two parts, such as a shaft and a hub.  The keyseat ensures that the key fits snugly and transmits torque effectively. | The machinist carefully cut the keyseat in the shaft. 机械师仔细地在轴上切出键槽。 The keyseat was machined to precise specifications. 键槽是按照精确的规格加工的。| keyway, key slot |  (反义词无直接对应，可考虑与其功能相反的部件描述)| 名词
