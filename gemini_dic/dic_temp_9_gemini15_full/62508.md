endowing:/ɪnˈdaʊɪŋ/| v.|赋予；捐赠；资助 |To provide or equip (someone or something) with a quality, ability, or asset.  |例句：The college is endowed with a large library. 这所大学拥有一个大型图书馆。(被动语态，表示大学被赋予了大型图书馆)

The scholarship endowed many students with the opportunity to pursue higher education. 这项奖学金为许多学生提供了深造的机会。(主动语态，表示奖学金赋予了学生机会)


近义词：bestowing, granting, providing, equipping


反义词：depriving, taking away, withholding


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The university endows scholarships annually. = 大学每年都提供奖学金。
She endows her paintings with a unique style. = 她赋予她的绘画以独特的风格。


一般过去时:
They endowed the hospital with a new wing. = 他们为医院捐赠了一座新楼。
The philanthropist endowed the charity with a substantial sum. = 慈善家向慈善机构捐赠了一大笔款项。


现在完成时:
The foundation has endowed numerous research projects. = 该基金会已经资助了无数的研究项目。
His generosity has endowed the community with many benefits. = 他的慷慨给社区带来了许多好处。


过去完成时:
Before the ceremony, they had endowed the museum with a priceless artifact. = 典礼之前，他们已将一件无价的文物捐赠给了博物馆。
The family had endowed the school with a new science lab. = 这个家庭已经为学校捐赠了一个新的科学实验室。


将来时:
The government will endow the project with significant funding. = 政府将为该项目提供大量资金。
She will endow her children with a good education. = 她将为她的孩子们提供良好的教育。


虚拟语气:
If they endowed the program with more resources, it would be more successful. = 如果他们为该项目提供更多资源，它将会更成功。
I wish they had endowed the library with more books. = 我希望他们已经为图书馆捐赠了更多书籍。


被动语态:
The school was endowed with a new computer lab. = 学校被捐赠了一个新的电脑实验室。
The project is being endowed with substantial funding. = 该项目正在获得大量资金。


疑问句:
Will the company endow the research team with the necessary equipment? = 公司会为研究团队提供必要的设备吗？
Does the trust endow scholarships for students? = 信托基金是否为学生提供奖学金？


否定句:
The government did not endow the project with sufficient funding. = 政府没有为该项目提供足够的资金。
They did not endow the charity with any money. = 他们没有向慈善机构捐款。


PHRASE->
endow with:  赋予……以……
endow a fund: 捐赠资金
endowment fund: 捐赠基金
endow a scholarship: 提供奖学金
endowment policy: 捐赠政策


词源:
源自古英语 *endōwen，意为“给予，授予”。  它与“gift”和“give”有词源联系。
