petrol:/ˈpɛtrəl/| n. |汽油，石油|A flammable liquid obtained from petroleum, used as fuel in internal combustion engines.|例句：The price of petrol has risen sharply recently.  最近汽油价格大幅上涨。|例句：My car needs petrol. 我的车需要加油。|近义词：gasoline, gas, fuel|反义词：无
