jitterbugged:/ˈdʒɪtərˌbʌɡd/| v.|跳吉特巴舞|To dance the jitterbug.  It refers to a fast, energetic swing dance popular in the 1930s and 40s, characterized by its improvisational nature and acrobatic moves.|例句：They jitterbugged the night away at the swing dance.  他们在摇摆舞会上跳了一整夜的吉特巴舞。|近义词：swing danced, Lindy hopped|反义词：slow danced, waltzed
