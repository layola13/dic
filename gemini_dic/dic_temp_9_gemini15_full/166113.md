prepped:/prɛpt/| adj. |准备好的；预先准备的|  Prepared beforehand; ready.  |例句：The chef prepped all the ingredients before starting to cook.  厨师在开始烹饪之前准备好了所有食材。

例句：The students were well prepped for the exam. 学生们为考试做好了充分的准备。


近义词：ready, prepared, arranged, organized |反义词：unprepared, disorganized, unprepared
