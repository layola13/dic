hydroponics:/ˌhaɪdrəˈpɒnɪks/ | n. | 水耕法，水培法 | The cultivation of plants in nutrient solutions, without soil.  Hydroponics allows for greater control over plant growth and can increase yields. | Example:  Many commercial growers are switching to hydroponics to improve crop production.  许多商业种植者正在转向水耕法以提高作物产量。 |近义词：soilless culture, aquaponics (if involving fish)|反义词：traditional farming, soil-based agriculture
