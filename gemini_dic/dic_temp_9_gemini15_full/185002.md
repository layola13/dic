retentive: /rɪˈtɛntɪv/ | adj. | 保持力强的；记忆力强的 | Having the ability to remember things easily; able to retain information. |


例句：

He has a remarkably retentive memory for faces.  他对于人脸有惊人的记忆力。

Her retentive mind easily grasped the complex concepts.  她记忆力强，很容易理解复杂的概念。


近义词：

memorious, mnemonic, retentive,  recollective


反义词：

forgetful, unmemorable


词性：形容词


词源:

Comes from the verb "retain," meaning "to keep or hold," ultimately derived from the Old French word "retenir," which combines "re-" (again) and "tenir" (to hold).  The "-ive" suffix transforms the verb into an adjective, describing something having the quality of retaining.
