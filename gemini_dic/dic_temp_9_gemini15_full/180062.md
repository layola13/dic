reflects:/rɪˈflɛkts/ | v. | 反映；反射；思考；表达 | To show an image of something; to show or be a sign of something; to think deeply or carefully. | 例句：The lake reflects the mountains.  湖面映照着群山。

The statistics reflect a worrying trend.  这些数据反映了一种令人担忧的趋势。


近义词：shows, indicates, mirrors, reveals | 反义词：obscures, conceals, hides | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mirror reflects my face. = 镜子反射着我的脸。
His behavior reflects his upbringing. = 他的行为反映了他的教养。

一般过去时:
The glass reflected the sunlight. = 玻璃反射了阳光。
The survey reflected public opinion. = 调查反映了公众舆论。

现在完成时:
The changes have reflected a shift in public opinion. = 这些变化反映了公众舆论的转变。
Her work has reflected her passion for art. = 她的作品反映了她对艺术的热情。

过去完成时:
The painting reflected the artist's earlier style. = 这幅画反映了艺术家早期的风格。
The newspaper article reflected the events that had occurred. = 报纸文章反映了已经发生的事情。

将来时:
The new policy will reflect the government's priorities. = 新政策将反映政府的优先事项。
The upcoming election results will reflect the voters' choices. = 即将到来的选举结果将反映选民的选择。

虚拟语气:
If the glass were cleaner, it would reflect the image more clearly. = 如果玻璃更干净，它会更清晰地反射图像。
I wish my work reflected my true potential. = 我希望我的作品能体现我的真实潜能。

被动语态:
The problem is reflected in the high unemployment rate. = 这个问题反映在高失业率上。
His mood was reflected in his somber expression. = 他的情绪反映在他阴沉的表情上。

疑问句:
Does this data reflect the actual situation? = 这些数据是否反映了实际情况？
What does this painting reflect? = 这幅画反映了什么？

否定句:
The numbers do not reflect the true cost. = 这些数字没有反映真实的成本。
The article didn't reflect the complexity of the issue. = 这篇文章没有反映问题的复杂性。


PHRASE->
reflect on:  仔细思考
reflect back: 回想
reflect upon: 深思熟虑
reflect light: 反射光线
reflect the reality: 反映现实
