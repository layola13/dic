continentalism:/ˌkɒntɪˈnentəlɪzəm/| n. |大陆主义；大陆统一论|The belief or policy of uniting or cooperating with other countries on the same continent.  It often implies a focus on regional interests and cooperation over global ones.  The term can also refer to the characteristics or culture of a particular continent.|Example: The rise of continentalism in Europe led to the formation of the European Union.  欧洲大陆主义的兴起导致了欧盟的形成。|近义词：regionalism, federalism|反义词：nationalism, isolationism
