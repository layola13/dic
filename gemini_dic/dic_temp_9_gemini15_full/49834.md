demonstrator: /ˈdɛmənˌstreɪtər/ | n. | 示威者；演示者；实验者 | a person who participates in a public demonstration, especially one that involves protesting against something.  Also, a person who demonstrates or explains how something works. | 例句：The demonstrators marched through the streets, chanting slogans. 示威者们走上街头，高喊口号。

The science teacher was a skilled demonstrator, able to make complex concepts easily understandable. 科学老师是一位熟练的演示者，能够轻松地解释复杂的概念。

|近义词：protester, exhibitor, presenter |反义词：observer, bystander |名词
