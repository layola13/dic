onomatopoeic: /ˌɒnəˌmætəˈpiːɪk/ /ˌɑːnəˌmætəˈpiːɪk/ | adj. | 象声的；拟声的 | Forming words from sounds |  The buzzing of bees is an onomatopoeic word.  蜜蜂的嗡嗡声是一个象声词。|近义词: imitative, mimetic | 反义词: abstract


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds chirp merrily. = 鸟儿快乐地啁啾。
The clock ticks steadily. = 时钟稳稳地滴答作响。


一般过去时:
The dog barked loudly last night. = 狗昨晚大声吠叫。
The thunder roared during the storm. = 暴风雨期间雷声隆隆。


现在完成时:
The rain has pattered against the window all morning. = 雨水整个上午一直在窗玻璃上发出滴答声。
The children have giggled throughout the movie. = 孩子们在看电影的整个过程中一直在咯咯地笑。


过去完成时:
The owl had hooted before I went to sleep. = 我睡觉前猫头鹰已经叫过。
The wind had whistled all night. = 风整夜都在呼啸。


将来时:
The baby will coo when she sees her mother. = 宝宝看到妈妈就会咕咕叫。
The train will whistle as it approaches the station. = 火车进站时会鸣笛。


虚拟语气:
If the train had whistled, we would have heard it. = 如果火车鸣笛，我们会听到的。
If the cat had meowed, I would have known it was hungry. = 如果猫叫了，我就知道它饿了。


被动语态:
The sound of the waterfall was described as a booming sound. = 瀑布的声音被描述为隆隆声。
The bird’s song was described as a chirping sound. = 鸟鸣声被描述为啁啾声。


疑问句:
Does the engine rumble loudly? = 引擎是不是发出很大的隆隆声？
Does the cat meow often? = 猫经常喵喵叫吗？


否定句:
The river did not gurgle gently. = 河水没有轻轻地流淌。
The leaves did not rustle in the breeze. = 叶子没有在微风中沙沙作响。


PHRASE->

onomatopoeic effect = 象声效果
onomatopoeic sounds = 象声音效
onomatopoeic language = 象声语言
