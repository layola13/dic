propatriotic: /ˌproʊˌpætriˈɒtɪk/ | adj. | 极度爱国的 | Extremely patriotic; excessively or blindly patriotic.  The term often implies an aggressive or intolerant form of patriotism. |例句：His propatriotic views led him to actively denounce anyone who criticized the government.  他的极端爱国观点导致他积极谴责任何批评政府的人。

His propatriotic rhetoric alienated many potential allies.  他极端爱国的言辞疏远了许多潜在的盟友。


近义词：ultra-patriotic, jingoistic, chauvinistic | 反义词：unpatriotic, traitorous


词性：形容词
