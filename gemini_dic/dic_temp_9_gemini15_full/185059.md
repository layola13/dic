rethreaten:/ˌriːˈθrɛtən/ | vt. |再次威胁；重新威胁 |To threaten again or anew.  This word is formed by adding the prefix "re-" (meaning again) to the verb "threaten". It implies a renewed or repeated threat after a previous one. |例句：The bully rethreatened the boy after he had already apologized.  霸凌者在男孩道歉后再次威胁了他。


The principal rethreatened suspension if the students didn't improve their behavior.  校长再次威胁说如果学生们不改掉他们的行为就会被停学。|近义词：reiterate a threat, renew a threat |反义词：assure, reassure
