misconceived:/ˌmɪskənˈsiːvd/| adj.|被误解的；错误观念的 | Based on a misunderstanding or a mistaken idea; wrongly conceived.  It implies a flawed understanding or interpretation of something. |例句：His plan was misconceived from the start.  他的计划从一开始就构思错误。

His theory of history is completely misconceived. 他的历史理论完全是错误的。


近义词：misunderstood, mistaken, erroneous, flawed, faulty

反义词：well-understood, accurate, correct, sound


词源:

* **mis-:**  A prefix meaning "wrongly," "badly," or "ill."
* **conceive:**  From Old French *concevoir*, from Latin *concipere*, meaning "to take in," "to become pregnant," and figuratively, "to form in the mind."
