festivities:/fɛsˈtɪvətiz/| n.|庆祝活动；节日庆祝|Activities that are done in celebration of a special occasion or event.  The word often refers to a series of events rather than a single event. |例句：The town's festivities included a parade, fireworks, and a street fair.  这个小镇的庆祝活动包括游行、烟花和街头集市。

The Christmas festivities were a lot of fun. 圣诞节的庆祝活动非常有趣。|近义词：celebrations, merrymaking, revelry|反义词：mourning, solemnity

词源:
