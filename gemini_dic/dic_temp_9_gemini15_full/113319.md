luwak:/ˈluːwæk/| n.|麝香猫|Luwak refers to the Asian palm civet, a nocturnal mammal known for its role in producing Kopi Luwak coffee.  The civet eats coffee cherries, and the beans are then processed from its feces.  The coffee produced is considered a delicacy and commands a high price.| Example: Kopi Luwak is a type of coffee made from coffee beans that have passed through the digestive system of the Asian palm civet (luwak).  麝香猫咖啡是一种由经过亚洲麝香猫（luwak）消化系统处理过的咖啡豆制成的咖啡。|近义词：Asian palm civet, civet cat|反义词：无
