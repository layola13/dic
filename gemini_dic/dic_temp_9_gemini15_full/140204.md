officers:/ˈɒfɪsəz/ | n. | 官员，警官 |  Members of a police force or other uniformed service, holding a position of authority.  Also refers to those holding administrative or managerial positions in an organization. | Example: The police officers arrested the suspect.  警察逮捕了嫌疑犯。 | Example: The company officers met to discuss the budget. 公司管理人员开会讨论预算。 |近义词: officials, executives, police, constables | 反义词: civilians, subordinates
