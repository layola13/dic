preevades:/ˌpriːɪˈveɪdz/| v.|弥漫，遍布，渗透 | To spread throughout and permeate; to be widespread.|例句：The aroma of freshly baked bread preevaded the entire house. 新鲜烘焙的面包香味弥漫了整个房子。


例句：The feeling of anxiety preevaded the room before the exam. 考试前焦虑的情绪弥漫了整个房间。


近义词：permeate, saturate, imbue, infuse


反义词：exclude, isolate, confine


词性:动词
