misreports:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːrts/ | vt. |错误报道，歪曲报道 |To give a false or inaccurate account of something.  |例句：The newspaper misreported the details of the accident. 报纸歪曲报道了事故的细节。 |例句：The politician consistently misreports his financial dealings.  这位政治家总是歪曲报道他的财务往来。|近义词：distort, misrepresent, falsify |反义词：accurately report, truthfully represent |动词
