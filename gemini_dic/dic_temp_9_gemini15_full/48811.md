defluoridation: /ˌdiːˌfluːəraɪˈdeɪʃən/ | n. | 除氟；脱氟作用 | The process of removing fluoride from a substance, typically water.  This is often done to reduce the risk of dental fluorosis or to meet water quality standards. | Example: The community implemented a defluoridation program to improve the safety of their drinking water.  社区实施了一项除氟计划，以提高饮用水的安全性。  | 近义词：fluoride removal, defluorination | 反义词：fluoridation |  词性：名词
