ergotine:/ˈɜːrɡətaɪn/| n. |麦角碱|A potent alkaloid found in ergot, a fungus that infects rye and other grains.  Ergotine has historically been used in medicine, though its use is now limited due to its toxicity and the availability of safer alternatives.
