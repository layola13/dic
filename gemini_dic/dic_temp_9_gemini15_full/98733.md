词源:

The word "insurmountableness" is derived from:

* **in-**: a prefix meaning "not" or "without"
* **surmount**:  verb meaning "to overcome, to conquer, to rise above"
* **-able**: suffix making an adjective meaning "capable of being"
* **-ness**: suffix forming a noun meaning "the state or quality of being"


Therefore, "insurmountableness" literally means "the state or quality of not being able to be overcome".
