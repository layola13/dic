lending-libraries: /ˈlɛndɪŋ ˈlaɪbrɛriz/ | n. | 借阅图书馆 | Lending libraries are institutions that provide books and other materials for borrowing.  They are often free to use and are a valuable resource for communities. | Example: Many schools and universities have extensive lending libraries for their students and faculty.  许多学校和大学为学生和教职工提供了大量的借阅图书馆。  | 近义词：public libraries, book lending services | 反义词：bookstores (selling books, not lending)
