enable:/ɪˈneɪbl/ | vt. | 使能够；使可能；授权 | To give someone the authority or power to do something.  | The new software enables users to access files remotely.  新的软件使用户能够远程访问文件。 | empower, authorize, facilitate | disable, prohibit, restrict | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enables his team to work efficiently. = 他使他的团队能够高效工作。
This tool enables faster data processing. = 这个工具使数据处理更快。

一般过去时:
The training enabled her to overcome her fear. = 培训使她克服了恐惧。
The key enabled him to open the door. = 钥匙使他打开了门。


现在完成时:
The new policy has enabled greater transparency. = 新政策促进了更大的透明度。
Technological advancements have enabled faster communication. = 技术进步使沟通更快。


过去完成时:
His previous experience had enabled him to handle the crisis effectively. = 他之前的经验使他能够有效地处理危机。
The earlier investment had enabled the company's expansion. = 早期投资使公司得以扩张。


将来时:
The new system will enable us to track progress more accurately. = 新系统将使我们能够更准确地追踪进度。
This innovation will enable a more sustainable future. = 这项创新将带来更可持续的未来。


虚拟语气:
If only I had the necessary skills, I would enable myself to do the job. = 如果我有必要的技能，我就能胜任这份工作。
If the software were improved, it would enable more efficient workflow. = 如果软件得到改进，它将使工作流程更高效。


被动语态:
They were enabled to participate in the project. = 他们能够参与这个项目。
The students were enabled to learn at their own pace. = 学生们能够按照自己的节奏学习。


疑问句:
Does this software enable remote access? = 这个软件支持远程访问吗？
Will this policy enable better collaboration? = 这项政策会促进更好的合作吗？


否定句:
The lack of funding disabled the project. = 资金不足使项目无法进行。
His illness prevented him from enabling his colleagues. = 他的病阻止了他帮助同事。


PHRASE->
enable somebody to do something  = 使某人能够做某事
enable something =  使…成为可能
enable access = 启用访问
enable features = 启用功能
enable-disable = 启用-禁用
