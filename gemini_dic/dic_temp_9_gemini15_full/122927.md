mom-and-pop: /ˌmɑːm ænd ˈpɑːp/ | adj. |夫妻开的；家庭式经营的 |  Describes a small business, typically a retail store or restaurant, that is owned and operated by a family, often a married couple.  It emphasizes the small scale, personal service, and often family-oriented atmosphere of such businesses. |  例句：That mom-and-pop diner has been a neighborhood staple for fifty years.  这家夫妻开的餐馆已经成为社区的支柱五十年了。


例句：We prefer to shop at mom-and-pop stores because they offer more personalized service. 我们更喜欢在夫妻开的商店购物，因为他们提供更个性化的服务。


近义词：family-owned, family-run, independent, small-scale


反义词：corporate, chain, large-scale, franchise


词性:形容词
