nondefiance:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈfaɪ.əns/| n. | 不反抗；顺从 | The absence of defiance; submission or compliance.  It suggests a passive acceptance rather than an active agreement.  | Example:  His nondefiance in the face of such blatant injustice was surprising.  他的这种面对如此公然不公正行为的不反抗令人惊讶。|近义词：submission, compliance, acquiescence, passivity |反义词：defiance, resistance, rebellion
