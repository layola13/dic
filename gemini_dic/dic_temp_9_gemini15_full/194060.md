self-healing: /ˈsɛlfˈhiːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我修复的，自愈的 | Possessing the ability to repair damage without external intervention.  This term is often used to describe materials, systems, or organisms that can automatically mend themselves. | 例句：The self-healing material quickly repaired the crack. = 自我修复材料迅速修复了裂缝。


The self-healing software automatically corrected the error. = 自我修复软件自动更正了错误。

近义词：self-repairing, restorative, regenerative | 反义词：irreparable, unrecoverable
