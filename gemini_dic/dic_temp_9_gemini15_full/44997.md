crystallographers:/ˌkrɪstəlˈɒɡrəfərz/ | n. | 晶体学家 | Scientists who study the structure and properties of crystals. They use various techniques, such as X-ray diffraction, to determine the arrangement of atoms and molecules within crystals.  |  Many crystallographers contributed to the discovery of the structure of DNA. 许多晶体学家为发现DNA的结构做出了贡献。|  mineralogists, physicists (in the field of solid-state physics) |  (无明确反义词，可考虑反义词为研究非晶体物质的科学家) | 名词
