employability: /ɪmˌplɔɪəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 就业能力，就业率 | The ability to get a job and keep it.  It encompasses a range of skills, qualities, and experiences that make an individual attractive to potential employers. | Example:  Improving your employability is crucial in today's competitive job market.  提升你的就业能力在当今竞争激烈的就业市场中至关重要。


Example:  He enhanced his employability by gaining relevant work experience. 他通过获得相关的实践经验来提升他的就业能力。


近义词: job prospects, career prospects, marketability


反义词: unemployability


词性: 名词
