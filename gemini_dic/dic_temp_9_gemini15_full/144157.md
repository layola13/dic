outshone:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊn/ | vt. |胜过；超过；比…更出色 |To surpass in brilliance, excellence, or achievement.  |例句：Her performance outshone all the others. 她的表演胜过所有其他表演。 |例句：The new design outshone the old one in both style and functionality. 新设计在风格和功能上都胜过旧设计。 |近义词：surpass, excel, eclipse, outdo |反义词：be outdone, be surpassed |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun outshines the moon in brightness. = 太阳的光芒胜过月亮。
His talent outshines his peers. = 他的天赋胜过他的同龄人。

一般过去时:
Her painting outshone all the others in the competition. = 她的画在比赛中胜过了所有其他的画。
The athlete outshone his rivals in the final race.  = 运动员在决赛中胜过了他的对手。

现在完成时:
His achievements have outshone all expectations. = 他的成就已经超出了所有人的预期。
The company's new product has outshone its competitors. = 公司的新产品已经胜过了竞争对手。

过去完成时:
His earlier work had already outshone his later attempts. = 他早期的作品已经胜过了他后来的尝试。
The team's performance had outshone all previous records. = 球队的表现已经超过了以往所有的纪录。

将来时:
This new technology will outshine all existing methods. = 这项新技术将胜过所有现有的方法。
We believe our strategy will outshine the competition. = 我们相信我们的策略将胜过竞争对手。

虚拟语气:
If only he had studied harder, he might have outshone his classmates. = 如果他当时学习更努力些，他也许就能胜过他的同学们。
If the project were better managed, it would outshine the previous one. = 如果这个项目管理得更好，它将会胜过之前的项目。

被动语态:
His work was outshone by a younger artist. = 他的作品被一位年轻的艺术家超越了。
The older model was outshone by the new features of the latest one. = 旧型号被最新型号的新功能超越了。

疑问句:
Does his work outshine others in the field? = 他的作品在该领域是否胜过其他人？
Will this design outshine its predecessors? = 这个设计会胜过它的前辈吗？

否定句:
His efforts did not outshine his colleagues'. = 他的努力没有胜过他的同事们。
The movie's plot did not outshine its special effects. = 这部电影的情节没有胜过它的特效。


PHRASE->
