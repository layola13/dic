comaraderie:/ˌkɒmˈrɑːdəri/|n.|同志情谊；友情；团结互助|A feeling of friendship and loyalty within a group; a spirit of good fellowship.|The team showed great camaraderie, supporting each other through thick and thin.  团队展现出极大的同志情谊，彼此互相支持，不离不弃。|friendship, solidarity, fellowship, companionship|antagonism, hostility, alienation, rivalry|名词
