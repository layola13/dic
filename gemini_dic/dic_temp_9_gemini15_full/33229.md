chronographer:/krɒˈnɒɡrɑːfər/ | n. |计时器；计时码表；记时员 | A chronometer is a highly accurate timepiece, often used for navigation. A chronograph is a stopwatch with added functions such as multiple timers and sometimes a calendar.  A chronographer is a person who records the time of events, often in sports or scientific experiments. | Example: He used a chronograph to time the runner's sprint. 他用计时码表来计时跑步者的短跑速度。 The chronographer meticulously recorded the time of each lap. 记时员一丝不苟地记录下每一圈的时间。 |近义词：stopwatch, timer, timekeeper |反义词：None specifically.
