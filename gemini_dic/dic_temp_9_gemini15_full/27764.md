cannelon:/ˌkænəˈlɒn/ | n. | 卡尼隆，千层面管 | A tube of pasta, typically large and wide, used in making cannelloni dishes.  Cannelloni are often filled with a mixture of cheese, meat, and vegetables, then baked in a sauce. | Example:  She served a delicious cannelloni with a creamy tomato sauce. 她端上了一份美味的千层面管，配以奶油番茄酱。 |近义词：tube pasta, large pasta tube | 反义词：无
