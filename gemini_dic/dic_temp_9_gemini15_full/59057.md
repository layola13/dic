dwelled:/dˈwelt/| v.|居住，沉思，详述 |To live in a place; to think deeply about something; to discuss something at length. |例句：They dwelt in a small cottage by the sea. 他们住在海边的一间小别墅里。 They dwelt on the details of the plan. 他们详细讨论了计划的细节。|近义词：resided, lived, pondered, mused, elaborated |反义词：departed, left, ignored, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dwells in a small apartment. = 他住在一间小公寓里。
She dwells on her past mistakes. = 她总是沉湎于过去的错误。


一般过去时:
They dwelt in that house for many years. = 他们在那所房子里住了很多年。
He dwelt upon the importance of education. = 他详述了教育的重要性。


现在完成时:
We have dwelt in this city for ten years. = 我们在这个城市住了十年了。
She has dwelt on that problem for days. = 她已经考虑那个问题好几天了。


过去完成时:
They had dwelt in the village before the war. = 战争前他们住在村里。
He had dwelt on the subject before concluding his speech. = 他在结束演讲前已经详细阐述了这个主题。


将来时:
They will dwell in a bigger house next year. = 他们明年将住进一所更大的房子。
She will dwell on the details of the report later. = 她稍后会详细讨论报告的细节。


虚拟语气:
If I dwelt in a castle, I would be happy. = 如果我住在一座城堡里，我会很高兴。
I wish I had dwelt more on the subject. = 我希望我当时能对这个主题多加考虑。


被动语态:
The issue was dwelt upon extensively in the meeting. = 会议上对这个问题进行了广泛的讨论。
The details of the crime were dwelt on in the news report. = 新闻报道中详细报道了犯罪的细节。


疑问句:
Where do they dwell? = 他们住在哪里？
What did he dwell on in his speech? = 他在演讲中详述了什么？


否定句:
They did not dwell in the city. = 他们不住在这个城市。
He did not dwell on the negative aspects. = 他没有详述消极方面。


PHRASE->
dwell on/upon: 详细叙述；仔细考虑
dwell in: 居住于
dwell among: 居住在…之中
dwell with: 与…同住
dweller in:居住在…的人
