ceasing:/siːsɪŋ/| v.|停止；终止；结束 |The act of bringing something to an end; stopping.  |例句：The rain is ceasing. = 雨停了。
The incessant noise was finally ceasing. = 那烦人的噪音终于停止了。|近义词：stopping, ending, halting, terminating |反义词：starting, beginning, commencing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is ceasing his efforts. = 他正在停止他的努力。
The factory is ceasing production. = 工厂正在停止生产。

一般过去时:
She ceased her complaints. = 她停止了抱怨。
The engine ceased functioning. = 引擎停止运转。

现在完成时:
They have ceased all communication. = 他们已经停止了一切联系。
The bleeding has ceased. = 出血已经停止了。

过去完成时:
He had ceased working before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前他已经停止工作了。
The music had ceased before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前音乐已经停止了。

将来时:
They will cease operations next month. = 他们下个月将停止运营。
The project will cease to exist after next year. = 该项目明年之后将不复存在。

虚拟语气:
If he ceased complaining, we could work more efficiently. = 如果他不抱怨，我们可以更高效地工作。
I wish he would cease interrupting me. = 我希望他能停止打断我。

被动语态:
Further expansion has been ceased due to the economic downturn. = 由于经济低迷，进一步扩张已被停止。
The project was ceased due to lack of funding. = 该项目因缺乏资金而被终止。

疑问句:
Has the bleeding ceased? = 出血停止了吗？
Will the protests cease soon? = 抗议活动很快就会停止吗？

否定句:
The rain has not ceased yet. = 雨还没有停。
He did not cease to amaze us. = 他一直让我们惊叹不已。


PHRASE->
cease and desist = 停止并放弃
cease fire = 停火
cease to exist = 不复存在
cease production = 停止生产
cease functioning = 停止运转


词源:
源自古法语 cesser，意为“停止”，最终源自拉丁语 cessare，意为“停止，放弃”。
