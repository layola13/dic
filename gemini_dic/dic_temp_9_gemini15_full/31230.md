chanter:/ˈʃæntər/ | n. |吟唱者；鸣禽 | A singer, especially one who sings in a liturgical or choral context.  Also refers to a type of bird known for its singing. |例句：The chanter led the congregation in a beautiful hymn.  吟唱者带领会众唱了一首优美的圣歌。|例句：The woods were alive with the song of the chanter.  树林里回荡着鸣禽的歌声。|近义词：singer, vocalist, songbird |反义词：listener
