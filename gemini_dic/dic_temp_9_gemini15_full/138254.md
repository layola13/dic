not-bad:/nɑːt bæd/ | adj. | 还不错，凑合 |  It means something is acceptable or satisfactory, though not excellent. It's an informal expression. |例句：The movie was not bad. = 这部电影还不错。

The food was not bad, but it wasn't great either. = 食物还不错，但也不是很好。|近义词：tolerable, acceptable, decent, alright |反义词：terrible, awful, dreadful, horrible |形容词
