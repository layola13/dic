neuropteran:/ˈnʊərəpˌtɛrən/| n. |脉翅目昆虫|A neuropteran is an insect belonging to the order Neuroptera, characterized by delicate, net-veined wings.  They are typically predatory insects, with some species exhibiting bioluminescence.|Example: Lacewings are a common example of a neuropteran insect.|近义词：lacewing (a type of neuropteran), antlion (a type of neuropteran)|反义词：无
