sejeant:/səˈʒɑːnt/| n.|警官，警长|A sejeant is a non-commissioned officer of the police force, ranking below a sergeant.  The term is archaic and largely obsolete in modern English, though it might still be encountered in historical contexts.|例句：In the old detective novels, the sejeant was often depicted as a gruff but loyal officer. = 在旧侦探小说中，警官常被描绘成一个粗暴但忠诚的警官。|近义词：sergeant, constable|反义词: civilian
