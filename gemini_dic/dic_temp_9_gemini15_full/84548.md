hammer-away:/ˈhæmər əˈweɪ/| v.|猛击；反复敲打；坚持不懈地做某事|to strike repeatedly and forcefully; to continue doing something persistently and forcefully.|例句：He hammered away at the nail until it finally went in. 他反复猛击钉子，直到钉子终于钉进去了。

例句：She hammered away at the problem until she found a solution. 她坚持不懈地解决这个问题，直到找到解决方案。


近义词：pound, batter, persist, persevere|反义词：give up, cease, stop|动词
