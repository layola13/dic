chalcedonic:/ˌkælˈsɛdənɪk/| adj.|玉髓质的，隐晶质的|Of or relating to chalcedony, a cryptocrystalline variety of quartz; characterized by a fine, dense texture.  Chalcedonic materials are typically translucent and exhibit a waxy luster.|The agate is a chalcedonic mineral with banded color patterns.|近义词：cryptocrystalline|反义词：macrocrystalline
