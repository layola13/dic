preconferences:/ˌpriːˈkɒnfərənsɪz/| n.|预备会议；会前会议|Meetings held before a larger conference or event to prepare for it.  These meetings often involve logistical planning, agenda setting, and speaker coordination.|例句：The preconferences for the international summit were held in Geneva.  国际峰会的预备会议在日内瓦举行。

例句：Several preconferences were necessary to organize the successful charity gala.  为了成功举办慈善晚会，需要召开几次预备会议。


近义词：preparatory meetings, preliminary meetings, advance meetings

反义词：post-conference meetings, follow-up meetings


词性:名词
