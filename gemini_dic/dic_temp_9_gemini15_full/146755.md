overhung:/ˌoʊvərˈhʌŋ/| v. |悬挂；覆盖；笼罩|To hang or extend over something.  It implies a state of being suspended or projecting beyond a point.|The branches overhung the path. 树枝悬在小路的上方。|The dark clouds overhung the town.  乌云笼罩着小镇。|近义词：hang over, overshadow, loom over|反义词：support, uphold


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The painting overhangs the fireplace. = 这幅画悬挂在壁炉上方。
The cliff overhangs the sea. = 悬崖峭壁俯瞰大海。


一般过去时:
The sword overhung the sleeping knight. = 宝剑悬在熟睡的骑士上方。
Dark clouds overhung the valley. = 黑云笼罩着山谷。


现在完成时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
