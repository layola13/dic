crystallised:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪzd/| v. adj. |结晶的；明确表达的|To change into crystals; to become clear and definite.|The sugar solution crystallised into beautiful sugar crystals. 糖溶液结晶成美丽的糖晶体。 The company's strategy has crystallised over the past year. 公司的战略在过去一年中已经明确了。|solidified, defined, clarified|amorphous, vague, unclear|动词，形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sugar crystallises slowly. = 糖慢慢结晶。
He crystallises his thoughts before speaking. = 他在说话前先理清思路。


一般过去时:
The solution crystallised overnight. = 溶液一夜之间结晶了。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
The project had crystallised before the funding was secured. = 项目在资金到位之前就已经明确了。
The artist had crystallised his vision into a masterpiece. = 艺术家已将他的愿景凝结成杰作。
