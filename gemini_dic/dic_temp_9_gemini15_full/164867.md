pre-free-trade:/ˌpriːˈfriːˌtreɪd/| adj.|  前自由贸易的，自由贸易之前的 |  Describing a period or system before the implementation of free trade policies.  It emphasizes the conditions existing before free trade became prevalent. | Example: Pre-free-trade economic policies often involved high tariffs and protectionist measures.  前自由贸易的经济政策通常涉及高关税和保护主义措施。|近义词：protectionist, pre-liberalization |反义词：free-trade, liberalized
