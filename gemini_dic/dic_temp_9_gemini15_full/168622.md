proclerical: /ˌproʊklɛrɪkəl/ | adj. | 亲教权的，支持教权的 |

英文解析: Favoring or supporting the clergy or the power of the church.  Characterized by or showing support for the clergy and their influence in society.

例句:

The proclerical government implemented policies that favored religious institutions.  亲教权的政府实施了有利于宗教机构的政策。


The newspaper's editorials were openly proclerical, often criticizing secular movements.  该报的社论公开地支持教权，经常批评世俗运动。


近义词:  clericalist, pro-church, religious, pietistic


反义词: anticlerical, secularist, irreligious


词性: adjective
