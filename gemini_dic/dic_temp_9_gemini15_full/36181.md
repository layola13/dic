coffle:/ˈkɒfl/| n.|驮队，牲口队|A coffle is a group of animals, especially mules or donkeys, travelling together, often carrying goods.  The term often implies a line of pack animals.  It can also refer to the group of people traveling with the animals.|例句：A long coffle of mules wound slowly up the mountain path. 一长串骡子驮队缓缓地沿着山路向上走。|例句：The traders led a large coffle across the desert. 商人们带领着一大队牲口穿过沙漠。|近义词：train, caravan, string|反义词：none|名词
