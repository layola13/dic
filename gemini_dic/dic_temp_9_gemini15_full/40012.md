consultum: /kənˈsʌltəm/ | n. |商议；决议；意见；咨询 | A formal decision or opinion, especially one reached by a council or other deliberative body.  It often refers to a decree or resolution made by an authoritative body. | Example: The senate's consultum was to declare war. 参议院的决议是宣战。  The consultum of the physicians recommended surgery. 医生们的商议结果建议进行手术。|近义词：resolution, decree, decision, opinion |反义词： (no direct antonym, but could consider  'disagreement' or 'rejection' as contextual opposites) |名词
