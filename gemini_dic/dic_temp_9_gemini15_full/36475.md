colead:/ˈkoʊliːd/| v.|共同领导，协同领导|To lead something jointly with someone else.  It implies shared responsibility and authority.|例句：The two companies will colead the project. = 两家公司将共同领导这个项目。

例句：She and her colleague will colead the workshop. = 她和她的同事将共同主持研讨会。

近义词：co-manage, jointly lead,  co-direct

反义词：solely lead, independently lead

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They colead the marketing team. = 他们共同领导营销团队。
We colead the fundraising effort. = 我们共同领导筹款工作。

一般过去时:
They co-led the project last year. = 他们去年共同领导了这个项目。

现在完成时:
They have co-led several successful projects. = 他们已经共同领导了几项成功的项目。
We have co-led the team for five years. = 我们共同领导这个团队五年了。


过去完成时:
They had co-led the company before the merger. = 合并之前，他们共同领导了这家公司。
We had co-led the initiative for six months. = 我们已经共同领导这项倡议六个月了。
