defected:/dɪˈfɛktɪd/| adj.|有缺陷的；不合格的|Having a defect or flaw; imperfect or faulty.  |例句：The factory recalled all the defected products. 工厂召回了所有有缺陷的产品。
The defected soldier was discharged from the army.  有缺陷的士兵被军队解雇了。|近义词：faulty, flawed, imperfect, defective|反义词：perfect, flawless, sound
