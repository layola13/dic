kingpin:/ˈkɪŋpɪn/| n. |核心人物；关键；大头针；|The central person in a criminal organization; a crucial element.  A crucial person or thing in a situation or system.  Also, a type of pin.|例句：He was the kingpin of the drug operation. 他是毒品交易的核心人物。

例句：The new software is the kingpin of their entire business model. 这款新软件是他们整个商业模式的核心。


近义词：mainstay, linchpin, pivotal figure, key player, mastermind

反义词：peripheral figure, unimportant element, insignificant detail
