propertied:/ˈprɒpətid/| adj.|拥有财产的，有产的|Having property; wealthy.|The propertied class benefited most from the economic boom.  拥有财产的阶级从经济繁荣中获益最多。|近义词：wealthy, affluent, well-to-do|反义词：propertyless, impoverished
