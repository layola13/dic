discretionally:/dɪˈskreʃənəli/ | adv. |自行决定地；谨慎地；随意地 |At one's own discretion; according to one's judgment.  Acting or done at one's own judgment. |例句：The manager can discretionally award bonuses to employees.  经理可以自行决定是否给员工发放奖金。

例句：He discretionally chose to ignore the rude comment. 他自行决定忽略了那个粗鲁的评论。


近义词：optionally, arbitrarily, voluntarily |反义词：compulsorily, mandatorily


词源:  From Middle English *discrecion, from Old French *discrecion, from Latin discret- “separated, distinct”, past participle of discernere “to separate, distinguish”. The adverbial form "-ally" was added later.
