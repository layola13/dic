persulphate: /pɜːˈsʌlfeɪt/ | n. | 过硫酸盐 | A salt of persulphuric acid, containing the anion S₂O₈²⁻.  Persulphates are strong oxidizing agents used in various applications, including polymerization and etching. | Example: Ammonium persulphate is a common persulphate used as an initiator in polymerization reactions. 铵过硫酸盐是一种常用的过硫酸盐，用作聚合反应的引发剂。 | 近义词: peroxydisulfate | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, but reducing agents are its opposite in function) |  词性:名词
