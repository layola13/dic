For example, benefits like health insurance are considered nonwage compensation. | The company offers a generous nonwage benefits package to its employees. 这家公司为其员工提供丰厚的非工资福利待遇。  | 近义词：non-salary, supplementary, fringe | 反义词：wage, salary
