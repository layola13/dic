que:/keɪ/| pron.|什么，那个|The word "que" is a pronoun used to ask questions or refer to something unspecified.|What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？|近义词：what, which|反义词:None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？


一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What time did the movie start? = 电影几点开始？


现在完成时:
What have you been doing? = 你一直在做什么？
What has she accomplished? = 她取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the party? = 他们在派对前吃了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like? = 天气将会怎样？


虚拟语气:
I wish I knew what to do. = 我真希望知道该怎么做。
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？


被动语态:
What is being discussed? = 正在讨论什么？
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道说什么。
She didn't understand what he meant. = 她不明白他的意思。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What's going on? = 发生了什么事？
What about...? = ...怎么样？
What if...? = 如果...将会怎样？
What for? = 为什么？/为了什么？
