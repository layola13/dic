dietitians:/ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃənz/| n. |注册营养师，饮食学家|Professionals who are qualified to give advice on food and nutrition. They assess people's dietary needs and create personalized eating plans.  |Many dietitians work in hospitals, clinics, or private practices.|近义词：nutritionists|反义词：无
