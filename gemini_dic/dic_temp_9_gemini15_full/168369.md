proabolition:/ˌproʊæbəˈlɪʃən/| adj.| 支持废除奴隶制(的)|Proabolition refers to being in favor of abolishing slavery or a similar oppressive system.  It describes a stance advocating for the ending of a practice of enforced servitude or exploitation.|The proabolition movement gained momentum in the 19th century.  支持废除奴隶制的运动在19世纪获得了发展势头。|近义词：anti-slavery, abolitionist|反义词：pro-slavery, slavery-supporting|形容词
