depeinct:/dɪˈpɪŋkt/| vt.| 描绘，描述 | To describe someone or something using words or a picture.  | The artist depicted the scene with great detail.  艺术家详细地描绘了这幅场景。| portray, represent, illustrate | distort, misrepresent | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The painter depicts the landscape beautifully. = 画家美丽地描绘了这片风景。
She depicts her childhood in her new novel. = 她在新小说中描绘了她的童年。


一般过去时:
The book depicted the war's horrors. = 这本书描绘了战争的恐怖。
He depicted the character as a villain. = 他将这个人物描绘成一个恶棍。


现在完成时:
The artist has depicted many famous people. =这位艺术家已经描绘了许多名人。
The media has depicted the situation unfairly. = 媒体对这种情况的描述不公平。


过去完成时:
The author had depicted the hero's journey in previous chapters. = 作者在之前的章节中已经描绘了英雄的旅程。
History books had depicted the event quite differently. = 历史书对这一事件的描述大相径庭。


将来时:
The movie will depict the events leading to the war. = 这部电影将描绘导致战争的事件。
The artist will depict a futuristic city in her next painting. = 艺术家将在她的下一幅画中描绘一个未来都市。


虚拟语气:
If he depicted the scene accurately, the painting would be more realistic. = 如果他准确地描绘了这个场景，这幅画就会更逼真。
I wish the article had depicted the issue more fairly. = 我希望这篇文章能更公平地描述这个问题。


被动语态:
The battle was depicted in vivid detail. = 这场战斗被生动地描绘出来。
The scene is depicted from a unique perspective. = 场景是从一个独特的视角描绘的。


疑问句:
How did the writer depict the main character? = 作者是如何刻画主人公的？
Does the painting accurately depict the historical event? = 这幅画是否准确地描绘了历史事件？


否定句:
The painting did not depict the landscape accurately. = 这幅画没有准确地描绘风景。
The report did not depict the situation honestly. = 这份报告没有诚实地描述情况。


PHRASE->
depict something vividly = 生动地描绘某事物
depict someone as something = 将某人描绘成某物
depict a scene = 描绘一个场景
depict the truth = 描绘真相
depict the reality = 描绘现实
