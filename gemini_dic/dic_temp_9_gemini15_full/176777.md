readiness:/ˈredinəs/| n. |准备状态， готовность |The state of being ready or prepared for something.  It implies a state of being well-equipped and prepared to act or respond effectively. This can refer to physical preparedness, mental preparedness, or a combination of both.|例句：The army showed its readiness for battle.  军队展现了其战斗准备状态。

例句：Her readiness to help others is admirable. 她乐于助人的准备状态令人钦佩。


近义词：preparedness, preparedness, readiness, willingness,  preparedness


反义词：unpreparedness, unpreparedness, reluctance


词性：名词
