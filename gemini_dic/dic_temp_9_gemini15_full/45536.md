curing:/ˈkjʊərɪŋ/| v. n.|治愈；处理；腌制|to restore to health; to preserve (food) by drying, salting, etc.|例句：The doctor is curing him of his illness. 医生正在治好他的病。The process of curing meat takes several weeks. 肉类腌制过程需要几周时间。|近义词：healing, treating, preserving|反义词：infecting, worsening
