overservicing:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvɪsɪŋ/ | n. |过度服务；服务过多 | The act of providing more service than is necessary or desired, often leading to negative consequences.  This can apply to various fields, including customer service, finance, and healthcare. |Example: The bank's overservicing led to unnecessary fees and complicated the customer's financial situation. 银行的过度服务导致不必要的费用，并使客户的财务状况复杂化。|近义词：over-attention, excessive service |反义词：underservicing, neglect
