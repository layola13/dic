centrifuge:/ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/|/ˈsɛntɹɪfjuːdʒ/| n. vt.|离心机；离心分离|A centrifuge is a machine that rotates at high speed to separate substances of different densities.  It's commonly used in laboratories to separate components of blood, for example.|The lab technician used a centrifuge to separate the blood plasma from the red blood cells. 实验室技术员用离心机将血浆与红细胞分离。|separator, separator|None.
