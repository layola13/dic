cradles:/ˈkreɪdəlz/| n. |摇篮；发源地；支架|A cradle is a bed for a baby, typically one that rocks. It can also refer to the origin or birthplace of something, or a support structure.|例句：The baby slept soundly in its cradle.  婴儿在摇篮里睡得很香。|例句：The cradle of civilization is often considered to be Mesopotamia. 文明的发源地通常被认为是美索不达米亚。|近义词：crib, cot, bassinet |反义词：adult bed
