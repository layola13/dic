linotype:/ˈlaɪnəʊtaɪp/| n. |排字机；铅字排印术 |A linotype machine is a typesetting machine that sets type mechanically from matrices.  It was a significant technological advancement in printing, enabling faster and more efficient typesetting.  |例句：The old newspaper office still had a functioning linotype machine. 这家旧报社仍然有一台运作的排字机。


例句：The invention of the linotype revolutionized the printing industry. 排字机的发明彻底改变了印刷业。|近义词：typesetter, composing machine |反义词：hand-typesetting (手工排版)
