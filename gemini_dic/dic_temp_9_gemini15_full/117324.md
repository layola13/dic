meddles:/ˈmɛdəlz/| v.|干涉；管闲事；篡改|To interfere in something that is not one's concern.|例句：He's always meddling in other people's affairs. 他总是干涉别人的事情。|例句：Don't meddle with my belongings! 不要乱动我的东西！|近义词：interfere, interfere with, tamper with|反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He meddles in his brother's business. = 他干涉他哥哥的生意。
She often meddles in her neighbor's affairs. = 她经常干涉邻居的事务。

一般过去时:
He meddled in the election campaign. = 他干涉了竞选活动。
They meddled with the evidence. = 他们篡改了证据。

现在完成时:
He has meddled in too many things lately. = 他最近干涉了太多的事情。
She has meddled with the settings on my computer. = 她篡改了我电脑的设置。

过去完成时:
He had meddled in the project before anyone noticed. = 在任何人注意到之前，他已经干涉了这个项目。
They had meddled with the documents before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，他们已经篡改了文件。

将来时:
He will meddle in the matter again, I'm sure. = 我肯定他会再次干涉这件事。
They will meddle with the machine and break it. = 他们会乱动机器，把它弄坏。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't meddled, things would have been different. = 如果他没有干涉，情况就会不同了。
I wish he wouldn't meddle in my life. = 我希望他不要干涉我的生活。

被动语态:
The documents were meddled with before the trial. = 文件在审判前被篡改了。
His affairs were meddled in by his jealous colleagues. = 他的事务被嫉妒他的同事们干涉了。

疑问句:
Does he often meddle in other people's business? = 他经常干涉别人的事吗？
Were the results meddled with? = 结果被篡改了吗？

否定句:
He doesn't meddle in politics. = 他不干涉政治。
She didn't meddle in my affairs. = 她没有干涉我的事情。


PHRASE->
meddle in = 干涉…
