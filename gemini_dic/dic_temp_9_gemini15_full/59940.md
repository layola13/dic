edging:/ˈɛdʒɪŋ/| n. v.|边缘；镶边；逐渐逼近|The act of creating or adding an edge or border;  a border or trim.  The slow or gradual approach to something.|The picture frame had a decorative edging.  这幅画框有一个装饰性的边饰。

The cat edged closer to the bird. 猫慢慢地靠近那只鸟。|border, trim, fringe, hem|center, middle|名词
