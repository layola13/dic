microtubules:/ˈmaɪkroʊtjuːbjuːlz/ | n. |微管|Hollow cylindrical structures found in most eukaryotic cells, composed of tubulin protein dimers.  They play crucial roles in various cellular processes, including cell division, intracellular transport, and maintaining cell shape. |例句：Microtubules are essential for chromosome segregation during mitosis. 微管对于有丝分裂期间染色体的分离至关重要。

例句：The disruption of microtubules can lead to cell death. 微管的破坏会导致细胞死亡。


近义词：microtubular structures| 反义词：None (no direct antonym)| 词性：名词
