para-aminobenzoic:/ˌpærəæmɪnoʊbɛnˈzoʊɪk/| n. |对氨基苯甲酸|Para-aminobenzoic acid (PABA) is an organic compound with the formula H2NC6H4CO2H.  It is a white crystalline powder that is slightly soluble in water. PABA is a component of folic acid and has been used as a sunscreen. However, its use as a sunscreen has declined due to concerns about its potential to cause allergic reactions.|Example:  PABA was once a common ingredient in sunscreens. 对氨基苯甲酸曾经是防晒霜中常见的成分。|近义词:  4-Aminobenzoic acid|反义词:  N/A
