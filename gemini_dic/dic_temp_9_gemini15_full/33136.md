christology:/kraɪˈstɒlədʒi/| n. |基督论，基督学|The study of the person and work of Jesus Christ, including his nature, his relationship to God the Father, and his significance for salvation.  It explores questions about Jesus' divinity, humanity, and the implications of his life, death, and resurrection. | Example:  Modern christology often grapples with the complexities of Jesus' historical context. 现代基督论常常努力应对耶稣历史背景的复杂性。  The course covered various aspects of christology, including the incarnation and atonement. 这门课程涵盖了基督论的各个方面，包括道成肉身和赎罪。|近义词：soteriology (救赎论), theology (神学)|反义词：无
