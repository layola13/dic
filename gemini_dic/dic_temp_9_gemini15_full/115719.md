marguerites:/ˈmɑːɡərɪts/| n. |雏菊，玛格丽特花 | Marguerite is a type of daisy, known for its delicate petals and cheerful appearance.  They come in various colors, including white, yellow, and pink.  They are popular garden flowers and are often used in bouquets. | Example:  The garden was filled with vibrant marguerites, their petals shimmering in the sunlight. 花园里盛开着鲜艳的雏菊，花瓣在阳光下闪闪发光。


近义词：daisies, oxeye daisies| 反义词：无
