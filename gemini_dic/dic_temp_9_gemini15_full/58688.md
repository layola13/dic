dumper:/ˈdʌmpər/| n.|倾卸车，垃圾车；甩掉某人的人|A dumper is a vehicle used for transporting and dumping materials, such as earth, gravel, or waste.  It can also refer to a person who abruptly ends a relationship.|例句：The construction site used a large dumper to move the rubble.  建筑工地用一辆大型倾卸车来搬运瓦砾。 He was a dumper, leaving her without explanation. 他是个甩人的人，没有解释就离开了她。|近义词：tipper, truck,  heartbreaker (for the person)|反义词：receiver (in the context of relationships),  keeper (in the context of relationships)
