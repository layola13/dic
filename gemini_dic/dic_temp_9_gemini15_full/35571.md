coalmine:/ˈkoʊlˌmaɪn/ | n. |煤矿 |A coalmine is an underground or surface excavation where coal is extracted.  Coalmines can be dangerous places to work, with risks of explosions, collapses, and exposure to harmful substances. |Example: The miners worked tirelessly in the deep coalmine.  矿工们在深邃的煤矿里辛勤工作。 The abandoned coalmine was filled with stagnant water.  废弃的煤矿充满了死水。|近义词：colliery, coal pit |反义词：N/A
