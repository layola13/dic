mammal-like: /ˈmæməlˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 哺乳动物似的，似哺乳动物的 | Resembling a mammal in some characteristics, often referring to features of the skeletal structure or physiology.  | Example:  The fossil showed several mammal-like characteristics, suggesting a transitional form.  化石显示出一些类似哺乳动物的特征，表明这是一种过渡形态。 | 近义词:mammalian,獸類的 | 反义词:reptilian,爬行动物的

词源:

The word is a compound of "mammal" and "-like,"  "-like" being a suffix indicating resemblance.  "Mammal" itself originates from Latin *mamma ("breast," "udder"), reflecting the defining characteristic of mammals: mammary glands for suckling their young.
