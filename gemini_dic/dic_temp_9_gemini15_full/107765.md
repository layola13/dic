The term often carries strong connotations of brutality and religious extremism. |Example:  The Bible describes several instances of lapidation.  圣经中描述了几个用石头打死的例子。  The threat of lapidation was used to control dissent.  用石头打死的威胁被用来压制异见。|近义词：stoning, execution (by stoning) |反义词：acquittal, pardon
