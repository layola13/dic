hallucinogen:/həˈluːsɪnədʒən/| n. |致幻剂；致幻物 |A hallucinogen is a substance that induces hallucinations.  It alters perception, thoughts, and feelings, often creating vivid and unrealistic sensory experiences. |Example: LSD is a powerful hallucinogen.  迷幻药LSD是一种强效致幻剂。  Many plants contain natural hallucinogens. 许多植物含有天然致幻剂。|近义词：psychedelic, psychotomimetic |反义词：sobering agent (反义词相对笼统，因为没有直接的反义词)|名词
