omohyoid:/ˈoʊmoʊhaɪɔɪd/| n. |肩胛舌骨肌|A muscle in the neck that helps with swallowing and head movement. It's named for its attachment to the hyoid bone and the scapula (shoulder blade).|The omohyoid muscle is located in the anterior triangle of the neck.|近义词：sternohyoid, sternothyroid|反义词:  None (no direct antonym)|名词
