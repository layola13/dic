haemocytolysis:/ˌhiːməʊsaɪˈtɒlɪsɪs/| n. | 溶血 | The destruction of blood cells, especially red blood cells.  This process can be caused by various factors, including autoimmune diseases, infections, or exposure to certain toxins.  | Example:  The patient's haemocytolysis was attributed to a severe allergic reaction.  患者的溶血是由严重的过敏反应引起的。


近义词：haemolysis, erythrocytolysis|反义词：haematopoiesis (blood cell formation)
