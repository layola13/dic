maternally:/məˈtɜːrnəli/| adv.| 母性地，像母亲一样地 | In a motherly manner; characteristic of a mother.  |例句：She cared for the orphaned children maternally. 她像母亲一样照顾那些孤儿。  She maternally guided her son through his difficulties. 她像母亲一样地引导儿子克服困难。|近义词：motherly, lovingly, tenderly |反义词：cruelly, harshly, unkindly

词源:
