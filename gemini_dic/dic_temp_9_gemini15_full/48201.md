deconcentrate:/ˌdiːkɒnˈsɛntreɪt/| vt.|使分散；使不集中；稀释|To reduce the concentration of something; to disperse or diffuse.|例句：The government is trying to deconcentrate population in big cities. 政府正试图分散大城市的人口。The acid was deconcentrated with water. 酸被用水稀释了。|近义词：disperse, diffuse, dilute|反义词：concentrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company deconcentrates its workforce to multiple locations. = 公司将其员工分散到多个地点。
He deconcentrates his efforts, making little progress. = 他分散精力，进展甚微。


一般过去时:
They deconcentrated the protesters by using tear gas. = 他们用催泪瓦斯驱散了抗议者。
The factory deconcentrated its production process to improve efficiency. = 工厂分散了生产流程以提高效率。


现在完成时:
The government has deconcentrated the power of the central authority. = 政府已经分散了中央政府的权力。
She has deconcentrated her investments across different sectors. = 她已经将投资分散到不同的行业。


过去完成时:
The company had deconcentrated its resources before the crisis hit. = 在危机来临之前，公司已经分散了资源。
They had deconcentrated their troops to avoid a surprise attack. = 他们已经分散了军队以避免遭到突然袭击。


将来时:
The company will deconcentrate its operations to reduce risk. = 公司将分散其业务以降低风险。
They will deconcentrate the chemicals before disposal. = 他们将在处理之前稀释化学品。


虚拟语气:
If the government deconcentrated the population, traffic congestion would be reduced. = 如果政府分散人口，交通拥堵就会减少。
It would be better if they deconcentrated their efforts on one project. = 如果他们将精力集中在一个项目上会更好。


被动语态:
The workforce was deconcentrated across the country. = 劳动力被分散到全国各地。
The solution was deconcentrated with distilled water. = 溶液用蒸馏水稀释了。


疑问句:
Was the power deconcentrated successfully? = 权力是否成功地分散了？
Will the company deconcentrate its production facilities? = 公司会分散其生产设施吗？


否定句:
The government did not deconcentrate its efforts on education. = 政府没有将精力集中在教育方面。
They did not deconcentrate the population enough to relieve pressure on the city. = 他们没有足够地分散人口以减轻城市压力。
