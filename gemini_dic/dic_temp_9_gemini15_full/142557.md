ostentatious: /ˌɒs(t)enˈteɪʃəs/ | /ˌɑːstənˈteɪʃəs/ | adj. | 炫耀的；卖弄的；招摇的 | Characterized by vulgar or excessive display; designed to impress or attract notice.  It suggests an attempt to impress through outward show rather than genuine merit. |

例句：

Her ostentatious jewelry was a distraction from her otherwise insightful presentation.  她的炫耀性珠宝分散了人们对她原本富有洞察力的演讲的注意力。

He drove an ostentatious sports car, clearly wanting to show off his wealth. 他开着一辆炫耀性的跑车，显然是想炫耀他的财富。


近义词：showy, flamboyant, pretentious, conspicuous, gaudy

反义词：modest, humble, unassuming, understated, simple


词性:形容词
