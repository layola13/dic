procrastinations:/prəˌkræstɪˈneɪʃənz/| n. |拖延；耽搁|The act of delaying or postponing something.  It often refers to the habitual or intentional delay of a task or activity despite knowing that it will likely have negative consequences. |例句：His constant procrastinations led to a backlog of unfinished work. 他的不断拖延导致积压了大量未完成的工作。

例句：Her procrastination on the project resulted in a missed deadline. 她拖延项目导致错过了截止日期。


近义词：delay, postponement, deferment, putting off


反义词：promptness, diligence, timeliness


词性:名词
