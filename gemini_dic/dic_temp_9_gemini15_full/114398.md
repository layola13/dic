make-belief:/ˈmeɪk bɪˈliːf/| adj.| 假装的；虚构的；模拟的 |  Pretend; imaginary; not real.  It refers to something that is not real but is pretended to be real, often for play or imaginative purposes. |例句：The children were engaged in make-belief play, pretending to be pirates.孩子们正在玩假装游戏，假装自己是海盗。

例句：Her story was pure make-belief; none of it was true. 她的故事纯属虚构；没有一个是真实的。

近义词：pretend, imaginary, simulated, fictitious

反义词：real, actual, factual, true


词性:形容词
