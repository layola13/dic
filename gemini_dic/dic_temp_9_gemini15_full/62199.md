encountered:/ɪnˈkaʊntərd/ | v. | 遇到，遭遇，偶然碰到 | to have met someone or something unexpectedly or unexpectedly | 例句：I encountered a problem while trying to install the software.  = 我在尝试安装软件时遇到一个问题。

例句：During my travels, I encountered many interesting people. = 在我的旅行中，我遇到许多有趣的人。


近义词：met, faced, experienced | 反义词：avoided, shunned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I encounter new challenges every day. = 我每天都遇到新的挑战。
We encounter many difficulties in our work. = 我们在工作中遇到很多困难。

一般过去时:
I encountered an old friend yesterday. = 我昨天遇到一位老朋友。
They encountered a storm during their voyage. = 他们在航行中遭遇风暴。

现在完成时:
I have encountered this problem before. = 我以前遇到过这个问题。
She has encountered many obstacles in her career. = 她在她职业生涯中遇到了许多障碍。

过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, they had already encountered several setbacks. = 我们到达时，他们已经遇到好几个挫折了。
Before the project started, we had encountered similar issues. = 项目开始前，我们已经遇到过类似的问题。

将来时:
I will encounter many difficulties during the competition. = 我将在比赛中遇到许多困难。
We believe we will encounter resistance to our plans. = 我们认为我们的计划会遇到阻力。

虚拟语气:
If I encountered him, I would apologize. = 如果我遇到他，我会道歉。
I wish I hadn't encountered such a difficult situation. = 我真希望我没有遇到这么困难的处境。

被动语态:
The team was encountered with unexpected delays. = 团队遭遇了意外的延误。
The problem was encountered during testing. = 在测试过程中发现了这个问题。

疑问句:
Have you encountered any problems so far? = 到目前为止你遇到任何问题了吗？
What difficulties did you encounter during the project? = 在项目期间你遇到了什么困难？

否定句:
I haven't encountered that problem before. = 我以前没有遇到过那个问题。
They didn't encounter any significant obstacles. = 他们没有遇到任何重大障碍。


PHRASE->
encounter a problem = 遇到问题
encounter difficulties = 遇到困难
encounter resistance = 遇到阻力
encounter a challenge = 遇到挑战
a chance encounter = 一次偶然的相遇
