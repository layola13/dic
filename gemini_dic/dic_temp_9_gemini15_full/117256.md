mechanically:/məˈkænɪkli/ | adj. | 机械地；呆板地；无感情地 |  Done in a mechanical way; without feeling or originality.  It implies a lack of spontaneity or human touch. | 例句：He answered the questions mechanically, without thinking. 他机械地回答问题，没有思考。

例句：The robot moved mechanically across the floor. 机器人机械地穿过地板。


近义词：automatically, routinely, systematically, impersonally, unfeelingly | 反义词：creatively, spontaneously, emotionally, thoughtfully


USAGE->

He mechanically repeats the same task every day. 他每天机械地重复同样的工作。
The machine mechanically sorts the items into different categories. 机器机械地将物品分类到不同的类别。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
