no-one:/ˈnoʊwʌn/| pron.|没有人，谁也没有|No-one means nobody. It is used to emphasize the absence of anyone.  It's more emphatic than "nobody" in some contexts.|例句：No-one answered the phone.  没有人接电话。

例句：No-one knows the answer. 没有人知道答案。


近义词：nobody, no body, none


反义词：someone, somebody, everyone, everybody


词性：pronoun
