macerators:/ˈmæsəreɪtərz/| n.|捣碎机；粉碎机|A macerator is a device that uses blades or other cutting mechanisms to reduce solid materials to a pulp or slurry.  It is often used in plumbing systems to grind up waste materials before they enter the sewer line, preventing clogs.  They are also used in industrial processes to break down various materials for further processing.|例句：The kitchen sink is fitted with a macerator to handle food waste.|近义词：pulper, disintegrator|反义词：agglomerator
