caravanning:/ˈkærəvænɪŋ/ | n. | 房车旅行；拖车旅行 | The activity of travelling and camping in a caravan.  It involves using a caravan, a mobile home, to travel and stay overnight in various locations. |  We're going caravanning in the Scottish Highlands this summer.  今年夏天我们要去苏格兰高地进行房车旅行。| camping, touring | staying in a hotel, fixed accommodation | 名词
