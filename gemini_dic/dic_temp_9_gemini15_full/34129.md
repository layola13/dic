cladonia:/kləˈdoʊniə/| n. |鹿角苔属植物|Cladonia is a genus of about 300 species of fruticose lichens in the family Cladoniaceae. They are commonly called reindeer moss or reindeer lichen, although they are not mosses.  These lichens are often found in arctic and subarctic regions, and are a significant food source for reindeer and caribou.
