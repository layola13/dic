deep-sea:/ˌdiːpˈsiː/| adj. |深海的；深海区的| relating to the depths of the ocean, beyond the continental shelf.  |例句：Deep-sea creatures are adapted to extreme pressure and darkness. 深海生物适应了极端的压力和黑暗。|例句：The deep-sea exploration revealed a new species of fish. 深海探险发现了一种新的鱼类。|近义词：abyssal, oceanic|反义词：shallow-water, coastal
