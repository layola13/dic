dermoid:/ˈdɜːrmɔɪd/| n. |畸胎瘤|A dermoid cyst is a type of benign (noncancerous) tumor that contains tissues from more than one germ layer (ectoderm, mesoderm, and endoderm).  These cysts typically develop during embryonic development and may contain hair, teeth, bone, or other tissues not normally found at the cyst's location. They are often slow-growing and can occur in various parts of the body, most commonly on the ovaries, face, and head.  |Example:  The surgeon successfully removed the dermoid cyst from the patient's ovary.  外科医生成功地切除了病人卵巢上的畸胎瘤。


The patient presented with a large dermoid cyst on her face, requiring cosmetic surgery. 病人脸上长了一个很大的畸胎瘤，需要做整形手术。|  近义词：teratoma (a more general term for a tumor containing multiple tissue types) |反义词：无|  词性:名词
