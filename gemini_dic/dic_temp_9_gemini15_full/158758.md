plowshare: /ˈplaʊʃeər/ | n. |犁铧；和平的象征 | The metal part of a plow that cuts the soil.  It is also used metaphorically to represent peace and the tools of peaceful cooperation. |例句：The farmer sharpened the plowshare before beginning to till the field.  农民在开始耕地之前磨快了犁铧。 The plowshare symbolizes the hope for lasting peace. 犁铧象征着持久和平的希望。|近义词：share, blade |反义词：sword, weapon
