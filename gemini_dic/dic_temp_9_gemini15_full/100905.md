intuitiveness:/ˌɪntuˈɪtɪvnəs/ | n. |直觉性，直观性 |The ability to understand or know something without any proof or conscious reasoning;  the quality of being intuitive.  It often refers to a quick, instinctive grasp of a situation or problem. |例句：Her intuitiveness allowed her to solve the complex puzzle quickly.  她的直觉使她能够迅速解开复杂的谜题。

例句：The software's intuitiveness makes it easy for even beginners to use. 这个软件的直观性使得即使是初学者也能轻松使用。


近义词：instinct, insight, perception, intuition |反义词：deliberation, analysis, logic
