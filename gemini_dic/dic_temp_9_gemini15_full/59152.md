dynastically: /daɪˈnæstɪkli/ | adv. | 王朝地；世袭地 | In a manner characteristic of or relating to a dynasty; relating to or characteristic of a succession of rulers from the same family.  |例句：The empire was ruled dynastically for centuries.  这个帝国世袭统治了几个世纪。

The country’s history has been shaped dynastically. 这个国家的历史受到了王朝的影响。


近义词：hereditarily, successively, traditionally


反义词：republically, democratically


词性: 副词
