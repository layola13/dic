self-infliction: /ˌsɛlfɪnˈflɪkʃən/ | n. | 自残；自我伤害 | The act of inflicting harm or injury upon oneself.  This can range from minor self-harm to suicide attempts.  It often stems from underlying mental health issues. |例句：His self-infliction was a cry for help. 他的自残行为是在呼救。|例句：The report detailed several cases of self-infliction among the prisoners. 报告详细描述了几起囚犯自残的案例。|近义词：self-harm, self-mutilation, self-abuse |反义词：self-care, self-preservation |名词
