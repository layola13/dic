roads:/roʊdz/| n.|道路，公路|A road is a path or way, especially one that is made of hard material and used by vehicles.  Roads are essential for transportation and connect different places.|例句：The roads were icy and dangerous. 道路结冰且危险。
The roads are congested during rush hour.  高峰期道路拥堵。|近义词：streets, highways, avenues, paths, ways|反义词：None (Antonyms depend on context, e.g., "off-road")|名词

The roads are wide and smooth. = 道路宽阔平坦。
Many roads connect the city to the countryside. = 许多道路连接城市和乡村。

将来时:

被动语态:

疑问句:
