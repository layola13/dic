confluence:/ˈkɒnflʊəns/ /ˈkɑːnflʊəns/| n. |汇合；融合；汇流处 |The point where two or more things, especially rivers, meet and combine.  A merging of different things. |例句：The confluence of the two rivers creates a large lake. 两条河流的汇合处形成一个大湖。

The confluence of art and technology is reshaping our world. 艺术与技术的融合正在重塑我们的世界。


近义词：junction, merging, joining, convergence, meeting |反义词：divergence, separation, division |词性:名词

词源:
Comes from the Latin word *confluere*, meaning "to flow together," from *con-* ("together") + *fluere* ("to flow").  The English word entered the language in the 16th century.
