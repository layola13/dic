maximises:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzɪz/| v.|最大化；使最大限度地发挥作用|To increase or make the most of something.|The company maximises profits by cutting costs.  这家公司通过削减成本将利润最大化。|He maximises his workout efficiency by focusing on compound exercises. 他专注于复合练习以最大限度地提高锻炼效率。|optimize, enhance, amplify|minimize, reduce, diminish|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He maximises his potential. 他最大限度地发挥了自己的潜能。
The system maximises efficiency.  该系统最大限度地提高了效率。

一般过去时:
