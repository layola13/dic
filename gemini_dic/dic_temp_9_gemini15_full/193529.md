self-acceptance: /ˌsɛlf əkˈsɛptəns/ | n. | 自我接纳 | The state of accepting oneself completely, including one's strengths and weaknesses.  It involves acknowledging and embracing all aspects of one's personality, experiences, and physical attributes without judgment or self-criticism. |  She achieved self-acceptance after years of struggling with body image issues. 她经过多年与体型形象问题的斗争后实现了自我接纳。


  He finally found self-acceptance and peace of mind. 他最终找到了自我接纳和内心的平静。| self-love, self-compassion | self-rejection, self-hatred | 名词
