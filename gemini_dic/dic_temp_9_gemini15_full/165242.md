preinspector:/ˌpriːɪnˈspɛktər/| n. |预检员，预先检查员 | A person who conducts a preliminary inspection before a main inspection.  This role often involves identifying potential problems or areas needing attention before a more thorough examination. | Example: The preinspector found several minor defects that needed fixing before the final quality check. 预检员发现了一些需要在最终质量检查前修复的小缺陷。 |近义词：inspector, examiner, surveyor |反义词：N/A
