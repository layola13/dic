premenace:/ˈpriːmɛnəs/| n. |先兆；预兆；潜在的威胁 |A premenace is something that shows signs of being a menace in the future; a potential threat or danger.  It suggests a threat that is not yet fully realized but is likely to develop into a serious problem if left unchecked.| Example: The rapid spread of the disease was a premenace to the entire region.  这种疾病的迅速蔓延对整个地区构成潜在威胁。|近义词：omen, portent, warning sign, harbinger, precursor|反义词：benignity, blessing, safeguard|名词
