gyrland:/ˈɡɪrlænd/| n.|花环，花冠|A garland is a wreath or chain of flowers, leaves, or other material, worn as an ornament or used for decoration.|例句：She wore a garland of roses in her hair. 她头上戴着玫瑰花环。|例句：The Christmas tree was decorated with a garland of tinsel. 圣诞树上装饰着金属丝花环。|近义词：wreath, chaplet|反义词：None
