counterirritate:/ˌkaʊntərˈɪrɪteɪt/| vt.| 反刺激；对抗刺激 | To stimulate an area of the body to relieve pain or irritation in another area.  This is done by creating a counter-stimulus that overrides the original pain signal.  | 例句：The doctor used a counterirritant cream to counterirritate the inflamed area.医生用了一种反刺激药膏来对抗发炎的区域。| 例句： Applying heat to the back can counterirritate a persistent cough. 将热敷在背部可以缓解顽固的咳嗽。|近义词：irritate (in the sense of stimulating to relieve pain), alleviate, soothe|反义词：soothe (directly),  calm
