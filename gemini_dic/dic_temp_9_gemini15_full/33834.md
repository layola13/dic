circumlocutionary: /ˌsɜːrkəmloʊˈkjuːʃənəri/ | adj. | 冗长的；拐弯抹角的；迂回的 |

英文解析:  Using many words where fewer would do, especially in a deliberate attempt to be vague or evasive.  Characterized by circumlocution (the use of many words where fewer would do).

例句:

His circumlocutionary style of speaking made it difficult to understand his point.  他的说话方式冗长，难以理解他的观点。

The politician gave a circumlocutionary answer, avoiding the direct question.  这位政治家给出了一个拐弯抹角的答案，回避了直接的问题。


近义词: indirect, roundabout, evasive, verbose, prolix, rambling

反义词: concise, succinct, direct, brief

词性: adjective
