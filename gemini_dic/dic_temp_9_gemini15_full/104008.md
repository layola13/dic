juice:/dʒuːs/| n.|果汁，汁液|The liquid part of a fruit or vegetable, extracted by squeezing or pressing.  It is often consumed as a beverage. |例句：Orange juice is my favorite breakfast drink.  橙汁是我最喜欢的早餐饮料。

例句：I squeezed the lemon to get some juice for my tea. 我挤压柠檬为我的茶榨取一些汁液。


近义词：extract, nectar, fluid


反义词：pulp (in the context of fruit)


词性:名词
