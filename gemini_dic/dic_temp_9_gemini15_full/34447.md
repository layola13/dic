claver:/ˈklævər/| v.|闲聊，喋喋不休 | To talk continuously and rapidly, especially about unimportant things. |例句：They were clavering away about the latest gossip. 他们喋喋不休地谈论着最新的八卦。|近义词：chatter, babble, prattle |反义词：be silent, be quiet |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They claver incessantly. = 他们没完没了地闲聊。
The children claver about their toys. = 孩子们喋喋不休地谈论着他们的玩具。


一般过去时:
She clavered on about her holiday for hours. = 她喋喋不休地谈论她的假期好几个小时。
They clavered excitedly after the match. = 比赛结束后，他们兴奋地聊了起来。


现在完成时:
They have clavered all afternoon. = 他们整个下午都在闲聊。
She has clavered so much today that she is hoarse. = 她今天说了太多话，嗓子都哑了。


过去完成时:
They had clavered for so long that everyone was tired. = 他们闲聊了很久，以至于每个人都累了。
She had clavered about her problems before I could offer any help. = 我还没来得及提供帮助，她就一直在喋喋不休地抱怨她的问题。


将来时:
They will claver about the movie later. = 他们稍后会聊聊这部电影。
I know they will claver for hours at the reunion. = 我知道他们在聚会上会聊上几个小时。


虚拟语气:
If they clavered less, we could get some work done. = 如果他们少说些闲话，我们就能完成一些工作。
I wish they wouldn't claver so loudly. = 我希望他们不要大声闲聊。


被动语态:
(Claver is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Were they clavering about anything important? = 他们在谈论什么重要的事情吗？
Do they often claver like this? = 他们经常这样闲聊吗？


否定句:
They didn't claver much at the meeting. = 他们在会议上没怎么闲聊。
She doesn't usually claver so much. = 她通常不会说那么多闲话。


PHRASE->
claver away:  不停地闲聊
claver on:  喋喋不休地谈论
claver about:  闲聊关于…
claver with: 和…闲聊
have a claver: 闲聊一会儿
