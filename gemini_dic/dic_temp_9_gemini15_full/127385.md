neaped:/nipt/| adj.|小潮的，潮汐低的| characterized by a neap tide; having a low tide.  Neap tides occur when the sun, Earth, and moon are at right angles, resulting in a smaller difference between high and low tides.| Example: The neaped waters revealed hidden rocks along the shore.  小潮的水位显露出了海岸边隐藏的岩石。|近义词：low,ebb|反义词：spring, high
