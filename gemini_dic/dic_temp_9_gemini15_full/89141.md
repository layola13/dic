homefield:/ˈhoʊmfiːld/| n. |主场；家乡球场|The advantage a team gains by playing on its home field.  It often refers to the psychological and environmental benefits, such as familiar surroundings and supportive crowd, that enhance a team's performance.  |例句：The homefield advantage was clear; the team played with more energy and confidence.  主场优势显而易见；这支球队打得更有活力和信心。

例句：Playing on their homefield gave the team a boost in morale. 在他们的主场作战让球队士气大增。


近义词：home ground, home turf, home advantage |反义词：away field, opponent's field


词源：home (家) + field (场地)  The term is a compound word, combining "home," referring to the location of a team's base, and "field," indicating the playing area.  Its usage naturally evolved from the context of sports, specifically team games played on a designated field.
