nonsaponifiable: /ˌnɒnˈsæpənɪfaɪəbl/ | adj. |不可皂化的 |  Not capable of being saponified; not forming soap when treated with an alkali.  These components are typically lipids that are resistant to hydrolysis by alkali.  | Example:  The nonsaponifiable fraction of the oil contained valuable sterols.  这油的不可皂化部分含有宝贵的甾醇。| 近义词: unsaponifiable | 反义词: saponifiable

词源:
The word "nonsaponifiable" is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not"), the verb "saponify" (to convert fat into soap by treating it with an alkali), and the suffix "-able" (meaning "capable of").  Therefore, it literally means "not capable of being converted into soap".
