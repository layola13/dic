decampment:/dɪˈkæmpmənt/| n. |撤营；迁离；离开 |The act of moving a camp or leaving a place, especially an army or group of people.  It often implies a hasty or sudden departure. |例句：The sudden decampment of the troops surprised the villagers.  军队突然撤营让村民们大吃一惊。

例句：After the heavy rain, the campers decided on a hasty decampment. 大雨过后，露营者决定匆忙撤营。


近义词：departure, evacuation, retreat, removal |反义词：arrival, encampment, settlement |词性：名词
