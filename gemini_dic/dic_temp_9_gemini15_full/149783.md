palaverists:/ˈpæləvərɪsts/| n. |喋喋不休的人；夸夸其谈者 |People who engage in excessive or idle talk; those who talk a great deal, often about unimportant matters.  They may be skilled in persuasion or manipulation through talk. |The palaverists at the meeting dominated the discussion, preventing any real progress.  会议上的喋喋不休的人主导了讨论，阻止了任何真正的进展。|近义词：gossips, chatterboxes, windbags, babblers |反义词：listeners, silent people
