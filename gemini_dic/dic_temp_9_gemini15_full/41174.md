copyediting:/ˈkɑːpiˌɛdɪtɪŋ/|/ˈkɒpiˌedɪtɪŋ/| n. | 校对；编辑；文本润色 |The process of reviewing and correcting written material for errors in grammar, spelling, punctuation, style, and consistency.  It aims to improve clarity, accuracy, and readability. | Example: The manuscript needs thorough copyediting before publication.  这份稿件需要在出版前进行彻底的校对。|近义词：proofreading, editing, revision|反义词：None (反义词的概念在此处不适用)|名词

词源:
The word "copyediting" is a compound word formed from "copy" and "editing".  "Copy" refers to the written material being worked on, and "editing" refers to the process of reviewing and improving it.  Both words have roots in Old English and Middle English.
