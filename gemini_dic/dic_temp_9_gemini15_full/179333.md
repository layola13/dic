redoubler:/ruːˈdʌblər/ | vt. | 加倍；增强；使加倍努力 | To double; to make or become twice as great; to increase the intensity or force of something. | 例句：The company redoubled its efforts to meet the deadline. 公司加倍努力以赶上最后期限。

例句：He redoubled his efforts to learn the language. 他加倍努力学习这门语言。


近义词：reinforce, intensify, strengthen | 反义词：weaken, diminish, lessen | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They redouble their efforts every year. = 他们每年都加倍努力。
The company redoubles its marketing campaign. = 公司加倍努力进行市场营销活动。

一般过去时:
She redoubled her training schedule before the competition. = 她在比赛前加倍了训练计划。
The government redoubled its security measures after the attack. = 袭击发生后，政府加倍加强了安全措施。

现在完成时:
He has redoubled his commitment to the project. = 他加倍致力于这个项目。
The team has redoubled its efforts to win the championship. = 球队加倍努力以赢得冠军。

过去完成时:
They had redoubled their efforts, but it was too late. = 他们已经加倍努力了，但为时已晚。
She had redoubled her precautions before traveling alone. = 她在独自旅行前加倍小心。

将来时:
We will redouble our fundraising efforts next month. = 我们下个月将加倍努力筹款。
The company will redouble its advertising campaign next quarter. = 公司下个季度将加倍投放广告。

虚拟语气:
If we redoubled our efforts, we might succeed. = 如果我们加倍努力，我们可能会成功。
I wish I had redoubled my efforts earlier. = 我真希望我早点加倍努力。

被动语态:
Efforts were redoubled to find a solution. = 加倍努力寻找解决方案。
The security measures were redoubled after the incident. = 事件发生后，安全措施得到了加强。

疑问句:
Will they redouble their commitment to the cause? = 他们会加倍致力于这项事业吗？
Should we redouble our efforts to reach our goals? = 我们应该加倍努力以实现我们的目标吗？

否定句:
They did not redouble their efforts, and the project failed. = 他们没有加倍努力，项目失败了。

词源：来自re- (again) + doubler (to double).  The word essentially means "to double again," implying an intensification of effort or effect.
