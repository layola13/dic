preconfide:/ˌpriːkənˈfaɪd/| vt.| 预先吐露；提前透露；预先告知 | To confide in someone before the usual or expected time.  It implies a disclosure made in advance of a planned or anticipated event or situation. | 例句：He preconfided his plans to his closest friend before making the public announcement. 他在公开宣布之前向他最亲密的朋友预先透露了他的计划。


例句：She preconfided her anxieties to her therapist a week before the surgery. 她在一周后的手术前向她的治疗师预先吐露了她的焦虑。


近义词：foretell, divulge, disclose, reveal, impart


反义词：conceal, keep secret, withhold


词性：动词
