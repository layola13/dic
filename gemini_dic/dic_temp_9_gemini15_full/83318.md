gypper's:/ˈɡɪpərz/| n. |骗子；江湖骗子|A gypper is a swindler or a cheat, often implying a less sophisticated or more opportunistic type of con artist than a sophisticated swindler.|例句：He was accused of being a gypper, always looking for an easy way to make money. 他被指控是个骗子，总是在寻找轻松赚钱的方法。|近义词：swindler, con artist, cheat, sharper|反义词：honest person, trustworthy person|名词
