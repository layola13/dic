revivalism:/rɪˈvaɪvəlɪzəm/| n. |复兴主义；复兴运动 |The religious, social, or cultural movement characterized by fervent religious enthusiasm and the revival of religious beliefs and practices.  It often involves intense emotional experiences, public meetings, and conversions.  |例句：The Second Great Awakening was a period of intense revivalism in the United States.  美国第二次大觉醒时期是美国强烈复兴主义的时期。

例句：The revivalism of traditional crafts has led to a renewed interest in handmade goods. 传统工艺的复兴导致人们对手工制品的兴趣重新燃起。

|近义词：rebirth, regeneration, renewal |反义词：decline, decay, stagnation |名词
