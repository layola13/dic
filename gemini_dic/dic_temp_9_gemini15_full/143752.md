outmanning: /ˌaʊtˈmænɪŋ/ | v. | 超过…的人数；人数超过 | To exceed in number; to outnumber.  | The rebels outmanned the government troops.  叛军人数超过政府军。 | surpass, exceed, outnumber | be outnumbered by, be undermanned | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The enemy outmans our soldiers in this battle. = 在这场战斗中，敌军人数超过我军。
This company outmans its competitors in terms of workforce. = 在员工数量方面，这家公司超过了竞争对手。


一般过去时:
Last year, the company outmanned its rivals by a significant margin. = 去年，该公司在人数上大大超过了竞争对手。
The invading army outmanned the defenders of the city. = 入侵的军队人数超过了城市的守卫者。


现在完成时:
The protesters have outmanned the police in several demonstrations. = 在几次示威中，抗议者的人数超过了警察。
Our team has outmanned the opposition in every game this season. = 本赛季我们的球队在每一场比赛中的人数都超过了对手。


过去完成时:

The rebels had outmanned the government forces before the peace talks began. = 在和平谈判开始之前，叛军已经超过了政府军的人数。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
