preconfesses:/ˌpriːkənˈfɛsɪz/| v.| 预先承认；提前坦白 | To confess beforehand; to admit something before it is discovered or asked about.  |例句：He preconfessed his involvement in the scheme to the police. 他向警方预先承认了他参与该计划。


例句：She preconfessed her mistakes to her boss, hoping for leniency. 她向老板预先承认了自己的错误，希望能得到宽大处理。


近义词：anticipate, preempt, forestall


反义词：conceal, hide, suppress


词性：动词
