gasteropod:/ˈɡæstrəpɒd/|/ˈɡæstrəpɑːd/| n. |腹足动物|A mollusk of the class Gastropoda, such as a snail or slug, having a usually spirally coiled shell (in snails) or no shell (in slugs).  They move by using a muscular foot. |例句：Snails and slugs are common examples of gastropods.  蜗牛和蛞蝓是腹足动物的常见例子。

例句：The gastropod left a silvery trail as it crawled across the leaf.这只腹足动物在叶子上爬行时留下一条银色的痕迹。


近义词：mollusk (more general term), snail (specific type), slug (specific type)
反义词：None (no direct antonym, as it refers to a broad taxonomic group)
词性：noun
