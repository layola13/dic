girded:/ˈɡɜːrdɪd/ | v. |束腰，包围，准备，加强 | To encircle or bind with a belt or band; to prepare oneself for something difficult or challenging; to strengthen or fortify. |例句：She girded herself for the difficult task ahead. 她为即将到来的艰巨任务做好了准备。

例句：He girded his loins and began the climb. 他束紧腰带开始攀登。


近义词：prepared, braced, fortified, encircled


反义词：unprepared, undefended, weakened


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He girds his loins before a strenuous workout. 他在剧烈运动前系紧腰带。
She girds herself with courage before facing her fears. 她鼓起勇气面对恐惧。


一般过去时:
The knight girded on his sword.  骑士佩上了他的剑。
They girded the city with walls. 他们用城墙包围了城市。


现在完成时:
He has girded himself for the upcoming election. 他为即将到来的选举做好了准备。
She has girded her waist with a strong belt. 她用结实的腰带束紧了腰部。


过去完成时:
He had girded himself with prayer before entering the battle. 在进入战斗之前，他通过祈祷来增强勇气。
They had girded the wagons in a circle for protection. 他们为了保护而将马车围成一个圈。


将来时:
She will gird herself with determination to complete the project. 她将下定决心完成这个项目。
He will gird his armor before the fight. 他将在战斗前穿上盔甲。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would gird myself for a long journey. 如果我是你，我会为漫长的旅程做好准备。
I wish I had girded myself with more patience. 我真希望我当时更有耐心。


被动语态:
The city was girded by high walls.  这座城市被高高的城墙包围着。
His waist was girded with a leather belt. 他的腰间系着皮带。


疑问句:
Is he girded for the challenge? 他准备好迎接挑战了吗？
Was the city girded with strong fortifications? 这座城市是否被坚固的防御工事包围？


否定句:
He did not gird himself for the unexpected difficulties. 他没有为意想不到的困难做好准备。
She had not girded her loins properly before the race. 比赛前她没有好好系紧腰带。
