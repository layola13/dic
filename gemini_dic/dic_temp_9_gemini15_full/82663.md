guessable:/ˈɡɛsəbəl/ | adj. | 可猜测的，易于猜测的 | Able to be guessed; easily predictable.  |The answer to the riddle was surprisingly guessable.  谜题的答案出奇地容易猜到。 | predictable, foreseeable | unpredictable, inscrutable | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The puzzle is guessable. = 这个谜题是可以猜到的。
This outcome is guessable. = 这个结果是可以预测的。

一般过去时:
The answer was guessable. = 答案是可以猜到的。
The thief's motive was guessable. = 小偷的动机是可以猜到的。

现在完成时:
The mystery has been guessable all along. = 这个谜团一直以来都是可以猜到的。
The solution has proven guessable. = 解决方案已经被证明是可以猜到的。

过去完成时:
The ending had been guessable from the beginning. = 从一开始结局就是可以猜到的。
The plot twist had been guessable to some. =  情节的转折对一些人来说是可以猜到的。

将来时:
The results will be guessable. = 结果是可以猜到的。
The winner will likely be guessable before the race ends. = 比赛结束前获胜者很可能就能猜到。

虚拟语气:
If the clue were more obvious, the answer would be guessable. = 如果线索更明显，答案就可以猜到了。
I wish the password were guessable. = 我希望密码是可以猜到的。

被动语态:
The answer is easily guessable. = 答案很容易猜到。
The motive is being considered guessable by the detectives. = 侦探们认为动机是可以猜到的。

疑问句:
Is the password guessable? = 密码容易猜到吗？
Is the next move in the game guessable? = 游戏中的下一步行动容易猜到吗？

否定句:
The ending was not guessable. = 结局是无法猜到的。
The criminal's identity was not guessable. = 罪犯的身份是无法猜到的。
