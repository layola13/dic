re-reveal:/ˌriːrɪˈviːl/| vt.|重新揭露；再次展现|To reveal something again, often after it has been hidden or forgotten.  It implies a second unveiling or a renewed emphasis on something already known but perhaps suppressed or overlooked.|例句：The newly discovered documents re-revealed the extent of the government's corruption.  新发现的文件再次揭露了政府腐败的程度。

例句：The artist re-revealed his masterpiece after a decade-long hiatus.艺术家在十年中断后重新展出了他的杰作。


近义词：rediscover, unveil, disclose, expose

反义词：conceal, hide, suppress, obscure

词性：动词
