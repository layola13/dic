hooks:/hʊks/| n. v.|钩子；钩住；吸引|A hook is a curved piece of metal or other material used for catching or holding things.  It can also refer to something that catches someone's attention or interest.|例句：He used a hook to hang the picture on the wall. 他用钩子把画挂在墙上。


The fisherman cast his line, hoping to snag a fish with the hook. 渔夫抛出鱼线，希望用鱼钩钩住一条鱼。|近义词：catch, snare, clasp, grab|反义词：release, unhook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hooks the fish. = 他钩住了鱼。
She hooks her bag onto the coat rack. = 她把包挂在衣帽架上。


一般过去时:
The pirate hooked the treasure chest. = 海盗钩住了宝箱。
I hooked the curtain to the rod. = 我把窗帘钩到了杆子上。


现在完成时:
They have hooked several large fish today. = 他们今天已经钓到几条大鱼了。
She has hooked her dress onto the door handle. = 她把裙子钩在了门把手上。


过去完成时:
He had hooked the bait before casting the line. = 他在抛线之前已经钩好了诱饵。
She had hooked the rug to the floor before the guests arrived. = 客人来之前，她已经把地毯钩到地板上了。


将来时:
I will hook the trailer to the car. = 我将把拖车钩到车上。
They will hook the rope to the ring. = 他们将把绳子钩到环上。


虚拟语气:
If I had a stronger hook, I would catch that fish. = 如果我有一个更结实的鱼钩，我就能钓到那条鱼了。
If she had hooked the dress properly, it wouldn't have ripped. = 如果她把裙子钩好，它就不会撕破了。


被动语态:
The fish was hooked by the angler. = 那条鱼被钓鱼人钩住了。
The picture was hooked onto the wall securely. = 图片被牢固地钩在了墙上。


疑问句:
Is the trailer properly hooked? = 拖车钩好了吗？
Are the curtains hooked to the rod? = 窗帘钩在杆子上了吗？


否定句:
He didn't hook any fish today. = 他今天一条鱼也没钓到。
She couldn't hook the dress onto the hook. = 她无法把裙子钩到钩子上。


PHRASE->
hook up: 连接，搭讪
hook into: 钩住，开始认真做
hooked on: 对...上瘾
get hooked: 上瘾
off the hook: 摆脱困境
