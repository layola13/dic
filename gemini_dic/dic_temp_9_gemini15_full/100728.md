intranet:/ˈɪntrənɛt/ | n. |内部网，内联网 | A private network accessible only to authorized users within an organization.  It typically uses internet technologies but is confined to the organization's internal network. | Example: Our company uses an intranet to share internal documents and communications.  我们的公司使用内联网来共享内部文件和沟通信息。 |近义词：internal network, corporate network |反义词：internet, extranet
