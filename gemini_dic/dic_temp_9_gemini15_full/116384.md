mastlike:/ˈmæstlaɪk/| adj.|像桅杆的；高耸的|Resembling a mast in shape or appearance, typically tall and slender.  The word combines "mast" (a tall upright post, especially on a ship) with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance.|例句：The tall, slender lighthouse stood mastlike against the stormy sky.  高耸的灯塔像桅杆一样矗立在暴风雨的天空下。 The towering skyscraper had a mastlike appearance from afar. 从远处看，高耸的摩天大楼像桅杆一样。|近义词：pole-like, spirelike, columnar|反义词：low-lying, squat, sprawling
