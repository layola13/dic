gainsays:/ˌɡeɪnˈseɪz/| vt.|反驳；否认；违背|To speak or act against; to contradict or deny.  The evidence gainsays his claim of innocence. |近义词：contradict, deny, oppose, refute |反义词：confirm, support, corroborate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Nothing gainsays the power of love. = 没有什么能否定爱的力量。
He gainsays everything I say. = 他反驳我说的每一句话。


一般过去时:
She gainsaid his accusations vehemently. = 她强烈地否认了他的指控。
The evidence gainsaid the defendant's alibi. = 证据驳斥了被告的不在场证明。


现在完成时:
His actions have gainsaid his earlier promises. = 他的行为违背了他之前的承诺。
No evidence has gainsaid the scientific findings. = 没有证据反驳科学发现。


过去完成时:
The witness testimony had gainsaid the initial police report. = 目击证人的证词驳斥了最初的警方的报告。
Historical records had gainsaid the legend's authenticity. = 历史记录反驳了这个传说的真实性。


将来时:
Nothing will gainsay the truth. = 没有什么能够否定真相。
His actions will gainsay his good intentions. = 他的行为将违背他的良好意图。


虚拟语气:
If only his actions did not gainsay his words. = 如果他的行为没有违背他的话就好了。
I wish the evidence didn’t gainsay her testimony. = 我希望证据没有反驳她的证词。


被动语态:
His claims were gainsaid by the court. = 法院驳斥了他的说法。
The theory was gainsaid by later research. = 这一理论被后来的研究推翻了。


疑问句:
Does anything gainsay the obvious truth? = 有什么能否定显而易见的真相吗？
Can we gainsay the power of nature? = 我们能违抗自然的力量吗？


否定句:
The evidence does not gainsay his innocence. = 证据并没有否定他的清白。
Nothing could gainsay the overwhelming evidence. = 没有什么能够反驳压倒性的证据。


PHRASE->
gainsay the truth = 否定真相
gainsay one's claim = 反驳某人的说法
gainsay a fact = 否认一个事实
gainsay the evidence = 反驳证据
cannot gainsay = 无法反驳


词源:
The word "gainsays" comes from the Old English "gegn-secgan," composed of "gegn" (against) and "secgan" (to say).  It literally means "to say against."
