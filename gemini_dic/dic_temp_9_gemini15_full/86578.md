heliocentric: /ˌhiːliəʊˈsentrɪk/ | adj. | 日心说的；以太阳为中心的 | Relating to the astronomical model in which the sun is considered to be the center of the universe, with the Earth and other planets revolving around it.  | Example: The heliocentric theory, proposed by Copernicus, revolutionized our understanding of the cosmos.  日心说，由哥白尼提出，彻底改变了我们对宇宙的理解。|近义词：solar-centered | 反义词：geocentric
