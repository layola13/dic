seethe:/siːð/| v.|沸腾；怒火中烧|To feel or show strong anger or excitement.  The word implies a suppressed, bubbling anger, rather than an outward explosion.|例句：He seethed with anger at the injustice. 他因不公正而怒火中烧。|例句：The pot of stew seethed on the stove.  炖锅里的炖菜在炉子上沸腾。|近义词：boil, fume, rage, simmer|反义词：calm, tranquil, placid
