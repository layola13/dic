orcharded:/ˈɔːrtʃərdɪd/| adj.|果园种植的；长满果树的|Having or relating to orchards or the planting of orchards.  The term often implies a landscape abundant with fruit trees.|例句：The orcharded hillsides were a beautiful sight in the spring. 春天果园遍布的山坡景色优美。The orcharded area produced a bountiful harvest this year.  今年果园的收成十分丰盛。|近义词：fruited, tree-lined|反义词：barren, treeless
