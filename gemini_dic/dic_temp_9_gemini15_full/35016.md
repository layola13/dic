cloning:/ˈkloʊnɪŋ/| n. v.|克隆；复制|The process of producing genetically identical copies of a biological entity.  This can refer to genes, cells, tissues, or even whole organisms. Cloning is achieved through various methods, including somatic cell nuclear transfer and gene cloning. |例句：Scientists successfully cloned a sheep named Dolly. 科学家成功克隆了一只名叫多莉的羊。

例句：The company is using cloning to produce large quantities of a specific plant. 这家公司正在利用克隆技术生产大量特定植物。

近义词：duplication, replication, reproduction|反义词：uniqueness, originality|词性:noun, verb
