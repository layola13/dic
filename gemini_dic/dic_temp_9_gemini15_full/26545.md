cabinetmaking: /ˈkæbɪnɛtˌmeɪkɪŋ/ | n. | 木柜制作；细木工 | The craft or occupation of making cabinets and other fine wooden furniture.  It involves designing, constructing, and finishing high-quality pieces. | The antique cabinetmaking in this shop is exquisite. 这家店的古董细木工活非常精美。  The course covered all aspects of cabinetmaking, from design to finishing. 这门课程涵盖了细木工的各个方面，从设计到完工。| carpentry, joinery | (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with mass-produced furniture)
