orchard:/ˈɔːrtʃərd/ | n. |果园 | An area of land where fruit trees are grown.  |例句：The orchard was filled with the scent of ripe apples.  这个果园里充满了成熟苹果的香味。|例句：We visited a beautiful orchard in the countryside and picked our own strawberries. 我们参观了乡下的一座美丽的果园，并自己采摘草莓。|近义词：grove, plantation |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a field of crops.
