postpalatal: /ˌpoʊstˈpælətl/ | adj. | 后腭的 | Relating to the area behind the hard palate in the mouth.  This area is involved in the production of certain speech sounds. | The postpalatal region is crucial for the articulation of some consonants. 后腭区对于某些辅音的发音至关重要。 |近义词：velar (partially),  pharyngeal (partially) |反义词：prepalatal
