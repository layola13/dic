laserdiscs: /ˈleɪzərˌdɪsks/ | n. |激光唱片|A laserdisc is a large-diameter optical disc that was used for storing and playing movies and other video content.  It offered higher quality video than VHS tapes but was eventually superseded by DVDs and other digital formats. |例句：My dad used to have a huge collection of laserdiscs. = 我爸爸以前收藏了很多激光唱片。

例句：We rented a laserdisc player to watch the movie in high definition. = 我们租了一台激光影碟机来观看高清电影。
