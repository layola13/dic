deluding: /dɪˈluːdɪŋ/ | v. | 欺骗；迷惑；哄骗 | To deceive someone into believing something that is not true.  | 例句：He was deluding himself into thinking he could win. 他自欺欺人地认为自己能赢。 | 例句：The magician deluded the audience with his clever tricks. 魔术师用他精巧的技巧迷惑了观众。 | 近义词： deceiving, misleading, fooling, cheating | 反义词：enlightening, informing, clarifying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He deludes himself with false hopes. = 他用虚假的希望欺骗自己。
They delude the public with their propaganda. = 他们用宣传欺骗公众。

一般过去时:
The salesman deluded the customer into buying a faulty product. = 销售员欺骗顾客买了一个有缺陷的产品。
She deluded herself that everything would be alright. = 她自欺欺人地认为一切都会好起来的。

现在完成时:
He has deluded himself for years. = 他多年来一直欺骗自己。
They have deluded many people with their lies. = 他们用谎言欺骗了许多人。

过去完成时:
She had deluded herself into believing his promises. = 她曾自欺欺人地相信了他的承诺。
They had deluded the investors before the scandal broke. = 丑闻爆发前，他们欺骗了投资者。

将来时:
He will delude himself again if he’s not careful. = 如果他不小心，他会再次欺骗自己。
The con artist will delude more victims with his new scheme. = 骗子将用他的新计划欺骗更多受害者。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't been deluded by his charm, I wouldn't have trusted him. = 如果我没有被他迷人的外表所迷惑，我就不会相信他了。
I wish I hadn't been deluded into believing his lies. = 我真希望我没有被他的谎言所欺骗。

被动语态:
The public was deluded by the false advertising. = 公众被虚假广告所欺骗。
Many people are being deluded by fake news. = 许多人正在被假新闻所欺骗。

疑问句:
Were you deluded by his promises? = 你被他那些承诺所欺骗了吗？
Is she deluding herself about her chances of success? = 她是否对成功的几率抱有不切实际的幻想？

否定句:
He did not delude anyone; he told the truth. = 他没有欺骗任何人；他说的是实话。
She wasn't deluded by his flattery; she saw through him. = 她没有被他的奉承所迷惑；她看穿了他。


PHRASE->
be deluded by = 被…迷惑

delude someone into doing something = 欺骗某人去做某事
be deluded about = 对…抱有幻想
free from delusion = 摆脱幻想
