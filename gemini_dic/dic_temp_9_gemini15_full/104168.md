juniper: /ˈdʒuːnɪpər/ | n. | 杜松 | A coniferous evergreen shrub or tree of the cypress family, having needle-like leaves and berry-like cones.  | Example: The juniper bushes provided shelter from the wind.  杜松灌木丛为我们遮挡了风。|近义词：cedar, cypress |反义词：无
