methodism:/ˈmɛθədɪzəm/| n. |卫理公会；卫理宗；方法论 |The system of Christian belief and practice founded by John Wesley, characterized by its emphasis on personal piety, social reform, and evangelism.  Methodism is a Protestant denomination that originated in England in the 18th century.  |例句：The Methodist church played a significant role in the social reforms of the 19th century. 卫理公会教堂在19世纪的社会改革中发挥了重要作用。

例句：His approach to problem-solving demonstrates a strong sense of methodism. 他解决问题的方法体现了很强的系统性。


近义词：Protestantism, evangelism, pietism |反义词：无
