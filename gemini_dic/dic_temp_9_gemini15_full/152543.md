pci:/ˈpiːsaɪ/|abbr.|外围组件互连|Peripheral Component Interconnect|PCI is a local bus standard used to connect peripherals to a computer motherboard.  It allows for high-speed data transfer between the CPU and various devices such as graphics cards, sound cards, and network adapters.|Example: My computer's PCI slot is broken, so I can't install a new sound card. 我的电脑PCI插槽坏了，所以我无法安装新的声卡。|近义词：PCIe (PCI Express)|反义词：N/A|abbr.
