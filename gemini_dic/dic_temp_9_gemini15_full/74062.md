fossilate:/ˈfɒsɪleɪt/| /ˈfɑːsəleɪt/ | vt. |使变成化石；使僵化；使陈腐|To change something into a fossil; to become fixed or rigid, losing flexibility or originality.  |例句：The ancient city was gradually fossilized by the sands of time.  古老的城市逐渐被时间的沙尘石化了。

The rapid technological advancements fossilized many traditional manufacturing processes. 快速的技术进步使许多传统的制造工艺变得僵化。

近义词： petrify, ossify, solidify | 反义词：revitalize, modernize, innovate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The museum fossilizes many important artifacts. = 博物馆保存着许多重要的文物。（此处用fossilize指保存，并非完全符合词义，但可理解为一种“石化”式的保存）
He fossilizes his ideas and refuses to change. = 他固执己见，拒绝改变。


一般过去时:
The volcanic eruption fossilized the prehistoric forest. = 火山爆发使史前森林变成了化石。
The old traditions were fossilized by generations of adherence. = 这些古老的传统由于几代人的坚持而变得僵化。


现在完成时:
The rapid changes have fossilized many of our old practices. = 快速的变化使我们许多旧的习惯变得僵化。
Millions of years of pressure have fossilized these ancient bones. = 数百万年的压力使这些古老的骨头变成了化石。


过去完成时:
Before the discovery, scientists believed the dinosaurs had already fossilized completely. = 在发现之前，科学家们认为恐龙已经完全变成了化石。
The company's policies had fossilized over decades of unchanging management. = 公司的政策在几十年不变的管理下变得僵化。


将来时:
New technologies will likely fossilize some current jobs. = 新技术可能会使某些现有工作岗位过时并消失。
The artist's style will fossilize in the art history books. = 艺术家的风格将在艺术史书籍中被定型。


虚拟语气:
If the organism hadn't been buried quickly, it wouldn't have fossilized. = 如果生物体没有被迅速掩埋，它就不会变成化石。
If the company had embraced change, it wouldn't have fossilized its operations. = 如果公司接受改变，它就不会使它的运营僵化。


被动语态:
Many ancient plants and animals were fossilized by natural processes. = 许多古代动植物通过自然过程变成了化石。
The old ideas are being fossilized by the resistance to new approaches. = 旧观念由于抵制新方法而变得僵化。


疑问句:
Will these footprints fossilize over time? = 这些脚印会随着时间的推移变成化石吗？
Has the company's culture fossilized to the point of stagnation? = 公司文化是否已经僵化到停滞不前的程度？


否定句:
The remains did not fossilize due to the lack of proper conditions. = 由于缺乏合适的条件，遗骸没有变成化石。
The government's approach did not fossilize its policies, adapting to new challenges. = 政府的做法并没有使它的政策僵化，而是适应了新的挑战。
