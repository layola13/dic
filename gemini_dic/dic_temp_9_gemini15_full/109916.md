libel:/ˈlaɪbəl/| n. |诽谤；中伤；(文字)诽谤罪| The act of publishing a false statement that injures someone's reputation.  It is a crime and a tort.  Libel is typically written, whereas slander is spoken. |例句：He was sued for libel after writing a defamatory article about the politician. 他因撰写一篇诽谤该政治家的文章而被起诉诽谤。

例句：The magazine was found guilty of libel for publishing false accusations against the celebrity. 这本杂志因发表针对该名人的虚假指控而被判犯有诽谤罪。


近义词：defamation, calumny, slander (slander is spoken libel)|反义词：praise, commendation, exoneration|名词
