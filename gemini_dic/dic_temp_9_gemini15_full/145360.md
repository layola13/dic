overburned: /ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrnd/ | adj. | 烧焦的；过度燃烧的 |  Overburned describes something that has been burned excessively, resulting in charring or damage beyond what is intended or desirable.  It implies a degree of damage greater than simply "burned". |  例句：The toast was overburned and inedible.  烤面包烧焦了，不能吃了。

The steak was overburned on the grill.  牛排在烤架上烤焦了。


近义词：charred, scorched, incinerated, singed | 反义词：undercooked, raw, unburnt


词性:形容词
