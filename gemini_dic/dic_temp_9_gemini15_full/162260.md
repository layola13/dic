pre-washes:/ˌpriːˈwɒʃɪz/| n.|预洗；预先洗涤|Pre-washes are a crucial step in preparing fabrics for dyeing or other finishing processes. 预洗是准备织物进行染色或其他整理工艺的关键步骤。|近义词：pre-treatments, preliminary washes|反义词：final washes, post-washes|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The laundry service offers pre-washes for all garments. = 洗衣服务为所有衣物提供预洗服务。
Pre-washes remove excess dirt and impurities. = 预洗去除多余的污垢和杂质。


一般过去时:
The factory pre-washed all the cotton before dyeing. = 工厂在染色前对所有棉花进行了预洗。
She pre-washed the delicate fabrics to protect them. = 她预洗了精致的织物以保护它们。

虚拟语气:
