distiller:/dɪˈstɪlər/ | n. | 蒸馏器；酿酒者 | A distiller is a person or apparatus that distills liquids, especially alcoholic beverages.  A distiller separates the components or substances of a liquid mixture by using evaporation and condensation. |例句：The whisky distiller uses a unique process to create its smooth flavour. 这家威士忌酒厂采用独特的工艺来酿造其醇厚的酒味。  The distiller separated the water from the alcohol. 蒸馏器将水和酒精分离。|近义词：refiner, brewer (for alcoholic beverages)|反义词：blender (in some contexts) |名词
