do-for:/duː fɔːr/| phrasal verb|为了...而做；对...有用|This phrase indicates the purpose or benefit of an action. It essentially asks "what is the reason for doing something?" or "what does this achieve?".|例句：What do you do it for? = 你为什么要这么做？ What are you doing that for? = 你为什么要那样做？|近义词:to, in order to, for the sake of|反义词:against, despite
