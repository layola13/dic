muley:/ˈmjuːli/| adj.|脾气暴躁的；倔强的；难以驾驭的|  A muley animal is one that is stubborn and difficult to control, often used to describe horses, mules, or other livestock.  It can also refer to a person with a similar temperament. |例句：The muley horse refused to budge from the spot. 这匹倔强的马不肯从原地动弹。

The rancher had a muley cow that always caused trouble.  这位牧场主有一头脾气暴躁的母牛，总是惹麻烦。|近义词：stubborn, obstinate, intractable, headstrong |反义词：docile, compliant, manageable, amenable
