mycetes: /maɪˈsiːtiːz/ | n. |菌类；真菌 |  Fungi; a class of organisms that includes yeasts, molds, and mushrooms.  They are characterized by their lack of chlorophyll and their ability to reproduce by spores. Mycetes are crucial in decomposition and nutrient cycling. | Example:  The study focused on the diverse mycetes found in the rainforest. 这项研究的重点是热带雨林中发现的多样性菌类。 | 近义词：fungi, molds | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用植物等来对比)  |  词性: 名词
