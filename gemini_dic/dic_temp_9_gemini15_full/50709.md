dermopteran:/dɜːrˈmɒptərən/| n. |飞狐, 鼯猴|A dermopteran is a nocturnal, gliding mammal belonging to the order Dermoptera. They are characterized by their patagium, a membrane of skin stretching from their neck to their limbs and tail, enabling them to glide between trees.  They are found in Southeast Asia. |Example:  The colugo, also known as a flying lemur, is a type of dermopteran.|近义词:colugo, flying lemur |反义词:  (no direct antonym;  consider contrasting terms like "non-gliding mammal")
