grossbeak:/ˈɡroʊsbiːk/|/ˈɡrəʊsbiːk/| n. |大嘴鸟|A bird with a large, strong beak, typically seed-eating.|Example: The grosbeak perched on the branch, cracking open a sunflower seed with its powerful beak.  大嘴鸟栖息在树枝上，用它强壮的喙敲开葵花籽。|近义词：hawfinch,  cardinal (some species)|反义词：None specifically, but birds with small, delicate beaks could be considered antonyms in terms of beak size.|名词
