culpable:/ˈkʌlpəbəl/| adj.|应受谴责的；有罪的；该负责的|Deserving blame or punishment.  It implies guilt or responsibility for wrongdoing.|The driver was culpable in the accident.  司机应对这起事故负责。|The jury found him culpable of murder. 陪审团认定他犯有谋杀罪。|guilty, blameworthy, at fault|innocent, blameless, faultless


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is culpable for the damage. = 他应对造成的损害负责。
She is culpable of negligence. = 她犯有玩忽职守的罪过。


一般过去时:
He was culpable in the incident. = 他在这起事件中应受谴责。
The company was culpable for the environmental damage. = 公司应对环境破坏负责。


现在完成时:
He has been culpable in many such incidents. = 他在许多此类事件中都应受谴责。
The government has been culpable in its handling of the crisis. = 政府在处理危机方面负有责任。


过去完成时:
It turned out that he had been culpable all along. = 原来他一直都应受谴责。
They discovered that the manager had been culpable in the fraud. = 他们发现经理在欺诈案中负有责任。


将来时:
He will be culpable if he continues this behavior. = 如果他继续这种行为，他将受到谴责。
The company will be culpable if they fail to address the safety concerns. = 如果公司未能解决安全问题，它将受到谴责。


虚拟语气:
If he were culpable, he would have been punished. = 如果他犯有罪过，他就会受到惩罚。
I wish the government were not culpable in this matter. = 我希望政府在这件事上没有责任。


被动语态:
He was found culpable by the court. = 他被法院认定有罪。
The company was deemed culpable for the pollution. = 公司被认为应对污染负责。


疑问句:
Is he culpable for the accident? = 他应对这起事故负责吗？
Was the company culpable in the scandal? = 公司在这场丑闻中是否应受谴责？


否定句:
He is not culpable in this matter. = 他在这件事上没有责任。
The evidence shows that she is not culpable. = 证据表明她没有责任。


PHRASE->
be culpable of = 对…负有责任
