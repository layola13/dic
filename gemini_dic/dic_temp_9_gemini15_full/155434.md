phenanthrene: /ˈfɛnænθriːn/ | n. | 菲，菲纳烯 | A colorless, crystalline polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbon found in coal tar. It is a structural isomer of anthracene and is used in the synthesis of various organic compounds. |  Phenanthrene is a crucial component in the production of certain dyes and pharmaceuticals. | 近义词: anthracene (structural isomer) | 反义词:  (no direct antonym)
