plasticizes:/ˈplæstɪsaɪzɪz/| vt.| 使塑化；使柔软；增塑 | To make (a material, especially a polymer) more pliable or flexible by incorporating a plasticizer.  This process reduces the brittleness and increases the workability of the material. | The manufacturer plasticizes the PVC to make it more flexible for use in clothing. 制造商对PVC进行塑化处理，使其更柔软，便于用于服装。 The addition of plasticizers plasticizes the resin, making it suitable for injection molding. 增塑剂的加入使树脂塑化，使其适合注塑成型。 | soften, flexibilize | harden, solidify | 动词
