get rid of:/ɡɛt ˈrɪd əv/ | phrasal verb |摆脱；除掉；处理掉 | To remove or eliminate something unwanted or undesirable.  |例句：I need to get rid of these old clothes. 我需要处理掉这些旧衣服。

例句：We got rid of the clutter in the garage. 我们清理了车库里的杂物。


近义词：dispose of, eliminate, remove, discard, get shot of

反义词：acquire, obtain, keep, retain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I get rid of my old books every year. = 我每年都处理掉旧书。
She gets rid of her trash regularly. = 她定期清理垃圾。


一般过去时:
He got rid of his old car last week. = 他上周处理掉了他的旧车。
They got rid of the problem quickly. = 他们迅速解决了这个问题。


现在完成时:
I have got rid of all my old clothes. = 我已经处理掉了所有旧衣服。
She has got rid of her bad habits. = 她已经改掉了坏习惯。


过去完成时:
He had got rid of the evidence before the police arrived. = 警官到达之前，他已经销毁了证据。
They had got rid of the pests before the harvest. = 收获前他们已经除掉了害虫。


将来时:
I will get rid of this junk tomorrow. = 我明天会处理掉这些垃圾。
She will get rid of her anxiety with therapy. = 她将通过治疗来消除焦虑。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would get rid of that old furniture. = 如果我是你，我会处理掉那些旧家具。
I wish I could get rid of this headache. = 我希望我能摆脱这个头痛。


被动语态:
The old building was got rid of to make way for a new park. = 那栋旧建筑被拆除，为新公园腾出空间。
The problem is being got rid of gradually. = 这个问题正在逐步解决。


疑问句:
How did you get rid of the ants? = 你是怎么除掉蚂蚁的？
What's the best way to get rid of weeds? = 除草的最佳方法是什么？


否定句:
I can't get rid of this cough. = 我治不好这个咳嗽。
He couldn't get rid of the stain on his shirt. = 他洗不掉衬衫上的污渍。
