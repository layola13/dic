encircles: /ɪnˈsɜːrkəlz/ | vt. |环绕；包围；围绕 | to surround something completely | The river encircles the town. 这条河环绕着这个小镇。  The planets encircle the sun. 行星环绕太阳运行。|近义词：surrounds, encloses, encompasses |反义词：leaves, abandons

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The moon encircles the earth. = 月亮环绕地球运行。
The wall encircles the garden. = 这堵墙环绕着花园。


一般过去时:
The soldiers encircled the enemy. = 士兵们包围了敌人。
The police encircled the building. = 警察包围了那座建筑物。


现在完成时:
The vines have encircled the old tree. = 爬山虎已经缠绕了那棵老树。
The rumor has encircled the whole community. = 谣言已经传遍了整个社区。


过去完成时:
The army had encircled the city before the attack. = 军队在进攻之前已经包围了这座城市。
The police had encircled the suspect's house before he could escape. = 警察在他逃跑之前已经包围了他的房子。


将来时:
The spaceship will encircle the planet three times. = 宇宙飞船将绕行星运行三圈。
They will encircle the mountain with a new road. = 他们将用一条新路环绕这座山。


虚拟语气:
If the road encircled the lake, it would be a beautiful sight. = 如果这条路环绕着湖泊，那将是一幅美丽的景象。
I wish the fence encircled our entire property. = 我希望围栏能围绕我们的整个房产。


被动语态:
The city was encircled by the enemy troops. = 这座城市被敌军包围了。
The area is encircled by a high wall. = 该区域被高墙环绕。


疑问句:
Does the road encircle the mountain? = 这条路环绕着山吗？
Does the fence encircle the whole garden? = 围栏环绕整个花园吗？


否定句:
The path doesn't encircle the lake completely. = 这条小路并没有完全环绕湖泊。
The ring doesn't encircle her finger properly. = 这枚戒指没有戴在她手指上。


PHRASE->
encircle with = 用…包围
encircle by = 被…包围
completely encircle = 完全包围
tightly encircle = 紧紧包围
slowly encircle = 缓缓包围

词源:
en- (表示“使…；使成为…”) + circle (圆圈；环绕)  The prefix "en-" indicates to cause to be in a state or condition, and "circle" means a round shape. Combining these elements, "encircle" means to cause something to be surrounded in a circle.
