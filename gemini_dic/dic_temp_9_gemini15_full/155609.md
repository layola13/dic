philosophist:/fɪˈlɒsəfɪst/ | n. |哲学家 |A philosopher is someone who studies or writes about philosophy.  They engage in critical thinking about fundamental questions concerning existence, knowledge, values, reason, mind, and language.  |Example: Plato was a renowned philosopher who greatly influenced Western thought.  柏拉图是一位著名的哲学家，对西方思想产生了巨大的影响。|近义词：thinker, scholar, metaphysician |反义词：N/A
