pricker:/ˈprɪkər/| n. |刺，尖物；扎人的人；（尤指）挑剔的人 | A pricker is a pointed instrument used for piercing or pricking something.  It can also refer to a person who is critical or fault-finding. |例句：The gardener used a pricker to thin out the seedlings. 园丁用刺针疏苗。 He's such a pricker; he always finds fault with everything. 他真是个挑剔的人；他总是对一切吹毛求疵。|近义词：thorn, point, critic, fault-finder |反义词：praiser, flatterer
