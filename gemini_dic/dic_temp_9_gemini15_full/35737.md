cochairmen:/ˈkoʊtʃɛrmən/| n. |共同主席；联合主席 |Two people who jointly chair a meeting or organization.  It refers to two people sharing the responsibilities and authority of a chairperson.|The co-chairmen opened the meeting with a joint statement. 共同主席以联合声明开会。 The co-chairmen of the committee worked collaboratively to achieve their goals. 委员会的共同主席们通力合作以实现他们的目标。|chairmen, joint chairs| (反义词无直接对应，可考虑single chairman) |名词
