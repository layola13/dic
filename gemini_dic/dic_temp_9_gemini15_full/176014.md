ravaged:/ˈrævɪdʒd/| adj. v.| 毁坏的；破坏的；蹂躏的 | severely damaged; devastated; laid waste | The hurricane ravaged the coastal town, leaving behind a trail of destruction.  飓风摧毁了沿海城镇，留下一片废墟。|近义词：destroyed, devastated, ruined, wrecked | 反义词：restored, repaired, rebuilt |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The war ravages the country. = 战争蹂躏着这个国家。
The disease ravages the population. = 疾病肆虐着人口。


一般过去时:
The fire ravaged the forest. = 大火烧毁了森林。
The flood ravaged the village. = 洪水摧毁了村庄。


现在完成时:
The drought has ravaged the farmland. = 干旱已经摧毁了农田。
Years of conflict have ravaged the nation. = 多年的冲突已经摧毁了这个国家。


过去完成时:
The army had ravaged the countryside before the peace treaty was signed. = 在和平条约签署之前，军队已经蹂躏了乡村。
The disease had ravaged the community before the cure was discovered. = 在治愈方法被发现之前，这种疾病已经摧毁了这个社区。


将来时:
If we don't act now, the insects will ravage the crops. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，昆虫将会毁坏庄稼。
The storm is expected to ravage the coastline. = 预计风暴将摧毁海岸线。


虚拟语气:
If the storm had come earlier, it would have ravaged the city more severely. = 如果风暴早点来，它会更严重地摧毁这座城市。
I wish the war hadn't ravaged our homeland. = 我希望战争没有摧毁我们的家园。


被动语态:
The city was ravaged by the earthquake. = 城市被地震摧毁了。
The landscape was ravaged by years of neglect. = 这片土地因多年的忽视而遭到破坏。


疑问句:
Was the village ravaged by the storm? = 村庄被风暴摧毁了吗？
Has the forest been ravaged by fire? = 森林被火烧毁了吗？


否定句:
The army did not ravage the villages. = 军队没有摧毁村庄。
The disease did not ravage the whole country. = 疾病并没有摧毁整个国家。


PHRASE->

ravaged by drought = 被干旱摧毁
ravaged by time = 被时间侵蚀
