procensorship: /proʊˈsɛnsərʃɪp/ | n. | 预先审查；事先审查 | The act of censoring something before it is published or released.  It often involves reviewing material for potentially offensive or harmful content before it reaches the public.  | Example: The government imposed strict procensorship on all news reports related to the political unrest.  政府对所有与政治动乱相关的新闻报道实行严格的预先审查。 | 近义词：pre-censorship, prior review | 反义词：post-censorship, no censorship |  名词
