paths:/pæθs/| n.|路径；道路；方法；途径|A path is a track or way, especially one that is made or worn by use.  It can also refer to a course of action or a line of development.|例句：We followed a narrow path through the woods. 我们沿着一条狭窄的小路穿过树林。

例句：There are many paths to success. 成功之路有很多条。


近义词：route, way, track, course, avenue


反义词：destination, end


词源：古英语 *path, from Proto-Germanic *pathō, from Proto-Indo-European *pet- ("to go").


USAGE->

The path leads to the village. = 这条路通向村庄。
Many paths connect the towns. = 许多道路连接着这些城镇。


一般过去时:

将来时:

被动语态:
