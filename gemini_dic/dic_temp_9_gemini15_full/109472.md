leporine:/ˈlepərɪn/| adj.|兔的；兔似的；像兔子的|Relating to or resembling a hare or rabbit.  The word often describes features characteristic of rabbits, such as long ears, a cleft lip, or a timid nature.|例句：The child had a leporine cleft lip.  孩子有兔唇。

例句：His long ears gave him a distinctly leporine appearance. 他长长的耳朵使他看起来很像兔子。


近义词：rabbit-like, hare-like


反义词：无


词性：形容词
