furnish:/ˈfɜːrnɪʃ/ | vt. |供应，提供；布置，装修；装备 | To supply or provide something needed or wanted.  To provide or equip (a room, building, etc.) with furniture or equipment. |例句：The hotel furnishes all the rooms with towels and soap.  这家酒店为所有房间提供毛巾和肥皂。

They furnished the apartment with antique furniture. 他们用古董家具布置了公寓。|近义词：supply, equip, provide, decorate, fit out |反义词：strip, empty, deplete |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They furnish the apartment with modern furniture. = 他们用现代家具布置公寓。
The company furnishes its employees with laptops. = 公司为员工提供笔记本电脑。


一般过去时:
She furnished the house with beautiful paintings. = 她用美丽的绘画装饰了房子。
They furnished the classroom with new desks and chairs. = 他们用新的课桌椅布置了教室。


现在完成时:
We have furnished the guest room for our visitors. = 我们已经为客人布置了客房。
They have furnished the report with all the necessary data. = 他们已经用所有必要的数据完成了报告。


过去完成时:
They had furnished the house before the party. = 他们在派对前已经布置好了房子。
She had furnished the police with all the information she knew. = 她已经向警方提供了她知道的所有信息。


将来时:
They will furnish the new office with ergonomic chairs. = 他们将用人体工学座椅布置新办公室。
We will furnish you with the details as soon as possible. = 我们将尽快向您提供详细信息。


虚拟语气:
If they furnished the room differently, it would look much better. = 如果他们以不同的方式布置房间，它看起来会好得多。
I wish they furnished the house with better quality furniture. = 我希望他们用更高质量的家具布置房子。


被动语态:
The room was furnished with antique furniture. = 房间里摆满了古董家具。
The report will be furnished to the board next week. = 报告将于下周提交给董事会。


疑问句:
Does the hotel furnish towels and toiletries? = 酒店提供毛巾和洗漱用品吗？
Will they furnish the necessary equipment for the project? = 他们会为这个项目提供必要的设备吗？


否定句:
The apartment wasn't furnished when we moved in. = 我们搬进去的时候，公寓还没有装修好。
They didn't furnish the report with enough evidence. = 他们没有在报告中提供足够的证据。
