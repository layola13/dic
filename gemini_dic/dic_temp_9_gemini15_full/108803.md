leatheriness:/ˈlɛðəriˌnɛs/ | n. |皮革般的感觉；坚韧；粗糙 |The quality or state of being leathery;  a tough, often inelastic texture resembling leather.  It can also refer to a hardened, inflexible or insensitive quality. |例句：The old leather boots had developed a distinct leatheriness. 这双旧皮靴已经有了明显的皮革感。

The sun had baked the apples until they had a strange leatheriness. 太阳把苹果晒得干巴巴的，摸上去像皮革一样。

近义词：toughness, rigidity, hardness, inelasticity |反义词：softness, suppleness, flexibility


词源：源自leather (皮革) + -iness (名词后缀，表示“性质”、“状态”)。  The suffix "-iness" transforms the adjective "leathery" into a noun describing the condition or quality of being leathery.
