reducers:/rɪˈduːsərz/| n.|化简器；还原剂；减少器|A reducer is a device or substance that reduces something, often in the context of size, amount, or chemical reaction.  In computer science, it's a function that takes multiple input values and returns a single output value. |例句：The factory uses a special chemical reducer to process the waste.  这家工厂使用一种特殊的化学还原剂来处理废物。

例句：In Redux, reducers update the application state. 在Redux中，reducers更新应用程序状态。|近义词：diminisher, lessener, converter |反义词：augmenter, enlarger


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine reduces the size of the paper. = 机器缩小纸张的尺寸。
This chemical reduces the risk of infection. = 这种化学物质降低了感染的风险。


一般过去时:
The engineer reduced the engine's noise. = 工程师降低了发动机的噪音。

现在完成时:

The company has reduced its operating costs. = 公司已经降低了运营成本。


过去完成时:

将来时:
