pujas:/ˈpuːdʒɑːz/ | n. |普迦节，祭祀仪式 | Religious offerings or worship, especially in Hinduism.  The word often refers to the act of worship itself, as well as the offerings made during the worship.  It can also refer to a festival celebrating a particular deity. | Example:  Many devotees attend the daily pujas at the temple.  许多信徒参加寺庙的每日普迦仪式。

The pujas during Diwali are particularly vibrant and colourful.  排灯节期间的普迦仪式尤其热闹多彩。


近义词：worship, offerings, rituals, ceremonies | 反义词：N/A

词源:
The word "puja" comes from the Sanskrit word पूजा (pūjā), meaning "worship," "reverence," or "veneration."  It reflects the central act of devotion in Hinduism and other related religions.
