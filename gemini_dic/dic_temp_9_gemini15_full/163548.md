precostal: /ˌpriːˈkɒstəl/ | adj. |肋骨前的，肋骨之前的| Situated or occurring in front of a rib or ribs.  The term is primarily used in anatomical contexts to describe the location of structures relative to the ribs. |Example: The precostal muscles are located anterior to the ribs. 肋骨前的肌肉位于肋骨前方。|近义词:anterior, ventral |反义词:posterior, dorsal
