pawns:/pɔːnz/ | n. | 棋子；典当；爪子；（被利用的）人 | A pawn is a piece in chess that can only move forward one square at a time.  It can also refer to a person or thing used by others for their own purposes. |例句：He moved his pawn two spaces. 他把他的棋子移了两格。

The corrupt official used his position to move his pawns around.  那个腐败的官员利用他的职位来摆布他的人。

近义词：pieces (in chess), puppets, tools | 反义词：king (in chess), master, controller | 词性：名词
