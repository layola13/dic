portioned:/ˈpɔːrʃənd/| adj. v.| 分配的；分成份的 | Divided into portions; allotted in shares. | 例句：The cake was portioned into eight slices. 蛋糕被切成了八块。  The land was portioned among the heirs. 土地被分给了继承人。|近义词：divided, shared, allotted, distributed |反义词：undivided, whole, combined


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He portions the food into equal servings. 他把食物分成等份。
She portions out the tasks fairly among her team. 她公平地将任务分配给她的团队。


一般过去时:
The teacher portioned the class into smaller groups. 老师把班级分成较小的组。
They portioned the inheritance equally among the siblings. 他们将遗产平均分配给兄弟姐妹。


现在完成时:
She has portioned out all the ingredients for the recipe. 她已经把食谱的所有配料都分好了。
The estate has been portioned among the beneficiaries.  遗产已分配给受益人。


过去完成时:
They had portioned the land before the dispute arose. 他们在纠纷发生之前就已经分割了土地。
She had already portioned the cake before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前，她已经把蛋糕分好了。


将来时:
We will portion the profits according to our investment. 我们将根据投资比例分配利润。
He will portion the work among his assistants. 他将把工作分配给他的助手们。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would portion my work more effectively. 如果我有更多时间，我会更有效地安排我的工作。
If she had planned better, she would have portioned the resources more wisely. 如果她计划得更好，她就会更明智地分配资源。


被动语态:
The food was portioned into individual containers. 食物被分装到单独的容器中。
The resources were portioned according to need. 资源根据需求进行分配。


疑问句:
How was the cake portioned? 蛋糕是怎么分的？
How will the work be portioned among the team? 工作将如何分配给团队？


否定句:
He didn't portion the food evenly. 他没有平均分配食物。
They hadn't portioned the inheritance before the lawyer arrived. 在律师到达之前，他们还没有分配遗产。
