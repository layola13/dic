pitied:/ˈpɪtiːd/| v.|同情，怜悯|To feel or show pity or sympathy for (someone or something).|例句：I pitied the lost dog wandering the streets. 我同情那只在街上流浪的迷路狗。


例句：She pitied the poor beggar who was shivering in the cold. 她同情那个在寒冷中瑟瑟发抖的可怜乞丐。


近义词：sympathized with, commiserated with, felt sorry for|反义词：envied, scorned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I pity him because he is so poor. = 我同情他，因为他很穷。
They pity the homeless people. = 他们同情无家可归的人。


一般过去时:
I pitied her when I heard about her misfortune. = 当我听说她的不幸时，我同情她。
We pitied the children who had lost their parents. = 我们同情那些失去父母的孩子。


现在完成时:
I have pitied many people in my life. = 我一生中同情过许多人。
She has pitied the suffering of the refugees. = 她同情难民的苦难。


过去完成时:
I had pitied him before I knew the truth. = 在我了解真相之前，我同情过他。
They had pitied the victims of the earthquake. = 他们同情过地震的受害者。


将来时:
I will pity him if he loses his job. = 如果他丢了工作，我会同情他。
We will pity the people who are affected by the flood. = 我们将同情受洪水影响的人们。


虚拟语气:
I would pity him more if he were more honest. = 如果他更诚实的话，我会更同情他。
If I were in her shoes, I would pity her situation. = 如果我处在她的位置，我会同情她的处境。


被动语态:
He was pitied by everyone in the village. = 他受到了村里所有人的同情。
The victims of the accident were pitied by the whole community. = 事故的受害者受到了整个社区的同情。


疑问句:
Do you pity the criminals? = 你同情罪犯吗？
Does she pity the poor? = 她同情穷人吗？


否定句:
I don't pity him; he deserves what he got. = 我不同情他；他罪有应得。
They didn't pity the thief; he had broken the law. = 他们不同情那个贼；他犯了法。


PHRASE->
feel pity for: 对...感到同情
have pity on: 对...表示同情
take pity on: 同情；怜悯
out of pity: 出于同情
worthy of pity: 值得同情的
