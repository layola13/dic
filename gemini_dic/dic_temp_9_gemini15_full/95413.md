incident-to: /ˈɪnsɪdənt tuː/ | adj. | 附带的；伴随的；附属于 |  Inherent in or accompanying something else; subordinate or secondary.  | 例句：The costs incident to the project were unexpectedly high. 项目的附带成本出乎意料地高。

例句：The minor inconveniences incident to travel are usually easily overcome. 旅行中的一些小不便通常很容易克服。


近义词：accompanying, attendant, concomitant, incidental | 反义词：primary, principal, main, essential


词性：形容词
