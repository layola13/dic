laudatory: /ˈlɔːdətəri/ | adj. | 赞美的；赞扬的；褒奖的 | Expressing praise or commendation.  | The laudatory comments about the film were well-deserved.  这部电影的赞扬性评论是当之无愧的。| complimentary, praising, commendatory | critical, condemnatory, disparaging | 形容词
