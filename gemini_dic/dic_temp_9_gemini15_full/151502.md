parrot-fashion:/ˈpærətˌfæʃən/| adj.|鹦鹉学舌般地，机械地，模仿地|Repeating words or phrases without understanding their meaning; mimicking someone's speech or actions mechanically.|He repeated the teacher's words parrot-fashion, without grasping their significance. 他鹦鹉学舌般地重复老师的话，并没有理解其含义。|近义词：mechanically, automatically, blindly|反义词：understandingly, thoughtfully, intelligently
