lieve:/liːv/ | v. |相信，认为；居住；留下；听任|To believe something is to accept that it is true. To live somewhere is to reside there. To leave something is to depart from it. To leave something to chance is to allow it to happen without interference. |例句：I believe in the power of positive thinking.我相信积极思考的力量。She lives in a small village. 她住在一个小村庄里。 He left the party early. 他早早离开了派对。They left the decision to me.他们把决定权交给了我。|近义词：think, trust, reside, depart, abandon|反义词：disbelieve, doubt, stay, remain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I believe you. = 我相信你。
She lives in London. = 她住在伦敦。

一般过去时:
I believed him at first. = 我一开始相信他。
They lived in Paris for ten years. = 他们曾在巴黎住了十年。


现在完成时:
I have believed in him all along. = 我一直相信他。
We have lived here for five years. = 我们住在这里五年了。


过去完成时:
I had believed that he was innocent. = 我曾相信他是清白的。
They had lived in the countryside before moving to the city. = 搬到城市之前，他们住在乡下。


将来时:
I will believe you when I see the proof. = 我看到证据才会相信你。
They will live in a bigger house next year. = 他们明年将住进更大的房子。


虚拟语气:
If I believed him, I would have helped him. = 如果我相信他，我就帮他了。
If I lived in the countryside, I would be happier. = 如果我住在乡下，我会更快乐。


被动语态:
(The verb "believe" is rarely used passively.)
His story was left untold. = 他的故事没有被讲述。


疑问句:
Do you believe in ghosts? = 你相信鬼魂吗？
Where do they live? = 他们住在哪里？


否定句:
I don't believe her story. = 我不相信她的故事。
They don't live here anymore. = 他们不住在这里了。


PHRASE->
believe in = 相信…
leave behind = 留下；遗留
leave out = 省略；遗漏
live with = 和…一起生活；忍受
live up to = 符合；不辜负
