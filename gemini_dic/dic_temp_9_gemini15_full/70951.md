fireflame:/ˈfaɪərˌfleɪm/| n. |火燄；火焰|A fireflame is a visual representation of fire, often depicted as a flickering, dancing light.  It can refer to the actual flame itself or a metaphorical representation of intense passion or energy.|例句：The fireflame danced in the hearth, casting flickering shadows on the wall.  壁炉里的火焰跳跃着，在墙上投下闪烁的阴影。

The intense fireflame of her passion burned brightly. 她热情奔放的火焰熊熊燃烧。|近义词：flame, blaze, fire|反义词：darkness, coldness
