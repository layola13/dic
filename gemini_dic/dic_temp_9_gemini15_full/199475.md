sincere:/sɪnˈsɪr/| adj.|真诚的；真心的；诚恳的|Free from hypocrisy or falseness; genuine.  Characterized by or proceeding from genuine feelings; earnest.|例句：He offered a sincere apology for his mistake. 他为自己的错误真诚地道歉了。

例句：Her sincere concern for others was evident in her actions.  她对别人的真诚关怀在她的行动中显而易见。


近义词：genuine, heartfelt, earnest, truthful, honest|反义词：dishonest, insincere, false, hypocritical, deceitful|词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is sincere in his beliefs. = 他对自己的信仰是真诚的。
She is sincere in her apologies. = 她道歉是真诚的。


一般过去时:
He was sincere in his efforts to help. = 他在努力帮助别人方面是真诚的。
She was sincere when she promised to call. = 她答应打电话时是真诚的。


现在完成时:
He has always been sincere in his dealings with others. = 他在与他人交往中一直是真诚的。
She has been sincere in her pursuit of knowledge. = 她一直真诚地追求知识。


过去完成时:
He had been sincere in his intentions, but things didn't work out. = 他原本是真诚的，但事情并没有成功。
She had been sincere in her application, but she was rejected. = 她申请时是真诚的，但是被拒绝了。


将来时:
He will be sincere in his future endeavors. = 他未来的努力将是真诚的。
She will be sincere in her testimony. = 她作证将是真诚的。


虚拟语气:
If he were sincere, he would admit his mistake. = 如果他是真诚的，他会承认自己的错误。
If she were sincere, she would have apologized. = 如果她是真诚的，她本应该道歉的。


被动语态:
His apology was perceived as sincere. = 他的道歉被认为是真诚的。
Her intentions were seen as sincere. = 她的意图被认为是真诚的。


疑问句:
Is he sincere in his offer of help? = 他提供帮助是真诚的吗？
Was she sincere in her expression of gratitude? = 她表达感激之情是真诚的吗？


否定句:
He was not sincere in his promises. = 他许下的诺言并非真诚。
She was not sincere in her attempts to deceive us. = 她试图欺骗我们并非真诚。
