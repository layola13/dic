词源:
The word "incivilly" is derived from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "civilly," which comes from the noun "civil" meaning "relating to citizens or the state; courteous, polite."  Therefore, "incivilly" means "not civilly," or lacking in politeness and good manners.
