lemmas:/ˈlɛməz/| n. |词素，词位，词条|A lemma is the base or headword form of a set of words that are related through inflection (e.g., "run," "runs," "running," "ran" are all forms of the lemma "run").  It's the dictionary form of a word.|The lemma for "running" is "run".|近义词：base form, headword|反义词:N/A
