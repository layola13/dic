indivinity:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪnɪti/| n. |神性；神格；神圣；崇高|The inherent divine nature or quality; godliness.  It refers to the state or quality of being divine, possessing the attributes of a god or gods.  This can refer to a specific deity or to a more general sense of spiritual power and perfection.|例句：The artist sought to capture the indivinity in the Madonna's face.  艺术家试图捕捉圣母像中神圣的面容。

例句：The poem explores the indivinity of nature and its connection to the human soul. 这首诗探讨了自然的崇高及其与人类灵魂的联系。


近义词：godliness, divinity, sacredness, holiness|反义词：profane, unholy, wickedness, ungodliness
