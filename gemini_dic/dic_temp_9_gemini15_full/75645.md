fuhrers:/ˈfuːrərz/| n.|元首们，领袖们|The plural of "führer," meaning leader or guide, especially referring to Adolf Hitler's title as the leader of Nazi Germany.|例句：The führers of the Axis powers met in secret.  轴心国的元首们秘密会面。
例句：Historians continue to debate the actions and motivations of the Nazi führers. 历史学家们继续辩论纳粹元首们的行为和动机。|近义词：leaders, chiefs, dictators|反义词：followers, subordinates|名词
