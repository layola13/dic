reappraiser:/ˌriːəˈpreɪzər/ | n. | 再评估者；重新估价者 | A person who reappraises something, such as property or assets.  It often refers to someone professionally involved in reassessing the value of something. | Example: The reappraiser carefully examined the house before determining its new market value.  再评估员仔细检查了房子后才确定了它的新市场价值。| 近义词：valuer, assessor, evaluator | 反义词：N/A
