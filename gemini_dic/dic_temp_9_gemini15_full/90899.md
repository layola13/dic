hunterlike: /ˈhʌntərlaɪk/ | adj. | 像猎人的；具有猎人人格的 | Resembling a hunter in appearance, manner, or skill; having the characteristics of a hunter.  | 例句：He moved with a hunterlike stealth through the undergrowth. 他以猎人的隐秘方式穿过灌木丛。

例句：Her hunterlike gaze fixed on the deer. 她像猎人一样凝视着鹿。


近义词：predatory, hunting, stalkerlike


反义词：preylike, docile, gentle


词性：形容词
