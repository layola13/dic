chimera:/kaɪˈmɪərə/| n.| 奇美拉；幻想；妄想；嵌合体 |A chimera is an imaginary creature composed of parts from different animals, often depicted as a lion with a goat's head and a serpent's tail. In biology, a chimera refers to an organism containing cells from two or more individuals. It can also describe a wildly improbable or unrealistic idea or hope. | Example: The project was a chimera, doomed to failure from the start. 这个项目就是一个不切实际的幻想，从一开始就注定要失败。 | 近义词：fantasy, illusion, delusion, hybrid | 反义词：reality, truth
