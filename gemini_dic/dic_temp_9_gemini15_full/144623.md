outwinging: /ˌaʊtˈwɪŋɪŋ/ | v. | 超越；胜过 |  To surpass or exceed in skill, power, or influence, especially in a competition or contest.  |  例句：The smaller team outwinged the larger one in terms of strategy and speed. 小球队在策略和速度上胜过了大球队。

例句：Our company's new marketing campaign is outwinging all expectations. 我们公司的新的营销活动超出了所有预期。


近义词：outshine, outmaneuver, surpass, excel, exceed | 反义词：underperform, be outdone, be surpassed | 词性：动词
