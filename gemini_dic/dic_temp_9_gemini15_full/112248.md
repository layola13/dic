looting:/ˈluːtɪŋ/| n. |抢劫；掠夺；洗劫|The act of stealing, especially during a riot, war, or natural disaster.  It involves taking goods or property illegally and often violently. |例句：The looting and rioting following the earthquake caused widespread damage and suffering.  地震后的抢劫和骚乱造成了广泛的破坏和痛苦。

例句：After the hurricane, there were reports of widespread looting in the affected areas. 飓风过后，受灾地区有广泛的抢劫报告。


近义词：pillaging, plundering, ransacking, robbing|反义词：donating, giving, contributing|词性：名词
