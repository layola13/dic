restarted:/ˌriːˈstɑːrtɪd/ | v. |重新开始，重启 |To begin something again after it has been stopped.  |例句：I restarted my computer after it crashed.  我电脑死机后重新启动了它。
例句：After a brief pause, the musicians restarted their performance.  短暂的停顿后，音乐家们重新开始了演出。|近义词：reactivated, recommenced, resumed |反义词：stopped, ended, ceased |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He restarts his car every morning. = 他每天早上都重新启动他的车。
They restart the project after a long break. = 他们经过长时间的休息后重新启动了项目。


一般过去时:
She restarted her computer because it was frozen. = 她的电脑死机了，所以她重新启动了它。
We restarted the meeting after a short coffee break. = 我们短暂的咖啡休息后重新开始了会议。


现在完成时:
I have restarted my phone several times today. = 我今天已经重启了好几次手机了。
They have restarted negotiations after a period of stalemate. = 僵局过后，他们已经重启了谈判。


过去完成时:
He had restarted the engine before the car started moving. = 在汽车开始移动之前，他已经重新启动了发动机。
They had restarted the experiment after correcting the initial error. = 他们在纠正了最初的错误后重新开始了实验。


将来时:
I will restart my computer later. = 我等会儿会重启我的电脑。
They will restart the program next week. = 他们下周将重新启动该程序。


虚拟语气:
If the system failed, we would restart it immediately. = 如果系统故障，我们会立即重启它。
I wish I had restarted my phone before the battery died. = 我真希望在电池没电之前我已经重启了手机。


被动语态:
The computer was restarted by the technician. = 电脑被技术人员重新启动了。

疑问句:

否定句:
