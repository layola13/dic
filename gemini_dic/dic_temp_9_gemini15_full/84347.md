halfserious: /ˌhæfˈsɪəriəs/ | adj. | 半认真的；不完全认真的 |  Partially serious; not entirely serious; showing a mixture of seriousness and playfulness.  It suggests an attitude that is not fully committed to seriousness, often implying a degree of jest or irony. |


例句：

He made a halfserious offer to buy the car.  他半开玩笑地提出要买这辆车。

She gave a halfserious warning about the dangers of smoking. 她半真半假地警告了吸烟的危害。


近义词：

partly serious, somewhat serious, semi-serious, playful, teasing


反义词：

completely serious, utterly serious, dead serious


词性：形容词
