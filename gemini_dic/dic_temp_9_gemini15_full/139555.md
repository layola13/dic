obturation:/ˌɒb.tjʊˈreɪ.ʃən/ | n. | 阻塞；填充；封闭 | The process of filling or closing a cavity or opening, especially in dentistry or surgery.  This often involves the use of a material to seal or block the opening. | Example: The obturation of the root canal was successful in preventing further infection. 根管的封闭成功地阻止了进一步的感染。|近义词：closure, sealing, plugging |反义词：opening, exposure
