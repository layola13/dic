linguist: /ˈlɪŋɡwɪst/ | n. |语言学家|A linguist is a person who studies linguistics, the scientific study of language.  They might research the structure, history, or social context of languages. Linguists often have expertise in multiple languages. | Example:  She's a renowned linguist specializing in ancient Greek. 她是一位专门研究古希腊语的著名语言学家。

| Example: The linguist presented her findings at the international conference.  这位语言学家在国际会议上介绍了她的研究成果。 |近义词: philologist, grammarian |反义词: (no direct antonym)|
