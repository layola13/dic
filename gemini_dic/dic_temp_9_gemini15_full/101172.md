invigilator:/ɪnˈvɪdʒɪleɪtə(r)/| n. |监考员|A person who supervises students during an examination to prevent cheating.  |Example: The invigilator warned the student for looking at another student's paper. 监考员警告学生不要看其他学生的试卷。|近义词：supervisor, proctor, examiner|反义词： examinee, student |名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The invigilator watches the students carefully. = 监考员仔细地监视着学生们。
The invigilators distribute the exam papers. = 监考员们分发试卷。


一般过去时:
The invigilator collected the exam papers at the end of the test. = 考试结束后，监考员收回了试卷。
The invigilators checked the students' ID cards. = 监考员们检查了学生的身份证。
