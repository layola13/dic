/ˈmiːtər/ | n. |米，公尺；计量器；韵律；节拍 | A unit of length equal to 39.37 inches (approximately 100 centimeters).  It is the base unit of length in the International System of Units (SI).  Also can refer to a device used for measuring something, or the rhythmic pattern in poetry or music. | Example: The room is five meters long.  这间房间长五米。 |近义词：meter, metre (British spelling) |反义词: (no direct antonym, context-dependent)| noun
