draftily:/ˈdræftɪli/| adv.| 草率地，粗略地 | In a hasty or careless manner; without sufficient care or attention to detail.  |例句：He draftily sketched the plan, leaving many details to be filled in later. 他草率地勾画了计划，留下许多细节待以后补充。

例句：The report was draftily written and contained numerous errors. 报告写得很草率，有很多错误。


近义词：carelessly, hastily, roughly, perfunctorily, sloppily

反义词：carefully, meticulously, thoroughly, precisely


词源：源自动词draft，意为“草拟”，加上副词后缀-ly构成。  The word ultimately derives from the Old Norse word *draga meaning "to draw" or "to pull," reflecting the act of pulling together the initial elements of a document or plan.
