词源:
The adverb "cheekily" is derived from the adjective "cheeky," which itself originates from the noun "cheek."  The sense of "cheek" referring to impudence or boldness developed in the 17th century.  The "-ily" suffix is a common adverbial suffix in English, indicating manner or way.
