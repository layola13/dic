missampled: /ˌmɪsˈsæmpld/ | adj. | 采样错误的，样本偏差的 |  Describes data or a sample that has been collected or selected in a way that introduces bias or inaccuracy, leading to unreliable or misleading results. It implies a flaw in the sampling process itself, rather than simply a small error in the data. | Example: The survey results were deemed unreliable because the sample was missampled, overrepresenting one demographic group.  The missampled data led to flawed conclusions about consumer preferences. | 近义词: biased, skewed, flawed, inaccurate | 反义词: representative, unbiased, accurate |  词性: adjective
