resaddled:/ˌriːˈsædld/ | vt. |重新上鞍；再次骑上；|To put a saddle back on (a horse) or to ride a horse again after a break.|例句：He resaddled his horse and continued his journey. 他重新上鞍，继续他的旅程。

例句：After a short rest, the rider resaddled his horse and set off again. 休息片刻后，骑手重新上鞍，再次出发。


近义词：remount, saddle again | 反义词：unsaddle
