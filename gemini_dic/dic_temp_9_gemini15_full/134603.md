/ˌnɒnˈluːkrətɪvnəsɪz/ | n. | 非营利性；非盈利性质 | The plural form of "nonlucrativeness," referring to instances or aspects of not generating profit.  It highlights the absence of profit-making as a characteristic or feature.  This word is rarely used in everyday conversation and is more common in academic or formal contexts.


例句：The report detailed the nonlucrativenesses inherent in the arts sector, highlighting the challenges faced by artists and cultural institutions in achieving financial sustainability. = 报告详细说明了艺术行业固有的非营利性，突出了艺术家和文化机构在实现财务可持续性方面面临的挑战。

The multiple nonlucrativenesses of the project led to its eventual cancellation. = 该项目的诸多非盈利性最终导致其被取消。


近义词：lack of profitability, unprofitability, non-profitability


反义词：profitability, lucrativeness


词性: noun (plural)
