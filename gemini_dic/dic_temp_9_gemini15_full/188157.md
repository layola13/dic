router:/ˈraʊtər/| n. |路由器，转接器 |A router is a networking device that forwards data packets between networks. It determines the best path for a packet to take to reach its destination.  |Example: My router is connected to the modem and distributes internet access to all the devices in my home. 我的路由器连接到调制解调器，并将互联网接入分配到我家的所有设备。|近义词：gateway, switch |反义词：N/A
