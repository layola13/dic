goddamned:/ˈɡɑːdˌdæm(d)/| adj.|该死的，可恶的，糟糕的|Extremely bad; damn; cursed.  The word is a more emphatic and profane version of "damned." It is often used as an intensifier to express anger, frustration, or annoyance.|例句：That goddamned car won't start! = 这该死的车发动不了！

例句：This goddamned weather is ruining my vacation. = 这该死的天气毁了我的假期！


近义词：damned, cursed, blasted, infernal, awful, terrible, horrible


反义词：blessed, wonderful, great, excellent


词性：形容词
