husking:/ˈhʌskɪŋ/| n. v.|脱壳；去皮|The process of removing the husk or outer covering from something, typically grain or nuts.  It can also refer to the act of removing the outer shell or covering from something.|例句：The farmers are busy with the husking of the corn. 农民们正忙着剥玉米皮。
The machine efficiently husks the rice. 这台机器有效地去壳稻米。|近义词：shelling, peeling, stripping|反义词：husking (反义词可以理解为“留壳”，但没有一个直接对应的词)|词性:n. v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He husks the corn every morning. 他每天早上都剥玉米。
She husks peanuts for a living. 她以剥花生为生。

一般过去时:
They husked the rice yesterday. 他们昨天剥了稻谷。
The workers husked all the coconuts by hand. 工人们手工剥了所有的椰子。

现在完成时:
We have husked all the walnuts. 我们已经剥完了所有的核桃。
She has husked enough corn for the winter. 她已经剥够了过冬的玉米。

过去完成时:
They had husked the beans before the storm hit. 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经剥好了豆子。
The farmer had husked the wheat before the sun set. 农民在日落前剥好了小麦。

将来时:
I will husk the peas tomorrow. 我明天将剥豌豆。
They will husk the barley after the harvest. 收获后他们将剥大麦。

虚拟语气:
If I had a machine, I would husk the rice much faster. 如果我有一台机器，我就能更快地剥稻米。
I wish I had husked all the corn yesterday. 我真希望昨天把所有的玉米都剥好了。

被动语态:
The corn is husked by machine. 玉米是用机器剥的。
The peanuts were husked by hand. 花生是手工剥的。

疑问句:
Have you husked the potatoes yet? 你剥土豆了吗？
Will they husk the soybeans today? 他们今天会剥大豆吗？

否定句:
They didn't husk the rice properly. 他们没有正确地剥稻米。
I haven't husked the almonds yet. 我还没剥杏仁。
