reenjoyed:/ˌriːɪnˈdʒɔɪd/| v.|再次享受；重新享受 |To experience the pleasure of something again. It implies a previous enjoyment that is being repeated or revisited.|例句：After a long break, she reenjoyed the thrill of skiing.  (长时间休息后，她再次享受了滑雪的刺激。)

例句：He reenjoyed the classic film after many years. (多年后，他又重新欣赏了这部经典电影。)


近义词：relish, savor, appreciate again, experience anew


反义词：dislike, abhor, detest


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I reenjoy my childhood memories every time I visit my hometown. = 我每次回老家都会重新享受童年记忆。
They reenjoy the peace and quiet of the countryside every weekend. = 他们每个周末都会重新享受乡村的宁静祥和。


一般过去时:
She reenjoyed the delicious meal she had last week. = 她重新回味了她上周吃的美味佳肴。
We reenjoyed the concert we attended last month. = 我们重新回味了上个月参加的音乐会。


现在完成时:
I have reenjoyed that book several times since I first read it. = 自从我第一次读这本书以来，我已经多次重新阅读并享受它了。
They have reenjoyed the benefits of their hard work. = 他们已经再次享受到了他们辛勤工作的成果。


过去完成时:
He had reenjoyed the feeling of success before he started his new project. = 在开始他的新项目之前，他已经再次回味了成功的喜悦。
She had reenjoyed her favorite song many times before the concert. = 在音乐会之前，她已经多次重新欣赏了她最喜欢的歌曲。


将来时:
I will reenjoy this moment in the future. = 我将来会再次享受这一刻。
We will reenjoy the beautiful scenery next year. = 我们明年将会再次欣赏美丽的风景。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would reenjoy my favorite hobby. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新享受我的爱好。
I wish I could reenjoy the feeling of being in love. = 我希望我能再次体验到恋爱的感觉。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
