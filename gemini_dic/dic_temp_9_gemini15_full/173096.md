quartzite:/ˈkwɔːrtsaɪt/ | n. |石英岩|A non-foliated metamorphic rock composed almost entirely of quartz.  It is formed from the metamorphism of quartz-rich sandstone.  The quartz grains are typically tightly cemented together, resulting in a hard, durable rock.|例句：The mountain range is composed largely of quartzite, making it incredibly resistant to erosion. 这座山脉主要由石英岩构成，使其极具抗侵蚀性。|例句：Quartzite is often used in construction due to its strength and durability. 由于石英岩的强度和耐用性，它常用于建筑。|近义词：metaquartzite,  (depending on context) sandstone (before metamorphism)|反义词：N/A (no direct antonym)|
