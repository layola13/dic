interastral: /ˌɪntərˈæstrəl/ | adj. |星际的，星间的| Relating to or situated between the stars.  The word suggests a space or realm existing among or between stars, often implying a vastness and mystery. |例句：The novel depicts an interastral journey across unimaginable distances. 这部小说描绘了一次跨越难以想象距离的星际旅行。

The concept of interastral travel is a staple of science fiction. 星际旅行的概念是科幻小说的一个主要内容。
