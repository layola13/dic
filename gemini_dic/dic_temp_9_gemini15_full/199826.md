sit-in:/ˈsɪtɪn/| n. |静坐；静坐示威|A sit-in is a form of protest where people occupy a place, usually a building, to show their disapproval of something.  It is a non-violent form of direct action. |例句：Students held a sit-in at the university administration building to protest tuition increases. 学生们在大学行政楼里举行了静坐示威，以抗议学费上涨。

例句：The activists staged a sit-in outside the government building to demand climate action. 积极分子在政府大楼外举行了静坐示威，要求采取气候行动。

近义词：demonstration, protest, occupation |反义词：compliance, obedience |词性:名词
