fusible:/ˈfjuːzəbəl/ | adj. | 可熔的；易熔的；可融化的 | Capable of being fused or melted.  Something that is fusible can be melted or joined by melting. | Example:  The fusible link in the sprinkler system melted, triggering the alarm.  自动喷水灭火系统中的熔断器融化了，触发了警报。

The fusible wire in the circuit burned out.电路中的熔丝烧坏了。|近义词：meltable,  fusable | 反义词：infusible, unmeltable
