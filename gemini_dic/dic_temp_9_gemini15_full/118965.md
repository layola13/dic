metering:/ˈmiːtərɪŋ/ | n. |计量；配给；节流；调节 | The act or process of measuring something, especially the flow of a substance or energy.  It can also refer to the distribution or allocation of something in measured amounts. |例句：The metering of water to the houses was carefully controlled to prevent wastage.  房屋的供水量受到严格控制，以防止浪费。

例句：Precise metering of fuel injection is crucial for optimal engine performance.  精确的燃油喷射计量对于最佳发动机性能至关重要。


近义词：measuring, rationing, regulation, dispensing, allocation | 反义词：wastage, overspending, profusion


词源:  Comes from the word "meter," meaning a measuring instrument, which in turn is from Old French *metre and ultimately from Greek *metron (μέτρον), meaning "measure."  The suffix "-ing" indicates the act or process of doing something.
