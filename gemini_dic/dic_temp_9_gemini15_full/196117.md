septembrist:/sɛpˈtɛmbrɪst/| n.|九月份革命者；支持九月份革命的人|A septembrist is a supporter of the Septembrist revolution in Portugal in 1910.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting someone who is revolutionary or radical.|例句：Many historians view the actions of the septembrists as necessary for Portugal's modernization, while others criticize their methods.  许多历史学家认为九月份革命者的行动对葡萄牙的现代化是必要的，而另一些人则批评他们的方法。|近义词：revolutionary, radical, insurgent|反义词：conservative, reactionary, monarchist|名词
