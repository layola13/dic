loser:/ˈluːzər/| n. |失败者，输家；无价值的人|A person who has failed or been defeated, especially in a competition or contest. It can also be used informally to describe someone considered unsuccessful, unpleasant, or pathetic.|例句：He felt like a loser after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后觉得自己像个失败者。

例句：She called him a loser for his bad behavior. 她因为他行为不端而称他为失败者。


近义词：failure, underdog, washout |反义词：winner, champion, victor |词性:名词


USAGE->

He is a loser. = 他是个失败者。
She feels like a loser today. = 她今天感觉自己是个失败者。
