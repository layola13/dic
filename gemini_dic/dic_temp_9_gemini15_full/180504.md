regenerative: /rɪˈdʒɛnərətɪv/ | adj. | 再生的；再生性的；可再生的 |  capable of regenerating or being regenerated; relating to or promoting regeneration.  |  The regenerative power of nature is amazing. 自然的再生能力令人惊叹。  The city is investing in regenerative agriculture.  这个城市正在投资再生农业。 |  renewable, restorative, reparative | degenerative, destructive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system regenerates energy efficiently. = 这个系统高效地再生能量。
This plant regenerates its leaves quickly. = 这株植物迅速再生叶子。


一般过去时:
The forest regenerated after the fire. = 森林在火灾后再生了。

现在完成时:
The city has regenerated its downtown area. = 这个城市已经更新了其市中心区域。
The ecosystem has regenerated after the pollution was cleaned up. = 生态系统在污染清除后已经再生了。
