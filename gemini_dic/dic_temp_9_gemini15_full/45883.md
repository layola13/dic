cutesiness:/kjuːtˈsɪnɪs/| n. |可爱，卖萌，过分可爱|Excessively cute behavior or appearance; an excessive display of cuteness.  It often implies a slightly irritating or affected quality. |例句：Her cutesiness grated on my nerves.  她的卖萌让我很烦。

例句：The cutesiness of the kittens was almost overwhelming. 小猫的可爱程度几乎让人难以承受。


近义词：sweetness, charm, adorableness, preciousness

反义词：ugliness, harshness, unpleasantness
