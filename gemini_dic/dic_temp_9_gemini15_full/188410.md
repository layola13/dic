ruche:/ruʃ/| n.|褶饰，皱褶|A ruche is a gathered or pleated strip of fabric, often used as a decorative trim on clothing or accessories.  It can also refer to a similar decorative effect created with other materials.|The dress featured a delicate ruche at the neckline. 这件裙子在领口处饰有精美的褶饰。 The chef garnished the dish with a ruche of herbs.  厨师用草药的褶饰装饰了这道菜。|frill, pleat, gathering|smooth, flat|名词
