overgild:/ˈoʊvərɡɪld/| vt.|镀金过度；过分装饰|To cover with more gold than is necessary or appropriate; to excessively decorate or embellish.|The sculptor overgilded the frame, obscuring its delicate carvings.  雕塑家在框架上镀了过多的金，遮蔽了它精细的雕刻。|近义词：overdecorate, overembellish|反义词：undergild, simplify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overgilds his cakes with frosting. = 他在蛋糕上涂了过多的糖霜。
They overgild their speeches with unnecessary jargon. = 他们在演讲中使用了过多的不必要的术语。


一般过去时:
She overgilded the picture frame, ruining its elegance. = 她给画框镀了过多的金，破坏了它的优雅。
The artist overgilded the statue, making it look gaudy. = 艺术家在雕像上镀了过多的金，使它看起来俗气。


现在完成时:
They have overgilded the entire building, making it tasteless. = 他们已经给整栋建筑镀了过多的金，使其显得俗不可耐。
She has overgilded the Christmas tree with too many ornaments. = 她给圣诞树装饰了过多的饰品。


过去完成时:
He had overgilded the furniture before the guests arrived. = 客人到来之前，他已经给家具镀了过多的金。
They had overgilded the crown, making it heavy and impractical. = 他们已经给皇冠镀了过多的金，使其变得沉重且不实用。


将来时:
They will overgild the new church altar. = 他们将给新的教堂祭坛镀过多的金。
She will overgild the cake with edible gold leaf. = 她将在蛋糕上镀过多的食用金箔。


虚拟语气:
If he overgilded the painting, it would lose its artistic value. = 如果他给画镀了过多的金，它就会失去艺术价值。
I wish they hadn't overgilded the monument; it looks tacky now. = 我真希望他们没有给纪念碑镀过多的金；它现在看起来俗气了。


被动语态:
The frame was overgilded, obscuring the intricate details. = 画框镀了过多的金，遮盖了复杂的细节。
The entire room was overgilded, making it feel oppressive. = 整个房间都镀了过多的金，让人感觉压抑。


疑问句:

否定句:
