exhume:/ɪɡˈzjuːm/ | vt. | 掘出；挖出（尤指埋葬物） | To exhume something, especially a body, means to dig it out of the ground.  |  Archaeologists exhumed the ancient tomb. 考古学家挖掘了古墓。 The police exhumed the body to perform a new autopsy. 警方掘出遗体进行新的尸检。 | unearth, disinter | bury, inter | 动词
