haecceity:/heˈsʌɪəti/| n. |此在性，此物性 |The inherent thisness of a particular thing, its unique individual existence as distinct from all others.  It emphasizes the concrete individuality of a thing, beyond its general properties or qualities.  The concept is often associated with Duns Scotus and is central to discussions of metaphysics and ontology. | Example:  The haecceity of this particular rose makes it unique, even if other roses share similar characteristics.  这朵玫瑰的此在性使它独一无二，即使其他玫瑰具有相似的特征。|近义词: individuality, uniqueness, particularity |反义词: generality, universality, commonality |名词
