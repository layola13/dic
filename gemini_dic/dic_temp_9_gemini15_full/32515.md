chinse:/tʃaɪˈniːz/ | adj. n. |中国的；中国人的；汉语 | Relating to China, its people, or its language.  |例句：She speaks fluent Chinese. 她流利地说中文。|近义词：Chinese-related, Sino- |反义词：None.
