emborder:/ɪmˈbɔːrdər/ | vt. | 以…为边界；加边于 | To form a border around something; to decorate the edge of something. | Example: The park is embordered by tall trees.  公园周围环绕着高大的树木。  The artist embordered the painting with a gold frame. 画家用金色的画框给画镶了边。 | 近义词：border, edge, fringe | 反义词：center, middle
