reproclaiming:/ˌriːprəˈkleɪmɪŋ/ | v. | 再次宣布；重新声明；重申 |  The act of proclaiming something again; restating or reaffirming a statement or declaration.  |例句：The government is reproclaiming its commitment to economic reform. 政府正在重申其对经济改革的承诺。|例句：The court is reproclaiming the defendant's innocence. 法院正在再次宣布被告无罪。|近义词：reasserting, reaffirming, reiterating | 反义词：retracting, recanting, denying | 动词
