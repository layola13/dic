sharp-eyed: /ʃɑːrpˈaɪd/ | adj. | 眼神锐利的，目光敏捷的 | Having keen eyesight; quick to notice things.  |例句：The sharp-eyed detective spotted the clue immediately.  眼神锐利的侦探立刻发现了线索。

例句：The sharp-eyed hawk saw the mouse from afar.  眼神锐利的鹰老远就看到了老鼠。


近义词：observant, keen-sighted, eagle-eyed, perceptive | 反义词：blind, unobservant, myopic


词源：源自 "sharp" (锐利的，敏捷的) + "eyed" (眼睛的)。 "Sharp"  在古英语中为"sċearp",  意为 "keen, pointed". "Eyed" 来自古英语 "ēage" (眼睛).  因此，"sharp-eyed" 字面意思是 "having sharp eyes".
