qcs:/kjuːˈsiːz/| n.|质量控制；质量检查|Quality Control System; a system of maintaining standards in manufactured products by testing a sample of the output against the standard.|例句：The factory has a strict qcs system in place.  这家工厂实施了严格的质量控制体系。|例句：We need to improve our qcs procedures to reduce defects. 我们需要改进质量控制程序以减少缺陷。|近义词：quality assurance, inspection|反义词：negligence, oversight|名词
