dingbats:/ˈdɪŋbæts/| n. |小装饰物；怪诞的人；胡言乱语；奇怪的符号| Pictures or symbols that are strange or unusual, often used in decorative contexts. The term can also refer to eccentric or odd individuals, or nonsensical talk.|例句：The children's book was full of whimsical dingbats. = 儿童读物充满了奇特的图案。  The old man was a bit of a dingbat, always rambling on about nonsense. = 那个老人有点古怪，总是胡言乱语。|近义词：doodads, gizmos, quirks, eccentrics|反义词：sensible, normal, logical
