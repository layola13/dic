neighborship:/ˈneɪbərhʊdʃɪp/ | n. | 邻里关系；睦邻；邻近 | The state or quality of being neighbors; the relationship between neighbors; a feeling of community among neighbors.  It can also refer to the geographical area surrounding a place. |例句：The friendly neighborship made the new residents feel welcome. 友好的邻里关系让新居民感到宾至如归。

例句：Good neighborship is essential for a peaceful community. 良好的邻里关系对于一个和平的社区至关重要。


近义词：neighborhood, community, proximity, fellowship

反义词：estrangement, isolation, antagonism


词源:  Derived from "neighbor" + "-ship," a suffix forming nouns denoting state, condition, or quality.  "Neighbor" itself has Germanic roots, ultimately related to words meaning "near" or "close by."
