opportunist:/ˌɒpəˈtjuːnɪst/|/ˌɑːpərˈtuːnɪst/| n. |机会主义者；投机取巧者 |A person who exploits opportunities for personal gain without regard for ethical considerations.  Opportunists are often seen as self-serving and lacking in principle.  They seize chances to advance themselves, even if it means taking advantage of others or compromising their values. |例句：He's a ruthless opportunist who will stop at nothing to get what he wants. 他是一个冷酷无情的机会主义者，为了达到目的无所不用其极。

例句：The company hired an opportunist to lead the new project, someone who could quickly exploit market trends. 公司聘请了一位机会主义者来领导新项目，一个能够迅速利用市场趋势的人。


近义词：exploiter, self-seeker, careerist, climber

反义词：idealist, altruist, principled person


词性: 名词
