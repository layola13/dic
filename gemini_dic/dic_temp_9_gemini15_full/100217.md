interrogating:/ɪnˈtɛrəɡeɪtɪŋ/| v.| 审问，讯问，质询 |  The act of questioning someone, especially in a thorough or aggressive way to obtain information.  | 例句：The police were interrogating the suspect for hours. 警方审问了嫌疑犯数小时。 | 例句：The journalist interrogated the politician about his controversial policies.  记者就这位政治家的争议性政策对其进行了质询。 | 近义词：questioning, examining, quizzing | 反义词：answering, responding, confirming
