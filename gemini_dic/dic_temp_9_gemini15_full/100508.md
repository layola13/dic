interventricular: /ˌɪntərˌvɛntrikjʊlər/ | adj. | 心室间的 | Relating to the space or structures between the ventricles of the heart.  It describes something situated between or connecting the two ventricles (lower chambers) of the heart. | Example: Interventricular septum is a crucial part of the heart. 心室中隔是心脏的重要组成部分。 | 近义词: interventricular septal | 反义词: intraventricular
