profitwise:/ˈprɑːfɪtwaɪz/ | adj. | 从利润角度来看；就盈利而言 |  This adjective is used to describe something from the perspective of profit or financial gain. It indicates that the following statement or idea is being considered in terms of its profitability or impact on profits.  |例句：Profitwise, the new product line is a success.  从利润角度来看，新产品线很成功。  |近义词：financially, economically, commercially |反义词：loss-wise
