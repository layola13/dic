jalopy:/ˈdʒæləpi/| n. |破旧的汽车；老爷车 |A jalopy is an old car that is in bad condition and is likely to break down.  It suggests a car that is unreliable and possibly in need of significant repair.  The term often carries a connotation of affection or humor, implying a certain charm despite its poor condition.|例句：He drove a rusty jalopy that constantly sputtered and coughed. 他开着一辆生锈的破旧汽车，车子总是发出噼里啪啦的响声。


例句：My grandfather’s jalopy, though old, held many happy memories.  尽管很旧，但我祖父的破车却承载着许多美好的回忆。


近义词：clunker, wreck, bucket of bolts, old banger


反义词：new car, luxury car, sports car


词性：名词
