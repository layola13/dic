predicative:/ˈprɛdɪkətɪv/| adj.| 谓语的，叙述的 | Relating to or functioning as a predicate.  A predicative adjective or noun is one that follows a linking verb and describes the subject. |例句：The predicative adjective "happy" in "She is happy" describes the subject "She".  她很高兴，其中的“高兴”是一个谓语形容词，描述的是主语“她”。|近义词：attributive (in contrast), descriptive |反义词： attributive
