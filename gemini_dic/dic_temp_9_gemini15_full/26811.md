cageful:/ˈkeɪdʒfʊl/| n. |一笼(的)…；装满笼子的…|A cageful refers to the amount or number of things that can fit into a cage.  It implies that the cage is full or nearly full.  The word is used with a noun indicating the contents of the cage.| Example: There was a cageful of birds singing merrily.  笼子里装满了快乐歌唱的小鸟。|近义词：cage of, a full cage|反义词:empty cage
