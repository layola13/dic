defeat:/dɪˈfiːt/| vt. n.|击败，战胜；挫败；使失败|To overcome in battle or contest; to win a victory over; to overcome or subdue (an opponent, enemy, etc.) ; to frustrate or thwart (a plan, effort, etc.).|例句：The army suffered a crushing defeat.  军队遭受了惨重的失败。

例句：Our team defeated their team by a score of 3-0.  我们的队伍以3比0的比分击败了他们。


近义词：conquer, vanquish, overcome, beat, subdue|反义词：victory, success, triumph|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army defeats the enemy. = 军队打败了敌人。
He defeats his opponent in the chess game. = 他在象棋比赛中击败了他的对手。


一般过去时:
The team defeated their rivals yesterday. = 球队昨天击败了他们的对手。
Napoleon was defeated at Waterloo. = 拿破仑在滑铁卢战役中被击败。


现在完成时:
They have defeated all their opponents so far. = 到目前为止，他们已经击败了所有对手。
She has never been defeated in a tennis match. = 她从未在网球比赛中输过。


过去完成时:
The general had already defeated the enemy before reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达之前，将军已经击败了敌人。
They had defeated many challenges before achieving their goal. = 在实现目标之前，他们已经克服了许多挑战。


将来时:
We will defeat our rivals next year. = 我们明年将击败我们的对手。
I believe we will defeat this difficult problem. = 我相信我们将克服这个难题。


虚拟语气:
If we had trained harder, we might have defeated them. = 如果我们训练更刻苦，我们也许就能击败他们。
I wish I had defeated my fear. = 我希望我已经战胜了我的恐惧。


被动语态:
The enemy was defeated by the superior force. = 敌人被强大的力量击败了。
The plan was defeated due to unforeseen circumstances. = 计划由于不可预见的情况而失败了。


疑问句:
Were they defeated in the competition? = 他们在比赛中被击败了吗？
Has the proposal been defeated? = 这个提案被否决了吗？


否定句:
We did not defeat the champion. = 我们没有打败冠军。
The army was not defeated easily. = 军队并非轻易被打败。
