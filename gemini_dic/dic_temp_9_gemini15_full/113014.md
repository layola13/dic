lumberyards:/ˈlʌmbərjɑːrdz/| n. |木材场，木材堆放场 |A lumberyard is a place where lumber (wood) is stored and sold.  They typically sell various types of wood, including planks, beams, and plywood, often to construction companies or individuals undertaking DIY projects. |The construction company purchased all the lumber from the local lumberyard.|近义词：timber yard, wood yard|反义词：无
