presupposes:/ˌpriːˈsʌpəʊzɪz/| v.|预设，预先假定|To assume something to be true before it has been proved.  It implies that something is already known or believed to be true. |例句：His argument presupposes a knowledge of economics.  他的论点预设了对经济学的了解。

例句：The statement presupposes that you are familiar with the subject. 这句话预设你熟悉这个主题。


近义词：assume, imply, suggest, postulate

反义词：deny, refute, contradict


词性:动词
