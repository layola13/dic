dolloping:/ˈdɒləpɪŋ/ | v. | 大量涂抹；大量添加 |  The act of applying or adding something in large, uneven blobs or lumps.  It often implies a generous or somewhat messy application. |例句：He dolloped cream onto his pie. 他在派上抹了厚厚一层奶油。

She dolloped paint onto the canvas. 她往画布上大量地涂抹颜料。


近义词：daubing, slathering, smearing, slapping


反义词：dabbing (applying a small amount), drizzling (applying a thin stream)


词性: 动词
