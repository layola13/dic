clings:/klɪŋz/| v.|粘着；依附；坚持|To hold on tightly or adhere to something; to remain attached.  The verb "cling" suggests a strong attachment or dependence.|例句：The child clung to his mother's leg.  孩子紧紧抓住母亲的腿。|例句：She clung to the hope of a better future. 她坚持对美好未来的希望。|近义词：adhere, stick, hold on, grasp|反义词：release, let go, detach


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ivy clings to the wall. = 常春藤攀附在墙上。
The scent of perfume clings to her clothes. = 香水的气味粘在她的衣服上。

一般过去时:
He clung to the railing as the boat rocked. = 船摇晃时，他紧紧抓住栏杆。
She clung to her beliefs despite the criticism. = 尽管受到批评，她仍然坚持自己的信念。

现在完成时:
The smell of smoke has clung to my clothes all day. = 烟味整天都粘在我的衣服上。
He has clung to power for many years. = 他多年来一直掌握着权力。

过去完成时:
She had clung to the hope of seeing him again before he died. = 他死前，她一直抱着一丝希望能够再见到他。
The survivors had clung to a piece of driftwood. = 幸存者们紧紧抓住了一块漂流木。

将来时:
I think he will cling to his position until the very end. = 我认为他会坚持到最后。
The climber will cling to the rock face as he ascends. = 登山者攀登时会紧紧抓住岩壁。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would cling to your principles. = 如果我是你，我会坚持自己的原则。
I wish I had clung to my original plan. = 我真希望我坚持最初的计划。

被动语态:
The poster was clinging to the wall. = 海报粘在墙上。
The fabric is designed to cling to the body. = 这布料设计成贴身的。

疑问句:
Is he still clinging to that idea? = 他还在坚持那个想法吗？
Why does the mud cling to your shoes? = 为什么泥巴粘在你的鞋子上？

否定句:
The paint didn't cling to the damp wood. = 油漆没有粘在潮湿的木头上。
He refused to cling to outdated traditions. = 他拒绝墨守陈规。
