grakle:/ˈɡrækl/| n.|拟椋鸟，卷尾椋鸟|A grakle is a type of bird in the family Icteridae, known for its dark plumage and often noisy calls.  They are intelligent and adaptable birds, found in various habitats across the Americas.|Example:  A large grakle perched on the fence post.|近义词：blackbird (a general term), crow (larger and generally more aggressive)|反义词：None specifically, as it refers to a specific type of bird.
