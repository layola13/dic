eyeballed:/ˈaɪˌbɔːld/ | vt. |粗略估计；用眼睛仔细察看；怒视|To estimate something quickly by looking at it; to examine something carefully with one's eyes; to glare at someone angrily. |例句：I eyeballed the distance and guessed it was about a mile. 我粗略估计了一下距离，猜想大约有一英里。  |例句：The mechanic eyeballed the engine to find the problem. 机械师仔细察看发动机以找出问题。|近义词：estimate, gauge, assess, scrutinize, glare at |反义词：precisely measure, ignore
