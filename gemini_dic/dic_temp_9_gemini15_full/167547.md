prewarmed:/ˌpriːˈwɔːrmd/| adj.|预热的，预先加热的|  Prewarmed refers to something that has been warmed up beforehand, in advance of its intended use or consumption.  It implies preparation and readiness.|例句：The prewarmed plates prevented the food from getting cold too quickly. 预热的盘子防止食物太快变冷。The coffee is prewarmed, ready for you to add the milk. 咖啡已经预热好了，你可以加牛奶了。|近义词：heated, warmed-up|反义词：cold, unheated
