podgy:/ˈpɒdʒi/| adj.| 微胖的，圆滚滚的 | Slightly fat, especially in a way that is noticeable but not excessively so.  The word often carries a less harsh connotation than "fat" or "obese," implying a softness or roundness. |例句：The podgy child giggled as he chased the ball.  那个微胖的孩子追着球咯咯地笑。  The cat had a podgy belly. 那只猫肚子圆滚滚的。|近义词：chubby, plump, stout, roly-poly |反义词：thin, slim, slender, skinny |形容词
