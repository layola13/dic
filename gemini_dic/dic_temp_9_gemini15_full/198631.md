shush:/ʃʌʃ/| interj. v.|嘘，安静|An interjection used to tell someone to be quiet.  It can also be used as a verb meaning to tell someone to be quiet.|例句：Shush! The baby is sleeping. 嘘！宝宝睡着了。  Please shush your children. 请让你的孩子们安静下来。|近义词：hush, be quiet, silence|反义词:shout, yell


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shushes the noisy children. = 他让吵闹的孩子们安静下来。
She shushes her dog when it barks too loudly. = 她狗狗叫得太大声时会让它安静。

一般过去时:
The librarian shushed the students. = 图书管理员让学生们安静下来。
He shushed me when I started to talk. = 我开始说话时，他让我安静。

现在完成时:
She has shushed the children several times this morning. = 今天早上她已经好几次让孩子们安静下来了。
The teacher has shushed the class many times. = 老师已经多次让全班安静下来了。


过去完成时:
The teacher had shushed the class before the principal arrived. = 校长来之前，老师已经让全班安静下来了。
She had shushed the baby multiple times before it finally fell asleep. = 宝宝最终睡着之前，她已经多次让它安静了。


将来时:
I will shush the children when they start fighting. = 当孩子们开始打架时，我会让他们安静下来。
She will shush him if he keeps talking during the movie. = 如果他看电影时一直说话，她会让他安静下来。


虚拟语气:
If only he would shush himself! = 但愿他能安静下来！
I wish she would shush those noisy neighbors. = 我希望她能让那些吵闹的邻居安静下来。

被动语态:
The children were shushed by the teacher. = 孩子们被老师让安静下来了。
The audience was shushed by the usher. = 观众被引座员让安静下来了。

疑问句:
Can you shush them? = 你能让他们安静下来吗？
Should I shush the children? = 我应该让孩子们安静下来吗？

否定句:
He didn't shush the dog. = 他没有让狗安静下来。
She couldn't shush the crying baby. = 她无法让哭闹的婴儿安静下来。


PHRASE->
shush someone up = 让某人安静下来
shush it = 安静！
Don't shush me! = 别让我安静！
keep shush = 保持安静
shush money = 封口费 (This phrase is less common than the others)
