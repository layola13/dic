muck-about:/ˈmʌk əˈbaʊt/ | v. |胡闹，瞎搞，浪费时间 | To spend time in a lazy, unproductive way; to fool around.  |例句：They spent the afternoon mucking about in the garden. 他们在花园里消磨了一下午的时光。|例句：Stop mucking about and get on with your work! 别胡闹了，赶紧工作！|近义词：mess around, fool around, dawdle, idle |反义词：work diligently, be productive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They muck about in the park every day. = 他们每天都在公园里瞎逛。
We muck about online a lot. = 我们经常在网上瞎逛。

一般过去时:
They mucked about in the river yesterday. = 他们昨天在河里瞎玩。
We mucked about all afternoon. = 我们整个下午都在瞎混。

现在完成时:
They have mucked about all summer. = 他们整个夏天都在瞎混。
We have mucked about too much recently. = 最近我们瞎混的时间太多了。

过去完成时:
They had mucked about for hours before they finally started working. = 他们在最终开始工作之前已经瞎混了好几个小时。
We had mucked about so much that we were behind schedule. = 我们瞎混的时间太多了，以至于进度落后了。

将来时:
They will muck about on the beach tomorrow. = 他们明天将在海滩上瞎玩。
We will muck about until it gets dark. = 我们会一直玩到天黑。

虚拟语气:
If they had not mucked about, they would have finished on time. = 如果他们没有瞎混，他们就能按时完成。
I wish I hadn't mucked about so much in my youth. = 我真希望我年轻的时候不要那么瞎混。

被动语态: (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice)
Time was mucked about. = 时间被浪费了。(This is an unusual and somewhat poetic use)
The project was delayed because time was mucked about. = 项目被耽搁了，因为时间被浪费了。(This is also unusual)

疑问句:
Are they mucking about again? = 他们又在瞎混了吗？
What are you mucking about with? = 你在瞎搞什么？

否定句:
They didn't muck about; they completed the task efficiently. = 他们没有瞎混；他们高效地完成了任务。
We won't muck about this time; we need to be serious. = 这次我们不会再瞎混了；我们需要认真对待。
