ferry:/ˈfɛri/ | n. vt. |渡船；渡口；摆渡；运送 | A ferry is a boat or ship used to carry passengers and goods across a body of water, especially a short distance.  It can also refer to the act of transporting something or someone by ferry. |例句：We took the ferry to the island. 我们乘渡轮去了岛上。

The ferry service was cancelled due to the storm. 由于暴风雨，渡轮服务被取消了。


近义词：boat, ship, transport | 反义词：N/A


词源:  The word "ferry" comes from the Old English word "ferian," meaning "to carry" or "to convey."  This in turn is related to the Proto-Germanic word "*ferjan," and ultimately to the Proto-Indo-European root "*per-", meaning "to pass through" or "to go across."
