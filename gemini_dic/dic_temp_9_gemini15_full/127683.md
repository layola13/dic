negating:/nɪˈɡeɪtɪŋ/ | v. |否定；取消；使无效 |The act of making something ineffective or untrue;  denying the truth or existence of something.  |例句：He was negating the evidence presented by the prosecution. 他在否定检方提出的证据。

例句：Her positive attitude negated the negative effects of the stressful situation. 她积极的态度抵消了压力情况的负面影响。


近义词：contradicting, denying, refuting, invalidating, nullifying | 反义词：affirming, confirming, validating, supporting


词性：动词
