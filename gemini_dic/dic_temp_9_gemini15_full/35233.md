clumper:/ˈklʌmpər/| n. |笨手笨脚的人；结块物；(尤指)笨重的鞋子|A clumper is a person who is clumsy or awkward in their movements, or a heavy, clumsy shoe or object that makes a clumping sound when walked in or moved.  It can also refer to something that forms clumps. |例句：He's a bit of a clumper, always tripping over things. 他有点笨手笨脚，总是绊倒东西。

例句：The gardener used a clumper to break up the large clods of earth. 园丁用大块的土块来敲碎大块的泥土。


近义词：klutz, oaf, lump, clod |反义词：graceful, nimble, deft
