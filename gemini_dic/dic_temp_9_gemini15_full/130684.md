nonaffiliates:/ˌnɒnəˈfɪlieɪts/ | n. | 非关联公司，非关联者 | Individuals or entities not connected to or part of a particular organization or group.  These are people or companies that have no formal relationship with each other. | Example: The study included data from both affiliates and nonaffiliates of the company. 这项研究包括了来自公司关联公司和非关联公司的的数据。 |近义词: outsiders, unconnected parties |反义词: affiliates, associates
