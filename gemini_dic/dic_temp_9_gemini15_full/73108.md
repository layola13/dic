for all one is worth:/fɔːr ɔːl wʌn ɪz wɜːrθ/ | phrase | 竭尽全力；拼尽全力 | This phrase means to do everything possible, even if it's not likely to succeed.  It implies a desperate or determined effort. |  He fought for all he was worth, but it wasn't enough. 他拼尽全力，但还是不够。

She tried for all she was worth to get the job. 她竭尽全力争取这份工作。


近义词：with all one's might, with all one's strength, to the best of one's ability,  by any means necessary

反义词： halfheartedly, lazily, carelessly


词性：Phrase (短语)
