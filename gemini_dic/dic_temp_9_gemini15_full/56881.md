doses:/ˈdoʊsiːz/ | n. |剂量，份量 |The amount of a medicine or drug taken at one time.  It can also refer to a quantity of something else, often something unpleasant. |例句：The doctor prescribed three doses of medicine a day. 医生每天开了三剂药。|例句：He received a heavy dose of criticism for his actions. 他因为自己的行为受到了严厉的批评。|近义词：amount, quantity, portion |反义词：none


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor prescribes three doses of medicine daily. = 医生每天开三剂药。
She takes two doses of vitamins each morning. = 她每天早上服用两剂维生素。

一般过去时:
He took his medication in two doses yesterday. = 他昨天分两次服药。
The patient received several doses of antibiotics. = 病人接受了几剂抗生素。


现在完成时:
She has already taken her morning dose of medication. = 她已经服用了早上的药。
I have not received the full dose of the treatment yet. = 我还没有接受全部剂量的治疗。

过去完成时:
He had already taken his dose before the doctor arrived. = 医生来之前他已经服药了。
They had administered several doses before realizing the side effects. = 他们在意识到副作用之前已经服用了几剂。

将来时:
I will take my dose of medicine before bed. = 我睡前会服用我的药。
The nurse will administer the doses at regular intervals. = 护士将定期服用药物。

虚拟语气:
If I took a larger dose, I might feel better. = 如果我服用更大剂量的药物，我可能会感觉好一些。
I wish I had taken a smaller dose. = 我真希望我服用较小剂量的药物。

被动语态:
The medicine was administered in three doses. = 药物分三次服用。
The patients were given varying doses of the drug. = 病人接受了不同剂量的药物。

疑问句:
How many doses of the medication should I take? = 我应该服用多少剂量的药物？
What is the recommended dose for children? = 儿童的推荐剂量是多少？

否定句:
I did not take my medication dose this morning. = 我今天早上没有服用药物。
The patient did not receive the correct dose of the medicine. = 病人没有接受正确的药物剂量。
