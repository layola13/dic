radiata:/reɪdiˈeɪtə/| adj.|辐射状的，放射状的| radiating from a center; arranged in rays.  The term often describes organisms or structures with a radial symmetry, meaning they can be divided into similar halves by any plane passing through the center.|例句：The starfish exhibits a radiata body plan.  海星呈现辐射状的身体结构。|例句：The flower petals were arranged in a beautiful radiata pattern. 花瓣以美丽的辐射状排列。|近义词：radial, rayed|反义词：bilateral
