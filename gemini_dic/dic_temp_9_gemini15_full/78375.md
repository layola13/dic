词源:
From gesture + -al.  The word "gesture" itself originates from the Old French *geste*, meaning "deed, action", ultimately derived from the Latin *gestus*, past participle of *gerere*, meaning "to carry, bear, do, perform".  The suffix "-al" indicates an adjectival form.
