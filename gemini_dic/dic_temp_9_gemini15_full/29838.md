causability:/ˌkɔːzəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 因果关系；可因果性 | The ability to establish a cause-and-effect relationship between events.  It explores the relationship between cause and effect, examining whether one event leads directly to another.  | Example:  The study aimed to establish the causality between smoking and lung cancer. 这项研究旨在确定吸烟和肺癌之间的因果关系。

The research questioned the causality of the observed phenomena. 这项研究对观察到的现象的因果关系提出了质疑。


近义词：causation, correlation (if implying a potential causal link) | 反义词：randomness, chance, coincidence | 词性: noun
