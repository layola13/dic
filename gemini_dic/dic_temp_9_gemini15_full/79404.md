glimpse:/ɡlɪmps/ | n. v. |一瞥；短暂的体验；偷偷看| a short, quick view of something |例句：I caught a glimpse of the ocean. 我瞥见了大海。 I glimpsed a figure in the distance. 我瞥见远处一个人影。|近义词：peek, glance, glimpse|反义词：stare, scrutinize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I glimpse the sunrise every morning. = 我每天早晨都会瞥见日出。
She glimpses the future through her dreams. = 她透过梦境瞥见未来。


一般过去时:
He glimpsed a familiar face in the crowd. = 他在人群中瞥见了一张熟悉的脸。
They glimpsed a deer running through the woods. = 他们瞥见一只鹿跑过树林。


现在完成时:
I have glimpsed the potential of this project. = 我已经瞥见了这个项目的潜力。
She has glimpsed the meaning behind the painting. = 她已经瞥见了这幅画背后的含义。


过去完成时:
Before the accident, he had glimpsed the danger ahead. = 事故发生前，他已经瞥见了前面的危险。
They had glimpsed the approaching storm before taking shelter. = 他们在躲避之前已经瞥见了逼近的风暴。


将来时:
I will glimpse the stars tonight. = 我今晚将会看到星星。
We will glimpse the results of our hard work soon. = 我们很快就会看到我们努力工作的成果。


虚拟语气:
If I could glimpse the future, I would be prepared. = 如果我能预见未来，我会做好准备。
I wish I could glimpse the solution to this problem. = 我希望我能瞥见这个问题的解决方案。


被动语态:
The mountain peak was glimpsed through the clouds. = 山峰透过云层隐约可见。
The rare bird was glimpsed only once. = 那只稀有的鸟只被看到过一次。


疑问句:
Did you glimpse anything unusual? = 你看到什么异常了吗？
Have you ever glimpsed a shooting star? = 你曾经看到过流星吗？


否定句:
I didn't glimpse anything suspicious. = 我没有看到任何可疑的东西。
They didn't glimpse the thief escaping. = 他们没有看到小偷逃跑。


PHRASE->
catch a glimpse of = 瞥见
a fleeting glimpse of = 一闪而过的景象
a quick glimpse of = 快速地瞥一眼
get a glimpse of = 瞥见
offer a glimpse of = 提供一瞥
