irritates:/ˈɪrɪteɪts/| v.|使恼怒；使烦躁；刺激|To annoy or bother someone; to cause a feeling of slight anger or impatience;  to act as a stimulant or irritant on (a part of the body).|例句：The constant noise irritates me.  持续的噪音让我很烦躁。|例句：The rough fabric irritates my skin.粗糙的布料刺激我的皮肤。|近义词：annoys, bothers, frustrates, aggravates|反义词：soothes, calms, pleases


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud music irritates me. = 吵闹的音乐让我心烦。
This constant nagging irritates her. = 这种不断的唠叨让她心烦。


一般过去时:
The smell of smoke irritated him. = 烟味让他感到不舒服。
The teacher's harsh words irritated the students. = 老师严厉的话语激怒了学生们。


现在完成时:
The dust has irritated my eyes. = 灰尘弄疼了我的眼睛。
His behaviour has irritated everyone in the office. = 他的行为惹恼了办公室里的每个人。


过去完成时:
The long wait had irritated the passengers. = 漫长的等待已经激怒了乘客们。
He had irritated his boss with his carelessness. = 他因粗心大意惹恼了他的老板。


将来时:
The heat will irritate her skin. = 炎热的天气会刺激她的皮肤。
The delay will irritate the customers. = 延误会激怒顾客。


虚拟语气:
If the noise didn't irritate me, I would stay longer. = 如果噪音不让我心烦，我会待久一点。
I wish the itchy rash wouldn't irritate my skin so much. = 我真希望这种痒痒的皮疹不会这么刺激我的皮肤。


被动语态:
He was irritated by the constant interruptions. = 他被不断的打断弄得很烦。
Many people are irritated by the bad traffic. = 很多人因为糟糕的交通而感到恼火。


疑问句:
Does the smoke irritate your eyes? = 烟熏到你的眼睛了吗？
Does this fabric irritate your skin? = 这布料会刺激你的皮肤吗？


否定句:
The gentle breeze did not irritate him. = 温柔的微风没有让他感到不舒服。
The soft music didn't irritate her at all. = 轻柔的音乐一点也没有让她心烦。
