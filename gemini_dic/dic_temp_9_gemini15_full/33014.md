chorography:/kɔːriˈɒɡrəfi/| n. |编舞；舞蹈设计；舞蹈编排 |The art or practice of designing sequences of movements for dance.  It involves planning and arranging steps, patterns, and formations to create a visually appealing and expressive performance. |例句：The choreography for the ballet was breathtaking.  这出芭蕾舞的编舞令人叹为观止。|例句：The choreographer worked closely with the dancers to perfect the choreography. 编舞师与舞者密切合作，以完善编舞。|近义词：dance composition, staging|反义词：无
