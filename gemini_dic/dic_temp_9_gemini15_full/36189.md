cofounder:/ˈkoʊfaʊndər/| n.|共同创始人|A cofounder is one of the people who jointly founded an organization, company, or other entity.  They share the responsibility and credit for its creation.|Example:  Steve Jobs was a cofounder of Apple. 史蒂夫·乔布斯是苹果公司的共同创始人。  Bill Gates and Paul Allen were cofounders of Microsoft. 比尔·盖茨和保罗·艾伦是微软的共同创始人。|近义词：joint founder, founding partner|反义词：sole founder
