kilometre:/ˈkɪləmiːtər/ | n. |千米，公里 | A kilometre is a unit of length equal to 1000 meters.  It is commonly used in many countries as the standard unit for measuring distance. | The distance between the two cities is approximately 200 kilometres.  两座城市之间的距离大约是200公里。  The marathon is a 42.195-kilometre race. 马拉松是一场42.195公里的比赛。|meter, mile |inch, foot |名词
