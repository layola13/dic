They often show disdain or disbelief towards something or someone. |例句：He was a scoffer of religious beliefs. 他是一个嘲笑宗教信仰的人。  |例句：The scoffer laughed at the idea of a peaceful solution.  嘲笑者嘲笑和平解决的方案。|近义词：mocker, jeerer, cynic, doubter |反义词：believer, admirer, supporter |名词
