dictagraph:/ˈdɪktəɡræf/| n. |录音机，窃听器|A machine for recording dictation or conversations, often secretly.  A dictagraph is a type of recording device, often associated with covert surveillance, capable of recording conversations without the knowledge of the participants.  |例句：The detective planted a dictagraph in the suspect's office.  侦探在嫌疑人的办公室里安装了一个窃听器。

例句：They used a dictagraph to gather evidence for the case.他们用录音机收集案件证据。


近义词：recording device, listening device, bug |反义词：None specifically, but terms implying open communication could be considered antonyms in context.  |词性：名词
