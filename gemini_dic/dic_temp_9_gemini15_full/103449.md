/dʒoʊ/| n. |乔（男子名）|A common given name for males, often short for Joseph.  |My friend's name is Joe. 我的朋友名叫乔。|Joseph, Joey|  


USAGE->
This word is a proper noun and doesn't have typical verb conjugations like "what."  Proper nouns don't conjugate.  The word "Joe" itself doesn't have tenses or voice changes.


PHRASE->
Joe Blow:  an ordinary or average person (普通老百姓)
Joe Sixpack: an average, ordinary working-class man (普通工人)
every Joe: every ordinary person (每个人)
plain Joe: an ordinary person, especially a man (普通人)
Joe Public: the average man or woman; the general public (大众)
