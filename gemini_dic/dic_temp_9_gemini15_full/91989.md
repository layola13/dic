hypernormality:/ˌhaɪpərˌnɔːrˈmæləti/| n. |超常性，过度正常|The state or condition of being excessively normal; a seeming normalcy that masks or hides underlying dysfunction or abnormality.|例句：The hypernormality of the company's financial reports hid the impending bankruptcy. 公司财务报表的超常性掩盖了即将到来的破产。  The hypernormality of his behavior masked a deep-seated anxiety. 他行为的超常性掩盖了他根深蒂固的焦虑。|近义词：supernormality, facade, normalcy (in a deceptive sense)|反义词：abnormality, dysfunction, irregularity
