coreplasty:/ˈkɔːrəplæsti/| n.|核心成形术|A surgical procedure to reshape or repair the core of a body part, often used in relation to the heart or other organs.  It is a relatively rare and specialized procedure.|例句：The surgeon performed a coreplasty to correct the structural defect in the patient's heart.  外科医生进行了核心成形术来矫正病人心脏的结构缺陷。|近义词：reconstruction, repair, reshaping|反义词：destruction, removal
