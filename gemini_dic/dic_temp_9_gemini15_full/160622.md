portamento:/pɔːrˈtɑːmentoʊ/ | n. | 滑音，连音 | A smooth transition between two adjacent notes, usually without a break in the sound.  It is a musical technique where the pitch glides smoothly from one note to the next, rather than playing each note separately. | Example: The violinist played with a beautiful portamento between the high C and the D.  小提琴家在高音C和D之间演奏了一个优美的滑音。|近义词：glide, slur | 反义词：staccato

词源:
