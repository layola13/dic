putt:/pʌt/| v. n.|轻推；轻击（高尔夫球）；（球）轻轻滚；|to gently tap or push something, especially a golf ball.|He putted the ball into the hole. 他轻轻一击将球击入洞中。 I need a new putter. 我需要一个新的推杆。|近义词：tap, nudge, roll|反义词：strike, hit hard


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He putts the ball every Sunday. = 他每周日都推杆击球。
She putts with great precision. = 她推杆非常精准。


一般过去时:
He putted the ball into the hole on his first try. = 他第一次尝试就将球推入洞中。
She putted well in the tournament. = 她在锦标赛中推杆表现出色。


现在完成时:
He has putted many balls in his lifetime. = 他一生中推过许多球。
She has never putted so well before. = 她以前从未推过这么好的球。


过去完成时:
He had putted several times before he finally sank the ball. = 他在最终将球击入洞之前已经推了几次了。
She had putted poorly throughout the round. = 她在整轮比赛中推杆表现不佳。


将来时:
He will putt first on the next hole. = 下一个洞他将首先推杆。
She will putt more confidently after her practice. = 练习之后她会更有信心地推杆。


虚拟语气:
If he putted better, he would win the match. = 如果他推杆更好，他就能赢得比赛。
I wish I could putt like a professional. = 我希望我能像职业选手一样推杆。


被动语态:
The ball was putted skillfully by the golfer. = 高尔夫球手熟练地推杆击球。
The putt was missed slightly. = 推杆稍微失误了。


疑问句:
Did he putt the ball well? = 他推杆击球好吗？
Will she putt first? = 她会先推杆吗？


否定句:
He didn't putt the ball very well. = 他推杆击球不太好。
She didn't putt for birdie. = 她没有为了小鸟球而推杆。
