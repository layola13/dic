dumbass:/ˈdʌmbæs/| n. |蠢货，笨蛋 | A stupid or foolish person.  The word is highly offensive and should be avoided in formal settings. | Example:  He's such a dumbass; he didn't even check his answers. 他真是个笨蛋，连答案都没检查。|近义词：idiot, moron, fool, jerk |反义词：genius, intelligent, smart |名词
