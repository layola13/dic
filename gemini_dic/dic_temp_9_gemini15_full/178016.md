recklessly:/ˈrɛklɪsli/ | adv. | 鲁莽地；不顾后果地 |  Acting without regard for the consequences; rashly.  | 例句：He recklessly spent all his money. 他鲁莽地花光了他的所有钱。

例句：She recklessly drove through the red light. 她鲁莽地闯红灯。


近义词：rashly, carelessly, heedlessly, imprudently, thoughtlessly

反义词：carefully, cautiously, prudently, deliberately


词源:  The word "recklessly" derives from the Old English word "recce," meaning "care,"  and "lēas," meaning "without."  Over time, "recce" evolved into "reck," and the addition of "-less" signified the absence of care.  The adverbial suffix "-ly" was then added to create "recklessly."
