corked:/kɔːrkt/| adj.|塞住的；有缺陷的；（酒）有塞味儿的|Having a cork in it; spoiled by cork taint (in wine).  A corked wine has a musty or moldy smell and taste. |例句：The bottle was tightly corked. 这瓶酒被紧紧地塞上了。

The wine was corked and undrinkable. 这酒有塞味，不能喝。|近义词：plugged, sealed, tainted |反义词：uncorked, open, fresh


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The bottle is corked. = 这瓶酒塞上了。
He corks the bottle every night. = 他每天晚上都塞酒瓶。


一般过去时:
She corked the jar yesterday. = 她昨天塞上了那个罐子。
The winemaker corked all the bottles. = 酿酒师塞上了所有的瓶子。


现在完成时:
I have corked all the bottles. = 我已经塞上了所有的瓶子。
They have corked the wine for aging. = 他们已经塞好酒以便陈酿。


过去完成时:
He had corked the bottle before he left. = 他离开前已经塞好了瓶子。
The wine had been corked for five years. = 这酒已经塞了五年了。


将来时:
I will cork the bottle tomorrow. = 我明天将塞上这个瓶子。
She will cork the wine after fermentation. = 发酵后她会塞上酒。


虚拟语气:
If I had corked the bottle properly, the wine wouldn't have spoiled. = 如果我当初把瓶子塞好，酒就不会坏掉了。
I wish I had corked the wine earlier. = 我真希望我早点把酒塞上。


被动语态:
The bottle was corked tightly. = 瓶子被紧紧地塞上了。
The wine is corked and cannot be sold. = 这酒有塞味，不能卖。


疑问句:
Is the bottle corked? = 瓶子塞上了吗？
Has the wine been corked properly? = 这酒塞好吗？


否定句:
The bottle isn't corked properly. = 瓶子没塞好。
He didn't cork the bottle. = 他没有塞瓶子。


PHRASE->

corked bottle:  塞好的瓶子
corked wine:  有塞味的酒
corked up:  塞住的，堵住的
tightly corked: 紧紧塞住的
well-corked:  塞得很好的


词源:

From Middle English *corke, from Old French *corc, from Latin *cortex "bark," from Proto-Indo-European *(k)or- "skin".  The sense development is from "bark" to "cork" (the bark of the cork oak) to "cork stopper".
