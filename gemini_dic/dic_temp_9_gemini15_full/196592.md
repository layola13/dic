setbacks:/ˈsɛtbæks/ | n. |挫折；倒退；障碍 | A setback is a difficulty or problem that delays or prevents progress.  It can refer to a temporary defeat or a reversal of fortune. |例句：The project suffered a major setback due to unforeseen circumstances.  这个项目由于不可预见的情况而遭受了重大挫折。  The recent economic downturn represents a significant setback for many businesses. 最近的经济衰退对许多企业来说是一个重大挫折。|近义词：reversal, impediment, obstacle, hindrance, reverse |反义词：advancement, progress, success
