reconfirmed:/ˌriːkənˈfɜːrmd/| v. |再次确认；重新确认| To confirm something again, especially after it has been questioned or doubted.  It implies a stronger emphasis on the confirmation than a simple confirmation. |例句：The airline reconfirmed my flight reservation after I called to check.  航空公司在我打电话查询后再次确认了我的航班预订。

例句：The committee reconfirmed its support for the project despite the recent setbacks. 尽管最近遭遇挫折，委员会还是再次确认了对该项目的支持。


近义词：confirmed, verified, validated, reaffirmed

反义词：denied, refuted, disproved

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reconfirm the booking every week. = 他们每周都再次确认预订。
We reconfirm our commitment to quality. = 我们再次确认我们对质量的承诺。

一般过去时:
He reconfirmed his intention to resign. = 他再次确认了他辞职的意图。
She reconfirmed the address before sending the package. = 她寄包裹之前再次确认了地址。

现在完成时:
They have reconfirmed their attendance at the meeting. = 他们已经再次确认了他们参加会议。
We have reconfirmed the details of the contract. = 我们已经再次确认了合同的细节。

过去完成时:
He had reconfirmed the appointment before the client arrived. = 客户到达之前，他已经再次确认了预约。
She had reconfirmed the flight schedule before leaving for the airport. = 她去机场之前已经再次确认了航班时刻表。

将来时:
They will reconfirm the order once they receive the payment. = 他们收到付款后将再次确认订单。
We will reconfirm the date with you tomorrow. = 我们明天将与您再次确认日期。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would reconfirm the reservation. = 如果我是你，我会再次确认预订。
I wish they had reconfirmed the delivery time. = 我希望他们已经再次确认了送货时间。

被动语态:
The reservation was reconfirmed by the hotel. = 预订已被酒店再次确认。
The details of the agreement were reconfirmed by both parties. = 双方再次确认了协议的细节。

疑问句:
Have they reconfirmed the shipment? = 他们是否再次确认了发货？
Did she reconfirm the meeting time? = 她是否再次确认了会议时间？

否定句:
He did not reconfirm his participation in the project. = 他没有再次确认他参与该项目。
They did not reconfirm the booking, so it was cancelled. = 他们没有再次确认预订，所以它被取消了。
