integument:/ɪnˈtɛɡjumənt/ | n. | 外皮；覆盖物 | A natural covering of an organism or one of its parts.  It refers to the outer protective layer of an organism, like skin in animals or bark in trees. | Example: The integument of the snake is smooth and scaly.  这条蛇的皮肤光滑且有鳞片。  The plant's integument protected the seed from the harsh environment. 植物的表皮保护种子免受恶劣环境的影响。 | 近义词：covering, skin, peel, rind | 反义词：interior, inside
