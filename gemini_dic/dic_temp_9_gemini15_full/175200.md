ragtimer:/ˈræɡˌtaɪmər/| n. |拉格泰姆音乐演奏者；拉格泰姆音乐作品|A ragtimer is a person who plays or composes ragtime music.  It can also refer to a piece of ragtime music itself. |Example:  Scott Joplin was a famous ragtimer.  斯科特·乔普林是一位著名的拉格泰姆音乐演奏者。The lively ragtimer filled the dance hall.  欢快的拉格泰姆音乐充满了舞厅。|近义词：ragtime musician, ragtime composer|反义词：无
