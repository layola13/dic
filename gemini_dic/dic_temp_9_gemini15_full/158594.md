pleuropneumonia: /ˌplʊərəʊpnuˈmoʊniə/ | n. |胸膜肺炎| A serious lung infection that affects both the lungs (pneumonia) and the pleura (the membrane surrounding the lungs). It's characterized by inflammation of both the lungs and the lining of the lungs.  The disease is often caused by bacteria, viruses, or fungi and can be life-threatening if left untreated. |The farmer reported a case of pleuropneumonia in his cattle.  这位农民报告说他的牛患上了胸膜肺炎。| pneumonia, lung infection | health, wellness | noun
