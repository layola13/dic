redemonstrations:/ˌriːˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃənz/| n.|再次示威，重新示威|Repeated demonstrations; a second or subsequent demonstration.  The word is formed by combining "re-" (again) with "demonstrations".|例句：Following the initial protests, there were several redemonstrations throughout the city. = 首次抗议之后，全市各地又进行了多次示威。

The redemonstrations against the new policy were even larger than the first. = 反对新政策的再次示威比第一次规模更大。|近义词：re-demonstrations, renewed protests|反义词：cessation, end, conclusion|名词
