leukaemia:/luːˈkiːmiə/| n. |白血病|A cancer of the blood or bone marrow, characterized by an abnormal increase of immature white blood cells.  It is a serious and often fatal disease, requiring intensive medical treatment.|例句：The diagnosis of leukaemia was a devastating blow to the family.  白血病的诊断对这个家庭来说是毁灭性的打击。|近义词：blood cancer, leukemia (American spelling)|反义词：N/A
