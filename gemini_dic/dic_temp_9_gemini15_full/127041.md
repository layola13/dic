nastier:/ˈnæstiər/| adj.|更令人作呕的，更讨厌的，更恶劣的|More unpleasant or offensive; more disagreeable.  It suggests a higher degree of unpleasantness than "nasty".|例句：The weather today is nastier than yesterday. = 今天的天气比昨天更恶劣。

例句：His remarks were nastier than I expected. = 他的话比我预料的更难听。


近义词：more unpleasant, more disgusting, more offensive, more hateful, more vile

反义词：nicer, pleasanter, kinder, sweeter


词性:形容词
