cocoon:/kəˈkuːn/ | n. |茧；保护层；庇护所 | A protective covering, usually of silk, spun by a larva (like a caterpillar) to protect itself during its pupal stage.  It can also refer to anything that provides shelter or protection. | Example: The caterpillar spun a silken cocoon around itself.  毛毛虫把自己裹在一个丝茧里。 The city became a cocoon of protection during the storm.  城市在暴风雨中成了一个安全的庇护所。 |近义词：shroud, shell, casing |反义词：exposure, openness |名词
