phenologists:/fɪˈnɒlədʒɪsts/| n. |物候学家|Scientists who study phenology, the study of the timing of natural events in plant and animal life cycles, such as the timing of flowering, migration, or insect emergence.  They observe and record these events to understand the impact of climate change and other environmental factors. |Phenologists are closely monitoring the effects of climate change on the timing of bird migrations.|近义词：naturalists, ecologists, biologists|反义词：N/A
