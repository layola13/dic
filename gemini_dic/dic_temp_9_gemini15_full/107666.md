languors:/ˈlæŋɡərz/| n.|倦怠；懒散；无力；萎靡|A state of being languid; sluggishness; listlessness.  It often refers to a pleasant, dreamy state of relaxation, but can also imply weakness or apathy.|例句：The afternoon languors overcame her, and she drifted off to sleep.  午后的倦怠袭来，她昏昏沉沉地睡着了。

例句：The heat induced a state of languor in the entire town. 炎热的天气使整个城镇都处于一种萎靡不振的状态。


近义词：lethargy, lassitude, indolence, torpor|反义词：energy, vigor, alertness, activity|词性:名词
