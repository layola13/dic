cyclorama:/ˌsaɪkləˈræmə/| n. |环幕剧场；全景画布|A cyclorama is a large, curved painted backdrop used in theaters and film studios to create a panoramic or immersive background for a scene.  It often depicts a landscape, sky, or other large-scale environment, giving the illusion of depth and vastness. |The cyclorama provided a convincing backdrop for the outdoor scene. 环幕剧场为户外场景提供了令人信服的背景。 The film crew carefully lit the cyclorama to create the illusion of a sunset. 电影摄制组仔细地照亮了环幕剧场，以营造日落的错觉。|panorama, backdrop | (反义词较少，因为其功能性强于描述性)|名词
