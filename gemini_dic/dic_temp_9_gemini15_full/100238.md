interrupted: /ˌɪntəˈrʌptɪd/ | adj. | 被打断的，中断的 |  Describes something that has been stopped before it was finished.  |例句：The meeting was interrupted by a sudden power outage.  会议因突然停电而中断。

例句：Our peaceful afternoon was interrupted by the loud noise from the construction site.  我们平静的下午被建筑工地上的巨大噪音打断了。


近义词：disrupted, halted, broken off, ceased


反义词：continued, uninterrupted, unbroken


词性：形容词 (Adjective)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The phone interrupts me constantly. = 电话不断地打断我。
Noise interrupts my concentration. = 噪音打断了我的注意力。

一般过去时:
The speaker was interrupted by a heckler. = 演讲者被一个捣乱者打断了。
Our plans were interrupted by the rain. = 我们的计划被雨打断了。

现在完成时:
The project has been interrupted several times. = 这个项目已经被中断了好几次。
My work has been interrupted by unexpected visitors. = 我的工作被意外的访客打断了。

过去完成时:
The game had been interrupted by a thunderstorm. = 比赛已经被一场雷雨打断了。
The meeting had been interrupted before I arrived. = 我到达之前会议已经被打断了。

将来时:
The concert will be interrupted by a technical issue. = 音乐会将会因为技术问题而中断。
I think our trip will be interrupted by bad weather. = 我想我们的旅行会被坏天气打断了。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't been interrupted, I would have finished the report. = 如果我没有被打断，我本可以完成报告。
I wish I hadn't been interrupted during my presentation. = 我真希望我的演讲没有被打断。

被动语态:
The broadcast was interrupted by a news bulletin. = 广播被新闻简报打断了。
The meeting was interrupted several times. = 会议被中断了好几次。

疑问句:
Was the performance interrupted? = 演出被打断了吗？
Were your studies interrupted? = 你的学习被打断了吗？

否定句:
The performance was not interrupted. = 演出没有被打断。
My work was not interrupted today. = 我的工作今天没有被打断。


PHRASE->
interrupt someone's speech = 打断某人的讲话

be interrupted by = 被……打断
interruptedly = 断断续续地
