jitter:/ˈdʒɪtər/ | n. v. |抖动；焦虑；干扰 |  A slight unsteady movement or vibration.  Also, a feeling of nervousness or anxiety.  As a verb, it means to move rapidly and irregularly. |例句：The camera's jitter made the footage unusable.  摄像机的抖动使画面无法使用。

The constant jitter of the train made me feel nauseous. 火车的持续抖动使我感到恶心。


近义词：tremble, shake, vibrate, quiver, anxiety, nervousness | 反义词：stillness, calmness, stability
