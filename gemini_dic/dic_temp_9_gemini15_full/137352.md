nontaxonomic:/ˌnɒnˌtæksəˈnɒmɪk/ | adj. |非分类学的；非系统分类的 | Not related to taxonomy or classification.  This term is used to describe systems, methods, or approaches that do not follow the principles of biological classification.  For example, a nontaxonomic grouping of organisms might be based on shared ecological roles rather than evolutionary relationships. |  The grouping of these insects is nontaxonomic, based solely on their habitat preferences.  |近义词:uncategorized, unclassified |反义词:taxonomic, systematic
