hares:/hɛərz/| n.|野兔；很多；|A hare is a type of fast-running mammal, similar to a rabbit but larger and with longer ears.|例句：The hunter chased the hare across the field.  猎人追逐着穿过田野的野兔。|例句：There are hares in the garden. 花园里有野兔。|近义词：rabbit, jackrabbit|反义词：无
