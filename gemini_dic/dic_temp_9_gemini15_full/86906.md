hemline:/ˈhɛmlaɪn/| n. |裙边，衣边 |The hemline refers to the edge of a garment, particularly a skirt or dress, that is folded and sewn to finish the lower edge.  It can also refer to the length of this edge.|例句：The hemline on her dress was uneven.  她的裙子下摆不齐。|例句：This season's fashion features high hemlines. 本季的时尚特点是高腰线。|近义词：bottom edge, skirt length |反义词： (no direct antonym, but contrasting lengths like high/low hemline exist) |名词
