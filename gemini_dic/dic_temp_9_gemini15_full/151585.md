parthenogenesis:/ˌpɑːrθɛnəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ | n. | 单性生殖；孤雌生殖 | The process of reproduction from an ovum without fertilization, especially as a normal process in some invertebrates and lower plants.  This means development of an embryo from an unfertilized egg.  | Example:  Many species of insects reproduce through parthenogenesis.  许多昆虫物种通过单性生殖繁殖。

The Komodo dragon is known to reproduce via facultative parthenogenesis. 科莫多巨蜥已知可以通过兼性孤雌生殖繁殖。 |近义词：asexual reproduction, virgin birth | 反义词：sexual reproduction | 词性：名词
