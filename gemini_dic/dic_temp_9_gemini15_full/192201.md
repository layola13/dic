USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have tenses like verbs.  It modifies verbs and describes how an action is performed.

Example 1: The surface is scratchably smooth. 表面光滑易刮花。
Example 2: The cat scratched the furniture scratchably. 猫很容易就抓坏了家具。
