denunciator:/dɪˈnʌnsieɪtər/| n. |告密者，检举者 | A person who informs against someone or something, especially to the authorities.  |The denunciator provided crucial evidence that led to the criminal's arrest.  告密者提供的关键证据导致罪犯被捕。 | informer, whistleblower, squealer | supporter, accomplice | 名词
