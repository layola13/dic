nonbelievers:/ˌnɒn.bɪˈliː.vəz/| n. |不信者，非信徒|People who do not believe in a particular religion or in the existence of God.  They may also be skeptical of certain claims or ideas.|Example:  The new scientific theory was met with resistance from many nonbelievers.  许多不信者对新的科学理论表示抵制。


Example: Many nonbelievers questioned the preacher's claims.  许多不信者质疑传教士的说法。


近义词: skeptics, doubters, atheists (if referring to religious belief), infidels (often used pejoratively)


反义词: believers, faithful, adherents


词性:名词
