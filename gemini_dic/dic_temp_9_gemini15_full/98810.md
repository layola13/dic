intellective: /ɪnˈtɛlektɪv/ | adj. | 智力的；理解力的；理智的 | Relating to or characteristic of intellect; intellectual.  The word emphasizes the cognitive and reasoning aspects of intelligence. |例句：His intellective abilities were exceptional, allowing him to solve complex problems with ease.  他的理解力非凡，能够轻松解决复杂的问题。

例句：The test measured the intellective skills of the participants, focusing on their reasoning and problem-solving capabilities.  这项测试测量了参与者的智力技能，重点是他们的推理和解决问题的能力。
