normal:/ˈnɔːrməl/ /ˈnɔːrməl/| adj.|正常的，标准的，普通的| conforming to a standard; usual, typical, or expected.|The temperature is normal today.|近义词：regular, typical, usual, standard|反义词：abnormal, unusual, exceptional, extraordinary


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The temperature is normal. = 温度正常。
He feels normal today.= 他今天感觉正常。

一般过去时:
Everything was normal yesterday. = 昨天一切正常。
The situation returned to normal after the storm. = 风暴过后情况恢复正常。

现在完成时:
Things have returned to normal. = 情况已经恢复正常。
She has felt normal since her recovery. = 她自康复以来感觉一直很好。

过去完成时:
Everything had been normal until the accident. = 直到事故发生前，一切都很正常。
The situation had returned to normal before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，情况已经恢复正常。

将来时:
Everything will be normal tomorrow. = 明天一切都会恢复正常。
I expect things will return to normal soon. = 我预计情况很快就会恢复正常。

虚拟语气:
If everything were normal, we wouldn't be so worried. = 如果一切正常，我们就不会这么担心。
I wish things were normal again. = 我希望事情能恢复正常。

被动语态:
The situation is considered normal by experts. = 专家认为这种情况是正常的。
Normal conditions were restored quickly after the fire. = 大火过后，情况很快恢复正常。

疑问句:
Is everything normal? = 一切正常吗？
Is that a normal reaction? = 那是正常的反应吗？

否定句:
It's not normal to feel this way. = 感觉这样是不正常的。
His behavior was not normal. = 他的行为不正常。
