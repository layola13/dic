defueling: /diːˈfjuːəlɪŋ/ | v. |  去燃料；排放燃料 | The process of removing fuel from something, typically a vehicle or a nuclear reactor.  | Example: The defueling of the nuclear reactor took several weeks.  核反应堆的去燃料过程花费了数周时间。|近义词：fuel removal, discharge | 反义词：refueling


USAGE->

They defuel the plane before maintenance. = 他们在维护飞机之前排放燃油。
The company defuels its vehicles regularly. = 这家公司定期为其车辆排放燃油。

一般过去时:
The engineers defueled the reactor safely. = 工程师安全地排放了反应堆的燃料。
We defueled the car before scrapping it. = 我们在报废汽车之前排放了汽油。
