sanny:/ˈsæni/| n. |圣人，圣徒；(印度教)苦行僧|A sannyasi is a renunciate in Hinduism who has given up worldly possessions and attachments.  |例句：He decided to become a sannyasi and live a life of asceticism. 他决定成为一名苦行僧，过着苦行生活。  |例句：Many sannyasins live in ashrams and dedicate their lives to spiritual practices. 许多苦行僧住在阿什拉姆，并将自己的一生奉献给灵性修行。|近义词：ascetic, hermit, monk |反义词：materialist, hedonist
