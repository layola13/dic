perfectionize:/pərˈfɛkʃənaɪz/ | vt. |使完美；完善；改进|To improve something to make it perfect or as close to perfect as possible.  This often implies a process of refinement and meticulous attention to detail. |例句：He spent years perfectionizing his technique. 他花了数年时间来完善他的技巧。

例句：The company is constantly striving to perfectionize its products. 这家公司不断努力完善其产品。


近义词：perfect, refine, polish, optimize, improve |反义词：ruin, spoil, impair, worsen |词性:动词
