leiger:/ˈleɪdʒər/| n. |分类账，总账|A leiger is a large book used for keeping accounts, especially a ledger used in a bank or other financial institution.  It's now largely obsolete due to computerized accounting systems.| The bank's leiger showed a significant increase in deposits last quarter.  银行的分类账显示上季度存款大幅增加。|account book, register|  (近义词缺乏直接对应，因为其指代特定类型的账本)|  (反义词缺乏直接对应)|名词
