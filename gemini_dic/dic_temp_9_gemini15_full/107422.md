lampad:/ˈlæmpæd/| n.|油灯，灯；(诗)火焰|A lampad is a type of lamp, often associated with religious ceremonies or symbolic representations of light and hope.  It is sometimes used poetically to refer to a flame.|The ancient temple was dimly lit by several lampads.|近义词：lamp, light, lantern|反义词：darkness
