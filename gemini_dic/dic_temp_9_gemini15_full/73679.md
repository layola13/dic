forgetfulness:/fərˈɡɛtnəs/| n. |健忘；遗忘；疏忽 |The state or condition of being forgetful; the inability or failure to remember something.  It can also refer to a lack of attention or care. |例句：His forgetfulness was becoming a problem at work. 他的健忘在工作中越来越成为一个问题。

例句：Her forgetfulness led to missed appointments and overdue bills.  她的健忘导致错过约会和逾期账单。


近义词：oblivion, amnesia, lapse of memory, neglect

反义词：remembrance, recollection, mindfulness


词性：名词
