overachievers:/ˌoʊvərəˈtʃivərz/| n. |高成就者，过度努力的人|People who consistently exceed expectations and achieve more than is considered normal or necessary. They often strive for perfection and may experience pressure to maintain high standards.|例句：Many overachievers burn out from the constant pressure to excel. 许多高成就者因为不断追求卓越的压力而筋疲力尽。

例句：She's an overachiever; she always gets top marks in every subject. 她是个高成就者；她每门功课都考第一。


近义词：high achievers, perfectionists, strivers|反义词：underachievers, slackers
