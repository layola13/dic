overexpand:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspænd/ | vt. |过度扩张；膨胀过度 | To expand beyond reasonable or safe limits.  |例句：The company overexpanded its operations and subsequently faced financial difficulties. 公司过度扩张业务，随后面临财务困境。|例句：The balloon overexpanded and burst. 气球过度膨胀并爆裂。|近义词：overextend, inflate excessively, expand too much |反义词：contract, shrink, downsize
