reseizure: /ˌriːˈsiːʒər/ | n. |  重新占有；再扣押 | The act of seizing again; the retaking of something that had been seized.  It often implies a legal or official process. |  例句：The government ordered the reseizure of the smuggled goods. = 政府下令重新查封走私货物。

例句：The reseizure of the property was met with protests from the previous owner. =  对该房产的重新查封遭到了前任业主的抗议。


近义词：recapture, repossession, retaking | 反义词：release, relinquishment


词源:  re- (again) + seizure (the act of seizing)  Seizure itself comes from the Old French  *seisir, meaning "to seize," which ultimately derives from the Latin *sapere, "to taste, to take hold of."
