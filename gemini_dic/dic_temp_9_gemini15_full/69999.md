feted:/ˈfeɪtɪd/| v.|庆祝，赞扬|to celebrate or honor a person or event with a festive gathering or entertainment.|The actress was feted at a lavish Hollywood party.  这位女演员在好莱坞的一个奢华派对上受到盛大的庆祝。|The newly elected mayor was feted by supporters.  新当选的市长受到了支持者的盛赞。|honor, celebrate, acclaim|ignore, neglect|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They fete the winners every year. = 他们每年都庆祝获奖者。
We fete our anniversary with a special dinner. = 我们用特别的晚餐来庆祝我们的周年纪念日。

一般过去时:
The team was feted after their victory. = 球队在胜利后受到庆祝。
She was feted for her contribution to science. = 她因对科学的贡献而受到赞扬。

现在完成时:
The artist has been feted worldwide for his groundbreaking work. =这位艺术家因其开创性的作品而享誉全球。
They have feted the new product launch with a huge marketing campaign. =他们为新产品的发布举办了一场大型的营销活动来庆祝。

过去完成时:
The company had feted its employees before the announcement of the merger. = 在宣布合并之前，公司已经款待了它的员工。
They had feted the retiring CEO with a grand farewell dinner.  他们为退休的首席执行官举办了一场盛大的告别晚宴。

将来时:
The city will fete the Olympic athletes next month. =下个月，这座城市将为奥运会运动员举行庆祝活动。
We will fete our parents' golden wedding anniversary. = 我们将庆祝父母的金婚纪念日。

虚拟语气:
If they won the game, they would be feted by the entire city. =如果他们赢得了比赛，他们将受到全城的庆祝。
I wish I could be feted for my achievements. =我希望我能因为我的成就而受到赞扬。

被动语态:
The champion was feted by the crowd. = 冠军受到了人群的庆祝。
The new movie is being feted by critics. =这部新电影受到了影评人的好评。

疑问句:
Will the author be feted at the book launch?  作者会在新书发布会上受到庆祝吗？
Was she feted after her successful performance?  她在成功演出后受到庆祝了吗？

否定句:
They did not fete the losing team. =他们没有庆祝输掉的球队。
The company didn't fete its employees this year. =今年公司没有款待员工。
