mand:/mænd/ | v. | 命令，指示，强制 | To give an order or instruction to someone; to compel someone to do something. | The general mandated a full retreat.将军下令全面撤退。 | order, command, instruct, direct | obey, comply | v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mandates a new policy. = 他强制实行一项新政策。
The law mandates that all drivers wear seatbelts. = 法律规定所有驾驶员必须系安全带。


一般过去时:
The king mandated the construction of a new palace. = 国王下令建造一座新宫殿。
She mandated a complete overhaul of the system. = 她强制要求对系统进行彻底检修。


现在完成时:
The government has mandated several new environmental regulations. = 政府已经强制实施了几项新的环境法规。
They have mandated a change in company policy. = 他们已强制执行公司政策的变更。


过去完成时:
The court had mandated the release of the prisoner. = 法院已下令释放囚犯。
Before the meeting, the CEO had mandated a review of the budget. = 会议前，首席执行官已下令审查预算。


将来时:
The council will mandate a ban on smoking in public places. = 理事会将强制禁止在公共场所吸烟。
The president will mandate economic reforms. = 总统将强制实行经济改革。


虚拟语气:
If the judge mandated a retrial, we would have to start again. = 如果法官下令重审，我们就必须重新开始。
I wish the boss hadn't mandated such strict deadlines. = 我真希望老板没有强制执行这么严格的最后期限。


被动语态:
A new tax was mandated by the government. = 新税收是由政府强制实施的。
Stricter safety measures were mandated following the accident. = 事故发生后，强制实施了更严格的安全措施。


疑问句:
Did the manager mandate overtime? = 经理是否强制加班？
What reforms did the government mandate? = 政府强制实施了什么改革？


否定句:
The court did not mandate the release of the suspect. = 法院没有下令释放嫌疑人。
The company did not mandate the use of that software. = 公司没有强制使用该软件。


PHRASE->
mandate a change = 强制改变
mandate a policy = 实施一项政策
mandate a review = 强制审查
mandate compliance = 强制遵守
under mandate = 根据授权
