domain:/dəˈmeɪn/| n.|领域，范围，领地|The domain of science is vast and ever-expanding. 科学领域广阔且不断发展。 |近义词：field, realm, area, territory |反义词：undefinable, limitless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This software is in the public domain. = 这个软件是公共领域的。
His area of expertise is the domain of astrophysics. = 他的专业领域是天体物理学。

一般过去时:
The king ruled his domain with an iron fist. = 国王铁腕统治他的领地。
The company's domain expanded significantly last year. = 该公司的领域去年大幅扩张。

现在完成时:
They have expanded their domain into new markets. = 他们已将业务领域扩展到新的市场。
The scientist has dedicated his life to the domain of biological research. = 科学家将毕生奉献给了生物学研究领域。

过去完成时:
Before the merger, the two companies had separate domains of expertise. = 在合并之前，两家公司拥有各自的专业领域。
The artist had already established a domain in the art world before winning the prestigious award. = 在获得该著名奖项之前，这位艺术家已经在艺术界确立了自己的地位。

将来时:
The company will expand its domain into the Asian market. = 公司将把业务领域扩展到亚洲市场。
We will explore the domain of artificial intelligence in the next project. = 我们将在下一个项目中探索人工智能领域。

虚拟语气:
If only I had the knowledge to enter this domain. = 要是我有进入这个领域的知识就好了。
It is important that we respect the domain of others. = 我们尊重他人的领域是很重要的。

被动语态:
The land was claimed as his domain by the conqueror. = 这片土地被征服者宣布为他的领地。
This topic is often considered to be outside the domain of philosophy. = 这个话题通常被认为不在哲学的范畴之内。

疑问句:
What is the domain of your research? = 你研究的领域是什么？
Does this problem fall within the domain of mathematics? = 这个问题属于数学领域吗？

否定句:
That issue is not within the domain of my expertise. = 那个问题不在我的专业领域之内。
This concept does not belong to the domain of physics. = 这个概念不属于物理学领域。
