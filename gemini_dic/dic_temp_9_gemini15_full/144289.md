outspeeds:/ˌaʊtˈspiːdz/| vt.|超过速度；跑赢|To surpass in speed; to exceed the speed of.|例句：The cheetah outspeeds most other land animals.  猎豹的速度超过大多数其他陆地动物。
The new car outspeeds its predecessor. 这辆新车比它的前代车型速度更快。|近义词：outstrip, outrun, surpass|反义词：be outsped by


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cheetah outspeeds most other land animals. = 猎豹的速度超过大多数其他陆地动物。
This train outspeeds that one. = 这列火车比那列火车快。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:

They had outsped the storm and reached shelter. = 他们已经超过了风暴并到达了避难所。

虚拟语气:
