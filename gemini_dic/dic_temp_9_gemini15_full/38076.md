compositor:/kəmˈpɒzɪtər/| n. |排字工人；排版员；合成器|A compositor is a person who sets type, arranges type, or composes pages for printing.  In the digital age, a compositor might also use computer software to arrange and format text and images for printing or online publication.| Example: In the old days, skilled compositors were essential for newspaper production.  从前，熟练的排字工人对于报纸的制作至关重要。|近义词：typesetter, typesetter, printer|反义词：reader (in the context of proofreading and correcting errors)|
