foresee:/fɔːˈsiː/ | vt. | 预见，预知 | To see or know beforehand; to predict.  |例句：Experts foresee a rise in inflation next year. 专家们预见明年通货膨胀将会上升。

例句：I could foresee the problems this would cause. 我能预见这会引起的问题。


近义词：predict, anticipate, foretell, envision | 反义词：overlook, miss, neglect | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I foresee a bright future for our company. = 我预见我们公司有一个光明的未来。
They foresee difficulties in implementing the new policy. = 他们预见在实施新政策方面会有困难。


一般过去时:
She foresaw the danger and avoided it. = 她预见了危险并避免了它。
We foresaw the need for more staff and hired accordingly. = 我们预见需要更多员工，并相应地进行了招聘。


现在完成时:
He has foreseen this problem many times before. = 他以前多次预见过这个问题。
They have foreseen the potential risks associated with the project. = 他们已经预见了与该项目相关的潜在风险。


过去完成时:
I had foreseen the possibility of rain and brought an umbrella. = 我已经预料到可能会下雨，所以带了把伞。
She had foreseen the outcome of the negotiations and prepared accordingly. = 她已经预料到了谈判的结果，并做好了相应的准备。


将来时:
We will foresee many challenges in the coming years. = 我们将在未来几年预见到许多挑战。
The experts will foresee a significant increase in energy demand. = 专家们将预见到能源需求的显著增长。


虚拟语气:
If I had foreseen the consequences, I would have acted differently. = 如果我预见了后果，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had foreseen the market crash. = 我真希望我预见了市场崩盘。


被动语态:
The difficulties were foreseen by the management team. = 管理团队预见了这些困难。
The potential risks were foreseen by the engineers before the launch. = 工程师在发射前预见了潜在的风险。


疑问句:
Do you foresee any problems with the project? = 你预见到项目中有什么问题吗？
Can they foresee the long-term effects of this decision? = 他们能预见到这个决定的长期影响吗？


否定句:
I did not foresee the sudden change in the weather. = 我没有预料到天气的突然变化。
They did not foresee the complexity of the task. = 他们没有预料到这项任务的复杂性。


PHRASE->
foresee the future = 预见未来
foresee a crisis = 预见危机
foresee trouble = 预见麻烦
foresee success = 预见成功
foresee a problem = 预见问题
