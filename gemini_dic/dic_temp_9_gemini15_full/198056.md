shoemakers:/ˈʃuːˌmeɪkərz/| n.|制鞋匠们，鞋匠们|People who make or repair shoes.  The term often refers to a group of shoemakers.|例句：The shoemakers in the village were renowned for their craftsmanship. 村里的制鞋匠以其精湛的工艺而闻名。

例句：Many shoemakers lost their jobs due to the rise of mass-produced shoes. 许多制鞋匠由于机器生产鞋子的兴起而失业。


近义词：cobblers (informal), bootmakers


反义词：无


词性:名词
