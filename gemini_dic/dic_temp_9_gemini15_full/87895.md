hierarchical:/ˌhaɪəˈrɑːrkɪkl/| adj.|等级制度的，分层的|Having a hierarchical structure; arranged in a hierarchy.  It refers to a system or organization in which people or things are ranked one above the other according to status or authority.|例句：The company has a hierarchical structure, with a CEO at the top and many levels of management below. 这家公司采用等级制度，首席执行官位于顶端，下面有多个管理层级。|例句：The data is organized in a hierarchical database. 数据以分层数据库的形式组织。|近义词：layered, stratified, graded|反义词：flat, egalitarian
