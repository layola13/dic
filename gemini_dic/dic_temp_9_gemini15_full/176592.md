reacceleration: /ˌriːækˌsɛləˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 再加速；重新加速 | The process of accelerating again after a period of deceleration or a pause.  It refers to a renewed increase in speed or rate of progress.  |例句：The car experienced a sudden reacceleration after slowing down for the turn.  这辆车在转弯减速后突然重新加速。

The company's reacceleration in sales growth surprised analysts. 公司销售增长的重新加速令分析师感到意外。


近义词：re-energizing, renewed acceleration, boost, resurgence


反义词：deceleration, slowdown, stagnation


词性: noun
