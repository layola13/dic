skout:/skaʊt/| v. n.|侦察，搜索；童子军成员；|To scout is to search an area thoroughly, often in a secretive manner, for a specific purpose.  It can also refer to a person who is a member of the Scouts, a youth organization. |例句：The general sent scouts ahead to check for enemy troops. 将军派侦察兵去前方侦察敌军。The boy scout earned a merit badge for his hiking skills.  这个童子军因为他的远足技能获得了奖章。|近义词：search, explore, reconnoiter, scout (n.)|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He scouts the area for potential threats. 他侦察该区域以寻找潜在威胁。
They scout for new talent every year. 他们每年都寻找新的天才。


一般过去时:
The soldiers scouted the forest before advancing. 士兵们在前进之前侦察了森林。
She scouted the neighborhood for her lost dog. 她搜寻了附近区域寻找她丢失的狗。


现在完成时:
We have scouted several locations for our new office. 我们已经考察了几个地方作为我们新办公室的选址。
They have scouted the market for innovative products.  他们已经考察了市场寻找创新产品。


过去完成时:
The explorers had scouted the terrain before attempting the climb. 探险家们在尝试攀登之前已经侦察了地形。
She had scouted the route multiple times before the race.  她在比赛之前多次勘察了路线。


将来时:
We will scout the area tomorrow. 我们明天将侦察该地区。
They will scout for new recruits next month. 他们下个月将寻找新兵。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would scout the area carefully before making a decision. 如果我是你，我会在做决定之前仔细侦察该地区。
I wish I had scouted the location beforehand. 我真希望我事先已经勘察过那个地方。


被动语态:
The area was scouted by the military. 该地区被军方侦察过。
The route was scouted thoroughly before the construction began.  在施工开始之前，路线被彻底勘察过。


疑问句:

Did they scout the area before the battle?  他们在战斗前侦察过该地区吗？


否定句:
They did not scout the area properly. 他们没有正确地侦察该地区。
He hasn't scouted the market for similar products. 他还没有考察市场寻找类似的产品。
