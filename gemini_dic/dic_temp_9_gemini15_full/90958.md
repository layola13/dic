hurrahing:/hʊˈrɑːɪŋ/| v. |欢呼；喝彩|The act of shouting "hurrah," expressing great joy, approval, or triumph.  It often involves enthusiastic cheering and celebration.|例句：The crowd was hurrahing wildly after the winning goal.  人群在获胜进球后疯狂欢呼。

The team's victory prompted a scene of hurrahing and jubilation. 球队的胜利引发了一片欢呼和喜庆的景象。|近义词：cheering, shouting, celebrating, applauding|反义词：mourning, lamenting, grieving
