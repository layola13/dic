honeybee:/ˈhʌnibiː/| n. |蜜蜂|A social insect that lives in colonies and produces honey. Honeybees are vital for pollination and are known for their complex social structure and hive organization.  |Example: The honeybee landed on the flower to collect nectar.  蜜蜂落在花朵上采集花蜜。|近义词：bee|反义词：无
