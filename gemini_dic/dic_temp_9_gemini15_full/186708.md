rightist:/ˈraɪtɪst/| adj. n.|右翼分子；右翼的|A rightist is a person who holds right-wing political views.  They typically favor policies that emphasize tradition, individual liberty, and limited government intervention.  The term can be used in a descriptive or pejorative way, depending on the context.|例句：He was labeled a rightist for his conservative views on social issues. 他因其在社会问题上的保守观点而被贴上右翼分子的标签。

The rise of rightist movements in Europe caused concern among many.  欧洲右翼运动的兴起引起了许多人的担忧。|近义词：conservative, reactionary|反义词：leftist, liberal, progressive|形容词，名词
