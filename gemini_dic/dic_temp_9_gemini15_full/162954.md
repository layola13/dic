词源:

* **precinct:**  From Old French *preinct, *preint, from Latin *praecinctus, past participle of *praecingere "to gird around," from *prae- "before" + *cingere "to gird."  Originally referred to a defined area or district, often with a boundary.

* **house:** From Old English *hūs, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch *huis and German *Haus.


The combination "precinct houses" is a descriptive term referring to the buildings that serve as the headquarters for police precincts.
