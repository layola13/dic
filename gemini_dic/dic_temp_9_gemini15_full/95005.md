in the wake of: /ɪn ðə weɪk əv/ | prep. |在…之后；随着…而来 |Following; as a result of.  This phrase refers to the period of time immediately following an event, often emphasizing the consequences or effects of that event. |例句：Many new businesses opened in the wake of the city's revitalization project.  在城市复兴项目之后，许多新企业纷纷开业。


例句：In the wake of the hurricane, many homes were destroyed.  飓风过后，许多房屋被毁。


近义词：following, after, subsequent to, in the aftermath of


反义词：before, prior to, preceding


词性：介词短语
