excerebration: /ˌɛksˌɛsɛˈbreɪʃən/ | n. |大脑摘除；去脑术 | The surgical removal of the cerebrum.  This is a rare and extreme procedure, typically performed only in cases of severe, untreatable brain damage.  | Example: Excerebration is a drastic measure, only considered as a last resort in cases of extreme intracranial pressure.  大脑摘除是一种极端措施，只有在颅内压极高的情况下才会考虑作为最后手段。| 近义词:  decortication (partial removal of the cerebral cortex),  encephalectomy (removal of the brain)| 反义词:  脑移植 (brain transplantation) -  there isn't a direct antonym as it's a destructive procedure.
