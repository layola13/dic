miniaturized:/ˌmɪniətʃəraɪzd/| adj. | 微型化的；小型化的 |  Reduced in size; made miniature.  The process of making something smaller. | 例句：The company is developing miniaturized electronics for use in wearable technology.  这家公司正在开发用于可穿戴技术的微型电子产品。


例句：The new camera is incredibly miniaturized, fitting easily into a pocket. 这款新相机非常小巧，可以轻松放入口袋。


近义词：small-scale, reduced, dwarfed


反义词：enlarged, magnified, giant


词性：形容词
