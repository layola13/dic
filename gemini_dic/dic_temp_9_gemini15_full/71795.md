fledge:/fledʒ/| vt. n.| 使…长羽毛；使…成熟；初飞；幼鸟 | To leave the nest after developing the ability to fly.  |例句：The young birds have fledged and flown away.  幼鸟已经长出羽毛并飞走了。 The fledgling company is still finding its feet. 这家新公司仍在站稳脚跟。|近义词：mature, develop, leave the nest |反义词：remain in nest, immature


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds fledge in the spring. = 鸟类在春天长出羽毛。
He diligently fledges his skills for the upcoming competition. = 他勤奋地磨练他的技能以应对即将到来的比赛。
