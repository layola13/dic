sleepwalkers: /ˈsliːpwɔːkərz/ | n. | 梦游者 | People who walk while asleep.  Sleepwalkers are often unaware of their actions and may exhibit unusual behavior. | 例句：The police found the sleepwalkers wandering near the highway.  警察发现梦游者在高速公路附近游荡。

例句：Several sleepwalkers were reported in the neighbourhood last night.  昨晚有人报告说附近有几个梦游者。


近义词：somnambulists


反义词：N/A (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with those who sleep soundly)


词性: 名词
