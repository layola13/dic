recommend:/ˌrekəˈmɛnd/ | vt. | 推荐，建议 | To suggest (someone or something) as being suitable for a particular purpose or role.  |例句：I recommend this restaurant; the food is excellent. 我推荐这家餐馆；食物很棒。
例句：The doctor recommended that he take a break from work. 医生建议他休息一下。| suggest, propose, advise |  discourage, oppose | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I recommend this book to you. = 我向你推荐这本书。
She recommends healthy eating habits. = 她推荐健康的饮食习惯。

一般过去时:
He recommended a good lawyer to me. = 他向我推荐了一位好律师。
They recommended that we book our flights early. = 他们建议我们尽早预订机票。

现在完成时:
I have recommended this solution to the team. = 我已经向团队推荐了这个方案。
She has recommended many students for scholarships. = 她已经推荐了许多学生获得奖学金。

过去完成时:
He had recommended the movie before I saw it. = 我看这部电影之前，他已经推荐过了。
They had recommended the candidate before the interview. = 面试之前，他们已经推荐了这位候选人。

将来时:
I will recommend you for the position. = 我会推荐你担任这个职位。
She will recommend a new strategy to the board. = 她将向董事会推荐一项新战略。

虚拟语气:
I would recommend that you consult a specialist. = 我建议你去咨询专家。
It is recommended that you arrive early for the meeting. = 建议您早到会议。

被动语态:
This product is highly recommended. = 此产品广受好评。
The plan was recommended by the committee. = 该计划得到了委员会的推荐。

疑问句:
Do you recommend this hotel? = 你推荐这家酒店吗？
Would you recommend any good books? = 你能推荐几本好书吗？

否定句:
I do not recommend that approach. = 我不推荐这种方法。
She didn't recommend the restaurant to us. = 她没有向我们推荐这家餐厅。


PHRASE->
recommend oneself = 自荐
recommend doing sth. = 建议做某事
recommend sb. for sth. = 推荐某人担任某职
highly recommend = 强烈推荐
I would recommend that...= 我建议...
