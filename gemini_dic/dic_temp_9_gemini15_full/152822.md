peculate:/ˈpɛkjuleɪt/| vt.|挪用；盗用；侵吞|To take money or goods that are not yours, especially from the place where you work.  This often involves a breach of trust.|例句：The treasurer was accused of peculating funds from the company. 出纳被指控挪用公司资金。|例句：He was found guilty of peculating public funds. 他被判犯有侵吞公款罪。|近义词：embezzle, misappropriate, defraud|反义词：donate, contribute


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He peculates funds from the company. = 他挪用公司资金。
She peculates small amounts of money regularly. = 她定期挪用少量钱款。


一般过去时:
The accountant peculated a large sum of money. = 会计挪用了一大笔钱。
They peculated from the charity for years. = 他们多年来一直侵吞慈善机构的钱财。


现在完成时:
He has peculated millions of dollars from the bank. = 他已从银行挪用数百万美元。
The government has discovered that officials have been peculating funds. = 政府发现官员们一直在挪用资金。


过去完成时:
It turned out that he had peculated money from his previous job. = 事实证明他之前的工作中挪用了钱财。
Before the audit, they had peculated significant resources. = 审计之前，他们已经挪用了大量资源。


将来时:
They will likely peculate more funds if they are not caught. = 如果他们不被抓住，他们很可能会继续挪用资金。
He will continue to peculate until he is stopped. = 他会继续挪用资金，直到被阻止。


虚拟语气:
If he had not peculated the money, he would not be in jail. = 如果他没有挪用这笔钱，他就不会在监狱里。
I wish they hadn't peculated those funds for their personal use. = 我真希望他们没有挪用那些资金用于个人用途。


被动语态:
Millions of dollars were peculated from the company last year. = 去年公司被挪用了数百万美元。
The funds were peculated by a trusted employee. = 资金被一名值得信赖的员工挪用了。


疑问句:
Did he peculate the money? = 他挪用了钱吗？
Who peculated the company's funds? = 谁挪用了公司的资金？


否定句:
He did not peculate the money; he borrowed it. = 他没有挪用这笔钱；他是借的。
She denies peculating any funds from the organization. = 她否认从该组织挪用任何资金。


PHRASE->
peculate funds = 挪用资金
peculate money = 挪用钱财
peculate assets = 挪用资产
peculate resources = 挪用资源
to peculate from = 从...挪用


词源:

来自拉丁语 *peculare，意思是“偷窃，抢劫”。  它与拉丁语 pecus ("牲畜") 有关，暗示着最初的含义可能是偷窃牲畜。  通过法语 piller  演变而来。
