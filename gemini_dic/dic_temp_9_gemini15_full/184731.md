resulting:/rɪˈzʌltɪŋ/ | adj. | 结果的；导致的 | Arising as a consequence or effect.  It describes something that is a consequence or outcome of a particular action or event. | Example: The resulting damage was extensive.  (由此造成的损害是巨大的。)  The resulting product is innovative. (最终产品是创新的。) |近义词：consequent, resultant, ensuing | 反义词：initial, preceding
