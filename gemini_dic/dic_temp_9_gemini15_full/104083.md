jump-at:/dʒʌmp æt/| phrasal verb|欣然接受；急切地抓住|to accept an offer or opportunity eagerly and immediately|例句：She jumped at the chance to travel to Europe. 她欣然接受了去欧洲旅行的机会。|例句：He jumped at the offer of a promotion. 他欣然接受了晋升的机会。|近义词：grab, seize, snatch|反义词：reject, refuse, decline

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They jump at every opportunity. = 他们抓住每一个机会。
We jump at the chance to help. = 我们欣然接受帮助的机会。

一般过去时:
He jumped at the invitation. = 他欣然接受了邀请。
She jumped at the idea. = 她对这个主意很感兴趣。

现在完成时:
They have jumped at every opportunity to learn. = 他们抓住每一个学习的机会。
I have jumped at the chance to work abroad. = 我欣然接受了去国外工作的机会。

过去完成时:
He had jumped at the offer before I could even speak. = 我还没来得及说话，他就欣然接受了那个提议。
She had jumped at the chance to buy the house.  = 她早就欣然接受了买房子的机会。


将来时:
They will jump at the chance to make some extra money. = 他们会抓住赚点外快的机会。
We will jump at the opportunity to collaborate. = 我们将欣然接受合作的机会。

虚拟语气:
If they offered me the job, I would jump at it. = 如果他们给我这份工作，我会欣然接受。
I wish I had jumped at the chance to study abroad. = 我真希望我当初抓住了出国留学的机会。

疑问句:
Would you jump at the opportunity to travel? = 你会抓住旅行的机会吗？

否定句:
He didn't jump at the chance. = 他没有抓住机会。
She didn't jump at the suggestion. = 她没有接受这个建议。
