multipliable: /ˈmʌltɪplaɪəbl/ | adj. | 可乘的，可倍增的 | Capable of being multiplied.  Something that can be multiplied is multipliable.  This term is less common than its related verb "multiply".  It emphasizes the potential for multiplication rather than the act itself. |

例句：
The numbers in the sequence are all multipliable.  =  序列中的数字都是可乘的。

The area of the rectangle is multipliable by increasing its length. = 通过增加矩形的长度，其面积是可以倍增的。


近义词：multiplicable


反义词：indivisible (in the mathematical sense)


词性：形容词
