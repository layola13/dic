readability:/ˌriːdəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可读性 | The quality of being easy to read and understand.  It considers factors such as sentence structure, vocabulary, and overall clarity. | The readability of the textbook is excellent; even beginners can understand it easily. 这本教科书的可读性极佳，即使是初学者也能轻松理解。 | comprehensibility, intelligibility | incomprehensibility, obscurity | 名词
