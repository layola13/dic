explores:/ɪkˈsplɔrz/| vt.|探索；探究；考察|To investigate or discuss a subject or issue systematically.|例句：The scientist explores the mysteries of the universe.  科学家探索宇宙的奥秘。|例句：The article explores the impact of social media on teenagers. 这篇文章探讨了社交媒体对青少年的影响。|近义词：investigate, examine, inquire into|反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He explores new ideas every day. = 他每天都在探索新的想法。
She explores the forest looking for mushrooms. = 她正在森林里寻找蘑菇。

一般过去时:
They explored the ancient ruins last year. = 他们去年探险了那些古老的遗址。
We explored the possibility of a new partnership. = 我们探讨了建立新的伙伴关系的可能性。

现在完成时:
She has explored many different career paths. = 她已经探索了许多不同的职业道路。
He has explored the Amazon rainforest extensively. = 他已经广泛地探索了亚马逊雨林。

过去完成时:
Before they moved to the city, they had explored the countryside extensively. = 在他们搬到城市之前，他们已经广泛地探索了乡村。
After she had explored all the options, she made her decision. = 在她探索了所有选择之后，她做出了决定。

将来时:
We will explore the caves next weekend. = 我们下周末将探索这些洞穴。
They will explore the possibility of a merger. = 他们将探索合并的可能性。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would explore other options. = 如果我有更多时间，我会探索其他选择。
I wish I could explore the world more freely. = 我希望我能更自由地探索世界。

被动语态:
The caves are being explored by archaeologists. = 这些洞穴正在由考古学家进行探索。
The new technology is being explored by many companies. = 许多公司正在探索这项新技术。

疑问句:
Does he explore new ideas regularly? = 他经常探索新想法吗？
Have you ever explored the possibility of working from home? = 你有没有想过在家工作的可能性？

否定句:
He didn't explore all the options before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前并没有探索所有的选择。
They haven't explored the market thoroughly yet. = 他们还没有彻底探索市场。
