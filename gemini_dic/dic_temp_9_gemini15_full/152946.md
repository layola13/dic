pedicellaria:/ˌpɛdɪsəˈlɛəriə/| n. | 棘皮动物的叉棘；(海星等棘皮动物体表的) 钳状附属物| A small, pincer-like appendage found on the skin of echinoderms such as starfish and sea urchins.  They are used for defense, cleaning, and feeding. | Example: The starfish used its pedicellariae to remove debris from its surface.  海星用它的叉棘清除表面的碎屑。|近义词：pincer, forceps (in function)|反义词:None
