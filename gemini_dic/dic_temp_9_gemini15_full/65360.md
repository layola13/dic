eulogizer:/ˈjuːlədʒaɪzər/ | n. | 颂扬者；赞美者 | One who delivers a eulogy.  A person who praises someone, especially someone who has recently died. | Example: The eulogizer spoke eloquently about the deceased's kindness and generosity.  颂扬者动情地讲述了死者善良和慷慨的一面。| 近义词：praiser, celebrator, commemorator | 反义词：criticizer, detractor
