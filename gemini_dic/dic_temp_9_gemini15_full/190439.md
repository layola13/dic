sashayed:/ʃæˈʃeɪd/| v.|轻盈地走动；扭动着走； |to walk in a flamboyant or showy manner, often with a swaying movement.|例句：She sashayed down the runway in a stunning gown. 她穿着华丽的礼服，轻盈地走过T台。  She sashayed into the room, attracting everyone's attention. 她扭动着走进了房间，吸引了所有人的注意。|近义词：strut, glide, swagger, promenade |反义词：shuffle, lumber, trudge


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She sashays down the street every day. = 她每天都轻盈地走在大街上。
He sashays when he dances. = 他跳舞时会扭动着走。

一般过去时:
The model sashayed across the stage. = 模特轻盈地走过舞台。
She sashayed gracefully into the ballroom. = 她优雅地扭动着走进了舞厅。

现在完成时:
He has sashayed into the limelight. = 他已经步入聚光灯下。
She has sashayed through life with confidence. = 她一直自信地生活。

过去完成时:
Before the performance, she had sashayed backstage. = 演出前，她轻盈地走过后台。
They had sashayed into the party late. = 他们很晚才扭动着走进了派对。

将来时:
She will sashay down the aisle at her wedding. = 她将在婚礼上轻盈地走过红毯。
He will sashay onto the stage tonight. = 他今晚将扭动着走上舞台。

虚拟语气:
If she were here, she would sashay across the floor. = 如果她在，她会在房间里轻盈地走动。
I wish I could sashay like a professional dancer. = 我希望我能像专业舞者一样轻盈地走动。

被动语态:  (The verb "sashay" is rarely used in the passive voice because it implies active, deliberate movement.)

疑问句:
Did she sashay into the room? = 她是不是扭动着走进了房间？
Will they sashay across the stage? = 他们会在舞台上轻盈地走动吗？

否定句:
He didn't sashay; he walked normally. = 他没有扭动着走，他是正常走路的。
She didn't sashay onto the stage; she rushed. = 她没有扭动着走上舞台；她是冲上去的。
