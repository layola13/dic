heresies:/ˈhɛrəsiz/ | n. |异端邪说，错误信仰 | Beliefs or opinions that contradict established religious doctrines.  The term often implies a deliberate rejection of orthodoxy. |Example:  The church condemned his heresies. 教会谴责了他的异端邪说。


The spread of these heresies threatened the unity of the faith. 这些异端的传播威胁到了信仰的统一。|近义词：false doctrines, unorthodox beliefs, heterodoxy |反义词：orthodox beliefs, dogma, orthodoxy |名词
