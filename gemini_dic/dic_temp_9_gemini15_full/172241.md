pushpull:/pʊʃpʊl/| n. v.| 推拉；（尤指）互相矛盾的力量；|A pushpull is a situation or system that involves both pushing and pulling forces, often in conflict. It can refer to a physical mechanism, a social dynamic, or a metaphorical concept.  |例句：The market is experiencing a pushpull between inflation and deflation. 市场正在经历通货膨胀和通货紧缩之间的拉锯战。The new policy created a pushpull between individual liberties and public safety. 新政策在个人自由和公共安全之间造成了冲突。|近义词：tug-of-war, conflict, tension |反义词：harmony, agreement, balance|名词，动词
