outcrop:/ˈaʊtkrɒp/| n. |露头；（岩石的）出露部分 | An outcrop is a rock formation that projects from the surface of the surrounding ground.  It's a part of a rock formation that is visible on the surface, often due to erosion. | Example: The hikers stumbled upon a fascinating outcrop of ancient granite.  徒步旅行者偶然发现了一处令人着迷的古老花岗岩露头。


The geologists studied the outcrop to understand the geological history of the area. 地质学家研究了露头以了解该地区的地质历史。|近义词：exposure, emergence |反义词：submergence, burial
