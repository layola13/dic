permittees:/pərˈmɪtiːz/| n. |许可证持有者，被许可人|Those who have been granted permission to do something, often under a specific permit or license.  They are individuals or entities authorized to engage in an activity or use a resource based on a granted permit.|Example: The permittees were responsible for cleaning up after the event.  许可证持有者负责活动后的清理工作。 The permittees were given strict instructions on how to use the equipment. 被许可人被给予了关于如何使用设备的严格指示。|近义词：licensees, permit holders|反义词：prohibited persons, unauthorized individuals|名词
