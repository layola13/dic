imbibes:/ɪmˈbɪbz/| vt.|吸收；饮用；接受；领会|To absorb or drink in (liquids). To absorb knowledge, ideas, or experiences.|例句：The dry soil imbibed the rainwater. 干燥的土壤吸收了雨水。  The young man imbibed the wisdom of his elders.  这个年轻人吸收了长辈的智慧。|近义词：absorb, soak up, assimilate, take in|反义词：expel, reject, exclude


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He imbibes knowledge readily. = 他很容易吸收知识。
She imbibes a glass of wine every evening. = 她每天晚上喝一杯葡萄酒。


一般过去时:
They imbibed the local culture during their trip. = 他们在旅行期间吸收了当地的文化。
He imbibed deeply from the well of wisdom. = 他从智慧的源泉中汲取了丰富的知识。
