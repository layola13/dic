heron:/ˈhɛrən/| n.|苍鹭|A large wading bird with long legs and neck, long pointed bill, and typically gray or white plumage.|例句：A heron stood motionless in the shallows. 一只苍鹭一动不动地站在浅滩上。

例句：We saw a great blue heron fishing in the lake. 我们看到一只巨大的蓝鹭在湖里钓鱼。|近义词：crane, egret|反义词:None. |词性:名词
