kickoff:/ˈkɪkɒf/| n., v.|开球；开始；启动|The act of kicking off a ball to start a game, or the start of something.|The game kicked off at 3 pm.  比赛在下午3点开始。
The new project kicked off last Monday. 新项目上周一启动。| commencement, launch, initiation | conclusion, ending |


USAGE->

The game kicks off at 7 pm. = 比赛在晚上7点开始。
He always kicks off the meeting with a joke. = 他总是以一个笑话开始会议。
