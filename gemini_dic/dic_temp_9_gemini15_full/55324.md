dissolve:/dɪˈzɒlv/| vt.|溶解；解散；消除；|to become or cause to become liquid; to bring to an end; to fade away|例句：The sugar dissolved in the water. 糖溶解在水里了。 The parliament was dissolved. 议会解散了。|近义词：melt, break up, disperse |反义词：solidify, form, coalesce
