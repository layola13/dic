contrabasso:/ˌkɒntrəˈbæsəʊ/ (英式)/ˌkɑːntrəˈbæsəʊ/ (美式)| n. | 倍大提琴；低音提琴 | A contrabasso is a large stringed bass instrument, typically played with a bow, used in orchestras and other musical ensembles. It produces the lowest tones among stringed instruments.  | Example: The orchestra's contrabasso section provided a deep, resonant foundation for the entire performance.  低音提琴组为整个演出提供了深沉、共鸣的基调。 | 近义词：double bass, bass viol | 反义词： 无  | 名词
