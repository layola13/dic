exornation:/ˌɛksɔːrˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 装饰；修饰；美化 | The act of adorning or decorating; embellishment.  It often implies excessive or ostentatious decoration. |例句：The exornation of the palace was excessive, with gold leaf covering every surface. 宫殿的装饰过于奢华，每个表面都覆盖着金箔。

The exornation of her writing style made it difficult to understand her main point. 她写作风格的过度修饰使得难以理解她的要点。


近义词：adornment, embellishment, ornamentation, decoration | 反义词：simplicity, plainness, austerity | 词性：名词


词源：源自拉丁语 *exornare，由 *ex- (表示“出去”或“彻底地”)和 *ornare (表示“装饰”)构成。
