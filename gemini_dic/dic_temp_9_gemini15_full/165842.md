preoccupied:/ˌpriːˈɒkjʊpaɪd/| adj. |全神贯注的；思想负担重的|Mentally occupied or engrossed; filled with anxiety or worry.|例句：She was so preoccupied with her work that she didn't hear the phone ring.  她工作太投入了，没听到电话响。|例句：He seemed preoccupied and withdrawn. 他看起来心事重重，沉默寡言。|近义词：absorbed, engrossed, engrossed, distracted, troubled |反义词：unconcerned, unconcerned, at ease


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is preoccupied with his studies. = 他全神贯注于他的学习。
She is preoccupied by her worries. = 她被她的担忧所困扰。


一般过去时:
He was preoccupied with his work yesterday. = 他昨天忙于工作。
She was preoccupied by a nagging cough. = 她被一阵阵咳嗽困扰着。


现在完成时:
He has been preoccupied with the project for weeks. = 他几周来一直忙于这个项目。
She has been preoccupied by anxieties about her health. = 她一直担心自己的健康问题。


过去完成时:
He had been preoccupied with his career and neglected his family. = 他一直忙于事业而忽略了家庭。
She had been preoccupied with her own thoughts and didn't notice him. = 她一直沉浸在自己的思绪中，没有注意到他。


将来时:
He will be preoccupied with his new job next week. = 他下周将忙于他的新工作。
She will be preoccupied by preparations for the wedding. = 她将忙于婚礼的准备工作。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't so preoccupied, he could help us. = 如果他不那么忙碌，他可以帮助我们。
If she hadn't been so preoccupied, she would have seen the danger. = 如果她当时没有那么心不在焉，她就会发现危险。


被动语态:
He was preoccupied by the news. = 他被这个消息弄得心烦意乱。(被动语态用在此处略显不自然，通常不用被动)
She was preoccupied with her thoughts. = 她被自己的思绪占据着。(被动语态用在此处略显不自然，通常不用被动)


疑问句:
Is he preoccupied with something? = 他是不是有什么心事？
Is she preoccupied with her work? = 她是不是忙于工作？


否定句:
He is not preoccupied with trivial matters. = 他没有被琐事困扰。
She was not preoccupied with her appearance. = 她并不在意自己的外表。


PHRASE->
be preoccupied with = 专注于……
become preoccupied = 变得心事重重
deeply preoccupied = 深陷于……
preoccupied expression = 心事重重的表情
remain preoccupied = 保持心事重重
