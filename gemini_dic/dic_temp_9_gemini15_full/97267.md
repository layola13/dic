inflowing:/ˈɪnˌfloʊɪŋ/| adj.|流入的；涌入的| flowing inward; entering.  The inflow of water into the lake was significant.|近义词：incoming, influent|反义词：outflowing, efflux


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river is inflowing into the ocean. = 河水流入大海。
Inflowing currents are strong today. = 今日的涌入水流很强。


一般过去时:
The water inflowed rapidly after the storm. = 暴雨过后，水流迅速涌入。
A large amount of money inflowed into the market. = 大量资金涌入市场。


现在完成时:
Much water has inflowed into the reservoir. = 很多水已经流入水库。
Significant investment has inflowed into the tech sector. = 大量投资已经流入科技领域。


过去完成时:
The dam had already inflowed before the heavy rains. = 大雨之前水库已经蓄满了水。
The company had inflowed millions of dollars before the crisis. = 公司在危机爆发前已获得数百万美元的资金流入。


将来时:
More water will inflow into the river after the snow melts. = 雪融化后，更多的水将流入河流。
We expect a large inflow of tourists next year. = 我们预计明年将会有大量游客涌入。


虚拟语气:
If the water inflowed more slowly, the dam wouldn't have broken. = 如果水流入速度慢一些，水坝就不会垮塌。
If more capital inflowed, the economy would improve. = 如果有更多资金流入，经济将会改善。


被动语态:
The reservoir was inflowed by the river.  = 水库是由河流注水的。(被动语态略显不自然，通常不用被动)
The city was inflowed by refugees. =难民涌入该城市。（被动语态更常用）


疑问句:
Is the river inflowing at a high rate? = 河水流入速度快吗？
How much water is inflowing into the lake per hour? = 每小时有多少水流入湖中？


否定句:
The water is not inflowing at the moment. = 目前水没有流入。
No significant funds were inflowing into the project. = 没有显著的资金流入该项目。


PHRASE->
inflowing current: 流入的洋流
inflowing tide: 涨潮
inflowing air: 流入的空气
inflowing capital: 流入的资本
inflowing migrants: 流入的移民
