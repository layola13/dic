palo:/ˈpɑːloʊ/ | n. |木棍；棍棒；栅栏；（菲律宾）帕洛市|A palo is a stick, rod, or pole, often used as a support or weapon. It can also refer to a fence or railing made of such sticks.  In the Philippines, Palo is a city.|例句：He used a palo to support the climbing plant. 他用一根棍子支撑攀援植物。 The farmer built a fence of palos. 农民用木棍建了一道栅栏。|近义词：stick, rod, pole, staff|反义词：None specifically.
