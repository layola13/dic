sleuthed:/ˈsluːθt/| v.|侦察；暗中调查；|To investigate or detect (something) in a thorough and often secretive manner.|例句：The detective sleuthed the case for months before finding the culprit.  侦探花了几个月时间调查此案，才找到罪犯。
例句：She sleuthed out the hidden meaning in the poem. 她侦破了诗歌中隐藏的含义。|近义词：investigated, detected, tracked, explored|反义词：ignored, overlooked
