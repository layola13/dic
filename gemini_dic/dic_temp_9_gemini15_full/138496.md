noviceship:/ˈnɒvɪsʃɪp/ | n. |新手期；初学者身份；入门；学习阶段 | The state or period of being a novice; the experience of being a beginner.  It often implies a period of learning and development before achieving proficiency. |例句：Her noviceship in the art world was evident in her early paintings.  她的早期绘画作品显示了她对艺术世界的生疏。

例句：He overcame the challenges of his noviceship and became a skilled craftsman. 他克服了新手阶段的挑战，成为了一名熟练的工匠。


近义词：apprenticeship, beginners’ experience, initiation, learning curve

反义词：expertise, mastery, proficiency, veteran status
