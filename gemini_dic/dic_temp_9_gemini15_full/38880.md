conferencings:/kənˈfərənsɪŋz/ | n. |会议; 会议活动|The act of holding or participating in conferences.  It often refers to the process or series of conferences, rather than a single event.  The term can also imply the use of technology to facilitate remote conferencing.|例句：The company is investing heavily in new conferencing technologies to improve communication. 这家公司正在大量投资新的会议技术以改善沟通。

例句：The ongoing conferencings between the two countries are aimed at resolving the trade dispute.  这两个国家正在进行的会谈旨在解决贸易争端。


近义词：meetings, discussions, consultations, deliberations | 反义词：None (antonyms are context-dependent, e.g.,  silence, inaction)
