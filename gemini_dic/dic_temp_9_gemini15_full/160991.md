postapoplectic: /ˌpoʊstæpəˈplɛktɪk/ | adj. | 中风后的；中风引起的；灾难后的 |

英文解析:  Relating to or resulting from an apoplexy (a stroke).  It can also be used more broadly to describe the state of things after a catastrophic event, similar to post-apocalyptic but with a less dramatic connotation.


例句:

The patient exhibited postapoplectic paralysis on the right side of his body. = 病人右侧出现中风后瘫痪。

The city's infrastructure was in a postapoplectic state after the hurricane. = 飓风过后，城市的公共设施处于灾后状态。


近义词: post-stroke, post-catastrophic,  aftermath (n.)


反义词:  pre-stroke, pre-catastrophic (These are not direct opposites, but describe the state before the event)


词性: adjective
