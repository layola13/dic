goosing:/ˈɡuːsɪŋ/| n. v.| 刺激；戏弄；轻推；(俚语)性骚扰 |英文解析：The act of playfully or aggressively pushing or poking someone; also, a slang term for sexual harassment.  As a verb, it means to playfully or aggressively push or poke someone. |例句：He gave me a playful goosing in the ribs. 他轻轻地推了我肋骨一下。


The teacher gave the students a goosing to wake them up.老师推了学生一下让他们清醒。

近义词：prodding, nudging, jabbing, (for sexual harassment) harassing, molesting |反义词：ignoring, comforting


词源: 源自古英语 *gōs, 指鹅，因鹅会啄人而引申出“轻推”的含义。  The slang meaning related to sexual harassment is a more recent development.
