cruciferous:/kruːˈsɪfərəs/ | adj. | 十字花科的；十字花形状的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Cruciferae (or Brassicaceae), a family of plants that includes cabbages, cauliflowers, and mustards, characterized by four-petaled flowers arranged in the shape of a cross. |  Cruciferous vegetables are a good source of vitamins. 十字花科蔬菜是维生素的良好来源。 The plant's flower was cruciferous in shape.  这种植物的花是十字形。|  Brassica,  cabbage-family |  (no direct antonym, but could use terms like "non-cruciferous" or specifying a different plant family) |  adj.
