malfeasance:/mælˈfiːzəns/ | n. |渎职；不法行为；违法行为 | Wrongdoing, especially by a public official.  It typically involves an abuse of power or trust for personal gain. |例句：The mayor was accused of malfeasance in office. 市长被指控渎职。

The investigation uncovered widespread malfeasance within the company.  调查揭露了公司内部广泛存在的违法行为。

近义词：misconduct, wrongdoing, misbehavior, corruption, fraud

反义词：probity, integrity, honesty
