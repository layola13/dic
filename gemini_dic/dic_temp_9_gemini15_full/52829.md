diluvial:/dɪˈluːviəl/| adj.|大洪水的；洪水的；大量的|relating to or caused by a great flood, especially the biblical flood;  characterized by or suggesting a great flood.  The word often implies overwhelming abundance or destruction.|例句：The diluvial deposits revealed evidence of ancient life.  大洪水沉积物揭示了古代生命的证据。The diluvial rain caused widespread flooding.  这场倾盆大雨造成了大范围的洪涝。|近义词：deluge, flood, cataclysmic|反义词：drought, arid
