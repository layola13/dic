modernity:/məˈdɜːrnɪti/| n. |现代性，现代，现代化 |The state or quality of being modern; recent times characterized by new ideas, methods, or inventions.  It often refers to the historical period following the Middle Ages and the Enlightenment, marked by significant social, political, and technological changes. |例句：The rapid pace of modernity has led to both progress and challenges. 现代化的快速发展既带来了进步也带来了挑战。

例句：The museum's exhibit explores the impact of modernity on art. 博物馆的展览探讨了现代性对艺术的影响。


近义词：modernization, contemporaneity, current times |反义词：antiquity, archaism, tradition


词性：名词
