decard:/dɪˈkɑːrd/| vt.|抛弃；丢弃；放弃|To discard something means to throw it away or get rid of it because it is no longer useful or wanted.|例句：He discarded the old newspapers. 他扔掉了旧报纸。|例句：She discarded her worn-out shoes. 她扔掉了她那双磨损的鞋子。|近义词：throw away, dispose of, jettison|反义词：keep, retain, preserve|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He discards old clothes regularly. = 他定期丢弃旧衣服。
They discard unwanted emails automatically. = 他们自动丢弃不需要的电子邮件。

一般过去时:
She discarded the broken vase. = 她丢弃了那个破花瓶。
We discarded the outdated information. = 我们丢弃了过时的信息。

现在完成时:
I have discarded all my old textbooks. = 我已经丢弃了我所有的旧课本。
They have discarded the faulty components. = 他们已经丢弃了有故障的部件。

过去完成时:
He had discarded the evidence before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他已经销毁了证据。
She had discarded her old habits before she moved to the city. = 在她搬到城市之前，她已经改掉了旧习惯。

将来时:
I will discard these documents after I'm finished with them. = 我用完这些文件后就会丢弃它们。
They will discard the broken machine next week. = 他们下周将丢弃那台坏掉的机器。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would discard that old furniture. = 如果我是你，我会丢弃那些旧家具。
I wish I had discarded that old dress sooner. = 我真希望我早点丢弃那件旧衣服。

被动语态:
The old furniture was discarded last week. = 那些旧家具上周被丢弃了。
The faulty products are being discarded by the manufacturer. = 有缺陷的产品正在被制造商丢弃。

疑问句:
What did you discard in the trash? = 你扔了什么垃圾？
Where are the discarded materials kept? = 废弃的材料存放在哪里？

否定句:
He didn't discard the letter. = 他没有丢弃那封信。
She doesn't discard anything useful. = 她不会丢弃任何有用的东西。
