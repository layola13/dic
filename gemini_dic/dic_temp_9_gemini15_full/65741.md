evergreen:/ˈɛvərˌɡriːn/| adj.|常绿的；永久的；经久不衰的|  Always green; retaining foliage throughout the year.  Also used to describe something that remains popular or relevant over a long period. |例句：Pine trees are evergreen. 松树是常绿的。

The popularity of Shakespeare's plays is evergreen.  莎士比亚戏剧的流行是经久不衰的。|近义词： perennial, enduring, lasting |反义词： deciduous
