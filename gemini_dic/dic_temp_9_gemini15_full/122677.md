module:/ˈmɒdʒuːl/ /ˈmɑːdʒuːl/| n. |模块，组件，单元|A self-contained unit of a larger system, often interchangeable with other similar units.  A module can be a physical component or a piece of software.|例句：The car's engine is made up of many modules. 这辆车的引擎是由许多模块组成的。|例句：This software module handles user authentication. 这个软件模块负责用户身份验证。|近义词：unit, component, section, element|反义词：whole, entirety
