prepledged: /ˌpriːˈplɛdʒd/ | adj. | 预先承诺的，预先抵押的 |  Previously pledged; committed in advance.  This word describes something that has been promised or committed beforehand, often in the context of financial resources or support. |

例句：
The organization received prepledged donations totaling $1 million. = 该组织收到了总计100万美元的预先承诺捐款。

The bank had prepledged assets to secure the loan. = 银行已预先抵押资产以担保贷款。


近义词：committed, guaranteed, promised, assured


反义词：uncommitted, unpromised, uncertain


词性：形容词
