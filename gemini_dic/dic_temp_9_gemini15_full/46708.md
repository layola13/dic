dance:/dæns/| /dɑːns/| n., v.|跳舞，舞蹈|The act of moving rhythmically to music; a series of movements performed to music.  It can also refer to a particular style of dancing or a social gathering involving dancing.|例句：She loves to dance. 她喜欢跳舞。

例句：The dancers performed a graceful ballet dance. 舞蹈演员表演了一支优雅的芭蕾舞。

词源:
The word "dance" comes from the Old English word "dansian," which in turn has roots in the Proto-Germanic word "*dansōjan," meaning "to stretch, leap, jump".  These roots highlight the energetic and often leaping movements associated with dancing.


近义词:

反义词:
(There isn't a direct antonym for "dance," but words implying stillness or inactivity could be considered opposites in context.)  sit, stand, remain still
