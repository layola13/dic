fears:/fɪərz/| n.|恐惧；担忧；害怕的事物|The word "fears" is the plural form of the noun "fear," referring to feelings of anxiety and apprehension caused by the anticipation of danger or misfortune.  It can also refer to the things that cause these feelings. |His fears were unfounded.他的恐惧是毫无根据的。|Her greatest fears centered around losing her family. 她最大的恐惧是失去家人。| anxieties, apprehensions, worries |courage, bravery|名词
