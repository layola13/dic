offloaders:/ˈɒfˌləʊdərz/ | n. |卸载器；卸载程序；卸货工人 |A person or thing that offloads something.  Typically refers to computer software, hardware devices, or people who move goods. |例句：The offloaders worked quickly to get the cargo off the ship. 卸货工人快速地将货物从船上卸下。  The new offloading software significantly improved data transfer speeds. 新的卸载软件大大提高了数据传输速度。|近义词：unloaders, dischargers|反义词：loaders
