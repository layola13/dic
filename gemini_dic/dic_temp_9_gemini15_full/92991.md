idiomatically:/ˌɪdiəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 惯用地；习语地 | In a way that is characteristic of or appropriate to an idiom or idioms; according to the idioms of a language.  | 例句：The phrase "kick the bucket" is used idiomatically to mean "to die".  这句话“踢桶子”惯常用来表示“死亡”。

例句：He spoke idiomatically, using expressions that were typical of his region. 他说得很地道，使用了该地区典型的表达方式。


近义词：figuratively, colloquially, expressively

反义词：literally, formally, plainly

词性：副词
