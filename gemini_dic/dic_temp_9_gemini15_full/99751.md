intermeation: /ˌɪntərˈmiːeɪʃən/ | n. | 互相渗透；相互交融；介入；调解 | The intermingling or penetration of different elements or substances.  It often refers to the gradual blending or mixing of two or more things. | 例句：The intermeation of cultures led to a unique blend of traditions. 文化的互相渗透导致了独特的传统融合。


The intermeation of different musical styles created a new genre. 不同音乐风格的相互交融创造了一种新的类型。| 近义词：intermingling, interpenetration, fusion, blending | 反义词：separation, segregation, isolation |  名词
