cowberry:/ˈkaʊbəri/| n.|越橘，蔓越莓|A small, red berry that grows on low-lying shrubs, often in bogs or acidic soils.  It has a tart flavor and is used in jams, jellies, and other food products.  The plant itself is a type of evergreen shrub. |The cowberry jam was delicious.|近义词：lingonberry|反义词：无
