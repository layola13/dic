loculose:/ˈlɒkjʊlˌoʊs/| n. |小室；小格；囊室 | A small cavity or compartment, especially one in an ovary or fruit containing a seed or ovule.  |Example: The locules of the orange were filled with juicy segments.  橘子的囊室里充满了多汁的果瓣。  The botanist carefully examined the locules of the flower's ovary. 植物学家仔细检查了花朵子房的小室。|近义词：cell, compartment, chamber |反义词：None specifically, but terms implying a large open space could be considered antonyms.
