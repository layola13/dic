house-warming:/ˈhaʊsˌwɔːrmɪŋ/| n. |乔迁宴，新居暖房宴|A party to celebrate moving into a new home.  It's a tradition to celebrate a new beginning and welcome guests into the new space.|例句：We're having a house-warming party next weekend. = 我们下周末要举行乔迁宴。|例句：They invited all their friends to their house-warming. = 他们邀请了所有朋友参加他们的乔迁宴。|近义词：housewarming party, moving-in party|反义词：moving-out party
