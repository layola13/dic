nonprovocation:/ˌnɒnˌprɒvəˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |非挑衅；不激怒 |The act or policy of not provoking or instigating aggression or conflict.  It emphasizes a commitment to peaceful resolution and avoidance of actions that could escalate tensions.|例句：The government's policy of nonprovocation aims to de-escalate the conflict. 政府的非挑衅政策旨在缓和冲突。


The principle of nonprovocation is crucial in international relations.  非挑衅原则在国际关系中至关重要。|近义词：pacifism, appeasement |反义词：provocation, aggression
