detail's:/ˈdiːteɪlz/| n. |细节，详情 |The details of the plan are still being worked out. 计划的细节仍在制定中。|近义词：particulars, specifics |反义词：overview, summary |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The report details the company's financial performance.  = 这份报告详细说明了公司的财务业绩。
She details every step of the process in her book. = 她在书中详细描述了这个过程的每一个步骤。


一般过去时:
He detailed his experiences in the war. = 他详细叙述了他战争中的经历。
The architect detailed the plans for the new building. = 建筑师详细阐述了新建筑的计划。


现在完成时:
She has detailed all the necessary information in her email. = 她在电子邮件中详细说明了所有必要的信息。
They have detailed their findings in a comprehensive report. = 他们在综合报告中详细说明了他们的调查结果。


过去完成时:
He had detailed his concerns to his supervisor before the meeting. = 在会议之前，他已经向他的主管详细说明了他的担忧。
The witness had detailed the events leading up to the accident. = 目击者已经详细描述了导致事故发生的事件。


将来时:
She will detail the steps involved in the project next week. = 她将在下周详细说明项目中涉及的步骤。
The speaker will detail his vision for the future of the company. = 演讲者将详细阐述他对公司未来的愿景。


虚拟语气:
If she detailed the problem more clearly, we could understand it better. = 如果她更清楚地说明问题，我们就能更好地理解它。
I wish he had detailed the instructions more carefully. = 我希望他更仔细地说明了指示。


被动语态:
The plan was detailed in the meeting. = 计划在会议上作了详细说明。
The steps involved were detailed in the manual. = 相关步骤在手册中作了详细说明。


疑问句:
Does the contract detail all the terms and conditions? = 合同是否详细说明了所有条款和条件？
Can you detail the process for submitting the application? = 你能详细说明提交申请的流程吗？


否定句:
The report did not detail the reasons for the delay. = 报告没有详细说明延误的原因。
He did not detail his feelings about the situation. = 他没有详细说明他对这种情况的感受。
