mishear:/ˌmɪsˈhɪr/| vt.|误听，听错|To hear something incorrectly.  |例句：I misheard what he said; I thought he said "yes" but he actually said "no". 我误听了他说的话；我以为他说“是”，但他实际上说的是“不是”。|近义词：misunderstand, mistake|反义词：hear correctly, understand


USAGE->

He mishears instructions frequently. = 他经常听错指示。
