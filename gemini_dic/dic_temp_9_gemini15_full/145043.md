overaggressively:/ˌoʊvərəˈɡrɛsɪvli/ | adv. | 过于咄咄逼人地，过于强势地 |  Describes an action or behavior that is excessively aggressive, going beyond what is considered appropriate or necessary.  It implies an excessive display of force, dominance, or assertiveness. |例句：He overaggressively pursued the deal, alienating potential partners. 他过于咄咄逼人地追求这笔交易，疏远了潜在的合作伙伴。

He responded overaggressively to the criticism, further escalating the conflict. 他对批评反应过于强势，进一步加剧了冲突。


近义词：too aggressively, excessively aggressively, fiercely, ruthlessly, brutally, domineeringly | 反义词：passively, mildly, gently, subtly, diplomatically
