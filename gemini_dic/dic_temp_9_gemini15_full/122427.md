USAGE->
一般现在时:
He moans every time he has a headache. 他每次头痛都会呻吟。
She moans about her job constantly. 她不断抱怨她的工作。

一般过去时:
The injured man moaned in pain. 受伤的男人痛苦地呻吟着。
She moaned about the long wait. 她抱怨漫长的等待。

现在完成时:
He has moaned about the weather all day. 他一整天都在抱怨天气。
She has moaned and groaned about the situation for weeks. 她已经抱怨这个情况几周了。

过去完成时:
He had moaned about the noise all night. 他整夜都在抱怨噪音。
She had moaned about the lack of support before the project started. 项目开始前她就抱怨缺乏支持。

将来时:
He will moan if he doesn't get his way. 如果他不遂心意，他会抱怨的。
She will moan and groan when she finds out. 当她发现的时候，她会唉声叹气的。

虚拟语气:
If he moaned, I would give him some medicine. 如果他呻吟，我会给他一些药。
I wish he wouldn't moan so much. 我希望他不要抱怨那么多。

被动语态:
His complaints were often moaned about by his colleagues. 他的抱怨经常被他的同事们抱怨。
The unfairness of the situation was moaned about by many.  许多人抱怨这种情况的不公平。

疑问句:
Does he often moan about his work? 他经常抱怨他的工作吗？
Why is she always moaning? 她为什么总是抱怨？

否定句:
He didn't moan about the extra work. 他没有抱怨额外的工作。
She didn't moan, but I could tell she was unhappy. 她没有抱怨，但我看得出她不高兴。
