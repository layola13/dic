overdeliberate: /ˌoʊvər dɪˈlɪbərət/ | adj. | 过度考虑的；过于谨慎的 | Excessively or unnecessarily careful or cautious; characterized by excessive deliberation.  |  例句：His overdeliberate approach to the problem prevented him from finding a quick solution. = 他对问题的过度谨慎态度妨碍了他快速找到解决方案。

His overdeliberate response to the crisis caused further delays. = 他对危机的过度谨慎回应导致了进一步的延误。


近义词：hesitant, painstaking, meticulous, scrupulous, cautious | 反义词：rash, impulsive, hasty, reckless | 词性:形容词
