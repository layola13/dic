finalizes:/ˈfaɪnəlaɪz/| vt.| 使最终确定；完成；定稿 | To make something final or complete.  |例句：He finalized the deal after weeks of negotiation. 他在几周的谈判后最终敲定了这笔交易。|例句：She finalized the report before submitting it.  她在提交报告前完成了定稿。|近义词：complete, conclude, finish |反义词：begin, start, initiate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He finalizes the documents every Friday. = 他每周五完成文件的最终定稿。
The committee finalizes the budget each year. = 委员会每年最终确定预算。

She finalized her thesis last night. = 她昨晚完成了论文的最终定稿。
