nonmalignancies:/ˌnɒnˈmælɪɡnənsiz/| n. |非恶性肿瘤，良性肿瘤|Nonmalignancies refer to growths or tumors that are not cancerous and do not spread to other parts of the body.  They are generally considered benign and do not pose a serious threat to life.| Example: The biopsy revealed the presence of several nonmalignancies. 活检结果显示存在几个良性肿瘤。|近义词：benign tumors, benign neoplasms |反义词：malignancies, cancers
