It encompasses the emotional, physical, and social aspects of raising children. |Example:  Parenthood is a challenging but rewarding experience. 为人父母是一项充满挑战但又令人欣慰的经历。

The joys of parenthood are immeasurable.  为人父母的快乐是无法衡量的。|近义词：parentage, family life, child-rearing |反义词：childlessness
