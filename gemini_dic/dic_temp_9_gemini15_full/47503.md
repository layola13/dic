dearie:/ˈdɪəri/| n.|亲爱的；宝贝儿|A term of endearment, often used to address someone loved or cherished.  It carries a sense of warmth and affection, sometimes with a slightly condescending or patronizing tone depending on context and intonation.|Example: "Oh, dearie, you've made a mess!"  哦，亲爱的，你弄得一团糟！|近义词： darling, sweetheart, honey, love|反义词： (no direct antonym, the opposite would be a term of insult or dislike)|名词
