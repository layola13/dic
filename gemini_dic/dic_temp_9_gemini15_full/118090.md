menials:/ˈmiːniəlz/| n.|仆人，佣人|Domestic servants, especially those doing the humblest work.  The word implies a low social status and often repetitive, unskilled labor.|The menials worked tirelessly to keep the grand estate running smoothly.  仆人们不知疲倦地工作，以确保庄园顺利运转。|servants, domestics, lackeys|masters, employers|名词
