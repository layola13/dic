groundsheet: /ˈɡraʊndʃiːt/ | n. | 地席，防潮垫 | A sheet of strong, waterproof material placed on the ground as a base for a tent or sleeping bag, to provide insulation and protection from dampness.  | Example: We laid down a groundsheet before pitching our tent to keep it dry. 我们在搭帐篷之前铺了一块地席，以保持帐篷干燥。 |近义词：ground cloth, tarp, footprint (for tents) | 反义词：N/A
