USAGE->

Hallelujah is an interjection, so it doesn't conjugate like verbs or nouns. It doesn't have tenses or other grammatical forms.  It's always used as an exclamation.

PHRASE->

There are no common phrases directly using "hallelujah" as a component.  Its usage is primarily standalone.
