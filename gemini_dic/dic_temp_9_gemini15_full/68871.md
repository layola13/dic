fast-track:/ˈfæst træk/| vt. n.|快速通道；快速提升；快速处理|To accelerate the progress or advancement of something or someone.|例句：The company fast-tracked the new product to meet the holiday demand. 公司为了满足节假日需求而加快了新产品的上市速度。|例句：He was fast-tracked to management after only two years. 他仅仅两年就被快速提升到了管理层。|近义词：accelerate, expedite, rush|反义词：delay, slow down, procrastinate|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They fast-track promising employees. = 他们快速提升有潜力的员工。
The government fast-tracks infrastructure projects. = 政府快速推进基础设施项目。


一般过去时:
The company fast-tracked the development process. = 公司加快了开发进程。
