memorizations:/ˌmɛməraɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |记忆；记住的行为 |The act of memorizing; the process of committing something to memory.  It often refers to the act of learning and retaining information, facts, or skills. Memorizations can be aided by techniques like repetition, mnemonics, and spaced repetition. |例句：His excellent memorizations of poetry impressed the judges. 他对诗歌极佳的记忆力给评委们留下了深刻的印象。

例句：The student's memorizations of the historical dates were thorough. 这个学生对历史日期的记忆非常全面。


近义词：committal to memory, learning by rote, rote learning |反义词：forgetting, oblivion


词性：名词
