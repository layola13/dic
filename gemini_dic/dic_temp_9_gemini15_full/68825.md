fascinator:/ˈfæsɪneɪtər/| n. |迷人装饰品，帽子装饰|A fascinator is a decorative headpiece, often worn to weddings or other formal occasions. It is usually smaller and less structured than a hat.  |Example: She wore a beautiful feather fascinator to the wedding. 她戴着一个美丽的羽毛装饰帽参加婚礼。|近义词：headpiece, hair ornament|反义词：None (No direct antonym)|名词
