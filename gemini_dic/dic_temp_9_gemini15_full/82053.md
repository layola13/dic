gristly:/ˈɡrɪstli/| adj.| 多筋的；坚韧的；难嚼的 |Containing or resembling gristle; tough and chewy.  The steak was gristly and difficult to chew. |近义词：tough, chewy, sinewy |反义词：tender, soft


例句：

The meat was gristly and full of cartilage.  = 这肉很筋道，全是软骨。

I found a gristly piece of chicken in my soup. = 我在汤里发现了一块多筋的鸡肉。


词源：

From gristle + -y.  Gristle itself derives from Old English *gristil, ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly related to words for "grit" or "grind."


用法说明：

gristly 主要用作形容词，用来描述食物质地，特别是肉类，指其含有大量软骨或筋，因此口感坚韧，难以咀嚼。  它通常带有负面的含义，暗示食物质量不高或口感不佳。
