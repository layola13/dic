overpurchasing:/ˌoʊvərˈpɜːrtʃeɪsɪŋ/| n. |过度购买，购置过多|The act of buying more of something than is needed or can be used.  This often leads to waste and financial strain.|例句：The company's overpurchasing of raw materials led to significant storage costs.  公司过度购买原材料导致了巨大的仓储成本。

例句：Her overpurchasing of clothes resulted in a cluttered closet and unnecessary debt. 她的过度购物导致衣柜杂乱无章，还背上了不必要的债务。


近义词：overbuying, excessive purchasing, surplus buying


反义词：underbuying, insufficient purchasing


词性:名词
