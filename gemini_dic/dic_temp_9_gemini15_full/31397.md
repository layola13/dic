charged:/tʃɑːrdʒd/| adj., v.| 收费的；充电的；控告；充满；|Having an electrical charge; filled or imbued with a specified quality or feeling; to demand (an amount) as a price; to accuse someone formally of a crime.|例句：The battery is fully charged.  电池已充满电。

The man was charged with murder.  这名男子被指控犯有谋杀罪。|近义词：accused, loaded, filled, energized|反义词：discharged, empty, unloaded


USAGE->
