non-skid:/ˌnɒnˈskɪd/ | adj. |防滑的|Preventing slipping or sliding;  having a surface that provides good traction.  The word is a compound of "non-" (meaning "not") and "skid" (meaning "to slide or slip").|例句：The non-skid surface of the boat deck prevented accidents. 船甲板的防滑表面防止了事故发生。 The shoes have non-skid soles for better grip on icy surfaces. 这双鞋的鞋底是防滑的，在冰面上抓地力更好。|近义词：antiskid, slip-resistant, anti-slip |反义词：slippery, skiddy
