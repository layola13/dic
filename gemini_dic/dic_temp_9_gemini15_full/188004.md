rough:/rʌf/| adj., n., v.|粗糙的；艰难的；近似的；草率的；粗略的 |  Unpleasant to the touch; not smooth; lacking refinement or elegance; approximate;  violent or stormy; difficult; to make something rough.|例句：The surface of the wood is rough.  木材表面粗糙。|The journey was rough. 这段旅程很艰难。|近义词：uneven, bumpy, harsh, difficult, approximate|反义词：smooth, polished, easy, refined, precise


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The road is rough. = 这条路很粗糙。
This task seems rough. = 这项任务看起来很艰难。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
