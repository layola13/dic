houseboys:/ˈhaʊsˌbɔɪz/ | n. | 男佣人，男仆 |  Houseboys are male domestic servants who perform various household tasks.  The term is considered outdated and potentially offensive in many contexts due to its colonial connotations. | Example: In some older novels, you might read about wealthy families employing several houseboys.  在一些旧小说中，你可能会读到富裕家庭雇佣几个男佣人的情况。  |近义词：male servants, domestic help (more neutral)| 反义词：employer, mistress |  名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The houseboys clean the house every day. = 男佣人们每天打扫房子。
He helps the houseboys with their work. = 他帮助男佣人们工作。


一般过去时:
The houseboys cleaned the house yesterday. = 男佣人们昨天打扫了房子。
They helped the houseboys with the heavy furniture. = 他们帮助男佣人们搬动沉重的家具。


现在完成时:
The houseboys have cleaned all the rooms. = 男佣人们已经打扫了所有房间。
We have given the houseboys their wages. = 我们已经给了男佣人们他们的工资。


过去完成时:
The houseboys had already cleaned the house before the guests arrived. = 客人们到达之前，男佣们已经打扫好房子了。
The master had dismissed the houseboys before the scandal broke. = 丑闻爆发前，主人已经解雇了男佣们。


将来时:
The houseboys will clean the house tomorrow. = 男佣人们明天将打扫房子。
The family will hire new houseboys next month. = 这个家庭下个月将雇佣新的男佣人。


虚拟语气:
If they were houseboys, they would have to work hard. = 如果他们是男佣人，他们就必须努力工作。
I wish I had more houseboys to help me. = 我希望我有更多的男佣人来帮我。


被动语态:
The house was cleaned by the houseboys. = 房子是由男佣人们打扫的。
The wages were paid to the houseboys on time. = 工资按时支付给了男佣人们。


疑问句:
Are those houseboys working hard? = 那些男佣人们努力工作吗？
How many houseboys does the family employ? = 这个家庭雇佣了多少个男佣人？


否定句:
The houseboys didn't clean the kitchen. = 男佣人们没有打扫厨房。
We don't need any more houseboys. = 我们不需要更多的男佣人了。



PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases directly using "houseboys".  The term itself is outdated and should be replaced with more appropriate and respectful alternatives like "domestic workers," "household staff," or "housekeepers".
