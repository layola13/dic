fanciest:/ˈfænsiɪst/| adj.|最别致的；最奇特的；最昂贵的|Most elaborate, showy, or expensive.  It is the superlative form of "fancy."|例句：She wore her fanciest dress to the ball. 她穿着最漂亮的裙子去参加舞会。

例句：That's the fanciest restaurant in town. 那是城里最豪华的餐厅。


近义词：most elaborate, most ornate, most luxurious, most stylish

反义词：plainest, simplest, most ordinary


词性:形容词
