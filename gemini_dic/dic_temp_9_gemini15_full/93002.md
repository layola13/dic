idiopathy:/ˌɪdiˈɒpəθi/ | n. |特发病，原发病|A disease of unknown cause; a disease that arises spontaneously and not as a result of another disease or known external cause.  |例句：The cause of his idiopathic thrombocytopenic purpura remains unknown.  他的特发性血小板减少性紫癜病因不明。 |例句：Researchers are working to identify the genetic factors that contribute to idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis. 研究人员正在努力确定导致特发性肺纤维化的遗传因素。|近义词：spontaneous disease, primary disease |反义词：secondary disease, symptomatic disease


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.

PHRASE->
Idiopathic disease = 特发性疾病
Idiopathic epilepsy = 特发性癫痫
Idiopathic thrombocytopenic purpura (ITP) = 特发性血小板减少性紫癜
Idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis (IPF) = 特发性肺纤维化
Idiopathic intracranial hypertension (IIH) = 特发性颅内压增高
