medicine:/ˈmɛdəsɪn/| n.|医学；医药；药物|The study and practice of healing diseases and injuries.  It encompasses various aspects like diagnosis, treatment, and prevention.  Also refers to drugs or remedies used to cure or alleviate illness.|例句：She studied medicine at Harvard. 她哈佛大学学习医学。

例句：The doctor prescribed some medicine for my cough. 医生给我开了些治咳嗽的药。


近义词：medication, drug, remedy, therapy|反义词：disease, illness
