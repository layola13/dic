locked:/lɒkt/ /lɑːkt/| adj. |锁上的；被锁住的；无法改变的；无法移动的|The door is locked.  The problem is locked in my mind. The price is locked in. The suspect is locked up.|近义词：fastened, secured, confined, immovable |反义词：unlocked, open, free


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The door is locked. = 门锁上了。
The safe is locked. = 保险箱锁上了。

一般过去时:
He locked the door. = 他锁上了门。
She locked her car. = 她锁上了她的车。

现在完成时:
I have locked the car. = 我已经锁了车。
They have locked up the evidence. = 他们已经锁起了证据。

过去完成时:
He had locked the door before he left. = 他离开前已经锁上门了。
She had locked the car up before going to sleep. = 她睡觉前已经锁好车了。

将来时:
I will lock the door when I leave. = 我离开时会锁上门。
He will lock the safe after putting the money in. = 他把钱放进去后会锁上保险箱。

虚拟语气:
If I had locked the door, the thief wouldn't have gotten in. = 如果我锁上门了，贼就不会进来了。
I wish I had locked my bike. = 我真希望我锁了我的自行车。

被动语态:
The door was locked. = 门被锁上了。
The car was locked by the owner. = 车被车主锁上了。

疑问句:
Is the door locked? = 门锁上了吗？
Are the windows locked? = 窗户锁上了吗？

否定句:
The door isn't locked. = 门没有锁上。
The safe wasn't locked. = 保险箱没有锁上。


PHRASE->
locked in:  (想法等) 固定的；(价格) 锁定的
locked down: 封锁；严格管控
locked away: 隐藏；锁起来
locked up: 被监禁；被关押
locked out: 被锁在门外；被排除在外
