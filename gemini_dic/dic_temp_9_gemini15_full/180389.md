refutable:/rɪˈfjuːtəbl/| adj.| 可反驳的，可驳斥的 |capable of being proved wrong or false.  Something that is refutable can be shown to be incorrect or untrue through evidence or argument. |例句：His theory is refutable; new evidence could easily disprove it. 他的理论是可反驳的；新的证据很容易就能证明它是错误的。

例句：The claim that the earth is flat is easily refutable. 声称地球是平的是很容易被驳斥的。


近义词：disputable, debatable, contestable, challengeable |反义词：irrefutable, undeniable, incontrovertible


词性:形容词
