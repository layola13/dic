deftnesses:/ˈdeftnəsɪz/| n. |熟练；灵巧；技巧；熟练技巧|The plural form of "deftness," referring to multiple instances of skill, dexterity, or adroitness.  It implies a range of abilities performed with ease and precision. |例句：Her deftnesses in handling the negotiations impressed everyone.  她的谈判技巧给每个人留下了深刻的印象。

例句：The pianist's deftnesses were evident in every note.  钢琴家的高超技巧在每一个音符中都显而易见。


近义词：skills, dexterities, adroitnesses, proficiencies, aptitudes


反义词：clumsinesses, awkwardnesses, ineptitudes


词性:名词


词源:  From Middle English *deft, from Old English *dæft "fit, suitable, proper," from Proto-Germanic *daptaz "suitable," from Proto-Indo-European *(h₁)dep- "to fit, adapt."  The "-ness" suffix is a common English noun suffix indicating a state, quality, or condition.
