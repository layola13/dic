folios:/ˈfoʊliˌoʊz/| n.| 作品集；大开本的书；文件夹 |A folio is a large sheet of paper folded once to make two leaves (four pages) or a book made of such sheets; it can also refer to a collection of works, typically by the same artist or author.  |例句：The artist's folio showcased her best paintings.  这位艺术家的作品集展示了她最好的画作。

例句：He keeps important documents in a folio. 他把重要的文件放在文件夹里。


近义词：portfolio, collection, volume |反义词：None specifically.


词源：From Middle French *folio, from Latin folium ("leaf").  The word originally referred to a single leaf of a manuscript, and later came to refer to the pages of a book.
