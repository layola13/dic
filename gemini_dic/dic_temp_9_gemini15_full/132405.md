nondenominationalisms:/ˌnɑːnˌdɪˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃənəlɪzəm/| n. |非教派主义；非宗派性|The plural form of "nondenominationalism," referring to the multiple instances or aspects of not being affiliated with a specific religious denomination.
