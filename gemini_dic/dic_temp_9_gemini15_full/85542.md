hatchery:/ˈhætʃəri/ | n. |孵化场；孵卵所；培育场 | A place where fish, eggs, or other animals are hatched artificially.  |Example: The salmon hatchery releases millions of young fish into the river each year.  鲑鱼孵化场每年向河流中放流数百万尾幼鱼。

|Example:  The turtle hatchery protects endangered sea turtle eggs from predators. 海龟孵化场保护濒危海龟蛋免受捕食者的侵害。| 近义词：breeding ground, nursery | 反义词：None specifically contrasting. |词性：名词
