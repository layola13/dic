pleasantest:/ˈplɛzəntɪst/| adj.|最令人愉快的；最舒适的|Most pleasant; most agreeable.  It is the superlative form of the adjective "pleasant".|例句：The pleasantest time of year is autumn. 一年中最好的时光是秋天。

例句：This is the pleasantest hotel I've ever stayed in. 这是我住过的最舒适的酒店。


近义词：most delightful, most enjoyable, most agreeable|反义词：most unpleasant, most disagreeable, most awful|词性:形容词
