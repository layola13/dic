exhilarates:/ɪɡˈzɪləreɪts/| vt.|使兴奋；使高兴；使振奋|To fill someone with excitement and happiness.|The roller coaster ride exhilarated the children.  过山车让孩子们兴奋不已。|The news exhilarated her. 这个消息让她欣喜若狂。|thrill, excite, invigorate|depress, discourage, dispirit|动词
