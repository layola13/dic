dusted:/ˈdʌstɪd/| v.| 拂去灰尘；掸去；(使)蒙上薄层灰尘 | To remove dust from something using a cloth or brush; to cover something lightly with dust. |例句：She dusted the furniture. 她擦拭了家具。

例句：A light layer of snow dusted the landscape. 一层薄薄的雪覆盖了这片景色。

近义词：cleaned, wiped, powdered

反义词：covered (with something other than dust)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dusts the shelves every week. = 他每周都擦拭架子。
She dusts the furniture before guests arrive. = 客人来之前，她会擦拭家具。


一般过去时:
I dusted the table yesterday. = 我昨天擦了桌子。
They dusted the antique clock carefully. = 他们小心翼翼地擦拭了古董钟。


现在完成时:
I have dusted the entire house. = 我已经打扫了整个房子。
She has dusted the paintings several times this month. = 这个月她已经擦拭了几次画作。


过去完成时:
He had dusted the car before he drove it. = 他开车前已经擦过车了。
They had dusted the artifacts before placing them in the display case. = 他们把文物放入陈列柜之前已经擦拭过了。


将来时:
I will dust the living room later. = 我待会儿会打扫客厅。
She will dust the shelves tomorrow. = 她明天会擦拭架子。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would dust the attic. = 如果我有更多时间，我会打扫阁楼。
I wish I had dusted the furniture before the party. = 我真希望在聚会前把家具擦干净。


被动语态:
The furniture is dusted every week. = 家具每周都擦拭。
The antique vase was carefully dusted by the curator. = 古董花瓶由馆长小心地擦拭。


疑问句:
Have you dusted the bookshelves yet? = 你已经擦拭书架了吗？
Will you dust the mantlepiece today? = 你今天会擦拭壁炉架吗？


否定句:
I didn't dust the floor today. = 我今天没扫地。
She hasn't dusted the blinds yet. = 她还没擦百叶窗。
