nondemonstrable: /ˌnɒnˈdɛmənstrəbl/ | adj. | 不可证实的，无法证明的 |  Not capable of being demonstrated or proved; incapable of being shown or explained.  It refers to something that lacks empirical evidence or logical proof. |


例句：

The existence of God is a nondemonstrable proposition for many people.  许多人认为上帝的存在是一个不可证实的命题。

His claims of innocence remain nondemonstrable without further evidence.  如果没有进一步的证据，他无罪的主张仍然无法证明。


近义词：unprovable, unproven, unverifiable, irrefutable(反义词,表示“不可反驳的”，但语义上有所不同)


反义词：demonstrable, provable, verifiable


词源:

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not"
* **demonstrable**:  from "demonstrate," ultimately from Latin *demonstrare*  ("to show," "to point out," from *de-* "down" + *monstrare* "to show").


USAGE->

(由于nondemonstrable是形容词，没有动词的时态变化。以下例句展示了其在不同句型中的用法)


肯定句:

The theory is nondemonstrable with the current technology.  目前的科技无法证明该理论。

His guilt is nondemonstrable without further evidence.  没有进一步的证据，他的罪行是无法证明的。


否定句:

The claim is not nondemonstrable; there is sufficient evidence to support it. 该说法并非不可证实；有足够的证据支持它。

It's not nondemonstrable that he was there; witnesses saw him.  他当时在那里并非不可证实；证人看到了他。


疑问句:

Is the hypothesis nondemonstrable given the limitations of our current research methods?  鉴于我们当前研究方法的局限性，这个假设是否不可证实？

Are the long-term effects of this drug truly nondemonstrable, or simply unstudied?  这种药物的长期影响真的不可证实吗，还是仅仅没有被研究过？


PHRASE->

(nondemonstrable本身很少与其他词构成固定词组，以下列举一些例句，体现其在不同语境下的搭配)

nondemonstrable hypothesis: 不可证实的假设

nondemonstrable claim: 不可证实的声明

nondemonstrable theory: 不可证实的理论

nondemonstrable proposition: 不可证实的命题

nondemonstrable truth: 不可证实的真理
