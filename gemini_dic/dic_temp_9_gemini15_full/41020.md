copastor:/ˈkoʊˌpæstər/ | n. | 副牧师 | A copastor is a pastor who shares the responsibilities of a church with another pastor.  They work collaboratively to lead and guide the congregation.  |  The copastor delivered a powerful sermon on Sunday.  副牧师在周日做了一场精彩的布道。 The senior pastor and copastor worked together to plan the church's annual retreat.  主任牧师和副牧师一起策划了教会的年度静修。 | co-pastor, associate pastor |  (no direct antonym)
