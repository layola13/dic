parching:/ˈpɑːrtʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 干渴的；干枯的；酷热的 | Extremely thirsty; drying out; intensely hot.  |例句：The parching heat of the desert made travel difficult.  沙漠中酷热难耐，使得旅行变得困难。

例句：After hiking for hours, I felt a parching thirst.  徒步旅行数小时后，我感到非常口渴。


近义词：arid, dry, scorching, sweltering, thirsty | 反义词：moist, damp, hydrated

词源: 源自古英语 *parcian "to scorch," from Proto-Germanic *parkjanan (source also of Old High German percnen "to parch," Old Norse þverka "to dry up," Old English þorcian "to be thirsty").  The meaning shifted to "to be thirsty" in Middle English.
