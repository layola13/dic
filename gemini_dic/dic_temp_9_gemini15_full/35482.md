coachy:/ˈkoʊtʃi/| adj.|像教练的；爱指点的；|having the characteristics of a coach; excessively instructive or guiding.  It often implies being overly assertive or bossy in offering advice or instruction.|例句：His coachy style of management made employees feel micromanaged.  他的教练式管理风格让员工感觉受到了过度管理。

例句：She gave me some coachy advice on how to improve my presentation skills. 她给了我一些关于如何提升我的演示技巧的指导性建议。（略带负面含义，暗示建议过多）


近义词：bossy, overbearing, instructive, guiding, mentoring


反义词：unhelpful, passive, uninvolved, laissez-faire


词性：形容词
