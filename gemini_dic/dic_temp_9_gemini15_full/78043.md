genuine:/ˈdʒenjuɪn/| adj.| 真正的；真实的；真诚的；非伪造的 | Authentic; real; not fake or imitation; sincere.  |例句：His genuine concern for others was evident. 他对别人的真诚关切是显而易见的。

例句：The painting was declared genuine by experts.  这幅画被专家认定为真迹。


近义词：authentic, real, true, sincere, original

反义词：fake, counterfeit, artificial, insincere, phony
