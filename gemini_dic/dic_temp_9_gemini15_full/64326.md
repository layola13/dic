era's:/ˈɪərəz/| n.|时代，年代；纪元|The possessive form of "era," indicating belonging to or associated with a particular era.  It refers to a specific period of time marked by notable events or characteristics. |例句：The 1920s were a golden era's of jazz music.  二十世纪二十年代是爵士乐的黄金时代。  |例句：This museum showcases artifacts from various era's of human history. 这个博物馆展出了各个时代的人类历史文物。|近义词：period, epoch, age|反义词：None (the antonym would depend on the specific era being contrasted)|名词
