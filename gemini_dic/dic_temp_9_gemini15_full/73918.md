forswear:/fɔːˈsweə(r)/| vt.|发誓放弃；弃绝；否认；摒弃|To renounce or reject (something), especially by oath or promise.  It often implies a formal or solemn renunciation.|例句：He forswore alcohol after his health scare. 他在健康受惊吓后发誓戒酒。  She forswore her allegiance to the king. 她放弃了她对国王的效忠。|近义词：renounce, abjure, repudiate, recant|反义词：affirm, endorse, embrace, uphold|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He forswears smoking. = 他发誓戒烟。
She forswears all forms of violence. = 她摒弃一切形式的暴力。

一般过去时:
He forswore his loyalty to the crown. = 他放弃了他对王室的效忠。
They forswore their previous beliefs. = 他们放弃了之前的信仰。

现在完成时:
She has forsworn gambling. = 她已经戒赌了。
He has forsworn all contact with his former associates. = 他已经与他以前的同伙断绝了所有联系。

过去完成时:
He had forsworn his ambition to become a lawyer. = 他之前已经放弃了他成为律师的雄心壮志。
They had forsworn violence before the negotiations began. = 在谈判开始之前，他们已经放弃了暴力。

将来时:
She will forswear revenge. = 她将放弃报复。
He will forswear all forms of dishonesty. = 他将放弃一切形式的不诚实行为。

虚拟语气:
If he forswore his bad habits, he would be much healthier. = 如果他戒掉坏习惯，他会健康得多。
I wish she had forsworn that terrible lifestyle. = 我真希望她能放弃那种糟糕的生活方式。

被动语态:
Alcohol was forsworn by many after the public health campaign. = 公共卫生运动之后，许多人戒酒了。(被动语态使用较少)
His loyalty was forsworn under duress. = 他的忠诚是在胁迫下放弃的。(被动语态使用较少)

疑问句:
Will he forswear his claim to the inheritance? = 他会放弃他继承遗产的权利吗？
Has she forsworn her previous statements? = 她是否放弃了她之前的声明？

否定句:
He did not forswear his right to appeal. = 他没有放弃他的上诉权。
She would not forswear her love for him. = 她不会放弃对他的爱。
