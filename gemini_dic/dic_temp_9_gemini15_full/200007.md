skeelgoose:/ˈskiːlɡuːs/| n. |笨蛋，傻瓜| A skeelgoose is a foolish or silly person; a term of mild abuse or ridicule.  The word is primarily found in dialects of Northern England and Scotland.|例句：He called me a skeelgoose for tripping over the cat. 他因为我被猫绊倒而叫我笨蛋。|近义词：fool, idiot, simpleton, nincompoop |反义词：genius, intellectual, clever
