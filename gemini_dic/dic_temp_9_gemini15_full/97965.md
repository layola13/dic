innutrition:/ˌɪnˌnjuːˈtrɪʃən/| n. |营养不良；营养不足 |The lack of proper nutrition, often resulting in a weakened physical condition.  It can be caused by insufficient intake of essential nutrients or impaired absorption of nutrients.  |例句：Malnutrition and innutrition are serious problems in many developing countries. 营养不良和营养不足在许多发展中国家都是严重的问题。

例句：Prolonged innutrition can lead to stunted growth and weakened immunity. 长期营养不良会导致生长迟缓和免疫力下降。


近义词：malnutrition, undernourishment, dietary deficiency |反义词：good nutrition, well-nourished
