/ˌɪntəˈpɑːləmənt/| n. |议会间的；跨议会的|Relating to or involving more than one parliament or legislature.  The term often refers to cooperation or interaction between different parliamentary bodies.|例句：Interparliamentary relations are crucial for international cooperation. 议会间的合作关系对国际合作至关重要。

例句：The interparliamentary delegation discussed trade agreements.  跨议会代表团讨论了贸易协定。


近义词：cross-parliamentary, transnational parliamentary


反义词：intraparliamentary (within a single parliament)


词性: n. (名词)
