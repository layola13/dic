lithotritor:/ˈlɪθəʊtraɪtər/| n. |碎石机；结石粉碎机|A lithotritor is a medical device used to break up stones (calculi) in the body, most commonly kidney stones, without the need for surgery.  It uses various methods, such as shock waves or lasers, to fragment the stones into smaller pieces that can then be passed naturally.| Example: The patient underwent lithotripsy using a sophisticated lithotritor to remove kidney stones.  病人接受了使用先进碎石机的体外碎石术以去除肾结石。|近义词： stone crusher,  calculus disintegrator |反义词：无
