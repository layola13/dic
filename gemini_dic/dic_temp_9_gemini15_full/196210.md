sequestrate:/ˈsiːkwɛstreɪt/| vt.|  sequester的变形 |没收；隔离；扣押；隔离；查封|To seize and hold (property) under legal authority, especially as security for a debt or fine; to isolate or separate from others; to confiscate.|The government sequestrated the assets of the drug cartel.  政府没收了贩毒集团的资产。|The judge sequestered the jury to prevent outside influence.  法官将陪审团隔离起来，以防止外界影响。|confiscate, impound, seize|release, restore, liberate|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government sequestrates assets involved in criminal activities. = 政府没收与犯罪活动有关的资产。
They sequester the jury during the trial. = 他们在审判期间将陪审团隔离。


一般过去时:
The court sequestrated the defendant's property. = 法院没收了被告的财产。
The scientists sequestered the virus in a secure laboratory. = 科学家们在一个安全的实验室里隔离了病毒。


现在完成时:
The authorities have sequestered millions of dollars in illicit funds. = 当局已经查封了数百万美元的非法资金。
They have sequestered the witnesses to protect them from intimidation. = 他们已经将证人隔离，以保护他们免受恐吓。


过去完成时:
The company had sequestered its funds before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，该公司已经冻结了其资金。
The police had sequestered the crime scene before the media arrived. = 在媒体到达之前，警方已经封锁了犯罪现场。


将来时:
The judge will sequester the jury until the verdict is reached. = 法官将隔离陪审团，直到做出裁决。
The bank will sequester the account until the legal dispute is resolved. = 银行将冻结账户，直到法律纠纷解决。


虚拟语气:
If the court sequestered all his assets, he would be bankrupt. = 如果法院没收了他所有的资产，他就会破产。
It would be better if the researchers sequestered the experimental samples more carefully. = 如果研究人员更仔细地隔离实验样本，那就更好了。


被动语态:
The assets were sequestered by the government. = 资产被政府没收了。
The jury was sequestered to avoid outside influence. = 陪审团被隔离以避免外界影响。


疑问句:
Will the court sequester the defendant's passport? = 法院会没收被告的护照吗？
Were the witnesses sequestered before giving their testimony? = 证人在作证前是否被隔离了？


否定句:
The judge did not sequester the jury in this case. = 在此案中，法官没有隔离陪审团。
The company did not sequester its profits, it distributed them to shareholders. = 公司没有没收其利润，而是将其分配给了股东。
