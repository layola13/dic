self-sway:/selfˈsweɪ/| v.|自我摇摆，自我控制不住|The act of swaying or rocking oneself, often unconsciously or due to emotional distress. It can also refer to the act of influencing or controlling oneself, often in a negative way, implying a lack of self-control.|例句：The anxious child began to self-sway in her chair.  焦虑的孩子开始在椅子上自我摇晃。|近义词：rock, sway, oscillate, waver|反义词：stillness, stability, composure|动词
