obligers:/əˈblaɪdʒərz/| n. |顺从者，讨好型人格|People who prioritize the needs and desires of others above their own, often to their own detriment. They are highly agreeable and have difficulty saying no.  |例句：She's such an obliger; she always puts everyone else's needs before her own. 她是个十足的顺从者，总是把别人的需求放在自己之前。|近义词：people-pleaser, appeaser|反义词：assertive person, self-centered person|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Obligers often neglect their own needs. = 顺从者经常忽略自己的需求。
Many obligers struggle with setting boundaries. = 许多顺从者难以设定界限。

一般过去时:
He obliged his friend by lending him money. = 他借钱给朋友，满足了他的请求。
She obliged everyone with her cheerful disposition. = 她以其开朗的性格取悦了所有人。


现在完成时:
She has obliged her family for years. = 她多年来一直顺从她的家人。
They have obliged their parents by attending college. = 他们通过上大学来顺从他们的父母。


过去完成时:
He had obliged her by completing the task early. = 他提前完成了任务，满足了她的要求。
She had obliged them with a delicious meal. = 她用美味佳肴款待了他们。


将来时:
I will oblige you by doing that for you. = 我会帮你做那件事，满足你的要求。
They will oblige their customers with excellent service. = 他们将以优质的服务取悦他们的顾客。


虚拟语气:
If I were an obliger, I would find it difficult to say no. = 如果我是个顺从者，我会发现很难说不。
I wish I hadn't obliged him so much. = 我真希望我没有那么顺从他。


被动语态:
His request was obliged by the manager. = 他的请求得到了经理的满足。
The audience was obliged by the performance. = 观众对演出感到满意。


疑问句:
Will you oblige me with a dance? = 你愿意和我跳支舞吗？
Does he often oblige others' requests? = 他经常满足别人的请求吗？


否定句:
I will not oblige his unreasonable demands. = 我不会满足他无理的要求。
She didn't oblige his request for money. = 她没有满足他借钱的要求。


PHRASE->

to oblige someone =  to do something for someone to help them
to be obliged to someone = to feel grateful to someone for their help
to feel obliged = 感觉有义务
to be under an obligation = 有义务
to oblige a request = 满足一个请求
