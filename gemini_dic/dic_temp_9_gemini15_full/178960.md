redecoration:/ˌriːˌdekəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |重新装饰，重新装修 |The act of decorating again;  renewing the decoration of something.  It involves changing the appearance of a space by repainting, adding new furnishings, or altering existing features. |例句：The redecoration of the living room was completed just in time for the party.  客厅的重新装修刚好赶上了派对。

例句：After years of neglect, the house is undergoing a major redecoration.  多年被忽视后，这房子正在进行大规模的重新装修。

近义词：renovation, refurbishment, remodeling |反义词：destruction, demolition |词性:名词
