outdone:/ˌaʊtˈdʌn/ | v. |胜过；超过；比…做得好|To surpass or exceed in performance or achievement.  It implies a competition or comparison.|例句：He was outdone by his younger sister in the singing competition. 他在歌唱比赛中被妹妹超过了。

例句：Her painting was completely outdone by the masterpiece she saw in the museum.  她的画作完全被她在博物馆看到的杰作比下去了。


近义词：surpass, excel, outperform, eclipse

反义词：be outperformed by, be surpassed by


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often outdoes his colleagues in the office. = 他经常在办公室里胜过他的同事。
She outdoes herself in every performance. = 她每次演出都超越自己。


一般过去时:
The team outdid their rivals in the final match. = 该队在决赛中战胜了对手。
He outdid his previous best time in the marathon. = 他在马拉松比赛中超越了他之前的最好成绩。


现在完成时:
She has outdone all expectations with her recent success. = 她最近的成功超出了所有人的预期。
They have outdone themselves in organizing this event. = 他们在这场活动的组织工作中超越了自我。


过去完成时:
By the time the race ended, he had outdone all the other competitors. = 到比赛结束时，他已经超过了所有其他竞争者。
She had outdone her previous record by a significant margin. = 她之前的记录被她大幅度地超越了。


将来时:
I am confident that I will outdo my previous score on the exam. = 我相信我会在考试中超过我之前的分数。
They believe they will outdo their competitors in the upcoming market. = 他们相信他们会在即将到来的市场竞争中胜过竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he might have outdone the champion. = 如果他训练更刻苦，他可能会胜过冠军。
I wish I had outdone my rival in the debate. = 我真希望我在辩论中胜过我的对手。


被动语态:
His work was outdone by a newer, more innovative design. = 他的作品被一个更新颖、更具创新性的设计所超越。
The previous record was outdone by a young athlete. = 之前的纪录被一位年轻运动员打破了。


疑问句:
Can you outdo your own personal best? = 你能超越你自己的最好成绩吗？
Did he outdo his opponent in the chess match? = 他在国际象棋比赛中战胜了他的对手吗？


否定句:
He did not outdo his opponent in the race. = 他在比赛中没有超过他的对手。
She couldn't outdo the established champion. = 她无法超越这位著名的冠军。
