outwatering: /ˌaʊtˈwɔːtərɪŋ/ | n. | 灌溉；排水 | The process of diverting water from a source to irrigate land, or the removal of excess water from an area.  | Example: The farmer relied on outwatering to keep his crops healthy during the dry season.  农民依靠灌溉来保持旱季作物健康。  | 近义词：irrigation, drainage, watering | 反义词：waterlogging, inundation |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer outwaters his fields every morning. = 农民每天早上都给他的田地灌溉。
They outwater the excess water from the mine. = 他们将矿井里的多余水排出。


一般过去时:
The team outwatered the flooded basement. = 团队排出了被淹的地下室的水。
He outwatered the garden yesterday. = 他昨天给花园浇了水。


现在完成时:
We have outwatered the entire area. = 我们已经排干了整个区域的水。
They have outwatered the field for weeks. = 他们已经给田地灌溉了好几个星期了。


过去完成时:
The crops had been outwatered before the drought. = 在干旱之前，庄稼已经灌溉过了。
The engineers had outwatered the tunnel before the inspection. = 工程师在检查之前已经排空了隧道的水。


将来时:
