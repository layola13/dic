nonconversantly:/ˌnɒnkənˈvɜːsəntli/ | adv. |非会话地，非交谈地 |  Not in a conversational manner; without engaging in conversation.  It implies a lack of interaction or dialogue, often suggesting a detached or uncommunicative approach. |  He stared nonconversantly at the wall, lost in thought. 他茫然地盯着墙壁，陷入沉思。  The two individuals exchanged only nonconversant glances.  两人只是交换了不带任何交流的眼神。|近义词：silently, taciturnly, uncommunicatively |反义词： conversationally, communicatively, interactively
