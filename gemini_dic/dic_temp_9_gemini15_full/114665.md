malices:/ˈmælɪsɪz/| n.|恶意；坏心肠；心怀恶意|The plural form of malice, referring to instances of ill will, spite, or wickedness.|例句：His actions were driven by pure malice.  他的行为完全是出于恶意。

例句：The rumors spread with malice aforethought. 这些谣言是蓄意散播的。


近义词：spite, ill will, malevolence, rancor, animosity|反义词：benevolence, kindness, goodwill, affection


词性:名词

词源:  From Old French *malice, from Latin malitia "badness, wickedness," from malus "bad."
