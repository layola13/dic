sidelight:/ˈsaɪdlaɪt/ | n. |侧灯；侧面；附加信息；间接的光线 | A sidelight is a secondary or incidental aspect of something, often revealing additional information or a different perspective.  It can also refer to a window or light source located on the side of a building or vehicle. |例句：The article provided a fascinating sidelight on the artist's early life. 这篇文章提供了关于这位艺术家早年生活的迷人细节。

The sidelights in the car allowed for better visibility at night. 车上的侧灯提高了夜间的视野。


近义词：aspect, detail, perspective, nuance | 反义词：main point, central focus

词源:

The word "sidelight" is a compound word, combining "side" (meaning a position to the side) and "light" (referring to a source of illumination or understanding).  Its usage as a metaphor to represent additional information developed over time.
