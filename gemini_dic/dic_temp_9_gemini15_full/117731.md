melanin:/ˈmɛlənɪn/| n. |黑色素 |A dark brown to black pigment occurring in the hair, skin, and iris of the eye in people and animals. It is responsible for tanning of skin exposed to sunlight.|例句：Melanin protects the skin from the harmful effects of ultraviolet radiation. 黑色素保护皮肤免受紫外线辐射的有害影响。|近义词：pigment |反义词：albinism
