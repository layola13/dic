deflagration:/ˌdɛfləˈɡreɪʃən/| n.|爆燃，爆轰|The rapid burning of a substance, typically with the production of heat and light, and the expansion of gases.|例句：The deflagration of the gunpowder caused a loud bang. 火药的爆燃引起了一声巨响。
例句：The rapid deflagration of the fuel-air mixture propelled the projectile forward. 燃料空气混合物的快速爆燃推动了弹丸前进。|近义词：combustion, explosion, detonation|反义词：deflagration is often contrasted with detonation, which is a much faster and more violent type of explosion. |
