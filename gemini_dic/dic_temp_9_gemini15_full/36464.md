coldest:/ˈkoʊldɪst/| adj.|最冷的|The superlative form of "cold," indicating the lowest temperature or the most intense feeling of coldness.|例句：December is the coldest month of the year. 十二月是一年中最冷的月份。
例句：The coldest part of the winter was the month of January. 冬季最冷的月份是一月份。|近义词：frostiest, iciest, chilliest|反义词：hottest, warmest
