preadvocacy:/ˈpriːˌædvəkəsi/| n. |预先宣传；提前倡导 |The act of advocating for something before it becomes a widely known issue or problem.  It involves raising awareness and building support for a cause before it gains significant public attention.  This approach can be crucial for preventing problems before they escalate or for ensuring smoother implementation of solutions. | Example:  The organization engaged in preadvocacy to secure funding for a new community center before the need became critical.  这个组织在需求变得紧迫之前就进行了预先宣传，以确保新的社区中心获得资金。|近义词：early advocacy, proactive advocacy, preventative advocacy |反义词：reactive advocacy, post-hoc advocacy |名词
