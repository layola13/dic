hosannah:/hɒzˈænə/| interjection | 歌颂；赞美；（尤指对上帝的）赞美歌 | An exclamation of praise or adoration, especially to God.  Often used in religious contexts, particularly in Christian services. | Example: The congregation shouted "Hosanna!" as the procession passed. 会众在队伍经过时高呼“何西阿那！” |近义词：praise, hallelujah, glory | 反义词：condemnation, damnation
