jut:/dʒʌt/ | vi. & n. | 突出，伸出；突出物 | To extend beyond or project from a surface; a projecting part. | The cliff jutted sharply out into the sea.  悬崖陡峭地伸向大海。 | The rocky outcrop was a jut of land.  岩石露头是一块突出地。 | 近义词：protrude, project, stick out | 反义词：recede, indent


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The roof juts out over the porch. 屋顶突出在门廊上方。
The land juts into the sea.  陆地伸入大海。


一般过去时:
The branch jutted out into the road. 树枝伸到路上。
The rock jutted from the ground.  岩石从地面突出。


现在完成时:
The building has jutted out into the street for years. 这栋建筑已经多年突出到街道上了。
The coastline has jutted out significantly over time.  海岸线随着时间的推移已经显著突出。


过去完成时:
The wall had jutted out before the extension was built. 在扩建之前，墙就已经突出来了。
The sign had jutted out further than expected. 标志的突出程度超过了预期。


将来时:
The new balcony will jut out from the building. 新的阳台将从建筑物突出。
The planned extension will jut out further than the current structure.  计划中的扩建部分将比现有结构更突出。


虚拟语气:
If the building jutted out less, there would be more space. 如果建筑物不那么突出，就会有更多空间。
I wish the window didn't jut out so much. 我希望窗户不要那么突出。


被动语态:
The rock was jutted out from the earth by an earthquake. 岩石被地震从地里震了出来。(注意：被动语态使用较少)
The platform is jutted out over the water.  平台突出在水上。(注意：被动语态使用较少)


疑问句:
Does the window sill jut out? 窗台突出吗？
Does the roofline jut out significantly? 屋顶线显著突出吗？


否定句:
The wall doesn't jut out. 墙壁没有突出。
The shelf doesn't jut out too far. 架子没有太突出。


PHRASE->
jut out:突出
jut forth: 伸出
jut over: 突出在…之上
a jut of land: 一块突出地
a jutting cliff: 一座突出的悬崖
