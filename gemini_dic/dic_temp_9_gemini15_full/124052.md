morphing:/ˈmɔːrfɪŋ/| v.|变形；逐渐改变；转化|The process of gradual transformation of one form into another, often through a continuous change of shape or appearance.  It can refer to both physical and abstract changes. |例句：The software allows for morphing images to create seamless transitions.  该软件允许图像变形以创建无缝过渡。

例句：The caterpillar morphed into a beautiful butterfly. 毛毛虫变成了美丽的蝴蝶。


近义词：transforming, metamorphosing, changing, evolving|反义词：remaining static, staying the same|动词
