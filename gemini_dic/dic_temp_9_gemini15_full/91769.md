hyperemphasizing: /ˌhaɪpərˈɛmfəsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过分强调；极度重视 |  The act of placing excessive or undue stress on something.  It suggests an over-the-top or disproportionate focus on a particular aspect. | 例句：He was hyperemphasizing the negative aspects of the report, ignoring the positive findings. 他过分强调报告中的负面内容，忽略了积极的发现。

例句：The marketing campaign hyperemphasized the product's novelty, neglecting its practical applications.  市场营销活动过分强调产品的创新性，而忽略了它的实际应用。


近义词：overemphasizing, stressing excessively, overstressing, highlighting excessively


反义词：underemphasizing, downplaying, minimizing, neglecting


词性:动词
