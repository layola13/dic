ension:/ɪnˈʃʌn/|n.|伸展；张力；紧张；范围|The physical act of stretching or extending something; a state of being stretched or strained;  a feeling of nervousness or anxiety; the scope or extent of something.|例句：The tension in the rope was almost unbearable.  绳子的张力几乎让人难以忍受。|例句：The tension in the room was palpable. 屋里的紧张气氛是显而易见的。|近义词：strain, stress, pressure, scope|反义词：relaxation, calmness, ease
