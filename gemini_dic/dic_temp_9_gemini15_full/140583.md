oligospermous:/ˌɒlɪɡəʊˈspɜːməs/| adj. | 寡精的，精子少的 | Having few seeds; producing few seeds.  The term is primarily used in botany to describe plants with a low seed count. | Example:  The oligospermous plant produced only a handful of seeds this season.  这株寡精植物本季只结出少量种子。 |近义词: pauciseed, few-seeded | 反义词: polyspermous, many-seeded
