quasi-prosperous: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈprɑːspərəs/ | adj. |表面繁荣的，貌似繁荣的 | Seemingly prosperous, but not truly so; exhibiting superficial signs of prosperity while underlying problems or weaknesses exist.  |例句：The city's economy appeared quasi-prosperous, but high levels of debt masked underlying fragility.  这个城市的经济看似繁荣，但高额债务掩盖了潜在的脆弱性。|近义词：apparently prosperous, seemingly affluent, superficially successful |反义词：truly impoverished, genuinely struggling, economically depressed
