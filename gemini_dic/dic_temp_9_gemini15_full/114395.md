make an end of: /meɪk ən ɛnd ɒv/ | phrasal verb | 结束；终止；消灭 | To bring something to a conclusion; to put an end to something; to destroy or eliminate something completely. | 例句：The police made an end of the riot. = 警察平息了暴乱。

例句：They made an end of the argument by agreeing to disagree. = 他们同意存在分歧，从而结束了争论。


近义词：finish, conclude, terminate, abolish, eliminate, destroy | 反义词：begin, start, commence, initiate, perpetuate


词性: phrasal verb
