life-or-death: /ˈlaɪf ɔːr deθ/ | adj. |生死攸关的，关系到生死的|  extremely important and may result in death if not handled correctly.  It describes a situation where the outcome is likely to be either life or death.|


例句：

The surgery was a life-or-death situation. = 这场手术是一场生死攸关的战斗。

He faced a life-or-death struggle in the wilderness. = 他在荒野中面临着生死攸关的搏斗。


近义词：critical, crucial, desperate, urgent, perilous

反义词：trivial, unimportant, inconsequential


词性：形容词
