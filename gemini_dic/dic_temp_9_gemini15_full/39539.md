cons:/kɒnz/| n.|缺点，弊端|Disadvantages or drawbacks; the opposite of "pros".|例句：We need to weigh the pros and cons before making a decision. 我们需要权衡利弊后再做决定。|近义词：drawbacks, disadvantages, defects|反义词：pros, advantages


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cons of this plan are numerous. = 这个计划的缺点很多。
What are the cons of this product? = 这个产品的缺点是什么？

一般过去时:
The cons of the old system were clear. = 旧制度的缺点很明显。
What were the cons of his proposal? = 他的提议有什么缺点？

现在完成时:
We have considered the cons carefully. = 我们已经仔细考虑了缺点。
Have you weighed the pros and cons? = 你权衡过利弊了吗？

过去完成时:
They had already discussed the cons before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就已经讨论过缺点。
I had overlooked some of the cons. = 我忽略了一些缺点。

将来时:
We will discuss the cons in the next meeting. = 我们将在下一次会议上讨论缺点。
What cons will we face in the future? = 我们将来会面临哪些缺点？

虚拟语气:
If there were fewer cons, I would support the project. = 如果缺点少一些，我会支持这个项目。
I wish there weren't so many cons. = 我希望缺点不要这么多。

被动语态:
The cons were clearly outlined in the report. = 报告中清楚地列出了缺点。
The cons are being addressed by the team. = 团队正在解决这些缺点。

疑问句:
What are the main cons? = 主要缺点是什么？
Are there any significant cons to this approach? = 这种方法有什么明显的缺点吗？

否定句:
There are no significant cons to this method. = 这种方法没有明显的缺点。
He didn't mention any cons during his presentation. = 他在演示中没有提到任何缺点。


PHRASE->
pros and cons = 利弊
consider the cons = 考虑缺点
weigh the pros and cons = 权衡利弊
list the cons = 列出缺点
address the cons = 解决缺点
