phlegmon:/ˈflɛɡmən/| n.|蜂窝织炎，急性弥漫性炎症|A diffuse, spreading inflammation of connective tissue, often caused by bacterial infection.  It is characterized by swelling, redness, pain, and often fever.|Example: The untreated wound led to a severe phlegmon in his leg.  未经处理的伤口导致他腿部严重的蜂窝织炎。|近义词：cellulitis,  diffuse inflammation|反义词：localized infection
