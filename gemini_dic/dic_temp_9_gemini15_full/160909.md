post-historic:/ˌpoʊst hɪˈstɔːrɪk/| adj.|后历史的，后历史时期的|Referring to a period or condition following the conventional understanding of history, often characterized by a questioning of traditional historical narratives and the dominance of metanarratives.|例句：Post-historic art often incorporates elements of popular culture and challenges traditional notions of artistic value. 后历史艺术通常融合流行文化元素，并挑战传统的艺术价值观念。  例句：Some scholars argue that we are currently living in a post-historic era, where grand narratives have lost their explanatory power. 一些学者认为我们目前正生活在一个后历史时期，宏大叙事已经失去了其解释力。|近义词：postmodern, late-modern|反义词：historical, traditional
