juicier:/ˈdʒuːsiər/| adj. | 多汁的；更有趣的；更吸引人的 | More full of juice; more interesting or exciting.  The adjective form of juicy, indicating a greater degree of juiciness or interest. |例句：The oranges were juicier than I expected.  这些橙子比我想象的更甜美多汁。

The new plot is juicier than the previous one. 新的情节比之前的更吸引人。|近义词：succulent, richer, more flavorful, more exciting |反义词：dry, bland, dull, uninteresting |词性：形容词
