inviolately: /ɪnˈvaɪələtli/ | adv. | 不容侵犯地；神圣不可侵犯地 |  Absolutely; without violation or infringement.  This adverb emphasizes the absolute and unbreakable nature of something, suggesting that it is sacred, protected, and must not be violated. |

例句：

The treaty was inviolately observed by both sides.  = 双方都严格遵守了条约。

The sanctity of the temple was inviolately maintained for centuries. = 寺庙的神圣性几个世纪以来一直保持不变。


近义词：sacredly, inviolate, religiously, strictly, immutably


反义词：violently, profanely, irreverently, carelessly


词性：副词
