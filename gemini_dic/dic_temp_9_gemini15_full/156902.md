pilage:/ˈpaɪlɪdʒ/| n.|掠夺；抢劫；洗劫|The act of plundering or looting, especially in wartime.  It involves the taking of goods or property by force or violence.|例句：The village was subjected to brutal pilage by the invading army.  这个村庄遭到了入侵军队残酷的洗劫。|例句：The pirates engaged in pilage, stealing everything of value from the ships they captured. 海盗们进行抢劫，偷走了他们俘获的船只上所有值钱的东西。|近义词：plunder, looting, robbery, sack, depredation|反义词：donation, contribution, restitution


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The army pillages villages. = 军队洗劫村庄。
He pillages information from his competitors. = 他从竞争对手那里窃取信息。

一般过去时:
The Vikings pillaged the coastal towns. = 维京人洗劫了沿海城镇。
The thieves pillaged the museum. = 窃贼洗劫了博物馆。

现在完成时:
The invaders have pillaged the entire region. = 入侵者已经洗劫了整个地区。
They have pillaged the country of its resources. = 他们掠夺了这个国家的资源。

过去完成时:
The city had been pillaged before the army arrived. = 在军队到达之前，这座城市已经被洗劫一空了。
The bandits had pillaged the caravans several times. = 匪徒们已经多次洗劫了商队。

将来时:
The rebels will pillage the capital city. = 反叛者将洗劫首都。
If they win, they will pillage the town. = 如果他们赢了，他们就会洗劫这座城镇。

虚拟语气:
If they had not pillaged the land, the people would have survived. = 如果他们没有洗劫这片土地，人们就能活下来。
I wish they hadn't pillaged the museum. = 我真希望他们没有洗劫博物馆。

被动语态:

The museum was pillaged last night. = 博物馆昨晚被洗劫了。

疑问句:
Was the village pillaged? = 村庄被洗劫了吗？

否定句:

They did not pillage the houses. = 他们没有洗劫房屋。
