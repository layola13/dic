nonconservation: /ˌnɒnkɒnsəˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 非守恒；不守恒性 | The lack of conservation; the violation of a conservation law.  It refers to a situation where a physical quantity is not conserved, meaning its value changes over time.  This is often seen in certain physical processes or systems. | Example: The nonconservation of parity in weak interactions was a groundbreaking discovery in physics.  非弱相互作用中的宇称不守恒是物理学中的一项突破性发现。 | 近义词：violation, breach, infringement | 反义词：conservation
