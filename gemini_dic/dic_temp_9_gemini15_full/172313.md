putative:/ˈpjuːtətɪv/| adj.|推定的；假定的；可能的|Supposed; believed or assumed to be true, but not definitely known.  It suggests a degree of uncertainty.|例句：The putative father of the child refused to take a DNA test.  推定的孩子的父亲拒绝进行DNA测试。

例句：The putative cause of the accident is still under investigation. 事故的推定原因仍在调查中。


近义词：presumed, alleged, supposed, suspected|反义词：proven, confirmed, established|词性：形容词
