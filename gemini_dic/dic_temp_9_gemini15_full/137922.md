USAGE->

The landscape features are primarily nonvolcanic in nature. = 这片景观的主要特征是非火山性的。

Negative Sentence:
The island is not nonvolcanic; it's a volcanic island. = 这个岛屿并非非火山性的；它是一个火山岛。
That type of rock isn't nonvolcanic, it's clearly igneous. = 那类岩石并非非火山性的，很明显是火成岩。


Question Sentence:
Is the geological activity in this region nonvolcanic? = 这个地区的地质活动是非火山性的吗？
Are these landforms of nonvolcanic origin? = 这些地貌是非火山成因的吗？
