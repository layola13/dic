recessively:/rɪˈsɛsɪvli/| adv. |隐性地，退行性地 |In genetics, recessively describes a trait that is only expressed when two copies of the recessive gene are present.| 例句：The gene for blue eyes is recessive; a person needs two copies of the gene to have blue eyes. 蓝眼睛的基因是隐性的；一个人需要两个基因拷贝才能拥有蓝眼睛。  The disease is inherited recessively.  这种疾病是隐性遗传的。|近义词：passively, indirectly |反义词：dominantly
