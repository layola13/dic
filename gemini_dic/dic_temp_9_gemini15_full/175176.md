raggie:/ˈræɡi/| n.|破布，碎布；蓬乱的头发|A raggie is a piece of old cloth, typically torn or ragged.|例句：He wiped his hands on a raggie. 他用一块破布擦了擦手。|例句：Her hair was a mass of raggies. 她的头发乱糟糟的，像一堆破布。|近义词：rag,tatter,shred|反义词：neat,tidy
