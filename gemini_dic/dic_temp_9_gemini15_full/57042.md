doubly:/ˈdʌbli/ | adv. | 双倍地；两倍地；加倍地 | Twice as much or as many; to a twofold extent.  | 例句：The company's profits were doubly impressive given the economic downturn.  考虑到经济低迷，公司的利润翻倍令人印象深刻。

例句：The task was doubly difficult because of the time constraints.  由于时间限制，这项任务加倍困难。


近义词：twice, twofold, doubly so


反义词：singly, once


词性：副词
