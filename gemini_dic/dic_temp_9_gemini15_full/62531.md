endures:/ɪnˈdʊərz/| v.| 忍受；持续；持久 | To continue to exist in the same state for a long period of time; to remain alive or in existence despite difficult conditions. |例句：She endures a lot of pain. 她忍受了很多痛苦。

例句：The company has endured many economic downturns. 这家公司经受住了许多经济衰退的考验。


近义词：sustain, tolerate, persevere, last, survive | 反义词：cease, perish, end, expire


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He endures the harsh weather. = 他忍受着恶劣的天气。
The tradition endures to this day. =  这个传统延续至今。


一般过去时:
She endured the long journey patiently. = 她耐心地忍受了漫长的旅程。
The castle endured for centuries. = 这座城堡屹立了几个世纪。


现在完成时:
We have endured many challenges. = 我们经受了许多挑战。
The building has endured many storms. = 这座建筑经受住了许多风暴。


过去完成时:
They had endured years of hardship before they finally succeeded. = 在他们最终成功之前，他们已经忍受了多年的苦难。
The empire had endured for over a thousand years before it finally collapsed. = 这个帝国存在了一千多年后才最终崩溃。


将来时:
We will endure whatever comes our way. = 无论发生什么，我们都会坚持下去。
The monument will endure for generations to come. = 这座纪念碑将流传后世。


虚拟语气:
If only I could endure this pain! = 但愿我能忍受这痛苦！
If he had endured a little longer, he would have succeeded. = 如果他能坚持久一点，他就会成功。


被动语态:
The pain was endured with stoicism. =  痛苦被坚忍地忍受着。
The hardships were endured by the pioneers. = 这些艰难困苦是由拓荒者们忍受的。


疑问句:
How long will this suffering endure? =  这种痛苦还要持续多久？
Can this relationship endure? = 这段关系能够维持下去吗？


否定句:
He couldn't endure the pressure any longer. = 他再也无法忍受压力了。
The old house didn't endure the earthquake. = 老房子没有经受住地震的考验。


PHRASE->
endure hardship = 忍受苦难

endure pressure = 忍受压力
endure temptation = 忍受诱惑

词源:
源自古法语 endurer， 来自拉丁语  endurare，由 en- (加强语气) + durare (持续)构成。
