overcensoring: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsərɪŋ/ | n. |过度审查；审查过度|The act of censoring excessively; removing or suppressing more content than is necessary or appropriate.  This can lead to the suppression of legitimate speech and information.  |例句：The government's overcensoring of the media resulted in a lack of public trust. 政府对媒体的过度审查导致公众缺乏信任。


The film suffered from overcensoring, losing much of its artistic merit. 这部电影因为过度审查而失去了很多艺术价值。|近义词：excessive censorship, suppression, overregulation |反义词：undercensorship, freedom of speech |词性:名词
