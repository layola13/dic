classics:/ˈklæsɪks/| n.|经典作品；古典文学；经典著作|Works of art, literature, music, etc., that are considered to be of the highest quality and to have lasting value.  They are often characterized by their enduring appeal and influence on subsequent generations.|例句：She enjoys reading classics such as Shakespeare and Jane Austen. 她喜欢阅读莎士比亚和简·奥斯汀等经典作品。|例句：The museum has a fine collection of classical sculptures. 这个博物馆收藏了许多精美的古典雕塑。|近义词：masterpieces, standards, canon|反义词：trash, junk,  modern works
