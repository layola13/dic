cureless:/ˈkjʊərləs/| adj.|无法治愈的，无可救药的|incapable of being cured; incurable.  The disease is cureless and will eventually lead to death.|近义词：incurable, irremediable |反义词：curable, remediable

USAGE->
一般现在时:
This disease is cureless. = 这种疾病无法治愈。
The situation seems cureless. = 情况似乎无可救药。

一般过去时:
The doctor said the injury was cureless. = 医生说这伤无法治愈。
They believed the problem was cureless at the time. = 当时他们认为这个问题无法解决。


现在完成时:
Unfortunately, the damage has proved cureless. = 不幸的是，损害已被证明是无法修复的。
The patient’s condition has remained cureless despite treatment. = 尽管接受了治疗，病人的病情仍然无法治愈。


过去完成时:
Before the new treatment, the illness had been considered cureless. = 在新的治疗方法出现之前，这种疾病被认为是无法治愈的。
They realized the damage had become cureless. = 他们意识到损坏已经无法修复了。


将来时:
Without a miracle, the illness will remain cureless. = 没有奇迹，这种疾病将仍然无法治愈。
Experts predict this problem will be cureless for years to come. = 专家预测这个问题在未来几年都无法解决。


虚拟语气:
If only there were a cure, it wouldn't be cureless. = 如果有治疗方法就好了，它就不会是无法治愈的了。
I wish this situation weren't cureless. = 我希望这种情况不是无可救药的。


被动语态:
The condition was declared cureless by the medical team. = 医疗团队宣布这种情况无法治愈。
The problem is considered cureless by many experts. = 许多专家认为这个问题是无法解决的。


疑问句:

Is this illness truly cureless? = 这种疾病真的无法治愈吗？


否定句:
The disease is not necessarily cureless.  = 这病未必无法治愈。
The situation is not entirely cureless; there's still hope. = 情况并非完全无可救药；仍然有希望。
