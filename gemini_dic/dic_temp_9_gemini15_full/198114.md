shooty:/ˈʃuːti/| adj.|爱射击的；射击很多的；像射击一样的|The word "shooty" is an informal adjective, often used to describe something related to shooting, especially firearms.  It can refer to a person who enjoys shooting, a place with lots of shooting activity, or something that resembles the action of shooting.  It lacks a formal definition and is often used in a playful or slightly exaggerated way.|例句：The game was pretty shooty, with lots of gunfire. 这游戏有很多枪战，很刺激。  He's a real shooty guy; he loves going to the shooting range. 他是个真正的射击迷；他喜欢去射击场。|近义词：gun-toting, trigger-happy (for people), action-packed (for events)|反义词：peaceful, calm
