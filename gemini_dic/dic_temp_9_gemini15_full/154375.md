permanent:/ˈpɜːrmənənt/| adj.|永久的，永恒的，不变的 | lasting or intended to last or remain unchanged indefinitely.|The museum has a permanent collection of ancient artifacts. 这个博物馆永久收藏着古代文物。| lasting, enduring, perpetual | temporary, transient, ephemeral | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
This job is permanent. = 这份工作是永久性的。
His love for his wife is permanent. = 他对妻子的爱是永久的。

一般过去时:
The stain was permanent. = 污渍是永久性的。
The decision was permanent and irreversible. = 决定是永久的且不可逆转的。

现在完成时:
The damage has proved permanent. = 损害已被证明是永久性的。
They have made a permanent commitment to the project. = 他们已经对这个项目做出了永久性的承诺。

过去完成时:
The effects of the accident had become permanent. = 事故的影响已经变得永久性了。
Before the surgery, they knew the damage might be permanent. = 手术前，他们知道损伤可能是永久性的。

将来时:
This will be a permanent solution to the problem. = 这将是解决这个问题的永久性方案。
The changes they will make will be permanent. = 他们将要做的改变将是永久性的。

虚拟语气:
If this were a permanent solution, we would be relieved. = 如果这是一个永久性的解决方案，我们会很欣慰。
I wish this feeling of happiness were permanent. = 我希望这种幸福的感觉是永久的。

被动语态:
The contract was made permanent. = 合同被永久化了。
The artwork is permanently displayed in the gallery. = 这件艺术品永久陈列在画廊里。

疑问句:
Is this a permanent position? = 这是个永久性的职位吗？
Is the damage permanent? = 损坏是永久性的吗？

否定句:
The job is not permanent; it's temporary. = 这份工作不是永久性的；它是临时的。
The effects of the medication are not permanent. = 药物的效果不是永久性的。
