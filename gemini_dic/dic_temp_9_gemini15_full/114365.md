majoring:/ˈmeɪdʒərɪŋ/| v. |主修，专攻|The act of specializing in a particular subject or field of study at a college or university.  It implies ongoing study, not just a completed degree.|例句：She is majoring in biology. = 她主修生物学。

She's majoring in computer science and hopes to get a job at Google. = 她主修计算机科学，希望能在谷歌找到工作。|近义词：specializing, concentrating on|反义词：minoring in


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am majoring in English literature. = 我主修英国文学。
He is majoring in mechanical engineering. = 他主修机械工程。

一般过去时:
I was majoring in history before I switched to economics. = 在我转到经济学之前，我主修历史。
She was majoring in physics, but found it too difficult. = 她主修物理，但发现太难了。

现在完成时:
I have been majoring in mathematics for three years. = 我主修数学已经三年了。
She has been majoring in art since she started college. = 自从她上大学以来，她就一直主修艺术。

过去完成时:
I had been majoring in chemistry for two years before I realized it wasn't for me. = 在我意识到那不适合我之前，我已经主修化学两年了。
Before transferring to another university, she had been majoring in psychology. = 在转学到另一所大学之前，她主修心理学。

将来时:
I will be majoring in computer science next semester. = 下个学期我将主修计算机科学。
He will be majoring in business administration next year. = 他明年将主修工商管理。

虚拟语气:
If I were majoring in music, I would be much happier. = 如果我主修音乐，我会快乐得多。
I wish I were majoring in something more practical. = 我希望我主修的东西更实用一些。

被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice, as it describes an active process.)


疑问句:
What are you majoring in? = 你主修什么？
What is he majoring in at university? = 他在大学里主修什么？

否定句:
I am not majoring in physics; I'm majoring in chemistry. = 我没有主修物理；我主修化学。
He is not majoring in law; he's majoring in medicine. = 他没有主修法律；他主修医学。


PHRASE->
major in = 主修
major field of study = 主要研究领域
declared major = 已声明专业
change of major = 转专业
double major = 双专业
