platyrhine:/ˈplætɪraɪn/| adj. n.|阔鼻猴的；阔鼻类动物|Having a broad, flat nose, characteristic of New World monkeys.  The term is used to describe both a type of primate and a characteristic of their nasal structure.|Example:  Many platyrhine monkeys are arboreal. 许多阔鼻猴是树栖的。|近义词：New World monkey (as noun)|反义词：catarrhine
