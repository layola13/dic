freebie:/ˈfriːbiː/| n. |免费赠品，免费的东西 |Something given free, especially as a promotional item.  A freebie is something you get for free, often as a bonus or promotional item.  |例句：The store gave away freebies to attract customers. 商店赠送免费赠品以吸引顾客。

例句：I got a freebie at the trade show – a cool pen! 我在贸易展上得到了一份免费赠品——一支很酷的笔！


近义词：giveaway, free gift, bonus, perk |反义词：purchase, paid item |词性:noun
