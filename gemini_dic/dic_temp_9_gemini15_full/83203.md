guyss:/ɡaɪz/| n. |伙计们，家伙们 |Guys is an informal plural of "guy," used to address a group of people, typically males, but often used inclusively.  It's a casual and friendly term. |Hey guys, let's go grab some pizza! 嘿，伙计们，我们去吃比萨吧！| fellas, dudes, mates | ladies, women (反义词较为宽泛，没有完全对应的词)|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The guys are playing basketball. = 这些家伙们正在打篮球。
Those guys are having a party. = 那些家伙们正在开派对。

一般过去时:
The guys played football yesterday. = 这些家伙们昨天踢足球了。
The guys went to the movies last night. = 这些家伙们昨晚去看电影了。

现在完成时:
The guys have finished their work. = 这些家伙们已经完成了他们的工作。
The guys have been to the beach. = 这些家伙们去过海滩。

过去完成时:
The guys had already left when I arrived. = 我到达的时候，这些家伙们已经离开了。
The guys had finished eating before the movie started. = 电影开始前，这些家伙们已经吃完了。

将来时:
The guys will go to the concert tomorrow. = 这些家伙们明天将去参加音乐会。
The guys will be playing video games later. = 这些家伙们稍后将玩电子游戏。

虚拟语气:
If the guys were here, they would help us. = 如果这些家伙们在这里，他们会帮助我们。
I wish the guys were less noisy. = 我希望这些家伙们不要那么吵。

被动语态:
The guys were invited to the party. = 这些家伙们被邀请参加派对。
The work was done by the guys. = 这项工作是由这些家伙们完成的。

疑问句:
Are the guys coming tonight? = 这些家伙们今晚会来吗？
Where are the guys? = 这些家伙们在哪里？

否定句:
The guys aren't ready yet. = 这些家伙们还没准备好。
The guys didn't go to school today. = 这些家伙们今天没去上学。
