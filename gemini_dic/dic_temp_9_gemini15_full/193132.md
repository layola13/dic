secur:/sɪˈkjʊr/ | adj., v. |安全的；牢固的；确定的；获得；使安全 |  To make safe; to obtain or acquire something;  to fasten or attach securely. |例句：The door is securely locked. 门被牢固地锁上了。  |近义词：safe,  certain, obtain, acquire |反义词：unsafe, insecure, uncertain, lose


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We secure the building every night. = 我们每晚都保护这座建筑的安全。
They secure funding for their project. = 他们为他们的项目获得了资金。


一般过去时:
He secured the package with rope. = 他用绳子捆绑了包裹。
She secured a job at the bank. = 她获得了一份银行的工作。


现在完成时:
They have secured the perimeter. = 他们已经确保了周边安全。
We have secured enough donations for the charity. = 我们已经为慈善机构筹集了足够的捐款。


过去完成时:
He had secured the contract before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经签订了合同。
They had secured their position in the market. = 他们已经巩固了他们在市场中的地位。


将来时:
We will secure the area before the event. = 我们将在活动前确保该区域的安全。
She will secure a loan to buy a house. = 她将获得贷款买房。


虚拟语气:
If we secured more funding, we could expand the business. = 如果我们能获得更多资金，我们就能扩大业务。
I wish I had secured a better deal. = 我真希望我当时能达成更好的交易。


被动语态:
The building was secured against intruders. = 这座建筑物防范了入侵者。
The loan was secured by the property. = 这笔贷款以房产作为抵押。


疑问句:
Have you secured the necessary permits? = 你已经获得必要的许可证了吗？
Is the package securely fastened? = 包裹绑牢了吗？


否定句:
They did not secure the necessary votes. = 他们没有获得必要的选票。
He couldn't secure a meeting with the CEO. = 他无法与首席执行官会面。



PHRASE->
secure a job = 找到一份工作

secure a victory = 获得胜利
secure the perimeter = 保卫周边地区
