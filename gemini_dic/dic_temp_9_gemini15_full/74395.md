frameworks:/ˈfreɪmˌwɜːrks/| n. |框架；结构；构架 |A framework is a basic structure underlying a system, concept, or text.  It provides support or guidance for building something else.  It can refer to a physical structure, a conceptual model, or a set of rules or principles. |例句：The new software uses a modular framework, making it easy to update.  这个新软件使用了模块化框架，使其易于更新。

例句：The house's wooden framework was visible after the fire.  火灾过后，房子的木质框架清晰可见。


近义词：structure, system, skeleton, foundation, architecture |反义词：chaos, disorder, randomness |词性:名词
