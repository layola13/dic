prestruggled: /ˌpriːˈstrʌɡld/ | adj. | 预先挣扎的，预先奋斗的 |  Describes a situation or person that has already undergone struggle or difficulty before a particular point in time.  This is not a standard dictionary word; it's a less common word formed by combining "pre-" (before) and "struggled" (past participle of struggle).  It implies a prior period of hardship. | Example:  The prestruggled artist finally achieved recognition.  这个预先经历过奋斗的艺术家最终获得了认可。 | 近义词:  previously challenged, beforehand strained, pre-stressed | 反义词:  untroubled, effortless, unchallenged |  词性: adjective
