nonnutrient: /ˌnɒnˈnjuːtrɪənt/ | adj. |非营养的|  Something that does not provide nutritional value.  It lacks essential nutrients needed for growth, maintenance, and repair of body tissues. | Example:  Dietary fiber is considered a nonnutrient because it's not digested and absorbed by the body.  膳食纤维被认为是非营养物质，因为它不被身体消化和吸收。| 近义词：innutritious, non-essential | 反义词：nutritious, nutrient-rich |  词性：形容词
