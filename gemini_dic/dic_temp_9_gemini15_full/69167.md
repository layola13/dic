fax:/fæks/ | n./v.|传真；发送传真|A fax is a method of transmitting documents over telephone lines.  A fax machine is used to send and receive faxes.|例句：I sent a fax to the office. 我给办公室发了一份传真。|例句：Please fax me the document as soon as possible. 请尽快把文件传真给我。|近义词：telefax, transmit|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I fax documents to my clients regularly. = 我定期向我的客户传真文件。
He faxes the report every Friday. = 他每个星期五都传真报告。

一般过去时:
She faxed the contract yesterday. = 她昨天传真了合同。
They faxed the information to the head office. = 他们把信息传真给了总部。

现在完成时:
I have faxed the application form. = 我已经传真了申请表。
We have faxed all the necessary documents. = 我们已经传真了所有必要的文档。

过去完成时:
He had faxed the report before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他已经传真了报告。
She had faxed the documents before leaving the office. = 离开办公室前她已经传真了文件。

将来时:
I will fax you the details tomorrow. = 我明天会把细节传真给你。
They will fax the order confirmation later today. = 他们今天晚些时候会传真订单确认书。

虚拟语气:
If I had a fax machine, I would fax the document. = 如果我有传真机，我会传真文件。
I wish I had faxed the report earlier. = 我真希望我早点传真了报告。

被动语态:
The documents were faxed to the wrong address. = 文件传真到了错误的地址。
The report is being faxed right now. = 报告正在被传真。

疑问句:
Did you fax the document? = 你传真文件了吗？
Has he faxed the order yet? = 他已经传真订单了吗？

否定句:
I didn't fax the report. = 我没有传真报告。
They haven't faxed the documents yet. = 他们还没有传真文件。


PHRASE->
