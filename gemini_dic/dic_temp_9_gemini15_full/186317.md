rhomboides:/ˈrɒmbaɪdz/| n. |菱形肌|A rhomboid is a muscle in the back that helps to stabilize the shoulder blades and allows for movement of the scapula.  It is named for its rhomboid shape.|The rhomboid muscles help to retract and rotate the scapula.|近义词：None specifically, but muscles with similar function could be considered.|反义词：None specifically.
