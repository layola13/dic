occupies:/ˈɒkjupaɪz/ /ˈɑːkjupaɪz/| vt.|占据，占用，占领，使忙碌|To take or fill a space, area, or time; to keep someone busy.|例句：The table occupies most of the room. 这张桌子占据了房间的大部分空间。

例句：Her work occupies most of her time. 她的工作占据了她大部分的时间。


近义词：take up, fill, engage, employ|反义词：vacate, leave, free


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The box occupies a lot of space. = 这个盒子占据了很多空间。
This task occupies my whole afternoon. = 这个任务占据了我整个下午。


一般过去时:
The army occupied the city. = 军队占领了这座城市。
The meeting occupied three hours. = 会议持续了三个小时。


现在完成时:
The project has occupied us for months. = 这个项目已经让我们忙了几个月了。
She has occupied herself with painting lately. = 她最近一直忙于绘画。


过去完成时:
They had occupied the building before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们已经占领了那栋建筑。
The issue had occupied his thoughts all day. = 这个问题困扰了他一整天。


将来时:
The conference will occupy the whole week. = 会议将持续整整一周。
We will occupy ourselves with gardening this weekend. = 我们这个周末将忙于园艺。


虚拟语气:
If the project occupied less time, we could finish earlier. = 如果这个项目占用时间少一些，我们就能早点完成。
I wish this task didn't occupy so much of my time. = 我希望这项任务不会占用我这么多时间。


被动语态:
The room was occupied by a large table. = 房间被一张大桌子占据了。
Most of his time is occupied by work. = 他大部分时间都被工作占据了。


疑问句:
Does this box occupy too much space? = 这个盒子占据的空间是否太多了？
What occupies your time these days? = 你这些天都在忙些什么？


否定句:
The room doesn't occupy much space. = 这个房间不占多少空间。
The job doesn't occupy all my time. = 这份工作并没有占据我所有的时间。


PHRASE->
occupy oneself with = 忙于…
occupy a position = 担任职位
occupy a place = 占据一个位置
occupy one's mind = 占据某人的心思
occupy the territory = 占领领土

词源:
occupy源自拉丁语occupare，由oc（加强意义）和capere（抓住，占据）构成，字面意思为“抓住，占据”。
