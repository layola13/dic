reperusal:/ˌriːˈpɛruːzəl/| n. |再读；复查|A second or repeated reading or examination of something.|例句：After the initial reading, a careful reperusal revealed several inconsistencies.  首次阅读后，仔细复查发现了一些不一致之处。|近义词：review, rereading, re-examination |反义词：first reading, cursory glance

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He does a reperusal of the contract every year. = 他每年都会仔细审查合同。
She often does a reperusal of her notes before exams. = 她考试前经常复习笔记。

The lawyer did a reperusal of the documents before going to court. = 律师在去法院之前仔细审查了文件。

现在完成时:
I have done a reperusal of the report and found a few errors. = 我已经复查了报告，发现了一些错误。
