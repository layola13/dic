lariat:/ˈlærɪət/| n.|套索，套马索|A lariat is a rope, typically made of strong fiber or leather, used for catching and restraining livestock, particularly cattle and horses. It’s usually thrown in a loop, with the end held by the person using it.  The loop is then tightened around the animal's neck or legs.|例句：The cowboy skillfully threw the lariat, ensnaring the runaway calf.  牛仔熟练地甩出套索，套住了逃跑的小牛。|The rancher used his lariat to secure the horse.  牧场主用他的套索套住了马。|近义词：lasso, rope|反义词:None
