camshafts:/ˈkæmʃɑːfts/ | n. |凸轮轴 |A rotating shaft with lobes that open and close the valves in an internal combustion engine.  It's a crucial component in controlling the engine's intake and exhaust cycles. |The worn camshafts were the cause of the engine's poor performance. 磨损的凸轮轴是发动机性能不佳的原因。 The mechanic replaced the camshafts to improve the engine's efficiency. 机械师更换了凸轮轴以提高发动机的效率。|crankshafts |(no direct antonym, but could be described as the opposite of a system *without* camshafts)|名词
