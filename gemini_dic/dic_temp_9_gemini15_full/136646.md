nonscientific: /ˌnɑːnˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪk/ | adj. | 非科学的；不科学的 | Not based on or conforming to the principles of science; lacking scientific basis or method.  | 例句：His explanation of the phenomenon was entirely nonscientific.  他的现象解释完全不科学。

例句：The theory, while interesting, lacks a nonscientific foundation. 虽然这个理论很有趣，但它缺乏科学基础。


近义词：unscientific, empirical, unprofessional, amateurish

反义词：scientific, methodical, rigorous, professional
