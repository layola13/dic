/ˌnɒnˌrezəˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 非预约；无预约 |  The absence of a reservation or booking; something not reserved or booked in advance.  It often implies a lack of planning or pre-arrangement. | Example:  The restaurant accepted walk-ins on a nonreservation basis. 这家餐厅接受非预约的散客。


Example:  We attempted a nonreservation dinner, but the restaurant was fully booked. 我们尝试了非预约的晚餐，但餐厅已经客满了。


近义词: walk-in, unreserved, spontaneous


反义词: reservation, booking, pre-arrangement


词性: noun
