labourers:/ˈleɪbərərz/| n. |劳动者，工人|People who do manual work, especially unskilled work.  They are often employed in construction, agriculture, or manufacturing. |例句：The labourers worked tirelessly under the hot sun.  那些劳动者在烈日下辛勤工作。

例句：Many labourers lost their jobs due to the factory closure.  由于工厂倒闭，许多工人失业了。


近义词：workers, manual workers, toilers, hands

反义词：employers, bosses, managers

词性:名词
