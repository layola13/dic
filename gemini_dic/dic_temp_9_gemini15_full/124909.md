mudflap: /ˈmʌdflæp/ | n. |挡泥板，挡泥瓣 | A flap of rubber or other material attached to the wheel arch of a vehicle to prevent mud and spray from being thrown up.  | Example: The truck's mudflaps were tattered and torn from years of driving on rough roads.  卡车的挡泥板由于多年的颠簸路面行驶而破烂不堪。|近义词：fender (in some contexts), splash guard |反义词：无
