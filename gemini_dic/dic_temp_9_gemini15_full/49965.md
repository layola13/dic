denier:/ˈdiːnɪər/|/ˈdɛniər/| n. |旦尼尔（重量单位）；否认者；怀疑论者 |A denier is a unit of weight, typically used for fabrics. It also refers to someone who denies something, especially the existence of climate change.  |Example:  The fabric has a denier of 30. 这块布料的旦尼尔是30。 He's a climate change denier. 他是一个气候变化怀疑论者。|近义词：disbeliever, doubter, skeptic |反义词：believer, supporter, advocate|名词
