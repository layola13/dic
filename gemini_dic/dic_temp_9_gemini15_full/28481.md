carceral:/ˈkɑːrsərəl/| adj.|监狱的；监禁的；与监禁相关的| Relating to a prison, jail, or other place of confinement; characterized by or suggestive of imprisonment.  The term often implies a broader system of control and surveillance beyond physical incarceration.|例句：The carceral state relies heavily on surveillance technologies. 监狱国家严重依赖监控技术。

例句：His writings explore the carceral aspects of modern society. 他的著作探讨了现代社会的监狱方面。


近义词：prison-like, penitentiary, incarcerative, custodial

反义词：free, liberated, open, unconfined


词性:形容词
