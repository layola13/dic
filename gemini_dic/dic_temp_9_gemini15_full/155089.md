petroglyph:/ˈpɛtrəɡlɪf/| n. |岩刻，石刻|A petroglyph is a rock carving, a form of rock art.  Petroglyphs are created by removing part of a rock surface by incising, picking, carving, or abrading.  They can depict a wide range of subjects, including animals, humans, and abstract symbols.|The ancient petroglyphs in the canyon are remarkably well-preserved.  古代峡谷中的岩刻保存得非常好。|rock carving, rock art| (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with pictograph (painted rock art))|名词
