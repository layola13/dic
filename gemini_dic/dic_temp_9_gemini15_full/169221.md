prologue:/ˈproʊlɒɡ/ /ˈproʊloɡ/| n. |序言，开场白 |An introductory section of a literary work, especially a poem or play, or a speech introducing a performance or event.  A prologue serves to set the scene, introduce characters, or provide background information.  |例句：The prologue hinted at the tragic events to come.  序言暗示了即将发生的悲剧事件。

The play opened with a lively prologue spoken by the chorus.  这出戏以合唱团朗诵的充满活力的序言开场。|近义词：preface, introduction, preamble, foreword |反义词：epilogue
