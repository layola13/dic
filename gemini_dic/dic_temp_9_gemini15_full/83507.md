hacksaw:/ˈhæksɔː/| n.|钢锯，弓锯|A hacksaw is a hand saw with a fine-toothed blade used for cutting metal.  It typically has a C-shaped frame and a replaceable blade.|例句：I used a hacksaw to cut the metal pipe. 我用钢锯切割金属管。

例句：He needed a hacksaw to remove the rusted bolt. 他需要一把钢锯来去除生锈的螺栓。
