cropped:/krɒpt/|ˈkrɑːpt/| adj. v.| 收获的；被剪短的；被裁剪的 |  Cut or shortened; having been cropped.  |例句：The photo was cropped to remove the distracting background.  这张照片经过裁剪，去掉了分散注意力的背景。
The farmer cropped the wheat last week.  农民上周收割了小麦。|近义词：trimmed, shortened, cut, harvested|反义词：extended, lengthened, uncut, unharvested|形容词，动词
