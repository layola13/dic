cognoscente:/ˌkɒnəˈʃɛntiː/| /ˌkɑːnəˈʃɛnti/ | n. |鉴赏家；行家；内行 |A person who has expert knowledge in a particular area, especially in the fine arts, food, or wine.  | 例句：He's a true cognoscente of Italian cinema. 他是意大利电影真正的鉴赏家。

例句：She is considered a cognoscente in the field of 18th-century French furniture. 她被认为是18世纪法国家具领域的专家。


近义词：expert, connoisseur, aficionado, authority


反义词：amateur, novice, beginner


词性:名词


词源:意大利语cognoscere (to know) 的过去分词cognosciuto (known) 的名词形式。
