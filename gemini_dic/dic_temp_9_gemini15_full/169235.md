prolongation:/ˌprɒlɒŋˈɡeɪʃən/| n.|延长，延期，拖延|The act of extending something in time or space.  It refers to the process of making something last longer. |例句：The prolongation of the war caused widespread suffering.  战争的延长造成了广泛的苦难。

例句：The prolongation of the meeting was unexpected. 会议的延长是出乎意料的。


近义词：extension, continuation, lengthening, deferment

反义词：shortening, abbreviation, curtailment

词性：名词
