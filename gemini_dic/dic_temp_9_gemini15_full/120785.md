misappropriating:/ˌmɪsˌæproʊpriˌeɪtɪŋ/| v.|挪用，盗用|The act of using something in a way that is not correct or legal, especially money or property that is not yours.|例句：He was accused of misappropriating company funds. 他被指控挪用公司资金。

例句：The government is investigating allegations of misappropriating public money. 政府正在调查挪用公款的指控。


近义词：embezzling, stealing, diverting, defrauding

反义词：donating, contributing, giving
