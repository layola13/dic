puerile:/ˈpjʊərəl/| adj.|幼稚的，孩子气的，不成熟的|Childish; immature; silly.  It suggests a lack of maturity or good judgment, often in a way that is annoying or embarrassing. |例句：His puerile jokes annoyed everyone at the meeting.  他的幼稚笑话惹恼了会议上的所有人。

例句：Her puerile behavior was inappropriate for a woman of her age. 她幼稚的行为对她这个年纪的女人来说是不合适的。


近义词：childish, immature, infantile, juvenile, silly, childish, jejune

反义词：mature, adult, sophisticated, sensible

词性:形容词


词源:源自于拉丁语 *puer（男孩）,  最终演变为现代英语的 puerile.  它反映了这种形容词所指的缺乏成熟和判断力的状态，如同一个孩子一样。
