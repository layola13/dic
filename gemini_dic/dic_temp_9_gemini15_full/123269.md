monodramatist: /ˌmɒnəˈdræməˌtɪst/ | n. | 独角戏演员；独幕剧作家 | A person who writes or performs monodramas.  A monodramatist is a playwright and/or performer who specializes in monodramas,  a one-person play.  |  例句：Sarah Bernhardt was a famous monodramatist of her time.  莎拉·伯恩哈特是她那个时代著名的独角戏演员。


The renowned monodramatist captivated the audience with her powerful performance.  这位著名的独角戏演员以她精彩的表演征服了观众。| 近义词：solo performer, one-person show performer, playwright (if writing only) | 反义词：ensemble cast member, chorus member
