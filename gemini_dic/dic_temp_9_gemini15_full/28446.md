carbuncles:/ˈkɑːrˌbʌŋklz/ | n. |痈，炭疽病 | A carbuncle is a cluster of boils (furuncles) that are interconnected and form a larger, more serious infection.  It's characterized by deep inflammation, swelling, and often significant pain.  Carbuncles usually occur on the back of the neck, face, or thighs. | Example: The doctor confirmed that the painful lump on his neck was a carbuncle and prescribed antibiotics.  医生确认他脖子上的疼痛肿块是痈，并开了抗生素。The patient presented with a large, painful carbuncle on his back requiring surgical drainage. 病人背部长了一个又大又痛的痈，需要手术引流。 |近义词：boil, abscess, furuncle |反义词：None (Antonyms are not readily applicable to medical conditions) |
