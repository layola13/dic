silicate:/ˈsɪlɪkeɪt/| n.|硅酸盐|A silicate is a chemical compound that contains silicon and oxygen, and usually one or more metals. Silicates are the most abundant minerals in the Earth's crust.  They are found in various forms, including rocks, sand, and clay.|例句：Feldspar is a common silicate mineral found in many rocks. 长石是一种常见的硅酸盐矿物，存在于许多岩石中。

The main component of glass is a silicate. 玻璃的主要成分是硅酸盐。|近义词：mineral (in some contexts)|反义词：无|名词
