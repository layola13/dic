patties:/ˈpætiz/| n.|肉饼；小馅饼|Flat, often round cakes of minced meat, fish, vegetables, or other mixtures, typically fried or grilled.  They are often served as a main course or as part of a burger.|例句：She made delicious beef patties for dinner. 她做了美味的牛肉饼当晚餐。

例句：The restaurant is famous for its crab patties. 这家餐馆以蟹肉饼而闻名。


近义词：burgers, cakes, cutlets|反义词：无|词性：名词
