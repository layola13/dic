monsieur: /mənˈsjøːr/ | n. |先生，阁下 | French term of address for a man, equivalent to "Mr." or "Sir".  It is a polite and formal way to address a man, particularly in a formal setting. | Example:  Bonjour, monsieur. =  你好，先生。  Monsieur Dupont, may I help you? = 杜邦先生，我能帮您什么忙吗？ | 近义词：Mr., sir, gentleman | 反义词：madame (for women)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The monsieur is waiting. = 先生在等候。
That monsieur speaks French fluently. = 那位先生说法语很流利。


一般过去时:
The monsieur left early. = 先生早些时候离开了。
That monsieur helped me yesterday. = 那位先生昨天帮助了我。


现在完成时:
The monsieur has arrived. = 先生已经到了。
The monsieur has finished his work. = 先生已经完成了他的工作。


过去完成时:
The monsieur had already left when I arrived. = 我到的时候先生已经离开了。
The monsieur had spoken before anyone else. = 先生在其他人之前已经发言了。


将来时:
The monsieur will come tomorrow. = 先生明天会来。
The monsieur will be here soon. = 先生很快就会到。


虚拟语气:
If the monsieur were here, he would help us. = 如果先生在这里，他会帮助我们。
I wish the monsieur were more helpful. = 我希望先生更有帮助。


被动语态:
The monsieur was respected by everyone. = 先生受 everyone 尊敬。
The monsieur is being waited for. = 大家正在等候先生。


疑问句:
Is the monsieur ready? = 先生准备好了吗？
Where is the monsieur going? = 先生要去哪里？


否定句:
The monsieur is not here. = 先生不在。
The monsieur did not attend the meeting. = 先生没有参加会议。


PHRASE->
Monsieur le Président:  先生总统 (Addressing the President)
Monsieur l'Ambassadeur: 先生大使 (Addressing the Ambassador)
Cher monsieur:  亲爱的先生 (Informal, friendly address)
Monsieur et Madame: 先生和夫人
Bonne journée, monsieur:  先生，祝您一天愉快 (A polite farewell)
