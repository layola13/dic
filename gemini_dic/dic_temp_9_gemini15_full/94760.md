improviser:/ɪmˈprɑːvʌɪzər/| n. |即兴表演者，临时应变者 | A person who improvises, especially a musician or actor.  Someone who creates or performs something spontaneously or without preparation. | Example: The jazz musician was a brilliant improviser, captivating the audience with his spontaneous melodies.  这位爵士乐音乐家是一位杰出的即兴演奏者，他即兴创作的旋律深深吸引了观众。|近义词：ad-libber, extemporizer |反义词：planner, pre-arranger |名词
