gonochorism:/ɡoʊˈnɒkərɪzəm/| n. |雌雄异体；性分化|The state of having separate sexes;  dioecy. Gonochorism is a reproductive strategy in which individuals are either male or female, producing only one type of gamete. This contrasts with hermaphroditism, where individuals possess both male and female reproductive organs.  |Example:  Many species of animals exhibit gonochorism, including mammals, birds, and most insects.|近义词：dioecy, unisexuality |反义词：hermaphroditism, monoecy
