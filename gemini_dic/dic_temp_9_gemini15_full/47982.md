decimalising: /ˈdesɪməlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 转换为十进制；实行十进制 | The act of converting something to the decimal system, typically currency or weights and measures.  It involves replacing non-decimal units with decimal ones. | 例句：The government is decimalising the currency.  政府正在实行货币十进制。| 例句：Decimalising the weights and measures system simplified calculations. 将度量衡系统转换为十进制简化了计算。| 近义词：decimalizing (American spelling) | 反义词：None directly, but the opposite would be converting from decimal to a non-decimal system.
