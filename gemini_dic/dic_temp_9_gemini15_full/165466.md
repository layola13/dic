prelimit:/ˈpriːlɪmɪt/| v.|预先限制，预先限定 |To set a limit beforehand; to restrict or confine in advance.  |例句：The rules prelimit the number of participants to 100.  规则预先限制了参与人数为100人。 |例句：The budget prelimits spending on non-essential items. 预算预先限制了非必需品的支出。|近义词：restrict, limit, constrain, confine|反义词：expand, enlarge, broaden
