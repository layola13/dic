poisoned:/ˈpɔɪzənd/| adj.|中毒的；受毒害的|Having been affected by poison; contaminated with poison.|例句：The water was poisoned. 水被污染了。

例句：The poisoned apple caused Snow White's death.  毒苹果导致白雪公主的死亡。


近义词：contaminated, tainted, infected, envenomed|反义词：purified, cleansed, detoxified|词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The food is poisoned. = 食物中毒了。
He is poisoned by jealousy. = 他被嫉妒蒙蔽了。


一般过去时:
The well was poisoned last week. = 上周井水被毒害了。
The soldiers poisoned the enemy's water supply. = 士兵们毒害了敌人的水源。


现在完成时:
The river has been poisoned by industrial waste. = 河水已被工业废料污染。
Many people have been poisoned by the contaminated food. = 很多人因为吃了受污染的食物而中毒。


过去完成时:
The villagers had been poisoned before help arrived. = 在救援到来之前，村民们已经中毒了。
They had poisoned the food before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经毒害了食物。


将来时:
The lake will be poisoned if we don't act now. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，湖泊将会被污染。
They will poison the crops to destroy the harvest. = 他们会毒害庄稼来毁掉收成。


虚拟语气:
If the water had not been poisoned, many people would not have died. = 如果水没有被污染，许多人就不会死了。
I wish I hadn't eaten that poisoned mushroom. = 我真希望我没有吃那个毒蘑菇。


被动语态:
The crops were poisoned by the farmer's neighbor. = 庄稼被农民的邻居毒害了。
The river is being poisoned by the nearby factory. = 河水正在被附近的工厂污染。


疑问句:
Was the food poisoned? = 食物中毒了吗？
Is the water poisoned? = 水被污染了吗？


否定句:
The water wasn't poisoned. = 水没有被污染。
The food wasn't poisoned, it just tasted bad. = 食物没有中毒，只是味道不好。
