seaboard:/ˈsiːbɔːrd/ | n. |海岸；海滨 |The land bordering the sea; a coastal region.  A seaboard is the land adjacent to the sea. It often features characteristic landscapes and ecosystems shaped by the proximity of the ocean, including beaches, cliffs, estuaries, and coastal plains.  |例句：The California seaboard is known for its beautiful beaches. 加利福尼亚海滨以其美丽的海滩而闻名。

The eastern seaboard of the United States is densely populated. 美国东海岸人口稠密。|近义词：coast, coastline, shore, waterfront |反义词：inland
