nonseparabilities: /ˌnɒnˌsepərəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |不可分离性，不可分割性 |The state or quality of being inseparable or not capable of being separated.  It often refers to concepts or phenomena that are intrinsically linked and cannot be meaningfully considered apart.  For example, in quantum physics, certain properties of particles exhibit nonseparability, meaning their states are interconnected regardless of the distance separating them. |  The nonseparabilities of quantum entanglement are a major focus of research.  量子纠缠的不可分离性是研究的重点。 The study highlighted the nonseparabilities between socioeconomic status and health outcomes. 这项研究强调了社会经济地位与健康结果之间的不可分离性。|近义词：interdependence, inseparability, interconnectedness |反义词：separability, independence
