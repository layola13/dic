fourierism:/ˈfʊriəˌrɪzəm/| n. |傅里叶主义|A utopian socialist system advocating the abolition of private property and the establishment of self-governing communities based on shared work and resources.|The Fourierist movement aimed to create phalanxes, self-sufficient communities where work was shared and resources were distributed according to need.|近义词：utopian socialism, communalism|反义词：capitalism, individualism|名词
