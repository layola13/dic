lessening:/ˈlɛsənɪŋ/| v. |减少；减轻；变小|The act of making something less; the state of becoming less.  It implies a gradual decrease or reduction.|例句：The lessening of tensions between the two countries is a positive development.  两国间紧张局势的缓和是一个积极的发展。

例句：The doctor prescribed medication to lessen the pain. 医生开了药来减轻疼痛。


近义词：decreasing, diminishing, reducing, mitigating|反义词：increasing, augmenting, enhancing, intensifying|动词
