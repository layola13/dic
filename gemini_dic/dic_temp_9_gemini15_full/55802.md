divertise:/daɪˈvɜːtɪs/|/dɪˈvɜːtaɪz/| vt.|消遣；使转向；转移（注意力）|to entertain or amuse someone; to distract someone from something; to cause something to change course|The clown's antics diverted the children. 小丑的滑稽表演逗乐了孩子们。
The river was diverted to irrigate the fields. 河水被改道用于灌溉田地。|amuse, entertain, distract|bore, focus|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He diverts himself with reading. = 他以阅读来消遣自己。
She diverts her attention from work. = 她转移了对工作的注意力。

一般过去时:
The game diverted the children for hours. = 游戏让孩子们玩了好几个小时。
The police diverted traffic away from the accident. = 警察将交通从事故现场转移开。

现在完成时:
They have diverted the river's course. = 他们已经改变了河道的走向。
She has diverted her energy into writing. = 她已将精力投入写作。

过去完成时:
He had diverted the funds for his own use. = 他已将资金挪作私用。
They had diverted the conversation away from the sensitive topic. = 他们已将谈话从敏感话题上转移开。

将来时:
We will divert the stream to create a pond. = 我们将改道溪流来创建一个池塘。
They will divert the flight to another airport. = 他们将把航班改到另一个机场。

虚拟语气:
If he diverted his attention, he would have finished the work. = 如果他转移注意力，他本可以完成工作的。
I wish she had diverted her anger toward a more constructive outlet. = 我希望她能将怒火发泄到更有建设性的渠道上。

被动语态:
The traffic was diverted due to roadworks. = 由于道路施工，交通被改道了。
His attention was diverted by a loud noise. = 他被一声巨响分散了注意力。

疑问句:
How can we divert the water from the flooded area? = 我们如何才能将水从洪水泛滥区转移出去？
What can we do to divert the children's attention? = 我们能做些什么来转移孩子的注意力？

否定句:
He did not divert his gaze from the painting. = 他没有把目光从画上移开。
The government did not divert funds to the project. = 政府没有将资金拨给该项目。
