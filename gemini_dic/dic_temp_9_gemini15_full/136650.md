nonscoring: /ˈnɑːnˌskɔːrɪŋ/ | adj. | 不计分的，无成绩的 | Not contributing to or affecting a score or total.  It typically refers to actions or events in a game or competition that don't add to or subtract from the final score. | 例句：The penalty was nonscoring; it didn't change the game's outcome.  这球罚不计分；它没有改变比赛的结果。


The referee called a nonscoring foul. 裁判判罚了一个不计分的犯规。


近义词：unofficial, inconsequential, irrelevant (in the context of scoring) | 反义词：scoring, point-scoring
