polyrhizous:/ˌpɒliˈraɪzəs/| adj. |多根的，多根状的 | Having or producing many roots.  The term describes plants that develop multiple roots from different points, often from stems or nodes, rather than a single taproot.  This characteristic allows for better stability and nutrient absorption. | Example:  Many grasses are polyrhizous, enabling them to spread rapidly and thrive in various conditions.  许多草本植物是多根的，这使得它们能够迅速蔓延并在各种条件下茁壮成长。|近义词：multirooted, many-rooted |反义词：monorhizous
