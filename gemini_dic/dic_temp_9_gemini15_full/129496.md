non-Arab: /ˌnɒnˈærəb/ | adj. | 非阿拉伯人的；非阿拉伯的 |  Describes something or someone that is not of Arab origin or ethnicity.  It is used to denote people, cultures, languages, or things not associated with the Arab world. |

例句：
The conference included speakers from both Arab and non-Arab countries. = 会议包括来自阿拉伯国家和非阿拉伯国家的演讲者。

The museum showcases both Arab and non-Arab art. = 博物馆展出了阿拉伯和非阿拉伯艺术。


近义词：non-Arabic, foreign (in certain contexts)


反义词：Arab, Arabic


词性: adjective
