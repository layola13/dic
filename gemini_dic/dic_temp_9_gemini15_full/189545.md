saleable: /ˈseɪləbl/ | adj. | 可销售的，畅销的 | Able to be sold; in demand.  |例句：The new product proved highly saleable.  新产品证明非常畅销。  |例句：This antique is very saleable. 这件古董很畅销。|近义词：marketable, sellable, vendible |反义词：unsaleable, unmarketable

The product is saleable in many countries. = 该产品在许多国家都畅销。
These goods are saleable throughout the year. = 这些商品全年都畅销。
