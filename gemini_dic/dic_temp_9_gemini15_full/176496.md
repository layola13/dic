re-presented:/ˌriːˈprɛzɛntɪd/| v. |重新提出；再次呈现；重新介绍 |To present (something) again; to introduce or show something again.  It implies a previous presentation. |例句：The evidence was re-presented to the jury.  证据再次提交给了陪审团。

例句：The professor re-presented the data in a more accessible format. 教授以更容易理解的方式重新呈现了数据。

近义词：reintroduced,  resubmitted,  re-examined

反义词：withdrew,  concealed,  suppressed

词性：动词
