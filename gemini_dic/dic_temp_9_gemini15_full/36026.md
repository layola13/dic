codirecting: /ˌkoʊdaɪˈrɛktɪŋ/ | v. | 共同导演；协同指导 |  The act of directing something jointly with another person or people. It implies shared responsibility and collaboration in the creative process. | 例句：They are codirecting a new film about the American Revolution. 他们正在共同导演一部关于美国革命的新电影。

They are codirecting a documentary exploring the impact of social media. 他们正在共同导演一部探索社交媒体影响的纪录片。


近义词：collaboratively directing, jointly directing | 反义词：solely directing, independently directing | 词性：动词
