overneutralize: /ˌoʊvərˈnuːtrəlaɪz/ | v. |过度中和；过度抵消| To neutralize something to such an extent that it goes beyond the desired point; to excessively counteract or balance something.  The process goes beyond simple neutralization, resulting in an opposite effect.| The base was overneutralized, resulting in an acidic solution.  碱被过度中和，导致溶液呈酸性。|近义词：overcompensate, overdo, counteract excessively |反义词：underneutralize
