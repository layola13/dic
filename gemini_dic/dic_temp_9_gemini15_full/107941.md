laryngoscope:/ˈlærɪŋɡəskoʊp/| n. |喉镜|A laryngoscope is a medical instrument used to examine the larynx (voice box) and other structures of the upper airway.  It typically consists of a handle, a light source, and a blade that is inserted into the mouth to lift the tongue and epiglottis, providing a clear view of the larynx. |The doctor used a laryngoscope to examine the patient's throat. 医生用喉镜检查了病人的喉咙。|endoscope, bronchoscope|  |n.
