devisen:/dɪˈvaɪz/| n.|设计；策略；方法；手段|a plan or invention; a clever scheme or trick; a means of achieving something.|例句：The company devised a new marketing strategy. 公司制定了一个新的营销策略。 |例句：His ingenious device solved the problem. 他巧妙的装置解决了这个问题。|近义词：plan, scheme, method, technique|反义词：chaos, disorder


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They devise new ways to improve efficiency. = 他们设计新的方法来提高效率。
We devise a plan to escape. = 我们制定了一个逃跑计划。

一般过去时:
The engineers devised a solution to the problem. = 工程师们设计出了一个解决问题的方案。
She devised a clever plan to get her way. = 她想出了一个巧妙的计划来达到自己的目的。

现在完成时:
They have devised a new system for tracking packages. = 他们已经设计了一个新的包裹追踪系统。
Scientists have devised a new vaccine. = 科学家们已经研制出一种新的疫苗。

过去完成时:
They had devised a plan before the meeting. = 他们在会议前就制定好了计划。
She had devised a strategy to win the argument. = 她已经制定了一个策略来赢得这场争论。

将来时:
We will devise a new marketing campaign next year. = 我们明年将设计一个新的营销活动。
They will devise a solution to the climate crisis. = 他们将想出一个解决气候危机的方案。

虚拟语气:
If they devised a better system, the process would be much faster. = 如果他们设计一个更好的系统，这个过程将会快得多。
I wish I had devised a better plan. = 我真希望我制定了一个更好的计划。

被动语态:
A new strategy was devised to increase sales. = 制定了一个新的策略来增加销售额。
A new method of teaching was devised by the professor. = 教授设计了一种新的教学方法。

疑问句:
What methods have they devised to solve the problem? = 他们设计了什么方法来解决这个问题？
What strategies did he devise to win the election? = 他设计了什么策略来赢得选举？

否定句:
They did not devise a suitable plan. = 他们没有制定一个合适的计划。
We haven't devised a solution to the issue yet. = 我们还没有想出解决这个问题的方法。
