caretook:/ˈkeərtʊk/| v.| 临时代理；照管；看管 |To temporarily take care of or manage something or someone.  |例句：He caretook the business while the owner was on vacation. 他在老板休假期间临时代理公司业务。|例句：She caretook her neighbor's cat while they were away.  她照料了她邻居的猫，直到他们回来。|近义词：managed, supervised, looked after |反义词：abandoned, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He caretakes the property for the absent owner. = 他为缺席的业主照管房产。
She caretakes her elderly mother. = 她照顾她年迈的母亲。

一般过去时:
He caretook the shop during the manager's illness. = 在经理生病期间，他负责照看这家商店。
They caretook the children while their parents were at work. = 他们的父母上班时，他们照顾孩子们。


现在完成时:
She has caretaken several properties this year. = 今年她已经照管了几处房产。
He has caretaken his neighbor's dog many times. = 他多次照顾过邻居的狗。


过去完成时:
They had caretaken the animals for weeks before the owner returned. = 在主人回来之前，他们已经照料这些动物好几个星期了。
She had caretaken the business successfully before handing it back. = 在交还公司之前，她成功地负责了公司的运营。


将来时:

She will caretake her friend's plants while they are on holiday. = 她的朋友度假期间，她将照顾朋友的植物。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would caretake your dog. = 如果我有时间，我会照顾你的狗。
I wish I could caretake the animals more often. = 我希望我能更经常地照顾这些动物。


被动语态:

否定句:
