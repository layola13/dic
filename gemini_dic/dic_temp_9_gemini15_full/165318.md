preinvest:/ˌpriːɪnˈvɛst/| v.|预先投资，提前投资 |To invest before a project begins or before a need arises.  It often involves making an investment in anticipation of future growth or opportunity. |例句：The company decided to preinvest in new technology to gain a competitive edge. 公司决定预先投资新技术以获得竞争优势。
例句：They preinvested in the infrastructure before starting construction. 他们在开始建设之前预先投资了基础设施。|近义词：invest in advance,  anticipatory investment |反义词：post-invest, delayed investment |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They preinvest in promising startups. = 他们预先投资有前景的初创企业。
The company preinvests in research and development. = 公司预先投资研发。


一般过去时:
We preinvested in the stock market before the boom. = 我们在繁荣之前预先投资了股市。
The government preinvested in disaster relief funds. = 政府预先投资了救灾资金。


现在完成时:
They have preinvested significantly in renewable energy. = 他们已经大量预先投资于可再生能源。
She has preinvested in several real estate projects. = 她已经预先投资了几个房地产项目。


过去完成时:
They had preinvested in the project before the funding was secured. = 在资金到位之前，他们已经预先投资了该项目。
The investors had preinvested in the company's IPO. = 投资者在公司IPO之前就已经预先投资了。


将来时:
We will preinvest in a new factory next year. = 我们明年将预先投资一个新工厂。
The firm will preinvest in employee training programs. = 公司将预先投资员工培训项目。


虚拟语气:
If we had preinvested, we would have avoided losses. = 如果我们预先投资了，我们就不会亏损。
I wish we had preinvested in that technology. = 我希望我们当时预先投资了那项技术。


被动语态:
Significant funds were preinvested in infrastructure development. = 大量资金预先投资于基础设施建设。
The project was preinvested in by several angel investors. = 该项目得到了几位天使投资人的预先投资。


疑问句:
Did they preinvest in the project? = 他们预先投资了这个项目吗？
Will the company preinvest in this new venture? = 公司会预先投资这个新项目吗？


否定句:
They did not preinvest in the failing business. = 他们没有预先投资那家失败的企业。
We will not preinvest without a detailed feasibility study. = 没有详细的可行性研究，我们不会预先投资。
