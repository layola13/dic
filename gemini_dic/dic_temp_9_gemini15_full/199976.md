skate:/skeɪt/| n. v.|滑冰；滑板；冰鞋；滑板鞋|A skate is a type of footwear with blades attached to the bottom, used for gliding on ice.  It can also refer to the act of gliding on ice or on a skateboard. |例句：She loves to go ice skating in winter. 她喜欢冬天去滑冰。
He showed off his skateboarding skills at the park. 他在公园里展示了他的滑板技巧。|近义词：glide, roll, slide|反义词：walk, run


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I skate every weekend. = 我每个周末都滑冰。
He skates with amazing grace. = 他滑冰姿态优美。

一般过去时:
We skated on the frozen lake. = 我们在冰冻的湖面上滑冰。
She skated beautifully in the competition. = 她在那场比赛中滑冰很漂亮。

现在完成时:
I have skated since I was a child. = 我从孩提时代就开始滑冰了。
They have skated many times this season. = 这个赛季他们已经滑冰很多次了。

过去完成时:
He had skated before, so he was confident. = 他以前滑过冰，所以他很有信心。
We had skated for hours before we got tired. = 我们滑了几个小时才感到疲倦。

将来时:
I will skate tomorrow if the ice is good. = 如果冰面良好，我明天会去滑冰。
They will skate in the upcoming competition. = 他们将在即将到来的比赛中滑冰。

虚拟语气:
If I could skate, I would join the team. = 如果我会滑冰，我就会加入球队。
I wish I could skate as well as you. = 我希望我能像你一样滑冰好。

被动语态:
The ice rink was skated on by many people. = 许多人都在这个溜冰场滑冰。
The competition will be skated in next month. = 比赛将在下个月举行。

疑问句:
Do you skate? = 你滑冰吗？
Can he skate well? = 他滑冰好吗？

否定句:
I don't skate. = 我不滑冰。
They didn't skate yesterday. = 他们昨天没有滑冰。


PHRASE->
ice skate = 冰鞋
roller skate = 旱冰鞋
skateboard = 滑板
go skating = 去滑冰
figure skating = 花样滑冰

词源:
The word "skate" comes from the Old Norse word "skata," meaning "a skate (fish)."  The connection is likely due to the shape of the fish and the early designs of ice skates.  The verb sense developed later, from the noun.
