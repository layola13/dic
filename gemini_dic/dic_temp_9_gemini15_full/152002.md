patavinity:/ˌpætəˈvɪnɪti/| n. |帕塔维尼式拉丁语；乡下口音；土话；粗俗的言辞|Provincial or rustic speech; an utterance or style of speaking regarded as coarse, vulgar, or unrefined. It specifically refers to a style of Latin spoken in Patavium (modern Padua), Italy, considered less refined than the classical form.|The actor's patavinity was evident in his pronunciation of Latin.  这位演员在拉丁语发音中明显的带有乡下口音。| 近义词：provincialism, rusticity, vulgarity | 反义词：elegance, refinement, sophistication
