data-base:/ˈdeɪtəbeɪs/| n. |数据库|A structured set of data organized and accessed electronically from a computer system.  A database is typically stored in a computer system and is designed to be easily searched and retrieved.  Databases can be small or extremely large, containing gigabytes or even terabytes of information.|例句：Our company uses a data-base to store customer information. = 我们公司使用数据库来存储客户信息。

例句：The research team built a comprehensive data-base on climate change. = 研究团队建立了一个关于气候变化的综合数据库。


近义词：data store, repository, archive

反义词：无


词性：名词
