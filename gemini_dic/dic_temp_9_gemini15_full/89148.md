homeland:/ˈhoʊmlænd/| n. |祖国，故乡 |The country or region of one's birth or descent; the place one considers home.  It evokes feelings of belonging, loyalty, and often nostalgia. |例句：After many years abroad, he returned to his homeland.  多年在国外生活后，他回到了祖国。

例句：She longed to return to the rolling hills of her homeland. 她渴望回到她家乡连绵起伏的山丘。


近义词：native land, motherland, fatherland |反义词：foreign land, exile


词性：名词
