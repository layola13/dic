postvelar: /ˌpoʊstˈvɛlər/ | adj. | 后齿龈的 | Relating to or denoting sounds produced with the back of the tongue near the soft palate (velum).  These sounds are typically consonants. |  The pronunciation of the English "k" sound is postvelar. | 近义词: velar (less specific) | 反义词: prevelar, alveolar, bilabial
