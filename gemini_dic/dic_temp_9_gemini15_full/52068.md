dialogued:/ˈdaɪəlɒɡd/ | v. | 对话；交谈 | To engage in conversation or dialogue.  |  They dialogued for hours about the project. 他们就项目进行了数小时的对话。 | conversed, discussed, talked |  monologued, remained silent | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They dialogue regularly about their work. = 他们定期就工作进行对话。
We dialogue with our customers daily. = 我们每天都与顾客进行对话。


一般过去时:
The two leaders dialogued extensively before reaching an agreement. = 两位领导在达成协议前进行了广泛的对话。
They dialogued for hours, but couldn't resolve the conflict. = 他们交谈了数小时，但未能解决冲突。


现在完成时:
The team has dialogued effectively throughout the project. = 团队在整个项目中都进行了有效的对话。
They have dialogued with stakeholders to gather feedback. = 他们已经与利益相关者进行了对话以收集反馈。


过去完成时:
They had dialogued for weeks before the final decision was made. = 在做出最终决定之前，他们已经讨论了几周。
After they had dialogued, a compromise was reached. = 在他们对话之后，达成了一项妥协。


将来时:
We will dialogue with the investors next week. = 我们下周将与投资者进行对话。
The committee will dialogue about the proposed changes. = 委员会将就拟议的更改进行对话。


虚拟语气:
If they had dialogued more openly, the problem might have been avoided. = 如果他们更公开地对话，这个问题本来是可以避免的。
I wish we had dialogued more before making the decision. = 我希望我们在做决定之前能多交流一些。


被动语态:
The issue was dialogued extensively in the meeting. = 会议上对这个问题进行了广泛的讨论。
The proposal will be dialogued upon at the next board meeting. = 该提案将在下一次董事会会议上讨论。


疑问句:
Did they dialogue about the budget? = 他们讨论过预算吗？
Have you dialogued with your supervisor about your concerns? = 你是否与你的主管讨论过你的担忧？


否定句:
They did not dialogue about the sensitive topic. = 他们没有讨论这个敏感话题。
We haven't dialogued enough about the next steps. = 我们对接下来的步骤讨论得还不够。
