de-icing:/ˌdiːˈaɪsɪŋ/| v.|除冰|The process of removing ice from a surface, typically an aircraft, road, or other structure.  It often involves the application of chemicals or mechanical methods to melt or break up the ice.|例句：The airport workers were busy with de-icing the planes before departure. 机场工作人员在飞机起飞前忙着除冰。
The highway department used de-icing salt to keep the roads clear.  高速公路部门使用融雪盐来保持道路畅通。|近义词：ice removal, defrosting|反义词：icing
