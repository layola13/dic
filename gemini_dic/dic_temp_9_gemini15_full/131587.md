noncompetency: /ˌnɒnkɒmpɪtənsi/ | /ˌnɑːnkɑːmpɪtənsi/ | n. | 无能力；不胜任；不合格 | Lack of ability or skill; incompetence.  It often refers to a legal or professional context, indicating a lack of qualification or fitness for a particular task or role. |

例句：

The lawyer's noncompetency was evident in his poor handling of the case. = 律师的不称职在其糟糕的案件处理中显而易见。

His noncompetency to manage the project led to its failure. = 他管理项目的不称职导致了项目的失败。


近义词：incompetence, inadequacy, incapability, unfitness, disability

反义词：competence, capability, proficiency, expertise, skill


词性：名词
