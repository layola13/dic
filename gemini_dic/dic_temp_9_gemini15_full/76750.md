gamekeeper:/ˈɡeɪmˌkiːpər/| n. |守猎人；狩猎场看守人|A gamekeeper is a person employed to protect game birds and animals on a large estate, often with the responsibility of managing the hunting of them.  They are responsible for the preservation and control of the game population, preventing poaching, and ensuring the smooth running of hunting activities.|例句：The gamekeeper was proud of his well-managed pheasant population.  守猎人对他的雉鸡种群管理得很好而感到自豪。|例句：The poachers evaded the watchful eye of the gamekeeper. 盗猎者躲过了守猎人的密切注视。|近义词：game warden, huntsman, keeper|反义词：poacher
