cleaves:/kliːvz/| v.|劈开；粘附；坚持；|To separate or divide something, especially along a natural line of weakness.  Also, to adhere or cling to something.|例句：The rock cleaved in two after the explosion. 爆炸后岩石裂成了两半。The two families have cleaved together through thick and thin.  这两个家庭风雨同舟。|近义词：split, divide, adhere, cling|反义词：unite, separate (in the sense of detaching), detach
