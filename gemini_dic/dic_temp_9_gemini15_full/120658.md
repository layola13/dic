mis-writes:/mɪsˈraɪts/| v.|写错，误写|To write something incorrectly.  It implies an error in spelling, grammar, or factual information.|例句：He mis-writes his own name frequently. 他经常把自己的名字写错。|例句：The student mis-wrote the answer, losing points on the exam.  学生写错了答案，考试丢了分。|近义词：misspell, err, make a mistake in writing|反义词：write correctly, spell correctly
