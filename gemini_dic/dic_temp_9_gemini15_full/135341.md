nonpenetrating: /ˌnɒnˈpɛnɪtreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非穿透性的，不穿透的 | Not penetrating; not passing through or into something.  This adjective describes something that does not pierce, enter, or affect something else deeply. |

例句：

The radiation was nonpenetrating, meaning it did not reach deep into the body. = 辐射是非穿透性的，这意味着它没有深入体内。

The nonpenetrating wound required only superficial treatment. =  这个非穿透性伤口只需要表面处理。


近义词：superficial, shallow, external, surface

反义词：penetrating, deep, pervasive


词性: 形容词
