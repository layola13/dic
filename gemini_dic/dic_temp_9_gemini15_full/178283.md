reconceiving:/ˌriːkənˈsiːvɪŋ/| v.|重新构想，重新设想 |To think about or plan something again, often in a new or different way.  It involves a fundamental shift in perspective or approach, going beyond a simple revision. |例句：The company is reconceiving its marketing strategy to appeal to a younger audience. 公司正在重新构想其营销策略以吸引更年轻的受众。

例句：The architect is reconceiving the building's design to maximize natural light. 建筑师正在重新设计建筑物，以最大限度地利用自然光。


近义词：reimagine, rethink, re-envision, reformulate

反义词：maintain, preserve, perpetuate (in the context of keeping an existing concept unchanged)

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reconceive the project every year. = 他们每年都会重新构想这个项目。
We reconceive our business model regularly. = 我们定期重新构想我们的商业模式。

一般过去时:
The artist reconceived his masterpiece after years of reflection. = 在多年的反思之后，这位艺术家重新构想了他的杰作。
She reconceived her approach to the problem after failing the first time. = 她第一次尝试失败后重新考虑了解决问题的方法。

现在完成时:
They have reconceived the entire curriculum. = 他们已经重新构想整个课程了。
We have reconceived our marketing strategy multiple times. = 我们已经多次重新构想我们的营销策略了。

过去完成时:
He had reconceived his life goals after the accident. = 事故发生后，他已经重新构想了他的人生目标。
They had reconceived the plan before presenting it to the board. = 他们在向董事会提交计划之前已经重新构想过了。

将来时:
They will reconceive the product line next year. = 他们明年将重新构想产品线。
We will reconceive the entire building design. = 我们将重新构想整个建筑设计。

虚拟语气:
If we reconceived our approach, we might succeed. = 如果我们重新构想我们的方法，我们可能会成功。
I wish I had reconceived my strategy earlier. = 我希望我早点重新构想我的策略。

被动语态:
The project was reconceived to meet new standards. = 该项目经过重新构想以满足新的标准。
The marketing plan is being reconceived by the team. = 营销计划正在由团队重新构想。

疑问句:

Will the company reconceive its product line? = 公司会重新构想其产品线吗？

否定句:
They did not reconceive their plan, despite the setbacks. = 尽管遭遇挫折，他们并没有重新构想他们的计划。
The government has not reconceived its economic policy. = 政府并没有重新构想其经济政策。
