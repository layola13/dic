hooky's:/ˈhʊkiːz/| n.|逃学，旷课|The plural form of "hooky," referring to instances of playing hooky or instances of absence due to playing hooky.|例句：The teacher suspected several hooky's during the field trip.  老师怀疑在实地考察期间有几次逃学的情况。|近义词：absences, truancies|反义词：attendance, presence|名词
