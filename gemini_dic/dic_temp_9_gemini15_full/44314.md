crop:/krɒp/|ˈkrɑːp/| n. v.|庄稼；收成；一批；剪短；种植|a plant or plant product that is grown by farmers, especially for food; to cut or remove something, especially hair or a plant's leaves or branches; to produce or yield something, especially a plant or plant product|The farmer's crop of wheat was excellent this year.  今年农民的小麦收成非常好。

The gardener cropped the roses to encourage new growth. 园丁修剪玫瑰以促进新枝生长。


近义词：harvest, yield, gather, cut, shear|反义词：sow (in the context of planting)|词性:n. v.
