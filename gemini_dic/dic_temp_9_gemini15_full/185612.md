revenges:/rɪˈvendʒɪz/| n. |复仇；报仇|The act of taking revenge or inflicting punishment in return for an injury or wrong.  It often implies a deliberate and planned action to inflict harm.|例句：He sought revenge for the death of his brother. 他为了兄弟的死寻求报复。

例句：Her revenges were swift and brutal. 她的报复又快又残酷。

近义词：retribution, vengeance, reprisal|反义词：forgiveness, pardon|名词
