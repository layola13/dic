pachisi:/pəˈtʃiːsiː/| n. |帕奇西（一种印度棋类游戏）|Pachisi is an ancient Indian board game for two to four players, played on a cross-shaped board with 68 squares.  The game involves moving pieces around the board according to the roll of dice, aiming to be the first to reach the home square.|Example: We spent the afternoon playing a game of pachisi.|近义词：ludo (a similar game)|反义词：无
