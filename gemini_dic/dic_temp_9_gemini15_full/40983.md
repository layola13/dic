coordinateness: /kɔʊˈɔːrdɪnətɪs/ | n. | 协调性，并列性，坐标性 | The state or quality of being coordinate;  the property of being in a coordinate system.  It refers to the harmonious arrangement or functioning of parts. In mathematics, it specifically refers to the relationship between points defined by coordinates. | Example: The coordinateness of the dancers' movements created a visually stunning performance.  The coordinateness of the Cartesian plane allows for precise location of points. | 近义词：coordination, harmony, congruity | 反义词：disorder, chaos, disharmony
