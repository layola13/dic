depilatory:/dɪˈpɪlətəri/| n. adj.|脱毛剂；去毛的|A depilatory is a substance used to remove hair from the body.  It typically works by dissolving the hair at the surface of the skin.  Depilatories are available in various forms, such as creams, lotions, and powders.|例句：She used a depilatory cream to remove the unwanted hair on her legs. 她用脱毛膏去掉了腿上的多余毛发。
The depilatory solution caused a slight burning sensation.  脱毛溶液引起轻微的灼烧感。|近义词：hair remover, epilator|反义词：hair grower (no direct antonym exists, as the opposite is the absence of depilation)|名词，形容词
