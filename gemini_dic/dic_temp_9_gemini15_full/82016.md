grippe:/ɡrɪp/| n.| 流行性感冒；（尤指）流感 |The word "grippe" is a slightly older and less common term for influenza, a highly contagious viral infection that causes fever, muscle aches, and respiratory symptoms.  It's largely been replaced by "influenza" or simply "the flu" in modern usage.  |例句：The grippe swept through the village last winter.  去年冬天，流感席卷了整个村庄。|例句：She was laid low by a nasty grippe. 她被严重的流感击垮了。|近义词：influenza, flu |反义词：health, wellness |名词
