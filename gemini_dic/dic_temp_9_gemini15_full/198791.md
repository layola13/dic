sickouts:/ˈsɪkˌaʊts/| n. |集体请病假；员工集体装病不上班|A sickout is a coordinated absence of employees from their work, often in protest or to exert pressure on employers.  It differs from a strike in that employees claim illness as their reason for absence rather than openly declaring industrial action.  |例句：Employees staged a sickout to protest the company's new policies. 员工们集体请病假以抗议公司的新政策。

The recent teacher sickout resulted in school closures. 最近教师的集体请病假导致学校停课。


近义词：work stoppage, protest, boycott |反义词：attendance, work, presence| 名词
