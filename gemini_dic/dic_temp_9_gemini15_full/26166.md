butanes:/bjuːˈteɪnz/ | n. |丁烷（复数） |Butanes are a group of saturated hydrocarbons with the chemical formula C₄H₁₀. They are colorless, odorless gases at room temperature and are highly flammable.  There are two isomers of butane: n-butane and isobutane.  They are commonly used as fuels and refrigerants. |例句：Liquefied butanes are used as fuel in lighters. 液化丁烷用作打火机燃料。|例句：The chemical properties of butanes are similar but not identical. 丁烷的化学性质相似但不完全相同。|近义词：hydrocarbons, alkanes |反义词：无
