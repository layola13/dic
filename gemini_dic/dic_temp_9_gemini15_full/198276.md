shotguns:/ˈʃɒtɡʌnz/ | n. |猎枪；霰弹枪 | A shotgun is a firearm designed to fire multiple projectiles (shot) simultaneously.  It is typically used for hunting, sport shooting, and self-defense.  The wide spread of the shot makes it effective at close range. |例句：He cleaned his shotguns after a day of hunting. 他打猎一天后清洁了他的猎枪。

例句：The police officer carried a shotgun to protect himself. 警官携带猎枪以保护自己。


近义词：firearm, scattergun | 反义词：rifle (in terms of projectile dispersion)
