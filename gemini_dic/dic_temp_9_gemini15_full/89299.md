homoeopaths:/ˌhoʊmiˈɒpæθs/| n. |顺势疗法医师|Practitioners of homeopathy, a system of alternative medicine.  Homeopaths believe in treating like with like, using highly diluted substances to stimulate the body's healing response.  They often take a holistic approach, considering the patient's physical, emotional, and mental state.|例句：Many homeopaths use remedies derived from plants, minerals, or animals. 许多顺势疗法医师使用源自植物、矿物或动物的药物。

例句：The homeopaths prescribed a remedy to alleviate her allergy symptoms. 顺势疗法医师开了药方来减轻她的过敏症状。


近义词：homeopathic practitioners, alternative medicine practitioners|反义词：allopathic doctors (conventional medicine practitioners)
