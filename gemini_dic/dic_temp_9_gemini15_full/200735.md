sleevelike:/ˈsliːvlaɪk/ | adj. | 像袖子的；袖状的 | Resembling a sleeve in form or shape.  The term describes something that has the characteristic shape or appearance of a sleeve, often implying a tubular or cylindrical form that is typically closed at one end and open at the other. | Example: The vase had a sleevelike neck.  这个花瓶有一个像袖子一样的瓶颈。 The geologist described the rock formation as sleevelike. 地质学家将岩层描述为袖状的。 | 近义词：tubular, cylindrical, sheathlike | 反义词：broad, wide, flared
