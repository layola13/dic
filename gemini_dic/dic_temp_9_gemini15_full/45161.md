cue-someone-in:/kjuː ˈsʌmwʌn ˈɪn/| phrasal verb|给某人提示；向某人透露信息；使某人了解情况|To give someone information or a hint that helps them understand something or be prepared for something.  |例句：The director cued the actors in on the surprise ending.  导演向演员们透露了意外的结局。

例句：I tried to cue him in on the plan, but he still seemed confused. 我试图让他了解计划，但他似乎仍然感到困惑。


近义词：inform, brief, hint, tip off, enlighten

反义词：misinform, mislead, confuse, obfuscate

词性: phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cues me in on the latest gossip every morning. 他每天早上都会向我透露最新的八卦消息。
She cues her team in on the project's deadline. 她会给她的团队提示项目的截止日期。


一般过去时:
They cued her in on the secret meeting. 他们向她透露了秘密会议的消息。
He cued me in on the hidden meaning of the poem. 他向我解释了这首诗的隐含意义。


现在完成时:
I've cued him in on the problem, but he still hasn't found a solution. 我已经向他说明了这个问题，但他仍然没有找到解决办法。
She has cued us in on the changes to the schedule. 她已经告诉我们日程安排的更改。


过去完成时:
They had cued her in on the plot twist before the movie started. 在电影开始之前，他们已经向她透露了剧情的转折。
I had cued them in on the surprise party, so they were all ready. 我已经告诉他们惊喜派对的事了，所以他们都准备好了。


将来时:
We will cue them in on the plan before the presentation. 我们将在演示之前向他们说明计划。
She will cue him in on the details later today. 她今天晚些时候会向他详细说明。


虚拟语气:
If I had cued him in earlier, he wouldn't have made that mistake. 如果我早点告诉他，他就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I had cued her in on the potential risks. 我真希望我之前已经向她说明了潜在的风险。


被动语态:
The participants were cued in on the experiment's objective. 参加者们被告知了实验的目标。
He was cued in on the latest developments by his boss. 他被老板告知了最新的进展。


疑问句:
Have you cued him in on the changes? 你已经告诉他那些变化了吗？
Did they cue you in on the new strategy? 他们是否已经向你透露了新的策略？


否定句:
They didn't cue me in on the changes. 他们没有告诉我那些变化。
I didn't cue him in on the details. 我没有告诉他细节。
