darns:/dɑːrnz/| n. |粗线缝补；诅咒| A darn is a stitch used to repair a hole or tear in fabric.  It's also a mild curse word. |例句：She carefully darned the hole in her sock. 她仔细地缝补了她袜子上的洞。|近义词：mend, repair, stitch, curse|反义词：destroy, rip, tear|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tailor darns socks for a living. = 裁缝靠缝补袜子为生。
She often darns her husband's shirts. = 她经常缝补她丈夫的衬衫。


一般过去时:
Grandma darned my favorite sweater. = 奶奶缝补了我最喜欢的毛衣。
