pct:/pəˈsɛnt/|abbr.|百分比，百分率|Percentage, a rate, number, or amount in each hundred.|The survey showed that 80 pct of respondents favored the proposal.  调查显示80%的受访者赞成该提案。|近义词：percentage, rate|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The humidity is 70 pct today. = 今天的湿度是70%。
The store offers a 10 pct discount. = 商店提供10%的折扣。


一般过去时:
The success rate was 95 pct last year. = 去年成功率为95%。
Interest rates fell by 2 pct. = 利率下降了2%。


现在完成时:
The company has increased its market share by 5 pct. = 公司的市场份额增加了5%。
Inflation has risen by 3 pct this year. = 今年通货膨胀上涨了3%。


过去完成时:
The stock price had dropped by 10 pct before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，股价已经下跌了10%。
Unemployment had fallen by 2 pct in the previous quarter. = 在上一季度，失业率下降了2%。


将来时:
The price is expected to increase by 15 pct next year. = 预计明年的价格将上涨15%。
We anticipate a 10 pct growth in sales. = 我们预计销售额增长10%。


虚拟语气:
If the inflation rate were only 2 pct, the economy would be healthier. = 如果通货膨胀率只有2%，经济就会更健康。
I wish the tax rate were lower by 10 pct. = 我希望税率能降低10%。


被动语态:
The data was analyzed using a 99 pct confidence interval. = 数据是使用99%的置信区间进行分析的。
The results were interpreted with a 5 pct margin of error. = 结果是用5%的误差幅度解释的。


疑问句:
What pct of the population voted? = 有多少百分比的人口投票了？
What pct of the budget is allocated to education? = 预算的多少百分比分配给教育？


否定句:
Less than 10 pct of the students failed the exam. = 少于10%的学生考试不及格。
The project did not achieve its target of 80 pct completion. = 该项目没有达到80%的完成目标。
