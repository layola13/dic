grinning:/ˈɡrɪnɪŋ/| adj.|露齿而笑，咧嘴笑|Showing a wide smile.  It often implies a joyful, sometimes mischievous or self-satisfied expression.|例句：He was grinning from ear to ear after winning the race. 他赢得比赛后咧嘴大笑。|例句：She gave me a wide, grinning smile. 她对我露出了灿烂的笑容。|近义词：smiling, beaming, smirking|反义词：frowning, scowling|形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is grinning at the camera. = 他对着镜头咧嘴笑。
The child is grinning mischievously. = 孩子淘气地咧嘴笑着。

一般过去时:
She grinned at me after I told the joke. = 我讲完笑话后，她朝我咧嘴一笑。
They grinned when they heard the good news. = 听到好消息后，他们都笑了。

现在完成时:
He has been grinning all day. = 他一整天都在咧嘴笑。
She has grinned at every joke. = 她对每一个笑话都笑了。


过去完成时:
He had grinned before he realized the joke was inappropriate. = 他在意识到笑话不合适之前就已经笑了。
They had grinned widely before the photo was taken. = 照片拍下之前，他们咧嘴笑了。


将来时:
She will grin when she sees her present. = 她看到礼物的时候会咧嘴一笑。
They will be grinning from ear to ear at the party. = 他们在派对上会咧嘴大笑。


虚拟语气:
If he won the lottery, he would be grinning. = 如果他中了彩票，他会咧嘴大笑。
I wish I could grin like that without feeling foolish. = 我真希望自己能像那样笑而不会感到傻。

被动语态:
(Grinning is rarely used in the passive voice.  A more natural phrasing would be needed.)  His face was lit up with a grin. = 他的脸上露出了笑容。
The picture showed him grinning broadly. = 照片显示他咧嘴大笑。


疑问句:
Is he grinning? = 他在笑吗？
Why is she grinning like that? = 她为什么那样笑？


否定句:
He isn't grinning; he's frowning. = 他没笑，他在皱眉。
She didn't grin at his attempt at humor. = 她对他的幽默尝试没有表示微笑。


PHRASE->
grin and bear it = 忍耐;默默忍受
grinning like a Cheshire cat = 咧嘴大笑（像柴郡猫一样）
a wide grin = 灿烂的笑容
to grin and bear it = 忍气吞声
a sheepish grin = 尴尬的笑容
