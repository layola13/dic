meltable: /ˈmeltəbl/ | adj. | 可融化的，易熔化的 | Capable of being melted.  Something that is meltable can be changed from a solid to a liquid by the application of heat. | Example:  Butter is meltable. 黄油是可融化的。  The chocolate is meltable in warm weather. 巧克力在温暖的天气里会融化。|近义词：fusible, liquefiable | 反义词：infusible, unmeltable
