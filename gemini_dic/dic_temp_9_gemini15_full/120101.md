mimesis:/maɪˈmiːsɪs/| n. |模仿，拟态，模拟|The imitation of the manners and actions of another person, especially for comic effect.  It also refers to the representation or imitation of the real world in art and literature.  |例句：The actor's mimesis of the politician was hilarious.  演员对这位政治家的模仿非常滑稽。|例句：The novel's mimesis of urban life was remarkably accurate. 这部小说对城市生活的模拟非常准确。|近义词：imitation, simulation, representation, copy |反义词：originality, innovation
