prerevenge:/ˌpriːrɪˈvɛndʒ/ | n. |预先报复；先发制人 |The act of taking revenge before being wronged, often as a preemptive strike or to prevent future harm. 例句：The company's prerevenge against the competitor was a swift and decisive legal action. = 公司对竞争对手的预先报复是一项迅速而果断的法律行动。


近义词：anticipatory retaliation, preemptive strike, preventive action |反义词：forgiveness, reconciliation
