frames:/freɪmz/| n.|框架；画面；眼镜框；结构|A frame is a rigid structure that provides support or encloses something.  It can refer to a single frame or a series of frames, such as those in a movie.|例句：The picture is in a beautiful gold frame. 这幅画在一个漂亮的金色相框里。

例句：The movie was made up of a series of frames. 这部电影是由一系列画面组成的。|近义词：framework, structure, setting|反义词：None specifically.
