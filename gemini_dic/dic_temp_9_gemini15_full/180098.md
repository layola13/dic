refloating: /ˌriːˈfloʊtɪŋ/ | v. | 使重新浮起；使恢复运转 | The act of making something float again, or restoring something to a functioning state after a period of inactivity or difficulty.  It often implies overcoming a setback or difficulty. |

例句：

The engineers worked tirelessly to refloat the stranded tanker. = 工程师们夜以继日地工作以使搁浅的油轮重新浮起。

The company is refloating its struggling subsidiary by injecting new capital. = 公司正在通过注入新的资本使陷入困境的子公司恢复运转。


近义词：refloat, relaunch, revive, resurrect

反义词：sink, submerge, fail, collapse


词性：动词
