segmented:/ˈsɛɡmɛntɪd/ | adj. | 分段的；分节的；有节的 | Divided into segments or sections.  | Example: The segmented worm moved slowly across the ground.  这条分节的蠕虫慢慢地爬过地面。 |近义词：divided, sectioned, portioned | 反义词：continuous, unbroken, whole

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The data is segmented by age group. = 数据按年龄组分段。
The road is segmented into several sections. = 这条路被分成几段。

一般过去时:
The researchers segmented the image into regions of interest. = 研究人员将图像分割成感兴趣的区域。
The teacher segmented the class into smaller groups. = 老师把班级分成更小的组。

现在完成时:
The company has segmented its market effectively. = 公司已有效地细分了市场。
We have segmented the project into manageable tasks. = 我们已将项目分解成易于管理的任务。

过去完成时:
They had segmented the data before running the analysis. = 他们在运行分析之前已经对数据进行了分段。
The artist had segmented the painting into distinct color blocks. = 艺术家已将绘画分割成不同的色块。

将来时:
We will segment the customer database by location. = 我们将按位置细分客户数据库。
The software will segment the video into individual scenes. = 该软件将视频分割成单独的场景。

虚拟语气:
If we segmented the market differently, we might see better results. = 如果我们对市场进行不同的细分，我们可能会看到更好的结果。
I wish we had segmented the project more effectively. = 我希望我们更有效地细分了项目。

被动语态:
The image was segmented using a sophisticated algorithm. = 使用复杂的算法对图像进行了分割。
The market is being segmented by demographics. = 市场正在按人口统计进行细分。

疑问句:

否定句:

The project has not yet been segmented into phases. = 项目尚未按阶段细分。
