overmerited:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛrɪtɪd/| adj.|过分称赞的，过誉的 |excessively praised or rewarded; deserving less praise or reward than received.  |The actor's performance, while good, was overmerited by the critics.  这位演员的表演虽然不错，却被评论家们过分赞扬了。|overpraised, overrated|underestimated, underrated|形容词
