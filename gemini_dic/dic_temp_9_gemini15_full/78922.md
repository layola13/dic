词源:
Gipsyism derives from "gipsy" (or "gypsy"), a term historically used to refer to the Roma people.  The suffix "-ism" denotes a system, practice, or characteristic.  Therefore, "gipsyism" indicates the characteristic ways of life, traditions, and cultural practices associated with the Roma people.  The term is now considered by many to be offensive, and more respectful terms like "Roma culture" are preferred.
