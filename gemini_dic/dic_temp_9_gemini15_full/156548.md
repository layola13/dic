词源:
The word "pickaxe" is a compound word.  "Pick" refers to the action of striking or pecking, and "axe" refers to a cutting tool.  The combination indicates a tool that both picks (strikes) and has an axe-like head.  The word's origins trace back to Old English and Germanic roots.
