decimals:/ˈdesɪməlz/| n.|小数，小数位|Numbers expressed in a system of tens, with a decimal point separating the whole number from the fractional part.  Decimals are used to represent numbers that are not whole numbers.|例句：The answer to the problem is 3.14, a decimal.  这道题的答案是3.14，一个小数。

例句：She rounded the decimals to the nearest tenth. 她将小数四舍五入到十分位。


近义词：fractional numbers, decimal fractions|反义词：integers, whole numbers|词性:noun
