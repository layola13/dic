electricity:/ˌɪˌlɛkˈtrɪsəti/ | n. | 电，电力 |  The flow of electrical power or charge.  Electricity is a form of energy resulting from the existence of charged particles (such as electrons or protons), either statically as an accumulation of charge or dynamically as a current. |  例句：The house is powered by electricity. 这房子用电供电。

例句：We need electricity to run our appliances. 我们需要电力来运行我们的家用电器。


近义词：power, current, energy | 反义词：None (electricity is a type of energy, not something with a direct opposite) | 词性: noun
