crispation:/krɪsˈpeɪʃən/| n. |卷曲；起皱；波纹|The act of crisping or the state of being crisped; a wrinkling or curling.  It often refers to a slight rippling or unevenness of a surface.  The term is used less frequently than its related words. |Example: The slight crispation of the water's surface indicated a gentle breeze.  水面轻微的褶皱表明微风拂过。|近义词：curling, wrinkling, rippling, undulation |反义词：smoothness, flatness |名词
