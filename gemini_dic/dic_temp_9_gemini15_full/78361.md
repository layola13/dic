gestations:/dʒɛˈsteɪʃənz/| n. |妊娠期；孕育；酝酿|The period of time during which an embryo or fetus is developing inside the uterus.  It also refers to the period of development or formation of something.|例句：The gestation period for elephants is exceptionally long.  大象的妊娠期特别长。

例句：The gestation of a new business plan can take months of careful planning. 新商业计划的酝酿可能需要数月的精心策划。


近义词：pregnancy, incubation, development, maturation|反义词：birth, delivery
