minever:/ˈmɪnɪvər/| n. |貂皮；貂；灰鼠|The word "minever" refers to the fur of the marten, a small, carnivorous mammal.  Historically, it was highly valued for its softness and luxurious appearance, often used in clothing and other high-end items. The term can also sometimes refer to the animal itself.|Example:  The queen's robe was trimmed with exquisite minever.|近义词：marten fur, sable (though sable is typically from a different species)|反义词：无|名词
