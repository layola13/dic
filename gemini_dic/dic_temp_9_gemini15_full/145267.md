overboldness:/ˌoʊvərˈboʊldnəs/| n. |鲁莽；放肆；过于大胆|Excessive boldness; impudence; audacity.  It often implies a lack of consideration for others or the consequences of one's actions.|例句：His overboldness in challenging the boss cost him his job.  他的鲁莽行为挑战老板让他丢了工作。

例句：The overboldness of their plan was immediately apparent to everyone. 他们的计划过于鲁莽，每个人都立刻看出来了。|近义词：audacity, presumption, temerity, brashness|反义词：timidity, shyness, caution, restraint|词性:名词

词源:

The word "overboldness" is a combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") and "boldness," which derives from the Old English word "beald," meaning "brave" or "confident."  Therefore, "overboldness" literally means "excessive boldness."
