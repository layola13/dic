malay:/məˈleɪ/| n. adj.|马来语；马来人；马来亚的；|The word "Malay" refers to the Malay language, the people of Malaysia, or things related to Malaysia.  It can be used as a noun or an adjective.|例句：Malay is the national language of Malaysia. 马来语是马来西亚的国语。

例句：He is of Malay descent. 他是马来人后裔。
