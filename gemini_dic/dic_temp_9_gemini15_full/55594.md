disuniter:/dɪsˈjuːnɪtər/| n.|离间者，挑拨离间的人|A person or thing that causes disunity or discord.  A disuniter sows discord and conflict, undermining unity and cooperation.|The politician was accused of being a disuniter, deliberately spreading misinformation to divide the community.  这位政治家被指控为离间者，故意散布不实信息以分裂社区。|近义词：agitator, troublemaker, discordant element|反义词：unifier, peacemaker, conciliator
