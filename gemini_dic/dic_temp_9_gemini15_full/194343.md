self-pleaser: /ˈsɛlf ˈpliːzər/ | n. | 自我满足者，只顾自己享受的人 | A person who prioritizes their own pleasure and gratification above all else, often at the expense of others' needs or feelings.  They may engage in self-indulgent behaviors and lack empathy. | Example:  He's a self-pleaser; he only cares about his own comfort and doesn't consider the impact on others. 他是个只顾自己享受的人；他只关心自己的舒适，不考虑对别人的影响。 | 近义词: egoist, narcissist, hedonist | 反义词: altruist, selfless person | 名词
