CPU:/ˌsiːpiːˈjuː/ | n. |中央处理器，中央处理单元 |The central processing unit (CPU) is the brain of a computer.  It executes instructions and performs calculations.  |例句：My computer's CPU is quite powerful. 我的电脑的CPU相当强大。

例句：The new CPU significantly improved the computer's processing speed. 新的CPU显著提高了电脑的处理速度。


近义词：processor, central processor


反义词：N/A


词源:  The acronym CPU stands for "Central Processing Unit."  It's a shortened form of a descriptive phrase, not derived from a single root word in the same way many words are.  The term emerged with the development of computers and the recognition of this core component.
