prehistories:/ˌpriːˈhɪstəriz/| n. |史前史；古代史|The study of prehistories involves examining artifacts and other evidence to reconstruct past societies and cultures before written records.|例句：The prehistories of many cultures are still largely unknown.  许多文化的史前史在很大程度上仍然未知。|例句：Research into the prehistories of the region revealed evidence of early human settlements.  对该地区史前史的研究揭示了早期人类定居点的证据。|近义词：ancient history, prehistory|反义词：recorded history
