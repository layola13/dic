expound:/ɪkˈspaʊnd/| vt.|详细解释，阐述|To explain or discuss something in detail.  |例句：The professor expounded on the theory of relativity. 教授详细解释了相对论。|例句：She expounded her views on the subject at length. 她详细阐述了她对这个主题的观点。|近义词：explain, elucidate, elaborate, clarify|反义词：obscure, conceal, mystify

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He expounds his ideas clearly. = 他清晰地阐述了他的想法。
The teacher expounds the grammar rules patiently. = 老师耐心地讲解语法规则。

一般过去时:
She expounded the poem's meaning to the class. = 她向班级解释了这首诗的意思。
The speaker expounded his political views to the audience. = 演讲者向听众阐述了他的政治观点。

现在完成时:
He has expounded this theory in several publications. = 他已在几份出版物中阐述了这一理论。
She has expounded on the benefits of exercise for years. = 多年来，她一直在阐述运动的好处。

过去完成时:
He had expounded the concept before the audience arrived. = 在观众到达之前，他已经阐述了这个概念。
They had expounded their reasons for the decision. = 他们已经阐述了他们做出决定的理由。

将来时:
She will expound on the topic in her next lecture. = 她将在下次讲座中详细阐述这个主题。
He will expound his findings at the conference. = 他将在会议上详细阐述他的发现。

虚拟语气:
If he were to expound on the subject, we would understand better. = 如果他详细阐述这个主题，我们会更好地理解。
I wish he would expound on the implications of his research. = 我希望他能详细阐述他的研究结果的意义。

被动语态:
The theory was expounded in great detail. = 该理论被详细地阐述了。
His ideas were expounded by several scholars. = 他的思想被几位学者详细阐述了。

疑问句:
Will the author expound on his new theory? = 作者会详细阐述他的新理论吗？
How did the speaker expound the complex issue? = 演讲者是如何阐述这个复杂问题的？

否定句:
He did not expound on the matter further. = 他没有进一步阐述此事。
The article did not expound the topic sufficiently. = 这篇文章没有充分阐述该主题。
