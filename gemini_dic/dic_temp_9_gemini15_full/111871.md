logman:/ˈlɒɡmən/| n. |伐木工人，管理员|A logman is a person who cuts down trees or manages a logging operation.  They may work in forests, using chainsaws and other equipment to fell trees, or they may oversee the entire process from felling to transportation. |例句：The logman skillfully felled the giant redwood. 伐木工人熟练地砍倒了巨大的红杉树。|例句：The logman managed a team of workers to harvest timber efficiently.  伐木工人管理着一支工人队伍，以高效的方式收获木材。|近义词：lumberjack, logger, woodsman |反义词：N/A
