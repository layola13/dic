godkin:/ˈɡɑːd.kɪn/| n.|上帝的亲属；（尤指）教父或教母的孩子|A godkin is a godchild, or sometimes more broadly, a person related to someone through a godparent relationship.  The term is less common than "godchild" and often implies a closer, almost familial connection.|例句：She considers her godkin as close as her own nieces and nephews. 她把她的教子当成自己的侄女侄子一样亲近。|近义词：godchild, spiritual child|反义词：N/A
