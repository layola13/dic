ponies:/ˈpoʊniz/| n.|小马驹，矮种马|A pony is a small horse, especially one that is less than 14.2 hands (58 inches or 147 cm) high.  Ponies are often stockier and more hardy than horses.|例句：The children loved riding the ponies at the fair.孩子们喜欢在游乐会上骑小马。|例句：She owns a small herd of Shetland ponies. 她拥有一小群设得兰矮种马。|近义词：horse, steed|反义词：stallion(成年种马),  (无直接反义词，可根据语境选择体型大的马匹)
