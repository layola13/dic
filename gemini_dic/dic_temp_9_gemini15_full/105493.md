kilnful:/ˈkɪlnfʊl/| n. |窑满的一窑（量）|The amount that a kiln will hold; a kiln load.|例句：The kilnful of bricks was ready to be unloaded.  这窑砖已经准备好卸货了。

The potter fired a kilnful of pottery. 陶工烧制了一窑陶器。

近义词：kiln load,  full kiln|反义词：无


USAGE->
无，kilnful作为名词，本身不涉及动词的时态变化。


PHRASE->
a kilnful of bricks 一窑砖
a kilnful of pottery 一窑陶器
a kilnful of tiles 一窑瓦片
a kilnful of ceramics 一窑陶瓷制品
a kilnful of stoneware 一窑粗陶器
