muhammadan:/ˌmuːˈhæmədən/ | adj. n. |穆斯林的；穆斯林 |  A follower of Islam; relating to Islam or Muslims. The term is now largely considered outdated and potentially offensive, as it implies a passive or subordinate relationship to Muhammad.  It's preferable to use terms like "Muslim" or "Islamic". | The muhammadan community celebrated Eid al-Fitr. 穆斯林社区庆祝开斋节。 He studied muhammadan law. 他学习伊斯兰教法。 | Muslim, Islamic | non-Muslim, non-Islamic | adjective, noun
