skill-lessness:/ˈskɪlˌləsnəs/ | n. |缺乏技能；笨拙；无能 |Lack of skill or ability; incompetence.  It often implies a lack of proficiency in a particular area, rather than a complete absence of skills. |例句：His skill-lessness in the kitchen resulted in a burnt dinner.  他的厨艺不精导致晚餐烧焦了。

His skill-lessness at playing the piano was evident in his clumsy performance. 他弹钢琴的技术拙劣，从他笨拙的演奏中可以看出。|近义词：incompetence, ineptitude, clumsiness, awkwardness |反义词：skill, expertise, proficiency, dexterity
