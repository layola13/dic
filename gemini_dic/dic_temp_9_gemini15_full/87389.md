herpetologist:/ˌhɜːrpəˈtɒlədʒɪst/| n. |爬虫学家|A herpetologist is a scientist who studies reptiles and amphibians.  Herpetologists conduct research on the biology, behavior, ecology, and conservation of these animals. They might work in museums, universities, zoos, or government agencies.|例句：Dr. Smith, a renowned herpetologist, discovered a new species of frog in the Amazon rainforest. 史密斯博士是一位著名的爬虫学家，他在亚马逊雨林中发现了一种新的青蛙物种。|例句：Many herpetologists are dedicated to protecting endangered reptiles and amphibians. 许多爬虫学家致力于保护濒危的爬行动物和两栖动物。|近义词：reptile specialist, amphibian specialist|反义词：无
