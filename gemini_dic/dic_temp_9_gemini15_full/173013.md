quarantiners:/ˈkwɒrənˌtaɪnərz/| n. |隔离者，隔离期的人|People who are undergoing quarantine.  Those subject to quarantine are called "quarantiners". The term often refers to a group of people in quarantine together. |例句：The quarantiners were all required to wear masks. 隔离人员都被要求戴口罩。

例句：The hotel housed a group of quarantiners from various countries. 这家酒店住着一群来自不同国家的隔离人员。


近义词：isolatees, those in isolation, people in quarantine


反义词：the unconfined, the free-roaming


词性:名词
