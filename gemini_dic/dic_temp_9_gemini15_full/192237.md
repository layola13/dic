scrawny:/ˈskrɔːni/| adj.|瘦弱的；骨瘦如柴的| extremely thin and bony;  weak and undernourished. |例句：The scrawny dog looked like it hadn't eaten in days. 这只瘦弱的狗看起来好几天没吃东西了。The scrawny kitten struggled to climb the tree.那只瘦弱的小猫费力地爬树。|近义词：thin, bony, lean, gaunt, emaciated|反义词：plump, stout, obese, corpulent


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is scrawny. 他很瘦弱。
The cat looks scrawny. 这只猫看起来很瘦弱。


一般过去时:
He was scrawny when he was young. 他年轻的时候很瘦弱。
The dog was scrawny and weak. 那只狗又瘦又弱。


现在完成时:
He has become scrawny recently. 他最近变得很瘦弱了。
The plant has become scrawny due to lack of water.  由于缺水，这株植物变得很瘦弱了。


过去完成时:
He had been scrawny for years. 他瘦弱了好几年了。
The horse had been scrawny before it received proper care. 这匹马在得到适当的照顾之前一直很瘦弱。


将来时:
He will be scrawny if he doesn't eat more. 如果他不多吃点东西，他会变得很瘦弱。
The puppy will likely be scrawny without enough milk. 小狗如果没有足够的奶喝，可能会很瘦弱。


虚拟语气:
If he were scrawny, he would need to gain weight. 如果他很瘦弱，他就需要增重。
I wish I weren't so scrawny. 我希望我不那么瘦弱。


被动语态:
(This adjective is rarely used in the passive voice.  It describes a state, not an action.)


疑问句:
Is he scrawny? 他瘦弱吗？
Does the bird look scrawny? 这只鸟看起来瘦弱吗？


否定句:
He is not scrawny; he's quite muscular. 他不瘦弱；他肌肉相当发达。
The child is not scrawny; she's just small for her age.  这个孩子不瘦弱；她只是比同龄人小。
