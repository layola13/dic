sashay:/ʃæˈʃeɪ/| v.|轻盈地走动；扭动着走；昂首阔步|To walk in a proud, confident, and often flamboyant way, typically with a swaying movement of the hips.|例句：She sashayed down the runway in a stunning gown. 她穿着华丽的长袍，在T台上轻盈地走动。|例句：The model sashayed across the stage, drawing gasps of admiration. 模特在舞台上扭动着走过，引来赞叹声。|近义词：strut, glide, swagger, promenade|反义词：shuffle, stumble, trudge


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She sashays down the street every morning. = 她每天早上都轻盈地走在街上。
He sashays into the room with confidence. = 他自信地昂首阔步走进房间。

一般过去时:
She sashayed onto the stage and began to sing. = 她轻盈地走上舞台，开始唱歌。
He sashayed past us without a word. = 他一言不发地从我们身边昂首阔步走过。

现在完成时:
She has sashayed through life with grace and style. = 她优雅而潇洒地走过人生。
He has sashayed his way to the top of his profession. = 他昂首阔步地走到了他职业生涯的巅峰。

过去完成时:
She had sashayed into the party before anyone else arrived. = 在其他人到达之前，她就轻盈地走进了派对。
He had sashayed through the crowd unnoticed. = 他在人群中昂首阔步走过，无人注意。

将来时:
She will sashay down the aisle at her wedding. = 她将在婚礼上轻盈地走过婚礼过道。
He will sashay into the meeting, ready to present his ideas. = 他将昂首阔步地走进会议，准备陈述他的想法。

虚拟语气:
If she sashayed with more confidence, she would be a better performer. = 如果她更自信地走动，她会成为一个更好的表演者。
I wish I could sashay like that professional dancer. = 我真希望我能像那个专业舞者那样轻盈地走动。

被动语态: (Sashay is rarely used in the passive voice)
The model was made to sashay down the runway. = 模特被安排在T台上轻盈地走动。(较不自然)


疑问句:
Does she sashay when she walks? = 她走路时会轻盈地走动吗？
Will he sashay into the room or just walk in? = 他会昂首阔步走进房间还是只是走进去？


否定句:
She didn't sashay; she walked normally. = 她没有轻盈地走动；她正常地走着。
He didn't sashay; he shuffled along. = 他没有昂首阔步；他拖着脚走。
