jigger's:/ˈdʒɪɡər/| n.|小酒杯；少量；(俚)骗子；(俚)注射器|A jigger is a small cup used for measuring liquor in cocktails. It can also refer to a small amount of something, or slangily, a swindler or a hypodermic syringe.|例句：He measured the rum with a jigger. 他用小酒杯量了朗姆酒。 He gave me a jigger of whiskey. 他给了我一小杯威士忌。|近义词：measure, shot, thimble|反义词：large quantity, much
