coordinating:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪŋ/ | v. adj. | 协调的；合作的；同等的 | Coordinating activities involves bringing various elements together to achieve a common goal.  协调活动包括将各种要素整合在一起以实现共同目标。 |  harmonizing, integrating, synchronizing | conflicting, disparate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is coordinating the project. = 他正在协调这个项目。
They are coordinating their efforts. = 他们正在协调他们的努力。


一般过去时:
She coordinated the event successfully. = 她成功地协调了这次活动。
We coordinated our schedules. = 我们协调了我们的日程安排。


现在完成时:
They have coordinated their responses. = 他们已经协调了他们的回应。
He has coordinated many large-scale events. = 他已经协调了许多大型活动。


过去完成时:
She had coordinated the team before the meeting. = 会议之前她已经协调好了团队。
They had coordinated their strategies prior to the launch. = 他们在启动之前已经协调了他们的策略。


将来时:
We will coordinate our actions. = 我们将协调我们的行动。
They will coordinate the logistics for the trip. = 他们将协调这次旅行的后勤工作。


虚拟语气:
If I were coordinating the project, I would do it differently. = 如果我负责协调这个项目，我会采取不同的方法。
It would be helpful if we could coordinate our schedules better. = 如果我们能更好地协调我们的时间安排，那就太好了。


被动语态:
The activities were coordinated by the project manager. = 这些活动是由项目经理协调的。
The meeting was carefully coordinated to maximize efficiency. = 会议经过精心协调以最大限度地提高效率。


疑问句:
Are you coordinating the team? = 你在协调团队吗？
Who is coordinating this effort? = 谁在协调这项工作？


否定句:
He is not coordinating the project effectively. = 他没有有效地协调这个项目。
They did not coordinate their actions properly. = 他们没有适当地协调他们的行动。


PHRASE->
coordinating conjunction: 协调连词
coordinating committee: 协调委员会
coordinating center: 协调中心
coordinating efforts: 协调努力
coordinating agency: 协调机构

词源:
源自拉丁语 "coordinatus,"  "co-" 表示 "together," "ordinatus" 表示 "ordered,"  因此字面意思是"一起被安排好"。
