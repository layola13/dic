sidewipes:/ˈsaɪdwaɪps/| n. v.|擦碰；轻微碰撞；轻扫|A sidewipe is a slight collision, usually involving the side of a vehicle.  It can also refer to a brief, glancing blow or remark.|例句：The car suffered only minor damage from the sidewipe. 这辆车在轻微碰撞中只受了轻微损坏。

The cyclist narrowly avoided a sidewipe with a passing car.  骑自行车的人险些与一辆经过的汽车发生擦碰。|近义词：brush, graze, scrape, glancing blow|反义词：head-on collision, direct hit|
