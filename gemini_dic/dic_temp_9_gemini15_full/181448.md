rejeopardize:/ˌriːˈdʒɛpərdaɪz/| vt.|再次危及；重新冒险|To put something in danger again after it seemed to be safe.  This word implies a previous period of risk that was seemingly overcome, only to be threatened anew.|例句：After the initial success, careless management rejeopardized the company's future.  最初的成功之后，粗心的管理再次危及了公司的未来。

例句：The peace treaty was rejeopardized by the recent border skirmishes. 最近的边境冲突使和平条约再次受到威胁。


近义词：re-endanger, imperil again, put at risk again|反义词：secure, safeguard, protect|词性:动词
