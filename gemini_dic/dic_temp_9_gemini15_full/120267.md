mini-van:/ˈmɪniˌvæn/| n. |小型货车，厢式客车 | A mini-van is a type of vehicle that combines the features of a car and a van, offering more passenger and cargo space than a car but being smaller and more maneuverable than a full-size van.  They are often used for families due to their spacious interiors and practicality. |  We bought a mini-van to accommodate our growing family. 我们买了一辆小型货车来容纳我们日益壮大的家庭。 | minivan, family car | sports car, sedan | 名词
