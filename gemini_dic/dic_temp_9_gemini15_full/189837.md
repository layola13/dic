samara:/səˈmɑːrə/ | n. |翅果，螺旋桨果 | A type of dry, one-seeded fruit with a wing that aids in wind dispersal.  It is characteristic of maples and ashes. | Example: The maple tree produced many samaras in the autumn.  秋季时枫树长出了许多翅果。|近义词：winged seed, key (informal)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with fruits that rely on other dispersal methods (e.g., berries).

词源:
