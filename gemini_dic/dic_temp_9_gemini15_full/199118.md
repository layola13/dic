silenced: /ˈsaɪlənst/ | v. | 使沉默；使安静；压制；使无效 | To make someone or something silent; to suppress or stop something from being heard or expressed. | 近义词：muffled, hushed, quieted, suppressed | 反义词：activated, amplified, encouraged


例句:

The protesters were silenced by the police.  = 抗议者被警察压制住了。

The loud music was silenced when the neighbors complained. = 邻居投诉后，吵闹的音乐声停止了。
