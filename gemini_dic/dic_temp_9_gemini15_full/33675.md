cinnabar:/ˈsɪnəbɑːr/| n.|朱砂，辰砂 |A heavy, red mineral consisting of mercury sulfide, used as a pigment and in traditional medicine.  It is also known as mercury sulfide. |Example: The ancient Egyptians used cinnabar as a pigment in their artwork. 古代埃及人用朱砂作为绘画颜料。The artist mixed cinnabar with other pigments to create a vibrant red hue.  艺术家将朱砂与其他颜料混合以创造出鲜艳的红色色调。|近义词：mercury sulfide, vermilion|反义词：无
