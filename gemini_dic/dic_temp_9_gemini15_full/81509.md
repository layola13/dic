gravid:/ˈɡrævɪd/| adj.|怀孕的，充满的|Pregnant; full of something.  The word typically refers to a pregnant female animal, but can also be used figuratively to describe something full or swollen.|例句：The gravid mare is expected to foal next month. 这匹怀孕的母马预计下个月分娩。|例句：The sky was gravid with storm clouds. 天空布满了暴风雨的乌云。|近义词：pregnant, teeming, replete, laden|反义词：barren, empty
