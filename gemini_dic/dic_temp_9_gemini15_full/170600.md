pseudo-English:/ˈsjuːdoʊˌɪŋɡlɪʃ/|/ˈsjuːdəʊˌɪŋɡlɪʃ/| adj. |伪英语；似是而非的英语 |English that superficially resembles English but is not grammatically correct or meaningfully coherent. It often appears in computer programming, technical documentation, or as a result of machine translation.  |Example:  The error message displayed a string of pseudo-English that was completely unintelligible.  这个错误消息显示出一串完全无法理解的伪英语。|近义词：jargon, gibberish, gobbledygook|反义词：clear English, plain English


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective, so verb conjugations don't apply directly to "pseudo-English" itself.  The usage would concern how to describe things *as* pseudo-English.

Example sentences illustrating its usage:

The software's documentation contained significant stretches of pseudo-English. 软件文档包含大量的伪英语。

His email was filled with what appeared to be pseudo-English, making it hard to understand. 他的邮件充满了看起来像是伪英语的内容，很难理解。


PHRASE->
There aren't common phrases directly incorporating "pseudo-English." The word itself describes a characteristic of language.  Instead, phrases might describe the *result* of encountering pseudo-English:

"That's complete pseudo-English!"  那完全是伪英语！
"The manual is full of pseudo-English."  手册里充满了伪英语。
"I couldn't understand a word – it was all pseudo-English." 我一个字都听不懂——全是伪英语。
"It reads like pseudo-English." 它读起来像伪英语。
"The translation resulted in utter pseudo-English." 翻译结果是完全的伪英语。
