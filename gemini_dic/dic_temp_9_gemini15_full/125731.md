community:/kəˈmjuːnɪti/ | n. | 社区，团体 | A group of people living in the same place or having a particular characteristic in common.  |例句：The community rallied together to help those affected by the hurricane. 社区团结起来帮助受飓风影响的人们。|例句：The scientific community is divided on the issue of climate change. 科学界对气候变化问题存在分歧。|近义词：society, group, association |反义词：individual, isolation
