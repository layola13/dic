necessitates: /nəˈsɛsɪteɪts/ | vt. | 使成为必要；需要 | To make something necessary.  | The urgency of the situation necessitates immediate action.  情况紧急，需要立即采取行动。| requires, demands, calls for | permits, allows | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This project necessitates careful planning. = 这个项目需要仔细的计划。
The situation necessitates a change in strategy. = 这种情况需要改变策略。


一般过去时:
The war necessitated rationing of food and fuel. = 战争使粮食和燃料配给成为必要。
The accident necessitated a complete overhaul of the safety procedures. = 事故需要对安全程序进行彻底检修。


现在完成时:
Recent events have necessitated a review of our security protocols. = 最近发生的事件使我们不得不审查安全协议。
The increase in demand has necessitated the hiring of additional staff. = 需求的增加使我们不得不雇佣更多员工。


过去完成时:
The previous crisis had necessitated a significant budget cut. = 此前的危机已迫使大幅削减预算。
The delay had necessitated a rescheduling of the conference. = 延误迫使会议重新安排时间。


将来时:
Future economic challenges will necessitate innovative solutions. = 未来经济挑战将需要创新解决方案。
Increased regulations will necessitate changes to our manufacturing process. = 越来越严格的法规将需要改变我们的生产流程。


虚拟语气:
If the situation were different, it wouldn't necessitate such drastic measures. = 如果情况不同，就不需要采取如此 drastic 的措施。
It would necessitate a significant investment if we were to expand our operations. = 如果我们要扩大业务，就需要大量投资。


被动语态:
Immediate action is necessitated by the crisis. = 危机需要立即采取行动。
A complete overhaul of the system is necessitated by the recent failures. = 最近的故障迫使系统进行彻底检修。


疑问句:
Does this situation necessitate further investigation? = 这种情况需要进一步调查吗？
What does the law necessitate in this particular case? = 在这种特殊情况下，法律需要什么？


否定句:
The current situation does not necessitate any immediate action. = 目前的情况不需要任何立即行动。
The project doesn't necessitate any special equipment. = 该项目不需要任何特殊设备。
