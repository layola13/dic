hammerlocks:/ˈhæmərˌlɒks/| n.| 锤锁；（摔跤中的）锤锁技；（尤指）控制某人的手腕或手臂的锁技|A hammerlock is a wrestling hold that involves controlling an opponent's arm or wrist, typically by bending it sharply.  It can be used offensively or defensively.  The term derives from the similarity of the grip to that of using a hammer.|例句：He applied a hammerlock to his opponent's arm, forcing a submission. 他对对手的手臂施以锤锁，迫使其屈服。

The referee stopped the match when one wrestler applied a hammerlock that seemed to be causing pain. 当一名摔跤手使用看似会造成疼痛的锤锁时，裁判叫停了比赛。|近义词：wrist lock, arm lock, control hold|反义词:release, free
