prove:/pruːv/| vt.  |证明；证实；检验|To establish the truth or validity of something.|The experiment proved his theory.  实验证明了他的理论。|demonstrate, verify, confirm |disprove, refute|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He proves his point with evidence. 他用证据证明了他的观点。
The data proves the hypothesis. 数据证明了这个假设。


一般过去时:
She proved her innocence. 她证明了自己的清白。
The test proved the medication effective. 测试证明这种药物有效。


现在完成时:
Scientists have proved the existence of black holes. 科学家们已经证明了黑洞的存在。
They have proved their commitment to the project. 他们已经证明了他们对项目的承诺。


过去完成时:
He had proved his loyalty to the company. 他已经证明了他对公司的忠诚。
The investigation had proved the suspect's guilt. 调查已经证明了嫌疑人的罪行。


将来时:
This research will prove the benefits of this new technology. 这项研究将证明这项新技术的益处。
Further tests will prove the effectiveness of the treatment. 进一步的测试将证明治疗的有效性。


虚拟语气:
If I could prove my innocence, I would be free. 如果我能证明我的清白，我就会自由。
If he had proved his case, he would have won. 如果他能证明他的论点，他就会赢。


被动语态:
His theory was proved wrong. 他的理论被证明是错误的。
The statement was proved to be false. 该声明被证明是虚假的。


疑问句:
Can you prove your identity? 你能证明你的身份吗？
How can we prove this statement? 我们如何证明这个说法？


否定句:
He could not prove his alibi. 他无法证明他的不在场证明。
They failed to prove their claims. 他们未能证明他们的说法。


PHRASE->
