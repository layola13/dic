outstrip:/ˌaʊtˈstrɪp/| vt.|超过，胜过|To surpass or exceed (someone or something)|The demand for the product outstripped the supply.  产品需求超过了供应。|  His achievements outstripped those of his contemporaries. 他的成就超过了他的同时代人。| surpass, exceed, excel, outdo| lag behind, fall short of |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outstrips his competitors in sales. = 他在销售额上超过了他的竞争对手。
The company outstrips its rivals in innovation. = 这家公司在创新方面超过了它的竞争对手。

一般过去时:
She outstripped all expectations. = 她超过了所有人的预期。
The runner outstripped the others in the final sprint. = 跑步者在最后的冲刺中超过了其他人。

现在完成时:
Our success has outstripped our wildest dreams. = 我们的成功超过了我们最狂野的梦想。
The project has outstripped its original budget. = 项目已经超过了最初的预算。

过去完成时:
By the end of the race, he had outstripped all his opponents. = 到比赛结束时，他已经超过了所有对手。
The company had outstripped its competitors in market share. = 这家公司在市场份额上已经超过了竞争对手。

将来时:
We expect that demand will outstrip supply in the coming months. = 我们预计未来几个月需求将超过供应。
They believe their technology will outstrip that of their competitors. = 他们相信他们的技术将超过竞争对手的技术。

虚拟语气:
If only we had worked harder, we might have outstripped our rivals. = 如果我们当时更努力工作，我们本可以超过竞争对手。
I wish my performance had outstripped the expectations of the judges. = 我希望我的表现能超过评委的预期。

被动语态:
Their achievements were outstripped by the economic downturn. = 他们的成就被经济衰退所超过。
His efforts were outstripped by the sheer scale of the problem. = 他的努力被问题的巨大规模所超过。

疑问句:
Does his income outstrip his expenses? = 他的收入超过他的支出吗？
Will technological advances outstrip our ability to control them? = 技术进步会超过我们控制它们的能力吗？

否定句:
His efforts did not outstrip those of his colleagues. = 他的努力没有超过同事的努力。
The team's performance didn't outstrip the expectations. = 球队的表现没有超过预期。
