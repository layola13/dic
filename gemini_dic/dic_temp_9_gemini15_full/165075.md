preimitate:/ˌpriːɪˈmɪteɪt/| v.|预先模仿，预演|To imitate or rehearse beforehand; to prefigure or foreshadow through imitation.|例句：The actors preimitated their scenes before the live performance.  演员们在现场表演前预演了他们的场景。
例句：Her early poems preimitated the style of her later, more mature works. 她早期的诗歌预示了她后来更成熟作品的风格。|近义词：rehearse, practice, simulate, foreshadow|反义词：improvise, wing it|动词
