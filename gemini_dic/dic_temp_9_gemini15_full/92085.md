hyperreactors:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈæktərz/| n.|高反应堆，超反应堆|A hyperreactor is a type of nuclear reactor that operates at a higher power density than conventional reactors.  This allows for a smaller reactor size for the same power output.  However, it also presents significant engineering challenges related to heat removal and safety.|例句：The design of the hyperreactor incorporates advanced cooling systems to manage the increased heat generation. = 超反应堆的设计结合了先进的冷却系统来管理增加的热量产生。

The development of hyperreactors is a focus of ongoing research in nuclear engineering. = 超反应堆的开发是核工程领域持续研究的重点。|近义词：advanced reactor, high-power reactor|反义词：low-power reactor, conventional reactor|名词
