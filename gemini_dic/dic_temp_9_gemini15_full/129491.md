non-applicability:/ˌnɒnˌæplɪkəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不适用性，无效性 | The state or quality of not being applicable; irrelevance.  It refers to a situation where something is not relevant or doesn't apply in a particular context. | Example:  The discount is not applicable to this item. 这项商品不适用折扣。

The rule of non-applicability is often used in legal and technical contexts.  该不适用规则通常用于法律和技术语境。

|近义词: irrelevance, inapplicability, inappositeness |反义词: applicability, relevance |词性: noun
