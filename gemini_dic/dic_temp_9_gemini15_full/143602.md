outing:/ˈaʊtɪŋ/ | n. |郊游；远足；短途旅行；(尤指为特定目的的)集体出游 | A short trip or excursion, especially one taken for pleasure or a particular purpose.  |例句：We're planning a family outing to the beach this weekend.  我们计划这个周末全家去海滩郊游。

例句：The school organized an outing to the museum for the students. 学校为学生们组织了一次去博物馆的集体出游。


近义词：excursion, trip, expedition, excursion, jaunt


反义词：staying home, confinement


词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The family is planning an outing to the zoo. = 这家人正在计划去动物园郊游。
She often goes on outings with her friends. = 她经常和朋友们一起出去玩。


一般过去时:
They had a wonderful outing in the park yesterday. = 他们昨天在公园里玩得很开心。
We went on an outing to the mountains last summer. = 去年夏天我们去山里郊游了。


现在完成时:
We have had several outings this month. = 这个月我们已经进行了几次郊游了。
They haven't been on an outing for a long time. = 他们很久没有出去郊游了。


过去完成时:
They had already finished their outing before it started to rain. = 下雨之前他们已经结束了郊游。
We had planned an outing, but it was cancelled due to the bad weather. = 我们计划了一次郊游，但是由于天气不好取消了。


将来时:
We will go on an outing next weekend. = 我们下周末将去郊游。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

词源:
The word "outing" developed from the verb "out," meaning "to go out," combined with the suffix "-ing," which forms nouns indicating an action or process.  Its meaning as a short trip or excursion evolved over time.
