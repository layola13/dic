fenci:/fənˈt͡sʰɪ/| n. |分词|A word formed from a verb and used as an adjective or adverb, participating in the formation of verb tenses and moods. 分词是由动词构成，用作形容词或副词的词，参与动词时态和语气的构成。|例句：The singing bird is beautiful.  唱歌的鸟很漂亮。|近义词：participle |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The singing bird is beautiful. = 唱歌的鸟很漂亮。
The falling leaves covered the ground. = 落叶覆盖了地面。


一般过去时:
The broken vase was irreparable. = 打碎的花瓶无法修复。
The completed project was a success. = 完成的项目很成功。


现在完成时:
Having finished my work, I went home. = 完成了工作，我就回家了。
Having seen the movie, she left the cinema. = 看完电影后，她离开了电影院。


过去完成时:
Having eaten lunch, I felt sleepy. = 吃完午饭后，我感到困倦。
Having studied hard, she passed the exam. = 努力学习后，她通过了考试。


将来时:
The arriving train will be late. = 将要到达的火车会晚点。
The departing plane will take off soon. = 即将来离开的飞机很快就会起飞。


虚拟语气:
If I were a bird, I would fly. = 如果我是一只鸟，我会飞。
If he had finished his work, he would have gone home. = 如果他完成了他的工作，他就会回家了。


被动语态:
The damaged car was repaired. = 受损的汽车被修理了。
The stolen painting was recovered. = 被盗的画被找回了。


疑问句:
What is your favorite color?  = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
Which book are you reading? = 你正在读哪本书？


否定句:
The unfinished task was not submitted. = 未完成的任务没有提交。
The unopened letter was left on the table. = 未拆封的信被留在了桌子上。


虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I would have passed the exam. = 如果我学习更努力，我就会通过考试。
If she were here, she would help us. = 如果她在这是，她会帮助我们的。


被动语态:
The problem was solved by the team. = 这个问题由团队解决了。
The project is being managed by John. = 这个项目由约翰管理。


疑问句:
Is this your writing? = 这是你的作品吗？
Was the game exciting? = 比赛激动人心吗？


否定句:
The letter was not sent. = 信没有寄出。
The work was not completed on time. = 工作没有按时完成。


PHRASE->
present participle (现在分词):  describing an ongoing action.
past participle (过去分词):  describing a completed action.
perfect participle (完成分词): describing an action completed before another.
participial adjective (分词形容词):  a participle used as an adjective.
participial phrase (分词短语): a participle with modifiers and complements.
