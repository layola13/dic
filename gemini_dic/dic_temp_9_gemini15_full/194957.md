semi-opal:/ˈsɛmiˌoʊpəl/| n.|半蛋白石|A semi-opal is a type of opal that doesn't exhibit the characteristic play-of-color found in precious opal.  It may still show milky or translucent qualities, but lacks the vibrant iridescence.  It is often less expensive than precious opal.|Example: The jewelry featured a beautiful semi-opal pendant, its milky white glow catching the light.  这条珠宝饰品有一枚美丽的半蛋白石吊坠，它乳白色的光芒吸引着人们的目光。|近义词：common opal, potch opal|反义词：precious opal
