preevolutionary: /ˌpriːˌɛvəˈluːʃənəri/ | adj. |进化前的；进化之前的 | Existing or occurring before the development of a particular species or biological trait through evolution.  This adjective describes something that existed or happened before the evolutionary process leading to the current state.  |例句：The preevolutionary forms of the species were significantly different.  = 该物种的进化前形式与现在大相径庭。

The study focused on the preevolutionary stages of the organism's development. = 这项研究侧重于该生物进化前的发育阶段。

近义词：prehistoric, antecedent, proto-

反义词：post-evolutionary, evolved
