hyphomycetes:/ˌhaɪfəˈmaɪsiːts/| n. |分生孢菌|Fungi that reproduce asexually by means of conidia.  They are characterized by the production of hyphae and conidia, but lack sexual structures or have not yet been observed to have them.  Many are important decomposers in various ecosystems, while some are plant pathogens or opportunistic human pathogens.|Example:  Many hyphomycetes are found in soil, playing a crucial role in nutrient cycling.  许多分生孢菌存在于土壤中，在养分循环中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：mitosporic fungi, conidial fungi|反义词：Ascomycetes (if considering sexual reproduction as defining characteristic)
