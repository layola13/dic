consolidation:/kənˌsɑːlɪˈdeɪʃən/|/kənˌsɒlɪˈdeɪʃən/| n. |巩固；合并；加强 |The process of making something stronger or more solid.  It can refer to combining things into a single, more powerful unit, or to strengthening something that already exists. |例句：The consolidation of their power was a major step towards unifying the country.  他们的权力巩固是走向国家统一的重要一步。

例句：The company's consolidation of its three factories into one resulted in significant cost savings. 公司将其三个工厂合并成一个，从而节省了大量成本。


近义词：strengthening, unification, amalgamation, integration, reinforcement

反义词：fragmentation, weakening, division, disintegration
