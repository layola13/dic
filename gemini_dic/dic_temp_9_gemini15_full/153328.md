pending:/ˈpɛndɪŋ/ | adj. | 未决的；待定的；悬而未决的 |  waiting to be decided or settled; not yet finalized.  |例句：The project is pending approval.  这个项目正在等待批准。

例句：The decision is pending further investigation.  这个决定有待进一步调查。


近义词：unresolved, undecided, awaiting, unsettled | 反义词：settled, decided, resolved | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The case is pending. = 此案尚未结案。
The application is pending review. = 申请正在审核中。


一般过去时:
The matter was pending for weeks. = 这件事悬而未决数周。
The deal was pending final signatures. = 这笔交易正等待最终签字。


现在完成时:
The issue has been pending since last year. = 这问题从去年就一直悬而未决。
The contract has been pending for months. = 合同已经搁置数月了。


过去完成时:
The decision had been pending before the new evidence emerged. = 新证据出现之前，该决定一直悬而未决。
The project had been pending for years before it finally got approved. = 这个项目在最终获得批准之前已经搁置多年了。


将来时:
The results will be pending until next week. = 结果要到下周才能出来。
The agreement will be pending until both parties sign. = 协议在双方签字之前都将处于待定状态。


虚拟语气:
If the funding were pending, we would postpone the project. = 如果资金未到位，我们将推迟项目。
I wish the decision wasn't pending for so long. = 我真希望这个决定不用等那么久。


被动语态:
The proposal is being pending consideration. = 提案正在等待考虑。
The request is being pending approval. = 请求正在等待批准。


疑问句:
Is the application pending? = 申请还在审核中吗？
Are there any pending issues? = 还有未决的问题吗？


否定句:
The matter is not pending anymore. = 这件事不再悬而未决了。
The case isn't pending; it's been resolved. = 此案已不再待定，已解决。


PHRASE->
pending approval = 等待批准
pending further notice = 待进一步通知
pending the outcome = 等待结果
pending investigation = 等待调查
pending decision = 等待决定
