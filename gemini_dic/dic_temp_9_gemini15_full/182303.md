remunerators: /rɪˈmjuːnəreɪtərz/ | n. | 报酬者；支付报酬者 | Those who pay remuneration; those who compensate for services rendered.  The company's remunerators are generous with bonuses. 公司的报酬者对奖金很慷慨。 |近义词：compensators, payers, benefactors |反义词：exploiters, defrauders

一般现在时:
The company remunerates its employees fairly. = 公司公平地支付员工报酬。
Our clients remunerate us promptly for our services. = 我们的客户及时支付我们的服务费用。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
