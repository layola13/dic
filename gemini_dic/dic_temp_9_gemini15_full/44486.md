crosscut:/ˈkrɒskʌt/| n. v.|横切，交叉切割|A crosscut is a cut made across the grain of wood or other material.  It can also refer to a shortcut or a method that bypasses usual procedures.|例句：The carpenter made a crosscut to shorten the board. 木匠横切木板以缩短长度。 The new strategy represents a crosscut of traditional methods. 新策略代表了对传统方法的绕行。|近义词：transverse cut, diagonal cut |反义词：longitudinal cut, lengthwise cut
