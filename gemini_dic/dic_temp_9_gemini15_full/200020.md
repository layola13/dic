skelder:/ˈskɛldər/| v. |责骂；斥责；大声喊叫 |To scold or rebuke harshly; to shout or yell loudly.  |例句：The teacher skeltered the students for their disruptive behavior. 老师因学生扰乱秩序而严厉斥责他们。|例句：He skeltered at the top of his lungs. 他大声喊叫。|近义词：scold, rebuke, berate, reprimand |反义词：praise, commend, compliment |动词
