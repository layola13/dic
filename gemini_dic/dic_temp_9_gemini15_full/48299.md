decrees:/dɪˈkriːz/| n. v.|法令；命令；判决|An official order issued by a person in authority.|The king decreed that all citizens must pay taxes. 国王下令所有公民都必须纳税。|edict, ordinance, mandate|request, suggestion|名词，动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The judge decrees a sentence. = 法官判决一个刑期。
The government decrees new regulations. = 政府颁布新的规章。


一般过去时:
The emperor decreed a new law. = 皇帝颁布了一项新法律。
The court decreed in favor of the plaintiff. = 法院判决原告胜诉。


现在完成时:
The council has decreed a new policy. = 理事会已经颁布了一项新政策。
She has decreed that no one can enter without permission. = 她已下令未经许可不得进入。


过去完成时:
The king had decreed the construction of a new palace. = 国王之前已经下令建造一座新宫殿。
They had decreed a state of emergency before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经宣布进入紧急状态。


将来时:
The president will decree a national holiday. = 总统将宣布全国假日。
The authorities will decree stricter measures against crime. = 当局将颁布更严格的反犯罪措施。


虚拟语气:
If the queen decreed it, it would be done. = 如果女王下令，那就会执行。
I wish the council had decreed a different outcome. = 我希望理事会做出不同的裁决。


被动语态:
A new law was decreed by the parliament. = 国会颁布了一项新法律。
Stricter penalties were decreed for traffic violations. = 对交通违规行为判处了更严厉的处罚。


疑问句:
What did the court decree? = 法院判决了什么？
What has the government decreed regarding immigration? = 政府关于移民问题下达了什么法令？


否定句:
The governor did not decree a curfew. = 州长没有下令宵禁。
The judge did not decree any additional penalties. = 法官没有判处任何额外的惩罚。


PHRASE->

decree of nullity = 无效判决
decree nisi = （离婚）暂时判决
decree absolute = （离婚）最终判决
