domes:/doʊmz/| n.|圆顶；穹顶|A dome is a rounded vault forming the roof of a building or structure.  It is typically hemispherical or semidomed in shape.|例句：The dome of the Capitol building is a magnificent sight. 国会大厦的圆顶景色壮观。|例句：Many ancient Roman buildings featured impressive domes. 许多古罗马建筑都拥有令人印象深刻的圆顶。|近义词：cupola, vault|反义词：flat roof
