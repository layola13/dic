self-damnations: /ˌsɛlf dæmˈneɪʃənz/ | n. |自我谴责，自我诅咒 | Acts of condemning oneself; expressions of self-reproach or self-condemnation.  It often implies a feeling of intense guilt or remorse. |例句：His self-damnations echoed in the empty room.  他的自我谴责在空荡的房间里回响。

例句：Her constant self-damnations were a burden she couldn't bear. 她持续不断的自我谴责是她无法承受的负担。


近义词：self-reproach, self-condemnation, self-criticism, self-recrimination

反义词：self-praise, self-congratulation, self-acceptance


词性: 名词 (plural)
