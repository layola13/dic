shoestrings:/ˈʃuːstrɪŋz/ | n. |鞋带；少量资金 |  Shoestrings are the strings used to tie shoes.  The phrase "on a shoestring" means with very little money. |例句：My old shoes need new shoestrings. 我的旧鞋需要新的鞋带。

例句：He started his business on a shoestring. 他白手起家创办了他的公司。

近义词：laces, minimal funds, paltry sum | 反义词：abundance, fortune, affluence
