kendo:/ˈkɛndoʊ/| n. |剑道|A Japanese martial art using a bamboo sword.  It emphasizes mental discipline, respect, and physical coordination.  |例句：He has been practicing kendo for over ten years. 他练习剑道已经超过十年了。|近义词：swordsmanship, martial art|反义词：None specifically.
