shilly-shallies:/ˈʃɪli ˈʃæli/ | v. |犹豫不决，畏首畏尾 |To be indecisive; to hesitate or waver in making a decision.  |He shilly-shallied for weeks before finally accepting the job. 他犹豫了几个星期才最终接受了这份工作。 | waver, hesitate, vacillate | decide, commit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shilly-shallies over every decision. = 他每个决定都犹豫不决。
They shilly-shally before making a choice. = 他们在做选择之前犹豫不决。

一般过去时:
She shilly-shallied about the offer for days. = 她对这个提议犹豫了几天。
He shilly-shallied all morning before finally leaving. = 他犹豫了整个上午才最终离开。

现在完成时:
I've been shilly-shallying about this for too long. = 我对这件事犹豫太久了。
They have shilly-shallied so much, nothing's been decided. = 他们犹豫了这么多，什么也没决定。

过去完成时:
He had shilly-shallied for months before making up his mind. = 他犹豫了几个月才下定决心。
She had shilly-shallied so much that the opportunity was lost. = 她犹豫了太多，机会错过了。

将来时:
I think he will shilly-shally until the last minute. = 我认为他会拖到最后一刻才决定。
They will likely shilly-shally over the terms of the contract. = 他们很可能会对合同条款犹豫不决。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't shilly-shallied, he would have gotten the promotion. = 如果他不犹豫，他就会得到晋升。
I wish I hadn't shilly-shallied; I missed a great opportunity. = 我真希望我当时没有犹豫，我错过了个好机会。

被动语态:  (Shilly-shallying is rarely used in the passive voice.)  The decision was delayed because of much shilly-shallying.  = 由于太多的犹豫，决定被推迟了。
The matter was stalled by the shilly-shallying of the committee. = 委员会的犹豫不决阻碍了事情的进展。

疑问句:
Is he shilly-shallying again? = 他又在犹豫不决了吗？
Are they still shilly-shallying over the details? = 他们还在为细节犹豫不决吗？

否定句:
He didn't shilly-shally; he made a quick decision. = 他没有犹豫，他很快做出了决定。
They didn't shilly-shally this time; they acted decisively. = 他们这次没有犹豫，他们果断地行动了。


PHRASE->
