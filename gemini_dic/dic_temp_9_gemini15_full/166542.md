presbyterium:/ˌprɛzbɪˈtɪəriəm/| n. |长老团；长老会；神学院| A presbyterium is a governing body of elders in certain Christian churches, particularly Presbyterian churches.  It's also sometimes used to refer to a seminary or theological college. |The presbyterium met to discuss the church's budget.  长老团开会讨论教会的预算。| synod, consistory, council | diocese, congregation |名词
