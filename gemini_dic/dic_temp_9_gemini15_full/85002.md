harakiri:/ˌhɑːrəˈkiːri/|/ˈhærəkiːri/| n. |切腹；自杀 |Ritual suicide by disembowelment in Japan, traditionally performed by samurai to atone for failure or to protest against injustice.  It is now rarely practiced. |The samurai committed harakiri to avoid capture.  武士为了避免被俘而切腹自杀。|suicide, self-destruction |life, survival |名词
