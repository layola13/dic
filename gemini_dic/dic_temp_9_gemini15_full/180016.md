refines:/rɪˈfaɪnz/| vt.|改进，精炼，使完善|To improve something by making small changes.|The sculptor refined his technique over many years.  雕塑家多年来一直在改进他的技法。|He refined his argument after hearing criticism. 他在听到批评后完善了他的论点。|improve, perfect, polish|coarsen, roughen|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She refines her writing style every day. = 她每天都在改进她的写作风格。
The company refines its processes to increase efficiency. = 公司正在改进流程以提高效率。

一般过去时:
He refined the design after receiving feedback. = 他在收到反馈后改进了设计。
They refined the oil before distribution. = 他们在分销前提炼了石油。

现在完成时:
The artist has refined his technique over decades. =这位艺术家几十年来一直在完善他的技法。
Scientists have refined the model to better predict the outcome. = 科学家们已经完善了模型，以便更好地预测结果。

过去完成时:
She had refined her presentation before giving it. =她在做演讲之前已经完善了她的演示文稿。
The engineers had refined the software before its release. =工程师们在软件发布之前已经对其进行了改进。

将来时:
They will refine the product based on customer reviews. =他们将根据客户评价改进产品。
We will refine our strategy to achieve better results. =我们将改进我们的策略以取得更好的结果。

虚拟语气:
If she refined her skills, she would get a promotion. =如果她提高了技能，她就会得到晋升。
I wish I had refined my essay before submitting it. =我希望我在提交论文之前能对它进行润色。

被动语态:
The process is refined to remove impurities. =该过程经过提炼以去除杂质。
The data was refined to ensure accuracy. =数据经过提炼以确保准确性。

疑问句:
How can we refine this process? =我们如何改进这个流程？
Will they refine their approach? =他们会改进他们的方法吗？

否定句:
He didn't refine his argument enough. =他并没有充分完善他的论点。
