satirizing:/ˈsætəraɪzɪŋ/| v.|讽刺，挖苦|The act of using humor, irony, exaggeration, or ridicule to expose and criticize folly or vice, particularly in contemporary politics and other topical issues.|例句：The cartoon satirized the politician's recent gaffe. 这幅漫画讽刺了这位政治家最近的失误。|例句：The writer satirized the hypocrisy of the wealthy elite.  这位作家讽刺了富裕精英的虚伪。|近义词：mocking, lampooning, ridiculing|反义词：praising, commending, flattering|动词
