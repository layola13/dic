decisions:/dɪˈsɪʒənz/ | n. | 决定，抉择 | The act of deciding or making up one's mind; a conclusion or resolution reached after consideration.  |  例句：The committee reached a decision after much deliberation.  委员会经过长时间的讨论后做出了决定。


例句：She made a wise decision to go to college. 她做了一个明智的决定去上大学。


近义词：determinations, resolutions, choices, conclusions | 反义词：indecisions, uncertainties


词源:  From Old French *decision, from Latin *dēcisiōn-, from dēcidere "to decide," from de- "off, away" + caedere "to cut, fall."  The sense development is from "cutting off" deliberation to "reaching a conclusion."
