parotid:/ˈpærəˌtɔɪd/| n. |腮腺的；腮腺|The parotid gland is the largest of the salivary glands. 腮腺是唾液腺中最大的腺体。|近义词：salivary (related to saliva)|反义词：None


英文解析:
Relating to or denoting the parotid gland, which is the largest of the salivary glands, situated below and in front of each ear.  The term can also refer to things located near the parotid gland.


例句:
The surgeon carefully avoided damaging the parotid duct during the operation. 外科医生在手术中小心地避免损伤腮腺导管。

He experienced parotid swelling after the infection. 感染后他经历了腮腺肿胀。


词源:
From Late Latin *parotis, from Greek parotis (παρωτίς), from para- ("beside") + ous, otos ("ear").  Literally meaning "beside the ear".
