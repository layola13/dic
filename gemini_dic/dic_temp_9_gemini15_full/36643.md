collectors:/kəˈlɛktərz/| n. |收藏家，收集者|People who collect items of a particular type.  Collectors often have a passion for their chosen area of collecting and may build extensive collections over time.  They may also participate in collector communities, sharing knowledge and trading items.|例句：Stamp collectors often attend conventions to buy and sell rare stamps.  邮票收藏家经常参加集会买卖珍稀邮票。

The museum showcases the impressive collection of a renowned art collector.  博物馆展示了一位著名艺术收藏家的令人印象深刻的收藏。|近义词：gatherers, enthusiasts, aficionados|反义词：dispersers, squanderers|名词
