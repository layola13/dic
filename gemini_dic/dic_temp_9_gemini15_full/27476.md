campanile:/ˌkæmpəˈniːliː/| n. |钟楼；高塔|A campanile is a bell tower, especially one freestanding from other buildings, typically associated with churches or cathedrals.  It's often tall and slender, and its primary purpose is to house bells. |The campanile of St. Mark's Square in Venice is a famous landmark.|近义词：bell tower, belfry|反义词：None specifically, but words implying a low or ground-level structure could be considered opposite in terms of height.
