outrivaled:/ˌaʊtˈraɪvəlzd/| v. |胜过；超过|To surpass or exceed in quality, ability, or achievement.|例句：His latest novel outrivaled all his previous works in both critical acclaim and commercial success.  他的最新小说在评论界赞誉和商业成功方面都超过了他以前的所有作品。

例句：Her performance outrivaled that of all the other contestants. 她的表现胜过所有其他参赛者。


近义词：surpass, excel, exceed, outdo, overshadow|反义词：be inferior to, be surpassed by |词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His company outrivals all others in the market. = 他的公司在市场上胜过所有其他公司。
This product outrivals its competitors in terms of quality. =这款产品在质量方面胜过其竞争对手。


一般过去时:
Last year's sales outrivaled those of the previous year. =去年的销量超过了前一年。
The team outrivaled their opponents in the final game. =该队在决赛中胜过了对手。


现在完成时:
Our team has outrivaled all others in the competition. =我们的团队在比赛中胜过了所有其他团队。
She has outrivaled her expectations in her career. =她在事业上超越了自己的预期。


过去完成时:
By the end of the project, he had outrivaled all his colleagues in productivity. =到项目结束时，他在生产力方面已经超过了所有同事。
They had outrivaled their rivals in the race for market share before the merger. =在合并之前，他们在争夺市场份额的竞争中已经超越了竞争对手。


将来时:
Their new product will outrival all existing models on the market. =他们的新产品将胜过市场上所有现有的型号。
We believe our strategy will outrival the competitors' plans. =我们相信我们的策略将胜过竞争对手的计划。


虚拟语气:
If they had invested more, they would have outrivaled their competitors. =如果他们投资更多，他们就会胜过竞争对手。
I wish I had outrivaled him in the competition. =我希望我在比赛中能胜过他。


被动语态:
His work was outrivaled by a younger generation of artists. =他的作品被年轻一代艺术家超越了。
Their performance was outrivaled only by the champions. =他们的表现仅次于冠军。


疑问句:
Does her talent outrival that of her siblings? =她的天赋是否超过她的兄弟姐妹？
Will their new technology outrival current standards? =他们的新技术能否超越目前的标准？


否定句:
His efforts did not outrival those of his team members. =他的努力并没有超过他的团队成员。
The company's marketing campaign failed to outrival its competitors'. =公司的营销活动未能超越竞争对手。
