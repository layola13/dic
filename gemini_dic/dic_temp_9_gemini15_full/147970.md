overrestraint: /ˌoʊvərɪˈstreɪnt/ | n. |过度克制；抑制过度 |  Excessive restraint or control; suppression of natural feelings or impulses.  It often implies a negative consequence, suggesting that the restraint is too much and hinders natural expression or healthy behavior. |

例句：

His overrestraint in expressing his emotions led to misunderstandings.  他的过度克制情感导致了误解。

The overrestraint of the economy stifled innovation and growth. 经济的过度控制扼杀了创新和增长。


近义词： repression, suppression, inhibition, constraint, stiffness


反义词：  abandon, freedom, release, spontaneity, exuberance


词性：名词
