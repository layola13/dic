jetsam:/ˈdʒɛtsəm/| n.|漂流物，（船难后）抛弃的货物|Goods or materials that have been thrown overboard from a ship, especially in an emergency, and found washed ashore.  It specifically refers to items intentionally jettisoned to lighten the ship. |The jetsam from the wrecked ship washed up on the shore.  这艘沉船的漂流物冲上了岸。|flotsam, wreckage|salvage|名词
