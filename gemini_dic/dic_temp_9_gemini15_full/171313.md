pub:/pʌb/ | n. | 酒馆，酒吧 | A pub is a place that serves alcoholic drinks and often food, typically in a relaxed, informal atmosphere.  It's a common social gathering place, especially in the UK and Ireland. | Example:  We met at the pub for a drink after work. 我们下班后在酒吧见面喝了一杯。 | 近义词：bar, tavern, inn | 反义词：无
