malting:/ˈmɔːltɪŋ/| n. v.|麦芽制作；发芽|The process of steeping grains in water to allow them to germinate and convert starches into sugars, used in brewing and distilling.|例句：The malting process is crucial for beer production. 麦芽制作过程对啤酒生产至关重要。

例句：The barley is undergoing malting before brewing. 大麦在酿造前正在进行麦芽化处理。


近义词：germination, brewing (in context of beer making)
反义词：None directly, but  "unmalted" could be considered an antonym.
词性：Noun, Verb
