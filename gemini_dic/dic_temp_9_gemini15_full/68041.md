factoryless:/ˈfæktəriless/| adj.|无工厂的，不依赖工厂的|Lacking factories or operating without factories.  This term often refers to business models that outsource manufacturing or utilize distributed production networks.|例句：The company boasts a factoryless manufacturing model, relying entirely on a network of independent contractors. 这家公司拥有一个无工厂的制造模式，完全依靠独立承包商的网络。|例句：The rise of factoryless production has transformed many industries. 无工厂生产的兴起改变了许多行业。|近义词：decentralized, distributed, virtual|反义词：factory-based, centralized
