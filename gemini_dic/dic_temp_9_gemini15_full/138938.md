nutter:/ˈnʌtər/| n. |疯子，傻瓜，怪人 |A nutter is a slang term for someone considered crazy, foolish, or eccentric.  The term is often used informally and can be considered offensive. |He's a complete nutter; he drives a car without a seatbelt.|近义词：lunatic, maniac, idiot, goofball |反义词：sane, sensible, rational |名词
