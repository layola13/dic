euchloric:/juːˈklɔːrɪk/| adj.|优氯的；含氯的|Relating to or containing chlorine, especially in a beneficial or healthy way.  The term often implies a beneficial effect of chlorine, in contrast to the potentially harmful effects of excessive or uncontrolled chlorine.|The  euchloric solution helped disinfect the water supply.  优氯溶液有助于消毒供水。|近义词：chlorinated, disinfected|反义词：aseptic (in the sense of lacking beneficial chlorine)
