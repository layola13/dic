nonpast:/ˈnɑːnpæst/| adj.|非过去的，现在时的|The term "nonpast" refers to verb tenses that do not explicitly indicate past time.  It encompasses the present tense, future tense, and sometimes the present perfect tense depending on the grammatical context.  It contrasts with "past tense".|The nonpast tense is used to describe actions happening now, habitually, or in the future.|

He eats breakfast every morning. = 他每天早上吃早餐。(Present Tense)

She will travel to Europe next year. = 她明年将去欧洲旅行。(Future Tense)


近义词：present, future (in certain contexts) |反义词：past
