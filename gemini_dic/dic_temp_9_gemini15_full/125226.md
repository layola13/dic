multicultural:/ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəl/| adj. |多元文化的；多种文化的 |  Relating to or representing several different cultures.  It describes a society, institution, or community that embraces the coexistence of different cultures. |例句：Our school is proud of its multicultural environment.  我们的学校以其多元文化环境而自豪。

例句：The city boasts a rich and vibrant multicultural heritage. 这个城市拥有丰富多彩的多元文化遗产。


近义词：multiethnic, diverse, cosmopolitan

反义词：monocultural, homogeneous
