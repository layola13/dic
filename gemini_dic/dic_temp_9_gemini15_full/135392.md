nonpermanently:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːrmənəntli/ | adv. |非永久地，暂时地 |  Not permanently; for a limited time.  The adjective "nonpermanent" indicates something that is not permanent, lasting, or fixed.  The adverbial form adds the sense of manner or time. | 例句：The exhibit is nonpermanently located in the west wing.  展览暂时位于西翼。

The building was nonpermanently decorated for the festival.  建筑物为节日进行了临时的装饰。


近义词：temporarily, provisionally, transiently,interimly

反义词：permanently, perpetually, enduringly


词性:副词
