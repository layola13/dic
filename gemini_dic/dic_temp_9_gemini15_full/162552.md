prealloting: /ˌpriːˈælɒtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先分配；预先拨给 |  The act of allocating something in advance.  It implies assigning resources, responsibilities, or tasks before they are actually needed or used.  This often involves planning and preparation to ensure efficient use of resources. | Example: The company is prealloting funds for the new project.  这家公司正在为新项目预先拨款。


The company is prealloting vacation time for all employees next year. 公司正在为所有员工预先分配明年的休假时间。


近义词: allocating in advance, assigning beforehand, preassigning


反义词: post-allocating, allocating later


词性: Verb
