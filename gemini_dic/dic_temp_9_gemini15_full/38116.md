comprehender:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛndə(r)/| n. |理解者，领悟者 |A person who comprehends; one who understands.  |例句：He is a keen comprehender of complex issues. 他是一个善于理解复杂问题的人。 |近义词：understander, interpreter |反义词：misunderstander,ignoramus

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The comprehender grasps the meaning immediately. = 理解者立刻抓住了意思。
She comprehends the nuances of the poem. = 她理解这首诗的细微之处。

一般过去时:
The audience comprehended the speaker's message. = 听众理解了演讲者的信息。
He comprehended the difficult concept after much study. = 他经过长时间的学习后理解了这个复杂的概念。

现在完成时:
I have comprehended the main points of the lecture. = 我已经理解了讲座的主要内容。
She has comprehended the implications of the decision. = 她已经理解了这个决定的含义。

过去完成时:
By the end of the book, I had comprehended the author's intentions. = 读完这本书后，我已经理解了作者的意图。
They had comprehended the danger before it was too late. = 在为时已晚之前，他们已经意识到了危险。

将来时:
I will comprehend the subject matter once I've studied it further. =  一旦我进一步学习，我就会理解这个主题。
She will comprehend the complexities of the situation with time. =  随着时间的推移，她会理解情况的复杂性。

虚拟语气:
If I comprehended this better, I would explain it more clearly. = 如果我更好地理解这一点，我会更清楚地解释它。
I wish I comprehended the meaning of his cryptic message. = 我真希望我能理解他那神秘的信息的意思。

被动语态:
The instructions were easily comprehended by the students. = 学生们很容易理解这些说明。
The poem's deeper meaning is not easily comprehended. = 这首诗更深层次的含义不容易理解。

疑问句:
Do you comprehend the gravity of the situation? = 你理解情况的严重性吗？
Does he comprehend the instructions? = 他理解这些指示吗？

否定句:
I do not comprehend your reasoning. = 我不明白你的推理。
He did not comprehend the subtleties of the argument. = 他没有理解论证的细微之处。
