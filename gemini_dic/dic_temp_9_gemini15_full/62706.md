engineers:/ˌɛnˈdʒɪnɪrz/ | n. |工程师们 |People who design, build, or maintain engines, machines, or structures.  Engineers apply scientific and mathematical principles to solve practical problems. |例句：The engineers are working on a new bridge design. 工程师们正在设计一座新的桥梁。|例句：Civil engineers are responsible for the design and construction of infrastructure. 土木工程师负责基础设施的设计和建设。|近义词：technicians, designers, architects |反义词：None  |名词
