cheongsams:/tʃɒŋˈsæmz/ | n. | 旗袍 | A traditional Vietnamese dress, a close-fitting, ankle-length gown with high collar and side slits.  It's often made of silk or other luxurious materials and is known for its elegant and form-fitting design. | The model looked stunning in her cheongsam.  这位模特穿着旗袍看起来惊艳。  |  qipao (traditional Chinese term),  traditional dress |  None | n.
