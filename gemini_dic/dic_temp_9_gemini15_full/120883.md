misbrewing:/ˌmɪsˈbruːɪŋ/| n.|酿造错误；酿造失败；错误的酿造|The act of brewing something incorrectly, resulting in a flawed or undesirable product.  This could refer to beer, coffee, or any other brewed substance.  It implies a mistake in the process, leading to a subpar result.|例句：The misbrewing of the batch resulted in a sour, undrinkable beer. = 这批啤酒酿造错误导致口感酸涩，无法饮用。

The misbrewing of the coffee led to a bitter and unpleasant taste. = 咖啡酿造错误导致苦涩难咽。|近义词：mismaking, botching, spoiling|反义词：perfect brewing, successful brewing
