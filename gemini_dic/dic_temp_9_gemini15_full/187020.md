ritratto:/rɪˈtrætɔː/ | n. | 肖像，画像 | A portrait is a painting, drawing, photograph, or other artistic representation of a person, especially one depicting the face.  It aims to capture the likeness and often the personality of the subject. | Example: The artist painted a stunning ritratto of the queen.  艺术家为女王画了一幅令人惊叹的肖像。The family commissioned a ritratto of their newborn baby.  这个家庭委托为他们的新生儿画了一幅肖像。 |近义词：portrait, likeness, effigy |反义词：N/A | 名词
