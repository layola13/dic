imagenet:/ˈɪmɪdʒˌnɛt/ | n. |图像网络，ImageNet |A large-scale hierarchical image database containing millions of images, used for training and benchmarking computer vision models.  It's organized according to the WordNet hierarchy, where each node of the hierarchy is depicted by hundreds and thousands of images. |Example:  Researchers used ImageNet to train their object recognition algorithm.  研究人员使用ImageNet训练他们的物体识别算法。|近义词：image database, visual data set |反义词：None |名词
