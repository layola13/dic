preprofesses:/ˌpriːprəˈfɛsɪz/| v.|公开宣称；假装；虚伪地声称 |To claim or profess publicly, often insincerely or falsely.  |例句：He preprofesses a deep love for art, yet he never visits museums. 他公开宣称热爱艺术，但却从未去过博物馆。|例句：She preprofesses her innocence, but the evidence suggests otherwise. 她公开宣称自己无罪，但证据表明并非如此。|近义词：pretend, feign, affect, simulate |反义词：honestly admit, confess, openly acknowledge |动词
