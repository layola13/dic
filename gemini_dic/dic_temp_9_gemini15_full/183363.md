reproducibles:/ˌriːprəˈdjuːsəblz/ | n. | 可复制的材料；可再现的结果 | Materials or results that can be easily replicated or reproduced.  The term often refers to experimental results that can be independently verified by others. | Example:  The experiment yielded reproducible results, confirming the initial hypothesis.  实验结果可重复，证实了最初的假设。  |近义词：replicable, repeatable | 反义词：unreproducible, irreproducible | 名词
