innovates:/ˈɪnəveɪts/| vt.|创新，革新|To introduce something new; to make changes or improvements to something.|例句：The company constantly innovates to stay ahead of its competitors.  这家公司不断创新以保持其竞争优势。|例句：She innovated a new teaching method. 她创新了一种新的教学方法。|近义词：create, invent, reform, pioneer |反义词：stagnate, maintain the status quo


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company innovates constantly. = 这家公司不断创新。
He innovates in his approach to problem-solving. = 他在解决问题的方法上不断创新。

一般过去时:
They innovated a new product last year. = 他们去年创新了一种新产品。
The artist innovated his painting style. = 这位艺术家创新了他的绘画风格。

现在完成时:
She has innovated several successful products. = 她已经创新了几种成功的产品。
The company has innovated its marketing strategy. = 这家公司已经创新了它的营销策略。

过去完成时:
They had innovated the design before the deadline. = 他们早在截止日期之前就对设计进行了创新。
The scientist had innovated the process several times. = 科学家已经多次改进这个过程。

将来时:
We will innovate more efficient solutions. = 我们将创新更有效的解决方案。
The team will innovate new technologies for the project. = 团队将为该项目创新新的技术。

虚拟语气:
If they innovated their business model, they might succeed. = 如果他们创新他们的商业模式，他们可能会成功。
I wish they innovated a more user-friendly interface. = 我希望他们能设计一个更友好的用户界面。

被动语态:
New techniques were innovated by the researchers. = 新技术是由研究人员创新的。
A new system is being innovated by the engineering team. = 一个新的系统正在由工程团队创新。

疑问句:
Does the company innovate frequently? = 这家公司经常创新吗？
Have they innovated any new features recently? = 他们最近创新了任何新功能吗？

否定句:
The company did not innovate enough. = 这家公司创新不足。
They haven't innovated anything significant this year. = 他们今年没有任何重大创新。


PHRASE->
