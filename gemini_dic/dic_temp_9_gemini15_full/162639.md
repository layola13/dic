preapproves:/ˌpriːəˈpruːvz/ | vt. |预先批准；预先核准 | To approve something in advance.  |例句：The bank preapproved her mortgage application. 银行预先批准了她的抵押贷款申请。

The lender preapproved my loan application, saving me time later in the process. 贷款机构预先批准了我的贷款申请，为我节省了后续流程的时间。


近义词：approve beforehand, authorize in advance | 反义词：reject, disapprove


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The bank preapproves many loan applications daily. = 银行每天预先批准许多贷款申请。
He preapproves all purchase orders over $1000. 他预先批准所有超过1000美元的采购订单。


一般过去时:
The committee preapproved the project last week. = 委员会上周预先批准了该项目。
She preapproved the expense report before the trip.  她在旅行前预先批准了费用报告。


现在完成时:
They have preapproved the budget for the next fiscal year. = 他们已经预先批准了下一财政年度的预算。
The board has preapproved several important initiatives. 董事会已经预先批准了几项重要举措。


过去完成时:
The manager had preapproved the contract before the client signed it. = 在客户签署合同之前，经理已经预先批准了它。
We had preapproved the design changes before presenting them to the client. 我们在向客户展示之前已经预先批准了设计更改。


将来时:
The company will preapprove your application within 24 hours. = 公司将在24小时内预先批准您的申请。
He will preapprove the necessary funding next week. 他将在下周预先批准必要的资金。


虚拟语气:
If the bank preapproved my loan, I would buy the house. = 如果银行预先批准我的贷款，我就会买下房子。
I wish the committee had preapproved the proposal earlier. 我希望委员会早点预先批准该提案。


被动语态:
The application was preapproved by the department head. = 该申请已获得部门主管的预先批准。
The budget will be preapproved by the finance committee. 预算将由财务委员会预先批准。


疑问句:
Has the loan been preapproved yet? = 贷款已经预先批准了吗？
Will the project be preapproved before we start? 项目在我们开始之前会预先批准吗？


否定句:
The company did not preapprove the expense. = 公司没有预先批准这笔费用。
They haven't preapproved the changes to the schedule.  他们还没有预先批准时间安排的更改。


PHRASE->

preapprove a budget = 预先批准预算
preapprove a project = 预先批准项目

preapprove a purchase order = 预先批准采购订单

词源:

pre- (前缀，表示“在……之前”) + approve (动词，表示“批准”)
