get a handle on: /ɡɛt ə ˈhændəl ɒn/ | phrasal verb | 理解；掌握；控制 | To gain understanding or control of something.  | 例句：I'm trying to get a handle on this new software. 我正在努力掌握这套新软件。

例句：

It took me a while to get a handle on the new procedures.  我花了一段时间才掌握新的程序。


We need to get a handle on the rising costs. 我们需要控制不断上涨的成本。


近义词：understand, grasp, manage, control, comprehend

反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret, lose control of


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I get a handle on the situation every day. = 我每天都能掌握局势。
They get a handle on the project’s progress regularly. = 他们定期掌握项目的进度。

一般过去时:
She got a handle on her emotions after a few deep breaths. = 她深呼吸几次后控制住了情绪。

过去完成时:

He had gotten a handle on his fear of public speaking after years of practice. = 通过多年的练习，他已经克服了害怕公开演讲的恐惧。

将来时:

They will get a handle on the new technology within a month. = 他们将在一个月内掌握这项新技术。

虚拟语气:
