crumpling:/ˈkrʌmpəlɪŋ/| v.|褶皱；弄皱；坍塌；衰弱 |To crush or become crushed into wrinkles or folds.  The process of something collapsing or falling down.  |例句：The old newspaper lay crumpled on the floor.  旧报纸皱巴巴地躺在地板上。

The accident caused the car to crumple like an accordion. 事故导致汽车像手风琴一样坍塌。


近义词：crushing, wrinkling, collapsing, crumbling

反义词：smoothing, straightening, unfolding
