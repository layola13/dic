mullers:/ˈmʌlərz/| n.|研磨器；研钵；穆勒氏(复数)|A muller is a tool used for grinding or pulverizing materials, typically in a circular motion.  It can refer to different types of grinding implements, from simple hand-held tools to larger, industrial machines. The term often appears in the context of mining, ceramics, and the processing of pigments.|例句：The artist used mullers to grind the pigments for her paints.  艺术家用研磨器研磨颜料作画。 The miners used mullers to crush the ore. 矿工用研磨器粉碎矿石。|近义词：grinder, crusher, pulverizer|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be to mix or combine without grinding.|名词
