loped:/lɔːpt/| v.| 慢跑；大步跑；（想法等）迅速发展 | to run at a steady, fairly fast pace, lifting the knees high |The horse loped gracefully across the field. 这匹马优雅地慢跑过田野。 The rumor loped through the town. 谣言迅速传遍了整个城镇。|cantered, trotted, galloped|walked, crawled|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lopes to work every morning. = 他每天早上慢跑上班。
The dog lopes happily through the park. = 狗快乐地慢跑穿过公园。

一般过去时:

词源:
The word "loped" comes from the Middle English word "loppen," meaning "to leap" or "to hop."  This, in turn, has roots in Old English and Germanic languages with similar meanings relating to leaping or bounding movement.
