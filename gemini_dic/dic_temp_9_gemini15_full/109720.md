levelheaded: /ˈlɛvəlˈhɛdəd/ | adj. | 沉着冷静的；头脑清醒的 |  Calm and sensible; having or showing good judgment.  | Example:  She remained levelheaded throughout the crisis. 她整个危机期间都保持着冷静。

Example: He’s a levelheaded young man and will make a good manager. 他是个头脑清醒的年轻人，会成为一个好经理。


近义词: calm, composed, rational, sensible, balanced, unflappable

反义词: hysterical, emotional, irrational, impetuous, flighty, unstable


词源:  The word combines "level" (meaning even, balanced, or steady) and "headed" (referring to the head, the seat of intellect and reason).  It emerged as a descriptive adjective in the late 19th century.
