dextrous: /ˈdekstrəs/ | adj. |灵巧的，熟练的 | skillful and clever, especially in the use of hands or body.  |He is dextrous with his hands, able to fix almost anything. 他手很巧，几乎什么都能修。|近义词：skillful, adept, nimble, deft |反义词：clumsy, awkward, inept


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is dextrous in his work. = 他工作很熟练。
She is dextrous with a needle and thread. = 她针线活儿很灵巧。


一般过去时:
The surgeon was dextrous during the operation. = 这位外科医生在手术中很熟练。
He was dextrous enough to open the lock without a key. = 他很灵巧，不用钥匙就能打开锁。


现在完成时:
She has been dextrous at knitting since childhood. = 她从小就擅长织毛衣。
The artist has become remarkably dextrous with his brush. =这位艺术家用画笔已经变得非常熟练了。


过去完成时:
