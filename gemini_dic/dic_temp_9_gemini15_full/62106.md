enchantments:/ɪnˈtʃæntmənts/ | n. |魔法；魅力；迷人的事物 |Enchantments are magical spells or charms used to produce a desired effect.  They can also refer to something captivating or delightful.|例句：The old woman's enchantments protected the village from harm. 老妇人的魔法保护了村庄免受伤害。

The garden was full of enchantments; the flowers, the birdsong, the gentle breeze, all combined to create a magical atmosphere. 花园充满了魅力；鲜花、鸟鸣、微风，所有的一切都融合在一起，创造出一种神奇的氛围。|近义词：spells, charms, magic, allure, fascination |反义词：disenchantments, repulsiveness, ugliness |名词
