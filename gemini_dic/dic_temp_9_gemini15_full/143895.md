outpower:/ˌaʊtˈpaʊər/| vt.|胜过；功率超过|To surpass in power or strength.  To exceed in power output.|例句：The new engine outpowers its predecessor. 新引擎的功率超过了它的前代产品。

例句：The larger company easily outpowered the smaller startup. 大公司轻松地压倒了规模较小的初创公司。


近义词：outperform, surpass, exceed, overshadow|反义词：be outpowered by, be inferior to|动词
