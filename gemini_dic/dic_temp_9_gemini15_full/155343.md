pharmacognosy:/ˌfɑːrməˈkɒɡnəsi/| n. |药物鉴定学；生药学|The study of the physical, chemical, biochemical, and biological properties of drugs, especially those derived from natural sources (plants, animals, and minerals).  Pharmacognosy involves the identification, isolation, and characterization of active compounds in these natural sources and their applications in medicine and other fields.|例句：The course in pharmacognosy covered the identification of various medicinal plants and their active constituents.  这门药物鉴定学课程涵盖了各种药用植物及其有效成分的鉴定。|近义词：ethnopharmacology, materia medica|反义词：N/A
