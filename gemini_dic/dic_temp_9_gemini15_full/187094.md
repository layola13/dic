rivets:/ˈrɪvɪts/| n. v.|铆钉；使固定|A rivet is a metal pin used to join pieces of metal together.  It is usually inserted through holes in the pieces and then hammered or pressed so that the end spreads out, holding the pieces firmly together. The verb form means to fasten something with rivets.|例句：The mechanic used rivets to secure the metal plates. 技工用铆钉固定金属板。

The bridge was reinforced with numerous rivets.  这座桥用许多铆钉加固。|近义词：bolt, fastener|反义词：unfasten, loosen|名词，动词
