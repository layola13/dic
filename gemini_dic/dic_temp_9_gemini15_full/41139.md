/kɒpts/| n. |科普特人；科普特教会；科普特语| Copts are the indigenous Christian population of Egypt.  They are known for their ancient Christian heritage and their distinct Coptic Orthodox Church.  The term also refers to their language and culture.|The Copts have a rich history dating back to the early days of Christianity.|近义词：Egyptian Christians|反义词：无
