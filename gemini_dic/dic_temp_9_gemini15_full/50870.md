desensitising: /ˌdiːˈsɛnsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 脱敏；使麻木；使不敏感 | The process of reducing or eliminating sensitivity to a stimulus, often through repeated exposure.  This can refer to physical sensations, emotional responses, or even moral considerations. | 例句：The constant exposure to violence on television is desensitising viewers to its horrors.  持续的电视暴力画面正在使观众对其恐怖之处麻木。


例句：Prolonged exposure to trauma can desensitise people to the suffering of others. 长时间的创伤经历可能会让人对别人的痛苦麻木不仁。


近义词：numbing, deadening, dulling


反义词：sensitizing, heightening, sharpening
