diminuendo:/dɪˌmɪnjuˈɛndoʊ/| n.|渐弱；减弱|A gradual decrease in loudness or intensity, especially in music.|例句：The orchestra played a beautiful diminuendo at the end of the movement.  乐队在乐章结尾演奏了一个优美的渐弱。|近义词：decrescendo, diminuzione|反义词：crescendo
