insatiately:/ɪnˈseɪʃiətli/| adv.|不知足地；贪得无厌地 |  Unsatisfied; unable to be satisfied; ravenously.  It describes an action or state characterized by an inability to be satisfied. |例句：He ate insatiately, devouring everything on the plate. 他吃得不知满足，把盘子里的东西都吃光了。 He consumed information insatiately. 他贪婪地吸收信息。|近义词：ravenously, greedily, voraciously, endlessly|反义词：sparingly, moderately, contentedly|副词
