overglad:/ˈoʊvərɡlæd/ | adj. | 过于高兴的，欣喜若狂的 | Excessively or overly glad; characterized by excessive happiness or joy.  This word is rarely used in modern English and is considered somewhat archaic or informal.  It implies a degree of happiness that is beyond the norm, possibly to the point of being excessive or slightly inappropriate. | 例句：He was overglad to see his long-lost friend. = 他见到失散多年的朋友欣喜若狂。  She was overglad about the news, almost giddy with excitement. = 她听到这个消息后欣喜若狂，几乎兴奋得晕头转向。 | 近义词：ecstatic, overjoyed, delighted, thrilled, exuberant | 反义词：dejected, unhappy, sad, despondent | 形容词
