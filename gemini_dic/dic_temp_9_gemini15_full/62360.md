indictment:/ɪnˈdaɪtmənt/| n. |控告；起诉书 |A formal accusation of a crime.  An indictment is a legal document that formally charges someone with a crime. It's issued by a grand jury after they have heard evidence and decided there is enough to proceed to trial.|例句：The grand jury returned an indictment against the former CEO. 大陪审团对前任首席执行官提出了起诉。


The indictment alleged fraud and conspiracy. 起诉书指控诈骗和共谋。|近义词：accusation, charge, arraignment |反义词：acquittal, exoneration
