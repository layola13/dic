nonlegume:/ˈnɑːnˌliːɡjuːm/| n. |非豆科植物|A plant that is not a legume; a plant that does not belong to the family Fabaceae (Leguminosae).  Nonlegumes lack the characteristic features of legumes, such as the production of pods containing seeds.|Example: Corn is a nonlegume, unlike soybeans. 玉米是非豆科植物，不像大豆。  Many nonlegume crops are important sources of food and fiber. 许多非豆科作物是重要的食物和纤维来源。|近义词：non-legume plant,  non-pulsed plant|反义词：legume
