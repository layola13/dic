laim:/kleɪm/| vt. n.|声称；索赔；要求|to state that something is true or is a fact, although you cannot prove it; to ask for something of value because you believe it is rightfully yours |例句：He claimed to have seen a UFO. 他声称看到过不明飞行物。|例句：She claimed compensation for her injuries. 她索赔以弥补她的伤亡。|近义词：assert, allege, demand|反义词：deny, refute


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He claims he is innocent. = 他声称自己是无辜的。
She claims the reward. = 她声称要奖赏。

一般过去时:
They claimed the land as their own. = 他们宣称这块土地是他们的。
He claimed to have been there. = 他声称自己曾经去过那里。

现在完成时:
She has claimed her prize. = 她已经领取了她的奖品。
They have claimed responsibility for the attack. = 他们已经对袭击事件负责。

过去完成时:
He had claimed the throne before the war. = 他在战争爆发前就已称王。
She had claimed she didn't know. = 她之前声称她不知道。

将来时:
They will claim their inheritance. = 他们将会继承遗产。
He will claim damages for the accident. = 他将就事故索赔。

虚拟语气:
If he claimed it, I would believe him. = 如果他声称那样，我会相信他。
I wish he hadn't claimed that. = 我希望他没有那样声称。

被动语态:
The land was claimed by the settlers. = 这块土地被定居者占领了。
The prize was claimed by several people. = 奖品被几个人认领了。

疑问句:
Does he claim to be a doctor? = 他声称自己是医生吗？
What does she claim to have seen? = 她声称看到了什么？

否定句:
He didn't claim to know anything. = 他不声称知道任何事情。
She didn't claim the money. = 她没有索要那笔钱。


PHRASE->
lay claim to = 宣称拥有
stake a claim = 提出要求
file a claim = 提出索赔
false claim = 虚假主张
a valid claim = 有效的索赔
