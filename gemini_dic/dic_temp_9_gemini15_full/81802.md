grenading:/ɡrɪˈneɪdɪŋ/| n.|榴弹攻击；投掷榴弹|The act of throwing or launching grenades.  It can refer to the action itself or the effect of multiple grenades being thrown.|例句：The enemy's grenading caused significant casualties.敌人的榴弹攻击造成了重大伤亡。
例句：The sound of continuous grenading filled the air.  持续不断的榴弹投掷声充满了空气。|近义词：bombardment, shelling|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with peaceful actions.|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The soldiers are grenading the enemy positions. = 士兵们正在向敌方阵地投掷榴弹。
He is grenading the building. = 他正在向建筑物投掷榴弹。


一般过去时:
They grenaded the bridge before retreating. = 他们撤退前炸毁了桥梁。
