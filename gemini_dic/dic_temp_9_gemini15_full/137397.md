nontensility: /ˌnɒnˈtɛnsɪlɪti/ | n. |非张力性，不可伸展性 | The inability of a material to be stretched or extended.  It refers to the resistance of a material to deformation under tensile stress.  This property is opposite to elasticity or extensibility. |Example: The nontensility of the rope made it unsuitable for climbing. 这根绳子的不可伸展性使其不适合攀登。|近义词：inextensibility, inelasticity |反义词：extensibility, elasticity |名词
