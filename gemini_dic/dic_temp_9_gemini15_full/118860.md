/ˈmɛtəˌplæst/| n. |变质作用；移变|The alteration of a tissue or structure to a different type, often as a result of injury or disease.  It involves the replacement of one type of cell or tissue with another. This process is often seen in response to injury or inflammation.|Example: Metaplasia of the bronchial epithelium is a common finding in smokers.|近义词：transformation, alteration|反义词：differentiation (in some contexts)


USAGE->

This word is primarily a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations like "hello."  The following examples illustrate its usage in different sentence structures:

In a declarative sentence:
Bronchial metaplasia is a serious condition. = 支气管化生是一种严重的疾病。
The pathologist observed significant metaplasia in the tissue sample. = 病理学家在组织样本中观察到明显的变质作用。


In an interrogative sentence:
Is this metaplasia reversible? = 这种变质作用是可逆的吗？
What type of metaplasia is present in this case? = 在这种情况下存在哪种类型的变质作用？


In a passive sentence:
The metaplasia was caused by chronic inflammation. = 变质作用是由慢性炎症引起的。
The changes were identified as squamous metaplasia. = 这些变化被确定为鳞状上皮化生。
