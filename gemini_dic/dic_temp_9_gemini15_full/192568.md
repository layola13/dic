sculls:/skʌlz/| n.|桨;头盖骨|Pairs of oars used for propelling a boat.  The bony framework of the head. |例句：He expertly handled the sculls, propelling the boat swiftly across the lake. 他熟练地操控着桨，迅速地将船划过湖面。

例句：The archaeologist carefully examined the ancient sculls found in the cave. 考古学家仔细检查了在洞穴中发现的古代头盖骨。

近义词：oars (for the boat propelling meaning), cranium (for the skull meaning)
反义词：无
