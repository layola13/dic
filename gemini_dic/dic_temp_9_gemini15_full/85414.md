harvested:/ˈhɑːr.vɛs.tɪd/| v.|收获的，收割的|To gather a crop from (a field, etc.).  It is the past participle of the verb "harvest".|例句：The farmers harvested the wheat last week.  农民上周收割了小麦。

例句：The company harvested a large amount of data from its users.  这家公司从用户那里收集了大量数据。


近义词：gathered, reaped, gathered in


反义词：planted, sowed


词性:动词(过去分词)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They harvest the crops every autumn. = 他们每年秋天收割庄稼。
Farmers harvest rice in the fall. = 农民在秋天收割稻米。

一般过去时:
We harvested all the apples yesterday. = 我们昨天收割了所有的苹果。
They harvested the corn before the rain came. = 他们在雨来之前收割了玉米。

现在完成时:
The farmers have harvested most of the wheat. = 农民已经收割了大部分小麦。
We have harvested a bountiful crop this year. = 今年我们收获了丰盛的庄稼。

过去完成时:
They had harvested the rice before the typhoon hit. = 台风来袭之前，他们已经收割了稻米。
The farmers had harvested the grapes before the frost arrived. = 霜冻来临之前，农民们已经收割了葡萄。

将来时:
We will harvest the tomatoes next week. = 我们下周将收割西红柿。
The farmers will harvest the barley in July. = 农民们将在7月收割大麦。

虚拟语气:
If we harvested earlier, we would have a better yield. = 如果我们早点收割，产量会更高。
I wish we had harvested the corn before the storm. = 我真希望我们在暴风雨来临之前收割了玉米。

被动语态:
The wheat was harvested by machines. = 小麦是用机器收割的。
The crops are being harvested at the moment. = 庄稼正在收割中。

疑问句:
Have they harvested the grapes yet? = 他们已经收割葡萄了吗？
When will the corn be harvested? = 玉米什么时候收割？

否定句:
They haven't harvested the rice yet. = 他们还没收割稻米。
We didn't harvest the apples because of the rain. = 因为下雨，我们没有收割苹果。
