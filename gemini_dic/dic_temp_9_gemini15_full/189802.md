salvaged:/ˈsæl.vɪdʒd/| v. |打捞；抢救；挽救；挽回|To save something or someone from being destroyed or lost.  The verb "salvage" implies rescuing something from a dangerous or difficult situation, often involving significant effort and overcoming obstacles.|例句：Firefighters salvaged several valuable paintings from the burning building. 消防队员从燃烧的建筑物中抢救了几幅珍贵的画作。

The crew salvaged the wrecked ship. 船员们打捞了失事的船只。|近义词：rescue, recover, retrieve, save |反义词：destroy, lose, ruin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They salvage wrecked ships. = 他们打捞失事的船只。
We salvage old furniture. = 我们修复旧家具。

一般过去时:
She salvaged her reputation. = 她挽回了自己的声誉。
The divers salvaged the sunken treasure. = 潜水员打捞了沉船宝藏。

现在完成时:
They have salvaged many items from the flood. = 他们从洪水中抢救出了许多物品。
We have salvaged the project from disaster. = 我们从灾难中挽救了这个项目。

过去完成时:
They had salvaged the data before the computer crashed. = 电脑崩溃之前，他们已经抢救了数据。
She had salvaged a few belongings from the fire. = 她从火灾中抢救出了一些财物。

将来时:
They will salvage the damaged car. = 他们将修理受损的汽车。
We will salvage what we can from the situation. = 我们将尽力挽回局面。

虚拟语气:
If we had acted sooner, we could have salvaged the situation. = 如果我们早点行动，就能挽回局面。
I wish I had salvaged that old photo album. = 我真希望我当时保存了那本旧相册。

被动语态:
The sunken treasure was salvaged by divers. = 沉船宝藏被潜水员打捞了。
The damaged building was salvaged and renovated. = 受损的建筑物被修复和翻新。

疑问句:
Can we salvage the situation? = 我们能挽回局面吗？
Were any valuable items salvaged from the fire? = 从火灾中抢救出了任何贵重物品吗？

否定句:
They couldn't salvage the damaged engine. = 他们无法修复损坏的发动机。
We failed to salvage the relationship. = 我们没能挽回这段关系。


PHRASE->
salvage operation: 打捞作业
salvage value: 残值；剩余价值
salvage rights: 打捞权
salvage yard: 拆车场
salvage team: 打捞队


词源:
From Old French *salver (to save), from Latin salvare (to save).  The original meaning was specifically related to saving goods from a shipwreck.
