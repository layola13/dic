furled:/fɜːld/| v. |卷起；收拢；放下；|To roll or fold something up, especially a sail or flag. |The sailor furled the sails as the storm approached. 水手在暴风雨来临之际卷起了船帆。|近义词：roll up, fold up, furl|反义词：unfurl


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He furls his umbrella. 他卷起他的雨伞。
She furls the banner carefully. 她小心翼翼地卷起旗帜。

一般过去时:
The captain furled the sails after the race.  比赛结束后，船长收起了船帆。
They furled their tents before the rain started.  下雨前他们收起了帐篷。

现在完成时:
The sailors have furled the sails for the night. 水手们已经收起了晚上的船帆。
She has furled the map and put it in her bag. 她已经卷起地图并把它放进包里。

过去完成时:
The storm had already started before they had furled the sails.  暴风雨已经在他们收起船帆之前开始了。
They had furled their banners before the ceremony began. 典礼开始前他们已经收起了旗帜。


将来时:
They will furl the sails when the wind dies down. 风停下来的时候，他们会卷起船帆。
He will furl the awning before the heavy rain comes. 大雨来临之前他会卷起遮阳篷。


虚拟语气:
If it were windy, we would furl the sails. 如果刮风，我们会卷起船帆。
I wish I had furled the umbrella before the downpour.  我希望我在倾盆大雨来临之前就把雨伞卷起来了。


被动语态:
The sails were furled by the crew. 船帆被船员们卷起来了。
The flag was furled at sunset. 旗帜在日落时被收起。

疑问句:
Have you furled the sails yet? 你已经卷起船帆了吗？
Did they furl the banners before the ceremony?  他们在仪式之前卷起旗帜了吗？

否定句:
He didn't furl the sail in time. 他没有及时卷起船帆。
They did not furl their tents before the storm. 他们没有在暴风雨来临之前收起帐篷。
