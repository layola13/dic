seep:/siːp/| vi.|渗出，渗漏|To pass slowly through small openings; to ooze or leak gradually.|The water seeped through the cracks in the dam. 水慢慢地渗透了大坝的裂缝。|The rain seeped into the tent. 雨水渗入了帐篷。|ooze, leak, percolate, filter|block, stop|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water seeps through the ground. = 水渗透到地下。
The oil seeps from the damaged pipe. = 油从破损的管道中渗出。

一般过去时:
The liquid seeped into the soil. = 液体渗入了土壤。
The dye seeped through the fabric. = 染料渗透了织物。

现在完成时:
The rain has seeped into the basement. = 雨水已经渗入了地下室。
The poison has seeped into the water supply. = 毒物已经渗入了供水系统。

过去完成时:
The water had seeped into the foundation before we noticed it. = 我们注意到之前，水已经渗入了地基。
The gas had seeped into the room, making everyone feel sick. = 气体已经渗入了房间，使每个人都感到恶心。

将来时:
The chemicals will seep into the groundwater. = 化学物质将会渗入地下水。
The dye will seep into the fibers of the cloth. = 染料会渗入布料的纤维。

虚拟语气:
If the dam breaks, the water would seep into the town. = 如果大坝决堤，水就会渗入镇里。
I wish the toxic waste hadn’t seeped into the river. = 我希望有毒废物没有渗入河流。

被动语态:
The basement was seeped by the rainwater. = 地下室被雨水渗透了。(较为不自然，主动语态更常用)
The soil is being seeped by the polluted water. = 土壤正在被污染的水渗透。(较为不自然，主动语态更常用)

疑问句:
Is the water seeping into the foundation? = 水是否正在渗入地基？
Is the oil still seeping from the pipe? = 油是否还在从管道中渗出？

否定句:
The water didn't seep through the roof. = 水没有渗透屋顶。
The liquid didn't seep into the container. = 液体没有渗入容器。


PHRASE->
seep into: 渗入
seep through: 渗透
seep out: 渗出
seep away: 慢慢消失
seep down: 渗下去
