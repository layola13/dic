publically:/ˈpʌblɪkli/| adv.|公开地，公然地|公开地；公然地；当众地  |例句：The company publicly apologized for its mistake. 公司公开为其错误道歉。  |例句：He publicly denounced the government's policies. 他公开谴责政府的政策。|近义词：openly, overtly, frankly|反义词：secretly, privately, clandestinely|副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They publicly support the new law. = 他们公开支持这项新法律。
We publicly announce our engagement. = 我们公开宣布订婚。

一般过去时:
She publicly criticized the mayor. = 她公开批评了市长。
He publicly admitted his guilt. = 他公开承认了自己的罪行。

现在完成时:
They have publicly stated their intentions. = 他们已经公开声明了他们的意图。
The company has publicly denied the allegations. = 公司已经公开否认了这些指控。

过去完成时:
They had publicly endorsed the candidate before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他们已经公开支持了这位候选人。
The artist had publicly displayed her work many times before the exhibition. = 在展览之前，这位艺术家已经多次公开展示过她的作品。

将来时:
They will publicly release the report next week. = 他们下周将公开发布这份报告。
The government will publicly address the issue tomorrow. = 政府明天将公开讨论这个问题。

虚拟语气:
If they publicly apologized, it might help. = 如果他们公开道歉，可能会有所帮助。
I wish they had publicly acknowledged their mistakes. = 我希望他们已经公开承认了他们的错误。

被动语态:
The information was publicly released. = 信息被公开发布了。
The decision was publicly criticized.  = 这一决定受到了公众的批评。

疑问句:
Was the meeting publicly announced? = 会议是否公开宣布了？
Will the results be publicly available? = 结果会公开吗？

否定句:
The company did not publicly comment on the situation. = 公司没有就此事公开发表评论。
He did not publicly express his opinions. = 他没有公开表达他的意见。
