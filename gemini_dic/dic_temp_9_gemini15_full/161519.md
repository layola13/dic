pothead:/ˈpɑːtˌhed/| n.|大麻吸食者；瘾君子|A pothead is a person who frequently smokes marijuana.  The term is considered slang and often carries a negative connotation.|例句：He's a pothead and always smells of weed. 他是个大麻吸食者，身上总是带着大麻味。

例句：The police arrested several potheads at the concert.  警方在音乐会上逮捕了几名大麻吸食者。

近义词：marijuana smoker, weed smoker, stoner

反义词：None directly opposite, but could be considered the opposite of someone who abstains from drugs.


词性:名词
