scrawl:/skrɔːl/| n., v.|潦草的字迹；乱涂乱画；潦草地写；乱涂乱画|To write or draw something carelessly or quickly.|例句：He scrawled his signature on the document. 他在文件上潦草地签了名。|例句：The child scrawled all over the wall.  孩子在墙上乱涂乱画。|近义词：scribble, doodle, daub|反义词：write neatly, print


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scrawls his name on every page. = 他在每一页上都潦草地写下自己的名字。
She scrawls a quick note to her friend. = 她潦草地给朋友写了个便条。


一般过去时:
He scrawled a message on the napkin. = 他在餐巾纸上潦草地写了一条信息。
She scrawled her address on the back of an envelope. = 她把地址潦草地写在信封背面。


现在完成时:
He has scrawled his signature on the contract. = 他已经在合同上潦草地签了名。
She has scrawled a number of quick sketches. = 她已经潦草地画了几幅速写。


过去完成时:
He had scrawled a note before he left. = 他离开前潦草地写了一张便条。
She had scrawled the answer before the teacher could call on her. = 在老师叫到她之前，她已经潦草地写下了答案。


将来时:
He will scrawl a message on the whiteboard. = 他会在白板上潦草地写一条信息。
She will scrawl a quick reply to the email. = 她会潦草地回复这封邮件。


虚拟语气:
If he had time, he would scrawl a letter. = 如果他有时间，他会潦草地写一封信。
I wish I hadn't scrawled my name so illegibly. = 我真希望我没有把我的名字写得那么潦草。


被动语态:
The message was scrawled on a piece of paper. = 这条信息潦草地写在纸上。
His name was scrawled across the page. = 他的名字潦草地写满了整页。


疑问句:

Has she scrawled anything else on the wall? = 她还在墙上乱涂乱画了吗？


否定句:
He didn't scrawl his signature; he printed it neatly. = 他没有潦草地签字；他是工整地打印的。
She didn't scrawl a note; she wrote a formal letter. = 她没有潦草地写便条；她写了一封正式信函。
