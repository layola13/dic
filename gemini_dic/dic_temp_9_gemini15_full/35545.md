coalesced: /ˌkoʊəˈlɛst/ | v. | 联合；合并；融合 | To come together and form one mass or whole.  |例句：The small villages coalesced into a large town over time.  随着时间的推移，这些小村庄合并成了一座大城镇。|近义词：merge, combine, unite, fuse |反义词：separate, divide, disperse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The droplets of water coalesce to form a larger drop. = 水滴融合成更大的水滴。
The different opinions coalesce into a single policy. = 不同的意见融合成一项单一政策。

一般过去时:
The two companies coalesced last year. = 这两家公司去年合并了。
The protesters coalesced around a common goal. = 抗议者们围绕着一个共同目标团结起来。

现在完成时:
The various factions have coalesced into a single powerful force. = 不同的派系已经合并成一股强大的力量。
Their efforts have coalesced into a successful project. = 他们的努力已经融合成一个成功的项目。

过去完成时:
By the time of the meeting, the different ideas had coalesced into a concrete plan. = 到会议召开时，不同的想法已经融合成一个具体的计划。
Before the merger, the two businesses had coalesced around a shared vision. = 在合并之前，这两家企业已经围绕着一个共同的愿景融合在一起。

将来时:
The two rivers will coalesce further downstream. = 这两条河将在下游进一步汇合。
We believe the different groups will coalesce into a unified movement. = 我们相信不同的群体将会融合成一个统一的运动。


虚拟语气:
If the different elements coalesced, the result would be spectacular. = 如果不同的元素融合在一起，结果将是壮观的。
I wish the various ideas had coalesced into a more coherent plan. = 我希望各种想法能够融合成一个更连贯的计划。

被动语态:
The different opinions were coalesced into a compromise. = 不同的意见被融合成一个妥协方案。
The small communities were gradually coalesced into a larger municipality. = 小社区逐渐合并成一个更大的市政单位。

疑问句:
Will the two groups coalesce into a single entity? = 这两个群体将会合并成一个单一实体吗？
Have the various ideas coalesced into a unified theory? = 各种各样的想法已经融合成一个统一的理论了吗？

否定句:
The two factions did not coalesce despite attempts at negotiation. = 尽管进行了谈判，但这两个派系并没有合并。
The disparate elements did not coalesce into a cohesive whole. = 不同的元素并没有融合成一个有凝聚力的整体。
