cnidoblast:/ˈnaɪdəˌblæst/| n. |刺细胞母细胞|A cnidoblast is an explosive cell containing a nematocyst, found in cnidarians (such as jellyfish, sea anemones, and corals).  It's responsible for capturing prey and defense.  It is also known as a cnidocyte.|Example: The jellyfish's cnidoblasts fired, stinging the small fish.|近义词：cnidocyte|反义词：无
