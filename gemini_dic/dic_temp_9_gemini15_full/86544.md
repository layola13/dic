/hɛˈlɛnɪk/| adj.|希腊的；希腊人的；希腊语的|Relating to ancient Greece, its people, language, or culture.  The term often refers to the classical period of ancient Greece (roughly 5th and 4th centuries BC), known for its advancements in philosophy, art, and democracy.  However, it can also encompass a broader period of Greek history and culture.|The Hellenic Games were a precursor to the modern Olympics.|近义词：Greek, Grecian|反义词：None
