seton:/ˈsɛtɒn/| n.|塞顿（外科手术用线）|A narrow strip of material, such as linen or silk, used in surgery to drain an abscess or other collection of pus.  It acts as a wick to draw out the infected material.|例句：The surgeon inserted a seton to drain the infected wound.  外科医生插入一根塞顿来引流感染的伤口。|近义词：drain, wick|反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑与“封闭”相关的词)|名词
