sleeping:/ˈsliːpɪŋ/ | adj. | 睡着的；睡眠的 |  Describes someone or something that is in the state of sleep.  | Example: The sleeping baby was peaceful.  睡着的婴儿很平静。 The sleeping dog didn't wake up.  睡着的狗没有醒来。|  asleep, dozing | awake, alert | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is sleeping soundly. = 他睡得很香。
The cat is sleeping on the mat. = 猫睡在垫子上。

一般过去时:
She was sleeping when the phone rang. = 电话响的时候她在睡觉。
The children were sleeping peacefully. = 孩子们睡得很香。

现在完成时:
I have been sleeping badly lately. = 我最近睡眠不好。
The baby has been sleeping for three hours. = 婴儿已经睡了三个小时了。

过去完成时:
I had been sleeping for hours before the alarm clock rang. = 闹钟响之前我已经睡了好几个小时了。
They had been sleeping soundly when the earthquake hit. = 地震发生时，他们睡得很香。

将来时:
I will be sleeping by ten o'clock tonight. = 我今晚十点钟之前就会睡觉了。
They will be sleeping at the hotel tonight. = 他们今晚将睡在酒店里。

虚拟语气:
If I were sleeping, I wouldn't hear the phone. = 如果我睡着了，我就听不到电话铃声了。
I wish I were sleeping instead of working. = 我希望我是在睡觉而不是在工作。

被动语态:
The sleeping bag was left in the tent.  睡袋被留在帐篷里了。
The room was prepared for the sleeping guests. 房间为睡觉的客人准备好了。

疑问句:
Is he sleeping? = 他睡着了吗？
Are the children sleeping? = 孩子们睡着了吗？

否定句:
He isn't sleeping. = 他没有睡着。
The baby isn't sleeping well tonight. = 婴儿今晚睡得不好。
