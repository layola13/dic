runts:/rʌnts/| n.|小矮子，劣等品，小动物|A runt is a small animal, especially a dog or pig, that is much smaller than others of its kind.  It can also refer to a person who is unusually small for their age. |例句：The runt of the litter was the smallest and weakest puppy.  这窝小狗里最小最弱的是一只小矮子。

The farmer sold the runts to a local butcher.  农场主把劣等猪卖给了一家当地的屠夫。|近义词：undersized, small, dwarf|反义词：giant, large, oversized
