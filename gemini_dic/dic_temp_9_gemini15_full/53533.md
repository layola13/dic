disavouch:/dɪsæˈvaʊtʃ/| vt.|否认；否定；拒绝承认；与…断绝关系|To deny; to refuse to acknowledge; to disown.  The statement disavows any responsibility for the accident.  |近义词：deny, repudiate, disclaim, renounce|反义词：affirm, avow, acknowledge, admit|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disavows all knowledge of the crime. = 他否认自己知道这起犯罪。
The company disavows the actions of its former CEO. = 公司否认其前任首席执行官的行为。


一般过去时:
She disavowed her earlier statement. = 她否认了她之前的声明。
The government disavowed responsibility for the attack. = 政府否认对这次袭击负责。


现在完成时:
He has disavowed his past beliefs. = 他已经否认了他过去的信仰。
The organization has disavowed any connection to the terrorist group. = 该组织已经否认与恐怖组织有任何联系。


过去完成时:
They had disavowed their allegiance to the king. = 他们已经否认了对国王的效忠。
She had disavowed her relationship with him before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，她已经否认了她与他的关系。


将来时:
He will disavow the contract if the terms are not met. = 如果不满足条款，他将否认该合同。
The company will likely disavow any responsibility for the product defect. = 公司很可能会否认对产品缺陷的任何责任。


虚拟语气:
If he disavowed his actions, perhaps the consequences would be less severe. = 如果他否认自己的行为，后果可能会不那么严重。
I wish he had disavowed his support for the dictator. = 我希望他早点否认他对他独裁者的支持。


被动语态:
His claims were disavowed by the court. = 他的说法被法院否认了。
The statement was disavowed by the spokesperson. = 该声明遭到发言人的否认。


疑问句:
Will the government disavow its earlier promises? = 政府会否认其早先的承诺吗？
Does he disavow the accusations against him? = 他是否否认对他提出的指控？


否定句:
He did not disavow his support for the candidate. = 他没有否认他对候选人的支持。
The company would not disavow its responsibility for the faulty product. = 公司不会否认其对有缺陷产品的责任。
