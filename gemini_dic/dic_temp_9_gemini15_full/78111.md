geologically:/ˌdʒiːəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adj. | 地质学上地；从地质学角度来看 | Relating to geology or the science of the earth's physical structure and substance.  |例句：Geologically, the region is very unstable.  从地质学角度来看，该地区很不稳定。|例句：The area's unique geological formations are geologically significant. 该地区独特的地质构造在地质学上具有重要意义。|近义词：geologically speaking, from a geological perspective |反义词：N/A
