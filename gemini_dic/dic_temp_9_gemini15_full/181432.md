reject:/rɪˈdʒekt/ | vt. n. |拒绝；驳回；淘汰；次品 | to refuse to accept, consider, or allow something or someone |The committee rejected his proposal. 委员会拒绝了他的提案。|近义词：refuse, decline, dismiss |反义词：accept, approve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rejects all offers of help. 他拒绝所有帮助。
They reject the idea completely. 他们完全拒绝了这个想法。


一般过去时:
She rejected his marriage proposal. 她拒绝了他的求婚。
The company rejected my application. 公司拒绝了我的申请。


现在完成时:
They have rejected several plans already. 他们已经拒绝了好几个计划。
He has repeatedly rejected my advice. 他一再拒绝我的建议。


过去完成时:
I had rejected his offer before he changed his mind.  在他改变主意之前，我已经拒绝了他的提议。
She had already rejected the job offer. 她已经拒绝了这份工作。


将来时:
They will reject the bill. 他们将会否决这项法案。
I think they will reject my application. 我认为他们会拒绝我的申请。


虚拟语气:
If they rejected the proposal, what would we do? 如果他们拒绝了这个提案，我们该怎么办？
I wish they hadn't rejected my idea. 我真希望他们没有拒绝我的想法。


被动语态:
His application was rejected. 他的申请被拒绝了。
The plan was rejected by the board.  这个计划被董事会否决了。


疑问句:
Will they reject my application? 他们会拒绝我的申请吗？
Did they reject your proposal? 他们拒绝你的提案了吗？


否定句:
They didn't reject my idea. 他们没有拒绝我的想法。
He didn't reject the gift. 他没有拒绝这份礼物。


PHRASE->
