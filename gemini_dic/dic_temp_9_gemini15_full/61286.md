embalmings:/ɪmˈbɑːlmɪŋz/| n.| 遗体防腐处理（复数）|The process of preserving a body after death, typically by treating it with preservatives to prevent decay.|例句：The ancient Egyptians were renowned for their elaborate embalming techniques. 古埃及人以其复杂的防腐技术而闻名。|例句：The embalmings were conducted according to religious rites. 遗体防腐处理是按照宗教仪式进行的。|近义词：preservation, mummification|反义词：decomposition, decay|名词
