overcoil:/ˈoʊvərˌkɔɪl/| n. |过卷，线圈过度缠绕|A condition where a coil of wire or other material is wound too tightly, often leading to damage or malfunction.  Overcoiling can occur in various applications, from electrical wiring to springs. |例句：The spring broke due to overcoiling. 弹簧由于过度缠绕而断裂。|The excessive overcoiling caused the motor to overheat. 过度缠绕导致电机过热。|近义词：overwinding, excessive winding|反义词：undercoiling, loose winding|名词
