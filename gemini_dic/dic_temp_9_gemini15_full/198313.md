shovelard:/ˈʃʌvəlɑːrd/| n. |铲斗船，挖泥船 | A shovelard is a type of boat, usually a barge or dredge, equipped with a large shovel-like apparatus for excavating or moving large quantities of earth, sand, or other materials.  They are commonly used in dredging operations, canal construction, and other earthmoving projects. |例句：The shovelard dredged the riverbed to deepen the channel. 这艘铲斗船挖掘河床以加深航道。


The shovelard efficiently removed the accumulated silt. 这艘铲斗船有效地清除了淤积的淤泥。|近义词：dredge, excavator, dragline|反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting opposite actions like "filler" or "construction" could be considered contextual opposites.
