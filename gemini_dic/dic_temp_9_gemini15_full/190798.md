savoury: /ˈseɪvəri/ | adj. | 咸的；美味的；可口的 | Having a salty or piquant flavor rather than a sweet flavor.  It often describes foods that are not sweet, but can also imply a pleasant taste or aroma. | 例句：The stew had a rich, savoury flavour. 这道炖菜有着浓郁的咸味。  The savoury pastry was filled with cheese and herbs.  咸味糕点里装满了奶酪和香草。 | 近义词：salty, piquant, appetizing, tasty, flavorful | 反义词：sweet
