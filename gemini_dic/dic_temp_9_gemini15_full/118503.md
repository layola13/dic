mesne:/miːn/| adj.|中间的；介于两者之间的；居间的|Intermediate; intervening; lying between.  The term often refers to an intermediate estate or interest in land.  |例句：He held a mesne interest in the property before it was sold to its current owner. 他在该房产出售给目前的业主之前拥有中间权益。

例句：The mesne profits were claimed by the rightful owner after the illegal occupation. 非法占用后，合法所有者索要中间收益。


近义词：intermediate, intervening, intermediary |反义词：ultimate, final, direct


词源：From Old French *mesne, from Latin medius ("middle").
