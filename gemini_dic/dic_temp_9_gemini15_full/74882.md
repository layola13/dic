freshened:/ˈfreʃənd/| v.|使清爽；使新鲜；使振作；|To make or become fresher or more lively.|The rain freshened the air.  雨水使空气清新了。|The news freshened his spirits.  这个消息使他精神振奋。|近义词：renew, revive, invigorate|反义词：stale, dull, weary|动词
