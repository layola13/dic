pricy:/ˈpraɪsi/| adj.|昂贵的，价格高的|Expensive; costing a lot of money.|That restaurant is pricy; we should find somewhere cheaper.|近义词：expensive, costly, dear|反义词：cheap, inexpensive, affordable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
That diamond necklace is pricy. = 那条钻石项链很贵。
These shoes are pricy for me. = 这些鞋子对我来说太贵了。


一般过去时:
The hotel was pricier than we expected. = 这家酒店比我们预期的要贵。
The concert tickets were pricy last year. = 去年演唱会门票很贵。


现在完成时:
Gas has become pricier recently. = 最近汽油涨价了。
The cost of living has become pricier. = 生活成本变得更高了。


过去完成时:
The car had been pricier before the sale. = 这辆车在促销之前更贵。
The house had been pricier than we could afford. = 这房子比我们能承受的还要贵。


将来时:
Flights will be pricier during the holiday season. = 假期期间航班会更贵。
The new phone model will be pricier than its predecessor. = 新款手机比之前的型号更贵。


虚拟语气:
If the restaurant weren't so pricy, we would go there more often. = 如果这家餐馆不那么贵，我们会经常去那里。
I wish that this bag wasn't so pricy. = 我希望这个包不要这么贵。


被动语态:
The goods are often priced pricily in this store. = 这家店的商品通常定价很高。
The new car was pricily marketed. = 这款新车被高价推销。


疑问句:
Is this painting pricy? = 这幅画很贵吗？
Are those designer clothes pricy? = 那些名牌服装很贵吗？


否定句:
The apartment wasn't pricy at all. = 这套公寓一点也不贵。
The meal wasn't pricy considering the quality. = 考虑到质量，这顿饭并不贵。


PHRASE->
a pricy item = 一件昂贵的物品
a pricy restaurant = 一家昂贵的餐厅
a pricy vacation = 一次昂贵的假期
pricy real estate = 昂贵的房地产
pricy tastes = 高消费的品味
