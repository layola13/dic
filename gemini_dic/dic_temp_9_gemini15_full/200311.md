skulk:/skʌlk/| v.|潜行；偷偷摸摸地走；躲藏|To move stealthily or secretly; to hide oneself.|The thief skulked in the shadows, avoiding detection. 小偷躲在阴影里，避免被发现。The children skulked behind the trees, playing hide-and-seek. 孩子们躲在树后玩捉迷藏。|sneak, slink, lurk| openly, boldly, confidently |动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He skulks around the corner. = 他在拐角处偷偷摸摸地走动。
They skulk in the darkness. = 他们在黑暗中躲藏。

一般过去时:

He skulked away when he saw me. = 当他看到我时，他偷偷溜走了。

现在完成时:
