一般现在时:
Their success relies on their hard work. = 他们的成功依赖于他们的努力工作。
My reliance on my parents is decreasing. = 我对父母的依赖正在减少。


一般过去时:
His reliance on his friends helped him through tough times. = 他对朋友的依赖帮助他度过了艰难的时期。
The company's reliance on a single supplier proved to be a mistake. = 公司对单一供应商的依赖被证明是一个错误。


现在完成时:
My reliance on technology has increased significantly. = 我对技术的依赖已经大大增加了。
Their reliance on outdated methods has hampered their progress. = 他们对过时方法的依赖阻碍了他们的进步。


过去完成时:
His reliance on his family had been a constant throughout his life. = 他对家人的依赖一直是他生命中不变的因素。
The company's reliance on fossil fuels had led to environmental problems. = 公司对化石燃料的依赖导致了环境问题。


将来时:
Their reliance on renewable energy will increase in the future. = 他们对可再生能源的依赖将在未来增加。
My reliance on public transport will likely decrease once I have a car. = 一旦我有车，我对公共交通的依赖可能会减少。


虚拟语气:
If my reliance on others were less, I would be more independent. = 如果我对别人的依赖少一些，我会更独立。
I wish my reliance on caffeine were not so strong. = 我希望我不那么依赖咖啡因。


被动语态:
The company's success was heavily reliant on its marketing strategy. = 公司的成功很大程度上依赖于其营销策略。
His decision was heavily reliant on the advice he received. = 他的决定很大程度上依赖于他收到的建议。


疑问句:
What is your reliance on this technology? = 你对这项技术的依赖是什么？
What is the company's reliance on external funding? = 公司对外部资金的依赖是什么？


否定句:
The project did not rely on external funding. = 该项目不依赖外部资金。
His success was not reliant on luck alone. = 他的成功不仅仅依赖于运气。
