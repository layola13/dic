preshipping: /ˌpriːˈʃɪpɪŋ/ | adj. | 预发货的；发货前的 |  Relating to or occurring before the shipment of goods.  It describes activities, processes, or preparations done before the actual shipping of products or goods. |  例句：Preshipping inspections are crucial to ensure product quality before they reach the customer. 预发货检查对于确保产品在到达客户之前具有高质量至关重要。| 近义词：pre-shipment, advance shipping, prior to shipping | 反义词：post-shipping
