flier:/ˈflaɪər/| n. |传单；飞行员；飞翔的人| A flier is a small printed sheet of paper that is used to advertise something.  It can also refer to a person who flies an aircraft or someone who is flying. |例句：I picked up a flier for a concert. 我捡到了一张音乐会的宣传单。

例句：She's a skilled flier, having logged thousands of hours in the air. 她是一位经验丰富的飞行员，飞行时间已达数千小时。


近义词：leaflet, handbill, flyer, pilot, aviator|反义词：N/A


词源：源自古英语“fleogan”（飞翔），演变为中古英语“flier”，意为“飞翔的人”。 后来扩展到指代印刷品，因为这些印刷品通常会像传单一样被分发。
