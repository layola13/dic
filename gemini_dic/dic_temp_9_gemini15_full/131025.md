nonauthor:/ˈnɒnɔːθər/| adj. n.|非作者的；非作者|Not written or created by an author; not attributed to a specific author.|例句：The essay is of nonauthor origin. 这篇文章并非出自特定作者之手。|近义词：anonymous, unattributed|反义词：authorial
