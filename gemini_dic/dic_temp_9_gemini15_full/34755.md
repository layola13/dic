cliffed:/klɪft/ | adj. |悬崖峭壁的；陡峭的|Having or characterized by cliffs.  A cliffed coastline is dramatic and often inaccessible. |例句：The cliffed coast offered stunning views but little access to the sea.  悬崖峭壁的海岸线景色壮观，但很难靠近大海。 The path was cliffed, making the climb challenging. 小路陡峭，使得攀爬具有挑战性。|近义词：precipitous, steep, sheer |反义词：gentle, sloping
