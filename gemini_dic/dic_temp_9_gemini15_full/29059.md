caruncular:/kəˈrʌŋkjələr/| adj.| 肉阜的；小肉瘤的 | Relating to or resembling a caruncle.  A caruncle is a small, fleshy protuberance. The term is often used in anatomical contexts to describe small, reddish, fleshy growths. | Example: The bird's caruncular wattle was brightly colored.  这只鸟的肉阜状肉垂颜色鲜艳。|近义词：fleshy, papillose|反义词：smooth, sleek
