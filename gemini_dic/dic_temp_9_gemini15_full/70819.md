finical:/ˈfɪnɪkəl/| adj.| 挑剔的；过分讲究的 | Extremely particular, especially about food; fastidious.  |例句：He's too finical about his food; he only eats organic, locally sourced ingredients. 他对食物过于挑剔；他只吃有机、本地采购的食材。

例句：She is so finical that she only wears clothes made of the finest silk. 她非常挑剔，只穿用最优质丝绸制成的衣服。


近义词：fastidious, fussy, picky, persnickety, scrupulous


反义词：unfussy, easygoing, tolerant


词性：形容词


词源：源自17世纪晚期的法语单词“finical”，其含义与现代英语中的“finical”相似，可能与“fine”有关，表示“精致的”或“讲究的”，但其确切词源尚不明确。
