fulltime:/fʊlˈtaɪm/| adj.|全职的；全时间的|Working full-time requires a significant commitment.|全职工作需要很大的投入。|近义词：full-time, permanent|反义词：part-time


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He works full-time at a software company. = 他在一家软件公司全职工作。
She is a full-time student at university. = 她是大学的一名全日制学生。


一般过去时:
He worked full-time before he retired. = 他退休前是全职工作的。
She was a full-time employee for many years. = 她做了多年的全职员工。


现在完成时:
She has worked full-time since graduating from college. = 她大学毕业后一直在全职工作。
He has been a full-time teacher for over a decade. = 他当了十多年全职教师了。


过去完成时:
Before starting this job, he had worked full-time at another company for five years. = 在开始这份工作之前，他在另一家公司全职工作了五年。
She had been a full-time nurse before she decided to pursue a different career path. = 在决定追求不同的职业道路之前，她曾做过多年的全职护士。


将来时:
He will work full-time after he completes his internship. = 实习结束后，他将全职工作。
She will be a full-time employee next year. = 明年她将成为一名全职员工。


虚拟语气:
If I had a full-time job, I would have more money. = 如果我有全职工作，我会更有钱。
I wish I had a full-time position. = 我希望我能有一份全职工作。


被动语态:
The position was advertised as a full-time role. = 该职位被宣传为全职职位。
Many full-time positions are currently being filled. = 许多全职职位目前正在招聘中。


疑问句:
Is this a full-time or part-time position? = 这是全职还是兼职职位？
Are you looking for a full-time job? = 你在找全职工作吗？


否定句:
He doesn't work full-time; he only works part-time. = 他不是全职工作；他只是兼职。
She isn't currently employed full-time. = 她目前不是全职工作的。
