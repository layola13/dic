fitting:/ˈfɪtɪŋ/| n. adj. v.|配件；安装；合适的；恰当的|A fitting is a piece of equipment or a part that is added to something else.  A fitting can also describe something that is appropriate or suitable. As a verb, it means to put something in place or to install it.|例句：The plumber installed the new bathroom fittings.  (水管工安装了新的浴室配件。)

The dress was a perfect fitting. (这件衣服非常合身。)


近义词：attachment, installation, suitable, appropriate, proper|反义词：inappropriate, unsuitable, improper|名词，形容词，动词
