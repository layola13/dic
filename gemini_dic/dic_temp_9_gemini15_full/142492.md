osmite:/ˈɒzmaɪt/| n. |锇矿石|Osmium ore; a naturally occurring mineral containing osmium.  Osmium is a rare and heavy platinum-group metal, and its ores are typically found in association with other platinum-group metals.  |例句：The discovery of a rich osmite vein significantly boosted the mine's production of osmium.  发现一条富含锇矿石的矿脉大大提高了矿山的锇产量。|近义词：osmiridium (a specific type of osmium ore)|反义词：N/A|名词
