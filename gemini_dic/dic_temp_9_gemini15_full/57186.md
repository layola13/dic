down-and-outer: /ˌdaʊn ænd ˈaʊtər/ | n. | 穷困潦倒的人，落魄的人 | A down-and-outer is someone who is very poor and has no luck or hope.  They are often homeless and without any support.  The term emphasizes a state of complete destitution and despair. | Example: He was a down-and-outer, sleeping on park benches and begging for food. 他是个穷困潦倒的人，睡在公园长椅上，靠乞讨为生。 | 近义词：vagrant, derelict, pauper, destitute | 反义词：wealthy, prosperous, successful | 名词
