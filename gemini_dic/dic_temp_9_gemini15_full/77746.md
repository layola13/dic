The singer's gender-bending style often blurs the lines between masculine and feminine aesthetics.  这位歌手打破性别刻板印象的风格常常模糊了男性和女性审美的界限。| 近义词：gender-fluid, non-conformist, transgressive | 反义词：traditional, conventional, conforming

词源:
The term is a compound word formed by combining "gender" and "bending."  "Gender" refers to the socially constructed roles, behaviors, expressions, and identities of individuals. "Bending" implies a deviation from or a challenge to established norms or expectations.  Therefore, "gender-bending" signifies a departure from traditional gender norms.
