dismembers:/dɪsˈmɛmbər/| vt.|肢解，使…丧失组织；使…瓦解|To cut or tear off the limbs of (a body).  To destroy the organization or unity of (something).|例句：The body was found dismembered in the woods.  尸体在树林里被发现肢解了。


The rebels attempted to dismember the country.  叛乱分子试图肢解该国。|近义词：mutilate, sever, dismantle, destroy|反义词：unite, assemble, reconstruct|动词
