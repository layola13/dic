foreshine:/ˈfɔːrʃaɪn/| v.|预示；预兆|To shine before or in advance; to precede as a precursor or omen.  The word suggests a light or radiance that precedes something else, often implying a foreshadowing of events.|例句：The early morning sun foreshone the coming heat.  清晨的阳光预示着即将到来的酷暑。

The unusually calm weather foreshone the impending storm.  异常平静的天气预示着即将到来的暴风雨。|近义词：prefigure, foreshadow, presage|反义词：follow, succeed
