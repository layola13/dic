dragon:/ˈdræɡən/| n. |龙，凶猛的人|A large, serpentine, fire-breathing creature in mythology.  Often depicted as powerful, wise, or evil, depending on the culture.|例句：In many cultures, dragons are symbols of power and strength.  在许多文化中，龙是力量和强度的象征。

例句：The knight bravely fought the fearsome dragon.  骑士勇敢地与可怕的龙战斗。


近义词：serpent, monster, beast|反义词：lamb, dove
