self-depriving: /ˌsɛlf dɪˈpraɪvɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我剥夺的；自我克制的 |  Describes the act of denying oneself something, often pleasure or satisfaction, intentionally.  It implies a conscious effort to abstain from something considered desirable. |例句：Her self-depriving lifestyle, while admirable, seemed unsustainable.  她自我克制的 生活方式虽然令人钦佩，但似乎难以维持。

Her self-depriving diet led to health problems. 她自我克制的饮食导致了健康问题。


近义词：self-denying, abstinent, ascetic, austere | 反义词：self-indulgent, hedonistic

一般过去时:

They self-deprived themselves of entertainment during the exam period. 他们在考试期间克制娱乐活动。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
