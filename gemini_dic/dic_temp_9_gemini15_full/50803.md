descent: /dɪˈsɛnt/ | n. | 血统；下降；倾斜；下坡 | The act of moving downward; a downward slope; lineage or ancestry. |例句：His descent from a long line of scholars is well known.  他的学者世家出身是众所周知的。

例句：The steep descent of the mountain was dangerous.  陡峭的山路很危险。


近义词：lineage, ancestry, origin, decline, drop, slope | 反义词：ascent, climb, rise


词源:  From Old French *descente, from descendre "to descend," from Latin descendere, from de- "down" + scandere "to climb."
