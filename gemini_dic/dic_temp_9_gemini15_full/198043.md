shoeboxes:/ˈʃuːbɒksɪz/ | n. |鞋盒；鞋盒状物 |Small cardboard boxes typically used to store shoes.  They are often used for other small items as well.|例句：I keep my old photos in shoeboxes in the attic.我把旧照片放在阁楼上的鞋盒里。


例句：He used empty shoeboxes to build a castle for his son. 他用空的鞋盒为儿子搭建了一座城堡。


近义词：boxes, containers


反义词：None (antonyms are not applicable for this noun)


词性:名词
