ponytail:/ˈpɔːniːteɪl/|/ˈpoʊniːteɪl/| n. |马尾辫 |A ponytail is a hairstyle in which the hair is pulled back and tied at the back of the head, resembling the tail of a pony.  |She wore her hair in a high ponytail.|近义词：pigtail, braid |反义词：loose hair
