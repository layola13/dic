displace:/dɪsˈpleɪs/ | vt. |移置；取代；使流离失所 | to move something or someone from their usual place or position |The factory closure displaced hundreds of workers.  工厂倒闭使数百名工人失业。

The new technology displaced the old methods. 新技术取代了旧方法。|replace, remove, supplant |retain, keep, maintain |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company displaces its waste responsibly. = 公司负责任地处理其废物。
He displaces his anger onto others. = 他把怒气发泄到别人身上。


一般过去时:
The flood displaced thousands of people from their homes. = 洪水使数千人流离失所。
The earthquake displaced the building's foundation. = 地震使建筑物的地基移位。


现在完成时:
Technological advancements have displaced many traditional jobs. = 技术进步已经取代了许多传统工作。
The recent storm has displaced several families. = 最近的风暴使几户人家流离失所。


过去完成时:
The war had displaced millions before it ended. = 战争结束前已使数百万人流离失所。
They had displaced the furniture before the party. = 他们在聚会前已经挪动了家具。


将来时:
Automation will displace many workers in the coming years. = 自动化将在未来几年取代许多工人。
The rising sea levels will displace coastal communities. = 海平面上升将使沿海社区流离失所。


虚拟语气:
If the dam broke, it would displace a large population. = 如果大坝决堤，它将使大量人口流离失所。
I wish the new system hadn't displaced my old favorite program. = 我真希望新系统没有取代我以前最喜欢的程序。


被动语态:
Many people were displaced by the hurricane. = 许多人被飓风迫使离开家园。
The old system was displaced by a more efficient one. = 旧系统被一个更有效的系统取代了。


疑问句:
Will this new software displace the current version? = 这款新软件会取代当前版本吗？
How many people were displaced by the conflict? = 冲突使多少人流离失所？


否定句:
The new policy did not displace the old one. = 新政策并没有取代旧政策。
The company tried to avoid displacing its workforce. = 公司试图避免裁员。
