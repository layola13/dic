词源:

The word "reviolating" is formed by adding the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "back") to the present participle of the verb "violating".  "Violating" comes from the Latin word "violare", meaning "to injure, profane, or violate".  Therefore, "reviolating" literally means "violating again".
