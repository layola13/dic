coalman:/ˈkoʊlmən/| n. |送煤工，卖煤人 | A coalman is a person who delivers or sells coal.  They were common before the widespread use of other heating fuels. | Example: The coalman arrived early this morning to deliver the winter's supply.  今早送煤工很早就来了，送来了过冬的煤。|近义词：coal merchant, coal dealer |反义词：无
