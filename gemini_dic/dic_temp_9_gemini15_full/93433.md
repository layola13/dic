illuminates:/ɪˈluːmɪneɪts/| vt.|照亮；阐明；使辉煌|to light up; to make clear or understandable; to make brilliant or glorious|例句：The moon illuminates the night. 月亮照亮了夜晚。|例句：His explanation illuminated the complex issue. 他的解释阐明了这个复杂的问题。|近义词：brighten, clarify, enlighten|反义词：obscure, darken, confuse|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun illuminates the earth. = 太阳照亮地球。
The artist's work illuminates the human condition. = 艺术家的作品阐明了人类的处境。

一般过去时:
The candles illuminated the room. = 蜡烛照亮了房间。
The professor's lecture illuminated the topic for the students. = 教授的讲座使学生们明白了这个话题。

现在完成时:

His research has illuminated the effects of climate change. = 他的研究阐明了气候变化的影响。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

If his explanation had illuminated the problem, we wouldn't be so confused. = 如果他的解释阐明了这个问题，我们就不会这么困惑了。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
