regripped:/ˌriːˈɡrɪpt/| v. |重新握住；重新缠绕（球拍手柄等）|To grip (something) again, especially a tennis racket or golf club, by replacing the grip.  This usually involves removing the old grip and applying a new one.|例句：I regripped my tennis racket after noticing the old grip was worn and slippery. 我注意到旧球拍握把磨损且滑，于是重新缠绕了我的网球拍。
例句：He decided to regrip his golf clubs before the tournament. 他决定在锦标赛前重新缠绕他的高尔夫球杆。|近义词：rehandle, rewrap|反义词：ungrip
