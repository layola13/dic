newbie's: /ˈnuːbiːz/ | n. | 新手的；菜鸟的 |  The possessive form of "newbie," referring to something belonging to or characteristic of a newbie.  It often implies inexperience or a lack of knowledge in a particular field or activity. | Example:  The newbie's question was quite basic. 新手的提问相当基础。 | 近义词: beginner's, novice's, tyro's | 反义词: expert's, veteran's, professional's | 名词性物主代词
