handrails:/ˈhændreɪlz/| n.|扶手，栏杆|A handrail is a rail providing support when someone is walking up or down stairs or along a walkway.|例句：The handrails on the stairs were worn and needed replacing. 楼梯上的扶手已经磨损，需要更换。|The old house had beautiful carved handrails. 老房子有精美的雕花扶手。|近义词：banister, railing, balustrade|反义词:none
