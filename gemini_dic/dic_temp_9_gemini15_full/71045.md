first-ever: /ˌfɜːrstˈɛvər/ | adj. | 首次的；有史以来的 |  Describes something that is the very first instance of its kind ever recorded or experienced.  | 例句：This is the first-ever manned mission to Mars.  这是人类首次载人火星任务。


例句：This is the first-ever time I've seen snow.  这是我第一次看到雪。


近义词：initial, inaugural, pioneering, unprecedented


反义词：latest, last, final


词性：形容词
