explorations:/ˌɛkspləˈreɪʃənz/ | n. |探险；探索；考察 | The act of exploring or investigating.  It refers to the process of discovering, examining, and learning about something new or unknown. This can range from geographical expeditions to scientific inquiries. |例句：His explorations of the Amazon rainforest yielded many important discoveries.  他的亚马逊雨林探险取得了许多重要发现。

例句：The team's explorations into the causes of the disease led to a breakthrough.  该团队对该疾病病因的探索取得了突破。


近义词：investigations, explorations, researches, studies | 反义词：ignorance, neglect
