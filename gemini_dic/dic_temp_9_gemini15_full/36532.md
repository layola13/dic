collaborating:/kəˈlæbəreɪtɪŋ/| v.|合作，协作|The act of working jointly on an activity or project.|例句：The two companies are collaborating on a new product. 两家公司正在合作开发一款新产品。

例句：Researchers are collaborating to find a cure for cancer. 研究人员正在合作寻找癌症的治疗方法。


近义词：cooperating, working together, teaming up|反义词：competing, working independently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are collaborating on a research project. = 他们正在合作一个研究项目。
We collaborate regularly to improve our service. = 我们定期合作以改善我们的服务。


一般过去时:
They collaborated on a successful film. = 他们合作拍摄了一部成功的电影。
The team collaborated effectively to meet the deadline. = 团队有效地合作以赶上最后期限。


现在完成时:
We have collaborated with many artists over the years. = 多年来，我们与许多艺术家合作过。
They have collaborated on several successful projects. = 他们已经合作完成了几项成功的项目。


过去完成时:
They had collaborated before on similar projects. = 他们之前曾在类似的项目上合作过。
The scientists had collaborated extensively before publishing their findings. = 科学家们在发表研究结果之前进行了广泛的合作。


将来时:
We will be collaborating with a new partner next year. = 我们明年将与一位新的合作伙伴合作。
They will collaborate on a new strategy for the company. = 他们将合作制定公司的新战略。


虚拟语气:
If they collaborated, they could achieve more. = 如果他们合作，他们可以取得更多成就。
I wish we had collaborated earlier on this project. = 我希望我们能早点在这个项目上合作。


被动语态:
The project was being collaborated on by several teams. = 这个项目正在由几个团队合作进行。
The new product was successfully collaborated on by two companies. = 这款新产品是由两家公司成功合作推出的。


疑问句:
Are they collaborating on this project? = 他们在这个项目上合作吗？
Will you be collaborating with other organizations? = 你会与其他组织合作吗？


否定句:
They are not collaborating on the project anymore. = 他们不再在这个项目上合作了。
We did not collaborate on that project. = 我们没有参与那个项目的合作。
