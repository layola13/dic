despotize:/ˈdɛspətaɪz/| vt.|专制统治；暴政统治；|To rule or govern in a tyrannical or oppressive manner; to exercise absolute power over.|例句：The dictator despotized the country for decades.  独裁者几十年来对这个国家实行专制统治。|例句：He attempted to despotize his family with his unreasonable demands. 他试图用不合理的要求来专制统治他的家人。|近义词：tyrannize, domineer, oppress|反义词：liberate, emancipate
