nonitemized:/ˌnɒnˈaɪtəmaɪzd/ | adj. |未列举的，未逐项列明的 | Not listed individually; presented as a single sum.  This term is often used in accounting and tax contexts. | Example:  He claimed a nonitemized deduction on his tax return. 他在他的纳税申报单上申报了未逐项列明的扣除额。 | 近义词：unitemized, lump-sum, aggregated | 反义词：itemized, detailed
