It describes the manner in which something is done, characterized by interruption or insertion. | Example: He spoke interposingly, cutting off the others before they could finish their sentences. 他插嘴说话，打断别人还没说完的话。  | 近义词：interruptingly, intrusively | 反义词：continuously, uninterruptedly | 副词
