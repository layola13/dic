lingula:/ˈlɪŋɡjʊlə/| n. |小舌，舌状突起|A small tongue-shaped structure or process.  The term is used in anatomy to describe various tongue-like projections in different parts of the body. For example, the lingula of the lung is a small, tongue-shaped projection of the superior lobe of the left lung.  It can also refer to a similar structure in other organs. |例句：The lingula of the lung is often involved in lung infections. 肺的小舌经常参与肺部感染。

The lingula of the mandible is a small, triangular projection. 下颌骨的小舌是一个小的三角形突起。|近义词：tonguelet, projection, appendage |反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，需根据语境选择)|名词
