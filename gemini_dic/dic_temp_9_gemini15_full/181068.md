reindeer:/ˈreɪnˌdɪr/| n. |驯鹿|A type of deer with large antlers, found in arctic and subarctic regions. They are known for their ability to pull sleds and their role in Santa Claus's Christmas tradition.|例句：Santa Claus's reindeer fly through the night sky. 圣诞老人的驯鹿在夜空中飞翔。|例句：Reindeer migrate long distances in search of food.  驯鹿为了寻找食物而长途迁徙。|近义词：caribou (in North America)|反义词：无
