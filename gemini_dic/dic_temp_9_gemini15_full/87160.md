herborist:/ˈɜːrbərɪst/| n. |草药学家，药剂师|A person who studies or practices herbal medicine.  They collect, identify, and prepare medicinal plants. |The herborist recommended chamomile tea for my headache.  这位草药学家建议我喝洋甘菊茶来缓解头痛。|herbalist, botanist| (无明显反义词)|名词
