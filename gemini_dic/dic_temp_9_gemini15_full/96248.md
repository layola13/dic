indicative-of: /ˌɪndɪˈkeɪtɪv ʌv/ | adj. | 表明…的；指示…的 |  indicative-of describes something that shows or suggests something else. It highlights a sign, symptom, or characteristic that points towards a particular conclusion or underlying condition.  |

例句：His flushed face was indicative of fever.  他的脸红了，这表明他发烧了。

例句：The high unemployment rate is indicative of a struggling economy. 高失业率表明经济正在挣扎。


近义词：suggestive of, suggestive, symptomatic of, characteristic of, suggestive, expressive of


反义词：uncharacteristic of, unlike


词性：形容词
