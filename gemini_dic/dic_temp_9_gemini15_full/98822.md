intellectualness: /ˌɪntəlɛktʃuˈælɪnəs/ | n. | 智力；才智；知识分子气派 | The quality or state of being intellectual;  intelligence and mental ability.  It can also refer to the characteristics associated with intellectuals, such as a scholarly or academic demeanor. |


例句：

His lectures were marked by a certain intellectualness and depth of thought.  他的讲座体现出某种智力和思想深度。

The book lacked intellectualness; it was superficial and lacked substance. 这本书缺乏智力深度；它肤浅且缺乏内涵。


近义词：intelligence, intellectuality, scholarship, sophistication

反义词：ignorance, stupidity, shallowness


词性：名词
