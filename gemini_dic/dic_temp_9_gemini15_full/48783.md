deflect:/dɪˈflɛkt/| vt.|使偏斜；使转向；转移；避开|to cause something to change direction, or to be deflected|The shield deflected the blow.  盾牌挡住了那一击。|The politician deflected criticism by blaming others.  这位政客通过责备他人来转移批评。|近义词：divert, deviate, avert|反义词：direct,straighten


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He deflects criticism with humor. 他用幽默化解批评。
The mirror deflects the sunlight. 镜子反射阳光。


一般过去时:
The ball deflected off the wall. 球从墙上弹开了。
She deflected his advances gracefully. 她巧妙地避开了他的追求。


现在完成时:
He has deflected all attempts to discredit him. 他已经成功化解了所有试图败坏他名声的企图。
The new policy has deflected public anger. 新政策转移了公众的愤怒。


过去完成时:
The driver had deflected the car to avoid an accident. 司机已经转向避开了事故。
They had deflected the enemy’s attack. 他们已经成功地化解了敌人的进攻。


将来时:
The missile will deflect from its course.  导弹将偏离航向。
She will deflect any questions about her past. 她会避开任何关于她过去的问题。


虚拟语气:
If he had deflected the punch, he wouldn't have been hurt. 如果他挡住了那一拳，他就不会受伤了。
I wish he had deflected the blame from me. 我真希望他能把责任从我身上推开。


被动语态:
The blow was deflected by the shield.  那一击被盾牌挡住了。
The questions were deflected by the spokesperson.  发言人避开了这些问题。


疑问句:
How can we deflect the negative publicity? 我们如何才能化解负面宣传？
Will the new technology deflect the asteroid? 新技术能否使小行星偏离轨道？


否定句:
He didn't deflect the blow. 他没有挡住那一击。
She couldn't deflect his persistent questioning. 她无法避开他坚持不懈的盘问。


PHRASE->
