reassuredly:/rɪˈʃʊərdli/| adv.| 安心地；放心地；确信地 | In a way that reassures; confidently and without doubt.  It expresses certainty and confidence, often implying the removal of anxiety or fear. |例句：He nodded reassuringly. 他安心地点了点头。
She spoke reassuringly to the frightened child. 她安心地对受惊吓的孩子说话。|近义词：confidently, assuredly, calmly |反义词：anxiously, nervously, doubtfully

She acts reassuringly in times of crisis. = 她在危机时刻表现得很镇定。
