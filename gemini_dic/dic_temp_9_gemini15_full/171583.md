pullover:/ˈpʊlˌoʊvər/|/ˈpʊlˌəʊvər/| n.|套衫，毛衣|A pullover is a piece of clothing, typically knitted or crocheted, that is pulled over the head to be worn. It usually has long sleeves and covers the torso.|例句：I wore a warm pullover to protect myself from the cold wind. 我穿了一件暖和的套衫来保护自己免受寒风的侵袭。|例句：She knitted a beautiful pullover for her grandson. 她为她的孙子织了一件漂亮的套衫。|近义词：sweater, jumper|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a button-up shirt or jacket.
