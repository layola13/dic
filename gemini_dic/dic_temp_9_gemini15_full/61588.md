emerging:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 新兴的；正在出现的；(问题、趋势等) 出现的 |  Developing or becoming prominent; coming into view; appearing.  | 例句：Emerging markets offer significant investment opportunities.  新兴市场提供了大量的投资机会。| 例句：The company is an emerging leader in the field of renewable energy. 这家公司是可再生能源领域的新兴领导者。 | 近义词：rising, developing, nascent, up-and-coming | 反义词：established, declining, waning


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun is emerging from behind the clouds. = 太阳从云层后面出来了。
A new trend is emerging in the fashion industry. = 时尚界正在兴起一种新的潮流。

一般过去时:
A new star emerged in the night sky. = 夜空中出现了一颗新星。
Problems emerged during the construction of the building. = 在建筑物建造过程中出现了一些问题。

现在完成时:
Several new technologies have emerged in recent years. = 近年来出现了几项新技术。
Many challenges have emerged since the start of the project. = 自项目启动以来，出现了许多挑战。

过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, the situation had already emerged. = 我到达时，情况已经显现出来了。
It had emerged that the witness was unreliable. = 后来发现证人不可靠。

将来时:
New opportunities will emerge as the market expands. = 随着市场的扩大，新的机遇将会出现。
We expect that further complications will emerge during the next phase. = 我们预计在下一阶段会出现进一步的复杂情况。

虚拟语气:
If a new problem emerged, we would have to find a solution. = 如果出现新的问题，我们就必须找到解决办法。
I wish that a better solution had emerged earlier. = 我希望早点出现更好的解决方案。

被动语态:
The truth was eventually emerged. = 真相最终大白于天下。
The problems were emerged as a result of poor planning. = 由于计划不周，问题出现了。

疑问句:
What new trends are emerging in the market? = 市场中正在出现哪些新趋势？
Are there any emerging risks associated with this project? = 与这个项目相关的任何新兴风险？

否定句:
No new evidence has emerged in the case. = 此案中没有出现新的证据。
The company failed to emerge from the crisis. = 这家公司未能摆脱危机。
