popsock:/ˈpɑːpsɑːk/| n. |手机指环扣；指环支架|A poppsock is a small, silicone or fabric grip that attaches to the back of a smartphone or other device to improve grip and provide a stand.|例句：I bought a poppsock for my phone to prevent it from slipping out of my hand. 我买了一个手机指环扣来防止我的手机滑落。|例句：The poppsock also acts as a stand, allowing me to watch videos hands-free.  这个手机指环扣还可以当支架用，让我可以解放双手看视频。|近义词：phone grip, phone stand|反义词：无
