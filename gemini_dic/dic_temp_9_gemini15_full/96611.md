indulge:/ɪnˈdʌldʒ/ | vt. & vi. | 沉溺；纵容；放纵 | To allow oneself to enjoy the pleasure of; to give oneself up to.  |  例句：He indulged in a long, hot bath. 他享受了一个长时间的热水澡。 | 例句：She indulged her children's every whim. 她纵容孩子们的每一个怪念头。 | 近义词：pamper, gratify, spoil | 反义词：deny, restrain, abstain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I indulge in a cup of coffee every morning. = 我每天早上都喝一杯咖啡。
They indulge themselves with expensive holidays. = 他们沉溺于昂贵的假期。


一般过去时:
She indulged her sweet tooth with a piece of chocolate cake. = 她吃了一块巧克力蛋糕来满足她的甜食欲望。
He indulged in a day of complete relaxation. = 他享受了一整天的彻底放松。


现在完成时:
I have indulged in too many sweets lately. = 我最近吃太多甜食了。
She has indulged her passion for painting for many years. = 她多年来一直沉迷于绘画。


过去完成时:
He had indulged in several glasses of wine before driving home. = 他在开车回家前喝了好几杯酒。
Having indulged in a large meal, she felt sleepy. = 饱餐一顿后，她感到困倦。


将来时:
I will indulge myself with a massage after the exam. = 考试后我会给自己做个按摩。
They will indulge in some serious shopping this weekend. = 他们这个周末会尽情购物。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would indulge in a hobby. = 如果我有更多时间，我会沉迷于一个爱好。
I wish I could indulge in a long vacation. = 我真希望我能享受一个长假。


被动语态:
His desires were indulged by his parents. = 他的愿望被父母纵容了。
The children were indulged with toys and sweets. = 孩子们被玩具和糖果宠坏了。


疑问句:
Do you often indulge in unhealthy snacks? = 你经常吃不健康的零食吗？
Does he indulge in gambling? = 他沉迷于赌博吗？


否定句:
I don't indulge in gossip. = 我不参与八卦。
She doesn't indulge her cravings for junk food. = 她不纵容自己对垃圾食品的渴望。



PHRASE->
indulge in = 沉溺于；纵容
indulge oneself = 放纵自己
indulge a habit = 纵容一个习惯
indulge one's appetite = 满足食欲
indulge one's fantasy = 沉溺于幻想
