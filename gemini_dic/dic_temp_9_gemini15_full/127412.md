nearsighted: /ˈnɪrˌsaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 近视的；目光短浅的 | Unable to see distant objects clearly; myopic.  Also implies a lack of foresight or long-term perspective. |

例句：

He's nearsighted and needs glasses to drive. 他近视，需要戴眼镜开车。

She made a nearsighted decision that hurt her business in the long run. 她做了一个目光短浅的决定，从长远来看损害了她的生意。


近义词：myopic, shortsighted

反义词：farsighted

词性：形容词

词源:

The word "nearsighted" comes from the combination of "near" (meaning close) and "sighted" (referring to vision).  The "near" part indicates the limitation of vision to nearby objects, while "sighted" refers to the ability to see.  It reflects a direct description of the condition.
