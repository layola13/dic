diarrhoetic:/ˌdaɪəˈriːtɪk/| adj. n.|泻药的；腹泻药|A diarrhoetic is a substance that causes diarrhoea (loose, watery stools).  It can be a medicine used to treat constipation, or it can be a food or substance that has this effect as a side effect.|Example:  The doctor prescribed a mild diarrhoetic to help relieve the patient's constipation.  医生开了温和的泻药来缓解病人的便秘。|近义词：purgative, laxative|反义词：constipant|形容词，名词
