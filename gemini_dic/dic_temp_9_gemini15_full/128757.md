niggle:/ˈnɪɡəl/| v. n.|  琐碎抱怨；小毛病；微不足道的问题 | To complain persistently about unimportant things; a minor complaint or worry. |例句：He kept niggling about the small details. 他不停地抱怨那些细枝末节。|例句：There are a few niggles to iron out before the launch.  在发布之前还有一些小问题需要解决。|近义词：fret, grumble, quibble, cavil |反义词：ignore, overlook


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He niggles about the cost of everything. = 他对每样东西的成本都斤斤计较。
She constantly niggles at her husband about his messiness. = 她不断地唠叨她丈夫的邋遢。

一般过去时:
He niggled about the unfairness of the situation. = 他抱怨情况的不公平。
She niggled at her colleagues about the project delay. = 她对同事们抱怨项目延误。


现在完成时:
They have been niggling at me all day about the report. = 他们整天都在为报告的事烦我。
He has niggled about the same thing for weeks. = 他已经抱怨同一件事几周了。


过去完成时:
She had niggled at him for days before he finally agreed. = 她在他最终同意之前已经对他唠叨了好几天了。
They had niggled about the plans before the meeting. = 他们在会议前就计划的事情争论不休。


将来时:
I know he will niggle about the bill. = 我知道他会为账单斤斤计较。
She will probably niggle at him again about the garden. = 她可能会再次因为他花园的事而唠叨他。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't niggled so much, we could have finished earlier. = 如果他不那么唠叨，我们本可以早点完成。
I wish he wouldn't niggle about every little thing. = 我希望他不要为每一件小事都唠叨。


被动语态:
His concerns were often niggled over by the team. = 他的担忧经常被团队反复提及并抱怨。
The details were niggled at until they were perfect. = 细节被反复挑剔直到完美无缺。


疑问句:
Is he still niggling about the paint color? = 他还在为油漆颜色唠叨吗？
What are they niggling about now? = 他们现在在为什么事争论不休？


否定句:
He didn’t niggle about the price. = 他没有为价格斤斤计较。
She didn’t niggle at him this time. = 她这次没有唠叨他。


PHRASE->
niggle at someone =  对某人唠叨
niggle over something =  对某事反复挑剔
a niggling doubt =  挥之不去的怀疑
a niggling feeling =  隐隐约约的感觉
niggling details =  琐碎的细节
