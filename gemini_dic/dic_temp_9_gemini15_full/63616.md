enwreathe:/ɪnˈwiːð/| vt.|环绕；缠绕；围绕|To surround or encircle with or as if with a wreath or garland; to envelop or cover.|The ivy enwreathed the old stone walls.  常春藤缠绕着古老的石墙。|The mountains were enwreathed in mist.  群山笼罩在薄雾中。|近义词：encircle, surround, envelop|反义词：unwind, disentangle|动词
