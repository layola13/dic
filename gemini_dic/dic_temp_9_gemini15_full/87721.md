hexed:/hɛkst/| v.|施巫术于；使受咒语影响；使迷惑；使厌恶|to put a hex on someone or something; to bewitch or jinx; to cast a spell on; to confuse or bewilder; to make someone feel disgusted or annoyed|例句：The witch hexed the prince, turning him into a frog.  巫婆对王子施了魔法，把他变成了青蛙。

The bad luck hexed her plans for the weekend.  坏运气破坏了她周末的计划。


近义词：bewitched, cursed, jinxed, enchanted, confounded|反义词：blessed, protected, aided


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He hexes his enemies regularly. 他经常对他的敌人施咒。
She hexes anyone who crosses her. 她诅咒任何与她作对的人。


一般过去时:
The old woman hexed the young man. 老妇人对那年轻人施了魔法。
The witch hexed the entire village. 巫婆诅咒了整个村庄。


现在完成时:
She has hexed me with her evil eye. 她用她的邪眼下了咒。
They have hexed the crops, causing them to fail. 他们对庄稼施了咒语，导致庄稼歉收。


过去完成时:
He had hexed the princess before she escaped. 公主逃跑前他已经对她施了咒语。
The villagers had hexed the stranger before he arrived. 村民们在陌生人到达之前就对他下了咒。


将来时:
I will hex you if you don't leave me alone. 如果你不离开我，我会对你施魔法。
She will hex him for his betrayal. 她会因为他背叛而对他施咒。


虚拟语气:
If I could hex him, I would. 如果我能对他施咒，我会这么做的。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

She hasn't hexed anyone today. 她今天没有对任何人施魔法。
