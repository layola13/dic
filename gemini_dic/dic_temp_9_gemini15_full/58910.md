durometer:/dʊˈrɒmɪtər/ | n. |硬度计；硬度测定仪 |A durometer is an instrument used to measure the hardness of materials, especially rubber and plastics.  It measures the resistance of a material to indentation by a specified indenter under a specific load. |Example: The technician used a durometer to test the hardness of the rubber seal. 技师用硬度计测试橡胶密封圈的硬度。|近义词：hardness tester, Shore durometer |反义词：无
