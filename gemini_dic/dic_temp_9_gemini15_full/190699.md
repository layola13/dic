sauropod:/ˈsɔːrəpɒd/| n. |蜥脚类恐龙|A sauropod is a type of large, herbivorous dinosaur characterized by its long neck, long tail, small head, and four thick legs. They were among the largest animals to ever walk the Earth.  |Example:  The museum boasts a magnificent skeleton of a Brachiosaurus, a well-known sauropod.  博物馆里有一具著名的蜥脚类恐龙——腕龙的壮丽骨架。

The discovery of a new sauropod species thrilled paleontologists worldwide. 新蜥脚类恐龙物种的发现让全世界的古生物学家兴奋不已。|近义词：dinosaur (general term)|反义词：None (no direct antonym)|
