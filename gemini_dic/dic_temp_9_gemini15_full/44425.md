cross-pollinating: /krɒsˈpɒləneɪtɪŋ/ | v. |异花授粉；交叉授粉；互相影响|The transfer of pollen from the flower of one plant to the flower of another plant of the same species. This process promotes genetic diversity and can lead to stronger, more resilient offspring.  |例句：Bees are essential for cross-pollinating many fruit trees. 蜜蜂对于许多果树的异花授粉至关重要。

例句：The ideas from different departments are cross-pollinating, leading to innovative solutions.  不同部门的想法互相影响，从而产生创新的解决方案。|近义词：hybridizing, interbreeding |反义词：self-pollinating|动词
