hyporadius: /ˌhaɪpəˈreɪdiəs/ | n. | 半径，半径线 |  A line segment from the center of a circle or sphere to any point on the circle or sphere. It is half the diameter.  | The hyporadius of the circle is 5 centimeters.  这个圆的半径是5厘米。 | radius, semidiameter | diameter | 名词
