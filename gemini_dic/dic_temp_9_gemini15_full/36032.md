codiscover:/ˌkoʊdɪˈskʌvər/| vt.|共同发现，合作发现|To discover something together or simultaneously with someone else.  It implies a shared effort or simultaneous independent discovery.| Example:  Watson and Crick codiscovered the structure of DNA.  沃森和克里克共同发现了DNA的结构。|近义词：discover jointly, jointly find, find together|反义词：conceal, hide
