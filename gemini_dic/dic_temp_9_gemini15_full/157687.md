pixelating: /ˈpɪksəleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 像素化；使成像素 | To break an image or video into pixels, often to obscure or censor details.  The process reduces image quality and resolution. | 例句：The video was pixelating due to a poor internet connection. 视频由于网络连接不良而出现像素化。  The police pixelated the suspect's face in the released footage. 警方在发布的录像中将嫌疑人的脸部像素化处理。| 近义词：blurring, obscuring, censoring | 反义词：sharpening, clarifying | 动词
