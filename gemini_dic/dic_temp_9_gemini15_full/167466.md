prevented:/prɪˈvɛntɪd/ | v. |阻止；预防 | To stop something from happening or someone from doing something. |例句：The rain prevented us from going to the park.  雨阻止了我们去公园。|例句：He prevented the accident by braking quickly. 他紧急刹车阻止了事故发生。|近义词：stopped, avoided, hindered, obstructed |反义词：allowed, permitted, enabled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prevents accidents by driving carefully. = 他谨慎驾驶，防止事故发生。
The government prevents pollution through new regulations. = 政府通过新的法规来防止污染。

一般过去时:
The heavy snow prevented the flight from taking off. = 大雪阻止了航班起飞。
A sudden illness prevented her from attending the meeting. = 突如其来的疾病阻止了她参加会议。

现在完成时:
Safety measures have prevented many accidents. = 安全措施已经阻止了许多事故。
His quick thinking has prevented a disaster. = 他迅速的思考避免了一场灾难。

过去完成时:
I had prevented the problem by taking action earlier. = 我通过早先采取行动避免了这个问题。
They had prevented the fire from spreading further. = 他们阻止了火势进一步蔓延。

将来时:
New laws will prevent further environmental damage. = 新法律将防止进一步的环境破坏。
The vaccine will prevent many people from getting sick. = 疫苗将阻止许多人患病。


被动语态:
The meeting was prevented by the heavy snow. = 会议被大雪阻止了。
Further damage was prevented by the quick response. = 快速的反应阻止了进一步的损坏。

疑问句:
What prevented you from coming yesterday? = 昨天是什么阻止了你来？
Did anything prevent them from finishing the project on time? = 有什么事情阻止他们按时完成项目吗？

否定句:
Nothing prevented him from achieving his goal. = 没有什么能阻止他实现目标。
The lack of funding didn't prevent them from continuing the research. = 资金不足并没有阻止他们继续研究。


PHRASE->
prevent from = 阻止…
prevent…from…= 阻止…做…
prevent the spread of = 阻止…的蔓延
prevent accidents = 预防事故
prevent disease = 预防疾病
