demi-devil:/ˈdɛmiˌdɛvəl/| n. |小恶魔，半恶魔|A demi-devil is a minor demon or a mischievous spirit, often depicted as less powerful or terrifying than a full-fledged devil.  The term suggests a being with some, but not all, of the characteristics of a devil. |Example: In the folklore of the region, demi-devils were said to haunt the old mill, playing tricks on travelers.|近义词：imp, sprite, hobgoblin|反义词：angel, saint
