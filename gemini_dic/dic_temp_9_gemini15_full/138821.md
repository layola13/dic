nuncius:/ˈnʌŋkiəs/| n. |使者，信使|messenger, envoy, one who announces officially;  The word derives from the Latin verb *nuntiāre*, meaning "to announce" or "to report." It's a person who delivers messages, often official ones, from one authority to another.  |Example: The nuncius arrived bearing urgent news from the emperor.  使者带着皇帝的紧急消息到达了。|近义词：messenger, envoy, emissary|反义词：recipient (in the context of receiving a message)
