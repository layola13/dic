fraternal:/frəˈtɜːrnl/| adj.|兄弟般的，手足的，兄弟会的|Relating to brothers; brotherly.  The term often implies a close bond of affection and shared experience between brothers, but can also refer more generally to the relationship or characteristics of brothers.|例句：They shared a fraternal bond forged in childhood adventures.  他们分享着在童年冒险中建立的兄弟情谊。

例句：The fraternal organization held a fundraising event. 兄弟会举办了一场募捐活动。


近义词：brotherly, sibling, fraternal twin


反义词：sororal (sisterly)


词性：形容词
