jump-starts: /ˈdʒʌmpˌstɑːrts/ | vt. |  使突然启动；使迅速恢复活力；给…以活力 |  To give something a sudden burst of energy or activity; to quickly revive something. | 例句：The new marketing campaign jump-started sales. = 新的营销活动使销售额迅速增长。  | 例句：A cup of coffee jump-started my morning. = 一杯咖啡使我早晨精神焕发。 | 近义词：revitalize, invigorate, boost, stimulate | 反义词：halt, stop, stifle, hinder | 动词


USAGE->
