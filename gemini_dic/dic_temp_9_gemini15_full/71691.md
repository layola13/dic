flauto:/ˈflaʊtoʊ/| n. |长笛|A flute is a musical instrument in the woodwind family. It produces sound when air is blown across a hole near one end, causing the air column inside to vibrate.  Flutes are known for their bright, clear tone and are used in a wide variety of musical genres. |The orchestra's flauto section played a beautiful melody. 乐团的长笛组演奏了一首优美的旋律。|近义词：pipe (in some contexts)|反义词：None.
