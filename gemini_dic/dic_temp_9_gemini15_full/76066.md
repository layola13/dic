furnishes:/ˈfɜːnɪʃɪz/| vt.|供应，提供；装备；布置；装饰|To provide or equip (a building, room, or other place) with furniture or other necessary items.|例句：The hotel furnishes each room with a television and a mini-bar.  这家酒店每个房间都配备电视和迷你吧。|例句：They furnished the apartment with antique furniture.他们用古董家具布置了公寓。|近义词：equip, supply, provide, fit out|反义词：strip, deprive, empty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company furnishes office supplies to its employees. = 公司为员工提供办公用品。
She furnishes her apartment with modern furniture. = 她用现代家具布置她的公寓。

一般过去时:
They furnished the house before they moved in. = 他们搬进去之前布置了房子。
The museum furnished the exhibit with rare artifacts. = 博物馆用稀有的文物布置了展览。

现在完成时:
He has furnished the details of the incident to the police. = 他已经向警方提供了事件的细节。
They have furnished the new office with state-of-the-art equipment. = 他们已经用最先进的设备布置了新办公室。

过去完成时:
She had furnished the room with flowers before the party. = 派对之前她已经用鲜花装饰了房间。
The builders had furnished the house with all the necessary appliances. = 建筑商已经为房子配备了所有必要的电器。

将来时:
They will furnish the report by the end of the week. = 他们将在周末前提供报告。
The company will furnish the necessary information to its clients. = 公司将向客户提供必要的信息。

虚拟语气:
If they furnished the house with better furniture, it would look much nicer. = 如果他们用更好的家具布置房子，它看起来会好得多。
I wish they furnished the office with more comfortable chairs. = 我希望他们能为办公室配备更舒适的椅子。

被动语态:
The room was furnished with elegant antiques. = 房间里摆满了优雅的古董。
The report will be furnished to the committee tomorrow. = 报告将于明天提交给委员会。

疑问句:
Does the hotel furnish towels and toiletries? = 酒店提供毛巾和洗漱用品吗？
Will the landlord furnish the apartment with appliances? = 房东会为公寓配备电器吗？

否定句:
The apartment wasn't furnished when we moved in. = 我们搬进去的时候公寓还没有装修。
They didn't furnish the necessary documents on time. = 他们没有按时提供必要的证件。
