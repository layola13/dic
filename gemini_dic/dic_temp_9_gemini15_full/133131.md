nonestimable: /nɒnˈɛstɪməbl/ | adj. | 不可估量的，无法估计的 |  incapable of being estimated or assessed; immeasurable.  | The value of her contribution to the company is nonestimable.  她的对公司的贡献是无法估量的。 | immeasurable, incalculable, unassessable, invaluable | estimable, measurable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The value of his loyalty is nonestimable. = 他忠诚的价值是不可估量的。
Her artistic talent is nonestimable. = 她的艺术天赋是不可估量的。

一般过去时:
The damage caused by the storm was nonestimable. = 风暴造成的损失是无法估量的。
The impact of his decision was nonestimable. = 他决定的影响是无法估量的。

现在完成时:
The impact of this technology on society has been nonestimable. = 这项技术对社会的影响是不可估量的。
Her influence on the art world has been nonestimable. = 她对艺术界的影响是不可估量的。

过去完成时:
The scale of the disaster had been nonestimable before the full reports came in. = 在完整的报告出来之前，灾难的规模是无法估量的。
The long-term consequences of the policy had been nonestimable at the time. = 那时这项政策的长期后果是无法估量的。

将来时:
The effects of climate change will be nonestimable in the future. = 气候变化的影响在未来将是不可估量的。
The benefits of this new discovery will be nonestimable. = 这项新发现的好处将是不可估量的。

虚拟语气:
If the risks were nonestimable, we wouldn't have undertaken the project. = 如果风险是不可估量的，我们不会承担这个项目。
I wish the benefits of this research were nonestimable. = 我希望这项研究的好处是不可估量的。（此处用法略有不自然，更合适的表达是强调其巨大而不易量化）

被动语态:

The project's long-term effects are nonestimable. = 该项目的长期影响是无法估量的。

疑问句:
Is the value of this antique nonestimable? = 这件古董的价值是否无法估量？
Are the consequences of this action nonestimable? = 这个行动的后果是否无法估量？

否定句:
The cost of the project was not nonestimable; it was actually quite high. = 这个项目的成本并非无法估量；实际上相当高。
The risks were not nonestimable; they were carefully assessed. = 风险并非无法估量；它们已被仔细评估。
