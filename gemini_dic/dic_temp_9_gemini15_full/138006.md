nooklet:/ˈnʊklət/| n.|小角落；小房间|A small nook or recess; a small, secluded place.  It is often used to describe a cozy and sheltered spot. |例句：The children found a nooklet behind the bookshelf where they could read in peace. 孩子们在书架后面找到一个可以安静阅读的小角落。


例句：She created a charming nooklet in her garden with a small table and chairs.  她在花园里用一张小桌子和椅子布置了一个迷人的小角落。


近义词：recess, alcove, cranny, corner


反义词：wide-open space, expanse


词源: nook + -let (diminutive suffix)  "nook" itself comes from Old English *nūca ("neck," "nape of the neck")  The diminutive suffix "-let" reduces the size or importance of the word it's attached to.
