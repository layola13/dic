similes:/ˈsɪməliz/| n. |明喻，比喻|A figure of speech involving the comparison of one thing with another thing of a different kind, used to make a description more emphatic or vivid.  It usually uses words such as "like" or "as".|例句：Her eyes were like the stars.  她的眼睛像星星一样明亮。
例句：He fought like a lion. 他像狮子一样战斗。|近义词：metaphor, analogy, comparison|反义词：literal statement
