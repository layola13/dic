function: /ˈfʌŋkʃən/ | n., v. | 功能；函数；作用；运转 |

英文解析:As a noun, "function" refers to the special purpose or activity for which a person, object, or institution is intended.  As a verb, it means to work or operate in a proper or intended way.

例句:
The function of the heart is to pump blood. = 心脏的功能是泵血。

The machine is not functioning properly. = 这台机器运转不正常。


近义词: (n.) role, purpose, duty, operation; (v.) operate, work, run


反义词: (n.) malfunction, failure; (v.) fail, malfunction


词性: noun, verb
