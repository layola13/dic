poppycockish:/ˈpɑːpɪkɔːkɪʃ/ | adj. | 荒谬的，无稽之谈的 |  Nonsense; absurd; ridiculous.  The word combines "poppycock" (nonsense) with the suffix "-ish," indicating a resemblance or tendency. It suggests something exhibiting the characteristics of poppycock, but perhaps to a lesser degree or in a more subtle way. | Example: His poppycockish explanation for being late didn't convince anyone.  他的迟到解释荒谬可笑，没有人相信。 | 近义词：absurd, ridiculous, nonsensical, preposterous, ludicrous | 反义词：sensible, logical, rational, reasonable |  词性: 形容词
