maharajah:/məˈhɑːrədʒə/|/ˈmɑːrɑːdʒɑː/| n. |大君，王公|A maharajah is a male monarch or ruler, especially in India.  The term is often used to refer to the former rulers of princely states in India.|Example: The maharajah lived in a magnificent palace.  这位大君住在一座宏伟的宫殿里。|近义词：prince, king, sovereign|反义词：subject, commoner|名词
