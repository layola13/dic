scootch:/skʊtʃ/| v.|轻移，蹭动|To move oneself slightly and usually quickly, often in a sideways or wriggling motion.|例句：I scootched over to make room for him. 我挪了挪位置给他腾地方。例句：She scootched closer to the fire. 她蹭近火堆取暖。|近义词：shift, nudge, wriggle|反义词：stay, remain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scootches his chair closer to the table. = 他把椅子轻轻挪近桌子。
They scootch over to make more room. = 他们挪动位置腾出更多空间。

一般过去时:
She scootched her bag onto the floor. = 她把包轻轻地滑到地板上。
I scootched closer to listen better. = 我挪近了一些以便更好地倾听。

现在完成时:
He has scootched over to the edge of his seat. = 他已经挪到座位边缘了。
She has scootched her way through the crowd. = 她已经挤过了人群。

过去完成时:
They had scootched into a corner before the lights went out. = 灯灭之前他们已经挤到一个角落里了。
She had scootched her chair closer, anticipating the announcement. = 她已经把椅子挪近了，预料到要宣布消息了。

将来时:
I will scootch over to let you pass. = 我会挪动一下让你过去。
He will scootch the papers to the side. = 他会把文件轻轻地移到一边。

虚拟语气:
If I had more space, I would scootch over. = 如果我有更多空间，我会挪动一下。
If she scootched closer, she would hear better. = 如果她挪近一些，她就能听得更清楚。

被动语态:  (Scootch is rarely used passively)
The chair was scootched closer to the table. = 椅子被挪近了桌子。（较为不自然）
The papers were scootched to the side. = 文件被移到了一边。（较为不自然）


疑问句:
Will you scootch over a bit? = 你能挪动一点吗？
Did he scootch the chair out of the way? = 他把椅子挪开了吗？


否定句:
He didn't scootch over, so I couldn't sit down. = 他没有挪动，所以我没法坐下。
She didn't scootch closer, even though it was cold. = 即使很冷，她也没有挪近。


PHRASE->
scootch over = 挪动一下

scootch aside = 挪到一边
scootch in = 挤进去
scootch along = 慢慢移动
