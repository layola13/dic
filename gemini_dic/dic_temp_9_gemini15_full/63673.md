epaulement:/eɪˈpɔːlmɒnt/|/ˈɛpoʊlmənt/| n. |护肩；肩部防护；（军事）肩部防御工事；（艺术）肩部姿势|A support or protection for the shoulder; in military contexts, a defensive work protecting a shoulder; in art, the position or pose of the shoulder.|例句：The soldier's epaulement protected him from enemy fire.  士兵的护肩保护他免受敌人的火力。

例句：The artist carefully studied the model's epaulement to capture the correct posture in the painting.艺术家仔细研究模特的肩部姿势，以便在绘画中捕捉正确的姿态。


近义词：shoulder defense, shoulder protection, rampart (in military context)| 反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to its opposite function, e.g., exposure, vulnerability.


词源:  From French *épaulement*, from *épaule* "shoulder".  Ultimately from Vulgar Latin *spaldua*, from Latin *spatula* "shoulder blade".
