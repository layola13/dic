editorial:/ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəl/ | n. |社论；编辑意见；评论文章 | A newspaper or magazine article that gives the editor's view on a subject.  It's often opinionated and aims to influence public opinion. | Example:  The newspaper published a strong editorial condemning the recent violence. 这家报纸发表了一篇强烈的社论，谴责最近的暴力事件。  |近义词：commentary, opinion piece, leader | 反义词：news report, factual account | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The newspaper publishes editorials daily. = 这家报纸每天都发表社论。
She writes editorials for a major magazine. = 她为一家大型杂志撰写社论。

一般过去时:
The editor wrote an editorial about the election. = 编辑写了一篇关于选举的社论。
They published an editorial criticizing the government's policy. = 他们发表了一篇社论批评政府的政策。

现在完成时:
The magazine has published several editorials on climate change. = 这本杂志已经发表了几篇关于气候变化的社论。
He has written many editorials throughout his career. = 他在职业生涯中撰写了许多社论。

过去完成时:
They had already published the editorial before the news broke. = 在新闻爆出之前，他们已经发表了社论。
She had written several drafts of the editorial before submitting it. = 在提交之前，她已经写了几稿社论。
