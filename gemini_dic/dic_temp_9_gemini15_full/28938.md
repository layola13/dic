carrot:/ˈkærət/| n.|胡萝卜|A long, orange root vegetable that is eaten as a food.  It is crunchy and slightly sweet.|例句：I added some carrots to the soup. 我在汤里加了一些胡萝卜。

例句：She grows her own carrots in her garden. 她在自己的花园里种植胡萝卜。|近义词：vegetable, root vegetable|反义词：无

词源:
The word "carrot" comes from the Old French word "carote," which ultimately derives from the Greek word "karoton," meaning "carrot."  The Greek word likely came from a word in a now-extinct language of Persia or the Middle East.
