feeding:/ˈfiːdɪŋ/| n. v.|喂养；饲养；供给|The act of giving food to someone or something; the process of supplying something with nourishment.  |例句：The feeding of the baby birds is a crucial task for their survival.  喂养幼鸟对它们的生存至关重要。

例句：The farmer is responsible for the feeding of his livestock.  这位农民负责喂养他的牲畜。


近义词：nourishing, supplying, provisioning|反义词：starving, depriving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He feeds his dog twice a day. = 他每天喂他的狗两次。
She feeds the birds in the park. = 她在公园里喂鸟。

一般过去时:
I fed the cat last night. = 我昨晚喂了猫。
They fed the animals before leaving. = 他们离开前喂了动物。

现在完成时:
We have fed the chickens. = 我们已经喂了鸡。
She has fed the baby. = 她已经喂了婴儿。

过去完成时:
The birds had already been fed before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前鸟儿已经被喂过了。
He had fed the horse before the race. = 他在比赛前已经喂了马。

将来时:
I will feed the fish tomorrow. = 我明天将喂鱼。
They will feed the refugees. = 他们将喂饱难民。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would feed the stray cats. = 如果我有更多时间，我会喂那些流浪猫。
I wish I had fed the dog earlier. = 我希望我早点喂了狗。

被动语态:
The animals are fed regularly. = 动物们定期被喂食。
The baby was fed with a bottle. = 婴儿是用奶瓶喂养的。

疑问句:
Do you feed your pets regularly? = 你定期喂养你的宠物吗？
How often do they feed the lions? = 他们多久喂一次狮子？

否定句:
I didn't feed the dog this morning. = 我今天早上没有喂狗。
They don't feed the birds anymore. = 他们不再喂鸟了。

词源:
Old English *fēdan, from Proto-Germanic *fōdjaną (source also of Old Saxon fōdian, Old High German fotan, Old Norse fóða, Gothic fodjan), from PIE root *peh₂- "to feed, fatten."
