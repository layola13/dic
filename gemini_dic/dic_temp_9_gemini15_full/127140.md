nattered:/ˈnætəd/| v.|喋喋不休地谈论，唠叨|To talk continuously and inconsequentially; chatter idly.|例句：They nattered on about their holidays for hours. 他们喋喋不休地谈论他们的假期好几个小时。|近义词：chattered, babbled, rambled|反义词：silent, quiet


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They natter about politics all day. = 他们整天喋喋不休地谈论政治。
He natters incessantly. = 他没完没了地唠叨。


一般过去时:
She nattered away to her friend about her new dress. = 她向她的朋友喋喋不休地谈论她的新衣服。
We nattered for hours about old times. = 我们聊了几个小时关于旧时光的事。


现在完成时:
They have nattered away the entire afternoon. = 他们整个下午都在喋喋不休。
She has nattered on about her problems for weeks. = 她已经抱怨她的问题好几个星期了。


过去完成时:
They had nattered for so long that everyone was tired. = 他们喋喋不休了这么久，以至于每个人都累了。
She had nattered until she was hoarse. = 她一直唠叨到声音嘶哑。


将来时:
They will natter away the whole evening. = 他们会整个晚上喋喋不休。
He will natter on about his accomplishments. = 他会喋喋不休地谈论他的成就。


虚拟语气:
If they nattered less, we could get some work done. = 如果他们少唠叨一些，我们就能完成一些工作。
I wish they wouldn't natter so much. = 我希望他们不要说得那么多。


被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Were they nattering about the game? = 他们在喋喋不休地谈论比赛吗？
Is he always nattering on like this? = 他总是这样喋喋不休吗？


否定句:
They didn't natter much at the party. = 他们在派对上没有怎么唠叨。
He didn't natter about his problems. = 他没有抱怨他的问题。
