reformat:/ˌriːˈfɔːrmæt/ | vt. |重新格式化；改版 | To change the format of something, especially a computer file or disk.  |例句：I need to reformat my hard drive. 我需要重新格式化我的硬盘。

例句：The editor decided to reformat the entire magazine. 编辑决定对整本杂志重新排版。


近义词：reshape, restructure, reorganize | 反义词：leave as is, maintain the original format | 词性：动词
