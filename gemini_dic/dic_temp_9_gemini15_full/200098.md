skiascope:/ˈskaɪəskoʊp/| n. |暗室检影仪；视网膜检影镜|An instrument used to examine the interior of the eye, especially the retina and the optic nerve.  It uses a light source and a series of lenses to illuminate and magnify the structures within the eye. |Example: The ophthalmologist used a skiascope to determine the patient's refractive error. 眼科医生使用暗室检影仪来确定病人的屈光不正。|近义词：retinoscope|反义词：无|名词
