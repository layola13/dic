inconsistent:/ˌɪnkənˈsɪstənt/| adj.|不一致的；前后矛盾的；反复无常的 | lacking consistency; not staying the same;  varying.  It implies a lack of uniformity or agreement between different parts or aspects of something.  |例句：His behavior is inconsistent; one minute he's happy, the next he's angry. 他的行为前后矛盾；一会儿高兴，一会儿生气。

例句：The data from the two experiments were inconsistent, requiring further investigation.  两个实验的数据不一致，需要进一步调查。


近义词：contradictory, conflicting, variable, erratic, fluctuating, unstable


反义词：consistent, uniform, harmonious, stable, unchanging


词性:形容词
