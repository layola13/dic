half-learned:/ˌhɑːfˈlɜːrnd/| adj. | 半懂的；一知半解的 | Partially understood or learned; having only a superficial knowledge of something.  |  He had a half-learned understanding of the subject. 他对这个主题只有一知半解。|近义词：partially learned, superficially learned, incompletely understood | 反义词：fully learned, mastered, proficient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is half-learned in mathematics. = 他的数学知识很肤浅。
The students are half-learned in this new technology. = 学生们对这项新技术一知半解。


一般过去时:
She was half-learned in the art of cooking. = 她烹饪技巧很生疏。
They were half-learned in the history of that country. = 他们对那个国家的历史了解不多。


现在完成时:
He has been half-learned in his studies. = 他的学习一直很粗浅。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
If I were half-learned in this topic, I wouldn't try to teach it. = 如果我对这个话题一知半解，我不会尝试去教它。

被动语态:
