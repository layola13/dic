dwellings:/ˈdwɛlɪŋz/ | n. | 住宅，住所 | Buildings or places where people live.  They can range from single-family homes to apartment buildings. | Example: The new housing development includes many modern dwellings. 新的住宅开发项目包括许多现代化的住宅。  The ancient dwellings were built into the cliffs. 古代的住宅建在悬崖上。 |近义词：homes, residences, abodes, houses | 反义词： (无明确的反义词，可考虑与“uninhabited places”相对) | 名词
