indignify:/ɪnˈdɪɡnɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 使不光彩；贬低；羞辱 | To lower the dignity or status of someone or something; to disgrace or dishonor. | 例句：His actions indignified the entire family. = 他的行为使整个家族蒙羞。

His behavior indignified the profession. = 他的行为败坏了这个行业的名声。


近义词：debase, degrade, humiliate, shame, disgrace | 反义词：honor, exalt, dignify, elevate


词源:  The word "indignify" comes from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") and the word "dignify," which itself derives from the Latin word "dignitas" (meaning "worthiness," "rank," or "dignity").  Therefore, "indignify" literally means "to not dignify" or "to make undignified."
