mennonite:/ˈmɛnənˌaɪt/| n. |门诺派教徒；门诺派教友|A member of a Protestant denomination that originated in the 16th century in Switzerland and the Netherlands. Mennonites are known for their pacifism, simple living, and community.  They often practice adult baptism and avoid military service.|  The Mennonite community is known for its charitable work. 门诺派教友社团以其慈善工作而闻名。| Amish, Anabaptist | Catholic, Baptist | 名词
