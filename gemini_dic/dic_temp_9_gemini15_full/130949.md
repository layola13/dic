nonassimilating: /ˌnɒnəˈsɪməleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 未同化的；不融合的 | Not becoming integrated into the dominant culture or society.  This adjective describes individuals, groups, or cultures that maintain their distinct identity and practices even when surrounded by a larger, more dominant culture. It implies a resistance to assimilation. | Example: The nonassimilating immigrant community maintained its traditional language and customs.  未同化的移民群体保留了其传统语言和习俗。 | 近义词: unintegrated, separate, distinct | 反义词: assimilated, integrated, acculturated
