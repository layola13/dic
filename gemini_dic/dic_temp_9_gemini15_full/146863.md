overindustrializes: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzɪz/ | vt. |过度工业化|To industrialize to an excessive degree, often resulting in negative consequences for the environment and society.  |例句：The rapid economic growth overindustrialized the region, leading to severe air pollution and water contamination.  迅速的经济增长使该地区过度工业化，导致严重的空气污染和水污染。|近义词：overdevelop, hyperindustrialize |反义词：deindustrialize, underdevelop |动词
