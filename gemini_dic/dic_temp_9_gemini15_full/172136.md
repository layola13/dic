purred:/pɜːrd/| v.|发出呼噜声，低声说|To make a low, continuous vibrating sound, like a cat.  It can also mean to speak softly or smoothly.|例句：The cat purred contentedly on my lap. 猫舒服地在我腿上发出呼噜声。

The engine purred quietly. 引擎发出轻微的呼噜声。|近义词：hummed, droned, murmured|反义词：roared, shrieked, screamed|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat purrs every time it sees its food.  = 猫每次看到食物都会发出呼噜声。
He purrs a sweet nothings in her ear. = 他在她耳边轻声细语甜言蜜语。


一般过去时:
The engine purred smoothly during the journey. = 旅途中引擎平稳地发出呼噜声。
She purred her agreement to the proposal. = 她轻轻地表示同意这项提议。


现在完成时:
The kitten has purred itself to sleep. = 小猫已经呼噜呼噜地睡着了。
The machine has purred consistently for years. = 这台机器多年来一直运行平稳。


过去完成时:
The cat had purred happily before falling asleep. = 猫在睡着前快乐地发出呼噜声。
She had purred the words with a satisfied smile. = 她带着满意的笑容轻声细语地说出那些话。


将来时:

I think he will purr with delight at the news. = 我想他会听到这个消息后欣喜若狂。


虚拟语气:
If the engine purred, it would mean the car is running fine. = 如果引擎发出呼噜声，那就意味着汽车运转良好。
I wish the machine would purr more quietly. = 我希望机器能更安静地运转。


被动语态:
The song was purred softly in the background. = 这首歌在背景中轻轻地哼唱着。 (Note: Passive voice is less common with "purr")


疑问句:
Does your cat purr when you pet it? = 你抚摸你的猫时，它会发出呼噜声吗？
Did the motor purr when you started it? = 你启动马达时，它发出呼噜声了吗？


否定句:
The engine didn't purr; it made a strange noise. = 引擎没有发出呼噜声；它发出了一种奇怪的声音。
She didn't purr her approval; she stated it directly. = 她没有轻声细语地表示赞同；她直接说了出来。
