gladdened:/ˈɡlædən(d)/| v. |使高兴；使欣喜|To make someone feel happy and pleased.  |例句：The good news gladdened her heart. 好消息使她欣喜若狂。 |例句：The children were gladdened by the unexpected gift. 孩子们收到意外的礼物后非常高兴。|近义词：delighted, pleased, cheered, thrilled |反义词：saddened, disheartened, depressed |动词
