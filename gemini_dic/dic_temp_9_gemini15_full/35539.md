coal-fired: /ˈkoʊl faɪrd/ | adj. | 燃煤的；用煤烧的 |  Describes something that is powered or heated by burning coal.  | 例句：The coal-fired power plant emits a significant amount of greenhouse gases.  燃煤发电厂排放大量的温室气体。

The factory relies heavily on coal-fired boilers for heating. 这家工厂严重依赖燃煤锅炉供暖。


近义词：coal-burning, coal-powered | 反义词：nuclear-powered, solar-powered, gas-fired

词性：形容词
