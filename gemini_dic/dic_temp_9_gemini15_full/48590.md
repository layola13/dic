defecated:/ˈdɛfɪkeɪtɪd/| adj. v.|排便的；已排便的| Having evacuated the bowels.  The term typically refers to the act of having a bowel movement, and can be used to describe a person, animal, or even a state of being. |例句：The baby defecated in its diaper. = 婴儿在尿布里大便了。

The dog defecated on the lawn. = 狗在草坪上拉屎了。


近义词：excreted, evacuated (bowels), eliminated (waste)
反义词：constipated (unable to defecate)
