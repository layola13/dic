caved:/keɪvd/| v.|坍塌；屈服；(尤指因压力)让步|To collapse, give way, or yield (especially under pressure).|例句：The cave caved in after the heavy rain. 大雨过后山洞坍塌了。|例句：After hours of negotiation, he finally caved and agreed to the deal.经过数小时的谈判，他最终让步并同意了这笔交易。|近义词：collapsed, surrendered, yielded, succumbed|反义词：resisted, persevered, held firm


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The ice caves in the winter. 冬天冰洞会坍塌。
He caves under pressure easily. 他很容易在压力下屈服。


一般过去时:
The roof caved in during the storm.暴风雨期间屋顶坍塌了。
She caved to his demands. 她屈服于他的要求。


现在完成时:
The mine has caved in several times this year.今年矿井已经坍塌了好几次了。
He has caved to public pressure. 他已经屈服于公众压力。


过去完成时:
The tunnel had caved in before the rescue team arrived.救援队到达之前，隧道已经坍塌了。
They had caved to the threat. 他们已经屈服于威胁。


将来时:
The old building will cave in soon if it is not repaired.如果这座旧建筑不进行维修，它很快就会坍塌。
I think he will cave in eventually. 我认为他最终会让步的。


虚拟语气:
If the ground were to cave in, we would be in danger.如果地面坍塌，我们将处于危险之中。
I wish I hadn't caved in to their demands. 我真希望我没有屈服于他们的要求。


被动语态:
The road was caved in by the earthquake. 道路被地震破坏了。
The agreement was caved in. 协议被放弃了。


疑问句:
Did the mountain cave in?  山崩了吗？
Will he cave in to my requests? 他会答应我的要求吗？


否定句:
The structure didn't cave in. 结构没有坍塌。
He didn't cave in under pressure. 他没有在压力下屈服。


PHRASE->
cave in to: 屈服于
cave in on: 向…坍塌
cave in under: 在…下坍塌
cave in completely: 完全坍塌
cave in and: 屈服并…
