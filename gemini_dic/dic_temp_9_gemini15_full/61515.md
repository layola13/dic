embryo:/ˈɛmbrɪoʊ/ | n. |胚胎，胚 | An embryo is an unborn or unhatched offspring in the process of development, in animals.  In plants, it refers to the young plant within the seed. | Example: The embryo is developing rapidly in the womb.  这个胚胎在子宫里快速发育。  The seeds contained fully developed embryos. 这些种子包含了完全发育的胚胎。 | 近义词：fetus (后期胚胎) | 反义词：adult
