reauthentications:/ˌriːˌɔːθɛn.tɪˈkeɪ.ʃənz/| n. |重新验证；再次认证|The process of verifying or authenticating something again, often after a period of time or due to suspicion of fraud or tampering.  It involves confirming the legitimacy or genuineness of something a second time.  |例句：The security system requires frequent reauthentications to ensure data security. 安全系统需要频繁的重新验证以确保数据安全。

The bank initiated reauthentications of all online transactions following a security breach.  银行在发生安全漏洞后启动了所有在线交易的重新验证。|近义词：revalidations, recertifications, reverifications|反义词：invalidations, denials
