epidemiologists:/ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiˈɑːlədʒɪsts/ | n. | 流行病学家 | Experts who study the patterns, causes, and effects of diseases in populations.  They investigate outbreaks of infectious diseases, analyze disease trends, and develop strategies for prevention and control. |  Epidemiologists are working to understand the spread of the new virus. 流行病学家正在努力了解这种新病毒的传播。  The team of epidemiologists traced the source of the outbreak to contaminated water. 流行病学家小组将疫情的源头追溯到受污染的水源。|  public health officials, disease detectives |  (无明显的反义词) | 名词
