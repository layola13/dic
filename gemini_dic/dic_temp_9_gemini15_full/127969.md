neotectonic:/ˌniːoʊtɛkˈtɑːnɪk/| adj. |新构造的，新构造运动的 |Relating to the most recent phase of tectonic activity, typically encompassing the last few million years.  This period is characterized by significant geological events such as earthquakes, volcanic eruptions, and mountain building, often still ongoing. |例句：The region's neotectonic activity is responsible for its frequent earthquakes.  该地区的新构造活动是其频繁发生地震的原因。

The neotectonic study of the area revealed a previously unknown fault line. 对该地区的新构造研究揭示了一条以前未知的断层线。|近义词：recent tectonic, late-stage tectonic |反义词：paleotectonic (ancient tectonic)
