imbosture: /ɪmˈbɒstʃər/ | n. | 欺骗；骗局；冒名顶替 | The act of imposing something falsely or deceptively; a fraud; an imposture.  It refers to a deliberate act of deception, often involving impersonation or misrepresentation. | 例句：His entire career was built on a massive imposture.  他的整个职业生涯都是建立在一个巨大的骗局之上。

His claim to be a doctor was revealed as an imposture. 他的医生身份被揭露为一个骗局。


近义词：fraud, deception, hoax, sham, swindle, trickery

反义词：truth, honesty, genuineness, authenticity


词性：名词
