compt:/kɒmpt/ | n. | 计算，计算器 |  An archaic term for a count or computation; a shortened form of "account" or "computation".  It is rarely used in modern English. |  The compt of the treasury was meticulously kept. = 国库的计算被一丝不苟地保存着。| 近义词: account, calculation, reckoning | 反义词: N/A | 名词


USAGE->
This word is largely obsolete and doesn't lend itself to grammatical tense variations in the way common verbs do.  The usage examples below illustrate its historical context, not modern applications.

一般现在时:
The compt shows a surplus. = 账目显示盈余。
The compt is being reviewed. = 账目正在被审查。


一般过去时:
The compt was inaccurate. = 账目不准确。
They completed the compt yesterday. = 他们昨天完成了计算。


现在完成时:
The compt has been finalized. = 账目已经最终确定。
We have not yet finished the compt. = 我们还没有完成计算。


过去完成时:
The compt had already been audited. = 账目已经过审计。
They had completed the compt before the meeting. = 他们在会议前完成了计算。


将来时:
The compt will be presented tomorrow. = 账目将在明天提交。
We will begin the compt next week. = 我们下周将开始计算。


虚拟语气:
If the compt were accurate, we wouldn't have this problem. = 如果账目准确，我们就不会有这个问题。
I wish the compt had been more detailed. = 我希望账目更详细一些。


被动语态:
The compt was prepared by the accountant. = 账目是由会计准备的。
The compt is being questioned. = 账目正在受到质疑。


疑问句:
Is the compt complete? = 账目完整吗？
What does the compt reveal? = 账目显示什么？


否定句:
The compt is not balanced. = 账目不平衡。
They did not complete the compt on time. = 他们没有按时完成计算。


PHRASE->
Because "compt" is archaic, there aren't common phrases associated with it in modern English.  Phrases using "account" or "computation" would be appropriate substitutes.  For example:


take into account = 考虑
on account of = 因为
account for = 解释
computation time = 计算时间
complex computation = 复杂计算
