neighbours:/ˈneɪbərz/ | n. |邻居们|People who live near you.|My neighbours are very friendly.我的邻居们都很友好。|近义词：residents,dwellers|反义词：strangers


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My neighbours often have barbecues. = 我的邻居们经常举行烧烤派对。
The neighbours next door are always noisy. = 隔壁的邻居总是很吵闹。


一般过去时:
My neighbours helped me move last week. = 我的邻居们上周帮我搬家。
The neighbours complained about the loud music. = 邻居们抱怨音乐声太大。


现在完成时:
My neighbours have lived here for ten years. = 我的邻居们在这里住了十年了。
The neighbours have never caused any trouble. = 邻居们从未惹过任何麻烦。


过去完成时:
I had already met my neighbours before the party. = 我在派对之前就已经见过我的邻居们了。
The neighbours had complained several times before calling the police. = 邻居们在报警之前已经抱怨过好几次了。


将来时:
My neighbours will be having a party next weekend. = 我的邻居们下周末将举行一个派对。
I think the neighbours will appreciate the quiet. = 我认为邻居们会喜欢这份宁静。


虚拟语气:
If I had better neighbours, I would feel safer. = 如果我有更好的邻居，我会感觉更安全。
I wish my neighbours were less noisy. = 我希望我的邻居们不那么吵闹。


被动语态:
The neighbours were disturbed by the construction work. = 邻居们被施工扰乱了。
The noise complaints were addressed by the neighbours' association. = 噪音投诉由邻居协会处理。


疑问句:
Do you know your neighbours well? = 你认识你的邻居们吗？
Have you ever had problems with your neighbours? = 你曾经与你的邻居们发生过冲突吗？


否定句:
I don't know my neighbours very well. = 我不太认识我的邻居们。
My neighbours didn't seem to mind the noise. = 我的邻居们似乎并不介意噪音。


PHRASE->
good neighbours = 好邻居
bad neighbours = 差邻居
noisy neighbours = 吵闹的邻居
helpful neighbours = 乐于助人的邻居
friendly neighbours = 友好的邻居

词源:
The word "neighbour" comes from the Old English word "neahgebur," which is a compound of "neah" (near) and "gebur" (dweller, inhabitant, farmer).  The modern spelling and pronunciation evolved over time through Middle English.
