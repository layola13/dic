dragging: /ˈdræɡɪŋ/ | v. | 拖拉；拖延；使缓慢移动 |  To pull (something heavy) along with effort; to make something move slowly and with difficulty; to prolong something in a tedious way. | 例句：He was dragging a heavy suitcase. 他拖着一个沉重的行李箱。

例句：The meeting was dragging on. 会议拖得很长。


近义词：pulling, hauling, towing, delaying, prolonging | 反义词：pushing, accelerating, hastening


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drags the heavy box across the room. 他把沉重的箱子拖过房间。
She is dragging her feet on the project. 她在这个项目上拖拖拉拉。


一般过去时:
They dragged the boat onto the shore. 他们把船拖到岸上。
He dragged his feet and missed the deadline. 他拖延了时间，错过了截止日期。


现在完成时:
I have been dragging myself to work all week. 我整个星期都在勉强自己去上班。
She has dragged the project out for months. 她把项目拖延了几个月。


过去完成时:
They had dragged the furniture out before the storm hit. 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经把家具搬了出去。
He had dragged his feet, resulting in a missed opportunity. 他磨磨蹭蹭，错失了一个机会。


将来时:
We will drag the canoe to the lake. 我们会把独木舟拖到湖边。
They will be dragging their feet until the very last minute. 他们会拖到最后一分钟。


虚拟语气:
If I had known it would be so heavy, I wouldn't have dragged it alone. 如果我知道它这么重，我不会一个人拖。
I wish I hadn't dragged my feet on that assignment. 我真希望我没有拖延那项作业。


被动语态:
The heavy table was dragged into the room. 那张沉重的桌子被拖进了房间。
The decision was dragged out for weeks.  这个决定被拖延了好几个星期。


疑问句:
Are you dragging your feet on this task? 你是不是在这个任务上拖延了？
Is he dragging that heavy object all by himself? 他一个人拖着那个沉重的东西吗？


否定句:
He didn't drag the bag, he carried it. 他没有拖袋子，他是扛着的。
She didn't drag her feet; she completed the project on time. 她没有拖延，她按时完成了项目。


PHRASE->
drag one's feet: 拖延；磨蹭
drag oneself:  勉强；费力地
drag someone down:  使某人消沉；拖累某人
drag something out: 拖延；拉长
drag on:  持续过久；拖延
