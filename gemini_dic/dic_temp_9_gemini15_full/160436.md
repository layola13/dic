popularises:/ˈpɒpjʊləraɪzɪz/ | vt. |使普及；推广；宣传 | To make (something) popular or widely known.  | 例句：The invention of the internet popularised instant communication.  互联网的发明使即时通讯普及化了。 | 例句：She popularised the use of herbs in cooking. 她推广了在烹饪中使用香草。 | 近义词：promote, disseminate, spread, publicize | 反义词：suppress, obscure, conceal | 动词


USAGE->
