overqualifications:/ˌoʊvərˌkwɑːlɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |资历过高；过度资格|Excessive qualifications for a job or position; possessing skills and experience exceeding the requirements of a role.  This can sometimes be a disadvantage to the applicant. |例句：Her overqualifications for the administrative assistant position made the hiring manager hesitant.  她的行政助理职位资历过高，让招聘经理犹豫不决。

例句：His extensive experience in finance led to overqualifications for the entry-level job. 他在金融领域的丰富经验导致他对于入门级工作的资历过高。


近义词：excess qualifications, excessive experience, over-skilled


反义词：underqualified, lacking experience


词性: 名词
