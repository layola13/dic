opinion:/əˈpɪnjən/| n.|观点，意见，看法|A belief or judgment about something; a personal view.  It often involves a judgment or assessment.|例句：What's your opinion on the new policy? 你对这项新政策有什么看法？

例句：I respect your opinion, even if I don't agree with it. 我尊重你的意见，即使我不同意。

近义词：view, belief, judgment, thought, assessment

反义词：fact, truth (in contrast to subjective opinion)


词性:名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your opinion on this matter? = 你对此事的看法是什么？
His opinion always matters to me. = 他的意见对我来说总是很重要。


一般过去时:
What was your opinion of the movie? = 你对这部电影的评价如何？
Her opinion changed after she learned more. = 在她了解更多之后，她的意见改变了。


现在完成时:
Have you formed an opinion yet? = 你已经形成意见了吗？
I have changed my opinion on that subject. = 我已经改变了对那个主题的看法。


过去完成时:
Had he already formed his opinion before the meeting? = 他在会议之前就已经形成自己的意见了吗？
She had changed her opinion by the time the debate ended. = 在辩论结束时，她已经改变了主意。


将来时:
What will be your opinion after you have considered it? = 你考虑之后会有什么看法？
I will let you know my opinion tomorrow. = 我明天会告诉你我的意见。


虚拟语气:
If I knew his opinion, I would act accordingly. = 如果我知道他的意见，我会相应地采取行动。
I wish I knew what his opinion was on this. = 我真希望知道他对这件事的看法。


被动语态:
His opinion was widely respected. = 他的意见受到广泛尊重。
Her opinion is often sought after by the team. = 她的意见经常被团队征求。


疑问句:
What is your opinion? = 你的意见是什么？
What's your opinion of the proposed changes? = 你对拟议的更改有何看法？


否定句:
I don't have a strong opinion on the matter. = 我对此事没有强烈的看法。
He didn't express his opinion during the meeting. = 他在会议上没有表达自己的意见。


PHRASE->
in my opinion = 依我看
have an opinion = 有看法
change one's opinion = 改变主意
express an opinion = 表达意见
strong opinion = 强烈的意见
