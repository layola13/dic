implores:/ɪmˈplɔːrz/| vt.|恳求；哀求；乞求|To ask someone earnestly or desperately to do something.  It implies a strong feeling of need or urgency.|例句：She implored him to reconsider his decision. 她恳求他重新考虑他的决定。
例句：The starving people implored the authorities for food. 饥饿的人们恳求当局提供食物。|近义词：beg, beseech, entreat, plead|反义词：command, order, demand|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He implores his boss for a raise. = 他恳求老板加薪。
She implores her friend for forgiveness. = 她恳求朋友原谅她。

一般过去时:
He implored his father to help him. = 他恳求父亲帮助他。
She implored the judge for mercy. = 她恳求法官开恩。

现在完成时:
He has implored her many times to leave. = 他已经多次恳求她离开。
She has implored God for a miracle. = 她一直在向上帝祈祷奇迹。

过去完成时:
He had implored her to reconsider before she left. = 他在她离开之前就恳求她重新考虑。
She had implored him to change his ways, but he refused. = 她曾恳求他改变自己的方式，但他拒绝了。

将来时:
He will implore his family for support. = 他将恳求家人支持。
She will implore the doctor to save her child. = 她将恳求医生救她的孩子。

虚拟语气:
I would implore him to change his mind if I thought it would help. = 如果我认为这会有帮助，我会恳求他改变主意。
If I were you, I would implore her to stay. = 如果我是你，我会恳求她留下。

被动语态:
His pleas were implored by the court. = 法院受理了他的恳求。
The prisoner was implored by his family to surrender. = 囚犯被家人恳求投降。

疑问句:
Will she implore him to stay? = 她会恳求他留下吗？
Did he implore you for help? = 他向你求助了吗？

否定句:
He did not implore the judge for leniency. = 他没有恳求法官从轻发落。
She didn't implore him to reconsider. = 她没有恳求他重新考虑。
