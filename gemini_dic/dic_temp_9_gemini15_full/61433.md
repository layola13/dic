embolite:/ˈɛmbəlaɪt/| n. |卤化银矿物|A mineral consisting of a silver chloro-bromide, (Ag(Cl,Br)), found in some silver ores. It is usually massive or in granular aggregates.  |Example:  The geologist identified the sample as embolite, a valuable source of silver. 地质学家将样本鉴定为角银矿，一种重要的银矿石。|近义词: cerargyrite |反义词: N/A
