circuit:/ˈsɜːkɪt/ | /ˈsɝːkɪt/| n. |电路，线路；巡回；赛道|A circuit is a closed path or loop through which an electrical current can flow.  It can also refer to a route or journey, especially one that is circular or involves returning to the starting point. |例句：The electrician checked the electrical circuit for any faults. 电工检查电路是否有故障。

例句：The racing circuit is known for its challenging turns.  这条赛车场以其具有挑战性的弯道而闻名。


近义词：course, route, loop, track | 反义词：None specifically.
