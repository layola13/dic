hernias:/ˈhɜːniəz/| n.|疝气；(肌肉或器官的)突出|A hernia is a protrusion of an organ or tissue through a weak area in the muscle wall that normally contains it.  Hernias can occur in various parts of the body, most commonly in the abdomen.|例句：He developed a hernia while lifting heavy boxes. 他在搬运重物时患了疝气。|例句：The surgeon repaired the inguinal hernia. 外科医生修复了腹股沟疝气。|近义词：protrusion, rupture|反义词：无
