The hikers, equipped with only basic supplies, were true self-survivors, navigating the challenging terrain and finding food and shelter on their own.|这些徒步旅行者只携带基本物资，是真正的自力更生者，他们独自穿越了充满挑战的地形，并找到了食物和住所。|近义词：self-reliant individuals, independent people, resourceful individuals |反义词：dependents, those in need of assistance
