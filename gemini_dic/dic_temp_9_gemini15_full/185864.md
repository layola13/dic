revocability:/ˌrɛvəˈkeɪbɪləti/| n. |可撤销性，可废止性 |The quality or state of being revocable; the possibility of being revoked or cancelled.  It refers to the ability to withdraw, cancel, or annul something previously granted or enacted. |Example: The contract lacked revocability, meaning it couldn't be canceled unilaterally.  合同缺乏可撤销性，这意味着它不能单方面取消。  The revocability of the license is a key consideration for users. 许可证的可撤销性是用户需要重点考虑的问题。|近义词：cancellability, revocability, annulment |反义词：irrevocability, permanence
