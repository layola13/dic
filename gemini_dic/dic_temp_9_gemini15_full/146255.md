overextravagant: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈstrævəɡənt/ | adj. | 过度奢侈的，极度浪费的 | Excessively extravagant; beyond what is reasonable or necessary.  It implies a degree of extravagance that is excessive and perhaps even foolish. |例句：Her overextravagant spending habits led to significant debt. 她的过度奢侈的消费习惯导致了巨额债务。

例句：The overextravagant wedding ceremony was criticized by many guests.  这场过度奢侈的婚礼仪式受到了许多宾客的批评。


近义词：excessive, prodigal, lavish, sumptuous, wasteful, immoderate

反义词：frugal, economical, thrifty, sparing, modest


词源:

* **over-**:  prefix indicating "excessive," "too much," or "beyond."  源自古英语 *ofer*,  具有“超过，越过”的意思。

* **extravagant**:  from Old French *extravagant*, from Latin *extravagans*, present participle of *extravagari* "to wander outside," literally "to wander beyond."  The sense of "excessive spending" developed later, referring to spending that goes beyond reasonable bounds.
