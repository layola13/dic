increments:/ˈɪŋkrɪmənts/ | n. |增量；增加；逐步增长 |An increment is an increase or addition, especially one of a series of increases.  It can refer to a step-by-step increase in size, amount, or degree. |例句：The salary increases were given in small increments.  薪水增长是以小额增量发放的。

The software updates were released in increments. 软件更新是分批发布的。

|近义词：increases, additions, additions, rises, steps |反义词：decrements, reductions, decreases |名词
