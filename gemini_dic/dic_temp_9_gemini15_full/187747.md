rootlet:/ˈruːtlət/| n. |小根；细根 | A small root, especially a lateral root branching from a larger root.  |例句：The rootlet system of the plant was surprisingly extensive. 这株植物的细根系统出乎意料地发达。

例句：The tiny rootlets absorbed water and nutrients from the soil.  细小的根须从土壤中吸收水分和养分。


近义词：fibril, radicle, rootlet  |反义词：trunk, stem |词性：名词
