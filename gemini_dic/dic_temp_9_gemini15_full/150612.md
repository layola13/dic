papery:/ˈpeɪpəri/| adj. |纸质的；像纸一样的；脆弱的 |resembling paper in thinness, texture, or brittleness.  The adjective "papery" describes something that is thin, light, and easily torn, like paper.  It can also refer to a pale, almost translucent quality. |例句：The old leaves were papery and brittle.  那些老叶子又薄又脆。

The petals of the flower were papery thin. 花瓣薄如纸。

近义词：thin, flimsy, delicate, fragile, diaphanous |反义词：thick, sturdy, robust, strong
