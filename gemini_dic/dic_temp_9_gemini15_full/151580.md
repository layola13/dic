parterre:/pɑːrˈtɛər/| n.| 花坛；（剧院）舞台正前方座席区 |A parterre is a decorative pattern or design of flowers or plants in a garden.  It can also refer to the area of level seating in front of a stage in a theater, especially one that is slightly sunken. |例句：The palace gardens boasted a magnificent parterre of colorful flowers. 皇宫花园里有着壮丽多彩的花坛。

The audience in the parterre had the best view of the stage. 舞台正前方的观众席视野最佳。|近义词：flower bed, garden plot,  orchestra (in theater context)|反义词：unplanted area, balcony (in theater context)|名词
