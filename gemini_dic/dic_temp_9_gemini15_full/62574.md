enervate:/ˈɛnərveɪt/| vt.|使衰弱；使无力；使疲惫|To weaken or destroy the strength or vitality of (someone or something).  It can refer to physical or mental weakening.|

例句：

The long journey enervated the travelers. = 漫长的旅程使旅行者疲惫不堪。

The heat enervated her, leaving her too tired to work. = 炎热的天气使她衰弱，让她疲惫不堪无法工作。


近义词：weaken, debilitate, sap, exhaust, drain

反义词：invigorate, strengthen, energize

词性：动词
