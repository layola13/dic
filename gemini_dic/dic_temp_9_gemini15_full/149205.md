oxygenous:/ɒkˈsɪdʒənəs/ | adj. |含氧的；产氧的|Containing or producing oxygen.  The term refers to substances or processes that are related to oxygen. For example, an oxygenous environment would be one rich in oxygen, while an oxygenous process might be one that generates oxygen.|Example:  The oxygenous atmosphere of Earth supports life. 地球富含氧气的环境支持生命。The process of photosynthesis is oxygenous. 光合作用是一个产氧过程。|近义词：oxygenated, aerated |反义词：anoxic, anaerobic|形容词
