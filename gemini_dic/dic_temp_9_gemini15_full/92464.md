hypostatically:/ˌhaɪpəʊˈstætɪkli/| adv.|  假设地；根据本体论地 |  In a manner pertaining to hypostasis; in a way that relates to a distinct substance or underlying reality.  It often refers to theological discussions about the nature of the Trinity. | Example: The theologian argued hypostatically for the distinct persons within the one Godhead.  神学家根据本体论论证了三位一体中各自独特的位格。 | 近义词: ontologically, essentially, fundamentally | 反义词: superficially, nominally, accidentally |  词性:副词

Example 1 (Theological context):
