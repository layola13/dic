judaize:/ˈdʒuːdeɪz/| vt.|使犹太化；按犹太教的仪式举行|To cause (someone) to adopt Jewish customs, beliefs, or practices.  It can also refer to interpreting something from a Jewish perspective.|例句：They attempted to judaize the conquered peoples.  他们试图使被征服的民族犹太化。

例句：The scholar judaized the interpretation of the ancient text.  这位学者从犹太人的角度解释了古代文本。


近义词：Jewish, Hebraize


反义词：de-Judaize, Christianize


词性：动词
