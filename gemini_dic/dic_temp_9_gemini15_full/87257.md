heritable:/ˈhɛrɪtəbl/| adj.|可遗传的，遗传的|capable of being inherited;transmissible from parent to offspring.|The study showed a strong heritable component to the disease. 这项研究表明该疾病具有很强的遗传成分。|近义词：inheritable,transmissible|反义词：nonheritable, acquired


USAGE->

一般现在时:
This trait is heritable. = 这个性状是可遗传的。
Height is partially heritable. = 身高部分是可遗传的。

一般过去时:
The gene was heritable. = 这个基因是可遗传的。
That characteristic was considered heritable. = 那个特性被认为是可遗传的。

现在完成时:
Scientists have found many heritable traits. = 科学家们已经发现了许多可遗传的性状。
Researchers have proven that the disease is heritable. = 研究人员已经证明这种疾病是可遗传的。

过去完成时:
It had been established that the condition was heritable. = 已经确定该疾病是可遗传的。
They had previously thought the trait was not heritable. = 他们以前认为该性状不可遗传。

将来时:
Future studies will explore the heritable aspects of this condition. = 未来的研究将探讨这种疾病的可遗传方面。
We will determine if this characteristic is heritable. = 我们将确定这个特性是否可遗传。

虚拟语气:
If this trait were heritable, we would see it in future generations. = 如果这个性状是可遗传的，我们会在后代中看到它。
I wish I knew if this disease was heritable. = 我希望我知道这种疾病是否可遗传。

被动语态:
Many diseases are known to be heritable. = 许多疾病已知是可遗传的。
The heritability of this gene is being studied. = 这个基因的可遗传性正在被研究。

疑问句:
Is this characteristic heritable? = 这个特性是可遗传的吗？
Are these traits heritable in humans? = 这些性状在人类中是可遗传的吗？

否定句:
This specific trait is not heritable. = 这个特定的性状不可遗传。
Acquired characteristics are not heritable. = 后天获得的特性不可遗传。


PHRASE->
highly heritable: 高度遗传的
partially heritable: 部分遗传的
heritability of a trait:  性状的可遗传性
heritable variation:  遗传变异
low heritability: 低遗传力


词源:
From heritable (16th century), from inherit + -able.  The word "inherit" has roots in Old French *inheriter, ultimately from Latin *haereditare, from haeres ("heir").  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or suitability.
