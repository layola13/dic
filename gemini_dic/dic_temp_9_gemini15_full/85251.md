harmine:/ˈhɑːrmiːn/| n.|哈尔明|A β-carboline alkaloid found in various plants, notably Peganum harmala, with psychoactive and potentially medicinal properties.  It's known for its MAO-inhibiting effects.|Harmine is a potent MAO inhibitor and has been studied for its potential use in treating depression and other neurological conditions.  However, it's also a powerful psychedelic and should only be used under strict medical supervision. |近义词：tetrahydroharmine, harmaline|反义词：无
