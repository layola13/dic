harum-scarum:/ˈhɛərəmˈskærəm/| adj.|慌慌张张的；鲁莽的；轻率的|  A harum-scarum person or activity is characterized by a lack of order, control, or caution; wild and reckless. It suggests a chaotic and uncontrolled manner.|例句：He was a harum-scarum young man, always getting into trouble. 他是个鲁莽的年轻人，总是惹麻烦。

例句：The harum-scarum dog ran into the street without looking.  那只鲁莽的狗没看就跑到了街上。


近义词：reckless, impetuous, headlong, wild, haphazard|反义词：cautious, careful, deliberate, methodical, planned
