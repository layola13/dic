preindulgent: /ˌpriːɪnˈdʌldʒənt/ | adj. | 过度放纵的，骄纵的 | Excessively indulgent; pampering.  Describes someone who is excessively permissive or lenient, often to a fault.  It suggests a prior history of indulgence that has led to a current state of excessive leniency. |  例句：His preindulgent parenting style resulted in a spoiled child.  他的过度溺爱养育方式导致孩子被宠坏了。  The preindulgent teacher allowed the students to get away with anything.  这位过度放纵的老师允许学生们为所欲为。| 近义词：spoiling, permissive, lenient, overindulgent | 反义词：strict, austere, demanding, disciplined |  词性:形容词
