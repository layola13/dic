pidginization:/ˌpɪdʒɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |皮钦语化；简化语言化 |The process by which a simplified language, a pidgin, develops from the contact of two or more languages.  This often involves reduction of grammatical complexity, vocabulary simplification, and the borrowing of elements from the contributing languages.  It's a dynamic process influenced by the social and linguistic contexts of its users. |例句：The pidginization of English in coastal areas led to the development of numerous creoles.  沿海地区的英语简化导致了众多克里奥尔语的发展。|近义词：simplification, creolization |反义词：standardization, formalization
