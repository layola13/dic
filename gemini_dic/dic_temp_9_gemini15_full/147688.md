overpractice:/ˈoʊvərˈpræktɪs/ | n. |过度练习|The act of practicing something excessively, often to the point of diminishing returns or negative effects.  Overpractice can lead to fatigue, injury, and decreased performance. |例句：His overpractice on the piano led to tendinitis in his wrist.  他的钢琴过度练习导致腕部肌腱炎。

例句：The team's overpractice before the game resulted in several players being too tired to perform well. 比赛前的过度训练导致几名球员过于疲惫，无法良好发挥。


近义词：overtraining, excessive practice, overexertion |反义词：underpractice, insufficient practice


词性：名词
