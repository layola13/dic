heroism:/ˈhɪərəʊɪzəm/| n. |英雄主义；英勇行为 |The act of being heroic; courageous action.  It often involves facing danger or adversity for a noble cause or the sake of others. |例句：Her heroism in saving the child from the burning building was widely praised.  她的英勇行为从燃烧的建筑物中救出了孩子，受到了广泛赞扬。

例句：The firefighters displayed incredible heroism during the wildfire. 消防员在野火中表现出令人难以置信的英勇行为。


近义词：bravery, courage, gallantry, valor, daring

反义词：cowardice, fearfulness
