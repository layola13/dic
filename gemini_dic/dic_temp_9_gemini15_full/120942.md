miscellany: /mɪˈsɛləni/ | n. | 杂项；混杂；各种各样的事物 | A collection of various things or items.  | The book is a miscellany of essays, poems, and short stories. 这本书是散文、诗歌和短篇故事的合集。 | anthology, assortment, collection | homogeneity, uniformity | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This box contains a miscellany of items. = 这个盒子包含各种各样的物品。
Her desk is a miscellany of papers and pens. = 她的书桌上摆满了各种各样的文件和笔。


一般过去时:
The museum displayed a miscellany of artifacts from different cultures. = 博物馆展出了来自不同文化的各种各样的文物。
He gave a miscellany of reasons for his lateness. = 他为他的迟到给出了各种各样的理由。

虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:

词源:
Middle English: from Old French *mescelanie, from Late Latin *miscellanea, plural of *miscellaneus ‘mixed’, from Latin miscere ‘to mix’.
