insurmountable: /ˌɪnsərˈmaʊntəbl/ | adj. | 不可逾越的，无法克服的 | incapable of being overcome; insuperable.  The task ahead seemed insurmountable.  | 近义词：insuperable, unconquerable, insuperable, invincible | 反义词：surmountable, conquerable


例句：

The challenges they faced were insurmountable. 他们面临的挑战是无法克服的。

The mountain was deemed insurmountable by the climbers. 登山者认为这座山是不可逾越的。


词源：

* **in-** :  prefix meaning "not" or "without"
* **sur-** :  prefix meaning "over" or "above"  (from Latin *super*)
* **mount** : verb meaning "to climb" or "to ascend" (from Old French *monter*, from Latin *montare*)
* **-able** : suffix meaning "capable of being"


用法说明：


insurmountable 主要用作形容词，修饰名词，通常用来形容困难、挑战、障碍等。它强调的是一种无法克服的程度，暗示了巨大的困难和绝望。


PHRASE->

an insurmountable obstacle  一个不可逾越的障碍
an insurmountable problem 一个无法解决的问题
an insurmountable difficulty  一个无法克服的困难
an insurmountable challenge 一个无法克服的挑战
an insurmountable gap 一个无法弥合的差距
