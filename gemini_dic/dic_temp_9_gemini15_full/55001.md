disput:/ˈdɪsˌpjuːt/| n. v.|争论，争议；争辩|A dispute is a disagreement or argument between people or groups.  It can also be the act of arguing or disagreeing.|例句：The dispute over the land ownership lasted for years. 这场关于土地所有权的争端持续了很多年。
The two companies are in dispute over a contract. 这两家公司就合同问题发生了争议。|近义词：argument, controversy, quarrel, contention|反义词：agreement, harmony, accord|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They dispute the findings of the report. = 他们对报告的调查结果提出异议。
He disputes his guilt. = 他否认自己的罪行。


一般过去时:
She disputed the claim vigorously. = 她强烈地反驳了这一说法。
They disputed the terms of the contract. = 他们对合同条款提出了异议。


现在完成时:
They have disputed the decision for months. = 他们已经就这个决定争论了几个月。
Scientists have disputed the theory for decades. = 科学家们几十年来一直在争论这个理论。


过去完成时:
He had disputed the will before his death. = 他在死前曾对遗嘱提出异议。
The neighbours had disputed the boundary line for years. = 邻居们多年来一直就边界线发生争执。


将来时:
They will dispute the election results. = 他们将对选举结果提出异议。
We will dispute the charges in court. = 我们将在法庭上对指控提出异议。


虚拟语气:
If they disputed the facts, we would have to provide further evidence. = 如果他们质疑事实，我们就必须提供进一步的证据。
I wish they hadn't disputed my authority in front of the clients. = 我真希望他们不要在客户面前质疑我的权威。


被动语态:
The findings were disputed by several experts. = 这一发现受到几位专家的质疑。
The contract is being disputed by both parties. = 合同正遭到双方争议。


疑问句:
Do you dispute the accuracy of this information? = 你是否质疑此信息的准确性？
Are they still disputing the ownership of the property? = 他们还在争夺该房产的所有权吗？


否定句:
He did not dispute the facts presented. = 他没有反驳所陈述的事实。
We do not dispute the need for reform. = 我们并不否认改革的必要性。


PHRASE->
in dispute: 有争议的
dispute over: 关于…的争论
settle a dispute: 解决争端
subject to dispute: 有待商榷的
beyond dispute: 无可争议的
