dervish:/ˈdɜːrvɪʃ/| n.|苦行僧，托钵僧|A member of a Muslim religious order, especially one that is characterized by ecstatic rituals.  The term often refers to whirling dervishes.|例句：The whirling dervishes spun gracefully, their long robes swirling around them.  旋转的苦行僧优雅地旋转，他们的长袍在他们周围旋转。|近义词：mendicant, ascetic, mystic|反义词：materialist, hedonist
