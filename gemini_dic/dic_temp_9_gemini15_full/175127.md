/ræft/| n. |木筏；救生筏；大量；群 |A raft is a flat structure, usually made of logs or planks, fastened together and used as a floating platform.  It can also refer to a large number or quantity of something. |例句：They built a raft to cross the river. = 他们造了一只木筏过河。

They floated down the river on a makeshift raft. = 他们在简易木筏上顺流而下。


近义词：float,  ferry(in some contexts) |反义词：N/A |名词
