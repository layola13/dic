credulity:/krɛˈdʒuːləti/ | n. |轻信；易信；愚昧 | The quality or state of being credulous; readiness or willingness to believe.  It suggests a lack of critical thinking and a tendency to accept things at face value, often without sufficient evidence. |例句：His credulity made him an easy target for con artists.  他的轻信使他很容易成为骗子的目标。

His credulity was exploited by the clever salesman.  他那轻信的性格被狡猾的推销员利用了。


近义词：gullibility, naivete, simplicity | 反义词：skepticism, cynicism, doubt
