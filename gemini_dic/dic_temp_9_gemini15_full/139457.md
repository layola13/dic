obstacle-races: /ˈɑːbstəklˌreɪsɪz/ | n. |障碍赛跑|A competition in which participants race through a course that includes various obstacles they must overcome.  Obstacle races often involve running, climbing, crawling, and other physical challenges.|例句：He signed up for an obstacle race to test his physical limits. 他报名参加障碍赛来测试他的体能极限。

例句：Many people participate in obstacle races for fun and fitness. 很多人参加障碍赛是为了娱乐和健身。


近义词：obstacle course, challenge race, adventure race|反义词：None|词性：n.
