serpentining: /ˈsɜːrpənˌtaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. |蜿蜒的，蛇形的 | Winding or curving like a serpent.  The word describes something that twists and turns in a sinuous manner, resembling the movement of a snake. |例句：The road serpentined through the mountains. 这条路蜿蜒穿过群山。

The river serpentined lazily through the valley.  这条河蜿蜒地流过山谷。|近义词：winding, meandering, sinuous, twisting |反义词：straight, direct, linear |

词源:
From the noun "serpent" (蛇), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *serp-. The "-ine" suffix creates an adjectival form.  The word thus literally means "like a serpent" or "having the characteristics of a serpent."
