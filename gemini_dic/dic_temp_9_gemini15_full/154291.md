peristome:/ˈpɛrɪstoʊm/ | n. | 周圍; (植物)蒴口; (軟體動物)口周圍 | The peristome refers to the structure surrounding an opening, particularly in botany and zoology. In botany, it typically describes the teeth or appendages surrounding the mouth of a moss capsule. In zoology, it often refers to the area around the mouth of certain invertebrates. |例句：The moss capsule's peristome was beautifully intricate.  苔藓蒴果的蒴口非常精巧。

The snail's peristome was stained with algae.  蜗牛的口周染上了藻类。| 近义词：rim, border, margin, mouth | 反义词：center, interior
