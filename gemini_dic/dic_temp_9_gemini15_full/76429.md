gaily:/ˈɡeɪli/| adj.|快乐地；兴高采烈地；鲜艳地|Cheerfully and brightly; in a lively and festive manner.  The word often describes actions, appearances, or decorations.|例句：The children played gaily in the park.孩子们在公园里快乐地玩耍。

The birds sang gaily in the morning sun.鸟儿在清晨的阳光下快乐地歌唱。|近义词： merrily, cheerfully, brightly, festively|反义词：sadly, gloomily, somberly
