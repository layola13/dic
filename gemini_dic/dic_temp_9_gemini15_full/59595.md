例句：The ebullition of the crowd after the winning goal was incredible.  球队进球后人群的沸腾令人难以置信。


近义词：outburst, eruption, effervescence, effervescency, boiling, bubbling, surging


反义词：calm, tranquility, stillness, quiescence
