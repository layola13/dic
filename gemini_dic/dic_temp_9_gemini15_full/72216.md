florentines:/ˌflɒrənˈtiːnz/| n. |佛罗伦萨饼干；佛罗伦萨人|A type of almond biscuit, typically crisp and buttery, often with chocolate.  They are named after Florence, Italy.|例句：These florentines are delicious; they're perfectly crisp and melt in your mouth. 这些佛罗伦萨饼干真好吃；它们又脆又入口即化。|例句：Many tourists buy florentines as souvenirs from Florence. 许多游客从佛罗伦萨购买佛罗伦萨饼干作为纪念品。|近义词：biscotti, cookies, almond biscuits|反义词：无
