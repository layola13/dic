lay an egg:/leɪ ən ɛɡ/| v. |下蛋；失败；产卵 |To produce an egg, as a bird or reptile.  Figuratively, to fail completely. |例句：The hen laid an egg this morning.  母鸡今天早上下了一个蛋。
The comedian completely laid an egg with his new material.  喜剧演员的新段子彻底失败了。|近义词：oviposit, produce (an egg), flop, bomb |反义词：succeed, triumph


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hen lays eggs every day. = 母鸡每天都下蛋。
She lays her eggs in a nest. = 她把蛋下在巢里。


一般过去时:
The bird laid a beautiful blue egg. = 那只鸟下了一个美丽的蓝色蛋。
He laid an egg in his presentation. = 他的演示彻底失败了。


现在完成时:
The hen has laid twenty eggs this week. = 这周母鸡已经下了二十个蛋。
The company has laid several eggs in the market recently. = 公司最近在市场上接连遭遇失败。


过去完成时:

Before the launch, they had already laid several metaphorical eggs. = 在发布之前，他们已经遭遇了几次象征性的失败。
