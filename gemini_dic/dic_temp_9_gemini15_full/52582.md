dig:/dɪɡ/| v.|挖掘；深入研究；理解|To move earth or similar material with a tool or the hands; to thoroughly investigate or explore something; to understand something deeply.|例句：The dog is digging a hole in the garden.  狗正在花园里挖洞。|例句：The police are digging into the case. 警方正在深入调查此案。|近义词：excavate, delve, explore, understand|反义词：bury, fill, conceal

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He digs a hole every day. = 他每天挖一个坑。
She digs for information about the company. = 她正在搜集关于那家公司的资料。

一般过去时:
They dug a well last week. = 他们上周挖了一口井。
He dug deep into the archives. = 他深入研究了档案。

现在完成时:
We have dug up all the potatoes. = 我们已经挖出了所有的土豆。
She has dug into the history of the family. = 她已经深入研究了家族历史。

过去完成时:
They had dug the foundation before the rain started. = 雨开始下之前，他们已经挖好了地基。
He had dug through several boxes before he found it. = 他翻了好几个箱子才找到它。

将来时:
We will dig a new pond next spring. = 我们明年春天会挖一个新的池塘。
They will dig deeper into this issue. = 他们会更深入地研究这个问题。

虚拟语气:
If I had a shovel, I would dig a garden. = 如果我有一把铲子，我会挖个花园。
I wish I could dig into the mystery. = 我真希望我能深入研究这个谜团。

被动语态:
The hole was dug by the workers. = 这个洞是由工人们挖的。
The truth will be dug up eventually. = 真相最终会被挖出来。

疑问句:
Are you digging in the wrong place? = 你是不是挖错地方了？
What are they digging for? = 他们在挖什么？

否定句:
I didn't dig the hole. = 我没有挖那个洞。
They didn't dig deep enough. = 他们挖得不够深。


PHRASE->
dig in: 开始吃；努力工作
dig up: 挖掘；找出
dig into: 深入研究；开始认真做某事
dig one's heels in: 坚持己见；固执己见
dig at: 挖苦；讽刺
