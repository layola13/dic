operative:/ˈɒpərətɪv/ /ˈɑːpərətɪv/| adj., n.| 操作的；有效的；特工；手术的 |  Working or functioning effectively; relating to or used in an operation; a person who is employed to carry out secret missions, especially one working for a government intelligence agency. |

例句：

The new system is fully operative. = 新系统完全可以使用。

The operative word in the sentence is "immediately". = 句中关键词是“立即”。


The police operative infiltrated the criminal gang. = 警方的特工渗透进了犯罪团伙。

He is a highly skilled operative in the field. = 他是该领域技术高超的特工。


近义词：effective, efficient, agent, spy

反义词：ineffective, inefficient, dormant


词性：形容词，名词
