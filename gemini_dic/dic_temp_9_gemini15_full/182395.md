renege:/rɪˈniːɡ/| v.|食言；违约；放弃 | To go back on a promise, undertaking, or agreement.  |例句：He reneged on his promise to help us. 他违背了他帮助我们的承诺。|例句：The company reneged on its contract.  公司违反了合同。|近义词：break one's word, go back on, welsh|反义词：keep one's word, honor, uphold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reneges on his promises frequently. = 他经常食言。
They renege on deals too often. = 他们太经常违反协议了。


一般过去时:
She reneged on the deal at the last minute. = 她在最后一刻违反了协议。
He reneged on his commitment to donate. = 他违背了他捐款的承诺。


现在完成时:
They have reneged on their agreement to pay. = 他们违背了支付的协议。
The government has reneged on its promise to reform. = 政府违背了改革的承诺。


过去完成时:
He had reneged on his agreement before I could intervene. = 在我能够干预之前，他已经违背了他的协议。
They had reneged on their support before the project was completed. = 在项目完成之前，他们就已经撤回了支持。


将来时:
