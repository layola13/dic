fluctuated:/ˈflʌktʃueɪtɪd/| v.|波动，起伏|To rise and fall irregularly in number, amount, or intensity.|The stock market fluctuated wildly throughout the day.  股市全天剧烈波动。|The temperature fluctuated between 20 and 30 degrees. 温度在20到30度之间波动。|rose, fell, varied, changed|stabilized, remained constant|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price fluctuates constantly. = 价格不断波动。
The temperature fluctuates throughout the day. = 温度全天都在波动。

一般过去时:
The stock price fluctuated dramatically yesterday. = 昨天的股价剧烈波动。
The river's level fluctuated after the heavy rain. = 大雨过后，河水水位波动。

现在完成时:
The value of the currency has fluctuated significantly this year. = 今年货币价值波动很大。
The patient's blood pressure has fluctuated since the surgery. = 手术后病人的血压一直波动。

过去完成时:
The market had fluctuated wildly before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，市场已经剧烈波动。
The company's profits had fluctuated for several years before the turnaround. = 在扭亏为盈之前，该公司利润已经波动了几年。

将来时:
The weather is forecast to fluctuate wildly next week. = 预计下周天气将剧烈波动。
The exchange rate is expected to fluctuate in the coming months. = 预计汇率将在未来几个月内波动。

虚拟语气:
If the price had not fluctuated so much, we would have made a profit. = 如果价格没有波动这么大，我们就能盈利了。
I wish the economy wouldn't fluctuate so unpredictably. = 我希望经济不要波动得如此不可预测。

被动语态:
The data was found to have fluctuated unexpectedly. = 发现数据意外地发生了波动。
The results were significantly fluctuated by external factors. = 结果受到外部因素的显著影响而波动。


疑问句:
Has the water level fluctuated recently? = 最近水位波动了吗？
Did the temperature fluctuate much yesterday? = 昨天的温度波动大吗？


否定句:
The stock price did not fluctuate significantly today. = 今天的股价没有大幅波动。
The readings did not fluctuate during the experiment. = 在实验过程中读数没有波动。


PHRASE->
fluctuate wildly = 剧烈波动
fluctuate between...and...= 在...和...之间波动
fluctuate slightly = 轻微波动
fluctuate dramatically = 急剧波动
fluctuate considerably = 相当波动


词源:
From Latin fluctuāre "to wave, surge," from fluctus "wave."  源自拉丁语 fluctuāre，意为“波动，汹涌”，来自 fluctus，“波浪”。
