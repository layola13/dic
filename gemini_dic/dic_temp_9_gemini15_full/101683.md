irremediable: /ˌɪrɪˈmiːdjəbl/ | adj. | 不可弥补的；无法补救的 | incapable of being remedied or rectified; incurable.  | The damage was irremediable.  损坏是不可弥补的。|近义词：incurable, irreparable, irreversible | 反义词：remediable, curable


词源:

* **ir-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "without".
* **remediable**:  from Latin *remediābilis*, from *remedium* ("remedy").

用法说明:

irremediable 主要用作形容词，修饰名词，表示某种情况或损害无法修复或补救。


例句:

* The damage caused by the fire was irremediable. 火灾造成的损害是不可弥补的。
* Her illness proved to be irremediable.  她的病证明是无法治愈的。
*  He felt an irremediable sense of loss after the death of his wife.  妻子去世后，他感到一种无法弥补的失落感。
* The mistake was irremediable; there was nothing we could do to fix it.  这个错误是不可弥补的；我们什么也做不了来纠正它。
* The situation seemed irremediable, but we were determined to try and improve things.  情况似乎不可挽回，但我们决心尝试改善。


近义词:

incurable, irreparable, irreversible, incurable, hopeless

反义词:

remediable, curable, reparable, reversible


PHRASE->

无常用的词组搭配，该词主要独立使用作形容词。
