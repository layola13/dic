inspiration: /ˌɪnspəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 灵感，启发 | The process of being mentally stimulated to do or feel something, especially to do something creative.  It can refer to the person or thing that stimulates this process. | 例句：The beautiful scenery provided the artist with inspiration for his painting.  美丽的风景为艺术家提供了绘画灵感。

例句：Her speech was a source of inspiration to us all. 她的演讲给了我们所有人灵感。


近义词：motivation, stimulus, impetus, influence | 反义词：uninspiration, discouragement


词源:  From Middle French *inspiration, from Latin inspiratio, from inspirare "to breathe in," from in- "in" + spirare "to breathe."  The original sense referred to the act of breathing in, which later extended to the metaphorical sense of "breathing in" ideas or feelings.
