The word "prerespectability" is a relatively modern coinage.  It's formed by combining the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before" or "in advance of") with the noun "respectability" (meaning the state or quality of being respectable).  The term highlights the act of achieving a semblance of respectability *before* genuinely earning it.
