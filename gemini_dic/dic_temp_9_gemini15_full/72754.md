fogger:/ˈfɒɡər/ /ˈfɑːɡər/| n. |喷雾器，造雾机|A fogger is a device that produces a fog or mist, often used for pest control, humidification, or special effects.|例句：The farmer used a fogger to apply pesticide to his crops.  农民用喷雾器给庄稼喷洒农药。|例句：The stage crew used a fogger to create a spooky atmosphere. 舞台工作人员用造雾机营造出一种恐怖的气氛。|近义词：mister, sprayer, nebulizer|反义词：None specifically, but consider devices that eliminate mist or fog.|名词
