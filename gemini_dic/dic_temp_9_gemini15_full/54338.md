disinheritance:/dɪsɪnˈhɛrɪtəns/ | n. | 剥夺继承权；被剥夺继承权 | The act of depriving someone of their inheritance.  It involves legally preventing someone from inheriting property or money that would otherwise be theirs by right.  | Example:  The disinheritance of his eldest son caused a major family rift.  = 他长子的被剥夺继承权导致了严重的家庭裂痕。The will detailed the disinheritance of his black sheep son. = 遗嘱详细说明了他不肖儿子的被剥夺继承权。| 近义词：disenfranchisement, deprivation | 反义词：inheritance, bequest | 名词
