lopeman:/ˈloʊpmæn/ | n. |牧羊人，赶羊人 |A lopeman is a person who herds sheep, especially on horseback.  The term is primarily used in historical or literary contexts, particularly associated with the American West. |Example: The lone lopeman guided his flock across the vast plains.  那位孤独的牧羊人引导他的羊群穿过广阔的平原。|近义词：shepherd, herder, drover |反义词：N/A
