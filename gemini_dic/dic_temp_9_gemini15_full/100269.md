interscience:/ˌɪntərˈsaɪəns/| n. |交叉学科；跨学科；国际科学|The study of the relationships between different scientific disciplines.  It often involves the integration of knowledge and methods from multiple fields to address complex problems. |例句：The research project is an example of interscience, combining elements of biology, chemistry, and engineering.  这个研究项目是交叉学科的例子，结合了生物学、化学和工程学的元素。|例句：The conference on interscience fostered collaboration between researchers from various fields. 这场交叉学科会议促进了来自各个领域的研究人员之间的合作。|近义词：multidisciplinarity, interdisciplinarity |反义词：specialization, single discipline
