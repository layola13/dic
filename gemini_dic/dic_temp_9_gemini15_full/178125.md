recognised:/ˈrekəɡnaɪzd/ | v. | 认出，承认，认可 | To identify someone or something or accept something as true or valid.  |例句：I recognised him from a photograph. 我从照片上认出了他。|例句：His achievements were finally recognised. 他的成就最终得到了认可。|近义词：identified, acknowledged, accepted |反义词：ignored, overlooked, denied |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recognises his mistakes. 他认识到自己的错误。
They recognise the importance of education. 他们认识到教育的重要性。

一般过去时:
She recognised her friend in the crowd.她在人群中认出了她的朋友。

现在完成时:
I have recognised the pattern. 我已经认出了这个模式。
We have recognised the need for change. 我们已经认识到改变的必要性。

过去完成时:
They had recognised the danger before it was too late. 他们在为时已晚之前就意识到了危险。
