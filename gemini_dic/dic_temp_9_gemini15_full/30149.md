celandine:/ˈsɛləndin/| n.|白屈菜|A small flowering plant with bright yellow flowers, having a long history of use in folk medicine.  It is known for its acrid, orange-yellow sap.|例句：The celandine's bright yellow flowers brightened the meadow. 白屈菜明亮的黄色花朵照亮了草地。

The celandine sap was traditionally used to treat warts.  传统上，白屈菜的汁液被用来治疗疣。|近义词：greater celandine, lesser celandine |反义词:  None specifically, but plants with contrasting properties could be considered opposite.
