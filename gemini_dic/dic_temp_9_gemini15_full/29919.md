cavalero:/ˌkævəˈlɛroʊ/| n. |骑士；男士；（尤指意大利语）先生|A cavalier is a gentleman, especially one of noble rank, who is skilled in horsemanship and the arts of war; in Italian, it's a polite form of address for "Mr." or "Sir".|例句：He was a brave cavalier, always ready for a fight. 他是一位勇敢的骑士，总是随时准备战斗。  He addressed the man as "Signor Cavalero". 他称呼那位男士为“卡瓦列罗先生”。|近义词：gentleman, knight, sir|反义词：villain, knave
