immute:/ɪˈmjuːt/| adj.| 免疫的；不受影响的 |  Not affected or influenced by something.  The word is primarily used in a specialized scientific or medical context, referring to resistance to a particular disease or substance. |Example:  He is immue to this virus. 他对这种病毒免疫。 |近义词：resistant, immune, unaffected |反义词：susceptible, vulnerable

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is immune to the disease. = 他对这种疾病免疫。
The plant is immune to pests. = 这种植物对害虫有免疫力。

一般过去时:
She was immune to the effects of the poison. = 她对毒药的影响免疫。
The city was largely immune from the storm's damage. = 这个城市在很大程度上免受风暴的破坏。

现在完成时:
They have become immune to the medication. = 他们对这种药物产生了免疫力。
The population has remained largely immune to the disease. = 人口在很大程度上对这种疾病免疫。

过去完成时:
He had been immune to the virus for many years. = 他多年来一直对这种病毒免疫。
The area had remained immune from development until recently. = 该地区直到最近才免受开发。

将来时:
She will likely remain immune to the disease. = 她很可能仍然对这种疾病免疫。
The new material will be immune to corrosion. = 新材料将具有耐腐蚀性。

虚拟语气:
If I were immune to criticism, I'd be a better leader. = 如果我对批评免疫，我会成为更好的领导者。
I wish I were immune to the effects of stress. = 我希望我对压力的影响免疫。

被动语态:
The patient was declared immune after treatment. = 治疗后，病人被宣布免疫。
The system is designed to be immune to hacking attempts. = 该系统旨在防止黑客攻击。

疑问句:
Is he immune to the effects of the drug? = 他对药物的影响免疫吗？
Are these plants immune to disease? = 这些植物对疾病免疫吗？

否定句:
He is not immune to the effects of stress. = 他对压力的影响并不免疫。
The software is not immune to viruses. = 该软件无法抵御病毒。
