hear:/hɪər/| /hɪr/| vt.|听见，听说；听取；审理|to perceive with the ear; to receive or learn of by report; to listen to and judge|例句：I hear a bird singing. 我听见一只鸟在唱歌。
I heard that he was ill. 我听说他病了。|近义词：listen to, overhear, perceive|反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I hear you clearly. = 我听得清清楚楚。
They hear the music from afar. = 他们从远处听到音乐。


一般过去时:
I heard a strange noise last night. = 我昨晚听到一个奇怪的声音。
She heard the news from her friend. = 她从朋友那里听到了这个消息。


现在完成时:
I have heard that song many times. = 我听过那首歌很多次了。
Have you heard from your family lately? = 你最近收到家人的来信了吗？


过去完成时:
I had heard the story before he told me. = 在他告诉我之前我已经听过这个故事了。
They had heard rumors about the company's financial problems. = 他们已经听说过关于公司财务问题的传闻。


将来时:
I will hear from you soon, I hope. = 我希望很快收到你的来信。
We will hear the results of the test tomorrow. = 我们明天将听到测试的结果。


虚拟语气:
I wish I had heard the lecture yesterday. = 我真希望昨天听了那场讲座。
If I had heard the warning, I would have left earlier. = 如果我听到警告的话，我会早点离开。


被动语态:
The judge heard the case. = 法官审理了此案。(主动语态)
The case was heard by the judge. = 此案由法官审理。(被动语态)
His concerns were heard by the committee. = 委员会听取了他的意见。(被动语态)


疑问句:
Do you hear me? = 你听到我说话了吗？
Can you hear the birds singing? = 你能听到鸟儿唱歌吗？


否定句:
I didn't hear what you said. = 我没听到你说什么。
They couldn't hear the speaker over the noise. = 他们听不清演讲者的声音，因为太吵了。


PHRASE->
hear from: 收到…的来信
hear of: 听说
hear out: 听完…的话
give ear to: 仔细听
within earshot: 在听力范围内
