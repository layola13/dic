inappropriate:/ˌɪnəˈproʊpriət/|/ˌɪnəˈprəʊpriət/| adj. | 不合适的；不相称的；不恰当的 | Not suitable or proper in the circumstances.  It suggests something is not fitting, right, or acceptable given the context. |例句：His joke was inappropriate for a funeral. 他的笑话在葬礼上很不合适。|例句：Wearing shorts to a formal dinner is inappropriate. 穿着短裤去正式晚宴是不合适的。|近义词：unsuitable, improper, unfit, unbecoming, indecorous |反义词：appropriate, suitable, proper, fitting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The comment is inappropriate. = 这条评论不合适。
That outfit is inappropriate for the occasion. = 那套衣服不适合这个场合。

一般过去时:
Her behavior was inappropriate at the meeting. = 她在会议上的行为不恰当。
His response was inappropriate given the situation. = 考虑到当时的形势，他的回应是不合适的。

现在完成时:
His actions have been inappropriate throughout the entire process. = 他在整个过程中行为一直不当。
We have found several inappropriate images on the website. = 我们在网站上发现了几张不合适的图片。

过去完成时:
I realized that my joke had been inappropriate after I told it. = 我讲完笑话后才意识到它很不合适。
They discovered that their earlier decision had been inappropriate. = 他们发现他们之前的决定是不合适的。

将来时:
His future actions will likely be inappropriate if he doesn't change his behavior. = 如果他不改变行为，他未来的行为很可能是不合适的。
The consequences will be inappropriate if we do not take action now. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，后果将不堪设想。(这里用inappropriate表达后果的严重性)

虚拟语气:
If he had chosen his words more carefully, his comment would not have been inappropriate. = 如果他更仔细地选择措辞，他的评论就不会不合适了。
I wish I hadn't made that inappropriate remark. = 我真希望我没有说那句不合适的话。

被动语态:
His suggestion was deemed inappropriate by the committee. = 他的建议被委员会认为是不合适的。
The movie was criticized for containing inappropriate content. = 这部电影因包含不当内容而受到批评。

疑问句:
Is this outfit inappropriate for the job interview? = 这套衣服不适合面试吗？
Was my joke inappropriate? = 我的笑话不合适吗？

否定句:
The speaker's tone was not inappropriate. = 演讲者的语气并不唐突。
Her choice of words was not inappropriate in that context. = 在那种情况下，她的措辞并不唐突。
