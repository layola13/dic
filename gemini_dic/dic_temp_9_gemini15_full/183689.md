reroofs:/ˌriːˈruːfs/| v.|重新盖屋顶|To put a new roof on (a building) again.  This implies a previous roofing process.|The old Victorian house needed reroofing after the storm damaged it.  暴风雨过后，那座古老的维多利亚式房屋需要重新盖屋顶。

The entire factory complex requires reroofing due to significant wear and tear. 由于严重的磨损，整个工厂建筑群都需要重新盖屋顶。|re-roof, re-cover|demolish, tear down.
