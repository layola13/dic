civics:/ˈsɪvɪks/| n.|公民学；公民教育|The study of the rights and duties of citizens.  It encompasses the structure and function of government, the rights and responsibilities of citizens, and the processes by which citizens participate in their government.|例句：My civics class taught me about the importance of voting. 我的公民课教我投票的重要性。


例句：We learned about the three branches of government in our civics lesson.  我们在公民课上学习了政府的三权分立。


近义词：political science, citizenship studies|反义词：N/A|词性：名词
