overappreciation:/ˌoʊvərəˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən/| n. |过高评价；过度欣赏|The overappreciation of the dollar made US exports less competitive. 美元升值过高使得美国出口产品竞争力下降。|近义词：overvaluation, excessive praise |反义词：underestimation, undervaluation


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The market shows an overappreciation of this stock. = 市场对这只股票估值过高。
He exhibits an overappreciation for his own talents. = 他对自己的才能评价过高。


一般过去时:
The overappreciation of the currency led to economic instability. = 货币升值过高导致经济不稳定。
The company's success was due to an overappreciation of its innovative products. = 公司的成功归因于对其创新产品的过度赞赏。


现在完成时:
The recent surge in housing prices shows an overappreciation of the market. = 最近房价飙升表明市场被高估了。
The critics' overappreciation of his work has led to unrealistic expectations. = 批评家对其作品的过度赞赏导致了不切实际的期望。


过去完成时:
The collapse of the dot-com bubble was partly due to the overappreciation of internet stocks. = 网络泡沫的破裂部分是由于互联网股票估值过高造成的。
Before the crisis, there had been an overappreciation of risk in the financial markets. = 在危机爆发之前，金融市场对风险有过高的估计。


将来时:
The continued overappreciation of the yen could hurt the Japanese economy. = 日元持续升值可能会损害日本经济。
Experts predict an overappreciation of art from this period in the coming years. = 专家预测未来几年这段时期艺术品的价值将被高估。


虚拟语气:
If there hadn't been such an overappreciation of the asset, the investment would not have failed. = 如果资产没有被高估到这种程度，投资就不会失败。
I wish there wasn't such an overappreciation for superficial achievements. = 我希望人们不要如此看重表面上的成就。


被动语态:
The company's stock was subject to an overappreciation in the early days. = 公司的股票在早期被高估了。
His talent has been subject to overappreciation by his peers. = 他的才华受到了同行的过度赞赏。


疑问句:
Is there an overappreciation of the risks involved in this project? = 这个项目中涉及的风险是否被高估了？
Does the current market reflect an overappreciation of this technology? = 当前市场是否反映了对这项技术的过高评价？


否定句:
There is no overappreciation of the challenges we face. = 我们面临的挑战并没有被高估。
His contributions were not subject to any overappreciation, rather a fair assessment. = 他的贡献并没有被过度赞扬，而是得到了公平的评价。
