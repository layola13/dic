saturate:/ˈsætʃəreɪt/ | vt. & adj. | 饱和；浸透；使充满 | To fill something completely so that no more can be added.  |例句：The sponge was saturated with water.  海绵吸饱了水。


例句：The market is saturated with similar products. 市场被类似的产品充斥着。


近义词：soak, impregnate, permeate | 反义词：dry, dehydrate


词性：动词和形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The soil is saturated with water after the heavy rain.  大雨过后土壤饱和了水分。
He saturates his work with passion. 他全身心地投入工作。


一般过去时:
They saturated the fabric with dye. 他们用染料将织物染透。
The heavy rain saturated the ground.  大雨使地面饱和。


现在完成时:
The market has been saturated by cheap imports. 市场已经被廉价进口商品充斥。
The company has saturated the advertising market with its new campaign.  该公司的新广告攻势已经充斥了广告市场。


过去完成时:
The ground had been saturated with water for days before the flood.  洪水来临前几天，地面就已被水浸透了。
The sponge had been saturated, so it couldn't absorb any more liquid. 海绵已经吸饱了，无法再吸收任何液体。


将来时:
The new product will saturate the market quickly. 新产品将会迅速占领市场。
We will saturate the area with posters. 我们将用海报把这个区域覆盖。


虚拟语气:
If we saturated the market with our product, we would have a bigger market share. 如果我们用我们的产品充斥市场，我们将会有更大的市场份额。
I wish we had saturated the market earlier. 我希望我们早点占领市场。


被动语态:
The fabric was saturated with dye. 织物被染料浸透了。
The market is being saturated with new products. 市场正被新产品充斥。


疑问句:
Is the market saturated? 市场饱和了吗？
Has the sponge been saturated? 海绵已经吸饱了吗?


否定句:
The soil is not yet saturated. 土壤还没有饱和。
The fabric wasn't completely saturated with the dye. 织物没有被染料完全浸透。
