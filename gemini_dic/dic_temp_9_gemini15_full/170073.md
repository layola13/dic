protestator: /ˈprəʊteˌsteɪtər/ | n. |抗议者，示威者 | A person who protests, especially in a public demonstration.  |例句：The protestator held a sign that read "End the War".  这个抗议者举着一个写着“结束战争”的标牌。

例句：Many protestors gathered outside the government building.  许多抗议者聚集在政府大楼外。


近义词：demonstrator, activist, marcher, rebel

反义词：supporter, conformist

词源:
The word "protestator" is derived from the verb "protest," which comes from the Latin word "protestari," meaning "to bear witness," "to declare publicly," or "to affirm solemnly."  The suffix "-ator" is a common agent noun suffix indicating the person who performs the action.
