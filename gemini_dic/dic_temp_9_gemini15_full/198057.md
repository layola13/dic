shoemaking:/ˈʃuːmeɪkɪŋ/| n.|制鞋业；制鞋术|The craft or business of making shoes.  It encompasses all aspects from design and pattern making to the selection of materials and the construction techniques used to create footwear.|例句：He learned shoemaking from his father and now runs a successful workshop. 他从他父亲那里学会了制鞋，现在经营着一个成功的作坊。|例句：The decline of the shoemaking industry in this town led to high unemployment.  这个城镇制鞋业的衰落导致了高失业率。|近义词：bootmaking, cobblery|反义词：无
