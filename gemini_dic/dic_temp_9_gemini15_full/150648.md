papoose:/pəˈpuːs/| n. |婴儿背带；婴儿|A papoose is a baby carrier, traditionally used by Native Americans, often made of cloth or leather, in which a baby is carried on the back or side of the parent.  |例句：The mother carried her papoose on her back while working in the fields.  母亲在田里干活时把婴儿背在背上。
例句：The museum displayed a variety of traditional papooses from different tribes. 博物馆展示了不同部落的各种传统婴儿背带。|近义词：baby carrier, sling|反义词：无|词性：名词
