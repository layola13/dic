coalesce: /koʊəˈles/ | v. | 结合；合并；融合 | To come together and form one mass or whole.  | The small streams coalesced to form a river. 小溪汇聚成一条河。 |  merge, combine, unite, fuse | separate, divide, disperse | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The droplets coalesce on the surface.  水滴在表面融合。
The different groups coalesce into a single unit.不同的群体合并成一个单一单位。


一般过去时:
The two companies coalesced last year. 两家公司去年合并了。
The different opinions coalesced into a consensus.不同的意见最终达成共识。


现在完成时:
The clouds have coalesced into a storm. 云已经汇集成暴风雨。
Their efforts have coalesced into a successful project.他们的努力已经融合成一个成功的项目。


过去完成时:
The ingredients had coalesced into a smooth paste. 配料已经融合成光滑的糊状物。
By the time the meeting ended, their ideas had coalesced into a plan. 等会议结束时，他们的想法已经融合成一个计划。


将来时:
The particles will coalesce under pressure. 颗粒会在压力下融合。
The two teams will coalesce to form a stronger force. 两支队伍将合并组成更强大的力量。


虚拟语气:
If the two groups coalesced, they would be much stronger. 如果这两个群体合并，他们将强大得多。
I wish the ideas would coalesce into a concrete plan. 我希望这些想法能融合成一个具体的计划。


被动语态:
The different elements were coalesced into a single compound.不同的元素被融合成单一化合物。
The opinions were coalesced to form a unified policy. 意见被整合以形成统一的政策。


疑问句:
Will the two factions ever coalesce? 这两个派别最终会合并吗？
Do you think these ideas can be coalesced into a single theory? 你认为这些想法可以融合成一个单一理论吗？


否定句:
The two groups did not coalesce. 这两个群体没有合并。
The different viewpoints did not coalesce into a unified perspective. 不同的观点并没有融合成一个统一的视角。



PHRASE->
coalesce into: 融合成
coalesce around:  围绕...聚集
coalesce with: 与...合并
coalesce from: 从...融合而来
coalesce to form: 合并形成
