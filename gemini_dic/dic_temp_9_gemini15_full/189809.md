salvationalism:/ˌsælveɪʃənəlɪzəm/| n. |救赎论；救世论|The theological doctrine emphasizing salvation, often through specific religious practices or beliefs.  It highlights the importance of personal salvation and often involves a strong emphasis on evangelism and missionary work. |例句：His sermons were steeped in salvationalism, focusing on the individual's journey to redemption.  他的布道浸透了救赎论，侧重于个人走向救赎的旅程。
|例句：The rise of salvationalism in the 19th century led to significant social reform movements. 19 世纪救赎论的兴起导致了重大的社会改革运动。|近义词：soteriology, redemptionism |反义词：damnationism, nihilism
