* **quasi-**:  From Latin "quasi," meaning "as if," "as it were," "almost," or "seemingly."  It indicates something that resembles or approximates but is not truly the thing described.

* **courteously**: From "courtesy," ultimately deriving from Old French "courtoisie" and Latin "curia" (court).  It signifies politeness and good manners.
