non-maternal: /ˌnɑːn məˈtɜːrnəl/ | adj. | 非母系的；非母性的 | Relating to or characteristic of someone other than a mother or relating to something not provided by a mother.  It often refers to care, relationships, or influences outside of the mother's role. |  例句：The study examined the effects of non-maternal care on child development. 这项研究考察了非母性照料对儿童发展的影响。

例句：The child received significant non-maternal support from her aunt.  这个孩子从她的阿姨那里得到了大量的非母系支持。


近义词：non-parental,  external (in the context of care or influence) | 反义词：maternal, parental
