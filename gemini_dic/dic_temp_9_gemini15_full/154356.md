perks:/pɜːrks/| n.|额外津贴，好处，特权|Advantages or benefits received in addition to one's salary.  These can include things like health insurance, paid time off, company car, etc. |Example:  The perks of this job include a company car and generous health insurance. 这份工作的额外津贴包括公司汽车和丰厚的健康保险。

The company offers excellent perks to its employees. 公司为其员工提供优厚的福利。|近义词：benefits, advantages, privileges|反义词：drawbacks, disadvantages, penalties
