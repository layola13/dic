dig-out: /dɪɡ aʊt/ | vt. | 挖出；找出；使摆脱困境 | to remove something from somewhere by digging, or to find something after searching for it.  It can also mean to rescue someone or something from a difficult situation. | 例句：The dog dug out a bone from the garden. = 狗从花园里挖出一根骨头。


They dug out the buried treasure. = 他们挖出了埋藏的宝藏。


近义词：excavate, unearth, discover, rescue | 反义词：bury, conceal, hide | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He digs out old photos every weekend. = 他每个周末都挖出旧照片。
We dig out information for our reports. = 我们为报告挖掘信息。

一般过去时:
She dug out her winter coat from the attic. = 她从阁楼里翻出了她的冬衣。
The firefighters dug out the survivors from the rubble. = 消防员从瓦砾中救出了幸存者。

现在完成时:
They have dug out all the weeds from the garden. = 他们已经把花园里的所有杂草都除掉了。
I have dug out some interesting facts for my presentation. = 我为我的演示文稿挖掘了一些有趣的事实。

过去完成时:
They had dug out a tunnel before the enemy arrived. = 敌人到达之前，他们已经挖好了一条隧道。
She had dug out all the relevant documents before the meeting. = 会议前，她已经找出了所有相关的文件。

将来时:
We will dig out the potatoes next week. = 我们下周将挖土豆。
They will dig out the truth behind this scandal. = 他们将挖掘出这起丑闻背后的真相。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would dig out more information. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会挖掘出更多信息。
I wish I could dig out the answer to this question. = 我希望我能找到这个问题的答案。

被动语态:
The fossils were dug out by archaeologists. = 这些化石是由考古学家挖出来的。
The truth will eventually be dug out. = 真相最终会被挖出来。

疑问句:
Have you dug out the old toys yet? = 你把旧玩具挖出来了吗？
Can they dig out the car from the snowdrift? = 他们能把车从雪堆里挖出来吗？

否定句:
They didn't dig out the truth. = 他们没有挖掘出真相。
I couldn't dig out the information I needed. = 我找不到我需要的信息。


PHRASE->
dig out information: 挖掘信息
dig oneself out: 自救；摆脱困境
dig out from under: 从...下挖出来
dig out of debt: 还清债务
dig out the truth: 找出真相
