outmeasuring: /ˌaʊtˈmɛʒərɪŋ/ | v. | 超过；胜过 | Exceeding in measurement; surpassing in size, extent, or amount.  | 例句：The new skyscraper outmeasures all the others in the city.  新的摩天大楼超过了城市里所有其他的建筑。

例句：Her achievements outmeasure those of her predecessors. 她的成就超过了她的前辈们。


近义词：surpassing, exceeding, outdoing, surpassing


反义词：undermeasuring, falling short of
