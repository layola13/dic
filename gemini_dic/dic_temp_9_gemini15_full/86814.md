hematitic: /ˌhɛməˈtɪtɪk/ | adj. | 赤铁矿的；含赤铁矿的 | Relating to or containing hematite (a mineral consisting of iron oxide).  Hematitic rocks are rich in iron and often have a reddish-brown color due to the presence of hematite. | The hematitic ore was mined for its high iron content.  这赤铁矿矿石因其高铁含量而被开采。 | ferruginous, iron-rich | non-hematitic |
