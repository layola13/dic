life-threatening: /ˈlaɪfˌθretnɪŋ/ | adj. |危及生命的；威胁生命的 |  Describes something that poses a serious risk of death.  |例句：The doctor said his condition was life-threatening.  医生说他的病情危及生命。

例句：The bleeding was life-threatening and required immediate medical attention. 出血危及生命，需要立即就医。


近义词：deadly, fatal, mortal, perilous


反义词：harmless, safe, innocuous


词性：形容词
