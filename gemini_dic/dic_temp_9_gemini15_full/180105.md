reflowered: /ˌriːˈflaʊərd/ | adj. | 再开花的；重新开花的 |  Having flowered again after a period of dormancy or having been cut back.  This word describes plants that have bloomed again. | 例句：The reflowered rose bushes were a stunning sight.  重新开花的玫瑰花丛是一道令人惊叹的景色。

The reflowered azaleas added vibrant color to the garden.  重新开花的杜鹃花为花园增添了鲜艳的色彩。

近义词：reblooming, blossoming again | 反义词：wilted, faded
