provide:/prəˈvaɪd/ | vt. | 提供，供应 | To make available for use; supply.  | The company provides excellent training for its employees.  这家公司为其员工提供优秀的培训。 | furnish, supply, offer | deny, withhold | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school provides books and materials for students. 学校为学生提供书籍和学习材料。
They provide excellent customer service. 他们提供优质的客户服务。


一般过去时:
She provided a delicious meal for her guests. 她为客人准备了一顿美味的饭菜。
The government provided aid to the victims of the flood. 政府向洪灾受害者提供了援助。


现在完成时:
They have provided all the necessary information. 他们已经提供了所有必要的信息。
We have provided support to the project since its inception. 自项目启动以来，我们一直在为其提供支持。


过去完成时:
He had provided everything needed for the party before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前，他已经准备好了派对所需的一切。
She had provided a detailed report before the meeting. 会议之前，她已经提供了一份详细的报告。


将来时:
The company will provide more jobs in the future. 公司将来会提供更多就业机会。
We will provide transportation to the airport. 我们将提供去机场的交通工具。


虚拟语气:
If only they had provided more information!  如果他们能提供更多信息就好了！
I wish they would provide better support. 我希望他们能提供更好的支持。


被动语态:
Food and water were provided to the refugees.难民们得到了食物和水的供应。
The necessary equipment will be provided by the company.  必要的设备将由公司提供。


疑问句:
Does the hotel provide breakfast? 这家酒店提供早餐吗？
What services does your company provide? 贵公司提供哪些服务？


否定句:
The company did not provide enough resources. 公司没有提供足够的资源。
They failed to provide the necessary documentation. 他们未能提供必要的文档。


PHRASE->
provide for:  养活；为…做准备
provide against: 预防；提防
provide with:  提供…给…
provided that:  如果；只要
make provision for: 为…做准备


词源:
From Old French *provéir, from Latin *prōvidēre "to foresee, provide," from prō- "before" + videre "to see."
