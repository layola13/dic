nodders:/ˈnɒdərz/| n.|点头的人，频频点头的人|People who frequently nod their heads, often implying agreement or understanding, sometimes in a way that suggests lack of critical thinking.|The meeting was filled with nodders, readily agreeing to everything the CEO proposed.  会议上坐满了点头称是的人，他们欣然同意首席执行官提出的所有建议。|The incessant nodders at the lecture were less than impressive.  讲座上那些不停点头的人并没有给人留下深刻的印象。|agreers, concurring individuals|objectors, dissenters


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The crowd nodders enthusiastically. = 人群热情地点头。
He nods his head in agreement. = 他点头表示同意。


一般过去时:
She nodded her head in understanding. = 她点头表示理解。
They nodded frequently throughout the presentation. = 他们在整个演示过程中频频点头。


现在完成时:
He has nodded off during the meeting. = 他在会议期间睡着了。(点头睡着)
They have nodded their agreement to the proposal. = 他们已经点头同意了该提案。


过去完成时:
She had nodded off before the speaker finished. = 演讲者结束之前她就睡着了。(点头睡着)
They had already nodded their heads before the question was fully asked. = 问题还没问完他们就已经点头了。


将来时:
He will nod his head in affirmation. = 他将点头表示肯定。
The audience will likely nod in agreement. = 观众很可能会点头表示同意。


虚拟语气:
If he understood, he would nod. = 如果他理解，他会点头。
I wish they would nod in agreement. = 我希望他们能点头表示同意。


被动语态:
Agreement was nodded into existence. = 达成协议。(点头表示同意而达成)
The proposal was nodded through without much discussion. = 该提案在没有太多讨论的情况下获得通过。(点头表示同意而通过)


疑问句:
Did he nod in response? = 他点头回应了吗？
Are they nodding their heads in agreement? = 他们点头表示同意吗？


否定句:
He didn't nod his head. = 他没有点头。
She didn't nod at all during the speech. = 她在演讲期间一次都没有点头。
