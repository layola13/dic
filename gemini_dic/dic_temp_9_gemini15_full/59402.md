earthbank:/ˈɜːrθbæŋk/|/ˈɝθbæŋk/| n. |河岸；堤岸；土堆|A bank or slope of earth, especially the bank of a river or stream.  It often refers to a naturally formed embankment.|例句：The children played on the earthbank by the river.孩子们在河边的土堆上玩耍。

例句：The farmer used the earthbank to protect his crops from flooding. 农民用土堤保护他的庄稼免受洪水侵袭。


近义词：riverbank, embankment, levee, bank


反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑与“riverbed”相对比


词性:名词
