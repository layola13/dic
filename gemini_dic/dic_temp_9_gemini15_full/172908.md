quaked:/kweɪkt/| v.|震动；颤抖；恐惧|To shake violently; to tremble, especially from fear or cold.  The earthquake quaked the ground violently. 地震剧烈地震动了地面。The child quaked with fear at the sight of the large dog.  看到大狗，孩子吓得发抖。|tremble, shake, shiver| remained still, calm |动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The ground quakes during an earthquake. = 地震时地面震动。
He quakes at the mere mention of spiders. = 他一提到蜘蛛就发抖。


一般过去时:
The building quaked during the storm. = 建筑物在暴风雨中摇晃。
My hands quaked as I held the fragile vase. = 我拿着易碎的花瓶时，双手颤抖。


现在完成时:
The earth has quaked several times this year. = 今年地球已经震动过几次了。
She has quaked at the thought of public speaking ever since childhood. = 从小她就害怕公开演讲。


过去完成时:
The house had quaked violently before collapsing. = 房子剧烈摇晃后才倒塌。
He had quaked with fear before he even entered the room. = 他甚至还没进房间就吓得发抖。


将来时:
The ground will quake if the volcano erupts. = 如果火山爆发，地面将会震动。
I think my hands will quake when I give the speech. = 我想我演讲的时候手会发抖。


虚拟语气:
If the ground quaked, we would run for cover. = 如果地面震动，我们会躲起来。
If I had known the truth, I wouldn't have quaked with fear. = 如果我知道真相，我就不会害怕发抖了。


被动语态:
The city was quaked by the powerful earthquake. = 这个城市被强烈的地震震动了。
His confidence was quaked by his failure. = 他的失败动摇了他的信心。


疑问句:
Did the ground quake last night? = 昨晚地面震动了吗？
Does he quake easily? = 他容易害怕吗？


否定句:
The ground did not quake during the night. = 夜间地面没有震动。
He did not quake in the face of danger. = 他面对危险时没有害怕。


PHRASE->

quake with fear = 害怕地颤抖
quake with cold = 冷得发抖
quake with anger = 气得发抖
quake in one's boots = 非常害怕
a quake of fear = 一阵恐惧
