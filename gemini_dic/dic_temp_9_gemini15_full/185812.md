revisiting:/ˌriːˈvɪzɪtɪŋ/| v. |重新访问；重新审视；重温 |To visit again; to consider or examine again.  |例句：We're revisiting the old strategies to see if they still work. 我们正在重新审视旧策略，看看它们是否仍然有效。

例句：I'm revisiting my childhood memories by looking through old photos. 我正在通过翻看旧照片来重温我的童年记忆。

近义词：reviewing, reconsidering, re-examining

反义词：ignoring, neglecting

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are revisiting their plans for the future. = 他们正在重新审视他们对未来的计划。
We revisit this topic regularly. = 我们定期重新审视这个话题。


一般过去时:
She revisited her favorite childhood restaurant. = 她重新光顾了她最喜欢的童年餐馆。
He revisited the crime scene. = 他重新探访了犯罪现场。


现在完成时:
I have revisited that museum several times. = 我已经多次重新参观过那个博物馆。
They have revisited the issue in light of new evidence. = 他们根据新的证据重新审视了这个问题。


过去完成时:
Before making a decision, she had revisited all the data. = 在做出决定之前，她已经重新审视了所有数据。
Having revisited the old house, he felt a sense of nostalgia. = 重新参观了老房子后，他感到一阵怀旧之情。


将来时:
We will revisit this matter next week. = 我们下周将重新讨论此事。
They will be revisiting their marketing strategy. = 他们将重新审视他们的营销策略。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would revisit Paris. = 如果我有时间，我会重游巴黎。
It would be helpful to revisit the original document. = 重新审阅原始文件将会有所帮助。


被动语态:
The proposal is being revisited by the committee. = 该提案正在被委员会重新审议。
The decision was revisited after further investigation. = 经过进一步调查后，该决定被重新审议。


疑问句:
Are you revisiting your earlier conclusions? = 你是否正在重新审视你之前的结论？
Will they be revisiting the project next year? = 他们明年会重新审视这个项目吗？


否定句:
He did not revisit the subject again. = 他没有再次重新讨论这个话题。
They are not revisiting the old methods. = 他们没有重新使用旧方法。
