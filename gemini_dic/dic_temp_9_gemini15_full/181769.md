religionism:/rɪˈlɪdʒənɪzəm/| n. |宗教主义；宗教狂热|The word "religionism" refers to excessive or fanatical adherence to religious beliefs and practices, often to the exclusion of other perspectives or values. It can also denote the tendency to impose religious beliefs on others or to use religion to justify actions or policies.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting intolerance, dogmatism, or the suppression of dissent.|例句：The rise of religionism led to increased social division and conflict. 宗教主义的兴起导致了社会分裂和冲突的加剧。
The politician's speech was criticized for its blatant religionism.  这位政治家的演讲因其公然的宗教主义而受到批评。|近义词：religious fanaticism, sectarianism, dogmatism, intolerance |反义词：secularism, tolerance, pluralism
