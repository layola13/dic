/ˈlaɪbreri/ | n. | 图书馆 | A place where books, periodicals, and other resources are kept for reading and study.  Libraries may be public, academic, or private.  | Example: The public library offers a wide range of services beyond just borrowing books.  公共图书馆提供各种各样的服务，而不仅仅是借书。 |近义词： archive, collection | 反义词：  None directly applicable.
