carabid:/ˈkærəbɪd/| n. |步甲科昆虫|A carabid is a beetle belonging to the family Carabidae.  Carabids are a large and diverse group of beetles, many of which are ground beetles known for their predatory habits. They are characterized by their elongated bodies, strong legs, and often dark coloration.  Many carabids are beneficial insects, helping to control pest populations in gardens and agricultural fields.|Example:  The researcher collected various carabid specimens for analysis.  研究人员收集了各种步甲科昆虫标本进行分析。|近义词：ground beetle (部分种类)|反义词：无|
