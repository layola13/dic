quasi-determined:/ˈkwɑːzaɪdɪˈtɜːrmɪnd/|/ˈkwɑːziːdɪˈtɜːrmɪnd/ | adj. | 准确定的；近乎确定的 |  Almost but not completely determined;  partially or approximately determined.
