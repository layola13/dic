cored:/kɔːrd/| adj.|去核的；核心部分的|Removed the core or central part.  The word refers to something that has had its core removed, or to the core itself once it has been removed.  It can also refer to something that is essential or central, although this usage is less common.|例句：The apples were cored before being used in the pie.  苹果在做馅饼之前去核了。The cored apple sat on the counter.  去核的苹果放在柜台上。|近义词：pitted, de-cored |反义词：whole, intact
