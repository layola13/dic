dentality:/denˈtælɪti/| n. |齿音性，齿音；牙性|The quality or state of being dental; the articulation of sounds using the teeth.  It refers to sounds produced with the tongue contacting the teeth. |Example: The dentality of the /θ/ and /ð/ sounds is a key feature distinguishing them from other fricatives.  齿音/θ/和/ð/的齿音性是区别它们与其他摩擦音的关键特征。|近义词：dental articulation|反义词：labiality (唇音性), velarity (颚音性)| noun
