governances:/ˈɡʌvənənsɪz/| n. |治理；管理；统治；管辖 |The term "governance" refers to the processes of governing, whether at the local, national, or international level.  It encompasses the structures, policies, and practices that guide decision-making and control resources.  It also includes the accountability mechanisms that ensure that those in power are responsible for their actions. | Example: The company's poor governance led to financial losses. 这家公司糟糕的治理导致了财务损失。The international community is working to improve governance in developing countries. 国际社会正在努力改善发展中国家的治理。|近义词：administration, management, rule, regulation |反义词：anarchy, chaos
