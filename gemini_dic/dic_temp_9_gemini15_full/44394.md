cross-fertilizing: /ˌkrɒsˈfɜːrtɪlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 交叉授粉；互相影响；思想交流 | The process of fertilizing a plant with pollen from a different plant of the same species, or the interaction between different ideas, cultures, or groups leading to mutual influence and innovation.  | Example: The company encourages cross-fertilizing ideas between different departments.  公司鼓励不同部门之间互相交流思想。


Example:  The research project benefited from cross-fertilizing ideas from various fields.  这个研究项目受益于来自各个领域的思想交流。


近义词:  interbreeding, hybridizing, influencing, interacting


反义词:  inbreeding, isolation, stagnation

Different cultures cross-fertilize each other through trade and migration. = 不同的文化通过贸易和迁移互相影响。
