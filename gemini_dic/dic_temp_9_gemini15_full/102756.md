january:/ˈdʒænjuˌɛri/ | n. | 一月 | January is the first month of the year. 一月是一年的第一个月。 | 近义词：January | 反义词:None

January comes after December.  = 一月在十二月之后。
January is a cold month in many parts of the world. = 在世界许多地方，一月是一个寒冷的月份。


一般过去时:
We went on a trip to Hawaii in January last year. = 我们去年一月去了夏威夷旅行。
