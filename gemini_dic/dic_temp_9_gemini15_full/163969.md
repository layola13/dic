英文解析:

"Predicating" describes the act of basing a statement, argument, or idea on something else. It can also mean to assert or affirm something.  The meaning often depends on context.  In logic, it refers to the part of a proposition that is asserted about the subject.


例句:

He was predicating his argument on faulty evidence. = 他将他的论点建立在错误的证据之上。

She predicated her success on hard work and dedication. = 她把她的成功归功于辛勤工作和奉献精神。
