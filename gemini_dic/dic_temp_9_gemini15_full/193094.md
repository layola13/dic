sectism:/ˈsɛktɪzəm/| n. |宗派主义；派系主义|Sectarianism; the belief that one's own sect or group is superior to others.  It often involves prejudice and discrimination against those belonging to different groups.| Example:  The rise of sectism in the country led to widespread social unrest.  这个国家宗派主义的兴起导致了广泛的社会动荡。|近义词：bigotry, sectarianism, factionalism|反义词：tolerance, inclusiveness|名词
