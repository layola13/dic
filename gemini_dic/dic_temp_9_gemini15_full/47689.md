debulliate:/dɪˈbʌliˌeɪt/| vt.|去除气泡；除去（液体中的）气泡|To remove bubbles from a liquid, typically by heating or vacuuming.  The process removes dissolved gases.|The brewer debulliated the wort to improve clarity.  酿酒师去除麦芽汁中的气泡以提高透明度。|The winemaker debulliated the wine to prevent cloudiness.  酿酒师去除葡萄酒中的气泡以防止浑浊。|近义词：degass, aerate (in the opposite sense)|反义词：aerate|动词
