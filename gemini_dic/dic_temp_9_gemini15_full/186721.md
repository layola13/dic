rightwardly:/ˈraɪtwərdli/| adv.|向右地，右方地|Moving or directed toward the right.  It describes the direction of movement or orientation.|例句：The car swerved rightwardly to avoid the obstacle.  汽车向右急转以避开障碍物。

例句：The arrow pointed rightwardly towards the treasure.  箭头向右指向宝藏。


近义词：to the right, rightwards, toward the right|反义词：leftwardly, to the left, leftwards
