matriculatory: /məˌtrɪkjuˈleɪtəri/ | adj. | 入学的；入学的 | Relating to matriculation; of or pertaining to admission to a college or university.  | 例句：The matriculatory examination was quite challenging.  入学考试相当具有挑战性。

The matriculatory process was lengthy and involved many forms. 入学流程冗长，涉及许多表格。


近义词：enrollment, registration, admission | 反义词：graduation, expulsion


词源:  From Late Latin *matriculatorius, from Latin *matriculāri "to enroll," from matricula "register," diminutive of matrix "mother," "womb," hence "source," "register."  The sense development is from "register" to "enrollment in a register" to "enrollment in a school."
