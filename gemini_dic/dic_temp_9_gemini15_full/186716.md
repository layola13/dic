rightsize:/ˈraɪtsaɪz/| vt.|调整规模；精简；使规模合适|To adjust the size or scale of something, typically a business or organization, to make it more efficient or profitable.  This often involves reducing staff or resources.|例句：The company decided to rightsize its workforce to cut costs.  公司决定精简员工以削减成本。|例句：They rightsized the project to meet the deadline. 他们缩减了项目规模以赶上最后期限。|近义词：downsize, streamline, optimize, restructure|反义词：expand, enlarge, increase


USAGE->

The company rightsizes its operations annually. = 公司每年都会调整其运营规模。
We rightsize our marketing budget based on performance. = 我们根据业绩调整营销预算。
