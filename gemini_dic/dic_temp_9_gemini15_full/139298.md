oblong:/ˈɒblɒŋ/ /ˈɑːblɔːŋ/| adj. n.|长方形的；椭圆形的；长方形|An oblong is a plane figure with four sides, having the opposite sides equal and parallel, and all angles right angles, except that two of its opposite sides are longer than the other two.  It's essentially a rectangle.|例句：The garden was an oblong shape. 花园是长方形的。|The table was oblong. 桌子是长方形的。|近义词：rectangular, elongated|反义词：square, round, circular|形容词，名词
