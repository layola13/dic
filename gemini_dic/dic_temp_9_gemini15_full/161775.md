powwower:/ˈpaʊwaʊər/| n. |巫师；施法者；(尤指美洲原住民的)巫医|A powwow is a person, usually a Native American, who practices magic or witchcraft, often for healing or other purposes.  They are sometimes associated with ceremonies and rituals. |例句：The tribe sought the powwow's help to cure the illness.  部落寻求巫医的帮助来治愈疾病。|近义词：medicine man, shaman, sorcerer |反义词：—
