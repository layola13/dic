rearticulate:/ˌriː.ɑːrˈtɪk.jə.leɪt/ | vt. |重新表达；重新阐明；清晰地表达 | To express something again or in a different way, often more clearly or effectively.  |例句：He rearticulated his position on the issue, clarifying any previous misunderstandings. 他重新阐述了他在这个问题上的立场，澄清了之前的任何误解。

例句：The professor rearticulated the complex theory in simpler terms for the students. 教授用更简单的术语重新解释了这个复杂的理论，以便学生理解。


近义词：rephrase, restate, reformulate, clarify, explain


反义词：mumble, obfuscate


词性：动词


USAGE->

He rearticulates his ideas regularly to ensure understanding. = 他定期重新阐述自己的想法以确保理解。
She rearticulates her concerns to the management team. = 她向管理团队重新表达了她的担忧。

否定句:

词源:
re- (prefix indicating again) + articulate (to express clearly)  The word "articulate" itself comes from the Latin "articulatus," the past participle of "articulare," meaning "to join together,"  referring to the joining of sounds in speech.
