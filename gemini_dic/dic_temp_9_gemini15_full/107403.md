laminating:/ˈlæməneɪtɪŋ/| v.|层压；覆膜|The process of bonding together multiple layers of material, often using heat and pressure.  This creates a single, more durable and often enhanced material.|例句：We're laminating the photographs to protect them from damage. 我们正在层压照片以保护它们免受损坏。

例句：The company laminates wood veneers to create beautiful and strong panels.  这家公司层压木单板以制造漂亮而坚固的板材。


近义词：bonding, coating, overlaying |反义词：separating, delaminating
