organism:/ˈɔːrɡənɪzəm/ | n. |有机体，生物| An individual living thing, such as an animal, plant, fungus, or bacterium, that is capable of growth, reproduction, and response to stimuli.|  Example: The amoeba is a single-celled organism.  变形虫是一种单细胞生物。|近义词：creature, being, living thing |反义词：inanimate object, nonliving thing
