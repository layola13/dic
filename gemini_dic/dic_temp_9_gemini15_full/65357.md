eulogizations:/ˌjuːləˈdʒaɪzeɪʃənz/| n. |颂词；赞美；悼词|The act of delivering eulogies; the practice of praising someone highly, especially in a formal speech, often at a funeral.  Eulogizations are often heartfelt expressions of admiration and respect for the deceased.|例句：The eulogizations at the memorial service were both moving and insightful.  纪念仪式上的悼词既感人又富有洞察力。


The eulogizations of her career highlighted her dedication and achievements. 对她职业生涯的赞扬突出了她的奉献和成就。|近义词：praises, tributes, commemorations, panegyrics |反义词：criticisms, condemnations, denouncements
