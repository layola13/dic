gyrating:/ˈdʒaɪreɪtɪŋ/| v. |旋转，回旋，摆动 | Moving in a circle or spiral; rotating rapidly.  |例句：The dancer was gyrating wildly on stage.  舞者在舞台上疯狂地旋转。|例句：The propeller was gyrating rapidly. 螺旋桨快速旋转。|近义词：rotating, spinning, whirling, revolving |反义词：stationary, still


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The top is gyrating. = 陀螺在旋转。
The dancer is gyrating gracefully. = 舞者优雅地旋转。

一般过去时:
The earth gyrated on its axis. = 地球绕其轴自转。
The dervish gyrated for hours. = 旋转舞者旋转了数小时。

现在完成时:
The top has gyrated for five minutes. = 陀螺已经旋转了五分钟。
The whirling dervishes have gyrated in a mesmerizing display. = 旋转舞者进行了令人着迷的旋转表演。

过去完成时:
The dancer had gyrated across the stage before the music stopped. = 音乐停止前，舞者已在舞台上旋转。
The wind had gyrated the leaves into a swirling vortex. = 风已将树叶卷成一个旋转的漩涡。

将来时:
The planet will gyrate around the sun. = 行星将绕太阳旋转。
She will gyrate to the music at the party. = 她将在派对上随着音乐旋转。

虚拟语气:
If the earth didn't gyrate, we would have no day and night. = 如果地球不旋转，我们将没有白天和黑夜。
I wish the fan would gyrate more slowly. = 我希望风扇转得慢一些。

被动语态:
The satellite is gyrated into orbit. = 卫星被旋转到轨道上。
The image was gyrated to create a dizzying effect. = 图像被旋转以产生令人眩晕的效果。

疑问句:
Is the wheel gyrating smoothly? = 轮子旋转平稳吗？
Are the planets gyrating in their orbits? = 行星在它们的轨道上旋转吗？

否定句:
The motor isn't gyrating. = 电机没有旋转。
The compass needle doesn't gyrate wildly. = 指南针指针不会剧烈摆动。


PHRASE->
gyrating motion = 旋转运动
rapidly gyrating = 快速旋转
freely gyrating = 自由旋转
slowly gyrating = 缓慢旋转
continuously gyrating = 持续旋转

词源:
源自希腊语 "gyros" (γῦρος)，意思是 "circle" 或 "ring"。  这个词根也出现在许多其他与旋转相关的词语中，例如 gyroscope (陀螺仪)。
