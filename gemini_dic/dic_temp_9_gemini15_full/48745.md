defining:/dɪˈfaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. |限定的；起决定作用的；定义性的 | Defining something means explaining its meaning or essential qualities.  It can also refer to something that is extremely important and shapes the characteristics of something else. |例句：The defining characteristic of this species is its bright red plumage.  这种物种的决定性特征是它鲜红的羽毛。

例句：The defining moment of the war was the battle of Gettysburg.  战争中最关键的时刻是葛底斯堡战役。


词源:  From the verb "define," ultimately from Latin "definire," meaning "to limit, to bound," from "de-" (intensive prefix) + "finire" (to finish, to limit).


近义词：determinative, crucial, characteristic, essential, significant


反义词：insignificant, unimportant, inconsequential, trivial
