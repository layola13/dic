gallantness:/ˈɡæləntnəs/| n. |勇敢；潇洒；殷勤；骑士风度 |The quality of being gallant;  bravery, courtesy, and especially chivalrous attention to women.  It suggests a noble and courteous demeanor, often associated with bravery and respect.|例句：His gallantness in rescuing the child from the burning building was praised by everyone. 他从燃烧的建筑物中救出孩子，其勇敢行为受到大家的赞扬。

例句：The gallantness he showed towards the elderly lady was truly admirable. 他对这位老妇人的殷勤关照令人钦佩。


近义词：bravery, chivalry, courage, courtesy, politeness, heroism


反义词：cowardice, rudeness, discourtesy, meanness


词源：源自古法语 *galant,  最终源自于拉丁语 *gallus ("Gaul, Frenchman"),  最初指法国人的勇敢和优雅。  随着时间的推移，其含义扩展到包含了骑士精神和对女性的殷勤。
