scarring:/ˈskɑːrɪŋ/| n.|疤痕形成；创伤；精神创伤|The process of forming scars on the skin or other tissue, or the scars themselves.  It can also refer to emotional or psychological trauma.|例句：The accident resulted in severe scarring on his face.  事故导致他脸上留下严重的疤痕。

例句：The experience left deep emotional scarring.  这次经历给他留下了深深的精神创伤。|近义词：cicatrization, wounding, trauma|反义词：healing, recovery


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The burn is causing scarring. = 烧伤正在造成疤痕。
Scarring is a natural part of healing. = 疤痕形成是愈合的自然过程。


一般过去时:
