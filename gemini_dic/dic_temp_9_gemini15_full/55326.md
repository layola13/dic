dissuadable: /dɪˈsweɪdəbl/ | adj. | 可劝阻的，可劝说的 | Capable of being dissuaded; open to persuasion.  |  He was surprisingly dissuadable, changing his mind after a brief conversation. 他令人惊讶地容易被劝服，在简短的交谈后就改变了主意。 | persuadable, amenable | unpersuadable, intractable, obstinate |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is dissuadable; a little gentle persuasion is all it takes. 他是可以劝说的；只需要一点温和的劝说。
She is usually dissuadable when presented with the facts. 当摆出事实时，她通常是可以劝说的。


一般过去时:
The child was dissuadable after his mother explained the dangers. 在母亲解释了危险之后，孩子可以被劝服了。
The stubborn man was not dissuadable despite repeated attempts. 尽管反复尝试，那个固执的男人还是无法被劝服。


现在完成时:
They have been dissuadable from their risky plan. 他们已经放弃了他们的冒险计划。
I have been dissuadable from smoking for several years now. 我已经戒烟好几年了。


过去完成时:
He had been dissuadable from going to the party, but later changed his mind. 他原本可以被劝服不去参加聚会，但后来改变了主意。
She had been dissuadable by her friends' concerns, but decided to proceed anyway. 她曾被朋友们的担忧劝退，但还是决定继续进行。


将来时:

We believe she will be dissuadable if we offer a better alternative. 我们相信如果我们提供更好的替代方案，她就会被劝服。


虚拟语气:
If he were dissuadable, we would have saved a lot of trouble. 如果他能被劝服，我们将省去很多麻烦。
I wish he were more dissuadable; this is a dangerous path he's on. 我希望他更容易被劝服；他正在走一条危险的道路。


被动语态:

疑问句:
