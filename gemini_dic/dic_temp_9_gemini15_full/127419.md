neatened:/ˈniːtənd/| v.|使整洁；使整齐|To make something tidy or orderly.  |例句：She neatened her desk before leaving work. 她下班前整理了她的桌子。|例句：He neatened the garden by trimming the hedges. 他修剪树篱使花园变得整洁。|近义词：tidying, cleaned, organized |反义词：mess up, disorganize.


USAGE->
