nonneural: /ˌnɒnˈnjʊərəl/ | adj. |非神经的；非神经元的| Not relating to or involving neurons or the nervous system.  The term refers to processes or systems that are not based on neural networks or biological neural activity. |例句：The researchers are developing a nonneural approach to artificial intelligence.  研究人员正在开发一种非神经的人工智能方法。


例句：This nonneural model simulates the behavior of the system without using artificial neural networks.  这个非神经模型在不使用人工神经网络的情况下模拟系统的行为。


近义词：non-nervous, extra-neural


反义词：neural, neuronal


词性：形容词
