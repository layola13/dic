plods:/plɒdz/| /plɑːdz/| v.| 沉重地走；缓慢地走；辛勤地工作 | To walk slowly and heavily, as if with great effort.  It can also refer to making slow but steady progress. |例句：He plodded through the mud. 他在泥泞中艰难地行走。

He plodded away at his work. 他辛勤地工作。|近义词：trudge, lumber, toil, drudge |反义词：speed, rush, sprint |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plods along the road. = 他沿着路缓慢地走着。
She plods through her homework. = 她辛勤地做着作业。

一般过去时:
They plodded home in the rain. = 他们冒雨缓慢地走回家。
He plodded through the thick forest. = 他艰难地穿过茂密的森林。

现在完成时:
She has plodded through many challenges in her life. = 她一生中克服了许多挑战。
We have plodded along steadily toward our goal. = 我们一直在稳步地朝着目标前进。

过去完成时:
He had plodded for hours before he reached the village. = 他走了好几个小时才到达村庄。
They had plodded through the snow all day. = 他们一整天都在雪地里艰难地跋涉。

将来时:
We will plod on, regardless of the difficulties. = 不管遇到什么困难，我们都会继续前进。
She will plod through the paperwork tomorrow. = 她明天将处理这些文件。

虚拟语气:
If he had plodded faster, he would have arrived earlier. = 如果他走得快一点，他就会早到。
I wish I hadn't plodded so slowly. = 我真希望我没有走得那么慢。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The journey was plodded through by the weary travelers. = 这段旅程被疲惫的旅行者艰难地走完了。(较为不自然)
The task was plodded through slowly but surely. = 任务虽然缓慢但最终完成了。(较为不自然)

疑问句:
Does he plod along like that every day? = 他每天都这样缓慢地走吗？
Will they plod through the assignment on time? = 他们会按时完成作业吗？

否定句:
He didn't plod along; he ran. = 他没有缓慢地走；他是跑的。
She didn't plod through the work; she finished it quickly. = 她没有辛勤地工作；她很快就完成了。


PHRASE->
plod along: 缓慢地前进
plod on: 坚持不懈
plod through: 艰难地完成
plod wearily: 疲惫地行走
plod steadily: 稳步前进
