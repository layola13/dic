drawee:/drɔːˈiː/| n. |受票人，付款人 |The drawee is the person, company, or bank ordered to pay a draft or bill of exchange.  A drawee is essentially the recipient of a financial instrument, typically a check or bill of exchange, which instructs them to pay a specified sum to a payee.  | Example: The bank was the drawee on the check.  这家银行是支票的受票人。  The drawee refused to honor the bill of exchange. 受票人拒绝承兑汇票。 |近义词：payee (in the context of receiving payment), acceptor |反义词：drawer (the person who creates the instrument)
