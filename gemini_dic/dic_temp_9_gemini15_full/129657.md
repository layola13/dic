non-debtor:/ˈnɑːnˈdɛtər/| n. |非债务人，非负债者 |A non-debtor is someone who does not owe money to anyone.  This term is often used in financial contexts, referring to individuals or entities without outstanding debts or liabilities. |Example: The bank's report showed a surprisingly high number of non-debtors this quarter.  这个季度的银行报告显示非债务人数量出乎意料地高。|近义词：non-borrower, non-obligor |反义词：debtor, obligor |名词
