proformas:/ˈproʊfɔːrməz/ | n. | 预估单；形式发票；格式；样表 | A proforma invoice is a preliminary invoice sent to a buyer before the goods or services are provided, outlining the terms of the sale. It is not a demand for immediate payment but serves as a confirmation of the order and details of the transaction.  | Example: We sent a proforma invoice to the client detailing the cost of the project.  我们向客户发送了一份详细说明项目成本的预估发票。

Example: The company uses proforma invoices to manage its sales orders efficiently. 公司使用预估发票来有效管理销售订单。


近义词：preliminary invoice, estimate, quotation | 反义词：final invoice, actual invoice | 词性：名词
