directive:/daɪˈrɛktɪv/| n. |指令，指示，法规 |A directive is an official instruction or order.  It can be a formal statement of policy or a specific command.  Directives are usually issued by someone in authority. |例句：The company issued a directive requiring all employees to attend the safety training. 公司发布了一项指令，要求所有员工参加安全培训。
The government's new directive on environmental protection is controversial. 政府关于环境保护的新指令存在争议。|近义词：instruction, order, command, mandate, guideline |反义词：suggestion, request, proposal
