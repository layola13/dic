onboarding:/ˈɒnˌbɔːdɪŋ/| n. v.|员工入职培训；新手引导；|The process of integrating a new employee into a company.  It involves training, orientation, and socialization to help the new hire become productive and engaged. |例句：The company has a comprehensive onboarding program for new employees. = 这家公司为新员工提供了一个全面的入职培训项目。

例句：Our onboarding process includes a mentor program to support new hires. = 我们的入职流程包括一个导师计划来支持新员工。


近义词：orientation, integration, induction, familiarization


反义词：offboarding, termination, dismissal
