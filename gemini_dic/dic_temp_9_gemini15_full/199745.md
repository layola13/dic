siren:/ˈsaɪrən/ | n. | 汽笛；警报器；妖女 | A siren is a device that makes a loud, wailing sound as a warning or signal, often used by emergency vehicles. It can also refer to a mythical creature, often depicted as a beautiful woman, who lured sailors to their deaths with her enchanting voice.  | Example: The siren of the ambulance wailed as it sped down the street.  救护车的警笛声在它飞驰而下时呜呜作响。The sailors were captivated by the siren's song. 水手们被妖女的歌声迷住了。 | 近义词：alarm, warning device,  (mythological) temptress | 反义词：silence, calm
