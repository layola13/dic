nontrusting: /ˌnɒnˈtrʌstɪŋ/ | adj. | 不信任的，猜疑的 | Not trusting; suspicious.  Characterized by a lack of trust or confidence. |


例句：

He had a nontrusting look on his face. = 他脸上带着不信任的表情。

Her nontrusting nature made it difficult to form close relationships. = 她不信任的性格使她难以建立亲密的关系。


近义词：suspicious, distrustful, skeptical, wary, leery


反义词：trusting, confident, believing


词性：形容词
