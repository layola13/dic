semirepublics:/ˌsɛmiˈpʌblɪks/| n.|半共和国；半共和政体|States that are partly republics and partly other forms of government, often possessing some characteristics of a monarchy or oligarchy alongside republican elements.  The term suggests an incomplete or transitional form of republican governance.|例句：The historical development of many nations reveals various stages, including periods characterized as semirepublics.|近义词：mixed government, quasi-republic|反义词：pure republic, absolute monarchy
