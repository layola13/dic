enhance:/ɪnˈhɑːns/ | vt. | 增强，提高，改进 | to improve the quality, amount, or strength of something | 例句：The new software enhances the computer's performance. 新软件增强了电脑的性能。

例句：The beautiful scenery enhanced our enjoyment of the trip.美丽的风景增强了我们旅行的乐趣。


近义词：improve, better, strengthen, amplify, boost | 反义词：reduce, diminish, weaken, impair, deteriorate | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enhances his presentation with visual aids. 他用视觉辅助工具来增强他的演示文稿。
We enhance the flavor of the dish with herbs. 我们用香草来增强这道菜的味道。


一般过去时:
The renovations enhanced the value of the house.  翻新工程提高了房子的价值。
She enhanced her resume with relevant experience. 她用相关的经验来增强她的简历。


现在完成时:
They have enhanced their security measures. 他们已经加强了安全措施。
The company has enhanced its customer service. 公司已经改善了它的客户服务。


过去完成时:
He had enhanced his skills before applying for the job. 他在申请工作之前已经提高了他的技能。
They had enhanced the product design before launching it. 他们在发布产品之前已经改进了产品设计。


将来时:
We will enhance the website with new features. 我们将用新功能来增强网站。
The update will enhance the game's graphics. 更新将增强游戏的画面。


虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we could enhance the project. 如果我们有更多资源，我们就能改进项目。
I wish I could enhance my writing skills. 我希望我能提高我的写作能力。


被动语态:
The image was enhanced using special software. 图片是用特殊软件增强的。
The security system is being enhanced to prevent future breaches. 安全系统正在被增强以防止未来的漏洞。


疑问句:
How can we enhance the learning experience? 我们如何才能增强学习体验？
What strategies can enhance productivity?  什么策略可以提高生产力？


否定句:
The poor lighting didn't enhance the mood of the room.  糟糕的灯光并没有增强房间的气氛。
He didn't enhance his argument with sufficient evidence. 他没有用足够的证据来增强他的论点。
