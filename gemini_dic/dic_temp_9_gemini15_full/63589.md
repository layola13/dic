envision:/ɪnˈvɪʒən/ | vt. |想象，设想，预想 | To form a mental picture of (something not yet existing); to imagine.  |例句：I envisioned a future where everyone has access to clean energy. 我设想了一个未来，在那里每个人都能获得清洁能源。

例句：She envisioned herself winning the gold medal. 她想象着自己赢得金牌。


近义词：imagine, visualize, foresee, contemplate | 反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I envision a brighter future. = 我设想一个更光明的未来。
They envision a successful product launch. = 他们设想产品发布成功。


一般过去时:
We envisioned a different outcome. = 我们预想了一个不同的结果。
She envisioned a life of luxury. = 她想象着奢华的生活。


现在完成时:
I have envisioned this moment for years. = 我已经想象这一刻多年了。
They have envisioned many scenarios. = 他们已经设想了许多情况。


过去完成时:
He had envisioned a peaceful retirement. = 他曾设想过一个平静的退休生活。
She had envisioned a different career path. = 她曾设想过不同的职业道路。


将来时:
We will envision a new strategy. = 我们将设想一个新的策略。
They will envision a better world. = 他们将设想一个更美好的世界。


虚拟语气:
If I envisioned success, I would work harder. = 如果我设想成功，我会更加努力工作。
I wish I had envisioned the consequences. = 我真希望我预见到后果。


被动语态:
A brighter future is envisioned by many. = 许多人设想了一个更光明的未来。
A new solution was envisioned by the team. = 一个新的解决方案是由团队设想的。


疑问句:
What do you envision for the future? = 你对未来有什么设想？
What did they envision for the project? = 他们对这个项目有什么设想？


否定句:
I don't envision any problems. = 我预见不到任何问题。
They didn't envision such a drastic change. = 他们没有预料到如此巨大的变化。


PHRASE->
envision a future = 设想未来

词源:
The word "envision" comes from the Old French word "envisager," which means "to face," "to look at," or "to consider."  The prefix "en-" intensifies the meaning, while "vision" refers to sight or mental imagery.  Therefore, "envision" literally means to "look at intensely" or "form a strong mental image of."
