noncrushable: /ˌnɒnˈkrʌʃəbəl/ | adj. |不可压碎的，坚不可摧的 | Not capable of being crushed or broken.  This word describes something that resists being compressed or fractured under pressure.  It implies a high degree of strength and resilience. |例句：The noncrushable container protected the fragile contents during shipping.  不可压碎的容器在运输过程中保护了易碎的物品。


The noncrushable helmet saved the cyclist's life in the accident.  不可压碎的头盔在事故中挽救了骑车人的生命。


近义词：indestructible, unbreakable, crushproof, impact-resistant |反义词：crushable, breakable, fragile, brittle


词源:  The word is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the word "crushable," which itself derives from the verb "crush" + "-able" (suffix indicating capability).  The verb "crush" ultimately traces back to the Old French word "cruser," meaning "to bruise, crush."
