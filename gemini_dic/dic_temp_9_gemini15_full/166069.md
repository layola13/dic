preponderance:/prɪˈpɒndərəns/| n. |优势；大多数；压倒性力量|The preponderance of evidence suggests he is guilty.  证据的优势表明他有罪。|The preponderance of opinion favored the proposal.  大多数意见赞成这项提议。|近义词：majority, predominance, superiority |反义词：minority, scarcity, deficiency


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The preponderance of evidence points to his guilt. = 证据的优势表明他有罪。
A preponderance of people support the new policy. = 大多数人支持这项新政策。


一般过去时:
The preponderance of votes decided the election. = 投票结果决定了选举。
A preponderance of opinions favored the plan last year. = 去年大多数意见都赞成这个计划。


现在完成时:
The preponderance of scientific evidence has confirmed the theory. = 大量的科学证据已经证实了这个理论。
A preponderance of studies has shown a link between smoking and cancer. = 大量的研究表明吸烟和癌症之间存在联系。


过去完成时:
By the end of the debate, a preponderance of arguments had favored the opposition. = 到辩论结束时，大部分论点都支持反对派。
The preponderance of evidence had already suggested his involvement before the trial. = 在审判之前，大量的证据已经表明他参与其中。


将来时:
A preponderance of votes will likely decide the election next year. = 明年的选举很可能由大多数选票决定。
We expect a preponderance of applications for the position. = 我们预计会有很多申请这个职位。


虚拟语气:
If a preponderance of evidence existed, the case would be closed. = 如果有大量的证据，案件就会结案。
I wish there were a preponderance of positive feedback on my work. = 我希望我的工作能得到大多数积极的反馈。


被动语态:
His guilt was determined by the preponderance of evidence. = 他的罪行是由大量的证据确定的。
The decision was swayed by the preponderance of public opinion. = 这个决定受到了公众舆论的强烈影响。


疑问句:
Does a preponderance of evidence support this theory? = 大量的证据是否支持这个理论？
Is there a preponderance of support for this candidate? = 这个候选人是否有压倒性的支持？


否定句:
There was not a preponderance of evidence to convict him. = 没有足够的证据定他的罪。
A preponderance of negative reviews did not discourage the author. = 大量的负面评论并没有让作者气馁。


PHRASE->
a preponderance of evidence = 大量的证据

a preponderance of power = 强大的力量
a preponderance of factors = 大多数因素
in the preponderance of cases = 在大多数情况下
