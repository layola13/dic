circle:/ˈsɜːrkl/| n. v.|圆圈，循环，圈子|A round plane figure whose boundary consists of points equidistant from a fixed center.  Also, a group of people with shared interests or activities.|例句：Draw a circle on the paper. 在纸上画一个圆圈。

The students formed a circle around the teacher. 学生们围成一个圈站在老师周围。|近义词：ring, round, sphere |反义词：line, square


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He circles the globe every year. 他每年环游世界。
The birds circle overhead. 鸟儿在头顶盘旋。


一般过去时:
She circled the block three times before finding her house. 她绕着街区转了三圈才找到她的房子。
They circled back to their starting point. 他们回到起点。


现在完成时:
The plane has circled the airport several times.  飞机已经绕机场盘旋了好几次。
We have circled the wagons to protect ourselves. 我们已经严阵以待，以保护自己。


过去完成时:
He had circled the number on the list before handing it in. 他在提交清单之前已经圈出了那个数字。
They had circled the mountain for hours before finding a pass. 他们在找到山口之前绕着山转了好几个小时。


将来时:
The helicopter will circle the accident site. 直升机将盘旋在事故现场。
She will circle back to that point later in her presentation. 她稍后会在她的演讲中回到这一点。


虚拟语气:
If I circled the earth, I would feel accomplished. 如果我环游世界，我会感到成就感。
I wish I had circled back to discuss that with you. 我真希望我当时回头和你讨论一下。


被动语态:
The city is circled by a protective wall. 城市被一道防护墙包围着。
The topic was circled in red ink.  主题用红墨水圈了出来。


疑问句:
Does this line circle the center point? 这条线是否围绕中心点？

否定句:
