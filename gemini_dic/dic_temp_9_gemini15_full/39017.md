confit:/kənˈfiːt/| n. v.| 烹调法；腌渍肉；油封肉 | Preserved food, typically meat (especially duck or goose), that is slowly cooked in its own fat.  | The duck confit was incredibly tender and flavorful. 油封鸭非常嫩而且美味。| 近义词：preserve, conserve | 反义词：spoil, rot
