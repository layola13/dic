osmate:/ˈɒzmeɪt/ | n. | 奥斯米酸盐 |  A salt or ester of osmic acid. Osmate salts are typically used in organic chemistry as oxidizing agents. They are characterized by the presence of the osmium(VIII) atom in an octahedral coordination geometry. | Example: Potassium osmate is a commonly used osmate. 钾奥斯米酸盐是一种常用的奥斯米酸盐。 |近义词: osmium compound |反义词: N/A
