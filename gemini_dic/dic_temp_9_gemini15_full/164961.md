preguide:/ˈpriːɡaɪd/| n. |预先指导，预先引导|A preguide is something that provides guidance or direction beforehand, often in the form of instructions, advice, or a preliminary plan.|例句：The preguide for the software installation was very helpful.  软件安装的预先指导非常有用。|近义词：preliminary instruction, advance guidance, prior direction|反义词：post-guidance, afterthought
