calf:/kæf/| n.|小牛；腓肠肌|A young bovine animal, especially one less than a year old. Also refers to the fleshy part of the back of the lower leg.|例句：The farmer raised several calves.  这位农民养了几头小牛。|例句：She pulled a muscle in her calf. 她拉伤了腓肠肌。|近义词：young bovine, heifer (female calf)|反义词：cow, ox (adult bovine)
