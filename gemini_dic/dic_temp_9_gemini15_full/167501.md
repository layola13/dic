preview:/ˈpriːvjuː/| n. v.|预览，预展；预告；初步察看|A preview is an opportunity to see or hear something before it is officially released to the public.  It is often a short version or a sample of the complete work.|例句：The film studio offered a preview of the new movie to critics. 电影制片厂向影评人提供了新电影的预告片。

例句：Let's have a preview of the presentation before the meeting. 我们在会议前先预览一下演示文稿。|近义词：preliminary showing, sneak peek, foretaste|反义词：final version, full release


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We preview the marketing materials before the campaign launches. 我们在活动开始前预览营销材料。
The company previews its new products at trade shows. 公司在贸易展会上预览其新产品。

一般过去时:
They previewed the new software last week. 他们上周预览了新软件。
I previewed the chapter before reading the whole book. 我在阅读整本书之前预览了章节。


现在完成时:
We have previewed all the designs for the project. 我们已经预览了项目的所有设计。
She has previewed the presentation several times. 她已经多次预览了演示文稿。


过去完成时:
They had previewed the movie before it was released to the public.  他们在电影公开放映前就看过预告片了。
We had previewed the contract before signing it.  我们在签署合同之前已经预览过它了。


将来时:
We will preview the new website next Monday. 我们下周一将预览新网站。
They will preview the album before its official release. 他们将在专辑正式发行前进行预览。


虚拟语气:
If I had previewed the document, I would have noticed the error. 如果我事先预览了文件，我就会注意到错误。
It would be helpful if we previewed the presentation one more time. 如果我们再预览一次演示文稿，将会很有帮助。


被动语态:
The movie was previewed at a special screening.  这部电影在特别放映会上进行了预览。
The software is being previewed by beta testers. 该软件正在由测试人员进行预览。


疑问句:
Have you previewed the report? 你预览过报告了吗？
Will they preview the product before launching it? 他们会在发布产品之前预览吗？


否定句:
I didn't preview the document before sending it. 我在发送文件之前没有预览。
They did not preview the design before printing it. 他们在打印之前没有预览设计。
