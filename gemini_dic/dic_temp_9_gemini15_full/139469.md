obstetrics:/ɒbˈstɛtrɪks/ | n. |妇产科，产科学 |The branch of medicine and surgery concerned with childbirth and the care of women during pregnancy and childbirth.  |例句：She is studying obstetrics and hopes to become a skilled obstetrician. 她正在学习妇产科，希望成为一名熟练的妇产科医生。|例句： Advances in obstetrics have significantly reduced maternal mortality rates. 妇产科的进步大大降低了孕产妇死亡率。|近义词：midwifery (助产学，侧重助产士的工作)|反义词：无|词性：名词
