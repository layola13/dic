customary:/ˈkʌstəmeri/| adj. |惯例的；习俗的；通常的|  Usual or expected according to tradition or custom.  It refers to something that is commonly done or practiced in a particular society, group, or situation. |例句：It is customary to tip waiters in many countries. = 在许多国家给服务员小费是惯例。

例句：Wearing a suit to a job interview is customary in many cultures. = 在许多文化中，去面试穿西装是惯例的。


近义词：usual, traditional, conventional, habitual, typical |反义词：unusual, unconventional, exceptional, extraordinary


词性:形容词
