rig:/rɪɡ/ | n./v.|  操纵；装备；钻井平台；装置 | To manipulate or control unfairly; to equip or furnish; a framework or structure, especially a drilling platform for oil extraction. | 例句：They rigged the election. 他们操纵了选举。  They rigged the boat with new sails. 他们用新帆装备了船。|近义词：manipulate, control, equip, outfit | 反义词：fairness, honesty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rigs the game every year. = 他每年都操纵比赛。
They rig the sails before the race. = 他们在比赛前安装帆。


一般过去时:
The workers rigged the scaffolding yesterday. = 工人们昨天搭建了脚手架。
She rigged the lights for the play. = 她为戏剧安装了灯光。


现在完成时:
They have rigged the voting machines. = 他们已经操纵了投票机。
He has rigged the boat with new equipment. = 他已经用新设备装备了船。


过去完成时:
They had rigged the system before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，他们就已经操纵了系统。
She had rigged the camera before the interview. = 她在采访之前就已经安装好了摄像机。


将来时:
We will rig the tent tomorrow. = 我们明天将搭建帐篷。
They will rig the oil platform next month. = 他们下个月将安装石油钻井平台。


虚拟语气:
If they rigged the election, they should be punished. = 如果他们操纵了选举，他们应该受到惩罚。
I wish they hadn't rigged the competition. = 我真希望他们没有操纵比赛。


被动语态:
The election was rigged. = 选举被操纵了。
The boat was rigged with new sails. = 船被装备了新帆。


疑问句:
Was the game rigged? = 比赛被操纵了吗？

否定句:
He didn't rig the election. = 他没有操纵选举。
They didn't rig the boat properly. = 他们没有正确地装备船。

词源:
The word "rig" ultimately derives from the Old Norse word "hrígr," meaning "ridge" or "back."  Its evolution through Middle English saw it applied to various structures and actions. The sense of "manipulating" arose later, reflecting the ability to "rig" something to one's advantage.
