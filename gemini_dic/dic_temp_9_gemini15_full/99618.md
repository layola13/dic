interjoist:/ˈɪntərˌdʒɔɪst/| n. |楼层搁栅，次梁|A horizontal structural member used in floor or ceiling construction, spanning between main joists.  They provide additional support and reduce the span of the main joists. |Example: The builder added interjoists to strengthen the floor system.  建造者增加了次梁以加强楼板系统。|近义词：secondary joist, bridging joist |反义词：main joist, primary joist
