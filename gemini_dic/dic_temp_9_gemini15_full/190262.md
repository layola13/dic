sappiness:/ˈsæpɪnəs/| n. |多愁善感；充满感情；愚蠢；软弱|Excessive sentimentality or emotionalism;  a lack of firmness or strength of character; foolishness.|例句：Her sappiness was endearing at first, but it grew tiresome.  她的多愁善感最初很可爱，但后来让人厌烦了。

例句：The sappiness of his romantic novel made it difficult to read. 他那本浪漫小说的矫揉造作让人难以卒读。


近义词：sentimentality, mushiness, mawkishness, weakness


反义词：hardness, strength, stoicism, cynicism


词源：源自sap (n.) "树液；活力；愚蠢的人"，其含义引申而来，暗示缺乏坚韧和力量。The suffix "-iness" denotes a state or quality.
