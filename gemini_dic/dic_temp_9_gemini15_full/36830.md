colonised: /ˈkɒlənaɪzd/ /ˈkɑːlənaɪzd/ | v. | 殖民；占领；开拓 | To establish control over (a place or people) and settle there, often imposing one's own culture and laws.  |  The Europeans colonised much of the Americas. 欧洲人殖民了美洲的大部分地区。 |The British colonised India for many years. 英国殖民印度很多年。|  settled, occupied, conquered | decolonised, liberated

USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government colonises new territories. = 政府正在殖民新的领土。
They colonise the minds of the young. = 他们正在殖民年轻人的思想。


一般过去时:
The Romans colonised Britain. = 罗马人殖民了不列颠。
Spain colonised much of South America. = 西班牙殖民了南美洲的大部分地区。


现在完成时:
European powers have colonised vast swathes of the globe. = 欧洲列强已经殖民了全球的大片地区。
They have colonised the market with their new product. = 他们用他们的新产品占领了市场。


过去完成时:
The indigenous population had been colonised for centuries. = 当地居民已经被殖民了几个世纪。
Before the arrival of the Europeans, the land had not been colonised. = 在欧洲人到来之前，这片土地没有被殖民。


将来时:
Will they colonise Mars in the future? = 他们将来会殖民火星吗？
We will not allow them to colonise our country. = 我们不会允许他们殖民我们的国家。


虚拟语气:
If they had colonised that area, they would have found gold. = 如果他们殖民了那个地区，他们会发现黄金。
I wish they hadn't colonised our homeland. = 我希望他们没有殖民我们的家园。


被动语态:
The island was colonised by the Spanish in the 16th century. = 这个岛屿在16世纪被西班牙人殖民。
Many countries have been colonised throughout history. = 许多国家在历史上都被殖民过。


疑问句:
Were the islands colonised by the British? = 这些岛屿是被英国人殖民的吗？
Has this area ever been colonised? = 这个地区曾经被殖民过吗？


否定句:
The colonists did not colonise the entire continent. = 殖民者并没有殖民整个大陆。
They did not intend to colonise the neighboring country. = 他们并不打算殖民邻国。
