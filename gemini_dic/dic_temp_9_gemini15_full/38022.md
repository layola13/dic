comply:/kəmˈplaɪ/ | v. |遵从，服从 | To act according to a wish, command, rule, etc.  |例句：You must comply with the regulations. 你必须遵守规章制度。

例句：The company failed to comply with safety regulations. 这家公司未能遵守安全条例。


近义词：obey, conform, adhere to | 反义词：disobey, defy, resist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He complies with all the rules. 他遵守所有规则。
We comply with the company's policies. 我们遵守公司的政策。

一般过去时:
She complied with the judge's order. 她遵守了法官的命令。
They complied with the request immediately. 他们立即照办了。

现在完成时:
He has complied with all the requirements. 他已经满足了所有要求。
We have complied with the law. 我们已经遵守了法律。

过去完成时:
They had complied with the instructions before the deadline. 他们在截止日期前已遵守了指示。
She had complied with the request, although reluctantly. 尽管不情愿，她还是照办了。

将来时:
He will comply with the new regulations. 他将遵守新的规章制度。
We will comply with your request as soon as possible. 我们将尽快满足您的要求。


虚拟语气:
If he complied, he would avoid punishment. 如果他服从，他就能避免惩罚。
I wish they would comply with our demands. 我希望他们能满足我们的要求。


被动语态:
The rules must be complied with. 必须遵守规则。
His request was complied with promptly. 他的请求得到了迅速的回应。


疑问句:
Will you comply with my request? 你会答应我的请求吗？
Did they comply with the terms of the contract? 他们是否遵守了合同条款？


否定句:
He refused to comply with the order. 他拒绝服从命令。
The company did not comply with the environmental regulations. 公司没有遵守环境法规。


PHRASE->
comply with = 遵守
comply fully = 完全遵守
comply strictly = 严格遵守
fail to comply = 未能遵守
comply in good faith = 善意遵守


词源:
From Old French *complir, from Latin complēre "to fill up, complete," from com- "completely" + plēre "to fill."  The sense shifted to "to fulfill" and then to "to comply."
