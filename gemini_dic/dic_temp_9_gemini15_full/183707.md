resaddles:/ˌriːˈsæd(ə)lz/| v.|重新上鞍；重新骑上|To put a saddle back on (a horse) after it has been removed; to remount a horse.|例句：The cowboy resaddled his horse after a short break. 牛仔短暂休息后重新上鞍。|例句：After the fall, the rider carefully resaddled his horse. 摔倒后，骑手小心翼翼地重新上鞍。|近义词：remount, saddle again|反义词：unsaddle
