panstereorama: /ˌpænˌstɪərəˈræmə/ | n. | 全景立体图，全景立体影像 | A panstereorama is a three-dimensional image or display that provides a panoramic view, offering a wider field of vision than a typical stereoscopic image.  It combines panoramic photography with stereoscopic techniques to create a more immersive and realistic experience. | Example: The museum showcased a stunning panstereorama of the Grand Canyon, allowing visitors to feel as if they were standing at the edge. | 近义词: panoramic view, 3D image, immersive experience | 反义词: flat image, two-dimensional image | 名词
