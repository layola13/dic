czars:/ˈzɑːrz/| n.|沙皇，独裁者，绝对统治者|A czar is a ruler with absolute power, especially the emperor of Russia before 1917.  The term is often used figuratively to refer to someone who wields significant authority or control in a particular field.|例句：The CEO is considered a czar in the company.  这位CEO被认为是公司里的独裁者。|例句：He acted like a czar, ignoring everyone's opinions. 他像个沙皇一样，无视所有人的意见。|近义词：autocrat, dictator, tyrant, potentate|反义词：democrat, egalitarian
