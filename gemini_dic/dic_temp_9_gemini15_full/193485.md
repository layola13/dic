selection:/səˈlɛkʃən/ | n. | 选择；选拔；精选；选集 | The act of selecting or the state of being selected; a person or thing that has been selected.  |例句：The selection process was rigorous and fair.  选择过程严格而公平。

例句：This anthology contains a fine selection of poems. 这本选集包含许多精选的诗歌。


近义词：choice, pick, option, excerpt | 反义词：rejection


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee makes its selection today.  委员会今天做出选择。
She selects her clothes carefully. 她仔细挑选衣服。

一般过去时:
They selected a new president last week.  他们上周选出了一位新总统。
I selected the red dress from the rack. 我从衣架上选了那件红裙子。

现在完成时:
He has selected a new car. 他已经选了一辆新车。
She has carefully selected her words. 她已经仔细斟酌了自己的措辞。

过去完成时:
They had already selected the winner before the announcement. 他们在宣布结果之前就已经选定了获胜者。
We had selected our team members by the end of the week. 我们在本周末之前已经选定了队员。


将来时:
They will select a new CEO next month.  他们下个月将选出一位新首席执行官。
She will select the winning entry next week. 她下周将选出获奖作品。

虚拟语气:
If I had selected a different path, my life would be different. 如果我选择了不同的道路，我的生活就会不同。
I wish I had selected a better gift for her. 我希望我为她挑选了一份更好的礼物。

被动语态:
The winner was selected by a panel of judges. 获胜者由评审团选出。
The candidates will be selected based on their qualifications. 候选人将根据他们的资格进行筛选。

疑问句:
Which candidate did you select? 你选择了哪个候选人？
What selection criteria are being used?  使用了哪些选择标准？

否定句:
She did not select the blue dress. 她没有选择蓝色的裙子。
They didn't select anyone for the position. 他们没有为这个职位选任何人。


PHRASE->
natural selection 自然选择

a wide selection of  各种各样的选择
