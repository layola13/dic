grilled:/ɡrɪld/ | adj. v. | 烤的；烧烤的；严厉盘问的 |  Cooked over a fire or on a grill.  To question someone severely. |

例句：
The grilled chicken was delicious. = 烤鸡很好吃。

We had grilled salmon for dinner. = 我们晚餐吃了烤三文鱼。


The police grilled the suspect for hours. = 警方严厉盘问了嫌疑犯数小时。

The witness was grilled about his alibi. = 证人被严厉盘问了他的不在场证明。


近义词：broiled, barbecued (for cooking); interrogated, questioned (for questioning)

反义词：boiled, raw (for cooking); ignored, overlooked (for questioning)

词性：形容词，动词


词源：源自古英语 *grill-,  指“格子”，后演变为指“烤架”，再引申出烤制食物以及严厉盘问的意思。

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grills the steaks every Sunday. = 他每周日都会烤牛排。
She grills vegetables for a healthier option. = 她烤蔬菜作为更健康的选择。

一般过去时:
They grilled the fish until it was cooked through. = 他们把鱼烤到熟透。
We grilled burgers at the park yesterday. = 昨天我们在公园里烤汉堡。

现在完成时:
I have grilled many different types of meat. = 我烤过许多不同种类的肉。
She has grilled chicken breasts for lunch today. = 她今天午餐吃了烤鸡胸肉。

过去完成时:
They had grilled the corn before the party started. = 派对开始前他们已经烤好了玉米。
He had grilled the peppers, preparing them for the stew. = 他已经烤好了辣椒，为炖菜做准备。

将来时:
We will grill some sausages for the picnic. = 我们将为野餐烤一些香肠。
They will grill the pizza on the outdoor oven. = 他们将在户外烤箱里烤披萨。

虚拟语气:
If I had a grill, I would grill some vegetables tonight. = 如果我有烤架，我今晚会烤一些蔬菜。
I wish I had grilled the fish longer; it was a bit undercooked. = 我希望我把鱼烤久一点；它有点没烤熟。

被动语态:
The steaks were grilled to perfection. = 牛排烤得完美无缺。
The vegetables are being grilled slowly over low heat. = 蔬菜正在文火慢烤。

疑问句:
Are you grilling the burgers now? = 你现在在烤汉堡吗？
Is the meat grilled enough? = 肉烤够了吗？

否定句:
We didn't grill the chicken; we baked it. = 我们没有烤鸡；我们是烤的。
She doesn't usually grill food; she prefers to bake. = 她通常不烤食物；她更喜欢烘焙。
