reconnaissance:/rɪˈkɒnɪs(ə)ns/| n.|侦察，勘察|The act of obtaining information about an enemy or a territory by sending out small groups of soldiers, or using aircraft, etc.|例句：The patrol went out on reconnaissance.  侦察队出发去侦察。

例句：The army conducted aerial reconnaissance before the attack.  军队在进攻前进行了空中侦察。


近义词：exploration, survey, scouting, investigation|反义词：None specifically.  (反义词需要根据语境而定，例如，如果reconnaissance指的是敌方侦察，那么“反义词”可能是“防御”或“反侦察”)|名词
