isolator:/ˈaɪsəleɪtər/| n.|隔离器；绝缘体|A device or material used to isolate something, typically electrical circuits or physical objects.  It prevents the flow of electricity or the transfer of heat or sound.|例句：The electrician installed a new isolator to protect the equipment from power surges. 电工安装了一个新的隔离器来保护设备免受电源浪涌的冲击。

例句：The scientist used a thermal isolator to maintain a constant temperature in the experiment. 科学家使用隔热器在实验中保持恒定的温度。


近义词：insulator, separator, barrier |反义词：connector, conductor


词源:  From isolate + -or.  "Isolate" comes from the Latin *insulare ("to make into an island"), from insula ("island").  The suffix "-or" indicates an agent or instrument.
