justly:/ˈdʒʌstli/ | adv. | 正义地；公正地；应受地 | In a just manner; fairly.  The punishment was justly deserved. | The judge ruled justly in the case.  法官公正地裁决了此案。 | fairly, equitably, rightly | unjustly, unfairly, wrongly | 副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He justly criticizes the government's policies. 他公正地批评政府的政策。
She justly deserves the award for her hard work. 她凭借辛勤工作理所当然地获得该奖项。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
