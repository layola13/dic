heterogeneous:/ˌhɛtərəˈdʒiːniəs/|/ˌhɛtəroʊˈdʒiːniəs/| adj. | 异质的；多种多样的；不均匀的 | Consisting of dissimilar or diverse elements; not uniform; varied.  | The class is heterogeneous, with students from diverse backgrounds and academic levels.  这个班级是异质的，学生来自不同的背景和学术水平。| homogeneous, uniform | homogeneous, uniform |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sample is heterogeneous in composition. = 样品的成分是异质的。
This population is heterogeneous in terms of age and income. = 就年龄和收入而言，这部分人口是多种多样的。

一般过去时:
The mixture was heterogeneous before it was stirred. = 混合物在搅拌前是不均匀的。
The results of the study were heterogeneous across different groups. = 研究结果在不同群体中差异很大。


现在完成时:
Scientists have found that the soil composition has become more heterogeneous. = 科学家们发现土壤成分变得更加不均匀了。
The effects of the policy have been heterogeneous across regions. = 该政策的影响在不同地区差异很大。


过去完成时:
The data had been heterogeneous, making analysis challenging. = 数据本来就多种多样，使得分析具有挑战性。
The population had become more heterogeneous due to immigration. = 由于移民，人口变得更加多元化了。


将来时:
The team anticipates that the data will be heterogeneous. = 团队预计数据将是多种多样的。
The product will likely appeal to a heterogeneous market. = 该产品可能会吸引异质市场。


虚拟语气:
If the sample were homogeneous, the experiment would be simpler. = 如果样品是均匀的，实验就会简单得多。
I wish the data were less heterogeneous. = 我希望数据不那么多种多样。


被动语态:
The samples were considered heterogeneous by the researchers. = 样本被研究人员认为是异质的。
The group was found to be heterogeneous in its opinions. = 发现该群体在意见上存在差异。


疑问句:
Is the population of this city heterogeneous? = 这个城市的居民构成是多种多样的吗？
Are the results of the experiment heterogeneous? = 实验的结果是多种多样的吗？


否定句:
The mixture is not heterogeneous; it's homogeneous. = 混合物不是异质的；它是均匀的。
The sample was not heterogeneous enough for the study. = 样本对于研究来说不够异质。
