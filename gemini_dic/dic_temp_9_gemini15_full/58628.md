dumb:/dʌm/| adj.|哑的；愚蠢的；令人震惊的| lacking the power of speech; stupid; astonishingly bad or unsuccessful. |例句：The baby was born dumb.  这个婴儿生下来就是哑巴的。|例句：That's a dumb idea.  那是个愚蠢的主意。|近义词：mute, stupid, foolish, silly,  unintelligent|反义词：intelligent, smart, clever, articulate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is dumb. = 他哑了。
She is dumb about this matter. = 她对这件事很迟钝。

一般过去时:
He was dumb with surprise. = 他惊呆了说不出话来。
They were dumb about the plan. = 他们对这个计划一无所知。

现在完成时:
He has been dumb since the accident. = 他自从事故后就哑了。
She has been dumbstruck by the news. = 她听到这个消息后惊呆了。

过去完成时:
He had been dumb for many years. = 他哑了很多年了。
She had been dumb about the secret. = 她对这个秘密一直守口如瓶。

将来时:
He will be dumb if he keeps eating that. = 如果他继续吃那个，他会哑的。
They will be dumb with shock. = 他们会被吓傻的。

虚拟语气:
If I were dumb, I couldn't communicate. = 如果我哑了，我就无法交流。
I wish I hadn't been so dumb. = 我真希望我当时不要那么傻。

被动语态:
He was made dumb by the disease. = 他被疾病弄哑了。
The plan was deemed dumb by the board. = 这个计划被董事会认为很愚蠢。

疑问句:
Is he dumb? = 他哑巴吗？
Are you dumb or something? = 你是傻了吗？

否定句:
He is not dumb; he's just shy. = 他不是哑巴，只是害羞。
I'm not dumb enough to fall for that. = 我没那么傻会相信那个。
