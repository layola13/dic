cow: /kaʊ/ | n. | 母牛，奶牛 | A fully grown female bovine animal kept for its milk or meat.  | Example: The farmer milked his cows every morning.  这个农夫每天早上都挤牛奶。 |近义词：bullock (年轻的公牛), heifer (未生育的母牛), bovine | 反义词: None (no direct antonym)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cow grazes in the pasture. = 这头牛在牧场上吃草。
Cows produce milk. = 奶牛产奶。


一般过去时:
The cow gave birth to a calf. = 母牛产下一头小牛。
The farmer sold his cows last year. = 农夫去年卖掉了他的牛。


现在完成时:
The farmer has milked the cows. = 农夫已经挤过牛奶了。
We have never owned a cow. = 我们从未养过牛。


过去完成时:
The calf had already been born when I arrived. = 我到达时小牛已经出生了。
They had not fed the cows before leaving. = 他们离开前没有喂牛。


将来时:
We will buy a cow next spring. = 我们明年春天会买一头牛。
The farmer will sell his cows at the auction. = 农夫会在拍卖会上卖掉他的牛。


虚拟语气:
If I had a cow, I would make cheese. = 如果我有一头牛，我会做奶酪。
I wish I had a cow to provide milk. = 我希望有一头牛可以提供牛奶。


被动语态:
The milk is produced by cows. = 牛奶是由奶牛生产的。
The cow was milked this morning. = 这头牛今天早上被挤奶了。


疑问句:
Is that your cow? = 那是你的牛吗？
How many cows do you own? = 你养了多少头牛？


否定句:
That is not my cow. = 那不是我的牛。
We don't have any cows. = 我们没有牛。
