It's often associated with the Roman Emperor Julius Caesar and the subsequent emperors who followed his model. |The rise of Caesarism in ancient Rome led to the end of the Republic. 恺撒主义在古罗马的兴起导致了共和国的终结。|autocracy, dictatorship, authoritarianism |democracy, liberalism |名词
