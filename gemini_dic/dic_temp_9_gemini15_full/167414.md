prevaccinating: /ˌpriːˈvæksɪneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先接种疫苗 | The act of vaccinating beforehand, typically to provide immunity before exposure to a disease.  |  例句：Prevaccinating the troops against yellow fever was crucial for their mission in the jungle. 预先为部队接种黄热病疫苗对他们在丛林中的任务至关重要。 |近义词：pre-immunizing |反义词：post-vaccinating
