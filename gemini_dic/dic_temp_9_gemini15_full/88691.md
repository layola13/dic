hobbes:/ˈhɒbɪz/| n.|霍布斯；（尤指）嗜好；|Hobbes refers primarily to Thomas Hobbes (1588-1679), a prominent English philosopher known for his political philosophy, particularly his book Leviathan.  It can also refer to a person's hobby or pastime. |例句：We studied Hobbes's political philosophy in class. 我们在课堂上学习了霍布斯的政治哲学。|例句：My hobby is collecting stamps. 我的爱好是集邮。|近义词：pastime, pursuit, avocation|反义词：N/A
