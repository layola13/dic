pinkest: /ˈpɪŋkɪst/ | adj. | 最粉红色的，最桃红色的 |  The superlative form of "pink," indicating the highest degree of pinkness. It describes something that is the pinkest among a group or is exceptionally pink. | 例句：Her cheeks were the pinkest I'd ever seen.  = 她的脸颊是我见过的最粉红色的。  Her dress is the pinkest shade imaginable. = 她的裙子是你可以想象到的最粉红色的色调。| 近义词：most rosy, most blush, deepest rose | 反义词：palest, whitest, most colorless |
