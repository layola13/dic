silkman: /ˈsɪlkmæn/ | n. | 丝绸商人；（尤指）卖丝绸的中国商人 | A silkman is a person who deals in silk, especially historically a Chinese merchant who traded in silk.  The term often carries a connotation of a wealthy and influential merchant involved in the lucrative silk trade. |  He was a wealthy silkman from Hangzhou, known for his exquisite silks. 他是来自杭州的富有的丝绸商人，以其精美的丝绸而闻名。  Many silkmen made their fortunes along the Silk Road. 许多丝绸商人都在丝绸之路上发了财。 | merchant, trader |  cotton farmer, wool merchant  | 名词
