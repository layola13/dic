overquantity: /ˌoʊvərˈkwɒntɪti/ | n. |过量，超量|Excessive quantity; more than is needed or desired.  It refers to an amount that surpasses the required or expected level.  The term often implies a negative consequence due to the excess.|例句：The overquantity of rain caused flooding in the streets.  = 雨水过多导致街道洪水泛滥。  The overquantity of sugar in his diet led to health problems. = 他饮食中糖分过多导致健康问题。|近义词：excess, surplus, superfluity|反义词：shortage, deficiency, scarcity|无
