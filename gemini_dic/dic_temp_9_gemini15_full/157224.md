pinko:/ˈpɪŋkoʊ/| n. adj.| 左倾分子；浅粉色；|英文解析：A derogatory term for someone who is considered to be a leftist or communist sympathizer.  The term originally referred to someone who held left-wing views but was not fully committed to communism. Now, it is primarily used as a pejorative. |例句：He was labeled a pinko by his conservative colleagues. 他被他的保守派同事贴上了左倾分子的标签。

He painted the wall a delicate pinko. 他把墙漆成了淡淡的粉色。|近义词：leftist, communist sympathizer, radical, red (derogatory) |反义词：conservative, rightist, reactionary
