incommunicado:/ˌɪnˌkəˈmjuːnɪkeɪdoʊ/| adj.|与外界隔绝的；无法联系的 | kept away from communication with other people; isolated | The prisoner was held incommunicado for days.  囚犯被单独监禁了好几天。|近义词：isolated, secluded, confined |反义词：accessible, communicative

一般现在时:
He is kept incommunicado. 他被与世隔绝。
The suspect remains incommunicado. 嫌疑人仍然与外界隔绝。


一般过去时:
They held him incommunicado for several weeks.  他们将他与世隔绝数周。

过去完成时:
