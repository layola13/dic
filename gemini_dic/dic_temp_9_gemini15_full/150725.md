paracetamol: /ˌpærəˈsɪtəmɒl/ /ˌpærəˈsiːtəmɔːl/ | n. |扑热息痛，对乙酰氨基酚 | Paracetamol is a common over-the-counter pain reliever and fever reducer.  It works by reducing the production of prostaglandins, chemicals that cause pain and inflammation.  | Example:  I took some paracetamol for my headache. 我服用了些扑热息痛来缓解头痛。  The doctor prescribed paracetamol for the child's fever.  医生给孩子开了扑热息痛来退烧。|近义词：acetaminophen (US), acetaminphen |反义词：无 |名词
