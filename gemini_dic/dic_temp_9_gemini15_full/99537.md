/ˌɪntərˈɡɑːsɪpɪŋ/ | v. |互相八卦；相互闲聊 | The act of gossiping with or among others.  It implies a reciprocal exchange of gossip, not just one person gossiping to others. | Example:  The coworkers were intergossiping about the CEO's new hairstyle.  同事们互相八卦着CEO的新发型。|近义词：mutually gossiping, exchanging gossip | 反义词：keeping secrets, remaining silent
