overshine:/ˌoʊvərˈʃaɪn/ | vt. |  使...显得暗淡；胜过；盖过 | To surpass or excel to such a degree that others seem insignificant or inferior.  |  Her talent overshadowed everyone else's.  她的才能使其他人的显得黯然失色。| surpass, eclipse, outshine | be overshadowed, be eclipsed | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun overshines the moon tonight. = 今晚太阳的光芒盖过了月亮。
His performance overshines all others in the competition. = 他在比赛中的表现胜过了所有其他人。


一般过去时:
Her beauty overshined all the other girls at the party. = 在派对上，她的美貌使其他女孩都黯然失色。
His success overshined his previous failures. = 他的成功掩盖了他之前的失败。


现在完成时:
Her achievements have overshined her early struggles. = 她的成就已经掩盖了她早期的奋斗。
The new technology has overshined the old methods. = 新技术已经超越了旧方法。


过去完成时:
His confidence had overshined his initial nervousness. = 他的自信已经克服了他最初的紧张。
The evidence had overshined any doubts about his guilt. = 证据已经消除了对他有罪的任何怀疑。


将来时:
The new building will overshine the old one in terms of design. = 新建筑在设计方面将胜过旧建筑。
His future success will overshine his current difficulties. = 他未来的成功将掩盖他目前的困境。


虚拟语气:
If only my talent could overshine my shortcomings. = 如果我的才能能够掩盖我的缺点就好了。
I wish my work could overshine theirs. = 我希望我的作品能够胜过他们的作品。


被动语态:

The small details were overshined by the overall grandeur of the project. = 项目的整体宏伟掩盖了那些小的细节。


疑问句:

否定句:
His efforts did not overshine the problems faced by the company. = 他的努力并没有掩盖公司面临的问题。
The supporting characters did not overshine the protagonist. = 配角并没有掩盖主角的光彩。
