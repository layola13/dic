imagination: /ɪˌmædʒɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |想象力，想象，幻想 |The ability of the mind to form new ideas, or to create new things.  It involves the ability to form images or concepts of external objects, not present to the senses. |例句：Her imagination ran wild as she listened to the spooky story.  她听着那个恐怖故事，想象力奔腾起来。

例句：The architect's imagination is evident in the building's unique design. 建筑师的想象力在建筑物的独特设计中显而易见。


近义词：fantasy, creativity, invention, vision |反义词：reality, fact


词源:  From Old French *imaginacioun, from Latin imaginatio, from imaginari "to imagine," from imago "image, likeness."
