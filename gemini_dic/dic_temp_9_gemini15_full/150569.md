papaver:/pəˈpeɪvər/| n. |罂粟属植物|Papaver is a genus of flowering plants in the family Papaveraceae, including the opium poppy and many ornamental varieties.  The genus is characterized by its showy flowers and often prickly seed pods.  Many species are cultivated for their ornamental value, while others have important medicinal or historical significance. |The field was ablaze with bright red papavers.|近义词：poppy (罂粟花，更常用)|反义词：无
