grandaunt:/ˈɡrændænt/| n. |曾祖母；曾外祖母|A grandaunt is a parent's aunt or uncle's wife/husband.  It refers to your aunt's or uncle's mother or your great-aunt.  The term can refer to both maternal and paternal sides of the family.|例句：My grandaunt Mary told me many stories about her childhood.  我的曾祖母玛丽给我讲了许多关于她童年的故事。

She inherited a valuable painting from her grandaunt. 她从她的曾祖母那里继承了一幅珍贵的画。|近义词：great-aunt|反义词：grandchild
