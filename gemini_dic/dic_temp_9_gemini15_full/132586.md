nondigestibility:/ˌnɒnˌdaɪdʒestəˈbɪləti/ | n. |难消化性，不能消化 | The inability of a substance to be digested or broken down by the body.  This often refers to food but can also apply to other materials. | Example: The high fiber content contributes to the nondigestibility of the food.  高纤维含量导致食物难以消化。  The nondigestibility of plastic is a major environmental concern. 塑料的不可降解性是一个主要的环保问题。 |近义词：indigestibility, undigestibility |反义词：digestibility
