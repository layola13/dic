monocotyledons:/ˌmɒnəʊkɒtɪˈliːdənz/| n. |单子叶植物 |Monocotyledons are flowering plants that possess only one cotyledon (embryonic leaf) in their seeds.  They are characterized by their parallel leaf venation, flower parts in multiples of three, and fibrous root systems. Examples include grasses, lilies, and orchids. |The fields were covered in monocotyledons, swaying gently in the breeze.  这片田野里长满了单子叶植物，在微风中轻轻摇曳。|dicotyledons |dicotyledons
