mullets:/ˈmʌlɪts/| n. |鲻鱼发型，男士短发|A mullet is a hairstyle in which the hair on the top and sides of the head is short, while the hair at the back is much longer.  It's often considered a retro or unconventional style.|Example: He sported a classic mullet, short on top and long in the back. 他留着经典的鲻鱼头，上面短，后面长。|近义词：business in the front, party in the back (informal)|反义词：None specifically, but styles with uniformly short hair could be considered opposites.|名词
