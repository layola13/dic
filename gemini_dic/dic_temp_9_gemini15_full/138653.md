nudged:/nʌdʒd/| v.|轻推，鼓励，暗示|To gently push or poke someone or something.|The mother gently nudged her child towards the swing set. 妈妈轻轻地把孩子推向秋千架。|He nudged me with his elbow to get my attention. 他用肘部轻轻推了我一下以引起我的注意。|push, prod, jostle|ignore, neglect|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He nudges the box across the floor. 他轻轻地把箱子推过地板。
She nudges her friend to remind her of the meeting. 她轻轻碰了碰她的朋友，提醒她开会。


一般过去时:
I nudged him to wake him up. 我轻轻推了他一下，把他叫醒。
She nudged the door open. 她轻轻地推开了门。


现在完成时:
He has nudged the project forward. 他已经推动了项目进展。
She has nudged me to apply for the job. 她一直在鼓励我申请这份工作。


过去完成时:
I had nudged the car to get it started. 我之前已经尝试推动汽车启动。
They had nudged the negotiations towards a settlement. 他们之前已经促使谈判达成和解。


将来时:
I will nudge him to finish his work. 我会催促他完成他的工作。
She will nudge her team towards the goal. 她将鼓励她的团队朝着目标前进。


虚拟语气:
If I nudged him, he might get up. 如果我推他一下，他可能会起来。
I wish she had nudged me sooner. 我希望她早点提醒我。


被动语态:
The car was nudged into the garage. 车被轻轻地推进了车库。
The project was nudged along by the new manager. 项目在新的经理推动下进展顺利。


疑问句:
Did he nudge you? 他推了你吗？
Will she nudge him to speak up? 她会鼓励他说出来吗？


否定句:
I didn't nudge him at all. 我根本没有碰他。
She didn't nudge the issue in the meeting. 她没有在会议上提及这个问题。


PHRASE->

nudge someone along  推动某人前进
