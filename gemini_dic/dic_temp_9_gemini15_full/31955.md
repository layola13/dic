chemic:/ˈkɛmɪk/| adj.|化学的；关于化学的|relating to chemistry or the chemical properties of matter|The chemic reaction produced a surprising result.  化学反应产生了令人惊讶的结果。|chemical,  chymical|non-chemic|形容词


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective, so tense doesn't apply in the same way as with verbs.  We use it to describe nouns.  Examples below show usage in different sentence structures.


Declarative Sentences:
The chemic properties of water are unique. 水的化学特性是独一无二的。
He studied chemic engineering in college. 他在大学里学习化学工程。


Interrogative Sentences:
Is this a chemic reaction? 这是一个化学反应吗？
Are there any chemic hazards involved?  这其中是否有任何化学危害？


Exclamatory Sentences:
What a chemic marvel this discovery is!  这个发现真是个化学奇迹！
How complex the chemic processes are! 化学过程是多么复杂啊！


Negative Sentences:
The substance is not chemic in nature.  这种物质本质上不是化学物质。
There are no chemic additives in this product.  此产品中不含任何化学添加剂。
