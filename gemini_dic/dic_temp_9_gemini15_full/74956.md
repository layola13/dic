friarly:/ˈfraɪərli/| adv.|像修士般地；质朴地；简朴地|In a manner resembling a friar; simply; plainly.|例句：He lived friarly, eschewing worldly possessions. 他过着简朴的生活，避免世俗的财产。例句：She dressed friarly, in simple brown robes. 她穿着朴素的棕色长袍。|近义词：simply, plainly, austerely|反义词：luxuriously, lavishly
