curlews:/ˈkɜːljuːz/| n. |卷羽鹈鹕（复数）|A curlew is a large wading bird with a long, down-curved bill.  They are known for their distinctive calls. |例句：We saw a flock of curlews feeding in the shallows. 我们看到一群卷羽鹈鹕在浅滩觅食。|例句：The curlews' mournful cries echoed across the marsh. 卷羽鹈鹕悲伤的叫声在沼泽地回荡。|近义词：sandpiper, snipe|反义词:|
