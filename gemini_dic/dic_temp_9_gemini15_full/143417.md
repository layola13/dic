outflashed:/ˌaʊtˈflæʃt/| v. |超过（光亮）；比…更耀眼|To surpass in brightness or brilliance.  This is a past tense form of the verb "outflash." It describes a situation where something shone or flashed more brightly than something else.|The diamond outflashed all the other jewels in the crown.  这颗钻石的光芒超过了皇冠上的其他所有珠宝。|outshine, overshadow, eclipse|dim, obscure, dull|动词

This diamond outflashes all the others. = 这颗钻石比其他所有钻石都更闪耀。
