planking:/ˈplæŋkɪŋ/| n.|平板支撑，平板支撑运动|The act of holding a rigid, plank-like pose, typically for a period of time as a fitness exercise.  Planking engages multiple muscle groups and is considered a good core strengthening exercise.|例句：He improved his core strength through daily planking. 他通过每天平板支撑来增强核心力量。|近义词：core workout, abdominal exercise|反义词:relaxation, rest
