dallier:/ˈdæliər/| v.| 嬉戏；玩弄；磨蹭；拖延 | To waste time; to trifle; to spend time idly and pleasantly; to flirt. |例句：He dallied with the idea of quitting his job. 他玩弄着辞职的想法。

例句：She dallied over her coffee, enjoying the quiet morning. 她慢慢地喝着咖啡，享受宁静的早晨。

近义词：linger, trifle, flirt, procrastinate, toy

反义词：hurry, rush, expedite
