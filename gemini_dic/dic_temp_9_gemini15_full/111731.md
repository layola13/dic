locule:/ˈlɒkjuːl/| n. |小室；小格；（植物）子房室|A locule is a small cavity or compartment, especially in a plant ovary or fruit, containing one or more ovules or seeds.  It refers to a small chamber or hollow space within a larger structure.|Example: The orange fruit has many locules, each containing several seeds.  橘子果实有很多小室，每个小室都包含几粒种子。 The ovary of this flower has three locules.  这朵花的子房有三个子房室。|近义词：compartment, cell, chamber |反义词：None specifically, but could be considered the opposite of a large, undivided space.
