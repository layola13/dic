phototherapy:/ˌfoʊtoʊˈθɛrəpi/ | n. | 光疗法 | Treatment of a disease or disorder using light.  It is often used to treat seasonal affective disorder (SAD) and some skin conditions. | Example:  The doctor recommended phototherapy to alleviate her symptoms of SAD. 医生建议她进行光疗以缓解SAD症状。|近义词：light therapy, heliotherapy |反义词：无
