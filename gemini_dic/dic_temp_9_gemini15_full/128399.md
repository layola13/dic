newborn:/ˈnuːbɔːrn/ | n. | 新生儿，初生的 | A newborn baby is a baby that has recently been born.  | Example: The newborn baby slept soundly in its crib. 新生儿在摇篮里睡得很香。 The hospital welcomed several newborns last night. 昨晚医院迎来了几个新生儿。|近义词：infant, baby, neonate |反义词：adult, grown-up


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The newborn cries frequently. = 新生儿经常哭闹。
The newborn sleeps most of the day. = 新生儿大部分时间都在睡觉。


一般过去时:
The newborn cried all night. = 新生儿整夜都在哭。
The newborn slept peacefully after feeding. = 新生儿喂奶后睡得很安稳。


现在完成时:
The mother has cared for the newborn since birth. = 母亲从出生起就一直照顾新生儿。
The newborn has gained weight steadily. = 新生儿体重稳步增加。
