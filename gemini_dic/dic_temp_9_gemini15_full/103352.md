jilts:/dʒɪlts/| vt.|抛弃；甩掉；拒绝；欺骗|To suddenly end a romantic relationship with (someone).  To disappoint or fail (someone) by not fulfilling an expectation.|例句：He jilted her at the altar. 他在婚礼祭坛上抛弃了她。

She jilted him for a younger man. 她抛弃了他，和一个更年轻的男人在一起了。


近义词：abandon, desert, forsake, dump, leave


反义词：support, cherish, remain faithful to


词性：动词
