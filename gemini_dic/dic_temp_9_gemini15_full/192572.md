sculpting:/ˈskʌlptɪŋ/| n.|雕塑；雕刻术|The art or process of shaping figures or objects out of a solid material such as clay, stone, or metal.  It involves the removal of material to create the desired form.|例句：He is a master of sculpting in marble. 他是位精通大理石雕塑的大师。|例句：The museum houses a vast collection of ancient Roman sculpting.  博物馆收藏了大量古罗马雕塑作品。|近义词：carving, molding, modelling|反义词：none
