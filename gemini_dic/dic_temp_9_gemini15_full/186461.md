ricepaper:/ˈraɪspeɪpər/ | n. |米纸；宣纸|A thin, translucent paper made from the pith of certain plants, especially the mulberry tree, used in Asian cooking and art.  It's often used as a wrapper for food or as a painting surface. |The spring rolls were wrapped in delicate rice paper. 春卷是用精致的米纸包裹的。|  She painted a beautiful landscape on rice paper. 她在一张米纸上画了一幅美丽的风景画。| tissue paper, parchment |  (No direct antonym)|名词
