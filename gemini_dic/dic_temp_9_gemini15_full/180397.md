refuters:/rɪˈfjuːtərz/ | n. |驳斥者，反驳者 |Those who refute or deny something.  People who offer arguments or evidence against a claim or theory. |例句：The scientists presented compelling evidence to silence the refuters of their climate change findings. 科学家们提出了令人信服的证据，以驳斥他们气候变化研究结果的反对者。 |例句：Despite the overwhelming evidence, some refuters continued to deny the link between smoking and lung cancer. 尽管证据确凿，一些反对者仍然否认吸烟与肺癌之间的联系。|近义词：contradictors, opponents, deniers |反义词：supporters, proponents, believers |名词
