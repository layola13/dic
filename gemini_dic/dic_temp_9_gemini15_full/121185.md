misdivided: /ˌmɪs.dɪˈvaɪ.dɪd/ | adj. | 分配错误的；划分错误的 | Incorrectly divided or distributed.  The inheritance was misdivided among the heirs. | The land was misdivided, leading to boundary disputes. |  近义词：improperly divided, wrongly apportioned, unfairly distributed | 反义词：equitably divided, fairly distributed, properly allocated |
