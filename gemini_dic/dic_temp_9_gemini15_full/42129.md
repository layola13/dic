cottonmouth:/ˈkɑːtnˌmaʊθ/| n. |棉嘴蛇；（尤指）水栖棉嘴蛇 |A cottonmouth is a venomous water snake, especially the species *Agkistrodon piscivorus*.  They are known for their dark, often black, mouths.  |Example:  The hiker saw a cottonmouth slithering near the river.  徒步旅行者看到一条棉嘴蛇在河边蜿蜒而行。
The biologist studied the venom of the cottonmouth. 生物学家研究了棉嘴蛇的毒液。|近义词：water moccasin (in some regions)|反义词：无|名词
