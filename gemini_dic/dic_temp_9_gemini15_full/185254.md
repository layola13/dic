retransferred:/ˌriːtrænˈsfɜːrd/| v. |再转让；再转移|To transfer something again or to a new person or place.  It implies a previous transfer has already occurred.|例句：The rights to the property were retransferred to the original owner.  房产权已重新转让给原业主。

例句：The student retransferred to a different university after a semester.  这名学生在一个学期后转学到了另一所大学。


近义词：relocate, transfer again, reassign|反义词：retain, keep, receive|动词
