semimountainous: /ˌsemiˈmaʊntənəs/ | adj. | 半山区的，丘陵的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a region that is partly mountainous, having a mixture of mountains and plains or valleys.  It suggests a landscape with rolling hills and some mountains, but not entirely mountainous. | 例句：The region is semimountainous, with gentle slopes interspersed with steeper, more rugged terrain. 这个地区是半山区的，平缓的斜坡与更陡峭、更崎岖的地形交错分布。  The semimountainous landscape provided stunning views and challenging hiking trails. 半山区景观提供了令人惊叹的景色和具有挑战性的徒步旅行路线。| 近义词：hilly, undulating, hilly and mountainous | 反义词：flat, plain, level | 词性:形容词
