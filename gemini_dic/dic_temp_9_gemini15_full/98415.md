insouciance:/ɪnˈsuːsiɑːns/| n. |无忧无虑；漠不关心；漫不经心 |A feeling or attitude of not worrying about anything; lighthearted unconcern.|例句：Her insouciance in the face of danger was remarkable. 她面对危险时表现出的无忧无虑令人惊叹。|例句：He treated the whole matter with a shrug of insouciance. 他对整件事都满不在乎地耸了耸肩。|近义词：carefreeness, nonchalance, unconcern |反义词：anxiety, worry, concern |名词
