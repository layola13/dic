counter-intuitive:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈtuːɪtɪv/| adj. |违反直觉的，与直觉相悖的 | Contrary to intuition or common sense; seemingly impossible or unlikely.  It describes something that seems illogical or unexpected based on one's initial understanding. |The experiment yielded counter-intuitive results.  实验结果违反直觉。|近义词： paradoxical, unexpected, illogical |反义词：intuitive, expected, logical


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The results of the study are counter-intuitive. = 这项研究的结果违反直觉。
This strategy seems counter-intuitive, but it works. = 这个策略看起来违反直觉，但它确实有效。

一般过去时:
His decision was counter-intuitive. = 他的决定违反直觉。
The findings were counter-intuitive to what we expected. = 这些发现与我们的预期相反，违反直觉。

现在完成时:
Many people have found this concept counter-intuitive. = 许多人发现这个概念违反直觉。
The new policy has proven to be counter-intuitive in practice. = 新政策在实践中证明是违反直觉的。

过去完成时:
We had initially thought the approach would be counter-intuitive, but we were wrong. = 我们最初认为这种方法会违反直觉，但我们错了。
The scientists had not anticipated such counter-intuitive results from the experiment. = 科学家们没有预料到实验会有如此违反直觉的结果。

将来时:
The effects of the medication will likely be counter-intuitive at first. = 这种药物的效果一开始可能会违反直觉。
Future research will explore why this phenomenon is counter-intuitive. = 未来的研究将探讨为什么这种现象违反直觉。


虚拟语气:
If the solution were more intuitive, it would be easier to understand. = 如果解决方案更直观，就更容易理解。（反向说明counter-intuitive）
It would be counter-intuitive to assume that higher prices mean better quality. = 假设价格越高质量越好，这是违反直觉的。


被动语态:
The complexity of the system is often considered counter-intuitive. = 系统的复杂性往往被认为是违反直觉的。
This effect is sometimes described as being counter-intuitive. = 这种效应有时被描述为违反直觉的。


疑问句:
Isn't it counter-intuitive that less sleep can sometimes improve performance? = 睡得少有时反而能提高效率，这难道不违反直觉吗？
Are the findings truly counter-intuitive, or is our understanding simply incomplete? = 这些发现真的是违反直觉的吗？还是我们的理解还不够完善？


否定句:
The instructions were not counter-intuitive; they were quite clear. = 说明书并不违反直觉；它们非常清晰。
The process is not counter-intuitive once you understand the underlying principles. = 一旦你理解了基本原理，这个过程就不会违反直觉了。
