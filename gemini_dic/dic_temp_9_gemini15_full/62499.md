endothelium:/ˌɛn.dəʊˈθiː.li.əm/ | n. |内皮|A single continuous layer of flattened cells (endothelial cells) lining the inner surface of blood vessels and lymphatic vessels, forming a semipermeable barrier between the blood and the surrounding tissue. It plays a crucial role in regulating blood flow, blood clotting, and immune responses.|Example: The endothelium plays a vital role in maintaining vascular homeostasis.  内皮在维持血管稳态中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：endothelial lining|反义词：无
