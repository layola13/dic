october:/ɒkˈtoʊbər/ /ɑːkˈtoʊbər/| n. |十月|October is the tenth month of the year in the Gregorian calendar.  It has 31 days.  The name derives from the Latin word "octo," meaning "eight," reflecting its original position as the eighth month in the Roman calendar.|例句：My birthday is in October. 我的生日在十月。

例句：October is a beautiful month in the fall. 秋天的十月是一个美丽的月份。
