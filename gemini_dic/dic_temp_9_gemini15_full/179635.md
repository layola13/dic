reendow:/ˌriːɪnˈdaʊ/| vt.|重新赋予；再次捐赠|To endow again; to provide with new resources or qualities.|The foundation plans to reendow the university's scholarship program.  基金会计划重新捐赠大学的奖学金项目。|近义词：reinstate, replenish, refurnish |反义词：deprive, divest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reendow the museum with new exhibits every year.  他们每年都为博物馆提供新的展览品。
The company reendows its employees with new skills through training programs. 公司通过培训项目为员工提供新的技能。

一般过去时:
The government reendowed the hospital with modern equipment. 政府为医院配备了现代化设备。
She reendowed her life with purpose after a period of reflection.  在一段时间的反思之后，她重新赋予了生活以目标。

现在完成时:

They have reendowed the park with new benches and lighting. 他们已经为公园配备了新的长椅和照明设施。

过去完成时:
