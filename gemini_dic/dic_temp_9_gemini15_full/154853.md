pervasive:/pərˈveɪsɪv/ | adj. | 普遍的；渗透的；弥漫的 |  widespread; spreading to all parts of something; prevalent.  It describes something that is present or spread throughout every part of something. | 例句：The pervasive smell of smoke filled the air.  浓烈的烟味弥漫在空气中。

The pervasive influence of social media is undeniable. 社交媒体的普遍影响是不可否认的。


近义词：ubiquitous, prevalent, widespread, omnipresent | 反义词：localized, restricted, limited, confined


USAGE->

The influence of technology is pervasive in modern society. = 科技的影响在现代社会中是普遍存在的。
This pervasive odor is making me nauseous. = 这股弥漫的气味让我恶心。
