coupon:/ˈkuːpɒn/ /ˈkuːpɑːn/| n. |优惠券，赠券 |A coupon is a ticket or voucher that can be exchanged for a discount or free item.  |例句：I used a coupon to get 20% off my groceries. 我用优惠券在我的杂货上打了八折。

例句：She redeemed a coupon for a free pizza. 她用优惠券兑换了一张免费披萨。


近义词：voucher, discount, ticket |反义词：full price, surcharge
