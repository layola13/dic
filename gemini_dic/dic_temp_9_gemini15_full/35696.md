cob swan:/kɒbˈswɒn/ | n. | 瘤鹄 | A cob swan is a male swan.  Cob is used specifically to refer to the male of the swan species.  The term highlights the sexual dimorphism present in swans, where males and females can be visually distinguished. | Example: The cob swan proudly displayed his elegant plumage.  这只瘤鹄骄傲地展示着它优雅的羽毛。 |近义词: male swan |反义词:pen (female swan)
