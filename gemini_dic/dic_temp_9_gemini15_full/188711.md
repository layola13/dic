runde:/ˈrʊndə/| n.|回合，循环，运行|A runde is a single complete cycle or iteration of a process, often in a game or competition. It can also refer to a period of time or a journey.|例句：The runner completed three runde around the track.  这位跑步者完成了三次绕跑道跑。
The game consists of several runde.  这场游戏包含几个回合。|近义词：round, cycle, turn|反义词：stop, halt
