refunded:/rɪˈfʌndɪd/ | v. |退款的；偿还的 |  Past participle of "refund".  It means that money has been returned, typically because a purchase was unsatisfactory, a service was not provided as agreed, or a payment was made in error.  |例句：The company refunded my money after I returned the faulty product. 公司在我退回有缺陷的产品后，退还了我的钱。
The customer service representative promised to have the amount refunded within 48 hours. 客服代表承诺在48小时内退款。| 近义词：reimbursed, repaid, returned | 反义词：charged, billed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They refund customers for damaged goods. = 他们会为损坏的商品给顾客退款。
The shop refunds the money if you are not satisfied. = 如果你不满意，商店会退钱。


一般过去时:
They refunded my money promptly. = 他们迅速地退还了我的钱。
The store refunded the purchase price. = 商店退还了购买价格。


现在完成时:
I have been refunded the full amount. = 我已经收回了全款。
They have refunded the money to his account. = 他们已经把钱退回到他的账户了。


过去完成时:
They had already refunded the money before I contacted them. = 在我联系他们之前，他们已经退钱了。
The company had refunded several customers that day. = 那天公司已经给好几个顾客退款了。


将来时:
They will refund your money if you return the item. = 如果你退货，他们会退你的钱。
The airline will refund the cost of the cancelled flight. = 航空公司将退还取消航班的费用。


虚拟语气:
If they refunded my money, I would be happy. = 如果他们退我的钱，我会很高兴。
I wish they had refunded my money sooner. = 我希望他们早点把钱退给我。


被动语态:
The money was refunded to my credit card. = 钱退回到我的信用卡里了。
The faulty item was returned, and the customer was refunded. = 有缺陷的物品被退回，顾客也得到了退款。


疑问句:
Were you refunded the shipping cost? = 你是否获得了运费退款？
Has the money been refunded yet? = 钱退了吗？


否定句:
They did not refund my money. = 他们没有退我的钱。
The company refused to refund the purchase. = 公司拒绝退款。
