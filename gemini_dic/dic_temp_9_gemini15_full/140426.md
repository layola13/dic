/oʊˈkɑːz/ | n. |时机；机会；场合 | An occasion is a particular time or instance of an event. It can also refer to a special event or celebration.  |例句：This is a special occasion, so we should celebrate.  这是一个特殊的场合，所以我们应该庆祝一下。
The party was a joyous occasion.  派对是一个快乐的场合。|近义词：event, time, instance |反义词：routine, everyday


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The occasion calls for a formal dress. = 这个场合需要穿正式服装。
This occasion demands careful consideration. = 这个场合需要仔细考虑。
