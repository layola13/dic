digastric:/daɪˈɡæstrɪk/| n. |二腹肌|A digastric muscle is a muscle with two bellies, connected by an intermediate tendon.  It's located in the neck and is involved in swallowing and opening the mouth. | Example: The digastric muscle is crucial for depressing the mandible.  二腹肌对于压低下颌骨至关重要。|近义词:biceps (in the sense of having two bellies)|反义词:None specifically applicable.|名词
