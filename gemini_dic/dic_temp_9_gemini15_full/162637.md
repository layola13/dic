preapprove:/ˌpriːəˈpruːv/| vt.|预先批准，预先核准|To approve something beforehand, before it is officially submitted or implemented.|The bank preapproved her loan application.  银行预先批准了她的贷款申请。|The committee preapproved the budget. 委员会预先批准了预算。|approve, authorize|disapprove, reject|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bank preapproves many loan applications. = 银行预先批准许多贷款申请。
They preapprove all the project proposals. = 他们预先批准所有项目提案。

一般过去时:
The manager preapproved the expense report. = 经理预先批准了费用报告。
She preapproved the design before the meeting. = 她在会议之前预先批准了设计方案。

现在完成时:
The company has preapproved several marketing campaigns. = 公司已经预先批准了几项营销活动。
We have preapproved the travel requests. = 我们已经预先批准了差旅申请。

过去完成时:
They had preapproved the contract before the client signed. = 他们在客户签字之前已经预先批准了合同。
The board had preapproved the investment strategy. = 董事会已预先批准了投资策略。

将来时:
The government will preapprove the infrastructure projects. = 政府将预先批准这些基础设施项目。
We will preapprove your application by next week. = 我们将在下周之前预先批准您的申请。

被动语态:
The application was preapproved by the committee. = 该申请已获得委员会的预先批准。
The budget was preapproved last month. = 预算在上个月预先批准了。

疑问句:
Has the loan been preapproved yet? = 贷款已经预先批准了吗？
Was the proposal preapproved before submission? = 提案在提交之前是否已预先批准？


否定句:
The committee did not preapprove the changes. = 委员会没有预先批准这些更改。
The request was not preapproved. = 该请求未获得预先批准。
