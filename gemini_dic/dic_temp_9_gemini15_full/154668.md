personalizing: /ˈpɜːrsənəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使个性化；定制 |  Making something specific to a particular person or group.  It involves tailoring products, services, or experiences to meet individual preferences and needs. |

例句：
The website allows you to personalize your homepage with your favorite news and weather. = 该网站允许你使用你最喜欢的新闻和天气来个性化你的主页。

The company is personalizing its marketing campaigns to target specific customer segments. = 公司正在个性化其营销活动以针对特定客户群体。


近义词：customizing, tailoring, individualizing, adapting

反义词：generalizing, standardizing


词性：动词
