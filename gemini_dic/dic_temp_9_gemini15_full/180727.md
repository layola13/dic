regroup:/ˌriːˈɡruːp/| vt.  |重新组合；重整旗鼓；重新分组 |To gather again in a new formation or organization. This often implies a previous dispersal or defeat, followed by a reorganization to regain strength or effectiveness. |例句：The soldiers regrouped after the initial attack.  士兵们在最初的攻击后重新集结。

The team regrouped and developed a new strategy. 团队重新集结并制定了新的策略。

|近义词：reorganize, reorganize, rally, reform |反义词：scatter, disperse, disband |动词
