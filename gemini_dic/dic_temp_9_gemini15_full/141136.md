onychia:/ɒˈnɪkiə/| n. |甲沟炎，甲床炎|Inflammation of the nail bed or matrix.  Onychia is a condition affecting the nail bed, the tissue under and surrounding the nail. It can manifest as redness, swelling, pain, and pus.  The cause can vary from bacterial or fungal infections to trauma or allergic reactions.|Example: The doctor diagnosed her with onychia after examining her inflamed fingernail.|近义词：paronychia (if involving the surrounding skin)|反义词：无
