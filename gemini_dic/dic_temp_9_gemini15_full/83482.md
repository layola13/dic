hackathon:/ˈhæθəθɑːn/ | n. |编程马拉松，黑客马拉松 | A hackathon is an event, typically lasting a few days, in which computer programmers and others involved in software development, including graphic designers, interface designers, project managers, and others, collaborate intensively on software projects.  | Example:  We participated in a hackathon over the weekend and built a mobile app.  我们周末参加了一个编程马拉松，开发了一个手机应用程序。 |近义词：coding event, programming competition | 反义词：N/A |名词
