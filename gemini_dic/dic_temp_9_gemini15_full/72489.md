fluores:/ˈflɔːrɛs/| /fluəˈrɛs/ | adj. | 荧光的；发出荧光的 |  Emitting or reflecting light as a result of and only during exposure to radiation, typically ultraviolet radiation.  | The poster features fluorescent colors that glow in the dark. 这张海报上有在黑暗中发光的荧光色。The fluorescent lights in the office hummed quietly. 办公室里的荧光灯发出轻微的嗡嗡声。 | luminous, phosphorescent | opaque, dull | 形容词

词源:
From French *fluorescence*, from *fluorer* "to flow," from Latin *fluere* "to flow," referring to the seemingly flowing nature of the emitted light.  The suffix "-escence" indicates a process or state of becoming.
