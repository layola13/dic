forgone:/fɔːrˈɡɒn/|/fɔːrˈɡɔːn/| adj.|放弃的；预先放弃的；已经发生的；不可避免的|Something that is forgone has been given up or lost, often willingly. It can also describe something that has already happened or is inevitable. |例句：The forgone conclusion was that she would win the election.  她会赢得选举，这是预料之中的事。


例句：He decided to forgo his right to appeal. 他决定放弃上诉权。


近义词：abandoned, relinquished, surrendered, waived, inevitable, predetermined


反义词：retained, kept, claimed, unavoidable


词性:形容词
