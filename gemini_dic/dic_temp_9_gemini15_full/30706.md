certifying:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. |证明；认证；批准 |The act of giving someone a certificate or official document that shows they have passed a test or have the necessary qualifications.  It implies an official confirmation of something’s authenticity or quality. |例句：The organization is responsible for certifying the quality of the products.  该组织负责认证产品的质量。

The university is certifying the student's graduation. 大学正在认证学生的毕业。


近义词：authorizing, validating, verifying, attesting, accrediting | 反义词：disproving, refuting, denying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is certifying the new software. = 公司正在认证新的软件。
He certifies the authenticity of the painting. = 他证明这幅画的真伪。


一般过去时:
They certified the results of the election. = 他们认证了选举的结果。
The doctor certified the cause of death. = 医生证明了死因。


现在完成时:
She has certified over 100 products this year. = 她今年已经认证了100多种产品。
They have certified the building as safe. = 他们已证明该建筑物安全。


过去完成时:
He had certified the documents before submitting them. = 他在提交文件之前已经对文件进行了认证。
The committee had certified the plan before the meeting. = 委员会在会议前已经批准了该计划。


将来时:
The laboratory will certify the sample next week. = 实验室下周将对样品进行认证。
We will certify the accuracy of the data. = 我们将证明数据的准确性。


虚拟语气:
If they certified the product, we would sell it. = 如果他们认证该产品，我们就会销售它。
I wish they had certified my application earlier. = 我希望他们早点认证我的申请。


被动语态:
The product was certified by a reputable agency. = 该产品已获得信誉良好的机构认证。
The results will be certified by an independent auditor. = 结果将由独立审计师认证。


疑问句:
Has the document been certified? = 文件已认证了吗？
Will the new system be certified soon? = 新系统很快就会认证吗？


否定句:
The company did not certify the product due to safety concerns. = 由于安全问题，公司没有认证该产品。
They have not yet certified the results of the experiment. = 他们尚未认证实验结果。


PHRASE->
certified copy = 认证副本
certified check = 保证支票
certified mail = 挂号信
certified public accountant (CPA) = 注册会计师
certified organic = 有机认证


词源:
From Old French *certifier, from Latin certificare "to make certain," from certus "sure, certain" + -ficare, suffix forming verbs of making.
