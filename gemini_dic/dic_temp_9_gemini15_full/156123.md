photostatter:/ˈfoʊtoʊˌstætər/| n. |复印机操作员；（旧式）静电复印机| A person who operates a photocopier, especially an older type using photostatic methods.  The term is largely outdated now that digital photocopiers are ubiquitous.|He was a photostatter at the law firm for many years.|近义词：photocopier operator, copier operator|反义词：N/A
