creeled:/kriːld/ | v. | 诱捕；用诱饵诱捕 | To trap or catch (fish) with a creel.  It also refers to the act of placing fish in a creel. |例句：The fisherman creeled a large salmon. 渔夫诱捕到一条大鲑鱼。| The angler carefully creeled his catch before heading home. 垂钓者在回家前小心地把捕获物放入鱼篓。 |近义词：trap, snare, catch | 反义词：release, free
