neophytes:/ˈniːəfaɪts/| n. |新手，初学者 |A neophyte is a person who is new to a subject, skill, or belief.  They are beginners, lacking experience.  |Example:  The neophytes in the yoga class struggled with the advanced poses.  新手瑜伽课上的人们难以完成高难度的姿势。


Example: As neophytes in the field of software development, they eagerly absorbed new knowledge. 作为软件开发领域的新手，他们渴望吸收新知识。|近义词：beginner, novice, tyro |反义词：expert, veteran, master
