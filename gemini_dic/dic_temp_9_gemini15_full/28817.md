词源:
The word "carouse" ultimately derives from the Middle Dutch word "carousen," meaning "to drink,"  which itself has roots in the Old French word "caroler," meaning "to sing and dance joyfully."  Over time, the meaning evolved to specifically encompass boisterous drinking.
