necrotomy:/nɛˈkrɒtəmi/ | n. |尸体解剖；死体解剖 | The surgical dissection of a dead body, typically for the purpose of determining the cause of death or studying anatomical structures.  Necrotomy is often performed in forensic investigations or anatomical research. | Example: The necrotomy revealed the presence of a previously undetected injury. 尸体解剖显示存在先前未发现的损伤。  The medical examiner performed a necrotomy to determine the cause of death. 验尸官进行了尸体解剖以确定死因。 |近义词：autopsy, post-mortem examination |反义词：活体解剖 (There isn't a direct antonym, as it's defined by its subject being deceased.)
