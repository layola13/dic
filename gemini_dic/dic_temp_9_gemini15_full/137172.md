nonsuccessionally:/ˌnɑːn səkˈsɛʃənəli/ | adj. |非继承地，非连续地 | Not in a successive or hereditary manner; not by succession.  This word describes something that does not follow in a sequence or inherit something from a predecessor. | 例句：The property was transferred nonsuccessionally, bypassing the usual inheritance laws.  这处房产是非继承性转移的，绕过了通常的继承法。


The title to the land was acquired nonsuccessionally, meaning it wasn't inherited. 土地所有权是非继承获得的，这意味着它不是继承来的。


近义词：discontinuously, non-hereditarily, separately, independently | 反义词：successively, hereditarily, consecutively
