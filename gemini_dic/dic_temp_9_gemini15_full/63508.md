enumerating: /ɪˈnuːməreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 列举，枚举 | To mention (a number of things) one by one; to count.  | 例句：He spent the morning enumerating his grievances. 他花了整个上午列举他的不满。

例句：She enumerated the benefits of the new system. 她列举了新系统的优点。


近义词：listing, cataloging, specifying, detailing


反义词：summarizing, omitting


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I enumerate the items on the list. = 我正在列举清单上的项目。
We enumerate the possibilities. = 我们正在枚举各种可能性。


一般过去时:
She enumerated the problems faced by the company. = 她列举了公司面临的问题。
They enumerated the advantages of their product. = 他们列举了他们产品的优点。


现在完成时:
He has enumerated all the reasons for his decision. = 他已经列举了他做出决定的所有原因。
I have enumerated the key points in my presentation. = 我已经列举了我演示文稿中的要点。


过去完成时:
She had enumerated the facts before presenting her argument. = 在陈述她的论点之前，她已经列举了事实。
They had enumerated the options before making a choice. = 他们在做出选择之前已经列举了各种选择。


将来时:
He will enumerate the steps involved in the process. = 他将列举该过程中涉及的步骤。
We will enumerate the challenges we face. = 我们将列举我们面临的挑战。


虚拟语气:
If I were to enumerate all the issues, it would take hours. = 如果我要列举所有问题，将需要数小时。
I wish I could enumerate all the possibilities. = 我希望我能列举所有可能性。


被动语态:
The items were enumerated in alphabetical order. = 这些项目按字母顺序排列。
The benefits of the program are enumerated in the brochure. = 该计划的优点在宣传册中已列出。


疑问句:
Can you enumerate the steps required? = 你能列举所需的步骤吗？
How will you enumerate the different types of data? = 你将如何枚举不同类型的数据？


否定句:
He did not enumerate all the details. = 他没有列举所有细节。
She failed to enumerate the potential risks. = 她未能列举潜在的风险。



PHRASE->
enumerate the possibilities = 列举可能性

enumerate the disadvantages = 列举缺点
enumerate the steps = 列举步骤

词源:
The word "enumerate" comes from the Late Latin word *enumerare*, which is a combination of the prefix *e-* (out) and *numerare* (to number, count).  Therefore, its core meaning is to count out or list out.
