shamba:/ˈʃɑːmbə/ | n. | 庄园；种植园；农场 | Swahili word for a farm or cultivated land;  a plot of land, often used for subsistence farming. It can also refer to a homestead or a smallholding. | 例句：He works on his shamba growing maize and beans. 他在他的农场种植玉米和豆子。 | 近义词：farm, plantation, ranch, homestead | 反义词：wilderness, desert
