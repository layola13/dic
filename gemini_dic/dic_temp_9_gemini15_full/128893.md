nimbler: /ˈnɪmblər/ | adj. | 更灵活的，更敏捷的 | More agile or quick-moving.  | The cat was nimbler than the dog.  这只猫比那条狗更灵活。  | agile, quick, spry | clumsy, slow, sluggish | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is nimbler than his brother. 他比他弟弟更灵活。
The squirrel is nimbler than the rabbit. 松鼠比兔子更灵活。


一般过去时:
She was nimbler in her youth. 她年轻时更灵活。
The dancer was nimbler than the other performers. 舞者比其他表演者更灵活。


现在完成时:
He has become nimbler with practice. 他经过练习后变得更灵活了。
The athlete has grown nimbler over the years. 运动员这些年来变得更敏捷了。


过去完成时:
She had been nimbler before the injury. 受伤前她更灵活。
The gymnast had already become nimbler before the competition. 体操运动员在比赛前已经变得更敏捷了。


将来时:
He will be nimbler after his rehabilitation. 康复后他会更灵活。
The young athlete will be nimbler as he trains more. 年轻运动员随着训练的增多会变得更敏捷。


虚拟语气:
If he were nimbler, he could participate in the competition. 如果他更灵活，他就能参加比赛。
I wish I were nimbler; then I could do parkour. 我希望我更灵活；那样我就能玩跑酷了。


被动语态:

否定句:
