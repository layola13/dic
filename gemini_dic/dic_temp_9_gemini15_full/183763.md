rescoring:/riːˈskɔːrɪŋ/| n. |重新评分，重新计分 |The act of assigning a new score or scores to something, typically after a review or reassessment.  This often involves re-evaluating the original scoring criteria or using different methods. |例句：The exam was rescored after several students complained about ambiguous questions.  考试在几名学生抱怨试题含糊不清后进行了重新评分。|近义词：regrading, reevaluation, recalculation |反义词：initial scoring, first scoring
