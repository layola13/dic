homaging:/ˈhɒmɪdʒɪŋ/| v.|致敬，表达敬意|The act of paying homage; expressing high regard or respect.|The film is a subtle homage to classic Hollywood musicals. 这部电影是对经典好莱坞音乐剧的巧妙致敬。|近义词：tribute,  acknowledgment,  reverence|反义词：disrespect,  contempt,  insult
