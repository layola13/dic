short-termist:/ˈʃɔːrtˈtɜːrmɪst/| adj. | 短视的；目光短浅的 | Focusing on short-term gains and neglecting long-term consequences.  Characterized by a lack of foresight and planning for the future. | The company's short-termist approach to investment led to long-term financial instability. 这家公司目光短浅的投资策略导致了长期的财务不稳定。 | 近义词：short-sighted, myopic, opportunistic | 反义词：far-sighted, long-term, strategic | 形容词
