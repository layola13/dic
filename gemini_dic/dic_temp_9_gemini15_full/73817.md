former's: /ˈfɔːrmərz/ | pron. | 前者的；前者的所有物 |  The possessive form of "former," indicating belonging to or relating to the first mentioned of two people or things.  It's used to avoid repetition and clarify possession. |例句：The former's proposal was more detailed than the latter's. 前者的提议比后者的更详细。

The former's car was faster. 前者的车更快。|近义词：the first one's, the first mentioned's |反义词：latter's
