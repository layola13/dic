harquebuse:/ˈhɑːrkɪbjuːs/| n. |火绳枪|A harquebus is a type of firearm, a long-barreled, matchlock gun, that was used in the 15th-16th centuries.  It was heavier and more powerful than earlier handguns, and was typically fired from a rest. |The soldiers lined up, their harquebuses ready to fire.  士兵们列队站好，他们的火绳枪准备射击。 The museum displays a beautifully preserved harquebus from the 16th century.  博物馆展示了一支保存完好的16世纪火绳枪。| arquebus, musket |  (近义词无明确反义词，因为是特定类型武器)|名词
