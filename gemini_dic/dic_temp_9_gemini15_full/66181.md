exchanger: /ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒər/ | n. | 交换机；兑换处；交换器 | A device or system that facilitates the exchange of something, such as currency, data, or heat.  It can refer to a physical location (like a currency exchange) or a piece of equipment (like a heat exchanger). | 例句：The airport has a currency exchanger where you can trade your dollars for euros. 机场有一个货币兑换处，你可以在那里用美元兑换欧元。

The heat exchanger in the car's engine prevents overheating. 汽车引擎中的热交换器防止过热。 | 近义词：converter, interchanger, trader | 反义词：receiver (in some contexts)
