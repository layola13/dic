colugo:/ˈkɒluːɡoʊ/| n. |鼯猴|A colugo is a gliding mammal of the order Dermoptera.  They are also known as flying lemurs, though they are not lemurs and cannot truly fly.  They glide between trees using a patagium, a membrane of skin stretching from their neck to their limbs and tail. | Example: The colugo gracefully glided from one tree to another.  鼯猴优雅地从一棵树滑翔到另一棵树。  |近义词：flying lemur (though inaccurate)|反义词：无
