nondamagingly:/ˌnɒnˈdæmɪdʒɪŋli/ | adv. | 无损地，不造成损害地 |  In a way that does not cause damage.  This adverb describes an action or process that is performed without causing harm or injury. | Example: The technician repaired the computer nondamagingly.  技术员无损地修理了电脑。  The surgeon removed the tumor nondamagingly. 外科医生无损地切除了肿瘤。 | 近义词：harmlessly, safely, gently | 反义词：destructively, harmfully, damagingly
