The ancient city is slowly fossilising under the desert sands.  =  古老的城市正在慢慢地被沙漠的沙子掩埋而变成化石。

The educational system risks fossilising if it doesn't adapt to new technologies. = 如果教育体系不适应新技术，它就有可能变得僵化。

反义词：modernizing, innovating, revitalizing
