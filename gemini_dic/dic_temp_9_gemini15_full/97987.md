inoculator:/ɪˈnɒkjʊleɪtə(r)/| n. |接种器；接种物；培养基；(尤指)疫苗接种者 |A device or agent used for inoculation, especially one for introducing a vaccine or other substance into a living organism.  It can also refer to a person who inoculates. |例句：The nurse used an inoculator to administer the flu vaccine. 护士用接种器注射流感疫苗。

例句：He is a skilled inoculator, known for his precise technique. 他是一位技术娴熟的接种员，以其精确的技术而闻名。


近义词：vaccinator, injector, inoculant (as a noun)

反义词：None directly;  Antonyms would relate to the opposite action of inoculation, such as "decontaminator" if referring to removing something rather than introducing it.

词性：名词
