palindrome:/ˈpælɪnˌdroʊm/| n. |回文，回文词 |A palindrome is a word, phrase, number, or other sequence of characters which reads the same backward as forward, such as madam or racecar.  |Example: "Madam, I'm Adam" is a famous palindrome.  "Madam, I'm Adam"是一个著名的回文句。|近义词：reversible, mirrored |反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to words or phrases that are not palindromes.
