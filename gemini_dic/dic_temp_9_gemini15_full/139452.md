obsoleting:/ˌɒbsəˈliːtɪŋ/ | v. | 使过时；淘汰；使废弃 |  The process of making something obsolete; causing something to become outdated or no longer useful.  | Example:  The company is obsoleting its older models in favor of newer, more efficient ones. 公司正在淘汰旧型号产品，转而采用更新、更高效的型号。  The rapid advancements in technology are obsoleting many traditional skills. 技术的快速发展正在使许多传统技能过时。 | 近义词：making obsolete, superseding, replacing, phasing out | 反义词：modernizing, updating, reviving


USAGE->

The company is obsoleting its older products. = 公司正在淘汰旧产品。
They are obsoleting the old system gradually. = 他们正在逐步淘汰旧系统。
