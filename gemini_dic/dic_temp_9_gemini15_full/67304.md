exsuction:/ɛkˈsʌkʃən/| n. |抽吸；抽出；吸出|The removal of fluid or gas by suction.  It is often used in medical contexts to remove fluids from wounds, body cavities, or during surgery. |例句：The surgeon performed an exsuction to remove excess fluid from the patient's chest cavity. = 外科医生进行了抽吸手术，以去除患者胸腔内的多余液体。

例句：The vacuum cleaner uses exsuction to remove dust and dirt from carpets. = 吸尘器利用抽吸来清除地毯上的灰尘和污垢。


近义词：aspiration, suction, drainage|反义词：injection, infusion|名词
