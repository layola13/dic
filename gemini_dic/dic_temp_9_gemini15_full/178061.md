reclean:/ˌriːˈkliːn/| vt.|再次清洁；重新清洁|To clean something again.  This is typically used when a first cleaning was insufficient or when something has become dirty again.|例句：I had to reclean the kitchen after the party.  派对后我不得不再次清洁厨房。


例句：The mechanic recleaned the engine parts before reassembly. 技工在重新组装之前再次清洁了发动机零件。


近义词：re-clean, clean again, cleanse again

反义词：dirty, soil


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recleans the bathroom every day. 他每天都重新打扫浴室。
She recleans her glasses after each use. 她每次使用后都会重新清洁眼镜。

一般过去时:
They recleaned the carpets after the spill. 他们洒了东西后重新清洁了地毯。
I recleaned the oven because it was still greasy. 我重新清洁了烤箱，因为它仍然很油腻。

现在完成时:
He has recleaned the wound several times. 他已经多次重新清洁了伤口。
She has recleaned the entire house since the storm. 暴风雨过后，她已经重新清洁了整个房子。

过去完成时:
They had recleaned the pool before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前，他们已经重新清洁了游泳池。
She had recleaned the car before taking it to the dealership. 在把车送到经销商那里之前，她已经重新清洁了它。

将来时:
I will reclean the windows tomorrow. 我明天将重新清洁窗户。
We will reclean the office after the renovations are complete. 装修完成后，我们将重新清洁办公室。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would reclean the entire house. 如果我有更多时间，我会重新清洁整个房子。
If she knew it was so dirty, she would have recleaned it earlier. 如果她知道这么脏，她会早点重新清洁的。

被动语态:
The room was recleaned by the cleaning staff. 房间由清洁人员重新清洁。
The spill was recleaned immediately. 溢出的东西立即被重新清洁。

疑问句:
Will the car be recleaned before delivery? 车子会在交付前重新清洁吗？
Has the equipment been recleaned? 设备被重新清洁了吗？

否定句:
They did not reclean the kitchen after dinner. 他们晚饭后没有重新清洁厨房。
I haven't recleaned the whiteboard yet. 我还没重新擦白板。
