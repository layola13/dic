decontaminates:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪts/| vt.|净化；消毒；去污|To remove contaminants from something, making it clean and safe.|例句：The hospital staff decontaminates the operating room after each surgery. 医院工作人员在每次手术后都会对手术室进行消毒。The hazmat team decontaminates the site after the chemical spill.  危险品处理小组在化学品泄漏后对现场进行了净化。|近义词：purify, sterilize, sanitize|反义词：contaminate, pollute


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company decontaminates its equipment regularly. = 公司定期对其设备进行净化。
The hospital decontaminates all surgical instruments. = 医院对所有手术器械进行消毒。


一般过去时:
The firefighters decontaminated the area after the fire. = 消防员在火灾后对该区域进行了净化。
The scientists decontaminated the lab before starting the experiment. = 科学家们在开始实验前对实验室进行了消毒。


现在完成时:
The team has decontaminated the site successfully. = 该团队已成功净化了现场。
They have decontaminated the water supply. = 他们已经净化了供水系统。


过去完成时:
The workers had decontaminated the spill before the inspectors arrived. = 在检查员到达之前，工人们已经清理了泄漏物。
The crew had decontaminated the ship before entering port. = 船员在进港前已经对船只进行了消毒。


将来时:
The crew will decontaminate the spacecraft upon its return. = 宇宙飞船返回后，工作人员将对其进行消毒。
The government will decontaminate the affected area. = 政府将对受灾地区进行净化。


虚拟语气:
If we had decontaminated the water sooner, there would have been fewer illnesses. = 如果我们早点净化水源，就不会有那么多人生病了。
It is vital that the equipment be decontaminated before use. = 使用前必须对设备进行消毒至关重要。


被动语态:
The area was decontaminated after the bomb threat. = 炸弹威胁过后，该区域进行了净化。
The equipment is being decontaminated right now. = 设备正在进行消毒。


疑问句:
Has the site been decontaminated yet? = 现场已经净化了吗？
Will the water be decontaminated before we drink it? = 我们喝水之前，水会净化吗？


否定句:
They did not decontaminate the area properly. = 他们没有正确地净化该区域。
The factory failed to decontaminate its waste products. = 工厂未能对其废品进行净化。
