polymathist:/ˈpɒliˌmætɪst/| n. |博学者，通才 |A polymath, or polymathist, is a person of wide-ranging knowledge or learning.  They excel in multiple fields of study. | Leonardo da Vinci was a renowned polymathist, excelling in art, science, and engineering.  |近义词：polymath, universalist, Renaissance person |反义词：specialist, expert in a single field
