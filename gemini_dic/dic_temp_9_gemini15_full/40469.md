contribute:/kənˈtrɪbjuːt/| vt. vi. n.|贡献，投稿，捐献 | to give something, especially money, goods, or time, to a common fund or cause, or to help to achieve something |例句：She contributed generously to the charity. 她慷慨地捐赠给了慈善机构。  He contributed a chapter to the book. 他为这本书贡献了一章。|近义词：donate, give, offer, supply|反义词：detract, withhold, take away


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They contribute to the local community. = 他们为当地社区做贡献。
I regularly contribute articles to the magazine. = 我定期向杂志投稿。


一般过去时:
She contributed a significant amount of money to the project. = 她为这个项目捐献了一大笔钱。
He contributed several ideas to the meeting. = 他在会议上提出了几个想法。


现在完成时:
We have contributed significantly to the success of the company. = 我们为公司的成功做出了重大贡献。
She has contributed many articles to this journal. = 她已经为这份期刊投稿了很多文章。


过去完成时:
They had contributed all their savings before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经捐出了所有的积蓄。
He had contributed to the research before the study was published. = 在研究发表之前，他已经为这项研究做出了贡献。


将来时:
I will contribute to the discussion tomorrow. = 我明天将参与讨论。
They will contribute financially to the new hospital. = 他们将为新医院提供资金支持。


虚拟语气:
If everyone contributed, we could reach our goal. = 如果每个人都贡献力量，我们就能实现目标。
I wish I had contributed more to the project. = 我希望我为这个项目贡献更多。


被动语态:
A large sum of money was contributed to the relief effort. = 一大笔钱被捐给了救济工作。
His ideas were not contributed to the final design. = 他的想法没有被纳入最终设计。


疑问句:
Will you contribute to the fundraising event? = 你会参加募捐活动吗？
How much did he contribute to the cause? = 他为这项事业捐了多少钱？


否定句:
He did not contribute to the team's success. = 他没有为团队的成功做出贡献。
She refused to contribute any further funds. = 她拒绝进一步捐款。


PHRASE->
contribute to:  对…做贡献
contribute to the success of:  为…的成功做贡献
contribute to the discussion: 参与讨论
contribute one's ideas: 贡献自己的想法
contribute to a magazine: 向杂志投稿

词源:
From Old French *contribuer, from Latin contribuere "to contribute," from com- "together" + tribuere "to give, assign."
