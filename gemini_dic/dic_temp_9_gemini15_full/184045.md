residentships:/ˈrezɪdəntʃɪps/| n.|住院医师职位；居住权；居住资格|The period of time a doctor spends training in a hospital after medical school, usually involving specialized training.  It also refers to the right to live in a particular place.|例句：He completed his residentships in neurosurgery and cardiology. 他完成了神经外科和心脏病学的住院医师培训。|例句：The new housing development offers residentships to families with young children. 新建的住宅区为有小孩的家庭提供居住权。|近义词：residency, fellowship (in a medical context)|反义词：无
