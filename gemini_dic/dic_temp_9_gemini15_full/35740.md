cochairs:/ˈkoʊtʃɛrz/| n. |共同主席；联合主席 |The word "cochairs" refers to two or more people who jointly preside over a meeting, organization, or event. It is a compound word formed by combining "co-" (meaning "together" or "jointly") and "chairs" (referring to the people who preside).|Example: The conference will be cochaired by Professor Smith and Dr. Jones.  这次会议将由史密斯教授和琼斯博士共同主持。

The annual meeting will be cochaired by two prominent figures in the industry.  年度会议将由业内两位知名人士共同主持。|近义词：joint chairs,  presidents (in some contexts)|反义词：sole chair, single chair|名词
