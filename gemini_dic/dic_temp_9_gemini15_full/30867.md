chaetognath:/ˈkɛtəɡnæθ/| n. |毛颚动物|A marine invertebrate of the phylum Chaetognatha, characterized by a transparent body, lateral fins, and grasping spines.  They are important predators in the plankton. |Example:  The researchers collected numerous chaetognaths during their oceanographic survey.  研究人员在海洋考察中采集了许多毛颚动物。  |近义词: arrow worm (although this is a common name, not a synonym)|反义词: None specifically;  consider contrasting with larger marine predators.
