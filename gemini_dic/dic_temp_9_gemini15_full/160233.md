pontiff:/ˈpɒntɪf/| n. |教宗，主教 |A pontiff is the Pope or a bishop.  The term often implies a high level of authority and religious power.  |Example: The pontiff delivered a powerful sermon on peace. 教宗发表了一篇关于和平的有力布道。|近义词：pope, bishop, prelate |反义词：layperson, parishioner


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pontiff leads the Catholic Church. = 教宗领导天主教教会。
He regularly addresses the pontiff's pronouncements. = 他定期发表对教宗声明的评论。


一般过去时:
The pontiff visited several countries last year. = 教宗去年访问了几个国家。
The previous pontiff reigned for many years. = 前任教宗执政多年。


现在完成时:
The pontiff has spoken out against injustice. = 教宗已经公开谴责不公正。
The Church has had many influential pontiffs throughout history. = 历史上教会涌现了许多有影响力的教宗。


过去完成时:
Before the pontiff's arrival, the crowds had gathered. = 在教宗到达之前，人群已经聚集起来。
They had already heard of the pontiff's decision before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，他们就已经听说过教宗的决定。


将来时:
The pontiff will address the congregation tomorrow. = 教宗明天将向会众发表讲话。
We believe the next pontiff will be a progressive reformer. = 我们相信下一任教宗将是一位进步的改革者。


虚拟语气:
If the pontiff were here, he would address the issue. = 如果教宗在这里，他会处理这个问题。
I wish the pontiff would speak more on social justice. = 我希望教宗能更多地谈论社会正义。


被动语态:
The pontiff's decisions are carefully considered. = 教宗的决定经过仔细考虑。
The sermon was delivered by the pontiff himself. = 布道是由教宗本人发表的。


疑问句:
Is the pontiff attending the meeting? = 教宗会参加会议吗？
What did the pontiff say about the current situation? = 教宗对当前局势说了什么？


否定句:
The pontiff did not approve of the proposal. = 教宗没有批准该提案。
He is not the first pontiff to face such challenges. = 他不是第一个面临这种挑战的教宗。


PHRASE->
the pontiff's pronouncements = 教宗的声明
the authority of the pontiff = 教宗的权威
the pontiff's visit = 教宗的访问
a papal pontiff = 教皇（强调罗马天主教教宗）
the pontiff's blessing = 教宗的祝福

词源:
源自拉丁语 *pontifex,  字面意思是“桥梁建造者”，后来引申为“大祭司”，最终演变为“教宗”。  The original meaning relates to the Roman *pontifex maximus, the chief priest in ancient Rome, who held considerable power and influence.
