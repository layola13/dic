servantess: /ˈsɜːrvəntɛs/ | n. |女仆，侍女 | A female servant.  The word is now largely archaic and considered somewhat offensive. It emphasizes the subservient role of the woman. |例句：The servantess quietly prepared the master's dinner.  这位女仆静静地准备了主人的晚餐。


The old manor house employed several servantesses.  那座古老的庄园雇佣了好几个女仆。


近义词：maidservant, housemaid, lady-in-waiting (in a historical context) | 反义词：mistress, employer | 词性：名词
