overprovide:/ˌoʊvərprəˈvaɪd/ | vt. | 过度提供，供应过多 | To provide more of something than is needed or wanted.  |例句：The government overprovided funds for the project, leading to waste.  政府为该项目提供了过多的资金，导致浪费。

例句：The company overprovided staffing for the event, resulting in extra payroll expenses. 公司为活动安排了过多的人员，导致额外的工资支出。


近义词：oversupply, surplus, exceed, glut | 反义词：underprovide, undersupply
