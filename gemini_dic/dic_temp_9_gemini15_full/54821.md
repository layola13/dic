dispersers:/dɪˈspɜːrsərz/ | n. | 分散者；扩散器 | A disperser is a person or thing that disperses something, such as seeds, light, or a crowd.  It can also refer to a device used to disperse a substance. | Example: The wind acted as a disperser of the pollen. 风作为花粉的传播媒介。  The paint sprayer is a type of disperser. 喷漆器是一种分散器。 | 近义词：scatterers, spreaders, distributors | 反义词：concentrators, collectors | 名词
