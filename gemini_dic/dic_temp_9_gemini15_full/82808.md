guised:/ɡaɪzd/| adj. |伪装的；假扮的 | disguised; concealed; masked |例句：The spy was disguised as a waiter. 间谍伪装成服务员。

The thief, disguised as a policeman, entered the bank.  小偷乔装成警察进入了银行。


近义词：masked, concealed, disguised, hidden | 反义词：unmasked, revealed, exposed | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is always disguised as a superhero. 他总是伪装成超级英雄。
The painting is cleverly disguised as a mirror. 这幅画巧妙地伪装成一面镜子。


一般过去时:
She disguised herself as a man to escape. 她乔装打扮成男人逃跑了。
The soldiers disguised their movements to avoid detection. 士兵们掩饰了他们的行踪以避免被发现。


现在完成时:
He has disguised his true intentions throughout the negotiation. 在整个谈判过程中，他一直掩盖了他的真实意图。
The company has disguised its financial losses through creative accounting. 公司通过创造性的会计手段掩盖了其财务损失。


过去完成时:
They had disguised the bomb as a package. 他们已将炸弹伪装成包裹。
She had disguised her feelings throughout the meeting. 在整个会议期间，她一直掩饰着自己的感情。


将来时:
He will disguise himself as a beggar to infiltrate the city. 他将伪装成乞丐潜入城市。
They will disguise the changes to the software to avoid alarming users. 他们将掩盖软件的更改，以免惊动用户。


虚拟语气:
If he disguised himself better, he would have escaped. 如果他伪装得更好，他就能逃脱了。
I wish I had disguised my anxiety more effectively. 我希望我当时能更有效地掩饰我的焦虑。


被动语态:
His identity was disguised by a clever makeup artist. 他的身份被一位化妆技巧高超的化妆师掩盖了。
The true nature of the problem was disguised by misleading statistics. 问题的真相被误导性的统计数据掩盖了。


疑问句:
Was the suspect disguised in any way? 嫌疑人是否进行了任何伪装？

否定句:
He was not disguised; everyone recognized him. 他没有伪装；每个人都认出了他。
The evidence was not disguised; it was clear for all to see. 证据没有被掩盖；所有人都看得清清楚楚。
