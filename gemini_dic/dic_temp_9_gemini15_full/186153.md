rheas:/ˈriːəz/| n. |美洲鸵鸟|A rhea is a large flightless bird native to South America, similar to an ostrich but smaller.  They are known for their long necks and legs, and three-toed feet.|  Example:  The rheas grazed peacefully in the pampas. 美洲鸵鸟在潘帕斯草原上平静地吃草。  The zoo boasts a healthy flock of rheas. 动物园拥有健康的一群美洲鸵鸟。|近义词：ostrich (though smaller)|反义词：N/A|名词
