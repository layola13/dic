clotheslessness:/ˈkloʊðzləsnəs/| n. |赤裸；衣不蔽体；缺乏衣物|The state of being without clothes; nakedness.  It can also refer to a lack of sufficient clothing, especially due to poverty.|例句：The photograph depicted the stark clotheslessness of the refugees. 这张照片描绘了难民赤身裸体的惨状。


例句：The clotheslessness of the homeless population is a serious social issue. 无家可归者的衣不蔽体是一个严重的社会问题。


近义词：nudity, nakedness, destitution (in the context of lack of clothing)


反义词：well-dressed, clothed, covered


词性:名词
