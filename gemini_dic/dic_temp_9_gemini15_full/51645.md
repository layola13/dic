deviants:/ˈdiːviənts/| n.| 离经叛道者；行为异常者；偏差值 | People or things that deviate from what is considered normal or acceptable.  They may exhibit unconventional behavior, beliefs, or characteristics. |例句：The study focused on the social behavior of deviants. 这项研究侧重于离经叛道者的社会行为。

例句：The data points that fall outside the range are considered deviants.  落在范围之外的数据点被认为是偏差值。


近义词：aberrations, eccentrics, nonconformists, outliers


反义词：conformists, mainstreamers, normies


词性:名词
