salt:/sɔːlt/ /sɒlt/| n. |盐；盐分；刺激；风趣|A crystalline compound, sodium chloride (NaCl), used to season food and in various industrial processes.  It is also a crucial element for many biological processes.|例句：We need to add more salt to the soup. 我们需要在汤里加更多的盐。

例句：The doctor advised him to reduce his salt intake. 医生建议他减少盐的摄入量。

近义词：sodium chloride, seasoning|反义词：freshwater, bland
