contour:/ˈkɒntʊə(r)/, /ˈkɑːntʊr/| n.|轮廓，曲线；等高线|The outline or external form of something.  A line on a map joining points of equal height.|例句：The contour of the coastline was irregular. 海岸线的轮廓不规则。

The map showed several contour lines. 地图上显示了几条等高线。


近义词：outline, profile, silhouette, shape|反义词：interior, inside
