rescission:/rɪˈsɪʒən/| n. |撤销；解除；废除 |The act of making a contract or agreement void; cancellation; annulment.  Rescission is a legal remedy that restores the parties to their pre-contractual positions.  It's often used when there's been a breach of contract, misrepresentation, or duress. |Example: The contract was subject to rescission if the buyer failed to meet the payment deadline.  合同如果买方未能在截止日期前付款，则可被撤销。

The company announced the rescission of its merger agreement. 公司宣布撤销其并购协议。


近义词：cancellation, annulment, revocation, termination |反义词：ratification, confirmation, validation |词性：名词
