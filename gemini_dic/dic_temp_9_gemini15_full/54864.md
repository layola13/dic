displeased:/dɪsˈpliːzd/ | adj. | 不高兴的；不满意的 | Feeling or showing annoyance or displeasure.  |例句：He was displeased with the results of the test. 他对考试的结果很不满意。
例句：Her displeased expression showed her disapproval. 她不满意的表情显示了她的反对。|近义词：annoyed, unhappy, dissatisfied, disgruntled |反义词：pleased, happy, satisfied, content |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is displeased with my work. = 他对我的工作不满意。
She is displeased by the news. = 她对这个消息感到不高兴。


一般过去时:
They were displeased at the delay. = 他们对延误感到不满。
I was displeased with his behavior. = 我对他行为感到不高兴。


现在完成时:
I have been displeased with his attitude for a long time. = 我对他这种态度已经不满很久了。
She has been displeased with the service since last week. = 自从上周以来，她一直对这项服务感到不满。


过去完成时:
He had been displeased with her performance before the final assessment. = 在最终评估之前，他对她的表现就已经感到不满。
They had been displeased with the outcome of the negotiations. = 他们对谈判的结果早就感到不满意。


将来时:
He will be displeased if you don't finish the project on time. = 如果你不能按时完成项目，他会不高兴。
She will be displeased to hear the bad news. = 她听到这个坏消息会不高兴的。


虚拟语气:
I would be displeased if he didn't apologize. = 如果他不道歉，我会不高兴。
She would have been displeased if she had known the truth. = 如果她知道真相，她会很不高兴。


被动语态:
His parents were displeased with his grades.  = 他的父母对他的成绩感到不满。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The manager was displeased by the employee's tardiness. = 经理对员工的迟到感到不满。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，主动语态更常用)


疑问句:
Is he displeased with the outcome? = 他对结果不满意吗？
Are they displeased with the new policy? = 他们对新政策不满吗？


否定句:
He is not displeased with the gift. = 他对这份礼物并不感到不满。
She wasn't displeased by his compliment. = 她并不讨厌他的赞美。


PHRASE->
feel displeased = 感到不快
express displeasure = 表达不满
show displeasure = 表现出不满
voice displeasure = 发表不满
become displeased = 变得不高兴
