dismount:/dɪˈsmaʊnt/| vt. vi. n.|下马；卸下；拆卸|To get off a horse, bicycle, or other mount; to remove something from its mount.|例句：He dismounted the horse carefully. 他小心地下马了。
例句：The technician dismounted the hard drive from the computer. 技术人员从电脑上卸下了硬盘。|近义词：get off, alight, descend|反义词：mount
