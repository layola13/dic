picada:/piˈkɑːdə/| n.|小吃，开胃菜；刺；叮；(尤指昆虫的)叮咬|A small, savory snack or appetizer; a prick, sting, or bite (especially from an insect).  |例句：We ordered several picadas before the main course arrived. 我们在主菜上桌前点了一些小吃。

例句：The mosquito's picada left a small red mark on my arm. 蚊子的叮咬在我的胳膊上留下了一个小红点。|近义词：appetizer, hors d'oeuvre, snack, sting, bite |反义词：main course, entree
