cut-it-out:/kʌt ɪt aʊt/| phrasal verb|停止；住手；别闹了|To tell someone to stop doing something annoying or inappropriate.|Stop making that noise! Cut it out! |近义词：stop it, cease, desist|反义词：continue, persist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cuts it out when his mom tells him to. = 他妈妈让他停止的时候，他就停下来了。
They cut it out as soon as the teacher entered the room. = 老师一进教室，他们就停止了。


一般过去时:
She cut it out after I warned her. = 我警告她之后，她就停止了。
We cut it out because it was getting late. = 因为时间晚了，我们停止了。


现在完成时:
I have cut it out since you asked me. = 自从你让我停止以来，我就停止了。
They have cut it out, haven't they? = 他们已经停止了，不是吗？


过去完成时:
He had cut it out before his father came home. = 他在他父亲回家之前就已经停止了。
They had cut it out by the time the police arrived. = 等警察到达时，他们已经停止了。


将来时:
I will cut it out as soon as possible. = 我会尽快停止的。
She will cut it out if you ask her nicely. = 如果你好好请求她，她会停止的。


虚拟语气:
If he were to cut it out, everyone would be happy. = 如果他停止，每个人都会很高兴。
I wish he would cut it out. = 我希望他能停止。


被动语态:
The noise was cut out by the manager. = 噪音被经理制止了。(This usage is less common for this phrasal verb)
The disruptive behavior should be cut out immediately. = 扰乱行为应该立即停止。(This usage is less common for this phrasal verb)


疑问句:
Will you cut it out? = 你会停止吗？
Can't you cut it out? = 你就不能停止吗？


否定句:
He didn't cut it out even when I asked him to. = 即使我让他停止，他也没有停止。
They wouldn't cut it out, no matter what we said. = 不管我们说什么，他们都不肯停止。


PHRASE->
cut it out now = 马上住手
cut it out completely = 完全停止
just cut it out = 就别闹了
cut it out, will you? = 好好停下来，好吗？
cut that out! = 少来这一套！
