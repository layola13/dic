desegregate:/ˈdiːˌseɡrɪɡeɪt/| vt.|取消种族隔离；使融合|To end the separation of different racial groups, especially in schools, housing, or other public places.|The city council voted to desegregate the public schools.|近义词：integrate, unify|反义词：segregate
