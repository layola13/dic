municipal:/mjuːˈnɪsɪpəl/| adj.|市政的；市立的；市营的|Relating to or characteristic of a municipality or its governing body.  Municipal services include things like garbage collection, water supply, and public transportation.|例句：The municipal government is responsible for maintaining the city's parks.  市政府负责维护城市公园。

例句：The municipal library offers a wide range of resources. 市立图书馆提供各种各样的资源。


近义词：civic, urban, city


反义词：rural, national


词源：From Late Latin *municipalis, from Latin municipium "municipality," from munus "duty, service" + capere "to take, receive."  The original meaning referred to a town granted certain rights and privileges by Rome.
