pensive:/ˈpɛnsɪv/| adj.|沉思的；忧郁的；若有所思的|Thoughtful, often in a sad or melancholy way; dreamily or wistfully thoughtful.  The adjective "pensive" describes someone who is deeply absorbed in thought, often with a hint of sadness or melancholy.  It suggests a quiet, reflective state, sometimes accompanied by a wistful or dreamy quality.|例句：She sat by the window, looking pensive. 她坐在窗边，若有所思。

She fell into a pensive mood after receiving the news.  收到消息后，她陷入了沉思。|近义词：thoughtful, reflective, contemplative, meditative, wistful, dreamy|反义词：cheerful, joyous, carefree, lighthearted
