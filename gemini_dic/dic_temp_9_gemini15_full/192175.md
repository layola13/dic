scraper:/ˈskreɪpər/| n. v.|刮刀；刮削器；网络爬虫|A scraper is a tool used for scraping or removing something from a surface.  It can also refer to a computer program that extracts data from websites.|例句：He used a scraper to clean the paint off the window. 他用刮刀把窗户上的油漆刮掉。  The website scraper collected all the product prices. 网站爬虫收集了所有产品价格。|近义词：scrapper, remover, web crawler|反义词：filler, applicator
