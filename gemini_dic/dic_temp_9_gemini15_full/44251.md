crofter:/ˈkrɒftə(r)/| n. |小农，佃农 |A crofter is a tenant farmer who cultivates a small farm, typically in Scotland, Orkney, and Shetland.  They often hold their land under traditional tenure systems. |Many crofters supplement their income with fishing or other activities.|近义词：smallholder, tenant farmer |反义词：landowner, large-scale farmer|名词
