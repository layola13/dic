corporally:/ˈkɔːrpərəli/ | adv. | 肉体上地；身体上地；物质上地 | In a bodily or physical way; relating to the body.  | The punishment was inflicted corporally. | 近义词：physically, bodily, carnally | 反义词：spiritually, mentally
