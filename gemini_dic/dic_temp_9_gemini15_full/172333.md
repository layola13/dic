putrefiable: /ˌpjuːtrɪˈfaɪəbl/ | adj. | 可腐烂的；易腐败的 | Susceptible to decay or decomposition; liable to rot.  | The putrefiable nature of organic matter makes it important to dispose of it properly. 有机物的易腐烂性质使得妥善处理它非常重要。 |近义词：perishable, decomposable, corruptible |反义词：imperishable, indestructible


USAGE->

The meat is putrefiable in warm weather. = 肉在温暖的天气里容易腐烂。
This food is putrefiable; we should eat it soon. = 这食物容易腐败；我们应该尽快吃掉它。
