seville:/səˈvɪl/| n. |塞维利亚|Seville is the capital and largest city of the autonomous community of Andalusia and the province of Seville, in southern Spain.  It is a major cultural centre and a significant tourist destination known for its rich history, architecture (including the Alcázar of Seville and the Seville Cathedral), flamenco, and tapas. | Seville is famous for its vibrant culture and stunning architecture.  塞维利亚以其充满活力的文化和令人惊叹的建筑而闻名。|  Cordoba, Granada |  None | noun
