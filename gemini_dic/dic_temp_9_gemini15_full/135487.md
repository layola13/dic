/ˌnɒnˈfɪləsəfiz/ | n. |非哲学；反哲学 |  A system of thought or belief that rejects or opposes the principles or methods of philosophy.  It often involves a deliberate avoidance of traditional philosophical inquiry or a critique of its limitations.  The term can be used to describe various approaches, from cynical dismissals of philosophy to alternative frameworks that challenge philosophical assumptions. |  Some postmodern thinkers might be described as proponents of nonphilosophies, challenging the very foundations of traditional philosophical inquiry.  | 近义词：anti-philosophy, heterodoxy | 反义词：philosophy, orthodoxy | 名词
