equilibrium:/ˌiːkwɪˈlɪbriəm/| n. |平衡；均衡；均势 | A state where opposing forces or influences are balanced.  It refers to a state of balance or stability, whether physical, chemical, or even emotional.  The word often implies a delicate balance that could easily be disrupted. |例句：The tightrope walker maintained his equilibrium with great skill.  走钢丝的人凭借高超的技巧保持平衡。

例句：After the storm, a fragile equilibrium was restored in the ecosystem.  暴风雨过后，生态系统恢复了脆弱的平衡。


近义词：balance, stability, poise, parity |反义词：imbalance, instability, chaos, disruption |词性: 名词

虚拟语气:
