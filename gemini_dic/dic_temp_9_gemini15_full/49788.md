demonization: /ˌdiːmənɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |妖魔化；恶魔化；歪曲；丑化 |The act of portraying someone or something as evil or demonic.  This often involves exaggerating negative aspects and ignoring positive ones, creating a distorted and unfair representation. |例句：The demonization of immigrants in the media fueled xenophobic attitudes.  媒体对移民的妖魔化加剧了仇外情绪。

例句：The political campaign relied heavily on the demonization of its opponent.  这场政治运动严重依赖于对对手的妖魔化。


近义词：vilification, denigration, defamation, stigmatization, blackening


反义词：glorification, idealization, sanctification


词性：名词
