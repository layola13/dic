The word often carries connotations of a romantic, bohemian lifestyle, but can also imply a lack of stability or a tendency towards vagrancy. |例句：She led a gypsylike existence, traveling from place to place with no fixed abode. 她过着吉普赛般的生活，四处游荡，没有固定的住所。
