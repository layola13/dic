commemoratives:/kəˈmɛməreɪtɪvz/| n. |纪念品，纪念物 |Items created to remember or celebrate a person, event, or anniversary.  They can be stamps, coins, medals, or other objects. |例句：The museum displayed a collection of commemorative stamps from the World War II era.  博物馆展出了二战时期的纪念邮票收藏。

例句：She received a commemorative medal for her years of service. 她获得了纪念奖章以表彰她多年的服务。


近义词：souvenirs, mementos, keepsakes |反义词：None specifically, but words implying forgetting or disregarding could be considered opposites. |词性 n.
