cannibalizing: /ˈkænɪbəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 蚕食；损耗；移用零件 | To use parts from one thing to repair or improve another; to exploit or destroy something for one's own advantage.  | The company is cannibalizing its own product line by releasing a cheaper, competing model.  这家公司通过发布一款更便宜的竞争产品来蚕食自己的产品线。 | exploit, undermine, deplete | enhance, support, preserve |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company cannibalizes its older products to fund new projects. = 公司蚕食其旧产品以资助新项目。
They are constantly cannibalizing their market share. = 他们不断蚕食自己的市场份额。


一般过去时:
The project cannibalized resources from other departments. = 该项目蚕食了其他部门的资源。
The storm cannibalized the coastal town's infrastructure. = 暴风雨蚕食了沿海城镇的基础设施。


现在完成时:
The new product has cannibalized sales of the old model. = 新产品已经蚕食了旧型号的销售额。
This strategy has cannibalized our profits. = 这一策略已经蚕食了我们的利润。


过去完成时:
The company had cannibalized its own market before realizing the mistake. = 公司在意识到错误之前就已经蚕食了自己的市场。
By the time the merger was completed, the larger company had already cannibalized the smaller one's market share. = 到合并完成时，大公司已经蚕食了小公司的市场份额。


将来时:
The new technology will cannibalize the market for traditional products. = 新技术将蚕食传统产品的市场。
They predict that the new app will cannibalize the existing ones. = 他们预测新应用程序将蚕食现有的应用程序。


虚拟语气:
If they released a cheaper version, it would cannibalize sales of the original. = 如果他们发布一个更便宜的版本，它将蚕食原版的销量。
I wish they hadn't cannibalized our resources for that project. = 我真希望他们没有为那个项目而蚕食我们的资源。


被动语态:
The market share was cannibalized by the competitor's new product. = 市场份额被竞争对手的新产品蚕食。
Resources were cannibalized from various departments to fund the project. = 资源被从各个部门抽调来为项目提供资金。


疑问句:
Is the new product cannibalizing sales of the older models? = 新产品是否正在蚕食旧型号的销量？
Will this strategy cannibalize our market share? = 这一策略会蚕食我们的市场份额吗？


否定句:
The company didn't intend to cannibalize its own product line. = 公司无意蚕食自己的产品线。
The new feature didn't cannibalize the old one's usage. = 新功能并没有蚕食旧功能的使用率。
