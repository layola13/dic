melodize:/ˈmɛlədaɪz/| vt.|使旋律化；使悦耳；谱曲|To make melodious; to sing or play a melody; to compose a melody.|He melodized the poem, turning it into a beautiful song. 他把这首诗谱成了优美的歌曲。|近义词：harmonize, tune,  vocalize|反义词：dissonance, cacophony
