damages:/ˈdæmɪdʒɪz/| n.|损害；赔偿金|Financial losses or physical harm resulting from an event or action.  Damages can refer to the harm itself or the monetary compensation awarded for it.|例句：The accident caused extensive damages to his car. 事故对他的汽车造成了巨大的损坏。

例句：The court awarded him substantial damages for his injuries. 法院因其受伤判给他巨额赔偿。

近义词：harm, injury, loss, impairment, compensation

反义词：repair, restoration, benefit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm damages the crops every year.  = 暴风雨每年都会损害庄稼。
This policy covers damages to your car. = 这项保单涵盖你汽车的损坏。


一般过去时:
The earthquake damaged many buildings. = 地震损坏了许多建筑物。
The accident damaged his reputation. = 那次事故损害了他的名誉。


现在完成时:
The company has paid damages to the victims. = 公司已经向受害者支付了赔偿金。
The flood has caused significant damages to the infrastructure. = 水灾对基础设施造成了巨大的破坏。


过去完成时:
They had already assessed the damages before the insurance adjuster arrived. = 在保险理赔员到达之前，他们已经评估了损失。
The factory had suffered extensive damages before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，工厂已经遭受了巨大的损失。


将来时:
The lawsuit will likely result in significant damages. = 这场诉讼很可能会导致巨额赔偿。
Further damages to the environment are expected. = 预计环境将进一步遭到破坏。


虚拟语气:
If the company had acted responsibly, it would not have incurred such damages. = 如果公司行事负责，就不会造成如此大的损失。
I wish the storm hadn't caused so much damages to my property. = 我真希望暴风雨没有对我的财产造成这么大的破坏。


被动语态:
The building was damaged by fire. = 这座建筑物被大火烧毁了。
Significant damages were caused by the hurricane. = 飓风造成了巨大的损失。


疑问句:
What damages were caused by the flood? = 水灾造成了哪些损失？
What are the damages we have to pay? = 我们必须支付多少赔偿金？


否定句:
The accident did not cause any damages. = 事故没有造成任何损失。
The investigation found that no damages were sustained. = 调查发现没有造成任何损失。
