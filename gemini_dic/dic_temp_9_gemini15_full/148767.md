overtwists:/ˌoʊvərˈtwɪsts/ | v. |过度扭曲；过度旋转|To twist or turn something excessively; to exaggerate or distort something beyond its reasonable limits.|例句：He overtwisted the wire, causing it to break. 他把电线拧得太紧，导致它断了。  例句：The media overtwisted the story, creating unnecessary panic. 媒体夸大了这个故事，造成了不必要的恐慌。|近义词：overrotate, distort, exaggerate|反义词：untwist, straighten, simplify|动词
