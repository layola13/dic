owning:/ˈoʊnɪŋ/ | v. |拥有；拥有权；承认；归属于 |The act of possessing something.  It can refer to physical possessions, rights, or responsibilities.  |例句：He owns a large house. 他拥有一栋大房子。


例句：She owns up to her mistakes. 她承认了自己的错误。


近义词：possessing, holding, claiming, admitting


反义词：losing, relinquishing, disowning


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He owns a car. = 他拥有一辆车。
They own a small business. = 他们拥有一家小企业。


一般过去时:
She owned a bookstore. = 她曾经拥有一家书店。
We owned the land for many years. = 我们拥有那块土地很多年了。


现在完成时:
I have owned this watch since 1990. = 我从1990年就拥有这只手表了。
They have owned the farm for generations. = 他们世世代代拥有这个农场。


过去完成时:
He had owned several businesses before he retired. = 他退休前拥有过几家企业。
She had owned the painting for decades. = 她拥有那幅画几十年了。


将来时:
We will own a yacht someday. = 我们总有一天会拥有一艘游艇。
They will own the company after the merger. = 合并后他们将拥有这家公司。


虚拟语气:
If I owned a yacht, I would sail around the world. = 如果我拥有一艘游艇，我会环游世界。
I wish I owned a bigger house. = 我希望我有一栋更大的房子。


被动语态:
The house is owned by my uncle. = 这房子是我叔叔拥有的。
The land was owned by the family for centuries. = 这块土地世世代代由这个家族拥有。


疑问句:
Do you own a pet? = 你养宠物吗？
Does he own that car? = 那辆车是他拥有的吗？


否定句:
I don't own a car. = 我没有车。
They didn't own the property. = 他们不拥有那处房产。


PHRASE->
owning up to = 承认
owning responsibility = 承担责任
joint owning = 共有
sole owning = 独有
owning shares = 持股
