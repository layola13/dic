cleaver:/ˈkliːvər/ | n. |肉刀，劈刀 | A cleaver is a heavy butcher's knife with a broad blade, used for chopping meat and bones.  | The butcher used a cleaver to chop the meat into smaller pieces.  屠夫用肉刀把肉剁成小块。 | hatchet, chopper |  knife (general term) | n.
