politic:/ˈpɒlətɪk/ /ˈpɑːlətɪk/| adj.|精明的；有策略的；明智的；政治上的|Showing a shrewd understanding and skillful management of politics;prudent and shrewd; political.|例句：It wasn't politic to criticize her in front of her boss. 在她老板面前批评她是不明智的。

例句：It was a politic decision to postpone the meeting until after the election. 推迟会议到选举之后是一个明智的决定。|近义词：shrewd, diplomatic, tactful, prudent|反义词：imprudent, tactless, indiscreet
