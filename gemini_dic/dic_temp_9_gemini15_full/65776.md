everyone's:/ˈɛvriwʌnz/| pron.|大家的，每个人的|  A possessive pronoun indicating belonging to everyone. It's a contraction of "everyone's" (everyone is/has).| Everyone's opinion matters in this decision.  大家的意见在这个决定中都很重要。|Everybody's, all people's|nobody's|pronoun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Everyone's happy with the result. = 大家对结果都很满意。
Everyone's working hard today. = 大家今天都很努力工作。


一般过去时:
Everyone's plans were disrupted by the storm. = 大家的计划都被暴风雨打乱了。
Everyone's mood improved after the good news. = 好消息传来后，大家的情绪都改善了。


现在完成时:
Everyone's finished their work. = 大家都完成了工作。
Everyone's seen that movie. = 大家都看过那部电影。


过去完成时:
Everyone's already eaten before the party started. = 派对开始前大家已经吃过了。
Everyone's already left by the time I arrived. = 我到的时候大家已经离开了。


将来时:
Everyone's going to the beach tomorrow. = 大家明天都要去海滩。
Everyone's going to be surprised by the announcement. = 大家都会对这个公告感到惊讶。


虚拟语气:
If everyone's honest, the problem will be solved easily. = 如果大家都诚实，问题就会很容易解决。
I wish everyone's health was better. = 我希望每个人的健康状况都更好。


被动语态:
Everyone's opinions were considered. = 大家的意见都被考虑到了。
Everyone's needs are being met. = 大家的需求都得到了满足。


疑问句:
Is everyone's homework completed? = 大家的作业都完成了吗？
Are everyone's bags packed? = 大家的包都收拾好了吗？


否定句:
Everyone's not happy with the changes. = 不是每个人都对这些改变感到满意。
Everyone's not going to the concert. = 不是每个人都会去音乐会。
