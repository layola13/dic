dismissions:/dɪsˈmɪʃənz/| n.|解雇；解散；驳回；忽视|The act of dismissing someone or something; the state of being dismissed.  It can refer to the dismissal of employees, the dismissal of a case in court, or the dismissal of an idea or suggestion.|例句：The company announced mass dismissions due to the economic downturn. =  由于经济低迷，该公司宣布大规模裁员。

例句：The judge's dismission of the charges was a relief to the defendant. =  法官驳回指控让被告松了一口气。

近义词：dismissals, discharges, releases, rejection

反义词：employments, hirings, acceptances, approvals
