/pleɪ/ | n. | 游戏，玩耍 |  A pleasurable activity or pastime; something done for fun or enjoyment.  | Example:  Children's play is essential for their development. 孩子们的游戏对他们的发展至关重要。|近义词：game, sport, amusement |反义词：work, labor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They play soccer every Saturday. = 他们每个星期六都踢足球。
We play cards after dinner. = 我们晚饭后打牌。

一般过去时:
She played the piano beautifully. = 她钢琴弹得真好。
He played football in high school. = 他高中时踢足球。

现在完成时:
I have played the violin for ten years. = 我拉小提琴十年了。
They have played this game many times. = 他们玩过很多次这个游戏了。

过去完成时:
We had played three games before it started raining. = 在开始下雨之前我们已经玩了三场比赛。
She had played tennis before she started running. = 在她开始跑步之前，她已经打过网球了。


将来时:
They will play basketball tomorrow. = 他们明天将打篮球。
We will play a board game this weekend. = 我们这个周末将玩一个棋盘游戏。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would play the guitar. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会弹吉他的。
I wish I could play the piano like you. = 我希望我能像你一样弹钢琴。

被动语态:
The game was played fairly. = 这场比赛打得很公平。
The music was played softly in the background. = 音乐在背景中轻轻地播放着。

疑问句:
Do you play any musical instruments? = 你会演奏任何乐器吗？
What games do they play? = 他们玩什么游戏？

否定句:
I don't play video games. = 我不玩电子游戏。
He didn't play well in the match. = 他在比赛中表现不好。


PHRASE->
at play = 在玩耍
in play = 在游戏中，有效
fair play = 公平竞争
play along = 跟着一起玩，配合
play it safe = 稳妥行事
