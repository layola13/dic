dehumanizes:/ˌdiːˈhjuːmənaɪz/| vt.|使失去人性；使没有人性；贬低人的尊严|To deprive of human qualities, personality, or individuality; to treat or regard as less than human.|例句：The factory work dehumanizes the workers, making them feel like mere cogs in a machine. 工厂的工作使工人失去了人性，让他们感觉自己只是机器上的齿轮。

例句：The harsh conditions of the prison dehumanize the inmates. 监狱的恶劣条件使囚犯失去了人性。


近义词：debase, degrade, depersonalize, brutalize|反义词：humanize, ennoble, elevate
