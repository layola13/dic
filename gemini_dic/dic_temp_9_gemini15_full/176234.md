re-cancels:/ˌriːˈkænsəlz/ | v. |重新取消；再次取消 | To cancel something that has already been canceled. This implies a second cancellation, often indicating a reversal of a previous decision. |例句：The airline re-canceled the flight due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，航空公司再次取消了航班。


The concert was re-canceled after the venue experienced a power outage. =  演出场地发生停电事故后，音乐会再次被取消。


近义词：countermand, revoke, annul |反义词：confirm, reinstate, approve |词性:动词
