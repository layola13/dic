rosefish: /ˈroʊzˌfɪʃ/ | n. |玫瑰鱼|A type of fish, usually referring to several species of the genus *Holocentrus*, characterized by their reddish or rose-colored bodies and large eyes. They are typically found in tropical and subtropical waters of the Atlantic, Indian, and Pacific Oceans.  They are popular in some cultures as a food fish. |例句：The rosefish is a beautiful fish with vibrant colors.  玫瑰鱼是一种颜色鲜艳的漂亮鱼类。|例句：We had grilled rosefish for dinner last night. 昨晚我们吃了烤玫瑰鱼。|近义词：squirrelfish, soldierfish |反义词：无
