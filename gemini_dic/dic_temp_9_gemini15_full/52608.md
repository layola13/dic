digestibility:/ˌdaɪdʒestəˈbɪləti/| n. |可消化性，易消化程度|The ease with which a food can be digested and absorbed by the body.  It depends on factors like the food's composition, cooking method, and individual digestive capacity.|例句：The digestibility of this food is quite high. 这食物的可消化性很高。|例句：We need to improve the digestibility of the infant formula. 我们需要提高婴儿配方奶粉的可消化性。|近义词：digestibility, assimilability|反义词：indigestibility
