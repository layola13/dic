frost:/frɒst/ /frɔːst/| n. |霜；严寒；结霜|A thin layer of ice formed on surfaces when the temperature falls below freezing.  Frost can also refer to a feeling of coldness or a depressing atmosphere. |例句：The car windows were covered in frost this morning.  今天早上汽车窗户上结了一层霜。
The frost on the pumpkin made it look beautiful. 南瓜上的霜让它看起来很漂亮。|近义词：freeze, ice, chill|反义词：heat, warmth, thaw|名词
