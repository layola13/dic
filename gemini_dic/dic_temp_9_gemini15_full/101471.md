iridaceous: /ˌɪrɪˈdeɪʃəs/ | adj. | 彩虹色的，像虹膜的 |  Having colors like those of the iris of the eye, often with a pearly or metallic sheen.  The word refers to the interplay of light and color creating an iridescent effect. |例句：The iridescence of the soap bubble fascinated the child.  肥皂泡的彩虹色泽让孩子着迷。

The iridescence of the butterfly's wings shimmered in the sunlight. 蝴蝶翅膀上彩虹般的光泽在阳光下闪耀。


近义词： iridescent, opalescent, pearly, nacreous | 反义词：dull, matte


词源: From Latin *iridaceus, from iris (iris, rainbow; also the iris of the eye).  The root word relates to the Greek word ἶρις (iris), meaning rainbow.  The suffix "-aceous" indicates "resembling" or "having the nature of."
