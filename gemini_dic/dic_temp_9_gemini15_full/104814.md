kebaya:/kəˈbaɪə/| n. |传统马来服装|A traditional blouse-like garment worn by women in Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore, and Brunei.  It is typically made of thin, often brightly coloured silk or cotton, and is often elaborately embroidered.  The style varies regionally and according to occasion.|She wore a beautiful kebaya to the wedding. 她穿着美丽的卡巴雅参加婚礼。|  sarong, baju kurung |  (无明确反义词)|名词
