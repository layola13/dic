harlock:/ˈhɑːrlɒk/| n. |野麦，毒麦 |A weed of the genus *Galium*, especially *Galium aparine*, which has prickly stems and leaves and is often found clinging to other plants.  It is also sometimes referred to as cleavers, goosegrass, or catchweed.|例句：The farmer struggled to remove the harlock from his wheat field.  = 农民努力地从他的小麦田中清除野麦。


The field was overrun with harlock, choking the other plants. = 田地里长满了野麦，窒息了其他植物。|近义词：cleavers, goosegrass, catchweed |反义词：None specifically.  (Antonyms would describe desirable plants rather than the weed itself.)|名词
