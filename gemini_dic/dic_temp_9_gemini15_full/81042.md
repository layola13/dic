graffiti:/ɡrəˈfiːti/| n.|涂鸦，壁画|Artwork or writing done on walls or other surfaces in public places.|The subway cars were covered in graffiti.|近义词：mural, tagging|反义词：cleanliness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist creates beautiful graffiti. = 艺术家创作美丽的涂鸦。
Graffiti covers the walls of the city. = 涂鸦覆盖着城市的墙壁。

一般过去时:
He painted graffiti on the building last night. = 他昨晚在建筑物上涂鸦。
They removed the graffiti from the bridge. = 他们清除了桥上的涂鸦。

现在完成时:
The city has spent a lot of money removing graffiti. = 城市已经花费了大量资金清除涂鸦。
They have added new graffiti to their collection. = 他们已经将新的涂鸦添加到他们的收藏中。

过去完成时:
The police had already seen the graffiti before they arrived. = 警察到达之前就已经看到了涂鸦。
The building had been covered in graffiti for years. = 这座建筑物多年来一直被涂鸦覆盖着。

将来时:
The city will clean the graffiti tomorrow. = 城市明天将清理涂鸦。
They will add more graffiti to the wall. = 他们会在墙上添加更多涂鸦。

虚拟语气:
If I could paint graffiti, I would create a mural. = 如果我能画涂鸦，我会创作一幅壁画。
I wish the city would remove the offensive graffiti. = 我希望城市能清除那些令人反感的涂鸦。

被动语态:
The graffiti was painted last night. = 涂鸦是昨晚画的。
The walls are being cleaned of graffiti. = 墙壁正在被清理涂鸦。

疑问句:
Is that graffiti art or vandalism? = 那是涂鸦艺术还是破坏行为？
Who painted the graffiti on the train? = 谁在火车上涂鸦的？

否定句:
That's not graffiti; it's a mural. = 那不是涂鸦；那是壁画。
He didn't paint the graffiti on the wall. = 他没有在墙上涂鸦。
