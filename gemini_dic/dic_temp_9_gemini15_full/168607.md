prociduous: /prəˈsɪdʒuəs/ | adj. | 早熟的；过早发展的 |  Prociduous refers to something that develops or matures prematurely.  It is often used in botany to describe plants that produce fruit or flowers earlier than usual.  | Example: The prociduous apples were harvested weeks ahead of schedule.  早熟的苹果比预定时间提前几周收获了。 | 近义词: premature, precocious | 反义词: late-developing, retarded
