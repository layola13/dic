referencer:/ˈrɛfərənsər/| n. |参考文献者；引证者；参考者 |A referencer is someone who provides references or citations in support of a claim or argument, or someone who consults references.  It can also refer to a tool or system that helps manage references.|例句：The research paper had a comprehensive list of referencers. 这篇研究论文有一个全面的参考文献列表。|近义词：citer, annotator, source |反义词：N/A
