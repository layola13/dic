redocketing: /ˌriːˈdɒkɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 再提交；重新提交 (案件等) | The act of resubmitting a case or other matter to a court or other authority for further consideration.  It often implies a previous attempt that was unsuccessful or incomplete. |  The lawyer decided to redocket the case after discovering new evidence. = 律师在发现新的证据后决定重新提交此案。

The defendant's appeal was re-docketed for a hearing next month. = 被告的上诉被重新安排在下个月听证。


近义词：resubmitting, refiling, reintroducing


反义词：dismissing, withdrawing


词性：动词
