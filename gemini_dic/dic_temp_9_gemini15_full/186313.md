rhombohedron:/ˌrɒmbəˈhiːdrən/| n. |菱面体|A three-dimensional figure with six faces that are parallelograms.  A rhombohedron is a six-sided figure where each face is a rhombus (a parallelogram with all sides equal in length). Unlike a cube, the angles between the faces of a rhombohedron are not necessarily right angles.  |The crystal structure displayed a rhombohedral shape.|近义词：parallelepiped|反义词：无
