nonpermissibility: /ˌnɒnpɜːmɪsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可允许性，不允许 | The state or quality of being impermissible; the fact of something not being allowed or permitted.  It often refers to an action, behavior, or situation that is prohibited by law, rule, or regulation. | Example: The nonpermissibility of smoking in public places is widely enforced. 公共场所禁止吸烟的规定得到广泛执行。  The nonpermissibility of unauthorized access to classified information is paramount. 未经授权访问机密信息是绝对不允许的。 | 近义词：prohibition, illegality, inadmissibility | 反义词：permissibility, allowance, authorization |  名词
