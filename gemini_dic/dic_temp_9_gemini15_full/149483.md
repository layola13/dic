padlocking: /ˈpædlɒkɪŋ/ | n. | 挂锁；上锁；用挂锁锁住 | The act of securing something with a padlock.  It can refer to the action of padlocking something or the state of being padlocked. | 例句：The gate was secured by padlocking it.  =  大门是用挂锁锁上的。

The heavy padlocking of the warehouse doors indicated a serious security breach. = 仓库大门上了厚重的锁，这表明发生了严重的安保事故。


近义词：locking, fastening, securing | 反义词：unlocking, opening, unfastening | 词性: 名词
