cubature:/ˈkjuːbətʃər/| n. |体积计算；容积；立体积|The mathematical determination of the volume of a solid figure. 例句：The architect's plans included detailed cubature calculations for the building's various components. 建筑师的图纸包括建筑物各个部件的详细体积计算。|近义词：volume calculation, volume measurement, capacity calculation|反义词：surface area calculation
