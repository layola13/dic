convivially:/kənˈvɪviəli/| adv.| 热情地；欢乐地；友好地 | In a convivial manner; in a merry and friendly way.  |例句：They greeted each other convivially.  他们热情地互相问候。|例句：The party was a convivial affair.  聚会非常热闹友好。|近义词：jovially, merrily, sociably |反义词：unsociably, sullenly, gloomily

词源:
From Latin *convivialis, from con- "together" + vivere "to live".  The word ultimately relates to the idea of living together in a festive and friendly manner.
