embossed:/ɛmˈbɔːst/| adj.| 浮雕的；压印的 | Raised or decorated with a design that is raised above the surface.  The design is created by pressing the surface into a mold. | 例句：The leather cover of the book was embossed with a golden design. 这本书的皮革封面印有金色图案。

例句：The company logo was embossed on every product.  公司的标志压印在每个产品上。


近义词：raised, impressed, relief


反义词：flat, plain, smooth


词性:形容词
