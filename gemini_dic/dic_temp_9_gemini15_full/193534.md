self-accused: /ˌsɛlf əˈkjuːzd/ | adj. | 自首的；自己承认有罪的 |  Describes someone who has admitted their guilt or wrongdoing, typically to authorities.  It implies a confession without external pressure. | Example: The self-accused murderer confessed to the crime in a detailed statement.  自首的凶手在详细的供词中承认了罪行。 | 近义词: self-confessed, admitting guilt, guilty | 反义词: innocent, denying guilt
