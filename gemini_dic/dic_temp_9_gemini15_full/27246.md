calories:/ˈkæləriːz/| n. |卡路里，热量 |A calorie is a unit of energy.  It's the amount of energy needed to raise the temperature of one gram of water by one degree Celsius.  Calories are used to measure the energy content of food and drinks.  |Example:  This candy bar contains 250 calories. 这块巧克力棒含有250卡路里。

The meal was high in calories. 这顿饭卡路里很高。|近义词：unit of energy, heat unit|反义词：N/A|名词
