slays:/sleɪz/| vt.| 杀死，击败，迷住|To kill (someone or something). To defeat or overcome decisively. To impress greatly; fascinate.|例句：The knight slayed the dragon.  骑士杀死了巨龙。

The team slayed their opponents.  这支队伍击败了他们的对手。


近义词：kill, defeat, conquer, impress, fascinate|反义词：save, revive, lose to, bore|动词
