penduline:/ˈpɛndjʊlɪn/ | adj. |悬挂的；摇摆的；下垂的 |  Hanging down; swinging; drooping.  The term often describes something hanging or swaying like a pendulum. It can also refer to the penduline tit, a small bird known for its hanging nest. | Example: The penduline branches brushed against the window.  悬挂的树枝拂过窗户。 The penduline earrings swung gently as she walked.  她走动时，下垂的耳环轻轻摇摆。 | 近义词: drooping, hanging, swaying, pendant | 反义词: erect, upright, stiff
