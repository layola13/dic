pillaring:/ˈpɪlərɪŋ/| v.|用柱子支撑；以…为基础；|The act of supporting or strengthening something, often metaphorically, like a pillar supports a building.  It can also refer to the process of establishing something firmly.|例句：They are pillaring the new bridge with massive concrete columns.  他们正在用巨大的混凝土柱支撑新桥。|近义词：supporting, underpinning, basing, grounding|反义词：undermining, weakening, destabilizing
