hotchpot:/ˈhɒtʃpɒt/| n.|大杂烩；混杂物|A hotchpotch is a mixture of different things, often in a disorganized or haphazard way. It can refer to a variety of things, from a dish of mixed ingredients to a collection of unrelated items.|例句：The garden was a hotchpotch of wildflowers and weeds.  花园里杂草和野花混杂在一起。|例句：His speech was a hotchpotch of unrelated ideas. 他的演讲是一堆毫不相关的想法。|近义词：hodgepodge, medley, jumble, mishmash|反义词：order, organization, system
