freewheelingness: /ˌfriːˈwiːlɪŋnəs/ | n. |自由自在；随意；无拘无束 | The state or quality of being freewheeling; characterized by spontaneity, lack of restraint, and a carefree attitude.  It suggests a sense of liberation and improvisation. |例句：The freewheelingness of his approach to life often surprised those who knew him.  他的生活方式的自由自在常常令认识他的人感到惊讶。

The band's performance was marked by a delightful freewheelingness.乐队的表演以其令人愉快的自由奔放而著称。


近义词：spontaneity, improvisation, unconventionality, informality, casualness |反义词：rigidity, formality, constraint, restraint, stiffness


词源:  Derived from "freewheel," which itself combines "free" (meaning "unrestricted") and "wheel" (referring to the action of a wheel turning freely without being connected to a power source).  The suffix "-ness" transforms the adjective "freewheeling" into a noun, signifying the quality or state of being freewheeling.
