overliberalized: /ˌoʊvərˈlɪbərəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度自由化的 | Excessively liberalized; characterized by excessive deregulation or relaxation of controls.  It implies a negative connotation, suggesting that the liberalization has gone too far, leading to undesirable consequences. | The overliberalized financial market led to a major economic crisis.  过度自由化的金融市场导致了严重的经济危机。 | 近义词：deregulated, unrestricted, uncontrolled | 反义词：strictly regulated, controlled, restricted |  形容词
