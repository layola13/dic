fierier:/ˈfaɪəriər/| adj.|更猛烈的；更热烈的；更火热的 | More intense in feeling or effect; showing more passion or anger.  It is the comparative form of "fiery".|例句：His fierier speeches often led to heated debates. 他更激烈的演讲常常导致激烈的辩论。


The debate became even fierier after the new evidence was presented. 新证据提交后，辩论变得更加激烈。|近义词：more fiery, more passionate, more intense, more fervent |反义词：calm, mild, subdued, tepid

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun is fierier today than yesterday.  = 今天太阳比昨天更猛烈。
His temper is fierier than his brother's. = 他的脾气比他兄弟更火爆。


一般过去时:
The argument was fierier than I expected. = 争论比我预期的更激烈。
Her performance was fierier than in previous years. = 她的表演比前几年更热烈。


现在完成时:
The competition has become fierier this year. = 今年的竞争变得更激烈了。
His love for his country has become fierier over time. = 他对祖国的爱随着时间的推移变得更加炽热。


过去完成时:
The situation had become fierier before the intervention. = 在干预之前，局势已经变得更加紧张。
The debate had been fierier than anyone remembered. = 那场辩论比任何人都记得的还要激烈。


将来时:
The race will be fierier than ever before. = 这次比赛将比以往任何时候都更加激烈。
The political climate will become fierier as the election nears. = 随着选举临近，政治气候将变得更加紧张。


虚拟语气:
If his temper were fierier, he would have reacted differently. = 如果他的脾气更暴躁一些，他的反应就会不同。
I wish the debate had been fierier; it was rather dull. = 我希望辩论更激烈一些；它相当沉闷。


被动语态:
The competition isn't made fierier by the presence of celebrities. = 名人的出现并没有使竞争更加激烈。
The atmosphere wasn't made fierier by the presence of the police. = 警方的出现并没有使气氛更加紧张。


疑问句:
Is the competition any fierier this year? = 今年的竞争更激烈了吗？
Is his love for music any fierier than before? = 他对音乐的热爱比以前更强烈了吗？


否定句:
The debate wasn't fierier than I'd anticipated. = 辩论没有比我预期的更激烈。
His reaction wasn't fierier than I expected, surprisingly. = 令人惊讶的是，他的反应并没有比我预期的更激烈。

词源:
From *fiery* + *-er* (comparative suffix).  "Fiery" itself originates from Old English *fyrig*, from *fyr* "fire".
