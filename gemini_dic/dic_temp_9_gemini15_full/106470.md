kowtow:/ˈkaʊtaʊ/| v.|叩头；卑躬屈膝 |To kneel and touch one's forehead to the ground as a sign of respect or submission, especially in some Asian cultures.  It often implies excessive subservience or flattery.| 例句：He kowtowed to the dictator to save his business. 他为了保住生意而对独裁者卑躬屈膝。

例句：They kowtowed to the demands of their powerful client. 他们屈服于他们强大的客户的要求。


近义词：prostrate, cringe, grovel, fawn, obsequiously, toady

反义词：defy, resist, stand up to, assert oneself


词性:动词
