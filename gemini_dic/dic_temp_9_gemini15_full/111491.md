loafed:/loʊfd/ | v. | 懒散地度过；闲逛；无所事事 | To spend time in a lazy, idle manner; to idle; to lounge. |例句：He loafed around the house all afternoon. 他整个下午都在家里懒散地度过。

例句：They loafed on the beach, soaking up the sun.  他们在海滩上懒洋洋地晒太阳。


近义词：idled, lounged, dawdled,  relaxed


反义词：hustled, worked, toiled,  busted


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He loafs around all day. = 他整天无所事事。
She loafs by the pool every afternoon. = 她每天下午都在泳池边闲逛。


一般过去时:
I loafed around the park yesterday. = 我昨天在公园里闲逛。
They loafed at home all weekend. = 他们整个周末都在家无所事事。


现在完成时:
He has loafed away the entire summer. = 他整个夏天都在懒散地度过。
She has loafed around since she lost her job. =自从她失业后，她就一直无所事事。


过去完成时:
I had loafed around for weeks before I finally got a job. = 我闲逛了好几个星期才最终找到工作。
They had loafed all morning before starting their project. = 他们在开始项目之前已经懒散地过了整个上午。


将来时:
I will loaf around tomorrow if the weather is nice. = 如果天气好，我明天就去闲逛。
They will loaf on the beach all day. = 他们会在海滩上整天懒洋洋地待着。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would loaf around more often. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常地去闲逛。
I wish I could loaf around all day every day. = 我希望我每天都能无所事事地待着。


被动语态:  (Loafed doesn't typically use the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Did he loaf around all day? = 他整天都在闲逛吗？
Were they loafing at the beach yesterday? = 他们昨天在海滩上闲逛吗？


否定句:
He didn't loaf around today. = 他今天没有闲逛。
They didn't loaf all weekend. = 他们整个周末没有懒散地度过。



PHRASE->
loaf around: 闲逛
loaf about: 闲逛
loaf it: 懒散地度过时光
loaf through:  懒散地度过（时间）
loaf on: 懒洋洋地躺在...上
