/ˌɪntərˈkəˈmɪʃən/ | n. | 互委；相互委托；（尤指国际组织间的）联合委员会 |  A joint commission or committee established by two or more organizations or countries to work together on a specific task or issue.  It often implies a reciprocal arrangement where each participating entity delegates members.  The term emphasizes cooperation and shared responsibility. | Example: The intercommission on environmental protection released a joint report on climate change.  （环境保护联合委员会发布了一份关于气候变化的联合报告。）


Example: The two nations formed an intercommission to address cross-border trade disputes. (两国成立了一个联合委员会来解决跨境贸易纠纷。)


近义词: joint commission,  cooperative committee, collaborative body


反义词:  N/A (The antonym would depend on the context,  e.g., unilateral action, independent investigation)


词性: 名词
