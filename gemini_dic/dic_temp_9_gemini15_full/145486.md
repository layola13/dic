overcleverness:/ˌoʊvərˈklɛvərnəs/ | n. | 过于聪明；自作聪明；卖弄聪明 | Excessive cleverness;  showing off one's intelligence in an annoying way.  It often implies a lack of judgment or tact. |例句：His overcleverness led him to make a series of disastrous decisions. 他的自作聪明导致他做出了一系列糟糕的决定。

例句：Her overcleverness in the debate alienated her from the audience. 她辩论中卖弄聪明的行为使她疏远了听众。


近义词：pretentiousness, smart-aleckyness, affected cleverness,  ostentation


反义词：simplicity, naiveté,  unpretentiousness


词性: 名词
