oversaucing: /ˌoʊvərˈsɔːsɪŋ/ | v. | 过度调味；加太多调味料 | To add too much sauce or seasoning to something, resulting in an overpowering or unpleasant taste. | Example:  He oversauced the pasta, making it too salty. 他把意面调味过重了，导致太咸了。 | 近义词: overseasoning, overspicing | 反义词: underseasoning
