internship:/ˈɪntɜːrnʃɪp/| n. |实习；实习期|A period of time when someone works for a company or organization in order to get experience of a particular type of work.  It's often a temporary position, and may be paid or unpaid. |例句：She completed a summer internship at a law firm. 她在一个律师事务所完成了暑期实习。|例句：He gained valuable experience during his internship. 他在实习期间获得了宝贵的经验。|近义词：training, apprenticeship, practicum |反义词：permanent employment, full-time job
