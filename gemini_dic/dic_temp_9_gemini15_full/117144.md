meaningfulness:/ˈmiːnɪŋfʊlnəs/ | n. |意义深远；有意义；重要性 | The significance or importance of something; the quality of being meaningful.  It refers to the extent to which something has purpose, value, or relevance. |例句：The meaningfulness of her work was evident in her dedication.  她的工作的意义深远体现在她的奉献精神上。


例句：He found meaningfulness in helping others. 他在帮助他人中找到了意义。


近义词：significance, importance, relevance, purposefulness, value


反义词：meaninglessness, pointlessness, insignificance, triviality


词性：名词
