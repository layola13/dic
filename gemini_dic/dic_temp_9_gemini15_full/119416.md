microfilms:/ˈmaɪkrəʊfɪlmz/| n. |缩微胶片；缩微胶卷|Reduced-size photographic film containing images of documents or other materials.  Microfilms are used for archiving and preserving documents due to their compact size and durability.|The library archives its historical documents on microfilms.|近义词：microfilm (单数), miniature film |反义词：full-size copies, original documents
