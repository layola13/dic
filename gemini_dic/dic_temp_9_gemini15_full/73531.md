foresightedly:/ˈfɔːrˌsaɪtɪdli/| adv.|有远见的；深谋远虑地|Acting with foresight; showing wisdom and careful planning for the future.  |例句：She foresightedly invested in technology stocks, securing her financial future. 她有远见地投资了科技股票，确保了自己的未来财务安全。

例句：The company foresightedly prepared for the economic downturn, avoiding significant losses.  这家公司有远见地为经济衰退做好了准备，避免了重大损失。


近义词：prudently, wisely, sagaciously, shrewdly

反义词：shortsightedly, imprudently, recklessly, thoughtlessly

词性: 副词
