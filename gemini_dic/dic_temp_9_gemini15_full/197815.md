shining:/ˈʃaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. |闪亮的，光亮的，杰出的 |  Shining describes something that emits or reflects light brightly. It can also refer to something excellent or outstanding.  |例句：The shining sun warmed our faces.  阳光温暖了我们的脸庞。

The shining star guided the lost sailors. 那颗闪亮的星星指引着迷失的船员。


近义词：bright, brilliant, lustrous, radiant, splendid | 反义词：dull, dim, dark, obscure


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sun is shining brightly today. = 今天阳光灿烂。
The star shines brilliantly in the night sky. = 星星在夜空中闪耀。


一般过去时:
The sun shone brightly yesterday. = 昨天阳光灿烂。
Her shoes shone under the stage lights. = 她的鞋子在舞台灯光下闪闪发光。


现在完成时:
The sun has been shining all day. = 太阳一整天都在照耀。
The diamond has been shining since it was polished.  = 这颗钻石自从被抛光以来就一直闪闪发光。


过去完成时:
The sun had been shining all morning before the rain started. = 下雨之前，太阳整个上午都在照耀。
The trophy had been shining when he first received it. = 他第一次收到奖杯时，奖杯闪闪发光。


将来时:
The sun will be shining tomorrow. = 明天阳光灿烂。
The newly polished car will be shining beautifully. = 这辆新抛光的车将会闪闪发光。


虚拟语气:
If the sun were shining, we would go to the beach. = 如果阳光灿烂，我们就去海滩。
I wish the stars were shining brighter tonight. = 我希望今晚星星更亮一些。


被动语态:
The car was being shined when I arrived. = 我到达时，汽车正在打蜡。
The silverware is being shined for the party. = 餐具正在为派对擦亮。


疑问句:
Is the sun shining today? = 今天阳光灿烂吗？
Are the stars shining tonight? = 今晚星星闪耀吗？


否定句:
The sun isn't shining today. = 今天阳光不灿烂。
The stars weren't shining last night. = 昨晚星星没有闪耀。


PHRASE->
shining example: 光辉的榜样
shining moment: 光辉时刻
shining star: 闪耀之星，杰出人物
shining light: 指路明灯
shining armor: 闪亮的盔甲 (比喻保护)
