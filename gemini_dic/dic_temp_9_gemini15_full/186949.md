ripple-soled: /ˈrɪpəlˌsoʊld/ | adj. | 波纹底的 | Having a sole with a ripple or wave-like pattern.  The term describes footwear with a sole that has a textured surface resembling small waves or ripples. This texture often provides better grip and traction. | Example:  Her ripple-soled sandals provided excellent grip on the wet rocks. =她的波纹底凉鞋在潮湿的岩石上提供了极好的抓地力。  The shoes, with their ripple-soled design, were perfect for hiking. = 这双鞋，凭借其波纹底设计，非常适合远足。| 近义词: textured-soled, patterned-soled | 反义词: smooth-soled, flat-soled |  形容词
