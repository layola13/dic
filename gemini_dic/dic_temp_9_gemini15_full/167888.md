princessdom: /ˈprɪnsɛsdəm/ | n. | 公主身份；公主的尊贵地位；公主统治时期 | The state or condition of being a princess; the privileges, power, or authority associated with being a princess; the reign or rule of a princess. |  例句：She enjoyed the luxuries and privileges of princessdom. 她享受着公主身份带来的奢华和特权。

例句：The princessdom was short-lived, ending with her marriage. 公主的地位很短暂，在她结婚后就结束了。


近义词：principality (if referring to a realm ruled by a princess), royalty, aristocracy


反义词：commoner, peasantry


词性：名词
