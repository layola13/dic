invadable: /ɪnˈveɪdəbl/ | adj. | 可入侵的，易受侵略的 | Capable of being invaded or attacked.  Something that is invadable is susceptible to intrusion, whether physical, metaphorical, or otherwise. |例句：The country's weak defenses made it invadable.  这个国家的薄弱防御使其容易遭受侵略。|例句：The open network is invadable by hackers.  这个开放的网络容易被黑客入侵。|近义词：vulnerable, susceptible, penetrable |反义词：impregnable, secure, invincible
