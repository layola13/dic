haughtiest:/ˈhɔːtiɪst/| adj.|最傲慢的，最自大的|Most arrogant and disdainful; showing a superior attitude.  It is the superlative form of "haughty."|例句：She treated the servants with the haughtiest disdain. 她以最傲慢的轻蔑态度对待仆人。

例句：His haughtiest demeanor alienated many potential allies. 他最傲慢的态度疏远了许多潜在的盟友。


近义词：supercilious, arrogant, disdainful, contemptuous, proud


反义词：humble, modest, meek, unassuming


词源：源自古法语 *hault,  意为“高，高贵的”，最终源自日耳曼语系。
