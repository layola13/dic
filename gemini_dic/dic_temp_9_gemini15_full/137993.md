noodleism:/ˈnuːdlɪzəm/| n. |糊涂主义；不认真；漫不经心|A casual and disorganized approach to life or work, characterized by a lack of seriousness or attention to detail.  It often implies a carefree attitude but can also be seen as irresponsible or lacking in ambition.|例句：His noodleism in handling the project led to missed deadlines and a lot of frustration.他的工作方式漫不经心，导致项目错过最后期限，并带来很多沮丧。|近义词：slackness, carelessness, nonchalance, apathy|反义词：diligence, meticulousness, seriousness, ambition
