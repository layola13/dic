pasteurizer:/ˈpæs.tʃə.raɪ.zər/| n. |巴氏杀菌器，巴氏消毒机 |A pasteurizer is a machine that uses heat to kill harmful bacteria in liquids, such as milk or juice, without significantly altering the taste or nutritional value.  |This dairy uses a high-capacity pasteurizer to process thousands of gallons of milk daily. 这家乳制品厂使用一台高容量巴氏杀菌器每天加工数千加仑的牛奶。| sterilizer, heat-treater |  (no direct antonym, but perhaps "unpasteurized product")|名词
