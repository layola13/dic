polytheize:/ˈpɑːliθiːz/ | vt. | 多神论化；奉为多神 | To believe in or worship many gods; to ascribe a plurality of gods to (a people or religion). |例句：Some scholars polytheize ancient Egyptian religion, while others argue for a more complex system of belief.  一些学者认为古埃及宗教是多神论的，而另一些学者则认为它是一个更复杂的信仰体系。

例句：The tendency to polytheize natural phenomena is common in many early cultures.  在许多早期文化中，将自然现象多神化是一种普遍现象。


近义词：deify (in the context of multiple gods),  paganize


反义词：monotheize


词性:动词
