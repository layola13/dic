morselling: /ˈmɔːrsəlɪŋ/ | n. | 分食；少量食物 | The act of giving or receiving food in small pieces; the process of eating small pieces of food.  | 例句：The morselling of the bread was a slow and deliberate process.  面包的分食是一个缓慢而谨慎的过程。  |近义词：portioning, dividing, distributing | 反义词：gorging, devouring
