rechecked:/ˌriːˈtʃekt/ | v. | 再检查；复查 | To check something again.  This implies a previous check has already been performed, and this is a second verification. |例句：I rechecked my calculations to ensure accuracy. 我再次检查了我的计算结果以确保准确性。

例句：The teacher rechecked the exam papers after a student pointed out a potential error.  老师在一名学生指出一个潜在错误后，再次检查了试卷。


近义词：verify, check again, review, double-check


反义词：neglect, overlook


词性：动词
