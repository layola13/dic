includes:/ɪnˈkluːdz/ | vt. |包含；包括；包含在内 |To contain or comprise (something) as part of a whole.  It also means to contain something within its limits or to encompass something.|例句：The price includes tax. 价格包含税费。

例句：The tour includes a visit to the museum.  这次旅行包括参观博物馆。


近义词：comprises, contains, encompasses, involves


反义词：excludes


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The package includes a free gift. = 这个包裹包含一份免费礼物。
This course includes grammar and vocabulary. = 这门课程包括语法和词汇。

一般过去时:
The meeting included a discussion of the budget. = 会议包括了关于预算的讨论。
The book included many interesting facts. = 这本书包含许多有趣的事实。

现在完成时:
The exhibition has included works by many famous artists. = 此展览已包含许多著名艺术家的作品。
My research has included interviews with several experts. = 我的研究包括对几位专家的采访。

过去完成时:
The report had included all the necessary data before submission. = 报告在提交之前已包含所有必要的数据。
She had included a personal note in the package. = 她已在包裹中放入了私人便条。

将来时:
The next update will include several bug fixes. = 下次更新将包含若干错误修复。
The conference will include a keynote speech by a leading expert. = 会议将包括一位主要专家的主题演讲。

虚拟语气:
If the plan included more details, it would be easier to understand. = 如果计划包含更多细节，就更容易理解。
I wish the contract included a clause about termination. = 我希望合同包含关于终止的条款。

被动语态:
Several new features are included in the latest version. = 最新版本包含了一些新功能。
All expenses were included in the final bill. = 所有费用都包含在最终账单中。

疑问句:
Does the price include shipping? = 价格包含运费吗？
What does the package include? = 包裹里包含什么？

否定句:
The agreement does not include any liability clauses. = 该协议不包含任何责任条款。
The initial proposal did not include a timeline. = 初始提案没有包含时间表。


PHRASE->
be included in = 包含在……中
include in = 包括在……中
included with = 附带……
to include = 包括
failure to include = 未包含
