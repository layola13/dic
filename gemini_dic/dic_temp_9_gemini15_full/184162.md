resized:/riˈsaɪzd/ | adj. | 调整大小的，已调整大小的 |  Describes something that has been changed in size, typically made smaller.  |  例句：The image was resized to fit the screen. 图片已调整大小以适合屏幕。

例句：The resized photo looked much better on the website.  调整大小后的照片在网站上看起来好多了。


近义词：scaled, re-sized, downsized (if smaller), enlarged (if larger) | 反义词：original-sized, unaltered | 词性：形容词
