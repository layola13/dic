reacquiring: /ˌriːəˈkwaɪərɪŋ/ | v. |重新获得，重新取得 | The act of acquiring something again after having lost it or given it up.  It emphasizes the process of regaining possession or control. | 例句：The company is focused on reacquiring its lost market share. 公司正专注于重新获得其失去的市场份额。

The museum is reacquiring a painting stolen decades ago.  博物馆正在重新获得几十年前被盗的一幅画。


近义词：regaining, recovering, repossessing, reacquishment

反义词：losing, relinquishing, surrendering

They are reacquiring lost property. = 他们正在重新获得丢失的财产。
The government is reacquiring land for a new park. = 政府正在重新获得土地用于建设新的公园。

过去完成时:
