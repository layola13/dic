nimbiferous:/nɪmˈbɪfərəs/| adj.|多雨的，阴雨的|Producing or bringing rain; rainy.  The word is formed from the Latin "nimbus" (rain cloud) and the suffix "-ferous" (bearing, producing).|例句：The nimbiferous clouds brought much-needed rain to the parched fields.  多雨的云层给干旱的田地带来了急需的雨水。The nimbiferous season led to widespread flooding in the lowlands.  多雨的季节导致低地地区洪水泛滥。|近义词：rainy, showery, pluvial|反义词：arid, dry, rainless
