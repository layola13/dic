capitatim:/ˌkæpɪˈteɪtɪm/| adv.|按人头；人均|By the head; per head; for each person.  The tax was levied capitatim.|这项税是按人头征收的。|近义词：per capita, individually|反义词：collectively, en masse


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  The usage is primarily in the context of distribution or calculation.

Example 1:
The funds were distributed capitatim among the workers. = 资金按人头分发给工人。

Example 2:
The poll tax was levied capitatim, regardless of income. = 人头税是按人头征收的，无论收入多少。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "capitatim".  Its usage is quite specific.
