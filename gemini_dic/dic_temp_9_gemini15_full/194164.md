self-interpretation:/ˌsɛlf ɪnˌtɜːrprɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |自我解释；自行诠释|The act of interpreting something for oneself; understanding or explaining something based on one's own perspective and understanding, without external input or guidance.| 例句：Her self-interpretation of the poem was quite different from the critic's analysis.  她的对这首诗的自我解释与评论家的分析大相径庭。

例句：His self-interpretation of the events led to a misunderstanding. 他对事件的自我解释导致了误解。|近义词：self-understanding, personal interpretation|反义词：external interpretation, objective analysis|名词
