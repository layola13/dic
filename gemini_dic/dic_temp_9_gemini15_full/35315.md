/hwɑːt/ | pron. | 什么 |  "What" is an interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something. It can refer to things, actions, qualities, or ideas. | What is your name?  = 你叫什么名字？ | which, who | nothing, nobody | pronoun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？

一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 聚会上发生了什么？

现在完成时:
What have you been doing? = 你一直在做什么？
What has she accomplished? = 她取得了什么成就？

过去完成时:
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had they already discussed? = 他们之前已经讨论了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天做什么？
What will the weather be like? = 天气会怎么样？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to expect. = 我希望我知道会发生什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built in that area? = 那个区域正在建造什么？

疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道该说什么。
I didn't understand what he meant. = 我不明白他的意思。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What's going on? = 发生了什么？
What if...? = 如果...怎么样？
What for? = 为什么？为了什么？
No matter what = 无论如何
