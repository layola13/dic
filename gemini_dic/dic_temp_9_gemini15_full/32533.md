chip-in: /ˈtʃɪp ɪn/ | v. | 凑钱；插话；贡献；参与 | To contribute a share of money or something else to a common fund or project; to interrupt a conversation by adding a comment or remark; to participate or contribute to a collective effort. | 例句：Let's chip in and buy her a present. = 我们一起凑钱给她买份礼物吧。  They chipped in with ideas for the new project. = 他们为新项目贡献了想法。

近义词：contribute, pitch in, join in, participate, interject | 反义词：withdraw, refrain, remain silent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We chip in for lunch every Friday. = 我们每个星期五一起凑钱买午餐。
He often chips in during meetings. = 他经常在会议上插话。

一般过去时:
They chipped in to help their friend move house. = 他们一起帮忙朋友搬家。
She chipped in with a witty remark. = 她插了一句俏皮话。

现在完成时:
We have chipped in enough money for the new equipment. = 我们已经凑够了买新设备的钱。
He has chipped in with some valuable insights. = 他贡献了一些宝贵的见解。

过去完成时:
They had already chipped in before I arrived. = 我到之前他们已经凑好钱了。
She had chipped in several times before he finally answered. = 在他最终回答之前，她已经插话好几次了。

将来时:
We will chip in to buy a gift for our boss. = 我们将一起凑钱买一份礼物给我们的老板。
I think he will chip in with his opinion later. = 我想他稍后会发表他的意见。

虚拟语气:
If everyone chipped in, we could afford the trip. = 如果每个人都凑钱，我们就能负担得起这次旅行。
I wish he hadn't chipped in so rudely. = 我真希望他不要那么粗鲁地插话。

被动语态:
Money was chipped in by the employees for the charity event. = 员工们为慈善活动捐款。 (Note: Passive voice is less common with "chip in")

疑问句:
Will you chip in for the pizza? = 你会一起买披萨吗？
Did anyone chip in with a solution to the problem? = 有人提出解决这个问题的方法吗？

否定句:
He didn't chip in any money for the party. = 他没有为聚会捐钱。
She didn't chip in during the discussion. = 她在讨论中没有插话。


PHRASE->
chip in with: 贡献；提供
chip in for: 凑钱购买
chip in to: 共同努力；一起做
chip in a few ideas: 提出一些想法
chip in and help: 帮忙；一起帮忙
