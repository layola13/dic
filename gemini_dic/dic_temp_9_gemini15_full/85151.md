hardship:/ˈhɑːrdʃɪp/ | n. |艰难，困苦，苦难 | Suffering or difficulty; something that causes suffering or difficulty.  |例句：The family faced many hardships during the war.  这个家庭在战争期间面临许多苦难。

例句：He endured great hardship to achieve his goal. 他为了实现目标忍受了巨大的苦难。


近义词：difficulty, adversity, trouble, suffering, privation

反义词：ease, comfort, luxury, prosperity


USAGE->

Hardship affects many people in developing countries. = 贫困国家很多人受苦难影响。
