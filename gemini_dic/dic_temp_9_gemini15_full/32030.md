cherish: /ˈtʃerɪʃ/ | vt. |珍爱；珍惜；爱护 |To hold dear; to value highly; to protect lovingly.  |例句：I cherish the memory of our time together. 我珍藏着我们在一起的时光的回忆。
例句：She cherishes her friendship with him. 她珍视与他的友谊。|近义词：treasure, value, prize, hold dear |反义词：neglect, despise, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I cherish my family. = 我珍爱我的家人。
We cherish every moment together. = 我们珍惜在一起的每一刻。

一般过去时:
She cherished the antique doll her grandmother gave her. = 她珍藏着祖母送给她的那个古董娃娃。
He cherished the hope of seeing his son again. = 他珍藏着再次见到儿子的希望。

现在完成时:
I have cherished this photograph for years. = 我多年来一直珍藏着这张照片。
They have cherished their privacy since moving to the countryside. = 他们自从搬到乡下后就一直珍视他们的隐私。

过去完成时:
She had cherished the belief that she would succeed. = 她一直坚信自己会成功。
He had cherished the memory of his childhood home. = 他一直珍藏着童年家园的记忆。

将来时:
I will cherish this experience forever. = 我将永远珍惜这段经历。
We will cherish the time we spend with our friends. = 我们将珍惜与朋友们一起度过的时光。

虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have cherished those moments more. = 如果我知道，我会更珍惜那些时刻。
I wish I had cherished my time with my grandfather more. = 我真希望我当时更珍惜与祖父在一起的时光。

被动语态:  (Cherish is rarely used passively)  This memory should be cherished. = 这段记忆应该被珍惜。(被动语态使用较少)
These moments will be cherished by us forever. = 这些时刻将被我们永远珍惜。(被动语态使用较少)


疑问句:  (Cherish is rarely used in questions directly) Do you cherish your freedom? = 你珍惜你的自由吗？(疑问句使用较少)
Does he cherish his family? = 他珍爱他的家人吗？(疑问句使用较少)

否定句:
I don't cherish the past. = 我并不珍惜过去。
They didn't cherish their opportunities. = 他们没有珍惜他们的机会。


PHRASE->
cherish a hope = 怀抱希望
cherish a memory = 珍藏记忆
cherish the illusion = 痴心妄想
cherish an ambition = 怀有抱负
cherish a dream = 珍藏梦想
