hyperconscientious: /ˌhaɪpərˌkɒnʃiˈenʃəs/| adj. |过分谨慎的；非常小心谨慎的| Extremely conscientious; scrupulous to an excessive degree.  This suggests a level of conscientiousness that is not only thorough but also potentially detrimental due to its excess. It implies an overwhelming concern with detail and potential negative consequences, often leading to anxiety and inefficiency.|例句：He was hyperconscientious about his work, double-checking everything multiple times, which often delayed his projects. 他对自己的工作过于谨慎，反复检查每一件事，这常常会延误他的项目。


例句：Her hyperconscientiousness prevented her from relaxing and enjoying life; she was constantly worried about making mistakes. 她的过分谨慎让她无法放松身心，享受生活；她总是担心犯错。


近义词：scrupulous, meticulous, painstaking, fastidious, overcautious


反义词：careless, negligent, reckless, lax, indifferent


词性:形容词
