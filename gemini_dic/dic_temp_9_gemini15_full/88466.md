histologist:/hɪˈstɑːlədʒɪst/| n.|组织学家|A histologist is a scientist who specializes in the study of tissues, their structure, and function.  Histologists use microscopes and various staining techniques to examine tissue samples and diagnose diseases.|例句：The histologist examined the biopsy sample under the microscope to determine the type of tissue.|近义词：tissue specialist, pathologist (if focusing on disease)|反义词: N/A
