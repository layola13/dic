nonmelting: /ˌnɒnˈmɛltɪŋ/ | adj. | 不熔化的；不融化的 | Not melting; resistant to melting.  Describes a substance that does not melt under normal conditions or within a specified temperature range. | The nonmelting ice cream was a mystery to the scientists.  这不会融化的冰淇淋让科学家们感到困惑。|  unmelting, unfused | melting, fusible


USAGE->
This word is an adjective and therefore does not conjugate through tenses like verbs.  It describes a state of being.  However, we can use it in various sentence structures:


Affirmative Sentences:
The nonmelting substance remained solid even at high temperatures.  即使在高温下，这种不熔化的物质也保持固体状态。
The experiment showed the nonmelting properties of the new material.  实验显示了这种新材料的不熔化特性。


Negative Sentences:
The ice cream was not nonmelting; it melted quickly in the sun.  冰淇淋并非不融化；它在阳光下很快就融化了。
This material is not nonmelting under extreme heat.这种材料在极端高温下不会不融化。


Interrogative Sentences:
Is the wax nonmelting?  这蜡是不融化的吗？
Is the core of the planet nonmelting?  地球的核心是不熔化的吗？
