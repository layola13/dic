relubricate:/ˌriˈluːbrɪkeɪt/| vt.|重新润滑|To lubricate again or anew.|The mechanic had to relubricate the engine after the oil leak.|近义词：reoil, lubricate again|反义词：dry out


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They relubricate the machinery every month. = 他们每月都会重新润滑机器。
The technician relubricates the equipment regularly. = 技术人员定期对设备进行重新润滑。

一般过去时:
The engineer relubricated the bearings after the inspection. = 检查后，工程师重新润滑了轴承。
We relubricated the bicycle chain before the long ride. = 长途骑行前，我们重新润滑了自行车链条。

现在完成时:
They have relubricated the engine twice this year. = 今年他们已经两次对发动机进行了重新润滑。
She has relubricated the hinges to prevent squeaking. = 她已经重新润滑了铰链以防止吱吱作响。

过去完成时:
The mechanic had relubricated the car's parts before the test drive. = 技师在试驾前已经重新润滑了汽车的零件。
They had relubricated the machinery before the power outage. = 停电前，他们已经对机器进行了重新润滑。

将来时:
We will relubricate the equipment before the next production run. = 我们将在下一次生产运行前重新润滑设备。
They will relubricate the engine after the race. = 比赛结束后，他们将重新润滑发动机。

虚拟语气:
If the machine was making noise, I would relubricate it immediately. = 如果机器发出噪音，我会立即重新润滑它。
I wish I had relubricated the hinges earlier to prevent rust. = 我希望我早点重新润滑铰链以防止生锈。

被动语态:
The engine was relubricated by the mechanic. = 发动机由技师重新润滑。
The machine needs to be relubricated. = 机器需要重新润滑。

疑问句:
Have you relubricated the machine yet? = 你已经重新润滑机器了吗？
Should we relubricate the gears now? = 我们现在应该重新润滑齿轮吗？

否定句:
They did not relubricate the equipment as scheduled. = 他们没有按照计划重新润滑设备。
We haven't relubricated the hinges for a long time. = 我们很长时间没有重新润滑铰链了。


PHRASE->

词源:
re- (again) + lubricate (to apply lubricant to)
