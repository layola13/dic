nongeometric:/ˌnɒnˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪk/| adj. |非几何的|Not relating to or involving geometry; not geometric.  The term often describes shapes, patterns, or designs that lack the regularity or precision of geometric forms.  It implies irregularity, randomness, or a lack of mathematical order.|例句：The artist's style is characterized by nongeometric abstract shapes.  这位艺术家的风格以非几何抽象形状为特征。

例句：The nongeometric design of the building made it stand out from the surrounding architecture. 这栋建筑物的非几何设计使其在周围建筑中脱颖而出。


近义词：irregular, asymmetrical, amorphous, formless, unstructured

反义词：geometric, symmetrical, regular, structured

词性：形容词
