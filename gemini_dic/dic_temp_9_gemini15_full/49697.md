demobbed:/dɪˈmɒbd/ | v. | 退伍；复员 | To be demobbed means to be discharged from military service, typically after a war or period of service.  It implies a return to civilian life. | He was demobbed in 1946 after serving in the war. 他在战争结束后于1946年复员。  |  The soldiers were finally demobbed and returned home.  士兵们最终复员并返回家园。 | discharged, released, mustered out | enlisted, recruited | 动词


USAGE->

He demobs next month. = 他下个月退伍。

过去完成时:
