meetups:/ˈmiːtʌps/| n.|聚会，见面会|Informal gatherings of people with shared interests, often organized online.|例句：I'm looking forward to the upcoming tech meetups. 我期待即将到来的科技聚会。|近义词：gatherings, meetings, assemblies|反义词：separations, partings


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The meetup group meets every Tuesday. = 这个聚会小组每周二都会见面。
We often have meetups at the local coffee shop. = 我们经常在当地的咖啡店聚会。

一般过去时:
The meetup was very successful last night. = 昨天的聚会非常成功。
They had a meetup to discuss their project. = 他们举行了一次聚会来讨论他们的项目。

现在完成时:
I have attended several meetups this year. = 我今年已经参加过几次聚会了。
They have organized many successful meetups in the past. = 他们过去组织过许多成功的聚会。

过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, the meetup had already started. = 当我到达时，聚会已经开始了。
They had planned the meetup for weeks before it happened. = 他们在那次聚会发生前的几周就做了计划。

将来时:
We will have a meetup next week to discuss the new plan. = 我们下周将举行一次聚会来讨论新计划。
The meetup will be held at the community center. = 聚会将在社区中心举行。

虚拟语气:
If I had known about the meetup, I would have attended. = 如果我知道这个聚会，我就去了。
I wish I had been able to attend the meetup last night. = 我真希望昨晚能参加那个聚会。

被动语态:
The meetup was organized by a group of volunteers. = 这个聚会是由一群志愿者组织的。
The date of the meetup was changed due to unforeseen circumstances. = 聚会的日期由于不可预见的情况而被更改。

疑问句:
Is there a meetup scheduled for next month? = 下个月有安排聚会吗？
What time does the meetup start? = 聚会几点开始？

否定句:
There wasn't a meetup this week. = 本周没有聚会。
I couldn't attend the meetup because I was sick. = 我因为生病而不能参加聚会。
