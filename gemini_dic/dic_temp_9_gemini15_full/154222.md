periphrases:/ˈpɛrɪfreɪzɪz/| n.| 迂回说法；绕圈子说话；转述；解释；释义 |The use of more words than necessary to express something; circumlocution.  It often involves substituting a descriptive phrase for a single word. |例句：His periphrases made the simple instructions confusing.  他的迂回说法使简单的指示变得令人困惑。  The author's periphrases obscured the central argument. 作者的迂回说法模糊了核心论点。|近义词：circumlocution, paraphrase, euphemism|反义词：conciseness, brevity|名词
