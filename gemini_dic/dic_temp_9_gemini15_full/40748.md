conveys:/kənˈveɪz/ | vt. | 传达；表达；运送 | To communicate or express something, especially information, ideas, or feelings.  It can also mean to transport or carry something from one place to another. | 例句：His words conveyed a sense of urgency.  他的话传达了一种紧迫感。

The truck conveys goods across the country. 这辆卡车在全国各地运送货物。|近义词：communicate, express, transmit, transport | 反义词：conceal, withhold, suppress | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conveys his ideas clearly. = 他清晰地表达了他的想法。
The bus conveys passengers to the city center. = 公共汽车把乘客送到市中心。


一般过去时:
She conveyed her condolences to the grieving family. = 她向悲伤的家庭表达了慰问。
The messenger conveyed the urgent message. = 信使传达了紧急信息。


现在完成时:
The artist has conveyed the beauty of nature in his paintings. = 艺术家在他的绘画中传达了大自然的美丽。
The company has conveyed all the necessary documents. = 公司已经传送了所有必要的文档。


过去完成时:
He had conveyed his concerns to the manager before the meeting. = 他在会议前已向经理表达了他的担忧。
The train had conveyed thousands of passengers before it broke down. = 火车在故障前已运送了数千名乘客。


将来时:
The diplomat will convey the message to the other country's leader. = 外交官将把信息传达给另一个国家的领导人。
The ship will convey the cargo to the port. = 船舶将把货物运送到港口。


虚拟语气:
If he had conveyed his feelings honestly, things might have been different. = 如果他诚实地表达了自己的感受，事情可能会有所不同。
I wish I had conveyed my gratitude more effectively. = 我希望我更有效地表达了我的感激之情。


被动语态:
The message was conveyed effectively. = 信息得到了有效的传达。
The goods were conveyed by truck. = 货物由卡车运送。


疑问句:
How does the author convey the theme of the novel? = 作者如何传达小说的主题？
How will the supplies be conveyed to the remote village? = 如何将物资运送到偏远的村庄？


否定句:
He failed to convey his meaning properly. = 他未能恰当地表达他的意思。
The system did not convey the data accurately. = 系统没有准确地传送数据。


PHRASE->
convey a message = 传达信息
convey an impression = 给人留下印象
convey one's feelings = 表达感情
convey meaning = 传达意义
convey information = 传递信息
