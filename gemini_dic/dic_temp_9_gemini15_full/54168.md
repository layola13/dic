disentitle:/dɪsɪnˈtaɪtl/ | vt. |取消…的权利；剥夺…的资格 | To deprive someone of a right or title.  |例句：The court ruling disentitled him from inheriting the property. 法院的裁决剥夺了他继承财产的权利。|例句：His criminal record disentitled him from holding public office. 他的犯罪记录使他失去了担任公职的资格。|近义词：deprive, disqualify, divest |反义词：entitle, qualify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company disentitles employees from using company phones for personal calls. = 公司禁止员工使用公司电话进行私人通话。
This policy disentitles students from receiving financial aid. =这项政策使学生丧失获得经济援助的资格。


一般过去时:

The accident disentitled him from driving for a year. = 这场事故使他一年内丧失了驾驶资格。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

The company didn't disentitle him from attending the conference. = 公司并没有取消他参加会议的资格。
