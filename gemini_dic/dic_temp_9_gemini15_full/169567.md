prophesier:/ˈprɒfɪsaɪər/ | n. |预言家，预言者 | A person who prophesies; one who claims to foretell future events.  |The ancient prophesier foretold the coming of a great king.  古代预言家预言一位伟大的国王即将到来。 | seer, prophet, oracle | skeptic, doubter | 名词

The prophesier speaks to the crowds. = 预言家对人群说话。
He prophesies the future every Sunday. = 他每个星期天都预言未来。
