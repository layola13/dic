hears:/hɪrz/ | v. |听见，听说|To perceive with the ear; to receive by the ear.  It implies a passive reception of sound, often without conscious effort. |例句：I heard a bird singing. 我听到一只鸟在唱歌。
He heard the news on the radio. 他在收音机里听到了这个消息。|近义词：listen to, overhear, perceive |反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hears the birds chirping every morning. 他每天早上都听到鸟叫。
She hears the waves crashing against the shore. 她听到海浪拍打海岸的声音。

一般过去时:
I heard a strange noise last night. 我昨晚听到奇怪的声音。
They heard the announcement over the loudspeaker. 他们通过扩音器听到了通知。

现在完成时:
I have heard that song many times. 我听过那首歌很多次了。
She has heard good things about the new restaurant. 她听说那家新餐馆不错。

过去完成时:
Before the storm, we had heard thunder in the distance.暴风雨来临之前，我们听到远处传来雷声。
He had heard rumors about the company's financial troubles. 他听说了关于公司财务问题的谣言。

将来时:
I will hear from my lawyer soon. 我很快就会收到律师的消息。
You will hear the results of the test next week. 你下周会听到考试的结果。

虚拟语气:
If I heard him correctly, he said he was leaving. 如果我听到的没错，他说他要离开了。
I wish I hadn't heard that gossip. 我真希望我没有听到那个八卦。

被动语态:
The music was heard throughout the building. 音乐声传遍了整栋楼。
His voice was barely heard above the noise. 他的声音几乎被噪音淹没了。

疑问句:
Did you hear what I said? 你听到我说的话了吗？
What did you hear at the concert? 你在音乐会上听到了什么？

否定句:
I didn't hear the phone ring. 我没有听到电话铃声。
They didn't hear a word he said. 他们一句话也没听到他说。
