riverside:/ˈrɪvərsaɪd/| n. adj.|河边；河畔；河滨的|The area near or along the banks of a river.  Riverside is often used to describe a location, a street, a park, or even a type of view.|例句：We had a picnic by the riverside. 我们在河边野餐。

例句：The riverside path is perfect for a leisurely stroll. 河边的路很适合悠闲漫步。


近义词：riverbank, waterfront, riparian

反义词：inland, upland

词性:名词，形容词
