dolmen:/ˈdəʊlmən/| n.|巨石墓|A dolmen is a prehistoric structure consisting of a large flat stone supported by two or more upright stones, forming a kind of table.  It is often part of a larger megalithic site and is believed to have been used as a burial chamber or a ritual site. |例句：The ancient dolmen stood as a testament to the skill and ingenuity of its builders.  古老的巨石墓证明了建造者的技巧和独创性。

例句：  Archaeologists discovered several artifacts inside the dolmen. 考古学家在巨石墓内发现了许多文物。


近义词：megalith, burial chamber |反义词：None.
