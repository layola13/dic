laser: /ˈleɪzər/ | n. |激光，激光器 | A device that amplifies light to produce a very intense, narrow beam.  Lasers are used in a wide variety of applications, from surgery to barcode scanners. |Example: The surgeon used a laser to remove the tumor.  外科医生用激光去除肿瘤。|近义词：laser beam, light beam |反义词：None
