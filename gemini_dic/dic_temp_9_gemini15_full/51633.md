devergency:/dɪˈvɜːrdʒənsi/ | n. |差异；分歧；背离 | Divergence refers to the act of separating or moving away from a common point or path. It implies a difference or disagreement, often in opinion, direction, or character.  The term often highlights a gradual process of separating rather than a sudden split. | Example: There is a significant devergency of opinion on the best course of action.  意见上存在很大的分歧。|近义词：divergence, difference, discrepancy, variation |反义词：convergence, agreement, conformity |名词
