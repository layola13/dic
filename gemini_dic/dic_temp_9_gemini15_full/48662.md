defensive:/dɪˈfɛnsɪv/ | adj. | 防御的；戒备的；自卫的 | Relating to or intended for defense; protective.  Characterized by or exhibiting defense or protection. |例句：He adopted a defensive posture when questioned.  他被质问时采取了防御姿态。

例句：The army built defensive fortifications along the border.  军队沿边境修建了防御工事。


近义词：protective, guarding, shielding, wary | 反义词：offensive, aggressive, attacking
