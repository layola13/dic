distraint:/daɪˈstreɪnt/| n.|扣押；查封；没收|The seizure of property by legal authority to satisfy a debt or judgment.  It's a legal process where assets are taken to settle a debt. |例句：The landlord obtained a distraint on the tenant's furniture for unpaid rent. = 房东因租金未付而对租户的家具进行了扣押。

例句：The court ordered a distraint of the company's assets to cover outstanding debts. = 法院命令扣押公司资产以偿还未清偿债务。


近义词：seizure, attachment, levy, forfeiture|反义词：release, restoration, relinquishment|名词
