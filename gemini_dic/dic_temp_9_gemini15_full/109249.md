leitmotivs: /ˈlaɪtməʊtivz/ | n. | 主导动机；反复出现的主题 |  A leitmotiv is a recurrent theme throughout a musical or literary composition, associated with a particular person, idea, or situation.  It serves to unify the work and to emphasize its meaning.  In literature, it can be a recurring image, symbol, phrase, or idea that develops the theme. In music, it is a short, constantly recurring melodic phrase. |


例句：

The novel's leitmotiv is the destructive nature of unchecked ambition.  这部小说的主导动机是不受约束的野心具有破坏性。

Wagner's operas are famous for their use of leitmotivs to represent different characters and emotions.  瓦格纳的歌剧以使用主导动机来代表不同的角色和情感而闻名。


近义词：theme, motif, recurring idea, dominant idea

反义词：inconsistency, digression


词性：名词
