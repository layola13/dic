disruptors:/dɪsˈrʌptərz/| n. |颠覆者；扰乱因素|People or things that cause significant disruption or change, often in a positive way, by challenging established systems or norms.  They introduce innovation and often lead to significant transformation. |例句：Tech disruptors are changing the way we live and work. 科技颠覆者正在改变我们的生活和工作方式。


例句：The rise of e-commerce has been a major disruptor in the retail industry. 电子商务的兴起是零售业的一个主要颠覆因素。


近义词：innovators, revolutionaries, game-changers, transformers


反义词：conservatives, traditionalists, status quo maintainers


词性: 名词
