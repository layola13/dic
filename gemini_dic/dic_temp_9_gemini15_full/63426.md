entrapping: /ɪnˈtræpɪŋ/ | adj. v. | 诱捕的；使落入陷阱的 |  The act of catching someone in a trap, either literally or figuratively. It implies a sense of deception or trickery.  |

例句：

The police used an entrapping tactic to catch the thief. = 警方用诱捕策略抓住了小偷。

His entrapping smile hid a cruel heart. = 他那诱人的笑容掩盖了一颗残酷的心。


近义词：ensnaring, trapping, ensnaring, alluring, captivating

反义词：releasing, freeing, liberating

词性：形容词，动词
