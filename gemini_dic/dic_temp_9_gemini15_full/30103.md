cecidomyia: /ˌsɛsɪˈdoʊmiə/ | n. | 瘿蚊属 | A genus of gall midges, small flies whose larvae form galls on plants.  Cecidomyias are known for their diverse host plant range and the variety of gall types they induce.  | Example: The cecidomyia species infesting the apple trees caused significant damage to the fruit.  苹果树上的瘿蚊种类导致果实严重受损。| 近义词: gall midge (更通用的说法) | 反义词:  无直接反义词
