liquidize:/ˈlɪkwɪdaɪz/| vt.|液化；使成液体|To make something into a liquid.  This typically involves breaking down solid matter into a liquid state, often through mechanical means like blending or using a high-speed blender.|例句：He liquidized the fruit to make a smoothie. 他把水果液化做成冰沙。|例句：The company decided to liquidize its assets to pay off its debts.  公司决定变卖资产以偿还债务。|近义词：blend, puree, homogenize|反义词：solidify, congeal|动词
