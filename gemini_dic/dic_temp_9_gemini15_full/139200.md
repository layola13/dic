objective:/əbˈdʒektɪv/| adj.|客观的，目标；|adj.  Not influenced by personal feelings or opinions in considering and representing facts.  A statement or description of facts is objective if it is not influenced by personal opinions or feelings.  As a noun, it refers to a thing aimed at or sought; a goal. |例句：The judge must remain objective when delivering a verdict.  法官在宣判时必须保持客观。  Her objective is to become a doctor. 她的目标是成为一名医生。|近义词：impartial, unbiased, neutral, goal, aim |反义词：subjective, biased, partial
