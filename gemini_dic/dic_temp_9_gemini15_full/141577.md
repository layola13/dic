oppressed:/əˈprɛst/ | adj. | 被压迫的；受压制的 |  suffering from oppression; burdened by cruel or unjust power.  |The oppressed people fought for their freedom. 被压迫的人民为争取自由而斗争。|近义词：subjugated, suppressed, downtrodden | 反义词：free, liberated, emancipated | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government oppresses its people. = 政府压迫人民。
Many people feel oppressed by the high cost of living. = 许多人感到生活成本高昂给他们带来了压力。


一般过去时:
The dictator oppressed his people for many years. = 独裁者多年来压迫人民。
They were oppressed by the harsh conditions. = 他们被恶劣的条件所压迫。


现在完成时:
The people have been oppressed for generations. = 人们世世代代都受到压迫。
She feels oppressed by the weight of expectation. = 她感到期望的重担让她喘不过气。


过去完成时:
The workers had been oppressed for decades before the revolution. = 工人们在革命前几十年一直受到压迫。
They had long been oppressed before they found the courage to fight back. = 在他们找到勇气反击之前，他们已经长期受到压迫。


将来时:
If the current policies continue, more people will be oppressed. = 如果目前的政策继续下去，更多的人将受到压迫。
The regime will likely continue to oppress its citizens. = 该政权可能会继续压迫其公民。


虚拟语气:
If the people were not oppressed, they would live peacefully. = 如果人民没有受到压迫，他们将和平地生活。
I wish the oppressed people could find freedom soon. = 我希望受压迫的人民能够尽快获得自由。


被动语态:
The people were oppressed by the ruling class. = 人民被统治阶级压迫。
The rights of the minority were oppressed by the majority. = 少数人的权利受到多数人的压迫。


疑问句:
Are the people oppressed in that country? = 那个国家的人民受到压迫吗？
Were the workers oppressed during the industrial revolution? = 工人在工业革命期间受到压迫吗？


否定句:
The government does not oppress its citizens. = 政府没有压迫其公民。
They were not oppressed, but rather encouraged to participate. = 他们没有受到压迫，而是被鼓励参与。


PHRASE->
oppressed minority 受压迫的少数民族

oppressed masses 被压迫的大众
feeling oppressed 感到压抑
systematically oppressed 系统性压迫
