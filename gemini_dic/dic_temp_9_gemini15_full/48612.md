defectives:/dɪˈfektɪvz/| n. adj.|有缺陷的；次品；缺陷者|Defectives are items or individuals that have flaws or imperfections.  They may be faulty products, people with disabilities, or anything that doesn't meet a standard.|例句：The factory recalled all the defective products.  这家工厂召回了所有有缺陷的产品。  The school provides special programs for students with learning defectives. 学校为有学习缺陷的学生提供特殊课程。|近义词：flawed, imperfect, faulty, deficient |反义词：perfect, flawless, sound
