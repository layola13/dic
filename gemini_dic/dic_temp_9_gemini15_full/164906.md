pregenerate:/ˌpriːˈdʒɛnəreɪt/| vt.|预先生成，预先产生|To generate or create something in advance.  This often refers to creating something before it is actually needed, often for efficiency or optimization purposes.|例句：The system pregenerates thumbnails for faster image loading. 系统预先生成缩略图以加快图像加载速度。

例句：The software pregenerates reports each night for the next day's use. 软件每晚预先生成报表，以供次日使用。


近义词：prepare, create beforehand, produce in advance, generate in advance|反义词：generate afterwards, produce on demand|词性:verb
