heartless:/ˈhɑːrtlɪs/ /ˈhɑːrtləs/ | adj. | 无情的；冷酷的；铁石心肠的 |  Lacking compassion or empathy; cruel.  |例句：He showed a heartless disregard for the feelings of others. 他表现出对他人感情的无情漠视。 |例句：The heartless killer showed no remorse for his crimes.  这名冷酷无情的杀手对其罪行毫不悔恨。 |近义词：cruel, ruthless, callous, unfeeling, pitiless |反义词：compassionate, merciful, kind, empathetic, tender
