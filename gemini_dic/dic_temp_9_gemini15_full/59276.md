eagernesses:/ˈiːɡərnəsɪz/| n.|热切;渴望;热衷|The plural form of eagerness, indicating multiple instances of intense desire or enthusiasm.  It refers to a strong feeling of wanting to do something or for something to happen.|例句：Her eagernesses to learn and to succeed were evident in her tireless work ethic.  她的求知欲和成功的渴望在她不知疲倦的工作态度中显而易见。
例句：His multiple eagernesses, from climbing mountains to learning Mandarin, kept him busy and fulfilled. 他多种多样的渴望，从爬山到学习普通话，让他忙碌而充实。|近义词：enthusiasms, passions, desires, zeal|反义词：apathy, indifference, unconcern|名词
