convectively:/kənˈvɛktɪvli/| adv.| 对流地，对流式地 | In a manner relating to or caused by convection.  Describes processes or movements driven by convection, the transfer of heat through the movement of fluids (liquids or gases).| 例句：Heat is transferred convectively through the atmosphere. = 热量通过大气对流传递。

The storm developed convectively over the warm ocean. = 暴风雨在温暖的海面上对流发展。


近义词：convectionally


反义词：conductively (传导地), radiatively (辐射地)


词性:副词
