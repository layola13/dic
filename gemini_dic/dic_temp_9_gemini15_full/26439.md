byproduct:/ˈbaɪˌprɑːdʌkt/|/ˈbaɪˌprɒdʌkt/| n. |副产品，副产物 |A byproduct is something that is produced during the making of something else.  It is often a secondary or unintended result. |例句：Ethanol is a byproduct of the fermentation process.  乙醇是发酵过程的副产品。

The manufacturing process generates several byproducts.  这个制造过程产生了几种副产品。

|近义词：secondary product, side effect, spin-off |反义词：main product, primary product |名词
