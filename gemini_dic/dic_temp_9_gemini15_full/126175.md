mutuals:/ˈmjuːtʃuəlz/ | n. |互粉，互关 |People who follow each other on social media.  It refers to the reciprocal relationship of following each other on platforms like Twitter, Instagram, or Tumblr. | Example:  I gained a lot of new mutuals after posting that video. 我发布那个视频后获得了许多新的互粉。|近义词:followers who follow back, reciprocal follows |反义词:one-sided followers, unfollowers

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are mutuals on Instagram. = 他们在Instagram上互粉。
We are mutuals; we follow each other. = 我们是互粉；我们互相关注。

一般过去时:
We were mutuals until she unfollowed me. = 在她取关我之前，我们是互粉。
They were mutuals for several months. = 他们互粉了好几个月。


现在完成时:
We have been mutuals for over a year. = 我们互粉已经超过一年了。
I have gained many mutuals recently. = 我最近获得了许多互粉。


过去完成时:
They had been mutuals before the argument. = 争吵之前他们是互粉。
Before the incident, we had been mutuals for years. = 事发之前，我们互粉多年了。


将来时:
I hope we will be mutuals in the future. = 我希望将来我们会成为互粉。
I think we will remain mutuals. = 我认为我们会保持互粉关系。


虚拟语气:
If only we were mutuals! = 要是我们互粉就好了！
If she followed me back, we would be mutuals. = 如果她回关我，我们就互粉了。


被动语态:
Mutuals are often created through shared interests. = 互粉通常是通过共同兴趣建立的。(This is a less common usage)
(A more natural passive would be focused on the action of following rather than the status of being mutuals)


疑问句:
Are we mutuals? = 我们互粉吗？
Is she one of my mutuals? = 她是我互粉之一吗？


否定句:
We are not mutuals; I don't follow her. = 我们不是互粉；我不关注她。
They are not mutuals anymore. = 他们不再是互粉了。


PHRASE->
gain mutuals = 获得互粉
lose mutuals = 失去互粉
mutuals only = 只关注互粉
mutuals with = 与...互粉
