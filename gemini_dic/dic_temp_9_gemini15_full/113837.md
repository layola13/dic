made:/meɪd/ | v. | 做的；制造的；使成为 |  Past tense and past participle of "make".  It indicates that something was created, produced, or caused to exist. It can also imply an act of forcing or compelling. |例句：She made a beautiful cake. 她做了一个漂亮的蛋糕。

She made him leave. 她让他离开了。|近义词：created, produced, constructed, formed, built |反义词：destroyed, ruined, undone, demolished |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I make breakfast every morning. = 我每天早上做早餐。
They make toys in that factory. = 那个工厂生产玩具。


一般过去时:
He made a mistake. = 他犯了一个错误。
We made a plan for the weekend. = 我们制定了一个周末计划。


现在完成时:
She has made significant progress. = 她已经取得了显著的进步。
They have made their decision. = 他们已经做出了决定。


过去完成时:
I had made the reservation before he called. = 我在他打电话之前已经预定了。
They had made several attempts before succeeding. = 他们在成功之前已经尝试过几次了。


将来时:
I will make a phone call later. = 我稍后会打个电话。
We will make sure everything is ready. = 我们会确保一切准备就绪。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would make a better cake. = 如果我有更多时间，我会做一个更好的蛋糕。
I wish I had made a different choice. = 我真希望我做了不同的选择。


被动语态:
The cake was made with fresh ingredients. = 这个蛋糕是用新鲜的食材做的。
The decision was made quickly. = 这个决定很快就做出了。


疑问句:
Who made this mess? = 谁弄的这烂摊子？
Did you make that yourself? = 你自己做的吗？


否定句:
I didn't make the bed this morning. = 我今天早上没有铺床。
They didn't make it to the meeting on time. = 他们没有准时参加会议。


PHRASE->
made of: 由...制成 (referring to materials)  The table is made of wood. 这张桌子是木头做的。
made from: 由...制成 (referring to a process) This bread is made from wheat. 这个面包是用小麦做的。
made up of: 由...组成  The team is made up of five members. 这个团队由五名成员组成。
made for: 为...而设计 This dress is made for you. 这件衣服是为你设计的。
be made to do sth: 被迫做某事 He was made to apologize. 他被迫道歉。
