pencilled-in: /ˈpɛnsəld ɪn/ | adj. | 预定的；初步安排的 |  Something that is pencilled in is tentatively planned or scheduled,  meaning it's not yet definite or confirmed. It suggests a degree of flexibility and the possibility of changes. |


例句：

We have a meeting pencilled in for next Tuesday. = 我们初步安排了下周二的会议。

I've pencilled in a trip to the beach next weekend, but it's not confirmed yet. = 我初步计划下周末去海滩旅行，但还没有确定。


近义词：tentatively scheduled, provisionally arranged, tentatively planned


反义词：confirmed, finalized, definite


词性：形容词
