ifa:/ˈɪfə/| conj.|如果，假使|If it rains, we'll stay home.  如果下雨，我们就待在家里。|近义词：provided that, supposing, in case|反义词：unless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
If he studies hard, he will pass the exam. =如果他努力学习，他就会通过考试。
If she works overtime, she gets paid extra. =如果她加班，她会得到额外的报酬。


一般过去时:
If it rained yesterday, the ground would be wet. =如果昨天下了雨，地面就会湿。
If they went to the party, they had a great time. =如果他们去了派对，他们玩得很开心。


现在完成时:
If she has finished her work, she can go home. =如果她完成了她的工作，她就可以回家了。
If he has saved enough money, he can buy a car. =如果他已经攒够了钱，他就可以买一辆车了。


过去完成时:
If I had known you were coming, I would have prepared a meal. =如果我知道你要来，我会准备一顿饭。
If they had studied harder, they would have passed the exam. =如果他们学习更努力，他们就通过考试了。


将来时:
If it rains tomorrow, we will stay inside. =如果明天下雨，我们就待在里面。
If she gets promoted, she will be very happy. =如果她升职了，她会非常高兴。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would apologize. =如果我是你，我会道歉。
If I had a million dollars, I would travel the world. =如果我有一百万美元，我会环游世界。


被动语态:
If the problem is solved quickly, the damage will be minimized. =如果问题迅速得到解决，损失将会最小化。
If the project is completed on time, we will receive a bonus. =如果项目按时完成，我们将获得奖金。


疑问句:
If he comes, what will we do? =如果他来了，我们将怎么办？
If she calls, will you answer the phone? =如果她打电话，你会接电话吗？


否定句:
If it doesn't rain, we will go for a picnic. =如果不下雨，我们将去野餐。
If she doesn't study hard, she will fail the exam. =如果她不努力学习，她就会考试不及格。


PHRASE->
if only = 但愿
if anything = 即使有什么
if necessary =如有必要
even if =即使
as if =好像
