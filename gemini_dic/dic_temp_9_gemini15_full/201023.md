sloping:/ˈsloʊpɪŋ/ | adj. |倾斜的，斜的 |Having a slope; inclined.  A sloping surface slants or inclines.  |例句：The roof had a gentle sloping surface. 屋顶有一个缓和的斜面。

例句：The hillside had a steep sloping path.  山坡有一条陡峭的斜路。


近义词：inclined, slanted, tilted, sloping, oblique | 反义词：level, flat, horizontal | 词性：形容词


词源:  The word "sloping" derives from the Old English word "slōp," meaning "slope."  This, in turn, is related to the Proto-Germanic word "*slopō," also meaning "slope." The suffix "-ing" is a participial ending, forming an adjective from the verb "slope."
