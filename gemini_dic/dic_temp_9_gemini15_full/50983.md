desired:/dɪˈzaɪərd/ | adj. | 期望的，想要的 |  Something that is desired is wanted or wished for. It expresses a strong feeling of wanting something. | Example: The desired outcome was a peaceful resolution.  期望的结果是和平解决。

The desired effect was achieved.  达到了预期的效果。|近义词：wanted, wished-for, longed-for |反义词：undesired, unwanted
