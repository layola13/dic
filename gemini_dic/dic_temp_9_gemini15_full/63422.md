entrapments: /ɪnˈtræpmənts/ | n. |圈套；陷阱；诱捕 |The act of entrapping; the state of being entrapped.  It often refers to situations or circumstances that restrict freedom or limit opportunities.  |例句：He fell victim to the entrapments of the city. 他成为了城市陷阱的受害者。

例句：The numerous entrapments of the legal system delayed the trial.  法律体系中大量的圈套拖延了审判。


近义词：snares, traps, pitfalls, deceits, tricks


反义词：freedom, liberation, release


词性：名词
