kunda:/ˈkʊndɑː/| n.|昆达(印度教女神)|Kunda is a Sanskrit word referring to a type of earthenware pot, often used in Hindu rituals and ceremonies.  It can also refer to a pond or pool of water, and figuratively, to a source or wellspring. In some contexts, it may be a personal name or a place name.|例句：The priest filled the kunda with sacred water for the puja.  祭司用圣水注满了昆达，准备进行普迦仪式。|近义词：pot, vessel, reservoir, wellspring|反义词：dryness, emptiness|名词
