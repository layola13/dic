electroplate:/ɪˈlektrəʊpleɪt/| vt. |电镀|To coat (a metal object) with a thin layer of another metal by electrolysis. This process uses an electric current to deposit the metal onto the surface.  Electroplating is used to improve the appearance, corrosion resistance, or other properties of the base metal.|例句：They electroplated the jewelry with gold to enhance its value. 他们用金电镀首饰以提高其价值。

例句：The car parts were electroplated with chrome for durability. 汽车零件镀铬以增强耐用性。


近义词：plate, coat


反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be to remove a plating.


词性:vt.
