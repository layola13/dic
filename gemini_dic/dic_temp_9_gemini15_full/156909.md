pilau:/pɪˈlaʊ/| n.|印度式米饭；印度炒饭|A pilau is a dish of rice cooked with spices and often meat or vegetables.  It's a staple in many South Asian cuisines, varying considerably in preparation depending on regional and family traditions. The rice is usually basmati, and common additions include onions, garlic, ginger, and various spices like cumin, turmeric, and coriander.|  Example:  The restaurant is famous for its delicious chicken pilau. 这家餐馆以其美味的鸡肉印度炒饭而闻名。|近义词：rice dish, spiced rice|反义词：无
