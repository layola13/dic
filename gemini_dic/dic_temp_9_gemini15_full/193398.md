seismic:/ˈsaɪzmɪk/| adj.|地震的；剧烈的|relating to or caused by earthquakes.  The word derives from the Greek word "seismos," meaning "earthquake." |例句：The seismic activity in the region is increasing.  该地区的地震活动正在增加。

例句：The earthquake caused widespread seismic damage. 那次地震造成了广泛的地震破坏。


近义词：earthquake, temblor, tremor|反义词：calm, stable


PHRASE->
seismic activity: 地震活动
seismic waves: 地震波
seismic risk: 地震风险
