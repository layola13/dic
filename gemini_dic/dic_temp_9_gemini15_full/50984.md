desiredly: /dɪˈzaɪrəbli/ | adv. | 如愿以偿地；理想地；合意地 | In a desirable manner; as one would wish.  |例句：The project was completed desiredly, on time and within budget. 项目如期完成，且在预算之内。

例句：The flowers bloomed desiredly, adding beauty to the garden. 花如愿以偿地盛开了，为花园增添了美丽。


近义词：ideally, perfectly, satisfactorily, optimally

反义词：undesirably, unhappily, unfortunately, disappointingly

词性：副词
