cres:/krɛs/ | n. |水田芥，豆瓣菜|  cress refers to several plants in the genus Lepidium and other genera, all of which are edible and have a peppery taste.  They are commonly used as a salad green or a garnish. |Example: I added some watercress to my sandwich for extra flavor. 我在我的三明治里加了一些水田芥来增加风味。|近义词：watercress, garden cress|反义词：无
