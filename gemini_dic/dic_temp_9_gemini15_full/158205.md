platterfuls:/ˈplætərˌfʊlz/| n.|盘子满的量；满满一盘|The word "platterfuls" is the plural form of "platterful," meaning the amount that fills a platter.  It indicates a substantial quantity of something served on a platter. |例句：The table was laden with platterfuls of delicious food. 桌子上摆满了美味佳肴。  She served platterfuls of cookies to the guests. 她给客人们端上了满满几盘饼干。|近义词：platefuls, heaps, piles|反义词：少量，少许
