chlorophylls:/ˈklɔːrəfɪlz/| n. |叶绿素|Chlorophylls are green pigments found in plants and other photosynthetic organisms, responsible for absorbing light energy to carry out photosynthesis.|Plants use chlorophylls to convert sunlight into energy.|近义词：pigments|反义词：无
