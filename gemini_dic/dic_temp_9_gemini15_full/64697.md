escapist: /ɪˈskeɪpɪst/ | adj. |逃避现实的；沉溺于幻想的 |  Relating to or characterized by escapism; seeking distraction and relief from unpleasant realities, often by indulging in fantasy.  | Example:  He read escapist fiction to forget his troubles. 他读逃避现实的小说来忘记他的烦恼。  |近义词：dreamy, fantasy-prone, unrealistic | 反义词：realistic, pragmatic


USAGE->

He is an escapist; he spends hours playing video games. 他是个逃避现实的人；他花几个小时玩电子游戏。
The movie provides an escapist experience for viewers. 这部电影为观众提供了逃避现实的体验。
