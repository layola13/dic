clearage: /ˈklɪərɪdʒ/ | n. | 清理；清除；清理费用 | The act of clearing something, especially of removing obstacles or debris.  It can also refer to the cost associated with clearing something. |例句：The clearage of the forest was a massive undertaking. 森林的清理是一项巨大的工程。

The clearage of the debris after the storm cost thousands of dollars.  风暴过后清理残骸的费用高达数千美元。|近义词：clearance, removal, elimination |反义词：accumulation, congestion


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The crew clears the runway every morning. = 机组人员每天早上清理跑道。
This process clears the data from the hard drive. = 这个过程会清除硬盘上的数据。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
