pluviameter:/pluːˈvɪæmɪtər/| n. |雨量计|An instrument for measuring rainfall.  It typically consists of a container with a calibrated scale to measure the amount of rain collected over a specific period.|例句：The pluviameter recorded 2 inches of rain in the last 24 hours.  过去24小时雨量计记录了2英寸的降雨。


The pluviameter is essential for accurate weather forecasting. 雨量计对于准确的天气预报至关重要。|近义词：rain gauge, ombrometer |反义词：无
