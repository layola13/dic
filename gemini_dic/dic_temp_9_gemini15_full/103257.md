jewels:/ˈdʒuːəlz/ | n. |珠宝，宝石 |Precious stones, especially when they are considered collectively or as ornaments.  Jewels are often valuable and beautiful, used in jewelry or as decorative items. |例句：She wore a necklace adorned with precious jewels. 她戴着一条缀满珍贵宝石的项链。

例句：The museum's collection of jewels is world-renowned.  博物馆的珠宝收藏举世闻名。


近义词：gems, precious stones, gems, ornaments


反义词：junk, trash, worthless items


词性：名词
