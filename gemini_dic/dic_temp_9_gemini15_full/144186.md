/ˈaʊtsaɪdədʌm/| n. |局外人地位；局外人身份；边缘人的处境|The state or experience of being an outsider; the condition of being marginalized or excluded from a group or society.  It often implies a sense of alienation or powerlessness. |例句：He felt a deep sense of outsiderdom after moving to a new city. 他搬到新城市后感到深深的局外人感。|例句：Her outsiderdom gave her a unique perspective on the company's internal politics. 她作为局外人的身份让她对公司的内部政治有了独特的视角。|近义词：marginality, alienation, exclusion, estrangement |反义词：inclusion, belonging, integration
