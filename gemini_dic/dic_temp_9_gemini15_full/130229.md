non-sharing: /ˈnɒnˈʃɛərɪŋ/ | adj. | 非共享的，不共享的 | Not involving or characterized by sharing;  lacking the quality of being shared.  This term is often used in contexts related to resources, information, or responsibilities. |


例句：

The non-sharing of resources led to conflict among the team members.  = 资源的不共享导致团队成员之间发生冲突。

The company's policy of non-sharing of profits demotivated employees. = 公司不共享利润的政策使员工缺乏动力。


近义词：unshared, exclusive, private, individual


反义词：shared, common, collective, collaborative


词性：形容词
