heavens:/ˈhɛvənz/| n.|天；天空；天堂；老天爷|The heavens are full of stars tonight. 今晚天上繁星点点。|近义词：sky, paradise, firmament|反义词：earth, hell


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The heavens declare the glory of God. = 天明上帝的荣耀。
The heavens are clear tonight. = 今晚天空晴朗。


一般过去时:
The heavens opened and it began to rain. = 天空打开，开始下雨了。
The heavens seemed to weep with sadness. = 天空似乎悲伤地哭泣。


现在完成时:
The heavens have opened up many times this week. = 这周天空已经多次放晴。
We have witnessed the beauty of the heavens many times. = 我们多次领略过天空的美丽。


过去完成时:
Before the storm, the heavens had been clear and blue. = 暴风雨来临之前，天空晴朗湛蓝。
The heavens had been dark and stormy for days. = 好几天天空都阴沉多雨。


将来时:
The heavens will likely be clear tomorrow. = 明天天空很可能晴朗。
We will look up at the heavens and admire the stars. = 我们将会仰望天空，欣赏星星。


虚拟语气:
If the heavens were to open, we would be caught in the rain. = 如果天空真的放晴，我们会淋雨。
I wish the heavens would clear up for the picnic. = 我希望天空能放晴以便野餐。


被动语态:
The heavens are often depicted in art. = 天空经常被描绘在艺术作品中。
The heavens were observed by the ancient astronomers. = 古代天文学家观察了天空。


疑问句:
What are the heavens made of? = 天空是由什么组成的？
Do you believe the heavens have a purpose? = 你相信天空有其目的吗？


否定句:
The heavens were not clear yesterday. = 昨天天空不晴朗。
I don't believe everything that happens is ordained by the heavens. = 我不相信发生的一切都是天意。
