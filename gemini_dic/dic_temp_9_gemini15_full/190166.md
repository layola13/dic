sanscrit:/ˈsænskɹɪt/| n. |梵文|Sanskrit is an ancient Indo-Aryan language of India, where it is still used in religious ceremonies, and it is the liturgical language of Hinduism, Buddhism, and Jainism. It is the ancestor of many modern languages, including Hindi, Bengali, and Marathi.|Example:  The ancient Sanskrit epics, the Mahabharata and the Ramayana, are still widely read and studied today.  今天，古老的梵文史诗《摩诃婆罗多》和《罗摩衍那》仍然被广泛阅读和研究。|近义词:Ancient Indian language|反义词:Modern languages
