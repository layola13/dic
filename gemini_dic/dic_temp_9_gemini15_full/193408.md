seismography:/saɪzˈmɒɡrəfi/ | n. |地震仪记录；地震学 |The scientific study and recording of earthquakes and seismic waves using instruments.  Seismography involves the use of seismographs to detect, measure, and record ground motion caused by seismic events.  The data obtained is crucial for understanding earthquakes, their causes, and effects. |The seismography showed a significant tremor originating near the fault line. 地震仪记录显示，断层附近发生了强烈震动。|seismic recording, earthquake recording |— |
