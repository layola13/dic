infanthoods:/ˈɪnfəntˌhʊdz/| n. |婴儿期（复数）|The plural form of "infancy," referring to multiple periods of infancy.  It is less commonly used than "infancies," which is preferred when referring to multiple instances of the state of being an infant.|例句：The study compared the developmental milestones across several infanthoods. 这项研究比较了几个婴儿期的发育里程碑。|近义词：infancies|反义词：adulthoods
