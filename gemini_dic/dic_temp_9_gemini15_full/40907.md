coolants:/ˈkuːlənts/| n.|冷却剂|A substance used to reduce or regulate temperature, typically in an engine or other machinery.|例句：The car's cooling system uses a special coolant to prevent overheating. 这辆车的冷却系统使用一种特殊的冷却剂来防止过热。
例句：We need to check the coolant level in the radiator. 我们需要检查散热器里的冷却液液位。|近义词：refrigerant, coolant liquid|反义词:heat source
