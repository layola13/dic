shines:/ʃaɪnz/ | v. |闪耀；发光；杰出|To emit or reflect light; to be brilliantly successful or outstanding. |例句：The sun shines brightly today.  今天阳光灿烂。|例句：Her talent shines through in her work. 她的才能在她的作品中闪耀。|近义词：gleams, glitters, excels,  outperforms |反义词：dulls, fades


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun shines every day. = 太阳每天都照耀。
The star shines brightly in the night sky. = 星星在夜空中闪耀。

一般过去时:
The sun shone brightly yesterday. = 昨天阳光灿烂。
The diamond shone brilliantly on her finger. = 钻石在她手指上闪闪发光。

现在完成时:
The sun has shone all day. = 太阳一整天都照耀着。
She has shone as a leader in her field. = 她作为该领域的领导者一直很优秀。

过去完成时:
The sun had shone all morning before the rain started. = 下雨之前，太阳整个上午都照耀着。
He had shone in his previous role before taking on this new challenge. = 在接受这个新挑战之前，他在之前的角色中表现出色。

将来时:
The sun will shine tomorrow. = 明天太阳会照耀。
I believe she will shine in her new job. = 我相信她会在新工作中大放异彩。

虚拟语气:
If the sun shone every day, we would be happier. = 如果每天阳光灿烂，我们会更快乐。
I wish my work shone more brightly. = 我希望我的工作能更加出色。

被动语态:
The light was shone on the painting. = 灯光照射在画上。
Her achievements were shone upon by the media. = 她的成就受到了媒体的关注。

疑问句:
Does the sun shine today? = 今天阳光灿烂吗？
Does his talent shine through his work? = 他的才能在他的作品中体现出来了吗？

否定句:
The sun doesn't shine today. = 今天没有阳光。
His efforts didn't shine through in the final result. = 他的努力在最终结果中没有体现出来。


PHRASE->
