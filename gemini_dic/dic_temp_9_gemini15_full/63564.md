envies:/ˈɛnviz/| v.|嫉妒，羡慕|To feel or show envy towards someone or something because they have something you want.|She envies her sister's success.|近义词：jealousy, covet|反义词:admiration, contentment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He envies his friend's new car. = 他羡慕他朋友的新车。
I envy her ability to speak so many languages. = 我羡慕她能说这么多语言的能力。


一般过去时:
She envied her classmate's high grades. = 她嫉妒她同学的高分。
They envied the family's large house. = 他们羡慕那个家庭的大房子。


现在完成时:
I have envied her talent since we were children. = 从小我就羡慕她的天赋。
He has envied his brother's success for many years. = 多年来，他一直嫉妒他哥哥的成功。


过去完成时:
She had envied his promotion before she received her own. = 在她自己升职之前，她一直嫉妒他的升职。
They had envied their neighbors' beautiful garden for years. = 多年来，他们一直羡慕邻居们美丽的庭院。


将来时:
I will envy her if she wins the lottery. = 如果她中了彩票，我会羡慕她的。
They will envy his new job once they hear about it. = 一旦他们听说这件事，他们就会羡慕他的新工作。


虚拟语气:
I wish I didn't envy their wealth so much. = 我真希望我不那么羡慕他们的财富。
If I were her, I wouldn't envy anyone else’s achievements. = 如果我是她，我不会羡慕任何人的成就。


被动语态:
His success was envied by many. = 他的成功被许多人羡慕。
Her talent is envied by all her peers. = 她的才能受到所有同龄人的羡慕。


疑问句:
Do you envy their lifestyle? = 你羡慕他们的生活方式吗？
Does he envy my new apartment? = 他羡慕我的新公寓吗？


否定句:
I don't envy her busy schedule. = 我并不羡慕她忙碌的日程安排。
They didn't envy his high salary. = 他们并不羡慕他高额的薪水。


PHRASE->
feel envy: 感到嫉妒
green with envy: 妒火中烧
be the envy of: 是...羡慕的对象
filled with envy: 充满嫉妒
die of envy: 羡慕得要死
