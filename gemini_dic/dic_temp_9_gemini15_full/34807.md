climbs:/klaɪmz/| v.|攀登；爬；上升|To go upwards using hands and feet, or to ascend gradually.|例句：The mountain climbers climbed the steep cliff. 登山者攀登了陡峭的悬崖。

例句：The plant's vines climbed the wall. 植物的藤蔓爬上了墙。|近义词：ascend, scale, mount|反义词：descend, drop, fall|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He climbs mountains every weekend. 他每个周末都爬山。
She climbs the stairs slowly. 她慢慢地爬楼梯。

一般过去时:
They climbed the tree to get the fruit. 他们爬上树去摘水果。
He climbed the ladder to fix the roof. 他爬上梯子去修理屋顶。

现在完成时:
She has climbed Mount Everest twice. 她已经两次攀登珠穆朗玛峰了。
He has climbed that mountain before. 他以前爬过那座山。

过去完成时:
They had climbed the hill before it got dark. 天黑之前他们已经爬上了小山。
She had climbed many mountains before this one. 在攀登这座山之前，她已经攀登过许多座山了。

将来时:
We will climb the highest peak next year. 我们明年将攀登最高峰。
He will climb the rock face tomorrow. 他明天将攀登岩壁。

虚拟语气:
If I were stronger, I would climb that mountain. 如果我更强壮，我会爬那座山。
I wish I could climb that cliff. 我希望我能爬上那悬崖。

被动语态:
The mountain was climbed by many people last year.  去年这座山被许多人攀登过。
The wall is being climbed by ivy.  墙上爬满了常春藤。

疑问句:
Does he climb often? 他经常爬山吗？
Can you climb that wall? 你能爬过那堵墙吗？

否定句:
She doesn't climb rocks. 她不爬石头。
He didn't climb the tree. 他没有爬树。
