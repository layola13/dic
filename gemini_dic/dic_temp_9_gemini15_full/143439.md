outflowed:/ˈaʊtˌfloʊd/| v.|流出，涌出 |To flow out; to stream out.  It describes the movement of something, typically liquid or gas, flowing out from a container or area. |例句：The water outflowed from the broken pipe. 水从破裂的管道流出。

The crowd outflowed from the stadium after the game.  比赛结束后，人群涌出体育场。|近义词：flowed out, poured out, gushed out |反义词：inflowed, poured in


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water outflowes from the dam.  水从水坝流出。
The air outflowes through the vent. 空气通过通风口流出。


一般过去时:
