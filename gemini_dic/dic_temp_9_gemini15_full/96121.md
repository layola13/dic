indelicately:/ɪnˈdɛləkətli/ | adv. | 不雅地；粗鲁地；不细致地 |  Acting or speaking without proper consideration or refinement; tactlessly.  It implies a lack of sensitivity or politeness, often in a way that is offensive or embarrassing. | 例句：He indelicately slurped his soup. 他不雅地喝汤。

He indelicately pointed out her weight gain. 他不客气地指出了她的体重增加。


近义词：crudely, roughly, tactlessly, unrefinedly, coarsely, bluntly


反义词：delicately, politely, tactfully, considerately, gracefully


词性：副词
