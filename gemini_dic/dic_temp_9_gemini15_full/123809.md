mopper:/ˈmɒpər/| n. |拖把者；用拖把擦拭的人|A person who mops.  A mopper is someone whose job involves mopping floors or surfaces.|例句：The mopper cleaned the spilled juice efficiently.  拖把工有效地清扫了洒出的果汁。  The diligent mopper ensured the kitchen floor was spotless.  勤劳的拖把工确保了厨房地板一尘不染。|近义词：cleaner, janitor|反义词：None specifically applicable.
