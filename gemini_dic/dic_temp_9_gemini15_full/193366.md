segue:/ˈsɛɡweɪ/| n., v.|  过渡；转场；自然地过渡| A smooth transition from one thing to another, especially in a performance or speech.  |例句：The band seamlessly segued into a faster tempo. 乐队无缝过渡到更快的节奏。


例句：The speaker segued from the topic of economics to the topic of politics. 演讲者从经济话题自然过渡到政治话题。


近义词：transition, shift, changeover, passage


反义词：abrupt halt, interruption, discontinuity


词性: Noun, Verb
