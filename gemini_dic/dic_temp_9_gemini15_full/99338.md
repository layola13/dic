interdebate:/ˌɪntərˈdeɪt/ | n. |  辩论；相互辩论 | A discussion or argument, especially one involving a formal exchange of opposing views.  It often implies a reciprocal exchange of arguments, unlike a one-sided debate. | 例句：The interdebate between the two candidates covered a wide range of issues.  两个候选人之间的辩论涵盖了广泛的问题。|近义词：discussion, argument, controversy,  disputation | 反义词：agreement, consensus, accord | 名词
