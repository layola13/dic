reconceptualizations:/ˌriːkənˌsɛptʃuəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n.|重新概念化；观念的重新构建|The act of rethinking and restructuring existing concepts or ideas, often leading to new understandings or interpretations.  It involves a fundamental shift in perspective or approach.|例句：The paper offers several reconceptualizations of the relationship between art and technology. 这篇论文提供了关于艺术与技术之间关系的几种重新概念化。

例句：His reconceptualizations of historical events challenged established narratives. 他对历史事件的重新概念化挑战了既定的叙事。


近义词：reframings, reinterpretations, redefinitions, reformulations|反义词：reinforcements (of existing concepts), confirmations|词性:noun
