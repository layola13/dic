dysphoria:/dɪsˈfɔːriə/| n. |焦虑；烦躁；(尤指)性别焦虑 |A feeling of unease or dissatisfaction with one's sex or gender role.  It's often associated with gender identity disorder, where individuals experience significant distress because their gender identity does not match their assigned sex at birth.  The feeling can manifest in various ways, from mild discomfort to intense distress and depression. | Example:  She experienced intense gender dysphoria, feeling trapped in a body that didn't reflect her true self. 她经历了强烈的性别焦虑，感觉被困在一个无法反映她真实自我的身体里。|近义词：unease, discomfort, distress, anguish |反义词：euphoria, contentment, satisfaction |名词
