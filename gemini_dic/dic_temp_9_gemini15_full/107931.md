laryngectomy:/ˌlæriŋˈɡɛktəmi/| n. |喉部切除术|The surgical removal of all or part of the larynx (voice box).  This procedure is often performed to treat laryngeal cancer.  |Example: The patient underwent a laryngectomy to remove a cancerous tumor. 患者接受了喉部切除手术以切除癌性肿瘤。|近义词：larynx removal, vocal cord removal |反义词：larynx reconstruction,  vocal cord repair |名词
