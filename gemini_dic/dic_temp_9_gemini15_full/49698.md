demobbing:/dɪˈmɒbɪŋ/ | v. | 复员；遣散（军队）；| The process of dismissing soldiers from military service, often after a war or conflict.  It also refers to the general dispersal or dismissal of a large group of people. | 例句：After the war, the demobbing of troops was a lengthy process.  战后，军队复员是一个漫长的过程。

The demobbing of the factory workers left the town economically depressed. 工厂工人的遣散使该镇经济萧条。| 近义词：discharge, dismissal, disbandment, demobilization | 反义词：mobilization, recruitment, enlistment |  动词
