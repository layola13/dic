desiccatory:/ˈdɛsɪkətɔːri/| n. |干燥器；干燥箱| A desiccator is an airtight container used to keep things dry by reducing the humidity. It typically contains a desiccant, a substance that absorbs moisture, such as silica gel.  | Example: The samples were stored in a desiccator to prevent them from absorbing moisture.  样品储存在干燥器中以防止吸湿。|近义词：desiccating chamber, drying cabinet |反义词：humidifier, moistener
