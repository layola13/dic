graveling:/ˈɡrævəlɪŋ/| n.|碎石路面，铺碎石|The process of covering a surface with gravel; a road or path surfaced with gravel.|例句：The driveway needs graveling. 这条车道需要铺碎石。|例句：The old road was just a simple graveling. 这条老路只是一条简单的碎石路。|近义词：stoning, paving (with gravel)|反义词：paving (with asphalt or concrete)
