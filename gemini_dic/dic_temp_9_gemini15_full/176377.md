re-forms:/ˌriːˈfɔːrmz/| n. |改革；改造；重组 |The re-forms aimed to improve the efficiency of the government.  这些改革旨在提高政府效率。|近义词：restructuring, reorganization, changes |反义词：stagnation, deterioration


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government re-forms its policies regularly. = 政府定期改革其政策。
She re-forms her approach to the problem. = 她调整了解决问题的方法。


一般过去时:
They re-formed the company after the merger. = 合并后，他们重组了公司。
The team re-formed after a short break. = 团队短暂休息后重新组队。


现在完成时:
The country has re-formed its education system. = 这个国家已经改革了教育体系。
They have re-formed their alliance. = 他们已经重组了他们的联盟。


过去完成时:
They had re-formed their strategy before the competition. = 他们在比赛前已经调整了策略。
The artist had re-formed his clay sculpture. = 艺术家已经重新塑造了他的粘土雕塑。


将来时:
The company will re-form its marketing team next year. = 公司明年将重组其营销团队。
They will re-form their opinions after more research. = 他们会在更多研究之后重新形成自己的观点。


虚拟语气:
If they re-formed the committee, it might be more effective. = 如果他们重组委员会，它可能会更有效。
I wish they had re-formed their approach earlier. = 我希望他们早点调整方法。


被动语态:
The policies were re-formed by the new government. = 这些政策由新政府改革。
The team was re-formed after several members left. = 几位成员离开后，团队进行了重组。


疑问句:
Will the company re-form its structure? = 公司会重组其结构吗？
Have they re-formed their plans yet? = 他们已经修改计划了吗？


否定句:
The government did not re-form its outdated laws. = 政府没有改革其过时的法律。
They did not re-form their habits easily. = 他们没有轻易改变他们的习惯。


PHRASE->

re-form a government = 组建政府
