depending:/dɪˈpɛndɪŋ/| preposition|取决于，视…而定|depending on the weather, we may go hiking or stay home.  取决于天气，我们可能会去远足也可能会待在家里。|近义词：conditioned on, contingent on|反义词：regardless of, irrespective of


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price depends on the quality. = 价格取决于质量。
It depends on how hard you work. = 这取决于你有多努力工作。

一般过去时:
The decision depended on the available resources. = 决定取决于可用的资源。
His success depended on his hard work and determination. = 他的成功取决于他的努力工作和决心。

现在完成时:
The outcome has depended on many factors. = 结果取决于许多因素。
Everything has depended on your timely decision. = 一切都取决于你及时的决定。

过去完成时:
The plan depended on whether they had received funding. = 计划取决于他们是否获得了资金。
Our success depended on whether we had secured the contract. = 我们的成功取决于我们是否获得了合同。

将来时:
The result will depend on the outcome of the election. = 结果将取决于选举的结果。
Our trip will depend on whether we get the visas. = 我们的旅行将取决于我们是否获得签证。

虚拟语气:
If it depended on me, I would choose differently. = 如果取决于我，我会做出不同的选择。
I wish the result didn't depend on luck. = 我希望结果不取决于运气。

被动语态:
The decision is depended on by the board of directors. = 该决定由董事会决定。
The outcome is depended on by many external factors. = 结果取决于许多外部因素。（此被动用法较少见）

疑问句:
Does the success depend on your effort? = 成功取决于你的努力吗？
Does it depend on what time we arrive? = 这取决于我们几点到达吗？

否定句:
The outcome does not depend on chance. = 结果不取决于偶然。
It doesn't depend on how much money you have. = 这不取决于你拥有多少钱。


PHRASE->
depending on = 取决于
depending how = 取决于如何
it depends = 看情况
that depends = 那要看情况
just depending = 仅仅取决于
