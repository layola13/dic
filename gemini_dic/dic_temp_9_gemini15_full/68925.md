fatalist: /ˈfeɪtəlɪst/ | n. | 宿命论者，认命的人 | A fatalist believes that all events are predetermined and inevitable; they accept whatever happens without resistance or struggle.  |  He's a fatalist; he believes whatever will be, will be. 他是个宿命论者；他相信凡事皆有定数。 | determinist, predestinarian | activist, free will believer | 名词
