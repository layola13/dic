histography:/hɪˈstɒɡrəfi/| n. |历史编纂学；史学理论|The study of the methods and principles used in writing history, including the selection of sources, interpretation of evidence, and presentation of narratives.|Example:  Histography has evolved significantly over the centuries, reflecting changes in historical methodology and social contexts.|近义词：historical methodology, historiography, history writing|反义词：无
