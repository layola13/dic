moneywort:/ˈmʌniwɜːrt/| n. |金雀花，金钱草|A low-growing, creeping plant with small, round, yellow flowers;  also known as creeping Jenny. It's often grown as a groundcover. |例句：The moneywort spread quickly across the garden. 金钱草迅速蔓延到花园里。|例句：We planted moneywort to cover the bare patches of soil. 我们种植金钱草来覆盖裸露的土壤。|近义词：creeping Jenny, herb-twopence|反义词：无
