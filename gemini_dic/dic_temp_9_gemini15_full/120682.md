misadjusting: /ˌmɪs əˈdʒʌstɪŋ/ | v. | 调整错误，失调 |  The act of improperly adjusting something; causing something to be out of alignment or not functioning correctly.  It implies a process of incorrect adjustment, rather than a static state of being misadjusted. |


例句：

He was misadjusting the carburetor, which led to the engine sputtering. = 他调整化油器的方式不对，导致发动机发出噼啪声。

The mechanic spent hours misadjusting the brakes before finally fixing them. = 机械师花了数小时错误地调整刹车，才最终修好。


近义词：maladjusting, improperly adjusting, setting incorrectly


反义词：adjusting correctly, fine-tuning, calibrating
