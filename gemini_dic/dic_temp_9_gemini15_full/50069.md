dent's:/dɛnts/| n.|牙齿；齿状物；凹痕|A dent is a small hollow in a hard surface, made by something hitting it.  It can also refer to a tooth.|例句：The car had a dent in its fender after the accident.  车祸后，汽车挡泥板凹了一个坑。|近义词：indentation, depression, notch|反义词：protrusion, bump


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car has a dent. = 这辆车有个凹痕。
He has a cavity in his upper left dent. = 他左上牙有个龋洞。


一般过去时:
The ball made a dent in the wall. = 球在墙上砸了个坑。
I got a dent in my car door. = 我的车门被撞凹了。


现在完成时:
The accident has left a dent in my car. = 事故在我的车上留下了一个凹痕。
This experience has made a dent in my confidence. =  这次经历打击了我的信心。


过去完成时:
The previous owner had already made a dent in the paintwork. = 前车主已经把油漆刮花了。
By the time I arrived, they had already put a dent in the project. = 我到的时候，他们已经在项目上取得了进展。


将来时:
That rock will make a dent in the glass. = 那块石头会砸碎玻璃。
I'm afraid this problem will make a dent in our budget. = 我担心这个问题会影响我们的预算。


虚拟语气:
If I had been more careful, I wouldn't have made a dent in the table. = 如果我更小心点，就不会把桌子弄凹了。
I wish I hadn't made a dent in my savings account. = 我真希望我没有动用我的储蓄。


被动语态:
The fender was dented in the accident. = 挡泥板在事故中被撞凹了。
The surface was dented by the impact. = 表面因撞击而凹陷。


疑问句:
Does your car have any dents? = 你的车有凹痕吗？
Are there any dents in this metal sheet? = 这块金属板上有凹痕吗？


否定句:
The impact didn't make a dent in the metal. = 撞击并没有使金属凹陷。
The news didn't make a dent in the stock prices. = 这个消息没有影响股价。


PHRASE->
make a dent in something = 对……产生影响
put a dent in something = 使某物受损
a dent in one's confidence = 对某人信心的打击
a dent in the market = 对市场的影响
a dent in the economy = 对经济的影响
