postgeniture:/ˌpoʊstˈdʒɛnɪtʃər/| n. |身后继承；死后继承|The legal term postgeniture refers to inheritance or succession that occurs after the death of a person. It specifically addresses the transfer of property or titles after the death of the owner.  This contrasts with inheritance during someone's lifetime.|Example: The will stipulated the postgeniture of the family estate to the eldest son.  遗嘱规定将家族产业死后继承给长子。|近义词：inheritance, succession, devolution|反义词：ante-mortem inheritance (inheritance during lifetime)|名词
