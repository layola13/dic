footpad:/ˈfʊtpæd/| n.| 歹徒；强盗；垫脚石 | A footpad is a robber who attacks people on foot.  The term is now largely archaic or used figuratively. |Example: In the dark alleys, one must beware of footpads. 在黑暗的小巷里，人们必须提防歹徒。|近义词：highwayman, robber, thief, mugger |反义词：police officer, law enforcement |词性:n.
