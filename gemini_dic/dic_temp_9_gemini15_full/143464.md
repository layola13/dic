outgabble:/ˌaʊtˈɡæbəl/| vt.|  超过…的说话速度；比…说得快| To speak faster or more volubly than (someone).|例句：The children outgabbled their parents during the family dinner. 孩子们在家庭晚餐期间比他们的父母说得更快。


例句：The auctioneer outgabbled the potential buyers, leaving them confused and unable to bid. 拍卖师说得比潜在买家快，导致他们感到困惑，无法出价。


近义词：outtalk, outspeak, surpass in speech


反义词：be outdone in speech, be silenced


词性:动词
