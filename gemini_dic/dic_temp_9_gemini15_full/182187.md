remolade:/ˌrɛməˈleɪd/| n.|调味酱，蛋黄酱|A creamy sauce typically made with mayonnaise, herbs, and seasonings, often including capers, mustard, and pickles. It's frequently used as a condiment for seafood, vegetables, or meats.|例句：The grilled fish was served with a delicious remoulade sauce. 烤鱼配上美味的调味酱。

例句：She made a remoulade to accompany the boiled shrimp. 她做了一份调味酱来搭配煮熟的虾。
