rehearing's: /ˌriːˈhɪəriŋz/ | n. | 复数再审理；复数再听证 | The plural form of "rehearing," referring to multiple instances of a legal process where a case is reviewed again.  It usually implies a court or tribunal is reconsidering a previous decision. | Example: The judge denied the rehearing's request for further evidence.  法官驳回了再次审理的请求，要求提供进一步的证据。 |近义词: re-examination, review, reconsideration |反义词: final judgment, initial ruling |名词
