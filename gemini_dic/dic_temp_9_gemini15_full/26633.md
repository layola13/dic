cackling:/ˈkæklɪŋ/| n. adj.|咯咯笑声；咯咯地叫|The sound of a hen or similar bird making a rapid succession of short, sharp sounds.  It can also describe the sound of human laughter that is similar in quality.|例句：The cackling of geese filled the farmyard.  鹅的咯咯叫声充满了农家院子。

The children's cackling laughter echoed through the house. 孩子们的咯咯笑声回荡在房子里。|近义词：chuckle, giggle, guffaw|反义词：silence, sobbing
