infratrochlear: /ˌɪnfrəˈtrɒkliər/ | adj. | 旋前肌下的；眶下神经的 | Relating to the area below the trochlea of the humerus or to the infratrochlear nerve.  The infratrochlear nerve is a branch of the nasociliary nerve, which itself is a branch of the ophthalmic nerve (V1). It supplies sensory innervation to the skin of the medial eyelid, the conjunctiva, and the lacrimal sac. | The injury affected the infratrochlear nerve, resulting in reduced sensation in his inner eyelid. | 近义词:  (lacking direct synonyms due to its specific anatomical reference) | 反义词: (lacking direct antonyms due to its specific anatomical reference)
