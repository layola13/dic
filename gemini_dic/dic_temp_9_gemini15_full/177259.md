reasserting:/ˌriːəˈsɜːrtɪŋ/| v. |重申；再次断言；重新确立|The act of stating something again with emphasis, often after it has been challenged or questioned. It implies a forceful re-establishment of a claim, position, or authority. |例句：The government is reasserting its control over the troubled region. 政府正在重新掌控动荡地区。

例句：He reasserted his innocence despite the mounting evidence against him.  尽管越来越多的证据对他不利，他仍然重申自己的清白。


近义词：reaffirm, reinstate, restate, re-emphasize


反义词：retract, deny, abandon, relinquish


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reasserts his dominance every day. 他每天都重申自己的统治地位。
They reassert their commitment to the project. 他们重申自己对项目的承诺。

一般过去时:
She reasserted her independence after the divorce. 离婚后，她重新确立了自己的独立性。
The company reasserted its market share.  这家公司重新确立了它的市场份额。

现在完成时:
He has reasserted his authority over his team. 他已经重新确立了对团队的权威。
The country has reasserted its sovereignty. 这个国家已经重新确立了自己的主权。

过去完成时:
After the setback, they had reasserted their confidence.  在遭遇挫折后，他们重新树立了信心。
Before the meeting, she had reasserted her position on the matter. 会议之前，她已经重申了她对这件事的立场。

将来时:
The government will reassert its power next year. 政府将在明年重新确立其权力。
He will reassert his claim to the throne. 他将再次宣称自己对王位的继承权。

虚拟语气:
If he reasserted his rights, he might get his property back. 如果他重申他的权利，他可能会取回他的财产。
I wish the manager would reassert control over the project. 我希望经理能够重新掌控这个项目。

被动语态:
His authority was reasserted by the board of directors. 他的权威被董事会重新确立。
The company's position was reasserted in the press release. 公司的立场在新闻稿中得到了重申。

疑问句:
Will they reassert their control over the situation? 他们会重新掌控局势吗？
Did he reassert his innocence at the trial? 他在审判中重申了自己的清白吗？

否定句:
He did not reassert his dominance. 他没有重申他的统治地位。
The company failed to reassert its market share. 这家公司未能重新确立其市场份额。
