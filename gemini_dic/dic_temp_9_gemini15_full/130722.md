nonalliance: /ˌnɒnəˈlaɪəns/ | n. | 非联盟；不结盟 | The state or policy of not forming alliances with other countries or groups.  It often refers to a nation's decision to remain neutral in international conflicts or not to participate in military alliances.  | 例句：The country maintained a strict policy of nonalliance during the war. = 在战争期间，这个国家坚持严格的不结盟政策。  Many smaller nations prefer nonalliance to avoid being drawn into larger conflicts. = 许多小国更倾向于不结盟，以避免卷入更大的冲突。 | 近义词：neutrality, nonalignment | 反义词：alliance, alignment | 名词
