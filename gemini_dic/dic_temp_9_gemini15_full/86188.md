heatedly:/ˈhiːtɪdli/| adv.|热烈地；激动地；愤怒地 |In a manner characterized by strong emotion, often anger or excitement.|例句：They argued heatedly about the election results. 他们就选举结果激烈地争论了一番。 They debated the issue heatedly. 他们就这个问题进行了激烈的辩论。|近义词：passionately, vehemently, angrily, fiercely|反义词：calmly, coolly, placidly, serenely
