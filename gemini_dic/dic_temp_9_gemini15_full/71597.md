flat-footedness:/ˈflætˈfʊtɪdnəs/| n. |扁平足；脚掌平平|The condition of having flat feet, lacking the normal arch in the sole of the foot.  This can lead to pain and discomfort, especially with prolonged standing or walking.  It's often associated with pronation, where the foot rolls inward excessively.|例句：His flat-footedness caused him significant pain when he ran long distances. = 他的扁平足在他长跑时给他造成了很大的痛苦。

例句：The doctor diagnosed her with flat-footedness and recommended supportive footwear. = 医生诊断她患有扁平足，并建议她穿支撑性强的鞋。


近义词：pes planus (medical term)


反义词：high-arched feet


词性：名词
