rebooked:/ˌriːˈbʊkt/| v.|重新预订|To book (something, such as a flight, hotel room, or appointment) again, often after a previous booking was canceled or changed.|例句：I had to rebook my flight because of the storm.  我因为暴风雨不得不重新预订我的航班。

例句：We rebooked our table at the restaurant for a later time. 我们把餐厅的桌子重新预订到稍晚的时间。


近义词：re-reserve, reschedule, re-arrange


反义词：cancel


词性:动词
