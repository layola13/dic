rashful: /ˈræʃfəl/ | adj. | 轻率的，鲁莽的 | Acting or done without careful consideration of the possible consequences; impetuous.  |  例句：His rashful decision to invest all his savings in a risky venture cost him dearly.  他的轻率决定，把所有积蓄投资于高风险企业，让他损失惨重。


例句：It was rashful of her to cross the busy street without looking. 她不看路就穿过繁忙的街道，真是鲁莽。


近义词：reckless, impetuous, impulsive, hasty, foolhardy


反义词：cautious, prudent, careful, deliberate
