groupings:/ˈɡruːpɪŋz/ | n. |分组，组合，群组 |The act or process of grouping things together; a collection of things grouped together.  It refers to the arrangement or classification of items into sets or categories. |例句：The groupings of students were based on their academic performance. 学生分组是根据他们的学业成绩进行的。|例句：The artist's use of color groupings created a striking visual effect. 艺术家对色彩分组的使用创造了一种引人注目的视觉效果。|近义词：clusters, sets, collections, aggregations |反义词：separations, divisions, scattering |名词
