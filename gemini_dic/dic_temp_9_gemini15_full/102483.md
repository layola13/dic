jackstraw: /ˈdʒækstrɔː/ | n. | 稻草人；随意堆放的物体；（游戏）拔草游戏 | A jackstraw is a small stick or straw, typically one of a number used in a children's game where players take turns removing straws from a pile without disturbing the others.  It can also refer to something carelessly arranged or a scarecrow. | Example: The children played jackstraws on the floor. 孩子们在地板上玩拔草游戏。  The old tools were stacked like jackstraws in the corner. 旧工具随意堆放在角落里，像一堆稻草。| 近义词：straw, stick, pile | 反义词：organized, arranged
