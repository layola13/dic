slangish: /ˈslæŋɪʃ/ | adj. |俚语的，非正式的 |  Characterized by the use of slang; informal in style.  It describes language that is casual, informal, and often uses slang terms, not suitable for formal settings. | 例句：His slangish comments made the professor uncomfortable.  他的俚语评论让教授感到不舒服。

例句：The email was too slangish for a business proposal. 这封邮件对于商业提案来说过于口语化。


近义词：colloquial, informal, casual, unrefined

反义词：formal, refined, polished, standard


词性: adjective
