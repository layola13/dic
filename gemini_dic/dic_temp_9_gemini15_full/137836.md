nonvicarious: /ˌnɒnvaɪˈkeəriəs/ | adj. | 非替代性的，非间接体验的 | Not experienced or felt indirectly through another person;  directly experienced.  The opposite of vicarious. | 例句：The nonvicarious suffering of the victims was evident in their haunted expressions.  受害者们非间接体验的痛苦在他们忧郁的表情中显而易见。


例句：He felt a nonvicarious sense of loss after the death of his friend. 他在朋友去世后感受到一种非间接体验的失落感。


近义词：direct, firsthand, personal, immediate


反义词：vicarious, indirect, secondhand


词性：形容词
