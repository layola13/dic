pastel:/ˈpæstəl/| n. adj.|彩色粉笔，柔和的色彩|A pastel is a stick of colored chalk or crayon used for drawing.  Pastel colors are soft and delicate.|The artist used pastels to create a dreamy landscape.  艺术家用彩色粉笔创作了一幅梦幻般的风景画。|近义词：crayon, chalk|反义词：vivid, bright
