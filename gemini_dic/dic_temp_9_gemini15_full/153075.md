pees:/piːz/| n.|尿，小便|Urine; the liquid waste excreted from the body by the kidneys.|Example: The dog peed on the carpet.  狗狗在毯子上尿尿了。|近义词：urine, wee|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pees frequently. = 他经常小便。
The dog pees outside. = 狗在外面尿尿。

一般过去时:
She peed on the flowerbed. = 她尿在花坛上了。
The baby peed through his diaper. = 婴儿尿湿了尿布。

现在完成时:
The cat has peed on the sofa again. = 猫又尿在沙发上了。
He has peed three times this morning. = 他今天早上已经尿了三次。

过去完成时:
The dog had peed before I got home. = 我回家之前，狗已经尿过了。
She had peed all over the floor. = 她把地板都尿湿了。

将来时:
He will pee before we leave. = 我们离开之前他会尿尿。
I think the baby will pee soon. = 我觉得婴儿很快就要尿尿了。

虚拟语气:
If he peed on the carpet again, I would be angry. = 如果他再尿在毯子上，我会很生气。
I wish the cat hadn't peed on my bed. = 我真希望猫没有尿在我的床上。

被动语态:
The carpet was peed on by the dog. = 地毯被狗尿湿了。(This is less common and sounds unnatural)
The floor has been peed on. = 地板被尿湿了。(This is less common and sounds unnatural)

疑问句:
Has the baby peed? = 婴儿尿了吗？
Where did he pee? = 他在哪儿尿的？

否定句:
The dog didn't pee inside. = 狗没有在屋里尿尿。
He hasn't peed yet. = 他还没尿。
