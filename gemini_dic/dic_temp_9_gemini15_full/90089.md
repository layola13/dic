hostility:/hɒˈstɪləti/ /hɑːˈstɪləti/| n. |敌意，对抗，不友好 | Unfriendliness or opposition.  It can refer to an attitude, action, or state of affairs. |例句：The hostility between the two countries led to war.  两个国家之间的敌意导致了战争。
例句：His hostility towards his brother was evident in his cold demeanor. 他对他兄弟的敌意在他冷漠的态度中显而易见。|近义词：antagonism, animosity, enmity, aggression |反义词：friendship, amity, goodwill, cordiality |名词
