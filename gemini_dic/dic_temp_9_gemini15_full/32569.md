chirality:/kaɪˈrælɪti/| n. |手性；对映异构性|The property of a molecule or ion that exists in two forms that are mirror images of each other but cannot be superimposed.|  Many biological molecules exhibit chirality, influencing their function and interactions.|近义词：handedness, enantiomerism|反义词：achirality


例句：

The chirality of carbon atoms is crucial in organic chemistry.  碳原子的手性在有机化学中至关重要。

The drug's chirality determines its effectiveness and potential side effects.  药物的手性决定了它的有效性和潜在的副作用。
