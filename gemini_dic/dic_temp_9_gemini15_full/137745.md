nonvaporously: /ˌnɒnˈveɪpərəsli/ | adv. |非蒸汽地，不蒸发地 |Not in the form of vapor; not evaporating.  |The substance reacted nonvaporously with the acid.  这种物质与酸发生非蒸发反应。|  vaporously |  (no direct antonym,  but "evaporatively" is a contextual opposite)| 副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have tenses like verbs or other grammatical variations like nouns or adjectives.  It describes how an action is performed.

Example Sentences:

The chemical reaction proceeded nonvaporously.  化学反应非蒸发地进行。

The experiment was conducted nonvaporously to prevent loss of volatile components. 实验以非蒸发方式进行，以防止挥发性成分的损失。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases directly using "nonvaporously".  Its usage is highly specific to scientific or technical contexts.
