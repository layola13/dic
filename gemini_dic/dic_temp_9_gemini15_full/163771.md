predelineated: /ˌpriːdɪˈlɪnieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 预先划定的；预先描绘的 |  Already marked out or described in advance.  The term implies a prior definition or outlining of boundaries, limits, or characteristics. |


例句：

The research project had a predelineated scope, focusing only on the effects of climate change on coastal communities. = 研究项目有预先划定的范围，只关注气候变化对沿海社区的影响。

The artist's vision for the mural was predelineated in a detailed sketch before any paint was applied. = 在任何颜料上色之前，艺术家对壁画的构想就已经在详细的素描中预先描绘出来了。


近义词：predetermined, prefigured, prearranged, pre-established


反义词：undefined, unspecified, uncharted


词性：形容词
