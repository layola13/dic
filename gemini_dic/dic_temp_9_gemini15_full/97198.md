inflamingly: /ɪnˈfleɪmɪŋli/ | adv. | 煽动地；激昂地；极度愤怒地 | In a manner that inflames or excites strong emotions, often anger or passion.  |例句：He spoke inflamingly about the injustice of the situation. 他激昂地谈论了这种情况的不公正之处。

例句：The politician delivered an inflamingly passionate speech, inciting the crowd to action.  这位政治家发表了一篇激情澎湃的煽动性演讲，煽动人群采取行动。


近义词：passionately, vehemently, fiercely, angrily, incendiaryly

反义词：calmly, placidly, mildly, temperately


词性：副词
