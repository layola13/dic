hairpin:/ˈhɛrpɪn/| n. |发夹，U型卡，急转弯 |A hairpin is a U-shaped piece of wire, plastic, or metal used to hold hair in place.  It can also refer to a sharp turn in a road. |例句：She used a hairpin to fasten her bun. 她用发夹固定她的发髻。

例句：The road ahead makes a sharp hairpin bend. 前面的路急转弯。


近义词：bobby pin (for hair), sharp bend, switchback |反义词：straight road (for the bend meaning)

词源:
