odontolite:/ˈɒdɒntaʊlaɪt/| n. |牙化石；牙石|A fossilized tooth or tooth-like structure, often composed of calcium phosphate.  Odontolites are typically found in sedimentary rocks and can provide valuable information about ancient ecosystems and the evolution of vertebrates.|Example: The paleontologist carefully examined the odontolite, hoping to identify the species of extinct fish it belonged to. = 古生物学家仔细检查了牙化石，希望能确定它所属的灭绝鱼类。 The discovery of numerous odontolites in the rock formation suggests a significant ancient aquatic habitat. = 在岩层中发现大量的牙化石表明存在重要的古代水生栖息地。|近义词：fossil tooth, tooth fossil|反义词：N/A
