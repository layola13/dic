geekiest:/ˈɡiːkiːst/| adj.|最极客的，最书呆子的|Most extreme or intense form of being a geek;  characterized by an excessive enthusiasm for and knowledge of a particular subject, especially technology, science fiction, or fantasy.  It implies a high level of expertise and often a slightly socially awkward or unconventional demeanor.|例句：He has the geekiest collection of comic books I've ever seen. 他拥有我见过的最极客的漫画书收藏。 He built the geekiest computer setup imaginable. 他组装了你能想象到的最极客的电脑配置。|近义词：nerdiest, dorkiest, most obsessive|反义词：uninterested, unengaged, untechnical
