over-suspicious: /ˌoʊvər səˈspɪʃəs/ | adj. | 过分怀疑的，疑心重的 | Excessively suspicious; tending to suspect wrongdoing where there is none.  | 例句：He's over-suspicious of everyone, even his closest friends. 他对每个人都过度怀疑，甚至包括他最亲密的朋友。  |近义词：distrustful, wary, skeptical, incredulous |反义词：trusting, credulous, gullible
