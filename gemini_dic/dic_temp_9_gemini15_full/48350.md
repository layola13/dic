decrypting:/dɪˈkrɪptɪŋ/ | v. | 解密 | The act of converting encrypted data into its original, readable form.  It involves reversing the encryption process to reveal the plaintext message.  This often requires a decryption key or algorithm. | Example: The police are decrypting the intercepted message to find clues to the crime.  警察正在解密拦截到的信息以寻找犯罪线索。

The process of decrypting the files took several hours. 解密这些文件花了几个小时。 |  enciphering, decoding | encrypting |
