double-check:/ˈdʌbəl tʃɛk/ | vt. |仔细检查，复查|To check something again to make sure it is correct or accurate.  It implies a more thorough and cautious examination than a single check.|例句：I double-checked my work before submitting it. 我提交作业前仔细检查了一遍。|例句：Let's double-check the figures to ensure accuracy. 让我们仔细检查一下数字以确保准确性。|近义词：verify, recheck, review, confirm |反义词：neglect, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I double-check my email before sending it. = 我发送邮件前都会仔细检查一遍。
They double-check the data every day. = 他们每天都会仔细检查数据。


一般过去时:
He double-checked the address before he left. = 他离开前仔细检查了地址。
She double-checked the calculations to avoid mistakes. = 她仔细检查了计算结果以避免错误。


现在完成时:
I have double-checked the report three times. = 我已经检查了报告三次。
They have double-checked all the equipment. = 他们已经仔细检查了所有设备。


过去完成时:
He had double-checked the flight schedule before arriving at the airport. = 他到达机场前已经仔细检查了航班时刻表。
She had double-checked the recipe before starting to cook. = 她开始做饭前已经仔细检查了食谱。


将来时:
I will double-check the contract tomorrow. = 我明天将仔细检查合同。
They will double-check the results before publishing the findings. = 他们将在发表研究结果之前仔细检查结果。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would double-check every detail. = 如果我有时间，我会仔细检查每一个细节。
It would be wise to double-check the information before making a decision. = 在做决定之前仔细检查信息是明智的。


被动语态:
The documents were double-checked by the manager. = 文件由经理仔细检查过。
The calculations need to be double-checked. = 计算结果需要仔细检查。


疑问句:
Did you double-check the answers? = 你检查过答案了吗？
Should we double-check the spelling? = 我们应该仔细检查一下拼写吗？


否定句:
I didn't double-check the email address. = 我没有检查邮件地址。
He didn't double-check his work, and made a mistake. = 他没有仔细检查他的工作，犯了一个错误。
