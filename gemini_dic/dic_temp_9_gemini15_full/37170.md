come-uppings:/ˈkʌmˌʌpɪŋz/| n. | 挫折；报应；教训 | Unexpected setbacks or punishments; a deserved rebuke or punishment.  It often implies a sudden or unexpected negative consequence. |例句：His arrogant behavior finally resulted in a come-uppance when he was publicly humiliated.  他的傲慢行为最终导致了他被公开羞辱的报应。

例句：After years of cheating, she got her comeuppance when she was caught and fired.  多年欺骗之后，她被抓住并解雇，受到了应有的惩罚。


近义词：rebuke, retribution, punishment, setback, defeat

反义词：reward, success, triumph


词性：名词
