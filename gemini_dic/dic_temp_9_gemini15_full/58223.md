druggist:/ˈdrʌɡɪst/| n. |药剂师，药商|A druggist is a person who prepares and sells medicines and drugs.  They are often found in pharmacies or drugstores.  In some places, the term is used interchangeably with pharmacist, while in others, a distinction may be made based on educational qualifications or scope of practice.|例句：The druggist filled my prescription quickly and efficiently.  这位药剂师迅速有效地为我配好了药。


例句：The local druggist was known for his friendly service and vast knowledge of herbal remedies. 当地药剂师以其友好的服务和丰富的草药知识而闻名。|近义词：pharmacist, chemist (in British English)|反义词：无|词性：名词
