roast:/roʊst/| /rəʊst/ | n., v. | 烤肉；烘烤；嘲弄 |

英文解析:As a noun, "roast" refers to meat that has been roasted. As a verb, it means to cook food by dry heat, typically in an oven, or to criticize someone harshly and humorously.

例句:
(Noun): The roast beef was delicious.  = 烤牛肉很美味。

(Verb - cooking):  We roasted a chicken for dinner. = 我们烤了一只鸡当晚餐。

(Verb - criticizing):  The comedians roasted the celebrities mercilessly. = 喜剧演员无情地嘲讽了那些名人。


近义词:
(Noun):  grilled meat, baked meat
(Verb - cooking): bake, grill, barbecue
(Verb - criticizing):  criticize, satirize, ridicule


反义词:
(Verb - cooking): boil, fry
(Verb - criticizing): praise, compliment


词性: Noun, Verb
