shopboy: /ˈʃɒpˌbɔɪ/ | n. | 店员，伙计 | A young male employee in a shop.  The term is now largely archaic or used in historical contexts, often implying a junior or unskilled position. | Example: The shopboy diligently swept the floor and helped customers.  这个店员勤奋地扫地并帮助顾客。 | 近义词：shop assistant, sales assistant, clerk  | 反义词：shop owner, manager
