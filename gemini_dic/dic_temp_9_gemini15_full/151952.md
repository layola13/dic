pastoralists:/ˈpæstərəlɪsts/| n. |游牧民族；牧民|People who practice pastoralism, a way of life based on herding livestock.  They often move seasonally to find pasture for their animals.|Example: The pastoralists migrated with their flocks to higher altitudes during the summer months. 游牧民族在夏季迁移到更高海拔地区放牧他们的牲畜。|近义词：nomads, herdsmen, shepherds|反义词：sedentary farmers
