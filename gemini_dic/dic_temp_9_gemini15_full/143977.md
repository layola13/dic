outrager:/aʊtˈreɪdʒər/| n. |令人愤慨的事；暴行；激怒者 |Something that causes great anger or indignation; a violent or aggressive action; a person who causes outrage. |例句：The bombing was an outrage against humanity.  这次爆炸是对人类的暴行。|例句：His behavior was an absolute outrage. 他的行为简直令人愤慨。|近义词：affront, scandal, atrocity |反义词：delight, pleasure
