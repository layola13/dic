nonvertebrates: /ˌnɒnˈvɜːtɪbrəts/ | n. | 无脊椎动物 | Animals lacking a backbone or spinal column.  They constitute the vast majority of animal species, including insects, mollusks, crustaceans, and many others. | Example:  Insects, spiders, and worms are all examples of nonvertebrates.  昆虫、蜘蛛和蠕虫都是无脊椎动物的例子。  |近义词：invertebrates | 反义词：vertebrates
