lad:/læd/| n. |小伙子，年轻人|A boy or young man.  The word often implies a friendly and informal tone. |例句：He's a nice lad. 他是个好小伙子。|例句：The lads went off to play football.  小伙子们去踢足球了。|近义词：boy, youth, young man, chap |反义词：old man, elderly gentleman


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a friendly lad. = 他是个友好的小伙子。
That lad works hard. = 那个小伙子工作很努力。

一般过去时:
The lad played football yesterday. = 那个小伙子昨天踢足球了。
He was a mischievous lad in his youth. = 他年轻时是个顽皮的小伙子。

现在完成时:
The lad has grown taller. = 这个小伙子长高了。
He has become a responsible lad. = 他已经成为一个负责任的小伙子了。

过去完成时:
The lad had already left when I arrived. = 我到的时候，那个小伙子已经离开了。
Before the competition, the lad had trained hard. = 比赛前，那个小伙子刻苦训练。

将来时:
The lad will become a doctor. = 那个小伙子将来会成为一名医生。
He will go to university next year. = 他明年将上大学。

虚拟语气:
If he were a lad, he would understand. = 如果他是个小伙子，他会理解的。
I wish I were a lad again. = 我真希望自己还是个小伙子。

被动语态:
The lad was praised for his bravery. = 那个小伙子因勇敢而受到表扬。
The task was given to the lad. = 任务交给了那个小伙子。

疑问句:
Is he a local lad? = 他是本地人吗？
Was that lad involved? = 那个小伙子参与了吗？

否定句:
He is not a bad lad. = 他不是个坏小伙子。
The lad didn't attend the meeting. = 那个小伙子没有参加会议。


PHRASE->
a good lad = 好小伙子
a cheeky lad = 顽皮的小伙子
a clever lad = 聪明的小伙子
a young lad = 年轻的小伙子
a poor lad = 可怜的小伙子


词源:
The word "lad" comes from Old English *ladd, meaning "boy, youth."  Its origins are ultimately Germanic.
