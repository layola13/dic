goosefoot:/ˈɡuːsfʊt/| n. |藜属植物；鹅足|A goosefoot is any plant of the genus Chenopodium, a group of herbaceous plants with many species, some of which are edible and others considered weeds.  They are characterized by their broad, somewhat triangular leaves that resemble a goose's foot. |Many goosefoot species are found growing wild along roadsides and in disturbed areas.  |近义词：Chenopodium (学名)|反义词：无
