lichenography:/ˌlaɪkənˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. |地衣学|The scientific study of lichens.  It encompasses their taxonomy, morphology, physiology, ecology, distribution, and uses.  |Example:  My research focuses on lichenography, specifically the symbiotic relationship between fungi and algae in lichen formation. 我的研究集中在地衣学上，特别是地衣形成过程中真菌和藻类之间的共生关系。|近义词:lichenology|反义词:None.
