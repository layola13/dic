reprivatized:/ˌriːˈpraɪvətaɪzd/ | adj. | 再私有化了的 |  Having been returned to private ownership or control after a period of public ownership.  This word describes something that was previously nationalized or owned by the government, and has now been returned to private hands. | Example: The recently reprivatized railway system is facing significant challenges. 最近重新私有化的铁路系统正面临重大挑战。 | 近义词：privatized, re-privatized | 反义词：nationalized, socialized
