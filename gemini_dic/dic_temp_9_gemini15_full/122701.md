moghul:/ˈmʊɡəl/| n. |莫卧儿人；莫卧儿王朝；莫卧儿风格|A member of a Muslim dynasty of Turkic-Mongol origin that ruled much of India from the 16th to the 19th century.  The term also refers to things associated with this dynasty, such as architecture and art.|例句：The Taj Mahal is a stunning example of Mughal architecture.  泰姬陵是莫卧儿建筑的惊人例子。

例句：The Mughal Empire exerted a profound influence on Indian culture. 莫卧儿帝国对印度文化产生了深远的影响。

近义词：Mogul (alternative spelling)


反义词：None directly applicable.  The antonym would depend on the context (e.g., if referring to the empire, a possible antonym could be "British Raj" in the Indian context).
