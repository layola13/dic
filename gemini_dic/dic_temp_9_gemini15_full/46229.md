cystocele:/ˈsɪstoʊˌsiːl/| n. |膀胱膨出（子宫脱垂的一种）|A cystocele is a condition in which the bladder protrudes into the vagina.  It's a type of pelvic organ prolapse, often caused by weakening of the pelvic floor muscles. Symptoms can include urinary frequency, urgency, incontinence, and a feeling of pressure or fullness in the vagina.
