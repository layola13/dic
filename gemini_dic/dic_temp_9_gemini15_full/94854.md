in a bad way:/ɪn ə bæd weɪ/| adj. phrase |非常糟糕地，严重地，极度地|This phrase describes something that is severely affected or in a very poor condition. It emphasizes the extent of the negative impact.|例句：His health is in a bad way.他的健康状况非常糟糕。
The economy is in a bad way.经济状况非常糟糕。|近义词：seriously, severely, terribly, drastically|反义词：in good condition, flourishing, thriving

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car is in a bad way. = 这辆车状况很糟糕。
His business is in a bad way. = 他的生意状况很糟糕。


一般过去时:
The house was in a bad way after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，房子状况很糟糕。
Her mood was in a bad way after the news. = 听到这个消息后，她的心情很糟糕。


现在完成时:
The bridge has been in a bad way for years. = 这座桥多年来状况都很糟糕。
My health has been in a bad way since the accident. = 自从事故发生后，我的健康状况一直很糟糕。


过去完成时:
The engine had been in a bad way for months before it finally broke down. = 引擎在最终坏掉之前，已经状况很糟糕好几个月了。
Their marriage had been in a bad way for a long time before they separated. = 在他们分开之前，他们的婚姻状况很糟糕已经很久了。


将来时:
If we don't fix it, the roof will be in a bad way soon. = 如果我们不修理它，屋顶很快就会状况很糟糕。
The situation will be in a bad way if we don't act quickly. = 如果我们不迅速采取行动，情况将会非常糟糕。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the car was in a bad way, I wouldn't have bought it. = 如果我知道这辆车状况很糟糕，我就不会买了。
I wish my finances weren't in a bad way. = 我希望我的财务状况不要这么糟糕。


被动语态:
The road was left in a bad way after the construction. = 建设结束后，道路状况很糟糕。
The garden has been left in a bad way by the recent frost. = 最近的霜冻使花园状况很糟糕。


疑问句:
Is the situation really in a bad way? = 情况真的这么糟糕吗？
Is your health in a bad way? = 你的健康状况很糟糕吗？


否定句:
The building isn't in a bad way. = 这栋建筑状况还不错。
His spirits aren't in a bad way. = 他的精神状态还不错。
