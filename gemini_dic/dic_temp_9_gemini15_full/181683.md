relenting: /rɪˈlɛntɪŋ/ | adj. | 不放松的；持续不断的；坚决的 |  unyielding; persistent; continuous; not softening or weakening |

英文解析：Relenting describes something that continues without stopping or weakening. It implies a firmness or persistence that doesn't give in easily.  It can refer to a person's actions, a situation, or a force of nature.

例句：

The unrelenting rain flooded the streets. = 持续不断的暴雨淹没了街道。

His unrelenting pursuit of justice finally brought the criminal to trial. = 他坚持不懈地追求正义，最终将罪犯绳之以法。


近义词：persistent, incessant, continuous, unwavering, relentless, steadfast

反义词：yielding, ceasing, stopping, intermittent, sporadic
