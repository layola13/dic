preinspect:/ˌpriːɪnˈspekt/| v.| 预先检查；事先检验 | To inspect something before it is put into use or before a particular event.  |例句：The engineer will preinspect the bridge before opening it to traffic.工程师将在桥梁通车前对其进行预先检查。

例句：We need to preinspect all the equipment before the experiment. 我们需要在实验前预先检查所有设备。


近义词：inspect beforehand, examine in advance, check beforehand

反义词：post-inspect (事后检查)


词性:动词
