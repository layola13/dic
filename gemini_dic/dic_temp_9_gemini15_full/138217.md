noshing:/ˈnɒʃɪŋ/ | n. | 随便吃点东西；吃零食 | The act of eating small amounts of food between meals, often snacks.  | 例句：We spent the afternoon noshing on popcorn and watching movies. 我们下午一边吃爆米花一边看电影。  | 例句：She was noshing on a bag of chips while she worked. 她一边工作一边吃薯片。 | 近义词：snacking, nibbling, munching | 反义词：gorging, feasting
