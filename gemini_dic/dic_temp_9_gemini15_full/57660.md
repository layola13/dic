drawbridge:/ˈdrɔːbrɪdʒ/ | n. |吊桥 |A bridge that can be raised or lowered to allow ships or boats to pass underneath.  It typically consists of one or two long sections that are hinged at one end and can be raised or lowered by means of a counterweight or other mechanism. |例句：The drawbridge slowly rose, allowing the large ship to pass through. 吊桥慢慢升起，让大型船只通过。

例句：The medieval castle was protected by a sturdy drawbridge. 中世纪城堡由坚固的吊桥保护着。


近义词：movable bridge, lifting bridge |反义词：fixed bridge, permanent bridge |词性：名词
