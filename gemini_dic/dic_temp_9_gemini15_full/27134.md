calisthenics:/ˌkælɪsˈθenɪks/ | n. | 健美操；体操；徒手操 |  Exercises done without equipment to improve strength and flexibility. Calisthenics often involves bodyweight exercises such as push-ups, pull-ups, squats, and lunges.  |  例句：He keeps fit by doing calisthenics every morning. 他每天早上做健美操来保持健康。

例句：  Her fitness routine includes yoga and calisthenics. 她的健身方案包括瑜伽和健美操。
