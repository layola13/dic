internationality: /ˌɪntərˌnæʃənˈæləti/ | n. | 国际性，国际关系 | The quality or state of being international; the involvement or participation of multiple nations.  It refers to the characteristics, relationships, or interactions that transcend national boundaries. | Example: The increasing internationality of the economy is evident in the global supply chains.  越来越多的经济国际化体现在全球供应链中。 The conference showcased the internationality of scientific collaboration.  此次会议展示了科学合作的国际性。|近义词：globality, universality, worldliness | 反义词：nationalism, provincialism
