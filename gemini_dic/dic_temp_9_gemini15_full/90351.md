hoveling:/ˈhɒvəlɪŋ/ | n. | 窝棚居住；简陋住所；栖身之所 |The act of living in a hovel;  dwelling in a small, poorly constructed dwelling.  It often implies poverty and substandard living conditions. |例句：The family's hoveling in the dilapidated shack was heartbreaking to witness.  这家住在破旧棚屋里的景象令人心碎。|近义词：squatting, makeshift housing, rudimentary dwelling |反义词： palatial living, luxurious accommodation
