orthocarbonic:/ˌɔːrθoʊkɑːrˈbɒnɪk/| adj. |正碳酸的；原碳酸的| Relating to or denoting orthocarbonic acid, a hypothetical acid with the formula C(OH)₄.|Orthocarbonic acid is a hypothetical compound, meaning it hasn't been directly observed.  Its esters, however, are known.  The term is used in chemistry to describe compounds derived from this theoretical acid.|The hypothetical orthocarbonic acid is related to methane by replacing each hydrogen atom with a hydroxyl group. |近义词：tetrahydroxymethane (as a chemical descriptor)|反义词：N/A
