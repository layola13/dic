clodhoppers:/ˈklɒdˌhɒpərz/| n.|笨手笨脚的人；穿着厚重笨拙鞋的人|A clodhopper is a person who is clumsy or awkward, often associated with rural areas and heavy, clumsy footwear.  The term implies a lack of grace and sophistication.|例句：He's a bit of a clodhopper, always tripping over his own feet. 他有点笨手笨脚，总是绊倒自己。|例句：The farmer, a true clodhopper, trudged through the muddy field.  那个农民，一个地地道道的粗人，在泥泞的田地里跋涉。|近义词：bumpkin, yokel, lout|反义词：refined person, sophisticated individual
