obesity:/oʊˈbiːsəti/|/əʊˈbiːsɪti/| n. |肥胖症，过度肥胖 |The state of being grossly fat or overweight.  Obesity is a serious health problem associated with various diseases. |例句：His obesity contributed to his heart problems. 他的肥胖导致了他的心脏问题。

例句：The doctor warned him about the dangers of obesity. 医生警告他肥胖的危害。


近义词：corpulence, plumpness, overweight

反义词：slimness, thinness


词性：名词
