hippophagi:/ˌhɪpəˈfædʒi/| n. |食马癖，食马症|The practice of eating horse meat.  This term is often used in a clinical or scientific context, referring to a compulsive disorder involving the consumption of horse flesh.|The study revealed a rare case of hippophagi in the patient.|近义词：equophagy|反义词：None specifically, but terms implying aversion to horse meat could be considered antonyms.
