词源:
The word "deckchair" is a compound word.  "Deck" refers to a wooden floor, often on a ship, but also used to refer to a terrace or patio. "Chair" is the common word for a seat. The combination implies a chair suitable for use on a deck or similar outdoor space.  The word's usage became popular during the Victorian era when outdoor leisure activities increased.
