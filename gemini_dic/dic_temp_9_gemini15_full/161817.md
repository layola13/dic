practical:/ˈpræktɪkəl/ | adj. |实际的；实用的；可行的；务实的 | Relating to practice or action, rather than theory; capable of being done or used.  | 例句：This is a practical solution to the problem.  这是一个解决问题的实用方案。
                  

                  The course provides practical training in car maintenance. 这门课程提供汽车保养方面的实践培训。


近义词：usable, workable, feasible, realistic | 反义词：theoretical, impractical, unfeasible, idealistic
