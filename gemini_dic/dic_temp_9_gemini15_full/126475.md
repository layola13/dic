mythologist:/mɪˈθɒlədʒɪst/| n. |神话学家，神话研究者 |A mythologist is a scholar who studies myths and legends.  Mythologists analyze the origins, structures, and meanings of myths across different cultures and time periods. They often consider the social, psychological, and religious functions of myths within their respective societies. |Example:  The mythologist presented a compelling argument for the symbolic significance of the dragon in ancient Chinese culture.  神话学家提出了一个令人信服的论点，论证了龙在中国古代文化中的象征意义。|近义词：folklorist, legend scholar |反义词：无
