condition:/kənˈdɪʃən/ | n., v. |条件，状况，情况；使适应，使符合条件 |


英文解析:As a noun, "condition" refers to a state or situation something is in, or a requirement that must be met. As a verb, it means to adapt or make something fit a particular requirement.


例句(Noun):

The condition of the car is poor. = 这辆车的状况很差。

We agreed to the deal under certain conditions. = 我们在某些条件下同意了这笔交易。


例句(Verb):

They conditioned the dog to sit on command. = 他们训练狗按照命令坐下。

The teacher conditioned the students to study hard. = 老师使学生养成努力学习的习惯。


近义词(Noun):state, situation, circumstance, requirement, stipulation

近义词(Verb):train, accustom, adapt


反义词(Noun):N/A (depends on context)  A direct antonym isn't always applicable.  For example, the opposite of "good condition" might be "poor condition".

反义词(Verb):N/A (depends on context)


词性:Noun, Verb


词源:From Old French *condicion, from Latin condicio "agreement, stipulation, state, quality," from con- "together" + dicere "to say," related to dictum "something said."


USAGE->

What condition is the house in? = 这房子是什么状况？
The terms and conditions state that... = 条款和条件规定……


一般过去时:
What was the condition of the patient? = 病人的情况如何？

现在完成时:
They have conditioned their dog to fetch. = 他们已经训练他们的狗去取物。
The rain has improved the condition of the crops. = 雨水改善了农作物的长势。


过去完成时:

将来时:
What condition will the patient be in tomorrow? = 病人明天会是什么情况？

虚拟语气:
If the conditions were better, we would have succeeded. = 如果条件更好，我们会成功的。
I wish the condition of the road was better. = 我希望路况更好。


被动语态:

The athletes were conditioned rigorously for the competition. = 运动员为比赛进行了严格的训练。


疑问句:

Under what conditions will this reaction occur? = 在什么条件下会发生这种反应？


否定句:
