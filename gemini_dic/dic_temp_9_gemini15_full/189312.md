safe:/seɪf/ | adj., n., v. | 安全的；保险箱；使安全 |

英文解析:
As an adjective, "safe" means free from danger or risk; secure.  As a noun, it refers to a strong metal box or container for storing valuables, typically with a lock. As a verb, it means to protect from harm or danger; secure.

例句:
adj: The children are safe at home. 孩子们在家很安全。

adj: It's not safe to swim in this river.  在这个河里游泳不安全。


n: He keeps his passport in a safe. 他把护照放在保险箱里。

n: The bank uses high-security safes. 银行使用高安全性的保险箱。


v:  Let's safe the documents before the fire spreads. 在火势蔓延之前让我们把文件保存好。

v: We need to safe the hostages. 我们需要保护人质。

反义词:
