outwriggles: /ˌaʊtˈrɪɡlz/ | v. |  蜿蜒而出，扭动着前进 | To wriggle or twist one's way out of something; to escape or extricate oneself by wriggling.  | 例句：The snake outwriggled from the narrow crevice.  蛇从狭窄的裂缝中蜿蜒而出。 The child outwriggled his mother's grasp. 孩子挣脱了母亲的抓握。 | 近义词： wriggle out, squirm out, extricate, escape | 反义词：  become entangled, get stuck
