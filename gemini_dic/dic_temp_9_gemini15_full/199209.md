silkies:/ˈsɪlkiːz/| n.|苏格兰羽衣甘蓝，丝绸般的，光滑的|Silkies refer to mythical beings in Scottish folklore, often described as having seal-like features and beautiful, silky hair.  They are also sometimes used to describe something with a silky texture.|例句：The legend of the selkies is a popular story in Scottish folklore. 苏格兰羽衣甘蓝的传说在苏格兰民间传说中很受欢迎。|近义词：merfolk, selkies (often used interchangeably)|反义词：rough, coarse.
