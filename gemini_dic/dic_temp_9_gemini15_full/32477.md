chincherie:/ʃɪnˈʃəriː/| n. |小玩意儿；小装饰品；琐碎的事物 |A collection of small, attractive, often inexpensive items; trifles; trinkets.  It often implies something showy but ultimately insignificant.  |例句：The shop was filled with a delightful chincherie of antique buttons and vintage postcards. 这家商店里摆满了古董纽扣和复古明信片等赏心悦目的精美小玩意儿。

例句：Her apartment was cluttered with chincherie, mostly useless but pretty things.  她的公寓里堆满了小玩意儿，大部分没用但很漂亮。


近义词：trinket, knick-knack, gewgaw, bauble, bibelot

反义词：essential, necessity, important item


词源：源自法语“chinerie”，意为“小玩意儿”，最终可能与“chinois” (中国人)相关，暗示着这些小玩意儿具有东方风格的精致感。  However, the etymological connection is not definitively established.
