sensational:/senˈseɪʃənəl/| adj.|轰动的；极好的；耸人听闻的| causing great public excitement or interest; extremely good.  |例句：The singer's new album has been a sensational success.  这位歌手的新专辑获得了巨大的成功。
The food at the restaurant was sensational.  这家餐馆的食物棒极了。|近义词：amazing, stunning, spectacular, extraordinary, remarkable|反义词：unremarkable, ordinary, mundane, unexciting
