discoloured: /dɪsˈkʌləd/ | adj. | 变色的，褪色的 | Having lost or changed colour; stained.  |例句：The old painting was discoloured with age.  这幅旧画因年代久远而褪色了。

The sun discoloured the curtains. 太阳晒褪了窗帘的颜色。


近义词：faded, stained, stained,  tarnished,  bleached


反义词：brightly coloured, vibrant


词性：形容词
