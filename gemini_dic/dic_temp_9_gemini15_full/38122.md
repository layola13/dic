comprehend:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛnd/ /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛnd/ | vt. |理解，领会|To understand something completely; grasp the meaning of something.|He finally comprehended the complex instructions.|近义词：understand, grasp, perceive|反义词：misunderstand, misconceive|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I comprehend the situation now. = 我现在理解这个情况了。
They comprehend the instructions perfectly. = 他们完全理解了这些指示。

一般过去时:
I comprehended the lecture after rereading the notes. = 在重新阅读笔记后，我理解了讲座的内容。
She comprehended the meaning of the poem only after several readings. = 她读了几遍之后才理解了这首诗的意思。

现在完成时:
I have comprehended the main points of the argument. = 我已经理解了论点的要点。
We have comprehended the importance of this project. = 我们已经理解了这个项目的重要性。

过去完成时:
I had comprehended the problem before he explained it. = 在他解释之前，我已经理解了这个问题。
She had comprehended the implications of her decision before she acted. = 在她采取行动之前，她已经理解了她决定的含义。

将来时:
I will comprehend this concept better after further study. = 进一步学习后，我将更好地理解这个概念。
They will comprehend the significance of this event in time. = 他们最终会理解这一事件的重要性。

虚拟语气:

I wish I comprehended quantum physics. = 我希望我能理解量子物理学。
