repatching:/ˌriːˈpætʃɪŋ/| n. v.|重新修补；再次修补|The act of repairing something that has already been patched, often implying a more extensive or thorough repair than the initial patching.  It can refer to the process itself or the result.|例句：The road required extensive repatching after the winter storms. 冬季暴风雨过后，这条路需要大规模的重新修补。

The repatching of the roof was a costly undertaking. 屋顶的重新修补是一项代价高昂的工作。|近义词：repairing, mending, patching, refixing|反义词：damaging, destroying, ruining|
