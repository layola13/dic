dachshund:/ˈdækshʊnd/ | n. |腊肠犬，达克斯猎犬 | A small dog with a long body and short legs, originally bred for hunting badgers.  |例句：My neighbor has a charming dachshund named Winston. 我的邻居有一只名叫温斯顿的可爱的腊肠犬。

例句：She adopted a smooth-haired dachshund from the animal shelter. 她从动物收容所领养了一只短毛腊肠犬。


近义词：sausage dog (informal) | 反义词：无


词源:  The word "dachshund" is of German origin. It combines "dachs" (badger) and "hund" (dog), literally meaning "badger dog".  This reflects the breed's original purpose of hunting badgers.
