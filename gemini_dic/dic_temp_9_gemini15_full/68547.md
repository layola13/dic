fanfair:/ˈfænfeər/| n. |隆重的欢迎；炫耀；吹捧 |A fanfare is a short, loud, and triumphant piece of music, typically played by brass instruments.  It is often used to announce the arrival of an important person or event.  It can also refer to a showy display of attention or publicity. |Example: The arrival of the president was heralded by a fanfare of trumpets.  总统的到来由一阵嘹亮的号角声预告。

The new product launch was accompanied by a great fanfare. 新产品的发布伴随着盛大的宣传活动。|近义词：flourish, display, show, celebration |反义词：silence, obscurity, humility
