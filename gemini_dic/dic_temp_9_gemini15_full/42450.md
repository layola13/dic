counterdraw:/ˈkaʊntədrɔː/ | v. | 反向描绘，摹绘反面 | To make a copy of a drawing by tracing the back of the original; to reproduce a design by transferring its impression from the reverse side.  | Example: The artist counterdrew the sketch onto the canvas, reversing the image.  艺术家将素描反向描绘到画布上，反转了图像。 |近义词：trace, copy, reproduce | 反义词：originate, create
