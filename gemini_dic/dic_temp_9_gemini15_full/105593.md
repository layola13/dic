kindly:/ˈkaɪndli/ | adv. | 亲切地；和蔼地；恳请 |  Kindly means in a kind, gentle, or helpful way.  It can also be used as a polite request. |例句：He kindly offered to help me with my bags. 他好心地帮我提行李。

例句：Would you kindly close the door? 请你关一下门好吗？


近义词：courteously, politely, graciously | 反义词：rudely, harshly, unkindly | 词性:副词
