dittoing:/ˈdɪtoʊɪŋ/| v.|重复；照抄；附议|The act of repeating or copying something, especially from a previous document or statement. It often implies a lack of originality or independent thought.  |例句：He was dittoing the same old excuses. 他在重复那些老套的借口。 |例句：The secretary was busy dittoing the documents.  秘书正忙着抄写文件。|近义词：repeating, copying, echoing, reiterating |反义词：originating, innovating
