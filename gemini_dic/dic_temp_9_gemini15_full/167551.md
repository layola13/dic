prewarned:/priˈwɔːrnd/| adj.|预先警告的，已被预先告知的|Having been warned in advance.  The adjective "prewarned" describes something or someone who has received a warning beforehand. It emphasizes the prior knowledge of a potential event or danger.|例句：The residents were prewarned about the impending storm.居民们已被预先告知即将到来的风暴。 The prewarned hikers took extra precautions.已被预先告知的徒步旅行者采取了额外的预防措施。|近义词：forewarned, alerted, notified|反义词：unwarned, unprepared
