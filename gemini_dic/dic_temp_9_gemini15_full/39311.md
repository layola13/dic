coniine:/ˈkɒniːn/ | n. | 毒芹碱 | A highly toxic alkaloid found in poison hemlock (Conium maculatum), responsible for its poisonous nature.  It acts as a neuromuscular blocking agent, paralyzing muscles and eventually causing respiratory failure. | Example: Socrates was famously executed using a drink containing coniine. 苏格拉底因饮用含有毒芹碱的饮料而被处死。 | 近义词：hemlock poison (referring to the source) | 反义词：无  |  名词
