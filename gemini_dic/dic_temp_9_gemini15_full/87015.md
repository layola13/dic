hens:/henz/| n.|母鸡，雌鸡|A hen is an adult female chicken, kept for its eggs or meat.  |例句：The hen laid a brown egg this morning.  今早母鸡下了一个棕色的蛋。

例句：We have several hens in our backyard. 我们后院养了几只母鸡。


近义词：female chicken, pullet (young hen)
反义词：rooster, cock (male chicken)
词性:名词

The hen lays eggs every day. 母鸡每天下蛋。

一般过去时:

将来时:
The hen will lay more eggs next week. 母鸡下周会下更多蛋。
