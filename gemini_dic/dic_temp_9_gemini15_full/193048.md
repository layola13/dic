secreter:/sɪˈkriːtər/| n.|分泌物，分泌者|A secreter is a substance that secretes something, or the thing secreted.  It's often used in a biological context, referring to a gland or cell that produces and releases a substance.|The pancreas is a secreter of digestive enzymes. 胰腺是消化酶的分泌器官。|The plant secretes a sticky secreter to trap insects. 植物分泌粘性分泌物来捕捉昆虫。|gland, producer|absorber(反义，就分泌物的吸收而言)|名词
