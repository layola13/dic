obsessed:/əbˈsɛst/ | adj. |着迷的；困扰的；沉迷的 | Extremely preoccupied with or engrossed in something.  Characterized by excessive or compulsive preoccupation with something. |例句：He's obsessed with computer games. 他沉迷于电脑游戏。

例句：She's obsessed with her weight. 她对自己的体重非常在意。


近义词：preoccupied, fixated, engrossed, captivated, hooked |反义词：uninterested, indifferent, unconcerned


词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is obsessed with conspiracy theories. = 他痴迷于阴谋论。
She is obsessed with her work. = 她沉迷于工作。


一般过去时:
He was obsessed with finding the lost treasure. = 他痴迷于寻找失落的宝藏。
She was obsessed with a particular pop star. = 她迷恋一位特定的流行歌星。


现在完成时:
I have been obsessed with this project for months. = 我已经痴迷于这个项目几个月了。
They have been obsessed with the idea of starting their own business. = 他们一直痴迷于自己创业的想法。


过去完成时:
He had been obsessed with the painting for years before he finally bought it. = 在他最终买下这幅画之前，他已经痴迷于它多年了。
She had been obsessed with her appearance since she was a teenager. = 从十几岁起，她就一直很在意自己的外表。


将来时:
I will be obsessed with my new phone for at least a week. = 我至少会沉迷于我的新手机一周。
He will probably be obsessed with this game until he finishes it. = 他可能会沉迷于这个游戏直到他完成它。


虚拟语气:
If I were obsessed with something, I would probably neglect my responsibilities. = 如果我沉迷于某事，我可能会忽略我的责任。
I wish I weren't obsessed with social media. = 我希望我不沉迷于社交媒体。


被动语态:  (Obsessed is rarely used passively)  被动语态通常不使用obsessed.


疑问句:
Is he obsessed with anything in particular? = 他特别痴迷于什么吗？
Are you obsessed with your fitness routine? = 你沉迷于你的健身计划吗？


否定句:
He is not obsessed with his job, he simply enjoys it. = 他并没有沉迷于他的工作，他只是享受它。
She is not obsessed with fashion, although she is stylish. = 她并不沉迷于时尚，尽管她很时尚。


PHRASE->
be obsessed with:  沉迷于...
become obsessed:  变得痴迷
obsessed fan:  狂热粉丝
obsessive behavior:  强迫行为
obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD): 强迫症
