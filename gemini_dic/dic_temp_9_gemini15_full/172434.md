pyemia:/paɪˈiːmiə/| n. |败血症，脓毒血症 | Septicemia caused by the presence of pus-forming bacteria in the blood.  Pyemia is a serious and potentially life-threatening condition characterized by the presence of pus-forming bacteria in the bloodstream, leading to the formation of abscesses in various organs. | Example: The patient presented with symptoms consistent with pyemia, including high fever, chills, and widespread abscess formation.  这位病人出现了败血症的症状，包括高烧、寒战和广泛的脓肿形成。|近义词：septicemia, septicaemia |反义词：None.
