overpurchase: /ˌoʊvərˈpɜːrtʃəs/ | vt. |过度购买，购置过多 | To buy more of something than is needed or can be used.  | 例句：The company overpurchased office supplies, leading to significant waste. 公司过度购买办公用品，导致大量浪费。| 例句：He overpurchased groceries and ended up throwing away half of them. 他买了过多的杂货，结果扔掉了一半。|近义词：overbuy, buy excessively | 反义词：underbuy, conserve
