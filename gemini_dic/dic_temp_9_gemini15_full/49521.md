demandingly:/dɪˈmændɪŋli/| adv.|苛求地；要求严格地；费力的|In a demanding manner; requiring much effort, skill, or attention.|例句：He looked at her demandingly. 他苛求地看着她。

例句：She demandingly asked for a promotion. 她要求晋升，语气十分强硬。


近义词：exactingly, rigorously, stringently, insistently

反义词：easily, readily, leniently


词源：源自demanding (adj.) + -ly (adv. suffix).  demanding itself comes from the verb "demand," ultimately from Old French *demander and Latin *demandare.
