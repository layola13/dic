prodromous: /prəˈdrōməs/ | adj. | 先兆的；预兆的 |  relating to or denoting symptoms that precede the onset of a disease.  It describes the early signs or symptoms that appear before a more serious illness or condition fully develops.  | 例句：The prodromous symptoms of the flu include fatigue and a headache.  流感的先兆症状包括疲劳和头痛。

The prodromous stage of measles is characterized by fever and a cough. 麻疹的先兆期以发烧和咳嗽为特征。


近义词：premonitory, preliminary, antecedent, initial

反义词：consequent, subsequent, following


词性：形容词
