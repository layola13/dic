scepterellate:/ˈsɛptərəleɪt/| adj.|权杖状的；有权杖的 | Having the form or appearance of a scepter; resembling a scepter.  The term is primarily used in a descriptive botanical context, referring to structures that are long, slender, and somewhat club-shaped, like a scepter. | Example: The flower's elongated pistil had a scepterellate form.  花朵细长的雌蕊呈权杖状。| 近义词:club-shaped, rod-like, wand-like | 反义词:None specifically, but words implying a different shape would be antonyms (e.g., flat, broad, rounded).
