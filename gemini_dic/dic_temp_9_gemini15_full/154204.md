peripatetic: /ˌpɛrɪpəˈtɛtɪk/ | adj. |巡游的；漫游的；流动的；（尤指）四处讲学的|

英文解析:  Relating to or denoting a person who travels from place to place, especially working or based in various places for relatively short periods.  Often used to describe a teacher who travels to different locations to teach.

例句:

He led a peripatetic life, moving from city to city in search of work.  = 他过着四处漂泊的生活，为了工作而辗转于各个城市之间。

Her peripatetic teaching career took her to schools all over the country. = 她四处奔波的教学生涯带她走遍了全国各地的学校。


近义词: itinerant, wandering, roaming, nomadic

反义词: sedentary, stationary, settled


词性: adjective (形容词)
