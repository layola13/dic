philosophism:/ˈfɪləˌsɒfɪzəm/ | n. |哲学思想；哲学体系；哲学空谈 | The pursuit of philosophical systems or doctrines, often with an emphasis on abstract speculation rather than practical application.  It can also refer to pretentious or shallow philosophical discussions. | Example: His writings are characterized by a certain kind of philosophism, more concerned with elegant argumentation than with addressing real-world problems.  他的著作以某种哲学思想为特征，更关注优雅的论证，而不是解决现实世界的问题。| 近义词：philosophy, metaphysics, speculation | 反义词：practicality, pragmatism | 名词
