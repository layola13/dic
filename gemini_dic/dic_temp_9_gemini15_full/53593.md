disburse:/dɪsˈbɜːrs/| vt.|支付；发放；散布|To pay out (money); to distribute.|The company disbursed bonuses to its employees at the end of the year.  公司在年底向员工发放奖金。|The government disbursed funds for disaster relief. 政府拨款用于救灾。|pay out, distribute, allocate|hoard, retain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company disburses salaries monthly. = 公司每月发放工资。
He disburses his time wisely. = 他明智地分配他的时间。


一般过去时:
They disbursed the funds to the charities. = 他们把资金发放给了慈善机构。
The bank disbursed a large sum of money. = 银行支付了一大笔款项。


现在完成时:
The organization has disbursed over $1 million this year. = 该组织今年已发放超过100万美元。
She has disbursed her inheritance carefully. = 她谨慎地分配了她的遗产。


过去完成时:
They had disbursed all the supplies before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经发放了所有物资。
The government had disbursed emergency aid before the crisis worsened. = 危机恶化之前，政府已经发放了紧急援助。


将来时:
The company will disburse profits to shareholders next month. = 公司下个月将向股东分配利润。
The school will disburse the grants to the students soon. = 学校很快就会向学生发放助学金。


虚拟语气:
If the government disbursed more funds, the project would succeed. = 如果政府拨出更多资金，该项目就能成功。
I wish they had disbursed the money more fairly. = 我希望他们能更公平地分配这笔钱。


被动语态:
The funds were disbursed to the various departments. = 资金被发放给了各个部门。
The money will be disbursed according to the agreement. = 钱将按照协议支付。


疑问句:
How will the money be disbursed? = 钱将如何分配？
When will the company disburse the bonuses? = 公司什么时候发放奖金？


否定句:
The bank did not disburse the loan as promised. = 银行没有按照承诺发放贷款。
They have not yet disbursed the payments to the contractors. = 他们尚未向承包商支付款项。

disburse information = 传播信息
disburse among = 在…之间分配
