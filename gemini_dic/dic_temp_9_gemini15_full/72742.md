fog-bound:/ˈfɒɡbaʊnd/ | adj. | 浓雾笼罩的，被雾阻碍的 |  Describes a place or situation that is enveloped in, or significantly affected by, dense fog, often implying obstruction or difficulty in movement or visibility. |例句：The mountain pass was fog-bound, delaying our journey.  山路被浓雾笼罩，延误了我们的行程。

The ship remained fog-bound in the harbor for three days. 这艘船在港口因浓雾滞留了三天。


近义词：fog-laden, mist-shrouded, obscured by fog | 反义词：clear, sunny, unobstructed | 词性：形容词
