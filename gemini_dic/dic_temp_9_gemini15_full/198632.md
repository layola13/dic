shushed:/ʃʌʃt/| v. adj.| 安静的；嘘声；使安静 | To make someone be quiet, or to become quiet.  |例句：The librarian shushed the noisy children. 图书管理员让吵闹的孩子们安静下来。|近义词：silenced, hushed, quieted |反义词：encouraged, incited


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shushes the baby. = 他让婴儿安静。
She shushes the noisy crowd. = 她让吵闹的人群安静下来。

一般过去时:
The teacher shushed the class. = 老师让全班安静。
He shushed her quickly. = 他迅速地让她安静下来。

现在完成时:
She has shushed the children many times today. = 她今天已经多次让孩子们安静下来了。
I have shushed them so many times I'm losing my voice. = 我已经多次让他们安静下来，我的声音都哑了。

过去完成时:
The teacher had shushed the students before the principal entered. = 校长进来之前，老师已经让学生们安静下来了。
They had shushed the audience before the performance began. = 演出开始前，他们已经让观众安静下来了。

将来时:
I will shush the dog if it barks again. = 如果那条狗再叫，我会让它安静。
She will shush him when he starts to complain. = 当他开始抱怨时，她会让他安静下来。

虚拟语气:
If I could shush the world, I would. = 如果我能让全世界安静下来，我会这么做的。
I wish I could shush my noisy neighbour. = 我希望我能让我吵闹的邻居安静下来。

被动语态:
The children were shushed by the librarian. = 孩子们被图书管理员让安静下来了。
The audience was shushed before the speaker began. = 演讲者开始演讲前，观众被要求安静。

疑问句:
Were the children shushed? = 孩子们被要求安静了吗？
Has she ever been shushed in a library? = 她曾在图书馆被要求安静吗？

否定句:
He didn't shush the dog. = 他没有让狗安静。
The teacher didn't shush the disruptive student. = 老师没有让那个扰乱课堂的学生安静。


PHRASE->
be shushed = 被要求安静
get shushed = 被嘘
shush someone down = 让某人安静
shush up = 闭嘴
a shushing sound = 嘘声
