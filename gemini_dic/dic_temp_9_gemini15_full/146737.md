overhotly: /ˌoʊvərˈhɑːtli/ | adv. | 过热地；过度热情地 | excessively hot; with excessive enthusiasm or passion. |  The engine was running overhotly.  引擎运转过热。


例句：

He reacted overhotly to the criticism. 他对批评反应过度。

She embraced the new opportunity overhotly. 她过于热情地拥抱了新的机会。


近义词：excessively, intensely, fervently, passionately


反义词：calmly, coolly, apathetically, indifferently


词性：副词


词源：

over- (prefix indicating excess or exaggeration) + hotly (adverb meaning in a hot manner)  "over-" 前缀表示过量或夸张，"hotly" 副词表示以热烈的方式。  因此，"overhotly" 指的是以过热或过度热情的方式。
