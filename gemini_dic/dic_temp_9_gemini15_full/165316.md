preinventory:/ˌpriːˈɪnvənˌtɔːri/| n. |盘点前的库存，预先盘点|An assessment of inventory made before a formal inventory count.  It helps to identify discrepancies and improve the accuracy of the final inventory. |Example: The preinventory showed a significant discrepancy in the number of widgets compared to the sales records. 预先盘点显示，与销售记录相比，部件数量存在重大差异。|近义词： preliminary inventory, pre-count |反义词： post-inventory, final inventory
