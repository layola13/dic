preapproval: /ˌpriːəˈpruːvəl/ | n. | 预先批准，预先核准 | Approval given in advance.  Preapproval often refers to a process where someone is given permission before they actually do something, avoiding delays later. This is common in financial transactions and applications. | 例句：The bank requires preapproval for all large loans. = 银行要求所有大额贷款都必须预先批准。

The company received preapproval for the project from the board. = 公司获得了董事会对该项目的预先批准。


近义词：prior authorization, advance approval, prior sanction


反义词：post-approval, retroactive approval


词性：名词
