sleeplike: /ˈsliːplaɪk/ | adj. | 像睡着一样的；昏昏欲睡的 | Resembling sleep; drowsy; lethargic.  |例句：He fell into a sleeplike state after taking the medication. 服药后，他陷入了一种昏昏欲睡的状态。

例句：The atmosphere in the room was sleeplike and peaceful. 房间里的气氛像睡着了一样宁静祥和。


近义词：drowsy, sleepy, lethargic, somnolent

反义词：wide-awake, alert, vigilant


词源:  The word "sleeplike" is a compound word formed by combining "sleep" (referring to the state of being asleep) and the suffix "-like," which indicates resemblance or similarity.  The suffix "-like" has Germanic origins.  The word "sleep" itself has Proto-Germanic roots.
