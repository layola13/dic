overexpending: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈspɛndɪŋ/ | v. |过度支出，超支 | Spending more money than is available or planned.  |例句：Overexpending on holiday gifts left them in debt for months.  过度购买节日礼物让他们负债数月。 |近义词：overspending, exceeding the budget, squandering |反义词：underspending, saving, economizing | 动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are overexpending on their new house. = 他们在新房子上超支了。
We are overexpending this month on entertainment. = 这个月我们在娱乐方面超支了。

一般过去时:
He overexpended on his last vacation. = 他上次度假时超支了。
They overexpended on the wedding. = 他们在婚礼上超支了。

现在完成时:
She has overexpended on clothes this year. = 她今年在衣服上花了太多钱。
We have overexpended on groceries lately. = 最近我们在杂货上超支了。

过去完成时:
They had overexpended before they realized their mistake. = 他们意识到错误之前就已经超支了。
She had overexpended on her credit card before she got a new job. = 在找到新工作之前，她信用卡已经超支了。

将来时:
He will overexpend if he doesn't budget better. = 如果他不更好地预算，他会超支的。
We will probably overexpend on the trip to Europe. = 我们去欧洲旅行可能会超支。

虚拟语气:
If I had known the cost, I wouldn't have overexpended. = 如果我知道价格，我就不会超支了。
I wish I hadn't overexpended on that car. = 我真希望我没有在那辆车上花那么多钱。

被动语态:
The project was overexpended due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，项目超支了。
The budget was overexpended by the marketing team. = 预算被营销团队超支了。

疑问句:

否定句:
We didn't overexpend on the conference. = 我们在会议上没有超支。
They haven't overexpended this quarter. = 他们本季度没有超支。
