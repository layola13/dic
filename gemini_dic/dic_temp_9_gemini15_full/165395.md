preknowledges:/ˌpriːˈnɒlɪdʒɪz/| n. |预备知识，先验知识 |Prior knowledge or information that someone has before starting a task or learning something new.  This word is rarely used and often considered incorrect;  "prior knowledge" or "previous knowledge" are preferred. |例句：The teacher assessed the students' preknowledges before starting the new unit. = 教师在开始新单元之前评估了学生的预备知识。  |近义词：prior knowledge, previous knowledge, background knowledge, prerequisite knowledge |反义词：ignorance, unfamiliarity |名词
