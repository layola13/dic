extending:/ɪkˈstɛndɪŋ/| v.|延伸；扩展；延长；给予；提供|To make something longer or larger; to offer or give something.|例句：The company is extending its operations to new markets. 公司正在将其业务扩展到新的市场。

例句：He extended his hand in greeting. 他伸出手打招呼。


近义词：prolonging, expanding, enlarging, offering, providing|反义词：shortening, contracting, reducing, withholding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is extending his stay. 他正在延长他的逗留时间。
The bridge extends across the river.  桥横跨河流。


一般过去时:
They extended the deadline. 他们延长了截止日期。
She extended a warm welcome to the guests. 她热情地欢迎客人。


现在完成时:
We have extended our services to include online booking. 我们已经扩展了我们的服务，包括在线预订。
The drought has extended across the region. 干旱已经蔓延到整个地区。


过去完成时:
The project had already been extended before the funding ran out.  项目资金耗尽前就已经延期了。
They had extended their holiday by a week. 他们把假期延长了一周。


将来时:
The government will extend the tax break. 政府将延长税收优惠。
We will extend our support to the victims. 我们将向受害者提供支持。


虚拟语气:
If they extended the deadline, we could finish the project. 如果他们延长截止日期，我们就能完成项目。
I wish they would extend the invitation to my colleague. 我希望他们能邀请我的同事。


被动语态:
The contract was extended for another year. 合同延长了一年。
The exhibition is being extended for another month. 展览将延长一个月。


疑问句:
How long will they extend the warranty? 他们会延长保修期多久？
Does the road extend to the village? 这条路通到村庄吗？


否定句:
They did not extend the offer to me. 他们没有把这个机会提供给我。
The company will not extend its operations to that region. 公司不会将其业务扩展到那个地区。
