semi-sterility: /ˌsɛmiˈstɛrɪləti/ | adj. | 半不育性，部分不育 |

英文解析:The state of being partially sterile;  incapable of producing offspring at a normal rate.  Often used in the context of genetics and plant breeding, referring to a reduced fertility compared to the fully fertile parent plants.

例句:

The hybrid plants exhibited semi-sterility, producing fewer seeds than their parent lines. = 杂交植物表现出半不育性，产生的种子比亲本系少。

Semi-sterility in this wheat cultivar is a significant limitation for commercial production. = 这个小麦品种的半不育性是商业化生产的一个重大限制。


近义词:partial sterility, reduced fertility

反义词:full fertility, complete fertility


词性:adjective (形容词)
