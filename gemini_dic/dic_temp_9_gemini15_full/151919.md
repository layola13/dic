pasteurism: /ˈpæstʃərɪzəm/ | n. |巴斯德消毒法；巴氏消毒法 | The process of heating a liquid, such as milk or juice, to a specific temperature for a specific period to kill harmful microorganisms while preserving the quality of the product.  |  例句：Pasteurism is a crucial step in ensuring the safety of many food and beverage products. 巴氏消毒法是确保许多食品和饮料产品安全性的关键步骤。

例句：The dairy farm uses pasteurism to extend the shelf life of its milk.  这家奶牛场采用巴氏消毒法延长牛奶的保质期。


近义词：heat treatment, sterilization (though sterilization is a more extreme process) |反义词：None (it's a process, not something with a direct opposite)|词性：名词
