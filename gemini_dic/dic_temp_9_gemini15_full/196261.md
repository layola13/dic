serenader:/ˌsɛrəˈneɪdər/| n. |夜曲演奏者；献殷勤者 |A serenader is a person who serenades someone, typically a lover, by singing or playing music outside their window at night.  |例句：The romantic serenader played a beautiful melody under her balcony.  浪漫的夜曲演奏者在她阳台上方演奏了一首优美的旋律。|近义词：musician, lover, suitor |反义词：none
