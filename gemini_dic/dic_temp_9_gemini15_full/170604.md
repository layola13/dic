pseudo-erotic:/ˌsuːdoʊˈɛrɒtɪk/ | adj. | 似是而非的色情的；假装色情的 |  Appearing to be erotic or sexually suggestive, but lacking genuine eroticism; often used to describe something that attempts to be sexually arousing but fails to achieve this effect, or something that exploits sexual themes for commercial gain without genuine artistic merit. |例句：The film's pseudo-erotic scenes felt contrived and lacked any real passion. 这部电影中似是而非的色情场景显得做作，缺乏真正的激情。|The novel's pseudo-erotic cover art was far more suggestive than the content within. 这本小说的似是而非的色情封面比内容更具煽动性。|近义词：suggestive, titillating, salacious (if negative connotation is intended) |反义词：genuinely erotic, truly passionate

词源:
