mobilization: /ˌmoʊbɪləˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 动员；调动；集结 | The process of assembling and organizing resources, personnel, or equipment for a specific purpose, often in response to a crisis or emergency.  It can refer to military mobilization, economic mobilization, or social mobilization. | 例句：The government ordered the mobilization of troops to the border.  政府下令将部队调动到边境。

The rapid mobilization of resources was crucial to the success of the relief effort.  迅速调动资源对于救援工作的成功至关重要。


近义词：muster, deployment, assembling, rallying


反义词：demobilization, dispersal


词性：名词
