redisciplining: /ˌriːˈdɪsɪplɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 再教育；重新训练；重新管教 | The act of disciplining again; subjecting someone or something to discipline anew.  This often implies a previous attempt at discipline that was unsuccessful or inadequate.  | 例句：The teacher is redisciplining the unruly students. 老师正在重新管教那些不守纪律的学生。  | 例句：The company is redisciplining its workforce to improve efficiency. 公司正在重新培训员工以提高效率。 | 近义词：re-educating, retraining, reforming, re-organizing | 反义词：neglecting, spoiling, indulging
