larder:/ˈlɑːrdər/| n. |食品储藏室；食物储备|A larder is a room or cupboard where food is stored.  It often refers to a cool, dark place designed to keep food fresh.|The larder was stocked with fresh vegetables and cheeses. 食品储藏室里摆满了新鲜的蔬菜和奶酪。|The family's larder was well-supplied for the winter. 这个家庭的食品储备足以应付整个冬天。|pantry, store-room|empty larder
