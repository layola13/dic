escort:/ˈɛskɔːrt/ | n., v. |护送，陪同；护卫；陪酒女 |

英文解析:As a noun, "escort" refers to a person who accompanies another for protection or courtesy.  As a verb, it means to accompany someone, often for protection or guidance.  The word can also have a negative connotation, referring to a person who provides sexual services for money.

例句(Noun):

The dignitary had a police escort to the airport.  这位要人由警方护送到机场。

She hired an escort to accompany her to the event. 她雇了一位护送人员陪她参加活动。


例句(Verb):

The soldiers escorted the prisoners to the jail. 士兵们将囚犯押送到监狱。

He escorted her home after the party. 派对后他护送她回家。


近义词: (Noun) companion, guide, chaperone, bodyguard; (Verb) accompany, guide, conduct, protect


反义词: (Noun) none directly applicable; (Verb) abandon, forsake, leave


词性: Noun, Verb
