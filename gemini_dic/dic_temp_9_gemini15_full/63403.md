entr'actes:/ˈɛntræktz/| n.|幕间休息；幕间剧|Intermissions or short plays performed between the acts of a longer play, opera, or other theatrical performance.  The word is often used in the plural.|

例句：The entr'actes featured short musical performances.  幕间休息期间有简短的音乐表演。

例句：The audience enjoyed the lively entr'actes during the opera.  观众很享受歌剧期间活泼的幕间休息。


近义词：intermissions, interludes

反义词：main performance, main act


词性：名词
