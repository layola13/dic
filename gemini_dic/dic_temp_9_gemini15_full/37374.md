comment:/ˈkɒment/ /ˈkɑːmɛnt/ | n., v. |评论；注释；意见 | to express an opinion or reaction |例句：He commented on the quality of the food. 他评论了食物的质量。|近义词：remark, observation, note |反义词：silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He comments on political issues regularly. = 他定期评论政治问题。
She comments on her friends' posts frequently. = 她经常评论朋友的帖子。


一般过去时:
They commented on the beauty of the scenery. = 他们评论了风景的美丽。
He commented that the meeting was unproductive. = 他评论说会议没有效率。


现在完成时:
She has commented on the article several times. = 她已经多次评论过这篇文章。
They have commented positively on the new policy. = 他们对新政策作出了积极评价。


过去完成时:
He had commented on the proposal before the meeting. = 他在会议之前已经对提案发表了评论。
She had commented that she was tired before going to bed. = 她睡觉前曾说过她很累。


将来时:
He will comment on the results tomorrow. = 他明天将对结果发表评论。
She will comment on the film after watching it. = 她看完电影后会发表评论。


虚拟语气:
If he commented honestly, people might listen. = 如果他诚实地评论，人们可能会听。
I wish she had commented on my work. = 我希望她能评论我的作品。


被动语态:
His work was commented on by many experts. = 他的作品受到了许多专家的评论。
The proposal was commented on extensively. = 该提案受到了广泛的评论。


疑问句:
What did he comment on? = 他评论了什么？
How did she comment on the situation? = 她对这种情况是如何评论的？


否定句:
He didn't comment on the incident. = 他没有评论那件事。
She didn't comment on his behavior. = 她没有评论他的行为。


PHRASE->
make a comment = 做出评论
offer a comment = 提供评论
leave a comment = 留下评论
add a comment = 添加评论
receive a comment = 收到评论
