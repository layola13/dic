polype:/ˈpɒlɪp/ /ˈpɑːlɪp/| n.|息肉；水螅；珊瑚虫|A polyp is a small, soft-bodied animal that typically lives attached to a surface, such as a rock or a coral reef.  Many polyps live in colonies, forming structures like coral reefs.  Some polyps are also found individually.|例句：The doctor removed a polyp from his colon. 医生切除了他结肠中的息肉。|例句：The coral reef is made up of thousands of tiny polyps. 珊瑚礁是由数千个微小的珊瑚虫构成的。|近义词：coral, hydra|反义词：无
