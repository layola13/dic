pre:/prɪ/| prep.| 之前，预先，在…之前 |  Indicates time, place, or order before something else.  It often shows priority or precedence.| 例句：He arrived pre-dawn. 他黎明前就到了。| 例句：Pre-flight checks are essential. 飞行前的检查至关重要。| 近义词：before, prior to, ahead of | 反义词：after, post-, following


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I pre-wash my clothes before putting them in the washing machine. = 我在把衣服放进洗衣机之前先预洗一下。
We pre-approve all loan applications. = 我们预先批准所有贷款申请。

一般过去时:
They pre-booked their tickets months in advance. = 他们提前几个月预订了机票。
She pre-heated the oven before baking the cake. = 她在烤蛋糕之前预热了烤箱。

现在完成时:
I have pre-paid for the conference. = 我已经预付了会议费用。
They have pre-selected the candidates for the interview. = 他们已经预先挑选了面试候选人。

过去完成时:
He had pre-arranged a meeting with his lawyer. = 他已经与他的律师预先安排了一次会面。
She had pre-empted my question with her own statement. = 她用自己的陈述抢先回答了我的问题。

将来时:
We will pre-order the new phone when it's released. = 我们将在新手机发布时预购。
They will pre-determine the outcome of the experiment. = 他们将预先确定实验的结果。

虚拟语气:
If I had pre-planned my trip, I would have avoided many problems. = 如果我提前计划好我的旅行，我就能避免很多问题。
I wish I had pre-warned them about the potential risks. = 我真希望我事先警告过他们潜在的风险。

被动语态:
The documents were pre-approved by the committee. = 文件已获得委员会的预先批准。
The data was pre-processed before analysis. = 数据在分析之前进行了预处理。

疑问句:
Was the meeting pre-scheduled? = 会议是预先安排的吗？
Are the ingredients pre-measured for the recipe? = 食谱的配料是预先量好的吗？

否定句:
The software wasn't pre-installed on my computer. = 我的电脑上没有预装该软件。
They didn't pre-test the system before launch. = 他们在发布之前没有预先测试系统。
