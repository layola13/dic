indehiscence:/ˌɪndɪˈhɪsns/| n. |不开裂；闭果；不裂果|The condition of a fruit or other plant structure not splitting open at maturity to release its contents.  It is the opposite of dehiscence.  The term is often used in botany to describe fruits that do not naturally open to disperse their seeds. |Example: The indehiscence of the nuts prevented the dispersal of seeds.  不开裂的坚果阻止了种子的传播。The indehiscence of the pod meant the seeds remained trapped inside.  豆荚不开裂意味着种子仍然被困在里面。|近义词：non-opening, closed, sealed|反义词：dehiscence|名词
