revolve:/rɪˈvɒlv/ /rɪˈvɑːlv/| vt. vi. n.|旋转；环绕；围绕；循环|To move in a circle around a central point; to go around something repeatedly; to be centered on something.|The Earth revolves around the sun. 地球绕太阳旋转。|The conversation revolved around his new job.  谈话围绕他的新工作展开。|rotate, orbit, circle|stationary, still|v. n.
