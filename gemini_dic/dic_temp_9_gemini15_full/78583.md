giantest:/ˈdʒaɪəntɪst/ | adj. | 最巨大的，最大的 |  This word is a superlative form of "giant," but it is considered non-standard and rarely used in formal writing. It implies the highest degree of gigantic size or importance.  While grammatically correct,  it sounds awkward and is better replaced with alternatives. | The giantest tree in the forest towered over all the others. = 森林里最大的树高耸于其他树木之上。  He claimed to have the giantest collection of stamps in the world. = 他声称拥有世界上最大的邮票收藏。| largest, biggest, hugest, immense | smallest, tiny, minuscule | 形容词
