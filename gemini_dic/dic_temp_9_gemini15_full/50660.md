derivative:/dɪˈrɪvətɪv/| n. adj.|衍生物；派生物；导数；衍生的|A derivative is something that is based on or derived from something else.  It can refer to a mathematical concept (a derivative in calculus), a financial instrument (a derivative in finance), or a work of art that imitates another. |例句：The new drug is a derivative of an existing one.  这种新药是一种现有药物的衍生物。

例句：The derivative of x² is 2x.  x²的导数是2x。


近义词：offshoot, spin-off, adaptation, copy, imitation|反义词：original, innovative, unique|名词，形容词
