confuses:/kənˈfjuːzɪz/ | v. | 使困惑；使糊涂 | To make someone unable to think clearly or understand something. | The instructions confused me. | 近义词：bewilder, perplex, mystify | 反义词：clarify, enlighten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The complex instructions confuse many users. = 复杂的说明使许多用户感到困惑。
This ambiguous statement confuses the audience. = 这句模棱两可的陈述使听众感到困惑。


一般过去时:
The sudden change in plans confused everyone. = 计划的突然改变使每个人都感到困惑。
The confusing directions confused him. = 令人费解的指示使他感到困惑。


现在完成时:
The frequent changes have confused the employees. = 频繁的变化已经使员工们感到困惑。
This new system has confused many of our customers. = 这个新系统已经让许多顾客感到困惑。


过去完成时:
I had been confused by the contradictory information. = 我一直被相互矛盾的信息弄糊涂了。
They had already confused the issue with irrelevant details. = 他们已经用无关紧要的细节把问题搞混了。


将来时:
The new rules will confuse a lot of people. = 新规会让很多人感到困惑。
The upcoming changes will likely confuse some of the staff. = 即将来临的变化可能会让一些员工感到困惑。


虚拟语气:
If you had explained it clearly, it wouldn’t have confused them. = 如果你解释清楚的话，就不会把他们弄糊涂了。
I wish I hadn't confused the dates. = 我真希望我没有弄混日期。


被动语态:
Many people are confused by the new tax laws. = 很多人对新的税法感到困惑。
The audience was confused by the speaker's contradictory statements. = 听众被演讲者相互矛盾的陈述弄糊涂了。


疑问句:
Does this instruction confuse you? = 这条指示让你困惑吗？
Are you confused about the assignment? = 你对作业感到困惑吗？


否定句:
The explanation did not confuse me. = 这个解释并没有让我困惑。
I wasn't confused by his complex argument. = 我没有被他复杂的论点弄糊涂。


PHRASE->
be confused about = 对...感到困惑
confused with = 与...混淆
get confused = 变得困惑
highly confused = 非常困惑
utterly confused = 完全困惑
