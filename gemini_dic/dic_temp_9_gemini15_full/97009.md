infatuate:/ɪnˈfætʃueɪt/| vt.|使迷恋；使倾心|To inspire or be inspired by foolish or unreasoning passion or love; to make foolishly in love.|例句：He was infatuated with the movie star. 他迷恋上了那位电影明星。
例句：She was completely infatuated by his charm. 她完全被他的魅力迷住了。|近义词：enamor, captivate, charm|反义词：disenchant, disillusion
