reptile:/ˈreptaɪl/| n. |爬行动物|A reptile is a cold-blooded vertebrate animal that typically has scales, breathes air, and lays eggs. Reptiles include snakes, lizards, turtles, crocodiles, and alligators.  They are characterized by their dry, scaly skin, and most lay amniotic eggs. |Example: Snakes, lizards, and crocodiles are all reptiles.  蛇、蜥蜴和鳄鱼都是爬行动物。 The ancient reptile fossils were found in the desert. 在沙漠中发现了古老的爬行动物化石。|近义词：crawling animal, scaled animal |反义词：mammal, bird, amphibian
