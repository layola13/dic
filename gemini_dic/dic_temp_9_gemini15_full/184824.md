resuspends:/ˌriːsəˈspɛndz/| vt.|重新悬浮；重新溶解|To suspend again; to put (something that has settled) back into suspension.|例句：The scientist resuspended the sediment in the water. 科学家将沉淀物重新悬浮在水中。

例句：The technician resuspended the cells in fresh medium. 技术人员将细胞重新悬浮在新鲜培养基中。


近义词：redisperse, reinstate, revive|反义词：settle, precipitate
