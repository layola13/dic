scribbled:/ˈskrɪbəld/| v. |潦草地写；乱涂乱画|To write or draw something quickly and carelessly.  |例句：He scribbled a note on a napkin. 他在餐巾纸上潦草地写了个便条。|例句：The child scribbled all over the wall.  孩子在墙上乱涂乱画。|近义词：scrawled, doodle, jotted|反义词：wrote carefully, meticulously inscribed|动词
