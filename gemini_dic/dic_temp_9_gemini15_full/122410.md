mnemosyne:/nɪˈmɒsɪniː/ | /nɪˈmɑːsɪniː/ | n. |记忆女神；记忆力|The Greek goddess of memory, Mnemosyne is the mother of the Muses in Greek mythology.  She represents memory, recollection, and the ability to retain information.  Her name is often used metaphorically to refer to memory or the act of remembering. | Example:  The poet invoked Mnemosyne for inspiration.  诗人祈求记忆女神缪斯给予灵感。 |近义词：memory, recollection, remembrance | 反义词：forgetfulness, amnesia | 名词
