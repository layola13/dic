overglide:/ˈoʊvərɡlaɪd/ | v. |滑翔过，越过滑翔 |To glide over or beyond (a point, area, etc.).  It implies a longer glide than a simple glide, often involving crossing a boundary or exceeding a limit. |例句：The glider overglided the designated landing zone. 滑翔机滑过了指定的着陆区。

The pilot overglided the mountain range, reaching the valley beyond. 飞行员越过山脉滑翔，到达了远处的山谷。


近义词：overshoot, surpass, exceed | 反义词：undershoot, fall short


词性:动词
