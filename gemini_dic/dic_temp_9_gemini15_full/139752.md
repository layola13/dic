ocr:/ˈɔːkɑːr/| n.|光学字符识别|Optical Character Recognition (OCR) is a technology that converts scanned images of text into machine-editable text.  It uses software to analyze the image and identify characters, then converts them into a digital format such as text files or word processing documents.|例句：The OCR software accurately converted the scanned document into a searchable PDF.  光学字符识别软件准确地将扫描文档转换为可搜索的PDF。|近义词：character recognition, text recognition|反义词：manual transcription
