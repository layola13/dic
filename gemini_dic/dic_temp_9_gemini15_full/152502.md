payment: /ˈpeɪmənt/ | n. | 支付，付款 | The act of paying someone or something.  It can refer to the money paid, the process of paying, or the thing paid for. |例句：The payment for the goods is due next week. 货物的付款将于下周到期。

例句：He received his payment promptly. 他及时收到了付款。


近义词：settlement, disbursement, remuneration | 反义词：receipt (in the sense of receiving payment)


词源:  Comes from the Old French word "paiement,"  which itself is derived from the verb "payer," meaning "to pay."  The ultimate origin traces back to the Latin word "pacare," meaning "to appease" or "to satisfy," which reflects the idea that payment satisfies a debt or obligation.

一般现在时:
The company makes monthly payments to its employees. = 公司每月向员工支付工资。
She pays her bills online. = 她在线支付账单。


一般过去时:
He paid for the dinner. = 他付了晚餐的钱。
They paid their taxes last month. = 他们上个月缴纳了税款。


现在完成时:
I have already made the payment. = 我已经付款了。
She has paid off her student loans. = 她已经还清了学生贷款。


过去完成时:
They had already paid the bill before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，他们已经付了账单。
He had paid his dues long ago. = 他很久以前就付清了会费。


将来时:
I will pay you back tomorrow. = 我明天还你钱。
We will make the payment next week. = 我们下周付款。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would make the payment immediately. = 如果我有钱，我会立即付款。
I wish I had made the payment earlier. = 我真希望我早点付款。


被动语态:
The bill was paid on time. = 账单按时支付了。
The payment is being processed. = 付款正在处理中。


疑问句:
When is the payment due? = 付款期限是什么时候？
How will you make the payment? = 你将如何付款？


否定句:
He did not make the payment on time. = 他没有按时付款。
She hasn't yet received her payment. = 她还没有收到付款。
