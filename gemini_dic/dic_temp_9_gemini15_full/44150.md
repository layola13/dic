criticalities:/ˌkrɪtɪˈkælɪtiz/ | n. | 重要性；关键点；危急关头 | The plural of criticality.  It refers to the state of being critical or crucial, often involving multiple aspects or factors.  It can also denote a situation of extreme danger or urgency. | Example: The project manager highlighted the criticalities of timely resource allocation and risk mitigation.  项目经理强调了及时分配资源和降低风险的重要性。  The multiple criticalities facing the company demanded immediate action. 公司面临的诸多关键问题需要立即采取行动。 |近义词：crucial points, key issues, vital aspects, emergencies | 反义词：unimportance, trivialities
