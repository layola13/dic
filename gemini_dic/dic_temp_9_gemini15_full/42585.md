counterpoint:/ˈkaʊntəpɔɪnt/| n.| 对位法；对比；反驳；对照 | A counterpoint is a contrasting melody, harmony, or rhythm that is played or sung at the same time as another.  It can also refer to a contrasting element or idea that provides a balance or contrast to something else. | Example: The composer skillfully used counterpoint to create a rich and complex musical texture.  作曲家巧妙地运用对位法创造出丰富而复杂的音乐织体。 The novel's lighthearted humor serves as a counterpoint to the serious themes it explores. 这部小说的轻松幽默与它探讨的严肃主题形成了对比。|近义词：contrast, opposition, foil |反义词：harmony (in the musical sense), agreement

一般现在时:
The melody provides a beautiful counterpoint to the bass line. = 这段旋律与低音线形成美丽的对位。
This argument serves as a counterpoint to his previous statement. = 这个论点与他之前的陈述形成对比。


一般过去时:
The artist used light and shadow as a counterpoint in his painting. = 艺术家在他的绘画中运用光影作为对比。
Her quiet demeanor acted as a counterpoint to her husband's boisterous personality. = 她安静的举止与她丈夫喧闹的个性形成了对比。


现在完成时:
The author has successfully woven several counterpoints into the narrative. = 作者已成功地将几个对比点编织到叙述中。
Her recent work has presented a fascinating counterpoint to established theories. = 她最近的作品对已有的理论提出了一个引人入胜的反驳。


过去完成时:
The composer had already established a strong counterpoint before introducing the main theme. = 作曲家在引入主题之前就已经建立了强烈的对位。
Critics had long noted the counterpoint between the novel's idyllic setting and its dark plot. = 批评家们早就注意到了小说田园诗般的背景与其阴暗情节之间的对比。


将来时:
His next book will offer a compelling counterpoint to current political discourse. = 他的下一本书将对当前的政治论述提供一个令人信服的反驳。
The architect's design will create a visual counterpoint to the surrounding buildings. = 建筑师的设计将与周围的建筑形成视觉上的对比。


虚拟语气:
If the music had a stronger counterpoint, it would be more interesting. = 如果音乐有更强的对位，它会更有趣。
If only there were a counterpoint to this relentless negativity. = 但愿有个东西能反驳这种无情的消极。


被动语态:
The main theme is beautifully complemented by the counterpoint. = 主旋律得到了对位法的完美补充。
The artist's use of color was subtly countered by the stark lines of the design. = 艺术家对色彩的使用被设计中简洁的线条巧妙地反衬。


疑问句:
Does the painting use counterpoint effectively? = 这幅画是否有效地运用了对比？
What is the counterpoint to this argument? = 这个论点的反驳是什么？


否定句:
The music lacks a strong counterpoint. = 这首音乐缺乏强烈的对位。
The essay fails to present a convincing counterpoint to the opposing view. = 这篇文章未能对反对意见提出令人信服的反驳。
