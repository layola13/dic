incanting:/ɪnˈkæntɪŋ/| v.|吟唱（咒语）；念咒|The act of chanting or reciting words, typically with the intention of producing a magical effect.  It often involves repetitive and rhythmic speech.|例句：The witch was incanting a spell to summon the demons. 女巫正在念咒召唤恶魔。

例句：He sat incanting softly to himself, seemingly lost in a world of his own. 他自言自语地低声吟唱着，似乎迷失在自己的世界里。


近义词：chanting, reciting, murmuring, muttering


反义词：speaking plainly, conversing normally


词性:动词
