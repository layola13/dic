kitchenet:/ˌkɪtʃəˈnet/| n. |小厨房；简易厨房 |A kitchenette is a small kitchen, often found in apartments or hotels, with basic cooking facilities.  It typically lacks some features of a full-size kitchen, such as a full-sized oven or dishwasher.|例句：The apartment had a kitchenette with a small refrigerator and a two-burner stove. 这套公寓有一个小厨房，配有小型冰箱和双头炉。The hotel room included a kitchenette, allowing guests to prepare simple meals.  酒店房间包括一个小厨房，让客人可以准备简单的饭菜。|近义词：mini-kitchen, compact kitchen|反义词：full kitchen, large kitchen
