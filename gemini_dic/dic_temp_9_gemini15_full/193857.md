self-discipline: /ˈsɛlfˌdɪsəplɪn/ | n. |自律，自我约束 | The ability to control one's feelings and overcome weaknesses; the ability to pursue what one thinks is important despite temptations and distractions.  |  He showed remarkable self-discipline in sticking to his diet. 他在坚持节食方面表现出了非凡的自律性。  She achieved her goals through sheer self-discipline. 她完全靠自律实现了目标。|  self-control, willpower, determination |  indulgence, lack of willpower, procrastination | 名词
