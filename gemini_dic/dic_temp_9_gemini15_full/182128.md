remit:/rɪˈmɪt/| vt. n.|汇款；减轻；赦免；提交；委任|To send or transmit (money) |The company will remit the funds to your account within three business days.  公司将在三个工作日内将款项汇入您的账户。|近义词：transmit, forward, pardon, alleviate|反义词：retain, withhold, aggravate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company remits payments monthly. = 公司每月汇款。
He remits his taxes on time. = 他按时缴税。

一般过去时:
She remitted the payment last week. = 她上周汇款了。
They remitted the prisoner. = 他们赦免了囚犯。

现在完成时:
We have remitted all the outstanding payments. = 我们已经汇付了所有未付的款项。
The government has remitted the debt. = 政府已经免除了债务。

过去完成时:
He had remitted the money before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已汇款。
They had remitted the sentence. = 他们已经减刑了。

将来时:
I will remit the payment as soon as possible. = 我会尽快汇款。
The court will remit the case to a lower court. = 法院将把此案发回下级法院。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would remit it immediately. = 如果我有钱，我会立即汇款。
If the judge had remitted the sentence, he would be free now. = 如果法官减刑，他现在就自由了。

被动语态:
The payment was remitted on time. = 款项已按时汇出。
The case was remitted to the appeals court. = 此案已提交上诉法院。

疑问句:
Has the payment been remitted yet? = 款项已经汇出了吗？
Will the sentence be remitted? = 判决会被减轻吗？

否定句:
I did not remit the payment. = 我没有汇款。
The judge did not remit the sentence. = 法官没有减刑。


PHRASE->
remit money = 汇款
remit a debt = 偿还债务
remit a sentence = 减刑
remit a case = 将案件发回
remit a sin = 赦免罪过


词源:
From Old French *remetre, from Latin remittere, from re- + mittere "to send".  The meaning evolved from "send back," "give back," to "forgive," then to "send (money)."
