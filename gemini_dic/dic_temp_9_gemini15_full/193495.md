seleniferous:/ˌsɛləˈnɪfərəs/| adj.|含硒的，富硒的|Containing or yielding selenium.  The term describes substances, soils, or water sources that contain significant amounts of the chemical element selenium.|The soil in this region is seleniferous, leading to high selenium levels in the locally grown crops.|近义词：selenium-rich|反义词：selenium-deficient
