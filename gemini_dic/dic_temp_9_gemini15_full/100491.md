intervener:/ˌɪntərˈviːnər/| n. |干预者，介入者 | A person who intervenes in a situation.  Someone who becomes involved in a situation to try to resolve it or influence its outcome. |Example: The intervener helped mediate the dispute between the two parties. 干预者帮助调解双方之间的争议。 The social worker acted as an intervener in the family's conflict. 社工在家庭冲突中充当调解人。|近义词：mediator, arbitrator, intercessor |反义词：bystander, onlooker
