modificate:/ˈmɒdɪfɪkeɪt/| v.|修改，更改，变更|To change some features of something; to alter or adjust.|The architect modified the building plans to accommodate the client's requests. 建筑师修改了建筑图纸以满足客户的要求。|alter, change, adjust|worsen, damage.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He modifies his approach regularly. = 他定期修改他的方法。
They modify the software frequently. = 他们经常修改软件。


一般过去时:
She modified her essay before submission. = 她提交前修改了她的论文。
He modified the car's engine. = 他改装了汽车的引擎。


现在完成时:
They have modified the design several times. = 他们已经修改了设计好几次。
We have modified our strategy to improve efficiency. = 我们已经修改了我们的策略以提高效率。


过去完成时:
She had modified the document before the meeting. = 她在会议之前修改了文件。
They had modified their plans after the unexpected change. = 他们在意外变化后修改了他们的计划。


将来时:
He will modify the contract tomorrow. = 他明天将修改合同。
They will modify the system next week. = 他们下周将修改系统。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would modify my statement. = 如果我是你，我会修改我的说法。
It would be better if they modified their behavior. = 如果他们改变行为就好了。


被动语态:
The rules were modified last year. = 规则去年被修改了。
The software is being modified at the moment. = 软件正在被修改。


疑问句:
Did they modify the schedule? = 他们修改了日程安排吗？
Will the policy be modified? = 政策会被修改吗？


否定句:
He did not modify his opinion. = 他没有改变他的意见。
They will not modify the existing system.  = 他们不会修改现有的系统。
