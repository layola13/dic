resented:/rɪˈzɛntɪd/| v. |怨恨；不满；生气|To feel bitterness or indignation at (something perceived as unfair, unjust, or wrong).  It implies a feeling of anger and resentment towards the person or situation causing the offense. |例句：She resented his interference in her affairs. 她对他的干涉感到不满。

例句：He resented being treated unfairly. 他对不公平的待遇感到愤愤不平。


近义词：dislike, begrudge, object to, take exception to, chafe under | 反义词：appreciate, enjoy, welcome, accept


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resents her constant criticism. 他讨厌她不断的批评。
They resent the unfair treatment they receive. 他们对受到的不公平待遇感到愤恨。


一般过去时:
I resented his condescending tone. 我讨厌他居高临下的语气。
She resented being ignored. 她讨厌被忽视。


现在完成时:
He has resented her interference for years. 他多年来一直对她干涉不满。
They have resented the lack of opportunity. 他们一直对缺乏机会感到不满。


过去完成时:
I had resented his behavior long before he apologized. 在他道歉之前很久我就已经对他行为不满。
She had resented his decision since it was made. 自从他做出决定以来，她就一直对此不满。


将来时:
I will resent any attempt to control me. 我将讨厌任何试图控制我的企图。
They will resent any further delays. 他们将对进一步的延误感到不满。


虚拟语气:
I would resent it if you told my secrets. 如果你泄露我的秘密，我会非常生气。
She would have resented his comments if she had heard them. 如果她听到他的评论，她会很生气。


被动语态:
His interference was resented by everyone. 他的干涉受到了所有人的反感。
Her decision was resented by many people. 她的决定受到了许多人的不满。


疑问句:
Do you resent his behavior? 你讨厌他的行为吗？
Does she resent being overlooked? 她讨厌被忽视吗？


否定句:
I don't resent your help. 我并不讨厌你的帮助。
He doesn't resent the extra work. 他并不讨厌额外的工作。


PHRASE->
resent doing something: 讨厌做某事
resent someone's actions: 讨厌某人的行为
resent being treated unfairly: 讨厌被不公平对待
resent having to do something: 讨厌不得不做某事
resent the fact that...: 讨厌…这个事实
