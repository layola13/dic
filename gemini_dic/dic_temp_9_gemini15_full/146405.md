overfocus: /ˌoʊvərˈfoʊkəs/ | v. |过度关注，专注过度 | To focus too much attention on something, often to the detriment of other things.  |例句：He overfocused on his work and neglected his health. 他过度专注于工作而忽略了自己的健康。  |近义词：hyperfocus, concentrate excessively |反义词：neglect, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overfocuses on minor details. = 他过度关注细枝末节。
She overfocuses on her appearance. = 她过度关注自己的外表。

一般过去时:
I overfocused on the deadline and missed other important tasks. = 我过度关注截止日期而错过了其他重要任务。
They overfocused on one aspect of the project. = 他们过度关注项目的某一方面。


现在完成时:
He has overfocused on this problem for weeks. = 他几周来一直过度关注这个问题。
She has overfocused on her career and neglected her family. = 她过度关注自己的事业而忽略了家庭。


过去完成时:
I realized I had overfocused on the negative aspects. = 我意识到我之前过度关注了消极方面。
They had overfocused on the short-term goals and missed the long-term vision. = 他们之前过度关注短期目标而错失了长期愿景。


将来时:
I fear I will overfocus on this presentation and forget to practice my speech. = 我担心我会过度关注这个演示文稿而忘记练习我的演讲。
She thinks she will overfocus on her exams and neglect her social life. = 她认为她会过度关注考试而忽略社交生活。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't overfocused on that one detail, I would have finished the project on time. = 如果我没有过度关注那个细节，我就能按时完成项目了。
I wish I hadn't overfocused on work last year; I missed so much. = 我真希望去年我没有过度关注工作；我错过了太多东西。


被动语态:
The project was overfocused on by the team leader. = 项目被团队负责人过度关注了。
The issue has been overfocused on by the media. = 这个问题已被媒体过度关注。


疑问句:
Is she overfocusing on her work again? = 她是不是又在过度关注工作了？
Are you overfocusing on this single point of view? = 你是不是过度关注这种单一观点？


否定句:
He didn't overfocus on the details; he saw the bigger picture. = 他没有过度关注细节；他看到了更大的图景。
She didn't overfocus on the negative aspects; she remained optimistic. = 她没有过度关注消极方面；她保持乐观。


PHRASE->
