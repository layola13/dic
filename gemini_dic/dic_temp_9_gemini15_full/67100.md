explore:/ɪkˈsplɔːr/| /ɪkˈsplor/| vt. & vi.|探索；探究；探险|To travel in or through (a place) in order to learn about it; to investigate or discuss a subject thoroughly.|例句：We explored the Amazon rainforest. 我们探索了亚马逊雨林。|例句：Let's explore the possibilities. 让我们探讨一下可能性。|近义词：investigate, examine, search, delve into|反义词：ignore, neglect, overlook|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They explore the cave. = 他们探索这个山洞。
We explore new ideas. = 我们探索新的想法。

一般过去时:
He explored the ancient ruins. = 他探索了古代遗址。
She explored the possibilities of a new career. = 她探索了新的职业可能性。

现在完成时:
They have explored the Amazon. = 他们已经探索过亚马逊河了。
We have explored many different options. = 我们已经探索了许多不同的选择。

过去完成时:
They had explored the area before. = 他们以前就探索过这个地区了。
She had explored all the avenues before giving up. = 在放弃之前，她已经探索了所有途径。

将来时:
We will explore the coast tomorrow. = 我们明天将探索海岸。
They will explore the implications of the new law. = 他们将探索新法律的影响。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would explore the subject further. = 如果我有更多时间，我会进一步探讨这个主题。
I wish I could explore the world more. = 我希望我能更多地探索世界。

被动语态:
The cave was explored by the team. = 这个山洞是由这个团队探索的。
The possibilities are being explored by the committee. = 委员会正在探索各种可能性。

疑问句:
Have you ever explored the Grand Canyon? = 你曾经探索过大峡谷吗？
Will they explore the matter further? = 他们会进一步探讨这个问题吗？

否定句:
We didn't explore the options fully. = 我们没有充分探索各种选择。
They haven't explored the possibility of a merger. = 他们还没有探索合并的可能性。


PHRASE->
