drop-the-ball:/drɑːp ðə bɔːl/|/ˈdrɒp ðə bɔːl/| phrasal verb| 犯错；失职；搞砸；未能完成任务|To fail to do something that you are responsible for, or to make a mistake that prevents something from being successful.|The team dropped the ball in the final quarter, leading to their defeat.  球队在最后一段时间犯了错，导致了他们的失败。

They dropped the ball on the marketing campaign, resulting in poor sales.  他们在市场营销活动中犯了错误，导致销售业绩不佳。

|近义词：fail, mess up, blunder, botch|反义词：succeed, excel, accomplish|phrasal verb
