retund:/rɪˈtʌnd/| adj.|冗长的，累赘的；多余的| verbose, prolix, excessive, superfluous; characterized by excessive length, fullness, or fullness of sound.  It often implies that something is unnecessarily wordy or drawn out. |例句：His speech was rotund and difficult to follow. 他的演讲冗长且难以理解。|例句：The rotund explanation made the simple concept seem complicated.  冗长的解释使简单的概念显得复杂。|近义词：verbose, prolix, wordy, diffuse, redundant|反义词：concise, succinct, brief, terse
