impracticableness: /ˌɪmpræktɪˈkeɪbəlnəs/ | n. | 不切实际；不可行性 | The state or quality of being impracticable; the impossibility or difficulty of doing something.  It refers to something that is not practical or feasible to do because of various reasons such as difficulty, expense, or lack of resources. | The impracticableness of the plan was evident from the start.  这个计划从一开始就显而易见地不可行。  The sheer impracticableness of the task discouraged many volunteers. 这项任务的极端不可行性让许多志愿者望而却步。 | impracticality, infeasibility, unfeasibility | feasibility, practicality | 名词
