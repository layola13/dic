USAGE->
This word is an adjective, and therefore doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".  It describes the shape of something.

例句1: The flower has a campaniform corolla. 这朵花的花冠呈钟形。
例句2: The fungus displayed a distinct campaniform structure.  这种真菌呈现出明显的钟形结构。
