matchers:/ˈmætʃərz/| n. |匹配器，匹配规则|A matcher is a component or algorithm that compares two or more items to determine if they match according to specified criteria.  It's commonly used in software development, particularly in testing and pattern matching. |Example: The regular expression matcher found three matches in the text.  正则表达式匹配器在文本中找到了三个匹配项。  The database query used a matcher to find all records with the specified attributes. 数据库查询使用匹配器查找具有指定属性的所有记录。|近义词：comparator,  pattern matcher|反义词：mismatcher (非正式)|名词
