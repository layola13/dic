relacquers:/ˌriːˈlækər/| vt.|重新涂漆|To apply a new coat of lacquer to (something).  This typically involves removing the old lacquer first, though sometimes it's applied over existing layers. The process aims to restore or refresh the finish.|例句：The antique table was beautifully relacquered, restoring its original luster. 这张古董桌子重新涂漆后，恢复了原来的光泽。

例句：He relacquered the chairs to match the new dining table. 他重新给椅子涂漆，以与新的餐桌相配。


近义词：revarnish, refinish, repaint|反义词：strip (of lacquer), damage, ruin


词性:动词
