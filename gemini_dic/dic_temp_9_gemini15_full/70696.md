finally:/ˈfaɪnəli/| adv.|最终地，终于|At last; after a long time or delay.|例句：Finally, after many attempts, she succeeded.  最终，经过多次尝试后，她成功了。|例句：We finally reached our destination after a long and tiring journey.  经过漫长而疲惫的旅程后，我们终于到达了目的地。|近义词：eventually, ultimately, at last, at length|反义词：initially, firstly, at first


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He finally understands the problem. = 他终于理解了这个问题。
She finally finishes her work. = 她终于完成了她的工作。


一般过去时:
They finally arrived home. = 他们终于到家了。
He finally admitted his mistake. = 他终于承认了他的错误。


现在完成时:
We have finally finished the project. = 我们终于完成了这个项目。
She has finally found her lost cat. = 她终于找到了她丢失的猫。


过去完成时:
After many delays, they had finally completed the construction. = 经过多次延误后，他们终于完成了建设。
I had finally understood the lesson after rereading the chapter. = 重读章节后，我终于理解了这节课。


将来时:
We will finally get a vacation. = 我们终于可以休假了。
They will finally be able to retire. = 他们终于可以退休了。


虚拟语气:
If he finally apologized, I would forgive him. = 如果他最终道歉了，我会原谅他。
I wish he would finally understand. = 我希望他最终能理解。


被动语态:
The agreement was finally reached. = 协议终于达成了。
The problem was finally solved. = 问题终于解决了。


疑问句:
Will they finally agree? = 他们最终会同意吗？
Have they finally understood? = 他们终于理解了吗？


否定句:
He didn't finally make the decision. = 他最终没有做出决定。
She didn't finally get the promotion. = 她最终没有得到晋升。
