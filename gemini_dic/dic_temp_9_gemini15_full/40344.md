contractees:/ˌkɒntrækˈtiːz/| n. |合同签订者，承包者 |Those who have entered into a contract; the parties to a contract.  |例句：The contractees were responsible for completing the project on time. 合同签订者负责按时完成项目。|例句：All contractees must sign the agreement before starting work. 所有承包者必须在开始工作前签署协议。|近义词：contractors, parties to a contract |反义词：contractors (in the sense of those who hire out services)
