indefensibility:/ˌɪnˌdɪfɛnsəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可辩护性，无法自卫|The indefensibility of his actions led to his dismissal.  他的行为无法辩护，导致了他被解雇。|近义词：unjustifiability, inexcusability |反义词：justifiability, defensibility |名词


英文解析：The state or quality of being indefensible; the inability to be defended or justified.  指某种行为或立场无法被辩护或证明正当的特性或状态。


例句：

The indefensibility of his claims was evident to everyone.  他的说法站不住脚，这一点对每个人来说都很明显。

The court found the defendant's actions to be indefensible. 法院认定被告的行为不可辩护。


词源:

* **in-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "without"
* **defensible**:  adj. capable of being defended or justified.  (来自 defend + -able)
* **-ity**: suffix forming nouns of state or quality.

因此，indefensibility  字面意思是“非可辩护的状态”。
