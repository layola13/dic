penchant:/ˈpɛntʃænt/| n. |偏好；嗜好；倾向 |A strong inclination or liking for something.  It suggests a fondness that is more than just a casual interest. |He has a penchant for spicy food. 他喜欢吃辛辣食物。|近义词：liking, fondness, preference, inclination |反义词：aversion, dislike, distaste


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He has a penchant for classic cars. = 他对经典汽车情有独钟。
She shows a penchant for wearing bright colors. = 她偏爱穿鲜艳的衣服。


一般过去时:
He had a penchant for collecting stamps. = 他过去喜欢集邮。
She showed a penchant for solitude in her younger years. = 她年轻时就表现出喜欢独处的倾向。


现在完成时:
He has developed a penchant for gardening. = 他培养了对园艺的爱好。
She has maintained her penchant for fine art throughout her life. = 她一生都保持着对美术的热爱。


过去完成时:
He had already developed a penchant for jazz before he moved to New Orleans. = 他搬到新奥尔良之前就已经对爵士乐产生了偏好。
She had always had a penchant for adventure, even in her childhood. = 她从小就对冒险有着强烈的爱好。


将来时:
He will likely develop a penchant for hiking as he gets older. = 他年纪大了可能会喜欢上远足。
She anticipates that her children will develop a penchant for reading. = 她预料她的孩子们会培养阅读的爱好。


虚拟语气:
If he had a penchant for cooking, he would open a restaurant. = 如果他喜欢烹饪，他会开一家餐馆。
I wish I had a penchant for early rising; it would make my life easier. = 我希望我早起，这会让我的生活更容易。


被动语态:
His penchant for gambling was noted by everyone. = 他嗜赌的毛病被每个人都注意到了。
Her penchant for philanthropy is widely admired. = 她乐善好施的倾向广受赞赏。


疑问句:
What is his penchant? = 他有什么嗜好？
Does she have a penchant for anything in particular? = 她有什么特别的喜好？


否定句:
He doesn't have a penchant for sports. = 他不喜欢运动。
She has no penchant for gossiping. = 她不喜欢八卦。


PHRASE->

a penchant for = 对…的爱好
have a penchant for = 有…的嗜好
show a penchant for = 表现出对…的偏好
develop a penchant for = 培养对…的爱好
a strong penchant for = 对…的强烈爱好
