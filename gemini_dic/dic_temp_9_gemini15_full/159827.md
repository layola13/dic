polygynia: /pɒlɪˈdʒaɪniə/ | n. | 多雌蕊现象；多雌蕊植物 | The condition in plants of having more than one pistil in a flower.  It contrasts with monogyny, which is having only one pistil.  Polygynia is a characteristic feature of many flowering plants, contributing to their reproductive success. | Example:  Many roses exhibit polygynia, having multiple pistils within a single flower.  = 许多玫瑰都表现出多雌蕊现象，在一朵花中有多个雌蕊。 | 近义词：multiple pistils | 反义词：monogyny
