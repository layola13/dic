klinometer:/klɪˈnɒmɪtər/| n. |测斜仪，倾斜计 |An instrument for measuring angles of slope or inclination.  It is used in surveying, mining, and other fields to determine the angle of a surface relative to the horizontal. |例句：The surveyor used a klinometer to measure the slope of the hill.  测量员用测斜仪测量山坡的坡度。|近义词： inclinometer, slope meter |反义词：None  |
