interspersedly:/ˌɪntərˈspɜːrsɪdli/ | adv. | 间或地；散布地；点缀地 |  Adverb meaning at intervals; here and there; scattered; sporadically.  It describes something happening or being placed not continuously but with gaps in between. | The wildflowers were interspersedly blooming along the roadside.  野花零星地开放在路边。


The trees were interspersedly planted across the field. 树木间或地种植在田野里。 | sporadically, occasionally, at intervals | continuously, uniformly | adv.
