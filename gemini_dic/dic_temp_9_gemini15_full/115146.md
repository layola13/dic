mangy:/ˈmæŋɡi/| adj.| 患皮肤病的；肮脏的；破旧的 | Suffering from mange;  covered with scabs or sores; shabby and neglected.  |例句：The dog had a mangy coat. 那条狗的毛皮很脏。

例句：The mangy old couch was finally thrown away. 那张破旧不堪的旧沙发终于被扔掉了。

近义词：scabby, scurfy, shabby, dilapidated

反义词：clean, healthy, pristine, well-maintained

词性：形容词
