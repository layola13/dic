ensample:/ɪnˈsæmpl/| n. |榜样，典范，示例 |An ensample is something or someone that serves as a model or example to be imitated.  It emphasizes the idea of a particularly noteworthy example, often one intended to guide or instruct. |例句：He is an ensample of courage and determination. 他是勇气和决心的典范。 |例句：The teacher used the painting as an ensample of good technique. 老师用这幅画作为良好技巧的示例。|近义词：example, model, pattern, exemplar |反义词：antithesis, opposite
