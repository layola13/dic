scripts:/skrɪpts/| n.| 剧本；手稿；文字；指令 | Written text for a play, film, broadcast, or other performance; a set of instructions.  | Example: The screenwriter polished the scripts for weeks before submitting them. 剧作家在提交剧本之前润色了好几个星期。  The pilot's scripts guided the aircraft's landing.  飞行员的指令引导飞机着陆。|近义词：manuscript, screenplay, text, instructions |反义词：improvisation, ad-lib
