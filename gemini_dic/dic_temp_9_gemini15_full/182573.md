reobtained:/ˌriːəbˈteɪnd/| v.|重新获得，收回|To obtain something again that was previously lost or given up.|例句：After years of searching, he finally reobtained his lost painting.  多年寻找后，他终于找回了丢失的画作。


例句：The company reobtained the patent after a lengthy legal battle.  经过漫长的法律诉讼后，该公司重新获得了专利。


近义词：regain, recover, retrieve|反义词：lose, forfeit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reobtains his belongings after the flood. 他洪水过后重新获得了他的财物。
They reobtain control of the situation. 他们重新掌控了局势。
