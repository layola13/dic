convalesces:/ˌkɒnvəˈlɛsɪz/| /ˌkɑːnvəˈlɛsəs/| vi.|恢复健康；逐渐康复|to recover health and strength after illness or injury|例句：He is convalescing at home after his operation. 他手术后在家休养。She is slowly convalescing from pneumonia. 她正在慢慢地从肺炎中恢复健康。|近义词：recover, recuperate, rehabilitate|反义词：worsen, deteriorate|动词
