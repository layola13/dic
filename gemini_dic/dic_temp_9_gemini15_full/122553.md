models:/ˈmɒdəlz/ /ˈmɑːdəlz/| n.|模型，模式，模特|A model is a simplified representation of something, often used for testing or prediction.  It can also refer to a person who poses for photographs or in fashion shows.|例句：The architect presented a scale model of the new building. 建筑师展示了新建筑的比例模型。|例句：She's a successful fashion model. 她是一位成功的时尚模特。|近义词：prototype, pattern, exemplar, sample|反义词：original, reality
