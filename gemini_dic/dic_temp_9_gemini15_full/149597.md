pain:/peɪn/ | n. |痛苦，疼痛，苦恼 |  Pain is an unpleasant sensation in a part of your body. It can be caused by injury, illness, or emotional distress.  | Example:  I have a pain in my shoulder.  我的肩膀疼。

| Example: The pain of losing a loved one is immense.失去亲人的痛苦是巨大的。 |近义词：ache, agony, suffering, distress |反义词：pleasure, comfort, ease |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wound pains me. = 伤口疼。
My back pains constantly. = 我的背总是疼。


一般过去时:
My head pained me all night. = 我整晚头疼。
The surgery pained him terribly. = 手术让他非常痛苦。


现在完成时:
I have pained my ankle playing basketball. = 我打篮球扭伤了脚踝。
She has pained her heart by his betrayal. = 她被他背叛伤透了心。


过去完成时:
I had pained my hand before I started painting. = 我开始画画之前就已经手痛了。
She had pained herself with worry before the exam. = 考试前她一直为考试担心而痛苦。


将来时:
This injury will pain me for weeks. = 这伤会让我疼好几周。
The news will pain her deeply. = 这个消息会让她非常伤心。


虚拟语气:
If I knew it would pain him so much, I wouldn't have said it. = 如果我知道这会让他这么痛苦，我就不说了。
I wish this headache wouldn't pain me so much. = 我希望这个头痛不会这么痛。


被动语态:
His feelings were pained by her rejection. = 他被她的拒绝伤透了心。
Her pride was pained by the insult. = 她的自尊心受到了侮辱。


疑问句:
Does your arm pain you? = 你的胳膊疼吗？
Does this operation pain much? = 这个手术很疼吗？


否定句:
My head doesn't pain me anymore. = 我的头不疼了。
This medicine doesn't pain my stomach. = 这药不伤我的胃。


PHRASE->
take pains = 煞费苦心
go to great pains = 非常努力
in pain = 痛苦地
be in pain = 感到疼痛
with a pain = 痛苦地
