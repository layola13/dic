cantation:/kænˈteɪʃən/| n. |吟唱；歌唱；祈祷；咒语|The act of chanting; singing; or reciting in a rhythmic or musical way, often with religious or ceremonial significance.  It can also refer to the text or words being chanted.|例句：The monk's cantation filled the temple with a sense of peace.  僧侣的吟唱使寺庙充满了宁静感。
The ancient ritual involved a complex cantation to summon the spirits.  古老的仪式包含一个召唤灵魂的复杂吟唱。|近义词：chant, recitation, incantation, hymn |反义词：silence, whisper
