slavelike:/ˈsleɪvlaɪk/| adj.|奴隶般的；卑躬屈膝的；盲目服从的|Resembling or characteristic of a slave; showing subservience or lack of independence.  It implies a lack of agency and an unquestioning obedience.|例句：He had a slavelike devotion to his boss. 他对老板有着奴隶般的忠诚。

例句：The workers were treated in a slavelike manner. 工人们受到了奴隶般的待遇。


近义词：submissive, subservient, obsequious, docile, unthinking


反义词：independent, autonomous, free, assertive, rebellious


词性:形容词
