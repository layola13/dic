carry-it-off:/ˈkæri ɪt ɒf/| phrasal verb|成功应付；成功做到；成功地完成；蒙混过关|To successfully deal with or complete a difficult task or situation, often in a skillful or deceptive way.  It implies overcoming a challenge or escaping negative consequences.|

例句：
She carried it off with remarkable grace. = 她以非凡的优雅成功地应付了。

He managed to carry it off despite the pressure. = 尽管压力很大，他还是成功地完成了。


近义词：succeed, manage, overcome, pull off, get away with


反义词：fail, falter, stumble


词性: phrasal verb
