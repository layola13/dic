price-fixing:/ˈpraɪsˌfɪksɪŋ/| n.|价格操纵；价格垄断|The act of colluding with competitors to set prices artificially high or low, preventing fair competition.  This is often illegal and harmful to consumers.|例句：The company was fined heavily for engaging in price-fixing with its rivals. 这家公司因与竞争对手串通操纵价格而被处以巨额罚款。|例句：The government is investigating allegations of price-fixing in the pharmaceutical industry. 政府正在调查制药行业价格操纵的指控。|近义词：cartel, collusion, price manipulation|反义词：free market, fair competition|名词
