overinvesting: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɛstɪŋ/ | v. |过度投资 | The act of investing more resources (money, time, effort, etc.) into something than is prudent or necessary, often leading to diminishing returns or losses.  |例句：Overinvesting in a failing business can lead to bankruptcy. 过度投资于一个亏损的企业可能导致破产。

例句：Their overinvesting in marketing proved ineffective, yielding minimal returns. 他们在市场营销上的过度投资证明是无效的，收益极少。


近义词：overspending, excessive investment, misallocation of resources | 反义词：underinvesting, prudent investment, efficient allocation of resources | 词性：动词
