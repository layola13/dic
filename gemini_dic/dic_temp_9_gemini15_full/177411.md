rebake:/ˌriːˈbeɪk/| vt.|重新烘焙；再次烘烤|To bake (something) again.  This usually implies baking something that has already been baked, perhaps to improve its texture or to finish cooking it.|例句：I had to rebake the bread because it was undercooked. 我不得不重新烘烤面包，因为它没烤熟。

例句：The cake was slightly undercooked, so I re baked it for another ten minutes. 蛋糕有点没烤熟，所以我又烤了十分钟。


近义词：bake again, reheat (if referring to a minor adjustment) | 反义词：underbake

词性:动词
