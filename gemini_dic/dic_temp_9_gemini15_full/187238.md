robin-hoodism:/ˈrɒbɪnˌhʊdɪzəm/| n. |罗宾汉主义；劫富济贫的行为|The practice of taking from the rich to give to the poor, often illegally or unethically.  This term often carries a connotation of romanticism and social justice, even if the actions themselves are illegal.|例句：The government's new tax policy is seen by some as a form of robin-hoodism, redistributing wealth from the wealthy to the less fortunate.  政府新的税收政策被一些人视为一种罗宾汉主义，将财富从富人手中重新分配给弱势群体。|近义词：social justice, wealth redistribution, egalitarianism|反义词：plutocracy, inequality, wealth concentration|名词
