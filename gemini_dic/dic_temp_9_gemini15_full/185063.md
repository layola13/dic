rethresh:/riːˈθreʃ/| vt.|再打谷，重新脱粒|To thresh again; to separate grain from stalks a second time.|例句：The farmer had to rethresh the barley because the first attempt was unsuccessful. 农民不得不再次打谷大麦，因为第一次尝试不成功。例句：After the rain, they had to rethresh the wheat that had been left in the field. 雨后，他们不得不重新脱粒留在田里的麦子。|近义词：thresh again, reprocess|反义词：harvest (in the sense of gathering without threshing)

一般现在时:
They rethresh the grain every year. = 他们每年都重新打谷。
He rethreshes the wheat after the rain. = 雨后他重新脱粒小麦。

一般过去时:
The farmer rethreshed the barley last week. = 农民上周重新打谷大麦。

过去完成时:

否定句:
