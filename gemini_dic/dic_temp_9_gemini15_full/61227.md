emaciated: /ɪˈmeɪʃieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 极度消瘦的；憔悴的 | Extremely thin and weak, usually because of illness or lack of food.  | The emaciated child looked like a skeleton.  这个极度消瘦的孩子看起来像个骷髅。 The long illness left him emaciated and weak.  这场漫长的疾病使他消瘦无力。 | gaunt, skeletal, wasted, thin, feeble | plump, corpulent, obese, stout
