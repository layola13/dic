comorans:/ˈkɒmərænz/| n. |鸬鹚| Cormorants are large, dark-colored seabirds with a long neck and a hooked bill. They are known for their diving abilities and their use in fishing.  | 例句：The cormorants were perched on the rocks, drying their wings.  鸬鹚栖息在岩石上，晾干翅膀。
