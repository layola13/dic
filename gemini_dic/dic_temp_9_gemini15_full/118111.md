mennonites:/ˈmɛnənˌaɪts/| n. |门诺派教徒|Members of a Protestant Christian group that originated in Switzerland and the Netherlands in the 16th century.  They are known for their pacifism, simple living, and commitment to community.  They often reject military service and certain modern conveniences. |The Mennonites in this community are known for their charitable work.  这个社区的门诺派教徒以他们的慈善工作而闻名。| Amish, Anabaptists |  Catholics, Baptists (in terms of theological difference)| 名词
