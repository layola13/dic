incontestably:/ˌɪnkənˈtɛstəbli/ | adj. |无可争议地；确定无疑地 | undeniably; certainly; without question.  The evidence incontestably proves his guilt.  | 近义词：undeniably, indisputably, undeniably, unquestionably | 反义词：disputably, debatably


例句:

His contribution to the field is incontestably significant.  他的贡献对该领域来说无可争议地意义重大。

The results of the experiment incontestably support the hypothesis.  实验的结果无可争议地支持了这个假设。
