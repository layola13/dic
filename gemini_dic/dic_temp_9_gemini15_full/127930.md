neonatally:/ˌniːoʊˈneɪtəli/| adv. |新生儿期地，在新生儿期|Of or relating to the neonatal period.  This adverb describes something that happens during the neonatal period, the first four weeks of a baby's life.|The baby was neonatally diagnosed with jaundice.|近义词：in the neonatal period, during the newborn period|反义词：postnatally
