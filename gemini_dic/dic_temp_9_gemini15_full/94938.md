in-passing:/ɪn ˈpɑːsɪŋ/ |adj.|顺便提一下；顺便说一句|Something said or done in passing is said or done casually or incidentally, without it being the main focus.|He mentioned the news in passing. 他顺便提到了这个消息。|近义词：incidentally, casually, briefly|反义词：deliberately, intentionally, formally

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mention the project in passing during meetings. = 他们在会议上顺便提到了这个项目。
She often greets her neighbors in passing. = 她经常顺便跟邻居打招呼。

一般过去时:
He mentioned his trip in passing. = 他顺便提到了他的旅行。
She dropped the comment in passing. = 她顺便提了一句。

现在完成时:
They've mentioned the problem in passing several times. = 他们已经好几次顺便提到了这个问题。
She has casually mentioned her plans in passing. = 她已经顺便随意地提到了她的计划。

过去完成时:
He had already mentioned it in passing before the meeting. = 他在会议前就已经顺便提到了。
She had casually alluded to the issue in passing. = 她之前已经顺便暗示过这个问题了。


将来时:
He will mention the issue in passing during the presentation. = 他将在演示中顺便提及这个问题。
She will likely mention the news in passing tomorrow. = 她明天可能会顺便提到这个消息。

虚拟语气:
If he were to mention it in passing, I'd be surprised. = 如果他顺便提起，我会很惊讶。
I wish she hadn't mentioned it in passing. = 我真希望她没顺便提起。

被动语态:
The news was mentioned in passing by the reporter. = 这个消息被记者顺便提到了。
The suggestion was dropped in passing during the discussion. = 这个建议在讨论中被顺便提了出来。

疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't mention the problem in passing. = 他没有顺便提及这个问题。
