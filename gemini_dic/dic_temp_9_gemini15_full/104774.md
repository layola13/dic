kayo:/ˈkaɪoʊ/| n. |歌谣，民歌|A traditional song, especially one of a particular region or culture.  Kayo often refers to songs passed down through generations, typically with a strong connection to a specific community or tradition.|例句：The villagers gathered to sing traditional kayo. 村民们聚集在一起唱传统的民歌。|例句：This kayo tells the story of their ancestors. 这首民歌讲述了他们祖先的故事。|近义词：ballad, folk song, traditional song|反义词：pop song, modern song
