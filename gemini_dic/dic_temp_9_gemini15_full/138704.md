nullibiety:/nʌlɪˈbaɪəti/| n. |不存在；虚无；无效|The concept of nullibiety refers to the state of not existing in any place or the state of being nonexistent.  It often implies a lack of presence, reality, or validity.  The term is rarely used in everyday conversation and is more common in philosophical or legal discussions.|例句：The argument for nullibiety in this case rests on the lack of any verifiable evidence.  这个案例中关于不存在的论点基于缺乏任何可验证的证据。
The alleged treaty's nullibiety was confirmed by the court.  法院证实了该条约的无效性。|近义词：nonexistence, nothingness, invalidity |反义词：existence, reality, validity |
