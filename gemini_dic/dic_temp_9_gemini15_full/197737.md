shiitake:/ʃiːˈtɑːki/| n. |香菇|A type of edible mushroom, highly prized in East Asian cuisine for its distinctive savory umami flavor and meaty texture.  Shiitake mushrooms are known for their nutritional benefits, including high levels of vitamins and minerals. |例句：The stir-fry was enhanced by the earthy flavor of shiitake mushrooms. 这道炒菜因香菇的泥土气息而更加美味。|近义词：mushroom, fungus |反义词:N/A
