reassimilated:/ˌriːəˈsɪməleɪtɪd/ | v. (过去分词) | 再同化；重新吸收 |  The process by which something previously excluded or rejected is integrated back into a system or group. This often involves a change in perspective or understanding.  | Example: After years of conflict, the opposing factions were reassimilated into the broader national identity.  = 多年冲突后，对立派系重新融入更广泛的民族认同。|近义词：reintegrated, absorbed, incorporated | 反义词：segregated, excluded, marginalized | 动词
