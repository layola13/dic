cheetahs:/ˈtʃiːtəz/| n. |猎豹（复数）|The plural form of "cheetah," referring to multiple animals of the species Acinonyx jubatus.  Cheetahs are known for their speed and spotted coats. |例句：The cheetahs gracefully chased the gazelle across the savanna.  猎豹优雅地追逐着穿过草原的瞪羚。

例句：We saw a pride of cheetahs resting in the shade. 我们看到一群猎豹在树荫下休息。

近义词：swift animals, spotted cats|反义词：None directly applicable, but could consider slower animals like tortoises as a contrasting example. |词性:名词
