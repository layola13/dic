homosapiens:/ˌhoʊmoʊˈseɪpiənz/| n. |智人|Homo sapiens is the binomial nomenclature (scientific name) for the only extant human species.  It is characterized by its large brain, bipedalism, and capacity for complex language and tool use.  The species originated in Africa and spread across the globe, eventually diversifying into various populations with distinct physical characteristics.  The term "sapiens" means "wise" or "knowing" in Latin, reflecting the species' advanced cognitive abilities.| Humans are Homo sapiens.  智人是智人。|近义词:human, humankind, mankind|反义词:N/A
