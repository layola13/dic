postpartum:/ˌpoʊstˈpɑːrtəm/| adj. |产后的，分娩后的| Relating to the period after childbirth.  The term encompasses the physical, emotional, and psychological changes experienced by a woman after giving birth. | Example: Postpartum depression is a serious condition affecting many new mothers. 产后抑郁症是一种影响许多新妈妈的严重疾病。|近义词：postnatal, after childbirth |反义词：antenatal, prepartum
