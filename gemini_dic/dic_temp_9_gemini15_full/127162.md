naturalesque:/ˌnætʃərəlˈɛsk/ | adj. | 自然风格的，仿自然的 |  Resembling or suggestive of nature; having a natural or unartificial appearance or style.  It often implies an idealized or romanticized version of nature, rather than a strictly accurate representation. | The painting had a naturalesque quality, capturing the essence of the landscape without being overly detailed. 这幅画有一种自然风格，捕捉了风景的精髓，而没有过于细节化。 |近义词：naturalistic, nature-like, realistic (in some contexts) | 反义词：artificial, unnatural, manufactured
