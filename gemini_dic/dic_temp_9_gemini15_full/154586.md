persecutor:/ˈpɜːrsɪkjuːtə(r)/| n. |迫害者，追捕者|A persecutor is someone who persecutes others, especially for their religious or political beliefs.  They inflict suffering, oppression, or injustice on others. |Example: The dictator was a ruthless persecutor of his people. 独裁者残酷地迫害人民。|近义词：oppressor, tormentor, harasser|反义词：protector, savior, benefactor

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The persecutor constantly harasses the victim. = 迫害者不断骚扰受害者。
He persecutes anyone who disagrees with him. = 他迫害任何不同意他的人。

一般过去时:
The persecutor imprisoned many innocent people. = 迫害者监禁了许多无辜的人。
They persecuted religious minorities for centuries. = 他们几个世纪以来一直迫害宗教少数群体。

现在完成时:
The government has persecuted political dissidents for years. = 政府多年来一直迫害政治异见人士。
He has persecuted his neighbors relentlessly. = 他无情地迫害了他的邻居。

过去完成时:
The regime had persecuted the population before the revolution. = 政权在革命之前就迫害了人民。
They had persecuted the artists for their controversial works. = 他们因为这些艺术家具有争议性的作品而迫害了他们。

将来时:
The dictator will persecute anyone who opposes him. = 独裁者将迫害任何反对他的人。
They will persecute those who speak out against the regime. = 他们将迫害那些公开反对政权的人。

虚拟语气:
If he were a persecutor, he would silence all opposition. = 如果他是迫害者，他会压制所有反对声音。
I wish the persecutor would stop their actions. = 我希望迫害者能停止他们的行为。

被动语态:
Many were persecuted for their beliefs. = 许多人因为他们的信仰而受到迫害。
The dissidents were persecuted by the authorities. = 异见人士受到当局的迫害。

疑问句:
Who is the persecutor in this situation? = 在这种情况下，迫害者是谁？
Was he a persecutor of religious minorities? = 他是宗教少数群体的迫害者吗？

否定句:
He was not a persecutor, but a victim. = 他不是迫害者，而是受害者。
She did not persecute anyone, despite the accusations. = 尽管有指控，她并没有迫害任何人。
