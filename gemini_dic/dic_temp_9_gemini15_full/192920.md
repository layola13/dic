seaweedy: /ˈsiːwiːdi/ | adj. | 海藻似的；像海藻的 |  Having the characteristics of seaweed; resembling seaweed in appearance or texture.  The term often implies a slightly unpleasant or unkempt appearance, similar to tangled seaweed. |例句：The long, seaweedy hair draped over her shoulders.  她的长发像海藻一样披散在肩上。

例句：The neglected garden had a seaweedy, overgrown look.  被忽视的花园看起来杂草丛生，像海藻一样。


近义词：tangled, matted, overgrown, weedy


反义词：neat, tidy, groomed, well-kept


词性：形容词
