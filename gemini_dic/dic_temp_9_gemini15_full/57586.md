dramaturgy:/ˈdræməˌtɜːrʒi/|/ˈdræməˌtɜːrdʒi/| n. |戏剧理论与实践；编剧艺术|The art and technique of writing plays; the theory and practice of dramatic composition and theatrical production.  It encompasses various aspects of playwriting, including plot, character development, dialogue, theme, and structure, as well as the staging and performance of plays.|The play's dramaturgy was masterful, seamlessly weaving together complex themes and compelling characters. 这部戏剧的戏剧理论与实践非常出色，巧妙地融合了复杂的主题和引人入胜的人物。|playwriting, scriptwriting, theatrical theory|n/a|名词
