insulting: /ɪnˈsʌltɪŋ/ | adj. | 侮辱的；无礼的 |  Offensive, rude, or disrespectful; causing someone to feel deeply offended.  | Example: His insulting remarks deeply hurt her feelings.  他的侮辱性言论深深伤害了她的感情。  |近义词：offensive, rude, abusive, disrespectful, contemptuous | 反义词：complimentary, respectful, polite, courteous
