nonanimal: /ˌnɒnˈænɪməl/ | adj. |非动物的；非动物性的 | Not relating to or derived from animals.  This adjective describes things that are not of animal origin or nature. It's often used in contexts discussing food, materials, or products. | 例句：The company produces nonanimal-based collagen. 这家公司生产非动物来源的胶原蛋白。


例句：Many vegetarians prefer nonanimal sources of protein. 许多素食者更喜欢非动物来源的蛋白质。


近义词：animal-free, plant-based


反义词：animal, animal-derived


词性：形容词
