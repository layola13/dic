indentation:/ˌɪn.denˈteɪ.ʃən/ | n. | 缩进，(文本的)缩排 | The process of setting a line or lines of text or a paragraph in from the margin. It's used to improve readability and structure in writing.  | Example: The indentation of the first paragraph was incorrect.  这个段落的首行缩进不正确。 |近义词：inset, recess | 反义词：flush, alignment
