heterosporous:/ˌhɛtərəˈspɔːrəs/| adj.| 异孢的 | Producing two types of spores, microspores and megaspores, which develop into male and female gametophytes respectively.  This is in contrast to homosporous plants, which produce only one type of spore. | Example:  Many ferns are homosporous, while Selaginella is a common example of a heterosporous plant.  许多蕨类植物是同孢的，而卷柏是异孢植物的常见例子。|近义词：differently sporing |反义词：homosporous
