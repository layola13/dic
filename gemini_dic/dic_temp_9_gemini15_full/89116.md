homburg:/ˈhɒmbɜːrɡ/ /ˈhɑːmbɜːrɡ/| n. |霍姆堡帽|A felt hat with a crown creased lengthwise and a ribbon band.  It is typically associated with men's formal wear.  |He wore a stylish homburg to the wedding.|近义词：fedora, trilby|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with informal headwear like a baseball cap.
