copses:/ˈkɒpsɪz/ | n. | 小树林，矮树丛 | A copse is a small group of trees, smaller than a wood or forest.  It is often dense and tangled. | Example: We took a shortcut through the copse. 我们抄近路穿过小树林。 |近义词：grove, thicket, woodlet |反义词：forest (in terms of size)

词源:
The word "copse" comes from the Old English word "cop," meaning "a small hill or clump of trees."  Over time, the meaning narrowed to refer specifically to a small group of trees.
