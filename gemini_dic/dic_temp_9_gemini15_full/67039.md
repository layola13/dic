explanatorily:/ɪkˈsplænətərili/ | adv. | 解释性地，说明性地 | In a way that explains or clarifies something.  It emphasizes the act of explaining. | The professor spoke explanatorily about the complex equation, ensuring everyone understood. 教授解释性地讲解了复杂的方程式，确保每个人都理解。 | clearly, illustratively, elucidatively | unclearly, confusingly | 副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have tenses like verbs.  Its usage is primarily about modifying verbs and clauses to indicate the manner in which an explanation is given.


Example Sentences:

He answered explanatorily, detailing each step of the process. 他解释性地回答，详细说明了每个步骤。

She wrote explanatorily about the historical context, providing ample evidence. 她解释性地写了历史背景，提供了充分的证据。



PHRASE->
There aren't common phrases directly using "explanatorily."  Its usage is more integrated into sentences rather than forming distinct phrases.  However, phrases that convey similar meanings include:


in an explanatory manner
by way of explanation
to explain fully
with clear explanation
for the sake of clarity
