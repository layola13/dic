endoskeletal:/ˌɛndoʊˈskɛlətəl/| adj.|内骨骼的|Having an internal skeleton; relating to an internal skeleton.  An endoskeletal structure provides support and protection from within the organism.|例句：Humans have an endoskeletal system. = 人类拥有内骨骼系统。

例句：The endoskeletal structure of a bird contributes to its ability to fly. = 鸟类的内骨骼结构有助于其飞行能力。


近义词：internal skeletal


反义词：exoskeletal


词性:形容词
