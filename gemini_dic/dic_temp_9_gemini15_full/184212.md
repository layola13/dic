resolicited:/ˌriːˈsɒlɪsɪtɪd/| adj. |再次请求的，再次征求的 |Again solicited; requested or sought again.  This usually implies a previous request that was unsuccessful or ignored.|The company resolicited bids after the initial round proved unsatisfactory. 公司在第一轮投标不令人满意后再次征求投标。|近义词：re-requested, re-solicited, renewed |反义词：rejected, refused


USAGE->

He resolicited donations for the charity. 他再次为慈善机构募捐。
The company resolicits customers for feedback regularly. 公司定期再次向客户征求反馈。


一般过去时:
They resolicited the contract after the initial negotiations failed.  他们第一次谈判失败后再次争取合同。
The artist resolicited opinions on his latest artwork.  这位艺术家再次征求了他最新艺术作品的意见。
