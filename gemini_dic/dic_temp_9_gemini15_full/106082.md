knickered:/ˈnɪkərd/| adj.|  裤子破了的；筋疲力尽的；|  Describes something, typically trousers or pants, that is torn or damaged.  It can also informally mean extremely tired or exhausted.|

例句：

My trousers are knickered after climbing that mountain. = 我爬山后裤子都破了。

I'm absolutely knickered after that marathon. = 马拉松跑完后我累坏了。


近义词：torn, ripped, exhausted, worn out, fatigued

反义词：intact, undamaged, energetic, refreshed

词性：形容词
