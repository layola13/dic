predestroy:/ˌpriːdɪˈstrɔɪ/| v.|预先破坏；事先摧毁 |To destroy something before it can be used or before a particular event.  This is a relatively rare word, often used in a technical or specialized context. It implies a proactive and preventative destruction, rather than a reactive one. |例句：The enemy attempted to predestroy the bridge to prevent our advance. 敌人试图预先摧毁桥梁以阻止我们的前进。The system is designed to predestroy any malware before it can infect the network. 该系统旨在在任何恶意软件感染网络之前将其预先摧毁。|近义词：preempt, sabotage, annihilate (in certain contexts)|反义词：preserve, protect, conserve|动词
