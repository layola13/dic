deaconry:/ˈdiːkənri/| n. |执事区；执事职务；执事住所|The area or district served by a deacon or deacons, often encompassing a church and its associated community.  It can also refer to the office or duties of a deacon, or the building where a deacon works or resides.|例句：The deaconry organized a community outreach program. 执事区组织了一个社区外展项目。
The new deaconry building is modern and spacious. 新的执事住所现代而宽敞。|近义词：parish, ministry|反义词：无
