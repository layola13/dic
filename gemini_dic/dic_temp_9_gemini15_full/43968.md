crickets:/ˈkrɪkɪts/| n.|蟋蟀；寂静|The sound of crickets filled the night. 蟋蟀的叫声充满了夜晚。|近义词：insect, grasshopper|反义词：noise, clamor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Crickets chirp at night. = 蟋蟀在晚上鸣叫。
Many crickets live in fields. = 许多蟋蟀生活在田野里。


一般过去时:
The crickets chirped loudly last night. = 蟋蟀昨晚叫得很响。
We heard crickets singing in the grass. = 我们听到草丛里蟋蟀在唱歌。


现在完成时:
The crickets have been chirping all night. = 蟋蟀整夜都在鸣叫。
Have you ever heard crickets chirping in the winter? = 你冬天听过蟋蟀鸣叫吗？


过去完成时:
The crickets had already stopped chirping before we arrived. =在我们到达之前，蟋蟀已经停止鸣叫了。
They had heard the crickets chirping earlier that evening. = 他们那天晚上早些时候听到过蟋蟀的鸣叫。

词源:
The word "cricket" comes from the Old French "criquet,"  ultimately deriving from the Old French word "criquer," meaning "to creak" or "to crack,"  referencing the characteristic sound the insect makes.
