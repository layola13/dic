mind-numbingly: /ˈmaɪndˌnʌmbɪŋli/ | adj. |令人极其乏味的，令人昏昏欲睡的 | Extremely boring or tedious to the point of causing mental numbness.  | The lecture was mind-numbingly boring. 这场演讲枯燥乏味到令人昏昏欲睡。|近义词： incredibly boring, excruciatingly dull, tediously monotonous |反义词： exciting, stimulating, interesting

词源:

* **mind:**  Old English *gemynd*, meaning "memory, intention, thought."  It's related to words like "remember" and "remind."
* **numb:** Old English *numbe*, meaning "stiff, benumbed."  It relates to a loss of feeling or sensation.
* **-ingly:**  Adverbial suffix, indicating manner or degree.

The combination creates a vivid image of the mind becoming "numb" from excessive boredom or tedium.
