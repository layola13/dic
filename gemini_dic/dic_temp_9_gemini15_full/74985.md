Friday:/ˈfraɪdeɪ/| n. |星期五 |The fifth day of the week, following Thursday and preceding Saturday.  It is often associated with the end of the work week and the beginning of the weekend. |例句：Friday is my favorite day of the week because I get to relax. 星期五是我最喜欢的日子，因为我可以放松一下。

例句：We're having a party this Friday. 我们这个星期五要举行一个派对。


近义词：Fri. (abbreviation)


反义词：无


词性：名词
