putrefied:/ˈpjuːtrɪfaɪd/| adj.|腐烂的；腐败的|Decayed and foul-smelling, typically due to the action of bacteria.  The term often implies a significant degree of decomposition. |例句：The putrefied carcass attracted a swarm of flies.  腐烂的尸体吸引了一群苍蝇。

The putrefied meat was unfit for consumption.  腐烂的肉不适合食用。


近义词：rotten, decayed, decomposed, corrupted

反义词：fresh, pristine, uncorrupted


词源:  From Middle English *putrefied, from Old French *putrefier, from Latin *putrefacere, from *putrē- (“rotten”) + *facere (“to make”).
