dodos:/ˈdoʊdoʊz/| n.| 渡渡鸟（复数）|The plural form of "dodo," referring to multiple extinct flightless birds of the genus Raphus, native to Mauritius.|The dodos were famously clumsy and easily hunted to extinction.|近义词：extinct birds|反义词：无
