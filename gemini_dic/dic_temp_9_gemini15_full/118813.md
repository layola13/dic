metamorphose:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəʊs/| v.| 变形；蜕变；转化 | To undergo a transformation or change in form or nature, often into something more advanced or beautiful.  |例句：The caterpillar will metamorphose into a butterfly.  毛毛虫将蜕变成蝴蝶。

The frog undergoes a complete metamorphosis. 青蛙经历了完全变态。


近义词：transform, transmute, change, evolve | 反义词：remain unchanged, stay the same | 词性: Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The caterpillar metamorphoses into a butterfly. = 毛毛虫蜕变成蝴蝶。
Nature metamorphoses constantly. = 自然界在不断地变化。


一般过去时:
The tadpole metamorphosed into a frog. = 蝌蚪变成了青蛙。
The rock formation metamorphosed over millions of years. = 岩石构造经过数百万年的变质。


现在完成时:
The artist has metamorphosed his style. = 艺术家已经改变了他的风格。
The company has metamorphosed into a tech giant. = 这家公司已经蜕变成了科技巨头。


过去完成时:
The caterpillar had metamorphosed before I saw it. = 我看到它之前，毛毛虫已经蜕变了。
The city had metamorphosed dramatically since the last time I visited. = 自我上次来访以来，这座城市发生了翻天覆地的变化。


将来时:
The larva will metamorphose into a beetle. = 幼虫将蜕变成甲虫。
The project will metamorphose into something truly impressive. = 这个项目将转变为真正令人印象深刻的东西。


虚拟语气:
If the prince kissed her, she would metamorphose into a princess. = 如果王子吻了她，她就会变成公主。
I wish my life would metamorphose for the better. = 我希望我的生活能变得更好。


被动语态:
The story was metamorphosed into a play. = 这个故事被改编成了一部戏剧。
The old building is being metamorphosed into a modern apartment complex. = 这座旧建筑正在改造成现代化的公寓楼。


疑问句:
Will the project metamorphose as planned? = 项目会按计划进行转变吗？
Does the caterpillar metamorphose quickly? = 毛毛虫蜕变快吗？


否定句:
The stone did not metamorphose. = 石头没有发生变质。
The plan did not metamorphose into reality. = 计划没有变成现实。


PHRASE->
metamorphose into = 变成...
undergo a metamorphosis = 经历蜕变
complete metamorphosis = 完全变态
gradual metamorphosis = 逐渐变态
a metamorphic rock = 变质岩

词源:
From the Greek word "metamorphoo" (μεταμορφόω), meaning "to transform" or "to change form," which is composed of "meta" (μετά), meaning "beyond" or "after," and "morphe" (μορφή), meaning "form" or "shape."
