deep-rooted:/ˌdiːpˈruːtɪd/| adj.|根深蒂固的，由来已久的| deeply established and firmly held; difficult to change or eradicate. |例句：His deep-rooted prejudices made it difficult to have a rational discussion. 他根深蒂固的偏见使理性讨论变得困难。

例句：The deep-rooted traditions of the community are slowly changing. 社区的根深蒂固的传统正在慢慢改变。


近义词：ingrained, entrenched, firmly established, fundamental


反义词：superficial, shallow, recent, newly formed
