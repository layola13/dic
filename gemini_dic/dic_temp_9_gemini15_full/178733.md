rectifies:/ˈrɛktɪfaɪz/| v. |纠正，矫正，修复 |To make or become correct, right, or accurate; to put right.  It implies correcting an error or fault.|The government is rectifying the economic imbalances. 政府正在纠正经济失衡。|He rectified the mistake in the report. 他纠正了报告中的错误。|correct, amend, adjust |worsen, exacerbate, deteriorate|动词
