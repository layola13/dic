nonpacifist: /ˌnɒnˈpæsɪfɪst/ | adj. | 非和平主义者；好战的 |  A person or group opposed to pacifism; advocating or engaging in war or violence.  | Example:  The general's nonpacifist stance was evident in his aggressive military strategies.  这位将军的好战立场在他的侵略性军事战略中显而易见。 | 近义词：bellicose, militant, hawkish | 反义词：pacifist
