preregulating: /ˌpriːˈreɡjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先监管；预先规范 |  The act of regulating something beforehand, often to prevent future problems or ensure compliance with regulations before an activity begins.  It implies proactive control and setting standards in advance. | 例句：The government is preregulating the new technology to minimize potential risks. 政府正在预先监管这项新技术以最大限度地减少潜在风险。


The company is preregulating its waste disposal practices to ensure environmental compliance.  该公司正在预先规范其废物处理措施以确保遵守环保法规。 | 近义词：pre-regulating, proactively regulating, pre-controlling | 反义词：deregulating, neglecting, ignoring | 动词
