mottes:/mɒts/| n.|土墩，土垒|A motte is a raised mound of earth, typically artificial, often forming the central feature of a motte-and-bailey castle.  It served as a defensive position, providing a strategic advantage.|例句：The Norman castle was built on a motte, offering excellent views and protection.  这个诺曼城堡建在一个土墩上，视野开阔，易守难攻。|近义词：mound, earthwork, rampart|反义词：valley, depression
