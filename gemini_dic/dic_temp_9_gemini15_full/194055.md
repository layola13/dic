self-harm:/ˌsɛlfˈhɑːrm/| n. v.|自残；自我伤害|The act of intentionally injuring oneself, typically as a coping mechanism for emotional distress.|例句：She engaged in self-harm as a way to deal with her intense anxiety. 她通过自残来应对强烈的焦虑。|近义词：self-mutilation, self-injury|反义词：self-care, self-preservation|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He self-harms regularly. = 他经常自残。
She self-harms to cope with stress. = 她通过自残来应对压力。

一般过去时:
He self-harmed last night. = 他昨晚自残了。
She self-harmed after the argument. = 争吵后她自残了。

现在完成时:
He has self-harmed many times before. = 他以前自残过很多次。
She has never self-harmed before this incident. = 在这次事件之前她从未自残过。

过去完成时:
He had self-harmed several times before seeking help. = 他在寻求帮助之前已经自残过几次了。
She had never self-harmed until that traumatic experience. = 直到那次创伤性经历，她才开始自残。

将来时:
He will likely self-harm again if he doesn't get help. = 如果他不寻求帮助，他很可能会再次自残。
She will probably self-harm again unless she addresses her underlying issues. = 除非她解决潜在问题，否则她可能会再次自残。

虚拟语气:
If he knew better, he wouldn't self-harm. = 如果他知道得更好，他就不会自残了。
If she had sought help sooner, she might not have self-harmed so much. = 如果她早点寻求帮助，她可能就不会自残那么多了。

被动语态:  (被动语态在描述自残行为时较少使用，因为自残是主动行为)
Self-harm is often a symptom of underlying mental health issues. = 自残通常是潜在心理健康问题的症状。
Self-harm is rarely discussed openly. = 自残很少被公开讨论。


疑问句:
Does he self-harm? = 他会自残吗？
Has she ever self-harmed? = 她曾经自残过吗？


否定句:
He doesn't self-harm anymore. = 他不再自残了。
She did not self-harm after the therapy. = 治疗后她没有自残。


PHRASE->
engage in self-harm = 从事自残行为
attempt self-harm = 企图自残
recover from self-harm = 从自残中恢复
prevent self-harm = 预防自残
signs of self-harm = 自残迹象
