clarify:/ˈklærɪfaɪ/ | vt. |澄清；阐明；使…清楚 |To make something clearer or easier to understand.  |例句：Can you clarify the instructions? 你能解释一下说明吗？|例句：The professor clarified the confusing concept. 教授阐明了那个令人困惑的概念。|近义词：explain, elucidate, illuminate |反义词：obscure, confuse, muddle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He clarifies his position every week. = 他每周都阐明自己的立场。
She clarifies the rules before the game. = 她在比赛前澄清规则。

一般过去时:
The teacher clarified the difficult questions. = 老师解释了那些难题。
He clarified his misunderstanding with his friend. = 他向朋友解释了他的误解。

现在完成时:
She has clarified the situation to the police. = 她已经向警方解释了情况。
We have clarified our concerns with the management. = 我们已经向管理层阐明了我们的担忧。

过去完成时:
They had clarified the details before the meeting. = 他们会在会议前澄清细节。
She had clarified her intentions to her family. = 她已经向家人解释了她的意图。

将来时:
He will clarify the issue in his next speech. = 他将在下一次演讲中阐明这个问题。
We will clarify the terms of the contract tomorrow. = 我们明天将阐明合同的条款。

虚拟语气:
If he clarified the instructions, we would understand better. = 如果他解释一下说明，我们会更容易理解。
I wish he had clarified the situation earlier. = 我希望他早点解释清楚情况。

被动语态:
The misunderstanding was clarified by the manager. = 误会被经理解释清楚了。
The issue will be clarified in the upcoming report. = 这个问题将在即将发布的报告中得到澄清。

疑问句:
Can you clarify what you mean? = 你能解释一下你的意思吗？
Will he clarify the doubts of the audience? = 他会解答观众的疑问吗？

否定句:
He didn't clarify his position on the matter. = 他没有就此事阐明他的立场。
She failed to clarify the ambiguous statement. = 她未能解释清楚含糊不清的陈述。


PHRASE->
clarify the situation = 澄清情况
clarify the issues = 阐明问题
clarify the meaning = 解释含义
clarify the doubts = 解答疑问
clarify the rules = 澄清规则


词源:
From Middle English *clarifien, from Old French clarifier, from clar, clair "clear" + -fier "to make".  The ultimate origin is from the Latin word *clarus meaning "clear, bright".
