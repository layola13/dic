premaintain:/ˌpriːmeɪnˈteɪn/| v.| 预先维护，提前维护 | To maintain something before a problem arises; preventative maintenance.  | Example:  The company premaintains all its equipment to minimize downtime.  这家公司对所有设备进行预先维护，以最大限度地减少停机时间。 | 近义词：preventative maintenance, proactive maintenance | 反义词：reactive maintenance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They premaintain the servers regularly. = 他们定期对服务器进行预先维护。
We premaintain our vehicles to prevent breakdowns. = 我们对车辆进行预先维护以防止故障。


一般过去时:
The engineers premaintained the bridge before the storm. = 工程师们在暴风雨来临之前对桥梁进行了预先维护。
The team premaintained the software system last month. = 团队上个月对软件系统进行了预先维护。


现在完成时:
They have premaintained the machinery for years. = 他们多年来一直在对机械进行预先维护。
The company has premaintained its network infrastructure successfully. = 公司成功地对网络基础设施进行了预先维护。


过去完成时:
The mechanic had premaintained the car, so the trip was smooth. = 机械师已经对汽车进行了预先维护，所以旅程很顺利。
They had premaintained the equipment, preventing a major failure. = 他们已经对设备进行了预先维护，防止了重大事故。


将来时:
We will premaintain the database next week. = 我们下周将对数据库进行预先维护。
They will premaintain the aircraft before the flight. = 他们将在飞行前对飞机进行预先维护。


虚拟语气:
If they had premaintained the system, the crash wouldn't have happened. = 如果他们对系统进行了预先维护，就不会发生崩溃了。
It would be wise to premaintain the building regularly. = 定期对建筑物进行预先维护是明智之举。


被动语态:
The equipment is premaintained monthly. = 设备每月进行预先维护。
The software will be premaintained by the IT team. = 软件将由 IT 团队进行预先维护。


疑问句:
Do they premaintain their tools? = 他们会对工具进行预先维护吗？
Has the system been premaintained recently? = 系统最近是否进行了预先维护？


否定句:
They don't premaintain the engines often enough. = 他们对发动机进行预先维护的频率不够高。
The company didn't premaintain its aging infrastructure. = 公司没有对其老化的基础设施进行预先维护。
