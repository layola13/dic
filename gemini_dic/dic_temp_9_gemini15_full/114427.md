make tracks:/meɪk træks/| phrasal verb|迅速离开；赶紧走；逃离|To leave quickly, especially to escape a dangerous or unpleasant situation.  It implies a hurried departure, often suggesting urgency or a desire to avoid something.|例句：The police arrived, and the thieves made tracks. 警察来了，小偷们赶紧逃跑了。|例句：Seeing the angry bull, the matador made tracks.  看到愤怒的公牛，斗牛士赶紧逃跑了。|近义词：run away, flee, bolt, scarper, hightail it|反义词：stay, remain, linger
