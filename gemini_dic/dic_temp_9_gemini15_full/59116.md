dynameter:/daɪˈnæmɪtər/| n. |测力计，测功器|A dynameter is an instrument used to measure force, power, or torque.  It can be used in various applications, from measuring the strength of a muscle to the power output of an engine.|例句：The engineer used a dynameter to measure the engine's horsepower. 工程师用测力计测量发动机的马力。

The mechanic employed a dynameter to assess the torque output of the vehicle's engine. 机械师使用测功器评估车辆发动机的扭矩输出。|近义词：dynamometer, force gauge, torque meter|反义词：None specifically, but instruments measuring opposite quantities (e.g., a speedometer) could be considered contrastive.|名词
