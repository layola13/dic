hooroo:/hʊˈruː/| int.|再见，再会|An informal Australian and New Zealand English term used as a farewell.|例句：Hooroo, mate!  再见，老兄！|近义词：bye, farewell, goodbye, cheerio|反义词：hello


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I say hooroo to my friends every day. = 我每天都对我的朋友说再见。
We usually say hooroo before we leave work. = 我们通常在收工前说再见。


一般过去时:
I said hooroo and left the party. = 我说了再见就离开了派对。
They said hooroo to each other and went their separate ways. = 他们互相道别后就各走各的路了。


现在完成时:
I have said hooroo to him several times today. = 我今天已经和他说了几次再见了。
She has said hooroo to her childhood home. = 她已经和她的童年故居告别了。


过去完成时:
I had already said hooroo before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经说再见了。
They had said hooroo to their families before embarking on their journey. = 他们在开始旅程之前已经和家人道别了。


将来时:
I will say hooroo to you tomorrow. = 我明天会和你道别的。
We will say hooroo after the meeting. = 会议结束后我们会道别的。


虚拟语气:
If I saw him, I would say hooroo. = 如果我看到他，我会和他道别的。
I wish I had said hooroo to her before she left. = 我真希望在她离开之前和她道别。


被动语态:
Hooroo is often said in Australia and New Zealand. = 再见经常在澳大利亚和新西兰被说。
(Note:  "Hooroo" is rarely used in passive voice)


疑问句:
Have you said hooroo to your parents? = 你和你父母道别了吗？
Will you say hooroo before you go? = 你走之前会道别吗？


否定句:
I didn't say hooroo to him. = 我没有和他道别。
She didn't say hooroo before leaving the house. = 她离开家前没有道别。


PHRASE->
Hooroo for now! = 暂时再见！
See you later, hooroo! = 等会儿见，再见！
That's all for now, hooroo! = 暂时就这些，再见！
Cheerio and hooroo! = 再见！（双重再见）
Have a good one, hooroo! = 祝你一切顺利，再见！
