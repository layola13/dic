It is less common than terms like "scapular spine" or "acromion". | Example: The mesoscapula showed signs of inflammation in the X-ray.  X光片显示肩胛骨中部有炎症迹象。 |近义词: mid-scapula (less precise) | 反义词:  None directly, but terms referring to other scapular regions (e.g., acromion, glenoid cavity) could be considered opposites in a positional sense.
