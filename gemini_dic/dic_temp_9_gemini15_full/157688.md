pixelation:/ˌpɪksəˈleɪʃən/| n. | 像素化，马赛克化 | The effect of an image being broken down into its individual pixels, often resulting in a blocky or grainy appearance.  This is often caused by low resolution, image compression, or zooming in too far on a digital image. |Example: The pixelation on the old video was quite noticeable.  这老视频的像素化相当明显。  The image suffered from severe pixelation after being compressed.  图片在压缩后出现了严重的像素化。|近义词： pixelization, graininess |反义词： high resolution, clarity |名词
