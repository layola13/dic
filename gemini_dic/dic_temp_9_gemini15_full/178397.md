reconnections: /ˌriːkəˈnekʃənz/ | n. |重新连接，恢复联系 | The act of connecting again; the re-establishment of a connection, relationship, or communication.  It often implies a prior connection that was lost or broken. |

After years of silence, their reconnection was filled with joy and nostalgia.  多年沉默之后，他们的重逢充满了喜悦和怀旧之情。

The reconnections made at the alumni reunion were heartwarming.  校友会上重逢令人感到温暖。


近义词：reunions, restorations, renewals, re-establishments

反义词：disconnections, separations, severances
