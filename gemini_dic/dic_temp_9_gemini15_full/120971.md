mischiefable:/ˈmɪstʃɪfəbəl/ | adj. | 淘气的；爱恶作剧的；顽皮的 | Capable of causing mischief; mischievous; playful.  This word suggests a tendency towards playful naughtiness, often without malicious intent. |例句：The mischievous children planned a mischiefable prank for their teacher. 那些淘气的孩子计划给他们的老师开个恶作剧玩笑。

The little dog, though mischiefable, was ultimately harmless.这只小狗虽然很顽皮，但最终是无害的。|近义词：mischievous, naughty, playful, impish |反义词：well-behaved, docile, obedient
