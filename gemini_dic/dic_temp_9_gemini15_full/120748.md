misanthropist:/ˈmɪzænθrəpɪst/| n. |厌世者，遁世者|A misanthropist is a person who dislikes humankind and avoids human society.|He became a misanthropist after a series of betrayals by people he trusted. 他在经历了一系列被信任的人背叛后变成了一个厌世者。| cynic, hermit, recluse | philanthropist, altruist | 名词

The misanthropist avoids social gatherings. = 这位厌世者避免参加社交聚会。

词源:

The word "misanthropist" comes from the Greek words "misos" (meaning "hate") and "anthropos" (meaning "human").  Therefore, it literally means "human-hater."
