plumber:/ˈplʌmər/| n. |管道工，水暖工 |A plumber is a tradesperson who specializes in installing and maintaining water and drainage systems, as well as central heating systems.  They work with pipes, fittings, and fixtures. |例句：We called a plumber to fix the leaky faucet. 我们叫了个管道工来修理漏水的龙头。|例句：The plumber replaced all the old pipes in the house. 管道工更换了房子里所有旧的管道。|近义词：pipefitter, сантехник (Russian)|反义词: 无
