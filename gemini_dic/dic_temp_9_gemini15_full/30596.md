cerebellum: /ˌserəˈbɛləm/ | n. |小脑 | The cerebellum is the part of the brain at the back of the head, involved in coordination of movement and balance.  | Example: Damage to the cerebellum can lead to problems with balance and coordination.  小脑损伤会导致平衡和协调问题。 |近义词： (no direct synonym,  functions can be described using terms like "motor coordination center") |反义词：(no direct antonym)
