词源:
The word "hubbub" is of uncertain origin, possibly imitative of the sound it describes.  Some etymologists suggest a connection to Dutch words meaning "to shout" or "to cry out," but this link isn't definitively established.  Regardless of its precise origin, its onomatopoeic quality is clear.
