downway:/ˈdaʊnweɪ/| n. |下坡路，下行路线 | A downway refers to a downward path or route, often implying a decline or deterioration. It can be literal, referring to a road going downhill, or figurative, referring to a decline in fortunes, health, or morale. |例句：The hikers took the downway to reach the valley.  徒步旅行者走下坡路到达山谷。 The company is on a downway after losing its major client.  公司在失去主要客户后走下坡路了。|近义词：decline, descent, downturn, slide |反义词：upway, ascent, upswing, rise |名词
