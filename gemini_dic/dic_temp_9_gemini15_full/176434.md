re-interred:/ˌriːɪnˈtɜːrd/| vt.|重新下葬|To bury (a body) again, especially after it has been exhumed.|例句：The remains were re-interred in a more suitable location.遗体被重新安葬在一个更合适的地方。

例句：After the investigation, the victim's body was re-interred with full honors.  调查结束后，受害者的遗体以最高的荣誉重新下葬。


近义词：rebury, reburial

反义词：exhume, disinter

词性：动词
