skydiving: /ˈskaɪˌdaɪvɪŋ/ | n. | 跳伞运动 | The activity of jumping from an aircraft and descending to earth using a parachute.  | Example:  He's always dreamed of skydiving over the Grand Canyon. 他一直梦想在科罗拉多大峡谷上空跳伞。

Example: My sister went skydiving for her birthday. 我姐姐过生日的时候去跳伞了。


近义词: parachuting


反义词:  无直接反义词，可考虑与地面活动相对比，例如：ground-based activities


词性: 名词
