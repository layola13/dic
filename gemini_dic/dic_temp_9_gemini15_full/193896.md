self-doctrine:/ˈsɛlf ˈdɑːktrɪn/| n. | 自创教义；个人信条 | A self-doctrine is a set of beliefs or principles that someone has developed for themselves, often without the influence of established religious or philosophical systems.  It's a personal creed or ideology.  | Example:  He developed a self-doctrine based on his own experiences and reflections. 他根据自己的经验和反思发展出一套自创教义。|近义词：personal creed, private belief system, individual philosophy |反义词：orthodox doctrine, established belief system | 名词
