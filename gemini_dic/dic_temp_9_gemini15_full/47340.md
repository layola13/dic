de-signs:/dɪˈzaɪnz/| n.|设计；图案；计划；企图| Designs refer to the creation of plans or drawings for the construction or manufacture of something.  They can also refer to the patterns or decoration on an object.  The word can also imply an intention or scheme, often with a negative connotation. |例句：The architect presented her designs for the new building. 建筑师展示了她对新建筑的设计。

The intricate designs on the carpet were beautiful. 地毯上复杂的花纹很漂亮。


近义词：plans, schemes, patterns, blueprints, intentions |反义词：chaos, disorder, randomness|名词


USAGE->

He designs beautiful dresses. = 他设计漂亮的连衣裙。
The company designs software for businesses. = 这家公司为企业设计软件。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
