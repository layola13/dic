koans:/ˈkoʊæn/| n.|公案；禅宗故事；|A koan is a paradoxical anecdote or riddle, used in Zen Buddhism to demonstrate the inadequacy of logical reasoning and to provoke enlightenment.|例句：The master presented his student with a koan about a barking dog.|近义词：parable, riddle, puzzle|反义词:explanation, solution
