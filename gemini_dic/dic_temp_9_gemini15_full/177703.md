recaned:/ˌriːˈkeɪnd/| v.|重新编织，重新用藤编制|To weave or reweave with cane.  The process involves repairing or replacing damaged cane webbing in furniture or other items.|例句：The antique chair was beautifully recaned, restoring its original elegance. 这把古董椅子被精美地重新编织，恢复了它最初的优雅。


例句：The broken seat was recaned by a skilled craftsman.  坏了的座位被一位熟练的工匠重新编织了。


近义词：re-cane, repair, renovate|反义词：destroy, ruin|词性：动词
