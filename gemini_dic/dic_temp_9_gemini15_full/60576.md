elastomers:/ɪˈlæstəmərz/| n. |弹性体|Elastic polymers that can be stretched and will return to their original shape.  They are used in a wide variety of applications, including tires, seals, and medical devices.|例句：Synthetic elastomers are used extensively in the automotive industry. 合成弹性体广泛应用于汽车工业。|例句：Natural rubber is an example of a natural elastomer. 天然橡胶是天然弹性体的一个例子。|近义词：rubber, polymer, flexible material |反义词：rigid material, inflexible material
