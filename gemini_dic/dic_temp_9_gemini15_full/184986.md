retell:/ˌriːˈtɛl/ | vt. |复述，重述 |To tell or relate (something) again; repeat in one's own words.  |例句：Please retell the story in your own words. 请用你自己的话复述这个故事。|例句：He retold the joke, adding his own humorous touches. 他复述了这个笑话，并添加了自己的幽默元素。|近义词：recount, narrate, relate, repeat |反义词：forget, omit |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retells the story to his children every night. = 他每晚都给孩子们复述这个故事。
She retells the events of the day to her husband. = 她每天晚上都会把当天发生的事情告诉她的丈夫。

一般过去时:
I retold the joke to my friends, but they didn't laugh. = 我把笑话复述给我的朋友们听，但是他们没有笑。
She retold the incident to the police officer. = 她向警官复述了这起事件。


现在完成时:
He has retold that anecdote countless times. = 他已经无数次地复述了那个轶事。
She has retold the story so many times that she almost knows it by heart. = 她已经复述了这个故事很多次，几乎能背下来了。


过去完成时:
By the time he finished, he had retold the whole saga. = 等他说完时，他已经复述了整个故事。
She had retold the story several times before anyone believed her. = 在任何人相信她之前，她已经复述了好几次这个故事了。


将来时:
I will retell the story to my grandchildren someday. = 总有一天我会把这个故事复述给我的孙子孙女们听。
She will retell the experience in her upcoming book. = 她将在即将出版的书中复述这段经历。


虚拟语气:
If I were to retell the events, I would focus on the key details. = 如果我要复述这些事件，我会关注关键细节。
I wish I could retell the story with more conviction. = 我希望我能更有说服力地复述这个故事。


被动语态:
The story was retold many times over the years. = 这个故事多年来被反复讲述。
The incident will be retold in the upcoming documentary. = 这件事将在即将上映的纪录片中被重述。


疑问句:
Will you retell the story for me? = 你会为我复述这个故事吗？
Can you retell what happened? = 你能复述一下发生了什么吗？


否定句:
I cannot retell the story accurately. = 我无法准确地复述这个故事。
He refused to retell the events of that night. = 他拒绝复述那天晚上的事情。


PHRASE->
retell a story = 复述一个故事
retell the facts = 复述事实
retell an experience = 复述一次经历
retell the events = 复述事件
retell the details = 复述细节
