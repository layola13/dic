chastise:/tʃæˈstaɪz/| vt.|惩戒；严厉批评|to reprimand or punish severely|例句：The teacher chastised the student for cheating on the exam.老师因考试作弊而严厉斥责了学生。
例句：He was chastised for his reckless behavior. 他因鲁莽行为受到斥责。|近义词：punish, rebuke, reprimand, scold|反义词：reward, praise, commend|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chastises his children for misbehaving. = 他因孩子行为不端而惩罚他们。
She often chastises her employees for being late. = 她经常因为员工迟到而训斥他们。


一般过去时:
The teacher chastised the student for talking in class. = 老师因为学生在课堂上讲话而惩罚了他。
My father chastised me for breaking the window. = 我父亲因为我打破窗户而责骂我。


现在完成时:
He has chastised his son many times for lying. = 他因为儿子说谎而多次惩罚他。
She has chastised her employees for their poor performance. = 她因为员工表现不佳而训斥了他们。


过去完成时:
The manager had chastised the employee before he resigned. = 经理在他辞职前已经训斥过这位员工了。
She had chastised him several times for his mistakes, but he continued to make them. = 她因为他的错误已经训斥过他好几次了，但他还是继续犯错。


将来时:
The principal will chastise the students for their disruptive behavior. = 校长将因学生的扰乱行为而惩罚学生。
I will chastise you if you disobey my instructions. = 如果你违抗我的指示，我会惩罚你。


虚拟语气:
If I were the teacher, I would chastise the student for their laziness. = 如果我是老师，我会因为学生的懒惰而惩罚他。
I wish I had chastised him earlier for his bad habits. = 我真希望我早点因为他的坏习惯而惩罚他。


被动语态:
The student was chastised for his disruptive behavior in class. = 学生因为在课堂上的扰乱行为而受到惩罚。
The employees were chastised for their failure to meet the deadline. = 员工因为未能按时完成而受到训斥。


疑问句:
Will the teacher chastise the students for their misbehavior? = 老师会因为学生的不良行为而惩罚他们吗？
Did your parents ever chastise you when you were a child? = 你小时候父母有没有惩罚过你？


否定句:
The teacher did not chastise the student for a minor mistake. = 老师没有因为一个小错误而惩罚学生。
My parents rarely chastised me when I was growing up. = 我小时候父母很少惩罚我。
