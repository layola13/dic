orgillous:/ɔːrˈɡɪləs/| adj.| 傲慢的；自大的；趾高气扬的 | Proud, haughty, and disdainful.  The word suggests a more arrogant and contemptuous pride than simply "proud."  It implies a feeling of superiority and a disdain for others. |例句：His orgillous demeanor alienated many of his colleagues. 他傲慢的态度疏远了他的许多同事。

例句：She spoke with an orgillous air, looking down her nose at everyone else. 她趾高气扬地说话，轻蔑地看待其他人。


近义词：haughty, arrogant, supercilious, proud, disdainful, pompous

反义词：humble, modest, meek, unassuming


词性：形容词


词源：源自古法语 *orgueilleux，最终源自拉丁语 *superbus（意为“傲慢的，高傲的”）。
