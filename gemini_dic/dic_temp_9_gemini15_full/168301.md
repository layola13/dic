pro-North:/ˌproʊˈnɔːrθ/ | adj. | 亲北的，支持北方的 | Favoring or supporting the North (typically in a political or historical context, often referring to the North in the American Civil War or the division between North and South Korea). | 例句：His pro-North stance was evident in his speeches. 他的亲北立场在他的演讲中显而易见。 |近义词：pro-Northern, northern-leaning |反义词：pro-South, pro-Southern, anti-North


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pro-North. = 他是亲北的。
They are pro-North Koreans. = 他们是支持朝鲜的。


一般过去时:
The senator was pro-North during the war. = 参议员在战争期间是亲北的。
She was known for her pro-North sympathies. = 她以其对北方的同情而闻名。


现在完成时:
He has been pro-North for many years. = 他多年来一直是亲北的。
The country has remained largely pro-North despite recent events. = 尽管最近发生了事件，但该国仍基本上亲北。


过去完成时:
Before the election, he had been more pro-North than he is now. = 在选举之前，他比现在更亲北。
It had become clear that the region was predominantly pro-North. = 很明显，该地区主要是亲北的。


将来时:
He will likely remain pro-North. = 他很可能仍然是亲北的。
The future of the region will depend on whether it remains pro-North. = 该地区的未来将取决于它是否仍然亲北。


虚拟语气:
If he were pro-North, he would support their policies. = 如果他是亲北的，他会支持他们的政策。
I wish the government were more pro-North in its approach. = 我希望政府在处理方式上更亲北一些。


被动语态:
His pro-North views were criticized by many. = 他的亲北观点受到许多人的批评。
The pro-North sentiment was suppressed by the authorities. = 当局压制了亲北情绪。


疑问句:
Is he pro-North or pro-South? = 他是亲北还是亲南？
Are the majority of the people pro-North? = 多数人是亲北的吗？


否定句:
He is not pro-North; he is pro-South. = 他不是亲北的，他是亲南的。
The movement was not pro-North, but rather neutral. = 这场运动并非亲北，而是中立的。
