fret:/fret/ | v., n. | 焦急；烦躁；（使）烦恼；（吉他等的）品柱 |

英文解析:
As a verb, "fret" means to worry or be anxious. As a noun, it refers to a feeling of worry or anxiety, or a ridge on the fingerboard of a stringed instrument.

例句:
Don't fret about the exam; you'll do fine. = 不要为考试担心；你会做得很好。

He fretted over his daughter's illness. = 他为女儿的病情而焦虑不安。


近义词:
worry,  anxiety,  chafe,  irritate

反义词:
calm,  relaxed,  peaceful


词性:
Verb, Noun
