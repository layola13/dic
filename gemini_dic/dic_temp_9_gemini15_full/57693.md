drawly:/ˈdrɔːli/| adj.| 拖长的，缓慢的，冗长的 |  Describes something that is slow, prolonged, or monotonous in speech or movement.  It often implies a lack of energy or a deliberate slowing down. |例句：He spoke in a drawly voice, each word stretched out. 他说话拖得很长，每个字都拉得很长。

例句：The film's pacing was incredibly drawly, making it hard to stay engaged.  这部电影的节奏非常缓慢，让人难以集中注意力。


近义词：slow, languid, sluggish, protracted, leisurely

反义词：quick, rapid, brisk, hasty, swift


词性：形容词
