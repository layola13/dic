chronique:/krɒˈniːk/| n.|编年史；评论；纪事|A chronicle is a detailed record of historical events, often arranged in the order in which they occurred.  It can also refer to a regularly appearing newspaper or magazine article or commentary on a particular subject.|例句：The historian meticulously compiled a chronicle of the king's reign.  历史学家一丝不苟地编纂了国王统治时期的编年史。

The newspaper published a weekly chronicle of local events. 这家报纸每周发表一篇当地事件的评论。|近义词：annals, record, account, review|反义词：summary, abstract|名词
