inadvisable:/ˌɪnədˈvaɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 不明智的；不可取的 |  Not advisable; unwise.  It suggests that a course of action is not recommended because it is likely to have negative consequences. | Example:  It is inadvisable to swim in the ocean during a storm.  在暴风雨中在海里游泳是不明智的。


Example:  Going out alone at night is inadvisable in this neighborhood.  在这个街区晚上独自外出是不明智的。


近义词: unwise, imprudent, ill-advised, foolish, reckless, unwise, injudicious


反义词: advisable, wise, prudent, judicious


词性: 形容词
