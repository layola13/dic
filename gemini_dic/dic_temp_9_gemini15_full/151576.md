parted:/ˈpɑːrtɪd/| /ˈpɑːrtɪd/| adj. v.| 分开的；分手的；离别的 | Divided; separated; no longer together. |

例句：
The parted lovers exchanged tearful goodbyes. 分手的恋人依依惜别。

The curtain parted, revealing the stage. 幕布拉开，露出了舞台。


近义词：separated, divided, severed, split, disunited

反义词：united, joined, together, combined


词性：
动词 (v.):  to separate; to divide; to go separate ways

形容词 (adj.): separated; divided


词源:
源自古英语 *partan，意为“分开，划分”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He parts his hair on the side. 他把头发梳向一边。
They part ways at the crossroads. 他们在十字路口分道扬镳。

一般过去时:
We parted company after the argument. 争吵后我们分道扬鑣。
She parted her lips in surprise. 她惊讶地张开了嘴。

现在完成时:
They have parted ways several times over the years. 多年来他们已经多次分道扬鑣。
My hair has parted in the middle naturally. 我的头发自然地分开了。

过去完成时:
They had parted before the war began. 战争开始之前他们就已经分开了。
She had parted with her beloved necklace. 她已经与她心爱的项链分别了。

将来时:
We will part ways at the airport. 我们将在机场分别。
They will part the curtains to reveal the painting. 他们将拉开窗帘以展现绘画。

虚拟语气:
If they parted, they would both be unhappy. 如果他们分手了，他们都会不快乐。
I wish we hadn't parted so abruptly. 我真希望我们没有那么突然地分开。

被动语态:
The crowd was parted by the police. 人群被警察分开。
The hair is parted in the middle. 头发从中间分开。

疑问句:
Where did they part company? 他们在哪里分手的？
How is your hair parted? 你的头发怎么分开的？

否定句:
They did not part easily. 他们没有轻易分开。
She will not part with her favorite doll. 她不会放弃她最喜欢的娃娃。
