caste:/kɑːst/|ˈkæst/| n.|种姓；阶级；等级制度|A caste is a social class in a society that is strictly defined and hereditary, with little or no social mobility.  Members of a caste typically marry within their own caste and have limited interaction with members of other castes. The concept is most closely associated with the historical social structure of India, but similar systems have existed in other parts of the world.|例句：The caste system in India has been a source of social inequality for centuries. 印度种姓制度几个世纪以来一直是社会不平等的根源。|例句：  His family belonged to a high caste and enjoyed considerable privilege.  他的家族属于高种姓，享有相当大的特权。|近义词：class, stratum, rank, order|反义词：equality, egalitarianism
