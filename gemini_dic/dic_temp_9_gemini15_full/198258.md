词源:

The word "shortstop" is a compound word formed from "short" and "stop."  "Short" refers to the relatively short distance the player needs to cover compared to other infield positions.  "Stop" refers to the player's role in stopping batted balls.  The term emerged in the development of baseball in the 19th century.
