electronics: /ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪks/ | n. | 电子学，电子产品 | The branch of physics and technology concerned with the design and application of electronic devices and systems, including the use of electricity for communication, control, and processing information.  It encompasses the study of electric current flow in vacuum tubes, transistors, and integrated circuits. | Example:  She's studying electronics at university. 她正在大学学习电子学。  The store sells a wide range of electronics. 这家商店销售各种各样的电子产品。 |近义词: electronic devices, electrics |反义词:  mechanics (in contrast)
