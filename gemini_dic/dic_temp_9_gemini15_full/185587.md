reveling: /ˈrɛvəlɪŋ/ | v. | 狂欢；陶醉；沉迷 |  To take great pleasure or delight in something; to enjoy oneself fully and unrestrainedly. | 例句：They were reveling in their newfound freedom.  他们陶醉于新获得的自由。

They spent the evening reveling in the success of their project. 他们整个晚上都在为他们项目的成功而狂欢。


近义词：celebrating, enjoying, indulging, luxuriating | 反义词：mourning, grieving, suffering, abstaining | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is reveling in his success. = 他正在享受他的成功。
They are reveling in the beautiful scenery. = 他们正在陶醉于美丽的景色。


一般过去时:
She reveled in the attention she received. = 她陶醉于她收到的关注。
We reveled in the delicious food and wine. = 我们尽情享受美味的食物和美酒。


现在完成时:
They have reveled in their victory for days. = 他们已经庆祝胜利好几天了。
She has reveled in the quiet solitude of her cabin. = 她一直沉醉于她小屋的宁静与孤独。


过去完成时:
They had reveled all night before the important meeting. = 他们在重要的会议前狂欢了一整夜。
He had reveled in his accomplishments before the setback. = 在遭遇挫折之前，他一直陶醉于自己的成就。


将来时:
We will be reveling in the sun on the beach tomorrow. = 我们明天将在沙滩上享受阳光。
They will revel in their retirement after years of hard work. = 他们将在多年的辛勤工作后享受退休生活。


虚拟语气:
If I had won the lottery, I would be reveling in my wealth. = 如果我中了彩票，我就会沉醉于我的财富。
I wish I were reveling in the Caribbean sun right now. = 我真希望我现在正在加勒比海的阳光下享受。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The victory was reveled in by the whole team. = 全队都沉浸在胜利的喜悦之中。(较为生硬)
His achievements were reveled in by his family. = 他的家人为他取得的成就而感到高兴。(较为生硬)


疑问句:
Are you reveling in your new job? = 你正在享受你的新工作吗？
Were they reveling in the music last night? = 他们昨晚沉醉于音乐之中吗？


否定句:
He wasn't reveling in the harsh criticism. = 他并没有沉迷于严厉的批评。
They didn't revel in their failure. = 他们并没有沉溺于他们的失败。



PHRASE->
revel in success = 陶醉于成功
revel in the moment = 享受当下
revel in luxury = 沉迷于奢华
revel in freedom = 陶醉于自由
revel in the details = 细细品味


词源:  源自古法语 *reveillier,  意思是“唤醒”，后演变为“欢闹”，这可能与中世纪的夜间狂欢活动有关。
