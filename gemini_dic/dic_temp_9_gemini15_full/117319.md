meddest:/ˈmɛdəst/| adj. |最爱管闲事，最爱干涉的|Most interfering; most meddlesome.  This word is a superlative form and not commonly used. It emphasizes the extreme degree of meddling. |例句：She was the meddest person in the entire neighborhood, always sticking her nose into everyone's business.  她简直是整个街区最爱管闲事的人，总是把鼻子伸到每个人的事里。|近义词：interfering, meddlesome, intrusive, nosy |反义词：uninvolved, unconcerned, indifferent
