calfs:/kævz/| n.|小牛(复数)|The plural of calf, referring to young cows.  Calves are known for their playful nature and dependence on their mothers.  |例句：The farmer is taking care of his calves. 这位农民正在照顾他的小牛。

例句：Several calves were grazing peacefully in the pasture. 几头小牛正在牧场里安静地吃草。


近义词：young cows, heifers (female calves)


反义词：bulls (adult male cattle), cows (adult female cattle)


词性:名词


词源:源自古英语*calf,  与古高地德语*kalbo, 古诺尔斯语*kalfi相关，其含义均为“小牛”。  最终源头可能是原始日耳曼语*kalbaz。
