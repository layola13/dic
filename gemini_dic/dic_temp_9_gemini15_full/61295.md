embarcation:/ˌɛmbɑːrˈkeɪʃən/| n. |上船；登机；(尤指为某事而)开始，着手|The act of embarking; the process of going aboard a ship or aircraft.  It can also refer to the beginning of a journey or project, especially one that is significant or challenging.|例句：The embarkation of troops began at dawn.  = 军队从黎明开始登船。

例句：The embarkation on this new project represents a significant step for the company. = 开始这个新项目对公司来说是一个重要的步骤。


近义词：boarding, embarking, commencement, inception|反义词：disembarkation, conclusion
