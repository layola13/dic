piecework:/ˈpiswɜːrk/| n.|计件工作；分件工作|Work paid for according to the amount produced.  It is a system of payment where workers are compensated based on the number of units they complete, rather than on an hourly or salaried basis.|例句：She earns a living through piecework sewing. 她靠缝纫计件工作谋生。|例句：The factory workers are paid by piecework, so they work very hard. 工厂工人按件计酬，所以他们工作非常努力。|近义词：contract work, task work|反义词：salary, hourly wage|名词
