/ˈkælsɪk/| adj. |含钙的，钙质的 |Containing calcium, or of the nature of calcium.  The term often refers to substances containing calcium compounds, particularly calcium carbonate.  |例句：The soil is calcic, rich in calcium carbonate. 这土壤是钙质的，富含碳酸钙。 |例句：The geologist identified the rock formation as being predominantly calcic in nature. 地质学家确定该岩层主要为钙质性质。|近义词：calcareous, chalky |反义词：calcium-deficient
