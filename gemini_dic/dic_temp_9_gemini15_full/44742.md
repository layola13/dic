crumbier:/ˈkrʌmbɪər/| adj.|更碎的，更渣的，更粗糙的|More crumbly; having a more crumbly texture.|The cake was crumbier than I expected.  这个蛋糕比我想象的更碎。|The old bread was crumbier than the fresh loaf.  旧面包比新鲜面包更粗糙。|crumbly, powdery|smooth, firm


USAGE->

The bread is crumbier today than yesterday. = 今天的面包比昨天更碎。
This cookie is crumbier than that one. = 这个饼干比那个更碎。
