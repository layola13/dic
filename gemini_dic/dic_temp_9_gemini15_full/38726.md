conditionality:/ˌkɒndiʃəˈnæləti/| /ˌkɑːndiʃəˈnæləti/ | n. | 条件性；条件性规定；附条件 | The state of being conditional; dependence on a condition or conditions.  It refers to the dependence of one thing upon another, often involving an "if-then" relationship.  This concept applies across various fields, including logic, grammar, and law. | Example: The success of the project is subject to conditionality of funding.  这个项目的成功取决于资金的到位。 The contract included a conditionality clause specifying the consequences of late delivery. 合同中包含一个条件条款，规定了延迟交货的后果。|近义词： contingency, dependence, proviso | 反义词：unconditionality, certainty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The contract's validity is conditional upon timely payment. = 合同的有效性取决于及时付款。
His happiness is conditional on his success. = 他的幸福取决于他的成功。


一般过去时:
The offer was conditional on the buyer's inspection. = 这份报价以买方的检查为条件。
Her acceptance was conditional upon receiving a scholarship. = 她的录取以获得奖学金为条件。


现在完成时:
The agreement has been conditional upon certain legal requirements. = 这项协议一直以来都以某些法律要求为条件。
The success has been conditional upon hard work and dedication. = 成功一直以来都以努力和奉献为条件。


过去完成时:
The deal had been conditional upon the market's approval. = 这笔交易此前一直以市场的认可为条件。
Their marriage had been conditional upon parental consent. = 他们的婚姻此前一直以父母的同意为条件。


将来时:
The grant will be conditional upon satisfactory progress reports. = 拨款将以令人满意的进度报告为条件。
The promotion will be conditional upon completing the training program. = 升职将以完成培训项目为条件。


虚拟语气:
If the weather were better, we would go on a picnic. = 如果天气好，我们就去野餐。(这里conditionality体现在从句对主句的影响)
If I had the money, I would buy a house. = 如果我有钱，我会买房子。(这里conditionality体现在从句对主句的影响)


被动语态:
The loan application was made conditional on a credit check. = 贷款申请以信用检查为条件。
The release of the prisoner was made conditional upon good behaviour. = 囚犯的释放以良好行为为条件。


疑问句:
Is the offer conditional on anything else? = 这份报价还有什么其他条件吗？
Are the terms of the contract conditional? = 合同条款是有条件的吗？


否定句:
The guarantee is unconditional. = 保证是无条件的。
The warranty is not conditional on proper maintenance. = 保修不以正确的维护为条件。
