furness:/ˈfɜːrnɪs/| n. |熔炉；炉膛；铁工厂；（英格兰）弗内斯|A furnace; a place where iron is smelted.  The word often refers to Furness Abbey in Cumbria, England, or the Furness Peninsula.|The blacksmith heated the iron in the furness.  铁匠在熔炉里加热铁。|The ancient furness still stands as a testament to the region's industrial past.  古老的熔炉仍然屹立着，证明了该地区工业的过去。|furnace, smelter| (反义词较少，因为其本身并非描述状态的词)|名词
