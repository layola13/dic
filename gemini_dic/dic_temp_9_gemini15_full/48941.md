defund: /ˌdiːˈfʌnd/ | vt. | 停止拨款；取消资助 | To withdraw funding from; to stop providing financial support for.  |例句：The city council voted to defund the police department. 市议会投票决定停止向警察部门拨款。 |例句：The organization was defunded after allegations of corruption.  该组织在被指控腐败后失去了资助。|近义词：disband, cut funding for, divest |反义词：fund, finance, support | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They defund the project every year. = 他们每年都取消该项目的拨款。
The government defunds many social programs. = 政府取消了许多社会项目的资助。


一般过去时:
The school defunded its music program last year. = 学校去年取消了它的音乐项目。
They defunded the research project after the initial findings. = 在初步调查结果出来后，他们取消了该研究项目的资助。


现在完成时:
They have defunded the arts program for several years. = 他们几年来一直取消艺术项目的拨款。
The organization has been defunded since the scandal. = 自丑闻发生以来，该组织一直没有获得资助。


过去完成时:
They had defunded the program before the controversy even started. = 在争议开始之前，他们就已经取消了该项目的拨款。
The city had defunded the library several times in the past decade. = 在过去的十年里，市政府已经多次取消图书馆的拨款。


将来时:
They will defund the project if the results are unsatisfactory. = 如果结果不令人满意，他们将取消该项目的拨款。
The government will likely defund the program next year. = 政府很可能明年将取消该项目的资助。


虚拟语气:
If they defunded the hospital, many people would suffer. = 如果他们取消医院的拨款，许多人都会受苦。
I wish they hadn't defunded the education initiatives. = 我真希望他们没有取消教育计划的资助。


被动语态:
The program was defunded due to budget cuts. = 该项目由于预算削减而被取消了资助。
The project is being defunded because of lack of progress. = 该项目由于进展缓慢而正在被取消资助。


疑问句:
Will they defund the program next year? = 他们明年会取消该项目的资助吗？
Have they defunded the initiative already? = 他们已经取消该倡议的资助了吗？


否定句:
They did not defund the project despite the criticism. = 尽管受到批评，他们并没有取消该项目的资助。
The government has not yet defunded the controversial program. = 政府尚未取消该有争议项目的资助。
