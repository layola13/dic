interflowing: /ˌɪntərˈfloʊɪŋ/ | adj. | 交错流动的；互相融合的 |  Describes something that flows or merges together in an interwoven or interconnected manner.  It implies a continuous and reciprocal movement or interaction between different elements. |


例句：
The interflowing currents of the river created a mesmerizing pattern. = 河流交错的洋流创造了一种令人着迷的图案。

The interflowing ideas from different cultures enriched the philosophical discussion. = 来自不同文化的思想交融丰富了哲学讨论。


近义词：intermingling, intertwining, merging, blending


反义词：separate, distinct, unconnected, isolated


词性：形容词
