outreproducing:/ˌaʊtprəˈduːsɪŋ/| v.|超过产量；产量超过|The act of producing more than something else; surpassing in production.  |例句：This new factory is outreproducing the old one by a significant margin. 这个新工厂的产量大大超过旧工厂。

例句：Our team outreproduced all other teams in the competition. 我们的团队在比赛中产量超过了所有其他团队。


近义词：surpassing, exceeding, outperforming, outstripping

反义词：underproducing, lagging behind

词性：动词(现在分词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory is outreproducing its competitors. = 这个工厂的产量超过了竞争对手。
They are outreproducing their previous best. = 他们的产量超过了之前的最高纪录。

一般过去时:
Last year, the farm outreproduced its quota. = 去年，这个农场超额完成了产量指标。
The company outreproduced its projections for the quarter. = 公司超额完成了本季度的预期产量。

现在完成时:
The new technology has outreproduced all expectations. = 新技术已经超出了所有预期。
They have outreproduced their rivals consistently. = 他们一直都比竞争对手产量高。

过去完成时:
By the end of the year, the mine had outreproduced its initial estimates. = 到年底，矿山的产量已经超过了最初的估计。
Before the improvements, the factory had never outreproduced its target. = 在改进之前，工厂从未超过其目标产量。

将来时:
We expect the new process will outreproduce the old one. = 我们预计新工艺将超过旧工艺的产量。
They believe the investment will allow them to outreproduce the market. = 他们相信这项投资将使他们能够超过市场产量。

虚拟语气:
If we had better equipment, we would be outreproducing our competitors. = 如果我们有更好的设备，我们的产量就会超过竞争对手。
I wish we were outreproducing our rivals in the market share. = 我希望我们在市场份额上能够超过竞争对手。

被动语态:
The old method was outreproduced by the new one. = 旧方法的产量被新方法超过了。
The previous record was outreproduced by a significant margin. = 之前的纪录被大幅超过了。

疑问句:
Is this machine outreproducing the others? = 这台机器的产量超过其他机器吗？
Are we outreproducing our targets this month? = 我们这个月的产量超过目标了吗？

否定句:
The small farm is not outreproducing the larger ones. = 小型农场没有超过大型农场的产量。
They were not outreproducing their competitors last year. = 他们去年没有超过竞争对手的产量。
