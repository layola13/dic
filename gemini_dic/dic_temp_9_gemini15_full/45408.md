cupolaed:/ˈkjuːpələd/ | adj. | 有圆顶的；带穹顶的 | Having a cupola or cupolas.  The word describes a structure or building featuring a cupola, a small dome-like structure on a roof. |例句：The cupolaed church stood majestically against the skyline.  这座圆顶教堂雄伟地矗立在城市天际线上。


The beautifully cupolaed building housed a collection of rare artifacts. 这座美丽的圆顶建筑收藏着许多珍贵的文物。

近义词：domed,  turreted (if the cupola resembles a turret)

反义词：flat-roofed


词性:形容词
