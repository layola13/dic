litigating:/ˈlɪtɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 诉讼；打官司 | Engaging in a lawsuit or legal proceedings.  | Example: The company is currently litigating against its former partner.  这家公司目前正在起诉其前任合作伙伴。 |近义词：suing, prosecuting, contesting | 反义词：settling, compromising


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are litigating the case in the Supreme Court. = 他们在最高法院打这场官司。
We are litigating a dispute over property rights. = 我们正在就财产权纠纷进行诉讼。

一般过去时:
The parties litigated the matter for several years. = 双方就此事进行了多年的诉讼。
She litigated her case successfully. = 她成功地打赢了官司。

现在完成时:
They have litigated this issue since 2020. = 自2020年以来，他们一直在就这个问题打官司。
He has litigated numerous cases throughout his career. = 他在职业生涯中打过无数场官司。

过去完成时:
Before the trial, they had litigated the matter in lower courts. = 在审判之前，他们已经在下级法院对该事项进行了诉讼。
The company had litigated similar cases before. = 这家公司以前也打过类似的官司。

将来时:
We will litigate this matter if necessary. = 必要时，我们将对这件事提起诉讼。
They will be litigating the case next month. = 他们下个月将对该案进行诉讼。

虚拟语气:
If we had more evidence, we would litigate this case aggressively. = 如果我们有更多证据，我们会积极地打这场官司。
I wish we hadn't litigated this matter so publicly. = 我真希望我们没有这么公开地打这场官司。

被动语态:
The case is being litigated in the county court. = 此案正在县法院审理。
The matter was litigated extensively. = 该问题已得到广泛审理。

疑问句:
Are they still litigating the divorce? = 他们还在打离婚官司吗？
Is she considering litigating the matter? = 她是否考虑对该事项提起诉讼？

否定句:
They are not litigating the case at this time. = 他们目前没有打这场官司。
We chose not to litigate the dispute. = 我们选择不打这场官司。


PHRASE->
litigating a case = 打官司
litigating a dispute = 诉讼纠纷
litigating the matter = 对该事项提起诉讼
litigating for years = 多年诉讼
litigating costs = 诉讼成本
