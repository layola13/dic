disseminate:/dɪˈsɛmɪneɪt/ | vt. |传播；散布；普及|To spread or disperse something, especially information or ideas.|The government disseminated propaganda to influence public opinion. 政府散布宣传以影响公众舆论。|近义词：spread, distribute, propagate, broadcast |反义词：conceal, suppress, withhold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The university disseminates research findings to the wider community. = 大学将研究成果传播给更广泛的社区。
They disseminate information through social media. = 他们通过社交媒体传播信息。


一般过去时:
The news was disseminated rapidly through the internet. = 新闻通过互联网迅速传播。
The company disseminated a press release to announce the merger. = 公司发布新闻稿宣布合并。


现在完成时:
The organization has disseminated countless educational resources online. = 该组织已在线传播了无数的教育资源。
Scientists have disseminated their research findings through various publications. = 科学家们通过各种出版物传播了他们的研究成果。


过去完成时:
Before the conference, the organizers had disseminated the schedule to all attendees. = 在会议之前，组织者已将日程安排分发给所有与会者。
The rumor had been disseminated widely before anyone could deny it. = 在任何人能够否认之前，谣言就已经广泛传播了。


将来时:
The company will disseminate the new product information next week. = 公司将于下周传播新产品信息。
The government will disseminate a public health warning about the virus. = 政府将发布关于病毒的公共卫生警告。


虚拟语气:
If they disseminated the information more effectively, more people would be aware of the issue. = 如果他们更有效地传播信息，更多的人就会意识到这个问题。
It is crucial that the information be disseminated widely and quickly. = 至关重要的是，信息必须广泛而迅速地传播。


被动语态:
The information was disseminated through various channels. = 信息通过各种渠道传播。
The new policy is being disseminated throughout the organization. = 新政策正在整个组织中传播。


疑问句:
How will the information be disseminated to the public? = 信息将如何传播给公众？
What methods are used to disseminate the company's message? = 使用什么方法来传播公司的信息？


否定句:
The company did not disseminate the negative news to the media. = 公司没有向媒体传播负面新闻。
They failed to disseminate the warning effectively. = 他们未能有效地传播警告。
