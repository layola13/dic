noodle:/ˈnuːdəl/| n.|面条，方便面|A noodle is a strip of dough, typically made from wheat flour, that is cooked in boiling water.  Noodles are a staple food in many cultures and come in a wide variety of shapes, sizes, and thicknesses.|例句：I had a bowl of noodles for lunch. 我午餐吃了一碗面条。

例句：We bought a pack of instant noodles for our camping trip. 我们为野营买了一包方便面。

近义词：pasta (in some contexts)

反义词：无
