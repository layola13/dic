polygraphy:/pɒˈlɪɡrəfi/| n. |测谎术；测谎仪记录|The scientific study and application of polygraph techniques to detect deception.  Polygraphy involves analyzing physiological responses, such as heart rate, blood pressure, and respiration, to determine truthfulness.|例句：The suspect underwent polygraphy to prove his innocence. 嫌疑人接受了测谎测试以证明自己的清白。|近义词：lie detection, deception detection|反义词：truth verification (indirectly)|名词
