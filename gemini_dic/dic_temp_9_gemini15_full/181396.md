reisolated: /ˌriːˈaɪsəleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 再隔离的，重新隔离的 |  Having been isolated again; subjected to isolation a second or subsequent time. This word is formed by combining "re-" (again) with "isolated" (separated).  It implies a return to a state of isolation after a period of non-isolation.  | Example:  After a brief period of freedom, the patient was reisolated due to a resurgence of symptoms.  =  在短暂的自由之后，由于症状再次出现，病人被再次隔离。 | 近义词：reconfined, sequestered again | 反义词：integrated, released
