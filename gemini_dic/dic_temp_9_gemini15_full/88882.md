hoker:/ˈhoʊkər/ | n. |小贩，流动摊贩 | A hawker is a person who sells goods, often food, from a stall or cart, typically in a public place.  |Example: The street was bustling with hokers selling everything from fresh fruit to spicy noodles. 街上熙熙攘攘，小贩们卖着从新鲜水果到辛辣面条的一切东西。 |近义词：vendor, peddler, street vendor |反义词：customer, buyer |名词
