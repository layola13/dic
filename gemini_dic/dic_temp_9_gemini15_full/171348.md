publicist: /ˈpʌblɪsɪst/ | n. |新闻发言人；宣传员；广告策划人 | A publicist is a person whose job is to create and maintain a positive public image for a person, company, or organization.  They often work in public relations and use various methods to promote their client's image, such as press releases, media appearances, and social media campaigns. | The movie star hired a publicist to handle her image during the scandal.  这位电影明星雇了一位新闻发言人来处理她在丑闻期间的形象。 | spokesperson, press agent, public relations officer |  (No clear antonym, but the opposite action would be damaging a public image.) | 名词
