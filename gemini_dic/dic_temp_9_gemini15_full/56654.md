do not: /duː nɒt/ | v. | 不要，别 |  "Do not" is a contraction of "do not," used as a negative imperative verb.  It commands someone not to do something.  | Example: Do not touch that! = 不要碰那个！  Do not forget to call me. = 不要忘记给我打电话。|近义词：don't, mustn't|反义词：do, must


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Do not eat that. = 不要吃那个。
Do not worry about it. = 不要为此担心。


一般过去时:
I did not see him. = 我没有看见他。
They did not come to the party. = 他们没有来参加聚会。


现在完成时:
I have not finished my work. = 我还没有完成我的工作。
She has not been to Paris. = 她还没有去过巴黎。


过去完成时:
I had not eaten anything before the meeting. = 我在会议前什么都没吃。
They had not seen each other for years. = 他们多年没见过面了。


将来时:
I will not go to the cinema tonight. = 我今晚不会去看电影。
We will not be late for the meeting. = 我们不会迟到会议。


虚拟语气:
If I did not know you, I wouldn't help you. = 如果我不认识你，我就不会帮助你。
I wish I did not have so much work. = 我希望我没有这么多工作。


被动语态:
The work was not finished on time. = 工作没有按时完成。
The letter was not sent. = 信没有寄出。


疑问句:
Do you not understand? = 你不明白吗？
Did they not tell you? = 他们没有告诉你吗？


否定句:
I do not like this movie. = 我不喜欢这部电影。
He does not play the piano. = 他不弹钢琴。


PHRASE->
Do not hesitate to = 请尽管...

Do not mind = 不介意
