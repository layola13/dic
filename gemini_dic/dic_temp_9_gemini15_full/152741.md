peatland:/ˈpiːtlænd/| n. |泥炭地；沼泽地 |An area of land that is saturated with water, resulting in the accumulation of partially decayed plant matter known as peat.  Peatlands are characterized by acidic and anaerobic conditions, supporting unique flora and fauna. |例句：The peatland ecosystem is incredibly important for carbon sequestration.  泥炭地生态系统对于碳封存极其重要。
例句：The hikers carefully navigated the treacherous terrain of the peatland.  徒步旅行者小心地穿越了泥炭地险峻的地形。|近义词：bog, mire, marsh, fen |反义词：dry land, upland
