presupposing:/ˌpriːsəˈpoʊzɪŋ/ | v. | 预想；假定；以…为前提 | Assuming something beforehand; implying or suggesting something as a necessary condition or consequence.  |例句：Presupposing his guilt, they proceeded to question him.  以他为有罪为前提，他们开始审问他。
例句：We're presupposing a positive outcome, which may not be realistic. 我们预想会有积极的结果，但这可能并不现实。|近义词：assuming, supposing, postulating |反义词：ignoring, disregarding, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is presupposing a favorable response. = 他预想会有积极的回应。
She presupposes that everyone understands. = 她假定每个人都理解。


一般过去时:
They presupposed the success of the project. = 他们预想项目会成功。
He presupposed her knowledge of the facts. = 他预想她了解事实。


现在完成时:
We have presupposed too much in this matter. = 我们在这件事上预想太多了。
She has presupposed his cooperation. = 她一直预想他会合作。


过去完成时:
They had presupposed a smooth transition. = 他们预想会有一个平稳的过渡。
He had presupposed the availability of funds. = 他预想资金是可用的。


将来时:
We will presuppose the availability of resources. = 我们将预想资源可用。
She will presuppose his agreement. = 她将预想他会同意。


虚拟语气:
If we were presupposing a different scenario, the outcome would be different. = 如果我们预想的是不同的情况，结果就会不同。
I wish we hadn't presupposed his honesty. = 我真希望我们没有预想他诚实。


被动语态:
The success of the plan was presupposed by the management. = 管理层预想计划会成功。
His innocence was presupposed until proven otherwise. = 在证明他有罪之前，预想他是无罪的。


疑问句:
Are we presupposing too much? = 我们预想得太多吗？
Is she presupposing his help? = 她预想他会帮忙吗？


否定句:
We are not presupposing any specific outcome. = 我们并没有预想任何特定的结果。
She did not presuppose any difficulties. = 她没有预想任何困难。


PHRASE->
presupposing that = 假设...
presupposing the existence of = 假设...存在
presupposing a certain level of = 预想一定程度的...
presupposing this to be true = 假设这是真的
presupposing the outcome = 预想结果
