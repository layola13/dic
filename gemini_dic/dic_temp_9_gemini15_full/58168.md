droves:/druːvz/| n.|大群；众多；成群结队|A large number of people or animals moving together.  It often implies a sense of movement and perhaps a lack of organization.|例句：Droves of tourists flocked to the beach.  成群结队的游客涌向海滩。

例句：Droves of cattle were driven across the plains.  成群的牛被赶过平原。


近义词：crowds, herds, flocks, masses, throngs

反义词：few, handful, scarcity

词性:名词


USAGE->

Large droves of sheep graze in the pasture. = 大群的羊在牧场上吃草。
Many droves of tourists visit the city every year. = 每年都有许多游客成群结队地访问这座城市。
