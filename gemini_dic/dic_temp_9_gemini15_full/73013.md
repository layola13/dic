footboard:/ˈfʊtˌbɔːrd/| n. |踏板；床头板|A footboard is a horizontal board at the foot of a bed, often forming part of the bed frame. It can also refer to a platform for the feet, such as on a vehicle or machine.|例句：The old footboard was worn and scratched.  旧踏板磨损且有划痕。

The footboard of the train was surprisingly comfortable. 火车的踏板令人惊讶地舒适。|近义词： footrest, step, platform |反义词: headboard
