dissing: /ˈdɪsɪŋ/ | v. | 贬低；轻视；批评；诋毁 | To disrespect or criticize someone or something.  |例句：He was dissing his opponent throughout the debate. 他在整个辩论中都在贬低他的对手。


例句：They were dissing the new movie on social media.  他们在社交媒体上批评这部新电影。


近义词：belittling, putting down, criticizing, slamming, badmouthing | 反义词：praising, complimenting, flattering


USAGE->
