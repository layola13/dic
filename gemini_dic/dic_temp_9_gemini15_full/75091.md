frilliness:/ˈfrɪlinəs/| n. |繁琐；华丽；装饰性|The excessive ornamentation and elaborate detail, often suggesting a lack of simplicity or practicality.  It can refer to both physical objects and abstract concepts, suggesting an unnecessary level of detail or complexity.|例句：The frilliness of the dress was overwhelming.  这件衣服的繁琐装饰令人难以招架。

The frilliness of the legal arguments obscured the central issue.  冗长的法律论证模糊了核心问题。|近义词：fussiness, ornateness, elaborateness, embellishment|反义词：simplicity, plainness, austerity
