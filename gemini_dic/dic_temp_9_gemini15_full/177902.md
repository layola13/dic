recharge:/ˌriˈtʃɑːrdʒ/ | vt. n. | 充电；重新充满活力；补充；再收费 | To charge (a battery, etc.) again; to restore energy to; to replenish; to charge again (a fee).|例句：I need to recharge my phone. 我需要给我的手机充电。
例句：After a long day, I like to recharge with a relaxing bath. 长期工作一天后，我喜欢泡个热水澡来恢复精力。


近义词：replenish, refill, revitalize | 反义词：discharge, deplete
