reapportion:/ˌriːəˈpɔːrʃən/ | vt. |重新分配，重新划分 |To divide or distribute something again, especially in a different way.  This often refers to reassigning representation in a legislative body based on new population figures.|例句：The state legislature reapportioned the congressional districts after the census.  州议会根据人口普查结果重新划分了国会选区。

例句：The company decided to reapportion its resources to focus on new product development. 公司决定重新分配资源，专注于新产品开发。


近义词：redistribute, reallocate, reallot | 反义词：concentrate, centralize | 词性：动词
