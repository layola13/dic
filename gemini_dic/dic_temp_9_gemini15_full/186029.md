rewinds:/ˌriːˈwaɪndz/| v.|倒带，倒退，使倒退 |To reverse the movement or action of something, especially a tape or film; to go back to a previous state or time. |例句：Please rewind the tape to the beginning. 请把磁带倒回开头。

例句：The film rewinds to show the scene again. 电影倒带重播那个场景。


近义词：reverse, replay, backtrack |反义词：fast-forward, advance

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rewinds the tape every time he finishes watching it. = 他每次看完录像带都会倒带。
The machine rewinds automatically. = 机器自动倒带。


一般过去时:
She rewinded the cassette and listened to the song again. = 她倒带并再次听了那首歌。
I rewinded the video to check the details. = 我倒带回去查看细节。


现在完成时:
He has rewinded the video several times. = 他已经倒带好几次了。
We have rewinded the conversation in our minds many times. = 我们在脑海中多次重温了这段对话。


过去完成时:
She had rewinded the tape before she realized she missed something. = 她意识到自己错过了一些东西后才倒带。
They had rewinded their plans several times before finalizing them. = 他们在最终确定计划之前已经多次修改了计划。


将来时:
I will rewind the movie and watch that part again. = 我将倒带电影并再次观看那部分。
He will rewind the clock and try again. = 他将倒回时间并再次尝试。


虚拟语气:
If I had a rewind button in life, I would use it wisely. = 如果我生活中有个倒退键，我会明智地使用它。
I wish I could rewind time and do things differently. = 我希望我能倒回时间，做些不一样的事情。


被动语态:
The tape was rewinded by the machine. = 磁带被机器倒带了。

疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't rewind the tape. = 他没有倒带。
She couldn't rewind the video player. = 她无法倒带录像机。
