shied:/ʃiːld/| v.| 躲避；保护；遮蔽 | to avoid something unpleasant or difficult; to protect someone or something from something harmful; to cover or screen something |例句：She shied away from the difficult questions. 她回避了那些难题。  He shielded his eyes from the bright sun. 他用手遮挡刺眼的阳光。|近义词：avoid, protect, shield, screen |反义词：confront, face, expose


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shies away from conflict. = 他回避冲突。
They shield their children from harm. = 他们保护孩子免受伤害。

一般过去时:
She shied away from the challenge. = 她回避了这个挑战。
He shielded her from the rain. = 他为她挡雨。

现在完成时:
He has shied away from responsibility all his life. = 他一辈子都逃避责任。
They have shielded their investments from market volatility. = 他们保护他们的投资免受市场波动影响。

过去完成时:
She had shied away from commitment in the past. = 她过去一直回避承诺。
They had shielded the witness from the press. = 他们此前一直保护证人免受媒体骚扰。

将来时:
He will shy away from public speaking. = 他将回避公开演讲。
They will shield the project from outside interference. = 他们将保护项目免受外部干扰。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would shy away from that deal. = 如果我是你，我会回避那笔交易。
I wish I had shielded myself from that experience. = 我真希望我能避免那次经历。

被动语态:
The witness was shielded from the public. = 证人被保护起来，不让公众接触。
The fragile painting was shielded from the sunlight. = 易碎的画被遮挡起来，避免阳光直射。

疑问句:
Does he shy away from difficult tasks? = 他回避困难的任务吗？
Are they shielding the truth from us? = 他们是否在隐瞒真相？

否定句:
He did not shy away from the fight. = 他没有回避战斗。
They did not shield the children from the truth. = 他们没有向孩子们隐瞒真相。
