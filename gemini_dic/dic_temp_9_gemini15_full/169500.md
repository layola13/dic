propagatress:/ˈprɒpəɡeɪtrəs/| n. |传播者（尤指女性）, 传播媒介|A propagatress is a woman who spreads or promotes something, especially an idea or belief.  It's a less common word, often used in a somewhat formal or even archaic context.  The word emphasizes the female gender.  |Example:  She was a tireless propagatress of feminist ideals. 她是一位不知疲倦地传播女权主义理想的传播者。

The zealous propagatress of the new religion converted many followers.  那位新宗教的狂热传播者赢得了许多信徒。|近义词：propagandist, disseminator, promoter, advocate (for female)|反义词：suppressor, opponent, detractor|名词
