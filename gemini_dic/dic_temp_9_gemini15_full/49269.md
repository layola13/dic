deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbərət/| adj., v.|深思熟虑的；故意的；从容的；讨论|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "deliberate" means done consciously and intentionally. It can also describe something done slowly and carefully, or a discussion or meeting held for a specific purpose.  As a verb, it means to think about or discuss something carefully.


例句:
The judge made a deliberate decision. = 法官做出了深思熟虑的决定。

He deliberately ignored my calls. = 他故意不接我的电话。


近义词:
intentional, purposeful, premeditated, considered, thoughtful, slow, careful


反义词:
accidental, unintentional, careless, hasty, rash, impulsive


词性:
adjective, verb
