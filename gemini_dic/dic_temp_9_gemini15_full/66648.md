exists:/ɪɡˈzɪsts/| v.|存在；生存|To have actual being; to be real; to be found in reality.|The problem still exists. = 问题仍然存在。  A large number of problems exist in our society. = 在我们的社会中存在许多问题。|近义词：persist, subsist, endure|反义词：cease, disappear, vanish

USAGE->
一般现在时:
This problem exists in many countries. = 这个问题存在于许多国家。
Does this belief still exist in modern society? = 这种信仰在现代社会中还存在吗？


一般过去时:
The dinosaur species existed millions of years ago. = 恐龙物种存在于数百万年前。
That theory no longer existed after the new research was published. = 新研究发表后，那个理论就不再存在了。


现在完成时:
The company has existed for over 50 years. = 这家公司已经存在了50多年。
This tradition has existed in our family for generations. = 这个传统在我们家族世代相传。


过去完成时:
Before the invention of the wheel, different forms of transportation existed. = 在发明轮子之前，存在着不同形式的交通工具。
It had existed for centuries before anyone discovered its true purpose. = 在有人发现其真正用途之前，它已经存在了几个世纪。


将来时:
This technology will likely still exist in the next decade. = 这项技术很可能在未来十年内仍然存在。
Do you think this custom will exist in the future? = 你认为这种习俗将来还会存在吗？


虚拟语气:
If this species didn't exist, the ecosystem would be significantly impacted. = 如果这个物种不存在，生态系统将受到严重影响。
I wish that discrimination didn't exist in the world. = 我希望世界上不存在歧视。


被动语态:
The problem is known to exist in various forms. = 已知该问题以各种形式存在。
It is believed that life existed on Mars billions of years ago. = 人们相信数十亿年前火星上存在生命。


疑问句:
Does life exist on other planets? = 其他星球上存在生命吗？
Does the solution to this problem still exist? = 这个问题的解决方法还存在吗？


否定句:
The rumor is not true; it does not exist. = 谣言不属实；它不存在。
Such a thing simply did not exist in those days. = 在那些日子里，根本不存在这样的事情。



PHRASE->
exist in = 存在于…中
exist on = 以…为食；靠…生存
exist as = 作为…而存在
exist for = 为了…而存在
exist by = 靠…生存
