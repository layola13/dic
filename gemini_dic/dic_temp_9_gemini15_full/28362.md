caravans:/ˈkærəvænz/| n.|大篷车，商队，房车|A caravan is a group of people, typically traders or travelers, journeying together, or a vehicle designed for living in, typically towed by a car.|例句：The caravan of camels crossed the desert.  骆驼商队穿过沙漠。|例句：We rented a caravan for our family holiday. 我们租了一辆房车作为家庭度假用车。|近义词：caravan, convoy, motorhome, camper van|反义词：stationary, single traveler
