probab: /ˈprɒbəb(ə)l/ | adj. | 可能的，大概的 |  likely, probable, possible;  It is probable that he will succeed. 他很可能会成功。| likely, possible | impossible, unlikely | 形容词


英文解析：Probable means something is likely to happen or be true, based on evidence or logic. It suggests a higher degree of likelihood than "possible," but less certainty than "certain."


例句：

The probable cause of the accident was driver error. 事故的可能原因是驾驶员失误。

It is probable that it will rain tomorrow. 明天可能下雨。


近义词：likely, possible, plausible

反义词：impossible, unlikely, improbable


词性：形容词
