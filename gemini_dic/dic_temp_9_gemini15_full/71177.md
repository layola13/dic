fissure:/ˈfɪʃər/| n.|裂缝；裂隙；分歧；隔阂 |A fissure is a narrow opening or crack, especially one in a rock face or in the earth.  It can also refer to a division or disagreement between people or groups. |例句：The earthquake caused a large fissure in the ground. 地震在地面上造成了一条大裂缝。

The fissure between the two political parties deepened. 两大政党之间的分歧加深了。|近义词：crack, cleft, rift, split, chasm |反义词：closure, unity, agreement
