nonimperious:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpɪəriəs/ | adj. | 不专横的；不傲慢的；谦逊的 | Not domineering or arrogant; humble.  The nonimperious manager fostered a collaborative work environment.  | 近义词：humble, modest, unassuming, meek | 反义词：imperious, arrogant, domineering, haughty


例句：

He possessed a nonimperious manner that put people at ease. 他举止谦逊，让人感到轻松自在。

Her nonimperious leadership style encouraged open communication and teamwork. 她不专横的领导风格鼓励了开放的沟通和团队合作。
