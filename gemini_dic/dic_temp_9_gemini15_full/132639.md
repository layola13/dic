nondiscordant: /ˌnɒnˈdɪskɔːrdənt/| adj. |不协调的，一致的 | Not discordant; harmonious; concordant.  The term often appears in genetics and related fields, referring to a lack of disagreement or inconsistency between observed data and expected results. | Example: The nondiscordant results from the twin study supported the hypothesis.  这项双胞胎研究的不协调结果支持了该假设。|近义词：concordant, harmonious, consistent |反义词：discordant, inconsistent, conflicting
