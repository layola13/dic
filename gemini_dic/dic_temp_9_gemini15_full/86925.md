hemorrhage:/ˈhɛməˌrɪdʒ/| n. v.| 大出血；内出血 | The loss of a large amount of blood from a ruptured blood vessel.  |例句：The accident victim suffered a massive hemorrhage.  事故受害者遭受了大出血。

The patient experienced a hemorrhage in the brain. 病人脑部出血。

近义词：bleeding, effusion |反义词：hemostasis


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hemorrhages frequently. = 他经常出血。
The wound hemorrhages profusely. = 伤口大量出血。

一般过去时:
She hemorrhaged after the surgery. = 她手术后出血了。
The patient hemorrhaged internally. = 病人内出血了。

现在完成时:
He has hemorrhaged several times this year. = 他今年已经出血好几次了。
The wound has stopped hemorrhaging. = 伤口已经停止出血了。

过去完成时:
He had hemorrhaged before the paramedics arrived. = 救护人员到达之前他已经出血了。
She had hemorrhaged so much blood that she fainted. = 她失血过多而昏过去了。

将来时:
The doctor fears he will hemorrhage again. = 医生担心他会再次出血。
If the pressure isn't controlled, she will hemorrhage. = 如果压力控制不住，她就会出血。

虚拟语气:
If he had received timely treatment, he might not have hemorrhaged. = 如果他及时接受治疗，可能就不会出血了。
I wish I hadn't hemorrhaged so much blood. = 我真希望我没有失血这么多。

被动语态:
The patient was hemorrhaging profusely when we arrived. = 我们到达时，病人正在大量出血。
The wound was treated to stop the hemorrhaging. = 伤口经过处理以止血。


疑问句:
Is he still hemorrhaging? = 他还在出血吗？
Was she hemorrhaging internally? = 她内出血了吗？


否定句:
He didn't hemorrhage during the operation. = 他手术期间没有出血。
The patient is not currently hemorrhaging. = 病人目前没有出血。


PHRASE->

词源:
From Medieval Latin *haemorrhagia, from Greek haimorrhagia, from haima "blood" + rhēgnumi "to burst forth".
