couchette:/kuˈʃɛt/| n. |卧铺（火车上的）|A couchette is a type of sleeping accommodation on a train, typically consisting of narrow bunks arranged in tiers.  It's usually less expensive than a sleeper cabin but offers more privacy than simply sitting up. |The overnight train to Paris had couchettes available for budget travelers. 这趟开往巴黎的夜班火车为经济型旅客提供卧铺。|sleeper berth, bunk|private cabin|n.
