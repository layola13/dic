saddens:/ˈsædnz/| v.|使悲伤；使忧愁|To make someone feel sad.  The news saddens me greatly.|近义词：depresses, grieves, disheartens|反义词：cheers, gladdens, delights|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bad news saddens her. = 这个坏消息让她很难过。
His behavior saddens his parents. = 他的行为让他的父母难过。

一般过去时:
The loss of his job saddened him deeply. = 失去工作让他非常难过。
The rain saddened the picnic. =  雨水让野餐扫兴了。

现在完成时:
This situation has saddened me for weeks. = 这种情况已经让我难过几周了。
The news has saddened many people. = 这个消息让很多人难过。

过去完成时:
I had already been saddened by the earlier news. = 我已经被之前的消息弄得很伤心了。
She had been saddened by his departure. = 她为他离开而感到难过。

将来时:
The news will sadden many people. = 这个消息将会让很多人难过。
I'm afraid this news will sadden you. = 我担心这个消息会让你难过。

虚拟语气:
It would sadden me to see you unhappy. = 看到你不开心我会很难过。
I wish it didn't sadden you so much. = 我希望它不会让你这么难过。

被动语态:
He was saddened by the news of the accident. = 他对事故的消息感到难过。
Many were saddened by the loss of the old building. = 许多人对那座旧建筑的消失感到难过。

疑问句:
Does this news sadden you? = 这个消息让你难过吗？
Does it sadden you that he is leaving? = 他要离开了，这让你难过吗？

否定句:
The good news did not sadden her. = 好消息并没有让她难过。
The positive outcome didn't sadden him at all. = 积极的结果一点也没有让他难过。


PHRASE->
