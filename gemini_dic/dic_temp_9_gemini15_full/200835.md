slights:/slaɪts/ | n. v. | 轻蔑；怠慢；忽视；轻微的冒犯；贬低 | A slight is a small insult or act of disrespect.  It can also refer to a minor offense or injury. As a verb, it means to treat someone or something with disrespect or disregard. |例句：He felt a slight when he wasn't invited to the party. 他因为没有被邀请参加聚会而感到受轻蔑。  She slighted her colleagues by ignoring their suggestions. 她无视同事们的建议，怠慢了他们。|近义词：insult, offense, disregard, snub, disrespect |反义词：respect, honor, courtesy, compliment
