gravity:/ˈɡrævəti/ | n. | 重力，地心引力；严重性，庄严；严肃 | The force that attracts a body towards the center of the earth, or towards any other physical body having mass.  It is also the seriousness or importance of a situation. | 例句：The moon's gravity affects the tides. 月球的引力影响着潮汐。

The gravity of the situation was not lost on him. 他意识到局势的严重性。| 近义词：gravitation, seriousness, importance | 反义词：lightness, triviality
