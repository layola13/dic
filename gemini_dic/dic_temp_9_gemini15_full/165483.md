prelocate:/ˌpriːˈloʊkeɪt/|/ˈpriːləʊkeɪt/| vt.|预先配置；预先安置|To move or place something in a location before it is needed.|The company prelocated its servers to the new data center before the launch. 公司在发布之前将服务器预先配置到了新的数据中心。|近义词：position beforehand, prepare in advance|反义词：postlocate (此词并非标准英语词汇)|动词
