causal:/ˈkɔːzəl/| adj.|因果的；原因的；由因果关系造成的| Relating to or indicating a cause or reason.  It suggests a relationship between cause and effect.|例句：There is a causal link between smoking and lung cancer. 吸烟和肺癌之间存在因果关系。

例句：The study explored the causal relationship between stress and illness. 这项研究探讨了压力与疾病之间的因果关系。


近义词：causative, effective, resultant


反义词：accidental, coincidental, unrelated


词性：形容词
