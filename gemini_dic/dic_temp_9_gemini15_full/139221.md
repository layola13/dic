objuration:/ˌɒbdʒʊˈreɪʃən/| n. |恳求；咒骂；严厉斥责；正式的谴责|A strong or urgent request;  a forceful denunciation or condemnation.  The act of adjuring or solemnly charging someone. |例句：His objuration to the heavens was met with silence.  他的向天恳求没有得到回应。
例句：The objuration of the judge silenced the courtroom.  法官的严厉斥责让法庭安静下来。|近义词：adjuration, denunciation, condemnation, rebuke, reprimand |反义词：praise, commendation, approval
