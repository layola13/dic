dean:/diːn/| n. |院长，系主任|A dean is a senior academic administrator, typically in a college or university, responsible for overseeing a particular school, faculty, or department.  They often have significant academic and administrative responsibilities, including managing budgets, overseeing faculty appointments, and representing the faculty to the university administration. |Example: The dean of the engineering school announced the new scholarship program. 工程学院院长宣布了新的奖学金项目。|近义词：head, director, principal |反义词：student, professor


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The dean oversees the department's budget. = 院长负责监督部门的预算。
The dean teaches a course in history. = 院长教授一门历史课程。


一般过去时:
The dean approved the research proposal. = 院长批准了研究提案。

现在完成时:
The dean has served the university for twenty years. = 院长为大学服务了二十年。
The dean has appointed three new professors this year. = 院长今年任命了三位新教授。


过去完成时:
The dean had already left for the conference before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，院长已经离开去参加会议了。
The dean had reviewed the student's application before making a decision. = 院长在做决定之前已经审查了学生的申请。


将来时:
The dean will address the graduating class. = 院长将向毕业生致辞。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
