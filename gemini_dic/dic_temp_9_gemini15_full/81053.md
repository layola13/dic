grahamite:/ˈɡræmʌɪt/| n. |格雷厄姆石|A hard, black, lustrous asphalt-like mineral, a complex mixture of hydrocarbons and other organic compounds.  It is usually found in veins and is used sometimes as a source of asphalt. |例句：Grahamite is a rare mineral found in specific geological formations.|近义词：asphalt, bitumen|反义词：无
