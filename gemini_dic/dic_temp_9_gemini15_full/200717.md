sleepyheaded: /ˈsliːpiˌhedɪd/ | adj. | 嗜睡的，昏昏欲睡的 |  Characterized by or inclined to sleepiness; drowsy; lethargic.  The word combines "sleepy" (feeling or causing sleep) and "headed" (having a head; in this context, implying the state of the head being dominated by sleepiness). |


例句：

The sleepyheaded student struggled to stay awake during the lecture.  = 那个嗜睡的学生在课堂上努力保持清醒。

He felt sleepyheaded after a night of poor sleep. = 他睡眠不足后感到昏昏欲睡。


近义词：drowsy, lethargic, somnolent, heavy-eyed, sluggish

反义词：alert, awake, wide-awake, vigilant


词性: 形容词
