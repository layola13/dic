contemplates:/ˈkɒntɛmpleɪts/ /ˈkɑːntəmˌpleɪt/ | vt. |沉思；冥想；考虑；打算 |To think deeply and carefully about something.  |She contemplated the meaning of life. 她沉思生命的意义。| He contemplated resigning from his job. 他考虑辞职。| considers, ponders, meditates, contemplates | acts rashly, decides impulsively


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He contemplates the vastness of the universe. 他沉思宇宙的浩瀚。
She contemplates her next move in the game.  她在游戏中思考下一步行动。


一般过去时:
They contemplated the consequences of their actions. 他们思考他们行为的后果。
He contemplated a career change for months. 他考虑换工作好几个月了。


现在完成时:
I have contemplated this problem for days. 我已经思考这个问题好几天了。
She has contemplated leaving her job several times. 她已经好几次考虑过辞职了。


过去完成时:
He had contemplated quitting his job before the promotion. 在升职之前，他曾考虑过辞职。
She had contemplated many options before making her decision.  在她做决定之前，她已经考虑过许多方案。


将来时:
I will contemplate my options before making a decision. 我将在做决定之前考虑我的选择。
They will contemplate the proposal carefully before responding. 他们会在回复提案之前仔细考虑。


虚拟语气:
If I had contemplated the risks more carefully, I wouldn't have made that mistake. 如果我更仔细地考虑过风险，我就不会犯那个错误。
I wish I had contemplated the consequences before acting. 我希望我在行动之前考虑过后果。


被动语态:
The plan was contemplated by the committee for weeks. 这个计划被委员会考虑了好几个星期。
The proposal is being contemplated by the board of directors.  这个提案正在由董事会考虑。


疑问句:
What are you contemplating? 你在想什么？
What were they contemplating during the meeting? 会议期间他们在考虑什么？


否定句:
He did not contemplate the possibility of failure. 他没有考虑失败的可能性。
She doesn't contemplate changing careers anytime soon. 她短期内不打算换工作。
