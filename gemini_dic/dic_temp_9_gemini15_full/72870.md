follicle: /ˈfɒlɪkl/ | /ˈfɑːlɪkl/ | n. | 毛囊；卵泡；滤泡 |

英文解析: A small cavity or sac, especially one in animal tissue, such as a hair follicle or an ovarian follicle.  It can also refer to a structure that contains or produces something, like a plant follicle containing seeds.

例句:

Each hair grows from a follicle in the skin. = 每根头发都从皮肤上的毛囊中长出。

The doctor examined the ovarian follicle. = 医生检查了卵泡。


近义词: sac, pouch, vesicle

反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可根据语境选择合适的反义词，例如：absence,  smooth skin (针对毛囊))
