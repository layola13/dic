mortgaging:/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒɪŋ/| v.|抵押；用…作抵押|The act of using property as collateral for a loan.  This involves pledging the property as security to the lender, who can seize it if the borrower defaults on the loan. |例句：They are mortgaging their house to pay for their son's education.  他们正在抵押他们的房子来支付儿子的教育费用。|例句：The company is mortgaging its assets to secure a large loan. 公司正在抵押其资产以获得一笔巨额贷款。|近义词：pledging, hypothecating|反义词：redeeming


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mortgages his property every year. = 他每年都抵押他的房产。
They mortgage their houses to get loans. = 他们抵押房屋获得贷款。

一般过去时:
She mortgaged her car to start a business. = 她抵押了她的车来创业。
He mortgaged his land to pay off his debts. = 他抵押了他的土地来偿还债务。


现在完成时:
They have mortgaged their house several times. = 他们已经抵押过几次他们的房子了。
We have mortgaged our assets to secure the investment. = 我们已经抵押了我们的资产来确保投资。


过去完成时:
They had mortgaged their farm before the drought hit. = 干旱来临之前，他们已经抵押了他们的农场。
She had mortgaged everything she owned to fund her research. = 她已经抵押了她拥有的所有东西来资助她的研究。


将来时:
He will mortgage his apartment to buy a bigger house. = 他将抵押他的公寓来买一栋更大的房子。
They will be mortgaging their business to expand operations. = 他们将抵押他们的生意来扩大经营。


虚拟语气:
If I needed money, I would mortgage my car. = 如果我需要钱，我会抵押我的车。
I wish I hadn't mortgaged my house so quickly. = 我真希望我没有这么快就抵押我的房子。


被动语态:
The property was mortgaged to the bank. = 该房产已抵押给银行。
His business is being mortgaged to secure a loan. = 他的生意正在被抵押以获得贷款。


疑问句:
Is he mortgaging his house? = 他正在抵押他的房子吗？
Have they ever mortgaged any property? = 他们曾经抵押过任何房产吗？


否定句:
He did not mortgage his property. = 他没有抵押他的房产。
They haven't mortgaged anything yet. = 他们还没有抵押任何东西。
