collapsability:/kəˌlæpsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可折叠性，可坍塌性 | The ability to collapse or be collapsed; the state of being collapsible.  This refers to the ease with which something can be folded or compressed into a smaller space.  It often implies a design feature allowing for this action. | 例句：The collapsability of the camping chair made it easy to transport.  (野营椅的可折叠性使其易于携带。) The tent's impressive collapsability allowed for quick packing. (帐篷令人印象深刻的可折叠性使得打包很快。) | 近义词：foldability, compressibility | 反义词：rigidity, inflexibility
