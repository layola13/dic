potentiate:/pəˈtɛnʃieɪt/ | vt. |增强，使更有效；赋予潜能 | To increase the strength or effectiveness of something.  |例句：The drug potentiates the effects of the anesthetic.  该药物增强了麻醉剂的效果。
例句：The teacher's encouragement potentiated the student's confidence. 老师的鼓励增强了学生的自信心。|近义词：enhance, amplify, strengthen, boost |反义词：weaken, diminish, reduce, impair |动词
