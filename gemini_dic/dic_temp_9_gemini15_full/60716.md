electro-oculographically: /ɪˌlɛktroʊˌɒkjuˈlɒɡrəfɪkli/ | adv. | 电眼图描记法地 |  By means of an electro-oculogram; using an electro-oculograph.  This adverb describes actions or processes performed using an electro-oculogram, a device that measures eye movements. |


例句：
The patient's eye movements were recorded electro-oculographically.  = 病人的眼球运动是用电眼图描记法记录的。

The study utilized electro-oculographically measured saccades to analyze visual attention. = 这项研究利用电眼图描记法测量的扫视来分析视觉注意力。


近义词： ophthalmographically (less common)


反义词：  无直接反义词，可考虑与不使用电眼图描记法的测量方法相对比。


词性：副词
