selloff:/ˈsɛlɔːf/| n. |抛售，减价出售 |A rapid decline in the price of a security or commodity due to mass selling.  It often occurs due to panic selling or a sudden loss of investor confidence. |Example: The stock market experienced a sharp selloff after the unexpected news.  股市在意外消息公布后经历了一次急剧抛售。The recent selloff in the housing market has left many homeowners underwater. 最近房地产市场的抛售使得许多房主资不抵债。|近义词：crash, slump, liquidation|反义词：surge, rally, boom|名词
