crippleness:/ˈkrɪplnəs/ | n. |残疾；跛行；衰弱 |The state of being crippled; disability; impairment. Crippleness can refer to physical disability, but also to a metaphorical sense of being weakened or hindered.  |例句：His crippleness prevented him from participating in sports. 他的残疾使他无法参加体育运动。

例句：The economic crippleness of the nation was evident in the widespread poverty.  国家的经济衰弱在普遍的贫困中显而易见。|近义词：disability, impairment, handicap, infirmity |反义词：ability, strength, health
