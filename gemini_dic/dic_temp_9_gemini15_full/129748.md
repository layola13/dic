non-evaluative: /ˌnɒnɪˈvæljʊeɪtɪv/ | adj. |非评价性的，不作评价的 | Not expressing or involving an evaluation or judgment; objective.  It describes something that avoids expressing opinions or making judgments, focusing on facts instead. |例句：The researcher maintained a non-evaluative stance throughout the interview, simply recording the participant's responses.  研究人员在整个采访过程中保持了非评价性的立场，只是记录了参与者的回答。

The teacher provided non-evaluative feedback, focusing on the student's technique rather than their overall performance. 老师提供了非评价性的反馈，侧重于学生的技巧而不是他们的整体表现。


近义词：objective, neutral, descriptive, unbiased, impartial

反义词：evaluative, judgmental, subjective, biased, opinionated

词性：形容词
