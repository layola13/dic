databank:/ˈdeɪtəˌbæŋk/| n. |数据库；资料库|A databank is a large, organized collection of data, often stored electronically and accessible by computer.  It can be used for various purposes, such as research, analysis, and information retrieval.|例句：The company uses a central databank to store customer information.  这家公司使用中央数据库来存储客户信息。|近义词：database, repository, archive|反义词：None
