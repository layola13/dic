partials:/ˈpɑːrʃəlz/ /ˈpɑːrʃəls/ | n. | 部分；局部；偏爱 |  Partials refer to something that is incomplete or only a part of a whole. It can also refer to a preference or bias towards something. |例句：The architect presented partials of the building design. 建筑师展示了建筑设计的局部。

例句：She showed a partials to her favorite color. 她对最喜欢的颜色表现出偏爱。

|近义词：fragments, portions, sections, preferences |反义词：whole, entirety, complete, impartial |词性：名词
