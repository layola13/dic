inaugurating: /ɪˈnɔːɡjʊreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 开始；为…举行就职典礼；揭幕；开创 |

英文解析:  The act of beginning something new, especially an important event or process, such as a new era, a project, or a period of office.  It often implies a formal or ceremonial beginning.

例句:

The president is inaugurating a new national park next week.  = 总统下周将为一个新的国家公园举行开幕典礼。

They are inaugurating a new system for managing waste. = 他们正在启用一个新的废物管理系统。


近义词: commencing, initiating, beginning, opening, launching, ushering in


反义词: concluding, ending, terminating, closing


词性: Verb (present participle)
