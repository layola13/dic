sharer:/ˈʃɛərər/| n.|分享者，合用者|A person who shares something with others, such as a resource, cost, or experience.  It can also refer to someone who uses a shared resource.|例句：He's a sharer of information, always willing to help others. 他是一个乐于分享信息的人，总是愿意帮助他人。


例句：My roommate is a sharer of expenses, splitting the bills fairly.我的室友是共同承担费用的伙伴，公平地分摊账单。


近义词：participant, contributor, partner|反义词：hoarder, keeper


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sharer distributes the files to the team. = 分享者将文件分发给团队。
He shares his knowledge freely. = 他无私地分享他的知识。

一般过去时:
She shared her toys with her friends. = 她把玩具和朋友们分享了。
They shared a pizza. = 他们一起分享了一个比萨饼。

现在完成时:
We have shared many experiences together. = 我们一起分享了许多经历。
He has shared his concerns with his manager. = 他已经和他的经理分享了他的担忧。

过去完成时:
They had shared the good news before the party started. = 他们在派对开始之前就分享了这个好消息。
She had already shared her ideas with the team. = 她已经和团队分享了她的想法。

将来时:
I will share my work with you tomorrow. = 我明天会把我的工作和你分享。
They will share the profits equally. = 他们将平均分配利润。

虚拟语气:
If he were a sharer, he would help others more. = 如果他乐于分享，他会更多地帮助别人。
I wish I were a better sharer of my time. = 我希望我更善于分配我的时间。

被动语态:
The resources are shared among the users. = 资源在用户之间共享。
The profits will be shared equally. = 利润将平均分配。

疑问句:
Is he a sharer of information? = 他是一个乐于分享信息的人吗？
Are they willing to be sharers of the costs? = 他们愿意共同承担费用吗？

否定句:
He is not a sharer of his personal life. = 他不分享他的私人生活。
They didn't share their feelings with each other. = 他们没有彼此分享他们的感受。
