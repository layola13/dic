resurge:/rɪˈsɜːrdʒ/ | vi. | 再起；复苏；复活 | To rise or grow again strongly after a period of decline or inactivity.  |例句：After a period of decline, the company is now resurgent.  公司在经历一段时间的衰退后，现在正在复苏。

例句：Interest in vinyl records has resurged in recent years. 近年来，对黑胶唱片的兴趣重新兴起。


近义词：revive, rebound, rally, recover | 反义词：decline, fade, wane, diminish | 词性:不及物动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The economy resurges after a period of recession. = 经济在经历衰退后复苏。
Hope resurges in the hearts of the people. = 希望重燃在人们的心中。


一般过去时:
The city resurged after the devastating earthquake. = 城市在毁灭性地震后复苏了。
His career resurged after a long break. = 他在长时间休息后事业重新兴起。


现在完成时:
The band has resurged in popularity. = 乐队的人气再次飙升。
The housing market has resurged after a period of low sales. = 房地产市场在经历了一段低迷的销售期后复苏了。


过去完成时:
By the time the war ended, hope had resurged among the people. = 当战争结束时，人们心中又燃起了希望。
Before the new CEO arrived, the company's morale had resurged. = 在新任CEO到来之前，公司的士气已经有所回升。


将来时:
The team will resurge next season. = 该队将在下个赛季卷土重来。
Experts predict the industry will resurge in the coming years. = 专家预测该行业将在未来几年复苏。


虚拟语气:
If the economy resurged, many people would benefit. = 如果经济复苏，许多人将从中受益。
I wish the team would resurge and win the championship. = 我希望球队能重新崛起并赢得冠军。


被动语态: (Resurge is intransitive and cannot be used in passive voice.)  resurge是不及物动词，不能用于被动语态。


疑问句:
Will the company resurge after the scandal? = 这家公司会在丑闻之后复苏吗？
When will the housing market resurge? = 房地产市场什么时候会复苏？


否定句:
The team did not resurge after their losing streak. = 这支球队在连败之后并没有复苏。
The economy has not yet resurged despite government efforts. = 尽管政府努力，经济尚未复苏。


PHRASE->
a resurgent economy  复苏的经济
a resurgent interest  重新燃起的兴趣
a resurgent force  一股强大的力量
a resurgent market  复苏的市场
a resurgent power  重新崛起的势力
