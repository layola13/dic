personality:/ˌpɜːrsəˈnæləti/| n. |个性；人格；性格|The sum total of a person's physical, mental, emotional, and social characteristics.  It encompasses their unique patterns of behavior, thoughts, and feelings.  Personality is a complex and multifaceted concept, influenced by genetics, environment, and experience.|例句：His personality is quite outgoing and friendly.  他的性格非常外向和友善。|例句：She has a strong personality and isn't afraid to speak her mind. 她性格坚强，敢于表达自己的想法。|近义词：character, individuality, temperament, disposition |反义词：N/A (Antonyms are context-dependent and would describe specific personality traits, not the overall concept.)|名词
