paganist:/ˈpeɪɡənɪst/| n.|异教徒；信奉异教的人|A paganist is a follower of paganism, a religion that is not one of the main world religions like Christianity, Islam, or Judaism. Paganism encompasses a wide variety of beliefs and practices, often involving nature worship and polytheism.  |Example:  Many ancient cultures were paganist in their religious beliefs.  许多古代文化在宗教信仰上是异教的。|近义词：pagan, polytheist, nature worshiper|反义词：monotheist
