midfield:/ˈmɪdfiːld/| n. |中场；中场区域|The area of a sports field, especially in football (soccer), between the defense and offense.  It refers to the players who occupy this area and their collective play.|例句：The midfielders controlled the tempo of the game. 中场球员控制了比赛节奏。|例句：Our team's midfield was significantly weaker than theirs. 我们队的中场明显比他们弱。|近义词：centre, middle|反义词：defence, attack, forward line
