de-numbers: /diːˈnʌmbərz/ | n. | 去编号；去除编号 |  The process of removing numbers from a list or sequence.  It involves deleting or obscuring numerical identifiers. De-numbering is often used to anonymize data or maintain confidentiality. | Example: The research team de-numbered the participants' data to protect their identities. = 研究小组对参与者的数据进行了去编号处理以保护他们的身份。The database administrator de-numbered the customer records before releasing the report. = 数据库管理员在发布报告之前对客户记录进行了去编号处理。| 近义词：remove numbers, anonymize, redact | 反义词：number, enumerate | 名词
