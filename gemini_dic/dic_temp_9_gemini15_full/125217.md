multicore:/ˈmʌltiˌkɔːr/| adj.|多核的|Having multiple processing cores within a single computing unit.  This allows for parallel processing, significantly increasing computing power and efficiency.  |例句：My new computer has a multicore processor, making it much faster. 我的新电脑有多核处理器，使其速度快得多。
例句：Multicore processors are essential for handling complex tasks like video editing and 3D rendering. 多核处理器对于处理视频编辑和3D渲染等复杂任务至关重要。|近义词：polycore|反义词：single-core|形容词


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb or noun conjugations in the typical sense.  Its usage is descriptive.  Examples illustrate its use in different sentence structures:

Declarative Sentences:
The server uses a multicore CPU. = 这台服务器使用多核CPU。
This software is optimized for multicore processors. = 此软件针对多核处理器进行了优化。


Interrogative Sentences:
Is your computer equipped with a multicore processor? = 你的电脑配备了多核处理器吗？
Does this application support multicore processing? = 此应用程序支持多核处理吗？


Exclamatory Sentences:
What a powerful multicore machine! = 多么强大的多核机器！
This multicore performance is incredible! = 这多核性能令人难以置信！
