emitted:/ɪˈmɪtɪd/ | v. | 发射；散发；发出 | Past tense and past participle of "emit".  To send out (something, such as light, heat, or a gas) from a source. |例句：The sun emitted a blinding light. 太阳发出耀眼的光芒。
The factory emitted harmful pollutants into the air. 工厂向空气中排放有害污染物。|近义词：radiated, released, expelled |反义词：absorbed, retained


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun emits light and heat. = 太阳发出光和热。
The volcano emits gases. = 火山释放气体。

一般过去时:
The factory emitted smoke yesterday. = 昨天工厂排放了烟雾。
The speaker emitted a loud sigh. = 演讲者发出一声长叹。

现在完成时:
The car has emitted a strange noise all day. = 这辆车整天都在发出奇怪的声音。
The plant has emitted toxins into the soil for years. = 该工厂多年来一直向土壤中排放毒素。

过去完成时:
The machine had emitted a warning signal before it broke down. = 机器在故障前发出过警告信号。
The volcano had emitted ash for weeks before the eruption. = 火山爆发前几周一直在喷出火山灰。

将来时:
The rocket will emit a bright flame on launch. = 火箭发射时会发出明亮的火焰。
The device will emit a signal when it detects motion. = 当该设备检测到运动时，它将发出信号。

虚拟语气:
If the device emitted a stronger signal, we could detect it. = 如果该设备发出更强的信号，我们就能探测到它。
I wish the factory didn't emit so much pollution. = 我希望工厂不要排放那么多污染物。

被动语态:
A strange sound was emitted from the engine. = 引擎发出了一种奇怪的声音。
Harmful gases are emitted by many industrial processes. = 许多工业过程都会排放有害气体。

疑问句:
What kind of radiation is emitted by the star? = 这颗恒星发出什么类型的辐射？
Is sound emitted from the speaker? = 声音是从扬声器发出的吗？

否定句:
The device did not emit any signal. = 该设备没有发出任何信号。
The sun doesn't emit visible light at night. = 太阳晚上不发出可见光。
