centerboard:/ˈsɛntərˌbɔːrd/ | n. |中板，升降板 | A retractable keel used on some sailing boats to provide lateral resistance.  It is typically located near the center of the boat and can be lowered into the water for stability and raised for shallow-water sailing. | The sailboat's centerboard was lowered to improve its stability in the strong wind. 这艘帆船的中板被放下以提高其在强风中的稳定性。  The shallow draft of the boat, thanks to its retractable centerboard, allowed it to navigate the narrow, shallow river.  这艘船由于其可伸缩的中板而吃水浅，这使得它能够在狭窄而浅的河流中航行。| keel, daggerboard |  (no direct antonym, but) fixed keel | 名词
