recapping:/ˈriːkæpɪŋ/| v.|总结，概括，重述|Summarizing or reviewing information previously discussed or presented.  It involves briefly going over the main points.|例句：I'm recapping the meeting for those who missed it. = 我正在为错过会议的人总结会议内容。|例句：Let's recap what we've learned so far. = 让我们回顾一下我们到目前为止学到的东西。|近义词：summarizing, reviewing, summarizing, reiterating|反义词：elaborating, detailing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recaps the main points at the end of each lecture. = 他在每次讲座结束时都会总结要点。
She recaps the news headlines every morning. = 她每天早上都会总结新闻标题。

一般过去时:
The teacher recapped the lesson before the test. = 老师在考试前回顾了课程。
He recapped the events of the day in his diary. = 他在日记中总结了当天的事件。

现在完成时:
She has recapped the project's progress for the board. = 她已经为董事会总结了项目的进展。
We have recapped the key findings of the research. = 我们已经总结了研究的主要发现。

过去完成时:
After the presentation, he recapped what he had discussed. = 演示结束后，他回顾了他讨论的内容。
They had recapped the agreement before signing the contract. = 他们在签署合同之前回顾了协议。

将来时:
She will recap the meeting notes tomorrow. = 她明天将总结会议记录。
He will recap the highlights of the trip for his family. = 他将为家人总结旅行的亮点。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would recap the entire book. = 如果我有更多时间，我会总结整本书。
I wish I could recap the entire conference, but it was too long. = 我希望我能总结整个会议，但它太长了。

被动语态:
The key points were recapped at the end of the meeting. = 会议结束时总结了要点。
The main ideas will be recapped in the conclusion. = 主要观点将在结论中总结。

疑问句:
Will you recap the instructions for me? = 你能为我总结一下说明吗？
Can you recap what happened last night? = 你能总结一下昨晚发生的事情吗？

否定句:
He didn't recap the details of the accident. = 他没有总结事故的细节。
She didn't recap her presentation effectively. = 她没有有效地总结她的演示文稿。
