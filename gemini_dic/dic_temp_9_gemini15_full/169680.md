proposer:/prəˈpoʊzər/| n. |提案者；建议者；求婚者 |One who proposes something, such as a plan, idea, or marriage.  |The proposer of the new marketing strategy presented their ideas to the board.  新营销策略的提案者向董事会展示了他们的想法。|suggestor, applicant, petitioner |opponent, rejector |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The proposer outlines the key features of the project. = 提案者概述了项目的关键特征。
He proposes a new solution every week. = 他每周都提出一个新的解决方案。


一般过去时:
The proposer presented their findings at the conference. = 提案者在会议上展示了他们的发现。
She proposed a toast to the happy couple. = 她提议为这对幸福的夫妇敬酒。


现在完成时:
The proposer has already submitted their proposal. = 提案者已经提交了他们的提案。
They have proposed several amendments to the bill. = 他们已经对该法案提出了几项修正案。


过去完成时:
The proposer had already finished their presentation before the questions began. = 在提问开始之前，提案者已经完成了他们的演示。
Before the meeting, she had proposed a compromise. = 会议之前，她已经提出了一种妥协方案。


将来时:
The proposer will present their plan next week. = 提案者下周将展示他们的计划。
He will propose a new policy to the committee. = 他将向委员会提出一项新政策。


虚拟语气:
If the proposer were here, they could answer your questions. = 如果提案者在这里，他们可以回答你的问题。
I wish the proposer had explained their reasoning more clearly. = 我希望提案者能更清楚地解释他们的理由。


被动语态:
The proposal was well-received by the proposer. = 提案受到了提案者的欢迎。
A new solution was proposed by the team. = 一个新的解决方案是由团队提出的。


疑问句:
Who is the proposer of this amendment? = 这项修正案的提案者是谁？
Who proposed the changes to the schedule? = 谁提议更改时间表？


否定句:
The proposer did not adequately address the concerns raised. = 提案者没有充分解决提出的担忧。
He did not propose a viable alternative. = 他没有提出一个可行的替代方案。
