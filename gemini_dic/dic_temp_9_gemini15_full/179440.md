reductionistic: /rɪˈdʌkʃənɪstɪk/ | adj. | 还原论的；简化论的 |  Analyzing complex systems by reducing them to simpler, often less significant, components.  It often overlooks the emergent properties of the whole system. | Example:  His reductionistic approach to understanding human behavior ignored the complexities of social interaction. 他理解人类行为的还原论方法忽略了社会互动的复杂性。  |近义词: simplistic, oversimplified,  | 反义词: holistic, comprehensive, systemic |


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The scientist uses a reductionistic method. = 科学家采用还原论的方法。
His explanation is too reductionistic. = 他的解释过于简化。


一般过去时:
The study employed a reductionistic approach. = 这项研究采用了还原论的方法。

现在完成时:
Researchers have increasingly questioned reductionistic models. = 研究人员越来越质疑还原论模型。
Science has moved beyond purely reductionistic explanations. = 科学已经超越了纯粹的还原论解释。


过去完成时:
They had already rejected the reductionistic theory. = 他们已经否定了还原论理论。
Scientists had long relied on reductionistic methods. = 科学家长期以来依赖于还原论方法。


将来时:
Future research will likely move away from reductionistic thinking. = 未来的研究可能会远离还原论思维。

虚拟语气:
If we adopted a more holistic approach, it would be less reductionistic. = 如果我们采用更整体的方法，它将不那么还原论。
I wish they would abandon their reductionistic assumptions. = 我希望他们放弃他们还原论的假设。


被动语态:
The problem was simplified by a reductionistic analysis. = 这个问题被还原论分析简化了。
Complex systems are often misunderstood when viewed reductionistically. = 从还原论的角度来看，复杂的系统往往会被误解。


疑问句:
Is this explanation too reductionistic? = 这个解释是否过于简化？
Isn't a holistic perspective preferable to a reductionistic one? = 整体的视角不比还原论的视角更好吗？


否定句:
This approach is not reductionistic; it considers the interaction of factors. = 这种方法不是还原论的；它考虑了因素之间的相互作用。
The study avoided a purely reductionistic interpretation of the data. = 这项研究避免了对数据的纯粹还原论解释。

词源:  From "reduction" (the act of reducing something to a simpler form) + "-istic" (forming adjectives).  "Reduction" ultimately derives from the Latin "reducere" (to lead back).
