repersuade: /ˌriːpərˈsweɪd/ | vt. | 再劝说；再次劝服 | To persuade someone again; to try to persuade someone who has already refused. |例句：I tried to repersuade him, but he remained adamant.  我试图再次劝说他，但他仍然坚决不同意。


例句：After his initial refusal, she repersuaded him to join the project.  在他最初拒绝之后，她再次劝说他加入项目。

近义词：persuade again, reconvince, re-convince

反义词：dissuade, discourage


词性:动词
