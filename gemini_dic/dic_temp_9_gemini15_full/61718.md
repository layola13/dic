emotionalism:/ɪˈmoʊʃənəlɪzəm/ | n. |情绪主义；感情用事；感伤主义 |The excessive display or indulgence of emotion, often considered excessive or inappropriate.  It can refer to a literary or artistic style characterized by an emphasis on emotion over reason or logic, or a philosophical position prioritizing feelings over intellect. |例句：His speech was full of emotionalism, lacking in concrete proposals. 他的演讲充满了情绪化，缺乏具体的建议。

例句：The novel is criticized for its excessive emotionalism and lack of plot. 这部小说因其过度的情感化和缺乏情节而受到批评。


近义词：sentimentality, emotionalism, romanticism | 反义词：rationalism, stoicism
