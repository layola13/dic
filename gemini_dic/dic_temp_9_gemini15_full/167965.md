prioritises:/praɪˈɒrɪtaɪzɪz/ | v. | 优先处理；优先考虑 | To give something a higher priority than other things.  To consider something more important than other things and deal with it first. |  She prioritises her family over her career. 她把家庭放在事业之上。|  She prioritises spending time with her children. 她优先考虑与孩子们共度时光。 |  emphasize, stress, highlight |  neglect, ignore, disregard | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He prioritises his work. = 他优先处理他的工作。
She prioritises her health. = 她优先考虑她的健康。

一般过去时:
They prioritised the project. = 他们优先处理了这个项目。
He prioritised his sleep last night. = 他昨晚优先保证了睡眠。

现在完成时:
She has prioritised her studies this semester. = 这个学期她一直把学习放在首位。
They have prioritised customer satisfaction. = 他们一直优先考虑客户满意度。


过去完成时:
He had prioritised his family before his career. = 他在事业之前优先考虑了他的家庭。
She had prioritised finishing the report before the deadline. = 她优先完成了报告，赶在截止日期之前。


将来时:
We will prioritise the most urgent tasks. = 我们将优先处理最紧急的任务。
They will prioritise research and development. = 他们将优先发展研发。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would prioritise my mental health. = 如果我是你，我会优先考虑我的心理健康。
I wish I had prioritised my education when I was younger. = 我希望我年轻的时候更重视我的教育。

被动语态:
The project was prioritised by the management. = 该项目被管理层优先处理。
Customer needs are prioritised by our company. = 我们公司优先考虑客户需求。


疑问句:
What are you prioritising today? = 你今天优先处理什么？
Which tasks should we prioritise first? = 我们应该优先处理哪些任务？

否定句:
He doesn't prioritise his personal life. = 他不重视他的个人生活。
They didn't prioritise safety measures. = 他们没有优先考虑安全措施。
