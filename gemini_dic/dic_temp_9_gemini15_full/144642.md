outworking: /ˌaʊtˈwɜːrkɪŋ/ | v. |胜过；超过；努力工作超过 |  To surpass or exceed in work or effort; to work harder than.  |例句：He outworked all his competitors and secured the promotion. 他比所有竞争对手都努力工作，并获得了晋升。

例句：The team's outworking of their opponents was evident in their decisive victory.  该队比对手更加努力，这在他们决定性的胜利中显而易见。


近义词：surpassing, excelling, exceeding, outperforming, besting

反义词：underperforming, lagging, falling behind

词源:
The word "outworking" is a combination of the prefix "out-" (meaning "to a greater extent than") and the verb "working" (from the Old English "wyrcan," meaning "to work").  Thus, it literally means "working to a greater extent than."
