detonator:/ˈdɛtənˌeɪtər/ | n. |雷管，引爆器 | A detonator is a device used to initiate an explosion in a larger charge of explosive material.  It is typically a small, sensitive explosive that is activated by a fuse, electrical current, or other means. | Example: The bomb squad carefully deactivated the detonator before disposing of the device.  拆弹小组在处理装置之前小心地拆除了雷管。The miner used a blasting cap as a detonator to set off the dynamite. 矿工用雷管引爆炸药。|近义词：exploder, igniter, fuse |反义词：N/A
