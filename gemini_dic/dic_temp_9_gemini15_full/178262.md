recomposer:/ˌriːkəmˈpoʊzər/ | v. |重新组合；使恢复平静；作曲家（罕用）|To compose again; to restore to composure; (rarely) a composer. |例句：She took a deep breath to recompose herself after the shocking news.  她深吸一口气，以恢复在听到令人震惊的消息后的平静。

例句：The artist recomposed the painting, adding new elements to the landscape.  艺术家重新创作了这幅画，在风景中添加了新的元素。


近义词：compose, calm, reassure, settle |反义词：agitate, disturb, unsettle
