pursuing:/pərˈsuːɪŋ/| v.|追求，从事，致力于|  To follow or chase something or someone. To engage in an activity or endeavor.|例句：He is pursuing a career in medicine. 他正在追求医学事业。

例句：She is pursuing her dream of becoming a writer. 她正在追求成为作家的梦想。


近义词：chasing, following, seeking, striving for, working on|反义词：abandoning, neglecting, giving up|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pursuing a master's degree. 他正在攻读硕士学位。
They are pursuing a new strategy. 他们正在实施一项新战略。


一般过去时:
She pursued her passion for art. 她追求了她对艺术的热情。
He pursued the thief down the street. 他追赶小偷跑下街。


现在完成时:
I have been pursuing this goal for years. 我多年来一直在追求这个目标。
They have pursued justice relentlessly. 他们一直不懈地追求正义。


过去完成时:
He had pursued a career in law before changing to medicine. 在转行学医之前，他曾从事法律工作。
She had pursued many different hobbies before settling on painting. 在专注于绘画之前，她曾尝试过许多不同的爱好。


将来时:
I will be pursuing my studies abroad next year. 明年我将在国外继续我的学业。
They will pursue this line of inquiry further. 他们将进一步探究这条思路。


虚拟语气:
If I were pursuing a different path, I would be happier. 如果我选择不同的道路，我会更快乐。
I wish I had pursued my dreams earlier. 我希望我早点追求我的梦想。


被动语态:
The case is being pursued by the police. 警方正在调查此案。
The matter is being actively pursued. 这件事正在积极处理中。


疑问句:
What are you pursuing right now? 你现在正在追求什么？
Are they pursuing a legal solution? 他们正在寻求法律解决办法吗？


否定句:
He is not pursuing a relationship at the moment. 他目前没有追求任何恋爱关系。
They are not pursuing this avenue of investigation. 他们没有沿着这条调查线索继续下去。


PHRASE->
pursuing a goal:追求目标
pursuing a career:追求事业
pursuing happiness:追求幸福
pursuing justice:追求正义
pursuing an interest:追求兴趣

词源：源自古法语 *pursuir，意为“追赶，追捕”，其词根来自拉丁语 *prosequi，意为“追赶，继续”。
