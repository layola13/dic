cabbed:/kæbd/| v.|乘出租车；搭乘出租车|to travel in a taxi or cab|I cabbed home after the party.  我派对后乘出租车回家了。|He cabbed to the airport. 他乘出租车去了机场。|drove,  took a taxi|walked, cycled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cabs to work every day. 他每天都乘出租车去上班。
She often cabs to the restaurant. 她经常打车去饭店。

一般过去时:
We cabbed home last night. 我们昨晚打车回家了。
They cabbed to the concert. 他们打车去音乐会了。

现在完成时:
I have cabbed several times this week. 我本周已经打过几次车了。
She has never cabbed alone at night. 她从未独自在晚上打过车。

过去完成时:
He had cabbed to the station before the train left. 火车离开前他已经打车到车站了。
We had cabbed all day before we finally arrived. 我们在最终到达之前已经打车一整天了。

将来时:
I will cab to the airport tomorrow. 我明天将打车去机场。
They will cab home after the show. 演出结束后他们将打车回家。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would cab to the meeting. 如果我有更多钱，我会打车去会议。
I wish I had cabbed instead of walking. 我真希望我当时是打车而不是走路。

被动语态:
The passenger was cabbed to the hotel.  乘客被出租车送到了酒店。(This usage is less common and sounds unnatural)
The tourists were cabbed to the airport by the hotel. 旅客们被酒店用出租车送到机场。(This is a more natural usage of the passive voice)

疑问句:
Did you cab to the party? 你打车去派对了吗？
Will they cab to the hospital? 他们会打车去医院吗？

否定句:
I didn't cab to work this morning. 今天早上我没有打车去上班。
They didn't cab home last night. 他们昨晚没有打车回家。


PHRASE->
