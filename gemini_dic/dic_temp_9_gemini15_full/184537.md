restimulating: /ˌriːˈstɪmjuleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 再刺激的；重新刺激的 |  Describes something that stimulates again or anew; causing renewed stimulation or excitement.  It implies a previous stimulation that has subsided and is now being rekindled. |


例句：

The restimulating effects of the music revived his spirits. =  音乐再次刺激的效果使他精神振奋。

The restimulating conversation reignited their passion. =  再次刺激的谈话重新点燃了他们的激情。


近义词：reinvigorating, re-energizing, revitalizing


反义词：depressant, inhibiting, deadening


词性：形容词
