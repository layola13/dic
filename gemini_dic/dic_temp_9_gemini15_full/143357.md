outfaces:/aʊtˈfeɪsɪz/| vt.|胜过，超过，比…更出色|To surpass or exceed someone or something in skill, ability, or performance.|例句：The young artist outfaced his more experienced rivals in the competition. 年轻艺术家在比赛中胜过了更有经验的对手。|例句：Her determination outfaced all obstacles in her path. 她的决心克服了她前进道路上的一切障碍。|近义词：outshine, outdo, surpass, exceed|反义词：underperform, fail, fall short

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outfaces his opponents in every debate. = 他在每一次辩论中都胜过对手。
The company outfaces its competitors in terms of innovation. = 这家公司在创新方面胜过竞争对手。

一般过去时:
She outfaced her fear and spoke up. = 她克服了恐惧，大胆地说了出来。
The team outfaced the challenges and won the game. = 球队克服了挑战并赢得了比赛。

现在完成时:
He has outfaced many difficulties in his career. = 他在他职业生涯中克服了许多困难。
The company has outfaced its rivals in the market. = 这家公司在市场上已经胜过了它的竞争对手。

过去完成时:
They had outfaced numerous setbacks before achieving success. = 他们在取得成功之前已经克服了许多挫折。
The project had outfaced significant technical hurdles. = 该项目已经克服了重大的技术障碍。

将来时:
She will outface any opposition to her plans. = 她将克服对她计划的任何反对。
The new product will outface all existing models on the market. = 新产品将胜过市场上所有现有的型号。

虚拟语气:
If only I could outface my shyness. = 但愿我能克服我的害羞。
If he had outfaced his doubts, he would have succeeded. = 如果他能克服他的疑虑，他就会成功。

被动语态:
His talent was outfaced by his lack of discipline. = 他的才能被他的缺乏纪律所掩盖。(被动语态较少使用，因为outface通常是主动行为)
The problem was outfaced by a new solution. = 这个问题被一个新的解决方案解决了。（被动语态较少使用，因为outface通常是主动行为）

疑问句:
Can she outface the pressure? = 她能承受住压力吗？（这里outface表示“克服，战胜”）
Does his performance outface expectations? = 他的表现是否超出预期？


否定句:
He didn't outface his opponent in the argument. = 他在争论中没有胜过他的对手。
The company failed to outface the economic downturn. = 这家公司未能克服经济衰退。
