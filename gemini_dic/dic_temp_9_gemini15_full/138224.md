nosing:/ˈnoʊzɪŋ/| v.| 嗅；探问；窥探；慢慢移动 |The act of smelling or investigating something with the nose; also, to search inquisitively or pry. It can also describe slow movement. |例句：The dog was nosing around the garden.  那条狗正在花园里四处嗅探。

The detective was nosing around for clues.  侦探正在四处探寻线索。


近义词：sniffing, investigating, snooping, poking around, creeping|反义词：ignoring, overlooking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He noses around the garden every morning. 他每天早上都在花园里四处嗅探。
She noses into other people's business. 她爱打听别人的事。


一般过去时:
The cat nosed around the corner. 猫从拐角处探出头来。
They nosed through the old documents. 他们仔细翻阅了那些旧文件。


现在完成时:
He has been nosing around my apartment. 他一直在我的公寓里四处打探。
She has nosed out the truth. 她已经查出了真相。


过去完成时:
He had nosed around the area before the incident. 事发前他曾在这个地区四处打探。
They had nosed through all the papers before finding the answer. 他们在找到答案之前已经仔细翻阅了所有文件。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish she wouldn't nose into my private life. 我希望她别再打听我的私事了。


被动语态:

The secret was nosed out by a journalist. 这个秘密是被一个记者挖出来的。


疑问句:

否定句:

PHRASE->

have one's nose to the grindstone: 埋头苦干


词源:
The word "nosing" comes from the Old English word "nosu," meaning "nose." The verb sense developed from the literal act of using one's nose to smell or investigate something.  Over time, it broadened to include the figurative sense of inquisitive searching or prying.
