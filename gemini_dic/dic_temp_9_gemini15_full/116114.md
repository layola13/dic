marvelling: /ˈmɑːvəlɪŋ/ | v. |惊叹；赞叹；对…感到惊奇 |  To feel great surprise and wonder at something.  | 例句：She stood marvelling at the beauty of the sunset. 她站在那里，惊叹于日落的美丽。  | 近义词：wondering, admiring,astonishing | 反义词：indifference, apathy


USAGE->

They marvel at the acrobat's skill. = 他们对杂技演员的技巧感到惊叹。
