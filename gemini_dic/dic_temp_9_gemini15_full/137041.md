nonsterilely:/ˌnɒnˈstɛraɪlli/ | adv. | 非无菌地，不无菌地 |  In a manner that is not sterile; without sterilization.  This term describes a process or condition lacking the absence of microorganisms. | Example: The instruments were handled nonsterilely, increasing the risk of infection. 器械处理不当，增加了感染的风险。 | 近义词：unsterilely, aseptically (反义词) | 反义词：sterilely, aseptically
