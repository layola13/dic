domite:/ˈdəʊmaɪt/| n.| 粗面岩 |A light-colored, glassy volcanic rock, typically light gray to nearly white, that is composed mainly of feldspar and quartz. It is often found in volcanic plugs and is characterized by its relatively high silica content and lack of mafic minerals.  |Example: The geologist identified the sample as a type of domite, based on its texture and mineral composition.|近义词：rhyolite, dacite|反义词：basalt
