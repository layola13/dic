on an even keel:/ˌɑːn ən ˈiːvən kiːl/ | adj. phrase | 平稳；稳定；平静 |  The phrase describes a state of stability, balance, and composure, often in the face of difficulties or challenges. It originates from the nautical term referring to a ship sailing smoothly and steadily. |例句：Despite the recent setbacks, the company is managing to stay on an even keel.  尽管最近遭遇挫折，公司仍然保持稳定运行。

The project is finally on an even keel after months of delays and setbacks.  经过数月的延误和挫折，项目终于步入正轨。


近义词：stable, steady, balanced, calm, composed | 反义词：unstable, unsteady, turbulent, chaotic, erratic | 词性：形容词短语


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ship is on an even keel. = 船舶航行平稳。
The company remains on an even keel despite the economic downturn. = 尽管经济低迷，公司仍然保持稳定。


一般过去时:
The project was on an even keel until the unexpected storm hit. =  直到意外的风暴来临之前，项目一直进展顺利。
Our finances were on an even keel before the unexpected medical bills arrived. = 在意外的医疗账单到来之前，我们的财务状况一直很稳定。


现在完成时:
The economy has been on an even keel for the past year. = 过去一年经济一直保持稳定。
Despite many challenges, the team has managed to stay on an even keel. = 尽管面临许多挑战，团队还是设法保持稳定。


过去完成时:
The business had been on an even keel for five years before the merger. = 在合并之前，这家企业已经稳定经营了五年。
Everything had been on an even keel until the new management took over. = 直到新管理层接手之前，一切都井然有序。


将来时:
We hope the project will be on an even keel by the end of the year. = 我们希望项目到年底能够稳定运行。
With careful planning, we expect the business to remain on an even keel. = 通过周密的计划，我们预计企业将保持稳定。


虚拟语气:
If the government hadn't interfered, the economy would have remained on an even keel. = 如果政府不干预，经济本来会保持稳定。
If we had better planning, things would be on an even keel. = 如果我们有更好的计划，情况就会稳定。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
