interpreting: /ɪnˈtɜːprɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 解释；翻译；诠释 |  The act of explaining, translating, or giving meaning to something.  It involves conveying the meaning of something in a different form or language.  This can involve spoken or written language, signs, symbols, or even behavior. |

例句：

The interpreter is interpreting the speech for the audience. = 翻译员正在为听众翻译演讲。

He is interpreting the data to find a solution. = 他正在解读数据以寻找解决方案。


近义词：translating, explaining, rendering, paraphrasing, elucidating

反义词：misunderstanding, misconstruing
