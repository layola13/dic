minibuses:/ˈmɪnɪbʌsɪz/| n.| 小型巴士 |A minibus is a small bus, typically carrying between 8 and 16 passengers.  It's smaller than a coach but larger than a car. They are often used for transporting small groups of people, such as school children or tourists.|例句：The school uses minibuses to transport students to field trips. 学校使用小型巴士运送学生参加实地考察。

例句：We hired a minibus to take us to the airport. 我们租了一辆小型巴士送我们去机场。


近义词：small bus, van (depending on size)
反义词：coach, large bus


词性：名词
