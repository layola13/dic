dusked:/dʌskt/| v. |黄昏降临；变暗；|To become dark or dim, especially as twilight approaches.  It implies a gradual darkening, often associated with the end of the day. |The sky dusked quickly after sunset. 天在日落后很快就暗了下来。 The room dusked as the sun went down.  随着太阳下山，房间暗了下来。| grew dark, darkened, grew dim | bright, illuminated | 动词
