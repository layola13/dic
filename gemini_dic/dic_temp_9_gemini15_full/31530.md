charter:/ˈtʃɑːrtər/| n., v.|宪章；特许状；包租；特许；包机|

英文解析：As a noun, a charter is a written grant by a ruler, government, or other authority conferring rights and privileges.  It can also refer to a document outlining the fundamental principles and rules of an organization or institution. As a verb, it means to hire or rent, particularly a vehicle like an aircraft or bus, for a specific purpose. It can also mean to grant a charter to.


例句：

The university received a royal charter in 1558.  = 这所大学在1558年获得了皇家特许状。

The company chartered a plane to fly its employees to the conference. = 公司包了一架飞机送员工去参加会议。


近义词：

(n.) constitution, document, licence, lease, contract

(v.) hire, rent, lease


反义词：

(n.) revocation, annulment

(v.)  revoke, cancel


词性：

Noun, Verb
