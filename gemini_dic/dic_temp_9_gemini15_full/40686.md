converse:/ˈkɒnvɜːs/ /ˈkɑːnvɜːrs/| n., v.|相反的事物；谈话；颠倒；反转 | to talk to someone; the opposite; to turn inside out or upside down |The converse of "All men are mortal" is "Some mortals are men".  所有男人都会死 的逆命题是有些凡人 是男人。|  They conversed for hours. 他们谈了几个小时。|opposite, reverse, inverse |same, identical|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They converse regularly. = 他们定期交谈。
We converse in English. = 我们用英语交谈。


一般过去时:
They conversed about the weather. = 他们谈论了天气。
We conversed late into the night. = 我们谈到深夜。


现在完成时:
They have conversed on many occasions. = 他们多次交谈过。
We have conversed about this before. = 我们之前讨论过这个。


过去完成时:
They had conversed before the meeting. = 他们会在会议前进行交谈。
We had conversed for hours before reaching a decision. = 在做出决定之前，我们已经谈论了好几个小时。


将来时:
They will converse tomorrow. = 他们明天会交谈。
We will converse at length about the proposal. = 我们将详细讨论这项提案。


虚拟语气:
If they conversed more openly, they might solve their problems. = 如果他们更坦诚地交谈，他们可能会解决他们的问题。
I wish we had conversed more before making the decision. = 我希望我们在做决定之前能够多谈谈。


被动语态:
The topic was conversed about extensively. = 这个话题被广泛讨论。
The matter was conversed upon during the meeting. = 会议期间讨论了这件事。


疑问句:
Do they converse frequently? = 他们经常交谈吗？
Will they converse before the game? = 他们会在比赛前交谈吗？


否定句:
They do not converse often. = 他们不经常交谈。
They did not converse about the sensitive issue. = 他们没有谈论这个敏感的问题。


PHRASE->
converse with someone = 与某人交谈
conversely = 相反地
in converse = 相反地
converse proposition = 逆命题
conversely speaking = 反过来说


词源:
From Old French *converser, from Latin conversari "to live, dwell, associate, converse," from con- "together" + versari "to turn, be occupied," from vertere "to turn."  The sense evolution is from "turning together" (as in associating) to "talking together."
