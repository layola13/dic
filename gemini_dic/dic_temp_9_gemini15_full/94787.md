impugnability: /ɪmˈpjuːɡnəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可驳斥性；可攻击性；易受攻击性 | The vulnerability of a claim or argument to attack or refutation; the state or quality of being open to challenge or criticism.  | The impugnability of his testimony was evident from the numerous inconsistencies.  他的证词的可驳斥性从众多矛盾之处显而易见。  | vulnerability, susceptibility, assailability | irrefutability, incontestability, unassailability | 名词
