dottiest:/ˈdɒtiɪst/ | adj. |最愚蠢的，最古怪的 | Most foolish or eccentric;  characterized by silliness or eccentricity.  It is the superlative form of "dotty". | 例句：He's the dottiest person I know. = 他是我认识的最傻的人。

The dottiest idea she ever had was to try and paint the house purple. = 她曾经最古怪的想法是试图把房子漆成紫色。

近义词：daft, silly, wacky, eccentric, crazy,  absurd | 反义词：sensible, rational, intelligent, wise

词源:  Derived from "dotty," meaning slightly mad or eccentric.  The suffix "-est" forms the superlative, indicating the highest degree.  "Dotty" itself likely originates from the idea of being "dotty" or having spots, suggesting a scattered or confused mind.
