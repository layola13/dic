USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have tense conjugations like verbs.  It modifies verbs, describing how an action is performed.


例句1: The metal bent deformedly under the immense pressure. 金属在巨大的压力下变形了。
例句2:  The flowers grew deformedly due to the lack of sunlight. 由于缺乏阳光，花朵长得畸形。

There aren't common phrases specifically using "deformedly".  Its use is generally standalone to describe the manner of an action or state.
