pushy:/ˈpʊʃi/ | adj. | 固执的；咄咄逼人的；强求的 |  Someone who is pushy is determined to get what they want, even if it means being unpleasant or annoying to other people. They are assertive to the point of being aggressive.  | 例句：He was a pushy salesman, always trying to convince customers to buy things they didn't need. 他是一个咄咄逼人的推销员，总是试图说服顾客购买他们不需要的东西。

She's a bit pushy; she always tries to get her way.  她有点咄咄逼人；她总是试图按照自己的方式行事。


近义词：aggressive, assertive, insistent, demanding, importunate | 反义词：passive, submissive, yielding, retiring
