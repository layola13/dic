schoolmasters:/ˈskuːlˌmɑːstərz/| n.| 男教师；校长；训导主任 | A schoolmaster is a male teacher, especially one in a private school or in the past. The term often implies a stricter or more traditional approach to teaching.  |  He was a strict schoolmaster known for his high expectations. 他是一位以高期望值而闻名的严厉校长。 The old schoolmaster retired last year. 老校长去年退休了。| teachers, instructors, educators | students, pupils | 名词
