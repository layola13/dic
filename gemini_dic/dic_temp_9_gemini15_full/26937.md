calcaneal:/ˌkælˈkeɪniəl/| adj.|跟骨的；踵骨的| Relating to the calcaneus (heel bone).  The calcaneal tendon is also known as the Achilles tendon. |例句：He suffered a calcaneal fracture. 他遭受了跟骨骨折。

例句：The doctor examined his calcaneal region for any signs of injury. 医生检查了他的跟骨区域是否有任何受伤迹象。


近义词：heel (in some contexts)


反义词：无


词性:形容词
