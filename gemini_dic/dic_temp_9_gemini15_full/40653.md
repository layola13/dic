conventionist:/kənˈvɛnʃənɪst/ | n. | 大会代表；墨守成规者；循规蹈矩者 | A person who attends or participates in a convention, especially a political convention.  Someone who adheres strictly to conventions or traditions. | Example:  Many conventionists attended the political party's national convention. 许多代表参加了该政党的全国代表大会。 He is a conventionist in his approach to art, preferring traditional techniques. 他在艺术创作方面墨守成规，偏爱传统技法。|近义词：delegate, traditionalist, conformist | 反义词：rebel, innovator, nonconformist
