gypsy:/ˈdʒɪpsi/| n. |吉普赛人；流浪者| A member of a wandering people originally from India, now scattered throughout the world.  The term is considered offensive by many. |例句：The gypsy fortune teller predicted a bright future for me. 吉普赛算命先生预言我会有一个光明的未来。

例句：A group of gypsies camped by the river. 一群吉普赛人在河边扎营。


近义词：Romany, traveler, nomad |反义词：settler
