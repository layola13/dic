rebrighten:/ˌriːˈbraɪtn/| vt.|使…重新明亮；使…恢复光彩；使…重获生机|To make something brighter or more cheerful again.  It implies a restoration of former brightness or cheerfulness after a period of dullness or gloom.|例句：The sunshine rebrightened the room.  阳光使房间重新明亮起来。

例句：The good news rebrightened her spirits.  好消息使她重拾精神。


近义词：revive, refresh, brighten, enliven|反义词：dim, dull, darken|动词
