conservational: /ˌkɒnsɜːˈveɪʃənəl/ | /ˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 保护的；注重保护的；保守的 | Relating to or characterized by conservation; tending to conserve.  It can refer to the act of preserving, protecting, or conserving something, typically natural resources, or to a mindset that favors traditional values and practices. |

例句：

The conservational efforts focused on protecting endangered species.  = 保护工作集中在保护濒危物种上。

The government implemented conservational policies to reduce pollution. = 政府实施了保护政策以减少污染。


近义词：preservationist, protective, safeguarding, traditional, cautious

反义词：destructive, wasteful, radical, progressive
