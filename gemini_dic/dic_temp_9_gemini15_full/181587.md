relater:/rɪˈleɪtər/ | n. |关联者，叙述者，相关者 |A person or thing that relates or connects things.  Someone who explains or describes something.  |例句：The teacher acted as a relater, connecting the historical events to the students' lives.  老师扮演了关联者的角色，将历史事件与学生的生活联系起来。

例句：The author is a skilled relater, weaving a captivating narrative. 作者是一位熟练的叙述者，编织了一个引人入胜的故事。


近义词：connector, narrator, explainer, correlator

反义词：separator, divider, isolator

词性：名词
