humanoid:/ˈhjuːmənɔɪd/ | n. | 人形机器人；类人动物 | A humanoid is a robot or creature with a human-like appearance.  It often refers to robots designed to resemble humans in form and function, though not necessarily in intelligence or emotions.  | Example:  The science fiction film featured a menacing humanoid robot. 这部科幻电影中出现了一个令人恐惧的人形机器人。
  The researchers are developing a humanoid robot for disaster relief. 研究人员正在研发用于灾难救援的人形机器人。|近义词：anthropomorphic, human-like |反义词：non-humanoid, machine-like |
