porpoising:/ˈpɔːrpəzɪŋ/| n. |海豚式跃水，(船舶)高速航行时产生的跳跃现象 |The act of a dolphin or other marine mammal leaping out of the water and then back in again, or the similar movement of a boat at high speed.  |例句：The boat was porpoising violently in the rough seas.  这艘船在波涛汹涌的海面上剧烈地跳跃着。|例句：We observed a pod of dolphins porpoising playfully in the bay. 我们观察到一群海豚在海湾里嬉戏地跃出水面。|近义词：bounding, leaping, jumping |反义词：submerging, diving
