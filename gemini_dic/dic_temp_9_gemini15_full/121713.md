misprinting:/ˌmɪsˈprɪntɪŋ/| n. |印刷错误，错印|The act of printing something incorrectly; an instance of this.  It refers to errors that occur during the printing process, leading to inaccuracies in the final printed material.  These errors can range from minor typos to more significant mistakes affecting the overall meaning or presentation.|例句：The misprinting of the address on the envelope caused the delay in delivery. 信封上的地址印错了，导致邮件延误。

There was a noticeable misprinting in the first edition of the book.  这本书的第一版有一个明显的印刷错误。|近义词：typo, error, mistake, fault|反义词：accurate printing, correct printing|名词
