multipiece:/ˈmʌltipiːs/| adj.|多件套的，多部分的|Consisting of or composed of many pieces or parts.  A multipiece product is made up of several separate components that are assembled together. |例句：This multipiece furniture set is easy to assemble.  这个多件套家具很容易组装。

例句：The engine is a complex multipiece mechanism. 这台发动机是一个复杂的多个部件组成的机构。


近义词：composite, multipart, multi-part, many-part

反义词：one-piece, single-piece, monolithic

词性:形容词
