self-disservice: /ˌsɛlf dɪˈsɜːrvɪs/ | n. | 自我损害；损人不利己的行为 |  An act that harms oneself; an action that is detrimental to one's own interests.  It implies a conscious or unconscious action that negatively impacts the individual performing the action.  | Example:  Continuously criticizing oneself, even when successful, is a form of self-disservice.  持续不断地批评自己，即使成功了，也是一种自我损害的行为。

|近义词: self-sabotage, self-destruction, self-harm | 反义词: self-care, self-improvement | 词性: noun
