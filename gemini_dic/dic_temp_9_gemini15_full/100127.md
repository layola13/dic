interprets:/ɪnˈtɜːrprɪts/ | vt. | 解释；诠释；理解；翻译 | To explain the meaning of something; to give one's own understanding of something. | 例句：The teacher interprets the poem for the students. 老师为学生们解释这首诗。  | 例句：He interprets the data in a different way. 他以不同的方式解读这些数据。 |近义词：explains, elucidates, translates, renders | 反义词：misunderstands, misconstrues | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He interprets the law differently. = 他对法律有不同的解读。
She interprets the painting as a symbol of hope. = 她将这幅画解读为希望的象征。

一般过去时:
The translator interpreted the speech for the audience. = 翻译员为听众翻译了演讲。
He interpreted her silence as a sign of disapproval. = 他把她的沉默理解为反对的标志。

现在完成时:
She has interpreted many classic novels. = 她已经翻译了许多经典小说。
He has interpreted the results of the experiment in his report. = 他在他的报告中解释了实验的结果。


过去完成时:
They had interpreted the message incorrectly. = 他们已经错误地理解了信息。
Before the meeting, she had interpreted the data several times. = 会议前，她已经多次解读了数据。


将来时:
He will interpret the dreams in his book. = 他将在他的书中解释这些梦。
The professor will interpret the findings of the study next week. = 教授下周将解释这项研究的结果。

虚拟语气:
If he interpreted the situation correctly, he would have acted differently. = 如果他正确地理解了情况，他就会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had interpreted his words more carefully. = 我真希望我当时更仔细地理解了他的话。

被动语态:
The poem was interpreted in various ways. = 这首诗被以各种方式解释。
The data will be interpreted by the experts. = 数据将由专家进行解读。

疑问句:
How do you interpret this passage? = 你如何解释这段文字？
How did he interpret her actions? = 他如何解读她的行为？

否定句:
He didn't interpret the situation correctly. = 他没有正确地理解情况。
She doesn't interpret the evidence as conclusive. = 她不认为证据是确凿的。


PHRASE->

misinterpret the meaning = 误解含义
