counter-charm:/ˈkaʊntərˌtʃɑːrm/| n. |反咒语，解咒|A counter-charm is a spell or action designed to counteract or neutralize the effect of another spell or charm.  It's often used in fictional contexts, especially in fantasy literature and games.  |例句：The witch cast a counter-charm to break the sleeping spell. 女巫施了一个反咒来解除催眠咒。

例句：The wizard used a powerful counter-charm to dispel the evil sorcerer's curse.  巫师用强大的反咒语驱散了邪恶术士的诅咒。


近义词：antidote, counter-spell, reversal


反义词：charm, spell (in the sense of enchanting)


词性:名词
