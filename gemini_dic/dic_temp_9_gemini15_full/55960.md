/dʒeɪˈeɪs/ |abbr.| DJ| 电台节目主持人，DJ| Disc jockey, a person who introduces and plays recorded music on a radio broadcast or at a nightclub. |例句：He's a famous DJ who spins records at nightclubs all over the world. 他是一位著名的DJ，在世界各地的夜总会播放唱片。|近义词：disc spinner, radio host|反义词：listener, audience


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The DJ plays music all night long. = DJ整夜播放音乐。
That DJ introduces the next song. = 那位DJ介绍下一首歌。


一般过去时:
The DJ played our favorite song last night. = DJ昨晚播放了我们最喜欢的歌。
The famous DJ performed at the festival. = 著名的DJ在节日上表演。


现在完成时:
The DJ has played many different genres of music. = DJ播放过许多不同类型的音乐。
The DJ has just finished his set. = DJ刚刚完成了他的演出。


过去完成时:
The DJ had already played that song before the request. = 在点播之前，DJ已经播放过那首歌了。
Before the party started, the DJ had set up all his equipment. = 派对开始前，DJ已经准备好所有设备。


将来时:
The DJ will play at the concert tomorrow. = DJ明天将在音乐会上演出。
The new DJ will start working next week. = 新的DJ下周开始工作。


虚拟语气:
If the DJ were here, he would play our request. = 如果DJ在这里，他会播放我们的点播。
I wish the DJ played more classic rock. = 我希望DJ播放更多经典摇滚。


被动语态:
The music was played by the DJ. = 音乐是由DJ播放的。
The new songs were introduced by the DJ. = 新歌是由DJ介绍的。


疑问句:
What kind of music does the DJ play? = DJ播放什么类型的音乐？
Who is the DJ tonight? = 今晚的DJ是谁？


否定句:
The DJ didn't play that song. = DJ没有播放那首歌。
The DJ won't play requests tonight. = DJ今晚不会播放点播。
