realisations: /ˌriːəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 实现；领悟；认识 | The act of realizing or understanding something; the process of becoming aware of something.  It can also refer to the finished product or result of a process. |


例句：

His realizations about his past mistakes led him to change his life.  | 他对过去错误的认识使他改变了自己的生活。

The museum's new wing is a stunning realisation of the architect's vision. | 博物馆的新翼是建筑师愿景的惊人实现。


近义词：understandings, insights, achievements, accomplishments, perceptions


反义词：misunderstandings, misinterpretations, failures


词性：名词
