idolized:/ˈaɪd əlaɪzd/| v. |崇拜；偶像化；视为偶像|To admire and respect someone very much, often to an excessive degree.|例句：Millions idolized the pop star.  数百万的人崇拜这位流行歌星。
例句：She idolized her older sister. 她崇拜她姐姐。|近义词：worship, adore, revere|反义词：despise, scorn, disdain|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They idolize the singer. = 他们崇拜这位歌手。
He idolizes his father. = 他崇拜他的父亲。

一般过去时:
She idolized him when she was young. = 她年轻时崇拜他。
We idolized the basketball team. = 我们崇拜这支篮球队。

现在完成时:
I have idolized that actor for years. = 我多年来一直崇拜那位演员。
They have idolized her since her debut. = 自从她出道以来，他们就一直崇拜她。

过去完成时:
He had idolized her for a long time before he met her. = 在他遇到她之前，他已经崇拜她很久了。
We had idolized the band before they became famous. = 在他们成名之前，我们就崇拜这个乐队了。

将来时:
She will continue to idolize him. = 她将继续崇拜他。
They will probably idolize this new artist. = 他们可能会崇拜这位新艺术家。

虚拟语气:
If I met him, I would probably idolize him too. = 如果我遇到他，我也可能会崇拜他。
I wish I hadn't idolized him so much. = 我真希望我不要那么崇拜他。

被动语态:
The singer is idolized by millions. = 这位歌手受到数百万人的崇拜。
The athlete was idolized by his fans. = 这位运动员受到粉丝们的崇拜。

疑问句:
Do you idolize any celebrities? = 你崇拜任何名人吗？
Did they idolize that musician? = 他们崇拜那位音乐家吗？

否定句:
I don't idolize anyone. = 我不崇拜任何人。
They didn't idolize him as much as they said. = 他们并没有像他们所说的那样崇拜他。


PHRASE->
be idolized by = 被…崇拜
idolize someone blindly = 盲目崇拜某人
idolize someone unconditionally = 无条件崇拜某人
become idolized = 成为偶像
idolize someone's work = 崇拜某人的作品
