distributer: /dɪˈstrɪbjuːtər/ | n. |分销商，经销商 |A distributor is a person or company that supplies goods to shops and businesses.  They act as an intermediary between the manufacturer and the end consumer. |Example:  Amazon is a major distributor of books and other products. 亚马逊是书籍和其他产品的**主要分销商**。 The company acts as a distributor for several European car manufacturers. 这家公司是几家欧洲汽车制造商的**经销商**。|近义词：dealer, wholesaler, supplier |反义词：consumer, retailer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The distributor supplies goods to retailers nationwide. = 该分销商向全国各地的零售商供应商品。
This company distributes products internationally. = 这家公司在国际上分销产品。

一般过去时:
The distributor delivered the shipment yesterday. = 分销商昨天送来了货物。
They distributed the flyers throughout the city. = 他们把传单分发到全城。

现在完成时:
The distributor has expanded its network significantly. = 分销商已大幅扩展了其网络。
They have distributed over a million units this year. = 今年他们已分发了超过一百万台设备。

过去完成时:
The retailer had already received the goods before the deadline. = 零售商在截止日期前已经收到了货物。
Before the launch, the company had distributed promotional materials. = 在发布之前，该公司已经分发了宣传材料。

将来时:
The distributor will launch a new product next month. = 分销商将于下个月推出新产品。
We will distribute the profits among the shareholders. = 我们将把利润分配给股东。

虚拟语气:
If we had a better distributor, our sales would be higher. = 如果我们有一个更好的分销商，我们的销量就会更高。
I wish the distributor had provided better customer service. = 我希望分销商能提供更好的客户服务。

被动语态:
The goods were distributed efficiently across the country. = 货物被高效地分发到全国各地。
The new product is being distributed by a major company. = 新产品正在由一家大公司分销。

疑问句:
