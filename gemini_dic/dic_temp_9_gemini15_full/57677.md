drawings:/ˈdrɔːɪŋz/| n.|图画；素描；图纸|Pictures or diagrams made with a pen, pencil, or crayon, often as a preliminary study for a painting or other artwork.  Drawings can also be technical diagrams or blueprints.|例句：The children's drawings adorned the walls of the classroom.孩子们在教室的墙上装饰着他们的图画。

例句：The architect showed us the detailed drawings for the new building. 建筑师向我们展示了新建筑的详细图纸。


近义词：illustrations, sketches, designs, blueprints, diagrams|反义词：无|词性:n.
