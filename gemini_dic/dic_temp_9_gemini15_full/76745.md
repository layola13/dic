gamechanging: /ˈɡeɪmˌtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 改变游戏规则的，具有突破性的 |  This adjective describes something that causes a significant and transformative shift, often in a positive way, disrupting existing norms or practices and creating a new paradigm. It implies a profound impact on the way things are done or understood.  | Example: The invention of the internet was a game-changing event in human history.  互联网的发明是人类历史上具有突破性的大事。  The new software is game-changing for our workflow. 这款新软件彻底改变了我们的工作流程。 | 近义词：revolutionary, transformative, groundbreaking, innovative, disruptive | 反义词：insignificant, inconsequential, incremental, minor
