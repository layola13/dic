pseudo-Norwegian:/ˌsuːdoʊˈnɔːrweɪʒən/| adj.|伪挪威的，假挪威的|Appearing to be Norwegian but not genuinely so; often used to describe something imitating or mimicking aspects of Norwegian culture, language, or style without authenticity.|The band's music, while influenced by Norwegian folk traditions, is considered pseudo-Norwegian by many critics due to its lack of genuine connection to the cultural context.|近义词：faux-Norwegian, sham Norwegian|反义词：authentic Norwegian
