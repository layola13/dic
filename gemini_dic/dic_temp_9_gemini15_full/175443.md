ramification:/ˌræmɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 后果，分支，分枝 | The consequences, implications, or effects of an action or event.  It can also refer to the branching structure of something, such as a tree or river. | Example: The ramifications of the decision were far-reaching.  这个决定的影响深远。

The long-term ramifications of climate change are still being assessed. 气候变化的长期影响仍在评估中。| consequence, implication, effect, outcome |  (no direct antonym, but opposites could be  'lack of consequences' or 'no effect') | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ramifications of this policy are clear. = 这项政策的后果很明显。
What are the ramifications of your actions? = 你行为的后果是什么？


一般过去时:
The ramifications of the accident were devastating. = 事故的后果是毁灭性的。
We didn't fully understand the ramifications of our decision at the time. = 我们当时并没有完全理解我们决定的后果。


现在完成时:
The ramifications of this scandal have been far-reaching. = 这场丑闻的影响已经波及甚广。
Have you considered the ramifications of your proposal? = 你考虑过你的提议的后果吗？


过去完成时:
By then, they had realized the full ramifications of their mistake. = 到那时，他们已经意识到了自己错误的全部后果。
They hadn't foreseen the serious ramifications of their actions. = 他们没有预料到自己行为的严重后果。


将来时:
The ramifications of this new law will be felt for years to come. = 这部新法的后果将在未来数年内显现。
What ramifications will this technology have in the future? =这项技术将来会有什么后果？


虚拟语气:
If we had anticipated the ramifications, we would have acted differently. = 如果我们预料到后果，我们会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had understood the ramifications before I acted. = 我希望在我行动之前就理解了后果。


被动语态:
The ramifications of the situation are being carefully studied. = 这种情况的后果正在被仔细研究。
The potential ramifications were not fully explained to us. = 潜在的后果没有完全向我们解释清楚。


疑问句:
What are the potential ramifications? = 潜在的后果是什么？
What are the social ramifications of this policy? =这项政策的社会后果是什么？


否定句:
There were no negative ramifications from the change. = 变化没有带来任何负面后果。
The company did not fully consider the ramifications of its decision. = 公司没有充分考虑其决定的后果。


PHRASE->
branching ramifications = 分支后果
far-reaching ramifications = 深远的影响
unforeseen ramifications = 不可预见的后果
serious ramifications = 严重的后果
immediate ramifications = 直接后果
