fallbacks:/ˈfɔːlbæks/| n.|后备方案；退路；备用计划|Alternative plans or options to be used if the primary plan fails.|The company has fallbacks in place in case of a sudden market downturn. 这家公司已制定了应对市场突然下滑的后备计划。|Alternatives, backups, contingencies|No direct antonym, but the opposite concept would be "no alternative," "no backup."|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are the fallbacks if the project fails? = 如果项目失败，有哪些后备方案？
Our fallbacks include a simpler design and a reduced scope. = 我们的后备方案包括一个更简单的设计和缩减的范围。


一般过去时:
What were the fallbacks they used during the crisis? = 他们在危机期间使用了哪些后备方案？
Their fallbacks proved insufficient to manage the situation. = 他们的后备方案不足以应对这种情况。


现在完成时:
Have you identified all the potential fallbacks for this initiative? = 你是否已经确定了这项倡议的所有潜在后备方案？
We have already implemented several fallbacks to mitigate risks. = 我们已经实施了几项后备计划来降低风险。


过去完成时:
Had they considered any fallbacks before launching the product? = 在推出产品之前，他们是否考虑过任何后备方案？
They had not developed sufficient fallbacks, resulting in significant losses. = 他们没有制定足够的备用计划，导致了巨大的损失。


将来时:
What will be your fallbacks if the negotiations break down? = 如果谈判破裂，你们的备用方案是什么？
We will have several fallbacks in place to ensure project continuity. = 我们将制定多个后备方案以确保项目的连续性。


虚拟语气:
If we had better fallbacks, the outcome would have been different. = 如果我们有更好的后备方案，结果就会有所不同。
I wish we had considered more fallbacks before making the decision. = 我真希望我们在做决定之前考虑过更多的后备方案。


被动语态:
Several fallbacks were developed to address potential problems. = 制定了多个后备方案来解决潜在问题。
The fallbacks were not adequately tested before implementation. = 后备方案在实施之前没有得到充分的测试。


疑问句:
Are there any fallbacks if the primary supplier fails to deliver? = 如果主要供应商未能交付，还有其他后备方案吗？
What are the key fallbacks for this critical system? = 这个关键系统的关键后备方案是什么？


否定句:
There are no fallbacks if the system crashes. = 如果系统崩溃，就没有后备方案。
They didn't have any fallbacks to rely on during the emergency. = 他们在紧急情况下没有任何可以依赖的后备方案。
