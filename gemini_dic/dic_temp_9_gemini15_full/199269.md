silver-plated:/ˈsɪlvə(r)ˌpleɪtɪd/| adj.|镀银的；银镀层的|Covered with a layer of silver, typically by an electrolytic process.  This process creates a surface layer of silver on a base metal, such as copper or nickel silver, to give the appearance of solid silver at a lower cost.|例句：The cutlery was silver-plated, not solid silver.  这套餐具是镀银的，不是纯银的。

The antique mirror had a beautiful silver-plated frame. 这面古董镜子有一个漂亮的镀银镜框。


近义词：silver-coated, plated|反义词：solid silver, unplated


词性:形容词
