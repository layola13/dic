sabot:/ˈsæboʊ/| n.|木底鞋；破坏行动；故意阻挠|A sabot is a type of shoe traditionally made from wood, often worn by peasants.  The word also refers to an act of sabotage or deliberate obstruction. |例句：The farmer wore wooden sabots to protect his feet in the muddy fields.  农民穿着木底鞋保护他在泥泞田地里的脚。  The workers engaged in a sabot to protest their low wages. 工人们采取破坏行动来抗议他们低廉的工资。|近义词：clog, sabotage, obstruction|反义词：assistance, support
