placards:/ˈplækɑːrdz/| n. |标语牌，告示牌 | A placard is a large notice or sign, often used for advertising or displaying a political message.  Placards are typically displayed in public places to attract attention. |例句：Protesters marched through the streets carrying placards demanding social justice. 抗议者们拿着标语牌游行，要求社会正义。

例句：The shop windows were covered with colorful placards advertising the sale.  商店橱窗贴满了五颜六色的广告牌，宣传促销活动。

近义词：sign, poster, banner, notice

反义词：无

词源:
From Middle French *placard, from Old Italian *piaccardo, from *piazzare "to place," from Latin *platea "broad street, public square".  The meaning evolved from "a notice posted in a public place" to the physical object itself.
