pixie:/ˈpɪksi/| n. |小精灵，仙女；顽皮的孩子|A pixie is a small, mischievous fairy in folklore, often depicted with pointed ears and wings.  Pixies are also sometimes used to describe someone small and mischievous.|例句：The garden was said to be haunted by pixies. 花园据说闹鬼。

例句：She's a little pixie, always getting into trouble. 她是个小淘气鬼，总是惹麻烦。|近义词：fairy, sprite, elf, imp|反义词：giant, ogre
