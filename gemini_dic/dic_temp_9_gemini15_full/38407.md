concern-with:/kənˈsɜːrn wɪð/ | v. phrasal | 与…有关；涉及；关心 |  This phrase indicates a relationship of involvement or relevance between a subject and an object.  It suggests a focus or preoccupation with a particular matter.  The verb "concern" takes a prepositional phrase with "with" to express this relationship. | Example:  His research concerns itself with the effects of climate change. 他的研究与气候变化的影响有关。


Example: The meeting concerned itself with budget allocation.  这次会议涉及预算分配。


近义词: relate to, involve, pertain to, be about, deal with | 反义词:  be unrelated to, be irrelevant to | 词性: phrasal verb
