/ˈɪntræp/| v.| 诱捕；使陷入困境 | to catch or get into a difficult situation from which it is hard to escape | The spider entrapped the fly in its web.  蜘蛛用网诱捕了苍蝇。 He felt entrapped by his responsibilities. 他感到责任压得他喘不过气来。| ensnare, snare, trap | free, liberate | 动词
