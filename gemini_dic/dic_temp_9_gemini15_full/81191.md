grandiose:/ɡrænˈdiəʊs/| adj. |宏伟的；夸大的；浮夸的 | excessively grand or ambitious.  Something grandiose is impressive and magnificent in scale, scope, or design, often to the point of being excessive or pretentious.  It can also describe plans or ideas that are overly ambitious or unrealistic. |例句：His grandiose plans for world domination were quickly thwarted.  他的世界统治宏伟计划很快就被挫败了。

例句：The new hotel boasts a grandiose entrance hall. 这家新酒店拥有一个宏伟的入口大厅。


近义词：magnificent, imposing, spectacular, ambitious, pompous, pretentious

反义词：modest, humble, unassuming, simple, down-to-earth
