英文解析:
Goodness is an exclamation expressing surprise, amazement, or gratitude.  It can also refer to the quality of being good, virtuous, or excellent.  As a plural noun, it emphasizes the abundance or multitude of good qualities or fortunate events.

例句:
The goodness of his heart shone through his actions.  他的善良体现在他的行动中。

The goodness of the food was undeniable. 这食物的好吃是毋庸置疑的。
