gawper:/ˈɡɔːpər/ | n. | 呆头呆脑地看的人，傻眼的人 | A gawper is someone who stares openly and stupidly, often at something unusual or unexpected.  They are characterized by their lack of subtlety or grace in their observation. | Example:  The gawpers lined the street, watching the parade go by.  呆头呆脑的人们排成一行，看着游行队伍走过。 | 近义词：gazer, starer, rubbernecker | 反义词：observer (subtle, discerning) | 词性: 名词
