legitimateness:/ləˌdʒɪtɪmətnəs/| n. |合法性；正当性；合理性|The state or quality of being legitimate.  It refers to the conformity to the law or to rules, or to the moral rightness of something. |例句：The legitimacy of the government is being questioned.  政府的合法性受到了质疑。

例句：The legitimacy of his claim to the throne was widely debated. 他对王位的继承权的合法性受到了广泛的争论。


近义词：validity, justification, rightfulness, authenticity

反义词：illegitimacy, invalidity, illegality


词源：源自于形容词legitimate (合法的，正当的)，加后缀-ness构成名词，表示“状态”或“性质”。 legitimate本身来源于拉丁语词根“legitimus”，意思是“法律的，合法的”。
