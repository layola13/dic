jenkins:/ˈdʒɛŋkɪnz/ | n. |詹金斯（人名）；詹金斯软件 |Jenkins is an open-source automation server.  It's widely used for continuous integration and continuous delivery (CI/CD).| 例句：We use Jenkins to automate our build and deployment process. 我们使用Jenkins来自动化我们的构建和部署流程。  |近义词：Hudson (早期版本)|反义词：无
