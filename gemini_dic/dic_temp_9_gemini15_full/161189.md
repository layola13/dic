posties:/ˈpɒstiz/| n.|邮递员（复数）|The plural of "postie," a colloquial term for a postman or postwoman.  It refers to postal workers who deliver mail.|Example: The posties were delayed by the heavy snow. 邮递员们被大雪耽搁了。|近义词：postmen, postwomen, mail carriers|反义词：无
