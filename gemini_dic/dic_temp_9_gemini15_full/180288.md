refret: /ˌriːˈfret/ | v. |  重新镶嵌琴弦；重新考虑；懊悔 | To restring (a musical instrument), especially a fretted instrument; to reconsider something; to feel remorse or regret. |

例句：

The guitarist had to refret his beloved acoustic guitar. = 吉他手不得不重新镶嵌他心爱的木吉他琴弦。

He refretted his decision to quit his job. = 他重新考虑了他辞职的决定。


近义词：restring, reconsider, repent

反义词：accept, confirm


词性：动词
