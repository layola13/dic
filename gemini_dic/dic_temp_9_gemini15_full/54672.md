disoccupy:/ˌdɪsˈɒkjupaɪ/ | vt. |使…失业；撤离；腾出(空间或时间)| To remove someone from a job or position; to vacate or leave a place; to free up space or time. |例句：The factory closure disoccupied hundreds of workers. 工厂倒闭使数百名工人失业。 The troops disoccupied the building after the mission was completed. 任务完成后，军队撤离了该建筑物。|近义词：unemploy, evacuate, vacate |反义词：employ, occupy
