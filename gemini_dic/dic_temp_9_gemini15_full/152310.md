patting:/ˈpætɪŋ/| v.|轻拍，抚摸|The act of gently stroking or tapping something or someone.  It implies a gentle, soothing, or comforting action.|例句：She was patting the dog's head. 她正在轻拍狗的头。|例句：He patted his pocket, checking for his keys. 他拍了拍口袋，检查钥匙。|近义词：stroking, tapping,caressing|反义词：punching, hitting,slapping


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pats the dog every day. = 他每天都拍拍狗。
She is patting the child's back to comfort him. = 她轻拍孩子的背安慰他。

一般过去时:
I patted him on the shoulder. = 我拍了拍他的肩膀。
She patted the dough to flatten it. = 她拍了拍面团把它压平。

现在完成时:
I have patted the cat many times. = 我已经拍过很多次猫了。
She has been patting her hair all morning. = 她整个上午都在整理她的头发。

过去完成时:
He had patted the horse before mounting it. = 他在骑上马之前拍了拍马。
They had patted down the suspect before searching him. = 他们在搜查嫌疑人之前已经搜了他的身。

将来时:
I will pat the dog when I see him. = 我看到狗的时候会拍拍它。
She will be patting her hair before the party. = 她将在聚会前整理她的头发。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would pat the dog. = 如果我是你，我会拍拍那条狗。
I wish I had patted the baby more gently. = 我希望我当时更温柔地拍拍婴儿。

被动语态:
The dog was patted by the child. = 狗被孩子拍了一下。
The dough is being patted by the baker. = 面团正在被面包师拍打。

疑问句:
Are you patting the cat? = 你在拍猫吗？
Was he patting the table nervously? = 他是不是在紧张地拍桌子？

否定句:
I didn't pat the dog. = 我没有拍那条狗。
She wasn't patting the child's head. = 她没有拍孩子的头。
