midwife:/ˈmɪdwɪf/| n. |助产士，稳婆 | A midwife is a person, typically a woman, trained to assist women in childbirth.  Midwives provide care during pregnancy, labor, delivery, and the postpartum period. They may work in hospitals, birthing centers, or in the woman's home. | Example: The midwife helped the mother deliver a healthy baby.  助产士帮助母亲顺利分娩了一个健康的婴儿。  The expectant mother chose to have a midwife attend her home birth.  准妈妈选择在家分娩，并请了一位助产士。|近义词：obstetrician (医生), doula (陪护)|反义词：N/A|名词
