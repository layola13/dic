out-waved:/ˌaʊtˈweɪvd/| v.|超过，胜过（尤指在数量或程度上）|to surpass or exceed, especially in quantity or degree|The new model out-waved the previous one in terms of sales.  新款车型在销量上超过了之前的型号。|The team out-waved their opponents in scoring.  这个团队在得分上超过了他们的对手。|surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip|underwhelm, underperform

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new technology out-waves the old one in efficiency. = 新技术在效率上超过了旧技术。
His generosity out-waves everyone else's. = 他的慷慨超过了其他所有人。


一般过去时:
Last year's harvest out-waved that of the previous year. = 去年收成超过了前年。
Her performance out-waved all expectations. = 她的表现超出了所有人的预期。


现在完成时:
The company has out-waved its competitors in market share. = 这家公司在市场份额上已经超过了竞争对手。
The innovation has out-waved all previous attempts. = 这项创新已经超过了所有之前的尝试。


过去完成时:
By the end of the game, our team had out-waved the other in points. = 到比赛结束时，我们队在得分上超过了对方。
Before the announcement, the anticipation had out-waved the reality. = 在公告发布之前，期待已经超过了现实。


将来时:
The upcoming product launch will out-wave all current offerings. = 即将推出的产品发布将超过所有现有产品。
We believe our new strategy will out-wave the competition. = 我们相信我们的新战略将胜过竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If we had invested more, we could have out-waved our rivals. = 如果我们投资更多，我们就能超过竞争对手。
I wish our sales figures had out-waved those of our competitors. = 我希望我们的销售额能超过竞争对手。


被动语态:
The previous record was out-waved by the new athlete. =  之前的纪录被这位新的运动员打破了。
Their efforts were out-waved by unforeseen circumstances. = 他们的努力被不可预见的情况所超过。


疑问句:
Does the new design out-wave the old one? = 新设计是否超过了旧设计？
Will this strategy out-wave the competitors' plans? = 这个策略能否超过竞争对手的计划？


否定句:
The small company did not out-wave the industry giant. = 这家小公司没有超过行业巨头。
Our sales did not out-wave our projections. = 我们的销售额没有超过我们的预期。
