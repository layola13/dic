prereceipting: /ˌpriːrɪˈsiːptɪŋ/ | n. | 预收款；预先收据 | The process of receiving payment before goods or services are delivered or rendered.  This practice is common in industries with long lead times or significant upfront costs. | Example: Prereceipting for large construction projects helps mitigate financial risk. = 大型建筑项目的预收款有助于降低财务风险。  The company implemented a new prereceipting system to improve cash flow. = 公司实施了新的预收款系统以改善现金流。 | 近义词：advance payment, prepayment, upfront payment | 反义词：postpayment, arrears payment |  词性:名词
