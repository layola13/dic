morphotic:/mɔːrˈfɑːtɪk/ | adj. |形态的；形态学的| Relating to morphology or form.  The term refers to the form and structure of organisms or their parts.  It's often used in biology and linguistics to describe the study of form and structure. |例句：The morphotic changes in the caterpillar during metamorphosis are remarkable.  毛虫在蜕变过程中形态的变化是显著的。

例句：Morphotic analysis of the fossil revealed details about the extinct species. 对化石的形态分析揭示了有关灭绝物种的细节。


近义词：structural, formal, morphological | 反义词：amorphous, formless

词性：形容词
