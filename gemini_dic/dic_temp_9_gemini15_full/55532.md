distribution:/ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən/| n. | 分布，分配，分发 | The geographical distribution of the species is quite wide.  物种的地理分布范围很广。 |The distribution of wealth in this country is uneven.  这个国家的财富分配不均。| concentration, allocation | collection, gathering | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company distributes its products worldwide. = 这家公司在全球范围内分销其产品。
The teacher distributes the tests to the students. = 老师把试卷分发给学生们。


一般过去时:
They distributed food and water to the victims. = 他们向受害者分发了食物和水。
The company distributed a press release yesterday. = 公司昨天发布了一份新闻稿。


现在完成时:
We have distributed all the flyers. = 我们已经分发了所有传单。
The organization has distributed aid to the affected areas. = 该组织已向受灾地区提供了援助。


过去完成时:
They had distributed the pamphlets before the meeting started. = 他们在会议开始之前已经分发了小册子。
The government had distributed the relief supplies before the storm hit. = 政府在暴风雨来临之前已经分发了救济物资。


将来时:
We will distribute the profits among the shareholders. = 我们将把利润分给股东们。
The company will distribute the new software next week. = 公司下周将发布新的软件。


虚拟语气:
If we had a better distribution system, we would reach more customers. = 如果我们有一个更好的分销系统，我们将能接触到更多的客户。
I wish the distribution of resources were more equitable. = 我希望资源分配更公平。


被动语态:
The goods were distributed efficiently. = 货物得到了有效的分配。
The information was distributed to all employees. = 信息已分发给所有员工。


疑问句:
How is the wealth distribution in this region? = 这个地区的财富分配如何？
What is the distribution channel for this product? = 此产品的分销渠道是什么？


否定句:
The company did not distribute the bonus this year. = 公司今年没有发放奖金。
The leaflets were not distributed effectively. = 传单没有得到有效的分发。
