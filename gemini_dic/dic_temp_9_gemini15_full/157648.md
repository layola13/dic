pitter-patter:/ˈpɪtərˌpætər/| n. v.|淅淅沥沥（的声音）,轻微的脚步声|The sound of light, quick steps or drops of rain.  It can be used as a noun to describe the sound itself, or as a verb to describe the action producing the sound.|例句：The rain was pitter-pattering against the window. 雨淅淅沥沥地打在窗户上。

The children's pitter-patter filled the hallway. 孩子们的轻快脚步声充满了走廊。|近义词：drip, patter, drizzle, tap,  (for footsteps) tiptoe, shuffle|反义词：loud, booming, crashing, thunderous|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain pitter-patters against the window. 雨淅淅沥沥地打在窗户上。
The little feet pitter-patter down the hall. 小小的脚步声淅淅沥沥地走在走廊上。


一般过去时:
The rain pitter-patted against the windowpane all night. 雨整夜淅淅沥沥地敲打着窗玻璃。
The children's pitter-patter faded as they reached their room. 孩子们的轻快脚步声在他们到达房间时消失了。

过去完成时:
The rain had pitter-patted for hours before the storm broke.  暴风雨来临之前，雨已经淅淅沥沥地下了好几个小时。
The children had pitter-patted through the house before settling down to watch TV. 孩子们在安静下来看电视之前，已经在房子里跑来跑去。

虚拟语气:

I wish the rain would pitter-patter instead of pouring down. 我希望雨能淅淅沥沥地下，而不是倾盆而下。


被动语态:
(The word "pitter-patter" is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes an action that is typically not done *to* something.)


疑问句:

否定句:
The rain didn't pitter-patter; it poured. 雨不是淅淅沥沥地下，而是倾盆而下。
I couldn't hear the pitter-patter of the mouse's feet. 我听不见老鼠脚步声。


PHRASE->
(Pitter-patter is generally not used in phrases, it is a word that stands alone to describe the sound.)
