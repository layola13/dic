overstatement:/ˌoʊvərˈsteɪtmənt/ | n. |夸张的说法；言过其实|An overstatement is a statement that exaggerates or magnifies the truth.  It presents something as more significant, impressive, or extreme than it actually is.  This can be done intentionally for effect, or unintentionally due to enthusiasm or bias. |例句：Saying that the movie was the best ever made is a bit of an overstatement.  说这部电影是有史以来最好的电影有点言过其实。

His claims of unparalleled success were a gross overstatement. 他声称取得了无与伦比的成功，这是一种严重的夸大其词。|近义词：exaggeration, hyperbole, inflation |反义词：understatement, downplaying
