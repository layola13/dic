emphasising: /ˈɛmfəsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 强调，着重 |  The act of giving special importance or prominence to something.  It's the present participle of the verb "emphasize". | 例句：He was emphasising the importance of punctuality. 他正在强调准时的重要性。

例句：The teacher was emphasising the need for hard work. 老师正在强调努力工作的重要性。


近义词： stressing, highlighting, underscoring, stressing, accentuating | 反义词： downplaying, minimizing, ignoring, neglecting | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He emphasises the need for patience. 他强调要有耐心。
She emphasises the importance of education. 她强调教育的重要性。


一般过去时:
He emphasised the urgency of the situation. 他强调了情况的紧急性。
She emphasised the need for immediate action. 她强调需要立即采取行动。


现在完成时:
They have emphasised the risks involved. 他们已经强调了所涉及的风险。
She has emphasised her commitment to the project. 她已经强调了她对项目的承诺。


过去完成时:
He had emphasised the dangers many times before. 他之前已经多次强调过危险。
She had emphasised the importance of safety procedures. 她已经强调了安全程序的重要性。


将来时:
He will emphasise the key points in his presentation. 他将在演示文稿中强调要点。
She will emphasise the benefits of the new policy. 她将强调新政策的好处。


虚拟语气:
If he emphasised the problem, they might understand. 如果他强调这个问题，他们可能会理解。
I wish she had emphasised the deadline earlier. 我希望她早点强调截止日期。


被动语态:
The importance of teamwork was emphasised in the meeting. 在会议上强调了团队合作的重要性。
The need for change is being emphasised by the government. 政府正在强调改变的必要性。


疑问句:
What points did he emphasise in his speech? 他在演讲中强调了哪些要点？
What aspects of the problem are you emphasising? 你正在强调问题的哪些方面？


否定句:
He didn't emphasise the difficulties. 他没有强调困难。
She didn't emphasise the negative aspects. 她没有强调消极方面。
