rehabilitates:/ˌriːhəˈbɪlɪteɪt/| vt.|康复；修复；恢复名誉|To restore someone to health or normal life through training and therapy after imprisonment, addiction, or illness.  To restore something damaged, neglected, or ruined to a good state of repair.|例句：The program rehabilitates drug addicts.  这个项目帮助吸毒者康复。

例句：The old building has been rehabilitated and is now a museum. 这栋老建筑经过修复，现在是一座博物馆。


近义词：restore, recover, reclaim, renovate

反义词：damage, destroy, ruin, impair

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The therapist rehabilitates patients with physical injuries. = 治疗师帮助患有身体损伤的病人康复。
The company rehabilitates old buildings. = 公司修复旧建筑。


一般过去时:
The prison program rehabilitated many inmates. = 监狱项目使许多囚犯改过自新。
She rehabilitated her reputation after the scandal. = 丑闻后她恢复了自己的名誉。


现在完成时:
They have rehabilitated the damaged bridge. = 他们已经修复了受损的桥梁。
The hospital has rehabilitated hundreds of patients this year. = 今年医院已经使数百名病人康复。


过去完成时:
He had rehabilitated himself before he found a new job. = 他在找到新工作之前已经重新振作起来了。
The city had rehabilitated the park before the summer. = 城市在夏天之前已经修复了公园。


将来时:
The government will rehabilitate the infrastructure. = 政府将修复基础设施。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:
