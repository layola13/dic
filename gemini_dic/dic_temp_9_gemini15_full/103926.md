judgemented:/ˈdʒʌdʒməntɪd/| adj.|  被判断的；已做出判断的 |  This word is rarely used and often considered incorrect.  It implies that something has been subjected to judgment or that a judgment has been made about it. However,  "judged" or other more appropriate phrasing is generally preferred. | The painting was judged to be a masterpiece. = 这幅画被认为是杰作。  The situation was judged carefully before a decision was made. = 在做出决定之前，情况经过仔细判断。 | judged, assessed, evaluated | unjudged, unevaluated | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He judges people too quickly. = 他判断人太快了。
She judges the quality of the products. = 她判断产品的质量。


一般过去时:
The judge judged the case fairly. = 法官公正地审理了此案。
He judged the distance incorrectly. = 他判断距离不准确。


现在完成时:
The jury has judged the defendant guilty. = 陪审团已判定被告有罪。
She has judged many baking competitions. = 她已经评判过许多烘焙比赛。


过去完成时:
They had judged the situation to be hopeless. = 他们之前已经判断情况毫无希望。
He had judged the weight of the package incorrectly. = 他之前已经错误地判断了包裹的重量。


将来时:
The judges will judge the contestants tomorrow. = 评委们明天将对参赛者进行评判。
She will judge the entries based on creativity. = 她将根据创造力来评判参赛作品。


虚拟语气:
If I judged correctly, we would have won. = 如果我判断正确，我们就赢了。
If she had judged better, the outcome might have been different. = 如果她判断得更好，结果可能会有所不同。


被动语态:
The winner was judged by a panel of experts. = 获胜者由专家组评选。
The painting is being judged for its artistic merit. = 这幅画正在接受艺术价值的评判。


疑问句:
How did you judge the situation? = 你如何判断这种情况？
How will the judges judge the performance? = 评委将如何评价表演？


否定句:
I didn't judge him harshly. = 我没有严厉地批评他。
She didn't judge the book by its cover. = 她没有以貌取人。


PHRASE->

judge by appearance = 以貌取人
judge for oneself = 自己判断
judge impartially = 公正地判断
judge severely = 严厉地判断
pass judgment = 下判断
