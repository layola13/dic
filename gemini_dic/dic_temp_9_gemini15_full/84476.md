halt:/hɔːlt/| /hɑːlt/| v. n.|停止；中断；暂停；终止|To bring or come to a stop; cease.|The train halted suddenly. 火车突然停了下来。The negotiations have come to a halt. 谈判陷入了僵局。|stop, cease, arrest|continue, proceed|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He halts his car at the red light. = 他在红灯处停车。
They halt their work for lunch. = 他们停下手头的工作去吃午饭。

一般过去时:
The soldiers halted their advance. = 士兵们停止了前进。
She halted abruptly in the middle of her speech. = 她突然在演讲中途停了下来。

现在完成时:
The project has halted due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，项目已经停止了。
We have halted production until further notice. = 我们已暂停生产，直至另行通知。

过去完成时:
They had halted their search before nightfall. = 他们在夜幕降临前停止了搜寻。
The army had halted its offensive before reinforcements arrived. = 援军到达之前，军队已经停止了进攻。

将来时:
We will halt the construction if the weather turns bad. = 如果天气变坏，我们将停止施工。
The government will halt the import of certain goods. = 政府将停止某些商品的进口。

虚拟语气:
If he had halted the car earlier, the accident could have been avoided. = 如果他早点停车，事故是可以避免的。
I wish they had halted the project before it was too late. = 我真希望他们在为时已晚之前就停止了这个项目。

被动语态:
The parade was halted by the heavy rain. = 游行因大雨而停止了。
Production was halted because of a machine malfunction. = 由于机器故障，生产被停止了。

疑问句:
Will the meeting be halted? = 会议会暂停吗？
Should we halt the experiment now? = 我们现在应该停止实验吗？

否定句:
He didn't halt his car in time. = 他没有及时停车。
They won't halt their efforts despite the setbacks. = 尽管遭遇挫折，他们也不会停止努力。
