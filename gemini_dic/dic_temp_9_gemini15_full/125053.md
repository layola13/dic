mules:/mjuːlz/| n.|骡子；木屐；走私者；(俚语)拖鞋|A mule is the offspring of a male donkey (jack) and a female horse (mare).  They are known for their strength and endurance.  The word can also refer to a type of clog, or to someone who smuggles goods, especially drugs.|例句：The farmer used mules to plow his fields.  这个农民用骡子耕地。


例句：The police arrested several mules involved in a drug trafficking operation.  警方逮捕了几名参与贩毒行动的走私者。


近义词：pack animal (as a beast of burden), smuggler, transporter|反义词：horse, donkey (depending on the context)
