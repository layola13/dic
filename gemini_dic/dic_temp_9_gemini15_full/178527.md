reconversion:/ˌriːkənˈvɜːrʒən/| n. |再转换；重新转换；复原|The process of converting something back to its original state or a different state.  It often implies a change back to a previous form or function.  The term is used in various contexts, including industrial processes, economic policy, and data processing. |例句：The factory underwent a reconversion to produce civilian goods after the war.  战后工厂进行了改造以生产民用商品。

The reconversion of the old building into a modern apartment complex was a success. 老建筑改造成现代公寓楼的项目很成功。

|近义词：transformation, reversion, conversion (in some contexts)|反义词：original form (depending on context) |名词
