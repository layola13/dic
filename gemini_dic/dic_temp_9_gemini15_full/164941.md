pregrievance:/ˌpriːˈɡriːvəns/ | n. | 预先申诉；事先抱怨 | A complaint or grievance made before the event or situation occurs.  It often refers to a preemptive complaint, an anticipated problem, or a proactive expression of dissatisfaction before it manifests. |  The employee submitted a pregrievance about the upcoming changes to the company's policy. 员工就公司即将改变的政策提交了一份预先申诉。  |近义词：precomplaint, anticipation of complaint, early warning | 反义词：post-grievance, after-the-fact complaint | 名词
