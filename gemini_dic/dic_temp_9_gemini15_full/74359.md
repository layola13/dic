frail:/freɪl/| adj. |虚弱的，脆弱的，易碎的 |weak and delicate; easily damaged or broken| The frail old woman needed help to walk.  这位虚弱的老妇人需要帮助才能行走。|The frail vase shattered when it fell.  那个脆弱的花瓶掉下来摔碎了。|strong, robust, sturdy |robust, strong|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is frail and needs assistance. 他很虚弱，需要帮助。
The old bridge is frail and needs repair. 这座旧桥很脆弱，需要维修。

一般过去时:
She was frail after her illness. 她生病后身体虚弱。
The antique chair was frail and easily broken. 这把古董椅子很脆弱，很容易坏。

现在完成时:
He has become frail in his old age. 他上了年纪后变得虚弱了。
The structure has become frail due to years of neglect. 由于多年的忽视，结构变得脆弱了。

过去完成时:
She had been frail for many years before she passed away. 在去世之前，她虚弱了很多年。
The building had become frail and unsafe before the demolition. 在拆除之前，这座建筑已经变得脆弱和不安全。

将来时:
He will become more frail as he gets older. 他年纪越大，就会越虚弱。
The bridge will become increasingly frail without repairs. 这座桥如果不维修，将会越来越脆弱。

虚拟语气:
If he were not so frail, he could travel more. 如果他不那么虚弱，他就能多旅行。
If the vase were not so frail, it wouldn't have broken. 如果花瓶不那么脆弱，就不会碎了。

被动语态:
His health is considered frail by the doctors. 医生认为他的健康状况很虚弱。
The old house was deemed frail and unfit for habitation. 那栋老房子被认为很脆弱，不适合居住。

疑问句:
Is he frail? 他虚弱吗？
Is the structure frail enough to collapse?  结构脆弱到足以坍塌吗？

否定句:
He is not frail; he is quite strong. 他不虚弱；他相当强壮。
The building is not frail; it's built to last. 这栋建筑不脆弱；它是建来经久的。
