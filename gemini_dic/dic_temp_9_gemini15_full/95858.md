incorruptibility: /ˌɪnˌkɔːrʌpˈtɪbɪləti/ | n. |不腐败；清廉；正直 | The quality or state of being incorruptible; the inability to be bribed or corrupted.  It refers to a steadfast resistance to bribery, temptation, or moral compromise. |例句：His incorruptibility was legendary; no amount of money could sway his judgment.  他的清廉是传奇般的；任何钱财都无法左右他的判断。

His incorruptibility in the face of immense pressure earned him the respect of his peers. 他面对巨大压力时的清廉赢得了同行的尊重。|近义词：integrity, honesty, probity, uprightness |反义词：corruption, venality, depravity
