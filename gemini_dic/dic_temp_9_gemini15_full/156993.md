pilling:/ˈpɪlɪŋ/| n. v.|起球，起毛球|The formation of small balls of fiber on the surface of a fabric.  This is a common problem with knitted or woven fabrics, especially those made from natural fibers like wool or cotton.|例句：The sweater is starting to pill after only a few washes. 这件毛衣只洗了几次就开始起球了。  The fabric pilled badly after just one wash.  这布料洗一次就起球得很厉害。|近义词：fuzzing, bobbling|反义词:smooth
