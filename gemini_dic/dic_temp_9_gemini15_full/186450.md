ribosomes:/ˈraɪbəsoʊmz/ | n. |核糖体 |Complex cellular structures composed of RNA and proteins, responsible for protein synthesis. Ribosomes are found in all living cells and are essential for life. They translate the genetic code from messenger RNA (mRNA) into proteins.  | Example:  Ribosomes are the protein factories of the cell. 核糖体是细胞的蛋白质工厂。

The ribosomes in the cytoplasm synthesize proteins for use within the cell. 细胞质中的核糖体合成供细胞内使用的蛋白质。|近义词:protein synthesizers|反义词: N/A
