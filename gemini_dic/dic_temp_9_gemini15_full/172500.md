pyre:/ˈpaɪər/| n.|火葬柴堆；烈火；大火|A pyre is a large pile of wood built for burning a corpse, especially as part of a funeral rite.  It can also refer to any large fire.|例句：The body was laid upon the pyre. 遗体被放在火葬柴堆上。

例句：The burning pyre illuminated the night sky.熊熊大火照亮了夜空。|近义词：fire, bonfire, stake|反义词：extinction, quenching|名词
