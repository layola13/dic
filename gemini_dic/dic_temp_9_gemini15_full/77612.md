geez:/dʒiːz/| int.|哎呀，天哪，我的天|An exclamation expressing surprise, annoyance, or disbelief.  It is a milder form of profanity.|例句：Geez, that was close! 哎呀，真是太险了！
例句：Geez, look at the time! 我的天，看看时间！|近义词：wow, gosh, man, my god|反义词:无


USAGE->
(Geez is an interjection, so it doesn't conjugate like verbs or nouns.  It doesn't have tenses or other grammatical forms.)


PHRASE->
Geez Louise:  (A more emphatic version of "Geez")  哎呀，我的老天！
Geez, I'm tired: 哎呀，我太累了！
Geez, that's a lot of work: 哎呀，那可是很多工作啊！
Geez, what a mess!: 我的天，真是一团糟!
Geez, you scared me!: 我的天，你吓到我了!
