outfencing: /ˌaʊtˈfɛnsɪŋ/ | n. |  围墙外；超越围墙；(尤指为保护而建造的)防护围栏 |  The act of building a fence around a property, especially one that extends beyond the property line or is designed to deter trespassers or animals. It can also refer to surpassing or exceeding a boundary or limit, figuratively. | 例句：The farmer built an outfencing to keep his sheep from wandering onto the neighbor's land.  = 这位农民建造了一道围墙以防止他的羊跑到邻居的土地上。


The new security measures included an extensive outfencing around the entire facility. = 新的安全措施包括在整个设施周围建造一个大型防护围栏。


近义词：fencing, enclosure, perimeter, barricade,  (figuratively) surpassing, exceeding


反义词：opening, breaching, entering (figuratively)  undermining, failing to reach


词性: 名词

词源:  The word "outfencing" is a relatively modern combination of "out-" (meaning beyond or exceeding) and "fencing" (the act of building fences).  It reflects the extension of fencing beyond a typical or expected boundary.
