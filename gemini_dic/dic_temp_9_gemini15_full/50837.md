desecrate:/ˈdɛsɪkreɪt/| vt.|亵渎，玷污，破坏（圣物等）|To violate the sacredness of (something); to treat with disrespect.|The vandals desecrated the cemetery by defacing the tombstones. 暴徒们毁坏墓碑，亵渎了墓地。|profane, violate, defile|revere, venerate, sanctify|动词
