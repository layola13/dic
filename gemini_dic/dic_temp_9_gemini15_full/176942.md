realize:/ˈriːəlaɪz/ | vt. |意识到，实现，了解 | To understand a situation, fact, or feeling completely; to achieve or fulfill something.  |例句：I suddenly realized my mistake. 我突然意识到我的错误了。|例句：He realized his ambition to become a doctor. 他实现了成为医生的抱负。|近义词：understand, comprehend, achieve, accomplish |反义词：misunderstand, ignore, fail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I realize the importance of this project. = 我意识到这个项目的重要性。
She realizes her dreams are within reach. = 她意识到她的梦想触手可及。


一般过去时:
He realized he was lost. = 他意识到自己迷路了。
They realized their mistake too late. = 他们太迟才意识到他们的错误。


现在完成时:
I have realized the potential of this technology. = 我已经意识到了这项技术的潜力。
She has realized her lifelong goal of writing a novel. = 她已经实现了毕生的写作目标。


过去完成时:
He realized he had made a serious error. = 他意识到自己犯了一个严重的错误。
They realized they had missed the train. = 他们意识到他们错过了火车。


将来时:
I will realize my potential in the future. = 我将来会实现我的潜力。
She will realize the benefits of her hard work soon. = 她很快就会体会到努力工作的益处。


虚拟语气:
If I had realized the consequences, I would have acted differently. = 如果我意识到了后果，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had realized it sooner. = 我希望我早点意识到这一点。


被动语态:
The problem was realized by the team. = 这个问题被团队意识到了。
The potential of the project was finally realized. = 项目的潜力最终得到了实现。


疑问句:
Do you realize the danger? = 你意识到危险了吗？
Does she realize how much I care? = 她意识到我有多在乎她吗？


否定句:
I didn't realize how late it was. = 我没意识到已经这么晚了。
He didn't realize the impact of his actions. = 他没有意识到他的行为的影响。


PHRASE->
realize a dream = 实现梦想
realize a profit = 实现盈利
realize one's potential = 发挥潜能
realize the importance of = 意识到...的重要性
realize an ambition = 实现抱负
