greed's: /ɡriːdz/ | n. | 贪婪的；贪欲的 | Greed's insatiable nature often leads to ruin. 贪婪的贪得无厌的本性往往导致毁灭。| 近义词：avarice, covetousness, rapacity | 反义词：generosity, altruism, selflessness
