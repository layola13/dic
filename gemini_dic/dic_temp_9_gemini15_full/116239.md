mass:/mæs/| n.|质量；大量；民众；弥撒|A large amount of something; a body of matter; a religious service in the Christian Church.|例句：The mass of the Earth is much greater than that of the Moon. 地球的质量远大于月球。|例句： A mass of people gathered in the square. 大批人群聚集在广场上。|近义词： bulk, quantity, multitude, congregation|反义词：少量，个体
