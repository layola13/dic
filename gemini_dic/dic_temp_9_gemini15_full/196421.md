serpentinous: /ˌsɜːrpənˈtɪnəs/ | adj. | 蛇形的；蜿蜒的；狡猾的；复杂的 |

英文解析:  Resembling a serpent; winding and twisting like a snake.  Can also refer to something devious or deceitful, like a serpent's cunning.

例句:

The road followed a serpentinous path through the mountains.  这条路蜿蜒穿过群山。

The negotiations were characterized by serpentinous tactics from both sides. 双方谈判都以狡猾的策略为特征。


近义词: winding, meandering, tortuous, devious, tricky, convoluted

反义词: straight, direct, straightforward, honest, simple


词性:  adjective
