involvements:/ɪnˈvɒlvmənts/ | n. |参与；牵涉；卷入 |The act or state of being involved.  It refers to participation in an activity, event, or situation, often implying a degree of commitment or responsibility. |例句：His involvements in the scandal were widely reported. 他的卷入丑闻被广泛报道。

例句：Her many involvements left her little free time.  她的诸多参与让她几乎没有空闲时间。


近义词：participation, engagement, entanglements, implication


反义词：disengagement, detachment, uninvolvement


词性：名词
