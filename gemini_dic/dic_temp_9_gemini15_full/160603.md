port:/pɔːrt/ /pɔːrt/ | n. v. | 港口；港湾；（葡萄酒的）酒；（电脑的）端口；搬运；携带 | A port is a town or city with a harbor where ships can load and unload goods.  A port can also refer to a type of wine, or a connector on a computer.  As a verb, it means to carry or transport something. | 例句：The ship docked at the busy port of Shanghai. 这艘船停靠在上海繁忙的港口。 The red wine was a fine port. 这瓶红酒是一款上好的波特酒。| 近义词：harbor, haven, terminal, outlet | 反义词：inland, interior


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The ship ports at the harbor every Tuesday. = 这艘船每周二停靠在港口。
He ports his goods to various cities. = 他把货物运送到各个城市。

一般过去时:
They ported the containers to the warehouse. = 他们把集装箱运送到仓库。
The ship ported in Rotterdam last week. = 这艘船上周停靠在鹿特丹。

现在完成时:
We have ported all the data to the new server. = 我们已经将所有数据传输到新的服务器。
They have ported the game to multiple platforms. = 他们已将游戏移植到多个平台。

过去完成时:
They had ported the goods before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经运送了货物。
The company had ported the software to Windows before releasing it. = 公司在发布软件之前已将其移植到Windows系统。

将来时:
They will port the application to iOS next year. = 他们明年将把应用程序移植到 iOS 系统。
We will port the goods to the new warehouse next week. = 我们下周将货物运送到新的仓库。

虚拟语气:
If we had ported the data earlier, we would have avoided the problem. = 如果我们早点传输数据，就能避免这个问题。
It would be beneficial if they ported the game to more platforms. = 如果他们将游戏移植到更多平台，将大有裨益。

被动语态:
The goods were ported to the destination safely. = 货物安全地运送到了目的地。
The data is being ported to the cloud server. = 数据正在被传输到云服务器。

疑问句:
Where does the ship port? = 这艘船停靠在哪里？
Will they port the software to Linux? = 他们会将软件移植到Linux系统吗？

否定句:
They didn't port the data on time. = 他们没有按时传输数据。
The company won't port the game to older consoles. = 公司不会将游戏移植到旧款游戏机。

词源:

The word "port" has a rich history, ultimately deriving from the Latin word "portus," meaning "harbor" or "haven."  This Latin word spread through various Romance languages before entering English. Its meaning has expanded over time to include the various uses described above.
