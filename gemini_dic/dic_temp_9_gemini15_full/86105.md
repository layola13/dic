heartache:/ˈhɑːrteɪk/ | n. | 心痛；伤心；悲痛 | A feeling of deep sadness and emotional pain, often caused by loss, disappointment, or grief.  | Example:  The heartache of losing a loved one is unbearable.  失去亲人的痛苦难以承受。  |近义词：anguish, sorrow, grief, distress | 反义词：joy, happiness, elation | 名词
