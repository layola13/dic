dilemmas:/dɪˈlɛməz/| n.|困境，进退两难的局面|a situation in which a difficult choice has to be made between two or more alternatives, especially equally undesirable ones.  |例句：He's in a dilemma about whether to take the job or not. 他左右为难，不知道是否该接受这份工作。

例句：The company faces a serious dilemma: whether to invest in new technology or lay off workers. 公司面临一个严重的困境：是投资新技术还是裁员。


近义词：predicament, quandary, plight, problem, difficulty


反义词：easy situation, straightforward choice


词性:名词
