impassivity:/ˌɪmpæˈsɪvɪti/| n. |冷漠；无动于衷；平静|The quality or state of being impassive; absence of feeling or expression; calmness; composure.  Impassivity is a state of not showing any emotion, even when something significant happens. It can be a conscious choice or a result of emotional numbness.|例句：Her impassivity in the face of such tragedy was unsettling.  面对如此悲剧，她的冷漠令人不安。

例句：The judge maintained an impassivity throughout the proceedings, giving no indication of his opinion.  法官在整个诉讼过程中保持冷漠，没有显示出他的意见。


近义词：apathy, indifference, calmness, composure, stoicism|反义词：emotionality, excitability, passion, feeling
