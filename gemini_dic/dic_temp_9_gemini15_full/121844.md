misrepeat:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpiːt/| vt.| 重复错误地；错误地复述 | To repeat something incorrectly or inaccurately.  This implies a failure to accurately reproduce the original information, often due to misunderstanding or memory lapse. | 例句：He misrepeated the instructions, leading to a significant error in the project. 他错误地重复了指示，导致项目出现重大错误。


He misrepeated the witness's testimony, causing confusion in the courtroom. 他错误地复述了证人的证词，导致法庭一片混乱。


近义词：misquote, misrepresent, paraphrase incorrectly, distort | 反义词：repeat accurately, reiterate precisely


词性：动词
