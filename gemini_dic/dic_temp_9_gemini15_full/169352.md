prompture:/ˈprɒmptʃər/| n. |提示；提示符；激发因素|A prompture is something that serves as a reminder or stimulus to action or thought.  It can be a cue, a suggestion, or an incentive.|例句：The teacher's question served as a prompture for further discussion.  老师的问题促使了进一步的讨论。|近义词：cue, stimulus, incentive, reminder|反义词：inhibition, deterrent
