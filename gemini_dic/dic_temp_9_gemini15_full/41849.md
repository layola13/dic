corymbose:/ˈkɒrɪmboʊs/| adj. |伞房花序的；伞房状的 |Having flower clusters arranged in a corymb.  A corymb is a flat-topped or slightly rounded inflorescence where the individual flower stalks arise from different points on the stem but reach approximately the same height.  This creates a relatively even, somewhat umbrella-like appearance.| The flowering cherry tree had corymbose clusters of blossoms.  这棵樱花树开满了伞房状的花簇。|racemose, umbel-like |  |  adj.
