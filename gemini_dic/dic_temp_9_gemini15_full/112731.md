lozenges:/ˈlɒzɪndʒɪz/| n.|锭剂，薄荷糖|A lozenge is a small, medicated tablet or sweet that dissolves slowly in the mouth.  They are often used to relieve sore throats or coughs.|例句：She sucked on a menthol lozenge to soothe her sore throat. 她含了一片薄荷锭来缓解喉咙痛。

例句：The doctor prescribed some lozenges to help with my cough. 医生开了些锭剂来帮助我止咳。|近义词：tablet, pastille, troche|反义词：None
