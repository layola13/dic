farm-out: /fɑːrm aʊt/ | vt. |分包；外包；转包 | To give a task or contract to an outside company or individual.  |例句：The company farmed out the manufacturing of its products to a cheaper overseas supplier. 公司将其产品的制造业务外包给了海外一家更便宜的供应商。

例句：We decided to farm out the software development to a specialist firm. 我们决定将软件开发外包给一家专业公司。


近义词：subcontract, outsource, contract out | 反义词：insource, handle internally | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company farms out its accounting work to a specialist firm. = 这家公司将其会计工作外包给一家专业公司。
They farm out much of their design work to freelancers. = 他们将大部分设计工作外包给自由职业者。

一般过去时:
Last year, we farmed out the project to a smaller company. = 去年，我们将项目外包给了一家规模较小的公司。
The construction company farmed out the electrical work to a subcontractor. = 这家建筑公司将电气工程外包给了一家分包商。

现在完成时:
They have farmed out several projects this year to improve efficiency. = 他们今年已经外包了好几个项目以提高效率。
We have farmed out the marketing campaign to a digital agency. = 我们已经将营销活动外包给了一家数字营销公司。

过去完成时:
Before the deadline, they had farmed out the remaining tasks to different teams. = 在截止日期之前，他们已将剩余的任务外包给不同的团队。
The company had farmed out similar projects in the past with positive results. = 该公司过去曾外包过类似的项目，并取得了积极的成果。

将来时:
We will farm out the data analysis to a consultant next month. = 我们下个月将数据分析外包给一位顾问。
The project manager said they will farm out the printing work to a local printer. = 项目经理说他们将把印刷工作外包给当地一家印刷厂。

虚拟语气:
If we farmed out the production, we could reduce costs significantly. = 如果我们将生产外包，我们可以大幅降低成本。
I wish we had farmed out the design work earlier; it would have saved us time. = 我希望我们早点把设计工作外包出去；那样可以节省我们的时间。

被动语态:
The project was farmed out to a team of experienced engineers. = 该项目外包给了一个经验丰富的工程师团队。
The manufacturing process is being farmed out to several different factories. = 制造过程正在外包给几个不同的工厂。

疑问句:
Will the company farm out the research to an external institution? = 公司会将研究外包给外部机构吗？
Have they decided to farm out the software development yet? = 他们是否已经决定将软件开发外包出去？

否定句:
The company does not farm out its core operations. = 公司不将其核心业务外包。
We didn't farm out the translation work; we did it in-house. = 我们没有将翻译工作外包出去；我们是内部完成的。


PHRASE->
farm out work = 外包工作
farm out a contract = 将合同外包
farm out the project = 外包项目
farm out the task = 外包任务
farm out the manufacturing = 外包制造


词源:
The term "farm out" originates from the agricultural practice of renting out land to tenant farmers.  The metaphorical extension to business is a relatively modern usage, reflecting the increasing prevalence of outsourcing.
