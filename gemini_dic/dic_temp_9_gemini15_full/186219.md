/raɪˈnæstər/| n. |犀牛鸟|Rhinaster is a genus of birds in the family Bucerotidae, commonly known as rhinoceros hornbills.  They are characterized by a large, casque-like structure on their beak.  The casque's function is not fully understood but is believed to play a role in communication and possibly thermoregulation.  Rhinoceros hornbills inhabit tropical forests of Southeast Asia.|The Rhinaster hornbill is a striking bird due to its unique casque.|近义词：hornbill (泛指),|反义词：无
