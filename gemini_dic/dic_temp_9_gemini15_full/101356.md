ionics:/aɪˈɒnɪks/| n.|离子学，离子；离子物质|The study of ions and their behavior in solutions and other media.  It encompasses various aspects of ionic compounds, including their properties, reactions, and applications. | Example:  Ionics is a crucial field in the development of advanced batteries. 离子学是先进电池研发中的一个关键领域。|近义词：electrochemistry (部分重叠)|反义词：无
