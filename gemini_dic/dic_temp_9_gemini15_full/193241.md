seed-bed:/ˈsiːdbed/| n. |苗床；种子床；培养基；孕育环境|A seedbed is a prepared area of ground where seeds are sown. It can also refer to a favorable environment for the development of something.|例句：The farmer carefully prepared the seedbed before planting the seeds.  农民在播种前仔细准备了苗床。|例句：The fertile soil provided a rich seedbed for the young plants. 肥沃的土壤为幼苗提供了丰富的生长环境。|近义词：nursery, breeding ground|反义词：harvest, reaping ground
