candlewickings:/ˈkændəlˌwɪkɪŋz/| n.|烛芯花纹，烛芯纹理|A type of fabric with a raised pattern resembling candlewick, often used in home décor.  The pattern is created by loops of yarn that are left on the surface of the fabric.|例句：The quilt was made from beautiful candlewickings, giving it a textured appearance. 这床被子是用美丽的烛芯花纹布料做的，使它看起来很有质感。  The candlewicking on the tablecloth added a touch of rustic charm. 桌布上的烛芯花纹增添了一丝乡村的魅力。|近义词：texture, pattern, embroidery|反义词：plain, smooth
