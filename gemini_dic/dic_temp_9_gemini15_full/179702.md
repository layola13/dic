reentered:/ˌriːˈɛntərd/| v. |重新进入，再次进入|To enter again; to go back into.|The astronauts reentered the Earth's atmosphere after their mission. 宇航员完成任务后重新进入地球大气层。 The thief reentered the house after the police left. 警察离开后，小偷又重新进入了房子。|近义词：re-enter, return to, go back into |反义词：exit, leave, depart


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reenters the room every five minutes. 他每五分钟就重新进入房间。
She reenters the competition next year. 她明年将重新参加比赛。


一般过去时:
They reentered the building after the alarm sounded. 警报响起后，他们重新进入了大楼。
He reentered the race after a brief pause. 他短暂暂停后重新加入了比赛。


现在完成时:
She has reentered the university after a long break. 她长时间休学后重新回到了大学。
The company has reentered the market with a new product. 公司带着新产品重新进入了市场。


过去完成时:
He had reentered the password multiple times before it was accepted. 他在密码被接受之前已经多次重新输入了密码。
After she had reentered the data, the program worked correctly. 她重新输入数据后，程序运行正确了。


将来时:
They will reenter the competition next month. 他们下个月将重新参加比赛。
The spacecraft will reenter the atmosphere tomorrow. 宇宙飞船将于明天重新进入大气层。


虚拟语气:

I wish I had reentered the contest; I might have won. 我真希望我重新参加了比赛；我可能就赢了。


被动语态:
