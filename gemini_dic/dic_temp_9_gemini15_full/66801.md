expandibility: /ɪkˌspændəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可扩展性，可扩张性 | The ability to be expanded or enlarged; the capacity for growth or development.  The degree to which a system can be expanded. |例句：The expandibility of the software allows for easy integration of new features.  软件的可扩展性允许轻松集成新功能。

The expandibility of the network is crucial for accommodating future growth. 网络的可扩展性对于适应未来的增长至关重要。


近义词：extensibility, scalability, adaptability, flexibility | 反义词：rigidity, inflexibility, limitation


词源:  Derived from the verb "expand," ultimately from the Latin "expandere" (to spread out).  The suffix "-ibility" indicates the capacity or capability of being expanded.
