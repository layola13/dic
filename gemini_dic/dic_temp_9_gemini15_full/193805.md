self-deprecations:/ˌsɛlfˌdɛprɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n.|自谦；自贬；自我贬低|The act of belittling or undervaluing oneself.  It can be a form of humor, a way to connect with others, or a manifestation of low self-esteem. |例句：Her self-deprecations were often hilarious, making her instantly likable.  她的自谦常常很滑稽，让她立刻变得讨人喜欢。

例句：His constant self-deprecations masked a deep-seated insecurity. 他不断地自贬掩盖着内心深处的不安全感。


近义词：humility, modesty, self-criticism, downplaying

反义词：self-aggrandizement, boasting, arrogance, conceit

词性：名词 (plural)
