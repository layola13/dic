problematic:/ˌprɒbləˈmætɪk/ /ˌprɑːbləˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 有问题的；成问题的；引起麻烦的 |  Difficult to deal with or solve; causing problems.  |例句：The project proved to be more problematic than we had anticipated.  这个项目被证明比我们预期的更棘手。

例句：His behavior is problematic and needs addressing. 他的行为有问题，需要解决。

近义词：troublesome, difficult, challenging, complex,  delicate,  controversial

反义词：easy, simple, straightforward, uncomplicated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The situation is problematic. = 情况很棘手。
His attitude is problematic. = 他的态度有问题。


一般过去时:
The negotiations were problematic. = 谈判过程很棘手。
The relationship proved problematic. = 这段关系证明是有问题的。


现在完成时:
The project has been problematic from the start. = 这个项目从一开始就一直很棘手。
The discussions have become increasingly problematic. = 讨论变得越来越棘手。


过去完成时:
The situation had already become problematic before the meeting. = 会议之前情况就已经很棘手了。
The team had encountered problematic issues in the previous phase. = 团队在上一阶段遇到了棘手的问题。


将来时:
The implementation will likely be problematic. = 实施很可能会遇到问题。
The outcome may prove problematic. = 结果可能会成问题。


虚拟语气:
If the design were less problematic, we could proceed. = 如果设计不那么棘手，我们就能继续进行。
I wish the situation were less problematic. = 我希望情况不那么棘手。


被动语态:
The issue is being considered problematic. = 这个问题正被认为是棘手的。
The matter was deemed problematic by the committee. = 委员会认为此事有问题。


疑问句:
Is the approach problematic? = 这种方法有问题吗？
Are there any problematic aspects to consider? = 有什么需要考虑的问题吗？


否定句:
The solution is not problematic. = 这个解决方案没有问题。
The process was not problematic at all. = 这个过程一点问题也没有。
