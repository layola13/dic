preimagine:/ˌpriːɪˈmædʒɪn/| v.|预先想象，预想 |To form a mental image of something beforehand; to anticipate mentally.  |例句：I preimagined the meeting going smoothly, but it was chaotic. 我预想会议会顺利进行，但实际上却很混乱。|例句：She preimagined her future career as a successful writer. 她预想她未来的职业生涯将成为一名成功的作家。|近义词：anticipate, foresee, envision |反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I preimagine the outcome of the project. = 我预想项目的成果。
They preimagine their vacation in Hawaii. = 他们预想他们在夏威夷的假期。

一般过去时:
He preimagined the consequences of his actions. = 他预想了自己行为的后果。
She preimagined a life free from stress. = 她预想了一种没有压力的生活。

现在完成时:
I have preimagined many scenarios for the presentation. = 我已经预想了许多演示文稿的情况。
We have preimagined the difficulties we might face. = 我们已经预想了我们可能面临的困难。

过去完成时:
They had preimagined the questions the interviewer might ask. = 他们已经预想到了面试官可能会问的问题。
She had preimagined the perfect wedding day. = 她已经预想了一个完美的婚礼日。

将来时:
I will preimagine the steps involved in the experiment. = 我将预想实验中涉及的步骤。
We will preimagine the potential risks before starting. = 我们将在开始之前预想潜在的风险。

虚拟语气:
If I had preimagined the problem, I would have prepared better. = 如果我预想到了这个问题，我会准备得更好。
I wish I had preimagined the traffic jam. = 我希望我预想到了交通堵塞。

被动语态:
The possible outcomes were preimagined by the team. = 团队预想了可能的结果。
The potential challenges were preimagined before the launch. = 在发布之前，已经预想了潜在的挑战。

疑问句:
Did you preimagine the success of the campaign? = 你预想到了这场运动的成功吗？
Have they preimagined the logistical challenges? = 他们预想到了后勤方面的挑战吗？

否定句:
I did not preimagine the level of opposition. = 我没有预想到反对的程度。
They did not preimagine the unexpected turn of events. = 他们没有预想到事情的意外转变。


PHRASE->
preimagine a scenario = 预想一种情况
preimagine the future = 预想未来
preimagine the consequences = 预想后果
preimagine a solution = 预想一个解决方案
preimagine the possibilities = 预想可能性
