overutilizing: /ˌoʊvərˈjuːtəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |过度使用，滥用 |  The act of using something excessively or beyond its intended capacity.  It implies a negative consequence due to the excessive use. |例句：Overutilizing the system caused it to crash.  过度使用系统导致它崩溃了。


The company is overutilizing its resources, leading to burnout among employees. 公司过度使用其资源，导致员工筋疲力尽。|近义词：misusing, abusing, exploiting, overworking |反义词：underutilizing, conserving, preserving |动词

词源:

* **over-**:  prefix indicating "excessively" or "too much"
* **utilize**: verb meaning "to make practical and effective use of"
* **-ing**: present participle suffix indicating an ongoing action


The word "overutilizing" is a relatively modern coinage, formed by combining the prefix "over-" with the verb "utilize" and adding the "-ing" suffix.  It reflects a growing awareness and concern about the negative impacts of excessive resource consumption.
