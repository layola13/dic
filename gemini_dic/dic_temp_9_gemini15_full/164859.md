preformulation:/ˌpriːfɔːrmjuˈleɪʃən/ | n. |制剂前研究；处方前研究 |The process of collecting physical and chemical information on a drug substance to facilitate the design of a suitable dosage form.  It involves characterization of the drug substance and its properties to optimize the formulation process and ensure drug product quality. | Example:  Preformulation studies are crucial for the successful development of a new drug product. 制剂前研究对于成功开发新药至关重要。|近义词：drug substance characterization, formulation development preliminary studies |反义词：N/A
