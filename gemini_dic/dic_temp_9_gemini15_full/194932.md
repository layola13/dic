semi-internally: /ˌsɛmiɪnˈtɜːrnəli/ | adj. | 半内部地；部分内部地 |  Describes something that is partially or to some extent internal, but not completely so.  It suggests a process or structure that involves both internal and external components. |


例句：

The company's restructuring involved semi-internally sourced funding, meaning they used a combination of internal reserves and external loans.  公司重组涉及部分内部融资，这意味着他们同时使用了内部储备和外部贷款。


The semi-internally driven system utilizes both internal sensors and external inputs for optimal performance.  这个半内部驱动的系统利用内部传感器和外部输入来实现最佳性能。



近义词：partially internal, partly internal

反义词：externally, entirely external

词性: adjective
