pretransacts:/ˌpriːtrænzˈækt/| v.|预先交易；预先办理交易|To conduct transactions or business dealings in advance or before a main event or agreement.|例句：The company pretransacts some of its major deals to secure favorable terms. 公司预先办理一些主要交易以确保有利条件。


例句：They pretransacted the sale of the property before the official announcement. 他们在正式宣布之前就预先办理了房产出售手续。|近义词：prearrange, prenegotiate|反义词：postpone, delay|词性:动词
