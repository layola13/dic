discontinued:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuːd/| adj. |已停产的；已终止的；中断的 | No longer produced, offered, or used.  Something that has been discontinued is no longer available. |例句：That model of car has been discontinued. = 那种型号的汽车已经停产了。

The bakery discontinued its popular sourdough bread. = 那家面包店已经停售它受欢迎的酸面包了。


近义词：terminated, ceased, ended, stopped, abandoned | 反义词：continued, resumed, restarted | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The product is discontinued. = 该产品已停产。
This service is discontinued in this area. =这项服务在该地区已停止提供。


一般过去时:
They discontinued the product last year. = 他们去年停产了该产品。
The train service was discontinued due to the storm. =由于暴风雨，火车服务已暂停。


现在完成时:
The company has discontinued several product lines. =该公司已停产了几条产品线。
The project has been discontinued due to lack of funding. =由于缺乏资金，该项目已被终止。


过去完成时:
They had discontinued the service before I called. =在我打电话之前，他们就已经停止了服务。
The factory had discontinued production before the accident. =事故发生前工厂就已经停产了。


将来时:
They will discontinue the product next month. =他们下个月将停产该产品。
The airline will discontinue its flights to that city. =航空公司将停止飞往该城市的航班。


虚拟语气:
If they discontinued the product, sales would drop. =如果他们停产该产品，销售额就会下降。
I wish they hadn't discontinued my favorite cereal. =我希望他们没有停产我最喜欢的麦片。


被动语态:
The product was discontinued due to low sales. =该产品因销量低而停产。
The service is being discontinued gradually. =该服务正在逐步停止。


疑问句:
Has the product been discontinued? = 该产品已停产了吗？
Is the service discontinued in this region? =这项服务在这个地区停止了吗？


否定句:
The product has not been discontinued. =该产品尚未停产。
The service is not discontinued yet. =服务尚未停止。


PHRASE->
be discontinued = 被停产
discontinued product = 已停产产品
discontinued service = 已停止服务
discontinued operation = 已停止运营
discontinued use = 已停止使用
