profit-sharing: /ˈprɒfɪt ʃeərɪŋ/ | n. |利润分享；盈利分享 | A system in which employees receive a share of the company's profits, in addition to their regular wages or salaries.  This is often done as an incentive to improve performance and boost morale.  | Example:  The company implemented a profit-sharing plan to motivate its employees. 公司实施了利润分享计划以激励员工。


The company's profit-sharing program is highly successful. 公司的利润分享计划非常成功。|近义词：bonus scheme, incentive plan, profit distribution |反义词：loss-sharing (反义词相对不常见，因为通常没有“亏损分享”这种正式机制) |名词

词源:

The term "profit-sharing" is a compound word combining "profit" (from Old French *profit, ultimately from Latin *proficere "to advance, make progress") and "sharing" (from Old English *scīrian "to divide, separate").  Therefore, it literally means the division or distribution of profits.
