overfertilized:/ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrtɪlaɪzd/| adj.|施肥过量的；过度施肥的|  Describes the state of soil or plants that have received an excessive amount of fertilizer.  This can lead to negative consequences for plant health and the environment.|

例句：

The overfertilized lawn turned brown and died. = 施肥过量的草坪枯黄而死。

The farmer's crops suffered from overfertilized soil. = 农民的庄稼遭受了土壤施肥过量的损害。


近义词：over-nourished, excessively fertilized

反义词：underfertilized, undernourished

词性：形容词
