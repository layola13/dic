felter:/ˈfɛltər/| n. v.|毛毡匠；制作毛毡的人；使…缠结；使…纠结|A felter is someone who makes felt.  The verb refers to the process of making felt or to something becoming tangled or matted.|例句：He was a skilled felter, creating beautiful hats from sheep's wool. 他是一位熟练的毛毡匠，用羊毛制作精美的帽子。  The wool felted easily in the hot water. 羊毛在热水中很容易毡化。|近义词：felt maker, weaver|反义词：unraveler, disentangler
