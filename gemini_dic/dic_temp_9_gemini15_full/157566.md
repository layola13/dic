pitched:/pɪtʃt/ | v. adj. n. | 投掷；倾斜；（音调）高亢的；推销；搭建 | to throw something; to be at a particular angle; having a high pitch; to try to persuade someone to buy something; to set up (a tent, etc.) | 近义词：(v.) throw, toss, hurl, slant, incline; (adj.) high-pitched, steep, sharp; (n.) sales pitch | 反义词：(adj.) low-pitched, flat, gentle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pitches the ball really hard. = 他用力地投球。
The roof pitches steeply. = 屋顶倾斜得很厉害。


一般过去时:
She pitched her tent by the river. = 她在河边搭起了帐篷。
They pitched the idea to the investors. = 他们向投资者推销了这个主意。


现在完成时:
He has pitched many successful products. = 他已经成功推销了很多产品。
The company has pitched its new strategy to the board. = 公司已经向董事会阐述了它的新战略。


过去完成时:
They had pitched the tent before it started to rain. = 下雨前他们已经搭好了帐篷。
Before the meeting, she had pitched her proposal to her boss. = 会议前，她已向老板陈述了自己的提案。


将来时:
He will pitch the winning idea at the competition. = 他将在比赛中展现获胜的想法。
We will pitch the new product at the trade show. = 我们将在贸易展会上推出新产品。


虚拟语气:
If he pitched the ball harder, he might have won the game. = 如果他投球更用力，他可能就赢了比赛。
If I had pitched my idea better, I might have gotten the job. = 如果我更好地陈述我的想法，我可能就得到了这份工作。


被动语态:
The tent was pitched quickly by the experienced campers. = 经验丰富的露营者迅速地搭起了帐篷。
The new product was pitched to potential customers. = 新产品向潜在客户进行了推销。


疑问句:
How high did he pitch his voice? = 他把声音提高到多高？
Where did they pitch their camp? = 他们在哪里安营扎寨？


否定句:
He didn't pitch the ball far enough. = 他没有把球投得够远。
They didn't pitch the idea effectively. = 他们没有有效地推销这个主意。


PHRASE->
pitch black: 漆黑一片
pitch in: 帮忙；贡献
pitch perfect: 完美无瑕的
make a pitch: 做推销；进行宣传
at a high pitch: 高音调
