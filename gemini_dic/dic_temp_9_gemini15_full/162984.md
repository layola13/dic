precipitation:/ˌprɛsɪpɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 降水，沉淀 | The process of water falling from the atmosphere to the ground, including rain, snow, sleet, and hail.  It also refers to the process of a substance separating from a solution as a solid. |例句：The heavy precipitation caused flooding in the lowlands.  这场大雨导致低地发生洪水。

例句：The precipitation of calcium carbonate formed stalactites in the cave.  碳酸钙的沉淀在洞穴中形成了钟乳石。

近义词： rainfall, downfall, sedimentation | 反义词： evaporation, sublimation | 词性: 名词
