cirrocumulous:/ˌsɪroʊˈkjuːmjʊləs/| adj.| 卷积云的 | Cirrocumulus clouds are high-altitude clouds composed of ice crystals, appearing as small, white patches or ripples.  They often indicate fair weather. | The sky was filled with beautiful cirrocumulus clouds, resembling small, fluffy cotton balls.  天空布满了美丽的卷积云，像小的蓬松棉花球。 |  altocumulus, stratocumulus | nimbostratus | adj.
