exaggerative: /ɪɡˈzædʒəreɪtɪv/ | adj. | 夸张的，言过其实的 |  Characterized by exaggeration; tending to exaggerate.  It describes something that is overstated or amplified beyond the truth. |例句：His account of the incident was highly exaggerative. 他对事件的描述非常夸张。

例句：The exaggerative claims made by the company were quickly debunked. 公司夸大的说法很快就被揭穿了。


近义词：hyperbolic, inflated, overstated, extravagant, bombastic

反义词：understated, modest, restrained, factual, literal
