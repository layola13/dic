irony:/ˈaɪrəni/| n. |反讽，讽刺；出乎意料的事 |The use of words to convey a meaning that is the opposite of its literal meaning; a situation that is strange or amusing because things happen in a way that seems deliberately contrary to what you expect.  |例句：The irony is that he became famous for preaching against fame.  讽刺的是，他因反对名利而闻名。|例句：It's ironic that the fire station burned down.  消防站烧毁了，这真讽刺。|近义词：sarcasm, paradox, incongruity |反义词：straightforwardness, literalness
