pontificates:/pɒnˈtɪfɪkeɪt/|/ˈpɑːntɪfɪkeɪt/| v.| 夸夸其谈；自以为是地宣讲；以权威的语气断言 |To speak or write in a pompous and dogmatic way.  |例句：He pontificates on every subject, whether he knows anything about it or not. 他对每个话题都夸夸其谈，不管他是否了解。

例句：The professor pontificated for an hour about the intricacies of quantum physics. 教授就量子物理的复杂性滔滔不绝地讲了一个小时。


近义词：oracularly, dogmatically, authoritatively, pronounce, preach, lecture

反义词：listen, inquire, question, doubt


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pontificates about politics every chance he gets. = 他一有机会就夸夸其谈政治。
She pontificates on social media, often without evidence. = 她在社交媒体上夸夸其谈，经常没有证据。


一般过去时:
He pontificated at the meeting, ignoring other's opinions. = 他在会议上夸夸其谈，无视其他人的意见。
She pontificated about the benefits of her diet, despite her weight. = 尽管她体重超标，她还是自以为是地宣扬她节食的好处。


现在完成时:
He has pontificated on this topic for years. = 他多年来一直对这个话题夸夸其谈。
She has pontificated so much that no one listens to her anymore. = 她夸夸其谈太多，以至于没有人再听她说话了。


过去完成时:
He had pontificated for hours before anyone dared to interrupt. = 他已经夸夸其谈了好几个小时，才有人敢打断他。
She had pontificated about her success before admitting her failures. = 在承认自己的失败之前，她已经夸夸其谈自己的成功了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If he pontificated less, people might listen more. = 如果他少夸夸其谈，人们可能会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't pontificate so much. = 我希望他不要夸夸其谈这么多。


被动语态:
His ideas were often pontificated upon by others without proper understanding. = 他的想法经常被别人不加理解地夸夸其谈。
The subject was pontificated on endlessly at the seminar. = 研讨会上对这个主题进行了没完没了的夸夸其谈。


疑问句:
Does he always pontificate like that? = 他总是那样夸夸其谈吗？

否定句:
He didn't pontificate; he simply stated the facts. = 他没有夸夸其谈；他只是陈述了事实。
She doesn't pontificate; she prefers to listen and learn. = 她不夸夸其谈；她更喜欢倾听和学习。
