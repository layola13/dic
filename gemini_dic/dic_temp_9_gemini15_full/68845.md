fashionable:/ˈfæʃənəbl/ | adj. | 时髦的，流行的 |  Stylish and up-to-date in appearance, manner, or style.  It refers to something or someone that conforms to the current trends and is considered attractive or desirable. | Example: Her fashionable dress turned heads at the party.  她的时髦连衣裙在派对上吸引了众人的目光。


Example: That restaurant is very fashionable; it's always booked solid. 那家餐馆很时髦，总是客满。


近义词:stylish, trendy, chic, smart | 反义词:outdated, old-fashioned, unstylish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dress is fashionable. = 这件衣服很时髦。
Her hairstyle is fashionable. = 她的发型很时髦。


一般过去时:
That style was fashionable last year. = 那种风格去年很流行。
Her shoes were fashionable in the 1980s. = 她的鞋子在20世纪80年代很流行。


现在完成时:
That design has been fashionable for many years. = 那个设计多年来一直很流行。
She has always been fashionable. = 她一直都很时髦。


过去完成时:
That hat had been fashionable in the previous decade. = 那顶帽子在上个十年很流行。
The style had already gone out of fashion before she bought it. = 她买之前，那种风格就已经过时了。


将来时:
This color will be fashionable next season. = 这种颜色下个季度会很流行。
That style will likely be fashionable for years to come. = 那种风格可能会流行很多年。


虚拟语气:
If that were fashionable, I would buy it. = 如果那很流行，我会买的。
I wish my clothes were more fashionable. = 我希望我的衣服更时髦一些。


被动语态:
The latest styles are considered fashionable. = 最新款式被认为是时髦的。
That trend is being declared fashionable by many. = 许多人认为那种趋势很时髦。


疑问句:
Is that style fashionable? = 那种风格时髦吗？
Are those shoes fashionable? = 那些鞋子时髦吗？


否定句:
That outfit is not fashionable. = 那套衣服不时髦。
His clothes are not fashionable at all. = 他的衣服一点也不时髦。


PHRASE->
be in fashion = 流行
go out of fashion = 过时
the height of fashion = 最流行的
set the fashion = 引领时尚
follow fashion = 跟随时尚
