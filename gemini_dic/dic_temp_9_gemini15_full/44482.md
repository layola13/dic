crossbred:/ˈkrɒsbred/| adj., n.|混血的；杂交的；混种的|An animal or plant that is the offspring of parents of different breeds, varieties, or species.|例句：The farmer bred crossbred cattle for their hardiness and milk production.  这位农民培育了混血牛，因为它们强壮且产奶量高。  The crossbred dog inherited the best traits from both its parents. 这只混血狗继承了父母双方的最佳特质。|近义词：hybrid, mixed-breed|反义词：purebred, thoroughbred


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The farmer crossbreeds different types of cattle. = 这位农民杂交培育不同类型的牛。
This company crossbreeds new varieties of flowers. = 这家公司杂交培育新品种的花。


一般过去时:
They crossbred horses to create a stronger breed. = 他们杂交培育马匹以创造更强壮的品种。
Scientists crossbred two species of plants for research. = 科学家为了研究而杂交了两种植物。


现在完成时:
