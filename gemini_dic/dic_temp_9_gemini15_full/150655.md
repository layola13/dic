pappose:/ˈpæpəʊs/| adj.| 羽冠状的；具冠毛的 | Having a pappus.  Refers to a structure, typically found on seeds or fruits of plants in the Asteraceae (daisy) family, consisting of fine, hair-like bristles or scales.  These help in seed dispersal by wind. | Example: The dandelion seed has a characteristic pappose structure.  蒲公英种子具有典型的羽冠状结构。 | 近义词：plumose, feathery, downy | 反义词： glabrous, smooth
