revict:/rɪˈvɪkt/| vt.|驱逐，收回（财产）|To evict (someone) from a property, especially by legal process.|例句：The landlord threatened to revict the tenants if they didn't pay the rent. 房东威胁说如果他们不付房租就将租户驱逐出去。|例句：The court ordered the reviction of the squatters. 法院下令驱逐非法占用者。|近义词：evict, oust, expel|反义词：allow, permit
