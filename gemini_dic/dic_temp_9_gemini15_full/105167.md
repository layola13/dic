keuper:/ˈkɔɪpər/| n. |克普阶|A geological formation, specifically the uppermost Triassic geological stage in Europe.  It is characterized by red mudstones and sandstones, often containing evaporites and fossils of various plants and animals. The Keuper Marl is a particularly notable formation within the Keuper. |The Keuper rocks of southwest Germany are rich in fossils.  |近义词：Triassic (in a broader sense)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with other Triassic stages (e.g., Muschelkalk)
