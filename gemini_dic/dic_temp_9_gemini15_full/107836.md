larcher:/ˈlɑːrtʃər/| n.|落叶松树, 骗子|A larch is a deciduous coniferous tree of the genus Larix.  The word can also be used informally to refer to a swindler or cheat. |例句：The larch trees turned golden in autumn. 秋天落叶松树叶变成了金色。|例句：He's a real larcher, always trying to cheat people. 他是个十足的骗子，总是试图欺骗别人。|近义词：conifer (for the tree), swindler, cheat, rogue|反义词：honest person (for the informal meaning), evergreen (for the tree)
