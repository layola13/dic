nonradiant: /ˌnɒnˈreɪdiənt/ | adj. | 非辐射的，不辐射的 | Not emitting radiation.  The term refers to something that does not emit electromagnetic radiation,  heat, or other forms of energy in the form of rays or waves. | Example:  The nonradiant heating system relies on convection to warm the room.  = 非辐射供暖系统依靠对流来温暖房间。


The nonradiant heat source warmed the room slowly. = 非辐射热源缓慢地温暖了房间。


近义词: non-emitting (in the context of radiation)


反义词: radiant


词性: adjective
