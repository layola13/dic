desensitize: /ˌdiːˈsɛnsaɪz/ | vt. | 使麻木；使不敏感；脱敏 | To reduce the sensitivity of (someone or something) to a particular stimulus or experience.  | 例句：The constant exposure to violence on television can desensitize viewers to its effects.  电视上不断暴力的曝光可能会使观众对其影响麻木。

例句：Prolonged exposure to trauma can desensitize individuals to emotional pain. 长期遭受创伤可能会使个人对情感上的痛苦麻木。


近义词：numb, deaden, dull, inure


反义词：sensitize, heighten, sharpen


词性：动词
