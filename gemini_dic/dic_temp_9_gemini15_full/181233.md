reinspected:/ˌriːɪnˈspɛktɪd/| v. |复查，再次检查|To inspect something again; to subject to a second inspection.  This implies a more thorough or focused examination than the initial inspection. |例句：The building was reinspected after the initial report revealed some safety concerns.  建筑物在初步报告显示一些安全问题后进行了复查。


The contractor reinspected the work after the client raised some questions. 承包商在客户提出一些问题后再次检查了工程。|近义词：re-examine, review, check again, reassess |反义词：ignore, overlook
