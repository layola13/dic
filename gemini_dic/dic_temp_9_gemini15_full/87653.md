hexad:/ˈhɛksæd/| n. |六个一组；六重奏；六元组 |A hexad is a group or set of six things.  It can refer to a musical group of six performers, a chemical compound with six units, or any other collection of six items. |Example: The composer wrote a hexad for six oboes.  作曲家为六支双簧管写了一首六重奏。|近义词：sextet, sextuple, six-pack |反义词：unit, single, individual
