fobbed:/fɒbd/ |v.| 欺骗，蒙骗，搪塞 | To deceive or trick someone, especially by giving them something worthless or false in place of something valuable.  |He fobbed off the reporters with a prepared statement. 他用一份准备好的声明搪塞记者。|近义词：deceive, trick, dupe, hoodwink |反义词：reveal, honestly inform


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He often fobs off his responsibilities onto others. = 他经常把责任推卸给别人。
She fobs off customers with cheap imitations. = 她用廉价的仿制品欺骗顾客。


一般过去时:
They fobbed me off with a flimsy excuse. = 他们用一个牵强的借口搪塞我。
The salesman fobbed off the faulty product on an unsuspecting customer. = 销售员把有缺陷的产品卖给了一个毫无戒心的顾客。


现在完成时:
He has repeatedly fobbed off his creditors with empty promises. = 他一再用空洞的承诺来搪塞债权人。
She has fobbed off her work on her colleagues for weeks. = 她几周来一直把工作推卸给同事。


过去完成时:
They had fobbed him off with a worthless piece of jewelry before he realized it. = 在他意识到之前，他们已经用一件毫无价值的珠宝骗了他。
She had fobbed off her responsibilities before going on vacation. = 在度假之前，她已经把责任推卸出去了。


将来时:
He will likely fob off the blame onto someone else. = 他很可能会把责任推卸给别人。
They will attempt to fob off the investors with false information. = 他们将试图用虚假信息来欺骗投资者。


虚拟语气:
If he had been honest, he wouldn't have fobbed off the customer. = 如果他诚实的话，他就不会欺骗顾客了。
I wish they hadn't fobbed me off with such a poor excuse. = 我真希望他们没有用这么差的借口搪塞我。


被动语态:
The customer was fobbed off with a substandard product. = 顾客被卖给了劣质产品。
The truth was fobbed off by the authorities. = 真相被当局隐瞒了。


疑问句:
Were you fobbed off with a fake product? = 你是不是被卖给了假货？
Did they fob him off with a lame excuse? = 他们是否用蹩脚的借口搪塞了他？


否定句:
He didn't fob off the responsibility, he took it on himself. = 他没有推卸责任，而是自己承担了。
She wouldn't fob off her work onto her colleagues. = 她不会把工作推卸给同事。


PHRASE->
fob off (on someone):  把...推卸给（某人）
fob something off as something else: 把某物伪装成其他东西
fob a person off with something: 用某物搪塞某人
to fob something off:  蒙混过关
fobbed off on: 被...欺骗
