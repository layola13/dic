detrition:/dɪˈtrɪʃən/| n. |磨损；磨蚀；损耗 |The gradual wearing away of a substance by friction.  Detrition is a process that occurs over time, resulting in the reduction or disintegration of a material.  |例句：The detrition of the teeth is a natural process that can be accelerated by various factors.  牙齿磨损是一个自然过程，但会被多种因素加速。

The continuous detrition of the road surface requires frequent repairs. 道路表面的持续磨损需要频繁维修。|近义词：abrasion, erosion, attrition, wear |反义词：growth, accretion
