redman:/ˈrɛdmæn/| n.|红人；印第安人(旧称，现多视为贬义)|A redman is a person with red skin, specifically referring to Native Americans.  The term is now widely considered offensive and outdated due to its historical association with colonialism and racist stereotypes. |例句：The term "redman" is considered offensive and should be avoided.  (术语“红人”被认为具有冒犯性，应该避免使用。) The old book used the term "redman" to describe Native Americans. (那本旧书用“红人”来形容美洲原住民。) |近义词：Native American, Indigenous person|反义词：无直接反义词，但可以考虑与“殖民者”或“侵略者”对比|名词
