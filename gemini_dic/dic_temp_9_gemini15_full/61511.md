embrown:/ɪmˈbraʊn/| vt.|使变成褐色；使变黑|To make something brown or darker in color.  The process often involves heat or chemical reactions.|例句：The sun embrowned his skin.  太阳晒黑了他的皮肤。

例句：The roast was beautifully embrowned in the oven. 烤肉在烤箱里烤成了美丽的褐色。


近义词：brown, tan, darken|反义词：bleach, lighten


词源：来自em- (表示“使成为”) + brown (褐色)。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun embrowns his skin every summer. = 每年夏天太阳都会晒黑他的皮肤。
The baker embrowns the bread crusts to perfection. = 面包师把面包皮烤成完美的褐色。


一般过去时:
The wood embrowned over time. = 木头随着时间推移变黑了。
She embrowned the butter in the pan before adding the vegetables. = 她把黄油在锅里炒成褐色后再加入蔬菜。


现在完成时:
The constant exposure to the sun has embrowned her complexion. = 持续的日晒使她的肤色变黑了。
Years of neglect have embrowned the once-bright paintwork. = 多年的忽视使曾经鲜艳的油漆变黑了。


过去完成时:
The wood had embrowned significantly before they discovered the damage. = 在他们发现损坏之前，木头已经明显变黑了。
She realized that she had embrowned the sauce too much, making it bitter. = 她意识到她把酱汁炒得太黑了，导致它变苦了。

虚拟语气:
