self-offenses:/ˌsɛlfˈɒfɛnsɪz/| n.|自我犯罪；自我伤害行为|Acts that harm oneself, either physically or psychologically.  These can range from self-harm like cutting or burning to more subtle forms of self-sabotage in relationships or career.  The term often implies a lack of conscious intent to cause harm, but rather a pattern of behavior that negatively impacts the individual.|例句：His self-offenses stemmed from deep-seated feelings of inadequacy.他的自我伤害行为源于根深蒂固的自卑感。

例句：Repeated self-offenses, such as procrastination and substance abuse, hindered his progress.  反复的自我伤害行为，例如拖延症和滥用药物，阻碍了他的进步。


近义词：self-destructive behavior, self-harm, self-sabotage|反义词：self-care, self-preservation|名词
