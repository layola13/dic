maturity:/məˈtʃʊrəti/| n. |成熟；成年；到期|The state of being mature; the process of becoming mature.  It can refer to the state of being fully developed, or the point at which something is due. |例句：He showed remarkable maturity for his age. 他表现出与年龄不相符的成熟。|例句：The maturity date of the bond is next year. 这张债券的到期日是明年。|近义词：ripeness, adulthood, completeness|反义词：immaturity, youth, infancy|名词
