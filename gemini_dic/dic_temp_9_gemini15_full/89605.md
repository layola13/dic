hoodoos:/ˈhuːduːz/| n.|胡德，奇特的岩石柱|Tall, slender rock formations, often found in arid regions, created by erosion.  They typically consist of a caprock protecting a softer layer of rock from erosion.|The Bryce Canyon National Park is famous for its stunning hoodoos.|近义词：spire, pinnacle, earth pyramid|反义词：plain, flat land
