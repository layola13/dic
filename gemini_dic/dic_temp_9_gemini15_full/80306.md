gold-digger:/ˈɡoʊldˌdɪɡər/| n. |淘金女，拜金女|A woman who seeks to marry a wealthy man for his money.  This term is often used derogatorily and implies a lack of genuine affection or interest in the man beyond his financial resources.|She was rumored to be a gold digger, only interested in his fortune.|近义词：fortune hunter, mercenary|反义词：altruist, philanthropist
