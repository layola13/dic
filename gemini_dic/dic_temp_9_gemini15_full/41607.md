corpuscule:/ˈkɔːrpʌskjuːl/| n. |微粒；小体 |A corpuscle is a tiny particle, especially one found in a body fluid.  It often refers to blood cells. | Example:  Red blood corpuscles carry oxygen throughout the body.  红血球在全身输送氧气。 The scientist examined the corpuscles under the microscope. 科学家在显微镜下检查了这些微粒。|近义词：particle, granule, cell |反义词：macromolecule, aggregate
