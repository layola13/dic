northness:/ˈnɔːθnəs/| n. |北方属性；向北的趋势；极北地区|The quality or state of being north; the direction or characteristic of the north.  It can also refer to the remoteness or severity of a northerly region.|例句：The northness of the landscape was emphasized by the bleak, windswept plains.  北方景观的荒凉和风swept平原突出了其北方属性。

The extreme northness of the expedition made resupply extremely difficult. 探险队极北的地理位置使得补给极其困难。|近义词：northernness, northwards tendency|反义词：southness
