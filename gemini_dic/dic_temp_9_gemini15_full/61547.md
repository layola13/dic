embush:/ˈɛmbʊʃ/ | n. v. |埋伏；伏击 |  To attack by surprise from a concealed position.  |例句：The soldiers lay in ambush, waiting for the enemy.  士兵们埋伏着，等待敌人。

例句：The guerrillas ambushed the convoy.游击队伏击了车队。


近义词：surprise attack, ambushment, raid, trap | 反义词：open attack, frontal assault


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They ambush their prey. = 他们伏击猎物。
The army ambushes the enemy regularly. = 这支军队定期伏击敌人。


一般过去时:
The soldiers ambushed the enemy. = 士兵们伏击了敌人。
They ambushed the patrol. = 他们伏击了巡逻队。


现在完成时:
The rebels have ambushed several convoys this month. = 叛军本月伏击了几支车队。
We have never been ambushed before. = 我们以前从未被伏击过。


过去完成时:
The enemy had ambushed the troops before they reached the village. = 敌人袭击了部队，然后他们才到达村庄。
They had ambushed the caravan several times. = 他们曾多次伏击商队。


将来时:
The general plans to ambush the enemy. = 将军计划伏击敌人。
They will ambush the enemy at dawn. = 他们将在黎明时伏击敌人。


虚拟语气:
If they had ambushed the enemy earlier, they would have won. = 如果他们早点伏击敌人，他们就会赢了。
I wish we hadn't been ambushed. = 我真希望我们没有被伏击。


被动语态:
The convoy was ambushed yesterday. = 车队昨天遭到伏击。
The enemy was ambushed by our troops. = 敌人被我们的军队伏击了。


疑问句:
Were the soldiers ambushed? = 士兵们遭到伏击了吗？
Have they been ambushed before? = 他们以前被伏击过吗？


否定句:
They did not ambush the enemy. = 他们没有伏击敌人。
The army hasn't ambushed the enemy yet. = 军队还没有伏击敌人。


PHRASE->
lay in ambush = 埋伏
lie in ambush = 埋伏
spring an ambush = 发起突然袭击
from ambush = 从伏击中
ambush an enemy = 伏击敌人

词源:
From Old French *emboscher, from bois "woods".  The original sense was "to hide in the woods."  The meaning then evolved to include the military tactic of hiding to attack.
