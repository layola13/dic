outmanoeuvring: /ˌaʊt.məˈnuː.vərɪŋ/ | v. |  策略性地击败；智胜；巧妙地避开 |  To outmaneuver someone or something means to cleverly overcome them by using superior skill or strategy. It implies a more subtle and indirect approach than simply overpowering them. |

例句：

The general outmaneuvered the enemy by launching a surprise attack. = 将军通过发动突然袭击击败了敌人。

The chess player skillfully outmaneuvered his opponent, leading to a checkmate. = 国际象棋选手巧妙地击败了他的对手，导致将死。


近义词：outwit, outsmart, circumvent, outplay,  surpass

反义词：be outmaneuvered, be defeated, be surpassed
