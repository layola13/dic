engineering: /ˌɛndʒɪˈnɪərɪŋ/ | n. | 工程学；工程；设计；建造 | The application of scientific and mathematical principles to practical ends such as the design, construction, and operation of structures, machines, and systems.  |例句：Civil engineering is crucial for building infrastructure. 土木工程对于基础设施建设至关重要。

例句：She's studying mechanical engineering at university. 她在大学学习机械工程。


近义词：technology, construction, design | 反义词：None
