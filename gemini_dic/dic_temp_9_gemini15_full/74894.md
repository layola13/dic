freshish:/ˈfreʃɪʃ/| adj.| 微新鲜的，有点新鲜的 | Somewhat fresh; slightly fresh.  It implies a degree of freshness that is not entirely full or complete.  |例句：The bread was freshish, but not perfectly fresh. 这面包有点新鲜，但不是完全新鲜。

例句：The salad was freshish, though some leaves were wilting.  沙拉有点新鲜，虽然有些叶子已经枯萎了。


近义词：fairly fresh, moderately fresh, kind of fresh |反义词：stale, old, withered


词性：形容词
