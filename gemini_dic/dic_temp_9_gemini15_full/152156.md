patisserie:/ˌpætɪˈsɛriː/| n. |糕点店；糕点制作坊 |A patisserie is a shop or a section of a shop that sells pastries and other sweet baked goods.  It often implies a higher level of quality and craftsmanship than a typical bakery.|例句：The patisserie on the corner makes the best croissants in town. 街角那家糕点店制作城里最好吃的羊角面包。|例句：She worked for years to perfect her recipes before opening her own patisserie. 在开设自己的糕点店之前，她花了数年时间来完善自己的食谱。|近义词：bakery, confectionery |反义词：butchery (反义词较为牵强，仅指性质相反)|名词
