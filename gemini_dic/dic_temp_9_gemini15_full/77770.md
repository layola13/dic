genera:/ˈdʒɛnərə/ | n. |属（生物分类单位）| Genera are taxonomic ranks used in biological classification, ranking below family and above species.  A genus typically comprises a group of closely related species sharing common characteristics. | Example:  The genus *Panthera* includes lions, tigers, leopards, and jaguars.  *Panthera属包括狮子、老虎、豹子和美洲豹。* |近义词：genus (singular)|反义词：species (个体物种)|名词
