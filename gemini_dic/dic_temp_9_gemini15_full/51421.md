deters:/dɪˈtɜːrz/| vt.|阻止，劝退|To discourage someone from doing something, typically by making them aware of the difficulties or dangers involved.|The high cost of living deters many young people from moving to the city. 高昂的生活成本阻止了许多年轻人搬到城市。|近义词：discourage, dissuade, prevent|反义词：encourage, incite


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The high price deters many buyers. = 高昂的价格阻止了许多买家。
Harsh punishments deter crime. = 严厉的惩罚可以阻止犯罪。


一般过去时:
The threat of rain deterred us from going for a picnic. = 下雨的威胁阻止了我们去野餐。
His warnings deterred me from taking the risk. = 他的警告阻止了我冒险。


现在完成时:
Nothing has deterred her from achieving her goals. = 没有什么能阻止她实现自己的目标。
The scandal has deterred many investors. = 这场丑闻吓退了许多投资者。


过去完成时:
Previous failures had deterred him from trying again. = 过去的失败阻止了他再次尝试。
The bad weather had deterred them from attending the event. = 恶劣的天气阻止了他们参加活动。


将来时:
Higher taxes will deter investment. = 更高的税收将阻止投资。
The new security measures will deter potential thieves. = 新的安全措施将阻止潜在的小偷。


虚拟语气:
If only the threat of punishment deterred crime, the world would be a better place. = 如果惩罚的威胁能阻止犯罪，世界将会变得更好。
I wish stricter laws would deter drunk driving. = 我希望更严格的法律能够阻止酒后驾车。


被动语态:
Many potential investors were deterred by the company's poor financial performance. = 许多潜在投资者被该公司糟糕的财务业绩吓退了。
He was deterred from applying for the job by the low salary. = 低薪阻止了他申请这份工作。


疑问句:
What would deter him from taking the job? = 什么会阻止他接受这份工作？
Does the fear of failure deter you from trying? = 对失败的恐惧会阻止你尝试吗？


否定句:
Nothing could deter her from pursuing her dreams. = 没有什么能够阻止她追求梦想。
The warnings did not deter him from continuing. = 警告并没有阻止他继续。


PHRASE->

deter from:阻止…做…
deter sb from doing sth:阻止某人做某事
deterrent to:对…的威慑
a strong deterrent:强有力的威慑
deterrent effect:威慑作用
