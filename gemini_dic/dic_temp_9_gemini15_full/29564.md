catchment:/ˈkætʃmənt/| n. |集水区，流域；覆盖范围|The area from which water drains into a river, lake, or reservoir.  It can also refer to the area served by a particular service or institution.|例句：The river's catchment area is vast. 这条河的集水区非常广阔。|例句：The school's catchment area includes several nearby villages. 这所学校的服务范围包括附近几个村庄。|近义词：watershed, drainage basin, area, reach|反义词：None specifically, but concepts like "outflow area" could be considered opposite in a hydrological context.|名词
