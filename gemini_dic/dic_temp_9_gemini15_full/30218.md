cellars:/ˈsɛlərz/| n.|地窖，地下室|A cellar is an underground room, typically used for storage.  It's often cool and damp.|例句：We keep our wine in the cellar. 我们把酒放在地窖里。|例句：The cellar was damp and smelled of mildew. 地窖潮湿，散发着霉味。|近义词：basement, underground room|反义词：attic, loft


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cellar stores our winter vegetables. = 地窖储存着我们的冬季蔬菜。
He checks the cellar for leaks every spring. = 他每年春天都检查地窖是否有漏水。


一般过去时:
They found some old jars in the cellar. = 他们在地窖里找到了一些旧罐子。
We cleaned out the cellar last weekend. = 我们上周末清理了地窖。


现在完成时:
They have stored their wine in the cellar for years. = 他们多年来一直把葡萄酒储存在地窖里。
