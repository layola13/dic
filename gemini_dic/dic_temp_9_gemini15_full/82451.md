grumbly:/ˈɡrʌmbli/| adj.|抱怨的；嘟囔的；脾气坏的|Characterized by grumbling or complaining; sullen or irritable.|The children were grumbly after their nap. 孩子们午睡后脾气很坏。  He gave a grumbly answer. 他不情愿地回答。|近义词：complaining, sulky, grumpy, irritable|反义词：cheerful, happy, pleasant
