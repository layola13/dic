misnaming:/ˌmɪsˈneɪmɪŋ/ | n. | 误称；错误命名 | The act of giving something the wrong name; incorrect naming.  It can refer to a person, place, thing, or concept.  | Example: The misnaming of the plant led to confusion among botanists.  植物的误称导致植物学家之间的困惑。  |近义词：misnomer, misidentification,  wrong naming | 反义词：correct naming, accurate designation | 名词
