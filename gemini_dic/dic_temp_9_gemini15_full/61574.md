emeralds:/ˈɛmərældz/ | n. |祖母绿（复数）| Emeralds are a variety of the mineral beryl colored green by trace amounts of chromium and sometimes vanadium.  They are highly prized gemstones, known for their intense green color and brilliance. |例句：The necklace was adorned with exquisite emeralds. 这条项链装饰着精美的祖母绿。

例句：She inherited a collection of valuable emeralds from her grandmother. 她从祖母那里继承了一批珍贵的祖母绿。


近义词：green gemstones, precious stones | 反义词：无


词源:  The word "emerald" has its origins in the Old French word "esmeraude," which itself derives from the Latin "smaragdus," ultimately tracing back to the Greek word "smaragdos" (σμαράγδος).  The Greek word likely came from an even older Semitic root.
