control:/kənˈtroʊl/ |/kənˈtrəʊl/| v., n.|控制，管理|To exercise restraining or directing influence over; govern or regulate.|The government controls the flow of information.|近义词：manage, govern, regulate|反义词：abandon, relinquish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She controls the company's finances. = 她控制着公司的财务。
The thermostat controls the room temperature. = 恒温器控制着室温。


一般过去时:
He controlled his anger. = 他控制住了他的怒火。
The pilot controlled the plane expertly. = 飞行员熟练地控制着飞机。


现在完成时:
They have controlled the spread of the disease. = 他们已经控制住了疾病的蔓延。
She has controlled her emotions throughout the ordeal. = 在整个磨难中，她一直控制着自己的情绪。


过去完成时:
He had controlled his impulses before speaking. = 他在说话之前已经控制住了自己的冲动。
They had controlled the fire before the firefighters arrived. = 在消防队员到达之前，他们已经控制住了大火。


将来时:
We will control the costs of the project. = 我们将控制项目的成本。
She will control the remote from across the room. = 她将从房间的另一端遥控。


虚拟语气:
If he could control his temper, he would be happier. = 如果他能控制住他的脾气，他会更快乐。
I wish I could control the weather. = 我希望我能控制天气。


被动语态:
The speed of the car is controlled by the driver. = 车速由驾驶员控制。
The temperature is controlled automatically. = 温度是自动控制的。


疑问句:
Who controls the media? = 谁控制着媒体？
Does the government control the internet? = 政府控制互联网吗？


否定句:
He could not control his laughter. = 他忍不住笑了。
They failed to control the situation.  = 他们未能控制住局面。


PHRASE->
take control = 接管；控制
lose control = 失去控制
in control = 在掌控中
out of control = 失控
remote control = 遥控器
