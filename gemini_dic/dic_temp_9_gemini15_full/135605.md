nonpossessively:/ˌnɒn.pəˈzesɪvli/ | adv. |非所有格地，非占有性地 |  The adverb "nonpossessively" means in a way that does not show ownership or possession. It indicates a lack of possessive qualities or actions. |例句：He referred to the house nonpossessively, as if it belonged to someone else. 他非所有格地提到那栋房子，好像它属于其他人一样。

He used the car nonpossessively during his stay. 他在逗留期间非占有性地使用了这辆车。|近义词：unpossessively, disinterestedly |反义词：possessively, ownerly
