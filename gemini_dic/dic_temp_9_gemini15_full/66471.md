exercise:/ˈɛksərsaɪz/ | n., v. |练习；运动；运用；行使 |  A physical activity to improve or maintain physical fitness and overall health.  The act of using or practicing something. |例句：I do exercise every morning. = 我每天早上都锻炼。  He exercised his right to vote. = 他行使了他的投票权。 |近义词：practice, drill, training, workout |反义词：inactivity, idleness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I exercise regularly. = 我定期锻炼。
She exercises her authority. = 她行使她的权力。


一般过去时:
I exercised yesterday. = 我昨天锻炼了。
He exercised his option to buy. = 他行使了他的购买权。


现在完成时:
I have exercised every day this week. = 我这周每天都锻炼了。
She has exercised her influence on the committee. = 她对委员会施加了影响。


过去完成时:
I had exercised before I went to work. = 我上班前已经锻炼过了。
They had exercised their right to protest. = 他们已经行使了抗议的权利。


将来时:
I will exercise tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上会锻炼。

虚拟语气:

I wish I exercised more regularly. = 我希望我能更规律地锻炼。


被动语态:
The muscles are exercised during the workout. = 肌肉在锻炼过程中得到锻炼。

否定句:
