outclassing:/ˌaʊtˈklæsɪŋ/ | v. |胜过，超越 |To surpass or exceed in quality, ability, or performance.  |例句：His performance outclassed all the others. 他的表演胜过所有其他人。 |例句：The new model outclasses its predecessor in terms of fuel efficiency. 新车型在燃油效率方面超越了其前代产品。|近义词：surpass, excel, outdo, overshadow |反义词：underperform, be inferior to


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The new technology outclasses the old one. = 新技术胜过旧技术。
Their team consistently outclasses the competition. = 他们的团队始终胜过竞争对手。


一般过去时:
Our company outclassed its rivals in the last quarter. = 我们公司在上个季度胜过了竞争对手。
The champion easily outclassed his opponent. = 冠军轻松击败了他的对手。


现在完成时:
This year's crop has outclassed all previous harvests. = 今年的收成超过了以往所有年份。
She has consistently outclassed her peers in academic performance. = 在学业成绩方面，她一直都胜过同龄人。


过去完成时:
By the end of the game, our team had outclassed the other team by a wide margin. = 到比赛结束时，我们的球队已以较大优势胜过对方。
He had outclassed his opponent in every round of the debate. = 他在辩论的每一轮中都胜过了对手。


将来时:
We expect our new product to outclass the competition in the market. = 我们预计我们的新产品将在市场上胜过竞争对手。
The upcoming model will undoubtedly outclass its predecessor. = 即将推出的车型无疑将超越其前代产品。


虚拟语气:
If we had invested more, we might have outclassed our competitors. = 如果我们投资更多，我们可能会胜过竞争对手。
I wish I had trained harder so I could have outclassed my opponent. = 我希望我训练更刻苦，这样我就能胜过我的对手了。


被动语态:
The previous record was outclassed by the new athlete. = 先前的记录被新的运动员超越了。
Their strategy was outclassed by our innovative approach. = 他们的策略被我们的创新方法所超越。


疑问句:
Does this new software outclass the existing one? = 这款新软件是否胜过现有的软件？
Will the new car outclass its rivals in terms of safety features? = 新车在安全性能方面是否会胜过竞争对手？


否定句:
Their arguments did not outclass ours. = 他们的论点没有胜过我们的论点。
The team did not outclass its opponents in the final match. = 该队在决赛中没有胜过对手。
