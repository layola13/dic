saibling:/ˈzaɪblɪŋ/| n. |红点鲑鱼|A species of salmonid fish, typically found in alpine lakes and rivers of central Europe.  It is known for its reddish spots and is considered a delicacy. |The saibling is a prized fish in Austrian cuisine.|近义词：char, trout|反义词:  None specifically.
