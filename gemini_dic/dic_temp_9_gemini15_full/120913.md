miscarrying:/ˈmɪskæriɪŋ/| v.|流产，失败|The act of a pregnancy ending prematurely, before the fetus is viable.  It can also refer to the failure of a plan or project.|例句：She sadly experienced a miscarriage in her first trimester. 她不幸在怀孕初期流产了。|例句：His business plan was miscarrying due to poor market research. 他的商业计划由于市场调研不足而失败了。|近义词：aborting, failing|反义词：succeeding, carrying to term
