dysphagia:/dɪsˈfeɪdʒə/ | n. |吞咽困难 | Difficulty swallowing, often due to a problem with the muscles or nerves involved in swallowing.  This can be caused by various conditions, including stroke, neurological disorders, or structural problems in the throat or esophagus. | Example:  The patient presented with dysphagia and difficulty breathing.  这位病人出现吞咽困难和呼吸困难。 |近义词: swallowing difficulty, difficulty in swallowing, aphagia (inability to swallow) |反义词: easy swallowing
