inworn:/ˈɪnwɔːrn/| adj.|磨损的，用旧的|worn or damaged by long use; showing the effects of time and use.  The meaning implies a gradual deterioration due to prolonged use, rather than sudden damage.|例句：The inworn carpet was threadbare in places. 这块地毯有些地方磨损得很厉害。 The inworn leather of the chair spoke of years of comfortable use. 椅子上磨损的皮革诉说着多年舒适的使用。|近义词：worn, frayed, shabby, used, timeworn|反义词：new, unworn, pristine
