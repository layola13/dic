interdenominational: /ˌɪntərˌdɪˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 超教派的；跨宗派的 |  Relating to or involving different Christian denominations.  It suggests cooperation or unity among different Christian churches or groups that usually wouldn't work together. |

例句：

The interdenominational church hosted a community service for all faiths. = 这个超教派的教堂为所有信仰举办了一场社区服务。

An interdenominational council was formed to address issues of common concern. =  一个超教派委员会成立，以处理共同关心的问题。


近义词： ecumenical, cross-denominational

反义词： denominational, sectarian


词性：形容词
