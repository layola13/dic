marionettist:/ˌmærɪəˈnɛtɪst/ | n. |木偶表演者；操纵木偶的人 | A marionettist is a person who manipulates marionettes, which are puppets controlled by strings. They are skilled in operating the strings to bring the puppets to life, creating a captivating performance.  | Example: The skilled marionettist moved her fingers deftly, making the marionette dance gracefully across the stage.  这位技艺精湛的木偶表演者灵巧地移动手指，使木偶优雅地跳跃在舞台上。|近义词：puppeteer |反义词：audience, viewer
