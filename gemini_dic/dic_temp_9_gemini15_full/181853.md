relocations:/ˌriːləʊˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. |迁移；搬迁；重新安置 | The process of moving something or someone to a new location; the act of relocating.  Relocations can refer to people, businesses, or even data. | Example: The company is undergoing significant relocations as part of its restructuring. 公司正在进行大规模的搬迁，作为其重组的一部分。The relocations of refugees to safer areas are a top priority. 将难民迁移到更安全的地区是当务之急。 |近义词：moves, transfers, removals | 反义词：settlements, installations
