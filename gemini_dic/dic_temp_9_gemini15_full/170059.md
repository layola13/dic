It often implies a lack of respect for authority or established norms.|例句：Her protervity in challenging the professor's authority surprised everyone.  她的行为举止大胆地挑战了教授的权威，这令所有人感到惊讶。|近义词：audacity, insolence, impertinence, presumption|反义词：humility, deference, respect, submissiveness|名词
