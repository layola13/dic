USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and as such doesn't conjugate through tenses or voice.  It describes the manner in which an action is performed.  Therefore, tense, voice, and other grammatical categories are not applicable to this word.

He inly regretted his hasty decision. 他内心深处后悔自己草率的决定。

She inly smiled at his joke, though outwardly she remained composed. 她对他的笑话暗自微笑，尽管表面上她保持平静。


PHRASE->
There are no common multi-word phrases directly incorporating "inly".  Its usage is primarily as a standalone adverb.
