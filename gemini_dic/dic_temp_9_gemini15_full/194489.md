self-righteous:/ˌsɛlfˈraɪtʃəs/| adj.|自以为是的，自以为正义的|Having or showing a strong belief that your own beliefs and actions are morally right, often without considering others' views.  It implies a smug and superior attitude.|例句：He was self-righteous in his condemnation of others' actions. 他自以为是地谴责别人的行为。

例句：Her self-righteous pronouncements annoyed everyone. 她自以为是的言论惹恼了所有人。


近义词：sanctimonious, holier-than-thou, self-satisfied, judgmental, moralistic


反义词：humble, modest, unassuming, tolerant, open-minded


词源:The word "self-righteous" is a compound word.  "Self" refers to oneself, and "righteous" means morally right or virtuous.  The combination emphasizes the excessive or unwarranted nature of the belief in one's own righteousness.  The word emerged in the 16th century.
