hellbent:/ˈhelbent/| adj.|一心一意要；坚决要；顽固的 | Determined to do something, often recklessly or stubbornly.  |例句：He was hellbent on proving his innocence. 他一心一意要证明自己的清白。|例句：Despite warnings, she remained hellbent on her dangerous plan. 尽管受到了警告，她仍然坚持执行她危险的计划。|近义词：determined, resolute, insistent, adamant |反义词：hesitant, reluctant, unwilling, ambivalent


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is hellbent on winning the race. 他一心想赢得比赛。
They are hellbent on revenge. 他们一心想要报复。

一般过去时:
She was hellbent on getting her way. 她当时一心想按照自己的想法去做。
He was hellbent on finishing the project before the deadline. 他当时一心想在截止日期前完成项目。

现在完成时:
They have been hellbent on this idea for years. 他们多年来一直坚持这个想法。
She has been hellbent on success since she was a child. 从小她就一心想要成功。

过去完成时:
He had been hellbent on his career, neglecting his family. 他曾一心扑在事业上，忽略了家庭。
They had been hellbent on finding the truth, regardless of the cost.  他们曾不惜一切代价一心寻找真相。

将来时:
He will be hellbent on finishing the marathon, even if he's injured. 他将一心要跑完马拉松，即使他受伤了。
They will be hellbent on getting their money back. 他们将一心想要拿回他们的钱。

虚拟语气:
If he were hellbent on quitting his job, nothing could stop him. 如果他一心要辞职，什么也阻止不了他。
I wish I hadn't been so hellbent on winning. 我真希望我当时没有那么一心想要赢。

被动语态:  (Hellbent is rarely used in the passive voice.)  The plan was seemingly hellbent on failure.  这个计划似乎注定要失败。(This example uses "hellbent" metaphorically to describe the plan's trajectory.)
The project seemed hellbent on self-destruction. 这个项目似乎注定要自我毁灭。(This example also uses "hellbent" metaphorically.)


疑问句:
Is he really hellbent on ruining his life? 他真的要毁掉自己的生活吗？
Are they hellbent on causing trouble? 他们一心想要惹麻烦吗？


否定句:
He wasn't hellbent on succeeding at any cost. 他并非不惜一切代价地追求成功。
She wasn't hellbent on making him angry. 她并非一心想让他生气。
