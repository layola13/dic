sight-sing:/ˈsaɪtˌsɪŋ/ | v. |视唱| To sing a piece of music from its written notation without having heard it previously.  This involves reading the musical notation and interpreting it vocally in real time. | Example: She can sight-sing any melody given to her. 她能视唱任何给她的旋律。|近义词：read music, sing at sight |反义词：memorize, learn by heart
