duty-frees:/ˈdjuːtiːfriːz/ | n. | 免税店，免税商品 | Duty-free shops are retail businesses that sell goods without levying the usual taxes and duties. These shops are commonly located in airports, border areas, and on cruise ships, offering travelers the opportunity to purchase goods at significantly reduced prices.  |  Many tourists visit duty-free shops at the airport to buy perfumes and liquor.  许多游客在机场的免税店购买香水和酒。|tax-free shops,  免税店|taxed goods,  应税商品|名词
