deoxygenate:/diːˈɒksɪdʒəneɪt/|/ˈdiːɑːkˌsɪdʒəneɪt/ | vt. |脱氧；除氧 |To remove oxygen from (a substance or environment).  This process can occur naturally or be induced artificially.  It is frequently used in the context of biological systems and industrial processes. |例句：The process deoxygenates the water, creating an anaerobic environment.  这个过程使水脱氧，创造了一个厌氧环境。

The scientists deoxygenated the sample before analysis. 科学家们在分析前对样品进行了脱氧处理。


近义词：deoxidize, reduce(in a chemical context) |反义词：oxygenate |动词
