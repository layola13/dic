shelling:/ˈʃɛlɪŋ/| n. v.|炮击；去壳；脱壳|The act of firing shells from a large gun, or the act of removing shells from something.  |例句：The city endured weeks of relentless shelling.  这座城市承受了数周无情的炮击。|例句：The shelling of the coastal towns caused widespread damage. 对沿海城镇的炮击造成了广泛的破坏。|近义词：bombardment, shelling, bombardment, shelling|反义词：peace, ceasefire|动词
