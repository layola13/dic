SCADA:/ˈskædə/ | n. | 监控和数据采集 | Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition.  A system used to monitor and control industrial processes, such as those in power plants, pipelines, and manufacturing facilities. It typically involves sensors, actuators, and a central computer system that collects and analyzes data to make decisions and take actions.  | Example: The power company uses a SCADA system to monitor voltage levels across the grid.  电力公司使用SCADA系统监测电网的电压水平。|近义词: industrial control system, monitoring system |反义词: manual control system |名词
