deservingness:/dɪˈzɜːrvɪŋnəs/| n. |应得；值得；价值|The state or quality of being deserving; merit; worth.  It often refers to the extent to which someone or something deserves something positive or negative. |例句：His deservingness of the award is unquestionable. 他获得该奖项是毋庸置疑的。

例句：The deservingness of their suffering is a complex moral question.  他们遭受苦难是否应得是一个复杂的道德问题。


近义词：merit, worthiness, entitlement, rightfulness

反义词：unworthiness, undeservedness, lack of merit


词性：名词
