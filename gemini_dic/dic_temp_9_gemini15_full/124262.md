mot:/moʊt/| n.|护城河；壕沟|A mot is a ditch or trench, typically one surrounding a castle or fortified place.|例句：The castle was surrounded by a deep mot.  这座城堡被一条深深的护城河包围着。|例句：The mot provided a crucial defense against attackers.  护城河为防御攻击者提供了至关重要的防御。|近义词：ditch, trench, moat|反义词：bridge, passage
