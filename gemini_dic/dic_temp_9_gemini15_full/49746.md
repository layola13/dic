demoing:/ˈdɛmoʊɪŋ/| v. | 演示；展示 |  To demonstrate or show something, typically a product or a skill.  |例句：They are demoing their new software at the conference. 他们在会议上演示他们的新软件。|例句：The chef was demoing a new cooking technique.  这位厨师正在演示一种新的烹饪技巧。|近义词：showing, exhibiting, presenting |反义词：hiding, concealing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are demoing the new features today. = 我们今天正在演示新功能。
They demo new products every week. = 他们每周演示新产品。


一般过去时:
I demoed the software to the client yesterday. = 我昨天向客户演示了该软件。
She demoed her painting skills at the art fair. = 她在艺术博览会上展示了她的绘画技巧。


现在完成时:
We have demoed this product to several potential buyers. = 我们已经向几位潜在买家演示了这款产品。
They have demoed the system successfully. = 他们已经成功演示了该系统。


过去完成时:
I had demoed the product before the meeting started. = 会议开始前我已经演示了该产品。
They had demoed the prototype several times before the final presentation. = 在最终演示之前，他们已经演示了几次原型。


将来时:
We will demo the updated version next week. = 我们下周将演示更新版本。
They will be demoing the new car at the auto show. = 他们将在车展上演示新车。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would demo the software more thoroughly. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更彻底地演示该软件。
I wish I had demoed the product more effectively. = 我希望我能够更有效地演示该产品。


被动语态:
The new software is being demoed at the trade show. = 新软件正在贸易展上演示。
The product was demoed to a large audience. = 该产品向众多观众进行了演示。


疑问句:
Are you demoing the new software? = 你正在演示新软件吗？
Will they be demoing the car at the show? = 他们会在展会上演示汽车吗？


否定句:
We didn't demo the product yesterday. = 我们昨天没有演示该产品。
They won't be demoing the system tomorrow. = 他们明天不会演示该系统。


PHRASE->
demo a product = 演示产品
live demo = 现场演示
product demo = 产品演示

demo video = 演示视频
