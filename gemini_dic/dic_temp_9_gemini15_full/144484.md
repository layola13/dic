outthrow:/ˈaʊtθroʊ/| vt.|抛出，扔掉，超过|To throw or cast something outward or away; to surpass or exceed.|例句：He outthrew the ball to the outfield. 他把球扔到外场。|例句：Her talent outthrew all her classmates. 她的天赋超过了所有同学。|近义词：throw, cast, surpass, exceed|反义词：retrieve, retain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outthrows the competition every year. = 他每年都胜过竞争对手。
The athlete outthrows his opponents consistently. = 这位运动员始终超越他的对手。

一般过去时:
She outthrew her personal best in the javelin throw. = 她标枪投掷的成绩超过了她个人的最好成绩。
The pitcher outthrew the batter with a fastball. = 投手用快速球击败了击球手。

现在完成时:
He has outthrown all expectations this season. = 这个赛季他超出了所有人的预期。
The team has outthrown its opponents in every game. = 这支球队在每一场比赛中都投掷得比对手更远。

过去完成时:
By the end of the game, they had outthrown the opposing team by a wide margin. = 到比赛结束时，他们已经以很大的优势超过了对方球队。
She had outthrown her previous record before the final attempt. = 在最后一次尝试之前，她已经打破了她之前的纪录。

将来时:
He will outthrow his rivals in the upcoming tournament. = 他将在即将到来的锦标赛中超越他的对手。
The new design will outthrow the old model in terms of efficiency. = 新的设计在效率方面将会超过旧的型号。

虚拟语气:
If he trained harder, he would outthrow anyone in the league. = 如果他训练更努力，他就能超过联盟中的任何人。
I wish I could outthrow that record. = 我希望我能打破那项纪录。

被动语态:
The old record was outthrown by a young athlete. = 老纪录被一位年轻运动员打破了。
His predictions were outthrown by the actual results. = 他的预测被实际结果所超过。

疑问句:
Can he outthrow the current world record holder? = 他能超过现任世界纪录保持者吗？
Does this new machine outthrow the old one in terms of power? = 这台新机器在动力方面是否超过了旧机器？

否定句:
He didn't outthrow his opponent in the final round. = 他在决赛中没有超过他的对手。
The company's sales didn't outthrow their projections. = 公司的销售额没有超过他们的预期。
