reallocation:/ˌriːˌæləˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |重新分配，重新配置 | The act of allocating something again; assigning or distributing something anew.  It often involves changing the original distribution or assignment of resources, funds, or responsibilities. |例句：The company announced a reallocation of resources to its new project. 公司宣布将资源重新分配给新的项目。

The government implemented a reallocation of funds to address the crisis. 政府实施了资金重新分配以应对危机。|近义词：redistribution, reassignment, realignment |反义词：allocation (in its original state), concentration


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company reallocates resources annually. = 公司每年重新分配资源。
She reallocates her time to prioritize tasks. = 她重新分配时间以优先处理任务。


一般过去时:
They reallocated the budget after reviewing the expenses. = 他们审查费用后重新分配了预算。
He reallocated his staff to different departments. = 他将员工重新分配到不同的部门。


现在完成时:
We have reallocated the funds to the most urgent projects. = 我们已将资金重新分配给最紧急的项目。
The government has reallocated resources to support small businesses. = 政府已重新分配资源以支持小型企业。


过去完成时:
They had reallocated the funds before the project started. = 项目开始前，他们已经重新分配了资金。
She had reallocated her time before the deadline approached. = 截止日期临近之前，她已经重新分配了时间。


将来时:
We will reallocate the budget next quarter. = 我们将在下个季度重新分配预算。
The company will reallocate its staff after the merger. = 合并后，公司将重新分配员工。


虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we would reallocate them to research. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们会将它们重新分配给研究。
It would be wise to reallocate the funds if the project fails. = 如果项目失败，重新分配资金将是明智之举。


被动语态:
Resources were reallocated to the marketing department. = 资源被重新分配给了市场部。
The budget was reallocated after a thorough review. = 预算在彻底审查后被重新分配。


疑问句:
Will the budget be reallocated this year? = 今年预算会重新分配吗？
How will the resources be reallocated? = 资源将如何重新分配？


否定句:
They did not reallocate the funds as planned. = 他们没有按照计划重新分配资金。
The company will not reallocate resources to that project. = 公司不会将资源重新分配给那个项目。
