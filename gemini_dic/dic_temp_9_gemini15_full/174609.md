quited:/ˈkwaɪtɪd/| v.|平静下来；使平静；(使)安静|to become quiet or to make something quiet|The children quited down after their nap.孩子们午睡后安静下来了。|She tried to quite the noisy dog. 她试图让那条吵闹的狗安静下来。|calm, hush, still|noisy, loud, boisterous|动词


USAGE->

The teacher quiets the class. = 老师让课堂安静下来。
