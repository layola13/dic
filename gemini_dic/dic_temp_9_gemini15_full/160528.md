pork:/pɔːrk/| n. |猪肉|Meat from a pig.  Pork is a common ingredient in many cuisines around the world, used in various dishes and preparations. |例句：We had roast pork for Sunday dinner. 我们周日晚餐吃了烤猪肉。

例句：I added some pork to the stir-fry. 我在炒菜里加了一些猪肉。


近义词：pig meat, swine |反义词：无


词源:  The word "pork" comes from the Old French word "porc," which in turn derives from the Latin word "porcus," meaning "pig."  The evolution reflects the historical importance of pigs as a food source.
