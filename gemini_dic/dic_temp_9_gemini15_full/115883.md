marlstone:/ˈmɑːlstəʊn/| n. |泥灰岩|A type of sedimentary rock composed primarily of calcium carbonate and clay minerals.  It is often found in layers and is relatively soft. Marlstone is used in some areas for construction and agriculture.|例句：The foundation of the old house was built from local marlstone. 这栋老房子的地基是用当地的泥灰岩建造的。
The farmer spread marlstone on his fields to improve the soil.  这位农民在田里撒了泥灰岩来改善土壤。|近义词：limestone, calcareous shale|反义词：sandstone, granite
