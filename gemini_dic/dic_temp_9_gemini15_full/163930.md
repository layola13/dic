predevising: /ˌpriːdɪˈvaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 预先策划；预先设计 |  The act of planning or designing something in advance.  It implies careful consideration and preparation before implementation. | 例句：The team spent weeks predevising their marketing strategy. = 团队花了数周时间预先策划他们的营销策略。


The company was predevising a new product line to meet growing customer demand. = 公司正在预先设计一条新的产品线以满足不断增长的客户需求。| 近义词：planning, designing, scheming, plotting | 反义词：improvising, winging it | 动词
