quaverer: /ˈkwɛvərər/ | n. |颤抖的人；声音颤抖的人 | A person whose voice trembles, especially from nervousness or emotion.  |The young quaverer barely managed to utter a few words before breaking down in tears. 年轻的颤抖者在哭泣前几乎说不出几句话。|近义词：trembler,vibrator|反义词：confident speaker, steady voice
