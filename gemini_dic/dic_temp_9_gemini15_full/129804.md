non-German: /ˌnɒnˈdʒɜːrmən/ | adj. | 非德语的；非德国的 | Not relating to or originating from Germany or the German language.  The word describes something that is not connected to Germany in terms of origin, language, or culture.  | 例句：The museum featured a collection of non-German art.  博物馆展出了一批非德国艺术品。

例句：He speaks several non-German languages fluently. 他流利地说几种非德语语言。


近义词：foreign (to Germany), non-Germanic


反义词：German, Germanic

词性：形容词
