renowned:/rɪˈnaʊnd/| adj.|著名的，知名的|Well-known and respected for a particular skill, talent, or quality.|例句：He is a renowned expert in the field of artificial intelligence. 他是人工智能领域一位著名的专家。|近义词：famous, celebrated, distinguished, eminent|反义词：unknown, obscure, infamous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is renowned for his culinary skills. = 他以其烹饪技巧而闻名。
The city is renowned for its beautiful scenery. = 这座城市以其美丽的景色而闻名。


一般过去时:
She was renowned as a talented actress. = 她曾是一位才华横溢的女演员而闻名。
The company was renowned for its innovative products. = 这家公司以其创新产品而闻名。


现在完成时:
He has become renowned for his groundbreaking research. = 他因其开创性的研究而声名鹊起。
The museum has long been renowned for its extensive collection. = 这个博物馆长期以来以其丰富的收藏而闻名。


过去完成时:
The author had already become renowned before the publication of his first novel. = 在他的第一部小说出版之前，这位作者就已经名声在外了。
The artist had been renowned for his unique style for decades. = 几十年来，这位艺术家以其独特的风格而闻名。


将来时:
She will undoubtedly become renowned for her contributions to science. = 她无疑将因其对科学的贡献而闻名。
The band is expected to become renowned throughout the country. = 预计这个乐队将在全国范围内成名。


虚拟语气:
If he were renowned for his kindness, he would be much loved. = 如果他以他的善良而闻名，他将被许多人喜爱。
I wish I were renowned for my writing skills. = 我希望我以我的写作技巧而闻名。


被动语态:
The painting was renowned for its vibrant colors. = 这幅画以其鲜艳的色彩而闻名。
He is renowned as a brilliant strategist. = 他被誉为一位杰出的战略家。(被动语态，但表达主动含义)


疑问句:
Is he renowned for his work in conservation? = 他以其在环保方面的成就而闻名吗？
Is the region renowned for its wine production? = 这个地区以其葡萄酒生产而闻名吗？


否定句:
The restaurant is not renowned for its fine dining. = 这家餐厅并不以其精致的餐饮而闻名。
He is not yet renowned in the international art world. = 他在国际艺术界还没有名气。
