resuppliers:/ˌriːsəˈplaɪərz/| n. | 再供应者，补充供应者 | Those who provide additional supplies after an initial supply.  They ensure the continuation of resources.  | Example: The army relied on their resuppliers to bring in ammunition and food.  军队依靠他们的后勤人员运送弹药和食物。 |近义词: suppliers, replenishers |反义词: consumers, depleters
