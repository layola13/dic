make rounds:/meɪk raʊndz/| v.|查房，巡视|To regularly visit patients in a hospital or other institution.  This term is commonly used in the medical profession.|例句：The doctor makes rounds every morning at 7 am.  医生每天早上7点查房。|例句：The nurse made rounds to check on the patients' vital signs. 护士查房以检查病人的生命体征。|近义词：visit, inspect, patrol|反义词：stay put, remain stationary


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor makes rounds every morning. = 医生每天早上查房。
The nurses make their rounds throughout the day. = 护士们整天都在查房。

一般过去时:
The doctor made rounds yesterday afternoon. = 医生昨天下午查房了。
The team made their rounds before the surgery. = 团队在手术前进行了查房。

现在完成时:
The doctor has already made rounds this morning. = 医生今天早上已经查房了。
The nurses have made their rounds for the day. = 护士们今天已经查房了。

过去完成时:
The doctor had made rounds before the emergency. = 紧急情况发生前医生已经查房了。
They had made their rounds before the fire alarm went off. = 火警响起之前他们已经查房了。

将来时:
The doctor will make rounds tomorrow morning. = 医生明天早上将查房。
The team will make their rounds after breakfast. = 团队将在早餐后查房。

虚拟语气:
If I were a doctor, I would make rounds every day. = 如果我是医生，我每天都会查房。
I wish I could make rounds with the chief physician. = 我希望我能和主任医生一起查房。

被动语态:
Rounds are made twice a day. = 一天查房两次。
The patients are checked during rounds. = 在查房期间会检查病人。

疑问句:
Does the doctor make rounds today? = 医生今天查房吗？
When do the nurses make their rounds? = 护士们什么时候查房？

否定句:
The doctor didn't make rounds yesterday. = 医生昨天没有查房。
The nurses didn't make their rounds on time. = 护士们没有按时查房。


PHRASE->
make hospital rounds = 医院查房
make ward rounds = 病房查房
make morning rounds = 早上查房
make rounds on patients = 查访病人
to make the rounds = 巡视 (更广泛的意义，不局限于医疗)
