make-with:/meɪk wɪð/| v. |用…制作，借助…制作|To create or produce something using specific materials or methods.|例句：I made this cake with fresh strawberries. 我用新鲜草莓做了这个蛋糕。|近义词：create with, construct with, fabricate with|反义词：destroy, demolish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She makes bread with whole wheat flour. = 她用全麦面粉做面包。
They make toys with recycled materials. = 他们用回收材料制作玩具。


一般过去时:
He made a birdhouse with scrap wood. = 他用废木头做了一个鸟舍。
We made pizza with leftover vegetables. = 我们用剩下的蔬菜做了披萨。


现在完成时:
She has made several sculptures with clay. = 她已经用粘土制作了几尊雕塑。
They have made a lot of progress with the project. = 他们在项目上取得了很大进展。


过去完成时:
He had made the cake with his grandmother's recipe. = 他按照他祖母的食谱做了蛋糕。
They had finished the painting with only a few brushes. = 他们只用几支画笔就完成了这幅画。


将来时:
I will make a new dress with this beautiful fabric. = 我要用这块漂亮的布料做一件新衣服。
We will build a house with sustainable materials. = 我们将用可持续材料建造房屋。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would make a quilt with colorful fabrics. = 如果我有更多时间，我会用色彩鲜艳的布料做一床被子。
I wish I could make a delicious meal with such limited ingredients. = 我真希望我能用这么少的食材做出一顿美味的饭菜。


被动语态:
The cake was made with organic ingredients. = 这个蛋糕是用有机原料制作的。
The house is being made with environmentally friendly materials. = 这栋房子正在用环保材料建造。


疑问句:
What did you make with the leftover chicken? = 你用剩下的鸡肉做了什么？
What kind of paint is this painting made with? = 这幅画是用什么颜料画的？


否定句:
I didn't make the soup with enough salt. = 我做的汤盐放的不够。
They haven't made the furniture with high-quality wood. = 他们没有用优质木材制作家具。
