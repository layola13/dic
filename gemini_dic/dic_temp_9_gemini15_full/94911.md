in-group:/ˈɪnɡruːp/| n. |内群，内群体 |A social group to which a person psychologically identifies as being a member.  Members of an in-group share a common identity and sense of belonging, often exhibiting loyalty and favoritism towards each other.  |例句：The team felt like a strong in-group, supporting each other through thick and thin.  团队感觉像一个强大的内群体，互相支持，风雨同舟。|例句：  He demonstrated in-group bias by consistently favoring his own colleagues in the project. 他通过在项目中始终偏袒自己的同事来表现出内群体偏见。|近义词：inner circle, clique, ingroup |反义词：outgroup
