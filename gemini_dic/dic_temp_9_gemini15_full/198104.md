shoot:/ʃuːt/| /ʃʊt/| vt. n.|射击；拍摄；发芽；快速移动|to fire a weapon; to take a photograph; to grow rapidly; to move quickly|例句：He shot the deer. 他射杀了鹿。|例句：The director shot the film in three months. 导演三个月内拍完了这部电影。|近义词：fire, film, sprout, dart|反义词：miss, fail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shoots hoops every day. = 他每天都投篮。
She shoots stunning photographs. = 她拍摄令人惊叹的照片。

一般过去时:
They shot a movie last year. = 他们去年拍了一部电影。
He shot the ball into the basket. = 他把球投进了篮筐。

现在完成时:
We have shot three films this year. = 今年我们已经拍摄了三部电影。
The plant has shot up considerably. = 植物长得相当高了。

过去完成时:
He had shot the target before the others even aimed. = 在其他人瞄准之前，他已经射中了目标。
The tree had shot up to ten feet before the frost hit. = 霜冻来临之前，树已经长到十英尺高了。

将来时:
They will shoot the wedding video next month. = 他们下个月将拍摄婚礼视频。
The seeds will shoot once the weather warms. = 天气暖和后，种子就会发芽。

虚拟语气:
If I had a gun, I would shoot. = 如果我有一把枪，我会射击。
If he had shot better, he would have won. = 如果他射得更好，他就会赢了。

被动语态:
The film was shot on location. = 这部电影是在外景地拍摄的。
The arrow was shot by a skilled archer. = 这支箭是由一个熟练的弓箭手射出的。

疑问句:
Did you shoot that bird? = 你射杀了那只鸟吗？
Will they shoot the movie in black and white? = 他们会用黑白胶片拍摄这部电影吗？

否定句:
He didn't shoot the bird. = 他没有射杀那只鸟。
I haven't shot a photo in years. = 我好几年没拍照了。


PHRASE->
shoot for the stars = 勇于追求伟大的目标

shoot up = 快速生长；迅速增加
