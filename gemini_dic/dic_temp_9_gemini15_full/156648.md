picture-postcard: /ˈpɪktʃə(r) ˌpoʊstˌkɑːrd/ | n. |明信片 | A picture postcard is a postcard with a picture on it, often used to send greetings or record a trip.  It's a common way to share scenic views or personal experiences with others. | Example: I sent a picture-postcard to my family from Paris. 我从巴黎寄了一张明信片给我的家人。  I received a picture-postcard showing a beautiful beach. 我收到一张明信片，上面是美丽的沙滩。|近义词：postcard, greeting card |反义词：letter (in terms of informality and brevity)


词源:

* **picture:**  From Old French *picture*, ultimately from Latin *pictura* ("painting").
* **post:** From Old French *poste*, ultimately from Latin *positus* ("placed").  Relates to the postal service.
* **card:** From Old French *carte*, from Latin *charta* ("paper").

用法说明：

Picture-postcard is primarily a noun.  It describes a type of postcard.  While it doesn't have verb or adjective forms, it can be used attributively (e.g., "a picture-postcard scene").
