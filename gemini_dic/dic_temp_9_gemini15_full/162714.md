prebarbaric: /ˌpriːbɑːrˈbærɪk/ | adj. | 前野蛮的，半野蛮的，处于野蛮状态之前的 |  Describes a state or period preceding full-blown barbarism, often characterized by rudimentary social structures, limited technology, and a lack of sophisticated culture, but not yet entirely lacking in order or organization.  It suggests a transition from a more primitive state towards, but not yet reaching, full barbarism. |  The prebarbaric era saw the development of basic agriculture and rudimentary tools.  前野蛮时代见证了基本农业和简陋工具的发展。  | 近义词：proto-barbaric, primitive, undeveloped | 反义词：civilized, cultured, sophisticated |  adj.
