remarker:/rɪˈmɑːrkər/ | n. | 评论者；说话者；备注者 | A person who makes remarks or comments.  Someone who notes or observes something and mentions it. |例句：The remarker at the meeting offered some insightful observations.  会议上的评论者提出了一些有见地的看法。

例句：The remarker on the article provided a critical analysis of the author's arguments. 文章的评论者对作者的论点进行了批判性分析。


近义词：commentator, observer, annotator, critic

反义词：listener (in the sense of not actively participating)


词性: 名词
