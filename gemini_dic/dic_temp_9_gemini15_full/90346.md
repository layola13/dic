hov:/hɒv/|v.|悬停，盘旋|To remain in one place in the air.|例句：The helicopter hovered above the building. 直升机盘旋在建筑物上方。例句：The bird hovered over its nest.  鸟儿盘旋在巢穴上方。|近义词：float, hang, drift|反义词：plunge, descend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The helicopter hovers above the city. = 直升机盘旋在城市上空。
The hummingbird hovers near the flower. = 蜂鸟盘旋在花朵附近。

一般过去时:
The hawk hovered over its prey. = 老鹰盘旋在猎物上方。
The drone hovered silently for a few minutes. = 无人机无声地盘旋了几分钟。

现在完成时:
The helicopter has hovered in the same spot for ten minutes. = 直升机已经在同一个位置盘旋了十分钟。
The bird has hovered over the field for hours. = 这只鸟已经在田地上空盘旋了好几个小时。

过去完成时:
The plane had hovered before it landed. = 飞机在降落前盘旋过。
The helicopter had hovered for a long time before the rescue. = 直升机在救援之前盘旋了很久。

将来时:
The drone will hover over the accident scene. = 无人机将盘旋在事故现场上方。
The helicopter will hover while they lower the supplies. = 直升机将在他们放下物资时盘旋。


虚拟语气:
If the helicopter hovered longer, we could have taken better photos. = 如果直升机盘旋的时间更长，我们就能拍到更好的照片。
I wish the hummingbird had hovered closer, so I could see it better. = 我希望蜂鸟能更靠近些盘旋，这样我就能看得更清楚。

被动语态:
The camera was hovered above the scene. = 相机被悬停在场景上方。
The drone is being hovered to get a better view. = 无人机正在被悬停以获得更好的视野。

疑问句:
Is the helicopter hovering safely? = 直升机悬停安全吗？
Does the bird usually hover for so long? = 这只鸟通常会盘旋这么久吗？

否定句:
The helicopter didn't hover long enough. = 直升机盘旋的时间不够长。
The bee didn't hover near the flower. = 蜜蜂没有在花朵附近盘旋。
