candied:/ˈkændid/| adj.|蜜饯的；糖渍的|Covered or preserved with sugar; sweet and sugary.|例句：The candied ginger was a perfect complement to the spicy curry.  蜜饯姜完美地补充了辛辣咖喱的味道。
例句：She enjoyed the candied fruit in her holiday cake. 她喜欢节日蛋糕里的蜜饯水果。|近义词：crystallized, sugared|反义词：unsweetened, plain
