densest: /ˈdɛnsɪst/ | adj. | 最密集的，最稠密的 |  Most dense; having the greatest density.  It describes the superlative degree of density, indicating the highest concentration or compactness. | 例句：The densest part of the forest was almost impenetrable.  森林中最稠密的部分几乎无法通行。

The densest population centers are located along the coast.  人口最密集的中心位于沿海地区。


近义词：thickest, most compact, most concentrated | 反义词：least dense, sparsest, most rarefied | 词性：形容词
