radiately:/ˈreɪdieɪtli/| adv.|辐射状地，辐射般地|In a radiating manner; spreading out from a central point.  The flowers bloomed radiately from the center of the plant.  花朵从植物中心辐射状地绽放。|近义词：radiantly, outwardly|反义词：centrally


USAGE->

The sun radiates heat. = 太阳辐射热量。
The light radiates outwards. = 光线向外辐射。
