noninheritable: /ˌnɒnɪnˈhɛrɪtəbl/ | adj. | 非遗传的，不可遗传的 | Not capable of being inherited genetically.  This adjective describes traits, characteristics, or diseases that are not passed down from parents to offspring through genes. | Example:  Acquired characteristics, such as calluses, are noninheritable.  获得性特征，例如老茧，是非遗传的。 | 近义词: acquired, nonhereditary | 反义词: heritable, hereditary, genetic
