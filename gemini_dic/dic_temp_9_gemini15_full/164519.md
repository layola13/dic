preessay:/ˈpriːˌɛseɪ/| n. |预习论文;预备论文|A preessay is a preliminary essay or a draft written before a final essay, often used for planning and outlining the main points and arguments.|Example: I wrote a preessay to organize my thoughts before starting my final paper.  = 我写了一篇预习论文来整理我的思路，然后再开始写我的最终论文。|近义词：draft, outline, preliminary draft|反义词：final essay
