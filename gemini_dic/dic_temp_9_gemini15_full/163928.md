predevised:/ˌpriːdɪˈvaɪzd/| adj.|预先设计的，预先计划的|  pre-planned or arranged in advance.  The strategy was predevised to minimize risk.|近义词：prearranged, preplanned, planned beforehand|反义词：improvised, spontaneous|


例句：

The team's predevised strategy led them to victory.  团队预先制定的策略使他们取得了胜利。

They had a predevised escape route in case of emergency. 他们预先设计了一条紧急逃生路线。


词源:

* **pre-**:  prefix meaning "before" or "in advance" (来自拉丁语prae)
* **devise**: verb meaning "to plan or invent something" (来自古法语 deviser,  最终来自拉丁语 dividere "to divide")

因此，predevised 字面意思是“在之前设计好的”。
