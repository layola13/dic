capitation:/ˌkæpɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |人头税；按人头付费；总额付费|The system of payment in which a physician or other healthcare provider receives a fixed amount of money per patient for a specified period of time, regardless of the number of services provided.|例句：The clinic operates under a capitation system, receiving a fixed fee per patient enrolled.|近义词：per capita payment, per head payment|反义词：fee-for-service
