pinnyed: /ˈpɪnid/ | adj. |别针别住的；用别针钉住的 |  Pinned describes something fastened or secured with a pin or pins.  It often implies a temporary fastening. | 例句：The notice was pinned to the board.  公告被别在布告板上。

The fabric was pinned together before sewing. 布料在缝制前被别在一起。


近义词：fastened, attached, secured | 反义词：unpinned, detached, loose | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The notice is pinned to the board. = 公告别在布告板上。
The map is pinned on the wall. = 地图别在墙上。

一般过去时:
She pinned the note to his jacket. = 她把便条别在他的夹克上。
He pinned the drawing to the corkboard. = 他把图画别在软木板上。

现在完成时:
I have pinned the fabric together. = 我已经把布料别在一起了。
She has pinned the decorations to the wall. = 她已经把装饰品别在墙上了。

过去完成时:
They had pinned the poster before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经把海报别好了。
He had pinned the photos to the album. = 他已经把照片别在相册上了。

将来时:
I will pin the shirt to the ironing board. = 我将会把衬衫别在烫衣板上。
She will pin the flowers to her dress. = 她将会把花别在她的裙子上。

虚拟语气:
If I had a pin, I would pin the paper together. = 如果我有一根别针，我会把纸别在一起。
I wish I had pinned the map before we left. = 我真希望我们离开前我把地图别好了。

被动语态:
The notice was pinned to the board by the secretary. = 公告被秘书别在布告板上。
The photograph was pinned onto the display. = 照片被别在展示板上。

疑问句:
Is the drawing pinned to the wall? = 图画别在墙上了吗？
Are the papers pinned together? = 纸张别在一起了吗？

否定句:
The fabric wasn't pinned properly. = 布料没有别好。
The poster isn't pinned to the wall. = 海报没有别在墙上。


PHRASE->
pin down: 确定；查明
pin one's hopes on: 把希望寄托于
pin someone down: 强迫某人表决；使某人无法动弹
pin something on someone: 将某事归咎于某人
pin up: 别住；张贴
