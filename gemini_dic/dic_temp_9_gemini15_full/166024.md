preperusing:/ˌpriːpəˈruːzɪŋ/| v.|预先研读，预习|To read or examine (something) beforehand; to study in advance.|例句：I spent the weekend preperusing the documents for the meeting. 我花了周末时间预先研读了会议的文件。


例句：She preperused the textbook chapters before attending the lecture. 她在上课前预习了课本章节。


近义词：reviewing, studying, previewing, examining


反义词：neglecting, ignoring


词性:动词
