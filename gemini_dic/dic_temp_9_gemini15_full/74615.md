freckly:/ˈfrɛkli/| adj.| 长满雀斑的；雀斑的 | Having many freckles.  |The girl had freckly skin and bright red hair.  那个女孩皮肤上长满了雀斑，头发是亮红色。|spotted, speckled|unblemished, clear


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is freckly. = 他长满雀斑。
She has freckly skin. = 她皮肤长满雀斑。

一般过去时:
He was freckly as a child. = 他小时候长满雀斑。
The boy was freckly and cute. = 那个男孩长满雀斑，很可爱。

现在完成时:
She has always been freckly. = 她一直都长满雀斑。
He has remained freckly despite his age. = 尽管上了年纪，他仍然长满雀斑。

过去完成时:
He had been freckly for many years. = 他多年来一直长满雀斑。
She had always been freckly, even as a baby. = 她从小就长满雀斑，即使是婴儿时期也是如此。


将来时:
He will probably still be freckly in ten years. = 十年后他可能仍然长满雀斑。
She will likely remain freckly throughout her life. = 她很可能一生都会长满雀斑。


虚拟语气:
If he were freckly, he would look different. = 如果他长满雀斑，他会看起来不一样。
I wish I were freckly; I think it's cute. = 我希望我长满雀斑；我觉得那很可爱。


被动语态:
(This adjective doesn't typically use the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Is he freckly? = 他长满雀斑吗？
Is she freckly-faced? = 她长着一张雀斑脸吗？


否定句:
He isn't freckly. = 他不长雀斑。
Her skin isn't freckly; it's clear. = 她的皮肤不长雀斑；它是干净的。


PHRASE->
freckly face = 雀斑脸
freckly complexion = 雀斑肤色
freckly nose = 长满雀斑的鼻子
sun-freckled = 晒斑的 (implies freckles caused by sun exposure)
lightly freckled = 稍微有点雀斑的


词源:
The word "freckly" derives from the Old English word "freccan," meaning "to sprinkle."  This reflects the appearance of freckles as small, scattered spots on the skin.
