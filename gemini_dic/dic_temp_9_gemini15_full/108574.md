USAGE->
This is a possessive form, not a word with independent grammatical tenses.  It shows possession and doesn't conjugate.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.


PHRASE->
Lea's house = 利亚的房子
Lea's car = 利亚的车
Lea's book = 利亚的书
Lea's idea = 利亚的想法
