repackager:/ˌriːˈpækɪdʒər/| n. |重新包装者；重新包装软件|A repackager is a person or company that takes existing software and repackages it for resale or redistribution, often with modifications or added features.  They may change the installer, add custom branding, or integrate with other applications. Repackaging can also involve changing the licensing model.|Example: The software repackager modified the installer to include a custom logo and support email address.  重新包装软件的公司修改了安装程序，加入了自定义的徽标和支持邮箱地址。|近义词:redistributor, reseller|反义词:developer, original creator|名词
