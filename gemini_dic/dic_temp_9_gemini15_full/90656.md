humus:/ˈhjuːməs/ | n. |腐殖质，腐殖土 |Dark organic matter that forms in soil when plant and animal matter decays.  It is a vital component of fertile soil, providing nutrients and improving soil structure. |The rich humus of the forest floor supported a thriving ecosystem.  这片森林的地表富含腐殖质，孕育着繁荣的生态系统。| fertile soil, topsoil | sandy soil, barren land |名词
