lettuces:/ˈlɛtɪsɪz/| n. |莴苣（复数）|Lettuce refers to the leaves of a leafy green plant, commonly used in salads and other dishes.  It is typically crisp and has a slightly bitter taste, though some varieties are milder. The plural form "lettuces" indicates more than one lettuce plant or head of lettuce.|例句：I bought several lettuces at the farmers market. 我在农贸市场买了好多莴苣。

The salad contained various lettuces, including romaine and butterhead.  沙拉里包含各种莴苣，包括罗马生菜和奶油生菜。|近义词：salad greens, leafy greens |反义词:None

词源:
The word "lettuce" comes from the Old French word "laitue," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "lactuca."  "Lactuca" is related to the Latin word "lac," meaning "milk," referring to the milky sap found in lettuce plants.
