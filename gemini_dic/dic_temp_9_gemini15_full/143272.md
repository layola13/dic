outdo:/ˌaʊtˈduː/| vt.|胜过，超过，比…做得好|To surpass or exceed in performance or achievement.|例句：He outdid himself in the competition. 他在比赛中超越了自己。|例句：Her latest novel outdoes all her previous works. 她的最新小说胜过她以前的所有作品。|近义词：surpass, excel, exceed, beat|反义词：underperform, fail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outdoes his rivals in every aspect. 他在各个方面都胜过他的对手。
They outdo each other in generosity. 他们互相比谁更慷慨。


一般过去时:
She outdid her best time in the race.  她在比赛中跑出了比以往最好的成绩还要好的时间。
The team outdid their expectations.  这个团队超越了他们的预期。


现在完成时:
He has outdone himself with this project. 他在这个项目中超越了自己。
She has outdone all her previous achievements. 她已经超越了她以前的所有成就。


过去完成时:
They had outdone their competitors by the end of the year. 到年底，他们已经超越了他们的竞争对手。
The company had outdone its sales targets for the quarter. 公司已经超过了本季度的销售目标。


将来时:
I believe she will outdo her previous records.我相信她会打破之前的纪录。
He will outdo himself at the next performance. 他将在下一次演出中超越自己。


虚拟语气:
If only I could outdo my fear.  如果我能克服我的恐惧就好了。
I wish I could outdo my brother in this competition. 我希望我能在这次比赛中胜过我的哥哥。


被动语态:
His performance was outdone by his rival. 他的表现被他的对手超越了。
The previous record was outdone last month. 上个月打破了之前的纪录。


疑问句:
Can he outdo his rivals' performance? 他能超越对手的表现吗？
Will they be able to outdo their competitors? 他们能够胜过竞争对手吗？


否定句:
He did not outdo his opponent. 他没有胜过他的对手。
They couldn't outdo the current market leader. 他们无法超越目前的市场领导者。


PHRASE->
outdo oneself:  超越自己
outdo the competition:  胜过竞争对手
outdo expectations: 超越预期
outdo the previous record:打破之前的记录
outdo in performance: 表现上胜过
