frond:/frɒnd/| n. |叶状体；(植物的)叶状枝；羽状叶|A frond is a large, leaf-like structure, especially of a fern or palm.  It often refers to the entire leaf-like structure, including the stem and its subdivisions.|The fern unfurled its new fronds in the spring.  蕨类植物在春天舒展出新的叶状体。|leaf, foliage|stem, trunk|名词
