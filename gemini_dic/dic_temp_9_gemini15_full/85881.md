headbutt:/ˈhɛdˌbʌt/| n. & v.|头槌；用头撞|To strike with the head.  A forceful strike with the head.|例句：He headbutted his opponent in a fit of rage. 他盛怒之下用头撞了他的对手。

例句：The goat headbutted the fence.  山羊用头撞了栅栏。


近义词：butt, ram, strike (with the head)|反义词：None specifically, but actions like gentle touch or caress are opposites in nature.
