legends:/ˈlɛdʒəndz/ | n. |传说，传奇人物，传奇故事 | Stories, often mythical or based on historical events, that have been passed down through generations.  Legends often blend fact and fiction and are told to explain natural phenomena, historical events, or cultural practices. | Example: The legends of King Arthur and his knights are still popular today.  =亚瑟王及其骑士的传说至今仍然广受欢迎。

The legend of Robin Hood is a classic tale of bravery and justice. = 罗宾汉的传说是一个关于勇敢和正义的经典故事。


近义词：myths, tales, stories, sagas | 反义词：facts, realities
