cubicles:/ˈkjuːbɪklz/ | n. |小隔间，小房间 | Small, enclosed areas, often found in offices, providing individual workspaces.  They are typically partitioned off from the rest of the room. |Example:  He worked in a small cubicle in a large office building. 他在一个大型办公楼里的小隔间里工作。


The employees were assigned to their own cubicles. 员工们被分配到他们自己的隔间。|近义词：compartments, stalls, booths |反义词：open plan office (开放式办公室)

词源:

The word "cubicle" comes from the Latin word "cubiculum," meaning "small chamber" or "bedroom."  It entered English in the 16th century.
