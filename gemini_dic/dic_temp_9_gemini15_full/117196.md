measureless: /ˈmɛʒərɪs/ | adj. | 无限的；无法测量的；广阔无垠的 |  Beyond measure; immeasurable; vast. The adjective "measureless" describes something so large, extensive, or abundant that it cannot be measured or quantified. It implies an overwhelming sense of scale or quantity. | Example: The desert stretched before them, a measureless expanse of sand and sun.  沙漠在他们面前绵延不绝，一片无垠的沙子和阳光。  The ocean's depth is measureless in many places.  在许多地方，海洋的深度是无法测量的。|近义词：boundless, immeasurable, limitless, infinite, vast |反义词：measurable, limited, finite
