codevelopers:/ˌkoʊdɪˈvɛləpərz/ | n. | 共同开发者，合作开发者 | People who work together to develop something, typically software or a project.  | Example: The two companies are codevelopers on a new mobile game.  两个公司是这款新手机游戏的共同开发者。 |近义词: collaborators, joint developers, partners |反义词:  competitors, rivals


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The codevelopers are working on the new feature. = 共同开发者们正在开发新功能。
Our team and their team are codeveloping a new marketing strategy. = 我们的团队和他们的团队正在共同制定新的营销策略。

一般过去时:
The codevelopers finished the project on time. = 共同开发者按时完成了项目。
They codeveloped a successful app last year. = 他们去年共同开发了一个成功的应用程序。
