self-banished:/ˌsɛlfˈbænɪʃt/| adj.|自我放逐的；自我流放的 |  Someone who is self-banished has voluntarily exiled themselves, usually as a form of punishment or to escape a difficult situation.  This implies a conscious decision to remove oneself from a particular place or community. | Example: The disgraced politician lived a self-banished life in a remote cabin.  这位名誉扫地的政治家在一个偏远的木屋里过着自我放逐的生活。  |近义词：exiled, self-imposed exile, ostracized |反义词：integrated, welcomed, accepted | 词性:形容词
