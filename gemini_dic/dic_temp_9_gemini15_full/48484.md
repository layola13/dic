deepavali:/ˌdiːpəˈvɑːli/| n. |排灯节，屠妖节|The festival of lights, celebrated by Hindus, Jains, and Sikhs to signify the victory of light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over ignorance.  It is marked by the lighting of lamps, fireworks, and festive gatherings. |例句：Deepavali is a vibrant and colourful festival.  排灯节是一个充满活力和色彩的节日。

例句：We celebrated Deepavali with our family and friends. 我们和家人朋友一起庆祝排灯节。


近义词：Diwali (alternative spelling)


反义词：None (as it's a festival, not an adjective or something with an inherent opposite)


词性：Noun
