filiciform:/ˈfɪlɪsɪfɔːrm/| adj.|蕨叶形的，似蕨叶的|Resembling a fern in form or shape, especially in having finely divided, feathery leaves.  The term often refers to the shape of certain organs or structures in plants or animals.|例句：The delicate, filiciform fronds of the maidenhair fern unfurled in the spring breeze. 春风中，铁线蕨纤细的蕨叶状叶片舒展开来。

The dissected, filiciform leaves of the plant mimicked the appearance of a miniature fern. 植物的解剖状蕨叶状叶子模仿了微型蕨类的外观。|近义词：fernlike, frondose|反义词：needle-like, scale-like
