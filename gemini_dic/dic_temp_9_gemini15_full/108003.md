lassoed:/læˈsoʊd/| v. |套索系住；用套索抓住| To catch or secure (something, especially an animal) with a lasso.  |The cowboy lassoed the runaway calf.  牛仔用套索套住了逃跑的小牛。|  The rancher lassoed the wild horse. 牧场主用套索套住了野马。|  capture, snare, rope |  free, release |动词
