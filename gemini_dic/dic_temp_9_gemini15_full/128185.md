neuralgic:/nʊˈrældʒɪk/| adj. |神经痛的，神经痛似的| Relating to or characterized by neuralgia; of, relating to, or affected by neuralgia.  Neuralgic pain is sharp, stabbing, and often comes in episodes.  It's typically caused by irritation or damage to a nerve.|例句：She suffered from severe neuralgic pain in her face. 她脸上遭受了剧烈的牙神经痛。|例句：The doctor suspected a neuralgic condition was causing his leg pain. 医生怀疑他的腿痛是由神经痛引起的。|近义词：nervy, painful, sharp|反义词：numb, painless
