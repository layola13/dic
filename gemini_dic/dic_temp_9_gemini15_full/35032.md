close-down:/ˈkloʊz daʊn/| v. n.| 关闭；停业；倒闭 | To cease operations; to shut down completely.  |例句：The factory will close down next month. = 这个工厂下个月将关闭。|例句：The government ordered a close-down of all non-essential businesses. = 政府下令关闭所有非必要企业。|近义词：shutdown, closure, cessation |反义词：opening, commencement, startup | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company closes down every year for the holidays. = 公司每年假期都会关闭。
They close down the shop early on Sundays. = 他们星期天很早就关店了。


一般过去时:
The restaurant closed down after only six months. = 那家餐馆只开了六个月就倒闭了。
They closed down the bridge for repairs. = 他们关闭了桥梁进行维修。


现在完成时:
The factory has closed down due to financial difficulties. = 工厂由于财务困难已经倒闭了。
They have closed down several branches of the bank. = 他们已经关闭了几家银行分行。


过去完成时:
The business had closed down before we even arrived. = 我们到达之前，那家企业就已经倒闭了。
The government had closed down the borders before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，政府已经关闭了边境。


将来时:
The store will close down at the end of the year. = 这家商店将在年底关闭。
They will close down the power plant next year. = 他们明年将关闭发电厂。


虚拟语气:
If the business didn't improve, they would close down. = 如果生意没有好转，他们就会倒闭。
I wish they hadn't closed down my favorite restaurant. = 我真希望他们没有关闭我最喜欢的餐馆。


被动语态:
The school was closed down due to safety concerns. = 学校由于安全问题被关闭了。
The project was closed down because of funding issues. = 该项目由于资金问题而被终止。


疑问句:
Will the factory close down? = 工厂会倒闭吗？
Did the company close down last year? = 那家公司去年倒闭了吗？


否定句:
The store didn't close down; it just moved locations. = 商店没有倒闭，只是搬迁了。
They didn't close down the highway despite the heavy snow. = 尽管大雪纷飞，他们也没有关闭高速公路。


PHRASE->
close down shop = 停业
close down a business = 关闭一家企业
close down a factory = 关闭一家工厂
close down a meeting = 结束会议
emergency close-down = 紧急关闭
