corsairs:/kɔːˈsɛrz/| n. |海盗；私掠船| A corsair is a pirate, especially one operating in the Mediterranean Sea.  Corsairs were often authorized by governments to attack enemy shipping, though they frequently operated outside of any legal framework. |The Barbary corsairs terrorized Mediterranean shipping for centuries.|近义词：pirate, privateer|反义词：navy, merchant
