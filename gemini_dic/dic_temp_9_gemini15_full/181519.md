rekindle: /riˈkɪndl/ | vt. | 重新点燃；复燃；重新唤起 | To revive or renew something, especially a feeling, relationship, or activity.  | 例句：They rekindled their romance after many years apart.  他们多年分离后重燃爱火。

例句：The couple rekindled their old flame after a long separation. 这对夫妇在长期分居后重燃旧情。


近义词：revive, reignite, reawaken, renew, reanimate | 反义词：extinguish, quench, suppress
