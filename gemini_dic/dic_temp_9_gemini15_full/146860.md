overindustrialisms: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌstriəlɪzəmz/ | n. |过度工业化 |The excessive development of industries, often leading to negative consequences for the environment and society.  It refers to the state or condition of having gone beyond a reasonable or sustainable level of industrialization.|例句：The overindustrialisms of the 20th century led to widespread pollution and resource depletion.  20世纪的过度工业化导致了广泛的污染和资源枯竭。|近义词：industrial excess, hyperindustrialization |反义词：deindustrialization, sustainable development
