conglutin:/kənˈɡluːtɪn/ | n. |胶结物；凝结物；凝聚剂 | A substance that causes things to stick together; a glue-like material.  It often refers to a protein found in plants. | Example: The conglutin in the seeds helped them adhere to the soil.  种子中的胶结物有助于它们粘附在土壤中。|近义词：adhesive, agglutinant, cement |反义词：dispersant, solvent |名词
