musky:/ˈmʌski/| adj.|麝香的；有麝香味的；强烈的；令人作呕的 |Having a strong, often unpleasant, smell like that of musk.  The smell is often described as earthy, sweet, and sometimes slightly fecal.|例句：The room had a musky odor.  房间里有一股麝香味。

The old book smelled musky and slightly damp. 这本旧书闻起来有股麝香味，还略带潮湿。

近义词：musky, fragrant, aromatic, perfumed, redolent, pungent, fetid, rank|反义词：fragrant (in the sense of pleasant smell), sweet-smelling

词源:  From Middle English *musk, from Old French musc, from Arabic مسك (musk).  The word ultimately derives from Sanskrit मुष्क (muṣka), meaning "testicle," referring to the source of musk in the musk deer.
