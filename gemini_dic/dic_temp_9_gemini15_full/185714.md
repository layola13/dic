reverter:/rɪˈvɜːrtər/ | n. | 回归者；恢复原状者；反转器 | A person or thing that reverts or causes reversion.  Someone or something that returns to a previous state or condition.  A device that reverses something, such as a direction or a process. | Example: The old building was slated for demolition, but a reverter stepped in to save it.  这个旧建筑原计划要拆除，但一位回归者介入并将其保存下来。|近义词：reverter, restorer,  returner |反义词：progressor, innovator


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system reverts to its default settings. = 系统恢复到默认设置。
The land reverts to the original owner. = 土地归还给原来的所有者。


一般过去时:
The river reverted to its original course. = 河流恢复了原来的河道。
The company reverted to its old methods. = 公司恢复了旧方法。


现在完成时:
The property has reverted to the crown. = 该房产已归还王室。
He has reverted to his old habits. = 他又恢复了旧习惯。


过去完成时:
The country had reverted to a dictatorship. = 该国已恢复为独裁统治。
The situation had reverted to its previous state. = 情况已恢复到以前的状态。


将来时:
The software will revert to the previous version. = 软件将恢复到以前的版本。
The land will revert to the state after his death. = 他死后土地将归还国家。


虚拟语气:
If he were to win, the throne would revert to his family. = 如果他赢了，王位将回到他的家族。
I wish the situation would revert to normal. = 我希望情况能恢复正常。


被动语态:
The decision was reverted by the higher authority. = 该决定被上级主管撤销。
The changes were reverted after numerous complaints. = 在收到大量投诉后，更改被撤销。


疑问句:
Will the system automatically revert to the backup? = 系统会自动恢复到备份吗？
Does the contract contain a reversion clause? = 合同中是否包含返还条款？


否定句:
The plan did not revert to the original design. = 该计划没有恢复到原始设计。
The government will not revert to its previous policies. = 政府不会恢复以前的政策。
