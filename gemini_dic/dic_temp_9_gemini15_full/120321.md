minier:/mɪˈniər/| n.|矿工，采矿者|A miner is a person who works in a mine, extracting minerals or other valuable substances from the earth.  Miners often work in challenging and dangerous conditions.|例句：The miners worked tirelessly to extract the coal. 矿工们不知疲倦地开采煤炭。

例句：Many miners lost their lives in the mine collapse. 许多矿工在矿难中丧生。


近义词：矿工，采矿工|反义词：无|词性：名词

词源:源于古法语 *miner,  最终源于拉丁语 *mināre “to dig, to undermine”.
