fattened:/ˈfætənd/| v. |使肥胖；使肥沃；使丰富|To make or become fat; to enrich or improve the quality of (something), often by adding something.|The farmer fattened the pigs for market.  农民把猪养肥了准备上市。|The rich soil was fattened with fertilizer. 肥沃的土壤施了肥。|近义词：plump up, enrich, improve|反义词：emaciate, impoverish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fattens his cattle before selling them. 他在卖牛之前把牛养肥。
The farmer fattens the land with manure. 农民用肥料使土地肥沃。
