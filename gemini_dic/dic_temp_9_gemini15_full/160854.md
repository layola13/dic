post:/poʊst/ /pəʊst/| n., v.| 邮件，职位；邮寄，张贴|

英文解析:As a noun, "post" can refer to a position or job, a piece of writing or message, or a physical post (like a fence post). As a verb, it means to send something by mail, to put up a notice, or to publish something online.


例句:
He holds a high post in the government. 他在政府里担任要职。

She posted a picture of her vacation on Instagram. 她把度假照片发到了Instagram上。


近义词:
(n.) position, job, office, publication, mail, message
(v.) mail, send, publish, affix, display


反义词:
(n.)  None directly opposite, depends on context (e.g., for "position," "unemployment")
(v.)  remove, take down


词性:
Noun, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He posts letters every week. 他每周都寄信。
She posts updates on her blog regularly. 她定期在博客上更新信息。


一般过去时:
I posted the package yesterday. 我昨天寄了包裹。
They posted the announcement on the bulletin board. 他们把公告贴在了公告栏上。


现在完成时:
She has posted several articles this month. 这个月她已经发表了几篇文章。
He has posted the photos online. 他已经把照片上传到网上了。


过去完成时:
They had already posted the letters before the office closed. 办公室关门之前他们已经把信寄出去了。
She had posted the notice before the meeting started. 会议开始之前她已经张贴了通知。


将来时:
I will post the parcel tomorrow. 我明天会寄包裹。
He will post the results online next week. 他下周会把结果发布到网上。


虚拟语气:
If I had posted the letter earlier, it would have arrived on time. 如果我早点寄信，它就能准时到达了。
I wish I had posted the package before the deadline. 我真希望我在截止日期前寄出了包裹。


被动语态:
The letters were posted yesterday. 信件昨天寄出了。
The announcement was posted on the website. 公告发布在网站上了。


疑问句:
Did you post the letter? 你寄信了吗？
When will the results be posted? 结果什么时候发布？


否定句:
I didn't post the package. 我没有寄包裹。
They haven't posted the photos yet. 他们还没有上传照片。
