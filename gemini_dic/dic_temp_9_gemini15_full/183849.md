resegregating:/ˌriːˈseɡrɪɡeɪtɪŋ/| v.|重新隔离，种族隔离再现 |The act or process of separating people into different groups, especially based on race, after a period of integration.  This often implies a return to previous discriminatory practices. |例句：The new housing policy is feared to be resegregating the community. 新的住房政策令人担心会使社区重新隔离。

例句：The school district is accused of resegregating its schools through subtle discriminatory practices. 学区被指控通过微妙的歧视性做法使学校重新隔离。


近义词：re-separating, reinstating segregation, re-establishing segregation|反义词：integrating, desegregating
