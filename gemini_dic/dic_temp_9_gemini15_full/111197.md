lithologies:/lɪˈθɒlədʒiz/| n.|岩性学；岩石类型|The study of the physical characteristics of rocks.  It involves describing and classifying rocks based on their mineral composition, texture, and structure.|例句：The lithologies of the area are predominantly sedimentary.  该地区的岩性主要为沉积岩。|例句：Understanding the lithologies is crucial for geological mapping. 了解岩性对于地质测绘至关重要。|近义词：petrology, rock types|反义词：无|名词
