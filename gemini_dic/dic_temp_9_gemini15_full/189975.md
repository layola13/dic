sanctuaries:/ˈsæŋktʃuˌɛriːz/| n. |圣地；庇护所；保护区 |A sanctuary is a place of refuge or safety, often a sacred or protected area.  It can refer to a place of worship, a refuge for animals, or a protected area for the preservation of nature. |Example:  The temple was a sanctuary for the villagers during the war.  庙宇在战争期间是村民们的避难所。

The national park provides a sanctuary for endangered species. 国家公园为濒危物种提供庇护所。|近义词：refuge, refuge, asylum, reserve |反义词：danger, threat, peril
