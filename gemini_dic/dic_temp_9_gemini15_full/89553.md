honorifical:/ˌɒnəˈrɪfɪkəl/ | adj. | 荣誉的；尊称的；敬辞的 | Relating to or denoting words used as titles of respect (e.g., Mr., Mrs., Dr.).  The word itself often implies something more than mere politeness, suggesting a deeper respect or deference. | Example:  The use of "esquire" is an honorifical title.  使用“先生”是一个敬称头衔。


Example:  Many honorifical titles are used in academic settings. 许多敬称头衔用于学术环境。


近义词: complimentary, respectful, deferential


反义词: disrespectful, insulting, derogatory


词性: adjective
