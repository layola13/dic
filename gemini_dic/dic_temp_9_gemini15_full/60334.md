egoity: /iˈɡoʊɪti/ | n. | 自我中心；自负；自我膨胀 |  The excessive sense of self-importance; an inflated sense of one's own worth or abilities.  It often manifests as a disregard for others' feelings and perspectives.  | 例句：His egoity prevented him from admitting his mistake.  他的自负使他无法承认自己的错误。
His egoity was evident in his constant boasting. 他的自我膨胀表现在他不断的吹嘘中。| 近义词：narcissism, self-importance, conceit, vanity | 反义词：humility, modesty, selflessness | 词性: 名词
