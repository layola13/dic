predetaching:/ˌpriːdɪˈtætʃɪŋ/| v.|预先分离；预先拆卸 |  The act of separating or detaching something beforehand.  It implies preparation or anticipation of a later detachment. | 例句：The engineers were predetaching the auxiliary power unit before the main engine shutdown. = 工程师们在主发动机关闭之前预先拆卸了辅助动力装置。


The technicians are predetaching the components for easier maintenance later. = 技术人员正在预先拆卸组件，以便以后更容易维护。


近义词：pre-separating, disconnecting in advance


反义词：attaching, connecting, joining

词性:动词
