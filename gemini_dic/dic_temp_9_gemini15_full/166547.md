presbytism:/ˈprɛzbɪtɪzəm/| n. |老年性远视|A condition of the eye that results in the loss of near vision.  It's a natural part of the aging process and is caused by the hardening and thickening of the lens of the eye, making it less flexible and unable to focus on nearby objects.  It usually begins to appear around age 40. |He suffers from presbytism and needs reading glasses. 他患有老年性远视，需要戴老花镜。|近视(myopia),老花眼(hyperopia)|无|名词
