runology:/ruːˈnɒlədʒi/ | n. |符文学，古北欧文字学 | The study of runes, the ancient Germanic alphabet.  Runology involves deciphering inscriptions, analyzing their linguistic and cultural context, and understanding their use in various historical periods. | Example:  Her expertise in runology allowed her to translate the ancient inscription on the Viking artifact. = 她对符文学的专业知识使她能够翻译维京文物上的古代铭文。|近义词：rune studies, epigraphy (broader)|反义词：无
