platform:/ˈplætˌfɔrm/| n.|平台；讲台；站台；政纲|A raised level surface, often used for a specific purpose.  It can refer to a physical structure, a conceptual framework, or a political agenda.|例句：The train pulled into the platform. 火车驶进了站台。

例句：The speaker stood on the platform and addressed the audience. 演讲者站在讲台上并向观众讲话。


近义词：stage, base, dais, stand, program, scheme|反义词：N/A|词性:名词


词源:  The word "platform" ultimately derives from the Old French word "plateforme," which itself is a combination of "plate" (flat) and "forme" (form, shape).  This reflects its original meaning as a flat, raised surface.
