remonstratively: /rɪˈmɒnstreɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 规劝地；抗议地；表示反对地 |  In a way that shows strong disapproval or protest.  It implies a forceful expression of objection or dissent. |例句：He remonstratively shook his head. 他抗议地摇了摇头。

例句：She remonstratively pointed out the flaws in his argument. 她抗议性地指出了他论证中的缺陷。


近义词：protestingly, objectingly, disputatiously


反义词：submissively, acquiescently, approvingly


词性：副词
