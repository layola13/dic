gynaecologists:/ˌɡaɪnɪˈkɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. |妇科医生 |A gynaecologist is a doctor specializing in the female reproductive system. They diagnose and treat conditions related to the vagina, uterus, ovaries, and breasts.  |Example:  I have an appointment with my gynaecologist next week.  下周我约了妇科医生。|近义词：obstetrician (if also dealing with pregnancy and childbirth) |反义词：无
