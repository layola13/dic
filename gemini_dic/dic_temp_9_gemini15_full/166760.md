preslavery:/ˈpriːˌsleɪvəri/| adj.|奴隶制以前的；支持奴隶制的|Relating to the period or system before the abolition of slavery.  The term often implies support for or defense of slavery.|例句：Preslavery attitudes persisted in the South long after the Civil War. 南方在内战很久以后仍然存在着支持奴隶制的态度。

例句：The preslavery South relied heavily on enslaved labor for its agricultural economy. 奴隶制以前的南方严重依赖奴隶劳动来维持其农业经济。


近义词：antebellum (referring to the period before the American Civil War), proslavery


反义词：post-slavery, abolitionist


词性:形容词
