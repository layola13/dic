interruptible: /ˌɪntəˈrʌptəbəl/ | adj. | 可打断的，可中断的 | Capable of being interrupted;  susceptible to interruption.  Something that is interruptible can be stopped or paused temporarily. |

例句：
The meeting was interruptible, as participants were free to leave and rejoin.  会议是可以被打断的，因为与会者可以自由离开和重新加入。

The program is interruptible; you can pause it at any time. 这个程序是可以中断的；你可以随时暂停它。


近义词：interferable,  susceptible to interruption


反义词：uninterruptible, continuous, uninterrupted


词性：形容词
