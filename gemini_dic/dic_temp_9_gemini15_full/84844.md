hang-up:/ˈhæŋˌʌp/| n., v.|挂断；难题；障碍；习惯性行为|

英文解析:
As a noun, "hang-up" refers to a persistent problem or difficulty, or a strong emotional fixation or obsession. As a verb, it means to end a telephone conversation by hanging up the receiver or to suddenly stop speaking or interacting with someone.


例句:
(Noun):
He has a real hang-up about cleanliness. 他对清洁有着挥之不去的执念。

She has a hang-up about her weight. 她对自己的体重很在意。


(Verb):
I hung up the phone after a heated argument.  我和对方激烈争吵后挂断了电话。

He hung up on me before I could explain.  在我还没来得及解释之前，他就挂断了我的电话。


近义词:
(Noun): problem, difficulty, obsession, fixation, complex
(Verb): disconnect, terminate, end


反义词:
(Verb): connect, continue, initiate


词性:
Noun, Verb


USAGE->
