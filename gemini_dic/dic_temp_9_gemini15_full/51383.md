determinate:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnət/| adj.|确定的；已决定的；有限的；有限定的 |Having a fixed, predetermined limit or boundary.  It suggests a clearly defined outcome or result that is not subject to change. |例句：The deadline for the project is determinate. 项目的截止日期是确定的。

The experiment yielded determinate results. 实验产生了确定的结果。


近义词：definite, fixed, established, resolved, certain |反义词：indeterminate, uncertain, indefinite, unresolved, ambiguous


词源：源自拉丁语“determinare”，意为“限定，决定”。  "de-" 表示“彻底”，"terminare"  源于"terminus"，意为“界限，终点”。
