palmitin:/ˈpæl.mə.tɪn/| n. |棕榈精, 棕榈油精|A type of fat (triglyceride) found in palm oil, composed of three palmitic acid molecules esterified to a glycerol molecule.  It is a saturated fat and solid at room temperature.|Palm oil is rich in palmitin, making it a common ingredient in many processed foods.|近义词：palm oil triglyceride|反义词：None specifically, but unsaturated fats are contrasting.
