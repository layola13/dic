extortionary: /ɪkˈstɔːʃənəri/ | adj. |敲诈的；勒索的；过高的；不合理的| Excessively high; exorbitant;  characterized by extortion.  |例句：The extortionary interest rates on the loan left him deeply in debt.  这笔贷款的敲诈性利率使他负债累累。

例句：They demanded an extortionary sum of money for the return of the stolen goods. 他们要求支付一笔巨额赎金才能取回被盗的货物。


近义词：exorbitant, exorbitant, usurious, oppressive, unconscionable

反义词：reasonable, fair, moderate, affordable


词源:  Derived from "extortion," which comes from the Old French word "extorcion" and ultimately from the Latin word "extorsionem," meaning "a wresting away" or "a twisting out."  The "-ary" suffix indicates an adjective.
