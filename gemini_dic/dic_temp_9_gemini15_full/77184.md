gasometry:/ɡæsˈɒmɪtri/ | n. | 气体分析法 | The measurement and analysis of gases, often in blood or other biological fluids.  It involves determining the partial pressures and concentrations of various gases. | Example:  Blood gasometry is crucial in assessing respiratory function.  血液气体分析法对于评估呼吸功能至关重要。 |近义词: blood gas analysis, gas analysis | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑与其相对的整体性分析方法) | 名词
