caddying:/ˈkædiɪŋ/| v.|当球童|The act of working as a caddie, carrying a golfer's clubs.  This involves carrying clubs, providing advice, and generally assisting the golfer.|例句：He earned extra money by caddying at the local golf course. 他通过在当地高尔夫球场当球童赚取额外收入。 He spent his summers caddying for wealthy golfers. 他夏天都在为富有的高尔夫球手当球童。|近义词：carrying clubs, assisting, helping|反义词: playing golf


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He caddies at the country club. = 他在乡村俱乐部当球童。
She caddies for professional golfers. = 她为职业高尔夫球手当球童。


一般过去时:
I caddied for my uncle last weekend. = 上周末我为我叔叔当了球童。
They caddied at the tournament. = 他们在那次锦标赛上当了球童。


现在完成时:
He has caddied for many famous golfers. = 他为许多著名的高尔夫球手当过球童。
She has caddied at this course for five years. = 她在这个球场当了五年球童。


过去完成时:
He had caddied for years before he got his own set of clubs. = 在他拥有自己的球杆之前，他已经当了几年的球童。
She had caddied for several professional golfers before retiring. = 在退休之前，她为几位职业高尔夫球手当过球童。


将来时:
I will caddie for my friend tomorrow. = 明天我将为我的朋友当球童。
He will caddie at the championship next month. = 下个月他将在锦标赛上当球童。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would caddie for you. = 如果我有时间，我会为你当球童。
I wish I could caddie in a major tournament. = 我希望我能在一场大型锦标赛中当球童。


被动语态:
The clubs were caddied by a young boy. = 球杆由一个年轻男孩拿着。 (Note:  Passive voice is less common with this verb)
The tournament is well-supported by many caddies. = 锦标赛得到了许多球童的大力支持。(Note:  Passive voice is used here to focus on the support)


疑问句:
Do you caddie at this course? = 你在这个球场当球童吗？
Will he be caddying this weekend? = 他这个周末会当球童吗？


否定句:
I don't caddie anymore. = 我不再当球童了。
She didn't caddie at the tournament. = 她没有在那次锦标赛上当球童。
