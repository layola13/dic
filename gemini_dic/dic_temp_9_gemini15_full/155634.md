phishers:/ˈfɪʃərz/| n.|网络钓鱼者|People who attempt to trick individuals into revealing sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details by disguising themselves as a trustworthy entity in electronic communication.|Example: The phishers created a fake website that looked exactly like the real bank's site to steal customers' information.  网络钓鱼者创建了一个与真实银行网站一模一样的假网站来窃取客户信息。|近义词：fraudsters, scammers, con artists|反义词：None.
