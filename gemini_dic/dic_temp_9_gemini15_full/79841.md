glyphosate:/ˈɡlaɪfoʊseɪt/| n. |草甘膦|A broad-spectrum systemic herbicide used to kill weeds, including those that are resistant to other herbicides.|例句：Glyphosate is the active ingredient in many weed killers.  草甘膦是许多除草剂中的活性成分。|例句：The overuse of glyphosate has led to the development of herbicide-resistant weeds. 过度使用草甘膦导致了抗除草剂杂草的产生。|近义词：herbicide, weed killer|反义词：fertilizer
