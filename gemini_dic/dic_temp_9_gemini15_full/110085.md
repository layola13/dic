lichenology:/ˌlaɪkənˈɒlədʒi/| n. |地衣学|The scientific study of lichens.  Lichenology involves the taxonomy, physiology, ecology, and other aspects of lichens.|例句：My research focuses on lichenology, specifically the symbiotic relationship between fungi and algae in lichen formation.  我的研究重点是地衣学，特别是地衣形成过程中真菌和藻类之间的共生关系。|近义词：lichen studies, mycology (partially)|反义词：无
