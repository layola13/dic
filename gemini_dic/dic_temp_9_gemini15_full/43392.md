crankest:/ˈkræŋkɪst/| adj.|最古怪的；最脾气暴躁的|Most eccentric or irritable;  the superlative form of "cranky". It emphasizes the extreme degree of crankiness.  |例句：He was the crankest old man in the village, always complaining about everything. 他是村里最古怪的老头，总是抱怨一切。He's been crankest ever since his dog died.自从他的狗死了以后，他就变得脾气暴躁极了。|近义词：grumpy, crotchety, cantankerous, irascible, testy|反义词：pleasant, amiable, agreeable, good-natured
