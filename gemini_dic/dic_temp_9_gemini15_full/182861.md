repent:/rɪˈpent/| v.|悔过；忏悔|To feel sorry for something you have done or not done and wish you could do it differently.|He repented his sins and asked for forgiveness. 他悔过自己的罪行并请求宽恕。|近义词：regret, atone, rue|反义词：celebrate, rejoice


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I repent my actions daily. = 我每天都为我的行为悔过。
They repent their mistakes sincerely. = 他们真诚地悔过自己的错误。


一般过去时:
He repented his youthful indiscretions. = 他为年轻时的轻率行为悔过。
She repented her hasty decision. = 她为她草率的决定悔过。


现在完成时:
I have repented my sins and turned to God. = 我已经悔过我的罪孽并转向上帝。
They have repented their harsh words. = 他们已经为他们刻薄的话悔过。


过去完成时:
He had repented his actions before he died. = 他死前已经为自己的行为悔过。
She had repented her lies before confessing. = 在坦白之前，她已经为她的谎言悔过。


将来时:
I will repent my errors in the future. = 我将来会为我的错误悔过。
They will repent their wickedness eventually. = 他们最终会为他们的邪恶悔过。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have repented sooner. = 如果我知道的话，我会早点悔过的。
I wish I had repented earlier. = 我希望我早点悔过。


被动语态:
His sins were repented for by many. = 许多人为他的罪过悔过。
The wrongs done were repented for. = 所犯的错误得到了悔过。


疑问句:
Will you repent your actions? = 你会为你的行为悔过吗？
Does he truly repent his sins? = 他真的为他的罪过悔过吗？


否定句:
He did not repent his misdeeds. = 他没有为他的恶行悔过。
They refused to repent their actions. = 他们拒绝为他们的行为悔过。


PHRASE->
repent of one's sins = 悔过自己的罪过
repent at leisure = 从容悔过
repent in dust and ashes = 痛悔不已
repent one's folly = 后悔自己的愚蠢
repent one's error = 后悔自己的错误
