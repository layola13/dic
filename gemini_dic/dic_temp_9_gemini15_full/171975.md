purchases:/ˈpɜːrtʃəsɪz/| n. |购买；采购；购买的物品 |The act of buying something; things that have been bought.  |例句：Her recent purchases include a new car and a holiday to Hawaii. 她最近的购买包括一辆新车和去夏威夷的假期。

例句：The company's annual purchases of raw materials have increased significantly.  公司的年度原材料采购量显著增加。


近义词：acquisitions, buys, procurements |反义词：sales, disposals


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He purchases groceries every week. = 他每周都购买杂货。
She purchases her clothes online. = 她在网上购买衣服。

一般过去时:
I purchased a new laptop last month. = 我上个月买了一台新笔记本电脑。
They purchased a house in the suburbs. = 他们在郊区买了一栋房子。

现在完成时:
We have purchased all the necessary supplies. = 我们已经购买了所有必要的用品。
She has purchased several books this year. = 她今年已经买了好几本书。

过去完成时:
He had purchased the tickets before the prices went up. = 价格上涨之前，他已经买好了票。
They had purchased the land long before they built the house. = 他们在建造房子很久以前就买下了那块地。

将来时:
I will purchase a new phone next week. = 我下周会买一部新手机。
They will purchase the property after the inspection. = 他们会在检查之后购买房产。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would purchase a yacht. = 如果我有更多钱，我会买一艘游艇。
I wish I could purchase that painting. = 我希望我能买到那幅画。

被动语态:
The tickets were purchased online. = 票是在网上购买的。
The car was purchased at a discount. = 这辆车是打折买的。

疑问句:
What did you purchase at the store? = 你在商店买了什么？
Where did they purchase the furniture? = 他们在哪里买的家具？

否定句:
I did not purchase anything today. = 我今天什么都没买。
They did not purchase the house because of the high price. = 因为价格太高，他们没有买下房子。


PHRASE->
make purchases = 购物
bulk purchases = 批量购买
online purchases = 网上购物
repeat purchases = 重复购买
planned purchases = 计划性购买
