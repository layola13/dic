evaluator: /ɪˈvæljueɪtər/ | n. | 评估者；评价者；鉴定者 | A person who evaluates something.  Someone who makes judgments of value, quality, or performance. |  The evaluator provided a detailed report on the project's success. 评估者提供了一份关于项目成功的详细报告。 The hiring evaluator carefully considered each applicant's qualifications. 招聘评估者仔细考虑了每位申请人的资格。 | assessor, judge, critic |  (no direct antonym, depends on context,  e.g.,  the opposite of a positive evaluator might be a harsh critic) | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The evaluator assesses the student's work. = 评估者评估学生的作业。
He evaluates the quality of the products. = 他评估产品的质量。


一般过去时:
The evaluator assessed the damage after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，评估者评估了损失。
She evaluated the performance of the employees. = 她评估了员工的表现。


现在完成时:
The evaluator has assessed all the submissions. = 评估者已经评估了所有提交的文件。
She has evaluated several projects this year. = 她今年已经评估了好几个项目。


过去完成时:
The evaluator had already assessed the situation before making a decision. = 评估者在做出决定之前已经评估了情况。
They had evaluated the risks before starting the project. = 他们在启动项目之前已经评估了风险。


将来时:
The evaluator will assess the impact of the new policy. = 评估者将评估新政策的影响。
He will evaluate the results of the experiment next week. = 他下周将评估实验的结果。


虚拟语气:
If the evaluator were here, he would assess the situation immediately. = 如果评估者在这里，他会立即评估情况。
I wish the evaluator had evaluated the proposal earlier. = 我希望评估者早点评估这个提案。


被动语态:
The student's work was assessed by the evaluator. = 学生的作业由评估者评估。
The project's success will be evaluated by an independent panel. = 项目的成功将由一个独立小组进行评估。


疑问句:
Who will be the evaluator for this competition? = 谁将是这次比赛的评估者？
Has the evaluator completed the assessment? = 评估者完成了评估吗？


否定句:
The evaluator did not assess the report thoroughly. = 评估者没有彻底评估报告。
He will not evaluate the project until next week. = 他直到下周才会评估这个项目。
