medicines:/ˈmɛdəsɪnz/| n.|药物；药品|Substances used to treat or prevent diseases.  Medicines can be in various forms, including pills, capsules, liquids, and injections.|例句：The doctor prescribed some medicines for my cough. 医生给我开了些治咳嗽的药。|例句：Many medicines have side effects. 许多药物都有副作用。|近义词：drugs, pharmaceuticals, remedies|反义词：None (antonyms are not applicable in this context;  it would be more accurate to give contrasting concepts like "placebos" or "home remedies")|名词
