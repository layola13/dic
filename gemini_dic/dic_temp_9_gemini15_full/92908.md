ideate:/aɪˈdiːeɪt/| vt.|构思；想出；设想|To form or have an idea; conceive.  The process of generating, developing, and communicating new ideas.|例句：The team ideated several innovative solutions to the problem. = 团队构思了几个解决问题的创新方案。

例句：We need to ideate a new marketing strategy. = 我们需要构思一个新的营销策略。


近义词：conceive, imagine, invent, brainstorm, conceptualize|反义词：ignore, neglect, overlook|动词
