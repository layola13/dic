presentation:/ˌprezenˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 演示；陈述；介绍；赠送 | The act of presenting something to someone; a formal talk or demonstration.  |例句：He gave a presentation on the new software. 他做了一个关于新软件的演示。|例句：The company's presentation to investors was very successful.  公司给投资者的演示非常成功。|近义词：demonstration, showing, exposition, offering |反义词：concealment, hiding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company gives a presentation every quarter. = 公司每个季度都会做一个演示。
She presents her research findings at conferences. = 她会在会议上展示她的研究成果。


一般过去时:
He gave a compelling presentation yesterday. = 他昨天做了一个令人信服的演示。
They presented their project to the board. = 他们向董事会展示了他们的项目。


现在完成时:
She has presented at numerous international conferences. = 她已经在许多国际会议上做过演示。
They have presented their findings in a clear and concise manner. = 他们已经以清晰简洁的方式呈现了他们的发现。


过去完成时:
He had already presented his ideas before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他已经陈述了他的想法。
They had presented the data several times before the final decision. = 在最终决定之前，他们已经多次展示了数据。


将来时:
I will present my work at the upcoming symposium. = 我将在即将举行的研讨会上展示我的作品。
She will be presenting her research next week. = 她下周将展示她的研究成果。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better presentation, I would have gotten the job. = 如果我有一个更好的演示，我就会得到这份工作。
It would have been better if he had presented the data more clearly. = 如果他更清晰地展示数据，那就更好了。


被动语态:
The results were presented in a graph. = 结果以图表的形式呈现。
The new product will be presented at the trade show. = 新产品将在贸易展览会上展出。


疑问句:
When will the presentation be? = 演示什么时候进行？
Who will be presenting the findings? = 谁将展示研究结果？


否定句:
He didn't present his findings effectively. = 他没有有效地展示他的研究结果。
The company did not give a presentation at the conference. = 公司在会议上没有做演示。


PHRASE->
