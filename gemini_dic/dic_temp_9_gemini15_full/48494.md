deepfake: /ˈdiːpfeɪk/ | n. |深度伪造，深度造假 |A deepfake is a form of synthetic media in which a person in an existing image or video is replaced with someone else's likeness.  This is achieved using artificial intelligence, specifically deep learning techniques.  Deepfakes can be used for entertainment, but also for malicious purposes like spreading misinformation and damaging reputations. | Example: The video showed a deepfake of the politician giving a speech he never actually made.  这段视频展示了一个政治家发表演讲的深度伪造视频，而他实际上从未发表过这样的演讲。| 近义词: fabricated video, synthetic media, AI-generated video | 反义词: genuine video, authentic footage |  名词
