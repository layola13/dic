preexhaustion: /ˌpriːɪɡzˈɔːʃən/ | n. | 预先筋疲力尽；预先耗竭 |  The state of being exhausted before undertaking a task or activity, often due to prior exertion or stress.  It can refer to both physical and mental fatigue. |  例句：The marathon runner experienced preexhaustion due to a rigorous training schedule.  马拉松运动员由于严格的训练计划而经历了预先筋疲力尽的状态。

例句：  The long hours of studying led to a state of preexhaustion before the final exam.  长时间的学习导致期末考试前筋疲力尽。


近义词：pre-fatigue, exhaustion, depletion, tiredness, weariness

反义词：invigoration, rejuvenation, revitalization

词性：名词
