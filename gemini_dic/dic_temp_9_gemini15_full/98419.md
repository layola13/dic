insourcing: /ˈɪnsɔːrsɪŋ/ | n. |内部采购；内部资源利用 | The practice of using internal resources or employees to perform tasks instead of outsourcing them to external vendors or contractors.  |例句：The company decided on insourcing its IT support to improve efficiency and control. 公司决定将IT支持内部化，以提高效率和控制力。

例句：To reduce costs, the manufacturing company implemented an insourcing strategy for its logistics. 为降低成本，这家制造公司实施了物流内部采购战略。


近义词：internalization, internal sourcing | 反义词：outsourcing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company insources its marketing efforts. = 公司将营销工作内部化。
We insource most of our software development. = 我们将大部分软件开发工作内部化。


一般过去时:
They insourced the project last year. = 他们去年将该项目内部化了。
The firm insourced its customer service department. = 该公司将客户服务部门内部化了。


现在完成时:
They have insourced several key functions. = 他们已经将几个关键职能内部化了。
The organization has insourced its data analysis. = 该组织已经将数据分析工作内部化了。


过去完成时:
They had insourced the manufacturing before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们已经将制造业务内部化了。
The company had insourced its training programs for years. = 这家公司多年来一直将培训项目内部化。


将来时:
We will insource the production process next quarter. = 我们将在下个季度将生产流程内部化。
The department will insource its graphic design team next year. = 该部门将在明年将平面设计团队内部化。


虚拟语气:
If we insourced the project, we could save money. = 如果我们将项目内部化，我们就能省钱。
It would be better if they insourced their IT infrastructure. = 如果他们将IT基础设施内部化会更好。


被动语态:
The project was insourced by the marketing team. = 该项目由营销团队内部化。
The software development was insourced to the internal team. = 软件开发工作被内部化到内部团队。


疑问句:
Will they insource the customer support? = 他们会将客户支持内部化吗？
Is the company considering insourcing its logistics? = 公司是否正在考虑将物流内部化？


否定句:
We did not insource the project; we outsourced it. = 我们没有将项目内部化；我们将其外包了。
The company didn’t insource the manufacturing process. = 公司没有将制造流程内部化。
