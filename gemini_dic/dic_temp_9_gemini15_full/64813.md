esotery:/ɪˈsɒtəri/ | n. |深奥的知识；秘传知识；神秘学 |  Knowledge that is restricted to a small group of people; the quality or state of being esoteric.  It often refers to specialized or obscure knowledge, practices, or beliefs that are understood only by a select few. | Example: The esoteric rituals of the ancient society were lost to time.  古代社团的深奥仪式随着时间的流逝而消失了。| 近义词：occultism, mysticism, arcana | 反义词：exoteric, commonplace, public
