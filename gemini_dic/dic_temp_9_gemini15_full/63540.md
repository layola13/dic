envelop:/ɪnˈvɛləp/ | vt. n. | 包裹；信封；覆盖 | to wrap something completely in something else; a covering or wrapper, especially for a letter. |  The fog enveloped the mountain.  雾笼罩着山峰。| He carefully opened the envelop. 他小心地打开信封。 | cover, wrap, enclose | uncover, reveal | 动词，名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He envelops the gift in wrapping paper. 他用包装纸包裹礼物。
The mist envelops the valley. 薄雾笼罩着山谷。


一般过去时:
She enveloped the child in a warm blanket. 她用一条温暖的毯子裹住孩子。
The darkness enveloped the city. 黑暗笼罩着城市。


现在完成时:
The snow has enveloped the entire town.  雪已经覆盖了整个城镇。
Fear has enveloped her since the accident. 自从事故发生以来，恐惧一直笼罩着她。


过去完成时:
The village had been enveloped in fog all day.  整个白天村庄都被雾笼罩着。
Before the storm hit, the sky had been enveloped in an eerie calm.  暴风雨来临之前，天空笼罩着一种令人毛骨悚然的平静。


将来时:
The army will envelop the enemy's position.  军队将包围敌人的阵地。
Darkness will envelop the land as night falls.  夜幕降临，黑暗将笼罩大地。


虚拟语气:
If I had a blanket, I would envelop myself in it. 如果我有一条毯子，我会用它把自己裹起来。
If only the love could envelop the world.  但愿爱能覆盖全世界。


被动语态:
The package was enveloped in brown paper. 包裹用牛皮纸包着。
The town was enveloped by a thick fog. 小镇被浓雾笼罩着。


疑问句:
Is the mountain enveloped in snow? 山上覆盖着雪吗？
Was the letter enveloped properly? 信封封好了吗？


否定句:
The package was not enveloped properly.  包裹没有包好。
The plan did not envelop all the details. 计划没有涵盖所有细节。



PHRASE->

envelop in: 包裹在……里
envelop oneself in:  沉浸在……中
be enveloped in: 被……包围/笼罩
enveloping atmosphere: 笼罩的气氛
enveloping sound: 环绕声
