preexpenditures: /ˌpriːɪkˈspɛndɪtʃərz/ | n. | 预付支出，前期支出 |  Expenses incurred before a project or undertaking officially begins.  These costs are often associated with planning, preparation, and initial setup. |


例句：The preexpenditures for the new factory included site preparation and architectural design.  = 新工厂的预付支出包括场地准备和建筑设计。


The company's preexpenditures on research and development were significant. = 公司在研发上的预付支出非常可观。


近义词：advance payments, upfront costs, preliminary expenses


反义词：post-expenditures, subsequent costs


词性: noun (名词)
