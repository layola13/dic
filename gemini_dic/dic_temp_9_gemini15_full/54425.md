disinfecting:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktɪŋ/| v. |消毒；杀菌|The act of cleaning something to remove germs and other microorganisms.  It involves the use of chemical agents or physical methods to kill or inactivate these harmful organisms. |例句：The nurse is disinfecting the surgical instruments. 护士正在消毒手术器械。

例句：We need to disinfect the kitchen counter after handling raw meat. 我们需要在处理生肉后对厨房台面进行消毒。


近义词：sterilizing, sanitizing, purifying

反义词：contaminating


词性：动名词 (gerund)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Disinfecting the surfaces regularly prevents the spread of germs. 定期消毒表面可以防止细菌传播。
He is disinfecting the wound with antiseptic. 他正在用消毒剂消毒伤口。


一般过去时:
She disinfected the bathroom thoroughly after the illness.  她病后彻底消毒了浴室。
They disinfected all the equipment before the experiment. 他们在实验前对所有设备进行了消毒。


现在完成时:
We have disinfected the entire house after the flood. 洪水过后，我们已经对整栋房子进行了消毒。
The hospital staff have disinfected the operating room. 医院工作人员已经对手术室进行了消毒。


过去完成时:
They had disinfected the area before the paramedics arrived.  医护人员到达之前，他们已经对该区域进行了消毒。
The workers had disinfected the tools before they started the job. 工人们在开始工作之前已经对工具进行了消毒。


将来时:
We will disinfect the pool before the swimming season begins. 游泳季开始前，我们会对游泳池进行消毒。
The cleaning crew will disinfect the entire building tonight. 清洁人员今晚将对整栋大楼进行消毒。


虚拟语气:
If we disinfected the surfaces more often, we would have fewer infections. 如果我们更频繁地消毒表面，感染就会更少。
I wish I had disinfected the cutting board before preparing the food. 我真希望在准备食物之前已经消毒了砧板。


被动语态:
The equipment is disinfected daily. 设备每天都进行消毒。
The operating room was disinfected before the surgery. 手术前对手术室进行了消毒。


疑问句:
Is the equipment being disinfected properly? 设备正在被正确消毒吗？
Have the surfaces been disinfected? 表面消毒了吗？


否定句:
They did not disinfect the tools before starting the work. 他们开始工作前没有对工具进行消毒。
The hospital did not disinfect the rooms frequently enough. 医院没有足够频繁地消毒房间。
