colorising: /ˈkʌləraɪzɪŋ/ | v. |着色，上色，给…着色 |The act of adding color to something that was previously black and white or grayscale.  It often refers to the process of digitally adding color to old photographs or films. |例句：He's colorising old family photos. 他正在给旧的家庭照片着色。

例句：The film studio is colorising its classic black and white movies. 电影制片厂正在为其经典的黑白电影着色。

近义词：coloring, tinting

反义词：desaturating, monochroming
