clear-out: /klɪər aʊt/ | vt. |清理，清除，赶走 | To remove or get rid of a large number of things; to force someone to leave a place. |例句：They're having a clear-out of old furniture. 他们正在清理旧家具。  |例句：The police cleared out the protesters. 警察驱散了抗议者。 |近义词：clean up, tidy up, remove, evict |反义词：accumulate, collect, hoard |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We clear out the garage every spring. = 我们每年春天都会清理车库。
She clears out her closet once a year. = 她每年清理一次衣橱。


一般过去时:
They cleared out the old files last week. = 他们上周清理了旧文件。
The landlord cleared out the tenants for non-payment. = 房东因未付款而将租户赶了出去。


现在完成时:
We have cleared out all the junk from the attic. = 我们已经清理了阁楼上的所有垃圾。
The government has cleared out the corrupt officials. = 政府已经清除腐败官员。


过去完成时:
They had cleared out the evidence before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们已经销毁了证据。
She had cleared out her desk before leaving the office. = 她在离开办公室之前已经清理了她的办公桌。


将来时:
We will clear out the basement tomorrow. = 我们明天将清理地下室。
The company will clear out all the outdated equipment. = 公司将清理所有过时的设备。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would clear out the whole house. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会清理整个房子。
I wish I had cleared out my inbox sooner. = 我真希望我早点清理我的收件箱。


被动语态:
The old building is being cleared out. = 旧建筑正在被清理。
The protesters were cleared out by the police. = 抗议者被警察驱散了。


疑问句:
When are you going to clear out the garage? = 你什么时候打算清理车库？
Have they cleared out the rubbish yet? = 他们清理垃圾了吗？


否定句:
We didn't clear out the attic last year. = 我们去年没有清理阁楼。
They haven't cleared out the old furniture yet. = 他们还没有清理旧家具。
