conductors:/kənˈdʌktərz/| n.|指挥家；导体；售票员|A conductor is a person who leads an orchestra or choir, or a material that allows electricity or heat to pass through it easily.  It can also refer to a person who collects fares on a public transport vehicle.|例句：The orchestra's conductor is renowned for his passionate interpretations.  乐团的指挥以其充满激情的演绎而闻名。|例句：Copper is a good conductor of electricity. 铜是良好的导电体。|近义词：leader, director, transmitter|反义词：insulator|名词
