repayment:/riˈpeɪmənt/| n. |偿还，还款 |The act of paying back money that has been borrowed or owed.  It involves returning the principal amount plus any accrued interest or fees. |例句：The repayment of the loan is due next month.  下个月到期偿还贷款。|例句：She made her monthly repayment on time. 她按时进行了每月还款。|近义词：reimbursement, refund |反义词：loan, borrowing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company makes its repayments on time. = 公司按时还款。
He repays his debts regularly. = 他定期偿还债务。

一般过去时:
She repaid the loan in full. = 她全额偿还了贷款。
They made a repayment last week. = 他们上周进行了还款。

现在完成时:
We have completed the repayment of our mortgage. = 我们已经还清了抵押贷款。
He has made several repayments this year. = 他今年已经进行了几次还款。

过去完成时:
They had already made several repayments before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经进行了几次还款。
She had repaid all her debts by the end of the year. = 到年底她已经还清了所有债务。

将来时:
I will make my repayment next week. = 我下周将进行还款。
They will complete the repayment soon. = 他们很快就会完成还款。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would make the repayment earlier. = 如果我有更多的钱，我会提前还款。
I wish I could make the repayment immediately. = 我希望我能立即还款。

被动语态:
The loan was repaid in full. = 贷款已全额偿还。
The repayments are being processed. = 还款正在处理中。

疑问句:
When is the repayment due? = 还款到期日是什么时候？
How much is the monthly repayment? = 每月还款额是多少？

否定句:
He did not make the repayment on time. = 他没有按时还款。
They have not yet completed the repayment. = 他们尚未完成还款。
