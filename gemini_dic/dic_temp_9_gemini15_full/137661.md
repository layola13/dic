nonunion:/ˌnɑːnˈjuːnjən/| adj.|非工会的；非联盟的|Not belonging to or supported by a labor union.  This term typically refers to workers, workplaces, or businesses that are not affiliated with a trade union.|例句：The factory employed a largely nonunion workforce. 这家工厂雇佣的主要是非工会工人。|例句：He worked at a nonunion construction site. 他在一个非工会的建筑工地上工作。|近义词：unorganized, independent|反义词：unionized
