incuriously:/ɪnˈkjʊəriəsli/| adv.|不好奇地，漠不关心|Showing or feeling no curiosity.|The child incuriously watched the street performer.  孩子漠不关心地看着街头表演者。|curiously, inquisitively|indifferently, apathetically|副词


USAGE->

This word is an adverb, and as such doesn't have verb conjugations like "is," "was," "will be," etc.  The usage is always modifying a verb.


Example sentences demonstrating different aspects of usage:

She listened incuriously to the gossip. 她不感兴趣地听着八卦。

He incuriously scanned the newspaper headlines. 他漫不经心地浏览报纸标题。

He responded incuriously to her questions. 他对她的问题漠不关心。

They observed the accident incuriously. 他们冷漠地观察了事故。


PHRASE->

There aren't common phrases specifically using "incuriously"  as it's not a frequently used word.  Phrases would be built around it, modifying a verb:

incuriously glanced: 漫不经心地一瞥
incuriously watched: 漠不关心地看着
incuriously listened: 不感兴趣地听着
incuriously observed: 冷漠地观察
incuriously examined: 漫不经心地检查
