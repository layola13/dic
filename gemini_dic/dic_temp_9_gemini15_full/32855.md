cholesterol:/kəˈlɛstərɒl/ /kəˈlɛstrɒl/| n. |胆固醇|A waxy, fat-like substance that's found in all the cells of your body. Cholesterol is necessary for building and maintaining healthy cells, but high levels of cholesterol in the blood can increase your risk of heart disease.|例句：High cholesterol can lead to heart problems. 高胆固醇会导致心脏问题。  My doctor advised me to lower my cholesterol levels. 我的医生建议我降低胆固醇水平。|近义词：lipid, sterol|反义词：无
