kned:/ned/| v.|揉捏；搓；按摩|To work dough or clay into a mass with the hands.  |例句：She kned the dough until it was smooth. 她揉面团直到它变得光滑。|例句：The therapist kned the muscles in his back.理疗师按摩了他的背部肌肉。|近义词：knead, mold, work|反义词：None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She kneds the bread dough every morning. = 她每天早上都揉面包面团。
He kneds clay to create sculptures. = 他揉捏粘土来创作雕塑。

一般过去时:
She kned the dough until it was smooth. = 她揉面团直到它变得光滑。
He kned the clay for hours. = 他揉捏粘土几个小时。

现在完成时:
She has kned the dough for the bread. = 她已经揉好了面包的面团。
He has kned many sculptures from clay. = 他已经用粘土揉捏了许多雕塑。

过去完成时:
She had kned the dough before adding the yeast. = 她加酵母之前已经揉好了面团。
He had kned the clay for several hours before starting the sculpture. = 他在开始雕塑之前已经揉捏粘土几个小时了。

将来时:
She will knead the dough tomorrow. = 她明天将揉面团。
He will knead the clay for his next project. = 他将为他的下一个项目揉捏粘土。


虚拟语气:
If she kneaded the dough longer, it would be smoother. = 如果她揉面团的时间更长，它会更光滑。
If he had kneaded the clay more carefully, the sculpture would be better. = 如果他更仔细地揉捏粘土，雕塑会更好。
