re-resolve:/ˌriːrɪˈzɒlv/ | vt. |重新解决；再次决定 | To resolve something again; to reconsider a decision.  |例句：The committee will re-resolve the issue at their next meeting. 委员会将在下次会议上重新解决这个问题。
例句：After further consideration, they re-resolved to pursue a different strategy.经过进一步考虑，他们决定采取不同的策略。|近义词：reconsider, reassess, redetermine |反义词：abandon, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-resolve the conflict peacefully. = 他们和平地重新解决冲突。
The company re-resolves its marketing strategy annually. = 公司每年重新确定其营销策略。

一般过去时:
The judge re-resolved the case after new evidence emerged. = 在出现新的证据后，法官重新审理了此案。
We re-resolved to finish the project by the deadline. = 我们再次决定在截止日期前完成项目。

现在完成时:
They have re-resolved to support the charity. = 他们已经再次决定支持这家慈善机构。
The team has re-resolved the technical problem. = 团队已经重新解决了技术问题。

过去完成时:
He had re-resolved to quit smoking before the incident. = 在那件事发生之前，他已经再次决定戒烟了。
She had re-resolved to apply for a new job after the interview. = 面试后，她已经再次决定申请一份新工作。

将来时:
We will re-resolve the matter after gathering more information. = 我们将在收集更多信息后重新解决此事。
The government will re-resolve the budget next month. = 政府下个月将重新确定预算。

虚拟语气:
If we had re-resolved the dispute earlier, the situation wouldn’t be so tense. = 如果我们早点重新解决纠纷，情况就不会这么紧张了。
I wish we had re-resolved the problem before it escalated. = 我希望我们在问题升级之前就重新解决了它。


被动语态:
The problem was re-resolved by the expert team. = 这个问题由专家团队重新解决了。
The issue will be re-resolved at the next board meeting. = 这个问题将在下一次董事会会议上重新解决。

疑问句:
Will they re-resolve the contract terms? = 他们会重新商定合同条款吗？
Has the committee re-resolved the funding issue? = 委员会是否重新解决了资金问题？

否定句:
They did not re-resolve the conflict. = 他们没有重新解决冲突。
The company has not re-resolved the problem of customer complaints. = 公司尚未解决客户投诉的问题。
