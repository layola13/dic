drilled:/drɪld/| vt.|钻孔；反复训练；灌输；|to make a hole in something using a drill; to practice something repeatedly; to teach or impress something firmly in someone's mind|例句：The workers drilled holes in the wall. 工人们在墙上钻孔。They drilled the soldiers until they were perfect. 他们反复训练士兵直到他们达到完美状态。|近义词：bored, rehearsed, ingrained|反义词：filled, ignored


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drills holes in wood every day. 他每天都在木头上钻孔。
The coach drills the team hard before every game. 教练在每场比赛前都对球队进行严格训练。

一般过去时:
They drilled a well in the desert. 他们在沙漠里打了一口井。
The teacher drilled the grammar rules into her students. 老师向学生反复灌输语法规则。

现在完成时:
She has drilled several holes in the metal plate. 她已经在金属板上钻了几个孔。
The team has drilled extensively for the upcoming tournament.  这支队伍为即将到来的锦标赛进行了大量的训练。

过去完成时:
They had drilled the tunnel before the earthquake. 地震发生前他们已经挖好了隧道。
The students had drilled the vocabulary words repeatedly before the test. 学生们在考试前已经反复练习了词汇。

将来时:
We will drill a new oil well next month. 我们下个月将钻一口新的油井。
The instructor will drill the students on pronunciation.  教练将会让学生们练习发音。

虚拟语气:
If they had drilled deeper, they might have found water. 如果他们挖得更深一些，他们也许就能找到水了。
I wish I had drilled more before the exam. 我真希望我在考试前多练习一些。

被动语态:
The holes were drilled by a professional. 这些孔是由专业人士钻的。

疑问句:

否定句:
