presubmitting:/ˌpriːsəbˈmɪtɪŋ/| v. |预提交，预先提交 |The act of submitting something before the official deadline or formal submission.  It often implies a preliminary or tentative submission. |例句：The author is presubmitting the manuscript to get early feedback. 作者正在预先提交手稿以获得早期反馈。

例句：The company is presubmitting its environmental impact report to the regulatory agency. 公司正在向监管机构预先提交其环境影响报告。


近义词：pre-filing, advance submission, preliminary submission | 反义词：post-submission, final submission


词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He presubmits his work regularly. = 他定期预先提交他的工作。
They presubmit their proposals before the final deadline. = 他们在最终截止日期之前预先提交他们的提案。

一般过去时:
She presubmitted her application last week. = 她上周预先提交了她的申请。

现在完成时:

They have presubmitted their project plans to the client for approval. = 他们已将他们的项目计划预先提交给客户以供批准。

过去完成时:

They had presubmitted their designs to the committee for consideration. = 他们曾在提交给委员会审议之前预先提交了他们的设计。

将来时:

They will presubmit their findings to the board before the meeting. = 他们将在会议之前向董事会预先提交他们的调查结果。

虚拟语气:
