croppers:/ˈkrɒpərz/| n.|歉收的人；彻底失败的人；|People who have experienced a crop failure; those who have completely failed.|The farmers suffered as croppers this year due to the drought. 今年由于干旱，农民们遭受了歉收的苦果。| 近义词：failures,bankrupts|反义词：successes,winners|名词

一般现在时:
The farmers are croppers this year. = 今年农民们歉收了。
They are complete croppers in their business. = 他们在生意上彻底失败了。

一般过去时:
The investors were croppers in the stock market crash. = 在股市崩盘中，投资者们损失惨重。
He was a cropper after trying to start his own business. = 他创业失败后成了个失败者。
