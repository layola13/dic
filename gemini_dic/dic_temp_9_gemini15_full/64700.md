escapologist:/ɪˈskeɪpɒlədʒɪst/| n. |逃脱艺术家，脱逃术表演者 |A person skilled in escaping from confinement, often as a form of entertainment.  An escapologist uses clever techniques and illusions to free themselves from seemingly inescapable situations, such as handcuffs, straitjackets, or locked containers. |The escapologist amazed the audience with his daring escape from a submerged water tank.  逃脱艺术家以他从沉没的水箱中大胆逃脱而令观众惊叹。|illusionist, magician |jailer, captor |
