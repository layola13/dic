obstructionists:/əbˈstrʌkʃənɪsts/ | n. |阻挠者，妨碍者 |People who deliberately hinder or obstruct progress, especially in politics or legislation. They often oppose change or progress and use tactics to delay or prevent action. |例句：The senate was gridlocked by a group of obstructionists who refused to compromise. 参议院被一群拒绝妥协的阻挠者阻碍了。

The obstructionists delayed the bill's passage by introducing countless amendments. 阻挠者们通过提出无数修正案来拖延法案的通过。|近义词：blockers, hinderers, opponents, resisters |反义词：supporters, collaborators, facilitators
