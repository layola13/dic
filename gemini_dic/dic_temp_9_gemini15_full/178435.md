reconsiders:/ˌriːkənˈsɪdər/| vt.|重新考虑，再考虑|To think again about a decision, plan, or opinion.|例句：He reconsidered his decision to quit his job. 他重新考虑了辞职的决定。|例句：She reconsidered the implications of her actions. 她重新考虑了她行为的影响。|近义词：rethink, review, reconsider, reassess|反义词：confirm, endorse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reconsiders his options carefully. 他仔细地重新考虑他的选择。
They reconsider the project's feasibility every quarter. 他们每个季度都会重新考虑项目的可行性。

一般过去时:
She reconsidered her impulsive purchase. 她重新考虑了她冲动的购买行为。
The board reconsidered the proposed merger. 董事会重新考虑了拟议中的合并。

现在完成时:
He has reconsidered his stance on the issue. 他已经重新考虑了他对这个问题的立场。
They have reconsidered the budget several times. 他们已经几次重新考虑了预算。

过去完成时:
She had reconsidered her initial plan before presenting it. 在提交计划之前，她已经重新考虑了最初的计划。
The committee had reconsidered the proposal before reaching a decision. 委员会在做出决定之前已经重新考虑了该提案。

将来时:
He will reconsider his offer if the conditions are met. 如果条件满足，他会重新考虑他的提议。
They will reconsider the matter after further investigation. 他们将在进一步调查后重新考虑此事。

虚拟语气:
If he reconsidered his decision, he might be happier. 如果他重新考虑他的决定，他可能会更快乐。
I wish she had reconsidered her words. 我希望她能重新考虑她说过的话。

被动语态:
His decision was reconsidered by the committee. 他的决定被委员会重新考虑了。
The proposal is being reconsidered by the management. 该提案正在由管理层重新考虑。

疑问句:
Will he reconsider his position? 他会重新考虑他的立场吗？
Has she reconsidered her options? 她是否重新考虑了她的选择？

否定句:
He did not reconsider his decision, despite the advice. 尽管得到了建议，他还是没有重新考虑他的决定。
She will not reconsider her plans under any circumstances. 无论如何，她都不会重新考虑她的计划。
