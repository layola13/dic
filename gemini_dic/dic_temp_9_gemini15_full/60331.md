egoistical: /ˌiːɡoʊˈɪstɪkəl/ | adj. | 自私的，利己的 | excessively self-centered; preoccupied with oneself and one's own interests.  |  He was so egoistical that he never considered anyone else's feelings. 他是如此的自私，从不考虑别人的感受。 |近义词：selfish, self-centered, narcissistic, egocentric |反义词： selfless, altruistic, unselfish
