evict:/ɪˈvɪkt/| vt.|驱逐，逐出|To force (someone) to leave a place, especially a house or other property, by legal means.|The landlord threatened to evict them if they didn't pay the rent.|近义词：expel, oust, eject|反义词：allow, admit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The landlord evicts tenants who fail to pay rent. = 房东驱逐那些不付房租的租户。
He evicts trespassers from his property regularly. = 他定期驱逐擅自进入他房产的人。


一般过去时:
The court ordered the landlord to evict the tenants. = 法院命令房东驱逐租户。
They were evicted from their apartment last month. = 他们上个月被赶出了公寓。


现在完成时:
The city has evicted several homeless camps this year. = 今年市政府已经驱散了几个无家可归者的营地。
She has been evicted twice in the past five years. = 在过去的五年里，她已经被驱逐了两次。


过去完成时:
They had been evicted before they could find a new place to live. = 他们在找到新的住处之前就被驱逐了。
The family had already been evicted when the social worker arrived. = 当社会工作者到达时，这个家庭已经被驱逐了。


将来时:
The tenants will be evicted if they don't pay their rent by Friday. = 如果租客们在周五之前不付房租，他们将被驱逐。
The court will evict the squatters next week. = 法院下周将驱逐非法居住者。


虚拟语气:
If the rent was lower, they wouldn't be evicted. = 如果房租低一些，他们就不会被驱逐了。
I wish they hadn't been evicted from their home. = 我真希望他们没有被赶出家门。


被动语态:
The tenants were evicted for non-payment of rent. = 租客们因为未付房租而被驱逐。
Many families are being evicted due to the economic downturn. = 由于经济低迷，许多家庭正面临被驱逐的困境。


疑问句:
Will the tenants be evicted? = 租客们会被驱逐吗？
Have they been evicted yet? = 他们已经被驱逐了吗？


否定句:
The landlord did not evict the tenants despite their late rent payments. = 尽管租客们拖欠了房租，房东也没有驱逐他们。
They were not evicted because they negotiated a payment plan. = 他们没有被驱逐，因为他们协商了一个付款计划。


PHRASE->
evict someone from = 从…驱逐某人
be evicted for = 因…而被驱逐
face eviction = 面临驱逐
avoid eviction = 避免被驱逐
threaten eviction = 威胁驱逐
