hotfooting:/ˈhɒtfʊtɪŋ/| v.|全力奔跑，快速逃离|To run swiftly; to flee quickly.|例句：The thief hotfooted it out of the bank after grabbing the cash. 小偷抢了钱后就飞快地跑出了银行。

例句：They hotfooted it across the field to escape the angry bull. 他们飞快地跑过田野，以躲避那头愤怒的公牛。

近义词：scurry, dash, bolt, flee

反义词：saunter, stroll, amble
