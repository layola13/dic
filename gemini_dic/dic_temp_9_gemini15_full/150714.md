paraboloid:/pəˈræbəlɔɪd/| n. |抛物面|A paraboloid is a quadric surface that has exactly one axis of symmetry and no center of symmetry.  It is a three-dimensional surface formed by rotating a parabola around its axis of symmetry.  There are two types: elliptic paraboloids and hyperbolic paraboloids.|The satellite dish is a classic example of a paraboloid, designed to focus incoming radio waves. | 近义词：quadric surface | 反义词：无
