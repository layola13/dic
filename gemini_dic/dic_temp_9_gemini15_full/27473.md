campanes:/kæmˈpeɪnz/| n.|钟形物；（尤指）教堂的钟；风铃|A campanile is a bell tower, typically found in Italy, often detached from the main church building and usually square or octagonal in plan.  Campanes are the bells themselves within such a structure, or similar bells found elsewhere.|The church bells, or campanes, rang out merrily on the wedding day.  教堂的钟声，或者说是钟形物，在婚礼那天欢快地鸣响。|bells, chimes|mute, silent|名词
