hoodwink:/ˈhʊdˌwɪŋk/| vt.|欺骗，蒙蔽|To deceive or trick someone.  |例句：The salesman hoodwinked the elderly woman into buying a useless product.  售货员欺骗了这位上了年纪的妇女，让她买了一个无用的产品。
|例句：He was hoodwinked by a clever con artist. 他被一个狡猾的骗子给骗了。|近义词：deceive, trick, cheat, dupe|反义词：enlighten, inform, reveal|动词
