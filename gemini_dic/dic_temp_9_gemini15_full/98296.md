insiders:/ˈɪnsaɪdərz/| n.|内部人士，知情人士|People who have special knowledge or influence within a particular organization or group.|例句：Insiders leaked the news to the press before the official announcement. 内部人士在官方宣布之前向媒体泄露了消息。|近义词：confidants, initiates, key players |反义词：outsiders, strangers
