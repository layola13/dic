researcher:/rɪˈsɜːrtʃər/ | n. |研究人员，研究者 |A person who systematically investigates and studies a subject in order to discover and explain new knowledge.  |例句：The researcher conducted experiments to test her hypothesis.  这位研究人员进行了实验来验证她的假设。|例句：Many researchers are working on a cure for cancer.  许多研究人员正在研究癌症的治疗方法。|近义词：investigator, scientist, scholar |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who avoids intellectual inquiry.
