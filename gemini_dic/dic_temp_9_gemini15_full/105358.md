kickers:/ˈkɪkərz/| n.|侧卫，（足球）任意球主罚队员，（美式足球）踢球手，（俚语）令人兴奋的事物|A kicker is a player in several sports who specializes in kicking the ball.  In soccer, a kicker is often the player who takes free kicks or corner kicks. In American football, a kicker is responsible for placekicks (field goals and extra points) and kickoffs.|例句：The team's kicker had a perfect record this season.  这个队的踢球手本赛季保持全胜记录。The soccer team's star kicker scored the winning goal with a powerful free kick. 足球队明星侧卫以一记强劲的任意球攻入制胜球。|近义词：placekicker (美式足球), free-kick taker (足球)|反义词：receiver (美式足球)
