dorkiest:/ˈdɔːrkiɪst/| adj.|最笨拙的；最傻气的；最古怪的|Most awkward, foolish, or eccentric.  It is the superlative form of "dorky."|例句：He wore the dorkiest outfit to the party. 他穿着最傻气的衣服去参加派对。

例句：That's the dorkiest thing I've ever seen! 这是我见过的最傻的事了！


近义词：goofiest, nerdiest, geekiest,  awkwardest, most eccentric

反义词：coolest, chicest, most stylish, sophisticated


词性：形容词
