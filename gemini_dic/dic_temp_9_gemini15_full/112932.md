luggage:/ˈlʌɡɪdʒ/| n.|行李|The collective term for bags, suitcases, and other containers used to carry personal belongings when traveling.|例句：I checked my luggage at the airport. 我在机场托运了我的行李。|例句：We had too much luggage for the small car. 我们的行李太多，小汽车装不下。|近义词：baggage, bags, belongings|反义词:None
