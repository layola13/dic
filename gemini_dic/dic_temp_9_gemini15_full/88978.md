holiness:/ˈhoʊlinəs/| /ˈhəʊlɪnɪs/| n. |神圣；圣洁；虔诚|The quality or state of being holy; sacredness; sanctity.  It often refers to the divine nature of God or the state of being consecrated or dedicated to religious purposes.  It can also describe the moral excellence and purity of a person or thing.|例句：The holiness of the temple was undeniable.  神殿的神圣不容置疑。

The holiness of her life inspired many. 她圣洁的一生激励了许多人。


近义词：sacredness, sanctity, piety, godliness|反义词：profane, unholy, wickedness, impiety|
