centralizing: /ˈsɛntrəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使集中；中央集权化 | The act of concentrating or bringing something together into one place or under one authority.  It often refers to the process of consolidating power or resources in a central location or authority. |


例句：
The company is centralizing its operations in order to improve efficiency. = 公司正在集中运营以提高效率。

The government is centralizing control over the healthcare system. = 政府正在加强对医疗系统的控制。


近义词：concentrating, consolidating, unifying


反义词：decentralizing, dispersing, distributing


词性：动词
