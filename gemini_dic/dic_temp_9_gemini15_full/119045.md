meticulous:/məˈtɪkjʊləs/ | adj. |一丝不苟的；细心的；精确的 | Extremely careful and precise.  Showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise. |例句：The artist was meticulous in her work, paying attention to every tiny detail.  艺术家一丝不苟地工作，注意每一个微小的细节。

例句：He meticulously planned his presentation, ensuring every slide was perfect. 他一丝不苟地策划了他的演示文稿，确保每一张幻灯片都完美无缺。


近义词： painstaking, careful, precise, thorough, exact, scrupulous | 反义词： careless, sloppy, negligent, haphazard
