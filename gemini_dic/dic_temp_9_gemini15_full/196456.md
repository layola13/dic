serrulate:/ˈsɛrjʊleɪt/| adj.|细锯齿状的，有细锯齿的|Having fine teeth or serrations, like a saw.  The term is typically used to describe the edges of leaves or other plant parts.|例句：The leaf margins were serrulate, giving the plant a delicate appearance.  叶缘呈细锯齿状，使植物显得精致。

The edges of the saw blade were finely serrulate. 锯齿刀片的边缘非常细小且呈锯齿状。|近义词：dentate, toothed, crenate|反义词：entire, smooth
