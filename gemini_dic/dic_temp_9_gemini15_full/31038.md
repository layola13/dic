chamberlain:/ˈtʃeɪmbərleɪn/| n. |侍从；大臣；管理者|A chamberlain is a high-ranking official, historically in charge of a royal household or other important buildings.  The term can also refer to someone managing a particular area or institution.|Example:  The king's chamberlain managed the royal household. 国王的侍从管理着王室家务。The headmaster appointed a chamberlain to manage the school finances. 校长任命了一位管理者来管理学校的财务。|近义词：steward, official, administrator|反义词：subordinate, underling|名词
