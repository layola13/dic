roundworms:/ˈraʊndwɜːrmz/| n. |线虫；圆虫|Roundworms are a type of worm with a cylindrical body. They are found in a wide variety of environments, including soil, water, and the bodies of plants and animals. Many species are parasitic, meaning they live off of a host organism.|例句：Many species of roundworms are parasitic and can cause disease in humans and animals.  许多种类的线虫是寄生的，会引起人和动物的疾病。|例句：The soil sample contained a high number of roundworms. 土壤样本中含有大量的线虫。|近义词：nematodes|反义词：无
