metallographist:/ˌmɛtəlˈɒɡrəfɪst/ | n. |金相学家|A metallographist is a scientist or technician who studies the structure and properties of metals and alloys using microscopic techniques.  They prepare samples, analyze microstructures, and interpret the results to understand material behavior and improve material performance. |The metallographist meticulously prepared the sample for microscopic examination.  金相学家一丝不苟地准备了样品进行显微镜检查。|metallurgist, materials scientist| (no direct antonym, but  a counterpoint might be someone who studies non-metallic materials)|名词
