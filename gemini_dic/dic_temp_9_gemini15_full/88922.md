hold-your-ground: /hoʊld jɔːr ɡraʊnd/ | phrasal verb | 坚持立场；坚守阵地；不屈服 | To maintain one's position or stance, especially in the face of opposition or pressure.  | 例句：Despite the criticism, she held her ground and defended her ideas. 尽管受到批评，她仍然坚持自己的立场，捍卫自己的想法。


例句：The soldiers held their ground against the enemy's attack.  士兵们抵挡住了敌人的进攻，坚守阵地。


近义词：stand firm, persevere, resist, remain steadfast


反义词：give in, surrender, yield, concede


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He holds his ground in every debate. = 他在每次辩论中都坚持自己的立场。
She holds her ground despite the pressure. = 尽管有压力，她仍然坚持自己的立场。

一般过去时:
They held their ground against the onslaught. = 他们抵挡住了猛烈的攻击，坚守阵地。
He held his ground even when faced with ridicule. = 即使面对嘲笑，他也坚持自己的立场。

现在完成时:
She has held her ground throughout the negotiations. = 在整个谈判过程中，她一直坚持自己的立场。
We have held our ground despite numerous setbacks. = 尽管遭遇多次挫折，我们仍然坚持自己的立场。

过去完成时:
They had held their ground for hours before reinforcements arrived. = 在增援部队到达之前，他们已经坚守阵地数小时了。
She had held her ground throughout the previous year's campaign. = 在过去一年的竞选中，她一直坚持自己的立场。

将来时:
He will hold his ground on this issue, no matter what. = 无论如何，他都会在这个问题上坚持自己的立场。
They will hold their ground until they receive further instructions. = 他们将坚守阵地，直到收到进一步的指示。

虚拟语气:
If I were in his position, I would hold my ground. = 如果我处于他的位置，我会坚持我的立场。
I wish I had held my ground during that argument. = 我真希望在那个争论中我坚持了自己的立场。

被动语态: (This phrase is rarely used passively)  该短语很少以被动语态出现


疑问句:
Will they hold their ground against the superior force? = 他们能抵挡住强大的敌人吗？
Did she hold her ground during the interrogation? = 在审讯期间她坚持自己的立场吗？


否定句:
He didn't hold his ground and eventually gave in. = 他没有坚持自己的立场，最终妥协了。
They didn't hold their ground and were forced to retreat. = 他们没有坚守阵地，被迫撤退了。


PHRASE->
hold your ground against = 顶住…的攻击；抵挡住…
hold your ground on = 就…坚持立场
hold your ground in the face of = 面对…坚持立场
hold your ground despite = 尽管…仍然坚持立场
to hold one's ground = 坚持立场
