ribozymes:/ˈraɪboʊzaɪmz/| n. |核酶|An RNA molecule capable of acting as an enzyme, catalyzing a chemical reaction.  Ribozymes are involved in various biological processes, including RNA splicing, RNA cleavage, and peptide bond formation.  They challenge the traditional view that only proteins can function as enzymes.|Example: The discovery of ribozymes revolutionized our understanding of enzyme function. 核酶的发现彻底改变了我们对酶功能的理解。|近义词:catalytic RNA, RNA enzyme |反义词:N/A
