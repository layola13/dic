quasi-internationally:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəli/| adj. |准国际的，似国际的|Almost, but not fully, international in scope or character.  It suggests a process or system that operates across national borders but lacks the full legal or organizational framework of a truly international entity. |例句：The company operates quasi-internationally, with offices in several countries but no unified global structure. 这家公司以准国际化的方式运营，在几个国家设有办事处，但没有统一的全球结构。

例句：The agreement is quasi-internationally binding, meaning it lacks the full force of international law but carries significant moral weight. 这项协议具有准国际约束力，这意味着它缺乏国际法的全部效力，但具有重要的道义分量。


近义词：transnational, multinational, supranational, international-like


反义词：national, domestic, local


词性:形容词
