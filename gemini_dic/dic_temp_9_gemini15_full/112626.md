low-keying: /ˈloʊkiːɪŋ/ | v. |低调处理；使低调；降低音调 | To make something less noticeable, intense, or prominent; to reduce the volume or intensity of a sound.  | Example:  The company low-keyed its new product launch to avoid attracting too much attention. 公司低调处理了新产品的发布，以避免吸引过多的关注。

Example: He low-keyed his voice to avoid disturbing others. 他压低了声音，以免打扰别人。 | 近义词: downplaying, toning down, muting, subduing | 反义词: highlighting, emphasizing, amplifying, boosting | 词性:动词
