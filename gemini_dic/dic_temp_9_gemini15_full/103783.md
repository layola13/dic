journeyman:/ˈdʒɜːrnɪmən/| n. |熟练工人，技工|A journeyman is a skilled worker who has completed an apprenticeship and is now qualified to work independently.  They are typically employed by others, as opposed to being self-employed.  They have a broad range of skills and experience within their trade. |Example: After completing his apprenticeship, he became a journeyman carpenter.  他完成学徒期后成为了一名熟练木匠。
He's a journeyman plumber with over ten years of experience. 他是一位拥有十年以上经验的熟练管道工。|近义词：craftsman, tradesman, artisan|反义词：apprentice, novice
