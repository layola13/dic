revilements:/rɪˈvaɪlmənts/| n.|辱骂；斥责；谩骂 |The act of reviling; vehement or bitter criticism; abuse.  It refers to a series of insults or criticisms, often harsh and abusive.|例句：The politician faced a torrent of revilements from the angry crowd.  这位政治家面临着愤怒人群的滔天辱骂。

例句：His book was met with a storm of revilements from critics. 他的书受到了评论家们的一片谩骂。


近义词：abuse, insults, vituperation, diatribes, condemnations


反义词：praise, commendation, applause, accolades


词性: 名词
