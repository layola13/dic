reference:/ˈrefrəns/ | n. | 参考；参照；引证；证明；推荐信 | a thing that is mentioned, described, or referred to.  |  The book contains many references to classical literature. 这本书有很多关于古典文学的参考文献。 | citation, allusion, mention | irrelevance | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This essay makes frequent reference to contemporary research. 这篇文章经常引用当代的研究成果。
The professor's lecture made reference to several key theories. 教授的讲座提到了几个关键理论。


一般过去时:
The lawyer referred to the case files during the trial. 律师在审判过程中参考了案件档案。
She made reference to her previous experience in the interview. 她在面试中提到了她之前的经验。


现在完成时:
He has referred to that article several times in his presentations. 他在他的演讲中多次引用过那篇文章。
Scientists have made numerous references to this groundbreaking study. 科学家们多次提到这项开创性的研究。


过去完成时:
They had already referred to the data before the meeting. 他们在会议之前就已经参考过这些数据了。
The author had made several references to ancient history in his novel. 作者在他的小说中多次提到古代历史。


将来时:
She will refer to the manual for instructions. 她将参考手册以获取说明。
The report will reference several government publications. 该报告将引用几份政府出版物。


虚拟语气:
If I had a reference book, I would look up the answer. 如果我有一本参考书，我会查一下答案。
I wish I had a reference to support my argument. 我希望我能找到一个论据来支持我的论点。


被动语态:
The study was frequently referenced in subsequent publications. 这项研究在随后的出版物中被频繁引用。
His work has been widely referenced by scholars in the field. 他的作品被该领域的学者广泛引用。


疑问句:
Do you have any references? 你有推荐信吗？
What references are listed in the bibliography? 参考文献中列出了哪些参考文献？


否定句:
The article makes no reference to the recent controversy. 这篇文章没有提到最近的争议。
He failed to provide any references for his claims. 他没有提供任何证据来支持他的说法。
