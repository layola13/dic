filling-up: /ˈfɪlɪŋ ʌp/ | v. | 填满；装满；补充 | The act of making something full or complete.  It often implies a gradual process of filling something to capacity. | 例句：I'm filling up the car with gas. = 我正在给汽车加油。

例句：
The reservoir is slowly filling up with water. = 水库正在慢慢地蓄水。


近义词：filling, completing, replenishing, topping up
反义词：emptying, draining, depleting

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is filling up his water bottle. = 他正在装满他的水瓶。
They fill up the tank every week. = 他们每周都加满油箱。


一般过去时:
