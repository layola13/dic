nonveracity: /ˌnɒn vəˈræsɪti/ | n. |不真实；虚假；谎言 | The lack of truth or accuracy; falsehood.  It refers to a statement or action that is not truthful or accurate.  |例句：The witness's testimony was marked by nonveracity, leading to the dismissal of the case.  目击证人的证词充满了谎言，导致案件被驳回。  The research paper was criticized for its nonveracity. 这篇研究论文因其不真实性而受到批评。|近义词：untruth, falsehood, falsity, inaccuracy, deception |反义词：truth, veracity, accuracy, truthfulness |名词
