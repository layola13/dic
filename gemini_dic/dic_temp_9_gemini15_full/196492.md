service:/ˈsɜːrvɪs/| /ˈsɝːvɪs/| n., vt.| 服务；服役；维修；招待 |  The act of helping or doing work for someone.  It can refer to helpful actions, religious ceremonies, or maintenance work. |例句：The hotel provides excellent room service.  酒店提供优质的客房服务。|例句：He served in the army for ten years. 他在军队服役十年。|近义词：assistance, aid, help, ministry, maintenance|反义词：disruption, neglect, hindrance


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He serves the community through volunteering. = 他通过志愿服务社区。
She serves coffee every morning. = 她每天早上供应咖啡。


一般过去时:
They served dinner at seven o'clock. = 他们七点钟供应晚餐。
The mechanic served the car. = 技工修理了这辆车。


现在完成时:
We have served many customers today. = 我们今天已经接待了许多顾客。
The company has served the community for over 50 years. = 这家公司服务社区已有50多年了。


过去完成时:
They had served breakfast before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经供应了早餐。
The technician had serviced the machine before the failure. = 在机器故障之前，技师已经检修了它。


将来时:
We will serve lunch at noon. = 我们中午供应午餐。
They will service the equipment next week. = 他们下周将维修设备。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would serve them with a smile. = 如果我是你，我会笑着为他们服务。
I wish I had served them better. = 我希望我服务得更好。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
