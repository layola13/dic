motherland:/ˈmʌðərˌlænd/| n. |祖国，母国 |The native land or country of someone.  It evokes a strong sense of belonging and emotional attachment. |例句：He fought bravely for his motherland. 他为祖国英勇战斗。|例句：Many immigrants retain a deep love for their motherland. 许多移民仍然深爱着他们的祖国。|近义词：fatherland, native country, homeland |反义词：foreign country
