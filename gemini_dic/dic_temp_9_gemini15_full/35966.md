codefendants:/ˌkoʊdɪˈfɛndənts/ | n. |共同被告 |People who are jointly accused in a lawsuit or criminal prosecution.  Codefendants are defendants who are charged with the same crime or crimes in the same case. They may or may not be acting in concert. |例句：The codefendants were found guilty of conspiracy.  共同被告被判犯有共谋罪。

The codefendants each presented their own alibi to the judge. 各个共同被告向法官陈述了自己的不在场证明。|近义词：joint defendants, fellow defendants |反义词：plaintiff, complainant
