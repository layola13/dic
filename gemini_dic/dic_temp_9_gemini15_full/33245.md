It's designed to maintain its accuracy over extended periods, even under varying conditions.  |例句：The captain relied on the ship's chronometer to determine their longitude. 船长依靠船上的精密计时器来确定他们的经度。

例句：Scientists use atomic chronometers for incredibly precise timekeeping in experiments. 科学家们在实验中使用原子钟进行极其精确的计时。
