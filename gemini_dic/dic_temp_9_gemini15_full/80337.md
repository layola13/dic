goldfinger:/ˈɡoʊldˌfɪŋɡər/|/ˈɡəʊldˌfɪŋɡər/| n. |金手指；（尤指）詹姆斯·邦德系列电影中的人物名 |Goldfinger refers to both a character and the title of a James Bond novel and film.  The character, Auric Goldfinger, is a wealthy and ruthless criminal mastermind obsessed with gold. The name itself evokes images of wealth and power, often associated with a villainous character.  |The film "Goldfinger" is a classic in the James Bond series.  |近义词: tycoon, magnate, plutocrat |反义词: pauper, beggar
