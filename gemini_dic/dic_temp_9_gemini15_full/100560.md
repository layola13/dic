interword:/ˈɪntərˌwɜːrd/ | adj. | 词间的；字间的 |  Relating to or occurring between words.  This term is primarily used in linguistics and computational linguistics to describe phenomena or processes that happen between words in a sentence or text. For example, it can refer to the spaces between words, the relationships between adjacent words, or the influence of one word on another in terms of meaning or pronunciation.  | Example:  Interword spacing is crucial for readability.  词间空格对可读性至关重要。 The interword relationship between "big" and "house" affects the overall meaning. “大”和“房子”之间的词语关系影响整体含义。 | 近义词：between-word | 反义词：intraword (within-word) | adj.
