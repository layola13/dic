mosaics:/moʊˈzeɪɪks/| n. |马赛克；镶嵌图案 |A mosaic is a picture or decoration made of small pieces of colored stone, glass, or tile.  Mosaics are often used to decorate floors, walls, and ceilings. |例句：The ancient Roman mosaics are remarkably well-preserved. 古罗马的马赛克保存得非常好。

例句：The artist created a stunning mosaic depicting a mythical scene.艺术家创作了一幅描绘神话场景的令人惊叹的马赛克作品。


近义词：inlay, tessellation |反义词：monolith, uniformity


词源:  The word "mosaic" comes from the Latin word "musivum," which ultimately derives from the Greek word "musaïkós" (μουσαϊκός), meaning "of the Muses," referring to the artistic skill involved in creating them.  The Muses were the goddesses of arts and sciences in Greek mythology.
