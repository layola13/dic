fledglings:/ˈfledʒlɪŋz/ | n. |雏鸟；新手；初学者 | Young birds that have recently left the nest and are not yet able to fly well.  Also refers to someone who is new and inexperienced in a particular field. |例句：The fledglings chirped loudly, begging for food from their parents.  这些雏鸟大声鸣叫，向父母要食物。

例句：He's a fledgling in the world of finance, still learning the ropes. 他是金融界的新手，仍在学习业务。


近义词：newbie, novice, beginner, tyro | 反义词：expert, veteran, professional | 词性：名词
