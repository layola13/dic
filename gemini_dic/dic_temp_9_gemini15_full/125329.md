multilaminate:/ˌmʌltiˈlæmɪneɪt/| adj. |多层合板的；多层压合的 |Consisting of or relating to multiple layers bonded together.  It often refers to materials like wood, plastic, or metal that have been laminated together.|The countertop is made of a multilaminate surface, resistant to scratches and heat.|近义词：stratified, layered, composite |反义词：monolithic, single-layered
