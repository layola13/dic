overclaiming:/ˌoʊvərˈkleɪmɪŋ/| v. |过分声称；夸大；索赔过多 |The act of claiming or stating something to be more or greater than it actually is; exaggerating one's accomplishments, abilities, or entitlements.  |例句：His overclaiming of expertise led to a disastrous project.  他的夸大其词导致项目失败。
His overclaiming on his tax return led to an audit. 他在纳税申报单上夸大金额导致了税务审计。|近义词：exaggeration, hyperbole, boasting, inflation |反义词：understatement, downplaying, minimizing
