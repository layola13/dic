pericarpic:/ˌpɛrɪˈkɑːrpɪk/| adj. |果皮的，关于果皮的|Relating to or characteristic of the pericarp, the fruit wall that develops from the ovary wall of a flower after fertilization.  The term often refers to the structure and properties of the pericarp itself, as well as its role in seed dispersal and protection.| Example: The pericarpic tissue of this fruit is particularly tough, protecting the seeds from damage.|近义词:fructescent,  fruit-related|反义词:seed-related
