nebula:/ˈnɛbjʊlə/|/ˈnɛbjʊlə/| n. |星云|A nebula is a cloud of gas and dust in outer space, visible as a luminous patch or spot.  Nebulae often form from the remnants of dying stars or serve as stellar nurseries, where new stars are born.|Example: The Orion Nebula is a famous example of a stellar nursery, visible to the naked eye.|近义词：cloud (in space), cosmic cloud|反义词：star (in contrast to a diffuse nebula)
