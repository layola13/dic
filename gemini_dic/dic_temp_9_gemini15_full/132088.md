noncreditableness:/ˌnɒnˈkredɪtəblənəs/ | n. |不可信度，不可靠性 |The lack of credibility; the state of being unworthy of belief or trust.  It refers to something that is not believable or trustworthy.  The noncreditableness of his story led to his arrest.  他的故事不可信，导致了他被捕。 |近义词：unreliability, incredibility, untrustworthiness |反义词：credibility, trustworthiness, reliability |名词
