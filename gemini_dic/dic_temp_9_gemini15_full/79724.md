gluer: /ˈɡluːər/ | n. v. | 胶粘剂；粘合剂；粘合的人 | A person or thing that glues; an adhesive substance. |例句：He used a strong gluer to repair the broken vase. 他用强力胶粘合剂修补了破裂的花瓶。  He's a gluer of communities, bringing people together. 他是社区的粘合剂，把人们团结在一起。|近义词：adhesive, binder, cement, paste |反义词：separator, divider


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He glues the pieces together. = 他把碎片粘在一起。

一般过去时:
