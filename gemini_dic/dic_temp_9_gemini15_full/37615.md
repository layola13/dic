communion:/ˈkəˈmjuːnjən/| n.|圣餐；共享；交流；团契|The act of sharing or exchanging something, especially feelings or ideas;  a religious ceremony, especially the Eucharist in Christianity.|例句：The communion service was held in the morning. 早上举行了圣餐仪式。|例句：We felt a deep sense of communion with nature. 我们感到与大自然的深刻联系。|近义词：fellowship, sharing, participation, unity|反义词：isolation, separation, alienation|名词
