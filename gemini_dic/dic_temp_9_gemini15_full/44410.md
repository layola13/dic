cross-license:/ˈkrɔːslaɪsns/| v. n.|交叉许可，互授许可|To grant each other licenses to use each other's intellectual property.|例句：The two companies agreed to cross-license their patents.  两家公司同意交叉许可他们的专利。|近义词：reciprocal license, mutual license|反义词：exclusive license


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cross-license their technologies. = 他们交叉许可他们的技术。
The companies cross-license their patents annually. = 各公司每年交叉许可其专利。


一般过去时:
We cross-licensed our software last year. = 我们去年交叉许可了我们的软件。
The firms cross-licensed their trademarks in 2020. = 这些公司在2020年交叉许可了它们的商标。


现在完成时:
They have cross-licensed their inventions since 2015. = 自2015年以来，他们一直在交叉许可他们的发明。
The two companies have cross-licensed their intellectual property for mutual benefit. = 两家公司交叉许可了彼此的知识产权以互惠互利。


过去完成时:
Before the merger, they had cross-licensed their key technologies for several years. = 在合并之前，他们已经交叉许可了他们的关键技术多年。
The negotiations had stalled because they couldn't agree on the terms of the cross-license. = 谈判陷入僵局，因为他们无法就交叉许可的条款达成一致。


将来时:
We will cross-license our new software next year. = 我们明年将交叉许可我们的新软件。
The research institutions plan to cross-license their research findings. = 这些研究机构计划交叉许可他们的研究成果。


虚拟语气:
If they agreed, we would cross-license our patents. = 如果他们同意，我们将交叉许可我们的专利。
It would be beneficial if both companies cross-licensed their respective technologies. = 如果两家公司交叉许可各自的技术，那将是有益的。


被动语态:
The patents were cross-licensed by the two firms. = 这两家公司的专利被交叉许可。
Their technologies are being cross-licensed under a new agreement. = 他们的技术正在根据一项新协议进行交叉许可。


疑问句:
Will the companies cross-license their inventions? = 各公司会交叉许可他们的发明吗？
Have they already cross-licensed the software? = 他们已经交叉许可软件了吗？


否定句:
They did not cross-license their technologies. = 他们没有交叉许可他们的技术。
The negotiations failed because they couldn't agree on a cross-license. = 谈判失败了，因为他们无法就交叉许可达成协议。
