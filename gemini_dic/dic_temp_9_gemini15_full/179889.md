refaced:/riːˈfeɪst/| vt.|重新装修；翻新；更换表面|To cover or treat (a surface) again, especially to improve its appearance or condition.  It often involves replacing a worn or damaged surface with a new one. |例句：The old building was refaced with modern materials. 这座旧建筑用现代材料进行了重新装修。

例句：They refaced the kitchen cabinets to update the look of the room.  他们重新装修了厨房橱柜以更新房间的外观。


近义词：remodel, renovate, refurbish, resurface

反义词：deface, ruin, damage

词性:动词
