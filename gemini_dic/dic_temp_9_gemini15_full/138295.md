notch:/nɑːtʃ/| n. v.|刻痕；凹口；等级；改进；切口；|A notch is a V-shaped cut, indentation, or groove.  It can also refer to a level or degree of quality or achievement.|例句：He carved a notch in the tree to mark his progress. 他在树上刻了个缺口来标记他的进度。  The company reached a new notch in sales this quarter.  这家公司本季度销售额达到了一个新的高度。|近义词：indent, groove, score, level, degree|反义词：smooth, flat
