deliracy:/ˈdɛləri/| n.|精神错乱，疯狂；胡言乱语|Deliracy refers to a state of severe mental derangement or insanity, characterized by irrational behavior and incoherent speech.  It often implies a loss of contact with reality.|例句：His deliracy was evident in his rambling speech and erratic behavior. 他的精神错乱在他的语无伦次和古怪行为中显而易见。|例句：The doctor attributed his deliracy to the effects of the drug. 医生认为他的精神错乱是药物的影响。|近义词：insanity, madness, lunacy, dementia|反义词：sanity, lucidity, rationality
