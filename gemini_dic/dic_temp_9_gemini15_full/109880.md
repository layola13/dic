liable:/ˈlaɪəbl/| adj.|有义务的；有责任的；易于…的；可能的 | Legally responsible; subject to; likely.  |例句：The company is liable for any damages caused by its products.  该公司对其产品造成的任何损害负有责任。
例句：He is liable to get angry if you criticize him. 如果你批评他，他很可能会生气。|近义词：responsible, accountable, subject to, prone to |反义词：irresponsible, immune, safe from


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is liable for the debt. = 他对这笔债务负有责任。
The company is liable to pay taxes. = 这家公司有义务纳税。

一般过去时:
They were liable for the accident. = 他们对这起事故负有责任。
The building was liable to collapse. = 那栋建筑物有倒塌的危险。

现在完成时:
He has been liable for the project since its inception. = 自项目启动以来，他一直对此负责。
The government has been liable to criticism for its handling of the crisis. = 政府因其处理危机的方式而一直受到批评。

过去完成时:
She had been liable for the previous project. = 她对之前的项目负有责任。
The bridge had been liable to collapse for years. = 那座桥多年来一直有倒塌的危险。


将来时:
They will be liable for any future losses. = 他们将对任何未来的损失负责。
The new policy will make companies liable for data breaches. = 新政策将使公司对数据泄露负责。


虚拟语气:
If he were liable, he would compensate the victim. = 如果他负有责任，他会赔偿受害者。
If the company were liable, it would have to pay a fine. = 如果公司负有责任，它将不得不支付罚款。

被动语态:
The driver was held liable for the damage. = 司机被判对损害负责。
The company is being held liable for environmental pollution. = 这家公司因环境污染而被追究责任。

疑问句:
Is the landlord liable for the repairs? = 房东有义务修理吗？
Are we liable for the costs incurred? = 我们要对发生的费用负责吗？

否定句:
The company is not liable for lost items. = 公司不负责丢失的物品。
He was not liable for the damages. = 他不应对损害负责。


PHRASE->
strictly liable = 严格责任
liable to = 易于…；可能…
jointly and severally liable = 共同及个别负有责任
liable for damages = 对损害赔偿负责
vicariously liable = 替代责任


词源:
The word "liable" comes from the Old French word "liable," meaning "subject to," which in turn derives from the Latin word "ligare," meaning "to bind."  The sense of legal responsibility developed later.
