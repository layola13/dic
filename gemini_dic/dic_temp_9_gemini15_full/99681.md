interlinking:/ˌɪntərˈlɪŋkɪŋ/| adj.|相互关联的，相互连接的|Describes things that are connected or linked together.  It emphasizes the reciprocal nature of the connection, suggesting a network or system of relationships.|The interlinking of roads in the city makes transportation efficient.|近义词： interconnected, interrelated, linked, connected|反义词：disconnected, separate, isolated|形容词
