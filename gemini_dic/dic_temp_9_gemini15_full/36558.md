collapsar:/kəˈlæpsɑːr/| n. |坍缩星|A collapsar is a type of astronomical object that forms when a massive star collapses at the end of its life, resulting in a spectacular explosion known as a hypernova.  This collapse creates a black hole and powerful jets of energy. |Example: The recent discovery suggests that the observed gamma-ray burst originated from a collapsar. 最近的发现表明，观测到的伽马射线暴起源于一颗坍缩星。|近义词：hypernova (in the context of the resulting explosion), stellar black hole (the resulting object)|反义词： expanding star (opposite process)|名词
