on-the-cards:/ˌɒnðəˈkɑːdz/| adj. |可能的，预料之中的|Something that is on the cards is likely to happen.  This idiom suggests that an event is a strong possibility, though not yet certain.|例句：With the current economic climate, a recession is on the cards.  在当前的经济环境下，经济衰退是不可避免的。

例句：Another interest rate hike is on the cards for next month.下个月再次加息的可能性很大。


近义词：likely, probable, expected, foreseeable

反义词：unlikely, improbable, unexpected, unforeseen

词性：形容词
