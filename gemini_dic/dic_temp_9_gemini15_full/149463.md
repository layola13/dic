paddles:/ˈpædəlz/ | n. v. |桨；划桨；拍打 |A paddle is a tool used for propelling a boat or canoe through water.  It can also refer to the act of using a paddle to move a boat or to the act of striking or slapping something. |例句：He used his paddles to navigate the calm waters. 他用桨在平静的水面上划行。


The children were happily paddling in the shallows. 孩子们在浅水区快乐地玩水。

近义词：oar, blade (in the context of boat propulsion)  spatula (in the context of cooking)

反义词：N/A (depends on context)


词性：名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He paddles the canoe across the lake. 他划着独木舟穿过湖泊。
She paddles in the swimming pool every morning. 她每天早上在游泳池里游泳。


一般过去时:
They paddled upstream against a strong current. 他们逆着强流向上游划去。
We paddled for hours before reaching the island. 我们划了几个小时才到达岛屿。


现在完成时:
They have paddled across the ocean. 他们已经划过海洋。
She has paddled many rivers in her life. 她一生中划过许多河流。


过去完成时:
They had paddled for miles before they realized they were lost. 他们在意识到迷路之前已经划了好几英里。
We had paddled all day and were exhausted. 我们划了一整天，筋疲力尽了。


将来时:
We will paddle down the river tomorrow. 我们明天将沿着河流向下游划去。
They will paddle to the next island next week. 他们下周将划到下一个岛屿。


虚拟语气:
If I had a kayak, I would paddle to the other side of the lake. 如果我有一艘皮划艇，我会划到湖的另一边。
I wish I could paddle like an expert. 我希望我能像专家一样划桨。


被动语态:
The canoe was paddled expertly across the white water. 这艘独木舟熟练地划过激流。
The river was paddled by many tourists. 这条河被许多游客划过。


疑问句:
Do you know how to paddle a canoe? 你知道如何划独木舟吗？
Will you paddle with me? 你愿意和我一起划船吗？


否定句:

词源:
The word "paddle" comes from the Old English word "paddan," meaning "to dabble in water" or "to paddle."  This is related to the Old Norse word "paddi," meaning "a small, flat piece of wood."  The modern meaning expanded to include the implement itself as well as the action of using it.
