rabbinical:/ræˈbɪnɪkəl/| adj.|犹太教拉比的；拉比的；| Relating to rabbis or Judaism.  The term often refers to the teachings, laws, or interpretations associated with rabbis and Jewish religious scholarship. |例句：He studied rabbinical literature for many years. 他学习犹太教拉比文献多年。

例句：The rabbinical court ruled on the matter. 拉比法庭对这件事作出了裁决。


近义词：rabbinic, talmudic (referring to the Talmud)


反义词：N/A (no direct antonym, but could consider secular as a contrasting term)

词性：形容词
