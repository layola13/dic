grey:/ɡreɪ/ | adj. |灰色的，阴沉的，暗淡的 | Describing the color between black and white; also used to describe something gloomy, dull, or lacking in brightness.  |The sky is grey today.  今天天空是灰色的。|The future looks grey. 未来看起来一片黯淡。|dark, dusky, drab |bright, colorful, vivid |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat is grey. = 猫是灰色的。
The sky is often grey in autumn. = 秋天天空经常是灰色的。

一般过去时:
Her hair was grey when she was young. = 她年轻的时候头发就灰白了。
The house was painted a dull grey. = 房子漆成了一种暗淡的灰色。

现在完成时:
My hair has gone grey. = 我的头发变白了。
The painting has become grey with age. = 这幅画随着时间的推移变得暗淡了。

过去完成时:
The walls had been grey before we repainted them. = 我们重新粉刷之前，墙是灰色的。
He had already gone grey by the time he was forty. = 他到四十岁的时候就已经头发花白了。


将来时:
The weather forecast says it will be grey tomorrow. = 天气预报说明天将是阴天。
I think the walls will look grey with that furniture. = 我认为用那套家具的话，墙壁看起来会是灰色的。


虚拟语气:
If the sky were grey, we would stay inside. = 如果天空是灰色的，我们就待在室内。
I wish the world wasn't so grey. = 我希望世界不是这么灰暗。


被动语态:
The walls were painted grey. = 墙壁被漆成灰色的。
The photograph was made to look grey. = 照片被处理成灰色的。


疑问句:
Is your car grey? = 你的车是灰色的吗？
Are those clouds grey? = 那些云是灰色的吗？


否定句:
Her eyes are not grey; they're blue. = 她的眼睛不是灰色的；是蓝色的。
The picture isn't grey; it's sepia. = 这张照片不是灰色的；是棕褐色的。
