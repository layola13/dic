proclaimers:/prəˈkleɪmərz/| n. |宣告者；公布者；声明者 |Those who publicly announce or declare something.  They often make known important information or decisions. |例句：The proclaimers of the new law were met with both cheers and protests. 新法的宣告者受到了欢呼和抗议的两种反应。

例句：The proclaimers of the revolution inspired many to join the cause.  革命的宣告者激励许多人加入事业。


近义词：announcers, heralds, declarers, proclaimers

反义词：concealers, suppressors, hushers


词性：名词
