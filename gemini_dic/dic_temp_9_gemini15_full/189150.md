society: /səˈsaɪəti/ | n. | 社会，社团，协会 |  Society is a group of people living together in a more or less ordered community.  It can also refer to a particular group of people with shared interests or activities. |  例句：Modern society is facing many challenges. 现代社会面临着许多挑战。  |  例句：She joined the local horticultural society. 她加入了当地的园艺协会。|近义词：community, association, group | 反义词：isolation, solitude | 名词
