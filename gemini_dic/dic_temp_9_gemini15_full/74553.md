fraudful:/ˈfrɔːdʒʊl/| adj.|欺诈的；不正当的| characterized by, involving, or obtained through fraud.  It implies intentional deception for personal gain.|例句：The company was accused of fraudulent accounting practices. 这家公司被指控犯有欺诈性会计行为。

例句：He was found guilty of fraudulent misrepresentation. 他被判犯有欺诈性虚假陈述罪。


近义词：deceitful, dishonest, deceptive, sham, fraudulent

反义词：honest, legitimate, truthful, genuine
