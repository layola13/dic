give-ear:/ɡɪvˈɪər/| v.|侧耳倾听，专心听|To listen attentively; to pay close attention to what is being said.|例句：The children gave ear to the storyteller's tales. 孩子们聚精会神地听着讲故事的人讲故事。|例句：She gave ear to the whispered conversation. 她仔细听着窃窃私语。|近义词：listen attentively, heed, pay attention|反义词：ignore, disregard, neglect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They give ear to the teacher's instructions. = 他们认真听老师的指示。
We give ear to the wise counsel of our elders. = 我们认真倾听长辈的明智建议。

一般过去时:
The audience gave ear to the speaker's passionate words. = 听众认真倾听了演讲者充满激情的演讲。
He gave ear to the rumours spreading in the town. = 他仔细听了镇上流传的谣言。

现在完成时:
I have given ear to your concerns, and I will address them. = 我已经认真听取了你的担忧，我会处理它们。
They have given ear to many different perspectives on the issue. = 他们已经认真倾听了关于这个问题的许多不同观点。

过去完成时:
She had given ear to the prophecy before making her decision. = 在做出决定之前，她已经听取了预言。
They had given ear to the warnings, but ignored them anyway. = 他们曾经听取过警告，但还是忽略了。

将来时:
We will give ear to the arguments presented by both sides. = 我们将认真倾听双方提出的论点。
The judge will give ear to the testimony of the witnesses. = 法官将认真倾听证人的证词。

虚拟语气:
If only I had given ear to his advice! = 要是我当初听取了他的建议就好了！
I wish I had given ear to my intuition. = 我真希望我当时听了我的直觉。

被动语态:
The speaker's words were given ear by the captivated audience. = 听众被演讲者的讲话深深吸引，认真倾听了。 (This is less common for this verb)
His concerns were not given ear by the management. = 管理层没有理会他的担忧。(This is less common for this verb)

疑问句:
Did they give ear to your complaints? = 他们有认真听取你的抱怨吗？
Will the committee give ear to our proposal? = 委员会会认真考虑我们的提案吗？

否定句:
They did not give ear to the warnings. = 他们没有理会警告。
The government didn't give ear to the people's pleas. = 政府没有理会人民的请求。


PHRASE->
give ear to gossip = 听信小道消息
give ear to reason = 听取劝告
give ear to complaints = 倾听抱怨
lend an ear = 倾听（更常用）
turn a deaf ear = 置若罔闻 (反义)
