interested:/ˈɪntrəstɪd/ | adj. | 感兴趣的，关心的 | Showing or feeling interest.  |例句：I am interested in learning about different cultures. 我对学习不同的文化感兴趣。|例句：She seemed genuinely interested in my problems. 她似乎真的关心我的问题。|近义词：curious, fascinated, eager, keen |反义词：uninterested, apathetic, indifferent |形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is interested in music. 他对音乐感兴趣。
They are interested in learning a new language. 他们对学习一门外语感兴趣。

一般过去时:
She was interested in history. 她对历史感兴趣。
We were interested in the new project. 我们对新项目感兴趣。

现在完成时:
I have been interested in photography for many years. 我对摄影感兴趣很多年了。
They have been interested in politics since college. 他们从大学时代就对政治感兴趣。

过去完成时:
He had been interested in art before he started his career. 他在他开始职业生涯之前就对艺术感兴趣。
She had been interested in medicine before she changed her major.  在她改变专业之前，她对医学感兴趣。


将来时:
I will be interested to see the results. 我很想知道结果。
They will be interested in hearing your opinion. 他们会很乐意听到你的意见。

虚拟语气:
If I were interested in politics, I would join a party. 如果我对政治感兴趣，我会加入一个政党。
I wish I were more interested in exercise. 我希望我对运动更感兴趣。


被动语态:
The students were interested in the topic that was discussed in class. 学生们对课堂上讨论的话题感兴趣。
The audience was interested in the speaker's presentation. 观众对演讲者的演讲感兴趣。


疑问句:
Are you interested in this job? 你对这份工作感兴趣吗？
Is he interested in joining our club? 他有兴趣加入我们的俱乐部吗？


否定句:
I am not interested in gossip. 我对八卦不感兴趣。
They weren't interested in attending the meeting. 他们对参加会议不感兴趣。


PHRASE->
be interested in 对…感兴趣
take an interest in 对…产生兴趣
show interest in 表现出对…的兴趣
have an interest in 对…有兴趣
lose interest in 对…失去兴趣
