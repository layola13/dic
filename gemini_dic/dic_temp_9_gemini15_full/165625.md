premillennial: /ˌpriːmɪˈleniəl/ | adj. | 千禧年前的；预言千年王国在基督再来之前实现的 |  Relating to or believing in the premillennial view of the Second Coming of Christ, in which Christ will return to Earth before a 1,000-year reign of peace (the millennium).  This view contrasts with postmillennialism and amillennialism. |  Many premillennial Christians believe in a literal interpretation of biblical prophecy. | 近义词：pre-millennial | 反义词：postmillennial, amillennial | 词性：形容词
