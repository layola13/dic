panspermatist: /ˌpænˌspɜːrmətɪst/ | n. | 泛种论者 | A believer in panspermia, the hypothesis that life exists throughout the Universe, distributed by meteoroids, asteroids, and comets, and that life on Earth may have originated from such extraterrestrial sources.  | Example:  The panspermatist argued that the discovery of organic molecules in meteorites supported their theory. 泛种论者认为在陨石中发现有机分子支持了他们的理论。 | 近义词：exobiologist (if focusing on the scientific aspect) | 反义词：N/A (no direct antonym, but someone who believes life originated solely on Earth could be considered an opposing view)
