scepticisms: /ˈsɛptɪsɪzəmz/ | n. | 怀疑主义；怀疑态度 | The plural form of skepticism, referring to multiple instances or types of doubt or disbelief.  It represents a general distrust or questioning of claims, assertions, or beliefs. |  例句：His numerous scepticisms regarding the project's feasibility delayed its commencement.  他的许多关于项目可行性的怀疑推迟了项目的启动。


例句：The prevailing scepticisms surrounding climate change are a significant obstacle to effective action.  围绕气候变化的普遍怀疑是有效行动的重大障碍。


近义词：doubts, uncertainties, misgivings, incredulity
