disburser:/dɪsˈbɜːrsər/ | n. | 支付者；发放者 | One who disburses funds or other resources.  A disburser is responsible for the distribution of money or other assets. | The company appointed a new disburser to manage its payroll.  这家公司任命了一位新的支付员来管理工资。  The disburser carefully checked the amounts before distributing the funds. 发放者在分发资金前仔细核对了金额。 | distributor, dispenser, payer | receiver, collector | 名词
