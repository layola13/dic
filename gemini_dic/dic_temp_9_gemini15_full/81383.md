graspers:/ˈɡræspərz/ | n. | 抓取器，掌握者 |  A grasping device or tool; someone who grasps or understands something.  |Example: The robot uses powerful graspers to manipulate objects.  机器人使用强大的抓取器来操纵物体。

The surgeon used specialized graspers to perform the delicate operation. 外科医生使用专门的抓取器进行精细手术。|近义词：grippers, holders, comprehenders |反义词：releasers, abandoners |名词
