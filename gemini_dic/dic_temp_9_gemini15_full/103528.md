joe-sixpack:/ˈdʒoʊ sɪksˈpæk/| n.| 普通老百姓，一般人 |An ordinary, average man, often used to represent the common working-class male. The term often implies a lack of sophistication or intellectualism.  |Example: Joe Sixpack is more concerned with the price of beer than the political climate.  普通老百姓更关心啤酒的价格而不是政治气候。|近义词：average man, common man, ordinary person|反义词：elite, intellectual, celebrity|名词
